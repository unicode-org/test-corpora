CHITSAUKO 33. Mutungamiri weSpeksnyder.

Nge ndaa ye vakuru ve whale-craft, iyi inoita inga ndau yakanaka inga iri yeshe yekuisa pashi chiro chidoko chepamuzi pa ship-board, chinobva pakuvapo kwe harpooneer kirasi yevakuru, kirasi isikazikanwi zvemene mune imweni marine kusiya kwe whale-fleet.

Kukosha kukuru kwakabatanidzwa kune harpooneer vocation kunoonekwa ngechokwadi, kuti pakutanga muDutch Fishery yekare, mazana maviri emakore uye kupfuura apfuura, murairo wengarava ye whale wakanga usikazi kugara zvakakwana mumunthu zvino unodaidzwa kuti captain, asi wakaparadzaniswa pakati pake nemukuru wehowe unozwi Specks. Kureva kuti izwi iri rinoreva kuti Mutema-Mafuta; kushandiswa, asi, munguva yakaita kuti zvienzane neMukuru Harpooneer. Mazuwa awo, simba ro mutungamiri wo ngarava rainga rinogumira basi pa kuhamba no kutonga ngarava; apo pamusoro pedhipatimendi rekuvhima howe uye zvese zvainesa, Specksnyder kana Mukuru Harpooneer akatonga zvakanyanya. Mu British Greenland Fishery, pashi pezina rakashata re Specksioneer, mukuru uyu wekare we Dutch achiri kuchengetedzwa, asi kuremekedzwa kwake kwekare kwakapfupiswa zvinosuruvarisa. Pazvino anogara zviri nyore semukuru weHarpooneer; uye sezvo zvakadaro, asi imwe yevatungamiriri vengarava vakaderera zvikuru. Zvakanyadaro, kudai nge kujibata kwakanaka kwe vafambisi ve harpooneers kubudirira kwe rwendo rwe howe rwe whale kunoenderana kakurutu, uye ngekuti mu American Fishery aasi mukuru we chikepe ega, asi muzviro zvimweni (kurinda usiku pa ndau ye whale) murairo we chikepe nge wakewo; saka chirevo chikuru chezvematongerwo enyika chegungwa chinode, kuti ararame ngezita akasiyana ngevarume pamberi pechikepe, uye ave ngenjira imwe kusiyaniswa semukuru wavo weunyanzvi; kunyange zvazvo nguva dzose, navo, zvakajairika zvinoonekwa sezvavo zvakaenzana zvemagariro.

Zvino, kusiyana kukuru kwakaitwa pakati pemukuru wehondo nemurume mugungwa, ndiwo uyu—wekutanga anogara kumashure, wekupeisira kuenda mberi. Ngo kudaro, mu ngarava jo whale no mu vatengesi, vandhu vanoroorana vanogara no mutungamiriri wo ngarava; uye saizvozvo, zvakare, mune vazhinji veAmerica whalers harpooneers vanogara muchikamu chekumashure chengarava. Kureva kuti, vanotora zvekurya zvavo mukamuri remukuru wengarava, voata pandau isina kunanga inobhuya nayo.

Kunyazi nguwa yakareba ye rwendo rweku Southern whaling (nge kuretu kupfuura rwendo rwese rwuripo zvino kana rwakamboitwa ngemunhu), ngozi dzinoshamisa dzaro, uye nharaunda yekudakarira inokunda pakati pekambani, veshe, vakakwirira kana vakaderera, vanovimba ngezvibereko zvavo, kwete pamubhadharo wakagadzwa, asi parombo ravo rakafanana uye hatrepidi yavo, pamwepo, pamwepo; kunyazi zviro zveshe izvi zvinoita mune zvimweni zviitiko zvinowanzobereka kurangwa kusinganyanyi kuoma kudarika kune vatengesi kazhinji; asi, musambofunga kuti zvakafanana zvakadini nemhuri yekare yeMesopotamia aya ma whalemen anga, mune zvimwe zviitiko zvekare, kugara pamwe chete; nge ndaa ye zveshe izvi, zviro zve kubanze zvinosisira maningi, zvishoma, zve kota-deck azvinyanyi kudekara, zve azvizi kupera. Igwinyiso, zvikepe zvakawanda zvo Nantucket zvamunozovona mutungamiri wo ngarava ecikhombijira ndau yake yo quarter-deck ngo kunaka kwakanyanya kucikazopindiki mu ngarava iri yese yo hondo; haiwa, kutora ruremekedzo rwakawanda rwekunze sekuti akapfeka ngubo yepepuru yeumambo, uye kwete shabbiest ye pilot-cloth.

Uye nyangwe pavanhu veshe mukuru wePequod wakaita zviro zvisina shwiro ndiye wakapiwa zvishomani kune rudzi urwu rwekufunga kusina kudzaka; uye kunyange zvazvo kuremekedza kwega kwaakamboita, kwaiva kuteerera kwakajeka, kwekukurumidza; kunyazi asizi kuda kuti munhu abvise shangu pamakumbo ake asati atsika pa quarter-deck; uye kunyange zvazvo paiva nenguva apo, nge ndaa ye mamiriro ezvinhu akasiyana-siyana akabatana ne zviitiko zvinotevera kuti zvinyatsotsanangurwa, akavabhuya nge mazwi asina kujairika, kana ekuzvininipisa kana in terrorem , kana zvimweni; asi kunyange Kapiteni Ahabhi aazivi kumbovona zviro zvinosisira maningi no kushandiswa kwe gungwa.

Kana, pamweni, azvizokoni kuzozwisiswa pakupejisira, kuti sure kwe mafomu aya no kushandiswa kwazvo, inga zvaibhuyiwa, iyena nguva jimweni waizvifisha; zvichiitika kuzvishandisa kune zvimwe uye zvakawanda zvepachivande zvinangwa kupfuura zvavaifanira kuita zviri pamutemo. Sultanism iyoyo ye pfungwa dzake, iyo yainge iripo mune imwe nzvimbo yakanaka yakaramba isingaratidzi; kuburikidza nemafomu iwayo kuti sultanism imwechete yakave muviri muhutongi hweudzvanyiriri husingadzivisiki. Kudai ngekuti kuite ungwaru hwemunhu hukuru hwezvinoda, haikoni kumbofunga zvinoshanda, zviripo hukuru pamusoro pevamweni varume, pasina rudetsero rwemhando imwe yeunyanzvi hwekunze uye entrenchments, nguva dzeshe, mukati mwavo, zvakawanda kana zvishoma paltry uye base. Izvi ndizvo, zvinochengeta mhetsa nariini machinda egwinyiso aMwari eUmambo kubva kune hustings dzenyika; uye inosiya kuremekedzwa kwakanyanya iyo mhepo iyi inokona kupa, kune avo varume vanova nemukurumbira zvikuru kuburikidza nekuderera kwavo kusingaperi kune kusarudza kwakavanzika kweruoko rweDivine Inert, pane kuburikidza nekupfuura kwavo kusina mubvunzo pamusoro pezinga rakafa revanhu vakawanda. Kunaka kukuru kwakadaro kunogara muzviro zvidoko izvi apo zvitendero zvakanyanya zvematongerwo enyika zvinozviisa, kuti mune dzimweni nguva dzeumambo kunyange kune benzi imbecility vakapa simba. Asi apo, inga zvakaitika kuna Nicholas the Czar, korona yakashongedzwa ngo utongi hwo ndau inotendeneja ngqondo yo umambo; zvino, mapoka e plebeian anogwadama akadzika pamberi pekuisa pakati zvakanyanya. Uyewo, mutambi unosuruvarisa uyo unozokhombise kusadzivirirwa kwevanhu mukutsvaira kwayo kwakadzara uye kutenderera kwakananga, achazombokanganwa chirevo, chinonyanya kukosha muunyanzvi hwake, sezvinorehwa zvino.

Asi Ahabhi, Kaputeni wangu, achiri kuhamba pamberi pangu mukushata kwake kweshe kweNantucket nekushata; uye muchikamu ichi chinobata Madzimambo nemadzishe, andifaniri kufisha kuti ndine zvekuita ngemurombo wekare whale-hunter kudai ndiye; uye, ngendaa iyoyo, zviro zveshe zvekubanze zvinokudzwa nedzimba zvinondirambidzwa. Haiwa, Ahabhi! chiinyi chinozoita chikuru mwauri, chinosisira kubviswa kubve mudenga, nekudzika mukati mwekudzaka, nekuonekwa mumhepo isina muiri!





CHITSAUKO 34. Kabhini-Tafura.

Kwava masikati; uye Dough-Boy, mutariri, achisundire chiso chake chakajeka chechingwa-chechingwa kubva mu cabin-scuttle, anozivisa kurya kwemadekoni kuna ishe wake na tenzi wake; uyo, akagara mu lee quarter-boat, achangobva kutora kuona zuva; uye zvino ari kuverenga latitude asina kubhuya pachinyorwa chakatsetseka, chakaita inga medallion, chakachengeterwa chinangwa ichocho chezuva ngezuva pachikamu chepamusoro chemurenje wake wenyanga yenzou. Kubva pa kutama kuzwisisa kwake kamare masoko aya, munozorangarira kuti Ahabhi wainga no mazwiro o kujidodokesa aazivi kuzwa kujidodokesa kwake. Asi nguva ino, akabata zvifukidzo zve mizen, anozvizunguza kuenda pachikepe, uye ngezwi rakaenzana, risingadakadzi, eiti, “Kudya kwemadekoni, VaStarbuck,” anonyangarika mukati mekamuri.

Panozopera echo yekupeisira ye nhanho yake ye sultan, uye Starbuck, Emir wekutanga, une chikonzero cheshe chekufunga kuti akagara, zvino Starbuck anomuka kubva mukunyarara kwake, anotora matende mashomani pamwepo nemapuranga, uye, mushure mwekupedza guva mu binnacle, anoti, ngekubata kumwe kwekudakadza Mr. Stubb, uye Dinner. Emir wechipiri unogara pashi pe rigging kwechinguwa, ochozozunguza zvishomani brace huru, kuti aone kuti zvichaita zvakanaka ere nge tambo iyo yakakosha, iyewo unotora mutoro wekare, uye ngekukasira “Kudya kwemadekoni, VaFlask,” unoteera sure kwevakamutangira.

Asi Emir wechitatu, zvino eidziona ari ega pa quarter-deck, unoita inga wakasunungurwa ngekudzivirira kumwe kwekuda kuziya; ngekuti, tipping mhando dzeshe dzekuziya winks mumhando dzeshe, nekurova shangu dzake, unorova mu squall yakapinza asi isina ruzha ye hornpipe pamusoro pesoro ra Grand Turk; uyezve, nge dexterous sleight, akasimudza chivharo chake mudenga mu mizentop kuti aite shelf, anodzika rollicking kusvika kure zvishoma sezvo anoramba achioneka kubva padhizaini, achidzosa mamwe mamwe maprocessions eshe, ngekuunza kumusoro kwesure ngemimhanzi. Asi asati apinda mumusiwo wekamuri iri pashi, unoema, unotumira chiso chitsva zvachose, uye, ipapo, Flask mudoko wakazvimirira, unoshekedza unopinda pamberi paMambo Ahabhi, muchimiro chaAbjectus, kana Muranda.

Izvo azvizi kuderera pakati pezviro zvinoshamisa zvakabarwa ngekugadzirwa kwakanyanya kwekushandiswa kwegungwa, kuti apo vari mumhepo yakajeka yechikepe vamweni vakuru vanozo, pakutsamwa, vanozvitakura ngekushinga uye ngekuzvidza zvakakwana kune mukuru wavo; asi, gumi kune umwe, ngaaite kuti vakuru avo vega nguwa inoteera vaende pashi ku kurya kwavo kwemadekoni kwetsika mukamuri remukuru wehondo imweyo, uye ngekukasira mhepo yavo isingakhuvazi, kwete kuronza mhepo inozvidza uye inozvininipisa kwaari, sezvaakagara pamusoro petebhura; izvi zvinoshamisa, dzimwe nguva zvinosekesa zvikuru. Ngenyi musiyano uwu uripo? Dambudziko here? Pamweni haiwa. Kuti aive Bherishazari, Mambo weBhabhironi; uye kuti aive Bherishazari, kwete nekuzvikudza asi nekuremekedza, izvo zvemene zvinofanira kuva nekubata kumwe kwehukuru hwepasi. Asi uwo uri mu mweya wakafanira wo utongi no wo ungwaru unotungamirira tafura yake yo kurya yo vandhu vakakokwa, simba ro mundhuwo ricikapikiswa no kutonga ko mundhu ega ega pa nguvayo; umambo hwo mundhuwo hunodarika hwa Bherishazari, ngokuti Bherishazari aacainga mukuru maningi. Ndiani asi akambodya shamwari dzake, akaravira kuti kuva Cæsar zvinomboita sei. I witchery ye czarship yemagariro iyo isina inodzivirira. Zvino, kana kufunga uku iwe ukawedzera hukuru hwepamutemo hwemukuru wengarava, saka, ngekufungidzira, uchawana chikonzero chekusiyana kwacho kwehupenyu hwegungwa hwatambotaurwa.

Pamusoro po tafura yake yakashongedzwa ngo nyanga yo nyanga, Ahabhi waitungamirira inga shumba yo mu jombe isizi kubhuya, ine bvudzi pa ndau yakacena yo coral, yakatendenejwa ngo vana vake vanoda hondo asi vaciri kureremeja. Pa nguva yake yakafanira, mukuru umwe nga umwe waivetera kuti ashandirwe. Vaiya vakadai ngevana vadoko pamberi paAhabhi; uye asi, muna Ahabhi, zvaiita inga pasina kuzvikudza kuduku kwevanhu. Nge pfungwa imwe, madziso avo akarongeka ese akasungirirwa pabanga remurume mukuru, apo aiveza ndiro huru pamberi pake. Andifungi kuti nge ndaa ye nyika vaizosvibisa nguwayo nge kuona zvishoma, kunyazwi pamusoro pe musoro usina kwawakarerekera kudai nge mamiriro ekunze. Hayiwa! Uye paakatambanudza banga rake ne forogo, pakati payo pakanga pakavharwa chikamu chenyama yemombe, Ahabhi wakakhombe ndiro ya Starbuck kwaari, mudiwa wakaashira nyama yake inga wakaashira zvipo; uye kuicheka zvinyoro-nyoro; uye zvishoma zvakatanga kana, zvichida, banga rakafudza ndiro; uye akazvitsenga pasina ruzha; uye akarimedza, kwete pasina kungwarira. Ngokuti, kudai nge mabiko e Coronation ku Frankfort, apo Mambo we German anodya zvikuru nge vasarudzi vanomwe ve Imperial, kudaro zvikafu izvi zve mu cabin zvainge zviri zvikafu zvakaremekedzwa, zvaidyiwa mukunyarara kunotyisa; asi patafura Ahabhi mukuru haana kurambidza kutaura; iye ega ndiye aive mbeveve. Zvaiva kurerukirwa kwakadini kuvhunga Stubb, apo mbeva yakaita racket yekukurumidza mu hold iri pashi. Uye Flask murombo mudoko, ndiye mwanakomana mudoko, uye mujaha mudoko wepati iyi yemhuri yakaremba. Ake aiva mapfupa enyama yemombe ine munyu; yake ingadai iri ngoma. Kuti Flask azvikudze kuti adzidetsere, izvi zvinofanira kunge zvakaita inga zvakafanana nekuba pakutanga. Dai akadzidetsera patafura iyoyo, pasina kumbozengurira, aisazombokwanisa kusimudza soro rake munyika ino yakatendeseka; zvisinei, zvinoshamisa kutaura kuti Ahabhi haana kumbomurambidza. Uye dai Flask akazvidetsera, mikana yainge iripo kuti Ahabhi aisambozviona. Zvishoma pane zveshe, Flask wakazvikudza kuti azvidetsere kuita butter. Chimweni chiro. Flask ndiye munhu wekupeisira kudzaka pakurya kwemadekoni, Flask ndiye munhu wekutanga kukwira. Kunyinda! Nekuti ngekudaro kudya kwemadekoni kwaFlask kwainge kwakanyanya kuvharwa munguwa. Starbuck na Stubb veshe vainge nekutanga kwake; uye asi vanewo chikomborero chekuzorora kumashure. Kana Stubb kunyange, uyo ari asi peg yakakwirira pane Flask, akaitika kuti aine asi chido chidoko, uye ngekukasira anokombidza zviratidzo zvekupedza repast yake, saka Flask anofanira kuzvibestir, haazowani zvinopfuura miromo mitatu zuva iroro; nokuti zvinopesana nekushandiswa kutsvene kuti Stubb atange Flask kuenda kudhizaini. Hino zvakaitika kuti Flask wakambotenda ari ega, kuti kubve paakasimuka ku chiremerera che mukuru wehondo, kubve nguwayo aazi kumboziya kuti chiinyi kuita zvimweni kusiya kwe nzara, zvakawanda kana zvishoma. Ngokuti zvaakarya azvizi kunyanya kurerutsa nzara yake, asi kuzvichengeta zvisingafi mwaari. Runyararo nekugutsikana, akafunga Flask, zvakabva zvachose mudumbu rangu. Ndiri mukuru wemauto; asi, ndinoshuva sei kuti ndibate hove shoma yemombe yekare mu forecastle, sezvandaiita pandaive pamberi pe mast. Pane michero yekusimudzira zvino; pane zvisina maturo zvekubwinya: pane upengo hwehupenyu! Padera peizvozvo, kudai zvaizoitika kuti mufambisi wengarava we Pequod wainge ne mwoyo wakashata kuna Flask mu ndau ya Flask, zveshe zvaifanira kuitwa nge mufambisi we ngarava, kuti awane kudzorera kwakakwana, kwaiva kuenda sure nguwa ye kurya kwemadekoni, ne kuona Flask kuburikidza nge chiedza che mudenga, akagara benzi uye akashamisika pamberi.

Zvino, Ahabhi no shamwari jake ndhatu vakaita cingadaninjwa kuti tafura yo kutanga mu kanyi yo Pequod. Musure mwekuenda kwavo, zvichiitika mukurongeka kwakadzoka kuguma kwavo, jira rechidziro rakacheneswa, kana kuti rakapetudzwa kune imwe kurongeka kwekukurumidza ngemutariri wakachena. Mukusiyana kunoshamisa nekudzivirira kusingakwanisi kushivirirwa uye kutonga kusingaoneki kusina mazita kwetafura yemukuru wengarava, kwaiva nechitupa chese chekusachengeteka uye kureruka, democracy yakapotsa yakashata yeavo vashomani vanoita zvekutamba. Kuti, mu cokwadi cokadi, vandhu vanoroorana no vanoita zviro zvo n’anga vo Pequod vangabhuyiwa zvakanaka kuti vaigara kubanze ko kambi kupinda mukati mwayo. Ngokuti pavakapinda mweiri, zvakaita inga musuwo wemugwagwa unopinda mumhatso; kutendeuka mukati kwechinguvana, kungotendeuka kunze kunotevera; uye, sechinhu chisingaperi, chinogara mumhepo yakasununguka. Uye avazi kurashikirwa nge zviro zvakawanda nge ndaa iyi; mukamuri remukati mwakanga pasina kushamwaridzana; mumagariro, Ahabhi aisakwanisa kusvika. Pikija hazvo kuti wakabatanijwa ngo zina pa kuverengwa ko maKristudhomu, iyena wainga aciri mumweni kwaari. Wakagara munyika, sezvo wekupedzisira weGrisly Bears aigara muMissouri yakagadzikana. Uye kudai ngekuti Spring ne Summer zvakaenda, Logan wemushango uyo wemusango, wakadziviga mugomba remuti, wakagara kubanze kwemuchando apo, eiyamwisa makumbo ake; saka, mu inclement yake, kuchembera kunoungudza, mweya waAhabhi, wakavharirwa mubako remuviri wake, apo wakadya pamakumbo akasuruvara echidima chawo!





CHITSAUKO 35. Musoro-weMast.

Yainge nguwa yekunakidza zvikuru, kuti mukutenderera kwakakodzera nevamwe vafambisi vezvikepe musoro wangu wekutanga we mast-head wakauya wakatenderera.

Mu whalemen dzakawanda dze America mast-heads anotungamirirwa nguva imweyo nge ngarava inobuda pa port yake; kunyazi angaite makiromita zviuru gumi neshanu, nezvimweni, kuti aende ngechikepe asati asvika pandau yake yakafanira yekuhamba. Uye kana, mushure mwe rwendo rwe makore matatu, mana, kana mashanu ari kuswedera pedyo ne kanyi ne chiro chisina chiro mukati mwake—tingabhuya kuti, chipunu chisina chiro kunyange—ipapo, misoro yake ye mast inochengetwa ine vanhu kuguma kumagumo; uye haiwa kusvikira skysail-poles yake inofamba pakati pezvikomo zvechikepe, anosiya zvachose tariro yekubata howe imwe chete.

Zvino, sezvo bhizimisi rekumira mast-heads, pamhenderekedzo kana pamhenderekedzo, riri rekare yaamho uye rinodakadza, ngatiite zvimweni zviyero pano. Ndinozvitora, kuti vamiri vekutanga vemast-heads vaiva vaEgipite vekare; nekuti, mukutsvakurudza kwangu kwese, handiwani chero pamberi pavo. Ngokuti kunyazwi madzinza avo, vaaki ve Bhabheri, vanofanira pasina kumboreketa, nge rusvingo rwavo, vakafunga kurera mast-head yakakwirira maningi mu Asia yeshe, kana Africawo; asi (ere rori yekupeisira yakaiswa pairi) sekuti nhanho iyo huru yemapuwe yavo ingabhuyiwa kuti yakaenda ngepa bhodhi, mudutu rinothya rekutsamwa kwaMwari; ngo kudaro, atingakwanisi kupa vaaki ava vo Bhabheri ndau yo kutanga kupinda vaEjipita. Uye kuti vaEgipite vainge rudzi rwe mast-head standers, ndiko kugondisa kunobva pakutenda kwakawanda pakati pe archæologists, kuti mapiramidhi ekutanga akatangwa kuitira zvinangwa zve nyeredzi: pfungwa imwe chete inotsigirwa nge peculiar stair-like formation yemativi ese mana ezvivakwa izvi; nge ndaa iyoyo, nge kusimudza kureba kwe mirenje yavo, avo vazivi ve nyeredzi vekare vaijaira kukwira kuguma pamusoro, ne kuimba nge nyeredzi itsva; kunyange zvazvo zvinotarisa-kunze kwengarava yemazuva ano zvinoimba kuti zvifambe, kana kuti hove huru inongooneka. Mu Saint Stylites, muKristu wakakurumbira wakagara ega wekare, wakamuaka mbiru yakakwirira yemabwe mugwenga akapedza chikamu chekupeisira cheupenyu hwake hweshe pamusoro payo, eisimudza chikafu chake kubva pashi nge tackle; mwaari tine muenzaniso unoshamisa wekumira-we-mast-heads asingatyi; uyo aisafanira kudzingwa kubva pandau yake nemhute kana chando, mvura inonaya, mvuramabwe, kana chando; asi valiantly kutarisana zvose kunze kusvika pakupedzisira, chaizvoizvo akafa pa post yake. Kune vanoema-ve-mast-misoro yemazuva ano tine asi seti isina hupenyu; varume vemabwe, simbi, nendarira chete; avo, kunyange zvazvo vanokwanisa kutarisana nedutu rakasimba, vachiri vasingakwanisi zvachose kuita bhizinesi rekuimba pavanowana chimwe chinhu chinoshamisa. Kune Napoleoni; uyo, pamusoro pembiru yeVendome, akaema akapeta maoko, anenge zana nemakumi mashanu emamita mumhepo; asina hanya, zvino, ndiani anotonga madhizaini pazasi; kana Luwi Firipi, Luwi Blanc, kana Luwi Dhiyabhorosi. Great Washington, nayewo, wakaema padera-padera pa n’anga yake yakakura maningi mu Baltimore, zve kudai nge imwe ye mbiru dza Hercules, mbiru yake inokombidza ndau iyoyo ye ukuru hwe anhu inopfuura iyo vashomani vanofa vanozoenda. Admiral Nelson, nayewo, ari pa chikepe che pfuti-simbi, wakaema musoro wake we pfuti mu Trafalgar Square; uye nguva dzeshe apo zvakanyanya kuvhiringidzwa ngeutsi ihwohwo hweLondon, chiratidzo chichiri kupiwa kuti gamba rakavanzika riripo; nekuti pane utsi, panofanira kuva nemwoto. Asi kunyazwi Washington mukuru, kana Napoleon, kana Nelson, avazodaviri mvuramabwe imwe kubva pashi, kunyazwi vakakumbirwa ngekupenga kuti vashamwaridzane nge mazano avo madhizaini akavhiringika ayo avanoningirana nawo; asi zvingafungidzirwa, kuti mweya yavo inopinda kuburikidza nge mhute yakaoma ye ramangwana, uye inoshora kuti ndeapi ma shoals uye ndeapi matombo anofanira kudziviswa.

Zvingaita inga zvicikafaneri kusanganisa mu njira iri yese vandhu vanoema pa musoro wo nyika no vanoema pa gungwa; asi kuti mu cokwadi azvizivi kudaro, zvinovoneswa pambhene ngo ciro cinozwi Obed Macy, mutari wo nhoroondo ega wo Nantucket, anozvidavirira. Obed wakafanira unotibhuyira kuti, mu nguva yo kutanga yo kubata hove yo whale, ngarava jicikatanga kuendeswa nguva jese kuti jiteverere mutambowo, vandhu vo pa ciwi ico vaiaka zviphambano zvakakwirira pa ndau iri phedo no gungwa, apo vandhu vaizokwira ngo kushandisa zviphambano zvakasungwa ngo zvipikiri, ciro cinoita inga shiri dzinokwira. Makore mashomanana apfuura urongwa humwe hwakaashirwa nge Bay whalemen ye New Zealand, avo, pavakashora mutambo, vakapa chiziviso kune zvikepe zvakagadzirira vanhu pedyo negungwa. Asi tsika iyi yava kupera simba zvino; tinotendeuka zvino kune imwe yakakodzera mast-head, iyo ye whale-ngarava mugungwa. Misoro mitatu ye mast inochengetedzwa ngevanhu kubva pakubuda kwezuwa kuguma pakuvira kwezuwa; vafambisi vezvikepe vechitora nguva dzavo dzenguva dzose (sepachigaro), uye vechirerutsana maawa maviri ega ega. Mu mamiriro ekunze akadzikama e tropics zvinodakadza zvikuru mast-head; haiwa, kumurume anofungisisa anorota zvinofadza. Ipopo unoema, makubiti zana pamusoro pemadhizaini akanyarara, weihamba ngenjira yakadzaka, ingatei ma masti aiva madziro makuru, apo pashi pako nepakati pemirenje yako, ingatei, unodhuvha zvipuka zvikuru zve gungwa, kunyazwi ngarava dzaimbohamba pakati pemabhutsu e Colossus ane mbiri ku Rhodes yekare. Ipopo unoema, wakarashika mumutsara usingaperi wegungwa, pasina chiro chakavhunduka asi masaisai. Ngarava yakadzikama inotenderera zvisina simba; mhepo dzinotengesa dzinorara dzinovhuvhuta; zviro zveshe zvinokugadzirisa kuti uite languor. Kune chikamu chikuru, muhupenyu huno hwekupisa whaling, kusaitika kwakanyanya kunokuisa; auzwi nhau; kuverenga hapana magazeti; extras ine nhoroondo dzinoshamisa dzenzvimbo dzakajairika hadzimbokunyengeri muzvifaro zvisingakoshi; unozwa kuti hapana matambudziko epamba; zvidziviriro zvakaparara; kuwa kwemasitoko; avamboneseki ngo kurangarira kuti munozoitanyi pa kurya ko madeko—ngokuti zvokuha zvenyu zvese ko makore matatu no kupfuura zvinongwaririhwa zvakanaka mu mabhokisi, zve mare yenyu yo kwenda haicinja.

Mu umwe wo vandhu vo ku maodzanyemba, pa rwendo rwakareba hwo makore matatu kana mana, inga zvinoitika kazinji, kusanganiswa ko nguva jakasiana-siana jamunopeja pa mast-head kwaizoguma pa mweji jakawanda jakakwana. Uye zvinosuruvarisa zvikuru kuti ndau iyo yaunopa chikamu chikuru chenguva yese yehupenyu hwako hwechisikigo, inofanira kunge yakasuruvarisa zvikuru isina chero chii zvacho chinoswedera kune inogara zvakanaka, kana yakagadzirirwa kubereka nzvimbo yakasununguka yekuzwa, kunge inoenderana nemubhedha, a hammock, a pulpit a, chero hearse a, uye snug contrivances umo varume vanozviparadzanisa kwenguva pfupi. Nzvimbo yako inonyanya kugara yekugara ndiyo soro re t' gallant-mast, apo unoema pamusoro pematanda maviri akaonda akasiyana (anoda kusiyana ne whalemen) anonzi t' gallant cross-trees. Pano, wakapondwa ngo gungwa, mundhu anotanga anojizwa wakadekara inga zvaaizoita paakaema pa nyanga yo mombe. Kuti uve no cokwadi, mu nguva yo kutonhora unokwanisa kuthwara nyumba yako mudenga, wakaita inga jasi ro kungwarira; asi kubhuya zvakanaka ngubo yekurinda yakanyanya kuoma haisi yemhatso kupfuura muiri usina kupfeka; ngokuti kudai nge mweya wakanamatira mukati mwe tabhenakeri yawo ye nyama, ne kutama kuhamba wakasununguka mweiri, kana kutobuda mweiri, usikaiti ngozi huru ye kuparadzwa (kudai nge mufambi usikazi kuziya unoyambuka Alps ine chando muchando); saka watch-coat haisi yakawanda yeimba sezvo iri hamvuropu chete, kana ganda rakawedzerwa rinokufukidza. Aukoni kuisa shelf kana bhokisi rezviro mumuiri wako, zve auzokwanisi kuitazve chitoro chakanaka che watch-coat yako.

Nge ndaa ye zveshe izvi, zvinosuruvarisa maningi kuti mast-heads yo ngarava yo whale yo kumaodzanyemba aizi kupiwa matende madoko aya anodakajisa kana mapuranga, anodaninjwa kuti crow’s-nests , umo maonero o Greenland whaler anongwaririhwa kubve ku mamiriro o nyika akashata o makungwa akaoma. Mu ndaa ye mwoto ya Kaputeni Sleet, ine musoro unoti “Rwendo pakati pe Icebergs, kutsvaka Greenland Whale, uye nge njira imweyo nge ndaa yekuwanazve Makoloni e Iceland Akarashika e Greenland Yekare;” mubhuku iri rinoyemurika, veshe vanoema pa mast-heads vanopuwa nge ndaa inodakadza ye mamiriro ezvinhu e crow’s-nest ye Glacier yakagadzirwa nguva pfupi yapfuura, iyo yaive zita re unyanzvi hwakanaka hwa Captain Sleet. Wakaidaidza kuti dendere re gunguwo re Sleet , kuzviremekedza; iye ari mugadziri wekutanga uye patentee, uye akasununguka kubva kune zveshe zvinoshekedza zvenhema delicacy, uye kubata kuti kana tikadaidza vana vedu pachedu ngemazina edu (isu madzibaba tiri vagadziri vekutanga uye patentees), saka zvakafanana tinofanira kudenominate mushure mwedu chero chimwe chishandiso chatinokona kubereka. Muchimiro, dendere regunguwo reSleet chiro chakadai nge tierce huru kana pombi; yakavhurika padera, asi, apo yakashongedzwa nge side-screen inofamba-famba kuti irambe iri kumhepo ye soro rako mu dutu rakasimba. Kudai wakaemeswa pashi pe mast, unokwira mukati mwayo kuburikidza nge trap-hatch shomani iri pashi. Ku divi ro sure, kana divi riri phedo no sure ko ngarava, kuno cigaro cakanaka, pashi paco pano ndau inogara masamburera, ma comforter, no ma jasi. Pamberi pane chigadziko cheganda, umo unochengeta hwamanda yako yekubhuya, pombi, teresikopu, nezvimweni zviro zvinodetsera mumvura. Paakange Captain Sleet pachake akaema mast-head yake mudendere rake re gunguwo iri, anotibhuyira kuti waigara aine pfuti naye (yakasungirirwawo mu rack), pamwepo ne powder flask ne shot, nge chinangwa chekubudisa narwhales dzakatsauka, kana vagters sea unicorns dzinoparadza iyo wa; ngokuti amukoni kubudirira kuvapfura kubve pa deck nge ndaa ye kuramba kwe mvura, asi kuvapfura pashi chiro chakasiyana maningi. Zvino, zvakajeka kuti mushando we rudo kuna Captain Sleet kutsanangura, sezvaanoita, zveshe zvishoma zvakadzama zvakadzama zve dendere rake re gunguwo; asi kunyazi akakura kudaro pamusoro pezvizhinji zveizvi, uye kunyazi akatibata nge ndaa ye sainzi maningi ye kuedza kwake mu dendere iri re gunguwo, nge kambasi doko yaakachengetapo nge chinangwa chekudzivirira zvishaishi zvinobva pane zvinozwi “kukwezva kwe ndau” kwe magnets eshe e binnacle; kukanganisa kunoronzwa nge ndau yakapamhama ye simbi iri mu mapuranga engarava, uye mu ndaa ye Glacier, zvimwe, nge ndaa yekuti pakanga pane vapfuri vakawanda vakaparadzwa pakati pe vashandi vake; Ndinoti, kuti kunyazwi Kaputeni wakangwara maningi ne science pano, asi, nge ndaa ye zveshe zvaakafunda “kusiyana kwe binnacle,” “kuona kwe kambasi ye azimuth,” ne “zvishaishi zvinofungidzirwa,” unoziya zvakanaka, Kaputeni Sleet, kuti aazi kunyatsonyudzwa mune iyo yakadzama yekufungisisa ye magnetic littledward, kuti akundire kuita . case-bottle, yakasungirirwa zvakanaka kurutivi rumwe rwedendere rake regunguwo, iri nyore kusvika ngenyara yake. Kunyazi, pamusoro pezveshe, ndinoyemura zvikuru uye ndinotoda Kaputeni wakashinga, wakagondeka, uye wakafunda; asi ndinozvitora zvakashata maningi kwaari kuti aizorekeredza zvachose bhodhoro re kesi iroro, kuona kuti shamwari yakagondeka nemunyaradzi waifanira kunge wakaita sei, apo aine zvigunwe zvakasungwa nge mittened uye soro rakavharwa nge hooded aifunda masvomhu mudenga apo mudendere reshiri iyoyo mukati mema perches matatu kana mana e tsanga.

Asi kana isu vabati vehove vekuMaodzanyemba tisingagari zvakanaka mudenga sezvakaita Captain Sleet neGreenlandmen yake; asi kusashata ikoko kunopesana zvikuru ngekudzikama kwakasiyana-siyana kwemakungwa aya anokwezva ayo isu vabati vehove veKumaodzanyemba tinonyanya kuyangarara. Kune imwe, ndaigara ndeizorora pamusoro pe rigging ndakasununguka maningi, ndeizorora pamusoro kuti ndibhuye na Queequeg, kana munhu weshe asisiri pamushando wandinokona kuwanapo; zvino kukwira njira shomani kuenda mberi, nekukanda murenje une usimbe pamusoro pechivanze chepamusoro-chechikepe, kutora maonero ekutanga emafuro emvura, uye saka pakupedzisira kukwira kuenda kwandinopedzisira kuenda.

Ngandiite zamu rakachena rayo pano, uye ndibvume zvakajeka kuti ndakachengeta asi sorry guard. Nge dambudziko re nyika yeshe richitenderera mukati mwangu, ndaizoita sei—ndakasiiwa zvachose ndega pa nzvimbo yakakwirira yakadaro inoita kuti ndifunge—ndaizoita sei asi kubata zvishoma zvisungo zvangu zvekuchengeta zvirevo zvese zve whale-ships zvakaema, “Ramba wakavhura ziso rako remamiriro ekunze, uye uimbe nguva dzose.”

Uye nganditenderei pandau iyi ndikupangirei ngekukurudzira, imwimwi varidzi vengarava veNantucket! Ngwarira kunyoresa muhove dzako dzakangwarira chero mukomana ane ganda rakaonda uye ziso rakadzara; kupiwa kune kufungisisa kusina nguva; uye uyo anopa kuendesa ngarava nePhædon panzvimbo yeBowditch musoro wake. Ngwarirai munhu wakadaro, ndinoti; whales dzako dzinofanira kuonekwa dzisati dzaurayiwa; uye uyu muPlatonist wechidoko ane maziso akadzika achakukwevera mawake gumi kutenderera nyika, uye haazombokuiti kuti pint imwe yembeu ipfume. Uyewo izvi zvirevo hazvidiwi zvachose. Kudai ngekuti mazuwa ano, kubata howe dze whale kunopa utiziro kune majaha akawanda anodanana, anosuruvara, uye asina pfungwa, anonyangadzwa ngekushungurudzika kwenyika, uye anotsvaka manzwiro mu tar uye blubber. Childe Harold aazi kugara eigara pashi pe musoro we chikepe chimweni chisina rombo rakanaka, uye nge mazwi anonyangadza anobudisa:—

“Kubudikira, iwewe gungwa rakadzaka uye rakasvipa rebhuruu, kubudikidza!
Zviuru gumi zvevavhimi ve blubber vanokutsvaira pasina.”

Kazinji maningi vatungamiriri vo ngarava jakadaro vanotora vazivi vocidoko vaya vasingazivi zviro, vecivatsiura ngo kutama kuzwa “kudakarira” kwakakwana pa rwendo hwo ngarava; hafu-hinting kuti vakarashika zvikuru pasina tariro kune zveshe zvinoremekedzwa zvido, sekuti mumweya yavo yakavanzika vanoda kusaona whales pane zvimweni. Asi zveshe zviri pasina; vechidoko avo vePlato vane pfungwa yekuti chiono chavo hachina kukwana; vanoona zvishoma; zvinoshandiswa chii, saka, kushungurudza tsinga yekuona? Vakasiya magirazi avo e opera-pamba.

"Ngei, iwewe mhuka," wakadaro harpooneer kune umweni wemajaha aya, "tainga teihamba ngechikepe zvine simba pamakore matatu, asi auzi kumbofuya howe. Howe dzinoshomeka kudai ngemazino ehuku nguwa yeshe yeunonge uri mudenga pano." Pamweni vainge vari; kana kuti pamwe pakanga paine mapoka avo munzvimbo iri kure; asi lulled mu opium-yakafanana listlessness ye vacant, isina kuziva reverie ndiyo iyi wechidoko asina pfungwa ngekusanganisa cadence yemasaisai nemifungo, kuti pakupedzisira anorasikirwa nekuzivikanwa kwake; anotora gungwa rakavanzika patsoka dzake kuitira mufananidzo unooneka wemweya iwoyo wakadzaka, webhuruu, usina mudziyo, unopararira vanhu uye zvisikwa; uye chiro cheshe chinoshamisa, chinooneka hafu, chinotsvedza, chakanaka chinomudzivirira; imwe neimwe dimly-yakaonekwa, kusimuka fin imwe isingazivikanwi chimiro, zvinoita kwaari embodiment kuti izvo zvisingazivikanwi pfungwa kuti chete vanhu mweya kuburikidza kuramba flitting kuburikidza nayo. Muchidakadaka ichi chekurota, mweya wako unoderera kuenda kwawakabva; inopararira kuburikidza nenguva uye nzvimbo; kunge Cranmer akasasa Pantheistic madota, achiumba pakupedzisira chikamu chegungwa rega rega nyika yakatenderera pamusoro.

Akuna upenyu mwauri, zvino, asi upenyu hunozunguzika hunopuwa nge ngarava inozunguzika zvinyoro-nyoro; naye, akakwereta kubva kugungwa; pedyo negungwa, kubva kumafungu asingazivikanwi aMwari. Asi apo hope iyi, hope iyi iri pamuri, fambisa rutsoka kana nyara yenyu inch imwe; kutsvedza kubata kwako zvachose; uye kuzivikanwa kwako kunodzoka mukutya. Pamusoro pemavhunduka eDescartian iwe unobhururuka. Uye pamwe, pakati peusiku, mumamiriro ekunze akanaka maningi, ngekushevedzera kumwe kwakadzingwa hafu unodonhera kuburikidza ngemhepo iyo yakajeka kupinda mugungwa remuchirimo, pasinazve kukwira mhetsa nariini. Muzvizwe zvakanaka, imwimwi vaPantheist!





CHITSAUKO 36. Chikwata-Dhizaini.

( Pinda Ahabhi: Pasurepo, zveshe. )

Asipi nguva yakareba sure kwe ndaa ye pombi, kuti rimweni mangwanani sure kwe kurya kwe mangwanani, Ahabhi, kudai nge tsika yake, wakakwira mu cabin-gangway kuenda ku deck. Ikoko vazhinji ve sea-captains vanowanzohamba nguwa iyoyo, sevakuru vemunyika, mushure mwekurya kumwe, vanotora matendero mashomani mubindu.

Ngekukasira, kuhamba kwake kwakagwinya, kwenyanga yenzou kwakazwika, sezvo kuenda ngekupetuka aihamba ngekutenderera kwake kwekare, pamapuranga aiziikanwa kakurutu ngekutsika kwake, zvekuti ainge akatsemuka eshe, kudai ngemabwe e geological, nge chiratidzo chinoshamisa chekuhamba kwake. Wakaningirazve ere, pamusoro pehuma iyoyo ine mbabvu uye yakatsemuka; apowo, waizovona zviro zvicikazivi kujeka—zviro zvo murangariro wake umwe basi ucikazivi kuata, waigara weihamba.

Asi pa mukuvo uri kubvunjwa, mavhundu aya aivoneka inga akadzaka, pikija hazvo nhanho yake yo kuthya mangwananiwo yakasiya ciro cakadzaka. Uye, wakadzara nge pfungwa yake ndi Ahabhi, zvekuti pakutendeuka kweshe kwakafanana kwaakaita, zvino pa main-mast uye zvino pa binnacle, waikona kuda kuona pfungwa iyoyo ichitendeuka mwaari paaitendeuka, nekuhamba mwaari paaihamba; saka kumubata zvakakwana, zvemene, kuti zveshe asi zvaiita inga chiumbwa chemukati chekufamba kweshe kwekunze.
