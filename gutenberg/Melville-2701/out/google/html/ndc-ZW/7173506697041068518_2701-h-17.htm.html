CHITSAUKO 86. Muswe.

Vamweni vadetembi vakabhuya nge rumbidzo dze ziso rakapfava re shiri, ne minhenga inodakadza ye shiri isingamboburuki; zvishoma zvekudenga, ndinopemberera muswe.

Kuerenga muswe we Sperm Whale wakakura maningi kuti utange pa ndau iyoyo ye muti apo unoderera kuguma pa girth yo mundhu, unosanganisira pashi pawo wepadera ega, ndau inokwana makumi mashanu emamita. Muiri wakatenderera wakabatana we nzira yawo unotambanuka kuita maoko maviri akafara, akasimba, akatsetseka kana ma flukes, zvishoma nezvishoma anotsvedza kusvika pasi pe inch muhupamhi. Pa crotch kana pa junction, ma flukes aya anobatana zvishoma, ochodzoka kumativi mamweni inga mapapiro, osiya ndau yakakura pakati. Mu chiro chipenyu hapana mitsara yekunaka inotsanangurwa zvakanaka kudarika mumiganhu ye crescent yema flukes aya. Pakukura kwayo kwakanyanya mu whale yakakura yakadzara, muswe unozopfuura zvikuru makubiti makumi maviri kupfuura.

Nhengo yeshe inoita inga yakaoma webbed mubhedha welded tsinga; asi cheka mukati mwayo, wowana kuti zvikamu zvitatu zvakasiyana zvinoiumba:—yepamusoro, yepakati, uye yepasi. The fibers ari pamusoro uye pasi ndiro, akareba uye yakapamhama; avo vepakati, vakapfupika zvikuru, uye vanomhanya crosswise pakati pezvikamu zvekunze. Iyi njira ye triune, kudai nge zvimweni zviro, inopa simba kumuswe. Ku mujiji wo madziro o kare o Roma, ndau yo pakati inozopa kusiyana kunodakajisa no njira yakatetepa yo matairi anogara eicinja-cinja no buwe mu zviro zvinoshamisa zvo kare, zve zvicikaiti mubvunjo zvinobesera maningi ku simba guru ro masonry.

Asi ingatei simba iri guru ro mundau iri mu muswe we tendinous rainga risizi kukwana, ndau yeshe ye leviathan yakarukwa nge warp ne woof ye muscular fibers ne filaments, iyo inopfuura kumativi maviri e chiuno ne kurumba kuenda mu flukes, inosanganiswa zvisina kujeka navo, uye inobatsira zvikuru kusimba ravo; kuti mumuswe confluent zvisingaenzaniswi simba hove yose inoita kunge yakawanda kusvika pane imwe pfungwa. Kuparadzwa kwaizoitika here kuti zvive nenyaya, ichi ndicho chaiva chinhu chekuzviita.

Uye izvi azviiti—simba rayo rinoshamisa, zvachose rinowanzoremara kutenderera kwakanaka kwekuhamba kwaro; apo hucheche hwekureruka hunotenderera kuburikidza neTitanism yesimba. Kusiapo izvo, mahambiro aya anowana kunaka kwawo kunothya maningi kubvira kwaari. Kusimba kwemene akumboshaishi kunaka kana kubatana, asi kazhinji kunozvipa; uye mune zvese imposingly zvakanaka, simba rine zvakawanda zvekuita nemashiripiti. Dusa tsinga dzakasungirirwa dzinoita inga dzinoputika dzeshe kubve pa marble mu Hercules yakavezwa, ne kunaka kwayo kwaizopera. Apo Eckerman wakadzipira wakasimudza chipepa che lineni kubva pamutumbi wa Goethe wakashama, wakadzara nge chifuva chikuru che murumbwana, chaiita inga chifuva che kukunda kwe Roma. Angelo paanopenda kunyange Mwari Baba muchimiro chemunhu, cherechedza kuti kusimba kwakadini kuripo. Uye chero chavanokona kuratidza nge rudo rwaMwari muMwanakomana, mifananidzo yakapfava, yakapetana, hermaphroditical yeItaly, umo mufungo wake wakabudirira zvikuru kusanganiswa; mifananidzo iyi, yakashata zvikuru sezvairi ye brawniness yeshe, haikombidzi chiro chesimba ripi neripi, asi yakashata, yechikadzi yekuzvininipisa nekushivirira, iyo panyara dzeshe inotenderwa, inoumba kunaka kunoshanda kunoshamisa kwedzidziso dzake.

Izvozvo ndizvo zvinoita kuti zviro zvisizi kujeka zve nhengo yandinobata, kuti kunyazwi zvakashandiswa mumutambo, kana nge mwoyo wese, kana nge kutsamwa, kunyazwi zviri mu mwoyo wakadini, kutenderera kwazvo kunogara kweikarakadzwa nge nyasha dzakawanda. Mukati mwakona hapana ruoko rwengano runogona kuzvipfuura.

Kufamba kukuru kushanu kunoshamisa kwairi. Kutanga, kana yakashandiswa se fin yekuenderera mberi; Chechipiri, kana ichishandiswa sepfumo muhondo; Chechitatu, mukutsvaira; Chechina, mukutamba; Chechishanu, mukupedza flukes.

Kutanga: Ngokuti muswe wo Revhiyatani wakaema pa ndau yawo, unoita zviro zvakasiyana no muswe wo zvipuka zvese zvo mu jombe. Hazvimbofi zvakavhiringika. Mumunhu kana hove, kuvhiringika chiratidzo chekuderera. Kune whale, muswe wake ndiwo chete nzira yekufambisa. Mupumburu-wakangwara wakaputirwa kumberi pashi pemuiri, uye wozokurumidza kubuda sure, ndizvo zvinopa kuti imwe darting, kusvetuka kufamba kune chikara kana furiously kudhuvha. Divi rake-fins chete kushumira kutungamirira ne.

Chechipiri: Zvinokosha zvishomani, kuti kunyazwi sperm whale imwe inorwa ngesoro rimweni ngetsaya, kunyazwi zvakadaro, mukukakavadzana kwake ngemunhu, iye chikuru ngekuzvidza anoshandisa muswe wake. Paanorova cikepe, anokasira kupetuja mafluke ake kubvira paco, zve kurova kunoitwa basi ngo kudzoka. Kana ikaitwa mumhepo isina kuvhiringidzwa, kunyanya kana ikadzika kusvika pachiratidzo chayo, kurova kunozongoita kusingadzivisiki. Hapana mbabvu dzemunhu kana chikepe chinokwanisa kuzvidzivirira. Ruponeso rwako rwega rwuri mukuzvidzivirira; asi kana ikauya parutii kuburikidza nemvura inopesana, zvino chikamu chinokonzerwa ngekureruka kwekubhururuka kwechikepe che whale, uye kutambanuka kwezviro zvacho, mbabvu yakatsemuka kana puranga rakatsemuka kana maviri, mhando yekusonwa parutii, ndiyo inowanzova mhedzisiro yakanyanya kukosha. Izvi zviro zvakanyudzwa mundau yekurova zvinonyanya kuashirwa muhowe, zvekuti zvinozwi kutamba kwevana basi. Mundhu umweni anobvisa ngubo, zve ndau inoemeswa.

Chechitatu: Andikoni kuzvikhombisa, asi zvinoita ingatei mandiri, kuti mu whale pfungwa yekubata inonyanya kuoneka mumuswe; nokuti muchiitiko ichi pane kunakidza mazviri kunoenzaniswa chete nekunakidza kwemuviri wenzou. Kudakadza uku kunovoneka maningi ngo kuita ko kutsvaira, apo ngo kupfava ko mwanakaji, hove huru inohamba ngo kunonoka kwakapfava kubve ku divi rimweni kuenda ku rimweni pashi po gungwa; uye kana akazwa asi ndebvu dzemufambisi wechikepe, nhamo kune mufambisi wechikepe uyo, ndebvu nezvose. I kupfava kwakadini kuripo pa kubata ko kutanga! Dai muswe uyu waizova nesimba rekubata, ndaifanira kufunga ngezve nzou yaDarmonodes yaigara yeiuya kumusika wemaruva, uye ngekukwazisa kwakadzaka yaipa madzimai kune vasikana, uye yozopuruzira ndau dzavo. Pa ndaa dzakawanda dzinopfuura imwe, zvinosiririsa kuti whale haina kunaka uku kwe prehensile mumuswe wake; ngokuti ndazwa ngendaa imweni nzou, iyo payakakuvadzwa muhondo, yakakotama yakatenderedza muviri wayo yobudisa museve.

Chechina: Kuba asikazii pamusoro pe whale mukuchengeteka kwakanaka pakati pemakungwa ari ega, unomuwana asizi kubhenda kubva kune yakakura yekuremekedza kwake, uye kitten-like, anotamba pamusoro pegungwa sekuti iri chitofu. Asi zvakadaro unoona simba rake mumutambo wake. Zanza rakafara remuswe wake rinotambanudzwa kumusoro mumhepo; zvino kurova pamusoro, kudengenyeka kwemabhanan'ana kunorira kwemakiromita. Ungapotsa wafunga kuti pfuti huru yakabudiswa; uye kana ukaona ruva rakajeka remhepo kubva ku spiracle kune imwe mugumo wake, waizofunga kuti ndiwo utsi kubva ku touch-hole.

Chechishanu: Kudai ngekuema kwakajairika kwekuyangarara kwe leviathan ma flukes anoata maningi pashi pe musana wake, anozoguma asikaoneki zvachose pashi pe nyika; asi paanenge ava kuda kudzika mukati mwemvura yakadzaka, mafluke ake eshe ane makubiti makumi matatu emuiri wake anokandwa akaema mumhepo, uye ngekudaro anoramba eidengenyeka kwechinguvana, kuguma achienda pashi akapfura asingaoneki. Kusiyapo kuparadzwa kunoshamisa —pa ndau imweni inozotsanangurwa —kukwirira uku ko n’anga yo whale’s flukes kangaije ndikona kukuru maningi kunovoneka mu zviro zvese zvinorarama. Kubva muzviro zvisina mudziyo muswe mukuru unoita inga unobvuta nge spasmodically kudenga kwakanyanya. Hino muzviroto, ndakaona ere Sathana ane mbiri eibudisa nzara yake huru yakatambudzwa kubva mumwoto weBaltic weGehena. Asi mukuningira zviro zvakadaro, zveshe zviri mu murangariro uripo; kana muDantean, madhimoni achauya kwauri; kana mune iyo yaIsaya, ngirozi huru. Ndakaema pa mast-head ye ngarava yangu nguwa yekubuda kwe zuwa raitsvuka denga ne gungwa, ndakamboona boka guru re whales kumabvazuva, dzeshe dzakananga kuzuwa, uye kwechinguvana dzichidedera mu concert ne peaked flukes. Inga zvaivoneka kwandiri pa nguvayo, kunamata vamwari kwakakura kudaro akuzi kumbovoneka, kunyazwi ku Persia, muzi yo vanonamata mwoto. Kudai ngekupupurira kwa Ptolemy Philopater ngendaa yenzou ye Africa, inini ndakazopupurira ngendaa ye whale, ndichimudaidza kuti wakadzipira kupfuura zvipuka zveshe. Ngokuti mukuzwirana na Mambo Juba, nzou dzehondo dzekare dzaigara dzichibonga mangwanani dzichisimudza matumbo adzo mukunyarara kwakanyanya.

Kuenzanisa kwemukana muchikamu ichi, pakati pehove huru nenzou, kusvika pane zvimweni zvikamu zvemuswe weumweni nemudzi weumweni zvine hanya, hazvifaniri kuita kuti nhengo mbiri idzodzo dzakasiyana dzienderane, kunyanya zvisikwa zvavanogara. Ngokuti inga nzou yakasimba maningi i terrier kuna Revhiathan, kudaro, kuenzaniswa no muswe wa Revhiathan, mudzi wake unoita inga tsanga yo lily. Kurova kwakanyanya kutyisa kubva mumuviri wenzou kwaiita inga pombi inotamba ye fan, kuenzaniswa nekupwanywa kusingaperi uye kupwanyika kwe sperm whale's ponderous flukes, iyo muzviitiko zvinodzokororwa ine imwe pashure peimweni inokanda zvikepe zveshe ngezvikepe zvayo zveshe uye vashandi mumhepo, zvakanyanya kuita semuIndia * bhora rake.

*Kunyazwi kuenzanisa kweshe ngenjira ye general bulk pakati pe whale ne nzou kunoita inga chiro chisina shwiro, ngekuti mune iyoyo nzou inoema mukuremekedza kwakafanana ne whale iyo imbwa inoita ku nzou; zvisinei, hapana vanoda dzimwe pfungwa dzekufanana kwekuda kuziva; pakati peizvi pane muromo. Zvinozivikanwa kakurutu kuti nzou inonyanya kudhonza mvura kana huruva mumuti wayo, yozoisimudza, yoibudisa murukova.

Pandinowedzera kufunga nezvemuswe uyu une simba, ndinonyanya kushungurudza kusakwanisa kwangu kuzvitaura. Nguwa imweni kune zviratidzo mukati mazvo, izvo, kunyange zvazvo zvingadakadza nyara yemunhu, zvinoramba zvisingatsananguriki zvachose. Muboka rakakura, zvinoshamisa yaamho, nguva ngenguva, ndizvo zviratidzo izvi zvechishamiso, zvekuti ndakazwa vavhimi vakazvizivisa zvakafanana nezviratidzo nezviratidzo zveFree-Mason; kuti whale, zvemene, nge njira idzi dzakabhuya ngo ungwaru no nyika. Uyewo hapana kuda kumwe kufamba kwe whale mumuviri wake wese, wakadzara zvishamiso, uye usingazvidaviri kune murwi wake ane ruzivo rwakanyanya. Dissect naye kuti ndingaita sei, saka, ini asi kuenda ganda rakadzika; Andimuzivi, zve andizombomuzivi. Asi kana ndisingazivi kana muswe wehove iyi, ndinozwisisa sei soro rake? zvikuru, ungazwisisa sei chiso chake, apo chiso chisina? Uchaona zvikamu zvangu zvemusana, muswe wangu, anoita kunge anoti, asi chiso changu hachizoonekwi. Asi andingakoni kuzwisisa zvakakwana zviro zvake zvo sure; uye hint zvaanoda pamusoro pechiso chake, ndinoti zvakare haana chiso.





CHITSAUKO 87. Armada Guru.

Peninsula yakareba uye yakatetepa ye Malacca, inoenderera kumaodzanyemba-kumabvazuva kubva kumatunhu eBirmah, inoumba nzvimbo yekumaodzanyemba kwakanyanya kuAsia yeshe. Mu mutsara unoenderera mberi kubva ku peninsula iyo inotambanudza zvitsuwa zvakareba zve Sumatra, Java, Bally, uye Timor; iyo, pamwepo no imweni yakawanda, inoumba n’anga yakakura, kana rusvingo, inobatanidza Asia no Australia ngo kureba, zve inoparadzanisa gungwa rakareba ricikazivi kutsemuka ro India kubvira ku zvitsuwa zvo kumabvazuva zvakazara maningi. Rusvingo urwu rwakabayiwa nge sally-ports dzakawanda kuti zviite nyore kuendesa ngarava ne whales; zvinooneka pakati pazvo zviri nzvimbo dzakaoma dzeSunda neMalacca. Nge straits ye Sunda, maningi-ningi, ngarava dzinosungirirwa ku China kubve kumadokero, dzinobuda mu makungwa e China.

Iyo migwagwa yakatetepa yeSunda inoparadzanisa Sumatra kubva kuJava; uye akaema pakati penjira mu rusvingo rukuru urwu rwe zvitsuwa, rwakatsigirwa nge nzvimbo iyoyo yakashinga yakasvibira, inoziikanwa nge vafambisi vezvikepe se Java Head; ivo havana zvishoma zvinoenderana nemusuwo wepakati unovhura kupinda mune imwe umambo hwakakura hwakashongedzwa ngemadziro: uye tichifunga nezveupfumi husingaperi hwezvinonhuwira, uye siriki, uye zvishongo, uye goridhe, uye nyanga yenzou, iyo zviuru zvezvitsuwa zvegungwa iroro rekumabvazuva zvakapfumiswa, zvinoita sezvinokosha kupa kwezvisikwa, kuti , iyo trech kutakura chitarisiko, kunyange zvisingashandi, zvekuchengetedzwa kubva kunyika yekumadokero inobata zvose. Mahombekombe e Straits of Sunda haana zviro zvinodiwa nge nhare idzo dzinotonga dzinorinda masuo e Mediterranean, Baltic, ne Propontis. Kusiyana ne Danes, ava Orientals avadi kuremekedzwa kwekudzikisira-pamusoro-sails kubva mukuhamba kusingaperi kwengarava pamberi pemhepo, iyo kwemazana emakore apfuura, usiku nemasikati, yakapfuura pakati pezvitsuwa zveSumatra neJava, yakatakurwa nemitoro inodhura zvikuru yekumabvazuva. Asi kunyazi vecisiya ngo kuzvidira mutemo wakadari inga uwu, avamborambi zvavanonanga kuti vanozobhadhara mutero wakagwinya maningi.

Nguwa isingazivikanwi, mapiratical proas eMalay, akavanda pakati pemakova ane mumvuri wakaderera uye zvitsuwa zveSumatra, akabuda pamusoro pezvikepe zvinofamba ngezvikepe, achikumbira mutero ngekutyisa pamusoro pemapfumo avo. Kunyazi nge kurangwa kakawanda kwe ngazi kwavakaashira mumaoko evafambisi vezvikepe veku Europe, ushingi hwevafambisi vezvikepe ava hwakambodziviswa; asi, kunyazwi muzuwa ranyamashi, tinozwa nguva ngenguva ngezvikepe zveChirungu neAmerica, izvo, mumvura iyoyo, zvakakwidzwa nekupambwa zvisina kuzvidemba.

Ne mhepo yakanaka, yakachena, Pequod yakanga yava kuswedera pedyo nezvikomo izvi; Ahabhi wakafunga kupfuura ndivo kupinda mugungwa re Javani, uye kubvapo, achihamba ngechikepe kuenda kuchamhembe, pamusoro pemvura dzinoziikanwa kuti dzinogara dzichiuya pano nepapo nge Sperm Whale, achitsvaira kumahombekombe ngezvitsuwa zvePhilippines, uye achiwana ndau iri kuretu yeJapan, munguwa yemwaka mukuru wekubata whale apo. Nge njira idzi, Pequod inotenderera yaizotsvaira ndau dzinenge dzeshe dzinoziikanwa dze Sperm Whale dzekuhamba ndidzo munyika, isati yadzaka pamusoro pe Line mu Pacific; apo Ahabhi, kunyazwi ndau dzeshe dzakakundikana mukuteera kwake, wakaerenga zvakasimba kupa hondo kuna Moby Dick, mugungwa raaiziikanwa kakurutu ngekugara; uye pamwaka apo angafungidzirwa kuti ari kuzvishungurudza.

Asi zvino zvinodini? mukutsvaka uku kwakaparadzaniswa, Ahabhi haabati nyika here? crew yake inomwa mhepo here? Ngo zvokadi, anozoema kuti awane mvura. Haiwa. Kwe nguwa yakareba, zvino, zuwa rinorumba circus rakarumba mukati mwe mhete yake ye mwoto, uye haridi chikafu asi zviri mukati mwake. Saka Ahabhi. Maka izvi, zvakare, mu whaler. Kunyazwi dzimweni hulls dzinoremerwa kudzaka nge zviro zve vamweni, kuti dziendeswe ku wharps dze dzimweni nyika; chikepe che whale chinohamba-hamba munyika hachitakuri zviro asi iye nevashandi, zvombo zvavo nezvavanoda. Ane zviri mukati megungwa rose zvakaiswa mubhodhoro muhondo yake yakakwana. Iyena ano ballasted nge zviro zvinoshandiswa; kwete zvachose nezvisingashandisi nguruve-kutungamira uye kentledge. Anotakura mvura yemakore mukati mwake. Mvura yakachena yekare yeNantucket; iyo, apo makore matatu achiyangarara, Nantucketer, muPacific, inoda kumwa asati mvura yemunyu, asi zuro yakasimudzwa mumakasi, kubva kumigero yePeruvian kana yeIndia. Hino ndizvona, kuti, apo dzimweni ngarava dzinokona kunge dzakaenda kuChina kubva kuNew York, nekupetukazve, dzichibata pazvikepe zvakawanda, chikepe che whale, munguwa yeshe iyoyo, chinokona kunge chisina kuona tsanga imwe yevhu; crew yake isina kuona murume asi vafambisi vezvikepe vanoyangarara sezvavo. Saka ndizvo wakavaendesa mashoko ekuti imwe mvura yakawanda yakauya; vaizongopindura—“Zvakanaka, majaya, heino ngarava!”

Zvino, sezvo Sperm Whales dzakawanda dzainge dzabatwa kumadokero kwemhenderekedzo yeJava, munharaunda iri pedyo neStraits yeSunda; zvirokwazvo, sezvo nyika zhinji, roundabout, yaiwanzoziikanwa ngevabati vehove sendau yakaisvonaka yekuhamba ngechikepe; saka, apo Pequod yakawana zvakawanda uye zvakawanda pamusoro peJava Head, vatarisi vakaramba veirumbidzwa, uye vakarairwa kuti varambe vakamuka. Asi kunyazi matombo e green palmy e nyika akakurumidza kuoneka pa bow ye starboard, uye nge mhino dzinodakara sinamoni itsva yakafema mumhepo, asi hapana jet imwe yakatsanangurwa. Kudai ngekusiya mifungo yeshe yekuwira mumutambo weshe uripo, ngarava yainge yava pedyo nekupinda muchikepe, apo kuchema kwekupemberera kwetsika kwakazwika kubva mudenga, uye ere nguva yakareba chiratidzo chekukudzwa kumwe chete chakatichingamidza.

Asi pano ngazviite premised, kuti nge ndaa ye mushando usikazi kuremba uwo wakazovhimwa ndiwo padera pemakungwa eshe mana, Sperm Whales, pane kugara yeihamba nge zvikepe mumakambani madoko akaparadzana, kudai nge nguwa dzekare, zvino vanogara vechisangana nge mapoka akawanda, dzimweni nguwa vakawanda vanozombundirana. ivo vainge vakapika solemn league uye sungano yekubatsirana uye kudzivirirana. Kuunganidza uku kwe Sperm Whale muzvikwata zvakakura kudaro, zvinokona kubekwa nge ndaa yekuti kunyazwi mundau dzakanakisisa dzekuhamba ndidzo, unokona zvino dzimweni nguwa kuhamba nge chikepe kwe mavhiki ne mwedzi mweshe, usikachingamidzwa nge muromo umwe; uye wozongoerekana wakwaziswa nezvinoita dzimwe nguva zviuru pazviuru.

Kufara pa uta hweshe huviri, pa kureba kwe mamaira maviri kana matatu, ne kuita semicircle huru, kumbundira hafu imwe ye level horizon, cheni inoenderera mberi ye whale-jets yainge yeikwidza-kutamba ne kupenya mumhepo ye masikati. Kusiyana nemapatya akarurama akarurama e Right Whale, ayo, akaparadzana pamusoro, anowa pamusoro pematavi maviri, kunge matavi anodonha akatsemuka e willow, muromo umwe chete unotenderera mberi we Sperm Whale unoratidza sango rakakora rakapoteredzwa re mhute chena kuenda kure uye inoramba ichikwira kure.

Kutanga, ma whale anoumba mupendero we gungwa redu akatanga kudzara zvishoma, uye akadonha umwe neumwe, kunge akasimudzwa nge hafu yakashandiswa billows kubva kure; ipapo gungwa pacharo rakatanga kusimuka zvishoma uye kuzvimba; makamuri emwenga epasi pemvura uye nzvimbo dzekuchengeta vana dzakanyangarika; muzviro zvakawanda zvinotenderera zvibvumirano, howe dziri mudenderedzwa repakati dzakatanga kushambira mumapoka anowedzera. Eya, kudzikama kwakareba kwaienda. Hum yakaderera yaienda mberi yakakurumidza kuzwika; uyezve kudai nge mapoka anoita mheremhere e block-ice apo rwizi rukuru rwe Hudson rwunoparadzana mu Spring, boka reshe re whales rakauya rechidonhera pakati pavo remukati, ingatei rinozviunganidza mugomo rimwe rakajairika. Pakarepo Starbuck neQueequeg vakachinja nzvimbo; Starbuck ichitora chikepe chekumashure.

"Zvikepe! Zvikepe!" akazevezera zvikuru, akabata chigaro chechikepe—"batai zvikepe zvenyu, uye mubate mweya yenyu, zvino! Mwari wangu, varume, emai! Musundirei kure, imwimwi Queequeg—howe huru apo!—mubaye!—murove! Simukai—emai, uye mugare kudaro! Spring, varume—vadhonzei sure, varume!—musamboita minscrape avo kuretu!”

Chikepe ichi chainge chavharika pakati pezvikepe zviiri zvakakura zvakasvipa, zvakasiya Dardanelles yakatetepa pakati pekureba kwazvo kwakareba. Asi nge kuedza kakurutu takapeisira tapfura kuvhurika kwe nguwa doko doko; wozosiya njira ngo kukasika, zve pa mukuvo umwewo wosotesesa ngo mwoyo wese kuti awane imweni njira yo kubuda. Musure mwekutiza kwakawanda kwakafanana nevhudzi-kufara, takapeisira ngekukasira kupinda mune zvainge zvangova chimwe chezvitenderedzwa zvekunze, asi zvino zvakayambuka nge random whales, zveshe zvichiita zvine simba kuti zvive pakati. Ruponeso urwu rwerombo rakanaka rwakatengwa ngemutengo usingadhuri ngekurasikirwa ngengowani yaQueequeg, uyo, paainge akaema mumauta kuti abaye howe dzakatiza, ngowani yake yakatorwa yakachena kubva musoro wake ngemhepo-eddy yakaitwa ngekukasira kukanda kwema flukes maviri akafara pedyo.

Riotous uye kusarongeka sezvo mheremhere yenyika yeshe yaive zvino, yakakurumidza kuzvigadzirisa kuita zvaiita inga kufamba kwakarongeka; ngokuti vakaungana pamwepo pa kupejisira mu muviri umwe wakaoma, vakazotangazve kubhururuka kwavo no kuengejera kukurumidza. Kuenderera mberi nekutevera kwainge kusina maturo; asi zvikepe zvakanga zvichiri kugara mukumuka kwazvo kuti zvitore zvipuka zvinodhaka zvingadonhedzwa kumashure, uye zvakafanana kuti zvichengetedze imwe iyo Flask akauraya uye aifa. Waif i pennoned pole, mbiri kana nhatu dzinotakurirwa nge chikepe chimwe nge chimwe; uye iyo, apo mutambo wekuwedzera uri pedyo, inoiswa yakarurama mumuiri unoyangarara we whale yakafa, zveshe kuisa chiratidzo chendau yayo pagungwa, uyewo sechiratidzo chekutanga kuva nayo, kana zvikepe zveimweni ngarava zvikaswedera pedyo.

Mugumisiro wo kudereja uku wakaita inga wakakhombija mazwi aya o ungwaru mu Fishery, —kuti howe dzakawanda howe dzinoguma padoko. Pakati pehowe dzese dzakadhakwa imwe chete yakabatwa. Vamweni vakasara vakafunga kutiza nge ndaa ye nguwa, asi kuti vatorwe basi, kudai nge zvinozooneka pasurepo, nge chikepe chimweni kusiyapo Pequod.





CHITSAUKO 88. Zvikoro ne Vadzidzisi veZvikoro.

Ndau yakapfuura yakapa nhau yemuviri wakakura kana boka reSperm Whales, uye pakanga painewo chikonzero chinokona kukonzera kuunganidza kwakawanda ikoko.

Zvino, kunyazwi miiri yakakura kudaro inosangana nguwa imweni, asi, kudai ngezvinofanira kuonekwa, kunyazwi muzuwa ranyamashi, mapoka madoko akaparadzana anoonekwa nguwa imweni, anombundirana kubva pamakumi mairi kuguma pamakumi mashanu umwe ngeumwe. Mabhendi akadaro anozivikanwa sezvikoro. Vanonyanya kuva vemhando mbiri; avo vanoumbwa vanoda kuguma vose ngo vakaji, zve avo vacikaunganiji asi madhodha madoko ano simba, kana nzombe, inga zvavanodanwa ngo njira yakajairika.

Mukuenda kwe cavalier pamusoro pechikoro chevakadzi, unogara uchiona murume akakura zvakakwana, asi asizi kukura; uyo, pane aramu chero ipi, anoratidza ushingi hwake nekudonha kumashure uye kufukidza kubhururuka kwemadzimai ake. Mugwinyiso, mundhu uwu ndi Ottoman wo upfumi, unodhuvha-dhuvha pa nyika yo mvura, wakatendenedzwa ngo kunyaradzwa kwese no kudakarira kwe harem. Kusiyana pakati peOttoman uyu nevarongo vake kunoshamisa; ngekuti, apo iye nguva dzeshe ari wezviyero zvakakura zve leviathanic, madzimai, kunyange pakukura kwakadzara, haapfuuri chikamu chimwe chezvitatu chehuwandu hwemurume wepakati nepakati. Vanoenzaniswa nekuoma, zvemene; Ndinoshinga kutaura, kwete kudarika hafu yemamita gumi nemaviri akatenderedza chiuno. Zvakanyadaro, azvingaiti kurambwa, kuti pa zveshe vanogara nhaka yavo en bon point .

Zvinodakajisa maningi kuvona harem iyi no tenji wayo veciita zviro zvavo zvicikaiti zviro. Kufanana ne fashoni, vanogara vari mukuhamba vachitsvaka zvakasiyana-siyana. Unosangana navo pa Line munguwa ye maruva akadzara e Equatorial feeding season, uchangopetuka, pamwe, kubva kupedza nguwa yekupisa mumakungwa eKuchamhembe, uye ngekudaro nguwa yekupisa yekubiridzira yekuremba kweshe kusingadakadzi nekudziya. Nguva yovanozoguma pa kugara vecikwira no kudzaka pa ndau inohamba-hamba yo Equator, vanotanga kuenda ku mvura jo ku Mabvazuva vecivetera nguva yo kutonhora yondhoyo, ngo kudaro vanotiza kupisha kakurutu ko gore.

Paanoenderera mberi ngokunyarara pa imwe yo rwendo urwu, kudari pane zviro zvinoshamisa zvinosotesesa zvinovoneka, ishe wangu whale anoramba akangwarira mbhuri yake inodakajisa. Kuti munhu ari wese anozoita zviro zvicikazivi kutendehwa ngo Leviathan mudoko anoviya ngo njira yondhoyo, anojikudza kuti anozokhwedera phedo no umwe wo vanakaji, ngo kunyangajwa kakurutu kwa Bashaw kunomurova, no kumudzinga! Nguwa dzakakwirira, zvemene, kana vadoko vasina mitemo vakadai ndiye vachizotenderwa kupindira mutsvene yekudakara kwepamuzi; kunyazi kuita zvinodiwa nge Bashaw, aakoni kuchengeta Lothario une mbiri yakashata maningi kuti asabuda pamubhedhu wake; ngekuti, maiwe! hove dzose mubhedha zvakafanana. Sekusina mvura, madzimai anowanzokonzera duels inotyisa zvikuru pakati pevanopikisa vanoyemura; zvakafanana ne whales, iyo dzimwe nguva inouya hondo inouraya, uye zvose nekuda kwerudo. Vanoita ruzhowa nge tsaya dzavo dzakareba dzepashi, dzimweni nguva vanodzivhara pamwepo, uye ngekudaro vanoshingairira kutonga kudai nge elks dzinobatana mvere dzadzo. Asipi vashomani vanobatwa vane mavanga akadzama ekusangana uku, — misoro yakavhurika, mazino akatsemuka, zviphenduko zvakavhurika; uye mune dzimwe nguva, miromo yakatsemuka uye yakasunungurwa.

Asi kufunga kuti mupambi wemufaro wepambatso azvitore kure pakurumire kwekutanga kwashe we harem, zvino zvinotsausa maningi kuona ishe uyo. Ngo kupfava, iyena wakaitazve zviro zvake zvakawanda pakati pavo zve wakadakarapo padoko, aciri phedo no Lothario mudoko, inga ndi Soromona anodira Mwari wainamata ngo kujipira pakati po varongo vake vanokwana zviuru. Kupa ma whale amweni kuti aonekwe, vabati vehove avanyanyi kupa kudzingirira umwe we Grand Turks aya; ngokuti aya maGrand Turks anonyanya kupfurikidza ngesimba rawo, ngendaa iyoyo kusazorora kwavo kuri kudoko. Kana vari vanakomana nevanasikana vavanobereka, ngei, vanakomana nevanasikana avo vanofanira kudzichengeta; zvishoma, nerubatsiro rweamai chete. Ngokuti kudai nge vamweni vanoda zviro zveshe vanohamba-hamba vanokona kudaidzwa, Mambo wangu Whale aazi kudakarira ndau yekuchengeta vana, kunyazwi yakawanda zvakadini ku bower; uye saka, ari mufambi mukuru, anosiya vana vake vasikazikanwi munyika yeshe; mwana wega wega an exotic. Mu nguva yakanaka, zvisinei, sezvo kushingaira kwevechidiki kunoderera; sezvo makore uye marara anowedzera; sezvo kufungisisa kunokweretesa kumbomira kwake kunoremekedza; muchidimbu, sezvo lassitude yakawanda inopfuura sated Turk; zvino rudo rwekureruka nekunaka runotsiva rudo rwevasikana; Ottoman wedu unopinda pamusoro pekusakwanisa, kutendeuka, kuraira chikamu chehupenyu, anopika, anoparadza harem, uye anokura kuita muenzaniso, sulky mweya wekare, anohamba-hamba ega pakati pe meridians uye parallels eibhuya minamato yake, uye anoyambira mumwe nemumwe wechidiki Leviathan kubva kune amorous error yake.

Zvino, inga harem ye whales inodanwa ngo vabati vehove kuti cikora, ndizvona zvinoitawo ishe no tenzi wo cikora ico vanozivikanwa ngo njira yo uzivi inga mukuru wo cikora. Ngokudaro, azvizi kuita zviro zvakaoma, kunyazwi zvinodakadza maningi, kuti pasure po kuenda kucikora iye ega, anozoenda ku nyika imweni acikafundisi zvaakafundayo, asi upenzi hwazvo. Zina rake, mudzidzisi wechikoro, rinozoita inga rakabva kuzina rakapuwa harem pacharo, asi vamweni vakafungidzira kuti murume wakatanga kudaidza kudaro rudzi urwu rweOttoman whale, anofanira kunge akaerenga memoirs yaVidocq, uye akazvizivisa kuti mhando ipi yemudzidzisi wechikoro wenyika-ocult yaive muFrance iyo yakakurumbira, iyo yaive nembiri zvidzidzo zvaakadzidza kune vamwe vadzidzi vake.

Kugara ega no kugara ega kunoitwa ngo whale yo mukuru wo cikora mu makore ake akura, ndizvona zvinoitika ku Sperm Whales jese jakakura. Pedyo no pasi pese, whale iri yega—inga zvinodanwa Leviathan iri yega—inokhombija kuti yainga yo kare. Kudai nge Daniel Boone une ndebvu dze moss anoremekedzeka, aazovi no mundhu ari phedo naye asi Nature ega; uye iye anomutora kuti aite mukadzi murenje remvura, uye ndiye mukadzi akanakisa, kunyange zvazvo achichengeta zvakavanzika zvakawanda zvemood.

Zvikora zvinoumba asina asi varume vadoko uye vane simba, vakamboreketwa, vanopa kusiyana kwakasimba nezvikoro zve harem. Ngokuti kunyazwi howe dzechikadzi idzo dzine maitiro anothya, majaha echirume, kana makumi mana-ma-barrel-bulls, sezvavanoadaidza, ndiwo anonyanya kurwisana pane maReviathan eshe, uye nge tsumo anonyanya kutyisa kusangana nawo; kunze kweizvo zvinoshamisa zvine misoro yakasvibira, grizzled whales, dzimwe nguva zvinosangana, uye izvi zvichakurwisa se grim fiends yakatsamwiswa nge penal gout.

Kudai nge boka re majaha anofunda ku chikora, vakadzara kurwa, kudakara, ne kushata, vechidonhera nyika nge njira isina kungwarira, inozunguzika, zvekuti apana munyori wakangwara unozova inshuwarisi kudai nge zvaaizoita mujaha unoita zviro zvisina shwiro ku Yale kana Harvard. Vanokasira kusiya mheremhere iyi asi, uye apo vanenge vatatu-varongomuna vakura, vanoparadzana, uye vakaparadzana vanoenda veitsvaka ndau dzekugara, kureva, harems.

Imweni pfungwa yekusiyana pakati pezvikoro zvevarume nevechikadzi ichiri kunyanya kuoneka kwevarume nevakadzi.
