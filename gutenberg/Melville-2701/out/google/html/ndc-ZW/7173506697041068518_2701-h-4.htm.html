Ndaida kukungwarirai pamusoro pe—esi musambofunga, musambofunga—zveshe chimwe, zveshe mumhatso zvakare;—chando chakapinza mangwanani ano, haiwa? Muende zvakanaka. Amuzomuonizve ngekukasira, ndinofungidzira kuti; kunze kwekuti zviri pamberi peGrand Jury." Uye nge mazwi aya akatsemuka akapeisira aenda, akandisiya, kwe nguwa doko doko, mu kushamisika kusizi kuderera nge kusashata kwake.

Pakupejisira, patakakwira ngarava yo Pequod, takawana zviro zvese zvakanyarara maningi, pasina mweya waihamba. Musuwo wekamuri wakavharwa mukati; ma hatches ainge akavhurwa ese, uye akashongedzwa nema coils e rigging. Kuenda mberi ku forecastle, takawana slide ye scuttle yakavhurika. Kuona chiedza, takadzaka, tikawana rigger rekare chete apo, rakaputirwa nge pea-jacket yakatsemuka. Wakakandwa ngekureba kwake kweshe pazvifuva zviiri, hope yake yakaningira pashi akavharwa ngemaoko ake akapeta. Kurara kwakadzama kwazvo kwakarara pamusoro pake.

“Vafambisi vezvikepe avo vatakaona, Queequeg, vangadai vakaenda kupi?” ndakadaro ndichitarisa munhu akarara zvine chokwadi. Asi zvaiita inga kuti, pavainge vari pa wharf, Queequeg aazi kumbozviona zvandaibhuya zvino; saka ndaizofunga kuti ndainge ndanyengerwa nemaziso munhau iyoyo, dai pasina mubvunzo waEria usingatsananguriki. Asi ndakarova chirocho pashi; uye zvakare kuisa chiratidzo kune akarara, jocularly hinted kuna Queequeg kuti pamwe zvainge zvakanaka kuti tigare pamusoro nemuviri; kumuudza kuti azvisimbise zvakakodzera. Wakabeka nyara yake pa sure pe mundhu wakaata, inga wakazwa kuti wakapfaa zvakakwana; uye ipapo, pasina kuwedzera ado, akagara chinyararire pasi ipapo.

"Mutsa! Queequeg, usagarapo," ndakadaro ini.

"O! chigaro che perry dood," akadaro Queequeg, "nzira yangu yenyika; haizomukuvadzi chiso."

"Hope!" ndakadaro ini, "daidza kuti chiso chake? chiso chine mutsa zvikuru ipapo; asi anofema zvakaoma sei, ari kuzviremera; buruka, Queequeg, unorema, zvirikugaya chiso chevarombo. Buruka, Queequeg! Tarisa, achakutsvedza ngekukasira. Ndinoshamisika kuti haamuki."

Queequeg wakadzibvisa kuenda mberi kwesoro remunhu wakaata, akatungidza pombi yake ye tomahawk. Ndakagara pamakumbo. Takaramba teiita kuti pombi ipfuure pamusoro pe sleeper, kubva kune imwe kuenda kune imwe. Nguwa idzodzo, pakumubvunza nge njira yake yakatsemuka, Queequeg wakandipa kuzwisisa kuti, munyika yake, nge ndaa ye kusavapo kwe ma settees nema sofa emhando dzeshe, mambo, madzishe, nge vandhu vakuru kakurutu, vainge vari mutsika yekukodza vamweni ve ma orders epashi e ottomans; uye kuti ugadzirise mhatso zvakanaka mune iyoyo, waifanira kutenga chete masere kana gumi ane usimbe, wovaisa vakatenderera mumapiers nema alcoves. Kunja kwa izvi, zvainga zvakanaka maningi pa kufamba; zviri nani maningi kupinda zvigaro zvo munda zvinoshandurwa kuva zvitanda zvo kuhamba ndizvo; pa mukuvo umweni, mukuru umweni waidanija mushandiri wake, womuda kuti aite ndau yake yo kugara pashi po muti wakapararira, kangaije pa ndau imweni yakanyorova yo matope.

Paaibhuya zviro izvi, nguva jese Queequeg paaiashira tomahawk kubve kwandiri, waibudirira hatchet-side yayo padera po soro ro mundhu wakaata.

“Izvozvo ndezvei, Queequeg?”

"Perry zviri nyore, kuuraya-e; oh! Perry zviri nyore!"

Aienderera mberi ngezvimwe zviyeuchidzo zvemusango pamusoro pe tomahawk-pipe yake, iyo, yaiita inga, yaive mukushandiswa kwayo kairi zvose zvakafunga vavengi vake uye zvakanyaradza mweya wake, apo isu takakwezverwa zvakananga kune rigger yakaata. Mvura yakasimba zvino yakazadza zvakakwana buri rakabvumirana, yakatanga kubhuya pamusoro pake. Wakafema nge mhando yekudzimwa; zvino akaita seakashungurudzwa mumhino; zvino akatenderera pamusoro kamwe kana kaviri; ndokugara akasimuka ndokuzora maziso ake.

“Holo!” akafema pakupedzisira, “ndiani imwimwi vanoputa?”

Ndakamupindura kuti: “Varume vakatumwa, anofamba rini?”

"Eya, ee, muri kupinda mweiri, ndizvona ere? Anohamba ngechikepe nyamashi. Kaputeni wakauya ngechikepe usiku hwapfuura."

“Kaputeni wei?—Ahabhi?”

“Ndiani asi iye zvemene?”

Ndainge ndava kuda kumubvunza mibvunzo imweni pamusoro paAhabhi, patakazwa ruzha padhizaini.

"Haiwa! Kuvhunduka kweStarbuck," wakadaro mutengesi. "Ndiye mutungamiri anorarama, uyo; murume akanaka, uye anodira Mwari; asi vese vanorarama zvino, ndinofanira kutendeukira kwaari." Uye achitaura kudaro akaenda pamusoro pechikepe, uye isu takatevera.

Zuva rainge rabuda zvakajeka. Ngekukasira vashandi vakapinda muchikepe vari vaviri nevatatu; vatengesi vakazvisimudza; vaviri vakaroorana vaishingaira kuroorana; uye vazhinji vevanhu vekumahombekombe vainge vakabatikana nekuunza zviro zvakasiyana-siyana zvekupedzisira muchikepe. Nguwa idzodzo Captain Ahab wakaramba akadzingwarira asikaoneki mukati mwekamuri rake.
