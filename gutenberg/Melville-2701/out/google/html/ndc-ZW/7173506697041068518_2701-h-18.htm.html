CHITSAUKO 89. Hove-Inokurumidza uye Hove-Inosununguka.

Kubhuya nge zve waif ne waif-poles mu chitsauko chekupeisira asi chimwe, zvinoda kuti pave nge ndaa ye mitemo ne zvirevo zvekubata howe dze whale, iyo waif ingaonekwa se mucherechedzo mukuru ne bheji.

Zvinoitika kakurutu kuti ngarava dzakawanda dziri kuhamba pamwepo, howe huru inokona kurohwa nge ngarava imwe, yozotiza, yozopeisira yaurawa yobatwa nge ngarava imweni; uye pano zvisina kunanga zvinosanganisira zvinhu zvakawanda zviduku zvinoitika, zvose zvichitora chikamu ichi chimwe chikuru. Inga muezaniso, —pasure po kudzingirira no kubata howe inorema zve ine ngozi, muiri unokona kusununguka kubve mu ngarava ngo ndava yo dutu guru; uye kuende kuretu kuenda kumhepo, kutorwazve nge whale yechipiri, iyo, mukudzikama, snugly inoikweva parutii, pasina ngozi yeupenyu kana mutsara. Ngokudaro kuganyirana kunonyangadza maningi no kunoita hondo kwaizovapo kazinji pakati po vabati ve hove, kudari painga pasina mutemo wakanyorwa kana usikazi kunyorwa, wo pasi pese, usikapokaniwi unoshanda pa ndaa jese.

Pamwe kodhi imwe chete yakarongeka yekubata howe yakabvumirwa ngemutemo, yaive yeHolland. Yakatemerwa nge States-General muna AD 1695. Esi kunyazi pasina imweni nyika yakamboita mutemo wakanyorwa wekubata howe dze whale, asi vabati vehove ve America vakaita vatongi vavo ne magweta avo pandaa iyi. Eya; mitemo iyi inganyorwa pa farthing ya Mambokadzi Anne, kana pa barb ye harpoon, yopfekwa pamutsipa, saka idoko.

I. A Fast-Fish ndeye bato inokurumidza kwairi.

II. Loose-Fish mutambo wakarurama kune chero munhu unokwanisa kukurumidza kubata.

Asi chinotamba kushata nge kodhi iyi ye masterly ndiko kupfupika kwayo kunoyemurika, kunoda bhuku rakakura rekuongorora kuti riitsanangure.

Kutanga: Hove inorumbire-kurumidza chiinyi? Howe inopona kana yakafa inorumbire, kana yakabatana ne ngarava kana chikepe chine vanhu, nge njira iri yeshe inodzorwa nge munhu anogara kana vanogara, — mast, oar, tambo ye nine-inch, waya ye telegraph, kana tambo ye cobweb, zveshe zvakafanana. Ngokudaro howe inorumbirepi ngo njira yo ungwaru painobara waif, kana ciro cimweni cinozivikanwa co kugara; saka chero bato waifing it zvakajeka evince kukwanisa kwavo chero nguva kuitora parutivi, uyewo chinangwa chavo saizvozvo.

Izvi ndizvo zvinobhuyiwa nge science; asi mhinduro dze whalemen pachavo dzimwe nguva dzinosanganisira mazwi akaoma uye kugogodza kwakaoma-Coke-upon-Littleton yechibhakera. Asi vamweni avamboiti zviro ngo kujidodokesa kudaro.

"Murumbwana wakakwana, madzimai akagara, uye regina caudam." Braktoni, ndi. 3, c. 3.

ChiLatin kubva mumabhuku e Mitemo ye England, iyo yakatorwa pamwepo ne ndaa, zvinoronza, kuti pa howe dzeshe dzakabatwa nge munhu weshe pamhenderekedzo ye nyika iyoyo, Mambo, se Honorary Grand Harpooneer, anofanira kuva nge soro, uye Mambokadzi anofanira kuratidzwa nge kuremekedza nge muswe.
