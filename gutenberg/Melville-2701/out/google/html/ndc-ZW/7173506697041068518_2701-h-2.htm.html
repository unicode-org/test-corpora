CHITSAUKO 4. Chipatara chekuona.

Pandakamuka mangwanani ondhowo inga masikati, ndakawana nyara ya Queequeg yakandiposhera ngo njira yo rudo no rudo maningi. Wainge watofunga kuti ndainge ndava mukadzi wake. Counterpane yainge ye patchwork, yakadzara nge zvikwereti zvidoko zvisizi kujeka zve mavara matatu; uye ruoko urwu rwake rwakanyorwa pose pose nge labyrinth ye Cretan isingaperi ye mufananidzo, hapana zvikamu zviviri zvayo zvaive zve mumvuri umwe chaiwo-nekuda kwe ndinofunga kuti akachengeta ruoko rwake mugungwa zvisina kurongeka muzuwa nemumvuri, maoko ake e mbatya akanga asina kurongeka akaunganidzwa nguva dzakasiyana-siyana-izvi zvake zvinoita inga ruoko rwe, quilt yekugadzirwa kwezvipfeko. Igwinyiso, chikamu chakaata pamusoro payo sezvakaita ruoko pandakatanga kumuka, ndaisakwanisa kuzviziya kubva pachidziro, vakasanganisa mavara avo pamwepo; uye zvaive chete nekunzwa kurema uye kumanikidzwa kuti ndizive kuti Queequeg aindimbundira.

Zvandaizwa zvaishamisa. Ngandiedze kuzvitsanangura. Pandainga ndiri mwana mudoko, ndinorangarira kamare mugariro wakafanana wakandiwira; kana zvaiva zvechokwadi kana zviroto, handina kumbokwanisa kunyatsogadzikana. Mamiriro ezvinhu ainge aya. Ndainge ndeitema caper kana imweni—ndinofunga kuti yainga yeizama kukwira mu chimney, kudai nge zvandainge ndaona kutsvaira kadoko kuchiita mazuwa mashomani apfuura; uye mai vangu vekubereka avo, ngenjira imwe kana imwe, vaigara veindirova ngeshamhu, kana kundituma kuende kooata ndisina kurya kwemadekoni, —mai angu vakandikweva ngemirenje kubuda muchimbuzi vakandipakira kuenda kooata, kunyazi yainge iri nguwa mbiri dzemasikati e 21st June, zuwa rakareba maningi mugore mu hemisphere yedu. Ndakazwa zvine kutya. Asi paiya pasina rudetsero rwazvo, saka ndakakwira masitepisi ndakaenda mukamuri rangu diki raiva pa floor yechitatu, ndakabvisa ngubo zvishomani zvishomani kuti ndiuraye nguwa, ndochofema kunovava ndakapinda pakati pemashizha.

Ndakaatapo ndakasuruvara ndichiverenga kuti maawa gumi nematanhatu akazara anofanira kupfuura ndisati ndave netariro yekumuka. Maawa gumi nematanhatu ari pamubhedha! chidiki chemusana wangu chairwadza ndichizvifunga. Uye zvakanga zvakareruka zvikuruwo; zuwa raipenya mukati ngepafafitera, nekutinhira kukuru kwemakochi munjira, nekuzwika kwemazwi evarume vanoita zvebonde mumhatso yeshe. Ndakazwa zvakanyanya kushata—pakupeisira ndakasimuka, ndakasimira, ndikadzaka zvinyoro-nyoro ngetsoka dzangu dzakapfekwa ngemasitokisi, ndakatsvaka mai angu ekubereka, ndikazvikanda ngekukasira patsoka dzavo, ndichivakumbira sechidakadaka chakanyanya kuti vandipe kutsvedza kwakanaka ngendaa yekusazvibata kwangu; chero chii zvacho asi kundipomera kurara abed nguva yakareba kudaro isingashiviririki. Asi iyena ndiyena wakanakisisa zve waingwarira maningi vanamai vokurera, zve kupetuka ndaidikana kuenda ku rumu yangu. Kwemaawa akati kuti ndakaatapo ndakamuka, ndeizwa zvakashata maningi kupinda zvandakamboita kubvira apo, kunyazwi kubve ku matambudziko makuru akateera. Pakupeisira ndinofanira kunge ndakawira muhope dzakaipa dzekurara; uye zvishoma nezvishoma ndichimuka kubva kwairi —hafu yakadzara ngezviroto —ndakavhura madziso angu, uye kamuri rainge risati ravhenekwa ngezuwa rainge raputirwa zvino ngerima rekunze. Pakarepo ndakazwa kuvhunduka kwairumba kuburikidza nefuremu yangu yese; hapana chaizoonekwa, hapana chaizozwika; asi ruoko rwematongerwo enyika rwaiita kunge rwakaiswa mune yangu. Ruoko rwangu rwakarembera pamusoro pe counterpane, uye chimiro chisina zita, chisingafungidziki, chinyararire kana phantom, iyo ruoko rwaive rwe, yaiita inga yakagara pedyo ne bed-side yangu. Ngokuti zvaiita inga makore akaunganidzwa pamakore, ndakaatapo, ndakaoma ngekutya kunotyisa maningi, ndisikashingi kukweva nyara yangu; asi ndichimbofunga kuti kana ndaigona asi kuisimudza inch imwe chete, chiro chinotyisa chaizotyorwa. Ndainga ndisikazii kuti kuziva uku kwakazoguma kudini pa kuzondisiya; asi kumuka mangwanani, ndakazvikarakadza zveshe ngekuvhunduka, uye kwemazuwa nemavhiki nemwedzi mushure mwazvo ndakarashika mukuedza kunovhiringidza kutsanangura chakavanzika. Haiwa, kuguma paawa ino, ndinonyanya kuzvivhiringidza ndizvo.

Zvino, bvisa kutya kunotyisa, uye kuzwa kwangu pakuzwa nyara yekudenga mune yangu kwakafanana zvikuru, mukushamisa kwavo, kune izvo zvandakaona pakumuka nekuona ruoko rwechihedheni rwaQueequeg rwakaposherwa pandiri. Asi pakupejisira zviitiko zveshe zvousiku hwapera zvakapetuka, chimwe ngechimwe, muchokwadi chakagadzikana, ndochoata ndeipona kwega kune dambudziko rinoshekedza. Ngokuti kunyazi ndakaedza kuhambisa nyara yake—kuvhura chibhakera chake chemuchadho—asi, akaata sezvaainge ari, wakaramba eindimbundira zvakasimba, ingatei hapana chimweni chiro asi rufu chinozotiparadzanisa tiri vaviri. Ndakaedza zvino kumumutsa—“Queequeg!”—asi mhinduro yake yaive yekufema. Ndakabva ndazviguqa, mutsipa wangu uchiita kunge uri muhosi yebhiza; uye kamwe kamwe akanzwa kutsvaira zvishoma. Kukanda parutii rwe counterpane, pakanga pakaata tomahawk yakaata parutii rwe savage, inga mwana ane chiso che hatchet. Pickle yakanaka, zvemene, ndakafunga; abed pano mumhatso isikazikanwi masikati akakura, nemunhu anodya vanhu uye tomahawk! “Mambokadzi!—muzina rekunaka, Mambokadzi, muka!” Pakupeisira, nge ndaa ye kushata kwakawanda, ne kubhuya nge izwi guru ne kusingaperi nge ndaa ye kusafanira kwake kumbundira umweni muisa nge njira iyoyo ye kuroorana, ndakabudirira kubudisa kugunun’una; uye nguva ino, wakadzosa ruoko rwake sure, wakadzizunungutsa pose peshe kudai ngembwa ye Newfoundland kubva mumvura, akagara pamubhedhu, akaoma kudai nge pike-staff, akandiningira, nekutsvoda madziso ake inga asikakarakadzi zvachose kuti ndakaguma sei kuti ndivepo, kunyazwi chiro chisina kujeka chakandiona ngekuziya slowly pamusoro pake. Nguvayo, ndakaata ndakanyarara ndecimuningirira, ndicikakhatari maningi wari, zve ndakakotama kuti ndivone cisikwa caida kuziva maningi. Paakapeisira, ngqondo yake yakaita inga yakagadzirwa kubata maitiro emunhu waaiata naye, uye akava, sekuti, kuyananiswa nechokwadi; akajamba kubanze pashi, uye ngezvimwe zviratidzo nezwi zvakandipa kuzwisisa kuti, kana zvakandidakadza, aizotanga kupfeka uye ozondisiya kuti ndipfeke mushure mwazvo, achisiya imba yeshe kwandiri ndega. Ndinofunga ini, Queequeg, pasi pemamiriro ezvinhu, iyi ndiyo overture yakabudirira yaamho; asi, chokwadi ndechekuti, aya ma savages ane pfungwa yekuzvarwa nayo yekunakidza, taura zvaunoda; zvinoshamisa kuti vanonyanya kuzvininipisa sei. Ndinobhadhara kurumbidza uku kwakanyanya kuna Queequeg, ngekuti wakandibata ngekudzikama kwakanyanya ngekufunga, apo inini ndainge ndine mhosva yekusadzikama kukuru; achimutarisa ari pamubhedha, uye achiona zvese zvaaiita muchimbuzi; nokuda kwenguva kuda kuziva kwangu kuwana nani kwekubereka kwangu. Zvakanyadaro, mundhu wakadai ndi Queequeg auoni zuwa nge zuwa, iyena ne njira dzake zvainga zvakasisira maningi zvisizi kujairika.

Wakatanga kusimira padera ngo kusimira ngubo yake yo beaver, yakareba maningi, ngo phedo, wozo—aciri kusiya mabhuruku ake—wakavhima mabhutsu ake. Chiinyi pashi pedenga chaakazviitira, andingakoni kuronza, asi kuhamba kwake kunoteera kwaiva kudzipwanya—mabhutsu munyara, ngengowani—pashi pemubhedhu; apo, kubva kune sundry mhirizhonga gaspings uye kushungurudzika, ndakafunga kuti aishanda zvakaoma booting pachake; kunyange zvazvo pasina mutemo wekuita zvakanaka wandakambozwa, pane murume anodikanwa kugara ari ega kana akapfeka shangu dzake. Asi Queequeg, munozvivona ere, wainga cipuka cainga mu ndau yo kucinja—acizivi kuita inga mhuka yo mhuka kana mhuka yo mhuka. Ainge akabudirira zvakakwana kuti akhombise kusiyana kwake ngenjira dzinoshamisa. Zvidzidzo zvake zvainge zvisati zvapera. Ainge ari mudzidzi wechikoro chepamusoro. Dai asizi kubudirira zvishoma, angadai asizi kuzvinesa nge mabhutsu zvachose; asi surepo, kudai asizi kumboita chikara, aazi kumborota kupinda pashi pemubhedhu kuti azvipfeke. Pakupeisira, wakabuda nge ngowani yake yakatsemuka maningi no kupwanywa pashi pa madziso ake, wotanga kuchema-chema no kuhamba-hamba mu rumu, inga kuti, aazi kujaira maningi kusimira mabhutsu, mabhutsu ake maviri akanyorova, akaunyana e ganda ro mwombe—zvingaita kuti aazi kuitwa kuitira kurairawo—asi akamubata no kumutambuja pakutanga.

Kuona, zvino, kuti paiya pasina maketeni ku fafitera, nekuti mugwagwa wainge wakatetepa maningi, mhatso yakatarisana yakaraira kuti munhu aone zvakajeka mumbatso, ne kuona zvakanyanya ne kunyanya mufananidzo usina kufanira wakaitwa ndi Queequeg, eihamba-hamba nge zvimweni zvishomani asi ngowani yake nge shangu dzake; Ndakamukumbira ngepeshe pandinokona, kuti akurumidze chimbuzi chake zvishoma, uye kunyanya kuti apinde mumapantaloons ake ngekukasira. Iyena wakazwira, wozoenderera mberi no kugeza. Nguwa iyoyo mangwanani muKristu weshe waizogeza hope yake; asi Queequeg, ndakashamiswa, akagutsikana nekudzivisa kugeza kwake pachipfuva chake, maoko, nemaoko. Wakazosimira chibhachi chake, akatora sipo yakaoma pa tafura ye pakati pe wash-stand, akainyika mumvura akatanga kuita mapundu kuhope yake. Ndainingira kuti ndione kuti wakachengeta kupi reza rake, apo lo and behold, wakatora harpoon kubva pakona yemubhedhu, wakatsvedza kubuda muchidziro chakareba chemapuranga, wakasunungura soro, wakaricheka zvishomani pabhutsu yake, uye wakakwira kuguma pane chikamu chegirazi pamusoro pemadziro, wakatanga kutsvaira zvine simba, kana kuti cheek yake. Ndinofunga ini, Queequeg, izvi zviri kushandisa zvipunu zvakanakisisa zvaRogers nekutsiva. Musure mwazvo ndakashamisika zvishomani pakushanda uku apo ndakazoziya kuti soro re harpoon rinogadzirwa ngesimbi yakanaka, uye kuti mipendero yakareba yakarurama inogara yakachengetedzwa yakapinza zvakanyanya sei.

Chimbuzi chake chakasara chakakasira kupera, wakabuda ngekudakara mumbatso, akaputira bhachi rake guru re pilot monkey, eitamba harpoon yake kudai nge baton ye marshal.





CHITSAUKO 5. Kudya kwemangwanani.

Ndakakurumidza kutevera, uye kudzika mu bar-room accosted grinning landlord zvakanaka zvikuru. Ndainga ndicikakhatari maningi ngo kunyangajwa kwaari, pikija hazvo wainge eciita skylarking neni haiwa zvishoma ngo pamusoro po mundhu wandaigara naye.

Asi, kuseka kwakanaka chiro chakanaka maningi, asi chiro chakanaka chinoshomeka maningi; the more ndizvo zvinozwisa tsitsi. Hino, kuti munhu umwe, mumunhu wake wakafanira, akakwanisa kuita zviro zvekuita jee rakanaka kune munhu, ngaasadzokere sure, asi ngaazvitendere ngekudakara kuti ashandise nekushandiswa ngenjira iyoyo. Uye muisa une chiro chinoshekedza ngezvake, iva nechokwadi chekuti pane zvakawanda mumurume iyeye kupfuura zvaungafunga.

Bar-room yainge yazara zvino ngevagari vemu boarder vainge veidonha husiku hwakapfuura, uye ini ndainge ndisati ndamboona zvakanaka. Vainge vari pedyo nevese ve whalemen; vakuru vehondo, nevechipiri, nevechitatu, nevavezi vemugungwa, nevagadziri vesimbi vemugungwa, nevapfuri vesimbi vemugungwa, nevapfuri vemapanga, nevachengeti vengarava; kambani ye brown uye brawny, ine ndebvu dze bosky; an asina kuchekwa, shaggy seti, vese vakapfeka mabhachi e monkey emangubo emangwanani.

Unokona kuvona pambhene kuti umwe nga umwe wainga akagara ku ndau iri ku ndau iri phedo no gungwa ko nguva yakadini. Dama remujaha uyu rine utano rakadai nge pear rakabikwa nge zuwa mu hue, uye rinozoita inga rinonhuhwirira kudai nge musky; haakwanisi kunge aive mazuva matatu akadzika kubva parwendo rwake rwekuIndia. Murume uyo ari pedyo naye anotaridzika mumvuri shoma akareruka; ungati kubata kwehuni hwe satin kuri maari. Muganda rechitatu richiri kugara tropic tawn, asi rakacheneswa zvishoma; pasina kumbozengurira akagara mavhiki ese ari kumhenderekedzo yegungwa. Asi ndiani waizokhombise dama rakafanana ne Queequeg? iyo, yakavharwa nge mavara akasiyana-siyana, yaiita inga yakaderera kumadokero kwe Andes, kukhombise mu njira imwe, kusiyana kwe mamiriro ekunze, ndau nge ndau.

"Kudya, ho!" zvino akachema muridzi weimba, achikanda musuwo, uye mukati takaenda kunodya zvemangwanani.

Vanonanga kuti vandhu vakavona nyika, ngo kudaro vanozova vakasununguka maningi mu njira, vanojibata maningi pavanogara pamwepo. Asi haiwa nguva jese: Ledyard, mufambi mukuru wo ku New England, na Mungo Park, wo ku Scotland; pavarume veshe, vainge nechivimbo chishomani mu parlor. Asi pamweni kungoyambuka Siberia nge sledge yakakwehwa ngembwa kudai nge zvakaita Ledyard, kana kutora rwendo rurefu rwemunhu ega ngemundani usina chiro, mumwoyo we negro we Africa, uwo waiva mubatanidzwa wezviro zvaiitwa ngevarombo vaMungo—rudzi urwu rwekuhamba, ndinoti, rinokona kunge risiri njira yakanakisisa yekugara pamwepo yekuwana polish a. Asi, kazinji, zviro zvakadaro zvinodikana kuwanika kwese.

Izvi zvinofungisisa pano zvega zvinokonzerwa nge ndaa yekuti pasure pekuti teshe takagara patafura, neni ndainge ndeinasirira kuzwa ndaa dzakanaka dzekubata howe; kushamisika kwangu kusina kushoma, anenge murume wese akachengetedza runyararo rwakadzama. Uye kwete izvozvo chete, asi vaiita kunge vakanyara. Eya, pano pakanga pane boka rembwa dzemugungwa, dzakawanda dzadzo dzisina kunyadziswa kadoko dzainge dzakakwira ma whale makuru mugungwa rakakura—vaeni veshe kwavari—vodziita duel dzakafa dzisingabvunzi; uyezve, pano vakagara patafura yekurya kwemangwanani kwevanhu —veshe vekudaidzwa kumwe, veshe vekuda kwehama —vechiningira kutenderera semakwai kune umwe neumwe ingatei avazi kumbobuda kunze kwekuonekwa kweimweni ndau yemakwai pakati peMakomo eGreen. Kuona kunoda kuziya; aya mabheya anonyara, aya anonyara varwi whalemen!

Asi nge ndaa ya Queequeg—ngei, Queequeg wakagarapo pakati pavo—pamusoro pe tafura, zvakare, zvakaitika nge kushata; inotonhorera sechando chechando. Kuti ndive nechokwadi handikwanisi kutaura zvakawanda nekubereka kwake. Muyemuri wake mukuru aisazokwanisa kuzviruramisira ngemwoyo wese kuunza harpoon yake kuzodya mangwanani naye, nekuishandisapo pasina mhemberero; kusvika pamusoro petebhura nayo, kune njodzi iri pedyo yemisoro yakawanda, uye kubata beefsteaks kuenda kwaari. Asi izvi zvakaitwa ngegwinyiso ngekutonhora maningi ndiye, nekuti munthu weshe unoziya kuti mukufungidzira kwevanhu vakawanda, kuita chiro cheshe ngekutonhorera kuchiita ngekudzikama.

Atizobhuyi nge zveshe zvakasiyana-siyana zve Queequeg pano; kuti akadzivirira sei kofi uye mabhuruku anopisa, uye akashandisa pfungwa dzake dzisina kuparadzaniswa kune beefsteaks, yakaitwa isingawanzoitika. Zvakakwana, kuti apo kurya kwemangwanani kwakapera wakadzoka kudai ngevamweni vakapinda mukamuri revanhu veshe, wakatungidza tomahawk-pipe yake, akagarapo akanyarara eigaya nekuputa ngengowani yake isingaparadzani, apo inini ndakabuda kuzohamba.





CHITSAUKO 6. Mugwagwa.

Dai ndakashamisika pakutanga ndakaona munhu wakasiyana nevanhu saQueequeg achitenderera pakati pevanhu vane hunhu vemu taundi rakabudirira, kushamisika uku kwakakurumidza kupera pandakatanga kufamba masikati mumigwagwa ye New Bedford.

Munjira huru dziri pedyo nezvikepe, chero chiteshi chikuru chegungwa chinowanzopa kuona queerest inotaridzika nondescripts kubva kune dzimweni nzvimbo. Kunyange mumigwagwa yeBroadway neChestnut, vafambisi vezvikepe veMediterranean dzimweni nguva vanosundire vakadzi vanotya. Mugwagwa weRegent hauzivikanwi ngeLascars uye Malays; uye kuBombay, muApollo Green, vanogara Yankees vanowanzotyisa vagari vemo. Asi New Bedford inokunda zvose Mvura Mugwagwa uye Wapping. Mundau idzi dzakapeisira kubhuyiwa unoona vafambisi vezvikepe vega; asi kuNew Bedford, vanodya vanhu chaivo vanoema vachibhuya pamakona emugwagwa; savages zvakananga; vazhinji vavo vachiri kutakura pamapfupa avo nyama isina kuchena. Zvinoita kuti munhu asikazikani atarise.

Asi, kunze kwevaFeegeean, vaTongatobooarrs, vaErromanggoans, vaPannangians, nevaBrighggians, uye, kunze kwezviratidzo zvemushango zvechikepe chekubata howe chisingateereri chinotenderera mumigwagwa, munozoona zvimweni zviro zvinooneka zvichiri kuda kuziya, zvemene zvinoshekedza. Pano vhiki nge vhiki vanoguma mu dhorobha iri vandhu vakawanda vo ku Vermont no New Hampshire, vese vano nyota yo kuwana zviro no kubwinya mu howe. Vanonyanya kuva vadoko, vemafuremu akasimba; vamweni vakatema masango, uye zvino vanotsvaka kudonhedza sanhu uye kubvuta whale-lance. Vazhinji vakasvibira seMakomo eGreen kwavakabva. Muzvimweni zviro waizozvifunga asi maawa mashomanana akare. Ningirai apo! chap iyoyo ichitenderera pakona. Anosimira ngowani ye beaver ne jasi re muswe we kumedza, rakasungirirwa nge bhande re mufambisi we ngarava ne banga re sheath. Heino inouya imwe ine sou '-kumadokero uye jasi rebombazine.

Akuna dandy wakabarwa mu taundi unozoenzaniswa nge wakabarwa mu nyika—ndinoreva dandy we bumpkin wakagwinya—munhu uyo, mumazuwa embwa, unozocheka maekare ake mairi mumagiravhusi eganda re mbudzi nge kuthya kutsvuka maoko ake. Zvino apo nyika dandy yakadai ngeyi inozvitora musoro wake kuti iite mukurumbira wakasiyana, uye inobatana ne whale-fishery huru, unofanira kuona zviro zvinoshekedza zvaanoita paanoguma pachikepe chegungwa. Mukubhuya ngezve ngubo yake ye gungwa, unoraira kuti mabhero-mabhatani aende ku zvipfeko zvake; mabhanhire kune mabhuruku ake echidziro. Aa, murombo Hay-Mbeu! zvinovava zvakadini zvinozopwanya mabhanhire aya mudutu rekutanga rinoboorera, apo iwe uchidzingwa, mabhanhire, mabhatani, nezvose, kudzaka pahuro yedutu.

Asi musarangarira kuti dhorobha iri rinozivikanwa rine ma harpooneer, vanodya vanhu, nema bumpkins kuti akombidze vaeni vake. Apana. Kunyange zvakadaro New Bedford inzvimbo inoshamisa. Dai tisipo vandhu vanoita zve whale, ndau iyoyo nyamasi uno ingadai iri mu mugariro wo kucema inga ndau iri phedo no gungwa ro Labrador. Sezvazviri, zvikamu zvenyika yake yekumashure zvakakwana kutyisa munhu, vanotaridzika zvakanyanya mapfupa. Taundi romene ndirona rinosisira maningi kugara, mu New England yese. Inyika yemafuta, zvemene: asi haiwa kudai ngeKanani; nyika, zvakare, yezviyo newaini. Migwagwa hairumbi nemukaka; kana munguva yechirimo-vanovaisa mazai matsva. Asi, kunyazwi nge izvi, apana pandau mu America yeshe yaunozowana mhatso dzakawanda dzinoita inga dze patrician; mapaki uye minda yakawanda, kupfuura muNew Bedford. Vakabva pari? zvakasimwa sei pamusoro peiyi yakambova scraggy scoria yenyika?

Enda woningira zviphenduso zvo simbi zvinokhombija zviro zvakatendeneja ndau yakakwirira yondhoyo, mubvunjo wako unozodavihwa. Eya; dzimba dzese idzi dzakashinga uye minda yemaruva yakabva kuAtlantic, Pacific, uye Indian gungwa. Chimwe chete, vakasungwa nge harpoon vakakwekwetwa kuenda pano kubva pashi pe gungwa. Herr Alexander unokwanisa kuita chiro chakadai ere?

Ku New Bedford, madzibaba, vanoti, vanopa whales kuti vape dowers kuvanasikana vavo, uye vanogovera vazukuru vavo nge porpoises shomani a-piece.
