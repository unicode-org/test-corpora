CHITSAUKO 55. Ye Mifananidzo Inotyisa Ye Whales.

Ndinozo ere long paint kwamuri kudai nge munhu unokona pasina canvas, chiro chakadai nge chimiro chemene che whale sezvaanooneka zvemene kuziso re whaleman apo mumuiri wake wakakwana whale yakasungirirwa parutii rwe whale-ship kuti akwanise kutsika zvakanaka apo. Zvinokona kuita zvakanaka, ngendaa iyoyo, pamberipo kuti tiziise kune avo vanoda kuziya zvifananidzo zvake zvinotoguma nyamashi zvinopokana ngekugonda kutenda kwemunhu wenyika. Yava nguva yekunasirisa nyika mundaa iyi, ngekukhombise mifananidzo yakadaro ye whale yeshe yakashata.

Zvingaita kuti ndau yo kutanga yo zviro zvese izvi zvinocengeja zvinozowanika pakati po zvivezwa zvo kare maningi zvo ciHindu, co Ejipita, no co Girisi. Kubva nguwa idzodzo dzekugadzira asi dzisina kutendeseka apo pamapuranga emabwe e tembere, pazvigaro zve zvifananidzo, uye pa nhoo, medallions, makapu, uye mare, dolphin yaidhirowa nge zviyero zve chain-armor kudai nge ya Saladin, uye soro rakashongedzwa nge ngowani kudai nge ra St. kubvira apo pane chimwe chinhu chemhando imwechete yechitupa chakakunda, kwete mumifananidzo yakawanda yakakurumbira ye whale chete, asi muzviratidzo zvakawanda zvesainzi pamusoro pake.

Zvino, nge zviro zveshe, mufananidzo we kare maningi uripo unozviita inga we whale, unowanika mu cavern-pagoda yakakurumbira ye Elephanta, mu India. VaBrahmin vanoramba veiti muzvivezwa zvisingaperi zve pagoda iyo isingakanganwiki, mabhizimisi eshe nekutsvaka, zveshe zvinofungidzirwa zvekuita zvemunhu, zvakafanofananidzwa makore asati aguma chimwe chazvo. Azvishamisi ngo kudaro, kuti mu njira imweni basa redu ro kucenesa ro kubata howe raidikana kunge riripo rakafukidzwa. I Hindoo whale inobhuyiwa, inoitika mudhipatimendi rakasiyana remadziro, inokhombidze kubarwa kwaVishnu muchimiro che leviathan, inoziikanwa ngekufunda seMatse Avatar. Asi kunyazwi chivezwa ichi chiri hafu yemunhu ne hafu ye whale, kuitira kuti chete kupa muswe wekupeisira, asi chikamu chidoko chake cheshe chakashata. Inoita inga muswe wakatetepa wo anaconda, kupinda nyara jakafara jo flukes jakakura jo whale yokadi.

Asi endai kuGaleries dzekare, muone zvino mufananidzo mukuru wemuKristu wekupenda wehove iyi; nokuti anobudirira kwete zviri nani kupfuura Hindoo yekare. Ndiwo mufananidzo waGuido waPerseus anonunura Andromeda kubva kugungwa-chikara kana whale. Guido wakawanepi muezaniso wo cipuka cinoshamisa kudaro? Uyewo Hogarth, mukupenda chiitiko chimwe chete mu “Perseus Anodzika” yake, haaiti kuti chimwe chete chive nani. Kukura kukuru kwechikara cheHogarthian kunotenderera pamusoro, kusinganyanyi kutora inch imwe yemvura. Ine mhando ye howdah pamushana wayo, uye muromo wayo wakatambanudzwa wakavhurika umo mabillows ari kutenderera, unokona kutorwa kuenda kuSuo reVatengesi rinotungamira kubva kuThames ngemvura kupinda muShongwe. Pasurepo, pano howe yo Prodromus yo Scotch Sibbald yo kare, no howe ya Jona, inga zvinokhombijihwa pa zvinyorwa zvo maBhaibheri o kare no kucekwa ko mabhuku o kutanga o kare. Zvinozobhuyiwa ngendaa dzeizvi? Kudai nge whale ye book-binder inotenderera kudai nge tsanga ye muzambiringa inotenderera mudziyo we chisimbiso chinodzaka—sezvakadhindwa ne kushongedzwa nge ndarama pa sure nge mapeji e musoro we mabhuku akawanda ekare nge matsva—izvo zvisikwa zvinodakadza maningi asi zvakachena, zvakatevedzerwa, ndinozvitora, kubve kune mifananidzo yakafanana ne vatique an. Kunyazi pasi pese inozwi dolphin, asi ndinodaidza hove iyi ye book-binder kuti kuedza whale; nekuti zvakanga zvakadaro zvakanangwa apo mudziyo wakatanga kuunzwa. Yakaunzwa nge muparidzi wekare we Italy kumweni muzana remakore rechi 15, nguwa ye Kumutswa kwe Kufunda; uye mumazuwa iwayo, uye kunyange kusvika panguva yakanonoka, dolphins yaizivikanwa kuti yaive mhando yeRevhiathan.

Mu vignettes ne zvimweni zviro zvinoshongedza mabhuku amweni ekare nguwa imweni unozosangana nge kubata kunoda kuziya maningi pa whale, apo mhando dzeshe dze spouts, jets d’eau, zvitubu zvinopisa ne zvinotonhora, Saratoga ne Baden-Baden, zvinouya zvichibuda kubva mu ngqondo yake isizi kuremba. Mu pheji ro kutanga ro bhuku ro kutanga ro “Kuenderera Mberi ko Kujija” munozowana ma whale anoda kuziva.

Asi kusiya kuedza kweshe uku kusizi kuite mushando, ngatiningire mifananidzo iyoyo ye leviathan inozviita inga yakangwara, mifananidzo yesainzi, ngeavo vanoziya. Mukuunganidza kwekare kwaHarris kwekuhamba kwezvikepe kune dzimwe ndiro dzehowe dzakabudiswa mubhuku rekuDutch rekuhamba, AD 1671, rine musoro unoti “Rwendo Rwekuenda kuSpitzbergen muchikepe Jonas muWhale, Peter Peterson weFriesland, tenzi.” Mu imwe ye ndiro idzona ma whale, akadai nge ma rafts makuru e matanda, anoemererwa akaata pakati pe ice-isles, nge ma bear machena anorumba pamusoro pe misana yawo inorarama. Mune imwe ndiro, blunder inoshamisa inoitwa yekumiririra whale ine flukes yakarurama.

Pasurepozve, pano quarto inoshamisa, yakanyorwa ngo umwe Kapiteni Colnett, Kapiteni wo Post mu hondo yo mu njiva yo Ingratera, ine musoro unoti “Rwendo rwo kutenderera Cape Horn kuenda ku Makungwa o Kumaodzanyemba, ngo cidisiso co kuengejera Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Mu bhuku iri muno mufananidzo unonanga kuti “Mufananijo wo Physeter kana Spermaceti whale, wakatorwa ngo cipimo kubvira pa imwe yakaurawa pa ndau iri phedo no gungwa ro Mexico, muna Nyamavhuvhu, 1793, yosimudzwa pa n’anga.” Ndinokahadzika kuti mutungamiri wengarava wakatora mufananidzo uyu wechokwadi kuti abatsire vashandi vake vemumvura. Kubhuya chiro chimwe basi ngendaa iyoyo, ngandibhuye kuti ine ziso raishandisa, mukuzwirana nechiyero chinoperekedza, ku sperm whale yakakura yakadzara, inozoita kuti ziso re whale iyoyo riite bow-window yakareba makubiti mashanu. Aa, mukuru wangu wakagwinya, ngenyi musikazi kutipa Jona einingira kubanze kweziso iroro!

Uyewo, kuunganidza zviro zvine hana yakawanda zve Natural History kuti zvidetsere vadoko nevadoko, vasina kushata kwakafanana kwekukanganisa. Tarisa bhuku iroro rakakurumbira rinonzi “Zviro Zvipenyu Zvemupfuri weGoridhe.” Mu bhuku rakapfupiswa ro ku London ro 1807, muno mapiritsi o cinyama cinonangwa kuti “whale” no “narwhale.” Andidi kuita inga ndicikakhatari, asi hove iyi isikavoneki inoita inga nguruve yakagurwa; uye, kana iri narwhale, kuona kamwe chete kwakakwana kushamisa munhu, kuti muzana rino remakore rechipfumbamwe hippogriff yakadaro yaikwanisa kuonekwa seyechokwadi pane chero ruzhinji rwakangwara rwevakomana vechikoro.

Pasurepo,zve, mu gore ro 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, mundhu anoziva maningi ngo pamusoro po zviro zvakasikwa, wakabudisa bhuku ro science ro kurongeka, rino mafodho akawanda o mihlobo yakasiana-siana yo Leviathan. Zveshe izvi azvizi kujeka kwega, asi mufananidzo we Mysticetus kana Greenland whale (kureva kuti, Right whale), kunyazwi Scoresby, murumbwana wakagara neruzivo rwekubata mhando iyoyo, unobhuya kuti aana mumwe wake mumasikirwo.

Asi kuiswa kwe cap-sheaf kune bhizimisi iri reshe rekuvhiringidza kwakachengeterwa mudzidzisi wesainzi Frederick Cuvier, hama yeBaron ane mbiri. Mugore ra 1836, wakabudisa bhuku rinozwi Natural History of Whales, apo wakapa zvaanodaidza kuti mufananidzo we Sperm Whale. Usati wakhombise mufananidzo iwoyo kune chero Nantucketer, zvaifanira kunge zvakanaka kupa kudzokera kwako kwekupfupikisa kubva kuNantucket. Mune izwi rimwe, Frederick Cuvier's Hove yeMbeu haisi Hove yeMbeu, asi chikwekwe. Eya, aazivi kumbobesehwa ngo kwenda koita hove yo whale (vandhu vakadaro avanyanyi kuzviita), asi wakawana kupi mufananijo wondhowo, ndiani ungazviziva? Pamwe wakazviwana semutungamiri wake wesainzi mumunda umwewo, Desmarest, wakawana imwe yekubvisa nhumbu yake yemene; ndiko kuti, kubva pane mufananidzo weChinese. Uye kuti vakomana vanorarama vane penzura avo veChinese ndivo, makapu akawanda e queer uye ndiro dzinotiziisa.

Kana iri ma whale e sign-painters anoonekwa munjira akarembera pamusoro pezvitoro zvevanotengesa mafuta, zvinozobhuyiwa ngezvawo? Vanonyanya kunge vari Richard III. whale, ine dromedary humps, uye mhuka zvikuru; kurya kwemangwanani pa tarts nhatu kana ina dzevafambisi vezvikepe, kureva zvikepe zve whale zvakadzara ngevafambisi vezvikepe: kuremara kwavo kunobhururuka mumakungwa engazi uye pendi yebhuruu.

Asi zvishaishi izvi zvakawanda pa kukhombija whale azvishamisi maningi pasure po zvese. Kunyinda! Zviro zvine zviro zvakawanda zvakadhirowa nge science zvakatorwa kubve ku howe dzakasungirirwa; uye izvi zvinenge zvakarurama semufananidzo wengarava yakaparadzwa, ine musana wakatsemuka, ingadai yakarurama kumiririra mhuka inokudzwa pachayo mukuzvikudza kwayo kwese kusina kuparadzwa kwe hull uye spars. Pikija hazvo kuti nzou dzakaema kuitira kureba kwadzo kweshe, Leviathan inopona haizi kumbozviyangarara zvakanaka kuti iite mufananidzo wake. Whale inopona, mukukura kwayo kwakadzara ngekukosha kwayo, inofanira kuonekwa kwega mugungwa mumvura dzisingazwisisiki; uye inoyangarara chikamu chikuru chake hachioneki, sechikepe chakatangwa mumutsara wehondo; uye kubva muchikamu ichocho chiro chisingaitike mhetsa nariini kuti munhu unofa amusimudze mumuiri mumhepo, kuti achengetedze kuzvimba kwake kweshe kwakasimba nekuvhunduka. Uye, tisikabhuyi nge musiyano unofungidzirwa zvikuru we contour pakati pe whale duku inoyamwa ne Leviathan ye Platonian yakakura; asi, kunyange munhau yeimwe yevana vadoko vanoyamwa vanosimudzwa pamusoro pechikepe, ndizvo zvinoitawo chimiro chake chekunze, chakaita se eel, limbered, chinosiyana-siyana, kuti kutaura kwake kwakanyatsojeka dhiabhorosi pachake aisakwanisa kubata.

Asi zvinokona kufungwa, kuti kubve mupfupa risina ngubo re whale yakasungirirwa, mazano akarurama anokona kutorwa anobata chimiro chake chemene. Apana. Ngokuti ndi ciro cimwe cinodakajisa maningi ngo pamusoro po Revhiatani uyu, kuti nyama yake inopa murangariro mudoko maningi ngo pamusoro po maumbirwo ake akawanda. Kunyazi kuti pfupa ra Jeremy Bentham, iro rinorembera kuita candelabra mu raibhurari yo umwe wo vaurayi vake, rinobudisa zvakanaka mufungo wo mundhu mukuru wakashata, wakaita zviro zvimweni zveshe zva Jeremy zvinotungamirira; asi hapana chimwe chinhu chemhando iyi chaigona kufungidzirwa kubva kumapfupa e leviathan chero api akabatana. Zvokadi, inga zvinonanga Muvhimi mukuru, nyama yo hove huru inobatana no mhuka yakazara zve yakavharwa inga zvinoita cipuka ku chrysalis inoifukidza ngo kutenderera. Kusiyana uku kunovoneka ngo njira inoshamisa musoro, inga zvinozovoneswa ngo njira imweni yo bhuku iri. Izvo zvinokhombiswazve ngo kuda kuziva maningi mu side fin, mafupa ayo anoda kuita inga mafupa o nyara yo mundhu, kusiyapo cigunwe cikuru basi. Fin iyi ine zvigunwe zvina zvenguva dzose zvemapfupa, index, pakati, mhete, uye chigunwe chidiki. Asi zvese izvi zvinogara kwenda no kwenda mu cifukijo cazvo co nyama, inga zvikunwe zvo mundhu mu cifukijo co kugadzirwa. Stubb waishekeja wakananga njiku imweni kuti: “Pikija hazvo nguva jimweni hove inotishandira ngo kutama kurangarira, aacaizombonangwa kamare kuti anotibata asina mittens.”

Nge ndaa ye zviro zveshe izvi, hino, nge njira iri yeshe yemungazviningira ndiyo, munosisira kupedzisa kuti Revhiatani mukuru ndicho chisikwa chimwe chete munyika chinofanira kugara chisina kupendwa kuguma pakupeisira. I cokwadi kuti mufananijo umwe ungaguma pa ciroco phedo maningi kupinda umweni, asi apana unokwanisa kucirova ngo njira yakajeka maningi. Ngo kudaro apana njira yo pasi pano yo kuziva kamare kuti whale inoita inga yakadini kamare. Uye njira imwe basi yaunokona kuwana ndiyo kunyazwi pfungwa inoshiviririka ye contour yake yekurarama, nge kuenda ku whaling iwewe; asi ngo kuita kudaro, amuiti ngozi shoma yo kuva citofu co kusingaperi no kunyudzwa ndiyena. Ngokudaro, zvinoita inga kuti zvainga zvakanaka kuti musanyanya kuite zviro ngo kuda kuziva ngo pamusoro po kubata Revhiatani uyu.





CHITSAUKO 56. Ye Mifananidzo Isinganyanyi Kukanganisa Ye Whales, uye Mifananidzo Yechokwadi Ye Zviitiko Zve Whaling.

Mukubatana nemifananidzo inotyisa ye whales, ndinoedzwa zvikuru pano kuti ndipinde pane iyo nyaya dzichiri dzinotyisa dzadzo dzinofanira kuwanikwa mumabhuku amweni, ekare nemazuwa ano, kunyanya muna Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, etc. Asi ndinopfuura ngendaa iyoyo.

Ndinoziya zvinyorwa zvirongomuna zvega zvakabudiswa zve Sperm Whale huru; Colnett, Huggins, Frederick Cuvier, uye Beale. Muchikamu chakapfuura Colnett naCuvier vakareketwa. Huggins's iri nani zvikuru kupfuura yavo; asi, nezvipingamupinyi zvikuru, yaBeale ndiyo yakanakisisa. Zveshe zvakadhirowa ndi Beale zve whale iyi zvakanaka, kusiya kwe mufananidzo wepakati pamufananidzo we whale nhatu mumafungiro akasiyana-siyana, capping chapter yake yechipiri. Mufananidzo wake wemberi, zvikepe zvinorwisa Sperm Whales, kunyange zvazvo pasina kumbozengurira zvakaverengwa kudakadza kusava nechokwadi kwevanhu vevamwe varume ve parlor, zvakarurama zvinoshamisa uye zvinofanana nehupenyu mune zvazvinoita. Zvimweni zve Sperm Whale zvakadhirowa muna J. asi zvakanyorwa zvinosuruvarisa. Asi, azvipi ndaa yake.

Kune Whale Yekurudyi, mifananidzo yakanakisisa yemufananidzo iri muScoresby; asi vanokweverwa pachiyero chidiki zvikuru kuti vabudise pfungwa inodiwa. Ane mufananidzo umwe basi wezviitiko zvekuvhima howe, uye izvi zvinosuruvarisa kushomeka, ngekuti ngemifananidzo yakadaro yega, kana yakaitwa zvakanaka, kuti unokwanisa kuwana chiro chakadai ngepfungwa yegwinyiso ye whale inopona sezvinoonekwa ngevavhimi vake vanopona.

Asi, zvakatorwa nge ndaa ye zveshe, nge kuretu zvakanakisisa, kunyazwi mune zvimweni zviro zvisizi kurongeka, mhemberero dze whales ne whaling scenes kuti dziwanike peshe peshe, zviro zviiri zvikuru zvakanyorwa nge French, zvakaurawa zvakanaka, uye zvakatorwa kubva kune zvakapendwa nge Garnery imwe. Zvichienderana, vanomiririra kurwiswa kweSperm uye Right Whale. Mukuvezwa kwekutanga Sperm Whale inokudzwa inokombidzwa mukukura kwakadzara kwesimba, yakangokwira pashi pechikepe kubva muhudzamu hwegungwa, uye yakatakura padera mumhepo pamushana wake kuparadzwa kunotyisa kwemapuranga echitofu. Musoro we chikepe auzi kutyoka zvishoma, uye unokwehwa uchingoenzanisa pamusana we chikara; uye wakaema mu prow iyoyo, nge ndaa ye nguwa imweyo isingaverengeki, unoona mufambisi we chikepe, hafu yakafukidzwa nge muromo unobika wakashata we whale, uye muchiito chekusvetuka, ingatei kubva mugomba. Chiito chechinhu chose chinoshamisa zvakanaka uye chechokwadi. Mutsara-tub isina chinhu hafu inoyangarara pamusoro pegungwa rakacheneswa; matanda emapuranga e harpoons akadururwa obliquely bob mukati mayo; misoro yevashandi vanoshambira yakapararira pamusoro pehove huru mukusiyana kwekutaura kwekutya; apo munzvimbo yakasviba yedutu ngarava iri kutakura pasi pamusoro pechiitiko. Mhosva yakakura ingawanikwa nge anatomical details ye whale iyi, asi ngazvipfuure; sezvo, nokuda kwehupenyu hwangu, ndaisakwanisa kudhirowa imwe yakanaka kudaro.

Mukuvezwa kwechipiri, chikepe chiri muchiito chekudhirowa parutii rwe barnacled flank ye Right Whale huru inorumba, iyo inounganidza ganda rayo rakasvipa rine masora mugungwa kunge imwe mossy rock-slide kubva kumawere ePatagonia. Jets dzake dzakaema, dzakadzara, uye dzakasvipa se soot; kuti kubva pakuwanda kwakanyanya kweutsi muchimbuzi, unofunga kuti panofanira kuva nekudya kwemadekoni kwakashinga kunobikwa mumatumbu makuru ari pashi. Shiri dzemugungwa dziri kupenya magakanje madoko, hove dzemashell, nezvimweni zvipo zvemugungwa nemaccaroni, ayo Right Whale dzimweni nguva inotakura pamushana wake une chirwere. Uye nguwa yeshe iyo leviathan ine miromo yakaoma iri kurumba ngekudzaka, ichisiya matani emafuta machena ane mheremhere mukumuka kwake, uye ichiita kuti chikepe chidoko chizunguzeke mumazvimbiremu kunge chikepe chakabatwa pedyo nemavhiri echikepe chegungwa. Ngokudaro, mberi kwese kunoitika mheremhere; asi sure, mukusiyana kunoyemurika kweunyanzvi, kune girazi regungwa rakadzikama, zvikepe zvisina starched zvakadonha zvengarava isina simba, uye inert mass ye whale yakafa, nhare yakakundwa, ine mureza wekubatwa une usimbe wakarembera kubva pa whale-pole yakaiswa mu spout-hole yake.

Ndiani Garnery mupendi, kana aive, handizivi. Asi upenyu hwangu nge ndaa yaro iyena wainga ari kubhuya nge ndaa yake, kana kuti waifundiswa ngo njira inoshamisa ngo mundhu wainge no ruzivo hwo whaleman. VaFrance ndivo vakomana vechiito chekupenda. Enda unoningira zviro zveshe zvakapendwa zve Europe, unozowanira kupi gallery yakadai yekupona nekufema mheremhere pa canvas, kudai nge mu horo iyoyo yekukunda ku Versailles; apo muoni anorwa njira yake, pell-mell, kuburikidza nehondo huru dzinotevedzana dzeFrance; apo banga rega rega rinoita inga rinopenya re Northern Lights, uye madzimambo anoteerana ane zvombo nemaEmperor anorumbire, kudai nge mhosva ye centaurs yakashongedzwa nge korona? Hazvina kufanira zvachose nzvimbo mu gallery iyoyo, ndizvo izvi gungwa hondo-zvikamu zve Garnery.

Kukwanisa kwechisikigo kwevaFrance kubata kunaka kwezviro kunoita inga kunooneka zvakasiyana ngezvipendwa nezvinyorwa zvavanoita zvezviitiko zvavo zvekubata howe. Kusina chikamu chimwe chegumi chekuziva kweEngland mukubata howe, uye pasina chikamu chechiuru chezveAmerica, vakapa marudzi maviri aya zvinyorwa zvega zvakapera zvachose zvinokwanisa kuronza mweya wemene wekuvhima whale. Kune chikamu chikuru, vadhirowa ve whale veChirungu neAmerica vanoita inga vakagutsikana zvakakwana ngekuronza mufananidzo wemamekanikani wezviro, kudai ngechimiro chisina chiro che whale; iyo, kusvika pa picturesqueness ye mhedzisiro inofunganya, inoda kufanana nekudhirowa purogiramu ye piramidhi. Kunyazi Scoresby, Right whaleman wakaziikanwa zvakanaka, pasure pokutipa urefu hwakaoma hwakadzara hwe Greenland whale, ne zvitatu kana zvirongomuna zvidoko zvidoko zve narwhales ne porpoises, unotibata nge mutsara we zvinyorwa zvekare zve zvikorekedzo zve chikepe, mapanga ekucheka, ne grapnels; uye ngekushingaira kwe microscopic kwe Leuwenhoeck inozviisa pasi pekuongororwa kwenyika inodedera makumi mapfumbamwe nematanhatu fac-similes yemakristasi echando eArctic akakudzwa. Ndinoreva kuti akuna kuzvidza kune mufambisi wakanaka maningi (ndinomuremekedza nge ndaa ye veteran), asi mu ndaa yakakosha maningi zvainga zvirokwazvo kutarisira kusawana kristaro imwe ngaimwe affidavit yakapika yakatorwa pamberi pe Greenland Justice of the Peace.

Kututsira zvinyorwa zvakanaka zvondhozvo zvakabva ku Garnery, kuno zvimweni zvinyorwa zviviri zvo ku French zvinosisira kucengejwa, ngo mundhu umweni anozvinyoresa kuti “H. Durand.” Umweni wavo, pikija usikazi kuenderana kamare no cidisiso cedu co wari, asi unodikana kubhuiwa pa mamweni masoko. I ndau yakanyarara yo masikati pakati po zvitsuwa zvo Pacifico; whaler yeFrance yakasungirirwa, kumahombekombe, yakadzikama, uye ine usimbe kutora mvura muchikepe; ma sail akasunungurwa engarava, uye mashizha marefu emichindwe ari kumashure, ese maviri aidonhera pamwe chete mumhepo isina mhepo. Izvo zvinoitika zvakanaka maningi, kudari zvecirangarirwa ngo pamusoro po kukhombija kwazvo vabati ve hove vakagwinya pashi po ciro cimwe cavo cidoko co kuzorora ko kumabvazuva. Imweni nharo yakasiyana maningi: ngarava inohamba-hamba pa gungwa rakavhurika, zve mu mwoyo wo upenyu hwo Leviathan, ine Right Whale parutii; mudziyo (muchiito chekucheka-mukati) hove pamusoro pechikara sekuti kune quay; uye chikepe, chinokurumidza kusundira kubva pane iyi nzvimbo yebasa, chiri pamusoro pekupa kudzingirira howe dziri kure. CHITSAUKO 59. Squid.

Zvishomana ngezvishomana zveihamba mumafuro e brit, Pequod wakaramba akabata munjira yake kuenda kuchamhembe-kumabvazuva kuenda kuchitsuwa che Java; mhepo yakapfava inosundidzira keel yake, zvekuti mukunyarara kwakatenderedza matanda ake matatu akareba anotetepa akasimudza zvinyoro-nyoro kumhepo iyoyo yakapfava, semichindwe mitatu yakapfava pabani. Uyezve, pa nguva jakakura mu usiku hwo sirivha, ndege iri yega, inokwezva yaizovoneka.

Asi mangwanani rimweni rakajeka rebhuruu, apo kunyarara kwakapotsa kwakapararira pamusoro pegungwa, kunyange zvazvo pasina kutarisirwa ngekudzikama kupi zvako kwakamira; apo zuva-glade rakareba rakapiswa pamvura raiita inga chigunwe chegoridhe chakaiswa pamusoro pavo, ichirayira chimwe chakavanzika; apo masaisai akatsvedza akazevezera pamwe chete sezvaairumba zvinyoro-nyoro; muhush iyi yakadzama ye nharaunda inooneka chimiro chinoshamisa chakaonekwa na Daggoo kubva ku main-mast-head.

Kuretu, chiro chikuru chakachena chakakwira ngekunonoka, nekukwira kuenda mudenga, nekudzisunungura kubva ku azure, pakupeisira chakapenya pamberi pe prow yedu kudai nge snow-slide, slid itsva kubva muzvikomo. Ngokudaro kupenya kwe nguva doko, inga zvishomani zvishomani zvakapera, zvonyura. Pasurepo wakamukazve, wakapenya chinyararire. Zvaiita inga kuti azvizi kuita whale; uye asi uyu ndi Moby Dick here? akafunga kudaro Daggoo. Zvakare phantom yakadzaka, asi pakuonekazve kamwe chete, ngekuchema kwakaita ngatei stiletto kwakavhundutsa muisa weshe kubva pakuguqa kwake, negro wakashevedzera—“Apo! apozve! apo anotyora! mberi pemene! Whale Chena, Whale Chena!”

Pa izvi, vafambisi vengarava vakarumba kuenda ku yard-arms, kudai nge nguwa yekubhururuka nyuchi dzinorumba kuenda ku matavi. Kuti kuvepo ko flitting attendance yo ndege imwe yainga yakanyarara zve iri yega kwainga kwashanda pa Ahabhi zvishomana ngo zvishomana, zvokuti iyena wainga wakanasirira kubatanija murangariro wo kupfava no kuzorora no kuvona kokutanga howe huru yaaiteverera; zvisinei izvi zvaive, kana kuti kuda kwake kwakamutengesa; chero njira ingadai yakaita, haana kukurumidza kuona zvakajeka mass machena, asi nekukurumidza simba akabva apa mirairo yekudzikisa.

Zvikepe zvirongomuna zvakakasira kuguma pamvura; Ahabhi ari pamberi, uye vese vakakurumidza kudhonza kuenda kune nyama yavo. Ngokukasika yakadzaka, zve apo, no zvikepe zvakasimudzwa, tainga tecivetera kuonekazve kwayo, wonai! munzvimbo imwecheteyo yayakanyura, kamwe zvakare yakasimuka zvishoma nezvishoma. Tichipotsa takariarwa kwechinguvana mifungo yeshe ya Moby Dick, zvino takaningira chiitiko chinoshamisa maningi icho makungwa akavanzika akazoratidza kuvanhu kuguma zvino. Mukuwo wakakura we pulpy, furlongs mukureba nekufara, we glancing cream-colour, wakaata weiyangarara pamusoro pemvura, maoko marefu asingaverengi aipenya kubva pakati pawo, uye aipenya nekutenderera sedendere re anacondas, kunge mapofu kubata chiro chisina mufaro chingasvika. Apana hope inooneka kana mberi yainga nayo; hapana chiratidzo chinofungidzirwa chekunzwa kana chisikwa; asi undulated apo pa billows, kuoneka kusingaoneki, kusina chimiro, mukana-wakafanana nehupenyu.

Kudai ngezwi rekuyamwa rakaderera rakanyangarikazve zvishomani, Starbuck achiri kuningira mvura yakavhiringika apo yainge yanyura, ngezwi remusango rakashevedzera—“Zvaipotsa zvaida kuti ndione Moby Dick ndorwa naye, pane kuti ndikuone, iwe mweya chena!”

“Chainge chiinyi, Mukuru?” akadaro Flask.

“Squid huru inorarama, iyo, vanonanga kuti, ngarava shoma jo whale dzakamboivona, vohwirira ku ndau javo jo kuendesa zvikepe kuti vabhuye ngo pamusoro payo.”

Zvakanyadaro, zviro zvipi zvinogondwa nge sperm whalemen muzviro zveshe zvakabatana ne kuona chiro ichi, zvemene ndizvona, kuti kuona kwacho kwakanyanya kusiyana, kuti mamiriro ezvinhu akaenda kuretu kuzviisa nge portentousness. Ngokudaro, azvinyanyi kuoneka, kuti kunyazwi umwe no veshe vanozvibhuya kuti ndizvona zviro zvakakura maningi zvinopona mu gungwa, asi vashomani maningi vavo vano zviro zvimweni asi zviro zvicikazivi kujeka ngo pamusoro po kuvepo kwazvo kwemene no maumbirwo azvo; zvisinei, vanotenda kuti inopa ku sperm whale chikafu chake chete. Nguva jimweni, paanoteverehwa phedo, anozobudisa zvinonangwa inga maoko akaparadzana o squid; vamwe vavo saizvozvo vakaratidza kudarika makumi maviri nemakumi matatu tsoka kureba. Vanofunga kuti chikara icho maoko aya aive acho kazhinji kacho chinobatirira navo pamubhedha wegungwa; uye kuti sperm whale, isina kufanana nedzimwe mhando, inopiwa mazino kuti ikwanise kurwisa nekuitsemura.

Kunoita ingatei imweni ndau yekufunga kuti Kraken mukuru wa Bishop Pontoppodan unozopeisira wazvigadzirisa kuita Squid. Njira iyo Bishop anozvitsanangure ndiyo, sekusiyana kwekukwira nekunyura, nezvimweni zviro zvaanorondedzera, mune zveshe izvi zviiri zvinoenderana. Asi abatement yakawanda inodiwa nekuremekedza bulk inoshamisa yaanoipa.

Nge vamweni vazivi ve zviro zvakasikwa vakazwa zvisizi kujeka ruzha rwe chisikwa chisingazivikanwi, chinobhuyiwa pano, chinosanganiswa pakati pe kirasi ye cuttle-fish, iyo, zvemene, mune zvimweni zviro zve kubanze ingaita inga ndeye, asi se Anak we rudzi.





CHITSAUKO 60. Mutsara.

Nge kubhuya nge ndaa ye whaling scene inozotsanangurwa nguva pfupi, ne kuzwisisa zviri nane zviro zveshe zvakafanana kumweni kwakaratidzwa, ndine pano kuti ndibhuye nge zve mashiripiti, dzimweni nguwa zvinotyisa whale-line.

Mutsara wakashandiswa pakutanga pakubata howe waiva we hemp yakanakisisa, yakaputika zvishoma nge tar, isizi kuzodzara ndiyo, kudai nge tambo dzakajairika; ngekuti apo tar, sezvinoshandiswa kakurutu, inoita kuti hemp iite nyore kune mugadziri wetambo, uyezve inoita kuti tambo iyo pachayo iite nyore kune mufambisi wengarava kuti ishandiswe ngengarava yakajairika; asi, haiwa basi kuti kuwanda kwakajairika kwaizoita kuti whale-line iome maningi kuitira kuti iite coiling yakavharika iyo inofanira kuiswa; asi inga vandhu vazinji vanofamba mu zvikepe vari kutanga kujija, tar mu njira yakawanda aizowedzerepi kugara kana kusimba ko tambo, pikija ingaita kuti ive yakabatana no kupenya.

Mumakore ekupedzisira tambo yeManilla ine muhove dzeAmerica yakapotsa yatsiva hemp sechiro che whale-lines; ngokuti, kunyazi asizi kugara kudai nge hemp, yakagwinya, zve yakapfaa maningi zve inotambanuka; uye ndichawedzera (sezvo pane æsthetics mune zviro zveshe), yakanyanya kunaka uye kuita kuchikepe, kupfuura hemp. Hemp ndeye dusky, munhu akasviba, mhando yeIndia; asi Manilla akafanana neCircassia ane bvudzi regoridhe kuti aone.

The whale-mutsara chete zvikamu zviviri pazvitatu zve inch mukuoma. Pakutanga, amuzorangariri kuti yakasimba maningi inga zvazviri kamare. Kubudikidza ngekuedza tambo dzayo imwe nemakumi mashanu imwe neimwe inozosimudza kurema kwemapaundi zana nemakumi maviri; kuti tambo yose ichatakura kushungurudzika kunoda kuenzana nematani matatu. Mukureba, mutsara wakajairika we sperm whale unoyera chiro chinodarika mazana maviri emamita. Kuenda ku sure kwe chikepe chinotenderera kuretu mu tub, haiwa kudai nge honye-pipe ye still though, asi kuti iite denderedzwa rimwe, rakaite inga cheese re “masheaves” akadzara, kana ma layers e concentric spiralizations, asina ndau yakadzara asi “mwoyo,” kana wakaumba cheese pa miniti yakaema tube. Sezvo kuvhiringika kushomani kana kuvhiringika mu coiling kunozoita, mukurumba kubuda, zvisingakanganisi kutora ruoko rwemunhu, murenje, kana muiri wese, kungwarira kwakanyanya kunoshandiswa pakuisa mutsara mu tub yayo. Vamweni vanoita zve harpooneer vanozopedza mangwanani ese mu bhizimisi iri, vechitwara mutsara wakakwira mudenga vozoudzokorora kuenda pashi kuburikidza nge chivharo kuenda ku tub, kudai nge chiito chekuzviputira kuti zvisunungurwe kubva kune zveshe zvinokwanisika zvekuunyana ne kutenderera.

Muzvikepe zveChirungu zvinoshandiswa mabhati maviri panzvimbo yeimwe; mutsara mumwechete uchienderera mberi uchipoteredzwa mumabhati ese maviri. Pane zvimwe zvinobatsira mune izvi; ngekuti mapatya aya ari madoko maningi anokwana nyore nyore muchikepe, uye haachisimbisire maningi; asi, tub ye America, ingangoita makubiti matatu muhupamhi uye yakadzika zvakaenzana, inoita zviro zvakawanda zvekutakura zvechikepe chine mapuranga ayo asi hafu-inch imwe chete muhupamhi; ngokuti pashi pe whale-boat yakadai nge chando chakakosha, chinozotakura uremu hwakaparadzirwa hwakanyanya, asi haiwa hwakanyanya kuungana. Panovharwa chivharo chechidziro chakapendwa pa line-tub ye America, chikepe chinoita inga chiri kudhonza nge keke guru remuchadho rinoshamisa kuti ripe ku whales.

Magumo ese maviri emutsara anoratidzwa; mugumo wepashi unopera muziso-splice kana loop inouya kubva pashi inopesana nerutivi rwe tub, uye inorembera pamusoro pemupendero wayo yakasununguka zvachose kubva kune zveshe. Kurongwa uku kwekupedzisira kwepasi kunodiwa pamaakaundi maviri. Kutanga: Kuti zvive nyore kusungirira kwairi mutsara wakawedzerwa kubva kuchikepe chiri pedyo, kana hove huru yakarohwa ikazozwika zvakadzama zvekuti inotyisidzira kutwara mutsara wese wakasungirirwa pakutanga pachikepe. Mu zviitiko izvi, whale zvemene inocinja inga kapu yo ale, inga zvakanangwa, kubvira pa cikepe cimweni kuenda ku cimweni; kunyange zvazvo chikepe chekutanga chinogara chichitenderera padhuze kuti chibatsire mukadzi wacho. Chechipiri: Kurongwa uku kunosisira maningi kuti vandhu veshe vangwarire; ngekuti kudai mugumo wepashi wemutsara wakabatanidzwa ngenjira iri yeshe kuchikepe, uye kudai hove huru yaizorumba mutsara kubanze kuguma kumagumo ingangoita muminiti imwe, inoputa sezvaanoita dzimweni nguwa, aisazoemapo, ngekuti chikepe chakaparadzwa chaizokwekwewa pasi pasina kukanganisa sure kwake kudzaka kwegungwa; uye muchiitiko ichi hapana town-crier aizombomuwana zvakare.

Vasati vadzasa chikepe kuti vadzingirire, mupendero wepadera wemutsara unotorwa sure kwe tub, wopfuura weitenderera loggerhead apo, unoendeswazve mberi kureba kweshe kwechikepe, wozorora pamuchinjiko pamusoro pe loom kana handle ye oar yemunhu weshe, kuti iite jogs pamusoro pechibhakera chake mukukwasva; uyewo kupfuura pakati pevarume, sezvavanochinjana kugara pa gunwales yakatarisana, kune chocks inotungamirirwa kana grooves mu prow yakanyanya yakananga yechikepe, apo pini yemapuranga kana skewer yakakura se quill yakajairika, inodzivirira kuti isatsvedza kunze. Kubva kumachocks inorembera mu festoon shomani pamusoro pemauta, uye inozopfuura mukati mwechikepe zvakare; uye mamita gumi kana makumi mairi (anozwi box-line) eiputirwa pabhokisi mumauta, inoenderera mberi ngenjira yayo kuenda ku gunwale ichiri mberi zvishomani kumashure, uye inozobatanidzwa kune short-warp—tambo iyo inokasira kubatanidzwa nge harpoon; asi pamberi pekubatana ikoko, pfupi-warp inopfuura nezvishamiso zvakasiyana-siyana zvinonesa zvikuru kunyatsojekesa.

Ngo kudaro, mutsara wo whale unopeta cikepe cese mu ma coil aco akaoma, wocitendeneja no kucitendeneja mu ndau jinokhwedera pa ndau jese. Vafambi veshe vanobatanidzwa mukuvhiringika kwayo kunotyisa; kuti kuziso rinothya ro mundhu wo mundau, vanoita inga vanoita zviro zvo kutamba zvo ku India, no nyoka jinouraya maningi jinoita inga jinodakajisa maoko avo. Uyewo hakuna mwanakomana wemukadzi unofa, kwekutanga, kugara pakati pezviro zvakaoma zve hempen, uye paanenge eiedza simba rake reshe pakukweva, mufunge kuti panguwa ipi zvayo isikazikanwi harpoon inokona kupondwa, uye zveshe izvi zvinotyisa zvingaiswa mumutambo kudai ngemheni dzine mhete; aakoni kuita kudaro asina kuvhunduka kunoita kuti mwongo wemene uri mumapfupa ake udedere mwaari kudai nge jeli rinozunguzika. Asi tsika—chiro chinoshamisa! chiinyi chisikakoni kuitwa nge tsika?—Gayer sallies, kudakara kwakawanda, majee ari nani, ne repartees dzakajeka, auzi kumbozwa pamusoro pe mahogany yako, kupfuura zvauchazwa pamusoro pe hafu-inch chena cedar ye whale-boat, apo yakasungirirwa kudaro muzvisungo zve hangman; uye, kunge burghers vatanhatu veCalais pamberi paMambo Edward, varume vatanhatu vanoumba chikwata vanokwevera mutsaya dzerufu, ne halter yakapoteredza mutsipa wega wega, sezvaungataura.

Pamweni murangariro mudoko maningi unozokutendera wari kubhuya ngo pamusoro po ngozi jondhojo jainga jeciitika kakurutu jo kuvhima howe—dzimweni shoma jayo jainga jakanyorwa ngo kutama kuziva—jo mundhu uwu kana mundhuwo wakabudiswa mu cikepe ngo n’anga, worashika. Ngokuti, apo mutsara uri kuhamba kubanze, kugarapo muchikepe, kwakafanana nekugara pakati pe manifold whizzings ye steam-engine mukutamba kwakadzara, apo danda reshe rinobhururuka, ne shaft, ne vhiri, zviri kukufudza. Zvakanyanya kushata; ngokuti amungakoni kugara mwecihamba-hamba mu mwoyo wo ngozi iji, ngokuti cikepe ciri kuzungunyika inga cibhegi, zve munokandwa ngo njira imwe no imweni, pasina kuziiswa kadoko; uye nge buoyancy imweni yekudzigadzirisa nge nguva imweyo yekuda ne kuita, unokona kutiza kuitwa Mazeppa we, ne kutiza nge apo zuwa rinoona zveshe pacharo risingambokoni kukubaya.

Uyezve: sekudzikama kwakadzama kunooneka kwega kunotangira nekuporofita dutu, zvingaite zvinotyisa kudarika dutu remene; ngokuti, zvemene, kudzikama kunongova chiputiro nehamvuropu yedutu; uye inozvibata mukati mwayo, sezvo pfuti inoita inga isina kukuvadza inobata upfu unouraya, uye bhora, uye kuputika; saka kuzorora kwakanaka kwemutsara, sezvaunonyarara serpentines pamusoro pevafambisi vezvikepe vasati vaunzwa mumutambo chaiwo-ichi chiro chinotakura zvakawanda zvekutya kwechokwadi kupfuura chimwe chikamu chenyaya iyi ine ngozi. Asi ngenyi munobhuya zvizinji? Varume veshe vanogara vakafukidzwa nge whale-mitsara. Veshe vanobarwa vaine zvipfeko pamutsipa wavo; asi pavanobatwa ngekukurumidza, kutendeuka kwekukurumidza kwerufu, ndipo apo vanhu vanofa vanoona njodzi dzakanyarara, dzisingaoneki, dzinogara dziripo dzehupenyu. Uye kana uri muzivi, kunyange wakagara muchikepe che whale, auzozwi mumwoyo kutya kumwe, pane kana wakagara pamberi pe mwoto wako we madekoni ne poker, uye kwete harpoon, parutii rwako.
