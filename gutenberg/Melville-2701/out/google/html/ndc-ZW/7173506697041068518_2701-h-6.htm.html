Ere kuti zviitike; ere chikepe chePequod chine masora chinoumburuka parutivi nechikepe che barnacled che leviathan; pakutanga zvakanaka asi kutarisira ndaa inoda kunge yakakosha kuzwisisa kwakadzara kwekubonga kwezvakazarurwa zvakakosha zvikuru zve leviathanic uye zvirevo zvemhando dzese dzinozotevera.

Izvo zvimwe zvakarongeka zvinoratidzwa zve whale mu genera yake yakafara, iyo ini ndaizoda zvino kuisa pamberi penyu. Asi asipi mushando wakapfava ere. Kupatsanurwa kwezvikamu zvenyonganyonga, hapana zvishoma pano essayed. Teererai zvakaiswa ngevakuru vakuru vakanakisisa uye vazvino.

Kapiteni Scoresby, mu gore ro 1820 AD, wakananga kudari: “Apana davi ro Zoologia rinobatanijwa maningi inga iro rinozwi Cetology.”

“Azvisipi cido cangu, kudari zvainga zviri mu simba rangu, kupinda mu kubvunjisa ngo pamusoro po njira yokadi yo kuparadzanisa cetacea mu zvikwata no mbhuri. * * * Kuvhiringika kwakanyanya kuripo pakati po vandhu vanoerenga ngo pamusoro po mhuka iyi” (sperm whale), unodaro Surgeon Beale, AD 1839.

Charley akataura zvisina kunaka kuti vaive humbug.

Ngazvizikanwe kuti, ndichisiya nharo dzeshe, ndinotora ndau yakanaka yekare yekuti whale hove, ndodaidza Jona mutsvene kuti anditsigire. Ichi chiro chakakosha chakagadzikana, pfungwa inotevera ndeyekuti, ndeipi kuremekedza kwemukati iyo whale yakasiyana nedzimwe hove. Padera apo, Linnæus wakakupa zviro izvona. Asi muchidimbu, ndizvo izvi: mapapu uye ngazi inodziya; asi, dzimwe hove dzese hadzina mapapu uye dzine ropa rinotonhora.

Zvinoteera: tinozopashangura kudini whale, nge zviro zvake zvo kubanze zvakajeka, kuitira kuti zvioneke kumunyora nguva jese jinozouya? Kuti tiite zvidoko, hino, whale hove inoputika ine muswe wakapamhama . Ndikona une iye. Zvisinei, iyo tsanangudzo ndiyo mugumisiro wekuwedzera kufungisisa. Walrus inobudisa mazwi akafanana ne whale, asi walrus haisi hove, ngekuti inogara pamhepo. Asi izwi rekupeisira re tsanangudzo richiri rakanyanya kukosha, sezvakabatana nekutanga. Pedyo no mundhu wese anodikana kuvona kuti hove jese jinozivikanwa ngo vandhu vanogara mu nyika adzina muswe wakatetepa, asi wakaema, kana wakakwira no kudzaka. Asi, pakati pehove dzinobudisa mvura muswe, kunyange zvazvo ungave wakagadzirwa zvakafanana, unogara uchitora nzvimbo yakatambanuka.

Nge tsanangudzo iri padera apo yekuti whale chiinyi, andizi kumbobvisa kubve muhama dze leviathanic chipuka chemugungwa cheshe chakaziikanwa kuguma zvino nge whale nge Nantucketers vanoziya zvakanakisisa; kana, kune rumwe rutivi, kubatanidza nayo hove chero ipi zvayo kusvika zvino inoonekwa sedzimweni nyika.* Ngokudaro, hove dzeshe duku, dzinoputika, uye dzine muswe wakapamhama dzinofanira kusanganiswa mune iyi ground-plan ye Cetology. Zvino, hino, unouya kupatsanurwa kukuru kwe whale host yese.

*Ndinoziya kuti kuguma nguwa ino, howe dzinozwi Lamatins ne Dugongs (Nguruve-hove ne Sow-hove dze Mabhokisi e Nantucket) dzinosanganiswa nge vazhinji vanofunda nge zve zviro zvakasikwa pakati pe howe huru. Asi sezvo hove-nguruwe idzi dziri ruzha, dzinozvidzwa, kakurutu dzinohwanda mumiromo ye nzizi, uye dzinodya uswa hwakanyorova, uye kunyanya sezvo dzisingabudisi, ndinoramba zvitupa zvadzo se whales; uye vakavapa mapasipoti avo kuti vasiyane neUmambo hweCetology.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Iyi howe, pakati peChirungu chekare inoziikanwa zvisina kujeka sehowe yeTrumpa, nehowe yePhyseter, nehowe yeAnvil Headed, ndiyo Cachalot yemazuwa ano yevaFrance, nePottsfich yeMacroce yeLongphalus, ne. Iyena, pasina kukana-kana, ndiyena wakakura maningi pa nyika yese; iyo inotyisa zvikuru pane zvose whales kusangana; iyo inonyanya kukudzwa muchikamu; uye yekupedzisira, nekure kure yakakosha zvikuru mukutengeserana; iye ari chisikwa chete chinowanikwa chinhu ichocho chakakosha, spermaceti. Zvese zvake zvakasiyana-siyana zvichawedzeredzwa, mune dzimwe nzvimbo dzakawanda. Ndizvo zvikuru nezita rake kuti ini zvino ndinofanira kuita. Kudai zveirangarirwa ngo njira yo filoloji, zvinoita inga zvicikazwisisiki. Mazana emakore apfuura, apo Sperm whale yakanga isikazikanwi zvachose muhupenyu hwake hwakanaka, uye apo mafuta ake aingowanikwa ngetsaona kubva kuhove yakasungirirwa; mu njiku jondhojo spermaceti, zvinoita inga kuti, zvairangarirwa ngo vandhu vazinji kuti zvaibva ku cipuka cakafanana no caizivika pa nguvayo mu England inga Greenland kana Right Whale. Inga mufungowo, kuti spermaceti imweyo yainga iyo inokurumidza kushekedza ye Greenland Whale iyo syllable yekutanga ye izwi inoronza zvemene. Nguwa idzodzo, zvakare, spermaceti yainga yakashomeka maningi, isikashandiswi kuita chiedza, asi inga mafuta no mutombo basi. Zvaifanira kungowanikwa kune vanotengesa mishonga sezvo iwe mazuva ano unotenga ounce ye rhubarb. Pa, sekufunga kwangu, ngekuhamba kwenguva, kuvepo kwemene kwe spermaceti kwakaziikanwa, zina rayo rekutanga rakanga richiri kuchengetedzwa ngevatengesi; hapana mubvunzo kuti kuwedzera kukosha kwayo nepfungwa saka inoshamisa inokosha yekushomeka kwayo. Uye nge ndaa ye izvi appellation inofanira kunge yakaguma pakupiwa kune whale iyo spermaceti iyi yakatorwa zvemene.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO II. ( Right Whale ).—Mu njira imweni iyi ndiyona inoremekejwa maningi pa ma leviathan, ngokuti ndiyona yokutanga kuvhimwa nguva jese ngo mundhu. Inobudisa nhau inonyanya kuziikanwa se whalebone kana baleen; uye mafuta anozivikanwa zvikuru se “mafuta e whale,” chinhu chisina kukosha mune zvekutengeserana. Pakati pevabati vehove, anosarudzwa zvisina kusarura ngemazina ese anotevera: The Whale; iyo Hove yeGreenland; iyo Whale Yakasvipa; iyo Whale Guru; iyo Whale Yechokwadi; iyo Whale Yekurudyi. Kune chibvumirano chisina kujeka pamusoro pekuzivikanwa kwemhando saka multitudinously yakabhabhatidzwa. Chiinyi zvino whale, iyo yandinosanganisa mumhando yechipiri yeFolios yangu? Ndiyo Mysticetus Mukuru wevadzidzisi vezvisikwa veChirungu; iyo Greenland Whale yevagari veChirungu; iyo Baleine yakajairika yevaFrance vanobata hove; hove yeWalfish yeGrowlands yeSweden. Ndiyo whale iyo kwemazana emakore anodarika maviri apfuura yakavhimwa ngeDutch ngeEnglish mumakungwa eArctic; ndiyo hove huru iyo vabati vehove veAmerica vakatevera kubvira kare mugungwa reIndia, paBrazil Banks, paNor’ West Coast, nedzimweni ndau dzakasiyana-siyana dzenyika, dzakasarudzwa ndivo Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Vamweni vanoita inga vanovona mupauko uripo pakati po whale yo Greenland yo ciNgezi no whale yo kurudyi yo vandhu vo ku Amerika. Asi vanotenderana kamare mu zviro zvavo zvese zvikuru; uye hapana kumboratidzwa chokwadi chimwe chete chakajeka chekuisa musiyano wakakura. Ndiyo nge kuparadzaniswa kusingaperi kunoenderana nekusiyana kusingaperi, kuti mamweni madhipatimendi enhoroondo yechisikigo anozoita zvakaoma. Whale yakarurama inozorapwa kumweni kwenguva yakareba, nekutaura nekujekesa sperm whale.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO III. ( Fin-Back ).—Pashi pe soro iri ndinokarakadza chikara icho, nge mazina akasiyana-siyana e Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, na Long-John, chakaonekwa pedyo ne gungwa reshe uye chinowanzova howe huru iyo ndege yayo iri kure inonyanya kutsanangurwa nge vafambi vanoyambuka Atlantic, mu New York packet-tracks. Mukureba kwaanoguma, uye mu baleen yake, Fin-back inofanana ne whale yekurudyi, asi ine girth shoma portly, uye ruvara rwakajeka, rwunoswedera kune olive. Miromo yake mikuru inokhombija ciro cakafanana no tambo, yakaumbwa ngo kubatana, kutenderera ko mavhinga makuru. Ciro cikuru cinomusiyanisa, fin, kwaakatora zina rake, kazinji ciro cinovoneka. Fin iyi inoita makubiti matatu kana mana kureba, inokura yakaema kubva kumashure kwemushana, ine chimiro chekona, uye ine mugumo wakapinza maningi. Pikija hazvo kuti ciro cimweni cicikavoneki, ciphenduko ici ciri pacedu, nguva jimweni, cinozovoneka pambhene cinobuda pashi. Pano gungwa rakadzikama zvishoma, uye rakanyorwa zvishomani ngemafungu akatenderera, uye iyi fin yakaita ngatei gnomon inoema uye inokanda mumvuri pamusoro penyika yakaunyana, zvinokona kufungwa kuti denderedzwa remvura rakaritendenedza rinoita inga rinofanana ne dial, ine maitiro ayo uye wavy hour-lines. Pa Ahazi-dial iyoyo mumvuri unowanzodzokera sure. Iyo Fin-Musana haisi yekubatana. Anoita inga anovenga whale, inga vamweni vanarume vanovenga vandhu. Kunyara maningi; nguva dzose kuenda ari ega; zvisingatarisirwi kukwira pamusoro mumvura iri kure uye yakanyanya kusuruvara; jet yake yakarurama uye imwe chete yakakwirira inokwira kunge pfumo rakareba misanthropic pamusoro pebani risina mvura; chipo che simba rinoshamisa kudaro ne kukurumidza mukudhuvha, zvekuti zvinoramba zveshe zvinoteverwa nge mundhu; leviathan iyi inoita inga Kaini wakadzingwa zve acikakundwa wo rudzi rwake, wakatakura ngo ndava yo cirovejeso cake co mufananijo wondhowo pa musana wake. Kubva pakuva ne baleen mumuromo make, Fin-Back dzimweni nguva inosanganiswa ne whale yakarurama, pakati pemhando ye theoretic inonzi Whalebone whales , kureva, howe dzine baleen. Pa zviro izvi zvinodanwa kuti Whalebone whales, zvinozoita inga kuti pano muhlobo jakawanda, asi maningi azvo azvizivikanwi maningi. Howe dzakafara-mhino uye howe dzine miromo; howe dzine musoro we pike; howe dzakabatana; whales dziri pasi-jawed uye rostrated whales, ndiwo mazita evabati vehove kune mhando shoma.

Mukubatana ne appellational iyi ye “Whalebone whales,” zvakakosha maningi kubhuya, kuti kunyazwi nomenclature yakadaro ingava yakanaka pakuita kuti zviro zvive nyore kubhuya ngezve muhlobo umweni we whales, asi azvina maturo kuedza kupatsanurwa zvakajeka kwe Leviathan, yakaemeswa nge baleen yake, kana teeen, kana hump,; pasinei nekuti zvikamu izvo zvakaiswa chiratidzo kana zvinhu zvakajeka zvikuru zvinoita inga zvakagadzirirwa zvirinani kuti zvikwanise kupa hwaro hwehurongwa hwenguva dzose hweCetology kupfuura chero humwe hurongwa hwemuviri hwakaparadzana, iyo whale, mumhando dzake, inoratidza. Zvino zvakadini? Iyo baleen, hump, musana-fin, uye mazino; izvi zviro zvine peculiarities zvakapararira zvisina kusarura pakati pemihlobo yeshe ye whales, pasina kufunga kuti zvingava chii chimiro chekugadzirwa kwazvo mune zvimweni uye zvakanyanya kukosha. Ngokudaro, hove huru yo mbeu no howe yo musana, imwe nga imwe ine musana; asi apo kufana kunopera. Pasurepo, hove iyi imweyo ine musana no howe yo Greenland, imwe nga imwe yo izvi ine baleen; asi apo zvakare kufanana kunopera. Uye zvakafanana chete nedzimwe nzvimbo dzakataurwa pamusoro. Mumhando dzakasiyana-siyana dze whale, dzinoumba musanganiswa wakadaro usina kurongeka; kana, muchiitiko cheumwe wavo akaparadzana, kuzviparadzanisa kusina kurongeka kwakadaro; sezvo zvakakwana kupikisa zvose zvakajairika methodization yakaumbwa pamusoro hwaro hwakadaro. Padombo iri umwe neumwe we whale-naturalists akaparadzana.

Asi zvinokona kufungwa kuti, muzvikamu zvemukati zve whale, mumuviri wake—apo, zvishoma, tinozokwanisa kurova kupatsanurwa kwakanaka. Haiwa; chii chinhu, somuenzaniso, chiri muGreenland whale anatomy inoshamisa kupfuura baleen yake? Asi takaona kuti nge baleen yake hazviiti zvakanaka kuparadzanisa Greenland whale. Uye kana ukadzika mumatumbu e leviathans akasiyana-siyana, ngenyi apo auzowani kusiyana chikamu chemakumi mashanu sezvinowanikwa kune systematizer seavo vekunze vakatoverengwa. Zvino chiinyi chinosara? hapana chimwe asi kubata whales mumuviri, mubhuku ravo rose rakasununguka, uye nekushinga kuzvisarudza nenzira iyoyo. Uye iyi ndiyo njira yeBhaibheri pano yakagamuchirwa; uye ndiyo yega inokwanisa kubudirira, nokuti ndiyo yega inokwanisika. Kuti zvienderere mberi.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ) CHITSAUKO IV. ( Hump Back ).—Imbwa iyi inonyanya kuoneka kumahombekombe ekumaodzanyemba kweAmerica. Akabatwa kazhinji kazhinji apo, uye akakwehwa kuenda kuchiteshi chengarava. Ane pakiti guru paari semutengesi; kana kuti ungamudaidza kuti Nzou uye Castle whale. Zvakanyadaro, zina rakazivikanwa ngo pamusoro pake arimusiyanisi zvakakwana, ngokuti sperm whale inewo hump pikija iri doko. Mafuta ake haana kukosha zvakanyanya. Ane mhepo. Ndiye unonyanya kutamba uye une mwoyo wakareruka pane howe dzeshe, achiita mapundu akawanda e gay uye mvura chena kazhinji kupfuura imwe yadzo.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO V. ( Musana we Razor ).—Ngezve whale iyi zvishoma zvinoziikanwa asi zina rayo. Ndakamuona ari kure neCape Horn. Yekusiya basa, anodzivirira vese vavhimi uye vazivi. Pikija asipi mundhu anothya, aazivi kumbokhombija ciro cimweni asi musana wake, unokwira mu ndau yakareba yakapinza. Murekere kuti aende. Ndinoziya zvishoma pamusoro pake, uye hapana mumwe munhu anoziva.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO VI. ( Sulphur Bottom ).—Umweni mundhu waisiya basa, wainga no ndani yakaita inga sarufa, acikakana-kana wakawana ngo kutsvaira mataira o Tartarian mu zvimweni zvo kudzika kwake zvakadzama. Haanyanyi kuonekwa; at least andizi kumbomuona asi mumakungwa ari kuretu ekumaodzanyemba, uyezve nguva dzeshe ari kuretu maningi kuti ndifunde chiso chake. Haambodzingiriwi; aizotiza netambo-kufamba kwemutsara. Zvishamiso zvinobhuyiwa ngezvake. Adieu, Sarufa Pashi! Andikoni kuronza zvimweni zviro zvemene pamusoro pemwi, kunyazwi muNantucketer mukuru maningi aakoni.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO I. ( Grampus ).—Pikija hazvo kuti hove iyi, iyo kufema kwayo kunozwika ngo simba, kana kuti kufema kwayo, kwakapa tsumo ku vandhu vo mu ndau, inozivika maningi inga inogara mu ndau yakadzaka, asi aizi kuzivikanwa maningi pakati po howe. Asi ngo kuva no zviro zvese zvakakura zvinosiyanisa leviathan, vandhu vazinji vanojija ngo pamusoro po zviro zvakasikwa vakamuziva ngo pamusoro po ciro cimwe. Iyena une octavo size iri pakati nepakati, inosiyana kubva pamamita gumi nemashanu kuguma pamamita makumi mairi nemashanu pakureba, uye ane zviyero zvinoenderana zvakatenderedza chiuno. Anoshambira mumapoka; haambofi akavhimwa nguva nenguva, kunyange zvazvo mafuta ake akawanda, uye akanaka kwazvo kune chiedza. Nge vamweni vabati vehove njira yake inoonekwa sechiratidzo chekuenderera mberi kwe sperm whale huru.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO CHII. ( Black Fish ).—Ndinopa mazina evabati vehove vanozivikanwa ngehove dzese idzi, ngekuti kazhinji ndidzo dzinonyanya kunaka. Panoitika kuti zina ripi neripi risingazivikanwi kana risingabhuyi, ndinozobhuya kudaro, ndozokarakadza rimweni. Ndinoita kudaro zvino, ndichibata Hove Yekudema, inodaidzwa kuti, ngekuti kudema ndiwo mutemo pakati pezvinenge zvese zve whale. Saka, mudaidze kuti Hyena Whale, kana uchida. Kudya kwake kunoziikanwa kakurutu, zve kubve mu mugariro wokuti makona o mukati o miromo yake akabhenda kuenda mudenga, wakatakura kushekerera kusingaperi kwe Mephistophelean pa hope yake. Whale iyi inosvika makubiti gumi nematanhatu kana gumi nemasere pakureba. Anowanikwa mune anenge ese latitudes. Ane njira inoshamisa yekukombidza dorsal hooked fin yake mukudhuvha, iyo inoita inga mhino yeRoma. Pavasingashandise zviro zvinodetsera maningi, vavhimi ve sperm whale nguva jimweni vanobata Hyena whale, kuti varambe veipa mafuta anodhura kuti vashandise pamuzi—inga vamweni vanochengeta pamuzi vanongwarira mare, pavasina shamwari, zve vari vega vega, vanopisha mafuta asizi kunaka pa ndau ye wax inonunhira. Kunyazi blubber yavo yakatetepa maningi, dzimweni dze whale idzi dzinozokupa mafuta anoguma makumi matatu emagaloni.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO CHECHITATU. ( Narwhale ) . Chipuka ichi chine urefu hwemamita anokwana kuita gumi nematanhatu, asi nyanga yacho inoita mamita mashanu, kunyazwi amweni anodarika gumi, uye anotoguma pamamita gumi nemashanu. Kubhuya zvakajeka, nyanga iyi inoita inga n’anga yakareba, inokura kubve mutsaya mumutsara wakadzara zvishomani kubve mu ndau yakapamhama. Asi inowanika basi ku divi rakashata, iro rine zviro zvakashata, rinopa muridzi waro chiro chakafanana ne chimiro che murume wekuruboshwe asina kujeka. Ndeipi chinangwa chaicho chenyanga iyi yenyanga yenzou kana pfumo inopindura, zvingaoma kutaura. Zvinoita inga kuti azvishandiswi inga banga ro hove yo banga no hove yo bill; kunyange zvazvo vamwe vafambisi vezvikepe vanondiudza kuti Narwhale inoishandisa se rake mukutendeuka pasi pegungwa kuti iwane chikafu. Charley Coffin wakati yaishandiswa kuita chiro chinobaya chando; ngekuti Narwhale, inokwira kuenda pamusoro peGungwa rePolar, uye inowana yakafukidzwa ngechando, inosundire nyanga yake mudenga, uye saizvozvo inopwanya. Asi amungakwanisi kukhombijira kuti ciro cimwe co zviro izvi cainga cakarurama. Mufungo wangu ngewekuti, kunyazwi nyanga iyi ine rutivi rumwe ingashandiswa ngegwinyiso nge Narwhale—kunyazwi izvi zvingaite—zvingaite zvakanaka maningi kwaari kuti aite folda mukuerenga mabhukwana. Narwhale yandazwa ichidaidzwa kuti Tusked whale, Horned whale, uye Unicorn whale. Iyena ndiye muenzaniso unoda kuziya we Unicornism unowanika munenge muumambo hwese hwezvisikwa zvinorarama. Kubva kune vamweni vanyori vekare vakaunganidza kuti nyanga imweyo ye gungwa-unicorn yaive mumazuwa ekare yaionekwa semushonga mukuru wekurwisa poison, uye sekudaro, kunasirisa kwayo kwaiunza mitengo yakakura. Iwo wakazosvibiswazve kuita munyu unoshanduka-shanduka kuitira madzimai anofenda, ngenjira imweyo iyo nyanga dzemombe dzechirume dzinogadzirwa kuti dziite hartshorn. Pakutanga yainge iri pachayo yakaverengerwa chinhu chekuda kuziva kukuru. Black Letter inondironzera kuti Sir Martin Frobisher paakapetuka kubva ku rwendo irworwo, apo Mambokadzi Bess akasimudza nyara yake yakashongedzwa nge ushingi kwaari kubva pa fafitera ye Greenwich Palace, apo ngarava yake yakashinga yaihamba kuenda mu Thames; Black Letter wakananga kuti: “Sir Martin paakapetuka kubve ku rwendo hwondhohwo, wakagwadama pashi wakakhombijira mukuru wake nyanga yakareba maningi yo Narwhale, iyo ko nguva yakareba pasure po kusungirirwa pa muzi wo mambo ku Windsor.” Munyori wekuIreland anoronzera kuti Earl weLeicester, akagwadama pamabvi, akapawo kuhukuru hwake nyanga imweni, inosanganisira chikara chenyika che unicorn.

Narwhale ine chimiro chinodakadza maningi, inoita inga ingwe, ine ruvara rwepasi rwakachena semukaka, ine mavara akatenderera uye akareba ekusvipa. Mafuta ake akakwirira zvikuru, akajeka uye akanaka; asi pane zvishoma zvacho, uye haawanzovhimwa. Anonyanya kuwanika mumakungwa e circumpolar.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO CHIVU. ( Killer ).—Pa howe iyi, zvishoma zvinozivikanwa kamare ngo vandhu vo ku Nantucket, zve apana ciro cinozivikanwa ngo mundhu anozviita kuti anoziva ngo pamusoro po zviro zvakasikwa. Kubva pane zvandakaona pamusoro pake ari kure, ndinofanira kutaura kuti aive anenge akakura se grampus. Iyena wakashata maningi—muhlobo wo hove yo Feegee. Nguwa imweni unobata howe huru dze Folio nge muromo, ochoremberapo kudai nge mhuka, kuguma chipuka chine simba chinoneseka kuguma parufu. Muurayi haambovhimwa. Handina kumbobvira ndanzwa kuti ane mafuta akaita sei. Kusiyapo kunokona kutorwa ku zina rakapuwa whale iyi, nge ndaa ye kusajeka kwayo. Ngokuti teshe tiri vaurayi, panyika neparuandhle; Bonapartes uye Shaki zvinosanganisirwa.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO V. ( Thrasher ).—Murume uyu une mbiri nge muswe wake, uwo waanoshandisa kuita ferule pakurova vavengi vake. Anokwira musana we Folio whale, uye paanenge eidhuvha, anoshanda njira yake ngekumurova; sezvo vamwe vadzidzisi vezvikoro vanowirirana munyika nenzira yakafanana. Kunyange zvakadaro zvishoma zvinozivikanwa neThrasher pane Muurayi. Veshe vari vaviri vanobudisa mutemo, kunyange mumakungwa asina mutemo.

Ndizvo zvinopera BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), uye inotanga BHUKU III. ( Vaviri nevaviri ).

DUODECIMOS.—Izvi zvinosanganisira howe duku. I. Mhuka ye Huzza. II. Iyo mhuka yeAlgeria. III. Iyo Porpoise ine muromo weMealy.

Kune avo vasizi kuwana mukana wakakosha wekufunda ndaa iyi, zvinokona kuita inga zvinoshamisa, kuti howe dzisinganyanyi kudarika makubiti mana kana mashanu dzinofanira kurongwa pakati pe WHALES—izwi, iro, ngenjira yakajairika, rinogara reipa mufungo wekukura. Asi zvipuka zvakaiswa pashi padera apo se Duodecimoes zviro zvisingashaishi, nge mazwi e tsanangudzo yangu yekuti whale chiinyi— kureva hove inoputika, ine muswe wakapamhama.

BHUKU III. ( Duodecimo ), CHITSAUKO 1. ( Mhuka ye Huzza ).—Iyi ndiyo mhuka inowanika munyika yeshe. Zina iri ndeyekupa kwangu; nokuti kune mhando dzakawanda dze porpoises, uye chimwe chinhu chinofanira kuitwa kusiyanisa. Ndinomudaidza kudaro, ngekuti unogara eidhuvha muzvikwereti zvinoshekedza, izvo pamusoro pegungwa rakafara zvinoramba zvichizvikanda kudenga kunge zvipfeko muchaunga cheChina-chaJuly. Kuoneka kwavo kunowanzorumbidzwa nemufaro nemufambisi wengarava. Vakazara ngo mweya yakanaka, vanogara veibva ku mhepo inopenya kuenda ku mhepo. Ndiwo majaha anogara eigara pamberi pemhepo. Vanoonekwa sechiratidzo cherombo rakanaka. Kana iwewe wega uchikwanisa kudzivirira kushekerera katatu pakuona howe idzi dzinorarama, zvino denga rinokudetsera; mweya wekutamba kwekunamata hauna mukati menyu. Huzza Porpoise yakadyiswa zvakanaka, yakakora inozokupa galoni imwe yakanaka yemafuta akanaka. Asi mvura yakanaka uye isina kusimba inobudiswa kubva mushaya dzake inokosha zvikuru. Iri mukukumbirwa pakati pevanogadzira zvishongo uye vanoita mawachi. Vafambisi vezvikepe vanozviisa pa hones dzavo. Nyama yePorpoise yakanaka kudya, munoziva. Zvinokona kunge zvisina kumbouya mumusoro mako kuti porpoise inoputika. I cokwadi, muromo wake mudoko maningi zvokuti auzivi kuvoneka ngo kukasika. Asi nguva inoteera paunozowana mukana, muone; uye uchazoona Sperm whale huru pachake mudiki.

BHUKU III. ( Duodecimo ), CHITSAUKO CHII. ( Mhuka yeAlgeria ).—Muparadzi wezvikepe. Savage zvikuru. Anowanikwa chete, ndinofunga, muPacific. Iyena wakakura zvishoma kupinda Huzza Porpoise, asi zviro zvakawanda zvinoita inga zvakafanana. Mutsamwise, uye achasungirira kune shark. Ndakamudzikisa nguva dzakawanda, asi handisati ndambomuona akabatwa.

BHUKU III. ( Duodecimo ), CHITSAUKO III. ( Porpoise ine muromo wakaoma ).—Muhlobo mukuru we Porpoise; uye inowanikwa chete muPacific, kusvika pazvinozivikanwa. Zina re Chirungu rimwe basi, raakadaidzwa ndiro kuguma zvino, ndiro re vabati vehove—Right-Whale Porpoise, kubve muchiitiko chekuti anonyanya kuwanika munharaunda ye Folio iyoyo. Muchimiro, anosiyana nge Huzza Porpoise, eiva ne girth isina kutenderera uye inodakadza; zvirokwazvo, iye ane chimiro chakachena uye chakaita semurume. Aana zviphenduko pamusana pake (vamweni porpoises vakawanda vane), ane muswe unodakadza, uye maziso eIndia ane manzwiro e hazel hue. Asi muromo wake we mealy unoshaishira zveshe. Pikija hazvo kuti musana wake wese kudzaka kuguma pa zviphenduriso zvake zvo ku divi wakaita inga sable yakadzaka, asi mutsara wo muganhu, wakasiyana inga ciro ciri mu ndau inogara ngarava, unodaninjwa kuti “chiuno cinojeka,” mutsara wondhowo unomutevera kubvira pa mudzi kuguma pa sure, no mavara maviri akasiyana, dema pa musoro no chena pashi. Muchena unosanganisira chikamu chesoro rake, nemuromo wake weshe, zvinoita kuti aite inga achangopukunyuka kubva mukushanyira kwechivi ku meal-bag. A zvakanyanya kuipa uye mealy chikamu! Mafuta ake akafanana zvikuru neaya porpoise yakajairika.

Kusiyapo DUODECIMO, njira iyi aienderi mberi, ngokuti Porpoise ndiyo doko maningi pa howe. Padera, muno maRevhiyatani ese anozivikanwa. Asi kune boka revanhu vasina chokwadi, vanotiza, hafu-fabulous whales, iyo, seAmerican whaleman, ndinoziya ngekuzivikanwa, asi kwete pachangu. Ndinozovaerenga nge mazita avo e mberi-castle; ngekuti zvingaite kuti rondedzero yakadaro inokona kukosha kune vaongorori vemunguva yemberi, vanogona kupedza zvandinazvo pano asi ndakatanga. Kana imwe yehowe dzinoteera, inozobatwa ngekuzoiswa chiratidzo, hino unokona kubekwa nyore nyore muSisitimu iyi, kudai ngekukura kwake kweFolio, Octavo, kana Duodecimo:—Whale yeBottle-Nose; iyo Whale yeJunk; iyo Whale ine Musoro wePudding; iyo Whale yeCape; iyo Whale Inotungamira; iyo Whale yeCannon; iyo Hove ye Scragg; iyo Whale Yemhangura; iyo Nzou Whale; iyo Whale yeIceberg; iyo Whale yeQuog; iyo Whale yeBhuruu; etc. Kubva ku Icelandic, Dutch, uye vakuru vekare veChirungu, panokona kudzokororwa mamweni mazita e whales asina kujeka, akakomborerwa nge mazita ese asina kufanira. Asi ndinozvisiya sezvisingashandi zvachose; uye hazvikwanisi kubatsira kuvafungidzira kuti vanongova mazwi, akazara neReviathanism, asi asingaratidzi chinhu.

Kupejisira: Zvakanangwa pa kutanga, kuti nyika ino aizovi pano, zve ngo kukasika, yakakwana. Aukoni asi kuona zvakajeka kuti ndakachengeta izwi rangu. Asi zvino ndinosiya cetological System yangu yakaema kudaro isizi kupera, kunyazwi Cathedral huru ye Cologne yakasara, ne crane ichiri yakaema padera pe shongwe isizi kupera. Kuitira kuti erections duku dzinokona kupera ngevagadziri vavo vekutanga; vakuru, vechokwadi, vanombosiya copestone kune vana vachauya. Mwari ndidzivirire kuti ndisambopedza chero chii zvacho.
