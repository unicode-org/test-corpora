IKYAV I TESE 24. Orjir.

Er mo vea Queequeg se lu eren tom u kôron ishu i i yer ér whale ne sha inja hegen yô; man er tom u kôron ishu i i yer ér whale ne u hingir u lun kwagh u ior mba ken tar ugen ve nengen ér ka kwagh u ior ve eren sha inja ga shi a lu a iti i dedoo ga yô; nahan, ishima ngi nyian mo cii sha u me na ne, ne ior mba ken tar ne, ne fa kwagh u ijirôron i sha mimi i i er se mba se lu mbakôronishu mba i yer ér whale la.

Sha hiihii la, alaghga a nenge ér ka kwagh u a lu hange hange ga u tesen ér, hen atô u ior kpishi, i nenge ér kpenga u kôron ishu i i yer ér whale la ngu kwagh môm a kwagh u i yer ér liberal professions la ga. Shi sha kwagh u i kaa ér tom wase ngu wang ga la yô, ica a gba ga tsô a hii u fan akaa agen a ior kpishi ve fe ga zan zan nyian yô, man akaa ne cii, aa na yô, tso u i yer ér sperm whale la una hemba lun ken akaa a hemban lun wang a tar u u lu wang ne. Gadia ka nyi i lu akaa a cieriyol a ior ve kav a mi la, aluer i tôô a kar sha akaa a cieriyol man akaa a kpilighyol a Aôndo a a zough ayol a ve la yô!

Kpa, shin er tar ne u lu keren se mba se lu mbakôronishu mba i yer ér whale la nahan kpa, u ngu nan se icivir i hemban cii sha apera ga; ee, ka mcivir u a hembe cii yô! gadia alaghga ikyav mbi tswamev man akaa a nan iwanger man akaa a nan iwanger a a lu yeghen sha tar cii la cii, nga yeghen, er ngise i lu sha ajiir a civir Aôndo kpishi nahan, sha ci u iengem yase!

Gadia, er shighe u mbayevkasev mba lun a ityough ki nyian, mba ve lu eren iniumbe la, ken uwer u Aipor man Mei, ve zende ve yem ken ya ken toho u u lu a mngerem kpishi, u u lu a ior kpishi ga la nahan; kon u oak u tse u u hembe lun a ikyav ga, u u hembe lun a ikyav mbi tswamev, u u hembe lun a ikyav mbi tswamev la je kpa, ua tindi a ikyav mbi tswamev mbigenev kpuaa, sha u ngohol mbavannya mba lun a ishima i saan saan nahan; nahan Ahaba er nahan, ken masejime yô, a er kwagh kpuaa sha akaa a eren anumbe a ior ve eren sha u eren anumbe a mlu u wankwase la. Hemba kwa môm yange a tese ikyav i mlu u ken ishima u a lu a mshi ga la, u yange ma lu ken hanma or ugen cii yô, ica a gba ga tsô ma a due ken igbar sha mciem yô.
