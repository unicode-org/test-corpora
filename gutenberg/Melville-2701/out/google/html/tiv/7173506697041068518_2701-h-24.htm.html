Man nyian kpa m lam a Starbuck hen ijiir la nahan, sha kwagh u tso wam u a vihi la. shi ere I could perish—Ka nyi i lu nahan?—Kwagh ugen ngu u hegen una na mbatomov mba gomoti mba ve lu ken mzough u mbaajiriv cii vea lu suen ve la cii vea lu a mciem iyol yô:—er zwa u ikyôhô nahan , ka u cir ityough yagh , nahan kpa!”

Shighe u iyange i mir la, i lu nengen a zege inyam i i yer ér whale la her.

Nahan i hide i pande ikyav mbi tsoo la, nahan hanma kwagh cii kar vough er tugh mbu i kar la nahan; kpa, i ungwa ikyenge i ikyav mbi utyaav, man ikyenge i iwen i eren tom a mi la zan zan iyange i wanger, er nomsombaiorov mbara lu eren tom sha akaa a tan iwanger sha u sôron utso mba ve lu her la vindi vindi shi sôron ikyav mbi utyaav vev mbi he mbi vea er tom a mi sha iyange igen la yô. Hen shighe ne, sha ikyagh ki tswamegh ki Ahaba ki ki vihi la, orgbanakaa la er un angahar agen; er lu her er tugh mbu i kar la nahan, Ahaba u lu a mciem iyol la tile her ken atô u tso na; mnenge na u ken myer, u a lu heliotrope la yange hide ijime sha dial na; tema vegher u sha ityoughkitaregh sha iyange i hiihii la.
