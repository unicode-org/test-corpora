IKYAV I TESE 4. Ityough ki sha 4.

Shighe u m nder pepe pepe er iyange i wanger nahan la, m nenge a uwegh ku Queequeg ku kende sha a mo sha gbenda u hemban lun a dooshima man u doon mo ishima cii. U vande henen wer m ngu kwase na. I er ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kiriki mbi i yer ér squares man triangles la sha kpekpe u iyou la; man uwegh nagh ku i nger kwagh sha mi cii sha ikyav i ior mba ken tar u Krete mba ve lu a mkur ga yô, avegher a ahar a lu a mlu môm vough vough ga—m hen mer ka sha ci u yange lu a uwegh nagh sha zegemnger sha gbenda u vough ga, sha iyange man mule la, ave a ikyondo na la lu cir sha ashighe kposo kposo—kwagh ne cii lu inja er ka uwegh ku m kaa mer, u ikyondo i patch. Sha kpôô yô, vegher ugen u m lu yaven sha mi er uwegh ku yange m hii u nderen la nahan, m kera fa kwagh u a lu sha ikyondo i i cir sha mi la ga, nahan ve nungwa mlu ve imôngo; man lu sha mlu u mlu u ken iyol man mlu u ken ishima la tseegh m fa mer Queequeg ngu kuvem ye.

Kwagh u yange m nenge a mi la lu kwagh u kpilighyol. De m nôngo m pase ve. Shighe u m lu iyev la, m umbur kwagh ugen u yange er mo, u lu kwagh môm a kwagh ne yô; aluer ka kwagh u mimi shin ka mnya kpa, mayange m kera lu a ian i eren kwagh ne vindi vindi ga. Mlu u kwagh la lu nahan. Yange m lu sôron caper shin akaa agen—m hen mer lu nôngon ér una cir sha mngerem ma eren tom la, er yange m nenge or u ôôn kwagh kpuaa er ayange kpuaa ken ijime nahan; man ngôm u sha uhar u yange, sha gbenda ugen shin sha gbenda ugen, lu gbidyen mo hanma shighe, shin tindin mo m za yav a kwaghyan u aikighe ga la,—ngom yange a urugh mo sha angahar a due a mo ken ikyagh ki usugh la, a cir mo sha u za yaven, shin er lu ahwa ahar atetan tseegh sha iyange i Jun 21, iyange i hemban lun ica ken inyom ken hemisphere wase nahan kpaa. Yange m cier iyol kpishi. Kpa iwasen lu sha mi ga, nahan m mough sha kpekpe m za nyôr ken iyou yam i kiriki i i lu sha kpoyou u sha utar la, m dugh akondo am kera sha er me wua shighe yô, maa m gba ôron kwagh sha ishimavihin m nyôr hen atô u ikyav mbi tswamev mbin.

M yav her sha ishimavihin m lu henen mer gba u ahwa pue kar ataratar jimin aa kar cii ve me mase lun a ishimaverenkeghen i mnder u shin ku ye. Ahwa pue kar ataratar ken gambe! mlu wam u kiriki la yange a vihim kpishi u henen sha kwagh ne. Man yange lu kwagh u wanger kpishi; iyange lu tan sha windoo, man zege ikyenge i kôchi sha ugodobi, man imo i mbayevnomso mba lun a isharen i yaven a nomsoor ken iyou la cii. Yange m hemba vihin cii—ken masejime yô, m mough, m hua akondo, m gba yevese sha angahar am a m lu a mi la, m ker ngôm u sha uhar la, maa m gba gban sha angahar a na fese, m zamber un er ka kwagh u doon tsung u nan mo ikyav mbi tswamev mbi dedoo sha ci u ieren yam i bo la; kwagh môm ga, saa di u nan mo ibo ér m yav mnya shighe gôgônan u m fetyô u wan ishima a mi ga la. Kpa lu un hemba doon shi hemba lun a imoshima i dedoo ken ungô mba kasev cii ye, nahan hiden yô, gba u me za nyôr ken iyou yam. M yav her ahwa imôngo m lu memen ga, m lu zungwen kpishi hemba er yange m lu a vande zungwen ga la, kua akaa a vihin a yange er mo ken masejime la je kpaa. Ken masejime yô, alaghga yange ma m gba ken mnyam ma cier iyol ma m yav yô; man m nder sha mi kpuaa kpuaa—m lu a mnyam ma cier mo iyol avegher ahar—m bugh ashe, man iyou i yange i lu a iyange i tan iwanger sha mi la cii i cir i ken ime i ken won hegen. Fese nahan m ungwa mciem gba yevese ken iyol yam cii; kwagh môm kpa lu u a nenge a mi ga, shi kwagh môm kpa lu u a ungwa ga; kpa lu inja er i ver uwegh ku hemban ku orumace ken uwegh agh nahan. Ikyev yam yange i lu sha ityough ki ikyônough ki torough kira, man mlu u a lu a iti ga, u i fe un ga, u a lu viing shin phantom, u uwegh ku lu sha mi la, lu inja er ngu ikyua ikyua a gambe wam nahan. Gadia kwagh u yange lu inja er ka anyom a i kohol sha anyom a karen la, m yav her, m lu a mciem iyol kpishi, m kera taver ishima u dughun uwegh am kera ga; nahan kpa m lu henen mer aluer m kôrcio u eren un inch môm tseegh yô, mciem mara ma vihi. Yange m fa er mlu u ken ishima ne yange mase duen ken a mo kera ga; kpa m nder pepe yô, m umbur akaa ne cii sha mciem, nahan ayange man ukasua man iwer ngi kar imôngo yô, m kera lu nôngon kpoghuloo mer me pase kwagh u a lu myer la ga. Ei, zan zan ihwa ne je kpa, ashighe kpishi ka m cir iyol yam kwagh sha mi.

Hegen yô, dugh mciem ma cieriyol mara kera, man mlu wam u ken ishima u ungwan uwegh ku hemban ku ku lu ken a mo la lu kwagh môm kpishi, sha mlu ve u kpilighyol la, a mba yange m nenge a mi zum u m nder m nenge uwegh ku mbaaôndo mbaaiev ku Queequeg ku cir sha a mo la. Kpa ken masejime yô, akaa a yange er hen tugh mbu i kar la cii hide er sha ishimavihin, môm môm, ken kwagh u mimi u a lu vough yô, maa m gba yaven uma sha ci u mlu u vihin tsung la tseegh. Gadia shin er yange m nôngo mer me cir uwegh nagh nahan kpaa—m bugh ikyav mbi nomsoor la—kpa, er a lu yaven nahan kpa, a kuvem kpoghuloo, inja er kwagh môm ngu ga saa di ku tseegh una pav se uhar nahan. Hegen yô, m nôngo mer me nder un—“Queequeg!”—kpa mlumun na lu u ôron kwagh sha mciem tseegh. M mase eren msen, m lu inja er m ngu ken ikyondo i anyinya nahan; man fese nahan m ungwa mciem kpuaa. Yange m kende ikyagh ki eren kwagh a mi la sha, m nenge a tomahawk la yav hen kpe u or u vihin tu la, inja er ka wanye u lun a ishigh ki hatchet nahan. A pickle u doon tsung, sha mimi yô, m hen mer; abed heen ken iyou igen ken iyange i vesen, vea or u yan ior man tomahawk! “Kweek!—ken iti i dedoo, Kweek, nder!” Ken masejime yô, sha ci u m lu eren ayôôso kpishi, shi m lu ôron kwagh genger genger sha kwagh u mlu u vough ga u kuve nomsoor ugen sha gbenda u ivesegh la yô, m kôrcio u duen a mciem iyol; man fese nahan, a hide a uwegh nagh ijime, a cir iyol na cii er ka aniwa u ken tar u Newfoundland u a dugh shin mnger nahan, maa a tema sha gambe, a cir er ka ikyagh ki pike nahan, a kenger mo, shi a cir ashe a na inja er a umbur er yange m va lu her la ga nahan kpaa, shin er kwagh ugen u a lu eren mo ken ishima u fan un la lu eren mo kwagh u doon ga nahan kpaa. Shighe ne, m yav m lu kenger un sha ashe ving, m kera lu a akperan kpishi hegen ga, shi m cir ishigh sha u kenger kwagh u i gbe un u a soo u fan kwagh kpishi la kpuaa. Shighe u ken masejime yô, mhen na lu inja er un wa ishima u benden a mlu u or u lu yaven a na imôngo la, nahan a hingir u lun inja er un hime a kwagh u mimi la nahan; a gba duen sha ityough ki ikyônough ki torough, man sha akav man ityogholough mbigenev yô, a na m kav mer, aluer doom yô, una vande eren ikyondo, una mase undun mo me wuha iyol shighe u m bee yô, nahan iyou la cii ia lu sha ci wam. I hen ér mo, Queequeg, sha mlu u a lu la, kwagh ne ka kwagh u a lu a mzehemen kpishi yô; kpa, mimi yô, mbaaferev mban mba a mlu u ken iyol u eren kwagh sha inja, ôr kwagh u u soo cii; ka kwagh u kpilighyol er ve lu a ilyahan ga yô. M wuese Queequeg ne jighilii, sha ci u yange er kwagh a mo sha inja shi wa mo ikyo kpishi, kpa mo yô, m lu a ibo i eren kwagh sha inja ga kpishi; u kenger un sha gambe, shi nengen a akaa a a lu eren ken iyouci la cii; sha ci u shighe u isharen yam i fan kwagh la lu hemban doon sha kwagh u mar wam la. Nahan cii kpa, or er Queequeg nahan u nengen a nan hanma iyange ga yô, un man igbenda na yange ve lu kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi.

Yange hii u huan ikyondo i sha ityou sha u huan ikyondo na i beaver, i i lu gôgônan kpishi la, hen kpe, maa—a kera lu a ikyondo i eren tom a mi ga her—a ker akôv a na. Kwagh u yange er sha mi shin inya la, m fa ga, kpa kwagh u yange er u dondon yô, lu u gbidyen iyol na—a kôr akôv sha uwe, shi a hua ikyondo i cir sha ityou—shin inya hen gambe; shighe u, sha m-er u eren kwagh sha tswam man m-er u eren kwagh sha inja ga la, m nenge mer lu eren tom kpoghuloo sha u sôron iyol na; shin er tindi u eren kwagh sha inja u m vande ungwan kwagh u a ga nahan kpa, i gba u hanma or nana lu ken myer shighe u nan lu huan akondo a nan yô. Kpa Queequeg yô, u nenge kpa, lu kwagh u i gbe un ken mlu u geman la—lu ikyôhô shin ikyôhô ga. Yange lu or u fan kwagh tsembelee je kuma u tesen mlu na u ken won la sha igbenda i hemban kpilighyol cii. Ityesen na lu a bee ga. Yange lu or u henen makeranta u vesen. Luun er yange una lu or u fan kwagh kpuaa ga yô, alaghga ma a vihi iyol na sha kwagh u ikyav mbi utyaav ga cii; kpa hen shighe la, yange una lu or u eren kwaghbo her ga yô, mayange ma a hen ér una nyôr shin inya gambe una wa ve sha mi ga. Ken masejime yô, a due a ikyondo na i i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev kpishi shi i lu cir sha ashe na la, maa a hii u eren ikyenge shi eren ikyenge ken iyou la, inja er, er un fe u huan akondo kpishi ga yô, akôv a na a a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér cowhide la—alaghga yange i er un sha tindi ga—kpa a gbidye un shi a tôv un a ican sha hiihii la.

Er m nenge, hegen, er ikyondo i cirin windoo i lu ga, shi gbenda la kpa u lu kpuaa kpishi yô, iyou i i lu vegher ugen la na zwa ér i nenge a mi ken iyou la wang, shi i lu nengen a mlu u injaa ga u Queequeg er la seer a seer, lu zenden zenden a kwagh ugen ga, saa di ikyondo na i sha ityou man akov a na tseegh; M zamber a na er m fetyô la cii, mer a er tom a toilet na fese kpuaa, man hemban je yô, a nyôr ken pantaloon na fese er a fetyô la cii. Yange er kwagh u i kaa la, maa za hemen u ôôn iyol. Hen shighe la pepe pepe hanma Orkristu cii yange nana ôô ishigh; kpa Queequeg yô, kwagh u kpiligh mo iyol yô, yange lumun ér a cir mngerem ma ôôn iyol na sha ityou man sha ave man sha ave na tseegh. Tsô a hua ikyondo na i cir sha ityou la, maa a tôô sabun u taver u a lu sha tebul u hen atô u ijiir i kilen akondo la, a wa ken mnger, a hii u ôôn ishigh nagh. M lu kenger sha u me nenge ape a ver ikyagh ki esen kwagh la yô, maa a tôô ikyagh ki esen kwagh ki ki lu sha ityough ki gambe la, a dugh ikyagh ki esen kwagh ki ki lu a ikyav mbi eren tom a mi la kera, a bugh ityough kira, a cir ki sha ikyondo na kpuaa, maa a gba yevese zan hen kpekpe u iyou la, a hii u eren kwagh sha mi kpoghuloo. Ka m hen mer, Queequeg, kwagh ne ngu eren tom a ikyagh ki Rogers ki hemban doon cii la sha u kimbin injo. Shighe kar yô, m pine mer, ka nyi i lu ikyagh ki tswamegh ki i er ityough ki ikyagh ki tswamegh ki i yer ér harpoon la, man er i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi doon tsung hanma shighe yô.

Ica i gbe ga yô, a kure iyou na i ôôn iyol i i shi her la, nahan a due ken iyou la kera sha iyolgengese, a cir zege jaket na u pilot monkey la, shi a lu eren anumbe a harpoon na er ka ikyagh ki orvesen u shoja nahan.





IKYAV I 5. Kwaghyan u aikighe.

M dondo un ijime fese, m sen m nyôr ken iyou i man msôrom la, m ôr or u nan lu a iyou la kwagh u doon tsung. Yange m nenge a na sha ishimavihin ga, shin er yange lu eren kwagh a mo sha kwagh u or u m lu yaven a nan la kpuaa ga nahan kpaa.

Kpa, m-er u dedoo ka kwagh u dedoo u ageegh, man ka kwagh u dedoo u a lu kpuaa kpishi yô; ka kwagh u hemban vihin je la. Nahan aluer or môm, ken iyol i nan, nan ngu a akaa a nan sha u eren kwagh u dedoo a nan yô, nana de hiden ijime ga, kpa nana de ér nan vihi inyaregh shi i vihi nan inyaregh sha imba gbenda la saan saan. Man or u nan lu a kwagh u doon tsung sha kwagh u nan yô, fa wer kwagh ngu ken or la hemba er alaghga u hen la.

Ijiir i man msôrom la iv a mbavannya mba ve lu tsan her tugh mbu i kar la, mba m lu a nenge a ve tsembelee ga yô. Ve lu mbakôronishu mba i yer ér whalemen la cii; mbahemenev mba shin zegemnger man mbatomov mba sha uhar man mba sha utar man mbatomov mba shin zegemnger man mba eren ikyav mbi utyaav mbi shin zegemnger man mba eren ikyav mbi utyaav mbi shin zegemnger man mba eren ikyav mbi utyaav man mba nengen sha utso; kômpeni u lun a ikyav mbi tswamev man mbi lun a ikyav mbi tswamev; set u i cir un ikyondo ga, u i cir un ikyondo yô, ve cii ve hua jaket mba monkey sha ci u akondo a pepe.

U nenge a shighe u hanmô ve nan lu hen kpezegemnger la wang. Ikyev i gumor ne i i lu gbang gbang la ngi er ka pear u i cir sha iyange nahan, shi ia lu inja er ngi a ihyuma er ka musky nahan; una lu a ian i nyôron shin tar ayange atar ken zende na u ken tar u India la ga. Or u a lu ikyua a na la ngu a ikyav mbi tswamev kpuaa; alaghga u kaa wer ikyav i satin wood ngi ken a na. Ken mlu u or u sha utar la, i lu a ikyav i tropic tawn her, kpa i cir i sha ityou kpuaa; a shi nan kpa yange tsa hen kpezegemnger kasua jimin. Kpa ka an nan a tese ikyav er Queequeg nahan? u yange i wa un ikyav mbi tswamev kposo kposo la, lu inja er ka kpekpe u Andes u a lu vegher u ken imbusutarimese la nahan, sha u tesen ken mzough môm, mlu u ahumbe u a lu kposo kposo la, hen ijiir kposo kposo.

“Grub, ho!” hegen yô, or u nan lu a iyou la vaa, a bugh hunda ugen, maa se nyôr se za ya kwaghyan u aikighe.

Ve kaa ér ior mba ve nenge a tar ne yô, ka ve hingir u lun a ieren i kundu kundu, ve hingir u lun a ieren i dedoo shighe u ve lu vea ior yô. Kpa ka hanma shighe cii ga: Ledyard, or u vesen u ken tar u New England u zenden sha la, man Mungo Park, u ken tar u Scotland la; ken ior cii, ve hemba lun a vangertiôr ken iyou i tsan la cii. Kpa alaghga u peren tar u Siberia sha mato u i yer ér sledge u iyôngo i lu tôôn un la tseegh er Ledyard nahan, shin u zenden ica i gba tswen tswen sha iyav mbu doon ga, ken atô u ior mba lun a ikyav mbi utyaav mba ken Afrika, u lu mzough u akaa a Mungo yange er sha inja ga la cii—m kaa mer, alaghga imba zende ne ia lu gbenda u hemban doon u zuan a mlu u ken nongoior ga. Nahan cii kpa, sha avegher kpishi yô, imba kwagh la ka i lu hanma ijiir cii.

Mbamhen mban heen tseegh ka sha ci u mlu u yange se cii se tema sha tebul, man m lu wan ago iyol u ungwan akaa a dedoo agen sha kwagh u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la; kwagh u kpiligh mo iyol yô, nôngo u lun hanma or cii yange nan lu viing viing. Man ka nahan tseegh ga, kpa ve nenge ér kunya kôr ve. Een, ior mbagenev mba ve lu a ikyav mbi utyaav mbi shin zegemnger yô, lu heen, mbi kpishi ken a mbi yange mbi nyôr zege ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér whales la sha zegemnger u vesen la, mbi lu mbavannya hen a ve jimin cii—ve nôngo num a mbi kpene kpene a u kenger ve ashe shio; man nahan cii kpa, heen ve tema sha tebul u yan kwaghyan u aikighe u ior ve kohol imôngo la—ve cii ve lu a iti i môm, ve cii ve lu a isharen i môm—ve lu kenger ayol a ve sha mciem er ka iyongo nahan, inja er ve lu a vande duen ken ikyônough ki torough ki iyongo ki ki lu hen atô u Uwo u Green la ga nahan. Kwagh u ior ve soo u fan yô; mbaatsator mba cian kwagh ne, mba ve lu mbautyaav mba cian kwagh ne!

Kpa kwagh u Queequeg yô—er nan ve, Queequeg tema her hen atô ve—sha ityough ki tebul la kpa, yange er kwagh sha mi ga; er ka ice nahan. U ôron kwagh sha mimi yô, me kera ôr kwagh kpishi sha kwagh u mar na ga. Or u hemban soon un la yange una na un ityôkyaa i dedoo i va a ikyagh ki tswamegh nagh hen kwaghyan u aikighe vea na, shi eren tom a mi her a mcivir shio ga; u zan sha tebul a mi, zan zan ityough kpishi ki lu nôngon u kpen, shi kôron ikyav mbi inyamtoho mbi i yer ér beefsteaks la sha a na. Kpa kwagh la yange er sha ishima i legh legh kpishi, man hanma or nan fa er sha mnenge u ior kpishi yô, u eren hanma kwagh cii sha ishima i legh legh ka u eren kwagh sha ishima i legh legh.

Se mba ôron kwagh u akaa a a lu kposo a Queequeg la cii heen ga; er yange palegh kôfi man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér hot rolls la, shi ver ishima na sha ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér beefsteaks, mbi i eren mbi kpishi ga la. Kwagh ne na yô, shighe u kwaghyan u aikighe bee la, a hide ijime er mbagenev mbara cii a nyôr ken iyou i ior ve kohol her la nahan, a ta usu u tomahawk-pipe na, a tema her ving lu yan kwagh shi man taav a ikyondo na i i pav un ga la, maa m due m za zende.





IKYAV I TESE 6. Igbenda i eren kwagh.

Aluer yange ma kpiligh mo iyol sha hiihii la m nenge a or u ken tar ugen er Queequeg nahan, lu zenden hen atô u ior mba lun a ilyahan ga mba ken gar u a lu a mzehemen yô, ica i gbe ga yô, kpiligh mo iyol shighe u m hii u zenden sha ugodobi mba ken gar u New Bedford la.

Sha igbenda i vesen i i lu ikyua a ajiir a ior ve nyôron tso her la, hanma kpezegemnger u vesen cii una na ior ian i nengen a akaa a a lu a inja ga a a dugh ken ityar igen yô. Sha igbenda i Broadway man Chestnut je kpa, ashighe agen mbatsoov mba ken Mediterenia ka ve gbidye kasev mba ve lu cian kwagh la. Ior mba ken tar u Lascar man mba ken tar u Malawi fa gbenda u i yer ér Regent Street la ga; man hen gar u Bombay, hen kpezegemnger u i yer ér Apollo Green la, ashighe kpishi ior mba ken tar u Yankee mba ve lu uma la ka ve na ior mba hen ijiir la mciem iyol. Kpa gar u New Bedford hemba gar u Water man gar u Wapping cii. Ken ajiir a i ter ken masejime ne yô, ka mbatsoov tseegh u nenge a mi ye; kpa ken gar u New Bedford yô, mbayan ior kpôô kpôô ka ve tile ve lu lamen sha akôv a ugodobi; savages jighilii; mba ve lu kpishi ken a ve la mba tôôn inyam i i lu icighan ga la her. Ka i na or u nan fe nan ga nan kenger nan.

Kpa, dugh ior mba ken tar u Feegee man mba ken tar u Tongatobooar man mba ken tar u Erromanggo man mba ken tar u Pannangi man mba ken tar u Brighggian kera yô, u nenge a akaa agen a ior ve nengen a mi a a hembe lun kwagh u doon tsung yô. Hanma kasua yô, ior mba ken tar u Vermont man New Hampshire mba lun a ikyav mbi utyaav kpishi ka ve va ken gar ne, ve cii ve lu a isharen i zuan a mtsera man iengem ken tom u koron ishu. Ve hemba lun agumaior, a lun a mlu u taver iyol; mba ve gber ikyon la, man hegen ve lu keren gbenda u vea haa ikyagh ki tswamegh ki i yer ér whale-lance la inya yô. Ior kpishi mba a ikyav mbi tswamev er Uwo u Green Mountains ape ve dugh la nahan. Ken akaa agen yô u hen wer ka ahwa kpuaa tseegh. Nenge ase heen! chap la ngu eren tom sha ityough ki ikyônough ki torough. A hua ikyondo i beaver man ikyondo i cir sha ityou i i yer ér swallow-tailed la, shi a kange ikyondo i i yer ér sailor-belt la ken iyol na, kua ikyagh ki tswamegh ki i yer ér sheath-knife la. Ka kwagh ugen u a lu a sou’-wester man ikyondo i bombazine je ne.

Dandy u i mar un ken gar môm kpa una kar sha u i mar un ken agar ga—m ngu ôron kwagh u dandy u bumpkin u a lu jighilii yô—or u, sha ayange a dog-days la, nana cir ikyav mbi nan mbi uhar mbi i yer ér buckskin gloves la sha ci u mciem ma tan ave a nan. Hegen yô, zum u or u ken tar u dandy er ngun nahan nan tôô ken ityou ér nana zua a iti i dedoo, shi nana nyôr nongo u vesen u kôron ishu i i yer ér whale la yô, doo u ú nenge a akaa a doon tsung a nan eren zum u nan za nyôr hen kpezegemnger yô. Er lu ôron kwagh u ikyondo na i ken zegemnger la yô, a kaa ér i wa un bell-buttons sha akondo a na a ken ityou; ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i er sha canvas la. Ah, Hay-Seed u a lu icankwase la! er ikyav mbi tswamev mbin mbi vihi kpishi ken ahumbe a hiihii a a lu yeghen la, zum u i zenda we, ikyav mbi tswamev man ikyav mbi tswamev man akaa cii, sha ityough ki ahumbe a taver la yô.

Kpa de henen wer gar u i fe un tsembelee ne ngu a mbautyaav man mbayan ior man mbaaferev mba tesen mbavannya nav tseegh ga. Venda. Kpa New Bedford ka ijiir i ior ve lu her yô. Luun er se mba se lu mbakôronishu mba i yer ér whale la ga yô, alaghga tsô, tar u u lu hen kpezegemnger u Labrador la ma u lu ken mlu u vihin tsung nyian. Er i lu nahan, avegher a tar na u ken ijime la kuma u nan or mciem iyol, ve lu er ka ikyav nahan. Alaghga gar ne iyol na ka ijiir i ior ve hembe soon u lun her yô, ken tar u New England cii.
