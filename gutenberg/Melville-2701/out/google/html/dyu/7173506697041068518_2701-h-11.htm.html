An tun ye min kofɔ, o ye ko: fɔɲɔ ni baji tun be sanfiɲɛw yira. Nka hali n’o tun tɛ, n’a sɔrɔ a tun tɛna don kurun kɔnɔ—n’an be kiti tigɛ a ka kɛwalew fɛ o kɔ o wagati ɲɔgɔnw na—n’a tun kɛra ten, foli kɛcogo fɛ, a ye ɲiningali min kɛ, a tun ye jaabili jugu sɔrɔ o la. Sabu, i n’a fɔ a labanna ka kɛ cogo min na, a tun t’a janto a kana jɛn ni kapitɛn lonan si ye, hali miniti duuru kɔnɔ, fɔɔ n’a tun be se ka kunnafoni dɔw di a tun be minw ɲinina kosɔbɛ. Nka nin bɛɛ tun be se ka to k’a jati ka ɲɛ, n’a tun ma fɔ yan balɛnminɛ kurunw ka baara kɛrɛnkɛrɛnninw koo la n’u be ɲɔgɔn kunbɛn jamana wɛrɛw ka bajiw la, ani sanko kurunboliyɔrɔ kelen na.

Ni lonan fila be Pine Barrens tigɛra Niyɔriki mara la, wala Salisbury Plain min lakolon lo o cogo kelen na Angletɛri mara la; ni ɲɔgɔn kunbɛnna o kongokolonw kɔnɔ minw tɛ mɔgɔw bisimila, o mɔgɔ fila, u ka ɲɛnamaya kama, u tɛ se ka ɲɔgɔn foli bali; ani ka lɔ wagati dɔɔnin ka kibaroyaw falen ɲɔgɔn na; ani, n’a sɔrɔ, ka sigi wagati dɔɔni ka lafiɲɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ: o tuma na, a ka ɲi ka kɛ ko baji ka Pine Barrens ani Salisbury Plains dantanw kan, balɛn minɛ kurun fila be ɲɔgɔn lɔgɔbɔra dugukolo labanw na—Fanning’s Island kelenpe gɛrɛfɛ, wala King’s Mills yɔrɔjan na; n’ b’a fɔ ko a ka ɲi ka kɛ ten, o cogo la, o kurunw man kan ka sanbɛrɛw dɔrɔn falen ɲɔgɔn na, nka u ka kan ka ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ tugun, ka kɛ teriw ye ani ka ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ. Ani kɛrɛnkɛrɛnniya la, o tun bena kɛ i n’a fɔ koo min ka ɲi ka kɛ, kurun minw be baji daa la, ani u ka kapitɛnw, u ka sɔrɔdasiw ani cɛɛw damadɔ tɛ ɲɔgɔn lɔn; ani o kama, ka sokɔnɔfɛn ɲuman sifa bɛɛ sɔrɔ ka kuma u kan.

Kurun min tɛ yen kabini wagatijan, n’a sɔrɔ kɛnɛma dancɛ be ni lɛtɛrɛw ye kurun kɔnɔ; a mana kɛ cogo o cogo, a bena la a la ko a bena a to sɛbɛ dɔw be sɔrɔ a ka dosiyew kan minw be yɛlɛmayɛlɛma ani u bolokunnandenw be n’u bolokunnandenw ye. Ani, o bonya sara la, kurun min be taga kɛnɛma, o tun bena balɛniw ka lɔnniya kura sɔrɔ ka bɔ kurun boliyɔrɔ la, a be se ka taga yɔrɔ min na, o min kɔrɔtanin lo a fɛ ka tɛmɛ fɛɛn bɛɛ kan. Ani, o bɛɛ bena kɛ tiɲɛn ye balɛni kurunw koo la minw be ɲɔgɔn ka sira tigɛ kurun boliyɔrɔ yɛrɛ la, hali n’u be wagatijan kɛra u ka soo. Sabu n’a sɔrɔ u dɔ ye lɛtɛrɛw ci ka bɔ kurun sabanan dɔ fɛ, ani sisan, kurun dɔ be yɔrɔjan; ani o lɛtɛrɛ dɔw be se ka kɛ kurun mɔgɔw ta ye, a be kurun min kunbɛnna sisan. Ka fara o kan, u tun be balɛni kibaroya fɔ ɲɔgɔn ye, ani ka baro kɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ cogo bɛnnin na. Sabu u tun tɛna kurunbolilaw ka hinɛ dɔrɔn lo sɔrɔ, nka u tun bena ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ fana ni ɲɔgɔnye kɛrɛnkɛrɛnninw ye minw be bɔ ɲɔgɔn ka baara la ani ɲɔgɔn ka dɛsɛw n’u ka faratiw la.

Jamana danfara fana tɛna danfara nafaman foyi lase mɔgɔ ma; o kɔrɔ, n’a y’a sɔrɔ u fila bɛɛ be kaan kelen fɔ, i n’a fɔ Amerikikaw ni Angilɛkanw. Hali ni, ka tiɲɛ fɔ, ka bɔ Angilɛw ka balɛnminɛlaw hakɛ dɔgɔyali la, o lajɛnw tɛ kɛ tuma caaman na, ani n’u kɛra, a ka ɲi kojugu ka maloya sugu dɔ kɛ u ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ; sabu i ka Angilɛkan ye mɔgɔ ye min be a yɛrɛ minɛ, ani i ka Yanke, a t’o ɲɔgɔn fɛ mɔgɔ si la fɔɔ ale yɛrɛ. Ka fara o kan, Angilɛw ka balɛnminɛlaw be nɔɔ to tuma dɔw la dugubaw ka fanga sugu dɔ kan Ameriki balɛnminɛlaw kan; ka ɲɛsin Nantucketer ma, min ka jan ani a ka ɲi, n’a ka marabolow ye minw tɛ se ka ɲɛfɔ, i n’a fɔ baji sɛnɛkɛla sugu dɔ. Nka o fanga min be Angilɛw ka balɛniw la, o be sɔrɔ yɔrɔ min na tiɲɛn na, a tun bena gwɛlɛya k’a fɔ, k’a ye ko Yankees ye tile kelen kɔnɔ, n’u bɛɛ lajɛnna, u be balɛni caaman faga ka tɛmɛ Angilɛw bɛɛ kan, n’u bɛɛ lajɛnna, saan tan kɔnɔ. Nka nin ye fiɲɛ fitinin ye min tɛ mɔgɔ tɔɔrɔ Angilɛw ka balɛni ɲinilaw fɛ, Nantucketer tɛ min jati kosɔbɛ; n’a sɔrɔ, sabu a b’a lɔn ko fiɲɛ damanin b’ale yɛrɛ la.

O kama, an b’a ye ko kurunw bɛɛ cɛma minw be taga baji kan u kelen kelen, kuun caaman b’a la ka jɛnɲɔgɔnya kɛ ni balɛnw ye ani o lo. Hali ni jagokɛla kurun dɔw be ɲɔgɔn tigɛra Atlantik baji cɛmancɛ la, tuma caaman na, u bena tɛmɛ k’a sɔrɔ u ma ɲɔgɔn lɔn, u be ɲɔgɔn tigɛ bajibaw kan i n’a fɔ danni fila Broadway la; ani wagati bɛɛ la, n’a sɔrɔ u be ɲɔgɔn kɔrɔfɔ cogo dan na. Kɛlɛdenw ta fan fɛ, n’u y’a sɔrɔ ka ɲɔgɔn kunbɛn baji kan, u be tɛmɛ fɔlɔ ka yɛlɛ ani ka ɲɔgɔn gosi ɲɔgɔn na, i n’a fɔ ka darapow gosi ɲɔgɔn na, fɔɔ a be komi ɲumanya ni balimaya kanuya tɛ yen fewu. As touching Slave-ships meeting, mun na, u be teliya la ten, u be boli ɲɔgɔn fɛ joona joona. Nka, Ala batobaga, mɔgɔ hɔɔrɔn, min tɛ mɔgɔ yɛrɛ yira, min be lonanw bisimila, min be mɔgɔw minɛ, min be hɔɔrɔnya ani min be nɔgɔya la, ale filɛ! Balɛni be mun lo kɛ n’a be balɛni wɛrɛ kunbɛn wagati ɲuman sifa o sifa la? “ Gam ” b’a bolo, kurun tɔɔw bɛɛ t’o fɛɛn lɔn pewu fɔɔ u yɛrɛ ma deli k’o tɔgɔ mɛn; ani n’u y’o mɛn, u be yɛlɛmisɛn dɔrɔn lo kɛ, ani ka segi tulonkɛfɛnw kan “spouters” ani “blubber-boilers” koo la, ani o ɲɔgɔnna kuma cɛɲumanw koo la. Mun na Jagokɛla-kuruntigiw bɛɛ, ani fana Piratiw ni Kɛlɛdenw bɛɛ, ani Jɔɔn-kuruntigiw bɛɛ, o be o ɲɔgɔnna yɛlɛko dusukunnakow jati Balɛn-kurunw koo la; nin ye ɲiningali ye min jaabili tun bena gwɛlɛya. Ani ka fara o kan, ni mɔgɔ dɔ kɔrɔtara o cogo kabakoman na, jusigilan ɲuman t’a fɛ a ka sanfɛla la. O kama, n’ b’a kuncɛ ko n’a b’a yɛrɛ bonya k’a fɔ ko a kɔrɔtara ka tɛmɛ balɛni kan, o kuma na, basigi barikaman tɛ jɛgɛminɛla fɛ a ka se ka lɔ min kan.

Nka, Gam ye mun lo ye? I be se k’i bolodennin sɛgɛ ka boli ka taga sanfɛ ani duguma daɲɛgafew ka yɔrɔw la, ani i t’o daɲɛ sɔrɔ abada. Dɔgɔtɔrɔ Johnson ma se o lɔnniya ma abada; Nuhun Webster ka kurun t’a minɛ. O bɛɛ n’a ta, o kumaden kelen min b’a yira ko mɔgɔw be min fɔra, o be to ka fɔ sisan kabi saan caaman Yankee mɔgɔ waa tan ni duuru ɲɔgɔn cɛma minw wolola tiɲɛn na.
