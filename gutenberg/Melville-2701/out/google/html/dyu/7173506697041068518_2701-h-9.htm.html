O cogo la, an tun be fani donna ani ka fani don ka taga yɔrɔ wɛrɛ tuma min na n’ y’a daminɛ ka mankan dɔ mɛn min tun be mɔgɔ kabakoya, min tun be mɔgɔw sama ka taga, ani min tun be dɔnkiliw la ani a tun tɛ dugukolo ta ye, fɔɔ hɔɔrɔnya bolokuru bɔra n’ bolo la, ani n’ lɔra ka sankabaw filɛ, o mankan bɔra yɔrɔ min na i n’a fɔ kaman. O Gay-Header fatɔ, Tashtego tun be sanfɛ kuruwa yiriw cɛma. A farikolo tun be yɛlɛla ka taga ɲɛfɛ ni kɔnɔgwɛ ye, a bolo tun be yɛlɛla i n’a fɔ bere ani wagati dɔɔni dɔɔni, a tun be to ka kasi. Tiɲɛn na, o mankan kelen lo tun be mɛnna o wagati yɛrɛ la, n’a sɔrɔ o tun be mɛnna bajiw bɛɛ la, ka bɔ balɛni kɛmɛ caaman ka filɛrikɛyɔrɔw la minw tun siginin be sanfɛ i n’a fɔ sanfɛ; nka o fɔgɔfɔgɔw damadɔw lo tun be se ka o kulekan kɔrɔ min degira, o tun be se ka mankan kabakoman bɔ i n’a fɔ Tashtego min bɔra Ɛndi jamana na.

Komi a tun lɔnin be yɛlɛla i kunfɛ a tilancɛ yɛlɛnin fiɲɛ na, a tun be filɛli kɛra sanfɛ ni kɔnɔgwɛ ye, i tun bena a miiri ko a ye kira wala yelikɛla dɔ ye min be Desizɔn ka sumaw filɛra, ani o kongokolon kulekanw fɛ minw b’u ka nali kofɔra.

“A be fiyɛra! a be fiyɛra! a be fiyɛra!”

“Yɔrɔ-min?”

“Lee-beam kan, kilomɛtɛrɛ fila ɲɔgɔn yɔrɔ la! U ka lakɔli!”

O yɔrɔnin bɛɛ, mankanba kɛra.

Baleine sperme be fiyɛ i n’a fɔ lɛrɛ be fiyɛ cogo min na, ani a be fiyɛ cogo kelen na min tɛ yɛlɛma ani min be se ka la a la. Ani, o cogo la, balɛnw be jɛgɛ nin danfara ka bɔ a bɔɲɔgɔnko kabila tɔɔw la.

“O yɔrɔ la, mɔgɔw be taga!” sisan, o tun ye kulekan ye min bɔra Tasitego; ani balɛnw tununa.

“Teliya, baarakɛla!” Akabu kulera. “Wagati! Wagati!”

Dough-Boy teliyara ka jigi duguma, ka lɛrɛ filɛ ani ka miniti tigitigi fɔ Akabu ye.

Sisan, kurun tun be yɔrɔjan fɔɲɔ ma ani a tun be tagama dɔɔni dɔɔni a ɲɛfɛ. Tashtego y’a fɔ ko baleniw jigira ka taga fɔɲɔ fan fɛ, an jigi tun b’a la k’u filɛ tugun ka kɔn an ka kala ɲɛ. O baara kelenpe min b’a yira tuma dɔw la, n’a be mankan kɛra n’a kuun ye fan kelen fɛ, o bɛɛ n’a ta, k’a to a dogonin be ji jukɔrɔ, a be mansinw yɛlɛma, ani a be jigi joona joona yɔrɔ wɛrɛ la—a ka nanbara tun tɛ se ka kɛ baara la sisan; sabu kuun tun tɛ yen k’a miiri ko Tasitego ka jɛgɛ min ye, o tun siranna cogo si ra, walama ko a tun b’an ka lamini lɔn pewu. Cɛɛ minw sugandira ka kɛ kurunw marabagaw ye, o kɔrɔ, minw ma sigi kurunw kɔnɔ, olu dɔ tun be Ɛndicɛ lafiya o wagati la kurun kunba kun na. Kurunbolilaw minw tun bɛ ɲafɛ ani mizzen, o tun jigira; lɔgɔw tun siginin bɛ o yɔrɔw ra; kurunw tun bɛ bɔra; luuba tun be kɔfɛ, kurun saba nunu tun be yɛlɛmayɛlɛma baji sanfɛ i n’a fɔ sampiri bɔgɔdaga saba farakurunw kan. O boonw kɔfɛ, u ka baarakɛlaw minw tun be ni kɔnɔgwɛ ye, olu tun be nɔrɔnin lo tɛrɛn na ni bolo kelen ye, k’a sɔrɔ u seen kelen tun be marifaw kan ni jigiya ye. O kama, kɛlɛkɛlaw ka kɛlɛdenw ka lɔgɔ jan filɛ u be ɲini ka fili juguw ka kurun kɔnɔ.

Nka o wagati gwɛlɛ nin na, kulekan dɔ barila ka mɛn min ye bɛɛ ɲɛɛ bɔ baleine kan. A y’a daminɛ ka Akabu filɛ ni ɲɛɛ gwansan ye, min tun lamininin lo ni jina duuru ye minw tun be komi u bɔra fiɲɛ na.
