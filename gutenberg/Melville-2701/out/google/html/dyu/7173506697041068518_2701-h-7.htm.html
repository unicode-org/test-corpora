O sultaniya dɔ min tun b’a kunsɛmɛ na, min tun be yen cogo wɛrɛ la, o ma bɔ kɛnɛ kan; o cogoyaw fɛ, o sultanism kelen kɛra farisogo ye dictature kɔnɔ min tɛ se ka kɛlɛ. Sabu ka kɛ mɔgɔ ka hakilimaya fanga ye min b’a fɛ, a tɛ se ka kɛ abada ka fanga sɔrɔ, min be sɔrɔ mɔgɔ tɔw kan, n’a ma dɛmɛ sɔrɔ kɛnɛma seko dɔw la ani u ka fanga, tuma bɛɛ, u yɛrɛ kɔnɔ, minw ka dɔgɔ wala minw ka dɔgɔ ani minw ka dɔgɔ. O ye nin ye, min bɛ Ala ta masaya kuntigiw tanga abada ka bɔ dunuɲa mɔgɔw ma; ani a be bonyabaw to yen, nin fiɲɛ be se ka minw di, o cɛɛw ma minw be tɔgɔba sɔrɔ u ka dɔgɔya dantan fɛ ka ɲɛsin Alako Inert bololafɛn dogonin sugandili ma, ka tɛmɛ u ka fanga kan siga t’a la ka tɛmɛ jama hakɛ salenw kan. O jogo ɲumanba be dogo o koo misɛnninw na ni politikikow ka lannakow juguw b’u minɛna, fɔɔ masacɛw ka koo dɔw la, hali u ye fanga di mɔgɔ hakilitanw ma. Nka tuma min na, i n’a fɔ Nikolasi masacɛ ka koo la, dugukolo masaya masafugula min be ni gɛrɛnw ye, o be masaya kunsɛmɛ dɔ lamini; o kɔ, plebeyi ka sagakuluw be kunbiri gwan ka kɔn centralizasiyɔnba ɲɛ. Ani fana, o dramukɛla min tun bena sayabaliya yira a ka yɛlɛmaniw n’a ka yɛlɛmaniw na, ale tɛna ɲinɛ miiriya dɔ kɔ abada, min kɔrɔtanin lo kosɔbɛ a ka seko la, i n’a fɔ min kofɔra sisan.

Nka Akabu, ne ka Kapitɛni, ale bele be tagamana ne ɲa kɔrɔ a ka Nantucket nɔgɔya n’a ka nɔgɔya bɛɛ la; ani nin yɔrɔ min bɛ maga Masacɛw ni Masacɛw ma, ne man kan ka dogo ko ne bɛ baara kɛra ni baleni ɲininikɛla kɔrɔ fantan dɔ dɔrɔn le ye i n’a fɔ ale; ani, o la, kɛnɛmafɛnw ni boonw bɛɛ banna ne la. Ani, n’a tun tɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ o koo la, n’a tun tɛ taga ni hop-skip-and-jump nimble ye, o tuma na, Tashtego tun be ni fɛɛrɛ dɔ ye min tɛ mɔgɔɲuman ye k’a teliya k’a kɔfɛla gosi, i n’a fɔ nɛgɛkunbɛnnan. Ani siɲɛ kelen, Daggoo, min minɛna ni yɛlɛko barikaman ye, a ye Dough-Boy hakili dɛmɛ k’a minɛ farikolo la, k’a kuun don yiribolo lakolonba dɔ kɔnɔ, k’a sɔrɔ Tashtego, muru b’a bolo, y’a daminɛ ka koori labɛn ka kɔn k’a kungolo gosi. A tun ye mɔgɔ fitinin dɔ ye min tun be siranna kosɔbɛ, a tun be yɛrɛyɛrɛra, o baarakɛla min ɲɛda tun be i n’a fɔ buru; buru dilanbaga dɔ min banna ani dɔgɔtɔrɔso dɔgɔtɔrɔmuso dɔ ka bɔnsɔnw. Ani, ni Akabu min tun be mɔgɔ farimanw ye, ale ka yɛlɛli min tun be kɛra tuma o tuma, ani o kongofɛn saba nunu ka viziti minw tun be kɛ tuma o tuma, Dough-Boy ka ɲɛnamaya bɛɛ tun ye dakunw yɛlɛli ye tuma bɛɛ. Tuma caaman na, n’a y’a ye ko nɛgɛsotigiw be ni fɛɛn o fɛɛn ye u be min ɲini, a tun be bɔ u bolo ka don a ka dumunikɛyɔrɔ fitinin kɔnɔ min tun be gɛrɛfɛ, ani a tun be siranna k’u filɛ a ka daa fiyentɔw fɛ fɔɔ ka taga se o bɛɛ ma.

O tun ye yelifɛn ye ka Queequeg ye a siginin be Tashtego ɲɛfɛ, a b’a ka ɲinw fileninw kɛlɛla ni Ɛndikaw ta ye: kuruwa fɛ u fɛ, Daggoo siginin be duguman, bari sigilan dɔ tun bena a kungolo min be i n’a fɔ sufɛwotoro, o lase karlini dugumaninw ma; a bolow ni senw belebelebaw tagamasiɲɛ kelen kelen bɛɛ la, o b’a to kabini ka boon dugumayɔrɔ be yɛrɛyɛrɛ, i n’a fɔ farafin ka sama be taga kurun kɔnɔ cogo min na. Nka o bɛɛ n’a ta, negro belebele tun be a yɛrɛ minɛ cogo kabakoman na, n’a sɔrɔ a tun be dainty fɔ. A tun be komi a tun tɛ se ka kɛ ko n’o dakun fitininw ye, a tun be se ka to ka ɲɛnamaya kɛ mɔgɔba, baroni ani mɔgɔba dɔ fɛ. Nka, siga t’a la, o kongosogo ɲuman tun be balo kosɔbɛ ani ka fiɲɛ caaman min ka caya; ani a nunwow fɛ, a tun bɛ nɔgɔ diɲɛw ka ɲanamaya sanuman na. Misi sogo wala buru tɛ, mɔgɔbaw be dilan wala k’u balo. Nka Queequeg, a tun be ni dawolo gosicogo dɔ ye min be mɔgɔ faga, min be mɔgɔ faga dumuni na—o mankan jugu lo—fo Dough-Boy min tun be yɛrɛyɛrɛra, ale tun be ɲini ka filɛli kɛ k’a filɛ ni ɲin tagamasiɲɛ dɔw be dogora a yɛrɛ bolo fitininw na. Ani, tuma min na a tun be Tashtego ka dɔnkili lamɛn walisa a k’a yɛrɛ bɔ, walisa u k’a kolow tigɛ, o baarakɛla hakilitan tun be minan minw tun sirinin be a lamini na dumunikɛyɔrɔ la, u tun b’u bɛɛ cici, a ka kirin barikamanw fɛ. Kabakuru min tun be sɔgɔlanw ni kɛlɛkɛminan wɛrɛw kɛ, o fana tun tɛ o ta bɔrɔw kɔnɔ; ani o kabakuru sɔgɔlanw le ra, surafana dumuni tuma na, o tun bɛ o ta muruw sɔgɔ ni yɛrɛyira ye; o mankan gɛrɛntɛ tun tɛ se ka Dough-Boy fantan hakili sigi fewu. A tun be se ka ɲinɛ cogo di ko a ka wagati la, Queequeg, a ka ɲi ka kɛ mɔgɔfagako dɔw ye minw be mɔgɔ faga ani u be mɔgɔw lasun u ka ɲɔgɔn minɛ. Ayiwa! Nka wagati bɛnnin na, min tun b’a ninsɔndiya kosɔbɛ, kɔgɔji kɛlɛkɛla saba nunu tun be wuli ka taga; a tulo minw be la a la ani u be nsiirinw fɔ, u ka kɛlɛbolo kolow bɛɛ be pɛrɛnna u kɔnɔ u ka tagamayɔrɔ bɛɛ la, i n’a fɔ Maurikanw ka sɔgɔlanw minw be nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw na.

Nka, hali ni o barikaw tun be dumuni kɛ boon kɔnɔ, ani u tun be sigi yen tɔgɔ la; o bɛɛ n’a ta, k’a sɔrɔ u tun tɛ sigiyɔrɔma dɔrɔn lo kɛ u ka delinankow la, u tun man teli ka don a kɔnɔ fɔɔ dumuni wagati, ani sanni sinɔgɔ wagati ka se, n’u tun be tɛmɛ a kɔnɔ ka taga u yɛrɛ ka yɔrɔw la.

O koo kelen na, a tun be komi Akabu tɛ mɔgɔ wɛrɛ ye Ameriki balɛnw ka kapitɛniw fanba fɛ, olu minw, i n’a fɔ jɛnkulu dɔ, u b’a miiri ko joow fɛ, kurun kɔnɔyɔrɔ ye u ta ye; ani ko bonya dɔrɔn lo b’a to mɔgɔ wɛrɛ be se ka don yen, wagati o wagati. O la, tiɲɛn na, a ka ɲi ka fɔ ko Pequod ka kurun ɲɔgɔnw n’a ka nɛgɛsotigiw tun be kurun kɔfɛ ka tɛmɛ kurun kɔnɔ. Sabu ni o donna a kɔnɔ, a tun bɛ i n’a fɔ sirada bɛ don bon kɔnɔ cogo min na; ka yɛlɛma kɔnɔna na wagati dɔɔnin, ka yɛlɛma ka bɔ kɛnɛma loon wɛrɛ; ani, i n’a fɔ fɛɛn min be to yen tuma bɛɛ, a be sigi kɛnɛma. O fana ma bɔnɛ fɛn caman na nin ko ra; kabini kɔnɔ, jɛnɲɔgɔnya tun tɛ yen; sigida la, Akabu tun tɛ se ka sɔrɔ. Hali n’a tɔgɔ tun sɛbɛra kerecɛnw ka jatebɔ la, a tun ye lonan ye hali bi. A tun be duniɲa kɔnɔ, i n’a fɔ Grisly Bears laban tun be Misuri mara la min siginin lo. ƝƐFƆLI 35. Masi-kun.

O wagati la, wagati tun ka di kosɔbɛ, n’ tun be yɛlɛmayɛlɛma cogo min na n’ ka kurunbolila tɔɔw ye, n’ ka kurun kungolo fɔlɔ nana yɛlɛma.

Ameriki ka balɛnw fanba la, kurun be bɔ a ka kurun lɔyɔrɔ la tuma min na, u be mɔgɔw ladon kurun kungolo la; hali ni kilomɛtɛrɛ waa tan ni duuru, ani minw ka ca n’o ye, a ka ɲi ka kurun boli sanni a ka se a ka kurun boliyɔrɔ la. Ani, ni saan saba, naani wala duuru kurun boli kɔ, a be surunyana a ka soo ni fɛɛn lakolon dɔ ye a kɔnɔ—i n’a fɔ, hali fiyɛ lankolon—o tuma na, a ka kurunw kuunw be to ni mɔgɔ ye fɔɔ ka taga se a laban ma; ani fɔɔ a ka sankolola kurunw be don kurun lɔyɔrɔ sanfɛlaw cɛma, a tɛ jigiya dabila pewu ko a bena balɛni kelen wɛrɛ minɛ.

Sisan, komi nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw lɔli baara, baji daa la wala jii kan, o ye koo kɔrɔman ye ani a ka di mɔgɔw ye, an k’o ɲɛfɔ yan cogo dɔ la. Ne b’a miiri ko mɔgɔ fɔlɔ minw tun bɛ kurun kunkolow lɔ, o tun ye Misirankakɔrɔw le ye; sabu, ne ta ɲininiw bɛɛ ra, ne ma foyi sɔrɔ ka kɔn o ɲɛ. Sabu hali ni o bɛnbaw, minw ye Babɛli dugu lɔbagaw ye, siga t’a ra, o ka sankaso fɛ, o tun b’a fɛ ka kurun kungolo min ka bon ni kurun tɔw bɛɛ ye Azi mara bɛɛ ra, walama Farafinna fana; o bɛɛ n’a ta (sanni u ka kamiyɔn laban bila a kan) i n’a fɔ o ka o kabakurukuruba min tun be yen, a be se ka fɔ ko a tɛmɛna yirikuru fɛ, Ala ka dimi fɔɲɔ jugu kɔnɔ; o kosɔn, an tɛ se ka o Babɛli lɔbagaw jate Misirankaw ye. Ani ko Ezipitikaw tun ye jamana ye min tun be lɔ nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw kan, o ye kuma ye min basiginin lo fɛnkɔrɔw sɛgɛsɛgɛbagaw ka lannaya kan, ko piramidi fɔlɔw lɔra sankololafɛnw sɛgɛsɛgɛri kama: miiriya min dɛmɛna kelen kelen ni boonw faan naani bɛɛ ka yɛlɛyɛlɛnan kɛrɛnkɛrɛnninw ye; o min fɛ, o sankololafɛnw lɔnbaga kɔrɔw tun delila ka yɛlɛ ka taga sanfɛ, ka dɔnkili la lolo kuraw ye; hali i n’a fɔ bi kurun ka filɛlikɛlaw be dɔnkili la cogo min na ka kurun boli, wala baleine min be mɔgɔw ɲɛɛ na dɔrɔn. Saint Stylites dugu la, kerecɛn tɔgɔtigi min tun be a yɛrɛ latanga, ale min ye kabakurun sanfɛla dɔ lɔ a ye kongokolon kɔnɔ ani a y’a ka ɲɛnamaya laban bɛɛ kɛ a sanfɛ, k’a ka dumuni wuli duguma ni minan dɔ ye; ale kɔnɔ, an be misaliya kabakoman dɔ sɔrɔ min b’a yira ko mɔgɔ min be lɔ nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw kan, a tɛ siran; min tun tɛna gwɛn ka bɔ a sigiyɔrɔ ra, bugun ni nɛnɛ, sanji, sanbɛrɛ, ani sanbɛrɛ; nka a ye jagwɛlɛya kɛ ka koo bɛɛ kunbɛn fɔɔ ka taga se a laban ma, a sara tiɲɛn tiɲɛn na a ka baara la. Bi bi la, mɔgɔw minw be kurunw lɔ, an be ni boon dɔ dɔrɔn lo ye min tɛ niin na; mɔgɔ gwansanw, ani nɛgɛw, ani siranɛgɛw; minw, hali n’u be se ka fɔɲɔba kunbɛn koɲuman, u bele tɛ se ka dɔnkili la pewu n’u ye fɛɛn kabakoman dɔ ye. Napoleon bɛ yen; min, Vendome ka boon sanfɛ, a lɔnin be a bolow nɔrɔnin ɲɔgɔn na, a be se mɛtɛrɛ kɛmɛ ni biduuru ɲɔgɔn ma fiɲɛ na; careless, sisan, min bɛ dekiw mara duguma; a ka kɛ Luwi Filipu, Luwi Bilan wala Luwi Sutana ye. Washingtɔnba fana lɔnin be sanfɛ a ka kurunba sanfɛ Baltimore dugu la ani i n’a fɔ Ɛrikili ka boonba dɔ, a ka boon be adamadenw ka bonya yɔrɔ yira, mɔgɔ satɔ damanin lo tɛna tɛmɛ o kan. Amiral Nelson fana, marifa-nɛgɛ ka kapitan dɔ kan, a lɔnin be a ka kurun kungolo kan Trafalgar kɛnɛba kan; ani tuma o tuma ni o Lɔnduru sisi be dibi ka tɛmɛ, tagamasiɲɛ be di hali bi ko cɛsiribaga dogonin dɔ be yen; sabu sisi bɛ yɔrɔ min na, tasuma ka kan ka kɛ o yɔrɔ le ra.» Nka Washingtɔn belebele, Napoleon, Nelson, u tɛna sanbɛrɛ kelen jaabi ka bɔ duguma, hali n’u y’u weele cogo o cogo ka kɛ teriw ye u ka ladilikanw fɛ, u be kurun minw filɛra, u hakili ɲagaminin lo; o bɛɛ n’a ta, a be se ka miiri ko u ka hakili be don siniɲasigi bugunba kɔnɔ, ani ka jikuruw ni farakurunw kɔrɔfɔ minw ka kan ka bɔ yen.

A be se ka kɛ i n’a fɔ a man ɲi ka dugukolo kan kurunw kuntigiw ni baji kan mɔgɔw fara ɲɔgɔn kan cogo si la; nka ko tiɲɛn na, o tɛ ten, o b’a yira ka gwɛ fɛɛn dɔ fɛ min koo la, Obed Macy, min ye Nantucket ka tariki sɛbɛbaga kelenpe ye. Obɛdi min ka kan n’o ye, ale y’a fɔ an ye ko balɛnw minɛ wagati daminɛ na, sanni kurunw ka to ka taga jɛgɛ minɛ tuma o tuma, o gun mɔgɔw tun be kurun janw lɔ baji daa la, ani mɔgɔw tun be yɛlɛ yɔrɔ minw na ni nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw ye, i n’a fɔ kɔnɔw be yɛlɛ cogo min na. A saan damanin ye nin ye, Nuvɛli Zelandi ka Bay whalemen y’o labɛn kelen lo ta, olu minw y’o tulon mafiɲɛya tuma min na, u y’o lasɔmi kurun labɛnninw na minw be baji daa gɛrɛfɛ. Nka o landa kɛra fɛn kɔrɔ ye sisan; o kɔ, an be yɛlɛma ka taga kurun kungolo kelenpe la, o min ye balɛni kurun ye kɔgɔji kan. O kurun saba nunu be mara mɔgɔ la k’a ta tilebɔ tuma na ka taga a bila tileben tuma na; kurunbolilaw tun b’u ka yɛlɛmaniw kɛ tuma o tuma (i n’a fɔ kurun ɲɛminɛbaga), ani u tun be ɲɔgɔn lafiya lɛri fila o lɛri fila. Tilebɔyɔrɔw ka wagati sumanin na, a ka di kosɔbɛ mast-head ye; ayi, mɔgɔ min be miiri siko la, o ka di ale ye. I lɔnin be yen, i be se mɛtɛrɛ kɛmɛ ɲɔgɔn ma kurun sanfɛ, i be tagamana ka tɛmɛ kurun kɔnɔ, i n’a fɔ kurunw ye lɔgɔ belebelebaw ye, k’a sɔrɔ i jukɔrɔ ani i seenw cɛma, i n’a fɔ, baji ka fɛnɲɛnamabaw be yɛlɛla, i n’a fɔ kurunw tun be tagama cogo min na fɔlɔ la, Kolosi tɔgɔtigi ka sanbaraw cɛma Rodi kɔrɔ la. I lɔnin be yen, i tununa baji ka tugutugu ɲɔgɔn kɔ dantanw na, foyi tɛ yɛlɛmayɛlɛma fɔɔ baji jikuruw. Kurun min tun be yɛlɛla, o be yɛlɛma yɛlɛma; jago fiɲɛ minw bɛ sinɔgɔ, o bɛ fiyɛ; fɛɛn bɛɛ b’i lasun ka languor kɛ. A fanba la, nin tropique baleine ɲɛnamaya kɔnɔ, sublime uneventfulness b’i ladon; aw tɛ kibaroya si mɛn; aw kana gafew kalan; extras minw be mɔgɔ kabakoya yɔrɔ gwansanw koo la, olu t’i lafili abada ka ninsɔndiya gwansanw kɛ; aw tɛ sokɔnɔ tɔɔrɔ si mɛn; wari minw be banki kɔnɔ; sɔngɔw benni; u tɛ hami abada k’a miiri i bena min dumu surafana na—bari i ka dumuniw bɛɛ be mara saan saba kɔnɔ ani saan caaman kɔnɔ, u be mara ka ɲɛ bɔrɔw kɔnɔ ani i ka boon sara wari tɛ yɛlɛma.

O worodugu fan fɛ balɛnw dɔ la, saan saba wala naani tagama jan dɔ la, i n’a fɔ a ka teli ka kɛ cogo min na, i be lɛri sifa caaman minw kɛ kurun sanfɛ, u bɛɛ lajɛnnin tun bena kɛ kalo dafanin caaman ye. Ani, a ka ɲi ka nisɔngoya ko yɔrɔ min na i be wagati caaman kɛ i ka ɲɛnamaya kɔnɔ, o ka kan ka kɛ yɔrɔ min na, fɛɛn si tɛ yen min be se ka kɛ sigiyɔrɔ ɲuman ye, wala min be se ka kɛ sababu ye ka dusukunnakow lawuli, i n’a fɔ min be kɛ dilan kan, nɛgɛkunbɛnnan fitinin dɔ la, nɛgɛso fitinin dɔ la, nɛgɛso dɔ kan, wala nɛgɛso wɛrɛ la. ani fɛɛrɛ ɲumanw minw na cɛɛw b’u yɛrɛ mabɔ mɔgɔw la wagati dɔɔni kɔnɔ. I ka teli ka lɔ yɔrɔ min na ka tɛmɛ tɔɔw bɛɛ kan, o ye t’ gallant-mast kungolo ye, i be lɔ yɔrɔ min na yiri misɛnnin fila kan minw be ɲɔgɔn kunbɛn (minw be se ka kɛ balɛniw ta ye) ani u be weele ko t’ gallant cross-trees. Yan, baji b’a yɛrɛ yuguyugura minkɛ, mɔgɔ min be baara daminɛna, ale b’a yɛrɛ lafiya i n’a fɔ a be lɔra misi ka biɲɛw kan cogo min na. Tiɲɛn na, nɛnɛ wagati la, i be se k’i ka soo ta ka taga n’a ye sanfɛ, i n’a fɔ mɔnturu; nka ka kuma fɔ ka ɲa, kɔlɔsilifani min ka bon ni bɛɛ ye, o tɛ bon ye ka tɛmɛ farikolo kan min ma don; sabu i n’a fɔ niin nɔrɔnin lo a ka fanibugu sogoman kɔnɔ cogo min na, a tɛ se ka tagama a yɛrɛ ma a kɔnɔ, hali ka bɔ a kɔnɔ, k’a sɔrɔ a ma faratiba lase a ma ka tunu (i n’a fɔ mɔgɔ min tɛ foyi lɔn, min be Alipi kuluw tigɛra nɛnɛ wagati la); o kama, mɔnturufani tɛ bon ye i n’a fɔ anvelɔpu dɔrɔn, wala golo wɛrɛ min b’i lamini. I tɛ se ka etazi wala kɛsu don i fari la ani i tɛ se k’i ka mɔnturu fani marayɔrɔ ɲuman dilan tugun.

O bɛɛ la, a be mɔgɔ dusukasi kosɔbɛ k’a ye ko worodugu fan fɛ balɛni kurun ka nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw tɛ ni o buguso misɛnninw wala minbɛnw ye minw be mɔgɔ ɲɛɛ sama, u be weele ko crow’s-nests , minw kɔnɔ, Grɛnlandi balɛnw filɛyɔrɔw be latanga baji nɛnɛmanw ka wagati juguw ma. Kapitɛn Sleet ka tasuma gɛrɛfɛ maana kɔnɔ, min tɔgɔ ko “Tagama min be kɛ jikuruw cɛma, ka Grɛnlandi balɛn ɲini, ani cogo dɔ la, ka Grɛnlandi kɔrɔ ka Grɛnlandi mara tununinw sɔrɔ tugun;” nin gafe kabakoman kɔnɔ, mɔgɔ minw bɛɛ be kurun kunkolow lɔ, u be ni maana ɲuman ye min be kuma o wagati la , kongokolon ka boon min dilanna kɔsa la, min be weele ko Glacier, o min tun ye kapitɛn Sleet ka kurun ɲuman tɔgɔ ye. A y’a weele ko Sleet ka kongokolon , k’a yɛrɛ bonya; ale lo ye fɛɛn dilanbaga fɔlɔ ye ani a ka patenti tigi ye, ani a tɛ ngalonfɛn nɔgɔmanw bɛɛ la, ani a b’a miiri ko n’an b’an yɛrɛ deenw weele an yɛrɛ tɔgɔw la (anw facɛw lo ye fɛɛn dilanbaga fɔlɔw ye ani patenti tigiw ye), o cogo kelen na, an ka ɲi ka minan wɛrɛw wolo an yɛrɛ tɔgɔ la. A cogoya la, Sleet ka kongokolon be komi tierce wala tiyo belebele dɔ; nka, a dayɛlɛnin lo sanfɛ, yɔrɔ min na a be ni gɛrɛfɛla dɔ ye min be se ka yɛlɛma walisa fɔɲɔba dɔ be na i kuun na. Komi i lɔnin be kurun sanfɛla la, i be yɛlɛ ka don a kɔnɔ nɛgɛdaga fitinin dɔ fɛ min be duguma.
