Afata tomahawk yiwe ku meza, arayisuzuma mu kanya gatoyi, hanyuma ayifata ku muco, akanwa kiwe kari ku gifata, aca apfuka ibicu bininibinini vy’umwotsi w’itabi. Mu kanya gakurikira umuco urazima, uwo munyanyamaswa w’umunyamaswa, tomahawk hagati y’amenyo yiwe, aca asimbira mu gitanda nanje. Naririmbye n’ijwi rirenga, ntaco nari gushobora gukora ubu; kandi atanga ugutontomera gutunguranye kw’ugutangara aca atangura kunyumva.

Nariko ndavuga ikintu n’ijwi rirenga, ntazi ico ari co, ndamukubita ku ruhome, hanyuma ndamubwira, uwo yoba ari we wese canke uwo ari we wese, ngo acereze, maze reke mpaguruke nongere ncane itara. Ariko inyishu ziwe z’uruyeri zaramfashije ubwo nyene ku buryo atatahura neza insobanuro yanje.

“Ni nde-e akudebel?”—amaherezo aravuga—“nta co uvuga-e, dam-me, ndakwica.” Kandi kuvuga gutyo tomahawk yamuritswe yatanguye gutera imbere irya n’ino mu mwiza.

“Nyen’inzu, kubera Imana, Petero Coffin!” » Nca nsemerera. « Shebuja! Raba! Isandugu! Abamarayika! munkize!»

"Vuga-e! mbwira-ee uwo-ee ari, canke dam-me, ndice-e!" uwo munyanyama yongera araboroga, mu gihe ugutera imbere kwiwe guteye ubwoba kw’inyamaswa yitwa tomahawk kwari gushwiragiza ivu ry’itabi ry’agahomerabunwa ryinjije gushika niyumvira ko impuzu yanje y’ilino izosha. Ariko shimira ijuru, muri ako kanya nyen’inzu yinjira mu cumba afise umuco mu minwe, maze nsimbuka mvuye ku gitanda ndamwirukirako.

Yaciye yongera atwenga ati: “Ntutinye ubu, Queequeg hano ntiyogirira nabi umushatsi wo mu mutwe wawe.”

Nanje ndasemerera nti: “Reka gutwenga, kandi ni kuki utambwiye ko uwo munyabute w’umuriro w’iteka ari umunyaruyeri?”

“Nari niyumviriye ko mubizi;—sinababwiye, yari umucuruzi w’ibintu vy’agaciro mu gisagara?—ariko musubire muhindukire mugende musinzire. Queequeg, raba hano—uransabaye, jewe sabbee—wewe uyu mugabo aragusinzira—urasabbee?”

“Me sabbee vyinshi”——Queequeg aca aratontomera, afuha umuyoboro wiwe maze aca yicara mu gitanda.

Yongeyeko ati: “You gettee in,” aca ankoresha ikimenyetso ciwe citwa tomahawk, aca atera iyo mpuzu ku ruhande rumwe. Ivyo vy’ukuri ntiyabigira mu buryo bw’abanyagihugu gusa, ariko vy’ukuri mu buryo bw’ubugwaneza n’ubugiraneza. Nahagaze ndamubona umwanya mutoyi. Kubera ibicapo vyiwe vyose, muri rusangi yari umuntu arya abantu, asukuye kandi asa n’uwuteye igomwe. Ivyo vyose nariko ndavuga ni ibiki, ni ko nibwira mu mutima wanje—uwo mugabo ni umuntu nkanje: afise imvo nyinshi zo kuntinya, nk’uko nanje ntegerezwa kumutinya. Ni vyiza kuryamana n’umuntu arya abantu ataco yiyumvira kuruta umukristo aborewe.

Ndamubwira nti: "Nyen'inzu, mubwire ashireyo tomahawk yiwe, canke umuyoboro, canke uko wovyita kwose; umubwire areke kunywa itabi, muri make, nanje nzokwinjirana na we. Ariko sinkunda ko umuntu anywa itabi kumwe nanje. Birateye akaga. Ikirenze ivyo, nta n'ubwishingizi mfise."

Ivyo bibwiwe Queequeg, aca aravyubahiriza, yongera arampa ikimenyetso c’indero ngo nshire mu gitanda—azunguruka ku ruhande rumwe nk’aho yovuga ati—“Sinzokora ku kuguru kwanyu.”

Ndamubwira nti: “Ijoro ryiza nyen’inzu, urashobora kugenda.”

Narahindukiye, kandi sinigeze nsinzira neza nk’uko mu buzima bwanje.
