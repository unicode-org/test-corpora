Kandi na we nyene arabizi. igihe reka umuntu agwe muri we mu mutima mwiza, kandi ng’aho vyagumye amezi cumi n’abiri canke arenga; ushireho ukuboko kumwe kubera agateka ko guhamba ukundi mu buryo bubereye, kuki muri ivyo bihe ukuboko ari ukwawe gusa ureke iyo balene iguhe uwundi mugisha vuba, ivyo ni vyo gusa.”

"Oya, urakoze, Bunger," Kapiteni w'Umwongereza aravuga, "arakaze ku kuboko afise, kuko ntashobora kubifasha, kandi sinari ndamuzi ico gihe; ariko si ku wundi. Nta yindi baleine yera kuri jewe; naramumanuye rimwe, kandi ivyo vyaramfashije. Hazoba ubuninahazwa bwinshi bwo kumenya ko hariho umuzigo w'intanga mu kumwica, we, ariko, hark ye, ni mwiza cane mureke, ntimwiyumvira gutyo, Kapiteni?”—akaraba ukuguru kw’inzovu;

"Ni we. Ariko azoguma ahigwa, kubera ivyo vyose. Ni igiki ciza, ico kintu cavumwe si co cama ari co gikwegera. Ni umusumari! Haciye ryari umubonye ubwa nyuma? Ni iyihe nzira uja?"

Bunger aca asemerera ati: “Muhezagire umutima wanje, muvume n’uw’uwo mudayimoni mubi,” agendagenda akikuje Ahabu, kandi nk’imbwa, ariko arapfukama mu buryo butangaje; “amaraso y’uyu mugabo—zana igipimo c’ubushuhe!—ari ku rugero rwo guteka!—umutima wiwe utuma izo mbaho ​​zitera!—mushingantahe!”—akura icumu mu mufuko wiwe, aca yegera ukuboko kwa Ahabu.

“Avast!” Ahabu araboroga, amutera ku bihome—“Mushire ubwato, buja hehe?”

“Imana nziza!” » ni ko Kapiteni w’Umwongereza asemerera, uwo na we akabazwa ikibazo. "Ni igiki? Yariko aja mu buseruko, nibwira.—Mbega Kapiteni wawe arasaze?" » ni ko Fedallah amwongorera.

Ariko Fedallah, ashira urutoke ku munwa, aca anyerera hejuru y’ibihome kugira ngo afate umugozi w’ubwato, Ahabu na we, azunguza igikoresho co guca ubwato, ategeka abasare guhagarara aho kugira ngo bamanuke.

Mu kanya gatoyi yari ahagaze inyuma y’ubwato, abagabo b’i Manilla bariko barasimbuka baja ku maguru yabo. Kapiteni w’Umwongereza yaramushimagije ubusa. Ahabu asubira mu bwato bw’abanyamahanga, mu maso hiwe hakaba nk’ibuye ry’agaciro, ahagaze agororotse gushika iruhande y’ikiyaga Pequod.
