UMUGAMBI WA 41. Igitabo ca Mobi.

Jewe Ishimayeli nari umwe muri abo bagize ubwato; induru zanje zari zaduze n’izindi; indahiro yanje yari yashizwe hamwe n’iyabo; kandi nca nsemerera cane, nca nkomeza gutera inyundo no gukomeza indahiro yanje, kubera ubwoba bwari mu mutima wanje. Inyiyumvo y’agaterasoni, y’amayobera, y’impuhwe yari muri jewe; Ubucuti bwa Ahabu butagira aho bugarukira bwasa n’ubwanje. Namenye n’amatwi y’ukunzi amateka y’ico gikoko c’umwicanyi jewe n’abandi bose twari twarahiye ubukazi n’ukwihora.

Haciye igihe kanaka, naho nyene mu bihe bimwebimwe gusa, iyo Balene Yera itagira uwuyiherekeza kandi yari ukwayo, yari yarateye ubwoba ivyo biyaga bitagira ubutekerezi, ahanini abarovyi b’Ibalene Sperme bakunda kujayo. Ariko si bose bari bazi ko ariho; bakeyi gusa muri bo, mu kugereranya, bari bamubonye babizi; mu gihe igitigiri c’abari bamuhaye urugamba vy’ukuri kandi babizi, cari gito vy’ukuri. Kuko, kubera umubare munini w’ubwato bugendera ku mazi y’amabaleine; uburyo butagira urutonde bwari bwamijagijwe ku muzingi wose w’amazi, benshi muri bo bagasunika ubushakashatsi bwabo mu bice vy’uburebure bw’amazi, ku buryo ari gake canke ata na rimwe mu mezi cumi n’abiri yose canke arenga, kugira ngo bahure n’ubwato bumwe buvuga amakuru bw’ubwoko bwose; uburebure burengeje urugero bw’urugendo rumwe rumwe rutandukanye; ukudatunganye kw’ibihe vyo gutwara ubwato uvuye i muhira; ivyo vyose, hamwe n’ibindi bintu, bitaziguye n’ibitaziguye, vyaramaze igihe kirekire bibujije inkuru idasanzwe yerekeye Moby Dick gukwiragira mu bwato bwose bwo kuroba baleine kw’isi yose. Nta co vyari bimaze gukekeranya, ko ubwato butari buke bwavuzwe ko bwahuye, mu gihe nk’ico canke nk’ico, canke kuri meridien nk’iyo canke nk’iyo, n’Ikigereranyo c’Ikigereranyo c’ubunini n’ububi budasanzwe, ico gikoko, kimaze gukorera ububisha bukomeye abagiteye, cari carabacitse ku ruhande rwose; ku vyiyumviro bimwebimwe ntivyari ukwiyumvira kutari kwo, ndavuga, ko iyo balene ivugwa itegerezwa kuba ata yindi yari atari Moby Dick. Yamara rero, kuva mu gihe ca kera, uburovyi bw’ibikoko vyitwa Sperm Whale bwari bwaranzwe n’ingero zitandukanye kandi zidasanzwe z’ubukazi bukomeye, ubuhendanyi n’ububisha mu gikoko catewe; ni co gituma vyabaye, ko abo mu mpanuka ataco bazi bahaye intambara Moby Dick; mwene abo bahigi, kumbure, ahanini, bari babumbwa no gushingira ubwoba budasanzwe yavyaye, nk’aho bwari bushingiye ku bibazo vyo kuroba baleine y’intanga muri rusangi, kuruta ku mvo y’umuntu ku giti ciwe. Muri ubwo buryo, ahanini, iyo ntambara y’agahomerabunwa hagati ya Ahabu n’ico gikoko c’amazi yari yarabonwa n’abantu gushika ubu.

Kandi ku bijanye n’abo, mbere y’aho bumvise ivy’Ikinyamasyo c’Ikinyamasyo, bakamubona ku mpanuka; mu ntango y’ico kintu bari bafise umwe wese muri bo, hafi, nk’uko bamumanuye n’ubushizi bw’amanga kandi ata bwoba, nk’uko bimeze ku yindi baleine iyo ari yo yose y’ubwo bwoko. Ariko amaherezo, mwene ivyo vyago vyarabaye muri ivyo bitero—ntivyari bigarukiye ku gukomeretswa kw’amaboko n’amaguru, ku kuvunika amaguru canke ku guca ibihimba vy’umubiri bishobora kurya—ariko vyari vyica umuntu gushika ku rugero rwa nyuma rw’urupfu; ivyo bisubirwamwo vy’ivyago, vyose bikaba vyirundanira kandi bikarundanya ubwoba bwavyo kuri Moby Dick; ivyo bintu vyari vyagiye kure cane kugira ngo bihungabanye uburindutsi bw’abahigi benshi b’intwari, abo inkuru y’Ikinyamasyo c’Umweru yari yaje gushikako amaherezo.

Eka mbere n’ibihuha vy’ubukazi vy’ubwoko bwose ntivyananiwe kurenga urugero, kandi n’ubu birarushiriza gutera ubwoba amateka y’ukuri y’ivyo bitero vyica abantu. Kuko si ibihuha vy’agatangaza gusa bikura mu buryo busanzwe biva mu mubiri w’ibintu vyose biteye ubwoba bitangaje, — nk’uko igiti gikubitwa kivyara ibiherere ku mubiri waco; ariko, mu buzima bwo mu kiyaga, kuruta kure n’iyo mu buzima bwo ku butaka, ibihuha vy’agahomerabunwa biragwiriye, aho hose hari ukuri guhagije kugira ngo bifatanye. Kandi nk’uko ikiyaga kirenze igihugu muri ivyo, ni ko n’uburovyi bw’ibikoko vyitwa baleine burengeye ubundi bwoko bwose bw’ubuzima bwo mu kiyaga, mu bitangaje no mu buteye ubwoba bw’ibihuha rimwe na rimwe bigendagenda ng’aho. Kuko si aba baleine gusa nk’umubiri badasonerwa ubwo bujuju n’ukwemera ibinyoma birazwe n’abasare bose; ariko mu bagendera ubwato bose, ni bo bashobora guhura ata guca ku ruhande n’ikintu cose gitangaje biteye ubwoba kiri mu kiyaga; amaso mu yandi ntibabona gusa ibitangaza vyayo bikomeye kuruta ibindi vyose, ariko, ukuboko ku rwasaya, barabarwanya. Wenyene, mu mazi ari kure cane, ku buryo naho woba wagendeye mu bwato ibilometero igihumbi, ukarenga inkengera igihumbi, ntuzoshika ku buye ry’umuriro ry’umuriro ry’agaciro, canke ikintu na kimwe co kwakira abashitsi kiri munsi y’ico gice c’izuba; muri izo latitude na longitude, akurikirana cane mwene ukwo guhamagarwa nk’uko abigira, uwo munyabalene arazingirizwa n’inkurikizi zose zishaka gutuma ivyipfuzo vyiwe bivyara inda nyinshi z’ububasha.

Ntibitangaje rero ko, ibihuha vy’Ikiyaga C’Umweru vyahora vyinjiramwo ubwoko bwose bw’ibimenyetso biteye ubwoba, n’ivyiyumviro vy’ibinyoma vy’ibigo bidasanzwe, ivyo bikaba vyatumye Mockby agira ubwoba biraboneka mu buryo buboneka. Ku buryo mu bihe vyinshi amaherezo yagira ubwoba nk’ubwo, ku buryo bakeyi kubera ivyo bihuha, n’imiburiburi, bari bumvise ivy’Ikinyamasyo c’Umweru, bakeyi muri abo bahigi bari biteguriye guhura n’akaga k’urwaya rwiwe.

Ariko rero, hariho n’ibindi bintu vy’ingirakamaro kandi bihambaye kuruta vyariko birakora. N’ubu nyene nta n’icubahiro c’intango c’Ikinyamasyo c’Ikinyamasyo, nk’uko cari gitandukanye n’ibindi bikoko vyose vy’ikinyamaswa citwa leviathan mu buryo buteye ubwoba, nticapfuye mu vyiyumviro vy’abanyabaline nk’umubiri. Hariho abo uno musi muri bo, naho nyene bafise ubwenge n’ubutwari bihagije mu gutanga urugamba ku balene ya Groenlande canke y’iburyo, kumbure boshobora—bivuye ku kutagira ubumenyi mu vy’umwuga, canke ku kutagira ubushobozi, canke kubera isoni, banka guhangana n’Ikiremwa c’Ikigereranyo; uko vyogenda kwose, hariho abahinga mu vy’amabalene benshi cane, canecane muri ayo mahanga y’abarovyi b’amabalene atagendera munsi y’ibendera rya Amerika, atigeze ahura n’Ikinyamasyo c’Ikinyamasyo mu buryo bw’urwanko, ariko ubumenyi bwabo bwonyene ku bijanye n’ico gikoko citwa leviathan bugarukira ku gikoko c’agaterasoni cari gikurikiranywe mu Buraruko mu ntango; bicaye ku bipfukisho vyabo, abo bagabo bazokwumviriza n’ugushira akanyota kw’abana n’ugutinya, inkuru z’agaterasoni kandi zidasanzwe z’uguroba baleine zo mu Bumanuko. Eka mbere nta hantu na hamwe umuntu ashobora gutahura neza ubunini bw’ico gikoko kinini citwa Sperm Whale, kuruta iyo ari ku bwato bw’ivyo bikoko bimutera.

Kandi nk’aho ukuri kw’ububasha bwiwe ubu kwageragejwe mu bihe vya kera vy’imigani kwari kwatereye igitutu cakwo imbere yaco; turasanga abahinga mu vy’ibidukikije bamwebamwe bo mu bitabu—Olassen na Povelson—bamenyesha ko iyo Baleine Sperme atari ikintu giteye ubwoba gusa ku kindi kiremwa cose co mu kiyaga, ariko kandi ko ari ubukazi budasanzwe ku buryo buguma bunyotewe amaraso y’umuntu. Eka mbere no gushika mu gihe ca kera cane nk’ica Cuvier, ivyo bintu canke hafi bisa n’ivyo ntivyazimanganywe. Kuko mu gitabu ciwe citwa Natural History, uwo Baron ubwiwe yemeza yuko iyo amafi yose (harimwo n’amafi y’umukara) abonye iyo Baleine Sperme, “aterwa n’ubwoba buteye ubwoba kuruta ubundi bwose,” kandi “kenshi mu gihe c’uguhunga kwayo, aca yirukira ku bitandara n’ubukazi bukomeye cane ku buryo ashobora gupfa ubwo nyene.” Kandi naho nyene ubumenyi rusangi mu bijanye n’uburovyi bushobora guhindura izo raporo nk’izi; yamara mu buteye ubwoba bwavyo bwose, mbere no ku kintu c’amaraso ca Povelson, ukwemera kw’ibinyoma kuri bo, mu bihinduka bimwebimwe vy’ihamagarwa ryabo, kurasubira kuvyuka mu vyiyumviro vy’abahigi.

Ku buryo, kubera ubwoba bw’ibihuha n’ibimenyetso vyerekeye we, si bake mu barovyi bibutse, mu kwerekeza kuri Moby Dick, imisi ya mbere y’uburovyi bw’amabaleine y’intanga, igihe kenshi vyari bigoye gutuma abarovyi b’amabaleine y’iburyo bamaze igihe kirekire bamenyerezwa, batangura ingorane z’iyo ntambara nshasha kandi y’ubushizi bw’amanga; mwene abo bagabo bariko bararwanya yuko naho abandi ba leviyatani boshobora gukurikirana n’icizigiro, ariko gukurikirana no gutera icumu ku yerekwa nk’iryo ry’Ikigereranyo c’Ikigereranyo ntivyari ivy’umuntu apfa. Ko kugerageza, vyoba ata kabuza gutabururwa mu buzima budahera bwihuta. Kuri uwo mutwe, hariho inyandiko zidasanzwe umuntu yoshobora kuraba.

Naho ari ukwo, hariho bamwebamwe, mbere n’imbere y’ivyo bintu bari biteguriye gukurikirana Moby Dick; kandi n’igitigiri kinini cane, kubera ko bashobora gusa kumwumva kure kandi ataco bamenye, ata n’ibintu vyihariye vyerekeye icago kinaka, kandi ata n’ivyo baherekeza vy’ibinyoma, bari bakomeye bihagije kugira ngo ntibahunge urugamba iyo bazoba bashikirijwe.

Kimwe mu vyiyumviro vy’agahomerabunwa vyavuzwe, nk’uko amaherezo vyaje guhuzwa n’Ikinyamasyo c’Umweru mu vyiyumviro vy’abakunda ukwizera ibinyoma, cari ubwibone butari ubw’isi bw’uko Moby Dick yari hose; ko mu vy’ukuri yari yarahuye n’abantu mu bice bitandukanye vy’uburebure mu gihe kimwe.

Eka mbere, naho mwene ubwo bwenge butegerezwa kuba bwari ubw’ukwizera, ukwo kwibona ntikwari kwose ata n’ikintu na kimwe kigaragaza ko hariho ivyiyumviro vy’ibinyoma. Kuko nk’uko amabanga y’imipfunda yo mu biyaga atarigeze amenyekana, mbere n’abashakashatsi b’abahinga cane; rero inzira zihishijwe z’Ikinyamasyo c’Ikinyamasyo iyo kiri munsi y’amazi ziguma, ahanini, zitagira ico zivuze ku bamukurikira; kandi rimwe na rimwe vyaratumye habaho ibitekerezo biteye ubwoba kandi bivuguruzanya cane ku bijanye na vyo, canecane ku bijanye n’uburyo bw’amabanga aho, amaze gutera ijwi gushika mu bwimbike bwinshi, yitwara n’ubwo bwihuta bwinshi cane aja mu bibanza vya kure cane.

Ni ikintu kizwi cane n’ubwato bw’amabalene bw’Abanyamerika n’ubw’Abongereza, kandi ni ikintu cashizwe ku nyandiko y’ububasha mu myaka iheze na Scoresby, ko hari amabalene yafashwe kure cane mu buraruko bwa Pasifike, mu mibiri yayo hakaba harimwo amahembe y’inkoni zatewe mu biyaga vya Greenland. Eka mbere ntaco bimaze, ko muri bimwe muri ivyo bihe vyamenyeshejwe ko igihe cari hagati y’ivyo bitero bibiri kitashobora kurenga imisi myinshi cane. Ni co gituma, biciye ku ciyumviro, vyemewe n’abahinga mu vy’amabaleine bamwebamwe, yuko inzira ya Nor’ West, ikaba yari ingorane kuva kera cane ku muntu, itigeze iba ingorane ku balene. Ku buryo aha, mu buzima nyakuri bw’abantu bazima, ibitangaza vyavugwa mu bihe vya kera vy’umusozi Strello wo mu gihugu hagati muri Portigal (hafi y’isonga ryawo bavuga ko hari ikiyaga aho ibisigazwa vy’ubwato vyareremba bikaja hejuru); n’iyo nkuru itangaje kuruta y’isoko ry’i Arethusa ryari hafi y’i Sirakuza (amazi yaryo vyemerwa ko yava mu Gihugu Ceranda biciye mu nzira yo munsi y’ubutaka); izo nkuru z’ibitangaza zirangana hafi n’ibintu vy’ukuri vy’abanya balene.

Kugoberwa rero no kumenyerana n’ibitangaza nk’ivyo; kandi bazi ko inyuma y’ibitero bisubirwamwo, ata co bimaze, iyo Baleine Yera yari yarokotse ari nzima; ntibishobora gutangaza cane ko abahinga bamwebamwe bo mu bwoko bwa baleine bogenda n’ubundi mu vyiyumviro vyabo vy’ibinyoma; gutangaza ko Moby Dick atari hose gusa, ariko ko adapfa (kuko ukudapfa ari hose gusa mu gihe); ko naho mu mpande ziwe hoba hari ibisaka vy’amacumu, yoguma yoga ata co akomerewe; canke iyo vy’ukuri azokwigera atuma asuka amaraso menshi, mwene ukwo kubona vyoba ari uguhendana guteye ubwoba gusa; kuko kandi mu bicu bitagira amaraso biri ku bilometero amajana n’amajana, indege yiwe idahumanye yosubiye kuboneka.

Ariko mbere n’aho ivyo vyiyumviro bidasanzwe vyari vyakuweko, hariho ibihagije mu vyo kw’isi n’akaranga katagira uko kangana k’ico gikoko kugira ngo gikubire mu vyiyumviro n’ububasha budasanzwe. Kuko, si ubunini bwiwe budasanzwe bwari bumutandukanya cane n’izindi baleine z’intanga, ahubwo, nk’uko vyari vyijugunywe hanze ahandi—uruhanga rwiwe rudasanzwe rwuzuye nk’urubura, n’igihimba kirekire c’umweru nk’igiti c’ipiramide. Ivyo ni vyo vyari bimugaragaza cane; ibimenyetso aho, no mu biyaga bitagira aho bigarukira, bitamenyekanye, yahishuriye akaranga kiwe, ari kure cane, ku bamuzi.

Ibindi bice vy’umubiri wiwe vyari bifise imirongo, kandi bifise amabara, kandi bifise ibara ry’agahama ry’ibara ry’agahama, ku buryo, mu mpera, yari yararonse izina ryiwe ridasanzwe ry’Ikinyamasyo c’Ikinyamasyo; izina, vy’ukuri, ryari rishingiye ku ruhande rwiwe rutomoye, iyo abonwa ariko aranyerera ku manywa y’ijoro mu kiyaga c’ubururu bwijimye, asigaye ari nk’amata y’urufuru rw’amavuta, vyose bikaba bica ibibatsi vy’inzahabu.

Eka mbere si ubunini bwiwe budasanzwe, canke ibara ryiwe ridasanzwe, canke urwaya rwiwe rwo hasi rwari rwacitse intege, vyatumye iyo balene igira ubwoba busanzwe, nk’ubwo bubi butagira akarorero, bw’ubwenge, nk’uko inkuru zitomoye zivyerekana, yari yaragaragaje incuro nyinshi mu bitero vyiwe. Ikiruta vyose, ugusubira inyuma kwiwe kw’ubuhemu kwateye ubwoba kuruta kumbure ikindi kintu cose. Kuko, igihe yariko arayoga imbere y’abamukurikira bariko baranezerwa, afise ikimenyetso cose kigaragara c’uguhagarika umutima, yari yaramenyekanye incuro nyinshi ko ahindukira bukwi na bukwi, maze, abamanutse, akatera ubwato bwabo ibice, canke akabusubiza mu bwato bwabo mu gahinda.

Amaze gupfa abantu benshi bari baje mu gukurikirana kwiwe. Ariko naho ivyago nk’ivyo, naho vyari bike gute ku nkombe, ntivyari bimenyerewe na gato mu bijanye n’uburovyi; yamara, kenshi na kenshi, ivyo vyasa n’uko ari vyo vyiyumviro vy’ubukazi vy’umuriro w’iteka vya Baleine Yera, ku buryo uguca ibice kwose canke urupfu rwose yatuma, ntivyabonwa ko vyatewe n’umuntu atagira ubwenge.

Mucamanza rero, ni ukuntu ubwenge bw’abahigi biwe bari bihebuye bwari bufise uburake bwinshi, igihe hagati mu bipande vy’ubwato bwari bwarataguwe, no mu bihimba vy’umubiri vy’abagenzi babo bariko bararohama, bariko barayoga bava mu mavuta yera y’uburake buteye ubwoba bw’ico gikoko, baja mu muco w’izuba, canke uwo umugeni.

Ubwato bwiwe butatu buramukikuje, n’amasozi n’abantu bose bariko barazunguruka mu mazi; umukuru w’ubwato umwe, afashe icumu ry’umurongo ryari ku mutwe wiwe wari wavunitse, yari yirukiye kuri iyo balene, nk’uko umukinyi w’umupira w’amaguru wo muri Arkansas yatera umwansi wiwe, arondera ata co abona n’imbugita y’amasentimetero atandatu kugira ngo ashike ku buzima bw’iyo balene bushika kure cane. Uwo mukuru w’ingabo yari Ahabu. Kandi rero niho, Moby Dick akubura bukwi na bukwi urwaya rwiwe rwo hasi rwari rumeze nk’umuhoro munsi yiwe, yari yarakuyeho ukuguru kwa Ahabu, nk’uko umukemuzi akubura ivyatsi vyo mu murima. Nta Muturuki yambaye igitambaro co mu mutwe, nta Munyavenezuwela canke Umumaleziya yari akoreshwa, yari gushobora kumukubita n’ububisha busa n’ubwo. Nta mvo n’imwe yariho rero yo gukekeranya yuko kuva aho Ahabu ahura n’ico gikoko cari hafi kumwica, yari yarakunda cane kwihora iyo balene, akarushirizaho gukundwa n’uko mu ndwara yiwe y’agahomerabunwa, amaherezo yaje kwifatanya na yo, atari amagorwa yiwe yose yo ku mubiri gusa, ariko n’ivyo yari afise vyose mu bwenge no mu vy’impwemu. Ico gikoko citwa Baleine White carayoze imbere yiwe nk’umuntu w’umunyaruyeri w’ivyo bigo vyose vy’ububisha abantu bamwebamwe bo mu bihe bikomeye bumva ko biri muri vyo, gushika aho basigara babaho bafise igice c’umutima n’igice c’amahaha. Ubwo bubi butaboneka bwabayeho kuva mu ntango; uwo mbere n’abakirisu bo muri iki gihe bavuga ko igice c’isi ari co gitera; iyo Abafofi ba kera bo mu buseruko bubaha mu gishushanyo cabo ca shetani;—Ahabu ntiyaguye hasi ngo agisenge nka bo; ariko mu kwimurira iciyumviro caco ku gikoko citwa baleine cera cari giteye isoni, aca yitera, vyose bimucitse ibihimba vy’umubiri, kugira ngo ayirwanye. Ivyo vyose bitera ubusazi kandi bibabaza cane; ivyo vyose bivyura ivyiyumviro vy’ibintu; ukuri kwose kurimwo ububisha; ivyo vyose bica imitsi bigaca ubwonko; ubudayimoni bwose buteye ubwoba bw’ubuzima n’ivyiyumviro; ikibi cose, kuri Ahabu w’umusazi, vyari bigaragara nk’umuntu, kandi vyahinduwe nk’ibishobora guterwa muri Moby Dick. Yarundanije ku gipfukisho cera c’ico gikoko c’amazi umubare w’ishavu n’urwanko vyose rusangi vyari vyumviwe n’ubwoko bwiwe bwose kuva kuri Adamu gushika hasi; hanyuma, nk’aho igifu ciwe cari igipfunsi, aca agiturikirako igipfukisho c’umutima wiwe ushushe.

Ntibishoboka ko iyo nzira y’ugukunda ibintu bimwe bimwe muri we yaje gutera imbere mu gihe nyaco c’ugucika intege kw’umubiri wiwe. Maze, mu gutera ico gikoko, afise icumu mu minwe, yari amaze guhagarika urwanko rw’umubiri rwari rutunguranye, rwuzuye urukundo; kandi igihe yaronka igitero camutaburuye, birashoboka ko yumvise gusa iyo nkuru y’umubiri ibabaje cane, ariko nta kindi yari yumvise. Yamara, igihe kubera iyo mpanuka yatuma umuntu ahindukira aja i muhira, kandi akamara amezi menshi y’imisi n’amayinga, Ahabu n’umubabaro bari baryamye hamwe mu gitereko kimwe, bakikuza hagati mu rushana iyo nkengera y’ikiyaga ca Patagonia y’agahinda kandi iboroga; ni ho rero, umubiri wiwe wataburutse n’umutima wiwe wacitse, biva amaraso mu bindi; kandi rero gutera imbere, vyatumye asaza.
