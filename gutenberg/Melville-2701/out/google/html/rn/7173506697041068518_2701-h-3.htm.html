UMUGAMBI WA 10. Umugenzi w’Igifu.

Nsubiye mu nzu y’abashitsi yitwa Spouter-Inn mvuye mu Rusengero, nasanze Queequeg ari ng’aho wenyene cane; we amaze kuva mu Rusengero imbere y’umuhezagiro igihe kanaka. Yari yicaye ku ntebe imbere y’umuriro, ibirenge vyiwe biri ku mfuruka y’umuriro, kandi mu kuboko kumwe yari afashe hafi mu maso hiwe ako gashushanyo gatoyi k’umuzungu; yihweza cane mu maso hayo, kandi afise imbugita y’umukaraba ariko arayikubita buhoro buhoro mu zuru, muri ico gihe ariko arivugira mu buryo bwiwe bw’ubupagani.

Ariko ubu amaze gucirwamwo, ashiraho ico gishushanyo; kandi bidatevye, aja ku meza, afata igitabu kinini ng’aho, aragishira ku bibero vyiwe atangura guharura amapaji ata guca ku ruhande; ku rupapuro rwa mirongo itanu rwose—nk’uko navyiyumviriye—ahagarara akanya gatoyi, araba ata co akora hirya no hino yiwe, maze akavuga amajambo y’ugutangara yamara igihe kirekire. Yari rero gusubira gutangura ku myaka mirongo itanu ikurikira; asa n’uwutangura ku rugero rwa mbere igihe cose, nk’aho atashobora guharura ibirenze mirongo itanu, kandi ni ukubera gusa ko imirongo itanu myinshi cane yabonetse hamwe, ni ho gusa yatangajwe n’ubwinshi bw’amapaji.

Naricaranye ndamubona n’umunezero mwinshi. Naho yari umunyamaswa, kandi yari afise ububabare buteye isoni mu maso—n’imiburiburi ku buryo nakunda—mu maso hiwe hari ikintu kitari co kitari co. Ntimushobora guhisha ubugingo. Biciye mu bishushanyo vyiwe vyose bitari ivy’isi, niyumvira ko mbona ibimenyetso vy’umutima woroshe w’ukuri; kandi mu maso yiwe manini manini, ateye igomwe, yirabura nk’umuriro kandi ateye ubwoba, hasa n’ibimenyetso vy’impwemu yoshobora gutera ubwoba amadayimoni igihumbi. Kandi uretse ivyo vyose, hariho inyifato yo hejuru ku Mupagani, mbere n’ubujuju bwiwe ntibwari gushobora kumumugaza burundu. Yari ameze nk’umuntu atigeze agira ubwoba kandi atigeze agira uwumufitiye umwenda. Nimba na vyo nyene ari uko umutwe wiwe wariko uramoshwa, uruhanga rwiwe rwari rwarakwezwe mu buryo buteye umwidegemvyo kandi rukeye, kandi rwasa n’urwagutse kuruta uko rwobaye, ivyo sinzokwigera nshaka gufata ingingo; ariko nta gukeka ko umutwe wiwe wari umutwe mwiza cane. Bishobora gusa n’ibiteye isoni, ariko vyanyibutse umutwe wa Jenerali Washington, nk’uko bigaragara mu bishushanyo vyiwe vy’abantu benshi. Yari ifise iyo nzira nyene ndende y’ugusubira inyuma iva hejuru y’ibipfunsi, na yo nyene yari igaragara cane, nk’imisozi ibiri miremire yari iteye ibiti vyinshi hejuru. Queequeg yari George Washington yateye imbere mu vy’ukurya abantu.

Igihe nariko ndamusuzuma gutyo, nkigira nk’aho ndiko ndarabira hanze mu gihuhusi ndi mu nzu y’ibarabara, ntiyigeze yumviriza ukuhaba kwanje, ntiyigeze yibabaza n’ukuntu n’amaso amwe; ariko asa n’uwuhugiye cane mu guharura amapaji y’ico gitabu gitangaje. Nzirikanye ingene twari twararyamye hamwe mu buryo bw’ubucuti ijoro ry’imbere y’aho, na canecane nzirikanye ukuboko kw’urukundo nari nasanze kunjugunye igihe navyuka mu gitondo, naciye mbona ko uku kutagira ico yitwararika kwiwe kwari ugutangaje cane. Ariko abanyamaswa ni ibiremwa bitangaje; rimwe na rimwe ntumenya neza ingene wobifata. Mu ntango bariko baraboroga cane; ukwikoranya kwabo gutuje kw’ukworohereza bisa n’ubwenge bwa Socrate. Nari nabonye kandi ko Queequeg atigeze agira ubucuti na gato, canke ariko buke cane, n’abandi basoda bo mu mazi bari mu nzu y’abashitsi. Nta n’iterambere na rimwe yagize; yasa n’uwutagira icipfuzo co kwagura uruzitiro rw’abo yari azi. Ivyo vyose vyankubitse nk’ubumwe bukomeye; yamara, iyo umuntu yiyumviriye ubwa kabiri, hari ikintu hafi co hejuru cane muri co. Aha hari umuntu yari ku bilometero nka ibihumbi mirongo ibiri uvuye i muhira, aciye mu nzira y’i Cape Horn, ni ukuvuga—ari yo nzira yonyene yari gushobora gushikayo—yajugunywe mu bantu bamumenye nk’aho yoba ari ku mubumbe wa Jupiter; kandi naho ari ukwo, yasa n’uwuruhutse rwose; kuzigama ugutekana gusumba kure; abumbwa n’ubugenzi bwiwe bwite; yama angana na we nyene. Nta gukeka ko ivyo vyari ugukora ku mutima kw’ubuhinga bwiza; naho ata gukeka ko atigeze yumva ko hariho ikintu nk’ico. Ariko, kumbure, kugira ngo tube abafilozofe nyakuri, twebwe abantu dupfa ntidukwiye kumenya ko tubaho gutyo canke ko twihatira gutyo. Rero, iyo numvise ko uwo mugabo canke uwo mugabo yitanga ngo abe umunyabwenge, nca mfata ingingo y’uko, cokimwe n’uwo mukecuru w’umunyamibabaro, ategerezwa kuba “yavunye igifungurwa ciwe.”

Nk’uko nari nicaye ng’aho muri ico cumba ubu cari irungu; umuriro waka hasi, muri iyo ntambwe y’ubugwaneza igihe, inyuma y’aho uburemere bwawo bwa mbere bushushe ikirere, uca ukayangana gusa kugira ngo umuntu arabe; ibitutu vyo ku mugoroba n’ibinyoma bikoranira hamwe bikikuje amabarabara, bikaducakira twebwe babiri bacereje, turi twenyene; igihuhusi gitera ata mu bivyimba vy’agaciro; Naciye ntangura gutahura inyiyumvo zidasanzwe. Numva nsunze muri jewe. Nta mutima wanje wacitsemwo ibice n’ukuboko kwanje kwasaze vyari bigihindukiriye isi y’ibirura. Uwo munyamaswa atera umunezero yari yarayicunguye. Aho ni ho yari yicaye, ukutagira ico yitwararika kwiwe nyene kuvuga kamere ata buryarya buteye imbere n’ubuhendanyi buteye isoni bwari bwihishijemwo. Yari umunyamaswa; ivyerekanwa cane vy’ivyerekanwa vyo kubona; yamara naciye ntangura kwumva nkwegerewe kuri we mu buryo bw’akabanga. Kandi ivyo bintu nyene vyari kwirukana benshi mu bandi, ni vyo nyene vyari vyankwegereye gutyo. Nzogerageza umugenzi w’umupagani, ni ko niyumvira, kuko ubuntu bwa gikirisu bwagaragaje ariko ubugwaneza butagiramwo ubusa. Narakwegereye intebe yanje hafi yiwe, maze nkora ibimenyetso n’ivyiyumviro vy’ubugenzi, nkora uko nshoboye kwose kugira ngo tuganire muri ico gihe. Mu ntango ntiyabona cane ivyo bitera imbere; ariko ubu, igihe navuga ibijanye n’ubwakiranyi bwiwe bw’ijoro rya nyuma, yaciye afata ingingo yo kumbaza nimba twosubira kuryamana. Nanje ndamubwira ngo ego; aho niyumvira ko asa n’uwunezerewe, kumbure ashimagizwa gatoyi.

Twaraciye duhindukiza ico gitabu turi kumwe, nanje ndagerageza kumusigurira intumbero y’icapa, n’insobanuro y’amashusho makeyi yari muri co. Gutyo ntatevye gukorana n’inyungu yiwe; kandi kuva aho twaciye tuja kuvuga ivyiza kuruta ibindi vyose dushoboye ku bijanye n’ibintu bitandukanye vyo hanze vyo kubona muri iki gisagara kizwi cane. Bidatevye, narasavye umwotsi w’imibano; kandi, asohora umufuko wiwe n’igiti ciwe citwa tomahawk, aca ambwira mu gacerere. Hanyuma twicara duhanahana imipfunda y’uwo muyoboro wiwe w’ishamba, kandi tukaguma tuwuca hagati yacu ubudasiba.

Nimba hariho urubura rwo kutagira ico ankunda rwari rwihishije mw’ibere ry’Umupagani, uwo mwotsi mwiza kandi w’ubugwaneza twari dufise, ntiwatevye kurufukura, maze uradusiga turi abagenzi. Yasa n’uwuntwara mu buryo busanzwe kandi ataco amusavye nk’uko nanje ndamufata; umwotsi wacu umaze guhera, aca afatanya uruhanga rwiwe n’urwanje, anfata mu mafyinga, aca ambwira ko kuva ubu twarongoranye; bisobanura, mu mvugo y’igihugu ciwe, ko twari abagenzi b’igikiriza; yari kunpfira n’umunezero, iyo bikenewe. Mu munyagihugu, iyo nkuru y’ubugenzi yatunguranye yari gusa n’iyitarashika kure cane, ikintu co kwizigira cane; ariko muri iki gikoko coroshe ayo mategeko ya kera ntiyokora.

Inyuma y’ifunguro rya nimugoroba, n’iyindi nkuru y’urukundo n’umwotsi, twagiye mu cumba cacu turi kumwe. Yampaye ingabirano y’umutwe wiwe wari ushizwemwo imiti; akurayo igikapu ciwe kinini cane c’itabi, aca akorakora munsi y’itabi, akuramwo amadolari nk’imirongo itatu y’ifeza; hanyuma akabishira ku meza, akabigabanya mu buryo bw’amashini mu bice bibiri bingana, asunika kimwe muri vyo kije aho ndi, aca ambwira ko ari icanje. Nari ngiye guhanura; ariko aca ancecekesha mu kubisuka mu mifuko y’amapantalo yanje. Narabaretse baguma. Yaciye aja mu masengesho yiwe yo ku mugoroba, akuramwo ikigirwamana ciwe, akuraho n’urubaho rw’umuriro rw’impapuro. Kubera ibimenyetso bimwebimwe, niyumvira ko asa n’uwuhangayikishijwe n’uko nokwifatanya na we; ariko kubera ko nari nzi neza ivyo vyari gukurikira, narazirikanye umwanya mutoyi nimba, mu gihe yoba antumiye, novyubahiriza canke ukundi.

Nari umukirisu mwiza; yavukiye kandi akurira mu gikiriza c’Ekleziya Presbytérienne itagira amahinyu. None nokwifatanya gute n’uwo musenga ibigirwamana w’agaterasoni mu gusenga igiti ciwe? Ariko none ugusenga ni iki? iciyumviro I. Woba wiyumvira ubu, Ishimayeli, ko Imana y’ubugwaneza y’ijuru n’isi—abapagani na bose barimwo—ishobora kuba igira ishari n’agace k’igiti c’umwirabura ata co kamaze? Ntibishoboka! Ariko none ugusenga ni iki?—gukora ivyo Imana igomba— ukwo ni ugusenga. Kandi ubugombe bw’Imana ni ubuhe?—gukorera mugenzi wanje ivyo noshima ko mugenzi wanje ankorera— ubwo ni bwo bugombe bw’Imana. None, Queequeg ni umuntu mugenzi wanje. Kandi jewe nipfuza ko uwo Queequeg ankorera iki? Emwe, mwifatanye nanje mu buryo bwanje bwo gusenga bwihariye bw’Abapresbiteriyani. Ku bw’ivyo, ntegerezwa rero kwifatanya na we mu vyiwe; ergo, ntegerezwa guhinduka umusenga ibigirwamana. Nuko ndacana ivyuma vy’ibitoke; yafashije gushigikira ico kigirwamana gitoyi kitagira icaha; amuha ibisiketi vy’umuriro biri kumwe na Queequeg; salamed imbere yiwe incuro zibiri canke zitatu; amusoma izuru; kandi ivyo birangiye, twarambara turaryama, mu mahoro n’ijwi ryacu ryo mu mutima be n’isi yose. Ariko ntitwaja kuryama ata gace gatoyi twavuganye.

Uko bimeze sinzi; ariko nta kibanza kimeze nk’igitanda co guhishura ibanga hagati y’abagenzi. Umugabo n’umugore, bavuga ko ari ho bafunguriranira ibwina ry’ubugingo bwabo; kandi ababiranye bamwebamwe basaza barakunda kubesha baganira ku bihe vya kera gushika hafi mu gitondo. Gutyo rero, mu kwezi kw’ubuki kw’imitima yacu, jewe na Queequeg twari turi abagenzi b’abanyarukundo kandi b’abanyarukundo.





UMUGAMBI WA 11. Impuzu yo mw’ijoro.

Twari twaryamye gutyo mu gitanda, turiko turayaga kandi turiko turasinzira mu bihe bigufi, maze Queequeg rimwe na rimwe akajugunya amaguru yiwe y’inzobe yari afise ivyandiko ku yanje abigiranye urukundo, hanyuma akayasubira inyuma; rero twari abantu bakundana n’abandi kandi twidegemvya kandi twiyoroshe; igihe, amaherezo, kubera ibiganiro vyacu, ako gahinda gatoyi kari kasigaye muri twebwe kagenda, maze twumva dushaka gusubira kuvyuka, naho nyene akaruhuko k’umutaga kari kakiri kure cane muri kazoza.

Ego cane, twacitse abavyuka cane; ku buryo aho twari twicaye hatanguye kuruhira, maze buhoro buhoro tugasanga twicaye; imyambaro yari itwihishije neza, twishingikirije ku rubaho rw’umutwe amavi yacu ane akwegereye hafi, n’izuru ryacu ryompi ryari ripfukamye hejuru yayo, nk’aho amavi yacu ari ibikoresho vyo gushushanya. Twumvise turi beza cane kandi twiyumvamwo neza, canecane kubera ko hanze hari ubukonje bwinshi; vy’ukuri n’impuzu zo mu gitanda, abonye ko ata muriro wari mu cumba. N’uko nyene, ndabivuga, kuko vy’ukuri kugira ngo umuntu aronke ubushuhe bwo mu mubiri, hari igice gitoyi cawe gitegerezwa kuba gikanye, kuko nta kamere iri muri iyi si itari iyo iri gusa mu gutandukanya. Nta kintu na kimwe kiriho ubwaco. Iyo wishimira ko vyose bimeze neza, kandi ko umaze igihe kirekire cane, rero ntushobora kuvugwa ko umeze neza kandi. Ariko nimba, nka jewe na Queequeg mu gitanda, isonga ry’izuru ryawe canke igitsibo c’umutwe wawe bikanye gatoyi, ni kuki none, vy’ukuri, mu bwenge rusangi wumva uryoshe cane kandi ushushe ata gukeka. Ni co gituma inzu yo kuryamamwo idakwiye kwigera igira umuriro, ivyo bikaba ari bimwe mu bintu vy’agaciro vy’abatunzi. Kuko uburebure bw’ubwo bwoko bw’uburyohe ni ukutagira ikindi kiretse uburengeti buri hagati yawe n’ugushikama kwawe n’ubukonje bw’ikirere co hanze. Hanyuma ng’aho uryamye nk’ikibiriti kimwe gishushe kiri mu mutima w’ikibiriti co mu karere ka arctique.

Twari tumaze igihe twicaye muri ubwo buryo bwo gupfukama, maze ubwo nyene niyumvira ko nzofungura amaso; kuko iyo ndi hagati y’ibitambaro, haba ku murango canke mw’ijoro, canke iyo nsinziriye canke ndi maso, ndafise uburyo bwo kwama nfunga amaso, kugira ngo ndushirize kwibanda ku ntumbero yo kuba mu gitanda. Kuko nta muntu ashobora kwigera yumva akaranga kiwe neza kiretse amaso yiwe apfutse; nk’aho umwijima woba vy’ukuri ari co kintu kibereye mu vyo dufise, naho umuco woba uryoshe cane ku gice cacu c’ibumba. Maze gufungura amaso yanje ico gihe, no kuva mu mwijima wanje bwite uryoshe kandi niremye, nkaja mu mwijima wo hanze wategetswe kandi w’agahomerabunwa w’isaha cumi n’ibiri z’ijoro zitagira umuco, narabonye ikintu kidashimishije. Eka mbere nta na rimwe narwanya iciyumviro cavuye kuri Queequeg c’uko kumbure vyoba vyiza duteye umuco, kuko twari turi maso cane; kandi uretse ivyo yumvise icipfuzo gikomeye co kugira amafufu makeyi y’agacerere avuye mu modoka yiwe yitwa Tomahawk. Bivugwe ko naho nari numvise inzigo nyinshi cane z’ukunywa itabi ryiwe mu gitanda ijoro ry’imbere y’aho, n’aho biri ukwo, mbona ingene urwanko rwacu rukomeye rukura iyo urukundo ruje kuzipfukamira. Kuko ubu nta kintu nakunda kuruta kuba Queequeg annywa itabi, mbere no mu gitanda, kuko yasa n’uwufise umunezero nk’uwo wo mu rugo utekanye ico gihe. Sinsubira kwumva nshavuye birenze urugero ku bijanye n’itegeko ry’ubwishingizi rya nyen’inzu. Nari muzima gusa kubera umunezero w’ibanga wo gusangira umuyoboro n’uburengeti n’umugenzi nyakuri. Twari dufise amakoti yacu y’ubwoya, ubu twaciye ku ndege ya Tomahawk tuva kuri imwe tuja ku yindi, gushika aho buhorobuhoro hadukura hejuru yacu igikoresho co kugerageza umwotsi c’ubururu kimanitse, kikamurikirwa n’urubeya rw’itara rishasha ryaka.

Nimba ari uko uwo mugerageza w’umupfunda yazungurutse uwo munyamaswa aja mu bibanza vya kure cane, sinzi, ariko ubu yavuga ivy’izinga ryiwe ry’amavukiro; kandi, nshaka cane kwumva amateka yiwe, naramwinginga ngo abandanye ayavuga. Yaravyemeye n’umunezero. Naho ico gihe ntatahura neza amajambo yiwe makeyi, ariko ivyo yahishuriwe mu nyuma, igihe nari naramenye neza amajambo yiwe yavunitse, ubu biratuma nshobora gushikiriza inkuru yose nk’uko yoshobora kwemezwa mu nkuru gusa ntanga.





UMUGAMBI WA 12. Ivyerekeye ubuzima bw’umuntu.

Queequeg yari imvukira y’i Rokovoko, izinga riri kure cane mu Burengero no mu Bumanuko. Nta n’aho iri hasi ku ikarita iyo ari yo yose; ibibanza vy’ukuri ntivyigera bibaho.

Igihe igikoko gishasha cavyaye kigendagenda mu mashamba yavukiyemwo mu mfuruka y’ivyatsi, gikurikiwe n’impene ziriko zirarya, nk’aho yoba ari igiti c’icatsi kibisi; mbere n’ico gihe, mu mutima wa Queequeg wari ufise icipfuzo co kuronka ubukuru, harimwo icipfuzo gikomeye co kubona ikintu kiruta ivyo abiyita abakirisu kuruta umurovyi w’i baleine w’akarorero canke ibiri. Se wiwe yari Umukuru Mukuru, Umwami; se wabo Umuherezi Mukuru; kandi ku ruhande rw’abavyeyi yarirata ba senge bari abagore b’abarwanyi bataneshwa. Hari amaraso meza cane mu mitsi yiwe—ibintu vy’ubwami; naho nyene yari yarahumanye bibabaje, ndatinya, n’agatima ko kurya abantu yari afise mu buto bwiwe butagira inyigisho.

Ubwato bwo ku Kivuko ca Sag bwaragendeye ikiyaga ca se, maze Queequeg arondera inzira aja mu bihugu vy’Abakirisu. Ariko ubwato, kubera ko bwari bufise abasare bose, bwaranse impuzu yiwe; kandi si Umwami se wiwe wese yashobora gutsinda. Ariko Queequeg yarahiye umuhigo. Yari wenyene mu bwato bwiwe, arasoza aja mu kiyaga ca kure, ico yari azi ko ubwato butegerezwa gucamwo igihe bwava muri iryo zinga. Ku ruhande rumwe hari urutare rw’amakorali; ku rundi ruhande hari ururimi rw’ubutaka rwo hasi, rwuzuye ibisaka vy’imiberoshi vyakura biva mu mazi. Yanyegeje ubwato bwiwe, bwari bukiri ku mazi, hagati muri ivyo bisaka, umutwe wabwo ukaba uri mu kiyaga, aca yicara inyuma, afise umugozi wo gusoza hasi mu kuboko; kandi ubwato bumaze guca, nk’umuco aca yiruka arasohoka; yaronse uruhande rwiwe; n’ikirenge kimwe c’inyuma c’ikirenge ciwe carahindukiye maze kiroha ubwato bwiwe; yaduze iminyororo; maze yijugunya mu burebure bwose ku gisenge, afata ng’aho umugozi w’impeta, arahira ko atazowureka, naho wari waracitse ibipande.

Uwo mukuru w’ubwato yarateye ubwoba ko azomujugunya mu kiyaga; yamanitse umuheto hejuru y’ibiganza vyiwe vy’ubwambure; Queequeg yari umuhungu w’Umwami, kandi Queequeg ntiyacitse intege. Uwo mukuru w’ubwato yari akubiswe n’ukuntu ata co yari afise co kwihebura, be n’icipfuzo ciwe c’ubukazi co kugendera abiyita abakirisu, amaherezo yarahevye, maze amubwira ko yoshobora kwigira nk’aho ari i muhira. Ariko uwo musore mwiza w’umunyamaswa—uwo Muganwa wa Pays de Galles wo mu kiyaga, ntiyigeze abona inzu ya Kapiteni. Bamushira hagati y’abasare, bamugira umuntu w’umunyabaleine. Ariko nka kumwe kwa Czar Peter yanezerewe no gukora cane mu bibanza vy’ubwato vyo mu bisagara vy’amahanga, Queequeg ntiyasuzugura ikintu na kimwe kimeze nk’ikiteye isoni, nimba muri ivyo yoshobora kuronka ububasha bwo gutanga umuco ku bantu bo mu gihugu ciwe batagira inyigisho. Kuko hasi—ni ko yambwiye—yavyurwa n’icipfuzo gikomeye co kwiga mu bakirisu, ubuhinga bwo gutuma abantu biwe baguma bafise umunezero kuruta uko bari; kandi birenze ivyo, biracari vyiza kuruta uko bari. Ariko, n’agahinda! imigenzo y’abanyabaleine ntiyatevye kumujijura yuko mbere n’abakirisu bashobora kuba abakene kandi ari ababisha; ataco bimaze, kuruta abapagani bose ba se. Amaherezo twashitse ku kivuko ca kera ca Sag; kandi abonye ivyo abasare bakora ng’aho; hanyuma baja i Nantucket, babonye ingene bakoresha umushahara wabo muri ico kibanza na co nyene, umukene Queequeg arawuheba kubera ko yazimiye. Yariyumviriye, ni isi mbi mu meridiens zose; Nzopfa ndi umupagani.

Kandi rero yari umusaza w’umusenga ibigirwamana mu mutima, yaguma aba muri abo bakirisu, yambara impuzu zabo, akagerageza kuvuga amajambo yabo y’ubujuju. Ni co gituma inzira zidasanzwe zimuvugako, naho ubu ari igihe kanaka ari kure y’i muhira.

Kubera ivyiyumviro, namubajije nimba ataco asaba gusubirayo, no kugira igitsibo c’ubwami; kuko ubu yoshobora kubona ko se wiwe yapfuye kandi atahari, yari ashaje cane kandi afise intege nke mu nkuru za nyuma. Arishura ngo oya, ntaco nkiri; kandi yongerako ko yari ubukirisu buteye ubwoba, canke ahubwo abakirisu, bwari bwamubujije kuduga ku ntebe y’ubwami isukuye kandi idahumanye y’Abami mirongo itatu b’abapagani bari bamubanjirije. Ariko buhoro buhoro, yavuze ko azogaruka, —akimara kwumva ko yongeye kubatizwa. Ariko rero, ku bijanye n’ivyo, yaciye asaba ko yogendagenda mu bwato, akabiba ivyamwa vyiwe vy’ishamba mu biyaga vyose bine. Bari bamugize umuheto, kandi ico cuma c’amabuye cari mu kibanza c’inkoni y’ubwami ubu.

Naramubajije ico yoba ari co coba ari intumbero yiwe y’ubu nyene, nkora ku mutima ivy’imigenderanire yiwe yo muri kazoza. Arishura, ngo asubire kuja mu kiyaga, mu muhamagaro wiwe wa kera.
