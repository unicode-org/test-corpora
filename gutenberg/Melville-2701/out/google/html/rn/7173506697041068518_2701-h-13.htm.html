UMUGAMBI WA 62. Umuheto.

Ijambo ryerekeye ikintu cabaye mu kigabane ca nyuma.

Dukurikije ukuntu uburovyi bukoreshwa ata guhinduka, ubwato bw’amabalene burasunika buva mu bwato, umukuru canke uwumwica baleine akaba ari we atwara ubwato mu gihe gito, uwurongora canke uwufatanya balene na we akakwega umugozi w’imbere, uwo na wo ukaba uzwi kw’izina ry’umuvuduko w’amabaleine. Ubu rero rikeneye ukuboko gukomeye kandi guteye ubwoba kugira ngo ritere icuma ca mbere mu fi; kuko kenshi, mu vyo bita umuheto muremure, ico gikoresho kiremereye gitegerezwa guterwa ku rugendo rw’amafuti mirongo ibiri canke mirongo itatu. Ariko naho iyo nzira yo gukurikirana yomara igihe kirekire kandi ikaba irushe, uwo muvuzi w’inkota yitezwe gukwega umugozi wiwe muri ico gihe gushika ku mpera; nkako, aritezwe gutanga akarorero k’igikorwa kirenze ico umuntu akora ku bandi, atari gusa mu gusoza mu buryo budasanzwe, ariko no mu gusubiramwo n’ijwi rirenga kandi ata bwoba; n’ico ari co kuguma asemerera hejuru y’ubusobanuro bw’ibintu vy’umuntu, mu gihe iyindi mitsi yose iguma ihagaze kandi ikaba itangura igice—ico ari co nta n’umwe abizi atari ababigerageje. Ku bwa mbere, sinshobora guboroga cane n’umutima wanje wose no gukora ataco yiyumvira igihe kimwe. Muri iyo nzira rero y’uguhagarika umutima, y’uguboroga, afise umugongo ku fi, uwo muvuzi w’inkota arushe aca yumva akaruru gateye umunezero, ati: “Haguruke, umuhe!” Ubu ategerezwa gutera no gukingira umugozi wiwe, agahindukira hagati yiwe hagati, agafata inkota yiwe mu rubavu, maze n’inkomezi nkeyi yoba asigaranye, akagerageza kuyitera mu buryo bumwe canke ubundi mu gikoko citwa baleine. Ntibitangaje rero, dufashe ubwato bwose bw’aba baleine mu mubiri umwe, ko mu mahirwe mirongo itanu abereye yo gutera umuheto, ata atanu ashobora kuroranirwa; ntibitangaje rero kubona abacuraranzi benshi cane b’abanyamibabaro bavumwe nk’abasazi kandi bagacika intege; ntibitangaje rero kubona bamwe muri bo mu vy’ukuri baturitse imitsi yabo y’amaraso mu bwato; ntibitangaje rero kubona abanyamaswa bamwebamwe b’intanga bataboneka imyaka ine bafise imibindi ine; ntibitangaje rero kubona ku bafise ubwato benshi, kuroba baleine ari ikintu gusa gitera ubwoba; kuko umunyabute ni we akora urugendo, kandi iyo ukuye impwemu mu mubiri wiwe wokwitega gute ko uzoyisanga ng’aho igihe benshi bayishaka!

Na none, iyo umuheto uroraniwe, rero mu gihe ca kabiri gihambaye, ni ukuvuga igihe iyo balene itangura kwiruka, uwurongoye ubwato n’uwuwutera inkota na bo nyene batangura kwiruka imbere n’inyuma, kugira ngo bo ubwabo n’abandi bose bashikirwe n’ingorane. Ni ho rero bahindura ibibanza; kandi umukuru w’ubwato, umukuru w’ubwato buto, afata ikibanza ciwe kibereye mu miheto y’ubwato.

None, sinzi uwugumiza ibihushanye n’ivyo, ariko ivyo vyose ni ubupfu kandi ntibikenewe. Umukuru w’ingabo akwiye kuguma mu miheto kuva ku ntango gushika ku mpera; yari akwiye gutera umuheto n’icumu, kandi nta n’ico akwiye kwitega ko asoza, kiretse mu bihe bigaragara ku murovyi uwo ari we wese. Ndazi ko ivyo vyotuma rimwe na rimwe umuntu atakaza umuvuduko mutoyi mu gukurikirana; ariko ubumenyi mfise igihe kirekire mu bijanye n’abarovyi batandukanye bo mu bihugu birenga kimwe bwanyemeje yuko mu bihe vyinshi vy’ukunanirwa mu bijanye n’uburovyi, atari mu buryo na bumwe ari umuvuduko w’iyo balene nk’uko urushe rw’umurovyi w’inkoni rwari rwavuzwe imbere y’aho ari rwo rwatumye ziruha.

Kugira ngo abatera imiheto bashobore gukora neza cane, abatera imiheto bo muri iyi si bategerezwa gutangura ku birenge vyabo bavuye mu bunebwe, atari mu bikorwa vy’ubutame.





UMUGAMBI WA 63. Igipfunsi.

Amashami arakura mu giti; muri vyo, amashami. Rero, mu nyigisho zitanga inyungu, nimukuze ibigabane.

Igihimba c’irondoka cavuzwe kuri paji iheruka gikwiye kuvugwa ku giti cacu. Ni inkoni ifise uturongo tw’uburebure budasanzwe, ikaba ifise uburebure bw’amafuti nka abiri, ikaba yinjizwa mu buryo bugororotse mu gipfukisho c’iburyo hafi y’umuheto, kugira ngo ihe ikibanza co kuruhukiramwo ku mpera y’umuheto y’ibiti, iyo mpera y’ubwato ikaba iri gusa kandi ifise amahembe, ikaba isohoka mu ruhande rw’imbere. Gutyo, iyo ntwaro ica igera hafi y’uwuyitera, na we akayikuramwo ataco yiteze nk’uko umunyagihugu w’inyuma azunguza imbunda yiwe ku ruhome. Ni umugenzo kugira inkoni zibiri ziruhukiye mu rubavu, zikaba zitwa ivyuma vya mbere n’ivya kabiri.

Ariko izo nkoni zibiri, imwe yose ifise umugozi wayo, zompi zifatanye n’umurongo; ikintu cari iki: kubitera bompi, nimba bishoboka, umwe akurikira uwundi mu gikoko kimwe; kugira ngo iyo, mu gukurura kuzoza, umwe ashobora gukura, uwundi ashobora kuguma afise aho afashe. Ni ugukubita kabiri amahirwe. Ariko kenshi cane birashika ko kubera ukuntu iyo baleine yiruka mu kanya nk’ako gukubita urugohe, n’ubukazi, n’uguhungabana iyo ironse icuma ca mbere, bica bigorana ko uwo muvuzi w’inkota, naho yoba ameze nk’umuravyo mu vyo yinyiganza gute, adashobora kuyitera icuma ca kabiri. Naho ari ukwo, nk’uko icuma ca kabiri gisanzwe gifatanye n’umurongo, kandi umurongo uriko uragenda, ni co gituma iyo ntwaro itegerezwa, uko vyogenda kwose, guterwa imbere y’igihe mu bwato, mu buryo bumwe canke bundi kandi ahantu kanaka; ahandi ho akaga gateye ubwoba kuruta ayandi yose koba gateye amaboko yose. Tumbled mu mazi, ni ko biri mu bihe nk’ivyo; ivyuma vy’inyuma vy’umurongo w’agasanduku (vyavuzwe mu kigabane c’imbere) bituma ico gikorwa, kenshi, gishoboka mu buryo bw’ubwenge. Ariko ico gikorwa gikomeye ntikiguma kitagira uwugicungera, kikaba ari co gitera abababaje kandi kigatuma abantu benshi bapfa.

Inyama zihindurwa imipira ingana n’imipira ya billard, kandi iyo zirunze neza kandi zikaba zifise ibirungo, zoshobora gufatwa nk’imipira y’inzoka canke y’inyana. Abamonki ba kera bo muri Dunfermline barabakunda cane. Bari bafise ingurane nini cane y’inyamaswa yitwa porpoise iva ku ngoma.

Ikintu nyamukuru ni uko mu bahigi biwe n’imiburiburi, iyo balene yobonwa n’amaboko yose nk’ifunguro ry’icubahiro, iyo ataba ari we afise vyinshi gutyo; ariko iyo uje kwicara imbere y’umukate w’inyama ufise uburebure bw’amafuti hafi ijana, bigukurako ivyipfuzo vyo kurya. Abantu batagira urwanko kuruta abandi bose nka Stubb, muri iki gihe ni bo bonyene bafungura ibikoko vyitwa baleines bitetswe; ariko Abaeskimaux si abantu b’abanyaruyeri gutyo. Twese turazi ingene babaho ku bikoko vyitwa baleines, kandi bafise amavuta ya kera y’amavuta y’igariyamoshi. Zogranda, umwe mu baganga babo bazwi cane, asaba ko abana bato bafata imirongo y’amavuta y’umubiri, kuko ari umutobe mwinshi cane kandi akagaburira. Kandi ivyo binyibutsa ko Abongereza bamwebamwe, kera cane basigaye muri Groenlande mu mpanuka n’ubwato bwo kuroba baleine—ko abo bagabo mu vy’ukuri babayeho amezi menshi ku bisigazwa vy’amabalene vyari vyasigaye ku nkombe bamaze kugerageza iyo nkoko. Mu ba baleine b’Abaholandi ivyo bice vy’ibisigazwa vyitwa “fritters”; ivyo, vy’ukuri, bisa cane, bikaba ari ivy’inzobe kandi bimeze nk’ibitoke, kandi bikaba bimota nk’ivy’abagore ba kera bo mu nzu bo muri Amsterdam vy’amafu canke vy’amavuta y’ibitoke, iyo ari bishasha. Zifise ishusho yo kuribwa ku buryo umunyamahanga yihakana cane adashobora guhagarika ibiganza vyiwe.

Ariko ikirushiriza gusenyura balene nk’ifunguro ry’abanyabwenge, ni ubutunzi bwayo burengeje urugero. Ni we nka y’agashimwe kanini y’ikiyaga, ivyibushe cane ku buryo idashobora kuba nziza cane. Raba igihimba ciwe c’umubiri, coba ari ciza cane co kurya nk’ica ntare (ico kibonwa ko ari ifunguro ridasanzwe), iyo kitaba ari piramide ikomeye nk’iyo y’ibinure. Ariko spermaceti ubwayo, ese ukuntu ari umunyu kandi ari nk’umunyu; nk’inyama y’igiti c’umukaraba ibonerana, ifise igice c’umunyu, yera mu kwezi kwa gatatu ikuze, yamara ikaba ari itunze cane ku buryo idashobora gutanga ico isubirira amavuta y’ibitoke. Naho ari ukwo, abaroba baleine benshi barafise uburyo bwo kuyinywa mu kindi kintu, hanyuma bakayisangira. Mu masaha maremare y’ijoro, ni ikintu gisanzwe ko abasare babika ibisuguti vyabo vy’ubwato mu nkono nini z’amavuta maze bakabireka bikagateka umwanya mutoyi. Nateguye gutyo ifunguro ryiza ryinshi ry’umugoroba.

Ku bijanye n’agakoko gatoyi k’intanga ubwonko bufatwa nk’ifunguro ryiza cane. Isanduku y’igihanga iramenagurwa n’ishoka, hanyuma izo nkoko zibiri zikomeye kandi zitukura zigakurwamwo (zisa cane n’ibifungurwa bibiri bininibinini), hanyuma zikavangwa n’ifu, zigatekwa zigacika umunyu uryoshe cane, mu buryo bumwe bumwe busa n’umutwe w’inyana, ivyo bikaba bimeze nk’ibifungurwa bimwe bimwe; kandi umuntu wese arazi ko hariho abana b’inka bo mu ba epicure, mu gukomeza kurya ubwonko bw’inyana, bukebuke bagaca bagira ubwonko butoyi bwabo bwite, kugira ngo bashobore gutandukanya umutwe w’inyana n’imitwe yabo bwite; ivyo, vy’ukuri, bisaba ivangura ridasanzwe. Kandi ni co gituma akagazi k’inyana gafise umutwe w’inyana usa n’uw’ubwenge imbere yako, mu buryo bumwe canke ubundi ari kimwe mu bintu bibabaje cane ushobora kubona. Umutwe uramuraba nk’uwumutuka, ukaba uvuga ngo “Et tu Brute!” imvugo.

Kumbure si kubera gusa iyo balene igira amavuta menshi cane ku buryo abanyagihugu basa n’ababona ko kuyirya ari ikintu giteye isoni; ivyo bisa n’ibiva, mu buryo bumwe, ku vyiyumviro vyavuzwe imbere: ni ukuvuga ko umuntu yorya ikintu c’ikiyaga gishasha cishwe, na co nyene akakirya n’umuco waco bwite. Ariko nta gukeka ko umuntu wa mbere yigeze kwica inka yabonwa ko ari umwicanyi; kumbure yari yamanitswe; kandi iyo ashirwa ku kigeragezo ciwe n’inka, nta gukeka ko yari kugeragezwa; kandi nta gukeka ko yari akwiye kubigira nimba hari umwicanyi yobigira. Genda mw’isoko ry’inyama ryo ku musi wa gatandatu mw’ijoro maze ubone amasinzi y’ibikoko vy’amaguru abiri bifise ubuzima bihanganye n’imirongo miremire y’ibikoko vy’amaguru ane vyapfuye. None ivyo bintu umuntu abona ntibikura iryinyo mu rwasaya rw’uwo murya abantu? Abarya abantu? ni nde atari umunyanyama? Ndababwiye ko bizoba vyihanganirwa cane ku ba Fejee bateye umunyu umumisiyonari w’umunyamibabaro mu nzu yiwe yo munsi y’ubutaka kubera inzara iriko iraza; bizoba vyoroshe kuri uwo Fejee w’umunyavyiyumviro, ndavuga, ku musi w’urubanza, kuruta wewe, umunyamideri w’umunyabwenge kandi w’umunyabwenge, utera imisumari hasi inzovu, ugafungura ku bihimba vyazo vy’umubiri bivyimvye mu paté-de-foie-gras yawe.

Ariko Stubb, arya iyo balene ku muco wayo bwite, si ko? kandi ivyo ni kwongera igitutsi ku gikomere, si ko? Raba igikoresho cawe co gufata imbugita, ng’aho, umunyabwenge wanje w’umunyabwenge kandi w’umuco ariko arafungura iyo nyama yokeje, iyo nkoko ikozwe mu ki?—ikihe atari amagufa ya mwenewabo w’iyo nka nyene uriko urarya? Kandi amenyo yawe utoragura iki, umaze kurya iyo nkoko y’umuvyibuho? N’amababa y’iyo nkoko nyene. Kandi ni uruhe rupapuro umunyamabanga w’ishirahamwe ry’uguhagarika ububisha kuri Ganders yakoresheje mu buryo butegekanijwe amakete yiwe? Mu kwezi kumwe canke bibiri biheze ni ho gusa iyo sosiyete yafata ingingo yo kutagira ikindi ishigikira atari amakaramu y’icuma.





UMUGAMBI WA 66. Ubwicanyi bwa Shark.

Iyo mu Burovyi bwo mu Bumanuko, baleine y’intanga yafashwe, inyuma y’ugukora cane kandi irushe, izanywe iruhande mw’ijoro ryinshi, nk’ikintu rusangi n’imiburiburi, si umugenzo guca ubwo nyene mu gikorwa co kuyikata. Kuko ico gikorwa ari igikorwa gikomeye cane; ntiheza vuba cane; kandi bisaba amaboko yose kugira ngo abikore. Ku bw’ivyo, ikoreshwa rusangi ni ugufata ubwato bwose; n’ugukubita umugozi a’lee; hanyuma murungike umwe wese ari musi ku nzira yiwe gushika ku mutaga, muzigamye ko, gushika ico gihe, abarinzi b’inanga bazogumaho; ni ukuvuga babiri n’ababiri mu kiringo c’isaha, umubano umwe wese, abakozi bariko barazunguruka bazoduga ku gitereko kugira ngo barabe ko vyose bigenda neza.

Ariko rimwe na rimwe, cane cane ku Murongo wo muri Pasifike, uwo mugambi ntuzokwishura na gato; kuko mwene izo ngabo zitagira uko zingana z’ibikoko vyitwa shark zikoranira hamwe zikikuje uwo muvyimba uboshe, iyo asigara gutyo amasaha atandatu, tuvuge ko ari ku ruhande rumwe, nta kintu gitoyi cobaye kibonetse mu gitondo. Ariko rero, mu bindi bice vyinshi vyo mu kiyaga, aho izo fi zitari nyinshi cane, rimwe na rimwe uburyarya bwazo butangaje burashobora kugabanywa cane, mu kuzivyura n’inguvu nyinshi n’ibikoresho vy’ugutera baleine, naho nyene ivyo bikaba bimeze nk’ibituma zirushiriza gukora cane. Ariko rero, ntivyari bimeze gutyo mu gihe c’ubu ku bijanye n’ibikoko vyitwa sharks vy’i Pequod; naho nyene, kugira ngo tubone neza, umugabo uwo ari we wese atamenyereye ivyo bintu, iyo yaravye hejuru y’uruhande rwiwe muri iryo joro, yari hafi kwiyumvira ko ikiyaga cose gikikuje cari iforomaje imwe nini cane, kandi izo nkoko zitwa shark zikaba ari izo nkoko ziri muri co.

Naho ari ukwo, igihe Stubb yashiraho nanga y’ibarabara inyuma y’aho ifunguro ryiwe ry’umugoroba rirangiriye; kandi igihe, bivanye n’ivyo, Queequeg n’umusare wo mu bwato bwa forecastle baza ku bwato, nta munezero mutoyi wavutse mu bikoko vy’amafi; kuko abo basore babiri baciye bahagarika ubwo nyene inzira zo gucapura hejuru y’uruhande, maze bagamanukana amatara atatu, ku buryo yatanga umuco muremure ku kiyaga gifise umuvurungano, batera amabuye yabo maremare yo gutera baleine, bagumye bica izo nkoko zitwa shark * ata guhagarara, bagatera igice c’ibihanga vyazo gusa c’icuma gikomeye cane. Ariko mu rujijo rw’amafu rw’abashitsi babo bavanze kandi bariko bararwana, abatera amabuye ntibashobora kwama batera ikimenyetso cabo; kandi ivyo vyatumye habaho amahishurirwa mashasha y’ubukazi budasanzwe bw’umwansi. Baca batera amabuye y’ubukazi, atari ku bihimba vy’umubiri vy’uwundi gusa, ahubwo bari nk’imiheto ishobora guhinduka, bapfukama, bagaruma ivyabo; gushika aho ayo mara asa n’ayamirwa incuro nyinshi n’akanwa kamwe, akagira ubusa mu buryo bunyuranye n’ico gikomere cari gipfutse. Eka mbere ivyo ntivyari vyose. Ntivyari vyiza kwivanga mu mivyimba n’imizimu y’ivyo bikoko. Ubwoko bw’ubuzima rusangi canke bw’Imana yose bwasa n’ubuhishije mu ngingo zabo nyene no mu magufa yabo, inyuma y’aho ico twokwita ubuzima bw’umuntu ku giti ciwe kivuyeho. Imwe muri izo nkoko yishwe n’uguterwa hejuru y’ubwato kubera urukoba rwiwe, yari hafi gukura ukuboko kw’umukene Queequeg, igihe yariko agerageza gufunga igipfukisho capfuye c’urwaya rwiwe rw’umwicanyi.

*Igikoresho co gutera baleine gikoreshwa mu gucapura gikozwe mu cuma ciza cane; ni ivyerekeye ubunini bw’ukuboko kw’umuntu kwambuye; kandi mu buryo rusangi, bihuye n’igikoresho co mw’itongo citiriwe; impande zaco gusa ni zo ziringaniye ata gatotsi, kandi impera yaco yo hejuru irake cane kuruta iyo hasi. Ico kirwanisho cama kigumye giteye akaga uko bishoboka kwose; kandi iyo ikoreshejwe rimwe na rimwe irayitoza, nk’inzobe. Mu gitereko caco, hashirwamwo igiti gikomeye, kuva ku metero mirongo ibiri gushika ku metero mirongo itatu, kugira ngo kibe igikoresho co gufata.

Uwo munyamaswa yavuze ati: “Queequeg ntaco yitwararika imana yamugize shark,” aca aduza ukuboko kwiwe hejuru no hasi mu buryo bubabaje; “Imana y’ubugeni yitwa Fejee canke imana ya Nantucket; ariko imana yitwa shark itegerezwa kuba ari urugomero rumwe Ingin.”
