SABABU 89. Jɛgɛ teliyalen ni jɛgɛ lafiyalen.

Waif ni waif-polew kofɔli sapitiri laban na nka kelen, o bɛ wajibiya ka balema mɔni sariyaw ni sariyakolo dɔw fɔ, waif bɛ se ka jate minnu taamasiyɛnba ni badge ye.

A ka c’a la, ni kurun caman bɛ ka taama ɲɔgɔn fɛ, balema dɔ bɛ se ka gosi kurun kelen fɛ, o kɔfɛ, a bɛ boli, ka laban ka faga ka minɛ kurun wɛrɛ fɛ; ani yan, ko misɛnnin caman bɛ kɛ cogo la min tɛ ɲɛ, minnu bɛɛ bɛ nin koba kelen in na. Misali la,—sɛgɛn ni faratilen kɔfɛ balema dɔ nɔfɛtaama n’a minɛni kɔfɛ, a su bɛ se ka bɔ kurun kɔnɔ sanfiɲɛba dɔ kosɔn; ani ka taa yɔrɔ jan ka taa fiɲɛ fɛ, ka segin ka minɛ balemakɛla filanan fɛ, min, a hakili sigilen na, a b’a sama ka taa kɛrɛfɛ, k’a sɔrɔ farati tɛ a ka ɲɛnamaya walima layini na. O cogo la, sɔsɔli minnu bɛ mɔgɔ jɔrɔ kosɛbɛ ani minnu ka jugu kosɛbɛ, olu tun bɛna kɛ tuma caman na mɔnikɛlaw ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ, ni sariya sɛbɛnnen walima sɛbɛnbali, diɲɛ bɛɛ ta, sɔsɔli tɛ min na, n’o bɛ tali kɛ ko bɛɛ la.

Laala, balema ɲinini sariya kelen min tun yamaruyara sariyasunba fɛ, o tun ye Hollande ta ye. A latigɛra jamana-jamanaw fɛ san 1695. Nka hali ni jamana wɛrɛ si ma deli ka balema-ɲini sariya sɛbɛn sɔrɔ, o bɛɛ n’a ta, Ameriki mɔnikɛlaw kɛra u yɛrɛ ka sariyasunbatigiw ni sariyatigiw ye o ko in na. U ye sigida dɔ di min bɛ tɛmɛn Justinian ka Pandects ni Sinuwa jamana ka sariyaw kan minnu bɛ u sen don mɔgɔw wɛrɛw ka jago la. Awɔ; o sariyaw bɛ se ka sɛbɛn Masamuso Anne ka farthing kan, walima harpoon barb kan, ka don u kɔ la, u ka dɔgɔ kosɛbɛ.

I. A Fast-Fish ye parti fast ka a ta ye.

II. Loose-Fish ye tulon tilennen ye mɔgɔ o mɔgɔ fɛ min bɛ se k’a minɛ joona.

Nka min bɛ juguya Kɛ ni nin seko ni dɔnko sariyasun in ye, o ye a surunyacogo ye min bɛ Tanu, min b’a To ɲɛfɔli camanba ka kan ka Kɛ walasa k’a ɲɛfɔ.

Fɔlɔ: Jɛgɛ teliyalen ye mun ye? Jɛgɛ ɲɛnama walima a salen bɛ teliya fɛɛrɛko siratigɛ la, n’a sirilen don ni kurun walima kurun minɛlen ye, ni fɛɛrɛ o fɛɛrɛ ye min bɛ se ka kɔlɔsi hali dɔɔnin a kɔnɔmɔgɔ walima a kɔnɔmɔgɔw fɛ,—masti, kurunba, kabɔri min janya ye santimɛtɛrɛ kɔnɔntɔn ye, telefɔni nɛgɛ, walima nɛgɛso jiribolo dɔ, o bɛɛ ye kelen ye. O cogo kelen na jɛgɛ bɛ teliya fɛɛrɛko siratigɛ la n’a bɛ waif ta, walima taamasiyɛn wɛrɛ min lakodɔnnen don a bolo; fo n’a y’a sɔrɔ ko parti min b’a waif, o b’a jira k’a jɛya k’u bɛ se waati o waati k’a ta k’a kɛ kɛrɛfɛ, ka fara u ka laɲini kan k’o kɛ.

Olu ye dɔnniya ɲɛfɔliw ye; nka balemakɛlaw yɛrɛ ka ɲɛfɔliw bɛ kɛ tuma dɔw la kuma gɛlɛnw ni gosili gɛlɛnw na— Coke-upon-Littleton of the fist. Tiɲɛ don, balemakɛla tilennenw ni bonyalenw cɛma, dɛmɛ bɛ di tuma bɛɛ ko kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw kama, yɔrɔ minnu na, a bɛ kɛ jogoɲumanya tilenbaliya ye min bɛ mɔgɔ dimi kosɛbɛ, n’a y’a fɔ ko balema dɔ b’a bolo, mɔgɔ wɛrɛ ye min gɛn ka kɔrɔ walima k’a faga. Nka dɔw fana tɛ u janto u yɛrɛ la ten cogo si la.

A bɛ san bi duuru bɔ sisan, ko kabakoma dɔ kɛra balema-ɲini-ko dɔ la min kɛra Angletɛri, o kɔnɔ, kiiritigɛlaw y’a jira ko balema dɔ nɔfɛtaama gɛlɛn kɔfɛ Worodugu kɔgɔjiw la ani tuma min na tiɲɛ na u (kiiritigɛlaw) tun sera ka jɛgɛ in ni harpoon ye; u labanna, u ka ɲɛnamaya farati fɛ, wajibiyara k’u ka layiniw dɔrɔn to yen, nka u ka kurun yɛrɛ fana to yen. A laban na, jalakilenw (kurun wɛrɛ baarakɛlaw) nana ni balema ye, k’a gosi, k’a faga, k’a minɛ, ka laban k’a ta kiiritigɛlaw ɲɛ yɛrɛ la. Wa, ni o jalakilenw sɔsɔra, u ka kapitɛni y’a bolokɔnincininw tigɛ kiiritigɛlaw ɲinw na, k’u hakili sigi ko doxology sira fɛ a ye min kɛ, a bɛna u ka layini, u ka bɔrɔw ani kurunba mara sisan, minnu tun nɔrɔlen bɛ balema na minɛni waati la. O de kama sisan, kiiritigɛlaw ye kiritigɛ kɛ walasa k’u ka balema, layini, harpoon ani kurun nafa sɔrɔ.

Cɛ Erskine tun ye jalakidenw ka ladilikɛla ye; Lord Ellenborough tun ye kiritigɛla ye. Lafasali senfɛ, Erskine hakilitigi taara a jɔyɔrɔ jira, a kɛtɔ ka kuma kojuguba dɔ kan min kɛra kɔsa in na. con. ko min na, cɛkɔrɔba dɔ, a kɛlen kɔ k’a ɲini fu k’a muso ka juguya kunbɛn, a laban na, a y’a bila ɲɛnamaya kɔgɔjiw kan; nka sanw tɛmɛnen kɔfɛ, a nimisalen o senna, a ye wale dɔ daminɛ walasa k’a ta kokura. Erskine tun bɛ fan dɔ fɛ; o kɔfɛ, a y’a dɛmɛ k’a fɔ ko hali ni cɛkɔrɔba in tun ye muso in siri ni nɛgɛ ye fɔlɔ, wa a tun y’a sunɔgɔ siɲɛ kelen, wa a ka juguya min tun bɛ ka jigin, o degunba dɔrɔn de kosɔn, a laban na, a y’a bila o bɛɛ n'a ta, a y'a bila a y'o kɛ, fo a kɛra jɛgɛ lafiyalen ye; o de kama, ni cɛkɔrɔba dɔ min tugura o kɔ, o y’a siri kokura, o kɔfɛ, muso in kɛra o cɛkɔrɔba nata ka nafolo ye, ka fara bɔrɔ fɛn o fɛn bɛ se ka sɔrɔ a nɔrɔlen bɛ a la.

Sisan, nin ko in na, Erskine y’a jira ko balema ni muso ka misaliw ye ɲɔgɔn jira ɲɔgɔn na.

O deliliw, ani counter deliliw, n’u mɛnna ka ɲɛ, kiritigɛla kalanden yɛrɛ de y’a latigɛ cogo latigɛlenw na, n’o ye,—Ko kurun ta fan fɛ, a y’a di kiiritigɛlaw ma, bawo u y’a bila dɔrɔn walasa k’u ka ɲɛnamaya kisi nka ko ka ɲɛsin balema sɔsɔli ma, harpoonw ani line ma, u tun ye jalakilenw ta ye; balema, bawo a tun ye Loose-Fish ye a minɛ laban waati la; ani harpoonw ni layini bawo ni jɛgɛ ye taa n’u ye, a (jɛgɛ) ye nafolo sɔrɔ o fɛnw na; o de y'a To mɔgɔ o mɔgɔ ye jɛgɛ in Tà o kɔ fɛ, o tigi tùn ka kan n'u ye. Sisan, jalakidenw ye jɛgɛ ta o kɔfɛ; ergo, barokun minnu fɔra ka tɛmɛ, olu tun ye u ta ye.

Mɔgɔ gansan min bɛ Kiiritigɛla kalanbagaba ka nin desizɔn in lajɛ, n’a sɔrɔla a bɛ se ka sɔsɔli kɛ o la. Nka n’a sɛnɛna fo ka se ko in fara fɔlɔ ma, sariyakoloba fila minnu sigilen don balema filaninw ɲinini sariya kɔnɔ minnu fɔra ka tɛmɛ, ani minnu waleyara ani k’u ɲɛfɔ Lord Ellenborough fɛ o ko fɔlen in na sanfɛ nin sariya fila minnu bɛ maga Fast-Fish ni Loose-Fish la, n b’a fɔ, n’u ye miiri, olu bɛna sɔrɔ hadamadenw ka sariyako bɛɛ jusigilanw ye; sabu hali n’a ka jagokɛcogo gɛlɛn don, Sariya Alabatoso, i n’a fɔ Filisitikaw ka Alabatoso, fɛn fila dɔrɔn de b’a bolo ka jɔ a kan.

Yala kuma tɛ mɔgɔ bɛɛ da la ko: Fɛn min bɛ mɔgɔ bolo, o ye sariya tilancɛ ye: o kɔrɔ ye ko fɛn mana kɛ fɛn min ye, o mana kɛ cogo o cogo wa? Nka tuma caman na, fɛntigiya ye sariya bɛɛ ye. Irisi jɔnw ni Repibiliki jɔnw farikolo ni u niw ye mun ye ni Fast-Fish tɛ, min sɔrɔli ye sariya bɛɛ ye? Mun ye sotigi jatigɛwalekɛla fɛ, o ye musocɛ salen ka wulu laban ye nka Fast-Fish? Fɛn min bɛ yen, mɔgɔ juguw ka marbre soba min ma dɔn ni da-plati ye waif kama; o ye mun ye ni Fast-Fish tɛ? Mordese, n’o ye dilanbaga ye, o bɛ jigitigɛ tiɲɛnen min sɔrɔ Woebegone faantan fɛ, banki fililikɛla, juru dɔ kan walasa ka Woebegone ka denbaya tanga kɔngɔ ma, o ye mun ye; o jigitigɛ tiɲɛnen ye mun ye ni Fast-Fish tɛ? Savesoul Episkɔpuba ka sɔrɔ ye mun ye, n’o ye £100.000 ye, min minɛna baarakɛla ba kɛmɛ caman ka buru ni foromazi dɔgɔyalen na, minnu kɔ karilen don (u bɛɛ dalen bɛ sankolo la k’a sɔrɔ Savesoul ka dɛmɛ si ma kɛ a la) o £100.000 globular ye mun ye ni Fast-Fish tɛ? Dunder Dunder ka ciyɛn duguw ni dugumisɛnw ye jumɛnw ye ni Fast-Fish tɛ? O harpooneer redoubted, John Bull, mun ye Irlandi faantan ye, nka Fast-Fish? O ciden ka nɛgɛsotigi, balimakɛ Jonathan, ye Texas ye nka Fast-Fish ye mun ye? Ninnu bɛɛ ko la, yala Possession tɛ sariya bɛɛ ye wa?

Nka ni Fast-Fish kalansira bɛ se ka waleya kosɛbɛ, Loose-Fish kalansira min bɛ tali kɛ ɲɔgɔn na, o bɛ ka caya hali bi. O bɛ tali kɛ diɲɛ kɔnɔ ani diɲɛ bɛɛ kɔnɔ.

Ameriki tun ye mun ye san 1492 ni Loose-Fish tɛ, min kɔnɔ Kolonbɔ ye Ɛsipaɲi ka sariyasun gosi a kɛtɔ k’a dabila a ka masakɛ makɛ n’a matigimuso ye? Pologne tun ye mun ye Zar fɛ? Gɛrɛsi jumɛn ka Turki? Ɛndujamana jumɛn ka taa Angletɛri? A laban na, Mɛkisiki bɛna kɛ mun ye Ameriki fɛ? Bɛɛ Loose-Fish.

Hadamaden ka josariyaw ni diɲɛ hɔrɔnyaw ye mun ye ni Loose-Fish tɛ? Cɛw bɛɛ hakili ni u hakilinaw ye mun ye ni Loose-Fish tɛ? Diinan dannaya sariyakolo jumɛn bɛ u la ni Jɛgɛ Lajɔlen tɛ? Mun ye kumalasela ŋanaw fɛ minnu bɛ u yɛrɛ jira, o ye miirilikɛlaw ka miiriliw ye nka Loose-Fish? Globe belebele yɛrɛ ye mun ye ni Loose-Fish tɛ? Wa i ye mun ye, kalanden, ni Loose-Fish ni Fast-Fish tɛ, i fana?





SABABU 90. Kungolow walima Kuluw.

“De balena vero bɛ se, si rex habeat kaput, et regina caudam.” Brakton, l. 3, c. 3. 3. .

Latinkan min bɔra Angletɛri sariyaw gafew kɔnɔ, n’u taara ni a kɔnɔkow ye, o kɔrɔ ye, ko balema minnu bɛɛ minɛna mɔgɔ o mɔgɔ fɛ o jamana kɔgɔjida la, Masakɛ, i n’a fɔ Honorary Grand Harpooneer, kunkolo ka kan ka kɛ a la, wa Masamuso ka kan ka kɛ ni bonya ye ni a kɔ ye. Tilali min, balema na, o bɛ i n’a fɔ pɔmi tilancɛ; cɛmancɛ tolen tɛ yen. Sisan, i n’a fɔ nin sariya in, cogo min na, a bɛ baara la fo ka na se bi ma Angilɛ jamana na; ani ikomi a bɛ anomali kabakoma dɔ di cogo caman na min bɛ maga Fast and Loose-Fish sariyaba la, a ɲɛfɔlen bɛ yan sapitiri danfaralen dɔ kɔnɔ, o sariyakolo kelen in kan min bɛ Angilɛw ka nɛgɛso bila u ka kɛ mobili danfaralen musaka la, min bilalen bɛ kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la masakɛw ka sigiyɔrɔ kama. A fɔlɔ la, k’a jira ko sariya min kofɔra sanfɛ, o bɛ baara la hali bi, n bɛ taa a fɛ ka ko dɔ bila aw ɲɛkɔrɔ, min kɛra san fila tɛmɛnenw kɔnɔ.

A bɛ iko Dover, walima Sandwich, walima Cinque Ports dɔ, kurun bolila lakika dɔw, u tun ye balema ɲuman dɔ faga ani k’a bila kɔgɔjida la, u tun ye min ɲɛfɔ fɔlɔ, yɔrɔ jan kɔgɔjida la. Sisan, Cinque Ports yɔrɔ dɔ walima cogo dɔ la polisikɛla walima biɲɛ sugu dɔ ka fanga kɔrɔ, n’o bɛ wele ko Lord Warden. Ka biro minɛ k’a ɲɛsin masafugulan ma, ne dalen b’a la ko masakɛ ka sarako bɛɛ min kɛra ka taa Cinque Port maraw la, o bɛ kɛ a ta ye baara fɛ. Sɛbɛnnikɛla dɔw fɛ o biro in bɛ Weele ko sinɛkɔrɔ. Nka o tɛ ten. Sabula Lord Warden bolo degunnen don tuma dɔw la a ka perquisites fobbing na; minnu ye a ta ye kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la o fobbing kelen in sababu fɛ u la.

Sisan, tuma min na nin kɔgɔjidala faantan minnu jeni tile fɛ, sennasanbaraw, n’u ka pantalonw wulilen don sanfɛ u sen nɔgɔlenw kan, u tun sɛgɛnnen don k’u ka jɛgɛ tulumaw sama ka taa sanfɛ ani ka ja, ka layidu ta u yɛrɛ ye ko u bɛna £150 ɲuman sɔrɔ tulu ni kolo nafama na ani miiriyajugu la, ka te min man ca n’u musow ye, ani ale ɲuman min n’u teriw ye, u kelen-kelen bɛɛ ka niyɔrɔ fanga kan; up steps cɛkɔrɔba kalanlenba ani Krecɛn ni dɛmɛnbaa dɔ, ni Blackstone kopi dɔ bɛ a bolo kɔrɔ; k’a da balema kunkolo kan, a ko—“Bolo bɔ! nin jɛgɛ in, ne matigiw, ye Jɛgɛ ye min bɛ teliya. Ne b’a minɛ i n’a fɔ Matigi Kɔlɔsilikɛla ta.” O kan, kurunbolilaw faantanw b’u ka bonya kabakoman kɔnɔ — tubabukan tiɲɛnen don ten — k’a sɔrɔ u t’a dɔn u bɛna min fɔ, u bɛ bin k’u kunkolo sɔgɔ ni fanga ye u lamini bɛɛ la; o waati kelen na, a bɛ ka ɲɛmajɔli kɛ ka bɔ balema na ka taa dunan fɛ. Nka o ma ko ɲɛnabɔ cogo si la, walima ka cɛkɔrɔba kalanlen dusukun gɛlɛn nɔgɔya hali dɔɔnin ni Blackstone kopi ye. A laban na, u dɔ, a kɛlen kɔ k’a ka hakilinaw sɛgɛsɛgɛ kabini tuma jan, a y’a jagɛlɛya ka kuma,

“N b’i deli, setigi, Matigi Kɔlɔsili ye jɔn ye?”

“Duke” ye.

“Nka dukɛ tun tɛ foyi kɛ ni jɛgɛ in ta ye wa?”

“A ta don.”

“An ye gɛlɛyaba sɔrɔ, ani faratiba la, ani musaka dɔw la, wa yala o bɛɛ bɛ taa Duke nafa la wa, an tɛ foyi sɔrɔ fewu an ka dimiw la ni an ka kurukuruw tɛ wa?”

“A ta don.”

“Yala Duke ye faantan ye kosɛbɛ fo a bɛ wajibiya ka nin balo sɔrɔcogo jigitigɛlen in kɛ wa?”

“A ta don.”

“Ne y’a miiri ka n ba kɔrɔ lafiya min tun bɛ dilan kan, n ka niyɔrɔ dɔ fɛ nin balema in na.”

“A ta don.”

“Duke tɛna wasa ni tilayɔrɔba kelen walima tila ye wa?”

“A ta don.”

Kuma kelen na, balema in minɛna ka feere, wa a ka Grace the Duke of Wellington ye wari sɔrɔ. Dugu diinɛ ɲɛmɔgɔ sɛbɛ dɔ y’a miiri ko n’i y’a lajɛ yeelen kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen dɔw la, o ko bɛ se ka jate ko gɛlɛn dɔ la, cogo fitinin dɔ la, ko a ka gɛlɛn dɔɔnin, dugu diinɛ ɲɛmɔgɔ sɛbɛ dɔ ye sɛbɛn dɔ ci a ka Grace ma ni bonya ye, k’a deli ko a k’o kurunbolilaw ka ko kɛlenw jateminɛ kosɛbɛ. O la, n Matigi Duke y’a jaabi (sɛbɛn fila bɛɛ bɔra) ko ale y’o kɛ kaban, wa a ye wari sɔrɔ, wa a bɛna wajibiya cɛkɔrɔba bonyalen in ma ni siniɲɛsigi kama ale (cɛkɔrɔba bonyalen) bɛna ban ka a sen don mɔgɔ wɛrɛw ka jago la. Yala nin ye cɛkɔrɔba ye min bɛ kɛlɛ kɛ hali bi, min jɔlen bɛ masaya saba kɔnɔna na, a bolo bɛɛ la ka delilikɛlaw wajibiya ka saraka di wa?

A bɛna ye nɔgɔya la ko nin ko in na, Duke ka jo min tun bɛ fɔ balema na, o tun ye jo ye min tun bilalen bɛ jamanakuntigi fɛ. An ka kan ka ɲininkali kɛ o tuma na sariyakolo jumɛn kan, Masakɛ in bilala o jo in na fɔlɔ. Sariya yɛrɛ fɔra kaban. Nka Plowdon b’o kun di an ma. Plowdon ko balema min minɛna o cogo la, o ye Masakɛ ni Masamuso ta ye, “k’a sababu kɛ a ka ɲɛtaa ka bon kosɛbɛ.” Wa, ɲɛfɔlikɛla ɲumanw fɛ, nin kɛra sɔsɔli jɛlen ye o ko suguw la.

Nka mun na Masakɛ ka kan ka kunkolo sɔrɔ, Masamuso ka kan ka kɛ kɔ ye? O kun dɔ don, aw sariyatigiw!

A ka sɛbɛn kɔnɔ min bɛ kuma “Queen-Gold” kan, walima Queen-pinmoney kan, Masakɛ ka Bench sɛbɛnnikɛla kɔrɔ dɔ, William Prynne dɔ, ye kuma fɔ nin cogo la: “Aw kɔ ye Masamuso ta ye, walasa aw Masamuso ka finidoncogo ka sɔrɔ ni aw balema kolo ye.” Sisan nin sɛbɛnna waati min na Gronland walima Right balema kolo nɛrɛma limber tun bɛ kɛ kosɛbɛ musow ka bodices kɔnɔ. Nka o kolo kelen in tɛ kùnkolo la; a bɛ kun na, o ye fili dusukasilen ye avoka hakilitigi fɛ i n’a fɔ Prynne. Nka yala Masamuso ye sirɛn ye, ka jira ni kɔ ye wa? Mankutu dɔ bɛ se ka dogo yan.

Masakɛ jɛgɛ fila bɛ yen, Angilɛw ka sariyasɛbɛnnaw ye minnu kɛ o cogo la, olu ye balema ni jɛgɛ ye min bɛ wele ko sturgeon; o fila bɛɛ ye masakɛ ka nafolo ye dan dɔw kɔnɔ, ani tɔgɔ la ka masafugulan ka sɔrɔ gansan bolo tannan di. N t’a dɔn ko sɛbɛnnikɛla wɛrɛ si ye hakilina di o ko in kan; nka n’i y’a jateminɛ, a bɛ n ɲɛ na ko jirisunba ka kan ka tila cogo kelen na ni balema ye, Masakɛ bɛ kunkolo min ka bon kosɛbɛ ani min bɛ se ka wuli, o min ye o jɛgɛ danma ye, n’a jatera taamasyɛn ye, n’a sɔrɔla o bɛ se ka kɛ nisɔndiyako ye ka da fɛn dɔw kan minnu bɛ miiri ko u bɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ. Wa o cogo la, a bɛ iko dalilu bɛ fɛn bɛɛ la, hali sariya la.





SABABU 91. Pequod bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ ni Rose-Bud ye.

“Fun a kɛra ka rake kɛ Ambergriese ye nin Leviathan in paunch kɔnɔ, inseverable fetor denying not inquiry.” Sir T. Browne, V. E., ani a ye baara kɛ
