An tun ye kuma min fɔ, kun min na Akabu ma don kurunba kɔnɔ, o tun ye nin ye: fiɲɛ ni kɔgɔji tun bɛ fɔɲɔba jira. Nka hali n’o tun tɛ, a bɛɛ kɔfɛ, laala, a tun tɛna don a kɔnɔ — n’an y’a jateminɛ a ka kɛwale kɔfɛ o ko suguw la — n’o tun kɛra ko, tanuli kɛcogo fɛ, a ye jaabi jugu sɔrɔ a ye ɲininkali min kɛ. Bari, i n’a fɔ a labanna ka kɛ cogo min na, a y’a janto a kana jɛɲɔgɔnya kɛ, hali miniti duuru kɔnɔ, ni kapitɛni dunan si ye, fo n’a sɔrɔla a tun bɛ se k’o kunnafoni dɔw di a tun bɛ minnu ɲini ni kɔnɔnafili ye. Nka nin bɛɛ bɛ se ka to jateminɛbaliya la, ni fɛn dɔ tun ma fɔ yan balema-kurunw ka baarakɛcogo danmadɔw kan n’u bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ jamana wɛrɛw ka kɔgɔjiw la, ani kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la kurunbokari-yɔrɔ kelen na.

Ni dunan fila bɛ Pine Barrens tigɛ New York mara la, walima Salisbury Plain min lakolon don o cogo kelen na Angletɛri; ni u bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ k’a sɔrɔ u ma kɛ cogo la o kungo suguw la minnu tɛ jatigila ye, nin fila ninnu, u ka ɲɛnamaya kama, u tɛ se ka u yɛrɛ tanga ɲɔgɔn foli ma kosɛbɛ; ani ka jɔ dɔɔnin walasa ka kibaruyaw ɲɔgɔn falen-falen; ani, laala, ka sigi waati dɔ kɔnɔ ka lafiɲɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ: o tuma na, a bɛ kɛ cogo min na, o ka fisa kosɛbɛ ni kɔgɔji dan tɛ Pine Barrens ni Salisbury Plains kan, balema-kurun fila minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn ɲɛfɔ dugukolo labanw na — Fanning’s Island kelen na, walima King’s Mills yɔrɔ jan na cogo min na, n b’a fɔ ko a bɛ kɛ cogo min na, ko o cogo la, nin kurun ninnu man kan ka sanbɛlɛw ɲɔgɔn falen-falen dɔrɔn, nka u ka kan ka na jɛɲɔgɔnya surun na hali bi, teriya la ani jɛɲɔgɔnya la. Wa kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la, a bɛna kɛ i n’a fɔ nin ye ko ye min bɛ kɛ, kurun minnu bɛ kɔgɔjida la, ani minnu ka kapitɛniw, sɔrɔdasi ɲɛmɔgɔw, ani cɛ damadɔw tɛ ɲɔgɔn dɔn u yɛrɛ fɛ ani o de kosɔn, ka sokɔnɔfɛn kanulen sugu bɛɛ sɔrɔ ka kuma u kan.

Kurun min tɛ yen kabini tuma jan, kɛnɛma-dancɛ, n’a sɔrɔla, batakiw bɛ a kɔnɔ; a mana kɛ cogo o cogo, a bɛna a to a ka don dɔ sɛbɛn dɔw sɔrɔ san kelen walima san fila kɔfɛ ka tɛmɛ laban kan a ka dosiyew kan minnu ma jɛya ani minnu bɛ don a bolokɔnincininw na. Wa o bonya in musaka la, kurun min bɛ taa kɛnɛma, o tun bɛna balema ɲinini hakilina labanw sɔrɔ ka bɔ kurunbokari yɔrɔ la, a bɛ se ka taa yɔrɔ min na, o ye fɛn ye min nafa ka bon a bolo kosɛbɛ. Wa, hakɛ dɔ la, nin bɛɛ bɛna kɛ tiɲɛ ye balema-kurunw ko la minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn ka sira tigɛ kurunbokari-yɔrɔ yɛrɛ kan, hali n’u tɛ so kɔnɔ kabini tuma jan o cogo kelen na. Sabu u dɔ la kelen bɛ se ka batakiw cili sɔrɔ ka bɔ kurun sabanan dɔw la, ani sisan min yɔrɔ ka jan kurunba la; ani o bataki dɔw bɛ se ka kɛ kurun kɔnɔmɔgɔw ta ye a bɛ min kunbɛn sisan. O tɛmɛnen 'kɔ, u tùn bɛ balema-ɲini kibaruyaw ɲɔgɔn falen-falen, ka baro Kɛ ni bɛnkan ye. Sabu u tun tɛna ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ dɔrɔn ni kurunbolilaw ka hinɛ bɛɛ ye, nka o cogo kelen na, u tun bɛna ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ ni nisɔndiya kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw bɛɛ ye minnu bɛ bɔ ɲɔgɔn ka ɲinini na ani ɲɔgɔn ka dɛsɛw ni faratiw la.

Jamana danfara fana tɛna danfara nafamaba si don; o kɔrɔ ye ko ni mɔgɔ fila bɛɛ bɛ kan kelen fɔ, i n’a fɔ a bɛ kɛ cogo min na Amerikikaw ni tubabukan na. Nka nin ye foyi fitinin ye min tɛ kojugu kɛ tubabu balema-ɲininaw la, Nantucketer tɛ min ta kosɛbɛ a dusukun na; n’a sɔrɔla, bawo a b’a dɔn ko dɛsɛ damadɔ b’a yɛrɛ la.

O la sa, an b’a ye ko kurunw bɛɛ la minnu bɛ taa kɔgɔji la u danma, kun caman bɛ balemakɛlaw bolo ka kɛ mɔgɔw fɛ—wa u bɛ ten.
