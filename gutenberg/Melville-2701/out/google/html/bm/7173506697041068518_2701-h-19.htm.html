Jusigilan tɛ don deki kɔnɔ, nka kabakurunw sirilen don kosɛbɛ duguma ni nɛgɛ gɛnɛgɛnɛ gɛlɛnw ye minnu b’a siri a fan bɛɛ la, k’a siri ka jigin fo yiriw la. Kɛrɛfɛla la, a bɛ kɛ ni jiri ye, wa a sanfɛ, a datugulen don pewu ni jirisunba dɔ ye min bɛ jɛngɛ, min bɛ ni bɔgɔdaga ye. SABABU 97. Lanpan.

Ni i tun jigira ka bɔ Pequod ka try-works la ka taa Pequod ka forecastle la, off duty watch tun bɛ sunɔgɔ yɔrɔ min na, waati kelen kɔnɔ, i tun bɛna ɲini k’a miiri ko i jɔlen bɛ masakɛw ni ladilikɛlaw ka batoso yeelenma dɔw la. O yɔrɔ la, u dalen bɛ u ka jirisunba-kɛrɛ saba-kɔnɔ-yɔrɔw la, kurunbolilaw kelen-kelen bɛɛ ye bobo ye min sɔgɔlen don; lanpan caman bɛ ka manamana a ɲɛw kan minnu bɛ ni finimugu ye.

Jagokɛlaw fɛ, tulu min bɛ kɛ kurunbolilaw ye, o ka dɔgɔ ka tɛmɛ masamusow ka nɔnɔ kan. Ka fini don dibi la, ka dumuni kɛ dibi la, ka sennateliya dibi la ka taa a ka palɛti la, nin ye a ka jate ye min bɛ kɛ tuma bɛɛ. Nka balematigi, a bɛ yeelen dumuni ɲini cogo min na, a bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ yeelen na ten. A b'a ka kurun jɔyɔrɔ kɛ Aladin ka lanpan ye, k'a da o kɔnɔ; walasa su min ka jugu kosɛbɛ, kurunba kɔnɔna nɛrɛma bɛ yeelen dɔ bila yen hali bi.

A filɛ ni hɔrɔnya bɛɛ ye cogo min na, balemakɛla bɛ taa n’a ka lanpan bolomafaraw ye — a ka c’a la, nka buteli kɔrɔw ni fiyɛlilan kɔrɔw, nka — ka taa nɛgɛ nɛnɛyɔrɔ la try-works la, ka u fa yen kokura, i n’a fɔ ale muguw bɔgɔdaga kɔnɔ. A bɛ tulu saniyalenba fana jeni, a cogo la min ma dila, wa, o la, a tɛ fɛn tiɲɛ; ji min tɛ dɔn tile, kalo walima sankololafɛnw fɛ kɔgɔjida la. A ka di i n’a fɔ binkɛnɛ tulu fɔlɔ awirilikalo la. A bɛ taa a ka tulu ɲini, walasa a ka da a la ko a kura don ani ko a ye tiɲɛ ye, hali i n’a fɔ taamakɛla min bɛ kungo kɔnɔ, o b’a yɛrɛ ka tulonkɛ sufɛdumuni ɲini cogo min na.





SABABU 98. Ka fɛnw mara duguma ani ka fɛnw saniya.

A fɔra kaban cogo min na leviyatan belebele bɛ yɔrɔ jan cogo min na, o ɲɛfɔlen don ka bɔ mast-kunkolo la; a bɛ gɛn cogo min na jibolisiraw kan, ka faga kɔdingɛw kɔnɔ; o kɔfɛ, u bɛ sama a kɛrɛfɛ cogo min na, ka a kunkolo tigɛ; ani cogo min na (sariyakolo min tun bɛ kɔrɔlen kuntigi bila fini minnu na, mɔgɔ kunkolo tigɛlen tun bɛ faga) a ka surtout belebeleba min bɛ kɛ ni padded ye, o bɛ kɛ a fagabaga ta ye cogo min na, waati bɛnnen na, a jalakilen don dagaw kɔnɔ, ani i n’a fɔ Sadrach, Mesak ani Abednego, a ka cɛya, tulu ani a kolo bɛ tɛmɛ tasuma fɛ, k’a sɔrɔ u ma foyi kɛ;—nka sisan, a tora ka nin ɲɛfɔli yɔrɔ in tilayɔrɔba laban kuncɛ ni degeliw kɛli ye — ka dɔnkili da, ni ne bɛ se — kanuya taabolo min bɛ kɛ ka a tulu bɔn ka don bɔgɔdaga kɔnɔ k’u gosi ka jigin ka don foro kɔnɔ, yɔrɔ min na siɲɛ kelen kokura leviatan bɛ segin a faso jugumanw na, ka sɛgɛn ka tɛmɛn dugukolo jukɔrɔ i n’a fɔ fɔlɔ; nka, bɔnɛ ye a sɔrɔ! abada ka wuli ka fiyɛ tugun.
