SABABU 52. Albatross ka muso.

Saheli-kɔrɔn fɛ ka bɔ Cape, Crozetts yɔrɔjanw kɛrɛfɛ, kurunbonkariyɔrɔ ɲuman ye Right Whalemen fɛ, kurunba dɔ tun bɛ ɲɛfɛ, Goney (Albatross) tɔgɔ la. A surunyalen dɔɔnin dɔɔnin, ka bɔ n ka sigiyɔrɔ kɔrɔtalen na ɲɛfɛ-masti-kun na, n ye o yecogo ye ka ɲɛ, o ye kabako ye kosɛbɛ tyro fɛ kɔgɔji jan mɔni na — balemakɛla min bɛ kɔgɔji la, wa a tun tɛ so kɔnɔ kabini tuma jan.

I n’a fɔ jikuruw tun falen don kosɛbɛ, o bololabaara in tun jɛlen don i n’a fɔ wuluwulu dɔ kolo. A kɛrɛw bɛɛ la, o spektral yecogo in tun bɛ tugu ni nɔgɔ bilenmanw ka sira janw ye, k’a sɔrɔ a ka spars bɛɛ n’a ka rigging tun bɛ i n’a fɔ jiribolo belebelebaw minnu furulen don ni hoar-frost ye. A duguma kurunw dɔrɔn de tun sigilen bɛ. Kungo-kɔnɔ-fɛnw ye k’a ka ɲɛda janw ye o masti-kunkolo saba la. A tun bɛ iko u tun bɛ finiw don baganw fari la, fini minnu tun ye san naani ɲɔgɔn kisi kurunbokari la, u tun tigɛra ani k’u tigɛ. U jɔlen nɛgɛberew kɔnɔ minnu sirilen bɛ kurunba la, u y’u yɛrɛyɛrɛ ani ka wuli kɔgɔji dɔ kan min tɛ se ka faamuya; hali ni o kɛra, kurun tun bɛ gɛrɛ dɔɔnin dɔɔnin an kɔfɛla jukɔrɔ tuma min na, an cɛ wɔɔrɔ minnu tun bɛ fiɲɛ na, an gɛrɛla ɲɔgɔn na fo a tun bɛ se ka kɛ ko an tun bɛ ɲini ka pan ka bɔ kurun kelen ka kurunba kunw na ka taa tɔ kelen ta la o bɛɛ n’a ta, o mɔnikɛla minnu ɲɛda tun bɛ i ko u ɲɛnafinna, n’u ɲɛw tun bɛ an ɲɛ ni dususuma ye u tɛmɛtɔ, u ma kuma kelen fɔ an yɛrɛ ka filɛlikɛlaw ye, k’a sɔrɔ sanbɛlɛ min tun bɛ ka bɔ duguma, o tun bɛ mɛn ka bɔ duguma.

“Kurun ahoy! Aw ye Balema Blanc ye wa?”

Nka, kapitɛni kabakoma in, n’a y’a yɛrɛ biri kogo jɛmanw kan, k’a ka burufiyɛn da a da la tuma min na, a binna cogo dɔ la ka bɔ a bolo la ka don kɔgɔji la; fiɲɛ min tun bɛ ka wuli sisan, a y'a cɛsiri fu ka a yɛrɛ mɛn ni o tɛ. O waati kɔnɔna na a ka kurun tun bɛ ka dɔ fara u ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ janya kan hali bi. K’a sɔrɔ Pequod kurunbolilaw tun bɛ k’u ka nin ko jugu in labatoli jira cogo suguya caman na ni makun ye, Balema Blanc tɔgɔ fɔli dɔrɔn fɔlɔ la kurun wɛrɛ la, Akab ye a jɔ dɔɔnin a tun bɛ ɲini ka kɛ i n’a fɔ a tun bɛna kurun jigin ka don dunan in na, ni fiɲɛ min tun bɛ mɔgɔ bagabaga, o tun ma a bali. Nka a kɛlen ka nafa sɔrɔ a ka fiɲɛbɔyɔrɔ la, a y’a ka burufiyɛ minɛ tugun, k’a dɔn a fan fɛ ko dunan kurun in ye Nantucketer ye, wa a bɛ taa so dɔɔnin, a ye tanuli kɛ ni kanba ye—“Ahoy yen! Nin ye Pequod ye, min sirilen bɛ diɲɛ fan bɛɛ la! A fɔ u ye ko u ka bataki nataw bɛɛ ci Pasifiki kɔgɔji ma! ani nin sen in na, san saba, ni ne tɛ so, a fɔ u ye u k’u ɲɛsin ——”

O waati yɛrɛ la, kununni fila ninnu tɛmɛna ka ɲɛ, o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ, ka kɛɲɛ n’u ka sira kelen-kelenw ye, jɛgɛ misɛnninw kuluw, minnu tun bɛ ka jigin an kɛrɛ fɛ tile damadɔ kɔnɔ ka kɔn o ɲɛ, olu bolila ka taa ni fɛn ye min tun bɛ i n’a fɔ u finmanw bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ, ka u yɛrɛ bila ɲɛfɛ ni kɔfɛ ni dunan kɛrɛfɛlaw ye. Hali n’a ka taamaw senfɛ tuma caman na, Ahab ka kan ka o yecogo ɲɔgɔn kɔlɔsi tuma caman na ka kɔn o ɲɛ, o bɛɛ n’a ta, cɛ kelen-kelen bɛɛ fɛ, fɛn fitininw yɛrɛ bɛ kɔrɔw ta ni kɔnɔnafili ye.

“Aw ka jigin ka bɔ ne la wa?” Akabu ye ŋunuŋunukan fɔ, k’a ɲɛ jɔlen to ji la. A tun bɛ iko kuma dɔɔnin dɔrɔn de tun bɛ kumaw kɔnɔ, nka o kumakan tun bɛ dusukasi juguba jira min tɛ se ka kɛ, ka tɛmɛ cɛkɔrɔba hakilintan in tun ma deli ka min jira fɔlɔ. Nka a y’a ɲɛsin kurun bolila ma, min tun bɛ kurun minɛ fiɲɛ fɛ fo ka na se bi ma walasa k’a ka ɲɛtaa dɔgɔya, a pɛrɛnna n’a ka waraba kan kɔrɔ ye ko: “Up helm! Keep her off round the world!”

Diɲɛ fan bɛɛ lamini! Ko caman bɛ o mankan na walasa ka kuncɛbaya dusukunnataw lawuli; nka o lamini bɛɛ bɛ taa min fɛ? Farati jatebaliw dɔrɔn de fɛ fo ka se an daminɛ yɔrɔ yɛrɛ la, an ye minnu to kɔfɛ lakana la, olu tun bɛ an ɲɛ tuma bɛɛ.

Ni nin diɲɛ in tun ye kɛnɛba ye min tɛ ban, ani ni kurun tun bɛ taa kɔrɔn fɛ, an tun bɛ se ka se yɔrɔjan kuraw la fo abada, ka yɔrɔw sɔrɔ minnu ka di ani minnu ka bon ka tɛmɛ Siklade walima Masakɛ Solomani ka gunw bɛɛ kan, o tuma na, layidu tun bɛ taama in na.
