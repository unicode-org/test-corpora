"Ha, ha, ne ka kurun! i bɛ se ka minɛ sisan ka kɛ tile ka kɔgɔji-wotoro ye. Aw, ho! aw siyaw bɛɛ ne ka kurun ɲɛfɛ, ne bɛ na ni tile ye aw fɛ! Joke on the further billows; hallo! a tandem, I drive the sea!

“Ne yɛrɛ bɛna balema ye fɔlɔ,”—a ko. “Ayi, Akabu ka kan ka kɛ ni dubloon ye!” a ye bɔgɔdaga dɔ sɔgɔ ni a yɛrɛ bolow ye; ani ka bolo dɔ ci san fɛ, ni bloki kelen ye min sɔgɔlen don, walasa ka a siri jirisunba kunkolo la, a ye juru min bɛ jigin ka taa duguma, o kun fila sɔrɔ; ani ka kelen nɔrɔ a ka segi la, o ye pini dɔ labɛn a dakun tɔ kelen kama, walasa k’a siri nɛgɛso la. O kɛlen, o laban bɛ a bolo hali bi, k’a jɔ pinɛ kɛrɛ fɛ, a y’a lamini lajɛ a ka kurun kɔnɔ, ka bɔ kelen na ka taa tɔ kelen na; a y’a ɲɛ jɔ dɔɔnin Daggoo, Queequeg, Tashtego kan; nka ka Fedallah yɛrɛ tanga; o kɔfɛ, a y’a ɲɛ jɔlen sigi furuɲɔgɔn kuntigi kan, k’a fɔ ko: “Jiri ta, matigi—n b’a di i bolo, Starbuck.” O kɔfɛ, a y’a mɔgɔ labɛn segi kɔnɔ, a ye kuma di u ma k’a kɔrɔta a ka sigiyɔrɔ la, Starbuck de ye juru siri laban na; o kɔfɛ, a jɔlen tora a kɛrɛfɛ. Wa o cogo la, ni a bolo kelen nɔrɔlen bɛ masakɛ ka kurunba la, Akabu ye kɔgɔji filɛ kɛnɛma kilomɛtɛrɛ caman ni kilomɛtɛrɛ caman kɔnɔ, — ɲɛfɛ, kɔfɛ, nin fan in fɛ, ani o fan fɛ, — sɛrɛkili bonyalenba kɔnɔ min yamaruyara sanfɛyɔrɔba in na.

Ni a bɛ baara kɛ n’a bolow ye yɔrɔ kɔrɔtalen dɔ la min bɛ ɲini ka kɛ yɔrɔ wɛrɛ la rigging kɔnɔ, n’a bɛ se ka kɛ ko a tɛ se ka senna-tɛgɛrɛ sɔrɔ, kɔgɔjida la kuruntigi bɛ kɔrɔta ka se o yɔrɔ ma, ka sabati yen juru fɛ o cogo la, a dakun sirilen bɛ deki kan tuma bɛɛ ni kuntigiya gɛlɛn ye cɛ kelen dɔ ma min ka kɔlɔsili kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen bɛ min bolo. Sabula o kungo sugu la min bɛ boli rigging, minnu ka jɛɲɔgɔnya suguya caman bɛ sanfɛ, olu tɛ se ka dɔn tuma bɛɛ fili tɛ min na, fɛn min bɛ ye u la deki la; ani ni o juruw dawolo bɛ ka fili miniti damadɔ o miniti damadɔ ka bɔ sirilanw na, o bɛ kɛ saya ye min bɛ kɛ a yɛrɛ la, ni, ni kɔlɔsilikɛla ma di a ma tuma bɛɛ, kurunbolila kɔrɔtalen ka kan ka fili kurun kɔnɔ baarakɛlaw ka jantobaliya dɔ fɛ, ka bin a bɛɛ kan ka wuli ka taa kɔgɔji la. O la sa, Akabu ka ko kɛlenw tun tɛ ko ye min tɛ deli ka kɛ; fɛn kelen min ye kabako ye u la, a kɛra i n’a fɔ, ko Starbuck, a bɛ se ka fɔ ko cɛ kelenpe de tun y’a yɛrɛ bila k’a kɛlɛ ni fɛn o fɛn ye hali dɔɔnin min bɛ gɛrɛ latigɛ la — o fana dɔ, min ka kantigiya la kɔlɔsili la, a tun bɛ iko a tun bɛ sigasiga dɔɔnin;—a tun ye kabako ye, ko nin tun ye cɛ yɛrɛ ye a ka kan ka min sugandi a ka kɔlɔsilikɛla ye k’a ka ɲɛnamaya bɛɛ di o mɔgɔ sugu bolo, n’o tɛ, dannaya tɛ min na.

Sisan, Akabu tun sigilen bɛ san fɛ siɲɛ fɔlɔ min na; ere a tun bɛ yen miniti tan; o kɔgɔjida-kɔnɔ-kɔnɔ-wulu bilenmanw dɔ la kelen min ka teli ka pan ka surunya cogo la min tɛ se ka kɛ ka balema-mɔgɔw ka masɔrɔ-kunkolow lamini minnu bɛ mɔgɔ bolo o latitudew la; o kɔnɔw dɔ nana ni wotoro ye ani ka pɛrɛn k’a kunkolo lamini, lamini teliya la min tɛ se ka tugu ɲɔgɔn kɔ. O kɔ, a ye sen ba kelen kɛ ka wuli ka wuli fiɲɛ na; o kɔ fɛ, a y'i spirali ka jigin, ka taa eddying tugun k'a kunkolo lamini.

Nka k’a sɔrɔ a ɲɛ jɔlen bɛ sankolo fitini ni yɔrɔ jan na, a kɛra i n’a fɔ Akabu tun tɛ nin kungo kɔnɔn in taamasiyɛn; tiɲɛ na, mɔgɔ wɛrɛ tun tɛna a taamasiyɛn kosɛbɛ fana, k’a sɔrɔ o tɛ ko ye min tɛ deli ka kɛ; sisan dɔrɔn, a bɛ iko ɲɛ min tun tɛ a janto kosɛbɛ, o tun bɛ kɔrɔ namara sugu dɔ ye a bɛ se ka fɔ ko a yecogo bɛɛ la.

“I ka fini, i ka fini, setigi!” o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ la, Sisili kɔgɔjidala kulela, min tun sigilen bɛ mizen-mast-head la, a jɔlen tora Akab kɔfɛ yɛrɛ, hali ni a tun ka dɔgɔ dɔɔnin ka tɛmɛ a hakɛ kan, wa fiɲɛba dɔ tun b’u tila.
