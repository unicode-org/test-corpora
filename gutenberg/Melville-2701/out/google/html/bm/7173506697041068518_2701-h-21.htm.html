SABABU 111. Pasifiki jamana.

Tuma min na an tun bɛ ka panpan Bashee gunw fɛ an labanna ka bɔ Saheli Kɔgɔjiba kan; ni ko wɛrɛw tun tɛ, ne tun bɛ se ka n ka Pasifiki kanulen fo ni barika dali ye min tɛ jate, bawo sisan ne ka kamalenninya delili jan jaabi; o kɔgɔji lafiyalen in tun bɛ girin ka taa kɔrɔn fɛ ka bɔ ne la ni bulu ba kelen ye.

Gundo duman min bɛ nin kɔgɔji in kan, mɔgɔ t’a dɔn, min ka wulicogo jugu nɔgɔlenw bɛ i n’a fɔ u bɛ kuma ni dogolen dɔw kan a jukɔrɔ; i n’a fɔ o nsiirin undulations minnu bɛ Efese sod kan Kibaru Duman fɔbaga senuma senuma kan. Wa, a bɛ bɛn, ko nin kɔgɔji-baganmarayɔrɔw kan, ji-yɔrɔba minnu bɛ girin ka bonya ani Bɔgɔdaga-foro minnu bɛ farafinna naani bɛɛ la, jikuruw ka kan ka wuli ka bin, ka jigin ka woyo k’a sɔrɔ u ma dabila sabu yan, biɲɛ ni biɲɛ ɲagaminen miliyɔn caman, sugo minnu binna ji la, sunɔgɔbaliya, sugo; an bɛ fɛn minnu bɛɛ wele ko ɲɛnamaya ni niw, nkalon bɛ sugo la, sugo la, hali bi; ka wuli i n’a fɔ sunɔgɔbagaw u ka dilanw kan; jikuru minnu bɛ wuli tuma bɛɛ nka u y’o kɛ u ka lafiyabaliya fɛ.

Magian rover over min bɛ miiri, o Pasifiki lafiyalen in, n’a y’a ye siɲɛ kelen, o ka kan ka kɛ a denfaya kɔgɔji ye abada. A bɛ diɲɛ ji cɛmancɛw lamaga, Ɛndu kɔgɔji ni Atlantiki kɔgɔji ye a bolow dɔrɔn ye. O jikuru kelenw bɛ Kalifɔrni dugu kura jɔlenw ka mɔliw ko, nka kunun, cɛw siya kɔsa in na, u falenna, ka Azi jamanaw ka finiw lave, nka u cɛ ka ɲi hali bi, minnu kɔrɔla ka tɛmɛ Ibrahima kan k’a sɔrɔ u bɛɛ bɛ korali gunw ka nɔnɔ siraw cɛ minnu bɛ panpan, ani gunw minnu bɛ duguma, dan tɛ minnu na, minnu tɛ dɔn, ani Zapɔn minnu tɛ se ka don. O cogo la, nin Pasifiki gundo, alaɲɛsiran in bɛ diɲɛ fanba bɛɛ lajɛlen kɛ; bɛ kɔgɔjida bɛɛ kɛ baji kelen ye a fɛ; a bɛ iko dugukolo dusukun min bɛ jikuruw gosi. Kɔrɔtalen o funu banbaliw fɛ, i magow ka kan ka kɛ ala lafilibaga tigi ye, k’i kunkolo biri Pan ɲɛ.

Nka Pan miirili damadɔw ye Akabu hakili lamaga, i n’a fɔ a jɔlen bɛ i n’a fɔ nɛgɛ ja a delila yɔrɔ min na mizen rigging kɛrɛfɛ, ni nugu kelen ye, a ye sukaro musk min bɔra Bashee gunw na (minnu ka kungo dumanw kɔnɔ, n’a sɔrɔla kanubaga nɔgɔlenw bɛ taama), ani tɔ kelen ye kɔgɔji sɔrɔlen kura kɔgɔ ninakili fiyɛ ni hakili ye o kɔgɔji min kɔnɔ, Balema Blanc koniyalen ka kan ka kɛ hali o waati la. A wulila ka jan nin ji laban ninnu kan, ka panpan ka taa Zapɔn kurunbokariyɔrɔ fan fɛ, cɛkɔrɔba in kuntilenna y’a yɛrɛ bonya. A dawolo gɛlɛnw ye ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ i n’a fɔ kojugu dawolo; a ɲɛda jolisiraw Delta tun bɛ funu i n’a fɔ bajiw minnu datugulen don; a sunɔgɔ yɛrɛ la, a ka kulekanba bolila ka tɛmɛn kurunba kɔnɔna na, ko: “Stern all! the White Whale spouts thick blood!”





SABABU 112. Nɛgɛsotigi.

A kɛlen k’a yɛrɛ nafa sɔrɔ waati nɔgɔlen, samiyɛ nɛnɛ na min tun bɛ masaya kɛ sisan o latitude ninnu na, ani k’a labɛn ka ɲɛsin ɲininiw ma minnu bɛ kɛ kosɛbɛ, minnu tun bɛna kɛ sɔɔni, Pɛrth, nɛgɛsotigi kɔrɔ min tun bɛ ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ, n’a kurulen don, o tun ma a ka nɛgɛso min bɛ se ka ta ka taa yɔrɔ wɛrɛ la tugun, a kɛlen kɔ k’a ka dɛmɛ baara kuncɛ Akabu sen na, nka a y’a to deki kan hali bi, teliya la, a sirilen bɛ ringbolts la ni foreast ye; ka kɛ sisan a bɛ ɲini ka wele tuma bɛɛ kuntigiw fɛ, ani bɔrɔkɛlaw fɛ, ani bɛlɛkisɛtigiw fɛ walasa u ka baara fitinin dɔ kɛ u ye; u ka marifa suguyaw ni kurun kɔnɔfɛnw cogoya caman cili, walima u dilancogo, walima u cogoya kura. Tuma caman na, a tun bɛna lamini ni sɛrɛkili ye min tun b’a fɛ kosɛbɛ, u bɛɛ tun bɛ makɔnɔni na ka baara kɛ u ye; kurun-sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛw, pike-kunkolow, harpoonw ani lances minɛ, ani k’a ka sooty lamaga-lamagali bɛɛ kɔlɔsi ni keleya ye, k’a to baara la. O bɛɛ n’a ta, nin cɛkɔrɔba in ta tun ye muɲu marifa ye, banabagatɔ bolo tun bɛ min minɛ. Muɲu si ma bɔ a la, muɲunbaliya si ma bɔ a la, petulance si ma bɔ a la. A makuntɔ, a bɛ kɛ dɔɔnin dɔɔnin, a bɛ kɛ ni bonya ye; a y’a biri ka taa a fɛ a kɔ karilen tolen kɔfɛ, a ye baara kɛ ka taa, i n’a fɔ sɛgɛn ye ɲɛnamaya yɛrɛ ye, wa a ka marifa gosili gɛlɛn y’a dusukun bugɔli gɛlɛn kɛ. Wa a kɛra ten.—A kɛra bɔnɛba ye!

Taama kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen dɔ kɛra nin cɛkɔrɔba in na, min tun bɛ i n’a fɔ a bɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ dɔɔnin nka a bɛ dimi a taamacogo la, o tun ye taama daminɛ waati dɔ la, o ye kurunbolilaw ka ɲininikɛcogo lawuli. Wa u ka ɲininkaliw minnu tun bɛ to senna, olu nafa tun ka bon kosɛbɛ, a labanna k’a yɛrɛ di; o cogo la, bɛɛ y'a ka siniɲɛsigi maloya maana dɔn sisan.

A tɛmɛna, wa a ma kɛ jalakibaliya ye, nɛnɛ jugu dɔ su tilancɛ, sira kan min bɛ boli jamana dugu fila cɛ, nɛgɛsotigi y’a ye ni hakilintan ye tilancɛ ye ko sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ fagalen in bɛ ka sonya a kan, ka dogoyɔrɔ ɲini bɔgɔdaga dɔ kɔnɔ min tun sirilen bɛ, min tiɲɛnen don. Ko min tùn bɛ yen, o tùn ye, sen fla bɛɛ dakunw bɔnɛni. O jirali in na, a yɔrɔ dɔ, a laban na, nisɔndiya wale naani bɔra, ani a ka ɲɛnamaya dramu dusukasi wale duurunan min janyalen don, ani min ma bala fɔlɔ.

A tun ye cɛkɔrɔba ye, min, a si tun bɛ san bi wɔɔrɔ ɲɔgɔn na, a tun ye o fɛn sɔrɔ kɔfɛ dusukasi fɛɛrɛ la min bɛ wele ko tiɲɛni. Nka su dɔ la, dibi datugulen kɔnɔ, ani a dogolen ka t’a fɛ ni namara ye kosɛbɛ, binkannikɛla jigitigɛlen dɔ y’a yɛrɛ sɛgɛn ka don a ka so nisɔndiyalen kɔnɔ, ka fɛn bɛɛ sonya u bɛɛ la. Wa, min ka di ka tɛmɛn fɔlɔ kan, nɛgɛsotigi yɛrɛ ye nin binkannikɛla in bila a ka denbaya dusukun na, k’a sɔrɔ a ma dɔn. O tun ye Butɛri Laɲinibaga ye! O kɔri fagalen da wulilen, jinɛ bɔra ka pan, ka a ka so sɔgɔsɔgɔ. Ni a kunsigi masirilen don, ani a sirilen bɛ ni wuluwulu fari ye, tile tilancɛ ɲɔgɔn, Pɛrth tun jɔlen bɛ a ka foroko ni a ka wuluwulu cɛ, a laban in bilalen bɛ nɛgɛbere dɔ kan, a bolo kelen tun bɛ pike kunkolo minɛ kɔɔriw kɔnɔ, ka tɔ kelen minɛ a ka foroko fogonfogon la, tuma min na, Kapiteni Akab nana, ka bɔrɔ fitinin dɔ ta a bolo, min tun bɛ i n’a fɔ nɛgɛso, nɔgɔlen don. I ka kan ka kɛ diɲɛnatigɛla ye, nɛgɛsotigi; a fɔ, mun na i tɛ dibi? I bɛ se ka muɲu cogo di k'a sɔrɔ i ma dibi? Yala sankolow bɛ i koniya hali bi, ko i bɛ se' kana dibi?—I tun bɛ mun dilan yen?”
