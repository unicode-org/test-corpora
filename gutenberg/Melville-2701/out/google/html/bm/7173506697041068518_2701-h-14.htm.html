SABABU 67. Tigɛ In.

O tun ye sibiri su ye, ani o lafiɲɛ don sugu min tugura o kɔ! Lafiɲɛ don tiɲɛni karamɔgɔ minnu bɛ baara kɛ ni u ye, olu bɛɛ ye balemakɛlaw ye. A fɔlɔ la, tigɛlan belebelebaw, ani fɛn gɛlɛn wɛrɛw, minnu bɛ kɛ ni blokiw kulu ye minnu pentalen don kosɛbɛ, wa mɔgɔ kelen tɛ se ka minnu kɔrɔta—o rɛzɛn kuluba in tun bɛ wuli ka taa fo sanfɛla belebeleba la, ka siri kosɛbɛ ka taa kurunba jukɔrɔla kunkolo la, yɔrɔ min barika ka bon yɔrɔ bɛɛ la kurunba sanfɛla sanfɛ. Jiri min bɛ i n’a fɔ hawser, o laban min bɛ wuli ka tɛmɛ o gɛlɛyaw fɛ, o kɔfɛ, o taara ni fiɲɛbɔlan ye, ka tackles duguma bloki belebeleba in wuli ka wuli balema sanfɛ o bloki in na, blubber hook belebeleba, min girinya bɛ kilo kɛmɛ ɲɔgɔn bɔ, o tun sirilen bɛ. O kɛlen, jiribolo belebeleba dɔ bɛ tigɛ ka dingɛ lamini, ka jɛgɛminɛ don a kɔnɔ, ka kurunba kɔnɔ baarakɛlaw farikoloba min bɛ kungo kɔnɔ dɔnkilida dɔ gosi, o bɛ daminɛ sisan ka wuli jamaba kelen kɔnɔ fiɲɛbɔlan na. Sisan, i n’a fɔ blubber bɛ balema datugu cogo min na tigitigi i n’a fɔ a ɲɛ bɛ oranjɛ kɛ cogo min na, o cogo kelen na, a bɛ bɔ farikolo la tigitigi i n’a fɔ oranjɛ bɛ bɔ a la tuma dɔw la ni a spirali ye. Sabu, sɛgɛn min bɛ to fiɲɛbɔlan fɛ, o bɛ to ka balema to a ka girin ka segin-ka-bɔ ji la, wa ni bɔgɔdaga min bɛ jiribolo kelen kɔnɔ, o bɛ bɔ cogo kelen na, layini min bɛ wele ko « scarf », o bɛ tigɛ waati kelen na Starbuck ni Stubb ka nɛgɛw fɛ, furuɲɔgɔnmaw ani a bɛ bɔ teliya la cogo min na o cogo la, wa tiɲɛ na o wale yɛrɛ fɛ, a bɛ kɔrɔta tuma bɛɛ san fɛ ka taa san fɛ fo a sanfɛla laban ka baganmara sanfɛla la o kɔfɛ, cɛ minnu bɛ fiɲɛbɔlan kan, olu bɛ wuli dabila, wa waati dɔɔnin walima waati fila kɔnɔ, joli-bɔn kabakoma in bɛ wuli ka taa ni ka segin i n’a fɔ a jigira ka bɔ san fɛ, wa mɔgɔ o mɔgɔ bɛ yen, o bɛɛ ka kan k’a janto kosɛbɛ k’a yɛrɛ tanga a ma n’a bɛ wuli, n’o tɛ, a bɛ se k’a tulo kɛ kɛsu kɔnɔ k’a kunkolo wuli kɔgɔji kan.

Sisan, harpooneer minnu bɛ na, olu dɔ bɛ taa ɲɛ ni marifa jan ye min ka gɛlɛn, n’o bɛ wele ko boarding-sword, wa a bɛ a ka cogoya kɔlɔsi, a bɛ dingɛba dɔ tigɛ ni seko ye, min bɛ jamaba jiginni duguma yɔrɔ la. O dingɛ in kɔnɔ, o kɔfɛ, fɛnba filanan min bɛ ɲɔgɔn falen-falen, o laban bɛ siri walasa ka minɛ ka to blubber kan, walasa ka min bɛ tugu o kɔ, o labɛn. O kɔfɛ, nin npantigi ŋana in, k’a bolo bɛɛ lasɔmi u ka jɔ, a bɛ dɔnniya-ci kɛ siɲɛ kelen kokura jama la, ka kɛrɛ fɛ, jigitigɛlen, fogonfogon sɔgɔli damadɔw kɛ, k’a tigɛ pewu fila ye walasa k’a sɔrɔ duguma yɔrɔ surun bɛ teliya hali bi, sanfɛla jan min bɛ wele ko blanket-piece, o bɛ wuli ka jɛya, wa a bɛɛ labɛnnen don ka jigin. Sisan, heavers minnu bɛ taa ɲɛfɛ, olu bɛ u ka dɔnkili daminɛ kokura, wa k’a sɔrɔ tackle kelen bɛ ka strip filanan bɔ balema na k’a kɔrɔta, tɔ kelen bɛ lafiya dɔɔnin dɔɔnin ka taa, ka jigin ka bɔ strip fɔlɔ la ka tɛmɛ hatchway belebele fɛ min bɛ a jukɔrɔ yɛrɛ, ka don parlor dɔ kɔnɔ min tɛ fɛn dilannenw ye, n’o bɛ wele ko blubber-room. Nin so sufɛla in kɔnɔ, bolo suguya caman bɛ to ka bɔrɔ jan in kurukuru i n’a fɔ sa ɲɛnamaba dɔ. Wa o cogo la, baara bɛ taa ɲɛ; tackles fila minnu bɛ wuli ni jigin waati kelen na; balema ni fiɲɛbɔlanw fila bɛɛ bɛ wuli, wulibagaw bɛ dɔnkili da, blubber-room cɛkɔrɔbaw bɛ ka wuli, furuɲɔgɔnmaw bɛ scarfing kɛ, kurun bɛ ka sɛgɛn, ani bolo bɛɛ bɛ kali tuma dɔw la, ka kɛɲɛ ni mɔgɔw bɛɛ ka ɲɔgɔnkanu nɔgɔyali ye.





SABABU 68. Kalansenw.

N ma n janto o ko la min tɛ mɔgɔ tɔɔrɔ, n’o ye balema fari ye. Ne ye sɔsɔliw sɔrɔ o ko la ni balemakɛla ŋanaw ye minnu bɛ ji la, ani nafamafɛnw dɔnbaga kalanbagaw ye kɔgɔjida la. Ne hakilina fɔlɔ ma Changé; nka hakilina dɔrɔn de don.

Ɲininkali ye nin ye, balema fari bɛ mun ani min? Kaban i b'a Dɔn a ka blubber ye min ye. O blubber ye fɛn ye min bɛ kɛ ni misisogo gɛlɛn ye, min ka surun, nka a ka gɛlɛn, a ka gɛlɛn ani a ka surun, wa a janya bɛ daminɛ santimɛtɛrɛ 8 walima tan na ka se santimɛtɛrɛ tan ni fila ma ani tan ni duuru ma.

Sisan, hali n’a bɛ se ka kɛ ko a daminɛ na, a bɛ se ka kɛ ko fu ye ka kuma danfɛn o danfɛn fari kan k’a fɔ ko a bɛ o cogo kelen na ani k’a bonya, o bɛɛ n’a ta, tiɲɛ yɛrɛ la, ninnu tɛ sɔsɔli ye o hakilina sugu sɔsɔli la sabu i tɛ se ka fɛn wɛrɛ kɔrɔta ka bɔ balema fari la ni o blubber kelen in tɛ; ani bagan o bagan kɛnɛman min bɛ datugu, ni a ka bon kosɛbɛ, o bɛ se ka kɛ mun ye ni fari tɛ? Tiɲɛ don, ka bɔ balema salen na min ma tiɲɛ, i bɛ se ka fɛn dɔ sɔgɔ ni i bolo ye min ka fin dan tɛ min na, min bɛ ye, min bɛ i n’a fɔ isinglass finmanw, dɔrɔn a bɛ ɲini ka se ka wuli ani ka nɔgɔya i n’a fɔ satin o kɔrɔ ye ko sani a ka ja, n’a tɛ dɔrɔn ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ ani ka bonya, nka a bɛ gɛlɛya dɔɔnin ani ka kari. O fɛn jalen sugu damadɔ bɛ n bolo, n bɛ baara kɛ ni minnu ye taamasiyɛnw kama n ka balema-gafew kɔnɔ. A bɛ kɛnɛya, i n’a fɔ n y’a fɔ cogo min na ka tɛmɛ; ani k’a da ɲɛ sɛbɛnnen kan, ne ye ne yɛrɛ diyabɔ tuma dɔw la k’a miiri ko a ye nɔba bila mɔgɔ la. A mana kɛ cogo o cogo, a ka di ka balemafɛnw ko kalan u yɛrɛ ka ɲɛkisɛw fɛ, i n’a fɔ i b’a fɔ cogo min na. Nka n bɛ bolifɛn min na yan, o ye nin ye. O fɛn kelen min ka fin dan tɛ min na, n’o ye isinglass ye, n sɔnna a ma, min bɛ balema farikolo bɛɛ bila, o tɛ jate kosɛbɛ i n’a fɔ danfɛn fari, i n’a fɔ fari fari, n’an b’a fɔ o cogo la sabu a tun ye yelifɛn ye dɔrɔn k’a fɔ ko balema belebeleba fari bɛnnen ka fin ani ka nɔgɔya ka tɛmɛ den bangenen kura fari kan. Nka nin ko in tɛna kɛ tugun.

Ni an y’a miiri ko blubber ye balema fari ye; o kɔfɛ, ni nin fari in, i n’a fɔ a bɛ kɛ cogo min na balema min ka bon kosɛbɛ, o bɛna tulu barili kɛmɛ fanba bɔ; ani, n’a jatera ko, a hakɛ la, walima a girinya la, o tulu, a cogo jiralen na, o ye tilayɔrɔ saba dɔrɔn ye, wa a tɛ kɛ finimugu kɔnɔfɛn bɛɛ ye o la, hakilina dɔw bɛ se ka sɔrɔ o jamaba ɲɛnama in bonya kan, min kɔnɔna dɔrɔn de bɛ jidaga dɔ bɔ i n’a fɔ o. Ni i ye barili tan jate tɔni kelen na, i bɛ tɔni tan sɔrɔ ka kɛɲɛ ni balema fari fɛnw tilancɛ saba dɔrɔn ye.

Ɲɛnamaya kɔnɔ, balema min bɛ wele ko Sperm Whale, o yɔrɔ yelen tɛ a dɔgɔyalenba ye a bɛ kabako caman minnu jira. A bɛ se ka fɔ ko a bɛɛ bɛ tigɛli kɛ k’a jɛngɛ ani ka segin ka tigɛ ni taamasiyɛn tilennen jatebaliw ye minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn cɛ, fɛn min bɛ i n’a fɔ Itali layini ja ɲumanw na. Nka a bɛ iko o taamasiyɛnw ma sɛbɛn isinglass fɛn min kofɔlen bɛ sanfɛ, nka a bɛ iko u bɛ ye o fɛ, i n’a fɔ u jalen don farikolo yɛrɛ kan. Nin bɛɛ fana tɛ. Tuma dɔw la, ɲɛ teliyalen, kɔlɔsilikɛla fɛ, o taamasiyɛn tilennenw, i n’a fɔ ja lakika la, nka u bɛ dugukolo di dantigɛli wɛrɛw ma minnu ka jan. Olu ye hieroglyphique ye; o kɔrɔ ye ko n’i y’o sifa gundo minnu bɛ piramidi kogow kan, olu wele ko hieroglyphiques, o tuma na, o ye daɲɛ bɛnnen ye ka baara kɛ ni min ye sisan jɛɲɔgɔnya kɔnɔ. N hakili tora ni sɛbɛnniw ye minnu bɛ balema sperme kelen kan kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la, o ye n kabakoya kosɛbɛ ni tabali ye min bɛ Ɛnduw ka mɔgɔ kɔrɔw jira minnu sɔgɔlen bɛ hieroglyphe palisade tɔgɔba dɔw kan, minnu bɛ Misisipi sanfɛla da la. I n’a fɔ o gundo faraw fana, balema min taamasiyɛn ye gundo ye, o bɛ to ka ɲɛfɔ. Nin kuma in min bɛ Ɛndu faraw kan, o bɛ n hakili jigin fɛn wɛrɛ la. Ka fara ko tɔw bɛɛ kan, baleine sperme kɛnɛma bɛ minnu jira, a man teli k’a kɔ jira, wa kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la a kɛrɛfɛyɔrɔw, minnu bɛ bɔ a cogo kɔrɔ la, k’a sababu kɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ caman ye minnu tɛ kɛ ni maloya ye, u bɛɛ lajɛlen ye fan ye min tɛ kɛ cogo la, min bɛ kɛ k’a sɔrɔ a ma kɛ cogo si la. N ka kan k’a fɔ ko Angletɛri kura faraw minnu bɛ kɔgɔjida la, Agassiz b’a miiri minnu na, olu bɛ ni sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ fariman taamasiyɛnw ye ni jikurubaw ye minnu bɛ panpan — n ka kan k’a fɔ, ko o faraw man kan ka bɔ balema Sɛrɛma la dɔɔnin nin kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya in na. A bɛ iko n bolo fana ko o sɔgɔsɔgɔ suguw bɛ balema na, n’a sɔrɔla o bɛ kɛ ni juguya jɛɲɔgɔnya ye ni balema tɔw ye; sabu n y’u Kɔlɔsi kosɛbɛ o suguya misiden belebelebaw la minnu falen don kosɛbɛ.

Kuma kelen walima fila wɛrɛ ka ɲɛsin nin ko in ma min ɲɛsinnen bɛ balema fari walima a bɔgɔbɔgɔ ma. A fɔra kaban, ko a bɛ bɔ a la yɔrɔ janw na, minnu bɛ wele ko blanket-pieces. I n’a fɔ kɔgɔji-daɲɛ fanba, nin kelen in bɛ nisɔndiya kosɛbɛ, wa a nafa ka bon. Sabu tiɲɛ na, balema sirilen bɛ a ka bɔgɔdaga la i n’a fɔ a bɛ bɔgɔdaga lakika walima a ka bɔgɔdaga kɔnɔ cogo min na; walima, min ka fisa hali bi, Ɛndu poncho dɔ y’a yɛrɛ sɔgɔ a kun na, k’a dancɛ lamini. O bɔgɔdaga duman in de kosɔn, balema bɛ se k’a yɛrɛ lafiya waati bɛɛ la, kɔgɔjiw bɛɛ la, waati bɛɛ la ani jikuruw bɛɛ la. Mun bɛna kɛ Gronland balema na, a fɔ, o Worodugu kɔgɔjiw la minnu bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ, nɛnɛ bɛ minnu na, ni a ma di a ka surtout duman ma? Tiɲɛ don, jɛgɛ wɛrɛw bɛ Sɔrɔ ka teliya kojugu o Hyperborean ji ninnu na; nka ninnu, a kɔlɔsira, olu ye aw ka jɛgɛw ye minnu joli nɛnɛnen don, fogonfogon tɛ minnu na, minnu kɔnɔ yɛrɛ ye frigow ye; danfɛnw, minnu b’u yɛrɛ sumaya jikuruba dɔ ka fiɲɛ kɔrɔ, i n’a fɔ taamakɛla bɛ a yɛrɛ lafiya cogo min na nɛnɛ waati la jatigila tasuma ɲɛkɔrɔ; k’a sɔrɔ i n’a fɔ hadamaden, fogonfogon ni joli sumalen bɛ balema na. Aw bɛ a joli bila nɛnɛ na, a bɛ sa. O tuma na fɛ, a ka di kosɛbɛ — n’a ma fɔ ɲɛfɔli kɔfɛ — ko nin waraba belebele in, farikolo funteni nafa ka bon min bolo i n’a fɔ a nafa ka bon hadamaden bolo cogo min na; a ka di kosɛbɛ k’a sɔrɔ a ka sɔrɔ so kɔnɔ, a su ji la a dawolo la a si bɛɛ la o Artik ji la! yɔrɔ min na, ni kɔgɔjilamɔgɔw binna kɔgɔji kan, u bɛ sɔrɔ tuma dɔw la, kalo caman o kɔfɛ, u bɛ jɛgɛn ka jɔ jikuru forow dusukunw na, i n’a fɔ wulu bɛ sɔrɔ cogo min na a nɔrɔlen bɛ amber la. Nka min bɛ mɔgɔ kabakoya kosɛbɛ, o ye k’a dɔn, i n’a fɔ a jirala cogo min na ni kɔrɔbɔli ye, ko balema Polar joli ka suma ka tɛmɛ Borneo negro ta kan samiyɛ fɛ.

A bɛ iko ne fɛ, ko yan, an bɛ mɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ ka ɲɛnamaya barikama nafa ye min man ca, ani kogo belebelebaw ka nafa min man ca, ani kɔnɔna bonya nafa min man ca. Oh, cɛ! i yɛrɛ ɲɛnajɛ ani ka i yɛrɛ kɛ misali ye balema in nɔfɛ! E fana ka to ka sumaya jikuruw cɛma. E fana ka ɲɛnamaya kɛ nin diɲɛ in kɔnɔ k’a sɔrɔ i ma kɛ a la. Aw ka kɛ nɛnɛ ye equator la; aw bɛ aw joli ji mara Pole la. I n’a fɔ Piɛrɛ senuma ka dôme belebele, ani i n’a fɔ balema belebele, i ka to a la, e cɛ! waati bɛɛ la, funteni bɛ kɛ i yɛrɛ ta ye.

Nka, o fɛn ɲumanw kalanni ka nɔgɔn, wa jigiya tɛ mɔgɔ la dɛ! Ka ɲɛsin cɛya ma, mɔgɔ damadɔw de bɛ kɛ dome ye i n’a fɔ Piɛrɛ senuma ta! danfɛnw ka dɔgɔ cogo min na, u bonya ka dɔgɔ i n’a fɔ balema!





SABABU 69. Kaburudo.

“Aw ka cakɛdaw sama, su ka taa kɔfɛ!”

Tackles belebelebaw y’u ka baara kɛ sisan. Balema kunkolo tigɛlen farikolo finman min bɔra, o bɛ manamana i n’a fɔ marbre kaburu; hali ni a ɲɛ Changé, a ma se ka bɔnɛ foyi la a caya la. A ka bon hali bi. Nɛmɛnɛmɛ, a bɛ panpan ka taa a fɛ, ji min b’a lamini, o bɛ tigɛtigɛ ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ, jɛgɛw fɛ minnu tɛ wasa, ani sanfɛla fiɲɛ bɛ jɔrɔ ni kɔnɔw pɛrɛntɔw ka wuluwulu juguw ye, minnu dawolo bɛ i n’a fɔ poniɛri camanba minnu bɛ balema mafiɲɛya. Fantom finman belebeleba min kunkolo tɛ, o bɛ pan ka taa ka taa a fɛ ka bɔ kurun kɔnɔ, wa bere o bere, a bɛ panpan ten, fɛn minnu bɛ i n’a fɔ jɛgɛw ka jiribolo kɛnɛw ani kɔnɔw ka wulu kubik, olu bɛ dɔ fara mɔgɔfagala mankan kan. Lɛrɛ caman ni lɛrɛ caman kɔnɔ ka bɔ kurunba la min bɛ ɲini ka jɔ, o yecogo jugu bɛ ye. Sankolo jɛman min tɛ sankaba ye ani min ka nɔgɔn, kɔgɔji duman ɲɛda cɛɲi kan, nisɔndiya fiɲɛw bɛ min wuli, o saya jamaba bɛ panpan ka taa a fɛ, fo ka tunun filɛli dan tɛ minnu na.

Sukununi dɔ bɛ yen min bɛ dusukasi kosɛbɛ ani min bɛ mɔgɔ nisɔndiya kosɛbɛ! Kɔgɔjida la wuluw bɛɛ bɛ dusukasi la min bɛ Ala ɲɛ, fiɲɛ-kɔnɔw bɛɛ bɛ u kɛ waati bɛnnen na ni u ye nɛrɛmuguma walima nɛrɛmuguma ye. Ɲɛnamaya kɔnɔ nka u damadɔ tun bɛna balema dɛmɛ, I ween, ni laala a mago tun bɛ a la; nka a sudon seli la, u bɛ wuli ni Ala ɲɛsiran ye kosɛbɛ. Oh, dugukolo kan wuluwulu juguba! min na, balema barikamaba tɛ hɔrɔnya ka bɔ min na.

Nin fana tɛ laban ye. Hali ni farikolo nɔgɔlen don, jinɛ min bɛ a yɛrɛ sara, o bɛ kisi, ka panpan a kan walasa ka siran. Kɛlɛcɛ maloyalen dɔw fɛ walima sɔrɔli-minɛn fililen dɔw ye a kɔlɔsi ka bɔ yɔrɔ jan, tuma min na yɔrɔjan min bɛ kɔnɔw sɔgɔsɔgɔ, o bɛɛ n’a ta, o bɛ hali bi jama finman jira min bɛ panpan tile la, ani fiyɛli finman bɛ ka wuli sanfɛ a kama o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ la, balema su min tɛ kojugu kɛ, n’a bolokɔnincininw bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ, o sigilen bɛ jirisunba kɔnɔ— jirisunw, faraw, ani karifɛnw yan: aw k’aw janto ! Wa san caman kɔnɔ o kɔfɛ, laala, kurunw bɛ u yɛrɛ tanga o yɔrɔ ma; ka pan a kan i n’a fɔ saga hakilintanw bɛ pan ka tɛmɛ vacuum kan, bawo u ɲɛmɔgɔ tun bɛ pan yen fɔlɔ ni bere dɔ minɛna. O la sa, hali ni ɲɛnamaya kɔnɔ, balema belebele farikolo bɛ se ka kɛ siranfɛn ye tiɲɛ na a juguw bolo, a ka saya la, a jinɛ bɛ kɛ siranfɛn ye min fanga tɛ diɲɛ dɔ bolo.

Yala i ye dannabaaw ye jinɛw la, ne terikɛ wa? Jinɛ wɛrɛw bɛ yen ni Cock-Lane ta tɛ, ani cɛ minnu ka dun kosɛbɛ ka tɛmɛ Dɔgɔtɔrɔ Johnson kan minnu dalen bɛ u la.





SABABU 70. Sfinx.

A tun man kan ka bɔ yen ko sanni a ka leviyatan farikolo bɔ pewu, a kunkolo tigɛra. Sisan, balema-banakisɛ kunkolo tigɛ ye dɔnniya farikolo-ɲɛnajɛ-ko ye, balema operelikɛla ŋanaw bɛ waso kosɛbɛ min na: wa a tɛ kɛ kun tɛ.

Aw k’a jateminɛ ko foyi tɛ balema na min bɛ se ka wele ka ɲɛ ko kɔ; o kɔ fɛ , a bɛ iko a kunkolo ni a farikolo bɛ fara ɲɔgɔn kan yɔrɔ min na , o yɔrɔ yɛrɛ la , a yɔrɔ min ka bon kosɛbɛ , o de bɛ yen . Aw k’aw hakili to a la fana ko operelikɛla ka kan ka opereli kɛ ka bɔ san fɛ, sen 8 walima tan ɲɔgɔn bɛ don a n’a ka mɔgɔ cɛ, wa o mɔgɔ bɛ ɲini ka dogo kɔgɔji kɔnɔ min ɲɛ bɛ wuli, a bɛ girin, wa tuma caman na, a bɛ kɛ ɲagami ye ani a bɛ pɛrɛn. Aw k’a to aw hakili la fana ko nin ko jugu ninnu na, a ka kan ka sen caman tigɛ farikolo la ka taa a fɛ; ani o dugujukɔrɔ cogo la, k’a sɔrɔ a ma hali ɲɛ kelen sɔrɔ o cogo la, a ka kan ka a yɛrɛ tanga ni seko ye ka a yɛrɛ tanga yɔrɔw bɛɛ ma minnu bɛ kɛrɛfɛ, minnu dagalen don, ani ka kɔkolo tila tigitigi yɔrɔ gɛlɛnba dɔ la a donli fɛ kungolo kɔnɔ. O la sa, yala i tɛ kabakoya Stubb ka yɛrɛbonya la, k’a fɔ ko a ye miniti tan dɔrɔn de ɲini walasa ka balema cɛya dɔ kunkolo tigɛ wa?

Ni a tigɛra fɔlɔ, a kun bɛ jigin kɔfɛ ka minɛ yen ni kabali ye fo ka a farikolo bɔ. O kɛlen, n’a ye balema fitinin dɔ ta ye a bɛ kɔrɔta kurunba kan walasa k’a fili ni laɲini ye. Nka, ni leviathan falen falen don o tɛ se ka kɛ; sabu balema cɛya kunkolo bɛ a ka fɛn bɛɛ lajɛlen tilancɛ kelen ɲɔgɔn minɛ, wa ka doni sugu jɔ pewu i n’a fɔ o, hali balemakɛla ka minɛn belebelebaw fɛ, o tun ye ko fu ye i n’a fɔ k’a ɲini ka Holandekaw ka bɔgɔdaga dɔ pese biyefeerelaw ka balansi kɔnɔ.

Pequod ka balema kunkolo tigɛlen ani a farikolo bɔra a la, a kunkolo kɔrɔtara kurun kɛrɛ fɛ — a bɛ se ka bɔ kɔgɔji la tilancɛ ɲɔgɔn na, walisa a fanba ka se ka wuli hali bi a faso kɔnɔfɛn fɛ. Wa yen ni bololabaarakɛminɛn gɛlɛn in y’i biri a ma kosɛbɛ, k’a sababu kɛ jiginɛba ye ka bɔ duguma-masi-kunkolo la, ani yaala-bolo kelen-kelen bɛɛ min bɛ o fan fɛ, o bɛ bɔ i n’a fɔ kurunba jikuruw kan yen, o kunkolo jolibɔlen tun sirilen bɛ Pequod cɛmancɛ la i n’a fɔ Holofernes cɛba ta ka bɔ Judith cɛsirilan na.

Nin baara laban in kɛra tuma min na, o kɛra tilelafana ye, kɔgɔjilamɔgɔw taara duguma ka taa u ka sufɛdumuni na. Makun tun bɛ deki kan min tun bɛ ɲagami fɔlɔ, nka sisan a tun lakolon don. Nɛgɛ lafiyalenba dɔ, i n’a fɔ diɲɛ bɛɛ ka lotus jɛman, tun bɛ ka a bulu sumanikɛbaliw dabɔ ka taa a fɛ kɔgɔji kan, mankan tɛ minnu na.

Yɔrɔ surun dɔ tɛmɛna, ka wuli ka don o mankanbaliya la, Akabu kelen nana ka bɔ a ka so kɔnɔ. A kɛlen ka wuli dɔɔnin kɛ quarter-deck kan, a y’a jɔ dɔɔnin walasa ka kɛrɛfɛla filɛ, o kɔfɛ, a donna dɔɔnin dɔɔnin cakɛda kunbabaw kɔnɔ a ye Stubb ka nɛgɛbere jan ta — a tora yen hali bi balema kunkolo tigɛlen kɔfɛ — k’a gosi ka don jama tilancɛ jɛgɛlen duguma yɔrɔ la, k’a dakun tɔ kelen bila a bolo kelen kɔrɔ i n’a fɔ bere, o cogo la, a jɔlen tora k’a biri ni ɲɛw ye minnu jɔlen don kosɛbɛ nin kun in kan.

O tun ye kunkolo nɛrɛma ye ani a tun bɛ ni bɔrɔ ye; ani ka dulon yen hakilisigi juguba in cɛma, a kɛra i n’a fɔ Sfinx ta kungo kɔnɔ. Akab y’a fɔ ni kɔnɔnajɛya ye ko: “Kunnafoni kɛ, e kunkolo belebeleba ni bonyalenba,” hali n’a ma masiri ni kunsigi ye, o bɛɛ n’a ta, a ɲɛda bɛ i ko a ɲɛ jɛlen don ni ntumuw ye yan ni yan, kuma, kunkolo barikama, ka gundolafɛn fɔ an ye min bɛ i kɔnɔ. Sumanw bɛɛ la, i ye i yɛrɛ su ji la kosɛbɛ. O kunkolo min kan sanfɛla tile bɛ manamana sisan, o jiginna nin diɲɛ jusigilanw cɛma tɔgɔw ni kɔgɔjidala sɔrɔdasiw bɛ nɔgɔ, ani jigiya ni kurunba minnu tɛ se ka fɔ, olu bɛ toli yɔrɔ min na, nin frigate in bɛ bala yɔrɔ min na ni mɔgɔ miliyɔn caman kolow ye minnu binna ji la, i tun bɛ yen min tun bɛ i dɔn kosɛbɛ, i tun bɛ yen, i tun bɛ yɔrɔ min na, i ma deli ka sunɔgɔ kuruntigi caman kɛrɛ fɛ duguma I ye kanubaganciw ye minnu datugulen don tuma min na u tun bɛ pan ka bɔ u ka kurun tasumaman na ka taa u dusukun na, tuma min na sankolo tun bɛ iko nkalontigɛlen u bolo tuma min na a filila ka bɔ su tilancɛ kɔnɔ, a binna su tilancɛ kɔnɔ hali bi; kan min ma foyi kɛ—k’a sɔrɔ sanpɛrɛn teliyalenw tun bɛ sigiɲɔgɔn kurun yɛrɛyɛrɛ min tun bɛna cɛ tilennen ta ka taa bolo kɔrɔtalenw na, e kunkolo, i ye fɛn caman ye min bɛ se ka dugukolow faranfasi ka Ibrahima kɛ kafiri ye, wa, daɲɛ kelen tɛ i ta ye!”

“Sail ho!” se sɔrɔli kan dɔ pɛrɛnna ka bɔ kunba-masti-kunkolo la.

“Ayi? Ayiwa, sisan, o ye nisɔndiya ye,” Akabu kulela, k’a yɛrɛ jɔ yɔrɔnin kelen, k’a sɔrɔ sankaba kuuru bɛɛ tun bɛ ka bɔ a ɲɛda la. “O kulekan ɲɛnama min bɛ nin hakilisigi fagalen in kan, o bɛ se ka mɔgɔ fisaman dɔ ladɔnniya.—Yɔrɔ yɔrɔ jumɛn?”

“Point saba bɛ kinin fɛ bow kan, setigi, ani k’a ka fiɲɛ jigin an fɛ!

"A ka fisa ani a ka fisa, cɛ. Yala sisan Paul senuma tun bɛna na o sira fɛ, ka na ni a ka fiɲɛ ye ne ka fiɲɛ tɛ min na! E Sikolo, ani hadamaden ni! i ka nɔrɔɲɔgɔnma minnu sirilen don ɲɔgɔn na, olu bɛ tɛmɛ kuma bɛɛ kan cogo di! atomi fitinin tɛ wuli walima ka ɲɛnamaya fɛn kan, nka a ka nanbarako fila bɛ a hakili la."(Alimankan na)





SABABU 71. Jeroboam ka maana.

Bolo ni bolo, kurun ni fiɲɛ ye fiɲɛ bila; nka fiɲɛ nana teliya la ka tɛmɛ kurun kan, a ma mɛn, Pequod y’a daminɛ ka yɛrɛyɛrɛ.

Dɔɔni dɔɔni, galasi fɛ, dunan in ka kurunw ni masti-kunkolo minnu bɛ mɔgɔ kɔnɔ, olu y’a jira ko a ye balema-kurun ye. Nka k’a sɔrɔ a tun yɔrɔ ka jan fiɲɛ fɛ, ani a tun bɛ marifa ci ka tɛmɛ, a bɛ iko a tun bɛ tɛmɛsira kɛ ka taa dugukolo wɛrɛ la, Pequod tun tɛ se ka jigiya sɔrɔ ka se a ma. O la sa, taamasiyɛn bilala walasa k’a dɔn jaabi min bɛna kɛ.

A fɔra yan, ko i n’a fɔ sɔrɔdasi kɔgɔjidala kurunw, Ameriki balema kulu ka kurunw kelen-kelen bɛɛ bɛ ni taamasiyɛn kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen ye ; o bɛɛ taamasiyɛnw bɛ lajɛ gafe dɔ kɔnɔ ni kurun kelen-kelen bɛɛ tɔgɔw nɔrɔlen bɛ a la, kapitɛni bɛɛ bɛ di a ma. O cogo la, balema kuntigiw bɛ se ka ɲɔgɔn dɔn kɔgɔji kan, hali ni u yɔrɔ ka jan kosɛbɛ, wa ni yɔrɔ fitinin tɛ yen.

Pequod ka taamasiyɛn labanna ka jaabi sɔrɔ dunan in ka a yɛrɛ ta sigili fɛ; min y’a jira ko kurun in tun ye Jeroboam ye min bɛ bɔ Nantucket. A y’a ka forow kɛ square la, a ye kurunba jigin, ka rangement abeam Pequod ka lee jukɔrɔ, ka kurun dɔ jigin; a ma mɛn, a surunyara; nka, k’a sɔrɔ kɛrɛfɛla-lada tun bɛ ka sɛgɛsɛgɛli kɛ Starbuck ka yamaruya fɛ walasa ka kapitɛni min tun bɛ taa bɔ a ye, o ladon, dunan min kofɔlen don, o y’a bolo wuli ka bɔ a ka kurun kɔfɛ k’a jira ko o ko in tɛ mako ɲɛ fewu. A y’a jira ko bana jugu dɔ tun bɛ Jeroboam kurun kɔnɔ, wa ko Mayhew, a ka kapitɛni tun bɛ siran ka Pequod ka sosiyete minɛ. Sabu, hali n’a yɛrɛ ni kurun kɔnɔmɔgɔw ma nɔgɔ, ani hali n’a ka kurun tun bɛ marifa tilancɛ bɔ, ani kɔgɔji ni fiɲɛ min tɛ se ka tiɲɛ, o tun bɛ ka girin ka woyo u ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ; o bɛɛ n’a ta, a dusukun y’a to jamana ka maloya la, a ma sɔn ka na ni Pequod ye k’a ɲɛsin a yɛrɛ ma.

Nka o ma kumaɲɔgɔnya bɛɛ bali cogo si la. K’a to mɛtɛrɛ damadɔw cɛma a ni kurun cɛ, Jeroboam ka kurun tun bɛ baara kɛ n’a ka kurunba ye tuma dɔw la, a ye fɛɛrɛ tigɛ walasa a ni Pequod ka kɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ, k’a sɔrɔ a tun bɛ ka kurunba tigɛ kosɛbɛ kɔgɔji kɔnɔ (k’a masɔrɔ nin waati in na, a tun bɛ fiɲɛ kura bɔ kosɛbɛ), k’a ka kurunba kunba kabakoya nka, tiɲɛ na, tuma dɔw la, jikuruba dɔ daminɛni barikama fɛ, kurun tun bɛ gɛrɛn ka taa ɲɛfɛ yɔrɔ dɔ la; nka sɔɔni, a tun bɛna na ni seko ye a ka fɛn bɛnnenw na tugun. Ka da o kan, ani o ɲɔgɔnna tigɛtigɛ wɛrɛw la sisan ni sisan, baro dɔ tun bɛ senna mɔgɔ fila ninnu cɛ; nka waati dɔw la, o tɛ ni tigɛ wɛrɛ ma kɛ min suguya wɛrɛ tɛ kelen ye kosɛbɛ.

Ka kurunba sama Jeroboam ka kurun kɔnɔ, o tun ye cɛ ye min ɲɛda tun ye kelen ye, hali o kungo kɔnɔ balema ɲinini ɲɛnamaya kɔnɔ, yɔrɔ min na mɔgɔ kelen-kelen kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw bɛ kɛ fɛn bɛɛ lajɛlen ye. A tun ye cɛ fitinin ye, a tun ka surun, a tun ye kamalennin ye, a ɲɛda bɛɛ tun bɛ seri ni wuluwuluw ye, wa a kunsigi jɛman tun tɛ foyi la. A ka fini janyalen dɔ, min tigɛlen don ni kaba ye, n’a ɲɛ ye noye ye min ɲɛnama don, o y’a datugu; bolokɔnincinin minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn kan, olu tun sirilen bɛ a bolokɔninw na. Delirium jugumanba dɔ tun bɛ a ɲɛ na, min tun sigilen bɛ, a tun bɛ mɔgɔ bila ka miiri.

O la sa, nin jate in ɲɛfɔra fɔlɔ dɔrɔn, Stubb tun y’a fɔ ni kɔnɔnajɛya ye ko—“Ale de don!, ale de don!—Town-Ho ka sosiyete y’a fɔ an ye ko scaramouch min tun bɛ ni tog jan ye!” Stubb tun bɛ kuma maana kabakoma dɔ kan yan min fɔra Jeroboam ko la, ani cɛ dɔ a ka kurun kɔnɔ, waati dɔ ka kɔn o ɲɛ, waati min na Pequod tun bɛ Town-Ho fɔ. Ka kɛɲɛ ni o maana ye ani min kalanna o kɔfɛ, a kɛra i n’a fɔ scaramuuch min kofɔlen don, o ye fanga kabakoma sɔrɔ Jeroboam kɔnɔ mɔgɔ bɛɛ kan. A ka maana tun ye nin ye:

A tun lamɔna fɔlɔ Neskyeuna Shakers ka jɛkulu dibi la, a tun ye kiraba ye yɔrɔ min na; u ka gundolajɛw la minnu karilen don, k’a sɔrɔ a jigira siɲɛ caman ka bɔ sankolo la jaan-da dɔ fɛ, k’a jira ko daga wolonwulanan dabɔli teliya la, a tun bɛ min ta a ka vest-pocket kɔnɔ nka, min, sanni marifakisɛ ka kɛ a la, a tun ka kan ka jalaki laudanum na. Cidenw ka ŋaniya kabakoma dɔ y’a minɛ, a tun bɔra Neskyeuna ka taa Nantucket, yɔrɔ min na, ni o namara ye min danfaralen don ni dibi ye, a y’a ta kɛnɛma yɔrɔ sabatilen, hakili ɲuman na, k’a yɛrɛ di i n’a fɔ bolo jɛman kandida Jeroboam ka balema ɲinini taama na. U ye furusiri kɛ a la; nka o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ kurun bɔlen kɔ dugukolo ɲɛ na, a ka hakilintan bɔra freshet kɔnɔ. A y’a jira ko ale ye mɛlɛkɛba Gabiriyɛli ye, ka ci bila kapitɛni ma ko a ka pan ka don baji kɔnɔ. A y’a ka manifesto bɔ, a y’a yɛrɛ bila o cogo la i n’a fɔ kɔgɔji gunw kisibaga ani Oceanica bɛɛ ka vikari-jenerali. A ye nin ko ninnu fɔ ni timinandiya ye min tɛ jɔrɔ;—a ka sunɔgɔbaliya, nisɔndiya miirili ka tulonkɛ dibi la, ni jagɛlɛya ye, ani deliriya lakika ka siranfɛnw bɛɛ minnu tɛ hadamadenw ta ye, olu bɛɛ farala ɲɔgɔn kan ka nin Gabiriyɛli in bila kurunba kɔnɔmɔgɔw fanba hakili la, minnu tɛ fɛn dɔn, ni senuya lafiya ye. Ka fara o kan, u siranna a ɲɛ. Nka, ikomi o cɛ sugu tun tɛ nafa caman sɔrɔ kurun kɔnɔ, kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la, a tun bɛ ban ka baara kɛ fo n’a tun b’a fɛ, kapitɛni min tun dalen tɛ a la, o tun bɛna fain ka bɔ a bolo nka a y’a dɔn ko o tigi ka laɲini tun ye k’a jigin kurunbonkariyɔrɔ fɔlɔ la min bɛnnen don, mɛlɛkɛba y’a ka taamasiyɛnw ni a ka dagaw bɛɛ da wuli o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ — a ye kurun ni a bolow bɛɛ di halakili ma, n’a sɔrɔla o ŋaniya in kɛra. A ye baara kɛ a ka kalandenw kan kosɛbɛ kurun kɔnɔ baarakɛlaw cɛma, fo a laban na, farikolo dɔ kɔnɔ u taara kurun ɲɛmɔgɔ fɛ k’a fɔ a ye ko ni Gabiriyɛli bilala ka bɔ kurun kɔnɔ, u cɛ si tɛna to. O la sa, a wajibiyara k’a ka laɲini bila. U tun tɛna sɔn fana Gabiriyɛli ka tɔɔrɔ cogo si la, k’a fɔ walima k’a sago kɛ; fo a kɛra ko Gabiriyɛli ye kurun hɔrɔnya dafalen sɔrɔ. O bɛɛ kɔlɔlɔ kɛra, ko mɛlɛkɛba tun t’a janto kosɛbɛ kapitɛni n’a furuɲɔgɔnw la kosɛbɛ walima ko a tun t’a janto foyi la; wa ikomi banakisɛ in bɔra kɛnɛ kan, a ye bolo sanfɛla ta ka tɛmɛn fɔlɔ kan; k'a jira ko banajugu in, i n'a fɔ a y'a wele cogo min na, o tun bɛ a kelenpe de bolo; a man kan ka to fana fo ka kɛɲɛ n'a diyanyeko ye. Kuruntigiw, minnu fanba ye jinɛ faantanw ye, olu ye u yɛrɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ, u dɔw ye u yɛrɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ a ɲɛkɔrɔ; a ka cikanw labatoli la, tuma dɔw la, k’a yɛrɛ bonya, i n’a fɔ ala dɔ. O ko suguw bɛ se ka kɛ i n’a fɔ u tɛ se ka da a la; nka, hali ni u ye kabako ye cogo o cogo, u ye tiɲɛ ye. Fanatikaw ka tariku fana tɛ tilancɛ ye ka ɲɛsin fanatika yɛrɛ ka yɛrɛnafili sumanikɛbali ma, i n’a fɔ a ka fanga sumanbali min bɛ mɔgɔ caman wɛrɛw lafili ani k’u lafili. Nka waati sera ka segin Pequod kan.

“Ne tɛ siran i ka bana ɲɛ, cɛ,” Akabu y’o fɔ ka bɔ kogow la, ka taa Kapiteni Mayhew ma, min jɔlen tun bɛ kurun kɔfɛ; “na don kurun kɔnɔ.”

Nka sisan, Gabiriyɛli y’a daminɛ ka jɔ.

“Miiri, miiri sumaya la, jɛmanw ni bilisi!Aw k’aw yɛrɛ kɔlɔsi bana juguba in na!”

“Gabriɛl! Gabriɛl!” Kapiteni Mayhew kulela; “i ka kan fana—” Nka o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ la, jikuru kunkolo dɔ ye kurunba ci ka taa ɲɛfɛ yɔrɔ jan, a ka wulilenw ye kuma bɛɛ su ji la.

“Yala i ye Balema Blanc ye wa?” Akabu ye ɲininkali kɛ, tuma min na kurun seginna ka segin.

“Miiri, miiri i ka balema-kurun na, tasumabɔlen ani min jiginna!I janto a kɔ juguba in na!”

“Ne b’a fɔ i ye tugun, Gabiriyɛli, ko—” Nka tuguni, kurun tigɛra ka taa ɲɛfɛ i n’a fɔ jinɛw y’a sama. Foyi ma fɔ waati dɔw kɔnɔ, k’a sɔrɔ jikuru ɲagaminenw tugu-tugulen ɲɔgɔn kɔ, minnu tun bɛ ka bin o kɔgɔjiw ka wuli-wuli tuma dɔw la dɔ fɛ, u tun tɛ ka a kɔrɔta.
