SABABU 86. A ka kùnkolo.

Poyikɛla wɛrɛw ye wuluwulu ɲɛ nɔgɔlen tanuli fɔ, ani kɔnɔnin min tɛ jigin abada, o ɲɛda kanulenw; less celestial, n bɛ seli kɛ wulu dɔ la.

Ni an y’a jate Minɛ ko Balema-kɔnɔ-fɛn min bonya ka bon kosɛbɛ k’a da Minɛ jirisunba yɔrɔ la a bɛ Dɔgɔya fo ka Se cɛ lamini ma, a bɛ Kɛ a sanfɛla dɔrɔn kan, a yɔrɔ min bɛ Se mɛtɛrɛ kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen bi duuru ma a dɔgɔyalenba la. A ju farikolo lamininen min bɛ ɲɔgɔn kan, o bɛ bonya ka kɛ bolokɔni fila ye minnu janya ka bon, minnu ka gɛlɛn, minnu ka fla, u bɛ wuli dɔɔni dɔɔni fo ka se santimɛtɛrɛ kelen ma. Kɔnɔbara walima ɲɔgɔndan na, o flukew bɛ ɲɔgɔn Da ɲɔgɔn kan dɔɔnin, o kɔ fɛ u bɛ kɔsegin ɲɔgɔn fɛ kɛrɛfɛ i n’a fɔ wuluw, ka yɔrɔba dɔ To u ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ. Fɛn ɲɛnama si la, cɛɲi siraw ɲɛfɔlen don ka ɲɛ ka tɛmɛ o fɛnɲɛnɛmaw dancɛw kan. A bonya laban na balema dafalen na, a kùnkolo bɛna Tɛmɛ kosɛbɛ mɛtɛrɛ mugan kan.

O tɔnden bɛɛ bɛ i n’a fɔ a ye dilan dense ye min bɛ wele ko webbed bed of welded sinews; nka tigɛ a kɔnɔ, i b’a ye ko sɛrɛ saba danfaralenw b’a da:—sanfɛ, cɛmancɛ ani duguma. Fɛnɲɛnɛma minnu bɛ sanfɛla ni duguma-dakun na, olu ka jan ani u bɛ jɛgɛn; cɛmancɛ ta ta, u ka surun kosɛbɛ, wa u bɛ boli ka kɛɲɛ ni kɛnɛma layiniw ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ. O sabatilen in, i n’a fɔ fɛn tɔw bɛɛ, o bɛ fanga Di kɔ ma. Romɛkaw ka kogo kɔrɔw kalanden fɛ, cɛmancɛ-yɔrɔ bɛna ɲɔgɔndɛmɛ kabakoma dɔ di ni tilew sira finman ye min bɛ ɲɔgɔn falen-falen tuma bɛɛ ni kabakurun ye o fɛn kɔrɔw kabakomaw kɔnɔ, wa siga t’a la ko olu bɛ dɔ fara kosɛbɛ kabakurun dilanni fanga bonya kan.

Nka i n’a fɔ nin sigida fanga belebeleba in min bɛ tendinous tail kɔnɔ, o ma se, leviathan fanba bɛɛ sirilen bɛ ni warp ni woof ye min bɛ kɛ ni muscle fibres ni filaments ye, minnu bɛ tɛmɛ kɔkiliw fan fila bɛɛ fɛ ka boli ka jigin ka don flukes kɔnɔ, u bɛ fara u kan cogo la min tɛ hakili la, wa u bɛ dɔ fara u fanga kan kosɛbɛ walasa a kɔ la, balema bɛɛ fanga sumanikɛbali min bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ, o bɛ i n’a fɔ a bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ fo ka se yɔrɔ dɔ ma. Yala halakili bɛ se ka kɛ ka kɛ fɛn ye, nin tun ye fɛn ye min ka kan k’a kɛ.

O fana tɛ—a fanga kabakoma, hali dɔɔnin, o b’a fɛ k’a lamaga-lamagaliw kɔrɔtacogo ɲuman nalo; yɔrɔ min na nɔgɔya denmisɛnninya bɛ wuli ka tɛmɛn fanga Titanisme dɔ fɛ. O kɔfɛ, o lamaga-lamagaliw bɛ u cɛɲi siranninba sɔrɔ o de la. Fanga lakika tɛ cɛɲi walima bɛnkan tiɲɛ abada, nka tuma caman na, a bɛ o di; ani fɛn o fɛn cɛ ka ɲi kosɛbɛ, fanga bɛ caman kɛ ni subagaya ye. Aw ye sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ sirilenw bɔ yen, a bɛ iko u bɛɛ bɛ pɛrɛn ka bɔ marbre la min bɛ Hercules jalen kɔnɔ, o la, a cɛɲi tun bɛna ban. Eckerman min tun bɛ bato kɛ kosɛbɛ, o tun bɛ ka finimugu kɔrɔta ka bɔ Goethe su farilankolon na tuma min na, o cɛ disi belebeleba ye a degun, o min tun bɛ iko Romɛkaw ka se sɔrɔli jirisunba. Ni Angelo bɛ hali Fa Ala ja hadamaden cogo la, i ka taamasiyɛn kɛ barika min bɛ yen. Wa u bɛ se ka fɛn o fɛn jira Ala ka kanuya la Denkɛ kɔnɔ, Itali ja nɔgɔlenw, kurulen, cɛɲɔgɔnɲini, a hakilina kɛra minnu kɔnɔ ka ɲɛ kosɛbɛ; nin ja ninnu, hali n’u ka fanga bɛɛ dɛsɛlen don, u tɛ fanga si jira, nka musoya jugu dɔrɔn de don, n’o ye kololi ni muɲuli ye, min bɛ sɔn a ma bolo bɛɛ fɛ, o de bɛ a ka kalansiraw kɛcogo ɲuman danmadɔw kɛ.

Ne bɛ farikolo yɔrɔ min furakɛ, o ka sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ nɔgɔlen ye ten, fo a kɛra farikoloɲɛnajɛ la wo, walima ni timinandiya ye wo, walima dimi la wo, a kɛra dusukunnata suguya o suguya ye, a jiginniw bɛ taamasiyɛn kɛ tuma bɛɛ ni nɛɛma tɛmɛnen ye. O kɔnɔ, nsiirin bolo si tɛ se ka tɛmɛ a kan.

Lamaga-lamagaliba duuru ye a danma ye. A fɔlɔ, ni a kɛra fin ye walasa ka ɲɛtaa sɔrɔ; Filanan, ni a kɛra mace ye kɛlɛ la; Sabanan, bɔgɔdaga la; Naaninan, lobtailing (lobtailing) la; Duurunan, a bɛ kɛ peaking flukes kɔnɔ.

Fɔlɔ: Ikomi Leviatan kɔ bɛ jɔ a jɔyɔrɔ la, a bɛ baara kɛ cogo wɛrɛ la ni kɔgɔji kɔnɔfɛn tɔw bɛɛ kɔ ye. A tɛ wuli abada. Cɛ walima jɛgɛ la, sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ ye dɔgɔya taamasyɛn ye. Balema fɛ, a kùnkolo kelenpe de b'a Laɲini. Scroll-wise coiled forwards beneath the body, ka sɔrɔ ka teliya ka sprung kɔ, o de bɛ o darting kelen-kelen, panni lamaga di waraba ma ni a bɛ naji kɛ ni dimi ye. A kɛrɛfɛ-fiɲɛw bɛ Kɛ dɔrɔn ka bolifɛn Tɛmɛ.

Filanan: A nafa ka bon dɔɔnin, ko hali n’a y’a sɔrɔ ko balema cɛya kelen bɛ balema cɛya wɛrɛ kɛlɛ dɔrɔn n’a kunkolo n’a dawolo ye, o bɛɛ n’a ta, a ni hadamaden ka kɛlɛw la, a bɛ baara kɛ n’a kɔ ye kosɛbɛ ani ni danbe ye. Kurun dɔ gosi la, a b’a ka flukew kuru joona ka bɔ a la, wa o gosili bɛ kɛ kɔsegin dɔrɔn de fɛ. Ni a dilannen don fiɲɛ na min ma bali, kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la n’a jigira ka se a taamasiɲɛ ma, o tuma na fɛ, kirinni tɛ se ka bali dɔrɔn. Mɔgɔ walima kurun si tɛ se k’o muɲu. I ka kisili kelenpe de bɛ i yɛrɛ tanga o ma; nka n’a nana kɛrɛ fɛ ji kɛrɛfɛ, o tuma na, a yɔrɔ dɔ bɛ bɔ balema-kurun ka yeelen wulicogo la, ani a fɛnw ka wulicogo la, kɔkili karilen walima pankurun tigɛlen walima fila, sɔgɔli sugu dɔ kɛrɛ fɛ, o de ye a nɔ ye min ka jugu kosɛbɛ. O kɛrɛfɛ-bɔgɔdaga minnu bɛ ji la, olu bɛ Sɔrɔ tuma caman na mɔni na, fo u bɛ Jate denmisɛnninw ka tulonkɛ dɔrɔn ye. Dɔ bɛ fini dɔ bɔ, ani dingɛ bɛ jɔ.

Sabanan : N tɛ se k’a jira, nka a bɛ iko n bolo, ko balema na, magali hakilina bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ a kɔ la; sabu o siratigɛ la, dumuni duman dɔ bɛ a kɔnɔ min bɛ bɛn waraba jiribolo duman dɔrɔn ma. O dumuni duman in bɛ ye kosɛbɛ sɛgɛsɛgɛli kɛcogo la, n’o ye sunguru dususuman na, balema ni sumaya nɔgɔlen dɔ b’a ka bɔgɔdaga belebelebaw lamaga ka bɔ fan dɔ la ka taa fan wɛrɛ fɛ kɔgɔji sanfɛ ani n'a y'a ye ko kurunbolila kunsigi dɔrɔn don, bɔnɛ bɛ o kuruntigi, wuluwuluw ni bɛɛ. O magan fɔlɔ in na, dususuma don dɛ! Ni nin kɔ in tun bɛ ni fanga ye min bɛ se ka minɛ, ne tun ka kan ka n miiri o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ la Darmonodes ka waraba la min tun bɛ to ka taa falenfɛnw feereyɔrɔ la ten, ani ni foli dɔgɔmanw ye ka nuguw di npogotigininw ma, ka sɔrɔ k’u ka zonew lamaga. Jatebla caman kan ka tɛmɛ kelen kan, makari don ko balema tɛ o jogo ɲuman minɛcogo in sɔrɔ a kɔ la; sabu ne ye waraba wɛrɛ ko mɛn, n’a jogin na kɛlɛ la, a y’a kuru k’a ka jiribolo lamini ka nɛgɛso bɔ.

Naaninan: Ka sonyali kɛ balema kan k’a sɔrɔ i ma a dɔn kɔgɔji kelenw cɛmancɛ lakanani miirilen na, i b’a sɔrɔ a ma biri a ka bonya farikoloba la, wa a bɛ i n’a fɔ wuludennin, a bɛ tulon kɛ kɔgɔji kan i n’a fɔ tasumabɔyɔrɔ. Nka hali bi i b'a ka fanga Ye a ka tulonkɛ la. A kùnkolo-yɔrɔ janw bɛ 'sèn Bɔ fiɲɛ na ka Taa san fɛ; o kɔfɛ, ka dugukolo gosi, sanpɛrɛn bɛ mankan bɔ kilomɛtɛrɛ caman kɔnɔ. I bɛna a miiri ko marifaba dɔ bɔra; ani n’i ye lafiɲɛbɔlan nɔgɔlen kɔlɔsi min bɛ bɔ jirisunba la a dakun tɔ kelen na, i b’a miiri ko o de ye sisi ye min bɛ bɔ maga-maga-yɔrɔ la.

Duurunan: I n’a fɔ leviatan jɔcogo gansan na, flukew bɛ da a kɔ hakɛ jukɔrɔ kosɛbɛ, o kɔfɛ, u tɛ ye pewu a sanfɛla jukɔrɔ; nka n’a bɛ ɲini ka Dòn ‘kɔnɔna na, a ka bɔgɔdaga bɛɛ n’a farikolo mɛtɛrɛ bisaba ni kɔ bɛ Dòn fiɲɛ na, wa o cogo la, u bɛ To ka Yɛrɛyɛrɛ dɔɔnin, fo k’u Jigin ka Fɔ ka Bɔ mɔgɔw ɲɛ na. N’a ma fɔ o tiɲɛniba ye —yɔrɔ wɛrɛ la min bɛ se ka ɲɛfɔ — o balema ka bɔgɔbɔgɔli kuncɛlen in ye laala yecogo belebeleba ye min bɛ ye fɛnɲɛnamaw bɛɛ la. Ka bɔ dugukolo jugumanw na minnu tɛ duguma, a bɛ iko a kɔba in bɛ ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ sankolo sanfɛla la. O la sa, sugo la, yala ne ye Sitanɛ belebeleba ye k’a ka nɛgɛ belebeleba tɔɔrɔlen bɔ ka bɔ Jahannama Baltik tasuma na wa. Nka o ɲɛnajɛ suguw filɛli la, i bɛ cogo min na, o bɛɛ bɛ kɛ; ni dankan na , jinɛw bɛna na i hakili la ; ni Esayi ta la, mɛlɛkɛ kuntigiw. N jɔlen n ka kurun kuncɛ la tilebɔ waati la min ye sankolo ni kɔgɔji bilen, n ye balema kuluba dɔ ye siɲɛ kelen kɔrɔn fɛ, u bɛɛ bɛ ka taa tile fan fɛ, wa waati dɔɔnin kɔnɔ u bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ ni flukes kuncɛlenw ye. I n’a fɔ a tun bɛ ne ɲɛ na o waati la cogo min na, o ala batocogo belebeleba sugu ma ye abada, hali Pɛrɛsi jamana na, tasuma batobagaw ka so. I n’a fɔ Ptolémée Filopater ye seereya kɛ cogo min na Afiriki waraba ko la, o kɔfɛ, ne ye seereya kɛ balema in ko la, k’a fɔ ko a ye fɛn ye min bɛ Ala bato ka tɛmɛ danfɛnw bɛɛ kan. Sabu masakɛ Juba ka fɔ la, kɔrɔlen sɔrɔdasi waraw tun bɛ to ka sɔgɔma tanu n’u ka jirisunw kɔrɔtalen ye makun juguba kɔnɔ.

Nin tilayɔrɔba in kɔnɔ, balema ni waraba cɛ, fo ka se kelen kɔ ni tɔ kelen kɔkolo fan dɔw ma, o man kan ka kɛ sababu ye k’o farikolo yɔrɔ fila minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔsɔ, olu bila bɛnkan kan, u kelen-kelen bɛɛ bɛ danfɛn minnu na, olu fana man kan ka kɛ. Sabu, i n’a fɔ waraba barikamaba ye Leviatan ka wuluwulu ye cogo min na, o cogo kelen na, n’i y’a suma ni Leviatan kɔ ye, a jiribolo ye lili ye dɔrɔn. Gosili min ka jugu kosɛbɛ ka bɔ waraba ka jiribolo la, o kɛra i n’a fɔ fiɲɛbɔlan tulonkɛ-pompi, n’i y’a suma ni balema-sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ ka bɔgɔdaga gɛlɛnw sɔgɔsɔgɔli n’u bɔgɔbɔgɔli ye min tɛ se ka suman, minnu bɛ segin-ka-bɔnye kɛ, minnu bɛ segin-ka-bɔnye kɛ, kelen kɔfɛ, ka kurun kuuru bɛɛ fili fiɲɛ na, n’u ka kurunw n’u ka kurun kɔnɔmɔgɔw bɛɛ ye, i n’a fɔ Ɛndu nɛgɛsotigi dɔ b’a ka bolow fili cogo min na.

*Hali n’a y’a sɔrɔ ko ɲɔgɔndan bɛɛ bɛ kɛ balema ni waraba cɛ camanba siratigɛ la, o ye fɛn ye min tɛ fosi ye, bawo o kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen na, waraba jɔlen bɛ balema siratigɛ la cogo kelen na, wulu bɛ min kɛ waraba la o bɛɛ n’a ta, fɛn dɔw tɛ ɲini minnu bɛ tali kɛ ɲininkaliw la; o dɔw ye sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ ye. A dɔnna kosɛbɛ ko tuma caman na, waraba bɛna ji walima buguri sama a ka jiribolo la, ka sɔrɔ k’a kɔrɔta, k’a wuli ka taa baji dɔ kɔnɔ.

Ni n bɛ nin kɔ barikama in jateminɛ ka taa a fɛ, n bɛ n dusu kasi ka taa a fɛ n ka sebaliya k’a fɔ. Tuma dɔw la, bololabaara dɔw bɛ kɛ a kɔnɔ, hali n’u bɛna hadamaden bolo diya kosɛbɛ, u bɛ to u ɲɛfɔbali ye pewu. Sagakuluba dɔ kɔnɔ, min bɛ mɔgɔ kabakoya kosɛbɛ, tuma dɔw la, nin gundolafɛnw bɛ yen, fo n ye kungokɔnɔfɛnw ɲininikɛlaw mɛn minnu y’a jira k’u ni Free-Mason taamasiyɛnw ni taamasiyɛnw bɛ tali kɛ ɲɔgɔn na; ko balema in, tiɲɛ na, o fɛɛrɛw fɛ, a ye baro kɛ ni hakili ye ni diɲɛ ye. Balema lamaga-lamagali wɛrɛw fana t’a fɛ a farikolo bɛɛ lajɛlen na, min falen bɛ kabako la, wa a tɛ jate a binkannikɛla ye min ka ko dɔn kosɛbɛ. Dissect a cogo min na ne bɛ se, o tuma, ne nka taa fari juguman; Ne t’a dɔn, wa ne tɛna a dɔn abada. Nka ni ne tɛ hali balema in kɔ dɔn, a kunkolo faamuya cogo di? caman wɛrɛ, a ɲɛda faamuya cogo di, k'a sɔrɔ ɲɛda tɛ a la? I na ne kɔ yɔrɔw ye, ne kɔ, a bɛ iko a b’a fɔ, nka ne ɲɛda tɛna ye. Nka n tɛ se k’a kɔ yɔrɔw dɔn pewu; ani ka hint a bɛna min kɛ a ɲɛda ko la, n b'a fɔ tugun ko ɲɛda tɛ a la.





SABABU 87. Armada belebeleba.

Malaka gun jan ni surun, min bɛ taa saheli-kɔrɔn fɛ ka bɔ Birmah maraw la, o bɛ kɛ Azi jamana bɛɛ saheli yɔrɔ ye. Layini dɔ la min tɛ ban ka bɔ o gun in na, Sumatra, Java, Bally ani Timor gun janw bɛ wuli; minnu ni caman wɛrɛw bɛ kɛ mɔli belebeleba ye, walima kogoba dɔ, min bɛ Azi ni Ɔsitarali cɛ janya la, ani ka Ɛndu kɔgɔji jan min ma kari, o tila ka bɔ kɔrɔnfɛ gunw na minnu falen bɛ jirisunba la. O kogo in bɛ sɔgɔ ni sally-port caman ye walasa kurunw ni balemaw ka nɔgɔya; minnu bɛ ye minnu cɛma, olu ye Sunda ni Malacca bajiw ye. Sunda kɔgɔjida la, kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la, kurun minnu bɛ Taa Sinuwa ka Bɔ tlebin fɛ, olu bɛ Bɔ Sinuwa kɔgɔjiw la.

O Sunda kɔji misɛnninw bɛ Sumatra ni Java cɛ; ani a jɔlen bɛ cɛmancɛ la o gunw kogo belebeleba in kɔnɔ, o kɔji jɛman jagɛlɛn min bɛ a sabati, kɔgɔjilamɔgɔw bɛ min dɔn ko Java Head; u tɛ bɛn dɔɔnin cɛmancɛda la min bɛ da wuli ka don mansamara belebeleba dɔw kɔnɔ minnu kogolen don: wa n’i y’a jateminɛ ko nafolo min tɛ ban, n’o ye tulumafɛnw ye, ani silikiw, ani kabakurunw, ani sanu, ani nɛgɛso, o kɔrɔnfɛla kɔgɔji gun ba caman bɛ nafolo sɔrɔ ni minnu ye, a bɛ iko o ye sigida labɛn nafama ye, ko o nafolo suguw, dugukolo jɔli yɛrɛ fɛ, a dɔgɔyalenba ka kan ka muɲu a yecogo la, o bɛɛ n’a ta nafa tɛ min na, k’a kɔlɔsi ka bɔ tilebin diɲɛ kɔnɔ min bɛ fɛn bɛɛ minɛ. Sunda kɔgɔjida kɔgɔjida la, o kɛlɛbolo fangatigiw tɛ sɔrɔ minnu bɛ Mediterane, Baltik ani Propontis dondaw kɔlɔsi. O kɔrɔnyanfan mɔgɔw tɛ i n’a fɔ Danmakɛw, u tɛ bonya dabɔlen ɲini sanfɛ-kurunw jiginlenw na ka bɔ kurunw ka taama banbali la fiɲɛ ɲɛfɛ, minnu tɛmɛna san kɛmɛ caman tɛmɛnenw kɔnɔ, su fɛ ani tile fɛ, Sumatra ni Java gunw cɛ, minnu bɛ doni ta ni kɔrɔnfɛla doni sɔngɔ gɛlɛnw ye. Nka hali n’u bɛ ban hɔrɔnya la seli dɔ la min bɛ i n’a fɔ nin, u tɛ ban u ka fɔta la cogo si la ko u bɛ se ka kɛ ni jatebɔ barikama ye ka tɛmɛ o kan.

Waati bɔlen kɔ an hakili la, Malaykaw ka jagokɛlaw ka proas, minnu bɛ u dogo Sumatra kɔw ni gunw cɛma, minnu sumayalen don kosɛbɛ, olu ye kurunw kan minnu bɛ tɛmɛ kɔjiw fɛ, ka jago ɲini kosɛbɛ u ka tamaw kun na. Sisan, hali n’o farikoloba suguw bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ tuma dɔw la, o bɛɛ n’a ta, i n’a fɔ a ka kan ka ye cogo min na, hali bi, kulu misɛnnin minnu faralen don ɲɔgɔn kan, olu bɛ kɔlɔsi tuma dɔw la, minnu kelen-kelen bɛɛ bɛ mɔgɔ mugan fo bi duuru minɛ. O fɔlikulu suguw bɛ Weele ko lakɔlisow. A ka c’a la, u ye sugu fla ye; minnu bɛ se ka kɛ musow ye, ani minnu tɛ mɔgɔ si lajɛ ni cɛ barikama denmisɛnninw tɛ, walima misiw, i n’a fɔ u bɛ fɔ cogo min na.

Sotigiw ka taa musow ka lakɔli la, i bɛ cɛ dɔ ye tuma bɛɛ min bonya dafalen don, nka a ma kɔrɔbaya; min, ni alarma o alarma kɛra, a b’a ka jagɛlɛya jira k’a bin kɔfɛ k’a ka musow ka boli datugu. Tiɲɛ na, nin cɛkɔrɔba in ye Ottoman cɛɲi ye, a bɛ jibɔ diɲɛ ji la, a lamini na ni harem ka dususalo ni kanu bɛɛ ye. Danfara min bɛ nin Ottoman in n’a musoɲɔgɔnw cɛ, o bɛ mɔgɔ kabakoya; sabu, k’a sɔrɔ a bɛ leviatan hakɛ cayalenba la tuma bɛɛ, musow, hali n’u bɛ bonya dafalen na, u tɛ tɛmɛ cɛ hakɛ danmadɔ fanba tilancɛ kelen kan. U ka nɔgɔn ni u bɛ tali kɛ ɲɔgɔn na, tiɲɛ na; N b’a ja gɛlɛya k’a fɔ ko, a kana tɛmɛ yaada tan ni tila kan ka cɛya lamini. O bɛɛ n’a ta, a tɛ se ka sɔsɔ, ko u bɛɛ lajɛlen na, u bɛ se ka en bon point sɔrɔ ciyɛn fɛ.

A ka di kosɛbɛ ka nin harem in n’a matigi kɔlɔsi u ka sɛgɛnlafiɲɛbɔw la. I n’a fɔ modɛliw, u bɛ taama na fo abada ka fɛn suguya caman ɲini lafiya la. I b’u kunbɛn Layini kan a waati la Ekwatori balo waati falen dafalen kama, k’a sɔrɔ i seginna sisan dɔrɔn, laala, ka bɔ samiyɛ kɛli la Worodugu kɔgɔjiw la, wa o cogo la ka samiyɛ nanbara sɛgɛn ni funteni duman bɛɛ la. Ni u ye 'yɔrɔ Lamini ka Taa san fɛ ka Jigin Equator (Equateur) taamayɔrɔ la waati dɔ la, u bɛ Daminɛ kɔrɔnfɛla ji la ka nɛnɛ waati makɔnɔ yen, wa o cogo la, u bɛ u yɛrɛ tanga san funteni tɛmɛnen tɔw ma.

Ni n bɛ taa ɲɛ ni hakilisigi ye o taama ninnu dɔ la kelen na, ni fɛn kabakoma dɔw ye minnu bɛ mɔgɔ sikɛ, n matigi balema bɛ a ɲɛ jɔlen to a ka denbaya nafama na. Ni mɔgɔ dɔ ka kan ka Leviatan kamalennin dɔ bali ka na o sira fɛ cogo la min tɛ se ka kɛ, i k’a miiri ko i bɛ gɛrɛ muso dɔ la gundo la, ni dimi kabakoma ye cogo min na, Bashaw bɛ bin a kan, k’a gɛn! Waati kɔrɔw, tiɲɛ na, ni sariyakolo tɛ minnu na i n’a fɔ ale, olu ka kan ka sɔn ka don sokɔnɔna nisɔndiya senuya la; hali ni Bashaw bɛna min kɛ, a tɛ se ka Lothario tɔgɔjuguba to a ka dilan kan; sabu, bɔnɛ ye! jɛgɛ dilan bɛɛ bɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ. Kɔgɔjida la, musow ka teli ka duel jugubaw kɛ u ka ɲɔgɔndankɛlaw cɛma; o cogo kelen na ni balemafɛnw ye, minnu bɛ na kɛlɛ fagalen na tuma dɔw la, wa u bɛɛ bɛ na kanuya de kama. U bɛ kogo Kɛ n’u duguma-dakun janw ye, tuma dɔw la k’u siri ɲɔgɔn na, wa o cogo la k’u cɛsiri ka fanga Sɔrɔ i n’a fɔ wulu minnu b’u ka wuluw siri ɲɔgɔn na ni kɛlɛ ye. Mɔgɔ damadɔ tɛ minɛ ni o ɲɔgɔnye ninnu nɔ jugumanw bɛ u la,—kunkolow kurulen, ɲinw karilen, wuluwulu sɔgɔlen; ani a dɔw la, da sɔgɔlenw ni da wulilenw.

Nka n’an y’a miiri ko sokɔnɔna nisɔndiya binkannikɛla bɛna a yɛrɛ ta ka taa harem matigi ka boli fɔlɔ la, o tuma na, yala a bɛ mɔgɔ bila ka o matigi kɔlɔsi kosɛbɛ wa. A b’a ka jamaba jira u cɛma ni dususuma ye tugun, ka ɲɛnajɛ kɛ yen waati dɔ kɔnɔ, hali bi a bɛ yɔrɔ la min bɛ Lotari kamalennin lafili, i n’a fɔ Solomani Alaɲɛsiranbaga min bɛ bato kɛ ni timinandiya ye a muso ba kelen cɛma. Ni mɔnikɛlaw y’a to balema wɛrɛw ka kɛ ɲɛ na, a man teli ka tugu o Turkiba ninnu dɔ kɔ; sabu nin Turkiba ninnu fanga ka bon kojugu, o de kosɔn u ka nɔgɔlenya ka dɔgɔ. U bɛ denkɛw ni denmuso minnu bange, mun na, o denkɛw ni denmusow ka kan k’u janto u yɛrɛ la; a dɔgɔyalenba la, ni denba ka dɛmɛ dɔrɔn de ye. Sabu i n’a fɔ kanubaga minnu bɛ yaala-yaala fɛn bɛɛ dunbaga wɛrɛw la, n’u tɔgɔ bɛ se ka fɔ, n Matigi Balema tɛ denmarayɔrɔ diya, hali ni bower (bower) ka di a ye cogo o cogo; wa o cogo la, k’a sɔrɔ a ye taamakɛla ŋana ye, a b’a denw to yen minnu tɔgɔ ma fɔ diɲɛ fan bɛɛ; den bɛɛ ye exotic ye. O bɛɛ n’a ta, waati ɲuman na, ni funankɛninw ka diya bɛ dɔgɔya; ni sanw ni binkannikɛyɔrɔw bɛ ka caya; i n’a fɔ hakilijakabɔ b’a lafiɲɛ kosɛbɛ cogo min na; kuma surun na, i n’a fɔ sɛgɛnba dɔ bɛ Turki fasalen minɛ cogo min na; o kɔfɛ, nɔgɔya ni jogo ɲuman kanu bɛ npogotigininw kanu nɔ bila; an ka Ottoman bɛ don ɲɛnamaya taabolo la min tɛ se ka nimisa, min bɛ nimisa, min bɛ laadi, ka kali, ka harem tiɲɛ, ka bonya ka kɛ misali ye, ni kɔrɔ sulky, a bɛ taa a kelen na meridianw ni parallels cɛma k’a ka deliliw fɔ, ani ka Leviathan kamalennin kelen-kelen bɛɛ lasɔmi a ka kanuya filiw la.

Sisan, i n’a fɔ mɔnikɛlaw b’a Weele ko balema-yɔrɔ min bɛ balema-yɔrɔ la, o cogo kelen na, o lakɔli matigi n’a matigi bɛ Weele cogo min na fɛɛrɛko siratigɛ la ko lakɔlikaramɔgɔw. O la, a tɛ jogo gɛlɛn na, hali n’a kɛra satiriki ye min bɛ se ka tanu, ko a yɛrɛ taalen kɔ lakɔli la, o kɔfɛ, a ka kan ka taa jamana wɛrɛ la, a ye min kalan yen, a kana o don a la, nka a ka nalomanya don a la. A ka tɔgɔ min ye, n’o ye lakɔlikaramɔgɔw ye, a bɛ iko a bɔra tɔgɔ la min dira harem yɛrɛ ma, nka dɔw y’a miiri ko cɛ min ye nin balema Ottoman suguya in tɔgɔ da fɔlɔ o cogo la, n’a sɔrɔla o ye Vidocq ka hakilinaw kalan, k’a yɛrɛ ladɔnniya ko o Faransikɛ tɔgɔba in tun ye jamana-kalanso ɲɛmɔgɔ sugu jumɛn ye a denmisɛnman, ani o jinɛweleli kalan suguw tun ye mun ye a donna a ka kalanden dɔw la.

Lakɔlikaramɔgɔw ka balema bɛ a yɛrɛ minɛ cogo min na a ka kɔrɔbayali la, o yɔrɔ kelen de bɛ kɛ balema kɔrɔlenw bɛɛ la. A bɛ se ka fɔ ko diɲɛ bɛɛ kɔnɔ, balema kelen—i n’a fɔ a bɛ fɔ cogo min na ko Leviatan kelenpe—o b’a jira ko balema kɔrɔlen don. I n’a fɔ Daniel Boone min ye moss-bearded bonyalen ye, mɔgɔ si tɛna kɛ a kɛrɛfɛ ni Nature yɛrɛ tɛ; ani a b'a ta ka kɛ muso ye ji kungokolon kɔnɔ, wa a ye muso bɛɛ la ɲuman ye, hali n'a bɛ gundo caman mara minnu bɛ mɔgɔ bila ka miiri.

Lakɔli minnu tɛ mɔgɔ si dabɔ ni cɛ fitininw ni cɛ barikamaw tɛ, n’an kofɔra ka tɛmɛ, olu bɛ danfaraba jira ni harem lakɔlisow ye. Sabu hali n’o balema musomanw ye maloyabagaw ye u ka jogo la, cɛdenmisɛnw, walima misi binaani-barili, i n’a fɔ u b’u wele cogo min na, olu de ka jugu kosɛbɛ ka tɛmɛ Leviatanw bɛɛ kan, wa ntalen na, farati ka bon u kunbɛnni na fo o balema kabakomaw kunkolo jɛmanw, grizzled, minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ tuma dɔw la, wa ninnu bɛna i kɛlɛ i n’a fɔ jinɛ juguw minnu dimina ni penal gout ye.

Barili binaani-bull lakɔlisow ka bon ni harem lakɔlisow ye. I n’a fɔ kolɛji kamalenninw ka jamakulu, u falen bɛ kɛlɛ, ɲɛnajɛ ani juguya la, u bɛ ka bin diɲɛ fan bɛɛ la ni hakilintanya ye, ni nisɔndiya ye, fo sɛbɛnnikɛla hakilitigi si tɛna u asiransi kɛ i n’a fɔ a bɛna kɛ cogo min na Yale walima Harvard cɛden ɲagaminen dɔ fɛ. U tɛ mɛn o ɲagami in dabila kɔni, wa ni u tilancɛ saba ɲɔgɔn kɔrɔla, u bɛ faranfasi, ka taa u dan na sigiyɔrɔw ɲinini na, o kɔrɔ ye ko haremw.

Danfara wɛrɛ min bɛ cɛw ni musow ka lakɔlisow cɛ, o ye cɛya ni musoya taamaʃyɛn ye halibi. A fɔ ko i ye misi binaani-barili dɔ gosi—jinɛ faantan! a jɛɲɔgɔnw bɛɛ y' a bila. Nka i ka harem lakɔliden dɔ gosi, a taamaɲɔgɔnw bɛ jigin a lamini na ni haminanko taamasiyɛn bɛɛ ye, tuma dɔw la, u bɛ to a kɛrɛfɛ kosɛbɛ ani ka mɛn, fo u yɛrɛw walasa ka bin fɛn dɔ kan.
