Kuv yuav tau pleev xim ntev rau koj nrog rau ib tus tuaj yeem tsis muaj canvas, ib yam dab tsi zoo li daim ntawv tiag tiag ntawm whale raws li nws tshwm sim rau lub qhov muag ntawm tus whaleman thaum nyob rau hauv nws tus kheej lub cev tag nrho cov whale yog moored nrog whale-nkoj kom nws thiaj li yuav ncaj ncees nce mus rau qhov ntawd. Nws yog lub sijhawm los teeb tsa lub ntiaj teb txoj cai hauv qhov teeb meem no, los ntawm kev ua pov thawj xws li cov duab ntawm whale txhua yam tsis raug.

Nws zoo nkaus li zoo li tus Tsov tus tw ntawm ib qho anaconda, dua li cov xib teg dav ntawm qhov tseeb whale lub majestic flukes.

Tab sis mus rau lub qub Galleries, thiab tam sim no saib ib tug zoo kawg thiab Christian painting tus duab ntawm tus ntses no; vim nws ua tsis tau zoo dua li cov antediluvian Hindoo. Nws muaj ib hom howdah ntawm nws nraub qaum, thiab nws lub qhov ncauj distended tusked nyob rau hauv uas lub billows dov, tej zaum yuav raug coj mus rau lub Traitors 'Gate uas coj los ntawm Thames los ntawm dej mus rau hauv lub Ntauwd. Tom qab ntawd, muaj Prodromus whales ntawm qub Scotch Sibbald, thiab Jonah's whale, raws li tau piav qhia nyob rau hauv cov ntawv luam ntawm cov ntawv qub qub thiab txiav cov qub qub. Yuav hais li cas txog cov no? Raws li rau phau ntawv-binder lub whale winding zoo li ib tug vine-stalk puag ncig lub Tshuag ntawm ib tug nqis thauj tog rau nkoj - raws li stamped thiab gilded nyob rau sab nraub qaum thiab cov nplooj ntawv npe ntawm ntau phau ntawv qub thiab tshiab - uas yog ib qho zoo nkauj heev tab sis zoo kawg nkaus tsiaj txhu, imitated, kuv coj nws, los ntawm cov duab zoo li qub ntawm cov vases qub. Txawm hais tias universally denominated ib tug ntses taub ntswg ntev, txawm li cas los xij, kuv tseem hu rau phau ntawv no-binder tus ntses ib sim ntawm whale; vim tias nws tau npaj siab heev thaum lub cuab yeej tau tshaj tawm thawj zaug. Nws tau qhia los ntawm ib tug qub Italian tshaj tawm qhov chaw nyob rau hauv lub xyoo pua 15th, thaum lub sij hawm Revival of Learning; thiab nyob rau hauv cov hnub ntawd, thiab txawm mus rau ib lub sij hawm piv rau lig, ntses taub ntswg ntev tau nrov yuav tsum yog ib hom ntawm Leviathan.

Nyob rau hauv vignettes thiab lwm yam embellishments ntawm ib co ancient phau ntawv koj yuav nyob rau lub sij hawm ntsib nrog xav paub heev kov ntawm whale, qhov twg txhua yam ntawm spouts, dav hlau d'eau, kub springs thiab txias, Saratoga thiab Baden-Baden, tuaj bubbling los ntawm nws lub hlwb unexhausted. Nyob rau hauv lub npe-nplooj ntawv ntawm thawj tsab ntawm "Kev Kawm Ua Ntej" koj yuav pom qee qhov xav paub whales.

Tab sis txiav tawm tag nrho cov kev sim tsis ua haujlwm no, cia peb saib ntawm cov duab ntawm leviathan uas qhia txog kev ua siab ntev, kev paub txog kev tshawb fawb, los ntawm cov neeg paub. Nyob rau hauv cov qub Harris sau los ntawm voyages muaj ib co daim hlau ntawm whales rho tawm los ntawm Dutch phau ntawv ntawm voyages, AD 1671, muaj cai "A Whaling Voyage rau Spitzbergen nyob rau hauv lub nkoj Jonas nyob rau hauv lub Whale, Peter Peterson ntawm Friesland, tswv." Nyob rau hauv ib qho ntawm cov phiaj, cov whales, zoo li cov rafts loj ntawm cov cav, yog cov sawv cev ntawm cov dej khov-isles, nrog cov bears dawb khiav hla lawv lub nraub qaum. Nyob rau hauv lwm lub phaj, lub prodigious blunder yog ua los ntawm sawv cev rau whale nrog perpendicular flukes.

Kuv tsis ntseeg tsis yog tus thawj coj muaj daim duab veracious no coj los siv rau nws cov tub rog. Hais txog tab sis ib yam hais txog nws, cia kuv hais tias nws muaj qhov muag uas siv, raws li qhov ntsuas nrog, mus rau qhov loj hlob phev whale, yuav ua rau lub qhov muag ntawm tus whale ib hneev-qhov rai qee qhov ntev tsib taw. Au, kuv tus thawj tub rog uas muaj hwjchim, ua cas nej tsis muab Yauna ntsia ntawm qhov muag ntawd rau peb!

Tsis yog cov feem ntau conscientious compilations ntawm Natural History rau cov txiaj ntsim ntawm cov tub ntxhais hluas thiab kev sib tw, dawb los ntawm tib heinousness ntawm yuam kev. Saib cov hauj lwm nrov "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." Nyob rau hauv tsab ntawv London tsab ntawm 1807, muaj cov phiajcim ntawm "whale" thiab "narwhale." Kuv tsis xav kom zoo li inelegant, tab sis qhov no unsightly whale zoo li ib tug amputated sow; thiab, raws li rau tus narwhale, ib ntsia ntawm nws yog txaus rau amaze ib tug, tias nyob rau hauv lub xyoo pua cuaj caug xyoo no xws li ib tug hippogriff yuav tsum tau palmed rau tiag tiag rau txhua tus neeg txawj ntse ntawm cov tub ntxhais kawm ntawv.

Tom qab ntawd, dua, nyob rau hauv 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, ib tug zoo naturalist, luam tawm ib tug scientific systemized whale phau ntawv, uas yog ob peb daim duab ntawm ntau hom ntawm Leviathan. Tag nrho cov no tsis yog tsis yog xwb, tab sis daim duab ntawm Mysticetus los yog Greenland whale (uas yog hais tias, Txoj Cai whale), txawm tias Scoresby, ib tug txiv neej ntev ntev li kov cov tsiaj, tshaj tawm tias tsis muaj nws tus yam ntxwv.

Tab sis qhov muab lub hau-sheaf rau tag nrho cov blundering lag luam no tau tshwj tseg rau scientific Frederick Cuvier, tus kwv tij rau lub npe nrov Baron. Xyoo 1836, nws tau luam tawm Phau Ntawv Keeb Kwm Ntawm Whales, uas nws muab qhov nws hu ua daim duab ntawm Sperm Whale. Ua ntej qhia cov duab ntawd rau ib qho Nantucketer, koj tau muab qhov zoo tshaj plaws rau koj cov ntsiab lus tawm ntawm Nantucket. Hauv ib lo lus, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale tsis yog Sperm Whale, tab sis yog squash. Tau kawg, nws yeej tsis tau txais txiaj ntsig ntawm kev caij nkoj whaling (cov txiv neej zoo li no tsis tshua muaj), tab sis thaum twg nws tau txais daim duab ntawd, leej twg tuaj yeem qhia tau? Tej zaum nws tau txais nws raws li nws qhov kev tshawb fawb ua ntej hauv tib daim teb, Desmarest, tau txais ib qho ntawm nws qhov tseeb rho menyuam; uas yog, los ntawm Suav daim duab. Thiab dab tsi ntawm cov menyuam yaus nrog cov cwj mem uas Suav yog, ntau lub khob khob thiab saucers qhia peb.

Raws li cov kws kos duab kos npe 'whale pom nyob rau hauv txoj kev dai ntawm cov khw muag roj, yuav hais li cas rau lawv? Txawm hais tias ntxhw tau sawv rau lawv qhov ntev, Leviathan uas muaj sia nyob tseem tsis tau floated nws tus kheej rau nws cov duab. Lub whale nyob, nyob rau hauv nws lub majesty thiab qhov tseem ceeb, tsuas yog pom nyob rau hauv hiav txwv nyob rau hauv unfathomable dej; thiab ntog rau ntawm nws qhov loj ntawm qhov tsis pom, zoo li lub nkoj uas tau pib ua rog; thiab tawm ntawm lub ntsiab lus ntawd nws yog ib yam uas nyob mus ib txhis tsis muaj peev xwm rau tib neeg hauv ntiaj teb los nqa nws lub cev mus rau saum huab cua, txhawm rau khaws tag nrho nws cov o muaj zog thiab undulations. Thiab, tsis txhob hais txog qhov sib txawv ntawm qhov contour ntawm ib tug hluas nqus whale thiab loj hlob Platonian Leviathan; Txawm li cas los xij, txawm hais tias ib tug ntawm cov tub ntxhais hluas nqus whales hoisted rau lub nkoj lub lawj, xws li yog lub outlandish, zoo li eel-zoo li, limbered, txawv ntawm nws, hais tias nws meej qhia dab ntxwg nyoog nws tus kheej yuav ntes tsis tau.

QHOV 56. Ntawm Cov Duab Tsis Txaus Siab ntawm Whales, thiab cov duab tseeb ntawm Whaling Scenes.

Hauv kev sib txuas nrog cov duab zoo nkauj ntawm whales, Kuv tau sim siab ntawm no kom nkag mus rau cov dab neeg tseem ceeb ntawm lawv uas yuav tsum muaj nyob hauv qee phau ntawv, ob qho tib si thaum ub thiab niaj hnub, tshwj xeeb tshaj yog hauv Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, thiab lwm yam. Tab sis kuv dhau qhov teeb meem los ntawm.

Kuv paub tsuas yog plaub daim ntawv luam tawm ntawm tus phev Whale zoo; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, thiab Beale's. Hauv tshooj dhau los Colnett thiab Cuvier tau raug xa mus. Huggins's yog qhov zoo dua li lawv li; tab sis, los ntawm qhov tsis txaus ntseeg, Beale's yog qhov zoo tshaj plaws. Tag nrho Beale cov duab kos ntawm lub whale no zoo, tshwj tsis yog cov duab nruab nrab hauv daim duab ntawm peb lub whales hauv ntau tus cwj pwm, capping nws tshooj thib ob. Nws frontispiece, tej nkoj nquam tawm tsam Sperm Whales, txawm hais tias tsis muaj qhov tsis ntseeg tau suav tias yuav ua rau muaj kev ntxhov siab rau pej xeem tsis txaus ntseeg ntawm qee tus txiv neej parlor, yog qhov tseeb thiab zoo li lub neej zoo li nws cov txiaj ntsig. Nws muaj tab sis ib daim duab ntawm whaling scenes, thiab qhov no yog ib tug tu siab tsis txaus, vim hais tias nws yog los ntawm xws li cov duab xwb, thaum tag nrho cov ua tau zoo, uas koj muaj peev xwm muab tau ib yam dab tsi zoo li lub tswv yim tseeb ntawm lub whale nyob raws li pom los ntawm nws cov neeg yos hav zoov.
