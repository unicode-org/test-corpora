VAHE 94. Ko e Faka'uli 'o e Nima.

Ko e tafola ko ia ʻa Stubb, ʻa ia naʻe fakatau mamafa ʻaupito, naʻe ʻomi totonu ia ki he tafaʻaki ʻo e Pequod, ʻa ia naʻe fou maʻu pē ai ʻa e ngaahi ngāue motuhi mo e hiki kotoa ko ia naʻe fakaikiiki kimuʻá, ʻo aʻu ki he baling ʻo e Heidelburgh Tun, pe Case.

Lolotonga e femoʻuekina ʻa e niʻihi ʻi he fatongia fakamuimui ko ʻení, naʻe fakangāueʻi ha niʻihi ʻi hono toho ʻo e ngaahi pā lalahi angé, ʻi he taimi pē naʻe fakafonu ai ʻa e huhuʻa tangatá; pea ʻi he aʻu mai ʻa e taimi totonú, naʻe fakaʻaongaʻi fakalelei ʻa e sperm tatau ko ʻení kimuʻa pea toki ʻalu ki he ngaahi try-works, ʻa ia ko e anon.

Naʻe momoko mo fakaʻau ke sioʻata ʻo aʻu ki ha tuʻunga, ʻi heʻeku tangutu hifo mo ha niʻihi kehe ʻi muʻa ʻi ha kaukau lahi ʻa Konisitanitaine ʻi aí, ne u ʻiloʻi ʻoku simaʻi ngali kehe ia ʻo hoko ko ha ngaahi foʻi ʻuhikiʻi pulu, ʻi heni mo hena ʻoku takai holo ʻi he konga vai. Ko ʻemau pisinisí ke toe lomiʻi ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻuhikiʻi ko ʻení ke hoko ko ha vai. Ko ha fatongia melie mo taʻeʻaonga! ‘Oku ‘ikai ha ofo ‘i he kuonga mu‘á ko e huhu‘a huhu‘a ko ení ko ha me‘a teuteu manakoa pehē ia. Ko ha mahino ange pehē! ko ha me‘a fakamelie pehē! ko ha me‘a fakamolū pehē! ko e molifier fakafiefia pehe! Hili ʻeku ʻi ai hoku ongo nimá ʻi ha ngaahi miniti siʻi pē, naʻe ongoʻi ʻe hoku ongo louhiʻi nimá ʻo hangē ha fanga ʻeá, pea kamata, ʻo hangē haʻane laú, ke serpentine mo spiralise.

ʻI heʻeku tangutu ʻi ai ʻi heʻeku fiemālié, ʻo fakakolosi hoku vaʻé ʻi he funga vaká; hili ʻa e feinga mamahi ʻi he windlass; ʻi lalo ʻi ha langi nonga lanu pulū; ko e vaka ʻi he lalo folau taʻeʻaongá, pea ʻoku lele fakanonga pehē ʻo ʻalu atu; ʻi heʻeku kaukauʻi hoku ongo nimá ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi foʻi pulupulu molū mo anga-malū ko ia ʻo e ngaahi tisiū kuo hū ki loto, ʻa ia naʻe lalanga ʻi he meimei houa ʻe tahá; ʻo hangē ko ʻenau motuhi koloaʻia ki hoku louhiʻi nimá, mo fakaʻauha ʻenau koloa kotoa pē, ʻo hangē ko e kālepi motuʻa kakató ʻenau uainé; ʻi heʻeku ngingila ʻa e nanamu taʻeʻuli ko iá, —ʻi he founga moʻoní mo moʻoní, ʻo hangē ko e nanamu ʻo e ngaahi vaioleti ʻo e faʻahitaʻu failaú; ʻOku ou fakahā atu kiate kimoutolu, ʻi he taimi ko iá naʻá ku moʻui ʻo hangē ʻi ha vaotā musie; Naʻe ngalo kotoa ʻiate au ʻema fuakava fakalilifú; ʻi he huhuʻa taʻefakamatalaʻi ko iá, ne u fufulu ai hoku ongo nimá mo hoku lotó; Naʻá ku meimei kamata ke fakamālōʻiaʻi ʻa e tui fakalotu motuʻa ʻa Palaselá ʻo pehē ʻoku ʻi ai ha ʻulungaanga lelei ʻoku tātātaha ke maʻu ʻe he huhuʻá ʻi hono fakanonga ʻo e vela ʻo e ʻitá; lolotonga ʻeku kaukau ʻi he kaukauʻanga ko iá, ne u ongoʻi tauʻatāina fakalangi mei he loto kovi kotoa pē, pe loto-kovi, pe loto-kovi, ʻi ha faʻahinga meʻa pē.

Tatau! tatau! tatau! ʻi he pongipongi kotoa pē; Naʻá ku lomiʻi ʻa e huhuʻa huhuʻa ko iá kae ʻoua kuó u meimei vaia au tonu ki ai; Naʻá ku lomiʻi ʻa e huhuʻa tangata ko iá kae ʻoua kuo hoko mai ha faʻahinga vale kehe ʻiate au; pea naʻá ku ʻiloʻi ʻoku ou ʻai taʻeʻiloʻi ʻa e nima ʻo hoku kaungā ngāué ʻi aí, ʻo fakahalaʻi honau nimá ki he ngaahi foʻi pulupulu anga-malū. Ko ha ongoʻi lahi, ʻofa, anga fakakaumeʻa, mo ʻofa pehē naʻe fakatupu ʻe he ʻavoká ni; ʻo pehē naʻe faifai peá u hokohoko atu ʻeku fō honau nimá, mo sio hake ki honau matá ʻi he ongoʻi; ʻo hangē pē ko e pehē, —ʻOiauē! si'oku kaunga 'ofa'anga, ko e ha 'oku tau toe faka'amua ai ha fa'ahinga acerbities fakasosiale, pe 'ilo'i ha ki'i ill-humor pe meheka! Haʻu; tau fī nima takatakai kotoa; ʻikai, tau fefakaʻaiʻaiʻaki kotoa kitautolu; tau fakaʻauʻauhifo kitautolu ʻi he ʻunivēsí ki he huʻakau mo e huhuʻa tangata ʻo e angaʻofá tonu.

‘Ofa pē ke u kei lava pē ‘o lī ‘a e huhu‘a ko iá ‘o ta‘engata! He ko ʻeni, talu mei he ngaahi aʻusia lahi ʻoku fuoloa mo toutou hokó, kuó u ʻiloʻi ʻi he ngaahi meʻa kotoa pē kuo pau ke faifai pea tukuhifo ʻe he tangatá, pe ʻikai toe siʻi hifo ʻi heʻene hiki, ʻene fakakaukau ki he fiefia ʻoku lava ke aʻusiá; ‘ikai ke fokotu‘u ia ‘i ha feitu‘u ‘i he ‘atamaí pe ‘i he fakakaukau faka‘ofo‘ofá; ka ʻi he uaifí, ko e lotó, ko e mohengá, ko e tēpilé, ko e nofoʻangá, ko e tafaʻaki afí, ko e fonuá; ko ʻeni kuó u ʻiloʻi kotoa ʻeni, ʻoku ou mateuteu ke squeeze case ʻo taʻengata. ʻI heʻeku fakakaukau ki he ngaahi vīsone ʻo e poʻulí, ne u sio ai ki ha ngaahi laine lōloa ʻo e kau ʻāngeló ʻi palataisi, ʻoku nau takitaha ʻi ha hina spermaceti hono ongo nimá.

Ko 'eni, lolotonga 'a e discoursing 'o e sperm, 'oku behooves ke lea ki he ngaahi me'a kehe 'oku tatau mo ia, 'i he pisinisi 'o e teuteu 'o e sperm whale ki he try-works.

‘Oku ‘uluaki ha‘u ‘a e hoosi hinehina, ‘a ia ‘oku ui ko iá, ‘a ia ‘oku ma‘u ia mei he konga ‘oku fakasi‘isi‘i ‘o e iká, pea mei he ngaahi konga matolu ange ‘o ‘ene ngaahi fo‘i iká foki. ‘Oku fefeka ia ‘i he ngaahi uoua ‘oku fakafefeka—ko ha fo‘i uoua—ka ‘oku kei ‘i ai pē ha lolo. Hili hono motuhi mei he tafola, 'oku 'uluaki motuhi 'a e hoosi hinehina ki he ngaahi oblongs to'oto'o ere 'alu ki he mincer. ʻOku nau hangē lahi ha ngaahi poloka maka māpele ʻo Berkshire.

Ko e Plum-pudding ko e fo‘i lea ia ‘oku foaki ki he ngaahi konga motumotu pau ‘o e kakano‘i tafola, ‘o pipiki ‘i heni mo hena ki he palanikete ‘o e blubber, pea ‘oku fa‘a kau ‘i ha tu‘unga lahi ‘i hono ‘ikai ke ‘i ai ha lolo. Ko ha meʻa fakafoʻou taha ia, fakafiefia, mo fakaʻofoʻofa ke sio ki ai. Hangē ko ia ʻoku fakahaaʻi ʻe hono hingoá, ʻoku fuʻu koloaʻia ʻaupito, lanu mataʻitofe, mo ha kelekele sinou mo koula bestreaked, ʻoku toti ʻaki ha ngaahi matala ʻo e kulokula mo e lanu vāleti loloto tahá. Ko e ngaahi palamu ia ʻo e ngaahi maka rubi, ʻi he ngaahi fakatātā ʻo e citron. Neongo ‘a e ‘uhingá, ‘oku faingata‘a ke ta‘ofi koe mei hono kai iá. 'Oku ou fakamo'oni, ko e taimi 'e taha na'a ku kaiha'a 'i mui 'i he foremast ke 'ahi'ahi'i ia. Naʻe ʻahiʻahiʻi ha meʻa ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku totonu ke u fakakaukau ki ha cutlet fakatuʻi mei he ʻuhiki ʻo Louis le Gros mahalo naʻe ʻahiʻahiʻi, ʻo pehē naʻe tāmateʻi ia ʻi he ʻuluaki ʻaho hili ʻa e faʻahitaʻu venison, pea ko e faʻahitaʻu venison makehe ko ia ʻoku fakataimi mo ha taʻu lelei taʻe angamaheni ʻo e ngaahi ngoue kālepi ʻo Champagne.

'Oku 'i ai ha me'a 'e taha, pea ko ha me'a 'oku fu'u singular, 'oku liliu hake 'i he lolotonga 'o e pisinisi ko 'eni, ka 'oku ou ongo'i 'oku fu'u puzzling fe'unga ke fakamatala'i. ʻOku ui ia ko e slobkolioni; ko ha appellation 'uluaki mo e kau tafola, pea na'a mo e natula 'o e me'a. Ko ha me'a ineffably oozy, stringy, 'oku fa'a ma'u lahi taha 'i he ngaahi tubs 'o e sperm, hili ha squeezing fuoloa, pea mo e decanting kimui ange. ʻOku ou pukepuke ia ko e ngaahi membrane manifinifi fakaofo mo movete ʻo e keisi, ʻoku nau fakatahatahaʻi.

Gurry, 'oku ui pehe, ko ha fo'i lea totonu 'oku kau ki he kau toutai tafola totonu, ka 'i he taimi 'e ni'ihi 'oku faka'aonga'i fakatu'upakee 'e he kau toutai sperm. ʻOkú ne fakaʻilongaʻi ʻa e meʻa fakapoʻuli mo pipiki ʻa ia ʻoku toʻo mei he tuʻa ʻo e Greenland pe tafola toʻomataʻú, pea ko e lahi taha ʻo e meʻa ko iá ʻokú ne ʻufiʻufi ʻa e ngaahi fungavaka ʻo e ngaahi laumālie māʻulalo ko ia ʻoku nau tuli ʻa e Livaiatani taʻefakaʻapaʻapa ko iá.

Ngaahi nipi. Strictly 'oku 'ikai fakafonua 'a e fo'i lea ko 'eni ki he lea 'a e tafola. Ka ‘i hono ngāue‘aki ‘e he kau tangata tafola, ‘oku hoko ia ‘o pehē. Ko e nipper 'o e tangata tafola ko ha fo'i 'akau nounou fefeka 'o e ngaahi me'a tendinous 'oku motuhi mei he konga tapering 'o e hiku 'o Leviatan: 'oku 'avalisi 'a e 'inisi 'i he matolu, pea ki he toenga, 'oku feunga mo e lahi 'o e konga ukamea 'o ha hoe. Edgewise hiki 'i he tafa'aki 'o e fungavaka lolo, 'oku ne ngaue 'o hange ha squilgee kili; pea ‘i he ngaahi faka‘ai‘ai ta‘ehingoa, ‘o hangē ha faimaná, ‘okú ne tohoaki‘i fakataha mo ia ‘a e ngaahi me‘a ‘uli kotoa pē.

Ka ke ako kotoa fekau'aki mo e ngaahi me'a fakapulipuli ko 'eni, ko ho'o founga lelei taha ko e hifo leva ki he blubber-room, pea fai ha talanoa loloa mo hono kau popula. Na'e lave ki he feitu'u ko 'eni kimu'a ko e 'ai'angakai 'o e ngaahi konga-palaniketi, 'i he taimi 'oku to'o ai mo hiki hake mei he tafola. ‘I he taimi ‘oku a‘u mai ai ‘a e taimi totonu ki hono motuhi ‘a hono ngaahi me‘a ‘i lotó, ko e fale nofo‘anga ko ení ko ha feitu‘u fakailifia ia ki he kau tyros kotoa pē, tautefito ‘i he po‘ulí. ‘I he tafa‘aki ‘e tahá, ‘oku tutu ‘e ha maama fakapo‘uli, kuo tuku ai ha feitu‘u ‘ata‘atā ki he kau ngāué. ‘Oku nau fa‘a ‘alu fakahoa, —ko ha tangata paike-mo-gaffman mo ha tangata-spede. Ko e whaling-pike 'oku tatau ia mo e me'atau heka 'a e vakapuna 'oku 'i ai hono hingoa tatau. Ko e gaff ko e me'a ia 'oku hange ha vaka-hook. ‘I he‘ene gaff, ‘oku tautau ai ‘a e gaffman ki ha la‘i blubber, pea feinga ke ta‘ofi ia mei he‘ene hē, ‘i he hē mo luelue holo ‘a e vaká. Lolotonga iá, ‘oku tu‘u ‘a e tangata-speá ‘i he la‘ipepá tonu, ‘o ne tu‘usi fakahangatonu ia ki he ngaahi konga-hoosi to‘oto‘ó. Ko e spade ko ʻení ʻoku māsila ʻo hangē ko e hone ʻe lava ke ne faí; ʻoku ʻikai ha sū ʻa e vaʻe ʻo e tangata spademan; ko e me‘a ‘okú ne tu‘u aí ‘e tō ta‘elavame‘a ia ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi meiate ia, ‘o hangē ha saliote toho tangatá. Kapau te ne motuhi ha taha ‘o hono ngaahi louhi‘i va‘é tonu, pe ko ha taha ‘o hono kau tokoní, te ke ofo lahi nai? ʻOku siʻisiʻi ʻa e ngaahi tuhuʻi vaʻé ʻi he kau tangata motuʻa blubber-room.





VAHE 95. Koe Kaso.

Naʻá ke laka ʻi he vakapuna Pequod ʻi ha taimi pau ʻo e post-mortemizing ko ʻeni ʻo e tafola; pea kapau naʻá ke lue atu ki muʻa ofi ki he windlass, ʻoku ou fakapapauʻi ʻaupito naʻá ke mei siakaleʻi ʻo ʻikai ha kiʻi fie ʻilo ha meʻa ngali kehe ʻaupito, fakapulipuli, ʻa ia naʻá ke mei sio ki ai ʻi ai, ʻoku tokoto fakalōloa ʻi he lee scuppers. ‘Ikai ko e vaitupu fakaofo ‘i he ‘ulu lahi ‘o e tafola; ʻikai ko e prodigy ʻo hono kauʻā ʻi lalo ʻoku ʻikai ke fakaʻilongaʻí; ʻikai ko e mana ʻo hono hiku fakafeʻunga; ʻoku ʻikai ha taha ʻo e ngaahi meʻá ni te ne fuʻu fakaʻohovaleʻi koe, ʻo hangē ko ha vaeua ʻo e sio nounou ʻo e foʻi koni taʻe-fakaʻuhingaʻi ko iá, —ʻoku lōloa ange ia ʻi ha Kentuckian ʻoku māʻolunga, ofi ki ha fute ʻe taha hono diameter ʻi he tuʻunga, pea ʻuliʻuli jet ʻo hangē ko Yojo, ko e ʻaitoli ʻēponi ʻo Queequeg. Pea ko e ‘aitoli, ko e mo‘oni, ko ia; pe, ko hono fakalea ʻe tahá, ʻi he kuonga muʻá, ko hono fakatātaá naʻe. Ko ha ‘aitoli pehē ‘o hangē ko ia na‘e ma‘u ‘i he ngaahi ngoue‘anga fakapulipuli ‘o Kuini Maaka ‘i Siuteá; pea koeʻuhi ko ʻene lotu ʻa ia, naʻe fakahifo ia ʻe he Tuʻi ko ʻAsa, ko hono foha, ʻo ne fakaʻauha ʻa e ʻaitoli, ʻo ne tutu ia ko ha meʻa fakalielia ʻi he vaitafe Ketiloni, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku fakapoʻuli ʻi he vahe 15 ʻo e ʻUluaki Tohi ʻo e Ngaahi Tuʻi.

Sioloto atu ki he tangata toutaí, ‘oku ui ko e mincer, ‘a ia ‘okú ne ha‘u he taimí ni, pea tokoni‘i ia ‘e ha ongo kaume‘a, ‘okú ne poupou‘i mamafa ‘a e grandissimus, ‘o hangē ko ia ‘oku ui ‘e he kau toutaí, pea ‘i he punou hono ongo umá, ‘okú ne tetetete atu mo ia ‘o hangē ko ha tokotaha tau pomu ‘okú ne ‘ave ha kaungāme‘a mate mei he mala‘é. ‘I hono fakalahi atu ia ‘i he funga ‘o e forecastle, ‘okú ne hoko atu he taimí ni ‘i he founga fakasilini ke to‘o hono kili ‘uli‘ulí, ‘o hangē ko ha tulimanu ‘Afilika ‘a e kili ‘o ha boa. Ko e fai ko 'eni 'oku ne liliu 'a e pelt ki loto ki tu'a, hange ha va'e pantaloon; ‘okú ne ‘oange ki ai ha fakaloloa lelei, ko ia ‘oku meimei ke liunga ua hono diameter; pea faifai pea tautau ia, ʻo mafola lelei, ʻi he rigging, ke fakamāfana. Ere loloa, kuo to'o ia ki lalo; ʻi hono toʻo ha fute ʻe tolu nai ʻo ia, ʻo fakahangatonu ki he ngataʻanga ʻoku tuhuʻí, pea toki motuhi ha ongo ava ki he ngaahi ava-nimá ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻe tahá, ʻokú ne fakaʻauʻauhifo fakalōloa ia ʻo fakasino ki ai. 'Oku tu'u 'a e mincer he taimi ni 'i mu'a 'i ho'o 'inivesi 'i he ngaahi canonicals kakato 'o hono uiui'i. ‘I he ‘ikai ke ne manatu‘i ‘a ‘ene tu‘utu‘uni kotoa pē, ko e ‘inivesi ko ‘ení pē te ne malu‘i fe‘unga ia, lolotonga ‘ene ngāue ‘i he ngaahi ngāue makehe ‘o hono ‘ōfisí.

Ko e ‘ōfisi ko iá ‘oku kau ki ai ‘a hono tuki ‘o e ngaahi konga hoosi ‘o e blubber ki he ngaahi ipu; ko ha ngaue 'a ia 'oku fakahoko 'i ha hoosi 'akau fie'ilo, 'oku to endwise ki he ngaahi bulwarks, pea mo ha capacious tub 'i lalo, 'a ia 'oku tō ki ai 'a e ngaahi konga minced, vave 'o hange ko e ngaahi la'ipepa mei ha tesi 'o e rapt orator. Teuteuʻi ʻi he ʻuliʻuli fakaʻapaʻapa; nofoʻi ha tuʻunga malanga ʻoku hā mahino; taumu'a ki he lau tohitapu; ko e kanititeiti mo'oni ki he 'eiki pīsope, ko e tamasi'i mo'oni ki he Pope 'a e mincer ko 'eni!*

VAHE 96. Koe Ngaahi Ngaue-Ahiga.

Makehe mei heʻene ngaahi vaka ʻoku hiki haké, ʻoku fakafaikehekeheʻi ʻi tuʻa ha tangata toutai tafola ʻAmelika ʻaki ʻene ngaahi try-works. 'Oku ne 'oatu 'a e anomaly fie'ilo 'o e masonry fefeka taha 'oku fakataha'i mo e 'oke mo e hemp 'i hono fa'u 'o e vaka kuo fakakakato. ‘Oku hangē pē ia mei he mala‘e ‘ata‘ataá na‘e ‘ave ai ha fu‘u ‘ai‘angakai piliki ki hono ngaahi laupapa.

'Oku to 'a e ngaahi feinga-ngaue 'i he vaha'a 'o e foremast mo e mainmast, 'a e konga roomy taha 'o e deck. Ko e ngaahi ‘akau ‘i laló ‘oku makehe ‘enau mālohí, ‘oku fe‘ungamālie ke ne pukepuke ‘a e mamafa ‘o ha fu‘u piliki mo e ‘umea meimei fefeka, ‘oku fute nai ‘e hongofulu ki he sikuea ‘e valu, pea ko e nima hono mā‘olungá. ʻOku ʻikai ke hū ʻa e fakavaʻé ki he funga vaká, ka ʻoku fakamaʻu fefeka ʻa e ʻaá ki he funga ʻo e ʻaá ʻaki ha ngaahi tuliki ukamea mamafa ʻoku nau fakamālohiʻi ia ʻi he tafaʻaki kotoa pē, pea fakapipiki ia ki lalo ki he ngaahi ʻakaú. ‘I he tafa‘akí ‘oku ‘ufi‘ufi ia ‘aki ‘a e ‘akau, pea ‘i ‘olunga ‘oku ‘ufi‘ufi kakato ia ‘e ha fu‘u hala hū‘anga lahi, ‘oku hekeheke, mo ‘ai ke ‘i ai ha ngaahi fo‘i ‘akau. To'o 'a e hatch ko 'eni 'oku tau fakahaa'i 'a e try-pots lahi, ua 'i he lahi, pea takitaha 'o e ngaahi pa'ale 'e ni'ihi 'a e malava. ‘I he taimi ‘oku ‘ikai ke ngāue‘aki aí, ‘oku tauhi kinautolu ke nau ma‘a fakaofo. Taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻoku fakangingila kinautolu ʻaki ʻa e maka sopu mo e ʻoneʻone, kae ʻoua kuo nau ulo ʻi loto ʻo hangē ha ngaahi ipu punch siliva. Lolotonga e ngaahi leʻo poʻulí ʻe totolo mai ha kau folau motuʻa fakaʻofa ʻe niʻihi ki ai ʻo nau fakaʻuliʻi kinautolu ʻi ai ke nau mohe. Lolotonga hono ngāue‘aki ‘a hono fakangingila kinautolú—ko ha tangata ‘e taha ‘i he ipu taki taha, ‘o fehangahangai—‘oku fakahoko ‘a e ngaahi fetu‘utaki fakapulipuli lahi, ‘i he ‘olunga ‘o e ngaahi loungutu ukameá. Ko ha feitu‘u foki ia ki he fakalaulauloto loloto fakafika. Naʻe ʻi he nima toʻohema ʻo e try-pot ʻo e Pequod, mo e maka sopu ʻoku faivelenga ʻo takai holo ʻiate aú, naʻá ku fuofua taaʻi taʻefakahangatonu ai ʻe he moʻoni fakaofo, ʻi he siometi ʻa e ngaahi sino kotoa pē ʻoku lele ʻi he cycloid, ko ʻeku maka soap ko e fakatātaá, ʻe hifo mei ha feituʻu pē ʻi he taimi tatau pē.

‘I hono to‘o ‘a e papa-afi mei mu‘a ‘i he ngaahi ngāue‘anga ‘ahi‘ahí, ‘oku fakahaa‘i ai ‘a e ngaahi maka ‘ata‘atā ‘o e tafa‘aki ko iá, ‘o hū ki ai ‘a e ongo ngutu ukamea ‘o e ngaahi fōnise, ‘i lalo tonu ‘i he ngaahi ‘ai‘angakai ‘o e manú.
