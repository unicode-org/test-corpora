VAHE 70. Ko e Sfinx.

Na‘e ‘ikai totonu ke li‘aki ‘a e pehē ki mu‘a ke to‘o faka‘aufuli ‘a e sino ‘o e levaiataní, na‘e tu‘usi hono ‘ulú. Ko ʻeni, ko hono tuʻusi ʻo e ʻulu ʻo e Sperm Whale ko ha ngāue fakasaienisi fakaʻanatomi, ʻa ia ʻoku pōlepole lahi ai ʻa e kau toketā tafa tafola taukeí: pea ʻoku ʻikai taʻeʻuhinga.

Ko ia ai, ‘ikai ‘okú ke ofo ‘i he pōlepole ‘a Stubb, ‘i he‘ene fiema‘u ha miniti pē ‘e hongofulu ke tu‘usi ‘a e ‘ulu ‘o ha tafola sperm?

‘I hono ‘uluaki motuhiá, ‘oku tuku hifo ‘a e ‘ulú ki mui pea puke ‘i ai ‘e ha uaea kae ‘oua kuo to‘o ‘a e sinó. That done, kapau 'oku kau ia ki ha ki'i tafola 'oku hiki hake ia 'i he deck ke 'osi fakakaukau'i 'o li'aki. Ka, ʻi ha lēviatani kuo tupu kakato ʻoku ʻikai ke malava ʻeni; he ‘oku ‘uma ‘a e ‘ulu ‘o e tafola sperm ki he meimei vahe tolu ‘e taha ‘o hono lahi kotoa, pea ke ta‘ofi faka‘aufuli ha kavenga pehē, na‘a mo e ngaahi me‘a lahi ‘aupito ‘a ha tangata toutai tafola, na‘e hoko eni ko ha me‘a ta‘e‘aonga ‘o hangē ko ha feinga ke fua ha fale tauhi‘anga manu Hōlani ‘i he me‘afua ‘a e kau siuelí.

Ko ha nonga kopa mālohi, hangē ha lotus kulokula faka‘univēsí, na‘e fakautuutu ange ‘ene fakaava hono ngaahi lau‘i‘akau ta‘efa‘alaua ta‘e‘iai ha longoa‘á ‘i he tahí.

VAHE 71. Koe Tala o Selopoame.

Nima ki he nima, naʻe puhi atu ʻa e vaká mo e matangí; ka naʻe vave ange ʻa e haʻu ʻa e matangí ʻi he vaká, pea ʻikai fuoloa kuo kamata ke lulululu ʻa e Pequod.

‘I he faai mai pē, ‘o fakafou ‘i he sio‘atá ‘a e ngaahi vaka ‘a e muli mo e ngaahi ‘ulu-pou ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e kau tangatá na‘e fakamo‘oni‘i ai ia ko ha vaka-tafola. Ka ʻi heʻene fuʻu mamaʻo ʻaupito ki he tafaʻaki ʻo e matangí, pea fanafana atu, ʻoku ngalingali naʻá ne fai ha hala ki ha kelekele kehe, naʻe ʻikai lava ʻe he Pequod ʻo ʻamanaki ke aʻu kiate ia. Ko ia na‘e fokotu‘u ai ‘a e faka‘ilongá ke sio pe ko e hā ‘a e tali ‘e faí.

Ke pehē heni, ʻo hangē ko e ngaahi vaka ʻo e kau tau tahi fakakautaú, ʻoku takitaha ʻi ai ha fakaʻilonga fakatāutaha ʻa e ngaahi vaka ʻo e American Whale Fleet; ko e ngaahi faka‘ilonga kotoa pē ‘oku tānaki ‘i ha tohi ‘oku fakapipiki ki ai ‘a e ngaahi hingoa ‘o e ngaahi vaka takitaha, ‘oku ‘oange ia ki he kapiteni kotoa pē. ‘I aí, ‘oku malava ai ‘a e kau ‘eikitau ‘o e tafola ke nau fe‘iloaki ‘i he ‘oseni, na‘a mo e ‘i he ngaahi mama‘o lahi pea ‘oku ‘ikai ha ki‘i fale.

Naʻe faifai pea tali ʻa e fakaʻilonga ʻa e Pequod ʻe he fokotuʻu ʻe he muli ʻa ʻene ʻaʻaná; ‘a ia na‘e fakamo‘oni‘i ai ko e vaká ko e Selopoame ‘o Nantucket. ʻI heʻene fakasiʻisiʻi hono ngaahi ʻiate, naʻá ne ʻave hifo, ranged abeam ʻi lalo ʻi he lee ʻo e Pequod, pea tuku hifo ha vaka; naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo ofi mai; ka, ʻi hono fakaʻaongaʻi ʻo e side-ladder ʻe he fekau ʻa Starbuck ke fakanofo ʻa e kapiteni ʻaʻahí, naʻe lulululu ʻe he muli ʻoku fai ki ai e fehuʻí hono nimá mei he mui ʻo hono vaká ko ha fakaʻilonga ʻo e ʻikai ʻaupito fie maʻu ʻa e meʻa ko iá. Naʻe ʻiloʻi naʻe ʻi ai ha mahaki fakaʻauha fakatuʻutāmaki ʻi he vaka Selopoamé, pea naʻe manavasiʻi ʻa Mayhew, ko ʻene kapitení, ke ne maʻu ʻa e kautaha ʻo e Pequod. He, neongo naʻe kei taʻeʻuliʻi pē ia mo e kau ngāue ʻo e vaká, pea neongo naʻe vaeua ʻene vaká ʻo fanaʻi ʻaki ʻa e meʻafaná, pea mo ha tahi mo ha ʻea taʻefaʻaʻauha ʻoku tafe mo tafe ʻi he vahaʻá; ka ‘i he‘ene pipiki fakakonisēnisi ki he kolonitini fakamā ‘o e fonuá, na‘á ne fakafisi fakamālohi ke fetu‘utaki hangatonu mo e kau Pequod.

Ka na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito ke ta‘ofi ‘e he me‘á ni ‘a e ngaahi fetu‘utaki kotoa pē. ʻI hono tauhi ha vahaʻataimi ʻo ha ngaahi ʻiate siʻisiʻi ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo ia mo e vaká, naʻe faʻu ʻe he vaka ʻo Selopoamé ʻaki hono fakaʻaongaʻi ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻene ngaahi foʻi ʻaá ke tauhi ke fakafehoanaki ki he Pequod, ʻi heʻene foʻi mamafa ʻi he tahí (he ʻi he taimi ko ʻení naʻe puhi foʻou ʻaupito), mo ʻene main-topsail ki mui; neongo ia, ko e mo‘oni, ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi ‘i he kamata fakafokifā ‘a ha fu‘u peau lahi ‘oku tafe mai, ‘e teke‘i ai ‘a e vaká ‘i ha tu‘unga ki mu‘a; ka ʻe vavé ni pē hono toe ʻomi pōtoʻi ia ki hono ngaahi tuʻunga totonú. ‘I he malumalu ‘o e me‘á ni, mo e ngaahi fakalavea kehe ‘o hangē ko iá he taimí ni mo e taimi ko iá, na‘e fakahoko ai ha fetalanoa‘aki ‘i he vaha‘a ‘o e ongo fa‘ahí; ka ʻi he ngaahi vahaʻataimi ʻoku ʻikai taʻe ʻi ai ha toe fakalavea ʻe taha ʻo ha faʻahinga kehe ʻaupito.

Ko hono toho ha foʻi ʻau ʻi he vaka ʻo Selopoamé, ko ha tangata ʻoku fōtunga makehe, naʻa mo e moʻui toutai tafola vao ko ia ʻa ia ʻoku faʻu ai ʻe he ngaahi notabilities fakafoʻituituí ʻa e ngaahi fakakātoa kotoa pē. Ko ha tangata siʻisiʻi, nounou, mo kei talavou, naʻe lulululu kotoa hono fofongá ʻaki ha ngaahi foʻi ʻuʻulu, pea naʻá ne tui ha fulufuluʻi ʻulu kulokula ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu lahi. Naʻe ʻufiʻufi ia ʻe ha kote lōloa, motuʻa faka-cabalistic ʻo ha lanu uolonati kuo ʻosi ʻosi; ko e ngaahi nima ‘oku ‘olunga ‘i aí na‘e takai‘i hake ia ‘i hono ongo louhi‘i nimá. Naʻe ʻi hono matá ha delirium loloto, nofoʻanga, mo fakaʻauha.

Ko ia ‘i he taimi pē na‘e ‘uluaki fakamatala‘i ai ‘a e fika ko ení, na‘e kaila ‘a Stubb —“Ko ia ia! Ko ia ia!—ko e scaramouch ‘oku fuoloa hono to‘oto‘ó na‘e tala mai ‘e he kautaha ‘a e Town-Ho kiate kimautolú!” Naʻe lave heni ʻa Stubb ki ha talanoa ngali kehe naʻe fai ʻo kau ki he Selopoamé, mo ha tangata pau ʻi heʻene kau ngāué, ʻi ha taimi kimuʻa ʻi he taimi naʻe lea ai ʻa e Pequod ʻi he Town-Ho. Fakatatau ki he fakamatala ko ení mo e me‘a na‘e ‘ilo ki mui aí, na‘e hā ngali na‘e ma‘u ‘e he scaramouch ‘oku fai ki ai ‘a e fehu‘í ha mafai fakaofo ‘i he meimei tokotaha kotoa pē ‘i he Selopoamé. Ko ʻene talanoá ʻeni:

Naʻe ʻuluaki tauhi ia ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e sōsaieti valevale ʻo e kau Neskyeuna Shakers, ʻa ia naʻá ne hoko ai ko ha palōfita maʻongoʻonga; ʻi heʻenau ngaahi fakataha mafesifesi mo fakapulipulí ʻi heʻene hifo tuʻo lahi mei he langí ʻi he hala ʻo ha matapā tauhele, ʻo fanongonongo ʻa e fakaava vave ʻo e hina hono fitú, ʻa ia naʻá ne ʻave ʻi hono kato vesí; ka, ‘a ia, ‘i he ‘ikai ke ‘i ai ‘a e pauta me‘afaná, na‘e totonu ke ‘i ai ‘a e laudanum. ʻI he maʻu ia ʻe ha fakakaukau fakaʻaposetolo ngali kehe, naʻá ne mavahe mei Neskyeuna ki Nantucket, ʻa ia, ʻi he kākā ko ia ʻoku makehe ki he valevalé, naʻá ne maʻu ai ha tuʻunga tuʻumaʻu, ʻatamai angamaheni, peá ne ʻoatu ia ko ha kanititeiti nima lanumata ki he folau toutai tafola ʻa Selopoamé. Naʻa nau fakamaʻu ia; ka ʻi he ʻalu leva ʻa e vaká mei he fonuá, naʻe kamata ʻene valevalé ʻi ha foʻi ʻohovale. Naʻá ne fakahā ko ia ʻa e ʻāngelo lahi ko Kepalelí, peá ne fekauʻi ʻa e ʻeikitaú ke ne puna ki tahi. Naʻá ne pulusi ʻene manifesto, ʻa ia naʻá ne fokotuʻu ai ia ko e tokotaha fakahaofi ʻo e ngaahi motu ʻo e tahí pea ko e fakafofonga-seniale ʻo e ʻOseniá kotoa.
