“ʻOiauē, ʻa koe ko e vaeuaʻanga fakapoʻuli ʻo natulá, ʻa ia ʻoku ʻi he ngaahi hui melemo kuó ke langa ho taloni mavahe ʻi ha feituʻu ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi tahi taʻefakaʻauha ko ʻení; ko ha tokotaha taʻetui koe, ʻa koe kuini, pea ʻoku fuʻu moʻoni ʻaupito hoʻo lea mai kiate au ʻi he Afā fakapoʻuli lahi ʻo e laʻā wward hili ʻa e tanu ʻo e hushed ko ʻení. naʻá ne tafoki hono ʻulu ʻoku mate, peá ne toe ʻalu takai holo, ʻo ʻikai haʻane lēsoni kiate au.

“ʻOiauē, trebly hooped mo e welded hip ʻo e mālohí! ʻOiauē, māʻolunga fakaʻamu, rainbowed jet!—ʻoku feinga ʻa e taha ko iá, ʻoku jet ʻe he taha ko ʻení ʻa e taʻeʻaonga! ʻOku taʻeʻaonga, ʻe tafola, ʻokú ke kumi ki ha ngaahi fakafetaʻi mo hoʻo laʻā fakaakeake kotoa pē, ʻa ia ʻokú ne ui mai pē ʻa e moʻuí, ka ʻoku ʻikai ke ne toe ʻomi ia kiate au. ko ha tui fakapoʻuli ange ʻoku ʻalu holo hoʻo ngaahi fefiofi taʻefaʻalaua kotoa pē ʻi hoku laló heni ʻoku fakaʻaiʻai au ʻe he ngaahi mānava ʻo e ngaahi meʻa naʻe moʻui ʻi ha taimi, ʻoku mānava mai ʻo hangē ha ʻeá, ka ko e vai he taimí ni;

“Pea ʻuha maka, ʻuha maka taʻengata, ʻe tahi, ʻa ia ʻoku maʻu ʻe he manupuna vao hono mālōlōʻanga pē ʻe taha ʻi hono ngaahi lī taʻengata: kuo fanauʻi ʻi he kelekele, ka ʻoku huhu ʻe he tahi: neongo naʻe faʻē au ʻe he moʻunga mo e teleʻa, ko hoku ngaahi tokoua tauhi ʻa kimoutolu ʻa e ngaahi peau!”





VAHE 117. Ko e Le'o Tafola.

Ko e fanga tafola ʻe fā naʻe tāmateʻi ʻi he efiafi ko iá kuo nau mate ʻo mavahevahe; ko e taha, mamaʻo ki he matangi; ko e taha, ʻoku siʻi ange ʻene mamaʻó, ki he matangi; taha ʻi muʻa; ko e taha ki mui. Naʻe ʻomi ʻa e toko tolu fakaʻosi ko ʻení ʻi he tafaʻakí kimuʻa pea tō ʻa e poʻulí; ka naʻe ʻikai lava ke aʻu ki he matangi ʻo aʻu ki he pongipongi; pea ko e vaka naʻa ne tāmateʻi ia naʻe tokoto ʻi hono tafaʻaki ʻi he pō kakato; pea ko e vaka ko ia naʻe ʻo ʻEhapi.

Naʻe lī hangatonu ʻa e waif-pole ki he ava ʻo e spout ʻo e tafola maté; pea ko e maama naʻe tautau mei hono tumuʻakí, naʻá ne ʻai ha ulo ngingila fakahohaʻa ki he tuʻa ʻuliʻuli mo ngingilá, pea mamaʻo atu ki tuʻa ʻi he ngaahi peau ʻo e vaeua poʻulí, ʻa ia naʻá ne fakaʻauha māmālie ʻa e tafaʻaki fōlahi ʻo e tafola, ʻo hangē ha ngalu molū ʻi ha matātahí.

Naʻe hangē naʻe mohe ʻa ʻĒhapi mo e kau ngāue kotoa ʻo ʻene vaká ka ko e kau Pālesí; ʻa ia naʻa nau punou ʻi he kaufaná, ʻo tangutu ʻo siofi ʻa e fanga ʻangá, ʻa ia naʻa nau vaʻinga fakaofo ʻo takai ʻi he tafola, pea tukituki ʻaki honau hiku ʻa e ngaahi laupapa sita maʻamaʻá. Ko ha leʻo hangē ko e tangi ʻi he ngaahi kulupú ʻi he ʻolunga ʻo e kau ʻAsifaliti ʻo e ngaahi laumālie taʻefakamolemole ʻo Komolá, naʻe lele tetetete ʻi he ʻeá.

Kamata mei heʻene mohé, naʻe sio ʻa ʻĒhapi, mata ki he mata, ki he Pālesi; pea hooped takai holo ʻe he fakapoʻuli ʻo e poʻulí naʻa nau hangē ko e kau tangata fakaʻosi ʻi ha māmani ʻoku tāfea. Na‘á ne pehē: “Kuó u toe misi ki ai.”

“ʻI he ngaahi saliote mate? ʻIkai kuó u pehē, motuʻa, ʻoku ʻikai lava ke ʻaʻau ha saliote mate pe ko ha puha mate?”

“Pea ko hai ‘oku fanongo ‘oku mate ‘i he tahí?”

“Ka naʻá ku pehē, motuʻa, kimuʻa peá ke mate ʻi he folau ko ʻení, kuo pau ke ke sio moʻoni ki ha ongo saliote mate ʻi he tahí; ko e ʻuluakí ʻoku ʻikai ngaohi ʻe ha nima fakamatelie; pea ko e ʻakau ʻoku hā mai ʻo e fakamuimui tahá kuo pau ke tō ia ʻi ʻAmelika.”

“ʻIo, ʻio! ko ha sio ngali kehe ʻa ia, Parsee:—ko ha saliote mate mo hono ngaahi fulufuluʻi ʻakau ʻoku lele ʻi he ʻoseni mo e ngaahi peau maʻá e kau fua pall. Ha! Ko ha sio pehē ʻe ʻikai vave ke tau sio ki ai."

“Tui pe ʻikai, he ʻikai ke ke mate kae ʻoua kuo sio ki ai, motuʻa.”

“Pea ko e hā ʻa e lea ko ia fekauʻaki mo koé?”

“Neongo ʻe aʻu ki he fakaʻosí, te u kei muʻomuʻa pē ʻiate koe ko hoʻo pailaté.”

“Pea ʻi hoʻo ʻosi pehē ʻi muʻá —kapau ʻe hoko mai ʻa e meʻa ko iá —pea kimuʻa peá u lava ʻo muimui atu aí, kuo pau ke ke kei hā mai kiate au, ke kei pailateʻi au?—ʻIkai naʻe pehē? Sai, ko ia, naʻá ku tui ki he meʻa kotoa pē ʻokú ke lea ʻakí, ʻe ʻeku pailate! ʻOku ʻi ai ʻeku ongo tukupā heni te u kei tāmateʻi ʻa Mopi Tiki pea moʻui mei ai.”

“Toʻo ha tukupā ʻe taha, motuʻa,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pālesí, ʻi he ulo hono matá ʻo hangē ha fanga lango afi ʻi he fakapoʻulí —“Ko e Hemp pē ʻe lava ke ne tāmateʻi koé.”

“Ko e tautau‘angá, ko ho‘omou ‘uhingá.—‘Oku ou ta‘efa‘amate leva, ‘i ‘uta mo tahi,” ko e kaila ia ‘a ‘Ēhapí, ‘i he‘ene kata manuki;—“Ta‘efa‘amate ‘i ‘uta mo tahi!”

Naʻá na toe fakalongolongo fakatouʻosi, ʻo hangē ha tangata pē ʻe tahá. Naʻe hoko mai ʻa e mafoa ʻa e atá, pea naʻe tuʻu hake ʻa e kau ngāue mohé mei he lalo vaká, pea ʻi he teʻeki ke hoʻatā mālié naʻe ʻomi ʻa e tafola maté ki he vaká.





VAHE 118. Koe Kuata.

Naʻe ofi mai ʻa e faʻahitaʻu ʻo e Laine ʻi he lōloá; pea ʻi he ʻaho kotoa pē ʻi he taimi ʻoku haʻu ai ʻa ʻĒhapi, ʻi heʻene haʻu mei hono lokí, ʻo sio ki ʻolunga, ʻe puke fakaʻaliʻali ʻe he tangata folau ʻāʻā ʻene ngaahi foʻi ʻuʻuú, pea lele vave ʻa e kau folau vēkeveké ki he ngaahi meʻa-fakaʻaiʻaí, pea te nau tuʻu ʻi ai ʻo sio fakamamaʻu kotoa honau matá ki he foʻi ʻakau ʻe ua kuo faʻó; ta‘ekātaki ki he tu‘utu‘uni ke tuhu‘i ‘a e tafa‘aki ki mu‘a ‘o e vaká ki he ‘ēkuatoá. ʻI ha taimi lelei naʻe hoko mai ʻa e ʻota. Naʻe fefeka ʻi he hoʻatā māʻolungá; pea ko ʻĒhapi, ʻi heʻene tangutu ʻi he ngaahi kaufana ʻo ʻene vaka naʻe hiki māʻolungá, naʻá ne teu ke fai ʻene siofi fakaʻaho ʻa e laʻaá ke fakapapauʻi hono latitūtí.

Ko ʻeni, ʻi he tahi Siapani ko iá, ʻoku hangē ʻa e ngaahi ʻaho ʻi he faʻahitaʻu māfaná ko e ngaahi ʻaho foʻou ʻo e ngaahi uloʻi ʻuhilá. Ko e la‘ā Siapani mo‘ui ta‘e‘ānaua ko iá ‘oku hā ngali ko e tokanga vela ia ‘o e sio‘ata vela ta‘efa‘alaua ‘o e ‘oseni sio‘atá. ʻOku hā ngali lacquer ʻa e langí; ʻao ʻoku ʻikai ha taha; ʻoku ʻalu holo ʻa e ʻataá; pea ko e telefua ko eni ‘o e ngingila ta‘efakanonga ‘oku hangē ia ko e ngaahi nāunau ta‘ealakātaki‘i ‘o e taloni ‘o e ‘Otuá. Sai ko e quadrant ko ia 'o 'Ehapi na'e fakanaunau'i 'aki 'a e ngaahi sio'ata lanu kehekehe, 'a ia ke fakafou ai 'a e sio ki he afi la'a ko ia. Ko ia, ‘i he‘ene ta‘alo‘alo hono fōtunga tangutu ki he takai ‘o e vaká, pea ‘i hono fokotu‘u ‘ene me‘angāue ‘oku fōtunga fakafetu‘ú ki hono matá, na‘á ne nofo pē ‘i he tu‘unga ko iá ‘i ha ngaahi momeniti ke ma‘u ‘a e momeniti tonu ‘oku totonu ke ma‘u ai ‘e he la‘aá ‘a hono meridian tonu. Lolotonga iá lolotonga ʻene tokanga kotoa, naʻe tūʻulutui ʻa e Pālesi ʻi lalo ʻiate ia ʻi he funga vaká, pea ʻoku lī hake hono fofongá ʻo hangē ko ʻĒhapí, ʻokú ne sio fakamamaʻu ki he laʻā tatau mo iá; ko e ngaahi ʻufiʻufi pē ʻo hono matá naʻe vaeua hono ʻufiʻufi honau ngaahi ʻōliá, pea naʻe fakavaivaiʻi hono fofonga vaó ki ha taʻeʻofa fakamāmani. Naʻe faifai pea toʻo ʻa e fakatokanga naʻe fie maʻú; pea ‘i he‘ene penivahevahé ‘i hono va‘e ‘aivolí, na‘e vave pē hono fika‘i ‘e ‘Ēhapi ‘a e me‘a kuo pau ke ‘i hono latitūte ‘i he taimi tonu ko iá. Pea ʻi heʻene tō ki ha kiʻi fakakaukau ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti, naʻá ne toe sio hake ki he laʻaá ʻo ne lāunga kiate ia pē: “ʻE fakaʻilonga ʻo e tahí! ʻE Pailate māʻolunga mo mālohí! ʻokú ke talamai moʻoni mai ʻa e feituʻu ʻoku ou ʻi aí —ka te ke lava nai ʻo lī mai ha kiʻi fakaʻilonga siʻisiʻi taha ki he feituʻu te u ʻi aí? Pe te ke lava ʻo talamai ʻa e feituʻu ʻoku ʻi ai ha meʻa kehe ʻi he tafaʻaki ko ʻení? kuo pau ke sio kiate ia.Ko hoku ongo matá ni ‘oku sio ia ki he mata tonu ‘oku sio kiate ia he taimí ni, pea ki he mata ‘oku sio tatau pē he taimí ni ki he ngaahi me‘a ‘i he tafa‘aki ta‘e‘iloá, ‘a koe la‘ā!”

Pea ‘i he‘ene sio fakamama‘u atu ki hono kuata, mo to‘oto‘o, ‘a e taha hili ‘a e taha, ‘a ‘ene ngaahi fakakaukau faka-cabalistical lahi, na‘á ne toe fakakaukauloto, peá ne lāunga: “Ko e me‘a va‘inga vale! Ko e me‘a va‘inga ‘a e fanga ki‘i pēpeé ‘a e kau ‘Atimila pōlepolé, mo e kau Komotoá, mo e kau Kapitení; ‘oku pōlepole ‘a e māmaní ‘iate koe, ‘i ho‘o kākaá mo e mālohí; 'oku hoko koe 'i he palanite 'oku laulahi ko 'eni, pea 'oku 'ikai te ke lava 'o tala 'a e feitu'u 'e taha 'o e mata'itofe 'o e ho'ata 'apongipongi; moʻui ʻa e moʻuí ka ʻokú ne velaʻi ia, ʻo hangē ko e ongo mata motuʻa ko ʻení ʻoku naʻa mo e taimí ni kuo vela ʻi hoʻo māmá, ʻE laʻā! dashing it to the deck, “he‘ikai te u toe tataki hoku hala fakamāmaní ‘iate koe; ko e kāpasa ‘o e vaká ‘oku lēvolo, mo e lau mate ‘oku lēvolo, ‘aki ‘a e ‘akau mo e laine; ko e ngaahi me‘á ni te nau tataki au, pea fakahaa‘i mai hoku feitu‘u ‘i he tahí. movete mo fakaʻauha koe!”

ʻI he lea pehē ʻa e motuʻa fakaʻauʻauhifo ko iá pea maumauʻi pehē ʻaki hono vaʻe moʻui mo maté, ko ha ikuna fakakata naʻe ngali ʻuhinga ia kia ʻĒhapi, mo ha loto-moʻua fakaʻauha naʻe ngali ʻuhinga kiate ia pē —naʻe fakalaka atu ʻeni ʻi he fofonga ʻo e Parsee ngutu mo taʻe-ngaué. Naʻá ne tuʻu hake ʻo ʻikai ke fakatokangaʻi ia ʻo lele atu; lolotonga ia, ‘i he‘enau ofo ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘o honau ‘eikitaú, na‘e fakatahataha fakataha ai ‘a e kau toutaí ‘i he ‘aá, kae ‘oua kuo kaila ‘a ‘Ēhapi, ‘i he‘ene luelue faingata‘a‘ia ‘i he funga vaká, —“Ki he ngaahi me‘a faka‘ai‘aí!

ʻI ha kiʻi momeniti siʻi naʻe taʻalo takai ʻa e ngaahi ʻiate; pea ʻi he vaeua ʻa e uili ʻa e vaká ʻi hono muivaʻé, ko hono ngaahi pou fakaʻofoʻofa ʻe tolu ʻoku nofo maʻu ʻoku tuʻu hangatonu ʻi hono ʻulu lōloa mo ʻuʻulu, naʻe hangē ʻoku ʻalu holo ʻa e Horatii ʻe toko tolú ʻi ha hoosi feʻunga ʻe taha.

ʻI heʻene tuʻu ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ongo ʻulu naite, naʻe siofi ʻe Starbuck ʻa e founga moveuveu ʻa e Pequod, pea mo ʻEhapi foki, ʻi heʻene ʻalu ʻo luelue holo ʻi he funga vaká.

“Kuó u tangutu ‘i mu‘a ‘i he afi mahoa‘a matolu ‘o siofi ia ‘oku ulo kotoa, ‘o fonu ‘i he‘ene mo‘ui ulo fakamamahi‘í; pea kuó u sio ki he‘ene faifai pea holo hifo, ‘o ‘alu hifo, ‘o hoko ko e efu valevale tahá.

“ʻIo,” ko e kaila ia ʻa Situpú, “ka ko e efuefu ʻo e mahoaʻa tahí —manatuʻi ia, Misa Sitāpaki —ko e mahoaʻa tahí, ʻo ʻikai ko hoʻo mahoaʻa angamahení. Sai, sai; naʻá ku fanongo ki he lāunga ʻa ʻĒhapi, ʻʻOku ʻai ʻe ha taha ʻeni ʻa e ngaahi kaati ko ʻení ki hoku ongo nima motuʻá ni; ʻokú ne fuakava kuo pau ke u vaʻingaʻi ia, kae ʻikai ha taha kehe.' Pea malaʻia au, ʻĒhapi, ka ʻokú ke ngāue totonu ʻi he vaʻingá, pea mate ai!”





VAHE 119. Koe gaahi Te'elango.

Ko e ngaahi ‘ea māfana tahá ka ‘oku nau tauhi ‘a e ngaahi nifo anga-fakamamahi tahá: ‘oku punou ‘a e taika ‘o Pengalí ‘i he ngaahi vaotā spiced ‘o e verdure ta‘etuku. Langi 'a e effulgent taha ka 'oku kato 'a e manamana fakatupu mate: gorgeous Cuba 'ilo'i 'a e ngaahi matangi na'e 'ikai 'aupito ke tafi tame 'a e ngaahi fonua fakatokelau. ‘Oku pehē pē mo ia, ‘i he ngaahi tahi ngingila ko eni ‘o Siapaní ‘oku fetaulaki ai ‘a e tokotaha toutaí mo e matangi fakalilifu taha ‘i he ngaahi matangi kotoa pē, ko e Afā. ‘E pa ia ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi mei tu‘a ‘i he langi ta‘e‘ao ko iá, ‘o hangē ha pomu ‘oku pā ‘i ha kolo ‘oku ‘ikai ke ‘i ai hano ‘uhinga mo mohe.

ʻI he fakaofi ki he efiafi ʻo e ʻaho ko iá, naʻe haeʻi ʻa e Pequod mei hono kānivá, pea tuku ʻo ʻikai haʻane pou ke tauʻi ha Afā ʻa ia naʻá ne taaʻi hangatonu ia ʻi muʻa. ʻI he hoko mai ʻa e fakapoʻulí, naʻe ngungulu mo mavahevahe ʻa e langí mo e tahí ʻi he manamana, pea ulo ʻi he ʻuhilá, ʻa ia naʻe fakahaaʻi ai ʻa e ngaahi pou faingataʻaʻiá ʻoku nau lele holo ʻi heni mo hena mo e ngaahi kofukofu ʻa ia naʻe tuku ʻe he ʻuluaki houhau ʻo e matangí ki hono sipoti hili iá.

ʻI hono puke ʻe Starbuck ha ʻufiʻufi, naʻe tuʻu ʻi he kuata-deck; ‘i he ulo kotoa pē ‘a e ‘uhila ‘oku sio ki ‘olungá, ke sio pe ko e hā ha toe fakatamaki na‘e hoko nai ki he hamper lavelavea ‘i aí; lolotonga ia ʻoku fakahinohinoʻi ʻe Stubb mo Flask ʻa e kau tangatá ʻi he hiki māʻolunga ange mo e lashing fefeka ange ʻo e ngaahi vaká. Ka naʻe hangē ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa ʻenau ngaahi mamahí kotoa. Neongo na‘e hiki hake ia ki he tumutumu ‘o e ngaahi cranes, na‘e ‘ikai ke hola ‘a e vaka kuata ‘oku ‘alu ki he matangí (‘a ‘Ēhapí). Ko ha tahi lahi ʻoku tafe, ʻo lele māʻolunga hake ʻo fakafepakiʻi ʻa e tafaʻaki tetetete māʻolunga ʻo e vaka ʻoku luelue, ʻo ne sitofu ʻi he lalo vaká ʻi he muiʻi vaká, peá ne toe tukuange ia, ʻo tō kotoa ʻo hangē ha siví.
