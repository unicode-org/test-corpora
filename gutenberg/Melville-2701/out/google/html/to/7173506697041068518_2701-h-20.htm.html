VAHE 101. Ko e Tikanita.

Ere 'a e vaka 'Ingilani mei he sio, pe 'oku fokotu'u hifo heni, na'a ne ha'u mei Lonitoni, pea na'e fakahingoa ia ki he pekia ko Samuela Enderby, ko e tangata fakatau 'o e kolo ko ia, ko e 'uluaki 'o e fale toutai tafola 'iloa 'o e Enderby & Sons; ko ha fale 'a ia 'i he'eku fakakaukau 'a e tangata tafola masiva, 'oku 'ikai ke fu'u mama'o ia mei mui 'i he ngaahi fale fakatu'i fakatahataha 'o e Tudors mo e Bourbons, 'i he tu'unga 'o e mahu'inga fakahisitolia mo'oni. Ko e hā hono fuoloa, kimuʻa ʻi he taʻu ʻo hotau ʻEikí 1775, ʻa e ʻi ai ʻa e fale toutai tafola lahi ko ʻení, ʻoku ʻikai ke fakamahino mai ʻe heʻeku ngaahi tohi-ika lahi; ka ‘i he ta‘u ko iá (1775) na‘á ne fakanaunau ai ‘a e ‘uluaki vaka ‘Ingilani na‘a nau tuli ma‘u pē ‘a e Sperm Whale; neongo ʻi ha ngaahi taʻu ʻe laui hongofulu tupu kimuʻa (talu mei he 1726) naʻe tuli ʻe heʻemau kau Puha Mate toʻa mo e kau Maceys ʻo Nantucket mo e Ngoue Vainé ʻi ha ngaahi vaka lalahi ʻa e Livaiatane ko iá, ka ʻi he ʻAtalanitiki Tokelaú mo e Tongá pē: ʻikai ʻi ha feituʻu kehe. Ke lekooti mahino heni, ko e kau Nantucketers na'a nau fuofua 'i he lotolotonga 'o e tangata ke nau 'ohofi 'aki 'a e ukamea sivilaise 'a e Sperm Whale lahi; pea ʻi ha vaeua senituli ko kinautolu pē ʻa e kakai ʻo e kolopé kotoa naʻa nau fakaʻauha pehē ia.

‘I he 1778, na‘e fakanaunau‘i ai ha vaka lelei, ko e Amelia, ki he taumu‘a fakavavevavé, pea ‘i he tokanga‘i pē ‘e he kau Enderbys mālohí, na‘a nau takai loto-to‘a ‘i Keipi Honi, pea ko e fuofua vaka ia ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e ngaahi pule‘angá ke ne tuku hifo ha vaka-tafola ‘o ha fa‘ahinga pē ‘i he Tahi Tonga lahí. Ko e folau ko iá ko ha folau pōtoʻi mo monūʻia; pea ʻi heʻene foki ki hono tauʻanga vaká mo ʻene puké ʻoku fonu ʻi he huhuʻa mahuʻingá, naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo muimui ʻa e ngaahi vaka kehe ʻi he sīpinga ʻa e vaka ʻAmeliá, ʻIngilani mo ʻAmelika, pea ko ia naʻe lī ai ʻa e ngaahi kelekele lahi ʻo e Sperm Whale ʻo e Pasifikí. Ka ʻi he ʻikai ke nau fiemālie ʻi he ngāue lelei ko ʻení, naʻe toe fakaʻau ke ʻohovale ʻa e fale taʻe-fakaʻauhá: Samuela mo hono ngaahi ʻAló kotoa —ko e toko fiha, ʻoku ʻiloʻi pē ʻe heʻenau faʻeé —pea ʻi honau malumalu vave tahá, pea ʻi ha konga, ʻoku ou fakakaukau, ʻi heʻenau fakamolé, naʻe fakaʻaiʻai ʻa e puleʻanga Pilitāniá ke fekauʻi atu ʻa e sloop-of-war Rattler ki ha folau toutai tafola ʻa e disco South. Naʻe fekauʻi ʻe ha Positi-Kapiteni ʻo e kau tautahí, naʻe fai ʻe he Rattler ha folau fakaʻohovale ʻo ia, pea naʻá ne fai ha ngaahi ngāue tokoni; ko e hā hono lahi ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻasi mai. Ka ʻoku ʻikai ko e meʻa pē ʻeni. ‘I he 1819, na‘e fakanaunau‘i ai ‘e he fale tatau ha vaka tafola ‘ilo‘i ‘anautolu pē, ke nau ‘alu ‘o folau ‘eve‘eva faka‘aonga‘i ki he ngaahi vai mama‘o ‘o Siapaní. Ko e vaka ko iá —na‘e ui lelei ko e “Syren” —na‘á ne fai ha folau ‘ahi‘ahi faka‘ei‘eiki; pea naʻe pehē ʻa e fuofua ʻiloa fakalūkufua ʻa e Feituʻu Tafola lahi ʻo Siapaní. Ko e Syren ʻi he folau ʻiloa ko ʻení naʻe fekauʻi ia ʻe ha ʻEikitau Kofini, ko ha tangata Nantucketer.

Ko ia ai, ko e faka'apa'apa kotoa pe ki he ongo Enderbies, 'a ia ko hona fale, 'oku ou pehe, 'oku kei 'i ai pe 'o a'u mai ki he 'aho ni; neongo ‘oku ‘ikai ha veiveiua kuo pau pē na‘e fuoloa ‘a e hū ‘a e ‘uluaki Sāmiuelá ‘ene keipo ki he Tahi Saute lahi ‘o e māmani ‘e tahá.

Ko e vaka naʻe fakahingoa kiate iá naʻe taau ke fakalāngilangiʻi ia, he ko ha vaka folau vave ʻaupito pea ko ha vaka fakaʻeiʻeiki ʻi he tafaʻaki kotoa pē. Naʻá ku heka tuʻo taha kiate ia ʻi he vaeua poʻulí ʻi ha feituʻu ʻi he matāfanga ʻo Patakoniá, peá u inu flip lelei ki lalo ʻi he forecastle. Ko ha gam lelei ia naʻá ma maʻu, pea ko e ngaahi talupite kotoa kinautolu —ko e laumālie kotoa pē ʻi he vaká. Ko ha moʻui nounou kiate kinautolu, mo ha mate fiefia. Pea ko e gam lelei ko ia naʻá ku maʻú —hili ha taimi fuoloa, fuoloa ʻaupito mei he ala ʻa e motuʻa ko ʻĒhapí ki hono ngaahi laupapa ʻaki hono muli ʻaivolí —ʻokú ne fakamanatu mai kiate au ʻa e talitali kakai fakaʻeiʻeiki, fefeka, mo Saxon ʻo e vaka ko iá; pea ke ngalo au ʻi heʻeku faifekaú, pea manatuʻi au ʻe he tēvoló, kapau ʻe ʻi ai ha taimi ʻe mole ai ʻeku sio ki aí. Vilohi? Na'a ku pehe na'a mau flip? ‘Io, pea na‘á ma flip ia ‘i he vave ‘o e kāloni ‘e hongofulu ‘i he houá; pea ʻi he taimi naʻe hoko mai ai ʻa e ʻahiohió (he ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e ʻahiohió ʻi ai ʻe Patagonia), pea ui ʻa e nima kotoa pē —kau ʻaʻahi mo e kotoa —ki he ngaahi folau ʻo e ʻaá, naʻa mau fuʻu top-heavy ʻo pau ke mau fetaʻaloʻaki ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he ngaahi laine ʻo e kaufana; pea naʻá ma fusiʻi taʻeʻilo ʻa e ngaahi ʻotu ʻo homau ngaahi kote ʻi he ngaahi folau ʻeveʻevá, ʻo mau tautau ai, ʻo mau ʻāʻā vave ʻi he matangi ʻuʻulu, ko ha sīpinga fakatokanga ki he ngaahi tā konā kotoa pē. Kae kehe, na‘e ‘ikai ke ‘alu ‘a e ngaahi poú ‘o ‘ova; pea ʻi heʻemau scrambled hifo, ʻo fuʻu fakakaukau lelei, ʻo pau ke mau toe fakalaka ʻi he flip, neongo ʻoku pa ʻa e spray māsima fakatupu ʻita ʻi he scuttle ʻo e forecastle, ʻo ʻikai fuʻu lahi hono fakavaivaiʻi mo pickled ia ki heʻeku manako.

Naʻe sai pē ʻa e kakanoʻi pulú —fefeka, ka naʻe ʻi ai ha sino ʻi ai. Naʻa nau pehē ko e pulu-pulu; ko e ni‘ihi, ko e kakano‘i pulu dromedary ia; ka ʻoku ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi, ʻo pau, pe naʻe fēfē ʻa e meʻa ko iá. Naʻe ʻi ai foki mo ʻenau dumplings; ko e fanga ki‘i fo‘i pulupulu iiki, ka ‘oku lahi, ‘oku fakafe‘unga, pea ‘oku ‘ikai lava ke faka‘auha. Naʻá ku fakakaukau te ke lava ʻo ongoʻi kinautolu, pea fakatakatakaʻi kinautolu ʻiate koe hili hono folo kinautolú. Kapau naʻá ke punou ʻo fuʻu mamaʻo ki muʻa, naʻá ke fakatuʻutāmakiʻi ʻenau lī atu meiate koe ʻo hangē ha ngaahi foʻi pulu piliati. Ko e maá —ka naʻe ʻikai lava ke tokoniʻi ia; ‘ikai ngata aí, ko ha faito‘o fakafepaki ia ki he sikó; ko hono fakanounoú, naʻe ʻi he maá ʻa e totongi foʻou pē ʻe taha naʻa nau maʻú. Ka naʻe ʻikai fuʻu maʻamaʻa ʻa e forecastle, pea naʻe faingofua ʻaupito ke ke laka atu ki ha tuliki fakapoʻuli ʻi he taimi naʻá ke kai ai iá. Ka ko e meʻa kotoa pē, ʻo ʻave ia mei he lolí ki he taumuʻá, ʻo fakakaukau ki he ngaahi tafaʻaki ʻo e ngaahi boiler ʻa e tokotaha kuká, kau ai ʻene ngaahi boiler kiliʻimanu moʻuí tonu; ʻi muʻa mo mui, ʻoku ou pehē, ko e Samuel Enderby ko ha vaka fiefia; ‘o e totongi lelei mo e lahi; flip lelei mo malohi; crack kaungame'a kotoa, mo e kolomu'a mei he boot muli ki he hat-band.

Ka ko e hā, ʻoku mou fakakaukau ai, ko e Samuel Enderby, mo ha kau toutai tafola ʻIngilani kehe ʻoku ou ʻiloʻí —neongo ʻoku ʻikai ko e kotoa —ko ha ngaahi vaka ʻiloa mo talitali kakai pehē; naʻe fakalaka takai ʻi he kakanoʻi pulú, mo e mā, mo e hina, mo e fakakata; pea naʻe ʻikai vave ke nau fiu ʻi he kai, mo e inu, mo e kata? Te u tala atu. Ko e fiefia lelei lahi ‘a e kau toutai tafola ‘Ingilani ko ení ko ha me‘a ia ki he fakatotolo fakahisitōliá. Pea naʻe ʻikai foki ke u ʻaupito fakaʻehiʻehi mei he fakatotolo fakahisitōlia ʻo e tafola, ʻi he taimi kuo ngali fie maʻu aí.

Naʻe muʻomuʻa ʻi he kau ʻIngilaní ʻi he toutai tafola ʻa e kau Hōlaní, kau Silá, mo e kau Tenimaʻaké; ‘a ia na‘a nau ma‘u mei ai ‘a e ngaahi fo‘i lea lahi ‘oku kei ‘i ai ‘i he toutaí; pea ko e meʻa ʻoku toe lahi angé, ko ʻenau ngaahi founga motuʻa ngako, ʻo uesia ʻa e lahi ʻo e kai mo e inu. He, ko e me'a fakalukufua, 'oku scrimps 'e he vaka fakatau 'Ingilani 'ene kau ngaue; ka ʻoku ʻikai pehē ʻa e tangata tafola ʻIngilaní. Ko ia ai, ‘i he lea faka-Pilitāniá, ko e me‘a ko eni ‘o e whaling good cheer ‘oku ‘ikai ko ha me‘a angamaheni mo fakanatula, ka ko ha me‘a fakatu‘upakē mo makehe; pea, ko ia ai, kuo pau ke ‘i ai ha tupu‘anga makehe, ‘a ia ‘oku fakahaa‘i heni, pea ‘e kei toe fakamahino‘i ange ia.

Lolotonga ʻeku ngaahi fekumi ʻi he ngaahi hisitōlia ʻo e Livaiataní, ne u tūkia ai ʻi ha voliume Hōlani motuʻa, ʻa ia, ʻi he nanamu ʻo e tafola ʻoku ʻi aí, ne u ʻiloʻi kuo pau pē ʻoku fekauʻaki ia mo e kau tafola. Ko e hingoá ko e, “Dan Coopman,” ko ia naʻá ku fakaʻosiʻaki ai kuo pau pē ko e ngaahi manatu mahuʻinga ʻeni ʻa ha kau ʻAmisitetā ʻe niʻihi ʻi he toutaí, ʻo hangē ko ia kuo pau ke ʻave ʻe he vaka tafola kotoa pē ʻene kopá. Naʻe fakamālohia au ʻi he fakakaukau ko ʻení ʻi heʻeku sio ko hono ngaohi ia ʻo ha “Fitz Swackhammer” ʻe taha. Ka ko hoku kaumeʻa ko Toketā Snodhead, ko ha tangata ako ʻaupito, ko ha palōfesa ʻo e lea faka-Hōlani Maʻulaló mo e lea faka-Siamane Māʻolungá ʻi he kolisi ʻo Santa Claus mo St. “Ko e Tangata Fakatau.” Ko hono fakanounoú, ko e tohi Low Dutch motu‘a mo ako ko ení na‘e lave ki he fefakatau‘aki ‘a Hōlaní; pea, ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e ngaahi kaveinga kehé, na‘e ‘i ai ha fakamatala mālie ‘aupito ‘o fekau‘aki mo ‘ene toutai tafola. Pea ‘i he vahe ko ení, na‘e ‘ulu‘i tohi, “Smeer,” pe “Fat,” na‘á ku ma‘u ai ha lisi fakaikiiki lōloa ‘o e ngaahi teunga ki he ngaahi fale tuku‘anga me‘akai mo e ngaahi loki ‘o e kau folau tafola Hōlani ‘e toko 180; mei he lisi ko iá, ʻo hangē ko hono liliu ʻe Toketā Snodhead, ʻoku ou hiki ai ʻa e ngaahi meʻa ko ʻení:

Ko e pauni ʻe 400,000. ‘o e kakano‘i pulú. 60,000 pauni. Puaka Falisilani. 150,000 ʻa e pauni. ‘o e ika sitoka. 550,000 ʻa e pauni. ʻo e pisikete. 72,000 pauni. ‘o e mā molū. 2,800 ʻo e ngaahi fikini ʻo e pata. 20,000 pauni. Tekisi & Leiteni siisi. 144,000 ʻa e pauni. siisi (mahalo ko ha fakamatala maʻulalo ange). 550 'o e ngaahi vakapuna 'o Sene. Ko e pa‘ale pia ‘e 10,800.

Ko e lahi taha 'o e ngaahi tepile fakasitetisitika 'oku parchingly momoko 'i he lau; ka ‘oku ‘ikai pehē ia ‘i he tu‘unga lolotongá, ‘a ia ‘oku tafea ai ‘a e tokotaha lautohí ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi paipa kakato, ngaahi hina, ngaahi kuata, mo e ngaahi gill ‘o e gin lelei mo e fiefia lelei.

‘I he taimi ko iá, na‘á ku tukutaha ai ha ‘aho ‘e tolu ki hono faka‘auha fakamātoato ‘o e pia, pulu, mo e mā kotoa ko ení, ‘a ia na‘e fokotu‘u mai fakatu‘upakē mai ai ha ngaahi fakakaukau loloto lahi kiate au, ‘a ia ‘oku malava ke fai ha ngāue‘aki fakaefakakaukau‘i mo faka-Palato; pea, ʻikai ngata aí, naʻá ku faʻu ha ngaahi tepile fakalahi ʻaʻaku, ʻo uesia ʻa e lahi ʻoku ngalingali ʻe ʻi ai ʻa e ika-sitoka, mo e alā meʻa pehē, ʻoku kai ʻe he kau toutai tafola Low Dutch kotoa pē ʻi he toutai tafola motuʻa ko ia ʻo Greenland mo Spitzbergen. ‘I he ‘uluaki feitu‘ú, ko e lahi ‘o e pata, mo e siisi Texel mo Leyden ‘oku kai, ‘oku hā ngali fakaofo. Kae kehe, ‘oku ou tukuaki‘i ia ki honau ngaahi natula fakanatula ‘oku ‘ikai ke ‘i ai ha me‘a, ‘i hono ‘ai ke toe ta‘e‘aonga ange ‘e he natula ‘o honau uiui‘í, pea tautautefito ki he‘enau tuli ki he‘enau va‘ingá ‘i he ngaahi Tahi Polar momoko ko iá, ‘i he ngaahi matātahi tonu ‘o e fonua ‘Esikimou ko iá ‘a ia ‘oku fefaka‘apa‘apa‘aki ai ‘a e kau nofo fonua anga-fakafiefiá ‘i he ngaahi bumpers ‘o e lolo lēlué.

Ko e lahi 'o e pia, foki, 'oku lahi 'aupito, 10,800 pa'ale. Ko eni, koe'uhi ko e ngaahi toutai polar ko ia na'e toki lava pe ke fakahoko 'i he fa'ahita'u mafana nounou 'o e 'ea ko ia, ko ia ko e folau kotoa 'a e taha 'o e kau tafola Holani ko 'eni, kau ai 'a e folau nounou ki he mo e mei he tahi Spitzbergen, na'e 'ikai ke lahi ange ia 'i he mahina 'e tolu, talamai, pea 'i hono lau 'o e kau tangata 'e toko 30 ki he'enau vakapuna takitaha 'o e 180 havea Holani folau,40; ko ia, ʻoku ou pehē, ʻoku ʻi ai ʻemau paʻale pia tonu ʻe ua ki he tangata, ki ha vahenga uike ʻe hongofulu mā ua, ʻo ʻikai kau ai ʻene vahe totonu ʻo e ʻanker ʻe 550 ko ia ʻo e gin. Ko ʻeni, pe ko e kau tangata ʻo e gin mo e pia ko ʻení, ʻoku fuʻu fuddled ʻo hangē ko ia ʻe fakakaukau ki ai ha taha, ko e faʻahinga tangata totonu ia ke nau tuʻu hake ʻi ha ʻulu ʻo ha vaka, pea fakataumuʻa lelei ki he fanga tafola lele; ‘e ngali ki‘i ta‘ealafakakaukau‘i ‘eni. Ka naʻa nau fakataumuʻa kiate kinautolu, pea nau taaʻi foki kinautolu. Ka naʻe mamaʻo ʻaupito ʻeni ʻi he Tokelaú, ke manatuʻi, ʻa ia ʻoku feʻunga lelei ai ʻa e piá mo e konisitūtoné; ‘i he ‘Ekuatoá, ‘i he‘emau toutai fakatongá, ‘e fe‘ungamālie ‘a e piá ke ne ‘ai ‘a e tokotaha toutai ‘aá ke ne mohe ‘i he ‘ulu-pou ‘o e vaká pea inu lahi ‘i hono vaká; pea ʻe ala hoko ha mole lahi ki Nantucket mo Niu Petifootí.

Ka ʻoku ʻikai toe; kuo fe‘unga ‘a e lea‘aki ke fakahaa‘i ko e kau toutai tafola motu‘a Hōlani ‘o e senituli ‘e ua pe tolu kuohilí ko e kau ‘ate mā‘olunga; pea kuo ʻikai ke liʻaki ʻe he kau toutai tafola ʻIngilaní ha sīpinga lelei ʻaupito pehē. He, ʻoku nau pehē, ʻi he taimi ʻoku folau ai ʻi ha vaka ʻataʻatā, kapau ʻoku ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo maʻu ha meʻa ʻoku lelei ange mei he māmaní, maʻu ha maʻu meʻatokoni efiafi lelei mei ai, ʻo ʻikai toe siʻi hifo. Pea ‘oku faka‘atā ‘e he me‘á ni ‘a e decanter.





VAHE 102. Koe Bower ihe Arsacides.

‘I he a‘u mai ki he taimí ni, ‘i he‘eku fakamatala‘i ‘a e Sperm Whale, kuó u nofotaha tefito ‘i he ngaahi me‘a fakaofo ‘o hono tafa‘aki ‘i tu‘á; pe kehekehe mo fakaikiiki 'i ha ngaahi me'a si'isi'i 'o e ngaahi 'ulungaanga fakafa'unga 'i loto. Ka ki ha mahino lahi mo faka'auliliki 'o e tafi'i ia, 'oku taau ke u he taimi ni ke vete ia 'o toe lahi ange, pea vete 'a e ngaahi poini 'o 'ene hose, vete hono garters, mo lī vete 'a e ngaahi matau mo e mata 'o e ngaahi hui 'o hono ngaahi hui 'i loto, fokotu'u ia 'i ho 'ao 'i he'ene ultimatum; ko hono ‘uhingá, ‘i hono hui ta‘efakangatangatá.

Ka ʻe fēfē ʻeni, ʻIsimeʻeli? ‘Oku anga-fēfē, ko koe, ko ha tokotaha folau pē ‘i he toutaí, ‘okú ke fakangalingali ‘okú ke ‘ilo ha me‘a fekau‘aki mo e ngaahi konga ‘i lalofonua ‘o e tafola? Na‘e fai ‘e he tangata poto ko Stubb, ‘a ia na‘e heka ‘i ho‘o capstan, ‘a e ngaahi malanga fekau‘aki mo e ‘anatomi ‘o e Cetacea; pea ‘i he tokoni ‘a e windlass, puke hake ha fo‘i hui fakatātā ke faka‘ali‘ali? Fakamatalaʻi koe, ʻIsimeʻeli. ‘E lava ke ke fakatū‘uta ha tafola kuo tupu kakato ‘i ho‘o funga vaká ke sivisivi‘i, ‘o hangē ko hono teuteu‘i ‘e ha tokotaha kuki ha puaka tunu? ‘Oku pau pē ‘oku ‘ikai. Kuo ke hoko ko e fakamoʻoni moʻoni ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimi ni, ʻe ʻIsimeʻeli; ka ke tokanga ki he founga te ke puke ai ‘a e monū‘ia ‘o Siona tokotaha pē; ko e monūʻia ke lea ʻi he ngaahi ʻakau mo e ngaahi ʻakau; ko e ngaahi ‘akau, pou-‘otu, ngaahi mohe, mo e ngaahi pou ‘i lalo, ‘o fa‘u‘aki ‘a e fakava‘e ‘o e levaiataní; pea hangē ko e ngaahi hina lolo, ngaahi loki huʻakau, ngaahi pata, mo e ngaahi siisi ʻi hono ʻutó.

Pea na‘a mo e ngaahi ‘aho ‘o Banks mo Solander, ko e ongo mataotao ‘i he natulá ‘a Cooke, ‘oku tau ma‘u ai ha mēmipa Tenima‘ake ‘o e Ako‘anga Saienisi ‘okú ne fokotu‘u hifo ha fanga Tafola ‘Aisilani pau (reydan-siskur, pe Wrinkled Bellies) ‘i ha ‘iate ‘e teau ma uofulu; ʻa ia ko e fute ʻe tolungeau ma onongofulu. Pea ko Lacépède, ko e mataotao fakanatula Falanisē, ‘i he‘ene hisitōlia faka‘āuliliki ‘o e fanga tafola, ‘i he kamata‘anga pē ‘o ‘ene ngāué (peesi 3), ‘okú ne fokotu‘u hifo ‘a e Tafola To‘omata‘ú ‘i he mita ‘e teau, fute ‘e tolungeau ma uofulu mā valu. Pea naʻe pulusi ʻa e ngāué ni ʻi he tōmui ʻo e AD 1825.

Ka ‘e tui nai ha tangata tafola ki he ngaahi talanoa ko ení? Ikai Ko e tafola 'o e 'aho ni 'oku lahi tatau pe ia mo 'ene fanga kui 'i he taimi 'o Pliny. Pea kapau te u ʻalu ki he feituʻu ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa Pliny, ko au, ko ha tangata tafola (lahi ange ʻiate ia), te u fai ʻa e loto-toʻa ke talaange kiate ia ʻo pehē. He ‘oku ‘ikai ke mahino kiate au ‘a e anga ‘o e me‘a ko iá, ‘i he lolotonga ‘a e ngaahi mummies ‘Isipite na‘e tanu ‘i he ngaahi ta‘u ‘e lauiafe ki mu‘a ke fā‘ele‘i na‘a mo Pliny, ‘oku ‘ikai ke nau fua lahi ‘i honau ngaahi puha maté ‘o hangē ko ha tokotaha Kentuckian ‘o onopōní ‘i hono ngaahi soté; pea lolotonga ‘oku fakamo‘oni‘i mahino ‘e he fanga pulú mo e fanga monumanu kehe ‘i he ngaahi lau‘i maka motu‘a taha ‘o ‘Isipité mo Ninivé, ‘i he ngaahi fakafuofua fakalūkufua ‘oku tā ai kinautolú, ‘o hangē pē ko e fakamo‘oni‘i mahino ko e fanga pulu tupu‘anga mā‘olunga, fafanga‘i ‘i he ngaahi falekoloá, pale ‘o Sāmitafilití, ‘oku ‘ikai ngata pē ‘i he‘enau tatau, ka ‘oku nau laka hake mama‘o ‘i he lahi ‘o e fanga ki‘i pulu ngako taha ‘o Feló; ‘i he fehangahangai mo e me‘á ni kotoa, ‘e ‘ikai te u fakahaa‘i ‘i he fanga monumanu kotoa pē na‘e totonu ke kovi ‘a e tafola pē.

Ka ‘oku kei toe pē ha faka‘eke‘eke ‘e taha; ko e taha ‘oku fa‘a fakahoha‘asi ‘e he kau Nantucketers ‘oku nau faka‘ai‘ai angé. Pe koeʻuhí ko e ngaahi sioʻata meimei ʻiloʻi ʻo e meʻa kotoa pē ʻi he ngaahi ʻuluʻi pou ʻo e ngaahi vaka-tafola, ʻoku hū atu he taimí ni naʻa mo e ngaahi ʻotu motu ʻo Behring, pea ki he ngaahi tuʻuʻanga mo e ngaahi loki fakapulipuli mamaʻo taha ʻo e māmaní; pea naʻe lele ʻa e ngaahi heleta mo e ngaahi tao ʻe taha afe ʻi he ngaahi matāfanga konitinēniti kotoa pē; ko e poini moot, pe 'e lava 'e Levaiatane 'o fuoloa 'ene kataki'i ha tuli lahi pehe, pea mo ha maumau ta'efakatomala pehe; pe kuo pau ke ʻoua naʻa fakaʻauha fakaʻosi ia mei he ngaahi vaí, pea ko e tafola fakaʻosí, ʻo hangē ko e tangata fakaʻosí, ʻokú ne ifi ʻene paipa fakaʻosí, pea toki ʻevaporate ia ʻi he puhi fakaʻosí.

ʻI hono fakafehoanaki ʻo e fanga tafola ʻoku ʻi ai honau ngaahi ʻuʻulu mo e fanga manupuna ʻo e fanga pafalo, ʻa ia, ʻi he taʻu ʻe fāngofulu kuohilí, naʻa nau mafola ʻe he laui hongofulu afe ʻa e ngaahi vaotā ʻo ʻIlinoisi mo Mīsulí, pea lulululu honau ngaahi ʻulu ukameá mo fakaʻuliʻulilātai ʻaki honau ngaahi laʻe ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e manamana ʻi he ngaahi feituʻu ʻo e ngaahi kolomuʻa ʻo e vaitafe ʻoku ʻikai ha kakai ʻi he kolomuʻa ʻo e vaitafé. ʻinisi; ‘i ha fakahoa pehē ‘e hā ngali ‘oku ‘omai ai ha fakakikihi ta‘ealafakafepaki‘i, ke fakahaa‘i ‘oku ‘ikai lava ke hao he taimí ni ‘a e tafola tulimanú mei he ‘auha vave.

Ka kuo pau ke ke vakai ki he meʻá ni ʻi he tafaʻaki kotoa pē. Neongo naʻe fuʻu nounou ʻaupito ʻi ha vahaʻataimi kuohilí —ʻikai ko ha moʻui lelei —naʻe mahulu hake ʻa e tohi ʻo e fanga pafalo ʻi ʻIlinoisí ʻi he tohi ʻo e kau tangata ʻi Lonitoni he taimí ni, pea neongo ʻi he ʻaho ní ʻoku ʻikai ke toe ʻi ai ha nifo pe vaʻe ʻe taha ʻo kinautolu ʻi he vahefonua kotoa ko iá; pea neongo ko e tupuʻanga ʻo e fakaʻauha fakaofo ko ʻení ko e tao ʻa e tangatá; ka ko e natula kehe mama‘o ‘o e tulimanu tafola ‘okú ne tapui faka‘aufuli ha ngata‘anga ta‘efakalāngilangi pehē ki he Līvaiataní. Ko e kau tangata ʻe toko fāngofulu ʻi ha vaka ʻe taha ʻoku nau tuli ʻa e fanga Sperm Whales ʻi ha māhina ʻe fāngofulu mā valu ʻoku nau fakakaukau kuo nau fuʻu lelei ʻaupito, pea fakafetaʻi ki he ʻOtuá, kapau ʻe faifai pea nau ʻave ki ʻapi ʻa e lolo ʻo e ika ʻe fāngofulú. Ka, 'i he ngaahi 'aho 'o e kau tulimanu mo e kau tauhele motu'a 'o Kanata mo 'Initia 'o e Hihifo, 'i he taimi na'e hoko ai 'a e hihifo mama'o ('a ia 'oku kei hopo hake 'a e la'aa 'o e la'a) ko ha toafa mo ha taupo'ou, ko e tokolahi tatau 'o e kau tangata moccasined, 'i he lahi tatau 'o e ngaahi mahina, heka hoosi kae 'ikai ko e folau 'i he buffalo for thousand, would have; ko ha mo‘oni‘i me‘a ‘a ia, kapau na‘e fiema‘u, ‘e lava ke fakahaa‘i fakasitetisitika.

Pe, fakakaukau'i 'oku totonu, 'oku 'ikai ngali ha fakakikihi 'i he poupou ki he 'auha fakautuutu 'o e Sperm Whale, hange ko 'eni, 'i he ngaahi ta'u kimu'a (ko e konga kimui 'o e senituli kuo 'osi, talamai) 'a e ngaahi Leviatans ko 'eni, 'i he fanga ki'i pods, na'e fetaulaki lahi ange 'i he lolotonga ni, pea, ko hono ola, na'e 'ikai foki ke fu'u lahi ange 'a e ngaahi folau mo e remu. Koe‘uhi, hangē ko ia kuo fakatokanga‘i ‘i ha ngaahi feitu‘u kehe, ko e fanga tafola ko iá, ‘i hono tākiekina ‘e he ngaahi vakai ‘e ni‘ihi ki he malu‘angá, ‘oku nau luelue he taimí ni ‘i he ngaahi fononga‘anga lahi ‘aupito, ko ia ‘i ha tu‘unga lahi ‘oku fakatahataha‘i he taimí ni ‘a e ngaahi solitaries, ngaahi ‘ioke, mo e ngaahi pods, mo e ngaahi ‘apiako ‘o e ngaahi ‘aho kehé ‘oku movetevete ki he ngaahi armi lalahi ka ‘oku mavahevahe lahi, ‘oku ‘ikai ke fa‘a hoko. Ko e meʻa pē ia. Pea 'oku ngali hala tatau pe 'a e conceit, koe'uhi ko e me'a 'oku ui ko e whale-bone whales 'oku 'ikai ke nau kei faka'auha 'a e ngaahi kelekele lahi 'i he ngaahi ta'u kimu'a 'oku fonu 'iate kinautolu, ko ia ai 'oku holo hifo foki 'a e fa'ahinga ko ia. He ‘oku kapusi pē kinautolu mei he ‘otu mo‘ungá ki he ‘otu mo‘ungá; pea kapau 'oku 'ikai ke toe fakamo'ui 'a e matātahi 'e taha 'aki 'enau ngaahi vakapuna, pea, fakapapau'i, kuo toki 'ohovale 'a e ngaahi 'otu kehe mo mama'o ange 'e he me'a faka'ofo'ofa ta'emaheni.

‘Ikai ngata aí: fekau‘aki mo e fanga Livaiatani ko eni na‘e lave ki ai fakamuimui tahá, ‘oku ‘i ai ‘enau ongo kolotau fefeka, ‘a ia, ‘i he ngaahi tu‘unga kotoa ‘o e tangatá, ‘e kei ‘ikai lava ke ikuna‘i ia ‘o ta‘engata. Pea hangē ko e ʻohofi ʻo honau ngaahi teleʻá, kuo holomui ʻa e kau Suisalani momokó ki honau ngaahi moʻungá; ko ia, ‘i hono tuli mei he ngaahi savanna mo e ngaahi vaotā ‘o e ngaahi tahi lotoloto, ‘oku lava ke faifai pea hū ‘a e fanga tafola hui-tafola ki honau ngaahi kolotau Polar, pea luelue ‘i lalo ‘i he ngaahi ‘ā sio‘ata faka‘osi mo e ngaahi ‘ā ‘i aí, ‘o nau ‘alu hake ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e ngaahi ngoue‘anga ‘aisí mo e ngaahi ‘aisi; pea ʻi ha siakale fakaʻofoʻofa ʻo Tīsema taʻengatá, bid defiance ki he tuli kotoa pē mei he tangatá.

Ka koe‘uhi mahalo ko e nimangofulu ‘o e fanga tafola ivi-tafola ko ení ‘oku nau ‘ai ke ‘i ai ha fo‘i ‘akau ‘e taha, kuo faka‘osi‘aki ai ‘e he kau filōsefa ‘e ni‘ihi ‘o e forecastle ko e maumau lelei ko ení kuó ne ‘osi fakasi‘isi‘i lahi ‘aupito ‘enau ngaahi kulupú. Ka neongo kuo 'osi ha taimi kuo 'osi ha tokolahi 'o e fanga tafola ko 'eni, 'oku 'ikai si'i hifo he 13,000, kuo tamate'i fakata'u 'i he nor' west coast 'e he kau 'Amelika pe; ka ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e ngaahi fakakaukau ‘oku nau ‘ai na‘a mo e tu‘unga ko ení ke si‘isi‘i pe ‘ikai ha fakamatala ko ha fakakikihi fakafepaki ‘i he me‘á ni.

Neongo ‘oku fakanatula pē ke ki‘i ta‘etui fekau‘aki mo e tokolahi ‘o e fanga ki‘i me‘amo‘ui lalahi ange ‘o e kolopé, ka ko e hā te tau lea‘aki kia Harto, ko e faihisitōlia ‘o Goá, ‘i he‘ene tala mai kiate kitautolu ‘i ha tulimanu ‘e taha na‘e ‘ave ai ‘e he Tu‘i ‘o Siamé ha fanga ‘elefānite ‘e toko 4,000; ‘i he ngaahi vahefonua ko iá ‘oku tokolahi ‘a e fanga ‘elefānité ‘o hangē ha fanga pulu ‘i he ngaahi feitu‘u ‘oku ‘ikai ke ‘i ai ha ‘eá. Pea ‘oku hā ngali ‘oku ‘ikai ha ‘uhinga ke veiveiua ai kapau ‘e tuli ‘a e fanga ‘elefānite ko ení, ‘a ia kuo tuli he taimí ni ‘i he laui afe‘i ta‘u, ‘e Semiramis, ‘e Porus, ‘e Hanipale, pea ‘e he ngaahi tu‘i hokohoko kotoa ‘o e Hahaké—kapau ‘oku nau kei mo‘ui ‘i ai ‘i he tokolahi lahi, ‘e lava ke lahi ange ‘a e tafola lahí ‘o lakasi ‘a e tulimanu kotoa pē, koe‘uhi ‘okú ne ma‘u ha kaikai‘anga ‘o hangē ko e lahi ‘o ‘Ēsiá ke fakamatala‘i kotoa. ʻAmelika, ʻIulope mo ʻAfilika, Niu Hōlani, mo e ngaahi ʻOtu Motu kotoa ʻo e tahí ʻo fakatahaʻi.

‘Ikai ngata aí: ‘oku totonu ke tau fakakaukau, mei he mo‘ui fuoloa lahi ‘oku fakakaukau‘i ‘o e fanga tafola, ‘oku nau a‘u nai ki he ta‘u motu‘a ‘o e senituli pea lahi ange, ko ia ai ‘i ha fa‘ahinga vaha‘a taimi pē ‘e taha, kuo pau ke ‘i ai ha ngaahi to‘utangata lalahi kehekehe ‘o e kuonga. Pea ko e hā ʻa e meʻa ko iá, mahalo ʻe vavé ni pē haʻatau maʻu ha fakakaukau ki ai, ʻaki ʻetau fakakaukauloto ki he ngaahi faʻitoka, ngaahi faʻitoka, mo e ngaahi fale fakafāmili kotoa ʻo e fakatupú ʻoku nau tukuange mai ʻa e sino moʻui ʻo e kau tangata, kau fafine, mo e fānau kotoa pē naʻa nau moʻui ʻi he taʻu ʻe fitungofulu mā nima kuohilí; pea tānaki atu ‘a e kau talitali ta‘efa‘alaua ko ení ki he tokolahi ‘o e tangata lolotonga ‘o e kolopé.

Ko ia ai, koeʻuhí ko e ngaahi meʻá ni kotoa, ʻoku tau lau ʻoku taʻefaʻamate ʻa e tafola ʻi hono faʻahinga, neongo ʻene ʻauha ʻi hono fakafoʻituituí. Naʻá ne luelue ʻi he ngaahi tahí kimuʻa pea motuhi ʻe he ngaahi konitinēnití ʻa e vaí; naʻá ne luelue ʻi ha taimi ʻi he feituʻu naʻe ʻi ai ʻa e Tuileries, mo e Kasele ʻo Uinisoá, mo e Kremlin. ‘I he lōmaki ‘o Noá na‘á ne manuki‘i ‘a e ‘A‘ake ‘o Noá; pea kapau ʻe toe tāfea ʻa e māmaní, ʻo hangē ko Netalení, ke tāmateʻi ʻene fanga kiʻi iká, pea ʻe kei moʻui pē ʻa e tafola taʻengatá, pea ʻi heʻene ʻohake ʻi he tumutumu ʻo e tāfea fakaʻekuatoá, ʻo ne puna ʻene fakafepaki ʻoku ʻi ai hono fulufuluʻi ʻakaú ki he langí.





VAHE 106. Koe Va'e o Ehapi.

Ko e founga fakavavevave naʻe mavahe ai ʻa e ʻEikitau ʻĒhapí ki he vaka Samuela ʻEnitā ʻo Lonitoní, naʻe ʻikai ke taʻetokangaʻi ia ʻaki ha kiʻi fakamālohi ki hono tokotaha pē ʻoʻoná. Naʻá ne maama ʻaki ha ivi lahi ʻi ha fakaʻauha ʻo ʻene vaká ʻo maʻu ai ʻe hono vaʻe ʻaivolí ha ʻoho ʻoku vaeua ʻene moveteveté. Pea ʻi he hili ʻene maʻu ʻene deck ʻaʻaná, mo ʻene pivot-hole ʻaʻaná ʻi aí, naʻá ne fuʻu vehemently wheeled takai mo ha fekau fakavavevave ki he steersman (naʻe, hangē ko ia ʻoku faʻa hokó, ko ha meʻa fekauʻaki mo ʻene ʻikai ke fakaʻuli inflexibly feʻunga); pea, naʻe maʻu ʻe he ʻaivoli kuo ʻosi luluʻí ha toe mioʻi mo ha meʻa fakaʻuli, ʻo neongo naʻe kei kakato pē, pea ki he ngaahi fōtunga kotoa pē ʻoku holi kovi, ka naʻe ʻikai ke lau ia ʻe ʻĒhapi ʻoku falalaʻanga kakato.

Pea, ko e mo‘oni, na‘e hā ngali si‘isi‘i ‘a e me‘a ke ofo ai, ‘i he‘ene ta‘efakakaukau‘i valevale mo mafola kotoa, na‘e tokanga fakalelei ‘a ‘Ēhapi ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi ki he tu‘unga ‘o e hui mate ko ia na‘á ne tu‘u ai ‘i ha konga. He naʻe teʻeki fuʻu fuoloa ʻa e folau ʻa e Pequod mei Nantucket, kuo maʻu ia ʻi ha pō ʻe taha ʻoku tokoto fakaʻauʻauhifo ʻi he kelekelé, pea ʻoku ʻikai ke ne ʻiloʻi ha meʻa; ʻe ha ngaahi lavea taʻeʻiloa, pea ngali taʻe lava ke fakamatalaʻi, taʻealafakakaukauʻi, kuo fuʻu fakamālohiʻi hono vaʻe ʻaivolí, ʻo ne taaʻi fakapotopoto ʻi he siteikí, pea ʻoku meimei ke ne tuʻusi kotoa hono kongalotó; pea na‘e ‘ikai foki ke ta‘e‘iai ha faingata‘a lahi ‘a hono fakamo‘ui kakato ‘a e lavea fakamamahí.

Pe, ‘i he taimi ko iá, na‘e ‘ikai ke hū ia ki hono ‘atamai monomaniac, ko e mamahi kotoa ‘o e mamahi ko ia ‘i he taimi ko iá ko e ‘īsiu fakahangatonu pē ia ‘o ha mala‘ia ki mu‘a; pea naʻe hangē naʻá ne sio mahino foki, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku fakatolonga ʻe he manu totolo kona taha ʻo e vaotaá hono faʻahinga ʻo hangē pē ko e hiva melie taha ʻo e vaotaá; ko ia, tatau mo e felicity kotoa pe, 'oku fakanatula 'a e ngaahi me'a fakaloloma kotoa pe 'oku nau fanau'i 'enau tatau. ʻIo, ʻoku lahi ange ʻi he tatau, ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻĒhapí; he 'oku fakatou 'alu mama'o ange 'a e tupu'anga mo e hako 'o e Loto-mamahi 'i he tupu'anga mo e hako 'o e Fiefia. He, ‘ikai ke faka‘ilonga‘i ‘a e me‘á ni: ko ha faka‘uhinga ia mei he ngaahi akonaki faka-Kanoni pau, ‘a ia neongo ‘e ‘ikai ha fānau ‘e fanau‘i mai ‘e he ngaahi fiefia fakanatula ‘e ni‘ihi heni ma‘á e māmani ‘e tahá, ka, ‘i hono kehé, ‘e muimui mai ‘a e fiefia-ta‘e-fānau ‘o e loto-mo‘ua kotoa ‘o helí; ka, ʻe kei fakatupu ʻe he ngaahi mamahi fakamatelie halaia ʻe niʻihi kiate kinautolu ha hako fakalakalaka taʻengata ʻo e ngaahi mamahi ʻi he tuʻa ʻo e faʻitoká; 'ikai 'aupito ke hint 'eni, 'oku kei ngali 'oku 'i ai ha ta'efa'atatau 'i he 'analaiso loloto ange 'o e me'a. He, ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻĒhapí, lolotonga naʻa mo e ngaahi fiefia fakamāmani māʻolunga tahá ʻoku ʻi ai ha faʻahinga meʻa siʻisiʻi taʻefakaʻilonga ʻoku toitoi ʻiate kinautolu, ka, ʻi laló, ko e ngaahi mamahi kotoa pē ʻo e lotó, ko ha ʻuhinga fakamisiteli, pea, ʻi he kau tangata ʻe niʻihi, ko ha maʻongoʻonga fakaʻāngelo lahi; ko ia 'oku 'ikai ke faka'ikai'i 'e he'enau ngaahi tracings-out faivelenga 'a e to'o mahino. Ke muimuiʻi ʻa e ngaahi tohi hohoko ʻo e ngaahi mamahi fakamatelie māʻolunga ko ʻení, ʻokú ne ʻave kitautolu ʻi he fakaʻosí ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi tupuʻanga taʻeʻaonga ʻo e ngaahi ʻotuá; ko ia, ‘i he ‘ao ‘o e ngaahi la‘ā fiefia kotoa pē, ‘oku ngaohi mohuku, mo e ngaahi sipela molū, ngaahi māhina ututa‘u takai, kuo pau ke tau tukulolo ki he me‘á ni: ‘oku ‘ikai ke fiefia ta‘engata ‘a e ngaahi ‘otuá tonu. Ko e fakaʻilonga fāʻeleʻi taʻe-faʻa-tamateʻi, fakaloloma ʻi he laʻe ʻo e tangatá, ko e sila pē ia ʻo e loto-mamahi ʻi he kau fakamoʻoní.

Kuo fakahā heni ha fakapulipuli ʻi he taʻeʻilo, ʻa ia mahalo ʻe totonu ange, ʻi ha founga kuo fokotuʻu, ke fakahā kimuʻa. ʻI he ngaahi fakaikiiki lahi kehe fekauʻaki mo ʻĒhapí, naʻe kei hoko maʻu pē ia ko ha misiteli ki ha niʻihi, ko e hā naʻe pehē aí, ʻi ha vahaʻataimi pau, kimuʻa pea mo e hili ʻa e folau ʻa e Pequod, naʻá ne fufū ia ʻo mamaʻo ʻaki ha faʻahinga fakaʻilonga makehe hangē ko e Grand-Lama; pea, ʻi he vahaʻataimi ʻe taha ko iá, naʻá ne kumi ki ha hūfangaʻanga taʻe-lea, ʻo hangē ko ia, ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e senato māpele ʻo e kau pekiá. Naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke hā feʻunga ʻa e ʻuhinga bruited ʻa Kapiteni Peleki ki he meʻá ni; neongo ia, ko e moʻoni, ʻi heʻene uesia ʻa e konga loloto kotoa ʻo ʻĒhapí, naʻe maʻu ʻe he fakahā kotoa pē ha fakapoʻuli mahuʻinga lahi ange ʻi he maama fakamatalá. Ka, ʻi he fakaʻosí, naʻe ʻasi kotoa mai; naʻe fai ʻe he meʻa ʻe taha ko ʻení, ʻi he siʻisiʻi tahá. Ko e fakatuʻutāmaki fakalilifu ko iá naʻe ʻi he uho ia ʻo ʻene nofo fakataimi ʻiate iá. Pea ʻikai ko ʻeni pē, ka ki he siakale ko ia ʻoku kei aleapauʻi, mo tō hifo ki ʻuta, ʻa ia, ʻi ha faʻahinga ʻuhinga pē, naʻá ne maʻu ʻa e monūʻia ʻo ha founga ʻoku siʻisiʻi ange hono tapuí kiate iá; ki he siakale fakamā ko iá ʻa e lavea ʻoku fakaʻilongaʻi ʻi ʻolungá —ʻo kei, ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe faí, ʻo ʻikai ke ʻiloʻi ʻe ʻĒhapí —naʻá ne ʻinivesi ia ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi fakailifia, ʻo ʻikai ke ne fakaʻauha kakato mei he fonua ʻo e ngaahi laumālié mo e ngaahi tangilāulaú. Ko ia, ʻi heʻenau faivelenga kiate iá, naʻa nau faʻufaʻu kotoa, ʻo fakatatau ki he meʻa naʻe ʻiate kinautolú, ke fakapuliki ʻa e ʻilo ki he meʻá ni mei he niʻihi kehé; pea ko ia ai, naʻe ʻikai ke ʻosi ha vahaʻataimi lahi, naʻe ʻasi ia ʻi he ngaahi fungavaka ʻo e Pequod.

Ka ke hoko ʻeni kotoa pē ʻo hangē ko ia ʻe hokó; tuku ke ‘i ai ha fekau‘aki pe ‘ikai ‘a e sinoti ta‘ehāmai mo ta‘emahino ‘i he ‘eá, pe ko e kau pilinisi mo e kau pule tautea ‘o e afí, mo ‘Ēhapi fakamāmaní, ka neongo ia, ‘i he me‘a lolotonga ko eni ‘o hono va‘é, na‘á ne fai ‘a e ngaahi founga ‘aonga mahino;—na‘á ne ui ‘a e tufunga ‘akaú.

Pea ʻi he hā mai ʻa e tangata ngāue ko iá ʻi hono ʻaó, naʻá ne fekauʻi ia ʻo ʻikai toe toloi ke ne kamata ke ngaohi ha vaʻe foʻou, peá ne fakahinohinoʻi ʻa e ongo hoá ke na sio kiate ia ʻoku ʻoange ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻakau kotoa pē ʻo e jaw-ivory (Sperm Whale) ʻa ia kuo tānaki ʻi he folaú ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimí ni, koeʻuhí ke lava ʻo fili fakalelei ʻa e stoutene, clearest stuff-gra. ʻI hono ʻosi ʻení, naʻe maʻu ʻe he tufunga ʻakaú ha ngaahi fekau ke fakakakato ʻa e vaʻé ʻi he pō ko iá; pea ke tokonaki ‘a e ngaahi me‘a kotoa pē ki ai, ‘o tau‘atāina mei he ngaahi me‘a fekau‘aki mo e tokotaha ta‘efalala‘anga ‘oku ngāue‘akí. ‘Ikai ngata aí, na‘e fekau‘i ke hiki hake ‘a e fale ngaohi‘anga vaka ‘o e vaká mei hono tu‘u ta‘e‘aonga fakataimi ‘i he tau‘anga vaká; pea, ke fakavave‘i ‘a e me‘á, na‘e fekau‘i ai ‘a e tufunga ukameá ke ne hoko atu leva ki hono ngaohi ‘o ha fa‘ahinga me‘angāue ukamea pē ‘e fiema‘u.
