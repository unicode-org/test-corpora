VAHE 61. Ko e Tamate'i 'e Stubb ha Tafola.

Kapau ki he Starbuck ko e hā mai ʻa e Squid ko ha meʻa ia ʻo e ngaahi fakaʻilonga, kia Queequeg ko ha meʻa kehe ʻaupito ia.

“Ka ke sio kiate ia ‘quid,” ko e lau ia ‘a e manu fekaí, ‘i he‘ene faka‘ai‘ai ‘ene fo‘i ‘akau ‘i he kaufana ‘o ‘ene vaka na‘e hiki haké, “pea ‘okú ke sio vave kiate ia ko e ‘parm whale.”

Naʻe fuʻu nonga mo vela ʻaupito ʻa e ʻaho hono hokó, pea ʻi he ʻikai ha meʻa makehe ke fakahoko ʻaki kinautolú, naʻe ʻikai ke meimei lava ʻe he kau ngāue ʻo e Pequod ʻo fakafepakiʻi ʻa e sipele ʻo e mohe ʻoku fakatupu ʻe ha tahi ʻataʻatā pehē. He ko e konga ko eni ‘o e ‘Ōseni ‘Initiá ‘a ia na‘a mau folau ai ‘i he taimi ko iá ‘oku ‘ikai ko e me‘a ia ‘oku ui ‘e he kau toutai tafola ko ha kelekele mo‘ui; ‘a ia, ‘okú ne ‘omai ‘a e ngaahi sio‘ata si‘isi‘i ange ‘o e fanga ki‘i porpoise, fanga tolofini, fanga ika lele, mo e fanga ki‘i manupuna mo‘ui kehe ‘o e ngaahi vai ‘oku fakatupu ‘ohovale ange, ‘i he ngaahi vai ‘i he matātahi ‘o Rio de la Plata, pe ko e kelekele ‘i he matātahi ‘o e matātahi ‘o Peluú.

Ko hoku taimí ia ke u tuʻu ʻi he ʻuluʻi pou-muʻá; pea ʻi he fakafalala hoku ongo umá ki he ngaahi kofu fakatuʻi kuo vaivaí, naʻá ku luelue holo ʻi he meʻa naʻe ngali ko ha ʻea fakaʻofoʻofá. Naʻe ʻikai ha fakapapau ʻe lava ke ne matuʻuaki ia; ʻi he ʻulungaanga fakaʻofoʻofa ko iá ʻo mole ʻeku ʻiló kotoa, naʻe faifai pea ʻalu atu hoku laumālié mei hoku sinó; neongo naʻe kei hokohoko atu pē ʻa e lulululu hoku sinó ʻo hangē ko e loto ʻo ha pendulum, hili ha taimi fuoloa mei hono toʻo ʻo e mālohi naʻá ne ʻuluaki ueʻi iá.

Ere ngalo fakaʻaufuli ʻiate au, naʻá ku fakatokangaʻi kuo ʻosi mohe ʻa e kau toutaí ʻi he main mo e mizzen-mast-heads. Ko ia naʻe faifai pea mau taʻalo taʻe-moʻui kotoa ʻa kimautolu toko tolú mei he ngaahi ʻaá, pea ʻi he taʻalo kotoa pē naʻa mau faí naʻe ʻi ai ha luelue mei lalo mei he tangata pule vakapuna ʻoku mohé. Ko e ngaahi peaú foki, na‘a nau luelue honau ngaahi tumutumu ta‘e‘aongá; pea ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo e ʻataʻatā lahi ʻo e tahí, naʻe luelue ʻa e hahaké ki he hihifó, pea ʻoku ʻi he meʻa kotoa pē ʻa e laʻaá.

Fakafokifā pē naʻe hangē kuo pa mai ha ngaahi pupula ʻi lalo ʻi hoku mata ʻoku moʻumoʻuá; hangē ha ngaahi angakoví naʻe puke ʻe hoku ongo nimá ʻa e ngaahi ʻufiʻufí; naʻe fakahaofi au ʻe ha tauʻatāina ke fili taʻehāmai mo ʻaloʻofa ʻe niʻihi; ʻi ha ʻohovale ne u toe foki mai ki he moʻuí. Pea vakai! ofi ʻi lalo ʻi homau ʻaá, ʻo ʻikai fute ʻe fāngofulu hono mamaʻó, naʻe tokoto ha fuʻu Sperm Whale ʻo takai holo ʻi he vaí ʻo hangē ha sino ʻo ha vaka tau kuo fulihi, ko hono tuʻa fōlahi mo ngingilá, ʻoku lanu faka-ʻItiopea, ʻo ngingila ʻi he huelo ʻo e laʻaá ʻo hangē ha sioʻatá. Ka ʻi heʻene luelue fakapikopiko ʻi he ʻaofi ʻo e tahí, pea ʻi he taimi kotoa pē ʻokú ne fakaʻauha fakanonga ʻene vakapuna kohu, naʻe hangē ʻa e tafola ko ha portly burgher ʻokú ne ifi ʻene paipa ʻo ha hoʻatā māfana. Ka ko e paipa ko ia, tafola masiva, ko ho'o faka'osi ia. Hangē naʻe taaʻi ʻe ha tokotoko ʻo ha tangata faimana, naʻe kamata leva ke ʻā ʻa e vaka mohé mo e tokotaha mohe kotoa pē ʻi aí; pea laka hake ʻi ha leʻo ʻe hongofulu mei he ngaahi konga kotoa ʻo e vaká, ʻi he taimi tatau mo e ngaahi nota ʻe tolu mei ʻolungá, naʻa nau kaila mai ʻa e tangi angamaheni, ʻi hono lī māmālie mo maʻu pē ʻe he ika lahí ʻa e vai māsima ngingilá ki he ʻeá.

“Fakaʻauha ʻa e ngaahi vaká! Luff!” ko e kaila ia ‘a ‘Ēhapí. Pea ‘i he‘ene talangofua ki he‘ene tu‘utu‘uni pē ‘a‘aná, na‘á ne lī hifo ai ‘a e taumu‘á ki mu‘a ke toki lava ‘e he tokotaha taumu‘á ‘o to‘o ‘a e ngaahi spoke.

Ko e ngaahi kaila fakafokifā ‘a e kau ngāué kuo pau pē na‘á ne fakatupu manavasi‘i ki he tafola; pea kimuʻa pea toki hifo ʻa e ngaahi vaká, ʻo ne tafoki fakaʻeiʻeiki, naʻá ne luelue atu ki he tafaʻaki ʻo e matangí, ka ʻi ha nonga tuʻumaʻu, pea naʻe siʻisiʻi ʻene ngaahi peau ʻi heʻene luelué, ʻo ne fakakaukau ʻi he hili ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē mahalo ʻe ʻikai ke ne kei manavasiʻí, naʻá ne fai ʻe ʻĒhapi ha ngaahi fekau ke ʻoua naʻa fakaʻaongaʻi ha foʻi ʻauʻau, pea ʻoku ʻikai totonu ke lea ha taha ka ʻi he muhumuhu. Ko ia ‘i he‘emau tangutu ‘o hangē ha kau ‘Initia ‘Onitelioó ‘i he ngaahi ‘ā ‘o e ngaahi vaká, na‘a mau folau fakavavevave ka ‘i he fakalongolongo atu; ko e nonga 'o e 'ikai ke fakamo'oni'i 'a e ngaahi folau ta'e-longoa'a 'oku fokotu'u. ʻI he taimi ní, ʻi heʻemau lele peheé ʻi he tulimanú, naʻe lele fakahangatonu ʻe he fuʻu manu fekaí hono hiku ʻi ha fute ʻe fāngofulu ki he ʻeá, peá ne toki ngoto atu ʻo ʻikai toe sio ki ai ʻo hangē ha taua kuo folo haké.

“ʻOku ʻalu ha ngaahi fluke!” ko e tangí ia, ko ha fanongonongo naʻe hoko atu leva ʻi hono ngaohi ʻe Stubb ʻene foʻi afi mo tutu ʻene paipá, he ko ʻeni naʻe ʻoange ha mālōlō. Hili e ʻosi ʻa e vahaʻataimi kakato ʻo ʻene leʻó, naʻe toe tuʻu hake ʻa e tafola, pea ʻi heʻene ʻi muʻa he taimí ni ʻi he vaka ʻo e tokotaha ifi tapaká, pea ofi ange ʻaupito ki ai ʻi ha taha ʻo e niʻihi kehé, naʻe lau ʻa Situpi ki he fakaʻapaʻapa ʻo e puké. Naʻe mahino, he taimí ni, kuo faifai pea ʻiloʻi ʻe he tafola ʻa e kau tuli iá. Ko ia ai, na‘e ‘ikai ke toe ‘aonga ‘a e fakalongolongo kotoa ‘o e tokangá. Naʻe tuku hifo ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻaá, pea naʻe leʻolahi mai ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻaá ʻo vaʻinga. Pea ʻi heʻene kei puhiʻi pē ʻene paipá, naʻe fakafiefiaʻi ʻe Stubb ʻene kau ngāué ki he ʻohofí.

‘Io, na‘e hoko mai ha liliu lahi ‘i he iká. Mo‘ui kotoa ki hono fakatu‘utāmakí, na‘á ne ‘alu “‘ulu ki tu‘a”; ko e konga ko ia ‘oku ‘asi fakahangatonu mai mei he ‘isite valevale na‘á ne ngaohi.*

*'E sio ki ai 'i ha feitu'u kehe 'a e me'a 'oku fu'u ma'ama'a 'aupito 'a e loto kotoa 'o e 'ulu lahi 'o e sperm whale. Neongo ‘oku hā ngali ko e lahi taha ia, ‘oku mama‘o ‘aupito ‘a e konga buoyant taha fekau‘aki mo iá. Ko ia ‘okú ne hiki hake ia ‘i he ‘eá ‘i he faingofua, pea ‘okú ne fai ma‘u pē ia ‘i he taimi ‘okú ne ‘alu ai ‘i hono vave tahá. ‘Ikai ngata aí, ‘oku pehē ‘a e fālahi ‘o e konga ‘i ‘olunga ‘o e mu‘a ‘o hono ‘ulú, pea ‘oku pehē ‘a e tapering cut-water formation ‘o e konga ‘i laló, ‘a ia ‘i hono hiki hake fakahangatonu hono ‘ulú, ‘e lava ke pehē ai ‘okú ne liliu ia mei ha galliot fakapikopiko ‘oku punou ‘i he bluff ki ha vaka pailate Niu ‘Ioke ‘oku māsila.

“Kamataʻi ia, kamataʻi ia, ʻeku kau tangata! ʻOua naʻa mou fakavavevave; toʻo ha taimi lahi —ka kamataʻi ia; kamataʻi ia ʻo hangē ha manamana, ko e meʻa pē ia,” ko e kaila ia ʻa Situpi, ʻo ne puhiʻi atu ʻa e kohu ʻi heʻene leá. “Kamata ia, he taimi ni; ʻoange kiate kinautolu ʻa e tā lōloa mo mālohi, Tashtego. Kamata ia, Tash, ʻeku tamasiʻi —kamata ia, kotoa; ka ke tauhi ke momoko, tauhi ke momoko —ko e kukama ko e foʻi lea ia —faingofua, faingofua —kamata pē ia ʻo hangē ha mate fakalilifú mo e kau tēvolo ʻoku nau malimali, pea fokotuʻu hake ʻa e kau mate tanu ʻo e Stardi ʻo ʻenau pertha't kotoa. ia!"

“Uo-hoo! Ua-hee!” ko e kaila ia ʻa e Gay-Header ʻi heʻene talí, ʻo ne hiki hake ha ngaahi tau-whoop motuʻa ki he langí; ʻi he puna taʻefakakaukauʻi ʻa e tokotaha folau kotoa pē ʻi he vaka kuo faingataʻaʻiá ki muʻa ʻaki ʻa e foʻi tā taki fakaofo ʻe taha ʻa ia naʻe fai ʻe he ʻInitia vēkeveké.

Ka naʻe tali ʻene ngaahi kaila vaó ʻe ha niʻihi kehe ʻoku fuʻu vao tatau pē. “Ki-hi! Ki-hi!” ko e kaila ia ʻa Daggoo, ʻo ne fakamālohiʻi ki muʻa mo mui ʻi hono seá, ʻo hangē ha taika ʻoku luelue ʻi hono ʻaiʻangakai ʻo e manú.

“Ka-la! Koo-loo!” ko e tangi ia ʻa Kuiki, ʻo hangē ʻokú ne tā hono loungutú ʻi ha ngutu ʻo e steak ʻa Grenadier. Pea ko ia ʻoku motuhi ʻe he ngaahi foʻi ʻaá ʻa e tahí ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi folau mo e ngaahi kaila. Lolotonga iá, naʻe kei fakalotolahiʻi pē ʻe Stubb hono tuʻunga ʻi he vení, ʻene kau tangatá ki he kamataʻangá, ʻi he taimi kotoa ko iá naʻá ne puhiʻi ʻa e kohu mei hono ngutú. Naʻa nau toho ʻo hangē ha kau loto-moʻuá pea nau fakamālohiʻi, kae ʻoua kuo ongo mai ʻa e kaila talitali leleí —“Tuʻu hake, Tasitego!—ʻoange ia kiate ia!” Naʻe lī ʻa e hāpuní. “Stern kotoa!” Naʻe poupouʻi ʻe he kau folaú ʻa e vaí; ʻi he momeniti tatau pē naʻe vela mo ngingila ha meʻa ʻi hona ongo louhiʻi nimá kotoa. Ko e laine fakameaʻá ia. ʻI ha kiʻi taimi nounou kimuʻa, naʻe maʻu vave ʻe Stubb ha toe tafoki ʻe ua mo ia ʻo takai ʻi he ʻuluʻi ʻakaú, mei ai, koeʻuhí ko ʻene fakautuutu ʻene ngaahi takai vave, naʻe puna hake he taimí ni ha kohu lanu pulū hempen ʻo fefiofi mo e ngaahi kohu tuʻumaʻu mei heʻene paipá. ʻI he ʻalu takai holo ʻa e lainé ʻi he ʻuluʻi ʻakaú; pehē foki, kimuʻa siʻi pē peá ne aʻu ki he tuʻunga ko iá, naʻá ne fou atu ʻo fou ʻi he ongo nima ʻo Stubb, ʻa ia naʻe tō fakatuʻupakē mei ai ʻa e ngaahi tupenu-nimá, pe ngaahi tapafā ʻo e kaniva kuo fakaʻaiʻai ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻi he ngaahi taimi ko ʻení. Naʻe hangē ia haʻo puke ʻa e heletā mata-ua māsila ʻa ha filí ʻi hono mataʻi heletā, pea feinga maʻu pē ʻa e fili ko iá ke toʻo ia mei hoʻo nimá.

“Fakamomoko ʻa e lainé!momoko ʻa e lainé!” ko e kaila ia ‘a Stubb ki he tokotaha folau ‘eve‘eva ‘i he tāpuní (na‘á ne tangutu ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘o e tāpuní) ‘a ia, ‘i he‘ene to‘o hono hēkesí, ‘o ne lī ki ai ‘a e vai-tahí.* Na‘e toe lahi ange ‘a e ngaahi afe, ko ia na‘e kamata ke pukepuke ‘e he lainé ‘a hono tu‘ungá. Naʻe lele leva ʻa e vaká ʻi he vai ʻoku velá ʻo hangē ha ʻangá ʻoku ʻi ai hono ngaahi kapakau kotoa pē. Naʻe fetongi heni ʻe Stubb mo Tashtego ʻa e ngaahi feituʻú —stem ki he stern —ko ha pisinisi fakaofo moʻoni ʻi he moveuveu lulululu ko iá.

*Ko e konga ke fakahaa'i 'a e mahu'inga 'o e ngaue ko 'eni, 'e lava ke fakaha heni, 'i he toutai motu'a 'a Holani, na'e ngaue'aki ha mopi ke dash 'aki 'a e laine lele 'aki 'a e vai; ‘i he ngaahi vaka lahi kehe, ‘oku vahe‘i ai ha puaka ‘akau, pe bailer, ki he taumu‘a ko iá. Kae kehe, ko ho‘o hēkesí ‘oku faingofua tahá.

Mei he laine ngalulululu ʻokú ne fakalahi atu ʻa e lōloa kotoa ʻo e konga ʻi ʻolunga ʻo e vaká, pea mei heʻene fufuuʻi ange he taimí ni ʻi ha afo haʻape, naʻá ke mei fakakaukau ʻoku ʻi ai ha ongo ʻuʻulu ʻo e vaká —ko e taha ʻokú ne motuhi ʻa e vaí, ko e taha ʻa e ʻeá —ʻi he ʻalu atu ʻa e vaká ʻo fou atu ʻi he ongo ʻelemēniti fehangahangaí ʻi he taimi pē ʻe taha. Naʻe vaʻinga ha cascade hokohoko ʻi he ngaahi kaufaná; ko ha ʻea ʻoku vilo taʻetuku ʻi hono ʻā; pea, ʻi he kiʻi ngaʻunu siʻisiʻi taha mei loto, naʻa mo ha kiʻi louhiʻi nima, naʻe luelue atu ʻa e vakapuna ngalulululu mo mafesifesi ʻi hono spasmodic gunwale ki he tahí. Naʻe pehē ʻenau lele; ko e tangata takitaha ʻaki ʻa e mālohi mo e tefito ʻo piki ki hono seá, ke taʻofi ʻa hono lī ia ki he foʻi ʻakaú; pea ko e fotunga mā‘olunga ‘o Tashtego ‘i he folau faka‘uli ‘oku punou meimei liunga ua, koe‘uhi ke ‘omai hifo hono senitā ‘o e kalāvité. Naʻe hangē naʻe fakalaka atu ʻa e ʻAtalanitiká mo e Pasifikí kotoa ʻi heʻenau fana ʻi honau halá, kae ʻoua kuo faifai pea kiʻi fakavaivaiʻi ʻe he tafola ʻene puná.

“Toʻo mai —toʻo mai!” ko e kaila ia ʻa Situpi ki he tangata fanafana! pea, ʻi heʻene hanga takai ki he tafola, naʻe kamata ke toho hake ʻe he nima kotoa pē ʻa e vaká kiate ia, lolotonga ia ʻoku kei toho atu ʻa e vaká. Naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo ʻalu hake ʻa Stubb ʻi hono tafaʻakí, ʻo ne tō maʻu hono tuí ʻi he cleat fakapikopikó, ʻo ne lele fakavavevave ki he ika lele; ʻi he lea ʻa e fekaú, naʻe fetongitongi ʻa e vaká ʻo ʻalu atu mei he hala ʻo e ʻalu fakalilifu ʻa e tafola, pea toki lele hake ki ha toe lele ʻe taha.

Naʻe lilingi mai leva ʻa e peau kulokulá mei he tafaʻaki kotoa ʻo e monumanú ʻo hangē ha ngaahi vaitafe ʻi ha moʻungá. Naʻe ʻikai ke takai holo hono sino fakamamahiʻí ʻi he vai māsimá ka ʻi he totó, ʻa ia naʻe pupula mo vela ʻi ha ngaahi furlong ʻi mui ʻi heʻenau ʻā haké. Ko e laʻā fakaʻuli ʻoku vaʻinga ʻi he vaitupu kulokula ko ʻeni ʻi he tahí, naʻá ne fakafoki mai hono ʻataá ki he fofonga kotoa pē, ʻo nau feʻauʻauhi kotoa ʻo hangē ha kau tangata kulokulá. Pea ʻi he taimi kotoa ko iá, naʻe fanaʻi fakamamahi mai ha jet ki he jet ʻo e kohu hinehina mei he spiracle ʻo e tafola, mo e puhi vehement ki he puff mei he ngutu ʻo e ʻulu fiefiá; hangē ko ia ʻi he foʻi fana kotoa pē, ʻo ne toho mai ʻene tao pikopikó (ʻi he laine naʻe fakapipiki ki aí), naʻe toutou fakatonutonu ia ʻe Situpi, ʻaki ha ngaahi tā vave siʻisiʻi ki he gunwale, peá ne toutou ʻave ia ki he tafola.

“Toʻo hake —toʻo hake!” naʻá ne tangi leva ki he tangata fanafanaú, ʻi he mālōlō ʻa e tafola ʻoku fakaʻau ke siʻisiʻí ʻi heʻene ʻitá. “Toʻo hake!—ofi ki he!” pea naʻe lele holo ʻa e vaká ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e iká. ʻI heʻene aʻu mamaʻo atu ʻi he kaufaná, naʻe lī māmālie ʻe Situpi ʻene tao māsila lōloá ki he iká, peá ne tauhi ia ʻi ai, ʻo ne lī fakalelei mo lī, ʻo hangē ʻokú ne feinga fakatokanga ke ongoʻi hili ha ngaahi uati koula ʻe ala folo ʻe he tafola, pea ʻa ia naʻá ne manavasiʻi ke motuhi kimuʻa peá ne toki lava ʻo tautau ia. Ka ko e uati koula ko ia naʻá ne fekumi ki aí ko e moʻui ʻi loto ʻo e iká. Pea ko eni kuo taaʻi ia; he, kamata mei heʻene fakatuʻutāmakí ki he meʻa taʻealafakalea ko ia ʻoku ui ko ʻene “flurry,” naʻe fakalilifu ʻa e monster wallowed ʻi hono totó, ʻo ne takaiʻi ia ʻi he taʻelavameʻa, vale, vela spray, ko ia ko e vakapuna fakatuʻutāmaki, ʻi he taimi pē ko iá ʻoku tō hifo ʻa e astern, naʻe lahi ʻa e ado fakakuihi ki he maama ʻo e ʻaho ko iá.

Pea ko ʻeni ʻi heʻene holo hifo ʻi heʻene leleí, naʻe toe fakatakataka mai ʻa e tafola ʻo sio ki ai; ‘oku ‘alu hake mei he tafa‘aki ki he tafa‘aki; spasmodically fakalahi mo fakasi'isi'i hono spout-hole, mo e ngaahi manava māsila, mafesifesi, mamahi. Faka‘osí, na‘e fana‘i atu ‘a e tafe mai ‘a e toto kulokula kuo faka‘au‘auhifo, ‘o hangē ko e ngaahi fo‘i huhu‘a lanu vāleti ‘o e uaine kulokulá, ki he ‘ea ilifiá; pea toe tō ki mui, lele ʻo tō hifo hono ongo tafaʻaki taʻe-ngaué ki he tahí. Naʻe pa hono lotó!

“Kuó ne mate, Misa Situpi,” ko e lea ia ʻa Daggoó.

“ʻIo;naʻe kohu ʻa e ongo paipá fakatouʻosi!” pea toʻo ʻene meʻa ʻaʻaná mei hono ngutú, naʻe fakamovetevete ʻe Situpi ʻa e efu mate ʻi he vaí; pea, ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti, naʻá ne tuʻu fakakaukau ʻo siofi ʻa e sino lahi naʻá ne ngaohí.





VAHE 62. Ko e Dart.

Ko ha lea fekauʻaki mo ha meʻa naʻe hoko ʻi he vahe fakaʻosí.

Fakatatau ki hono ngāue‘aki ta‘e-liliu ‘o e toutaí, ‘oku teke‘i atu ‘a e vaka-tafola mei he vaká, ‘o hoko ai ‘a e ‘ulu‘i vaká pe ko e tokotaha tāmate-tafola ko e tokotaha faka‘uli fakataimi, pea ko e tokotaha ‘oku ne toho ‘a e ‘a‘alo pe ko e tokotaha-fakama‘u-tafola ‘a e fo‘i ‘a‘alo mu‘omu‘á, ‘a ia ‘oku ‘iloa ko e ‘a‘alo-‘a‘alo. Ko ʻeni ʻokú ne fie maʻu ha nima mālohi mo neave ke ne taaʻi ʻa e ʻuluaki ukameá ki he iká; he ko e taimi lahi, ʻi he meʻa ʻoku ui ko e foʻi ʻakau lōloá, kuo pau ke lī ʻa e meʻangāue mamafá ki he mamaʻo ʻo e fute ʻe uofulu pe tolungofulu. Ka neongo ‘a e lōloa mo e fakaongosia ‘o e tulimanú, ‘oku ‘amanekina ‘e toho ‘e he tokotaha toutai ‘aá ‘ene fo‘i ‘aá ‘i he lolotonga ko iá ‘o a‘u ki he ngata‘angá; ko e mo‘oni, ‘oku ‘amanekina ke ne fokotu‘u ha sīpinga ‘o e ngāue mahulu hake ‘i he tangatá ki he toengá, ‘o ‘ikai ngata pē ‘i he folau ‘eve‘eva ta‘ealafakakaukau‘i, ka ‘i he toutou kaila le‘o-lahi mo ta‘eto‘a; pea mo e meʻa ʻoku ʻuhinga ki ai ʻa e kei kaila ʻi he tumutumu ʻo e kāpasa ʻa ha taha, lolotonga ia ʻoku mafasia ʻa e ngaahi uoua kehé kotoa pea kamata vaeua —ko e hā ia ʻoku ʻikai ha taha ʻokú ne ʻiloʻi ka ko kinautolu pē kuo nau ʻahiʻahiʻi iá. Ko e taha, ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo bawl loto-māfana ʻaupito mo ngāue taʻefakakaukauʻi ʻaupito ʻi he taimi pē ʻe taha. Ko ia ai, ‘i he tu‘unga faingata‘a mo ngungu ko ení, ‘i hono tu‘á ki he iká, ‘oku fanongo leva ai ‘a e tokotaha tā-ha‘apo hela‘iá ki he kaila fakafiefia—“Tu‘u hake, ‘o ‘oange ia kiate ia!” Kuo pau ke ne tuku hifo he taimí ni mo fakamaʻu ʻene foʻi ʻaá, tafoki takai ʻi hono lotomālié ʻi he vaeuaʻanga ʻo e halá, puke ʻene foʻi ʻaá mei he kongalotó, pea ʻi he kiʻi mālohi pē ʻe toé, ʻokú ne ʻēsei ke lī ia ʻi ha faʻahinga founga ki he tafola. ‘Oku ‘ikai ha ofo, ‘i hono to‘o ‘a e fu‘u vakapuna kotoa ‘o e kau tangata tafola ‘i ha sino, mei he ngaahi faingamālie totonu ‘e nimangofulu ki ha fo‘i tā, ‘oku ‘ikai ke lavame‘a ‘a e toko nima; ‘oku ‘ikai ha ofo ‘i he tokolahi ‘o e kau harpooneers ta‘emonū‘ia ‘oku nau fakamala‘ia‘i valevale mo fakaanga‘i; ‘oku ‘ikai ha ofo ‘i he pa mo‘oni ‘a e ni‘ihi ‘o kinautolu honau ngaahi halanga totó ‘i he vaká; ‘oku ‘ikai ha ofo ‘i he ‘ikai ke ‘i ai ha fanga tafola sperm ‘e ni‘ihi ‘i he ta‘u ‘e fā mo e ngaahi pa‘ale ‘e fā; ‘oku ‘ikai ha ofo ‘i he tokolahi ‘o e kau ma‘u vaká, ko e toutai tafola ko ha hoha‘a pē ia ‘oku mole; he ko e tokotaha 'oku ne fai 'a e folau, pea kapau te ke to'o 'a e manava mei hono sino 'e anga fefe ha'o 'amanaki ke ke ma'u ia 'i ai 'i he taimi 'oku fiema'u ai 'e he tokolahi taha!

Toe, kapau ʻe lavameʻa ʻa e foʻi ʻakaú, pea ʻi he taimi mahuʻinga hono uá, ʻa ia, ʻi he taimi ʻoku kamata lele ai ʻa e tafola, ʻoku kamata ke lele tatau pē ʻa e tokotaha ʻoku ʻi he vaká mo e tokotaha ʻoku ʻi he ʻaá ki muʻa mo mui, ki he fakatuʻutāmaki vave ʻo kinaua mo e tokotaha kotoa pē. Ko e taimi ia ‘oku nau fetongi feitu‘u aí; pea ko e ‘ulu, ko e ‘ōfisa pule ‘o e ki‘i vaká, ‘okú ne ma‘u hono tu‘unga totonú ‘i he ngaahi kaufana ‘o e vaká.

Ko ʻeni, ʻoku ʻikai ke u tokanga pe ko hai ʻokú ne tauhi ʻa e fehangahangaí, ka ko e meʻa kotoa ko ʻení ʻoku fakatou fakavalevale mo taʻe fie maʻu. ʻOku totonu ke nofo ʻa e ʻuluʻi ʻulú ʻi he ngaahi kaufaná mei he ʻuluaki ki he fakaʻosí; ‘oku totonu ke ne fakatou fana‘i ‘a e heletaá mo e taó, pea ‘oua ‘e folau ‘eve‘eva ha me‘a pē ‘oku totonu ke ‘amanekina meiate ia, tuku kehe pē ‘i ha ngaahi tu‘unga ‘oku hā mahino ki ha taha pē ‘o e kau toutaí. ʻOku ou ʻiloʻi ʻe kau heni he taimi ʻe niʻihi ha kiʻi mole ʻo e vave ʻi he tulituliá; ka ko e a‘usia fuoloa ‘i he kau toutai tafola kehekehe ‘o e pule‘anga lahi ange ‘i he tahá kuó ne fakatuipau‘i au ‘i he lahi taha ‘o e ngaahi ta‘elavame‘a ‘i he toutaí, na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito ko e vave ‘o e tafola ‘o hangē ko e vaivai ‘a e tokotaha to‘o tafola na‘e fakamatala‘i ki mu‘á ‘a ia kuó ne fakatupunga kinautolú.

Ke fakapapauʻi ʻa e ola lelei taha ʻi he foʻi ʻakaú, kuo pau ke kamata ʻa e kau fanafana ʻo e māmani ko ʻení ki honau vaʻé mei he ʻikai ke nau ngāué, kae ʻikai mei he ngāue mālohí.





VAHE 63. Ko e Koloti.

Mei he fu‘u ‘akaú, ‘oku tupu ai ‘a e ngaahi va‘á; meiate kinautolu, ko e ngaahi va‘a. Ko ia, ‘i he ngaahi kaveinga ‘oku ola leleí, fakatupulaki ‘a e ngaahi vahé.

Ko e crotch na'e lave ki ai 'i ha peesi kimu'a 'oku taau ke lave tau'ataina ki ai. Ko ha fuʻu ʻakau notched ia ʻo ha fotunga makehe, ʻoku fute ʻe ua nai hono lōloá, ʻa ia ʻoku fakahū fakahangatonu ki he starboard gunwale ofi ki he kaufana, ʻi he taumuʻa ke ʻoatu ha mālōlōʻanga ki he ngataʻanga ʻakau ʻo e harpoon, ʻa ia ko hono ngataʻanga telefua kehe, barbed slopingly projects mei he prow. ‘I aí ‘oku ofi leva ai ‘a e me‘ataú ki hono tokotaha lī ‘o e me‘ataú, ‘a ia ‘okú ne to‘o vave ia mei hono mālōloó ‘o hangē pē ko hono ta‘alo ‘e ha tangata ‘akau ‘i mui ‘ene me‘afaná mei he ‘ā. ‘Oku anga-maheni‘aki ke ‘i ai ha ongo fo‘i ‘akau ‘oku na mālōlō ‘i he kongalotó, ‘a ia ‘oku ui fakahokohoko ko e ‘uluaki mo e ua ‘o e ‘aioné.

Ka ko e ongo foʻi ʻakau ko ʻení, ʻoku takitaha ʻi hono afo ʻoʻoná, ʻokú na fakatou fehokotaki mo e laine; ko e meʻa ko ʻení: ke fakaʻauha kinaua fakatouʻosi, kapau ʻe lava, ʻo hili pē ʻa e taha ʻi he taimi pē ko iá ki he tafola tatau; koeʻuhí kapau, ʻi he toho ʻoku haʻú, ʻe toʻo mai ʻe he taha, ʻe kei lava pē ʻe he taha ʻo pukepuke. Ko e liunga ua ia ʻo e ngaahi faingamālié. Ka ‘oku fa‘a hoko ‘aupito koe‘uhi ko e lele fakafokifā, fakamālohi, mo faka‘au‘auhifo ‘a e tafola ‘i he‘ene ma‘u ‘a e ‘uluaki ‘aioné, ‘oku hoko ai ‘o ta‘emalava ki he tokotaha ‘oku ‘i he ‘aá, neongo ‘ene hangē ha ‘uhilá ‘i he‘ene ngaahi nga‘unu, ke ne lī ‘a e ‘aione hono uá kiate ia. Ka neongo ia, ʻi he ʻosi fehokotaki ʻa e ukamea hono uá mo e lainé, pea ʻoku lele ʻa e lainé, ko ia ai kuo pau ke ʻamanaki ke lī ʻa e meʻatau ko iá, ʻi he meʻa kotoa pē, ki tuʻa mei he vaká, ʻi ha faʻahinga founga mo ha feituʻu; ka ʻikai ko e fakatuʻutāmaki fakalilifu tahá ʻe kau ai ʻa e nima kotoa pē. Tumbled ki he vai, 'oku fakatatau ia ki he ngaahi me'a pehe; ko e ngaahi coils spare 'o e laine 'o e puha ('oku lave ki ai 'i ha vahe kimu'a) 'o 'ai 'a e feat ko 'eni, 'i he ngaahi me'a lahi taha, 'oku lava ke fakahoko fakapotopoto. Ka ʻoku ʻikai ke taʻetokangaʻi maʻu pē ʻa e ngāue fakatuʻutāmaki ko ʻení mo e ngaahi lavea fakaloloma mo fakatupu mate tahá.

‘Ikai ngata aí: kuo pau ke ke ‘ilo‘i ‘i he taimi ‘oku lī ai ‘a e ‘aione hono uá ki ‘uta, ‘oku hoko ia mei ai ko ha me‘a fakailifia ‘oku tautau, ‘o mata māsila, ‘o ne pikopiko faka‘ohovale ‘o takai holo ‘i he vaká mo e tafola fakatou‘osi, ‘o ne lavelavea ‘a e ngaahi lainé, pe motuhia kinautolu, pea ‘okú ne fai ha ongo fakaofo ‘i he ngaahi tafa‘aki kotoa pē. Pea, ‘i he tu‘unga fakalūkufuá, ‘oku ‘ikai malava ke toe malu‘i ia kae ‘oua kuo puke totonu ‘a e tafola pea ko ha sino mate.

Fakakaukau angé, he taimí ni, ki he founga kuo pau ke hoko ‘i he tu‘unga ‘o e ngaahi vaka ‘e fā ‘oku nau fetaulaki kotoa mo ha tafola ‘e taha ‘oku mālohi, longomo‘ui, mo ‘ilo‘ilo pau ta‘e angamaheni; ‘i he taimi koe‘uhi ko e ngaahi ‘ulungaanga ko eni ‘iate iá, pea pehē ki he ngaahi fakatu‘utāmaki ‘e lauiafe ‘oku hoko fakataha ‘i ha ngāue loto-to‘a pehē, ‘oku tautau nai ‘i he taimi tatau ‘a e ngaahi ‘aione sekoni ‘e valu pe hongofulu ‘oku vetevete ‘iate ia. He, ko e mo‘oni, ‘oku ‘oange ki he vaka taki taha ha ngaahi fo‘i ‘akau lahi ke punou ki ai ki he lainé kapau ‘e ‘ikai ola lelei hono ‘ohofi ‘a e ‘uluaki vaká ‘o ‘ikai ha toe fakaakeake. ʻOku fakamatalaʻi faivelenga heni ʻa e ngaahi fakaikiiki kotoa ko ʻení, he ʻe ʻikai ke nau taʻe fakamahinoʻi ha ngaahi konga mahuʻinga taha ʻe niʻihi, neongo ʻene faingataʻá, ʻi ha ngaahi fakatātā ʻi he hili ʻeni ʻe tā valivalí.





VAHE 64. Koe Kai Efiafi a Stubb.

Naʻe ʻosi tāmateʻi ʻa e tafola ʻa Stubb ʻi ha kiʻi mamaʻo mei he vaká. Ko ha nonga ia; ko ia, ʻi heʻemau faʻu ha vaka ʻe tolu, naʻa mau kamata ʻa e pisinisi māmālie ʻo hono toho ʻo e ipu ki he Pequod. Pea ko ʻeni, ʻi heʻemau ngāue māmālie ʻa e kau tangata ʻe toko hongofulu mā valu mo homau ongo nima ʻe tolungofulu mā ono, mo e ngaahi louhiʻi nima mo e louhiʻi nima ʻe teau valungofulu, ʻi he houa ki he houa ʻi he sino mate taʻeʻaonga mo fakapikopiko ko ia ʻi he tahí; pea naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai ke fuʻu ngaʻunu, tukukehe pē ʻi ha ngaahi vahaʻataimi lōloa; na‘e ‘omai heni ha fakamo‘oni lelei ki he lahi ‘o e fu‘u kakai na‘a mau hiki atú. He, ‘i he fu‘u kanali lahi ‘o Hang-Ho, pe ko e hā pē ha me‘a ‘oku nau ui ki aí, ‘i Siaina, ‘e to‘o mai ai ‘e ha kau ngāue ‘e toko fā pe toko nima ‘i he hala va‘é ha veve lahi ‘oku uta ‘i he vave ‘o e maile ‘e taha ‘i he houa; ka ko e argosy lahi ko ʻení naʻa mau toho mamafa ʻo ngaohiʻaki, ʻo hangē ʻoku uta ʻaki ʻa e puaka-lead ʻi he lahi.

Naʻe hoko mai ʻa e fakapoʻulí; ka naʻe tataki fakapoʻuli ʻemau halá ʻe ha ngaahi maama ʻe tolu ʻi ʻolunga mo lalo ʻi he main-rigging ʻo e Pequod; kae ʻoua kuo mau ofi ange ʻo sio kia ʻĒhapi ʻokú ne fakatō hifo ha taha ʻo e ngaahi maama lahi ange ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he ngaahi ʻā maluʻí. ʻI heʻene sio ʻataʻatā pē ki he tafola ʻoku ʻalu haké ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti, naʻá ne ʻoatu ʻa e ngaahi fekau angamaheni ki hono maluʻi ia ki he pō ko iá, peá ne ʻoange leva ʻene māmá ki ha tangata toutai, ʻo ne ʻalu atu ki he loto lokí, pea ʻikai ke ne toe haʻu ki muʻa kae ʻoua kuo pongipongi.

Neongo, ‘i he‘ene tokanga‘i ‘a e tuli ‘o e tafola ko ení, na‘e fakamo‘oni‘i ai ‘e Kapiteni ‘Ēhapi ‘a ‘ene ngāue anga-mahení, ke ui pehē; ka ko ʻeni kuo mate ʻa e meʻamoʻuí, naʻe hangē naʻe ngāue ʻiate ia ha taʻefiemālie taʻemahino, pe taʻekātaki, pe loto-moʻua; hangē naʻe fakamanatu ange ʻe he sio ki he sino mate ko iá ʻoku teʻeki ke tāmateʻi ʻa Mopi Tiki; pea neongo naʻe ʻomi ha fanga tafola kehe ʻe taha afe ki hono vaká, ka ʻe ʻikai ke lava ʻe he meʻa kotoa ko iá ʻo fakalakalaka ʻene meʻa fakaʻeiʻeiki mo monomaniac. Naʻe vave ʻaupito hoʻo fakakaukau mei he leʻo ʻi he ngaahi fungavaka ʻo e Pequod, ʻoku teuteu ʻa e nima kotoa pē ke lī taula ʻi he lolotó; he 'oku toho 'a e ngaahi seini mamafa 'i he fungavaka, pea 'oku 'ohofi 'a e ngaahi ava 'o e taulanga. Ka ‘i he ngaahi fehokotaki‘anga longoa‘a ko iá, ko e sino fu‘u sinó tonu, ‘o ‘ikai ko e vaká, ‘oku totonu ke tau‘anga. ‘I hono ha‘i ‘e he ‘ulú ki he mui‘i vaká, pea ‘i he hiku ki he ngaahi kaufaná, ‘oku tokoto he taimí ni ‘a e tafola mo hono sino ‘uli‘ulí ‘o ofi ki he sino ‘o e vaká pea ‘oku sio ki ai ‘i he fakapo‘uli ‘o e po‘ulí, ‘a ia na‘á ne fakapo‘uli‘i ‘a e ngaahi spars mo e rigging ‘i ‘olungá, ko e ongo me‘á ni—vaka mo e tafola, na‘e hā ngali ‘ioke fakataha ‘o hangē ha fanga ki‘i pulu lalahi ‘e tahá, ‘oku kei ‘i ai pē ‘a e fanga pulu lalahi ‘e tahá.

Ko e puke malohi mo falala'anga taha 'oku ma'u 'e he vaka ki he tafola 'i he taimi 'oku tau ai 'i he tafa'aki, ko e 'i he ngaahi flukes pe hiku; pea hangē ko ia mei hono lahi ange ‘o e density ‘oku mamafa ange ‘a e konga ko iá ‘i ha toe konga kehe (tukukehe ‘a e ngaahi side-fins), ko ‘ene fe‘unu‘aki na‘a mo e mate, ‘okú ne fakatupunga ia ke ne ngoto ma‘ulalo ‘i lalo ‘i he fukahi tahí; koe‘uhi ke ‘oua ‘e lava ke ke ma‘u ia ‘aki ‘a e nimá mei he vaká, koe‘uhi ke ke ‘ai ‘a e seini ‘o takatakai‘i ia. Ka ‘oku ikuna‘i fakapotopoto ‘a e faingata‘á ni: ‘oku teuteu‘i ha ki‘i laine mālohi mo ha fo‘i ‘akau ‘i hono ngata‘anga ki tu‘á, pea mo ha me‘a mamafa ‘i hono lotolotongá, lolotonga ia ‘oku fakama‘u ‘a e ngata‘anga ‘e tahá ki he vaká. ‘I hono pule‘i pōto‘í ‘oku ‘ai ai ‘a e fo‘i ‘akaú ke ne ‘alu hake ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘e taha ‘o e fu‘u ‘akaú, ko ia ‘i he taimi ní kuo ha‘i ai ‘a e tafola, ‘oku faingofua ke ngaohi ‘a e seini ke ne muimui ki ai; pea ‘i hono faka‘au‘auhifo ‘i he sinó, ‘oku faifai pē ‘o loka‘i vave ‘o takai ‘i he konga si‘isi‘i taha ‘o e hiku, ‘i he feitu‘u ‘oku fetaulaki ai mo hono ngaahi fo‘i fulufulu‘i‘akau pe ngaahi fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i ‘uhiki‘i ‘ulú.

Kapau naʻe fakalongolongo kotoa ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi he taimí ni, ʻo ʻikai toe siʻi hifo ʻi he meʻa ʻe lava ke ʻiloʻi ʻi he funga vakapuná, naʻe lavakiʻi ʻe Stubb, ko hono hoa hono uá, ʻi heʻene ikunaʻí, ha fiefia taʻe angamaheni ka ʻoku kei lelei pē. Ko ha fuʻu femoʻuekina taʻefaʻalaua pehē naʻá ne ʻi heʻene fakafisi fakalongolongo ʻa e Starbuck tuʻumaʻú, ko hono pule fakaʻofisialé, kiate ia ʻi he taimi ko iá ʻa e puleʻi pē ʻe taha ʻo e ngaahi meʻá. Ko e kiʻi tupuʻanga tokoni ʻe taha ʻo e moʻui kotoa ko ʻeni ʻi Stubb, naʻe vave hono fakahaaʻi fakaofo. Ko Stubb ko ha ʻate māʻolunga; naʻá ne kiʻi manako taʻefakaʻatuʻi ʻi he tafola ko ha meʻa fakaʻofoʻofa ki hono palaté.

'I heni ke 'iloa, neongo 'oku 'ikai ko e kau toutai vao ko 'eni, ko ha me'a fakalukufua, pea fakatatau ki he maxim fakakautau lahi, 'ai 'a e fili defray 'a e ngaahi fakamole lolotonga 'o e tau ('i he si'isi'i taha kimu'a pea toki fakatokanga'i 'a e pa'anga 'oku ma'u mei he folau), ka 'i he taimi ni pea 'oku ke ma'u 'a e ni'ihi 'o e kau Nantucketers ko 'eni 'oku nau ma'u ha mo'oni 'o e Stubble makehe 'o e tisaini 'e he S; ‘oku kau ai ‘a e ngata‘anga ‘o e sinó ‘oku faka‘au‘auhifo.

ʻI he vaeua poʻulí nai naʻe motuhia mo tunu ʻa e siteki ko iá; pea ʻi hono tutu ʻe ha ongo maama lolo ʻo e sperm, naʻe tuʻu fefeka hake ʻa Stubb ki heʻene maʻu meʻatokoni efiafi spermaceti ʻi he ʻulu-kapiteni, ʻo hangē ko e kapiteni ko iá ko ha laupapa tafaʻaki. Pea naʻe ʻikai foki ko Stubb pē naʻá ne kai ʻa e kakanoʻi tafola ʻi he pō ko iá. ‘I he‘ene fefiofi ‘enau ngaahi ngungu mo ‘ene ngaahi mastications pē ‘a‘aná, ko e fanga ‘anga‘anga ‘e lauiafe ‘i he lauiafe, ‘oku nau ‘oho takai holo ‘i he lēvaiatani maté, ‘o nau kai faka‘aufuli ‘i hono ngakó. Naʻe faʻa ʻohovale ʻa e kau mohe tokosiʻi ʻi lalo ʻi honau ngaahi mohengá ʻi he tā māsila ʻo honau hiku ʻi he ʻaá, ʻi ha ngaahi ʻinisi siʻi pē mei he loto ʻo e kau mohé. ʻI hoʻo sio atu ki he tafaʻakí te ke lava pē ʻo sio kiate kinautolu (hangē ko ia kimuʻa peá ke fanongo kiate kinautolú) ʻoku nau luelue ʻi he ngaahi vai ʻuliʻuli mo fakaʻuliʻulí, pea nau tafoki ʻi honau tuʻá ʻi heʻenau toʻo mai ha ngaahi konga lalahi ʻo e tafola ʻo e lahi ʻo e ʻulu ʻo ha tangatá. Ko e me‘a makehe ko eni ‘a e ‘angá ‘oku hā ngali fakaofo kotoa.
