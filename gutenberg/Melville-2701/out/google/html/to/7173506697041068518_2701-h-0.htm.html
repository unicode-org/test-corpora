NGAAHI TOʻO. (ʻOku ʻomi ʻe ha Pule Laipeli Siʻisiʻi).

'E sio ki ai ko e burrower mo e grub-worm mamahi noa'ia ko 'eni 'o ha tevolo masiva 'o ha Sub-Sub 'oku ngali na'a ne fou 'i he ngaahi Vatikano loloa mo e ngaahi falekoloa 'i he hala 'o e mamani, 'o ne to'o hake ha fa'ahinga random allusions ki he fanga tafola 'e lava ke ne ma'u 'i ha fa'ahinga tohi pe, toputapu pe fakalielia. Ko ia ai kuo pau ke ʻoua naʻá ke, ʻi he meʻa kotoa pē ʻo ʻikai toe siʻi hifo, ʻo toʻo ʻa e ngaahi fakamatala ʻo e tafola higgledy-piggledy, neongo pe ko e hā hono moʻoní, ʻi he ngaahi konga ko ʻení, ki ha cetology fakakosipeli moʻoni. Mamaʻo mei ai. Hange ko e uesia 'a e kau fa'u tohi 'o e kuonga mu'a fakalukufua, pea pehe ki he kau fa'u-tohi 'oku 'asi heni, 'Oku mahu'inga pe fakafiefia 'a e ngaahi konga ko 'eni, 'i he affording ha glancing bird's eye view ki he me'a kuo promiscuously lea'aki, fakakaukau, fancied, mo hiva'i 'o e Livaiatane, 'e he ngaahi pule'anga mo e ngaahi to'utangata lahi, kau ai 'etau ngaahi to'utangata.

Ko ia 'oku ke lele lelei, tevolo masiva 'o ha Sub-Sub, 'a ia ko 'ene fakamatala au. ʻOkú ke kau ki he matakali taʻeʻamanaki mo lanu ko ia ʻoku ʻikai ha uaine ʻo e māmani ko ʻení te ne fakamāfanaʻí; pea kiate kinautolu naʻa mo Pale Sherry ʻe fuʻu rosy-mālohi; ka ko ia ʻoku ʻofa ha taha ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ke tangutu mo ia, pea ongoʻi masiva-tēvolo, foki; pea tupu ʻo angaʻofa ʻi he loʻimatá; pea lea fakahangatonu kiate kinautolu, ʻi he mata fonu mo e sioʻata ʻataʻatā, pea ʻi ha loto-mamahi ʻoku ʻikai fakaʻau ke taʻefakafiefia —Tukuange ia, Sub-Subs! He ko e lahi ange hoʻomou faingataʻaʻia ke fakafiefiaʻi ʻa e māmaní, ko e lahi ange ia hoʻomou ʻalu taʻefakafetaʻi ʻo taʻengatá! Pe na'a ku lava 'o fakama'a 'a e Hampton Court mo e Tuileries ma'amoutolu! Ka mou folo hifo homou loʻimatá pea ʻalu hake ki he pou fakatuʻí ʻaki homou lotó; he ko ho ngaahi kaumeʻa kuo nau ʻalu kimuʻá ʻoku nau fakamaʻa ʻa e ngaahi langi fungavaka fitú, pea ʻoku nau ngaohi ʻa e kau sola ʻo Kepaleli, Maikeli, mo Lafaeli kuo nau fakaʻofoʻofaʻia fuoloá, ke fakafepakiʻi hoʻo haʻú. ʻOku mou taaʻi fakataha heni ʻa e ngaahi loto ka ʻoku moveteveté —ʻi aí, te mou taaʻi ha ngaahi sioʻata taʻe-movetevete!

“Fakafokifā pē kuo ‘asi hake ha fu‘u me‘a lahi mei he vaí, ‘o fana fakahangatonu hake ki he ‘eá. Ko e tafola ia.” — Ko Miliame Kofini pe ko e Toutai Tafola .

“Ko e Tafola ‘oku ‘ai ke fakapapau‘i: kae fakakaukau atu, ‘e anga-fēfē ho‘o pule‘i ha ki‘i ‘asi mālohi ‘oku te‘eki ke motu‘a, ‘aki pē ‘a e me‘angāue ‘o ha maea ‘oku ha‘i ki he aka ‘o hono hiku.” — Ko ha Vahe ki he Tuli Tafola ‘i he Ngaahi Hui mo e Ngaahi Loli .

“ʻI ha taimi ʻe taha naʻá ku sio ai ki ha ongo manu fekai ko ʻení (tafola) mahalo ko e tangata mo e fefine, ʻokú na luelue māmālie, ʻo hokohoko atu, ʻi ha vahaʻataimi ʻoku siʻi hifo ʻi ha foʻi maka ʻe taha mei he matāfangá” (Terra Del Fuego), “ʻa ia naʻe fakaloloa atu ʻe he ʻakau ko e pií hono ngaahi vaʻá.” — Ko e Folau ‘a Tāvini ‘o ha Tokotaha Fakanatula .

“‘Stern kotoa!’ ko e kaila ia ‘a e hoá, ‘i he‘ene tafoki hono ‘ulú, na‘á ne sio ai ki he ngaahi nifo ‘o ha fu‘u Sperm Whale ‘oku mafola ‘o ofi ki he ‘ulu ‘o e vaká, ‘o fakamanamana‘i ia ‘aki ‘a e faka‘auha vave;—‘Stern all, for your lives!’” — Wharton the Whale Killer .

“Ko ia mou fiefia, ‘eku kau tama, ‘oua na‘a teitei tō homou lotó, Lolotonga ‘oku taa‘i ‘e he tokotaha tā-ha‘apo loto-to‘á ‘a e tafola!” — Hiva ʻo Nanitā .

“ʻOiauē, ko e Tafola motuʻa ʻoku ʻikai ke faʻa maʻú, ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e afā mo e matangi .
‘I hono ‘api ‘oseni ‘e .
Ko ha fu‘u tangata ‘i he mālohi, ‘a ia ‘oku totonu ai ‘a e mālohí, .
Pea ko e Tu‘i ‘o e tahi ta‘efakangatangata.”
— Hiva ʻo e Tafola .
