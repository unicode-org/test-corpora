Ka ko e taʻeʻaonga ke fakaʻaongaʻi ʻa e ngaahi meʻa lolotó, pea ʻoku loloto ʻa e moʻoni kotoa pē. ʻI heʻemau vilo mamaʻo hifo mei loto ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e Hōtele de Cluny ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻakau ko ʻení ʻa ia ʻoku mau tuʻu ai hení —neongo pe ko e hā hono lahi mo e fakaofo, tuku ia he taimí ni; —pea mou ʻalu homou halá, ʻa kimoutolu ngaahi laumālie fakaʻeiʻeiki ange, mo lotomamahi angé, ki he ngaahi holo lahi ko ia ʻo Loma ʻi Temesí; ʻa ia ʻoku mamaʻo ʻi lalo ʻi he ngaahi taua fakaofo ʻo e māmani ʻi ʻolunga ʻo e tangatá, ʻa hono aka ʻo e maʻongoʻongá, ʻoku tangutu ai hono ʻulungaanga fakalilifu kotoa ʻi he tuʻunga ʻuluʻulu; ko ha me‘a motu‘a ‘oku tanu ‘i lalo ‘i he ngaahi me‘a motu‘á, pea fakanofo ‘i he ngaahi sino! Ko ia ‘i ha taloni maumau, ‘oku manuki‘i ai ‘e he ngaahi ‘otua lalahí ‘a e tu‘i pōpula ko iá; ko ia hangē ha Caryatid, ʻokú ne tangutu kātaki, ʻo poupouʻi ʻi hono laʻe ʻaisi ʻa e ngaahi entablatures kuo fakaputu ʻo e ngaahi kuongá. Mou matangi hifo ʻi ai, ʻa kimoutolu ngaahi laumālie pōlepole mo lotomamahi ange! fehuʻi ʻa e tuʻi pōlepole mo lotomamahi ko iá! Ko ha fakatātā fakafāmili! ʻio, naʻá ne fanauʻi ʻa kimoutolu, ʻa kimoutolu kau talavou fakatuʻi fakaheeʻí; pea mei ho'o grim sire pe 'e ha'u ai 'a e State-secret motu'a.

Ko ʻeni, ʻi hono lotó, naʻe ʻi ai ha kiʻi sio ʻa ʻĒhapi ki he meʻá ni, ʻa ia: ʻoku ʻatamai lelei ʻeku ngaahi meʻangāué kotoa, ʻoku vale ʻeku taumuʻá mo ʻeku meʻá. Ka ʻoku ʻikai ha mālohi ke tāmateʻi, pe liliu, pe fakaʻehiʻehi mei he moʻoní; naʻá ne ʻiloʻi foki naʻá ne fuoloa ʻene fakangalingali ki he faʻahinga ʻo e tangatá; ʻi ha faʻahinga, naʻe kei. Ka ko e meʻa ko ia ʻo ʻene fakapulipulí naʻe moʻulaloa pē ia ki heʻene perceptibility, ʻo ʻikai ki hono loto ke fakapapauʻí. Ka neongo ia, naʻá ne lavameʻa ʻaupito ʻi he fakapulipuli ko iá, ʻo ʻi heʻene laka fakaʻosi ki ʻuta mo e vaʻe ʻaivolí, naʻe ʻikai ha taha ʻo Nantucketer naʻá ne fakakaukau kehe kiate ia ka ko e loto mamahi fakanatula pē, pea ko e meʻa ia ki he vave, ʻi he lavea fakalilifu naʻá ne maʻu iá.

Ko e līpooti ‘o ‘ene delirium ta‘ealafaka‘ikai‘i ‘i tahí na‘e toe pehē ia ‘e he kakaí ki ha tupu‘anga fekau‘aki. Pea pehē foki, ko e ngaahi loto-mamahi kotoa pē naʻe tānaki mai ʻa ia naʻe ʻi ai maʻu pē ʻi he hili iá, ʻo aʻu ki he ʻaho tonu ʻo e folau ʻi he Pequod ʻi he folau lolotongá, naʻe tangutu ʻo fakakaukauloto ʻi hono laʻé. Pea ʻoku ʻikai foki ke fuʻu ngalingali, ʻi he mamaʻo mei he taʻefalala ki heʻene feʻunga ki ha toe folau toutai tafola, koeʻuhí ko e ngaahi fakaʻilonga fakapoʻuli peheé, naʻe hehema ʻa e kakai fakafuofua ʻo e motu fakapotopoto ko iá ke tauhi ʻa e pōlepolé, ʻo pehē koeʻuhí ko e ngaahi ʻuhinga tonu ko iá naʻá ne taau lelei ange mo fokotuʻu ʻi he tafaʻaki, ki ha tulituli ʻoku fonu ʻi he ʻita mo e hunoness. Gnawed 'i loto pea vela 'i tu'a, mo e infixed, ta'etuku 'a e fangs 'o ha ngaahi fakakaukau ta'efaito'o; ko e tokotaha peheé, kapau na‘e lava ke ma‘u ia, ‘e ngali ko e tangata tonu ia ke ne fana‘i ‘ene ‘aioné pea hiki hake ‘ene taó ki he fanga manu fakalilifu taha ‘o e fanga manu fekai kotoa pē. Pe, kapau ‘i ha ‘uhinga ‘oku fakakaukau ai ‘oku ta‘emalava fakaesino ia ki he me‘a ko iá, ka ‘e ngali superlatively taukei ‘a e tokotaha peheé ke fakafiefia‘i mo tangi ‘i hono kau ngāue ‘i laló ki he ‘ohofí. Ka neongo ia, ʻoku pau pē, ʻi he fakapulipuli vale ʻo ʻene ʻita taʻetuku ʻoku fakaʻauha mo fakaʻaongaʻi ʻiate iá, naʻe fakataumuʻa ʻa ʻĒhapi ke folau ʻi he folau lolotongá mo e meʻa pē ʻe taha mo fakaʻofoʻofa kotoa pē ko hono tuli ʻo e Tafola Hinehina. Kapau naʻe ʻi ai ha taha ʻo hono ngaahi maheni motuʻá ʻi ʻuta ka naʻá ne misi vaeua ʻi he meʻa naʻe toitoi ʻiate ia ʻi he taimi ko iá, ʻe vave fēfē hono haeʻi ʻe honau laumālie fakaʻohovale mo māʻoniʻoní ʻa e vaká mei ha tangata fakatēvolo pehē! Naʻa nau punou ki he ngaahi folau vakapuna ʻaonga, ko e paʻangá ke lau hifo ʻi he paʻanga mei he miniti. Naʻá ne fakataumuʻa ki ha tautea loto-toʻa, taʻe-faʻa-fakaʻatuʻi, mo fakanatula.

Ko ia, naʻe ʻi heni ʻa e motuʻa ʻulu hina mo taʻefakaʻotua ko ʻení, ʻokú ne tuli ʻaki ha ngaahi fakamalaʻia ha tafola ʻa Siope ʻi he māmaní, ʻi he ʻulu ʻo ha kau folau, foki, ʻoku tefito ʻi he kau fakafepaki mongrel, mo e kau liʻaki, mo e kau kai tangata —naʻe fakavaivaiʻi fakaʻulungaanga foki, ʻe he taʻemalava ʻo e virtundebu-right or mere unaidemick, . jollity ta'e-lavea 'o e ta'etokanga mo e ta'efakakaukau 'i Stubb, pea mo e mediocrity 'oku mafola 'i Flask. Ko ha kau folau pehē, ʻoku ʻōfisaʻi pehē, naʻe ngali fili makehe mo fakafonu ʻe ha ngaahi mate fakaheli ke tokoniʻi ia ki heʻene tautea monomaniac. Ko e hā hono lahi ʻo ʻenau tali lahi ʻaupito ʻa e ʻita ʻa e motuʻá —ʻi he meʻa fakaofo kovi naʻe maʻu ʻe honau laumālié, ʻo hangē ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻene fehiʻá ʻoku meimei ʻanautolu ia; ko e Tafola Hinehina ‘o hangē pē ko honau fili ta‘ealakātaki‘í ‘o hangē ko ‘ene fili; ko e founga naʻe hoko ai ʻa e meʻá ni kotoa —ko e hā ʻa e Tafola Hinehina kiate kinautolú, pe ko e hā ki heʻenau mahino taʻeʻiloʻí, foki, ʻi ha founga fakapoʻuli, taʻefakakaukauʻi, mahalo naʻá ne ngali ko e tēvolo lahi ʻoku lele holo ʻo e ngaahi tahi ʻo e moʻuí, —ko e meʻa kotoa ko ʻení ke fakamatalaʻí, ʻe hoko ia ko ha luelue loloto ange ʻi he meʻa ʻe lava ke ʻalu ki ai ʻa ʻIsimeʻelí. Ko e tokotaha keli ‘i lalofonua ‘oku ngāue ‘iate kitautolu kotoá, ‘e lava fēfē ke ‘ilo‘i ‘e ha taha ‘a e feitu‘u ‘oku taki atu ai ‘ene va‘é ‘i he le‘o ‘oku hiki ma‘u pē mo fakalongolongo ‘o ‘ene piká? Ko hai ‘oku ‘ikai te ne ongo‘i ‘a e toho ta‘ealafakafepaki‘i ‘o e nimá? Ko e hā ha vaka ʻoku toho ʻe ha fitungofulu mā fā ʻe lava ke tuʻu maʻu? Ko e taha, naʻá ku tukuange au ki he liʻaki ʻo e taimí mo e feituʻú; ka lolotonga ʻoku kei lele kotoa ke fetaulaki mo e tafola, naʻe ʻikai ke nau lava ʻo sio ki ha meʻa ʻi he manu fekai ko iá ka ko e mahaki fakatupu mate tahá.





VAHE 42. Koe Hinehina oe Tafola.

Ko e me'a na'e hoko 'a e tafola hinehina kia 'Ehapi, kuo faka'ilonga'i; ko e hā, ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi, na‘á ne hoko kiate aú, ‘oku te‘eki ai ke lea‘aki.

Makehe mei he ngaahi fakakaukau mahino ange ko ia ʻoku uesia ʻa Mopi Tiki, ʻa ia naʻe ʻikai lava ke ne fakaakeake ʻi ha ngaahi taimi ʻi ha laumālie ʻo ha tangata ha faʻahinga fakatokanga, naʻe ʻi ai ha fakakaukau ʻe taha, pe ʻoku sai ange ke pehē, ko ha fakalilifu taʻemahino, taʻehingoa fekauʻaki mo ia, ʻa ia ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻi hono mālohí naʻá ne ikunaʻi fakaʻaufuli ʻa e toenga kotoa pē; pea neongo ia naʻe fuʻu fakamisiteli mo meimei ʻikai lava ke fakamatalaʻi ia, ʻo u meimei loto-moʻua ke fokotuʻu ia ʻi ha founga ʻoku mahino. Ko e hinehina ʻo e tafola ʻa ia naʻá ne fakaʻohovaleʻi au ʻi he meʻa kotoa pē. Ka ʻe anga fēfē haʻaku ʻamanaki ke fakamatalaʻi au heni; pea neongo ia, ʻi ha founga fakapoʻuli, fakatuʻupakē, fakamatalaʻi au kuo pau ke u, ka ʻikai mahalo ʻe ʻikai ha meʻa ʻa e ngaahi vahe kotoa ko ʻení.

Neongo ‘i he ngaahi me‘a fakanatula lahi, ‘oku fakalahi fakalelei‘i ai ‘e he hinehina ‘a e faka‘ofo‘ofá, ‘o hangē ‘okú ne ‘omai ha ‘ulungaanga lelei makehe ‘o‘ona, ‘o hangē ko ia ‘i he ngaahi maka māpelé, japonicas, mo e mata‘itofé; pea neongo kuo ‘ilo‘i ‘e he ngaahi pule‘anga kehekehe ‘i ha fa‘ahinga founga ha tu‘unga fakatu‘i pau ‘i he lanu ko ení; na‘a mo e ngaahi tu‘i motu‘a faka‘ei‘eiki mo faka‘ei‘eiki ‘o Peku ‘oku nau fokotu‘u ‘a e hingoa “‘Eiki ‘o e Ngaahi ‘Elefānite Hinehina” ‘o mahulu hake ‘i he‘enau ngaahi fakamatala faka‘ofo‘ofa kehe kotoa pē ‘o e pulé; pea ko e ngaahi tuʻi ʻo Siam ʻi onopōní ʻoku nau fakaava ʻa e manupuna vaʻe fā hinehina sinou tatau ʻi he fuka fakatuʻí; pea mo e fuka ʻo Hanover ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e fakatātā ʻe taha ʻo ha charger hinehina sinou; pea ko e ‘Emipaea lahi ‘o ‘Aositelēliá, ko Cæsarian, ko e ‘ea ki he pule ‘o Loma, ‘o ne ma‘u ki he lanu faka‘emipolá ‘a e lanu faka‘emipolá tatau; pea neongo ‘oku kaunga ‘a e mu‘omu‘a ko eni ‘i aí ki he fa‘ahinga ‘o e tangatá tonu, ‘o ‘oange ai ki he tangata hinehina ‘a e pule faka‘ofo‘ofa ki he matakali fakapo‘uli kotoa pē; pea neongo, tuku kehe ‘a e me‘á ni kotoa, kuo a‘u ‘o ‘ai ‘a e hinehina ke faka‘uhinga‘i ‘a e fiefiá, he ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e kau Lomá na‘e faka‘ilonga‘i ai ‘e ha maka hinehina ha ‘aho fiefia; pea neongo ʻi he ngaahi kaungāongoʻi mo e ngaahi fakataipe fakamatelie kehé, ʻoku ngaohi ʻa e lanu tatau ko ʻení ko e fakaʻilonga ʻo e ngaahi meʻa ongo mo fakaʻeiʻeiki lahi —ʻa e taʻehalaia ʻo e kau mali malí, ʻa e angaʻofa ʻo e taʻu motuʻá; neongo ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e Kau Tangata Kulokula ʻo ʻAmeliká ko hono foaki ʻo e fusi hinehina ʻo e wampum ko e tukupā loloto taha ia ʻo e fakaʻapaʻapá; neongo 'i he ngaahi 'ea lahi, 'oku fakataipe 'e he hinehina 'a e faka'ei'eiki 'o e Fakamaau Totonu 'i he ermine 'o e Fakamaau, pea tokoni ki he tu'unga faka'aho 'o e ngaahi tu'i mo e ngaahi kuini 'oku toho 'e he fanga hoosi hinehina hu'akau; neongo na‘a mo e ngaahi misiteli mā‘olunga ange ‘o e ngaahi lotu faka‘ei‘eiki tahá kuo ‘ai ia ko e faka‘ilonga ‘o e ta‘emele mo e mālohi faka‘otuá; ‘e he kau lotu afi Pēsiá, ko e ulo hinehina ‘oku mafola ‘oku pukepuke ia ‘o mā‘oni‘oni taha ‘i he ‘ōlitá; pea ‘i he ngaahi talatupu‘a faka-Kalisí, ko Siove Lahi tonu na‘e fakakakano‘i ia ‘i ha pulu hinehina sinou; pea neongo ki he kau ʻIlokou fakaʻeiʻeikí, ko e feilaulau ʻi he vaeuaʻanga ʻo e faʻahitaʻu momokó ʻo e Kuli Hinehina toputapú ko e kātoanga māʻoniʻoni taha ia ʻo ʻenau teolosiá, ʻi hono tauhi ʻo e meʻamoʻui taʻemele mo faivelenga ko iá ko e talafekau maʻa taha te nau lava ʻo fekauʻi atu ki he Laumālie Lahí mo e ongoongo fakataʻu ʻo ʻenau faitōnunga pē ʻanautolú; pea neongo ‘oku hangatonu mei he fo‘i lea faka-Latina ki he hinehina, ‘oku ma‘u ‘e he kau pātele Kalisitiane kotoa pē ‘a e hingoa ‘o e konga ‘e taha ‘o honau kofu toputapú, ko e alb pe tunic, ‘oku tui ‘i lalo ‘i he cassock; pea neongo ‘oku ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e ngaahi faka‘ilonga mā‘oni‘oni ‘o e tui faka-Lomá, ‘oku ngāue‘aki makehe ‘a e hinehina ‘i hono kātoanga‘i ‘o e Mamahi ‘a hotau ‘Eikí; neongo ʻi he Vīsone ʻa Sā Sioné, ʻoku ʻoange ha ngaahi kofu hinehina ki he kau huhuʻí, pea ʻoku tuʻu ʻa e kau mātuʻa ʻe toko uofulu mā faá ʻo kofu hinehina ʻi he ʻao ʻo e taloni hinehina lahi, pea ko e Tokotaha Māʻoniʻoni ʻoku ʻafio ʻi aí ʻoku hinehina ʻo hangē ha fulufuluʻisipí; ka neongo ʻa e ngaahi feohiʻanga kotoa ko ʻeni kuo tānaki, mo ha meʻa pē ʻoku melie, mo fakaʻapaʻapaʻia, mo fakaʻeiʻeiki, ʻoku kei ʻi ai ha meʻa ʻoku ʻikai ke maʻu ʻi he fakakaukau ʻi loto ʻo e lanu ko ʻení, ʻa ia ʻokú ne taaʻi lahi ange ʻa e manavasiʻi ki he laumālié ʻi he kulokula ko ia ʻoku fakailifia ʻi he totó.

Ko e ʻulungaanga faingataʻa ko ʻení, ʻa ia ʻokú ne fakatupu ʻa e fakakaukau ʻo e hinehina, ʻi he taimi ʻoku vete ai mei he ngaahi feohi angaʻofa angé, pea fakatahaʻi mo ha meʻa pē ʻoku fakalilifu ʻiate ia pē, ke ne fakalahi ʻa e ilifia ko iá ki he ngaahi ngataʻanga mamaʻo tahá. Fakamo‘oni ki he pea hinehina ‘o e ngaahi poú, pea mo e ‘anga hinehina ‘o e ngaahi feitu‘u fakatalopikí; ko e hā ka ko honau hinehina ngingila mo flaky ʻokú ne ʻai kinautolu ko e ngaahi fakalilifu transcendent ʻoku nau ʻi aí? Ko e hinehina fakalilifu ko ia ʻokú ne ʻomi ha anga-malū fakalielia pehē, ʻo aʻu ʻo fakalielia ange ʻi he fakalilifú, ki he gloating ngutu ʻo honau tafaʻakí. Koe‘uhi ke ‘oua ‘e lava ‘e he taika nifo-fefeka ‘i hono kote faka‘ilonga ‘o fakatupu loto-to‘a ‘o hangē ko e pea pe ‘anga‘anga ‘oku ‘ufi‘ufi hinehina.*

*'I he lave ki he Polar bear, 'e malava ke faka'ai'ai 'e ia 'e fain 'alu 'o kei loloto ange ki he me'a ni, 'oku 'ikai ko e hinehina, kehekehe 'a e vakai ki ai, 'oku ne fakalahi 'a e ta'elavame'a 'o e hideousness 'o e brute ko ia; he, ʻi hono ʻanalaiso, ʻoku tupulaki ʻa e fakalielia ko iá, ʻe lava ke pehē, ʻoku tupu hake pē ia mei he tūkungá, ʻoku tuʻu ʻa e anga-fefeka taʻefatongia ʻo e meʻamoʻuí ʻo ʻinivesi ʻi he fulufuluʻisipi ʻo e taʻehalaia mo e ʻofa fakasilesitialé; pea ko ia ai, ‘i hono ‘omai fakataha ‘a e ongo ongo‘i fehangahangai pehē ‘i hotau ‘atamaí, ‘oku fakailifia‘i ai kitautolu ‘e he pea Polar ‘aki ha fu‘u kehekehe ta‘efakanatula. Ka naʻa mo hono fakakaukauʻi ʻoku moʻoni kotoa ʻeni; ka, kapau na‘e ‘ikai ko e hinehina, he‘ikai te ke ma‘u ‘a e ilifia fakautuutu ko iá.

Ko e meʻa ki he ʻanga hinehina, ko e ghostliness hinehina gliding ʻo e mālōlō ʻi he meʻamoʻui ko iá, ʻi he taimi ʻoku sio ki ai ʻi hono ngaahi ongo angamahení, ʻoku tallies ngali kehe mo e ʻulungaanga tatau ʻi he Polar quadruped. Ko e me‘a makehe ko ení ‘oku lavea lahi taha ia ‘e he kau Falaniseé ‘i he hingoa ‘oku nau ‘oange ki he ika ko iá. Ko e misa faka-Loma maʻá e kau pekiá ʻoku kamata ia ʻaki ʻa e “Requiem eternam” (mālōlō taʻengata), ʻa ia ʻoku fakaʻilongaʻi ai ʻe he Requiem ʻa e misa ʻiate ia pē, mo ha faʻahinga mūsika kehe ʻo e meʻafakaʻeikí. Ko ʻeni, ʻi heʻene lave ki he fakalongolongo hinehina mo fakalongolongo ʻo e maté ʻi he ʻanga ko ʻení, mo e mate anga-malū ʻo ʻene ngaahi tōʻongá, ʻoku ui ia ʻe he kau Falaniseé ko Requin .

Fakakaukau atu ki he ʻalapatosi, ʻoku haʻu mei fē ʻa e ngaahi ʻao ko ia ʻo e ofo fakalaumālie mo e manavahē engeenga, ʻa ia ʻoku folau ai ʻa e ʻata hinehina ko iá ʻi he ngaahi fakakaukau kotoa pē? Naʻe ʻikai ko Coleridge naʻá ne ʻuluaki lī ʻa e sipele ko iá; ka ko e pale lahi mo ta‘efaka‘apa‘apa ‘a e ‘Otuá, ko Natula.*

*'Oku ou manatu'i 'a e fuofua 'alapati na'a ku sio ki ai. Ko e lolotonga ia ‘o ha matangi lōloa, ‘i he ngaahi vai fefeka ‘i he ngaahi tahi ‘o e ‘Anitātiká. Mei he‘eku le‘o ho‘atā ‘i laló, na‘á ku ‘alu hake ai ki he fungavaka ‘ao‘aofiá; pea ʻi ai, ʻi heʻeku lele atu ʻi he ngaahi matapā lalahí, ne u sio ai ki ha meʻa fakatuʻi, fulufulua ʻo e hinehina taʻeʻila, pea mo ha mataʻitofe, fakaʻofoʻofa faka-Loma. ‘I ha ngaahi vaha‘a taimi, na‘á ne faka‘au‘auhifo atu ai hono ngaahi kapakau ‘āngelo-pule lalahí, ‘o hangē ‘okú ne ‘uma ki ha ‘a‘ake mā‘oni‘oní. Tala mai kiate au, ko e hā ʻoku mamaʻo ai ʻa e kiʻi foʻi pulu mālohi ko ʻení, ʻi ha teleʻa melino ʻo Veamoní, mei he fanga manu fekai kotoa pē —ko e hā ʻoku pehē ai ʻi he ʻaho laʻā tahá, kapau te ke lulululu pē ha kofu pafalo foʻou ʻi hono tuʻá, koeʻuhí ke ʻoua naʻá ne lava ʻo sio ki ai, ka ʻokú ne nanamuʻi pē ʻa hono mata ʻo e monumanu kaivaó ʻoku ʻi ai hono mata ʻo e monumanu vaó ʻoku ʻikai ke ne ʻiloʻi, kelekele 'i he phrensies 'o e ilifia? ʻOku ʻikai ha manatu ʻiate ia ki ha ngaahi gorings ʻo e fanga kiʻi meʻamoʻui vao ʻi hono ʻapi lanu mata ʻi he tokelaú, ko ia ʻoku ʻikai lava ʻe he muskiness ngali kehe ʻokú ne nanamuʻí ʻo fakamanatu kiate ia ha meʻa ʻoku fekauʻaki mo e aʻusia ʻo e ngaahi fakatuʻutāmaki kimuʻá; he ko e hā ʻokú ne ʻiloʻi, ʻa e kiʻi pulu Niu ʻIngilani ko ʻení, ki he fanga bisoni ʻuliʻuli ʻo ʻOlikoni mamaʻó?

‘Ikai: ka ‘oku ke sio heni na‘a mo ha manu fekai noa, ki he ‘inasi ‘o e ‘ilo ki he fakatēvolo ‘i māmani. Neongo ʻoku maile ʻe lauiafe mei ʻOlikoni, ka ʻoku kei ʻi ai pē ʻi heʻene nanamuʻi ʻa e musie fekai ko iá, ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e fanga kiʻi manupuna bison ʻoku nau haehae mo ʻohofi ʻo hangē pē ko e foʻi pulu vao liʻaki ʻo e ngaahi vaotaá, ʻa ia ʻi he momeniti ko ʻení ʻoku nau malaki nai ʻo hoko ko e efu.

Ko ia ai, ko e ngaahi takai fakalongolongo ‘o ha tahi hu‘akau; ko e ngaahi longoa‘a fakapo‘uli ‘o e ngaahi ‘uha momoko faka‘ofo‘ofa ‘o e ngaahi mo‘ungá; ko e ngaahi hiki maomaonganoa ‘o e ngaahi sinou ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e matangi ‘o e ngaahi vaotaá; ko e ngaahi me‘á ni kotoa, kia ‘Isime‘eli, ‘oku hangē ia ko e lulululu ‘a e kofu pafalo ko iá ki he ki‘i ‘asi ilifiá!

Neongo ‘oku ‘ikai ‘ilo‘i ‘e ha taha ‘o kinaua ‘a e feitu‘u ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e ngaahi me‘a ta‘ehingoa ‘a ia ‘oku ‘omai ‘e he faka‘ilonga fakamisiteli ‘a e ngaahi fakahinohino peheé; ka ʻiate au, ʻo hangē ko e ʻuhikiʻi ʻasí, kuo pau ke ʻi ai ha feituʻu ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e ngaahi meʻa ko iá. Neongo ‘i hono ngaahi tafa‘aki lahi ‘oku hā ngali na‘e fa‘u ‘a e māmani hā mai ko ení ‘i he ‘ofá, ka na‘e fa‘u ‘a e ngaahi fo‘i ‘ata ta‘ehāmaí ‘i he manavahē.

Ka ʻoku teʻeki ke tau solova ʻa e fakaʻaiʻai ʻo e hinehina ko ʻení, pea ʻiloʻi ʻa e ʻuhinga ʻokú ne fakaʻaiʻai ai ʻaki ha mālohi pehē ki he laumālié; pea ‘oku toe ngali kehe ange mo faka‘ilonga mama‘o ange—ko e hā, ‘o hangē ko ia kuo tau sio ki aí, ‘oku hoko ia ‘i he taimi pē ko iá ko e faka‘ilonga ‘uhinga lahi taha ‘o e ngaahi me‘a fakalaumālié, ‘ikai, ko e veili tonu ia ‘o e Faka‘otua ‘o e Kalisitiané; pea neongo ia ʻoku totonu ke hangē ko iá, ko e meʻa fakatupu ʻi he ngaahi meʻa ʻoku fakalilifu taha ki he faʻahinga ʻo e tangatá.

Ko ia ‘i hono ta‘efakangatangatá ‘okú ne faka‘ata‘atā mai ai ‘a e ngaahi ‘ata‘atā mo e ngaahi me‘a lahi ta‘e‘ofa ‘o e ‘univēsí, pea ‘okú ne hoka‘i ai kitautolu mei mui ‘aki ‘a e fakakaukau ‘o e faka‘auhá, ‘i he‘etau sio ki he loloto hinehina ‘o e hala hu‘akaú? Pe ko ia, ‘o hangē ko ia ‘i hono ‘ulungāngá ‘oku ‘ikai ko ha lanu lahi ‘a e hinehina ‘o hangē ko e ‘ikai ha lanu ‘oku hā maí; pea ‘i he taimi tatau ko e sima ‘o e ngaahi lanu kotoa pē; ko e ngaahi ʻuhinga ko ʻení ʻoku ʻi ai ai ha ʻataʻatā fakalongolongo pehē, fonu ʻi he ʻuhingá, ʻi ha fonua lahi ʻo e sinoú —ko ha taʻelanu, lanu kotoa ʻo e fakaʻikaiʻi ʻOtuá ʻa ia ʻoku tau holomui mei ai? Pea ʻi heʻetau fakakaukau ki he teolia kehe ko ia ʻa e kau filōsefa fakanatulá, ko e ngaahi lanu kehe kotoa pē ʻo e māmaní —ʻa e ngaahi fakaʻofoʻofa fakaʻeiʻeiki pe fakaʻofoʻofa kotoa pē —ko e ngaahi lanu melie ʻo e langi tō ʻa e laʻaá mo e vaotaá; ʻio, mo e ngaahi veliveli koula ʻo e fanga manupuná, mo e ngaahi kouʻahe manupuná ʻo e kau taʻahine kei talavoú; ko e ngaahi meʻa kotoa ko ʻení ko ha ngaahi kākā fakapulipuli pē, ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai moʻoni ʻi he ngaahi meʻá, ka ʻoku fakatoka pē mei tuʻa; ko ia ‘oku valivali faka‘aufuli ‘a e Natula faka‘otua kotoa pē ‘o hangē ko e fe‘auakí, ‘a ia ko ‘ene ngaahi fakatauele ‘oku ‘ikai ke ne ‘ufi‘ufi ha me‘a ka ko e fale-charnel ‘i lotó; pea ʻi heʻetau hoko atu ʻo toe lahi ange, pea fakakaukau ko e meʻa teuteu fakamisiteli ʻokú ne fakatupu ʻa e taha kotoa pē ʻo hono ngaahi lanú, ʻa e tefitoʻi moʻoni lahi ʻo e māmá, ʻoku kei hinehina pe taʻelanu ʻo taʻengata ʻiate ia pē, pea kapau ʻe ngāue taʻe ʻi ai ha meʻa ʻi he meʻá, ʻe lave ki he ngaahi meʻa kotoa pē, naʻa mo e ngaahi tulip mo e ngaahi lose, mo hono lanu ʻataʻatā pē ʻoʻoná ʻi muʻa ʻi he ʻuniveesi ko ʻení ko ha liper kotoa —ponde ʻa e palsiring; pea hangē ko e kau fononga loto-lelei ‘i Lapland, ‘a ia ‘oku nau fakafisi ke tui ‘a e ngaahi sio‘ata lanu mata mo lanu mata ‘i honau matá, ‘oku pehē pē ‘a e sio fakakuihi ‘a e tokotaha ta‘etui faka‘ofa‘iá kiate ia tonu ki he kofukofu hinehina fakamanatu ‘okú ne takai‘i ‘a e ‘amanaki kotoa pē ‘oku takatakai‘i iá. Pea ‘i he ngaahi me‘á ni kotoa ko e tafola ‘Alipinó ‘a e faka‘ilonga. ‌ʻOku mou ofo leva ʻi he tulimanu vela?
