‘Uluakí: Ko e tu‘unga ta‘epau, ta‘efakafiemālie ‘o e saienisi ko eni ‘o e Cetology ‘oku ‘i he vestibule tonu ia ‘oku fakamo‘oni‘i ‘e he mo‘oni‘i me‘á, ‘i he ngaahi feitu‘u ‘e ni‘ihi ‘oku kei hoko pē ia ko ha poini moot pe ko ha tafola ko ha ika. ‘I he‘ene System of Nature, AD 1776, ‘oku fakahā ai ‘e Linnæus, “‘Oku ou fakamavahe‘i heni ‘a e fanga tafola mei he iká.” Ka ʻi heʻeku ʻilo pē ʻaʻakú, ʻoku ou ʻiloʻi ʻo aʻu ki he taʻu 1850, naʻe kei maʻu pē ʻa e fanga ʻangá mo e shad, alewives mo e herring, ʻo fakafepakiʻi ʻa e tuʻutuʻuni fakahāhā ʻa Linnæus, ʻoku nau vahevahe ʻa e maʻu ʻo e ngaahi tahi tatau mo e Livaiataní.

Ko hono me‘a fakafaikehekehe‘i faka‘ei‘eikí, ‘a e fin, ‘a ia ‘okú ne ma‘u mei ai hono hingoá, ‘oku fa‘a hoko ia ko ha me‘a ‘oku hā mahino. Ko e fin ko ení ‘oku fute nai ‘e tolu pe fā hono lōloá, ‘oku tupu fakahangatonu mei he konga ki mui ‘o e tu‘á, ‘oku fōtunga fakatuliki, pea ‘oku māsila ‘aupito hono ngata‘angá. Neongo kapau ‘oku ‘ikai ke hā mai ha ki‘i konga kehe ‘o e me‘amo‘uí, ‘e sio mahino ‘a e ‘uhiki‘i manupuna mavahe ko ení, ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi, ‘oku ‘asi mai mei he fukahi tahí. ‘I he taimi ‘oku fakafuofua‘i ai ‘a e nonga ‘a e tahí, pea ki‘i faka‘ilonga‘i ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi peau fakafuopotopoto, pea tu‘u hake ‘a e fin hangē ha gnomon ko ení ‘o ne ‘ai ‘a e ngaahi ‘ata ki he funga ‘oku ngingilá, ‘e lava ke fakakaukau lelei ‘oku ki‘i hangē ‘a e siakale vai ‘oku takatakai‘i iá ko ha fo‘i taila, ‘a hono sitailá mo e ngaahi laine houa ‘oku peau‘i. ‘I he taimi ko ia ‘o ‘Āhasí ‘oku fa‘a foki ai ‘a e ‘ataá ki mui. Ko e Fin-Back 'oku 'ikai ke fakatahataha. ‘Oku hā ngali ko ha tokotaha fehi‘a tafola ia, ‘o hangē ko e kau tangata ‘e ni‘ihi ‘oku nau fehi‘a tangata. Mā ʻaupito; ʻalu tokotaha maʻu pē; ‘oku ‘alu hake ta‘e‘amanekina ki he fukahi tahí ‘i he ngaahi vai mama‘o mo fakapo‘uli tahá; ko ʻene jet māʻolunga hangatonu mo ʻe taha ʻoku ʻalu hake ʻo hangē ha tao misanthropic māʻolunga ʻi ha teleʻa taʻeʻaonga; ‘oku me‘a‘ofa‘aki ‘a e mālohi mo e vave fakaofo ‘i he luelue, ‘o ne fakafepaki‘i ‘a e tuli kotoa pē ‘oku lolotonga ‘i aí mei he tangatá; ko e levaiatani ko ení ‘oku hā ngali ko e Keini kuo kapusi mo ta‘efa‘a-ikuna‘i ‘o hono matakalí, ‘okú ne ‘ave ko hono faka‘ilonga ‘a e sitaila ko iá ‘i hono tu‘á. Mei he ma'u 'o e baleen 'i hono ngutu, 'Oku fakakau 'a e Fin-Back 'i he taimi 'e ni'ihi mo e tafola totonu, 'i he lotolotonga 'o ha fa'ahinga fakateolosia 'oku ui ko e Whalebone whales , 'a ia, ko e tafola mo e baleen. ‘I he ngaahi tafola ko eni ‘oku ui ko e Whalebone, ‘e hā ngali ‘oku ‘i ai ha ngaahi fa‘ahinga kehekehe, ka ko e lahi taha ‘o kinautolú, ‘oku si‘i ‘a e ‘ilo ki aí. Ko e tafola ihu-laulahi mo e tafola ngutu; ko e fanga tafola ‘ulu-paike; ngaahi tafola fakatahataha; ko e tafola under-jawed mo e tafola rostrated, ko e hingoa ia ‘o e kau toutaí ki ha ngaahi fa‘ahinga si‘isi‘i.

‘I he fekau‘aki mo e appellative ko eni ‘o e “Whalebone whales,” ‘oku mahu‘inga lahi ke lave ki ai, neongo ‘e lava ke faingofua ‘a e fa‘ahinga hingoa peheé ‘i hono fakafaingofua‘i ‘a e ngaahi lave ki ha fa‘ahinga tafola, ka ‘oku ta‘e‘aonga ke feinga ki ha fakakalasi mahino ‘o e Livaiataní, ‘o makatu‘unga ‘i hono fin baleen, pe hump; neongo ko e ngaahi konga pe ngaahi 'ulungaanga ko ia kuo faka'ilonga'i 'oku mahino 'aupito 'oku ngali lelei ange 'a e fe'unga ke ne lava 'o ma'u 'a e makatu'unga ki ha founga angamaheni 'o e Cetology 'i ha toe ngaahi fakafaikehekehe'i fakasino kehe 'oku mavahe, 'a ia 'oku 'omi 'e he tafola, 'i hono fa'ahinga. Fēfē leva? Ko e baleen, hump, mui-fin, mo e nifo; ko e ngaahi me‘a eni ‘oku movetevete ta‘efakafaikehekehe‘i ‘a hono ngaahi me‘a makehé ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e ngaahi fa‘ahinga tafola kotoa pē, ‘o ‘ikai ha tokanga ki he me‘a nai ‘e hoko ki he natula ‘o honau fa‘unga ‘i he ngaahi me‘a kehe mo mahu‘inga angé. Ko ia ai, ko e tafola sperm mo e tafola humpbacked, ‘oku taki taha ‘i ai ‘ene fo‘i ‘uhiki; ka ʻoku ngata ai ʻa e fakatataá. Pea, ko e tafola humpbacked tatau ko ení mo e tafola Greenland, ‘oku taki taha ‘e he ongo me‘á ni ‘a e baleen; ka ʻoku toe ngata ai ʻa e fakatataá. Pea 'oku tatau pe ia mo e ngaahi konga kehe kuo lave ki ai 'i 'olunga. ‘I he ngaahi fa‘ahinga kehekehe ‘o e fanga tafola, ‘oku nau fa‘u ai ‘a e ngaahi fakataha‘anga ta‘efakalao pehē; pe, ʻi he tuʻunga ʻo ha taha ʻo kinautolu ʻoku mavahe, ko ha fakamavaheʻi taʻefakalao pehē; ‘o hangē ko e fakafepaki‘i faka‘aufuli ‘a e ngaahi founga fakalūkufua kotoa pē ‘oku fa‘u ‘i ha makatu‘unga pehē. ‘I he maka ko ení kuo mavahevahe ai ‘a e tokotaha kotoa pē ‘o e kau tafola-natulasí.

Ka ‘e lava ke fakakaukau‘i, ‘i he ngaahi konga ‘i loto ‘o e tafola, ‘i hono ‘anatomí—‘i ai, ‘i he si‘isi‘i tahá, te tau lava ai ‘o taa‘i ‘a e fakakalakalasi totonú. ʻIkai; ko e hā ha me‘a, hangē ko ‘ení, ‘oku ‘i he ‘anatomi ‘o e tafola Kalinilaní ‘oku fakaofo ange ia ‘i he‘ene baleen? Ka kuo tau sio ‘i he‘ene baleen ‘oku ‘ikai malava ke fakakalasi totonu ‘a e tafola Greenland. Pea kapau te ke hifo ki he ngaahi kete 'o e ngaahi leviatan kehekehe, ko e ha 'oku 'ikai te ke ma'u ai 'a e ngaahi fakafaikehekehe ha konga hono nimangofulu 'o hange ko ia 'oku ma'u 'e he systematizer 'o hange ko e ngaahi me'a ko ia 'i tu'a kuo 'osi lisi. Ko e hā leva ‘oku toé? ‘ikai ha me‘a ka ko hono puke fakasino ‘a e fanga tafola, ‘i honau voliume tau‘atāina kotoa, pea fakahokohoko loto-to‘a kinautolu ‘i he founga ko iá. Pea ko e founga Faka-Bibliographical ‘eni ‘oku ngāue‘aki heni; pea ko e taha pē ia ‘e lava ke malava ke lavame‘á, he ko ia pē ‘oku malava ke fakahoko. Ke hoko atu.

TOHI I. ( Folio ) VAHE IV. ( Hump Back ).—Ko e tafola ko ení ‘oku fa‘a sio ki ai ‘i he matāfanga fakatokelau ‘o ‘Ameliká. Kuo toutou puke ia 'i ai, pea toho ki he taulanga. ‘Oku ‘i ai ‘ene pa‘ake lahi ‘iate ia ‘o hangē ha tokotaha fakataú; pe te ke ui nai ia ko e tafola ʻElefānite mo e Kasele. Ko e hā pē ha tu‘unga, ko e hingoa manakoa kiate iá ‘oku ‘ikai ke ne fakafaikehekehe‘i fe‘unga ia, koe‘uhi ‘oku toe ‘i ai ‘a e fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i manu ‘o e sperm whale neongo ko ha fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i manu si‘isi‘i ange. ʻOku ʻikai fuʻu mahuʻinga ʻene lolo. ʻOku ʻi ai ʻene baleni. Ko ia 'a e gamesome mo e ma'ama'a taha 'o e ngaahi tafola kotoa pe, 'o ne ngaohi 'a e foam gay lahi ange mo e vai hinehina fakalukufua 'i ha taha kehe 'o kinautolu.

TOHI I. ( Folio ), VAHE V. ( Razor Back ).—Ko e tafola ko eni 'oku si'isi'i 'a e me'a 'oku 'ilo ki ai ka ko hono hingoa pe. Kuó u sio kiate ia ʻi ha mamaʻo mei Keipi Honi. Ko e natula mālōlō, ‘okú ne kalo mei he kau tulimanu mo e kau filōsefa fakatou‘osi. Neongo ‘oku ‘ikai ko ha tokotaha ilifia, ‘oku te‘eki ai ke ne fakahaa‘i ha konga ‘o ia ka ko hono tu‘á pē, ‘a ia ‘oku ‘alu hake ‘i ha ‘otu māsila lōloa. Tuku ke ne ‘alu. ʻOku siʻisiʻi ange ʻeku ʻilo kiate iá, pea ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻilo ia ʻe ha taha kehe.

TOHI I. ( Folio ), VAHE VI. ( Sulphur Bottom ).—Ko ha tangata‘eiki mālōlō ‘e taha, mo ha kete sūlifa, ‘oku ‘ikai ha veiveiua na‘á ne ma‘u ia ‘aki ‘ene to‘oto‘o ‘i he ngaahi taila ‘o Tātaá ‘i he ni‘ihi ‘o ‘ene ngaahi luelue loloto angé. ʻOku ʻikai faʻa sio kiate ia; ko e siʻisiʻi tahá kuo teʻeki ai ke u sio kiate ia tukukehe pē ʻi he ngaahi tahi fakatonga mamaʻo angé, pea ʻi he taimi ko iá ʻoku fuʻu mamaʻo maʻu pē ke ako hono fofongá. ‘Oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ke tuli ia; te ne hola mo e ngaahi maea-ʻeveʻeva ʻo e lainé. ʻOku talanoa ki he ngaahi meʻa fakaofó fekauʻaki mo ia. Adieu, Lalo Sulifa! ʻOku ʻikai te u lava ʻo lea ʻaki ha meʻa lahi ange ʻoku moʻoni kiate kimoutolu, pea ʻoku ʻikai foki ke lava ʻe he tokotaha Nantucketer motuʻa tahá.

Ko ia 'oku ngata ai 'a e TOHI I. ( Folio ), pea 'oku kamata leva 'eni 'a e TOHI II. ( ʻOkatavo ).

OCTAVOES.*—Ko e ngaahi me'a ni 'oku nau 'uma ki he ngaahi tafola 'oku lolotonga lahi, 'a ia 'oku lava ke lau 'i he lotolotonga 'o e fanga tafola 'oku 'i ai:—I., ko e Grampus ; II., ko e Ika 'Uli'uli ; III., ko e Na'a ; IV., ko e Tala ; V., ko e Fakapō .

*Ko e ha 'oku 'ikai ke fakahingoa ai 'a e tohi ko 'eni 'o e fanga tafola ko e Quarto 'oku fu'u mahino 'aupito. He, lolotonga ko e ngaahi tafola ‘o e tu‘utu‘uni ko ení, neongo ‘oku nau si‘isi‘i ange ‘i he ngaahi tafola ‘o e tu‘utu‘uni ki mu‘á, ka ‘oku nau kei ma‘u pē ha faitatau fe‘ungamālie kiate kinautolu ‘i he fakatātaá, ka ‘oku ‘ikai ke tauhi ‘e he voliume Quarto ‘a e tokotaha-tohí ‘i hono tu‘unga fakafuofua‘í ‘a e fōtunga ‘o e voliume Folio, ka ‘oku tauhi ia ‘e he voliume Octavo.

TOHI II. ( Octavo ), VAHE I. ( Grampus ).—Neongo ko e ika ko ení, ‘a ia ko ‘ene mānava le‘o-lahí, pe ‘oku lelei ange ke pehē, kuó ne ‘omai ha palōveepi ki he kau tangata fonuá, ‘oku ‘iloa lahi ia ko ha tokotaha nofo ‘i he lolotó, ka ‘oku ‘ikai ke fakakalasi ia ‘e he kakaí ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e fanga tafola. Ka ‘i he‘ene ma‘u ‘a e ngaahi ‘ulungaanga makehe faka‘ofo‘ofa kotoa ‘o e leviatané, ko e tokolahi taha ‘o e kau mataotao ‘i he natulá kuo nau ‘ilo‘i ia ‘i ha taha. Ko hono lahi fakafuofua ʻo e ʻokatavo, ʻoku kehekehe mei he fute ʻe hongofulu mā nima ki he fute ʻe uofulu mā nima hono lōloá, pea ʻoku feʻunga hono ngaahi tafaʻakí ʻo takai ʻi he kongalotó. ‘Okú ne luelue ‘i he ngaahi tākanga; 'oku 'ikai 'aupito ke tuli ma'u pe ia, neongo 'oku lahi 'ene lolo 'i he lahi, pea 'oku 'aonga 'aupito ki he maama. ‘Oku lau ‘e he kau toutai ‘e ni‘ihi ‘a ‘ene foungá ko ha faka‘ilonga ia ‘o e laka ki mu‘a ‘a e tafola lahi ko e sperm whale.

TOHI II. ( ʻOkatavo ), VAHE II. ( Ika ‘Uli‘uli ).—‘Oku ou ‘oatu ‘a e ngaahi hingoa ‘o e kau toutai manakoá ki he ngaahi ika kotoa ko ení, he ‘i he tu‘unga fakalūkufuá ko e lelei tahá ia. Ko e feituʻu ʻe hoko ai ha hingoa ʻoku ʻikai mahino pe ʻikai fakahaaʻi, te u lea ʻaki ia, pea fokotuʻu atu ha hingoa kehe. ʻOku ou fai ia he taimí ni, ʻo u ala ki he Ika ʻUliʻulí, ʻoku ui, koeʻuhí he ko e ʻuliʻulí ʻa e tuʻutuʻuni ʻi he meimei fanga tafola kotoa pē. Ko ia, ui ia ko e Hyena Whale, kapau te ke loto ki ai. ‘Oku ‘iloa ‘ene voracity, pea mei he tu‘unga ‘oku pikopiko ai ‘a e ngaahi tuliki ‘i loto ‘o hono loungutú ki ‘olunga, ‘okú ne ‘ave ai ha malimali ta‘engata ‘a Mephistophelean ‘i hono fofongá. Ko e tafola ko ení ‘oku ‘avalisi ki he fute ‘e hongofulu mā ono pe hongofulu mā valu nai hono lōloá. ʻOku maʻu ia ʻi he meimei latitūte kotoa pē. ‘Oku ‘i ai ‘ene founga makehe ‘o hono fakahaa‘i ‘a hono dorsal hooked fin ‘i he luelue, ‘a ia ‘oku hā ngali ko ha me‘a ‘oku hangē ha ihu Lomá. ‘I he taimi ‘oku ‘ikai ke nau ngāue‘aki ‘aonga angé ai, ‘oku puke ‘e he kau tulimanu tafola sperm ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi ‘a e tafola Hyena, ke tauhi ‘a e tokonaki ‘o e lolo ma‘ama‘a ki he ngāue ‘i ‘apí—‘o hangē ko ia ‘oku tutu ‘e he kau tauhi ‘api fakapotopoto ‘e ni‘ihi, ‘i he ‘ikai ha kautaha, pea ‘oku nau ‘iate kinautolu pē, ‘a e ngako ta‘efaka‘ofo‘ofa kae ‘ikai ko e fulufulu‘i‘akau namu-kovi. Neongo ʻoku fuʻu manifinifi ʻenau blubber, ka ʻe ʻoatu ʻe he niʻihi ʻo e fanga tafola ko ʻení ha lolo ʻo laka hake ʻi he kāloni ʻe tolungofulu.

TOHI II. ( ʻOkatavo ), VAHE III. ( Narwhale ), ‘a ia, ko e Nostril whale .—Ko ha toe fakatātā ‘e taha ‘o ha tafola na‘e fakahingoa fie‘ilo, ‘oku ou pehē na‘e fakahingoa ia mei hono nifo makehé ‘a ia na‘e ‘uluaki fakahala‘i ko ha ihu ‘oku tumutumu. Ko e meʻamoʻuí ʻoku fute ʻe hongofulu mā ono nai hono lōloá, ka ko hono meʻatuí ʻoku ʻavalisi ki he fute ʻe nima, neongo ʻoku laka hake ʻi he hongofulu ʻa e niʻihi, pea ʻoku aʻu ʻo aʻu ki he fute ʻe hongofulu mā nima. ‘I hono fakalea fakamātoato, ko e nifo ko ení ko ha nifo pē ia kuo fakalōloa, ‘o tupu mei he kavá ‘i ha laine ‘oku ki‘i faka‘au‘auhifo mei he fakahosite. Ka ‘oku ma‘u pē ia ‘i he tafa‘aki fakatupu maumaú, ‘a ia ‘oku ‘i ai hono ola kovi, ‘o ‘oange ai ki he tokotaha ‘oku ‘a‘aná ha me‘a ‘oku tatau mo e tafa‘aki ‘o ha tangata nima to‘ohema fakapikopiko. Ko e hā ‘a e taumu‘a pau ‘oku tali ‘e he nifo pe tao ‘aivoli ko ení, ‘e faingata‘a ke tala. ‘Oku ‘ikai ke hā ngali ‘oku ngāue‘aki ia ‘o hangē ko e mata ‘o e ika-heletaá mo e ika-pilí; neongo 'oku talamai 'e he kau folau 'e ni'ihi kiate au 'oku ngaue'aki ia 'e he Narwhale ko ha rake 'i hono liliu 'o e lalo tahi ke ma'u ha me'akai. Naʻe pehē ʻe Sālesi Kofini naʻe fakaʻaongaʻi ia ki ha meʻa fakaʻauha ʻaisi; he ko e Narwhale, 'i he'ene 'alu hake ki he funga 'o e Tahi Polar, pea 'ilo kuo 'osi 'aisi, 'oku ne teke hake hono nifo, pea ko ia 'oku ne motuhi. Ka he‘ikai lava ke ke fakamo‘oni‘i ‘oku tonu ha taha ‘o e ongo fakafuofua ko ení. Ko ʻeku fakakaukau pē ʻaʻakú, neongo pe ko e hā hono fakaʻaongaʻi moʻoni ʻe he Narwhale ʻa e nifo tafaʻaki taha ko ʻení —neongo pe ko e hā hono meʻa ko iá —ʻe pau pē ʻe fuʻu faingofua kiate ia ki ha folder ʻi hono lau ʻo e ngaahi tohi iiki. Ko e Narwhale kuó u fanongo ki ai ʻoku ui ko e tafola Tusked, ko e tafola Horned, mo e tafola Unicorn. Ko e moʻoni ko ha sīpinga fie ʻilo ia ʻo e Unicornism ke maʻu ʻi he meimei puleʻanga kotoa pē ʻo e natula moʻuí. Mei he kau faʻu tohi motuʻa cloistered pau kuó u tānaki mai ko e nifo ʻo e sea-unicorn tatau ko ʻení naʻe lau ia ʻi he ngaahi ʻaho ʻo e kuonga muʻá ko e faitoʻo lahi ia ki he koná, pea ʻi heʻene peheé, naʻe ʻomi ʻe he ngaahi teuteu ki aí ha ngaahi totongi lahi ʻaupito. Na‘e toe distilled ia ki ha ngaahi māsima ‘oku ‘ikai ke tu‘u ma‘u ki he kau fefine ‘oku nau tō, ‘i he founga tatau ‘oku ngaohi ai ‘a e ngaahi nifo ‘o e ‘asi tangatá ke hoko ko e hartshorn. ‘I he ‘uluaki taimí na‘e lau ia ‘iate ia pē ko ha me‘a ‘o e fie‘ilo lahi. ʻOku talamai ʻe he Tohi ʻUliʻulí kiate au ko Sir Martin Frobisher ʻi heʻene foki mai mei he folau ko iá, ʻi he taimi naʻe lulululu loto-toʻa ai ʻe Kuini Pesi hono nima siuelí kiate ia mei ha matapā sioʻata ʻo e Palasi Greenwich, ʻi he folau hifo ʻene vaka loto-toʻá ʻi he Thames; “‘i he foki mai ‘a Sir Martin mei he folau ko iá,” ko e lau ia ‘a Black Letter, “na‘á ne punou ‘o ne ‘oatu ki he‘ene ‘afio‘angá ha nifo lōloa fakaofo ‘o e Narwhale, ‘a ia ‘i ha vaha‘a taimi lōloa hili iá na‘e tautau ia ‘i he fale fakatu‘i ‘i Windsor.” ‘Oku pehē ‘e ha tokotaha-tohi ‘Ailani ko e ‘Eiki ‘o Leisitā, ‘i he‘ene punou, na‘á ne ‘oatu foki ki hono mā‘olungá ha nifo ‘e taha, ‘o fekau‘aki mo ha manu fekai ‘o e fonua ‘o e natula ‘unikoni.

Ko e Narwhale 'oku 'i ai 'ene sio faka'ofo'ofa 'aupito, hange ha leopaati, 'oku lanu kelekele hinehina-hu'akau, 'oku toti 'aki 'a e ngaahi fo'i 'uli'uli takai mo fakaloloa. Ko ‘ene lolo ‘oku mā‘olunga ‘aupito, mahino mo lelei; ka ʻoku siʻisiʻi ʻene ʻi aí, pea ʻoku ʻikai faʻa tuli ia. ʻOku lahi taha hono maʻu ʻi he ngaahi tahi circumpolar.

TOHI II. ( ʻOkatavo ), VAHE IV. ( Killer ).—Ko e tafola ko ení ‘oku si‘isi‘i ‘a e me‘a ‘oku ‘ilo‘i pau ‘e he kau Nantucketer, pea ‘oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ha me‘a ‘e he tokotaha ‘oku pehē ko e mataotao ‘i he natulá. Mei he meʻa kuó u sio ki ai ʻi he mamaʻó, ʻoku totonu ke u pehē naʻá ne meimei tatau mo e lahi ʻo ha kalama. ʻOkú ne fuʻu anga-fakamamahi ʻaupito —ko ha faʻahinga ika Feegee. 'Oku ne puke 'i he taimi 'e ni'ihi 'a e fanga tafola lalahi Folio 'i he ngutu, 'o tautau ai 'o hange ha 'uhiki'i manupuna, kae 'oua kuo hoha'a 'a e manu fekai malohi 'o mate. 'Oku 'ikai 'aupito ke tuli 'a e Fakapo. Naʻe ʻikai ke u teitei fanongo pe ko e hā e faʻahinga lolo ʻokú ne maʻú. ‘E lava ke tukukehe‘i ‘a e hingoa na‘e ‘oange ki he tafola ko ení, ‘i he makatu‘unga ‘i he‘ene ta‘emahinó. He ko e kau fakapō kotoa kitautolu, ‘i ‘uta mo tahi; Bonapartes mo e Sharks kau ai.

TOHI II. ( Octavo ), VAHE V. ( Thrasher ).—Ko e tangata‘eikí ni ‘oku ‘iloa ia ‘i hono hiku, ‘a ia ‘okú ne ngāue‘aki ko ha ferule ‘i hono taa‘i hono ngaahi filí. ‘Okú ne heka ‘i he tu‘a ‘o e tafola Folio, pea ‘i he‘ene luelué, ‘okú ne ngāue‘i ‘ene halá ‘aki ‘ene taa‘i ia; hangē ko ia ‘oku feohi ‘a e kau faiako ‘e ni‘ihi ‘i he māmaní ‘aki ha founga meimei tatau. ʻOku kei siʻisiʻi ange ʻa e meʻa ʻoku ʻilo ki he Thrasher ʻi he Killer. Ko e ongo outlaw fakatou‘osi, na‘a mo e ‘i he ngaahi tahi ta‘efakalaó.

Ko ia ‘oku ngata ai ‘a e TOHI II. ( Octavo ), pea kamata 'aki 'a e TOHI III. ( Tootesima ).

KO E NGAAHI DUODECIMOS.—‘Oku kau heni ‘a e fanga tafola iiki angé. I. Ko e Popi Huzza. II. Ko e Popi ʻAlisiá. III. Ko e Popisi ngutu-Mealy.

Kiate kinautolu kuo te‘eki ke nau faingamālie makehe ke ako ‘a e kaveingá, ‘e ngali kehe nai, ‘a e totonu ke fakatahataha‘i ‘a e fanga ika ‘oku ‘ikai ke fa‘a laka hake ‘i he fute ‘e fā pe nima ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e NGAAHI TALOFÁ—ko ha fo‘i lea, ‘a ia, ‘i he ‘uhinga manakoá, ‘okú ne ‘omai ma‘u pē ha fakakaukau ‘o e lahi faú. Ka ko e ngaahi meʻamoʻui ʻoku fokotuʻu ʻi ʻolunga ko e Duodecimoes ko e fanga tafola taʻehalaia, ʻi he ngaahi tuʻunga ʻo ʻeku fakaʻuhinga ki he meʻa ko e tafola— ʻa ia ko ha ika ʻoku ʻuʻulu, mo ha hiku fakahosite.

KO E TOHI III. ( Duodecimo ), VAHE 1. ( Huzza Porpose ).—Ko e poa anga-maheni eni ‘oku ma‘u ‘i he meimei kotoa ‘o e kolopé. Ko e hingoá ko ʻeku foaki pē ʻaʻakú; he ‘oku lahi ange ‘i he fa‘ahinga ‘e taha ‘o e fanga popoá, pea kuo pau ke fai ha me‘a ke fakafaikehekehe‘i ai kinautolu. ʻOku ou ui pehē ia, koeʻuhí he ʻokú ne luelue maʻu pē ʻi ha ngaahi ʻotu fakakata, ʻa ia ʻi he tahi lahí ʻoku nau kei lī pē kinautolu ki he langí ʻo hangē ha ngaahi pulou ʻi ha fuʻu kakai ʻo e ʻaho Fā ʻo Siulaí. Ko honau fōtungá ‘oku fakahīkihiki‘i fakalūkufua ia ‘i he fiefia ‘e he tokotaha toutaí. ‘I he‘enau fonu ‘i he ngaahi laumālie leleí, ‘oku nau ha‘u ma‘u pē mei he ngaahi ‘au‘au ‘o e matangí ki he tafa‘aki ‘o e matangí. Ko e kau tama kinautolu 'oku nau mo'ui ma'u pe 'i mu'a 'i he matangi. ‘Oku lau kinautolu ko ha faka‘ilonga monū‘ia. Kapau te ke lava ʻe koe tonu ʻo matuʻuaki ha ngaahi fiefia ʻe tolu ʻi hoʻo sio ki he fanga ika moʻui ko ʻení, pea ʻe tokoniʻi koe ʻe he langí; ʻoku ʻikai ʻiate kimoutolu ʻae laumālie ʻoe vaʻinga fakaʻotua. Ko ha Huzza Porpoise ʻoku fafangaʻi lelei mo fuopotopoto te ne ʻoatu kiate koe ha kāloni lelei ʻe taha ʻo e lolo leleí. Ka ko e vai lelei mo ma‘ama‘a ‘oku to‘o mei hono ngaahi kavá ‘oku fu‘u mahu‘inga ‘aupito. ‘Oku kole ia ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e kau ngaohi siuelí mo e kau ngaohi uati. Naʻe ʻai ia ʻe he kau toutaí ʻi honau hones. Ko e kakano'i manu 'o e Porpoise 'oku kai lelei, 'oku ke 'ilo. Mahalo pē na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito ke ha‘u ki ho ‘atamaí ‘oku ‘u‘ulu ha fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i manupuna. Ko e mo‘oni, ‘oku fu‘u si‘isi‘i ‘aupito hono fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i pulú ‘o ‘ikai fu‘u faingofua ke ‘ilo‘i ia. Ka ko e taimi hoko mai te ke maʻu ai ha faingamālié, siofi ia; pea te ke sio leva ki he Sperm whale lahi ko iá tonu ‘i he fakatātā si‘isi‘í.

KO E TOHI III. ( Tuotesima ), VAHE II. ( Ko e Popoisi ʻAlisiá ).—Ko ha kaihaʻa. Savage 'aupito. ʻOku maʻu pē ia, ʻi heʻeku fakakaukaú, ʻi he Pasifikí. 'Oku ki'i lahi ange ia 'i he Huzza Porpoise, ka ko e lahi taha 'o e make lahi tatau. Faka'ita'i ia, pea te ne buckle ki ha 'anga. Kuó u tuku hifo maʻana ʻi ha ngaahi taimi lahi, ka ʻoku teʻeki ai ke u sio ʻoku puke ia.

KO E TOHI III. ( Tuotesima ), VAHE III. ( Ko e Poposi ngutu-Mea ).—Ko e fa‘ahinga Poposi lahi tahá; pea 'oku toki ma'u pe 'i he Pasifiki, 'o fakatatau ki he me'a 'oku 'ilo'i. Ko e hingoa faka-Pilitānia pē ʻe taha, ʻa ia kuo fakahingoa ʻaki ia ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimí ni, ko e hingoa ʻo e kau toutaí —ko e Right-Whale Porpoise, mei he tūkunga ʻoku tefito ʻene maʻu ʻi he feituʻu ʻo e Folio ko iá. ‘I hono fōtungá, ‘okú ne kehe ‘i ha tu‘unga mei he Huzza Porpoise, ‘i he‘ene si‘i ange ‘a e fuopotopoto mo e fiefia; ko e mo‘oni, ‘okú ne ‘i ha fōtunga mātu‘aki maau mo hangē ha tangata‘eikí. ʻOku ʻikai haʻane ngaahi kafo ʻi hono tuʻá (ko e lahi taha ʻo e fanga porpoise kehé ʻoku ʻi ai), ʻoku ʻi ai hono hiku fakaʻofoʻofa, mo e mata ʻInitia fakaeloto ʻo ha lanu hazel. Ka ko hono ngutu mealy ‘okú ne maumau‘i kotoa. Neongo ko hono tuʻa kotoa ʻo aʻu ki hono ngaahi ʻuhikiʻi tafaʻakí ʻoku ʻi ha sable loloto, ka ko ha laine fakangatangata, ʻoku makehe ʻo hangē ko e fakaʻilonga ʻi he ʻaofinima ʻo ha vaká, ʻoku ui ko e “konga ngingilá,” ʻoku fakaʻilongaʻi ia ʻe he laine ko iá mei he ʻakaú ki he muiʻi vaká, ʻo ʻi ai ha ongo lanu kehekehe, ʻuliʻuli ʻi ʻolunga pea hinehina ʻi lalo. Ko e hinehina 'oku kau ai 'a e konga 'o hono 'ulu, mo e kotoa 'o hono ngutu, 'a ia 'oku ne 'ai ia ke ne hange na'a ne toki hola mei ha 'a'ahi fakamālohi ki ha kato-me'akai. Ko ha tafaʻaki mean mo mealy taha! Ko ‘ene lolo ‘oku meimei tatau ia mo e lolo ‘o e porpoise anga-mahení.

'I he tafa'aki 'o e DUODECIMO, 'oku 'ikai ke hoko atu 'a e sisitemi ko 'eni, 'i he 'uhinga ko e Porpoise 'oku si'isi'i taha 'i he fanga tafola. 'I 'olunga, 'oku ke ma'u kotoa 'a e Livaiatani 'o e fakatokanga. Ka ʻoku ʻi ai ha fuʻu kulupu ʻo e fanga tafola taʻepau, hola, vaeua-fabulous, ʻa ia, ʻi heʻeku hoko ko ha tangata tafola ʻAmelika, ʻoku ou ʻiloʻi ʻi he ongoongo, ka ʻoku ʻikai fakatāutaha. Te u lau kinautolu ʻaki honau ngaahi hingoa ʻo e fore-castle; he mahalo ʻe mahuʻinga nai ha lisi pehē ki he kau fakatotolo ʻi he kahaʻú, ʻa ia te nau lava ʻo fakakakato ʻa e meʻa ʻoku ou maʻu hení ka kuó u kamataʻí. Kapau 'e 'i ai ha taha 'o e fanga tafola ko 'eni, 'e ma'u mo faka'ilonga'i hili 'eni, pea 'e lava leva ke fakakau vave ia ki he Sisitemi ko 'eni, 'o fakatatau ki hono lahi Folio, Octavo, pe Duodecimo:—Ko e Tafola Bottle-Nose; ko e Tafola Junk; ko e Tafola ‘Ulu-Pudingí; ko e Tafola Keipi; ko e Tafola Takimuʻá; ko e Tafola Kanoni; ko e Tafola Scragg; ko e Tafola Kopa; ko e Tafola ʻElefānité; ko e Tafola ʻAisipekí; ko e Tafola Quog; ko e Tafola Puluú; etc. Mei he kau ma‘u mafai ‘Aisilani, Hōlani, mo e kau ‘Ingilani motu‘á, ‘e lava ke lave ki ai ha ngaahi lisi kehe ‘o e fanga tafola ta‘epau, ‘oku tāpuekina ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi hingoa ta‘efaka‘apa‘apa kehekehe. Ka ʻoku ou liʻaki kinautolu ʻo pehē ʻoku ʻikai ke nau ʻaonga ʻaupito; pea ‘oku ‘ikai meimei lava ke tokoni ke mahalo‘i kinautolu ko ha ngaahi le‘o noa‘ia pē, ‘oku fonu ‘i he lotu faka-Livaiataní, ka ‘oku ‘ikai ke nau faka‘ilonga‘i ha me‘a.

Faka‘osí: Na‘e fakahaa‘i ‘i he kamatá, ‘e ‘ikai ke ‘i heni ‘a e fokotu‘utu‘u ko ení, pea ‘i he taimi pē ko iá, ‘e fakahaohaoa‘i. He ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo taʻe sio mahino kuó u tauhi ʻeku leá. Ka ʻoku ou tuku he taimí ni ʻeku Sisitemi fakasetolosiá ke tuʻu pehē ʻo ʻikai ʻosi, ʻo hangē pē ko ia naʻe tuku ʻa e Falelotu Lahi ʻo Koloní, ʻo kei tuʻu pē ʻa e crane ʻi he tumutumu ʻo e taua ʻoku teʻeki ke ʻosi. He ko e fanga ki‘i langa iiki ‘e lava ke ‘osi ia ‘e honau ‘uluaki kau ‘ākitekí; ko e kau maʻongoʻongá, ko e kau moʻoní, ʻoku nau tuku ʻi ha taimi ʻa e maka fakaʻauhá ki he hakó. Ke ta'ofi au 'e he 'Otua mei ha'aku fakakakato ha me'a. Ko e tohi kotoa ko ʻení ko ha fakakaukau pē —ʻikai, ka ko ha fakakaukau ʻo ha fakakaukau. ʻOiauē, Taimi, Mālohi, Paʻanga, mo e Kātaki!
