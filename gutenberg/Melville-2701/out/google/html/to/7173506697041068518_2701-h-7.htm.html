VAHE 33. Ko e kau Sipekisini.

Fekau'aki mo e kau 'ofisa 'o e vaka-tafola, 'oku ngali ko ha feitu'u lelei 'eni 'o hange ko ha toe feitu'u ke fokotu'u ai ha ki'i peculiarity fakalotofonua 'i he vaka-vaka, 'oku tupu mei he 'i ai 'a e kalasi harpooneer 'o e kau 'ofisa, ko ha kalasi 'oku 'ikai 'ilo'i 'o e mo'oni 'i ha toe tautahi kehe mei he whale-fleet.

Ko e mahu'inga lahi 'oku 'oange ki he uiui'i 'o e harpooneer 'oku fakamo'oni'i ia 'e he mo'oni, 'i he kamata'anga 'i he motu'a 'o e Dutch Fishery, 'i he senituli 'e ua mo e lahi ange kuo hili, na'e 'ikai ke lodged kakato 'a e fekau 'o ha vaka tafola 'i he tokotaha 'oku ui he taimi ni ko e kapiteni, ka na'e vahevahe ia 'i hono vaha'a mo ha 'ofisa Specksny 'oku ui ko e Specks. ʻOku ʻuhinga fakafoʻilea ʻa e foʻi leá ni ki he Fat-Cutter; ka ko hono ngāue‘akí, ‘i he faai mai ‘a e taimí na‘e ‘ai ai ia ke tatau mo e Chief Harpooneer. ‘I he ngaahi ‘aho ko iá, na‘e fakangatangata pē ‘a e mafai ‘o e ‘eikitaú ki he folau mo e pule‘i fakalūkufua ‘o e vaká; lolotonga ‘ene pule‘i ‘a e potungāue tulimanu tafola mo ‘ene ngaahi hoha‘a kotoa pē, na‘e pule mā‘olunga taha ‘a e Specksnyder pe Chief Harpooneer. ‘I he Toutai Greenland ‘a Pilitāniá, ‘i he hingoa fakapikopiko ko e Specksioneer, ‘oku kei tauhi pē ‘a e ‘ōfisa motu‘a Hōlani ko ení, ka ‘oku fakanounou‘i fakaloloma hono lāngilangi ki mu‘á. ʻI he lolotongá ni ʻokú ne tuʻunga pē ko e Harpooneer māʻolunga; pea ʻi heʻene peheé, ko e taha pē ia ʻo e kau subalterns maʻulalo ange ʻo e ʻeikitaú. Ka neongo ia, hange ko e 'ulungaanga lelei 'o e kau toutai tafola 'oku makatu'unga lahi 'a e lavame'a 'o ha folau toutai tafola, pea koe'uhi 'i he Toutai 'Amelika 'oku 'ikai ngata pe 'i he'ene hoko ko ha 'ofisa mahu'inga 'i he vaka, ka 'i he ngaahi tu'unga pau (le'o po'uli 'i ha mala'e toutai tafola) ko e fekau 'o e fungavaka 'o e vaka 'oku 'a'ana foki; ko ia ‘oku fiema‘u ai ‘e he maxim fakapolitikale lahi ‘o e tahí, ke ne mo‘ui mavahe fakahingoa mei he kau tangata ‘i mu‘a ‘i he pou vaká, pea ke fakafaikehekehe‘i ‘i ha fa‘ahinga founga ko honau pule fakapalofesinalé; neongo maʻu pē, ʻe kinautolu, ʻoku lau maheni ko honau tatau fakasōsiale.

Ko ʻeni, ko e faikehekehe lahi ʻoku fai ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ʻōfisá mo e tangatá ʻi tahí, ko ʻení —ʻoku moʻui ʻa e ʻuluaki moʻuí ʻi mui, ko e fakaʻosí ʻi muʻa. Ko ia ai, ‘i he ngaahi vaka tafola mo e kau fakatau fakataú, ‘oku ma‘u ‘e he ongo hoá honau ngaahi nofo‘angá mo e ‘eikitaú; pea pehe foki, 'i he lahi taha 'o e kau toutai tafola 'Amelika 'oku nofo 'a e kau harpooneers 'i he konga ki mui 'o e vaka. Ko hono 'uhinga, 'oku nau to'o 'enau me'akai 'i he fale 'o e kapiteni, pea mohe 'i ha feitu'u 'oku nau fetu'utaki ta'efakahangatonu mo ia.

Neongo ko e vaha'ataimi loloa 'o ha folau toutai tafola 'i he Tonga ('oku mama'o 'a e loloa taha 'o e ngaahi folau kotoa pe he taimi ni pe kuo fai 'e he tangata), 'a e ngaahi fakatu'utamaki makehe 'o ia, mo e komiuniti 'o e mahu'inga'ia 'oku ma'u 'i he lotolotonga 'o ha kautaha, 'a ia kotoa pe, ma'olunga pe ma'ulalo, 'oku fakafalala ki he'enau pa'anga, 'ikai 'i he totongi pau, ka 'i he'enau monū'ia angamaheni mo e vigility hatrepidi, fakataha mo, 'enau monū'ia angamaheni; neongo ‘oku hehema ‘a e ngaahi me‘á ni kotoa ‘i he ngaahi tu‘unga ‘e ni‘ihi ke fanau‘i ha akonaki ‘oku si‘i ange ‘ene fefeká ‘i he kau fakatau fakalūkufuá; ka, ‘oua ‘e tokanga ki he lahi ‘o e hangē ha fāmili motu‘a ‘o Mesopotēmiá ‘e lava ke nofo fakataha ‘a e kau tangata tafola ko ení, ‘i he ngaahi tu‘unga motu‘a ‘e ni‘ihi; mo e me'a kotoa pe, 'oku 'ikai ke fa'a fakanonga fakamatelie 'a e punctilious externals, 'i he si'isi'i taha, 'o e kuata-deck, pea 'i ha taimi 'oku 'ikai ke fai 'o 'osi. Ko e mo‘oni, ‘oku lahi ‘a e ngaahi vaka Nantucket ‘a ia te ke sio ai ki he ‘eikivaká ‘i he‘ene faka‘ali‘ali ‘a hono kuata-deck ‘i ha faka‘ofo‘ofa fiefia ‘oku ‘ikai lava ke laka hake ‘i ha kau tautahi fakakautau; ‘ikai, ‘o ne fakamālohi‘i ‘a e meimei lahi ‘o e faka‘apa‘apa ‘i tu‘á ‘o hangē pē na‘á ne tui ‘a e lanu vāleti faka‘emipolá, pea ‘ikai ko e tupenu pailate ‘oku kovi tahá.

Pea neongo ʻi he kakai kotoa pē ko e kapiteni moody ʻo e Pequod naʻe siʻisiʻi taha ʻene ʻoange ki he faʻahinga fakakaukau loloto taha ko iá; pea neongo ko e fakaʻapaʻapa pē ʻe taha naʻá ne fai, ko e talangofua fakapulipuli, ʻi he taimi pē ko iá; neongo naʻe ʻikai ke ne fie maʻu ha tangata ke ne toʻo ʻa e ngaahi sū mei hono vaʻé kimuʻa peá ne laka ʻi he kuata-ʻaá; pea neongo naʻe ʻi ai ha ngaahi taimi, koeʻuhí ko ha ngaahi tūkunga makehe ʻoku fekauʻaki mo e ngaahi meʻa ʻe hoko ʻi he hili ʻení ke fakaikiiki, naʻá ne lea kiate kinautolu ʻi ha ngaahi founga taʻe angamaheni, tatau ai pē pe ko e fakaʻapaʻapa pe ʻi he terrorem , pe ko ha toe founga kehe; ka na‘a mo e ‘Eikitau ko ‘Ēhapí na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito ke ne ta‘etokanga‘i ‘a e ngaahi fōtunga mo e ngaahi ngāue‘aki mahu‘inga taha ‘o e tahí.

Pe, mahalo, ‘e ‘ikai ke faifai pea ‘ilo‘i, ‘i mui ‘i he ngaahi founga mo e ngaahi ngāue‘aki ko iá, ‘o hangē ko ia ‘oku ‘i aí, na‘á ne fakapulipuli‘i ai ia ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi; ‘i he founga fakatu‘upakē ‘o ngāue‘aki kinautolu ki ha ngaahi taumu‘a kehe mo fakafo‘ituitui ange ‘i he me‘a na‘e fakataumu‘a fakalao ke nau tauhi aí. Ko e sultanism pau ko ia ʻo hono ʻutó, ʻa ia naʻe ʻi ha tuʻunga lelei kehe, naʻe kei ʻikai ke fakahaaʻi ia; ‘o fakafou ‘i he ngaahi founga ko iá na‘e hoko ai ‘a e sultanism tatau ‘o fakakakano ‘i ha pule fakatikitato ta‘ealafakafepaki‘i. He ke hoko ko e ma'olunga faka'atamai 'o ha tangata 'a e me'a 'oku ne loto ki ai, 'e 'ikai lava ke ne ma'u 'a e ma'olunga 'aonga, 'oku ma'u 'i he kau tangata kehe, ta'ekau ai 'a e tokoni 'a ha fa'ahinga 'o e ngaahi 'aati 'i tu'a mo e entrenchments, ma'u pe, 'iate kinautolu pe, lahi ange pe si'isi'i ange 'a e paltry mo e base. Ko e meʻa ʻeni, ʻokú ne tauhi ʻo taʻengata ʻa e kau pilinisi moʻoni ʻo e ʻOtuá ʻo e ʻEmipaeá mei he ngaahi hustings ʻa e māmaní; pea 'oku ne tuku 'a e ngaahi faka'apa'apa ma'olunga taha 'e lava ke foaki 'e he 'ea ko 'eni, ki he kau tangata ko ia 'oku nau hoko 'o 'iloa lahi ange 'i he'enau ma'ulalo ta'efakangatangata ki he fili fufuu'i nima 'o e Divine Inert, 'i he'enau ma'olunga ta'eveiveiua 'i he tu'unga mate 'o e fu'u kakai tokolahi. Ko e anga-lelei lahi peheé ‘oku toitoi ia ‘i he fanga ki‘i me‘a iiki ko ení ‘i he taimi ‘oku ‘inivesi ai kinautolu ‘e he ngaahi tui fakapolitikale mātu‘aki tōtu‘á, ‘a ia ‘i he ngaahi tu‘unga fakatu‘i ‘e ni‘ihi na‘a mo e idiot imbecility kuo nau ‘oange ai ‘a e mālohí. Ka ‘i he taimi, ‘o hangē ko ia na‘e hoko kia Nicholas the Czar, ‘oku ‘ātakai‘i ai ‘e he kalauni mama ‘o e ‘emipaea fakasiokālafí ha ‘uto faka‘emipolá; pea, 'oku punou hifo 'a e fanga manu plebeian 'i he 'ao 'o e centralization lahi. Pe, ʻe ʻikai ngalo ʻi he tokotaha faiva fakamamahi ʻa ia te ne fakatātaaʻi ʻa e taʻe-malava ke mapuleʻi fakamatelié ʻi hono tafi kakato mo e taʻalo fakahangatonú, ha fakaʻilonga, ʻoku fuʻu mahuʻinga ʻaupito ʻi heʻene ʻātí, ʻo hangē ko e taha ʻoku lave ki ai he taimí ni.

Ka ʻoku kei ngaʻunu pē ʻa ʻĒhapi, ko ʻeku ʻEikivaká, ʻi hoku ʻaó ʻi heʻene fakaʻofa mo fakaʻofaʻia kotoa ʻi Nantucket; pea ʻi he konga ko ʻeni ʻoku uesia ai ʻa e kau ʻEmipolá mo e kau Tuʻí, ʻoku ʻikai totonu ke u fakapulipuliʻi ʻoku ʻi ai pē ʻeku kaunga ki ha motuʻa tulimanu tafola masiva hangē ko iá; pea, ko ia ai, ʻoku fakaʻikaiʻi au ʻa e ngaahi tauhele mo e ngaahi fale fakaʻeiʻeiki kotoa pē ʻi tuʻá. ʻOiauē ʻEhapi! ko e meʻa ʻe fakaʻofoʻofa ʻiate koé, kuo pau ke toʻo ia mei he langí, pea luelue ʻi he lolotó, pea fakaʻaliʻali ʻi he ʻea taʻesino!

VAHE 35. Koe Ulu-Mast.

Ko e lolotonga ia ʻo e ʻea fakafiefia angé, ʻi heʻeku feʻauhi totonu mo e kau toutai kehé naʻe takai mai ai hoku ʻuluaki ʻulu-poú.

‘I he lahi taha ‘o e kau tafola ‘Ameliká ‘oku meimei taimi tatau pē ‘a e kau ngāue ‘i he ngaahi ‘ulu-pou ‘o e vaká mo e mavahe ‘a e vaká mei hono taulangá; neongo ʻe ʻi ai nai haʻane maile ʻe hongofulu mā nima afe, pea lahi ange, ke ne folau kimuʻa peá ne aʻu ki hono feituʻu folau totonú. Pea kapau, hili ha folau taʻu ʻe tolu, fā, pe nima ʻokú ne ofi mai ki ʻapi mo ha meʻa ʻataʻatā ʻiate ia —tau pehē, ko ha hina ʻataʻatā naʻa mo ia —pea, ʻoku tauhi hono ngaahi ʻulu-poú ke ʻi ai ha tangata ʻo aʻu ki he fakaʻosí; pea ‘oku ‘ikai ke ne tukuange faka‘aufuli ‘a e ‘amanaki ke toe puke ha fo‘i tafola ‘e taha kae ‘oua kuo folau mai ‘ene ngaahi pou skysail ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e ngaahi ‘ā ‘o e taulangá.

Ko ʻeni, ko e pisinisi ʻo e tuʻu mast-heads, ʻi ʻuta pe ʻi he ʻaá, ko ha meʻa motuʻa mo mālie ʻaupito, tau expatiate heni ʻi ha faʻahinga meʻafua. 'Oku ou to'o ia, ko e kau fuofua tu'u 'o e ngaahi 'ulu-pou ko e kau 'Isipite motu'a; he, ʻi heʻeku ngaahi fekumi kotoa pē, ʻoku ʻikai ke u maʻu ha taha kimuʻa ʻiate kinautolu. He neongo ko honau ngaahi kui, ko e kau langa ‘o Pēpelí, ‘oku ‘ikai ha veiveiua, ‘i honau tauá, na‘a nau fakataumu‘a ke langa hake ‘a e ‘ulu-pou mā‘olunga taha ‘i ‘Ēsia kotoa, pe ko ‘Afilika foki; ka (ʻi he teʻeki ke ʻai ʻa e loli fakaʻosí ki ai) ʻo hangē ko ia ʻe lava ke pehē naʻe ʻalu atu ʻenau pou maka lahi ko iá ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e laupapa, ʻi he matangi fakalilifu ʻo e houhau ʻo e ʻOtuá; ko ia ai, he ʻikai ke tau lava ʻo fakamuʻomuʻa ʻa e kau langa ko ʻeni ʻo Pēpelí ʻi he kau ʻIsipité. Pea ko e kau ‘Isipité ko ha pule‘anga ‘o e kau tu‘u ‘ulu-pou, ko ha fakapapau ia ‘oku makatu‘unga ‘i he tui fakalūkufua ‘a e kau ‘ākeolosiá, na‘e fokotu‘u ‘a e ‘uluaki ngaahi pilamití ki ha ngaahi taumu‘a faka‘asitalonomia: ko ha teolia ‘oku poupou‘i fakafo‘ituitui ‘e he fa‘unga makehe ‘o e ngaahi tafa‘aki ‘e fā kotoa ‘o e ngaahi fale ko iá ‘o hangē ha sitepú; ʻa ia, ʻi he ngaahi hiki hake lōloa fakaofo ʻo honau vaʻé, naʻe angamaheni ʻaki ʻe he kau ʻasitalonoma motuʻa ko iá ke nau heka ki he tumutumú, ʻo hivaʻi ha ngaahi fetuʻu foʻou; na‘a mo e hiva ‘a e ngaahi sio‘ata ‘o ha vaka fakaeonopōní ki ha folau, pe ko ha tafola ‘oku ‘asi mai pē. ‘I Saint Stylites, ko e tokotaha nofo faka-Kalisitiane ‘iloa ‘o e kuonga mu‘á, ‘a ia na‘á ne langa ma‘ana ha pou maka mā‘olunga ‘i he toafá pea fakamoleki ‘a e konga fakamuimui kotoa ‘o ‘ene mo‘uí ‘i hono tumutumú, ‘o ne hiki hake ‘ene me‘akaí mei he kelekelé ‘aki ha me‘angāue; ʻoku tau maʻu ʻiate ia ha sīpinga fakaofo ʻo ha tokotaha tuʻu taʻemanavahē ʻo e ngaahi ʻulu-poú; ʻa ia naʻe ʻikai ke kapusi mei hono feituʻú ʻe he kakapu pe ʻuha momoko, ʻuha, ʻuha maka, pe ʻuha momoko; ka ʻi heʻene fehangahangai loto-toʻa mo e meʻa kotoa pē ʻo aʻu ki he fakaʻosí, naʻá ne mate moʻoni ʻi hono lakangá. ‘I he kau tu‘u-‘o-e-‘ulu-pou ‘o onopōní ‘oku tau ma‘u pē ha seti ta‘emo‘ui; ko e kau tangata maka, ukamea, mo e palasa noa pē; ‘a ia, neongo ‘oku nau malava lelei ke fehangahangai mo ha matangi fefeka, ‘oku nau kei ta‘efe‘unga kakato ki he pisinisi ‘o e hiva ‘i he‘enau ‘ilo‘i ha me‘a ngali kehe ‘oku nau sio ki aí. ʻOku ʻi ai ʻa Napoleone; ʻa ia, ʻi he tumutumu ʻo e pou ʻo Vendome, ʻokú ne tuʻu mo fusi hono ongo nimá, ʻo fute ʻe teau nimangofulu nai ʻi he ʻeá; taʻetokanga, he taimí ni, ko hai ʻokú ne puleʻi ʻa e ngaahi fungavaka ʻi laló; pe ko Luisi Filipe, Luisi Palani, pe ko Luisi ko e Tēvoló. Ko Uasingatoni Lahi foki, ʻokú ne tuʻu māʻolunga ʻi ʻolunga ʻi hono pou lahi māʻolunga ʻi Pōlitimoá, pea hangē ko e taha ʻo e ngaahi pou ʻo Hekulesí, ʻoku fakaʻilongaʻi ʻe hono pou ʻa e poini ko ia ʻo e maʻongoʻonga fakaetangatá ʻa ia ʻe tokosiʻi pē ʻa e kakai matelié ʻe ʻalu atu ki aí. Ka he ʻikai ke tali ʻe Uasingatoni lahi, pe Napoleone, pe Nelson, ha ʻuha maka ʻe taha mei lalo, neongo pe ko e hā hono ui fakavalevale ʻe heʻenau ngaahi faleʻí ke nau fakakaumeʻa mo e ngaahi fungavaka fakahohaʻasi ʻoku nau sio fakamamaʻu ki aí; ka neongo ia ʻe lava ke fakafuofuaʻi, ʻoku hū honau laumālié ʻi he ʻaoʻaofia matolu ʻo e kahaʻú, pea fakaangaʻi ʻa e ngaahi ʻotu mo e ngaahi maka kuo pau ke fakaʻehiʻehi mei aí.

Naʻá ne ui ia ko e ʻuhikiʻi manupuna ʻa Sleet , ʻi hono fakaʻapaʻapaʻi ia; ko ia ʻa e ʻuluaki faʻu mo e patente, pea tauʻatāina mei he ngaahi meʻakai loi fakakata kotoa pē, pea ʻokú ne pukepuke kapau te tau ui ʻetau fānaú tonu ʻaki hotau hingoá (ko kitautolu ngaahi tamaí ko e kau ʻuluaki faʻu mo pateni), ʻoku totonu ke pehē pē mo ʻetau fakahingoa ʻiate kitautolu ha toe meʻangāue kehe te tau lava ʻo fanauʻi. ‘I hono fōtungá, ko e ‘uhiki‘i manupuna ‘o e Sleet ko ha me‘a ia ‘oku hangē ha tierce pe paipa lahi; ‘oku fakaava ia ‘i ‘olunga, ka neongo ia, ‘a ia ‘oku fakanaunau‘i ai ha tafa‘aki-screen ‘oku lava ke nga‘unu ke tauhi ki he matangi ‘o ho ‘ulú ‘i ha matangi mālohi. ‘I hono tu‘u ma‘u ‘i he tumutumu ‘o e pou vaká, ‘okú ke ‘alu hake ki ai ‘o fakafou ‘i ha ki‘i tauhele-‘i he laló. ʻI he tafaʻaki ki muí, pe tafaʻaki ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e mui ʻo e vaká, ʻoku ʻi ai ha sea fakafiemālie, mo ha loki ʻi lalo ki he ngaahi fakamalu, ngaahi meʻa fakafiemālie, mo e ngaahi kote. ‘I mu‘á ‘oku ‘i ai ha fata kili‘imanu, ‘a ia ke tauhi ai ho‘o talupite leá, paipá, telesikopí, mo e ngaahi me‘a kehe ‘oku ngāue‘aki ‘i he tahí. ʻI he taimi naʻe tuʻu fakafoʻituitui ai ʻa Kapiteni Sleet ʻi hono ʻulu-pou ʻi he ʻuhikiʻi manupuna ko ʻeni ʻoʻoná, ʻokú ne talamai kiate kitautolu naʻá ne maʻu maʻu pē ha meʻafana mo ia (naʻe toe fakapapauʻi ʻi he ʻaiʻangakai ʻo e manú), fakataha mo ha foʻi hina efuefu mo e fana, ʻi he taumuʻa ke popping off ʻa e fanga narwhales hēhētuʻú, pe vagrant sea unicorns infesting that wa; he he ʻikai ke ke lavameʻa ʻi hono fanaʻi kinautolu mei he funga vaká koeʻuhí ko e fakafepaki ʻa e vaí, ka ko e fanaʻi hifo kiate kinautolú ko ha meʻa kehe ʻaupito ia. Ko ʻeni, naʻe mahino ko ha ngāue ʻofa ia kia Kapiteni Sleet ke ne fakamatalaʻi, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻokú ne faí, ʻa e fanga kiʻi meʻa fakafiemālie fakaikiiki kotoa ʻo ʻene ʻuhikiʻi manupuná; ka neongo ‘okú ne fakalahi pehē ‘a e ngaahi me‘a lahi ‘o e ngaahi me‘á ni, pea neongo ‘okú ne fakafeangai mai kiate kitautolu ha fakamatala fakasaienisi ‘aupito ‘o ‘ene ngaahi ‘ahi‘ahi ‘i he ‘uhiki‘i manupuna ko ení, ‘aki ha ki‘i kāpasa na‘á ne tauhi ai ‘i he taumu‘a ke fakafepaki‘i ‘a e ngaahi fehālaaki ‘oku tupu mei he me‘a ‘oku ui ko e “tohoaki‘i fakalotofonua” ‘o e ngaahi mākiniti binnacle kotoa pē; ko ha fehālaaki ‘oku lava ke faka‘uhinga‘i ki he tafa‘aki fakahosite ‘o e ‘aione ‘i he ngaahi laupapa ‘o e vaká, pea ‘i he tu‘unga ‘o e Glacier, mahalo pē, ki he tokolahi fau ‘a e kau tufunga ukamea kuo maumau‘i ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o ‘ene kau ngāué; ʻOku ou pehē, neongo ʻoku fuʻu fakapotopoto mo fakasaienisi ʻa e ʻEikí heni, ka, ki heʻene ako kotoa ʻo e “ngaahi hē ʻo e binnacle,” “ngaahi vakaiʻi ʻo e kāpasa ʻazimuth,” mo e “ngaahi fehālaaki fakafuofua,” ʻokú ne ʻiloʻi lelei ʻaupito, ʻEikitau Sleet, naʻe ʻikai ke ne fuʻu fakauku ʻi he ngaahi fakalaulauloto loloto ko ia ʻo e fakamakinito replenishely wellidward, ʻo hangē ko e taʻelavameʻa oliccasional be . case-bottle, naʻe fakahū fakaʻofoʻofa ʻaupito ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo hono ʻuhikiʻi manupuna, ʻo faingofua ke aʻu ki ai hono nimá. Neongo, ʻi hono fakakātoá, ʻoku ou saiʻia lahi pea aʻu ʻo ʻofa ʻi he ʻEikitau loto-toʻa, faitotonu, mo ako; ka ʻoku ou fuʻu kovi ʻaupito ʻiate ia ʻi heʻene fuʻu taʻetokangaʻi ʻaupito ʻa e hina-keisi ko iá, ʻi heʻene sio ki heʻene hoko ko ha kaungāmeʻa mo ha fakafiemālie faivelenga, lolotonga ia ʻokú ne ako ʻa e fika ʻi ʻolunga ʻi ai ʻi he ʻuhikiʻi manupuna ko iá ʻi ha tuʻunga ʻe tolu pe fā ʻo e pou ʻoku ʻi ai hono ngaahi louhiʻi nima mo e ʻulu ʻoku ʻufiʻufi.

Ka ‘o kapau ‘oku ‘ikai ke fu‘u nofo‘i lelei kimautolu kau toutai tafola ‘i he Tongá ‘i ‘olunga ‘o hangē ko Kapiteni Sleet mo ‘ene kau Greenlandmen; ka ko e faingata‘a‘ia ko iá ‘oku fakafepaki‘i lahi ia ‘e he nonga kehekehe lahi ‘o e ngaahi tahi fakatauele ko ia ‘a ia ‘oku lahi taha ai ‘a e ‘alu holo ‘a kimautolu kau toutai Tongá. Ko e taha, naʻá ku faʻa lounge hake ʻi he rigging ʻo fuʻu mālōlō, ʻo mālōlō ʻi ʻolunga ke fai ha talanoa mo Queequeg, pe ko ha taha kehe ʻoku ʻikai ʻi he ngāué ʻa ia te u ala maʻu ʻi ai; pea ʻalu hake ʻi ha kiʻi hala ʻo toe mamaʻo ange, pea lī ha vaʻe paie ʻi he lotoʻā ʻo e folau ʻi ʻolungá, ʻo fai ha vakai fakamuʻomuʻa ki he ngaahi kaikaiʻanga vaí, pea ko ia ʻi heʻeku ʻalu hake fakaʻosí ki hoku taumuʻa fakaʻosí.

Tuku ke u fai ha fatafata maʻa ʻo ia heni, pea fakahaaʻi fakahangatonu naʻá ku tauhi ka ʻoku fakamolemole leʻo. ʻI he vilo ʻa e palopalema ʻo e ʻunivēsí ʻiate aú, te u lava fēfē —ʻi hono tuku kakato au kiate au ʻi ha māʻolunga fakatupu fakakaukau pehē —te u lava fēfē ʻo pukepuke maʻamaʻa hoku ngaahi fatongia ke tauhi ʻa e ngaahi tuʻutuʻuni tuʻu ʻa e ngaahi vaka tafola kotoa pē, “Tauhi hoʻo mata ʻo e ʻeá ke ʻā, pea hivaʻi ʻi he taimi kotoa pē.”

Pea tuku ke u ʻi he feituʻú ni ʻo akonaki fakaueʻiloto atu kimoutolu, ʻa kimoutolu kau maʻu vaka ʻo Nantucket! Tokanga ke fakakau ʻi hoʻo ngaahi toutai ʻāʻā ha tamasiʻi ʻoku ʻi ai hono laʻe sino mo e mata ʻoku ava; ‘oange ki he fakalaulauloto ta‘efa‘alaua; pea ko ia 'oku ne 'oatu ke vaka mo e Phædon kae 'ikai ko e Bowditch 'i hono 'ulu. Tokanga ki ha taha pehē, ʻoku ou pehē; kuo pau ke sio ki hoʻo fanga tafola kimuʻa pea toki tāmateʻi kinautolú; pea ko e talavou Palatoni mata ngoto ko ʻení te ne toho koe ʻi ha ngaahi ʻā ʻe hongofulu ʻi he māmaní, pea ʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke ne ʻai koe ke ke koloaʻia ange ʻi ha paini ʻe taha ʻo e huhuʻa tangatá. Pea ‘oku ‘ikai foki ke ‘ikai fiema‘u ‘a e ngaahi monition ko ení. He ‘i he ngaahi ‘aho ní, ‘oku ‘omai ‘e he toutai tafola ha hūfanga‘anga ki he kau talavou tokolahi ‘oku nau ‘ofa, loto-mamahi, mo ‘ikai ke nau fakakaukau, ‘oku nau fakalili‘a ‘i he ngaahi tokanga fakatupu ‘o e māmaní, pea kumi ki he ongo‘i ‘i he tā mo e blubber. 'Oku 'ikai ke fa'a perches 'a Childe Harold ia 'i he 'ulu 'o e pou 'o ha ngaahi vaka tafola fakame'apango'ia ta'emonū'ia, pea 'i he kupu'i lea moody ejaculates:—

“Taka atu, ʻa koe ʻoseni lanu pulū loloto mo fakapoʻuli, taʻaki!
‘Oku tafi‘i ta‘e‘aonga koe ‘e he kau tulimanu blubber ‘e toko hongofulu afe.”

‘Oku fa‘a hoko ‘aupito ke ‘ave ‘e he kau ‘eikivaka ‘o e ngaahi vaka peheé ‘a e kau filōsefa kei talavou ‘oku ‘ikai ke nau fakakaukau leleí, ‘o nau valoki‘i kinautolu ‘i he ‘ikai ke nau ongo‘i “mahu‘inga‘ia” fe‘unga ‘i he folaú; half-hinting ʻoku nau fuʻu mole taʻeʻamanaki ki he ngaahi holi fakaʻapaʻapa kotoa pē, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻi honau laumālie fakapulipulí ʻoku nau saiʻia ange ke ʻoua naʻa nau sio ki he fanga tafola ʻi ha toe meʻa kehe. Ka naʻe taʻeʻaonga kotoa; ‘oku ma‘u ‘e he kau Palato kei talavou ko iá ha fakakaukau ‘oku ta‘ehaohaoa ‘enau vīsoné; ‘oku nau sio nounou; ko e hā leva ‘a e ‘aonga ke fakamafasia‘i ‘a e neave sió? Kuo nau tuku ʻenau ngaahi sioʻata ʻōpelá ʻi ʻapi.

"Ko e hā, ʻa koe manuki," ko e lea ia ʻa ha tangata tau haʻapo ki he taha ʻo e kau tama ko ʻení, "kuo mau folau mālohi he taimí ni ʻi ha taʻu ʻe tolu, pea ʻoku teʻeki ke ke tauhi ha tafola. ʻOku tōnounou ʻa e tafola ʻo hangē ko e nifo ʻo e moá ʻi ha taimi pē ʻokú ke ʻi ʻolunga ai heni." Mahalo naʻa nau pehē; pe mahalo naʻe ʻi ai nai ha ngaahi kulupu ʻo kinautolu ʻi he ʻata mamaʻó; ka ʻoku lulled ki ha listlessness hangē ha ʻōpiume ʻo e reverie ʻataʻatā, taʻeʻiloʻi ʻa e toʻu tupu ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai haʻane fakakaukau ko ʻení ʻe he fefiofi ʻa e cadence ʻo e ngaahi peaú mo e ngaahi fakakaukaú, ʻo iku ʻo mole ai hono ʻulungāngá; ‘okú ne to‘o ‘a e ‘oseni fakamisitelí ‘i hono va‘é ki he ‘īmisi ‘oku hā mai ‘o e laumālie loloto, lanu pulū, ta‘e‘iai ha lalo ko iá, ‘a ia ‘okú ne ‘ākilotoa ‘a e fa‘ahinga ‘o e tangatá mo natulá; pea mo e meʻa ngali kehe, ʻoku vaeua ʻene sio ki aí, ʻoku lele holo, mo fakaʻofoʻofa kotoa pē ʻoku kalo meiate iá; ko e fin kotoa pē ‘oku ‘ikai ke ‘ilo‘i, ‘oku ‘alu hake ‘o ha fa‘ahinga founga ‘oku ‘ikai lava ke ‘ilo‘i, ‘oku hā kiate ia ko e fakafotunga ia ‘o e ngaahi fakakaukau faingata‘a ko ia ‘oku kakai pē ‘a e laumālié ‘aki ‘enau lele hokohoko atu ‘i aí. ʻI he ʻulungaanga fakaʻofoʻofa ko ʻení, ʻoku ʻalu hifo ho laumālié ki he feituʻu naʻá ne haʻu mei aí; ‘oku hoko ‘o mafola ‘i he taimí mo e feitu‘ú; hangē ko e efuefu Pantheistic naʻe lulululu ʻe Cranmer, ʻo ne faʻu fakaʻosi ha konga ʻo e matāfanga kotoa pē ʻoku ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he kolopé takaí.

ʻOku ʻikai ha moʻui ʻiate koe, he taimí ni, tukukehe pē ʻa e moʻui lulululu ko ia ʻoku ʻomi ʻe ha vaka ʻoku tafe māmālié; ʻe ia, naʻe nō mei he tahí; ‘i he tafa‘aki tahí, mei he ngaahi peau ta‘ealafaka‘uhinga‘i ‘a e ‘Otuá. Ka ʻi he lolotonga ʻo e mohe ko ʻení, ʻoku ʻiate kimoutolu ʻa e misi ko ʻení, hiki homou vaʻé pe nimá ʻi ha ʻinisi; slip ho'o puke 'i he me'a kotoa pe; pea 'oku foki mai ho'o identity 'i he ilifia. 'I he ngaahi vortices 'o Tesikati 'oku ke hover. Pea mahalo, ʻi he vaeuaʻahó, ʻi he ʻea lelei tahá, ʻi ha kaila vaeua ʻe taha ʻokú ke tō hifo ʻi he ʻea ʻataʻatā ko iá ki he tahi ʻo e faʻahitaʻu māfaná, ʻo ʻikai toe ʻalu hake ʻo taʻengata. Tokanga lelei ki ai, ʻa kimoutolu kau Panteisi!





VAHE 36. Koe Kuata-Teki.

( Hu mai ʻa ʻĒhapi: Pea, kotoa. )
