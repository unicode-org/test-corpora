Aju´ tzalajb´il teju´ xjal at nim tq´ob´ n-chi onin ti´j, aj t-xi´ t-xo´n bark te twitz tx´otx´ mya b´a´n, ex n-chi kub´ t-xiten, ex n-el t-xo´n tkyaqil il tjaq´. t-xb´alin kye Senadores ex kye jwes. q´onk t-xew, b´a´n tu´n t-xi tq´ama´n tuk´e t-xew t-xew—O Tat!—b´a´n t-xilen weye tu´n t-xi´ya t-xew—mortal moqa mya b´a´n n-kyimle.

Mintiʼtl xi tqʼamaʼn juntl maj, noq oʼkx chebʼe chebʼe xi t-xoʼn jun kʼiwlabʼil, ok tmaqsiʼn twitz tukʼil tqʼabʼ, ex iktzun tten kyaj ten ẍmejeʼ, tzmaxi tej kyxiʼ kykyaqil xjal, ex kyaj ten tjunalx toj lugar.
