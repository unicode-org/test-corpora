Dalillaaji ɗi Linnæus yiɗi riiwtude baleeji ɗii e nder ndiyam ɗii, o wiyi ko nii : « Sabu ɓernde maɓɓe ɓuuɓnde, ɓuuɓri maɓɓe, gite maɓɓe dillooje, noppi maɓɓe ɓuuɓɗi, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem », e joofnirde « ex merito lege naturæ jure ». Miɗo rokka cfuum fof e sehilaa6e am Simeon Macey e Charley Coffin, mo Nantucket, kam6e fof ko messmates am e nder laana ndiwoowa, 6e kawri e miijo wonde dalillaaji 6e 6eydi heen fof ina pamcfi. Charley hollitii e dow majjere wonde ko ɓe humbug.

Anndee, so mi woppii kala hujja, mi ƴettat leydi ɓooyndi moƴƴiri wonde baleejo oo ko liɗɗi, mi nodda Yunus ceniɗo oo yoo wallu am. Ndee huunde teskinnde jooɗiima, toɓɓere aroore ndee ko, e hol fannu ndernderiijo baleejo oo seerti e liɗɗi goɗɗi ɗii. Dow, Linnæus rokki on ɗeen geɗe. Kono e tonngol, ko ɗeeɗoo : ɓuuɓri e ƴiiƴam ngulɗam ; tawi noon, liɗɗi keddiiɗi ɗii fof ko ɗi ɓuuɓɗi, ɗi ƴiiƴam toowɗam.

Ko adii fof : hol no cifori-ɗen baleejo oo, e yaajde mum laaɓtunde, haa laaɓta ngam limtude ɗum e kala sahaa garoowo ? Ndeen noon, so en ƴettii yeru, baleejo ko liɗɗi ɓuuɓɗi, jogiiɗi wutte ɓuuɓɗo . Ko ɗoon njogiɗaa mo. Ko waawi heen wonde fof, ndeen firo ko batte miijo yaajngo. Walrus ina ɓuuɓtoo no feewi no baleejo nii, kono walrus wonaa liɗɗi, sibu ko amfibiijo. Kono helmere sakkitiinde e firo ndee ina ɓuri tiiɗde haa jooni, no hawri e adannde ndee nii. Fotde neɗɗo fof ina foti teskaade wonde liɗɗi ɗi yimɓe leydi ndii nganndi ɗii fof ngalaa wutte ɓuuɓɗo, kono ko wutte gonɗo dow, walla dow-dowre. So tawii noon, e nder liɗɗi ɓuuɓɗi, wutte oo, hay so ina waawi nanndude e mbaadi mum, ina jogoo darnde ŋorol.

E sifaa gonɗo dow oo, ko woni baleejo, mi haɗataa hay dara ittude e banndiraagal leviathan kala tagoore geec nde Nantucketers ɓurɓe humpitaade haa jooni anndini baleejo oo; walla, bannge goɗɗo oo, jokkondirde e maggal kala liɗɗi ɗi haa jooni njiytiraa ko no janani nii.* Ko ɗum waɗi, liɗɗi tokoosi, ɓuuɓɗi, e wutteeji ɓuuɓɗi ɗii fof ina poti naatde e ndeeɗoo peeje leydi Cetology. Jooni noon, ngaree diɗɗal mawngal hoɗorde baleeji ɗii fof.

( Fin-Back ).—Les ndee ɗoo hoore miɗo sikki ko mboddi ndi, e inɗe ceertuɗe Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, e Long-John, yi’aama ko famɗi fof e kala geec, ko heewi ko baleejo mo jet mum woɗɗuɗo heewi siforaade no feewi e yahooɓe taƴooɓe Atlantik, e nder packet-tracks New York. Leppi makko mawɗi ina kollita mbaadi nanndundi e kaɓirɗe, ko ɓuuɓri ɓuuɓndi, ɓuuɓndi ɓuuɓri mawndi. E oon Ahaz-dial, ɗoyngol ngol ina heewi hootde caggal. Fin-Back wonaa jom suudu. O wayi ko no baleeji-hater nii, no won e worɓe njiyrata neɗɗo nii. Semmbini no feewi; sahaa fof ina yaha tan; ummoraade e dow leydi tawa sikkaano e nder ndiyam ɓurɗam woɗɗude e ɓuuɓɗam; jet makko toowɗo laaɓɗo, gooto ina ummoo no laana misanthropic toowka e dow ŋoral ɓuuɓngal; dokkaaɗo doole e jaawle kaawniiɗe e nder njulaagu, haa ina waawi haɓaade kala ko neɗɗo jogori rewde jooni koo; oo leviathan wayi ko no Kayin riiwaaɗo mo foolaaka, mo leñol mum, ina jogii maande mum oon style e dow ɓalewol mum. E jokkondiral e ndeeɗoo appellaasiyoŋ « baleeji baleeji », ina waɗi nafoore mawnde siftinde, wonde noddaango ngoo ina waawi weeɓtinde alkule won e sifaaji baleeji, kono tan, ko meere etaade seerndude laaɓtuɗo Leviathan, tuugiiɗo e baleen mum, walla e hump mum ; hay so tawii noon ɗeen geɗe walla sifaaji maantinɗi ina laaɓi no feewi ina nanndi e ko ɓuri moƴƴude ngam waawde jogaade tuugnorgal njuɓɓudi Cetology (Cetologie) jowitiindi e kala ceertugol ɓalli ceertuɗi, ɗi baleejo, e sifaaji mum, hollirta. Hol no ndeen? Baleen, hump, ɓalndu ɓaleeru, e dente; ko ɗeen geɗe ɗe keeri mum en ceerti e nder kala sifaa baleeji, tawa alaa ko woni heen so wonaa ko waawi wonde sifaa mbaydi mum en e nder geɗe goɗɗe ɓurɗe teeŋtude. Kono ina tawee tan e bannge bonɗo, jogiiɗo batte bonɗe, rokka joom mum huunde nanndunde e mbaadi neɗɗo nano ɓuuɓɗo. Hol faandaare laaɓtunde nde ooɗoo luggere walla lance ivoire jaabotoo, ina saɗi ko haalata. Ina wayi no nde huutoraaka no ƴiyal liɗɗi kaafaahi e liɗɗi bill nii; hay so tawii won laanaaji diwooje ina mbiya mi wonde Narwhale ina huutoroo ɗum ngam rafi e wirnude les maayo ngam heɓde nguura. Charley Coffin wiyi ko ɗum huutortenoo ngam ɓuuɓnude ice ; sabu Narwhale, ummiima e dow maayo Polar, tawi ina 6uu6i, ina 6uu6na 6uu6ol mum, ina 6uu6too. Kono a waawaa hollirde hay gooto e ɗeeɗoo teskaaɗe ina selli. Miijo am e hoore am ko, no ndee ɗoo feggere bannge wootere waawirta huutoreede tigi rigi e Narwhale – no ɗum waawirta nii – ina gasa tawa ina weeɓi e makko no feewi ngam foldere e nder jaŋngugol defte. Narwhale mo nan-mi ina wiyee baleen Tusked, baleen Horned, e baleen Unicorn. Ko goonga ko o yeru teskinɗo e Unicornism tawa ina tawee e fotde kala laamu tagoore wuurnde. Gila e won e winndooɓe ɓooyɓe cloistered mi mooftii wonde ooɗoo oon luggere sea-unicorn wonnoo ko e jamaanu ɓooyɗo, ko ɗum huunde mawnde e tooke, kadi ko noon, peewnugol maggol addani ɗum cogguuli keewɗi. Kadi nde distiraama e salteeji ɓuuɓɗi ngam rewɓe ɓuuɓɓe, hono no ƴiye deer gorko peewnirtee e hartshorn nii. E fuɗɗoode ko e hoore mum hiisaama ko huunde teskinnde no feewi. Black Letter ina haalana mi wonde Sir Martin Frobisher e gartugol mum e oon laana, nde laamɗo debbo biyeteeɗo Bess waɗi e cuusal mum, ina ƴetta junngo mum jeewte e makko gila e windo galle laamorɗo Greenwich, nde laana makko cuusal ina diwa ina jippoo e maayo Tamise; “nde Sir Martin arti e oon laana,” Black Letter wiyi, “e dow koppi mum ɓuuɓɗi, o rokki toowɗo makko luggere juutnde mawnde, nde Narwhale, nde juuti caggal nde nde fawi e nder galle laamorɗo to Windsor.” Winndiyanke Irlande gooto hollitii wonde Earl of Leicester, e dow koppi mum, waɗii noon, rokki toowɗo mum luggere woɗnde, jowitiinde e daaba leydi ndi sifaa unicorn.

Narwhale ina jogii mbaydi ɓuuɓndi no feewi, nanndundi e lewru, ina waɗi mbaydi leydi ɓaleeri no kosam nii, ina waɗi toɓɓe ɓaleeje cirƴaaɗe e juutɗe. Nebam makko ina ɓura no feewi, ina laaɓi, ina laaɓi; kono ina famɗi heen, tee o seeɗa dogde. O ɓuri taweede ko e maayo cirkompolaar.

DEFTERE II. ( Oktavo ), MOƳƳERE IV. ( Waroowo ).—E oo baleejo, ko seeɗa tan anndi tigi rigi e Nantucketer, hay huunde e annduɓe tagoore wiyeteende. E ko nji’mi e makko e woɗɗude, miɗo foti wiyde ko o mawnugol grampus. O ladde no feewi—no sifaa liɗɗi Feegee. Oon sahaa ina nannga baleeji Folio mawɗi ɗii e hunuko, o fawa heen no lewru nii, haa ɓiɗngel doole ngel hulɓina haa maayde. Waroowo oo meeɗaa fiyde. Mi meeɗaa nande hol nebam mo o jogii. Ina waawi ƴetteede ko seerti e innde rokkaande ndeeɗoo baleejo, e dow daliilu ŋakkeende mum. Sibu enen fof ko en warooɓe, e dow leydi e dow maayo; Bonaparte e Requin ina heen.

DEFTERE II. ( Octavo ), MOƳƳERE V. ( Thrasher ).—Oo gorko ina anndaa e wutte mum, mo o huutortoo ngam ferule e fiyde añɓe makko. O fawi e dow ɓalewol baleejo Folio, omo lummboo, omo gollira laawol makko e fiyde ɗum; no won e jannginooɓe duɗe njiylotoo e nder winndere ndee e laawol nanndungol e ngol. Haa jooni ko famɗi fof ko anndaa e Thrasher ko e Killer. Kamɓe ɗiɗo fof ko ɓe ƴaañooɓe, hay e nder geecuuji ɗi ngalaa sariya.

Ko noon DEFTERE II ndee joofiri. ( Oktavo ), e fuɗɗoraade DEFTERE III. ( Duɗo Desimo ).

Wonande ɓeen ɓe keɓaani fartaŋŋe teeŋtuɗo janngude ndeeɗoo toɓɓere, ina gasa tawa ina haawnii, sibu liɗɗi ɗi keewaani ɓurde meeteruuji nay walla joy ina poti marsandiis e nder BALLEEN – konngol ngol, e nder maanaa yimɓe fof, ina addana sahaa kala miijo mawnugol. Kono tagooje gonɗe dow ɗee ko Duodecimoes ko baleeji ɗi ngalaa majjere, e sifaa firo am ko woni baleeji ɗii— woni liɗɗi ɓuuɓɗi, jogiiɗi wutte ɓuuɓɗo.

DEFTERE III. ( Duodecimo ), MOƳƳERE 1. ( Ɓoornugol Huzza ).—Ko ɗum ɓiɗɓe leɗɗe keewɗe tawaaɗe ko famɗi fof e winndere ndee kala. Innde ndee ko dokkal am; sabu ko ɓuri sifaa gooto ina woodi, ina haani huunde waɗee ngam seerndude ɗum en. Miɗo nodda mo noon, sabu omo lummboo sahaa kala e nder ɓulli ɓuuɓɗi, ɗi dow maayo jaajngo ina keddii e ɓuuɓde haa asamaan no cappanɗe e nder jamaanu Nayaɓo Siilo. Nafoore majji ina heewi weltineede e weltaare e laana ndiwoowa. Heewɓe ruuhuuji laaɓɗi, ɗi ummotoo ko e ɓuuɓol henndu haa e henndu. Ko kamɓe ngoni sukaaɓe wuurɓe sahaa kala hade henndu. Ɓe limtee ko maande saɗtunde. So tawii aan e hoore maa aɗa waawi muñde weltaare tati e yiyde ɗee liɗɗi ɓutte, ndeen kam asamaan wallu ma; ruuhu njurum Alla alaa e mon. Huzza Porpoise ñamlaaɗo no feewi, ɓuuɓɗo, maa rokku ma gallon gooto moƴƴo nebam moƴƴam. Kono ndiyam laaɓɗam e ɓuuɓɗam ɗam yaltinaa e ƴiye makko ina heewi nafoore no feewi. Ko e ɗaɓɓaande hakkunde jeewte e waɗooɓe waktuuji. Laanaaji ndiwoowa mbaɗi ɗum e hones mum en. Kosam porpoise ina moƴƴi ñaamde, aɗa anndi. Ina gasa tawa meeɗaa miijaade wonde porpoose ina ɓuuɓtoo. Ndeke, ɓuuɓol makko ina famɗi no feewi, ngol yiyataa no feewi. Kono sahaa garoowo so aɗa jogii fartaŋŋe, ndaartu mo; kadi aɗa yi’a ndeen baleen mawɗo oo e hoore mum e nder miniature.

DEFTERE III. ( Duudesimo ), MOƳƳERE II. ( Porpoise Alseri ).—Piraat. Safaara no feewi. O tawaa tan, miɗo sikki, ko e Pasifik. O ɓuri Huzza Porpoise mawnude seeɗa, kono ko heewi e make seneraal gooto. Tikkin mo, o buckle to shark. Mi jippinii mo laabi keewɗi, kono mi meeɗaa yiyde mo nanngaa.

DEFTERE III. ( Duuɓi ), MOƳƳERE III. ( Porpoise mo hunduko mum ɓuri mawnude ).—Porpoise ɓurɗo mawnude; e tawaa tan ko e Pasifik, haa ko anndiraa. Innde Engele tan, nde o toɗɗaa haa jooni, ko innde liɗɗi—Right-Whale Porpoise, gila e ngonka ka o ɓuri taweede ko e saraaji oon Folio. E mbaadi, omo seerti e Huzza Porpoise e won e mbaadiiji, sibu ko o ɓuuɓɗo, ɓuuɓɗo, ɓuuɓɗo ; goonga, ko o neɗɗo laaɓɗo no feewi, nannduɗo e gorko. O alaa ƴiye e ɓalndu makko (ko ɓuri heewde e porpooji goɗɗi ina njogii), omo jogii wutte belɗo, e gite sentimental indiyankooje ɗe mbaydi hazel. Kono hunduko makko mealy ina bonna fof. Hay so tawii ɓalndu makko fof haa les haa e ŋoral makko ko ŋoral luggiɗngal, kono ko ŋoral keeriwal, ceertungal no maande e nder ŋoral laana, ina wiyee “ɓalndu jaayndu”, ngal ŋoral ina ɓuuɓna mo gila e ŋoral haa e ŋoral, ina waɗi nooneeji ɗiɗi ceertuɗi, ɓaleejo dow, boɗeejo les. Daneejo oo ina hawra e feccere e hoore makko, e hunduko makko fof, ko cfuum wacfi o nanndi e njillu bonngu e nder saak nyaamdu. Feere ɓurnde meere e mealy! Nebam makko ina nanndi no feewi e nebam porpoose.

Ko wonaa DUODECIMO, ngool laawol yahrataa yeeso, sibu ko Porpoise ɓuri famɗude e baleeji ɗii. Dow, aɗa jogii Lewiatanuuji teskinɗi ɗii fof. Kono ina woodi rabbin baleeji ɗi ngalaa hoolaare, dognooɓe, feccere fabulous, ɗi, miin, miin baleeji Ameriknaajo, nganndu-mi e innde, kono wonaa e hoore am. Mi limtan ɗe e inɗe maɓɓe yeeso-castle; sabu ina gasa tawa hono ndee doggol ina waawi naftoraade wiɗtooɓe janngo, ɓe mbaawa timminde ko njogiimi ɗoo koo kono puɗɗii-mi. So tawii gooto e baleeji ɗii, maa caggal ɗuum nannge, waɗee maande, ndeen noon omo waawi naatde e ndeeɗoo Siistem, fawaade e mawneeki makko Folio, Octavo, walla Duodecimo:—Baleen Bottle-Nose; ko Baleen Junk; baleeji hoore pudding; ko bale Kap; Baleen Ardiiɗo oo; ko Baleen Kanko; ko bale biyeteeɗo Scragg; ko Baleen Kuuɓal; ko Baleen Elefant; ko Baleen Iceberg ; ko Baleen Quog; ko Baleen Bulu; ekn... Gila e laamuuji Islande, Holannda, e Engele ɓooyɗi, ina waawi tawa ina woodi doggol baleeji goɗɗi ɗi ngalaa ɗo kaaɗi, barkinɗi e kala sifaa inɗe ɗe ngonaa laaɓtuɗe. Kono mi woppii ɗe no ɗe ɓooyi nii; e waasde wallude sikkude ɗe ko sonndu tan, keewndu Leviathanism, kono alaa ko firti.

E joofnirde : Haalaama e fuɗɗoode, wonde ngool laawol wontataa ɗoo, e sahaa gooto, timmaani. A waawaa yiyde laaɓndal wonde miɗo jokki konngol am. Kono jooni miɗo accida Siistem am cetological ina darii nii haa timmaani, hay so tawii noon ko Katedraal mawɗo Koloñ heddii, tawi kraan oo ina darii haa jooni e dow ŋoral toowngal ngal timmaani. Sibu erections tokoose ina mbaawi timmineede e mahooɓe mum en adanɓe ; mawɓe, gooŋɗinɓe, meeɗaa accude copestone e ɓiɓɓe. Alla haɗa mi timminde hay huunde.
