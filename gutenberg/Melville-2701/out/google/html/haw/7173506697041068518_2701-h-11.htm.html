MOKUNA 53. Ka Gam.

ʻO ke kumu i ʻike ʻole ʻia ai ka holo ʻole ʻana o ʻAhaba ma luna o ka moku okohola a mākou i ʻōlelo ai, ʻo ia ka makani a me ke kai i ka ʻino. Ina nae aole pela, aole paha oia e kau aku maluna ona—no kana hana ana mahope iho o na manawa like—ina paha ma ke kaʻina o ka hailing, ua loaa iaia he pane ino i ka ninau ana i waiho ai. No ka mea, ʻo ia ka mea i ʻike ʻia, ʻaʻole ʻo ia i manaʻo e hoʻolauna, ʻoiai ʻelima mau minuke, me kekahi kāpena malihini, koe wale nō ka hiki iā ia ke hāʻawi i kekahi o nā ʻike āna i ʻimi nui ai. Akā, ʻaʻole i manaʻo ʻia kēia mau mea a pau, ʻaʻole i ʻōlelo ʻia ma ʻaneʻi no ka hoʻohana ʻana i nā moku koholā i ka wā e hui ai kekahi i kekahi ma nā moana ʻē, a ʻoi aku hoʻi ma kahi o ka holo ʻana.

Ina elua malihini e hele ana ma na Pine Barrens ma ka Mokuaina o Nu Ioka, a i ole i ka papu like me Salisbury Plain ma Enelani; ina e halawai maalea kekahi i kekahi ma ia ano hihiu, aole hiki ia laua ke pale aku i ke aloha pu ana; a ku iho la no ka hoololi ana i ka nuhou; a malia paha, e noho ana ilalo a e hoomaha ana i ka ahamele: alaila, he oi aku ka oi aku o ka oi aku ma ka palena palena ole o Pine Barrens a me Salisbury Plains o ke kai, elua mau moku okohola e wehewehe ana i kekahi i kekahi ma na welau o ka honua—ma ka Mokupuni Fanning wale no, a i ole ka King's Mills mamao loa; ʻOi aku ka maʻamau, ke ʻōlelo nei au, ʻaʻole pono kēia mau moku e hoʻololi wale i ka huahekili, akā e hele mai i kahi pilina pili loa, ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi a me ka launa pū. A o ka oi loa aku, he mea pono keia, no na moku i paa ma ke awa hookahi, a nona na kapena, na luna, a me na kanaka i ike pono ia e kekahi i kekahi; a no laila, loaʻa i nā ʻano mea aloha hale āpau e kamaʻilio ai.

No ka moku lōʻihi i kaʻawale, ʻo ka palena i waho, aia paha nā leka ma luna o ka moku; ʻoiai, e ʻae ʻo ia e loaʻa iā ia kekahi mau pepa o ka lā i hoʻokahi makahiki a i ʻole ʻelua ma hope o ka pepa hope loa ma kāna mau faila ʻāwīwī a me nā manamana lima. A ma ka uku ʻana i kēlā lokomaikaʻi, e loaʻa ana i ka moku holo waho ka ʻike koho kohola hou loa mai ke kahua huakaʻi e hiki ai iā ia ke hoʻoholo, he mea nui loa iā ia. A ma ke kekelē, e paʻa ana kēia mau mea a pau e pili ana i nā moku koholā e holo ana i ke ala o kēlā me kēia ma ke kahua holo ponoʻī, ʻoiai ua lōʻihi ko lākou kaʻawale ʻana mai ka home. No ka mea, ua loaʻa paha i kekahi o lākou ka hoʻoili ʻana i nā leka mai kahi hapakolu, a i kēia manawa mamao loa; a ʻo kekahi o ia mau leka no ka poʻe o ka moku āna e hālāwai nei. Ma waho aʻe, hoʻololi lākou i ka nūhou koholā, a me ke kamaʻilio ʻoluʻolu. No ka mea, ʻaʻole wale lākou e hālāwai me nā ʻoluʻolu a pau o nā luina, akā pū kekahi me nā ʻano like ʻole a pau e puka mai ana mai ka ʻimi like ʻana a me nā hemahema a me nā pilikia.

ʻAʻole hoʻi e ʻokoʻa ka ʻokoʻa o ka ʻāina; ʻo ia hoʻi, inā ʻōlelo nā ʻaoʻao ʻelua i ka ʻōlelo hoʻokahi, e like me ke ʻano o ka poʻe ʻAmelika a me ka ʻōlelo Pelekania. ʻOiai, ʻoiai, mai ka liʻiliʻi o ka poʻe moku okohola ʻEnelani, ʻaʻole hiki ke ʻike pinepine ʻia kēlā mau hālāwai, a i ka wā e hiki mai ai, hiki ke ʻano hilahila ma waena o lākou; no ka mea, ua mālama ʻia kāu kanaka Pelekane, a ʻo kāu Yankee, ʻaʻole makemake ʻo ia i kēlā ʻano mea i loko o kekahi wale nō. Ma waho aʻe o kēia, ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o nā moku okohola ʻEnelani i kekahi manawa ma mua o nā moku okohola ʻAmelika; e pili ana i ka Nantucketer lōʻihi, wīwī, me kāna mau provincialism nondescript, ma ke ʻano he ʻano mahiʻai kai. Akā, ma kahi o kēia kūlana kiʻekiʻe o ka poʻe koholā ʻEnelani, he mea paʻakikī ke ʻōlelo ʻia, ʻoiai ʻo ka Yankees i ka lā hoʻokahi, hui pū ʻia, pepehi ʻoi aku ka nui o nā koholā ma mua o ka poʻe Pelekane āpau, hui pū ʻia, i nā makahiki he ʻumi. Akā, he mea ʻino ʻole kēia i ka poʻe hahai koholā ma ʻEnelani, ʻaʻole i manaʻo nui ka Nantucketer; paha, no ka mea, ua ike oia he mau hemahema kona.

No laila, ke ʻike nei mākou, ʻo nā moku holo kaʻawale i ke kai, ʻo ka poʻe kohola ke kumu nui e launa pū ai—a pēlā nō. No ka mea, o kekahi mau moku kalepa e holo ana ma ke ala o kekahi ma waena o ʻAkelanika, e hele pinepine ana me ka ʻole o kahi huaʻōlelo hoʻomaopopo, e ʻoki ana kekahi i kekahi ma ke kai kiʻekiʻe, e like me ke kāʻei o nā dandies ma Broadway; a i na manawa a pau e hoowahawaha ana ma ka rig o kekahi. ʻO ka Men-of-War, i ka wā e hālāwai ai lākou ma ke kai, e hele mua lākou ma ke ʻano o nā kulou lapuwale a me nā ʻokiʻoki ʻana, ʻo ia hoʻi o ka hāliʻi ʻana o nā hae, me he mea lā ʻaʻole nui ka manaʻo maikaʻi a me ke aloha hoahānau. E pili ana i ka hui ʻana o nā Kauā, no ke aha, ua wikiwiki loa lākou, holo koke lākou mai kekahi i kekahi. A ʻo ka Pirates, i ka wā e hiki ai iā lākou ke ʻaʻa i nā iwi keʻa o kekahi, ʻo ka huahekili mua—“Ehia mau iwi poʻo?”—e like me ka huahekili o ka poʻe kohola—“Ehia mau barela?” A ua pane ʻia kēlā nīnau, hoʻokele koke ka poʻe pōā, no ka mea, he poʻe hewa lākou ma nā ʻaoʻao ʻelua, a ʻaʻole makemake lākou e ʻike nui i nā ʻano like ʻole o kekahi.

Akā, e nānā i ke akua, ʻoiaʻiʻo, ʻike ʻole, hoʻokipa, launa, manuahi a maʻalahi! He aha ka hana a ka mea kohola ke hālāwai ʻo ia me kekahi moku okohola i ke ʻano o ka wā maikaʻi? Loaʻa iā ia he " Gam ," he mea i ʻike ʻole ʻia e nā moku ʻē aʻe a pau ʻaʻole lākou i lohe i ka inoa; a inā paha e lohe lākou, e ʻakaʻaka wale lākou, a e haʻi hou i nā mea pāʻani e pili ana i nā "spouters" a me nā "blubber-boilers," a me nā mea like me nā leo leo maikaʻi. No ke aha la i makemake ai na Luina Kalepa a pau, a me na kanaka Pirates a me na kanaka o ke Kaua, a me na luina moku kauwa, i ka manao hoowahawaha i na moku okohola; he nīnau paʻakikī kēia e pane. No ka mea, ma ka hihia o ka poʻe pōā, e ʻōlelo, makemake wau e ʻike inā he hanohano ʻē aʻe kā lākou ʻoihana. Hoʻopau i kekahi manawa i kahi kiʻekiʻe maʻamau, ʻoiaʻiʻo; aka, ma ke olokea wale no. Eia kekahi, ke hookiekieia ke kanaka ma ia ano ano e, aole ona kumu kupono no kona kiekie kiekie. No laila, ke manaʻo nei au, i ka haʻaheo ʻana iā ia iho he kiʻekiʻe ma luna o ke kanaka koholā, ma ia ʻōlelo ʻana, ʻaʻohe kumu paʻa o ka pōā e kū ai.

Akā he aha ka Gam? Hiki iā ʻoe ke hoʻopau i kou manamana lima e holo ana i luna a i lalo i nā kolamu o nā puke wehewehe ʻōlelo, a ʻaʻole loaʻa ka huaʻōlelo. ʻAʻole i loaʻa iā Kauka Johnson kēlā naʻauao; ʻAʻole paʻa ka pahu o Noa Webster. Eia naʻe, ua hoʻohana mau ʻia kēia huaʻōlelo like ʻole i kēia manawa no nā makahiki he nui i waena o kahi ʻumikūmālima tausani Yankees hānau maoli. ʻOiaʻiʻo, pono ia i kahi wehewehe, a pono e hoʻokomo i ka Lexicon. Me kēlā manaʻo, e ʻae mai iaʻu e wehewehe pono.

GAM. NOUN— He halawai pilikanaka o elua ( a oi ) mau moku okohola, ma ke kahua holo moku; a pau ka huahekili, hele mai lakou e na luina waapa: noho na kapena elua maluna o ka moku hookahi, a o na hoa alii elua ma kekahi moku.

Aia kekahi mea liʻiliʻi e pili ana i ka Gamming i poina ʻole ma aneʻi. Loaʻa i nā ʻoihana āpau kā lākou mau kikoʻī liʻiliʻi; pela no ka lawaia kohola. I loko o ka moku pōā, kanaka-kaua, a i ʻole ka moku kauā, i ka wā e hoe ai ke kapena ma nā wahi ʻē aʻe o kona waʻa, e noho mau ana ʻo ia ma ka ʻaoʻao hope ma luna o kahi noho ʻoluʻolu, i kekahi manawa cushioned ma laila, a hoʻokele pinepine iā ia iho me kahi mea mahiʻai liʻiliʻi nani i hoʻonani ʻia me nā kaula gay a me nā lipine. Akā, ʻaʻohe noho ma hope o ka waʻa koholā, ʻaʻohe sofa o ia ʻano, ʻaʻohe mea mahiʻai. ʻO ka manawa kiʻekiʻe, inā e huila ʻia nā kāpena koholā e pili ana i ka wai ma luna o nā kaola e like me nā ʻelemakule gouty ma nā noho patent. A ʻo ka mea mahi ʻai, ʻaʻole ʻae ka waʻa koholā i kēlā ʻano wahine; a no laila, e like me ka pāʻani ʻana o ka poʻe holo moku holoʻokoʻa e haʻalele i ka moku, a no laila ʻoiai ʻo ka mea hoʻokele waʻa a i ʻole ka mea hoʻokele waʻa ka helu, ʻo ia ka mea hoʻokele ma luna o ka manawa, a ʻo ke kapena, ʻaʻohe ona wahi e noho ai, huki ʻia ʻo ia i kāna kipa ʻana a pau e kū ana me he lāʻau paina lā. A e ike pinepine oe i ka ike ana i na maka o ke ao holookoa e kau mai ana maluna ona mai na aoao mai o na moku elua, ua ola keia kapena ku i ka pono o ka malama ana i kona hanohano ma ka malama ana i kona mau wawae. ʻAʻole hoʻi kēia he mea maʻalahi loa; no ka mea, aia ma kona hope ka hoe hookele nui e ku'i ana iaia i keia manawa a ma kona kua li'ili'i, e pa'i ana ka hoe mahope ma ke kukuki ana i kona mau kuli i mua. No laila, ua pili pono ʻo ia ma mua a ma hope, a hiki iā ia ke hoʻonui iā ia iho ma ka ʻaoʻao ma ka noho ʻana ma luna o kona mau wāwae lohi; aka, e hele pinepine ana ka waapa e hoohiolo ia ia, no ka mea, he mea ole ka loa o ke kahua me ka laula ole. E hana wale i kahi kihi pāhola o nā lāʻau ʻelua, ʻaʻole hiki iā ʻoe ke kū i luna. A laila, eia hou, ʻaʻole loa ia e hana i mua o nā maka liʻiliʻi o ke ao nei, ʻaʻole loa e hiki, ke ʻōlelo nei au, no ka ʻike ʻia ʻana o kēia kapena kaʻa e hoʻokūpaʻa ana iā ia iho i kahi ʻāpana liʻiliʻi ma ka hopu ʻana i kekahi mea me kona mau lima; ʻoiaʻiʻo, ʻo ia ka hōʻailona o kāna kauoha holoʻokoʻa holoʻokoʻa, lawe mau ʻo ia i kona mau lima i loko o nā ʻeke o kona ʻeke; aka, no ka nui loa a me ke kaumaha o na lima, lawe no oia ia mau lima no ka ballast. Eia naʻe, aia kekahi mau manawa i hōʻoia maikaʻi ʻia, kahi i ʻike ʻia ai ke kāpena no kahi manawa koʻikoʻi ʻole ʻelua a ʻelua paha, i ka ʻōlelo ʻino ʻana—e hopu i ka lauoho o ka mea hoe kokoke loa, a paʻa i laila e like me ka make ʻino.





MOKUNA 54. Ka Moolelo Kaona-Ho.

( E like me ka mea i haʻi ʻia ma Golden Inn. )

ʻO ka Cape of Good Hope, a me nā wahi wai a puni a puni, ua like ia me nā kihi ʻehā i ʻike ʻia o ke ala nui, kahi āu e hālāwai ai i nā huakaʻi ʻoi aku ma mua o nā wahi ʻē aʻe.

ʻAʻole i liʻuliʻu loa ma hope o ke kamaʻilio ʻana me ka Goney, ua ʻike ʻia kekahi kanaka koholā i ka home, ʻo Town-Ho. Ua mālama ʻia ʻo ia e nā poʻe Polenekia. I loko o ka pāʻani pōkole i hāʻawi mai iā mākou i ka nūhou ikaika o Moby Dick. I kekahi poʻe, ua hoʻonui nui ʻia ka hoihoi nui i ka Whale Keʻokeʻo ma muli o ke ʻano o ka moʻolelo o Town-Ho, i ʻike ʻia e pili pū ana me ka kohola i kahi kipa kupanaha a hoʻohuli ʻia o kekahi o nā mea i kapa ʻia ʻo nā hoʻoponopono a ke Akua i ʻōlelo ʻia e loaʻa i kekahi mau kānaka. ʻO kēia kūlana hope loa, me kāna mau pūʻali ponoʻī, i hoʻokumu i ka mea i kapa ʻia ʻo ka ʻāpana huna o ka pōʻino e haʻi ʻia ana, ʻaʻole i hiki i nā pepeiao o Kāpena ʻAhaba a me kāna mau hoa. No ka mea, ʻaʻole i ʻike ʻia kēlā wahi huna o ka moʻolelo e ke kāpena o ke Kaona-Ho ponoʻī. He waiwai ponoi ia no na luina keokeo hui ekolu o kela moku, me he mea la, ua hai aku kekahi o lakou ia Tasetego me na olelo kauoha malu o Roma, aka, ia po iho, ua ohumu iho la o Tasetego i ka hiamoe, a hoike mai i ka nui o ia mea ma ia ano, a i kona ala ana, aole hiki ia ia ke aua i ke koena. Akā naʻe, no ka ikaika o ka mana o kēia mea ma luna o kēlā mau luina o ka Pequod i hiki i ka ʻike piha o ia mea, a ma muli o ka ʻono ʻē aʻe, e kapa ʻia ʻo ia, inā lākou i alakaʻi ʻia ma kēia mea, ua mālama lākou i ka mea huna i waena o lākou iho i ʻole e ʻike ʻia ma hope o ke kia nui o ka Pequod. I ka hui ʻana ma kona wahi kūpono i kēia moʻolelo ʻeleʻele me ka moʻolelo i haʻi ʻia ma luna o ka moku, ke hoʻomau nei au i ka hoʻopaʻa ʻana i ka moʻolelo mau loa.

*Ka uē koholā kahiko i ka ʻike mua ʻana i ke koholā mai ke poʻo kia, i hoʻohana mau ʻia e ka poʻe koholā i ka ʻimi ʻana i ka terrapin kaulana ʻo Gallipagos.

No kaʻu mea hoʻohenehene, e mālama au i ke ʻano aʻu i haʻi mua ai ma Lima, i kahi pōʻai hoʻomaha o koʻu mau hoaaloha Sepania, hoʻokahi ahiahi o ka haipule, e puhi ana ma luna o ka piazza kilika manoanoa o ka Golden Inn. ʻO kēlā mau koa cavaliers maikaʻi, ʻo nā Dons ʻōpio, ʻo Pedro lāua ʻo Sebastian, ua pili kokoke mai iaʻu; a no laila nā nīnau hoʻopili a lākou e waiho ai i kekahi manawa, a i pane pono ʻia i kēlā manawa.

“Ma kahi o elua makahiki ma mua o ko'u a'o mua ana i na hana a'u e hoike aku nei ia oukou, e na keonimana, e holo ana ka Town-Ho, Sperm Whaler o Nantucket, ma ko oukou Pakipika nei, aole he nui na la o ka holo ana mai ka hikina mai o keia hale hookipa maikai, aia oia ma ka aoao akau o ka Laina. Ua hou mai ka i'a pahi ia ia, e na keonimana, me ka manao o ke kapena, ua kakali mai ka pomaikai ma ia mau latitu, a no ia mea, aole i manao ia he mea weliweli ka leak, aka, aole nae i loaa ia lakou ma ke ala haahaa e like me ka hiki ke hoomau i ka holo ana o ka moku; ʻO ka pōmaikaʻi, ua hala nā lā he nui, ʻaʻole wale i ʻike ʻia ka leak, akā ua hoʻonui nui ʻia, no laila, i kēia manawa i ka hoʻomaka ʻana o ke kapena, e holo ana i ke awa kokoke loa i waena o nā mokupuni, i laila e hoʻokuʻu ai i kona kino.

"Aole nae he wahi ala liilii mamua o kona alo, aka, ina i loaa ka manawa kupono loa, aole loa oia i hopohopo o hookumuia kona moku ma ke ala, no ka mea, o ka maikai loa o kana mau pauma, a i ka hoomaha ana ia lakou i kela manawa, keia manawa, ua hiki i kela mau kanaka eono a me kanakolu, e malama wale i ka moku, aole e manao ina e palua ka lika maluna ona. ua hōʻea ʻoiaʻiʻo me ka palekana maikaʻi loa ma kona awa me ka loaʻa ʻole o ka mea make liʻiliʻi loa, inā ʻaʻole no ka hana hoʻomāinoino ʻana o Radney, ka hoa, he Malawaina, a me ka hoʻopaʻi hoʻopaʻi ʻawaʻawa o Steelkilt, he Lakeman a me ka desperado mai Buffalo.
