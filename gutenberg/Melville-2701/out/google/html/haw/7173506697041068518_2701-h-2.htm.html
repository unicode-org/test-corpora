MOKUNA 4. Ka Counterpane.

I koʻu ala ʻana i ke kakahiaka aʻe ma kahi o ke ao, ʻike wau i ka lima o Queequeg i hoʻolei ʻia ma luna oʻu ma ke ʻano aloha a aloha. Ua aneane ʻoe i manaʻo ʻo wau kāna wahine. ʻO ka pākuʻi he ʻano paʻi, piha i nā ʻāpana kala liʻiliʻi ʻē aʻe a me nā huinakolu; A ʻo kēia lima o kona peʻa a puni me ka labyrinth Cretan pau ʻole, ʻaʻohe ʻelua ʻāpana o ka malu hoʻokahi - no ka mea, ke manaʻo nei au i kona mālama ʻana i kona lima i ke kai me ka ʻole o ka lā a me ka malu, ua ʻōwili pololei ʻia kona mau lima lima i nā manawa like ʻole-ʻo kēia lima hoʻokahi o kāna, wahi aʻu, ua ʻimi ʻo ia i ke ao holoʻokoʻa e like me ke kāʻei lole. ʻOiaʻiʻo, e moe ana ma luna ona e like me ka hana ʻana o ka lima i koʻu ala mua ʻana, ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke haʻi aku mai ka quilt, ua hui pū lākou i ko lākou mau hue; a ma muli wale nō o ke kaumaha a me ke koʻikoʻi i hiki ai iaʻu ke ʻike e ʻapo mai ana ʻo Queequeg iaʻu.

He mea e ko'u mau manao. E ho'āʻo wau e wehewehe iā lākou. I koʻu wā kamaliʻi, hoʻomanaʻo maikaʻi wau i kekahi ʻano ʻano like i loaʻa iaʻu; inā he ʻoiaʻiʻo a he moeʻuhane paha, ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke hoʻoholo pono. ʻO kēia ke kūlana. Ua ʻokiʻoki au i ka ʻeke a i ʻole nā ​​​​mea ʻē aʻe—Manaʻo wau e hoʻāʻo ana ia e kolo i luna o ke kapuahi, e like me kaʻu i ʻike ai i kahi kahili liʻiliʻi e hana ana i kekahi mau lā ma mua; a ʻo koʻu makuahine hānai, ʻo ia hoʻi, e hahau ana iaʻu i nā manawa a pau, a i ʻole e hoʻouna iaʻu e moe me ka ʻaina ʻole, - huki mai koʻu makuahine iaʻu ma nā wāwae i waho o ke kapuahi a hoʻopaʻa iaʻu e moe, ʻoiai ʻo ka hola ʻelua wale nō ia o ka ʻauinalā o Iune 21, ʻo ka lā lōʻihi loa o ka makahiki ma ko mākou honua. Ua weliweli wau. Akā, ʻaʻohe mea kōkua no ia mea, no laila, piʻi au i ke alapiʻi i koʻu lumi liʻiliʻi ma ke kolu o ka papahele, me ka wehe ʻana iaʻu iho me ka mālie e like me ka hiki ke pepehi i ka manawa, a me ke kaniʻuhū ʻawaʻawa i loaʻa ma waena o nā pale.

Ua moe au ma laila me ka helu ʻino ʻana he ʻumikumamāono mau hola holoʻokoʻa e hala ma mua o koʻu manaʻolana no ke ala hou ʻana. He ʻumikūmāono hola ma kahi moe! ua ʻeha ka liʻiliʻi o koʻu kua i ka noʻonoʻo ʻana. A he māmā nō hoʻi ia; ka la e puka mai ana ma ka puka makani, a me ka halulu nui o na kaa ma na alanui, a me ke kani ana o na leo kane a puni ka hale. Ua oi loa ko'u manao, a i ka hope, ku ae la au iluna, aahu, a iho malie ilalo ma ko'u mau wawae komo, imi i ko'u makuahine, a lele koke iho la au ma kona mau wawae, me ke noi aku iaia e haawi mai ia'u i ka pahee maikai no ka'u hana kolohe; ʻo ka hoʻāhewa wale ʻana iaʻu e moe i kahi lōʻihi ʻole o ka manawa. Akā, ʻo ia ka mea maikaʻi loa a me ka noʻonoʻo o nā makuahine hānai, a ua pono wau e hele i koʻu lumi. No kekahi mau hola e moe ana au ma laila me ke ala ākea, me ka manaʻo ua ʻoi aku ka ʻino ma mua o kaʻu i hana ai mai ia manawa mai, mai nā pōʻino nui loa ma hope. ʻO ka hope loa, ua hāʻule paha wau i loko o kahi moeʻuhane pilikia o kahi hiamoe; a me ke ala lohi ʻana mai ia mea - he hapalua i loko o nā moeʻuhane - wehe wau i koʻu mau maka, a ua uhi ʻia ka lumi i hoʻomālamalama ʻia e ka lā i waho o ka pōʻeleʻele. Ua ʻike koke wau i ka haʻalulu e holo ana ma koʻu kino a pau; ʻaʻohe mea i ʻike ʻia, ʻaʻohe mea i lohe ʻia; aka, me he mea la ua kau mai ka lima kupua iloko o'u. Kau ʻia koʻu lima ma luna o ka counterpane, a ʻo ka inoa ʻole, ʻike ʻole ʻia, ʻano leo ʻole a i ʻole phantom, nona ka lima, me he mea lā e noho kokoke ana ma koʻu ʻaoʻao moe. No ka mea i ʻike ʻia he mau makahiki he mau makahiki, moe au i laila, me ka makaʻu weliweli loa, ʻaʻole wau i ʻaʻa e kauo aku i koʻu lima; e noʻonoʻo mau ana inā hiki iaʻu ke hoʻoulu i hoʻokahi ʻīniha hoʻokahi, e haki ana ka ʻuhane weliweli. ʻAʻole au i ʻike i ka lele ʻana o kēia ʻike mai oʻu aku nei; aka, i ke ala ana i ke kakahiaka, ua hoʻomanaʻo wau me ka haʻalulu i nā mea a pau, a no nā lā a me nā pule a me nā mahina ma hope iho ua nalowale wau iaʻu iho i nā hoʻāʻo hoʻohilahila e wehewehe i ka mea pohihihi. ʻAʻole, a hiki i kēia manawa, hoʻohāhā pinepine wau iaʻu iho me ia.

ʻĀnō, e lawe aku i ka makaʻu weliweli, a ʻo koʻu mau manaʻo i ka manaʻo ʻana i ka lima kupua i loko oʻu, ua like loa ia, i ko lākou ʻano ʻano, me nā mea aʻu i ʻike ai i ke ala ʻana a ʻike i ka lima pagan o Queequeg i hoʻolei ʻia mai iaʻu. Akā i ka lōʻihi, ua hoʻi hou nā hanana a pau o ka pō i hala, i kēlā me kēia, i ka ʻoiaʻiʻo paʻa, a laila ua waiho wau i ke ola wale nō i loko o ka pilikia ʻakaʻaka. No ka mea, ʻoiai ua hoʻāʻo au e hoʻoneʻe i kona lima—e wehe i kona puʻu kāne mare—eia naʻe, e hiamoe ana ʻo ia, ua pūliki paʻa mai ʻo ia iaʻu, me he mea lā ʻaʻole na ka make e hoʻokaʻawale iā kāua. I kēia manawa ua hoʻoikaika au e hoʻāla iā ia—“Queequeg!”—akā, ʻo kāna pane wale nō ʻo ka hū. Olokaa ae la au, me he mea la iloko o ke kola lio; a ua ʻike koke ʻo ia i kahi ʻoki liʻiliʻi. I ke kiola ʻana i ka pā, aia ka tomahawk e moe ana ma ka ʻaoʻao o ka mea ʻino, me he pēpē lā ke alo. He pickle nani, ʻoiaʻiʻo, manaʻo wau; abed ma kahi hale ʻē i ka lā ākea, me ka ʻai ʻai a me ka tomahawk! "Queequeg!—ma ka inoa o ka maikaʻi, Queequeg, e ala!" I ka manawa lōʻihi, ma muli o ka nui o ka wīwī, a me ka leo nui a me ka hoʻomau mau ʻana i ke kūpono ʻole o kona ʻapo ʻana i kekahi hoa kāne i kēlā ʻano ʻano male, ua lanakila wau i ka huki ʻana i kahi ʻōhumu; a i keia manawa, huki hou mai la oia i kona lima, lulu iho la ia ia iho me he ilio la o Newfoundland mai ka wai mai, a noho iho la iluna o kahi moe, oolea e like me ke kookoo pike, nana mai la ia'u, a hamo aku la i kona mau maka me he mea la aole oia i hoomanao loa i ko'u hiki ana mai ilaila, oiai nae me he mea la e pohihihi ana ka ike i kekahi mea e pili ana ia'u. I kēia manawa, moe mālie wau i ka nānā ʻana iā ia, ʻaʻohe hopohopo koʻikoʻi i kēia manawa, a kūlou i ka nānā ʻana i kahi mea ʻike. I ka manawa hope, ua like kona manao e pili ana i ke ano o kona hoa moe, a ua kuikahi oia me he mea la; Lele aku la oia iluna o ka papahele, a ma kekahi mau hoailona a me na leo i haawi mai ia'u e hoomaopopo, ina e oluolu ia'u, e kahiko mua no oia a waiho mai ia'u e kahiko ia'u mahope, e waiho ana i ke keena holookoa ia'u. Ke manao nei au, e Queequeg, malalo o keia mau kulana, he hana makaainana loa keia; akā, ʻo ka ʻoiaʻiʻo, he ʻano ʻono maoli kēia mau mea ʻino, e ʻōlelo i kāu makemake; he mea kupanaha ke ʻano koʻikoʻi o lākou. Ke haawi aku nei au i keia mahalo nui ia Queequeg, no ka mea, ua malama mai oia ia'u me ka noonoo nui a me ka noonoo, oiai ua hewa au i ka hana kolohe nui; e nana ana iaia mai kahi moe mai, a me ka nana ana i na hana a pau o kona halepaku; no ka manawa e hoʻonui ai koʻu hoihoi i kaʻu hānai. Eia naʻe, ʻo ke kanaka e like me Queequeg ʻaʻole ʻoe e ʻike i kēlā me kēia lā, ʻo ia a me kāna mau ʻaoʻao he mea ʻokoʻa loa ia.

Ua hoʻomaka ʻo ia e ʻaʻahu i luna me ka ʻaʻahu ʻana i kona pāpale beaver, kahi lōʻihi loa, ma ka ʻaoʻao, a laila - me ka hoʻemi ʻana i kāna mau ʻaʻahu - ʻimi ʻo ia i kona mau kāmaʻa. No ke aha ma lalo o ka lani, ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke haʻi aku, akā, ʻo kāna neʻe hou aʻe, ʻo ia ke uhaʻi iā ia iho—nā kamaa ma ka lima, a me ka pāpale—ma lalo o kahi moe; i ka wā, mai nā ʻūhā like ʻole a me ke kaumaha, ua manaʻo wau ua paʻakikī ʻo ia i ka hana ʻana iā ia iho; ʻoiai ʻaʻole ma ke kānāwai kūpono aʻu i lohe ai, pono ke kanaka e noho kaʻawale i ke kau ʻana i kona mau kamaa. Akā ʻo Queequeg, ke ʻike nei ʻoe, he mea ola ia i ka pae hoʻololi—ʻaʻole enuhe a ʻaʻole hoʻi he pepeke. Ua lawa pono ʻo ia i ka makaʻāinana e hōʻike i kona ʻano ʻokoʻa ma nā ʻano ʻano ʻē. ʻAʻole i pau kāna aʻo ʻana. He haumāna lae ʻo ia. Inā ʻaʻole ʻo ia i kamaʻāina liʻiliʻi, ʻaʻole paha ʻo ia e hoʻopilikia iā ia iho me nā kamaa; akā, inā ʻaʻole ʻo ia i ʻano ʻino, ʻaʻole loa ʻo ia i moeʻuhane e komo i lalo o kahi moe e hoʻokomo iā lākou. ʻO ka hope loa, puka maila ʻo ia me kona papale i haʻihaʻi ʻia a paʻipaʻi ʻia ma luna o kona mau maka, a hoʻomaka ʻo ia e ʻuwē a me ka haʻalulu a puni ka lumi, me he mea lā, ʻaʻole i maʻa loa i nā kāmaʻa kāmaʻa, ʻo kāna mau ʻili bipi pulu a wiliwī - ʻaʻole paha i kauoha ʻia ʻo ia - akā ua ʻuʻi ʻia a hōʻeha iā ia i ka hele mua ʻana o ke kakahiaka anuanu.

I ka ike ana i keia manawa, aohe paku o ka pukaaniani, a no ka haiki loa o ke alanui, ua ike pono ia ka hale e ku pono ana iloko o ka rumi, a e nana aku ana i na kii hoonanea a Queequeg i hana'i, e ku ana me na mea e ae, aka o kona papale a me na kamaa ma; Ua noi au iā ia e like me kaʻu e hiki ai, e hoʻolalelale i kona halepaku, a e komo koke i loko o kāna mau pantaloons. Ua hoʻokō ʻo ia, a laila hoʻomaka e holoi iā ia iho. I kēlā manawa i ke kakahiaka, holoi kekahi Karistiano i kona maka; aka, o Queequeg, i ko'u kahaha, ua hooluolu iaia iho i ka hooki ana i kona mau holoi ana ma kona umauma, na lima, a me na lima. Ua ʻaʻahu ʻo ia i kona ʻaʻahu, a lawe aʻela i kahi ʻāpana kopa paʻa ma luna o ka pākaukau waena, hoʻopaʻa i loko o ka wai a hoʻomaka i ka hamo ʻana i kona mau maka. E nana ana au e ike i kona wahi i waiho ai i kana pahi, aia hoi, lawe ae la oia i ka harpoon mai ke kihi o kahi moe, a hemo i ke kumu laau loloa, wehe ae la i ke poo, hookala liilii i kona kamaa, a hele aku la a hiki i ke aniani ma ka paia, hoomaka i ka wili ikaika ana, a i ole ia, o ka wili ana o kona mau papalina. Manaʻo wau, ʻo Queequeg, ke hoʻohana nei kēia i ka ʻokiʻoki maikaʻi loa a Rogers me ka hoʻopaʻi. Mahope iho, ua emi iho ko'u noonoo ma keia hana i ko'u ike ana i ke kila maikai i hanaia e ke poo o ka harpoon, a me ka oi loa o na aoao pololei loihi e malama mau ia.

Ua hoʻokō koke ʻia ke koena o kona halepaku, a hele haʻaheo ʻo ia i waho o ke keʻena, ua kāʻei ʻia ʻo ia i kona ʻaʻahu monkey hoʻokele maikaʻi, a hoʻokani ʻo ia i kāna harpoon e like me ka lāʻau o ka ilamuku.





MOKUNA 5. Ka aina kakahiaka.

Ua hahai koke au, a i ka iho ʻana i loko o ke keʻena bar, ua hoʻokipa maikaʻi ʻia ke konohiki ʻakaʻaka. ʻAʻohe oʻu manaʻo ʻino iā ia, ʻoiai ʻaʻole i liʻiliʻi ʻo ia me aʻu e pili ana i koʻu hoa moe.

Eia naʻe, ʻo ka ʻakaʻaka maikaʻi he mea maikaʻi loa ia, a ʻoi aku ka liʻiliʻi o kahi mea maikaʻi; ʻoi aku ka minamina. No laila, inā hāʻawi kekahi kanaka ma kona ʻano ponoʻī i mea hoʻohenehene maikaʻi i kekahi, mai hoʻi hope ʻo ia, akā e ʻae ʻo ia me ka ʻoliʻoli e hoʻolilo a hoʻolilo ʻia ma ia ʻano. A ʻo ke kanaka i ʻakaʻaka nui ʻia e pili ana iā ia, e hoʻomaopopo ʻoe he nui aku i loko o kēlā kanaka ma mua o kou manaʻo.

Ua piha ka rumi hale i ka poʻe i hāʻule i ka pō i hala, a ʻaʻole au i nānā maikaʻi i kēia manawa. Kokoke lakou a pau he poe kohola; na hoa nui, a me na hoa lua, a me na hoa ekolu, a me na kamamana, a me na mea hoahu kai, a me na elele moana, a me na mea hookani, a me na kahu moku; he hui ʻulaʻula a ʻulaʻula, me nā ʻumiʻumi; he kapa ʻaʻahu ʻaʻahu ʻole, ʻaʻahu a pau i nā ʻaʻahu monkey no nā ʻaʻahu kakahiaka.

Hiki iā ʻoe ke ʻike maopopo i ka lōʻihi o ka pae ʻana o kēlā me kēia i uka. ʻO ka papalina olakino o kēia ʻōpio ua like ia me ka pea i hoʻomoʻa ʻia e ka lā i ka hue, a me he mea lā ua like ia me ka ʻala; ʻAʻole hiki iā ia ke pae ʻekolu lā mai kāna huakaʻi India. Ua ʻoi aku ka māmā o kēlā kanaka ma hope ona; hiki paha iā ʻoe ke ʻōlelo he paʻi lāʻau satin i loko ona. Ma ka ili o ka hapakolu, e mau ana ka tropical tawn, aka, ua bleached iki; He kanalua ole ua noho oia i na pule a pau i uka. Akā ʻo wai ka mea hiki ke hōʻike i ka papalina e like me Queequeg? ka mea i pale ʻia me nā ʻoni like ʻole, me he mea lā e like me ke komohana komohana o Andes, e hōʻike ʻia i loko o kahi papa hoʻokahi, ʻokoʻa ka piʻi ʻana, kēlā me kēia ʻāpana.

“Grub, ho!” i kēia manawa i uē ai ke konohiki, e wehe ana i ka puka, a komo mākou i ka ʻaina kakahiaka.

'Ōlelo lākou,ʻo nā kānaka iʻike i ke ao nei, ua lilo lākou i meaʻoluʻolu i keʻano, i mea nona iho i ka hui. ʻAʻole naʻe i nā manawa a pau: ʻo Ledyard, ka mea mākaʻikaʻi nui o New England, a me Mungo Park, ka Scotch; o na kanaka a pau, ua loaa ia lakou ka hooiaio liilii loa ma ka hale keena. Akā, ʻo ka hele wale ʻana o Siberia ma kahi kaʻa i huki ʻia e nā ʻīlio e like me Ledyard, a i ʻole ka hele lōʻihi me ka ʻōpū ʻole, i loko o ka puʻuwai negero o ʻApelika, ʻo ia ka huina o nā hana ʻilihune a Mungo - ʻo kēia ʻano huakaʻi, ke ʻōlelo nei au, ʻaʻole paha ʻo ia ke ʻano maikaʻi loa e loaʻa ai kahi poʻokela kiʻekiʻe. Eia nō naʻe, no ka hapa nui, e loaʻa kēlā ʻano mea ma nā wahi āpau.

ʻO kēia mau noʻonoʻo ma ʻaneʻi, ʻo ia ke kumu ma hope o ko mākou noho ʻana a pau ma ka papaʻaina, a ke mākaukau nei au e lohe i kekahi mau moʻolelo maikaʻi e pili ana i ke koholā; ʻO koʻu manaʻo nui ʻole, kokoke kēlā me kēia kanaka e noho mālie. A ʻaʻole ʻo ia wale nō, ua hilahila lākou. ʻAe, eia kekahi mau ʻīlio kai, a ʻo ka hapa nui o lākou me ka hilahila ʻole, ua kau lākou i nā kohola nui ma ke kai kiʻekiʻe—he poʻe malihini iā ​​lākou—a ua hoʻopaʻapaʻa iā lākou me ka make ʻole; Eia naʻe, eia nō lākou i noho ai ma kahi papaʻaina ʻaina kakahiaka—ʻo nā leo like a pau, nā ʻano ʻono like ʻole o nā ʻohana a pau-e nānā a puni lākou i kekahi i kekahi me he mea lā ʻaʻole lākou i nalowale i ka ʻike ʻana o kekahi mau pa hipa i waena o nā Mauna ʻōmaʻomaʻo. He ʻike ʻike; ʻO kēia mau bea hilahila, kēia mau koholā koa wiwo ʻole!

Aka, no Queequeg—no ke aha, noho iho la o Queequeg iwaena o lakou—ma ke poo o ka papaaina, pela no; me he hau hau. No ka maopopo ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke ʻōlelo nui no kāna hānau ʻana. ʻAʻole hiki i kāna mea mahalo nui ke hōʻoiaʻiʻo i kona lawe ʻana i kāna harpoon i ka ʻaina kakahiaka me ia, a hoʻohana ʻia ma laila me ka ʻole o ka hana ʻana; me ia ma luna o ka papaʻaina, i ka pōʻino o nā poʻo he nui, a me ka grappling i nā pipi pipi iā ia. Akā, ua hana maikaʻi loa ia e ia, a ua ʻike kēlā me kēia mea i ka manaʻo o ka hapa nui o ka poʻe, ʻo ka hana ʻana i kekahi mea me ka mālie.

ʻAʻole mākou e kamaʻilio e pili ana i nā mea ʻokoʻa a pau o Queequeg ma ʻaneʻi; pehea ʻo ia i haʻalele ai i ke kofe a me nā ʻōwili wela, a hoʻohana i kona nānā pono ʻole i nā pipi pipi, hana ʻole ʻia. Ua lawa, i ka pau ana o ka aina kakahiaka, haalele iho la ia e like me na mea i koe iloko o ka rumi lehulehu, a hoomalamalama aku la i kona paipu tomahawk, a e noho malie ana i ka ai ana a puhi pu me kona papale hiki ole ke kaawale, ia'u i kaahele aku ai.





MOKUNA 6. Ke Alanui.

Ina paha ua pihoihoi au i ka ike mua ana aku i kekahi kanaka e like me Queequeg e kaapuni ana i waena o ka hui hanohano o ke kaona makaainana, ua haalele koke iho la kela kahaha i ko'u hele ana i ka la mua ma na alanui o New Bedford.
