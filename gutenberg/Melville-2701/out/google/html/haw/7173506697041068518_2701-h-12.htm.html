MOKUNA 55. No na kii weliweli o na kohola.

E pena aku au ia oe e like me ka mea hiki ole ke kala, e like me ke ano maoli o ke kohola e like me kona ike maoli ana i ka maka o ke kohola i kona kino ponoi e paa ana ke kohola ma ka aoao o ka moku kohola i hiki iaia ke hehi pono ia. No laila, he mea pono paha ke hoʻolaha mua ʻia i kēlā mau kiʻi noʻonoʻo ʻino nona a hiki i kēia lā me ka wiwo ʻole i ka manaʻoʻiʻo o ke kanaka ʻāina. ʻO ka manawa kēia e hoʻoponopono ai i ka honua i kēia mea, ma ka hōʻoia ʻana i nā kiʻi o ke koholā he hewa ʻole.

E ʻike ʻia paha ke kumu mua o kēlā mau kiʻi hoʻopunipuni ma waena o nā kiʻi kiʻi kahiko o Hindoo, ʻAigupita, a me Helene. No ka mea, mai kēlā mau manawa noʻonoʻo ʻole ʻole i ka wā ma luna o nā panellings marble o nā luakini, nā paepae o nā kiʻi, a me nā pale, nā mekala, nā kīʻaha, a me nā kālā, ua huki ʻia ka dolphin i nā unahi kaulahao e like me ko Saladin, a me ke poʻo pāpale e like me St. Mai ia manawa mai, ua lanakila ka laikini like, ʻaʻole wale ma nā kiʻi kaulana loa o ke koholā, akā ma nā hōʻike ʻepekema he nui nona.

I kēia manawa, ʻo ke kiʻi kahiko loa i manaʻo ʻia he koholā, ʻo ia ke kiʻi i loko o ke ana kaulana o Elephanta, ma India. Manaʻo ka poʻe Brahmins i loko o nā kiʻi ʻaneʻane pau ʻole o kēlā pagoda immemorial, ʻo nā ʻoihana a me nā ʻimi a pau, nā manaʻo a pau o ke kanaka, ua hoʻohālikelike ʻia i nā makahiki ma mua o ka hiki ʻana mai o kekahi o lākou. No laila, ʻaʻole ia he mea kupanaha, ma kekahi ʻano o kā mākou ʻoihana hanohano o ka lawaiʻa ʻana ma laila i uhi ʻia. ʻO ka koholā Hindoo i ʻōlelo ʻia, aia ma kahi ʻāpana ʻokoʻa o ka pā, e hōʻike ana i ke ʻano o Vishnu ma ke ʻano o leviatana, i ʻike ʻia ʻo Matse Avatar. Akā, ʻoiai he hapa kanaka a he hapalua kohola kēia kiʻi, no ka hāʻawi ʻana i ka huelo o ka hope, ʻo kēlā ʻāpana liʻiliʻi ʻo ia ka hewa. ʻOi aku kona ʻano me ka huelo liʻiliʻi o kahi anaconda, ma mua o nā poho ākea o nā ʻōpala nani o ke kohola maoli.

Aka, e hele i na Hale Kii kahiko, a e nana i keia manawa i ke kii o keia i'a a ke kanaka pena Kalikiano nui; no ka mea, ʻaʻole i ʻoi aku kona lanakila ma mua o ka Hindoo kahiko. ʻO ia ke kiʻi a Guido o Perseus e hoʻopakele ana iā Andromeda mai ke kai-monster a i ʻole ke koholā. Ma hea i loaʻa ai iā Guido ke kumu hoʻohālike o kahi mea ʻē e like me kēlā? ʻAʻole hoʻi ʻo Hogarth, i ka pena ʻana i ka hiʻohiʻona hoʻokahi i kāna "Perseus Descending," e hana i kahi mea ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi. ʻO ke kino nui o kēlā monster Hogarthian e hoʻoheheʻe ʻia ma ka ʻili, ʻaʻole hiki ke huki i hoʻokahi ʻīniha wai. He ʻano ʻano howdah ma kona kua, a ʻo kona waha ʻōpuʻu i loko o kahi e kaʻa ai nā ale, hiki ke lawe ʻia no ka Puka Kumakaia e alakaʻi ana mai Thames ma ka wai a hiki i ka Hale Kiaʻi. A laila, aia nā koholā Prodromus o Scotch Sibbald kahiko, a me Jonah's Whale, e like me ka mea i hōʻike ʻia ma nā paʻi o nā Baibala kahiko a me nā ʻokiʻoki o nā kumu mua. Pehea e olelo ai no keia mau mea? ʻO ke kohola o ka mea hoʻopaʻa puke e wiliwili ana e like me ke kumu waina a puni ke kumu o ka heleuma iho—e like me ka hehi ʻia a hoʻopaʻa ʻia ma nā kua a me nā ʻaoʻao poʻomanaʻo o nā puke kahiko a me nā mea hou-he mea nani loa ia, akā nani maoli, lawe ʻia, mai nā kiʻi like ma nā ipu kahiko. ʻOiai ka inoa o ke ao holoʻokoʻa he dolphin, ua kapa naʻe au i kēia iʻa hoʻopaʻa puke he hoʻāʻo i ke koholā; no ka mea, ua manaʻo ʻia i ka wā i hoʻokomo mua ʻia ai ka mea hana. Ua hoʻolauna ʻia e kekahi mea hoʻopuka Italia kahiko ma kahi o ke kenekulia 15, i ka wā o ka Revival of Learning; a i kēlā mau lā, a hiki i ka manawa like ʻole, ua manaʻo nui ʻia nā iʻa he ʻano o ka Leviatana.

I loko o nā vignettes a me nā mea hoʻonani ʻē aʻe o kekahi mau puke kahiko e hālāwai ai ʻoe me nā hoʻopā ʻana loa i ke koholā, kahi e puka mai ai nā ʻano puna like ʻole, nā jet dʻeau, nā pūnāwai wela a me ke anu, ʻo Saratoga a me Baden-Baden, mai kona lolo pau ʻole. Ma ka ʻaoʻao poʻomanaʻo o ka paʻi kumu o ka "Advancement of Learning" e ʻike ai ʻoe i kekahi mau koholā.

Akā, ke haʻalele nei i kēia mau hoʻāʻo pono ʻole, e nānā kākou i kēlā mau kiʻi o leviatana i manaʻo ʻia he ʻano kuoʻo, wehewehe ʻepekema, e ka poʻe ʻike. I loko o ka hōʻiliʻili o nā huakaʻi kahiko a Harris aia kekahi mau papa koholā i unuhi ʻia mai kahi puke huakaʻi Dutch, AD 1671, i kapa ʻia ʻo "A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson of Friesland, master." Ma kekahi o ia mau papa, ua hoikeia na kohola, e like me na laau laau nui, e moe ana mawaena o na mokupuni hau, me na bea keokeo e holo ana ma ko lakou kua. Ma kekahi papa ʻē aʻe, hana ʻia ka blunder prodigious no ka hōʻike ʻana i ke koholā me nā flukes perpendicular.

Eia hou, aia kekahi quarto hanohano, i kakauia e kekahi Kapena Colnett, he Kapena Leta ma ka mokukaua Enelani, i kapaia "A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, for the purpose of extending the Spermaceti Whale Fisheries." Aia i loko o kēia puke kekahi papa kuhikuhi i manaʻo ʻia he "Picture of a Physeter or Spermaceti whale, i kaha ʻia ma ka unahi mai kekahi i pepehi ʻia ma kahakai o Mekiko, ʻAukake, 1793, a kau ʻia ma luna o ka papa." ʻAʻole oʻu kanalua ua kiʻi ʻia kēia kiʻi ʻoiaʻiʻo i ke kapena no ka pono o kāna mau koa. Hoʻokahi wale nō mea e pili ana i ia mea, e ʻae iaʻu e ʻōlelo aku he maka i pili, e like me ka unahi e pili pū ana, i ke kohola sperm ulu piha, e hoʻolilo i ka maka o kēlā koholā i puka aniani ma kahi o ʻelima kapuaʻi ka lōʻihi. Auē, e kuʻu kapena koa, no ke aha lā ʻoe i hāʻawi ʻole mai ai iā Iona e nānā i waho o kēlā maka!

ʻAʻole nō hoʻi nā hui ʻoi loa o ka Natural History no ka pono o ka ʻōpio a me ka palupalu, ʻaʻole i kaʻawale i ka hewa like ʻole. E nānā i kēlā hana kaulana "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." Ma ka paʻi pōkole ʻo Lākana o ka makahiki 1807, aia nā papa o ka "whale" a me ka "narwhale." ʻAʻole oʻu makemake e ʻike ʻole ʻia, akā, ua like kēia koholā maikaʻi ʻole me ka puaʻa i ʻoki ʻia; a no ka narwhale, ua lawa ka nana aku i keia mea e kahaha ai, i keia keneturia umikumamaiwa, ua hiki ke hoohana ia keia hippogriff i ka lima maoli maluna o kekahi lehulehu naauao o na keiki kula.

A laila, i ka makahiki 1825, ua paʻi ʻo Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, he kanaka ʻike kūlohelohe, i kahi puke koholā i hoʻonohonoho ʻia i ka ʻepekema, aia nā kiʻi he nui o nā ʻano like ʻole o ka Leviathan. ʻAʻole hewa wale kēia mau mea a pau, akā ʻo ke kiʻi o ke koholā Mysticetus a i ʻole Greenland (ʻo ia hoʻi, ʻo ke kohola ʻākau), ʻoiai ʻo Scoresby, he kanaka ʻike lōʻihi e pili ana i kēlā ʻano, ʻōlelo ʻo ia ʻaʻole i loaʻa kona ʻano like.

Akā ʻo ke kau ʻana i ka puʻu pāpale i kēia ʻoihana blundering āpau i mālama ʻia no ka ʻepekema ʻo Frederick Cuvier, ke kaikaina o ka Baron kaulana. I ka makahiki 1836, ua paʻi ʻo ia i kahi Natural History of Whales, kahi āna i hāʻawi ai i ke kiʻi o ka Sperm Whale. Ma mua o ka hōʻike ʻana i kēlā kiʻi i kekahi Nantucketer, pono ʻoe e hoʻolako i kāu hōʻuluʻulu hoʻi ʻana mai Nantucket. ʻO ka huaʻōlelo, ʻaʻole ʻo Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale he Sperm Whale, akā he ʻōpae. ʻOiaʻiʻo, ʻaʻole i loaʻa iā ia ka pōmaikaʻi o ka holo ʻana i ke koholā (kakaikahi ka poʻe i loaʻa i kēlā mau kāne), akā no hea mai ia kiʻi i loaʻa mai ai, ʻo wai ka mea e ʻike? Ua loaʻa paha iā ia ma ke ʻano he mea ʻepekema mua ma ia kahua hoʻokahi, ʻo Desmarest, ua loaʻa i kekahi o kāna mau hoʻohemo ʻoiaʻiʻo; ʻo ia hoʻi, mai kahi kiʻi Pake. A he aha ke ʻano o nā keiki ʻoliʻoli me ka penikala ʻo ia mau Pake, nui nā kīʻaha a me nā kīʻaha e hōʻike mai iā mākou.

No na kohola kii hoailona i ikeia ma na alanui e kau ana maluna o na hale kuai aila, pehea la lakou? ʻO lākou ʻo Richard III. nā koholā, me nā puʻu dromedary, a me nā mea ʻino loa; ʻO ka ʻaina kakahiaka i ʻekolu a ʻehā paha luina tart, ʻo ia hoʻi nā waʻa kohola i piha i nā luina: ʻo ko lākou mau deformations e kahe ana i ke kai o ke koko a me ka pena uliuli.

Akā, ʻaʻole ia he mea kupanaha loa kēia mau hewa he nui i ka hōʻike ʻana i ke koholā. E noʻonoʻo! Ua lawe ʻia ka hapa nui o nā kiʻi ʻepekema mai ka iʻa i pae ʻia; a ua like loa keia mau mea me ke kaha kii o ka moku nahaha, me ke kua i haki, e hoike pololei ana i ka holoholona hanohano iloko o kona haaheo pau ole o ka hull a me na spars. ʻOiai ua kū nā ʻelepani no ko lākou mau lōʻihi, ʻaʻole i lana maikaʻi ka Leviatana ola iā ia iho no kāna kiʻi. ʻO ke koholā ola, i kona nani a me kona ʻano nui, ʻike ʻia ma ke kai i nā wai hiki ʻole ke ʻike ʻia; a e lana ana ka hapa nui o ia i ka ike ole ia, me he moku kaua la; a mailoko mai o ia mea he mea hiki ole i ke kanaka kino ke hookiekie ia ia ma ke kino i ka lewa, i mea e malama ai i kona mau pehu a me na ohe. A ʻaʻole hoʻi e ʻōlelo i ka ʻokoʻa i manaʻo nui ʻia ma waena o kahi kohola omo ʻōpiopio a me kahi Leviatana Platonian nui; aka, ina no kekahi o kela mau kohola omo waiu opio i kauia maluna o ka papa moku, pela no ke ano okoa, like me ka puhi, a me kona ano like ole, i hiki ole i ka diabolo ke hopu i kana olelo pololei.

Akā, manaʻo ʻia paha, mai ka iwi ʻōlohelohe o ke koholā i pae ʻia, hiki ke loaʻa nā ʻōlelo hōʻike pololei e pili ana i kona ʻano maoli. ʻaʻole iki. No ka mea, ʻo ia kekahi o nā mea ʻē aʻe e pili ana i kēia Leviatana, ʻo kona iwi iwi e hāʻawi liʻiliʻi loa i kona ʻano nui. ʻOiai ʻo ka iwi o Jeremy Bentham, e kau ana no ka ipukukui i loko o ka hale waihona puke o kekahi o kāna mau mea hoʻokō, hōʻike pololei i ka manaʻo o kahi kanaka kahiko utilitarian ʻulaʻula, me nā hiʻohiʻona pilikino ʻē aʻe o Jeremy; ʻAʻohe mea o kēia ʻano i hiki ke hoʻomaopopo ʻia mai nā iwi o ka leviatana. ʻO ka ʻoiaʻiʻo, e like me ka ʻōlelo a ka Hunter nui, ʻo ka ʻiʻo wale nō o ke koholā e pili ana i ka holoholona i hoʻopiha piha ʻia a i hoʻopaʻa ʻia e like me ka hana ʻana o ka nalo i ka chrysalis e hoʻopuni puni ana iā ia. Hōʻike ʻia kēia ʻano ʻokoʻa ma ke poʻo, ʻoiai e hōʻike ʻia ana ma kekahi ʻāpana o kēia puke. Hōʻike ʻia nō hoʻi ma ka ʻaoʻao hope, ʻaneʻane pane pololei nā iwi i nā iwi o ka lima o ke kanaka, a koe wale nō ka manamana lima. ʻEhā mau manamana lima maʻamau o kēia peʻe, ʻo ka kuhikuhi, waena, apo, a me ka manamana liʻiliʻi. Akā, ua hoʻopaʻa mau ʻia kēia mau mea a pau i loko o ko lākou uhi ʻiʻo, e like me nā manamana lima o ke kanaka i kahi uhi hana. "Akā naʻe me ka noʻonoʻo ʻole e lawelawe mai ka koholā iā mākou i kekahi manawa," wahi a Stubb ʻakaʻaka i kekahi lā, "ʻaʻole hiki ke ʻōlelo maoli ʻia ʻo ia e lawelawe iā mākou me ka ʻole o nā miteni."

No kēia mau kumu a pau, no laila, i kēlā me kēia ʻano āu e nānā ai, pono ʻoe e hoʻoholo ʻo ka Leviatana nui ka mea hoʻokahi o ka honua e noho ʻole i pena ʻia a hiki i ka hope. ʻOiaʻiʻo, hiki i kekahi kiʻi ke paʻi i ka māka ma mua o kekahi, akā ʻaʻole hiki i kekahi ke paʻi iā ia me kahi kiʻekiʻe o ka pololei. No laila, ʻaʻohe ala honua e ʻike pono ai i ke ʻano o ke koholā. A ʻo ke ʻano wale nō e hiki ai iā ʻoe ke loaʻa i kahi manaʻo ʻoluʻolu o kona ʻano ola, ʻo ka hele ʻana i ke koholā iā ʻoe iho; akā, ma ka hana ʻana pēlā, ʻaʻole liʻiliʻi ʻoe e lilo i kapuahi mau loa a hoʻopaʻa ʻia e ia. No laila, i koʻu manaʻo, ʻaʻole pono ʻoe e hoʻokē ʻai i ka hoʻopā ʻana i kēia Leviatana.





MOKUNA 56. No na kii lalau iki o na kohola, a me na kii oiaio o na kii kohola.

Ma ka hui pu ana me na kii weliweli o na kohola, ua hoowalewale loa au maanei e komo i kela mau moolelo weliweli loa o lakou e ikeia ana ma kekahi mau buke kahiko a me keia wa, oi aku hoi ma Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, a pela aku.

ʻEhā wale nō kaʻu i ʻike i nā papa kuhikuhi o ka Sperm Whale nui; Na Colnett, Huggins, Frederick Cuvier, a me Beale. Ma ka mokuna mua, ua kuhikuhi ʻia ʻo Colnett lāua ʻo Cuvier. ʻOi aku ka maikaʻi o kā Huggins ma mua o kā lākou; aka, ma ka nui loa, o Beale ka oi. Maikaʻi nā kaha kiʻi a pau a Beale no kēia koholā, koe naʻe ka helu waena ma ke kiʻi o nā koholā ʻekolu ma nā ʻano ʻano like ʻole, e pani ana i kāna mokuna ʻelua. ʻO kāna ʻaoʻao mua, ʻo nā waʻa e hoʻouka ana i Sperm Whales, ʻoiai ʻaʻohe kānalua i manaʻo ʻia e hoʻonāukiuki i ka kānalua kīwila o kekahi poʻe hale hale, he pololei a like me ke ola i kona hopena maʻamau. ʻO kekahi o nā kaha kiʻi ʻo Sperm Whale ma J. Ross Browne he pololei loa i ka contour; aka, ua kalai ino ia. ʻAʻole naʻe kāna hewa.

ʻO ka Whale Pono, aia nā kiʻi outline maikaʻi loa ma Scoresby; akā, ua huki ʻia lākou ma kahi pālākiō liʻiliʻi e hōʻike i kahi manaʻo i makemake ʻia. Hookahi wale no kii o ke kohola, a he hemahema keia, no ka mea, ma ia mau kii wale no, ke hana maikai ia, e hiki ai ia oe ke loaa ka manao oiaio o ke kohola ola e like me ka mea i ike ia e kona poe hahai holoholona.

Akā, i lawe ʻia no nā mea a pau, ʻo ka mea maikaʻi loa, ʻoiai ma kekahi mau kikoʻī ʻaʻole pololei loa, nā hōʻike o nā koholā a me nā hiʻohiʻona koholā e ʻike ʻia ma nā wahi āpau, ʻelua mau kaha kiʻi Farani nui, i hana maikaʻi ʻia, a lawe ʻia mai nā kiʻi pena e kekahi Garnery. Ma muli o kēia, hōʻike lākou i ka hoʻouka ʻana i ka Sperm and Right Whale. Ma ke kaha mua, ua hōʻike ʻia ka Sperm Whale me ka hanohano piha o ka mana, e kū ana ma lalo o ka waʻa mai ka hohonu o ka moana, a e kau ana i ka lewa ma luna o kona kua i ka pōʻino weliweli o nā papa kapuahi. ʻAʻole nahā ʻia ka ihu o ka waʻa, a ua huki ʻia me ke kaulike ʻana ma luna o ka iwi kuamoʻo; a e ku ana iloko o kela ihu, no ka manawa hookahi hiki ole ke helu, aia oe i ka hoe waa, ua uhiia ka hapalua e ka hu'i hoala o ke kohola, a me ka lele ana, me he pali la. ʻO ka hana o ka mea holoʻokoʻa he maikaʻi a he ʻoiaʻiʻo. E lana ana ka ipu laina hapalua i ke kai keokeo; ʻO nā ʻauamo lāʻau o nā harpoons i hoʻoheheʻe ʻia i loko o ia mea; ʻO nā poʻo o ka poʻe ʻauʻau ʻauʻau ʻaʻole i hoʻopuehu ʻia e pili ana i ke koholā ma nā ʻano ʻokoʻa o ka weliweli; oiai ma ka mamao ino eleele e ku ana ka moku maluna o ke kahua. Loaʻa ka hewa nui i nā kikoʻī anatomical o kēia koholā, akā e hoʻokuʻu i kēlā; no ka mea, no koʻu ola ʻana, ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke huki i kahi mea maikaʻi loa.

I ka lua o ke kahakaha ana, e kaha ana ka waapa ma ka aoao o ka aoao o kekahi Whale Akau e holo ana, e olokaa ana i kona puu weede eleele i ke kai e like me ka pahee pohaku mossy mai na pali Patagonian. ʻO kona mau jet ua kū pololei, piha, a ʻeleʻele e like me ka ʻōpala; no laila, no ka nui ʻana o ka uahi i loko o ke kapuahi, manaʻo paha ʻoe he ʻahaʻaina koa e kuke ana i ka ʻōpū nui ma lalo. Ke paʻi nei nā moa o ke kai i nā pāpaʻi liʻiliʻi, nā pūpū-iʻa, a me nā lole kai ʻē aʻe a me ka macaroni, a ka Whale ʻĀkau e hali ana i kona kua i ka maʻi ahulau. A i nā manawa a pau e holo wikiwiki ana ka leviatana lehelehe mānoanoa i ka hohonu, e waiho ana i nā tona o nā ʻeke keʻokeʻo haunaele ma kona ʻaoʻao, a e haʻalulu ana ka waʻa liʻiliʻi i loko o ka pehu me he waʻa lā i hopu ʻia i nā huila hoe o ka mokuahi moana. No laila, ʻo ka mua ka haunaele nui; aka, ma hope, ma ke ano o ka nani nani, aia ka ilikai aniani o ke kai e hoonioni ia ana, na pe'a maloo ole o ka moku mana ole, a me ka nui o ke kohola make, he pakaua lanakila, me ka hae o ka hopu e kau molowa ana mai ka pou kohola i hookomoia iloko o kona puuwai.

E pili ana i ke ʻano koholā e wehewehe koke ʻia, a no ka hoʻomaopopo maikaʻi ʻana i nā hiʻohiʻona like a pau ma nā wahi ʻē aʻe i hōʻike ʻia, eia wau e kamaʻilio e pili ana i ka laina koholā weliweli i kekahi manawa.
