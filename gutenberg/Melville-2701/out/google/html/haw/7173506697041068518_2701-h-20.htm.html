MOKUNA 101. Ka Puuku.

Ma mua o ka nalo ʻana o ka moku ʻEnelani mai ka ʻike ʻana aku, e waiho ʻia ma ʻaneʻi, ua haʻi mai ʻo ia mai Ladana mai, a ua kapa ʻia ʻo ia ma muli o ka mea kūʻai aku ʻo Samuel Enderby, ka mea kālepa o ia kūlanakauhale, ke kumu o ka hale koholā kaulana o Enderby & Sons; he hale i ko'u manao ilihune okohola, aole i mamao loa mahope o na hale alii hui o na Tudors a me Bourbons, ma ke ano o ka hoihoi moolelo maoli. Pehea ka loihi, mamua o ka makahiki o ko kakou Haku 1775, keia hale kohola nui, aole maopopo ka'u mau palapala i'a; akā, i kēlā makahiki (1775) ua hoʻokomo ʻia nā moku ʻEnelani mua i ʻimi mau i ka Sperm Whale; ʻoiai no kekahi mau makahiki i hala aku nei (mai ka makahiki 1726) ʻo kā mākou Coffins wiwo ʻole a me nā Maceys o Nantucket a me ka māla waina i nā ʻauwaʻa nui i alualu i kēlā Leviatana, akā aia wale nō ma ka ʻAkelanika ʻĀkau a me Hema: ʻaʻole ma kahi ʻē aʻe. E hoʻopaʻa ʻia ma ʻaneʻi, ʻo ka poʻe Nantucketers ka poʻe mua i waena o nā kānaka i hoʻopaʻa me ke kila civili i ka Sperm Whale nui; a ʻo lākou wale nō ka poʻe o ka honua holoʻokoʻa i hoʻopaʻa iā ia no ka hapalua haneli.

I ka makahiki 1778, ua hoomakaukauia kekahi moku maikai, o Amelia, no ka manao maopopo, a ma ka malama hookahi o na Enderbys ikaika, hoopuni wiwo ole ia Cape Horn, a oia ka mea mua iwaena o na lahui i hooku iho i ka waapa kohola o kela ano keia ano ma ke Kai Hema. He mākau a laki ka huakaʻi; a i ka hoi ana aku i kona wahi moe me kona paa piha i ka sperm makamae, ua hahai koke ia ka laana o ka Amelia e na moku e ae, Enelani a me Amerika, a pela i hooleiia aku ai ka pa nui o ka Sperm Whale o ka Pakipika. ʻAʻole naʻe i ʻoluʻolu i kēia hana maikaʻi, ua hoʻohauʻoli hou ka hale luhi iā ia iho: ʻo Samuel a me kāna mau keiki kāne a pau - ʻehia ka nui, ʻo ko lākou makuahine wale nō ka mea i ʻike - a ma lalo o ko lākou mālama koke ʻana, a ʻo kekahi, ke manaʻo nei au, ma muli o kā lākou uku, ua hoʻohuli ʻia ke aupuni Pelekāne e hoʻouna i ka sloop-of-war Rattler ma kahi huakaʻi kohola e ʻike ai i ke Kai Hema. Ua kauoha ʻia e ke Kāpena Leta manuwa, ua holo ka Rattler i ia huakaʻi, a ua lawelawe ʻo ia; pehea ka nui o ka ike ole. Akā ʻaʻole kēia wale nō. I ka makahiki 1819, ua hoʻokomo ka hale hoʻokahi i kahi moku koholā i ʻike ʻia no lākou iho, no ka holo ʻana i kahi moku hoʻāʻo i nā kai mamao o Iapana. ʻO kēlā moku - i kapa ʻia ʻo "Syren" - i hana i kahi moku hoʻokolohua hanohano; a pela i ike mua ia ai ke kahua okohola nui o Iapana. ʻO ka Syren i kēia huakaʻi kaulana i alakaʻi ʻia e kahi Kāpena Coffin, he Nantucketer.

Na Enderbie ka hanohano, no laila, ke manaʻo nei au e noho nei ko lākou hale a hiki i kēia lā; ʻoiai ʻaʻole naʻe ua hoʻoheheʻe ʻo Samuel mua i kāna uwea no ke Kai Hema nui o ka honua ʻē aʻe.

ʻO ka moku i kapa ʻia ʻo ia ka mea kūpono i ka hanohano, ʻo ia ka holo wikiwiki loa a me ka ʻoihana hanohano ma nā ʻano āpau. Ua kau au iā ia i hoʻokahi manawa i ke aumoe ma kahi o ke kahakai ʻo Patagonian, a inu maikaʻi wau i ka lele ma ka wānana. He pāʻani maikaʻi ia i loaʻa iā mākou, a he mau pū lākou a pau—ʻo kēlā me kēia ʻuhane ma luna o ka moku. He ola pōkole iā lākou, a he make hauʻoli. A ʻo kēlā pāʻani maikaʻi i loaʻa iaʻu - lōʻihi, lōʻihi loa ma hope o ka hoʻopā ʻana o ʻAhaba kahiko i kona mau papa me kona kuʻekuʻe wāwae niho ʻelepani—ʻo ia kaʻu e noʻonoʻo nei i ka hoʻokipa hanohano, paʻa, Saxona o kēlā moku; a na ka'u kahunapule e hoopoina ia'u, a e hoomanao mai ka diabolo ia'u, ke nalo au. Hoʻolele? Ua ʻōlelo anei au ua pili mākou? ʻAe, a ua hoʻohuli mākou iā ia ma ka wikiwiki o ka ʻumi galani i ka hola; a i ka hiki ana mai o ka ino (no ka mea, aia ma laila e Patagonia), a ua kaheaia na lima a pau—ka malihini a me na mea a pau—e hele i na peaau, ua kaumaha loa makou, a ua pono makou e kowali i kekahi i kekahi maluna o na ipu; a ua hoolei naaupo makou i na lepa o ko makou mau lole komo iloko o na pea, a e kau ana makou malaila, me ka uwau ana i ka makani ino, he kumu hoohalahala no na ta ona a pau. ʻAʻole naʻe i hele nā ​​kia i ka moana; a ma ia manawa, ua kuoo makou ilalo me ke kuoo loa, a ua pono ia makou e hele hou i ka paakai, oiai nae ua poha ka ehu paakai ino i lalo o ka scuttle wanana, ua oi loa aku ka diluted a picked it to my taste.

Maikaʻi ka pipi—ʻoʻoleʻa, akā me ke kino i loko. ʻŌlelo lākou he pipi bipi; kekahi, he pipi dromedar; akā, ʻaʻole maopopo iaʻu, pehea ia. Ua loaʻa iā lākou nā dumplings; nā dumplings liʻiliʻi, akā nui, symmetrically globular, a hiki ʻole ke luku ʻia. Manaʻo wau e hiki iā ʻoe ke ʻike iā lākou, a ʻōwili iā lākou i loko ou ma hope o ka ʻai ʻia ʻana. Inā ʻoe i kūlou i mua, ua pilikia ʻoe i ko lākou lele ʻana mai ou aku e like me nā pōpō pila. ʻO ka berena—akā ʻaʻole hiki ke kōkua; Eia kekahi, he anti-scorbutic; ʻo ka pōkole, ʻo ka berena wale nō ka meaʻai hou i loaʻa iā lākou. Akā, ʻaʻole māmā loa ka wanana, a ua maʻalahi loa ka hele ʻana i kahi kihi ʻeleʻele ke ʻai ʻoe. Akā ʻo nā mea a pau, e lawe ana iā ia mai kahi kaʻa a hiki i ke alakaʻi, me ka noʻonoʻo ʻana i ka nui o nā paila o ka mea kuke, me kāna mau paila palaka ola; mua a ma hope, ke ʻōlelo nei au, he moku hauʻoli ka Samuel Enderby; maikaʻi a nui; ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi a me ka ikaika; e hoʻopili i nā hoa a pau, a me ke kālā mai nā kuʻekuʻe wāwae a hiki i ka hui pāpale.

Aka, no ke aha la oukou, o Samuel Enderby, a me kekahi mau moku okohola e ae o Enelani a'u i ike—aole nae—he mau moku kaulana a hookipa; ka mea i hele a puni i ka pipi, a me ka berena, a me ke kini, a me ka hoohenehene; a ʻaʻole i luhi koke i ka ʻai ʻana, a me ka inu ʻana, a me ka ʻakaʻaka? E hai aku au ia oe. He mea nui ka ʻoliʻoli nui o kēia poʻe koholā Pelekane no ka noiʻi mōʻaukala. ʻAʻole hoʻi au i mālama iki i ka noiʻi koholā i ka wā i manaʻo ʻia he pono.

ʻO ka poʻe ʻEnelani ma mua o ka lawaiʻa koholā e ka poʻe Hollanders, Zealanders, a me Denemaka; mai ia lakou mai na huaolelo he nui i koe ma ka lawaia; a ʻo nā mea hou aku, ʻo ko lākou mau kahiko momona, pili i ka ʻai a me ka inu nui. No ka mea, ma ke ʻano maʻamau, hoʻopau ka moku kalepa ʻEnelani i kāna poʻe holo moku; aka, aole pela ka mea moku okohola Enelani. No laila, ma ka ʻōlelo Pelekania, ʻaʻole kēia mea o ka hauʻoli koholā he mea maʻamau a kūlohelohe wale nō, akā he mea pili wale nō; a no laila, pono e loaʻa kekahi kumu kūikawā, i kuhikuhi ʻia ma ʻaneʻi, a e wehewehe hou ʻia.

I kaʻu mau noiʻi ʻana i nā moʻolelo Leviatanic, ua ʻike au i kahi puke Dutch kahiko, ʻo ia hoʻi, ma muli o ke ʻala ʻala o ia mea, ua ʻike au e pili ana i ka poʻe koholā. ʻO ke poʻo inoa, "Dan Coopman," no laila ua manaʻo wau he mau moʻomanaʻo maikaʻi loa kēia o kekahi moku ʻo Amsterdam ma ka lawaiʻa, no ka mea, pono i kēlā me kēia moku koholā e lawe i kāna cooper. Ua hoʻoikaika ʻia au i kēia manaʻo ma ka ʻike ʻana ʻo ia ka hana ʻana o kahi "Fitz Swackhammer." MOKUNA 102. He Bower ma na Arsacides.

I kēia manawa, ma ka wehewehe ʻana i ka Sperm Whale, ua noʻonoʻo nui au i nā mea kupanaha o kona ʻano waho; a i ʻole kaʻawale a me nā kikoʻī ma luna o kekahi mau hiʻohiʻona o loko. Aka, no ka hoomaopopo ana aku ia ia, he mea pono ia'u e wehe hou aku ia ia, a wehe i na maka o kona pu, wehe ae i kona mau pale, a wehe i na makau a me na maka o na ami o kona mau iwi o loko, e hoonoho ia ia imua ou i kona ultimatum; ʻo ia hoʻi, i loko o kona iwi kino ʻole.

Aka, pehea keia, e Isemaela? Pehea ʻoe, he kanaka hoe wale i ka lawaiʻa, e hoʻohuoi i ka ʻike i nā wahi o lalo o ke koholā? Ua haʻi ʻo Stubb akamai, kau ʻia ma luna o kou capstan, i nā haʻiʻōlelo e pili ana i ke anatomy o ka Cetacea; a ma ke kōkua ʻana o ke aniani makani, e hāpai i kahi iwi ʻaoʻao no ka hōʻike? E wehewehe mai oe ia oe iho, e Isemaela. Hiki iā ʻoe ke hoʻopaʻa i ke kohola nui ma luna o kou papa no ka nānā ʻana, e like me ka mea kuke i ka puaʻa kalua? ʻAʻole loa. He hoike oiaio no oe, e Isemaela, mamua; aka, e malama oe i kou lawe ana i ka pono o Iona wale no; ka pono o ke kamaʻilio ʻana ma luna o nā ʻili a me nā kaola; ʻO nā ʻauamo, nā kia kuapo, nā mea moe, a me nā paʻi ma lalo, i hana ʻia i ke ʻano o ka leviatana; a e like me nā kīʻaha, nā keʻena waiu, nā pata, a me nā cheeseries i loko o kona ʻōpū.

Ke hai aku nei au, mai Iona mai, he kakaikahi na kohola i komo loa malalo o ka ili o ke kohola makua; akā naʻe, ua hoʻopōmaikaʻi ʻia au i ka manawa kūpono e wehe iā ia ma ke ʻano liʻiliʻi. I loko o kahi moku i noho ai au, ua hoʻokiʻekiʻe ʻia ke kino ʻo Sperm Whale i ka papa no kāna ʻeke a i ʻole ʻeke, e hana i nā pū no nā ʻau o nā harpoons, a no nā poʻo o nā ihe. Ke manaʻo nei ʻoe ua hoʻokuʻu wau i kēlā manawa, me ka hoʻohana ʻole ʻana i kaʻu ʻoki waʻa a me ka pahi jack, a me ka haki ʻana i ka sila a heluhelu i nā mea āpau o kēlā keiki ʻōpio?

A ʻo koʻu ʻike pono ʻana i nā iwi o ka leviatana i ko lākou ulu nui ʻana, piha i ka ulu ʻana, no kēlā ʻike kakaʻikahi, ua aie wau i koʻu hoa aliʻi ʻo Tranquo, ke aliʻi o Tranque, kekahi o nā Arsacides. No ko'u noho ana ma Tranque, he mau makahiki i hala aku nei, i ka wa i pili ai i ka moku kalepa Dey o Algiers, ua konoia au e hoohala i kekahi hapa o na la hoomaha Arsacidean me ka haku o Tranque, ma kona hale palma i hoomahaia ma Pupella; ʻaʻole mamao loa mai ka mea a ko mākou poʻe luina i kapa ʻia ʻo Bamboo-Town, kona kapikala.

Ma waena o nā ʻano maikaʻi ʻē aʻe he nui, ʻo koʻu hoa aliʻi ʻo Tranquo, i hāʻawi ʻia i ke aloha haipule i nā mea āpau o ka vertu barbaric, ua hoʻākoakoa ʻo ia ma Pupella i nā mea laʻa loa i hiki i kona poʻe akamai ke hana; nā lāʻau kalai aliʻi o nā mea kupanaha, nā pūpū ʻokiʻoki, nā ihe i hoʻokomo ʻia, nā hoe kūʻai nui, nā waʻa ʻala; a ʻo kēia poʻe a pau i hāʻawi aku i nā mea kupanaha maoli, nā ʻale hoʻokupu i hoʻolei ʻia ma kona mau kahakai.

ʻO ke poʻokela ma waena o kēia mau mea hope, ʻo ia ka Sperm Whale, ka mea, ma hope o ka makani ʻino ʻē aʻe, ua ʻike ʻia ua make a paʻa ʻia, me kona poʻo e kū pono ana i kahi kumulāʻau niu, ʻo kona ʻano huluhulu, me he mea lā ʻo ia kona ʻano lele. I ka wa i hemo ai ke kino nui i ka hohonu anana, a maloo na iwi i ka la, alaila, lawe akahele ia ka iwi i ka Pupella glen, kahi i malu ai ka heiau alii.

Ua kau ʻia nā iwi ʻaoʻao me nā kīʻaha; ua kālai ʻia ka vertebræ me nā moʻolelo Arsacidean, ma nā hieroglyphics ʻē; i loko o ka iwi poʻo, mālama nā kāhuna i ka lapalapa ahi ʻaʻole i pio, a hoʻopuka hou ke poʻo pohihihi i kāna ʻūhā mahu; oiai, e kaulia ana mai ka lala, ua oni ae la ka auwae haahaa weliweli maluna o ka poe haipule a pau, e like me ka pahikaua i kau lauoho i makau loa ia Damocles.

He mea kupanaha ia. He ʻōmaʻomaʻo ka lāʻau e like me nā mosses o ka Icy Glen; ku kiekie a haaheo na laau, me ka manao i ko lakou wai ola; ʻO ka honua ikaika ma lalo, ua like ia me ka mea ulana lole, a me ke kāʻei nani ma luna, kahi i hana ai nā ʻōpuʻu kumu waina i ka maʻawe loloa a me ka pokopoko, a ʻo nā pua ola ke kiʻi. ʻO nā lāʻau a pau, me nā lālā kaumaha o lākou; ʻo nā kumu lāʻau a pau, a me nā ferns, a me nā mauʻu; ka ea lawe leka; ua hooikaika mau keia mau mea a pau. Ma o na lacing o na lau, ua like ka la nui me he kaa lele e ulana ana i ka uliuli maloo ole. ʻŌ, ka mea ulana paʻahana! ka mea ulana ʻike ʻole ʻia!—hoʻomaha!—hoʻokahi huaʻōlelo!—ma hea ke kahe o ka lole? He aha ka hale aliʻi e kau ai? No ke aha la keia mau luhi mau loa? E ʻōlelo, e ka mea ulana lole!—e kū i kou lima!—akā, hoʻokahi huaʻōlelo me ʻoe! ʻAʻole—lele ka kaʻa — lana nā kiʻi mai waho aku o ka mea ulana lole; ua paheʻe ke kāpeti e holo wikiwiki ana. ʻO ke akua ulana, nāna i ulana; a ma ia ulana ana, ua kuli ia, aole i lohe i ka leo make; a ma ia humming ana, ua kuli no makou e nana i ka wili; a i ka wa e pakele ai kakou, e lohe kakou i na leo tausani e olelo mai ana ma ia wahi. Ke kū nei au i waena o kēia mau iwi ʻili Leviatana ikaika, nā iwi poʻo, nā ʻāʻī, nā ʻāʻī, nā iwi ʻaoʻao, a me ka vertebræ, nā mea i ʻike ʻia e nā ʻano like ʻole me nā ʻano ʻano o nā moʻo kai; aka, i ka manawa like e pili ana i na mea like me ka Leviatana antichronical i hoopauia, ko lakou poe kahiko hiki ole ke helu; ʻO wau, e ka waikahe, hoʻihoʻi ʻia i kēlā manawa kupanaha, ʻaʻole hiki ke ʻōlelo ʻia ua hoʻomaka ka manawa; no ka mea, ua hoomaka ka manawa me ke kanaka. Eia na'e ka haunaele hina o Saturn ma luna o'u, a loaa ia'u na hiohiona pohihihi me ka haalulu i kela mau Polar; i ka wā i paʻi ikaika ai nā pā hau hau ma luna o ka Tropics i kēia manawa; a ma na mile he 25,000 o ka puni honua, aole i ikeia ka laula o ka aina. A laila, na ke koholā ka honua holoʻokoʻa; a, ʻo ke aliʻi o ka hana, ua haʻalele ʻo ia i kona ala ma nā laina o nā Andes a me nā Himmalehs i kēia manawa. ʻO wai ka mea hiki ke hōʻike i ka moʻopuna e like me Leviatana? Ua hookahe ko Ahaba i ke koko kahiko mamua o ko Parao. Methuselah he keiki kula. Nānā au a puni e lulu lima me Sem. Ua weliweli wau i kēia noho mua ʻole ʻana o nā mea weliweli hiki ʻole ke haʻi ʻia o ke koholā, ka mea, ma mua o ka manawa a pau, pono e ola ma hope o ka pau ʻana o nā makahiki kanaka.

ʻAʻole naʻe ʻo kēia Leviatana wale nō i haʻalele i kāna mau meheu pre-adamite ma nā papa stereotype o ke ʻano, a ma ka limestone a me ka marl i hoʻoili i kona ʻūhā kahiko; akā, ma luna o nā papa ʻAigupita, nona ka mea kahiko i ʻōlelo ʻia he ʻano ʻano fossiliferous no lākou, ʻike mākou i ka paʻi maopopo ʻole o kāna ʻili. Ma kahi keʻena o ka luakini nui ʻo Denderah, ma kahi o kanalima mau makahiki i hala aku nei, ua ʻike ʻia ma luna o ke kaupaku pōhaku kahi kiʻi kiʻi ʻia a pena ʻia, nui nā keneta, nā griffins, a me nā dolphins, e like me nā kiʻi grotesque ma ka honua lani o ka wā hou. E lele ana i waena o lākou, ʻauʻau ʻo Leviatana kahiko e like me ka wā ma mua; Aia ma laila e ʻauʻau ai i kēlā poʻo honua, he mau keneturi ma mua o ko Solomona hānai ʻia ʻana.

ʻAʻole hoʻi e haʻalele ʻia kekahi hōʻike ʻē aʻe o ka wā kahiko o ke koholā, i loko o kāna ʻano maoli osseous post-diluvian, e like me ka mea i haʻi ʻia e John Leo, ka mea huakaʻi Barbary kahiko.

“Aole mamao loa mai ke Kai aku, he Luakini ko lakou, o na Ohola a me na Kaola i hana ia me na Iwi Ohola, no ka mea, ua kiola pinepine ia na kohola nunui a make maluna o kela kapa. MOKUNA 105. E emi anei ka nui o ke kohola?—E make anei ia?

No ka mea, oiai keia Leviatana e lele mai ana maluna o kakou mai ke poo-wai o ka Mau Loa, e ninau pono ia, ina paha, i ka loihi o kona mau hanauna, aole ia i emi mai ka nui mua o kona mau makua.

Akā ma ka hoʻokolokolo ʻana, ʻike mākou, ʻaʻole ʻoi aku ka nui o nā koholā o kēia mau lā ma mua o ka poʻe i loaʻa nā koena mōʻalihaku i loko o ka ʻōnaehana Tertiary (e hoʻopili ana i kahi wā honua ma mua o ke kanaka), akā ʻo nā koholā i loaʻa ma ia ʻano Tertiary, ʻoi aku ka nui o nā kohola i ʻoi aku ka nui o nā kohola mua.

ʻO nā koholā a pau ma mua o ka adamite i ʻeli ʻia, ʻo ka Alabama ka mea nui loa i ʻōlelo ʻia ma ka mokuna hope, a ʻaʻole i emi iho ma mua o kanahiku kapuaʻi ka lōʻihi o ka iwi. ʻOiai, ua ʻike mua mākou, ʻo ke ana lipi e hāʻawi ana i kanahikukūmālua kapuaʻi no ka iwi o ke koholā nui o kēia wā. A ua lohe au, ma ka mana o nā koholā, ua hopu ʻia nā koholā ma kahi kokoke i hoʻokahi haneli kapuaʻi ka lōʻihi i ka manawa i hopu ʻia ai.

Aole nae paha, oiai na kohola o keia hora, ua oi mua ia mamua o na kohola o na au honua mamua; ʻAʻole paha, mai ka wā o ʻAdamu, ua emi lākou?

ʻOiaʻiʻo, pono mākou e hoʻoholo pēlā, inā mākou e hōʻaiʻē i nā moʻolelo o nā keonimana e like me Pliny, a me ka poʻe naturalists kahiko. No ka mea, ua haʻi mai ʻo Pliny iā mākou i nā koholā e pili ana i nā ʻeka o ka nui o ke ola, a me Aldrovandus o nā mea ʻē aʻe i ana i ʻewalu haneli kapuaʻi ka lōʻihi—Rope Walks and Thames Tunnels of Whales! A i nā lā o Banks lāua ʻo Solander, ka poʻe loea kūlohelohe o Cooke, ʻike mākou i kekahi lālā Denemaka o ka Academy of Sciences e hoʻonoho ana i kekahi mau kohola Iceland (reydan-siskur, a i ʻole Wrinkled Bellies) ma kahi haneli me iwakālua iwilei; ʻo ia hoʻi, ʻekolu haneri me kanaono kapuaʻi. A ʻo Lacépède, ka mea ʻike kūlohelohe Farani, ma kāna moʻolelo kikoʻī o nā koholā, i ka hoʻomaka ʻana o kāna hana (ʻaoʻao 3), hoʻonoho i ka Whale ʻĀkau ma kahi haneli mika, ʻekolu haneri me ka iwakāluakūmāwalu kapuaʻi. A ua paʻi ʻia kēia hana i ka makahiki 1825.

Akā, e manaʻoʻiʻo anei kekahi kanaka koholā i kēia mau moʻolelo? ʻAʻole. Ua like ka nui o ke koholā o kēia mau lā me kona mau kūpuna i ka wā o Pliny. A inā e hele au i kahi o Pliny, ʻo wau, he kanaka koholā (ʻoi aku ma mua o ia), e wiwo ʻole e haʻi aku iā ia. No ka mea, aole hiki ia'u ke hoomaopopo i ke ano o ia mea, oiai na mumi o Aigupita i kanuia he mau tausani makahiki mamua o ka hanau ana o Pliny, aole i ana nui lakou i loko o ko lakou mau pahu kupapau e like me ke Kentuckian hou iloko o kona mau soke; a oiai na pipi a me na holoholona e ae i kalaiia ma na papa kahiko loa o Aigupita a me Nineva, e like me ka hooiaio maopopo ana i na bipi momona o Smithfield, aole i like wale, aka, ua oi aku ka nui o ka nui o na bipi momona o Parao; i mua o keia mau mea a pau, aole au e ae aku, o na holoholona a pau, o ke kohola wale no ka i degenerated.

Akā, ke waiho nei kekahi nīnau; Hoʻopilikia pinepine ʻia e nā Nantucketers hou hou. Ma muli paha o ka ʻike ʻike ʻana i nā poʻo kia o nā moku kohola, e komo ana i kēia manawa ma nā kowa o Behring, a i loko o nā pahu huki huna a me nā laka o ka honua; a holo aku la ua tausani harpoon a me na ihe ma na kapakai aina a pau; ʻO ka moot point, inā paha e hiki i ka Leviatana ke hoʻomanawanui i kahi alualu ākea, a me ka mihi ʻole i ka pōʻino; ʻAʻole paha e hoʻopau ʻia ʻo ia mai ka wai, a ʻo ke koholā hope, e like me ke kanaka hope, e puhi i kāna paipu hope loa, a laila e hoʻoheheʻe ʻia i ka puff hope loa.

I ka hoohalike ana i na ohana kohola hupe me na bufalo hupe, aole he kanaha makahiki aku nei, ua palahalaha ae e na umi tausani ma na papu o Ilinoi a me Misuri, a lulu iho la i ko lakou mau mane hao a hupo me ko lakou mau lae hekili maluna o na kahua o na kapikala nui o ka muliwai, kahi e kuai aku ai ke kanaka kalepa maikai ia oe ma ke dala hookahi iniha; ma ia hoʻohālikelike ʻana, ua hoʻolako ʻia ka hoʻopaʻapaʻa hiki ʻole ke hoʻopaʻapaʻa, e hōʻike ana ʻaʻole hiki i ke koholā hahai ʻia ke pakele koke i ka pau ʻana.

Akā, pono ʻoe e nānā i kēia mea i kēlā me kēia ʻano. ʻOiai pōkole loa i ka wā i hala aku nei—ʻaʻole i maikaʻi ke ola ʻana—ua ʻoi aku ka helu ʻana o ka bufalo ma Ilinoi ma mua o ka helu ʻana o nā kānaka i kēia manawa ma Lādana, a ʻoiai i kēia manawa ʻaʻole i koe hoʻokahi pepeiaohao a me ka maiuu o lākou ma ia wahi a pau; a ʻo ke kumu o kēia luku kupanaha ʻana, ʻo ka ihe a ke kanaka; akā, ʻo ke ʻano ʻokoʻa loa o ka ʻimi koholā e pāpā loa i ka pau ʻana o ka Leviatana. He kanaha kanaka o ka moku hookahi e hahai ana i na Sperm Whales no kanahakumamawalu malama, ua manao lakou ua hana maikai loa lakou, a e hoomaikai aku i ke Akua, ina lakou e lawe i ka home i ka aila o na ia he kanaha. Oiai, i ka wa o na kanaka hahai holoholona kahiko o Kanada a me Inia o ke Komohana, he waonahele a me ka puupaa ke komohana loa (ma ka napoo ana o ka la) he puupaa ka heluna like o na kanaka moka, no na malama like, e kau ana maluna o ka lio ma kahi o ka holo ana ma na moku, aole lakou i pepehi i na bufalo he kanaha, aka, he kanaha tausani a oi aku; he ʻoiaʻiʻo, inā pono, hiki ke hōʻike ʻia ma ka helu helu.

ʻAʻole hoʻi i manaʻo ʻia he kūpono, ʻaʻole paha he manaʻo hoʻopaʻapaʻa e pili ana i ka pau ʻana o ka Sperm Whale, no ka laʻana, i nā makahiki i hala (ʻo ka hapa hope o ke kenekulia i hala, e ʻōlelo ʻia) ʻoi aku ka nui o kēia mau Leviathans, i loko o nā ʻōpala liʻiliʻi, i kēia manawa, a no laila, ʻaʻole i hoʻolōʻihi ʻia nā huakaʻi, a ʻoi aku ka uku. No ka mea, e like me ka mea i ʻike ʻia ma nā wahi ʻē aʻe, ʻo kēlā mau kohola, i hoʻohuli ʻia e kekahi mau manaʻo i ka palekana, ke ʻauʻau nei i ke kai ma nā kaʻa kaʻa nui, no laila i ka nui o nā poʻe ʻauamo, nā ʻauamo, a me nā ʻōpala, a me nā kula o nā lā ʻē aʻe, ua hui pū ʻia i nā pūʻali koa ākea akā kaʻawale ʻole. ʻO ia wale nō. Me he mea la he hewahewa ka manao hookiekie, no ka mea, aole e hele hou ana na kohola-iwi i kapaia i na kumu he nui i na makahiki mua e nui ana me lakou, nolaila ke emi nei keia ano. No ka mea, ua kipaku wale ia lakou mai ka piko a i ka aahu; a inā ʻaʻole ola hou kekahi kapakai me kā lākou mau mokulele, a laila, e ʻoiaʻiʻo, ua hoʻopuʻihaʻi ʻia kekahi mau ʻāpana ʻē aʻe a mamao loa e ka ʻike ʻike ʻole.

Eia kekahi: e pili ana i kēia mau Leviatana i ʻōlelo ʻia hope loa, he mau pā kaua paʻa ko lākou, ʻo ia hoʻi, ma ke ʻano o nā kānaka a pau, e mau loa ʻole ke pio. A e like me ka hoouka kaua ana mai o ko lakou mau awawa, ua hoi hope ka poe hau hau i ko lakou mau mauna; no laila, e ʻimi ʻia mai nā savannas a me nā glades o ke kai waena, hiki i nā koholā iwi ke hele i ko lākou mau hale kiaʻi Polar, a luʻu ma lalo o nā pale aniani hope loa a me nā pā ma laila, e piʻi mai i waena o nā māla hau a me nā lele; a i loko o ka pōʻai hoʻohiwahiwa o Kekemapa mau loa, e ʻōlelo kūʻē i nā ʻimi āpau mai ke kanaka.

Akā, ʻoiai he kanalima paha o kēia mau kohola iwi kohola i hoʻopaʻa ʻia i hoʻokahi kīʻaha, ua manaʻo kekahi poʻe akeakamai o ka wānana ua hoʻemi nui kēia pōʻino maikaʻi i kā lākou mau pūʻali koa. Aka, no kekahi manawa i hala aku nei kekahi mau kohola, aole i emi iho malalo o 13,000, ua pepehiia i kela makahiki i keia makahiki ma ke kapakai komohana e na Amerika wale no; akā, aia nā manaʻo e hoʻolilo i kēia kūlana i mea liʻiliʻi a ʻaʻole i manaʻo ʻia he hoʻopaʻapaʻa kūʻē i kēia mea.

Me he mea la e hoohuoi loa ia ana no ka lehulehu o na mea ola nui loa o ka honua, heaha nae ka kakou e olelo aku ai ia Harto, ka mea kakau moolelo o Goa, i kona hai ana mai ia kakou, ma ka hahai holoholona ana, ua lawe ka Moi o Siama i 4,000 elepani; ma ia mau wahi, ua like ka nui o na Elepani e like me ka nui o na bipi ma ke anu. Aohe kumu e kanalua ai, ina paha o keia mau Elepani, ka mea i imiia no na tausani makahiki, e Semiramis, e Porus, e Hannibal, a me na mo'i hope a pau o ka Hikina—ina e ola nui lakou malaila, e oi aku ka nui o ke kohola nui mamua o ka hahai holoholona ana, no ka mea, he wahi hanai holoholona kana e hele aku ai, oia hoi ka nui o Amerika, o Asia a me Asia a pau. Hoʻohui ʻia nā mokupuni o ke kai.

Eia kekahi: e noʻonoʻo kākou, mai ka manaʻo ʻia o ka lōʻihi o ke ola ʻana o nā koholā, ʻo ka hiki ʻana paha o ko lākou mau makahiki i hoʻokahi haneli a ʻoi aku, no laila i kēlā me kēia manawa, pono nā hanauna makua ʻokoʻa i kēia manawa. A ʻo ia hoʻi, e loaʻa koke iā mākou kekahi manaʻo, ma ka noʻonoʻo ʻana i nā hale kupapaʻu a pau, nā ilina, a me nā waihona ʻohana o ka hana ʻana e hāʻawi ana i nā kino ola o nā kāne, nā wahine, a me nā keiki a pau i ola i nā makahiki he kanahikukumamālima i hala; a me ka hoʻohui ʻana i kēia pūʻali lehulehu i ka heluna kanaka o ka honua i kēia manawa.

No laila, no kēia mau mea a pau, ke manaʻo nei mākou i ka make ʻole o ke koholā i kona ʻano, eia naʻe he palaho i kona ʻano. Ua ʻau ʻo ia i ke kai ma mua o ka haki ʻana o nā ʻāina; Ua ʻauʻau ʻo ia ma luna o ke kahua o nā Tuileries, a me Windsor Castle, a me Kremlin. I ke kaiakahinalii o Noa, ua hoowahawaha oia i ka halelana o Noa; a ina e wai hou ia ka honua, e like me Holani, e pepehi i kona mau iole, alaila, e ola mau ana ke kohola mau loa, a e hanai ana ma ka piko o ka waikahekahe, e hoopuka aku i kona hoole i ka lewa.





MOKUNA 106. Ka wawae o Ahaba.

ʻO ke ʻano o ka haʻalele ʻana o Kāpena ʻAhaba i ka Samuel Enderby o Lādana, ʻaʻole i mālama ʻia me kahi hana ʻino liʻiliʻi i kona kino iho. Ua hoʻomālamalama ʻo ia me ka ikaika nui ma luna o kahi kaohi o kona waʻa i loaʻa ai i kona wāwae ʻelepani ka haʻalulu hapa. A ma hope o ka loaʻa ʻana o kona papa ponoʻī, a me kāna lua pivot ma laila, ua huila ikaika ʻo ia me ke kauoha wikiwiki i ka hoʻokele (ʻo ia kekahi mea e pili ana i kāna hoʻokele ʻole ʻana); a laila, loaʻa hou i ka niho ʻelepani i hoʻoluliluli ʻia i kahi wili hou a me ka wili, ʻoiai e mau ana ia i ke ʻano holoʻokoʻa, a me nā mea ʻino a pau, ʻaʻole naʻe i manaʻo ʻo ʻAhaba he mea hilinaʻi loa ia.

A he mea ʻuʻuku hoʻi ia no ke kahaha, no kona ʻano pupule a pau, ua mālama pono ʻo ʻAhaba i kekahi manawa i ke kūlana o kēlā iwi make āna i kū ai. No ka mea, ʻaʻole i liʻuliʻu loa ma mua o ka holo ʻana o ka Pequod mai Nantucket mai, ua ʻike ʻia ʻo ia i kekahi pō e moe ana i lalo i ka lepo, a me ka noʻonoʻo ʻole; e kekahi poʻe i ʻike ʻole ʻia, a i ʻike ʻole ʻia, ʻaʻole hiki ke noʻonoʻo ʻia, ua hoʻoneʻe ʻia kona lālā niho ʻelepani, a ua hahau ʻia ʻo ia, a ua hou nā mea āpau i kona ʻāʻī; ʻaʻole hoʻi me ka pilikia loa i hoʻōla ʻia ka ʻeha ʻeha.

ʻAʻole hoʻi, i kēlā manawa, ʻaʻole i komo i loko o kona manaʻo monomaniac, ʻo ka ʻeha a pau o kēlā ʻeha e kū nei i kēia manawa, ʻo ia wale nō ka pilikia o ka pōʻino mua; a me he mea la ua ike pono ia, e like me ka mea kolo loa o ka puuwai e hoomau ana i kona ano e like me ka mea hoolea maikai loa o ka ululaau; no laila, e like me kēlā me kēia leʻaleʻa, hana maoli nā hanana pōʻino i ko lākou like. He oiaio, oi aku mamua o ka like, i manao ai o Ahaba; no ka mea, ʻoi aku ka ʻoi aʻe o nā kūpuna a me nā hanauna o ke kaumaha ma mua o ka ʻohana a me nā mamo a Joy. No ka mea, ʻaʻole e hōʻike i kēia: he manaʻo ia mai kekahi mau aʻo canonic, ʻoiai ʻo kekahi mau leʻaleʻa kūlohelohe ma ʻaneʻi ʻaʻole e hānau keiki na lākou no ka honua ʻē aʻe, akā, ma ka mea ʻē aʻe, e ukali ʻia e ka hauʻoli-ke keiki ʻole o ke kaumaha o Gehena; aka, e hoohanau mau ana kekahi mau poino kino hewa no lakou iho i mau mamo holomua mau loa o na ehaeha ma o aku o ka luakupapau; ʻAʻole loa e hōʻike i kēia, aia nō ke ʻano like ʻole i ka loiloi hohonu o ia mea. No ka mea, manaʻo ʻo ʻAhaba, ʻoiai ʻo nā mea ʻoluʻolu kiʻekiʻe loa o ka honua i loaʻa kahi mea ʻino ʻole i loko o lākou, akā, ma lalo, ʻo nā puʻuwai a pau, he mea pohihihi, a i kekahi mau kānaka, he hanohano archanglic; no laila, ʻaʻole e hōʻoiaʻiʻo kā lākou ʻimi ʻana i ka unuhi ʻike.
