"Ma Cherries? Ndalombozya kuti nitwakali aawo mpozikomena. Oh, Stubb, ndisyoma kuti bamaama bangu bacete bakabweza mali yangu kaitanasika eechi; ikuti naa pe, mbasyoonto ba copper lino bayooboola kuli mbabo, nkaambo lweendo lwamana."

Kuzwa kumagolelo aabwaato, basikweenzya bwato boonse lino tiibakali kubeleka pe; nkomo, zibeela zyamaplanga, mivwi, amabbomba, zyakajatwa mumaanza aabo, mbubonya mbuli mbobakali kuzwa kumilimo yabo iindene-indene; meso aabo oonse aasalala akali kulanga nswi mpati, eeyo kuzwa kulubazu kuya kulubazu yakali kuzungaanya mutwe wakwe, yakatumina ntambo mpati yabulongo buli akati-kati kunembo lyakwe naakali kuzuza. Kupilusya, kupilusya chakufwambaana, bubi butamani zyakali mumubili wakwe woonse, mpawo nikuba kuti muntu wakali kukonzya kuchita zyoonse, chikwabilizyo chaankumu yakwe chakauma buta bwabwaato, kusikila bantu azisamu zyakazungaana. Bamwi bakawida ansi. Mbuli myootokala yakagwisigwa, mitwe yabasikuuma nkoli mujulu yakali kuzungaana kunsingo zyabo zyakali mbuli ŋombe. Kwiinda mumulyango, bakamvwa maanzi kaatila, mbuli malundu mbwaazwa mumulonga.

"Bwaato! Mootokala wazifwide!—mota yabili yazifwide!" wakapozomoka Ahabu kuzwa mubwato; "chisamu chacho chilakonzya kuba chaku Amelika luzutu!"

Kakusotokela munsi lyabwaato bwakali kukkala, nswi mpati yakazuza kazungaana aansi aachikobela chayo; pesi wakasanduka munsi lyamaanzi, wakafwambaana kufwusa kuya kujulu alimwi, kule abwaato bumbi, pesi mumayaadi masyoonto kuzwa kubwaato bwa Ahabu, ooko, kwaachiindi, nkwaakakkede.

"Ndasandula mubili wangu kuzwa kuzuba. Ino ho, Tashtego! ndileke ndimvwe nkomo yako. Oh! nduwe mitwe yangu yotatwe iitalipede; yebo ntambo iitakwe nsingo; amubili uulemekwa alikke; yebo bbuwa liyumu, amutwe uulisumpula, amutwe uujisi nsingo,—bwato bujisi bulemu bwalufu, mpawo weelede kufwa mpawo ndazwa kulindime kakunyina ndime! kulisumpula kwabasilutwe babwaato bakatyoka? kusikila kumamanino ndilakulwana ; kuzwa mumoyo wa gehena ndilakufwusa;

Chibbudu chakafwuswa; nswi mpati yakaumwa yakawuluka kuya kunembo; akufwambaana kwakuyaka mulilo mulongo wakainda mumigodi;—wakasofwaazya. Ahabu wakakotama kuti achisalazye; wakachisalazya; pesi kuuluka kwakamujata munsingo, mpawo kakwiina jwi mbuli ba Turkey nibakali kufwusa ntambo yamuntu ngobakali kujaya, wakadubulwa kuzwa mubwaato, kabatanazyiba basikweenzya bwato kuti wazwa. Muchiindi chifwiifwi, ntambo yameso ilemu kumamanino aantambo yakauluka kuzwa mubbokesi lyakanyina chintu, yakagonka sikukwela bwato, mpawo yakauma lwizi, yakasweeka mukati loko.

Kwakaindi kaniini, basikweenzya bwato bwakaliimvwi; mpawo wakasanduka. "Bwaato? Leza Mupati, ino bwato buli kuli?" Muchiindi chifwiifwi kwiinda munzila zitaboneki kabotu, zikankamanisya bakabona chikozyano chakwe chizimaana, mbuli mu Fata Morgana; buyo zibulo zyaatala kapati zyakazwa mumaanzi; nikuba kuti bakabikkilwa maanu, naa kusyomeka, naa chiindi, kuzyuuno zyabo zyakali atala, basikwiimba bachikombelo bakazumanana kulanga lwizi. Mpawo lino, zizyungulukide zyakajata bwato bulikke, ababelesi boonse, abwato buli boonse bwakali kweenda, amusako woonse, mpawo zyakali kuzungaana, zipona azitaponi, zyoonse zyakali kuzinguluka mumulengalenga omwe, zyakali kunyamuna kabeela kaniini kapati ka Pequod kuti katabonwi pe.

Pesi aawo nkondo zyakumamanino nizyakali kulitila atala amutwe wamuIndiya wakabbila abwaato bupati, akusiya masentimita masyoonto aamugwagwa uuimvwi uulibonya, antoomwe amayaadi malamfu aakali kuzwa fuleegi, eeyo yakali kuzungaana cakukkazika moyo, kakuli kuseka-seka, atalaa mayuwe aakali kunyonyoona akali afwaafwi kuguma—nkomo eeyo iisalala pyu, mpoonya-mpoonya; wakasumpulwa musule mumulengalenga, mumulimo wakuuma fuleegi chakufwambaana asi chakufwambaana kuchibaka chakali kuyaansi. Inswi iitegwa sky-hawk eeyo yakatobela loli mpati kuya ansi kuzwa kubusena bwayo akati kanyenyeezi, kailya fuleegi, mpawo kachisa Tashtego aawo; oyu muyuni lino wakajana choolwe chakujata mababa aakwe mapati aakali kuzungaana aakati ka nyundo achisamu; mpawo aawo mpawo kumvwa kusekelela ooko, munyama wakabbizyigwa munsi, mukuyoya kwakwe kwalufu, wakabamba nkomo yakwe kuti kiimvwi aawo; mpawo muyuni wakujulu, akulila kwa bangelo bapati, amulomo wakwe wabwami kausunkwa mujulu, mpawo chiimo chakwe choonse chabuzike chakavwumbilidwe mufulegi lya Ahabu, wakaseluka abwato bwakwe, oobo, mbuli Saatani, butakali kuyoobbila muhelo mane kusikila wakakwelelezya chibeela chakujulu chipona antoomwe anguwe, mpawo wakalikwabilila.

Lino tuyuni tusyoonto twakali kuuluka kabakoongolola atala aamulyango wakali kuvwuuma; mayuwe aatuba aatakwe bbivwe akali kuuma kumabazu aayo aatantamuka; mpawo zyoonse zyakawa, mpawo chivwumbyo chipati chalwizi chakazungaana mbuli mbuchakazungaana myaaka iili zyuulu zyosanwe yakainda.





Mamanino

"ALIMWI NDAKATIJA BUYO NDIKWAAMBILA" Jobu.

Cisobano chamana. Ino nkaambo nzi aawa muntu uuzwa anze?—Nkaambo umwi wakafwutuka kukunyonyooka kwabwaato.

Chakachitika biyo, kuti Parsee naakamana kusweeka, ndime ooyo Fates ngwaakabikka kuti ndibweze busena bwa Ahabu sikuuma buta, aawo sikuuma buta ooyo naakabweza busena butakwe bantu; nguwenya, ooyo, aawo mubuzuba bwamamanino baalumi botatwe nibakasowelwa kuzwa mubwaato bwakali kuzungaana, wakawisigwa kusule. Aboobo, kanditanta mumbali aachikozyano chakatobela, mpawo kandili mukubona koonse, aawo kuyoya kwabwaato bwakabbila nikwakasika kulindime, ndakali mpawo, pesi asyoonto-asyoonto, kweenzyegwa kuya kumuwo uujala. Nindakasika kuli ncico, chakaba zyiba lyamaanzi. Kuzinguluka akuzinguluka, mpawo, mpawo kazizungaana kuya kuli bbatani lisiya lili mbuli bbatani lisiya aawo mpaakali kuzungaana asyoonto-syoonto, mbuli Ixion aimbi njindakali kuzungaana. Kusikila, kujana busena oobo buyandika, bbola lisiya kuya mujulu lyakapwasuka; mpawo lino, yakaangununwa akaambo kachikozyano chayo chibotu, mpawo, akaambo kakutanta kwayo kupati, katanta anguzu zipati, bbokesi lyakufwutula lyakafwutuka kuzwa mulwizi, lyakawida ansi, mpawo lyakazuzaana kumbali lyangu. Ndakali kusungwaazigwa abbokesi lyamutunta, kwachiindi chilamfu amasiku woonse, ndakatanta atala abwaato buteteete alimwi buli mbuli lwiimbo lwabuumba. Inswi zitakwe ntenda, zyakali kweenda mbuli kuti zyakalijisi zijazyo kumilomo yabo; nziba zyamulwizi zyakali kutanta bwato amilomo yakavwumbidwe. Mubuzuba bwabili, lwizi lwakasika afwaafwi, afwaafwi, mpawo lwakandibweza kumamanino. Wakali Rachel wakali kweenda munzila iitali kabotu, ooyo mukuyandaula kwakwe bana bakwe bakasweeka, wakajana buyo mucaala umbi.
