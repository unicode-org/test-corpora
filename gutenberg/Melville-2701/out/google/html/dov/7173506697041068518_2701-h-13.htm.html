Ijwi lijatikizya chakachitika muchibalo chamamanino.

CHAANDANO 63. Chibuno.

Kuzwa mumusamu, mitabi ilakomena; kuzwa kuli mbabo, mitabi. Aboobo, mumakani aagwasya, kozyanya zibeela.

Icibuno chakaambwa apeeji lyakainda cheelede kwaambwa. Nchisamu chili amagolelo chachiimo chilibedelede, chili aamaulu obile mubulamfu, eecho chibikkidwe mubbokesi lyakumusanza afwaafwi amutwe, kuchitila kuti kube busena bwakulyookezyela kumusela wachisamu, ooyo uumbi uunyina mababa uuzwa kunembo. Mpawo eechi chilwanyo chilasika kuli sikuchisowa, walo wakachibweza chakufwambaana mbuli muntu uukkala musokwe mbwazungaanya chibbudu chakwe kuzwa kubwaanda. Nchintu chidumide kuba azilwanyo zyobile zili muchibuno, eezyo ziitwa kuti nsimbi yakusaanguna ayabili.

Pesi aaya ma harpoon obile, umwi awumwi ulaa ntambo yawo, zyoonse ziliswaangene amutambo; makanze ngakuti: kubafwusa boonse bobile, kuti kacikonzeka, umwi mpawo-mpawo katobela umwi mukati ka nswi yomwe; kuchitila kuti, mukukwela kuboola, umwi wagwisya, umwi ulakonzya kuzumanana kujata. Nkuyungizya zyoolwe ziindi zyobile. Pesi chiindi chiingi zilachitika kuti akaambo kakufwambaana, changuzu, akuzungaana kwa nswi mpati njaakatambula butale bwakusaanguna, chakaba chintu chiyumu kuli sikuuma nswi, nikuba kuti uli mbuli malabi mukweenda kwakwe, kuti afwuse butale bwabili muli nguwe. Nikuliboobo, mbuli butale bwabili mbobuswaangene kale amutambo, mpawo mutambo ulikweenda, aboobo eecho chilwanyo cheelede, muchiindi choonse, kusowelwa anze lyabwaato, munzila imwi akubusena bumwi; naakuti ntenda mpati loko inga yajatikizya maanza woonse. Kuwida mumaanzi, mbubwenya mbuli muzyiimo ziliboobu; zibeela zyamabbokesi (zyaambwa muchibalo chakayinda) zipa kuti mulimo ooyu, muziindi zyiingi, uchitike kabotu. Pesi eechi chintu chikatazya tachichitiki lyoonse kakunyina kulanganizigwa pe nkaambo kuli bantu bachiswa kapati.

Kuya kunembo: weelede kuziba kuti aawo butale bwabili nobusowelwa mulwizi, kuzwa waawo buba chintu chilengaana, chiyoosya, chizyunguluka bwato a nswi mpati, akujata ntambo, naa kuzigela, mpawo akupa kuti kube kukankamana kupati mumabazu oonse. Alimwi, munzila iigaminide, tacikonzyeki pe kuchikwabilila alimwi kusikila nswi mpati ya whale yajatwa kabotu-kabotu akuba mutunta.

Yeeya, lino, mboceelede kuba mumaato one woonse kabelesya nswi yomwe iilaanguzu, iibeleka, alimwi iizyi kabotu; eelyo akaambo kabube oobu buli muli nguwe, antoomwe antenda zili zyuulu zyakali kuchitika mumulimo ooyu, zintu zili musanu atutatu naa kkumi zilakonzya kuzyunguluka nguwe. Nkaambo, masimpe, bwato buli boonse bulapegwa zilwanyo zyiingi zyakukotama kumulongo kuti bwato butaanzi bwafwuswa kakunyina kuchita obotu. Zyoonse eezyi zyaambwa kabotu-kabotu aawa, nkaambo tazikonzyi kwaalilwa kupandulula zibeela ziyandikana loko, nikuba kuti zilakatazya kumvwisisisya, muzibeela ziyoopentwa kumbele.

Zogranda, umwi wabasilisi babo bazyibidwe kapati, ulalumbaizya zibeela zya blubber kubana bavwanda, kuti zilatontola loko alimwi zilagwasya. Eezi zindiyeezya kuti bamwi baEngland, aabo kaindi bakasiigwa muGreenland abwato bwakuzuba nswi mpati—kuti aaba baalumi bakapona myeezi myiingi azibeela zyanswi zyakasyaala kunkomwe yalwizi nikwakamana kusola mafuta. Akati kaba Dutch whalemen eezyi zintu zyakasyeede zyaambwa kuti "ma fritters"; eeyo, masimpe, njiikozyenye kapati, kaili mubala wasalala pyu alimwi kaili njumu, alimwi inunkilila mbuli nzungu zyabamakaintu bakaindi baku Amsterdam naa ma oly-cooks, eelyo nozili zipya. Zilibonya mbuli kuti zilakonzya kulya zyakuti mweenzu uulyaandaanya takonzyi kukwabilila maanza aakwe.

Asi eecho chiyungizya kuchesya nswi mpati mbuli chakulya chibotu, mbuvwubi bwakwe bupati. Ngu ŋombe mpati ya bulumbu bwamulwizi, ineneede loko kuti ibe kabotu. langa chikumba chakwe, eecho chikonzya kuba chakulya chibotu mbuli cha buffalo (eecho chiyeeyelwa kuti nchilyo chitavwuli kujanika), ikuti nikwatali mbuli pyramid yamafuta. Asi spermaceti lwini, mbuli mbuuli buteteete alimwi buli kkilimu; mbuli nyama iisalala pyu, iitakwe jeli, iituba ya cocoanut mumwezi watatu wakukomena kwayo, pesi ilivwubide loko kuti ilakonzya kunjila mubusena bwa bbata. Nikuliboobo, bantu banji bavwubide nswi mpati balaa nzila yakunjizya muchintu chimwi, mpawo balalya. Muchiindi chilamfu chakulindila masiku nchintu chidumide kuti basikweenzya bwato babike bbisiketi lyabo mumabbodela mapati aamafuta mpawo bazileke kuti zifwe aawo kwakaindi kaniini. Ndakabamba chakulya chamansiku chibotu loko.

Muchiimo cha Sperm Whale muniini boongo bulabonwa mbuli chakulya chibotu. Bbokesi lyamutwe lyakatyolwa amusako, mpawo zibeela zyobile zipati, zituba zyakagwisigwa (zili mbuli mapudingi obile mapati), mpawo zyakasanganyigwa afulawa, mpawo zyakajikwa kuba chintu chibotu loko, mubunoti buli mbuli mutwe wamoombe, oobo buli mbuli chakulya chibotu kuli bamwi ba epicures; alimwi boonse balizyi kuti bamwi bavwubwa akati kaba epicures, kwiinda mukuzumanana kulya boongo bwamoombe, asyoonto-asyoonto balajana boongo bwabo beni, kuchitila kuti bakonzye kwaandaanya mutwe wamoombe kuzwa kumitwe yabo beni; eecho, masimpe, chiyanda kusalulula kutazyibidwe pe. Mpawo nkaambo kaako nziba nsyoonto iijisi mutwe wamoombe uuboneka kuti musongo kunembo lyakwe, nchimwi chazintu ziusisya loko nzyokonzya kubona. Mutwe wamulanga mbuli kuti ulamusampaula, akwaamba kuti "Et tu Brute!" kulitondeezya.

Teesi, ambweni, nkaambo kakuti nswi iitegwa whale ilaa mafuta loko zyakuti bantu bamunyika balabona kuti kulya kwayo nchintu chisesemya pe; eecho chilibonya kuti chizwa, munzila imwi, kuzwa kukuyeeya kwakaambwa musule: nkokuti muntu weelede kulya chintu chamulwizi chakajayigwa, mpawo ulye awalo mumuni wacho. Pesi kakunyina kudooneka muntu wakusaanguna kujaya ŋombe wakali kubonwa mbuli mujayi; ambweni wakaangidwe; alimwi kuti naakabikkwa mumulandu wakwe a ŋombe, masimpe naakabikkwa; alimwi wakali kweelela ncobeni kuti naa kuli mujayi uuchita oobo. Koya kumusika wanyama mu Mujibelo masiku mpawo ubone makamu aabantu bapona amawulu obile kabalangide kumilongo milamfu yabanyama bafwide bajisi maulu one. Hena kubona ooku takugwisyi lino mumulomo wamuntu uulya bantu? Basikulya bantu? nguni uutali sikulya bantu? Ndakwaambila kuti chiyooba chiyumu kuli Fejee ooyo wakajaya mumisyinali mutete mukaanda kakwe kakulwana nzala yakalikuboola; chiyooba chiyumu kuli yooyo Fejee uulanganya, ndaamba, mubuzuba bwalubeta, kwiinda nduwe, sikulya uuzyibidwe alimwi uuzyibidwe, ooyo uuma nziba ansi akulya zizo zyabo zizwide mu paté-de-foie-gras yako.

Asi Stubb, ulalya nswi mpati kwiinda mumumuni wayo, tee kayi? mpawo eecho nkuyungizya kutukila kukuchisa, tee kayi? Langa kujatisya nkomo yako, aawo, sikulya wangu uuzyibidwe loko uulya nyama yangombe yakangwa, ino janza eeli lyakabambwa aanzi?—ino mafwuwa aamunyina ng'ombe njoolya? Alimwi ino ubelesya nzi meno aako, wamana kulya nziba eeyo mpati? Aboya bwa nkuku njiyonya. Alimwi ino nkulemba nzi Mulembi wa Sosaiti Yakulesya Lunya kuli Ganders nkwaakabelesya kukazya magwalo aakwe? Mumwezi omwe naa yobile yainda biyo mbunga eeyo yakabikka chizuminano chakutagwasyilizya chintu chili coonse kunze lyamapeni aabutale.





CHAANDANO 66. Kujaigwa kwa Shark.

Eelyo mu Southern Fishery, Sperm Whale wakajatwa, nikwakainda kubeleka kwachiindi chilamfu, wakaletwa kumbali mansiku, tachili, mbuli chintu chidumide pe, chiyanza chakufwambaana kuchita mulimu wakumugela. Nkaambo mulimu oyo muyumu loko; tachimani pe; alimwi kuyandika maanza woonse kuti achite. Aboobo, nzila iibelesyegwa kapati njakubweza lwizi loonse; kuuma nkoli a'lee; mpawo utume umwi awumwi munsi kuchibbeeke chakwe kusikila zuba, kakuli lusyomo lwakuti, kusikila chiindi eecho, basikulindila bayoobambwa; nkokuti, zyobile azyobilo kwawoola lyomwe, umwi awumwi, babelesi kabazungaana beelede kutanta bwato kuti babone kuti zyoonse zyeenda kabotu.

Pesi chimwi chiindi, ikapati ku Muzila mu Pacific, muzeezo ooyu taukonzyi kwiingula pe; nkaambo nkamu zya shaka zitabaliki zilabungana kuzinguluka mutunta wakaangidwe, kuti naakasiigwa oobo kwamahola musanu aabili, atwaambe, muchiindi chilamfu, chifuwa chakalinga kulibonya mafwumofwumo. Mumasena manji aalwizi, nokubaboobo, ooko nswi eezyi nkozitavwuli pe, kulya kwazyo kulakonzya kucheya, kwiinda mukuzisungwaazya amapepa aakuzuba nswi, nzila eeyo, muziindi zimwi, ilibonya buyo kuti ilakonzya kuziyungizya. Pesi teensi mbocakabede muchiimo chiliwo lino aaba Pequod's sharks; nikubaboobo, masimpe, kufumbwa mwaalumi uutazyibide kubona zintu ziliboobu, kuti wakalanga kulubazu lwakwe masiku aayo, wakali kunga wayeeya kuti lwizi loonse lwakali chiizi chipati, mpawo nswi eezyo nswi zyakali mukati.

Nikuliboobo, aawo Stubb naakabikka ntambo yakukwabilila nikwakamana kulya chakulya chakwe chamansiku; mpawo aawo, kweendelana, Queequeg asikweenzya bwato bwa forecastle nibakasika abwaato, kwakaba lukkomano luniini akati kabaswi; nkaambo mpawo-mpawo bakaimika zibeela zyakutebula kumbali, akusezya malambe otatwe, kuchitila kuti akamweke mumuni mulamfu atala aalwizi lutakwe muzuzumo, aaba basikweenzya bwato bobile, kabafwusa nswi zyabo zilamfwu, bakazumanana kujaya nswi mpati, * kwiinda mukuuma nsimbi ziyumu mukati kapati mumutwe wazyo, kakuli buyo cibeela cabo ciyandika kapati. Pesi mukunyongana kwabasinkondonyina bakali kulwana, basikuuma tiibakali kukonzya kusika aawo mpobakagolela; mpawo eezi zyakaleta ziyubunuzyo zipya zyabukali bupati bwasinkondonyina. Bakafwusa changuzu, kutali buyo aawo umwi awumwi naakali kuzwa, pesi mbuli buta butazungaani, bakakotamika, mpawo bakaluma lwabo beni; kusikila zilonda eezyo zyakalibonya kuti zyamenwa cakwiinduluka-induluka amulomo omwe, kuti zyakagwisigwa acilonda cakali kuvwunguluka.
