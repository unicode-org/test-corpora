CHAANDANO 53. I Gam.

Kaambo kalibonya kakapa kuti Ahabu atanjile mubwato bwakuzuba nswi njotwakali kwaambuula nkakuti: guwo alwizi zyakali kutondezya guwo pati. Asi nikuba kuti teensi mbocakabede, naatakali kunga, ambweni, wakamutanta—kweelanisya abukkale bwakwe muziindi zikozyenye—ikuti nikwakali boobo, kwiinda mukutamba, wakajana bwiinguzi butali kabotu kumubuzyo ngwaakabuzya. Nkaambo, mbuli mbukwakazooba, tanaakabikkila maanu kukuyanzana, nokuba kwamaminiti osanwe, amweendelezi uuli woonse ngwatazyi, kunze kwakuti wakali kukonzya kupa makani amwi ngaakali kuyandaula. Pesi zyoonse eezyi zilakonzya kuzumanana kutayeeyelwa kabotu, ikuti kakunyina kwaambwa aawa aatala akubelesya kwa mato aakuzuba nswi mpati aawo noziswaangana mulwizi lwaanze, ikapati aawo mpobeendelezya bwato.

Kuti beenzu bobile kabazabuka Pine Barrens muNew York State, naakuti Salisbury Plain muEngland; kuti kabaswaangana kakunyina kuyeeyela mumasena aatakwe luyando, aaba bobile, mubuumi bwabo, tabakonzyi kutantamuka kwaanzyanya; akuima kwakaindi kaniini kuti bachinche makani; alimwi, amwi, kukkala ansi kwakaindi kaniini akulyookezya antoomwe: mpawo, nchintu chibotu loko kuti atala aa Pine Barrens a Salisbury Plains aalwizi, mato obile aakuzuba nswi mpati kaali mukulwana kumamanino aanyika—kuzwa ku Fanning's Island, naakuti ku King's Mills; nchintu chilikabotu biyeni, ndaamba, kuti mubukkale buliboobu mato aaya taayelede buyo kucincanya mabwe, pesi ayelede kusika afwaafwi, kuba abulongwe alimwi akuyanzana. Alimwi ikapati eezi inga zyaboneka mbuli kuti nchintu cheenzu, muchiimo chamaato aali mubusena bomwe bwalwizi, alimwi aayo basilutwe, basilutwe, alimwi kutali basyoonto akati kabaalumi bazyibidwe kuli umwi amweenzinyina; mpawo, kuba azintu zyoonse zyamuŋanda nzyotukonzya kwaambuula.

Kubwaato butazwide kwachiindi chilamfu, sikuzwa anze, ambweni, ulaa magwalo mubwaato; kufumbwa mbocibede, uyoosinizya kuti ulamuzumizya kuti abe amapepa amwi aabuzuba bwakuzyalwa mwaka omwe naa yobile musule lyayamamanino mumafayela aakwe aataboneki kabotu alimwi aajisi chaala. Mpawo mukupilusya bulemu oobo, bwato bwakali kuya anze bwakali kuyootambula busongo bwakuzuba nswi mpati kuzwa kubusena bwakutanta ooko nkobwakali kukonzya kuya, chintu chakali kuyandikana kapati kulinguwe. Alimwi muchiimo, zyoonse eezyi ziyooba zyamasimpe kujatikizya mato aakuzuba nswi mpati aainda munzila yamwi awumwi aabusena bwakutanta bwato, nikuba kuti taali aŋanda kwachiindi chilamfu. Nkaambo umwi wabo ulakonzya wakatambula kutuminwa kwamagwalo kuzwa kuli umwi watatu, mpawo lino ulikule loko; alimwi amwi aamagwalo aaya alakonzya kuba aabantu bamubwaato mbwaswaangana aabo lino. Kunze lyaboobo, bakali kwaambuula makani aakuzuba nswi mpati, mpawo kababandika kabotu. Nkaambo teensi buyo kuti bakali kuyooswaangana alweetelelo lwabasikutanta bwato, pesi mbubwenya buyo abukkale boonse bulibedelede buzwa mukuyandaula antoomwe akubula zintu antoomwe antenda.

Alimwi kwiimpana kwacisi takukonzyi kuchita kwiimpana kupati pe; nkokuti, kufwumbwa buyo kuti boonse bobile kabaambaula mwaambo omwe, mbuli mbocibede kubantu baku Amelika aba Chingisi. Nikubaboobo, masimpe, kuzwa kumweelwe musyoonto wabasikuzuba nswi mpati bakuEngland, miswaangano iiliboobu taichitiki pe, mpo kuti yachitika kuli nchobeni akati kabo; nkaambo muna England wako ulalikwabilila, mpawo mu Yankee wako, tayandi chintu chiliboobo muli umwi pesi nguwe mwini. Kunze lyaboobo, basikuzuba nswi bakuEngland chimwi chiindi balaba aanguzu kwiinda basikuzuba nswi baku Amelika; kujatikizya Nantucketer mulamfu, muteteete, abukombi bwakwe butaambiki, mbuli mulimi wamulwizi. Asi aawo bupati oobu bwaba English whalemen nkobuli, inga tiikwaba pe kwaamba, nkaambo ba Yankees mubuzuba bomwe, antoomwe, bajaya nswi zyiingi kwiinda ba England boonse, antoomwe, mumyaka iilikumi. Aboobo, mpawo, twabona kuti mumato woonse aayenda mulwizi, basikuzuba nswi mbabajisi twaambo tunji twakuba abukkale bwakuyanzana—mpawo mbubo. Mpawo, mato amwi aabasambazi azabuka lwizi akati-kati ka Atlantic, kanjikanji alainda kakwiina kwaamba jwi lyomwe lyakuzyibya, akukosolana mulwizi lupati, mbuli nkamu yaba dandies mu Broadway; mpawo chiindi choonse kabachita, ambweni, mukusampaula kwamuntu umwi awumwi. Kujatikizya baalumi bankondo, nibajana choolwe chakuswaangana mulwizi, basaanguna kwiinda mumulongo wakukotamika akufwubaazya, mbuli kuvwundauka zitondezyo, zyakuti takuboneki kuti kuli luyandisisyo lupati lwamoyo aluyando lwabunyina pe. Mbuli mbukwakaswaangana mato aabazike, nkamboonzi, bali mukufwambaana loko, balatija kuzwa kuli umwi amweenzinyina mbuli mbukonzeka. Alimwi kuli ba Pirates, aawo nibajana choolwe chakuzabuka mafwuwa aabamwi, chivwulamabwe chakusanguna nchakuti—“Ino mitwe mingayi?”—mbubonya mbuli bazubi ba nswi mpati—“Migolo zyongaye?” Mpawo mubuzyo ooyu niwakaingulwa, basikubba balazwangana, nkaambo mbantu babyaabi kumabazu oonse obile, alimwi tabayandi kubona bubi bwamuntu umwi awumwi.

Pesi langa kuli Leza, uusyomeka, uutalibonyi, uusamausya beenzu, uuyandana abantu, ulaangulukide alimwi uutakatazyi pe! Ino sikuzuba nswi mpati njachita biyeni aawo naswaangana asikuzuba nswi umbi muchiindi chili kabotu? Ulijisi " Gam ," chintu chitazyibidwe pe kumaato amwi woonse zyakuti kunyina nibakamvwa zina eeli pe; mpawo kuti naa bamvwa, balamweta-mweta biyo, akwiinduluka zintu zisesemya aatala a "basikuvwundauka" a "basikujika mafwuta," azimwi zilimbuli kukankamuka kubotu. Nkaambo nzi kuti boonse basimakwebo-basambazi, alimwi abasikalumamba boonse ba Pirates a Man-of-War, abasikutanta mato aabazike, ncobakkomanina kulimvwa kusampaula kuli mato aa Whale; ooyu mubuzyo uunga ulakatazya kwiingula. Nkaambo, muchiimo chabasikubba, atwaambe kuti, inga ndayanda kuziba kuti naa mulimo wabo ulaabulemu buli boonse. Chimwi chiindi chimana mukutanta kutazyibidwe pe, masimpe; pesi buyo aawo mpaakali kukankaminwa. Alimwi kunze lyaboobo, eelyo muntu nasumpulwa munzila iitazyibidwe, tajisi ntalisyo yeelede yakusumpulwa kwakwe. Aboobo, ndamanizya, kuti mukulidunda kuti ulisumpukide kwiinda sikulwana nswi mpati, mukwaamba ooku sikubba nswi tajisi kaambo kasimide kakwiima.

Asi ino Gam ninzi? Ulakonzya kumana munwe wako wakutondeezya kweendeenda kuya mujulu akuseluka mumilongo yamabbuku aamabala, mpawo tokonzyi kujana bbala. Dr. Johnson kunyina naakasika aluzibo oolo; bwato bwa Noah Webster tabujisi pe. Nikuliboobo, eeli bbala ndyoonya lyakutondezya lino kwamyaka myiingi lyakali kubelesyegwa lyoonse akati kabantu bali zyuulu zili kkumi amusanu bazyalwa ncobeni ba Yankees. Masimpe, chiyanda bupanduluzi, mpawo cheelede kubikwa mubbuku lyamabala. Aamuzeezo ooyo, amuleke ndipandulule kabotu.
