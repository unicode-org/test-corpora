CHAANDANO 86. Muchila.

Bamwi basikweema bakalumbaizya liso litete lya antelope, amababa mabotu aabayuni bataboni pe; kutali kujulu, ndilasekelela muchila.

Kuyeeyela kuti mucila wa Sperm Whale mupati kwiinda boonse utalikila aawo mpougolela kusika abwaanda bwamuntu, ulaa busena bwaatala alikke, busena butasiki kumakkilomita aali makumi musanu. Mubili uuzungulukide wamuyanda wayo ulakomena kuba zibeela zyobile zipati, ziyumu, zipapalete naa zibeela, asyoonto-asyoonto zilatanta kusikila munsi aa inchi yomwe mubufwaafwi. Aawo mpokugolela naakuti aawo mpokuswaangana, ma fluke aaya alavwumbilila mbichani, mpawo azwa kuli umwi amweenzinyina mbuli mababa, akusiya busena bupati aakati. Taakwe chintu chipona muli mitwe yabubotu iipanduludwe kabotu kwiinda muminyinza iilibonya mbuli mwezi. Kukukomena kwayo kupati mu nswi mpati, mucila ulainda mamita aali makumi abili kuya mujulu.

Cibeela choonse chilibonya mbuli bulo bujisi nsinga ziyumu; pesi kogela muli ncico, mpawo ulajana kuti zibeela zyotatwe zyaandeene nzizyo zipanga:—atala, akati, aansi. Insinga zili atala azyaansi, zilamfu alimwi ziliimvwi; eezyo zyaakati, zifwiifwi loko, mpawo zyeenda munzila iiteendelani akataa zibeela zyaanze. Oobu bupange butatu, mbuli kufumbwa chintu, bupa manguzu kumuchila. Kumuntu uuyiiya maanda aakaindi aaku Loma, bbazu lyaakati liyoopa kukozyanya kupati amatayile aateteete lyoonse aacincanya abbwe muzintu eezyo zibotu zyakaindi, alimwi eezyo zitakwe kudooneka zigwasya kapati mukuyuma kwamabwe.

Asi mbuli kuti manguzu aaya mapati mumusela wa tendinous taakwe naakali kuzula pe, bunji bwa leviathan buli aamasusu aajisi nsinga zyanyama, eezyo ziinda kumabazu oonse obile aachibuno mpawo zyeenda ansi kuya muma flukes, ziswaangana ambabo, mpawo zigwasya kapati munguzu zyabo; kuchitila kuti mumusela nguzu zitaambiki zya whale yoonse zilibonya kuti zilibungene kusikila aawo. Ikuti naa kunyonyoonwa inga kwachitika, eechi nchintu cheelede kuchitwa.

Alimwi eezi—nguzu zyakwe zigambya, zilakonzya kulesya kweenda kwazyo; ooko buvwanda bwakuubauba nkobuzungaana kwiinda mu Titanism yanguzu. Kakuchili boobo, eezyo kweendeenda zijana bubotu bwazyo bupati kuzwa kuli ncico. Nguzu zini-zini kunyina nozinyonganya bubotu naa kumvwana, pele kanjikanji zilazipa; alimwi muzintu zyoonse zibotu loko, manguzu alaswaangene amasalamuzi. Gwisya nsinga zyaangidwe eezyo zyoonse zilibonya mbuli kuti zilapwasuka kuzwa ku bbwe lyamabwe mu Hercules, mpawo bubotu bwayo inga bwamana. Aawo Eckerman uusyomeka naakanyamuna cisani cha lineni kuzwa kumubili wa Goethe, wakazundwa achifuwa chipati chamwaalumi, eecho chakali kulibonya mbuli chikozyano chakuzunda chabana Loma. Eelyo Angelo naakapenta Leza Taata muchimo chamuntu, langa buyumuyumu buliwo. Alimwi kufumbwa ncobakonzya kuyubununa kujatikizya luyando lwabulemu mu Mwana, zifwanikiso zyaku Italy ziteteete, zivwungulukide, zya hermaphroditical, oomo muzeezo wakwe mowakazwidilila kapati; eezyi zifwanikiso, mbuli mbozitakwe busicamba boonse, tazitondezyi nguzu zili zyoonse, pele zili buyo zibyaabi, zyabunakazi zyakulibombya akukkazika moyo, eezyo mumaanza oonse zizuminwa, zipanga bubotu bulibedelede bwanjiisyo zyakwe.

Ooku nkuyuma kwacibeela ncondilanganya, kuti nikuba kuti chibelesegwa mumisobano, naa muchiyandisyo, naa mubukali, nikuba kuti chili mubukkale buli buti, kuzungaana kwacho kulatondezyegwa aluzyalo lupati. muli ncico kunyina janza lyamulengalenga likonzya kwiinda.

Kweendeenda kuli musanu kulibedelede kuli ncico. Chakusanguna, nikubelesyegwa mbuli chintu chakuya kunembo; Cabili, aawo noibelesyegwa mbuli nkoli munkondo; Chatatu, mukuyobola; Chachina, mukuzungaana; Cha musanu, mukuzwidilila.

Chakusanguna: Mbuli mboibede, mucila wa Leviathan ulabeleka munzila yiindene amicila yazilenge zyamulwizi. Tachizungaani pe. Mumuntu naakuti muswi, kuzungaana nchitondezyo chakutabikkila maanu. Ku nswi mpati, mucila wakwe njenzila ilikke iikonzya kweenda. Kuzungaana kuya kunembo munsi lyamubili, mpawo akujokela musule, nzizyo zipa kuti silunya azuze, akuzuza kuchinyama eecho nichidubbwa chakunyema. Mababa aakwe aali kumbali abelekela buyo kweenzya.

Cabili: Nchintu chipati, kuti nikuba kuti nswi imwi ilwana amwi nswi imbi amutwe amulomo, nokubaboobo, mukulwana kwakwe amuntu, ulabelesya muchila wakwe munzila yakusampaula. Mukuuma bwato, wakafwambaana kugolela kuzwa kuli mbubo, mpawo kuuma kwakachitwa biyo akujokela musule. Kuti yachitwa mumuwo uutakwe chisinkilizyo, ikapati kuti yaseluka kusikila aawo mpoigolela, ikuuma ooku takukonzyi kukazyigwa pe. Taakwe mbabo zyamuntu naa bwato zikonzya kuliyumya. Lufutuko lwako lulikke luli mukuchitantamuka; pesi kuti yasika kumbali kwiinda mumaanzi aambi, mpawo akaambo kakutanta kwabwaato bwa whale, akuyuma kwazibelesyo zyawo, mbaba iiteteete naakuti bbwe lityokede naa zyobile, musyobo wakusonka kumbali, nchintu chipati loko. Eezyi zilwanyo zyakumbali zili mumaanzi zilatambulwa chiindi chiingi mubusena bwakuzuba nswi, zyakuti zilabonwa kuti nkusobana buyo kwamwana. Umwi ulagwisya chibaki, mpawo mpako ilajalwa.

Chatatu: Tandikonzyi kuchitondezya, pesi kulindime, kulibonya kuti, mu nswi mpati iitegwa whale kulimvwa kwakuguma kuli mumusela; nkaambo mumakani aaya kuli bubotu muli ncico bukonzya kweezyanisyigwa buyo abubotu bwamubili wa nzovu. Ooku kubota kulabonwa kapati mumulimo wakusweeka, eelyo mububombe bwabusimbi nswi mpati ya whale akufwambaana kuzungaanya masusu aakwe mapati kuzwa kulubazu lumwi kuya kulubazu lumwi atalaa lwizi; mpawo kuti walimvwa buyo ndevu zyasikweenzya bwato, maawe kuli yooyo sikutanta bwato, ndevu azyoonse. Eelo kaka kuli buteteete mukuguma ooko kwakusaanguna! Kuti mucila ooyu nowakajisi nguzu zyakujata, nindali kunga ndayeeya nzovu ya Darmonodes eeyo yakali kujanika kumusika wamaluba, mpawo yakali kupa bana basimbi zintu zyakusama, mpawo yakali kubafwukatila. Kuli twaambo tunji, nchintu chipa kuusa kuti nswi iitegwa whale taijisi bubotu bwakujata mumucila wakwe; nkaambo ndakamvwa aatala amwi nzovu, eeyo niyakalichisa mukulwana, yakazyunguluka chikumba chayo mpawo yakagwisya musemo.

Chachina: Kubba nswi mpati kakunyina kuzyiba akati kalwizi luli alikke, ulamujana katakwe mpuwo kuzwa kubulemu bwakwe bupati, mpawo ulimbuli kkayi, ulasobana mulwizi mbuli kuti nduuwo. Asi ulakonzya kubona manguzu aakwe mukusobana kwakwe. Ziboko zyamuchila wakwe zipati zilazungaana mujulu; mpawo akuuma atala, kuzungaana kwamuzuzumo kwakamvwika kwamakkilomita manji. Ulakonzya kuyeeya kuti mfwulumende mpati yakazwa; mpawo kuti wabona busi buzwa ku spiracle kumamanino aakwe, inga wayeeya kuti oobo bwakali busi buzwa ku touch-hole.

Cha musanu: Mbuli muchiimo chakweendeenda cha leviathan, ma flukes alala munsi loko aamusana wakwe, mpawo taalibonyi pe munsi lyamaanzi; pesi aawo naakali afwaafwi kunjila mumaanzi, zilonda zyakwe zyoonse zili aamaulu aatasiki kumakumi otatwe aamubili wakwe zilazungaana mujulu, mpawo zilazumanana kuzungaana kwakaindi kaniini, kusikila zyakazwa ansi. Kusiya buyo kutyompokelwa kupati —kubusena bumbi bukonzya kupandululwa—ooku kuvwuuma kwa nswi zya whale ambweni ncecintu cipati kwiinda zyoonse zikonzya kubonwa muzilenge zyoonse zipona. Kuzwa mubunji butakwe mpo mucila mupati ulibonya mbuli kuti uli mukuzungaana kujulu kapati. Aboobo muziloto, sena ndakabona Saatani uulemekwa kagwisya nsingo yakwe mpati iipenzyegwa kuzwa mumulilo wa Baltic waku Gehena. Pesi mukulanga zintu ziliboobu, zyoonse zili mbuli mbomulimvwa; kuti mu Dantean, madaimona alaboola kuli nduwe; kuti muli ceeco ca Isaya, bangelo bapati. Ndakaimvwi amutwe wabwaato bwangu aawo zuba nolyakali kuzwa lyakali kusalala mujulu alwizi, ndakabona butanga bupati bwaba whale kujwe, boonse bakali kuya kuzuba, mpawo kwakaindi kaniini bakali kuzungaana antoomwe akuvwuuma. Mbuli mbocakalibonya kulindime achiindi eecho, kukomba baleza kupati kuliboobu tiikwakali kubonwa pe, nokuba muPersia, munzi wabakombi bamulilo. Mbuli Ptolemy Philopater mbwaakapa bumboni kujatikizya nzovu yaku Afulika, andime ndakapa bumboni kujatikizya nswi mpati, ndaamba kuti ngomuntu uulyaabide kwiinda zilenge zyoonse. Nkaambo kweendelanya aMwami Juba, nzovu zyabasikalumamba zyakaindi zyakali kusekelela mafwumofwumo amitunta yabo kaili mujulu mubuumba bupati.

Kweezyanisya kwachiindi chifwiifwi muchibalo eechi, aakati ka nswi iitegwa whale anzovu, kusikila kuli zimwi zibeela zyamuchila wamwi amubili wamwi, takweelede kubikka zizo eezyo zyobile ziimpene, mpawo asyoonto buyo zilenge nzyobajisi. Mbuli nzovu mpati mbuli nzovu mpati kuli Leviathan, mbuboobo, kweelanisigwa amuchila wa Leviathan, chisiko chakwe nchisiko buyo cha lily. Kuumwa kuyoosya kapati kuzwa kumubili wa nzovu kwakali mbuli kuuma kwachipekupeku, kweezyanisya akupwasuka kutakwe mweelwe kwa nswi mpati zya sperm whale, eezyo muziindi zyiingi zyakasowa mato oonse amabbiza ababelesi mujulu, mbubonya mbuli mu India uusobana mabbola aakwe.

*Nikuba kuti kweezyanisya koonse munzila yabupati akati ka nswi mpati a nzovu nchintu chitali kabotu pe, nkaambo muchiimo eecho nzovu iliimvwi munzila njiyonya mbuli mubwa mbwachita kunzovu; nikubaboobo, taakwe twaambo tumwi tuyandika kukozyanya; aakati kazeezi kuli mulomo. Chilizyibidwe kuti nzovu ilakonzya kuyobola maanzi naa bulongo mumubili wayo, mpawo akuyisumpula, akuyisowela mumulonga.

Mbondiyaabulanga-langa mucila ooyu mupati, mbondiyaabuusa akaambo kakutakonzya kwangu kuutondezya. Chimwi chiindi kuli zitondezyo muli ncico, eezyo, nikuba kuti zilakonzya kubotezya janza lyamuntu, zilazumanana kutapandululwa pe. Mubutanga bupati, zigambya loko, chimwi chiindi, nzitondezyo zyamaseseke, zyakuti ndakamvwa bavwimi aabo bakaamba kuti zili mbuli zitondezyo azitondezyo zya Free-Mason; kuti nswi mpati, masimpe, kwiinda munzila eezyi yakabandika anyika. Alimwi kunyina kweenda kumbi kwa nswi mpati mumubili wakwe woonse, kuzwide zintu zitazyibidwe, alimwi kutakonzyi kulingula kuli sikulwana wakwe uucibwene kapati. Amumukosole mbuli mbondikonzya, mpawo, ndilanjila mukati loko; Tandimuzyi pe, alimwi tandikoomuzyi pe. Asi kuti nsezi nokuba muchila wa nswi eyi, ino mbuti mbondikonzya kumvwisisisya mutwe wakwe? kwiinda waawo, ino mbuti mbomukonzya kumvwisisisya busyu bwakwe, kakuli busyu mbwatajisi? Uyoobona musule wangu, muchila wangu, mbuli mbwaamba, asi busyu bwangu tabukabonwi pe. Pesi tandikonzyi kuziba kabotu zibeela zyakwe zyakusule; mpawo nditondezye nzyayanda kujatikizya busyu bwakwe, ndaamba alimwi kuti tajisi busyu pe.





CHAANDANO 87. Nkamu mpati yabasikalumamba.

Nsumbu ndamfu alimwi nsyoonto ya Malacca, iitola kumusanza lwaajwe kuzwa kuzisi zya Birmah, njiipanga busena bwakumusanza kwiinda yoonse ya Ezhiya. Mumulongo uuzumanana kuzwa kunsumbu eeyo kuli nsumbu zilamfu zya Sumatra, Java, Bally, antoomwe a Timor; eezyo, antoomwe azimwi zyiingi, zipanga lwizi lupati, naakuti bwaanda, buswaanganya Ezhiya aAustralia, akwaandaanya lwizi lwa Indian kuzwa kuzilundu zizwide zintu zyakujwe. Eeyi mpako ilibikkidwe amasena manji aakulyookezyela kuchitila kuti mato ama whale agwasigwe; zilibonya akati kazyeezyo nzibeela zya Sunda a Malacca. Kumbali aalwizi lwa Sunda, ikapati, mato aakali kuya ku China kuzwa kujwe, azwa mulwizi lwa China.

Milonga eeyo mifwaafwi ya Sunda ilaandaanya Sumatra kuzwa ku Java; mpo kaimvwi akati-kati kabwaanda bupati bwansumbu, kazikwabilidwe achilundu chibotu, chizyibidwe kuli basimaanzi kuti Java Head; tazyeendelani pe amulyango waakati-kati uujalula bwami bupati bujisi bulambo: mpawo notulanga buvubi butamani bwa tununkilizyo, a silika, a mabwe mayandisi, a ngolide, a nzovu, eezyo nsumbu zili zyuulu zya lwizi lwa kujwe nkozivwubidwe, kuboneka kuti nchintu chipati loko, kuti lubono luliboobu, kwiinda mukupangwa kwa nyika, lweelede kuba asyoonto kulibonya, nikuba kuti takugwasyi pe, kwakukwabililwa kuzwa kunyika yakujwe iijisi zyoonse. Nkomwe zya Straits of Sunda tazikwe maanda aayo aakwabilila milyango ya Mediterranean, Baltic, a Propontis. Kwiindana aba Denmark, aaba bantu bakujwe tabayandi bulemu bwakusekelela ntalisyo zyaatala kuzwa kunzila iitamani yamato aayinda guwo, alo kwamyaanda yamyaka yainda, masiku asyikati, akali kwiinda akati kansumbu zya Sumatra a Java, kazijisi zintu zidula kapati zyakujwe. Asi nikuba kuti balaleka kuchita chiyanza chili mbuli echeechi, tabakaki pe kuti bayanda kubbadela mutelo uusimide.

Kakwiindide chiindi, basibbivwe ba Malay, bakali kuyubila akati kazilundu zya Sumatra, bakazwa mumaato aakali kweenda mumilonga, kabayanda kuti bapegwe mutelo kumasumo aabo. Nikuba kuti akaambo kakusubulwa kwabulowa nkobakatambula kuzwa kumaanza aaba European cruisers, busicamba bwaba corsairs bwakasinkililwa; pesi, nikuba sunu, chimwi chiindi tulamvwa aatala abwato bwaba England aba Amelika, oobo, mumaanzi aayo, bwakatanta akubbidwa kakunyina kuusa.

Kakuli guwo libotu, libotu, Pequod lino lyakali afwaafwi kusika kumasena aaya; Ahabu wakakanzide kwiinda muli mbabo kuya mulwizi lwa Javan, mpawo kuzwa aawo, katanta bwato kuya kunyika, atala aamaanzi aazyibidwe kuti alajanika aawa aawo a Sperm Whale, mpawo wakasika kunkomwe ya Philippine Islands, mpawo wakasika kunkomwe yaku Japani, muchiindi chakuzuba nswi mpati ooko. Kwiinda munzila eezyi, bwato bwa Pequod bwakali kuzyunguluka bwato bwakali kukonzya kuyobola masena oonse aazyibidwe aa Sperm Whale munyika yoonse, kaatanasika a Line mu Pacific; ooko Ahabu, nikuba kuti koonse koonse kwakaalilwa mukutobela kwakwe, wakasyoma kuti ulakonzya kulwana Moby Dick, mulwizi nkwaakali kuzibinkene kuunka; alubo muchiindi nchakonzya kuyeeyelwa kuti ulikuchipenzya.

Asi ino mbuti lino? mukuyandaula ooku, sena Ahabu tanaakajata nyika? sena babelesi bakwe banywa muuya? Masimpe, ulaima kuyobola maanzi. Peepe. Kwachiindi chilamfu, lino, zuba lizuzaana mukati ka mulilo wakwe, mpo taliyandi pe chakulya pesi chili muli nguwe. Aboobo Ahabu. Kolemba eechi, awalo, mu whale. Aawo amwi mabbiza alizwide zintu zyabantu bazwa kuzisi zimbi, kuti zitolwe kumasena aambi; bwato bwa whale buzyunguluka nyika yoonse tabujisi zintu pele buyo nguwe ababelesi, zilwanyo zyabo azintu nzyobayanda. Ulijisi zintu zili mukati kazyiba lyoonse zyakabikkidwe mumabbodela mubbodela lyakwe lipati. Ulijisi zintu zyakubelesya; kutali zyoonse antoomwe a ngulube-lead a kentledge zitabelesyegwi pe. Ulijisi maanzi aamyaka myiingi muli nguwe. Maanzi aa Nantucket aasalala; eeyo, nikwakainda myaaka yotatwe, Nantucketer, muPacific, yakayandisya kunywa kakutanaba maanzi aamunyo, pesi jilo yakazwa mumabbodela, kuzwa kumilonga yakuPeru naa yakuIndiya. Aboobo mbocibede, kuti, nokuba kuti mato aambi alakonzya kuti akaunka ku China kuzwa ku New York, akujokela alimwi, akusika kumato manji, bwato bwa whale, muciindi coonse eeco, inga tiibwakabona kabeela komwe kanyika; basikweenzya bwato bakwe kabatanabona muntu pele buyo basikweenzya bwato batanta mbuli mbabo. aboobo wakabatola mulumbe wakuti zambangulwe limbi lyakasika; bakali kwiingula biyo—“Mboobu, balombwana, aawa mpali bwato!”

Lino, mbuli ba Sperm Whales banji mbobakajatwa kunkomwe yakujwe ya Java, afwaafwi a Straits of Sunda; masimpe, mbuli bunji bwanyika, kuzinguluka, bwakali kuzyibwa abasikuzuba nswi kuti mbusena bubotu bwakutanta bwato; aboobo, mbuli Pequod mbwaakali kuyaabuvwula a Java Head, basikulangilila bakalumbaizyigwa cakwiinduluka-induluka, akucenjezyegwa kuti kabasinsimuka. Asi nikuba kuti mabwe aasalala pyu aanyika akafwambaana kuboneka kulubazu lwakumusanza, mpawo amapunga aakkomene sinamoni mupya wakafweba mumuwo, pesi taakwe ndeke yomwe yakaambwa. Aafwaafwi kusiya mizeezo yoonse yakuwida mumusobano uuli woonse aawa, bwato bwakali afwaafwi kunjila mumaanzi, aawo kulila kwakusekelela kwakamvwika kuzwa mujulu, mpawo kakutanainda chiindi chilamfu kwakatutambula chisobano chabulemu.

Pesi aawa mpokugolela, kuti akaambo kakubeleka changuzu nchobakavwima muchiindi chifwiifwi chiinda mulwizi lone, Inswi zya Sperm Whales, muciindi chakweenda mumaanzi masyoonto, mbuli musule, lino zilajanika mumatanga mapati, chimwi chiindi kabajata nkamu mpati loko, zyakuti inga kwaboneka mbuli kuti mazina manji ngaakakonkezyegwa chizuminano chakugwasyanya akukwabilila. Kubunganya ooku kwa Sperm Whale muzibeela zipati zili boobu, inga kwaambwa kuti nokuba mumasena mabotu aakutanta bwato, lino zimwi ziindi mulakonzya kutanta bwato kwamvwiki amyezi antoomwe, kakunyina kutambula mulomo omwe; mpawo mpawo mpawo watambula bantu bamwi balibonya mbuli kuti bali zyuulu azyuulu.

Kulampa kumabbuwa oonse obile, musinzo wamakkilomita obile naa otatwe, mpawo kachipanga chibeela chipati, chakavwumbilide chibeela chamulengalenga, nketani yama whale-jets akali kusobana akumweka-mweka mumuwo wamazuba. Kakutali mbuli ma twin-jets aa Right Whale, aayo, aaandaana atala, awida mumitabi yobile, mbuli mitabi ya willow, mulomo omwe uulangide kunembo wa Sperm Whale utondeezya chisaka chilamfu chitete, chizumanana kuya mujulu akuseluka.

Kubonwa kuzwa abwaato bwa Pequod, mpawo, mbuli mbwaakali kunga watanta acilundu chilamfu chalwizi, eeyi nkamu yamapopwe aajisi busi, yomwe-yomwe yakali kuvwunguluka mumulengalenga, mpawo yakabonwa kwiinda mumulengalenga uusanganizidwe wamuwo uusalala pyu, yakali kutondezya mbuli zyuulu zyama chimney aakkomanisya aamudolopo limwi lipati, lyakapandululwa mbuli mafwumofwumo aacilimo, amuntu umwi uutanta bbiza.

Mbuli basilumamba nibakali kweenda kabaswena afwaafwi abusena butali kabotu mumalundu, kabafwambaanya kweenda kwabo, boonse kabayandisya kubikka nzila eeyo iiyoosya kusule lyabo, mpawo kabayungizya lubo mubukwabilizi bweelene abwaanda; nikuba boobo nkamu mpati yaba whale lino yakali kulibonya kuti ilafwambaana kuya kunembo kwiinda mumilonga; asyoonto-asyoonto kabavwundauka mababa aa semicircle yabo, akudubbwa kuya kumbele, mucibaka comwe ciyumu, pele cicili akati ka mwezi.

Kazwide mabbiza woonse, Pequod yakabatobela; basikuuma nkoli kabajata zilwanyo zyabo, akusekelela kapati kuzwa kumitwe yamaato aabo aakali kwiimvwi. Kuti guwo nolyakajatilila, kakunyina kudooneka kuti, eeyo yakatandaanya kwiinda mu Straits of Sunda, basilumamba banji bakali kunga baunkila buyo mulwizi lwa Oriental kuti bakabone kujatwa kwabantu basyoonto akati kabo. Alimwi nguni wakali kukonzya kwaamba kuti, mukabunga aaka kakabungene, Moby Dick lwakwe mwini ulakonzya kutadubbwa kwakaindi kaniini, mbuli nzovu iituba iikombwa mumulongo wakubikkwa kwamwami waba Siamese! Aboobo a stun-sail yakabikkidwe a stun-sail, twakatanta bwato, katukwela ma leviathan aaya kunembo lyesu; aawo, mpawo, jwi lya Tashtego lyakamvwika, lyakali kusololela mizeezo kulichimwi chintu twakali kulangila.

"Mabbiza! Mabbiza!" wakavwiyaula loko, wakajata nkoli—“amujate mato aanu, akujatisya myuuya yanu, lino! Leza wangu, nobaalumi, amuzumanane! Amumutandane, nywebo Queequeg—nswi mpati iili awo!—amumuume!—amuume! Amunyampuke—amwiime, alimwi amukkale boobo! Cilimo, nobaalumi—amukwelele, nobaalumi; mutabikkili maanu kumusana wabo—mubafwuse!”

Bwaato lino bwakajatikizyigwa aakati kazyintu zyobile zipati zisiya, akusiya Dardanelles nsyoonto aakati kabwaato bwazyo bulamfu. Pesi kwiinda mukusoleka changuzu twakasika kumamanino muchiindi chifwiifwi; mpawo akuleka chakufwambaana, mpawo aawo mpawo kakulangilila chakumaninina kuti kuli nzila imbi yakuzwa. Musule akutija ziindi zyiingi zili mbuli masusu, twakafwambaana kunjila muli ceeco cakali buyo chimwi chazizyungulukide zyaanze, asi lino chakazabuka nswi mpati, zyoonse zyakali kuchita kuti kube busena bomwe. Ooku kufutulwa kwacoolwe kwakaulwa akubula mali manji kwiinda mukusweekelwa chikumba cha Queequeg, walo, naakaliimvwi mumabbuwa kuti akaume nswi mpati, wakagwisigwa chikumba chakwe kuzwa kumutwe wakwe akaambo kakuzungaana kwamuwo kwakachitwa akusowelwa kwamaluba obile afwaafwi.

Mbuli manyongwe akubula luumuno mbuli manyongwe munyika yoonse mbocakabede lino, chakafwambaana kulisanduka kuba chintu chakali kulibonya mbuli kuti nchintu chibikkidwe kabotu; nkaambo nibakabungene antoomwe kumamanino mumubili omwe, mpawo bakapilusya kuuluka kwabo kabajisi nguzu zyakutanta. Kuzumanana kutobela tiikwakali kugwasya pe; pesi mato akachili kuzumanana kuchitila kuti abweze nswi mpati zyakali kukonzya kuwisigwa kusule, mpawo akukwabilila yomwe eeyo Flask njaakajaya akuleka. I waif ninsamu iijisi penon, yobile naa yotatwe ilanyangwa abwaato buli boonse; alimwi eezyo, aawo banyama bambi nobaswena afwaafwi, zilabikkwa mumubili wamulwizi uufwide, zyoonse zyobile kuti zitondezye busena bwazyo mulwizi, alimwi akuti zitondezye kuti wakalijisi kale, ikuti naa mato abwato bumbi bwaswena afwaafwi.

Ikuzwa mukucheesya ooku kwakali kutondezya kaambyo kabusongo mu Fishery,—nswi zyama whale zyiingi nswi nsyoonto. Akati kama whale oonse aakapegwa misamu, omwe buyo wakajatwa. Bamwi boonse bakakanza kutija kwachiindi eecho, pesi bakatolwa buyo, mbuli mbokuyoobonwa kumbele, abwato bumbi kunze lya Pequod.





CHAANDANO 88. Zikolo abapati bazikolo.

Chaandano chakayinda chakapa makani aajatikizya mubili mupati naakuti butanga bwaba Sperm Whales, mpawo kwakapegwa kaambo kakali kukonzya kupa kuti kube kubungana ooku kupati.

Lino, nikuba kuti mibili mipati iliboobu ilaswaangana chimwi chiindi, pesi, mbuli mbokweelede kuti kwabonwa, nokuba mazubaano, tubunga tuniini twaandaanizyidwe tulabonwa chimwi chiindi, katujisi bantu bali makumi obile kusikila kumakumi musanu umwi awumwi. Nkamu ziliboobu zilizyibidwe kuti zikolo. Kanjaanji zili mumisyobo yobile; eezyo zipangidwe abamakaintu, mpawo eezyo zitakwe pe pele buyo baalumi baniini, naakuti ŋombe, mbuli mbobazibidwe.

Mukujanika kwabasikalumamba kuchikolo chabanakazi, lyoonse ulabona mwaalumi mupati, pesi katachembede pe; ooyo, aawo nayoowa, utondeezya busicamba bwakwe kwiinda mukuwida musule akuvwumba kutija kwabanakazi bakwe. Mubwini, oyu mwaalumi mubotu ngu Ottoman uulemekwa, uzuzaana atalaa nyika yamaanzi, kazingulukidwe aluumbulizyo loonse aluyandisisyo lwa harem. Kusiyana aakati ka Ottoman ooyu abamakaintu bakwe bakwe kulakankamanisya; nkaambo, nikuba kuti lyoonse uli abulemu bupati, bamakaintu, nikuba kuti bakomena, tabaindili achisela chamwaalumi uuli akati-akati. Nchintu chiteteete loko, masimpe; Ndaamba kuti, kutainda acisela chama yaadi aali kkumi aabili kuzyunguluka chiwuno. Nikuliboobo, takukonzyi kukazigwa pe, kuti mubwini balaa nguzu zyakutambula en bon point .

Nchintu chikankamanisya loko kweebela harem eeyi asimalelo wayo mukweendeenda kwabo kwabufwubafwuba. Mbuli zintu zyakusama, zilazumanana kweenda mukuyandaula zintu ziindene-indene. Ulaswaanganya aMuzila muchiindi chamaluba aazwide aachiindi chakulya chaEquatorial, mpawo wajokela, ambweni, kuzwa kukukkala muchiindi chachilimo mulwizi lwakunyika, mpawo akubeja chiindi chachilimo chakukatala akupya koonse kutali kabotu. Aawo nibakamana kukkala ansi akuseluka mumugwagwa wa Equator, batalika kuya kumaanzi aakujwe kabalangila chiindi chakutontola ooko, aboobo balatantamuka kupya kupati kwamwaka.

Aawo naakali kuya kunembo mulweendo oolu, ikuti naa kuli zintu zitali kabotu zibonwa, simalelo wangu wa whale ulalanga kabotu mukwasyi wakwe uukkomanisya. Kuti naa kuli umwi uukonzya kunyonganya Leviathan muniini kuboola munzila eeyo, ulakonzya kuswena munsi-munsi amuntu umwi wabamakaintu, abukali bupati Bashaw mbwamuuma, akumutandaanya! Ziindi zisumpukide, masimpe, kuti bana batakwe njiisyo mbuli nguwe bazumizigwa kunjila mubusalali bwalukkomano lwamuŋanda; nikuba kuti wakachita nzyayanda Bashaw, takonzyi kukwabilila Lothario uuzyibidwe kapati kuti azwe abulo bwakwe; nkaambo, maawe! nswi zyoonse zili antoomwe. Mbuli kunkomwe yalwizi, bamakaintu aaba kanjikanji balachita mazwanga mapati aakati kabakkomanina babo; mbubwenya buyo abaswi, aabo chimwi chiindi baboola kunkondo iijaya, zyoonse nkaambo kaluyando. Zilakwabilila amilomo yabo milamfwu, chimwi chiindi balazijala antoomwe, aboobo balasolekesya kuba anguzu mbuli nswi zya elk eezyo zilwana ameja aabo. Tabali basyoonto bajatwa kabajisi zilonda zipati zyakuswaangana ooku,—mitwe iiteteete, meno aatyokede, mababa aatyokede; alimwi muziindi zimwi, milomo yakatyoka akuzwa.

Pesi kuti sikulwana lukkomano lwamuŋanda walifwutuka aawo simalelo wa harem naakatalika kuzuza, mpawo nchintu chibi loko kulangilila simalelo oyo. Mubombe moyo wakabikka bupati bwakwe akati kabo alimwi mpawo wakasekelela aawo kwakaindi kaniini, kachili afwaafwi amulombwana Lothario, mbuli Solomoni uulemeka Leza uukomba cakulipeda akati kabamakaintu bakwe bali zyuulu. Kuzumizya zimwi nswi kuti ziboneke, bazubi banswi tabakonzyi kutandaanya umwi waba Grand Turks; nkaambo aaba ba Grand Turks bali anguzu zyabo zyiingi, aboobo kubula mafuta kwabo nkusyoonto. Kujatikizya bana basankwa abasimbi mbobazyala, nkaambo nzi, aabo bana basankwa abasimbi beelede kulilanganya lwabo beni; asyoonto, alugwasyo lwabanyina luzutu. Mbuli bamwi bayandisya kweendeenda balya zintu zyoonse bakonzya kwaambwa zina, Mwami wangu Whale tayandi pe busena bwakukomezya bana, nikuba kuti ulaa chiyandisyo cha bower; aboobo, mbwali mweenzu mupati, wasiya bana bakwe batazyibidwe nyika yoonse; mwana uli oonse uliindene. Muchiindi cheelede, nikubaboobo, mbuli busungu bwabukubusi mbobuyaansi; mbuli myaka akusowa mboziyaabukomena; mbuli kuyeeya mbokwapa kuti alyookezye; mubufwiifwi, mbuli kubula nguzu mbokwainda mu Turk uukkutide; mpawo luyando lwakuubauba abubotu lulanjila mubusena bwaluyando lwabasimbi; Ottoman wesu wanjila mubuumi bwakubula nguzu, kweempwa, akulaya, wakakonkezya, wakamwaya ŋanda yabantu bavwubide, mpawo wakakomena kuba muntu uulemekwa, wakaba aamoyo mubotu, wakaunka alikke akati kama meridians a parallels kaamba mipailo yakwe, akuchenjezya Leviathan umwi awumwi kuzwa kukulubizya kwakwe kwaluyando.

Lino, mbuli ŋanda yaba whale mboiitwa abasikuzuba nswi kuti chikolo, mbubonya mbuli simalelo asimalelo wachikolo eecho uuzyibidwe kuti mweendelezi wachikolo. Aboobo tacili cintu ciyumu, nokuba kuti ncintu cisesemya, kuti naakamana kuya kucikolo lwakwe mwini, weelede kuunka kunze aacisi kutali kuyiisya nzyaakaiya ooko, pele bufwubafwuba bwazyo. Zina lyakwe lyakuti, mwiiyi wachikolo, lilakonzya kuboneka kuti lyakazwa kuzina lyakapegwa ku harem, asi bamwi bayeeya kuti mwaalumi wakasaanguna kupa zina lyamusyobo ooyu wa Ottoman whale, weelede kuti wakabala bbuku lya Vidocq, mpawo wakalizibya kuti wakali mwiiyi wachikolo wakumuunzi oyo muntu uuzyibidwe kapati waku France muchiindi chakwe, alimwi ino nziiyo nzi nzyaakaiya wakayiisya bamwi bana bakwe.

Kukkala alikke akuliimya ooko nswi mpati yachikolo nkwalijana muminyaka yakwe yakukomena, nkwamasimpe kuli ba Sperm Whale boonse bachembaala. Aafwaafwi koonse koonse, nswi iitegwa lone whale—mbuli Leviathan ilikke mbwayiitwa—itondezya kuti ninsiku. Mbuli Daniel Boone uulemekwa uujisi ndevu zya moss, taakwe muntu uyooba afwaafwi anguwe pele buyo Chilengedwe; mpawo wamubweza kuti abe mukaintu wakwe munkanda yamaanzi, mpawo ngomukaintu mubotu kwiinda boonse, nikuba kuti ulabamba maseseke manji.

Zikolo zitakwe naba omwe pele buyo baalumi baniini alimwi bajisi nguzu, zyaambwa musyule, zipa kwiindana kupati azikolo zya harem. Nkaambo nikuba kuti nswi eezyo nkazi zilayoowa, nswi zyabaalumi, naakuti nziba zyamabwe aali makumi one, mbuli mbobaziita, nzizyo zilwana kwiinda nswi zyoonse, alimwi nzizyo ziyoosya kapati kuswaangana anzizyo; kunze lya nswi eezyo zigambya zili amutwe uusalala pyu, zimwi ziindi zilaswaangana, mpawo eezyi zilakulwana mbuli banyama babi bachisidwe abulwazi bwa gout.

Zikolo zya Forty-barrel-bull nzipati kwiinda zikolo zya harem. Mbuli nkamu yabakubusi, balizwide kulwana, kusekelela, abubi, kabazungaana munyika yoonse munzila iitakwe maanu, iikonzya kuzungaana, cakuti taakwe sikulemba mali uucenjede uukonzya kubakwabilila mbuli mbwakonzya kuchita kumwana musankwa uuchita manyongwe ku Yale naa ku Harvard. Bakafwambaana kuleka manyongwe aaya, mpawo aawo zyuulu zyotatwe muli zyone nibakakomena, balaandaana, mpawo baunka kuyooyandaula busena bwakukkala, nkokuti, ma harems.

Acimbi cintu cipa kuti kube kwiindana akati kazikolo zyabasankwa abasimbi nkwiinda mukulanga-langa baalumi abanakazi. Atwaambe kuti wauma ŋombe yamabbola aali makumi one—dyabooli mucete! beenzinyina boonse bakamusiya. Pesi kouma umwi wachikolo cha harem, mpawo beenzinyina baladubbwa kumuzinguluka kabajisi penzi lyoonse, chimwi chiindi balakkala afwaafwi anguwe kwachiindi chilamfu, mbuli kuti mbabo beni balakonzya kufwa.
