Lino eeyi ambergris nchintu chikankamanisya loko, alimwi nchintu chiyandikana loko mbuli chintu chamakwebo, zyakuti mu 1791 umwi wakazyalilwa ku Nantucket Captain Coffin wakabuzyigwa-buzyigwa kubbaala lya England House of Commons aatala a makani aaya.
