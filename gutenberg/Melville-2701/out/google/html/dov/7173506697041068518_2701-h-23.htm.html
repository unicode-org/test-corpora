Lino muchiindi cheelede abusena, musule alweendo lwakusaanguna lwachiindi chilamfu, Ahabu,—maanzi woonse aakuzuba nswi mpati—wakali kulibonya kuti wakatandaanya sinkondonyina mulwizi, kuti amujaye kabotu-kabotu ooko; lino, kuti wakajana kuti wakali muyumu aawo mpaakali kuchisa chilonda chakwe; lino mbwaanga bwato bwakaambwa oobo buzuba bwakainda bwakaswaangana a Moby Dick;—alimwi lino mbwaanga miswaangano yakwe yoonse yakatobelana amaato aasiyene-siyene yakazuminana kutondezya kutabikkila maanu kwabadaimona ooko nswi iitegwa white whale nkwaakakosola basikuvwima bakwe, nikuba kuti wakabisya naa wakabisizya; lino kwakali chintu chakali mumeso aamucembele, eecho chakali kukatazya kubantu batakwe nguzu kubona. Mbuli nyenyezi ya polar iitazungaani, eeyo mubusiku bwa myezi iili cisambomwe iipona, ya arctic, ilazumanana kulanga kwayo kubotu, kutazungaani, akati; aboobo makanze aa Ahabu lino akali kumweka-mweka aawo akati kamasiku aabasimilimo bakali kuusa. Yakali kweendelezya atala lyabo, zyakuti zyoonse nzyobakali kuyeeya, kudooneka, kudooneka, kuyoowa, zyakali kukonzya kuyuba munsi lyamyuuya yabo, mpawo tiizyakali kuzwa sumo naa tuzunda.

Muchiindi eechi chakutondezya kunembo, kuseka koonse, kwakusinikizyigwa naa kwacilengwaleza, kwakamana. Stubb tanaakachili kusoleka pe kumweta-mweta; Starbuck tanaakachili kusola pe kulanga yomwe. Mbubwenya buyo, lukkomano abuumba, bulangilizi akuyoowa, zyakali kulibonya mbuli kuti zyakagaya kusikila kubulongo bubotu, mpawo zyakabikwa, muchindi eecho, mumusemu wakajatikizyidwe wamoyo wabutale wa Ahabu. Mbuli minchina, bakali kweendeenda kakunyina kwaambaula, kabazyi kuti liso lyamucembele lyakali kuli mbabo.

Asi wakamulanga-langa loko muchiindi chakwe chamaseseke; aawo naakayeeya kuti taakwe kulanga pesi komwe kwakali kulinguwe; mpawo nowakabona kuti nikuba kuti meso aaAhabu akali kugambya ababelesi, kulanga kwa Parsee kutamvwisisika kwakagambya kwakwe; naakuti munzila imwi, munzila imwi iitali kabotu, chimwi chiindi yakachijatikizya. Kuyungizigwa kuliboobu, kutamvwisisisya kwakatalika kubikka Fedallah mutete lino; kuzungaana kutamani kwakamuzungaanya; kuti baalumi aaba bakali kumulanga cakudooneka; kakunyina kusinizya, mbuli mbukwakalibonya, kuti naa wakali chintu chifwa, naakuti wakali mweenzu uuzungaana wakabikkwa abwaato amubili wamuntu umwi uutabonwi. Mpawo chimvwule eecho chakali kuzungaana aawo. Nkaambo kutali masiku, nokuba, Fedallah naakazyibidwe kuti ula koona, naa kuya munsi. Wakali kwiima kwachiindi chilamfu: pesi tanaakali kukkala naa kusendama pe; meso aakwe aatakwe nguzu pesi aagambya akaamba antangalala kuti—Swebo tobili balindizi tatulyookezyi pe.

Alimwi, kufumbwa chiindi, masiku naa sikati basikweenzya bwato tiibakali kukonzya kutanta bwato, kunze kwakuti Ahabu wakali kunembo lyabo; naa kwiima mumulyango wakwe, naa kweenda kabotu-kabotu amapulanga aakati kamigaano yobile iitazungaani,—nkomo mpati a mizen; naakuti bakamubona kaimvwi mukaanda,—kuulu kwakwe kupona kwakaunkila kunembo abwaato, mbuli kuti wakali kuyanda kweenda; nkapu yakwe yakavwumbilide loko atala aameso aakwe; aboobo nikuba kuti wakaliimvwi katazungaani, nikuba kuti mazuba amasiku akayungizyigwa, tanaakazungaana muchibbeeke chakwe; pesi kabasisikide munsi lyachikobela eecho, teebakakonzya pe kuzyiba kuti, nkaambo kazeezi zyoonse, meso aakwe akali kujalwa ncobeni chimwi chiindi; naa kuti wakachili kuzilanga loko; kunyina makani, nikuba kuti wakaima muchibbeeke kwawoola lyomwe, mpawo busi bwamasiku butabikkilwi maanu bwakabungene mumitwe yamume aachibaki eecho chakabezegwa amabwe. Zizwaato nzyaakajisi masiku, mumuni wazuba wakayuma alinguwe; aboobo, buzuba abuzuba, amasiku amasiku; tanaakachili kuya munsi lyamapulanga; kufumbwa nchaakali kuyanda kuzwa mukaanda eecho chintu nchaakali kutumina.

Wakalya mumulengalenga nguwenya; nkokuti, zyakulya zyakwe zibili zilikke,—chakulya chakuseni achakulya chamansiku: chakulya chamansiku tanaakajata pe; nokuba kutebula ndevu zyakwe; eeyo yakakomena mumudima yoonse, mbuli miyanda yazisamu yakavwukkulwa, eeyo yakakomena kakunyina kuchita chintu, nikuba kuti yakafwa mujulu. Asi nikuba kuti buumi bwakwe boonse bwakaba wachi yomwe buyo; alimwi nikuba kuti chiindi cha Parsee chakulindila chakanyina kulyookezya mbuli chakwe mwini; pesi aaba bobile tiibakali kulibonya kuti balaambuula—umwi muntu kuli umwi—kunze kwakuti muchiindi chilamfu kaambo kamwi katali kabotu kakapa kuti kuyandika. Nikuba kuti chikozyano changuzu chakali kulibonya kuti chakaswaanganya aaba bobile; antangalala, alimwi kubabelesi bakagambwa, bakali kulibonya mbuli kuti bakali kwaandaana. Kuti masyikati bakajana choolwe chakwaamba jwi lyomwe; masiku, baalumi batakwe maanu bakali boonse bobile, mbuli mbukwakabede kukanana asyoonto. Chimwi chiindi, kwamahola malamfu, kakwiina chivwulamabwe, bakali kwiima kabaandaanizyidwe mumumuni wanyenyeezi; Ahabu muchibbeeke chakwe, Parsee abwaato bupati; pesi kabalangide umwi amweenzinyina; mbuli kuti mu Parsee Ahabu wakabona mweenzu wakwe wakasowelwa, muli Ahabu mu Parsee wakasiya lubono lwakwe.

Alimwi, munzila imwi, wakachita Ahabu—mubuumi bwakwe mwini, mbuli buzuba abuzuba, awoola, achiindi choonse, mbwaakali kuyubununwa kuli baabo bakali munsi lyakwe,—Ahabu wakali kulibonya mbuli simalelo uulyaandaanya; mu Parsee pesi muzike wakwe. Alimwi boonse bobile bakali kulibonya kuti bakaswaanganizyigwa antoomwe, mpawo silunya uutabonwi wakali kubasololela; mumvwula uuteteete uuli kumbali lyambambo njumu. Nkaambo kufumbwa mbwakonzya kuba Parsee, mbabo zyoonse zyakali Ahabu uusimide.

Kuchiindi chakusanguna kupya kwabucedo, jwi lyakwe lyabutale lyakamvwika kuzwa kusule,—“Muntu uutegwa mast-heads!”—alimwi buzuba boonse, kusikila zuba lyakabbila alimwi nikwakainda kumangolezya, jwi ndyoonya eelyo lyoonse, aawo bbela lyasikweenzya bwato nolyakali kulila, lyakali kumvwika—“Ncinzi ncomubona?—cibosya!!”

Asi nikwakainda mazuba otatwe naa one, nikwakamana kuswaangana a Rachel wakali kuyandaula bana; alimwi taakwe mulomo wakabonwa pe; mwaalumi uchembede uuyandisya zintu zilikke wakali kulibonya kutasyoma kusyomeka kwababelesi bakwe; asyoonto, akati kabantu boonse kunze lyabasikwiimba bachikombelo; wakali kulibonya kuti uladooneka, nokuba, kuti naa Stubb a Flask inga tiibakali kuyanda kubona ncaakali kuyandaula. Asi kuti eezyi nzyaakali kudooneka zyakali zyakwe ncobeni, wakakaka kwaambuula amajwi, nikuba kuti nzyaakali kuchita zyakali kukonzya kulibonya kuti zyakali kutondezya.

"Ndiyoobona nswi mpati ndemwini," - wakaamba. "Iyi! Ahabu weelede kuba a doubloon!" mpawo amaanza aakwe mwini wakabamba chisaka chamabbomba; mpawo wakatuma janza lyakwe mujulu, achibulo chomwe chakakosolwa, kuti ajatisye kumutwe wamusamu mupati, wakatambula mamanino obile aantambo yakali kuyaansi; mpawo wakabikka chimwi achibbokesi chakwe wakabambila pini kumamanino aambi, kuchitila kuti chijatilile kumugwagwa. Eezi zyakachitika, kajisi mamanino mujanza lyakwe mpo kaimvwi kumbali aapini, wakalanga kuzyunguluka kuli basimilimo bakwe, kabakunkumuna kuzwa kuli umwi kuya kuli umwi; wakaimika kulanga kwakwe kwachiindi chilamfu kuli Daggoo, Queequeg, Tashtego; pesi kutantamuka Fedallah; mpawo wakabikka meso aakwe aasimide kuli mweenzinyina mupati, wakati,—“Bweza ntambo, taata—ndilaipa mumaanza aako, Starbuck.” Mpawo wakabamba muntu wakwe muchibbeeke, wakapa malailile aakuti bamunyamune aawo mpaakali kukkala, Starbuck ngonguwe wakajata ntambo kumamanino; mpawo wakaima afwaafwi anzizyo. Mpawo, kajisi janza lyomwe kalijatilide nkoli yabwami, Ahabu wakalanga kunze alwizi kwamakkilomita manji,—kunembo, kusule, kulubazu oolu, alubo,—mukati kamuzyunguluko mupati wakalaililwa mujulu kapati.

Eelyo naakali kubeleka amaanza aakwe kubusena bumwi busumpukide buli kule loko mubwaato, oobo butakwe busena bwakukkala, sikutanta bwato uuli mulwizi ulasumpulwa kubusena oobo, mpawo ulajatililwa aawo antambo; mubukkale oobu, mamanino aayo aajatikizyidwe abwaato lyoonse alapegwa kumuntu umwi uujisi mulimo waalubazu wakuchilanga. Nkaambo munkanda iili boobu yakuzuzaana, ooko kujatana kwazyo kusiyana-siyana mujulu takukonzyi kuzyibwa kakunyina kulubizya kwiinda muli ceeco cibonwa kuli mbabo abwaato; mpawo aawo ntambo eezyi nozisowelwa ansi mumaminiti masyoonto kuzwa kuzijazyo, inga caba buyo ntenda, ikuti, kakwiina mulindizi uuzumanana, sikutanta bwato uunyampudwe ulakonzya kusowelwa mumaanzi akuwida mulwizi. Aboobo nzila nzyaakachita Ahabu mumakani aaya tiizyakali zyeenzu pe; chintu chomwe chakankamika aatala ambabo chakalibonya kuti, Starbuck, wakali mwaalumi alikke wakali kumukazya aachintu chili coonse chakali afwaafwi kusala—umwi wabaabo awalo, ooyo wakali kudooneka asyoonto kusyomeka kwakwe mukulangisisya;—cakali kugambya, kuti ooyu ngomuntu wini ngwaakeelede kusala kuti abe mulangizi wakwe; kwaaba buumi bwakwe boonse mumaanza aamuntu ngwaatakali kusyomwa pe.

Lino, chiindi chakusanguna Ahabu wakakkede mujulu; katanasika aawo maminiti aali kkumi; imwi ya nswi zyamulwizi zijisi nsingo zituba eezyo ziuluka afwaafwi kapati kuzinguluka mitwe yabantu bavwubwa muma latitude aaya; umwi wabayuni aaba wakaboola kavwunguluka akukoongolola kumutwe wakwe mumugwagwa uuzungaana chakufwambaana. Mpawo yakazuzaana kuya mujulu kwamaulu aali cuulu; mpawo wakazungaana kuya ansi, mpawo wakaunka kuzyunguluka mutwe wakwe alimwi.

Pesi kalangilide kubusena butakwe mumuni, Ahabu wakali kulibonya kuti tanaakabikkila maanu kumuyuni ooyu wamusyokwe; alimwi, masimpe, taakwe naba omwe wakali kunga wachibikkila maanu pe, nkaambo teensi nchintu chidumide pe; lino biyo liso litabikkili maanu lyakali kubona bupanduluzi bumwi bwabucenjezu muzintu zyoonse zyakali kubonwa.

"Chikumba chako, chikumba chako, taata!" mpawo-mpawo wakalila sikutanta bwato waku Sicily, ooyo wakaliimvwi kumutwe wa mizen-mast, wakaima musule lya Ahabu, nikuba kuti wakali munsi-munsi aawo mpaakali kukkala, mpawo wakabaandaanya muya mupati.

Pesi kale bbazu lya sable lyakali kunembo lyameso aamwaalumi uchembede; mulomo mulamfu uujisi nkomo kumutwe wakwe: akuyobeka, nziba isiya yakazuzaana abulumbu bwakwe.

Inyuni yakauluka ziindi zyotatwe kuzinguluka mutwe wa Tarquin, yakagwisya nkapu yakwe kuti ibweze busena, mpawo Tanaquil, imukayintu wakwe, wakaambilizya kuti Tarquin uyooba mwami waku Loma. Pesi biyo kwiinda mukuchincha chikumba mpawo eecho chitondeezyo chakaba chibotu. Cisani ca Ahabu kunyina nocakabambululwa pe; inziba yamusokwe yakauluka kuya kunembo anjiyo; kule kunembo abwaato: mpawo kumamanino wakazimaana; mpawo kuzwa aawo mpaakasweeka, kabala kasiya kaniini kakabonwa, kakali kuwa kuzwa mujulu kapati kuya mulwizi.
