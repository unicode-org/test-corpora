CHAANDANO 55. Zifwanikiso Ziyoosya Zya Nswi.

Ndilakupenta kwachiindi chilamfu mbuli muntu mbwakonzya kuchita kakunyina chisani, chintu chilimbuli chiimo cheenzu cha nswi mpati mbuli mbwalibonya kumeso amuntu uulanganya nswi mpati aawo mumubili wakwe mwini nswi mpati nswi mpati njaangidwe kumbali abwato bwa nswi mpati kuchitila kuti akonzye kunyangwa kabotu aawo. Zilakonzya kugwasya, aboobo, musule kwaambilizya kuli eezyo zifwanikiso zyakwe zyakuyeeyela eezyo kusikila sunu zikazya lusyomo lwamuntu wanyika. Nchiindi chakululamika nyika mumakani aaya, kwiinda mukutondezya kuti zifwanikiso ziliboobu zya nswi mpati zililubide.

Zilakonzya kuba kuti ntalisyo yazyintu zyoonse eezyo zyakuyeeyela ilakonzya kujanwa akati kazifwanikiso zyakaindi zya Hindoo, Egepita, a Grecian. Kuzwa muchiindi eecho chabusongo pesi chakubula lusyomo aawo atala aamabwe aamabwe aatempele, aazikobela zyazikozyanyo, aazikwabilizyo, zikobela, nkomeki, amali, inswi ya dolphin yakali kweezyegwa muzikwabilizyo zya nketani mbuli ya Saladin, amutwe uujisi nkomo mbuli ya St. George; kuzwa muchiindi eecho kuli chimwi chintu chili mbuli cheecho chizumizyidwe, kutali buyo muzifwanikiso zyiingi zya nswi mpati, pesi amuzitondezyo zyiingi zyasayaansi zyakwe.

Lino, munzila zyoonse, mufanikisyo wakaindi kapati uuliwo uutegwa ngwa nswi mpati, ulajanwa mumpangala iizyibidwe kapati ya Elephanta, mu India. Ba Brahmins baamba kuti muzifwanikiso zitamani zya busena oobo bwakusaanguna, milimo yoonse amilimo, zyoonse nzyaakali kuyanda muntu, zyakali kuyeeyelwa kale kakutanaba kufumbwa akati kazintu eezyo. Tazigambyi pe, kuti munzila imwi mulimo wesu mubotu wakuzuba nswi mpati wakali kweelede kuti wakasinkilidwe. Inswi mpati ya Hindoo yaambwa, ilajanika muchibeela chimwi chabwaanda, itondezya kuzyalwa kwa Vishnu muchimo che leviathan, uutegwa Matse Avatar. Asi nikuba kuti eechi chibezwa nchisela chamuntu achisela cha nswi mpati, kuchitila kuti chipe buyo muchila wamamanino, pesi eecho chibeela chakwe chililubide. Chilibonya mbuli muchila uuteteete wa anaconda, kwiinda maanza mapati aa nswi mpati ya whale.

Pesi koya kuGalleries yakaindi, mpawo ulange lino mufanikisyo wasikupenta muna Kristu mupati wa nswi eeyi; nkaambo tazwidilili pe kwiinda ba Hindu bakaindi. Ncifwanikiso cha Guido cha Perseus kavwuna Andromeda kuzwa kuchinyama chamulwizi naakuti nswi mpati. Ino Guido wakajana kuli chikozyano chachilengwa chili mbuli eecho? Awalo Hogarth, mukupenta chikozyano chomwe mu "Perseus Descending" yakwe, tanaakachita kabotu pe. Bupati bupati bwachinyama eecho cha Hogarthian bulazungaana atala, kakunyina kukwela inchi yomwe yamaanzi. Ilijisi musyobo wa howdah kumusana, amulomo wayo uulaa meno aazwide oomo mayuwe mozivwunguluka, ulakonzya kutolwa ku Mulyango wa Basikukazya uuzwa ku Thames kwiinda mumaanzi kuya ku Ngazi. Mpawo, kuli nswi zya Prodromus zya Scotch Sibbald, antoomwe a Jonah's whale, mbuli mbokutondezyedwe mumaBbayibbele aakaindi alimwi akuchekwa kwamabbuku aakaindi. Ino ncinzi chiyooambwa aatala aaba? Kujatikizya nswi mpati yabasikubamba mabbuku iizungaana mbuli musamu wamusaansa kuzinguluka nsingo iiselemuka—mbuli mboilembedwe akubikkwa ngolida kusule amapeeji aamutwe wamabbuku manji aakaindi amapya—eecho nchintu chibotu loko pesi nchintu chibotu loko, chikozyanisigwa, ndachibweza, kuzwa kuzifwanikiso zikozyania zili aamaanda aakaindi. Nikuba kuti munyika yoonse mbwiizulwa wiitwa kuti dolphin, ndilaamba kuti nswi eeyi yakubamba mabbuku nkusola kujata nswi mpati; nkaambo chakakanzidwe aawo eechi chibelesyo nichakatalisyigwa. Lyakatalisyigwa asikumwaya wakaindi waku Italy aawo mumwaanda wamyaka wakkumi amusanu, muchiindi chakubukulusya lwiiyo; alimwi mumazuba aayo, kusikila kumamanino, ma dolphin akali kuyeeyelwa kuti ngamusyobo wa Leviathan.

Muzifwanikiso azimwi zintu zyakubotezya zyamabbuku amwi aakaindi chimwi chiindi ulakonzya kuswaangana azintu zikankamanisya kapati kujatikizya nswi mpati, ooko misyobo yoonse yamapopwe, ma jets d'eau, tusensa twamaanzi aapya alimwi akutontola, Saratoga a Baden-Baden, nkozizwa kazizwa mubongo bwakwe butakwe nguzu. Mupeeji lyamutwe wabbuku lyakusaanguna lya "Kusumpuka kwakwiiya" ulajana nswi zimwi ziyandisya kuziba.

Pesi kuleka kusoleka koonse ooku kutali kwabusongo, atulange zifwanikiso eezyo zya leviathan kazitondezya kuti zili kabotu, zyasayaansi, abaabo bazyi. Mubbuku lya Harris lyakaindi lyalweendo lwamumaanzi kuli zimwi zibeela zya nswi mpati zyakagwisigwa mubbuku lya Dutch lya nyendo, AD 1671, lilaa mutwe wakuti "Lweendo lwakuzuza nswi kuya ku Spitzbergen mubwato bwa Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson waku Friesland, simalelo." muli chimwi chazipekupeku eezyo nswi mpati, mbuli mato mapati aazisamu, zitondezyedwe kazilede akati kansumbu zyaaisi, antoomwe amabbeya aatuba kaabalikila atalaa misana yabo. Kuli chimwi chipekupeku, kulubizya kupati kwakacitwa kwiinda mukwiiminina nswi mpati iijisi mabala aaimvwi nji.

Mpawo alimwi, kuli quarto mpati, yakalembwa amwi Kapitani Colnett, Kapitani waPositi muNyika yamumaanzi, iitegwa "Lweendo lwakuzinguluka Cape Horn kuya muLwizi lwaKujwe, amakanze aakuyungizya Nswi zyaSpermaceti Whale." Mubbuku eeli kuli chitondeezyo chaamba kuti "Cifwanikiso cha Physeter naa Spermaceti whale, chakajanwa achipimyo kuzwa kuli yooyo wakajaigwa kunkomwe ya Mexico, Mukubwekaangala, 1793, mpawo wakasumpulwa abwato." Tandidoneeki kuti mukapokola wakabweza mufanikisyo ooyu wakachitilwa kugwasya basilumamba bakwe. Kwaamba buyo chintu chomwe aatala anzizyo, ndileke ndaambe kuti ilijisi liso lyalo lyakabelesegwa, kweendelanya achipimyo, ku nswi iitegwa sperm whale, inga lyapa kuti liso lya whale eelyo libe mbuli windo lyakukomena lyamaulu aali musanu mubulamfu. Ah, silutwe wangu sicamba, nkamboonzi nchowatakatupa Jona kalangilila anze lyaliso eelyo!

Alimwi tazili zyakubunganya zya makani aazilengwa leza zyalo zigwasya bana baniini, zitakwe kulubizya kuli boobu. langa mulimo oyo uudumide "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." Mubbuku lya London lyakafwiinsigwa lya 1807, kuli mapuleti aa “whale” a “narwhale.” Tandiyandi kulibonya mbuli kuti nditakwe mpuwo, pesi eeyi nswi iitali kabotu ilibonya mbuli ngulube yakakosolwa; alimwi, mbuli narwhale, kwiibona biyo biyo kulakonzya kugambya muntu, kuti mumwaanda wamyaka wakkumi amusanu atune hippogriff iliboobu ilakonzya kuzyibwa kuti njamasimpe kubantu boonse basongo babana bachikolo.

Mpawo, alimwi, mu 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, syaazibwene mumakani aabulenge, wakamwaya bbuku lyasayaansi lya whale, oomo muli zifwanikiso zyiingi zyamisyobo yiindene-indene ya Leviathan. Zyoonse eezyi tazili buyo zilubide pe, pesi mufanikisyo wa Mysticetus naa Greenland whale (nkokuti, Right whale), nokuba Scoresby, mwaalumi wakajisi luzibo lwachiindi chilamfu kujatikizya musyobo ooyu, wakaamba kuti taakwe mweenzinyina muzilengwa leza.

Pesi kubikka chikozyano mumakwebo aaya aalubide kwakali buyo kuli Frederick Cuvier, munyina wa Baron uuzyibidwe. Mu 1836, wakamwaya bbuku lya makani aabuumi bwa nswi mpati, oomo mwaakapa nzyaamba kuti mufanikisyo wa nswi mpati. Kotana utondezya mufanikisyo oyo kuli umwi muna Nantucket, weelede kubamba chiindi chako chakufwambaana kuzwa ku Nantucket. Mubbala lyomwe, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale teensi Sperm Whale pe, pesi nkumba. Masimpe, kunyina naakajana mpindu yalweendo lwakuzuba nswi mpati (bantu baliboobu tabakonzyi pe), asi ino wakajana kuli mufanikisyo ooyo, ino nguni uukonzya kuzyiba? Ambweni wakachijana mbuli sikwiiya wakusaanguna wakwe mumulimo nguwenya, Desmarest, wakajana chimwi chakugwisya mada; nkokuti, kuzwa kumufanikisyo waku China. Alimwi ino mbuti balombwana bajisi pensulo aabo bana China, nkomeki zyiingi zitali kabotu zituzyibya.

Kujatikizya nswi zyabasikupenta zitondezyo zibonwa mumigwagwa kazilengelela atalaa maanda aasambalila mafuta, ino inga twaambwa nzi kujatikizya nzizyo? Kanjikanji mba Richard Watatu. nswi mpati, zili amababa aa dromedary, alimwi zililunya loko; kulya chakulya chakuseni ama tart otatwe naa one, nkokuti mato aazwide basikweenzya bwato: kulemana kwabo kwakali kuzungaana mulwizi lwabulowa apenti yamubala wa bbululu.

Pesi kulubizya ooku kunjaanji mukutondezya nswi iitegwa whale takugambyi pe. Ziyeesesye! Bunji bwazifwanikiso zyasayaansi zyakagwisigwa kunswi zyakajatwa; mpawo eezyi zililuzi mbuli mufanikisyo wabwato bwakanyonyooka, amusana utyokede, inga wiiminina kabotu munyama uulemekwa mukulisumpula kwakwe koonse kwamubili. Nikuba kuti nzovu zyakaima mubulamfwu bwazyo boonse, Leviyatani uupona tanaakali kweenda kabotu kuti ajane mufanikisyo wakwe. Inswi iipona, mubulemu bwakwe boonse akuyandikana, ilakonzya kubonwa buyo mulwizi mumaanzi aatakonzyi kuzyibwa; mpawo kali aamaanzi bunji bwakwe tabulibonyi pe, mbuli bwato bwamulongo wankondo; mpawo kuzwa muchintu eecho nchintu chiyumu kukabe kutamani kumuntu uufwa kuti amunyamune mubili wakwe mujulu, kuchitila kuti akwabilile manguzu aakwe woonse. Alimwi, kakutali kwaambuula aatala akusiyanasiyana kupati aakati ka nswi iitegwa whale nsyoonto a Platonian Leviathan; pesi, nikuba muchiimo chakamwi akati kabana bavwanda bavwubwa bakasumpulwa abwaato, mbubonya mbuli nswi mpati, mbuli nswi, kulema, kusiyana kwachiimo chakwe, kuti busyu bwakwe bwini dyabulosi lwakwe mwini tanaakali kukonzya kujata.

Pesi chinga chayeeyelwa, kuti kuzwa kuchifuwa cha nswi mpati ya whale yakaangidwe, twaambo tuluzi tulakonzya kujanwa kujatikizya bube bwakwe bwini. Taakwe pe. Nkaambo nchintu chimwi chakankamanisya aatala aLeviyatani ooyu, kuti chifuwa chakwe chipa muzeezo muniini aatala achiimo chakwe. Nikuba kuti chifuwa cha Jeremy Bentham, eecho chilengelezyegwa mbuli malambe mu layibrari yaumwi wabasikumujaya, chitondeezya kabotu muzeezo wa mwaalumi uchembede uulaa nsingo mpati, antoomwe abube bumbi bwa Jeremy; pesi kunyina chintu chamusyobo ooyu chikonzya kuzyibwa kuzwa kumafwuwa aa leviathan. Mubunike, mbuli Muvwimi mupati mbwaamba, mafwuwa buyo aamunyama uutegwa whale ulaa chilongwe chomwe amunyama uuzwide mpawo uuzwide mbuli tuzunda mbutujisi ku chrysalis eeyo iimuzingulukide. Ooku kulibedelede kulabonwa mumutwe, mbuli muchibeela chimwi chabbuku eeli mbokuyootondezyegwa. Alimwi chilatondezyegwa munzila iigambya muchipapa chakumbali, mafwuwa aayo aakonzya kwiingula mafwuwa aajanza lyamuntu, kakunyina buyo chaala. Eeyi nsingo ilijisi minwe yone yamafwuwa, yachikozyano, yaakati, yachikobela, amunwe muniini. Pesi zyoonse eezyi zilakkalilila kukabe kutamani muchivwumbyo chazyo chanyama, mbuli minwe yamuntu muchivwumbyo chakupanga. "Nikuba kuti nswi iitegwa whale ilakonzya kutubelekela biyeni," wakaamba Stubb uuseka buzuba bumwi, "takonzyi kwaambwa kuti ulakonzya kutujata kakunyina zikobela."

Nkaambo katwaambo toonse ootu, aboobo, kufumbwa mbokonzya kuzilanga, weelede kumanizya kuti Leviyatani mupati ncecilenge comwe munyika ceelede kuzumanana kutapentwa kusikila kumamanino. Masimpe, chifanikisyo chimwi chilakonzya kusika afwaafwi kwiinda chimwi, pesi taakwe chikonzya kuchisika kabotu loko. Aboobo taakwe nzila yaanyika yakujana kuti nswi mpati ilibonya biyeni. Alimwi nzila ilikke njokonzya kujana nokuba muzeezo uukonzya kuzuminwa wabuumi bwakwe, nkwiinda mukuunka kuyoozuba nswi mpati omwini; pesi mukuchita obo, tokwe ntenda yakuba chitofu chakumaninina akubbizyigwa anguwe. Aboobo, kulibonya kulindime kuti teelede kuba aluyandisisyo lwakuyanda kuziba aatala aLeviyatani ooyu.





CHAANDANO 56. Zifwanikiso Zitali Zyakulubizya Zya Nswi Zitegwa Whale, a Zifwanikiso Zyamasimpe Zyakuzuba Nswi.

Kweelana azifwanikiso ziyoosya zyaba whale, ndilasunkwa loko aawa kuti ndijane twaano tuyoosya kapati twabo ootu tujanika mumabbuku amwi, aakaindi aamazubaano, ikapati muli Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, azimwi. Pesi ndilainda aawo.

Ndilizyi buyo zibeela zyone zyakasimbwa zya Sperm Whale mpati; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, a Beale's. Muchibalo chainda Colnett a Cuvier bakaambwa. Huggins's njibotu kwiinda yabo; pesi, kakunyina kudooneka, Beale's ngomubotu kwiinda. Zifwanikiso zyoonse zya Beale zya nswi eyi zili kabotu, kunze lyachifanikisyo chaakati-akati muchifanikisyo cha nswi zyotatwe zili mubukkale bwiindene-indene, kumanizya chaandano chakwe cabili. Chitondeezyo chakwe, mato alwana ba Sperm Whales, nikuba kuti chakayeeyelwa kuti chikankaizye kudooneka kwabantu bamwi, chililuzi loko mpawo chilimbuli buumi. Zimwi zifwanikiso zya Sperm Whale muli J. Ross Browne zililuleme loko; pesi zililembedwe munzila iitali kabotu. Ooko takuli mulandu wakwe pe.

Ku Right Whale, zifwanikiso zibotu zili mu Scoresby; pesi zilembedwe achiimo chiniini loko kuti zitondezye muzeezo mubotu. Ulijisi buyo mufanikisyo omwe wazifwanikiso zyakuzuba nswi mpati, mpawo eezi nkubula kuusa, nkaambo nzifwanikiso ziliboobu luzutu, kuti zyachitwa obotu, ooko nkokonzya kujana muzeezo wini-wini wa nswi mpati iipona mbuli mboibonwa abavwimi bakwe bapona.

Pesi, kuti twabweza zyoonse antoomwe, ziinda kubota, nikuba kuti muzibeela zimwi tazili ziluzi pe, zitondezyo zya nswi mpati alimwi azifwanikiso zyakuzuba nswi mpati zikonzya kujanwa kufumbwa busena, nzifwanikiso zyobile zipati zyaku France, zyakabambwa kabotu, alimwi zyakatolwa kuzwa kuzifwanikiso zyakapentwa a Garnery umwi. Mbubwenya, ziiminina kulwanwa kwa Sperm a Right Whale. Mukuzekaula kutaanzi, Nswi iitegwa Sperm Whale ilibonya munguzu zyoonse, yakanyampuka munsi lyabwaato kuzwa mukati kalwizi, mpawo yakanyamwide mujulu amusana wakwe ntenda mpati yamaplanga aachitofu. Kunembo kwabwaato takutyokede pe, mpo kukwelwa biyo kweendelana amusana wachinyama; mpawo koliimvwi kunembo, kwachiindi chomwe chikonzya kubalilwa, yebo wabona sikutanta bwato, wakavwumbidwe aakati-kati amulomo uupya wa nswi mpati, mpawo kali muchiimo chakusotokela, mbuli kuti wakazwa mumulonga. Ikuchita kwachintu choonse nkubotu loko alimwi nkwamasimpe. Ibbaaji lya line-tub litakwe chintu lilatanta aalwizi lusalala; zisamu zyazisamu zyama harpoon zyakatilwa zilazungaana muli ncico; mitwe yabasikudubbwa ilimwaikide kuzyunguluka nswi iitegwa whale kabatondezya kuyoowa kusiyana; aawo muchiindi chilamfu chamuwo uusiya bwato bwakali kusika ansi aawo mpaakali kuchitika. Kulubizya kupati kulakonzya kujanwa muzibeela zyamubili wa nswi eeyi, pesi eezi ziinde; nkaambo, mubuumi bwangu, teensi ndakali kukonzya kuzekaula chibotu loko.

Mukuzekaula kwabili, bwato buli mukuzekaula kumbali lya nswi mpati ya Right Whale, eeyo iizungaanya mubili wayo mulwizi mbuli bbwe lyamusokwe lizwa mumalundu aa Patagonian. Ma jeti aakwe aliimvwi, alizwide, alimwi alisiya mbuli bulongo; kuchitila kuti kuzwa kukuvwula kwabusi mumulyango, inga wayeeya kuti kweelede kuti kuli chakulya chamansiku chijika mumatumbu mapati munsi. Inyuni zyamulwizi zilaluma nkala, nswi zyamumaanzi, azimwi ziswiiti zyamulwizi ama maccaroni, eezyo nswi zyamulwizi zimwi ziindi nzyanyamuna kumusana wakwe uujaya. Mpawo muchiindi choonse eecho, leviathan iilaa milomo mipati ili mukuchijaana mumaanzi, akusiya matani aamafuta aatuba, mpawo akupa kuti bwato buniini buzungaane mumaanzi mbuli bwato bujatikizyidwe afwaafwi amavwili aabwaato bwamulwizi. Aboobo, kunembo kuli lupyopyongano lupati; pesi musule, mukwiimpana kwabusongo, kuli lwizi lwagilazi lwakakkazyika moyo, maseya aatakwe nguzu aatakwe nguzu, antoomwe abunji bwa nswi mpati iifwide, ŋanda yakazundwa, fuleegi lyakujata lyakali kulengaana kuzwa kumusamu wa nswi mpati yakabikkwa mumulyango wakwe.

Ino Garnery sikupenta nguni, naakuti wakali, nsezi pe. Pesi buumi bwangu wakalizi kabotu makani aakwe, naakuti wakayiisigwa amuntu umwi uuchibwene. Ba Fulenci mbabaalumi bayandisya kupenta zintu. Koya ukalangisye zifwanikiso zyoonse zyakuYulopu, mpawo ino nkuli nkoyoojana busena bwakutondezya zintu zipona akuyoya atala aachisani, mbuli muholo eeyo yakuzunda kuVersailles; ooko sikulanga nkwalwana nzila yakwe, pell-mell, kwiinda munkondo zipati zitobelana zyaku France; ooko panga lyoonse nkoliboneka mbuli mumuni wakunyika, mpawo bami abami batobelana bajisi zilwanyo balainda, mbuli nkondo yaba centaurs bajisi musini? Tazyaambi kuti tazyeelede pe kuba abusena muchibaka eecho, nzintu eezyi zyankondo zyamulwizi zya Garnery.

Busongo bwaba Fulenci bwakujata bubotu bwazintu bulibonya kuti bulatondezyegwa kapati muzifwanikiso nzyobapenta alimwi azifwanikiso nzyobajisi zyakuzuba nswi mpati. Kakwiina luzibo lwa England mukuzuba nswi, mpawo kakunyina luzibo lwaba Amelika, nikubaboobo bakapa zisi zyoonse zyobile zifwanikiso zyakamanizyigwa zikonzya kutondezya muuya wini wakuvwima nswi mpati. Muchiindi chiingi, basikuzekaula nswi mpati bakuEngland aba Amelika balibonya kuti balikkomene akutondezya mbozipangidwe zintu, mbuli chiimo cha nswi mpati; eecho, kweendelanya akuboneka kabotu, chili mbuli kuzekaula chikozyano cha pyramid. Nobaba ba Scoresby, ba Right whaleman bazyibidwe kapati, nikwakamana kutupa bulamfwu buzulide bwa Greenland whale, antoomwe azifwanikiso zyotatwe naa zyone zya narwhales a porpoises, wakatupa zifwanikiso zyakaindi zyazikobela zyabwaato, zipeni zyakutebula, alimwi ama grapnels; alimwi akubeleka changuzu kwa Leuwenhoeck wakapa kuti abone nyika iizungaana, ma fac-similes aali makumi osanwe amusanu atubili aama crystals aa Arctic aasumpukide. Tandiyandi kusampaula sikutanta bwato mubotu (ndilamulemeka nkaambo wakali silumamba), pesi mumakani aayandika kapati kwakali kutabikkila maanu kutajana lugwalo lwakukonkezya lwakapegwa kuli Mubetesi waLuumuno wakuGreenland.

Mukuyungizya kuzifwanikiso eezyo zibotu zyakazwa kuli Garnery, kuli zimwi zifwanikiso zyobile zyaku France zyeelede kuzyibwa, zyakalembwa amuntu umwi uulyaamba kuti "H. Durand." Chimwi chazyo, nikuba kuti tiichakweendelana amakanze eesu, pesi chileelede kwaambwa mumakani aambi. Nchibaka chamasikati chitakwe muzuzumo akati kansumbu zya Pacific; sikuzuba nswi mpati yaku France wakaima, kunkomwe yalwizi, mubusena butakwe ntenda, mpawo kabweza maanzi mubwaato; maseelo aabwaato aakaangununwa, amatu malamfu aamisamu yamusaansa kusule, zyoonse zyobile kazilengaana antoomwe mumuwo uutakwe guwo. Ikuzwidilila kuli kabotu loko, kuti kwalangwa-langwa mbuli mbokutondezya bazubi banswi basimide munsi lyachimwi chazibeela zyabo zisyoonto zyakulyookezya kwakujwe. Ikuzekaula kumwi nchintu chiliimpene: bwato bwakatanta aalwizi, mukati-kati kabuumi bwa Leviathanic, antoomwe a Right Whale kumbali; bwato (muchiimo chakugela) bwazuzaana kuya kuchinyama mbuli kuti bwatanta bwato; mpawo bwato, bwakafwambaana kuzwa aawo mpaakali kuchitwa zintu, buli afwaafwi kutandaanya nswi mpati zili kulaale. CHAANDANO 60. Muzila.

Kwiinda mukwaamba zyakuzuba nswi mpati zili afwaafwi kupandululwa, antoomwe akuteelela kabotu zibeela zyoonse zikozyenye eezyo zitondezyedwe, aawa ndaamba zyamasalamuzi, zimwi ziindi ziyoosya zyamulongo wa nswi mpati.

Tambo lyakabelesegwa kumatalikilo mukuzuba nswi lyakali lya hemp liinda kubota, lyakavwumbidwe mbichani, litakwe bbivwe, mbuli ntambo zyabuzubaa buzuba; nkaambo kakuli tar, mbuli mboibelesyegwa lyoonse, ipa kuti hemp ibe kabotu kuli sikupanga ntambo, mpawo ilapa kuti ntambo ibe kabotu kuli sikutanta bwato kuti ibelesyegwe mubwaato; pesi, takuli buyo kuti mweelwe uuzyibidwe ulakonzya kuyumya mulongo wa whale kuti ukonzye kuzinguluka afwaafwi ooko nkoyelede kuchitwa; pesi mbuli basimaanzi biingi mbobatalika kwiiya, ntambo taikonzyi kuyungizya kuyuma kwatambo, nikuba kuti ilakonzya kupa kuti ibe njumu akumwekamweka.

Muminyaka misyoonto yainda ntambo ya Manilla mukuzuba nswi yaku Amelika yakatola busena bwa hemp mbuli chintu chakupanga nswi mpati; nkaambo, nikuba kuti tiiyumu mbuli hemp, iliyumu, alimwi iliteteete loko alimwi ilakonzya kunyamuka; mpawo ndiyooyungizya (nkaambo kuli æsthetics muzintu zyoonse), nchintu chibotu loko alimwi chili kabotu kubwaato, kwiinda hemp. Hemp nchintu chisiya, chisiya, mbuli muIndiya; pesi Manilla ulimbuli muna Circassian uujisi masusu aangolida.

Muzila wa whale uli buyo mubufwaafwi bwazibeela zyobile zyatatu zya inchi. Kumatalikilo, tokonzyi kuyeeya kuti nchintu chiyumu mbuli mbuchili ncobeni. Kwiinda mukusola, ntambo zyayo lyomwe amakumi musanu zilakonzya kunyamuna kulema kwamapaundi aali mwaanda amakumi obile; kuchitila kuti ntambo yoonse ikonzye kunyamuna mukuli uukonzya kusika matani otatwe. Mubulamfu, mulongo wa sperm whale uudumide ulapima chimwi chintu chiinda kuma fathoms aali myaanda yobile. Kuya kusule lyabwaato lilazunguluka mubbokesi, kutali mbuli mupaipi wa njuka, pesi kuchitila kuti lipange chimwi chizungulukide, chiboneka mbuli chiizi cha "ma sheaves" aajisi bulo buzwide, naakuti zibeela zya concentric spiralizations, kakunyina busena buli boonse pesi "moyo," naa tube tuniini tuimvwi nji tupangwa aachizi. Mbuli kuvwundauka kusyoonto naa kuvwundauka mubbokesi mbokunga, mukumanizya, kugwisya janza lyamuntu, kuulu, naa mubili woonse, kucenjela kupati kulabelesyegwa mukubikka ntambo mubbokesi lyayo. Bamwi basikulizya nkoli balakonzya kubelesya mafwumofwumo woonse mumulimo ooyu, kabanyamuna ntambo kuya mujulu mpawo bayifwusa ansi kwiinda mubbokesi kuya kuchibbeeke, kuchitila kuti kabaivwungulula kuti itabi aamabwe woonse.

Mumaato aachiNgisi kubelesegwa zibbeeke zyobile muciindi chachimwi; muzila omwe kauzumanana kuvwungailwa mumabbodela oonse obile. Kuli bubotu bumwi muli eci; nkaambo aaya ma twin-tubs mbwaanga masyoonto loko alakonzya kunjila mubwaato, mpawo taali kukatazya pe; mpawo, nkapu yaku Amelika, iili afwaafwi kusika kumaulu otatwe mubwamba antoomwe akuya ansi kweelene, ilapa kuti kube mukuli mupati loko kubwaato bujisi mapulanga aali buyo acisela cha inchi mubufwaafwi; nkaambo munsi lyabwaato bwa whale kuli mbuli aisi iikatazya, eeyo iyoonyamuna kulema kupati, pesi kutali kulema kupati pe. Eelyo chivwumbyo chakupenta nichakakwa abwaato bwaku Amelika, bwato bulaboneka mbuli kuti buli mukunyamuka akkekke lyabwiinga lipati loko lyakupa nswi mpati.

Mamanino oonse obile aamuzila ali antangalala; mamanino aansi aamanizya muchikwabilizyo chameso naakuti chikozyano chizwa ansi kuya kulubazu lwa bbafa, mpo chilengelela atalaa munyinza wacho kakunyina kujatikizyigwa azyoonse. Ooku kubamba kwamamanino aansi kulayandika mumakani obile. Chakusanguna: Mukuchitila kuti kube kujatanisya ntambo iimbi kuzwa kubwaato buli afwaafwi, kuti naa nswi iitegwa whale yakaumwa yalila mukati kapati cakuti ilakonzya kunyamuna ntambo yoonse yakaangidwe ku ntambo. Muziindi eezyi, nswi mpati iitegwa whale ilasunkwa mbuli nkomeki ya bukoko, mbuli kuti, kuzwa kubwaato bumwi kuya kuli bumbi; nikuba kuti bwato butaanzi bulaafwaafwi kugwasya mweenzinyina. Cabili: Bubambe oobu bulayandika kapati kutegwa bantu boonse kabakwabilidwe; nkaambo kuti mamanino aansi aamulongo naakali kujatikizyidwe kubwaato, mpawo kuti nswi mpati yakali kukonzya kuzuza ntambo kusikila kumamanino mukati kaminiti yomwe, mbuli mbwachita chimwi chiindi, tanaakali kunga wiima aawo, nkaambo bwato bwakali kunyonyoonwa bwakali kuyookwelwa munsi lyakwe kuya mukati kalwizi; mpawo muchiimo eecho taakwe sikwiiminina mumunzi uunga wamujana alimwi.

Kakutanaba kuselusya bwato kuti butandaanye, mutwe wamulongo wakujulu ulatolwa kusule kuzwa mubbokesi, mpawo wainda kuzinguluka mutwe wabwaato, alimwi ulatolwa kuya kunembo mubulamfu boonse bwabwaato, akulyookezya munzila iiteendelani aachibelesyo chabwaato bwamuntu uli woonse, kuchitila kuti bujate kujanza lyakwe mukweenda; alimwi akwiinda aakati kabaalumi, mbuli mbobakali kukkala mumasena aaimpene, kuya kumagolelo aajisi nsingo naakuti migodi iili kunembo lyabwaato, ooko nkoli chisamu naakuti chikungu chipati mbuli chikumba chabantu boonse, nkochikwabilila kuti litazwi. Kuzwa kuzikobela zilalengaana muchikozyano chiche atala aamabuwa, mpawo zilainda mukati kabwaato alimwi; alimwi aali kkumi naa makumi obile (aitwa kuti bbokesi-muzila) kazibikkidwe atalaa bbokesi mumabbokesi, lilazumanana nzila yalyo kuya ku gunwale kachili musule mbichani, mpawo lijatikizigwa ku short-warp—ntambo eeyo iiswaangene a harpoon; pesi kakutanaba kujatana ooku, chiindi chifwiifwi chiinda muzintu zisesemya zikatazya kumvwisisisya.

Aboobo mulongo wa whale ulavwungaila bwato boonse muzibeela zyawo zikatazya kumvwisisisya, akuzunguluka akuzinguluka mumbazu zyoonse. Boonse basikweenzya bwato balijatikizyidwe mumapenzi aakwe; aboobo kuliso lyamuntu uulaanyika, balibonya mbuli basikusobana bakuIndiya, anzoka zijaya kapati kazibamba zizo zyabo. Alimwi taakwe mwana wamwanakazi uufwa, muchiindi chakusanguna, takonzyi kukkala aakati kazyintu eezyo zikatazya kumvwisisisya, mpawo kalikusoleka kusikila mpogolela kukwasula bwato, akumuyeeya kuti kufumbwa chiindi chitali chizyibidwe nkoli ilakonzya kuumwa, mpawo zyoonse eezyi zintu ziyoosya zilakonzya kubelesegwa mbuli malabi aajisi nkomo; takonzyi kuba muchiimo eechi kakunyina kuzungaana ooko kupa kuti bongo buli mumafwuwa aakwe buzungaane mbuli jeli iizungaanisigwa. pesi chiyanza—chintu cheenzu! nchinzi nchitachikonzyi kuchitwa azilengwa?—Kusekelela kusekelela, kusekelela kubotu, masesya mabotu, alimwi akuvwiila kubotu, kunyina nowakamvwa atala aamusamu wako wa mahogany, kwiinda mboyoomvwa atalaa cisamu ca cedar cituba cilampa inchi yacisela cabwaato bwa whale, eelyo nocilengeledwe muzikwabilizyo zyamuntu uujatikizyidwe; alimwi, mbuli ba burgers bali cisambomwe baku Calais kabatanasika Mwami Edward, baalumi bali cisambomwe ibakali kubamba nkamu yabwato bakanjila mumilomo yalufu, kabajisi nkomo munsingo zyoonse, mbuli mbomunga mwaamba.

Ambweni muzeezo muniini ulakonzya kukugwasya kuti ukonzye kupandulula ntenda zyakuzuba nswi mpati—zimwi zyazyo zyakalembwa biyo—zyamuntu oyu naakuti muntu ooyo wakagwisigwa mubwaato amutambo, mpawo wakasweeka. Nkaambo, aawo mulongo nuuzwa, kukkala mubwaato, chilimbuli kukkala aakati kakuvwuuma kwa injini ya busi, aawo ntambo zyoonse ziuluka, ankomo, anvwili, nozikukwela. Nchibi kwiinda; nkaambo tokonzyi kukkala kotazungaani mukati kantenda eezyi, nkaambo bwato buli mukuzungaana mbuli chibbeeke, mpawo yebo uliimvwi kulubazu lumwi, kakunyina kuchenjezegwa pe; mpawo biyo kwiinda mukulibambila akulibambila antoomwe akuyanda akuchita, ulakonzya kutija kuchitwa Mazeppa, mpawo utije aawo zuba libona zyoonse ndyotakonzyi kukugonka.

Alubo: mbuli kukkalikana kupati ooko kulibonya buyo kuti kulasaanguna akusinsima guwo pati, ambweni nkuyoosya kwiinda guwo lwini; nkaambo, masimpe, luumuno nchintu chivwungaila buyo guwo pati; mpawo ilijisi mulinjiyo, mbuli mfwulumende iilibonyaanga taikwe ntenda mboijisi busi bujaya, abbola, akupwasuka; aboobo kulyookezya kubotu kwamulongo, mbuli mbouzungaana abasikutanta bwato kakutanaba kuchitwa—eechi nchintu chiyoosya loko kwiinda zimwi zintu zilimuchintu eechi chiyoosya. Asi nkamboonzi nchotwaamba zyiingi? Bantu boonse bakkala kabazingulukidwe amilongo ya nswi mpati. Boonse bazyalwa kabajisi zikwabilizyo munsingo zyabo; pesi nkuli buyo aawo nobajatwa mukufwambaana, kusanduka kwalufu, mpawo bantu bazyiba ntenda zyabuumi zitalibonyi, zitalibonyi, ziliwo lyoonse. Alimwi kuti koli musongo, nikuba kuti ukkede mubwaato bwabaswi, tokonzyi kulimvwa kuyoowa pe, kwiinda kuti koli ukkede kunembo lyamulilo wako wakumangolezya koli a poker, ikutali harpoon, kumbali lyako.
