CHAANDANO 82. Bulemu abulemu bwakuzuba nswi mpati.

Kuli makwebo amwi mwalo kutabamba kabotu-kabotu mokuli nzila yamasimpe.

Mbondiyaabunjila mumakani aaya aakuzuba nswi mpati, akusumpula buvwuntauzi bwangu kusikila kumatalikilo aazyo mbondiyaabugambwa abulemu bwawo bupati abwakaindi; alimwi ikapati aawo nondijana baleza banji bapati abasikalumamba, basinsimi bamisyobo yoonse, aabo munzila imwi naa imwi bakatondezya kwaandaana kuli ncico, ndilatolwa akuyeeya kuti mebo lwangu ndili, nokuba kuti ndili munsi, kuciinga cabunyina cilemekwa kapati.

Silumamba Perseus, mwana wa Jupiter, wakali silumamba wakusaanguna; alimwi kubulemu butamani bwa kwiitwa kwesu kwaambwa, kuti nswi mpati yakusaanguna yakalwanwa abunyina bwesu tiiyakajaigwa amakanze mabi pe. Aayo akali mazuba aabusikalumamba mumulimo wesu, aawo notwakali kunyamuna zilwanyo kugwasya bachiswa, kutali kuzuzya malambe aabantu. Muntu woonse ulizyi makani mabotu aa Perseus a Andromeda; mbuli Andromeda mubotu, mwana musimbi wamwami, mbwaakaangidwe kubbwe kunkomwe yalwizi, mpawo Leviathan naakali kuyanda kumunyamuna, Perseus, mwami waba whalemen, wakaya ambele, wakauma silunya, mpawo wakamugwisya akukwata mubelesi. Chakali chintu chibotu loko chabusongo, chakali kuchitwa chiindi chiingi abasikulizya nkoli bamazubaano; nkaambo Leviyatani ooyu wakajayigwa kumusemo wakusaanguna. Alimwi kutabi muntu uudooneka makani aaya aa Arkite; nkaambo mu Joppa yansiku, lino Jaffa, kunkomwe ya Syria, muli limwi lyamatempele aa Bakomba mituni, kwakaliimvwi kwamyaka myiingi chifwuwa chipati cha nswi mpati, eeyo twaano twamunzi abantu boonse mbobakali kwaamba kuti akali mafwuwa aakozyenye aachinyama chakajaya Perseus. Chiindi baRoma nibakabweza Jopa, chifuwa chomwe chakatolwa ku Italy mukuzunda. Eecho chilibonya kuba chintu chipati loko mukaano aaka, nkakuti: kwakali kuzwa ku Jopa ooko Jona nkwaakatanta bwato.

Kweelana akweendeenda kwa Perseus a Andromeda—nchoonzyo, kuli bamwi bayeeyelwa kuti kwakazwa kuli ncico—nkaambo aaka kadumide ka St. George a Dragon; nguuli syuumbwa ngondiyeeya kuti wakali nswi mpati; nkaambo mumakani manji aakaindi, nswi mpati abasyuumbwa balasanganizigwa antoomwe, mpawo kanjikanji balaiminina umwi amweenzinyina. "Uli mbuli syuumbwa wamaanzi, mbuli syuumbwa wamulwizi," mbwaamba Ezekieli; aawa, chaamba antangalala nswi mpati; mubwini, zimwi mabbuku aaBbayibbele abelesya bbala eelyo. Kunze lyaboobo, inga chagwisya bulemu bwakubeleka kwa St. George pesi wakaswaangana achinyama chivwundauka chaanyika, muciindi chakulwana achinyama chipati chamulwizi. Muntu uli oonse ulakonzya kujaya nzoka, pesi buyo Perseus, St. George, Bbokesi lyaMufu, mbabajisi moyo muli mbabo kuti baunke chabusicamba kuya ku nswi mpati.

Utalekeli kuti zifwanikiso zyamazubaano zyachikozyano eechi zituyenge; nkaambo nikuba kuti chilengwa chakaswaangana amuntu ooyo uutegwa whale sicamba wakaindi chakatondezyegwa munzila iitasalali mbuli nswi mpati, alimwi nikuba kuti nkondo itondezyedwe anyika mpawo musalali utantide bbiza, pesi katulanga kutaziba kupati kwachiindi eecho, eelyo chiimo cheenzu cha whale nichatakazyibidwe abasikuzekaula; mpawo katuyeeya kuti mbuli muchiimo cha Perseus, nswi mpati ya St. George ilakonzya kuti yakatanta kuzwa mulwizi kunkomwe yalwizi; mpawo katuyeeya kuti munyama wakatantide a St. George ulakonzya kuti wakali buyo chiyuni chipati, naakuti bbiza lyamulwizi; kakuli zyoonse eezyi mumizeezo, tazikonzyi kulibonya kuti tazyeendelani pe amakani aasetekene antoomwe amalembe aakaindi aachikozyano, kujata eechi chiitwa kuti syuumbwa kutali umbi kunze lya Leviyatani mupati lwakwe mwini. Mubunike, kazibikkidwe kunembo lya kasimpe kasimide, makani woonse aaya ayoochita mbuli nswi, nyama, anyuni mutuni waba Filisiti, Dagoni muzina; ooyo naakabikkwa kunembo lyabbokesi lyaIsrayeli, mutwe wabbiza lyakwe amaanza aakwe oonse obile zyakawa kuzwa kuli nguwe, mpawo kwakasyeede buyo chisiko naakuti nswi yakwe. Aboobo, mpawo, umwi wabantu besu balemekwa, nikuba muvwubi, ngusikukwabilila waku England; alimwi kwiinda muzyeelelo zibotu, swebo tobasikuuma nkwela baku Nantucket tweelede kulembwa mukabunga ka St. George. Mpawo, mutalekeli basilumamba bachiinga eecho chilemekwa (kunyina naba omwe akati kabo, mbondikonzya kwaamba, wakajisi nswi mpati mbuli mugwasyi wabo mupati), batalangi Nantucketer chakusampaula, nkaambo nikuba muzisani zyesu zyaboya bwambelele azyakusama zyesu zyamatala tulakonzya kutambula zyakusama zya St. Georges kwiinda mbabo.

CHAANDANO 83. Jona wakalangwa mumakani aakaindi.

Kwakaambwa makani aakaindi aa Jona a nswi mpati muchibalo chakayinda. Lino bamwi bana Nantucketers tabasyomi makani aakaindi aa Jona a nswi mpati. Pesi kwakaba bamwi ba Giliki aba Loma bakali kudooneka, aabo, kabaimvwi kuzwa kubakomba mituni bachiindi chabo, bakali kudooneka makani aa Hercules a nswi mpati, a Arion a dolphin; pesi kudooneka kwabo zilengwa eezyo tiikwakapa kuti zilengwa eezyo zibe twaambo tuniini, nkaambo kazyoonse eezyo.

Pesi, kuli ceeci, bwiinguzi bwa Bishopo Jebb bwakulangila bulibambilide. Tachiyandiki pe, wakaamba Bishopo, kuti tubone Jona mbuli kuti wakazikkwa mwida lyanswi mpati, pesi kuti wakakkala muchbeela chamulomo wakwe kwakaindi kaniini. Ambweni, awalo, Jona wakali kukonzya kulivwumbilila muzinyo litakwe mpako; pesi, mumizeezo yabili, Nswi yakululyo tiikwe meno pe.

Kaambo kambi Sag-Harbor (wakali kwiitwa azina eelyo) nkaakakulwaizya akaambo kakubula lusyomo mumakani aaya aamusinsimi, kakali chintu chitalibonyi kabotu kujatikizya mubili wakwe wakaangidwe antoomwe amaanzi aamubili wa nswi mpati. Pesi ooku kukazya akwalo kwawida ansi, nkaambo sikupandulula makani waku German uyeeya kuti Jona weelede kuti wakayubila mumubili wa nswi mpati iifwide —nikuba kuti basilumamba baku France munkondo yaku Russia bakasandula mabbiza aabo afwide kuba matente, mpawo bakanjila muli ngawo. Kunze lyaboobo, kwakasinsimwa abamwi basikwaambilizya makani, kuti aawo Jona naakasowelwa mulwizi kuzwa mubwato bwaJopa, wakafwambaana kutija kuya kubwato bumbi bwakali afwaafwi, bwato bumwi bwakajisi nswi mpati mbuli mutwe; alimwi, inga ndayungizya, ambweni wiitwa kuti "I Whale," mbuli zimwi zintu mazubaano mboziitwa kuti "Shark," "Gull," "Eagle." Alimwi taakwe basyaazibwene batakwe makani aabo bakaamba kuti nswi mpati yaambwa mubbuku lya Jona yakali kwaamba buyo chintu chikwabilila buumi—nkomo yamuwo—eeyo musinsimi wakali muntenda yakumana nkwaakadubbwa, aboobo wakafutulwa kuzwa kukunyonyoonwa kwamaanzi. Sag-Harbor mubi, aboobo, ulibonya kuti ulibija koonse koonse. Pesi wakalijisi kaambo kambi kakapa kuti atabi alusyomo. Cakali ceeci, ikuti kandiyeeya kabotu: Jona wakamenwa a nswi mpati mu Lwizi lwa Mediterranean, mpawo nokwakainda mazuba otatwe wakaswida kubusena bumwi mukati kalweendo lwamazuba otatwe kuzwa ku Nineveh, munzi uuli ku Tigris, kwiinda lweendo lwamazuba otatwe kuzwa kubusena buli afwaafwi kunkomwe ya Mediterranean. Ino mbuti oobo?

Asi teensi kwakanyina nzila imbi yakuti nswi iitegwa whale kusika kumusinsimi musinzo mufwaafwi kuzwa kuNineveh? Iyii. Ulakonzya kuti wakamunyamuna munzila ya Cape of Good Hope. Pesi twaambo toonse ootu twabufwubafwuba twa Sag-Harbor wakaindi twakatondezya buyo kulisumpula kwakwe kwabufwubafwuba—chintu chichili chibi muli nguwe, nkaambo wakajisi buyo lwiiyo lusyoonto kunze lyazyeezyo nzyaakaiya kuzwa kuzuba amulwizi. Ndaamba kuti chitondezya buyo bufubafuba bwakwe, kulisumpula kwakwe kutali kabotu, akuzanga kwakwe kusesemya, kwabudyabulosi kuli basololi bazikombelo balemekwa.
