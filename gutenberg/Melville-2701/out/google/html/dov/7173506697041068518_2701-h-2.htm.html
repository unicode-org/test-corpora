CHAANDANO 5. Chakulya chakuseni.

Ndakafwambaana kutobela, mpawo ndakaseluka mukaanda kakunywa bukoko ndakamujata kabotu mukamwini ŋanda wakali kumweta-mweta. Teensi ndakajisi bbivwe kuli nguwe, nikuba kuti wakali kundifwubaazya asyoonto mumakani aamuntu ngondakali kukkala limwi.

Nokuba boobo, kuseka kubotu nchintu chibotu loko, mpawo nchintu chiche loko; luzyalo ndoluyaabuvwula. Aboobo, ikuti umwi muntu, mubuntu bwakwe mwini, ulakonzya kusekelela kufumbwa muntu, atabi musule, pesi aleke kuzumizya kuti abelesye mali mpawo abelesyegwe munzila eeyo. Alimwi mwaalumi uulaa chintu chisesemya aa nguwe, kosinizya kuti kuli zyiingi muli oyo mwaalumi kwiinda mboyeeyela.

Ing'anda yakunywida yakalizwide bantu bakali kuboola masiku aakainda, aabo mbondatakali kubalanga kabotu. Aafwaafwi boonse bakali ba whalemen; bapati, ababili, abatatu, ababezi bamulwizi, abasikupanga zintu zyamulwizi, abasikufula zintu zyamulwizi, abasikulizya nkoli, abasikubamba mato; nkamu yabantu basiya alimwi bajisi manguzu, bajisi ndevu ziyumu; bantu batakwe masusu, batakwe masusu, boonse kabasamide mabbaaji aamasusu aakusama mafwumofwumo.
