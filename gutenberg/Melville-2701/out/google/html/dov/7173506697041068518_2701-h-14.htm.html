CHAANDANO 68. Chisani.

Kufumbwa mbocibede, ncintu cikkomanisya kubala makani aajatikizya nswi zya whale kwiinda muzipekupeku zyabo beni, mbuli mbomunga mwaamba. Asi ncondiyanda kwaamba aawa nceeci. Eecho chintu chiteteete loko, isinglass, eecho, ndazumina, chibikka mubili woonse wa whale, tachikonzyi kubonwa mbuli chikumba chachilengwa, mbuli chikumba chachikumba, mbutwaamba; nkaambo chakali chintu chisesemya kwaamba, kuti chikumba cheelede cha nswi mpati mpati nciteteete alimwi nciteteete kwiinda chikumba chamwana uuzyalwa. Kuyeeyela kuti mafuta ngachikumba cha nswi mpati; mpawo, eelyo chikumba eechi, mbuli muchiimo cha Sperm Whale mpati loko, chiyoozyala bunji bwamigolo yamafuta iili mwaanda; alimwi, aawo notuyeeyelwa kuti, mubunji, naakuti mubulemu, mafuta aayo, muchiimo chawo, ali buyo muzibeela zyotatwe zyane, kutali zyoonse zili muchikobela; muzeezo umwi ulakonzya kujanwa aatala abupati bwachintu eecho chipona, cibeela buyo chachikumba chacho chipa kuti kube lwizi lwamaanzi mbuli oolo. Kubala migolo iili kkumi ku toni, ulaa matani aali kkumi aabulemu bwazyotatwe zyachikumba cha nswi mpati.
