Aawo, nswi mpati njaakauma awalo weelede kuti wakali mulweendo lwakwe; kakunyina kudooneka wakazinguluka nyika ziindi zyotatwe, akusanzya ankomwe zyoonse zya Afulika; pesi kakunyina mpindu. Muchiindi chamyaka yotatwe, chakachitika kuti ndakali mubwaato ziindi zyoonse zyobile, kutaanguna akumamanino, mpawo kumamanino ndakabona kabotu-kabotu chintu chipati loko munsi lyaliso lyanswi mpati, eeyo njondakabona myaaka yotatwe yakainda.
