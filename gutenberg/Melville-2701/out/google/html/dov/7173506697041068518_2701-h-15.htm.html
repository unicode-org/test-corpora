CHAANDANO 74. Mutwe wa Nswi Yamusankwa—Kulanga Kwaandeene.

Aawa, lino, kuli nswi zyobile zipati, kazibikka mitwe yazyo antoomwe; atusangane ambabo, mpawo tubike antoomwe zyesu.

Akati kazyintu zipati zya folio leviathans, Sperm Whale a Right Whale nzizyo zizyibidwe kapati. Mbaalikke ba whale bavwimwa abantu. Ku Nantucketer, batondezya zibeela zyobile zyamisyobo yoonse ya nswi mpati. Mbuli kwiimpana kwaanze aakati kabo mbokubonwa mumitwe yabo; alimwi mbuli mutwe wamuntu umwi awumwi mpawo eechi chiindi chilengelela kuzwa kulubazu lwa Pequod; mpawo mbuli mbutukonzya kweenda kuzwa kuli chimwi kuya kuli chimwi, kwiinda mukuzabuka biyo bbuwa:—ino nkuli, nkondiyanda kuziba, nkomunga mwajana choolwe chibotu chakwiiya lwiiyo lwa cetology kwiinda aawa?

Kumatalikilo, wagambwa akusiyana aakati kamitwe eeyi. Boonse bobile mbapati loko mumizeezo yoonse; pesi kuli kweendelana kwa nsamu muli Sperm Whale ooko Right Whale nkoitakwe. Kuli bube bwiingi mumutwe waSperm Whale. Mbuli mbomubona, mulapa bupati bupati kuli nguwe, munzila yabulemu buzwide. Muchikozyano chiliwo lino, akwalo, bulemu oobu bulasumpulwa amubala wapepper amunyo wamutwe wakwe atala, kutondezya kuti wakomena aluzibo lunji. Mubufwiifwi, nguwenya ooyo bazubi banswi mbobaamba kuti "nswi iitegwa grey-headed whale."

Lino atubone eecho chiteendelani kapati mumitwe eeyi—nkokuti, zizo zyobile ziyandikana loko, liso akutwi. Kusyule kumbali lyamutwe, mpawo munsi, afwaafwi ambali lyamulomo wa nswi mpati, kuti wayanduulisisya, uyoobona liso litakwe nsingo, eelyo ndyokonzya kuyeeya kuti ndiliso lyamwana; aboobo kunze lyakweezyanisya koonse nkukozyanya abupati bwamutwe.

Lino, kuzwa kubusena oobu butaliboteli bwameso aanswi mpati, nchaantangalala kuti takonzyi kubona chintu chili kunembo, mbuli mbwakonzya kubona kusule. Mubbala lyomwe, busena bwameso aamulwizi bulikozyenye amatwi aamuntu; mpawo ulakonzya kuliyeeyela, lwako omwini, mbochinga chikuchitika kuli nduwe, naa wakalanga zintu kumbali kwiinda mumatwi aako. Ulakonzya kujana kuti ulakonzya buyo kulailila madigilii aali makumi otatwe aakubona kunembo lyamuzila uululeme wakubona; abamwi bali makumi otatwe musule lyawo. Kuti sinkondonyoko uuyoosya naakali kweenda kululama kuli nduwe, kajisi panga lisumpukide masiku, nowatakakonzya kumubona, mbweenya mbuli kuti naakali kukubba kuzwa musule. Mubbala lyomwe, ulakonzya kuba amisana yobile, mbuli mbutwaamba; pesi, muchiindi chomwe, alimwi, kunembo kobile (kumbele kumbali): nkaambo nchinzi chipa kuti muntu abe kunembo—nchinzi, masimpe, kunze lyameso aakwe?

Kwiindawo, kakuli mubanyama bamwi mbondikonzya kuyeeya lino, meso alibikkidwe munzila iitakonzyi kubonwa kuti asanganye nguzu zyabo zyakubona, kuchitila kuti azwe mufanikisyo omwe kutali zyobile kubongo; busena bulibedelede bwameso aa whale, aaandaanizyidwe mbuli mbwaabede ama cubic feet manji aamutwe muyumu, ooyo uuli akati kabo mbuli kalundu kapati kaandaanya madambwa obile mumakkuti; eezi, masimpe, zyeelede kwaandaanya mizeezo eeyo chibeela chilichose chili alikke nchochipa. Inswi mpati, aboobo, yeelede kubona mufanikisyo omwe kulubazu oolu, mpawo achimwi mufanikisyo kulubazu oolo; kakuli zyoonse zili akati kweelede kuba mudima mupati akubula chintu kulinguwe. Muntu ulakonzya, mubwini, kwaambwa kuti ulalanga kunze aanyika kuzwa kubbokesi lyakulindila lijisi zikwabilizyo zyobile zijatikizyidwe aawindo lyakwe. Pesi a nswi mpati, aaya mabbaaji obile alabikkwa ambali, akupanga mawindo obile aandeene, pesi mubuumba anyonganya kubona. Ooku kulibedelede kwameso aanswi mpati nchintu cheelede kuyeeyegwa lyoonse mukuzuba nswi; alimwi akuyeeyegwa asikubala muzibeela zimwi zitobela.

Mubuzyo uugambya uugambya ulakonzya kutalika kujatikizya makani aaya aalibonya mbuli kuti aajatikizya Leviathan. Asi ndeelede kukkutisikana amuzeezo. Kufumbwa buyo kuti meso aamuntu kaajulidwe mumumuni, mulimo wakubona tauli wakuyanda pe; nkokuti, takonzyi kugwasya kubona zintu zili kunembo lyakwe. Nikuliboobo, kufumbwa muntu wakamuchitikila ulakonzya kumuyiisya, kuti nikuba kuti ulakonzya kubweza zintu kakunyina kusalulula mukulanga biyo, nchintu chiyumu kuli nguwe, chakubikkila maanu, akuzula, kuti abone zintu zyobile—nikuba kuti nzipati buti naakuti nziniini—muchiindi chomwe; utabi apenzi kuti balalala kumbali akujatana. Asi kuti lino waboola kwaandaanya zintu eezyi zyobile, mpawo uzingulukide chimwi achimwi achizyungulukilo chamudima mupati; mpawo, kuti ubone chimwi chazyo, munzila iikonzya kuleta mizeezo yako kuli ncico, chimwi chiyoogwisigwa mumizeezo yako yachiindi eecho. Ino mbuti, aboobo, a whale? Masimpe, meso aakwe obile, muli ngawo, ayelede kubeleka achiindi chomwe; pesi sena boongo bwakwe bulaampindu, bulaswaanganya, alimwi bulaampuwo kwiinda bwamuntu, zyakuti ulakonzya achiindi chomwe kulanga-langa zintu zyobile ziindene, chimwi chili kulubazu lumwi lwakwe, mpawo chimwi chili kulubazu lumbi? Kuti ulakonzya, mpawo nchintu chibotu muli nguwe, mbuli kuti muntu ulakonzya kwiinda muzitondezyo zyamapenzi obile aaindene muli Euclid. Alimwi, kakulangwa-langwa loko, taakwe kutamvwana mukweezyanisya ooku.

Inga kacili buyo kuyandisya buyo, pesi lyoonse kulindime chakalibonya kuti, kweendeenda kupati kweendeenda kwakatondezyegwa abamwi nswi zyama whale nibazingulukidwe amaato otatwe naa one; kuyoowa akuba amulandu wakuyoowa bantu batakwe mpuwo, nchintu chidumide kuli ba whale baliboobu; Ndiyeya kuti zyoonse eezyi zizwa kukunyongana kwakuyanda, oomo nguzu zyabo zyaandeene azyaandeene loko zyakulanga mozyeelede kubajatikizya.

Asi kutwi kwa nswi mpati ilizwide luyandisisyo mbuli liso. Kuti koli mweenzu kumusyobo wabo, ulakonzya kuyandaula mitwe eeyi yobile kwamahola manji, mpawo tokonzyi kujana chibeela eecho. Kutwi takukwe tuzunda twaanze pe; mpawo mumulyango wini tokonzyi pe kubikka chipepa, aboobo nchintu chifwiifwi loko. Chili musule lyaliso asyoonto. Kujatikizya matwi aabo, ooku kusiyana kupati kweelede kubonwa aakati ka sperm whale akululyo. Nikuba kuti kutwi kwakusaanguna kulijisi mulyango waanze, ooko kwamamanino kuvwumbidwe koonse-koonse alimwi kweendelana acikanda, cakuti takulibonyi pe kuzwa anze.

Hena tazili zintu zigambya, kuti chintu chipati mbuli nswi mpati ilakonzya kubona nyika ameso masyoonto, mpawo akumvwa muzuzumo kwiinda mukutwi kaniini kwiinda kaziba? Asi kuti meso aakwe naakali mapati mbuli lensi ya Herschel's telescope mpati; amatwi aakwe akali mapati mbuli maanda aazikombelo; sena eecho chikonzya kumupa kuti ataboni kabotu, naa kumvwa kabotu? Peepe.—Nkaambo nzi ncosoleka "kukomezya" boongo bwako? Chibelesye.

Lino atuchite zintu zyoonse nzyotujisi, tukonzye kutanta mutwe wa sperm whale, kuchitila kuti ulale ansi kuya mujulu; mpawo, kutanta achitantaala kuya atala, kolanga ansi kumulomo; alimwi kuti nikwatali kuti mubili lino wakaandaanizyigwa kuzwa kuli nguwo, a lampi notwakali kukonzya kuseluka mu Mpako mpati ya Kentucky Mammoth ya mwida lyakwe. Pesi atujatisye aawa lino, mpawo tulange aawo mpotubede. Eelo kaka mulomo mubotu ncobeni alimwi uusalala! kuzwa ansi kusikila kujulu, yakabikkwa mumulongo, naa kuti yakabikkwa mapepa aasalala pyu, iimwekamweka mbuli ma satini aabanabwiinga.

Pesi kozwa anze lino, mpawo ulange eechi chintu chili munsi, chilibonya mbuli chivuniko chilamfu chifwiifwi chabbokesi pati lyakufweba, lijisi chikwabilizyo kumamanino, kutali kulubazu lomwe. Kuti wayinyamuna, kuchitila kuti ibweze atala, mpawo uyubunune milongo yamenyo, ilibonya mbuli kuti nchintu chibotu loko; azimwi ziliboobu, maawe! chakatondezya kulibanji kuti mbantu batakwe luzibo mumulimo wakuzuba nswi, aabo mbawida nswi eezyi amanguzu. Pesi nchintu chiyoosya loko kubona, aawo notusika ansi mulwizi, wabona nswi iitegwa sulky whale, kayiimvwi aawo kaimvwi, amulomo wakwe mupati, uulampa mamita aali kkumi amusanu, kauimvwi ansi munzila iiluzi amubili wakwe, kunyika yoonse mbuli bwato mbobuzungaana. Eeyi nswi mpati tiifwide pe; ulipengede biyo; kunze lyamisyobo, ambweni; hypochondriac; mpawo wakalala ansi, zyakuti zijazyo zyamulomo wakwe zyakaanguluka, akumusiya aawo muchiimo eecho chitali kabotu, kusampaula kumusyobo wakwe woonse, walo, kakunyina kudooneka, beelede kumusampaula milomo.

Muzyiindi zyiingi, mulomo ooyu wansi—uukonzya kuvwungululwa asikuzekaula uuchibwene—ulagwisigwa akusumpulwa atala abwaato kuchitila kuti agwisye meno aanzovu, mpawo ape chifuwa cha whale chiyumu eecho bazubi nswi nchobabelesya kupanga zintu zyamisyobo yoonse zigambya, kubikkilizya ankoli, maambulela, zibelesyo zyakubelesya kupanga nswi.

Kakuli chintu chilamfu, chitede, nsingo ilakwelwa mubwaato, mbuli kuti ninsinga; mpawo chiindi cheelede nichasika—mazuba masyoonto kuzwa mulimu umbi—Queequeg, Daggoo, a Tashtego, kabali basilisi bameno bacibwene, balibambilide kugwisya meno. Kajisi nkomo yakutebula, Queequeg ulauma nsingo; mpawo nsingo yakaangidwe ansi kuma ringbolts, mpawo chibelesyo chakabikkwa mujulu, balakwela meno aaya, mbuli ŋombe zya Michigan mbozikwela zisamu zya oak zyakale kuzwa mumasena aamusyokwe. Kanjaanji kuli meno aali makumi aane aabili woonse; mubaswi bakaindi, bakamana, pesi tiibakabola pe; nokuba kuzuzya kweendelana anzila yesu yakupanga. Mulomo ulasekwa kuba mabwe, mpawo wabikkwa mbuli mabwe aakuyaka maanda.





CHAANDANO 75. Mutwe wa Nswi Yalulyo—Kulanga Kwaandeene.

Kuzabuka bwato, lino atulange kabotu mutwe wa Nswi Yalulyo.

Mbuli mbobubede mutwe wa Sperm Whale uulemekwa ulakonzya kweezyanisyigwa ankalaki yankondo yabana Loma (ikapati kunembo, ooko nkoili muzingulukide); aboobo, mukubona kupati, mutwe wa Nswi Yalulyo ulaa kukozyanya kutali kabotu a nsapato mpati iijisi minwe ya galliot. Myaaka iili myaanda yobile yainda sikutanta bwato wakuDutch wakakozyanisya chiimo chawo kuli sikupanga nsapato wamamanino. Alimwi muchibaka eechi chamamanino, ooyo mukaintu uchembede wamuchikozyano chabana, abana bakwe, ulakonzya kukkala kabotu, walo abana bakwe boonse.

Asi mbuli mbouyaabuswena afwaafwi amutwe ooyu mupati ulatalika kuba azintu zisiyene, kweendelanya ambozibona zintu. Kuti waima atala lyayo mpawo walanga kumapopwe aaya obile aali muchikozyano cha F, ulakonzya kubweza mutwe woonse mbuli bass-viol mpati, antoomwe ama spiracles aaya, ma apertures aali mubbodela lyayo lyakulizya. Mpawo, alimwi, kuti walanga liso lyako aachintu eechi chikankamanisya, chilamfu, chikozyano chakamba atala aachintu eechi—chintu eechi chisalala pyu, chilamfu, eecho baGreenlanders ncobaamba kuti "musini," mpawo balobi bakumusanza "bbonet" ya Right Whale; kolanga biyo kuli ceeci, ulakonzya kubweza mutwe wamusamu mupati wa oak, uujisi chisaka chabayuni muchibuno chawo. Kufumbwa mbocibede, eelyo nolanga nkala eezyo zipona zikkede aawa aachisani eechi, muzeezo uliboobu ulakonzya kuboola mumizeezo yako; kunze kwakuti, masimpe, kuyandisya kwako kwabambululwa abbala lyakuti "musini" alyalo lyakapegwa kuli ndilyo; muchiimo eecho ulakonzya kubikkila maanu kapati mukuyeeya kuti eechi chinyama changuzu nchobeni mwami walwizi uusamide nkomo, ooyo musini wakwe uusalala pyu wakabikkwa antoomwe munzila iigambya. Asi kuti oyu nswi mpati kaili mwami, ulalangika mbuli kuti ulaa bbivwe loko kuti ulakonzya kusama mutwe wabwami. langa mulomo wansi uulengaana! nkunyema akufwubaazya kuli buti! bbivwe akufwubaazya, kweelanisigwa asikubeza, aali makumi obile mafiti mubulamfu amafiti osanwe kuya ansi; kufwubaazya akufwubaazya ooko kuyoopa malita aali 500 aamafuta azimwi.

Nchipa kuusa kupati, lino, kuti eeyi nswi mpati iitakwe coolwe yeelede kuba milomo ya hare. Mulyango uli afwaafwi afuti lyomwe kuya mujulu. Kulangilwa kuti banyina muchiindi chibotu bakali kutanta bwato kuya kunkomwe ya Peru, aawo mizuzumo yanyika niyakapa kuti lwizi lujaluke. Atala aamulomo ooyu, mbuli atala aamulyango uutezemuka, lino twanjila mumulomo. Mumajwi aangu kuti nindali ku Mackinaw, ndakeelede kubweza kuti eechi ncechibaka cha Indian wigwam. Mwami mubotu! eyi njenzila njaakainda Jona? Ciluli chili aamafiti aali kkumi aabili kuya mujulu, mpo chilaunka kukkona libotu, mbuli kuti kuli ntambo yachilundu aawo; aawo mabali aaya aajisi mbabo, aajisi nsinga, aboya, atutondezya zintu zigambya, ziimvwi nji, zilimbuli zibeela zyacifuwa ca whale, atwaambe kuti zili myaanda yotatwe kulubazu, eezyo zyeendelana amutwe naa chifuwa chamutwe, zipanga zivwunisyo zya Venetian eezyo zyaambwa kubusena bumbi. Kumbali aamafwuwa aaya kuli nsinga zyaboya, oomo Right Whale moibelesya kusefa maanzi, mpawo mubukkale bwayo ulajatisya nswi nsyoonto, aawo noijalukide mulomo ulainda mulwizi lwa brit muchiindi chakulya. Mukati kazivwunisyo zyamafwuwa, mbuli mboziimvwi munzila zyazyo, kuli zitondezyo zimwi zikankamanisya, zigolela, zikobela, alimwi azibeela, oomo bamwi basikuvwuba nswi mpati mobabala myaka yacilenge, mbuli myaka yamusamu wa oak kwiinda muzikobela zyakwe zizungulukide. Nikuba kuti kusyomeka kwachiimo eechi takukonzyi kutondezyegwa, pesi kuli bubotu bwakukozyanisya. Kufumbwa mbocibede, kuti twazumina, tweelede kupa myaka minji kapati ku Right Whale kwiinda mboikonzya kuboneka kuti ilakonzyeka.

Muchiindi, kwakali kulibonya kuti kwakali kuyandisya kuziba kapati kujatikizya zivwunisyo eezyi. Umwi mweenzu waku Purchas wakabaita kuti "masusu" mabotu mukati kamulomo wa nswi mpati;* umwi, "masusu aangulube"; mwaalumi watatu uchembede ku Hackluyt ubelesya mwaambo mubotu ooyu: "Kuli mababa aasika ku myaanda yobile amakumi osanwe aakomena kulubazu lomwe-lomwe lwachikobela chakwe chakujulu, alo aakomena atala alulimi lwakwe kulubazu lomwe-lomwe lwamulomo wakwe."

*Eezi zituyeezya kuti Nswi yakululyo ilijisi ndevu, naakuti ndevu, ilijisi masusu masyoonto aatuba aamwaikide atala aamulomo wansi. Chimwi chiindi eezyi zikobela zipa chiimo chabubbi kubusyu bwakwe butakwe bulemu.

Mpawo mbuli bamakaintu aabo bakaindi nibakali kweendeenda kabakkomene, nikuba kuti bakali mumilomo ya nswi mpati, mbuli mbomunga mwaamba; nikubaboobo, mukusamba, kakunyina kuyeeya kuliboobu, sena mazubaano tuuluka munsi lyamilomo yomwe kuti tukwabililwe; ambulela kali tente lyakavwumbidwe atalaa chifuwa chomwe.

Pesi lino koziluba zyoonse zyamabbusu amalezu kwakaindi kaniini, mpawo, koliimvwi mumulomo waNswi mpati, kolanga koonse koonse. Kubona zyoonse eezyi zibeela zyamafwuwa kazizyungulukide munzila iili kabotu, sena inga toyeeyi kuti uli mukati kacibeela cipati ca Haarlem, akulanga mipaipi yaco iili zyuulu? Nchineneede loko alimwi nchiteteete, mpawo chilakonzya kutyoka muzibeela-beela nichisumpulwa abwato. Ooyu lulimi lino kunembo lyesu; mukulanga biyo inga ndaamba kuti yakali yamabbola aali cisambomwe; nkokuti, chiyookupa mafuta aali mbuli mweelwe ooyo.

Ere eci, weelede kuti wakabona kasimpe kaceeco ncendakatalika—kuti Nswi ya Sperm Whale a Right Whale zilijisi mitwe yiindene. Mubufwaafwi, mpawo: mu Right Whale taakwe mugoti mupati wamaanzi; taakwe meno aabazovu pe; kutali mulamfu, mulomo mutete wamulomo wansi, mbuli wa Sperm Whale. Alimwi mu Sperm Whale taakwe zimwi zyazifwuwa eezyo; taakwe mulomo mupati wansi; alimwi kunyina chintu chili coonse cha lulimi. Alubo, Nswi Yalulyo ilijisi zibulo zyobile zyaanze, Nswi Yamusankwa ulijisi buyo yomwe.

Kolanga kumamanino, lino, kumitwe eeyi iilemekwa iijisi zikobela, nibachili koona antoomwe; nkaambo umwi ulafwambaana kubbila, katalembedwe, mulwizi; aimwi taikabi chiindi chilamfu mukutobela.

Sena ulakonzya kumvwa busyu bwaba Sperm Whale aawo? Nzimwi nzyaakafwa anzizyo, buyo zimwi zilonda zilamfu ankumu zyakamana. Ndiyeya kuti nsingo yakwe mpati ilizwide luumuno luli mbuli lwamucisaka, lwakazyalwa akutabikkila maanu kulufwu. Pesi lemba chiimo chamutwe umbi. Bona mulomo wansi uugambya, wakasinikizyigwa kulubazu lwachilonda, kuchitila kuti ujate nsingo. Hena mutwe woonse ooyu taulibonyi kuti ulaambuula aatala akusala kupati loko mukulanga lufu? Eeyi Nswi Yalulyo Ndibona kuti wakali Stoic; I Sperm Whale, muna Plato, ooyo wakali kukonzya kubweza Spinoza muminyaka yakwe yamamanino.





CHAANDANO 76. I-Battering-Ram.

Kakutanaba kuleka, kwachiindi chifwiifwi, mutwe wa Sperm Whale, inga ndayanda kuti yebo, mbuli syaazibwene mumakani aabuumi, kolanga-langa loko kunembo, mukubunganya kwawo koonse. Ndayanda kuti uchivwuntauzye lino amakanze aakulipangila omwini kweezyeezya kutakwe kuyungizigwa, kweezyeezya kwabusongo kwanguzu zyakuuma zikonzya kuba aawo. Aaka nkakaambo kayandikana; nkaambo weelede kulibambila makani aaya omwini, naakuti kukabe kutamani mbuli chintu chiyoosya loko, pesi kutali chamasimpe pe, ambweni kufumbwa nkochikonzya kujanwa mumakani woonse aalembedwe.

Wabona kuti muchiimo chakudubbwa cha Sperm Whale, kunembo lyamutwe wakwe lilaboneka mbuli kuti uliimvwi mumaanzi; yebo wabona kuti cibeela caansi chakumbele eecho chiseluka kuya musule, kuchitila kuti chipe busena bwakujokela musule kuchibaka chilamfu chitambula mulomo wansi uuli mbuli bbola; ulabona kuti mulomo uli munsi lyamutwe, mbubonya, masimpe, mbuli kuti mulomo wako uli munsi lyachilezu chako. Aboobo, lino weelede kuti wakabona kuti kunembo lya mutwe wa Sperm Whale mbubwaanda bufwide, butaboneki, butakwe chibeela nichiba chimwi naakuti chiimo chibotu chamusyobo uliwoonse. Kuya kunembo, lino weelede kuyeeya kuti buyo mumbali, munsi, musule, kunembo lyamutwe, mpokuli kabeela kaniini kachifuwa; alimwi kutali kusikila wasikila afwaafwi aamaulu aali makumi abili kuzwa ankumu kuti usike kukukomena kwamutwe koonse. Kakkede muzibeela zitazyibidwe, munzila, alimwi akuvwunguluka, mukuyoowa kwabo, kulibonya kuti cileendelana amuzeezo wanguzu zyakwe zyoonse zyakulanga cibeela cakwe camaseseke kuti ncecuuno cabusongo bwakwe.
