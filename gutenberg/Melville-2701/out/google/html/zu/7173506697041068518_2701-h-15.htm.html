ISAHLUKO 74. Ikhanda Lomkhomo Wembewu—Umbono Ohlukene.

Nansi, manje, imikhomo emibili emikhulu, ibeka amakhanda ayo ndawonye; masizihlanganise, sibeke okwethu.

Ohlelweni oluhle lwe-folio leviathan, i-Sperm Whale kanye ne-Right Whale yizona eziphawuleka kakhulu. Iwukuphela kwemikhomo ezingelwa umuntu njalo. Ku-Nantucketer, bethula ukweqisa okubili kwazo zonke izinhlobo ezaziwayo zomkhomo. Njengoba umehluko wangaphandle phakathi kwabo ubonakala ikakhulukazi emakhanda abo; futhi njengenhloko ngayinye yilesi sikhathi esilenga ohlangothini lwePequod; futhi njengoba singase sisuke komunye siye komunye ngokukhululeka, ngokumane siwele idekhi:—lapho, ngithanda ukwazi, ingabe uzothola ithuba elingcono lokufunda i-cetology esebenzayo kunalapha?

Okokuqala, uhlatshwa umxhwele umehluko ojwayelekile phakathi kwala makhanda. Zombili zinkulu ngokwanele kuwo wonke unembeza; kodwa kukhona ukulinganisa okuthile kwezibalo ku-Sperm Whale lapho i-Right Whale ishoda ngokudabukisayo. Kukhona umlingiswa owengeziwe ekhanda leSperm Whale. Njengoba uyibona, unikezela ngokuzenzakalelayo ukuphakama okukhulu kakhulu kuye, ngokwephuzu lokugcwala kwesithunzi. Esimeni samanje, futhi, lesi sithunzi siphakanyiswa umbala wepelepele nosawoti wekhanda lakhe engqungqutheleni, okunikeza uphawu lokuguga kanye nolwazi olukhulu. Kafushane, uyilokho abadobi abakubiza ngobuchwepheshe ngokuthi “umkhomo onekhanda elimpunga.”

Manje ake siphawule ukuthi yini engafani nhlobo kula makhanda—okungukuthi, izitho ezimbili ezibaluleke kakhulu, iso nendlebe. Ingemuva elikude eceleni kwekhanda, futhi liphansi phansi, eduze ne-angle yomhlathi womkhomo, uma uphenya kancane, ekugcineni uzobona iso elingenasici, obungafisa ukuba iso lethole lethole; ngakho kuya ngesilinganiso kuye ngobukhulu bekhanda.

Manje, kulendawo eyinqaba eseceleni yamehlo omkhomo, kusobala ukuthi ayikwazi ukubona into engaphambili ncamashi, njengoba nje ingakwazi ukubona impumalanga ncamashi. Ngamafuphi, ukuma kwamehlo omkhomo kufanelana nendawo yezindlebe zomuntu; futhi ungase uzicabangele ngokwakho, ukuthi kuzokuhambela kanjani, ingabe uye wahlola izinto eceleni ngezindlebe zakho. Uzothola ukuthi ungayala kuphela amadigri angamashumi amathathu okubona ngaphambi komugqa oqondile oseceleni wokubona; futhi cishe amashumi amathathu ngaphezulu ngemuva kwayo. Uma isitha sakho esibuhlungu besihamba siqonde kuwe, siphakamise inkemba emini, ubungeke ukwazi ukusibona, njengoba nje besikweba ngemuva. Ngamafuphi nje, ungaba nemihlane emibili, ngomqondo ongokomfanekiso; kodwa, ngesikhathi esifanayo, futhi, izinhlangothi ezimbili (izinhlangothi ezisemaceleni): ngoba yini eyenza ingaphambili lomuntu - yini ngempela, ngaphandle kwamehlo akhe?

Ngaphezu kwalokho, nakuba kwezinye izilwane eziningi engingazicabanga manje, amehlo atshalwe ngendlela engaqondakali ukuze ahlanganise amandla azo okubona, ukuze enze isithombe esisodwa hhayi ezimbili ebuchosheni; indawo engavamile yamehlo omkhomo, ahlukaniswe ngokuphumelelayo njengoba engamamitha ama-cubic amaningi ekhanda eliqinile, eliphakeme phakathi kwawo njengentaba enkulu ehlukanisa amachibi amabili ezigodini; lokhu, kunjalo, kufanele kuhlukanise ngokuphelele imibono enikezwa isitho ngasinye esizimele. Ngakho-ke, umkhomo kufanele ubone isithombe esisodwa esihlukile kulolu hlangothi, nesinye isithombe esihlukile kuloluya hlangothi; kuyilapho konke okuphakathi kufanele kube ubumnyama obukhulu futhi kube yize kuye. Umuntu kungase kuthiwe ubuka umhlaba esebhokisini lomlindi elinamasashi amabili ahlangene efasiteleni lakhe. Kodwa nge-whale, lawa ma-sashes amabili afakwe ngokuhlukana, okwenza amafasitela amabili ahlukene, kodwa ngokudabukisayo akhinyabeze ukubuka. Lokhu okungavamile kwamehlo omkhomo kuyinto okufanele ihlale ikhunjulwa endabeni yokudoba; kanye nokukhunjulwa ngumfundi kwezinye izigcawu ezilandelayo.

Kungase kuqalwe umbuzo onelukuluku nodidayo mayelana nale ndaba ebonwayo njengokuthinta uLeviyathani. Kodwa kufanele ngenelise ukusikisela. Uma nje amehlo omuntu esavuleke ekukhanyeni, isenzo sokubona asisona sokuzithandela; okungukuthi, akakwazi ukusiza ngokubona noma yiziphi izinto eziphambi kwakhe. Noma kunjalo, noma imuphi okuhlangenwe nakho komuntu kuyomfundisa, ukuthi nakuba engase akwazi ukushanela izinto ngokungakhethi kanye nje, akunakwenzeka ngaye, ngokucophelela, futhi ngokuphelele, ukuba ahlole noma yiziphi izinto ezimbili—kungakhathaliseki ukuthi zinkulu kangakanani noma zincane kangakanani—ngesikhathi esisodwa noma esifanayo; kungabi ndaba zalutho uma zilala zihlangene futhi zithintana. Kodwa uma manje niza ukuzohlukanisa lezi zinto ezimbili, futhi nihaqe ngayinye ngesiyingi sobumnyama obukhulu; khona-ke, ukuze ubone enye yazo, ngendlela yokuthi ulethe ingqondo yakho kukho, enye izokhishwa ngokuphelele ekuqapheleni kwakho kwamanje. Unjani-ke ngomkhomo? Yiqiniso, womabili amehlo akhe, kuwo ngokwawo, kumelwe enze kanyekanye; kodwa ingabe ingqondo yakhe ibanzi, ihlanganisa, futhi icashe kakhulu kunomuntu, kangangokuthi ngesikhathi esifanayo angahlola ngokucophelela amathemba amabili ahlukene, elinye libe ngakolunye uhlangothi lwakhe, kanti elinye libheke kolunye uhlangothi? Uma ekwazi, ingabe kuyinto emangalisayo kuye, sengathi umuntu ukwazi kanyekanye ukudlula imibukiso yezinkinga ezimbili ezihlukene e-Euclid. Futhi, uma kuphenywa ngokuqinile, akukho ukungahambisani nalokhu kuqhathaniswa.

Kungase kube ukufisa okungenzi lutho, kodwa kuye kwahlala kubonakala kimi, ukuthi ukuguquguquka okungavamile kokunyakaza okuboniswa eminye imikhomo lapho ivinjezelwa izikebhe ezintathu noma ezine; uvalo kanye nesibopho sokwethuka, okuvame kakhulu emikhomo enjalo; Ngicabanga ukuthi konke lokhu ngokungaqondile kuphuma ekudidekeni okungenakuzisiza kwentando yeningi, lapho amandla abo okubona ahlukene futhi ahluke kakhulu kufanele ababandakanye.

Kodwa indlebe yomkhomo igcwele ilukuluku njengeso. Uma ungumuntu ongaziwa ngokuphelele emncintiswaneni wabo, ungase uzingele phezu kwalawa makhanda amabili amahora, futhi ungalokothi uthole leso sitho. Indlebe ayinalo iqabunga elingaphandle; futhi embobeni ngokwayo awukwazi ukufaka i-quill, ngakho-ke umzuzu omangalisayo. Ifakwe ngemuva kancane kweso. Ngokuphathelene nezindlebe zabo, lo mehluko obalulekile kufanele ubonwe phakathi kwe-sperm whale kanye kwesokudla. Ngenkathi indlebe yangaphambili inembobo yangaphandle, leyo yokugcina imbozwe ngokuphelele futhi ngokulinganayo phezu kolwelwesi, ukuze ingabonakali kahle ngaphandle.

Akuyona yini ilukuluku, ukuthi isidalwa esikhulu kangaka njengomkhomo kufanele sibone umhlaba ngeso elincane kangaka, futhi sizwe ukuduma kwendlebe ngendlebe encane kunogwaja? Kodwa uma amehlo akhe ayebanzi njengelensi yesibonakude esikhulu sikaHerschel; nezindlebe zakhe zivulekile njengemipheme yezindlu eziphakeme; lokho kungamenza angabe esakwazi ukubona, noma ukuzwa okubukhali? Lutho neze.—Kungani-ke uzama “ukukhulisa” ingqondo yakho? Yibeke ngaphansi.

Manje masibe nanoma yiziphi izihibe nezinjini zesitimu esinazo, singakwazi ukungena phezu kwekhanda lomkhomo wesidoda, ukuze ulale phansi phezulu; khona-ke, ukhuphuka ngeladi ukuya esiqongweni, ube nokulunguza emlonyeni; futhi ukube bekungengenxa yokuthi umzimba manje usuhlukene ngokuphelele nawo, ngesibani besingase sehlele eMgedeni omkhulu waseKentucky Mammoth wesisu sakhe. Kodwa ake sibambelele lapha ngalelizinyo, futhi sibheke ngathi lapho sikhona. Yeka umlomo omuhle ngempela nobukeka umsulwa! ukusuka phansi kuye kusilingi, kufakwe ulayini, noma kunalokho kufakwe iphepha ngolwelwesi olumhlophe olubenyezelayo, olucwebezelayo njengama-satin omshado.

Kodwa phuma manje, futhi ubuke lo mhlathi omangalisayo ongezansi, obonakala njengesivalo eside esiwumngcingo sebhokisi elikhulu lesinemfu, elinehinji ekugcineni, esikhundleni sohlangothi olulodwa. Uma uyihlwitha, ukuze uyithole phezulu, futhi uveze imigqa yayo yamazinyo, ibonakala iyi-portcullis esabekayo; Futhi, maye! kufakazela kwabaningi abampofu emkhakheni wezokudoba, okuwela phezu kwabo la ma-spikes ngamandla okubethelwa. Kodwa okubi kakhulu kakhulu ukubona, lapho kufiphala phansi olwandle, ubona umkhomo omanzi, untanta lapho ulenga, nomhlathi wawo omangalisayo, ubude obungamafidi ayishumi nanhlanu, ulenga phansi ngqo kuma-engeli angakwesokudla nomzimba wakhe, kuwo wonke umhlaba njenge-jib-boom yomkhumbi. Lomkhomo awufile; udangele kuphela; ngaphandle kwezinhlobo, mhlawumbe; i-hypochondriac; futhi uphansi phezulu, kangangokuthi izintiko zemihlathi yakhe ziye zaxega, zamshiya lapho ekulolo hlobo losizi olubi, isihlamba kuso sonke isizwe sakubo, okufanele, ngokungangabazeki, simbethe imihlathi.

Ezimweni eziningi lo mhlathi ongezansi—ongagonwa kalula umdwebi owuqeqeshelwe umsebenzi—uyakhishwa futhi uphakanyiswe emphemeni ngenjongo yokukhipha amazinyo endlovu, futhi unikeze ithambo lomkhomo elimhlophe eliqinile abadobi abaklama ngalo zonke izinhlobo zezinto ezithandwayo, okuhlanganisa izinduku, izambulela, nezibambo zokusebenzisa iziswebhu.

Ngokuphakamisa okude, okukhathele umhlathi uhudulwa ebhodini, njengokungathi ihange; futhi lapho kufika isikhathi esifanele—ezinsukwini ezimbalwa ngemva komunye umsebenzi—uQueequeg, uDaggoo, noTashtego, njengoba bonke bengodokotela bamazinyo abanekhono, baqala ukudonsa amazinyo. Nge-spade ebukhali, i-Queequeg ihlaba izinsini; bese kuthi umhlathi uboshelwe phansi ube ama-ringbolt, futhi insimbi igetshengwa phezulu, bawakhiphe lamazinyo, njengoba izinkabi zaseMichigan zidonsa iziphunzi ze-oki amadala emazweni asehlathini. Ngokuvamile kunamazinyo angamashumi amane nambili esewonke; emikhomo emidala, eguge kakhulu, kodwa engaboli; futhi igcwaliswe ngemfashini yethu yokwenziwa. Umhlathi emva kwalokho usahwa ube ngama-slabs, bese unqwabelaniswa njengezinsimbi zokwakha izindlu.





ISAHLUKO 75. Ikhanda Lomkhomo Wakwesokudla—Umbono Ohlukene.

Seqa umkhumbi, manje ake sibheke kahle ikhanda le-Right Whale.

Njengokwakheka okujwayelekile ikhanda le-Sperm Whale elihloniphekile lingase lifaniswe nenqola yempi yamaRoma (ikakhulukazi ngaphambili, lapho iyindilinga ebanzi kakhulu); ngakho-ke, ekubukeni okubanzi, ikhanda le-Right Whale liphethe ukufana okungafani nesicathulo esikhulu se-galliot-toed. Eminyakeni engamakhulu amabili edlule umkhumbi waseDutch omdala wafanisa ukuma kwawo nokokugcina komthungi wezicathulo. Futhi kulesi sicathulo esifanayo sokugcina, leso salukazi sasenkulisa, esinezingane eziswebezelayo, singahlala ngokunethezeka, sona nayo yonke inzalo yaso.

Kodwa njengoba usondela kuleli khanda elikhulu iqala ukuthatha izici ezihlukene, ngokombono wakho. Uma umi esiqongweni sayo bese ubheka lezi zimpophoma ezimbili ezimise okuka-F, ungathatha ikhanda lonke libe i-bass-viol enkulu, kanye nalezi zimbobo, izimbobo ebhodini layo lokukhala. Khona-ke, futhi, uma ugxilisa iso lakho kulokhu kugqunywa okungavamile, okunezimbotshana, okunjengekhekheba phezulu kwenqwaba—lento eluhlaza, i-barnacle, abantu baseGreenland abayibiza ngokuthi “umqhele,” nabadobi baseNingizimu “ngebhonethi” yoMkhomo Olungile; ugxilisa amehlo akho kulokhu kuphela, wawuthatha ikhanda ulibhekise esiqwini se-oki esikhulukazi, esinesidleke senyoni endaweni yaso. Kunoma ikuphi, uma ubuka lezo izinkalankala ezihlala lapha kule bhonethi, umqondo onjalo cishe uzofika kuwe; ngaphandle uma, ngempela, ukuthanda kwakho kumiswe yigama lobuchwepheshe elithi “umqhele” nalo elinikezwe wona; lapho uyoba nesithakazelo esikhulu ekucabangeni ukuthi lesi silo esinamandla empeleni siyinkosi yolwandle enomqhele, umqhele wayo oluhlaza ubekwe ndawonye ngalendlela emangalisayo. Kodwa uma lo mkhomo uyinkosi, ungumuntu obukeka enokuthukuthela ukuze ahlobise umqhele. Bheka lolodebe lwangezansi olulengayo! yeka i-sulk ne-pout enkulu lapho! i-sulk ne-pout, ngesilinganiso sombazi, cishe amafidi angamashumi amabili ubude namamitha amahlanu ukushona; i-sulk ne-pout ezokunikeza amalitha angama-500 kawoyela nangaphezulu.

Kuyadabukisa kakhulu, manje ukuthi lo mkhomo oneshwa kufanele ube ne-hare-lipped. Umfantu ucishe ube yimitha ukuwela. Mhlawumbe umama ngesikhathi esibalulekile wayehamba ngomkhumbi ehla ogwini lwasePeru, lapho ukuzamazama komhlaba kubangela ukuba ulwandle lugcwale amanzi. Phezu kwaloludebe, njengaphezu kombundu oshelelayo, manje sesishelela emlonyeni. Ngezwi lami ngangiseMackinaw, kufanele ngithathe lokhu kube ingaphakathi le-wigwam yaseNdiya. Kuhle Nkosi! yiyona ndlela uJona ahamba ngayo na? Uphahla lucishe lube ngamafidi ayishumi nambili ukuphakama, futhi lugijima luye endaweni ecijile kahle, njengokungathi kukhona isigxobo esivamile lapho; kuyilapho lezi zinhlangothi ezinezimbambo, ezinama-arched, ezinoboya, zisethula nalawo ma-slats amangalisayo, amile kancane, amise okwe-scimetar e-whalebone, athi amakhulu amathathu ohlangothini, okuncike engxenyeni engenhla yekhanda noma ithambo lomqhele, akha lezo zimpumputhe zaseVenice eziye zashiwo ngokusheshisa kwenye indawo. Imiphetho yalawa mathambo ithosiwe ngemicu enoboya, lapho umkhomo ofanele ugobe khona amanzi, futhi agcina izinhlanzi ezincane, lapho evulelekile uhamba olwandle lweBrit ngesikhathi sokudla. Ezingxenyeni ezimaphakathi zethambo, njengoba emi ngokulandelana kwazo okungokwemvelo, kunezimpawu ezithile ezinelukuluku, amajika, izimbobo, namagquma, lapho amanye ama-whalemen abala iminyaka yesidalwa, njengobudala be-oki ngamasongo aso ayindilinga. Nakuba isiqiniseko salesi sici sisekude nokukhonjiswa, nokho sinephunga elimnandi lamathuba okufanisa. Kunoma ikuphi, uma sivumela kukho, kufanele sinikeze iminyaka engaphezulu kakhulu ku-Right Whale kunalokho okuyobonakala kunengqondo ekuqaleni.

Ezikhathini zasendulo, kubonakala sengathi kuye kwanqoba izifiso ezinelukuluku mayelana nalezi zimpumputhe. Omunye umgibeli ePurchas uwabiza ngokuthi “amadevu” amangalisayo angaphakathi emlonyeni womkhomo;* omunye, “amaklebe ezingulube”; ikhehla lesithathu eHackluyt lisebenzisa ulimi olumnandi olulandelayo: “Kunezimpiko ezingaba amakhulu amabili namashumi amahlanu ezimila ohlangothini ngalunye lwenqunu yalo engaphezulu, ezigobe phezu kolimi lwayo ohlangothini ngalunye lomlomo walo.”

*Lokhu kusikhumbuza ukuthi i-Right Whale inohlobo lwendevu ngempela, noma kunalokho inamadevu, ahlanganisa izinwele ezimhlophe ezimbalwa ezisabalele engxenyeni engenhla yomhlathi ongezansi ongaphandle. Ngezinye izikhathi lawa ma-tuft aveza ukubukeka kwakhe okunesizotha.

Njengoba wonke umuntu azi, zona lezi “zingulube zezingulube,” “izimpiko,” “izindevu,” “izimpumputhe,” nanoma yini oyithandayo, zinikeza abesifazane amabhasi abo nezinye izinto eziqinisayo. Kodwa kulokhu, isidingo sekuyisikhathi eside sincipha. Kwakungesikhathi seNdlovukazi u-Anne lapho ithambo lase likhazimula khona, i-farthingale yiyona yonke imfashini. Futhi njengoba lawo ma-dame asendulo ayehambahamba kancane, nakuba emihlathini yomkhomo, njengoba ungase usho; noma kunjalo, eshaweni, ngokungacabangeli okufanayo, namuhla sindiza ngaphansi kwemihlathi efanayo ukuze sivikeleke; isambulela siyitende elendlalwe phezu kwethambo elifanayo.

Kodwa manje khohlwa konke mayelana nezimpumputhe nentshebe isikhashana, futhi, ukuma emlonyeni we-Right Whale, qala kabusha eduze kwakho. Uma ubona zonke lezi zinsika zamathambo zihamba ngendlela ehlelekile, ubungeke yini ucabange ukuthi ubungaphakathi kwesitho esikhulu se-Haarlem, futhi ugqolozele amapayipi ayo ayinkulungwane? Ngokhaphethi esithweni sinombhede weTurkey ethambile kakhulu-ulimi, olunamathele, njengokungathi, phansi emlonyeni. Ikhuluphele kakhulu futhi ithambile, futhi ikwazi ukuklebhula izicucu ngokuyiphakamisa emphemeni. Lolu limi oluphambi kwethu manje; ngokubuka nje ngingasho ukuthi kwakuyimiphongolo eyisithupha; okungukuthi, izokunikeza cishe lelo nani lamafutha.

Ere nje lokhu, kufanele ukuthi wabona iqiniso lokuthi engiqale ngawo - ukuthi umkhomo we-sperm kanye nomkhomo ofanele amakhanda ahlukene ngokuphelele. Ukuhlanganisa-ke: ku-Right Whale's awukho umthombo omkhulu wesidoda; awekho nhlobo amazinyo endlovu; isikhathi eside, i-mandible encane yomhlathi ongezansi, njenge-Sperm Whale. Futhi ku-Sperm Whale azikho zalezo zimpumputhe zethambo; akukho udebe olukhulu olungezansi; futhi kayilutho ngolimi. Futhi, i-Right Whale inezimbobo ezimbili ezingaphandle, i-Sperm Whale eyodwa kuphela.

Bheka okokugcina, manje, kulawa makhanda ahloniphekile anezigqoko, ngenkathi elele ndawonye; ngoba umuntu uzocwila masinyane, engabhalwanga, olwandle; omunye ngeke abe mude kakhulu ekulandeleni.

Ungakwazi yini ukubamba inkulumo ye-Sperm Whale lapho? Kuyafana nokufa nayo, eminye imibimbi emide esebunzini ibonakala manje isiphelile. Ngicabanga ukuthi ibunzi lakhe elibanzi ligcwele i-placidity efana ne-prairie, ezalwa ngokunganaki okucatshangelwayo mayelana nokufa. Kodwa maka isisho selinye ikhanda. Bheka lolo debe olumangalisayo olungezansi, olucindezelwe ngephutha ohlangothini lomkhumbi, ukuze ubambe umhlathi ngokuqinile. Ingabe lonke leli khanda alibonakali likhuluma ngesinqumo esikhulu esisebenzayo sokubhekana nokufa? Lo Mkhoma Olungile ngiwuthatha ngokuthi wawungumStoyiku; i-Sperm Whale, i-Platonia, okungenzeka ukuthi wathatha i-Spinoza eminyakeni yakhe yokugcina.





ISAHLUKO 76. Inqama Eshayayo.

Ngaphambi kokuyeka, okokuqala, ikhanda leSperm Whale, ngingathanda ukuthi, njengesazi sokusebenza komzimba esinengqondo, umane-uphawule isici saso sangaphambili, kukho konke ukuqoqwa kwawo okuhlangene. Ngicela ukuphenye manje ngombono owodwa wokuzakhela isilinganiso esingenahaba, esihlakaniphile sanoma yimaphi amandla enqama yokushaya angafakwa lapho. Nali iphuzu elibalulekile; ngoba kufanele uxazulule ngokugculisayo lolu daba nawe, noma uhlale ungongakholwayo ngesinye sezehlakalo ezimbi kakhulu, kodwa hhayi ezeqiniso elincane, mhlawumbe noma yikuphi okungatholakala kuwo wonke umlando oqoshiwe.

Uyabona ukuthi endaweni evamile yokubhukuda ye-Sperm Whale, ingaphambili lekhanda layo lethula indiza ecishe iqonde ngokuphelele emanzini; uyabona ukuthi ingxenye engezansi yaleyo ngaphambili yehlela kakhulu emuva, ukuze inikeze indawo eningi yokuhlehla yesokhethi ende ethola umhlathi ongezansi onjenge-boom; uyabona ukuthi umlomo ungaphansi kwekhanda ngokuphelele, ngendlela efanayo, ngempela, njengokungathi owakho umlomo ungaphansi kwesilevu sakho ngokuphelele.
