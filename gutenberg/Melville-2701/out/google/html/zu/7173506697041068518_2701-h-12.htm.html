ISAHLUKO 55. Sezithombe Ezinyantisayo Zemikhomo.

Ngizokwengeza upende omude kuwe kanye nomuntu ongakwazi ngaphandle kweseyili, into efana nefomu langempela lomkhomo njengoba ebonakala esweni lomkhomo lapho emzimbeni wakhe ophelele umkhomo uboshelwe eceleni komkhumbi-mkhomo ukuze ukwazi ukunyathelwa kahle lapho. Ngakho-ke, kungase kube usizo, ngaphambili ukukhangisa kulezo zithombe zakhe ezicatshangelwayo ezinelukuluku, ezibekela inselele ukholo lukamninimhlaba kuze kube namuhla. Sekuyisikhathi sokulungisa umhlaba kahle kule ndaba, ngokufakazela izithombe ezinjalo zomkhomo ukuthi azilungile.

Kungenzeka ukuthi umthombo oyinhloko wazo zonke lezo zinkohliso ezingokomfanekiso uyotholakala phakathi kwezithombe eziqoshiwe ezindala zamaHindoo, zaseGibhithe, naseGrisi. Kusukela ngalezo zikhathi zokusungula kodwa ezingenanembeza lapho emapulangeni emabula emathempelini, izisekelo zezithombe, nasemahawuni, izindondo, izinkomishi, nezinhlamvu zemali, ihlengethwa lalidwetshwa ngezikali zamaketanga zempi njengeSaladin, kanye nekhanda eliyisigqoko njengeSt. George; kusukela ngaleso sikhathi kunokuthile okufana nelayisense okukhona, hhayi kuphela ezithombeni ezidumile zomkhomo, kodwa ezinhlelweni eziningi zesayensi ngaye.

Manje, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi yini, isithombe sasendulo esisekhona noma kunjalo okuthiwa esomkhomo, sitholakala e-cavern-pagoda edumile yase-Elephanta, eNdiya. AmaBrahmin akholelwa ukuthi emifanekisweni eqoshiwe ecishe ingapheli yalelothempeli lasendulo, yonke imisebenzi yezentengiselwano neminako, yonke into ecatshangwayo yomuntu, yayiyizinkathi ezafanekiselwa ngaphambi kokuba noma iyiphi yazo ibe khona. Akumangazi-ke, ukuthi ngandlela thize umsebenzi wethu omuhle wokudoba imikhomo bekufanele ngabe ufihlwe lapho. Umkhomo we-Hindoo okukhulunywe ngawo, uvela emnyangweni ohlukile wodonga, obonisa ukuzalwa kukaVishnu osesimweni seleviathan, eyaziwa ngokuthi i-Matse Avatar. Kodwa nakuba lesi sithombe esiqoshiwe siyingxenye yomuntu nengxenye yomkhomo, ukuze nje ukunikeza umsila wakamuva, nokho leso sigaba esincane sakhe asilungile. Ibukeka kakhulu njengomsila wenhlwathi ogobhozayo, kunezandla ezibanzi zezinhlanzi ezinkulu zomkhomo wangempela.

Kodwa hamba uye kumaGalari amadala, futhi ubuke manje umfanekiso womdwebi omkhulu ongumKristu wale nhlanzi; ngoba akaphumelelanga ukwedlula iHindoo yangaphambi kwemvula. Isithombe sika-Guido sika-Perseus ehlenga i-Andromeda ku-sea-monster noma umkhomo. UGuido wawuthathaphi umfuziselo wesidalwa esiyinqaba njengaleso? Futhi uHogarth, ekudwebeni isiqephu esifanayo kweyakhe ethi “Perseus Descending,” akenzi ngisho nokukodwa okungcono. I-corpulence enkulu yaleso silo se-Hogarthian inyakaza phezulu, idonsa kancane iyintshi eyodwa yamanzi. Inohlobo oluthile lwe-howdah emhlane wayo, futhi umlomo wayo onezimbotshana ezigoqekile okugingqika kuwo, ungase uyiswe eSangweni Lamambuka elisuka eMfuleni iThames ngamanzi liya eMbhoshongweni. Khona-ke, kukhona imikhomo i-Prodromus ye-Scotch Sibbald endala, nomkhomo kaJona, njengoba kuboniswe emibhalweni yamaBhayibheli amadala kanye nokusikeka kwama-primer amadala. Kuyakuthiwani ngalaba? Ngokuqondene nomkhoma wohlanganisa izincwadi osongwayo onjengesiqu somvini esizungeza isitokwe sehange elehlayo—njengoba sigxivizwe futhi sigxivizwe emhlane nasemakhasini ezihloko zezincwadi eziningi ezindala nezintsha—lokho kuyisilwane esihle kakhulu kodwa esihle kakhulu, esilingiswayo, ngiyasithatha, emifanekisweni enjengamavazi asendulo. Nakuba emhlabeni wonke ihlengethwa, nokho ngibiza le nhlanzi ye-book-binder ngokuthi umzamo wokuthola umkhomo; ngoba kwakuhloswe ngakho lapho idivayisi yethulwa okokuqala. Yethulwa umshicileli omdala wase-Italy ndawana thize cishe ngekhulu le-15, phakathi Nokuvuselelwa Kokufunda; futhi ngalezo zinsuku, ngisho nasekupheleni kwesikhathi, amahlengethwa ayedume kakhulu ukuba abe uhlobo lukaLeviyathani.

Kumavignettes nokunye okuhlotshiswe ngezincwadi zasendulo ngezinye izikhathi uyohlangana nokuthinta i-whale, lapho zonke izinhlobo ze-spouts, jets d'eau, iziphethu ezishisayo nokubanda, uSaratoga no-Baden-Baden, beza bephuphuma ebuchosheni bakhe obungaphelile. Ekhasini lesihloko lohlelo lokuqala "lweNtuthuko Yokufunda" uzothola imikhomo enelukuluku.

Kodwa ngokuyeka yonke le mizamo engafanele, ake sithi nhla kulezo zithombe zikaleviyathani okuthiwa zinengqondo, izincazelo zesayensi, yilabo abaziyo. Eqoqweni likaHarris elidala lohambo lwasolwandle kunezingcwecwe zemikhomo ezithathwe encwadini yesiDashi yohambo, AD 1671, enesihloko esithi “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen ngomkhumbi u-Jonas in the Whale, uPeter Peterson waseFriesland, umpetha.” Kwenye yalezo zingcwecwe imikhomo, njengezihlenga ezinkulu zezingodo, imelelwe ilele phakathi kweziqhingi zeqhwa, namabhere amhlophe egijima phezu kwemihlane yawo ephilayo. Kwelinye ipuleti, iphutha elimangalisayo lenziwa ngokumelela umkhomo onama-perpendicular flukes.

Khona-ke futhi, kukhona i-quarto ebabazekayo, eyabhalwa omunye uKaputeni uColnett, uKaputeni wePosi ebuthweni lasolwandle lamaNgisi, enesihloko esithi “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, ngenjongo yokwelula iSpermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Kule ncwadi kunohlaka okuthiwa “Isithombe Se-Physeter noma i-Spermaceti whale, esidwetshwe ngesilinganiso sisuka kowabulawa ogwini lwaseMexico, ngo-August, 1793, futhi saphakanyiswa emphemeni.” Angingabazi ukuthi ukapteni uthathwe lesi sithombe esiyiqiniso ukuze kuzuze osolwandle bakhe. Uma ngibala nje into eyodwa ngawo, mangisho ukuthi uneso elasebenza, ngokwesilinganiso esihambisana naso, kumkhomo osukhule ngokugcwele, elingenza iso lalowo mkhomo libe iwindi lomnsalo elingamamitha amahlanu ubude. Awu, nduna yami eyiqhawe, kungani ungasinikezanga uJona ebuka ngalelo liso!

Futhi ukuhlanganiswa okucophelela kakhulu kwe-Natural History akukhona ukuthi kuzuze abasha nabathambile, akunabo ububi obufanayo bephutha. Bheka leyo ncwadi ethandwayo ethi “Imvelo Enyakazayo Yomkhandi Wegolide.” Ohlelweni olufushanisiwe lwaseLondon luka-1807, kunezingcwecwe okuthiwa “umkhomo” kanye “ne-narwhale.” Angifisi ukubonakala ngiyisigebengu, kodwa lo mkhomo ongabukeki ufana nengulube yengulube; futhi, ngokuqondene nomkhomo, ukuwuthi shazi nje kwanele ukuba kusimangaze umuntu, ukuthi kuleli khulu leshumi nesishiyagalolunye imvubu enje ingase igawulwe ngesundu ngobuqotho kunoma yimuphi umphakathi ohlakaniphile wabafana besikole.

Khona-ke, futhi, ngo-1825, uBernard Germain, u-Count de Lacépède, isazi semvelo esikhulu, wanyathelisa incwadi yesayensi ye-whale, lapho kunezithombe eziningana zezinhlobo ezahlukene zeLeviyathani. Konke lokhu akukona nje kuphela okungalungile, kodwa isithombe se-Mysticetus noma i-Greenland whale (okungukuthi, i-Right whale), ngisho ne-Scoresby, indoda enolwazi olude ngokuthinta lelo zilwane, ithi ayinayo umlingani wayo emvelweni.

Kodwa ukubekwa kwe-cap-sheaf kulo lonke leli bhizinisi eliphuthayo kwakugcinelwe uFrederick Cuvier wesayensi, umfowabo kaBaron odumile. Ngo-1836, wanyathelisa Umlando Wemvelo Wemikhomo, lapho enikeza lokho akubiza ngokuthi isithombe seSperm Whale. Ngaphambi kokubonisa leso sithombe kunoma iyiphi i-Nantucketer, ubuhlinzeke kangcono ngokuhlehla kwakho okufingqiwe e-Nantucket. Ngamafuphi nje, i-Sperm Whale kaFrederick Cuvier ayiyona i-Sperm Whale, kodwa i-squash. Yebo, akakaze abe nenzuzo yohambo lomkhomo (abantu abanjalo abavamile ukuthola), kodwa wasithathaphi leso sithombe, ubani ongasho? Mhlawumbe wakuthola njengomanduleli wakhe wezesayensi emkhakheni ofanayo, u-Desmarest, wathola okunye kokuhushula isisu okuyiqiniso; okungukuthi, kusukela emdwebeni waseShayina. Futhi ukuthi lawo maShayina aluhlobo luni lwabafana abadlangile abanepensela, izinkomishi eziningi zama-queer namasoso ziyasazisa.

Ngokuqondene nemikhomo yabadwebi bezimpawu ebonakala emigwaqweni ilenga phezu kwezitolo zabadayisi bamafutha, kuzothiwani ngayo? Ngokuvamile banguRichard III. imikhomo, enama-dromedary humps, futhi anonya kakhulu; ukudla kwasekuseni ngamatilosi amathathu noma amane, okungukuthi izikebhe zomkhomo ezigcwele amatilosi: ukukhubazeka kwawo kuntanta ezilwandle ezinegazi nopende oluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka.

Kodwa la maphutha ahlukahlukene ekufanekiseni umkhomo awamangazi kangako. Cabanga! Iningi lemidwebo yesayensi ithathwe ezinhlanzini eziboshiwe; futhi lezi cishe zinembile njengoba umdwebo womkhumbi ophahlazekile, ophukile umhlane, ungamelela kahle isilwane esihloniphekile ngokwaso kukho konke ukuziqhenya kwaso okungakaqedwa kwesikhumba nezinhlamvu. Nakuba izindlovu ziye zamelela ubude bazo obugcwele, uLeviyathani ophilayo akakaze azintante kahle ukuze abone isithombe sakhe. Umkhomo ophilayo, ngobukhosi nokubaluleka kwawo okuphelele, ubonakala kuphela olwandle emanzini angenakulinganiswa; futhi intantayo inqwaba yakhe ayibonakali, njengomkhumbi wempi othuthwayo; futhi kuleso sici kuyinto engenakwenzeka phakade ngomuntu ofayo ukuba amphakamisele emoyeni emoyeni, ukuze alondoloze konke ukuvuvuka kwakhe okunamandla nokuguquguquka. Futhi, ngingasakhulumi ngomehluko ocatshangelwayo omkhulu phakathi komkhomo osemusha omunca kanye noLeviyathani kaPlato okhule ngokugcwele; nokho, ngisho nasendabeni yomunye waleyo mikhomo emincane emincane ephakanyiselwe emphemeni womkhumbi, unjalo-ke isimo sakhe esingavamile, esinjenge-eel, esinezitho, esihlukahlukayo, kangangokuthi inkulumo yakhe enembile udeveli ngokwakhe akakwazanga ukuyibamba.

Kodwa kungase kucatshangelwe, ukuthi kusukela kuhlaka lwamathambo olunqunu lomkhomo ovalelwe, kungase kutholakale imibono enembile ngokuthinta isimo sakhe sangempela. Lutho neze. Ngoba kungenye yezinto ezithakazelisa kakhulu ngale Leviathan, ukuthi uhlaka lwamathambo akhe lunikeza umqondo omncane kakhulu wokuma kwakhe okujwayelekile. Nakuba uhlaka lwamathambo kaJeremy Bentham, olulenga ku-candelabra emtatsheni wezincwadi womunye wabaphathi bakhe, luwudlulisela kahle umqondo wendoda endala eyisisetshenziswa esinempakamo, enazo zonke ezinye izici zikaJeremy eziphambili; nokho akukho lutho lwalolu hlobo olwalungathathwa kunoma yimaphi amathambo kaLeviyathani ashiwo. Eqinisweni, njengoba uHunter omkhulu esho, uhlaka lwamathambo omkhomo lunobuhlobo obufanayo nesilwane esitshalwe ngokugcwele nesinamapheshana njengoba isinambuzane senza kuma-chrysalis esiwuvala ngokuyindilinga. Lokhu okukhethekile kuvezwa ngokumangalisayo ekhanda, njengoba engxenyeni ethile yale ncwadi izoboniswa ngengozi. Iphinde ikhonjiswe ngokumangalisayo kufin eseceleni, amathambo alo acishe aphendule kahle emathanjeni esandla somuntu, kukhishwe isithupha kuphela. Le fin ineminwe emine evamile yamathambo, inkomba, ephakathi, indandatho, nomunwe omncane. Kodwa zonke lezi zivalwe unomphela esembozweni sazo esiyinyama, njengeminwe yomuntu esembozweni sokwenziwa. “Nokho ngokunganaki umkhomo ungase usikhonze ngezinye izikhathi,” kusho uStubb ohlekisayo ngolunye usuku, “akunakushiwo ngempela ukuthi usiphatha ngaphandle kwezintwala.”

Ngakho-ke, ngenxa yazo zonke lezi zizathu, noma iyiphi indlela ongase uyibheke ngayo, kumelwe uphethe ngokuthi uLeviyathani omkhulu uyileso sidalwa esisodwa emhlabeni okufanele sihlale singapendiwe kuze kube sekugcineni. Yiqiniso, isithombe esisodwa singase sifinyelele uphawu eduze kakhulu kunomunye, kodwa akekho ongasishaya nganoma yiliphi izinga elikhulu lokunemba. Ngakho ayikho indlela yasemhlabeni yokuthola ngokunembile ukuthi unjani ngempela umkhomo. Futhi okuwukuphela kwemodi ongathola ngayo ngisho nomqondo obekezelelekayo wekhonsathi yakhe ephilayo, iwukuba uzibambele umkhomo; kodwa ngokwenza kanjalo, awunangozi encane yokuba nesitofu saphakade futhi ucwiliswe nguye. Ngakho-ke, kimi kubonakala kungcono ukuthi ungasheshi kakhulu ekufuneni ukwazi ukuthinta le Leviyathani.





ISAHLUKO 56. Kwezithombe Ezinephutha Kancane Zemikhomo, kanye Nezithombe Zangempela Zezigcawu Zomkhomo.

Ngokuhambisana nezithombe ezinyantisayo zemikhomo, ngilingeka kakhulu lapha ukuba ngingene kulezo zindaba ezisanyantisa kakhudlwana zazo ezitholakala ezincwadini ezithile, zasendulo nezanamuhla, ikakhulukazi kuPliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, njll. Kodwa ngiyayidlulisa leyo ndaba.

Ngazi ngezinhlaka ezine kuphela ezishicilelwe zeSperm Whale omkhulu; UColnett, uHuggins, uFrederick Cuvier, noBeale. Esahlukweni esandulele uColnett noCuvier kubhekiselwe kubo. I-Huggins's ingcono kakhulu kuneyabo; kodwa, ngezinkinga ezinkulu, i-Beale iyona engcono kakhulu. Yonke imidwebo kaBeale yalo mkhomo mihle, ngaphandle kwesibalo esiphakathi esithombeni semikhomo emithathu ezimweni ezihlukahlukene, efaka isahluko sakhe sesibili. Indawo yakhe engaphambili, izikebhe ezihlasela ama-Sperm Whales, nakuba ngokungangabazeki zilinganiselwa ukuvusa ukungabaza komphakathi kwamanye amadoda ahlala endlini, zilungile ngendlela encomekayo futhi zifana nempilo ngomphumela wazo ojwayelekile. Eminye yemidwebo ye-Sperm Whale ku-J. Ross Browne ilungile ku-contour; kodwa zilotshwe kabuhlungu. Nokho akulona iphutha lakhe lelo.

Ku-Right Whale, izithombe ezinhle kakhulu zohlaka ziku-Scoresby; kodwa zidwetshwe ngesilinganiso esincane kakhulu ukudlulisa umbono ofiselekayo. Unesithombe esisodwa sezigcawu zomkhomo, futhi lokhu kuwukuntula okudabukisayo, ngoba kungenxa yezithombe ezinjalo kuphela, lapho wenza kahle, lapho ungathola noma yini efana nombono oyiqiniso womkhomo ophilayo njengoba ubonwa abazingeli bakhe abaphilayo.

Kodwa, uma kuthathwe kukho konke, okuhle kakhulu, nakuba kweminye imininingwane kungeyona elungile kakhulu, izethulo zemikhoma nezigcawu zomkhomo ezingatholakala noma kuphi, ziyimidwebo emibili emikhulu yesiFulentshi, eqoshwe kahle, futhi ethathwe emidwebeni yeGarnery eyodwa. Ngokulandelana, bamele ukuhlaselwa kwe-Sperm kanye ne-Right Whale. Embhalweni wokuqala i-Sperm Whale ehloniphekile iboniswa ngobukhosi obugcwele bamandla, isanda kuphakama ngaphansi kwesikebhe kusukela ekujuleni kolwandle, futhi ithwala phezulu emoyeni emhlane wayo ukuphahlazeka okukhulu kwamapulangwe eziko. Ingaphambili lesikebhe aliphukile ngokwengxenye, futhi lidwetshwa nje lizinza phezu komgogodla wenunu; futhi emi kulowo mzuzu ongaphambili, ngenxa yalokho kuphazima okukodwa okungenakuqhathaniswa kwesikhathi, ubona umgwedli, ingxenye yawo embozwe imfucumfucu ebilayo yomkhomo, futhi egxuma, njengokungathi uvela eweni. Isenzo sayo yonke into sihle ngokumangalisayo futhi siyiqiniso. Ubhavu kalayini ongenalutho untanta olwandle olumhlophe; izigxobo zokhuni zama-harpoons achithekile zibhoboza kuwo; amakhanda ababhukudi ahlakazekile mayelana nomkhomo ngezindlela ezihlukile zokwethuka; ngenkathi umkhumbi ubheke phansi endaweni yesigameko. Kungase kutholakale iphutha elikhulu ngemininingwane ye-anatomical yalo mkhomo, kodwa lokho makudlule; kusukela, impilo yami, angikwazanga ukudweba enhle kangaka.

Emqoshweni wesibili, isikebhe sisemkhankasweni wokudweba eceleni kohlangothi lwe-barnacle yoMkhomo omkhulu ogijimayo, ogingqa inqwaba yokhula olumnyama olwandle njengetshe le-mossy rock-slide elisuka emaweni asePatagonian. Amajezi akhe aqondile, agcwele, amnyama njengomle; ukuze ngenxa yokuchichima kwentuthu kushimula, ungacabanga ukuthi kufanele kube nokupheka isidlo sakusihlwa esinesibindi emathunjini amakhulu ngezansi. Izinyoni zasolwandle zicothoza izinkalankala, izinhlanzi ezigobolondo, namanye amaswidi asolwandle nama-maccaroni, ngezinye izikhathi i-Right Whale eyithwala emhlane wawo ogulayo. Futhi sonke lesi sikhathi ileviyathani enezindebe eziwugqinsi igijima ekujuleni, ishiya amathani amatshe amhlophe anyakazayo ekuvukeni kwayo, futhi ibangela ukuba isikebhe esincane sinyakaze sinyakaze njengesikebhe esibanjwe eduze kwamasondo esigwedlo sesikebhe solwandle. Ngakho, ingaphambili liwukuxokozela okukhulu; kodwa ngemuva, ngokuphambene okubabazekayo kwezobuciko, izinga lengilazi lolwandle elizolile, oseyili olengayo ongenasitashi omkhumbi ongenawo amandla, kanye nenqwaba ye-inert ye-whale efile, inqaba enqotshiwe, nefulegi lokubanjwa elilenga ngokuvilapha esigxotsheni somkhomo elifakwe emgodini wakhe we-spout.

Ubani uGarnery umdwebi, noma wayengubani, angazi. Kodwa impilo yami ngenxa yayo wayekwazi ukukhuluma nesifundo sakhe, noma wafundiswa ngendlela emangalisayo omunye umkhomo onolwazi. AmaFulentshi angabafana besenzo sokudweba. Hamba uyobuka yonke imidwebo yaseYurophu, futhi uzoyitholaphi igalari yesiphithiphithi esinjalo sokuphila nokuphefumula kumaseyili, njengakulelo hholo lokunqoba eVersailles; lapho obukele elwa ngendlela yakhe, u-pell-mell, ezimpini ezinkulu ezilandelanayo zaseFrance; lapho yonke inkemba ibonakala njengokukhanya kwezibani zaseNyakatho, futhi amakhosi ahlomile alandelanayo nama-Emperors edlula, njengenkokhelo yama-centaur athweswe umqhele? Ayifanele neze indawo kuleyo galari, lezi zingcezu zasolwandle zaseGarnery.

Ikhono lemvelo lamaFulentshi lokubamba ubuhle bezinto libonakala libonakala ngokucacile kulokho imidwebo nemidwebo abanayo ezigcawini zabo zokudoba imikhomo. Ngaphandle kokukodwa kweshumi kokuhlangenwe nakho kweNgilandi kwezokudoba, futhi kungeyona ingxenye eyinkulungwane yaleyo yabantu baseMelika, nakuba kunjalo baye banikeza izizwe zombili imidwebo ewukuphela kwayo eqediwe ekwazi ukudlulisa umoya wangempela wokuzingela imikhomo. Ngokwengxenye enkulu, abadwebi bama-whale bamaNgisi nabaseMelika babonakala beneliseke ngokuphelele ngokwethula uhlaka lwezinto, njengephrofayili engenalutho yomkhomo; okuthi, mayelana nokubukeka komphumela, kufana nokudweba iphrofayili yephiramidi. Ngisho ne-Scoresby, i-Right whaleman edume ngokufanele, ngemva kokusinika ubude obuqinile obuphelele bomkhomo waseGreenland, kanye nezithombe ezincanyana ezintathu noma ezine ezintekenteke zama-narwhale nama-porpoise, usiphatha ngochungechunge lwemibhalo yakudala yezingwegwe zezikebhe, imimese yokusika, nama-grapnel; futhi ngokukhuthala okuncane kwe-Leuwenhoeck inikezela ekuhlolweni komhlaba oqhaqhazelayo izifaniso ezingamashumi ayisishiyagalolunye nesithupha zamakristalu eqhwa ase-Arctic akhulisiwe. Angiqondile ukudelela umhambi ohamba phambili (ngimhlonipha njengomakadebona), kodwa odabeni olubaluleke kangaka bekuwukwengamela ukuthi kungenzeki kulo lonke ikristalu i-afidavithi efungelwe ethathwe phambi kweJustice Lokuthula laseGreenland.

Ngoba nakuba ezinye izinhlobo zemikhomo zithola ukudla kwazo ngaphezu kwamanzi, futhi zingase zibonwe umuntu lapho ezondla, i-spermaceti whale ithola ukudla kwayo yonke ezindaweni ezingaziwa ngaphansi komhlaba; futhi ngombono kuphela ukuthi noma yimuphi umuntu angasho ukuthi, ngokunembile, lokho kudla kuqukethe ini. Ngezinye izikhathi, lapho esukelwa eduze, uyokhipha lokho okufanele kube izingalo ezihlukanisiwe ze-squid; amanye awo abonisa kanjalo ayedlula amafidi angamashumi amabili nantathu ubude. Bathanda ukuthi lesi silo okwakungezaso lezi zingalo ngokuvamile sinamathela phezu kolwandle; nokuthi i-sperm whale, ngokungafani nezinye izinhlobo, inikezwa amazinyo ukuze iwuhlasele futhi uyiklebhule.

Kubonakala kunesizathu sokucabanga ukuthi i-Kraken enkulu yoMbhishobhi uPontoppodan ingase igcine izixazulule ibe Squid. Indlela uMbhishobhi akuchaza ngayo, njengokuvuka nokucwila ngokushintshana, neminye imininingwane ayilandisayo, kukho konke lokhu kuhambisana kokubili. Kodwa ukuncishiswa okuningi kuyadingeka ngokuphathelene nenqwaba emangalisayo ayinikezayo.

Abanye ososayensi bemvelo abake bezwa ngamahemuhemu ngesidalwa esiyimfihlakalo, okukhulunywa ngaso lapha, sifakwe phakathi kwesigaba se-cuttlefish, okuyinto, ngempela, ezicini ezithile zangaphandle kubonakala sengathi ingeyaso, kodwa njengama-Anak kuphela wesizwe.





ISAHLUKO 60. Umugqa.

Ngokuphathelene nesimo sokudotshwa kwemikhomo esizochazwa maduze, kanye nokuqonda kangcono zonke izigcawu ezifanayo kwenye indawo ezethulwa, ngilapha ukukhuluma ngomugqa womkhomo omlingo, kwesinye isikhathi owesabekayo.

Ulayini owawusetshenziswa ekuqaleni embonini yokudoba wawungowe-hemp engcono kakhulu, ehwamuke kancane ngetiyela, engafakwanga kuyo, njengasendabeni yezintambo ezijwayelekile; ngoba ngenkathi itiyela, njengoba livame ukusetshenziswa, lenza i-hemp ithandeke kakhulu kumenzi wezintambo, futhi futhi yenza intambo ngokwayo ibe lula kumatilosi ukuze asetshenziswe umkhumbi ovamile; kodwa, ubuningi obujwayelekile ngeke nje buqinise kakhulu umugqa womkhomo ukuze uzisongele eduze okumele uqondiswe kukho; kodwa njengoba amatilosi amaningi eseqala ukufunda, itiyela ngokuvamile alengezi neze ekuqineni kwentambo noma emandleni, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi lingayinika kangakanani ukubumbana nokucwebezela.

Eminyakeni yakamuva intambo ye-Manilla endaweni yokudoba yaseMelika icishe yathatha indawo ye-hemp njengento esetshenziswa imigqa yomkhomo; ngoba, nakuba ingeqini njenge-hemp, inamandla, futhi ithambe kakhulu futhi iyanwebeka; futhi ngizonezela (njengoba kukhona i-æsthetics kuzo zonke izinto), muhle kakhulu futhi kufanele esikebheni, kune-hemp. I-Hemp iwumuntu omnyama, omnyama, uhlobo oluthile lwamaNdiya; kodwa uManilla ufana ne-Circassian enezinwele zegolide okufanele uyibuke.

I-whale-line ingama-intshi amabili kwezintathu kuphela ngokuqina. Uma uqala ukubona, ubungeke ucabange ukuthi inamandla njengoba injalo ngempela. Ngokuhlola imicu yayo eyodwa namashumi amahlanu ngayinye izomisa isisindo samapondo ayikhulu namashumi amabili; ukuze intambo yonke ithwale ubunzima obucishe bulingane namathani amathathu. Ngobude, i-sperm whale-line evamile ikala okuthile okungaphezu kwamafathom angamakhulu amabili. Ngasemuva kwesikebhe isongelwe ngokuzumayo kubhavu, hhayi njengepayipi lesikelemu likashizi, kodwa ukuze kwakheka insalela eyodwa eyindilinga, emise okukashizi “yezithungu” eziminyene, noma izingqimba ze-spiralizations ezigxilile, ngaphandle kwanoma iyiphi imbobo kodwa “inhliziyo,” noma ishubhu elincane elimile elakhiwe eksisini kashizi. Njengoba ukugoqa okuncane noma ukugoba kwekhoyili, lapho iphela, kususe ingalo yomunye umuntu, umlenze, noma umzimba wonke, ukuqapha okukhulu kusetshenziswa ekumiseni umugqa kubhavu wayo. Amanye ama-harpooneers azodla cishe usuku lonke lwasekuseni kuleli bhizinisi, ethwala umugqa uphezulu phezulu abese ewuhlehlisa phansi ngebhlokhi ebheke kubhavu, ukuze enze isenzo sokusonga ukuwukhulula kuyo yonke imibimbi nokusonteka okungaba khona.

Ezikebheni zamaNgisi kusetshenziswa obhavu ababili esikhundleni soyedwa; umugqa ofanayo uhlanganiswa ngokuqhubekayo kuwo womabili amabhavu. Kukhona inzuzo kulokhu; ngenxa yokuthi la ma-twin-tubs mancane kakhulu angena kalula esikebheni, futhi awawucindezeli kangako; kanti, ubhavu waseMelika, ocishe ube ngamafidi amathathu ububanzi futhi ujule ngokulinganayo, wenza umthwalo omkhulu womkhumbi onamapulangwe awugqinsi oluyingxenye ye-intshi eyodwa; ngoba phansi kwesikebhe somkhomo kufana neqhwa elibucayi, elizothwala isisindo esisabalele, kodwa hhayi eliningi kakhulu eligxilile. Lapho ikhava kaseyili ependiwe ishaywa izandla kubhavu kalayini waseMelika, isikebhe sibukeka sengathi sisuka ngekhekhe lomshado elikhulu elimangalisayo ukuze silethulele imikhomo.

Zombili iziphetho zomugqa zivezwe; isiphetho esingezansi esinqamula ku-splice yeso noma iluphu ekhuphuka isuka phansi ibheke ohlangothini lwebhavu, futhi ilenga onqenqemeni lwalo ikhishwe ngokuphelele kukho konke. Lokhu kuhlelwa kwesiphetho esiphansi kuyadingeka kuma-akhawunti amabili. Okokuqala: Ukuze kube lula ukuqiniswa komugqa owengeziwe osuka esikebheni esingumakhelwane, uma kwenzeka umkhomo oshayiwe uzwakala ujule kakhulu kangangokuba usongela ukukhipha wonke umugqa oxhunywe kwi-harpoon ekuqaleni. Kulezi zimo, i-whale yebo igudluzwa njengenkomishi ye-ale, njengokungathi, ukusuka kwesinye isikebhe kuya kwesinye; nakuba isikebhe sokuqala sihlala sizungeza ukuze sisize umlingane waso. Okwesibili: Leli lungiselelo libalulekile ngenxa yokuphepha okufanayo; ngoba uma isiphetho esiphansi somugqa nganoma iyiphi indlela sasixhunywe esikebheni, futhi umkhomo ngaleso sikhathi wawugijima umugqa uphume kuze kube sekugcineni cishe ngomzuzu owodwa, obhemayo njengoba ngezinye izikhathi enza, wayengeke ayeke lapho, ngoba isikebhe esizobhujiswa sasiyodonselwa phansi ngemva kwakhe ekujuleni kolwandle; futhi uma kunjalo akekho umuntu omemeza edolobheni owayeyophinde amthole.

Ngaphambi kokwehliswa kwesikebhe ukuze sijahe, ingxenye engenhla yomugqa ithathwa isuka kubhavu, bese idlula i-loggerhead lapho, iphinde iqhutshekiselwe phambili bonke ubude besikebhe, iphumule ngokuphambana phezu kohlaka noma isibambo sesigwedlo sawo wonke umuntu, ukuze igijime isihlakala sakhe lapho igwedla; futhi futhi edlula phakathi kwamadoda, njengoba eshintshana ehlezi kwezinye izibhamu eziphambene, ukuya emigodini enomthofu noma emiseleni engaphambili elicijile ngokwedlulele lesikebhe, lapho iphini lokhuni noma isikeyi esilingana nequill evamile, siyivimbela ukuba ingaphumi. Kusukela kuma-chocks ilenga e-festoon encane phezu kweminsalo, bese idluliselwa ngaphakathi kwesikebhe futhi; futhi amafathomu angaba yishumi noma angamashumi amabili (okuthiwa umugqa webhokisi) asongelwe phezu kwebhokisi elisemicibisholo, aqhubeke nendlela yawo eya ku-gunwale esele kancane ngemva kwalokho, abese exhunywa kuyi-short-warp—intambo exhunywa ngokushesha ne-harpoon; kodwa ngaphambili kwalokho kuxhunyaniswa, i-short-warp idlula ekungaqondakalini okuhlukahlukene okukhathazayo ngemininingwane.

Ngakho-ke i-whale-line igoqa sonke isikebhe ngamakhoyili aso ayinkimbinkimbi, isonteka futhi izungeza cishe yonke indawo. Bonke abagwedli bahileleke ekulimazeni kwayo okuyingozi; kangangokuthi emehlweni omesaba womhlaba, babonakala bengama-juggle aseNdiya, anezinyoka eziyingozi kakhulu ezihlobisa izitho zabo. Futhi akukho ndodana yowesifazane ofayo, ngokokuqala ngqá, engakwazi ukuhlala phakathi kwalabo bunzima be-hempen, futhi ngenkathi idonsa kanzima ekugwedleni, icabange ukuthi nganoma isiphi isikhathi esingaziwa i-harpoon ingase igibele, futhi zonke lezi zihlubuki ezimbi zidlalwa njengombani onezintambo; akanakubhekana kanjalo ngaphandle kokuthuthumela okwenza wona kanye umnkantsha emathanjeni akhe unyakaze phakathi kwakhe njengejeli elinyakaziswayo. Nokho umkhuba-into engavamile! yini engenakufezwa umkhuba?—Ama-Gayer sallies, injabulo eyengeziwe, amahlaya angcono, nama-repartees agqamile, awukaze uzwe phezu kwe-mahogany yakho, kunalokho oyokuzwa phezu komsedari omhlophe oyisigamu se-intshi wesikebhe somkhomo, lapho ulenga kanjalo ezihibeni ze-hangman; futhi, njengabafo abayisithupha baseCalais ngaphambi kweNkosi u-Edward, amadoda ayisithupha ahlanganisa ithimba adonsela emihlathini yokufa, ene-halter entanyeni yonke, njengoba ungase usho.

Mhlawumbe ukucabanga okuncane kakhulu manje kuzokwenza ukwazi ukulandisa ngalezo zinhlekelele zokudotshwa kwemikhomo ngokuphindaphindiwe—ezinye ezimbalwa zazo ezilotshwe nje ngokungalindelekile—zale ndoda noma leyo ndoda ekhishwa ulayini esikebheni, yalahleka. Ngokuba, lapho ulayini usuphuma, ukuhlala ngaleso sikhathi esikebheni, kufana nokuhlala phakathi kokukhala okuphindaphindeka kwenjini yesitimu idlala ngokugcwele, lapho yonke imishayo endizayo, nomphini, namasondo, kukudla. Kubi kakhulu; ngoba awukwazi ukuhlala unganyakazi enhliziyweni yalezi zingozi, ngoba umkhumbi uyazamazama njengembeleko, futhi uphonswa ngapha nangapha, ngaphandle kokuxwayiswa nakancane; futhi kuphela ngokunyakazisa okuthile okuzilungisayo kanye kanyekanye kokuthanda nokwenza, ungakwazi ukubalekela ukwenziwa i-Mazeppa, futhi ubalekele lapho ilanga elibona yonke into uqobo lwayo lingasoze likubhoboze.

Futhi: njengoba ukuzola okujulile okubonakala kuphela kwandulela futhi kuprofetha ngesiphepho, mhlawumbe kubi kakhulu kunesiphepho ngokwaso; ngokuba, impela, ukuthula kuyisimbozo nemvilophi yesiphepho; futhi likuqukethe ngokwaso, njengoba isibhamu esibonakala singenangozi siphethe impushana ebulalayo, nebhola, nokuqhuma; ngakho ukuphumula okuhle komugqa, njengoba ukhuluma buthule ngabagwedli ngaphambi kokuba ulethwe ekudlalweni kwangempela—lena into ethwala ukwesabeka kweqiniso kakhulu kunanoma iyiphi enye ingxenye yale ndaba eyingozi. Kodwa kungani usho okwengeziwe? Wonke amadoda ahlala embozwe imigqa yomkhomo. Bonke bazalwa benezinsika entanyeni; kodwa kulapho kuphela lapho bebanjwe ogwini lokufa olusheshayo, oluzumayo, lapho abantu abafayo bebona khona ubungozi bokuphila obuthule, obucashile, obuhlala bukhona. Futhi uma uyisazi sefilosofi, nakuba uhlezi esikebheni somkhomo, ubungeke enhliziyweni uzwe ukwesaba okukhulu, kunokuba uhlezi phambi komlilo wakho wakusihlwa ne-poker, futhi hhayi i-harpoon, eceleni kwakho.
