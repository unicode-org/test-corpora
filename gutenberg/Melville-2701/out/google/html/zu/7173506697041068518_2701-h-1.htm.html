“Umnikazi wendawo,” kusho mina, “mtshele ukuthi afihle i-tomahawk yakhe lapho, noma ipayipi, noma yini oyibizayo; mtshele ukuthi ayeke ukubhema, ngamafuphi, ngizophendukela kuye. Kodwa angikuthandi ukuba nendoda ebhema nami embhedeni. Kuyingozi. Ngaphandle kwalokho, anginayo umshwalense.”

Lokhu kutshelwa uQueequeg, wavuma ngaso leso sikhathi, waphinde wangikhombisa ngesizotha ukuthi ngingene embhedeni—ngigingqike ngibheke ohlangothini olulodwa kangangokuthi athi—“Ngeke ngithinte umlenze wakho.”

“Ulale kahle, mnikazi wendawo,” kusho mina, “ungahamba.”

Ngaphenduka, futhi angizange ngilale kangcono empilweni yami.
