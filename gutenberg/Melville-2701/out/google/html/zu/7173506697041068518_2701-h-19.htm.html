ISAHLUKO 94. Ukukhama Kwesandla.

Lowo mkhomo we-Stubb's, othengwe kakhulu, walethwa ngokufanelekile ohlangothini lwe-Pequod, lapho yonke leyo misebenzi yokusika nokuphakamisa ngaphambili eyayinemininingwane, yayivame ukudlula, kuze kube yilapho i-Heidelburgh Tun, noma i-Case.

Ngenkathi abanye bematasa nalo msebenzi wakamuva, abanye babeqashwe ekuhuduleni amabhavu amakhudlwana, ngokushesha nje lapho egcwele isidoda; futhi lapho isikhathi esifanele sifika, lesi sidoda esifanayo sasetshenziswa ngokucophelela ngaphambi kokuya kuma-try-works, okuyi-anon.

Yayipholile futhi yacwebezela ngezinga elikhulu, kangangokuthi lapho, nabanye abaningana, ngihlala phansi ngaphambi kokugeza okukhulu kukaConstantine, ngayithola ifakwe ukhonkolo ngendlela exakile yaba izigaxa, igingqika lapha nalaphaya engxenyeni ewuketshezi. Bekuwumsebenzi wethu ukukhama lezi zigaxa zibuyele oketshezini. Umsebenzi omnandi futhi ongathandeki! Akumangalisi ukuthi ezikhathini zakudala lesi sidoda sasiyisimonyo esiyintandokazi. Kucace kakhudlwana! i-sweetener enjalo! isithambisi esinjalo! i-molifier emnandi kangaka! Ngemva kokufaka izandla zami kuyo imizuzu embalwa nje, iminwe yami yaba sengathi ama-eels, futhi yaqala ukuba inyoka futhi izungeleze.

Njengoba ngangihlezi lapho ngikhululekile, ngiphambanise imilenze phezu komkhumbi; ngemuva kokuzikhandla okubabayo ku-windlass; ngaphansi kwesibhakabhaka esinokuthula esiluhlaza; umkhumbi ngaphansi kukaseyili ovilaphayo, futhi untweza ngokuzolile; njengoba ngangigeza izandla zami phakathi kwalawo maqhubu athambile, athambile ezicubu ezingene, ezalukwe cishe phakathi nehora; njengoba bephule ngokucebile eminweni yami, futhi bekhipha konke ukunatha kwabo, njengezithelo zomvini ezivuthiwe ngokugcwele iwayini labo; njengoba ngangihogela lelo phunga elingangcolisiwe,—ngokoqobo futhi ngempela, njengephunga lezimbali zasentwasahlobo; Ngiyanitshela, ukuthi ngesikhathi engasihlala enkangala; Ngakhohlwa konke ngesifungo sethu esibi; kuleso sidoda esingachazeki, ngigeze izandla zami nenhliziyo yami kuso; Ngacishe ngaqala ukuncoma inkolelo-ze endala yakwaParacelsan yokuthi isidoda siwubuhle obungavamile ekudambiseni ukushisa kwentukuthelo; ngenkathi ngigeza kuleyo bhavi, ngazizwa ngikhululekile ngokwaphezulu kukho konke ukungafuni, noma ukuthuka, noma inzondo, yanoma yiluphi uhlobo.

Khama! cindezela! cindezela! ekuseni yonke; Ngasikhama leso sidoda ngaze ngacishe ngancibilika kuso; Ngasikhama leso sidoda ngaze ngafikelwa wuhlanya oluyinqaba; futhi ngazithola ngingaqondile ngicindezela izandla zalabo engisebenza nabo, ngiphambanisa izandla zabo ngama-globule athambile. Umzwelo onjalo ochichimayo, wothando, wobungane, wothando owazala lokhu; ukuthi ekugcineni ngangilokhu ngicindezela izandla zabo, futhi ngibheka phezulu emehlweni abo ngozwela; ngisho nokuthi,—O! Bantu bakithi abathandekayo, kungani kufanele sisazise noma yiziphi izingxabano zomphakathi, noma sazi amahlaya amancane noma umona! Woza; asicindezele izandla nxazonke; cha, asizimpintsheni sonke; masizikhame emhlabeni wonke kulo kanye ubisi kanye nesidoda somusa.

Sengathi ngingaqhubeka ngicindezela leso sidoda kuze kube phakade! Okwamanje, njengoba ngokuhlangenwe nakho okuningi okwesikhathi eside, okuphindaphindiwe, ngiye ngabona ukuthi kuzo zonke izimo umuntu kufanele ehlise, noma okungenani agudluke, ukuzidla kwakhe ngenjabulo engafinyeleleka; ungabeki noma yikuphi ekuhlakanipheni noma ekucabangeni; kodwa kunkosikazi, enhliziyweni, embhedeni, etafuleni, kwisihlalo sehhashi, endaweni yomlilo, ezweni; manje njengoba sengikubonile konke lokhu, sengilungele ukumpintsha icala phakade. Ngemicabango yemibono yasebusuku, ngabona imigqa emide yezingelosi epharadesi, ngayinye ifake izandla zayo embizeni ye-spermaceti.

Manje, ngenkathi kuxoxwa ngesidoda, kufanele kukhulunywe ngezinye izinto ezifana naso, ebhizinisini lokulungisa umkhomo wesidoda ukuze usebenze.

Okokuqala kufika ihhashi elimhlophe, elibizwa kanjalo, elitholakala engxenyeni yenhlanzi egoqayo, futhi nasezingxenyeni eziminyene zama-flukes akhe. Iqinile njengoba iminyene imisipha—imisipha—kodwa isenawo amafutha. Ngemva kokuhlukaniswa nomkhomo, ihhashi elimhlophe liqale lisikwe libe ama-oblong aphathwayo ngaphambi kokuya kumcuphi. Abukeka njengamabhulokhi e-Berkshire marble.

I-Plum-pudding yigama elinikezwa izingxenye ezithile eziyizicucu zenyama yomkhomo, lapha nalaphaya zinamathela engubeni ye-blubber, futhi ngokuvamile zibamba iqhaza ngezinga elithile ekungabini nangqondo kwawo. Kuyinto eqabulayo kakhulu, ephilayo, nenhle kakhulu ongayibona. Njengoba igama layo lingenisa kwamanye amazwe, inombala ocebe ngokwedlulele, onamabala, inhlabathi eqhwakele kakhulu nesagolide, enamachashaza abomvu klebhu nokunsomi. Kungama-plums amarubi, ezithombeni ze-citron. Naphezu kokucabanga, kunzima ukuzigcina ungakudli. Ngiyavuma, ukuthi ngake ngantshontsha ngemuva kwenduna ukuze ngizame. Kwanambitha okuthile njengoba kufanele ngikhulelwe i-cutlet yasebukhosini ethangeni likaLouis le Gros okungenzeka ukuthi yanambitha, ngicabanga ukuthi wabulawa ngosuku lokuqala ngemuva kwesizini yenyamazane, kanye naleyo nkathi ethile yenyamazane eyayiphila ngaleso sikhathi ngokuvunwa okuhle okungajwayelekile kwezivini zeChampagne.

Kukhona enye into, futhi eyodwa kakhulu, evela ngesikhathi saleli bhizinisi, kodwa engizwa sengathi idida kakhulu ukuyichaza. Ibizwa ngokuthi i-slobgollion; i-appellation yasekuqaleni nama-whalemen, futhi kunjalo nemvelo yento. Kuyinto evuzayo ngendlela engaqondakali, enezintambo, evame ukutholakala kubhavu wesidoda, ngemva kokumpintsha isikhathi eside, nokuhoxiswa okulandelayo. Ngiyibamba njengezacile ngendlela emangalisayo, ulwelwesi oludabukile lwecala, luyahlangana.

I-Gurry, ebizwa kanjalo, yigama elifanele abadobi bemikhomo, kodwa ngezinye izikhathi lisetshenziswa abadobi besidoda. Isho into emnyama, edlayo ekhishwe ngemuva kweGreenland noma i-right whale, futhi iningi layo elimboze izitezi zaleyo miphefumulo esezingeni eliphansi ezingela leyo Leviathan engenasici.

Ama-Nippers. Empeleni leli gama akulona elomdabu esilulundweni somkhomo. Kodwa njengoba kusetshenziswa ama-whalemen, kuba njalo. I-whaleman nipper iyisiqephu esifushane esiqinile sezinto eziqinile ezisikwe engxenyeni egoqayo yomsila kaLeviyathani: isilinganiso siyisentimitha ngokuqina, kanti okunye, silingana nengxenye yensimbi yegeja. I-Edgewise ihamba eduze kwedekhi enamafutha, isebenza njenge-squilgee yesikhumba; nangokuhlambalaza okungenamagama, njengomlingo, ukuheha kanye nakho konke ukungcola.

Kodwa ukuze ufunde konke mayelana nalezi zindaba zokuhlenga, indlela yakho engcono kakhulu ukuthi wehlele ngaso leso sikhathi egumbini lamanzi, futhi ube nengxoxo ende neziboshwa zakhona. Le ndawo ngaphambilini ibike yashiwo njengendawo yokwamukela izingcezu zengubo, lapho zikhumula futhi ziphakanyiswa emkhomo. Lapho kufika isikhathi esifanele sokusika okuphakathi, leli fulethi liyindawo esabekayo kuwo wonke ama-tyros, ikakhulukazi ebusuku. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, kukhanyiswe ilambu elilufifi, isikhala sishiywe sivulekile kubasebenzi. Ngokuvamile bahamba ngababili,—i-pike-and-gaffman kanye ne-spade-man. I-whaling-pike ifana ne-frigate's boarding-weapon yegama elifanayo. I-gaff yinto efana ne-boat-hook. Nge-gaff yakhe, i-gaffman ixhunywe eshidini le-blubber, futhi ilwela ukulibamba lingasheleli, njengoba umkhumbi uhamba futhi uhambahamba. Phakathi naleso sikhathi, i-spade-man ime eshidini ngokwalo, i-perpendicularly inquma izingcezu zamahhashi eziphathwayo. Le has ibukhali njengoba uju lungayenza; izinyawo zomhlaseli azigqokile; into ami phezu kwayo ngezinye izikhathi iyoshelela isuke kuye ngendlela engenakuvinjelwa, njengesihlibhi. Uma enquma omunye wezinzwane zakhe, noma omunye wabasizi bakhe, ungamangala kakhulu? Izinzwane ziyayivelakancane phakathi kwamadoda angomakadebona.





ISAHLUKO 95. I-Cassock.

Ukube uke wanyathela i-Pequod ngesikhathi esithile salokhu ngemuva kokushona komkhomo; futhi ukube uqhubekile uye phambili eduze kwengilazi yomoya, ngiqinisekile ukuthi ubungeke uhlole ngokuncane ilukuluku into exakile, eyimpicabadala, obungayibona lapho, ilele ngobude kuma-lee scuppers. Hhayi umthombo omangalisayo osekhanda elikhulu lomkhomo; hhayi ubukhazikhazi bomhlathi wakhe ongezansi ongaboshiwe; hhayi isimangaliso umsila wakhe symmetrical; akukho kulokhu okungakumangaza, njengokubona ingxenye yaleyo khoni engaphenduleki,—inde kunomuntu wase-Kentuckian mude, ucishe ube yifithi ububanzi phansi, futhi umnyama njengo-Yojo, isithixo se-ebony saseQueequeg. Futhi isithombe ngempela; noma, kunalokho, ezikhathini zakudala, ukufana kwayo kwakunjalo. Isithombe esinjengaleso esatholakala ezihlahleni zendlovukazi uMahakha eJudiya; nangokukhuleka, iNkosi u-Asa, indodana yakhe, eyamehlisa, yasichitha isithombe, yasishisa njengesinengiso emfudlaneni iKedroni, njengoba kubekwe ngokufiphala esahlukweni se-15 seNcwadi yokuQala yamaKhosi.

Bheka itilosi, elibizwa ngomcuphi, manje eliqhamukayo, futhi lisizwa ngabangane ababili, lihlehla kakhulu i-grandissimus, njengoba amatilosi ewubiza kanjalo, futhi ngamahlombe agobile, ligxadazela lihamba nalo njengokungathi liyibhomu elithwele umngane oshonile ophuma ensimini. Eyinweba phezu kwedekhi yesibikezelo, manje uqhubeka ngesilinda ukuze asuse ucwecwe lwalo olumnyama, njengomzingeli wase-Afrika i-boa. Lokhu kwenziwa uphendulela i-pellet ngaphakathi, njengomlenze we-pantaloon; inikeza ukunwebeka okuhle, ukuze kucishe kabili ububanzi bayo; futhi ekugcineni iyalengiswa, isakazwe kahle, emshinini wokugaya, ukuze yome. Ngaphambi kwesikhathi eside, yehliswa; lapho esusa amafidi amathathu kuwo, eya ngasemkhawulweni okhonjiwe, bese esika izimbobo ezimbili zezimbobo zengalo ngakolunye uhlangothi, ngobude uzishutheka kuwo ngomzimba. Umenzi wemigodi manje umi phambi kwakho utshale imali kuma-canonicals aphelele obizo lwakhe. Isikhumbuzo sawo wonke umyalo wakhe, lolu phenyo lulodwa luzomvikela ngokwanele, kuyilapho eqashwe emisebenzini engavamile yehhovisi lakhe.

Lelo hhovisi lihlanganisa ukugawula izingcezu zamahhashi zamabhodwe; ukuhlinzwa okwenziwa ehhashini lokhuni elinelukuluku, elitshalwe ekugcineni ngasezivikelweni, kanye nobhavu ovulekile ngaphansi kwalo, lapho izingcezu ezigayiwe ziwela khona, ngokushesha njengamashidi avela etafuleni lesikhulumi se-rap. Ivunule ngombala omnyama ohloniphekile; ukuhlala epulpiti elisobala; inhloso emakhasini eBhayibheli; yeka ikhandidethi lokuba umbhishobhi omkhulu, yeka insizwa esebenzela uPapa lo mfanyana!*

ISAHLUKO 96. I-Try-Works.

Ngaphandle kwezikebhe zakhe eziphakanyisiwe, umdobi wama-whale waseMelika ubonakala ngaphandle ngokuzama imisebenzi yakhe. Wethula okudidayo okudidayo kwesakhiwo samatshe esiqinile esihlangana ne-oki ne-hemp ekwakheni umkhumbi oqediwe. Kunjengokungathi kuthuthwa ihhavini lezitini liyiswe emapulangweni akhe.

I-try-works itshalwe phakathi kwe-foremast ne-mainmast, ingxenye evulekile kakhulu yedekhi. Izingodo ezingaphansi zinamandla angavamile, afakwe ukuze asekele isisindo esicishe siqine sezitini nodaka, esingamafidi ayishumi ububanzi nayisikwele esiyisishiyagalombili, nezikwele ezinhlanu ukuphakama. Isisekelo asingeni emphemeni, kodwa amatshe amatshe avikelwe ngokuqinile phezulu ngamadolo aqinile ensimbi ayiqinisa nhlangothi zonke, futhi ayijikijela phansi kuze kufike ezingodweni. Emaceleni imbozwe ngokhuni, futhi phezulu imbozwe ngokuphelele umhume omkhulu, owehlelayo, oshayekile. Ukususa le hatch sidalula ama-try-pot, amabili ngenombolo, kanye nomthamo wemiphongolo eminingana. Lapho zingasetshenziswa, zigcinwa zihlanzekile ngendlela emangalisayo. Kwesinye isikhathi zipholishwa ngetshe lensipho nesihlabathi, zize zikhanye ngaphakathi njengezitsha zesiliva. Phakathi namawashi asebusuku amanye amatilosi amadala angenangqondo azokhasa kuwo futhi aziholele lapho ukuze alale. Nakuba kusetshenziswa ekuzipholisheleni—indoda eyodwa embizeni ngayinye, ihlangene—kuye kwenziwa ukuxhumana okuningi okuyimfihlo, phezu kwezindebe zensimbi. Kuyindawo futhi yokuzindla okujulile kwezibalo. Kwakusesandleni sokunxele sebhodwe lokuzama le-Pequod, itshe lensipho lingizungeza ngokuzimisela, lapho ngafikelwa khona okokuqala yiqiniso elimangalisayo, ukuthi ku-geometry yonke imizimba etshuza eduze kwe-cycloid, isibonelo sami sensipho, izokwehla kunoma iyiphi indawo ngesikhathi esifanayo.

Ukukhipha i-fire-board ngaphambili kwe-try-works, i-masonry engenalutho yalolo hlangothi iyavezwa, ingene ngemilomo yensimbi emibili yezithando, ngokuqondile ngaphansi kwamabhodwe. Le milomo ifakwe izicabha ezisindayo zensimbi. Ukushisa okukhulu komlilo kuvinjelwa ekuzikhulumeni kuye emphemeni, ngokusebenzisa i-reservoir engajulile enwebeka ngaphansi kwayo yonke indawo evaliwe yemisebenzi. Ngomhubhe ofakwe ngemuva, leli chibi ligcinwa ligcwaliswe ngamanzi ngokushesha njengoba lihwamuka. Abekho oshimula bangaphandle; avule aqonde odongeni olungemuva. Futhi lapha ake sibuyele emuva umzuzwana.

Cishe kwakuyihora lesishiyagalolunye ebusuku lapho ama-try-works e-Pequod aqala khona kulolu hambo lwamanje. KwakungekaStubb ukwengamela ibhizinisi.

“Usulungile lapho? Chamusela, bese umqalisa. Wena pheka, ushise imisebenzi.” Lokhu kwakuyinto elula, ngoba umbazi ubelokhu ehlohla amashefa akhe esithandweni kulo lonke leli phaseji. Lapha makushiwo ukuthi ohambweni lomkhomo umlilo wokuqala ku-try-works kufanele udliwe okwesikhashana ngezinkuni. Ngemva kwalokho akukho zinkuni ezisetshenziswayo, ngaphandle nje kokuthi kube yindlela yokuthungela ngokushesha kuphethiloli oyinhloko. Ngamafuphi, ngemva kokuvivinywa, ama-blubber ashwabene, manje abizwa ngokuthi ama-scraps noma ama-fritters, asaqukethe izici zawo eziningi ezingathandeki. Lezi zinhlayiya zidla amalangabi. Njengomfel’ ukholo ovuthayo, noma umfel’ ukholo ozidlayo, owake wavutha, umkhomo uzikhiphela owakhe uphethiloli bese uyasha ngomzimba wakhe. Sengathi angashisa owakhe umusi! ngoba intuthu yakhe iyesabeka ukuyihogela, futhi uyihogele kufanele, futhi hhayi lokho kuphela, kodwa kufanele uphile kuyo isikhathi. Inephunga elingakhulumeki, lasendle, le-Hindoo ngakho, njengokuthi lingase licashe eduze kwama-funereal pyres. Kunuka uphiko lwesokhohlo losuku lokwahlulela; kuyingxabano yomgodi.

Phakathi kwamabili imisebenzi yayisisebenza ngokugcwele. Sasiphumile esidunjini; kwase kwenziwa useyili; umoya wawuqabula; ubumnyama bolwandle lwasendle babunamandla. Kodwa lobo bumnyama bakhonjwa amalangabi avuthayo, okuthi ngezikhathi ezithile aphume emithonjeni ye-sooty, futhi akhanyise zonke izintambo eziphakeme ezindongeni, njengomlilo wamaGreki wodumo. Umkhumbi ovuthayo waqhubeka, njengokungathi uthunywe isenzo sokuziphindiselela. Ngakho izikebhe ezithwala isibabule zeHydriote, eCanaris, eziphuma ezikhumulweni zazo zaphakathi kwamabili, ezinamalangabi abanzi oseyili, zehlela phezu kwama-frigate aseTurkey, futhi zawagoqa ekushiseni.

I-hatch, ekhishwe phezulu kwemisebenzi, manje isinikeze indawo yomlilo ebanzi phambi kwazo. Kulokhu kwakumi ama-Tartarean ama-harpooneers angamaqaba, njalo ama-stoker omkhumbi we-whale. Ngezigxobo ezinkulu ezinde babephonsa izixuku zamalahle ezimbizeni ezivuthayo, noma babase imililo ngaphansi, kwaze kwaba yilapho amalangabi anjengenyoka ephuma eminyango ukuze ababambe ngezinyawo. Intuthu yagoqeka yaba yizinqwaba ezimnyama. Kuzo zonke izikhala zomkhumbi kwakukhona iphimbo lamafutha abilayo, okwakubonakala sengathi ukulangazelela ukugxumela ebusweni babo. Ngokuphambene nomlomo wemisebenzi, ngakolunye uhlangothi lweziko lokhuni olubanzi, kwakukhona i-windlass. Lokhu kwasebenzela usofa wasolwandle. Lapha bavule iwashi, lapho bengasebenzi ngenye indlela, bebuka ukushisa okubomvu komlilo, kuze kube yilapho amehlo abo ezwa ukushisa emakhanda abo. Izici zabo ezimfishane, manje zonke ezigcwele intuthu nezithukuthuku, izintshebe zabo ezinamathele, kanye nokucwebezela okuhlukile kwamazinyo abo, konke lokhu kwembulwa ngokumangalisayo emihlobiso engaqondakali yemisebenzi. Njengoba babexoxela omunye nomunye izigigaba zabo ezingengcwele, izindaba zabo ezesabekayo zixoxwa ngamazwi entokozo; njengokuhleka kwabo okungaphucukile kwaphumela kubo phezulu, njengamalangabi esithandweni; lapho naphambili, phambi kwabo, ama-harpooneers adlalisa umzimba ngezimfoloko namadiphu awo amakhulu; njengoba umoya uklabalasa, nolwandle lugxuma, nomkhumbi wabubula futhi watshuza, futhi nokho ugxilile udubula isihogo sawo esibomvu uqhubekele phambili nasebumnyameni bolwandle nobusuku, futhi ngokudelela wabhuquza ithambo elimhlophe emlonyeni walo, futhi wawukhafulela ngonya nxazonke; khona-ke i-Pequod egijimayo, egcwele izixhwanguxhwangu, futhi igcwele umlilo, futhi ishisa isidumbu, futhi ingena kulobo bumnyama bobumnyama, yabonakala ifana nomphefumulo womkhuzi wakhe we-monomaniac.

Kwabonakala kanjalo kimi, njengoba ngimi enqoleni yakhe, futhi amahora amaningi buthule ngiqondisa indlela yalo mkhumbi womlilo olwandle. Ngisongwe ngaleso sikhathi, ebumnyameni ngokwami, kodwa ngakubona kangcono ububomvu, ubuhlanya, ukunyanyeka kwabanye. Ukubona okuqhubekayo kwezimo ezimbi phambi kwami, kugcwele ingxenye yentuthu nengxenye emlilweni, lokhu ekugcineni kwazala imibono ehlobene emphefumulweni wami, ngokushesha nje lapho ngiqala ukuyekelela kulobo buthongo obungenakulandisa obake bangehlela phakathi kwamabili.

Kodwa ngalobo busuku, ikakhulukazi, kwenzeka into eyinqaba (futhi kusukela ngaleso sikhathi engaqondakali) kimi. Kusukela ekubuthongweni obuncane obuma, ngase ngazi ukuthi kukhona okungahambi kahle. Umgawuli womhlathi wangishaya uhlangothi, olwancika kulo; ezindlebeni zami kwakunoseyili ophansi, oqala nje ukuzamazama emoyeni; Ngacabanga ukuthi amehlo ami avulekile; Ngangiqaphela kancane ukuthi ngibeka iminwe yami ezivaloni futhi ngizelula ngomshini ziqhelelene kakhulu. Kodwa, naphezu kwakho konke lokhu, angizange ngibone ikhampasi phambi kwami ​​ukuze ngiqondise; nakuba kwakubonakala kodwa umzuzu nje kusukela kade ngibuka ikhadi, ngesibani sebhinacle esingaguquki esilikhanyisa. Ngokuphazima kweso wayemi ngemva kwesikebhe, futhi amadoda aseManilla ayesephuma egwedla. Kwaba nhlanga zimuka nomoya uKapteni wamaNgisi. Ngokubuyela emkhunjini wezihambi, futhi ubuso bubekwe njengensengetsha kowakhe, u-Ahabi wema waqonda eduze kwePequod.
