Okulandelayo: sizoyichaza kanjani i-whale, ngokungaphandle kwayo okusobala, ukuze ibhalwe ngokucacile ngaso sonke isikhathi esizayo? Ukuba mfushane-ke, umkhomo uyinhlanzi ephumayo enomsila ovundlile . Ukhona naye. Nokho inenkontileka, leyo ncazelo iwumphumela wokuzindla okunwetshiwe. I-walrus iqhuma kuhle komkhomo, kodwa i-walrus ayiyona inhlanzi, ngoba iphila emanzini. Kodwa ithemu yokugcina yencazelo isaqinile, njengoba ihlanganiswe neyokuqala. Cishe noma ubani kumelwe ukuba uye waqaphela ukuthi zonke izinhlanzi ezijwayele abantu basemhlabeni azinalo iflethi, kodwa ezinomsila oqondile, noma osuka phansi naphezulu. Nakuba, phakathi kwezinhlanzi eziphuma emanzini umsila, nakuba ungase umise ngendlela efanayo, uhlala ume ovundlile.

Ngencazelo engenhla yokuthi uyini umkhomo, angikushiyi nakancane ubuzalwane beleviathanic noma yisiphi isidalwa sasolwandle kuze kube manje esihlotshaniswa nomkhomo ngamaNantucketer anolwazi olungcono kakhulu; futhi, ngakolunye uhlangothi, ayixhumani nayo noma iyiphi inhlanzi ebithathwa ngokugunyazwayo njengeyangaphandle kuze kube manje.* Ngakho-ke, zonke izinhlanzi ezinomsila ezincane, ezipopozayo, nezivundlile kumele zifakwe kulolu hlelo lwesayensi yesayensi. Manje-ke, kuza ukuhlukana okukhulu kwawo wonke umkhomo.

*Ngiyaqaphela ukuthi kuze kube manje, izinhlanzi ezibizwa ngokuthi ama-Lamatins nama-Dugong (I-Pig-fish ne-Sow-fish of the Coffins of Nantucket) zifakwe izazi eziningi zemvelo phakathi kwemikhomo. Kodwa njengoba lezi zinhlanzi zezingulube ziyisethi enomsindo, edelelekile, ikakhulukazi ecashe emilonyeni yemifula, futhi idla utshani obumanzi, futhi ikakhulukazi njengoba zingaphumi, ngiyaziphika izimfanelo zazo njengemikhomo; futhi babanikeze namapasi abo ukuze bayeke i-Kingdom of Cetology.

INCWADI I. ( Folio ), ISAHLUKO I. ( I-Sperm Whale ).— Lomkhomo, phakathi kwamaNgisi asendulo ngokungaqondakali owaziwa ngokuthi umkhomo we-Trumpa, kanye ne-Physeter whale, kanye ne-Anvil Headed whale, uyiCachalot yamanje yamaFulentshi, kanye ne-Pottsfich yamaJalimane, kanye ne-Macrocephalus of the Long Words. Ngokungangabazeki, uyisakhamuzi esikhulu kunazo zonke emhlabeni; owesabekayo kunayo yonke imikhomo ongahlangana nayo; isici esihle kakhulu; futhi okokugcina, okubaluleke kakhulu kwezohwebo; ungukuphela kwesidalwa okutholakala kuso leyo nto eyigugu, i-spermaceti. Zonke izici zakhe ezingavamile, kwezinye izindawo eziningi, zizokwandiswa. Ikakhulu ngegama lakhe okumele ngikwenze manje. Kucatshangelwa ngokwefilosofi, akunangqondo. Emakhulwini athile eminyaka adlule, lapho i-Sperm whale yayingaziwa ngokuphelele ngobuntu bayo obufanele, nalapho uwoyela wayo wawutholakala ngephutha kuphela ezinhlanzini ezivalekile; ngalezo zinsuku i-spermaceti, kubonakala sengathi, yayidume kakhulu ukuthi ithathwe esidalweni esifanayo naleso esaziwa ngaleso sikhathi eNgilandi ngokuthi iGreenland noma i-Right Whale. Kwakuwumbono futhi, ukuthi le spermaceti efanayo kwakuyilawa amahlaya asheshayo eGreenland Whale lapho uhlamvu lokuqala lwegama oluvezwa ngokoqobo. Ngalezo zikhathi, futhi, i-spermaceti yayiyindlala kakhulu, ingasetshenziselwa ukukhanya, kodwa njengendawo yokugcoba kanye nemithi. Bekuzotholwa kuphela kubathengisi bezidakamizwa njengoba namuhla uthenga i-rhubarb ounce. Lapho, njengoba ngiphawula, ngokuhamba kwesikhathi, isimo sangempela se-spermaceti saziwa, igama layo langempela lisagcinwe abathengisi; ngokungangabazeki ukuthuthukisa ukubaluleka kwayo ngombono obaluleke ngokumangalisayo wokuntuleka kwayo. Ngakho-ke igama kufanele ekugcineni linikezwe umkhomo lapho le spermaceti ethathwe kuyo ngempela.

INCWADI I. ( Folio ), ISAHLUKO II. ( Umkhomo Olungile ).— Ngandlela thize lena iwuLeviyathani ohlonishwa kakhulu, kuba owokuqala ezingelwa umuntu njalo. Iveza isihloko esivame ukubizwa ngokuthi i-whalebone noma i-baleen; namafutha aziwa ngokukhethekile ngokuthi “amafutha omkhomo,” okuyisihloko esisezingeni eliphansi kwezentengiselwano. Phakathi kwabadobi, uqokwa ngokungakhethi yizo zonke iziqu ezilandelayo: Umkhomo; umkhomo waseGreenland; Umkhomo Omnyama; uMkhomo Omkhulu; Umkhomo Weqiniso; i-Right Whale. Kunokuningi okungaqondakali mayelana nokuthi hlobo luni lwezinhlobo ezibhabhadiswa ngobuningi. Uyini-ke umkhomo, engiwufaka ohlotsheni lwesibili lwamaFolio ami? Kuyi-Mysticetus Enkulu yezazi zemvelo zamaNgisi; umkhomo waseGreenland we-whalemen yamaNgisi; i-Baleine Ordinaire yama-whalemen aseFrance; i-Growlands Walfish yaseSweden. Ngumkhomo osekuyiminyaka engaphezu kwamakhulu amabili edlule ezingelwa amaDashi namaNgisi olwandle lwase-Arctic; iwumkhomo osekuyisikhathi eside abadobi baseMelika bewulandela olwandle lwaseNdiya, eBrazil Banks, oGwini lwaseNor' oluseNtshonalanga, nakwezinye izingxenye zomhlaba ezihlukahlukene, eziqokwa yibona i-Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Abanye benza sengathi babona umehluko phakathi komkhomo waseGreenland wamaNgisi kanye nomkhomo wamaMelika. Kodwa bavumelana ngokunembile kuzo zonke izici zabo ezinhle; futhi akukavezwa nelilodwa iqiniso elinqunyiwe elingamisa phezu kwalo umehluko omkhulu. Kungokwehlukana okungapheli okusekelwe ekwehlukeni okungaphelele, lapho eminye iminyango yomlando wemvelo iba inkimbinkimbi ephicanayo. Umkhomo olungile uzophathwa kwenye indawo isikhathi eside, kubhekiselwa ekucaciseni umkhomo wesidoda.

INCWADI I. ( Folio ), ISAHLUKO III. ( Fin-Back ).—Ngaphansi kwaleli khanda ngibala isilo, esiye sabonakala cishe kuzo zonke izilwandle, ngamagama ahlukahlukene okuthi Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, kanye noLong-John futhi ngokuvamile umkhomo ondiza yawo ekude evame ukuchazwa abagibeli abawela i-Atlantic, kuyi-packet tracks yaseNew York. Ngobude abufinyelelayo, futhi ku-baleen yakhe, i-Fin-back ifana ne-whale efanele, kodwa i-portly girth encane, nombala okhanyayo, isondela kumnqumo. Izindebe zakhe ezinkulu ziveza isici esinjengekhebula, esakhiwe ukugoqa okugoqekayo, okugoqekayo kwemibimbi emikhulu. Isici sakhe esihle esihlukanisayo, i-fin, athola kuso igama lakhe, ngokuvamile into ephawulekayo. Le fin ingamamitha amathathu noma amane ubude, ikhula iqonde ukusuka engxenyeni engemuva yengemuva, imise okwe-angular, futhi inomkhawulo ocijile kakhulu. Ngisho noma kungeyona ingxenye encane yesidalwa engabonakali, lesi siphetho esingasodwa, ngezinye izikhathi, siyobonakala siphuma phezulu. Lapho ulwandle luzolile ngokusesilinganisweni, futhi luphawulwe kancane ngeziyingi eziyindilinga, futhi leli phiko elifana ne-gnomon liyasukuma futhi lenze ithunzi endaweni eshwabene, kungase kucatshangwe ukuthi indingilizi enamanzi elizungezile ifane nokudayela, okuqoshwe kuyo isitayela nemigqa yehora ejikajikayo. Kulelo dayela lika-Ahazi ithunzi livame ukuhlehla. I-Fin-Back ayinabo abantu. Ubonakala engumuntu ozonda imikhomo, njengoba abanye abantu bezonda abantu. Amahloni kakhulu; njalo ehamba yedwa; akhuphukele phezulu ngokungalindelekile emanzini akude kakhulu futhi anolaka kakhulu; indiza yakhe eqondile nephakeme eyodwa ikhuphuka njengomkhonto omude ongathandeki ethafeni eliwugwadule; ephiwe amandla amangalisayo kanye nesivinini sokubhukuda, njengokudelela konke okufunwa umuntu; loLeviyathani ubonakala enguKayini oxoshiwe futhi onganqotshwa wohlanga lwakhe, ephethe uphawu lwakhe leso sitayela emhlane wakhe. Kusukela ekubeni ne-baleen emlonyeni wakhe, i-Fin-Back ngezinye izikhathi ifakwe nomkhomo wangakwesokudla, phakathi kwezinhlobo ze-theoretic ezihlotshaniswa nemikhomo ye-Whalebone , okungukuthi, imikhomo ene-baleen. Kulawa okuthiwa ama-Whalebone whales, kubonakala sengathi kunezinhlobo eziningana, eziningi zazo, nokho, ezaziwa kancane. Imikhomo enamakhala abanzi nemikhomo enoqhwaku; imikhomo enamakhanda e-pike; imikhomo ehlangene; imikhomo engaphansi kwemihlathi kanye nemikhomo erostrated, amagama abadobi ezinhlobo ezimbalwa.

Maqondana nalesi sikhalazo se-“Whalebone whales,” kubaluleke kakhulu ukusho, ukuthi noma kunjalo i-nomenclature enjalo ingase ibe lula ekusizeni ukubhekisela kohlobo oluthile lwemikhomo, nokho kuyize ukuzama ukuhlukaniswa okucacile kweLeviyathani, okusekelwe phezu kwe-baleen, noma i-hump, noma i-fin, noma amazinyo; naphezu kokuthi lezo zingxenye ezimakiwe noma izici ngokusobala zibonakala zivumelaniswe kangcono ukuze zikwazi ukukhokhela isisekelo sesimiso esivamile se-Cetology kunanoma yikuphi okunye ukuhlukaniswa komzimba okuhlukanisiwe, okuvezwa umkhomo, ngezinhlobo zawo. Kanjani pho? I-baleen, i-hump, i-back-fin, namazinyo; lezi yizinto ezinezici zazo ezihlakazekile ngokungakhethi phakathi kwazo zonke izinhlobo zemikhomo, ngaphandle kokubheka ukuthi yini engaba uhlobo lwesakhiwo sayo kweminye imininingwane ebaluleke kakhulu. Ngakho, i-sperm whale kanye ne-humpbacked whale, ngamunye une-hump; kodwa lapho isifaniso siyaphela. Khona-ke, wona lo mkhomo onesifumbu nomkhomo waseGreenland, ngamunye kulawa une-baleen; kodwa lapho futhi isifaniso siyaphela. Futhi kuyafana nje nezinye izingxenye ezishiwo ngenhla. Ezinhlotsheni ezihlukahlukene zemikhomo, yakha inhlanganisela enjalo engajwayelekile; noma, esimweni sanoma yimuphi omunye wabo, ukuzihlukanisa okungajwayelekile; njengokudelela ngokuphelele zonke izindlela ezijwayelekile ezakhiwe phezu kwaleso sisekelo. Kuleli dwala wonke ama-whale-naturalists ahlukene.

Kodwa kungase kucatshangwe ukuthi, ezingxenyeni zangaphakathi zomkhomo, ku-anatomy yakhe-lapho, okungenani, sizokwazi ukushaya isigaba esifanele. Cha; Iyiphi into, ngokwesibonelo, ekhona ku-anatomy yomkhomo waseGreenland emangalisa kakhulu kune-baleen yakhe? Nokho sibonile ukuthi nge-baleen yakhe akunakwenzeka kahle ukuhlukanisa umkhomo waseGreenland. Futhi uma wehlela ezibilinini zamaleviyathani ahlukahlukene, kungani lapho ungeke uthole ukuhlukanisa ingxenye yamashumi amahlanu etholakala ku-systematizer njengoba lezo zangaphandle sezivele zibaliwe. Kuyini-ke okusalayo? lutho ngaphandle kokubamba imikhomo ngomzimba, ngevolumu yayo yonke ekhululekile, futhi bayihlukanise ngesibindi ngaleyo ndlela. Futhi lolu uhlelo lwe-Bibliographical lapha olwamukelwayo; futhi iyona yodwa engaphumelela, ngoba iyodwa ingenzeka. Ukuze uqhubeke.

INCWADI I. ( Folio ) ISAHLUKO IV. ( Hump Back ).— Lo mkhomo uvame ukubonakala ogwini olusenyakatho yeMelika. Uthunjwe kaningi lapho, futhi wadonselwa ethekwini. Uphethe iphakethe elikhulu njengomthengisi; noma ungambiza ngomkhomo weNdlovu noCastle. Kunoma ikuphi, igama lakhe elidumile alimehluli ngokwanele, njengoba i-sperm whale nayo ineqhubu nakuba lincane. Amafutha akhe awabalulekile kakhulu. Une-baleen. Ungumdlali odlala kakhulu futhi onenhliziyo elula kunayo yonke imikhomo, okwenza igwebu lezitabane namanzi amhlophe ngokuvamile kunanoma iyiphi enye yazo.

INCWADI I. ( Folio ), ISAHLUKO V. ( Razor Back ).— Kuncane okwaziwayo ngalo mkhomo ngaphandle kwegama lakhe. Ngike ngimbone kude le eCape Horn. Ngokwemvelo yokuyeka, uyababalekela kokubili abazingeli nezazi zefilosofi. Yize engelona igwala kodwa akakaze aveze ingxenye yakhe ngaphandle komhlane wakhe ovuka ngokhakhayi olude olucijile. Akahambe. Kuncane engikwaziyo ngaye, futhi akekho omunye umuntu engimaziyo.

INCWADI I. ( Folio ), ISAHLUKO VI. ( Sulfur Bottom ).—Enye indoda eyekayo, enesisu sesibabule, ngokungangabazeki yazuza ngokukhuhla amathayili eThartari kwezinye zezindawo zayo zokutshuza ezijulile. Akavamile ukubonakala; okungenani angikaze ngimbone ngaphandle kwezilwandle ezikude eziseningizimu, bese kuba njalo ebangeni elikhulu kakhulu ukufunda ubuso bakhe. Akajahwa neze; wayebaleka nezintambo zomugqa. UProdigies utshelwa ngaye. Adieu, Sulfur Bottom! Akukho okunye engingakusho okuyiqiniso ngawe, noma iNantucketer endala kakhulu.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO I. ( Grampus ).— Nakuba le nhlanzi, ephefumula ngokuzwakalayo, noma kunalokho ukuphephetha kwayo, kuye kwanikeza abantu basemaphandleni isaga, yaziwa kakhulu ngokuthi iyindawo ehlala ekujuleni, nokho ayihlukaniswa ngokudumile phakathi kwemikhomo. Kodwa njengoba inazo zonke izici ezinhle eziphawulekayo zeleviyathani, izazi zemvelo eziningi ziye zaqaphela ukuthi iyiyo. Unosayizi we-octavo omaphakathi, uyahluka ukusuka kumafidi ayishumi nanhlanu kuye kwangamashumi amabili nanhlanu ubude, kanye nobukhulu obuhambisanayo obuzungeza ukhalo. Ubhukuda emihlambini; akazingelwa njalo, nakuba amafutha akhe emaningi ngobuningi, futhi ekulungele ukukhanya. Abanye abadobi indlela yakhe ithathwa njengesandulela sokuthuthuka komkhomo omkhulu wesidoda.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO II. ( Izinhlanzi Ezimnyama ).—Nginikeza amagama abadobi abathandwayo kuzo zonke lezi zinhlanzi, ngoba ngokuvamile zingcono kakhulu. Lapho noma yiliphi igama lenzeka lingacacile noma lingachazeki, ngizokusho lokho, bese ngiphakamisa elinye. Ngenza kanjalo manje, ngithinta iNhlanzi Emnyama, okuthiwa, ngoba ubumnyama buwumthetho cishe kuyo yonke imikhomo. Ngakho-ke, uma uthanda, mbize ngomkhomo. I-voracity yakhe iyaziwa, futhi kusukela esimweni sokuthi ama-engeli angaphakathi ezindebe zakhe agobile phezulu, uphethe i-Mephistophelean grin yaphakade ebusweni bakhe. Lo mkhomo unesilinganiso esingamamitha ayishumi nesithupha noma ayishumi nesishiyagalombili ubude. Utholakala cishe kuzo zonke izindawo. Unendlela engavamile yokubonisa iphiko lakhe elibanjwe emhlane lapho ebhukuda, elifana nekhala laseRoma. Uma bengaqashiwe kakhulu, abazingeli be-sperm whale ngezinye izikhathi babamba umkhomo we-Hyena, ukuze baqhubeke nokuhlinzeka ngamafutha ashibhile omsebenzi wasekhaya - njengabanye abagcini bezindlu abayongayo, ngaphandle kwenkampani, futhi bebodwa, bashisa i-tallow engathandeki esikhundleni se-wax enephunga elimnandi. Nakuba ama-blubber abo emancane kakhulu, eminye yale mikhomo izokunikeza amafutha angaphezu kwamalitha angamashumi amathathu.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO III. ( Narwhale ), okungukuthi, umkhomo we-Nostril .—Esinye isibonelo somkhomo owaziwayo ngegama, oqanjwe kanjalo ngicabanga ukuthi ophondo lwakhe olungavamile ekuqaleni kwakucatshangwa ukuthi lunekhala eliphakeme. Isidalwa singamafidi ayishumi nesithupha ubude, kanti uphondo lwaso luba ngamafidi amahlanu, nakuba amanye adlula ishumi, futhi aze afinyelele kumafidi ayishumi nanhlanu. Uma sikhuluma nje, lolu phondo luyibamba elide, eliphuma emhlathini lilandelana elicindezelekile kancane ukusuka ovundlile. Kodwa itholakala kuphela ohlangothini olubi, olunomphumela ongemuhle, inika umnikazi wayo into efana nesici sendoda eyinxele exakile. Iyiphi injongo eqondile lolu phondo noma umkhonto ophendulayo, kungaba nzima ukusho. Ayibonakali isetshenziswa njengenkezo yenkemba ne-bill-fish; nakuba amanye amatilosi engitshela ukuthi iNarwhale iyisebenzisela ireki ekuphenduleni phansi kolwandle ukuze ithole ukudla. UCharley Coffin uthe isetshenziselwa ukubhoboza iqhwa; ngoba iNarwhale, ikhuphukela phezu koLwandle Olumhlophe, futhi iluthola luneqhwa, iphakamise uphondo lwayo, kanjalo ibhodloze. Kodwa awukwazi ukufakazela noma iyiphi yalezi zilinganiso ukuthi ilungile. Owami umbono uwukuthi, nakuba lolu phondo olunohlangothi olulodwa lungasetshenziswa ngempela yiNarwhale—noma ngabe lokho kungaba njalo—kungaba lula kakhulu kuye ukuthola ifolda yokufunda amapheshana. I-Narwhale engiyizwile ibizwa ngokuthi i-Tusked whale, i-Horned whale, kanye ne-Unicorn whale. Impela uyisibonelo esinelukuluku lokwazi se-Unicornism esitholakala cishe kuyo yonke imibuso yemvelo egqwayizayo. Kubabhali abathile abadala abanolwazi ngiye ngaqoqa ukuthi lolu phondo lwe-unicorn yasolwandle ezikhathini zasendulo lwalubhekwa njengekhambi elikhulu lokulwa nobuthi, futhi ngenxa yalokho, ukulungiswa kwalo kwaletha amanani amakhulu. Yabuye yagaywa yaba usawoti oshisayo wabesifazane abaqulekayo, ngendlela efanayo nezimpondo zenyamazane yesilisa ezikhiqizwa ngayo zibe i-hartshorn. Ekuqaleni kwakubhekwa njengento ethakazelisa kakhulu. IBlack Letter ingitshela ukuthi uSir Martin Frobisher ekubuyeni kwakhe kulolo hambo, lapho uQueen Bess enyakazisa ngesandla sakhe esibucwebe kuye ngefasitela laseGreenwich Palace, njengoba umkhumbi wakhe onesibindi wehla ngoMfula iThames; “Lapho uSir Martin ebuya kulolo hambo,” kusho uBlack Letter, “eguqe ngamadolo wethula uphondo olude olumangalisayo lweNarwhale, olwahlala isikhathi eside ngemva kokulenga esigodlweni saseWindsor.” Umbhali wase-Ireland uyakuphikisa ukuthi u-Earl waseLeicester, eguqe ngamadolo, naye wethula phezulu kwakhe olunye uphondo, oluphathelene nesilo sasemhlabeni semvelo ye-unicorn.

I-Narwhale inokubukeka okuhle kakhulu, okufana nengwe, inombala omhlophe wobisi-omhlophe, onamachashaza amnyama ayindilinga nayi-oblong. Amafutha akhe aphakeme kakhulu, acacile futhi mahle; kodwa incane, futhi akavamile ukuzingelwa. Utholakala kakhulu olwandle oluyindilinga.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO IV. ( Killer ).— Ngalo mkhomo okuncane kwaziwa ngokunembile yiNantucketer, futhi akukho lutho kulowo othi isazi semvelo. Ngokubona kwakhe le kude, mangisho ukuthi wayekhuluma ngobukhulu be-gramus. Unonya kakhulu—uhlobo lwezinhlanzi ze-Feegee. Ngezinye izikhathi ubamba imikhomo emikhulu ye-Folio ngodebe, futhi ilenga lapho njengomlwelwe, kuze kube yilapho isilokazane esinamandla sikhathazeka size sife. UMbulali akazingelwa neze. Angikaze ngizwe ukuthi unamafutha anjani. Okuhlukile kungase kuthathwe egameni elinikezwe lo mkhomo, ngenxa yokungacaci kwawo. Ngokuba sonke singababulali emhlabeni nasolwandle; I-Bonapartes ne-Sharks ifakiwe.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO V. ( Thrasher ).— Lo mnumzane udume ngomsila wakhe, awusebenzisela i-ferule ekushayeni izitha zakhe. Ugibela umhlane womkhomo we-Folio, futhi njengoba ebhukuda, usebenza ngendlela yakhe ngokumbhaxabula; njengoba abanye abafundisi besikole bezwana emhlabeni ngenqubo efanayo. Kuncane okwaziwayo nge-Thrasher kune-Killer. Zombili zingamahlakani, ngisho nasezilwandle ezingenamthetho.

Iphetha kanjalo INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), futhi iqala INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ).

DUODECIMOES.—Lena ihlanganisa nemikhomo emincane. I. I-Huzza Porpoise. II. I-Algerine Porpoise. III. I-Porpoise Enomlomo Wommbila.

Kulabo abangazange bathole ithuba lokutadisha le ndaba ngokukhethekile, kungase kubonakale kuyinqaba, ukuthi izinhlanzi ngokuvamile ezingeqi amamitha amane noma amahlanu kufanele zihlanganiswe phakathi kwe-WHALES—igama, ngomqondo ovamile, elihlale lidlulisela umqondo wobukhulu. Kodwa izidalwa ezibekwe ngenhla njengama-Duodecimoes ziyimikhomo engenaphutha, ngokwemibandela yencazelo yami yokuthi uyini umkhomo— okungukuthi inhlanzi ephuma emanzini, enomsila ovundlile.

INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ), ISAHLUKO 1. ( I-Huzza Porpoise ).— Lena i-porpoise evamile etholakala cishe kuwo wonke umhlaba. Igama ngelami; ngoba kunezinhlobo ezingaphezu koyedwa wezimbila, futhi kumelwe kwenziwe okuthile ukuze zihlukaniseke. Ngimbiza kanjalo, ngoba uhlale ebhukuda ezinhlwini eziwubukhazikhazi, ezilokhu zijikijela ezulwini olwandle njengamakepisi esixukwini sangomhla wesine kuJulayi. Ukubukeka kwabo ngokuvamile kunconywa umatilosi. Egcwele imimoya emihle, ahlala ephuma eziphephweni ezihelezayo aye ngasemoyeni. Yibo abafana abahlale bephila phambi komoya. Babhekwa njengebika lenhlanhla. Uma wena ngokwakho ungamelana nezinjabulo ezintathu ekubukeni lezi zinhlanzi ezinonya, khona-ke izulu lizokusiza; umoya wobunkulunkulu awukho kini. I-Huzza Porpoise eyondleke kahle, eningi izokunikeza igalani elilodwa lamafutha amahle. Kodwa uketshezi olucolekile noluthambile olukhishwe emihlathini yakhe lubaluleke kakhulu. Kucelwa phakathi kwabasiki bebucwebe nabakhi bamawashi. Amatilosi ayelubeka ezicijini zawo. Inyama yengulube idla kahle, uyazi. Kungenzeka akukaze kwenzeke kuwe ukuthi imbovane iqhume. Ngempela, i-spout yakhe incane kangangokuthi ayibonakali kalula. Kodwa ngokuzayo uma uthola ithuba, mbuke; futhi uzobona-ke umkhomo omkhulu we-Sperm ngokwakhe emncane.

INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ), ISAHLUKO II. ( I -Algerine Porpoise ).— Isigebengu. Kunonya kakhulu. Utholakala kuphela, ngicabanga, ePacific. Mkhulu ngandlela thize kune-Huzza Porpoise, kodwa iningi lezinto ezijwayelekile ezifanayo. Mthukuthelise, futhi uyobopha ushaka. Sengimehlise izikhathi eziningi, kodwa angikaze ngimbone ebanjwa.

INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ), ISAHLUKO III. ( I-Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).— Inyamazane enkulu kunazo zonke; futhi itholakala kuphela ePacific, kuze kube manje njengoba yaziwa. Okuwukuphela kwegama lesiNgisi, aqokwe ngalo kuze kube manje, ngelikabadobi—i-Right-Whale Porpoise, esimweni sokuthi utholakala ikakhulukazi eduze kwaleyo Folio. Ngokwesimo sakhe, uhlukile ngezinga elithile ku-Huzza Porpoise, ngokuba yi-rotund encane ne-jolly girth; ngempela, ungumuntu ohlanzekile futhi onjengomnumzane. Akanawo amaphiko emhlane (amanye ama-porpoise amaningi), unomsila omuhle, namehlo amaNdiya azwelayo anombala we-hazel. Kepha umlomo wakhe onempuphu uphanga konke. Nakuba wonke umhlane wakhe wehlele emaphikweni asemaceleni uyisabuli ejulile, nokho umugqa womngcele, ohlukile njengophawu olusemkhunjini womkhumbi, olubizwa ngokuthi “ukhalo olukhanyayo,” lowo mugqa uyamdabula usuka esiqwini uye ngemuva, ngemibala emibili ehlukene, emnyama phezulu nomhlophe ngezansi. Umhlophe uhlanganisa ingxenye yekhanda lakhe, kanye nomlomo wakhe wonke, okumenza abukeke sengathi usanda kuphunyuka ekuvakasheleni isihluku esiya esikhwameni sokudla. Isici esibi kakhulu nesiphundu! Amafutha akhe afana kakhulu nalawo ezimbila ezivamile.

Ngale kwe-DUODECIMO, lolu hlelo aluqhubeki, njengoba nje i-Porpoise iwumkhomo omncane kunawo wonke. Ngenhla, unawo wonke amaLeviyathani abalulekile. Kodwa kukhona inqwaba yemikhomo engaqinisekile, ebalekayo, enengxenye-fabulous, okuyinto, njenge-whaleman yaseMelika, engiyazi ngedumela, kodwa hhayi uqobo. Ngizobala ngezibizo zabo zenqaba; ngoba mhlawumbe uhlu olunjalo lungaba usizo kubaphenyi bakusasa, abangase baqedele enginakho lapha kodwa sebeqalile. Uma noma yimuphi wale mikhomo elandelayo, uzobanjwa futhi umakwe ngemuva kwalokhu, khona-ke angafakwa kalula kulolu hlelo, ngokuya nge-Folio, Octavo, noma i-Duodecimo magnitude yakhe:—I-Bottle-Nose Whale; iJunk Whale; Umkhomo Onekhanda Lempuphu; umkhomo waseKapa; Umkhomo Oholayo; i-Cannon Whale; iScragg Whale; umkhomo wethusi; Umkhomo Wendlovu; umkhomo we-Iceberg; i-Quog Whale; i-Blue Whale; njll. Ezikhulwini zesi-Icelandic, Dutch, nesiNgisi sakudala, kungase kucashunwe ezinye izinhlu zemikhomo engaqinisekile, ebusiswe ngazo zonke izinhlobo zamagama angama-uncouth. Kodwa ngiyazishiya njengeziphelelwe yisikhathi; futhi ngeke akwazi ukusiza ukuwasola ngemisindo nje, egcwele ubuLeviyathani, kodwa engasho lutho.

Ekugcineni: Kwashiwo ekuqaleni ukuthi lesi simiso ngeke sibe khona, futhi ngaso leso sikhathi, sipheleliswe. aniboni ngokusobala ukuthi ngigcinile izwi lami. Kodwa manje ngishiya iSistimu yami yesayensi yesayensi yemvelo imile kanjalo ingakaqedwa, njengoba kwasala iKhathedrali elikhulu laseCologne, ukhreyini usamile phezu kombhoshongo ongaqediwe. Ukuze ukwakhiwa okuncane kungase kuqedwe ngabakhi babo bokuqala; abakhulu, beqiniso, bahlala beshiya i-copestone ezizukulwaneni zabo. UNkulunkulu angigcine ngingaqedi lutho.
