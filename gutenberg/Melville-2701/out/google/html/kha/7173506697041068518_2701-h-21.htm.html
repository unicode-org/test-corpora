Mynta, da kaba lum ia ki dieng spare na trai, bad da kaba jied ia uwei na u dieng hickory, ryngkat bad ka sniehdoh kaba dang pynrung ia u, u Ahab u la pyndait ia ka tduh ha ka soket jong ka nar. Nangta la pyllait ia kawei ka coil jong ka tow-line kaba thymmai, bad katto katne ki phut jong ka la rah sha ka jingpynher lyer, bad la pynjrong ia ka haduh ka jingkhluit kaba khraw. Da kaba pynkhih ia la ka kjat halor jong ka, haduh ba ka lynti kan sawa kum ka lyngkhot lyngkhai, nangta da ka jingsngewtynnat u la pyndem halor jong ka, bad haba um iohi ba ym don jingsahkut, u Ahab u la pyrta, “Ka bha! bad mynta na ka bynta ki jingkem.”

Ha kawei ka tduh ka lynti ka long kaba khlem jingteh, bad ki shniuh ba la pynphriang kyrpang baroh la pyndait bad la shna sawdong ka tduh jong ka harpoon; nangta la pynher jur ia u dieng shapoh ka soket; na ka tduh kaba shapoh la bud ia ka lynti shiteng lynti ha ka jingjrong jong u dieng, bad la pynskhem skhem kumta, da kaba pynskhem ia ki nar. Kane ka jingleh, u dieng, ka nar bad ka lynti — kum ki Lai tylli ki Jinglong — ki la sah khlem lah ban pyniakhlad, bad u Ahab u la lyngngoh bad u la iaid lynti bad ka atiar; ka jingsawa jong ka kjat sniehdoh jong u, bad ka jingsawa jong u dieng hickory, baroh ar ki sawa jylliew ha man la ka dieng. Hynrei shuwa ba un rung ha ka kamra jong u, la iohsngew ia ka sur kaba jem, kaba ym long tynrai, kaba shiteng lyngngoh, hynrei kaba sngewsih tam. Ah, u Pip! ka jingrkhie bein kaba sngewsih jong phi, ka khmat kaba sngap jar hynrei kaba khlem shongthait; baroh ki jingkynthoh phylla jong phi kim shym la iasoh khlem jingmut bad ka jingsngewsih kaba dum jong ka lieng kaba sngewsih, bad ki la rkhie bein ia ka!
