LYNNONG 55. Ki Dur Bashyrkhei jong ki Whale.

Ngan sa rong jrong ia phi katba lah khlem ka canvas, kaei kaei kaba kum ka dur bashisha jong u dohkha kumba u paw ha ka jingshisha ha ka khmat jong u nongri dohkha haba ha la ka jong ka met ba pura la pyndait ia u dohkha hajan ka lieng dohkha khnang ba un lah ban iaid beit hangta. Ka lah ban long kaba donkam, namarkata, mynshuwa ban pynbna ia kita ki dur ba sngewtynnad ba la pyrkhat shaphang jong u kiba wat haduh kine ki sngi ki tuklar da ka jingsngewskhem ia ka jingngeit jong u nongshong shnong. Ka la dei ka por ban pynbeit ia ka pyrthei ha kane ka kam, da kaba pynshisha ba kum kine ki dur jong u dohkha baroh ki long kiba bakla.

Ka lah ban long ba ia ka tynrai ba kongsan jong kita ki jingpynbyrngia ba la shna da ki dur baroh yn shem hapdeng ki jingtei ba rim tam jong ki Hindu, ki Ijipt bad ki Grik. Namar naduh kito ki por kiba thymmai hynrei kiba khlem akor haba ha ki jingker maw marble jong ki tempi, ha ki nongrim jong ki mot, bad ha ki ryntieh, ki medal, ki khuri bad ki pisa, la dro ia u dolphin ha ki jingthew jong ki ryntieh kum u Saladin, bad ka khlieh kaba la sop da ka ryndang kum ka jong u St. naduh kata ka por ka la don kaei kaei kaba kum kata ka jait laisen kaba la synshar, ym tang ha ki dur kiba pawnam tam jong u dohkha whale, hynrei ha kiba bun ki jingpyni science shaphang jong u.

Mynta, da ki jingeh baroh, ka dur kaba rim tam kaba dang don mynta la kumno kumno kaba pynpaw ba ka dei ka jong u dohkha, ka dei ban shem ha ka krem-pagoda ba pawnam jong ka Elephanta, ha ri India. Ki Brahmin ki pynskhem ba ha ki jingtei ba la jan kut jong kata ka pagoda bymjukut, baroh ki kam khaii bad ki jingpyrshang, kawei pa kawei ka jingtrei kaba lah ban pyrkhat jong u briew, la buh lypa ia ki rta shuwa ba uno uno na ki un mih shisha. Kam long kaba phylla te, ba ha kano kano ka rukom ka kam ba donburom jong ngi kaba long ka kam ri dohkha ka la dei ban don hangta kaba la shad dur. U dohkha Hindoo ba la kdew, u mih ha ka bynta ba kyrpang jong ka kynroh, kaba pyni ia ka jingwan long briew jong u Vishnu ha ka dur jong u leviathan, uba la tip da ki nongpule kum u Matse Avatar. Hynrei wat la kane ka jingtei ka long shiteng briew bad shiteng dohkha, kumta tang ban ai ia ka tdong jong uba hadien, hynrei kata ka bynta kaba rit jong u ka long kaba bakla baroh. Ka kham long kum ka tdong kaba rit jong u dohkha anaconda, ban ia ki kti kiba jylliew jong ki bniat ba itynnad jong u dohkha whale bashisha.

Hynrei leit sha ki Gallery kiba rim, bad peit mynta ia ka dur jong uwei u nongdro dur Khristan uba khraw jong kane ka dohkha; namar um jop kham bha ban ia u Hindu ba mynshuwa ka jingshlei um. Ka dei ka dur jong u Guido jong u Perseus uba pyllait im ia ka Andromeda na u mrad duriaw ne u dohkha whale. Naei u Guido u ioh ia ka nuksa jong kum kata ka jingthaw kaba phylla kum kata? Bad u Hogarth ruh, ha kaba dro ia kajuh ka dur ha la ka jong ka “U Perseus Uba Hiar,” um pynpaw kham bha ia kawei ka dur. Ka jingkhluit kaba khraw jong uta u mrad Hogarth ka lyngngoh ha ka sla um, tang khyndiat eh ka tan ia ka um tang shi inshi ruh. U don ka jait howdah ha ka tyrpeng jong u, bad ka shyntur jong u kaba don ki bniat kiba la pynjrong ha kaba ki umsaw ki tuid, lah ban shim ia ka na ka bynta ka Khyrdop jong ki Nongshetkylla kaba leit na ka Thames da ka um shapoh ka Tower. Nangta, ki don ki dohkha Prodromus jong u Scotch Sibbald uba rim, bad u dohkha Jonah, kumba la pyni ha ki jingthoh jong ki Baibl barim bad ki jingot jong ki kot sdang barim. Yn ong aiu shaphang kine? Katba ia ka dohkha whale jong u nongpynbeit kot kaba iaid sawdong kum u tnat sohjew sawdong ka stock jong ka lyngkhot lyngkhai kaba hiar — kumba la shon bad pynitynnad ha ki tyrpeng bad ki sla kyrteng jong kiba bun ki kot kiba rim bad kiba thymmai — kata ka long ka jingthaw kaba itynnad bha hynrei kaba pura kaba phylla, kaba la pyni nuksa, nga shim ia ka, na ki dur kiba kum kita bad ki dur kiba kum kita. Wat la la khot ia ka ha ka pyrthei baroh kawei kum ka dohkha dolphin, hynrei nga khot pynban ia kane ka dohkha jong u nongpynbeit kot ka jingpyrshang ia u dohkha whale; namar la thmu kumta ha ka por ba la pynmih nyngkong ia ka tiar. La pynrung ia ka da uwei u nongpynmih kot barim jong ka ri Italy hangno hangno ha ka spah snem kaba 15, ha ka por jong ka Jingpynmih pat ia ka Jingpule; bad ha kito ki sngi, bad wat haduh ka por kaba kham slem bha, la ju shim ba ki Dolphin ki dei ki jait dohkha Lebiathan.

Ha ki jingpynitynnad bad kiwei kiwei ki jingpynitynnad jong ki katto katne ki kot hyndai phin sa iakynduh teng teng ia ki jingktah kiba sngewtynnad shibun ha u dohkha, ha kaba ki jait um, ki tyllong um ba shit bad ba khriat, ka Saratoga bad ka Baden-Baden, ki wan bthei na ka jabieng jong u kaba khlem sangeh. Ha ka sla-kyrteng jong ka kot tynrai jong ka “Ka Jingkiew shaphrang jong ka Jingpule” phin shem katto katne ki dohkha whale kiba kwah tip.

Hynrei da kaba iehnoh ia kine ki jingpyrshang bym long tbit baroh, to ngin peit bniah ia kita ki dur jong u leviathan uba pynpaw ba ki long ki jingpynpaw ba stad, kiba stad, da kito kiba tip. Ha ka jinglum jingtip jong u Harris barim shaphang ki jingleit jingwan ki don katto katne ki pliang jong ki dohkha ba la sei na ka kot jong ki nong Dutch shaphang ki jingleit jingwan, A.D. 1671, kaba la ai kyrteng “A Whale Voyage to Spitzbergen ha ka lieng Jonas in the Whale, u Peter Peterson na Friesland, master.” Ha kawei na kita ki pliang la pyni ia ki dohkha whale, kum ki lyngkha baheh jong ki dieng, ba ki thiah hapdeng ki ice-isle, bad ki blang lieh kiba mareh halor ki tyrpeng jong ki kiba dang im. Ha kawei pat ka pliang, la shna ia ka jingbakla kaba phylla da kaba pyni ia ka dohkha whale da ki bniat kiba jrong.

Nangta pat, ka don ka quarto kaba itynnad, kaba la thoh da uwei u Kapten Colnett, u Kapten Post ha ka kynhun shipai duriaw jong ka ri Bilat, kaba la ai kyrteng “Ka Jingleit kylleng ka Cape Horn sha ki Duriaw South, na ka bynta ban pyniar ia ka jaka ri dohkha Spermaceti Whale.” Ha kane ka kot ka don ka jingthoh kaba la pynpaw ba ka long ka “Dur jong u dohkha Physeter ne Spermaceti, ba la ring da ka jingthew na uwei uba la shah pyniap ha rud duriaw jong ka Mexico, ha u bnai August, 1793, bad la pynkiew ia u halor ka lieng.” Ngam artatien ba u kapten u la shim ia kane ka dur kaba shisha na ka bynta ka jingmyntoi jong ki shipai duriaw jong u. Ban kdew tang kawei shaphang jong ka, to ngan ong ba ka don ka khmat kaba iadei, kat kum ka jingthew kaba iadon ryngkat, bad u dohkha uba la san pura, kan pynlong ia ka khmat jong uta u dohkha kum ka jingkhang-bow kaba jrong kumba san phut. Ah, ko kapten shlur jong nga, balei phim ai ia ngi ia u Jonah uba peit na kata ka khmat!

Bad ki jinglum jingtip kiba pyrkhat jylliew tam ia ka History Mariang na ka bynta ka jingmyntoi jong ki samla bad kiba rit mynsiem, kim long ruh kiba lait na kajuh ka jingbakla kaba shyrkhei. Peit ia kata ka kot kaba pawnam “Ka Mariang ba la pynim jong u nongshna ksiar.” Ha ka jingpynmih lyngkot jong ka London jong ka snem 1807, ki don ki pla jong uwei u “doh” bad u “doh” ba la kynnoh. Ngam kwah ban long kumba nga long uba khlem akor, hynrei kane ka dohkha whale kaba sniew dur ka long kum ka sniang kaba la ot noh; bad, katba ia u narwhale, tang kawei ka jingpeit ia u ka biang ban pynlyngngoh ia u briew, ba ha kane ka spah snem kaba khatkhyndai lah ban pyni ia kum uta u bsein uba shisha ha kano kano ka paidbah kaba stad jong ki khynnah skul.

Nangta pat, ha ka snem 1825, u Bernard Germain, u Count de Lacépède, u riewstad ba khraw jong ka mariang, u la pynmih ia kawei ka kot ba la pynbeit ryntih da ka saian, ha kaba la don katto katne ki dur jong ki jait dohkha bapher bapher jong u Leviathan. Kine baroh kim long tang kiba bakla, hynrei ka dur jong u Mysticetus ne Greenland whale (kata ka mut, u Right whale), wat u Scoresby, u briew uba la shemphang la slem bha kum uba ktah ia kata ka jait dohkha, u pynbna ba um don ia la u paralok ha ka mariang.

Hynrei ia ka jingbuh ia ka lyngkhot lyngkhai ha kane ka kam kaba bakla baroh la buh kyrpang na ka bynta u Frederick Cuvier uba stad, u hynmen jong u Baron ba pawnam. Ha ka snem 1836, u la pynmih ia ka kot Natural History of Whales, ha kaba u ai ia kaei kaba u khot ka dur jong u Sperm Whale. Shuwa ban pyni ia kata ka dur ha uno uno u nong Nantuket, ka bha ba phin pynbiang ia ka jingleit phai lyngkot jong phi na Nantuket. Ha kawei ka kyntien, u ‘Sperm Whale’ jong u Frederick Cuvier um dei u Sperm Whale, hynrei u dei u squash. Hooid, um shym la ioh jingmyntoi satia na ka jingleit khwai dohkha whale (kum kine ki briew kim ju ioh), hynrei nangno u la ioh ia kata ka dur, mano ba lah ban iathuh? Lehse u la ioh ia ka kumba u nongmihkhmat jong u ha kajuh ka phang, u Desmarest, u la ioh ia kawei na ki jingpynmih khun ba shisha jong u; kata, na ka jingdro jong ki nong China. Bad kiei ki jait shynrang kiba im bad kiba don ka pensil kita ki nong China, bun ki khuri bad ki pliang kiba phylla ki pyntip ia ngi.

Katba ia ki dohkha whale jong ki nongdro dak kiba iohi ha ki surok kiba la jrong halor ki dukan jong ki nongdie umphniang, yn ong aiu shaphang jong ki? Ki long kyllum lang u Richard III. ki dohkha whale, kiba don ki bniat kiba rit, bad kiba shyrkhei bha; ka jingbam mynstep ha ki lai ne saw tylli ki tart jong ki nongleit jingleit, kata ki lieng whale kiba dap da ki nongleit jingleit: ki jingduna met jong ki ki tuid ha ki duriaw jong ka snam bad ka rong blue.

Hynrei kine ki jingbakla kiba bun jait ha kaba pyni ia u dohkha whale kim da long kiba phylla than hadien baroh. Pyrkhat! Kiba bun ki dur science la shim na ki dohkha kiba la shah rong noh; bad kine ki long kumba thikna kum ka jingdro ia ka lieng kaba la shah pynpra, kaba don ka tyrpeng kaba la shah pynpra, kan pyni thikna ia ka mrad kaba donburom hi ha ka jingsarong jong ka baroh kaba khlem shah pynpra jong ka tdong bad ka tdong. Wat la ki hati ki la ieng na ka bynta ki jingjrong ba pura jong ki, hynrei u Lebiathan uba im um pat ju pynkhih hok ia lade na ka bynta ka dur jong u. U dohkha whale uba im, ha ka jingkhraw bad jingkordor ba pura jong u, u long tang ban iohi ha duriaw ha ki um bym lah ban batai; bad ha um ka bynta kaba heh jong u ka long kaba lait na ka jingpeit, kum ka lieng kaba la pynher ia ka lain thma; bad na kata ka element ka long kaei kaei kaba ym lah ban long junom ia u briew uba iap ban pynkiew ia u da ka met sha suinbneng, khnang ban pynneh pynsah ia ki jingkhih bad ki jingkhih ba khlain jong u baroh. Bad, ban ym kren ia ka jingiapher kaba lah ban shim ba ka long kaba khraw bha hapdeng u khun dohkha uba dang rit bad u dohkha Plato uba la san pura; hynrei, wat ha ka jingjia jong uwei na kita ki khun dohkha ba dih um ba la pynkiew sha ka tduh jong ka lieng, kum kata ka long ka dur jong u kaba phylla, kaba kum ka eel, kaba jem, kaba pher bapher, ba ka jingpynpaw kaba thikna jong u u ksuid hi um lah ban kem.

Hynrei lah ban pyrkhat, ba na ka sniehdoh kaba lyngkhuid jong u dohkha whale uba la shah rong noh, lah ban ioh ia ki jingpynpaw kiba thikna kiba ktah ia ka dur bashisha jong u. Khyndiat ruh em. Namar ka long kawei na kiei kiei kiba kham sngewphylla shaphang une u Lebiathan, ba ka sniehdoh jong u ka ai tang khyndiat eh ka jingmut shaphang ka dur ba kyllum jong u. Wat la ka sniehdoh jong u Jeremy Bentham, kaba la pyndait na ka bynta ka sharak ha ka library jong uwei na ki nongpyniap briew jong u, ka pynpaw thikna ia ka jingmut jong u tymmen uba heh khmat uba pyndonkam ia ki jingdonkam, ryngkat bad kiwei pat ki jinglong ba pawnam jong u Jeremy baroh; hynrei ym lah ban shim ia kaei kaei kaba kum kane na kano kano ka shyieng ba la pyniasoh jong u leviathan. Ha ka jingshisha, kumba ong u Nongleit lyngkha uba khraw, ka sniehdoh kaba tang jong u dohkha whale ka don kajuh ka jingiadei bad u mrad uba la pyndait pura bad uba la pyndap pura kumba u khniang u don bad u chrysalis uba tap ia u haduh katta katta. Ia kane ka jinglong kyrpang la paw shynna ha ka khlieh, kumba ha katto katne ki bynta jong kane ka kot yn pyni da kaba shu jia ryngkhat. La pyni ruh ia u da kaba sngewtynnad shibun ha ka side fin, ki shyieng jong ka ki iahap la jan thikna bad ki shyieng jong ka kti briew, lait noh tang ia u thylliej. Kane ka fin ka don saw tylli ki kti shyieng ba man ka por, ka kti ba pyni, ka kti pdeng, ka kti ba rit bad ka kti barit. Hynrei kine baroh ki sah junom ha ka jingtap doh jong ki, kumba ki kti briew ha ka jingtap ba la shna da ki briew. “Katno katno ruh u dohkha whale u lah ban shakri ia ngi teng teng,” la ong u Stubb uba sngewtynnad ha kawei ka sngi, “ym lah ban ong shisha ba un bat ia ngi khlem ki mits.”

Namar kine ki daw baroh, te, la kumno kumno phi lah ban peit ia ka, phi dei ban pynkut nia ba u Lebiathan uba khraw u long uta uwei u jingthaw ha ka pyrthei uba dei ban sah khlem da rong haduh kaba kut. Hooid, kawei ka dur ka lah ban hiar ha ka dak kaba kham jan ban ia kawei pat, hynrei ym don uwei ruh uba lah ban hiar ia ka da kano kano ka jinglong kaba thikna kaba thikna bha. Kumta ym don ka lad pyrthei ban lap thikna kumno u dohkha whale u long shisha. Bad tang kawei ka rukom ha kaba phi lah ban ioh wat ia ka jingmut kaba lah ban shah ia ka jinglong ba im jong u, ka long da kaba leit wad dohkha hi; hynrei da kaba leh kumta, phi mad ia ka jingma kaba khraw ban shah pynjot bad shah pynngam junom ha u. Namarkata, ka long kumba nga sngew ba phi la bha ban ym long kiba sngewrit than ha ka jingkwah tip jong phi ban ktah ia une u Lebiathan.





LYNNONG 56. Na ki dur kiba kham duna ka jingbakla jong ki dohkha Whale, bad ki dur kiba shisha jong ki jingjia ha kaba ri dohkha Whale.

Ha kaba iadei bad ki dur ba shyrkhei jong ki dohkha whale, nga shah pynshoi jur hangne ​​ban rung sha kita ki jingiathuhkhana kiba kham shyrkhei shuh shuh shaphang jong ki kiba dei ban shem ha ki katto katne ki kot, kiba rim bad kiba mynta, khamtam eh ha u Pliny, u Purchas, u Hackluyt, u Harris, u Cuvier, bad kiwei kiwei.

Nga tip tang saw tylli ki jingthoh ba la pynmih shaphang u Sperm Whale uba khraw; u Colnett, u Huggins, u Frederick Cuvier bad u Beale. Ha ka lynnong kaba la dep la kdew shaphang u Colnett bad u Cuvier. Ka jong u Huggins ka kham bha shibun ban ia ka jong ki; hynrei, da ka jingeh kaba khraw, ka jong u Beale ka long kaba bha tam. Baroh ki jingdro jong u Beale ia une u dohkha ki long kiba bha, lait noh tang ia u dur ba pdeng ha ka dur jong ki lai tylli ki dohkha ha ki jinglong bapher bapher, kaba pynkut ia ka lynnong kaba ar jong u. Ka jingthoh ba shakhmat jong u, ki lieng kiba hiar thma ia ki Sperm Whale, wat la khlem artatien la khein ban pynkhih ia ka jingsngew artatien jong ki katto katne ki briew kiba shong ha ka parlor, hynrei ka long kaba dei kaba sngewtynnad bad kaba long kum ka jingim ha ka jingktah kaba kyllum jong ka. Katto katne na ki jingdro jong ki Sperm Whale ha u J. Ross Browne ki long kiba thikna bha ha ka rukom pyni dur; hynrei ki long kiba la thoh da kaba sngewsih. Kata kam dei ka jingbakla jong u watla.

Na ka Right Whale, ki dur outline kiba bha tam ki long ha Scoresby; hynrei la dro ia ki ha ka scale kaba rit palat ban pynpaw ia ka jingsngew kaba kwah. U don tang kawei ka dur jong ki jingjia ha kaba ri dohkha, bad kane ka long ka jingduna kaba sngewsih, namar dei tang da kum kine ki dur, haba la leh bha satia, ba phi lah ban ioh ia kaei kaei kum ka jingmut kaba shisha shaphang u dohkha whale uba im kumba la iohi da ki nongleit khwai dohkha jong u kiba im.

Hynrei, lada shim na ka bynta baroh, haduh katta katta kiba bha tam, wat la ha ki katto katne ki jingtip ba bniah kim long kiba thikna tam, ki jingpyni ia ki dohkha bad ki jingjia ha kaba ri dohkha ban shem ha kano kano ka jaka, ki long ar tylli ki jingdro ba heh jong ki nong France, ba la pyndep bha, bad ba la shim na ki jingdro da kawei ka Garnery. Ha kawei pa kawei, ki pyni ia ki jingshah thombor ha ki Sperm bad ki Sperm Whale. Ha ka jingdro kaba nyngkong la pyni ia u Sperm Whale uba donburom ha ka jingkhraw ba pura jong ka bor, uba dang shu kiew hapoh ka lieng na ki jingjylliew jong ka duriaw, bad uba kit sha jrong ha ka suinbneng halor ka tyrpeng jong u ia ka jingtwa kaba shyrkhei jong ki dieng lyngkhot lyngkhai. Ka tduh jong ka lieng ka long kaba khlem bthei shibynta, bad la tan ia ka tang da kaba pyniadei halor ka tyrpeng jong u mrad khlaw; bad da kaba ieng ha kata ka prow, na ka bynta kata kawei ka jingshai jong ka por kaba ym lah ban khein, phi iohi ia u nongniah lieng, uba la tap shiteng da ka shyntur kaba rkhiang kaba la bthei jong ka dohkha whale, bad ha ka jingleh ban kiew, kumba na ka thliew. Ka jingleh jong ka jinglong baroh kawei ka long kaba phylla kaba bha bad kaba shisha. Ka lain-tub kaba la thylli shiteng ka jngi halor ka duriaw kaba la lieh; ki dieng jong ki harpoon ba la bthei ki bob lyngkot ha ka; ki khlieh jong ki nongleit jngi ki ia saphriang sawdong ka dohkha whale ha ki jingpynpaw ba iapher jong ka jingtieng; katba ha ka jingjngai kaba dum bad ka eriong ka lieng ka dang kiew shapoh halor ka jaka. Lah ban shem ia ka jingbakla kaba jur ha ki jingtip ba bniah jong ka met jong une u dohkha whale, hynrei to ai ba kata kan iaid noh; namar, na ka bynta ka jingim jong nga, ngam lah ban dro ia kawei kaba bha kum kata.

Ha ka jingdro kaba ar, ka lieng ka don ha ka jingleh ban dro ryngkat bad ka tduh ba jyrngam jong u Right Whale uba heh uba iaid, uba pynkhih ia ka tduh jong u kaba jyrngam kaba lieh ha duriaw kum katto katne ki mawsiang kiba jyrngam na ki kliar lum jong ka Patagonia. Ki jet jong u ki long kiba ieng, kiba dap bad kiba lieh kum ka tdem; kumta na ka jingbun palat ka lyer ha ka thliew ding, phin pyrkhat ba dei ban don ka jingbam janmiet kaba shlur kaba la shet ha ki bniat kiba heh kiba don shapoh. Ki sim duriaw ki dang bthei ia ki dohkha rit, ki dohkha rit bad kiwei kiwei ki jingbam duriaw bad ki makkaroni, kiba u Right Whale u ju rah teng teng ha ka tyrpeng kaba pang jong u. Bad baroh shilynter kata ka por u leviathan uba don ki rmiang shyntur kiba jrong u dang mareh lyngba ka jingjylliew, u da iehnoh da ki ton ki snier lieh kiba jynjar ha ka jingkynjah jong u, bad u da pynlong ia ka lieng kaba rit ban khih ha ki thliew kum ka lieng kaba la shah kem hajan ki tyrpeng jong ka lieng duriaw. Kumta, ka phang pdeng ka long baroh ka jingkhihwin kaba jur; hynrei shadien, ha ka jingïapher ba itynnad jong ka art, ka don ka jinglong rnong jong ka duriaw kaba la pynjem, ki ryndang bym don star kiba la hap jong ka lieng kaba khlem bor, bad ka jingbun ba khlem treikam jong u dohkha ba la ïap, ka kut ba la jop, ryngkat bad ka lama jong ka jingkem kaba la pyndait lyngkot na u dieng dohkha ba la pynrung ha ka thliew um jong u.

Uei u Garnery u nongdro dur, ne u la long, ngam tip. Hynrei ka jingim jong nga na ka bynta kata u la long uba la nang bha ia ka sobjek jong u, lane lym kumta u la shah hikai phylla ha ki katto katne ki nongri dohkha kiba la tbit. Ki Phareng ki dei ki lad na ka bynta ka jinglehkai shna dur. Leit bad peit ia ki jingdro baroh jong ka Europe, bad hangno phin shem ia kum kane ka gallery jong ka jingkhihwin kaba im bad kaba ring mynsiem halor ka canvas, kum ha kata ka iing jong ka jingjop ha Versailles; ha kaba u nongpeit u ialeh la ka lynti, pell-mell, lyngba ki thma bah kiba bud ryntih jong ka ri France; ha kaba kawei pa kawei ka waitlam ka long kumba ka jingshai jong ki Jingshai Sepngi, bad ki syiem bad ki Syiem kiba don tiar ialeh kiba bud ryntih ki iaid lyngba, kum ka jingshah thombor jong ki centaur ba la pynkup pansngiat? Kim long kiba khlem bit satia ban ioh jaka ha kata ka gallery, kine ki jingshna thma duriaw jong ka Garnery.

Da kaba kdew sha ka jingjia jong ka jingleit ri dohkha kaba lyngkot ban batai, bad kumjuh ruh na ka bynta ban sngewthuh kham bha ia baroh ki jingjia kiba kum kita shawei pat kiba la pyni, nga don hangne ​​ban kren shaphang ka jinglong phylla jong ki dohkha whale kaba shyrkhei teng teng.
