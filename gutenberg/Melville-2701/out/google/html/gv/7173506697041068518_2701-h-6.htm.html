Son ny ta er n’yannoo scansh er y chooish, t’eh ry-akin dy vel eh s’diuagh, dy beagh iasgyn nagh vel erskyn ceithir ny cwoaie feet marshallagh ry-cheilley rish WHALES—fockle, ta, ayns y chiart-chraueeaght, dagh ooilley traa cur stiagh ymmyd jeh’n aght s’diuney. Agh ta ny cretooryn ta ry-hoi ry-hoi myr Duodecimoes, ny baljyn dy bragh, liorish my mharkey jeh cre ta baljyn— ie, iasg spouting, lesh eayst horizontal.

LIBAR III. ( Duodecimo ), CAPPYL 1. ( Porpoise Huzza ).—S'eh shoh y porpoise coontit ta ry-gheddyn chamoo er ooilley ny cruinney. Ta’n ennym jeh my ghraih hene; son ta ny smoo na un sorch porpoise, as shegin da red erbee ve jeant dy ymmyrkey ad. Ta mee gra rish myr shen, son t’eh dy bragh s’lesh s’lesh s’lesh, ta er y marrey leah geddyn stiagh ayns niau myr cappanyn ayns sluight y Cheyoo-Iuly. Ta'n appearance oc ry-akin lesh boggey ec y mariner. Lesh spioradyn fine, t'ad dy bragh cheet veih'n ghrian-chree gys y gheay. Ta’d ny laddyn dy bragh beaghey roish y gheay. T'ad er nyn goyrt myr omen fortunate. Mannagh vod oo hene streeu noi trimmid cheer tra t’ou uss cur-my-ner ny iasgyn beo shoh, eisht nee’n neear dty chooney; cha vel y spyrryd dy ghraih diuish ayndiu. Huzza Porpoise s'goaill rish, s'goaill rish, ver eh dhyt gallon mie dy oil mie. Agh ta’n fliaghey fine as delicate ta’n ghrogh-chleaysh er ny ghoaill ass e ghlioonyn, ard-vooaralagh. Ta'n ghrian ry-gheddyn mastey jewelry as watchmakers. Cur ny marrey eh er nyn honey. Ta feoh porpoise goaill rish, ta fys ayd. Fakin nagh row eh er jeet gys dty chree dy vel porpoise spout. Ta e spout cho beg as nagh vel eh feer glen. Agh y traa elley ta coonrey ayd, cur-my-ner eh; as bee oo er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen y Sperm whale mooar hene ayns miniature.

LIBAR III. ( Duoish ), CAPPYL II. ( Porpoise Algerinagh ).—Pirate. Feer savage. Ta'n 'Pacific' ry-gheddyn, ta mee credjal. Ta'n Huzza Porpoise ry-akin, agh t'eh ry-akin rish y 'general make'. Provoke eh, as bee eh buckley rish shark. Ta mee er n’immeeaght er e hon ymmodee keayrtyn, agh cha vel mee er vaik eh er ny ghlackey.

LIBAR III. ( Dwoaie ), CAPPYL III. ( Porpoise lesh beeal-bea ).—Yn seihll smoo jeh Porpoise; as cha nel eh ry-gheddyn agh ayns y Phacific, choud as ta fys ayn. Yn un ennym Saggyrt, lesh t'eh er ny enmyssey derrey shoh, she ennym ny iaskee—Right-Whale Porpoise, veih'n stayd dy vel eh er ny gheddyn ayns y chied cheayrt ayns thie y Folio shen. Ayns cummey, t'eh differit ayns red erbee veih'n Huzza Porpoise, er-yn-oyr dy vel eh jeh girth ny s'crooie as ny s'jolly; dy firrinagh, t'eh jeh figur glen as glen. Cha nel sniaghtey er e dhrogh-chree (ta'n chooid smoo jeh porpoiseyn elley), ta sniaghtey s'brinnagh echey, as sooillyn Indiagh sentimental jeh rang hazel. Agh ta e veeal-meal spoilt ooilley. C’raad as dy vel e dhrogh-chree ooilley sheese gys e shliawnyn-taarnee jeh sable dooghyssagh, agh t’eh ry-akin liorish line boundary, ry-akin myr y marke ayns cronk lhong, enmyssit “bright waist,” t’eh ry-akin ec y line shen veih stem gys stern, lesh daa lioar ry-akin, dooghyssagh erskyn as geal fo. Ta'n white cummal seose ayrn jeh e chree, as ooilley e bee, as shen t'eh jannoo eh myr dy beagh eh er n'eiyrt magh ass felonic visit gys meal-bag. Aspect ny s'mean as mealy! Ta'n ooil echey feer sheean rish ooil y porpoise coontit.

Ta'n DUODECIMO er-y-fa shen, cha nel y system shoh goll er, son y Porpoise y s'beggey jeh'n baljyn. Sushtal, ta ooilley ny Leviathanyn ry-akin. Agh ta rabble dy baljyn neu-chiaralagh, fugitive, lesh leshtal, ta, myr baljyn American, ta mee toiggal liorish cliaghtey, agh cha nee persoonagh. Ta mee er n'enmyssey ad liorish nyn enmyn-castell; son might y list shoh ve ard-vooaralagh da rannsagheeaghyn 'sy toshiaght, as might ad cloie ny ta aym 'sy cheer shoh agh t'ad er toshiaght. As my bee fer jeh ny baljyn shoh, er ny ghlackey as er ny markee er-dy-henney, eisht t'eh er ny choyrt stiagh 'sy System shoh, cordail rish e mhean Folio, Octavo, ny Duodecimo:—Yn Baljyn Bottle-Nose; yn Balley Junk; yn Balley-Chean Pudding; yn Balley Cape; yn Balley Lead; yn Balley Cannon; yn Balley Scragg; yn Balley Copper; yn Balley Elefant; yn Balley Iceberg; yn Balley Quog; yn Balley Glass; etc. Veih ard-whaiylaghyn Icelandic, Dutch, as seihll Saggyrt, might listyn elley jeh baljyn neu-chiaralagh ve currit magh, bendigit lesh ooilley ny h-enmyn uncouth. Agh ta mee cur ad ersooyl myr obsolete dy chooilley red; as cha vod eh agh ymmyd y yannoo jeh son soilshaghyn s’leaie, lyn Leviathanism, agh nagh vel ad soilshaghey nhee erbee.

Er-jerrey: V’eh er ny ghra ec y toshiaght, nagh bee’n system shoh ec y traa t’ayn, as ec y traa cheddin, perfectit. Cha vod shiu agh fakin dy soilshey dy vel mee er choadey my ghoo. Agh ta mee nish fakin my System cetological seose myr shen cha row eh er ny chlashtyn, myr v’eh er-mayrn ayns Cathedral mooar Cologne, as y crane er-mayrn foast er barr y toor nagh row er ny chlashtyn. Son might erections beggey ve cliaghtey liorish nyn chied architectyn; grand, glen, t'ad er-dy-henney y copestone y laghyn. Dy row Jee er my choadey veih jannoo red erbee. Ta’n lioar shoh ooilley ny s’cosoylagh—cha nel, agh ny s’cosoylagh. Oh, Am, Neart, Argid, as Paas!
