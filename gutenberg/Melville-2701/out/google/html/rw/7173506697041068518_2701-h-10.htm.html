Muri ubwo bwirabura bwose bwibintu, Ahabu, nubwo wibwiraga ko igihe cyose itegeko ryakomeje gukomezwa ryurugero rwumye kandi ruteye akaga, rwerekanye ububiko bwijimye; kandi ni gake cyane kuruta uko yabwiraga abo bashakanye. Mubihe byumuyaga nkibi, nyuma yibintu byose hejuru no hejuru bimaze kugerwaho, ntakindi gishobora gukorwa usibye guhita dutegereza ikibazo cya gale. Noneho Kapiteni nabakozi bahinduka abapitaliste bifatika. Noneho, ukuguru kwe kwinzovu kwinjijwe mu mwobo wari umenyereye, kandi ukuboko kumwe gufatiye ku mwenda umwe, Ahabu amasaha n'amasaha yahagararaga yitegereza yapfuye kugeza umuyaga, mu gihe rimwe na rimwe urubura cyangwa urubura byose byahurizaga hamwe. Hagati aho, abakozi basunikaga mu gice cy’imbere cy’ubwato n’inyanja yangiritse yamenetse hejuru y’umuheto, bahagarara ku murongo ugana ibihome mu rukenyerero; kandi ibyiza ni ukwirinda imiraba isimbuka, buri mugabo yari yinjiye mu muheto umeze neza kuri gari ya moshi, aho yazungurukaga nko mu mukandara urekuye. Amagambo make cyangwa ntavuzwe; n'ubwato bucecetse, nkaho bukoreshwa n'abasare bashushanyije mu gishashara, umunsi ku wundi watanyaguye ibisazi byihuse n'ibyishimo by'imiraba ya dayimoni. Mwijoro nijoro ubwumvikane buke bwikiremwamuntu mbere yuko induru yinyanja yiganje; baracecetse abagabo bazungurutse munda; Ahabu aracyafite ijambo Ahaguruka ahagarara hejuru. Ndetse iyo kamere yarambiwe isa nkaho isaba kuruhuka ntabwo yashakisha iyo myidagaduro muri hammock. UMUTWE WA 52. Albatros.

Amajyepfo-uburasirazuba uva Cape, hafi ya Crozetts ya kure, ahantu heza ho gutemberera Iburyo bwa Whalemen, ubwato bwari imbere, Goney (Albatross) mwizina. Igihe yegeraga buhoro buhoro, mvuye ku cyicaro cyanjye cyo hejuru ku mutwe-wa-mast, nabonye neza iryo yerekwa ku buryo budasanzwe kuri tiro yo mu burobyi bwo mu nyanja ya kure - umucuruzi wo mu nyanja, kandi kuva kera akaba adahari mu rugo.

Nkaho imiraba yari yuzuye, ubu bukorikori bwahumishijwe nka skeleti ya walrus yahagaze. Hirya no hino ku mpande zayo, iyi sura yagaragaye yakurikiranwe n'imirongo miremire y'ingese itukura, mu gihe ibiceri bye byose hamwe no gutitira kwe byari bimeze nk'amashami manini y'ibiti yazengurutswe n'ubukonje bwinshi. Gusa ubwato bwe bwo hasi bwashyizweho. Kubona ishyamba kwari ukubona ubwanwa bwe burebure bwo kureba kuri iyo mitwe itatu. Basaga nkaho bambaye uruhu rwinyamaswa, nuko barashwanyaguritse kandi bakubita imyenda yari imaze imyaka hafi ine itembera. Bahagaze mu byuma byometse ku musumari, baranyeganyega maze bazunguruka hejuru y'inyanja itagira ubwenge; kandi nubwo, ubwo ubwato bwatembaga buhoro buhoro munsi yinyuma yacu, twe abagabo batandatu mwikirere twegereye cyane kuburyo dushobora kuba twarasimbutse tuvuye mumitwe yimitsi yubwato bumwe tujya mubindi; nyamara, abo barobyi basa nubusambanyi, batwitegereza bitonze uko barengana, nta jambo na rimwe twigeze tubwira abo tureba, mu gihe urubura rwa kimwe cya kane rwumvikanaga hepfo.

"Ship ahoy! Wabonye Whale Yera?"

Ariko nka capitaine udasanzwe, yegamiye hejuru yinkuta za pallid, yari mugikorwa cyo gushyira impanda ye kumunwa, hari ukuntu yaguye mumaboko ye igana mu nyanja; n'umuyaga noneho uzamuka amain, yihatiye kwiyumvisha ntawe. Hagati aho ubwato bwe bwari bugikomeza intera hagati. Mu gihe mu buryo butandukanye bwo guceceka, abasare ba Pequod barimo berekana ko babonye iki kibazo kibi ku ncuro ya mbere havuzwe izina rya Whale White ku rindi bwato, Ahabu aceceka gato; byasaga nkaho yaba yaramanuye ubwato kugira ngo yinjire uwo atazi, iyo umuyaga uteye ubwoba utabuza. Ariko yifashishije umwanya we uhuha, yongera gufata impanda ye, kandi azi neza ko ubwato butazi bwari Nantucketer kandi urugo rwatinze, yahise asakuza cyane ati: “Ahoy ngaho! Iyi ni Pequod, iboshye isi yose! Babwire bandike amabaruwa yose azaza ku nyanja ya pasifika! Kandi iki gihe, niba ntari mu rugo, ubabwire - -”

Muri ako kanya, ibyuka byombi byambutse neza, hanyuma, ako kanya, ukurikije inzira zabo zidasanzwe, shoals y’amafi mato atagira icyo yangiza, ko muminsi mike mbere yuko yoga koga iruhande rwacu, ikagenda ikagenda isa nkaho ihinda umushyitsi, maze ikazunguruka imbere na aft kuruhande rwumunyamahanga. Nubwo mu rugendo rwe ruhoraho Ahabu agomba kuba yarigeze kubona ibintu bisa, nyamara, kubantu bose ba monomaniac, utuntu duto duto cyane dufite ibisobanuro.

“Koga kure yanjye, si byo?” yitotombera Ahabu, yitegereza mu mazi. Byasaga nkaho ari bike mumagambo, ariko ijwi ryerekanaga umubabaro mwinshi utagira kirengera kuruta umusaza wumusazi wigeze kubaho. Ariko ahindukirira umuyobozi, wari umaze gufata ubwato mu muyaga kugira ngo agabanye inzira ye, yatakambiye mu ijwi rye ry'intare ishaje, ati: “Uzamuke hejuru! Mumurinde ku isi!”

Uzenguruke isi! Hariho byinshi muri iryo jwi bitera ibyiyumvo byubwibone; ariko whereto ibyo byose bizenguruka bikora? Gusa binyuze mubyago bitabarika kugeza aho twatangiriye, aho abo twasize inyuma yumutekano, igihe cyose cyatubanjirije.

Iyaba iyi si yari ikibaya kitagira iherezo, kandi mugihe twerekeje iburasirazuba twashoboraga guhora tugera kure, kandi tukavumbura ahantu heza kandi hatangaje kuruta Cyclades cyangwa Ibirwa byumwami Salomo, noneho habaye amasezerano murugendo. Ariko mugukurikirana ayo mayobera ya kure turota, cyangwa mukubabazwa twirukana iyo dayimoni abadayimoni, mugihe runaka cyangwa ikindi, koga imbere yimitima yabantu yose; mugihe twirukanye nkuyu isi yose, baratuyobora muri mazasi yuburumbuke cyangwa hagati bakadusiga bazunguruka.
