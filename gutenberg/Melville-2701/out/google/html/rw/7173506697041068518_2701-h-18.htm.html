UMUTWE WA 89. Ifi yihuta-Ifi-Ifi.

Kwerekeza kuri waif na waif-pole mu gice giheruka ariko kimwe, bisaba inkuru zimwe zerekeye amategeko n'amabwiriza y’uburobyi bwa baleine, aho waif ishobora gufatwa nkikimenyetso gikomeye na badge.

Bikunze kubaho ko mugihe amato menshi arimo kugendana, balale irashobora gukubitwa nubwato bumwe, hanyuma igahunga, amaherezo ikicwa igafatwa nubundi bwato; kandi hano harimo mu buryo butaziguye ibintu byinshi bito bito, byose bisangira iyi ngingo imwe ikomeye. Kurugero, -Nyuma yo kwirukana ananiwe kandi iteje akaga no gufata igifi kinini, umubiri urashobora kuva mu bwato kubera umuyaga ukaze; no gutembera kure kugera ku gisonga, usubirwemo na whale wa kabiri, we, mu ituze, yikubita hasi iruhande, nta kaga k'ubuzima cyangwa umurongo. Rero, amakimbirane akomeye kandi yubukazi yakunze kuvuka hagati yabarobyi, iyo hatabaho amategeko yanditse cyangwa atanditswe, isi yose, ntavuguruzanya akoreshwa mubibazo byose.

Ahari kodegisi yonyine yemewe yemerewe no gushyiraho amategeko, ni iy'Ubuholandi. Ryemejwe na Leta nkuru muri AD 1695. Ariko nubwo nta kindi gihugu cyigeze kigira itegeko ryandika ry’inyamanswa, nyamara abarobyi b'Abanyamerika babaye abashingamategeko n’abavoka muri iki kibazo. Batanze uburyo bwo kugereranya ibintu byose birenze Pandect ya Justinian hamwe n’amategeko ngengamikorere ya Sosiyete y'Abashinwa yo guhashya kwivanga hamwe n’ubucuruzi bw’abandi. Yego; aya mategeko arashobora kwandikwa kuri farting yumwamikazi Anne, cyangwa akabari ka inanga, kandi akambarwa mu ijosi, ni nto cyane.

I. Ifi-Ifi ni iy'ishyaka byihuse.

II. Ifi-Ifi ni umukino mwiza kubantu bose bashobora kuyifata vuba.

Ariko ikinisha ibibi hamwe niyi code yubuhanga nubunini bushimishije bwayo, bisaba ubwinshi bwibitekerezo kugirango tubisobanure.

Icya mbere: Ifi yihuta ni iki? Ifi nzima cyangwa yapfuye ifi yihuta muburyo bwa tekiniki, iyo ihujwe nubwato cyangwa ubwato bwigaruriwe, nuburyo ubwo aribwo bwose bushobora kugenzurwa nuwabituye cyangwa abayirimo, -imashini, inkono, umugozi wa santimetero icyenda, insinga ya telegraph, cyangwa umugozi wa cobweb, byose ni bimwe. Mu buryo nk'ubwo, ifi yihuta mubuhanga iyo ifite waif, cyangwa ikindi kimenyetso cyemewe cyo gutunga; igihe cyose ishyaka ryayirengagije byerekana neza ubushobozi bwabo igihe icyo aricyo cyose cyo kujyana, kimwe nubushake bwabo bwo kubikora.

Ibi nibisobanuro bya siyansi; ariko ibisobanuro bya baleine ubwabo rimwe na rimwe bigizwe namagambo akomeye no gukomanga cyane - Coke-kuri-Littleton yikiganza. Nibyo, mu ndamunite zigororotse kandi ziyubashye zitangwa buri gihe ku manza zidasanzwe, aho byaba ari akarengane gakabije kumuco umwe avuga ko afite igifi kinini cyirukanwe cyangwa cyiciwe nundi muburanyi. Ariko abandi ntabwo arikose.

Hashize imyaka igera kuri mirongo itanu habaye ikibazo cy’amatsiko ya bale-trover yaburanishijwe mu Bwongereza, aho abarega bagaragaje ko nyuma yo kwirukana bikomeye igifi kinini mu nyanja y’Amajyaruguru; kandi mugihe rwose (abarega) bari barashoboye kuvuza amafi; amaherezo, bari mu kaga k'ubuzima bwabo, bategekwa kureka imirongo yabo gusa, ahubwo ubwato bwabo ubwabwo. Amaherezo, abaregwa (abakozi bo mu bundi bwato) bazanye balale, bakubita, barica, barafata, amaherezo barayigabiza imbere y’abarega. Igihe kandi abo baregwa bongeye kugarukwaho, kapiteni wabo yinjije intoki mu menyo y’abarega, abizeza ko binyuze mu magambo ya doxologiya ku gikorwa yakoze, noneho azagumana umurongo wabo, inanga, n'ubwato, bwari bwarakomeje kwizirika ku nyanja igihe cyafatwaga. Kubera iyo mpamvu, abarega noneho bareze kugaruza agaciro ka baleine yabo, umurongo, inanga, n'ubwato.

Bwana Erskine yari umwunganira abaregwa; Lord Ellenborough yari umucamanza. Erskine yari hakurya; hanyuma arabishyigikira avuga, ko nubwo nyakubahwa yabanje kuvuza inanga umudamu, kandi akaba yarigeze kwiyiriza ubusa, kandi kubera gusa impungenge zikomeye z’ubugome bwe bukabije, amaherezo yaramutaye; nyamara kumutererana yarabikoze, ku buryo yahindutse ifi irekuye; nuko rero mugihe nyakubahwa wakurikiyeho yongeye kumucuranga, umudamu yahise aba umutungo wa nyakubahwa wakurikiyeho, hamwe ninanga zose zishobora kuba zaramubonye.

"De balena vero irahagije, si rex habeat caput, et regina caudam." Bracton, l. 3, c. 3.

Ikilatini kiva mu bitabo by'Amategeko y'Ubwongereza, cyajyanye n'ibivugwamo, bisobanura ko mu nyanja zose zafashwe n'umuntu uwo ari we wese ku nkombe z'icyo gihugu, Umwami, nk'icyubahiro gikomeye Harpooneer, agomba kuba afite umutwe, kandi Umwamikazi ashyikirizwa icyubahiro umurizo. Igabana, muri baleine, ni nko kugabanya pome; ntagisigaye kiri hagati. Noneho nkuko iri tegeko, muburyo bwahinduwe, kugeza nubu rikurikizwa mubwongereza; kandi nkuko itanga muburyo butandukanye anomaly idasanzwe ikora kumategeko rusange yihuta na Loose-Fish, hano ivurwa mugice cyihariye, kumahame amwe yubupfura atuma gari ya moshi yicyongereza itwara imodoka yihariye, yagenewe cyane cyane icumbi ryubwami. Ubwa mbere, mubimenyetso byamatsiko byerekana ko amategeko yavuzwe haruguru agikurikizwa, ndakomeza kubashyira imbere yawe ibintu byabaye mumyaka ibiri ishize.

Kuberako Lord Warden akoreshwa cyane mugihe kimwe cyo guhindagura perquisite; aribwo ahanini abikesheje iyo fobbing yabo.

Byongeye kandi, nkaho abona amaherezo ko aramutse akwiye guha Pip inama zidahwitse umutimanama, yaba amusize intera ndende kuburyo atasimbuka ejo hazaza; Stubb yahise areka inama zose, maze asoza ategeka ati: "Komera ku bwato, Pip, cyangwa na Nyagasani, ntabwo nzagutwara uramutse usimbutse; uzirikane ko. Ntidushobora kwihanganira gutakaza balale nkawe; balale yagurisha inshuro mirongo itatu ibyo wifuza, Pip, muri Alabama. Uzirikane ibyo, kandi ntuzongere gusimbuka ukundi." Ahari ahari Stubb yabigaragaje mu buryo butaziguye, ko nubwo umuntu yakundaga mugenzi we, nyamara umuntu ni inyamanswa yinjiza amafaranga, ibyifuzo bikunze kubangamira ineza ye.

Ariko twese turi mumaboko yimana; na Pip irongera irasimbuka. Byari mubihe bisa cyane nibikorwa byambere; ariko noneho ntiyigeze asohora umurongo; nuko rero, igihe balale yatangiraga kwiruka, Pip yasigaye inyuma yinyanja, nkigiti cyurugendo rwihuta. Yoo! Stubb yari ukuri ariko cyane kubwijambo rye. Wari umunsi mwiza, wuzuye, ubururu; inyanja izunguruka ituje kandi ikonje, kandi irambuye neza, impande zose, kugera kuri horizon, nkuruhu rwa beater-zahabu yakubiswe kugeza ku mpera. Kwikubita hasi no muri iyo nyanja, Pip's ebon umutwe yerekanaga nkumutwe wibiti. Nta cyuma-cyuma cyazamuye igihe yagwaga cyane. Umugongo udasobanutse wa Stubb wamuhinduye; na baleine yari ifite amababa. Mu minota itatu, ibirometero byose by'inyanja idafite inkombe byari hagati ya Pip na Stubb. Kuva mu nyanja rwagati, Pip wumukene yahinduye umushyitsi, uhindagurika, umutwe wumukara ku zuba, undi mwirondoro wigunze, nubwo uri hejuru cyane kandi mwinshi.

Noneho, mubihe bituje, koga mumyanyanja yuguruye byoroshye kuboga koga nko kugendera mumasoko-yimodoka. Ariko ubwigunge buteye ubwoba ntibwihanganirwa. Kwishyira hamwe kwinshi hagati yubunini butagira umutima, Mana yanjye! ni nde ushobora kubivuga? Shyira akamenyetso, burya iyo abasare biyuhagira batuje mu nyanja yuguruye - berekana uburyo bahobera ubwato bwabo kandi bakambuka impande zose.

Ariko koko Stubb yaratereranye umukene muto negro kumateka ye? Oya; ntabwo yashakaga kuvuga byibuze. Kubera ko yari akurikiranye amato abiri, kandi yatekerezaga, nta gushidikanya, ko byanze bikunze baza kuri Pip vuba cyane, bakamutwara; nubwo, mubyukuri, ibitekerezo nkibi kubatoza byabangamiwe no gutinyuka kwabo, ntabwo buri gihe bigaragazwa nabahiga mubihe byose bisa; kandi ingero nkizo ntizikunze kubaho; hafi buri gihe muburobyi, ikigwari, icyo bita, cyaranzwe no kwangwa urunuka rubi rwihariye rwabasirikare ningabo.

Ariko byarabaye, kuburyo ayo mato, atabonye Pip, ahita atata balale hafi yabo kuruhande rumwe, arahindukira, yirukana; kandi ubwato bwa Stubb bwari kure cyane, kandi we hamwe nabakozi be bose bagambiriye ifi ye, kuburyo Pip yatambitse itangira kwaguka kumukikije. Ku bw'amahirwe make ubwato ubwabwo amaherezo bwaramutabaye; ariko guhera kuri iyo saha negro ntoya yazengurutse igorofa umuswa; nkabo, byibuze, bavuze ko ari. Inyanja yari ifite urwenya umubiri we utagira ingano, ariko urohama ubugingo bwe butagira akagero. Ntabwo yarohamye rwose, nubwo. Ahubwo yajyanwe ari muzima kugeza ubujyakuzimu butangaje, aho imiterere idasanzwe yisi yisi yambere idakinze yerekeje hirya no hino mumaso ye atuje; na miser-merman, Ubwenge, yahishuye ibirundo bye bibitse; kandi mubyishimo, bidafite umutima, burigihe-bwana-bwana, Pip yabonye udukoko twinshi, Imana-ibaho hose, udukoko twa korali, ko mumazi yamazi yazamuye orbose nini. Yabonye ikirenge cy'Imana kuri podiyumu, aravuga; nuko rero abo basangiye ubwato bamwitaga umusazi. Ubusazi bwumuntu rero ni imyumvire yijuru; no kuzerera kubera impamvu zose zipfa, umuntu araza amaherezo kuri icyo gitekerezo cyo mwijuru, kubwimpamvu, kidasobanutse kandi giteye ubwoba; no gukiza cyangwa ishyano, yumva noneho atabangamiwe, atitaye ku Mana ye.

Kubandi, ntugashinje Stubb cyane. Ikintu kirasanzwe muri ubwo burobyi; no murukurikirane rwibisobanuro, noneho bizagaragara uko gutererana byambayeho.
