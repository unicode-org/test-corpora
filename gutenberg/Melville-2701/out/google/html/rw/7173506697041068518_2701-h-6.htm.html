IGITABO I. ( Folio ), UMUTWE WA I. ( Ifi ya Sperm ). Nta gushidikanya, ni we muturage munini ku isi; cyane cyane muri baleine zose guhura; icyubahiro gikomeye cyane mubice; hanyuma, hanyuma, kugeza ubu agaciro gakomeye mubucuruzi; niwe kiremwa cyonyine gikuramo icyo kintu cyagaciro, spermaceti. Umwihariko we wose, ahandi henshi, uzagurwa. Ahanini nizina rye ubu ngomba gukora. Urebye kuri filozofiya, ntibisobanutse. Mu binyejana byashize, igihe igifi cyintanga nticyamenyekanye rwose muburyo bwe bwite, kandi mugihe amavuta ye yabonetse kubwimpanuka gusa mumafi yahagaze; muri iyo minsi spermaceti, bisa nkaho, abantu benshi bakekaga ko yakomotse ku kiremwa gisa nicyo cyahoze kizwi mu Bwongereza nka Greenland cyangwa Whale iburyo. Byari igitekerezo kandi, ko iyi spermaceti imwe yari iyo gusetsa byihuse bya Greenland Whale inyuguti ya mbere yijambo isobanura neza. Muri ibyo bihe, nanone, spermaceti yari mike cyane, ntabwo yakoreshwaga kumucyo, ahubwo nk'amavuta n'imiti. Byagombaga kuba gusa kubanywa ibiyobyabwenge nkuko muri iki gihe ugura isima imwe ya rhubarb. Iyo, nkuko mbitekereza, mugihe cyigihe, imiterere nyayo ya spermaceti yamenyekanye, izina ryayo ryambere ryagumishijwe nabacuruzi; ntagushidikanya kuzamura agaciro kayo mubitekerezo bidasanzwe bifite akamaro gake. Kandi rero ubujurire bugomba amaherezo bwaje gutangwa kuri baleine iyi spermaceti yakomotsemo rwose.

Bamwe bitwaza kubona itandukaniro riri hagati ya baleine ya Greenland yicyongereza nigifi cyiburyo cyabanyamerika. Ariko baremeranya neza mubintu byabo byose bikomeye; eka mbere ntiharatangwa ikintu na kimwe cyemeza gushingiraho itandukaniro rikomeye. Nibice bitagira ingano bishingiye kubitandukaniro bidasobanutse, niho amashami amwe yamateka karemano aba akomeye cyane. Ikintu gikomeye cyo gutandukanya ibintu, fin, aho akomora izina rye, akenshi ni ikintu kigaragara. Iyi fin ifite uburebure bwa metero eshatu cyangwa enye, ikura ihagaritse kuva igice cyinyuma cyinyuma, cyimiterere, kandi gifite impera ityaye cyane. Nubwo bitaba ikindi gice gito cyikiremwa kigaragara, iyi fin yitaruye, rimwe na rimwe, izagaragara igaragara hejuru. Iyo inyanja ituje mu buryo bushyize mu gaciro, kandi ikagaragazwa gato n’umuvuduko ukabije, kandi iyi fin isa na gnomon irahaguruka igatera igicucu hejuru y’iminkanyari, birashoboka ko umuntu yakeka ko umuzenguruko w’amazi uzengurutse umeze nkumurongo, hamwe nuburyo bwacyo hamwe numurongo wamasaha wanditseho. Kuri iyo Ahaz-kanda igicucu akenshi gisubira inyuma. Impera-Inyuma ntabwo ari gregari. Asa nuwanga balale, kuko abagabo bamwe banga abantu. Isoni cyane; burigihe ujya wenyine; mu buryo butunguranye kuzamuka hejuru mumazi ya kure kandi yijimye; indege ye igororotse kandi imwe ihanamye izamuka nk'icumu rirerire ridahwitse ku kibaya kitagira ubutayu; impano n'imbaraga zidasanzwe n'umuvuduko wo koga, nko kwanga ibintu byose biriho kuva kumuntu; uyu leviathan asa nkuwirukanye kandi udatsindwa Kayini wubwoko bwe, yikoreye ikimenyetso cye ubwo buryo kumugongo. Kuva afite umunwa mu kanwa, Fin-Back rimwe na rimwe ishyirwa hamwe na baleine iburyo, mu bwoko bwa teoretiki bwitwa baleine ya Whalebone , ni ukuvuga balale hamwe na baleen. Muri ibyo bita Whalebone whale, hasa nkaho hari ubwoko butandukanye, ibyinshi ariko, ntibizwi. Ifi nini nini nini nini nini; pike ifite imitwe; inyanja nini; munsi yinyanja nini nini nini, ni amazina yabarobyi kubwoko butandukanye.

Kubijyanye nubu bujurire bwa "Whalebone whalebone," ni ngombwa cyane kuvuga, ko nubwo izina nk'iryo rishobora kuba ryoroshye mu koroshya kwerekeza ku bwoko bumwe na bumwe bw'inyamanswa, nyamara ni iby'ubusa kugerageza gutondekanya neza Leviathan, ishingiye ku gihu cye, cyangwa ikibuno, cyangwa fin, cyangwa amenyo; tutibagiwe ko ibyo bice byerekanwe cyangwa ibiranga bigaragara ko bisa nkaho byahujwe no kugura ishingiro rya sisitemu isanzwe ya Cetologiya kuruta ubundi buryo butandukanye bwo gutandukanya umubiri, iyo baleine, mubwoko bwayo, itanga. None bite? Baleen, hump, back-fin, namenyo; ibi nibintu bifite umwihariko ukwirakwizwa muburyo butandukanye bwinyamanswa, utitaye kubishobora kuba imiterere yimiterere yabyo mubindi kandi byingenzi. Rero, baleine yintanga na baleine ya baleine, buriwese afite igituba; ariko ngaho similitude irahagarara. Noneho, iyi baleine imwe nini hamwe na baleine ya Greenland, buri kimwe muri ibyo gifite baleen; ariko ngaho nanone similitude irahagarara. Kandi ni kimwe gusa nibindi bice byavuzwe haruguru. Mu bwoko butandukanye bwa baleine, bakora ibintu nkibi bidasanzwe; cyangwa, mugihe hari umwe muribo witandukanije, ubwo bwigunge budasanzwe; nkugusuzugura rwose uburyo rusange bwashizweho kubwibyo. Kuri uru rutare buri wese mubantu ba baleine-naturiste baracitsemo ibice.

Ariko birashoboka ko dushobora gutekereza ko, mubice byimbere ya baleine, muri anatomiya ye - byibuze, tuzashobora gukubita urwego rukwiye. Oya; ni ikihe kintu, nk'urugero, muri anatomiya ya baleine ya Greenland itangaje kurusha baleen we? Nyamara twabonye ko na baleen ye bidashoboka neza gutondekanya igifi cya Greenland. Niba kandi umanutse munda ya leviathans zitandukanye, kuki ngaho utazabona itandukaniro igice cya mirongo itanu nkuko biboneka kuri sisitemu nkuko izo hanze zimaze kubarwa. Ni iki gisigaye? ntakindi uretse gufata balale kumubiri, mubunini bwabo bwubuntu, no kubitinyuka kubitondekanya gutya. Kandi ubu ni gahunda ya Bibliografiya hano yemejwe; kandi niyo yonyine ishobora gutsinda, kuko yonyine irashoboka. Gukomeza.

IGITABO I. ( Folio ) UMUTWE WA IV. ( Hump Back ). - Iyi bale ikunze kugaragara ku nkombe za Amerika y'Amajyaruguru. Yafatiwe kenshi aho ngaho, akururwa ku cyambu. Afite ipaki nini kuri we nk'umucuruzi; cyangwa urashobora kumwita Inzovu na Castle. Ibyo ari byo byose, izina rizwi kuri we ntirimutandukanya bihagije, kubera ko igifi cy'intanga nacyo gifite igituba nubwo ari gito. Amavuta ye ntabwo afite agaciro gakomeye. Afite baleen. Niwe ukina cyane kandi ufite umutima woroshye muri baleine yose, bigatuma abaryamana bahuje ibitsina n'amazi yera muri rusange kurusha ayandi yose.

IGITABO I. ( Folio ), UMUTWE WA V. ( Razor Inyuma ). - Muri iyi baleine izwi cyane ariko izina rye. Namubonye kure ya Cape Horn. Muri kamere yizabukuru, yanga abahiga nabafilozofe. Nubwo nta bugwari, ntabwo yigeze agaragaza igice cye ariko umugongo we, uzamuka mumisozi miremire ityaye. Mureke agende. Nzi byinshi kuri we, nta n'undi muntu ubizi.

IGITABO I. ( Folio ), UMUTWE WA VI. . Ntaboneka gake; byibuze sinigeze mubona usibye muri remoter yinyanja yepfo, hanyuma burigihe burigihe kinini cyane kuburyo ntashobora kwiga mumaso ye. Ntiyigera yirukanwa; yakwiruka afite imigozi-igenda kumurongo. Prodigies bamubwira ibye. Adieu, Hasi ya Sufuru! Nta kindi nshobora kuvuga cyukuri kuri wewe, cyangwa Nantucketer wa kera.

( Octavo ), UMUTWE WA III. .​ Ikiremwa gifite uburebure bwa metero cumi n'esheshatu, mugihe ihembe ryacyo rigereranya metero eshanu, nubwo zimwe zirenga icumi, ndetse zikagera kuri metero cumi n'eshanu. Mu magambo make, irihembe ni umutsi muremure, ukura kuva mu rwasaya kumurongo wihebye gato uva kuri horizontal. Ariko iboneka gusa kuruhande rubi, rufite ingaruka mbi, igaha nyirayo ikintu gisa nicyerekezo cyumugabo wibumoso. Niyihe ntego nyayo aya mahembe yinzovu cyangwa lance asubiza, biragoye kubivuga. Ntabwo bisa nkaho bikoreshwa nkicyuma-amafi yinkota-amafi; nubwo abasare bamwe bambwira ko Narwhale ayikoresha kugirango ahindure epfo yinyanja kugirango arye. Charley Coffin yavuze ko yakoreshejwe mu gutobora urubura; kuri Narwhale, izamuka hejuru yinyanja ya Polar, ugasanga yuzuyeho urubura, itera ihembe, nuko iracamo. Ariko ntushobora kwerekana kimwe muribi bitekerezo. Igitekerezo cyanjye bwite ni uko, icyakora irihembe ryuruhande rumwe rishobora rwose gukoreshwa na Narwhale - nubwo ibyo aribyo byose - rwose byamworohera cyane kububiko bwo gusoma udutabo. Narwhale numvise yitwa Tusked whale, balale yamahembe, na baleine Unicorn. Nukuri rwose ni urugero rwamatsiko ya Unicornism iboneka mubwami hafi ya kamere yimiterere. Kuva mubanditsi bamwe bashaje banditse nakusanyije ko ihembe rimwe ryinyanja-unicorn ryahozeho kera ryafatwaga nkumuti ukomeye wo kurwanya uburozi, kandi nkukwo, imyiteguro yacyo yazanye ibiciro byinshi. Yashizwemo kandi umunyu uhindagurika kubadamu bacitse intege, nkuko amahembe yimpara yabagabo akorerwa muri hartshorn. Ubanza byari ubwabyo byari ikintu cyamatsiko menshi. Ibaruwa yirabura ambwira ko Sir Martin Frobisher agarutse avuye muri urwo rugendo, igihe Umwamikazi Bess yamuzungurukaga ukuboko kwe kw'amabuye avuye mu idirishya ry'ingoro ya Greenwich, ubwo ubwato bwe butinyutse bwanyuraga muri Thames; Black Letter yagize ati: “Igihe Sir Martin yagarukaga avuye muri urwo rugendo, yapfukamye yerekanaga icyubahiro cye ihembe rirerire rya Narwhale, mu gihe kirekire nyuma yo kumanikwa mu gihome i Windsor.” Umwanditsi wo muri Irilande yanga ko Earl ya Leicester, apfukamye, na we yerekanaga ubukuru bwe irindihembe, ryerekeye inyamaswa yo mu butaka ifite kamere imwe.

Narwhale ifite isura nziza cyane, isa n'ingwe, ifite ibara ryera-ryera ryubutaka, ryerekanweho ibibara byirabura kandi birebire byirabura. Amavuta ye ararenze cyane, asobanutse kandi neza; ariko hari bike muri byo, kandi ni gake ahigwa. Aboneka cyane mu nyanja ya circumpolar.

DUODECIMOES.-Ibi birimo baleine ntoya. I. Ikiraro cya Huzza. II. Ikibaya cya Alijeriya. III. Umunwa wuzuye umunwa.

Kubatagize amahirwe yihariye yo kwiga kuriyi ngingo, birashoboka ko bisa nkibitangaje, ko amafi adakunze kurenga metero enye cyangwa eshanu agomba guhuzwa na WHALES - ijambo, muburyo rusange, rihora ritanga igitekerezo cyo guhobera. Ariko ibiremwa byashyizwe hejuru nka Duodecimoes ni balale idashidikanywaho, nkurikije ibisobanuro byanjye byerekana icyo baleine aricyo - ni ifi yizunguruka, ifite umurizo utambitse.

IGITABO III. ( Duodecimo ), UMUTWE WA 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ). - Iyi ni pisine isanzwe iboneka hafi yisi yose. Izina niryo ryanjye bwite; kuberako hari ubwoko burenze bumwe bwa porpoises, kandi hagomba gukorwa ikintu cyo kubatandukanya. Ndamwita gutya, kuko ahora yoga muri shoals isekeje, hejuru yinyanja yagutse ikomeza kwijugunya mwijuru nkumutwe mumutwe wa kane-Nyakanga. Isura yabo muri rusange irashimwa cyane na marine. Yuzuye imyuka myiza, ihora ituruka kumuyaga uhuha ugana umuyaga. Nibo basore bahora babaho mbere yumuyaga. Babarirwa ibimenyetso byamahirwe. Niba wowe ubwawe ushobora kwihanganira impundu eshatu kubona aya mafi afite imbaraga, noneho ijuru riragufasha; umwuka wumukino wubaha Imana ntabwo uri muri mwe. Kugaburirwa neza, kuvoma Huzza Porpoise bizaguha litiro nziza yamavuta meza. Ariko amazi meza kandi meza yakuwe mu rwasaya afite agaciro gakomeye. Irasabwa mubutunzi nabakora amasaha. Abasare babishyize kuri hone yabo. Inyama za porpoise nibyiza kurya, urabizi. Ntabwo bishobora kuba byarigeze bikubaho ko poroise spout. Mubyukuri, spout ye ni nto cyane kuburyo itagaragara neza. Ariko ubutaha ufite amahirwe, umwitegereze; hanyuma uzabona Sperm nini nini cyane muri miniature.

IGITABO III. ( Duodecimo ), UMUTWE WA II. ( Poriporo ya Alijeriya ) .— Pirate. Ubunyamaswa cyane. Aboneka gusa, ngira ngo, muri pasifika. Afite ubunini burenze Huzza Porpoise, ariko ibyinshi muri rusange bikora. Mumurakaze, kandi azagundira inyanja. Namumanuye inshuro nyinshi, ariko sinigeze mbona ko yafashwe.

IGITABO III. ( Duodecimo ), UMUTWE WA III. ( Mealy-umunwa wa Porpoise ). kandi iboneka gusa muri pasifika, kugeza ubu bizwi. Izina ryonyine ry'icyongereza, kugeza ubu ryamenyekanye kugeza ubu, ni iry'abarobyi-Iburyo-Whale Porpoise, uhereye ku bihe usanga ahanini aboneka hafi ya Folio. Mu miterere, aratandukanye kurwego runaka na Huzza Porpoise, kuba afite rotund nkeya na jolly girth; mubyukuri, afite ishusho nziza kandi yitonda. Ntafite amababa ku mugongo (andi makariso menshi afite), afite umurizo mwiza, n'amaso yu Buhinde amaso ya hazel hue. Ariko umunwa we wuzuye umunwa wangiza byose. Nubwo umugongo we wose wamanutse kugeza ku rubavu rwe rw'uruhande rufite uruzitiro rwimbitse, nyamara umurongo uhana imbibi, utandukanye nk'ikimenyetso kiri mu bwato bw'ubwato, bwiswe “ikibuno cyaka cyane,” uwo murongo uramuvana ku giti kugera ku gice, gifite amabara abiri atandukanye, umukara hejuru n'umweru munsi. Umweru ugizwe igice cyumutwe we, numunwa wose, bigatuma asa nkaho yahunze uruzinduko rukabije rwo gusura igikapu. Ikintu kibi cyane kandi cyuzuye! Amavuta ye ameze nkay'ibisanzwe bisanzwe.

Kurenga DUODECIMO, iyi sisitemu ntabwo ikomeza, kubera ko Porpoise ari ntoya muri baleine. Hejuru, ufite Abalewiyani bose b'inyandiko. Ariko hariho urujya n'uruza rw'inyanja idashidikanywaho, ihunze, igice-cyiza cyane, ibyo, nkumunyamerika wumunyamerika, ndabizi kubwizina, ariko sibyo kugiti cyanjye. Nzabarura nkoresheje ubujurire bwabo bwimbere; kuberako birashoboka urutonde nkurwo rushobora kuba ingirakamaro kubashakashatsi bazaza, bashobora kuzuza ibyo mfite hano ariko byatangiye. Niba hari imwe muri baleine ikurikira, izafatwa hanyuma igashyirwaho ikimenyetso, noneho ashobora guhita yinjizwa muri iyi Sisitemu, ukurikije ubunini bwe bwa Folio, Octavo, cyangwa Duodecimo: -Icupa-Amazuru; inyanja ya Junk; Ifi-Umutwe; inyanja ya Whale; Ifi Yambere; Ikibaya cya Cannon; Igicucu cya Scragg; Ifi Yumuringa; Ifi y'inzovu; Ifi ya iceberg; inyanja ya Quog; Ifi y'Ubururu; n'ibindi Kuva mubuyobozi bwa Isilande, Ubuholandi, hamwe nabayobozi ba kera b'Abongereza, hashobora kuvugwa izindi ntonde za baleine itazwi, ihezagiwe n'amazina y'ubwoko bwose. Ariko ndabasize nkibishaje rwose; kandi ntishobora gufasha kubakeka kumajwi gusa, yuzuye Leviathanism, ariko ntacyo bivuze.

Hanyuma, byavuzwe mugitangira, ko iyi sisitemu itari kuba hano, kandi icyarimwe, itunganijwe. Ntushobora kubona neza ko nakomeje ijambo ryanjye. Ariko ubu nsize Sisitemu yanjye ya cetologiya ihagaze kuburyo itarangiye, nubwo Cathedrale nini ya Cologne yari isigaye, hamwe na kane iracyahagaze hejuru yumunara utaruzura. Kubyubaka bito birashobora kurangizwa nabubatsi babo ba mbere; abuzukuru, abukuri, burigihe usige copestone kubyara. Imana ikundinde kuzuza ikintu icyo ari cyo cyose. Iki gitabo cyose ni umushinga-oya, ariko umushinga wumushinga. Yoo, Igihe, Imbaraga, Amafaranga, no Kwihangana!
