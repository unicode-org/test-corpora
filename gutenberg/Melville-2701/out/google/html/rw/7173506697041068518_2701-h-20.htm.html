UMUTWE WA 101.

Ere ubwato bwabongereza bugenda bugabanuka, bwaba bwashyizwe hano, ko yakomotse i Londres, kandi yitiriwe nyakwigendera Samuel Enderby, umucuruzi wuwo mujyi, umwimerere winzu izwi cyane ya whale ya Enderby & Sons; inzu mubitekerezo byanjye byumukene whaleman, itaza inyuma yinzu yumwami ihuriweho na Tudors na Bourbons, mubyifuzo byamateka nyayo. Mugihe kingana iki, mbere yumwaka wUmwami wacu 1775, iyi nzu nini ya whale yariho, inyandiko zanjye nyinshi zamafi ntisobanura neza; ariko muri uwo mwaka (1775) yashyizeho amato yambere yicyongereza yigeze guhiga buri gihe Intanga ngabo; nubwo kumanota yimyaka yashize (kuva 1726) Intwari zacu zintwari na Maceys za Nantucket na Vineyard bari mumato manini bakurikiranye iyo Leviathan, ariko muri Atlantike y'Amajyaruguru n'Amajyepfo gusa: ntahandi. Byaba byanditswe hano, ko Nantucketers babaye aba mbere mubantu bavuza inanga hamwe nicyuma cyimico nini ya Sperm Whale; kandi ko mu kinyejana cyakabiri aribo bantu bonyine kwisi yose bamucuranga.

Mu 1778, ubwato bwiza, Amelia, bwashyizweho kugira ngo bugaragaze neza, kandi bwashinzwe na Enderbys ikomeye, buzengurutse Cape Horn ashize amanga, kandi ni bwo bwa mbere mu bihugu byamanuye ubwato bwa baleine y'ubwoko bwose mu nyanja nini y'Amajyepfo. Urwo rugendo rwari umuhanga kandi ufite amahirwe; hanyuma agaruka ku cyambu cye afite intanga zuzuye, intangarugero ya Amelia yahise ikurikirwa n’andi mato, Icyongereza n’Abanyamerika, bityo ikibanza kinini cya Sperm Whale yo muri pasifika kirajugunywa. Ariko ntibanyuzwe n'iki gikorwa cyiza, inzu itarambirwa yongeye kwitanga: Samuel n'abahungu be bose - ni bangahe, nyina wabo arabizi gusa - kandi babifashijwemo na bo, kandi igice kimwe, ndatekereza ko, ku bw'amafaranga yabo, guverinoma y'Ubwongereza yashishikarijwe kohereza Rattler mu ntambara yo mu nyanja mu majyepfo. Iyobowe na Post-Kapiteni wo mu mazi, Rattler yakoze urugendo ruteye ubwoba, kandi akora umurimo runaka; ni bangahe bitagaragara. Ariko ibi sibyose. Mu 1819, inzu imwe yashizeho ubwato bwa baleine bavumbuye ubwabo, kugira ngo bajye mu kato karyoshye ku mazi ya kure y'Ubuyapani. Ubwo bwato - bwiswe “Syren” - bwakoze urugendo rwiza rwo kugerageza; nuko rero niho hambere Ubuyapani bukomeye bwa Whaling Ground bwamenyekanye muri rusange. Syren muri uru rugendo ruzwi yategekwaga na Kapiteni Coffin, Nantucketer.

Icyubahiro cyose kuri Enderbies rero, inzu yabo, ngira ngo, iriho kugeza na nubu; nubwo ntagushidikanya Samweli wumwimerere agomba kuba yarashize kera umugozi winyanja nini yepfo yisi.

Ubwato bwitiriwe izina rye bwari bukwiye icyubahiro, kuba umusare wihuta cyane nubukorikori buhebuje muburyo bwose. Namwinjiye rimwe mu gicuku ahantu runaka ku nkombe za Patagoniya, kandi nanywa inzoga nziza mu iteganyagihe. Wari umukino mwiza twari dufite, kandi bose bari impanda - buri muntu mubwato. Ubuzima bugufi kuri bo, n'urupfu rusekeje. Kandi uwo mukino mwiza nari mfite-ndende, ndende cyane nyuma yuko umusaza Ahabu akora ku rubaho rwe agatsinsino k'inzovu - biranyibutsa ubwakiranyi bukomeye, bukomeye, Saxon yakiriye ubwo bwato; kandi parson wanjye anyibagirwe, kandi satani anyibuke, niba narigeze kubibagirwa. Flip? Navuze ko dufite flip? Nibyo, kandi twarayihinduye ku gipimo cya litiro icumi ku isaha; kandi igihe inkongoro yazaga (kuko irikumwe na Patagonia), kandi amaboko yose - abashyitsi ndetse bose - bahamagariwe hejuru yinyanja, twari turemereye cyane kuburyo byabaye ngombwa ko tuzunguruka hejuru muri bowline; kandi tutabizi twahinduye umwenda w'amakoti yacu mu bwato, ku buryo twamanitse aho, twihuta cyane mu kangaratete, urugero rwo kuburira kuri tari zose zasinze. Ariko, masta ntiyagiye hejuru; kandi twagiye twikubita hasi, twirinda cyane, ku buryo byabaye ngombwa ko twongera guca kuri flip, nubwo umuti wumunyu wa kinyamanswa waturitse uteganya guhanura, ahubwo ukavangwa cyane kandi nkawunyunyuza uburyohe.

Inyama y'inka yari nziza - irakomeye, ariko irimo umubiri. Bavuze ko ari inyana y'inka; abandi, ko ari inyama zinka; ariko sinzi, byanze bikunze, uko byari bimeze. Bafite kandi ibibyimba; ntoya, ariko ikomeye, ihuza isi yose, kandi idashobora kurimburwa. Nifuzaga ko ushobora kubyumva, ukabizunguruka muri wowe nyuma yo kumirwa. Ariko muri rusange, kumuvana mu gikamyo akajya ku buyobozi, urebye ibipimo by'ibikoni bitetse, harimo n'amashanyarazi ye bwite; imbere na aft, ndavuga, Samuel Enderby yari ubwato busekeje; by'ibiciro byiza kandi byinshi; flip nziza kandi ikomeye; gukubita bagenzi bose, hamwe nigishoro kuva inkweto za boot kugeza kuri hat-band.

UMUTWE WA 102. Bower muri Arsacide.

Kugeza ubu, mu gusobanura mu buryo busobanutse bwo kuvura intanga ngabo, ahanini natuye ku bitangaza byo hanze; cyangwa ukwayo kandi muburyo burambuye kubintu bimwe byimbere imbere. Ariko kubwinshi kandi bunonosoye kumwumva, birankundira noneho kumukingura kurushaho, no gukuramo ingingo za hose, gukuramo imyenda ye, no guta ibifuniko n'amaso y'amahuriro y'amagufwa ye y'imbere, bimushyira imbere yawe muri ultimatum ye; nukuvuga, muri skeleton ye itagabanijwe.

None ubu, Ishimayeli? Bishoboka bite, ko wowe, inkuge gusa mu burobyi, witwaza ko hari icyo uzi ku bice byo munsi yisi ya baleine? Ese erudite Stubb, yashyizwe kuri capstan yawe, yatanze ibiganiro kuri anatomiya ya Cetacea; kandi ubifashijwemo nikirahure, fata imbavu ntangarugero kugirango imurikwe? Wisobanure, Ishimayeli. Urashobora gushira igifi kinini cyakuze kumurongo wawe kugirango usuzume, nkuko umutetsi atetse ingurube ikaranze? Nta nkeka. Kugeza ubu wabaye umuhamya w'ukuri, Ishimayeli; ariko witondere uburyo ufata amahirwe ya Yona wenyine; amahirwe yo kuganira ku ngingo no ku biti; ibisumizi, imisozi-pole, ibitotsi, hamwe na pin-pinning, bigize ikadiri-umurimo wa leviathan; na belike ya vatow ndende, ibyumba byamata, amavuta, na foromaje munda.

Ndatuye, ko kuva Yona, abanyamurwango bake binjiye cyane munsi yuruhu rwa baleine ikuze; nonese, nahawe amahirwe yo kumutandukanya muri miniature. Mu bwato nari ndimo, akana gato ka Sperm Whale yigeze kuzamurwa mu mubiri kugira ngo agaragaze poke cyangwa igikapu cye, kugira ngo akore ibyatsi byo kogosha inanga, no ku mitwe y'amacumu. Tekereza ko naretse ayo mahirwe akagenda, ntakoresheje ubwato bwanjye-bwato na jack-icyuma, no kumena kashe no gusoma ibikubiye muri kiriya cyana gito?

Naho kubijyanye n'ubumenyi bwanjye bwukuri kumagufa ya leviathan mumajyambere yabo akomeye, akuze yuzuye, kubwubwo bumenyi budasanzwe ndashimira inshuti yanjye ya cyami nyakwigendera Tranquo, umwami wa Tranque, umwe muri Arsacide. Kubera ko nari muri Tranque, mu myaka yashize, ubwo nifatanyaga n'ubwato bw'ubucuruzi Dey bwa Algiers, natumiriwe kumarana igice cy'ikiruhuko cya Arsacidean hamwe na nyagasani wa Tranque, muri villa y'imikindo yasezeye i Pupella; glen kuruhande rwinyanja itari kure cyane yibyo abasare bacu bitaga Bamboo-Town, umurwa mukuru we.

Mu zindi mico myinshi myiza, inshuti yanjye yumwami Tranquo, yahawe impano yurukundo rwinshi kubintu byose bya vertic barbari, yari yarahurije i Pupella ibintu byose bidasanzwe ubwenge bwubwoko bwe bwashoboraga guhimba; cyane cyane ibiti bibajwe mubikoresho byiza, ibisasu byacagaguritse, amacumu yometseho, padi ihenze, ubwato bwa aromatic; kandi ibyo byose byakwirakwijwe mubitangaza bisanzwe, imizigo itwara imizigo, imisoro itanga imisoro yari yataye ku nkombe ze.

Umukuru muri aba nyuma yari Sperm Whale nini, nyuma y’umuyaga muremure udasanzwe, basanze yapfuye kandi irahagarara, umutwe we uhanganye n’igiti cyitwa cocoa-nut, kikaba gifite ibimera bisa n’ibimera bisa nkindege ye nziza. Igihe umurambo munini wari wambuwe amaherezo yimbitse, hanyuma amagufwa ahinduka umukungugu wizuba ku zuba, noneho skeleti yatwarwaga yitonze hejuru ya pupella glen, aho urusengero runini rwimikindo ya nyagasani rwarikingiye.

Urubavu rwamanitsweho ibikombe; vertebræ yashushanyijeho amateka ya Arsacidean, muri hieroglyphics idasanzwe; mu gihanga, abapadiri bagumishaga urumuri rutameze neza, ku buryo umutwe w'amayobera wongeye kohereza umuyaga wacyo; mugihe, yahagaritswe kumashami, urwasaya ruteye ubwoba rwanyeganyega abihaye Imana bose, nkinkota imanitse umusatsi itera Damocles cyane.

Byari ibintu bitangaje. Igiti cyari icyatsi nka mose ya Icy Glen; ibiti byahagaze hejuru kandi birata, bikumva ibimera bizima; isi ikora cyane yari imeze nk'ububoshyi bw'ububoshyi, ifite itapi nziza cyane, aho imizabibu-imizabibu yubutaka yabaga ifite ubudodo nubudodo, nindabyo nzima imibare. Umudozi-mana, araboha; kandi kubwo kuboha ni igipfamatwi, ko atumva ijwi ryica; kandi nukuvuza induru, natwe, abareba umwenda twumva; kandi nitumara guhunga tuzumva amajwi igihumbi abivugaho. Muri ubwo buryo, hagaragaye abagome. Ah, bapfa! nimwitondere; kuberako, muribi byose byisi yisi ikomeye, ibitekerezo byawe byihishe bishobora kumvikana kure.

Noneho, hagati yicyatsi kibisi, kitarangwamo ubuzima bwicyo giti cya Arsacidean, skeleti nini, yera, yasengwaga yari aryamye - idakora cyane! Nyamara, nkigihe cyogosha cyogosha cyogosha nubudodo buvanze kandi bugahuzagurika, umudozi ukomeye wasaga numuboshyi w'amayeri; we ubwe yambaraga imizabibu; buri kwezi dufata icyatsi kibisi, gishya; ariko we ni skeleti. Noneho, ubwo hamwe na Tranquo yumwami nasuye iyi baleine itangaje, mbona igihanga cyurutambiro, numwotsi wubukorikori uzamuka uva aho indege nyayo yatangiriye, natangajwe nuko umwami agomba gufata ishapeli nkikintu cya vertu. Ariko bidatinze umurongo wanjye wasohotse; no kuyikurikira inyuma, nasohotse mfungura aho ninjiye. Nta kintu kizima nabonye imbere; nta kindi cyari gihari uretse amagufwa.

Kunkata icyatsi kibisi-gipima, nongeye kwibira muri skeleton. Bahereye ku myambi yabo ya gihanga mu gihanga, abapadiri bambonye mfata ubutumburuke bw'urubavu rwa nyuma, “Ubu se!” bavuza induru; “Ntushobora gupima iyi mana yacu! Kuberako birukanwa gusa muri promontory kugera cape; kandi niba inkombe imwe itagikoreshwa nindege zabo, noneho, menya neza, izindi ngendo zimwe na remoter zaherutse gutungurwa cyane nibintu bitamenyerewe.

Ikigeretse kuri ibyo: kubijyanye naba Leviathan baheruka kuvugwa, bafite ibihome bibiri bihamye, mubishoboka byose byabantu, bizahoraho iteka ryose. Nko ku gitero cy’ibibaya byabo, Abasuwisi bakonje basubiye mu misozi yabo; rero, guhigwa muri savannas na glade yinyanja yo hagati, balale-igufwa ryamagufa irashobora kwifashisha imigi yabo ya Polar, hanyuma ikarohama munsi yinzitizi zikirahure ninkuta zaho, izamuka mumirima yubukonje nubutaka; no muruziga rwiza rwukuboza iteka, gusaba kutubahiriza ibintu byose biva kumuntu.

Ariko nkuko wenda mirongo itanu muribi bine byamagufwa byitwa baleine byacuranzwe kuri cachalot imwe, abahanga mu bya filozofiya bahanuye bemeje ko iri hohoterwa ryiza rimaze kugabanya cyane bataillon zabo. Ariko nubwo hashize igihe kitari gito umubare munini wi baleine, utari munsi ya 13.000, buri mwaka bicwa ku nkombe z’iburengerazuba cyangwa 'iburengerazuba n’abanyamerika bonyine; nyamara hari ibitekerezo bitanga ndetse nibi bihe bito cyangwa bidafite konti nkimpaka zinyuranye muriki kibazo.

Kamere nkuko bikwiye kuba bimwe bitangaje kubyerekeranye nubwinshi bwibinyabuzima binini cyane byo ku isi, nyamara twabwira iki Harto, umuhanga mu by'amateka ya Goa, igihe atubwira ko igihe kimwe cyo guhiga Umwami wa Siam yatwaye inzovu 4000; ko muri utwo turere inzovu ari nyinshi nk'imikumbi y'inka mu gihe cy'ubushyuhe. Kandi nta mpamvu n'imwe yatuma dushidikanya ko niba izo nzovu, zimaze imyaka ibihumbi zihigwa, na Semiramis, na Porusi, na Hannibal, hamwe n'abami bose bagiye bakurikirana bo mu Burasirazuba - niba zikiriho ku bwinshi, birashoboka cyane ko inyanja nini ishobora guhiga abantu bose, kubera ko ifite urwuri rwo mu mahanga, muri Afurika ndetse no muri Amerika, ndetse no muri Amerika yose, inyanja hamwe.

Byongeye kandi, tugomba gutekereza, ko duhereye ku gitekerezo cyo kuramba gukomeye kwi baleine, birashoboka ko bagera ku kinyejana ndetse nibindi byinshi, kubwibyo rero mugihe icyo aricyo cyose, ibisekuru byinshi bitandukanye byakuze bigomba kuba bigezweho. Kandi ibyo aribyo, dushobora guhita twunguka igitekerezo, mugutekereza imva zose, imva, amarimbi, hamwe nububiko bwimiryango yaremye bitanga imibiri nzima yabagabo, abagore, nabana bose bari bazima mumyaka mirongo irindwi n'itanu ishize; no kongeramo iyi hosti itabarika kubantu babantu bisi kwisi.

Kubwibyo, kuri ibyo bintu byose, tubara igifi kinini kidapfa mubwoko bwe, nubwo cyangirika kumuntu. Yoga inyanja mbere yuko imigabane imena amazi; yigeze koga hejuru yikibanza cya Tuileries, na Windsor Castle, na Kreml. Mu mwuzure wa Nowa yasuzuguye Isanduku ya Nowa; kandi niba isi izongera kurengerwa n’umwuzure, kimwe n’Ubuholandi, kugira ngo yice imbeba zayo, noneho balale ihoraho izakomeza kubaho, kandi irerereye hejuru y’umwuzure w’ubutayu, isuzugura ikirere.





UMUTWE WA 106. Ukuguru kwa Ahabu.

Uburyo imvura yaguyemo Kapiteni Ahabu yari yararetse Samuel Enderby w’i Londres, ntabwo yari yarigeze yitabwaho n’urugomo ruto ku muntu we. Yari afite ingufu nyinshi ku bwato bwe ku buryo ukuguru kwe kw'inzovu kwakubiswe n'igice. Igihe kandi amaze kubona igorofa ye bwite, hamwe na pivot-umwobo we aho, yahise azunguruka cyane azengurutse itegeko ryihutirwa abayobora (byari nkibisanzwe, ikintu kijyanye no kutayobora ku buryo budahagije); hanyuma, amahembe y'inzovu yari amaze kunyeganyezwa yakiriye iyindi mpinduramatwara, kuburyo nubwo yagumye yuzuye, kandi kubigaragara byose irarikira, nyamara Ahabu ntiyigeze abona ko ari iyo kwizerwa rwose.

Kandi, mubyukuri, byasaga nkibintu bito bitangaje, kuba Ahabu yabaga afite uburangare, umusazi, rimwe na rimwe yitonderaga neza uko igufwa ryapfuye yari ahagararaho igice. Kuberako ntibyatinze cyane mbere yuko Pequod afata ubwato avuye i Nantucket, ko bamusanze ijoro rimwe aryamye hasi, kandi ntabyumva; na bamwe batazwi, kandi bisa nkaho bidasobanutse, umuntu udashobora gutekerezwa, igihimba cye cy amahembe yinzovu kimaze kwimurwa ku buryo bukabije, ku buryo cyakubiswe n’igiti, kandi byose ariko byacumise mu rukenyerero; eka mbere ntibyari bigoye cyane ko igikomere kibabaza cyakize rwose.

Ntabwo, muri kiriya gihe, niba byarananiranye kwinjira mu bwenge bwe bwa monomaniac, ko umubabaro wose w’ubwo bubabare bwa none wari ikibazo ariko kitaziguye cy’amakuba yahoze; kandi na we yasaga nkaho yabonaga, ko nkibikururuka by’uburozi cyane byo mu gishanga bikomeza ubwoko bwe byanze bikunze nkindirimbo nziza iryoshye yo mu ishyamba; rero, kimwe na buri cyubahiro, ibintu byose bibabaje mubisanzwe bibyara nkibyo. Yego, birenze kimwe, yatekereje Ahabu; kubera ko ibisekuruza n'ibisekuruza by'akababaro bigenda birenze ibisekuruza n'ibyishimo bya Byishimo. Kuberako, tutibagiwe ibi: ko ari umwanzuro uva ku nyigisho zimwe na zimwe zemewe, ko mugihe ibinezeza bimwe na bimwe hano bitazagira abana bababyariye kubwisi, ariko, kurundi ruhande, bizakurikirwa numunezero-utagira umwana wo kwiheba kwi kuzimu; mu gihe, bimwe mu byago byica abantu bizakomeza kubyara ubwabo urubyaro ruhoraho rwakababaro rurenze imva; ntabwo aribyose kugirango yerekane ibi, haracyagaragara ubusumbane mubisesengura byimbitse kubintu. Kuberako, Ahab yatekereje, mugihe na felicies zo hejuru kwisi zigeze zigira ubunebwe butagaragara muri bo, ariko, hepfo, imitima yose, ibisobanuro byamayobera, kandi, mubagabo bamwe, icyubahiro gikomeye cya marayika mukuru; kora rero umwete wabo ushakisha-hanze ntukizere kugabanuka kugaragara. Kugirango dukurikirane ibisekuruza byibi byago bipfa gupfa, bitujyana amaherezo muri primogenitures idafite imana; kugira ngo, imbere yizuba ryishimye, rikora ibyatsi, hamwe nizuba ryoroshye, ukwezi gusarura, tugomba gukenera kubitanga: kugirango imana ubwayo itazigera yishima. Ikimenyetso kidasubirwaho, kibabaje-ikimenyetso cyamavuko mumutwe wumuntu, ni ariko kashe yumubabaro mubasinya.

Utabishaka hano ibanga ryashyizwe ahagaragara, birashoboka ko bishoboka cyane, muburyo bwagenwe, ryamenyekanye mbere. Hamwe nibindi bintu byinshi byerekeranye na Ahabu, buri gihe byari bikomeje kuba amayobera kuri bamwe, impamvu byari bimeze, ko mugihe runaka, haba mbere na nyuma yubwato bwa Pequod, yari yihishe hamwe nki kintu cyihariye cya Grand-Lama; kandi, muri kiriya gihe kimwe, yashakishije ubuhungiro butavuga, nkaho, muri sena ya marble y'abapfuye. Impamvu yakomeretse ya Kapiteni Peleg kuri iki kintu yagaragaye ntabwo ihagije; nubwo, mubyukuri, nko gukora ku gice cyimbitse cya Ahabu, ihishurwa ryose ryagize umwijima mwinshi kuruta urumuri rusobanura. Ariko, amaherezo, byose byasohotse; iki kibazo kimwe cyakoze, byibura. Ayo makosa ateye ubwoba yari munsi yubusa bwe bwigihe gito. Kandi ntabwo aribi gusa, ahubwo kuri buri gihe cyamasezerano, guta uruziga ku nkombe, kubwimpamvu iyo ari yo yose, yari afite amahirwe yo kumwiyegereza bitabujijwe; Kuri urwo ruziga ruteye ubwoba twavuze ko hapfuye abantu benshi - basigaye, nkuko byagenze, Ahabu atigeze amenyekana neza - yashora imari mu bwoba, ntabwo yakuwe mu gihugu cy'imyuka n'imiborogo. Kugira ngo, kubera ishyaka bamugirira, bose bari baracuze umugambi, kugeza aho bari barimo, kugira ngo bahindure ubumenyi bw'iki kintu ku bandi; nuko rero, ni uko kugeza igihe kitari gito cyashize, cyabaye kuri paje ya Pequod.

Ariko ube ibyo byose uko bishoboka; reka sinodi itagaragara, idasobanutse mu kirere, cyangwa ibikomangoma byihorera hamwe nimbaraga zumuriro, bigomba gukora cyangwa kutabikora kuri Ahabu wo ku isi, nyamara, muriki kibazo cyukuguru kwe, yafashe inzira zifatika; -yita umubaji.

Igihe kandi uwo mukozi yamubonekeye, yamusabye bidatinze atangira gukora ukuguru gushya, maze ategeka abo bashakanye kumubona yahawe sitidiyo zose hamwe n’ibihwagari by’inzovu (Sperm Whale) kugeza ubu byari byegeranijwe mu rugendo, kugira ngo hatorwe neza ibintu bikomeye kandi byuzuye neza. Ibi byakozwe, umubaji yakiriye amabwiriza yo kurangiza ukuguru muri iryo joro; no gutanga ibyangombwa byose kuri yo, bitagendeye kubireba ibyiringirwa bikoreshwa. Byongeye kandi, ubwato bw'ubwato bwategetswe kuzamurwa kubera ubushomeri bwabwo bw'agateganyo; kandi, kugira ngo ibintu byihute, umucuzi yategetswe guhita akora icyarimwe cyo guhimba ibyuma byose bishobora gukenerwa.
