BUNG 44. Chart.

Captain Ahab-a chu a crew-te nena a thiltum savawm taka an nemngheh hnu zan lama thlipui tleh hnua a cabin chhungah i zui thla ta a nih chuan, transom chhunga locker pakhatah chuan a kal tih i hmu ang a, tuifinriat chart sen hring hring roll lian tak, wrinkled roll lian tak a rawn chhuah chhuak a, a dawhkan screwed-down-ah chuan a hmaah a theh darh ta a ni. Tichuan a hmaah chuan thuin, chutah chuan a mit nena inhmuhna line leh shading hrang hrangte chu ngun takin a zir tih i hmu ngei ang; leh pencil slow mahse steady tak hmangin additional courses over spaces that before blank. Hun engemaw chen chu a bula log-book hlui phum khawmte a sawi ang a, chutah chuan lawng hrang hranga an zin tawhna hrang hrangah sperm whale an man emaw, an hmuh emaw hun leh hmunte an ziak a ni.

Mahse, a bik takin he zan hian a ni lo va, a cabin-a mahni chauha awm laiin, Ahaba chuan chutiang chuan a chart-te chu a ngaihtuah a ni. Zan tin deuhthaw chuan an rawn hruai chhuak thin a; zan tin deuhthaw pencil chhinchhiahna ṭhenkhat chu tihbo a ni a, ṭhenkhat chu thlak a ni bawk. A hmaa tuifinriat palite chart-te nen chuan, Ahaba chuan a thlarau chungchânga chu ngaihtuahna pakhat chauh tihhlawhtlinna chiang zâwk tûrin, tuifinriat leh thlipui hrual maze chu a thread a ni si a.

Tunah chuan, leviathan-te kawng hre chiang lo tu pawh tan chuan, chutianga he planet-a tuipui kamah hian thilsiam pakhat chauh zawng chhuah chu beiseina nei lo ang maiin a lang thei a ni. Mahse, chutiang chu a ni lo, tuifinriat leh tuifinriat zawng zawng set-te hre chiangtu Ahaba tan chuan; tin, chu chuan sperm whale ei tur driftings te chu a chhut chhuak a; tin, latitude biktea a man hun pangngai, hriat chian tawhte pawh ngaihtuah nawn lehin; a sa ei tur zawng tura he emaw, chu hmun emawa a awm hun tur ni chungchangah chuan, thil chiang tak tak hnaih tawh tak tak, rinhlelhna dik tak a siam thei a ni.

Chutiang khawpa rintlak tak chu, sperm whale-in tui pek a hman hunbi neia a innghahna chungchangah hian, ramsa mantu tam tak chuan khawvel pumah ngun taka enfiah leh zir chian theih a ni ang em tih an ring a whale lawng zawng zawng zinkawng khat atana logs te chu uluk taka dahkhawm a nih chuan, sperm whale-te pem chhuahna chu herring-shoals emaw, swallow-te thlawhna emaw nen a inmil lo tih hmuhchhuah a ni ang. Chu bâkah, ei tûr hmun pakhat a\anga hmun danga kalna an siam hian, sperm whale-te chu, instinct dik lo engemaw zât—Pathian hnên aṭanga finna thup sawi ni ila, kaihhruaina hnuaia awmte chuan a tam zâwk chu vein -ah an inbual ṭhîn , an tih angin;.. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. tuifinriat kawngpui pek tawh pakhatah chuan chutiang taka dik lo takin an kal chhunzawm zel a, chutiang khawpa dikna mak tak maia sawma pakhat khat pawhin, eng chart pawh hmangin, lawng tumahin a kalna tur chu an kal ngai lo. Heng hunah hian whale pakhat pawhin a kalna tur kawng chu surveyor-te parallel angin dinglam ni mah se, hmalam panna line chu a pumpelh theih loh, straight wake-ah khauh takin tihkhawtlai ni mah se, heng hun laia a inbual nia sawi vein duhthusam chuan a tlangpuiin mel tlemte engemaw zat a zau (a tam emaw a tlem emaw, chu vein chu a zau emaw, a tlahniam emaw nia ngaih a nih angin) a huam a mahse, he magic zone-a fimkhur taka a tlan lai hian, whale-ship-a mast-heads aṭanga hmuh theiha sweep chu a pel ngai lo. A thupui ber chu, chutiang zau zawng leh chu kawngpui dunga hunbi bikteah chuan, migrating whale-te chu rinna nasa tak nen zawn theih a ni.

Tin, chuvângin, hun dik takah chauh ni lovin, chaw ei tûr hmun hran hriat lar takah chauh ni lovin, Ahaba chuan a sa ei tûr chu a tawk thei a ni; mahse, chu hmun inkar tui zau ber berte a paltlangnaah chuan a kutchhuak hmangin a kalna kawngah chuan a hmun leh hun a hmang thei a, chutih lai pawh chuan inhmuhkhawmna beisei nei lovin a awm vek lo ang.

Thil awmdan pakhat a awm a, chu chu a hmasa berah chuan a delirious mahse methodical scheme chu a inzawm khawm ang mai a ni. Mahse a takah chuan chutiang chu a ni lo mai thei. Gregarious sperm whale-te hian hmun bik atan an hun pangngai nei mah se, a tlangpuiin kumin chhunga chutiang leh chutiang latitude emaw longitude-a awm ran rualte chu, sawi ta ila, a hmaa season hmasa lama chutah chuan hmuh tawhte nen an inang vek ang tih hi i thutlukna siam thei lo mahse, hei hi a kalh zawnga a lan chhuahna thil danglam leh zawhna awm lo tak tak a awm a ni. A tlangpuiin, chutiang thusawi tho chu, a huam chin zau lo zawk chhungah chauh, sperm whale puitling, kum upa tawh tak takte zinga mahni chauha awm leh hermit-te tan pawh a hman theih a ni. Chutiang chuan Moby Dick hi kum hmasa khan hmuh ni mah se, entirnan, Indian tuipuiah Seychelle ground an tihah emaw, Japanese Coast-a Volcano Bay an tihah emaw mahse, chu chu a zui lo, Pequod hian a hnu lama season inmil apiangah chu hmun pahnih zinga pakhat tal chu tlawh ta se, chutah chuan dik lo takin a tawk ang tih a ni. Chutiang bawkin, chaw ei tur dang thenkhat, a chang chuan a inpuang tawhna hmunah pawh. Mahse heng zawng zawng hi a casual stop-place leh ocean-inns chauh niin a lang a, sawi dan chuan hun rei tak a awmna hmun a ni lo. Tin, tun thlenga Ahaba’n a thil tum tihhlawhtlin theihna chance sawi a nihna hmunah chuan, eng way-side, antecedent, extra prospects pawh a ta ni se, ere a particular set time or place were attained, chutih hunah chuan thil awm thei zawng zawng chu probabilities a lo ni ang a, Ahaba’n duhthusamin a ngaihtuah angin, thil awm thei zawng zawng chu thil chiang tak a lo ni ang. Chu hun leh hmun ruat bik chu technical phrase pakhat—Season-on-the-Line-ah chuan a inzawm khawm a ni. Chutah leh chutah chuan, kum engemaw zat a zawnin, Moby Dick chu a hun hunah sawi a ni tawh a, chu tuiah chuan rei vak lo a awm a, ni chu, kum tin a round-ah chuan, Zodiac chhinchhiahna pakhatah pawh hun bituk chhung atan a loiter thin a ni. Chutah pawh chuan white whale nena inhmuhna thihna thlen tam zawk chu a thleng tawh a; chutah chuan thlipui chu a thiltih nen an sawi a; chu hmun lungchhiatthlak tak chu a awm bawk a, chutah chuan putar monomaniac chuan a phuba lak chhan rapthlak tak a hmu a ni. Mahse, Ahaba’n a thlarau ngaihtuahawm tak chu he ramsa man sual takah hian fimkhur taka a luhtirna leh fimkhur lohnaah chuan, a chunga kan sawi tak thudik lallukhum khumtu pakhatah hian a beiseina zawng zawng chu nghah a phal lo vang a, chu beiseinate chu eng ang pawhin a hlimthla pawh ni se a thutiam mutthilh lohnaah pawh hian a thinlung muang lo tak chu a ti thlamuang thei lo va, a inrawlh zawng zawng chu a sawn hla thei lo bawk.

Tunah chuan Pequod chu Season-on-the-Line tan tirh lamah khan Nantucket atangin a lo chhuak tawh a. Chutih laia hmalakna awm thei reng reng chuan a sipai hotu chuan khawthlang lam panna kawngpui ropui tak, Cape Horn chu a let hnih a siam thei lo va, chutah chuan latitude degree sawmruk zeta tla thlain equatorial Pacific-ah a hun takah a thleng thei lo va, chutah chuan lawngin a kal thei ta a ni. Chuvangin season lo awm tur chu a nghah a ngai a ni. Mahse, Pequod lawnga a kal hun hma hun chu, Ahaba chuan dik takin a thlang a ni mai thei, he thil rawng hring tak tak hi ngaihtuah chungin. A chhan chu, ni leh zan za thum leh sawmruk leh panga inkar chu a hmaah a awm a; tuipui kama dawhthei lo taka tuar chhuah ai chuan thil chi hrang hranga ramsa man tura a hman tur interval; a nih loh leh, White Whale chuan a chawlh hun chu a hun bi neia a chaw ei ṭhinna hmun aṭanga hla tak tuipuiah a hmang a, a hmui hrual chu Persian Gulf aṭangin emaw, Bengal Bay-ah emaw, China Tuipuiah emaw, a hnamin a tihbuai tui dang eng pawhah emaw a tisang a nih chuan. Chutiang chuan Monsoon te, Pampas te, Nor’-Westers te, Harmattans te, Trades te; Levanter leh Simoon tih loh thli eng pawhin Moby Dick chu Pequod-a circumnavigating wake devious zig-zag world-circle-ah a thlawh lut thei a ni.

Mahse heng zawng zawng hi phalsak chungin; mahse, fimkhur tak leh cool taka en chuan, ngaihtuahna rilru na tak mai a ni lo niin a lang, hei hi; tuifinriat zau tak, ramri nei lo takah chuan, whale pakhat chauh, a tawk a nih pawhin, Constantinople khawpui kawngpuiah mipui tamna hmuna Mufti hmul dum nei ang pawhin, a ramsa mantu hnen atanga mimal hriat theih anga ngaih tur a ni tih? Tin, hetah hian a rilru â chu thawk chhuak thei lo intlansiakah a tlan zel ang; ngaihtuahna hahna leh rilru hahna chu a chungah a lo thleng ta a; tin, deck boruak zau takah chuan a chakna lak let leh a tum ang. Ah, Pathian! chu mipa chuan phuba lak duhna pakhat tihhlawhtlin loha ei zo tawh chu eng hrehawm tawrhna trances nge a tuar thin. Kut hup chungin a muhil a; leh a kutphahah a thisen chhuak nail ngei nen a harh thin.

Vawi tam tak chu, zan lama mumang hahthlak leh tuar theih loh khawpa nung tak takte hmanga a hammock atanga nawr luih a nih chuan, chu chuan nilengin a ngaihtuahna nasa tak chu a chhunzawm leh a, chu chu phrensies inhnialna karah a kalpui a, a thluak kang takah chuan a rawn vawrh kual vel a, a nunna hmun ri bengchheng tak tak chu tuar theih loh hrehawmna a lo nih thlengin tin, a châng chuan a awm ṭhin angin, a chhûnga heng thlarau lam hrehawmnate hian a bul aṭanga a awmna chu a tisang a, a chhûngah chuan khur a inhawng ang maiin a lang a, chuta ṭang chuan meialh leh êng chhuakin a lo chhuak a, ramhuai chibai bukte chuan an zînga zuang thla tûrin an ko a he hremhmun ngei hian a hnuaiah a rawn beng a, lawng chhungah chuan au thawm nasa tak a ri ang a; tin, mitmeng hring dup chungin Ahaba chu a state room aṭangin a puak darh ang a, khum kang aṭanga tlanchhuak ang maiin a rawn chhuak ang. Mahse, hengte hi chak lohna thup engemaw, emaw, a thutlukna avanga hlauhna emaw, thunun theih loh chhinchhiahna ni lovin, a nasatzia chhinchhiahna chiang ber mai a ni mai thei. Chutiang hunah chuan, mi â Ahaba, mi â, rilru hahdam lo taka white whale mantu kha; he Ahaba hi a hammock-a kal tawh hi, chutianga hlauthawng taka chuta tang chuan a rawn puak chhuak leh tatu agent a ni lo. A hnuhnung zawk chu a chhunga chatuan, nung thu bul emaw, thlarau emaw a ni a; tin, muthilh lai chuan, hun dangah chuan a pawn lam lirthei emaw, a agent emaw atana hmangtu rilru ziarang a\angin hun engemaw chen a in\hen a, chu chu a takin thil buai tak, a inzawm tlat (scorching contiguity) a\angin tlanchhuahna a zawng a, chu chu, chutih hun lai chuan, chu chu a pawimawh ber a ni tawh lo. Mahse rilru chu thlarau nena inzawm tlat a nih loh chuan a awm loh angin, chuvangin, Ahaba-a chungchangah chuan, a ngaihtuahna leh duhthusam zawng zawng chu a thiltum sang ber pakhatah a hlan vek a ni ngei ang chu thiltum chuan, a duhthlanna inveteracy mai mai hmangin, pathian leh diabola lakah chuan mahni inngaihtuah, mahni inrintawkna nei, mahni inrintawkna nei lo chi khatah a inhnukdawk ta a ni. Ni lo, lungchhiatthlak takin a nung thei a, a kang thei a, chutih lai chuan a inzawmkhawmna chakna tlangpui chu, koh loh leh pa nei lo pianna a\ang chuan hlauhawm takin a tlanchhia a ni. Chuvangin, taksa mit atanga lo lang chhuak thlarau hrehawm tak chu, Ahaba ang maia a room atanga a rawn tlan chhuah lai khan, chu chu hun atan chuan thil awm lo mai mai, formless somnambulist being, êng nung ray, a chiang a, mahse rawng siamna tur thil nei lo, chuvangin a takah chuan blankness a ni. Pathianin pui che, putar, i ngaihtuahnain i chhungah thilsiam a siam a; tin, chutianga a ngaihtuahna nasa tak chuan Prometheus-a siamtu chu; vulture chuan chu thinlung chu chatuan atan a ei a; chu vulture chu a siam thilsiam ngei chu.





BUNG 45. Affidavit tih chu.

He lehkhabuah hian thawnthu (narrative) awm thei tur thlengin; tin, dik tak chuan, sperm whale-te tih dâna thil chiang tak leh ngaihnawm tak tak pakhat emaw, pahnih emaw chu indirect-a khawih a nih angin, a hmaa bung hi, a hmasa berah chuan, he volume-a hmuh tûr ang bawka pawimawh a ni a mahse, a thupui ber chu hriatthiamna tling a nih theih nân, a thupui pum pui hriat lohna thuk tak chuan rilru ṭhenkhatah rin lohna eng pawh a thlen thei, he thila thupui ber berte pianpui dikna chungchânga rin lohna eng pawh chu lak bo a ngai a ni.

Ka hnathawhna hmun hi methodical taka thawh loh ka ngaihtuah a; mahse, whaleman anga ka hriat, practically emaw, rintlak emawa thil awmte citation hrang hrang hmanga duhthusam chu a siam chhuah theih nan a lungawi ang tin, heng citations a\ang hian ka la a ni—a tum ber thutlukna chu a pianphungah a zui ve ngei ang.

Pakhatna: Keimah ngei pawhin whale pakhatin harpoon a dawn hnua tlanchhuahna tluantling a thlen dan vawi thum ka hre tawh a; tin, hun engemaw chen (kum thum chhunga vawi khat) hnuah, chu kut vek chuan vuak lehin, thah a ni leh ta a; thir pahnih, private cypher khata chhinchhiah ve ve chu taksa atanga lak chhuah a nih chuan. Harpoon pahnih thlawh chhuah inkara kum thum a inrawlh lai khan; tin, chu aia tam pawh a ni mai thei niin ka hria; the man who darted them happening, in the interval, to go in a trading ship on a travel to Africa, chutah chuan tuipuiah a kal a, discovery party a zawm a, ram chhung hla takah a lut a, chutah chuan kum hnih dawn lai a zin a, rul, savawm, sakeibaknei, poisonous miasmas te hlauhawmah a awm fo thin a, a dang zawng zawng nen chuan a common perils incident to wandering in the heart of unknown regions. Chutih lai chuan a whale a vuak pawh kha a zinnaah a awm ve ngei ang; rinhlelh rual lohvin khawvel pum hi vawi thum a fang kual tawh a, Africa tuipui kam zawng zawng chu a sir lamah a brush vek tawh a; mahse, tum engmah lovah. He pa leh he whale hi an rawn inzawm leh a, pakhat chuan pakhat chu a hneh ta a ni. Kei, keimah ngei pawh hian hetiang ang chiah hian instance pathum ka hre tawh ka ti a; chu chu pahnihah chuan whale-te an vuak ka hmu a; tin, a vawi hnihnaah chuan, a chhunga chhinchhiahna hrang hranga siam thir pahnih chu a hmu a, chu chu sangha thi aṭanga lak chhuah a ni. Kum thum chhunga thil thlengah chuan a tla nasa hle a, lawngah hian vawi hnih, a hmasa ber leh a hnuhnung ber ka chuang a, a vawi khatna atan chuan whale mit hnuaia mole lian tak chi danglam tak, kum thum kalta khan chutah chuan ka lo hmu tawh tih chiang takin ka hre ta a ni. Kum thum ka ti a, mahse chu aia tam a ni tih ka chiang khawp mai. Chuti a nih chuan, keimah ngei pawhin a dikna ka hriat instance pathum chu hetiang hi a ni; mahse, he thubuaiah hian an dikna chu impeach theihna tur chhan ṭha tak nei lote hnen aṭangin thil dang tam tak ka hre tawh bawk.

Pahnihnaah chuan: Sperm Whale Fishery-ah chuan tuipui kama khawvel hian eng ang pawhin hre lo mah se, tuifinriata whale pakhat bik chu hun hla tak leh hmun hla tak takah mipui hriat theih a nih tawhna chanchin hriatreng tlak tak tak engemaw zat a awm tih hriat hlawh tak a ni. Chutiang whale chu chutianga chhinchhiah a nih chhan chu a pum pui leh a tir lamah chuan whale dangte aiin a taksa danglam bik vang a ni lo va; chutiang lamah chuan chance whale eng pawh chu eng ang pawhin danglam bik mah se, rei lo teah a danglam bikna chu an that a, hriak hlu danglam takah an chhum thla ta a ni. Ni lo: a chhan chu hei hi a ni: chu chu sangha mantu thil tawn thihna thlentu a\ang chuan Rinaldo Rinaldini-a chungchânga chutiang whale chungchânga hlauhawmna ropui tak a innghat a, chutiang chuan sangha mantu tam zâwk chuan tuifinriata an lounging lai an hmuh chhuah hunah, inhriatthiamna inpawh zâwk neih tum lovin, an tarpaulin-te khawih mai maiin an hre thei ta a ni. Tuipui kama ramhuai rethei thenkhat, mi ropui thinrim tak hre ve mai mai ang bawkin, kawngpuiah chuan hla tak atanga hmuh theih loh chibai bukna an siam a, chuti lo chuan an hmelhriat chu an zui lehzual a nih chuan an inngaihluhna avanga summary thump an dawng mai ang.

Mahse, heng whale hmingthang tak takte hian mimal hmingthanna ropui tak an nei vek mai ni lovin—Ni lo, tuifinriat pum huapa hmingthanna i ti mai thei; nunah a hmingthang a, tunah chuan thih hnua forecastle thawnthuah thi thei lo a ni mai ni lovin, hming pakhat dikna, chanvo leh danglamna zawng zawngah pawm a ni a Cambyses emaw Cæsar emaw angin hming a nei tak zet a ni. BUNG 46. Surmises.

A thiltum meipui lum takin a kangral tawh chung pawhin, Ahaba chuan a ngaihtuahna leh thiltih zawng zawngah Moby Dick-a man hnuhnung ber chu a ngaihtuah ngai lo va; chu duhthusam pakhatah chuan thi thei thil zawng zawng hlan vek turin a inpeih tawh niin a lang a; chuti chung pawh chuan a pianphung leh hun rei tak a lo awm tawhna avang chuan meialh whaleman-te kawng nen a innei lutuk a, chu lawnga zin chhuahna collateral prosecution chu a bânsan vek mai thei a ni. A nih loh leh a tlem berah chuan hei hi a danglam a nih chuan a chungah nghawng nei lian zawk thiltum dang duhna a awm lo. A monomania ngaihtuah pawhin, White Whale laka a phuba lakna chu eng emaw chen chu sperm whale zawng zawngah pawh a inzar pharh mai thei tih sawi chhuah chu a tithianghlim lutuk mai thei a, chutiang taka ramhuai a tihhlum tam poh leh a hnua whale a tawn apiang chu a man thin huat ber a nih theihna chance a tipung nasa zawk a ni. Mahse chutiang hypothesis chu a danglam bik tak zet a nih chuan, a rorel duhna ramhuai angin khauh takin ni lo mah se, engti kawng mahin a rilru tibuaitu thei lo ngaihtuah tur dang a la awm tho.

A thil tum hlen chhuah nân Ahaba chuan hmanrua a hmang a ngai a; tin, thla thlalak hnuaia hmanraw hman zawng zawng zingah chuan mihringte hi an inthlak danglam thei ber a ni. Entîr nân, eng ang pawhin magnetic a ascendency chu kawng ṭhenkhatah chuan Starbuck chungah a awm tih a hria a, chuti chung pawh chuan chu ascendency chuan thlarau lam mi famkim chu a khuh lo va, taksa lama chungnunna mai mai chuan intellectual mastership a keng tel lo va thlarau lam chauh mite tan chuan mi fing lam erawh chu taksa inzawmna chi khatah an ding reng a ni. Starbuck-a taksa leh Starbuck-a nawr luih duhna chu Ahab-a ta a ni a, chu chu Ahab-a’n a magnet chu Starbuck thluak-ah a dah chhung chuan; chuti chung pawh chuan heng zawng zawng avang hian a kawppui ber chuan a thlarauah a captain-in a zawn chu a huat thu a hria a, chuta tang chuan hlim takin a inthen darh thei ang em, a tibuaitu pawh a ni thei ang. White Whale hmuh hma hian hun rei tak a liam mai thei.
