BUNG 53. The Gam.

Kan sawi tawh whaler-a Ahaba a chuang loh chhan langsar tak chu hei hi a ni: thli leh tuifinriat chuan thlipui a tilang a ni. Mahse, chutiang chu ni lo se chuan, a tawpah chuan, a chungah chuan a chuang lo mai thei—chutiang hun laia a hnu lama a thiltih dan a\angin—chutiang a nih chuan, fakna kalphung a\angin, a zawhna zawh chhanna \ha lo tak a hmu ta a ni. A tâwpah a lo awm tawh angin, chu thu hriatna chu a rilru hneh taka a zawn ṭhenkhat chu a thawh chhuah theih loh chuan, minute nga chhûng pawh ni se, captain mikhual tu emaw nêna inneih loh chu a ngaihtuah a ni. Mahse heng zawng zawng hi chhut chhuah tling lo a la ni thei a, hetah hian ram dang tuipuiah, a bik takin cruising-ground inang takah whaling-vessel hman dan danglam tak chungchang sawi a ni lo a nih chuan.

New York State-a Pine Barrens lui, a nih loh leh England rama Salisbury Plain, chutiang bawka ramngaw hring dup mi hriat loh pahnih an paltlang a nih chuan; chutiang ramngaw mikhual lo takah chuan awlsam takin an inhmu a nih chuan, heng pahnih hian, an nunna atan, chibai bukna chu an pumpelh thei lo va; leh thuthang inthleng turin rei vak lo chawlhsan a; tin, rei vak lo thut a, inrem taka chawlh hahdam pawh a ni mai thei: chuti a nih chuan, tuifinriat tawp nei lo Pine Barrens leh Salisbury Plains-a lei tawp lama whale man lawng pahnih inhriatthiam tawnna—off lone Fanning’s Island, emaw, hla tak King’s Mills emaw aiin chutiang dinhmun hnuaiah chuan heng lawngte hian rial an inthlak mai ni lovin, inzawmna hnai zawk, inpawh zawk leh inpawh zawka lo awm tur hi eng ang chiahin nge a natural zawk ka ti. Tin. tin, chuvang chuan, in chhung thil duh tak chi hrang hrang sawi tur nei rawh.

Hun rei tak awm tawh lo lawng tan chuan pawn lam ramri chuan lehkha a nei mai thei; eng pawh ni se, a file blur leh thumb-worn-a a hnuhnung ber aiin kum khat emaw kum hnih emaw hnuah date paper thenkhat a neih tir ngei ang. Tin, chu zahna chu a let leh nan chuan pawn lam pan tur lawng chuan a kalna tur cruising-ground atang chuan whaling intelligence thar ber ber a dawng ang a, chu chu a tan chuan thil pawimawh ber a ni. Tin, degree-ah chuan, heng zawng zawng hi whaling lawngte chu cruising-ground-ah ngei pawha an track inkara an inkalpawhna chungchangah a dik ang, chutiang bawkin an in atanga rei tak awm lo mah se. An zinga pakhat chuan hmun thuma ṭhena hmun khat aṭanga lehkha transfer a dawng a ni thei a, tunah chuan lawng hla tak a ni thei bawk; tin, chu lehkha ṭhenkhat chu tuna a hmuh lawnga mite tân pawh a ni thei bawk. Chu bâkah, whale man chanchin chu an inthleng a, inrem takin an inbia bawk. Lawng khalhtute lainatna zawng zawng chauh ni lovin, chutiang bawkin thil tum inang leh inhmangaih tawnna leh harsatna insem a\anga lo chhuak inhmangaihna danglam tak zawng zawngte pawh an tawk dawn si a.

Ram hrang hrangte pawhin danglamna pawimawh tak a siam lo vang; chu chu, American leh English-ho ang bawkin, a pahnih hian ṭawng pakhat an hman phawt chuan a ni. Mahse, a dik tak chuan English whaller tlemte a\angin chutiang inhmuhkhawmna chu a thleng fo lo va, a lo awm chuan an inkarah zakzumna chi khat a awm thei lutuk a i English mi chu a reserved zawk si a, i Yankee chu amah chauh lo chu tumah chutiang thil chu a fancy lo. Chu bâkah, English whaller-te hian American whaller-te aiin metropolitan-a chungnung zâwkna chi khat an nghawng ṭhîn a; Nantucketer sei tak, tha lo tak, a nondescript provincialisms te nen, tuipui lama loneitu chi khat anga ngaih. Mahse, English whaleman-te zinga he chungnunna hi khawiah nge a awm tak tak tih chu sawi harsa tak a ni ang, Yankees-ho hian ni khatah, an vaiin, English zawng zawng aiin, an vaiin, kum sawm chhungin whale an that tam zawk tih hmuh a ni. Mahse hei hi English whale-hunter-te tan chuan harmless little foible a ni a, chu chu Nantucketer hian a thinlungah a la vak lo va; amah ngei pawhin chak lohna tlemte a nei tih a hriat vang pawh a ni mai thei.

Chuvângin, tuipuiah lawng hrang hranga kal zawng zawng zîngah chuan, whaller-te chuan inpawh taka awm chhan an nei tam ber tih kan hmu a—chutiang chu an ni. Chuti chung chuan, Atlantic tuipui laihawl vela sumdawng lawng thenkhat chuan an inhnukdawk tawn a, hriatna thumal khat pawh ni lovin, Broadway-a dandies brace ang maiin tuifinriat sang takah chuan an inthlau tawn fo ang leh, engtik lai pawhin, an rig-a finical criticism-ah an inhmang reng mai thei bawk. Men-of-War chungchang erawh chuan tuipuiah an inhmuhna hun remchangah chuan chutiang khawpa bengchheng tak tak bowing leh scrapings, chutiang ducking of ensigns chu an paltlang hmasa thin a, chuvang chuan right-down hearty good-will leh brotherly love tam tak a awm lo niin a lang lo. As touching Slave-ships meeting, engvangin nge, chutiang khawpa hmanhmawhthlak takin an awm a, a rang thei ang bera an tlanchhiatsan ta a ni. Tin, Pirates chungchang erawh chuan, an cross-bones an in cross-bone chance an neih hian, rial tla hmasa ber chu—“Sruak engzat nge?”—whaller-te’n rial an tih ang bawkin—“Barrel engzat nge?” Tin, chu zawhna chu vawi khat chhan a nih chuan, pirate-te chu an inthlau nghal vek a, a chhan chu an sir lehlamah infernal villain an ni a, anmahni villanous likenesses tam lutuk hmuh an duh lo.

Mahse, Pathian ngaihsak, rinawm, inngaitlawm lo, mikhual lawm, midang nena inpawh, zalen leh awlsam whaler hi han en teh! Whaler chuan eng boruak mawi takah pawh whaler dang a hmuh chuan eng nge a tih? “ Gam ” a nei a, chu chu lawng dang zawng zawngin an hriat loh em em a, chu hming pawh an hre ngai lo; tin, accident-a an hriat chuan an nuihzat mai mai a, “spouters” leh “blubber-boilers” chungchang gamesome tak tak an sawi nawn leh a, chutiang chu pretty exclamations ang chi te pawh an sawi nawn leh bawk. Engvangin nge Merchant-seamen zawng zawng te, leh Pirate leh Man-of-War-a mi zawng zawng te, leh Slave-ship sailor zawng zawng te hian Whale-ship-te chunga hetiang rilru na tak hi an ngaihlu em em a; hei hi zawhna chhan harsa tak a ni ang. A chhan chu, pirate-te chungchangah chuan, sawi rawh, chu an hnathawhna chuan ropuina danglam tak a nei em tih hriat ka duh a ni. A châng chuan elevation danglam takah a tawp ṭhin, a dik tak zet a ni; mahse, gallows-ah chauh a ni. Tin, chu bâkah, chutiang thil mak tak maia mipa chu chawisang a nih chuan a sâng zâwkna tûr lungphum mumal tak a nei lo. Chuvangin, ka thutlukna chu, whaleman aia sang anga inchhuangnaah chuan, chu thusawiah chuan pirate hian ding tur nghet tak a nei lo.

Mahse, Gam chu eng nge ni? Dictionary column-a i index-finger tlan chho leh chho chu i hmang zo lo mai thei a, chu thumal chu i hmu ngai lo mai thei. Dr. Johnson-a chuan chu erudition chu a thleng ngai lo; Noah Webster-a lawng chuan a chelh lo. Chuti chung pawh chuan, he thumal sawifiahna tho hi tunah chuan kum tam tak chhung chu Yankees piang dik tak sang sawm leh panga vel zingah hman a ni reng a ni. Tih tak zetin, hrilhfiahna a mamawh a, Lexicon-ah pawh dah tel a ngai a ni. Chu ngaihdan nen chuan zir chhuakin ka sawifiah ang.

GAM a ni. NOUN— Whaleship pahnih ( emaw a aia tam emaw ) awmna social meeting , a tlangpuiin cruising-ground-a awm; rial an inthleng hnua lawnga thawkte tlawhna an inthleng lai chuan: lawng pakhatah chuan captain pahnih chu, chutih hun chhung chu lawng pakhatah an awm a, lawng dangah chuan an ṭhian lian pahnih chu an awm bawk.

Gamming chungchangah hian item tlemte dang a awm a, chu chu hetah hian theihnghilh loh tur a ni. Profession zawng zawng hian a chipchiar zawnga thil danglam bik tlemte an nei vek a; chutiang bawkin whale sangha man pawh a ni. Pirate, man-of-war, sal lawngah chuan, captain chu a lawnga khawi hmunah pawh lawng khalh a nih chuan, chutah chuan seat nuam tak, a chang chuan cushioned-ah chuan stern sheet-ah a thu fo thin a, gay cord leh ribbon hmanga chei milliner’s tiller te tak te mawi tak hmangin a in khalh fo thin. Mahse whale-boat hian seat astern a nei lo va, chutiang sofa pawh a nei lo va, tiller pawh a nei lo. High times indeed, whaling captain te chu patent chair-a thut gouty old aldermen ang maia castor-a tui chungchangah wheel-a khalh ni ta se. Tin, tiller chungchang erawh chuan, whale-boat hian chutiang hmeichhe nihna chu a pawm ngai lo; tin, chuvangin gamming-ah chuan lawng thawktu tling tak chuan lawng chu an chhuahsan tur a ni a, chuvangin lawng khalhtu emaw, harpooneer emaw chu a zat zinga mi a nih angin, chu a hnuaia mi chu a hun hunah chuan khalhtu a ni a, captain chu ṭhutna tur hmun nei lovin, pine thing ang maia ding zawng zawng chu a tlawhnaah hruai chhuah a ni. Tin, vawi tam tak chu lawng pahnih sir atanga khawvel hmuh theih zawng zawng mit a chunga innghat chu hre rengin, he ding captain hi a ke vawng reng chunga a zahawmna vawn nun a pawimawhziaah a nung vek tih i hmu bawk ang. Hei pawh hi thil awlsam tak a ni lo; a hnung lamah chuan a hnungzang te tak teah chuan steering oar lian tak projecting tak chuan a rawn deng a, a hnunglam oar chuan a hma lamah a khup rap-in a rawn let leh thin. Chutiang chuan a hma leh hnung lamah a inkhung zo vek a, a ke hrual chungah chuan a insiamrem chauhvin a sir lamah a inzai thei a; mahse, lawng ri rang tak, tharum thawhna nasa tak chuan a tikehsawm turin hla takah a kal fo ang, a chhan chu lungphum sei zawng chu a zau zawng inang lo chuan engmah a ni lo. Pole pahnih spread angle siam mai la, i ding thei lo. Tichuan, hetah pawh hian khawvel mit riveted tak takte hmuh theih lohvin a ti ngai lo vang, he straddling captain hi a kut hmanga eng thil pawh man a, a particle tlemte pawh a insiamrem lai hmuh a nih chuan a ti ngai lo vang, ka ti a dik tak chuan, a self-command zawng zawng, buoyant tak chhinchhiahna atan, a tlangpuiin a kut chu a trowsers pocket-ah a keng a; mahse, a tlangpuiin kut lian tak, rit tak a nih avangin ballast atan a phur a ni mai thei. Chuti chung pawh chuan thil thleng a awm a, a dik tihdikna pawh a awm a, chutah chuan captain chu hun pawimawh lo tak tak emaw, hun hnih emaw chhung atan hriat a ni tawh a, chu chu rang takin thlipui tleh ang maiin sawi rawh—a hnai ber lawng khalhtu sam chu man a, chutah chuan thihna rapthlak tak ang maia chelh tlat a ni.





BUNG 54. Khawpui-Ho chanchin.

( Golden Inn-a an sawi angin. )

Cape of Good Hope, leh a chhehvela tui awmna hmun zawng zawngte chu kawngpui lian tak kil li hriat hlawh thenkhat ang deuh a ni a, chutah chuan hmun dang zawng aiin khualzin tam zawk i hmu thin.

Goney tawng an sawi hnu rei vak lovah chuan in lam pan tur whaleman dang, Town-Ho* chu an tawk ta a. Amah hi Polynesian-hoin an thawk vek tawh mai. A hnua gam tawi lo awmah chuan Moby Dick chungchang thu chiang tak min pe a. Mi thenkhat tan chuan White Whale ngaihvenna tlangpui chu tunah chuan Town-Ho chanchin dinhmun pakhat chuan nasa takin a ti sang ta a, chu chuan whale nen hian Pathian rorelna an tih zinga pakhat tlawhna mak tak, inherted tak, a chang chuan mi thenkhatte hneh nia sawi chu a inzawm tlat niin a lang. He dinhmun hnuhnung zawk hi, amah ngeiin a zuitu bikte nen, sawi tur lungchhiatthlak tak thuruk tia sawi theih chu a siam a, Captain Ahaba emaw, a kawppuite emaw beng a thleng ngai lo. Chu thawnthu thuruk chu Town-Ho captain ngei pawhin a hre lo si a. Chu lawnga confederate white seamen pathumte mimal thil a ni a, an zinga pakhat chuan, Romish injunctions of secrecy hmangin Tashtego hnenah a hrilh niin a lang a, mahse a tuk zan chuan Tashtego chu a muhil laiin a ramble a, chutiang chuan a tam lutuk a, a harh chuan a bak zawng chu a thup thei lo. Chuti chung pawh chuan, he thil hian Pequod-a tuipui lama mi, a hriatna famkim lo thlengte chungah nghawng nasa tak a nei a, chutiang thil tui tak mak tak maiin, chutianga sawi tur chuan, he thila rorel an nih chuan, chu thuruk chu Pequod-a main-mast hnung lamah a lo lang ngai lo turin anmahni zingah an vawng tlat a ni. A hmun dik takah he thread thim zawk hi lawnga vantlang hriata sawi angin thawnthu nen inzawmkhawmin, he thil mak tak zawng zawng hi tunah chuan record nghet takah ka dah ta a ni.

*Hmanlai whale-cry chu mast-head atanga whale hmuh hmasak ber a ni a, whaleman ten Gallipagos terrapin hmingthang tak an man nan an la hmang reng a ni.

Ka hlimthla atan chuan, vawi khat chu Lima-a ka sawi dan style kha ka vawng reng ang, ka Spanish ṭhiante lounging circle, mi thianghlim pakhat hma lawk, Golden Inn-a thick-gilt tiled piazza-a meizuk lai hnenah. Chu cavalier tha tak tak zingah chuan Dons naupang, Pedro leh Sebastian te chu ka bulah an inkawp tlat a; tin, chuta ṭang chuan zawhna inkara an zawh ṭhin, chutih laia dik taka chhân ṭhinte pawh a awm a ni.

“Ka hriat hmasak ber tur thilthleng ka hriat hma kum hnih vel khan, mi felte u, Town-Ho, Nantucket khuaa Sperm Whaler chu hetah hian in Pacific-ah hian a chuang a, he Golden Inn tha tak eaves atang hian ni tam tak chhungin hmar lam pan lovin a kal a. Line chhim lam khawi emaw laiah a awm a. Zing khat chu pumps a enkawl lai hian, nitin hman danah chuan tui a siam tam zawk tih hmuhchhuah a ni in her hold than common khaw lum leh khaw vawt chuan lawng chuan a la zin chhunzawm zel a, lawng khalhtute chuan pump-ahte chuan hun rei tak leh awlsam takin hna an thawk a, mahse ni tam zawk chu vanneihna a lo thleng ta lo va, chu chu a leak chu hmuhchhuah a la ni lo mai ni lovin, a tipung ta hle a, tunah chuan alarm engemaw zat la in, captain chuan, a zawng zawng chu lawng chawlhna hmunah chuan a ding chhuak ta a ni siamthat a ni.

“A hmaah chuan kawngpui tenau lo awm lo mah se, chuti chung pawh chuan, chance tlanglawn berin a duhsak a nih chuan, a lawng chu a dintu tur a ni tih a hlau lo tak zet a ni, a chhan chu a pump-te chu a tha ber zinga mi a nih avangin, a hun hunah chuan a rilru a hahdam a, chu a mipa paruk leh sawmthum chuan awlsam takin lawng chu a zalen thei a; never mind if the leak should double on her.
