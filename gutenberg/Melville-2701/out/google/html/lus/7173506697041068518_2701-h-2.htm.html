BUNG 4. Counterpane tih hi a ni.

A tuk zingah ni êng lam ka harh chuan Queequeg-a kut chu hmangaihna leh duhawm berin ka chungah a rawn vawrh chhuak tih ka hmu a. A nupui ka ni tawh tih i lo ngaihtuah tawh mai. Counterpane chu patchwork a ni a, odd little parti-coloured square leh triangle-in a khat a; tin, he a kut hi a pum puiah hian Cretan labyrinth tawp thei lo, figure pakhata tattoo a ni a, a hmun hnih chu a hmêl ang chiah chiah a ni lo—a kut chu tuipuiah ni leh êng hnuaiah hmanraw awm lo taka a vawn tlat vang niin ka hria, a kawrfual kawrfual chu hun hrang hrangah mumal lo takin a inbel a—he kut vek hian, ka sawi dan chuan, chu patchwork quilt pakhata strip ang maiin khawvel zawng zawng a zawng a ni. Dik tak chuan, ka harh hmasak ber ṭum ang bawkin a chungah chuan a ṭhenin a mu a, quilt aṭang chuan ka hre thei lo, an rawng chu an inzawm khawm nasa hle a; tin, ka rit leh pressure hriatna atang chauhvin Queequeg-a’n min kuah tih ka hre thei bawk.

Ka sensations chu a mak khawp mai. Chungte chu sawifiah ka tum ang u. Ka naupan lai chuan ka chunga thil thleng ang chiah chiah hi ka hre chiang hle a; thil tak tak emaw, mumang emaw pawh ni se, ka insiamrem vek thei ngai lo. Thil awmdan chu hetiang hi a ni. Caper emaw, a dang emaw ka lo cut tawh a—ni engemaw zat kalta a sweep tlemte tih ka hmuh tawh ang khan chimney-ah chuan tlan chhoh tum niin ka hria; leh ka nupui upa zawk, engtin emaw tak emaw, zanriah ei lovin khumah min tir reng thin,—ka nu chuan chimney atang chuan ka ke atangin min hruai chhuak a, khumah min phur chhuak a, mahse, June ni 21 chawhnu dar hnih chauh a ni a, chu chu kan hemisphere-a kum khat chhunga ni rei ber a ni. Hlauthawng takin ka inhria. Mahse, chutiang tur chuan tanpuina a awm lo va, stairs-ah chuan ka room te tak te, third floor-ah chuan ka kal a, hun tihlum nan a theih ang anga zawi zawiin ka inbel a, thawk hahdam tak nen sheet inkarah chuan ka lut ta a.

Thawhlehna ka beisei hmain darkar sawm leh paruk pum pui a liam a ngai tih chu lungngai takin ka mu a. Darkar sawm leh paruk chhung khumah! ka hnungzang te tak te chu ngaihtuah chuan a na hle. Tin, a êng lutuk bawk a; window-a ni eng rawn chhuak, leh kawngpuiah coach ri nasa tak, leh in chhung zawng zawnga gay aw ri te. Ka rilru a na zual sauh sauh a—a tawpah chuan ka tho a, ka inchei a, ka ke stocking-ah chuan zawi zawiin ka chhuk a, ka nu upa zawk chu ka zawng a, rang takin a ke bulah ka inhnukdawk a, ka nungchang tha lo avanga slippering tha tak min pe turin thilpek bik angin ka ngen a eng thil pawh ni se, chutiang hun rei tak tuar theih loh khawpa dawt sawi tura min thiam loh chantirna. Mahse, stepmother zingah chuan a tha ber leh conscientious ber a ni a, back ka room-ah ka kal a ngai ta a ni. Darkar engemaw zat chu ka muhil a, chuta ṭang chuan ka tih tawh zawng zawng aiin nasa takin ka hre lo va, a hnu lama vanduaina lian ber ber aṭang pawhin. A tawpah chuan mutthilh lai mumang buai takah ka tlu ta ngei ang; tin, zawi zawiin chuta tang chuan ka harh chhuak a—mumang chanve vel chu ka mit ka meng a, ni êng hmaa pindan chu tunah chuan pawn lam thimah chuan a khuh ta a ni. Instantly ka frame zawng zawngah chuan shock pakhat a tlan chhuak tih ka hria a; engmah hmuh tur a awm lo va, engmah hriat tur a awm lo; mahse, ka kutah chuan thiltihtheihna mak tak mai chu dah niin a lang. Ka kut chu counterpane chungah a innghat a, hming nei lo, ngaihtuah theih loh, ngawi renga awm form emaw phantom emaw, kut chu a ta chu ka khum bulah chuan a thu hnai hle niin a lang. Kum tam tak a lo awm tawh ang maia lang chu, hlauhna rapthlak ber berte nen chuan ka muhil a, ka kut chu ka hrual ngam lo va; mahse, inch khat lek pawh ka hrual thei a nih chuan, chu spell rapthlak tak chu a chhe vek ang tih ka ngaihtuah fo. He hriatna hi a tawpah chuan engtin nge ka hnen atangin a tlanbo ta tih ka hre lo; mahse, zing ka harh chuan khur chungin ka hrechhuak vek a, chu mi hnu ni leh kar leh thla tam tak chhung chu thuruk sawifiah tumna buaithlak takah ka bo ta a ni. Ni lo, tun darkar thleng hian ka inpuzzle fo thin.

Tunah chuan, hlauhna rapthlak tak chu la bo la, ka kut chhunga thil mak tak tak ka hriatna chu, an mak takah chuan, ka harh a, Queequeg-a pathian dang betu kut min rawn vawrh ka hmuh khan ka tawn tawh nen chuan a inang hle a ni. Mahse a tawpah chuan zan kalta lama thil thleng zawng zawng chu fimkhur takin a lo thleng leh ta a, pakhat hnu pakhatin, thil tak takah chuan, chutah chuan harsatna nuihzatthlak takah chuan ka muhil ta chauh a ni. A kut chu tihchet tum mah ila—a mo neitu kawrfual chu hawnsak ka tum a—mahse, a mut lai chuan, thihna tih loh chu engmahin min then darh lo ang maiin min la kuah nghet tlat si a. Tunah chuan ka tiharh tumin ka bei ta a—“Queequeg!”—mahse a chhanna awm chhun chu bengchheng mai mai a ni. Chutah ka inher a, ka kawr chu sakawr kawrfual chhunga awm ang maiin ka hria a; tin, rang takin a hrual deuh tih a hria. Counterpane chu paih bovin, chutah chuan tomahawk chu savawm bulah chuan a mu a, hatchet-faced naute ang maiin a mu a. A pretty pickle, dik tak chuan, ka ti a; abed here in a strange house in the broad day, mi ei thin leh tomahawk nen! “Queequeg!—thatna hmingin, Queequeg, harh rawh!” A tawpah chuan, chutiang nupa nunphung style-a a mipa pui pakhat a kuah chu a dik lo tih sawi chhuahna ring tak leh tawp lo tak tak hmangin, grunt ka la chhuak thei ta a tin, tunah chuan a kut chu a hnungtawlh a, tui atanga lo chhuak chauh Newfoundland ui ang maiin a taksa zawng zawngah a inbeng kang a, khumah chuan a thu a, pike-staff ang maia nghet takin min en a, chutah chuan ka awm dan chu a hrechhuak vek lo ang maiin a mit a rawn hrual a, mahse ka chanchin hriatna thim tak chu zawi zawiin a chungah a rawn lang chhuak ta a ni. Chutih lai chuan ngawi rengin ka mitmeng a, tunah chuan rinhlelhna lian tak ka nei tawh lo va, chutiang thilsiam hriat duh tak chu narrowly observing-ah ka innghat ta a. A tâwpah chuan a rilru chu a mutpui mizia khawih tûra siam a nih ang maia a lan a, thil awmzia nên a inrem ta ang maiin; a zuang chhuak a, chhinchhiahna leh thawm thenkhat hmangin, ka lawm chuan a inchei hmasa ang a, a hnuah inchei turin min kalsan ang a, apartment pumpui chu keimah chauhvin ka dah mai ang tih ka hrethiam ta a ni. Thinks I, Queequeg, dinhmun hnuaiah chuan hei hi civilized overture tak a ni; mahse, thudik tak chu, heng savawmte hian innate sense of delicacy an nei a, i duh angin sawi rawh; an nihna takah chuan an zahawmzia chu mak tak a ni. Queequeg-a hi he fakna bik hi ka pe a, a chhan chu, mi rilru na tak leh ngaihsakna nasa tak nen min enkawl a, chutih lai chuan zah lohna nasa tak ka nei a; khum atang chuan a melh reng a, a toilette che vel zawng zawng chu a en reng bawk; for the time ka hriat duhna chuan ka breeding a ti chak zawk a ni. Chuti chung pawh chuan Queequeg ang mipa nitin i hmuh ngai loh chu, amah leh a kalphung chu ngaihtuah tur danglam tak a ni.

A chung berah chuan a beaver kawr, sang tak tak, by the by-ah ha chungin a inchei tan a, chutah—a kawrfual chu a la paih chhuak reng a—a boot chu a zawng chhuak ta a ni. Van hnuaiah eng atan nge a tih tih chu ka hre thei lo, mahse a thil tih leh tur chu a inhnim hrual a ni—a kutah boot a keng a, kawr a ha bawk a—khum hnuaiah a inhnim hrual a; chutih lai chuan, sundry violent gaspings and strainings atang chuan, amah leh amah boot-a thawkrim tak a ni tih ka infer a; mahse, ka hriat ngai loh dan dik lo engmahin mipa tu pawhin a kekawr bul a hak hunah mimal taka awm a ngai lo. Mahse Queequeg hi, i hmu em, transition stage-a thilsiam a ni a—caterpillar emaw butterfly emaw pawh a ni lo. A civilized tawk chauh a, a outlandishness chu mak tak maiin a lantir thei chauh a ni. A zirna pawh a la zo lo. Undergraduate a ni a. Degree tlemte civilized lo ni lo se chuan boots hmangin a buai lo tak tak lo mai thei; mahse, chutih lai chuan, savawm tak a la ni lo a nih chuan, khum hnuaia luh chu a mumang pawh a ni lo vang. A tâwpah chuan a kawrfual chu a hrual nasa hle a, a mit chungah a tla thla a, pindan chhungah chuan a bengchheng hle a, a bengchheng ta hle a, boot a thiam vak loh avangin, a bawng vun hnim leh hnim hring pahnih—thupek anga siam pawh a ni lo mai thei—zing khaw vawt tak atanga a chhuah hmasak ber ṭumin a rawn pinched leh torment zawk ang maiin.

Tunah chuan, window-ah chuan curtain a awm lo tih leh kawngpui chu a zim hle tih a hmuh chuan, a hma lam in chuan pindan chhungah chuan chiang takin a hmu thei a, Queequeg-a siam mipa mawi lo tak chu a en zual sauh a, a lukhum leh a kekawr hak tih loh chu thil dang tlemte chauh ha chungin a tlan kual vel a;.. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. Ka theih ang tawkin ka ngen a, a toilet chu eng emaw chen ti chak turin ka ngen a, a bik takin a pantaloon-ah a rang thei ang bera lut turin ka ngen a. A zawm a, chutah chuan a inbual ta a ni. Chutih lai chuan zîngah Kristian tu pawhin a hmai an sil ang; mahse, Queequeg-a chuan mak ka ti hle a, a inbualna chu a rilru, a kut leh a kut chauha tihkhawtlai maiah a lungawi ta a ni. Chutah a kawrfual chu a ha a, wash-stand center table-a sahbawn khauh tak chu a la a, tuiah a thun a, a hmai chu a lather tan ta a. A razor dahna hmun chu ka en reng a, lo and behold, khum kil atang chuan harpoon chu a la chhuak a, thing stock sei tak chu a hlip chhuak a, a lu chu a phelh a, a boot-ah tlem a whet a, bang-a darthlalang bit tak thleng chuan a ke a pen chho a, chak takin a hrual tan a, a nih loh leh a hmui a harpoon tan ta a ni. Thinks I, Queequeg, hei hi Rogers-a cutlery tha ber berte chu phuba lakna nen a hmang a ni. Chumi hnuah chuan he operation-ah hian harpoon lu chu eng thir tha tak nge siam tih ka hriat chuan ka ngaihtuah tlem zawk a, a sir sei tak dinglam chu engtiang chiahin nge a hrual lutuk tih ka hriat chuan.

A toilet dang zawng zawng chu a tihlawhtling thuai a, chhuang takin pindan atang chuan a chhuak ta a, a pilot monkey jacket ropui tak chu a khuh a, a harpoon chu marshal baton ang maiin a sport a.





BUNG 5. Chawhmeh.

Ka zui nghal vat a, bar-room chhunga chhuk chuan in neitu nuihzatthlak tak chu nuam takin ka rawn bei ta a. Ka bedfellow chungchangah hian tlemte pawh ni lovin min skylarking tawh nachungin a chungah hian malice engmah ka cherish lo.

Mahse, nuih ṭhat chu thil ṭha chak tak a ni a, thil ṭha tlem lutuk a ni zawk; a tam zawk chu a khawngaihthlak zawk. Chuvangin, mi pakhatin, a nihna takah chuan, tu hnenah pawh fiamthu tha tak atan thil a lei thei a nih chuan, hnungtawlh lovin, hlim takin chutianga sum hman leh hman chu phalsak rawh se. Tin, a chunga nuihzatthlak tak tak nei mipa chuan, chu mipaah ​​chuan i ngaihtuah aia tam a awm tih hria ang che.

Tunah chuan bar-room chu a hma zan lama boarder lo tla tawh, tun thlenga ka la en chian loh boarder-te chuan an khat ta vek a. Whaleman zawng zawng deuhthaw an ni a; kawppui lian ber ber te, kawppui pahnihna te, kawppui pathumna te, tuifinriat thingrem siamtu te, tuifinriat siamtu te, tuifinriata dar siamtu te, harpooneer te, lawng vengtute te; company dum leh brawny tak, hmul bosky nei; an unshorn, shaggy set, zing kawr hak tur chuan monkey jacket an ha vek.

Eng chen nge an awm tawh tih chu chiang takin i hre thei. He tlangval hmui hrisel tak hi ni-a toasted pear in hue ang mai a ni a, a rim pawh a musky ang maiin a lang ang; India rama a zin aṭang khan ni thum zet a la thla thei lo. A bula mipa chu shade tlemte a light zawkin a lang a; satin thing touch khat pawh a chhungah a awm i ti mai thei. Pathumna vun rawng-ah chuan tropic tawn a la awm reng a, mahse a bleach deuh withal; kar tam tak chhung chu tuipuiah a awm tawh tih rinhlelh rual a ni lo. Mahse, Queequeg ang hmui chu tuin nge lantir thei ang? chu chu tint hrang hranga khar a ni a, Andes chhim lam tlang chhip ang maiin a lang a, array khata lang tur, boruak inang lo tak tak, zone khat hnu zone khata inthlauhna.

“Grub, ho!” tunah chuan in neitu chuan a ti a, kawngkhar pakhat a hawng a, chawhmeh ei turin kan lut ta a.

Khawvel hmu tawh mipa te chu, chutiang chuan an awm danah an hlim hle a, company-ah pawh mahni inngaitlawm takin an awm ta niin an sawi. Mahse, a ni ngai lo: New England khualzin ropui Ledyard leh Scotch khualzin Mungo Park te; mipa zawng zawng zingah chuan parlor-ah chuan rintlakna tlem ber an nei a ni. Mahse, Ledyard-a ang bawka ui-te’n an hruai sledge-a Siberia ram kan pelh mai mai emaw, Africa ram negro thinlunga pum ruak chunga mahni chauha kal rei tak mai emaw pawh a ni mai thei, chu chu Mungo rethei tak takte performance zawng zawng zatve a ni—hetiang zinkawng hi, ka sawi dan chuan, social polish sang tak neih theihna kawng ṭha ber a ni lo mai thei. Mahse, a tam zawkah chuan chutiang thil chu khawi hmunah pawh neih tur a ni.

Hetah chauh hian heng ngaihtuahnate hi dawhkan bula kan thut vek hnua, whale man chungchanga thawnthu tha tak tak ngaihthlak ka inbuatsaih hnua thil awmdan avanga lo awm a ni a mak ka tih ber chu mipa zawng zawng deuhthaw chuan ngawi rengin thuk takin an awm a. Tin, chu mai ni lovin, an mualpho hmel hle bawk. Ni e, hetah hian tuifinriat ui set khat an awm a, an zinga tam tak chuan zak lo mah se tuifinriat sang takah chuan whale lian tak tak—an hriat loh tak tak—chuan an chuang a, mitmeng tichhe lovin thiin an duel a tin, chuti chung pawh chuan, hetah hian social breakfast table-ah an ṭhu a—kohna inang vek, an duh zawng inang vek—Green Mountains zînga berâm huan ṭhenkhat hmuh theih loh ang maiin berâm ang maiin an in en kual a. Thil mak tak mai; heng bear zakzum tak takte hi, heng warrior whalemen zakzum tak takte hi!

Mahse Queequeg chungchang erawh chu—engvangin nge, Queequeg chu an zingah chuan a thu a—dawhkan lu lamah pawh, chutiang chuan a thleng ta a; icicle ang maia lum. Dik tak chuan a chi thlah dan chungchangah hian thil tam tak ka sawi thei lo. A ngaihsan ber chuan a harpoon chu chawhmeh einaah a rawn keng tel a, chutah chuan inkhawmpui awm lovin a hmang a, chu chu thinlung takin a dik thei lo va; chu chuan dawhkan chungah chuan a kut a phar a, lu tam tak hlauhawmna lo thleng tur thlengin, bawnghnute chu a lam hawiin a rawn man ta a. Mahse chu chu a thil tih cool tak a ni ngei ang, mi tam zawk ngaihdanah chuan eng thil pawh cool taka tih chu genteelly tih a ni tih mi zawng zawngin an hria.

Hetah hian Queequeg-a thil danglam bik zawng zawng hi kan sawi lo vang; coffee leh hot rolls a pumpelh dan te, a ngaihvenna insem loh chu beefsteaks, done rare-a a hman dan te. Enough, chawhmeh ei zawh chuan midang ang bawkin vantlang pindan chhungah a inhnukdawk a, a tomahawk-pipe chu a hal a, chutah chuan ngawi rengin a kawr inthen theih loh bun chungin a digesting leh meizuk lai chuan a thu a, chutih lai chuan ka sallied out for a stroll.





BUNG 6. Kawngpui.

Khawpui civilized society zahawm tak zingah Queequeg ang mimal mak tak mai chu a tir lamah chuan mak ka ti hle a nih chuan, chu mak tihna chu New Bedford khawpui kawngpuiah ni ênga ka kal hmasak ber ṭumin a bo ta thuai a ni.

Dock bula kawngpuiah chuan, tuipui kama lawng chawlhna hmun lian tak tak eng pawhin ram dang hmun aṭanga queerest looking nondescripts en theihna a pe fo ang. Broadway leh Chestnut kawngpuiah pawh Mediterranean tuipui lama thawkte chuan hmeichhe hlauthawng tak takte chu an nawr fo ṭhin. Regent Street hi Lascar leh Malay-ho hriat loh a ni lo va; tin, Apollo Green-a Bombay-ah chuan live Yankees-te chuan ram mipuite chu an ti hlauhawm fo bawk. Mahse New Bedford hian Water Street leh Wapping zawng zawng an hneh vek. Heng haunts sawi hnuhnung berteah hian lawng khalhtute chauh i hmu a; mahse New Bedford-ah chuan mihring ei thin tak takte chu kawngpui sir lamah an ding a; savages te chu a chhuak vek a; tam tak chuan an ruhte chu tisa thianghlim lo an la keng reng a ni. Mi hriat loh hmel zetin a en tir a.

Mahse, Feegeeans, Tongatobooarrs, Erromanggoans, Pannangians, leh Brighggians te bakah, leh, kawngpui lam hawia reel ngaihsak lo whaling-craft-a ramsa specimen bakah hian, thil hmuhnawm dang hmuh tur dang i hmu ang a, a la mak zawk, a nuihzatthlak zawk ngei ang. Kar tin he khawpuiah hian Vermont leh New Hampshire mipa hring tam tak an lo thleng a, an vai hian sangha man kawngah hlawkna leh ropuina an tuihal vek a ni. A tam zawk chu naupang, frame nghet tak nei an ni a; fellow-te chuan ramngaw an tikehsawm tawh a, tunah chuan axe thlak a, whale-lance chu laksak an tum a ni. Mi tam tak chu an lo chhuahna Green Mountains ang maia green an ni. Thil thenkhatah chuan i ngaihtuah ang mahse darkar tlemte chauh a liam tawh. Chutah chuan han en teh! chu chap chu a strutting round a. Beaver kawr leh swallow-tailed coat a ha a, sailor-belt leh sheath-knife a vuah bawk. Hetah hian sou’-wester leh bombazine puan keng dang a rawn lang leh a.

Khawpui chhunga piang dandy tu mahin ram chhunga piang dandy nen tehkhin tur an awm lo—downright bumpkin dandy ka sawina a ni—a fellow, dog-day-ah chuan a kut tanna hlauvin a acre hnih chu buckskin gloves-ah a sawh ang. Tunah chuan hetiang ram dandy hian hmingthang tak siam turin a lu-ah a la lut a, whale-fishery ropui takah a tel ve chuan tuipui kama a thlen veleh thil nuihzatthlak tak tak a tih thinte chu i hmu tur a ni. A sea-outfit sawinaah chuan a kawrfual-ah bell-button a order a; a canvas trowser-ah chuan a strap a. Ah, Hay-Seed rethei tak chu! eng angin nge chu kawrfual chu thlipui tleh hmasa berah chuan a puak darh ang, thlipui tleh karah, kawrfual, button leh a zawng zawng, i khalh lai chuan.

Mahse, he khawpui hmingthang tak hian a tlawhtute hmuhtirna tur harpooneer, cannibal leh bumpkin chauh a nei tih hi ngaihtuah suh. Ni miah lo. Still New Bedford hi hmun queer tak a ni. Keini whaleman-te hi ni lo ila chuan, chu ram zau tak chu tûn laiah hian Labrador tuipui kamah au thawm ang maiin a awm mai thei. A nihna takah chuan a hnunglam ram hmun thenkhat chu mi pakhat ti hlauhawm turin a tawk a, ruh ang maiin an lang. Khawpui ngei pawh hi New England ram zawng zawngah chuan chenna hmun duh ber a ni mai thei. Hriak ram a ni a, a dik tawk a ni: mahse Kanaan angin a ni lo; ram, buh leh uain pawh a awm bawk. Kawngpuiah hian bawnghnute a tlan lo; thlasik lai pawhin artui thar hmangin an pave ngai lo. Mahse, chuti chung pawh chuan America ram pumah khawiah mah patrician ang chi in hi i hmu lo vang; park leh huan hausa zawk, New Bedford aiin. Khawi atanga lo kal nge an nih? engtin nge ram pakhata he vawi khata scraggy scoria chungah hian phun?

Kal la, chuta in sang tak vêla thir emblematical harpoon-te chu en la, i zawhna chu chhan a ni ang. Awle; heng in huaisen tak tak leh pangpar huan zawng zawng hi Atlantic, Pacific leh Indian tuipui atanga lo chhuak a ni. Pakhat leh pakhat, harpoon hmangin tuifinriat hnuai atang hian hetah hian an hruai chho ta a ni. Herr Alexander hian chutiang thil ropui tak chu a ti thei ang em?

New Bedford-ah chuan pa te chuan an fanute hnenah dower atan whale an pe a, an nieces te chu porpoise tlemte a-piece hmangin an portion off niin an sawi bawk. Inneihna ropui tak hmuh tur chuan New Bedford-ah i kal a ngai a; in tinah hriak dahkhawmna hmun an nei a, zan tin ngaihtuahna fim lo takin an sei zawng chu spermaceti meialh-ah an hal thin niin an sawi a ni.

Thlasik lai chuan khawpui hi hmuh a thlum a; maple ṭha tak takin a khat—green leh rangkachak hmanga siam kawngpui sei tak takte. Tin, August thlaah chuan boruak sang takah chuan, sakawr-chestnut mawi leh tam tak, candelabra ang maia awmte chuan, kal peltu hnenah an pangpar inzawmkhawm, tapering upright cones an pe a. Chutiang chuan engkim tithei chu art a ni; chu chuan New Bedford district tam takah chuan thilsiam ni hnuhnung bera paih chhuah, bawlhhlawh lung hring hring chungah pangpar terrace eng tak takte chu a superinduced a ni.
