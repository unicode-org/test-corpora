BUNG 22. Krismas hlim takin hmang rawh u.

A tawpah chuan chawhnu lam velah chuan lawng rigger-te hnawhchhuahna hnuhnung ber a nih hnuah, leh Pequod chu lawng chawlhna atanga an chhuah hnuah, leh ngaihtuahna seng reng Charity chu whale-boat-ah a lo chhuk hnuah, a thilpek hnuhnung ber nen—Stubb, a kawppui pahnihna, a pasalṭha, leh a enkawltu tan zan lama kawrfual—hei zawng zawng hnuah, Captain pahnih, Peleg leh Bildad, cabin atanga chhuak, chief mate lam hawi chuan Peleg chuan a ti a:

“Tûnah chuan, Mr. Starbuck, engkim a dik tih i chiang em? Captain Ahab chu a inpeih vek tawh—a biak mai mai a ni—tuipui aṭanga hmuh tur dang a awm lo, eh? Awle, kut zawng zawng ko rawh, chuti a nih chuan. Muster ’em aft here—blast ’em!”

Bildad-a chuan, “Thu bawlhhlawh tak tak sawi a ngai lo, eng ang pawhin hmanhmawh mah se, Peleg, mahse, kal bo rawh, ṭhian Starbuck, kan thupek ti rawh,” a ti a.

Tunah chuan engtin nge! Tin, Captain Ahaba erawh chu a chhinchhiahna engmah hmuh tur a la awm lo; chauh, cabin chhungah a awm tih an sawi. Mahse, chutih lai chuan, ngaihtuahna chu, lawng chu a rit zawng tihtlem nan leh tuipuiah a hruai chhuah theih nan a awmna chu engti kawng mahin a ngai lo tih hi a ni. Dik tak chuan, chu chu a thil tih dik tak a nih loh avangin, pilot hna a ni zawk a; tin, a la dam kim loh avangin—chutiang chuan an ti a—chuvangin, Captain Ahaba chu a hnuaiah a awm ta a ni. Tin, heng zawng zawng hi thil awmdan pangngai angin a lang a; a bik takin sumdawng rawngbawlnaah chuan captain tam tak chuan anchor an chawisan hnuah hun rei tak chhung chu deck-ah an inlan ngai lo va, cabin dawhkan chungah an awm reng a, an tuipui kama ṭhiante nen farewell merry-making an nei a, chutah chuan pilot nen lawng chu a tawp thlengin an chhuahsan ta a ni.

Mahse, chu thil chu ngaihtuah theihna hun remchang a awm lo, tunah chuan Captain Peleg chu a dam vek tawh a ni. Thusawi leh thupek tam zawk chu a thawk niin a lang a, Bildad-a ni lovin.

“Hetah hian, nangni bachelor fapate u,” a ti a, lawnga chuangte chu main-mast-ah an awm rei hle. “Mr. Starbuck, khalh rawh u.”

“Chutah chuan puan in chu vuak rawh!”— tih chu a hnuaia thupek a ni. Ka sawi tawh ang khan he whalebone marquee hi port-ah chauh lo chuan an pitch ngai lo; tin, Pequod lawngah chuan kum sawmthum chhung zet chu puan in vuak tura thupek chu anchor chawi sang tur a nih thu hriat lar tak a ni.

“Man the capstan! Thisen leh rial!—zump!”—chu thupek dang a ni a, lawnga thawkte chu handspike zawng turin an tlan phei ta a.

Tunah chuan getting under weigh-ah chuan pilot-in a luah tlangpui station chu lawng hmalam a ni. Tin, hetah hian Bildad-a, Peleg-a nen pawh ni se, a officer dangte bakah, lawng chawlhna hmuna pilot phalna nei zinga mi a ni tih hriat a ni—a ngaihtuah lawng zawng zawngte tana Nantucket pilot-fee a humhim theih nan pilot-ah a insiam nia rinhlelh a ni a, a chhan chu lawng dang a khalh ngai lo a ni—Bildad-a chu, ka sawi dan chuan, tunah chuan a rawn hnaih tur lawngte thlir chungin active takin a inhmang tih hmuh a ni mai thei anchor, leh hun engemaw chen chu fakna lungphun lungchhiatthlak tak ang maia hla sain, Booble Alley-a hmeichhe naupangte chungchang chorus chi khat, thinlung taka duhsakna nena a rawn ri chhuak thin windlass-a kut chu lawmpui turin. Chuti chung pawh chuan, ni thum kalta pawh ni lovin, Bildad-a chuan Pequod-ah hian hla bawlhhlawh tak tak, a bik takin under weigh-a luh phal a nih loh thu a lo hrilh tawh a tin, a farnu Charity-i chuan tuipui kama chuang tinte berth-ah Watts copy tlemte duhthlan tur a dah bawk.

Chutih lai chuan lawng hmun dang enkawltu chuan Captain Peleg chuan a rip a, a hnunglam chu hlauhawm berin a tiam a. Anchor a din hmain lawng chu a pil tir mai ang tih ka ngaihtuah mai mai a; duh loh zawngin ka handspike-ah chuan ka chawl a, Queequeg-a chu chutiang bawka ti turin ka hrilh a, kan pahnih hian kan tlanchhiatna tur hlauhawmte chu ngaihtuah chungin, pilot tan chutiang diabola nen chuan lawng kan tan tir a. Mahse, Pathian ngaihsak Bildad-aah chuan chhandamna engemaw chen hmuh theih a ni ang tih ngaihtuahnain ka in thlamuan a, a lay zasarih leh sawmsarih leh pasarih ni mah se; ka hnung lamah rang taka vuakna na tak ka hriat chuan, ka hawi let leh lai chuan, ka bula awm hnai tak atanga a ke lak chhuah tum Captain Peleg-a inlarna chu ka hlauthawng hle. Krismas tawi tak, vawt tak a ni a; tin, hmar lam ni tawi chu zanah a inzawm khawm a, thlasik tuifinriat chungah chuan kan zau deuh hlek a, chu tuifinriat vawt tak chuan ice-ah min khuh a, ralthuam thianghlim ang maiin. Living green inthuam chungin ding rawh.
Chutichuan Judate hnenah chuan Kanaan hlui chu a ding a;
Jordan chu a inkar ah a roll lai chuan.”

Chu thu thlum tak takte chu chutih lai aia thlum zawk chu ka tan chuan a ri ngai lo. Beiseina leh rah chhuahin an khat a ni. Atlantic tuipui kamah hian thlasik zan lum tak karah pawh hian, ka ke hnim leh jacket hnim zawk ka neih lai pawhin, chutih lai chuan ka ngaihdan chuan, hmun nuam tak tak tam tak a la awm reng a leh meads leh glades te chu chatuan atan vernal takin a awm a, chuvang chuan thlasikin a kah chhuah thlai, a ke pen loh, a hmin loh chu thlasik lai takah a awm reng a ni.

A tawpah chuan chutiang offing chu kan nei ta a, pilot pahnih chu an mamawh tawh lo. Kan zuitu sail-boat nghet tak chu a sir lamah chuan a rawn tlan tan ta a.

Hetih lai hian Peleg leh Bildad-a te chu engtin nge an nghawng tih chu thil mak tak leh lawmawm lo tak a ni lo, a bik takin Captain Bildad-a chu. Kalsan chu a huat em em a, mahse; chhuahsan a huat em em a, a ṭha zawngin, chutiang khawpa rei leh hlauhawm taka zin lawng—thlipui tleh Cape pahnih kal tlang; lawng pakhat, a thawhrimna dollar sang tam tak dahna; lawng, lawngpui hlui pakhat captain anga a kalna; amah ang bawka upa mi pakhat, vawi khat chu khawngaihna nei lo hmui hlauhawm zawng zawng chu a tawk leh ta a; thil pakhat, kawng tinrênga a ngaihven zawng zawnga khat thil chu chibai bûk a duh lo va,—Bildad upa rethei tak chu rei tak a awm a; deck chu lungkham takin a kal a; chutah chuan thlahna thu dang sawi turin cabin chhungah chuan a tlan thla a; deck-ah a lo kal leh a, thli lam chu a hawi leh a; tui zau tak leh tawp nei lo lam hawiin, Hmarchhak Continent hla tak tak, hmuh theih lohte chauhin a hual vel a; ram lam a hawi a; chunglam chu a han en a; dinglam leh veilam a en a; hmun tinah leh khawiah mah a en lo; tin, a tâwpah chuan, a pin-ah chuan mechanical-in hrui a hrual a, Peleg-a nghet tak chu a kut aṭangin a chelh a, lantern pakhat chu a han phar a, rei vak lo chu a hmai chu huaisen takin a en a, “Chuti chung pawh chuan, ṭhian Peleg, ka tuar thei a; ni e, ka tuar thei a ni,” tih ang maiin a ding ta a ni.

Peleg-a ngei erawh chu philosopher ang deuhin a la a; mahse a philosophy zawng zawngah chuan a mittui a tla a, chu chu lantern a hnaih lutuk chuan a ni. Tin, ani pawh chu cabin khat atanga deck dangah tlem a tlan lo—tunah chuan a hnuaiah thumal khat, tunah chuan chief mate Starbuck nen thumal khat a awm tawh bawk.

Mahse, a tawpah chuan a thianpa lam chu a hawi a, a chungchang chu a tawp ber angin a en a,—“Captain Bildad—lo kal rawh, lawngpui hlui, kan kal a ngai a ni. Chutah chuan main-yard-ah chuan kir leh rawh! Lawng ahoy! A bulah hnaih turin ding rawh, tunah chuan! Fimkhur rawh, fimkhur rawh!—lo kal rawh, Bildad, tlangval—i hnuhnung ber sawi rawh. Luck to you, Starbuck—luck to ye, Mr. Stubb—luck to ye, i, luck to ye, i, i, i vannei hle mai. Mr. Flask—in zavaia thlah leh vanneihna—vawiin hian kum thum chhung chu Nantucket hluiah leh hla takah in tan zanriah lum tak ka ei ang!”

“Pathianin malsawm che u la, a vawn thianghlimnaah awm rawh u, mihringte u,” tiin tar Bildad-a chuan a phunnawi a, a inmil lo hle mai. “Tunlai hian khaw lum tha tak in neih ka beisei a, chutiang chuan Captain Ahaba chu in zingah a inher thuai thei ang—ni duhawm tak chu a mamawh vek a ni a, in kalna tropic voyage-ah chuan in nei tam hle ang. Ramsa man kawngah fimkhur rawh u, nangni kawppuite u. Lawngte chu a tul lovin stave suh u, nangni harpooneer-te u; good white cedar plank is raised full three per cent. within the year. “Lo kal rawh, lo kal rawh, Captain Bildad;palavering kha bansan rawh,—away!” tichuan, chutiang chuan Peleg chuan a sir lamah chuan a tlanpui nghal vat a, an pahnih chuan lawngah chuan an tla thla ta a.

Lawng leh lawng chu a inthen darh a; zan thli lum leh vawt tak chu a inkar ah a tleh a; gull au chhuak chu a lu chungah a thlawk a; hull pahnih chu nasa takin a rawn inher chhuak a; thinlung rit takin vawi thum kan au a, mitdel ang maiin Atlantic tuipuiah chuan chanvo ang maiin kan tlu lut ta a ni.





BUNG 23. Lee Shore-a awm.

Bung engemaw zat kalta khan Bulkington pakhat chungchang sawi a ni a, lawng khalhtu sang tak, newlanded mariner, New Bedford-ah inn-ah an tawk a.

Chu thlasik zan hlim tak karah chuan Pequod chuan a phuba lakna tur thal chu tuipui sual tak tak zingah chuan a thun lut a, Bulkington tih loh chu a kaihhruaina hnuaia ding chu tu nge ka hmuh ang! Kum li chhunga lawnga zin hlauhawm tak aṭanga thlasik laihawla lo tla thla chauh, chutiang taka chawlh hahdam lo taka thlipui tleh hun dang atan a nawr leh thei tawh chu khawngaihthlak takin hlauhna leh hlauhna nen ka en a. Ram chu a ke bulah a kang ang maiin a lang. Thil mak ber ber chu sawi theih loh a ni fo thin; hriatrengna thuk tak chuan epitaph a siam lo; he inch ruk zeta sei bung hi Bulkington-a thlan lung awm lo a ni. Thlipuiin a rawn nuai lawng, leeward land-a lungngai taka tlantu ang bawkin a chungah a kal tih chauh ka sawi ang. Port chuan tanpuina a pe thei lo ang; lawng chawlhna chu a khawngaihthlak hle a; lawng chawlhna hmunah chuan himna, thlamuanna, meipui lung, zanriah, puan lum, ṭhiante, kan thihnate tana ngilnei zawng zawng a awm. Mahse chu thlipuiah chuan lawng chawlhna hmun, ram chu chu lawng hlauhawm ber a ni a; mikhual lawmna zawng zawng a thlawh chhuah vek tur a ni; ram khawih vawi khat, keel chu ei mai mai ni mah se, a khur chhuak vek ang. A chakna zawng zawng nen mipui zawng zawng chu tuipui kamah lawngin an kal vek a; chutianga a tih chuan, ‘fain-in a in lam pan tûra thlipui ngei chu a do a; lashed sea ram nei lo zawng zawng a zawng leh a; inhumhimna avângin lungngai taka hlauhawma tlan luh; a ṭhian awmchhun a hmelma rilru na ber!

Tunah hian i hre tawh em, Bulkington? Chu thi thei khawpa tuar theih loh thutak chu tlemte in hmu niin a lang; ngaihtuahna thuk tak, taima tak zawng zawng chu a tuifinriat zalenna zau tak vawng reng tura thlarauin huaisen taka a beihna mai a ni tih; van leh lei thlipui tleh ber berte chuan bumna, saltanna tuipui kamah paih thlak tumin an inrawlh laiin?

Mahse, ram nei lovah chauhvin thutak sang ber, tuipui kam nei lo, Pathian anga hun bituk nei lo a awm ang bawkin—chutiang chuan, chu tawp nei lo au thawm takah chuan boral chu a tha zawk a, chu chu himna ni mah se, chawimawi lo taka lee-a tlak ai chuan! Worm ang chi tan chuan, chuti a nih chuan, aw! tuin nge thlawh chhuah tumin crawn crawn ang! Hlauhawm tak tak! he hrehawmna zawng zawng hi a hlawhtling lo em ni? Thinrim la, thinlung takin awm rawh, Aw Bulkington! Pathian chanve, lungngai takin tuar rawh! I tuifinriat-a boralna spray atang chuan—dik takin, i apotheosis chu a zuang thla ta!





BUNG 24. Thurawn petu.

Tunah chuan Queequeg nen hian he whale man sumdawnna hi fair takin kan inhmang tawh a; tin, he whale man sumdawnna hi engtin emaw takin ram neitute zingah hla phuah thiam lo leh hmingchhiatna nei lo deuha ngaih a nih tawh avangin; chuvangin, nangni ram mite u, he thila dik lo taka keini whale mantute chunga tih dik lohna hi hriattir ka tum vek a ni.

A hmasa berah chuan, mipui tam tak zingah chuan whale man sumdawnna hi liberal profession an tihte nen a inmil lo tih hi din chhuah chu thil tul lo deuh anga ngaih theih a ni. Mi hriat loh chu metropolitan society hrang hrangah pawh luhtir ni ta se, a thatna chungchanga ngaihdan tlangpui chu tlem a ti hmasawn zawk ang, company hmaah harpooneer anga hlan a nih chuan, sawi ta ila tin, naval officer-te entawn turin a visiting card-ah initials SWF (Sperm Whale Fishery) a dah a nih chuan chutiang kalphung chu pre-eminently presuming leh ridiculous anga ngaih a ni ang.

Ringhlelhawm lohvin khawvelin keini whaleman-te chawimawi a duh loh chhan lian ber pakhat chu hei hi a ni: an ngaihdan chuan, a tha berah chuan kan kohna hi sa thatna chi (butchering sort of business) ang mai a ni a tin, chutah chuan active taka kan inhmang hian bawlhhlawh chi hrang hrangin min hual vel tih pawh a sawi bawk. Butcher kan ni a, chu chu a dik a ni. Mahse, sa thattute, chutiang bawkin, leh thisen chhuak ber badge sa thattute chu khawvelin chawimawi a duh reng Martial Commander an ni vek a ni. Tin, kan sumdawnna bawlhhlawh nia sawi chungchangah chuan, tun thlenga hriat loh thudik thenkhatah in inhmang thuai ang a, chu chuan, a pum puia thlir chuan, he lei thianghlim ber thil thianghlim ber zingah pawh hnehna changin sperm whale-ship hi a phun ang. Mahse, thubuai sawi tur chu a dik tih pawm pawh; eng whale-ship-a deck hrual (disordered slippery decks) nge sipai tam takin hmeichhe fakna zawng zawnga zu in tura an kir lehna indona hmuna sawi theih loh carrion nen tehkhin theih? Tin, hlauhawm ngaihtuahna hian sipai hnathawh chungchanga mipui inngaihluhna chu nasa takin a tipung a nih chuan; battery thlenga zalen taka kal tawh veteran tam tak chuan sperm whale hmui lian tak lo lang chu rang takin an hnungtawlh ang tih ka tiam ang che u, a lu chunga boruak chu eddies-ah an fan ang. Pathian hlauhawmna leh thilmak inzawmkhawmte nena khaikhin chuan mihring hriatthiam theih thil hlauhawmte chu eng nge ni si a!

Mahse, khawvel hian keini whale mantute hi min scout mahse, hriat lohvin chawimawina thuk ber min pe a ni; a ni, biakna zawng zawng tam tak a ni! khawvel pum huapa taper, lamp leh candle kang zawng zawng deuhthaw chu hmun thianghlim tam tak hma ang bawkin kan ropuina atan an kang vek si a!

Mahse, he thu hi êng dangin han en teh; tehfung chi hrang hrangah rit rawh; keini whalemen-te hi eng nge kan nih, eng nge kan nih tawh tih en rawh.

Engvangin nge De Witt-a hunlai khan Dutch-ho hian an whaling fleet-ah admiral an neih? Engvangin nge Louis XVI. of France, a mimal sum senso hmangin, Dunkirk atanga whaling lawngte chu a fit out a, chu khawpuiah chuan kan ram Nantucket atanga chhungkaw sawmhnih emaw pahnih emaw vel chu zahawm takin a sawm a? Engvangin nge kum 1750 leh 1788 inkar khan Britain hian a whaleman-te hnenah £1,000,000 aia tam bounties-in a pek? Tin, a tawp berah chuan engtin nge tunah chuan America rama whalemen-te hian khawvela banded whaleman dang zawng zawng kan tluk phah ta; lawng zasarih aia tam nei tuipui sipai lawng khalh rawh; mi sang sawm leh pariat zetin an thawk a; kum khatah dollar 4,000,000 a hmang thin; lawngte chu, an kal lai khan, $20,000,000 man a ni! tin, kum tin kan lawng chawlhna hmunah hian buh seng ṭha tak $7,000,000 kan rawn luhtir bawk. Engtin nge heng zawng zawng hi a lo awm theih, whaling-ah hian thil puissant tak a awm lo a nih chuan?

Mahse hei hi a chanve a ni lo; en leh rawh.

Zalen takin ka sawi chhuak a, cosmopolite philosopher chuan, a dam chhung zawngin, remna zawnga nghawng pakhat chauh a kawhhmuh thei lo va, chu chu kum sawmruk kalta chhung khan khawvel zau tak zawng zawng chungah, aggregate khata lak chhuah, whale man sumdawnna sang leh chak tak aiin a thawk thei zawk a ni. Kawng khat leh kawng dangah chuan thilthleng mak tak tak, leh a thupui inzawm zelah pawh pawimawh tak tak a hring chhuak a, chuvang chuan whale man chu chu Aigupta nu, a pum a\anga naupai taka fa nei anga ngaih theih a ni. Heng zawng zawng hi catalog-a dah vek chu beiseina nei lo, tawp nei lo hna a ni ang. Kut tlemte chauh pawh ni se. Kum tam tak kal ta atang khan whale-ship hi leilung hmun hla ber leh hriat hlawh lo ber berte ferret chhuahna kawngah hmahruaitu a ni. Chart nei lo tuipui leh thliarkarte a tlawh tawh a, chutah chuan Cook emaw Vancouver emaw pawh lawngin an la kal ngai lo. Tunah chuan American leh European indo mite chu remna zawngin hmân lai lawng savawm tak takah chuan an chuang a nih chuan, a tir lama kawng kawhhmuhtu, anmahni leh savawmte inkara hrilhfiah hmasa bertu whale-ship chawimawina leh ropuina atan chibai buk rawh se. Exploring Expeditions-a mi huaisen, i Cook-te, i Krusensterns-te chu an lawm ang bawkin an lawm mai thei; mahse, Nantucket aṭangin hming hriat loh Captain tam tak an chhuak tawh tih ka sawi a, chu chu i Cook leh i Krusenstern aia ropui ang bawka ropui leh ropui zawk an ni. An tanpuina nei lo kut ruakah chuan, anmahni chuan, hnam dang shark tuiah te, leh record loh, javelin thliarkar tuipui kamah te chuan, Cook-a chuan a marines leh muskets zawng zawngte nen duhthusamin an ngam loh tur virgin thilmak leh hlauhawm tak takte nen an indo si a. South Sea Voyages hluiah chuan hetiang taka lo thanglian tawh zawng zawng hi, chu thilte chu kan Nantucketer huaisen tak takte nun chhunga thil awm dan pangngai mai a ni. Vawi tam tak chu, adventures which Vancouver dedicates three chapters to, heng mite hi lawnga common log-a dah tlak lohah an ngai thin. Ah, khawvel hi! Aw, khawvel!

Whale sangha man hian Cape Horn a rawn vel thleng khan Europe leh Pacific tuipui kama Spanish province hausa tak takte line rei tak inkarah hian colonial tih loh chu sumdawnna engmah a awm lo va, colonial tih loh chu inpawlna a awm lo. Spanish lallukhum huatna policy chu bawhchhia hmasa bertu chu whaleman kha a ni a, chu colony-te chu a khawih a; tin, hmun a awm chuan, chu whaleman-te a\ang chuan a tawpah chuan Peru, Chili, leh Bolivia-te chu Old Spain nghawngkawl a\anga chhuah zalen a nih dan leh, chu hmuna chatuan democracy din a nih dan chu chiang takin a lantir thei bawk ang.

Chu America ropui tak, sphere lehlamah, Australia chu khawvel êng nei tawhte hnenah whaleman-in a pe a ni.
