BUNG 55. Whale-te thlalak mak tak takte chungchang.

I shall ere long paint to you as well as one can without canvas, something like the true form of the whale as he actually appears to the eye of the whaleman, a taksa pum puia whale chu whale-ship bulah a moored a nih chuan chutah chuan fair taka ke pen theih a ni. Chuvangin, a hma chuan a thlalak suangtuahnaa thil mak tak takte, tun thlenga ram neitu rinna chu rintlak taka dodaltu te chu puan chhuah a hlawk mai thei. Hemi kawngah hian khawvel hi siamthat a hun tawh a, chutiang whale thlalak chu a dik lo vek tih finfiah a ni.

Chutiang pictorial delusions zawng zawng bulpui ber chu Hindoo, Aigupta, leh Grecian lemziak hlui ber berte zingah hmuh tur a ni mai thei. Biak in-a marble panelling-a, milem pedestal-a siam, leh phaw, medallion, no, leh pawisa fai chunga dolphin chu Saladin-a ang chain-armor scale-a siam, leh St. George-a ang helmet vuah lu hmanga siam a nih lai aṭang tawh khan; chuta ṭang chuan chutiang chiah license chu a lo awm ta a, chu chu whale thlalak lar ber berteah chauh ni lovin, science lama a chanchin tam takah pawh a lo awm ta a ni.

Tunah chuan, by all odds, engti kawng pawhin whale ta nia sawi, extant portrait hlui ber chu, India rama Elephanta cavern-pagoda hmingthang takah hmuh tur a awm ta a ni. Brahmin-ho chuan chu pagoda hriatrengna lemziak tawp nei lo tluk zetah chuan, sumdawnna leh thil zawn zawng zawng, mihring hnathawh ngaihtuah theih zawng zawngte chu, an zinga pakhat mah a lo awm tak tak hma kum tam tak chhung chu a lo awm tawh a ni, tiin an vawng tlat a ni. Chuti a nih chuan, eng emaw ti takin kan whale man hna ropui tak chu chutah chuan a lo lang chhuak tur a ni tih chu thil mak a ni lo. Hindoo whale sawi hi, kulh chhunga department hran pakhatah a awm a, Vishnu taksaah leviathan angin a inlan a, chu chu Matse Avatar tia hriat lar tak a ni. Mahse he lemziak hi mihring chanve leh whale chanve ni mah se, a hnuhnung zawkte hmui pe thei tur chauh ni mah se, chu a section tlemte chu a dik lo vek a ni. Anaconda tail tapering ang deuh a ni zawk a, whale dik tak majestic flukes-a kutphah zau tak aiin a lang zawk.

Mahse Galleries hluiah kal la, tunah hian Kristian lemziak thiam ropui takin he sangha lem a ziah hi han en teh; tuilian hmaa Hindoo-ho aiin a hlawhtling lo zawk si a. Guido-a thlalak a ni a, Perseus-a’n Andromeda chu sea-monster emaw whale emaw atanga a chhanchhuah lai a ni. Chutiang thilsiam mak tak model chu Guido hian khawi atanga a hmuh? Hogarth-a pawhin, a mah ngeiin “Perseus Descending”-a scene ang chiah a lemnaah hian, a siam ṭha zawk lo bawk. Chu Hogarthian ramhuai taksa lian tak chu a chung lamah chuan a rawn inher chhuak a, tui inch khat pawh a la chhuak thei lo. A hnungzang lamah howdah chi khat a nei a, a hmui zau tak, billows-te a inher chhuahna chu Thames lui atanga Tower-a tui hmanga kal Traitors’ Gate atan lak theih a ni. Tichuan, Scotch Sibbald hlui Prodromus whale te, Bible hlui print leh primer hlui cut-a tarlan angin Jonah’s whale te pawh a awm bawk. Chûngte chungchâng chu eng nge sawi ang? Book-binder-a whale-in anchor tla thla stock-a vine-stalk ang maia a inher lai chungchang erawh chu—lehkhabu hlui leh thar tam tak hnungzang leh title-page-a stamp leh rangkachak vuah angin—chu chu thilsiam mawi tak, mahse fabulous tak tak, entawn, ka la a, antique vase-a lem ang chite aṭanga siam a ni. Khawvel pumah dolphin tia koh ni mah se, chuti chung pawh chuan he lehkhabu ziaktu sangha hi whale tih tumna ka ti a; a chhan chu device an rawn luh tirh khan chutiang taka ruahman a nih vang a ni. Kum zabi 15-na vel, Zirna Harhna hun lai, khawi emaw laiah Italian publisher hlui pakhatin a rawn chhuah a; tin, chutih lai chuan, leh hun hnuhnung lam thleng pawhin, dolphin-te chu mipuiin Leviathan chi khat anga ngaih an ni.

Hmanlai lehkhabu thenkhat vignette leh embellishment dangteah chuan a chang chuan whale-ah hian khawih mak tak tak i tawk ang a, chutah chuan spout chi hrang hrang, jet d’eau, hot springs leh cold, Saratoga leh Baden-Baden te chu a thluak chau lo atang chuan bubbling up in an lo chhuak thin a ni. “Advancement of Learning” original edition title-page-ah chuan whale hriat duh tak tak i hmu ang.

Mahse, heng professional lo taka tih tumna zawng zawng hi bansanin, chutiang leviathan thlalak, sober, scientific delineation, a hretute’n an sawite chu han thlir ila. Hmanlai Harris-a’n lawng zin chhuahna a khawlkhawmnaah hian Dutch-ho lawng bu, AD 1671 aṭanga lak chhuah whale plate ṭhenkhat a awm a, chu chu “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson of Friesland, master” tih a ni. Chu plate pakhatah chuan whale-te chu, thinghnah lian tak tak ang maiin, ice-isle-a mu chungin an entir a, an hnungzang nung chungah chuan bear dum an tlan a ni. Plate dang pakhatah chuan, prodigious blunder chu whale chu perpendicular flukes hmanga entir a ni.

Tichuan, quarto ropui tak a awm leh a, chu chu English navy-a Post Captain Captain Colnett pakhatin a ziak a, a thupui chu “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, for the purpose of extending the Spermaceti Whale Fisheries” tih a ni. He lehkhabuah hian “Physeter emaw Spermaceti whale emaw thlalak, Mexico tuipui kama August, 1793-a thah pakhat aṭanga tehfung hmanga lak chhuah, deck-a chawi sang” tih anga sawi outline a awm a. Captain hian he thlalak dik tak hi a marines te hlawkna tur atan a la tir lo tih ka ringhlel. Chumi chungchanga thil pakhat chauh sawi tur chuan, a mit a nei tih ka sawi ang a, chu chu a kaihhnawih scale ang chuan sperm whale puitling takah hman chuan chu whale mit chu feet nga vel zeta sei bow-window-ah a siam ang. Ah, ka sipai huaisen, engvangin nge chu mit atanga thlir chuan Jona min pek loh!

Chutiang bawkin, naupang leh mi rilru na tak takte hlawkna tur atana Natural History-a chhia leh tha hriatna nena inzawm ber, tihsual tenawm tak ang bawka fihlim a ni lo bawk. Chu lehkhabu lar tak “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature” chu han en teh. Kum 1807-a London edition tawifel takah chuan “whale” tia sawi leh “narwhale” tia sawi plate a awm a. Inelegant anga lang hi ka duh lo va, mahse he whale hmuhnawm lo tak hi chu bawnghnute ke ruh tliak ang mai a ni a; tin, narwhale chungchang erawh chu, a hmuhnawm vawi khat chauh pawh hian mak tih tur a tawk a, he kum zabi sawm leh kua-naah hian chutiang hippogriff chu sikul naupang mipui fing tak takte chungah chuan a tak tak angin palmed theih a ni.

Tichuan, kum 1825 khan Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, naturalist ropui tak chuan scientific systemized whale book a tichhuak leh a, chutah chuan Leviathan chi hrang hrangte thlalak engemaw zat a awm a. Heng zawng zawng hi a dik lo mai ni lovin, Mysticetus emaw Greenland whale (chu chu Right whale tihna a ni) thlalak pawh a ni a, chu chi chu khawih chungchanga hun rei tak tawnhriat nei tawh Scoresby pawhin a pianphungah a tlukpui a nei lo tih a puang a ni.

Mahse he sumdawnna tihsual zawng zawnga cap-sheaf dahna hi Baron lar tak unaupa scientific Frederick Cuvier tan chauh a ni. Kum 1836 khan Natural History of Whales a tichhuak a, chutah chuan Sperm Whale thlalak a tih chu a pe a ni. Chu thlalak chu Nantucketer tu hnenah pawh i entir hmain Nantucket atanga i summary retreat tur chu i ruahman hmasa phawt ang. Thumal khata sawi chuan Frederick Cuvier-a Sperm Whale hi Sperm Whale ni lovin, squash a ni. Whale-a zin chhuahna hlawkna chu a nei ngai lo tih chu a dik a ni (chutiang mite chuan an nei tlem hle), mahse chu thlalak chu khawi aṭanga lak chhuah nge tih chu tuin nge sawi thei ang? Chutiang bawka a scientific hmasa ber Desmarest-a’n a nau pai dik tak pakhat a neih ang khan a hmu a ni mai thei; chu chu Chinese drawing atanga lak a ni. Tin, chu Chinese-te chu eng ang tlangval nung tak nge pencil nei an nih tih chu queer cup leh saucer tam takin min hriattir bawk.

Kawngpuiah oil zuartu dawr chunga innghat sign-painter-te whale-te chungchang erawh chu engtin nge an sawi ang? Anni hi a tlangpuiin Richard III an ni. whale, dromedary humps nei, leh savawm tak tak; sailor tart pathum emaw pali emaw-a chawhmeh ei, chu chu lawng khalhtu tamna whaleboat a ni: an pianphungte chu thisen leh blue paint tuipuiah a tla thla a ni.

Mahse heng whale lemchan danah hian tihsual hrang hrang hi chutiang chuan mak a ti vak lo a ni. Ngaihtuah! Science lama lemziak tam zawk hi sangha tangkhang (stranded fish) atanga lak a ni a; tin, chûngte chu lawng chhe tawh, a hnungzang chhe tawh lem chuan ran ropui tak ngei chu a hull leh spar-a a inchhuanna tihchhiat loh zawng zawngah dik takin a entîr ang tluk zetin a dik a ni. Sakeibaknei te hian an full-length atan ding mah se, Leviathan nung hian a thlalak atan hian dik takin a la inphah ngai lo. Whale nung chu a ropuizia leh a awmzia famkim takah chuan tuipuiah chuan tui hriatthiam theih loha hmuh tur chauh a ni a; tin, tui chungah chuan a tam zawk chu hmuh theih lohvin a awm a, line-of-battle lawng thlawh chhuah ang maiin; tin, chu element atang chuan mihring thi thei tan chuan a taksain boruakah a chawi sang thei lo va, a swell leh undulations chak tak zawng zawng chu a humhim theih nan chatuan atana thil theih loh thil a ni. Tin, sucking whale naupang leh Platonian Leviathan puitling tak inkara contour danglamna rinhlelhna sang tak chu sawi loh; mahse, lawng chung lama chawi sang, chu whale hnek naupang zinga pakhat chungchângah pawh, chutih lai chuan a pianzia danglam tak, eel ang, limbered, danglam tak chu chutiang chu a ni a, a hmel lan dân dik tak chu Diabola ngei pawhin a man thei lo.

Mahse, ngaihtuah theih a ni mai thei, chu whale tangkhang ruhro saruak atang chuan a pianzia dik tak khawih chungchangah hian thudik dik tak a chhuak thei a ni. Ni miah lo. He Leviathan chungchanga thil mak zawk pakhat a nih avangin, a ruhro hian a pianzia tlangpui chu a hre tlem hle a ni. Jeremy Bentham-a ruhro, a executor pakhat library-a candelabra atana innghat chuan, burly-browed utilitarian old gentleman ngaihtuahna chu dik takin a thlen a, Jeremy-a mimal mizia dang hmahruaitu zawng zawng nen mahse, chutiang thil chu eng leviathan ruh ruh (articulated bones) aṭang pawhin chhui chhuah theih a ni lo. Dik tak chuan, Hunter ropui takin a sawi angin, whale ruhro mai mai hian fully invested leh padded animal nen hian inzawmna a nei ve tho a, chu chu insect chuan a roundingly envelope chrysalis nen a inzawmna a nei ve tho a ni. He thil danglam tak hi lu-ah hian mak tak maiin a lang a, chu chu he lehkhabu hmun ṭhenkhatah hian a rualin a lang ang. Side fin-ah pawh mak tak maiin a lang a, a ruhte hian mihring kut ruhte chu a chhang chiah chiah a, kutpui ruh chauh minus. He fin hian ruh-finger pangngai pali a nei a, chungte chu index, middle, ring leh little finger te an ni. Mahse heng zawng zawng hi an tisa khuhnaah chuan an awm reng a, mihring kut zungtangte chu thil siam chhuah khuhnaah an awm ang. Ni khat chu nuihzatthlak Stubb-a chuan: “Engtiang pawhin ngaihsak lo takin whale hian a châng chuan min rawng a bâwl mai thei a, mittens tel lovin min khawih thei tih sawi theih a ni ngai lo,” tiin a sawi.

Chuti a nih chuan, heng zawng zawng avang hian engti kawng pawhin i en chuan, Leviathan ropui tak chu khawvela thilsiam pakhat, a tawp thlenga chei loha awm reng tur chu a ni tih i thutlukna siam a ngai a ni. Dik tak chuan, thlalak pakhat chuan a dang aiin chhinchhiahna chu a hnaih hle thei a, mahse, engmahin a dikna nasa takin a hit thei lo. Chuvângin, whale chu eng ang chiah nge a nih tih chiang taka hriat theihna tûr lei lam kawng a awm lo. Tin, a living contour chungchanga tolerable idea pawh i derive theihna mode awmchhun chu, nangmah ngeiin whaling-a kal hi a ni; mahse, chutianga i tih chuan chatuan atâna stove leh a pil bo theihna hlauhawmna tlemte pawh i nei lo. Chuvangin, he Leviathan khawih chungchanga i hriat duhnaah hian i fastidious lutuk loh a tha berin ka hria.





BUNG 56. Whale-te thlalak dik lo zawkte chungchang, leh Whale-te thlalak dik tak chungchang.

Whale thlalak rapthlak tak takte nena inzawm hian hetah hian an chanchin rapthlak zawk, lehkhabu thenkhat, hmân lai leh tunlaia hmuh tur awmte, a bik takin Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, etc.-a hmuh tur awmte chu luhchilh tumin nasa takin ka thlem a, mahse chu thil chu ka paltlang ta a ni.

Sperm Whale ropui tak chungchang outline chhuah pali chauh ka hria a; Colnett-a te, Huggins-a te, Frederick Cuvier-a te leh Beale-a te an ni. Bung hmasa lamah khan Colnett leh Cuvier te hi kan sawi tawh a. Huggins-a ta hi anmahni aiin a tha zawk daih a; mahse, by great odds, Beale-a hi a tha ber. Beale-a’n he whale lem a ziah zawng zawng hi a tha vek a, rilru put hmang hrang hrang nei whale pathum thlalak a middle figure tih loh chu, a bung hnihna chu a capping a ni. A frontispiece, Sperm Whales beitu lawngte, parlor mi thenkhat civil skepticism tiharh tura chhut ni mahse, a general effect-ah chuan a dik a, nunna ang mai a ni tih rinhlelh rual a ni lo. J. Ross Browne-a Sperm Whale lemziak thenkhat chu contour-ah chuan a dik khawp mai; mahse, lungchhiatthlak takin an ziak a ni. Chu chu a sualna a ni lo nain.

Right Whale zingah chuan outline thlalak tha ber ber chu Scoresby-ah a awm a; mahse, duhthusam anga ngaih theih loh khawpa scale tê têa ziah an ni. Whale man scene thlalak pakhat chauh a nei a, hei hi a tlakchhamna lungchhiatthlak tak a ni, a chhan chu chutiang thlalak hmang chauhvin, a tih that tak tak a nih pawhin, a ramsa mantu nungte hmuh angin whale nung chungchanga ngaihdan dik tak ang chi eng pawh i hmu thei a ni.

Mahse, a vaiin lak chuan, hmun dangah pawh hmuh theih tur whale leh whale man scene-te presentation ṭha ber ber, a chipchiar ṭhenkhatah chuan dik ber ni lo mah se, French engraving lian pahnih, ṭha taka siam, Garnery pakhat lemziak aṭanga lak a ni. Anni hian Sperm leh Right Whale te beihna an entir a ni. Engraving hmasa berah chuan Sperm Whale ropui tak chu chakna ropui takin a lang a, tuifinriat thuk tak atanga lawng hnuaiah a tho chhuak chauh a, a hnungzang chungah boruak sang takah chuan stoven plank-a thil chhe rapthlak tak chu a phur a ni. Lawng hmalam chu a then chu a chhe lo va, ramhuai ruhro chungah a inthlau mai mai a ni a; tin, chu prow-ah chuan ding chungin, chu hun chhut theih loh pakhat chauh avang chuan, lawng khalhtu pakhat, whale-a rimchhia chhuak chhuak chuan a chanve a khuh a, a zuang chhuak a, chu chu tlang chhip atanga lo chhuak ang maiin i hmu a ni. Thil zawng zawng thiltih hi mak tak maiin a tha a, a dik bawk. Line-tub half-emptied chu tuifinriat var takah chuan a float a; harpoon luang chhuak thingpui note chu oblique-in a bob a; swimming crew lute chu hlauhna lantir dan danglam takin whale-ah hian an darh darh a; chutih laiin thlipui dum atanga hla takah chuan lawng chuan a hmun chu a rawn bear down ta a ni. He whale taksa peng hrang hrangah hian dik lohna lian tak hmuh tur a awm thei a, mahse chu chu kal tlang rawh se; ka nunna atan chuan chutiang khawpa tha chu ka ziak thei lo a nih avangin.

Engraving pahnihnaah chuan lawng hian Right Whale lian tak tlan lai barnacled flank bulah a drawing a, chu chuan a black weedy bulk chu tuipuiah Patagonian cliffs atanga mossy rock-slide thenkhat ang maiin a roll a ni. A jet-te chu a ding nghet a, a khat a, soot ang maiin a dum a; chutichuan, chimney-a meikhu a tam em avangin, a hnuaia rilpui lian takah chuan zanriah huaisen tak siam a awm ngei ang tih i ngaihtuah ang. Tuipui savate chuan crab te te, shell-fish te, leh tuifinriat candy leh maccaroni dangte chu an peck a, chu chu Right Whale hian a hnungzang hripui tak chungah a phur fo thin. Tin, chutih lai zawng chuan hmui thuk tak leviathan chuan thuk takah chuan a tlan lut a, a hnungah chuan curd dum buai tak tak ton tam tak a hnutchhiah a, lawng tlemte chu tuifinriat steamer paddle-wheel hnaih taka skiff man ang maiin a hring dup zingah chuan a tichhe ta a ni. Chutiang chuan, hmalam chu buaina nasa tak a ni vek a; mahse, a hnung lamah chuan, artistic contrast ropui takah chuan, tuifinriat glassy level chu a reh a, thiltihtheihna nei lo lawng starch nei lo sail-te chu a tla thla a, a spout-hole-a whale-pole dah luh aṭanga man flag chu lazi takin a innghat a, chu whale thi, hneh tawh, inert mass chu a ni.

Garnery the painter chu tu nge a nih, a nih leh nih loh, ka hre lo. Mahse ka nun chu a subject chu practically conversant a ni emaw, a nih loh leh mak tak maiin whaleman tawnhriat ngah tak takin a tutor emaw a ni. French ho hi painting action lama lads an ni. Europe rama lemziak zawng zawngte chu kal la, en la, khawiah nge hetiang gallery, canvas-a buaina nung leh thawk chhuak, Versailles-a chu hnehna channa hmuna awm ang kha i hmuh ang entu chuan France ram indona ropui tak tak a zawnin a kalna tur, pell-mell-a a beih na hmun; chutah chuan khandaih tin chu Northern Lights eng ang maiin a lang a, lal leh Emperor-te ralthuam kengin an rawn tlan pel a, lallukhum khum centaur-te charge ang maiin? Chu gallery-a hmun luah tlak lo tak tak lo, heng Garnery-a tuifinriat indona hmunte hi an ni.

French-hoin thil mawi tak tak an man theihna tur natural aptitude chu an whaling scene-a eng painting leh engraving nge an neihah hian a danglam bik hle niin a lang. England-in sangha man lama an tawnhriat hmun sawma ṭhena hmun khat pawh ni lovin, American-ho tawnhriat aṭanga hmun sang khat pawh ni lovin, chuti chung pawh chuan hnam pahnihte chu whale man thlarau tak tak thlen thei tur sketch siam zawh tawh awmchhun an pe vek a ni. A tam zawkah chuan English leh American whale draughtsman-te chu thil awm dan mechanical outline, whale-a vacant profile ang chi, an rawn present-naah an lungawi vek niin a lang a chu chu, picturesqueness of effect chungchangah chuan, pyramid profile sketch-na nen a inang a ni. Scoresby, dik taka hmingthang Right whaleman pawhin Greenland whale sei zawng zawng khauh tak min pek hnuah, leh narwhale leh porpoises te tak te nalh tak tak pathum emaw pali emaw min pek hnuah, lawng kawr, chopping knives, leh grapnels te classical engravings series hmangin min treat a tin, Leuwenhoeck pakhat microscopic diligence hmangin khawvel khur chhuak mek enfiahnaah a inpe a, fac-similes sawmkua leh paruk zeta lian Arctic snow crystals lian tham tak takte chu a ni. I mean no dispararagement to the excellent voyager (veteran a nih avangin ka chawimawi a ni), mahse thil pawimawh takah chuan Greenland Justice of the Peace hmaa crystal tin tan sworn affidavit lak chhuah loh chu ngaihsak lohna a ni ngei ang.

Garnery-a aṭanga chutiang lemziak ṭha tak takte bâkah, French-a lemziak dang pahnih hriat tlâk tak tak a awm a, chu chu mi tu emaw chuan amah ngeiin “H. Durand” a subscribe a ni. An zînga pakhat chu tûn laia kan thiltum nêna inmil tak ni lo mah se, chuti chung pawh chuan thuziak dangteah pawh sawi tûr a ni. Pacific thliarkar zingah chuan chawhnu lam hmun reh tak a ni a; French whaler pakhat chu lawngah anchor-in, tuipui kamah, thlamuang takah, leh rilru hah takin tuipuiah tui a la a; lawng kawngkhar hrual hrual te, leh a hnung lama kutphah hnah sei tak takte, thli tleh loh boruak karah chuan an tla dun vek a ni. A nghawng chu a tha hle a, chu chu sangha mantu harsa tak takte chu an hmar lam chawlh hahdamna kawng tlemte zinga pakhat hnuaia a present-na nen ngaihtuah chuan. Engraving dang chu thil danglam tak a ni: lawng chu tuipui zau takah a hove-to a, Leviathanic nun chhungril takah pawh, a kiangah Right Whale a awm bawk lawng (cutting-in act-ah) chu lawng chawlhna hmuna kal ang maiin ramhuai lam pan chuan a zuang thla a; tin, lawng pakhat, he thiltihna hmun a\anga hmanhmawh taka tlan chhuak chu hla takah whale-te chu a rawn um zui ta a ni. Harpoon leh lance te chu hman theih turin level-in an mu a; lawng khalhtu pathum chuan a khur chhungah chuan mast chu an dah mai mai a; tuifinriat hrual nghal a\angin, chu lawng lian lo tak chu tui a\angin a chanve zetin a ding chhuak a, sakawr enkawltu ang maiin a ding a ni. Lawng atang chuan whale lum tak hrehawm tawrhna meikhu chu smithies khua chunga meikhu ang maiin a chho ta a; tin, thli tleh lam pan chuan, chhum dum, thlipui leh ruah nasa tak nena lo chhuak chuan tuipui lama thawktu phur tak takte hnathawh chu a ti chak hle niin a lang bawk.

Experiment hmangin a yarn pakhat leh sawmnga chuan pound za leh sawmhnih zeta rit chu a suspend vek ang; chutiang chuan hrui pum pui chuan ton thum tluk zeta strain a tuar ang. A sei zawngah chuan common sperm whale-line hi fathom za hnih chuang zet a ni. Lawng hnung lam pan chuan tub chhungah chuan spirally coiled away a ni a, still-a worm-pipe ang ni lovin, mahse, cheese ang maia lian, densely bedded “sheaves,” emaw, layer of concentric spiralizations mass pakhat siam turin, hollow engmah nei lovin “heart,” emaw minute vertical tube cheese axis-a siam a ni. Coiling-a tangle emaw kink emaw tlem ber pawhin, a tlan chhuah chuan, mi pakhat kut, ke, a taksa pum pui chu tihsual nei lovin a la chhuak ang a, fimkhur ber chu a tub-a line dahnaah hman a ni. Harpooneer thenkhat chuan he sumdawnna ah hian zingkar pum pui deuhthaw an ei ang a, line chu sang takin an phur ang a, chutah chuan block pakhat kaltlangin hnuai lam hawiin tub lam pan chuan an reeving ang a, chutiang chuan coiling act-ah ang bawkin wrinkles leh twists awm thei zawng zawng atanga chhuah tir ang.

English lawngah chuan tub pakhat aiah pahnih hman a ni a; tub pahnihah hian line inang chu coil chhunzawm zel a ni. Hemiah hian hlawkna engemaw zat a awm a; a chhan chu heng twin-tub te hi a te em em a, lawng chhungah hian an in fit awlsam zawk a, chutiang chuan an ti hrual lo va; chutih laiin, American tub, feet thum vel zeta sei leh a thuk tawk tak chuan, a plank-te chu inch chanve chauh a thuk craft tan chuan bungraw phurhna lian deuh a siam a whale-boat hnuai lam chu critical ice ang a ni si a, chu chuan distributed weight nasa tak a phur ang a, mahse concentrated erawh chu a phur vak lo vang. American line-tub-a painted canvas cover chu kutbeng a nih chuan lawng chu whale-te hnena hlan tur wedding-cake ropui tak ropui tak nen a chhuak ang maiin a lang.

Line tawp pahnih chu a lang chhuak vek a; a hnuai lam tawp chu eye-splice emaw loop emaw-ah a tawp a, a hnuai lam atanga tub sir lam hawia lo chhuak a, a sir chungah a innghat a, engkim atanga inthen vek a ni. Hetianga lower end ruahmanna hi account pahnih-ah a ngai a ni. Pakhatna: A kianga lawng a\anga line dang a chungah a khin awlsam theih nan, a vuak tawh whale chu a thuk lutuk a, a tir lama harpoon-a line inbun zawng zawng chu phurh chhuah vek a hlauhawm phah a nih chuan. Heng thil thlengah hian whale chu a mug of ale ang maiin a inthlak danglam a, sawi dan chuan lawng pakhat atanga lawng dangah a inthlak a; lawng hmasa ber chu a nupui ṭanpui tûrin a kutah a thlawk fo ṭhin nachungin. Pahnihna: He ruahmanna hi himna tlangpui atan chuan a pawimawh hle a; a chhan chu, line hnuai lam tawp chu engti kawng mahin lawngah a inzawm tlat a nih chuan, chutih hunah chuan whale chuan a châng angin minute khat lek chhungin a tawp thlengin line chu a tlan chhuak ta a nih chuan, chutah chuan a ding lo vang, lawng boral tawh chu tihsual nei lovin a hnungah tuifinriat thuk takah a hruai thla dawn a ni tin, chutiang a nih chuan khawpui au tu tumahin an hmu leh tawh lo vang.

Chase atan lawng chu an tihhniam hmain, line chunglam tawp chu tub atanga hnunglam atanga lak a ni a, chutah chuan loggerhead vel chu kal tlangin, lawng sei zawng zawng chu hmalam pan lehin, mi tinte lawng khalhtu loom emaw, a kutkawr emaw chungah crosswise-in a innghat a, chutiang chuan lawng khalh laiin a kutphah chungah a jog a tin, mipa inkara kal tlangin, gunwale inhmachhawn takah an thut inthlak laiin, lawng hmalam hrual (extreme pointed prow)-a leaded chocks emaw grooves emaw thlengin, chutah chuan thing pin emaw skewer emaw, common quill ang maia lian chuan a chhuah loh nan a veng a ni. Chocks atang chuan bows chungah festoon tlemte angin a innghat a, chutah chuan lawng chhungah a pass leh a; tin, fathom sawm emaw sawmhnih emaw vel (box-line tia koh) chu bow-a box chungah coiled a nih avangin, a hnunglam tlem a la awm gunwale lam pan chuan a kal chhunzawm a, chutah chuan short-warp-ah a inzawm leh a—chu hrui chu harpoon nena inzawm nghal a ni mahse, chu inzawmna hma chuan short-warp chuan sundry mystifications too tedious to detail a paltlang a ni.

Chutiang chuan whale-line chuan lawng pum pui chu a coil buaithlak takah chuan a fold a, a chhehvel zawng zawngah te chuan a inher leh a inher kual vel a ni. Oarsmen zawng zawng chu a perilous contortions-ah hian an inrawlh vek a; chutiang chuan ram neitu mit zakzum tak tan chuan Indian juggler ang maiin an lang a, rul thihpui ber berte pawhin an taksa pengte chu sportive takin an chei a ni. Hmeichhe thi thei fapa tu pawhin, a vawi khatna atan, chutiang hempen thil buaithlak tak takte karah chuan a thu thei lo va, lawng chawlhna hmunah chuan theihtawp a chhuah laiin, engtik lai pawhin hriat lohvin harpoon chu a darh thei a, heng contortion rapthlak tak tak zawng zawng hi ringed lightning ang maiin inkhelh theih a ni tih a ngaihtuah thei lo bawk chutiang chuan a ruh chhunga marrow ngei pawh chu jelly nghing ang maia a chhunga khuh tirtu khur chhuak lovin a awm thei lo. Mahse, tih dan—thil mak tak! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, merry mirth tam zawk, joke tha zawk, leh brighter repartees, i mahogany chungah i hre ngai lo va, chutianga hangman’s noose-a khai a nih chuan whale-boat-a cedar dum inch chanve zeta sei chungah i hre ngai lo vang tin, Lal Edward-a hmaa Calais-a burgher parukte ang bawkin, lawnga thawktu phuahtu mi parukte chuan thihna hmuiah an hruai lut a, i sawi angin an kawr tinah halter an vuah a ni.

Tunah chuan ngaihtuahna tlemte chauh hian chutiang whale man chhiatna thleng nawn fo—a ṭhen chu a ṭhen chu a chanchin pangngaia ziah a ni—he mi emaw, chu mi emaw chu lawng aṭanga line-a hruai chhuah a nih a, a bo tihte chu a chhang thei mai thei che a ni. A chhan chu, line a dart chhuah lai chuan, chutih laia lawnga thut chu, steam-engine-a manifold whizzings-a full play-a thut ang mai a ni a, chutih lai chuan flying beam tin, leh shaft, leh wheel tin hian an rawn ran che a ni. A pawi zawk a ni; heng hlauhawmte thinlungah hian che lovin i thu thei lo vang, a chhan chu lawng chu kekawr bul ang maiin a nghing a, kawng khatah leh kawng dangah i ding a, vaukhânna tlemte pawh nei lovin tin, mahni insiamrem thei buoyancy engemaw tak leh duhthlanna leh thiltih a ruala awm vang chauhvin Mazeppa siam a nih loh nan i tlanchhuak thei a, engkim hmutu ni ngei pawhin a chhuah theih ngai lohna hmunah chuan i tlanbo thei bawk.

Hetah pawh hian: thlipui hma leh hrilhlawkna chauh nia lang, muanna thuk tak chu thlipui aiin a rapthlak zawk mai thei; a chhan chu, dik takin, thlamuanna chu thlipui khuh leh khuh mai a ni si a; tin, a chhungah a keng tel bawk a, rifle hlauhawm lo anga lang chuan thihna thlen thei powder, leh ball, leh puak chu a chelh ang bawkin; chutiang chuan line-a chawlh hahdamna mawi tak chu, inkhelh tak takah hruai luh a nih hmaa lawng khalhtute chungchang ngawi rengin a serpentines angin—hei hi he thil hlauhawm tak lam zawng zawng aiin hlauhna dik tak kengtu thil a ni.
