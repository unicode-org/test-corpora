JAPTER 55. Kin pija ko rekajur an Whale ko.

Inaaj lukkun peint ñan yuk einwot an juon maroñ ilo ejelok canvas, juon men einwot mol eo an whale eo einwot an walok ñan mejen whaleman eo ke ilo enbwinnin make whale eo ej bed iturin whale-ship eo bwe en maroñ in jimwe an etetal ion ijo. Emaroñ in emman, kin menin, mokta ñan kwalok ñan pija ko rekabwilōnlōn kin e me ñan rainin rej kalijeklok tõmak eo an ri ailiñ eo. Ej ien eo ñan kōmman bwe lal in en jimwe ilo men in, ilo ad kalikar bwe pija kein kin whale rej bōd.

Emaroñ bwe unin aolep pija kein renaj walok ilo pija ko etto tata an Hindoo, Egypt, im Grecian. Jen ien ko rekar kōmman men ko rekãl ak rar jab jimwe ke ion marble panelling ko an temple ko, ion pedestal ko an statue ko, im ion shield ko, medallion ko, cup ko, im coin ko, dolphin eo kar jibwe ilo joñan chain-armor einwõt an Saladin, im juõn bõran einwõt an St. George; jen ien eo ekar wōr juon men eo ej einwot in ej walok, jab wōt ilo pija ko rebuñbuñ kin whale eo, ak ilo elōn pija in science ko kin e.

Kiiō, ilo aolep wāween ko, pija eo etto tata ej an whale eo, ej pād ilo cavern-pagoda eo eļap an Elephanta, ilo India. Brahmin ro rej ba bwe ilo ekjab ko ejelok jemlokin ilo pagoda eo etto, aolep jerbal ko im jerbal ko, aolep jerbal ko an armij, kar kōmmani mokta jen an kar walok. Ejelok bwilōñ innem, bwe ilo jet wāween jerbal eo ad eutiej in ekkatak whale en kar bed ijo ilo an jako. Hindoo whale eo kar konono kake, ej walok ilo juon jikin eo ejenolok ilo worwor eo, ej kwalok kin Vishnu ilo wāween leviathan, eo kar jelā kake einwot Matse Avatar. Ak meñe pija in ej jimettan armij im jimettan whale, bwe en baj kwalok wōt loñin eo āliktata, ak mõttan eo edik an ej aolep bōd. Ej einwot loñin juon anaconda, jen pein whale eo emol.

Ak etal ñan jikin pija ko etto, im lale kiõ juon pija in ek in; bwe ejjab tōprak jen antediluvian Hindoo eo. Ej pija eo an Guido kin Perseus ej lomoren Andromeda jen sea-monster ak whale. Ia eo Guido ear bōk ie pija in juon menin mour eo eoktak einwot in? Bareinwõt Hogarth, ilo an kar pijaik ejja pija in wõt ilo “Perseus Descending” eo an make, ejjab kõmman juõn men emõnlok. Eļap an ļap an Hogarthian monster eo ej m̧akūtkūt ioon mejān, ejjab lukkuun bōk juon inch in dān. Ewōr juon kain howdah ilo likio, im loñin eo elap an rup im menin mour ko rej toor ie, emaron in bōk jikin ñan Traitors' Gate eo ej tellok jen Thames ilo den ñan Tower eo. Innem, ewōr Prodromus whale ko an Scotch Sibbald eo etto, im Jonah's whale, einwot an walok ilo pija in Bible ko etto im pija in primer ko etto. Ta eo renaj ba kin men kein? Kin book-binder's whale eo ej mōjno einwot juon wine-stalk iturin stock in juon anchor eo ej wanlaḷtak—āinwōt kar stamp im gilded ilo likio im taitōl-page ko an elōn bok ko etto im rekãl—men in ej juon menin mour eo elukkuun aiboojoj ak elukkuun in kabwilōñlōñ, kar anõke, ij bōk e, jān pija ko āinwōt in antiquees. Meñe aolep rej naetan juon dolphin, ak ij naetan ek in ej juon menin kajeoñ in bōk juon whale; kinke kar kōmmane ke kar jino an walok kein jerbal in. Kar jinoe jen juon ri bok in Italy eo etto ilo 15th century, ilo ien Revival of Learning; im ilo ien ko, im bareinwõt ñan juon ien eo etto, dolphin ko rar lemnok bwe rej juon kain Leviathan.

Ilo vignette ko im bar jet men ko jet ilo jet bok ko etto kwonaj jet ien iion men ko rebin ñan whale eo, ijo aolep kain spout ko, jets d'eau, hot springs im cold, Saratoga im Baden-Baden, rej itok jen buruen eo ejjab mōjno. Ilo taitōl-peij eo an jinoin “Advancement of Learning” kwonaj lo jet whale ko rekeie.

Ak ilo ad jolok aolep men kein rejjab jimwe, jen lale pija ko an leviathan rej ba rej jab idaak, melele ko jen science, jen ro rejelā. Ilo bok in ito-itak ko an Harris etto ewōr jet pileij in whale ko kar bōk jen juon bok in ito-itak an Dutch, AD 1671, etan “Juon Ito-itak in Whale ñan Spitzbergen ilo wa eo Jonas ilo Whale eo, Peter Peterson jen Friesland, master.” Ilo juon ian pileij kein, whale ko, einwot wa ko relap, rej kwalok ir rej babu ibwiljin ice-isles, ippāñ white bear ko rej ettōr ion likiir. Ilo bar juon pileij, bōd eo elap ej kōmmane ilo an kwalok kin whale eo ippāñ mōttan ko rejimwe.

Bar juon alen, ewōr juon quarto eo elap an emman, kar jeje jen juon Captain Colnett, juon Post Captain ilo English navy, etan “Juon ito-itak iturin Cape Horn ñan South Seas, ñan unin an kalaplok Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Ilo bok in ewōr juon pija in juon Physeter ak Spermaceti whale, kar bōk jen juon eo kar mõn e ilo Mexico, August, 1793, im kar likit ion deck eo. Ijjab pere bwe kapen eo ear bōk pija in emol ñan tokjen ñan ri tarinae ro an. Ñan kwalok juon wōt men kake, kōtlok bwe in ba bwe ewōr juon an mejen eo ej jerbal, ekkar ñan joñan eo ej kobalok, ñan juon sperm whale eo emōj an rūttolok, enaj kar kōmman bwe mejen whale eo en juon bow-window enañin lalem ne aitokan. Ah, kapen eo aō ekajoor, etke kwaar jab letok n̄an kōm Jonah ej kalimjōk jān mej eo!

Bareinwõt, bok ko rebintata kin bwebwenato in lal ko ñan tokjen eo an jodikdik ro im redik, ejelok air bōd jen ejja bwid in wõt. Lale jerbal eo elap an armij ro jela kake “Goldsmith's Animated Nature.” Ilo London edition eo edik an 1807, ewōr pileij ko an juon “whale” im juon “narwhale.” Ijjab kōnaan bwe in einwot juon eo ejjab aiboojoj, ak whale in ejjab emman ej einwot juon sow eo emōj an mōttan; im, kin narwhale eo, juon wōt kalimjōk e ej bwe ñan kabwilōñ juon, bwe ilo ebeben in kein kajoñoul lalem kain hippogriff rot in emaron kar bōk jikin ñan jabdewōt armij ro remāletlet an rijikuul ro.

Innem, bar juon alen, ilo 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, juon ri meletlet kin menin mour ko, ear kwalok juon bok in menin mour ko, ijo ewōr jet pija in kain Leviathan ko. Men kein otemjej rejjab baj bōd wōt, ak pija eo an Mysticetus ak Greenland whale (meļeļein, Right whale eo), barāinwōt Scoresby, juon armej eo eļap an en̄jake kōn an un̄ur kain menin mour in, ej kwaļo̧k bwe ejjeļo̧k an m̧ōttan ilo menin mour ko.

Ak likit cap-sheaf eo ñan aolep jerbal in ejjab jimwe kar likit ñan scientist eo Frederick Cuvier, būratin Baron eo elap an jelā kake. Ilo 1836, ear kwalok juon Natural History of Whale, eo ear kwalok ta eo ej naetan juon pija in Sperm Whale. Mokta jen am kwalok pija eo ñan jabdrewot armij in Nantucket, emman tata am kar kōmmane am retreat jen Nantucket. Ilo juon nan, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale ejjab juon Sperm Whale, ak juon squash. Emool, ear jab bōk tokjen jen juon ito-itak in whale (armej rot kein rejjab lukkun bōk), ak ia eo ear bōk pija in jen e, wōn emaron ba? Bōlen ear bōke einwot an scientific eo mokta jen e ilo ejja field eo wot, Desmarest, ear bōk juon ian abortion ko an remol; melelen, jen juon pija in China. Im ta kain likao ro remourur kin pencil eo ri China rein, elōn cup ko im saucer ko rej kwalok ñan kij.

Kin whale ko an ri-kōmman sign ko rej walok ilo ial ko rej dāpij ion jikin wia ko an ri-wia oil ro, ta eo renaj ba kake ir? Rej lukkun Richard III. whale ko, ippāer dromedary humps, im lukkun nana; mōñā in raelep ilo jilu ak emān sailor tarts, eo ej wa ko reobrak kin ri ito-itak ro: aer mōjno rej mōñā ilo lojet in bōtōktōk im blue paint.

Ak bōd kein ilo kwalok kin whale eo rejjab lukkun kabwilōñlōñ. Kaelmenḷọkjān! Elōñ ian pija in science ko kar bōk jen ek ko rar bed ilo lojet; im men kein rej jimwe einwot juon pija in juon wa eo emōj an jorrāān, im ej rup likio, enaj kar jimwe an kwalok menin mour eo eutiej ilo aolep an utiej buruen kin wa eo ejorrāān im spars ko an. Meñe elephant ko rar jutak kin joñan aer depakpak, Leviathan eo emour ear jab lukkun maroñ in dāpij e make ñan pija eo an. Whale eo emour, ilo an aiboojoj im aorōk, ej baj walok wōt ilo lojet ilo den ko rebin; im ilo ial eo elap an jab alikar, einwot juon wa in tarinae eo emōj an ettōr; im jen men in ej juon men eo ejjab maroñ in indeeo ñan armij eo en maroñ in kōttāik e ilo mejatoto, bwe en maroñ in kejbãrok aolep an kajoor im an mōkaj. Im, jab konono kin oktak eo elap an walok ikõtan juon whale edik im juon Platonian Leviathan eo emwij an rittolok; ak, meñe ilo jekjek eo an juon iaan whale ko reddik rej m̧ōn̄ā kar likūt ioon deck eo an wa eo, āindein ej āinwōt juon eel, āinwōt eel, limbered, oktak wāween an waļo̧k, bwe an devil eo make ejjab maron̄ in loe.

Ak emaron in ļōmņak, bwe jen ānbwinnin whale eo ej pād wōt, meļeļe ko rejim̧we remaron̄ in bōk jikin kōn wāween eo an em̧ool. Ejab ñan jidik. Bwe ej juon ian men ko rekabwilōnlōn kin Leviathan in, bwe ānbwinnin ej letok jidik wōt melele kin jekjek eo an. Meñe skeleton eo an Jeremy Bentham, eo ej bed ñan candelabra ilo library eo an juon ian ro rar kōmmane, ej kwalok lemnak eo kin juon emaan eo edik im elap an maroñ in jerbal, ippāñ aolep kadkad ko jet an Jeremy; ak ejelok men rot in emaron kar walok jen di ko an leviathan. Ilo mol, einwot an Hunter eo elap ba, ānbwinnin whale eo ej bōk ejja jemjerā eo wōt ñan menin mour eo eobrak im m̧ōrā āinwōt an insect eo kōm̧m̧ane n̄an chrysalis eo ej lukkuun kalibubuik e. Men in ej alikar ilo bōran, einwot ilo jet mõttan in bok in enaj alikar ilo jidimkij. Ej barāinwōt lukkun alikar an walok ilo side fin eo, di ko an rej lukkun uwaak ñan di ko in pein armij, ijellokin wōt thumb eo. Fin in ewōr jiljino pein di ko, pein indik, pein eo ikōtaan, pein ring, im pein edik. Ak men kein otemjej rej bed wot ilo kilin kanniōk eo air, einwot pein armij ilo juon kilin eo emōj kōmmane. “Jekdon ewi joñan an whale eo maroñ in jerbal ñan kij,” Stubb ear ba juon raan, “ejjab maroñ in ba bwe ej jerbal ñan kij ilo ejelok mittens.”

Kin aolep un kein, innem, ilo jabdewōt wewein kwomaroñ lale, kwoj aikuj in jemlok bwe Leviathan eo elap ej juon menin mour ilo lal in eo ej aikuj in bed wot im jab peint ñan jemlokin. Emool, juon pija emaron tōpar joñan eo elaplok an epaak jen bar juon, ak ejelok emaron tōpar joñan eo elap an jejjet. Innem ejelok juon ial in lal in ñan jella ta eo whale eo ej lukkun einwot. Im wāween eo wot kwomaroñ bōk juon lemnok eo emman kin wāween an mour, ej ilo am make etal im whale; ak ilo am kōmmane, kwoj bōk jorrāān in bwe kwon inebata im mōjņo jen e. Kin menin, ej einwot ñan ña bwe kwon jab lukkun mōkaj ilo am kōnan jella kin Leviathan in.





JAPTER 56. Jen pija ko rejjab lukkun bōd kin Whale ko, im pija ko remol kin Whale ko.

Ilo an wōr pija in whale ko rekabwilōnlōn, ij lukkun mālijjon ijin ñan deloñ ilo bwebwenato ko rebinlok kin ir me rej bed ilo jet bok ko, jimor etto im kiõ, elaptata ilo Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, im ko jet. Ak ij jolok men in.

Ijelā wōt emān outline ko kar kwaļo̧ki kōn Sperm Whale eo eļap; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, im Beale's. Ilo jebta eo lok Colnett im Cuvier kar konono kaki. An Huggins elaplok an emman jen aer; ak, ilo an lukkun oktak, Beale's ej eo emman tata. Aolep pija ko an Beale kin whale in rej emman, ijellokin pija eo ilo lukkun pija in jilu whale ko ilo wāween ko reoktak, ej kajemlok chapter eo an kein karuo. Men eo an ilo maan, wa ko rej tarinae Sperm Whale ko, meñe ejelok bere kar kōmmane ñan kōmman an jet armij ro ilo jikin wia eo jab tõmak, ej lukkun jimwe im einwot mour ilo an jerbal. Jet ian pija ko an Sperm Whale ilo J. Ross Browne rej lukkun jimwe ilo contour; ak rej lukkun nana aer jeje. Ejjab bōd eo an.

Kin Right Whale eo, pija ko remman tata rej bed ilo Scoresby; ak rej je ilo juon joñan edik ñan kwalok juon lemnok emman. Ewōr wōt juon an pija in pija in whale ko, im menin ej juon likjab eo ekaburom̧ōjm̧ōj, kōnke ej kōn pija kein wōt, n̄e em̧m̧an aer kōm̧m̧an, bwe kwomaron̄ bōk jabdewōt āinwōt juon ļōmņak em̧ool kōn whale eo emour āinwōt an kar loe jān ri-kattōr ro an remour.

Ak, bōk ñan aolep, ilo joñan eo emman tata, meñe ilo jet melele ko rejjab lukkun jimwe, kwalok kin whale ko im pija in whale ko ñan loe jabdewōt jikin, rej ruo French engraving ko relap, emman aer kōmmane, im bōk jen pija ko an juon Garnery. Ilo kajjojo, rej jutak kin tarinae ko ion Sperm im Right Whale. Ilo pija eo moktata juon Sperm Whale eo elap an utiej ej kwalok ilo an kajoor, ej kab wanlōñtak iumin wa eo jen joñan an utiej lojet, im ej bōk ion mejatoto ion likio jorrāān eo elap an stoven plank ko. Prow in wa eo ejjab lukkun rup, im ej jibwe wōt ilo an bed ion spine eo an monster eo; im jutak ilo prow eo, ñan juon wōt ien eo ejjab maroñ in bwine, kwoj lo juon ri-eo, jimettan ej kalibubu kin an bwil spout eo an whale eo, im ilo an ettōr, einwot jen juon precipice. Jerbal in men eo ej lukkun emman im mol. Line-tub eo ejjelok armij ie ej ettōr ion lojet eo emōrā; wōjke ko an harpoon ko rekar tulo̧k rej m̧akūtkūt ilowaan; bōran ri ikkure ro rej jepel iturin whale eo ilo wāween ko reoktak jen dron kin mijak; ilo ien eo ettolok wa eo ej itok ñan jikin eo. Bōd ko relap remaroñ walok ilo enbwinin whale in, ak kwon kõtlok bwe en jako; kinke, ñan mour eo aõ, Ijjab maroñ in je juon eo emman.

Ilo pija eo kein karuo, wa eo ej ilo an jibwe turin barnacled flank eo an juon Right Whale eo elap ej ettōr, eo ej jeblak ilo lojet einwot jet rock-slide ko jen Patagonian cliffs. Jet ko an rej jutak, obrak, im marok einwot soot; bwe jen joñan an lõñ baat ilo chimney eo, kwonaj kar lemnok bwe ej aikuj in wor juon mõñã in jibboñ ej kōmat ilo lojen ko relap iumwin. Bao in lojet rej mōñā crab ko redik, shell-fish, im candies ko jet jen lojet im maccaroni, eo Right Whale ej bōk ion likio eo an. Im ilo ien in leviathan eo ej ettōr ilo lojet, im likit elōñ curd ko rekilmeej ilo an etal, im kōmman bwe wa eo edik en mōkaj ilo mōttan ko einwot juon wa eo ej jibwe iturin paddle-wheel ko an juon wa in lojet. Kin menin, ilo maan ej aolep jorrāān; ak itulik, ilo juon oktak eo emman, ej joñan an juon lojet eo emōj an kalmenlokjen, wa ko rejjab kajoor an wa eo ejelok an kajoor, im juon whale eo emej, juon jikin eo emōj an anjo ion, ippāñ flag in jibwe eo ej dāpij wōt jen whale-pole eo kar likūt ilo spout-hole eo an.

Wōn Garnery ri pija eo, ak kar, Ijjab jelā. Ak mour eo aõ ñan e ekar lukkun jelā kin unin kōnono eo an, ak kar katakin e jen juon whaleman eo elap an jelālokjen. Ri France ro rej ļaddik ro ñan kōmman pija. Etal im lale aolep pija ko ilo Europe, im ia eo kwonaj loe juon jikin pija in mour im menono ion canvas, einwot ilo jikin anjo eo ilo Versailles; ijo eo ej loe ej tarinae ilo ial eo an, pell-mell, ilo tarinae ko relap an France; ijo jāje otemjej rej einwot juon meram in Northern Lights, im king ro im Emperor ro rej ettōr lok, einwot juon tarinae in centaur ro? Ejjab lukkun jab ekkar ñan juon jikin ilo jikin pija eo, rej kein tarinae in lojet ko an Garnery.

Maroñ eo an ri France ro ñan bōk pija in men ko ej einwõt ej alikar ilo ta pija ko im pija ko rej kõmmani kin pija in whale ko air. Ilo an ejelok juon mõttan joñoul in iminene eo an England ilo jerbal in ekkat, im jab juon mõttan joñoul in an ri America ro, rar lelok ñan ailiñ kein ruo pija ko rekar dedelok im maroñ in kwalok kin jitõb eo emol an whale hunt eo. Ilo elaptata, ri-Ingland im ri-Amedka ro rej kōmman pija in whale rej lukkun mõnõnõ in kwalok kin mechanical outline in men ko, einwõt profile eo ejelok armij ie kin whale eo; eo, joñan an aiboojoj, ej einlok wōt juon pija in juon pyramid. Bareinwõt Scoresby, Right whaleman eo elap an jelā kake, elikin an kar letok ñan kim juon pija in Greenland whale eo, im jilu ak emen pija ko redik an narwhale im porpoises, ej kwalok ñan kim juon lajrak in pija in boat hooks, chopping knives, im grapnels; im ilo an Leuwenhoeck maroñ in lale juon lal eo ej mōkaj 96 fac-similes in Arctic snow crystals ko relap. Ijjab kōnaan kajirere ñan ri ito-itak eo emman (Ij kautiej e kin an kar juon ri tarinae), ak ilo juon men eo elap an aorōk ekar juon menin likjab ñan jab bōk ñan kajjojo crystal juon affidavit eo emōj an kallimur kar bōke iman juon Greenland Justice of the Peace.

Ijellokin pija ko remman jen Garnery, ewor bar ruo pija in French ko rejimwe, jen juon eo ej naetan e make “H. Durand.” Juon ian men kein, meñe ejjab lukkun ekkar ñan unin ad kiõ, ak ej tellokin kwalok kake ilo bwebwenato ko jet. Ej juon jikin eo edik ilo raelep ibwiljin aelõñ ko ilo Pacific; juon French whale ej ankōr, iturin lojet, ilo juon jikin eo ebin, im ej bōk den ilo wa eo; sail ko rekar ruprup an wa eo, im bōlōk ko reaitok an wōjke ko ilo lik, jimor rej wōtlo̧k ippān doon ilo mejatoto eo ejjeļo̧k kōto ie. Elukkun emman tokjen eo, ñe jej lemnok kin an kwalok ñan ri eñwõd ro rekajur iumin juon ian jet ian men ko rej bed ilo oriental repose. Engraving eo juon ej juon men eo eoktak: wa eo ej ettōr ion lojet eo elap, im ilo buruen mour in Leviathanic, ippāñ juon Right Whale iturin; wa eo (ilo an mōttan) ej ettōr ñan monster eo einwot ñe ej etal ñan juon quay; im juon wa, ej mōkaj an etal jen jikin in, ej kin an lukwarkwar whale ko ilo ettolok. Harpoon ko im lance ko rej bed ilo jimwe ñan kajerbali; jilu ri-uwe rej baj likit wōt mast eo ilo hole eo an; ilo ien eo jen juon mōkaj in mōkaj an lojet, wa eo edik ej jutak jimettan jen den eo, einwot juon kidia ej eddeklok. Jen wa eo, baat in eñtan ko an whale eo ej bwil ej wanlōñtak einwot baat eo ion juon bukwōn in smithies; im ñan kōto, juon kōdọ ebōrōrō, ej wanlōñtak ippān m̧akūtkūt ko reļļap im wōt ko, ej āinwōt kōm̧ōkajļo̧k m̧akūtkūt eo an ripālele ro rem̧ōņōņō.





JAPTER 57. Kin Whale ko ilo Paint; ilo Teeth; ilo Wood; ilo Sheet-Iron; ilo Dekā; ilo Toļ ko; ilo Stars.

Ilo Tower-hill, ilo am wanlaḷḷọk ñan jikin wa ko ilo London, kwomaroñ kar lo juon ri-akweḷap (ak kedger , āinwōt an ri-wa ro ba) ej jibwe juon board eṃōj peint iṃaan mejān, ej jutak kōn pija eo ekabūroṃōjṃōj ke ear jako neen. Ewōr jilu whale im jilu wa; im juon ian wa ko (kar lemnok bwe ewōr neen eo ej jako ilo aolepen an kar jinoin) ej rup kin loñin whale eo moktata. Jabdewōt ien ilo yio kein joñoul, rej jiron io, emaan eo ear jibwe pija eo, im kwalok menin ñan juon lal eo ejjab tõmak. Ak ien eo ñan kōwānōk e emoj an itok. Whale ko an jilu rej einwot whale ko remman einwot kar kwaloki ilo Wapping, ilo jabdewōt ien; im wijki eo an ej juon wijki eo ejelok kajitõk kake einwõt jabdewõt eo kwonaj loe ilo jikin ko rebin ilo western. Ak, meñe ej bed ion wijki eo, ejelok juon nan in wijki ej kōmmane whaleman eo ejeramel; ak, ippāñ mejen ko rebin, ej jutak ilo an būromõj im lemnok kin an make mwijbar.

Einwot an Hawaiian savage, einwot an white sailor-savage. Ilo ejja kijenmij eo wot, im ejja menin idaak in shark eo wot, jen juon jack-knife eo an, enaj kōmmane ñan yuk juon mōttan menin mour, jab einwot juon ri jerbal, ak einwot an epaak ilo an ļap an kōmmane, einwot Greek savage eo, Achilles's shield; im eobrak kin jitõb in nana im lemnak, einwõt pija ko an ri Dutch eo etto, Albert Durer.

JAPTER 60. Line eo.

Ilo an jitōñlok ñan jikin wia eo ej itok jen an naaj kwalok kake, im bareinwõt ñan melele eo emmanlok kin aolep pija ko einlok wõt in ilo jikin ko jet rej kwalok kaki, ij bed ijin ñan konono kin menin bwilōñ eo, jet ien ekauwatata.

Liin eo kar jinoin kar kajerbale ilo jikin ekkatak eo ekar jen hemp eo emman tata, ekar mōrā jidik kin tar, ekar jab mōrā kake, einwot ilo jekjek in rōp ko jet; bwe tar, einwot an kōjerbale, ej kōmman bwe hemp eo en maroñ in mōrā ñan ri-kōmman rōp eo, im barāinwōt kōmman bwe rōp eo make en emmanlok ñan ri-kōmman wa eo ñan kōjerbale ilo wa ko; ak, ejjab baj bwe joñan eo ekkā enaj kar lukkun kōmman bwe en penlok whale-line eo ñan an epaak an aikuj in bed iumwin; ak einwot an elaptata ri wa ko jino katak, tar ilo aolep ien ejjab kobaiktok ñan an pen ak kajoor to eo, jekdon ewi joñan an maroñ lewoj an mōrā im meram.

Ilo yio ko remootlok, Manilla rope eo ilo jikin ek ko an Amedka ear lukkun bōk jikin hemp einwot juon kein jerbal ñan whale-line ko; bwe, meñe ejjab lukkun pen einwot hemp, ej kajoorlok, im elaplok an mōrā im mōrā; im inaj kobaiktok (kinke ewōr juon æsthetics ilo men otemjej), elaplok an aiboojoj im ekkar ñan wa eo, jen hemp. Hemp ej juon menin mour eo emarok, juon kain Indian; ak Manilla ej einwot juon Circassian eo ewōr bōran gold ñan lale.

Whale-line eo ej ruo-mōttan-jilu in juon inch ilo depakpakin. Ilo jinoin, kwojjab lemnok bwe elap an kajoor einwot an mol. Ilo an kōmmane juon im lemñoul yarn ko an renaj kajjojo dāpij juon eddo in juon buki im rualitōk paun; bwe aolepān rōp eo en maroñ in bōk juon eddo eo enañin jilu tōn. Ilo joñan depakpak, sperm whale-line eo ej joñan elaplok jen ruo bukwi fathom. Ñan tulik in wa eo ej spirally coiled ilo tub eo, jab einwot worm-pipe eo an juon still though, ak bwe en ejaak juon round, cheese-shaped mass in densely bedded “sheaves,” ak layers in concentric spiralizations, ejelok jabdewōt hollow ak “heart,” ak minute vertical tube eo ej ejaak ilo axis in cheese eo. Einwot an diklok an jorrāān ak jorrāān ilo coiling eo, ilo an ettōr, bōk pein, neen, ak aolepān enbwinin juon armej, kōjparok eo eļaptata ej kōjerbale ilo likūt line eo ilo tub eo an. Jet ri-kōmman harpoone renaj jolok enañin juon jibboñ ilo jerbal in, bōk line eo ñan lañ im innem jolok e ñan lal ilo juon block ñan tub eo, einwot ilo jerbal in coiling ñan kōtlok jen aolep wrinkles im twist ko.

Ilo wa ko an ri England rej kajerbal ruo tūb ijellokin juon; ejja line eo wot ej wōnmanlok wōt ilo tub ko ruo. Ewōr jet tokjen ilo menin; kinke twin-tub kein rej dik rej maroñ in deloñ ilo wa eo, im rejjab lukkun inebata; ilo ien eo, American tub eo, enañin jilu ne depakpakin im joñan an depakpak, ej kōmman juon men eo elap an ļap ñan juon wa eo me wōjke ko an rej baj juon jimettan inch depakpakin; bwe lal in wa eo ej einwot ice eo elap an aorōk, eo enaj bōk juon eddo eo elap, ak ejjab lukkun juon eo elap. Ñe ej dāpij canvas eo emwij peint ion American line-tub eo, wa eo ej einwot ñe ej ettōrlok ippāñ juon wedding-cake eo elap an emman ñan lelok ñan whale ko.

Jemlokin line eo ruo rej alikar; jemlokin eo iumin ej jemlok ilo juon eye-splice ak loop ej itok jen lal ñan turear in tub eo, im ej dāpij tōrerein eo an ejjelok jen aolep men. Karõk in an jemlokin iumin ej menin aikuj kin ruo un ko. Mokta: Ñan kōmman bwe en bin an maroñ in likit bar juon line jen wa eo ej bed iturin, elañe whale eo ej jorrāān ej ainikien eo elap an kajoor bwe en maroñ in bōk aolep line eo kar likit ilo harpoon eo. Ilo ien kein, whale eo ej etal einwot juon kap in ale, einwot an kar ba, jen juon wa ñan eo juon; meñe wa eo moktata ej bed iturin ñan jibañ lio pāleen. Kein karuo: Karõk in ej menin aikuj ñan kejbãrok armij otemjej; bwe elañe jemlokin line eo ilo jabdrewot wewein ej kobalok ñan wa eo, im elañe whale eo enaj kar ettōr line eo ñan jemlokin ilo juon wōt minit, ej baat einwot an kōmmane jet ien, ejamin kar bōjrak ijo, bwe wa eo emōj an jako enaj kar lukkun jibwe lok iman ilo lojet; im ilo ien in ejelok ri-kōmmanwa enaj kar bar loe.

Mokta jen an kōttāik wa eo ñan an etal, jemlokin line eo ej bōk jen tub eo, im etal iturin loggerhead eo ijo, bar bōklok ñan maan ilo aolepān tōrerein wa eo, ej bed ilo crosswise ioon loom ak handle in oar eo an kajjojo armej, bwe en jog ņae wrist eo an ilo an rowing; im barāinwōt etal ikōtaan em̧m̧aan ro, ke rej jijet ilo gunwales ko jet, n̄an lead chocks ak grooves ko ilo extreme pointed prow in wa eo, ijo juon pin wōjke ak skewer jon̄an wōt juon common quill, ej bōbrae an m̧ōkaj an diwōjļo̧k. Jen chock ko ej dāpij ilo juon festoon edik ion bow ko, innem ej bar deloñ ilowaan wa eo; im jet joñoul ak rualitōk fathom (naetan box-line) ej dāpij ioon box eo ilo bow ko, ej wōnm̧aanļo̧k wōt ilo iaļ eo an n̄an gunwale eo ej pād wōt jidik ālikin, innām ej dāpij wōt n̄an short-warp eo—rope eo ej m̧ōkaj an kobaļo̧k ippān harpoon eo; ak mokta jen kōtaan eo, short-warp eo ej etal ilo elōn kain men ko rebin ñan kwalok kaki.

Kin menin whale-line eo ej dāpij wa eo ilo coil ko an rebin, ej mōkaj im mōkaj iturin ilo aolep jikin ko. Aolep ri wa ko rej bōk koņaer ilo jorrāān ko an; bwe ñan mejen armij eo ej mijak, rej einwot ri Indian juggler ro, ippāñ jedpānit ko rekajur tata rej ikkure ilo neir. Jabdewōt nejin kōrā eo ej mej, ñan ien eo moktata, ejjab maroñ jijet make ibwiljin men ko rebin, im ilo an kate e ilo oar eo, lemnok bwe ilo jabdewōt ien eo ejjab jelā kake harpoon eo emaron in kar jolok, im aolep men kein renana renaj walok einwot ļan̄ ko; ejjab maroñ in walok ilo ejelok an mōkaj eo ej kōmman bwe mōttan eo ilo di ko an en mōkaj ilo e einwot juon jelly eo emōj an mōkaj. Ak mwil—men eo ekabwilōñlōñ! ta eo ejjab maroñ in kōmmane?—Gayer sallies, elaplok merry mirth, joke ko remmanlok, im brighter repartees, kwoj jan kar roñ ion mahogany eo am, jen am naj roñ ion cedar eo mouj jimettan inch an whale-boat eo, ñe ej bed ilo to ko an hangman; im, einwot jiljino burgher ro an Calais mokta jen King Edward, jiljino emaan ro rej kōmmane kumi in rej jibwe ilo lojen mij, ippāñ juon halter iturin aolep bōran, einwot am maroñ ba.

Bōlen juon lemnok edik enaj kōmman bwe kwon maroñ in kwalok kin jorrāān ko rar walok ilo whale—jet iaer rej baj bwebwenato kaki—an armij in ak armij eo kar bōk e jen wa eo ilo line eo, im jako. Bwe, ñe line eo ej ettōrlok, ñan jijet ilo wa eo, ej einwot am jijet ilo lukkun ainikien ko an juon steam-engine ilo an lukkun jerbal, ke aolep beam ko rej ettōr, im shaft, im wheel, rej mōñā eok. Ej nanalok; bwe kwojjab maroñ jijet ilo buruen jorrāān kein, kinke wa eo ej m̧akūtkūt āinwōt juon jikin kiki, im kwoj pād ilo juon iaļ im bar juon, ilo ejjeļo̧k jidik kakkōl; im ilo wōt juon wāween eo ej make kōmman an make im ilo ien eo wōt an kōnan im jerbal, kwomaroñ ko jen am kōmmane juon Mazeppa, im ettōr jen ijo al eo ej loe men otemjej ejjab maroñ in jolok eok.

Bar juon alen: einwot kalmenlokjen eo elap eo ej walok mokta im kanan kin jorrāān eo, bōlen elaplok an nana jen jorrāān eo make; bwe, emool, kalmenlokjen ej baj wōt menin kalibubu im kalibubu in jorrāān eo; im ej bed ilo e make, einwot an rifle eo ej einwõt ejelok jorrãn ej bõk powder eo enana, im ball eo, im rup eo; innem kakije eo emman an line eo, ke ej jab konono kin ri wa ko mokta jen an kar bōktok ilo ikkure eo emol—men in ej juon men eo ej bōktok elaplok an mijak jen jabdrewot men ko jet ilo men in ekauwatata. Ak etke ba elaplok? Aolep armij rej jokwe ilo ļain in whale ko. Aolep rej lotak ippāñ halter ko ilo bōraer; ak ej wōt ñe rej bōk ilo mōkaj, oktak in mij, bwe armij ro rej kile jorrāān ko rejimwe, redik, im rej bed wōt ilo mour. Im elañe kwoj juon ri meletlet, meñe kwoj jijet ilo wa in whale eo, kwoban kar eñjake juon wōt mijak, jen ñe kwoj jijet iman kijeek eo am ilo jota ippāñ juon poker, im jab juon harpoon, iturin yuk.
