Bōran Tashtego eo elap, emera, im emera, bōran eo eutiej, im mejen ko rekilmeej—ñan juon Indian, Oriental ilo aer ļap, ak Antarctic ilo aer ļap—aolep men kein raar lukkuun kwaļo̧k bwe ej juon rijolōt in bōtōktōk eo an rikōm̧akoko ro reutiej, ro, ilo aer pukōt New England moose eo eļap, kar bow in pein aboriginal eo bukwōn ko an eo elap. Ak ejjab bar mōñā ilo ial in kidu ko ilo bukwōn eo, Tashtego ej kiiō mōñā ilo ial in whale ko relap ilo lojet; harpoon eo ejjab bōd an ļaddik eo ej bōk jikin ārooj eo ejjab bōd an sires ro. Ñan lale kilin neen im neen, kwonaj kar bōk tõmak ko an jet ian Puritan ro mokta, im tõmak bwe Indian in ej juon nejin Prince in Kajoor ko an Air. Tashtego ekar Stubb squire eo an mate eo kein karuo.

Kein kajilu ian rikōmman harpoone ro kar Daggoo, juon menin mour eo elap, ej āinwōt coal-black negro-savage, ippāñ juon neen einwot juon lion—juon Ahasuerus ñan an loe. Kar dāpij jen lojilñin ruo hoop ko gold, joñan aer ļap bwe ri-wa ko raar ņa etaer ring-bolt ko, im renaaj kar kōnono kōn kōjparok top-sail halyards ko n̄an er. Ilo ien jodikdik eo an Daggoo ear make uwe ilo juon wa in whaler, ej bed ilo juon jikin ejjelok armij ie ilo tōrerein lojet eo an. Im ejjañin kar etal ñan jabdewōt jikin ilo lal in ak ilo Africa, Nantucket, im jikin ko an ri pagan ro me elaptata an whalemen etal ie; im kin an kar tōl iumin elōn yio ko ilo mour in ek ko ilo wa ko an ro ewōr ippāer ro rejjab lukkun lale ta kain armij ro rej jilkinlok ir; Daggoo ear kejbãrok aolep kadkad ko an remõn, im ear jutak einwõt juõn giraffe, ear etal ibelakin deck ko ilo aolepen aiboojoj in jiljino ne im lalem ilo socks ko an. Ekar wor juon ettā ilo an kar kalimjōk e; im juon emaan ekilmeej ej jutak iman mejen ej einwot juon flag ekilmeej ej itok in kajjitōk bwe en jemlok juon mweo. Ilo aō kōnan jella, negro in, Ahasuerus Daggoo, ekar Squire eo an Flask eo edik, eo ekar einwot juon chess-man iturin. Kin men ko jet ilo Pequod's company, jen ba, bwe ilo ien in ejelok juon ian ruo ian taujin armij ro mokta jen mast eo rej jerbal ilo American whale fishery, rej ri America ro rar lotak, meñe enañin aolep opiija ro rej. Ilo ijin ej einlok wōt juon ippān jikin ek ko an Amedka āinwōt ippān jarin tariņae eo an Amedka im jarin tariņae ko im riwia ro, im jarin tariņae ko kar jerbal ilo kalōk Canal ko im Railroad ko an Amedka. Ejja einwot, ij ba, kinke ilo aolep jekjek kein ri Amedka ro rej lelok bōro ko, ro jet ilo lal in rej lelok muscle ko. Ejelok oran in ri wia rein rej bed ilo Azores, ijo ri wia ro ilo Nantucket rej etal ñan bar juon jikin ekkã air itok ñan kalaplok armij ro air jen ri kallip ro rekajur ilo parijet ko rebin. Ilo ejja wāween in wōt, ri-kōm̧m̧an wa ko ilo Greenland rej etal jān Hull ak London, kar pād ilo Shetland Islands, n̄an bōk aolepān rijerbal ro aer. Ilo ial eo ñan mweo, rej bar jolok ir ijo. Ewi wawein, ejelok melele, ak armij in ailiñ ko rej einwot rej kōmman whalemen ro remman tata. Rej kar aolep armij in ailiñ ko ilo Pequod, Isolatoes bareinwõt, ij naetan, jab kile bwe armij ro rej jokwe ie, ak kajjojo Isolato ej jokwe ilo juon jikin eo ejenolok an make. Ak kiõ, kobalok ibben don ilo juon keel, ewi joñan an kar Isolatoes rein! Juon Anacharsis Clootz deputation jen aolep aelõñ ko ilo lojet, im aolep jemlokin lal, ej etal ibben Old Ahab ilo Pequod eo ñan kwalok inebata ko an lal in iman bar eo me ejjab lõñ iair rej jeblak jen e. Black Little Pip—ekar jab—oh, jaab! ear etal mokta. Ļaddik in Alabama eo ejeram̧ōl! JAPTER 28. Ehab.

Iumin elōn raan ko elikin an kar etal jen Nantucket, ejelok men ilōñ in Kapen Ahab kar loe. Ripālele ro rar lukkun jibañ don ilo ien lale ko, im kin jabdrewot men ko rar maroñ in loe, rar einwõt ir make iroij in wa eo; ak jet ien rej diwōjtok jen cabin eo kin kakien ko relukkun mõkaj im rebin, bwe elikin men otemjej ekar alikar bwe rej ak kakien ilo jikin ko jet. Aet, iroij eo air eutiejtata im iroij eo ekar bed ijo, meñe ekar jab loe jen jabdrewot mejen ekar jab kōtlok an deloñ ilo jikin eo ekwojarjar kiõ ilo cabin eo.

Aolep ien ij wanlōñlok ñan deck eo jen watch ko ao iumin, ij kalimjōk lik ñan lale elañe ewōr turin mejān ej walok; bwe inebata eo aõ moktata kin kapen eo ejelok jelā kake, kiõ ej bed ilo jikin eo ej makelok ie ilo lojet, ear erom juon menin inebata. Men in ear laplok an laplok jet ien kin an kar Elijah eo ebbweer kar bar walok ñan ña, kin juon kajoor eo edik ikar jab maroñ in kar lemnak kake mokta. Ak ikar jab maroñ in jutak bin ir, einwot ilo jet wāween ko ikar lukkun pojak in ettōn̄ dikdik kin an rikanaan eo ekabwilōñlōñ an wharf ko. Ak jekdon ta eo ej kin inebata ak inebata—ñan naetan āindein—eo ikar eñjake, ak jabdewōt ien ikar itok im kalimjōk eō ilo wa eo, ekar einwõt ejjab jimwe ñan kautiej kain eñjake kein. Bwe meñe ri tarinae ro, ippāñ jar in ri jerbal ro, rar juon kumi eo elaplok an nana, ri ailiñ ko jet, im oktak jen jabdrewot ian wa ko redik me eñjake ko ao mokta rar kōmman bwe in jelā kaki, ak io kar likit men in—im ilo jimwe ikar likit e—ñan joñan an oktak wāween eo an Scandinavian eo ear jino an etal. Ak ekar lukkun wāween an jilu ri utiej ro ilo wa eo, ro mōttan, eo ekar lukkun kajoor an kar kōmmane ñan jolok lemnok kein, im kōmman bwe en wor liki im lañliñ ilo aolep ien ko ilo ito-itak eo. Jilu ri utiej ro im armij ro remmanlok, kajjojo ilo wāween eo an make, rar jab maroñ in loe, im rar aolep ri Amedka ro; juon Nantucketer, juon Vineyarder, juon armij in Cape. Kiiō, ej Kūrijmōj ke wa eo eaar ettōr jān jikin kōttar eo an, n̄an juon jikin kōm kar bōk weather in Polar, men̄e aolep iien kōm kar ettōr jān e n̄an turear; im ilo kajjojo degree im minute in latitude eo jekar etal ie, jidik kin jidik likit winter eo ejelok tiriamo, im aolep weather ko an rebin jen kim. Ear juon ian jibboñ ko ediklok an diklok, ak ear lukkun bwil im marok jibboñ ko ilo ien oktak eo, ke ippāñ juon kōto emman wa eo ear ettōr ilo den eo ilo juon kain ettōr im mōkaj in buromõj, bwe ke iar wanlōñtak ñan deck eo ilo ien eo ej itok ilo ien lale raelep, ke iar kalimjōk tabora eo, ear ettōr ioō. Mol eo ear laplok jen mijak; Kapen Ehab ear jutak ion wa eo waan.

Ejelok kakōlkōl in nañinmij in enbwinin, ak an kar mour jen jabdrewot. Ear einwot juon armij eo kar bukwe jen wōjke eo, ke kijeek eo ear lukkun kokkure aolep pein im neen ilo an jab tile ir, ak bōklok juon mõttan jen aer kajoor. Aolepān wāween eo an eutiej, eļap, eaar āinwōt kōm̧m̧an jān bronze eo em̧wilaļ, im kar kōm̧m̧ane ilo juon m̧ōttan eo ejjab maron̄ oktak, āinwōt Cellini's cast Perseus. Ej etal jen ibwiljin koot ko an rekilmeej, im wōnmanlok wōt ilo juon turin turin mejān im bōran eo ekar bwil, mae ien eo ekar jako ilo nuknuk eo an, kwar lo juon kakōļļe edik āinwōt juon wōjke, emera im emera. Ej einwot seam eo ej jutak ilo juon wōjke elap, ñe ļan̄ eo ilōñ ej mōkaj an mōkaj an mōkaj, im ilo an jab m̧wijit juon wōt ra, ej mōttan im mōttan kilin wōjke eo jān ilōn̄ n̄an laļ, m̧okta jān an ettōrļo̧k n̄an bwidej, im likūt wōjke eo ej pād wōt ilo an m̧ōrā, bōtaab ej mour wōt. Eļan n̄e kakōļļe eo eaar ļotak ippān, ak eļan̄n̄e eaar m̧ōttan eo eaar likūt jān juon kinej eo eļap, ejjeļo̧k juon emaron̄ kar ba. Ilo jet kōtlok, ilo aolepen ito-itak eo edik ak ejelok kar kwalok kake, elaptata jen ro mōttan. Ak juon ien Tashtego's senior, juon Gay-Head Indian eo etto ibwiljin ri jerbal ro, ear ba ilo an tõmak bwe ejjab mae ien eo ear 40 an yio ke Ahab ear erom menin, innem ear itok ion, jab ilo illu in jabdrewot tarinae, ak ilo juon tarinae ilo lojet. Ak, melele in ej einwot ejjab emman, jen ta eo juon Manxman ebōrōrō ekar kwalok kake, juon emaan eo etto, eo, ekar jab kar etal jen Nantucket, ekar jab loe Ahab eo ebin. Meñe eindrein, manit ko etto kin lojet, tõmak ko etto, rar lelok ñan Manxman in etto maroñ in kile. Bwe en ejelok juon ri wa eo ekilmeej ear lukkun jumae e ke ear ba bwe elañe Kapen Ahab enaj kar babu ilo ainemon—eo emaron kar jab walok, innem ear mōñā—innem, jabdewōt eo enaj kar kōmmane jerbal eo eliktata ñan ro remij, enaj kar lo juon kakōlkōl in lotak ion jen bōran ñan neen.

Joñan an kar kajoor an aolep men ko renana an Ahab jelet io, im joñan an nana, bwe ilo ien ko moktata ikar jab lukkun kile bwe ejjab dik ian men in enana ej walok kin neen mouj eo ear jutak ion. Ear itok ñan ña bwe ne in ivory in kar ilo lojet kar kōmmane jen di ko an sperm whale eo. “Aet, ekar lukkun ebbweer jen Japan,” Gay-Head Indian eo etto ekar ba juon ien; “ak einwot wa eo an eo ekar jorrāān, ekar jilkintok bar juon mast ilo an jab itok ñan mweo. Ewōr juon an quiver in 'em.”

Ikar bwilōñ kin wāween an kar jutak. Ilo kajjojo turin Pequod's quarter deck eo, im epaak ñan mizzen shrouds ko, ekar wor juon auger hole, ekar bore enañin jimettan inch ak āindein, ilo plank eo. Neen eo ebin ear bin ilo hole eo; juon pein ej utiej, im jibwe kin juon nuknuk; Kapen Ahab ekar jutak wōt, im kalimjōk ļo̧k itulikin wa eo ej m̧akūtkūt wōt. Ekar wor juon joñan in kajoor eo ebin, juon kijejeto eo ebin, im jab maroñ in jolok, ilo an kar lukkun im ejelok mijak, ilo an kalimjōk eo. Ejelok juon nan ear konono; im ri utiej ro an rar jab ba jabdrewot ñan e; meñe ilo aolep menin kōmman ko air im kalikar ko air, rar alikar air kwalok air jab emõn, elañe jab metak, kin air bed iumin juon mejen iroij eo ej inebata. Im jab men in wōt, ak Ahab eo ekar illu ekar jutak im̧aer kōn juon debwāāl ilo mejān; ilo aolep utiej in irooj eo ejjelok etan an jet jorrāān ko rekajur.

Ejjab to, jen an kar lolok moktata ilo mejatoto, ear etal ilo cabin eo an. Ak elikin jibboñ eo, ekar aolep raan walok ñan ri jerbal ro; ej jutak ilo pivot-hole eo an, ak jijet ion juon jea eo ekar kōmmane jen ivory; ak etetal ilo deck eo. Ke lañ eo ear diklok an marok; emool, ekar jino an eddek jidik an jouj, ekar diklok an diklok an juon eo ej make iaer; einwot ke, ke wa eo ear etal jen mweo, ejelok men ak wōt marok in lojet ear kōmman an makelok jen e make. Im, tokelik, ekar walok, bwe ekar lukkun aolep ien ilo mejatoto; ak, ilo ien in, kin aolep men ko ear ba, ak ear kōmmane, ilo deck eo emera, ear einwõt ejelok tokjen ie einwõt bar juõn mast. Ak Pequod eo ekar baj kōmman juon iaḷ kiiō; jab aolep ien etal ilo wa ko; enañin aolep rikōmman whale ro rej aikuj lale ro mōttaer rar lukkun maroñ, bwe en dik ak ejelok, jen e make, ñan kōjerbal ak kōketak Ahab, kiõ; im āindein kadiwōjļo̧k, ilo juon wōt iien, kōdo̧ ko me raar ļapļo̧k raar ļapļo̧k ioon bōran, āinwōt an aolep kōdo̧ ko kālet tōrerein ko reutiej tata n̄an likūt er make ioon.

Meñe āindein, ejjab to, m̧ōņōņō eo, m̧ōņōņō eo an m̧ōņōņō eo, iien kakkije eo kōm kar itok n̄ane, eaar āinwōt jidik kōn jidik kar kareel e jān m̧wil eo an. Bwe, einwot ke leddik ro rej eb, Eprōl im Māe, rej etal ñan mweo ñan bukwōn ko rebin; meñe oak eo ediktata, ebintata, im elaptata an rup enaj jilkintok jet wōjke ko rekilmeej, ñan karwaineneik rilotok ro remõnõnõ; innem Ahab ear, ilo jemlokin, jidik uwaak ñan ikkure ko an leddik eo. Elōñlok jen juon alen ear kwalok juon kalimjōk eo edik, eo, ilo jabdewōt armij, enaj kar mōkaj an walok ilo juon ettōñ dikdik.
