Kōjām in deloñ eo ilo cabin eo ear kilōk ilowaan; kōjām ko rar aolep jerbal, im kar obrak kin coil in rigging ko. Ilo ad etal ñan forecastle eo, jekar loe slide eo an scuttle eo ej bellok. Ke jekar lo juon meram, jekar wanlaḷḷọk, im lo wōt juon rigger eo etto ijo, ekar dāpij ilo juon pea-jacket eo ekar rup. Kar jolok e ilo aolepān tōrerein ioon ruo bōjo̧, mejān ej pād laļļo̧k im kar dāpij ilo pein ko rekar dāpij. Kiki eo elaptata ear kiki ion.

“Ri wa ko jekar lo ir, Queequeg, ia eo remaroñ kar etal ñane?” io ekar ba, im kalimjōk armij eo ej kiki. Ak ekar einwõt, ke ekar bed ilo wharf eo, Queequeg ekar jab lukkun kile ta eo ij kiõ konono kake; kin menin ikar lemnok bwe ikar m̧oņe ilo menin, eļan̄n̄e en kar jab kōn kajjitōk eo an Elijah eo ejjab maron̄ meļeļe. Ak io kar jolok men eo; im bar kakōļļeik ri kiki eo, ilo an kajjirere kar kōmeļeļeik n̄an Queequeg bwe bōlen em̧m̧an tata kōm kar jijet ippān ānbwin eo; jiron e bwe en kajutak e make ekkar ñan men in. Ear likit pein ion turin ri kiki eo, einwõt ñe ej eñjake elañe ear lukkun mōrā; innem, ilo ejelok bar inebata, jijet ilo jidimkij ijo.

“Jouj! Queequeg, jab jijet ijo,” Iar ba.

“O! Perry dood seat,” Queequeg eaar ba, “iaļ eo aō; eban kōmetak mejān.”

"Mejan armij!" io ekar ba, “kōmman bwe turin mejān? turin mej eo elap an jouj innem; ak ewi joñan an pen an menono, ej eddo; wanlaḷtak, Queequeg, kwōj eddo, ej ṃōñā turin mejān ro rejeramōl. wanlaḷtak, Queequeg! Lale, enaaj ṃōkaj an ṃōñā eok. Ij bwilōñ bwe ejjab ruj.”

Queequeg ear etal jen turin bōran ri kiki eo, im ear urur tomahawk pipe eo an. Iar jijet iturin neen. Kōm kar kōmman bwe paip eo en etal ion ri kiki eo, jen juon ñan eo juon. Ilo ien in, ke ikar kajitõk ippān ilo wāween eo an ebōd, Queequeg ear letok ñan ña melele bwe, ilo āne eo an, kin an ejelok jikin jijet ko im sofa ko an aolep kain, irooj eo, irooj ro, im armij ro reutiej ilo aolepen, rar bed ilo manit in kōmman bwe jet ian ro rediklok ren mōrā ñan ottoman ro; im ñan kōmman juon mweo ilo wāween in, kwoj aikuj wōt wiaik jiljino ak joñoul armij ro rejjab jerbal, im likit ir ilo pier ko im alcove ko. Bareinwõt, ear lukkun emman ilo juon tūreep; elaplok an emman jen jea ko ilo jikin kallib ko me remaroñ ukōti ñan kein etetal ko; ilo ien ko jet, juon iroij ej kirtok rijerbal eo an, im kōnaan bwe en kōmman juon jikin jijet iumwin juon wijki eo ej eddeklok, bōlen ilo juon jikin ebwil.

Ke ej kwalok kin men kein, aolep ien Queequeg ej bōk tomahawk eo jen io, ej likit hatchet-side eo an ion bõran ri kiki eo.

“Ta men in, Queequeg?”

“Perry easy, kill-e; oh! perry easy!”

Ear wōnm̧aanļo̧k wōt kōn jet kememej ko rekabwilōnlōn̄ kōn tomahawk-pipe eo an, eo, eaar āinwōt, eaar ilo ruo kōjerbal ko an kar kōm̧m̧an bwe rikōjdat ro an ren ļōmņak im kaenōm̧m̧an jetōb eo an, ke kōm kar lukkuun m̧ōņōņō kōn rigger eo ej kiki. Kōto eo ekajur ej kiiō lukkun kobrak hole eo ekar rup, ekar jino an walok ñan e. Ear menono kin juon kain būromōj; innem ear einwõt inebata ilo lojen; innem kar jeblak juon ak ruo alen; innem jijet im rub mejen.

“Holloa!” ear menono ilo jemlokin, “wōn kom ri idaak?”

“Em̧m̧aan ro kar jilkinļo̧k,” Iaar uwaak, “n̄āāt ej etal?”

“Aet, aet, komij etal ilo wa eo, ke? Ej etal rainin. Kapen eo ear itok ilo wa eo bon eo lok.”

“Ta Kapen eo?—Ehab?”

“Wōn ijellokin e?”

Ikar kōnan kajitõk ibben jet bar kajitõk ko ikijen Ahab, ke jekar roñ juon ainikien ilo deck eo.

“Holloa! Starbuck's astir,” ekar ba ri-kōmman wa eo. “He's a lively chief mate, that; good man, and a pious; but all alive now, I must turn to.” Im ke ej ba men in ear etal ñan deck eo, im kōm kar lor.

Kiiō ekar alikar an al tak. Ejjab to rijerbal ro rar itok ilo wa eo ilo ruo im jilu; ri-kōmman ri-kōmman wa ro rar kōmman aer make; ripālele ro rar lukkun jerbal; im elōn ian armij ro ilo parijet rar boub in bōktok men ko jet ilo wa eo. Ilo ien in Kapen Ahab ekar bed wot ilo an jab alikar an bed ilowaan mweo imōn.
