Ngaphandle kwesicu, emagala ayakhula; kuwo, emagala. Ngako-ke, etifundvweni letikhicitako, khulisa tehluko.

I-crotch lokukhulunywa ngayo ekhasini lelidlule ifanelwe kukhulunywa ngayo ngekutimela. Kuyinduku lenetintfo letinetintfo letingakavami, lelingaba ngemamitha lamabili ngebudze, lefakwa ngendlela lecondzile ku starboard gunwale ngase bow, ngenhloso yekuniketa indzawo yekuphumula yekuphela kwetigodvo te harpoon, lenye incenye yayo lengcunu, lehlabako iphuma ngekuthambekela kusuka e prow. Ngaleyondlela lesikhali sivele sibe sesandleni semjikijeli waso, losihlwitsa kalula lapho siphumule khona njengobe umuntfu losebenta emuva ajikijela sibhamu sakhe eludongeni. Kulisiko kutsi kube nema harpoons lamabili laphumula esifubeni, ngekulandzelana abitwa ngetinsimbi tekucala netesibili.

Kodvwa letikhali letimbili, ngayinye ngentsambo yayo, totimbili tihlangene nelayini; intfo lekhona kutsi: kubashaya bobabili, uma kungenteka, munye ngekushesha ngemuva kwalomunye abe ngumkhoma munye; kute nangabe, ekudvonseni lokutako, lomunye kufanele advonse, lomunye angahle asagcine kubamba. Kuyintfo lephindvwe kabili ematfuba. Kepha kuvame kakhulu kwenteka kutsi ngenca yekugijima lokusheshako, lokunebudlova, lokuvuvukako kwemkhoma ngesikhatsi atfola insimbi yekucala, kuba matima kutsi umdlali we harpooneer, noma ngabe afana nembane ngekunyakata kwakhe, aphose lensimbi yesibili kuye. Nomakunjalo, njengoba lensimbi yesibili seyivele ixhumene nelayini, futsi lelayini iyagijima, kungako leso sikhali kumele, kutotonkhe tigameko, silahlwe ngaphandle kwesikebhe ngekulindzela, ngandlela tsite futsi ndzawonye; ngaphandle kwaloko ingoti lembi kakhulu beyitawufaka ekhatsi tonkhe tandla. Futsi, kutsi ngesikhatsi saHenry VIIIth, umpheki lotsite wenkantolo watfola umvuzo lomuhle ngekusungula isosi lenconywako kutsi idliwe ngema porpoises lashisiwe, le, uyakhumbula, luhlobo lwemkhoma. Ema Porpoises, ngempela, kuze kube ngulamuhla atsatfwa njengekudla lokuhle. Lenyama yentiwa ibe ngemabhola lalingana nemabhola e billiard, futsi ngekufakwa kahle kanye nekufakwa tinongo kungatsatfwa njenge turtle-balls noma emabhola enkhomo. Tinyanga letindzala taseDunfermline betibatsandza kakhulu. Bebanesibonelelo lesikhulu se-porpoise lesivela emqheleni.

Liciniso likutsi, emkhatsini webatingeli bakhe lokungenani, lomkhoma bewutawutsatfwa ngato tonkhe tandla njengesidlo lesihloniphekile, kube bekute lokunyenti ngaye; kodvwa uma ufika uhlale phansi embikwe meat-pie lecishe ibe ngemamitha lalikhulu ngebudze, ikususa indlala. Kuphela emadvodza langabandlululi kakhulu njenga Stubb, kulamalanga ahlanganyela imikhoma lephekiwe; kodvwa ema-Esquimaux akasheshi kangako. Sonkhe siyati kutsi tiphila njani ngetulu kwemikhoma, futsi tinema vintages lamadzala langakavami emafutsa esitimela lamadzala lahamba embili. Zogranda, lomunye wabodokotela babo labadvumile, uncoma imigca ye blubber yetinswane, njengaleyo lejusi kakhulu futsi leyondlako. Futsi loku kungikhumbuta kutsi emaNgisi latsite, lashiywa ngengoti eGreenland kudala ngumkhumbi wekudoba imikhoma—kutsi lamadvodza empeleni aphila tinyanga letimbalwa etincenyeni letinesikhunta temikhoma lebetishiywe elugwini ngemuva kwekuzama le blubber. Emkhatsini wema Dutch whalemen letintfo letilahliwe tibitwa ngekutsi “fritters”; loku, ngempela, kufana kakhulu, kuba nsundvu futsi kucwebetela, futsi kunuka intfo lefana ne dough-nuts noma oly-cooks yebafati basendlini labadzala base Amsterdam, uma isha. Tinekubukeka lokudliwako kangangekutsi umfokati lotiphika kakhulu akakhoni kubamba tandla takhe.

Kepha lokuchubeka kwehlisa umkhoma njengesidlo lesiphucukile, kungceba kwakhe kakhulu. Nguye inkhomo lenkhulu yemklomelo yaselwandle, lekhuluphele kakhulu kutsi ingaba yinhle. Buka le hump yakhe, lebeyitawuba kudla lokuhle njengalenyathi (lehlonishwa njengesidlo lesingakavami), kube bekungasiyo iphiramidi lecinile yemafutsa. Kepha i-spermaceti yona, kutsi i-bland futsi ikhilimu kangakanani; njengenyama lebonakalako, lehhafu-jellied, lemhlophe ye coconut ngenyanga yesitsatfu yekukhula kwayo, kepha icebe kakhulu kutsi ingakhona kuniketa sikhundla se butter. Nomakunjalo, bantfu labaningi labadla imikhoma banendlela yekuyimunca ibe ngulenye intfo, bese bayayidla. Ekulindzeni lokudze kwekuzama ebusuku kuyintfo levamile kutsi bantfu labahamba ngemikhumbi bacwilise libhisikidi labo lemkhumbi etimbiteni letinkhulu temafutsa bese bayativumela kutsi titose lapho sikhatsi lesitsite. Ngikwentile kudla kwakusihlwa lokunyenti lokuhle ngaleyo ndlela.

Esimeni se Sperm Whale lencane ingcondvo ibalwa njengesidlo lesihle. Le casket ye skull iyaphulwa ngembazo, bese lama lobes lamabili lakhuluphele, lamhlophe ayahociswa (afana ngco nema puddings lamabili lamakhulu), bese ayahlanganiswa nafulawa, bese ayaphekwa abe yi mess lemnandzi kakhulu, ngekunambitseka ngandlela tsite kufana nenhloko yematfole, lokuyintfo lefana ne epicures; futsi wonkhe umuntfu uyati kutsi lamanye emabhubesi lamancane emkhatsini wema epicures, ngekuhlala adla ebuchosheni bematfole, ngekuhamba kwesikhatsi atfola kuba nebuchopho lobuncane babo, kwentela kutsi bakhone kutjela inhloko yelitfole kusuka etinhloko tabo; lokuyintfo, mbamba, ledzinga kubandlululwa lokungakavami. Futsi nguleso sizatfu lesenta kutsi imbuzi lencane lenenhloko yelitfole lebukeka ihlakaniphile embikwayo, ngandlela tsite ngulenye yetintfo letidzabukisako longatibona. Inhloko ibuka ngandlela tsite ngekumsola, ngekutsi “Et tu Brute!” ukusho.

Akusiko, mhlawumbe, ngalokuphelele ngoba lomkhoma unemafutsa ngekweqile kangangekutsi bantfu bendzawo babonakala bakutsatsa ngekwenyanya kudliwa kwakhe; lokubonakala kuvela, ngandlela tsite, ekucabangeni lokushiwo ngaphambilini: lokusho kutsi umuntfu kufanele adle intfo lesanda kubulawa yaselwandle, futsi ayidle nayo ngekukhanya kwayo. Kodvwa akungabateki kutsi umuntfu wekucala lowake wabulala inkhomo bekatsatfwa njengembulali; mhlawumbe walengiswa; futsi kube bekavivinywa ngetinkhomo, nakanjani bekatawuvivinywa; futsi bekafanelwe ngalokucinisekile nangabe kukhona umbulali lokwentako. Iya emakethe-yenyama yeMgcibelo ebusuku ubone ticumbi tetilwane letiphilako letinetinyawo letimbili tibuka etulu emigceni lemidze yetilwane letinetinyawo letine letifile. Loko kubona akulikhiphi yini litinyo emhlathini wemuntfu lodla bantfu? Bantfu labadla bantfu? ngubani longasiye umdlali webantfu? Ngiyakutjela kutsi kutawubeketeleleka kakhulu kuFejee lowafaka luswayi phansi sitfunywa selivangeli lesinciphile endzaweni yakhe lephansi yekulwa nendlala letako; kutawubeketeleleka kakhulu kulowo Fejee lonakekelako, ngitsi, ngelilanga lekwehlulela, kunawe, gourmand lophucukile nalokhanyiselwe, lofaka emahansi ngetikhonkwane phansi futsi udla tibindi tawo letivuvukile ku paté-de-foie-gras yakho.

Kepha Stubb, udla umkhoma ngekukhanya kwawo, angitsi? futsi loko kwengeta inhlamba ekulimaleni, akunjalo? Buka sibambo sakho semmese, lapho, gourmand yami lephucukile nalekhanyisekile idla leyo nyama yenkhomo leyosiwe, leso sibambo sakhiwe ngani?—yini ngaphandle kwematsambo emnakabo yona kanye lenkhomo loyidlako? Futsi ucosha ngani ematinyo akho, ngemuva kwekudla lelihansi lelikhuluphele? Ngetinsiba tenkhukhu lefanako. Futsi nguyiphi i-quill Mabhalane weNhlangano Yekucindzetela Lunya KuGanders layikhombisa ngalokusemtsetfweni emasekhula akhe? Kuphela ngenyanga leyengcile noma letimbili lapho khona lowo mphakatsi wakhipha sincumo sekungasekeli lutfo ngaphandle kwemapeni ensimbi.





SEHLUKO 66. Kubulawa Kwebantfu Boshaka.

Uma eSouthern Fishery, iSperm Whale lebanjiwe, ngemuva kwekusebenta sikhatsi lesidze futsi ledziniwe, iletfwa eceleni ebusuku kakhulu, akusiko, njengentfo levamile lokungenani, lisiko kutsi uchubeke ngaso leso sikhatsi ebhizinisini lekumsika. Ngoba lelo bhizinisi liyintfo lecindzetelako kakhulu; akukaphelelwa masinyane kakhulu; futsi kudzinga tonkhe tandla kutsi tibeke ngako. Ngako-ke, kusetjentiswa lokuvamile kutsi kutsatfwe yonkhe imikhumbi; lasha sihloko a'lee; bese utfumela wonkhe umuntfu ngephansi e-hammock yakhe kuze kube lilanga, ngekubekelwa kutsi, kuze kube nguleso sikhatsi, ema-anchor-watches atawugcinwa; lokusho kutsi, lababili nalababili lihora, umbhangqwana ngamunye, licembu ngekujikeleta litawugibela umkhumbi kubona kutsi konkhe kuhamba kahle.

Kepha ngalesinye sikhatsi, ikakhulukati etulu kweLine ePacific, loluhlelo ngeke luphendvule nhlobo; ngoba emabutfo lamanjalo langabalwa aboshaka abutsana azungeze sidvumbu lesiboshelwe, kutsi abeshiywe kanjalo emahora lasitfupha, sitsi, ngekwelula, kancane kakhulu kunematsambo bekutawubonakala ekuseni. Kuletinye tindzawo letiningi taselwandle, nomakunjalo, lapho khona letimfishi tingabi khona kakhulu, kudla kwato lokumangalisako ngalesinye sikhatsi kungancishiswa kakhulu, ngekutivusa ngamandla nge whaling-spades lekhaliphile, inchubo nomakunjalo, le, ngalesinye sikhatsi, kubonakala ngatsi tiyakitata kakhulu tibe stiller. Kepha bekunganjalo kulelicala lelikhona nyalo ngaboshaka basePequod; nomakunjalo, kuciniseka, noma nguyiphi indvodza lengakajwayele kubona tintfo letinjalo, kutsi ibuke ngaseceleni kwakhe ngalobo busuku, beyitawucishe icabange kutsi lonkhe lwandle loluyindingilizi bekulishizi linye lelikhulu, nalabo boshaka tibungu letikulo.

Nomakunjalo, ngesikhatsi Stubb abeka i-anchor-watch ngemuva kwekudla kwakhe kwakusihlwa sekuphelile; futsi ngesikhatsi, ngekuvumelana, Queequeg kanye nemkhumbi we forecastle befika emkhunjini, kute injabulo lencane leyadalwa emkhatsini waboshaka; ngoba ngekushesha bamisa tigaba tekusika eceleni, futsi behlisa tibane letintsatfu, kute tikhiphe kukhanya lokudze elwandle loludzabukile, laba lababili labahamba ngemikhumbi, bashaya tikhali tabo letindze tekubulala imikhoma, bachubeka nekubulala boshaka ngalokungapheli, * ngekushaya tinsimbi letikhaliphile kuphela letibona kujule etincenyeni tabo. Kepha ekudidekeni lokunegwebu kwetihambi tabo letihlangene naletilwako, labashayako bebangakhoni ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi kushaya liphawu labo; futsi loku kwaletsa kwembulwa lokusha kwelunya lolungakholeki lwesitsa. Bashaya ngesihluku, hhayi kuphela ngekukhishwa kwematfumbu alomunye nalomunye, kepha njengeminsalo legucugucukako, bagoba bajikeleta, futsi baluma lababo; kwaze kwaba ngulapho lawo mathumbu abonakala agwinywa ngekuphindzaphindza ngumlomo munye, kutsi abe lite ngendlela lephikisanako nesilonda lesivulekile. Futsi bekungasiko konkhe loku. Bekungakaphephi kufaka ekhatsi tidvumbu netipoko taletilwane. Luhlobo lwekuphila lokujwayelekile noma kwe Pantheistic kwabonakala kucashe emalungwini abo kanye nematsambo abo, ngemuva kwaloko lokungabitwa ngekutsi yimphilo yemuntfu ngamunye seyihambile. Wabulawa futsi waphakanyiswa emkhunjini ngenca yesikhumba sakhe, lomunye walaboshaka wacishe watsatsa sandla saQueequeg lophuyile, ngesikhatsi azama kuvala sivalo lesifile semhlathi wakhe lobulalako.

*I-whaling-spade lesetjentiselwa kusika-ngekhatsi yentiwa ngensimbi lencono kakhulu; kumayelana nebukhulu besandla semuntfu lesivulekile; futsi ngesimo jikelele, ihambisana nesisetjentiswa sengadze ngemuva kwaloko ibitwa ngaso; emaceleni ayo kuphela lasicaba ngalokuphelele, futsi umkhawulo wayo longetulu umncane kakhulu kunalowo longephansi. Lesikhali sihlala sigcinwa sikhaliphile ngendlela lekhonakala ngayo; futsi uma isetjentiswa iyahlonishwa ngetikhatsi letitsite, njengesikele. Esokhethini layo, kufakwa sigodvo lesicinile, kusuka kumamitha lamashumi lamabili kuya kumashumi lamatsatfu ngebudze, kwentela kubamba.

“Queequeg akanandaba kutsi ngumuphi nkulunkulu lowamenta waba ngushaka,” kwasho le savage, ngebuhlungu iphakamisa sandla sayo etulu naphansi; “umshado Fejee nkulunkulu noma Nantucket nkulunkulu; kepha de nkulunkulu wat wenta shaka kumele kube lidamu linye Ingin.”
