SEHLUKO 53. I Gam.

Sizatfu lesibonakalako sekutsi kungani Ahabi angazange agibele umkhumbi wemkhoma lebesikhulume ngawo bekungulesi: umoya nelwandle bekukhombisa tiphepho. Kepha noma ngabe loku bekungasiko, bekangeke ngemuva kwako konkhe, mhlawumbe, amgibele—kwehlulela ngekutiphatsa kwakhe lokwalandzela ngetikhatsi letifanako—nangabe bekunjalo kutsi, ngenchubo yekubingelela, bekatfole imphendvulo lengakalungi yembuto lawubuta. Ngoba, njengoba kwagcina kuvele, bekangakhatsateki kutsi angahlangani, ngisho nemizuzu lesihlanu, nanoma ngumuphi kapteni langamati, ngaphandle kwekutsi angafaka sandla ngalolunye lwalolwatiso labelufuna ngendlela lemangalisako. Kepha konkhe loku kungahlala kulinganiswa ngalokwenele, bekungasiyo intfo leshiwo lapha ngekusetjentiswa lokungakavami kwemikhumbi yekudoba imikhoma uma ihlangana etilwandle tangaphandle, futsi ikakhulu endzaweni levamile yekuhamba.

Nangabe bantfu lababili labangabati bewela iPine Barrens eSifundzeni saseNew York, nobe iSalisbury Plain leseNgilandi lecindzetelekile ngendlela lefanako; uma bahlangana ngekungacabangi emahlatsini langamukeleki kangaka, laba lababili, ngekuphila kwabo, abakhoni kahle kugwema kubingelelana; kanye nekuma sikhashana kute bashintjane ngetindzaba; futsi, mhlawumbe, kuhlala phansi sikhatsi lesitsite futsi kuphumule ngekuhlangana: ke, kuyintfo lemvelo kangakanani kutsi etulu kwe Pine Barrens kanye ne Salisbury Plains letingenamkhawulo elwandle, imikhumbi lemibili yekudoba imikhoma lehlambalatana emaphetselweni emhlaba—eceleni kwe Sichiwi sase Fanning lesisodwa, noma eKing’s Mills lekhashane; kangakanani kwemvelo, ngitsi, kutsi ngaphansi kwetimo letinjalo lemikhumbi akukafaneli kutsi ishintjane ngesangcotfo kuphela, kepha ifike ekuchumaneni lokusedvute kakhulu, lokunebungani kanye nekuhlanganyela. Futsi ikakhulukati loku bekungabonakala kuyintfo lekhonako, esimeni semikhumbi lephethwe litheku linye, futsi lebakaputeni bayo, tikhulu, futsi hhayi labambalwa bemadvodza labatiwako mathupha; futsi ngenca yaloko, babe nato tonkhe tinhlobo tetintfo tasekhaya letitsandzekako tekukhuluma ngato.

Kulomkhumbi longekho sikhatsi lesidze, umncele wangaphandle, mhlawumbe, unetinhlavu emkhunjini; noma ngabe ngusiphi silinganiso, utawuciniseka kutsi umvumela kutsi abe nemaphepha latsite elusuku ngemuva kwemnyaka nobe lemibili kunaleyo yekugcina kumafayela akhe langabonakali kahle futsi lagcotjwe ngesitfupha. Futsi ngekubuyisela leyo nhlonipho, lomkhumbi loya ngaphandle bewutawutfola buhlakani bamuva bekubulala imikhoma kusuka endzaweni yekuhamba ngemikhumbi lapho kungenteka kutsi uya khona, intfo lebaluleke kakhulu kuye. Futsi ngesigaba, konkhe loku kutawuba liciniso mayelana nemikhumbi yekubulala imikhoma lewela umzila walomunye nalomunye endzaweni yekuhamba ngemikhumbi yona, noma ngabe ingekho sikhatsi lesidze ngendlela lefanako ekhaya. Ngobe kungenteka kutsi lomunye wabo utfole kudluliselwa kwetincwadzi letivela kulomunye umkhumbi wesitsatfu, futsi nyalo sewukhashane kakhulu; futsi letinye taleto tincwadzi kungenteka kutsi betingetebantfu labasemkhunjini lahlangana nabo nyalo. Ngaphandle kwaloko, bebatawushintjana ngetindzaba tekubulala imikhoma, futsi babe nengcoco levumelanako. Ngoba bebangahlangani kuphela nako konkhe kuzwelana kwebatimeli, kepha ngendlela lefanako nato tonkhe tintfo letimangalisako letivela ekuphishekeleni lokufanako kanye nekuhlupheka lokufanako kanye nebungoti.

Futsi umehluko welive bewungeke wente umehluko lobaluleke kakhulu; lokusho kutsi, uma nje bobabili labakhuluma lulwimi lunye, njengobe kunjalo ngebantfu baseMelika nesiNgisi. Noma, kuciniseka, kusuka esibalweni lesincane semikhoma yemaNgisi, imihlangano lenjalo ayikavami kakhulu kwenteka, futsi uma yenteka kunekufaneleka kakhulu kutsi kube neluhlobo lwemahloni emkhatsini wabo; ngoba umNgisi wakho ugodliwe, futsi Yankee wakho, akatsandzi lolohlobo lwentfo kunobe ngubani ngaphandle kwakhe. Ngaphandle kwaloko, ema whalers aseNgilandi ngalesinye sikhatsi atsintsa luhlobo lwebukhulu bemadolobha lamakhulu ngetulu kwema whalers ase Melika; mayelana ne Nantucketer lendze, lencane, ngetifundza takhe letingachazeki, njengeluhlobo lwemlimi waselwandle. Kepha lapho khona lobukhulu ku whalemen yemaNgisi bukhona mbamba, kungaba matima kusho, kubona kutsi ema Yankees ngelilanga linye, ngekuhlanganyela, abulala imikhoma leminyenti kunawo onkhe emaNgisi, ngekuhlanganyela, eminyakeni lelishumi. Kepha loku kuyintfo lencane lengalimati kubatingeli bemikhoma baseNgilandi, le Nantucketer ingatsatsi kakhulu ngenhlitiyo; mhlawumbe, ngoba uyati kutsi unebutsakatsaka lobumbalwa yena.

Ngako-ke, siyabona kutsi kuyo yonkhe imikhumbi lehamba ngekwehlukana elwandle, labadoba imikhoma banesizatfu lesikhulu sekuhlanganyela nebantfu—futsi banjalo. Ngesikhatsi, leminye imikhumbi yekutsengiselana lewela lomunye nalomunye emkhatsini we Atlantic, itawuvama kudlula ngaphandle kweligama linye lekubonwa, isika lomunye nalomunye etilwandle letiphakeme, njenge brace yema dandies eBroadway; futsi sonkhe sikhatsi bazitika, mhlawumbe, ekugcekeni finical phezu rig ngamunye. Njenge Men-of-War, uma batfola litfuba lekuhlangana elwandle, bacala ngekutsi badlule emgceni wekukhotsama lokungenangcondvo kanye nekukhuhla, kugoba lokunjalo kwema ensigns, kutsi akubonakali kukhona lokunyenti lokufanele-phansi kwenhlitiyo lenhle kanye nelutsandvo lwebuzalwane ngako nhlobo. Njengobe kutsintsa Slave-imikhumbi umhlangano, kungani, baphutfuma kangaka prodigious, babaleka kulomunye nalomunye ngekushesha. Futsi mayelana nemaPirates, uma atfola litfuba lekwewela ematsambo awo laphambukile, sangcotfo sekucala sitsi—“Tingaki tigogo?”—ngendlela lefanako naleyo labadvonsa imikhoma labashaya ngayo—“Tingaki imiphongolo?” Futsi lowo mbuto uma sewuphendvuliwe, ema pirates ayacondza, ngoba angabantfu lababi labahlaselako kuto totimbili tinhlangotsi, futsi abatsandzi kubona kakhulu imifanekiso lembi yalomunye nalomunye.

Kodvwa buka lomdlali wemkhoma lohlonipha Nkulunkulu, lowetsembekile, longatibonisi, lonemusa tivakashi, lotsandza kuhlalisana, lokhululekile futsi lolula! Yini leyentiwa ngumkhoma uma ahlangana nalomunye umkhoma kunobe ngusiphi simo selitulu lesihle? Une “ Gam ,” intfo lengatiwa ngalokuphelele ngiyo yonkhe leminye imikhumbi kangangekutsi beyingakaze ilive ngaleligama ngisho; futsi uma ngabe ngengoti kufanele beve ngako, bavele bamomotseke, futsi baphindza tintfo letidlalako mayelana “nema-spouters” kanye “ne-blubber-boilers,” kanye nalokufana nekubabata lokuhle. Kungani kutsi bonkhe batsengisi-bemikhumbi, kanye nawo onkhe emaPirates kanye nemadvodza eMan-of-War, kanye nebashayeli bemikhumbi yeTigcila, bawutsatsa njengalobalulekile umuva wekwedzelela lonjalo mayelana nemikhumbi yeMkhoma; lo ngumbuto lokungaba matima kuwuphendvula. Ngoba, esimeni se-pirates, sitsi, ngingatsandza kwati kutsi ngabe lowo msebenti wabo unenkhatimulo lengakavami ngayo. Ngalesinye sikhatsi iphela ngekuphakama lokungakavami, ngempela; kodvwa kuphela endzaweni yekulengiswa. Futsi ngaphandle kwaloko, nangabe umuntfu aphakanyiswa ngaleyo ndlela lengakavami, akanaso sisekelo lesifanele sekuphakama kwakhe lokusetulu. Ngako-ke, ngiphetsa ngekutsi, ngekutigabatisa ngekutsi uphakamiselwe ngetulu kwemkhoma, kuleso sivumelwano lomphangi akanasizatfu lesicinile sekuma kuso.

Kodvwa yini iGam? Ungakhatsala umunwe wakho wekukhomba ugijima etulu naphansi emakholomu etichazamagama, futsi ungalitfoli leligama. Nakanjani, kudzinga inchazelo, futsi kufanele kufakwe ku-Lexicon. Ngalowo mbono, ake ngiwuchaze ngekufundza.
