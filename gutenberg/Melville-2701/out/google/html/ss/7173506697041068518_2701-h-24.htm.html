Ngako-ke umkhumbi waphindze wafinyetwa, futsi konkhe kwendlula cishe njengasebusuku bangaphambilini; kuphela, umsindvo wetinsando, kanye nekuhubha kwelitje lekugaya kwavakala kwaze kwaba cishe ekuseni, njengoba lamadvodza asebenta kamatima ngetibane ekuhlanganiseni lokuphelele nalokucophelelako kwetikebhe letisele kanye nekulola tikhali tawo letinsha tekusasa. Ngaleso sikhatsi, nge-keel lephukile yemkhumbi wa-Ahabi lophukile umbati wamentela lomunye umlente; ngesikhatsi asafana njengasebusuku bangaphambilini, Ahabi lobekadzabukile wema acinile ngaphakatsi kwesikebhe sakhe; kubuka kwakhe lokufihlekile, kwe-heliotrope lokulindzelekile kwabuyela emuva ku-dial yayo; wahlala ngasemphumalanga kute atfole lilanga lekucala.
