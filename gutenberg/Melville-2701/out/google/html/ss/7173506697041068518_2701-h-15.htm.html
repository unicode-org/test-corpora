SEHLUKO 74. Inhloko Yemkhoma Wesilisa—Umbono Lohlukile.

Naku, nyalo, imikhoma lemibili lemikhulu, ibeke tinhloko tayo ndzawonye; asihlanganise nabo, sibeke ndzawonye letfu.

Kuloluhlelo lolukhulu lwema folio leviathans, iSperm Whale kanye ne Right Whale ngiyo lephawuleka kakhulu. Yiyo kuphela imikhoma letingelwa njalo ngumuntfu. Ku Nantucketer, betfula kweqisa lokubili kwato tonkhe tinhlobo letatiwako temkhoma. Njengobe umehluko wangaphandle emkhatsini wabo ubonakala kakhulu etinhloko tabo; futsi njengenhloko yaloyo naloyo lomzuzu ulenga eceleni kwePequod; futsi njengoba singahamba ngekukhululeka kusuka kulomunye kuya kulomunye, ngekutsi nje sinyatsele ngaphesheya kwemkhumbi:—kuphi, ngingatsandza kwati, utawutfola litfuba lelincono lekufundza i-cetology lesebentako kunalapha?

Ekucaleni, ushaywa ngumehluko jikelele emkhatsini waletinhloko. Bobabili bakhulu ngalokwenele kuwo wonkhe unembeza; kepha kunekulinganisa lokutsite kwetibalo ku Sperm Whale's le Right Whale's ngekudzabukisa kushoda. Kukhona umlingisi lomnyenti enhloko yeSperm Whale. Njengobe ukubona, ngekungafuni unikela ngebukhulu lobukhulu kuye, endzabeni yesitfunti lesigcwele. Kulesikhatsi lesikhona, futsi, lesitfunti siphakanyiswa ngumbala wephepha neluswayi wenhloko yakhe esicongweni, kuniketa luphawu lweminyaka lesetulu kanye nesipiliyoni lesikhulu. Ngamafuphi, nguye loyo labadobi labakubita ngekwebuchwephesha ngekutsi “ngumkhoma lonenhloko lempunga.”

Nyalo asesicaphele kutsi yini lengafani kakhulu kuletinhloko—lokunguletitfo letimbili letibaluleke kakhulu, liso nendlebe. Emuva khashane eceleni kwenhloko, futsi phansi phansi, edvute ne engela yemhlathi wemkhoma noma nguyiphi, uma usesha kancane, utawugcina ubone liso lelite ema lash, longatsandza kutsi libe liso le colt lencane; ngako-ke kuto tonkhe tilinganiso kusebukhuluni benhloko.

Manje, kusuka kulesikhundla lesingakavami eceleni semehlo emkhoma, kuyacaca kutsi angeke aze abone intfo lesembili ngco, hhayi ngetulu kwekutsi angabona lenye leseceleni ngco. Ngalamanye, indzawo emehlo emkhoma ihambisana neyetindlebe temuntfu; futsi ungacabanga, wena, kutsi bekutawuhamba njani nawe, ngabe bewuhlola tintfo eceleni ngetindlebe takho. Utawutfola kutsi ungayala kuphela emadigri langaba ngemashumi lamatsatfu ekubona kusengaphambili kwelayini lecondzile yeseceleni yekubona; futsi kwaba nalamanye langaba ngemashumi lamatsatfu ngemuva kwayo. Kube sitsa sakho lesibuhlungu kakhulu besihamba sicondze kuwe, siphakamise inkemba emini, bewungeke ukhone kusibona, njengasekutsi besikweba ngemuva. Ngeligama linye, bewutawuba nemihlane lemibili, ngaleyo ndlela; kepha, ngesikhatsi lesifanako, futsi, emaphambili lamabili (emaphambili laseceleni): ngobe yini leyenta phambili kwemuntfu—yini, mbamba, kepha emehlo akhe?

Ngetulu kwaloko, ngesikhatsi kuletinye tilwane letiningi lengingacabanga ngato nyalo, emehlo ahlanyelwe ngendlela lengabonakali kutsi ahlanganise emandla awo ekubona, kute akhicite sitfombe sinye hhayi letimbili ebuchosheni; indzawo lemangalisako yemehlo emkhoma, lehlukaniswe ngemphumelelo njengoba anjalo ngetinyawo letiningi te cubic tenhloko lecinile, lephakeme emkhatsini wato njengentsaba lenkhulu lehlukanisa emachibi lamabili etigodzini; loku, vele, kumele kuhlukanise ngalokuphelele imibono leniketwa sicu ngasinye lesitimele. Ngako-ke, lomkhoma kumele ubone sitfombe sinye lesehlukile kuloluhlangotsi, nalesinye sitfombe lesehlukile kuloluhlangotsi; ngesikhatsi konkhe lokusemkhatsini kufanele kube bumnyama lobujulile futsi kungabi lutfo kuye. Ecinisweni, umuntfu angatsiwa ubuka emhlabeni asebhokisini lekugadza lelinemabhande lamabili lahlanganisiwe elifasitelo lakhe. Kepha ngemkhoma, lama sashes lamabili afakwa ngekwehlukana, kwenta emafasitelo lamabili lahlukile, kepha ngekudzabukisa kukhubateka kubona. Loku lokuhlukile kwemehlo emkhoma yintfo lekumele ihlale ikhunjulwa ekudobeni; futsi akhunjulwe ngumfundzi kuletinye tindzawo letilandzelako.

Umbuto wekulangatelelela lwati futsi lodida kakhulu ungacala mayelana nalendzaba lebonakalako njengekutsintsa iLeviathan. Kepha kufanele ngenetiseke ngekutsi ngibe nesiluleko. Ngako-ke uma nje emehlo emuntfu avulekile ekukhanyeni, sento sekubona asitivumeli; lokusho kutsi, angeke-ke akhone kusita ngemshini kubona nobe ngutiphi tintfo letisembikwakhe. Noma kunjalo, lokuhlangenwe nako kwanoma ngubani kutamfundzisa, kutsi noma angatsatsa tintfo letingakhetsi ngekubuka kanye, akunakwenteka ngalokuphelele kutsi, ngekunaka, futsi ngalokuphelele, ahlole nobe ngutiphi tintfo letimbili—noma ngabe tikhulu kangakanani nobe tincane kangakanani—ngesikhatsi sinye; never mind uma balala eceleni batsintsana. Kepha uma nyalo ufika wehlukanisa letintfo letimbili, bese utungeleta ngayinye ngembutsano webumnyama lobujulile; ke, kute ubone lomunye wabo, ngendlela yekutsi uletse ingcondvo yakho kutsi ibeketelele kuyo, lomunye utawukhishwa ngalokuphelele ekucaphelweni kwakho kwesikhatsi lesiphila kuso. Kunjani-ke ngalomkhoma? Kuliciniso, womabili emehlo akhe, kuwo, kufanele ente ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi; kodvwa ingcondvo yakhe iphelele kakhulu yini, iyahlanganisa futsi ayicashile kunaleyo yemuntfu, kangangekutsi ngesikhatsi sinye angakhona kuhlola ngekunakisisa ematfuba lamabili lahlukile, linye liseceleni kwakhe, lelinye liseceleni kwakhe ngco? Uma angakhona, ngako-ke kuyintfo lemangalisako yini kuye, kube shengatsi umuntfu bekakhona ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi kudlula emibonisweni yetinkinga letimbili letehlukene ku-Euclid. Nobe, kuphenywe ngalokucinile, kukhona yini incongruity kulokucatsanisa.

Kungenteka kutsi kube yintfo lengasebenti, kepha bekusolo kubonakala kimi, kutsi kugucugucuka lokumangalisako kwekuhamba lokukhonjiswa nguletinye imikhoma uma tihlaselwe tikebhe letintsatfu noma letine; emahloni kanye nemtfwalo wekwesaba kwe queer, lokuvamile kangaka kumikhoma lenjalo; Ngicabanga kutsi konkhe loku kuvela ngalokungacondzile ekudidekeni lokungenalusizo kwekutsandza, lapho khona emandla abo lahlukene futsi laphikisanako embono kumele abafake khona.

Kepha indlebe yemkhoma igcwele kulangatelela kwati njengeliso. Uma ungumfokati lophelele esiveni sabo, ungatingela ngetulu kwaletinhloko letimbili emahora, futsi ungaze utfole leso sicu semutimba. Indlebe ayinalo licembe langaphandle noma ngabe yini; futsi emgodzini wona ngekwawo ungakhona kufaka i-quill, ngako-ke umzuzu lomangalisako. Ihlala kancane ngemuva kweliso. Ngekuphatselene netindlebe tabo, lomehluko lobalulekile kumele ubukwe emkhatsini we sperm whale kanye nekwekudla. Ngesikhatsi indlebe yaleyo yekucala inekuvuleka kwangaphandle, leyo yekugcina ivalwe ngalokuphelele futsi ngekulingana ngelucwencwe, kwentela kutsi ingabonakali ngalokuphelele kusuka ngaphandle.

Akumangalisi yini, kutsi silwane lesikhulu kangaka njengemkhoma sibone umhlaba ngeliso lelincane kangaka, futsi sive kudvuma ngendlebe lencane kuneyemvundla? Kepha nangabe emehlo akhe abebanti njengelensi yesibonakudze lesikhulu saHerschel; netindlebe takhe tivulekile njengemaveranda emasontfo lamakhulu; loko bekutawenta kutsi angasaboni yini, nobe eve kahle? Cha nhlobo.—Kungani-ke wetama ‘kwandzisa’ ingcondvo yakho? Lihlanyele.

Ake manje nganoma ngutiphi ti-levers netinjini temfutfo lesinato esandleni, cant ngetulu kwenhloko ye-sperm whale, kute ilale phansi etulu; bese, ukhuphuka ngeliladi uye esicongweni, ube nekubuka phansi emlonyeni; futsi kube bekungasiko kutsi lomtimba nyalo sewuhlukaniswe ngalokuphelele nawo, ngesibane besingahle sehlele eMhumeni lomkhulu wase Kentucky Mammoth wesisu sakhe. Kepha asibambelele lapha ngalelitinyo, sibuke ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi lapho sikhona khona. Yeka umlomo lomuhle mbamba futsi lobukeka umsulwa! kusuka phansi kuya eluphahleni, kulayiniwe, noma kunaloko kubhalwe emaphepha nge membrane lemhlophe lecwebetelako, lecwebetelako njenge satins yemakoti.

Kepha phuma nyalo, ubuke lomhlathi longephansi lophawulekako, lobonakala njengesivalo lesidze lesincane se snuff-box lenkhulu kakhulu, lene hinge ngakulolunye luhlangotsi, esikhundleni seluhlangotsi lunye. Uma uyi-pry up, kwentela kutsi uyitfole ngetulu kwenhloko, bese uveta imigca yayo yematinyo, kubonakala ngatsi yi-portcullis lemangalisako; futsi lokunjalo, maye! kufakazela labaningi wight lephuyile ekudobeni, lapho khona letinhlavu tiwela ngemandla ekubethela. Kepha kwesabeka kakhulu kubona, uma uhamba phansi elwandle, ubona umkhoma lotsite lone sulky, untanta lapho ulengiswe, nemhlathi wawo lomkhulu, longaba ngemamitha lalishumi nesihlanu ngebudze, ulenga ucondzile phansi ngema-angle lafanele nemtimba wakhe, kuwo wonkhe umhlaba njenge jib-boom yemkhumbi. Lomkhoma awukafi; uphelelwa ngemoya kuphela; ngaphandle kwetinhlobo, mhlawumbe; sifo sekuphelelwa ngemandla; futsi supine kangaka, kangangekutsi emahinji emihlathi yakhe akhululekile, amshiya lapho kuleyo ndlela ungainly simo, inhlazo kuso sonkhe sive sakhe, lokufanele, ngaphandle kwekungabata, imprecate lock-jaws phezu kwakhe.

Etimeni letinyenti lomhlathi longephansi—lokhululwa kalula ngumdvwebi lofundzisiwe—uyakhululwa bese uphakanyiswa emkhunjini ngenhloso yekukhipha ematinyo etindlovu, kanye nekuniketa leyo whalebone lecinile lemhlophe lapho khona badobi benta tonkhe tinhlobo tetintfo letimangalisako, kufaka ekhatsi tindvuku kuya ku-umbrellastoles.

Nge hoist lendze, ledziniwe umhlathi udvonswa ebhodini, kube shengatsi sikhonkwane; futsi uma sikhatsi lesifanele sifika—emalanga lambalwa ngemuva kwalomunye umsebenti—Queequeg, Daggoo, na Tashtego, njengabodokotela bematinyo labaphumelelako, babekelwa kudvweba ematinyo. Ngesipedi sekusika lesikhaliphile, iQueequeg ishaya tinsini; bese lomhlathi uyashaywa phansi kuya kuma ringbolts, bese i tackle iyalungiswa kusuka etulu, badvonsa lamatinyo, njengoba tinkhomo tase Michigan tidvonsa ticu tema oak lamadzala ngaphandle kwemhlaba wetigodvo tasendle. Kuvame kuba nemazinyo langemashumi lamane nakubili kuwo onkhe; emikhomeni lendzala, legugile kakhulu, kepha lengakaboli; noma kugcwaliswe ngemuva kwemfashini yetfu yekufakelwa. Lomhlathi ngemuva kwaloko uyasahlwa ube ngema slabs, bese uyahlanganiswa njenge joists yekwakha tindlu.





SEHLUKO 75. Inhloko Yemkhoma Losekudla—Umbono Lohlukile.

Kwewela umkhumbi, manje asibuke sikhatsi lesidze kahle inhloko yeMkhoma Wesekudla.

Njengoba ngekwakheka jikelele inhloko ye Sperm Whale lehloniphekile ingafaniswa nencola yemphi yemaRoma (ikakhulu ngaphambili, lapho khona iyindingilizi kakhulu); ngako, ngekubuka lokubanti, inhloko ye Right Whale ithwala kufana lokungakafaneli nesicathulo lesikhulu lesinetinzwane te galliot. Eminyakeni lengemakhulu lamabili leyengcile umhambi lomdzala waseDashi wafananisa kwakheka kwayo nekwakheka kwekugcina kwemkhiciti weticatfulo. Futsi kulesicatfulo lesifanako sekugcina noma, lowo mkhulu wendzaba yekukhulisa, nebantfwana lababhubhisako, bekangahlala kahle kakhulu, yena kanye nayo yonkhe inzalo yakhe.

Kepha njengoba usondzela kulenhloko lenkhulu icala kutsatsa tincenye letehlukene, ngekuya kwembono wakho. Uma ume esicongweni sayo ubuke letimbobo letimbili letimise ngendlela ye F, ungatsatsa yonkhe inhloko ye bass-viol lenkhulu, kanye naleti spiracles, tikhala letise sounding-board yayo. Bese, futsi, uma ubeka liso lakho kulentfo lemangalisako, lene crested, lefana neliklamo lesetulu kwalesicumbi—lentfo leluhlata, lene barnacled, lebantfu base Greenland labayibita ngekutsi “umchele,” bese badobi baseNingizimu batsi “bonnet” ye Right Whale; ugcoba emehlo akho kuloku kuphela, bewutsatsa inhloko yesicu se-oak letsite lenkhulu, lenesidleke senyoni esifubeni sayo. Noma ngabe ngusiphi silinganiso, uma ubuka leto tinkalankala letiphilako letihlala lapha kulebhonethi, umcondvo lonjalo utawucishe uciniseke kutsi utawufika engcondvweni yakho; ngaphandle nangabe, mbamba, kutsandza kwakho kulungiswe ligama lebuchwepheshe lelitsi “umchele” nalo leliniketwe lona; lapho khona utawutsatsa sitsakatelo lesikhulu sekucabanga kutsi lesilwane lesikhulu lesinemandla empeleni siyinkhosi yaselwandle lene-diadem, umchele waso loluhlata lobekwe ndzawonye kuye ngalendlela lemangalisako. Kepha uma ngabe lomkhoma uyinkhosi, ungumfana lobukeka sulky kakhulu kutsi angahlobisa i-diadem. Buka leyo mlomo lengephansi lelengako! yini sulk lenkhulu kanye ne pout lapho! i sulk ne pout, ngesilinganiso sekubata, cishe ngemamitha lamashumi lamabili ngebudze kanye nemamitha lasihlanu ngekujula; i sulk ne pout letakunika emalitha langaba ngu 500 emafutsa kanye nalokunye.

Kubuhlungu lokukhulu, nyalo, kutsi lomkhoma lonelishwa kufanele ube nemilomo yemvundla. Le fissure ingaba ngunyawo ngaphesheya. Kungenteka kutsi make ngesikhatsi lesibalulekile bekahamba ngemkhumbi ehla elugwini lwasePeru, ngesikhatsi kutamatama kwemhlaba kwenta kutsi lolwandle luvuleke. Ngetulu kwalomlomo, njengangetulu kwemkhawulo loshelelako, nyalo siyashelela emlonyeni. Ngetulu kwelivi lami bengise Mackinaw, kufanele ngitsatse loku kutsi kube ngekhatsi kwe wigwam yaseNdiya. Nkhosi lenhle! nguyona yini indlela Jona lahamba ngayo? Loluphahla lucishe lube ngemamitha lalishumi nakubili ngekuphakama, futsi lugijima luye ekhoneni lelikhaliphile lelihle, kube shengatsi kune ridge-pole levamile lapho; ngesikhatsi letinhlangotsi letinetimbambo, letigobile, letinetinwele, tisiniketa leto tinhlangotsi letimangalisako, letihhafu letimile, letimise njenge scimetar te whalebone, sitsi emakhulu lamatsatfu eceleni, lokuya kusuka endzaweni lengetulu yenhloko noma litsambo lemchele, takha leto timphumphutse tase Venetian letikhulunywe ngato kuletinye tindzawo. Emaphetselo alamatsambo anemicu leneboya, lapho khona iRight Whale icindzetela emanti, futsi lapho khona intfo leyinkimbinkimbi igcina imfishi lencane, uma ivulekile idlula etilwandle te brit ngesikhatsi sekudla. Ema blinds lasemkhatsini ematsambo, njengoba amile ngekulandzelana kwawo kwemvelo, kunemamaki latsite lafuna kwati, emagoba, imigodzi, kanye netigodzi, lapho khona labanye baholi babala iminyaka yalesilwane, njengeminyaka ye oak ngemaringi ayo layindingilizi. Noma kuciniseka kwalendlela kukhashane nekubonisa, kepha kunekunambitseka kwematfuba lafanako. Noma ngabe ngusiphi silinganiso, uma sivumelana nayo, kumele sinikete iminyaka lemikhulu kakhulu ku Right Whale kunaloko lokutawubonakala kunengcondvo ekucaleni.

Etikhatsini takadzeni, kubonakala shengatsi bekunetintfo letifunwa kakhulu letiphatselene naletimphumphutse. Lomunye umhambi wasePurchas uwabita ngekutsi “tinwele” letimangalisako letingekhatsi emlonyeni wemkhoma;* lomunye, “tinwele tetingulube”; umnumzane wesitsatfu lomdzala eHackluyt usebentisa lolulwimi lolulandzelako loluhle: “Kunetimphiko leticishe tibe ngemakhulu lamabili nemashumi lasihlanu letikhula ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi se- upper chop yakhe , letigoba ngetulu kwelulwimi lwakhe ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi semlomo wakhe.”

*Loku kusikhumbuta kutsi iRight Whale ngempela ineluhlobo lwetinwele, noma-ke emadevu, lafaka tinwele letimbalwa letimhlophe letisakateke endzaweni lengetulu yekuphela kwangaphandle kwemhlatsi longephansi. Ngalesinye sikhatsi lama tufts aniketa inkhulumo kunaloko brigandish ebusweni bakhe ngalenye indlela solemn.

Njengobe wonkhe umuntfu ati, leti “tinwele tengulube,” “timphiko,” “tinwele,” “timphumphutse,” nobe yini lokutsandzako, tiniketa lamakhosikati emabhus abo kanye naletinye tintfo tekucinisa. Kepha kuloku lokukhetsekile, kufuna sekuyisikhathi lesidze kwehla. Kwaba ngesikhatsi seNdlovukati Anne lapho khona lelitsambo belisenkhatimulweni yalo, ifarthingale beyikhona ngaleso sikhatsi yonkhe imfashini. Futsi njengobe lawo makhosikati asendvulo bekahambahamba ngekujabula, noma nje emihlatsini yemkhoma, njengobe ungasho; noma kunjalo, eshaweni, ngekungacabangi lokufanako, ngabe tsine kulamalanga sindiza ngaphansi kwemihlatsi lefanako kute sivikeleke; isambulela kuba lithende lelisakateke etulu kwelitsambo linye.

Kepha nyalo khohlwa konkhe ngetimphumphutse kanye netinwele sikhashana, futsi, umile emlonyeni weMkhoma Lofanele, buka lokutungeletile kabusha. Ngekubona onkhe lama colonnades ematsambo ahamba ngendlela lefanele, bewungeke yini ucabange kutsi ungekhatsi kwesicu lesikhulu sase Haarlem, futsi ubuka emaphayiphi aso layinkhulungwane? Kute sibe nekhaphethi kulesicu sine-rug ye-Turkey lethambile kakhulu—lulwimi, lolunamatsele, njengobe bekungatsiwa, phansi emlonyeni. Ikhuluphele kakhulu futsi ithambile, futsi ifanele kutsi idzabuke ibe tincetu ekuyiphakamiseni emkhunjini. Lolulwimi lolutsite nyalo lolusembikwetfu; ngekubuka lokudlulako kufanele ngitsi bekuyi-six-barreller; lokusho kutsi itakunika emafutsa langaba ngaleyo ndlela.

Ere loku, kufanele kutsi ulibonile ngalokucacile liciniso laloko lengicale ngako—kutsi iSperm Whale neRight Whale inetinhloko leticishe tihluke ngalokuphelele. Kufinyeta, ke: ku Right Whale’s kute umtfombo lomkhulu wesilisa; kute ematinyo etindlovu nhlobo; kute i-mandible lendze, lencane yemhlathi longephansi, njengaleyo ye-Sperm Whale. Noma eSperm Whale kute leto timphumphutse tematsambo; kute umlomo lomkhulu longephansi; futsi cishe lutfo lwelulwimi. Kuphindze futsi, iRight Whale inemigodzi lemibili yangaphandle ye spout, iSperm Whale yinye kuphela.

Buka kwekugcina kwakho, nyalo, kuletinhloko letihloniphekile letinetimphahla, ngesikhatsi tisalele ndzawonye; ngobe munye utawucwila masinyane, angabhalwa, elwandle; lomunye angeke abe sikhatsi lesidze kakhulu ekulandzeleni.

Ungakhona yini kubamba inkhulumo ye Sperm Whale lapho? Kuyafanana lafa ngako, kuphela letinye tishwapha letindze esimongweni letibonakala setiphelile nyalo. Ngicabanga kutsi siphongo sakhe lesibanti sigcwele kuthula lokufana ne-prairie, lokutalwa ngekunganaki lokucabangako mayelana nekufa. Kodvwa maka indlela lenye inhloko lekhuluma ngayo. Bona lowo mlomo longephansi lomangalisako, locindzetelwe ngengoti eceleni kwemkhumbi, ngako-ke ngekuciniseka kutsi umukele umhlatsi. Yonkhe lenhloko ayibonakali yini ikhuluma ngesincumo lesikhulu lesisebentako ekubhekaneni nekufa? LoMkhoma Lolungile ngiwutsatsa kutsi bewunguStoic; i-Sperm Whale, umPlato, lokungenteka kutsi watsatsa Spinoza eminyakeni yakhe yekugcina.





SEHLUKO 76. I-Ram-Leshayako.

Ere kuyekela, for the nonce, inhloko ye Sperm Whale, bengingatsandza wena, njenge physiologist lenengcondvo, kalula-ikakhulukati uphawule sici sayo sangaphambili, kuko konkhe kuhlanganiswa kwayo lokuhlanganisiwe. Ngingatsandza kutsi uyiphenye nyalo ngembono kuphela wekwakha kuwe silinganiso lesingahabisi, lesihlakaniphile sanoma ngumaphi emandla ekushaya-ram langafakwa lapho. Kuphela lokunye kuphela lokumnandzi lokungakhunjulwa kalula—kufa lokumnandzi kwemtingeli weluju wase Ohio, lobekafuna luju esifubeni sesihlahla lesinembobo, watfola sitolo lesikhulu kakhulu salo, kangangekutsi ancike kakhulu, lamncela, kangangekutsi wafa agcotjiwe. Bangaki, nicabanga kutsi, labawele ngendlela lefanako enhloko yaPlato yeluju, futsi babhubha lapho kamnandzi?





SEHLUKO 79. I-Prairie.

Kuhlola imigca yebuso bakhe, noma kuva emabhubhu enhloko yale Leviathan; loku yintfo lekute iPhysiognomist noma iPrenologist lesengakayenti. Libhizinisi lelinjalo belingabonakala licishe libe nelitsemba njenga Lavater kutsi ahlole imibimbi eDwaleni lase Gibraltar, noma Gall agibele liladi futsi aphatse kabi iDome ye Pantheon. Noma kunjalo, kulomsebenti wakhe lodvumile, Lavater akagcini nje ngekuphatsa buso bebantfu lobehlukahlukene, kepha uphindze afundze ngekunakisisa buso bemahhashi, tinyoni, tinyoka, kanye netimfishi; futsi uhlala kabanti ekuguculweni kwekuveta lokubonakalako kuyo. Futsi Gall nemfundzi wakhe Spurzheim behlulekile kulahla letinye tintfo letitsintsa timphawu te-phrenological taletinye tidalwa ngaphandle kwemuntfu. Ngako-ke, noma ngingafaneleki kahle kutsi ngibe lihlahlandlela, ekusebentiseni lama-semi-sciences lamabili kumkhoma, ngitawenta umtamo wami. Ngizama tonkhe tintfo; Ngiyakufeza loko lengingakhona kukwenta.

Uma kubukwa ngekwemtimba, iSperm Whale silwane lesingakavami. Akanayo imphumulo lefanele. Futsi njengobe imphumulo iyinhloko futsi ibonakala kakhulu etimphawu; futsi njengobe mhlawumbe iningi ligucula futsi ekugcineni lilawule kuveta kwabo lokuhlanganisiwe; ngako bekungabonakala shengatsi konkhe kungabikhona kwayo, njengesijobelelo sangaphandle, kumele kutsintse kakhulu buso bemkhoma. Ngoba njengasekulima ingadzeni yendzawo, i-spire, i-cupola, sikhumbuto, noma umbhoshongo weluhlobo lolutsite, kutsatfwa njengalokucishe kube yintfo ledzingekako ekucedzeni lendzawo; ngako kute buso lobungaba physiognomically ekugcineni ngaphandle kwe belfry lephakeme levulekile-yemsebenti wemphumulo. Dash imphumulo kusuka kuPhidias emabula Jove, futsi yini insali ledzabukisako! Noma kunjalo, Leviathan unemandla lamakhulu kakhulu, tonkhe tilinganiso takhe tiphakeme kakhulu, kangangekutsi kushoda lokufanako lokukhona kuJove lobatiwe bekuyintfo lembi kakhulu, kuye kute sici nhlobo. Cha, kubukhulu lobwengetiwe. Imphumulo kumkhoma bekutawuba yintfo lengakafaneli. Njengoba eluhambeni lwakho lwe physiognomical uhamba ngesikebhe ujikeleta inhloko yakhe lenkhulu esikebheni sakho se jolly-boat, imibono yakho lehloniphekile ngaye ayize itfukwe ngumcabango wekutsi unemphumulo letawudvonswa. Kutigcabha lokubhubhisako, lokuvame kakhulu kuphikelela kutsi kuphazamise ngisho nalapho kubona i-beadle yasebukhosini lenemandla kakhulu esihlalweni sakhe sebukhosi.

Kuletinye tintfo, mhlawumbe umbono lomangalisako kakhulu we physiognomical kutsi ube nawo we Sperm Whale, ngulowo wekucala lokugcwele kwenhloko yakhe. Lesi sici siphakeme kakhulu.

Emcabangweni, siphongo semuntfu lesihle sifana neMphumalanga uma sikhatsateka ngekusa. Ekuphumuleni kwelidlelo, siphongo lesigobile senkunzi sinekutsintsa kwe grand kuwo. Kuphusha umbhobho losindzako etulu entsabeni kungcolisa, siphongo sendlovu sinemandla. Umuntfu noma silwane, lesiphongo lesiyimfihlakalo sifana naleso siphawu lesikhulu seligolide lesifakwe babusi baseJalimane emitsetfweni yabo. Lisho—“Nkulunkulu: kwentiwe lamuhla ngesandla sami.” Kepha etidalweni letinyenti, cha kumuntfu yena, imvamisa kakhulu siphongo simane nje sibe ngumgca wemhlaba we-alpine lolele eceleni kwemgca welichwa. Bambalwa emabunti lafana nekukhuphuka kwaShakespeare noma kwaMelancthon ngetulu kakhulu, futsi kwehla phansi kakhulu, kangangekutsi emehlo wona ngekwawo abonakala acacile, aphakadze, emachibi etintsaba langenawo emagagasi; futsi konkhe ngetulu kwawo ekushwaphaneni kwesimongcondvo, ubonakala ulandzelela imicabango lenetimphondvo lehla lapho iyonatsa, njengoba batingeli base Highland balandzelela imibhalo yelichwa yenyamatane. Kepha ku Sperm Whale lenkhulu, lesitfunti lesiphakeme nalesinemandla lesifana nankulunkulu lesikhona esiphongweni sikhuliswa kakhulu, kangangekutsi kubuka kuso, kulokubuka lokugcwele kwangaphambili, uva Bunkulunkulu kanye nemandla ekwesaba ngemandla kakhulu kunasekuboneni noma nguyiphi lenye intfo emvelweni lephilako. Ngobe kute lobonako liphuzu ngalokucondzile; kute sici sinye lesehlukile lesembulwako; kute imphumulo, emehlo, tindlebe, nobe umlomo; kute buso; akanawo, lokufanele; lutfo ngaphandle kwalowo mkhatsi munye lobanti wesimongcondvo, logcwele timphicaphicwano; dumbly kwehlisa ngekubhujiswa kwetikebhe, nemikhumbi, nebantfu. Noma, kuphrofayela, ingabe le brow lemangalisako iyancipha; noma ngaleyo ndlela ibukwa bukhulu bayo akubusi kuwe kanjalo. Kuphrofayili, uyabona ngalokucacile kutsi kucindzeteleka lokuvundlile, lokusemkhatsini wesimongcondvo, lokutsi, emuntfwini, luphawu lwaLavater lwebuhlakani.

Kodvwa kanjani? I-Genius ku-Sperm Whale? Ingabe iSperm Whale yake yabhala incwadzi, yakhuluma inkhulumo? Cha, buhlakani bakhe lobukhulu bumenyetelwa ngekungakwenti kwakhe lutfo lolutsite kute akufakazele. Kumenyezelwa ngaphezu kwaloko ekuthuleni kwakhe kwe-pyramidical. Futsi loku kungikhumbuta kutsi kube iSperm Whale lenkhulu beyatiwa nguMhlaba lomncane waseMphumalanga, ngabe waba ngunkulunkulu ngemicabango yabo yebantfwana-bemilingo. Bawenta ingwenya yaseNayili ibe ngunkulunkulu, ngobe ingwenya ayinalulwimi; futsi iSperm Whale ayinalo lulwimi, noma lokungenani yincane kakhulu, kutsi ayikhoni kuphuma. Uma ngabe ngemuva kwaloku noma ngusiphi sive lesinemasiko laphakeme, lesinetinkondlo sitawuheha emuva elungelweni labo lekutalwa, bonkulunkulu labajabulisako be-May-day basendvulo; futsi ababeke esihlalweni sebukhosi ngekuphila esibhakabhakeni lesinekutigcabha nyalo; entsabeni leseyingasekho tipo; bese uciniseka, uphakanyiselwe esihlalweni lesiphakeme saJove, iSperm Whale lenkhulu itawubusa.

Champollion wacondza emagama labhalwe ngemagama labhalwe ngemagama lashwabene. Kepha kute iChampollion yekuchaza iGibhithe yebuso babo bonkhe bantfu nabo bonkhe bantfu. Physiognomy, njengayo yonkhe lenye isayensi yebantfu, kepha inganekwane lendlulako. Uma ngabe ke, Sir William Jones, lowafundza ngetilwimi letimashumi lamatsatfu, angakhoni kufundza buso bemlimi lobulula kakhulu ngetinshokutsi tayo letijulile naleticashile, Ishmael longafundzanga angaba njani nelitsemba lekufundza iChaldee lembi yesiphongo seSperm Whale? Mine kepha ngibeka leso siphongo embikwakho. Lifundze nangabe ungakhona.





SEHLUKO 80. I Nut.

Uma iSperm Whale ibe yi Sphinx ngekwemtimba, ku phrenologist ingcondvo yakhe ibonakala ngatsi ngumbutsano we geometric lokungenakwenteka kutsi ube sikwele.

Kulesilwane lesikhulile lesigcwele le skull itawukala lokungenani emamitha langemashumi lamabili ngebudze. Unhinge umhlatsi longephansi, kanye nekubuka eceleni kwale skull njengeseceleni kwendiza lethambekele ngesilinganiso lephumula kulo lonkhe esigabeni. Kepha ekuphileni-njengoba sibonile kuletinye tindzawo-lendiza letsembele igcwele ngendlela ye-angular, futsi icishe ibe sikwele ngebuningi lobukhulu be-superincumbent be-junk kanye nesilisa. Ekupheleni lokusetulu le skull yakha i crater kuya embhedzeni leyo ncenye ye mass; ngesikhatsi ngaphansi kwemhlabatsi lomudze walomgodzi—kulomunye umgodzi longakavami kwedlula emasentimitha lalishumi ngebudze futsi lokunyenti ngekujula—kuphumula idlanzana nje lengcondvo yalesilwane lesikhulu. Ingcondvo lokungenani ngemamitha langemashumi lamabili kusuka esimongweni sakhe lesibonakalako empilweni; ifihleke khashane ngemuva kwemisebenti yayo lemikhulu, njenge citadel lengekhatsi kakhulu ngaphakatsi kwetinqaba letikhulisiwe tase Quebec. Ngako njengelibhokisi lekukhetsa lifihlekile kuye, kutsi ngati labanye baholi labaphika ngalokucinile kutsi iSperm Whale inalenye ingcondvo ngaphandle kwaleyo semblance lephatsekako yaleyo leyakhiwa ngema cubic-yards emagazini wakhe we sperm. Kulele emagoceni langaketayeleki, tifundvo, kanye nekugucuka, ngekwesaba kwabo, kubonakala kuhambisana kakhulu nemcondvo wemandla akhe jikelele kutsatsa leyo ncenye yakhe lemfihlakalo njengesihlalo sekuhlakanipha kwakhe.

Kuyacaca, ngako-ke, kutsi ngekwe-phrenologically inhloko yale Leviathan, esimeni sesidalwa lesiphilako lesingakaphatfwa, yinkhohliso yonkhe. Njengobe ingcondvo yakhe sibili, ungaboni-ke tinkhomba tayo, futsi ungativa tikhona. Umkhoma, njengato tonkhe tintfo letinemandla, ugcoka siphongo semanga emhlabeni lowetayelekile.

Uma wehlisa luhlaka lwakhe lwetincumbi tesilisa bese ubuka ngemuva kwekuphela kwalo lokungemuva, lokungumkhawulo lophakeme, utoshaywa kufana kwalo nelukhanda lwemuntfu, kubonakala esimeni lesifanako, futsi kusuka endzaweni lefanako yekubuka. Ngempela, beka le skull lebuyiselwe emuva (lelinganiswe phansi kuya ebukhuluni bemuntfu) emkhatsini welipuleti le skulls yemadvodza, futsi ungayididanisa nabo ngekungafuni; futsi uphawula kucindzeteleka kulenye incenye yesicoco sayo, ngemusho we phrenological ungatsi—Lendvodza beyite kutihlonipha, futsi beyite kuhlonipha. Futsi ngalabo negations, kucatshangelwa kanye neliciniso lelicinisekisako le prodigious bulk yakhe kanye nemandla, ungakwakha kahle kuwe wena liciniso, noma kungasiwo umcondvo lojabulisa kakhulu kutsi yini potency lephakeme kakhulu.

Kepha uma kusuka etilinganisweni tekucatsanisa tengcondvo lefanele yemkhoma, ubona kutsi ayikhoni kubhalwa ngalokwenele, ngako-ke nginalomunye umbono kuwe. Uma ubuka ngekucophelela cishe noma ngumuphi umgogodla we quadruped, utoshaywa kufana kwe vertebræ yayo nemgaco wemtsambo lonetintsambo wema skulls lamancane, onkhe aphetse kufana kwe rudimental ne skull proper. Kuyintfo yekutigcabha yaseJalimane, kutsi ema vertebræ tigogo letingakatfutfuki ngalokuphelele. Kepha kufana kwangaphandle lokunelukuluku, ngitsatsa emaJalimane bekungasiwo emadvodza ekucala kutsi abone. Umngani wami waseveni langaphandle wake wangikhomba, esifubeni sesitsa lasibulalako, futsi nge vertebræ lebekayifaka, ngendlela ye basso-relievo, umlomo wesikebhe sakhe. Manje, ngicabanga kutsi bo-phrenologists bashiye intfo lebalulekile ekungaphusheni luphenyo lwabo kusuka ku-cerebellum ngemsele wemgogodla. Ngobe ngiyakholwa kutsi lokunyenti kwesimo semuntfu kutawutfolakala kubekwe emgogodleni wakhe. Ngingatsandza kuva umgogodla wakho kunekuva lukhanda lwakho, noma ngabe ungubani. I-joist lencane yemgogodla ayikaze isekele umphefumulo logcwele futsi lohloniphekile. Ngiyajabula emgogodleni wami, njengasetisebenti leticinile letinesibindzi taleyo mjeka lengijikijela hhafu emhlabeni.

Faka leligala lemgogodla le-phrenology ku-Sperm Whale. Umgodzi wakhe we-cranial uyachubeka ne-vertebra yekucala yentsamo; futsi kuleyo vertebra phansi kwemsele wemgogodla kutawukala emasentimitha lalishumi ngaphesheya, kube siphohlongo ngebudze, kanye nesibalo lesincantsatfu ngesisekelo lesibheke phansi. Njengoba idlula kule vertebræ lesele lomsele uyancipha ngebukhulu, kepha libanga lelidze lihlala linemandla lamakhulu. Nyalo, vele, lomsele ugcwele intfo lefanako kakhulu lenemicu lemangalisako—umgogodla—njengengcondvo; futsi ikhulumisana ngco nengcondvo. Futsi lokunye lokunyenti, tinyawo letinyenti ngemuva kwekuphuma emgodzini wengcondvo, umgogodla uhlala une-girth lenganciphi, lecishe ilingana naleyo yengcondvo. Ngaphansi kwato tonkhe letimo, bekungaba yintfo lengenangcondvo yini kuhlola kanye nekubeka umgogodla wemkhoma ngendlela ye-phrenologically? Ngobe, uma kubukwa ngalendlela, buncane lobumangalisako lobucatsaniswako bengcondvo yakhe lefanele bungetulu kwekuncephetelwa bukhulu lobumangalisako lobucatsaniswako bemgogodla wakhe.

Kepha kushiya lomkhondvo kutsi usebente njengoba kungenteka nge phrenologists, ngingavele ngitsatse le theory yemgogodla sikhashana, ngekubhekisa ku hump ye Sperm Whale. Le hump le august, uma ngingaphutsi, ikhuphuka ngetulu kwalenye ye vertebræ lenkhulu, futsi, ngakoke, ngendlela letsite, sikhunta sangaphandle lesivulekile sayo. Kusuka esimeni sayo lesihlobene ngaleso sikhatsi, kufanele ngibite le hump lesetulu ngesicu sekucina noma kungabi nemandla ku Sperm Whale. Futsi kutsi lesilwane lesikhulu asikhoni kuncotjwa, usatawuba nesizatfu sekwati.
