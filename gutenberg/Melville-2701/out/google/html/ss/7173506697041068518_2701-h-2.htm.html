SEHLUKO 4. I-Counterpane.

Ngesikhatsi ngivuka ekuseni ngakusasa cishe ekuseni, ngatfola umkhono waQueequeg uphoswe kimi ngendlela lenelutsandvo nelutsandvo kakhulu. Bewucishe ucabange kutsi ngibe ngumfati wakhe. Le counterpane beyiyi patchwork, igcwele tikwele letincane letingakavami letinembala we parti kanye netintsatfu; futsi lomkhono wakhe bewubhalwe i-tattoo yonkhe indzawo nge-labyrinth ye-Chrethe lengapheli yesibalo, kute tincenye letimbili tayo lebetinemtfunti munye locondzile—ngenca yekutsi ngicabanga kutsi bekagcina umkhono wakhe elwandle ngendlela lengakafaneli elangeni nasemtfuntini, imikhono yehembe lakhe beyigocotwa ngendlela lengakavami ngetikhatsi letehlukahlukene—loku kubukeka kwakhe lokufanako kwami, quilt yekuhlanganisa. Ngempela, incenye ilele kuyo njengoba umkhono wenta ngesikhatsi ngicala kuvuka, bengingakhoni kuyibona kusuka ku quilt, bebahlanganisa kanjalo imibala yabo ndzawonye; futsi bekungumuva wesisindvo nekucindzeteleka kuphela lebengikhona kubona kutsi Queequeg uyangigona.

Imiva yami beyimangalisa. Asengizame kutichaza. Ngesikhatsi ngisemncane, ngikhumbula kahle simo lesifanako ngandlela tsite lesangehlela; noma ngabe bekuliciniso nobe liphupho, angizange ngikhone kuhlala ngalokuphelele. Simo bekungulesi. Bengisolo ngisika i-caper noma lenye—ngicabanga kutsi beyizama kunyonyoba etulu eshimela, njengoba bengibone kushanyela lokuncane kwenta emalangeni lambalwa langaphambilini; kanye namake wami wekucala lobeka, ngandlela tsite noma ngalenye indlela, ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi angishaya ngetibhakela, noma angitfumela embhedzeni ngingakadli sidlo sakusihlwa, —make wangidvonsa ngemilente waphuma eshimela futsi wangipakisha kutsi ngiyolala, noma bekulihora lesibili kuphela ntsambama mhla tinge-21 Inhlaba, lilanga lelidze kakhulu emnyakeni ku hemisphere yetfu. Ngativa ngekwesaba. Kepha bekute lusito ngako, ngako-ke ngenyuka ngetitebhisi ngaya ekamelweni lami lelincane esitezi sesitsatfu, ngakhumula kancane kancane kute ngibulale sikhatsi, futsi ngekububula lokubabako ngafika emkhatsini wemashidi.

Ngalala lapho ngidzabukile ngibala kutsi kufanele kudlule emahora lalishumi nesitfupha laphelele ngaphambi kwekutsi ngibe nelitsemba lekuvuswa kwalabafile. Emahora lalishumi nesitfupha embhedzeni! lokuncane kwemhlane wami bekubuhlungu nangicabanga ngako. Futsi bekulula kakhulu futsi; lilanga likhanya efasitelweni, kanye nekukhala lokukhulu kwemakhoshi emigwacweni, kanye nemsindvo wemavi ebantfu labadvumile endlini yonkhe. Ngativa ngibi kakhulu—ekugcineni ngasukuma, ngagcoka, futsi ngehla kancane ngetinyawo tami letifakwe emasitokisi, ngafuna make wami wekucala, futsi ngavele ngatiphonsa etinyaweni takhe, ngimncenga njengemusa lotsite kutsi angiphe kushelela lokuhle ngekutiphatsa kwami ​​kabi; noma yini ngempela ngaphandle kwekungilahla kutsi ngilale abed sikhatsi lesidze kangaka lesingabeketeleleki. Kepha bekanguye lohamba embili futsi lonembeza kakhulu kubomake labatsatfwako, futsi emuva bekufanele ngiye ekamelweni lami. Kwaphela emahora lamanyenti ngilele lapho ngivukile, ngitiva ngibi kakhulu kunaloko lebengike ngakwenta kusukela ngaleso sikhatsi, ngisho nasemashwa lamakhulu kakhulu lalandzelako. Ekugcineni kufanele kutsi ngawela ephusheni lelibi lelikhatsatako lekulala; futsi ngivuka kancane kancane kuyo—ngihhafu ngigcwele emaphupho—ngavula emehlo ami, futsi leligumbi lebelikhanyiswe lilanga ngaphambilini nyalo beseligocotwe bumnyama bangaphandle. Ngekushesha ngeva kwetfuka kugijima kulo lonkhe luhlaka lwami; kute lebekubonakala, futsi kute lebekuvakala; kodvwa sandla lesingetulu kwemvelo sabonakala sibekwe kimi. Umkhono wami bewulenga ngetulu kwe counterpane, futsi lendlela lengenagama, lengacabangeki, lethulile noma i-phantom, lapho khona lesandla besikhona, bekubonakala ngatsi sihleti edvute eceleni kwembhedze wami. Ngobe loko lebekubonakala ngatsi iminyaka inqwabane, bengilele lapho, ngifriziwe ngekwesaba lokubi kakhulu, ngingabi nesibindzi sekudvonsa sandla sami; nobe kunjalo ngihlala ngicabanga kutsi nangabe ngingakhona kepha kuyivusa intshi yinye, lesipelingi lesitfusako besitawuphuka. Bengingati kutsi lokucaphela kwagcina kushelele njani kimi; kepha nangivuka ekuseni, ngakukhumbula konkhe ngekuvevetela, futsi emalanga nemaviki netinyanga ngemuva kwaloko ngalahleka emizamweni ledidako yekuchaza lemfihlakalo. Cha, kuze kube ngulelihora, ngivame kutiphicaphica ngayo.

Manje, susa kwesaba lokubi kakhulu, futsi imiva yami yekuva sandla lesingetulu kwemvelo kimi beyifana kakhulu, ngekumangalisa kwayo, kuleto lebengihlangabetana nato ngekuvuka futsi ngibona umkhono waQueequeg wemahedeni ujikijelwa kimi. Kepha ekugcineni tonkhe tintfo letenteka ebusuku lobudlule taphindzaphindzeka, ngamunye ngamunye, ecinisweni lelicinile, ngase ngilala kuphela ngiphila kulesimo lesimatima lesihlekisako. Ngobe noma ngazama kunyakata umkhono wakhe—kuvula sibopho sakhe semkhwenyana—nobe kunjalo, alele njengobe abelele, bekasolo angigona kakhulu, kwaba shengatsi kute lokunye ngaphandle kwekufa lokufanele kusihlukanise sobabili. Nyalo ngazama kumvusa—“Queequeg!”—kodvwa imphendvulo yakhe bekukukhonkotsa kuphela. Ngabese ngiyagijima, intsamo yami itiva shengatsi isekhola yelihhashi; futsi ngekushesha weva kuklwebheka kancane. Elahlela eceleni le counterpane, lapho bekulele khona i tomahawk ilele eceleni kwe savage, kwaba shengatsi ngumntfwana lobuke nge hatchet. I-pickle lenhle, ngempela, ngacabanga; abed lapha endlini lengatiwa emini lelibanzi, ne canibal kanye ne tomahawk! “Ndlovukati!—egameni lebuhle, Indlovukati, vuka!” Ekugcineni, ngekutsi ngigucuke kakhulu, futsi ngikhulume kakhulu futsi ngingapheli ngekungafaneli kwakhe kugona umdvuna ngaleyo ndlela yemshado, ngaphumelela kukhipha kububula; futsi njengamanje, wabuyisela emuva umkhono wakhe, watinyakatisa yonkhe indzawo njengenja yase Newfoundland nje kusuka emantini, futsi wahlala embhedzeni, acinile njenge pike-staff, wangibuka, futsi wahlikihla emehlo akhe kwangatsi akakhumbuli ngalokuphelele kutsi ngafika njani lapho, noma ngabe kukhona lokufiphele kwangibona slowly ngaye. Ngaleso sikhatsi, bengilele ngithule ngimbuka ngemehlo, ngingasenakungabata lokukhulu nyalo, futsi bengigobekile kutsi ngibuke kancane lesilwane lebesifuna kwati kangaka. Ngesikhatsi, ekugcineni, ingcondvo yakhe ibonakala yentiwe ngekutsintsa simo saloyo lalala naye, futsi waba, kwaba shengatsi, wabuyisana neliciniso; wagcuma waphuma phansi, futsi ngetimphawu letitsite nemisindvo yanginika kuvisisa kutsi, uma kungijabulisa, bekatawugcoka kucala bese uyangishiya kutsi ngigcoke ngemuva kwaloko, ashiye lonkhe lifulethi kimi. Ucabanga mine, Queequeg, ngaphansi kwetimo, lena yi-overture lephucukile kakhulu; kepha, liciniso likutsi, letilwane letihlaselako tinemuva wekutalwa wekunambitseka, sho loko lokutsandzako; kuyamangalisa kutsi banenhlonipho ngalokuyinhloko kangakanani. Ngikhokha lokuncoma lokukhetsekile ku Queequeg, ngoba wangiphatsa ngekuhlonipha lokukhulu kanye nekucabangela, ngesikhatsi mine nginelicala lekungabi nesimilo lesikhulu; amgcobagcoba embhedzeni, futsi abuka tonkhe tinyatselo takhe te-toilet; ngesikhatsi ilukuluku lami lekutfola ncono kwekuzalanisa kwami. Noma kunjalo, indvodza lefana naQueequeg awuyiboni onkhe malanga, yena netindlela takhe bekufanele kahle lokungakavami mayelana.

Wacala kugcoka ngetulu ngekugcoka sigcoko sakhe se beaver, lesidze kakhulu, ngaseceleni, wase—asasusa emabhulukwe akhe—watingela emabhudzi akhe. Yini ngaphansi kwemazulu lakwentela yona, angikhoni kutjela, kepha kuhamba kwakhe lokulandzelako bekukutichobosela—emabhudzi esandleni, nesigcoko—ngaphansi kwembhedze; lapho, kusuka ekuphefumuleni lokunebudlova lokuhlukahlukene kanye nekucindzeteleka, ngaphetsa ngekutsi bekasebenta kamatima atibhutha; noma ngabe kute umtsetfo wekufaneleka lengike ngava ngawo, ngabe kukhona yini umuntfu lodzingeka kutsi abe ngasese nakagcoka emabhudzi akhe. Kodvwa Queequeg, uyabona yini, bekusilwane lesisesigabeni sekugucuka—bekungasiyo sibungu nobe luvivane. Wavele waphucuka ngalokwenele kutsi akhombise buve bakhe ngendlela lemangalisako. Imfundvo yakhe beyisengakapheli. Bekafundza ticu tekucala. Kube akazange abe nelizinga lelincane leliphucukile, cishe ngabe akazange atihluphe ngemabhutsi nhlobo; kepha-ke, kube bekangakabi ngumuntfu lonelunya, bekangasoze waphupha ngekungena ngaphansi kwembhedze kute atigcoke. Ekugcineni, waphuma nesigcoko sakhe lesinemigodzi kakhulu futsi sichoboshelwe phansi emehlweni akhe, futsi wacala kukhwesha nekukhukhumala ekamelweni, kwaba shengatsi, akazange ajwayele kakhulu emabhudzi, umbhangqwana wakhe wemanti, loshwabene wesikhumba senkhomo—mhlawumbe bekungakentiwa ngeku-oda noma—kunaloko wamcindzetela futsi wamhlupha ekucaleni.

Ngekubona, nyalo, kutsi bekute emakhethini efasitelweni, nekutsi umgwaco bewuncane kakhulu, indlu lebhekene nayo beyiyalela kubuka lokucacile ekamelweni, futsi ibuka kakhulu sibalo lesingakafaneli lesentiwa ngu Queequeg, ahambahamba ngetintfo letincane ngaphandle kwesigcoko sakhe nemabhutsi; Ngamncenga ngendlela lengingakhona ngayo, kutsi asheshise indlu yakhe yangasese ngandlela tsite, futsi ikakhulukati kutsi angene emapantaloons akhe ngekushesha. Wavumelana, wase uyachubeka uyageza. Ngaleso sikhatsi ekuseni nobe ngumuphi umKhristu bekatawugeza buso bakhe; kepha Queequeg, ngekumangala kwami, wenetiseka ngekunciphisa kugeza kwakhe esifubeni sakhe, emikhonweni, nasetandleni takhe. Wabese ugcoka i-waistcoat yakhe, futsi watsatsa insipho lecinile etafuleni lelisemkhatsini we-wash-stand, wayicwilisa emantini futsi wacala kugcuma ebusweni bakhe. Bengibuka kutsi ugcine kuphi insingo yakhe, ngesikhatsi lo and behold, utsatsa le harpoon ekhoneni lembhedze, akhiphe sitoko lesidze setigodvo, akhulule inhloko, ayihlabe kancane ebhuthini lakhe, bese ukhuphukela esibukweni lesibhekene nelubondza, ucala kuklwebha ngamandla, noma kunaloko i-harpoo yakhe. Ucabanga mine, Queequeg, loku kusebentisa titja taRogers letinhle kakhulu ngekutiphindzisela. Ngemuva kwaloko ngamangala kutsi kancane kangakanani kulokusebenta ngesikhatsi ngati kutsi nguyiphi insimbi lenhle inhloko ye harpoon yentiwa, nekutsi imiphetfo lemidze lecondzile ihlala igcinwa kangakanani.

Lokusele kwendlu yakhe yangasese kwasheshe kwafezwa, futsi ngekutigcabha waphuma ekamelweni, agocote lijazi lakhe lelikhulu lenkawu yemshayeli, futsi adlala i-harpoon yakhe njenge-baton ya marshal.





SEHLUKO 5. Sidlo sasekuseni.

Ngasheshe ngalandzela, futsi ngehlela egumbini lekuphuza ngahlasela umnikati welikhaya lobekabobotheka ngendlela lemnandzi kakhulu. Angizange ngibe nelunya kuye, noma nje bekasolo akhuluma nami hhayi kancane endzabeni yemuntfu lebengilala naye.

Kepha, kuhleka lokuhle yintfo lenhle kakhulu, futsi kunaloko yintfo lenhle leyindlala kakhulu; lapho khona kuba nesihawu. Ngako-ke, nangabe kukhona umuntfu munye, ngemuntfu wakhe lofanele, akhona tintfo tekuhlekisa lokuhle kunobe ngubani, angabe asabuyela emuva, kepha akavumele ngenjabulo kutsi acitse futsi asetjentiswe ngaleyo ndlela. Futsi lendvodza lenentfo lehlekisako ngayo, ciniseka kutsi kukhona lokunyenti kuleyondvodza kunaloko mhlawumbe lokucabangako.

I-bar-room manje beseyigcwele bantfu labahlala emabhodini lebebawela ebusuku bangaphambilini, futsi lebengingakababuki kahle. Bebacishe bonkhe bebabantfu bemikhoma; laba labayinhloko, nebalingani besibili, nebalingani besitsatfu, nebabati baselwandle, nebakhi betinsimbi elwandle, nebakhandi betinsimbi elwandle, nebakhandi betinsimbi, nebagcini bemikhumbi; inkampani lensundvu nalenetinwele, lenetintshebe letibosky; isethi lengakagundvwa, lenemabala, bonkhe bagcoke emajazi etimfene emagown asekuseni.

Bewubona kahle kutsi ngamunye wabo abeselugwini sikhatsi lesingakanani. Sihlathi salomfana lomncane lesinemphilo sifana ne pear letostiwe lilanga ngembala, futsi bekungabonakala kunuka cishe njenge musky; angeke kube nemalanga lamatsatfu afika emhlabatsini kusuka eluhambeni lwakhe lwaseNdiya. Leyondvodza leseceleni kwakhe ibukeka ilula ngemithunzi lembalwa; ungasho kutsi touch ye satin wood ikuye. Embaleni wesitsatfu solo kuhlala i-tropic tawn, kepha ihlanjululwe kancane nge; akungabateki kutsi uhlale emaviki onkhe elugwini. Kepha ngubani longakhombisa sihlathi njengaQueequeg? le, ivinjelwe ngema tints lahlukahlukene, bekubonakala njenge Andes’ slope yasenshonalanga, kukhombisa ngendlela yinye, simo selitulu lesehlukile, indzawo ngendzawo.

"Sidla, hawu!" manje kwakhala umnikati welikhaya, avula umnyango ngekujikijela, futsi sangena sayodla sidlo sasekuseni.

Batsi emadvodza labone umhlaba, ngaleyo ndlela aba ngempela akhululekile ngendlela, ngempela self-possessed enkampanini. Hhayi ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi, nomakunjalo: Ledyard, umhambi lomkhulu wase New England, kanye na Mungo Park, loyo wase Scotland; kuwo onkhe emadvodza, bebanesiciniseko lesincane kakhulu e-parlor. Kepha mhlawumbe kwewela nje iSiberia nge sledge ledonswa tinja njengoba Ledyard enta, noma kuhamba sikhatsi lesidze wedwa ngesisu lesingenalutho, enhlitiyweni ye negro yase Afrika, lebekuyisamba semidlalo ya Mungo lephuyile—loluhlobo lwekuhamba, ngitsi, kungenteka kungabi yindlela lephakeme kakhulu yetenhlalakahle yekutfola i polish. Noma kunjalo, esikhatsini lesinyenti, lolohlobo lwentfo kufanele lube khona noma ngukuphi.

Lemicabango lapha nje ibangelwa simo sekutsi ngemuva kwekutsi sonkhe sihleli etafuleni, futsi bengilungiselela kuva tindzaba letinhle ngekudoba imikhoma; kwangimangalisa kakhulu kutsi cishe wonkhe umuntfu bekathula kakhulu. Futsi hhayi loko kuphela, kepha bebabukeka banemahloni. Yebo, naku bekunelicembu letinja taselwandle, letinyenti tato ngaphandle kwekuba nemahloni lamancane betigibele imikhoma lemikhulu etilwandle letiphakeme—tihambi letiphelele kuto—futsi tilwa nato tifile ngaphandle kwekucwayiza; futsi nomakunjalo, lapha bebahlala etafuleni lekudla kwasekuseni kwetenhlalakahle—bonkhe bebabitwa ngendlela lefanako, bonkhe bebanekunambitseka lokufanako—babukana ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi njengetimvu kube shengatsi bebangakaze baphume emehlweni esibaya lesitsite setimvu emkhatsini wetintsaba letiluhlata. Umbono wekufuna kwati; lamabhele lanemahloni, lama whalemen lanemahloni!

Kepha mayelana ne Queequeg—kungani, Queequeg ahleti lapho emkhatsini wabo—enhloko yelitafula, futsi, kwaba khona ngengoti; kupholile njenge- icicle. Kuciniseka angeke ngisho lokunyenti ngekuzalanisa kwakhe. Umdvumi wakhe lomkhulu bekangakhoni kufakazela ngemusa kuletsa kwakhe i-harpoon yakhe esidlweni sasekuseni naye, futsi ayisebentise lapho ngaphandle kwemkhosi; kufinyelela ngetulu kwelitafula ngalo, kuya engotini lesedvute yetinhloko letinyenti, futsi abamba ema-beefsteaks abheke kuye. Kepha loko bekwentiwa nguye ngekupholisa kakhulu, futsi wonkhe umuntfu uyati kutsi ngekulinganisa kwebantfu labanyenti, kwenta noma ngabe yini ngekupholisa kwenta ngekucolisa.

Angeke sikhulume ngato tonkhe tintfo letikhetsekile taQueequeg lapha; indlela lagwema ngayo likhofi kanye nema rolls lashisako, futsi wasebentisa kunaka kwakhe lokungahlukaniswa kuma beefsteaks, lentiwa ngendlela lengakavami. Kwanele, kutsi kwatsi nasekuphelile kudla kwasekuseni wahoca njengalabanye wangena ekamelweni lemmango, wakhanyisa i-tomahawk-pipe yakhe, futsi bekahleti lapho athule agaya futsi abhema agcoke sigcoko sakhe lesingahlukaniseki, ngesikhatsi ngiphuma ngekushesha ngiyohambahamba.





SEHLUKO 6. Umgwaco.

Uma ngabe bengimangala ekucaleni ngibona umuntfu longakavami njenge Queequeg ajikeleta emkhatsini wemmango lonesihlonipho welidolobha leliphucukile, loko kumangala kwasheshe kwahamba ngesikhatsi ngitsatsa luhambo lwami lwekucala lwekukhanya kwemini etitaladini tase New Bedford.

Emigwacweni lemikhulu lesedvute nemadokodo, noma nguliphi litheku lelikhulu litawuvame kuniketa kubuka tintfo letibukeka tingachazeki letivela etindzaweni tangephandle. Ngisho nasemigwacweni yaseBroadway naseChestnut, ngaletinye tikhatsi bantfu labahamba ngemikhumbi baseMedithera batawucindzetela labafati labatfukile. Kepha iNew Bedford ishaya wonkhe umgwaco wemanti neWapping. Kuletindzawo letishiwo ekugcineni ubona bantfu labahamba ngemikhumbi kuphela; kepha eNew Bedford, bantfu labadla bantfu mbamba bame bacoca emakhoneni emigwaco; savages ngalokucondzile; labanyenti babo basaphetse ematsambo abo inyama lengakangcweli. Kwenta umuntfu langamati agcobagcobe.

Ekukhulumeni ngengubo yakhe yaselwandle, uyala tinkinobho tetinsimbi kuma-waistcoats akhe; emabhande emabhulukweni akhe e-canvas.
