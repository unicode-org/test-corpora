Futsi ngaphandle kwaletinye tizatfu, kulesiphetfo ngikhutsatwa, ngekucabanga lokutsintsa sitfunti lesikhulu lesikhona kanye nekuphakama kwe Sperm Whale; Ngimlandzisa njengemuntfu lovamile, longajulile, njengoba kuliciniso lelingaphikiswa kutsi akaze atfolakale ema soundings, noma edvute nelugwini; yonkhe leminye imikhoma ngalesinye sikhatsi injalo. Uyazindla futsi uyajula. Futsi ngiyaciniseka kutsi kusuka etinhloko tabo bonkhe labajulile labazindlako, njengaPlato, Pyrrho, Develi, Jupiter, Dante, njalonjalo, kuhlala kukhuphuka umfutfo lotsite lobonakalako, ngesikhatsi usesenzweni sekucabanga imicabango lejulile. Ngesikhatsi ngibhala i-treatise lencane nge-Eternity, ngaba nelukuluku lekubeka sibuko embi kwami; futsi ere long saw kubonakala lapho, i curious kufaka ekhatsi tibungu kanye undulation emkhatsini phezu inhloko yami. Umswakama longagucuki wetinwele tami, ngesikhatsi ngicwila emcabangweni lojulile, ngemuva kwetinkomishi letisitfupha telitiye lelishisako e-attic yami lencane lene-shingled, yemini ya-August; loku kubonakala kuyimphikiswano lengetiwe yekucabanga lokungenhla.

Futsi kuphakamisa kangakanani kutigcabha kwetfu ngalesilwane lesikhulu lesinemandla, lesinenkungu, kusibona sihamba ngemkhumbi ngekutimisela elwandle loluthulile lwasetindzaweni letishisako; inhloko yakhe lenkhulu, lemnene beyilenga ngempheme wemhwamuko, lowatalwa kucabanga kwakhe lokungatseleleki, futsi lowo mhwamuko—njengobe utawubona ngaletinye tikhatsi—ukhatimuliswa ngumtfunti wemvula, kube shengatsi Lizulu ngokwalo libeke luphawu lwalo emicabangweni yakhe. Ngobe, d'ye uyabona, imisebe yemvula ayivakasheli umoya lohlantekile; tikhipha umhwamuko kuphela. Futsi ngako, kuyo yonkhe inkhungu lendze yekungabata lokufiphele engcondvweni yami, imiva yaphezulu nyalo bese iyadubula, ivutsa inkhungu yami ngemisebe yasezulwini. Futsi ngaloko ngiyambonga Nkulunkulu; ngobe bonkhe banekungabata; labanyenti bayaphika; kepha kungabata noma kuphika, bambalwa kanye nabo, bane-intuitions. Kungabata ngato tonkhe tintfo tasemhlabeni, kanye nekucabanga ngaletinye tintfo tasezulwini; le nhlanganisela ayikholwa noma ingakholwa, kepha yenta umuntfu lobabuka bobabili ngeliso lelilinganako.
