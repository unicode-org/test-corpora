Tonkhe letinchubo letimangalisako takhulisa kungakhululeki kwami, futsi ngimbona nyalo akhombisa timphawu leticinile tekuphetsa imisebenti yakhe yelibhizinisi, futsi agcuma embhedzeni nami, ngacabanga kutsi sesikhatsi lesiphakeme, nyalo noma kungaze, ngaphambi kwekutsi kukhanya kucime, kutsi ngiphule sipelingi lebengiboshwe kuso sikhatsi lesidze kangaka.

Kepha sikhatsi lengisicitse ngicabanga kutsi ngitsini, bekungulesibulalako. Watsatsa tomahawk yakhe etafuleni, wahlola inhloko yayo sikhashana, wase uyayibamba ekukhanyeni, umlomo wakhe usesibambeni, wakhukhumula emafu lamakhulu entfutfu yeligwayi. Ngemzuzu lolandzelako kukhanya kwacima, futsi lomdlali webantfu wasendle, tomahawk emkhatsini wematinyo akhe, wagcuma waya embhedzeni nami. Ngahlabelela, bengingakhoni kutibamba nyalo; futsi aniketa kububula lokungakalindzeleki kwekumangala wacala kungiva.

Ngikhuluma ngekukhukhumala lokutsite, bengingati kutsi yini, ngagijima kuye ngabhekana nelubondza, ngabese ngiyamloya, noma ngabe ngubani noma ngabe yini, kutsi athule, futsi angivumele ngisukume ngiphindze ngikhanyise sibane. Kepha timphendvulo takhe te-guttural tangenetisa ngaso leso sikhatsi kutsi yena kodvwa akazange ayicondze inchazelo yami.

“Ngubani-e debel wena?”—wagcina atsi—“wena awukhulumi-e, dam-me, ngiyabulala-e.” Futsi atsi kanjalo i-tomahawk lekhanyisiwe yacala kuchuma ngami ebumnyameni.

“Mnikati welikhaya, ngenca yaNkulunkulu, Peter Coffin!” kwamemeta mine. "Mnikati welikhaya! Buka! Libhokisi! Tingelosi! ngisindzise!"

"Khuluma-e! ngitjele-ee kutsi ngubani-ee, noma dam-me, ngibulale-e!" kwaphindze kwabhodla lomdlali webantfu, ngesikhatsi kuchuma kwakhe lokubi kakhulu kwe tomahawk kusakata umlotsa weligwayi lelishisako ngami ngaze ngacabanga kutsi lineni lami litawuvutsa umlilo. Kepha ngiyabonga lizulu, ngaleso sikhatsi umnikati welikhaya wangena ekamelweni kukhanya ngesandla, futsi ngigcuma embhedzeni ngagijima ngasondzela kuye.

“Ungesabi nyalo,” washo, waphindze wabobotheka, “Queequeg lapha angeke alimate tinwele tenhloko yakho.”

Ngamemeta ngatsi: “Yekela kumomotseka, futsi kungani ungangitjeli kutsi lowo mdlali we- harpooneer wasesihogweni bekangumuntfu lodla bantfu?”

“Bengicabanga kutsi niyati;—angizange nginitjele, bekangumtsengisi lohamba edolobheni?—kodvwa phindze ujike ema-flukes ulale. Queequeg, buka lapha—uyangi sabbee, mine uyangi-sabbee—wena lendvodza uyakulala—wena sabbee?”

“Me sabbee lokunyenti”—kwabubula Queequeg, aphefumula liphayiphi lakhe futsi ahlala embhedzeni.

“You gettee in,” wengeta, wangikhombisa nge- tomahawk yakhe, futsi wajikijela letimphahla eceleni. Ngempela wakwenta loku hhayi ngendlela yesive kuphela kodvwa ngendlela lenemusa futsi lenemusa mbamba. Ngama ngambuka sikhashana. Kuwo onkhe ema-tattoo akhe bekangumuntfu lohlantekile, lobukeka kahle njengemuntfu lodla bantfu. Yini yonkhe le msindvo lebengisolo ngiyenta ngayo, ngacabanga ngekhatsi kimi—lendvodza ngumuntfu njengami: unesizatfu lesifanako sekungesaba, njengobe nami kufanele ngimesabe. Kuncono kulala nemuntfu lodla bantfu lodzakiwe kunekutsi ulale umKhristu lodzakiwe.

"Mnikati welikhaya," ngasho mine, "mtjele kutsi afake i-tomahawk yakhe lapho, noma liphayiphi, noma ngabe yini loyibita ngayo; mtjele kutsi ayekele kubhema, ngamafuphi, futsi ngitawujika naye. Kepha angitsandzi kuba nendvodza lebhema embhedzeni nami. Kuyingoti. Ngaphandle kwaloko, angikavikeleki."

Loku kutjelwa Queequeg, wavele walandzela, futsi waphindze wangikhombisa ngesizotsa kutsi ngilele embhedzeni—agijima ngaseceleni kakhulu njengekutsi atsi—“Ngeke ngitsintse umlente wenu.”

Ngatsi: “Ulale kahle, mnikati welikhaya, ungahamba.”

Ngajika, futsi angizange ngilale ncono empilweni yami.
