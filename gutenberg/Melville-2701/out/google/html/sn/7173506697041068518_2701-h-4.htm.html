Akafema nekusagadzikana; akabva aita seanonetseka mumhino; ndokuzotenderera kamwe chete kana kaviri; akabva agara ndokupukuta maziso ake.

“Holloa!” akatura befu pakupedzisira, “muri vaputi ndiani?”

"Varume vanotakurwa," ndakapindura, "anofamba rinhi?"

"Ehe, iwe, iwe uri kupinda maari, iwe? Anofamba nechikepe nhasi." Kaputeni akauya nezuro manheru.

“Mukuru Wei?—Ahabhi?

"Ndiani kunze kwake?"

Ndanga ndichazomubvunza mimwe mibvunzo maererano naAhabhu, patakanzwa ruzha padhishi.

"Holloa! Starbuck's astir," akadaro mutyairi. "Ndiye mukuru weshamwari, uyo; murume akanaka, uye anonamata; asi vese vapenyu ikozvino, ndinofanira kutendeukira kwaari." Akati akwira pamusoro pechikepe, isu tikamutevera.

Kunze kwakanga zvino kwachena. Nenguva isipi vashandi vakapinda muchikepe vari vaviri-vatatu; vapfuri vakazvibata; vakaroorana vakanga vachibatikana; uye vanoverengeka vevanhu vomumhenderekedzo yegungwa vakanga vakabatikana mukuunza zvinhu zvakasiyana-siyana zvokupedzisira mungarava. Zvichakadaro Kaputeni Ahabhu akaramba akavharirwa mukati meimba yake.
