Iye zvino ngaticherechedzei zvisinganyanyi kusiyana pamisoro iyi—zvinoti, nhengo mbiri dzinonyanya kukosha, ziso nenzeve. Zvino, kubva padivi penzvimbo iyi yemaziso ewhale, zviri pachena kuti haigoni kuona chinhu chiri mberi chaiko, sezvaanogona kuona kuzasi chaiko. Mushoko rimwe, nzvimbo yemaziso ewhale inofanana neyenzeve dzomunhu; uye unogona kuzvifungira, kuti zvichakufambira sei kwauri, waiongorora zvinhu nemunzeve dzako. Kana muvengi wako ane hasha achifamba akananga kwauri, munondo wakasimudzwa masikati machena, haungamuoni, kungofanana kana akakuba pamusoro pako kubva kumashure. Mushoko, unenge uine misana miviri, sekutaura; asi, panguva imwe cheteyo, maivawo nemativi maviri: nokuti chii chinogadzira mberi kwemunhu, asi maziso ake?

Uyezve, nepo mune dzimwe mhuka dzakawanda dzandinogona kufunga nezvadzo zvino, maziso akadyarwa zvisingaoneki kuti asanganise simba rawo rokuona, kuti abudise mufananidzo mumwe chete kwete miviri kuuropi; nzvimbo yechienzi yemaziso ewhale, akakamurwa zvinobudirira sezvaari nemakubhiki mafiti akawanda emusoro wakasimba, uyo unopfumbira pakati pawo segomo guru rinoparadzanisa madziva maviri mumipata; izvi, zvamazvirokwazvo, zvinofanirwa kupatsanura zvizere zvinoonekwa zvinopihwa nenhengo yega yega yakazvimirira. Iyo whale, naizvozvo, inofanira kuona mufananidzo wakajeka kune rumwe rutivi, uye mumwe mufananidzo wakasiyana kune iyo; nepo zvose zviri pakati zvinofanira kuva rima guru uye pasina chinhu kwaari. Munhu anogona kunge achinzi anotarisa pasirese kubva mubhokisi revarindi rine masashi maviri akabatanidzwa pahwindo rake. Asi ne whale, mabhanhire maviri aya akaiswa zvakasiyana, achiita mahwindo maviri akasiyana, asi zvinosuruvarisa kukanganisa maonero. Ichi chimiro chemaziso ewhale chinhu chinofanira kugara chichiyeukwa mupfungwa mukubata hove; uye kurangarirwa nemuverengi mune zvimwe zviitiko zvinotevera.

Mubvunzo unokatyamadza uye unokatyamadza ungatangwa pamusoro pechinhu ichi chinooneka sekubata Revhiatani. Asi ndinofanira kugutsikana nezano. Chero bedzi maziso omunhu akasvinura muchiedza, chiito chokuona hachidikanirwi; ndiko kuti, haakwanisi ipapo kubatsira kuona chero zvinhu zviri pamberi pake. Kunyanguvezvo, chinoitika chomunhu upi noupi chichamudzidzisa, kuti kunyange zvazvo iye achigona kutsvaira kusingasaruri kwezvinhu nokutarira kumwe chete, hakubviri zvikuru kuti iye, nokungwarira, uye zvakakwana, anzvere zvinhu zviviri zvipi nezvipi—zvisinei zvikuru sei kana kuti zviduku sei—panguva imwe uye imwe cheteyo; hazvinetse kana vakarara nedivi vakabatana. Zvino sei ne whale? Chokwadi, maziso ake ose ari maviri mavari, anofanira kuita zvinhu panguva imwe chete; asi uropi hwake hunonyatsonzwisisa here, hunobatanidza, uye husina mano kupfuura hwomunhu, zvokuti panguva imwe cheteyo anogona kunyatsoongorora kariro mbiri dzakasiyana, imwe kune rimwe divi rake, uye imwe iri kune rimwe divi chairo? Kana achikwanisa, saka chinhu chinoshamisa maari, sekunge murume aikwanisa panguva imwe chete kuratidzwa kwezvinetso zviviri zvakasiyana muEuclid. Uyewo, kunyatsoongororwa, hapana kusawirirana mukuenzanisa uku.

Kungangove kungova kupenga, asi zvakagara zvichiratidzika kwandiri, kuti kusagadzikana kunoshamisa kwekufamba kunoratidzwa nemamwe mawhale kana akakomberedzwa nezvikepe zvitatu kana zvina; kutya uye mutoro wekutyisa zvinotyisa, zvakajairika kune akadaro whale; Ini ndinofunga kuti zvese izvi zvisina kunanga zvinobva mukukanganisika kusingabatsiri kwekuzvidira, umo masimba avo akapatsanurwa uye akapesana nepakati ekuona anofanira kuvasanganisa.

Asi nzeve yewhale yakazara kuda kuziva seziso. Kana iwe uri mutorwa zvachose kurudzi rwavo, unogona kuvhima pamusoro pemisoro miviri iyi kwemaawa, uye usamboona iyo nhengo. Nzeve haina shizha rekunze chero; uye mugomba pacharo haugone kupinza quill, saka zvinoshamisa miniti ndiyo. Yakaiswa zvishoma kuseri kweziso. Nekuremekedza nzeve dzavo, musiyano unokosha uyu unofanirwa kuonekwa pakati pesperm whale uye yakarurama. Nepo nzeve yepakutanga iine kuvhura kwekunze, iyo yekupedzisira yakazara uye yakaenzana yakavharwa ne membrane, kuitira kuti isanyatso kuoneka kubva kunze.

Hazvisi kuda kuziva here, kuti chisikwa chakakura kudai sewhale chinofanira kuona nyika neziso diki, uye kunzwa kutinhira nenzeve idiki pane yetsuro? Asi kana maziso ake aive akafara selenzi yeteresikopu huru yeHerschel; nzeve dzake dzakadzivira semaberere edzimba dzokutonga; Izvozvo zvingamuita kuti aonezve kana kupinza pakunzwa here? Ngatiregei zvino nezvipimo nezvipi-injini zvatinazvo pedyo, tisingakwanise pamusoro pemusoro wesperm whale, kuti irare pasi kumusoro; zvino, uchikwira nemanera kuenda kunhongonya, tarisa pasi muromo; uye dai pasina kuti mutumbi zvino waparadzaniswa zvachose nawo, nerambi taigona kudzika muKentucky Mammoth Cave guru redumbu rake. Muromo wakanaka chaizvo uye wakachena! kubva pauriri kusvika kusirin'i, ine mitsetse, kana kuti tingatoti yakanamirwa nepepa jena rinopenya, rinopenya sema satin emwenga.

Asi buda zvino, uye utarise shaya iri yezasi inoshamisa, inoita sevhavha refu nhete yebhokisi refodya hombe, rine hinji kumucheto, panzvimbo yedivi rimwe. Kana iwe ukaiputira kumusoro, kuitira kuti uiise pamusoro, uye ibudise mitsara yemazino ayo, inoita seyakaipa portcullis; uye zvakadaro, maiwe! zvinoratidza kuvazhinji varombo mukubata hove, pavari pavari mapikisi aya anowira nesimba rokuroverera. Asi zvinotonyanya kutyisa kuona, kana mafatomes ari pasi mugungwa, unoona whale yakanyungudika, ichiyangarara ipapo yakarembera, neshaya yaro inoyevedza, yakareba mafiti gumi neshanu, yakarembera yakananga kumakona ekurudyi nemuviri wake, kupasi rose sejib-boom yengarava. Hove iyi haina kufa; anongoora moyo; kunze kwemhando, pamwe; hypochondriac; uye kunyungudika, zvokuti mahinji eshaya dzake asununguka, zvichimusiya arimo murudzi rwakaipa kudaro rwemamiriro ezvinhu, ruzvidzo kurudzi rwake rwose, uyo anofanira, pasina panikiro, kuroverera shaya dzokukiya paari.

Muzviitiko zvizhinji iyi shaya yepazasi—ichisununguka nyore nyore nenyanzvi yokudhirowa—inobviswa uye inosimudzwa pamusoro pedenga nechinangwa chokubvisa mazino enyanga dzenzou, uye kupa mugove weiyo whalebone yakaoma chena iyo vabati vehove vanogadzira nayo marudzi ose ezvinhu zvinoda kuziva, kubatanidza tsvimbo, maamburera, uye mibato yezvikwapuro.

Nekurebesa, kuneta, rushaya runokweverwa pabhodhi, sechinhu chinomisa; uye apo nguva yakafanira inosvika—mamwe mazuva mashomanene pashure perimwe basa—Queequeg, Daggoo, naTashtego, vose vari vanachiremba vemazino vane unyanzvi, vanogadzirira kukweva mazino. Nekunaka kwekucheka-spade, Queequeg inobaya matinji; zvino shaya inoroverwa pasi kusvika kumabhaudhi, uye simbi ichicherwa kubva kumusoro, vanokweva mazino aya, semombe dzeMichigan dzinokweva zvigutsa zveoki kubva musango. Kazhinji kune mazino makumi mana nemaviri pamwe chete; mumawhale ekare, akasakara zvakanyanya, asi asina kuora; kana kuzadzwa nefashoni yedu. Rushaya rwunozocherwa kuita zvidimbu, zvounganidzwa semajoini ekuvaka dzimba.





CHITSAUKO 75. Musoro Werudyi Whale—Maonero Akasiyana.

Tichiyambuka dhizaini, ngativei nekutarisa kwenguva refu kumusoro weRight Whale.

Sezvo muchimiro chakazara musoro weSperm Whale wakaisvonaka ungaenzaniswa nengoro yehondo yeRoma (zvikurukuru pamberi, apo wakatenderedzwa zvakafara); saka, pakuona kwakafara, musoro weRight Whale unobereka zvakada kufanana neshangu huru-yechigunwe. Makore mazana maviri apfuura mumwe mufambi wechiDutch akafananidza chimiro chayo nechokupedzisira chomugadziri weshangu. Uye mune iyi yekupedzisira kana shangu, chembere iya yengano, ine vana vanofamba, inogona kugara zvakanaka, iye nevana vake vese.

Asi paunosvika padhuze nemusoro uyu mukuru unotanga kutora mativi akasiyana, zvichienderana nemaonero ako. Kana iwe ukamira pamusoro wayo uye ukatarisa aya maviri-akaita F-spoutholes, iwe unotora musoro wese kune yakakura bass-viol, uye aya maspiracles, maapertures mubhodhi rayo rekuridza. Bva, zvakare, kana ukanangidzira ziso rako pane uku kurovererwa kwechienzi, kwakaumbwa, kwakafanana nomuzinga pamusoro pomurwi—ichi chinhu chakasvibira, chakatsvinda, icho vaGreenland vanodana kuti “korona,” uye varedzi vokuMaodzanyemba “bhoneti” reRight Whale; uchitarisisa maziso ako pane izvi chete, waizotora musoro kuhunde yemumwe muouki muhombe, une dendere reshiri pachidyiro chawo. Chero zvazvingava, kana uchiona aya magaka mhenyu anorara pano pabhoneti iyi, pfungwa yakadai inenge ine chokwadi kuti ichaitika kwauri; kunze kwekunge, zvirokwazvo, fashoni yako yakagadziriswa nehunyanzvi izwi rekuti "korona" rakapihwawo pairi; muchiitiko icho iwe uchafarira zvikuru kufunga kuti ichi chikara chine simba chiri chaizvoizvo mambo wegungwa ane korona, ane korona yakasvibira yakaiswa pamwe chete nokuda kwake nenzira iyi inoshamisa. Asi kana hove iyi iri mambo, munhu ane hunyoro zvikuru kuti agadzirire korona. Tarisa muromo wezasi wakarembera! kwakakura sei sulk uye pout iripo! nechiyero chomuvezi, chakaita mamita makumi maviri pakureba, namamita mashanu pakadzika; sulk uye pout izvo zvinokupa iwe marita mazana mashanu emafuta uye anopfuura.

Tsitsi huru, ikozvino, kuti iyi whale inosuruvarisa inofanirwa kuve hare-lipped. Kutsemuka kunenge tsoka imwe kuyambuka. Zvichida amai panguva inokosha vaifamba nechikepe vachidzika nemhenderekedzo yePeruvia, apo kudengenyeka kwenyika kwakaita kuti mahombekombe egungwa apenye. Pamusoro pemuromo uyu, sepamusoro pechikumbaridzo chinotsvedza, isu zvino tinotsvedza mumuromo. Pamusoro peshoko rangu ndaiva kuMackinaw, ndinofanira kutora izvi kuva mukati meIndia wigwam. Makanaka Ishe! ndiyo nzira yakaenda Jona here? Denga racho rinosvika mamita gumi nemaviri pakukwirira, uye rinomhanya kukona kwakapinza, sekunge kune nguva dzose danda-danda ipapo; nepo mativi aya ane mbabvu, arched, ane mvere, anotipa neaya anoshamisa, hafu yakatwasuka, scimetar-shaped slats of whalebone, anoti mazana matatu parutivi, izvo zvinoenderana kubva kumusoro chikamu chemusoro kana korona pfupa, zvinoumba iwo mapofu eVenetian ayo kune imwe nzvimbo ambotaurwa. Pakati pemapofu emapfupa emapfupa, sezvaanomira muhurongwa hwawo hwemuzvarirwo, pane mamwe mavanga ekuda kuziva, macurve, makomba, uye mitsetse, umo mamwe mawhalemen anoverenga zera rechisikwa, sezera remuoki nemarin'i awo akatenderera. Kunyangwe chokwadi chechirevo ichi chiri kure nekusanotaridza, asi chine kunhuhwirira kweanoroji yemukana. Chero zvazvingava, kana tikazvibvumira, tinofanira kupa zera rakakura zvikuru kuRight Whale kupfuura pakutanga kutarisa kuchaita sekwakanaka.

Munguva dzakare, zvinoita sekunge zvakakunda zvishuwo zvekuda kuziva nezvemapofu aya.
