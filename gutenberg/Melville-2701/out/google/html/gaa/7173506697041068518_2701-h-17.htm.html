Yɛ gbɛ ni yaa nɔ kɛjɛɔ nakai ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ŋshɔkpɔi kakadaji ni ji Sumatra, Java, Bally, kɛ Timor lɛ tsɔɔ mli; ni kɛ ekrokomɛi babaoo feɔ ŋshɔkpɔ wulu ko, loo gbogbo, ni kɛ Asia kɛ Australia tsaa yɛ elɛɛmɔ naa, ni ejaraa India ŋshɔ kakadaŋŋ ni etseee lɛ mli kɛjɛɔ bokagbɛ ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkpɔi ni etse amɛhe banee lɛ ateŋ. Akɛ sally-ports babaoo efo gbogbo nɛɛ mli koni lɛlɛi kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi anyɛ aya; nɔ ni jeɔ kpo faŋŋ yɛ amɛteŋ ji Sunda kɛ Malacca ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ. Yɛ Sunda ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, titri lɛ, lɛlɛi ni jɛɔ anaigbɛ kɛyaa China lɛ jeɔ kpo kɛyaa China ŋshɔi lɛ amli.

Nakai Sunda ŋshɔkpɔi bibii lɛ gbala Sumatra kɛ Java teŋ; ni edamɔ gbɛ lɛ teŋgbɛ yɛ nakai ŋshɔkpɔi wuji lɛ anɔ, ni nakai ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ eŋɔli ni yɔɔ ekãa, ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi le lɛ akɛ Java Head lɛ fiɔ esɛɛ; amɛ kɛ shinaa ni yɔɔ teŋgbɛ ni gbeleɔ kɛyaa maŋtsɛyeli wulu ko ni awo he gbogbo awo he lɛ kpãaa gbee fioo: ni kɛji akɛ asusu tsofai ni haa mɔ he jɔɔ lɛ, kɛ siliki, kɛ jwetrii, kɛ shika tsuru, kɛ shwuɔ wui, ni akɛwo nakai bokagbɛ ŋshɔ lɛ ŋshɔkpɔi akpei lɛ anɔ nii lɛ ahe lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni esa akɛ eji adebɔɔ mli gbɛjianɔtoo ko ni he hiaa waa, ni le . hiɛ su, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni etsuuu nii aha lɛ, akɛ abuɔ he kɛjɛɔ anaigbɛ je ni nuɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ he lɛ he. Sunda Ŋshɔ lɛ ŋshɔnaa lɛ bɛ nakai ŋshɔkpɔi ni yeɔ nɔ ni buɔ Mediteranea, Baltic, kɛ Propontis ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ anaa. Nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ Denmarkbii lɛ, nɛkɛ Bokagbɛbii nɛɛ biɔ ni ajie lɛlɛi ni bɛ naagbee ni nyiɛɔ kɔɔyɔɔ hiɛ, ni afii ohai abɔ ni eho lɛ, gbɛkɛ kɛ shwane, etsɔ Sumatra kɛ Java ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ ateŋ, ni akɛ bokagbɛ jatsui ni jara wa fe fɛɛ lɛ woɔ lɛlɛi ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi lɛ anɔ lɛ awo amɛ hiɛ nyam. Shi yɛ be mli ni amɛjɛɔ amɛsuɔmɔ mli amɛjieɔ kusum nifeemɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kpo lɛ, amɛkpooo amɛhe ni amɛkɛɛ akɛ esa akɛ amɛwo too ni mli wa fe tsutsu lɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ kwraa.

Be ni eho lɛ, Malaybii ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ ni yɔɔ hulutsoo kɛ ŋshɔkpɔi bibii ni yɔɔ Sumatra lɛ ateŋ lɛ, efo ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi ni tsɔɔ ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ lɛ anɔ, ni amɛmiibi too yɛ amɛ akpɔlɔi lɛ anaa waa. Eyɛ mli akɛ kɛtsɔ toigbalamɔ ni lá hoɔ yɛ mli ni amɛná shii abɔ yɛ Europa ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi lɛ adɛŋ lɛ nɔ moŋ, shi yɛ sɛɛ mli nɛɛ, ajie ekãa ni nɛkɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi nɛɛ yɔɔ lɛ kpo yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ; shi kɛlɛ, yɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ po lɛ, wɔnuɔ Ŋleshi kɛ Amerika lɛlɛi ahe yɛ bei komɛi amli, ni yɛ nakai nui lɛ amli lɛ, amɛbote mli ni amɛju amɛnibii ni amɛshwaaa amɛhe lɛ ahe.

Kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ kpakpa ni mli tse lɛ, Pequod lɛ miibɛŋkɛ ŋshɔkpɔi nɛɛ ahe agbɛnɛ; Ahab to eyiŋ akɛ ebaatsɔ amɛmli kɛya Javan ŋshɔ lɛ mli, ni kɛjɛ jɛmɛ lɛ, efã gbɛ kɛya kooyigbɛ, yɛ nu ni ale akɛ Sperm Whale lɛ fɔɔ jɛmɛ yaa biɛ kɛ biɛ lɛ nɔ, ni etsɔ Philippine Ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ kɛya ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni enine ashɛ Japan ŋshɔnaa ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ nɔ, yɛ be ni sa mli kɛha ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ be wulu ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ. Kɛtsɔ gbɛi nɛɛ anɔ lɛ, Pequod ni bɔleɔ ŋshɔ lɛ he lɛ baafo nɔ ni miihe ashɛ he fɛɛ he ni ale akɛ Sperm Whale lɛ fãa gbɛ yɛ je lɛŋ lɛ, dani ebaa shi yɛ Line ni yɔɔ Pasifik lɛ nɔ; he ni Ahab, eyɛ mli akɛ he fɛɛ he kroko lɛ eyeee omanye yɛ esɛɛtiumɔ mli lɛ, ekɛ ehe fɔ shi waa akɛ ebaawu eshi Moby Dick, yɛ ŋshɔ ni ale lɛ akɛ eyaa jɛmɛ shii abɔ lɛ mli; kɛ be mli ni abaanyɛ asusu akɛ eji mɔ ni haoɔ lɛ.

Shi te agbɛnɛ te efeɔ tɛŋŋ? yɛ nɛkɛ zoned taomɔ nɛɛ mli lɛ, ani Ahab etaaa shikpɔŋ ko he? ani e-crew lɛ nuɔ kɔɔyɔɔ? Eji anɔkwale akɛ, ebaakpa kɛha nu. Dabi. Be kakadaŋŋ nɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ, ni hulu ni joɔ foi yɛ circus mli lɛ efo foi yɛ e-la ring lɛ mli, ni niyenii ko he ehiaaa lɛ fe nɔ ni yɔɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ emli lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, Ahab. Kadimɔ enɛ hu, yɛ whaler lɛ mli. Yɛ be mli ni akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ nibii ni jɛ maŋsɛɛ lɛ woɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi krokomɛi amli, koni ajie kɛya maŋsɛɛ ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ lɛlɛdaamɔhei lɛ; ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛ ni nyiɛɔ je lɛŋ lɛ ehiɛɛɛ jatsu ko kwraa fe lɛ diɛŋtsɛ kɛ emli nitsulɔi lɛ, amɛtawuu nii kɛ nibii ni amɛtaoɔ. Eyɛ ŋshɔ muu fɛɛ mli nibii ni ekɛwo tɔ mli yɛ e-hold ni fa babaoo lɛ mli. Akɛ nibii ni akɛtsuɔ nii lɛ ebɔ ehe; jeee kɛ tsina-lead kɛ kentledge ni akɛtsuuu nii kwraa. Ehiɛɔ afii babaoo nu yɛ emli. Nantucket nu ni hi jogbaŋŋ momo lɛ mli tse; ni, kɛ́ afii etɛ eho lɛ, Nantucketer, ni yɔɔ Pasifik lɛ sumɔɔ ni enu dani nu ni mli edɔ lɛ aba, shi nyɛsɛɛ nɛɛ ekɛ ŋshɔ lɛ fã gbɛ yɛ tsɛnsii amli, kɛjɛ Peru loo India faai lɛ amli. No hewɔ lɛ eji akɛ, yɛ be mli ni ekolɛ lɛlɛi krokomɛi tee China kɛjɛ New York, ni amɛku amɛsɛɛ amɛba ekoŋŋ, ni amɛyashɛ lɛlɛdaamɔhei babaoo ahe lɛ, ekolɛ ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ lɛ, yɛ nakai be lɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, enaaa shikpɔŋ ŋmɔtɔ kome po; egbɛfalɔi lɛ enaaa gbɔmɔ ko fe ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi ni nyiɛɔ nu hiɛ tamɔ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe. No hewɔ lɛ, okɛ sane ni ji akɛ nu afua kroko eba lɛ tee amɛ; nɔ pɛ ni amɛbaaha hetoo ji akɛ—“Ojogbaŋŋ, gbekɛbii hii, adeka lɛ nɛ!”

Amrɔ nɛɛ, taakɛ amɔmɔ Sperm Whales babaoo yɛ Java anaigbɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa, yɛ he ni bɛŋkɛ Straits of Sunda lɛ; lɛɛlɛŋ, taakɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ fa ko, ni ji ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ kpɛlɛ nɔ akɛ eji he ko ni hi jogbaŋŋ kɛha ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfaa; no hewɔ lɛ, beni Pequod lɛ náa Java Yitso lɛ nɔ babaoo lɛ, ajie mɛi ni kwɛɔ jɛmɛ lɛ ayi shii abɔ, ni awo amɛ ŋaa koni amɛhiɛ ahi amɛhe nɔ jogbaŋŋ. Shi eyɛ mli akɛ etsɛɛɛ ni shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ gɔji ni ji tsei eŋɔli lɛ je kpo yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ abɛkugbɛ, ni akɛ miishɛɛ ŋmɔlɔi shwie sinamon hee lɛ nɔ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli moŋ, shi atsɔɔɔ jet kome po mli. Shwɛ fioo ni lɛlɛ lɛ baakpa susumɔ fɛɛ ni ji akɛ ebaagbee shwɛmɔ fɛɛ shwɛmɔ mli yɛ biɛ lɛ, no mli lɛ eshwɛ fioo ni ebaabote ŋshɔ lɛ naa, beni anu kusum nifeemɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ kɛjɛ ŋwɛi, ni etsɛɛɛ tsɔ ni nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ waa ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ bahere wɔ yi.

Shi, esa akɛ asusu akɛ, yɛ nitsumɔ ni bɛ nijiaŋwujee ni akɛtsu nii yɛ ŋshɔi ejwɛ lɛ fɛɛ nɔ lɛ hewɔ lɛ, Sperm Whales lɛ, yɛ nɔ najiaŋ ni amɛaafã gbɛ yɛ kui bibii amli, tamɔ tsutsu lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ akɛ amɛ kpeɔ shii abɔ yɛ kui babaoo amli, bei komɛi lɛ, amɛyi fa babaoo, amɛkã kpaŋmɔ kɛ kpaŋmɔ ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ kɛha amɛhe yelikɛbuamɔ kɛ amɛhebuu kitã. Abaanyɛ akɛ shihilɛ ni ji akɛ, yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfaa hei ni hi fe fɛɛ lɛ po mli lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ nyɛbaanyɛ nyɛfee ekome kɛfã gbɛ otsii kɛ nyɔji babaoo, ni ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔŋloo kome po ehereee nyɛ; ni no sɛɛ lɛ, nɔ ni bei komɛi lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni akpei abɔ yɛ akpei abɔ nɔ lɛ baahere bo yi trukaa.

Elɛɛmɔ yɛ ŋshɔkei enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ amɛ teŋ jɛkɛmɔ aaafee kilomitai enyɔ loo etɛ, ni efee kooloo fã agbo ko, ni efua ŋwɛi ŋshɔkei lɛ fã kome, ŋshɔŋlooi ni ji whale-jets ni yaa nɔ lɛ miitswa amɛhe ni amɛmiikpɛ yɛ shwane kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli. Nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ Right Whale lɛ mli ŋshɔŋlooi enyɔ ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ, ni, ni amɛjaa yɛ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ, amɛgbeeɔ shi yɛ niji enyɔ mli, tamɔ willow tso niji ni shwieɔ shi lɛ, Sperm Whale lɛ ŋshɔŋloo kome ni tsɔɔ hiɛ lɛ jieɔ ŋshɔkei yɛŋ ni mli tse ni etse ehe kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ kpo, ni eyaa ŋwɛi be fɛɛ be.

No hewɔ lɛ, kɛji akɛ ana lɛ kɛjɛ Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ, beni ebaakwɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ gɔŋ ko ni nɔ kwɔ nɔ lɛ, nɛkɛ lasu ni hoɔ nɛɛ, ni amɛteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ shwieɔ ehe kɛyaa kɔɔyɔɔ mli, ni anaa kɛtsɔɔ kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli edɔ ni mli efutu mli lɛ, jieɔ tamɔ lasu tsuji akpe ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni yɔɔ maŋtiase ko ni mli eyi obɔ lɛ mli, ni mɔ ko tsɔɔ mli yɛ a.

Beni asraafoi ni nyiɛɔ lɛ miibɛŋkɛ he ko ni bɛ naanyobɔɔ he ko ni yɔɔ gɔji lɛ amli lɛ, amɛnyiɛɔ oya, kɛ miishɛɛ fɛɛ ni amɛkɛ nakai gbɛ ni yɔɔ oshara lɛ baawo amɛsɛɛ, ni amɛgbɛ amɛshwã shikome ekoŋŋ yɛ shweshweeshwe ni akɛtoɔ he lɛ mli yɛ shikpɔŋ kplanaa lɛ nɔ; nakai nɔŋŋ po ji bɔ ni etamɔ nɔ ni nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi akuu wulu nɛɛ miiho oya kɛmiitsɔ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ agbɛnɛ; amɛmiifo amɛ semicircle lɛ fɛ̃i lɛ mli fiofio, ni amɛmiiya nɔ amɛmiifo, yɛ ŋwɛi ŋta kome ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ, shi eyɛ ŋwɛi ŋta lolo lɛ mli.

Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ fɛɛ eyi amɛsɛɛ; harpooneers ni miitsu amɛtawuu nii lɛ ahe nii, ni amɛmiitswa amɛhe waa kɛmiijɛ amɛlɛlɛi ni efo shi lolo lɛ ayitsei amli. Kɛji kɔɔyɔɔ ni nyiɛɔ Sunda Ŋshɔkpɔi nɛɛ anɔ lɛ pɛ, ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ, gbɔi babaoo lɛ baaya Bokagbɛ ŋshɔi lɛ amli pɛ koni amɛyaye bɔ ​​ni amɔmɔ amɛyibɔ ​​ni jeee mɛi fioo ko pɛ lɛ he odase. Ni namɔ baanyɛ akɛɛ kɛji akɛ, yɛ nakai gbɛfalɔi akuu ni ebua amɛhe naa lɛ mli lɛ, ekolɛ Moby Dick diɛŋtsɛ efoŋ nu mli yɛ be kukuoo mli, tamɔ shwuɔ yɛŋ ni ajaa lɛ yɛ Siamesebii lɛ amaŋtsɛyeli he gbɛfaa lɛ mli lɛ! No hewɔ lɛ, wɔkɛ stun-sail ni ato stun-sail nɔ lɛ fã gbɛ, ni wɔkɛ nɛkɛ leviathan nɛɛ nyiɛ wɔhiɛ; beni, trukaa lɛ, anu Tashtego gbee, ni ekɛ gbee ni wa miikudɔ jwɛŋmɔ kɛmiiya nɔ ko ni yɔɔ wɔhiɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ.

Nɔ ni kɛ nyɔŋtsere ni yɔɔ wɔtsɔne lɛ mli lɛ kpãa gbee lɛ, wɔna ekroko hu yɛ wɔsɛɛ. Etamɔ nɔ ni lasu yɛŋ ni etse ehe lɛ ji nɔ ni fee, ni teɔ shi ni egbeeɔ shi tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ashishifãi; shi amɛbaaa ni amɛyaaa nakai kwraa; ejaakɛ amɛhoɔ amɛhe be fɛɛ be, ni naagbee lɛ amɛlaajeee. Beni Ahab kɛ eglase lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ nɔ ni ena nɛɛ mli lɛ, etsɔ ehe oya yɛ e-pivot-hole lɛ mli, ni ebolɔ akɛ, “Yɛ ŋwɛi jɛmɛ, ni okɛ rig ŋmɔlɔi kɛ baagii awo lɛlɛ lɛ mli nu;—Malaybii, onukpa, kɛ wɔsɛɛ!”

Taakɛ eji akɛ amɛhi shi be kakadaŋŋ tsɔ yɛ ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ ayitsei lɛ asɛɛ, kɛyashi kulɛ esa akɛ Pequod lɛ abote ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, nɛkɛ Asiabii ni yɔɔ basabasa nɛɛ miitiu amɛsɛɛ amrɔ nɛɛ, koni amɛkɛwo amɛsɛɛ ni amɛkpa ni amɛkwɛ amɛhe nɔ jogbaŋŋ fe nine lɛ he nyɔmɔ. Shi beni Pequod ni he yɔɔ oya lɛ, kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ hee ni nyiɛɔ hiɛ lɛ, lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ji mɔ ni miitiu lɛ waa lɛ; bɔ ni nɛkɛ mɛi ni feɔ mɛi ejurɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ nɛɛ ji mɛi ni yeɔ ebuaa lɛ ni ekɛ oyaiyeli yaa nɔ ni lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehala lɛ nɔ, —ni ji tsɔne ni akɛtaraa tsɔne nɔ kɛ tsɔne ni akɛtaraa tsɔne nɔ kɛkɛ kɛha lɛ, akɛ amɛji. Taakɛ eji yɛ glase shishi yɛ nine shishi lɛ, Ahab nyiɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ kɛtee kɛba; yɛ ehiɛgbɛ lɛ mli lɛ, enaa kooloi wuji ni etiuɔ lɛ, ni yɛ esɛɛgbɛ lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ ojotswalɔi ni lá shweɔ amɛ lɛ miitiu lɛ ; ekomɛi ni yɔɔ fɛo tamɔ nɔ ni awie he yɛ ŋwɛi nɛɛ tamɔ lɛ nɔ. Ni beni ekwɛ nu mli mujiwoo ni lɛlɛ lɛ nyiɛ mli yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ gbogboi eŋɔlii lɛ anɔ lɛ, ni esusu akɛ nakai shinaa lɛ ji gbɛ ni kɛ mɔ yaa ehe nyɔmɔwoo lɛ mli lɛ, ni ena bɔ ni nakai shinaa lɛ nɔŋŋ mli etsɔɔ lɛ amrɔ nɛɛ ni atiuɔ lɛ ni atiuɔ lɛ kɛyaa egbele naagbee lɛ; ni jeee nakai pɛ, shi moŋ ŋshɔ hiɛ ojotswalɔi akuu ko ni bɛ henumɔi kɛ daimonioi ni heee Nyɔŋmɔ nɔ amɛyeee lɛ miiwo lɛ hewalɛ waa kɛ amɛloomɔi lɛ;—beni nɛkɛ henɔwomɔi nɛɛ fɛɛ tsɔ ejwɛŋmɔ mli lɛ, Ahab hiɛ efee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni ehiɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, tamɔ ŋshɔnaa ŋã diŋ ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ yɛ be mli ni ahum ko ni tswaa kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ sɛɛ.

Shi susumɔi ni tamɔ enɛɛmɛi hao mɛi fioo ko pɛ ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli ni amɛfeee nɔ ko lɛ; ni beni, beni ekɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ojotswalɔi lɛ shwie shi ni amɛshwie shi fiofio sɛɛ lɛ, naagbee lɛ Pequod lɛ tswa Cockatoo Point ni yɔɔ Sumatra fã lɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji amli lɛ, ni eje kpo yɛ naagbee yɛ nui wuji ni yɔɔ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ anɔ; no sɛɛ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ŋshɔŋlooi ni tswaa ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ miiye awerɛho waa akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni he yɔɔ oya lɛ miiya nɔ amɛye lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ, fe bɔ ni amɛmiishɛ amɛhe akɛ lɛlɛ lɛ eye Malaybii lɛ anɔ kunim nɛkɛ. Yɛ gbɛi fɛɛ nɔ ni amɛgbɛɔ amɛshwaa yɛ kooyigbɛ wuji ni bɛ gbɛjianɔtoo mli, ni amɛfoɔ kɛyaa biɛ kɛ biɛ ni yiŋtoo ko bɛ he, kɛtsɔ amɛ ŋmɔlɔ kukuji ni mli tse lɛ nɔ lɛ, amɛjie amɛ gbeyeishemɔ ni gbalaa amɛjwɛŋmɔ lɛ kpo faŋŋ. Eji nɛkɛ Leviatanbii nɛɛ ji tooi akuu ko ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko kɛkɛ, ni tsinabii etɛ ni he yɔɔ gbeyei nyiɛɔ amɛsɛɛ yɛ kooloi alɛɛhe lɛ kulɛ, amɛnyɛŋ amɛjie haomɔ ni fe nine ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kpo. Shi nɛkɛ hiɛgbele ni baa yɛ bei komɛi amli nɛɛ ji nɔ ni miihe ashɛ bɔɔ nii ni kwɛɔ kooloi anɔ lɛ fɛɛ asu. Eyɛ mli akɛ amɛbua amɛhe naa yɛ akpei nyɔŋmai abɔ mli moŋ, shi Anaigbɛ tsinabii ni hiɛ jata tsitsii lɛ ejo foi kɛjɛ okpɔŋɔ nɔtalɔ ko ni yɔɔ ekome too lɛ hiɛ. Odase, adesai fɛɛ hu, bɔ ni kɛ́ abua amɛnaa yɛ tooi akuu ni yɔɔ sinifeemɔ he bu ko mli lɛ, amɛbaafee, yɛ la he ŋmɛlɛtswaa fioo ko pɛ mli lɛ, amɛbaajo helter-skelter kɛya hei ni ajieɔ nibii kɛjɛɔ mli lɛ, amɛbaabua amɛhe naa, amɛbaanyiɛ amɛhe, amɛbaatswa amɛhe, ni amɛbaatswa amɛhe kɛya gbele mli ni amɛshwaaa amɛhe. No hewɔ lɛ, nɔ ni hi fe fɛɛ ji ni otsi ohe kɛjɛ naakpɛɛ nii ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni yɔɔ wɔhiɛ lɛ ahe lɛ he, ejaakɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ kooloi lɛ ashishiumɔ ko bɛ ni gbɔmɛi ashishiumɔi lɛ nyɛŋ aye nɔ ni bɛ naagbee.

Eyɛ mli akɛ, taakɛ awie lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ babaoo miifee basabasa moŋ, shi esa akɛ akadi akɛ, kɛ abua naa lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi akuu lɛ fɛɛ eyaaa hiɛ loo ekuuu esɛɛ, shi moŋ amɛ fɛɛ amɛhi he kome. Taakɛ eji kusum nifeemɔ yɛ nakai shihilɛi lɛ amli lɛ, lɛlɛi lɛ mli gbala amrɔ nɔŋŋ, ni eko fɛɛ eko fee ŋshɔŋloo kome pɛ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa. Yɛ aaafee minitii etɛ mli lɛ, atswa Queequeg harpoon lɛ; loo ni atswa lɛ lɛ shwieɔ nu ni haa mɔ shwilafoi lɛ eshwieɔ wɔhiɛ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekɛ wɔ joɔ foi tamɔ la, ni ekɛ tsɔne lɛ kudɔɔ tɛ̃ɛ kɛyaa tooi akuu lɛ tsui lɛ mli. Eyɛ mli akɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ fã ni tamɔ nɛkɛ lɛ tswa yɛ shihilɛi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli moŋ, shi jeee nɔ ko ni eko bako nakai dã yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ; ni lɛɛlɛŋ, aaafee be fɛɛ be lɛ, akpaa gbɛ babaoo loo fioo; shi kɛlɛ, ekɛ shihilɛi ni tsakeɔ ni yɔɔ oshara fe fɛɛ yɛ wuoyeli mli lɛ ateŋ ekome baa. Ejaakɛ be mli ni kooloo ni he yɔɔ oya lɛ gbalaa bo kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ mli kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ, okɛ adieu haa koni okwɛ shihilɛ mli jogbaŋŋ ni ohi shi yɛ gbee ni mli wa mli pɛ.

Beni, shwilafo kɛ mumui lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ shwie ehe kɛtee hiɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni kɛtsɔ oyaiyeli hewalɛ nɔ ejie dade ŋshɔŋloo ni ekpɛtɛ ehe lɛ; beni wɔgbala ŋshɔ lɛ mli ŋmei yɛŋ ko mli yɛ nɛkɛ gbɛ nɔ, ni bɔɔ nii ni egbɔjɔ ni miiho kɛmiiba kɛmiiba wɔhe lɛ miiwo wɔhe gbeyei yɛ he fɛɛ he beni wɔfoɔ lɛ; wɔ lɛlɛ ni ebɔle ehe lɛ tamɔ lɛlɛ ko ni ais ŋshɔkpɔi ebɔle ehe yɛ ahum ko mli, ni eebɔ mɔdɛŋ ni etsɔ amɛ gbɛjegbɛi kɛ ŋshɔkpɔi ni mli wawai lɛ anɔ, ni eleee be mli ni ekolɛ ebaatswa lɛ ni etswa lɛ.

Shi Queequeg esheee gbeyei fioo, shi ekɛ nuufeemɔ kudɔ wɔ; amrɔ nɛɛ ni amɛmiishi kɛmiijɛ kooloo wulu nɛɛ he tɛ̃ɛ kɛmiitsɔ wɔ gbɛ lɛ nɔ kɛtsɔ hiɛ; amrɔ nɛɛ eetsi ehe kɛmiijɛ nakai, ni e-fluks wuji lɛ fĩ eyiteŋ lɛ he, yɛ be mli ni be fɛɛ be lɛ, Starbuck teɔ shi yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ amli, ni ehiɛ lance, ni ekɛ ŋshɔŋlooi fɛɛ ni ebaanyɛ ekɛ ŋshɔŋlooi kukuji ashɛ he lɛ miitswa wɔgbɛ lɛ, ejaakɛ be bɛ ni ekɛbaafee ŋshɔŋlooi kakadaji. Ni ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ hu efeee mɛi ni bɛ nɔ ko kwraa, eyɛ mli akɛ agbɛnɛ ajie amɛ nitsumɔ ni amɛtsuɔ daa lɛ kwraa moŋ. Amɛyaa nitsumɔ lɛ fã ni ji bɔlɔmɔ lɛ mli titri. “Jee gbɛ lɛ nɔ, Komodo!” mɔ kome bolɔ, kɛha dromedary agbo ko ni te shi trukaa kɛ gbɔmɔtso kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ, ni ewo wɔhe gbeyei akɛ ebaatsɔ nu mli be kukuoo ko. “Kɛ otsitsi lɛ aba shi waa, jɛmɛ!” bolɔ sɛkɛndi kome kɛha mɔ kroko, ni, beni ebɛŋkɛ wɔ gunwale lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ etsitsi ni tamɔ fan lɛ miiha ehe miijɔ lɛ yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli.

Whale lɛlɛi fɛɛ hiɛɔ nibii komɛi ni haa mɔ taoɔ ele nɔ ni yaa nɔ, ni Nantucket Indiabii lɛ ji mɛi ni fee shishijee, ni atsɛɔ amɛ tsofai fɔji. Akɛ tsei enyɔ ni mli tse ni dalɛ tamɔ amɛhe lɛ ebɔ he waa, bɔni afee ni amɛfo amɛhe ŋmeii lɛ amli yɛ ŋwɛigbɛ ni ja; no sɛɛ lɛ, akɛ kpãa ni kɛlɛ waa fĩɔ nɛkɛ blɔki nɛɛ teŋgbɛ, ni akɛ kpãa lɛ naagbee kroko lɛ fɔ̃ɔ lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛkpɛtɛ ŋtayaa he yɛ be kukuoo mli. Gallied whales lɛ ateŋ titri ji he ni akɛ tsofa nɛɛ tsuɔ nii yɛ. Ejaakɛ no lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi babaoo ebɔle ohe fe bɔ ni obaanyɛ otiu amɛ yɛ be kome mli. Shi jeee daa gbi akɛ sperm whale kpeɔ; no hewɔ lɛ, yɛ be mli ni obaanyɛ lɛ, esa akɛ ogbe nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni obaanyɛ. Ni kɛji onyɛɛɛ ogbe amɛ fɛɛ shikome lɛ, esa akɛ okɛ fɛ̃i atswa amɛ, koni no sɛɛ lɛ, anyɛ agbe amɛ yɛ ohejɔɔmɔ be mli. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ bei ni tamɔ enɛɛmɛi amli lɛ, tsofai fɔji lɛ baa mli. Akɛ amɛteŋ etɛ wo wɔlɛlɛ lɛ mli. Atswa klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ lɛ kɛ nɔ ni ji enyɔ lɛ yɛ omanyeyeli mli, ni wɔna ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ni amɛmiijo foi kɛ naakpɛɛ, ni tsofa ni akɛgbalaa ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ni tsiɔ amɛnaa babaoo ni yɔɔ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ tsiɔ amɛnaa. Amɛfee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ tamɔ efɔŋfeelɔi ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ŋwɛi kɛ bɔɔlu lɛ. Shi beni etswa nɔ ni ji etɛ lɛ, yɛ nifeemɔ ni ji tso ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ ni ekɛshwieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ mli lɛ, emɔ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛii lɛ eko shishi, ni yɛ be kukuoo mli lɛ, efo mli ni ekɛtee, ni ekɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ shwie lɛlɛ lɛ shishi beni sɛi lɛ shwieɔ kɛjɛɔ eshishi lɛ. Yɛ afãi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ lɛ, ŋshɔ lɛ ba yɛ tsei ni epila lɛ ahe, shi wɔkɛ drawa kɛ atadei enyɔ loo etɛ wo mli, ni no hewɔ lɛ wɔkpa nu ni hoɔ lɛ kɛha be lɛ.

No mli lɛ, eshwɛ fioo ni anyɛɛɛ atswa nɛkɛ tsofai fɔji ni akɛwo mli nɛɛ, ani jeee akɛ beni wɔyaa hiɛ kɛboteɔ tooi akuu lɛ mli lɛ, wɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ gbɛ lɛ ba shi kwraa; kɛfata he lɛ, akɛ beni wɔyaa shɔŋŋ lolo kɛjɛɔ basabasafeemɔ ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ he lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni basabasafeemɔi ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ miiba shi. No hewɔ lɛ, beni naagbee lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ni tswaa lɛ je kpo, ni ŋshɔŋloo ni gbalaa lɛ lɛ laaje kɛtee afã; no sɛɛ lɛ, kɛtsɔ ehe ni ekɛgbala ehe lɛ mli hewalɛ ni miiba shi lɛ nɔ lɛ, wɔfo ŋshɔŋ loofɔji enyɔ ateŋ kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ mligbɛ fe fɛɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni wɔjɛ gɔŋ ko nɔ faa ko mli wɔfo kɛtee jɔɔ lɛ mli ŋshɔkpɔ ko ni yɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ mli. Biɛ ji he ni ahum ni tswaa yɛ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji ni yɔɔ kponɔgbɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ ateŋ lɛ, anuɔ he shi anuuu he. Yɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔkpɔ ni yɔɔ teŋgbɛ nɛɛ mli lɛ, ŋshɔ lɛ jieɔ nakai ŋshɔ ni tamɔ satin ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni atsɛɔ lɛ sleek, ni ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ esusumɔi ni yɔɔ dioo fe tsutsu lɛ mli lɛ haa lɛ kpo. Hɛɛ, wɔyɛ nakai toiŋjɔlɛ ni amɛkɛɛ ehiɔ basabasafeemɔ fɛɛ basabasafeemɔ mli lɛ mli agbɛnɛ. Ni lolo yɛ he ni ajie wɔjwɛŋmɔ kɛjɛ nɔ lɛ, wɔna basabasafeemɔi ni yɔɔ kponɔgbɛ ŋwɛibɔfoi ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ amli lɛ, ni wɔna ŋshɔŋlooi akui ni nyiɛɔ amɛsɛɛ, ekpaa loo nyɔŋma yɛ eko fɛɛ eko mli, ni amɛmiiya oya kɛmiibɔle amɛhe, tamɔ okpɔŋɔi ni ebɔle amɛhe babaoo yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi amli; ni amɛbɛŋkɛ amɛhe kpaakpa waa, ni ekolɛ Titanic circus kudɔlɔ ko baanyɛ atsɔ teŋgbɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ yɛ gbɛ ni waaa nɔ, ni nakai nɔŋŋ ebaatsɔ amɛsɛɛ kɛbɔle amɛhe. Akɛni ŋshɔŋlooi ni miihoso amɛhe lɛ ayi fa babaoo, ni amɛbɔle ŋshɔŋlooi akuu lɛ fã ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ he amrɔ nɔŋŋ hewɔ lɛ, hegbɛ ko bɛ ni wɔɔtsɔ nɔ wɔjo foi amrɔ nɛɛ. Esa akɛ wɔkwɛ koni gbogbo ni hiɛ ka ni bɔle wɔhe lɛ mli etse; gbogbo ni eŋmɛ wɔ gbɛ pɛ koni ekɛtsi wɔ naa lɛ. Akɛni wɔhi ŋshɔ lɛ teŋgbɛ hewɔ lɛ, tsinai bibii kɛ tsinabii ni atsɔse amɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ basaraa wɔ yɛ bei komɛi amli; nɛkɛ gbɔfeelɔ ni aye enɔ kunim nɛɛ yei kɛ gbekɛbii lɛ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛ akɛ bei komɛi amli gbɛ ni mli kwɔ ni yɔɔ kponɔgbɛ kooyigbɛ ni tsɔɔ ehe lɛ ateŋ lɛ fata he, kɛ gbɛ ni kã podi srɔtoi ni yɔɔ nakai kooyii lɛ eko fɛɛ eko mli lɛ ateŋ lɛ fata he lɛ, esa akɛ kpokpaa muu lɛ fɛɛ ni yɔɔ nɛkɛ be nɛɛ mli, ni asafo muu lɛ fɛɛ ekpɛlɛ nɔ lɛ, kɛ hoo lɛ, ehiɛ sqɛɛ mile enyɔ loo etɛ. Bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ—eyɛ mli akɛ kaa ni tamɔ nɛkɛ yɛ be ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ baanyɛ afee lakamɔ moŋ—shi abaanyɛ ana ŋmeii ni jɛɔ wɔlɛlɛ ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ ni tamɔ nɔ ni miitswa kɛmiijɛ ŋwɛi ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ mli. Mitsĩɔ shihilɛ nɛɛ tã, ejaakɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni ajɛ yiŋtoo mli atswa tsinai kɛ tsinabii lɛ awo nɛkɛ mligbɛ tsinai akuu nɛɛ mli; ni tamɔ nɔ ni bɔ ni tooi akuu lɛ fa babaoo kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ etsĩ amɛ gbɛ koni amɛkase nɔ pɔtɛɛ ni ha amɛkpa nitsumɔ lɛ; aloo, ekolɛ, akɛ eji oblanyo fioo, ni ebɛ ŋaa, ni ebɛ efɔŋ ko ni bɛ niiashikpamɔ yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ; shi kɛlɛ, ekolɛ eji lɛ, nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi bibii nɛɛ—ni amrɔ nɛɛ kɛ agbɛnɛ amɛbasaraa wɔlɛlɛ ni efee dioo lɛ kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ—jieɔ gbeyeishemɔ kɛ hekɛnɔfɔɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ kpo, loo kɛ jeee nakai lɛ, gbeyeishemɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lolo ni anyɛɛɛ afee lɛ naakpɛɛ. Tamɔ shia gbeei amɛba ni amɛmiibɔle wɔhe, kɛyashi wɔ tui lɛ amli tɔ̃ɔ, ni amɛta amɛhe; kɛyashi eshwɛ fioo ni ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni shɛɛ sane ko etsɔ amɛ shia trukaa. Queequeg tswa amɛhiɛ; Starbuck kɛ e-lance lɛ shwie amɛsɛɛ; shi esheɔ nɔ ni baaba lɛ gbeyei, ejaakɛ be lɛ tsi ehe kɛjɛ he ni ekɛbaatsu nii lɛ he.

Shi yɛ nɛkɛ naakpɛɛ je ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi nɛɛ shishi shɔŋŋ lɛ, jeŋ kroko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lolo lɛ kɛ wɔhiŋmɛii kpe beni wɔkwɛɔ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ. Ejaakɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ anyɛmɛi ni haa amɛbii lɛ ahe mfonirii lɛ, kɛ mɛi ni kɛtsɔ amɛhe ni amɛhe wa waa lɛ nɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni etsɛɛɛ ni amɛbaatsɔ nyɛmɛi lɛ, ni akɛfɔ̃ nakai nu mli tsũi lɛ amli lɛ. Taakɛ mitsɔɔ mli lɛ, ŋshɔ lɛ mli kwɔ waa ni eyɛ faŋŋ fe nine; ni akɛ adesai abifabii ni miiha nu lɛ baakwɛ nu lɛ mli yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ kɛ hiɛdɔɔ mli, tamɔ nɔ ni amɛhiɔ shi yɛ shihilɛi srɔtoi enyɔ mli yɛ nakai beaŋ; ni yɛ be mli ni amɛmiiye gbele niyenii lolo lɛ, nyɛyea mumɔŋ niyenii lolo yɛ kaimɔ ko ni jeee shikpɔŋ nɔ nɔ lɛ nɔ;—nakai nɔŋŋ po etamɔ nɔ ni nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi nɛɛ abifabii lɛ miikwɛ wɔ nɔ, shi jeee wɔ, tamɔ nɔ ni wɔji Gulfweed bibioo ko kɛkɛ yɛ amɛhiŋmɛii ni afɔ lɛ ehee lɛ ahiɛ. Beni amɛmiiho yɛ amɛsɛɛ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni nyɛmɛi lɛ hu miikwɛ wɔ dioo. Nɛkɛ abifabii bibii nɛɛ ateŋ mɔ kome, ni kɛjɛ okadii komɛi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ amli lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni eyeko gbi kome pɛ lɛ, ekolɛ elɛɛmɔ ji aaafee ninetalɔi nyɔŋma kɛ ejwɛ, ni elɛɛmɔ aaafee ninetalɔi enumɔ. Efee mɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ fioo; eyɛ mli akɛ etamɔ nɔ ni egbɔmɔtso lɛ he ehiaaa lolo moŋ shi ehiɛ edɔ kɛjɛ nakai shihilɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni ehi mli nyɛsɛɛ nɛɛ yɛ nyɛ lɛ mli lɛ mli; he ni, kɛjɛ etsitsi nɔ kɛyashi eyitso nɔ, ni fɛɛ efee klalo kɛha naagbee fɛ̃i be lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ni afɔ lɛ lɛ kã shi ni ekoto tamɔ Tartar ŋshɔŋloo ko. Etsitsii ni yɔɔ esɛɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ, kɛ etsitsii lɛ ashishifãi lɛ, hiɛ abifao toi ni akpɛ́ ni ejɛ maŋsɛɛ hei eba ehee lɛ he mfoniri lɛ mli ehee lolo.

"Line! line!" Queequeg bolɔ, ni ekwɛ gunwale lɛ nɔ; “eya oya! eya oya!—Namɔ line lɛ! Namɔ tswa lɛ?—Whale enyɔ; eko agbo, eko bibioo!”

“Mɛni yeɔ bo, nuu?” Starbuck bolɔ.

Queequeg kɛɛ, ni ekɛ enine tsɔɔ shi akɛ: “Kwɛmɔ-e biɛ.”

Taakɛ eji beni ŋshɔŋloo ni atswa lɛ lɛ, nɔ ni jɛ nubu lɛ mli lɛ efo kpãa ni kɛlɛ ji ninetalɔi ohai abɔ; be mli ni, beni etswa gbee ni mli kwɔ sɛɛ lɛ, efoɔ kɛyaa ŋwɛi ekoŋŋ, ni etsɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ ni miiya ŋwɛi ni ekɛ ehe miibɔle ehe kɛmiiya kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ; no hewɔ lɛ amrɔ nɛɛ, Starbuck na Madame Leviathan fɔmɔ kotoku lɛ mli tsɔne kakadaŋŋ abɔ he, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ gbekɛ fioo lɛ ebɔ e-dam lɛ he lolo. Efɔɔɔ baa akɛ yɛ sɛɛtiumɔ ni yaa nɔ oya lɛ mli lɛ, nɛkɛ adebɔɔ mli line nɛɛ, ni nyɛ naagbee lɛ etse ehe lɛ, kɛ hempen nɔ lɛ feɔ ekome, bɔni afee ni no hewɔ lɛ, gbekɛ lɛ amɔmɔ. Etamɔ nɔ ni ajie ŋshɔi lɛ amli teemɔŋ saji ni yɔɔ mlɛo fe fɛɛ lɛ ekomɛi akpo atsɔɔ wɔ yɛ nɛkɛ nu ŋshɔ ni akɛ ŋmiŋmi fee nɛɛ mli. Wɔna oblahii kɛ oblayei Leviatan amours yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli.*

*Sperm whale lɛ, taakɛ eji yɛ Leviathan loo krokomɛi fɛɛ ahe lɛ, shi etamɔɔɔ loo krokomɛi babaoo lɛ, efɔɔ bii ni esusuuu he yɛ be fɛɛ mli; yɛ fɔmɔ ni ekolɛ abaato he gbɛjianɔ yɛ nyɔji nɛɛhu sɛɛ, ni efɔɔ shi ekomekomei; eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ shihilɛi fioo komɛi ni ale mli lɛ, efɔ Esau kɛ Yakob ko:—nifeemɔ ko ni baa trukaa ni ato he gbɛjianɔ yɛ fɔmɔ kotokui enyɔ mli, ni yɔɔ he ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, eko yɛ fɔmɔ kotoku lɛ fã fɛɛ fã; shi tsitsii lɛ diɛŋtsɛ tsɔɔ ŋwɛi kɛjɛɔ jɛmɛ. Kɛji akɛ nɛkɛ fãi ni jara wa ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni miiye nu lɛ mli lɛ ba trukaa lɛ, gbɔbilɔ lɛ akpɔlɔ lɛ shwieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli nu kɛ elá ni ekɛshwieɔ shi lɛ nɔ lɛ haa ŋshɔ lɛ mli su tsakeɔ kɛtaoɔ tso. Miliki lɛ ŋɔɔ waa ni eyɛ sɛɛnamɔ; gbɔmɔ eka lɛ ekwɛ; ekolɛ ebaatsu nii jogbaŋŋ kɛ stroberi. Kɛji akɛ bulɛ ni amɛyɔɔ kɛha amɛhe lɛ eyi obɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ jieɔ hominum yi babaoo .

Ni nakai ji bɔ ni, eyɛ mli akɛ nɛkɛ bɔɔ nii ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ amɛ shishi ni yɔɔ teŋgbɛ nɛɛ kɛ amɛhe wo toiŋjɔlɛ he saji fɛɛ amli yɛ heyeli kɛ gbeyeishemɔ mli; hɛɛ, amɛmiiye amɛhe fɛo yɛ dalliance kɛ miishɛɛ mli. Shi kɛlɛ, yɛ Atlantik ŋshɔ ni etswa mihe lɛ mli lɛ, ani mi diɛŋtsɛ mikɛ mihe woɔ mli lolo kɛya naanɔ yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli; ni yɛ be mli ni shibɔlemɔ ŋulamii ni susuɔ mɔ he ni haomɔ ni sɛɛ efooo lɛ bɔleɔ mihe lɛ, yɛ jɛmɛ kɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ mli shɔŋŋ lɛ, mikɛ naanɔ miishɛɛ mlijɔlɛ juɔ mihe lolo.

Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, beni wɔkã shi yɛ nɛkɛ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, nibii ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli ni baa trukaa yɛ shɔŋŋ lɛ jieɔ lɛlɛi krokomɛi lɛ anitsumɔ kpo, ni amɛkɛ amɛhe miiwo ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ gbɔi lɛ husu lɛ naa lɛ amli lolo lɛ anitsumɔ; aloo ekolɛ amɛyaa nɔ amɛwuɔ ta lɛ yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ kooyigbɛ, he ni aha amɛ gbɛ babaoo kɛ hei komɛi ni hi jogbaŋŋ ni amɛbaanyɛ amɛku amɛsɛɛ amɛya lɛ. Shi ŋshɔŋlooi ni mli efu ni awo amɛ tsofai fɔji lɛ ni amɛmiiho kɛmiitsɔ shwilafoi amli amrɔ nɛɛ kɛ agbɛnɛ lɛ, jeee nɔ ko kwraa kɛha nɔ ni naagbee lɛ wɔkɛ wɔhiŋmɛii kpe lɛ. Bei komɛi lɛ eji kusum nifeemɔ akɛ, kɛ́ mɔ ko miiya oya kɛmiiya ŋshɔŋloo ko ni yɔɔ hewalɛ ni ehiɛ kã shi fe bɔ ni afɔɔ feemɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, aatao ni atswa lɛ tsitsi, taakɛ atsɔɔ mli lɛ, kɛtsɔ etsitsi wulu lɛ ni etseɔ loo efiteɔ lɛ nɔ. Afeɔ kɛtsɔ tsɔne ni akɛfoɔ nii ni nine kuku yɔɔ mli ni akɛkpɛtɛɔ he, ni akɛ kpãa fĩɔ he koni akɛgbala lɛ kɛku sɛɛ ekoŋŋ lɛ nɔ. Whale ko ni pila (tamɔ bɔ ni wɔkase yɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ) yɛ nɛkɛ fã nɛɛ mli, shi jeee yɛ gbɛ ni mɔɔ shi nɔ, taakɛ etamɔ nɔ ni lɛ, efo kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni ehiɛ harpoon tsɔne lɛ fã kɛtee; ni yɛ pilamɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ eefo kɛmiitsɔ ŋwɛibɔfoi ni miitsi amɛhe lɛ ateŋ tamɔ Arnold ni ji mɔ kome pɛ ni ta okpɔŋɔ nɔ ni enijiaŋ eje wui lɛ, yɛ Saratoga ta lɛ mli, ni ekɛ nijiaŋwujee miiwo he fɛɛ he ni eya.

Shi bɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ pilamɔ ji lɛ, eji nɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa, yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ; shi kɛlɛ gbeyeishemɔ krɛdɛɛ ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛwo tooi akuu ni eshwɛ lɛ mli lɛ, jɛ nɔ ko ni shishijee lɛ gbɛ ni kã amɛteŋ lɛ ha wɔfee duŋ lɛ hewɔ. Shi yɛ naagbee lɛ wɔyɔse akɛ yɛ osharai ni anyɛɛɛ asusu he yɛ ŋshɔŋ loomɔ nitsumɔ lɛ mli lɛ ateŋ ekome mli lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ etsɔ harpoon-line ni ekɛgbala lɛ mli; ejo foi hu kɛ tsɔne ni akɛfoɔ nii lɛ yɛ emli; ni yɛ be mli ni kpãa ni akɛkpɛtɛ nakai tawuu nii lɛ he lɛ naagbee ni bɛ nɔ ko lɛ, emɔ harpoon-line ni bɔle etsitsi lɛ he lɛ mli daa lɛ, tsɔne ni akɛfoɔ nii lɛ diɛŋtsɛ etsu nii ni ejɛ eheloo lɛ mli. No hewɔ lɛ, akɛni apila lɛ kɛyashi etsɔ shwilafo hewɔ lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ eetsɔ nu lɛ mli, ni ekɛ etsitsi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ miitswa yiwalɛ, ni ekɛ tsɔne ni mli wa lɛ miitswa ehe, ni epila lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enanemɛi lɛ ni egbe amɛ.

Etamɔ nɔ ni nɛkɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɛɛ kaiɔ tooi akuu muu lɛ fɛɛ kɛjɛɔ amɛ gbeyeishemɔ ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ lɛ mli. Klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ, ŋshɔŋ loofɔji ni feɔ wɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ bɔi amɛhe shweremɔ fioo, ni amɛgbee amɛhe shi, tamɔ nɔ ni ŋshɔkei ni fã ni efite lɛ ewo amɛ nɔ kɛjɛ shɔŋŋ; no sɛɛ lɛ, ŋshɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ bɔi shwiemɔ fioo ni ebɔi shwiemɔ; ŋshɔ shishi ayemforo-tsũi kɛ gbekɛbii akwɛmɔhei lɛ laaje; yɛ ŋshɔkei ni shwereɔ babaoo lɛ amli lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ teŋgbɛ lɛ bɔi ŋshɔkei lɛ amli shwiemɔ yɛ kui ni mli ewo wuji amli. Hɛɛ, toiŋjɔlɛ kakadaŋŋ lɛ miiho. Etsɛɛɛ ni anu gbee ko ni baa shi ni miiya hiɛ; ni no sɛɛ lɛ tamɔ block-ice babaoo ni feɔ basabasa beni Hudson faa wulu lɛ gbáa yɛ Fɛ̃i be mli lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi akuu muu lɛ fɛɛ ba ni amɛbagbee amɛmligbɛ teŋgbɛ lɛ nɔ, tamɔ nɔ ni amɛmiibua amɛhe naa yɛ gɔŋ kome ni mɛi fɛɛ yɔɔ mli lɛ nɔ. Amrɔ nɔŋŋ ni Starbuck kɛ Queequeg tsake amɛhe; Starbuck ni miiye lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ.

"Lɛlɛi! Lɛlɛi!" ekɛ gbee ni mli wa wie, ni emɔ lɛlɛ kudɔmɔ lɛ mli—“nyɛmɔ nyɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔnei lɛ, ni nyɛmɔ nyɛsusumai amli, agbɛnɛ! Mi-Nyɔŋmɔ, hii, nyɛdaamɔ shi! Nyɛtsia lɛ, nyɛ Queequeg—ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ!—tswaa lɛ!—tswaa lɛ! Te shi—te shi, ni nyɛhi shi nakai! Fɛ̃i, hii—gbala amɛ sɛɛ, hii kɔkɔɔkɔ!—ka minspe kɔkɔɔkɔ!”

Agbɛnɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fɛɛ ebɔle ŋshɔkei diji wuji enyɔ ateŋ, ni eshi Dardanelles bibioo ko yɛ amɛhelɛtemɔ kakadaŋŋ lɛ teŋ. Shi kɛtsɔ mɔdɛŋbɔɔ ni mli wa nɔ lɛ, naagbee lɛ wɔtswa tu kɛbote be kukuoo mli gbɛ ko mli; no sɛɛ lɛ, eŋmɛɔ gbɛ oya, ni yɛ nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ lɛ, ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ kwɛɔ gbɛ kroko ni etsɔɔ nɔ ejɛɔ mli. Yɛ jofoi babaoo ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni tamɔ yitsɔi lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, naagbee lɛ wɔfo oya kɛbote nɔ ni ji kponɔgbɛ kooyii lɛ ateŋ ekome pɛŋ lɛ mli, shi amrɔ nɛɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko lɛ efo wɔhe, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛmiifee basabasa kɛmiiya he kome. Nɛkɛ yiwalaheremɔ ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ nɛɛ he jara waaa kɛtsɔ Queequeg tsitsi ni elaaje lɛ nɔ, ni beni edamɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli koni ekɛtswa ŋshɔŋlooi ni joɔ foi lɛ, kɔɔyɔɔ-eddy ni ŋshɔŋlooi ni mli kwɔlɔ enyɔ ni bɛŋkɛ lɛ ni shwieɔ shi trukaa lɛ fee lɛ jie etsitsi lɛ kɛjɛ eyitso nɔ.

Bɔ ni jeŋ muu fɛɛ basabasafeemɔ lɛ ji basabasafeemɔ kɛ basabasafeemɔ amrɔ nɛɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni etsɔ nɔ ni tamɔ nɔ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ; ejaakɛ naagbee lɛ amɛbua amɛhe naa yɛ gbɔmɔtso kome ni mli eyi obɔ lɛ mli lɛ, no sɛɛ lɛ amɛfee amɛ foidamɔ ni yaa hiɛ lɛ ehee kɛ oyaiyeli ni yaa hiɛ. Sɛɛnamɔ ko bɛ sɛɛtiumɔ kroko he; shi lɛlɛi lɛ tee nɔ amɛhi amɛsɛɛ lolo koni amɛyajie ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛ tsofai fɔji ewo mli lɛ ni abaanyɛ ashɛrɛ ashwie ŋshɔ lɛ sɛɛ, ni nakai nɔŋŋ hu koni amɛkwɛ eko ni Flask gbe ni ekɛwo mli lɛ nɔ. Waif lɛ ji tso ni akɛ pennon ewo mli, ni lɛlɛ fɛɛ lɛlɛ woɔ enyɔ loo etɛ; ni kɛ́ shwɛmɔ kroko fata he lɛ, akɛwoɔ ŋshɔŋloo ni egbo lɛ gbɔmɔtso ni hoɔ lɛ mli tɛ̃ɛ, koni akɛkadi he ni eyɔɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu akɛ okadi ni tsɔɔ akɛ eyɛ dani eba, kɛji akɛ lɛlɛ kroko lɛlɛi lɛ bɛŋkɛ.

Nɔ ni jɛ nɛkɛ shigbeemɔ nɛɛ mli ba lɛ ji nakai wiemɔ ni yɔɔ ŋaa ni yɔɔ Fishery lɛ mli lɛ he mfoniri yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, —bɔ ni ŋshɔŋlooi babaoo yɔɔ lɛ, nakai loo lɛ faaa. Yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛ tsofai fɔji wo amɛmli lɛ fɛɛ ateŋ lɛ, mɔ kome pɛ ji nɔ ni amɔmɔ. Mɛi ni eshwɛ lɛ to gbɛjianɔ koni amɛjo foi kɛha be lɛ, shi koni akɛ lɛlɛ kroko fe Pequod lɛ aŋɔ amɛ pɛ, taakɛ abaana yɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ.





YITSƆƆ 88. Skul kɛ Skul Nɔkwɛlɔi.

Yitso ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ ha Sperm Whales akuu ko loo kuu ko ni da waa he amaniɛbɔɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ, aha nɔ ni ekolɛ no ji nɔ ni ha nakai ŋmeii babaoo lɛ ba lɛ hu.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ bei komɛi lɛ akɛ gbɔmɔtsei wuji ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kpeɔ moŋ, shi, taakɛ esa akɛ ana lɛ, yɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ po lɛ, anaa kui bibii ni etse amɛhe lɛ yɛ bei komɛi amli, ni amɛteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ fuaa aŋkroaŋkroi kɛjɛ nyɔŋmai enyɔ kɛyashi nyɔŋmai enumɔ. Ale lalai akui ni tamɔ nɛkɛ akɛ skul. Bei pii lɛ, amɛji henɔi enyɔ; mɛi ni nɔ ni miihe ashɛ yei pɛ ji mɛi ni yɔɔ mli lɛ, kɛ mɛi ni ebuaaa amɛhe naa fe hii bibii ni yɔɔ hewalɛ, loo tsinahii, taakɛ ale amɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ.

Yɛ okpɔŋɔ nɔtaralɔi ni yaa yei askul lɛ nɔ lɛ mli lɛ, be fɛɛ be lɛ, onaa nuu ko ni eda kwraa, shi jeee mɔ ni eda; ni, yɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa fɛɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa nɔ lɛ, ejieɔ ekãa kpo kɛtsɔ sɛɛ ni egbeeɔ ni ehaa eyei lɛ afoijee lɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ. Yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, nuu nɛɛ ji Ottomannyo ko ni yɔɔ shika babaoo, ni ekɛ ehe miiwo nu mli je lɛ nɔ, ni harem lɛ mli miishɛjemɔi kɛ suɔmɔi fɛɛ fata ehe. Srɔtofeemɔ ni yɔɔ Ottoman nɛɛ kɛ ehefatalɔi lɛ ateŋ lɛ sa kadimɔ waa; ejaakɛ, yɛ be mli ni eji leviathanic fãi ni da fe fɛɛ lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome be fɛɛ be lɛ, yei lɛ, kɛji amɛdara kwraa po lɛ, amɛfeee nuu ni dalɛ ji mlɛbo lɛ mlijaa etɛ mli ekome. Amɛhe yɛ sɛɛnamɔ waa kɛ akɛto he, lɛɛlɛŋ; Mikɛɔ akɛ, akafee nɔ ni fe yards nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ fã ni bɔleɔ tsitsi lɛ he. Shi kɛlɛ, anyɛɛɛ akpoo, akɛ yɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, amɛyɛ hegbɛ akɛ amɛnáa en bon point .

Eji nɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli waa akɛ oookwɛ nɛkɛ harem nɛɛ kɛ enuŋtsɔ lɛ yɛ amɛ shwɛmɔi ni bɛ sɛɛnamɔ mli lɛ amli. Taakɛ atadei ni yɔɔ fashion lɛ ji lɛ, amɛyaa nɔ amɛyaa nɔ amɛtaoɔ nibii srɔtoi yɛ hejɔlɛ mli be fɛɛ be. Okɛ amɛ kpeɔ yɛ Line lɛ nɔ yɛ be ni sa mli kɛha Equatorial niyeli be lɛ mli fɔfɔi ni eyi obɔ, ni oku osɛɛ kɛba, ekolɛ, kɛjɛ hulutsoo be ni okɛyeɔ yɛ Kooyigbɛ ŋshɔi lɛ amli lɛ mli, ni no hewɔ lɛ hulutsoo be ni shishiuɔ mɔ ni tɔlɛ kɛ hulutsoo ni esaaa fɛɛ yɔɔ mli lɛ.
