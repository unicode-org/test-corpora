YITSƆƆ 48. Klɛŋklɛŋ Shigbeemɔ.

Ejaakɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ŋwɛibɔfoi lɛ miiho yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fa kroko lɛ nɔ, ni, kɛ oyaiyeli ni bɛ gbee lɛ, amɛmiijie lɛlɛ ni shwieɔ shi yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ mli nibii kɛ ehe tsɔnei lɛ. Abuɔ lɛlɛ nɛɛ akɛ eji lɛlɛi ni eshwɛ lɛ ateŋ ekome be fɛɛ be, eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ ŋaa gbɛ nɔ lɛ atsɛɔ lɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ nɔ, yɛ bɔ ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ hewɔ. Mfoniri ni damɔ ekpɔlɔi lɛ ahe amrɔ nɛɛ kɛlɛ ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni ŋmei yɛŋ kome je kpo yɛ enaabu ni tamɔ dade lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ fɔŋ nɔ. Chinese jakɛti ko ni akɛ cotton diŋ fee ni ekpɔtɔ lɛ wo lɛ gbonyo bu mli, ni ekɛ trosai diji ni mli kwɔ ni akɛ nakai nibii ni yɔɔ diŋ lɛ fee lɛ wo mli. Shi nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni fe nɛkɛ ebonness nɛɛ ji yitsɔi yɛŋ ni akpɛ́ ni kpɛɔ, ni akpɛ yitsɔi ni hiɛ ka lɛ ni abɔle ehe kɛbɔle eyitso he. Ni mɛi ni fata nɛkɛ mfoniri nɛɛ he lɛ ji mɛi ni he yɔɔ fɛo, ni ji jata-yellow ni ji Manillas maŋbii lɛ ateŋ mɛi komɛi ahewolonɔ su;—hewolonɔ su ko ni ehe gbɛi waa yɛ ŋaa gbɛ ko nɔ, ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi blɔfomɛi anɔkwafoi komɛi susuɔ akɛ amɛji shikpálɔi ni awoɔ amɛ nyɔmɔ kɛ teemɔŋ saji ahe nitsulɔi lɛ. counting-room ni amɛsusuɔ akɛ eyɛ he kroko lɛ.

Beni lɛlɛ mli kuu ni miifee naakpɛɛ lɛ miikwɛ gbɔi nɛɛ lolo lɛ, Ahab bolɔ etsɛ nuumo ni wo tsitsi yɛŋ ni yɔɔ amɛyitso lɛ akɛ, “Nyɛfee klalo yɛ jɛmɛ, Fedallah?”

Hetoo ni akɛ fã ko ha lɛ ji akɛ: “Mifee klalo.”

“No hewɔ lɛ, shi shi; onuɔ?” ni amɛmiibolɔ kɛmiitsɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ. “Okɛ ohe awo shi yɛ jɛmɛ, mikɛɛ.”

Egbee lɛ naa wa waa, ni yɛ amɛnaakpɛɛ ni amɛfee lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, hii lɛ jo foi kɛtsɔ oketeke gbɛ lɛ nɔ; ŋmeii lɛ shwieɔ amɛhe yɛ blɔkii lɛ amli; kɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ, lɛlɛi etɛ lɛ gbee ŋshɔ lɛ mli; yɛ be mli ni, kɛ ekãa ni yɔɔ ŋaa, ni bɛ nine, ni aleee yɛ nitsumɔ kroko mli lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ, ni tamɔ tooi lɛ, tsɔ lɛlɛ ni miihoso lɛ sɛɛ kɛtee lɛlɛi ni ashɛrɛ ashwie shi yɛ shishi lɛ amli.

Etsɛɛɛ tsɔ ni amɛje kpo kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ shishi, ni ŋshɔkei ni ji ejwɛ, ni jɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ, gbala kɛbɔle lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ, ni etsɔɔ gbɔi enumɔ lɛ ni miikudɔ Ahab, ni amɛdamɔ shi shiŋŋ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ, ni amɛjie Starbuck, Stubb, kɛ Flask yi waa, koni amɛgbɛ amɛshwã, koni amɛgbɛ amɛhe amɛshwã. Shi beni amɛhiŋmɛii fɛɛ kwɛɔ Fedallah ni ji swart lɛ kɛ egbɛfalɔi lɛ anɔ ekoŋŋ lɛ, gboklɛfoi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛi krokomɛi lɛ amli lɛ booo famɔ lɛ toi.

Starbuck kɛɛ akɛ: “Kapten Ahab?—”

Ahab bolɔ akɛ: “Nyɛgbɛa nyɛhe; "Nyɛhaa gbɛ, lɛlɛi ejwɛ lɛ fɛɛ. Bo, Flask, gbala babaoo kɛya kɔɔyɔɔŋ!"

“Aye, aye, onukpa,” King-Post bibioo lɛ kɛ miishɛɛ bolɔ, ni ekɛ etsɔne kudɔmɔ tsɔne wulu lɛ miibɔle ehe. “Kã ohe!” ni ekɛ e-nitsulɔi lɛ miiwie lɛ. “Jɛmɛ!—jɛmɛ!—jɛmɛ ekoŋŋ! Jɛmɛ etswaa kɔɔyɔɔ yɛ hiɛ tuutu, gbekɛbii hii!—nyɛkɛ nyɛhe awo sɛɛ!”

“Kaabo gbekɛbii hii ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ atoi kɔkɔɔkɔ, Archy.”

Archy kɛɛ akɛ: “Oo, misusuuu amɛhe, onukpa; "Mile fɛɛ dani amrɔ nɛɛ. Ani minuuu amɛ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli? Ni mikɛɛɛ Cabaco yɛ biɛ? Mɛni nyɛkɛɔ, Cabaco? Amɛji stowaways, Mr. Flask."

“Gbalamɔ, gbalamɔ, mitsui kpakpai-ni hiɛ ka; gbalamɔ, mibii; gbalamɔ, mibii bibii,” Stubb kɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli ŋmɔ etsɔɔ etsulɔi lɛ, ni amɛteŋ mɛi komɛi jieɔ henumɔi ni mli efu lolo lɛ kpo. "Mɛni hewɔ nyɛkuuu nyɛsɛɛ, mibihii? Mɛni ji nɔ ni nyɛkwɛɔ? Nakai chaps ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ? Tut! Amɛji niji enumɔ krokomɛi pɛ ni ba koni amɛbaye amɛbua wɔ—ekɔɔɔ he eko he ni amɛjɛ—bɔ ni miishɛɛ babaoo yɔɔ lɛ. Gbalamɔ, no hewɔ lɛ, feemɔ; gbalamɔ; kaasusu sulfa lɛ he kɔkɔɔkɔ—abonsamɛi ji nanemɛi kpakpai ni bɛ jɛmɛ; No hewɔ lɛ nyɛ. pounds akpe; no sɛɛ lɛ:—blɛoo, no ji nɔ ni! Ha gbɛ yɛ jɛmɛ, nyɛ ragamuffin rapscallions; ginger-cakes nyɛgbalaaa?—gbalamɔ ni nyɛgbalaa nɔ ko, ni nyɛjea nyɛhiŋmɛii amli yɛ Biɛ! ni ekɛ tsɔne ni mli wa lɛ miitswa kɛjɛ etsitsi mli; "nyɛ bi fɛɛ nyɛ binuu jieɔ e-knife, ni ekɛ ŋmei lɛ gbalaa yɛ enyanyɔji ateŋ. No ji—no ji. Agbɛnɛ nyɛfea nɔ ko; ni tamɔ nakai, mi dade-fãi. Nyɛjea lɛ shishi—jemɔ lɛ shishi, mi shika-spunii! Nyɛjea lɛ shishi, marling-spikes!"

Akɛ Stubb exordium kɛha e-crew lɛ ha yɛ biɛ yɛ gbɛ ni da nɔ, ejaakɛ eyɛ gbɛ krɛdɛɛ ko ni etsɔɔ nɔ ekɛ amɛ wieɔ yɛ gbɛ ni da nɔ, ni titri lɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ jamɔ ni ekɛwoɔ amɛmli lɛ mli. Shi esaaa akɛ osusuɔ kɛjɛɔ eshiɛmɔi lɛ amli nɔkwɛmɔnɔ nɛɛ mli akɛ ekɛ esafo lɛ bɔi akɔnɔi ni mli wa pɛŋ. Jee nakai kraa; ni nɔ titri ni yɔɔ mli ji nɔ ni yɔɔ srɔto. Ebaakɛɛ egbɛfalɔi lɛ nibii ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ, yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ ni hiɛtserɛjiemɔ kɛ mlifu fata he, ni mlifu lɛ tamɔ nɔ ni abu he akɔntaa akɛ tsofa-kɛ-ŋma kɛkɛ kɛha hiɛtserɛjiemɔ lɛ, ni ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko bɛ ni nyɛŋ anu nɛkɛ tsɛmɔi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni egbalaaa kɛha wala ni jara wa, ni kɛlɛ egbalaa kɛha nɔ lɛ he shwɛmɔ kɛkɛ. Kɛfata he lɛ, be fɛɛ be lɛ, lɛ diɛŋtsɛ etamɔ mɔ ko ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni ebɛ nitsumɔ, ni ekɛ etsɔne kudɔmɔ tsɔne lɛ tsuɔ nii jogbaŋŋ, ni ekɛ ehiɛ tsɔɔ ŋwɛi—ekɛ enaabu gbeleɔ yɛ bei komɛi amli—ni haa mɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni ŋmɔɔ lɛ ni enaa kɛkɛ, kɛtsɔ srɔtofeemɔ ni yɔɔ mli lɛ nɔ lɛ, efeɔ tamɔ nɔ ko ni haa lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ feɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ. Kɛkɛ ni ekoŋŋ lɛ, Stubb ji nakai shwɛlɔi ni yɔɔ srɔto lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome, ni bei komɛi lɛ amɛmiishɛɛ lɛ haaa ale nɔ ni yaa nɔ, ni ehaa mɛi ni baa shi lɛ fɛɛ buɔ amɛhe yɛ amɛtoiboo he sane lɛ mli.

Yɛ okadi ko ni jɛ Ahab ŋɔɔ lɛ toiboo mli lɛ, agbɛnɛ Starbuck miigbala Stubb gãimlibii lɛ yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ; ni beni miniti kome loo nɔ ni fe nakai lɛ, lɛlɛi enyɔ lɛ bɛŋkɛ amɛhe jogbaŋŋ lɛ, Stubb jie hefatalɔ lɛ yi.

"Owula Starbuck! lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ, ahoy! wiemɔ kome kɛ nyɛ, onukpa, kɛji nyɛsumɔɔ!"

“Halo!” Starbuck ku esɛɛ eba, ni etsɔ ehe ni ekɛ ehe wooo mli inchi kome po beni ewieɔ lɛ; ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ lolo shi ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ miiwo e-nitsulɔi lɛ hewalɛ; ehiɛ fee tamɔ tɛ ni jɛ Stubb nɔ lɛ mli.

“Mɛni nyɛsusuɔ yɛ nakai gbekɛbii hii ni ji ŋmɔŋ lɛ ahe, onukpa!”

“Akɛwo lɛlɛ lɛ mli, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, dani lɛlɛ lɛ fã gbɛ. (Hewalɛ, hewalɛ, gbekɛbii hii!)” yɛ gbee bibioo mli etsɔɔ etsulɔi lɛ, kɛkɛ ni ewie waa ekoŋŋ akɛ: “Nitsumɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho, Mr. Stubb! (seethe her, siethe her, my lads!) shi ekɔɔɔ he eko, Mr. Lett. mi hii, fɛ̃i be!) Hogsheads ni ji sperm yɛ hiɛ, Mr. Stubb, ni no ji nɔ ni nyɛba kɛha.

"Aye, aye, misusu nakai nɔŋŋ," Stubb wie ekomekomei, beni lɛlɛi lɛ fã amɛhe lɛ, "beni mikɛ mihiɛ fɔ̃ amɛnɔ pɛ kɛkɛ ni misusu nakai. Aye, ni no ji nɔ ni etee sɛɛ hold lɛ mli, shii abɔ, taakɛ Dough-Boy susu be kakadaŋŋ lɛ. Akɛ amɛ tee jɛmɛ. The Well at the Whale,' ye abua bo!

Amrɔ nɛɛ gbɔi ni jɛ maŋsɛɛ nɛɛ aba yɛ be ni he hiaa waa mli tamɔ lɛlɛi lɛ ni ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ lɛlɛ lɛ shishi lɛ, enɛ eteee apasa hemɔkɛyeli naakpɛɛ nii ko shi yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli kuu lɛ mli bii lɛ ateŋ mɛi komɛi amli yɛ gbɛ ni jwɛŋmɔ bɛ mli nɔ; shi Archy nibii ni ena ni esusu he akɛ eji be ko ni eho lɛ ha amɛtee maŋsɛɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ ajieee yijiemɔ kpo yɛ nakai beaŋ moŋ, shi enɛ esaa amɛ kɛha nifeemɔ lɛ yɛ gbɛ bibioo ko nɔ. Ejie amɛnaakpɛɛ nii lɛ naagbee ni fe fɛɛ lɛ; ni no hewɔ lɛ, nɔ ni kɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ kɛ Stubb hekɛnɔfɔɔ gbɛ ni etsɔɔ nɔ ebuɔ amɛhefɛo he akɔntaa lɛ, amɛye amɛhe kɛjɛ apasa hemɔkɛyelii ahe susumɔi amli kɛha be lɛ; eyɛ mli akɛ sane lɛ shi gbɛ babaoo lolo kɛha susumɔi srɔtoi ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni kɔɔ Ahab hegbɛ pɔtɛɛ ni yɔɔ duŋ mli yɛ sane lɛ mli kɛjɛ shishijee lɛ he moŋ. Yɛ migbɛfaŋ lɛ, mikaiɔ naakpɛɛ ŋmɔlɔi ni mina ni miiho yɛ Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ mli yɛ Nantucket leebi maŋkpa lɛ mli lɛ dioo, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu, Elia ni anyɛɛɛ abu he akɔntaa lɛ he susumɔi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ.

Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, Ahab, ni enuuu esraafoi lɛ ahe lɛ, etsɔ he ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ kɛtee kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ he lɛ, no mli lɛ enyiɛɔ lɛlɛi krokomɛi lɛ ahiɛ lolo; shihilɛ ko ni tsɔɔ bɔ ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi akuu ko miigbala lɛ kɛ hewalɛ waa ha. Nakai tiger yellow bɔɔ nii ni eyɔɔ lɛ tamɔ dade kɛ whale wu fɛɛ; tamɔ trip-hammers enumɔ amɛteɔ shi ni amɛgbeeɔ shi kɛ hewalɛ ni amɛtswaa daa, ni be kɛ bei amli lɛ, lɛlɛ lɛ jeɔ shishi yɛ nu lɛ he tamɔ tsɔne ni tswaa kɛjɛɔ Mississippi lɛlɛ ko mli ni etswaa lɛ. Yɛ Fedallah ni ana lɛ ni ekɛ harpooneer lɛlɛ lɛ miigba lɛ lɛ, ekɛ ejakɛti diŋ lɛ efɔ shi, ni ejie etsitsi ni bɛ atade lɛ kpo ni egbɔmɔtso lɛ fã muu lɛ fɛɛ kã gunwale lɛ nɔ, ni efo mli faŋŋ yɛ nu ni tsakeɔ ehe lɛ he; yɛ be mli ni Ahab yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ naagbee gbɛ kroko lɛ nɔ lɛ, ni ekɛ enine kome, tamɔ mɔ ko ni tswaa ŋtayaa lɛ nɔ lɛ, shwieɔ fã kɛyaa sɛɛ kɛyaa kɔɔyɔɔ mli, tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛbaatsu su fɛɛ su ni haa mɔ shwieɔ shi lɛ he nii; Ana Ahab ni ekɛ etsɔne kudɔmɔ tsɔne lɛ miitsu nii fiofio tamɔ yɛ lɛlɛ akpe ni aba shi dani White Whale lɛ gbá lɛ lɛ mli. Shikome pɛ nine ni akpã mli lɛ fee nɔ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto ni no sɛɛ lɛ ehi shi shiŋŋ, yɛ be mli ni ana lɛlɛ lɛ mli tsɔnei enumɔ lɛ ni amɛshɛ amɛhe yɛ be kome mli. Lɛlɛ lɛ kɛ emli nitsulɔi lɛ tara ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ ni amɛtsiii amɛhe. Amrɔ nɔŋŋ ni lɛlɛi etɛ ni gbɛ eshwa yɛ sɛɛ lɛ kpa amɛ gbɛfaa lɛ. Ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ehi shi yɛ bluu lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ, ni no hewɔ lɛ amɛhaaa okadi ko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni tsɔɔ akɛ amɛmiiya, eyɛ mli akɛ Ahab jɛ he ni ebɛŋkɛ lɛ lɛ ena enɛ moŋ.

“Mɔ fɛɛ mɔ kwɛ etsɔne lɛ mli!” Starbuck bolɔ. “Bo, Queequeg, te shi!”

Beni ekɛ oyaiyeli shwieɔ shi yɛ adeka ni yɔɔ koŋ etɛ ni awo nɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ nɔ lɛ, kooloo lɛ damɔ jɛmɛ, ni ekɛ hiŋmɛii ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ waa kwɛ he ni atsɔɔ sɛɛtiumɔ lɛ mli naagbee kwraa lɛ. Nakai nɔŋŋ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛgbɛ he ni ekɛ tu lɛ hu ebɔ he yɛ koŋ etɛ mli lɛ, ana Starbuck diɛŋtsɛ ni ekɛ ehe miiwo e-chip ni ekɛtswaa lɛ mli, ni ekwɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ hiŋmɛi bluu wulu lɛ dioo.

Flask lɛlɛ ni bɛ shɔŋŋ tsɔ lɛ hu kã shi ni ebɛ mumɔ; e-komanda lɛ damɔ tso lɛ yiteŋ yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ, ni ji tso ko ni wa ni shishifãi ewo lɛlɛ lɛ shishi, ni ekwɔ aaafee ninetalɔi enyɔ kɛteke lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ kpoku lɛ nɔ. Akɛtsuɔ nii kɛmɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ. Eyiteŋ lɛ mli elɛɛɛ fe gbɔmɔ nineshi nine, ni beni Flask damɔ shishitoo ni tamɔ nɛkɛ nɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni eta lɛlɛ ko ni gbee shi kɛtee mɛi fɛɛ anɔ ja etsɔne wului lɛ anɔ lɛ yitso nɔ. Shi Maŋtsɛ-Post bibioo lɛ ji bibioo ni eyɛ kuku, ni yɛ nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ lɛ, Maŋtsɛ-Post bibioo lɛ eyi kɛ hiɛdɔɔ agbo ni kwɔ, no hewɔ lɛ nɛkɛ eshidaamɔ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ nɛɛ haaa Maŋtsɛ-Post tsui anyɔ emli kwraa.

“Minaaa ŋshɔi etɛ ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ; ha wɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔne ko yɛ jɛmɛ, ni oha maya jɛmɛ.”

Yɛ enɛ nɔ lɛ, Daggoo, ni ekɛ enine enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ kã tu lɛ nɔ koni ekɛha egbɛ lɛ ahi shi lɛ, tsɔ oya kɛtee sɛɛ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekɛ ehe wo mli ni ekɛ etsitsii ni nɔ kwɔlɔ lɛ ha akɛ shishitoo nɔ.

"Mast-yitso ni hi tamɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ, onukpa. Ani obaatara nɔ?"

“Miisumɔ, ni miida bo shi waa, minaanyo kpakpa, shi miisumɔ ni okɛ ohe awo mli ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai enumɔ pɛ.”

No sɛɛ lɛ, beni ekɛ enaji fɔ̃ɔ lɛlɛ lɛ tsei enyɔ ni kɛ amɛhe kpãa gbee lɛ anɔ lɛ, negro wulu lɛ, ni ekoto fioo lɛ, kɛ enine ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ ha Flask naji, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekɛ Flask nine lɛ fɔ eyitso ni tamɔ gbonyo tsɔne lɛ nɔ ni ekɛɛ lɛ akɛ efo tamɔ bɔ ni esa akɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ efo lɛ, ni ekɛ etsitsi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ shwieɔ shi. Ni Flask damɔ shi bianɛ, Daggoo kɛ enine kome ni ewó nɔ lɛ miiha lɛ tsitsi nɔ tsɔne ni ekɛbaafɔ̃ ehe nɔ ni ekɛma shi shiŋŋ yɛ nɔ.

Gbee lɛ bɛŋkɛ kpaakpa; afua ni mli edɔ lɛ mli etse fioo kɛtsɔ nɔ ko wulu ko ni bɛ faŋŋ nɔ. Yɛ gbeyeishemɔ hewɔ lɛ, wɔ fɛɛ wɔjo foi kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ mli beni naagbee lɛ lɛlɛ lɛ baje kpo, ni eba shi yɛ wɔ nɔ tɛ̃ɛ yɛ he ko ni kɛ lɛ teŋ jɛkɛmɔ shɛɛɛ elɛɛmɔ lɛ.

Beni wɔmiiho yɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ anɔ lɛ wɔna lɛlɛ ni ashi lɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni be kukuoo ko mli lɛ, efo ni efo yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ shishigbɛ tamɔ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkei ashishi; ni no sɛɛ lɛ, lɛlɛ wulu lɛ tsɔ ehe, ni anaaa lɛ dɔŋŋ kɛyashi eba ŋwɛi ni ekɛ ehe miitswa sɛɛgbɛ. Wɔfo nu lɛ mli ekoŋŋ kɛtao lɛ, ni ŋshɔi lɛ shwie wɔ nɔ, ni naagbee lɛ, ajie wɔ ni wɔyashɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli shweshweeshwe. Beni ahum lɛ bɛŋkɛɔ lɛ, no mli lɛ lɛlɛi krokomɛi lɛ efo amɛloo lɛ mli ni amɛku amɛsɛɛ amɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ mli yɛ be ni sa mli. Lɛlɛ lɛ eŋmɛɛ wɔhe, shi eefã gbɛ lolo, kɛji ekolɛ ebaashɛ okadi ko ni tsɔɔ akɛ wɔmiigbo lɛ nɔ, —lɛlɛ lɛ mli tsɔne loo tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ lɛlɛ mli.





YITSƆƆ 49. Hyena lɛ.

Be kɛ bei komɛi yɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni efutu ni wɔtsɛɔ lɛ wala nɛɛ mli beni gbɔmɔ ko kɛ jeŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ tsuɔ nii kɛhaa shwɛmɔ kpele ko ni anyɛɔ atsuɔ he nii, eyɛ mli akɛ eyɔseɔ emli ŋaa lɛ jogbaŋŋ moŋ, ni esusuɔ akɛ shwɛmɔ lɛ jeee mɔ ko mɔ ko shika shi moŋ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enɔ. Shi kɛlɛ, nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ni nijiaŋ eje wui, ni etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ko bɛ ni he hiaa yɛ be mli ni ajeɔ ŋwane lɛ. Ekɛ nibii fɛɛ ni tee nɔ lɛ, hemɔkɛyelii fɛɛ, kɛ hemɔkɛyelii, kɛ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔi, nibii ni wa fɛɛ ni anaa kɛ nɔ ni anaaa lɛ shwieɔ shi, ekɔɔɔ bɔ ni knobby yɔɔ ha lɛ he kɔkɔɔkɔ; taakɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ hewalɛ ni shwieɔ nii amli lɛ yeɔ tu kɛ tu tɛi ni akɛshwieɔ shi lɛ. Ni yɛ naagbai bibii kɛ haomɔi ahe lɛ, trukaa oshara he gbɛkpamɔi, wala kɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ he oshara; enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ, kɛ gbele diɛŋtsɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni eji ŋaa, su kpakpa ni akɛtswaa, kɛ miishɛɛ ŋmɔlɔi ni tswaa yɛ sɛɛgbɛ ni shwɛlɔ onukpa ni anaaa lɛ ni abuuu he akɔntaa lɛ kɛhaa lɛ pɛ kɛha lɛ. Nakai jwɛŋmɔ ni ejaaa ni miwieɔ he lɛ, baa gbɔmɔ nɔ yɛ amanehulu ni naa wa waa be ko mli pɛ; ebaa yɛ ehiɛdɔɔ lɛ teŋgbɛ diɛŋtsɛ, bɔni afee ni nɔ ni tsutsu ko lɛ ekolɛ ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni he hiaa fe fɛɛ kɛha lɛ lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ etamɔ shwɛmɔ ni afɔɔ feemɔ lɛ fã ko kɛkɛ. Nɔ ko bɛ ni tamɔ osharai ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ mli lɛ ni baaha nɛkɛ jeŋ nilee ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli nɛɛ aba; ni no ji nɔ ni mikɛsusuɔ Pequod lɛ gbɛfaa nɛɛ fɛɛ he agbɛnɛ, kɛ White Whale kpeteŋkpele lɛ akɛ nɔ ni ekɛtsuɔ nii.

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Queequeg,” beni amɛgbala mi, naagbee nuu lɛ kɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ lɛ, ni mikɛ mihe miikpokpo lolo yɛ mijakɛti lɛ mli koni mikɛshwie nu lɛ nɔ; “Queequeg, minaanyo kpakpa, ani nɛkɛ nibii nɛɛ fɔɔ baa?” Eyɛ mli akɛ ebɛ henumɔ babaoo, eyɛ mli akɛ etsɔ mli tamɔ mi pɛpɛɛpɛ moŋ, shi eha minu shishi akɛ bei pii lɛ nibii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ baa mli.

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ, “Owula Stubb,” ni mitsɔ mihe miha nakai mɔ ni sa lɛ, ni ekɛ e-oil-jakɛt lɛ ebɔ he lɛ, ni amrɔ nɛɛ ekɛ toiŋjɔlɛ miishɛre epaipi lɛ yɛ nugbɔ lɛ mli lɛ; "Owula Stubb, misusuɔ akɛ minu ni okɛɛ akɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi fɛɛ ni okɛkpe pɛŋ lɛ ateŋ lɛ, wɔhefatalɔ nukpa, Mr. Starbuck, ji mɔ ni kwɛɔ ehe nɔ jogbaŋŋ ni ehiɛ kã shi fe fɛɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, misusuɔ akɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ni miiho lɛ nɔ ni oooya ni okɛ o-lɛlɛ lɛ awo afua mli lɛ ji ŋshɔŋloo ko hiɛshikamɔ ni fe fɛɛ?"

"Eji anɔkwale. Miba shi kɛha ŋshɔŋlooi kɛjɛ lɛlɛ ko ni miiho yɛ ahum ni naa wa ko ni yɔɔ Cape Horn lɛ mli."

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Owula Flask,” ni mitsɔ mihe mikwɛ King-Post bibioo ni damɔ jɛmɛ lɛ; "oyɛ niiashikpamɔ yɛ nibii nɛɛ amli, ni mi hu mibɛ niiashikpamɔ. Ani obaakɛɛ mi kɛji eji mla ni anyɛɛɛ atsake yɛ wuoyeli nɛɛ mli, Mr. Flask, akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko baafo lɛ diɛŋtsɛ esɛɛ ni egbala ehe kɛba sɛɛ-hiɛ kɛya gbele tsitsii amli?"

“Onyɛŋ otsɔ nakai bibioo lɛ nɔ?” Flask kɛɛ. "Hɛɛ, nakai ji mla lɛ. Esa akɛ masumɔ ni mana lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi ni miiku nu lɛ sɛɛ kɛmiiya ŋshɔŋloo ko hiɛ fe fɛɛ. Ha, ha! ŋshɔŋloo lɛ baaha amɛ ŋmɔlɔ kɛha ŋmɔlɔ, jwɛŋmɔ nakai!"

No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ biɛ lɛ, kɛjɛ odasefoi etɛ ni kwɛɛɛ hiɛaŋ lɛ adɛŋ lɛ, miná sane muu lɛ fɛɛ he sane ko ni mijɛ yiŋtoo mli miwie. No hewɔ lɛ, kɛji akɛ asusu akɛ ahum ni tswaa kɛ ŋshɔkei ni shwieɔ shi yɛ nu lɛ mli kɛ ŋshɔkei ni jɛɔ mli baa lɛ ji saji ni fɔɔ baa yɛ shihilɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ; kɛ misusu akɛ yɛ be ni he hiaa fe fɛɛ ni mikɛyaa ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he lɛ mli lɛ, esa akɛ mikɛ miwala awo mɔ ni kudɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ dɛŋ lɛ mli—bei pii lɛ, eji mɔ ko ni yɛ nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ lɛ, eyɛ oyaiyeli mli ni ekɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-stamps ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa lɛ baatswa lɛlɛ lɛ; kɛji akɛ wɔsusu akɛ oshara pɔtɛɛ ni ba wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ ji nɔ titri ni esa akɛ akɛwo Starbuck tsɔne ni ekɛtee e-whale lɛ nɔ ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaashɛ ŋshɔkei ni tswaa lɛ mli lɛ he, ni wɔsusu akɛ Starbuck, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, ehe gbɛi waa yɛ ehiɛdɔɔ kpele ni eyɔɔ yɛ ŋshɔŋ loomɔ mli lɛ hewɔ; kɛ misusu akɛ mifata Starbuck lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ hiɛshikamɔ ni bɛ tɛ̃ɛ nɛɛ he lɛ; ni naagbee lɛ, beni misusuɔ nɔ ni abonsam sɛɛtiumɔ ji ni mikɛ mihe wo mli lɛ he, ni mita White Whale lɛ he lɛ: kɛ mikɛ nibii fɛɛ to he lɛ, mikɛɛ, misusu akɛ ekolɛ ebaahi jogbaŋŋ akɛ maya shishi ni mafee misuɔmɔnaa nii lɛ he wolo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ. Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Queequeg, ba, obaafee mi mlalelɔ, mihe nitsulɔ, kɛ mi gboshinii.”

Ekolɛ ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ akɛ yɛ gbɔmɛi fɛɛ ateŋ lɛ, esa akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi afee amɛnaagbee woji kɛ kpaŋmɔi lɛ ahe mfoniri, shi gbɔmɛi komɛi bɛ je lɛŋ ni sumɔɔ nakai sɛɛkuu lɛ fe amɛ. Enɛ ji shi ni ji ejwɛ yɛ mishihilɛ mli yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfaa mli ni mifee nakai nɔŋŋ. Beni agbe nifeemɔ lɛ naa yɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ lɛ, minu he akɛ mihe efee mlɛo kwraa; ajie tɛ ko kɛjɛ mitsui mli. Kɛfata he lɛ, gbii fɛɛ ni esa akɛ mahi shi amrɔ nɛɛ baafee nɔ ni hi tamɔ gbii ni Lazaro hi shi yɛ eshitee lɛ sɛɛ lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ; sɛɛnamɔ ni he tse ni fata he ni ji nyɔji loo otsii babaoo bɔ ni ebaaba lɛ. Mi diɛŋtsɛ mije mli; migbele kɛ mifuumɔ lɛ efo mitsitsi mli. Mikwɛ mihe kɛkpe yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli kɛ tsui ni nyɔɔ mɔ mli, tamɔ mumɔ ko ni yɔɔ dioo ni hiɛ henilee ni he tse ni eta weku shĩa ko ni yɔɔ fɛo lɛ mli.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, misusu he, ni mikɛ mi atade lɛ niji miifolɔ mihe ni mileee, biɛ yaa kɛha ŋshɔ ni mli ejɔ, ni abua naa yɛ gbele kɛ hiɛkpatamɔ nɔ, ni abonsa lɛ baaŋɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ sɛɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ.





YITSƆƆ 50. Ahab Lɛlɛ lɛ kɛ Etsulɔi lɛ. Fedala.

“Namɔ baasusu he, Flask!” Stubb bolɔ; “kɛji miyɛ naji kome pɛ lɛ, obaamɔŋ mi yɛ lɛlɛ mli, ja ekolɛ okɛ mi tso nanetsitsi lɛ atsĩ plɔg-bu lɛ naa. Oh! Nuumo kpakpa ji lɛ!”

Flask kɛɛ akɛ: “Misusuuu akɛ eji naakpɛɛ nii, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, yɛ nakai sane lɛ hewɔ.” "Eji enane lɛ je etsitsi nɔ, agbɛnɛ, ebaafee nɔ kroko. No baaha egbɔjɔ; shi eyɛ enane kome, ni ekroko lɛ fã kpakpa eshwɛ, ole."

“Mileee nakai, mi gbɔmɔ bibioo, minako lɛ ni ekoto shi dã.”

Bei pii lɛ, awieɔ yɛ mɛi ni le ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nii lɛ ateŋ akɛ, kɛji akɛ asusu bɔ ni ewala he hiaa fe fɛɛ kɛha gbɛfaa lɛ omanyeyeli lɛ he lɛ, eja akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsulɔ ko kɛ nakai wala lɛ awo oshara mli yɛ osharai ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ asɛɛtiumɔ lɛ mli lɛ mli. No hewɔ lɛ bei pii lɛ, Tamerlane asraafoi lɛ kɛ yaafo nuɔ he ŋwane, kɛji esa akɛ akɛ nakai ewala ni jara wa lɛ awo tawuu ni mli wa fe fɛɛ lɛ mli.

Shi yɛ Ahab gbɛfaŋ lɛ, sanebimɔ lɛ bafee nɔ ko ni atsake. Kɛ asusu akɛ gbɔmɔ ni yɔɔ naji enyɔ lɛ ji tsɔne ko ni shwieɔ shi kɛkɛ yɛ osharai bei fɛɛ amli lɛ; kɛji akɛ asusu akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi asɛɛtiumɔ yɛ naagbai wuji ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ shishi be fɛɛ be lɛ; akɛ be fɛɛ be aŋkroaŋkro, lɛɛlɛŋ, no lɛ oshara ko fata he; yɛ shihilɛi nɛɛ amli lɛ, ani nilee yɛ mli akɛ nuu ko ni egbɔjɔ lɛ baabote ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ mli yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔ mli? Taakɛ eji nɔ ko ni afɔɔ feemɔ lɛ, ekolɛ Pequod lɛ mli bii ni fata he lɛ asusu he faŋŋ akɛ jeee nakai.

Ahab le jogbaŋŋ akɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ enanemɛi ni yɔɔ shia lɛ susuuu lɛlɛ mli ni eboteɔ lɛlɛ mli yɛ shihilɛi komɛi ni bɛ awui ni akɛtoɔ he lɛ mli lɛ he moŋ, shi yɛ he ni ebɛŋkɛ he ni nifeemɔ lɛ yaa nɔ yɛ lɛ ni ekɛ efãmɔi baaha lɛ diɛŋtsɛ hewɔ lɛ, shi kɛlɛ, koni Kapten Ahab aha lɛlɛ ko ni akɛha lɛ diɛŋtsɛ lɛlɛ lɛ aha lɛ— lɛlɛ ni ji lɛlɛ ni akɛtoɔ he daa lɛ fɛɛ ji . ni akɛ hii enumɔ krokomɛi aba, akɛ nakai lɛlɛ lɛ nɔŋŋ mli nitsulɔi lɛ, ele jogbaŋŋ akɛ henɔwomɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni jɛ mlihilɛ mli lɛ eboteee Pequod lɛ nuŋtsɔmɛi lɛ ayitsoŋ kɔkɔɔkɔ. No hewɔ lɛ ebiko lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi kɛjɛɛɛ amɛdɛŋ, ni etsɔɔɔ nɔ ni esumɔɔ yɛ nakai yitso lɛ he yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ ehe ewo teemɔŋ gbɛjianɔtoi ni kɔɔ nakai sane lɛ fɛɛ he lɛ mli. Kɛyashi be mli ni Cabaco na nɔ ni akala lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ esusuuu he jogbaŋŋ, eyɛ mli akɛ koni amɛná nɔmimaa akɛ, beni amɛje ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ be fioo sɛɛ lɛ, niji fɛɛ egbe kusum nitsumɔ ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛi lɛ ahe nitsumɔ naa; Beni be ko sɛɛ lɛ, Ahab na ehe akɛ ekɛ eniji miifee thole-pins kɛha nɔ ni asusuɔ akɛ eji lɛlɛi ni eshwɛ lɛ ateŋ ekome, ni ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ po miifo tsei bibii ni akɛfoɔ skewers lɛ, ni kɛ́ line lɛ miiho lɛ, akɛmiitswa ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ, atade ni akɛwoɔ lɛlɛ lɛ shishi, tamɔ nɔ ni ebaaha enyɛ edamɔ etsitsi ni akɛ shwuɔ wu ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ mli lɛ naa jogbaŋŋ; kɛ agbɛnɛ hu haomɔ ni ejie lɛ kpo yɛ bɔ ni efee thigh board, loo clumsy cleat, taakɛ atsɛɔ lɛ bei komɛi lɛ, ni ji fã ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ ni akɛfiɔ naji ahe yɛ ŋshɔŋ loo ni akɛtswaa ŋshɔŋ loo lɛ mli lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ mli; beni ana shii abɔ ni eteɔ shi yɛ nakai lɛlɛ lɛ mli ni ekɛ enane kome pɛ lɛ fĩɔ ŋwɛi ŋta ni yɔɔ ŋta lɛ mli lɛ mli, ni ekɛ kapintɛfonyo ŋta lɛ shwieɔ shi fioo yɛ biɛ ni ekɛjajeɔ fioo yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ; mikɛɛ akɛ nibii nɛɛ fɛɛ etee miishɛɛ kɛ shwelɛ babaoo shi yɛ nakai beaŋ. Shi nɔ ni miihe ashɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ susu akɛ esa akɛ nɛkɛ hesaamɔ pɔtɛɛ ni yɔɔ Ahab mli nɛɛ afee kɛ yiŋtoo akɛ abaatiu Moby Dick lɛ naagbee pɛ; ejaakɛ ejie eyiŋtoo akɛ ebaatao nakai kooloo ni gboɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ. Shi susumɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ lɛ kɔɔɔ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ ni kɔɔ lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi komɛi ni akɛ amɛ ewo nakai lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ he lɛ he kwraa.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛ ŋwɛibɔfoi ni yɔɔ shishi lɛ, nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni eshwɛ lɛ ba shi etsɛɛɛ; ejaakɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko mli lɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni naakpɛɛ nii baa shi. Kɛfata he lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ kɛ agbɛnɛ lɛ, jeŋmaji ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni anyɛɛɛ abu he akɔntaa kɛ amɛnaagbee lɛ jɛɔ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ hei ni aleee kɛ sũ mli ŋmeii ni aleee lɛ amli kɛbaa nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛlɔi ni fãa gbɛ nɛɛ anɔ; ni bei pii lɛ, lɛlɛi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ holeɔ bɔɔ nii ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni anaa amɛmiiho yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli yɛ tsei anɔ, ŋshɔkei ni efite lɛ bibii, ŋshɔkei ni akɛyaa ŋshɔ hiɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛi, ŋshɔkei bibii, Japanbii akutseiaŋ nibii ni efite, kɛ nɔ krokomɛi anɔ; koni Beelzebub diɛŋtsɛ akwɔ sɛɛgbɛ ni eba shi kɛbote lɛlɛ lɛ mli koni ekɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ agba sane, ni ekɛ miishɛɛ ko ni anyɛɛɛ akudɔ aba yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli.

Shi bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni enɛ fɛɛ ji lɛ, eji nɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ akɛ, yɛ be mli ni etsɛɛɛ ni ŋwɛibɔfoi ni yɔɔ shishi lɛ na amɛhe yɛ lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ ateŋ lɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ esoro lɛ kwraa yɛ amɛhe yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ moŋ, shi nakai Fedallah ni hiɛ yitsɔi lɛ tee nɔ efee teemɔŋ sane ko ni bɛ faŋŋ kɛyashi naagbee lɛ. He ni ejɛ eba yɛ jeŋ ni yɔɔ jeŋba kpakpa mli tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ, mɛɛ tsakpaa ni anyɛɛɛ abu he akɔntaa lɛ nɔ etsɛɛɛ ni etsɔɔ akɛ ekɛ Ahab shihilɛi ni yɔɔ srɔto lɛ yɔɔ tsakpaa; daabi, kɛyashi he ni ebaanyɛ ená hewalɛ ko ni tsɔɔ fã kɛ fã; Ŋwɛi le, shi ekolɛ eji hegbɛ po ni yɔɔ enɔ; enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ lɛ, mɔ ko mɔ ko leee. Shi mɔ ko nyɛŋ ahiɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni bɛ he miishɛɛ yɛ Fedallah he lɛ mli. Eji adebɔɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni mɛi ni yɔɔ jeŋba kpakpa, ni yɔɔ shia ni yɔɔ he ni hulutsoo be yɔɔ lɛ naa yɛ amɛlamɔi amli pɛ, ni no pɛ ji nɔ ni anaa yɛ amɛlamɔi amli; shi mɛi ni tamɔ amɛ amrɔ nɛɛ kɛ agbɛnɛ lɛ shwereɔ yɛ Asia akutsei ni tsakeee lɛ ateŋ, titri lɛ Bokagbɛ ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ shikpɔŋ wulu lɛ bokagbɛ lɛ—nakai maji ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ, ni bɛ kaimɔ, ni anyɛɛɛ atsake, ni yɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ bei nɛɛ amli po lɛ, amɛhiɛɔ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ shishijee yinɔi lɛ ateŋ babaoo mli lolo. gbɔmɛi fɛɛ ni ji eseshibii lɛ, ni amɛleee he ni ejɛ lɛ, kwɛɔ amɛhe tamɔ ŋwɛibɔfoi diɛŋtsɛ, ni amɛbiɔ hulu kɛ nyɔŋtsere lɛ nɔ hewɔ ni abɔ amɛ kɛ nɔ ni akɛfee; shi, taakɛ 1 Mose tsɔɔ lɛ, ŋwɛibɔfoi lɛ kɛ gbɔmɛi abiyei lɛ fee ekome diɛŋtsɛ, daimonioi lɛ hu, kɛ Rabifoi ni bɛ mla naa lɛ fata he, ni kɛ amɛhe woɔ je lɛŋ suɔmɔi amli lɛ.





YITSƆƆ 51. Mumɔ-Spout lɛ.

Gbii, otsii ho, ni yɛ lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo shishi lɛ, Pequod ni akɛ shwuɔ wu fee lɛ efo ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ hei srɔtoi ejwɛ fiofio; ni yɔɔ Azores lɛ; yɛ Kap de Verdes ŋshɔ lɛ naa; yɛ Plɛte lɛ nɔ (ni atsɛɔ lɛ nakai), ni ejɛ Rio de la Plata lɛ naa lɛ; kɛ Carrol Ground, ni ji he ko ni tsei bɛ nɔ, ni nu yɔɔ mli, ni yɔɔ St.

Beni amɛmiiho kɛmiitsɔ naagbee nui nɛɛ amli lɛ ji nakai gbɛkɛ ko ni yɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ ni nyɔŋtsere lɛ kpɛɔ lɛ, beni ŋshɔkei lɛ fɛɛ shwieɔ amɛhe tamɔ shika wolokpoi lɛ; ni, kɛtsɔ amɛ shãa ni yɔɔ blɛoo ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ nɔ lɛ, efee nɔ ni tamɔ shika tsuru dioofeemɔ, shi jeee he ko ni mɔ kome pɛ yɔɔ; yɛ gbɛkɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni efee dioo lɛ mli lɛ, ana shika tsuru tsɔne ko ni etsɔ hiɛ shɔŋŋ fe ŋmeii yɛŋ ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ lɛ. Nyɔŋtsere lɛ kpɛ lɛ, ni etamɔ ŋwɛi nɔ; etamɔ nɔ ni nyɔŋmɔ ko ni hiɛ ŋmeii ni kpɛɔ miite shi kɛmiijɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli. Fedallah ji mɔ klɛŋklɛŋ ni tsɔɔ jet nɛɛ mli. Ejaakɛ nɛkɛ nyɔŋtsere la kpɛɔ gbɛkɛ nɛɛ mli lɛ, eji esu akɛ ekwɔ kɛya ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔne wulu lɛ yitso nɔ, ni edamɔ jɛmɛ ni ekwɛ kpo lɛ nɔ, kɛ pɛpɛɛpɛfeemɔ tamɔ nɔ ni hulu lɛ eshɛ. Ni kɛlɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ anaa ŋshɔŋlooi akui gbɛkɛ moŋ, shi ŋshɔŋlooi ohai ateŋ mɔ kome po kɛ ehe wooo mli ni ekɛ amɛ baa shi. Belɛ, ekolɛ obaasusu henumɔi ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi lɛ kɛna Bokagbɛnyo onukpa nɛɛ ni eta ŋwɛi yɛ ŋmɛlɛtswai ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ he; eyitsoŋtsɔi kɛ nyɔŋtsere lɛ, nanemɛi yɛ ŋwɛi kome mli. Shi beni, beni eye e-uniform interval lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ gbɛkɛ babaoo ni nyiɛ sɛɛ ni ewieee gbee kome po lɛ; beni, yɛ dioofeemɔ nɛɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, anu egbee ni jeee shikpɔŋ nɔ nɔ lɛ ni miitswa nakai shika tsuru, ni nyɔŋtsere kpɛɔ lɛ he adafi lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ fɛɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni ekã shi lɛ je enaji anɔ tamɔ nɔ ni mumɔ ko ni yɔɔ fɛ̃i ekpɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni ejie ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ni gboiɔ lɛ ayi. “Jɛmɛ ji he ni etswaa!” Eji atswa kojomɔ tɛtɛrɛmantɛrɛ lɛ kulɛ, amɛnyɛŋ amɛhoso fe nɛkɛ; shi kɛlɛ amɛnuuu gbeyeishemɔ ko he lolo; moŋ lɛ, miishɛɛ. Ejaakɛ eji ŋmɛlɛtswaa ko ni afɔɔɔɔ namɔ moŋ, shi bɔlɔmɔ lɛ ha mɔ naa kpɛ ehe waa, ni eha mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli waa, ni nɔ ni miihe ashɛ susuma fɛɛ susuma ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ miisumɔ ni aba shi kɛjɛ fɔmɔ mli.

Beni Ahab nyiɛɔ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ kɛ oyaiyeli ni tsɔɔ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ, efã akɛ ato t’gallant lɛlɛi lɛ kɛ maŋtsɛmɛi akui lɛ ahe gbɛjianɔ, ni lɛlɛ fɛɛ lɛlɛ ni haa mɔ he jɔ̃ɔ ehe lɛ gbɛ eshwa. Esa akɛ mɔ ni hi fe fɛɛ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ akudɔ lɛlɛ lɛ. Kɛkɛ ni, beni mɔ ko yɔɔ lɛlɛ yitso fɛɛ yitso mli lɛ, lɛlɛ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ hiɛ. Taffrail kɔɔyɔɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli, ni woɔ mɔ nɔ lɛ ni eyiɔ lɛlɛi babaoo ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ amli lɛ ha lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ ni shwieɔ shi, ni shwieɔ shi lɛ nuɔ he tamɔ kɔɔyɔɔ ni yɔɔ naji ashishi; yɛ be mli ni ekɛ oyaiyeli nyiɛɔ lolo lɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni hewalɛi enyɔ ni teɔ shi woɔ lɛ miiwuu yɛ emli—kome koni ekwɔ kɛya ŋwɛi tɛ̃ɛ, ekroko lɛ koni ekɛ tsɔne lɛ aya ŋwɛi tɛ̃ɛ kɛya oti ko ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ he. Ni eji okwɛ Ahab hiɛ nakai gbɛkɛ lɛ kulɛ, obaasusu akɛ nibii sɔrɔtoi enyɔ miiwu yɛ emli hu. Yɛ be mli ni enane kome ni hiɛ kã lɛ feɔ gbee ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ he lɛ, enine ni egbo lɛ he gbee fɛɛ gbee ni etswaa lɛ gbɛɛmɔ tamɔ gbonyo adeka mli gbee. Wala kɛ gbele nɔ nuumo nɛɛ nyiɛ. Shi eyɛ mli akɛ lɛlɛ lɛ tee oya waa, ni eyɛ mli akɛ hiŋmɛi fɛɛ hiŋmɛi, tamɔ akpɔlɔi, hiŋmɛii ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ lɛ tswa moŋ, shi anaaa shika tsuru jet lɛ dɔŋŋ nakai gbɛkɛ lɛ. Ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ fɛɛ kudɔlɔ kã kitã akɛ ena lɛ shikome, shi jeee shii enyɔ nɔ.

Shwɛ fioo ni nɛkɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa teŋ tsɔne nɛɛ baatsɔ nɔ ko ni hiɛ ekpa nɔ, beni, gbii komɛi asɛɛ lɛ, naa! yɛ nakai ŋmɛlɛtswaa ni afee dioo lɛ nɔŋŋ mli lɛ, atswa he adafi ekoŋŋ: mɛi fɛɛ tsɔɔ mli ekoŋŋ; shi beni ekɛ lɛlɛ fã gbɛ koni eyashɛ ehe lɛ, shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, elaaje tamɔ nɔ ni ebɛ pɛŋ. Ni nakai esɔmɔ wɔ gbɛkɛ fɛɛ gbɛkɛ, kɛyashi mɔ ko mɔ ko booo lɛ toi akɛ ja efee lɛ naakpɛɛ. Akɛ teemɔŋ shwieɔ nyɔŋtsere la ni mli tse, loo ŋulami la ni mli tse lɛ mli, taakɛ ebaafee lɛ; ni elaajeɔ ekoŋŋ gbi muu kome, loo gbii enyɔ, loo etɛ; ni yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni yɛ be fɛɛ mli ni atĩɔ mli lɛ mli lɛ, eyaa hiɛ lolo yɛ wɔ tsɔne lɛ mli, ni etamɔ nɔ ni nɛkɛ tsɔne ni yɔɔ ekome too nɛɛ miilaka wɔ kɛya naanɔ.

Ni kɛ amɛ hewolonɔ su lɛ mli apasa hemɔkɛyeli ni bɛ kaimɔ, kɛ nɔ ni kɛ adebɔɔ hiɛ nibii ni baa lɛ kpãa gbee, taakɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ Pequod lɛ wo nibii babaoo mli lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi ni kãa kitã akɛ be fɛɛ be kɛ he fɛɛ he ni atsɔɔ mli lɛ ateŋ mɛi komɛi miitao; bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni bei ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ yɔɔ ha, loo yɛ latitude kɛ longitude ni kɛ amɛhe jɛkɛ ha lɛ mli lɛ, nakai nu ni anyɛɛɛ abɛŋkɛ lɛ ji nɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo kome pɛ ji mɔ ni shwie; kɛ nakai ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, Moby Dick. Yɛ be ko mli lɛ, gbeyeishemɔ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto hu ye nɔ yɛ nɛkɛ kpojiemɔ ni shwieɔ shi nɛɛ he, tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ lakamɔ miitsɔɔ wɔ kɛmiiya kɛmiiya, koni kooloo lɛ atsɔ ehe kɛba wɔ nɔ, ni naagbee lɛ efo wɔ yɛ ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ lɛ amli.

Nɛkɛ be kukuoo mli gbeyeishemɔi nɛɛ, ni bɛ faŋŋ shi eyɛ gbeyei waa nɛɛ, ná naakpɛɛ hewalɛ ko kɛjɛ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ ni yɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ mli, ni, yɛ e-bluu bluu lɛ fɛɛ shishi lɛ, mɛi komɛi susu akɛ abonsam ŋmɔlɔ ko yɛ, tamɔ gbii kɛ gbii ni wɔfã gbɛ, kɛtsɔ ŋshɔi anɔ kɛ tɔlɛ, shoofeemɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo, ni wɔ fɛɛ wɔ ni ejieɔ ehe kɛjɛɔ wala mli yɛ wɔ ŋshɔ ni tamɔ urn lɛ hiɛ.

Shi, naagbee lɛ, beni wɔtsɔ wɔhe kɛtee bokagbɛ lɛ, Cape kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ bɔi ŋmɔlɔ ni ebɔle wɔhe, ni wɔte shi ni wɔgbee ŋshɔi kakadaŋŋ agboi ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ anɔ; beni Pequod ni hiɛ shwuɔ wu lɛ koto shi waa eha ŋshɔkei ni tswaa lɛ, ni ekɛ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ duŋ lɛ tswa ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ duŋ lɛ yɛ eshwilafofeemɔ mli lɛ, kɛyashi, tamɔ shika tsuru ŋmɔtɔi ni shwieɔ shi lɛ, ŋshɔkei ni etse amɛhe lɛ fã kɛho egbogboi lɛ anɔ; no sɛɛ lɛ, shihilɛ ni efee amaŋfɔ nɛɛ fɛɛ tee, shi ekɛ nibii ni yɔɔ awerɛho fe tsutsu lɛ ha gbɛhe.

Yɛ wɔ ŋshɔkei lɛ ahe lɛ, nibii ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni yɔɔ nu lɛ mli lɛ shwieɔ wɔhiɛ kɛyaa biɛ kɛ biɛ; be mli ni ŋshɔ mli ŋmeii ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ amɛ shishi lɛ fãa yɛ wɔsɛɛ lɛ. Ni daa leebi, ni amɛtara he ni wɔhiɔ lɛ, anaa nɛkɛ okpoi nɛɛ ni ato amɛhe gbɛjianɔ; ni yɛ wɔ gbee ni wɔtswaa lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, amɛkɛ amɛhe kpɛtɛ hemp lɛ he be kakadaŋŋ, tamɔ nɔ ni amɛbu wɔlɛlɛ lɛ akɛ eji lɛlɛ ko ni miiho, ni mɔ ko mɔ ko bɛ mli; nɔ ko ni ato he gbɛjianɔ kɛha amaŋfɔfeemɔ, ni no hewɔ lɛ esa akɛ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ ni amɛbɛ shia lɛ ahi shi yɛ. Ni ŋshɔ diŋ lɛ miiho ni eho, ni ekɛ ehe wooo mli lolo, tamɔ nɔ ni e-nugbɔshiimɔi wuji lɛ ji henilee; ni je lɛŋ susuma kpeteŋkpele lɛ yɛ haomɔ kɛ shwamɔ mli yɛ esha kɛ piŋmɔ kakadaŋŋ ni ekɛba lɛ hewɔ.

Hiɛnɔkamɔ Kpakpa Ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ, ani amɛtsɛɔ nyɛ? Shi moŋ, Cape Tormentoso, taakɛ atsɛɔ lɛ tsutsu lɛ; ejaakɛ dioofeemɔi ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni ba wɔŋɔɔ tsutsu lɛ gbala wɔ be kakadaŋŋ, ni wɔna wɔhe akɛ wɔkɛ wɔhe wo ŋshɔ ni apila wɔ nɛɛ mli, he ni etamɔ nɔ ni abu adesai ni yeɔ fɔ ni amɛtsake amɛhe amɛfee nakai kooloi kɛ loo nɛɛ, ni abu amɛ fɔ koni amɛfo kɛya naanɔ ni amɛbɛ he ko ni amɛbaahi, loo amɛye nakai kɔɔyɔɔ diŋ lɛ nɔ ni amɛbɛ ŋwɛi ŋwɛi. Shi eyɛ kpoo, eyɛ snoo, ni etsakeee; ni etsɔɔ e-fɛ̃i nu jɛɛhe lɛ gbɛ lolo kɛyaa ŋwɛi; ni miitsɔɔ wɔ lolo kɛjɛ tsutsu lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ abaatsɔɔ kɔɔyɔŋ lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ ekomekomei lɛ mli.

Yɛ nɛkɛ hulutsoo be lɛ mli diŋŋ nɛɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, Ahab, eyɛ mli akɛ esusu akɛ eji mɔ ni miihe ashɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ ni mli ewo nu ni yɔɔ oshara lɛ he famɔ ni yaa nɔ daa lɛ moŋ, shi ejie nɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ lɛ kpo; ni efɔɔɔ ehefatalɔi lɛ awiemɔ fe tsutsu ko. Yɛ ahum bei ni tamɔ enɛɛmɛi amli lɛ, beni akwɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi kɛ ŋwɛi lɛ nɔ jogbaŋŋ sɛɛ lɛ, anyɛɛɛ afee nɔ ko dɔŋŋ akɛ ja afee nɔ ko nɔ ko ni akɛaamɛ ahum lɛ he sane lɛ. Kɛkɛ ni Kapten kɛ lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ batsɔ fatalists ni anyɛɔ atsuɔ he nii. No hewɔ lɛ, ni ekɛ enane ni akɛ shwuɔ wu fee lɛ ewo ebu ni ekɛtsuɔ nii daa lɛ mli, ni ekɛ enine kome emɔ atade ko mli shiŋŋ lɛ, Ahab baadamɔ shi ŋmɛlɛtswai babaoo ni ekwɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ ni egbo, yɛ be mli ni snoo loo snoo ni tswaa yɛ bei komɛi amli lɛ baaha ehiŋmɛii ahe tsɔi lɛ fɛɛ afee ekome. Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi ni ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ oshara ni tswaa yɛ eshishigbɛ lɛ tsiɔ amɛ kɛjɛɔ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ fã lɛ mli lɛ damɔ shi yɛ line mli yɛ gbogboi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ shishi lɛ ahe; ni bɔ ni ehi jogbaŋŋ akɛ ebaabu ehe kɛjɛ ŋshɔkei ni shwieɔ shi lɛ ahe lɛ, nuu fɛɛ nuu kɛ ehe ewo ŋshɔkei ni akɛfiɔ oketeke lɛ he lɛ mli, ni ekɛ ehe shwieɔ mli tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ ehe woɔ bɛlɛ ni ajie lɛ mli. Wiemɔi fioo ko pɛ awie loo awieee nɔ ko kwraa; ni lɛlɛ ni efee dioo lɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ni akɛ ŋmɔtɔ ekpɛ amɛhe lɛ ji mɛi ni kwɛɔ nɔ lɛ, gbi fɛɛ gbi lɛ, etsɔɔ daimonioi a-ŋshɔkei lɛ a-shwilafofeemɔ kɛ miishɛɛ ni yaa oya lɛ fɛɛ mli daa gbi. Gbi gbɛkɛ lɛ, adesai a-mumufeemɔ ni yɔɔ dani ŋshɔ lɛ mli gbeei lɛ baa lɛ nɔŋŋ ye nɔ; yɛ dioofeemɔ mli lolo lɛ, hii lɛ shwieɔ amɛhe yɛ gãimlibii lɛ amli; Ahab ni ewieee lolo lɛ te shi ewo tsɔne ni tswaa lɛ. Beni etamɔ nɔ ni adebɔɔ ni etɔ lɛ biɔ hejɔɔmɔ po lɛ, etaooo nakai hejɔɔmɔ lɛ yɛ e-hammock lɛ mli. Starbuck hiɛ nyɛŋ akpa nuumo lɛ fã lɛ nɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ, beni gbɛkɛ ko beni eyaa shi kɛboteɔ tsɔne lɛ mli koni eyakadi bɔ ni barometer lɛ damɔ shi eha lɛ, ena lɛ ni ekɛ ehiŋmɛii efo mli ni eta esɛi ni akɛ shikpɔŋ ebɔ he lɛ mli tɛ̃ɛ; nugbɔ kɛ ahum ni mli edɔ fã ni ekɛ be fioo ko eho lɛ je kpo lɛ, ni miiho fiofio lolo kɛmiijɛ tsitsi kɛ atade ni ajieee lɛ mli. Nakai ŋshɔkei kɛ ŋshɔkei ni awie he tsutsu lɛ ahe woji lɛ ateŋ ekome ni ajie lɛ kpo lɛ kã okpɔlɔ ni yɔɔ emasɛi lɛ nɔ. E-lantern lɛ shwie shi kɛjɛ enine ni emɔ mli waa lɛ mli. Eyɛ mli akɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ damɔ shi moŋ, shi ashɛrɛ yitso lɛ ashwie sɛɛ bɔni afee ni hiŋmɛii ni agbe naa lɛ atsɔɔ tell-tale lɛ tsɔne ni shwieɔ shi kɛjɛɔ tso ko ni yɔɔ ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ.*

*Atsɛɔ cabin-compass lɛ akɛ tell-tale, ejaakɛ kɛji eyaaa kɔmpas ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ kudɔmɔ he lɛ, Kapten lɛ, be mli ni eyɔɔ shishi lɛ, baanyɛ aha ehe ale bɔ ni lɛlɛ lɛ yaa nɔ ehaa.

Nuumo ni yɔɔ gbeyei! Starbuck susu he kɛ ŋmɔlɔ, ni ewɔ ahum ni naa wa nɛɛ mli, shi loloolo lɛ, okɛ ohiɛ fɔ̃ɔ oyiŋtoo lɛ nɔ.





YITSƆƆ 52. Albatros lɛ.

Yɛ anaigbɛ-bokagbɛ kɛjɛ Cape, yɛ Crozetts ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ masɛi, ni ji he ko ni hi jogbaŋŋ ni Right Whalemen lɛ fãa gbɛ lɛ, lɛlɛ ko ni ji Goney (Albatross) lɛ miiba hiɛ. Beni ebɛŋkɛ fiofio, kɛjɛ mi sɛkɛ ni nɔ kwɔ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yitso lɛ nɔ lɛ, mina nakai nibii ni sa kadimɔ waa kɛha tyro ko ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ wuoyeli hei ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ jogbaŋŋ—whale wuo ko ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ, ni ebɛ shia be kakadaŋŋ.

Taakɛ eji akɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ eyi obɔ lɛ, afee lɛlɛ nɛɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ tamɔ walrus ni egbo lɛ fãji. Yɛ efãi fɛɛ amli lɛ, nɛkɛ hiɛ ni yɔɔ fɛo nɛɛ ji nɔ ni ŋmei ni etsɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni etse ehe lɛ tsɔɔ, yɛ be mli ni e-spar kɛ e-rigging lɛ fɛɛ tamɔ tsei niji ni mli tse ni fɛ̃i ni mli edɔ ebɔle amɛhe lɛ. Eshishigbɛ lɛlɛ lɛ pɛ ji nɔ ni ato. Eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa akɛ aaana etsitsi kakadaŋŋ ni ekwɛɔ nakai mast-yitsei etɛ lɛ. Etamɔ nɔ ni amɛwo kooloi ahewolo, no hewɔ lɛ amɛgbala atadei ni amɛye nɔ ni miihe ashɛ afii ejwɛ ni amɛkɛfã gbɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ mli lɛ mli ni amɛfo mli. Amɛdamɔ dade tsɔji ni akɛkpɛtɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ amli, ni amɛhosoɔ ni amɛhosoɔ yɛ ŋshɔ ko ni shishinumɔ bɛ he lɛ nɔ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ, beni lɛlɛ lɛ shwieɔ shi fiofio kɛbɛŋkɛɔ wɔsɛɛgbɛ lɛ, wɔ hii enumɔ ni yɔɔ kɔɔyɔɔ mli lɛ wɔbɛŋkɛ wɔhe aahu akɛ ekolɛ eshwɛ fioo ni wɔbaajɛ lɛlɛ kome lɛlɛ lɛ yitsei lɛ anɔ kɛya ekroko lɛ yitsei lɛ anɔ; shi kɛlɛ, nakai wuolɛɛmɛi ni tamɔ mɛi ni egbɔjɔ lɛ, ni kɛ blɛoo kwɛɔ wɔ beni amɛhoɔ lɛ, ewieee wiemɔ kome po kɛhaaa wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔkwɛlɔi lɛ, yɛ be mli ni anuɔ nyɔŋmɔŋtɛi ni shɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ fã lɛ nɔ lɛ kɛjɛɔ shishigbɛ lɛ.

"Lɛlɛ ahoy! Ani nyɛna Ŋshɔloo Eyɛŋ lɛ?"

Shi beni ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafonyo nukpa ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ gbogboi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ anɔ lɛ miifee nifeemɔ ni ekɛ etɛrɛmantɛrɛ lɛ miitswa enaa lɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, ejɛ enine mli egbee shi kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ mli; ni kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ miitswa agbɛnɛ lɛ, ebɔ mɔdɛŋ yakatswaa koni eha anu ehe kɛji kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ bɛ mli. Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, elɛlɛ lɛ miiya hiɛ lolo yɛ gbɛ ni kã amɛteŋ lɛ nɔ. Beni Pequod ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ kɛ gbɛi srɔtoi ni yɔɔ dioo nɔ miitsɔɔ bɔ ni amɛkwɛ nɛkɛ sane ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɛɛ yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ be mli ni atsĩ White Whale gbɛi lɛ tã kɛkɛ kɛha lɛlɛ kroko lɛ, Ahab mɛ fioo; eshwɛ fioo ni ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni ebaajie lɛlɛ ko shi koni ekɛbote gbɔ lɛ mli, eji kɔɔyɔɔ ni woɔ mɔ he gbeyei lɛ eguuu lɛ. Shi beni ekɛ ehe ni eyɔɔ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli lɛ tsu nii lɛ, emɔ etɛrɛmantɛrɛ lɛ ekoŋŋ, ni akɛni ele kɛtsɔ ehiɛ nɔ akɛ gbɔ lɛlɛ lɛ ji Nantucketernyo ni etsɛɛɛ ni ebaaya shia lɛ hewɔ lɛ, ekɛ gbee ni wa jie yi akɛ—“Ahoy jɛmɛ! ——”

Nakai beaŋ lɛ, afo ŋshɔkei enyɔ lɛ amli jogbaŋŋ, ni amrɔ nɔŋŋ, yɛ amɛ gbɛi komekomei lɛ anaa lɛ, loo bibii ni bɛ awui, ni gbii komɛi ni eho nɛ lɛ, amɛmiiho yɛ wɔmasɛi yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli lɛ, kɛ nɔ ni tamɔ ŋshɔkei ni hosoɔ lɛ jo foi kɛtee, ni amɛkɛ gbɔ lɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ tee hiɛ kɛ sɛɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ gbɛfaa ni eyaa nɔ lɛ mli lɛ, esa akɛ Ahab ana nɔ ko ni tamɔ nakai shii abɔ dani eba moŋ, shi kɛha gbɔmɔ fɛɛ gbɔmɔ ni sumɔɔ nɔ kome pɛ lɛ, nibii bibii ni fe fɛɛ lɛ hiɛ shishinumɔi yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ.

“Nyɛfoɔ nu kɛjɛɔ mihe, aloo jeee nakai?” Ahab wie huhuuhu, ni ekwɛ nu lɛ mli. Etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ko fioo pɛ yɔɔ wiemɔi lɛ amli, shi gbee lɛ jieɔ awerɛho ni mli kwɔ ni nyɛɛɛ nɔ ko afee lɛ kpo fe bɔ ni nuumo ni egbɔjɔ lɛ ejie lɛ kpo pɛŋ. Shi beni etsɔ ehe ekwɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ, ni kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ ehiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli koni ekɛba ehiɛyaa lɛ shi lɛ, ekɛ ejata gbee momo lɛ bolɔ akɛ,—“Wó lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ!

Je lɛŋ he fɛɛ he! Nibii babaoo yɛ nakai gbee lɛ mli ni baaha henɔwomɔ henumɔi aba; shi nɛgbɛ nakai ŋwɛibɔfoi lɛ fɛɛ kɛ amɛhe yaa? Kɛtsɔ osharai ni anyɛɛɛ akane nɔ pɛ kɛyashɛ he ni wɔje shishi kɛjɛ, he ni mɛi ni wɔshi amɛ yɛ sɛɛ shweshweeshwe lɛ, yɔɔ wɔhiɛ be fɛɛ be lɛ.

Kɛji akɛ je nɛɛ ji shikpɔŋ kplanaa ni bɛ naagbee, ni kɛtsɔ lɛlɛ ni wɔɔtsɔ kɛtee bokagbɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ wɔbaanyɛ wɔyashɛ hei ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ kɛya naanɔ, ni wɔna nibii ni ŋɔɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ fe Cyclades loo Maŋtsɛ Salomo Ŋshɔkpɔi fɛɛ lɛ, no lɛ shiwoo yɛ gbɛfaa lɛ mli. Shi yɛ nakai teemɔŋ saji ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni wɔlaa lɛ asɛɛtiumɔ mli, loo yɛ nakai daimonioi ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ni, yɛ be ko mli loo be kroko mli lɛ, efoɔ yɛ adesai atsuii fɛɛ ahiɛ lɛ sɛɛtiumɔ mli; yɛ be mli ni amɛtiuɔ mɛi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ yɛ shikpɔŋ muu ni yɔɔ kokroo nɛɛ nɔ lɛ, amɛnyiɛɔ wɔhiɛ yɛ gbɛjegbɛi ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ loo amɛshiɔ wɔ yɛ gbɛ teŋgbɛ.
