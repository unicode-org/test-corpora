Klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ, okpɔŋɔ yɛŋ, ni atsɛɔ lɛ nakai lɛ baa, ni anáa kɛjɛɔ loo lɛ fã ni mli edɔ lɛ mli, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu kɛjɛɔ efluki lɛ fãi ni mli edɔ lɛ amli. Beni ajie okpɔŋɔ yɛŋ lɛ kɛjɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli sɛɛ lɛ, afoɔ okpɔŋɔ yɛŋ lɛ mli klɛŋklɛŋ ni efeɔ oblongs ni anyɛɔ atereɔ dani aya mincer lɛ ŋɔɔ. Amɛtamɔ Berkshire tɛi ni jara wa waa lɛ ahe blɔkii waa.

Eji rubi tɛi ni ji plums, ni yɔɔ citron mfonirii amli lɛ. Yɛ yiŋtoo ni yɔɔ he lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, ewa akɛ oootsi ohe kɛjɛ he ni oyeɔ lɛ he. YITSƆƆ 95. Kaso lɛ.

Ani obote Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ mli yɛ be ko mli yɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ gbele sɛɛ nɛɛ mli; ni eji onyiɛ hiɛ kɛbɛŋkɛ kɔɔyɔɔŋ tsɔne lɛ kulɛ, miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ obaakwɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ waa, ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ waa, ni obaana yɛ jɛmɛ, ni ekã shi yɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ amli. Amaga ni tamɔ nɔ ni ana yɛ Maŋnyɛ Maaka teemɔŋ tsei ni yɔɔ Yudea lɛ amli lɛ; ni yɛ jamɔ ni Maŋtsɛ Asa, ebinuu lɛ jie lɛ kɛjɛ maŋtsɛyeli lɛ nɔ lɛ hewɔ, ni ekpãtã amaga lɛ hiɛ, ni eshã lɛ akɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ nyaŋemɔ yɛ Kedron faa lɛ naa, taakɛ atsɔɔ mli yɛ duŋ mli yɛ Maŋtsɛmɛi A-Klɛŋklɛŋ Wolo lɛ yitso 15 lɛ mli lɛ.

Enɛ ni efee lɛ etsɔɔ hewolo lɛ mli kɛyaa kpo, tamɔ pantaloon naji; ehaa ekpãa jogbaŋŋ, bɔni afee ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaashɛ elɛɛmɔ he toi enyɔ; ni naagbee lɛ, ekɛfɔ̃ɔ shi, ni egbɛ eshwa jogbaŋŋ, yɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ mli, koni egbi. Yɛ jarayelɔi agbɛfaŋ lɛ, mu ni akɛhaa lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ he ehiaaa fe maŋnyɛi amli nu. Ni esaa ehe yɛ duŋ mli, ni eye nii yɛ duŋ mli, ni etɔtɔ yɛ duŋ mli kɛya e-pallet lɛ nɔ lɛ, enɛ ji nɔ ni efɔɔ feemɔ. Yɛ naagbee lɛ, kɛ́ awo naagbee pint lɛ mli, ni nɔ fɛɛ nɔ edɔ lɛ, no lɛ agbeleɔ ŋshɔnaa ŋshɔi wuji lɛ, ashwieɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni ŋshɔ lɛ mli ŋshɔi lɛ yaa shi kɛyaa amɛnaagbee hejɔɔmɔhe yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli. Afee enɛ, atsakeɔ ŋwɛitsu lɛ naa, ni aŋaa lɛ jogbaŋŋ, tamɔ ŋwɛitsu ko ni awo gbogbo awo he.

Yɛ sperm mli wuoyeli mli lɛ, ekolɛ enɛ ji nibii ni sa kadimɔ waa yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe jarayeli nitsumɔi fɛɛ amli lɛ ateŋ ekome. Gbi ko lɛ, lá kɛ fɔ ni hoɔ yɛ tsei lɛ anɔ lɛ hoɔ; yɛ ŋshɔkpɔ krɔŋkrɔŋ ni ji quarter-deck lɛ nɔ lɛ, ato ŋshɔŋloo lɛ yitso lɛ mli ŋshɔŋlooi wuji babaoo yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ muji nɔ; tsɛnsii wuji ni mli edɔ lɛ kã he fɛɛ he, tamɔ yɛ dãafeemɔ he ko; lasu ni jɛɔ kaa-nitsumɔhei lɛ efite gbogboi lɛ fɛɛ; ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ yaa kɛbaa ni amɛhe eyi kɛ muji; lɛlɛ muu lɛ fɛɛ tamɔ nɔ ni ji leviathan kpeteŋkpele lɛ diɛŋtsɛ; yɛ be mli ni yɛ niji fɛɛ nɔ lɛ, gbɛɛmɔ lɛ haa mɔ toiŋ jɔ̃ɔ ehe.

Shi gbi kome loo gbii enyɔ sɛɛ lɛ, okwɛɔ ohe kɛkpeɔ, ni okɛ otoii shwieɔ nɛkɛ lɛlɛ nɛɛ nɔŋŋ mli; ni eji jeee lɛlɛi ni akɛgbaa sane kɛ nitsumɔi ni akaa mɔ akwɛɔ lɛ kulɛ, nyɛ fɛɛ nyɛbaaka kitã akɛ nyɛnyiɛ jarayeli lɛlɛ ko ni efee dioo lɛ nɔ, ni ehiɛnyiɛlɔ ko ni yɔɔ fɛo fe fɛɛ lɛ yɔɔ mli. Nu mli fɔ ni afeee lɛ yɛ su kpakpa ko ni haa mɔ he tseɔ. Enɛ ji nɔ ni haa ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi lɛ efeee futafuta kɔkɔɔkɔ tamɔ nɔ ni amɛtsɛɔ lɛ mu he sane lɛ sɛɛ pɛ. Kɛfata he lɛ, akɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli nibii ni ashã lɛ ashwishwɛ lɛ feɔ lye ni mli wa oya; ni be fɛɛ be ni nɔ ko ni akɛkpɛtɛɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ sɛɛ lɛ baakã he akpɛtɛ esɛɛ lɛ, nakai lye lɛ kpataa lɛ oya. Niji yaa kɛ hiɛdɔɔ nyiɛɔ gbogboi lɛ anɔ, ni amɛkɛ nu baagi kɛ atadei ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ haa amɛfeɔ amɛhe fɛo kwraa. Akɛ sũ lɛ shwieɔ shishigbɛ rigging lɛ nɔ. Akɛ anɔkwayeli tsuɔ nibii babaoo ni akɛtsuɔ nii lɛ fɛɛ ahe nii nakai nɔŋŋ ni akɛtoɔ he kroko. Akɛ tsɔne wulu lɛ shwieɔ shi ni akɛwoɔ try-works lɛ anɔ, ni akɛ tsɛnsii lɛ teeɔ kwraa; tsɛnsi fɛɛ tsɛnsi bɛ hiŋmɛi nɔ; abɔle tackles fɛɛ he yɛ hei ni anaaa; ni kɛ́ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ lɛlɛ mli kuu muu lɛ fɛɛ nitsumɔ ni akɛfee ekome kɛ nitsumɔ ni afeɔ yɛ be kome mli lɛ gbe henilee nitsumɔ nɛɛ fɛɛ naa yɛ naagbee lɛ, no lɛ lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ yaa amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe shwiemɔ he; amɛshiɔ amɛhe kɛjɛɔ ŋwɛi kɛyaa amɛnaji ahe; ni naagbee lɛ, ajie lɛ kpo kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ, ni eyɛ ehee ni ekpɛɔ, beni ayemforowui heei ni jɛ Holland ni yɔɔ fɛo fe fɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛ miishɛɛ ni amɛnyiɛɔ lɛ, amɛnyiɛɔ tsei lɛ anɔ enyɔ kɛ etɛ, ni amɛkɛ ŋmɔlɔ wieɔ parlours, sofas, kapɛtii, kɛ cambrics kpakpai ahe; tsɔɔ akɛ afee mat lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ; susumɔ he akɛ oyɛ ŋwɛigbɛ; shitee-kɛ-woo akɛ akaye tii yɛ nyɔŋtsere la mli yɛ forecastle lɛ piazza lɛ nɔ. Kɛji akɛ abaatsɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni yɔɔ mu, kɛ wu, kɛ blubber lɛ, eji ekãa fioo ko pɛ. Amɛleee nɔ ni owieɔ he kɛjɛɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ. Shi, ni okɛ napkins aba wɔ!

Shi kadimɔ: yɛ ŋwɛi jɛmɛ, yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ yitsei etɛ lɛ ahe lɛ, hii etɛ damɔ shi ni amɛmiitao amɛkwɛ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji babaoo, ni kɛji amɔmɔ amɛ lɛ, tɔmɔ ko bɛ he akɛ amɛbaafite oak tso mli tsɔji momo lɛ ekoŋŋ, ni kɛ hoo lɛ, amɛbaashɛre mu bibioo kome yɛ he ko. Hɛɛ; ni be babaoo ji be, beni, yɛ nitsumɔi ni mli wa fe fɛɛ ni akpaaa naa, ni leee gbɛkɛ lɛ sɛɛ; ni eyaa nɔ tɛ̃ɛ kɛyashiɔ ŋmɛlɛtswai nyɔŋmai nɛɛhu kɛ ekpaa; beni amɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, he ni amɛwo amɛniji amli kɛ gbi muu lɛ fɛɛ ni amɛnyiɛɔ yɛ Line lɛ nɔ lɛ, —amɛyaa lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ pɛ koni amɛwo ŋwɛi ŋtayaai wuji, ni amɛwo kɔɔyɔɔŋ tsɔne ni tsii lɛ nɔ, ni amɛfolɔ ni amɛfolɔ, hɛɛ, ni yɛ amɛ latsaa lɛ diɛŋtsɛ mli lɛ, amɛka amɛ la ni amɛshã ekoŋŋ kɛtsɔ equatorial hulutsoo kɛ equatorial la ni akɛfee ekome lɛ nɔ; beni, yɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, naagbee lɛ amɛtswa amɛhe amɛwo mli koni amɛtsumɔ lɛlɛ lɛ he, ni amɛfee lɛ ŋmãa tsu ni muji bɛ he lɛ; babaoo ji be ni ohiafoi lɛ, ni amɛmiitswa amɛ atadei ni he tse lɛ akuɛ mli kɛkɛ lɛ, amɛhe feɔ amɛ yaa kɛ bɔlɔmɔ ni ji “Jɛmɛ etswaa!” ni amɛfoɔ kɛyaa koni amɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo kroko ayawu, ni amɛtsɔɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni etɔ lɛ lɛ mli ekoŋŋ. YITSƆƆ 99. Doubloon lɛ.

Ere amrɔ nɛɛ agba bɔ ni Ahab sumɔɔ ni ekɛ e-quarter-deck lɛ aya hiɛ, ni etsakeɔ ehe daa yɛ husu lɛ eko fɛɛ eko nɔ, binnacle kɛ mainmast lɛ; shi yɛ nibii krokomɛi babaoo ni biɔ sanegbaa lɛ mli lɛ, akɛfatako bɔ ni bei komɛi yɛ nyiɛmɔi nɛɛ amli, beni mɛi babaoo kɛ amɛhe woɔ esusumɔi amli lɛ, efɔɔ ekpamɔ yɛ he fɛɛ he, ni edamɔ jɛmɛ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ ni ekwɛɔ nɔ pɔtɛɛ ni yɔɔ ehiɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ he. Beni ekpa yɛ binnacle lɛ hiɛ, ni ekɛ ehiŋmɛii ekwɛ tsɔne ni yɔɔ kɔmpas lɛ mli lɛ nɔ lɛ, nakai hiŋmɛi lɛ tswa tamɔ akpɔlɔ kɛ eyiŋtoo lɛ mli hewalɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ; ni beni ebɔi enyiɛmɔ lɛ ekoŋŋ lɛ, emɛ ekoŋŋ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ wulu lɛ hiɛ, no sɛɛ lɛ, beni hiŋmɛi ni akɛ ŋtayaa fee lɛ nɔŋŋ kwɛɔ shika tsuru shika ni akɛ ŋtayaa ebɔ he lɛ nɔ yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ, ewo ŋtayaa ni akɛtswa shi shiŋŋ lɛ fã kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ lolo, ni ekɛ shwelɛ ko ni mli wa ko pɛ shwieɔ shi, kɛ jeee hiɛnɔkamɔ.

Shi gbi ko leebi, beni etsɔ ehe koni etsɔ doubloon lɛ nɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni mfonirii kɛ niŋmaai ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni akadi nɔ lɛ gbala lɛ ehee, tamɔ nɔ ni amrɔ nɛɛ eji klɛŋklɛŋ be ni ebɔi shishinumɔ fɛɛ shishinumɔ ni baanyɛ ahi mli lɛ shishitsɔɔmɔ kɛha lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe yɛ gbɛ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto nɔ. Ni shishinumɔ pɔtɛɛ ko yɛ nibii fɛɛ amli, kɛjee nakai lɛ, nibii fɛɛ he ehiaaa tsɔ, ni je lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ji nɔ ko ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko, ja akɛ ahɔ̃ɔ kɛtsɔ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ nɔ, taakɛ amɛfeɔ gɔji ni bɔle Boston he lɛ, koni akɛwo ŋmeii komɛi amli yɛ Milky Way lɛ mli.

Agbɛnɛ, nɛkɛ doubloon nɛɛ ji shika tsuru ni he tse fe fɛɛ, ni ajie kɛjɛ gɔji fɛfɛji lɛ ateŋ, he ni, bokagbɛ kɛ anaigbɛ, yɛ shika tsuru ŋãi anɔ lɛ, Pactolus ŋshɔkei babaoo ayitso-nui lɛ hoɔ kɛjɛɔ jɛmɛ. Ni eyɛ mli akɛ amrɔ nɛɛ akɛ dade ŋtayaai ni etsilɛ kɛ akɔɔble ŋtayaai ni etse lɛ fɛɛ ewo mli moŋ, shi kɛlɛ, anyɛɛɛ ata he ni muji ko bɛ he moŋ, shi ehiɛ e-Quito la ni tsoɔ lɛ mli lolo. Ni eyɛ mli akɛ akɛwo ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi akuu ko ni mli efu lɛ ateŋ ni ŋmɛlɛtswaa fɛɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa ni niji ni mli efu bɛ mli hoɔ, ni duŋ ni mli ewo duŋ ni baanyɛ aha gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ ni atsɔɔ nɔ ajuɔ nii lɛ ehà nɔ lɛ mli gbɛkɛ fɛɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ, shi kɛlɛ, hulushitee fɛɛ hulushitee naa ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ he ni hulushinyɔɔ lɛ shiɔ lɛ yɛ naagbee lɛ. Ejaakɛ ajie lɛ kpo ni afee lɛ krɔŋkrɔŋ kɛyashɛ naagbee kome ni yɔɔ gbeyei; ni bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni amɛ lɛlɛ kudɔmɔ gbɛi lɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ha, ekome kɛ fɛɛ lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ buɔ lɛ akɛ ŋshɔŋloo yɛŋ lɛ tsɔne. Bei komɛi lɛ, amɛwieɔ he yɛ bulɛ ni etɔ lɛ mli gbɛkɛ, ni amɛsusuɔ mɔ nɔ ni ebaafee yɛ naagbee lɛ he, kɛji ebaahi shi gbi ko ni ekɛye be lɛ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ nakai shika tsuru shika ni hi jogbaŋŋ ni yɔɔ South America lɛ ji hulu lɛ he shika kɛ tropic okadi nibii. Biɛ ji tsei, alpacas, kɛ lasu gɔji; hulu diski kɛ ŋulamii; ecliptics, akoloŋtoi babaoo, kɛ aflaŋaai ni jara wa ni miihoso, yɛ shika babaoo ni akɛkadi amɛ; bɔni afee ni etamɔ nɔ ni eshwɛ fioo ni shika tsuru ni jara wa lɛ baaná shika ni jara wa kɛ anunyam kroko ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli, kɛtsɔ nakai shika tsuru ni jara wa lɛ ni etsɔɔ mli, ni ji Spanish lalafoo wiemɔi lɛ amli lɛ nɔ.

Eba trukaa akɛ Pequod lɛ doubloon lɛ ji nɛkɛ nibii nɛɛ ahe nɔkwɛmɔnɔ ni yɔɔ nii fe fɛɛ. Yɛ ehusu ni yɔɔ kokroo lɛ nɔ lɛ, aŋmala niŋmaa okadii nɛɛ akɛ, REPUBLICA DEL ECUADOR: QUITO.
