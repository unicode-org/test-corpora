YITSƆƆ 107. Kapintɛfonyo lɛ.

Okɛ ohe awo Saturn nyɔji lɛ ateŋ yɛ sultan gbɛ nɔ, ni okɛ gbɔmɔ ni nɔ kwɔ ni yɔɔ abstract lɛ pɛ atsu nii; ni etamɔ naakpɛɛ nii, agbojee, kɛ haomɔ. Shi kɛjɛ he kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ nɔ lɛ, ŋɔɔ adesai babaoo, ni yɛ bei babaoo mli lɛ, amɛtamɔ nɔ ni amɛji kuu ko ni feɔ ekome ni he ehiaaa, ni ji ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ nɔ kɛ gboshinii fɛɛ. Shi eyɛ mli akɛ eba ehe shi fe fɛɛ moŋ, shi ekɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ ni nɔ kwɔ, ni ji adesa nɔ ni bɛ faŋŋ lɛ hamɔ teŋ jɛkɛ; Pequod kapintɛfonyo lɛ jeee nɔ ni tamɔ nakai; no hewɔ lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ eba diɛŋtsɛ yɛ kpoku nɛɛ nɔ.

Taakɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ he kapintɛfoi fɛɛ ji lɛ, ni titri lɛ mɛi ni fata ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛlɛi ahe lɛ, lɛ hu eyɛ niiashikpamɔ yɛ jarayeli nitsumɔi kɛ tsɛmɔi babaoo ni akɛwoɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enɔ lɛ mli kɛyashi he ko; kapintɛfonyo lɛ sɛɛtiumɔ ji blema nibii ni akɛ niji tsuɔ babaoo ni kɔɔ tso he akɛ yelikɛbuamɔ nibii lɛ fɛɛ ashishi ni ejɛɔ mli baa lɛ. Shi, kɛfata bɔ ni akɛ wiemɔ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi nɛɛ tsu nii aha lɛ lɛ he lɛ, nɛkɛ Pequod kapintɛfonyo nɛɛ tsu nii jogbaŋŋ yɛ nakai tsɔnei ahe osharai akpe ni bɛ gbɛi ni baa be fɛɛ be yɛ lɛlɛ agbo ko mli, yɛ afii etɛ loo ejwɛ gbɛfaa mli, yɛ ŋshɔi ni bɛ jeŋba kpakpa ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ amli. Ejaakɛ wɔkawie bɔ ni esaa ehe yɛ nitsumɔi ni afeɔ daa lɛ amli lɛ he:—stof lɛlɛi asaamɔ, sprung spars, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ ahe mfoniri tsakemɔ, tsina hiŋmɛii ni akɛwoɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, loo tso-ŋmeii heei ni akɛwoɔ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ tsei lɛ amli, kɛ nibii srɔtoi krokomɛi ni kɔɔ enitsumɔ lɛ he tɛ̃ɛ; kɛfata he lɛ, eji mɔ ko ni he esa waa yɛ nyɛmɔi srɔtoi fɛɛ ni teɔ shi woɔ amɛhe, ni he yɔɔ sɛɛnamɔ kɛ nɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ fɛɛ mli ni eshashaooo shi.

Nɔ kome ni da ni ekɛ efãi srɔtoi lɛ fɛɛ tsu nii yɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ srɔtoi nɔ lɛ ji esɛɛyelɔ; okpɔlɔ kakadaŋŋ ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni akɛ nibii fɔji babaoo ewo mli, ni amɛdalɛi srɔtoi, ni akɛ dade kɛ tso fɛɛ fee. Be fɛɛ be, ja be mli ni ŋshɔŋlooi yɔɔ masɛi lɛ, akɛ nɛkɛ sɛi nɛɛ fĩɔ Try-works lɛ sɛɛ jogbaŋŋ.

Anaa belaying pin ko ni da tsɔ ni anyɛɛɛ akɛwo ebu lɛ mli mlɛo: kapintɛfonyo lɛ tswaa lɛ ewoɔ e-vices ni efee klalo be fɛɛ be lɛ ateŋ ekome mli, ni amrɔ nɔŋŋ lɛ ekɛwoɔ mli bibioo. Shikpɔŋ nɔ loofɔlɔ ko ni elaaje ni hiɛ tsitsii ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ fã gbɛ kɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni afee lɛ nyɔŋ: kɛ right-whale wui ni atsɔ mli ni he tse, kɛ sperm whale shwuɔ wui ni akɛkpɛtɛɔ he lɛ, kapintɛfonyo lɛ feɔ ŋtayaa ko ni tamɔ pagoda kɛhaa lɛ. Mɔ ko ni fãa ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ shwieɔ enine nɔ: kapintɛfonyo lɛ feɔ tsofa ko ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli. Stubb shwe waa akɛ afee ŋulamii tsuji yɛ e-oar lɛ fɛɛ tsɔne lɛ tsɔne lɛ nɔ; ni ekɛ tsɔne lɛ eko fɛɛ eko shwieɔ etso agbo lɛ mli lɛ, kapintɛfonyo lɛ kɛ ŋulamii akuu lɛ haa yɛ gbɛ ni sa nɔ. Ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko sumɔɔ ni ewo toi ni akɛ shark wu fee lɛ: kapintɛfonyo lɛ shwieɔ etoi. Mɔ kroko hu yɛ ŋmei: kapintɛfonyo lɛ jieɔ pincers, ni ekɛ enine kome tswaa esɛi lɛ nɔ ni ekɛɛ lɛ akɛ eta jɛmɛ; shi ohiafo lɛ ŋmɔɔ yɛ opireshɛn ni agbeko naa lɛ shishi ni anyɛɛɛ akudɔ lɛ; ni ekɛ ehe miibɔle e-vice ni akɛ tso fee lɛ nine lɛ he lɛ, kapintɛfonyo lɛ feɔ lɛ okadi koni etswa etsitsi yɛ nakai mli, kɛji ebaasumɔ ni ejie ŋmei lɛ.

No hewɔ lɛ, asaa kapintɛfonyo nɛɛ he yɛ he fɛɛ he, ni nakai nɔŋŋ esusuuu he ni ebɛ bulɛ yɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ mli. Ebu nyanyɔji akɛ shwuɔ wui bibii; yitsei ni esusu shi moŋ top-blocks; hii lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehiɛ amɛ mlɛo akɛ kapstans. Shi yɛ be mli ni amrɔ nɛɛ eyɔɔ ŋmɔ ko ni da waa ni atsu he nii yɛ gbɛi srɔtoi anɔ ni ekɛ ŋaalee ni yɔɔ wala mli tamɔ nɛkɛ hu yɔɔ mli lɛ; etamɔ nɔ ni enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ miije nilee mli hewalɛ ko ni afɔɔɔɔ namɔ he ŋwane. Shi jeee nakai pɛpɛɛpɛ. Ejaakɛ nɔ ko bɛ ni sa kadimɔ waa fe nɛkɛ nuu nɛɛ, fe ŋmɔlɔ ko ni bɛ gbɔmɔtso mli tamɔ bɔ ni eji lɛ; nɔ ni bɛ gbɔmɔtso, mikɛɛ; ejaakɛ etsɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ kɛtee nibii ni bɛ naagbee ni ebɔle ehe lɛ amli, ni etamɔ nɔ ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni anaa yɛ jeŋ fɛɛ ni anaa lɛ mli; ni yɛ be mli ni etsuɔ ​​nii ni ekpaaa yɛ gbɛi ni anyɛɛɛ akane nɔ lɛ, ehiɛɔ etoiŋjɔlɛ mli lolo kɛya naanɔ, ni ekuɔ ehiɛ eshwieɔ onɔ, eyɛ mli akɛ otsaa shishitoo kɛha sɔlemɔtsui wuji moŋ. Shi kɛlɛ, nɛkɛ fã-kɛ-fã ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɛɛ yɛ emli, ni ekɔɔ, taakɛ etamɔ lɛ, tsui ni bɛ ni tsɔɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ mli lɛ hu he;—shi kɛlɛ, egbɛ eshwa yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ yɛ bei komɛi amli, kɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni etsɛ, ni tamɔ tso, ni yɔɔ nu afua lɛ hiɛ, ni ŋmɔlɔ yɔɔ mli, ni jeee nɔ ni bɛ ŋmɔlɔ amrɔ nɛɛ kɛ agbɛnɛ kɛ ŋmɔlɔ ko; nɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni baanyɛ atsu nii koni akɛho be lɛ mli yɛ gbɛkɛ teŋ buu ni yɔɔ Noa adeka lɛ hiɛ ni yɔɔ tsitsii lɛ nɔ lɛ mli. Ani eji akɛ nɛkɛ kapintɛfonyo onukpa nɛɛ ji mɔ ko ni fãa gbɛ yɛ ewala be fɛɛ mli, ni egbɛfaa babaoo, kɛyaa kɛbaa lɛ, jeee akɛ ebuaaa ŋmɔtɔ ko naa kɛkɛ; shi nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, eji ejie kponɔgbɛ nibii bibii fɛɛ ni baanyɛ afee nɔ ni kɔɔ ehe kɛjɛ shishijee lɛ? Eji mɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ; integral ni ajaaa mli; ni ekɛ ehe wooo mli tamɔ abifao ni afɔ lɛ ehee; ni amɛhiɔ shi ni amɛsusuuu je nɛɛ loo nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ he momo. Ekolɛ eshwɛ fioo ni obaakɛɛ, akɛ nɛkɛ naakpɛɛ nifeemɔ ni bɛ mli nɛɛ kɔɔ nilee ni bɛ mli ko he; ejaakɛ yɛ enitsumɔi babaoo lɛ amli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni etsuuu nii babaoo nakai kɛtsɔ jwɛŋmɔ loo fɔmɔ su nɔ, loo akɛni atsɔɔ lɛ nii kɛkɛ hewɔ, loo kɛtsɔ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ ni akɛfutu mli, po loo nɔ ni ejaaa nɔ; shi moŋ kɛtsɔ mumuii kɛ mumuii, nifeemɔ diɛŋtsɛ ko ni baa trukaa kɛkɛ nɔ. Eji mɔ ko ni tsuɔ nii yɛ gbɛ krɔŋŋ nɔ; kɛji akɛ eyɛ eko pɛŋ lɛ, esa akɛ ejwɛŋmɔ lɛ atsɔ mli mra kɛbote eniji ahe fãji lɛ amli. Etamɔ mɛi ni bɛ jwɛŋmɔ shi amɛhe yɛ sɛɛnamɔ waa lolo lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome, multum in parvo , Sheffield tsɔnei, ni esusuɔ akɛ kponɔgbɛ—eyɛ mli akɛ efu fioo moŋ—ji kotoku mli tsɔne ni afɔɔ nitsumɔ; shi jeee ŋmeii ni dalɛi srɔtoi yɔɔ mli pɛ yɔɔ mli, shi moŋ skru-draiva, kɔk-skru, tweezers, awls, pens, rulers, nail-filers, countersinkers hu yɔɔ mli. No hewɔ lɛ, kɛji akɛ e-nukpai lɛ miisumɔ ni amɛkɛ kapintɛfonyo lɛ atsu nii kɛfee skru-draiva lɛ, nɔ pɛ ni esa akɛ amɛfee ji ni amɛgbele nakai fã lɛ, ni skru lɛ yaa oya: loo kɛji akɛ eji tweezers lɛ, amɛwo lɛ enaji anɔ, ni amɛyɔɔ jɛmɛ.

Shi, taakɛ atsɔɔ mli kɛtsɔ hiɛ lɛ, nɛkɛ kapintɛfonyo ni yɔɔ nibii fɛɛ, ni gbeleɔ ehe ni egbeleɔ ehe nɛɛ jeee tsɔne ko kɛkɛ ni tsuɔ nii diɛŋtsɛ. Kɛji akɛ ebɛ susuma kome yɛ emli lɛ, no lɛ eyɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ tsuɔ enitsumɔ yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ. Nɔ ni no ji, kɛji eji quicksilver mli nu, loo hartshorn mli nu fioo ko lɛ, mɔ ko mɔ ko nyɛŋ atsɔɔ. Shi jɛmɛ ji he ni eyɔɔ; ni ehi jɛmɛ aaafee afii nyɔŋmai ekpaa loo nɔ ni fe nakai bianɛ. Queequeg ohiafo! beni lɛlɛ lɛ mli etse aaafee fã lɛ, kulɛ esa akɛ okoto lɛlɛ lɛ naa, ni okwɛ lɛ shi yɛ jɛmɛ; he ni, ejie ehe atadei kɛtee etsɔnei ni akɛ kooloo yitsɔi fee lɛ amli lɛ, kooloo ni atɛŋ ehe mfoniri lɛ miiho yɛ nakai ŋmɔtɔ kɛ ŋmɔtɔ lɛ mli, tamɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni yɔɔ nu bu ko shishi lɛ. Ni nu bu, loo ais-shĩa, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, ejie ehe kpo etsɔɔ lɛ akɛ eji wɔŋjalɔ ohiafo; he ni, nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ji akɛ, yɛ e-sweating lɛ mli latsaa lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, emɔ fɛ̃i ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni tsɔ atridii mli; ni naagbee lɛ, yɛ gbii komɛi ni ekɛye amanehulu lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ lɛ fɔ e-hammock lɛ mli, ni ebɛŋkɛ gbele shinaa lɛ naa diɛŋtsɛ. Bɔ ni efite ni efite yɛ nakai gbii fioo ni heɔ be kakadaŋŋ lɛ amli, kɛyashi etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ko fioo pɛ eshwɛ lɛ ja egbɔmɔtso lɛ kɛ ehe mfoniri ni ekɛwo ehe lɛ. Shi beni nibii krokomɛi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ emli lɛ miigbɔjɔ, ni etsitsii ahe wui lɛ miifee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ehiŋmɛii lɛ, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, miifee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni ehiɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ; amɛbatsɔ mɛi ni yɔɔ blɛoo ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni kpɛɔ; ni ekɛ mlijɔlɛ shi ekwɛ bo yɛ jɛmɛ kɛjɛ ehela lɛ mli, naakpɛɛ odaseyeli ni kɔɔ nakai hewalɛ ni gbooo ni yɔɔ emli ni nyɛŋ agbo, loo egbɔjɔ lɛ he. Ni tamɔ kooloi ni yɔɔ nu lɛ nɔ, ni beni amɛgbɔjɔɔ lɛ, amɛgbɛɔ amɛshwaa; no hewɔ lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ehiŋmɛii lɛ miibɔle amɛhe ni amɛmiibɔle amɛhe, tamɔ Naanɔ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai lɛ. Gbeyee ko ni anyɛɛɛ atsɛ gbɛi baaju bo beni ota nɛkɛ kooloo fɔŋ ni miigbɔjɔ nɛɛ masɛi lɛ, ni ona nibii ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ yɛ ehiɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ni ji mɛi ni kwɛɔ jɛmɛ beni Zoroaster gbo lɛ na lɛ. Ejaakɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni ji naakpɛɛ kɛ gbeyei diɛŋtsɛ yɛ gbɔmɔ mli lɛ, akɛwoko wiemɔi loo woji amli lolo. Ni Gbele ni bɛŋkɛɔ, ni kɛ fɛɛ feɔ ekome lɛ haa mɛi fɛɛ naa kpɛɔ amɛhe kɛ naagbee kpojiemɔ ko, ni woloŋmalɔ ko ni jɛ gbohii ateŋ pɛ baanyɛ akɛɛ bɔ ni sa. No hewɔ lɛ—ha wɔkɛɛ ekoŋŋ—Kaldeanyo loo Helanyo ko ni miigbo lɛ bɛ susumɔi ni nɔ kwɔlɔ ni yɔɔ krɔŋkrɔŋ fe mɛi ni ona amɛteemɔŋ ŋmɔlɔi lɛ ni amɛmiiho kɛmiitsɔ Queequeg ohiafo lɛ hiɛ nɔ lɛ, beni ekã shi dioo yɛ e-hammock ni miihoso lɛ mli lɛ, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ŋshɔ ni miihoso lɛ miihoso lɛ blɛoo kɛmiiya enaagbee hejɔɔmɔhe ni nɔ kwɔ fe lɛ, ni e-ocean’s flood- ŋwɛi ni ato he gbɛjianɔ.

Jeee lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ ateŋ mɔ ko shi ekɛ lɛ ha; ni yɛ Queequeg diɛŋtsɛ gbɛfaŋ lɛ, nɔ ni esusuɔ yɛ esane lɛ he lɛ ji nɔ ni ejie lɛ kpo yɛ yiwalɛ gbɛ nɔ kɛtsɔ ejurɔfeemɔ ko ni ebi ni ekɛtao ele nɔ ni yaa nɔ lɛ nɔ. Etsɛ mɔ kome kɛba eŋɔɔ yɛ leebi ŋmɛlɛtswaa ni mli edɔ lɛ mli, beni gbi lɛ jeɔ shishi kɛkɛ lɛ, ni emɔ enine mli, ni ekɛɛ akɛ beni eyɔɔ Nantucket lɛ, ená hegbɛ ena lɛlɛi bibii komɛi ni akɛ tsei ni tsuɔ nii yɛ mli fee, tamɔ tawuu tsei ni yɔɔ emaŋ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ; ni beni ebi lɛ saji lɛ, ekase akɛ, akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi fɛɛ ni gboi yɛ Nantucket lɛ, wo nakai lɛlɛi ni yɔɔ duŋ lɛ nɔŋŋ amli, ni akɛ, bɔ ni akɛ amɛ wo nakai lɛlɛi lɛ ahe fɛo lɛ eha ená he miishɛɛ waa; ejaakɛ esoro lɛ kwraa yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eweku lɛ kusum nifeemɔ he, ni beni ekɛ tsofa wo tatsɛ ko ni egbo lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ lɛlɛ lɛ shwieɔ enɔ, ni no hewɔ lɛ eshiɔ lɛ koni ekɛ lɛ aya ŋshɔkpɔi ni ŋulamii eyi nɔ obɔ lɛ anɔ; ejaakɛ jeee akɛ amɛheɔ amɛyeɔ akɛ ŋulamii lɛ ji ŋshɔkpɔi kɛkɛ, shi moŋ akɛ, amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ ŋshɔi ni mli jɔ ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko lɛ, kɛ ŋwɛi bluu lɛ hoɔ; ni nakai amɛfeɔ milky way lɛ mli ŋmeii yɛŋ lɛ. Ekɛfata he akɛ, ekpokpo beni esusuɔ akɛ abaafu lɛ yɛ e-hammock lɛ mli, taakɛ ŋshɔ mli kusum ni afɔɔ feemɔ lɛ ji lɛ, ni ashɛrɛ ashwie tamɔ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei aha ŋshɔŋlooi ni yeɔ gbele lɛ. Dabi: esumɔɔ lɛlɛ ko tamɔ Nantucket nɔ lɛ, ni ehi jogbaŋŋ kɛha lɛ waa, akɛni eji ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsulɔ hewɔ lɛ, akɛ tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ nɛɛ nɛkɛ adeka-lɛlɛi nɛɛ bɛ ŋshɔŋlooi; eyɛ mli akɛ no biɔ kudɔmɔ ni bɛ shiŋŋ, kɛ gbɛ babaoo ni tsɔɔ afii ni mli ewo duŋ lɛ amli moŋ.

Agbɛnɛ, beni ajie shihilɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɛɛ kpo yɛ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ, afã kapintɛfonyo lɛ amrɔ nɔŋŋ koni efee Queequeg famɔ lɛ, nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni baanyɛ afata he. Ayɛ tsei momo komɛi ni tamɔ gbonyo adeka yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni yɛ gbɛfaa kakadaŋŋ ko ni eho lɛ mli lɛ, afo kɛjɛ Lackaday ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ aboriginal tsei lɛ amli, ni nɛkɛ tsei ni tsuɔ diŋ nɛɛ ji nɔ ni atsɔɔ akɛ akɛfee gbonyo adeka lɛ. Etsɛɛɛ ni kapintɛfonyo lɛ le famɔ lɛ he sane, fe ni eŋɔ emla lɛ, ni amrɔ nɔŋŋ kɛ esubaŋ ni bɛ he miishɛɛ lɛ fɛɛ, tee nɔ ebote tsɔne ni akɛtsɔɔ hiɛ lɛ mli ni ekɛ Queequeg susumɔ lɛ tsu nii pɛpɛɛpɛ, ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ shwieɔ Queequeg gbɔmɔtso lɛ nɔ daa beni etsakeɔ mla lɛ.

Long Island ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ shwie shi akɛ: “Ah! ohiafo! Esa akɛ egbo bianɛ.”

Beni kapintɛfonyo lɛ yaa esɛɛyelɔ sɛi lɛ nɔ lɛ, koni ekɛha ehiɛ ahi ehe nɔ ni etsɔɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ mli lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ ekɛ tsɔne lɛ susuɔ bɔ ni esa akɛ gbonyo adeka lɛ kɛlɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ lɛ he yɛ nɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ eha tsɔne lɛ sɛɛ tsɛɔ daa kɛtsɔ ŋtayaai enyɔ ni efoɔ yɛ enaagbee lɛ nɔ. Beni efee enɛ lɛ, eto tsei lɛ kɛ edɛŋdadei lɛ ahe gbɛjianɔ, ni ekɛtsu nii.

Beni atswa naagbee ŋmei lɛ, ni ato naa lɛ he gbɛjianɔ jogbaŋŋ ni ekɛwo mli lɛ, ekɛ gbonyo adeka lɛ wo etsitsi nɔ blɛoo ni ekɛtee hiɛ, ni ebi kɛji amɛfee klalo kɛha lɛ lolo yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ.

Beni Queequeg nu bɔ ni mɛi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ lɛ bɔi gbonyo adeka lɛ shwiemɔ kɛyaa lɛ, ni eha mɔ fɛɛ mɔ he fee lɛ yaa lɛ, efã akɛ akɛ nɔ lɛ aba eŋɔɔ amrɔ nɔŋŋ, ni mɔ ko mɔ ko hu bɛ ni baakpoo lɛ; ni ena akɛ, yɛ gbɔmɛi ni gboiɔ lɛ fɛɛ ateŋ lɛ, gbɔmɛi komɛi ni miigboi lɛ ji yiwalɔi fe fɛɛ; ni lɛɛlɛŋ, akɛni etsɛŋ ni amɛbaahao wɔ fioo kɛya naanɔ hewɔ lɛ, esa akɛ ajie ohiafoi lɛ akpo.

Queequeg ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ e-hammock lɛ nɔ lɛ kwɛ gbonyo adeka lɛ kɛ hiŋmɛi ni kwɛɔ nii jogbaŋŋ be kakadaŋŋ. Kɛkɛ ni etsɛ e-harpoon lɛ, ni eha ajie tso ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ kɛjɛ mli, ni no sɛɛ lɛ eha akɛ dade fã lɛ wo gbonyo adeka lɛ mli kɛfata elɛlɛ lɛ mli tsɔnei lɛ eko he. No sɛɛ lɛ, yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ebimɔ naa lɛ, no sɛɛ lɛ, abɔleɔ biskit he yɛ mligbɛ: akɛ nu kpakpa ni akɛwoɔ tsɛnsi mli lɛ woɔ eyitso, ni akɛ shikpɔŋ bibioo ko ni akɛ tso fee lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ mli yɛ najiaŋ; ni akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ he mama ko miibɔle ehe koni akɛfee tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ, Queequeg kpa fai agbɛnɛ koni awo lɛ nɔ kɛya enaagbee saatso lɛ nɔ, koni enyɛ eka ehe miishɛjemɔi lɛ ekwɛ, kɛji akɛ eyɛ eko. Eka shi ni eshiuuu ehe minitii fioo komɛi, kɛkɛ ni ekɛɛ mɔ kome akɛ eya ebaagi lɛ mli ni ekɛ enyɔŋmɔ bibioo, Yojo lɛ aba. Kɛkɛ ni ekɛ eniji efo etsitsi nɔ ni Yojo kã eteŋ, ni etsɛ koni akɛ gbonyo adeka lɛ naa (hatch ni etsɛɔ lɛ) lɛ awo enɔ. Yitso lɛ fã lɛ tsɔ ehe kɛ hewolo nɔ tsɔne ko, ni Queequeg kã egbonyo adeka lɛ mli ni nɔ ko fioo pɛ enaa ja ehiɛ ni yɔɔ kpoo lɛ pɛ. “Rarmai” (ebaatsu nii; eyɛ mlɛo), ewie huhuuhu yɛ naagbee, ni ekɛ enine wo shishi koni atsake lɛ yɛ e-hammock lɛ mli.

Shi dani enɛ baafee lɛ, Pip, ni kɛ ŋmɔlɔ shwie ehe yɛ be nɛɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ tsi ebɛŋkɛ lɛ yɛ he ni ekã lɛ, ni ekɛ yaafo ni yɔɔ blɛoo mɔ enine mli; yɛ ekroko lɛ mli lɛ, ehiɛ e-tambu lɛ.

"Ohiafo ni fãa gbɛ! Ani nyɛfeŋ nɛkɛ gbɛfaa ni egbɔjɔ nɛɛ fɛɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ? Nɛgbɛ nyɛyaa bianɛ? Shi kɛji nu lɛ hoɔ nyɛ kɛyaa Antilles gɔji ni ŋɔɔ lɛ anɔ, he ni ŋshɔnaa tsei pɛ etswa lɛ, ani nyɛbaatsu nitsumɔ bibioo ko oha mi? Taomɔ Pip kome, ni elaaje be kakadaŋŋ: Misusuɔ akɛ eyɛ An comfort himn for her tild; awerɛho sane waa; ejaakɛ kwɛmɔ, eshi e-tambu lɛ yɛ sɛɛ;

"Minu," Starbuck wie huhuuhu, ni ekwɛ ŋshɔ lɛ shishi, "akɛ yɛ yiwalɛ atridii amli lɛ, gbɔmɛi, ni amɛleee nii fɛɛ, ewie blema wiemɔi amli; ni akɛ, kɛji atao teemɔŋ sane lɛ mli lɛ, ebafeɔ be fɛɛ be akɛ yɛ amɛgbekɛbiiashi ni amɛhiɛ ekpa nɔ kwraa lɛ, awie nakai blema wiemɔi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ yɛ amɛtoi mli kɛtsɔ mihenumɔi ni mli wa waa anɔ. yɛ e-lunacy lɛ mli ŋɔɔmɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɛɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ ŋwɛi woji ni kɔɔ wɔ ŋwɛi shiai fɛɛ ahe lɛ baa Nɛgbɛ ekase nakai, shi jɛmɛ?—Ewieɔ ekoŋŋ: shi ewieɔ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɔ fe tsutsu lɛ.”

“Nyɛfea enyɔ kɛ enyɔ! Ha wɔfee lɛ Tatsɛ! Ho, nɛgbɛ e-harpoon lɛ yɔɔ? Nyɛkɛmaa biɛ.—Rig-a-dig, dig, dig! huzza! Oh kɛha shwɛmɔ kooloo bianɛ ni eta eyitso nɔ ni etswa! shwɛmɔ, shwɛmɔ, shwɛmɔ Pip, egbo gbeyeishelɔ fɛɛ a'shiver; gboɔ yɛ biɛ.Daabi, hiɛgbele aba amɛnɔ—Ha amɛyabote nu mli tamɔ Pip, ni jo foi kɛjɛ ŋshɔŋlooi a-lɛlɛ mli lɛ!”

Yɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ mli lɛ, Queequeg ka shi ni ekɛ ehiŋmɛii efo mli, tamɔ nɔ ni eyɛ lamɔ mli. Akɛ Pip tee, ni akɛ helatsɛ lɛ wo e-hammock lɛ najiaŋ.

Shi amrɔ nɛɛ ni etamɔ nɔ ni efee hesaamɔ fɛɛ hesaa kɛha gbele lɛ; amrɔ nɛɛ ni ana akɛ egbonyo adeka lɛ sa jogbaŋŋ lɛ, Queequeg bua ehe naa trukaa; etsɛɛɛ ni etamɔ nɔ ni kapintɛfonyo adeka lɛ he ehiaaa: ni no sɛɛ lɛ, beni mɛi komɛi jie amɛnaakpɛɛ ni amɛyɔɔ lɛ kpo lɛ, lɛ, yɛ shishinumɔ naa lɛ, ekɛɛ akɛ nɔ ni kɛ ehewalɛ trukaa lɛ ba ji enɛ;—yɛ be ko ni he hiaa waa mli lɛ, no mli lɛ ekai nitsumɔ bibioo ko ni etsuɔ ​​yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni eshiɔ ni etsuuu lɛ; ni no hewɔ lɛ etsake ejwɛŋmɔ yɛ gbele he: enyɛŋ egbo lolo, ekɛɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, amɛbi lɛ akɛ, kɛji akɛ ebaahi shi loo ebaagbo lɛ ji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ esuɔmɔnaa nii kɛ nɔ ni esumɔɔ. Eha hetoo, lɛɛlɛŋ. Yɛ wiemɔ kome mli lɛ, eji Queequeg henɔwomɔ, akɛ kɛji gbɔmɔ ko kpɛ eyiŋ akɛ ebaahi shi lɛ, hela kɛkɛ nyɛŋ agbe lɛ: nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ja ŋshɔŋloo ko, loo ahum ni naa wa, loo yiwalɛ, ni anyɛɛɛ aye nɔ, ni bɛ nilee ni fiteɔ nii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, srɔtofeemɔ ni sa kadimɔ nɛɛ yɛ kooloi ni yɔɔ gbeyei kɛ mɛi ni yɔɔ jeŋba kpakpa ateŋ; akɛ yɛ be mli ni helatsɛ ko ni yɔɔ jeŋba kpakpa lɛ baanyɛ aná nyɔji enumɔ ni ekɛmiiya ehe lɛ, kɛ aawie he lɛ, helatsɛ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ miihe ashɛ fã ekoŋŋ yɛ gbi kome mli. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ be kpakpa mli lɛ, mi-Queequeg lɛ ná hewalɛ; ni yɛ be kakadaŋŋ mli beni eta kɔɔyɔɔŋ tsɔne lɛ nɔ gbii fioo komɛi ni ebɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko (shi eyeɔ nii kɛ niyenii he akɔnɔ ni naa wa) lɛ, ete shi trukaa, ekɛ eniji kɛ enaji shwie shi, ekɛ ehe shwie shi jogbaŋŋ, ekɛ ehe shwie shi fioo, ni no sɛɛ lɛ efo kɛbote lɛlɛ ni ewo nɔ lɛ yitso mli, nɔmɔ.

Agbɛnɛ ekɛ egbonyo adeka lɛ fee ŋshɔ-adeka; ni ekɛ e-kanvas atadei kotoku lɛ wo mli, ni ekɛto he gbɛjianɔ yɛ jɛmɛ. Ekɛ ŋmɛlɛtswai babaoo ni ekɛtsuuu nii, yɛ ŋwɛi ŋta lɛ he mfonirii kɛ mfonirii ni yɔɔ gbeyei srɔtoi fɛɛ ni ekɛkpɛɔ lɛ mli; ni etamɔ nɔ ni kɛtsɔ enɛ nɔ lɛ, eebɔ mɔdɛŋ, yɛ egbɛ ni bɛ bulɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ fãi komɛi ni atɛŋ ni atɛŋ yɛ egbɔmɔtso lɛ he lɛ afee ekome. Ni nɛkɛ mfoniri ni atɛŋ yɛ ehe lɛ ji gbalɔ kɛ ninalɔ ko ni egbo ni yɔɔ eŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ nitsumɔ, ni kɛtsɔ nakai hieroglyphic okadii lɛ anɔ lɛ eŋma ŋwɛi kɛ shikpɔŋ he susumɔ ni yeɔ emuu, kɛ teemɔŋ wolo ko ni kɔɔ bɔ ni anáa anɔkwale lɛ he ŋaa lɛ he; bɔni afee ni Queequeg yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ egbɔmɔtso lɛ mli lɛ ji naakpɛɛ sane ko ni esa akɛ ajie lɛ kpo; naakpɛɛ nitsumɔ ko ni yɔɔ wolokpo kome mli; shi ni lɛ diɛŋtsɛ po nyɛɛɛ akane eteemɔŋ saji lɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ etsui ni hiɛ kã lɛ tswaa amɛ; ni no hewɔ lɛ, ato teemɔŋ saji nɛɛ ahe gbɛjianɔ yɛ naagbee akɛ amɛkɛ wolokpo ni hiɛ ka ni aŋmala amɛ awo nɔ lɛ baatsɔ amɛhe, ni no hewɔ lɛ, abaanyɛ atsu he nii kɛyashi naagbee.
