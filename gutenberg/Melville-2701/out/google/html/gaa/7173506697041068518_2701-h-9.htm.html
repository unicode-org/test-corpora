YITSƆƆ 43. Bo!

"HIST! Ani onu nakai dede lɛ, Cabaco?"

No ji teŋgbɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ: nyɔŋtsere la ni hi jogbaŋŋ; ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi lɛ damɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔkpɔ ko nɔ, ni amɛjeɔ shishi kɛjɛɔ nu-ŋma ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ ateŋ ekome nɔ kɛyaa scuttle-butt ni bɛŋkɛ taffrail lɛ nɔ. Yɛ nɛkɛ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, amɛtsɔɔ baagi lɛ mli koni amɛkɛwo scuttle-butt lɛ mli. Beni amɛdamɔ shi, yɛ bei babaoo mli lɛ, yɛ ŋwɛitsu lɛ fã krɔŋkrɔŋ lɛ nɔ lɛ, amɛkwɛ jogbaŋŋ koni amɛkawie loo amɛkatswa amɛnaji. Kɛjɛ nine nɔ kɛtee nine nɔ lɛ, baagi lɛ tee dioofeemɔ ni mli kwɔ fe fɛɛ mli, ni lɛlɛ ko ni tswaa yɛ bei komɛi amli lɛ pɛ ji nɔ ni fiteɔ, kɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa ni yaa hiɛ ni ekpaaa lɛ gbee ni yaa nɔ daa lɛ.

Yɛ hejɔɔmɔ nɛɛ mli ji be ni Archy, ni ji cordon lɛ mlinyo kome, ni e-post lɛ bɛŋkɛ sɛɛ-hatches lɛ, wie wiemɔi ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi nɛɛ etsɔɔ ekutsoŋnyo, ni ji Cholonyo lɛ.

"Hist! Ani onu nakai dede lɛ, Cabaco?"

“Ŋɔɔ baagi lɛ, ani obaasumɔ, Archy? mɛɛ dede d’ye tsɔɔ?”

“Eyɛ jɛmɛ ekoŋŋ—yɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai lɛ ashishi—ani onuuu he—ŋɔɔmɔ—egbɛɛmɔ tamɔ ŋtayaa.”

"Kɔɔyɔɔ afolɔ enaa! Tsɔ nakai sɛɛkuu baagi lɛ he."

“Jɛmɛ ekoŋŋ—yɛ jɛmɛ!—egbɛɛmɔ tamɔ mɛi enyɔ loo etɛ ni ewɔ lɛ miitsɔ amɛhe, bianɛ!”

"Caramba! nyɛfee, lɛlɛ mlinyo, ani nyɛbaafee? Biskit etɛ ni afɔ mli ni nyɛyeɔ kɛha gbɛkɛ niyenii lɛ ji nɔ ni miitsake yɛ nyɛmli—nɔ kroko bɛ. Kwɛmɔ baagi lɛ mli!"

“Wiemɔ nɔ ni osumɔɔ, lɛlɛ mli hefatalɔ, miyɛ toi ni naa wa.”

“Aye, bo ji chap lɛ, aloo jeee nakai, ni nu Quakeress onukpa lɛ tsɔne ni akɛkpɛɔ nii lɛ gbee ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ kilomitai nyɔŋmai enumɔ kɛjɛ Nantucket lɛ; bo ji chap lɛ.”

"Kwɛmɔ shi; wɔbaakwɛ nɔ ni baaba. Bo, Cabaco, mɔ ko yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ sɛɛ ni anako lɛ lolo yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ; ni misusuɔ akɛ wɔ Mogul onukpa lɛ hu le nɔ ko yɛ he. Mi nu Stubb kɛɛ Flask, leebi ŋmɛlɛtswaa ko, akɛ nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli."

"Tish! baket lɛ!"





YITSƆƆ 44. Taomɔ wolo lɛ.

Eji onyiɛ Kapten Ahab sɛɛ kɛtee etsu lɛ mli yɛ ahum ni tswa yɛ gbɛkɛ ni ekɛ etsulɔi lɛ ye eyiŋtoo lɛ nɔ mi lɛ sɛɛ kulɛ, obaana lɛ ni etee tsɔne ko ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ mli, ni ekɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ mfonirii ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ eko ba, ni ekɛgbɛ eshwa ehiɛ yɛ ekpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ. Kɛkɛ ni kɛ́ eta ehiɛ lɛ, kulɛ obaana lɛ ni ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ miikase line kɛ shading srɔtoi ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ ni ekɛ ehiŋmɛi kpeɔ lɛ; ni okɛ pensil ni yɔɔ blɛoo shi ehiɔ shi shiŋŋ lɛ atsɔɔ gbɛ krokomɛi ni fata he yɛ hei ni tsutsu lɛ efee efolo lɛ anɔ. Yɛ be kɛ bei amli lɛ, ebaawie woji momoi ni ato he gbɛjianɔ ni yɔɔ emasɛi lɛ ahe, ni aŋmala bei kɛ hei ni, yɛ tsutsu lɛlɛi srɔtoi agbɛfaai srɔtoi amli lɛ, amɔmɔ loo ana sperm whales lɛ awo mli.

Beni akɛtsuɔ nii nakai lɛ, pewter kane ni tsii ni akɛwoɔ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai amli lɛ fɔ̃ɔ eyitso nɔ, ni ekpokpoɔ be fɛɛ be kɛ lɛlɛ lɛ nyiɛmɔ, ni ekɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai ni tsakeɔ kɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai shwieɔ ehiɛ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ nɔ kɛyaa naanɔ, aahu akɛ eshwɛ fioo ni ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni be mli ni lɛ diɛŋtsɛ hu ekadiɔ ŋtayaai kɛ gbɛjegbɛi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ni etsɔɔ ehiɛ he mfoniri ni akadi lɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ nɔ.

Shi jeee gbɛkɛ nɛɛ pɔtɛɛ ji be ni Ahab susu e-chart lɛ he yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ, yɛ etsu lɛ mli he ko ni eyɔɔ lɛ. Nɔ ni miihe ashɛ daa gbɛkɛ lɛ, ajieɔ amɛ kpo; nɔ ni miihe ashɛ daa gbɛkɛ lɛ, ajieɔ pensil he okadii komɛi, ni akɛ ekrokomɛi yeɔ najiaŋ. Ejaakɛ Ahab miitsɔ ŋshɔi ejwɛ lɛ fɛɛ ahe mfonirii ni yɔɔ ehiɛ lɛ anɔ, ni ekɛ esusuma he susumɔ ni kɔɔ mɔ kome he lɛ baatsu nii jogbaŋŋ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛha mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ni leee leviathan lɛ gbɛi lɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ, ekolɛ ebaafee tamɔ nitsumɔ ko ni hiɛnɔkamɔ bɛ mli ni jwɛŋmɔ bɛ mli ni no hewɔ lɛ aaatao adebɔɔ kome ni yɔɔ shibɔlemɔ ŋulami nɛɛ ŋshɔi ni bɛ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ amli. Shi etamɔɔɔ nɔ ni Ahab, ni le ŋshɔkei kɛ ŋshɔkei fɛɛ ni hoɔ lɛ ahe gbɛjianɔtoo lɛ jeee nakai; ni no hewɔ lɛ abuɔ bɔ ni sperm whale lɛ niyenii lɛ shwieɔ shi lɛ he akɔntaa; kɛ, agbɛnɛ hu, ni ekaiɔ be ni akɛtsuɔ nii daa, ni ana jogbaŋŋ ni akɛtaoɔ lɛ yɛ latitude pɔtɛɛ komɛi amli lɛ; baanyɛ ashɛ susumɔi ni jwɛŋmɔ yɔɔ mli ahe, ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaashɛ nɔmimaa he, yɛ gbi ni sa fe fɛɛ ni esa akɛ ehi shikpɔŋ nɛɛ loo nakai shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ koni eyatao nɔ ni ekɛbaagbe lɛ lɛ he.

Anɔkwale ni kɔɔ bɔ ni sperm whale lɛ kɛ ehe woɔ nu ni aha lɛ mli yɛ be kɛ bei amli lɛ he lɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ nɔmimaa waa, ni no hewɔ lɛ, gbɔbilɔi babaoo heɔ amɛyeɔ akɛ, ani abaanyɛ akwɛ lɛ jogbaŋŋ ni akase lɛ yɛ je lɛŋ he fɛɛ he; kɛji akɛ abua ŋshɔŋlooi akuu muu lɛ fɛɛ gbɛfaa kome he tsei lɛ anaa jogbaŋŋ lɛ, no lɛ abaana akɛ sperm whale lɛ fãmɔi lɛ kɛ herring-shoals loo ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ afãmɔ lɛ kpãa gbee yɛ tsakemɔ ni tsakeee mli. Yɛ nɛkɛ ŋaawoo nɛɛ nɔ lɛ, abɔ mɔdɛŋ ni afee sperm whale lɛ gbɛfaa he mfoniri ni mli ka shi faŋŋ.*

*Akɛni aŋma nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi nɛɛ hewɔ lɛ, akɛ miishɛɛ woɔ sane lɛ mli
kpo kɛtsɔ wolo ko ni Lieutenant Maury, ni jɛ
maŋ lɛ mli nibii ni akwɛɔ yɛ maŋ lɛ mli lɛ, Washington, yɛ April 16, afi 1851. Kɛtsɔ
nakai circular lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni chart ni tamɔ nɛkɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ mli
gbɛ ni atsɔɔ nɔ agbeɔ naa; ni akɛ emli fãi komɛi haa yɛ
kpokpaa lɛ nɔ. “Chart nɛɛ jaa ŋshɔ lɛ mli ewoɔ kpokpai amli
ni ji latitude digrii enumɔ kɛ longitude digrii enumɔ;
tsɔɔ kpokpai nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ lɛ eko fɛɛ eko mli
nyɔji nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ lɛ he ŋwɛitsu; kɛ ŋwɛigbɛ kɛtsɔ eko fɛɛ eko nɔ
ni kpokpai lɛ ji linei etɛ; ekome ni akɛtsɔɔ yibɔ lɛ
gbii ni akɛtsu nii yɛ nyɔŋ fɛɛ nyɔŋ nɔ yɛ nyɔŋ fɛɛ nyɔŋ nɔ
kpokpaa lɛ nɔ, kɛ ekrokomɛi enyɔ lɛ koni akɛtsɔɔ gbii abɔ ni yɔɔ mli
ni ŋshɔŋlooi, sperm loo ninejurɔ, ana.”

Kɛfata he lɛ, kɛ́ amɛmiifee gbɛ kɛmiijɛ niyelihe kome kɛmiiya ekroko mli lɛ, sperm whales lɛ, ni su ko ni tɔmɔ bɛ he kudɔɔ amɛ—kɛ wɔkɛɛ lɛ, teemɔŋ nilee ni jɛ Nyɔŋmɔyeli lɛ ŋɔɔ lɛ—bei pii lɛ amɛfoɔ yɛ lá fãji amli , taakɛ atsɛɔ amɛ lɛ; ni amɛtsa amɛ gbɛ nɔ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛ ko nɔ kɛ pɛpɛɛpɛfeemɔ ni sɛɛ efooo, ni lɛlɛ ko bɛ ni efã gbɛ pɛŋ, kɛtsɔ wolo ko nɔ, kɛ naakpɛɛ pɛpɛɛpɛfeemɔ mlijaa nyɔŋma mli ekome tamɔ nɛkɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ, yɛ shihilɛi nɛɛ amli lɛ, gbɛ ni ŋshɔŋloo kome tsɔɔ nɔ lɛ ja tamɔ mɔ ni kwɛɔ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ni eyɛ mli akɛ hiɛyaa lɛ ji nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ ajie lɛ kpo, shi kɛlɛ, gbɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni akɛɛ efoɔ yɛ nɛkɛ bei nɛɛ amli lɛ, bei pii lɛ, ehiɛɔ shitoi fioo komɛi ni bɛ presmo loo width ( kpaŋmɔ); shi eteee hiɛ fe bɔ ni ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛ lɛ yitsei lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ hiŋmɛi nɔ lɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ, kɛ́ ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ miiho kɛmiitsɔ majik he nɛɛ. Nɔ ni abua naa ji akɛ, yɛ bei pɔtɛɛ komɛi amli yɛ nakai ŋshɔ lɛ mli kɛ nakai gbɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛ hekɛnɔfɔɔ babaoo atao ŋshɔŋlooi ni fãa gbɛ lɛ.

Ni no hewɔ lɛ jeee bei ni atsɔɔ mli, yɛ niyelihei ni yɔɔ srɔtoi ni ale jogbaŋŋ lɛ pɛ mli Ahab baanyɛ ahiɛ ahi ehe nɔ akɛ ekɛ e-kooloi lɛ baakpe; shi yɛ nu ni mli kwɔ fe fɛɛ ni efo mli yɛ nakai shikpɔji lɛ ateŋ lɛ mli lɛ, ebaanyɛ, kɛtsɔ eŋaalee lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekɛ ehe awo he ni ekɛ be awo egbɛ nɔ, tamɔ nɔ ni no mli po lɛ, efeee mɔ ni bɛ kpee ko he gbɛkpamɔ kwraa.

Shihilɛ ko yɛ ni yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ kwɛmɔ mli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ egbɛjianɔtoo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ shi eji gbɛjianɔtoo lolo lɛ ebɔle ehe. Shi jeee nakai eji yɛ anɔkwale mli, ekolɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ gregarious sperm whales lɛ yɛ amɛ be ni amɛkɛtsuɔ nii daa kɛha shikpɔŋ pɔtɛɛ ko moŋ, shi yɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, onyɛŋ omu sane naa akɛ, kɛ wɔkɛɛ lɛ, tsinai akui ni shwere yɛ latitude loo longitude ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli yɛ afi nɛɛ mli lɛ baafee nɔ kome kɛ mɛi ni ana yɛ jɛmɛ yɛ be ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ mli lɛ; eyɛ mli akɛ bei komɛi yɛ ni yɔɔ srɔto ni sanebimɔ ko bɛ he ni nɔ ni teɔ shi woɔ enɛ lɛ ebafee anɔkwale moŋ. Yɛ gbɛ ni waaa nɔ lɛ, wiemɔ kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ, yɛ husu ni mli kwɔ fioo pɛ mli lɛ kɔɔ mɛi ni yɔɔ amɛkomekomei kɛ mɛi ni yɔɔ sperm whales ni eda ni amɛdara lɛ ateŋ lɛ ahe. No hewɔ lɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ ana Moby Dick yɛ tsutsu afi ko mli moŋ, shi ákɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ lɛ, yɛ he ni atsɛɔ lɛ Seychelle shikpɔŋ ni yɔɔ India ŋshɔ lɛ mli, loo Volcano Bay ni yɔɔ Japan Ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ nɔ; shi kɛlɛ enyiɛɛɛɛ sɛɛ, akɛ kɛji Pequod lɛ basara nakai hei lɛ eko yɛ be fɛɛ be ni kɛ lɛ kpãa gbee lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ lɛ baakpe yɛ jɛmɛ ni tɔmɔ ko bɛ he. Nakai eji yɛ niyelihei krokomɛi hu ahe, he ni ejie ehe kpo yɛ bei komɛi amli lɛ. Shi enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ tamɔ hei ni ekɛ ehe kpaa shi yɛ be fɛɛ mli kɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɔiatoohei pɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, shi jeee hei ni ehiɔ be kakadaŋŋ. Ni he ni awie hegbɛi ni Ahab yɔɔ akɛ ebaatsu eyiŋtoo lɛ he nii kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ, awieɔ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ, nɔ ni tsɔ hiɛ, gbɛkpamɔi krokomɛi ni eyɔɔ lɛ pɛ he, dani ashɛɔ be loo he pɔtɛɛ ko he, be mli ni nibii fɛɛ ni baanyɛ aba lɛ baatsɔ nɔ ni baanyɛ aba, ni, taakɛ Ahab susu he kɛ miishɛɛ lɛ, nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni baanyɛ aba lɛ baatsɔ nɔ ko ni nyiɛ sɛɛ . Akɛ nakai be kɛ he pɔtɛɛ ni ato lɛ fata he yɛ ŋaa wiemɔ kuku kome mli—Be-Nɔ-Line lɛ. Ejaakɛ jɛmɛ kɛ no sɛɛ lɛ, yɛ afii babaoo ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ amli lɛ, atsɔɔ Moby Dick mli yɛ be kɛ bei amli, ni ehiɔ nakai nui lɛ amli be fioo, beni hulu lɛ, yɛ e-afii sɛɛkuu lɛ mli lɛ, fãa gbɛ kɛyaa be ko ni atsɔɔ hiɛ atsɔɔ yɛ Zodiac okadi lɛ eko fɛɛ eko mli lɛ. Jɛmɛ hu ji he ni gbele ni akɛ ŋshɔŋloo yɛŋ lɛ kpeɔ lɛ ateŋ babaoo ba yɛ; jɛmɛ ji he ni agba enifeemɔi lɛ ahe saji yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli; nakai he ni yɔɔ awerɛho lɛ hu yɛ he ni nuumo ni yɔɔ jwɛŋmɔ kome lɛ na yiŋtoo ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni tsirɛ lɛ ni ekɛwo ehe nyɔmɔ lɛ. Shi yɛ bɔ ni Ahab kɛ esusuma ni miisusu nii ahe lɛ shwie nɛkɛ gbee ni sɛɛ efooo nɛɛ mli jogbaŋŋ lɛ mli lɛ, eŋmɛŋ gbɛ ni ekɛ ehiɛnɔkamɔi fɛɛ ama anɔkwale kome ni atsĩ tã yɛ yiteŋgbɛ nɛɛ nɔ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni ebaafee nɔ ni haa nakai hiɛnɔkamɔi lɛ feɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ha; ni asaŋ yɛ eshiwoo lɛ mli ni ewɔɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ, enyɛŋ eha etsui ni efee dioo lɛ afee dioo bɔ ni sa ni ekɛ taomɔ nibii fɛɛ ni kɛ ehe woɔ mli lɛ atsake.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, Pequod lɛ efã gbɛ kɛjɛ Nantucket yɛ Be ni Akɛtsuɔ Nii lɛ shishijee mli tɔ̃ɔ. No mli lɛ, mɔdɛŋbɔɔ ko bɛ ni baanyɛ aha e-tatsɛ lɛ anyɛ atsɔ gbɛ wulu lɛ nɔ kɛya anaigbɛ, ni ji Cape Horn ni ji shii enyɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ejo foi kɛya latitude digrii nyɔŋmai ekpaa kɛyashɛ equatorial Pasifik lɛ mli yɛ be ni sa mli koni efã gbɛ kɛya jɛmɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, esa akɛ emɛ be ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, ekolɛ Ahab hala ŋmɛlɛtswaa ni Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ fãa gbɛ lɛ jogbaŋŋ, kɛ susumɔ ni kɔɔ nibii ni yɔɔ srɔtoi nɛɛ diɛŋtsɛ he. Ejaakɛ, shwane kɛ gbɛkɛ ohai etɛ kɛ nyɔŋmai ekpaa kɛ enumɔ teŋgbɛ kã ehiɛ; be ko ni, yɛ nɔ najiaŋ ni ekɛ tsuishitoo baaŋmɛ etsui shi yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, ebaaye yɛ gbɔbimɔ nibii srɔtoi amli; kɛji akɛ ebalɛ akɛ White Whale, ni yeɔ ehejɔɔmɔ be lɛ yɛ ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ kɛjɛ he ni eyeɔ nii yɛ be kɛ bei amli lɛ, tsɔ ehiɛ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ nɔ kɛjɛ Persian Ŋshɔ lɛ naa, loo yɛ Bengal Ŋshɔ lɛ naa, loo China Ŋshɔi lɛ amli, loo yɛ nu kroko ni ehewolonɔ su lɛ yɔɔ mli lɛ mli. No hewɔ lɛ, Monsoons, Pampas, Nor’-Anaigbɛ, Harmattan, Jarayeli; kɔɔyɔɔ fɛɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ja Levanter kɛ Simoon lɛ, baanyɛ atswa Moby Dick kɛya Pequod ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ zig-zag ni yɔɔ shishiumɔ lɛ mli.

Shi enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ ni akɛhaa lɛ; shi kɛlɛ, kɛ akwɛ lɛ yɛ hiɛshikamɔ kɛ mlijɔlɛ mli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni jeee susumɔ ko ni yɔɔ sɛkɛ, enɛ; akɛ yɛ ŋshɔ ni mli kwɔ ni husu bɛ he lɛ mli lɛ, esa akɛ asusu akɛ ŋshɔŋloo kome pɛ, kɛji akɛ akɛ lɛ kpe po lɛ, ebaanyɛ eyoo lɛ aŋkroaŋkro kɛjɛ egbɔbilɔ lɛ dɛŋ, tamɔ Mufti ni hiɛ tsitsii yɛŋ po yɛ Constantinople gbɛjegbɛi wuji ni eyi obɔ kɛ gbɔmɛi lɛ anɔ? Hɛɛ. Ejaakɛ Moby Dick hiɛ ni yɔɔ snoo yɛŋ ni yɔɔ srɔto lɛ, kɛ etsitsi ni yɔɔ snoo yɛŋ lɛ nyɛŋ afee nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsake. Ni ani mikaneee ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, Ahab baawie etsɔɔ ehe, tamɔ nɔ ni beni ekwɛ ewoji lɛ amli kɛyashi be kakadaŋŋ sɛɛ lɛ, ebaashɛ ehe eshwie sɛɛ yɛ lamɔi amli—ekane lɛ, ni ani ebaajo foi? Etsitsii ni mli kwɔ lɛ egbɔjɔ, ni efo tamɔ too toi ni elaaje! Ni yɛ biɛ lɛ, ejwɛŋmɔ ni etsɔ shwilafo lɛ baaya nɔ yɛ foidamɔ ni mumɔ bɛ mli mli; kɛyashi tɔlɛ kɛ jwɛŋmɔŋ nijiaŋwujee ba enɔ; ni yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ kɔɔyɔɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ lɛ mli lɛ, ebaatao ni ená hewalɛ ekoŋŋ. Amrɔ nɛɛ, taakɛ tawuu lɛlɛ ko tsɔɔ Dardanelles lɛ mli oya lɛ, no hewɔ lɛ sperm whale baanyɛ, kɛtsɔ gbɛ kome nɔ nɔŋŋ nɔ, atsɔ Mediteranea Ŋshɔ lɛ mli kɛya Propontis lɛ mli.

Yɛ Propontis lɛ mli lɛ, bɔ ni manyɛ makase lɛ, anaaa nakai nɔ pɔtɛɛ ni atsɛɔ lɛ brit lɛ eko kwraa, ni ji right whale lɛ niyenii. Shi miyɛ yiŋtoo fɛɛ yiŋtoo ni madamɔ nɔ mahe maye akɛ sperm whale lɛ niyenii—squid loo cuttle-fish—hiɔ nakai ŋshɔ lɛ shishi, ejaakɛ ana bɔɔ nii wuji, shi jeee nakai ŋshɔ lɛ mli nɔ ni da fe fɛɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ. Belɛ, kɛji akɛ okɛ wiemɔi nɛɛ to he jogbaŋŋ, ni osusu he fioo lɛ, obaana faŋŋ akɛ, taakɛ adesai ajwɛŋmɔi fɛɛ tsɔɔ lɛ, esa akɛ Procopius ŋshɔ mli kooloo lɛ, ni kɛ afii ohai fã shã Roma Maŋtsɛ ko lɛlɛi lɛ, yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, esa akɛ efee sperm whale.





YITSƆƆ 46. Susumɔi.

Eyɛ mli akɛ, akɛni eyiŋtoo lɛ mli la ni tsoɔ lɛ efite lɛ hewɔ lɛ, Ahab yɛ ejwɛŋmɔi kɛ enifeemɔi fɛɛ amli lɛ esusu Moby Dick ni abaamɔ lɛ naagbee lɛ he pɛŋ; eyɛ mli akɛ etamɔ nɔ ni efee klalo akɛ ekɛ gbele mli nibii fɛɛ baasha afɔle eha nakai suɔmɔ kome lɛ moŋ; shi kɛlɛ ekolɛ eji akɛ, kɛjɛ adebɔɔ mli kɛ be kakadaŋŋ ni eyɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni la tsoɔ waa lɛ gbɛi ebote gbalashihilɛ mli tsɔ, ni ekpa gbɛfaa lɛ he sɛɛfimɔ lɛ kwraa. Aloo kɛ hoo lɛ, kɛji akɛ enɛ jeee nakai lɛ, no lɛ yiŋtoi krokomɛi ni yɔɔ hewalɛ fe lɛ lɛ ataooo. Ebaafee nɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli tsɔ, ekolɛ, kɛji akɛ asusu e-monomania lɛ he po lɛ, ni etsɔɔ akɛ ehe ni ekɛwoɔ White Whale lɛ he nyɔmɔ lɛ baanyɛ ashɛ sperm whales fɛɛ he yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, ni akɛ bɔ ni egbeɔ kooloi wuji babaoo lɛ, nakai ebaaha hegbɛi ni eyɔɔ akɛ ekɛ amɛteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ baakpe lɛ yaa hiɛ. Shi kɛji akɛ susumɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ srɔto diɛŋtsɛ lɛ, no lɛ nibii krokomɛi yɛ ni esa akɛ asusu he lolo ni, eyɛ mli akɛ ekɛ enɔyeli he suɔmɔ lɛ kpãaa gbee kwraa moŋ, shi amɛnyɛŋ amɛtsirɛ lɛ kwraa.

Bɔni afee ni Ahab atsu eyiŋtoo lɛ he nii lɛ, esa akɛ ekɛ dɛŋdadei atsu nii; ni yɛ dɛŋdadei fɛɛ ni akɛtsuɔ nii yɛ nyɔŋtsere lɛ shishi lɛ ateŋ lɛ, gbɔmɛi ji mɛi ni he esa fe fɛɛ ni amɛjeɔ gbɛjianɔtoo mli. Ákɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ lɛ, ele akɛ bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni ehiɛyaa lɛ yɛ gbɛi komɛi anɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ Starbuck nɔ lɛ, shi kɛlɛ nakai hiɛyaa lɛ haaaa mumɔŋ gbɔmɔ ni yeɔ emuu lɛ nɔ tamɔ bɔ ni gbɔmɔtsoŋ nɔyeli kɛkɛ kɔɔ nilee mli nɔyeli he lɛ; ejaakɛ kɛha mumɔŋ nɔ pɛ lɛ, nilee shi damɔ gbɔmɔtsoŋ wekukpaa ko mli. Starbuck gbɔmɔtso lɛ kɛ Starbuck suɔmɔnaa nii ni anyɛɔ nɔ lɛ ji Ahab nɔ, be fɛɛ be ni Ahab kɛ emagnet lɛ baahi Starbuck jwɛŋmɔ mli lɛ; loloolo lɛ ele akɛ yɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ hewɔ lɛ, hefatalɔ nukpa lɛ, yɛ esusuma mli lɛ, nyɛɔ e-kapita lɛ taomɔ nii lɛ, ni kɛji ebaanyɛ, ekɛ miishɛɛ baagbala ehe kɛjɛ mli, loo ebaafite lɛ po. Ekolɛ be kakadaŋŋ baaho dani ana White Whale lɛ. Yɛ nakai be kakadaŋŋ lɛ mli lɛ, Starbuck baafee mɔ ni sa akɛ ebaagbee atuatsemɔ ni teɔ shi ewoɔ e-kapita lɛ hiɛnyiɛmɔ lɛ mli ekoŋŋ, ja akɛ hewalɛ komɛi ni yɔɔ mlɛo, ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ, ni jɛɔ shihilɛ mli baa lɛ ba enɔ. Jeee nakai pɛ, shi moŋ Ahab shwilafofeemɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni ekɛbuɔ Moby Dick lɛ jie ehe kpo yɛ gbɛ ni sa kadimɔ waa nɔ fe eshishinumɔ ni nɔ kwɔ kɛ nilee ni eyɔɔ yɛ etsɔɔ hiɛ akɛ, kɛha ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ lɛ, esa akɛ ajie nakai jwɛŋmɔŋ nifeemɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni kɛ ehe wo mli lɛ kɛjɛ gbɔbimɔ lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ; akɛ esa akɛ ajie gbɛfaa lɛ mli gbeyeishemɔ muu lɛ fɛɛ kɛya sɛɛgbɛ ni bɛ faŋŋ lɛ mli (ejaakɛ hii fioo ko pɛ yɔɔ ekãa ji odaseyeli ni teɔ shi ewoɔ jwɛŋmɔ ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ ni nifeemɔ haaa mɔ he ajɔ lɛ); akɛ kɛ́ amɛdamɔ amɛ gbɛkɛ bulɔi kakadaŋŋ lɛ, esa akɛ esraafoi lɛ kɛ ehii lɛ aná nibii komɛi ni bɛŋkɛ amɛ ni amɛsusu he fe Moby Dick. Ejaakɛ bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ kɛ miishɛɛ kɛ oyaiyeli jie etaomɔ lɛ he adafitswaa lɛ yi lɛ; shi kɛlɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi fɛɛ ji mɛi ni feɔ amɛnii yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ, ni anyɛɛɛ akɛ he afɔ̃ amɛnɔ—amɛhiɔ shi yɛ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔi ni yɔɔ kponɔgbɛ lɛ amli, ni amɛmuɔ emli tsakemɔi lɛ—ni kɛ́ akɛ amɛ tee nɔ ko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko yɛ sɛɛtiumɔ mli lɛ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, esa akɛ shiwoo ni kɔɔ wala kɛ suɔmɔ he yɛ naagbee lɛ atsu nii, shi nɔ ni fe fɛɛ ji nibii ni he hiaa ni akɛ he woɔ mli. ajie kɛjɛ mli kɛha naagbee dash lɛ.

Ni jeee akɛ Ahab susuuu nɔ kroko hu he. Yɛ henumɔi ni mli wa bei amli lɛ adesai buɔ shishijee susumɔi fɛɛ akɛ nɔ ko; shi bei ni tamɔ nɛkɛ lɛ hoɔ oya. Ahab susu akɛ, shihilɛ ni yɔɔ gbɔmɔ ni afee lɛ lɛ mli daa lɛ ji mujiwoo. Kɛji akɛ akpɛlɛ nɔ akɛ White Whale lɛ kanyaa mi kuu ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɛɛ atsui kwraa, ni amɛshwɛmɔ ni akɛshwɛɔ lɛ haa amɛfeɔ mɛi ni feɔ mɛi awuiyelɔi lɛ, shi kɛlɛ, yɛ be mli ni amɛtiuɔ Moby Dick yɛ suɔmɔ ni amɛyɔɔ lɛ hewɔ lɛ, esa akɛ amɛná niyenii hu kɛha amɛ daa gbi niyenii ni amɛsumɔɔ waa lɛ. Ejaakɛ blema Krusebii ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ni amɛhe wa waa lɛ po tsui enyɔɔɔ amɛmli akɛ amɛbaafo shikpɔŋ ni kɛlɛ ji kilomitai akpei enyɔ koni amɛyawu amɛha amɛ gbonyobu krɔŋkrɔŋ lɛ, ni amɛfeee julɔi, amɛfooo kotokui amli, ni amɛnáaa Nyɔŋmɔjamɔ he nibii krokomɛi yɛ gbɛ nɔ. Eji akɛ amɛhiɛ amɛnaagbee nɔ kome kɛ suɔmɔ he lɛ mli pɛpɛɛpɛ—nakai naagbee kɛ suɔmɔ nɔ lɛ kulɛ, mɛi babaoo tsɔ baatsɔ amɛhe kɛ heguɔgbee. Ahab susu akɛ, mijieŋ shika he hiɛnɔkamɔi fɛɛ kɛjɛŋ hii nɛɛ adɛŋ—hɛɛ, shika. Ekolɛ amɛbaabu shika he guɔ bianɛ; shi ha nyɔji komɛi atsɔ, ni shiwoo ko bɛ he aha amɛ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ, nɛkɛ shika nɛɛ nɔŋŋ ni yɔɔ dioo lɛ fɛɛ baate shi shikome yɛ amɛmli, etsɛŋ ni nɛkɛ shika nɛɛ nɔŋŋ baafee shikatoohe Ahab.

Ni asaŋ, yiŋtoo kroko ni kɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ he ni kɔɔ Ahab diɛŋtsɛ he lɛ hu bɛ. Akɛni Ahab jie yiŋtoo titri ni yɔɔ Pequod lɛ gbɛfaa lɛ sɛɛ shi eji teemɔŋ sane lɛ kpo yɛ oyaiyeli mli, ni ekolɛ ejie yiŋtoo titri ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ lɛ kpo mra fioo hewɔ lɛ, yɛ nakai feemɔ mli lɛ, ejie ehe kpo yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ kɛha fɔbuu ni anyɛɛɛ aha hetoo akɛ eji maŋ lɛ nɔyeli lɛ; ni kɛ toigbalamɔ ni yeɔ emuu, yɛ jeŋba kɛ mla gbɛfaŋ lɛ, kɛji akɛ egbɛfalɔi lɛ sumɔɔ nakai, ni amɛhe esa kɛha nakai yiŋtoo lɛ, amɛbaanyɛ amɛkpoo toiboo kroko fɛɛ ni amɛbaafee amɛha lɛ lɛ, ni amɛkɛ yiwalɛ po ajie famɔ lɛ kɛjɛ edɛŋ. Kɛjɛ nɔ ni awieɔ he fioo akɛ eji nɔ ni aŋɔ lɛ po nɔ, kɛ nɔ ni baanyɛ ajɛ susumɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni atsi naa lɛ mli aba lɛ mli lɛ, ekolɛ Ahab ji mɔ ni hao fe fɛɛ akɛ ebaabu ehe. Nakai hebuu lɛ baanyɛ ahi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ejwɛŋmɔ kɛ etsui kɛ enine ni yeɔ nɔ lɛ pɛ mli, ni jwɛŋmɔ ni mli wa, ni ebuɔ akɔntaa jogbaŋŋ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli hewalɛ ni ebaanyɛ ekɛ ehe awo mli lɛ mli lɛ fiɔ sɛɛ.

Yɛ yiŋtoi nɛɛ fɛɛ ahewɔ yɛ nakai beaŋ, kɛ ekrokomɛi ni ekolɛ amɛpɛiɔ mli tsɔ ni anyɛɛɛ akɛ wiemɔi atsɔɔ mli yɛ biɛ lɛ hewɔ lɛ, Ahab na faŋŋ akɛ esa akɛ eya nɔ eye anɔkwa lolo yɛ gbɛ kpakpa nɔ yɛ adebɔɔ mli yiŋtoo ni yɔɔ Pequod gbɛfaa lɛ he lɛ he; kwɛmɔ kusum nifeemɔi fɛɛ ni akɛtsuɔ nii; ni jeee no pɛ, shi moŋ enyɛɔ ehe nɔ koni ejie miishɛɛ fɛɛ ni ale jogbaŋŋ ni eyɔɔ yɛ enitsumɔ lɛ he lɛ kpo.

Bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni enɛ fɛɛ ji lɛ, bei pii lɛ anuɔ egbee agbɛnɛ ni eejieɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔnei etɛ lɛ ayitsei lɛ ayi ni ewoɔ amɛ ŋaa koni amɛkwɛ amɛhe jogbaŋŋ, ni amɛkajie ŋshɔŋloo ko po he amaniɛbɔɔ. Nɛkɛ hiɛdɔɔ nɛɛ etsɛɛɛ ni nyɔmɔwoo bɛ he.





YITSƆƆ 47. Mat-Felɔ lɛ.

No mli lɛ, atatu kɛ hulutsoo be ji shwane; ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ kɛ ŋmɔlɔ miibɔle ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi lɛ ahe, loo amɛmiikwɛ nu ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ mli ni nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ mli. Akɛ mi kɛ Queequeg tsu nii yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ blɛoo nɔ ni wɔkpɛ nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ klante-mat, koni akɛwo wɔlɛlɛ lɛ he tsɔne kroko. No hewɔ lɛ, nɔ ni yaa nɔ lɛ fɛɛ efee dioo ni eyɛ shiŋŋ ni kɛlɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, etsɔɔ hiɛ, ni jwɛŋmɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ shwereɔ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli, ni etamɔ nɔ ni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ fɛɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni efee dioo lɛ ekpɛ eyiŋ akɛ ebaatsɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe ni anaaa lɛ nɔ.

Miji Queequeg lɛ sɔɔlɔ loo baafa lɛ, beni mibɛ deka yɛ mat lɛ nɔ lɛ. Beni miyaa nɔ mihoɔ ni mikɛ marline ni awo mli loo tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ hoɔ yɛ tsɔne lɛ mli tsɔne lɛ mli tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ, ni mikɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ minine tsuɔ nii kɛyaa tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ, ni Queequeg, ni damɔ afã lɛ, ekɛ e-oak klante ni tsii lɛ shwieɔ tsɔne lɛ mli be fɛɛ be, ni ekwɛɔ nu lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ kɛ tsɔne lɛ fɛɛ wieɔ akɛ: lamɔ ye nɔ yɛ jɛmɛ yɛ no mli lɛ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fɛɛ nɔ kɛ ŋshɔ lɛ fɛɛ nɔ, ni klante lɛ gbɛɛmɔ ni bɛ faŋŋ ni tsɔɔ mli lɛ pɛ fite, ni etamɔ nɔ ni enɛ ji Be lɛ Loom lɛ, ni mi diɛŋtsɛ miji tsɔne ko ni akɛ tsɔne tsuɔ nii kɛkpɛɔ nii ni ekɛkpɛɔ nii kɛyaa Fates lɛ. Jɛmɛ ji he ni tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ mli kpãai ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ lɛ yɔɔ ni tsɔne kome pɛ ni hosoɔ, ni ekuɔ esɛɛ be fɛɛ be, ni tsakeee lɛ yeɔ nɔ, ni nakai tsɔne lɛ hosoɔ bɔ ni sa kɛkɛ ni ekpɛlɛɔ tsɔnei krokomɛi ni kɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enɔ lɛ futuɔ amɛhe lɛ nɔ. Etamɔ nɔ ni nɛkɛ warp nɛɛ he miihia; ni biɛ, misusu akɛ, mikɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ minine shwieɔ mi diɛŋtsɛ mi tsɔne lɛ nɔ ni mikɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ misɛɛgbɛ lɛ shwieɔ nɛkɛ kpãai ni tsakeee nɛɛ amli. Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, Queequeg klante ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli, ni esusuuu he lɛ, ni bei komɛi lɛ etswaa tsɔne lɛ sɛɛgbɛ, loo egbɛɔ eshwaa, loo etswaa waa, loo ebɛ hewalɛ, taakɛ ebaafee lɛ; ni kɛtsɔ srɔtofeemɔ nɛɛ ni yɔɔ naagbee tswaa lɛ mli lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekɛ srɔtofeemɔ ni sa yɛ mama ni agbe naa lɛ naagbee fã lɛ mli baa; nɛkɛ kooloo fɔŋ nɛɛ klante nɛɛ, misusu he, ni no hewɔ lɛ naagbee lɛ ehaa tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli kɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ fɛɛ feɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ; esa akɛ nɛkɛ klante ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni kɔɔɔ he eko nɛɛ afee nɔ ni ba trukaa—aye, nɔ ni ba trukaa, heyeli, kɛ hiamɔ nii—ni kɛ amɛhe kpaaa gbee yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ—ni fɛɛ kɛ amɛhe miitsu nii yɛ gbɛ ni kɛ amɛhe kpãa gbee nɔ. Hiamɔ nii ni ja lɛ, ni esaaa akɛ ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ enaagbee gbɛ lɛ nɔ—e-vibration fɛɛ vibration ni tsakeɔ, lɛɛlɛŋ, ni tsɔɔ nakai pɛ; heyeli ni yɔɔ heyeli lolo ni ekɛ e-shuttle lɛ atsu nii yɛ tsɔji ni aha lɛ ateŋ; kɛ hegbɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ atsĩɔ enaa yɛ eshwɛmɔ mli yɛ hiamɔ nii ni ja lɛ amli, kɛ afã yɛ egbɛfaa ni heyeli suɔmɔ kudɔɔ lɛ mli, eyɛ mli akɛ amɛyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ amɛtsɔɔ nakai moŋ, shi hegbɛ kɛtsɔ sɛɛkuu nɔ lɛ yeɔ nɔ, ni eyɛ naagbee nɔ ni jieɔ ehe kpo yɛ nibii ni yaa nɔ lɛ amli.

No hewɔ lɛ wɔmiikpa ni wɔmiikpa kɛmiiya beni mije shishi kɛ gbee ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, ni agbala be kakadaŋŋ, ni lala gbɛfaŋ lɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni jeee shikpɔŋ nɔ nɔ, ni heyeli bɔɔlu lɛ gbee shi kɛjɛ minine mli, ni midamɔ shi ni mikwɛ atatui ni nakai gbee lɛ jɛ mli lɛ gbee shi tamɔ fɛ̃i. Nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi shɔɔŋ yɛ krɔs tsei lɛ ateŋ ji nakai Gay-Yitso ni etsɔ shwilafo, Tashtego. Egbɔmɔtso lɛ miishɛ ehe kɛmiiya hiɛ kɛ miishɛɛ, enine mli ekpã mli tamɔ tso, ni yɛ be kukuoo mli trukaa lɛ, etsa ebolɔmɔ lɛ nɔ. Kɛha nɔmimaa lɛ, gbee kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ ji nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ ni ekolɛ anuɔ yɛ ŋshɔi lɛ fɛɛ amli, kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ohai abɔ ni kwɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni tara shi yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ adɛŋ; shi nakai fɛ̃i momo ni afɔ lɛ lɛ baanyɛ ajɛ nakai fɛ̃i lɛ mli fioo ko pɛ aná gbee ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ tamɔ nɔ ni jɛ Tashtego Indianyo lɛ nɔ lɛ mli.

Beni edamɔ shi ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ onɔ ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ kɔɔyɔɔ mli fã, ni ekɛ miishɛɛ kɛ miishɛɛ miikwɛ ŋwɛi ŋwɛi lɛ, kulɛ obaasusu akɛ eji gbalɔ loo ninalɔ ko ni miikwɛ Fate lɛ he mfonirii lɛ, kɛtsɔ nakai gbeei ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni tswaa amɛba lɛ he adafi lɛ nɔ.
