Eji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he kpojiemɔ ko ni ato he gbɛjianɔ yɛ egbɛjianɔtoi babaoo lɛ amli, ni amrɔ nɛɛ masumɔ ni mikɛwo nyɛhiɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, ani ejeee nitsumɔ ko ni yɔɔ mlɛo. Nibii ni feɔ basabasa lɛ mligbalamɔ, nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ni baa shi ji nɔ ni aŋmala yɛ biɛ. Bo nɔ ni nɔyeli hegbɛi ni hi fe fɛɛ kɛ nɔ ni eba ehee lɛ eto lɛ toi.

Kapten Scoresby, AD 1820 lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Koolooi ahe nikasemɔ nitsumɔhe nine ko bɛ ni kɛ ehe woɔ mli babaoo tamɔ nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ Cetology lɛ.”

“Jeee miyiŋtoo ji, kɛji miyɛ hewalɛ mli, akɛ mabote sanebimɔ ni kɔɔ gbɛ diɛŋtsɛ ni atsɔɔ nɔ ajaa cetacea lɛ mli awoɔ kui kɛ wekui amli lɛ mli. * * * Futumɔ ni mli wa yɛ kooloo nɛɛ he yinɔsaneŋmalɔi ateŋ” (sperm whale), taakɛ Surgeon Beale, AD 1839 lɛ kɛɛ lɛ.

“Ehe ehiaaa ni wɔtiu wɔ niiamlitaomɔ lɛ sɛɛ yɛ nu ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ mli lɛ mli.” “Musu ni anyɛɛɛ abote mli ni haa wɔ nilee ni kɔɔ cetacea he lɛ nɔ.” ŋm., ŋmãa ni ŋmei ehà nɔ.” “Nɛkɛ nibii ni tsɔɔ nɔ ni eshɛɛɛ he nɛɛ fɛɛ shi amɛtsuɔ nii koni amɛkɛpiŋ wɔ adebɔɔ he nilelɔi lɛ.”

No hewɔ lɛ awie ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, Cuvier kpeteŋkpele lɛ, kɛ John Hunter, kɛ Lesson, nakai kooloi ahe nilee kɛ gbɔmɔtso he nilee kanei lɛ ahe. Shi kɛlɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ nilee diɛŋtsɛ faaa moŋ, shi woji babaoo yɛ; ni nakai eji yɛ gbɛ bibioo ko nɔ, kɛ cetology, loo ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nilee. Hii babaoo ji, bibii kɛ wuji, onukpai kɛ eheei, shikpɔŋ nɔ bii kɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi, ni aŋmala ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he saji babaoo loo fioo. Jofoi kɛho fioo komɛi ahe:—Biblia lɛ Ŋmalɔi lɛ; Aristotle; Plinius; Aldrovandi; Nuŋtsɔ Tɔmas Browne; Gesnɛ; Ray; Lineo; Rondeletio; Wilobi; Eŋɔli; Artɛdi; Sibald; Brison; Marten; Lasepɛdi; Bonɛtɛre; Desmarest; Barɔn Kuvier; Frederik Kuvier; Yohane Gbɛfalɔ; Owen; Skɔresby; Beale; Bennɛt; J. Ross Browne; Miriam Adeka Wolo lɛ Ŋmalɔ lɛ; Olmstɛd; kɛ Sɔlemɔtsɛ T. Cheever. Shi nɔ ni ji yiŋtoo ni fe fɛɛ ni enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ ŋma lɛ, kukuji ni atsĩ tã yɛ ŋwɛi nɛɛ baatsɔɔ.

Yɛ gbɛi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe woji aŋmalalɔi agbɛi nɛɛ amli lɛ, mɛi ni nyiɛ Owen sɛɛ lɛ pɛ ena ŋshɔŋlooi ni hiɛ ka pɛŋ; ni shi amɛteŋ mɔ kome ji ŋshɔŋlooi kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nilelɔ diɛŋtsɛ. Mitsɔɔ Kapten Scoresby. Yɛ Greenland loo right-whale he sane ni yɔɔ srɔto lɛ he lɛ, lɛ ji hegbɛ ni hi fe fɛɛ ni yɔɔ. Shi Scoresby leee nɔ ko ni ewieee nɔ ko yɛ sperm whale kpeteŋkpele lɛ he, ni kɛ akɛto Greenland whale lɛ he lɛ, eshwɛ fioo ni esaaa akɛ atsĩɔ etã. Ni biɛ ji he ni akɛɛ, akɛ Greenland ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ji mɔ ko ni heɔ ŋshɔi lɛ amaŋtsɛ sɛi lɛ nɔ. Jeee lɛ ji ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ mɔ ni da fe fɛɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ po. Shi, yɛ bɔ ni ewiemɔi lɛ he hiaa ha be kakadaŋŋ lɛ hewɔ, kɛ nilee ni bɛ ni mli kwɔ ni, kɛyashi aaafee afii nyɔŋmai kpawo ni eho nɛ lɛ, ekɛ sperm-whale ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ loo aleee kwraa yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ wo mli lɛ hewɔ, ni nilee ni bɛ kɛbashi ŋmɛnɛ lɛ yeɔ nɔ lolo yɛ jeŋ nilee mli sɛɛkuu kɛ whale-lɛdaamɔhei fioo komɛi pɛ amli ja; nɛkɛ maŋ nɔyeli nɛɛ eye emuu yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ. Nɔ ni miihe ashɛ leviathanic wiemɔi fɛɛ ni lalafolɔi wuji ni hi shi yɛ be ni eho lɛ amli lɛ wieɔ he lɛ baaha omii ashɛ ohe akɛ Greenland ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, ni mɔ ko mɔ ko bɛ ni kɛ lɛ yeɔ akaŋ lɛ ji ŋshɔi lɛ anɔ maŋtsɛ kɛha amɛ. Shi naagbee lɛ, be eshɛ kɛha jajemɔ hee ko. Enɛ ji Charing Krɔɔs; nyɛboa toi! gbɔmɛi kpakpai fɛɛ, —ajie Greenland ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛjɛ maŋtsɛyeli lɛ nɔ, —sperm ŋshɔŋloo kpeteŋkpele lɛ miiye nɔ agbɛnɛ!

Woji enyɔ pɛ yɔɔ ni feɔ amɛhe tamɔ nɔ ni amɛkɛ sperm whale ni hiɛ ka lɛ miifɔ ohiɛ, ni yɛ nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ lɛ, yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ nɔ lɛ, amɛyeɔ omanye yɛ mɔdɛŋbɔɔ lɛ mli. Nakai woji lɛ ji Beale kɛ Bennett woji lɛ; amɛ fɛɛ yɛ amɛbeaŋ opireshɛnfeelɔi kɛha Ŋleshi South-Sea whale-lɛlɛi, kɛ hii pɔtɛɛ ni anyɛɔ akɛ he fɔ̃ɔ amɛnɔ fɛɛ. Shishijee nɔ ni taa sperm whale lɛ he ni abaana yɛ amɛ volumes lɛ amli lɛ ji nɔ bibioo diɛŋtsɛ; shi bɔ ni eshɛɔ he ni eshɛɔ lɛ, ehi jogbaŋŋ, eyɛ mli akɛ jeŋ nilee mlitsɔɔmɔ pɛ ji nɔ ni akɛtsɔɔ mli babaoo moŋ. Shi kɛlɛ, kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ, sperm whale lɛ, jeŋ nilee loo lalafoo wiemɔ mli shihilɛ lɛ eshɛɛɛ he yɛ wolo ko mli. Nɔ ni fe ŋshɔŋlooi krokomɛi fɛɛ ni agbeɔ amɛ lɛ, enɔ ji wala ni aŋmalako.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, ŋshɔŋ loofɔji srɔtoi lɛ ahe miihia srɔtofeemɔ ko ni ehe gbɛi waa, kɛji akɛ eji gbɛjianɔtoo ko ni yɔɔ mlɛo kɛha ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ, ni sɛɛ mli lɛ, nitsulɔi ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ baawo mli yɛ enitsumɔhei lɛ fɛɛ amli. Akɛni gbɔmɔ kpakpa ko bɛ ni yaa hiɛ ni ekɛ sane nɛɛ baatsu nii hewɔ lɛ, mikɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ mimɔdɛŋbɔɔ ni ehiii lɛ miiha yɛ biɛ. Miwooo nɔ ko nɔ ko ni yeɔ emuu shi; ejaakɛ adesa nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni esa akɛ eye emuu lɛ, esa akɛ nakai yiŋtoo lɛ nɔŋŋ hewɔ lɛ efee nɔ ni tɔmɔ bɛ he. Mifeŋ mihe tamɔ mɔ ko ni miitsɔɔ bɔɔ nii sɔrɔtoi lɛ ahe minitii fioo ko, loo—kɛ hoo lɛ, yɛ nɛkɛ he nɛɛ—mifeee mihe tamɔ nɔ ni mitsɔɔ mli babaoo. Miyiŋtoo yɛ biɛ ji ni matsɔɔ cetology he gbɛjianɔtoo ko he mfoniri kɛkɛ. Mi ji tsumalɔ, shi jeee tsumalɔ.

Shi eji nitsumɔ ko ni he hiaa waa; woji-halamɔ tsɔne folo ko bɛ Post-Office lɛ mli ni kɛ lɛ yeɔ egbɔ. Koni mikɛ mihe awo ŋshɔ lɛ shishi kɛnyiɛ amɛsɛɛ; ni mɔ ko aná eniji yɛ je lɛ shishitoo, tsitsii, kɛ tsitsii diɛŋtsɛ ni awieee he lɛ ateŋ; enɛ ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei. Mɛni ji mi ni esa akɛ mafee essay koni mikɛwo leviathan nɛɛ gugɔ mli! Heguɔgbee ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni yɔɔ Hiob mli lɛ baanyɛ aha mihe afee mi yaa. Ani lɛ (leviatan lɛ) kɛ bo baafee kpaŋmɔ? Naa, hiɛnɔkamɔ ni yɔɔ enɔ lɛ ji yaka! Shi mitsɔ wojiatoohei amli ni mikɛ lɛlɛ etsɔ ŋshɔi amli; Ehe ebahia ni mikɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ niji ni anaa nɛɛ atsu nii; Mikɛ hiɛdɔɔ miitsu nii; ni maka. Shishijee nibii komɛi yɛ ni esa akɛ atsu he nii.

Klɛŋklɛŋ: Shihilɛ ni bɛ faŋŋ, ni bɛ shiŋŋ ni yɔɔ Cetology he nilee nɛɛ mli lɛ yɛ vestibule lɛ diɛŋtsɛ mli ni anɔkwale ni eji lɛ yeɔ he odase, akɛ yɛ hei komɛi lɛ, eji sane ko ni awieɔ he lolo kɛji akɛ ŋshɔŋloo ji loo. Linnæus jaje yɛ ewolo ni ji System of Nature, AD 1776 lɛ mli akɛ, “Mikɛ enɛ gbalaa ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ kɛjɛɔ loo lɛ mli.” Shi yɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ minilee naa lɛ, mile akɛ kɛyashi afi 1850 lɛ, ana ŋshɔŋlooi ni ji shark kɛ shad, alewives kɛ herring, ni teɔ shi woɔ Linnæus famɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ lɛ lolo ni amɛmiija ŋshɔi kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ mli nibii ni amɛyɔɔ lɛ mli kɛ Leviathan.

Nɔ ni Linnæus baadamɔ nɔ eshwie ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ kɛjɛ nu lɛ mli lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Yɛ amɛtsui ni mli edɔ ni yɔɔ hiŋmɛii enyɔ, amɛflufla, amɛhiŋmɛii ni tsiɔ amɛhe, amɛtoi ni mli ewo muji, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem,” kɛ naagbee lɛ, “ex merito lege naturæ jure” lɛ hewɔ. Mikɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ ha minanemɛi Simeon Macey kɛ Charley Coffin, ni jɛ Nantucket, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛfata mihe yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfaa ko mli lɛ, ni amɛfee ekome yɛ susumɔ akɛ yiŋtoi ni awie he lɛ faaa kwraa. Charley kɛ wiemɔ fɔŋ tsɔɔ akɛ amɛji humbug.

Ale akɛ, kɛji akɛ mikɛ ŋwanejee fɛɛ miikpa lɛ, mikɛ tsutsu shishitoo kpakpa ni ji akɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ji loo lɛ tsuɔ nii, ni mitsɛɔ Yona krɔŋkrɔŋ lɛ koni ekɛfi misɛɛ. Nɛkɛ shishijee nɔ nɛɛ eba shi, nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ ji, yɛ mɛɛ mligbɛ bulɛ mli esoro ŋshɔŋloo lɛ yɛ loo krokomɛi ahe. Yɛ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ, Linnæus kɛ nakai nibii lɛ eha bo. Shi yɛ kukufoo mli lɛ, amɛji enɛɛmɛi: fɛ̃i kɛ lá ni mli edɔ; yɛ be mli ni, loo krokomɛi lɛ fɛɛ bɛ fɛ̃i ni amɛlá mli ejɔ.

Nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ ji: te wɔɔfee tɛŋŋ wɔtsɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ shishi, kɛtsɔ esɛɛgbɛ nibii ni yɔɔ faŋŋ lɛ anɔ, bɔni afee ni efee nɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ ni wɔkɛwo lɛ gbɛi kɛha be fɛɛ ni baaba lɛ? No hewɔ lɛ, kɛ aaawie kuku lɛ, ŋshɔŋ loo ji loo ko ni shwieɔ nu ni ehiɛ shwieɔ shi . Jɛmɛ ji he ni oyɔɔ lɛ yɛ. Bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni eji lɛ, nakai shishitsɔɔmɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni jɛɔ nii anɔ jwɛŋmɔ ni mli kwɔ mli baa. Walrus shwieɔ ŋmɔlɔ tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo, shi walrus jeee loo, ejaakɛ ehiɔ nu mli. Shi naagbee wiemɔ ni yɔɔ shishitsɔɔmɔ lɛ mli lɛ mli wa lolo, taakɛ akɛ klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ lɛ fata he lɛ. Ekolɛ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ eyɔse akɛ loo fɛɛ ni shikpɔŋ nɔ bii le lɛ bɛ tsitsi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, shi moŋ eyɛ tsitsi ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ, loo tsitsi ni tsɔɔ ŋwɛi kɛbaa shi. Yɛ be mli ni, yɛ loo ni shwieɔ nu lɛ ateŋ lɛ, tsitsi lɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ ebaanyɛ efee nɔ ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ moŋ, shi be fɛɛ be lɛ, ehiɛɔ shi.

Kɛtsɔ shishitsɔɔmɔ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ni kɔɔ nɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo ji lɛ he lɛ nɔ lɛ, mijieee ŋshɔ mli adebɔɔ ko ni Nantucketbii ni le nii jogbaŋŋ fe fɛɛ lɛ etsɔɔ mli kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ akɛ eji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛjɛɛɛ leviathanic nyɛmifeemɔ lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ kwraa; ni asaŋ, yɛ gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ, ekɛ loo ko ni kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ abuɔ lɛ akɛ eji gbɔ lɛ abɔ.* No hewɔ lɛ, esa akɛ akɛ loo bibii, ni shwieɔ ŋmɔlɔ, kɛ tsitsii ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ fɛɛ awo Cetology shikpɔŋ-gbɛjianɔtoo nɛɛ mli. Belɛ, agbɛnɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi akuu muu lɛ fɛɛ mlijaai wuji lɛ eba.

*Mile akɛ kɛbashi ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ lɛ, loo ni atsɛɔ lɛ Lamatins kɛ Dugongs (Pig-fish kɛ Sow-fish ni yɔɔ Nantucket Adekai lɛ amli) lɛ ji nɔ ni adebɔɔ he nilelɔi babaoo kɛwoɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ. Shi akɛni nɛkɛ tsina-looi nɛɛ ji kuu ko ni feɔ hoo, ni abuuu amɛ, ni amɛteŋ babaoo shwieɔ amɛhe yɛ faai anaabui amli, ni amɛyeɔ jwɛi ni efɔ lɛ, ni titri lɛ akɛni amɛjieee amɛhe kpo hewɔ lɛ, mikpooɔ amɛhegbɛi akɛ amɛji ŋshɔŋlooi; ni amɛkɛ amɛ passport lɛ eha amɛ koni amɛkpa Cetology Maŋtsɛyeli lɛ.

Klɛŋklɛŋ: Taakɛ bɔ ni ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ dara ha lɛ naa lɛ, mijaraa ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ amli miwoɔ shishijee WOJI etɛ mli (ni ajaa mli bibii awoɔ YITSƆƆI amli), ni enɛɛmɛi baanu amɛ fɛɛ shishi, bibii kɛ wuji fɛɛ.

I. FOLIO WHALƐ; II. ni ji OKTAVO Ŋshɔ lɛ; III. ni ji DUODƐSIMO Ŋshɔ lɛ.

Taakɛ FOLIO lɛ henɔ lɛ ji lɛ mikɛ Sperm Whale lɛ haa ; ni ji OCTAVO lɛ, ni ji Grampus lɛ ; ni ji DUODECIM lɛ, ni ji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ .

WOJI. Yitsei ni nyiɛ sɛɛ nɛɛ fata enɛɛmɛi ahe yɛ biɛ:—I. Sperm Whale lɛ ; II. ninejurɔgbɛ Whale lɛ ; III. Fin-Back Whale lɛ ; IV. ni ji Whale ni yɔɔ Hump sɛɛ lɛ ; V. Razor Sɛɛ Whale lɛ ; VI. Sulfur Shishi Ŋshɔŋloo lɛ .

WOJI I. ( Folio ), YITSƆƆ I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ, ni fata blema Blɔfobii ni ale amɛ jogbaŋŋ akɛ Trumpa ŋshɔŋloo, kɛ Physeter ŋshɔŋloo, kɛ Anvil Headed ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ateŋ lɛ ji Frenchbii lɛ a-Cachalot ni yɔɔ amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛ Pottsfich ni ji Macrocebii lɛ a-Germanbii lɛ a-Longphalus lɛ. Ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ, lɛ ji mɔ ni fa fe fɛɛ yɛ shikpɔŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ nɔ; ŋshɔŋlooi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ ni akɛbaakpe; nɔ ni yɔɔ nyam fe fɛɛ yɛ egbɛfaŋ; kɛ naagbee lɛ, nɔ ni jara wa fe fɛɛ yɛ jarayeli mli; akɛni lɛ pɛ ji adebɔɔ ni anáa nakai nibii ni jara wa, ni ji spermaceti lɛ kɛjɛɔ mli lɛ hewɔ. Abaaha egbɛi ni yɔɔ srɔto lɛ fɛɛ, yɛ hei krokomɛi babaoo, aya hiɛ. Egbɛi lɛ ji nɔ titri ni esa akɛ mafee amrɔ nɛɛ. Kɛ asusu wiemɔ he nilee gbɛfaŋ lɛ, eji nɔ ni jwɛŋmɔ bɛ mli. Afii ohai komɛi ni eho nɛ, beni eshwɛ fioo ni aleee Sperm whale lɛ kwraa yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ egbɔmɔtso ni sa mli, kɛ beni aná e-oil lɛ kɛjɛ loo ni efo lɛ mli yɛ oshara mli pɛ lɛ; yɛ nakai gbii lɛ amli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni mɛi babaoo susuɔ akɛ spermaceti lɛ jɛ adebɔɔ ko ni tamɔ nɔ ni ale lɛ yɛ nakai beaŋ yɛ England akɛ Greenland loo Right Whale lɛ mli. Eji susumɔ lɛ hu, akɛ nɛkɛ spermaceti nɛɛ nɔŋŋ ji Greenland Whale lɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli ni wiemɔ lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ niŋmaa okadii lɛ jieɔ lɛ kpo diɛŋtsɛ lɛ. Yɛ nakai bei lɛ amli hu lɛ, spermaceti lɛ faaa kwraa, akɛtsuuu nii kɛha la, shi moŋ akɛ mu kɛ tsofa pɛ. No mli lɛ, esa akɛ aná kɛjɛ tsofafeelɔi lɛ adɛŋ pɛ taakɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ oheɔ rhubarb ounce kome lɛ. Beni, taakɛ misusuɔ lɛ, yɛ be ni eho lɛ mli lɛ, ale bɔ ni spermaceti ji diɛŋtsɛ lɛ, mɛi ni hɔ̃ɔ lɛ hiɛ eshishijee gbɛi lɛ mli lolo; ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ ebaaha ejara walɛ aya hiɛ kɛtsɔ susumɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ waa ni kɔɔ bɔ ni ebɛ sɛɛnamɔ lɛ he lɛ nɔ. Ni no hewɔ lɛ esa akɛ naagbee lɛ akɛ gbɛi nɛɛ aha ŋshɔŋloo ni ajie nɛkɛ spermaceti nɛɛ kɛjɛ mli diɛŋtsɛ lɛ.

WOJI I. ( Folio ), YITSƆƆ II. ( Right Whale ).—Yɛ gbɛ kome nɔ lɛ, enɛ ji leviathani lɛ ateŋ nɔ ni abuɔ lɛ fe fɛɛ, ejaakɛ eji klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ ni gbɔmɔ shwieɔ lɛ daa. Ehaa nɔ ni afɔɔ namɔ akɛ whalebone loo baleen lɛ baa; kɛ mu ni ale lɛ titri akɛ “whale oil,” ni ji nɔ ko ni baa shi yɛ jarayeli mli lɛ. Yɛ wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ ateŋ lɛ, akɛ gbɛi ni nyiɛ sɛɛ nɛɛ fɛɛ woɔ lɛ gbɛi ni mɔ ko mɔ ko bɛ mli: Whale; Greenland Ŋshɔŋloo lɛ; ŋshɔŋloo Diŋ lɛ; Ŋshɔ Kpeteŋkpele lɛ; Anɔkwale Ŋshɔ lɛ; ni ji Whale ni Ji Ja lɛ. Nɔ ko ni bɛ faŋŋ yɛ nɔ ni kɔɔ kooloi srɔtoi ni abaptisi amɛ babaoo yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ lɛ ahe. No hewɔ lɛ, mɛni ji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, ni mikɛfataa mi Folios lɛ mli ŋshɔŋlooi ni ji enyɔ lɛ ahe lɛ? WOJI I. ( Folio ), YITSƆƆ III. ( Fin-Back ).—Yɛ yitso nɛɛ shishi lɛ, mibuɔ kooloo ko ni, yɛ gbɛi srɔtoi ni ji Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, kɛ Long-John lɛ, ana yɛ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ ŋshɔ fɛɛ ŋshɔ mli, ni bei pii lɛ eji ŋshɔŋloo ni gbɛfalɔi ni foɔ Atlantik Ŋshɔ lɛ tsɔɔ e-jet ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ mli shii abɔ, yɛ New York packet-tracks lɛ amli. Yɛ elɛɛmɔ ni eshɛɔ he lɛ mli, kɛ e-baleen lɛ mli lɛ, Fin-back lɛ tamɔ right whale lɛ, shi eyɛ ŋmɔlɔ fioo, ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔ, ni ebɛŋkɛɔ oliv he. Enaabu wuji lɛ jieɔ nɔ ko ni tamɔ kpãa kpo, ni ŋmeii wuji ni ebɔle amɛhe ni ebɔle amɛhe lɛ ji nɔ ni feɔ. Bei pii lɛ, nɔ ni haa esoroɔ lɛ waa, ni ji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, ni ejɛ mli ená egbɛi lɛ ji nɔ ko ni anaa. Nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ kɛlɛ ji aaafee ninetalɔi etɛ loo ejwɛ, ni edaa kɛjɛɔ sɛɛgbɛ fã lɛ mli, ni eyɛ ŋwɛigbɛ, ni eyɛ naagbee ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ waa. Kɛji akɛ anaaa adebɔɔ lɛ fã kroko bibioo po lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ, abaana nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ nɛɛ ni ejeɔ kpo faŋŋ kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ. Kɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli efee dioo yɛ gbɛ ni sa nɔ, ni ŋshɔkei ni tamɔ ŋshɔkei ekadi amɛ fioo, ni nɛkɛ ŋshɔkei ni tamɔ gnomon nɛɛ te shi ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ shwie ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ ni ebɔle ehe lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekolɛ abaanyɛ asusu akɛ nu ni ebɔle ehe lɛ tamɔ dial yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, ni egbɛjianɔtoo kɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkei lɛ anɔ lɛ yɔɔ mli. Yɛ nakai Ahaz-dial lɛ nɔ lɛ, bei pii lɛ, hulutsoo lɛ yaa sɛɛ. Fin-Back lɛ jeee kuu. Etamɔ mɔ ko ni nyɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi, taakɛ hii komɛi ji gbɔmɔ he nyɛlɔi lɛ. Ehiɛ gboɔ waa; be fɛɛ be lɛ, eyaa ekome too; ni teɔ shi trukaa kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ yɛ nu ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ ni mli ewo muji fe fɛɛ lɛ mli; e-jet ni nɔ kwɔ ni ja ni ji ekome lɛ miiya ŋwɛi tamɔ misanthropic akpɔlɔ kakadaŋŋ yɛ shikpɔŋ kplanaa ko ni nɔ bɛ nɔ; ni akɛ naakpɛɛ hewalɛ kɛ oyaiyeli ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ha lɛ yɛ nu mli shwiili mli, ni ekɛteɔ shi ewoɔ nibii fɛɛ ni gbɔmɔ taoɔ amrɔ nɛɛ; etamɔ nɔ ni nɛkɛ leviathan nɛɛ ji Kain ni jɛ eweku lɛ mli ni ashwie lɛ ni anyɛɛɛ aye enɔ kunim lɛ, ni ehiɛ nakai style lɛ yɛ esɛɛ akɛ ekadi. Kɛjɛ baleen lɛ ni eyɔɔ enaa lɛ mli lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ, akɛ Fin-Back lɛ fataa ŋshɔŋloo ni ja lɛ he, yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni atsɛɔ amɛ Whalebone whales , ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ baleen lɛ ateŋ. Etamɔ nɔ ni nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni atsɛɔ amɛ Whalebone whales nɛɛ ateŋ srɔtoi babaoo yɛ, shi kɛlɛ, aleee amɛteŋ babaoo jogbaŋŋ. Ŋshɔŋlooi ni hiɛ tsitsii ni mli kwɔ kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ amɛnaabu; ŋshɔŋlooi ni hiɛ pike yitso; ŋshɔŋlooi ni abua naa; ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ shishi kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋ, ji ŋshɔŋlooi ni wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ kɛtsɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi srɔtoi fioo komɛi.

Yɛ nɛkɛ gbɛi ni ji “Whalebone whales” nɛɛ he lɛ, ehe miihia waa akɛ atsĩ tã, akɛ bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni gbɛi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ baanyɛ afee nɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi komɛi ahe wiemɔi amli lɛ, shi kɛlɛ, eji yakatswaa akɛ aaaka Leviathan lɛ mligbalamɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ, ni damɔ e-baleen, loo e-hump, lɛ nɔ; eyɛ mli akɛ nakai fãi loo nibii ni akadi lɛ tamɔ nɔ ni ehi jogbaŋŋ koni ekɛ shishitoo nɔ aha Cetology gbɛjianɔtoo ni afeɔ daa lɛ fe gbɔmɔtso mli srɔtofeemɔi krokomɛi ni ajie amɛhe kpo, ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, yɛ ehenɔi lɛ amli lɛ, jieɔ lɛ kpo lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ. Belɛ te fee tɛŋŋ? Baleen, hump, sɛɛ-fɛ̃i, kɛ ŋmeii; enɛɛmɛi ji nibii ni amɛhe srɔtofeemɔi lɛ gbɛ eshwa ŋshɔŋlooi srɔtoi fɛɛ ateŋ ni ajieee mɔ ko mɔ ko kpo, ni asusuuu nɔ ni baanyɛ afee bɔ ni amɛto amɛhe gbɛjianɔ aha yɛ nibii krokomɛi ni he hiaa fe fɛɛ lɛ ahe. No hewɔ lɛ, sperm whale kɛ humpbacked whale lɛ, eko fɛɛ eko yɛ ŋmei; shi jɛmɛ ji he ni nɔ ni tamɔ amɛhe lɛ kpaa. Kɛkɛ ni, nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋ lɛ nɔŋŋ kɛ Greenland ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, enɛɛmɛi ateŋ eko fɛɛ eko yɛ baleen; shi jɛmɛ hu ji he ni nɔ ni tamɔ nakai lɛ kpaa. Ni nakai nɔŋŋ eji yɛ fãi krokomɛi ni atsĩ tã yɛ yiteŋgbɛ lɛ hu ahe. Yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi srɔtoi amli lɛ, amɛfeɔ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni bɛ gbɛjianɔtoo mli nɛɛ; aloo, yɛ amɛteŋ mɔ kome ni ajie lɛ kpo lɛ mli lɛ, nɛkɛ he ni ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ mɛi ahe ni bɛ gbɛjianɔtoo mli lɛ; akɛ eji nɔ ni teɔ shi ewoɔ gbɛjianɔtoi fɛɛ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ yɛ shishitoo ni tamɔ nɛkɛ nɔ lɛ kwraa. Yɛ tɛsaa nɛɛ nɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nilelɔi lɛ ateŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ mli egbala.

Shi ekolɛ abaasusu akɛ, yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mligbɛ fãi lɛ amli, yɛ egbɔmɔtso lɛ mli—kɛ hoo lɛ, jɛmɛ ji he ni wɔbaanyɛ wɔtswa srɔtofeemɔ ni ja lɛ. Dabi; ákɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ lɛ, mɛɛ nɔ ko yɛ Greenland ŋshɔŋloo lɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli ni sa kadimɔ fe e-baleen lɛ? Shi kɛlɛ wɔna akɛ kɛtsɔ e-baleen lɛ nɔ lɛ, anyɛɛɛ ajara Greenland ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli jogbaŋŋ. Ni kɛji akɛ oya shi kɛtee leviatan srɔtoi lɛ amli lɛ, mɛni hewɔ onaaa srɔtofeemɔi ni ji fã ni ji nyɔŋmai enumɔ mli ekome ni yɔɔ gbɛjianɔtoofeelɔ lɛ dɛŋ tamɔ nakai kponɔgbɛ nɔ ni akane momo lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, mɛni ji nɔ ni eshwɛ? nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ja ni emɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ amli yɛ gbɔmɔtsoŋ, yɛ amɛ liberal volume muu lɛ fɛɛ mli, ni ekɛ ekãa ato amɛ he gbɛjianɔ nakai. Ni enɛ ji Woloŋlee gbɛjianɔtoo ni akɛtsuɔ nii yɛ biɛ lɛ; ni no pɛ ji nɔ ni baanyɛ aye omanye, ejaakɛ no pɛ ji nɔ ni anyɛɔ atsuɔ he nii. Koni aya nɔ.

WOJI II. ( Oktavo ), YITSƆƆ III. ( Narwhale ) . Mi diɛŋtsɛ misusumɔ ji, akɛ bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni nɛkɛ akoloŋto ni hiɛ fã kome nɛɛ baanyɛ afee nɔ ni Narwhale lɛ kɛbaatsu nii diɛŋtsɛ—bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni nakai baanyɛ afee lɛ—ebaafee nɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ kɛha lɛ diɛŋtsɛ kɛha folda ni akɛkaneɔ woji bibii. Narwhale ni minu akɛ atsɛɔ lɛ Tusked ŋshɔŋloo, Horned ŋshɔŋloo, kɛ Unicorn ŋshɔŋloo. Eji Unicornism he nɔkwɛmɔnɔ ni haa mɔ taoɔ ele nɔ ni yɔɔ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ maŋtsɛyeli fɛɛ maŋtsɛyeli ni wala yɔɔ mli lɛ mli lɛ diɛŋtsɛ. Mibua naa kɛjɛ woloŋmalɔi momo komɛi ni yɔɔ cloistered lɛ adɛŋ akɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔ mli-unicorn akoloŋto nɛɛ nɔŋŋ ji nɔ ni abuɔ yɛ blema bei amli akɛ eji tsofa kpeteŋkpele ni teɔ shi ewoɔ poison, ni akɛni nakai eji hewɔ lɛ, ehesaamɔ kɛ jara ni fa babaoo baa. Akɛwo mli hu ni efee ŋmei ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ kɛha yei ni egbɔjɔ, tamɔ bɔ ni afeɔ ŋshɔŋlooi hii akoloŋtoi lɛ nɔŋŋ koni amɛfee hartshorn lɛ. Shishijee lɛ, abuɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ akɛ eji nɔ ko ni mɛi taoɔ ale waa. Black Letter kɛɛ mi akɛ Sir Martin Frobisher beni eku esɛɛ kɛjɛ nakai gbɛfaa lɛ mli lɛ, beni Maŋnyɛ Bess kɛ ekãa tswa enine ni akɛ jwetrii fee lɛ kɛjɛ Greenwich Maŋtsɛwe lɛ samflɛ ko mli etsɔɔ lɛ, beni elɛlɛ ni yɔɔ ekãa lɛ fãa gbɛ kɛtsɔɔ Thames lɛ; Black Letter kɛɛ akɛ: “beni Sir Martin ku esɛɛ kɛjɛ nakai gbɛfaa lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ enaji ni ekoto shi lɛ ha e-maŋtsɛyeli lɛ Narwhale lɛ akoloŋto kakadaŋŋ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekɛ be kakadaŋŋ fĩ maŋtsɛwe ni yɔɔ Windsor lɛ mli.” Ireland woloŋmalɔ ko kɛɛ akɛ Leicester maŋtsɛ lɛ, ni ekoto shi lɛ hu kɛ akoloŋto kroko ha e-maŋtsɛyeli lɛ, ni kɔɔ shikpɔŋ nɔ kooloo ko ni ji unicorn su lɛ he.

Narwhale lɛ yɛ fɛo waa, ni etamɔ ŋmei, ni eyɛ shikpɔŋ su ni tamɔ mliki, ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni ji ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni ji diŋ. E-mu lɛ nɔ kwɔ waa, eyɛ faŋŋ ni ehi jogbaŋŋ; shi nɔ ko fioo pɛ yɔɔ mli, ni bei pii lɛ, agbeɔ lɛ. Anaa lɛ yɛ circumpolar ŋshɔi lɛ amli babaoo.

WOJI II. ( Oktavo ), YITSƆƆ IV. ( Killer ).—Nantucketerbii lɛ leee nɔ ko kwraa yɛ ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ he, ni mɔ ni kɛɔ akɛ eji adebɔɔ he nilelɔ lɛ leee nɔ ko kwraa. Kɛjɛ nɔ ni mina yɛ ehe yɛ shɔŋŋ lɛ mli lɛ, esa akɛ makɛɛ akɛ etamɔ grampus ko dalɛ. Eji gbɔmɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa—Feegee loo ko. Bei komɛi lɛ, emɔɔ Folio ŋshɔŋlooi wuji lɛ anaabu, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ jɛmɛ tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo, kɛyashiɔ be mli ni kooloo ni he wa lɛ haoɔ kɛyashiɔ gbele mli. Agbeee Gbel lɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ. Minuko mu ni eyɔɔ lɛ he kɔkɔɔkɔ. Abaanyɛ ajie gbɛi ni akɛwo ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ mli lɛ kpo, yɛ bɔ ni etsɔɔɔ mli jogbaŋŋ lɛ hewɔ. Ejaakɛ gbɔmɔgbelɔi ji wɔ fɛɛ, yɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ kɛ ŋshɔ nɔ; Bonapartes kɛ Sharks fata he.

WOJI II. ( Octavo ), YITSƆƆ V. ( Thrasher ).—Nɛkɛ nuu nɛɛ ehe gbɛi waa yɛ etsitsi, ni ekɛtsuɔ nii akɛ ferule yɛ ehenyɛlɔi lɛ atswamɔ mli lɛ hewɔ. Etaraa Folio ŋshɔŋloo lɛ sɛɛ, ni beni efoɔ nu lɛ, etsuɔ ​​gbɛ ni etsɔɔ nɔ lɛ he nii kɛtsɔ ŋmɔlɔ ni ekɛtswaa lɛ lɛ nɔ; taakɛ skul tsɔɔlɔi komɛi kɛ amɛhe yeɔ yɛ je lɛ mli kɛtsɔɔ gbɛ ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ nɔ lɛ. Nɔ ko fioo pɛ ale yɛ Thrasher he lolo fe Gbelɔ lɛ he. Mɛi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ ji mlakwalɔi, yɛ ŋshɔi ni mla bɛ mli lɛ amli po.

Nakai ji bɔ ni WOJI II lɛ gbeɔ naa. ( Octavo ), ni ejeɔ shishi yɛ WOJI III lɛ mli. ( Nyɔŋma kɛ Enyɔ ).

DUODECIMOS.—Wɔji bibii lɛ fata enɛɛmɛi ahe. I. Huzza ŋshɔŋloo lɛ. II. Algeria ŋshɔŋloo lɛ. III. Porpoise ni yɔɔ daaŋ ni mli tse lɛ.

Kɛha mɛi ni enako hegbɛ krɛdɛɛ ni amɛkɛkase sane lɛ, ekolɛ ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, akɛ esa akɛ ato loo ni bei pii lɛ eshɛɛɛ ninetalɔi ejwɛ loo enumɔ he gbɛjianɔ yɛ WHALES ateŋ—wiemɔ ko, ni, yɛ shishinumɔ ni ehe gbɛi naa lɛ, etsɔɔ bɔ ni amɛdara ha lɛ he susumɔ be fɛɛ be. Shi bɔɔ nii ni ato amɛhe gbɛjianɔ yɛ ŋwɛi akɛ Duodecimoes lɛ ji ŋshɔŋlooi ni tɔmɔ bɛ he, yɛ mishishitsɔɔmɔ ni kɔɔ nɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo ji lɛ he lɛ naa —ni ji loo ni shwieɔ nu, ni ehiɛ tsitsi ni etse ehe lɛ.

WOJI III. ( Duodecimo ), YITSƆƆ 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Enɛ ji ŋshɔŋloo ni afɔɔ namɔ ni anaa yɛ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ shikpɔŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ nɔ lɛ. Gbɛi lɛ jɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ mihe; ejaakɛ ŋshɔŋlooi henɔi fe ekome yɛ, ni esa akɛ afee nɔ ko koni akɛyoo srɔtofeemɔ ni yɔɔ amɛteŋ. Mitsɛɔ lɛ nakai, ejaakɛ efoɔ nu yɛ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ amli be fɛɛ be, ni ŋshɔ ni da lɛ nɔ lɛ yaa nɔ amɛshwieɔ amɛhe kɛyaa ŋwɛi tamɔ tsitsii ni yɔɔ July Ejwɛ asafo babaoo mli lɛ. Bei pii lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ kɛ miishɛɛ jieɔ amɛhefɛo lɛ yi. Amɛyi obɔ kɛ mumɔi kpakpai, ni amɛjɛɔ kɔɔyɔɔ ni tswaa lɛ mli kɛyaa kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ he be fɛɛ be. Amɛji gbekɛbii hii ni hiɔ shi yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ hiɛ be fɛɛ be lɛ. Abuɔ amɛ akɛ amɛji shweremɔ okadi. Kɛji bo diɛŋtsɛ obaanyɛ odamɔ miishɛɛ shii etɛ naa kɛji ona loo ni yɔɔ hewalɛ nɛɛ, no lɛ ŋwɛi aye abua bo; Nyɔŋmɔjamɔ shwɛmɔ mumɔ lɛ bɛ nyɛmli. Huzza Porpoise ni ayeɔ niyenii jogbaŋŋ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ baaha oná mu kpakpa galɔŋ kome kpakpa. Shi nu kpakpa ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ etsitsii amli lɛ jara wa waa diɛŋtsɛ. Eji nɔ ni abiɔ yɛ jwetrifeelɔi kɛ watsifeelɔi ateŋ. Ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi kɛwoɔ amɛ hones lɛ anɔ. Porpoise loo ji niyenii kpakpa, ole. Ekolɛ ebaako ojwɛŋmɔ mli pɛŋ akɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko shwieɔ ŋmɔtɔ. Lɛlɛŋ, e-spout lɛ ji bibioo kwraa bɔ ni anyɛɛɛ ana lɛ oya kwraa. Shi be kroko kɛ́ oná hegbɛ lɛ, kwɛmɔ lɛ; ni no sɛɛ lɛ obaana Sperm whale kpeteŋkpele lɛ diɛŋtsɛ yɛ bibioo mli.

WOJI III. ( Duodecimo ), YITSƆƆ II. ( Algeria Porpoise ).—Ŋshɔ hiɛ ojotswalɔ ko. Eyɛ gbeyei waa. Misusuɔ akɛ, ana lɛ yɛ Pasifik ŋshɔ lɛ mli pɛ. Eda fe Huzza Porpoise lɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, shi nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, asraafonyo nukpa lɛ nɔŋŋ feɔ. Tsɔɔmɔ lɛ, ni ebaabɔ ŋshɔŋloo ko. Miba shi miha lɛ shii abɔ, shi minako lɛ ni amɔ lɛ pɛŋ.

WOJI III. ( Duodecimo ), YITSƆƆ III. ( Porpoise ni yɔɔ daaŋ ni mli tse ).—Porpoise henɔ ni da fe fɛɛ; ni anaa yɛ Pasifik Ŋshɔ lɛ mli pɛ, bɔ ni ale lɛ. Blɔfo gbɛi pɛ ni akɛtsɛɔ lɛ, ni akɛtsɛɔ lɛ kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ ji wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ agbɛi—Right-Whale Porpoise, kɛjɛ shihilɛ ni anaa lɛ titri yɛ nakai Folio lɛ he lɛ mli. Yɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ, esoro lɛ yɛ Huzza Porpoise lɛ he yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, ejaakɛ ebɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni eyɛ miishɛɛ; lɛɛlɛŋ, eji mɔ ko ni yɔɔ fɛo kwraa ni etamɔ nuu kpakpa. Ebɛ ŋmeii yɛ esɛɛ (porpoises krokomɛi babaoo yɛ), eyɛ tsitsi ni yɔɔ fɛo, kɛ India hiŋmɛii ni nuɔ he ni yɔɔ hazel su. Shi enaabu ni mli edɔ lɛ fiteɔ fɛɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ esɛɛ fɛɛ kɛyashi esɛɛ tsitsii lɛ ji sable ni mli kwɔ moŋ, shi kɛlɛ husu ko ni yɔɔ srɔto tamɔ okadi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ mli lɛ, ni atsɛɔ lɛ “tsitsi ni kpɛɔ” lɛ, nakai husu lɛ tsɔɔ ehe kɛjɛɔ eshishifã lɛ nɔ kɛyaa esɛɛgbɛ, ni eyɛ sui sɔrɔtoi enyɔ, ediŋŋ yɛ ŋwɛi kɛ eyɛŋ yɛ shishi. Eyɛŋ lɛ ji eyitso lɛ fã ko, kɛ enaabu muu lɛ fɛɛ, ni no haa efeɔ tamɔ nɔ ni ejo foi kɛjɛ efɔŋfeemɔ ko ni etee niyeli kotoku ko mli lɛ mli kɛkɛ. Nɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ kɛ mealy fã! E-mu lɛ tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo ni afɔɔ namɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ.

Yɛ DUODECIM lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, gbɛjianɔtoo nɛɛ eyaaa hiɛ, ejaakɛ Porpoise ji ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ bibioo fe fɛɛ. Yɛ ŋwɛi lɛ, oyɛ Leviatanbii ni sa kadimɔ waa lɛ fɛɛ. Makane amɛ yɛ amɛ gbɛi ni ji maŋtsɛwe lɛ hiɛ lɛ naa; ejaakɛ ekolɛ wolo ni tamɔ nɛkɛ baanyɛ afee nɔ ni he baaba sɛɛnamɔ kɛha wɔsɛɛ be mli niiamlitaolɔi, ni baanyɛ agbe nɔ ni miyɔɔ yɛ biɛ shi mije shishi lɛ naa. Kɛji akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni nyiɛ sɛɛ nɛɛ ateŋ eko, abaamɔ lɛ ni akadi lɛ yɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ, no lɛ abaanyɛ akɛwo Gbɛjianɔtoo nɛɛ mli oya, yɛ e-Folio, Octavo, loo Duodecimo magnitude lɛ naa:—Bottle-Nose Whale lɛ; ŋshɔŋloo ni ji Junk lɛ; ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ Pudding Yitso lɛ; Kap Ŋshɔŋloo lɛ; Whale ni Nyiɛɔ Hiɛ lɛ; Kanɔn Ŋshɔ lɛ; Scragg Ŋshɔŋloo lɛ; Akɔɔble Whale lɛ; Shɔjiaŋ Ŋshɔ lɛ; Iceberg Ŋshɔŋloo lɛ; Quog Ŋshɔŋloo lɛ; Blue Whale lɛ; etc. Kɛjɛ Iceland, Dutch, kɛ blema Ŋleshi maŋ onukpai lɛ adɛŋ lɛ, abaanyɛ atsĩ ŋshɔŋlooi krokomɛi ni bɛ nɔmimaa, ni ajɔɔ amɛ kɛ gbɛi ni bɛ bulɛ srɔtoi fɛɛ lɛ ahe saji asɛɛ. Shi mijieɔ amɛ kɛjɛɔ mli akɛ nɔ ni etsɛ kwraa; ni enyɛŋ eye ebua kwraa ni esusu amɛhe akɛ amɛji gbeei kɛkɛ, ni Leviatan jamɔ eyi mli obɔ, shi amɛtsɔɔɔ nɔ ko nɔ ko.
