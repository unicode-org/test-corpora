YITSƆƆ 53. Gam.

Yiŋtoo ni tamɔ nɔ ni ha Ahab eyaaa ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ ni wɔwie he lɛ mli ji enɛ: kɔɔyɔɔ kɛ ŋshɔ lɛ tsɔɔ ahum. Shi eji jeee nakai po kulɛ, ekolɛ, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, eboteŋ lɛlɛ lɛ mli—kɛ wɔkojo kɛtsɔ ejeŋba ni efee yɛ sɛɛ mli yɛ bei ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ amli lɛ nɔ—kɛji nakai eji akɛ, kɛtsɔ gbɛ ni atsɔɔ nɔ ajieɔ yi lɛ nɔ lɛ, enine shɛ hetoo ni ejaaa nɔ yɛ sanebimɔ ni ebi lɛ nɔ. Ejaakɛ, taakɛ eba lɛ yɛ naagbee lɛ, esusu he akɛ ekɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko ni eleee lɛ akabɔ, minitii enumɔ po, ja ebaanyɛ ekɛ nakai saji ni etaoɔ waa lɛ ekomɛi aha. Shi enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ baanyɛ ahi shi akɛ nɔ ni asusuuu he jogbaŋŋ, ani jeee nɔ ko ni awie yɛ biɛ yɛ bɔ ni ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛlɛi kɛ amɛhe kpeɔ yɛ maŋsɛɛ ŋshɔi amli, ni titri lɛ yɛ he ko ni ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ fãa gbɛ yɛ lɛ he.

Kɛji gbɔi enyɔ ni miifo Pine Barrens ni yɔɔ New York Maŋ lɛ mli lɛ, loo Salisbury Gɔŋkpɔ ni yɔɔ England ni efee amaŋfɔ tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ lɛ; kɛji akɛ amɛkɛ amɛhe kpe yɛ kooi ni bɛ gbɔfeemɔ mli tamɔ nɛkɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ, nɛkɛ mɛi enyɔ nɛɛ, yɛ amɛwala hewɔ lɛ, nyɛŋ atsi amɛhe kɛjɛ amɛhe ŋamɔ he jogbaŋŋ; kɛ shimɔ fioo koni akɛtsake adafitswaa lɛ; kɛ, ekolɛ, ni amɛta shi be fioo ni amɛjɔɔ amɛhe yɛ lala mli: no lɛ, kwɛ bɔ ni eji adebɔɔ naa nɔ akɛ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli Pine Barrens kɛ Salisbury Gbɛjegbɛi ni husu bɛ he lɛ anɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛlɛi enyɔ ni miiye amɛhe fɛo yɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ naagbeehei lɛ—yɛ Fanning Ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ kome pɛ, loo King’s Mills ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ nɔ; bɔ ni eji adebɔɔ naa nɔ ha, mikɛɔ akɛ, yɛ shihilɛi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ, esaaa akɛ nɛkɛ lɛlɛi nɛɛ tsakeɔ nyɔŋmɔŋtɛi kɛkɛ, shi moŋ amɛbaafee tsakpaa ni bɛŋkɛ kpaakpa, ni ji naanyobɔɔ kɛ gbɔmɛi ateŋ tsakpaa babaoo. Ni titri lɛ, ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni enɛ ji sane ko ni yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ, yɛ lɛlɛi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkpɔ kome nɔ, ni amɛ onukpai, asraafoi onukpai, kɛ hii lɛ ateŋ mɛi fioo ko pɛ le amɛhe diɛŋtsɛ lɛ ahe; ni nɔ ni jɛɔ mli baa ji akɛ, aná shia nibii ni asumɔɔ lɛ srɔtoi fɛɛ ni awieɔ he.

Kɛha lɛlɛ ni bɛ be kakadaŋŋ lɛ, ekolɛ kponɔgbɛ husu lɛ yɛ woji yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli; bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, ebaakwɛ koni eha ená woji komɛi ni kɔɔ gbi ko he yɛ afi loo enyɔ sɛɛ fe naagbee nɔ lɛ yɛ efaili ni bɛ faŋŋ ni ekɛ eniji ewo mli lɛ nɔ. Ni yɛ nakai bulɛ lɛ najiaŋ lɛ, lɛlɛ ni yaa kponɔgbɛ lɛ baaná ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nilee hee kɛjɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔkpɔ ni ekolɛ ebaaya lɛ nɔ, ni eji nɔ ko ni he hiaa lɛ fe fɛɛ. Ni yɛ gbɛ ni yaa hiɛ nɔ lɛ, enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ baafee anɔkwale yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛlɛi ni foɔ amɛhe gbɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ anɔ lɛ he, eyɛ mli akɛ amɛ hu amɛbɛ shia be kakadaŋŋ nakai nɔŋŋ moŋ. Ejaakɛ ekolɛ amɛteŋ mɔ kome nine shɛ woji ni akɛjɛ lɛlɛ ko ni ji etɛ nɔ, ni amrɔ nɛɛ eyɛ shɔŋŋ lɛ dɛŋ lɛ nɔ; ni ekolɛ nakai woji lɛ ekomɛi ji mɛi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ ni ekɛkpeɔ amrɔ nɛɛ mli lɛ anɔ̃. Kɛfata he lɛ, amɛbaatsake ŋshɔŋlooi ahe adafitswaa, ni amɛbaagba sane ni amɛkpɛlɛɔ nɔ. Ejaakɛ jeee akɛ amɛkɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ ahenumɔi fɛɛ pɛ baakpe, shi moŋ amɛkɛ miishɛɛ krɛdɛɛi fɛɛ ni jɛɔ nɔ kome ni amɛtaoɔ kɛ osharai ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛyɔɔ lɛ mli baa lɛ baakpe.

Ni maŋ srɔtofeemɔ hu haŋ srɔtofeemɔ ko ni he hiaa waa aba; ni ji, be fɛɛ be ni kui enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ wieɔ wiemɔ kome, taakɛ eji yɛ Amerikabii kɛ Blɔfobii ahe lɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ, kɛha nɔmimaa lɛ, kɛjɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛlɔi ayibɔ fioo lɛ mli lɛ, kpeei ni tamɔ nɛkɛ baaa shii abɔ moŋ, ni kɛ́ amɛba lɛ, ehi jogbaŋŋ tsɔ akɛ hiɛgbele ko baaba amɛteŋ; ejaakɛ o-Blɔfonyo lɛ ji mɔ ko ni kɛ ehe wooo mli moŋ, ni o-Yankee lɛ, esumɔɔɔ nakai nɔ lɛ yɛ mɔ ko mɔ ko he fe lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe. Kɛfata he lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ, Ŋleshi ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ náa maŋtiase mli nɔyeli ko nɔ hewalɛ yɛ Amerika ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ anɔ; yɛ Nantucketernyo kakadaŋŋ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ he, kɛ ekpokpaa nɔ nifeemɔi ni atsɔɔɔ mli lɛ, akɛ ŋshɔ mli okwaafonyo ko. Shi he ni nɛkɛ nɔyeli ni fe fɛɛ yɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ amli nɛɛ yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ lɛ, ebaawa akɛ aaakɛɛ, akɛni Yankeesbii lɛ gbeɔ ŋshɔŋlooi babaoo yɛ gbi kome mli, kɛ abua naa lɛ, fe Ŋleshibii lɛ fɛɛ, kɛ abua naa, yɛ afii nyɔŋma mli lɛ. Shi enɛ ji gbɔjɔmɔ bibioo ko ni bɛ awui yɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbeelɔi lɛ amli, ni Nantucketerbii lɛ kɛwooo etsui mli tsɔ; ekolɛ, ejaakɛ ele akɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eyɛ gbɔjɔmɔi fioo komɛi.

No hewɔ lɛ, wɔnaa akɛ yɛ lɛlɛi fɛɛ ni nyiɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli srɔtoi lɛ ateŋ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɛi lɛ ji mɛi ni yɔɔ yiŋtoo ni fe fɛɛ ni amɛaatsɔ nɔ amɛfee mɛi ni kɛ amɛhe feɔ sharamɔ—ni nakai amɛji. Yɛ be mli ni, jarayeli lɛlɛi komɛi ni foɔ amɛhe yɛ Atlantik Ŋshɔ lɛ teŋgbɛ lɛ, bei pii lɛ amɛbaaho ni amɛwieee nɔ ko kwraa ni tsɔɔ akɛ amɛyooo amɛhe, ni amɛfoɔ amɛhe yɛ ŋshɔi wuji lɛ anɔ, tamɔ dandies ni yɔɔ Broadway lɛ akuu ko; ni be fɛɛ be lɛ, ekolɛ, amɛkɛ amɛhe woɔ shimɔ ni bɛ naagbee mli yɛ amɛhe rig lɛ he. Yɛ Men-of-War gbɛfaŋ lɛ, kɛ́ amɛná hegbɛ ni amɛkɛkpe yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ, amɛtsɔɔ kwashiai asane kɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli klɛŋklɛŋ, ni ji aflaŋaai ni akɛwoɔ ŋwɛi, ni etamɔ nɔ ni tsuiŋ suɔmɔ kpakpa kɛ nyɛmimɛi asuɔmɔ babaoo bɛ he kwraa. As touching Slave-ships kpee lɛ, mɛni hewɔ, amɛyɛ oyaiyeli ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ waa mli, amɛjoɔ foi kɛjɛɔ amɛhe oya bɔ ni amɛaanyɛ. Ni yɛ Pirates gbɛfaŋ lɛ, kɛ́ amɛná hegbɛ amɛfo amɛhe krɔs wui lɛ, klɛŋklɛŋ nyɔŋmɔŋtɛi lɛ ji—“Kɔɔyɔɔji enyiɛ?”—gbɛ ni ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ tswaa lɛ nɔŋŋ nɔ—“Barrel enyiɛ?” Ni kɛ́ aha nakai sanebimɔ lɛ hetoo lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ ojotswalɔi lɛ tsiɔ amɛhe kɛjɛɔ amɛhe tɛ̃ɛ, ejaakɛ amɛji hɛl gbɔmɛi fɔji ni yɔɔ afãi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ, ni amɛsumɔɔɔ ni amɛnaa amɛhe mfoniri fɔji babaoo fe nine.

Shi kwɛmɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ Nyɔŋmɔ gbeyeishemɔ, anɔkwafo, ni ejieee ehe kpo, gbɔfeemɔ, mɛi ni kɛ amɛhe feɔ sharamɔ, ni yɔɔ heyeli ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ! Mɛni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ feɔ kɛ́ ekɛ ŋshɔŋloo kroko kpe yɛ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ mli? Eyɛ “ Gam ,” nɔ ko ni lɛlɛi krokomɛi fɛɛ leee kwraa, ni amɛnuko gbɛi lɛ po he dã; ni kɛji akɛ amɛnu he yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ, amɛfeɔ ŋmɔlɔ kɛkɛ, ni amɛtĩɔ shwɛmɔ nibii ni kɔɔ “spouters” kɛ “blubber-boilers,” kɛ nibii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni tamɔ ŋmɔlɔ fɛfɛji lɛ amli ekoŋŋ. Nɔ hewɔ ni eji akɛ Jarayelɔi-ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi fɛɛ, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu Pirates kɛ Man-of-War hii fɛɛ, kɛ Nyɔŋ-lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ, sumɔɔ henumɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni ji heguɔgbee ni amɛyɔɔ kɛha Whale-lɛlɛi lɛ; enɛ ji sanebimɔ ko ni ebaawa akɛ aaaha hetoo. Ejaakɛ, yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ojotswalɔi agbɛfaŋ lɛ, kɛɛmɔ akɛ, esa akɛ masumɔ ni male kɛji nakai amɛnitsumɔ lɛ yɛ anunyam krɛdɛɛ ko yɛ he. Bei komɛi lɛ, egbeɔ naa yɛ nɔ ni afɔɔɔɔ feemɔ mli, lɛɛlɛŋ; shi yɛ tso lɛ nɔ pɛ. Ni kɛfata he lɛ, kɛ́ awo gbɔmɔ nɔ yɛ nakai gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ lɛ, ebɛ shishitoo kpakpa kɛha ehiɛ ni nɔ kwɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, mimuɔ naa akɛ yɛ ehe ni ekɛwoɔ ehe nɔ akɛ ewó ehe nɔ fe ŋshɔŋloo ko lɛ, yɛ nakai wiemɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ ojotswalɔ lɛ bɛ shishitoo kpakpa ni ebaadamɔ nɔ.

Shi mɛni ji Gam? Obaanyɛ okɛ onine ni tsɔɔ nɔ ko lɛ atsu nii ni okɛjo foi kɛya ŋwɛi kɛ shikpɔŋ yɛ wiemɔi ashishitsɔɔmɔ woji amli, ni onaaa wiemɔ lɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ. Dr. Johnson enine shɛɛɛ nakai nilee lɛ nɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ; Noa Webster adeka lɛ nyɛɛɛ ahiɛ mli. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, nɛkɛ wiemɔ ni jieɔ mɔ he kpo nɛɛ nɔŋŋ ji nɔ ni akɛtsuɔ nii be fɛɛ be amrɔ nɛɛ afii babaoo nɛ yɛ Yankeebii ni afɔ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ aaafee akpei nyɔŋma kɛ enumɔ ateŋ. Eji anɔkwale akɛ, ehe miihia ni atsɔɔ shishi, ni esa akɛ akɛwo Lexicon lɛ mli. Kɛ nakai susumɔ lɛ, ha mikɛ nikasemɔ atsɔɔ mli.

GAM. NOUN— Whaleships enyɔ ( loo nɔ ni fe nakai ) akutsoŋ kpee , ni bei pii lɛ efeɔ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ gbɛfaa he; beni, beni amɛtsake nyɔŋmɔŋtɛi lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ tsakeɔ amɛsaramɔi: lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi enyɔ ni eshwɛ, kɛha be lɛ, yɛ lɛlɛ kome mli, kɛ lɛlɛ mli hefatalɔi onukpai enyɔ lɛ yɛ ekroko lɛ mli.

Nɔ bibioo kroko hu yɛ ni kɔɔ Gamming he ni esaaa akɛ wɔhiɛ kpaa nɔ yɛ biɛ. Nitsumɔi fɛɛ yɛ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ nibii bibii ni kɔɔ amɛhe fitsofitso; nakai nɔŋŋ eji yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhe lɛ hu he. Yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ mli, tawuu mli gbɔmɔ, loo nyɔŋyeli lɛlɛ mli lɛ, kɛ́ akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ miiwo he fɛɛ he yɛ elɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ, be fɛɛ be lɛ, etaa lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ tsɔji lɛ anɔ yɛ sɛi ko ni yɔɔ fɛo ni bei komɛi lɛ, ekɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ nɔ yɛ jɛmɛ, ni bei pii lɛ, ekɛ milliner tsɔne bibioo ko ni yɔɔ fɛo ni akɛ gay kpãai kɛ tsɔnei ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ ewula ehe lɛ kudɔɔ ehe. Shi whale-lɛlɛ lɛ bɛ sɛi yɛ sɛɛgbɛ, sofa ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ko bɛ, ni ebɛ tsɔne ni akɛkwɛɔ lɛ kwraa. Be ni nɔ kwɔ lɛɛlɛŋ, kɛji akɛ atsɔ shwiili nɔ yɛ nu lɛ mli tamɔ onukpai ni egbɔ ni yɔɔ patent sɛii amli lɛ. Ni yɛ tiller he lɛ, whale-lɛlɛ lɛ ekpɛlɛɛɛ yoofeemɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ nɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ; ni no hewɔ lɛ taakɛ eji yɛ shwɛmɔ mli lɛ, esa akɛ lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi fɛɛ ashi lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni no hewɔ lɛ akɛni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ loo harpooneer lɛ ji mɔ ni fata he hewɔ lɛ, nakai mɔ ni yɔɔ shishi lɛ ji lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ yɛ be lɛ mli, ni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ, ni bɛ he ko ni ebaata lɛ, agbala lɛ kɛyaa esaramɔ lɛ mli ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛdamɔ shi tamɔ pine tso. Ni bei pii lɛ obaana akɛ, akɛni ehiɛ kã je muu lɛ fɛɛ ni anaa lɛ hiŋmɛii ni kã enɔ kɛjɛɔ lɛlɛi enyɔ lɛ asɛɛ hewɔ lɛ, nɛkɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni damɔ shi nɛɛ fɛɛ yɛ wala mli yɛ bɔ ni ehe hiaa ni ekwɛ ebulɛ nɔ kɛtsɔ enaji ni ekwɛɔ nɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ he. Ni enɛ hu jeee sane ko ni yɔɔ mlɛo kwraa; ejaakɛ yɛ esɛɛ lɛ, tsɔne kudɔmɔ tsɔne wulu ni jeɔ kpo lɛ miitswa lɛ amrɔ nɛɛ yɛ esɛɛ bibioo lɛ mli, ni tsɔne ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ miitswa ehe kɛtsɔ enaji ni ekɛtswaa ehiɛ lɛ nɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, efo ehe kwraa yɛ hiɛ kɛ esɛɛ, ni ebaanyɛ egbɛ eshwa kɛya afã pɛ kɛtsɔ enaji ni ekpã lɛ anɔ ni eeehi shi lɛ nɔ; shi bei pii lɛ, lɛlɛ lɛ gbee ni tswaa trukaa ni yɔɔ basabasa lɛ baaya shɔŋŋ ni ekɛbaagbee lɛ shi, ejaakɛ shishitoo lɛ kɛlɛ jeee nɔ ko ni elɛɛmɔ ni kɛ lɛ kpãa gbee bɛ mli. Feemɔ tsei enyɔ ni gbɛ eshwa kɛkɛ, ni onyɛŋ odamɔ shi. No sɛɛ lɛ, ekoŋŋ lɛ, efeŋ kɔkɔɔkɔ yɛ je lɛ hiŋmɛii ni ebɔle amɛhe lɛ ahiɛ, efeŋ kɔkɔɔkɔ, mikɛɛ, kɛha nɛkɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafonyo nukpa ni shwieɔ shi nɛɛ ni ana lɛ ni ekɛ ehe miiwo nɔ bibioo ko mli kɛtsɔ eniji ni ekɛmɔɔ nɔ ko nɔ lɛ nɔ; lɛɛlɛŋ, akɛ ehe-kɛ-afɔleshaa fɛɛ ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ lɛ he okadi lɛ, bei pii lɛ ekɛ eniji woɔ etrɔsa kotokui amli; shi ekolɛ akɛni bei pii lɛ amɛji niji ni da waa ni tsii hewɔ lɛ, ewoɔ amɛ kɛyaa jɛmɛ kɛha ballast. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, nɔkwɛmɔnii komɛi eba, ni ji nɔ ni ama nɔ mi jogbaŋŋ hu, ni ale lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ yɛ be ko loo enyɔ ko ni he hiaa waa mli, yɛ trukaa ahum ni tswaa lɛ mli—koni emɔmɔ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni bɛŋkɛ lɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ yitsɔi, ni emɔ jɛmɛ tamɔ gbele ni yɔɔ gbeyei.





YITSƆƆ 54. Maŋ-Ho Sane lɛ.

( Taakɛ akɛɛ yɛ Golden Inn lɛ. )

Cape of Good Hope, kɛ nu kpokpai fɛɛ ni ebɔle jɛmɛ lɛ tamɔ gbɛjegbɛ wulu ko mli koŋji ejwɛ komɛi ni akadi, he ni okɛ gbɛfalɔi babaoo kpeɔ yɛ fe hei krokomɛi fɛɛ.

Beni ewie Goney wiemɔ lɛ sɛɛ etsɛɛɛ tsɔ ni akɛ ŋshɔŋloo kroko ni miiya shia, ni ji Town-Ho * lɛ kpe. Nɔ ni miihe ashɛ Polynesiabii fɛɛ ji mɛi ni kwɛɔ enɔ. Yɛ gam kuku ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ, eha wɔ Moby Dick he adafitswaa ni mli wa. Yɛ mɛi komɛi agbɛfaŋ lɛ, miishɛɛ ni amɛyɔɔ yɛ White Whale lɛ he lɛ tee hiɛ waa agbɛnɛ kɛtsɔ shihilɛ ko ni yɔɔ Town-Ho sane lɛ mli lɛ nɔ, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ naakpɛɛ, ni aku sɛɛ akɛ nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ Nyɔŋmɔ kojomɔi ni bei komɛi lɛ akɛɛ eyeɔ gbɔmɛi komɛi anɔ lɛ ateŋ ekome saramɔ ko fata ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he.
