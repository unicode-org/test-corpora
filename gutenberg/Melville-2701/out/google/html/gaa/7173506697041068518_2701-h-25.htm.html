Epilɔg

“NI MI KƐ MIJO JI NI MIBAAKƐ BO” Hiob.

Drama lɛ egbe naa. Belɛ mɛni hewɔ mɔ ko jeɔ kpo yɛ biɛ lɛ?— Ejaakɛ mɔ kome je lɛlɛ ni efite lɛ mli diɛŋtsɛ.

Eba trukaa, akɛ yɛ Parsee lɛ laajemɔ sɛɛ lɛ, mi ji mɔ ni Fates lɛ hala koni maŋɔ Ahab gãimlibii lɛ najiaŋ, beni nakai gãimlibii lɛ ŋɔ gbɛhe ni ehiɛ efolo lɛ; lɛ nɔŋŋ, ni beni yɛ naagbee gbi lɛ nɔ lɛ, ajie hii etɛ lɛ kɛjɛ lɛlɛ ni hosoɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ashwie lɛ kɛtee sɛɛgbɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, beni mifoɔ yɛ nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ naagbee lɛ nɔ, ni mikwɛɔ lɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ, beni lɛlɛ ni gbee shi lɛ mli nu ni efite lɛ fã lɛ shɛ mihe lɛ, no mli lɛ, shi fiofio lɛ, agbala mi kɛtee ŋshɔkei ni gbeɔ naa lɛ ahe. Beni miyashɛ he lɛ, no mli lɛ eba shi ni etsɔ nubu ko ni mli edɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, ebɔle ehe kɛbɔle ehe, ni ekɛ ehe miibɔle ehe be fɛɛ be kɛmiiya ŋmei diŋ ni tamɔ bɔtin lɛ he yɛ nakai kooloo ni shwieɔ shi blɛoo lɛ sɛɛgbɛ, tamɔ Ixion kroko ni mifee lɛ. Till, ni enine shɛ nakai ŋwɛi ŋta ni he hiaa lɛ nɔ lɛ, ŋmei diŋ ni yaa ŋwɛi lɛ tswa; ni agbɛnɛ, akɛni ejie ehe yɛ e-spring ni yɔɔ ŋaa lɛ hewɔ lɛ, ni, yɛ bɔ ni ekɛ hewalɛ babaoo teɔ shi lɛ hewɔ lɛ, adeka wala-buoy ni tu kɛjɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli kɛtee shɔŋŋ lɛ, egbee shi, ni efo yɛ mimasɛi. Akɛni nakai gbonyo adeka lɛ tsirɛ mi hewɔ lɛ, nɔ ni miihe ashɛ gbi kome kɛ gbɛkɛ muu fɛɛ lɛ, mikɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔkpɔ ko ni yɔɔ blɛoo ni tamɔ dirge lɛ nɔ. Sharks ni yeee mɔ awui lɛ, amɛhoɔ kɛhoɔ tamɔ nɔ ni amɛkɛ dadei ewo amɛdaaŋ; ŋshɔ mli ŋmeii ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ fã gbɛ kɛ amɛnaabui ni awo amɛhe tsɔi. Yɛ gbi ni ji enyɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, lɛlɛ ko bɛŋkɛ, ebɛŋkɛ, ni eŋɔ mi yɛ naagbee. Rachel ni tsɔɔ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ ji mɔ ni yɛ ebii ni elaaje lɛ asɛɛtaomɔ mli lɛ, ena ohiafo kroko pɛ.
