YITSƆƆ 101. Dekanta lɛ.

Ere Ŋleshi lɛlɛ lɛ baafo kɛjɛ hiŋmɛi nɔ, kɛji akɛ ato shi yɛ biɛ lɛ, no mli lɛ ejɛ London, ni atsɛ lɛ yɛ Samuel Enderby ni egbo, nakai maŋ lɛ mli jarayelɔ, ni ji Enderby & Sons ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ shĩa ni ehe gbɛi waa lɛ shishijee lɛ gbɛi nɔ; shĩa ko ni yɛ mi-whaleman ohiafo lɛ susumɔ naa lɛ, ebaaa Tudor kɛ Bourbon maŋtsɛmɛi ashiai ni efee ekome lɛ sɛɛ shɔŋŋ, yɛ yinɔsane mli miishɛɛ diɛŋtsɛ he. Be enyiɛ, dani wɔ-Nuŋtsɔ lɛ afi 1775 lɛ, nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ashihilɛhe wulu nɛɛ hi shi lɛ, mi-loo-woji babaoo lɛ etsɔɔɔ mli faŋŋ; shi yɛ nakai afi lɛ mli (1775) lɛ, ekɛ klɛŋklɛŋ Ŋleshi lɛlɛi ni kwɛɔ Sperm Whale lɛ nɔ daa lɛ wo mli; eyɛ mli akɛ afii nyɔŋmai abɔ ni eho lɛ amli (kɛjɛ afi 1726 kɛbaa nɛɛ) wɔ Coffins kɛ Maceys ni yɔɔ ekãa ni jɛ Nantucket kɛ Vineyard lɛ kɛ lɛlɛi wuji etiu nakai Leviathan lɛ sɛɛ, shi yɛ Atlantik Kooyigbɛ kɛ Anaigbɛ pɛ: jeee he kroko. Aŋma yɛ biɛ faŋŋ akɛ, Nantucketbii lɛ ji mɛi klɛŋklɛŋ ni kɛ dade ni yɔɔ jeŋba kpakpa tswa Sperm Whale kpeteŋkpele lɛ yɛ adesai ateŋ; ni akɛ afii ohai fã mli lɛ, amɛ pɛ ji gbɔmɛi ni yɔɔ shikpɔŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ nɔ ni tswa lɛ tsɔne ni tamɔ nɛkɛ.

Yɛ afi 1778 lɛ, lɛlɛ kpakpa ko, ni ji Amelia lɛ, asaa he kɛha yiŋtoo ni yɔɔ faŋŋ lɛ, ni yɛ Enderbybii ni he wa lɛ pɛ akudɔmɔ mli lɛ, ekɛ ekãa bɔle Cape Horn, ni eji klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ yɛ maji lɛ ateŋ ni shwie ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ ni ji whale-lɛlɛ srɔtoi fɛɛ yɛ South Sea wulu lɛ nɔ. Gbɛfaa lɛ ji ŋaa kɛ naagba; ni eku esɛɛ kɛtee he ni eyɔɔ lɛ ni ekɛ nu ni jara wa lɛ eyi ehiɛ lɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni lɛlɛi krokomɛi, Ŋleshi kɛ Amerika lɛ nyiɛ Amelia nɔkwɛmɔnɔ lɛ sɛɛ, ni no hewɔ lɛ, ashɛrɛ Sperm Whale shikpɔŋ wulu ni yɔɔ Pasifik lɛ mli lɛ ashwie shi. Shi akɛni amɛtsui enyɔɔɔ amɛmli yɛ nifeemɔ kpakpa nɛɛ hewɔ lɛ, shĩa ni nijiaŋ ejeee wui lɛ tsi ehe ekoŋŋ: Samuel kɛ ebihii lɛ fɛɛ—mɛi enyiɛ, amɛnyɛ pɛ le—ni amɛkwɛ amɛ nɔ amrɔ nɔŋŋ, ni misusuɔ akɛ, yɛ amɛshika nɔ lɛ, no tsirɛ Britania nɔyeli lɛ koni etsu tawuu lɛlɛ ni ji Rattler lɛ kɛtee ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa ni ji South disco lɛ mli. Rattler lɛ ni ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafoi a-Post-Captain ko ji mɔ ni kudɔɔ lɛ lɛ, efã gbɛ ni ekɛ gbee ni wa tsuɔ nii, ni etsu sɔɔmɔ ko; bɔ ni fa ha lɛ ejeee kpo. Shi jeee enɛ pɛ kɛkɛ. Yɛ afi 1819 lɛ, nakai shia lɛ nɔŋŋ fee amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ discovery whale lɛlɛ ko, koni amɛyakwɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ amli. Nakai lɛlɛ lɛ—ni atsɛɔ lɛ jogbaŋŋ akɛ “Syren” lɛ—fee ŋshɔ hiɛ kaa ni nɔ kwɔ; ni nakai ji bɔ ni Japanbii Whaling Ground wulu lɛ bafee nɔ ni ale klɛŋklɛŋ kwraa. Kapten Coffin, ni ji Nantucketnyo lɛ ji mɔ ni kudɔ Syren lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfaa ni ehe gbɛi nɛɛ mli lɛ.

No hewɔ lɛ, woo fɛɛ kɛha Enderbibii, ni misusuɔ akɛ amɛshia lɛ yɛ kɛbashi ŋmɛnɛ lɛ; eyɛ mli akɛ ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ esa akɛ shishijee Samuel lɛ atsɔ ekpãa lɛ nɔ kɛtee je kroko lɛ Anaigbɛ Ŋshɔ wulu lɛ mli be saŋŋ ni eho moŋ.

Lɛlɛ ni akɛ egbɛi wo mli lɛ sa woo, ejaakɛ eji lɛlɛ ni fãa oya waa, ni eji lɛlɛ ni hi jogbaŋŋ yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ. Mibote mli shikome gbɛkɛ teŋ yɛ he ko ni bɛŋkɛ Patagonia ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni minu flip kpakpa down yɛ forecastle lɛ mli. Eji gam kpakpa ko ni wɔyɔɔ, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛji trumps—susuma fɛɛ susuma ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ. Wala kuku kɛha amɛ, kɛ gbele ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ. Ni nakai gam kpakpa ni miyɔɔ lɛ—be kakadaŋŋ, be kakadaŋŋ sɛɛ beni Ahab onukpa lɛ kɛ etsitsi ni akɛ shwuɔ wu fee lɛ ta etsei lɛ ahe lɛ—haa misusuɔ nakai lɛlɛ lɛ Saxon gbɔfeemɔ ni nɔ kwɔ, ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ lɛ he; ni mi-sɔfo lɛ hiɛ akpa minɔ, ​​ni abonsam akai mi, kɛji mihiɛ kpa nɔ gbi ko. Tsɔlɛ? Ani mikɛɛ wɔyɛ flip? Hɛɛ, ni wɔfoɔ mli yɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa kome mli galɔn nyɔŋma mli; ni beni hulutsoo be lɛ ba (ejaakɛ eyɛ hulutsoo yɛ jɛmɛ kɛtsɔ Patagonia), ni atsɛ niji fɛɛ—saralɔi kɛ mɛi fɛɛ—kɛtee ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi anɔ lɛ, wɔhe wa waa aahu akɛ ehe bahia ni wɔkɛ wɔhe awo ŋwɛi yɛ ŋshɔkei amli; ni wɔkɛ wɔ jakɛtii lɛ ashishifãi lɛ wo lɛlɛ lɛ mli yɛ nilee ni bɛ mli, bɔni afee ni wɔkɛ wɔhe awo jɛmɛ, ni wɔkɛ ahum ni naa wa waa lɛ shwie shi oya, ni eji kɔkɔbɔɔ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ kɛha tar fɛɛ ni etɔ dãa lɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi lɛ eyaaa ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ; ni be fioo ko lɛ, wɔfo shi, ni wɔhiɛ kã shi, ni ehe bahia ni wɔho flip lɛ he ekoŋŋ, eyɛ mli akɛ ŋmei ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni shwieɔ shi lɛ tswaa forecastle scuttle lɛ nɔ lɛ, moŋ lɛ, etsɔ mli babaoo ni ekɛwo mli bɔ ni sa mihe.

Tsina loo lɛ hi jogbaŋŋ—ewa, shi eyɛ gbɔmɔtso. Amɛkɛɛ tsina-tsina loo ni; mɛi krokomɛi hu, akɛ dromedary tsina loo ni; shi mileee, kɛ nɔmimaa, bɔ ni fee ni no ba lɛ. Amɛyɛ dumplings hu; dumplings bibii, shi amɛhe wa, ni amɛji ŋwɛibɔfoi ni tamɔ amɛhe, ni amɛnyɛŋ amɛfite. Misusu akɛ obaanyɛ onu amɛhe, ni okɛ amɛ abɔle omli beni ami amɛ sɛɛ. Kɛji okoto shi kɛtee hiɛ tsɔ lɛ, okɛ ohe woɔ oshara mli ni amɛbaatswa bo tamɔ billiard-bɔɔlu. Blodo lɛ—shi anyɛɛɛ aye abua no; kɛfata he lɛ, eji tsofa ni gbeɔ skorbut; yɛ kukufoo mli lɛ, tsɔne he nyɔmɔwoo hee pɛ ni amɛyɔɔ lɛ yɔɔ blodo lɛ mli. Shi forecastle lɛ mli waaa kwraa, ni ewaaa kwraa akɛ oootsɔ mli kɛya koŋ ko ni mli ewo duŋ nɔ kɛ́ oye. Shi kɛ akɛ fɛɛ to he lɛ, ekɛ lɛ tee lɔle mli kɛtee tsɔne kudɔmɔ he, ni esusu bɔ ni nihoolɔ lɛ tsɔnei ni akɛshãa nii lɛ dalɛ ji lɛ he, ni lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-parchment tsɔnei ni yɔɔ wala mli lɛ fata he; hiɛ kɛ sɛɛ, mikɛɔ akɛ, Samuel Enderby lɛ ji lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ; ni ji gbɛfaa kpakpa kɛ babaoo; flip kpakpa kɛ hewalɛ; crack nanemɛi fɛɛ, kɛ kapital kɛjɛ bɔɔtu tsitsii kɛyashi hat-band.

Shi mɛni hewɔ, nyɛsusuɔ he akɛ, Samuel Enderby, kɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔŋlooi krokomɛi ni mile amɛ lɛ—shi jeee amɛ fɛɛ—ji lɛlɛi ni ehe gbɛi, ni feɔ gbɔi lɛ tamɔ nɛkɛ; ni hoɔ tsina loo lɛ, kɛ blodo lɛ, kɛ kan lɛ, kɛ shwɛmɔ lɛ he lɛ; ni etsɛɛɛ ni nijiaŋ eje wui yɛ niyeli, kɛ ninumɔ, kɛ ŋmɔlɔ he? Makɛɛ bo. Miishɛɛ kpakpa ni fa babaoo ni nɛkɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔŋlooi nɛɛ yɔɔ lɛ ji nɔ ko ni esa akɛ akwɛ yinɔsane mli. Ni mikɛ mihe wooo yinɔsane mli ŋshɔŋlooi ahe niiamlitaomɔ mli kwraa, yɛ be mli ni etamɔ nɔ ni ehe miihia lɛ.

Hollandbii, Zealandbii, kɛ Denmarkbii tsɔ Ŋleshibii lɛ ahiɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛ mli; ni amɛná wiemɔi babaoo ni yɔɔ lolo yɛ wuoyeli mli lɛ kɛjɛ edɛŋ; kɛ nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, amɛ blema nibii ni eyi obɔ kɛ fɔ, ni taa niyenii kɛ ninumɔ babaoo he. Ejaakɛ, taakɛ eji nɔ ko ni afɔɔ feemɔ lɛ, Ŋleshi jarayeli lɛlɛ lɛ shwieɔ mɛi ni tsuɔ nii yɛ mli lɛ anɔ; shi jeee nakai eji yɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhe lɛ he. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ Blɔfo mli lɛ, nɛkɛ nɔ ni ji whaling good cheer nɛɛ jeee nɔ ni yaa nɔ daa kɛ adebɔɔ naa nɔ, shi moŋ nɔ ni baa trukaa kɛ nɔ ko pɔtɛɛ; ni, no hewɔ lɛ, esa akɛ ehiɛ shishijee krɛdɛɛ ko, ni atsɔɔ mli yɛ biɛ, ni abaagbala mli jogbaŋŋ lolo.

Beni mitaoɔ Leviathan yinɔsaji amli lɛ, mikɛ blema Dutch wolo ko kpe, ni yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe ŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ mli lɛ hewɔ lɛ, mile akɛ esa akɛ ekɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe. Yitso lɛ ji, “Dan Coopman,” no hewɔ lɛ mimu sane naa akɛ esa akɛ enɛ afee Amsterdam ŋshɔŋloo ko ni tsuɔ nii yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhe lɛ kaimɔi ni jara wa waa, ejaakɛ esa akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛ fɛɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛlɛ atere ekɔɔŋtso lɛ. Awo mi hewalɛ yɛ susumɔ nɛɛ mli kɛtsɔ mina akɛ eji “Fitz Swackhammer” kome ni afee lɛ nɔ. Shi minaanyo Dr. Snodhead, nuu ko ni le nii waa, ni ji Low Dutch kɛ High German tsɔɔlɔ yɛ Santa Claus kɛ St. “Grayelɔ lɛ.” Yɛ kukufoo mli lɛ, nɛkɛ blema Low Dutch wolo ni akaseɔ nii nɛɛ wieɔ Holland jarayeli he; ni, yɛ saji krokomɛi ateŋ lɛ, amaniɛbɔɔ ko ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ waa ni kɔɔ bɔ ni ekɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni ji whale lɛ yeɔ lɛ he lɛ fata he. Ni yɛ yitso nɛɛ mli ji, ni yitso ji, “Smeer,” loo “Fat,” ni mina atadei ni akɛwoɔ Dutch ŋshɔŋlooi akui 180 ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ mli lɛ kɛ amɛ ŋshɔŋ lɛlɛi amli lɛ ahe saji kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ fitsofitso; kɛjɛ mli, taakɛ Dr. Snodhead tsɔɔ shishi lɛ, miŋmalaa nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ nɛɛ:

400,000 lbs. ni ji tsina loo. 60,000 lbs. Frisland kpootoo loo. 150,000 lbs. ni ji stock loo lɛ. 550,000 lbs. ni ji biskit. 72,000 lbs. ni ji blodo ni mli jɔ. 2,800 ni ji bɔta. 20,000 lbs. Texel kɛ Leyden tsiisi. 144,000 lbs. cheese (ekolɛ eji niŋmaa ko ni baa shi). 550 ni jɛ Geneva lɛ. Beer tsɛnsii 10,800.

Akɔntaabuu okpɔlɔi lɛ ateŋ babaoo ji parchingly dry yɛ nikanemɔ lɛ mli; shi kɛlɛ, jeee nakai eji yɛ sane ni yɔɔ amrɔ nɛɛ mli, he ni paipii muu, tsɛnsii, quarts, kɛ gills ni ji gin kpakpa kɛ miishɛɛ kpakpa eyi nikanelɔ lɛ mli obɔ lɛ.

Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, mikɛ gbii etɛ tsu nɛkɛ beer, tsina loo, kɛ blodo nɛɛ fɛɛ he nikasemɔ he nii, ni yɛ be nɛɛ mli lɛ, atsɔɔ mi susumɔi babaoo ni mli kwɔ yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ, ni baanyɛ atsu nii yɛ gbɛ ni fe fɛɛ kɛ Plato nɔ; ni, kɛfata he lɛ, mibua mi diɛŋtsɛ mi okpɔlɔi ni fata he lɛ anaa, ni mita bɔ ni ekolɛ Low Dutch harpooneer fɛɛ harpooneer yeɔ yɛ nakai blema Greenland kɛ Spitzbergen whale ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ayibɔ he. Yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, bɔ ni bɔta, kɛ Texel kɛ Leyden tsiisi ni ayeɔ lɛ fa ha lɛ tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, mikɛwoɔ amɛ adebɔɔ mli su ni bɛ muji lɛ mli, ni amɛ nitsumɔ lɛ su lɛ haa amɛfeɔ mɛi ni bɛ muji lolo, ni titri lɛ kɛtsɔ amɛshwɛmɔ ni amɛtiuɔ sɛɛ yɛ nakai Polar Ŋshɔi ni mli ejɔ lɛ amli, yɛ nakai Esquimaux maŋ lɛ ŋshɔnaa he ni maŋbii ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ lɛ woɔ amɛhe shi yɛ oketekei amli lɛ nɔ.

Beer lɛ falɛ hu fa waa, eji tsɛnsii 10,800. Amrɔ nɛɛ, akɛni abaanyɛ aye nakai polar ŋshɔŋ wuoyeli lɛ nɔ pɛ yɛ nakai kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ lɛ mli hulutsoo be kuku lɛ mli hewɔ lɛ, no hewɔ lɛ Dutch ŋshɔŋlooi nɛɛ ateŋ mɔ kome gbɛfaa lɛ fɛɛ, ni gbɛfaa kuku ni efã kɛtee Spitzbergen ŋshɔ lɛ mli kɛbaa lɛ fata he lɛ, efeee nyɔji etɛ kwraa, kɛ wɔkɛɛ lɛ, ni abuɔ hii 30 he akɔntaa kɛyaa amɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ni ji 180 sail,40; no hewɔ lɛ, mikɛɛ, wɔyɛ beer tsɛnsii enyɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ kɛha gbɔmɔ kome, kɛha otsii nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ nyɔmɔwoo, ni egbɛfaŋnɔ ni sa ni ji nakai gin ankers 550 lɛ fataaa he. Amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛji akɛ nɛkɛ gin kɛ beer harpooneers nɛɛ, ni amɛhe ejɔ amɛ waa bɔ ni mɔ ko baanyɛ asusu akɛ amɛji lɛ ji hii ni sa ni baate shi yɛ lɛlɛ yitso mli, ni amɛkwɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni foɔ lɛ anɔ jogbaŋŋ; enɛ baafee tamɔ nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsu he nii yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ. Shi kɛlɛ, amɛtu amɛhiɛ amɛshwie amɛnɔ, ni amɛtswa amɛ hu. Shi enɛ ji Kooyigbɛ ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ waa, akai, he ni beer kɛ maŋ mla lɛ kpãa gbee jogbaŋŋ yɛ lɛ; yɛ Equator lɛ nɔ, yɛ wɔ kooyigbɛ wuoyelihe lɛ, beer baafee nɔ ni sa jogbaŋŋ ni baaha harpooneer lɛ awɔ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yitso he ni ekɛ dãa awo elɛlɛ lɛ mli; ni fitemɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho baanyɛ aba Nantucket kɛ New Bedford nɔ.

Shi jeee dɔŋŋ; awie nɔ ni fa bɔ ni sa ni tsɔɔ akɛ Dutch ŋshɔŋlooi wuji wuji ni hi shi afii ohai enyɔ loo etɛ ni eho nɛ lɛ ji ŋshɔŋlooi ni nɔ kwɔlɔ; kɛ akɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛlɔi lɛ ekuuu amɛhiɛ amɛshwieee nɔkwɛmɔnɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ tamɔ nɛkɛ nɔ. Ejaakɛ amɛkɛɛ, kɛ́ oonyiɛ lɛlɛ ni nɔ ko bɛ mli lɛ mli lɛ, kɛji akɛ onyɛɛɛ ona nɔ ko ni hi fe enɛ kɛjɛ je lɛ mli lɛ, ná gbɛkɛ niyenii kpakpa kɛjɛ mli, kɛ hoo lɛ. Ni enɛ haa dekan lɛ mli nu lɛ hoɔ.





YITSƆƆ 102. Bower ko yɛ Arsacides lɛ mli.

Kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ, yɛ Sperm Whale lɛ he wiemɔ ni mitsɔɔ mli lɛ mli lɛ, mikɛ mihe ewo naakpɛɛ nii ni yɔɔ ekponɔgbɛ lɛ mli titri; aloo srɔtofeemɔ kɛ fitsofitso yɛ mligbɛ gbɛjianɔtoo he nibii fioo komɛi anɔ. Shi kɛha shishinumɔ ni da ni mli kwɔ ni kɔɔ ehe lɛ, esa akɛ agbɛnɛ majie ehe tsɔne lɛ mli kɛya hiɛ lolo, ni majie e-hose lɛ he okadii lɛ, majie e-garters lɛ, ni majie ŋmeii kɛ ewui ni yɔɔ emli fe fɛɛ lɛ ahe ŋmeii lɛ ahiŋmɛii lɛ, ni mikɛ lɛ ama ohiɛ yɛ e-ultimatum lɛ mli; ni ji akɛ, yɛ egbɔmɔtso ni nɔ ko bɛ he lɛ mli.

Shi te agbɛnɛ, Ishmael? Te efeɔ tɛŋŋ ni bo, ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi akudɔlɔ kɛkɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhe lɛ, feɔ ohe tamɔ mɔ ko ni le nɔ ko yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ashishigbɛ fãi lɛ ahe? Ani Stubb ni le nii waa, ni ta o-capstan lɛ nɔ lɛ ha wiemɔi yɛ Cetacea lɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ he; ni kɛtsɔ kɔɔyɔɔŋ tsɔne lɛ yelikɛbuamɔ nɔ lɛ, ewoɔ tsitsi nɔ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ ko nɔ koni akɛjie lɛ kpo? Shi yɛ niiamlitaomɔ mli lɛ wɔnaa akɛ, jeee akɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ dara fe mɛi ni ana amɛ sũ mli nibii ni eshwɛ yɛ Tertiary gbɛjianɔtoo lɛ mli lɛ pɛ (ni kɛ shikpɔŋ he nilee be ko ni yɔɔ srɔto dani gbɔmɔ ba lɛ tsuɔ nii), shi moŋ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni ana yɛ nakai Tertiary gbɛjianɔtoo lɛ mli lɛ ateŋ lɛ, mɛi ni fata esɛɛgbɛ gbɛjianɔtoi lɛ ahe lɛ fa fe nɔ ni yɔɔ mli lɛ yɛ dalɛ mli.

Yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi fɛɛ ni ajie dani adamite ba lɛ ateŋ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ni da fe fɛɛ ji Alabama ŋshɔŋlooi ni atsĩ tã yɛ naagbee yitso lɛ mli lɛ, ni ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ kɛlɛ shɛɛɛ ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai kpawo yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ amli. Yɛ be mli ni, wɔna momo, akɛ tɛp-measure lɛ haa ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai kpawo kɛ enyɔ kɛha ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni da waa lɛ fãji. Ni minu, yɛ whalemen hegbɛ naa, akɛ amɔmɔ Sperm Whales ni kɛlɛ miihe ashɛ ninetalɔi oha yɛ be mli ni amɔmɔ amɛ lɛ.

Shi nyɛkafee nakai, akɛ yɛ be mli ni ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji lɛ ji hiɛyaa yɛ amɛdalɛ mli fe shikpɔŋ he nilee bei fɛɛ ni eho lɛ amli lɛ; ani ejeee akɛ, kɛjɛ Adam beaŋ kɛbaa nɛɛ, amɛfite?

Eji anɔkwale akɛ, esa akɛ wɔmu sane naa nakai, kɛji akɛ wɔbaawo hii tamɔ Pliny, kɛ blema adebɔɔ he nilelɔi lɛ fɛɛ amaniɛbɔi lɛ ahe nyɔmɔ. Ejaakɛ Pliny kɛɛ wɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni fua ŋshɔŋlooi ni wala yɔɔ amɛmli babaoo ni falɛ ji acrei babaoo lɛ ahe sane, kɛ Aldrovandus hu wieɔ ekrokomɛi ni kɛlɛ ji ninetalɔi ohai kpaanyɔ he—Rope Walks kɛ Thames Tunnels of Whales! Ni yɛ Banks kɛ Solander, ni ji Cooke adebɔɔ he nilelɔi lɛ agbii amli po lɛ, wɔnaa Denmarknyo ko ni yɔɔ Jeŋ Nilee He Nikasemɔhe lɛ mlinyo ko ni kɛ Iceland Whales (reydan-siskur, loo Musui ni Egbɔjɔ) komɛi eto shi yɛ ŋwɛi ŋsɔɔdoi ohai kɛ nyɔŋmai enyɔ; ni ji, ninetalɔi ohai etɛ kɛ nyɔŋmai ekpaa. Ni Lacépède, French adebɔɔ he nilelɔ lɛ, yɛ e-whale he yinɔsane ni mli ka shi faŋŋ lɛ mli, yɛ enitsumɔ lɛ shishijee mli tɔ̃ɔ (baafa 3), kɛ Right Whale lɛ to shi yɛ mitai ohai kome, ninetalɔi ohai etɛ kɛ nyɔŋmai enyɔ kɛ kpaanyɔ nɔ. Ni akala wolo nɛɛ sɛɛ mli yɛ AD 1825 mli.

Shi ani ŋshɔŋloo ko baahe saji nɛɛ eye? Dabi, ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ ŋshɔŋ loo lɛ da tamɔ eblematsɛmɛi ni hi shi yɛ Pliny beaŋ lɛ. Ni kɛji akɛ mitee he ni Pliny yɔɔ lɛ pɛŋ lɛ, mi, ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsulɔ (fe lɛ), mafee ekãa ni makɛɛ lɛ nakai. Ejaakɛ minyɛɛɛ manu bɔ ni eji lɛ shishi, akɛ yɛ be mli ni Mizraim gbohiiashitee ni afu afii akpei abɔ dani afɔ Pliny po lɛ, esusuuu babaoo yɛ amɛ gbohiiashitee adekai amli tamɔ bɔ ni ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ Kentuckynyo ko susuɔ yɛ esɔks mli lɛ; ni yɛ be mli ni tsinai kɛ kooloi krokomɛi ni akpɛ́ yɛ Mizraim kɛ Ninive tɛi ni etsɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ anɔ lɛ, kɛtsɔ bɔ ni amɛyɔɔ ha lɛ nɔ lɛ, maa nɔ mi faŋŋ akɛ Smithfield tsinai ni akwɛɔ amɛ jogbaŋŋ, ni ayeɔ amɛ yɛ shĩa mli, ni jara wa lɛ, jeee akɛ amɛkɛ amɛhe yeɔ egbɔ kɛkɛ, shi moŋ amɛfeɔ Farao tsinai ni egbɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ kwraa yɛ dalɛ mli’; yɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ hiɛ lɛ, mikpɛlɛŋ nɔ akɛ yɛ kooloi fɛɛ ateŋ lɛ, kulɛ esa akɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ pɛ afite.

Shi sanebimɔ kroko hu yɛ ni esa akɛ abi; eko ni Nantucketbii ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ fɔɔ mlifu. Kɛji akɛ eji mɛi ni kwɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛi lɛ a-mast-yitsei lɛ anɔ ni miihe ashɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ he lɛ hewɔ jio, ni amrɔ nɛɛ amɛboteɔ Behring ŋshɔ lɛ naa po, kɛyaa teemɔŋ tsɔnei kɛ tsɔnei ni akɛwoɔ mli ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ yɛ je lɛŋ lɛ amli lɛ hewɔ jio; ni ŋshɔkei kɛ ŋshɔkei akpe lɛ shwie shi yɛ shikpɔŋ wulu lɛ ŋshɔnaa hei fɛɛ; nɔ ni yɔɔ mli ji, kɛji Leviathan baanyɛ aŋmɛ etsui shi be kakadaŋŋ yɛ sɛɛtiumɔ ni mli kwɔ tamɔ nɛkɛ mli, kɛ fitemɔ ni shwamɔ bɛ mli nɛkɛ mli; kɛji esaaa akɛ agbeɔ lɛ kɛjɛɔ nu lɛ mli yɛ naagbee, ni naagbee ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, tamɔ naagbee gbɔmɔ lɛ, eshɛreɔ enaagbee paipii lɛ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ efoɔ yɛ naagbee ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli.

Kɛ akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi akui ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ to tsinabii akui ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ ahe, ni jeee afii nyɔŋmai ejwɛ ni eho nɛ lɛ, amɛgbɛ amɛshwã Illinois kɛ Missouri ŋmɔji lɛ amli akpei nyɔŋmai abɔ, ni amɛkpokpo amɛ dade tsitsii ni amɛkɛ amɛhiɛ ni tamɔ ŋwɛi ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ ŋmɔ hei ni gbɔmɛi babaoo yɔɔ faa lɛ nɔ lɛ, maŋtiase lɛ nɔ. inchi; yɛ nɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni akɛtoɔ he lɛ mli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni akɛ sanegbaa ni anyɛɛɛ akpoo aha, koni akɛtsɔɔ akɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni agbeɔ lɛ lɛ nyɛŋ ajo foi oyaiyeli naa foi agbɛnɛ.

Shi esa akɛ okwɛ sane nɛɛ yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ. Eyɛ mli akɛ be kukuoo ko ni eho moŋ—jeee wala be kpakpa—shi tsinabii ayibɔ ni akaneɔ yɛ Illinois lɛ fe hii ni akaneɔ yɛ London amrɔ nɛɛ, ni eyɛ mli akɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ lɛ amɛteŋ akoloŋto loo amɛtsitsi kome po eshwɛɛɛ yɛ nakai kpokpaa lɛ fɛɛ nɔ moŋ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ nɔ ni kɛ naakpɛɛ hiɛkpatamɔ nɛɛ ba ji gbɔmɔ akpɔlɔ moŋ; shi kɛlɛ bɔ ni ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbee lɛ yɔɔ srɔto kwraa lɛ tsiɔ Leviathan naagbee ni bɛ nyam nɛkɛ lɛ naa kwraa. Hii nyɔŋmai ejwɛ ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ kome mli ni miitao Sperm Whales lɛ nyɔji nyɔŋmai ejwɛ kɛ kpaanyɔ lɛ susuɔ akɛ amɛfee nɔ ni hi fe nine, ni amɛdaa Nyɔŋmɔ shi, kɛji naagbee lɛ amɛkɛ loo nyɔŋmai ejwɛ fɔ lɛ tee shia. Yɛ be mli ni, yɛ Canada kɛ Indiabii blema gbɔbilɔi kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ Anaigbɛ lɛ agbii amli, beni anaigbɛ shɔŋŋ (ni hulu ni enyɔ shi yɛ mli lɛ teɔ shi lolo yɛ mli lɛ) ji ŋa nɔ kɛ obalayoo fro lɛ, hii ni yɔɔ moccasin lɛ ayibɔ ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ, kɛha nyɔji ayibɔ ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ, ni tara okpɔŋɔi anɔ yɛ najiaŋ ni amɛaaya buffalo kɛ buffalo kɛha thousand, baaná; anɔkwale ko ni, kɛ ehe miihia lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛ akɔntaabuu atsɔɔ mli.

Ni kɛji akɛ asusu he akɛ eja lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni sanegbaa ko bɛ ni fiɔ Sperm Whale lɛ sɛɛfomɔ fiofio lɛ sɛɛ, ákɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ lɛ, akɛ yɛ tsutsu afii lɛ amli (afii ohai ni eho lɛ naagbee fã lɛ, kɛ wɔkɛɛ) nɛkɛ Leviathan nɛɛ, ni yɔɔ ŋmeii bibii amli lɛ, akɛkpeɔ shii abɔ fe amrɔ nɛɛ, ni, nɔ ni jɛ mli ba ji akɛ, gbɛfaai lɛ kɛ gbɛfaa lɛ hu etsɛɛɛ tsɔ. Ejaakɛ, taakɛ akadi yɛ hei krokomɛi lɛ, nakai ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ, ni susumɔi komɛi ni kɔɔ shweshweeshwe shihilɛ he lɛ ená amɛnɔ hewalɛ lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ amɛfoɔ ŋshɔi lɛ amli yɛ gbɛfalɔi akui wuji amli, bɔni afee ni yɛ gbɛ ni da nɔ lɛ, gbii krokomɛi amli ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ amɛkomekomei, ŋshɔŋlooi, kɛ skulbii ni egbɛ eshwã lɛ abua amɛhe naa amrɔ nɛɛ amɛfee ŋshɔŋlooi wuji shi amɛjara amɛhe waa, ni amɛfɔɔɔ baa. No pɛ ji. Ni etamɔ nɔ ni henɔwomɔ lɛ ji amale nakai nɔŋŋ, akɛ akɛni nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ whale-bone whales lɛ bɛ shikpɔji babaoo nɔ dɔŋŋ yɛ tsutsu afii ni amɛyi obɔ lɛ amli hewɔ lɛ, no hewɔ lɛ nakai ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ hu miiba shi. Ejaakɛ ashwieɔ amɛ kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ kɛyaa ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ pɛ; ni kɛji akɛ ŋshɔnaa he kome kɛ amɛ jet lɛ haaa wala dɔŋŋ lɛ, no lɛ, ná nɔmimaa akɛ, ŋshɔnaa kroko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ etsɔ naakpɛɛ nii ni aleee lɛ eha etsɛko tsɔ.

Agbɛnɛ hu: yɛ nɛkɛ naagbee Leviatans ni atsĩ amɛtã nɛɛ ahe lɛ, amɛyɛ ŋshɔkpɔi enyɔ ni ma shi shiŋŋ, ni, yɛ adesai asusumɔi fɛɛ naa lɛ, amɛbaakã he ahi shi kɛya naanɔ ni anyɛɛɛ aye nɔ kunim. Ni taakɛ eji yɛ amɛjɔɔ lɛ mli tutuamɔ mli lɛ, Switzerlandbii ni fɛ̃i yɔɔ mli lɛ eku amɛsɛɛ kɛtee amɛ gɔji lɛ amli; no hewɔ lɛ, akɛni ataoɔ amɛ kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ teŋgbɛ lɛ amli ŋmɔji kɛ ŋmɔji amli lɛ hewɔ lɛ, yɛ naagbee lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋ lɛ baanyɛ aya amɛ Polar ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ, ni amɛfo amɛhe yɛ glase gbɛtsii nii kɛ gbogboi ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ ashishi, ni amɛba ŋmɔji ni mli edɔ kɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ ateŋ; ni yɛ naanɔ December kooloo ko ni yɔɔ fɛo mli lɛ, ekɛ shitee-kɛ-woo ha nibii fɛɛ ni gbɔmɔ taoɔ lɛ.

Shi akɛni ekolɛ atswaa nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi nɛɛ ateŋ nyɔŋmai enumɔ ni akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi wui tsuɔ nii kɛhaa cachalot kome hewɔ lɛ, jeŋ nilelɔi komɛi ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛ emu sane naa akɛ nɛkɛ fitemɔ ni hi nɛɛ eha amɛ asraafoi akui lɛ eba shi waa momo. Shi eyɛ mli akɛ be ko ni eho nɛ ni Amerikabii pɛ gbeɔ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi nɛɛ ayibɔ, ni shɛɛɛ 13,000 daa afi yɛ nor’ anaigbɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa moŋ; shi kɛlɛ, nibii komɛi yɛ ni asusuɔ he ni haa shihilɛ nɛɛ po feɔ nɔ ko fioo loo nɔ ko kwraa akɛ sanegbaa ni teɔ shi woɔ ehe yɛ sane nɛɛ mli.

Eyɛ mli akɛ eji adebɔɔ naa nɔ akɛ aaayeee nɔ ko yɛ bɔ ni shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ bɔɔ nii ni da fe fɛɛ lɛ ayi fa ha lɛ he moŋ, shi mɛni ji nɔ ni wɔbaakɛɛ Harto, ni ji Goa yinɔsaneŋmalɔ lɛ, beni ekɛɛ wɔ akɛ yɛ gbɔbimɔ ko mli lɛ, Siam Maŋtsɛ lɛ ŋɔ shwuɔi 4,000; akɛ yɛ nakai kpokpai lɛ anɔ lɛ, shwuɔi babaoo tamɔ tsinai akui ni yɔɔ hei ni hulutsoo be yɔɔ lɛ. Ni etamɔ nɔ ni yiŋtoo ko bɛ ni baaha yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ aba akɛ kɛji akɛ nɛkɛ shwuɔi nɛɛ, ni Semiramis, Porus, Hannibal, kɛ Bokagbɛ maŋtsɛmɛi fɛɛ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ miitao amɛ afii akpei babaoo nɛ lɛ—kɛji amɛhi shi lolo yɛ jɛmɛ yɛ yibɔ babaoo mli lɛ, no lɛ ekolɛ ŋshɔŋloo wulu lɛ baanyɛ ahi shi fe gbɔbimɔ fɛɛ, ejaakɛ eyɛ kooloi alɛɛhe ni ekɛbaatsu nii yɛ Asia. Amerika, Europa kɛ Afrika, New Holland, kɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa Ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ fɛɛ kɛfata he.

Kɛfata he lɛ: esa akɛ wɔsusu he akɛ, kɛjɛ bɔ ni asusuɔ akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ hiɔ shi be kakadaŋŋ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekolɛ amɛye afii ohai kome kɛyaa, no hewɔ lɛ yɛ be kome mli lɛ, esa akɛ yinɔi srɔtoi ni eda lɛ afee mɛi ni yɔɔ be kome mli. Ni nɔ ni no ji lɛ, ekolɛ etsɛŋ ni wɔbaaná he susumɔ ko, kɛtsɔ gbohiiashitee hei, gbohiiashiteehei, kɛ wekui adebɔɔ mli ŋtayaai fɛɛ ni kɛ hii, yei, kɛ gbekɛbii fɛɛ ni hiɛ ka afii nyɔŋmai kpawo kɛ enumɔ ni eho nɛ lɛ agbɔmɔtsei ni hiɛ ka lɛ haa lɛ he susumɔ nɔ; kɛ nɛkɛ gbɔi ni anyɛɛɛ akane nɛɛ ni akɛfata adesai ayibɔ ni yɔɔ shikpɔŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ nɔ bianɛ lɛ he.

No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ nibii nɛɛ fɛɛ hewɔ lɛ, wɔbuɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ akɛ eji mɔ ni gbooo yɛ egbɔmɔtso lɛ mli, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni efiteɔ yɛ egbɔmɔtso lɛ mli ha. Efo ŋshɔi lɛ amli dani shikpɔŋ wuji lɛ fã nu; be ko lɛ, efo he ni Tuileries, kɛ Windsor Maŋtsɛwe lɛ, kɛ Kremlin lɛ yɔɔ lɛ. Yɛ Noa nu afua lɛ mli lɛ ebuuu Noa Adeka lɛ; ni kɛji akɛ esa akɛ nu afua aba je lɛ nɔ ekoŋŋ, tamɔ Netherlands, koni egbe e-rats lɛ, no lɛ naanɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ baahi shi lolo, ni ebaatsɔ equatorial nu afua lɛ yiteŋgbɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ eshishiumɔ ni mli ewo ŋmɔtɔ lɛ baatsɔɔ ŋwɛi.





YITSƆƆ 106. Ahab Naji.

Gbɛ ni Kapten Ahab tsɔ nɔ eshi Samuel Enderby ni jɛ London lɛ oya lɛ, jeee nɔ ni mɔ ko mɔ ko kwɛɛɛ nɔ kɛ yiwalɛ bibioo ko ni ekɛba lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe. Ekɛ hewalɛ etswa la yɛ elɛlɛ lɛ fã ko ni efite lɛ nɔ aahu akɛ enaji ni akɛ shwuɔ wu fee lɛ etswa fã ni fã. Ni beni enine shɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-deck lɛ nɔ, kɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-pivot-hole lɛ nɔ yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ, ekɛ oyaiyeli famɔ ko ni ekɛha tsɔne kudɔlɔ lɛ (eji, taakɛ eji daa lɛ, nɔ ko ni kɔɔ bɔ ni ekɛ tsɔne kudɔmɔ lɛ tsuuu nii jogbaŋŋ bɔ ni sa lɛ he); no sɛɛ lɛ, shwuɔ wu ni ekpokpo momo lɛ ná tsɔne kroko ni akɛfata he, ni eyɛ mli akɛ ehi shi lolo, ni etamɔ nɔ ni eyɛ akɔnɔ moŋ, shi Ahab ebuuu akɛ anyɛɔ akɛ he fɔ̃ɔ enɔ kwraa.

Ni, lɛɛlɛŋ, etamɔ nɔ ni eji naakpɛɛ sane bibioo ko, akɛ yɛ ehiɛ ni egbɛ eshwa, ni efee shwilafo lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ Ahab boɔ nakai wu gbonyo ni edamɔ nɔ lɛ fã ko lɛ shihilɛ lɛ jogbaŋŋ. Ejaakɛ etsɛɛɛ tsɔ dani Pequod lɛ fã gbɛ kɛjɛ Nantucket, ni ana lɛ gbɛkɛ ko ni ekã shikpɔŋ ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ shi, ni ebɛ jwɛŋmɔ; kɛtsɔ pilamɔ ko ni aleee, ni etamɔ nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ mli, ni anyɛɛɛ asusu he lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ shwuɔ wu lɛ fã kɛjɛ he ni eyɔɔ lɛ waa, ni etswa lɛ tamɔ tso, ni efo etsitsi mli fɛɛ; ni asaŋ jeee akɛ naagba ko kwraa bɛ mli akɛ pilamɔ ni yeɔ mɔ awui lɛ fɛɛ tsa.

Ni asaŋ, yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, eboteee ejwɛŋmɔ ni ji monomaniac lɛ mli hu, akɛ nakai beaŋ piŋmɔ ni yɔɔ amrɔ nɛɛ mli haomɔi lɛ fɛɛ ji tsutsu haomɔ ko he sane tɛ̃ɛ kɛkɛ; ni etamɔ nɔ ni lɛ hu ena faŋŋ, akɛ taakɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli kooloo ni shwereɔ ni yɔɔ poison fe fɛɛ lɛ haa ehenɔi lɛ yaa nɔ tamɔ bɔ ni lalafolɔ ni ŋɔɔ fe fɛɛ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ ji lɛ; no hewɔ lɛ, taakɛ eji yɛ miishɛɛ fɛɛ miishɛɛ he lɛ, nibii ni yɔɔ awerɛho fɛɛ ni baa lɛ fɔɔ amɛhe tamɔ amɛ yɛ adebɔɔ naa. Hɛɛ, fe nakai nɔŋŋ, Ahab susu he; ejaakɛ Awrɛhoyeli blematsɛmɛi kɛ seshibii fɛɛ yaa shɔŋŋ fe Miishɛɛ seshibii kɛ seshibii fɛɛ. Ejaakɛ, jeee ni mikɛ enɛ atsɔɔ: akɛ eji nɔ ko ni jɛɔ tsɔɔmɔi komɛi ni yɔɔ wolo lɛ mli lɛ amli baa, akɛ yɛ be mli ni adebɔɔ mli miishɛɛ komɛi ni yɔɔ biɛ lɛ bɛ bii ni afɔɔ amɛ ahaa je kroko lɛ, shi moŋ, nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ enɛ lɛ, hɛl hiɛnɔkamɔ ni bɛ lɛ fɛɛ mli miishɛɛ-bi ni bɛ lɛ baanyiɛ sɛɛ; yɛ be mli ni, gbele mli amanehului komɛi ni yeɔ fɔ lɛ baafɔ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe awerɛhoyeli seshi ni yaa hiɛ kɛya naanɔ yɛ gbonyobu lɛ sɛɛ lolo; jeee kwraa kɛha enɛ he tsɔɔmɔ, etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ko ni kɛ amɛhe kpaaa gbee yɛ nɔ lɛ mlipɛimɔ ni mli kwɔ lɛ mli lolo. Ejaakɛ, Ahab susu akɛ, yɛ be mli ni shikpɔŋ nɔ miishɛɛ ni nɔ kwɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ po yɛ nɔ ko bibioo ko ni tsɔɔɔ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ mli lɛ, shi, yɛ shishigbɛ lɛ, tsui mli haomɔi fɛɛ, teemɔŋ shishinumɔ ko, kɛ, yɛ gbɔmɛi komɛi amli lɛ, ŋwɛibɔfo nukpa lɛ agbojee; nakai nɔŋŋ amɛ hiɛdɔɔ tracings-out lɛ yeee nɔ ni ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ mli ni yɔɔ faŋŋ lɛ nɔ. Kɛji akɛ wɔɔnyiɛ nɛkɛ gbele mli amanehului ni nɔ kwɔlɔ nɛɛ awekukpaai amli lɛ, ekɛ wɔ yaa nyɔŋmɔi lɛ ashishijee wekukpaai ni bɛ jɛɛhe lɛ ateŋ yɛ naagbee; bɔni afee ni, yɛ hulu ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ, ni feɔ jwɛi, kɛ ŋmɛlɛtswai ni yɔɔ blɛoo, ni ji kpamɔ-nyɔji ni yɔɔ kokroo lɛ fɛɛ ahiɛ lɛ, esa akɛ wɔkɛ wɔhe aha enɛ: akɛ nyɔŋmɔi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ amii eshɛɛɛ amɛhe kɛya naanɔ. Fɔmɔ he okadi ni anyɛɛɛ ajie, ni yɔɔ awerɛho ni yɔɔ gbɔmɔ hiɛ lɛ ji awerɛho he okadi ni yɔɔ mɛi ni kɛ amɛhe wo mli lɛ amli kɛkɛ.

Ajie teemɔŋ sane ko kpo yɛ biɛ yɛ jwɛŋmɔ ni bɛ mli, ni ekolɛ ekolɛ ebaahi jogbaŋŋ, yɛ gbɛ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ nɔ lɛ, ajie lɛ kpo dani eba. Kɛfata saji krokomɛi babaoo ni kɔɔ Ahab he lɛ ahe lɛ, be fɛɛ be lɛ, eji teemɔŋ sane kɛha mɛi komɛi, nɔ hewɔ ni eji lɛ, akɛ yɛ be pɔtɛɛ ko mli, dani Pequod lɛ fãa gbɛ kɛ sɛɛ fɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ ehe etee shɔŋŋ kɛ nɛkɛ Grand-Lama pɛ he ni eyɔɔ lɛ; ni, yɛ nakai be kome lɛ mli lɛ, etao abobaahe ni wiemɔ bɛ mli, taakɛ atsɔɔ mli lɛ, yɛ gbohii lɛ a-marble senate lɛ ateŋ. Yiŋtoo ni Kapten Peleg kɛtsu nii yɛ nɛkɛ nɔ nɛɛ he lɛ tamɔɔɔ nɔ ni sa kwraa; shi kɛlɛ, yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, akɛni ekɔɔ Ahab fã ni mli kwɔ lɛ fɛɛ he hewɔ lɛ, kpojiemɔ fɛɛ kpojiemɔ kɛ duŋ ni he hiaa fe la ni tsɔɔ mli lɛ mli gbɛfaŋnɔ. Shi, yɛ naagbee lɛ, fɛɛ je kpo; sane kome nɛɛ fee nakai, kɛ hoo lɛ. Nakai oshara ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ ji nɔ ni ha ekɛ ehe wo be kukuoo mli lɛ mli. Ni jeee enɛ pɛ, shi moŋ kɛha nakai kooloo ni shwieɔ shi be fɛɛ be, ni shwieɔ shi yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, ni, yɛ yiŋtoo fɛɛ yiŋtoo hewɔ lɛ, eyɛ hegbɛ ni ekɛ ehe kpaa gbee ni aguuu lɛ; kɛha nakai kuu ni yɔɔ hiɛgbele lɛ, gbele ni awie he yɛ ŋwɛi nɛɛ—ni eshwɛ, taakɛ efee lɛ, ni Ahab kɛ ehe wooo mli yɛ jwɛŋmɔŋ lɛ—kɛ gbeyeishemɔi wo ehe, ni jeee nɔ ni jɛ mumɔi kɛ yaafoi ashikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ kwraa. No hewɔ lɛ, kɛtsɔ ekãa ni amɛyɔɔ kɛha lɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, amɛ fɛɛ amɛkpa gbee, bɔ ni amɛyɔɔ lɛ, koni amɛtsi nɛkɛ sane nɛɛ he nilee lɛ naa kɛjɛ mɛi krokomɛi ahe; ni no hewɔ lɛ ebalɛ akɛ, jeee kɛyashi be kakadaŋŋ ho dani eba Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ.

Shi bɔ ni enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ baafee lɛ; ha synod ni anaaa, ni shishinumɔ bɛ mli ni yɔɔ kɔɔyɔɔ mli lɛ, loo maŋtsɛmɛi kɛ la he hewalɛi ni yeɔ he nyɔmɔ lɛ afee nɔ ko loo amɛfeee nakai, shi kɛlɛ, yɛ enane he sane ni yɔɔ amrɔ nɛɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ gbɛjianɔtoi ni yɔɔ faŋŋ ni anyɛɔ atsuɔ he nii tsu nii;—etsɛ kapintɛfonyo lɛ.

Ni beni nakai nitsulɔ lɛ jie ehe kpo yɛ ehiɛ lɛ, ekɛɛ lɛ oya akɛ ebɔi naji hee feemɔ, ni etsɔɔ ehefatalɔi lɛ koni amɛkwɛ lɛ ni akɛ ŋmeii kɛ ŋmeii ni akɛfee ŋmei (Sperm Whale) ni abua naa kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ yɛ gbɛfaa lɛ mli lɛ fɛɛ eha lɛ, bɔni afee ni ahala ŋmei ni wa fe fɛɛ, ni mli ka shi faŋŋ fe fɛɛ lɛ jogbaŋŋ. Beni afee enɛ lɛ, kapintɛfonyo lɛ nine shɛ famɔi anɔ koni egbe naji lɛ naa nakai gbɛkɛ lɛ; ni ekɛ nibii fɛɛ ni he hiaa lɛ aha, ni esoro nɔ ni kɔɔ mɔ ni akɛ he fɔ̃ɔ enɔ ni akɛtsuɔ nii lɛ he lɛ he. Agbɛnɛ hu, afã koni ajie lɛlɛ lɛ tsɔne lɛ kɛjɛ he ni eyɔɔ be kukuoo ko ni ekɛtsuuu nii yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ mli; ni, bɔni afee ni sane lɛ aya oya lɛ, afã dadefeelɔ lɛ koni eya nɔ amrɔ nɔŋŋ ni ekɛ dade nibii fɛɛ ni he baahia lɛ afee.
