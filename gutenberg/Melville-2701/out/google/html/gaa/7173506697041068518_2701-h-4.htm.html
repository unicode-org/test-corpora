Queequeg yɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ, ni eji anɔkwale akɛ ekɛ susumɔi ni bɛ shishinumɔ fe fɛɛ ni kɔɔ Yojo kɛ e-Ramadan lɛ he lɛ miitsu nii;—shi no hu? Eyɛ naakpɛɛ, misusu he; shi bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, akɛni harpoon lɛ damɔ jɛmɛ, ni efɔɔɔ maŋsɛɛ yaa loo eyaaa kɔkɔɔkɔ ni ebɛ eko hewɔ lɛ, no hewɔ lɛ esa akɛ ehi mli yɛ biɛ, ni tɔmɔ ko bɛ ni baanyɛ aba.

Mitee ni miyasaa saatso lɛ nɔ yɛ leebi niyenii sɛɛ, ni aŋa shinaa lɛ; ni anuuu ŋmei ko; ni efee dioo kɛkɛ kɛjɛ nakai be lɛ mli. Shi misusu akɛ, ekolɛ, nyɛyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ nyɛtee ni nyɛŋa nyɛjatsui lɛ awo mli koni akɛto shweshweeshwe. Mr!— la!, matress!' apoplexy!”—ni ekɛ bolɔmɔi nɛɛ jo foi kɛtee ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ, ni mi hu minyiɛ esɛɛ.

“Tso-shĩa!” Mibolɔ akɛ, “mɛɛ gbɛ nɔ kɛyaa jɛmɛ? Jofoi yɛ Nyɔŋmɔ hewɔ, ni oyahe nɔ ko ni okɛgbele shinaa lɛ—ŋmei lɛ!—ŋmei lɛ! ená strok; okɛ ohe afɔ̃ nɔ!”—ni mikɛɛ nakai akɛ mikɛ oyaiyeli miikwɔ atswele lɛ nɔ ekoŋŋ kɛ nine folo, beni Mrs. ehiɛ.

“Mɛni eba onɔ, oblanyo?”

“Ŋɔɔ ŋmei lɛ, yɛ Nyɔŋmɔ hewɔ lɛ, jofoi kɛya datrɛfonyo lɛ ŋɔɔ, mɔ ko, be mli ni migbeleɔ lɛ!”

Shiatsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Kwɛmɔ biɛ,” ni ekɛ vinegar-cruet lɛ wo shi oya, bɔni afee ni enine kome afee ehe; “kwɛmɔ biɛ; ani oowie mishinaai lɛ eko ni ooogbele lɛ he?”—ni ekɛ nakai wiemɔi lɛ wie ni emɔ minine mli. “Mɛni eba onɔ? Mɛni eba onɔ, lɛlɛ mlinyo?”

Yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ kpoo, shi oya bɔ ni manyɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, miha enu sane lɛ fɛɛ shishi. Beni etswaa vinegar-cruet lɛ ehiɛ yɛ ehiɛ fã kome lɛ, esusu he fioo; kɛkɛ ni ebo akɛ—“Dabi!, kɛjɛ be mli ni mikɛma jɛmɛ lɛ, minako dã.” Beni ejo foi kɛtee tsɔne bibioo ko ni yɔɔ atswele lɛ shishi lɛ mli lɛ, ekwɛ mli, ni beni eku esɛɛ eba lɛ, ekɛɛ mi akɛ Queequeg harpoon lɛ bɛ. Efo akɛ: “Egbe ehe.” “Eji nɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho akɛ Stiggs fee nɔ ko ekoŋŋ—eyɛ counterpane kroko ni yaa—Nyɔŋmɔ aná enyɛ ohiafo lɛ mɔbɔ!—ebaafee mishia lɛ fitemɔ. Ani gbekɛ nuu ohiafo lɛ yɛ nyɛmi yoo? Nuŋtsɔ lɛ ana e-mumɔ lɛ mɔbɔ! Mɛni ji nakai dede ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ? Bo, oblanyo, avast yɛ jɛmɛ!”

Ni beni ejo foi kɛba misɛɛ lɛ, emɔ mi beni mibɔ mɔdɛŋ ekoŋŋ akɛ mikɛ hewalɛ agbele shinaa lɛ.

"Miŋmɛɛɛ gbɛ; mihaŋ mihe lɛ afite. Yaa oyatao kladoai ahe nitsulɔ lɛ, eko yɛ aaafee kilomita kome kɛjɛ biɛ. Shi avast!" ni ekɛ enine wo esɛɛ kotoku lɛ mli lɛ, “safe ko ni baashɛ he nɛ, misusuɔ akɛ; ha wɔkwɛ.” Ni kɛ nakai lɛ, etsɔ mli yɛ ŋwɛitsu lɛ mli; shi, shwane! Queequeg bolt ni fata he lɛ tee nɔ ehi shi ni ajieee kɛjɛ mli.

“Esa akɛ mafo mli,” mikɛɛ, ni miijo foi kɛmiijɛ shinaa lɛ naa fioo, kɛha shishijee kpakpa, beni shiatsɛ lɛ mɔ mi, ni ewo mi shi ekoŋŋ akɛ esaaa akɛ mifiteɔ eshia lɛ; shi mijie mihe kɛjɛ ehe, ni mikɛ gbɔmɔtsoŋ oyaiyeli ni baa trukaa lɛ tswa mihe kɛkpe okadi lɛ he.

Queequeg ta jɛmɛ, ni eyɛ kpoo kwraa ni ebua ehe naa; yɛ tsu lɛ teŋgbɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ; ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ e-hams lɛ anɔ, ni ehiɛ Yojo yɛ eyitso nɔ. Ekwɛɛɛ gbɛ kome loo gbɛ kroko nɔ, shi moŋ eta shi tamɔ amaga ko ni akpɛ́ ni wala ni tsuɔ nii waa he okadi ko bɛ he.

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Queequeg,” ni mitee eŋɔɔ, “Queequeg, mɛni eba onɔ?”

Mibɔi lɛ mlifu feemɔ; etamɔ nɔ ni jwɛŋmɔ bɛ mli kwraa ni efee shwilafo akɛ eta jɛmɛ gbi muu lɛ fɛɛ kɛ gbɛkɛ fã yɛ e-hams lɛ nɔ yɛ tsu ko ni mli ejɔ mli, ni ehiɛ tso kuku ko yɛ eyitso nɔ.

Shi beni hulu lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ bote samflɛ lɛ mli pɛ kɛkɛ ni ete shi, ni ekɛ fãji ni mli wa ni shwieɔ shi, shi ekɛ miishɛɛ hiɛ; tsɔ ehe kɛba miŋɔɔ yɛ he ni mikã lɛ; ekɛ ehiɛ miimɔ minɔ lɛ nɔ ekoŋŋ; ni ekɛɛ akɛ e-Ramadan lɛ efo.

Shi kɛ́ nuu ko jamɔ bafee basabasa diɛŋtsɛ; beni eji piŋmɔ ni hi kɛha lɛ lɛ; ni, yɛ naagbee lɛ, ehaa wɔshikpɔŋ nɛɛ feɔ gbɔiatoohe ni bɛ hejɔlɛ ni wɔɔhi mli; no sɛɛ lɛ misusuɔ akɛ be eshɛ ni esa akɛ mikɛ nakai aŋkro lɛ aya he kroko ni mikɛ lɛ aje sane lɛ he ŋwane.

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Queequeg, yaa saatso lɛ nɔ bianɛ, ni omale ni obo mi toi.” No sɛɛ lɛ, mitee nɔ, kɛjɛ blema jamɔi lɛ ashishijee kɛ amɛhiɛyaa nɔ, ni miba shi kɛtee ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ jamɔi srɔtoi lɛ anɔ, ni yɛ nakai be lɛ mli lɛ, mitsu nii waa koni matsɔɔ Queequeg akɛ nɛkɛ Lent, Ramadan, kɛ ham-squattings ni heɔ be kakadaŋŋ yɛ tsũi ni mli ejɔ ni miishɛɛ bɛ mli nɛɛ fɛɛ ji kwashiai asane kwraa; ehiii kɛha gbɔmɔtsoŋ hewalɛnamɔ; sɛɛnamɔ ko bɛ he kɛha susuma lɛ; te shi ewo, yɛ kukufoo mli lɛ, mlai ni yɔɔ faŋŋ ni kɔɔ Hetsemɔ kɛ jwɛŋmɔ kpakpa he lɛ. Mikɛɛ lɛ hu akɛ, akɛni eji mɔ ko ni yɔɔ jwɛŋmɔ waa ni yɔɔ ŋaa waa yɛ nibii krokomɛi amli hewɔ lɛ, eye mi awui, eye mi awui waa, akɛ mina lɛ amrɔ nɛɛ akɛ eji kwashia ni yɔɔ awerɛho waa yɛ e-Ramadan ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ nɛɛ he. "Eji anɔkwale, eji anɔkwale—hɛɛ, amɛ fɛɛ amɛji anɔkwale bɔ ni sa. Shi esa akɛ ofo kɛji ekɛ famɔ ha. Onyiɛ ni obɔi gbee; obolɔ ni oya—no ji wiemɔ ni kɔɔ Kapten Ahab he. Shi nɔ ko bɛ ni kɔɔ nakai nɔ ni ba enɔ yɛ Cape de Horn, be saŋŋ ni eho lɛ he, beni ekã shi tamɔ gbonyo gbii etɛ kɛ skri lɛ; afɔleshaa latɛ lɛ hiɛ yɛ Santa?—nuuu nɔ ko yɛ shika tsuru ni ekɛshwie mli lɛ he, taakɛ gbalɛ lɛ tsɔɔ lɛ. ekolɛ, nyɛnu akɛ agba naji lɛ he sane, kɛ bɔ ni elaaje lɛ, aye, nyɛnu nakai sane lɛ he, mikɛ ekãa kɛɛ akɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ le a’most—mitsɔɔ akɛ amɛle akɛ eji naji kome pɛ;

"Minaanyo," mikɛɛ lɛ, "nɔ ni nɛkɛ owiemɔi nɛɛ fɛɛ kɔɔ he lɛ, mileee, ni misusuuu he tsɔ; ejaakɛ etamɔ nɔ ni esa akɛ oyitso efite fioo. Shi kɛji oowie Kapten Ahab he, yɛ nakai lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ he, Pequod lɛ, no lɛ ha makɛɛ bo less lɛ he, akɛ mile lɛlɛ lɛ fɛɛ he."

“ Ehe fɛɛ , eh—eyɛ faŋŋ akɛ ofeɔ nakai?—fɛɛ?”

Gbɛi ni aŋmala awo woji lɛ anɔ? Ojogbaŋŋ, ojogbaŋŋ, nɔ ni awo shishi lɛ, awo shishi; ni nɔ ni baafee lɛ, baafee; ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekoŋŋ, ekolɛ ebaafee nɔ ni bɛ, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ. Bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, ato fɛɛ he gbɛjianɔ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ a’lorose jogbaŋŋ akɛ esa akɛ ekɛ lɛ aya; hii krokomɛi fɛɛ, Nyɔŋmɔ afee amɛ mɔbɔ Leebi, nyɛ lɛlɛ mli bii, leebi ni ŋwɛi ni anyɛɛɛ awie he lɛ ajɔɔ nyɛ;

"Ni awie lɛ jogbaŋŋ waa, ni misumɔɔ ni manu nuu ko ni wieɔ nakai; bo ji gbɔmɔ pɛ kɛha lɛ—ni tamɔ nyɛ. Leebi kɛha nyɛ, lɛlɛ mli bii, leebi! Oh! kɛ nyɛshɛ jɛmɛ lɛ, kɛɛmɔ amɛ akɛ mikpɛ miyiŋ akɛ mifeŋ amɛteŋ mɔ kome."

“Ah, misuɔmɔ naanyo, onyɛŋ oshishiu wɔ nakai—onyɛŋ oshishiu wɔ. Eji nɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo fe fɛɛ yɛ je lɛŋ akɛ gbɔmɔ baafee tamɔ nɔ ni eyɛ teemɔŋ sane wulu ko yɛ emli.”

“Leebi nyɛ, lɛlɛ mli bii, leebi.”

"Leebi eba," mikɛɛ. "Ba, Queequeg, ha wɔshi nuu shwilafo nɛɛ. Shi kpa, kɛɛmɔ mi ogbɛi, aloo obaasumɔ?"

“Eliya.”

Eliya! misusu he, ni wɔnyiɛ, ni wɔyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ wɔmiiwie, yɛ wɔteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ su naa, yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ onukpa ni egbɔjɔ nɛɛ he; ni ekpɛlɛ nɔ akɛ jeee nɔ ko nɔ ko ji lɛ fe humbug, ni miitao ni efee bugbear. Yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, bɔ ni mina lɛ lɛ gba mi naa waa, ni mikɛɛɛɛ Queequeg nɔ ko yɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ sɛɛ lɛ he, shi moŋ mikɛ minaanyo lɛ ho, ni miitao ni mana kɛji gbɔ lɛ baatsɔ ŋwɛigbɛ ni wɔtsɔ mli lɛ nɔŋŋ. Efee nakai; ni no sɛɛ lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ wɔ miibɔ gbee, shi kɛ yiŋtoo ni minyɛɛɛ masusu he kɛha miwala. Shihilɛ nɛɛ, kɛ ewiemɔ ni mli ekɔɔɔ he eko, ni tsɔɔ nɔ ko fã, ni jieɔ mɔ kpo fã kɛ fã, ni eha ehe lɛ, fɔ naakpɛɛ nibii ni bɛ faŋŋ kɛ gbeyeishemɔi ni fã fã yɔɔ mli srɔtoi fɛɛ yɛ mimli agbɛnɛ, kɛ fɛɛ ni kɛ Pequod lɛ yɔɔ tsakpaa; kɛ Kapten Ahab; kɛ naji ni elaaje lɛ; ni Kap Horn lɛ sa; kɛ jwiɛtɛi tsɛnsi lɛ; kɛ nɔ ni Kapten Peleg kɛɛ yɛ ehe, beni mishi lɛlɛ lɛ mli gbi ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ; kɛ squaw Tistig gbalɛ lɛ; ni gbɛfaa lɛ ni wɔkpɛ wɔyiŋ akɛ wɔbaafã lɛ; kɛ nibii krokomɛi oha ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ.

Mikpɛ miyiŋ akɛ mafee mihe miishɛɛ kɛji akɛ nɛkɛ Elijah ni egbɔjɔ nɛɛ miitswa wɔ gbee diɛŋtsɛ loo daabi, ni kɛ nakai yiŋtoo lɛ mikɛ Queequeg fo gbɛ lɛ, ni yɛ nakai fã lɛ nɔ lɛ, wɔku wɔsɛɛ wɔnyiɛ wɔsɛɛ. Shi Elia ho, ni etamɔ nɔ ni eyɔseee wɔ. Enɛ ha mihe jɔ mi; ni shikome ekoŋŋ, ni naagbee lɛ bɔ ni etamɔ mi lɛ, mitsɛ lɛ yɛ mitsui mli akɛ, humbug.





YITSƆƆ 20. Astir fɛɛ.

Gbi kome loo gbii enyɔ ho, ni nitsumɔ babaoo tee nɔ yɛ Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ mli. Jeee akɛ asaa lɛlɛ momoi lɛ pɛ, shi moŋ lɛlɛ heei miiba lɛlɛ lɛ mli, kɛ kanvas bolts, kɛ rigging coils; yɛ kukufoo mli lɛ, nɔ fɛɛ nɔ tsɔɔ akɛ lɛlɛ lɛ he saamɔi lɛ miiya naagbee oya. Kapten Peleg eyaaa ŋshɔ lɛ naa loo eyaaa ŋshɔ lɛ naa kɔkɔɔkɔ, shi moŋ etaa e-wigwam lɛ mli ni ekwɛɔ niji lɛ jogbaŋŋ: Bildad feɔ nibii fɛɛ ni aheɔ kɛ nibii ni akɛhaa yɛ shwapoi lɛ amli lɛ; ni hii ni tsuɔ nii yɛ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ kɛ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ miitsu nii kɛyashi be kakadaŋŋ beni gbɛkɛ eshɛ.

Yɛ gbi ni nyiɛ sɛɛ beni Queequeg kɛ enine wo saji lɛ amli lɛ, awie yɛ gbɔiatoohei fɛɛ ni lɛlɛ kuu lɛ damɔɔ lɛ, akɛ esa akɛ amɛ adekai lɛ ahi lɛlɛ lɛ mli dani gbɛkɛ ashɛ, ejaakɛ atsɔɔɔ bɔ ni ekolɛ lɛlɛ lɛ baaje gbɛ etsɛŋ. No hewɔ lɛ, mi kɛ Queequeg shi wɔ tsɔnei lɛ, ni wɔkpɛ wɔyiŋ, shi kɛlɛ, akɛ wɔbaawɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa kɛyashi naagbee. Shi etamɔ nɔ ni amɛtswaa adafi be kakadaŋŋ waa yɛ saji nɛɛ amli be fɛɛ be, ni lɛlɛ lɛ eyaaa gbii babaoo. Shi ebɛ naakpɛɛ; nitsumɔ kpakpa ko yɛ ni esa akɛ atsu, ni anyɛɛɛ akɛɛ nibii babaoo ni esa akɛ asusu he, dani Pequod lɛ aná nibii fɛɛ.

Mɔ fɛɛ mɔ le bɔ ni nibii babaoo—saji, tsɛnsii ni akɛwoɔ mli, tsɔji kɛ tsɛnsii, shɔvel kɛ tsɛnsii, tsɔji ni akɛwoɔ mli, nut-crackers, kɛ nɔ ni fata he lɛ he hiaa waa yɛ shia nɔkwɛmɔ nitsumɔ lɛ mli. Nakai eji yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔ he, ni biɔ ni mɔ ko akwɛ shia afii etɛ yɛ ŋshɔ wulu lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ jarayelɔi, jarayelɔi, datrɛfoi, aboloofeelɔi, kɛ shikatoohe nitsulɔi fɛɛ teŋ jɛkɛ. Ni eyɛ mli akɛ enɛ ji anɔkwale yɛ jarayeli lɛlɛi hu ahe moŋ, shi jeee yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ tamɔ bɔ ni eji yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛlɛi ahe lɛ. Ejaakɛ kɛfata ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa lɛ sɛɛ tsɛɔ waa lɛ he lɛ, nibii babaoo ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔ lɛ he lɛ, kɛ bɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsake amɛ yɛ ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni afɔɔ jɛmɛ yaa lɛ anɔ lɛ, esa akɛ akai akɛ, yɛ lɛlɛi fɛɛ ateŋ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛlɛi ji nɔ ni kɛ osharai kɛ nibii ni fiteɔ nii babaoo kpeɔ fe fɛɛ. gbɛfaa lɛ omanyeyeli lɛ damɔ nɔ fe fɛɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, lɛlɛi ni akɛtoɔ he lɛ, spars ni akɛtoɔ he lɛ, kɛ lines kɛ harpoons ni akɛtoɔ he lɛ, kɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni akɛtoɔ he lɛ, nɔ ni miihe ashɛ, shi Kapten ni akɛtoɔ he kɛ lɛlɛ ni ji enyɔ.

Yɛ be mli ni wɔyashɛ Ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, no mli lɛ eshwɛ fioo ni abaagbe Pequod lɛ ni akɛtoɔ nibii ni tsii fe fɛɛ lɛ naa; ni etsina loo, blodo, nu, fuɛl, kɛ dade hoops kɛ tso lɛ fata he. Shi, taakɛ atsɔɔ mli kɛtsɔ hiɛ lɛ, be ko ni eho lɛ, nibii srɔtoi ni yɔɔ srɔtoi, wuji kɛ bibii fɛɛ lɛ, ni akɛbaatsu nii ni akɛwo lɛlɛ mli lɛ yaa nɔ.

Mɛi ni fee nɛkɛ nibii ni akɛbaatsu nii nɛɛ ateŋ mɔ ni fe fɛɛ ji Kapten Bildad nyɛmi yoo, yoomo ko ni he waaa ni yɔɔ mumɔ ni mli wa ni ebɛ nijiaŋwujee, shi eyɛ mlihilɛ waa, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekpɛ eyiŋ akɛ, kɛji ebaanyɛ eye ebua lɛ, esaaa akɛ anaaa nɔ ko nɔ ko yɛ Pequod lɛ mli, beni eshɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa shikome pɛpɛɛpɛ sɛɛ. Be ko lɛ, ebaabote lɛlɛ lɛ mli kɛ pickles tsɛnsi kɛha shĩa nɔkwɛlɔ lɛ niyelihe lɛ; be kroko hu kɛ quills ni akɛwoɔ hefatalɔ nukpa lɛ okpɔlɔ nɔ, he ni ekɛ ewolo lɛ toɔ lɛ; shi ni ji etɛ lɛ kɛ flannel wolokpo kɛha mɔ ko sɛɛ ni hela lɛ mli bibioo lɛ. Yoo ko bɛ ni sa egbɛi ni ji Charity—Aunt Charity, taakɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ tsɛɔ lɛ lɛ fe lɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ. Ni tamɔ bɔ ni ejurɔfeemɔ nyɛmi yoo ko ji lɛ, nɛkɛ ejurɔfeemɔ nyɛmi yoo Charity nɛɛ fã gbɛ kɛtee biɛ kɛ biɛ, ni efee klalo akɛ ebaatsɔ enine kɛ etsui nɔ ewo nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni woɔ shi akɛ ebaaha mɛi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ ko ni enyɛmi nuu ni esumɔɔ lɛ waa ni atsɛɔ lɛ Bildad lɛ mli lɛ aná shweshweeshwe shihilɛ, miishɛjemɔ, kɛ miishɛjemɔ, ni lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eyɛ dɔlai enyɔ loo shika babaoo yɛ mli lɛ mli.

Shi eji nɔ ni haa mɔ naa kpɛɔ ehe akɛ ooona nɛkɛ Quakeress ni hiɛ tsui kpakpa nɛɛ ni eba lɛlɛ lɛ mli, taakɛ efee yɛ naagbee gbi lɛ nɔ lɛ, ni ehiɛ mu-tsɛnsi kakadaŋŋ yɛ enine kome mli, ni ekɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛgbeɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni kɛlɛ fe no lolo lɛ ehiɛ ekroko lɛ mli. Ni asaŋ Bildad diɛŋtsɛ kɛ Tatsɛ Peleg hu efooo sɛɛ kwraa. Shi Bildad lɛ, ekɛ saji ni he hiaa lɛ ahe wolo kakadaŋŋ yaa, ni be fɛɛ be ni ebaaba ehee lɛ, ekadi ni kɛ nakai saji lɛ kpãa gbee lɛ baa shi yɛ wolo lɛ nɔ. Be fɛɛ be lɛ, Peleg baa ni ekɛ ehe shwieɔ shi kɛjɛɔ e-whalebone ŋshɔ lɛ mli, ni ebolɔɔ etswaa hii ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, ni ebolɔɔ kɛyaa ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔne kudɔlɔi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ yitso lɛ anɔ lɛ anɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ egbeɔ naa kɛ gbee ni ekɛkuɔ esɛɛ kɛyaa e-wigwam lɛ mli.

Yɛ nɛkɛ hesaamɔ gbii nɛɛ amli lɛ, mi kɛ Queequeg fɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ saramɔ, ni taakɛ mibiɔ shii abɔ lɛ, mibiɔ Kapten Ahab he sane, kɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ ha, kɛ be ni ebaaba elɛlɛ lɛ mli. Amɛbaahã sanebimɔi nɛɛ ahetoo, akɛ eeya hiɛ jogbaŋŋ, ni akpaa gbɛ akɛ ebaaya lɛlɛ lɛ mli daa gbi; yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi enyɔ lɛ, Peleg kɛ Bildad baanyɛ akwɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni he hiaa koni lɛlɛ lɛ ashɛ gbɛfaa lɛ he. Eji miye mihe anɔkwale kulɛ, mana yɛ mitsui mli faŋŋ akɛ mikɛ mihe wo gbɛfaa kakadaŋŋ nɛɛ mli, ni mikɛ mihiŋmɛii efɔɔɔ nuu ni baafee nɔyelɔ ni yeɔ nɔ kwraa lɛ nɔ shikome po, beni lɛlɛ lɛ fã gbɛ kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ pɛ kɛkɛ. Shi kɛ́ nuu ko susu nɔ ko ni ejaaa he lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ ebaa lɛ akɛ kɛji akɛ ekɛ ehe ewo sane lɛ mli momo lɛ, ebɔɔ mɔdɛŋ ni ekɛ jwɛŋmɔ ni bɛ mli lɛ aha eyiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔi lɛ anɔ yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe po nɔ. Ni babaoo nɛkɛ ji bɔ ni eji yɛ migbɛfaŋ. Mikɛɛɛ nɔ ko, ni mibɔ mɔdɛŋ akɛ misusuuu nɔ ko he.

Naagbee lɛ, aha akɛ enɔ jetsɛremɔ lɛ be ko lɛ, lɛlɛ lɛ baafã gbɛ diɛŋtsɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, enɔ jetsɛremɔ lɛ, mi kɛ Queequeg je shishi mra waa.





YITSƆƆ 21. Lɛlɛ lɛ Mli Yaa.

No mli lɛ, eshwɛ fioo ni ŋmɛlɛtswai ekpaa baashɛ, shi leebi maŋkpa ni mli edɔ ni eyeee emuu pɛ, beni wɔbɛŋkɛ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ.

Mikɛɛ Queequeg akɛ: “Lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi komɛi yɛ ni miijo foi kɛmiiya hiɛ yɛ jɛmɛ, kɛji mina jogbaŋŋ lɛ, enyɛŋ efee hulutsoo; misusuɔ akɛ eshi kɛyashi hulu lɛ baate shi; ba!”

“Avast!” gbee ko bolɔ, ni enuŋtsɔ lɛ yɛ nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ lɛ ebɛŋkɛ wɔsɛɛ, ni ekɛ enine fɔ wɔtsitsii enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ nɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekɛ ehe wo wɔteŋ, edamɔ shi ni ekoto hiɛ fioo, yɛ hulutsoo be ni nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ mli lɛ mli, ni ekwɛ mi yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ kɛjɛ Queequeg kɛba miŋɔɔ. Eliya ni.

“Oyaa lɛlɛ lɛ mli?”

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Okɛ oniji ajie, ani obaafee nakai?”

Queequeg kɛɛ, ni ekpokpo ehe akɛ: “Kwɛmɔ biɛ, yaa ‘gbɛ!”

“Belɛ, ani oyaaa lɛlɛ lɛ mli?”

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ, "Hɛɛ, wɔji, shi mɛni ji bo sane? Ani ole, Mr. Elijah, akɛ mibuɔ bo akɛ mɔ ko ni bɛ bulɛ fioo?"

Elijah kɛɛ blɛoo ni ekɛ naakpɛɛ kwɛ Queequeg, kɛ hiŋmɛii ni anyɛɛɛ abu he akɔntaa fe fɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Daabi, daabi, daabi, mileee nakai.”

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ, "Elijah, obaaha mi kɛ minaanyo lɛ atsu nii kɛtsɔ sɛɛkuu nɔ. Wɔmiiya India kɛ Pasifik Ŋshɔi lɛ amli, ni wɔbaasumɔ ni akamɔ wɔ."

"Ye be, ye? Nyɛkuɔ nyɛsɛɛ nyɛbaa dani nyɛyeɔ leebi niyenii?"

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Ekpã, Queequeg, ba.”

“Holo!” Elia ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ lɛ bolɔ, ni ejie wɔyi beni wɔjie wɔhe fioo lɛ.

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Kaasusu ehe kɔkɔɔkɔ, Queequeg, ba.”

Shi eju kɛba wɔŋɔɔ ekoŋŋ, ni ekɛ enine tswa mitsitsi trukaa, ni ekɛɛ—“Ani nyɛna nɔ ko ni tamɔ hii ni miiya nakai lɛlɛ lɛ he be ko ni eho nɛ?”

Akɛni sanebimɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ ni kɔɔ anɔkwale he nɛɛ gba mi naa hewɔ lɛ, miha hetoo akɛ, “Hɛɛ, misusu akɛ mina hii ejwɛ loo enumɔ, shi edɔ tsɔ ni minyɛŋ mafee nɔmimaa.”

Elia kɛɛ akɛ: “Efee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ waa, ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ waa.” “Leebi kɛha nyɛ.”

Shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ wɔkpa lɛ; shi shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, eba blɛoo yɛ wɔsɛɛ; ni eta mitsitsi he ekoŋŋ, ni ekɛɛ, “Kwɛmɔ kɛji obaanyɛ ona amɛ bianɛ, ani obaanyɛ ona?

“Taomɔ namɔ?”

“Leebi aha nyɛ, leebi aha nyɛ!” ekɛ ehe wo mli ekoŋŋ, ni efã ekoŋŋ. "Oh! Miiya ni mabɔ nyɛ kɔkɔ yɛ—shi ekɔɔɔ he eko, ekɔɔɔ he eko—ekome pɛ, fɛɛ yɛ weku lɛ mli hu;—fɛ̃i ni naa wa ŋmɛnɛ leebi, aloo jeee nakai? Nyɛkɛ nyɛ aya. Misusuɔ akɛ nyɛnaaa nyɛ dɔŋŋ etsɛŋ tsɔ; ja eba yɛ Kojolɔi Akuu Wulu lɛ hiɛ." Ni kɛ wiemɔi ni egbɛ eshwa nɛɛ eshi yɛ naagbee, ni eshi mi, yɛ be kukuoo mli, yɛ naakpɛɛ ni jeee bibioo yɛ ehiɛ ni ekɛwoɔ mli lɛ hewɔ.

Yɛ naagbee lɛ, beni wɔbote Pequod lɛ mli lɛ, wɔna nɔ fɛɛ nɔ yɛ dioofeemɔ ni mli kwɔ mli, ni susuma ko bɛ ni miitsi ehe. Atswa tsɔne lɛ shinaa lɛ naa yɛ mli; ŋwɛitsu lɛ fɛɛ nɔ, ni akɛ tsɔnei ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ ebɔ he. Beni wɔyaa hiɛ kɛyaa ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, wɔna akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ ni akɛshwieɔ shi lɛ mli egbele. Beni wɔna kane ko lɛ, wɔba shi, ni wɔyana tsɔne momo ko pɛ yɛ jɛmɛ, ni akɛ pea-jakɛt ni efite ehà ehe. Akɛ lɛ fɛɛ shwie adekai enyɔ anɔ, ni ehiɛ shwie shi ni ekɛ eniji ni efolɔ mli lɛ ebɔ he. Wɔɔ ni mli kwɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ wɔ enɔ.

“Nakai lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ni wɔna lɛ, Queequeg, nɛgbɛ amɛbaanyɛ amɛya?” mikɛɛ, ni mikɛ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ miikwɛ mɔ ni ewɔ lɛ. Shi etamɔ nɔ ni, beni Queequeg yɔɔ lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ, eyɔseko nɔ ni miwieɔ he bianɛ lɛ kwraa; no hewɔ lɛ kulɛ masusu akɛ alaka mi yɛ hiŋmɛi nɔ yɛ nakai sane lɛ mli, eji jeee Elia sanebimɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ mli yɛ gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ hewɔ. Shi mitswa nɔ lɛ mishwie shi; ni ekadi mɔ ni ewɔ lɛ ekoŋŋ, ni ekɛ shwɛmɔ tsɔɔ Queequeg akɛ ekolɛ ehi jogbaŋŋ akɛ wɔkɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ baata shi; ni ekɛɛ lɛ akɛ eto ehe shi yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ. Ekɛ enine fɔ mɔ ni ewɔ lɛ sɛɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni enuɔ he akɛ eyɛ blɛoo bɔ ni sa; ni no sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ nɔ ko kwraa tsuuu nii, ni eta shi dioo yɛ jɛmɛ.

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Gracious! Queequeg, kaata jɛmɛ.”

Queequeg kɛɛ akɛ: “Oo! perry dood sɛi lɛ, mimaŋ gbɛ; eyeŋ lɛ hiɛ awui.”

"Hiɛɛ!" mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ, "tsɛ nakai ehiɛ? hiɛ ni mli hi waa nakai beaŋ; shi bɔ ni emuɔ waa ha, ekɛ ehe miiwo mli; shi, Queequeg, ohe wa, eegbala ohiafoi lɛ ahiɛ. Shi, Queequeg! Kwɛmɔ, ebaatsi bo etsɛŋ. Misusuɔ akɛ eteee shi."

Queequeg jie ehe kɛtee mɔ ni ewɔ lɛ yitso lɛ sɛɛ pɛ, ni ekɛ e-tomahawk paipi lɛ shã. Mitara enaji ahe. Wɔhaa paipii lɛ hoɔ mɔ ni wɔɔ lɛ nɔ, kɛjɛɔ eko nɔ kɛyaa ekroko nɔ. Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, beni Queequeg bi lɛ saji yɛ egbɛ ni efite lɛ nɔ lɛ, eha minu shishi akɛ, yɛ eshikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ lɛ, yɛ sɛii kɛ sofai srɔtoi fɛɛ ni bɛ lɛ hewɔ lɛ, maŋtsɛ lɛ, onukpai lɛ, kɛ gbɔmɛi wuji lɛ fɛɛ yɛ kusum ni ji ni amɛha mɛi ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ lɛ ateŋ mɛi komɛi agbɔjɔ amɛha ottomanbii; ni bɔni afee ni okɛ shĩa ko awo mli jogbaŋŋ yɛ nakai gbɛfaŋ lɛ, nɔ pɛ ni he hiaa ji ni ohé shwɛlɔi ekpaa loo nyɔŋma, ni okɛ amɛ awo ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ kɛ ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ. Kɛfata he lɛ, ehi jogbaŋŋ yɛ gbɛfaa mli; ehi kwraa fe nakai abɔɔ mli sɛii ni anyɛɔ atsakeɔ amɛfeɔ tsei ni akɛnyiɛɔ lɛ; yɛ bei komɛi amli lɛ, onukpa ko tsɛɔ etsulɔ lɛ, ni esumɔɔ ni efee ehe sɛi yɛ tso ko ni egbɛ eshwa lɛ shishi, ekolɛ yɛ he ko ni ŋmɔtɔ yɔɔ.

Beni Queequeg gbaa nibii nɛɛ ahe sane lɛ, be fɛɛ be ni Queequeg baaná tomahawk lɛ kɛjɛ midɛŋ lɛ, ekɛ hatchet-side ni yɔɔ mli lɛ shwereɔ yɛ mɔ ni ewɔ lɛ yitso nɔ.

“Mɛni hewɔ nakai, Queequeg?”

"Efee mlɛo, gbe-e; oh! efee mlɛo!"

Ekɛ kaimɔi komɛi ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni kɔɔ e-tomahawk-paipi lɛ he lɛ miiya nɔ, ni etamɔ nɔ ni yɛ ekɛ nitsumɔi enyɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ ehenyɛlɔi lɛ ajwɛŋmɔi ni eha esusuma lɛ fɛɛ tsui nyɔɔ emli, beni wɔgbala wɔhe tɛ̃ɛ kɛtee rigger ni ewɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ. Agbɛnɛ lasu ni naa wa ni eyi bu ni egbɔjɔ lɛ mli obɔ kwraa lɛ, ebɔi ehe wiemɔ. Ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ ko ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli mu; no sɛɛ lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ehao yɛ ehiɛ; no sɛɛ lɛ, ebɔle ehe shikome loo shii enyɔ; kɛkɛ ni ete shi ni ekɛ ehiŋmɛii shwie shi.

“Holo!” emu naagbee lɛ, “namɛi ji nyɛ tawa shɛrelɔi?”

Miha hetoo akɛ: “Hii ni akɛ lɛlɛ tsuɔ nii, mɛɛ be efãa gbɛ?”

"Aye, aye, nyɛmiiya mli, aloo jeee nakai? Efãa gbɛ ŋmɛnɛ. Kapten lɛ ba lɛlɛ lɛ mli nyɛsɛɛ gbɛkɛ."

“Mɛɛ Kapten?—Ahab?”

“Namɔ ja lɛ diɛŋtsɛ?”

Miiya ni miyabi lɛ saji krokomɛi ni kɔɔ Ahab he, ni wɔnu dede ko yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ.

Mɔ ni tsuɔ nii yɛ tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Holoa! Starbuck lala lɛ.” “Eji hefatalɔ nukpa ni hiɛ kã, nakai; gbɔmɔ kpakpa, kɛ Nyɔŋmɔ jalɔ; shi mɛi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ wala mli bianɛ lɛ, esa akɛ matsɔ mihe kɛya.” Ni ekɛɛ nakai lɛ, etee lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ, ni wɔnyiɛ esɛɛ.

Agbɛnɛ hulu lɛ miite shi faŋŋ. Etsɛɛɛ ni lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ ba lɛlɛ lɛ mli enyɔ enyɔ kɛ etɛ; riggers lɛ tsɔ amɛhe; hefatalɔi lɛ kɛ amɛhe wo gbalashihilɛ mli kɛ miishɛɛ; ni ŋshɔ lɛ naa bii lɛ ateŋ mɛi babaoo kɛ amɛhe wo naagbee nibii srɔtoi ni amɛkɛbaa lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ mli. Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, Kapten Ahab tee nɔ ehi etsu lɛ mli ni anaaa lɛ.
