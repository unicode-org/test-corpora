YITSƆƆ 1. Nibii ni miiba.

Nyɛtsɛa mi Ishmael. Afii komɛi ni eho nɛ—ekɔɔɔ he eko be pɔtɛɛ ni ebaahe lɛ—akɛ shika fioo ko pɛ yɔɔ mikotoku lɛ mli loo nɔ ko kwraa bɛ mihe, ni nɔ ko pɔtɛɛ ko bɛ ni baaha miná he miishɛɛ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, misusu akɛ mafã gbɛ fioo ni mayakwɛ je lɛŋ fã ni nu yɔɔ lɛ. Eji gbɛ ko ni miyɔɔ ni mitsɔɔ nɔ mijieɔ shwiili lɛ kɛjɛɔ mli ni mikɛtoɔ lá ni hoɔ yɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli lɛ he gbɛjianɔ. Be fɛɛ be ni mana akɛ mihe miiwo mihe gbeyei yɛ daaŋ lɛ he; be fɛɛ be ni ebaafee November ni mli edɔ ni nugbɔ nɛɔ yɛ misusuma mli; be fɛɛ be ni mana akɛ mikɛ mihe wooo mli yɛ gbonyo adekai atoohei lɛ ahiɛ, ni mikɛ gbonyo bu fɛɛ gbonyo bu ni mikɛkpeɔ lɛ sɛɛ baa; ni titri lɛ be fɛɛ be ni mi-hypos lɛ baaná minɔ hewalɛ waa, ni ebiɔ jeŋba he shishitoo mla ni mli wa ni baatsi mi gbɛ koni mikajɛ mihe miwo gbɛjegbɛ lɛ nɔ, ni mikɛ gbɛjianɔtoo ko atswa mɛi atsitsii—no lɛ, mibuɔ akɛ be eshɛ ni esa akɛ maya ŋshɔ hiɛ oya bɔ ni manyɛ. Enɛ ji nɔ ni mikɛtoɔ pistol kɛ bɔɔlu najiaŋ. Cato kɛ jeŋ nilee mli shweremɔ shwieɔ eklante lɛ nɔ; Mikɛ mihe woɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli dioo. Nɔ ko bɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ yɛ enɛ mli. Kɛji amɛle lɛ, nɔ ni miihe ashɛ hii fɛɛ ni yɔɔ amɛ gbɛhe lɛ mli, yɛ be ko mli loo be kroko mli lɛ, hiɛɔ henumɔi ni miihe ashɛ ŋshɔ lɛ he lɛ nɔŋŋ waa kɛ mi.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, omaŋ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ ni ji Manhattoes lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ, ni ŋshɔkpɔi ni ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ ebɔle he tamɔ India ŋshɔkpɔi ni koral ŋshɔkei ebɔle he lɛ—jarayeli kɛ e-surf lɛ ebɔle ehe. Ninejurɔgbɛ kɛ abɛkugbɛ lɛ, gbɛjegbɛi lɛ kɛ bo yaa nu lɛ mli. Emaŋ lɛ teŋgbɛ ni fe fɛɛ ji batiri lɛ, he ni ŋshɔkei lɛ hoɔ nakai ŋshɔŋloo ni hi jogbaŋŋ lɛ he, ni kɔɔyɔɔ ni ŋmɛlɛtswai fioo komɛi ni eho lɛ, shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ bɛ lɛ haa ejɔɔ lɛ. Kwɛmɔ mɛi babaoo ni kwɛɔ nu yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ.

Bɔɔ maŋ lɛ he yɛ lamɔ Hejɔɔmɔ Gbi shwane mli. Yaa kɛjɛ Corlears Hook kɛya Coenties Slip, ni kɛjɛ jɛmɛ lɛ, kɛtsɔ Whitehall nɔ, kɛya kooyigbɛ. Mɛni onaa?—Amɛto amɛhe tamɔ bulɔi ni efee dioo yɛ maŋ lɛ he fɛɛ, ni gbɔmɛi akpei abɔ ni gboiɔ lɛ damɔ shi ni amɛmiikwɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ susumɔi amli. Mɛi komɛi kɛ amɛhe fɔ̃ɔ ŋmeii lɛ anɔ; mɛi komɛi eta ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ yitsei lɛ anɔ; mɛi komɛi miikwɛ lɛlɛi ni jɛ China lɛ agbogboi lɛ anɔ; ekomɛi yɛ ŋwɛi shɔɔŋ yɛ tsɔne lɛ mli, tamɔ nɔ ni amɛmiibɔ mɔdɛŋ koni amɛna ŋshɔ lɛ he mfoniri ni hi fe tsutsu lɛ lolo. Shi mɛi nɛɛ fɛɛ ji shikpɔŋ nɔ bii; ni ji otsi mli gbii ni akɛ lath kɛ plasta ewo mli—ni akɛfiɔ shikatoohei ahe, ni akɛfiɔ sɛii ahe, ni akɛfiɔ okpɔlɔi ahe. Belɛ te enɛ feɔ tɛŋŋ? Ani ŋmɔji ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji amli lɛ etee? Mɛni amɛfeɔ yɛ biɛ?

Shi kwɛmɔ! biɛ ji he ni asafoi babaoo baa, ni amɛnyiɛ tɛ̃ɛ kɛmiiya nu lɛ mli, ni etamɔ nɔ ni amɛmiiya nu lɛ mli. Naakpɛɛ! Nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ni baaha amɛtsui anyɔ amɛmli akɛ ja shikpɔŋ lɛ husu ni fe fɛɛ lɛ; ni oooya shi yɛ jɛmɛ nitoohei lɛ ashishi lɛ faaa. Dabi, esa akɛ amɛbɛŋkɛ nu lɛ he bɔ ni amɛaanyɛ ni amɛgbeeee mli. Ni jɛmɛ ji he ni amɛdamɔ—amɛteŋ jɛkɛmɔ ji kilomitai babaoo—ligii. Mɛi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ maŋ lɛ mli lɛ, amɛjɛɔ gbɛjegbɛi kɛ gbɛjegbɛi bibii, gbɛjegbɛi kɛ gbɛjegbɛi bibii anɔ—kooyigbɛ, bokagbɛ, wuoyigbɛ, kɛ anaigbɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, amɛ fɛɛ amɛfeɔ ekome yɛ biɛ. Kɛɛmɔ mi, ani magnetic virtue ni yɔɔ nakai lɛlɛi lɛ fɛɛ akɔmpas lɛ atsɔnei lɛ amli lɛ gbalaa amɛ kɛyaa jɛmɛ?

Shikome ekoŋŋ. Kɛɛmɔ akɛ oyɛ maŋ lɛ mli; yɛ ŋshɔkpɔi ni nɔ kwɔlɔ komɛi anɔ. Nɔ ni miihe ashɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ ni osumɔɔ lɛ, ni nyɔŋma kɛ ekome lɛ, ekɛ bo baa shi yɛ ŋshɔkpɔ ko nɔ, ni eshiɔ bo yɛ jɛmɛ yɛ nu ŋshɔ ko ni yɔɔ faa lɛ mli lɛ naa. Majik yɛ mli. Ha gbɔmɛi ni bɛ jwɛŋmɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ abote ejwɛŋmɔi ni mli kwɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ amli—damɔ nakai gbɔmɔ lɛ nɔ yɛ enaji anɔ, ekɛ enaji ama shi, ni ebaakudɔ bo kɛya nu mli ni tɔmɔ bɛ he, kɛji nu yɛ nakai kpokpaa lɛ fɛɛ nɔ. Kɛji akɛ esa akɛ kumai aye bo yɛ Amerika ŋa wulu lɛ nɔ pɛŋ lɛ, kaa kaa nɛɛ okwɛ, kɛji akɛ ebalɛ akɛ akɛ metaphysics tsɔɔlɔ ko ha ogbɛfaa lɛ. Hɛɛ, taakɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ le lɛ, jwɛŋmɔ kɛ nu ji gbalashihilɛ kɛya naanɔ.

Shi nitɛŋlɔ ko nɛ. Esumɔɔ ni efee bo mfoniri ni fe fɛɛ, ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni yɔɔ dioo fe fɛɛ, ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ fe fɛɛ yɛ Saco jɔɔ lɛ mli fɛɛ. Mɛni ji nɔ titri ni ekɛtsuɔ nii? Etsei lɛ damɔ jɛmɛ, ni eko fɛɛ eko hiɛ ŋmei, tamɔ nɔ ni hermit kɛ sɛŋmɔtso ko yɛ mli; ni biɛ ji he ni eŋmɔ lɛ wɔɔ, ni jɛmɛ ji he ni ekooloi lɛ wɔɔ; ni ŋwɛi kɛjɛɔ jɛmɛ shĩa bibioo lɛ mli lasu ni haa mɔ wɔɔ yaa. Kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ tswaa kɛyaa kooi ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ amli shɔŋŋ, ni eshɛɔ gɔji ni ebɔle amɛhe ni amɛwo amɛhe bluu ni yɔɔ gɔŋ lɛ sɛɛ lɛ ahe. Shi eyɛ mli akɛ mfoniri lɛ kã shi tamɔ nɛkɛ, ni eyɛ mli akɛ nɛkɛ pine tso nɛɛ hosoɔ eŋmɔlɔi tamɔ baai yɛ tookwɛlɔ nɛɛ yitso nɔ moŋ, shi fɛɛ ji yakayaka nii, ja tookwɛlɔ lɛ hiŋmɛi kwɛ majik faa ni yɔɔ ehiɛ lɛ nɔ. Yaa saramɔ Prairies yɛ June mli, beni okɛ kilomitai babaoo nyiɛɔ Tiger-lilies ateŋ kɛyashɛɔ onaji ahe lɛ—mɛɛ ji nɔ ni charm kome lɛ taoɔ?—Nu—nu ŋmɔtɔ kome po bɛ jɛmɛ! Ani Niagara ji ŋã ko kɛkɛ kulɛ, ani obaafã gbɛ oshitoi akpei lɛ koni oyakwɛ? Mɛni hewɔ lalafolɔ ohiafo ni yɔɔ Tennessee lɛ, beni enine shɛ shika ni eyi obɔ kɛ niji enyɔ nɔ trukaa lɛ, esusu he akɛ ebaahe atade, ni ehe hiaa lɛ yɛ awerɛho gbɛ nɔ lɛ eha lɛ, loo ekɛ eshika lɛ baawo gbɛfaa ni mɛi ni nyiɛɔ shi kɛyaa Rockaway Beach lɛ mli lɛ? Mɛni hewɔ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ gbekɛ nuu fɛɛ gbekɛ nuu ni yɔɔ hewalɛ ni yɔɔ susuma ni yɔɔ hewalɛ ni yɔɔ emli lɛ, yɛ be ko mli loo be kroko mli lɛ, efeɔ shwilafo koni eya ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ? Mɛni hewɔ yɛ oklɛŋklɛŋ gbɛfaa lɛ mli akɛ gbɛfalɔ lɛ, bo diɛŋtsɛ onu teemɔŋ ŋmɔlɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ he, beni akɛɛ bo klɛŋklɛŋ akɛ bo kɛ olɛlɛ lɛ bɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ agbɛnɛ lɛ? Mɛni hewɔ blema Persiabii lɛ buɔ ŋshɔ lɛ akɛ eji krɔŋkrɔŋ lɛ? Mɛni hewɔ Helabii lɛ ha lɛ nyɔŋmɔ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto, kɛ Jove diɛŋtsɛ nyɛmi nuu lɛ? Eji anɔkwale akɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ jeee nɔ ni shishinumɔ bɛ he. Ni nakai Narcissus sane lɛ shishinumɔ mli kwɔ fe tsutsu lɛ, mɔ ni akɛni enyɛɛɛ enu amaga ni piŋɔ mɔ, ni mli jɔ ni ena yɛ nu hiɛ lɛ mli hewɔ lɛ, ebote mli ni nu lɛ shwie lɛ lɛ mli. Shi nakai mfoniri lɛ nɔŋŋ lɛ, wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔnaa yɛ faai kɛ ŋshɔi fɛɛ amli. Eji wala mli ŋmiŋmi ni anyɛɛɛ anu shishi lɛ he mfoniri; ni enɛ ji nɔ ni haa enɛ fɛɛ baa.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛ́ mikɛɛ akɛ miyɛ su akɛ miyaa ŋshɔ hiɛ be fɛɛ be ni mibɔi hiŋmɛii lɛ ahe ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni mibɔi miflufla lɛ he jwɛŋmɔ fe nine lɛ, mitsɔɔɔ akɛ mahã asusu akɛ mitee ŋshɔ hiɛ pɛŋ akɛ gbɛfalɔ. Ejaakɛ bɔni afee ni oya akɛ gbɛfalɔ lɛ, esa akɛ oná shika kotoku, ni shika kotoku ji atade kɛkɛ ja oyɛ nɔ ko yɛ mli. Kɛfata he lɛ, gbɛfalɔi lɛ náa ŋshɔ mli hela—amɛfeɔ béi—amɛwɔɔɔ gbɛkɛ—amɛnáaa amɛhe miishɛɛ babaoo, akɛ nɔ ni afɔɔ feemɔ;—daabi, miyaaa akɛ gbɛfalɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ; ni asaŋ, eyɛ mli akɛ miji ŋmei nɔ ko moŋ, shi miyaaa ŋshɔ hiɛ pɛŋ akɛ Komodo, loo Kapten, loo Nihoolɔ. Mishiɔ nitsumɔi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ anunyam kɛ srɔtofeemɔ lɛ mihaa mɛi ni sumɔɔ amɛ lɛ. Yɛ migbɛfaŋ lɛ, misumɔɔɔɔ nitsumɔi ni yɔɔ bulɛ, kaai, kɛ amanehului srɔtoi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ bulɛ. Eji nɔ ni fa bɔ ni manyɛ mafee koni makwɛ mihe, ni mikwɛɛɛ lɛlɛi, barques, brigs, schooners, kɛ nɔ krokomɛi anɔ. Ni yɛ nɔ ni kɔɔ nihoolɔ ni yaa lɛ he lɛ, —eyɛ mli akɛ mijajeɔ akɛ anunyam babaoo yɛ mli moŋ, shi nihoolɔ ji asraafonyo nukpa ko ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ mli—shi kɛlɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, misusuuu kooloi ni ashãa lɛ ahe kɔkɔɔkɔ;—eyɛ mli akɛ be ko ni eho lɛ, ashã lɛ, akɛ bɔta wo mli yɛ bulɛ mli, ni akɛ ŋoo kɛ ŋmɔtɔ wo mli yɛ bulɛ mli moŋ, shi mɔ ko mɔ ko bɛ ni baakɛɛ akɛ, kooloo ni ashã fe bɔ ni masumɔ. Mizraimbii blemabii lɛ amaga jamɔ he suɔmɔ ni amɛyɔɔ yɛ ibis ni ashã kɛ faa mli okpɔŋɔ ni ashã lɛ mli ji he ni onaa nakai bɔɔ nii lɛ agboi yɛ amɛ shãa-tsũi wuji ni ji piramidii lɛ amli lɛ.

Dabi, kɛ mitee ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ, miyaa tamɔ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ folo, yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ tuutu, ni mikɛ mihe woɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ, yɛ ŋwɛi jɛmɛ kɛyaa maŋtsɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yitso lɛ nɔ. Eji anɔkwale akɛ, amɛfãa mi yɛ ekomɛi ahe moŋ, ni amɛhaa mituɔ kɛjɛɔ spar kome mli kɛyaa spar kroko mli, tamɔ ŋmei ni yɔɔ May ŋmɔ mli lɛ. Ni shishijee lɛ, nɛkɛ nibii nɛɛ esaaa mɔ he bɔ ni sa. Etaa mɔ ko bulɛ henumɔ he, titri lɛ kɛji ojɛ weku momo ko ni ato ama shi yɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ, Van Rensselaers, loo Randolphs, loo Hardicanutes mli. Ni nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, kɛji akɛ dani okɛ onine baawo tar-tsɛnsi lɛ mli pɛ lɛ, no mli lɛ oye nɔ akɛ maŋ skul tsɔɔlɔ, ni oha gbekɛbii hii ni kɛlɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ damɔ shi kɛ gbeyeishemɔ yɛ ohiɛ. Mikɛ nɔmimaa haa bo akɛ tsakemɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni mli wa waa, kɛjɛ skul tsɔɔlɔ nɔ kɛtee lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ nɔ, ni ebiɔ Seneca kɛ Stoabii lɛ a-decoction ni mli wa koni onyɛ okɛ ŋmɔlɔ atsu nii ni okɛtsu nii. Shi enɛ po fiteɔ yɛ be ni sa mli.

Ni kɛji akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko ni egbɔ lɛ ekomɛi fã mi koni mayahe tsɔne ni akɛshwieɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ? Mɛni nakai bulɛ ni bɛ lɛ ji, kɛ asusu, mitsɔɔ, yɛ Kpaŋmɔ Hee lɛ sɛkɛns lɛ nɔ? Ani osusuɔ akɛ ŋwɛibɔfo nukpa Gabriel susuɔ nɔ ko ni baa shi yɛ mihe, ejaakɛ miboɔ nakai nuu momo lɛ toi oya kɛ bulɛ yɛ nakai shihilɛ pɔtɛɛ lɛ mli? Namɔ jeee nyɔŋ? Kɛɛmɔ mi nakai. Ojogbaŋŋ, no hewɔ lɛ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafoi akuu lɛ mli onukpai lɛ baafã mi kɛya he fɛɛ he—bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni amɛbaatswa mi ŋmɔlɔ ni amɛtswa mi ŋmɔlɔ, miyɛ tsui ni nyɔɔ mɔ mli akɛ mile akɛ ehi; akɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ kroko ji gbɛ kome loo gbɛ kroko nɔ ni asɔmɔɔ lɛ yɛ gbɛ ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ nɔ—yɛ gbɔmɔtsoŋ loo metaphysical susumɔ mli, ni ji; ni nakai ji bɔ ni jeŋ muu fɛɛ thump lɛ hoɔ kɛbɔleɔ he, ni esa akɛ niji fɛɛ atsɔ amɛhe tsitsi nɔ, ni amɛmii ashɛ amɛhe.

Shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, miyaa ŋshɔ hiɛ be fɛɛ be akɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ, ejaakɛ amɛfeɔ amɛyiŋ akɛ amɛbaawo mi nyɔmɔ yɛ mihaomɔ lɛ hewɔ, yɛ be mli ni amɛwooo gbɛfalɔi lɛ shika bibioo ko po ni minu he pɛŋ lɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ. Nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ enɛ lɛ, esa akɛ gbɛfalɔi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ awo nyɔmɔ. Ni srɔtofeemɔ fɛɛ ni yɔɔ nyɔmɔwoo kɛ nyɔmɔwoo ni awoɔ mɔ lɛ teŋ yɛ je lɛŋ. Ekolɛ nyɔmɔwoo nifeemɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni haaa mɔ he ajɔ lɛ fe fɛɛ ni tso yibii abɔɔ mli julɔi enyɔ lɛ kɛba wɔ nɔ. Shi ni awoɔ lɛ nyɔmɔ ,—mɛɛ nɔ akɛbaato he? Maŋtiase mli nitsumɔ ni gbɔmɔ kɛnáa shika lɛ ji naakpɛɛ nii diɛŋtsɛ, kɛ wɔsusu he akɛ wɔheɔ wɔyeɔ waa akɛ shika ji shikpɔŋ nɔ helai fɛɛ ashishijee, ni akɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, gbɔmɔ ni yɔɔ shika lɛ nyɛŋ abote ŋwɛi. Ah! kwɛ bɔ ni wɔkɛ miishɛɛ haa wɔhe woɔ hiɛkpatamɔ mli!

Yɛ naagbee lɛ, miyaa ŋshɔ hiɛ be fɛɛ be akɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ, yɛ gbɔmɔtsoŋ hewalɛnamɔ ni hi kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ krɔŋŋ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ lɛ hewɔ. Ejaakɛ taakɛ eji yɛ je nɛɛ mli lɛ, kɔɔyɔɔ ni jɛɔ yitso nɔ lɛ fa babaoo fe kɔɔyɔɔ ni jɛɔ anaigbɛ (ni ji, kɛji okuuu Pythagoras mla lɛ mli kɔkɔɔkɔ), no hewɔ lɛ yɛ bei babaoo mli lɛ, Commodore ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fã lɛ mli lɛ naa e-atmosphere lɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ lɛ adɛŋ. Esusuɔ akɛ emuɔ klɛŋklɛŋ; shi jeee nakai. Nakai nɔŋŋ ji bɔ ni mɛi ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ nyiɛɔ amɛhiɛnyiɛlɔi lɛ ahiɛ yɛ nibii krokomɛi babaoo amli, yɛ be kome too mli ni hiɛnyiɛlɔi lɛ susuuu he kwraa lɛ. Shi mɛni hewɔ ebalɛ akɛ, beni minu ŋshɔ lɛ he shii abɔ akɛ jarayelɔ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, esa akɛ mikɛwo miyitsoŋ agbɛnɛ koni maya ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa; nɛkɛ ji Fates polisifonyo ni anaaa lɛ, ni kwɛɔ minɔ be fɛɛ be, ni ekɛ mi feɔ gbee yɛ teemɔŋ, ni enáa minɔ hewalɛ yɛ gbɛ ko ni anyɛɛɛ abu he akɔntaa nɔ—ebaanyɛ eha hetoo jogbaŋŋ fe mɔ fɛɛ mɔ. Ni, ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ, miyaa nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa nɛɛ mli lɛ, fata Providence gbɛjianɔtoo wulu ni ato be saŋŋ ni eho lɛ he. Eba akɛ lala kuku ko ni akɛwoɔ mli kɛ solo ni yɔɔ nifeemɔi ni mli kwɔ babaoo ateŋ. Misusuɔ akɛ esa akɛ nɛkɛ mla nɛɛ fã nɛɛ atsu nɔ ko tamɔ nɛkɛ:

“ Shikpɔŋhosomɔ Kpeteŋkpele ni Atswa Kɛha United States Nɔyeli lɛ Nɔyeli lɛ. “WHALING VOYAGE NI ISHMAEL KO TSƆƆ. “TA NI LAA HE TSƆƆ YƐ AFFGANISTAN.”

Eyɛ mli akɛ minyɛɛɛ makɛɛ nɔ hewɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ ni nakai shwɛmɔhe nɔkwɛlɔi lɛ, ni ji Fates lɛ, kɛ mi wo shi kɛha ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa lɛ fã ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ nɛɛ, beni ato mɛi krokomɛi ahe gbɛjianɔ kɛha fãi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ yɛ osharai ni nɔ kwɔlɔ amli, kɛ fãi kukuji ni yɔɔ mlɛo yɛ shwɛmɔi ni yɔɔ fɛo amli, kɛ fãi ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ yɛ shwɛmɔi amli lɛ—eyɛ mli akɛ minyɛɛɛ makɛɛ nɔ hewɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ moŋ; shi, amrɔ nɛɛ ni mikaiɔ shihilɛi lɛ fɛɛ lɛ, misusuɔ akɛ manyɛ mana nɔ ko fioo yɛ jɛɛhei kɛ yiŋtoi ni akɛ ŋaa gbɛ nɔ ajie lɛ kpo atsɔɔ mi yɛ gbɛi srɔtoi anɔ lɛ, ni tsirɛ mi koni mibɔi nitsumɔ ni mitsu lɛ, kɛfata he lɛ, ekɛ mi wo lakamɔ ni ji akɛ eji halamɔ ni jɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ misuɔmɔnaa nii ni bɛ hiɛaŋkwɛmɔ kɛ yiŋkpɛɛ mli lɛ mli fioo.

Yiŋtoi nɛɛ ateŋ nɔ titri ji susumɔ ni fe fɛɛ ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo wulu lɛ diɛŋtsɛ he lɛ. Nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ te mishwelɛ fɛɛ shi. Kɛkɛ ni ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ ŋa nɔ kɛ ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni ekɛ e-ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ fa lɛ shwie nɔ lɛ; osharai ni anyɛɛɛ ajie, ni bɛ gbɛi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he lɛ; enɛɛmɛi, kɛ naakpɛɛ nibii fɛɛ ni baa ni ji Patagonia nibii ni anaa kɛ gbeei akpe lɛ ye ebua ni ekanya mi kɛtee nɔ ni misumɔɔ lɛ nɔ. Ekolɛ, kɛji akɛ hii krokomɛi yɛ lɛ, kulɛ nibii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ efeŋ nɔ ni tsirɛɔ mɔ; shi mi lɛ, naanɔ ŋmɔlɔ miipiŋ mi yɛ nibii ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ahewɔ. Misumɔɔ ŋshɔi ni agu lɛ anɔ nyiɛmɔ, kɛ shikpɔŋ yɛ ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ gbeyei anɔ. Mikuuu mihiɛ mishwieee nɔ ni hi nɔ, miyɔseɔ gbeyeishemɔ ko oya, ni manyɛ mikɛ lɛ ahi shi lolo—ani amɛbaaŋmɛ mi gbɛ—ejaakɛ eji nɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ akɛ mikɛ gboklɛfoi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ he ni mɔ ko yɔɔ lɛ aaahi naanyobɔɔ mli.

No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ nibii nɛɛ ahewɔ lɛ, ahere ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa lɛ hiɛmɛɛ; naakpɛɛ je lɛ mli nu afua shinaai wuji lɛ gbele amɛhe, ni yɛ koo mli henɔwomɔ ni tsirɛ mi kɛtee miyiŋtoo lɛ mli lɛ, enyɔ kɛ enyɔ ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ ho kɛbote misusuma ni yɔɔ mli lɛ mli, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ agbɛfaa ni bɛ naagbee, ni, amɛteŋ mɛi babaoo ateŋ lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ko ni hiɛ tsitsi wulu, tamɔ snoo gɔŋ ko ni yɔɔ kɔɔyɔɔ mli.





YITSƆƆ 2. Kapɛt-Baagi lɛ.

Mikɛ atade kome loo enyɔ wo mi-kapɛt-baagi momo lɛ mli, ni mikɛwo minine shishi, ni mije shishi kɛmiiya Cape Horn kɛ Pacific. Beni mishi maŋ kpakpa ni ji Manhatto momo lɛ mli lɛ, mishɛ New Bedford yɛ gbɛ ni sa nɔ. No mli lɛ, eji Hɔgbaa gbɛkɛ ko yɛ December mli. Minijiaŋjewui waa beni mikase akɛ paketi bibioo ni akɛyaa Nantucket lɛ efã gbɛ momo, ni gbɛ ko bɛ ni aaatsɔ nɔ ashɛ nakai he lɛ, kɛyashi Hɔɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ.

Beni oblahii kɛ oblayei babaoo ni miitao piŋmɔi kɛ toigbalamɔi ni jɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ mli baa lɛ kpaa New Bedford nɛɛ nɔŋŋ, kɛjɛ jɛmɛ koni amɛje amɛ gbɛfaa lɛ shishi lɛ, ekolɛ abaanyɛ akɛɛ hu akɛ mi, yɛ mɔ kome gbɛfaŋ lɛ, mibɛ nakai feemɔ he jwɛŋmɔ ko kwraa. Ejaakɛ mikpɛ miyiŋ akɛ mikɛ lɛlɛ kroko bɛ fe Nantucket lɛlɛ ko, ejaakɛ nɔ ko ni hi jogbaŋŋ, ni feɔ hoo yɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni kɔɔ nakai ŋshɔkpɔ momo ni ehe gbɛi waa lɛ he yɛ, ni enɛ ha miná miishɛɛ yɛ naakpɛɛ gbɛ nɔ. Kɛfata he lɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ New Bedford kɛ ehe ewo ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ nitsumɔ lɛ mli fiofio, ni eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ sane nɛɛ mli lɛ, Nantucket onukpa ohiafo lɛ efo esɛɛ babaoo bianɛ moŋ, shi Nantucket ji eshishijee maŋ wulu lɛ—Carthage nɛɛ Taya lɛ;—he ni klɛŋklɛŋ Amerika ŋshɔŋlooi ni egbo lɛ shwie shi yɛ. Nɛgbɛ kroko ji he kroko ni nakai aboriginal ŋshɔŋlooi, ni ji Red-Men lɛ, je kpo klɛŋklɛŋ yɛ lɛlɛi amli koni amɛyatiu Leviathan lɛ, ja Nantucket? Ni nɛgbɛ ji he ni nakai klɛŋklɛŋ lɛlɛ bibioo ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni akɛ tɛi ni akɛjɛ maŋsɛɛ aba lɛ fã ko eyi mli obɔ lɛ je kpo, ja Nantucket hu—koni sane lɛ yaa nɔ—koni ekɛshwie ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ anɔ, koni ena be mli ni amɛbɛŋkɛ bɔ ni sa ni amɛbaanyɛ amɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni jɛ bowsprit lɛ mli lɛ awo oshara mli?

Amrɔ nɛɛ ni miyɛ gbɛkɛ kome, gbi kome, kɛ gbɛkɛ kroko hu ni nyiɛɔ misɛɛ yɛ New Bedford, dani manyɛ mabote lɛlɛ lɛ mli kɛya lɛlɛdaamɔhe ni miyaa lɛ, ebafee nɔ ni haoɔ mi yɛ he ni esa akɛ maye nii yɛ ni mawɔ yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ. Eji gbɛkɛ ko ni yɔɔ ŋwanejee waa, daabi, gbɛkɛ ko ni mli ewo duŋ ni eyɛ awerɛho waa, ni edɔ waa ni miishɛɛ bɛ mli. Mileee mɔ ko mɔ ko yɛ jɛmɛ. Mikɛ grapnels ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ etswa mikotoku lɛ, ni mikɛ shika fioo ko pɛ ba,—No hewɔ lɛ, he fɛɛ he ni obaaya, Ishmael, mikɛɛ mihe, beni midamɔ gbɛjegbɛ ko ni mli ewo wu lɛ teŋgbɛ ni mikɛ mibaagi lɛ miiwo mitsitsi nɔ, ni mikɛ duŋ ni yɔɔ kooyigbɛ lɛ toɔ duŋ ni yɔɔ kooyigbɛ lɛ he kɛ—nilee ni yɔɔ bo lɛ baanyɛ aha ohe afee shoo kɛha bo . gbɛkɛ, misuɔlɔ Ishmael, kwɛmɔ ni obi jara lɛ, ni okafee nɔ ko pɔtɛɛ tsɔ.

Mikɛ gbɛjegbɛi ni ŋmɛɔ shi lɛ nyiɛ gbɛjegbɛi lɛ anɔ, ni miho okadi ni ji “The Crossed Harpoons” lɛ he—shi etamɔ nɔ ni ejara wa tsɔ ni eyɛ miishɛɛ yɛ jɛmɛ. Kɛfata he lɛ, kɛjɛ “Sword-Fish Inn” lɛ samfɛji tsuji ni kpɛɔ lɛ amli lɛ, la ni tsoɔ waa baa, ni etamɔ nɔ ni eha snoo kɛ ais ni eyi obɔ kɛ shĩa lɛ hiɛ lɛ mli edɔ, ejaakɛ he fɛɛ he kroko lɛ, ais ni edɔ lɛ shwie shi yɛ gbɛjegbɛ ni wa ni akɛ asphalt fee lɛ nɔ, ni mikɛ mihe wo mli, —shi moŋ lɛ, wɔ . projections, ejaakɛ kɛjɛ sɔɔmɔ ni wa, ni shwamɔ bɛ mli lɛ mli lɛ, mi-boots lɛ shishi yɛ shihilɛ ni yɔɔ awerɛho waa mli. Ejara wa tsɔ ni eyɛ miishɛɛ, misusu he ekoŋŋ, ni mimɛ fioo koni makwɛ hulutsoo ni mli kwɔ ni yɔɔ gbɛjegbɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ni minu glasei ni tswaa yɛ mli lɛ agbɛɛmɔi. Shi yaa nɔ, Ishmael, mikɛɛ naagbee akɛ; ani onuuu? nyɛtsia nyɛhe kɛjɛɔ shinaa lɛ hiɛ; o-boots ni akɛkpa lɛ miitsi gbɛ lɛ naa. No hewɔ lɛ, mitee. Agbɛnɛ, kɛtsɔ mifɔmɔ nɔ lɛ, mikɛ mihe woɔ gbɛjegbɛi ni kɛ mi yaa nu lɛ mli lɛ asɛɛ, ejaakɛ ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ, jɛmɛ ji hei ni jara waaa fe fɛɛ, kɛ jeee gbɔiatoohei ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ.

Gbɛjegbɛi ni yɔɔ awerɛho tamɔ nɛkɛ! blɔkii ni ji ediŋŋ, shi jeee tsũi, yɛ niji enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ nɔ, kɛ kane ko ni yɔɔ biɛ kɛ biɛ, tamɔ kane ko ni nyiɛɔ yɛ gbonyobu mli. Yɛ gbɛkɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa nɛɛ nɔ, yɛ otsi lɛ naagbee gbi lɛ nɔ lɛ, nakai maŋ lɛ fã ni ji ejwɛ lɛ bafee he ko ni mɛi bɛ jɛmɛ. Shi amrɔ nɛɛ mibashɛ kane ko ni mli edɔ ni miijɛ tsu ko ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ ni mli kwɔ mli, ni shinaa lɛ damɔ shi ni egbele ni fɔ̃ɔ mɔ nine lɛ he. Eyɛ hiɛ ni bɛ hiɛdɔɔ, tamɔ nɔ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ koni maŋbii lɛ kɛtsu nii; no hewɔ lɛ, beni miboteɔ mli lɛ, klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ ni mifee ji ni mikɛ sũ-adeka ko ni yɔɔ kpoku lɛ nɔ lɛ tswa. Ha! misusu akɛ, ha, beni eshwɛ fioo ni ŋmeii ni foɔ lɛ baagbe mi lɛ, ani nɛkɛ sũ nɛɛ jɛ nakai maŋ ni akpata hiɛ, Gomora lɛ mli? Shi “Harpoons ni Afolɔ He,” kɛ “Klante-Loo lɛ?”—enɛ, no lɛ esa akɛ efee “Tɔmɔ” lɛ he okadi. Shi kɛlɛ, miwo mihe nɔ ni minu gbee ko ni naa wa yɛ mli lɛ, mikɛ mihe wo mli ni migbele shinaa ni ji enyɔ, ni yɔɔ mli lɛ.

Etamɔ nɔ ni Black Parliament kpeteŋkpele lɛ ta Tophet. Hiɛi diji oha tsɔ amɛhe yɛ amɛ sɛɛkuu lɛ mli koni amɛkwɛ; ni sɛɛ mli lɛ, Doom Ŋwɛibɔfo diŋ ko miitswa wolo ko yɛ wiemɔ kpoku ko nɔ. Negro sɔlemɔ ji no; ni shiɛlɔ lɛ niŋmaa lɛ kɔɔ duŋ diŋŋ lɛ he, kɛ yaafo kɛ ŋmɔlɔ kɛ ŋmeii ni atswaa yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ he. Ha, Ishmael, mikɛ mihe wo mli, ni mikɛ mihe ku sɛɛ, Hiɛtserɛjiemɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho yɛ ‘Tɔmɔ lɛ’ okadi lɛ he!

Beni miyaa nɔ lɛ, naagbee lɛ mibashɛ kane ko ni mli edɔ waa ni kɛ lɛlɛdaamɔhei lɛ teŋ jɛkɛɛɛ lɛ he, ni minu gbee ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni miitswa yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli; ni beni ekwɛ ŋwɛi lɛ, ena okadi ko ni miihoso yɛ shinaa lɛ nɔ ni atɛŋ mfoniri yɛŋ ko yɛ nɔ, ni damɔ shi kɛha afua ni shwieɔ shi ni yɔɔ tɛ̃ɛ ni kɛlɛ fioo, kɛ wiemɔi nɛɛ ni yɔɔ shishi lɛ—“Spouter Inn:—Peter Coffin.”

Coffin?—Spouter?—Eji nɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei moŋ yɛ nakai tsakpaa pɔtɛɛ lɛ mli, misusu he Shi eji gbɛi ni afɔɔ namɔ yɛ Nantucket, amɛkɛɛ, ni misusuɔ akɛ nɛkɛ Peter ni yɔɔ biɛ nɛɛ ji mɔ ko ni fã kɛjɛ jɛmɛ. Beni kane lɛ mli ewo wu waa, ni he lɛ, yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, efee dioo bɔ ni sa, ni tso tsũ bibioo ni efite lɛ diɛŋtsɛ tamɔ nɔ ni ekolɛ ajie lɛ kɛjɛ kpokpaa ko ni shã lɛ mli fitemɔi lɛ amli kɛba biɛ, ni akɛni okadi ni shwieɔ shi lɛ yɛ ohia-he-nifeemɔ ko ni haa mɔ he jɔ̃ɔ ehe lɛ hewɔ lɛ, misusu akɛ biɛ ji he ni shika babaoo yɔɔ.

Eji he ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ—shia momo ko ni yɔɔ ŋwɛitsu nɔ, ni efã kome egbo tamɔ nɔ ni egbɔjɔ, ni ekɛ awerɛho efo ehe. Edamɔ koŋ ko ni mli ewo duŋ waa nɔ, he ni nakai ahum ni naa wa ni ji Euroclydon lɛ tee nɔ ebɔi gbee ni ehiii fe tsutsu lɛ yɛ Paulo ohiafo lɛ lɛlɛ ni etswa lɛ lɛ he lɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, Euroclydon ji zephyr ni ŋɔɔ waa kɛha mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ni yɔɔ shia mli, ni ekɛ enaji kã tsɔne ni akɛhooɔ nii lɛ nɔ ni ekɛmiitswa niyenii dioo kɛha saatso. Niŋmalɔ ko ni egbɔ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Kɛ́ akojo nakai kɔɔyɔɔ ni naa wa ni atsɛɔ lɛ Euroclydon lɛ—ni miyɛ enitsumɔi lɛ eko pɛ ni yɔɔ lɛ—ehaa srɔtofeemɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ baa, kɛji okwɛ lɛ kɛjɛ glase samflɛ mli he ni fɛ̃i lɛ fɛɛ yɔɔ kponɔgbɛ lɛ, loo kɛji okwɛ lɛ yɛ he ni fɛ̃i bɛ ni yɔɔ samflɛ lɛ mli lɛ, . ni wight Gbele lɛ pɛ ji glaze lɛ.” Eji anɔkwale akɛ, misusu he, beni kuku nɛɛ ba mijwɛŋmɔ mli lɛ—niŋmaa diŋ momo, osusuɔ nii ahe jogbaŋŋ. Hɛɛ, nɛkɛ hiŋmɛii nɛɛ ji samfɛji, ni migbɔmɔtso nɛɛ ji shia lɛ. Mɛɛ mɔbɔnalɛ eji akɛ amɛkpaaa ŋmeii kɛ ŋmeii lɛ anɔ moŋ, ni amɛkɛ ŋmeii fioo komɛi wo mli yɛ biɛ kɛ biɛ. Shi etsɛ tsɔ ni abaanyɛ afee hiɛyaa ko bianɛ. Jeŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ egbe naa; copestone lɛ yɛ nɔ, ni ajie chips lɛ afii akpekpei kome ni eho nɛ. Lazaro ohiafo ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ, ni ekɛ enyanyɔŋ miitswa gbɛjegbɛ tɛ lɛ he kɛha etsitsi, ni ekɛ etsitsii ni ekpokpo lɛ miihoso etsitsii ni ekpɔtɔ lɛ, ebaanyɛ ekɛ tsɔji ni efite lɛ atswa etoi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ, ni ekɛ ŋmãa ŋmɔtɔ awo enaa, ni kɛlɛ no haŋ Euroclydon ni ahum miitswa waa lɛ akabote mli. Euroklidon! Dives onukpa lɛ kɛɛ, yɛ e-silki tsuru ni ekɛwoɔ mli lɛ mli—(eyɛ eko ni tsuru fe no yɛ no sɛɛ) pooh, pooh! Mɛɛ fɛ̃i gbɛkɛ kpakpa po eji nɛkɛ; bɔ ni Orion kpɛɔ ehaa; mɛɛ kooyigbɛ kanei po nɛ! Ha amɛwie amɛ bokagbɛ hulutsoo be mli kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔi ni ji naanɔ nibii ni ato he gbɛjianɔ lɛ ahe; ha mi hegbɛ akɛ mikɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ mi-coals afee mi diɛŋtsɛ mi-summer.

Shi mɛni ji Lazaro susumɔ? Ani ebaanyɛ ekɛ eniji bluu lɛ adɔ kɛtsɔ amɛ ni ekɛbaawo kooyigbɛ kanei wuji lɛ anɔ lɛ nɔ? Ani Lazaro sumɔɔɔ ni ehiɔ Sumatra moŋ fe biɛ? Ani ekɛ lɛ baafee shɔŋŋ moŋ ni ekɛ lɛ aka shi yɛ equator lɛ he lɛ; hɛɛ, nyɛ nyɔŋmɔi lɛ! shi kɛya la bu lɛ diɛŋtsɛ mli, koni ekɛtsi fɛ̃i nɛɛ naa?

Amrɔ nɛɛ, akɛ Lazaro baakã jɛmɛ ni ekɛ ehe ewo gbɛjegbɛ lɛ he tɛ ni yɔɔ Dives shinaa lɛ hiɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, enɛ ji naakpɛɛ nii fe ni akɛ ais gɔŋ ko aaakpɛtɛ Molucca gɔji lɛ eko he. Shi Dives diɛŋtsɛ, lɛ hu ehiɔ shi tamɔ Czar yɛ ais maŋtsɛwe ko ni akɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni mli edɔ fee lɛ mli, ni akɛni eji kuu ko ni yeɔ amɛhe nɔ jogbaŋŋ lɛ sɛinɔtalɔ hewɔ lɛ, enuɔ gbekɛbii ni bɛ fɔlɔi lɛ yaafo ni mli edɔ lɛ pɛ.

Shi nɛkɛ blubbering nɛɛ bɛ dɔŋŋ bianɛ, wɔmiiya a-whaling, ni nakai babaoo yɛ ni baaba lolo. Nyɛhaa wɔfolɔ ais lɛ kɛjɛ wɔnaji ni edɔ lɛ anɔ, ni wɔkwɛ he ko ni nɛkɛ “Spouter” nɛɛ baanyɛ afee.





YITSƆƆ 3. Spouter-Inn lɛ.

Beni oboteɔ nakai Spouter-Inn ni yɔɔ ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ona ohe yɛ ŋwɛitsu ko ni mli kwɔ, ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ, ni yɔɔ ŋwɛitsu ni yɔɔ ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ lɛ mli, ni ekaiɔ ŋwɛitsu lɛ mli ŋwɛitsu lɛ eko ni abu lɛ fɔ lɛ. Yɛ fa kome lɛ, akɛ mu mfoniri agbo ko ni akɛ la ewo mli jogbaŋŋ, ni efite yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ, ni yɛ kanei ni kɛ amɛhe kpaaa gbee ni okwɛɔ lɛ mli lɛ, eji nikasemɔ ni akɛ hiɛdɔɔ kɛ saramɔi ni ato he gbɛjianɔ ni yaa nɔ, kɛ akutsoŋbii lɛ abibii amɛ saji jogbaŋŋ pɛ ji nɔ ni ha obaanyɛ oshɛ yiŋtoo ko he. Nibii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni anyɛɛɛ abu he akɔntaa, ni shishijee lɛ, eshwɛ fioo ni obaasusu akɛ oblanyo fioo ko ni miitao gbɛhe ni nɔ kwɔ, yɛ New England hags lɛ abeaŋ lɛ, ebɔ mɔdɛŋ ni etsɔɔ basabasafeemɔ ni akɛ ŋmiŋmi fee lɛ mli. Shi kɛtsɔ susumɔ babaoo kɛ hiɛdɔɔ nɔ, kɛ susumɔi ni afɔɔ feemɔ shii abɔ, kɛ titri lɛ kɛtsɔ samflɛ bibioo ni okɛshwieɔ shinaa lɛ sɛɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, naagbee lɛ obaamu sane naa akɛ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni susumɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ji lɛ, ekolɛ ejeee nɔ ni esaaa kwraa.

Shi nɔ ni gba onaa fe fɛɛ ni eha oyiŋ futu bo ji nɔ ko kakadaŋŋ, ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, ni ji ediŋŋ ni miiho yɛ mfoniri lɛ teŋgbɛ yɛ bluu, ni mli ewo duŋ, ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ etɛ ni hoɔ yɛ ŋmɔtɔ ko ni bɛ gbɛi mli lɛ nɔ. Mfoniri ni mli ewo muji, ni mli ewo muji, ni mli ewo muji diɛŋtsɛ, ni fa bɔ ni sa ni baaha nuu ko ni he efee shoo lɛ ajwɛŋmɔ aje nɔ. Shi ani nɔ ko ni bɛ naagbee, ni ashɛ he fã, ni anyɛɛɛ asusu he yɛ he ni ha okɛ ohe wo mli jogbaŋŋ, kɛyashi be mli ni okɛ ohe kã kitã kɛjɛ otsui mli koni otao nɔ ni nakai naakpɛɛ mfoniri lɛ tsɔɔ. Be fɛɛ be lɛ, susumɔ ko ni mli tse, shi, shwane, lakamɔ susumɔ ko baaha okɛ ohe awo mli.—Eji Ŋshɔ Diŋ lɛ yɛ ahum ni tswaa shwane teŋ mli.—Eji shishijee nibii ejwɛ lɛ ateŋ ta ni jeee adebɔɔ naa nɔ.—Eji heath ni etswa.—Eji Hyperborean fɛ̃i be mli shihilɛ ko ni kɔɔ Time-up the break. Shi naagbee lɛ, nɛkɛ nibii ni yɔɔ fɛo nɛɛ fɛɛ ha nakai nɔ ko ni yɔɔ mfoniri lɛ teŋ lɛ ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ. No be ko lɛ, ana, ni nɔ ni eshwɛ lɛ fɛɛ yɛ faŋŋ. Shi kpa; ani ekɛ loo wulu ko kpaaa gbee fioo? leviatan kpeteŋkpele lɛ diɛŋtsɛ po?

Yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, bɔ ni nitɛŋmɔ nitsulɔ lɛ fee lɛ tamɔ nɛkɛ: mi diɛŋtsɛ mi naagbee susumɔ, ni fã ko damɔ mɛi ni edara babaoo ni mikɛ amɛ gba sane yɛ sane lɛ he lɛ ajwɛŋmɔi ni abua naa lɛ nɔ. Mfoniri lɛ damɔ shi kɛha Cape-Horner ko ni yɔɔ ahum wulu ko mli; lɛlɛ ni ato shishi fã ni miiho yɛ jɛmɛ ni eshishitoo mlai etɛ ni etse lɛ pɛ ji nɔ ni anaa lɛ; ni ŋshɔŋloo ko ni mli efu waa, ni eto eyiŋ akɛ ebaafo lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, miifee nifeemɔ kpeteŋkpele ni ji ehe ni ekɛbaakpɛtɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yitsei etɛ lɛ ahe lɛ mli.

Akɛ wɔŋjalɔi akuu kɛ akpɔlɔi ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni ato he gbɛjianɔ lɛ fĩ gbogbo ni kɛ nɛkɛ shinaa nɛɛ kpãa gbee lɛ he fɛɛ. Akɛ ŋmeii ni kpɛɔ tamɔ ŋmeii ni akɛ ŋmei fee lɛ ewo ekomɛi amli ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ; akɛ adesa yitsɔi ni ebɔle mɛi krokomɛi ahe; ni eko tamɔ ŋmei, ni ehiɛ nine agbo ko ni shwieɔ shi tamɔ fã ni tsɔne ni akɛfoɔ jwɛi ni hiɛ nine kakadaŋŋ afeɔ yɛ jwɛi hee ni afo lɛ mli lɛ. Okpokpo beni okwɛɔ lɛ, ni osusu nɔ ni ji gbɔmɔyelɔ kɛ kooloo ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa ni baanyɛ aya gbele-kpamɔ mli kɛ tsɔne ni akɛtswaa nibii ni yɔɔ gbeyei tamɔ nɛkɛ lɛ he. Nɔ ni akɛfutu enɛɛmɛi ji ŋshɔŋlooi awuiyeli akpɔlɔi momoi ni efite kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛtswaa ŋshɔŋlooi ni fɛɛ ekumɔ ni efite lɛ. Ekomɛi ji tawuu nii ni ato he gbɛjianɔ. Afii nyɔŋmai enumɔ ni eho nɛ lɛ, Nathan Swain gbe ŋshɔŋlooi nyɔŋma kɛ enumɔ yɛ hulushitee kɛ hulushinyɔɔ teŋ. Ni nakai harpoon lɛ—ni tamɔ corkscrew amrɔ nɛɛ—ashɛrɛ ashwie Javan ŋshɔi lɛ amli, ni ŋshɔŋloo ko jo foi kɛtee, ni afii komɛi asɛɛ lɛ egbe lɛ yɛ Blanco Ŋshɔ lɛ naa. Shishijee dade lɛ bote mli yɛ tsitsi lɛ he, ni, tamɔ tsɔne ni bɛ hejɔɔmɔ ni hiɔ gbɔmɔ gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli lɛ, enyiɛ ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai ejwɛ muu fɛɛ, ni naagbee lɛ ana akɛ ebote tsitsi lɛ mli.

Kɛ́ ofo nɛkɛ gbɛ ni mli ewo duŋ nɛɛ nɔ, ni otsɔ gbɛ ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ lɛ nɔ—otsɔ nɔ ni esa akɛ eji lasu tsu wulu ko ni yɔɔ teŋgbɛ ni lasu tsu lɛ ebɔle he fɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ—oboteɔ maŋ tsu lɛ mli. He ko ni mli ewo duŋ lolo ji biɛ, ni tsei ni tsii ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ lɛ yɔɔ ŋwɛi, kɛ tsei momoi ni egbɔjɔ yɛ shishi, ni eshwɛ fioo ni obaasusu akɛ onyiɛ lɛlɛ momo ko mli tsɔne kudɔmɔ hei lɛ anɔ, titri lɛ yɛ gbɛkɛ ni bɔlɔɔ waa tamɔ nɛkɛ mli, beni nɛkɛ adeka momo ni yɔɔ koŋ nɔ nɛɛ hosoɔ kɛ mlifu waa lɛ. Okpɔlɔ kakadaŋŋ ko ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ ni tamɔ shɛlɛf ni akɛ glase kotokui ni egbɛ eshwa ehà nɔ lɛ damɔ efã kome, ni sũ nibii ni anaaa ni abua naa kɛjɛɔ je lɛŋ hei ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ nɛɛ eyi mli obɔ. Kɛjɛ tsu lɛ mli he kroko lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ko ni tamɔ duŋ damɔ shi—ni ji dãatɔɔhe lɛ—ni ji mɔdɛŋ ni abɔɔ yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ yɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko yitso he. Bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ tsitsi wu wulu ni ebɔle ehe lɛ damɔ jɛmɛ, ni elɛɛ waa, ni eshwɛ fioo ni tsɔne kudɔlɔ baanyɛ akudɔ tsɔne yɛ eshishi. Yɛ mli lɛ, shɛlɛf ni efite, ni akɛ dekantɛ momoi, tɔi, flaskii ebɔle he; ni yɛ nakai hiɛkpatamɔ nɔ oya lɛ tsitsii lɛ amli, tamɔ Yona kroko ni alomɔ lɛ (ni amɛtsɛɔ lɛ gbɛi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ lɛ), nuumo fioo ko ni egbo, ni, yɛ amɛshika hewɔ lɛ, ekɛ shika babaoo hɔ̃ɔ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ deliriums kɛ gbele lɛ miitsu nii.

Nɔ ni yɔɔ nyaŋemɔ ji tsɛnsii ni ekɛ e-poison lɛ woɔ mli lɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ anɔkwa tsilindai ni bɛ—mli moŋ, shi glasei ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ tsɔ lakamɔ gbɛ nɔ amɛba shi kɛtee shishigbɛ ni shishiuɔ mɔ. Meridians ni kɛ amɛhe kpãa gbee ni akɛwo glase lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ, ebɔle nɛkɛ najiaŋdamɔlɔi nɛɛ ahe. Okɛ okadi nɛɛ awo mli, ni oshika lɛ ji shika bibioo kome pɛ; kɛfata enɛ he lɛ, shika bibioo ko fata he; kɛ nakai nɔŋŋ kɛyashi glase ni eyi obɔ lɛ nɔ—Cape Horn susumɔ lɛ, ni obaanyɛ okɛ shilingi kome ashɛ shi.

Beni mibote jɛmɛ lɛ mina ŋshɔ hiɛ oblahii kɛ oblayei komɛi ni ebua amɛhe naa yɛ okpɔlɔ ko he, ni amɛmiikwɛ ŋshɔ mli ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ amli kɛtsɔ kane bibioo ko nɔ. Mitao shikpɔŋtsɛ lɛ, ni mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ miisumɔ ni aha mi tsu ko, ni minine shɛ hetoo nɔ akɛ eshia lɛ eyi obɔ—jeee saatso ni mɔ ko mɔ ko bɛ nɔ. “Shi avast,” ekɛfata he, ni etswa ehiɛ, “obɛ shitee-kɛ-woo ko akɛ okɛ harpooneer blanket baaja, aloo jeee nakai? Misusuɔ akɛ oyaa a-whalin’, no hewɔ lɛ ebaahi akɛ okɛ nakai nɔ lɛ baatsɔ ohe.”

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ misumɔɔɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ akɛ mikɛ mɛi enyɔ wɔɔ saatso kome nɔ; akɛ kɛji mifee nakai pɛŋ lɛ, ebaadamɔ mɔ ni baanyɛ afee harpooneer lɛ nɔ, ni akɛ kɛji akɛ lɛ (shikpɔŋtsɛ lɛ) bɛ he kroko diɛŋtsɛ kɛha mi, ni harpooneer lɛ jeee mɔ ko ni teɔ shi ewoɔ lɛ, mɛni hewɔ moŋ ni mafolɔ maŋ kroko mli kɛya shɔŋŋ yɛ gbɛkɛ ni mli wa waa mli lɛ, maŋmɛ mitsui shi yɛ blanket fɛɛ nuu ni sa lɛ fã he’.

"Misusu nakai. Ehi; ta shi. Gbi gbɛkɛ niyenii?—osumɔɔ gbɛkɛ niyenii? Gbi gbɛkɛ niyenii lɛ baafee klalo tɛ̃ɛ."

Mitara tso sɛi momo ko nɔ, ni akpɛ́ he fɛɛ tamɔ sɛi ni yɔɔ Batiri lɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ. Yɛ naagbee lɛ, tar ko ni miihoso lɛ kɛ e-jack-knife lɛ miiwula lɛ lolo, ni ekoto shi ni ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ miitsu nii kɛmiijɛ he ni kã enaji ateŋ lɛ. Eka enine ekwɛ yɛ lɛlɛ ko ni ŋshɔ lɛ eyi mli obɔ lɛ shishi, shi eyaaa hiɛ tsɔ, misusu he.

Naagbee lɛ, atsɛ wɔteŋ mɛi aaafee ejwɛ loo enumɔ koni wɔbaye nii yɛ tsu ko ni bɛŋkɛ wɔ lɛ mli. Edɔ tamɔ Iceland—la bɛ kwraa—shikpɔŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ enyɛŋ ehé. Nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ja tallow kanei enyɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho, ni eko fɛɛ eko yɛ wolo ni tsɔɔ mli lɛ mli. Wɔmiisumɔ waa ni wɔkɛ wɔ ŋmeii jakɛtii lɛ awo wɔdaaŋ, ni wɔkɛ wɔ niji ni edɔ fã lɛ amɔ tii ni mli edɔ lɛ kɔɔpoi lɛ ahe. Shi tsɔne he nyɔmɔwoo lɛ ji nɔ ni jara wa fe fɛɛ—jeee loo kɛ ŋmɔtɔ pɛ, shi moŋ dumplings; ŋwɛi kpakpai! dumplings kɛha gbɛkɛ niyenii! Obalanyo ko ni wo adeka mli atade eŋɔli lɛ, wie ehe etsɔɔ nɛkɛ dumplings nɛɛ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa nɔ.

Shiatsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Mibi nuu, obaala sartainty ni egbo lɛ lamɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ.”

Mikɛ gbee bibioo kɛɛ akɛ: “Shikpɔŋtsɛ, jeee harpooneer lɛ ji lɛ?”

“Oh, daabi,” ekɛɛ, ni efee tamɔ mɔ ko ni yɔɔ daimonio ŋmɔlɔ, “harpooneer lɛ ji nuu ko ni he yɔɔ diŋ. Eyeee dumplings kɔkɔɔkɔ, eyeee—eyeee nɔ ko nɔ ko fe tsina loo, ni esumɔɔ 'em rare.”

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Abonsam ji mɔ ni feɔ nakai. Nɛgbɛ nakai harpooneer lɛ yɔɔ? Ani eyɛ biɛ?”

Hetoo lɛ ji akɛ: “Ebaaba biɛ etsɛɛɛ tsɔ.”

Minyɛɛɛ mafee nɔ ko, shi mibɔi yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ yɛ nɛkɛ “hewolonɔ su diŋ” harpooneer nɛɛ he. Bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, mikpɛ miyiŋ akɛ kɛji eba akɛ esa akɛ wɔwɔ ekome lɛ, esa akɛ ejie atadei ni ekɛwɔ saatso lɛ nɔ dani mikɛ mihe awo mli.

Beni aye gbɛkɛ niyenii lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, kuu lɛ ku esɛɛ kɛtee dãatɔɔhe lɛ, beni, akɛni mileee nɔ kroko ni mafee mihe hewɔ lɛ, mikpɛ miyiŋ akɛ mikɛ gbɛkɛ ni eshwɛ lɛ baatsu nii akɛ mɔ ni kwɛɔ nɔ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ anu basabasafeemɔ gbɛɛmɔ ko ni bɛ. Beni shikpɔŋtsɛ lɛ jeɔ shishi lɛ, ebolɔ akɛ, "Grampus lɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ nɛ. Mibɔ lɛ amaniɛ yɛ gbɛfaa mli ŋmɛnɛ leebi; afii etɛ gbɛfaa, kɛ lɛlɛ ni eyi obɔ. Hurrah, gbekɛbii hii; agbɛnɛ wɔbaaná adafitswaa hee kɛjɛ Feegees lɛ adɛŋ."

Anu ŋshɔ mli atadei ni akɛnyiɛɔ shi lɛ he yɛ shinaa lɛ naa; agbele shinaa lɛ naa, ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi akuu ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni fa bɔ ni sa ba. Amɛwo amɛ watsi atadei ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni amɛkɛ miishɛjemɔ atadei ni akɛ kooloo yitsɔi fee lɛ ewo amɛyitsei amli, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛhe ewo ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni amɛhe efee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni amɛtsitsii lɛ amli eyi kɛ ais, ni amɛtamɔ nɔ ni ŋmeii ni jɛ Labrador lɛ miitswa amɛhe. No mli lɛ, amɛkpeleke shi kɛjɛ amɛlɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni enɛ ji klɛŋklɛŋ shia ni amɛbote mli. No hewɔ lɛ, ebɛ naakpɛɛ akɛ amɛfee ŋshɔŋloo lɛ daaŋ tɛ̃ɛ—ni ji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ—beni etsɛɛɛ ni Yona onukpa bibioo ni egbɔjɔ, ni tsuɔ nii yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ, shwie amɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni ebɔle amɛhe lɛ. Mɔ kome wie huhuuhu yɛ fɛ̃i gbonyo ko ni yɔɔ eyitsoŋ lɛ he, ni Yona kɛ tsofa ko ni tamɔ ŋmei ni ji gin kɛ molasses futu mli, ni ekã kitã akɛ eji tsofa ni hi jogbaŋŋ kɛha fɛ̃i kɛ tsɛŋemɔ helai fɛɛ, ekɔɔɔ he eko be ni ekɛhi shi, loo kɛji akɛ emɔ lɛ yɛ Labraceis ŋshɔ lɛ naa, loo yɛ shikpɔŋ ko nɔ.

Etsɛɛɛ ni dãa lɛ bote amɛyitsei amli, taakɛ eji yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔkei ni jɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ eba shi ehee lɛ po ahe lɛ, ni amɛbɔi amɛhe ŋmɔlɔ waa.

Shi kɛlɛ, mina akɛ amɛteŋ mɔ kome tsi ehe kɛjɛ he fioo, ni eyɛ mli akɛ etamɔ nɔ ni eesumɔ ni ekɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehiɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ akafite enanemɛi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ mli lɛ ahiɛtserɛjiemɔ lɛ moŋ, shi yɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, ekpa gbee babaoo tamɔ mɛi krokomɛi lɛ. Nuu nɛɛ ná mihe miishɛɛ amrɔ nɔŋŋ; ni akɛni ŋshɔ nyɔŋmɔi lɛ eto akɛ etsɛŋ ni ebaatsɔ mihefatalɔ yɛ lɛlɛ mli (eyɛ mli akɛ eji mihefatalɔ ni mikɛwɔɔ moŋ, yɛ sanegbaa nɛɛ he hewɔ lɛ), mikɛ mihe baawo ehe shishitsɔɔmɔ fioo ko mli yɛ biɛ. Edamɔ shi ni ekɛlɛ ji ninetalɔi ekpaa, ni eyɛ tsitsii ni nɔ kwɔlɔ, kɛ adeka ni tamɔ adeka-dam. Minako brawn ni tamɔ nɛkɛ yɛ nuu ko mli tsɔ. Ehiɛ efee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ waa ni eshã, ni eha enyanyɔji yɛŋ lɛ miikpɛ yɛ srɔtofeemɔ ni yɔɔ mli lɛ hewɔ; yɛ be mli ni ehiŋmɛii amli ŋmɔlɔ ni mli kwɔ lɛ mli lɛ, kaimɔi komɛi ni tamɔ nɔ ni haaa ena miishɛɛ babaoo lɛ miiho. Egbee lɛ tswa adafi amrɔ nɔŋŋ akɛ eji Anaigbɛnyo, ni kɛjɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ fɛo ha lɛ mli lɛ, misusu akɛ ekolɛ ebaafee nakai gɔŋkɔlɔi kakadaji ni jɛ Alleghanian Ridge ni yɔɔ Virginia lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome. Beni enanemɛi lɛ ahiɛtserɛjiemɔ lɛ tee hiɛ waa lɛ, nuu nɛɛ shwie shi ni mɔ ko mɔ ko enaaa lɛ, ni minaaa lɛ dɔŋŋ kɛyashi ebatsɔ minaanyo yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ. Shi kɛlɛ, yɛ minitii fioo komɛi asɛɛ lɛ, enanemɛi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ ahiɛ kpa enɔ, ni akɛni etamɔ nɔ ni yɛ yiŋtoo ko hewɔ lɛ, eji mɔ ko ni amɛsumɔɔ waa hewɔ lɛ, amɛbolɔ waa akɛ “Bulkington! Bulkington! nɛgbɛ Bulkington yɔɔ?” ni ekɛ oyaiyeli je kpo kɛjɛ shia lɛ mli koni eyatiu lɛ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ eshɛ aaafee ŋmɛlɛtswai nɛɛhu, ni etamɔ nɔ ni tsu lɛ mli efee dioo yɛ nɛkɛ shwɛmɔi nɛɛ asɛɛ, ni mibɔi mihe yijiemɔ yɛ gbɛjianɔtoo bibioo ko ni ba mijwɛŋmɔ mli dani ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi lɛ abote mli lɛ hewɔ.

Nuu ko bɛ ni sumɔɔ ni ekɛ mɛi enyɔ awɔ saatso kome nɔ. Yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, obaasumɔ ni okɛ bo diɛŋtsɛ onyɛmi awɔ moŋ. Mileee bɔ ni eji, shi mɛi sumɔɔ ni amɛfee amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe kɛji amɛmiiwɔ. Ni kɛ eba akɛ okɛ gbɔ ko ni oleee lɛ baawɔ, yɛ gbɔiatoohe ko ni oleee, yɛ maŋ ko ni oleee mli, ni nakai gbɔ lɛ ji mɔ ko ni tswaa harpoon lɛ, no lɛ oshitee-kɛ-woo lɛ yaa hiɛ babaoo kɛya naanɔ. Ni shikpɔŋ nɔ yiŋtoo ko hu bɛ ni baaha mi akɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ mawɔ mɛi enyɔ yɛ saatso kome nɔ, fe mɔ fɛɛ mɔ; ejaakɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ewɔɔɔ saatso kome nɔ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ dɔŋŋ, tamɔ bɔ ni Maŋtsɛmɛi ni bɛ wu lɛ wɔɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ. Hiɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ! Eyɛ su ni tsuɔ, ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni ekɛ shwɛmɔi wuji ni tamɔ ediŋŋ ekpɛtɛ he yɛ biɛ kɛ biɛ. Hɛɛ, taakɛ misusu lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ, eji mɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa; ekɛ ehe ewo nɔmɔ mli, afo lɛ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɔ, ni naa lɛ, kɛjɛ opireshɛnfeelɔ lɛ ŋɔɔ kɛkɛ. Shi yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, ekɛ ehe wo mli ni etsɔ ehiɛ kɛtee kane lɛ he, ni mina faŋŋ akɛ amɛnyɛŋ amɛfee sticking-plasters kwraa, nakai shwɛmɔi diji ni yɔɔ etsitsi nɔ lɛ. Amɛji muji ko loo nɔ kroko. Shishijee lɛ mileee nɔ ni mafee yɛ enɛ he; shi etsɛɛɛ ni anɔkwale lɛ he susumɔ ko ba mijwɛŋmɔ mli. Mikai blɔfonyo ko sane ko—ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi hu—ni beni egbee gbɔmɔyelɔi lɛ ateŋ lɛ, amɛfee lɛ tattoo lɛ he. Mimu sane naa akɛ nɛkɛ harpooneer nɛɛ, yɛ egbɛfaa ni yaa shɔŋŋ lɛ mli lɛ, ekolɛ ekɛ shihilɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kpe. Ni mɛni ji no, misusu he, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ! E kponɔgbɛ pɛ ji lɛ; nuu baanyɛ aye anɔkwa yɛ hewolonɔ su fɛɛ hewolonɔ su mli. Shi no sɛɛ lɛ, mɛni ji nɔ ni esa akɛ afee yɛ ehewolonɔ su ni jeee shikpɔŋ nɔ nɔ lɛ he, nakai fã lɛ, mitsɔɔ, ni ekã shi ebɔle ehe, ni ebɛ ehe kwraa kɛjɛ shwɛmɔi ni akɛwoɔ mɔ he lɛ ahe. Kɛ aaawie he lɛ, ekolɛ jeee nɔ ko nɔ ko fe atade kpakpa ni akɛwoɔ hulutsoo be mli lɛ; shi minuko akɛ hulu ni tsoɔ waa lɛ eha nuu blɔfo ko etsɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni tsuɔ tamɔ ŋmɔtɔ. Shi kɛlɛ, miyako South Seas lɛ pɛŋ; ni ekolɛ hulu ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ ji nɔ ni ha nɛkɛ hewalɛi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɛɛ ba hewolo lɛ nɔ. Amrɔ nɛɛ, beni susumɔi nɛɛ fɛɛ hoɔ kɛtsɔɔ mimli tamɔ sarawagbamɔ lɛ, nɛkɛ harpooneer nɛɛ eyɔseee mi kwraa. Shi, beni ekɛ naagba ko kpe beni egbele ebaagi lɛ naa lɛ, ebɔi mli ŋmɔlɔ, ni amrɔ nɔŋŋ ejie tomahawk henɔ ko, kɛ seal hewolo kotoku ko ni yitsɔi yɔɔ nɔ lɛ. Beni ekɛ enɛɛmɛi fɔ adeka momo ni yɔɔ tsu lɛ teŋgbɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, no sɛɛ lɛ eŋɔ New Zealand yitso lɛ—ni ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa—ni ekɛwo kotoku lɛ mli. Agbɛnɛ ejie etsitsi—beaver tsitsi hee—beni mibɛŋkɛ ni mikɛ naakpɛɛ hee miitswa lala lɛ. Yitsɔi ko bɛ eyitso nɔ—kɛ hoo lɛ, eko bɛ ni awieɔ he—nɔ ko bɛ ja yitsɔi bibioo ko ni akɛkpɛtɛ ehiɛ he. Agbɛnɛ eyitso ni etsɔ ŋmɔtɔ lɛ miitao je lɛŋ fɛɛ tamɔ yitso ni etsɔ ŋmɔtɔ. Eji gbɔ lɛ damɔɔɔ mi kɛ shinaa lɛ teŋ kulɛ, mikɛ bolt baaje mli oya fe bei fɛɛ ni mikɛ bolt tswa gbɛkɛ niyenii.

Taakɛ eji lɛ po lɛ, misusu nɔ ko he akɛ mafo kɛtsɔ samflɛ mli, shi eji tsũ lɛ yiteŋ ni ji enyɔ lɛ sɛɛ. Mijeee gbeyeishelɔ, shi nɔ ni esa akɛ mafee yɛ nɛkɛ yitsoŋwalɔ ni hiɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ nɛɛ he kwraa fe mishishinumɔ. Nilee ni bɛ ji gbeyeishemɔ fɔlɔ, ni akɛni mibɛ nɔ ko kwraa ni miyiŋ efutu mi yɛ gbɔ lɛ he hewɔ lɛ, mijajeɔ akɛ amrɔ nɛɛ misheɔ lɛ gbeyei tamɔ nɔ ni abonsa lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ji mɔ ni bote mitsu lɛ mli yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ gbɛkɛ mli lɛ. Yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, mishe lɛ gbeyei waa aahu akɛ mijeee shwɛmɔ ni fa bɔ ni sa yɛ nakai beaŋ pɛ ni mikɛbaawie, ni mabi hetoo ni haa miishɛɛ yɛ nɔ ni tamɔ nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ mli yɛ emli lɛ he.

Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, etsa nitsumɔ ni ji atadei ni ejieɔ lɛ nɔ, ni naagbee lɛ ejie etsitsi kɛ eniji kpo. Taakɛ mihiɔ shi lɛ, akɛ shwɛmɔi ni tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ ehiɛ lɛ nɔŋŋ fee ehe fãi ni aha nɔ nɛɛ; esɛɛ hu, ebɔle ŋwɛi ŋtayaai ni yɔɔ duŋ lɛ nɔŋŋ he fɛɛ; etamɔ nɔ ni ebote Afii Nyɔŋmai Etɛ Ta ko mli, ni ejo foi kɛjɛ mli kɛkɛ kɛ atade ni akɛ plasta ekpɛtɛ he. Nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, akadi enaji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni ŋmeii ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ akuu ko miiho kɛmiiya tsei bibii lɛ akutsei lɛ anɔ. Agbɛnɛ efee faŋŋ kwraa akɛ esa akɛ efee kooloo ko ni yɔɔ nyaŋemɔ loo mɔ kroko ni akɛwo lɛlɛ mli yɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni yɔɔ Anaigbɛ Ŋshɔi lɛ amli lɛ mli, ni no hewɔ lɛ eshɛ Kristofoi amaŋ nɛɛ mli. Mikpokpo beni misusu he lɛ. Yitsei ahe jarayelɔ hu—ekolɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enyɛmimɛi ayitsei. Ekolɛ ebaasumɔ mi—ŋwɛi! kwɛmɔ nakai tomahawk lɛ!

Shi be ko bɛ ni akɛbaakpokpo, ejaakɛ agbɛnɛ kooloo lɛ tee nɔ efee nɔ ko ni gbala mijwɛŋmɔ kwraa, ni eha miná nɔmimaa akɛ esa akɛ efee wɔŋjalɔ diɛŋtsɛ. Beni eya e-grego, loo wrapall, loo dreadnaught ni tsii, ni ekɛwo sɛi ko nɔ tsutsu lɛ, ekɛ ehe wo kotokui lɛ amli, ni ekɛ mfoniri bibioo ko ni efite ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ esɛɛ, ni eyɛ Congo abifao ni eye gbii etɛ lɛ su pɛpɛɛpɛ kpo yɛ be kakadaŋŋ mli. Beni mikaiɔ yitso ni awo lɛ tsofa lɛ, shishijee lɛ eshwɛ fioo ni masusu akɛ nɛkɛ manikin diŋ nɛɛ ji abifao diɛŋtsɛ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ yɛ gbɛ ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ nɔ. Shi beni mina akɛ ejeee limber kwraa, ni ekpɛɔ jogbaŋŋ tamɔ ebony ni akpɛ́ lɛ, mimu sane naa akɛ esa akɛ efee nɔ ko nɔ ko fe tso amaga, ni ejie ehe kpo diɛŋtsɛ. Ejaakɛ amrɔ nɛɛ, kooloo lɛ yaa la-he ni nɔ ko bɛ mli lɛ, ni ejieɔ la-tso ni akɛ wolo ewo mli lɛ, ni ekɛ amaga bibioo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ nɛɛ, tamɔ tenpin, toɔ andirons lɛ ateŋ. Lasu tsu lɛ mli tsɔnei lɛ kɛ brikii fɛɛ ni yɔɔ mli lɛ mli ewo la waa, no hewɔ lɛ misusu akɛ nɛkɛ la-he nɛɛ fee shĩa bibioo loo sɔlemɔtsu ni sa jogbaŋŋ kɛha e-Congo amaga lɛ.

Agbɛnɛ mikɛ mihiŋmɛii kwɛ amaga ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ fã lɛ nɔ waa, ni minuɔ he shi mihe ejɔɔɔ mi yɛ nakai beaŋ—koni mana nɔ ni baaba yɛ sɛɛ mli. Klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ, ejieɔ ŋmeii ni falɛ aaafee shii enyɔ kɛjɛɔ e-grego kotoku lɛ mli, ni ekɛtoɔ amaga lɛ hiɛ jogbaŋŋ; no sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ lɛlɛ mli biskit fioo ko fɔ nɔ ni ekɛ la ni jɛɔ kane lɛ mli lɛ wo nɔ, ni ekɛ la ni akɛfolɔ mli lɛ shã afɔleshaa la. Amrɔ nɛɛ, beni ekɛ oyaiyeli shwie la lɛ mli babaoo sɛɛ, ni ekɛ eniji lɛ jie oya lolo (ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛshã amɛ waa lɛ), naagbee lɛ eye omanye ni ejie biskit lɛ; kɛkɛ ni ekɛ la kɛ sũ lɛ shwie shi fioo, ni ekɛ bulɛ ha negro bibioo lɛ. Shi etamɔ nɔ ni abonsam bibioo lɛ sumɔɔɔ tsɔne ni mli egbi tamɔ nɛkɛ kwraa; etsiii enaabu kɔkɔɔkɔ. Nɔ ni fata nɛkɛ nifeemɔi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɛɛ fɛɛ he ji gbee ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lolo ni jɛɔ mɔ ni kɛ ehe eha lɛ ŋɔɔ, ni etamɔ nɔ ni esɔleɔ yɛ lala-lala mli loo eela wɔŋjalɔi alala ko loo ekroko, ni yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ ehiɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ gbɛ ni jeee adebɔɔ naa nɔ fe fɛɛ nɔ. Yɛ naagbee beni egbe la lɛ, ewo amaga lɛ nɔ yɛ gbɛ ni esaaa nɔ kwraa, ni ekɛwoɔ e-grego kotoku lɛ mli ekoŋŋ kɛ hiɛdɔɔ tamɔ nɔ ni eji shwɛlɔ ko ni kɛ tso ni egbo woɔ kotoku mli.

Nɛkɛ nifeemɔi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɛɛ fɛɛ ha mihe ejɔɔɔ mi, ni beni mina lɛ ni ejieɔ okadii ni mli wa ni tsɔɔ akɛ egbe ejarayeli nitsumɔi lɛ anaa lɛ kpo, ni ekɛ mi miiho kɛtee saatso lɛ nɔ lɛ, misusu akɛ be eshɛ, bianɛ loo kɔkɔɔkɔ, dani agbe kane lɛ, koni mafo shɛɛ sane ni akɛ mi ewo mli be kakadaŋŋ lɛ mli.

Shi be ni mikɛsusu nɔ ni makɛɛ lɛ he lɛ, ji be ni gbeɔ mɔ. Beni eŋɔ e-tomahawk lɛ kɛjɛ okpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekwɛ eyitso lɛ mli fioo, ni no sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛwo kane lɛ he, ni ekɛ enaabu ŋmɛɔ ehiɛ, ni ekɛ tawa lasu atatui wuji shwieɔ shi. Enɔ jetsɛremɔ lɛ, kane lɛ gbo, ni nɛkɛ koo mli gbɔmɔyelɔ nɛɛ, ni ji tomahawk ni yɔɔ enyanyɔŋ teŋ lɛ, kɛ mi jo foi kɛtee saatso lɛ nɔ. Mila kɛ gbee ni wa, minyɛɛɛ mafee nɔ ko bianɛ; ni ekɛ naakpɛɛ gbee ni haa trukaa lɛ bɔi mihe numɔ.

Beni mikɛ nɔ ko miiwie, ni mileee nɔ ni ji lɛ, mitsi mihe kɛjɛ ehe kɛkpe gbogbo lɛ he, ni no sɛɛ lɛ mikɛ lɛ, mɔ fɛɛ mɔ loo nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni eji lɛ, fee dioo, ni eha mite shi ni misu kane lɛ ekoŋŋ. Shi ehetooi ni ekɛhaa yɛ gbee mli lɛ ha mitsui nyɔ mimli amrɔ nɔŋŋ akɛ enuuu nɔ ni mitsɔɔ lɛ shishi jogbaŋŋ.

“Namɔ-e debel bo?”—naagbee lɛ ekɛɛ—“owieee-e, dam-mi, migbe-e.” Ni nakai wiemɔ ni tomahawk ni tsoɔ lɛ bɔi shweremɔ yɛ mihe yɛ duŋ lɛ mli.

“Shikpɔŋtsɛ, yɛ Nyɔŋmɔ hewɔ, Peter Coffin!” I bolɔ akɛ: “Shikpɔŋtsɛ! Kwɛmɔ! Gbohiiaje! Bɔfoi! heremɔ mi!”

“Wiemɔ-e! kɛɛmɔ-ee mi mɔ-ee ji, loo dam-mi, migbe-e!” gbɔmɔyelɔ lɛ ŋmɔ ekoŋŋ, yɛ be mli ni e-tomahawk lɛ shweremɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ gbɛɔ tawa sũ ni mli edɔ lɛ eshwaa mihe aahu kɛyashi misusu akɛ mi-linɛn lɛ baatswa la. Shi shidaa aha ŋwɛi, nakai beaŋ lɛ, shikpɔŋtsɛ lɛ ba tsu lɛ mli ni ehiɛ kane, ni mijo foi kɛjɛ saatso lɛ nɔ mijo foi kɛtee eŋɔɔ.

Ekɛɛ, ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ wie ekoŋŋ akɛ: “Kaashe gbeyei bianɛ, Queequeg ni yɔɔ biɛ lɛ yeee oyitso yitsɔi kome po awui.”

Mibolɔ akɛ: “Kpa oŋmɔlɔ lɛ, ni mɛni hewɔ okɛɛɛɛ mi akɛ nakai hɛl harpooneer lɛ ji gbɔmɔyelɔ?”

“Misusu akɛ nyɛle;—ani mikɛɛɛɛ nyɛ, eji mɔ ko ni hɔ̃ɔ nibii yɛ maŋ lɛ mli?—shi nyɛtsɔa nyɛhe ekoŋŋ ni nyɛyaa wɔɔ. Queequeg, kwɛmɔ biɛ—o sabbee mi, mi sabbee—bo nuu nɛɛ wɔɔ bo—o sabbee?”

“Me sabbee babaoo”—Queequeg ŋmɔ, ni ekɛ epaipi lɛ shwie shi ni ete shi yɛ saatso lɛ nɔ.

Ekɛfata he akɛ: “You gettee in,” ni ekɛ e-tomahawk lɛ tsɔɔ mi, ni ekɛ atadei lɛ shwie he kome. Efee enɛ diɛŋtsɛ, jeee yɛ maŋ gbɛ nɔ pɛ, shi moŋ yɛ mlihilɛ kɛ ejurɔfeemɔ gbɛ nɔ diɛŋtsɛ. Midamɔ shi ni mikwɛ lɛ fioo. Yɛ e-tattooi lɛ fɛɛ hewɔ lɛ, eji gbɔmɔyelɔ ni he tse, ni ehe yɔɔ fɛo. Mɛni ji basabasafeemɔ ni mifee nɛɛ fɛɛ, misusu he yɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ mihe—nuu lɛ ji gbɔmɔ tamɔ mi pɛpɛɛpɛ: eyɛ yiŋtoo babaoo ni ekɛbaashe mi gbeyei, taakɛ esa akɛ mishe lɛ gbeyei lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ. Ehi akɛ okɛ gbɔmɔyelɔ ni hiɛ kã shi awɔ fe Kristofonyo ni etɔ dãa.

"Shikpɔŋtsɛ," mikɛɛ lɛ, "kɛɛmɔ lɛ ni ekɛ e-tomahawk lɛ ato jɛmɛ, loo paipi, loo nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni otsɛɔ lɛ; kɛɛmɔ lɛ ni ekpa tawa shɛremɔ, yɛ kukufoo mli, ni mikɛ lɛ baaya. Shi misumɔɔɔ akɛ nuu ko kɛ mi baashɛre tawa yɛ saatso lɛ nɔ. Eyɛ oshara. Kɛfata he lɛ, mibɛ inshuɔrans."

Beni akɛɛ Queequeg enɛ lɛ, ekpɛlɛ nɔ amrɔ nɔŋŋ, ni ekɛ bulɛ fee mi okadi ekoŋŋ koni mikɛ mihe awo saatso lɛ nɔ—ekɛ ehe shwie shi kɛtee afã kome tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛɛ—“Mitaŋ nyɛnaji kome po he.”

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ, “Gbi kpakpa, shikpɔŋtsɛ, obaanyɛ oya.”

Mikɛ mihe wo mli, ni miwɔko jogbaŋŋ fe nɛkɛ yɛ mishihilɛ mli kɔkɔɔkɔ.
