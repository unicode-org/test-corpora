YITSƆƆ 10. Naanyo ko ni yɔɔ Bosom.

Beni miku misɛɛ kɛjɛ Sɔlemɔtsu lɛ mli kɛtee Spouter-Inn lɛ, mina Queequeg yɛ jɛmɛ pɛ; eshi Sɔlemɔtsu lɛ mli dani jɔɔmɔ lɛ ba be ko. Eta sɛi ko nɔ yɛ la lɛ hiɛ, ni ekɛ enaji ema stof la lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ enine kome hiɛ nakai e-negro amaga bibioo lɛ ni ebɛŋkɛ ehiɛ; ni ekwɛɔ ehiɛ mli waa, ni ekɛ jack-knife miitswa ehiɛ blɛoo, ni yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ ekɛ ehe miitswa ŋmɔlɔ yɛ e-wɔŋjalɔi agbɛ nɔ.

Shi akɛni agbɛnɛ atsĩ enaa hewɔ lɛ, ekɛ amaga lɛ ma shi; ni etsɛɛɛ tsɔ, ni eya okpɔlɔ lɛ he, ni eŋɔ wolo agbo ko yɛ jɛmɛ, ni ekɛwo etsitsi nɔ ni ebɔi baafai lɛ akanemɔ kɛ yiŋtoo akɛ ekaneɔ daa; yɛ baafa ni ji nyɔŋmai enumɔ fɛɛ baafa mli—tamɔ bɔ ni misusuɔ he lɛ—ekpaa fioo, ni ekwɛɔ ehe ni ebɔle ehe lɛ ni nɔ ko bɛ mli, ni ekɛ wiemɔ ko haa naakpɛɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa ni tswaa be kakadaŋŋ. No sɛɛ lɛ, ebaaje shishi ekoŋŋ yɛ afii nyɔŋmai enumɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ mli; etamɔ nɔ ni ejeɔ shishi kɛjɛɔ yibɔ kome nɔ be fɛɛ be, tamɔ nɔ ni enyɛŋ ekane nɔ ni fe nyɔŋmai enumɔ, ni nyɔŋmai enumɔ ni fa babaoo tamɔ nɛkɛ ni ana yɛ ekomefeemɔ mli lɛ pɛ ji nɔ ni ha enaa kpɛ ehe yɛ baafai babaoo lɛ he lɛ.

Mikɛ miishɛɛ babaoo tara shi ni mikwɛ lɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ eji mɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei moŋ, shi ehiɛ efite yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɔ moŋ—kɛ hoo lɛ, taakɛ misumɔɔ lɛ—ehiɛ yɛ nɔ ko yɛ mli ni jeee nɔ ni esaaa kwraa. Onyɛŋ okɛ susuma lɛ ato. Kɛtsɔ e-tattooi ni jeee shikpɔŋ nɔ nɔ lɛ fɛɛ nɔ lɛ, misusu akɛ mina anɔkwa tsui ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ he okadii; ni yɛ ehiŋmɛii wuji ni mli kwɔ, ni ji la diŋ ni yɔɔ ekãa lɛ amli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni mumɔ ko ni baaha daimonioi akpe afee ekãa lɛ he okadii yɛ mli. Ni kɛfata enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ he lɛ, nɔ ko ni nɔ kwɔ yɛ Wɔŋjalɔ lɛ he, ni ehiɛ ni bɛ bulɛ po nyɛŋ afite kwraa. Etamɔ mɔ ko ni ekpokpoko pɛŋ ni ebɛ mɔ ko ni ekɛwo lɛ nyɔmɔ pɛŋ. Kɛji akɛ eji, hu akɛ, ajie eyitso, ni ajie ehiɛ kpo yɛ heyeli ni mli tse mli, ni etamɔ nɔ ni egbɛ eshwa fe bɔ ni ebaafee lɛ, mikɛ mihe wooo mli akɛ makpɛ miyiŋ yɛ enɛ he; shi nɔmimaa ji akɛ eyitso ji nɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ yɛ phrenologically. Ekolɛ ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni eji ŋmɔlɔ, shi ekai mi General Washington yitso, taakɛ anaa yɛ ehe mfoniri ni ehe gbɛi waa lɛ mli lɛ. Eyɛ ŋshɔ kakadaŋŋ ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ daa kɛjɛɔ hiɛi lɛ anɔ, ni nakai nɔŋŋ ejie ehe kpo waa, tamɔ ŋwɛi ŋshɔ kakadaŋŋ enyɔ ni tsei eyi nɔ obɔ lɛ. Queequeg ji George Washington ni jɛ gbɔmɔyeli mli.

Yɛ be mli ni mikwɛɔ lɛ jogbaŋŋ nakai, ni mifeɔ mihe tamɔ mɔ ko ni miikwɛ ahum lɛ kɛmiijɛ ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ lɛ, ebooo mihe ni miyɔɔ lɛ toi kɔkɔɔkɔ, ekɛ hiŋmɛi kome pɛ gbaaa ehe naa kɔkɔɔkɔ; shi etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ ehe wo naakpɛɛ wolo lɛ baafai akanemɔ mli kwraa. Kɛ misusu bɔ ni wɔkɛ wɔhe ebɔ wɔhe yɛ gbɛkɛ ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ mli lɛ he, kɛ titri lɛ, kɛ misusu suɔmɔ nine ni mina akɛ ashɛrɛ ashwie minɔ beni mite shi leebi lɛ he lɛ, misusu akɛ nɛkɛ he ni esusuuu he nɛɛ ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ waa. Shi kooloi ni yɔɔ gbeyei ji adesai ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ; bei komɛi lɛ oleee bɔ ni ooofee okɛ amɛ atsu nii jogbaŋŋ. Shishijee lɛ amɛmiifo fe nine; amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe ni amɛbuaa naa yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo nɔ lɛ tamɔ nɔ ni eji Socrates nilee. No mli lɛ, miyɔse hu akɛ Queequeg kɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi krokomɛi ni yɔɔ gbɔiatoohe lɛ ebɔko kɔkɔɔkɔ, loo ekɛ amɛ ebɔɔɔ fioo pɛ. Eyaaa hiɛ kwraa; etamɔ nɔ ni ebɛ shwelɛ ko akɛ ebaaha mɛi ni ele amɛ lɛ akuu lɛ aya hiɛ. Enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ gba mi naa akɛ eji mɔ kome ni he wa; shi kɛlɛ, kɛ́ asusu he shii enyɔ lɛ, nɔ ko ni miihe afee nɔ ni nɔ kwɔ yɛ mli. Nuu ko ni kɛ shia teŋ jɛkɛmɔ aaafee kilomitai akpei nyɔŋmai enyɔ lɛ yɛ biɛ, kɛtsɔ Cape Horn gbɛ lɛ nɔ, ni ji—ni ji gbɛ pɛ ni etsɔɔ nɔ eshɛɔ jɛmɛ—ni ashɛrɛ ashwie gbɔmɛi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ kɛha lɛ lɛ ateŋ tamɔ nɔ ni eyɛ shibɔlemɔ ŋulami ni ji Jupiter lɛ nɔ; ni kɛlɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ehe ejɔ lɛ kwraa; ni abuɔ toiŋjɔlɛ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ he; etsui enyɔ emli yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enaanyobɔɔ lɛ he; ekɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe yeɔ egbɔ be fɛɛ be. Eji anɔkwale akɛ enɛ ji jeŋ nilee kpakpa ko ni akɛtaa he; eyɛ mli akɛ ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ enuko pɛŋ akɛ nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ yɛ moŋ. Shi, ekolɛ, bɔni afee ni wɔfee anɔkwa jeŋ nilelɔi lɛ, esaaa akɛ wɔ gbɔmɛi ni gboiɔ lɛ wɔhiɛ kã shi loo wɔbɔɔ mɔdɛŋ waa lɛ nɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, kɛ́ minu akɛ nuu ni tamɔ nɛkɛ loo nuu ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kɛ ehe miiha kɛha jeŋ nilelɔ pɛ kɛkɛ ni mimuɔ sane naa akɛ, tamɔ yoomo ni yeɔ dyspeptic lɛ, ekolɛ “efo e-digester lɛ mli.”

Beni mita jɛmɛ yɛ nakai tsu ni amrɔ nɛɛ efee ekome lɛ mli lɛ; la ni tsoɔ fioo lɛ, yɛ nakai fã ni mli jɔ lɛ mli beni, beni eklɛŋklɛŋ hewalɛ lɛ eha kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli edɔ sɛɛ lɛ, no sɛɛ lɛ ekpɛɔ kɛkɛ ni esa akɛ akwɛ; gbɛkɛ mli hulutsoo be kɛ ŋwɛibɔfoi ni buaa amɛhe naa kɛbɔleɔ ŋwɛitsu lɛ he, ni amɛkwɛɔ wɔyi enyɔ ni wɔfee dioo, ni wɔkome too wɔyɔɔ lɛ; ahum ni miitswa ni ebɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ; Mibɔi henumɔi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ahe jwɛŋmɔ. Minu he akɛ mihe miiwo la. Mitsui ni mli egbala kɛ minine ni efee shwilafo lɛ etsɔko ehe dɔŋŋ kɛte shi ewo ŋmɔtɔ je lɛ. Nɛkɛ kooloo fɔŋ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli nɛɛ ekpɔ̃ lɛ. Jɛmɛ ji he ni eta, ni esusumɔi ni bɛ mli lɛ diɛŋtsɛ wieɔ su ko ni osatofoi ni yɔɔ jeŋba kpakpa kɛ lakamɔi ni bɛ sɛɛnamɔ ko bɛ mli lɛ he. Eji kooloo; nibii ni anaa diɛŋtsɛ ni esa akɛ ana; shi kɛlɛ mibɔi henumɔ akɛ mihe miigba mihe kɛmiiba ehe yɛ teemɔŋ gbɛ nɔ. Ni nakai nibii ni kulɛ ebaashwie mɛi krokomɛi babaoo lɛ, amɛji magnɛt diɛŋtsɛ ni gbalaa mi yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ lɛ. Misusu akɛ maka wɔŋjalɔ naanyo ko makwɛ, ejaakɛ Kristofoi amlihilɛ ejie bulɛ ni bɛ sɛɛnamɔ kpo. Mikɛ misɛi lɛ tsi mibɛŋkɛ lɛ, ni mifee naanyobɔɔ okadi kɛ ŋaawoi komɛi, ni mibɔ mɔdɛŋ fɛɛ ni manyɛ koni mikɛ lɛ agba sane yɛ nakai beaŋ. Shishijee lɛ, eyɔseee hiɛyaai nɛɛ tsɔ; shi amrɔ nɛɛ, beni miwie gbɔfeemɔ ni efee nyɛsɛɛ gbɛkɛ lɛ he lɛ, efee nɔ ko koni ebi mi kɛji wɔbaayawɔ ekoŋŋ lo. Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ hɛɛ; he ni misusu akɛ ehiɛ miishɛɛ, ekolɛ ajie eyi fioo.

Kɛkɛ ni wɔfee ekome kɛgbele wolo lɛ mli, ni mibɔ mɔdɛŋ mitsɔɔ lɛ yiŋtoo hewɔ ni akala wolo lɛ, kɛ mfonirii fioo ni yɔɔ mli lɛ ashishinumɔ. No hewɔ lɛ etsɛɛɛ ni mikɛ ehe wo miishɛɛ mli; ni kɛjɛ no mli lɛ wɔtee jabbering ni hi fe fɛɛ ni wɔbaanyɛ wɔfee yɛ kponɔgbɛ nibii srɔtoi ni abaana yɛ maŋ ni ehe gbɛi waa nɛɛ mli lɛ he. Etsɛɛɛ ni mikɛ gbɔmɛi ateŋ lasu he ŋaawoo ha; ni beni ejie ekotoku lɛ kɛ tomahawk lɛ kpo lɛ, ekɛ ŋmɔtɔ ko ha mi dioo. Ni no sɛɛ lɛ wɔtara shi ni wɔmiitsake ŋmɔtɔi ni jɛ nakai e-paip ni yɔɔ ŋa lɛ mli lɛ, ni wɔha etsɔ wɔteŋ daa.

Kɛji akɛ ais ko ni tsɔɔ akɛ misusuuu he lɛ yɛ Pagan lɛ tsitsi mli lolo lɛ, nɛkɛ lasu ni ŋɔɔ ni wɔyɔɔ nɛɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni efo mli, ni eshi wɔ akɛ nanemɛi. Etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ mi tee yɛ adebɔɔ naa ni ekɛ mi tee lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ tamɔ bɔ ni mi hu mikɛ lɛ tee lɛ; ni beni wɔ lasu lɛ ba naagbee lɛ, ekɛ ehiɛ fĩ minɔ lɛ he, ni ekɛ mi fĩ mihe, ni ekɛɛ akɛ kɛjɛ bianɛ kɛyaa lɛ wɔbote gbalashihilɛ mli; shishinumɔ, yɛ emaŋ lɛ wiemɔ kuku mli lɛ, akɛ wɔji nanemɛi ni yɔɔ tsitsi nɔ; ekɛ miishɛɛ baagbo eha mi, kɛ ehe miihia. Yɛ maŋnyo mli lɛ, kulɛ naanyobɔɔ la ni tsoɔ trukaa nɛɛ baafee tamɔ nɔ ni eshɛɛɛ be kwraa, nɔ ko ni esaaa akɛ akɛ he fɔ̃ɔ nɔ kwraa; shi yɛ nɛkɛ kooloo ni yɔɔ mlɛo nɛɛ mli lɛ, nakai mlai momo lɛ tsuuu nii.

Beni wɔye gbɛkɛ niyenii, kɛ gbɔmɛi ateŋ sanegbaa kroko kɛ tawa shɛremɔ sɛɛ lɛ, wɔfee ekome kɛtee wɔtsu lɛ mli. Ekɛ eyitso ni awo lɛ tsofa lɛ fee mi nikeenii; ejie e-tawa kotoku wulu lɛ, ni ekɛ enine shwie tawa lɛ shishi lɛ, ejie shika aaafee dɔlai nyɔŋmai etɛ; no sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ amɛ gbɛ eshwa okpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ tsɔne ja amɛ mli enyɔ ni yeɔ egbɔ, ni ekɛtsi amɛteŋ ekome kɛba miŋɔɔ, ni ekɛɛ mi nɔ ni. Miiya ni mikɛ mihe awo mli; shi eha mifee dioo kɛtsɔ amɛ ni ekɛwo mitrɔsa kotokui lɛ amli lɛ nɔ. Miha amɛhi shi. Kɛkɛ ni etee gbɛkɛ sɔlemɔi lɛ anɔ, ejie emaga lɛ, ni ejie wolo ni akɛshãa la lɛ. Kɛtsɔ okadi kɛ okadii komɛi anɔ lɛ, misusu akɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ehaoɔ koni mikɛ mihe awo mli; shi akɛni mile nɔ ni baaba yɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ jogbaŋŋ hewɔ lɛ, misusu he fioo akɛ, kɛji efɔ mi nine lɛ, mafee nɔ ko loo mafee nakai.

Kristofonyo kpakpa ji mi; afɔ lɛ ni atsɔse lɛ yɛ Presbyterian Sɔlemɔ ni tɔmɔ bɛ mli lɛ mli. Belɛ te mafee tɛŋŋ mikɛ wɔŋjalɔ ni yɔɔ ŋa nɔ nɛɛ afee ekome yɛ etso kuku lɛ jamɔ mli? Shi mɛni ji jamɔ? susumɔ I. Ani osusuɔ agbɛnɛ, Ishmael, akɛ ekolɛ ŋwɛi kɛ shikpɔŋ Nyɔŋmɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ—wɔŋjalɔi kɛ mɛi fɛɛ fata he lɛ—baanyɛ aŋɔ awuŋa yɛ tso diŋ bibioo ko ni he ehiaaa lɛ he? Ebaŋ lɛ nakai! Shi mɛni ji jamɔ?—ni afee Nyɔŋmɔ suɔmɔnaa nii— no ji jamɔ. Ni mɛni ji Nyɔŋmɔ suɔmɔnaa nii?—ni mafee minaanyo gbɔmɔ nɔ ni misumɔɔ ni minaanyo gbɔmɔ afee mi lɛ— no ji Nyɔŋmɔ suɔmɔnaa nii. Amrɔ nɛɛ, Queequeg ji minaanyo gbɔmɔ. Ni mɛni ji nɔ ni mishweɔ akɛ nɛkɛ Queequeg nɛɛ afee mi? Mɛni hewɔ, okɛ mi afee ekome yɛ mi Presbyterian jamɔ pɔtɛɛ lɛ mli. Nɔ ni jɛ mli ba ji akɛ, esa akɛ no sɛɛ lɛ mikɛ lɛ afee ekome yɛ e-; ergo, esa akɛ matsɔ wɔŋjalɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, mikɛ la shã ŋmeii lɛ; eye ebua ni ekɛfi amaga bibioo ni efeko efɔŋ lɛ sɛɛ; ekɛ biskit ni ashã kɛ Queequeg ha lɛ; salamed yɛ ehiɛ shii enyɔ loo shii etɛ; ekɛ ehiɛ shwie ehiɛ nɔ; ni beni wɔfee nakai lɛ, wɔjie atadei ni wɔyawɔ, ni wɔkɛ wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔhenilee kɛ je lɛ fɛɛ yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli. Shi wɔwɔɔɔ ni wɔgbaaa sane fioo ko.

Bɔ ni eji lɛ mileee; shi he ko bɛ ni tamɔ saatso kɛha teemɔŋ saji ni nanemɛi jieɔ lɛ kpo yɛ. Amɛkɛɛ akɛ, nuu kɛ ŋa gbeleɔ amɛsusumai lɛ ashishigbɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhaa amɛhe yɛ jɛmɛ; ni bei pii lɛ, gbalashihilɛ mli hefatalɔi komɛi ni edara lɛ maleɔ ni amɛgbaa blema saji ahe sane kɛyashiɔ be ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaaba. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ wɔtsuii amli honeymoon lɛ mli lɛ, mi kɛ Queequeg—ni ji gbalashihilɛ mli hefatalɔi ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ, ni yɔɔ suɔmɔ.





YITSƆƆ 11. Gbɛkɛ atade.

Wɔkã saatso lɛ nɔ nakai, ni wɔmiigba sane ni wɔmiiwɔ yɛ be kukuoo mli, ni Queequeg kɛ suɔmɔ shwieɔ enaji ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni atɛŋ lɛ nɔ yɛ minɔ lɛ nɔ be fɛɛ be, ni no sɛɛ lɛ egbalaa amɛ kɛyaa sɛɛ; no hewɔ lɛ wɔji mɛi ni kɛ gbɔmɛi yeɔ jogbaŋŋ ni wɔyeɔ wɔhe ni wɔfeɔ mlɛo; beni, naagbee lɛ, yɛ wɔ sanegbaai lɛ ahewɔ lɛ, nɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli fioo ni eshwɛ yɛ wɔmli lɛ shi kwraa, ni wɔnu he akɛ wɔbaate shi ekoŋŋ, eyɛ mli akɛ gbi-hejɔɔmɔ lɛ ji nɔ ko ni baa shi lolo yɛ wɔsɛɛ be mli moŋ.

Hɛɛ, wɔbatsɔ mɛi ni hiɛ kã shi waa; aahu akɛ wɔshidaamɔ ni wɔkã shi lɛ bɔi nijiaŋwujee, ni fiofio lɛ wɔna wɔhe akɛ wɔte shi; atadei ni akɛwo wɔhe jogbaŋŋ, ni wɔkɛ wɔhe fɔ̃ɔ yitso-tso lɛ nɔ ni wɔkɛ wɔkuɛi ejwɛ lɛ ebɔle amɛhe, ni wɔgugɔi enyɔ lɛ ekoto amɛhe, tamɔ nɔ ni wɔkuɛi lɛ ji tsɔnei ni akɛwoɔ la mli. Wɔnu he akɛ wɔhe yɛ fɛo waa ni wɔhe ejɔ wɔ, titri lɛ akɛni hulu lɛ miitso waa yɛ shinaa lɛ naa lɛ hewɔ; lɛɛlɛŋ, kɛjɛ saatso atadei hu amli, ni ena akɛ la bɛ tsu lɛ mli. Mikɛɔ akɛ, bɔ ni eji lɛ, ejaakɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, bɔni afee ni oná gbɔmɔtsoŋ dɔlɛ mli ŋɔɔmɔ lɛ, esa akɛ ohe fã bibioo ko afee fɛ̃i, ejaakɛ su ko bɛ je nɛɛ mli ni jeee nɔ ni eji kɛtsɔ srɔtofeemɔ ko kɛkɛ nɔ. Nɔ ko bɛ ni yɔɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ emli. Kɛji akɛ okɛ ohe fɔ̃ɔ nɔ akɛ oyɛ hejɔlɛ fɛɛ, ni okɛ be kakadaŋŋ ehi shi nakai lɛ, no lɛ anyɛɛɛ akɛɛ akɛ ohe ejɔ bo dɔŋŋ. Shi kɛji akɛ, tamɔ mi kɛ Queequeg ni wɔyɔɔ saatso lɛ nɔ lɛ, ogɔŋ naa loo oyitso nɔ lɛ mli ejɔ fioo lɛ, mɛni hewɔ, yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, yɛ jwɛŋmɔŋ nibii fɛɛ mli lɛ onuɔ he akɛ oyɛ miishɛɛ fe fɛɛ ni ohe ejɔ bo ni yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ ko bɛ he. Yɛ yiŋtoo nɛɛ hewɔ lɛ, esaaa akɛ akɛ la woɔ shĩa ni awɔɔ mli kɔkɔɔkɔ, ni eji niiatsɛmɛi ashihilɛ mli nibii ni jara wa ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ ateŋ ekome. Ejaakɛ nɛkɛ niyenii ni ŋɔɔ nɛɛ nɔyaa ji ni obɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko fe blanket ni kã bo kɛ osnugness kɛ kponɔgbɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli ejɔ lɛ teŋ lɛ. Kɛkɛ ni jɛmɛ ji he ni okã shi tamɔ la kome ni mli edɔ yɛ arctic kristalo tsui mli lɛ.

Wɔtara shi yɛ nɛkɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ nɛɛ mli be ko, ni shikome pɛ misusu akɛ magbele mihiŋmɛii; ejaakɛ kɛ́ miyɛ tsɔji ateŋ, kɛji shwane loo gbɛkɛ, kɛji miwɔ loo mite shi lɛ, miyɛ gbɛ ni mitsɔɔ nɔ mikɛ mihiŋmɛii ŋmɛɔ be fɛɛ be, bɔni afee ni manyɛ mikɛ mijwɛŋmɔ ama bɔ ni miyɔɔ saatso lɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ jogbaŋŋ. Ejaakɛ gbɔmɔ ko nyɛŋ anu lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe jogbaŋŋ kɔkɔɔkɔ ja eŋmɛ ehiŋmɛii; tamɔ nɔ ni duŋ ji nɔ ni sa yɛ wɔhe nibii amli diɛŋtsɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ la baafee nɔ ni sa wɔ shikpɔŋ nɔ fã lɛ jogbaŋŋ moŋ. Beni migbele mihiŋmɛii yɛ nakai beaŋ, ni mije mi diɛŋtsɛ mi duŋ ni ŋɔɔ ni mi diɛŋtsɛ mibɔ lɛ mli kɛtee kponɔgbɛ duŋ ni akɛwoɔ mɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni ji ŋmɛlɛtswai nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ ni kane bɛ mli lɛ mli lɛ, mina heguɔgbee ko ni esaaa. Ni mikɛ mihe wooo ŋaawoo ni jɛ Queequeg ŋɔɔ akɛ ekolɛ ehi jogbaŋŋ akɛ wɔbaatswa kane ko lɛ hu mli kwraa, akɛni wɔte shi babaoo hewɔ lɛ; ni kɛfata he lɛ enu shwelɛ ni mli wa he akɛ ebaaná ŋmɔlɔ fioo komɛi ni yɔɔ dioo kɛjɛ e-Tomahawk lɛ mli. Akɛɛ akɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ minu he akɛ miyɛ hetsɛ̃ ni mli wa tamɔ nɛkɛ yɛ tawa shɛremɔ ni eshɛreɔ yɛ saatso lɛ nɔ gbɛkɛ ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ he moŋ, shi kɛlɛ na bɔ ni wɔ jwɛŋmɔ ni ejaaa ni mli wa lɛ yaa hiɛ ehaa beni suɔmɔ ba shikome ni ekɛ amɛ baabɔle amɛhe lɛ. Ejaakɛ amrɔ nɛɛ misumɔɔɔ nɔ ko ni hi fe ni mahã Queequeg ashɛre tawa yɛ mihe, yɛ saatso lɛ nɔ po, ejaakɛ etamɔ nɔ ni eyi obɔ kɛ shia miishɛɛ ni yɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ tamɔ nɛkɛ yɛ nakai beaŋ. Minuuu he akɛ misusuɔ shikpɔŋtsɛ lɛ inshuɔrans he mla lɛ he fe nine dɔŋŋ. Mihiɛ kã kɛha teemɔŋ hejɔɔmɔ ni yɔɔ kuku ni ji paipii kɛ blanket ni mikɛ minaanyo diɛŋtsɛ ko jaa lɛ pɛ. Agbɛnɛ wɔtsɔ Tomahawk lɛ nɔ kɛjɛ eko nɔ kɛtee ekroko nɔ, ni wɔkɛ wɔjakɛtii ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ ebɔle wɔtsitsii ahe, ni fiofio lɛ, lasu bluu ni fĩɔ shi ni kane ni ashã lɛ ehee lɛ la lɛ kpɛɔ lɛ ba yɛ wɔ nɔ.

Kɛji akɛ nɛkɛ kaalɔ ni shwieɔ shi nɛɛ shwie kooloo lɛ kɛtee hei ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ, mileee, shi agbɛnɛ ewie emaŋ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ he; ni akɛni miisumɔ waa ni manu esane lɛ hewɔ lɛ, mikpa lɛ fai koni eya nɔ egba. Ekɛ miishɛɛ tsu he nii. Eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ minuuu ewiemɔi fioo komɛi ashishi moŋ, shi nibii ni ajie lɛ kpo yɛ sɛɛ mli, beni mile ewiemɔi ni efite lɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ, haa minyɛɔ mikɛ sane muu lɛ fɛɛ haa tamɔ bɔ ni ebaanyɛ etsɔɔ yɛ fãji kɛkɛ ni mikɛhaa lɛ mli lɛ.





YITSƆƆ 12. Wala he sane.

Queequeg ji Rokovoko, ni ji ŋshɔkpɔ ko ni yɔɔ Anaigbɛ kɛ Anaigbɛ shɔŋŋ lɛ maŋnyo. Ebɛ shikpɔŋ he mfoniri ko nɔ; anɔkwa hei bɛ nakai kɔkɔɔkɔ.

Beni kooloo ko ni afɔ lɛ ehee lɛ joɔ foi kɛyaa emaŋ kooi lɛ amli yɛ jwɛi ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ mli, ni tooi ni ŋmɔɔ nii lɛ nyiɛɔ esɛɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni eji tso bibioo ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ; yɛ nakai beaŋ po lɛ, shwelɛ ni mli wa ko ni yɔɔ Queequeg susuma ni miitao gbɛhe lɛ mli akɛ ebaana nɔ ko ni fe Kristendom he nɔ ko fe ŋshɔŋlooi ni atsɛɔ lɛ whale lɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ kome loo enyɔ. Etsɛ ji Onukpa Kpeteŋkpele, Maŋtsɛ; etsɛkwɛ̃ ji Osɔfonukpa; ni yɛ nyɛ gbɛfaŋ lɛ, ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ tsɛkwɛmɛi ni ji tawuulɔi ni anyɛɛɛ aye amɛnɔ kunim lɛ aŋamɛi lɛ anɔ. Là ni hi jogbaŋŋ yɛ efãji amli—maŋtsɛyeli nibii; eyɛ mli akɛ efite yɛ awerɛho gbɛ nɔ moŋ, shi misheɔ gbeyei, yɛ gbɔmɔyeli su ni etsɔse lɛ yɛ oblahiiaŋ ŋmɔnɔ ni akaseko lɛ mli lɛ hewɔ.

Sag Harbour lɛlɛ ko basara etsɛ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ, ni Queequeg tao gbɛ kɛya Kristofoi ashikpɔji anɔ. Shi lɛlɛ lɛ, ni eyɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi babaoo lɛ, ekpoo etade lɛ; ni jeee Maŋtsɛ ni etsɛ yɔɔ lɛ fɛɛ nyɛ eye kunim. Shi Queequeg wo shi. Ekome pɛ ekɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fã gbɛ kɛtee ŋshɔkpɔ ko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ nɔ, ni ele akɛ esa akɛ lɛlɛ lɛ atsɔ mli beni eshi ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ. Koral ŋshɔ ko yɛ efã kome; yɛ gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ, shikpɔŋ lilɛi ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ, ni mangrove tsei ni kwɛ̃ɔ kɛyaa nu lɛ mli lɛ ehà nɔ. Beni ekɛ elɛlɛ ni yɔɔ nu lɛ nɔ lolo lɛ tee nɛkɛ tsei nɛɛ ateŋ, ni ehiɛ tsɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ, eta shi yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ, ni ekɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli tsɔne lɛ shwie shi; ni beni lɛlɛ lɛ miiho lɛ, ejo foi tamɔ la ni tsoɔ; ná efã; kɛ enane kome ni tsɔɔ sɛɛ lɛ tsɔ ehe ni ekɛ elɛlɛ lɛ bote nu mli; ekwɔ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai lɛ anɔ; ni ekɛ ehe fɛɛ shwie lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ, ni emɔ ŋwɛi ŋtayaa ko mli yɛ jɛmɛ, ni ekã kitã akɛ eŋmɛɛɛ gbɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ efo mli bibii moŋ.

Efolo lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ wo ehe gbeyei akɛ ebaashɛrɛ lɛ ashwie ŋshɔ lɛ mli; ekɛ cutlass fĩ eniji ni bɛ atade lɛ anɔ; Queequeg ji Maŋtsɛ ko binuu, ni Queequeg tsiii ehe. Akɛni ehiɛ ni egbo ni ebɛ gbeyeishemɔ, kɛ eshwelɛ ni mli wa akɛ ebaayasara Kristendom lɛ gba lɛ naa hewɔ lɛ, yɛ naagbee lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ ŋmɛ etsui shi, ni ekɛɛ lɛ akɛ ekolɛ ebaafee ehe tamɔ mɔ ko ni yɔɔ shia. Shi nɛkɛ oblanyo fioo fɛfɛo nɛɛ—nɛkɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ Wales Maŋtsɛbi nɛɛ, enaaa Kapten lɛ shĩa lɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ. Amɛkɛ lɛ to lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ ateŋ, ni amɛfee lɛ ŋshɔŋlooi. Shi taakɛ Czar Peter tsui nyɔɔ emli akɛ ebaatsu nii waa yɛ maŋsɛɛ maji amli lɛlɛfeemɔ hei lɛ, Queequeg ebuuu hiɛgbele ko kwraa, kɛji akɛ ebaanyɛ ekɛ miishɛɛ aná hewalɛ ni ekɛaaha emaŋbii ni atsɔɔɔ amɛ nii lɛ ale nii. Ejaakɛ yɛ shishigbɛ—tamɔ bɔ ni ekɛɛ mi lɛ—shwelɛ ni mli kwɔ ni eyɔɔ akɛ ebaakase nii yɛ Kristofoi lɛ ateŋ lɛ ji nɔ ni tsirɛ lɛ, ni ji ŋaa nitsumɔi ni akɛbaahã ewebii lɛ aná miishɛɛ lolo fe bɔ ni amɛyɔɔ lɛ; ni nɔ ni fe nakai lɛ, ehi lolo fe amɛ. Shi, shwane! etsɛɛɛ ni ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsulɔi anifeemɔi lɛ ha ena nɔmimaa akɛ Kristofoi po baanyɛ afee mɛi ni yɔɔ awerɛho kɛ mɛi fɔji fɛɛ; nɔ ni bɛ naagbee, fe etsɛ jeŋmaji fɛɛ. Naagbee lɛ, eshɛ Sag Ŋshɔkpɔ momo lɛ nɔ; ni amɛna nɔ ni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ feɔ yɛ jɛmɛ; ni no sɛɛ lɛ, beni etee Nantucket, ni ena bɔ ni amɛfiteɔ amɛnyɔmɔwoo lɛ yɛ nakai he lɛ hu lɛ, Queequeg ohiafo lɛ ŋmɛ gbɛ akɛ elaaje. Esusu akɛ, eji jeŋ fɔŋ ko ni yɔɔ meridians fɛɛ mli; Magbo akɛ wɔŋjalɔ.

Ni nakai eji amaga jalɔ onukpa ko ni yɔɔ tsui mli, ni ehi Kristofoi nɛɛ ateŋ lolo, ewo amɛ atadei, ni ebɔ mɔdɛŋ ni ewie amɛ wiemɔi ni bɛ sɛɛnamɔ. No hewɔ lɛ gbɛi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni kɔɔ ehe lɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ amrɔ nɛɛ be ko kɛjɛ shia.

Kɛtsɔ ŋaawoi anɔ lɛ, mibi lɛ kɛji ekɛ ŋaawoo haaa akɛ ebaaku esɛɛ, ni efee maŋtsɛyeli he gbijurɔyeli; akɛni agbɛnɛ ekolɛ ebaabu etsɛ akɛ egbo ni etee hewɔ lɛ, ejaakɛ egbɔ waa ni ebɛ hewalɛ yɛ naagbee amaniɛbɔɔi lɛ amli. Eha hetoo akɛ daabi, dabi lolo; ni ekɛfata he akɛ eji Kristojamɔ ni sheɔ gbeyei, loo Kristofoi moŋ, ni eha esaaa akɛ ekwɔ maŋtsɛ sɛi ni he tse ni he tseee ni wɔŋjalɔi nyɔŋmai etɛ ni tsɔ ehiɛ lɛ yɔɔ nɔ lɛ. Shi ekɛɛ akɛ, be fioo ko lɛ, ebaaku esɛɛ eba, —kɛji enu he akɛ abaptisi lɛ ekoŋŋ pɛ kɛkɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, kɛha nɔ ko fioo lɛ, esusu akɛ ebaafã gbɛ kɛya he fɛɛ he, ni edu e-wild oats lɛ yɛ ŋshɔi ejwɛ lɛ fɛɛ mli. Amɛfee lɛ harpooneer, ni nakai dade ni yɔɔ ŋmeii lɛ ji maŋtsɛtso najiaŋ bianɛ.

Mibi lɛ nɔ ni baanyɛ afee eyiŋtoo amrɔ nɔŋŋ, ni mita bɔ ni ebaafã gbɛ wɔsɛɛ lɛ he. Eha hetoo, koni eya ŋshɔ hiɛ ekoŋŋ, yɛ etsɛmɔ momo lɛ mli. Yɛ enɛ nɔ lɛ, mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ ji mi diɛŋtsɛ miyiŋkpɛɛ, ni mikɛɛ lɛ miyiŋtoo akɛ mafã gbɛ kɛjɛ Nantucket, akɛ eji lɛlɛdaamɔhe ni hi fe fɛɛ ni ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛlɔ ni yɔɔ he miishɛɛ lɛ baanyɛ ajɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli. Ekpɛ eyiŋ amrɔ nɔŋŋ akɛ ebaafata mihe kɛya nakai ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ, ekɛ lɛlɛ awo lɛlɛ kome mli, ebote watsi kome mli, lɛlɛ kome mli, ekɛ mi afee basabasafeemɔ kome, yɛ kukufoo mli lɛ, ekɛ mi baagba mihe saji fɛɛ; kɛ miniji enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ yɛ edɛŋ, ni mikɛ ekãa awo jeŋ enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ Potluck lɛ mli. Mikɛ miishɛɛ kpɛlɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ nɔ; ejaakɛ kɛfata suɔmɔ ni minuɔ he amrɔ nɛɛ mihaa Queequeg lɛ he lɛ, eji mɔ ko ni yɔɔ niiashikpamɔ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔ mli, ni akɛni nakai eji hewɔ lɛ, enyɛŋ ekpa mɔ ko ni, tamɔ mi lɛ, leee ŋshɔŋlooi ahe teemɔŋ saji kwraa, eyɛ mli akɛ ele ŋshɔ lɛ jogbaŋŋ, taakɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ jarayelɔi le lɛ.

Beni esane lɛ ba naagbee kɛ epaipi lɛ naagbee ŋmɔlɔ ni miigbo lɛ, Queequeg fua mi, ni ekɛ ehiɛ mii minɔ lɛ nɔ, ni etswa kane lɛ, wɔfo wɔhe kɛjɛ wɔhe, nɛkɛ gbɛ kɛ nɛkɛ gbɛ nɔ, ni etsɛɛɛ tsɔ ni wɔwɔ.





YITSƆƆ 13. Shikpɔŋ nɔ tsɔne.

Enɔ jetsɛremɔ, Hɔgbaa, beni mikɛ yitso ni awo lɛ tsofa lɛ shwie yitsɔi-kpalɔ ko ŋɔɔ, kɛha blɔki kome lɛ, mikɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ mi kɛ minaanyo lɛ nyɔmɔwoo lɛ to; shi kɛlɛ, mikɛ minaanyo lɛ shika tsu nii. Etamɔ nɔ ni shikpɔŋtsɛ lɛ ni miiŋmɔ lɛ, kɛ mɛi ni yɔɔ shia lɛ mli lɛ hu miifee amɛ naakpɛɛ yɛ naanyobɔɔ ni ba trukaa yɛ mi kɛ Queequeg teŋ lɛ hewɔ—titri lɛ akɛni Peter Coffin tsina kɛ tsina he saji ni kɔɔ ehe lɛ eha mihe efee mi yaa tsutsu ko yɛ mɔ diɛŋtsɛ ni mikɛ lɛ yɔɔ amrɔ nɛɛ he lɛ hewɔ.

Wɔfa shwiili, ni wɔkɛ wɔnibii, ni mi diɛŋtsɛ mi-kapɛt-baagi ni ehiii lɛ fata he, kɛ Queequeg kanvas kotoku kɛ hamɔk lɛ wo mli, ni wɔtee shi kɛtee “Moss,” ni ji Nantucket paketi lɛlɛ bibioo ni akɛma ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ. Beni wɔyaa lɛ, gbɔmɛi lɛ kwɛ wɔ; jeee yɛ Queequeg tsɔ—ejaakɛ amɛle gbɔmɔyelɔi tamɔ lɛ ni amɛnaa yɛ amɛ gbɛjegbɛi anɔ lɛ, —shi moŋ yɛ mi kɛ lɛ ni amɛnaa yɛ teemɔŋ saji ni tamɔ nɛkɛ nɔ lɛ mli. Shi wɔbooo amɛ toi, ni wɔyaa nɔ wɔfoɔ tsɔne lɛ mli kɛyaa kɛbaa, ni Queequeg kpaɔ shi bianɛ kɛ agbɛnɛ koni etsake tsɔne ni ekɛwoɔ e-harpoon barbs lɛ nɔ lɛ mli tsɔne lɛ. Mibi lɛ nɔ hewɔ ni ekɛ nɔ ko ni haoɔ mɔ tamɔ nɛkɛ tee ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, kɛji ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛi fɛɛ ni akɛyaa ŋshɔŋ lɛ enaaa amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ ŋshɔŋlooi. Yɛ enɛ he lɛ, eha hetoo akɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ nɔ ni mitsɔɔ lɛ ji anɔkwale moŋ, shi eyɛ suɔmɔ pɔtɛɛ ko kɛha lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-harpoon lɛ, ejaakɛ eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ nɔmimaa, ni aka lɛ akwɛ jogbaŋŋ yɛ gbele mli tawuu babaoo mli, ni ekɛ ŋshɔŋlooi atsuii kpaa gbee waa. Yɛ kukufoo mli lɛ, taakɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ mli ŋmɔshi nibii akpɛlɔi kɛ ŋmɔshi nibii ahe nitsulɔi babaoo, ni kɛ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ ŋmeii yaa okwaafoi lɛ aŋmɔji amli lɛ—eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, eji gbɛnaa nii ni kãaa amɛnɔ akɛ amɛha amɛ—shi nakai po lɛ, Queequeg, yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eyiŋtoi ahewɔ lɛ, sumɔɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-harpoon lɛ.

Beni ejie tsɔne lɛ kɛjɛ minine mli kɛtee enine mli lɛ, egba mi sane ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ ni kɔɔ klɛŋklɛŋ tsɔne ni shwiili ena pɛŋ lɛ he. No mli lɛ, eyɛ Sag Ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ. Etamɔ nɔ ni elɛlɛ lɛ nuŋtsɔmɛi lɛ efa lɛ eko, ni ekɛbaawo etsitsi ni tsii lɛ kɛya he ni eyɔɔ lɛ. Bɔni afee ni efee tamɔ mɔ ko ni leee nɔ ko kwraa—eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, eji nakai kwraa, yɛ gbɛ pɔtɛɛ ni esa akɛ etsɔ nɔ ekwɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ nɔ lɛ he—Queequeg kɛ etsitsi fɔ̃ɔ nɔ; etswaa lɛ oya; ni no sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ tsɔne lɛ woɔ etsitsi nɔ ni enyiɛɔ kɛyaa ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ. Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Mɛni hewɔ, Queequeg, ekolɛ ole jogbaŋŋ fe nakai, mɔ ko baasusu he. Ani gbɔmɛi lɛ ŋmɔee?”

Yɛ enɛ nɔ lɛ, egba mi sane kroko. Etamɔ nɔ ni mɛi ni yɔɔ eŋshɔkpɔ ni ji Rokovoko lɛ nɔ lɛ jieɔ ŋmeii bibii amli nu ni ŋɔɔ lɛ kpo yɛ amɛgbalashihilɛ mli, ni amɛfeɔ ŋmeii agbo ko ni mli ewo muji tamɔ punchbowl; ni nɛkɛ punchbowl nɛɛ feɔ ŋwɛi ŋtayaa kpeteŋkpele ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ŋta ni akpɛ́ yɛ he ni ayeɔ gbijurɔyeli lɛ yɛ lɛ nɔ be fɛɛ be. Amrɔ nɛɛ, jarayeli lɛlɛ wulu ko ba Rokovoko be ko, ni e-komanda lɛ—kɛjɛ saji fɛɛ amli lɛ, nuu ko ni yɔɔ nyam waa ni yeɔ be nɔ, kɛ hoo lɛ, kɛha ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko—afɔ̃ nɛkɛ kudɔlɔ nɛɛ nine kɛtee Queequeg nyɛmi yoo, maŋtsɛbi yoo fɛfɛo ko ni eye afii nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ lɛ yookpeemɔ gbijurɔyeli lɛ shishi. Nubu; beni abua yookpeemɔ gbɔi lɛ fɛɛ anaa yɛ ayemforo lɛ ŋmei shĩa lɛ, nɛkɛ Kapten nɛɛ nyiɛɔ kɛboteɔ mli, ni akɛni aha lɛ woo nitsumɔ lɛ hewɔ lɛ, ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ punchbowl lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ Osɔfonukpa lɛ kɛ emaŋtsɛyeli Maŋtsɛ lɛ teŋ, ni ji Queequeg tsɛ. Kɛ akɛɛ duromɔ lɛ, —ejaakɛ nakai gbɔmɛi lɛ yɛ amɛduromɔ lɛ tamɔ wɔ nɔŋŋ—eyɛ mli akɛ Queequeg kɛɛ mi akɛ, etamɔɔɔ wɔ, ni yɛ bei ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ wɔkwɛɔ shi kɛyaa wɔ tsɛnsii lɛ anɔ lɛ, amɛ, yɛ nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ enɛ lɛ, amɛfeɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ahe mfoniri, amɛkwɛɔ ŋwɛi kɛyaa gbijurɔyelii fɛɛ Halɔ kpeteŋkpele lɛ nɔ—Duromɔ lɛ, mikɛɛ, akɛɛ, Osɔfonukpa ni yɔɔ imme lɛ mli lɛ gbeleɔ ban the imme the; ni ji, ni ekɛ eniji ni atsɔɔ nɔ kɛ nɔ ni atsɔɔ nɔ lɛ baawo tsɛnsi lɛ mli dani dãa ni ajɔɔ lɛ lɛ abɔle ehe. Beni ena akɛ akɛ lɛ ema Osɔfo lɛ masɛi, ni ekadi nifeemɔ lɛ, ni esusu ehe—ni eji lɛlɛ ko nɔ Kapten lɛ—akɛ eyɛ hegbɛ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ fe ŋshɔkpɔ nɔ Maŋtsɛ ko kɛkɛ, titri lɛ yɛ Maŋtsɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ shia lɛ—Kapten lɛ kɛ mlijɔlɛ tee nɔ efɔ eniji ahe yɛ punchbowl fingge supper lɛ mli;-taking a huglass I. Queequeg kɛɛ akɛ: “Amrɔ nɛɛ, nɔ ni okɛtinkiɔ amrɔ nɛɛ?—Ani wɔmaŋbii lɛ eŋmɔɔɔ?”

Naagbee lɛ, wɔwo gbɛfaa lɛ he nyɔmɔ, ni wɔwo jatsui lɛ shweshweeshwe, ni wɔdamɔ lɛlɛ bibioo lɛ mli. Beni ewó lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, efo kɛtee Acushnet faa lɛ nɔ. Yɛ fa kome lɛ, New Bedford te shi yɛ gbɛjegbɛi ni yɔɔ ŋwɛitsu nɔ lɛ anɔ, ni amɛtsei ni ais ehà nɔ lɛ fɛɛ miikpɛ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli tse ni mli ejɔ lɛ mli. Akɛ gɔji wuji kɛ gɔji ni akɛ tsɛnsii ni yɔɔ tsɛnsii anɔ lɛ ebɔ he yɛ e-lɛlɛdaamɔhei lɛ anɔ, ni ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛi ni nyiɛɔ je lɛŋ fɛɛ lɛ kã shi dioo ni amɛma shi shweshweeshwe yɛ naagbee lɛ; yɛ be mli ni kapintɛfoi kɛ dadefeelɔi agbɛɛmɔ jɛɔ mɛi krokomɛi adɛŋ, kɛ la kɛ dadei ni akɛfutuɔ nii ni akɛshãa ŋtayaa lɛ mli gbɛɛmɔi, ni fɛɛ tsɔɔ akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi heei miije shishi; akɛ gbɛfaa kome ni yɔɔ oshara fe fɛɛ ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ lɛ ba naagbee lɛ, jeɔ sɛkɛndi kome pɛ shishi; ni nɔ ni ji enyɔ lɛ ba naagbee, nɔ ni ji etɛ lɛ pɛ jeɔ shishi, kɛyaa nɔ, kɛyaa naanɔ kɛya naanɔ. Nakai ji naagbee ni bɛ, hɛɛ, shikpɔŋ nɔ mɔdɛŋbɔɔ fɛɛ ni anyɛɛɛ akpɛlɛ nɔ lɛ.

Beni enine shɛɔ nu ni mli tse babaoo lɛ nɔ lɛ, kɔɔyɔɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ bafee ehee; Moss bibioo lɛ shwieɔ oyaiyeli ni jɛɔ e-bows lɛ mli lɛ, tamɔ teji gbekɛ ni shwieɔ e-snortings lɛ. Kwɛ bɔ ni mikɛ nakai Tartar kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ shwie shi!—kwɛ bɔ ni mikɛ nakai shikpɔŋ ni tsɔɔ gbɛjegbɛ nɔ lɛ shwie shi!—nakai gbɛjegbɛ wulu ni mɛi fɛɛ fɔɔ nɔ lɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ eyi kɛ nyɔŋyeli naji kɛ tsitsii ahe okadii; ni etsɔ mihe koni mikɛjie ŋshɔ lɛ dalɛ ni baaŋmɛ gbɛ ni aŋmala nɔ ko nɔ ko lɛ he miishɛɛ.

Yɛ nakai foam-nubu lɛ nɔŋŋ mli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni Queequeg kɛ mi nuɔ nu ni ekɛ mi shwieɔ nu. Ehiŋmɛii ni mli ewo duŋ lɛ shwie shi; ejie enyanyɔŋ ni efilɛ kɛ enyanyɔŋ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ kpo. On, on wɔfo; ni wɔ offing lɛ ná, Moss lɛ wo tswaa lɛ hiɛ nyam; shwie shi ni ekɛ ekpɔlɔi lɛ shwie shi akɛ nyɔŋ yɛ Sultan lɛ hiɛ. Wɔkɛ wɔhe fɔ̃ɔ afã, ni wɔjo foi kɛtee afã; kpãa fɛɛ kpãa ni tswaa tamɔ waya; ŋshɔkei enyɔ ni kɛlɛ ni shwieɔ shi tamɔ Indiabii akutsei ni yɔɔ shikpɔŋ nɔ ahum ni tswaa lɛ mli lɛ. Wɔyi obɔ kɛ nɛkɛ shihilɛ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli nɛɛ, beni wɔdamɔ bowsprit ni miiho lɛ masɛi lɛ, aahu akɛ wɔyɔseee gbɛfalɔi lɛ ahiŋmɛii ni kɛ ŋmɔlɔ kwɛɔ wɔ, ni ji kuu ko ni tamɔ lubber, ni naa kpɛɔ amɛhe akɛ esa akɛ nanemɛi adesai enyɔ afee nanemɛi nɛkɛ; tamɔ nɔ ni blɔfonyo ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ bulɛ fe mɔ diŋ ni awo lɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ. Shi boobies kɛ bumpkins komɛi yɛ jɛmɛ, ni, yɛ amɛ ŋmɔshi nibii ni mli wa lɛ hewɔ lɛ, ekolɛ amɛjɛ ŋmɔji fɛɛ tsui kɛ amɛteŋgbɛ. Queequeg mɔ nɛkɛ tsei bibii nɛɛ ateŋ ekome ni ekaseɔ lɛ yɛ esɛɛ. Misusu akɛ bumpkin lɛ hiɛkpatamɔ ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ eba. Beni ekɛ e-harpoon lɛ shwie shi lɛ, kooloo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ mɔ lɛ yɛ eniji amli, ni kɛtsɔ ŋaa kɛ hewalɛ ni miihe afee naakpɛɛ nɔ lɛ, etsu lɛ kɛtee ŋwɛi shɔɔŋ yɛ gbɔmɔtso mli kɛtee kɔɔyɔɔ mli; no sɛɛ lɛ, beni etswa esɛɛ fioo yɛ somerset teŋgbɛ lɛ, naanyo lɛ gbee shi kɛ fɛ̃i ni tswaa yɛ enaji anɔ, yɛ be mli ni Queequeg, ni etsɔ esɛɛ ekwɛ lɛ lɛ, shã e-tomahawk paipi lɛ ni ekɛha mi koni mikɛfutu.

"Kapting! Kapting!" bumpkin lɛ bolɔ, ni ejo foi kɛtee nakai asraafonyo nukpa lɛ ŋɔɔ; “Kapting, Kapting, abonsam nɛ.”

“Hallo, bo onukpa,” Kapten lɛ, ni ji ŋshɔ lɛ mli fã ko ni egbɔjɔ lɛ bolɔ, ni enyiɛɔ Queequeg ŋɔɔ, “mɛɛ ŋwɛi ŋwɛi otsɔɔ yɛ enɛ mli? Ani oleee akɛ ekolɛ ogbe nakai nuu lɛ?”

“Mɛni ekɛɛ?” Queequeg kɛɛ, beni ekɛ blɛoo tsɔ ehe kɛba miŋɔɔ lɛ.

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Ekɛɛ akɛ obɛŋkɛ kill-e nakai nuu lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ,” ni ekɛ enine tsɔɔ greenhorn ni miikpokpo lolo lɛ nɔ.

“Gbe-e,” Queequeg bolɔ, ni etsɔ ehiɛ ni atɛŋ yɛ ehe lɛ mli kɛfee heguɔgbee kpojiemɔ ni jeee shikpɔŋ nɔ nɔ, “ah! lɛ bevy loo bibioo-e; Queequeg egbeee-e loo bibioo-e; Queequeg gbe-e ŋshɔŋloo agbo!”

Kapten lɛ bolɔ akɛ: “Kwɛmɔ bo, magbe bo , gbɔmɔyelɔ, kɛji oka onifeemɔi lɛ ekomɛi okwɛ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli yɛ biɛ; no hewɔ lɛ kwɛmɔ ohiŋmɛi jogbaŋŋ.”

Shi eba mli yɛ nakai beaŋ pɛpɛɛpɛ, ni be eshɛ ni Kapten lɛ baasusu lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehiŋmɛi he. Nɔ ni tsii waa ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ wulu lɛ nɔ lɛ egba kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ he wolo lɛ mli, ni agbɛnɛ boom kpeteŋkpele lɛ miiho kɛmiijɛ he kome kɛmiiya he kroko, ni ekɛmiifo lɛlɛ lɛ fã fɛɛ sɛɛ kwraa. Ohianyo ni Queequeg kɛ lɛ ye basabasa waa lɛ, shwie lɛ kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ mli; niji fɛɛ yɛ gbeyeishemɔ mli; ni akɛ aaabɔ mɔdɛŋ ni aŋɔ boom lɛ koni akɛtsi enaa lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni eji shwilafofeemɔ. Efo kɛjɛ ninejurɔgbɛ kɛtee abɛkugbɛ, ni eku esɛɛ ekoŋŋ, nɔ ni miihe ashɛ yɛ watsi kome mli, ni be fɛɛ be lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ebaatswa ni etsɔ fãi bibii. Afeee nɔ ko nɔ ko, ni etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ni abaanyɛ afee; mɛi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ lɛ kɛ oyaiyeli tee ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni amɛdamɔ shi ni amɛmiikwɛ boom lɛ tamɔ nɔ ni eji ŋshɔŋloo ko ni mli efu waa lɛ shishigbɛ tsitsi. Yɛ nɛkɛ haomɔ nɛɛ mli lɛ, Queequeg gbee shi yɛ ŋaa gbɛ nɔ kɛba enaji anɔ, ni efolɔ boom lɛ gbɛ lɛ shishi, emɔ kpãa ko mli, ekɛ naagbee kome fĩ gbogboi lɛ ahe, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekɛ ekroko lɛ shwie shi tamɔ lasso, emɔ lɛ ebɔle boom lɛ he beni efoɔ yɛ eyitso ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ ehe miitswa, helemɔ. Akɛ skun lɛ jo foi kɛtee kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli, ni beni niji lɛ miijie lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, Queequeg, ni ejie ehe atadei kɛyashɛ etsitsi nɔ lɛ, kɛ ŋwɛi ŋta kakadaŋŋ ni hiɛ ka lɛ jo foi kɛjɛ sɛɛgbɛ. Ana lɛ minitii etɛ loo nɔ ni fe nakai ni efoɔ nu tamɔ tsina, ni ekɛ eniji kakadaji lɛ shwieɔ shi tɛ̃ɛ yɛ ehiɛ, ni ekɛ ehe jieɔ etsitsii ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ kpo kɛtsɔɔ fɛ̃i ni mli edɔ lɛ mli. Mikwɛ gbɔmɔ wulu ni yɔɔ nyam lɛ, shi minaaa mɔ ko mɔ ko ni abaahere lɛ yiwala. No mli lɛ, greenhorn lɛ eba shi. Beni Queequeg tu ehe tu kɛjɛ nu lɛ mli lɛ, agbɛnɛ ekwɛ ehe be kukuoo ko, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ena bɔ ni saji lɛ yɔɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ lɛ, efo shi ni elaaje. Minitii fioo komɛi krokomɛi, ni ete shi ekoŋŋ, ni enine kome miitswa lolo, ni ekɛ ekroko lɛ miigba su ko ni wala bɛ mli lɛ naa. Etsɛɛɛ ni lɛlɛ lɛ hole amɛ. Akɛ bumpkin ohiafo lɛ ba ekoŋŋ. Niji fɛɛ hala Queequeg akɛ trump ni nɔ kwɔ; lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ kpa lɛ fai ni ekɛke lɛ. Kɛjɛ nakai ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ nɔ kɛbaa nɛɛ, mikɛ mihe woɔ Queequeg mli tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo; hɛɛ, kɛyashi be mli ni Queequeg ohiafo lɛ tsɔ enaagbee ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔ kakadaŋŋ lɛ mli.

Ani jwɛŋmɔ ni bɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ eba pɛŋ? Etamɔ nɔ ni esusuuu akɛ esa kwraa akɛ aná shika kɛjɛ Humane kɛ Magnanimous Societies lɛ adɛŋ. Nu pɛ ebi—nu kpakpa—nɔ ko ni akɛbaatsumɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni yɔɔ mli lɛ; ni efee nakai lɛ, ewo atadei ni egbi, etswa epaipi lɛ, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ gbogboi lɛ anɔ, ni ekɛ hiŋmɛii kwɛɔ mɛi ni ebɔle ehe lɛ blɛoo, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛɛ ehe akɛ—“Eji jeŋ ni mɛi fɛɛ kɛ amɛhe feɔ ekome, yɛ meridians fɛɛ mli. Esa akɛ wɔ gbɔmɛi ni yeɔ gbɔmɔ lɛ wɔye wɔbua Kristofoi nɛɛ.”





YITSƆƆ 14. Nantukɛti.

Nɔ ko bako fe nakai yɛ kuku lɛ nɔ ni sa akɛ atsĩ tã; no hewɔ lɛ, beni wɔjo foi jogbaŋŋ sɛɛ lɛ, wɔyashɛ Nantucket shweshweeshwe.

Nantukɛt! Jiemɔ oshikpɔŋ he mfoniri lɛ ni okwɛ. Naa bɔ ni je lɛŋ koŋ diɛŋtsɛ ji lɛ; bɔ ni edamɔ jɛmɛ, yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa shɔŋŋ, ni efee ekome fe Eddystone kanetsu lɛ. Kwɛmɔ lɛ—gɔŋ bibioo ko kɛkɛ, kɛ ŋã ni ebɔle ehe; ŋshɔnaa fɛɛ, ni sɛɛgbɛ ko bɛ he. Ŋã babaoo yɛ jɛmɛ fe bɔ ni okɛbaatsu nii yɛ afii nyɔŋmai enyɔ mli akɛ nɔ ni akɛtoɔ blotting wolo najiaŋ. Gamesome wights komɛi baakɛɛ bo akɛ esa akɛ amɛdu jwɛi yɛ jɛmɛ, amɛkwɛɛɛ yɛ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe; akɛ amɛkɛ Canada ŋmeii baa; akɛ esa akɛ amɛtsu kɛya ŋshɔi lɛ asɛɛ koni amɛyatao spile ko ni baatsi nu ni hoɔ yɛ mu tsɛnsi mli lɛ naa; akɛ ajieɔ tsei ni yɔɔ Nantucket lɛ amli fãi tamɔ anɔkwa krɔs lɛ mli fãi ni yɔɔ Roma lɛ; ni mɛi ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ duɔ ŋmeii yɛ amɛshiai ahiɛ, koni amɛbote hulutsoo be mli; akɛ jwɛi ŋmei kome feɔ oasis, ŋmei etɛ yɛ gbi kome nyiɛmɔ mli lɛ feɔ ŋmɔ; akɛ amɛwoɔ quicksand tokotai, nɔ ko tamɔ Laplander snoo tokotai; ni amɛtsi amɛhe, amɛbɔle amɛhe, amɛtsi amɛhe gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ, amɛbɔle amɛhe, ni amɛfee amɛ ŋshɔkpɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa kwraa, ni bei komɛi lɛ, abaana ŋshɔŋlooi bibii ni kɛ amɛhe kpɛtɛɔ amɛ sɛii kɛ okpɔlɔi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ahe, tamɔ ŋshɔ mli ŋshɔŋlooi asɛɛ. Shi nɛkɛ nifeemɔi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɛɛ tsɔɔ pɛ akɛ Nantucket jeee Illinois.

Agbɛnɛ kwɛmɔ naakpɛɛ kusum sane ni kɔɔ bɔ ni red-men lɛ ba ŋshɔkpɔ nɛɛ nɔ lɛ he lɛ. Nakai ji bɔ ni abɛbua lɛ yaa nɔ. Yɛ blema beaŋ lɛ, okropɔŋ ko shwie shi kɛba New England ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni ewo Indianyo abifao ko yɛ enajiaŋ. Fɔlɔi lɛ kɛ awerɛho ni naa wa na amɛbi lɛ ni ajie amɛ kɛjɛ hiŋmɛi nɔ yɛ nu ni mli kwɔ lɛ nɔ. Amɛkpɛ amɛyiŋ akɛ amɛbaanyiɛ gbɛ kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ sɛɛ. Beni amɛje gbɛ yɛ amɛlɛlɛi lɛ amli lɛ, yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ oshara nɔ lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, amɛyana ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ, ni amɛyana shwuɔ wu adeka ko ni nɔ ko bɛ mli yɛ jɛmɛ, —Indianyo bibioo ohiafo lɛ fãji.

Belɛ, mɛɛ naakpɛɛ sane ji akɛ, esa akɛ nɛkɛ Nantucketbii nɛɛ, ni afɔ́ amɛ yɛ ŋshɔnaa lɛ aya ŋshɔ lɛ naa koni amɛyaná amɛshihilɛ! Amɛmɔ ŋshɔŋlooi kɛ quohogs klɛŋklɛŋ yɛ ŋã lɛ mli; beni amɛná ekãa lɛ, amɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi shwieɔ ŋshɔŋ loo ni ji mackerel lɛ nɔ; ni amɛyɔɔ niiashikpamɔ babaoo lɛ, amɛtsi amɛhe kɛtee lɛlɛi amli ni amɛmɔmɔ cod; ni naagbee lɛ, ekɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi wuji akuu ko tee ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ, ni ekwɛ nu mli je nɛɛ mli; okɛ ŋwɛibɔfoi akuu ni bɛ naagbee lɛ abɔle he; kwɛ Behring Ŋshɔ lɛ mli; ni yɛ be fɛɛ mli kɛ ŋshɔi fɛɛ amli lɛ, ejaje naanɔ ta kɛ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ wala ni he wa fe fɛɛ ni eye wala yɛ nu afua lɛ mli lɛ; nɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ kɛ gɔji fe fɛɛ! Nakai Himmalehan, ŋshɔ mli Mastodon ni yɔɔ ŋmei mli lɛ, ni ewo hewalɛ ni bɛ jwɛŋmɔ he okadi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ, ni eshe gbeyei waa lɛ ji nɔ ni esa akɛ ashe gbeyei fe etutuamɔi ni bɛ gbeyeishemɔ kɛ jwɛŋmɔ fɔŋ fe fɛɛ lɛ!

Ni nakai ji bɔ ni nɛkɛ Nantucketbii ni bɛ atadei nɛɛ, nɛkɛ ŋshɔ mli mɛi ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ, ni jɛɔ amɛ ant-hill ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ mli lɛ, eye nu mli je lɛ nɔ kunim ni eye nɔ kunim tamɔ Alexanderbii babaoo; ni amɛjara Atlantik, Pasifik, kɛ India ŋshɔi lɛ amli amɛwo amɛteŋ, taakɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ojotswalɔi ahewalɛi etɛ lɛ fee Poland lɛ. Ha Amerika kɛ Mexico afata Texas he, ni ekɛ Cuba ato Canada nɔ; ha Ŋleshibii lɛ aye India fɛɛ nɔ, ni amɛkɛ amɛ aflaŋaa ni tsoɔ lɛ afɔ̃ hulu lɛ nɔ; nɛkɛ shikpɔŋ ni yɔɔ shikpɔŋ nɔ nɛɛ mlijaa etɛ mli enyɔ ji Nantucketer nɔ. Ejaakɛ ŋshɔ lɛ ji lɛ; lɛ ji mɔ ni yɔɔ, taakɛ Maŋtsɛmɛi yɔɔ maŋtsɛyelii lɛ; ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi krokomɛi ni yɔɔ hegbɛ pɛ ni amɛtsɔɔ mli. YITSƆƆ 16. Lɛlɛ lɛ.

Wɔto gbɛjianɔi ni wɔto kɛha enɔ jetsɛremɔ lɛ yɛ saatso lɛ nɔ. Shi nɔ ni fee mi naakpɛɛ ni jeee nɔ ko bibioo ni haoɔ mi ji akɛ, Queequeg ha minu shishi agbɛnɛ, akɛ ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ miisusu Yojo—enyɔŋmɔ bibioo diŋ lɛ gbɛi lɛ he—ni Yojo ekɛɛ lɛ shii enyɔ loo etɛ fe nakai, ni ema nɔ mi waa yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ, akɛ, yɛ nɔ najiaŋ ni wɔbaafee ekome kɛya ŋshɔŋ loofɔji lɛ ateŋ lɛ, ŋshɔŋ loofɔji lɛ amli ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ; yɛ enɛ najiaŋ lɛ, mikɛɛ akɛ, Yojo kɛ hiɛdɔɔ fã akɛ esa akɛ lɛlɛ lɛ halamɔ lɛ akã minɔ kwraa, yɛ be mli ni Yojo eto eyiŋ akɛ ekɛ wɔ baafee naanyo lɛ; ni, bɔni afee ni efee nakai lɛ, ekɛ ehe ema tsɛnsi ko nɔ momo, ni kɛji akɛ ashi lɛ aha mi diɛŋtsɛ mihe lɛ, mi, Ishmael, baatswa nɔ ni tɔmɔ ko bɛ he, ejaakɛ je lɛ fɛɛ tamɔ nɔ ni eba trukaa; ni esa akɛ mikɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ mihe awo nakai lɛlɛ lɛ mli amrɔ nɔŋŋ, kɛha ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ ni ekɔɔɔ Queequeg he.

Mihiɛ ekpa nɔ akɛ matsĩ tã akɛ, yɛ nibii babaoo mli lɛ, Queequeg kɛ ehe fɔ̃ Yojo kojomɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ kɛ nibii ahe gbalɛ ni haa mɔ naa kpɛɔ ehe lɛ nɔ waa; ni ekwɛ Yojo waa kɛ bulɛ babaoo, akɛ nyɔŋmɔ ko ni hi jogbaŋŋ, ni ekolɛ etsɔɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ jogbaŋŋ, shi yɛ shihilɛi fɛɛ amli lɛ eyeee omanye yɛ ejurɔfeemɔ gbɛjianɔtoi lɛ amli.

Nitsumɔ ko ni hi jogbaŋŋ, shi yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, eji nɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho fe fɛɛ! Akɛ nakai sane lɛ taa nibii fɛɛ ni nɔ kwɔlɔ ahe.

Agbɛnɛ beni mikwɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fã lɛ he fɛɛ, koni mana mɔ ko ni yɔɔ hegbɛ, koni mikɛ mihe aha akɛ mɔ ni baahala ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfaa lɛ, shishijee lɛ minaaa mɔ ko mɔ ko; shi minyɛɛɛ makwɛ buu ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, loo wigwam, ni ama yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔne wulu lɛ sɛɛ fioo lɛ nɔ jogbaŋŋ. Eyɛ tamɔ ŋwɛi ŋta, ni elɛɛmɔ aaafee ninetalɔi nyɔŋma; ni ji limber black bone ni kɛlɛ ni ji wuji wuji ni ajie kɛjɛ right-whale lɛ tsitsii lɛ ateŋgbɛ kɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi fe fɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ yɔɔ mli. Akɛ amɛnaagbee ni mli kwɔ lɛ wo lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ, nɛkɛ tɛi nɛɛ ahe kooloo ko ni akɛkpɛtɛ he, ni amɛteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ shwie ehe, ni yɛ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ, efee ekome yɛ he ko ni ebɔle ehe, he ni yitsɔi ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ shwieɔ kɛyaa kɛbaa tamɔ ŋwɛi ŋta ni yɔɔ Pottowottamie Sachem yitso ko nɔ lɛ. Eta sɛi ko ni akɛ tso fee ni etsɛ waa lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ nibii ni akpɛ́ ni haa mɔ taoɔ ele nɔ ni yaa nɔ lɛ miihoso ehe fɛɛ; ni shishigbɛ lɛ ji nɔ ko ni akɛ elastic nibii ni akɛma wigwam lɛ nɔŋŋ fee lɛ.

Nɔ ko bɛ ni yɔɔ srɔto kwraa tamɔ nɛkɛ, ekolɛ, yɛ nuu onukpa ni mina lɛ hefɛo lɛ he; eyɛ blɔfo ni eyɛ tsitsi, tamɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi onukpai babaoo, ni ekɛ bluu pilot-cloth, ni akpɛ yɛ Quaker gbɛ nɔ lɛ ebɔle ehe waa; nɔ pɛ ni yɔɔ ji akɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ko ni hi jogbaŋŋ ni miihe afee nɔ ni anaaa lɛ ji ŋshɔŋlooi bibii ni ebɔle ehiŋmɛii ahe, ni esa akɛ ejɛ efãa gbɛ ni eyaa nɔ yɛ ahum ni naa wa babaoo mli, ni ekwɛɔ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ be fɛɛ be lɛ mli;—ejaakɛ enɛ haa fãji ni bɔleɔ hiŋmɛii lɛ ahe lɛ feɔ ekome. Hiŋmɛi-ŋmeii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ tsuɔ nii jogbaŋŋ waa yɛ ŋmɔlɔ mli.

“Ani Pequod lɛ Kapten lɛ nɛ?” mikɛɛ, ni mitee hiɛ kɛtee buu lɛ shinaa lɛ naa.

"Oji, ani oji? Mina akɛ jeee Nantucketer ji bo—obote stof lɛlɛ mli pɛŋ?"
