YITSƆƆ 55. Yɛ Whale Mfonirii ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ ahe.

Mikɛ mfoniri ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ baaha bo bɔ ni mɔ ko baanyɛ afee ni ekɛ kanvas tsuuu nii, nɔ ko tamɔ bɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ji diɛŋtsɛ lɛ bɔ ni ejieɔ ehe kpo ehaa yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hiŋmɛi mli beni yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ egbɔmɔtso muu lɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ fĩ ŋshɔŋloo lɛlɛ lɛ masɛi bɔni afee ni anyɛ atsɔ enɔ jogbaŋŋ yɛ jɛmɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, ekolɛ ebaafee nɔ ni sa akɛ atswa nakai mfonirii ni asusuɔ he ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli ni kɛ hekɛnɔfɔɔ teɔ shikpɔŋ nɔ gbɔmɔ lɛ hemɔkɛyeli lɛ shi kɛbashi ŋmɛnɛ po lɛ he adafi dani. Be eshɛɛ ni abaajaje je lɛ yɛ sane nɛɛ mli, kɛtsɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he mfonirii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni abaatsɔɔ akɛ ejaaa lɛ fɛɛ nɔ.

Ekolɛ abaana nakai mfonirifeemɔ mli lakamɔi lɛ fɛɛ jɛɛhe titri lɛ yɛ Hindubii, Mizraimbii, kɛ Helabii amagai ni etsɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ ateŋ. Ejaakɛ kɛjɛ nakai bei ni afee nibii heei shi amɛbɛ jwɛŋmɔ kpakpa lɛ amli beni yɛ sɔlemɔtsui lɛ amli tɛi ni jara wa, amagai lɛ ashishitoo, kɛ gãimlibii, shika tsuru, kɔɔpoi, kɛ shika nɔ lɛ, akɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai ni akɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai fee tamɔ Saladin nɔ lɛ, kɛ yitso ni akɛ dadefai ewo mli tamɔ St. George nɔ lɛ gbalaa dolphin lɛ; kɛjɛ nakai beaŋ kɛbaa nɛɛ, nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ gbɛŋmɛɛ wolo lɛ eye kunim, jeee yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he mfonirii ni ehe gbɛi babaoo lɛ amli pɛ, shi moŋ yɛ jeŋ nilee mli saji babaoo ni ajieɔ lɛ kpo yɛ ehe lɛ amli.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, blema mfoniri ni yɔɔ fe fɛɛ ni tsɔɔ akɛ eji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni anaa yɛ bu-pagoda ni ehe gbɛi waa ni atsɛɔ lɛ Elephanta, yɛ India lɛ mli. Brahminbii lɛ kɛɔ akɛ yɛ nakai blema sɔlemɔtsu lɛ mli amagai ni miihe ashɛ naagbee ni bɛ naagbee lɛ amli lɛ, jarayeli kɛ nibii fɛɛ ni afeɔ, gbɔmɔ nitsumɔ fɛɛ nitsumɔ ni anyɛɔ asusuɔ he lɛ ji nɔ ni atsɔɔ hiɛ atsɔɔ afii babaoo dani amɛteŋ eko ba diɛŋtsɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, ebɛ naakpɛɛ akɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, kulɛ esa akɛ wɔ nitsumɔ ni nɔ kwɔ ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ lɛ afee nɔ ni ajie lɛ kpo yɛ jɛmɛ. Hindoo ŋshɔŋloo ni awieɔ he lɛ, baa yɛ gbogbo lɛ fã ko ni yɔɔ srɔto mli, ni etsɔɔ Vishnu gbɔmɔtso ni ba yɛ leviathan su mli, ni nikasemɔ le lɛ akɛ Matse Avatar. Shi eyɛ mli akɛ nɛkɛ amaga nɛɛ ji gbɔmɔ fã kɛ ŋshɔŋloo fã, bɔni afee ni akɛ sɛɛ mli nɔ lɛ tsitsi pɛ aha moŋ, shi kɛlɛ nakai efã bibioo lɛ fɛɛ ejaaa. Etamɔ anaconda tsitsi ni mli edɔ lɛ fe anɔkwa ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ nyam lɛ ashishifãi ni mli kwɔlɔ lɛ.

Shi yaa Galleries momo lɛ, ni okwɛ Kristofonyo mfonirifeelɔ kpeteŋkpele ko mfoniri ni kɔɔ loo nɛɛ he lɛ agbɛnɛ; ejaakɛ eyeee omanye fe Hindubii ni hi shi dani nu afua lɛ ba lɛ. Eji Guido mfoniri ni tsɔɔ Perseus ni ejie Andromeda kɛjɛ ŋshɔ mli kooloo loo ŋshɔŋloo lɛ dɛŋ lɛ. Nɛgbɛ Guido ná adebɔɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ tamɔ nɛkɛ he mfoniri lɛ kɛjɛ? Ni Hogarth hu, yɛ shihilɛ kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ ni efee yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-“Perseus Descending” lɛ mli lɛ haaa nɔ ko nɔ ko afee nɔ ni hi fe no. Nakai Hogarthian kooloo wulu lɛ gbɔmɔtso wulu lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ, ni eshɛɛɛ ni ekɛ nu ni shɛɔ inchi kome po shwieɔ shi. Eyɛ howdah ko yɛ esɛɛ, ni enaabu ni mli egbɛ eshwa ni ŋshɔkei lɛ miiho kɛmiibote mli lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛya Traitors’ Gate ni jɛɔ Thames kɛtsɔɔ nu nɔ kɛyaa Mɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ. Agbɛnɛ hu, Prodromus ŋshɔŋlooi ni ji Scotch Sibbald blema lɛ, kɛ Jonah ŋshɔŋlooi, taakɛ atsɔɔ mli yɛ Bibliai momoi ni akala kɛ shishijee woji momoi ni afolɔ mli lɛ hu yɛ. Mɛni abaakɛɛ yɛ enɛɛmɛi ahe? Yɛ wolo-ŋmalɔ lɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni bɔleɔ ehe tamɔ wain tso ni bɔleɔ ŋshɔŋloo ni baa shi lɛ he lɛ he lɛ—tamɔ bɔ ni akadi ni akɛ shika tsuru ewula woji babaoo ni etsɛ kɛ ehee lɛ asɛɛ kɛ amɛyitso baafai lɛ ahe lɛ—no ji adebɔɔ ko ni yɔɔ fɛo waa shi eji naakpɛɛ nii pɛpɛɛpɛ, ni akaseɔ, miŋɔ lɛ, kɛjɛ mfonirii ni tamɔ nakai ni yɔɔ nɔ lɛ amli. Eyɛ mli akɛ atsɛɔ lɛ dolphin yɛ jeŋ fɛɛ moŋ, shi kɛlɛ mitsɛɔ wolo-ŋmalɔ loo nɛɛ akɛ mɔdɛŋ ni abɔɔ koni atswa ŋshɔŋloo ko; ejaakɛ nakai ji bɔ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ beni ajie tsɔne nɛɛ kpo klɛŋklɛŋ kwraa lɛ. Italy shiɛlɔ momo ko ji mɔ ni kɛba yɛ he ko yɛ aaafee afii ohai 15 lɛ mli, yɛ Nikasemɔ He Shitee-kɛ-woo lɛ mli; ni yɛ nakai gbii lɛ amli, kɛyashi be ko ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ po lɛ, mɛi babaoo susuɔ akɛ dolphin ji Leviathan ko.

Yɛ blema woji komɛi amli mfonirii kɛ nibii krokomɛi ni akɛwulaa amɛhe lɛ amli lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ obaakpe nibii ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli waa yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he, he ni ŋshɔŋlooi srɔtoi fɛɛ, jets d’eau, nu jɛɛhei ni mli edɔ kɛ fɛ̃i, Saratoga kɛ Baden-Baden, jɛɔ ejwɛŋmɔ ni etɔɔɔ lɛ mli kɛbaa lɛ. Yɛ “Nikasemɔ Hiɛyaa” lɛ shishijee wolo lɛ yitso-baafa lɛ mli lɛ obaana ŋshɔŋlooi komɛi ni miitao amɛle nɔ ni yaa nɔ lɛ.

Shi kɛ wɔkpa nɛkɛ mɔdɛŋbɔɔi ni jeee ŋaa nitsumɔ nɛɛ fɛɛ lɛ, nyɛhaa wɔkwɛa nakai leviathan mfonirii ni tsɔɔ akɛ eji jeŋ nilee mli mfonirii ni mɛi ni le lɛ fee lɛ. Yɛ Harris blema gbɛfaa he saji ni ebua naa lɛ mli lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ahe plɛtei komɛi yɛ ni ajie kɛjɛ Dutch gbɛfaa he wolo ko mli, AD 1671, ni yitso ji “Ŋshɔŋlooi Agbɛfaa kɛmiiya Spitzbergen yɛ lɛlɛ ni ji Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson ni jɛ Friesland, nuŋtsɔ lɛ mli.” Yɛ nakai plɛtei lɛ eko mli lɛ, afee ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ahe mfoniri, tamɔ tsei wuji ni akɛfee ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ, ni amɛkã ais-ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ ateŋ, ni ŋshɔŋlooi yɛŋ miijo foi kɛmiitsɔ amɛsɛɛ ni hiɛ ka lɛ nɔ. Yɛ plɛte kroko mli lɛ, afee blunder ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ akɛ eji ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ lɛ he mfonirifeemɔ.

Agbɛnɛ hu, quarto ko ni yɔɔ nyam waa yɛ, ni Kapten Colnett ko, ni ji Post Kapten yɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafoi lɛ amli lɛ ŋma, ni eyitso ji “Gbɛfaa kɛbɔle Cape Horn kɛmiiya Anaigbɛ Ŋshɔi lɛ amli, kɛ yiŋtoo akɛ abaaha Spermaceti Whale Fisheries lɛ aya hiɛ.” Yɛ wolo nɛɛ mli lɛ, ayɛ gbɛjianɔtoo ko ni akɛɛ eji “Physeter loo Spermaceti ŋshɔŋloo ko he mfoniri, ni akɛ sɛkɛl fee kɛjɛ eko ni agbe yɛ Mexico ŋshɔ lɛ naa, yɛ August, afi 1793, ni awo nɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ mli.” Miyiŋ ekɔɔɔ mihe akɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ ha ajie mfoniri ni ji anɔkwale nɛɛ koni e-ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafoi lɛ aná he sɛɛ. Kɛ matsĩ nɔ kome pɛ ni kɔɔ he lɛ tã lɛ, ha makɛɛ akɛ eyɛ hiŋmɛi ko ni kɛ akɛto, taakɛ sɛkɛl ni fata he lɛ tsɔɔ lɛ, kɛha sperm whale ni eda jogbaŋŋ lɛ, ebaaha nakai whale lɛ hiŋmɛi lɛ afee bow-window ni kɛlɛ ji aaafee ninetalɔi enumɔ. Ah, mi asraafonyo nukpa ni yɔɔ ekãa lɛ, mɛni hewɔ okɛ Yona ni kwɛɔ kɛmiijɛ nakai hiŋmɛi lɛ mli lɛ haaa wɔ!

Ni nakai nɔŋŋ hu jeee Adebɔɔ He Yinɔsane ni abua naa ni akɛ henilee tsuɔ nii fe fɛɛ kɛha oblahii kɛ oblayei ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ ahe sɛɛ, ni amɛye amɛhe kɛjɛ tɔmɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni tamɔ nɛkɛ he. Kwɛmɔ nakai wolo ni ehe gbɛi waa ni ji “Goldsmith Animated Nature” lɛ. Yɛ London wolo ni afo mli kuku ni je kpo yɛ afi 1807 lɛ mli lɛ, ayɛ plɛtei ni akɛɛ eji “whale” kɛ “narwhale” ko. Misumɔɔɔ ni mafee tamɔ mɔ ko ni bɛ fɛo, shi nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni bɛ fɛo nɛɛ tamɔ tsinayoo ni afo lɛ waa; ni, yɛ narwhale lɛ he lɛ, ekwɛmɔ fioo ko pɛ fa bɔ ni sa ni baaha mɔ ko naa akpɛ ehe, akɛ yɛ afii ohai 19 nɛɛ mli lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛ hippogriff ni tamɔ nɛkɛ awo skulbii ni yɔɔ nilee lɛ ateŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ nɔ akɛ eji anɔkwale.

Kɛkɛ ni, ekoŋŋ yɛ afi 1825 lɛ, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, ni ji adebɔɔ he nilelɔ kpeteŋkpele lɛ kala jeŋ nilee mli ŋshɔŋlooi ahe wolo ko ni ato he gbɛjianɔ, ni Leviathan ŋshɔŋlooi srɔtoi lɛ ahe mfonirii babaoo yɔɔ mli. Jeee akɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ ejaaa kɛkɛ, shi moŋ Mysticetus loo Greenland whale (ni ji, Right whale) lɛ mfoniri lɛ, Scoresby po, ni ji nuu ko ni yɔɔ niiashikpamɔ be kakadaŋŋ akɛ eta nakai ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he lɛ jajeɔ akɛ ebɛ nɔ ni tamɔ nakai yɛ adebɔɔ mli.

Shi akɛ cap-sheaf lɛ ni akɛwo nɛkɛ nitsumɔ ni ejaaa nɛɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ ji nɔ ni akɛto jeŋ nilelɔ Frederick Cuvier, ni ji Baron ni ehe gbɛi waa lɛ nyɛmi nuu lɛ pɛ he. Yɛ afi 1836 lɛ, ekala wolo ko ni ji Natural History of Whales, ni ekɛ nɔ ni etsɛɔ lɛ akɛ Sperm Whale lɛ he mfoniri ha yɛ mli. Dani obaajie nakai mfoniri lɛ kpo otsɔɔ Nantucketnyo ko lɛ, no lɛ ehi jogbaŋŋ akɛ ooofee gbɛjianɔ kɛha osɛɛkuu kuku kɛjɛ Nantucket. Yɛ wiemɔ kome mli lɛ, Frederick Cuvier Sperm Whale lɛ jeee Sperm Whale, shi moŋ eji ŋshɔŋloo. Eji anɔkwale akɛ, enaaa ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa lɛ he sɛɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ (hii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ efɔɔɔ namɔ), shi nɛgbɛ ená nakai mfoniri lɛ kɛjɛ, namɔ baanyɛ akɛɛ? Ekolɛ enine shɛ nɔ tamɔ bɔ ni jeŋ nilelɔ ni tsɔ ehiɛ yɛ nakai nitsumɔ lɛ nɔŋŋ mli, Desmarest, ná efɔfɔi diɛŋtsɛ lɛ ateŋ ekome lɛ; ni ji, kɛjɛ Chinese mfoniri ko mli. Ni mɛɛ gbekɛbii hii ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni hiɛ pensil lɛ nakai Chinesebii lɛ ji, queer cups kɛ saucers babaoo haa wɔleɔ.

Yɛ okadi-feelɔi lɛ a-whale ni anaa yɛ gbɛjegbɛi lɛ anɔ ni fĩɔ mu-hɔ̃ɔlɔi ashwapoi lɛ anɔ lɛ he lɛ, mɛni ji nɔ ni abaakɛɛ yɛ amɛhe? Bei pii lɛ, amɛji Richard III. ŋshɔŋlooi, ni yɔɔ dromedary tsitsii, ni amɛhe yɔɔ gbeyei waa; niyenii ni amɛyeɔ leebi yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi a-tart etɛ loo ejwɛ nɔ, ni ji ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi ni eyi kɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi: amɛhe ni amɛgbɔjɔ lɛ miiho yɛ ŋshɔi ni lá kɛ bluu pɛnte yɔɔ mli lɛ amli.

Shi nɛkɛ tɔmɔi srɔtoi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he mfonirifeemɔ mli nɛɛ efeee mɔ naakpɛɛ tsɔ yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ. Kwɛmɔ ekoŋŋ! Ajie jeŋ nilee mli mfonirii lɛ ateŋ babaoo kɛjɛ loo ni efo lɛ mli; ni enɛɛmɛi ja tamɔ bɔ ni lɛlɛ ni efite, ni esɛɛ efo lɛ he mfoniri ni afee lɛ baadamɔ shi aha kooloo ni nɔ kwɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ yɛ ehenɔwomɔ fɛɛ ni ji lɛlɛ kɛ eshishifãi ni efiteee lɛ mli lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ shwuɔi damɔ shi amɛha amɛhe fɛɛ moŋ, shi Leviathan ni hiɛ ka lɛ efoko ehe yɛ gbɛ ni ja nɔ kɛha emfoniri lɛ pɛŋ. Ŋshɔŋloo ni hiɛ ka lɛ, yɛ ehiɛ nyam kɛ eshishinumɔ fɛɛ mli lɛ, ji nɔ ni anaa pɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ yɛ nu ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ shishi mli; ni efã babaoo ni yɔɔ nu lɛ nɔ lɛ bɛ hiŋmɛi nɔ, tamɔ lɛlɛ ni akɛwo tawuu mli ni ajie lɛ kpo; ni kɛjɛ nakai nɔ lɛ mli lɛ eji nɔ ko ni nyɛŋ aba mli kɛya naanɔ akɛ gbɔmɔ ni gboɔ lɛ baanyɛ awó lɛ nɔ yɛ gbɔmɔtsoŋ kɛya kɔɔyɔɔ mli, koni ebu ehewalɛ ni efoɔ kɛ ehewalɛ ni efoɔ lɛ fɛɛ he. Ni, ni wɔkawie srɔtofeemɔ ni asusuɔ akɛ eji nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo bibioo ko ni nuɔ nu kɛ Plato Leviathan ni eda jogbaŋŋ lɛ teŋ lɛ he; shi kɛlɛ, yɛ nakai ŋshɔŋlooi bibii ni nuɔ nu ni awo amɛ kɛtee lɛlɛ mli lɛ ateŋ ekome po mli lɛ, nakai beaŋ lɛ, bɔ ni eyɔɔ srɔto, ni tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo, ni egbɔjɔ, ni esoro lɛ, ni ehiɛ su pɔtɛɛ ni abonsam diɛŋtsɛ nyɛɛɛ amɔ lɛ ji lɛ.

Shi abaanyɛ asusu he akɛ, kɛjɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni egbo lɛ fã ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko lɛ mli lɛ, abaanyɛ ana ŋaawoi ni ja ni kɔɔ bɔ ni eyɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ lɛ he. Jee nakai kraa. Ejaakɛ eji nibii ni haa mɔ taoɔ ele nɔ ni fe fɛɛ yɛ Leviatan nɛɛ he lɛ ateŋ ekome, akɛ egbɔmɔtso lɛ haaa bɔ ni eyɔɔ ha lɛ he susumɔ fioo ko pɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ Jeremy Bentham gbɔmɔtso lɛ, ni akɛkpɛtɛɔ kane he yɛ mɛi ni gbe lɛ lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome wojiatoohe lɛ jieɔ susumɔ ni ji nuumo ko ni hiɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni sumɔɔ nibii ni akɛtsuɔ nii, ni Jeremy gbɔmɔtsoŋ sui krokomɛi fɛɛ ni nyiɛɔ hiɛ lɛ yɔɔ mli lɛ kpo yɛ gbɛ ni ja nɔ moŋ; shi kɛlɛ anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli kɛjɛ leviathan wui ni yɔɔ fãi lɛ eko mli. Yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, taakɛ Hunter kpeteŋkpele lɛ kɛɛ lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ fãji kɛkɛ lɛ hiɛ wekukpaa kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ kɛ kooloo ni ekɛwo mli kwraa ni ekɛ ehe wo mli lɛ tamɔ bɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛ chrysalis ni ebɔle ehe lɛ he lɛ yɔɔ tsakpaa lɛ nɔŋŋ. Ajieɔ nɛkɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ srɔto nɛɛ kpo yɛ yitso lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni sa kadimɔ waa nɔ, taakɛ abaajie lɛ kpo yɛ wolo nɛɛ fã ko mli lɛ yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ. Ajieɔ lɛ kpo yɛ sɛɛgbɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hu mli yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ waa nɔ, ni nɔ ni miihe ashɛ ewui lɛ haa adesa nine wui lɛ ahetoo pɛpɛɛpɛ, ni ajieɔ ŋshɔŋloo bibioo lɛ pɛ kɛjɛɔ mli. Nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ yɛ wu-niji ejwɛ ni yɔɔ daa, ni ji tsɔɔlɔ, teŋgbɛ, ŋmɛlɛtswaa, kɛ nine bibioo. Shi enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ hiɔ amɛheloo ni akɛhaa amɛhe lɛ mli daa, tamɔ bɔ ni adesa niji yɔɔ nɔ ko ni akɛfee lɛ mli lɛ. Stubb ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ kɛɛ gbi ko akɛ: “Bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni bei komɛi lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ baanyɛ asɔmɔ wɔ yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ, anyɛɛɛ akɛɛ diɛŋtsɛ akɛ ekɛ wɔ tsuɔ nii ni ekɛ mittens tsuuu nii kɔkɔɔkɔ.”

No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ yiŋtoi nɛɛ fɛɛ ahewɔ lɛ, gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ ni oootsɔ nɔ okwɛ lɛ lɛ, esa akɛ omu sane naa akɛ Leviatan kpeteŋkpele lɛ ji nakai adebɔɔ kome ni yɔɔ je lɛŋ ni esa akɛ ahi shi ni akafee lɛ mfoniri kɛyashi naagbee lɛ. Eji anɔkwale akɛ, mfoniri kome baanyɛ abɛŋkɛ okadi lɛ he kwraa fe ekroko, shi eko kwraa nyɛŋ atswa lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ jogbaŋŋ. No hewɔ lɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ gbɛ ko bɛ ni aaatsɔ nɔ ale bɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ. Eyɛ mfoniri kome pɛ ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni agbeɔ lɛ ahe, ni enɛ ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ awerɛho ni faaa, ejaakɛ mfoniri ni tamɔ nɛkɛ pɛ nɔ, kɛji akɛ afee lɛ jogbaŋŋ kwraa lɛ, obaanyɛ oná nɔ ko tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni hiɛ ka lɛ ahe susumɔ ni ji anɔkwale taakɛ egbɔbilɔi ni hiɛ ka lɛ naa lɛ.

Shi, kɛ akɛ fɛɛ to he lɛ, nɔ ni hi fe fɛɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ fitsofitso komɛi amli lɛ, jeee nɔ ni ja fe fɛɛ, ni ajieɔ ŋshɔŋlooi kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nibii ni anaa yɛ he fɛɛ he lɛ akpo lɛ ji French mfonirii wuji enyɔ ni akpɛ́, ni afee lɛ jogbaŋŋ, ni ajie kɛjɛ mfonirii ni Garnery kome fee lɛ amli. Yɛ gbɛ kome nɔ lɛ, amɛdamɔ shi kɛha tutuamɔi ni akɛbaa Sperm kɛ Right Whale lɛ nɔ. Yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ mfoniri ni akpɛ́ lɛ mli lɛ, ajie Sperm Whale ko ni yɔɔ nyam lɛ kpo yɛ hewalɛ ni eyɔɔ mli lɛ mli, ni ete shi kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ shishi kɛkɛ kɛjɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli, ni ehiɛ tsɔne ni akɛshãa la lɛ mli tsei ni efite ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli yɛ esɛɛ. Lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ fã ko efoko, ni agbala lɛ ni ekɛ ehe kpãa gbee kɛkɛ yɛ kooloo wulu lɛ sɛɛgbɛ; ni kɛ́ odamɔ nakai ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ, yɛ nakai be kome pɛ ni anyɛɛɛ abu he akɔntaa lɛ mli lɛ, onaa lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko, ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mlifu ni mli ewo la lɛ ehà ehe fã, ni ekɛ ehe miiwo ŋshɔ lɛ mli, tamɔ nɔ ni ejɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli. Nɔ fɛɛ nɔ nifeemɔ lɛ hi jogbaŋŋ ni eji anɔkwale yɛ naakpɛɛ gbɛ nɔ. Line-tub ni fã efo mli lɛ hoɔ yɛ ŋshɔ ni efee futafuta lɛ nɔ; tsei ni akɛwoɔ harpoons ni ashwie shi lɛ mli lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ mli; mɛi ni shwieɔ nu lɛ ayitsei egbɛ eshwã ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he yɛ gbeyeishemɔ wiemɔi ni yɔɔ srɔtoi amli; yɛ be mli ni yɛ ahum diŋŋ ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ mli lɛ, lɛlɛ lɛ miiho kɛmiiba he ni eyɔɔ lɛ. Abaanyɛ ana tɔmɔ ko ni mli wa yɛ ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ he saji amli fitsofitso, shi ha no atsɔ mli; ejaakɛ, yɛ miwala be fɛɛ mli lɛ, minyɛɛɛ mafo eko ni hi tamɔ nɛkɛ.

Yɛ mfoniri ni ji enyɔ lɛ mli lɛ, lɛlɛ lɛ miifee mfoniri yɛ Right Whale agbo ko ni miijo foi lɛ sɛɛ, ni ekɛ e-weed black bulk lɛ shwieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli tamɔ tɛ ko ni mli edɔ ni jɛ Patagonia gɔji lɛ amli lɛ. E-jet lɛ damɔ shi, eyi obɔ, ni eyɛ ediŋŋ tamɔ sũ; bɔni afee ni kɛjɛ lasu ni fa babaoo yɛ lasu tsu lɛ mli lɛ, obaasusu akɛ esa akɛ gbɛkɛ niyenii ko ni yɔɔ ekãa ni ahoɔ yɛ shishigbɛ fɔmɔ kotokui wuji lɛ amli lɛ aba. Ŋshɔ mli loofɔji miiye ŋshɔŋlooi bibii, ŋshɔŋlooi, kɛ ŋshɔ mli ŋshɔŋlooi krokomɛi kɛ maccaroni, ni bei komɛi lɛ Right Whale lɛ woɔ esɛɛ ni hela lɛ emɔ lɛ lɛ. Ni be nɛɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, leviathan ni hiɛ enaabu ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ miiho kɛmiitsɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli, ni eshiɔ ŋshɔŋlooi yɛŋ ni feɔ basabasa tɔni abɔ yɛ esɛɛ, ni ehaa lɛlɛ bibioo lɛ hosoɔ yɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ amli tamɔ lɛlɛ bibioo ko ni emɔ ŋshɔ mli lɛlɛ ko ni akɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ shwieɔ shi lɛ shwiilii lɛ ahe. No hewɔ lɛ, nɔ ni yɔɔ hiɛ lɛ fɛɛ ji basabasafeemɔ ni mli wa waa; shi yɛ sɛɛgbɛ, yɛ nitɛŋmɔ he srɔtofeemɔ ni sa yijiemɔ mli lɛ, ŋshɔ ni efee dioo ni tamɔ glase lɛ, lɛlɛ ni bɛ hewalɛ lɛ mli ŋshɔkei ni bɛ hewalɛ ni shwieɔ shi lɛ, kɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni egbo ni bɛ hewalɛ, maŋtsɛwe ko ni aye nɔ kunim, ni mɔmɔ aflaŋaa lɛ fĩɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ tso lɛ he ni ekɛwoɔ e-spout-hole lɛ mli lɛ.

Namɔ Garnery mfonirifeelɔ lɛ ji, loo eji lɛ, mileee. Shi mishihilɛ kɛha lɛ lɛ, eji mɔ ko ni le esane lɛ jogbaŋŋ, loo ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nilelɔ ko ni yɔɔ niiashikpamɔ tsɔɔ lɛ yɛ naakpɛɛ gbɛ nɔ. Frenchbii lɛ ji gbekɛbii hii kɛha mfonirifeemɔ nifeemɔ. Yaa ni oyakwɛ Europa mfonirii lɛ fɛɛ, ni nɛgbɛ obaana basabasafeemɔ ni hiɛ ka ni miimu ni yɔɔ kanvas nɔ lɛ he mfonirifeemɔ he mfonirifeemɔ he mfonirifeemɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ yɛ nakai kunimyeli asa ni yɔɔ Versailles lɛ mli lɛ; he ni mɔ ni kwɛɔ lɛ lɛ wuɔ egbɛ, pell-mell, kɛtsɔɔ France tai wuji ni nyiɛɔ amɛsɛɛ lɛ anɔ; he ni klante fɛɛ klante tamɔ nɔ ni ji Kooyigbɛ Lai lɛ akpɛ́, ni maŋtsɛmɛi kɛ Maŋtsɛmɛi ni hiɛ tawuu nii ni nyiɛɔ amɛsɛɛ lɛ hoɔ oya kɛhoɔ, tamɔ centaurs ni awo amɛ akekerei lɛ? Jeee akɛ esaaa he ko kwraa yɛ nakai ŋwɛitsu lɛ mli, nɛkɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ tawuu-nibii ni yɔɔ Garnery nɛɛ.

Etamɔ nɔ ni Frenchbii lɛ adebɔɔ mli nyɛmɔ ni amɛkɛmɔɔ nibii ni yɔɔ fɛo lɛ ahe lɛ jeɔ kpo yɛ gbɛ krɛdɛɛ ko nɔ yɛ mfonirii kɛ nibii ni amɛkpɛ yɛ amɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni amɛgbeɔ lɛ ahe lɛ mli. Akɛni amɛbɛ England niiashikpamɔ yɛ ŋshɔŋ loomɔ mli lɛ mlijaa nyɔŋma mli ekome, ni amɛbɛ Amerikabii lɛ anɔ lɛ mlijaa akpe mli ekome hewɔ lɛ, amɛha maji enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ efee mfoniri pɛ ni agbe naa kwraa ni baanyɛ ajie ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔ mli mumɔ diɛŋtsɛ lɛ kpo. Yɛ bei babaoo mli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni Ŋleshibii kɛ Amerikabii ni feɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe mfoniri lɛ amii shɛ amɛhe kwraa akɛ amɛjieɔ nibii ni yɔɔ tsɔne mli lɛ akpo, tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ahe mfoniri ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko lɛ; ni, yɛ bɔ ni nɔ ni jɛɔ mli baa lɛ yɔɔ fɛo ha lɛ he lɛ, ekɛ piramid ko he mfonirifeemɔ yeɔ egbɔ. Scoresby po, ni ji Right whaleman ni ehe gbɛi yɛ gbɛ ni ja nɔ lɛ, beni ekɛ Greenland whale lɛ fã ni mli wa ni mli wa, kɛ narwhales kɛ porpoises bibii etɛ loo ejwɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo ha wɔ sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ lɛlɛ mli ŋmeii, ŋmeii ni akɛfoɔ nii, kɛ ŋmeii ni akɛfoɔ nii ahe mfonirii ni yɔɔ tsutsu lɛ feɔ wɔ; ni kɛ Leuwenhoeck ko hiɛdɔɔ ni akɛkwɛɔ nii bibii lɛ haa jeŋ ko ni hosoɔ lɛ mlikwɛmɔ ni ji fac-similes nyɔŋmai nɛɛhu kɛ enumɔ ni ji Arctic snoo kristalo ni ajieɔ lɛ kpo lɛ. Mitsɔɔɔ akɛ mibuuu gbɛfalɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ lɛ he guɔ (miwoɔ lɛ hiɛ nyam akɛ eji asraafonyo nukpa), shi yɛ sane ko ni he hiaa waa mli lɛ, eji nɔkwɛmɔ diɛŋtsɛ akɛ ahaaa kristalo fɛɛ kristalo wolo ni aka kitã ni akɛtee Greenland Toiŋjɔlɛ Kojolɔ ko hiɛ.

Kɛfata nakai mfonirii fɛfɛji ni jɛ Garnery lɛ ahe lɛ, French mfonirii krokomɛi enyɔ hu yɛ ni sa kadimɔ, ni mɔ ko ni kɛ ehe woɔ “H. Durand” mli lɛ fee. Eyɛ mli akɛ amɛteŋ ekome kɛ wɔyiŋtoo ni yɔɔ amrɔ nɛɛ kpãaa gbee pɛpɛɛpɛ moŋ, shi kɛlɛ esa akɛ atsĩ tã yɛ amaniɛbɔi krokomɛi amli. Eji shwane mli he ko ni yɔɔ dioo yɛ Pasifik ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ ateŋ; French whale lɛlɛ ko ma ŋshɔ lɛ naa, yɛ he ko ni yɔɔ dioo, ni ekɛ shwilafofeemɔ miiye nu kɛmiiya lɛlɛ lɛ mli; lɛlɛ lɛ mli tsɔnei ni ajie lɛ, kɛ tsei ahe baai kakadaji ni yɔɔ sɛɛ lɛ, ni fɛɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni kɔɔyɔɔ bɛ mli lɛ mli. Nɔ ni jɛɔ mli baa lɛ hi waa, kɛ asusu he yɛ bɔ ni ekɛ wuolɛɛmɛi ni he wa lɛ haa yɛ amɛ bokagbɛ hejɔɔmɔ lɛ fãi fioo lɛ ateŋ ekome shishi lɛ he. Nɔ kroko ni akpɛ́ lɛ ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto kwraa: lɛlɛ lɛ hove-to yɛ ŋshɔ ni mli tse lɛ nɔ, kɛ Leviathan shihilɛ lɛ mli diɛŋtsɛ, ni Right Whale ko yɛ emasɛi; lɛlɛ lɛ (yɛ nifeemɔ ni ji ŋmɔlɔ mli lɛ mli) shwieɔ shi kɛyaa kooloo wulu lɛ nɔ tamɔ nɔ ni eyaa ŋshɔkpɔ ko nɔ; ni lɛlɛ ko, ni miitsi ehe kɛmiijɛ nifeemɔ he nɛɛ oya lɛ, miitao ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ asɛɛtiumɔ. Harpoons kɛ lances lɛ kã shi shiŋŋ koni akɛtsu nii; mɛi etɛ ni fãa ŋshɔ lɛ miiwo lɛlɛ lɛ shishitoo lɛ mli yɛ ebu lɛ mli kɛkɛ; yɛ be mli ni kɛjɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli ni shwieɔ shi trukaa lɛ, lɛlɛ bibioo lɛ damɔ shi fã kɛjɛ nu lɛ mli, tamɔ okpɔŋɔ ni miikwɛ. Kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ni miihoso lɛ piŋmɔi lɛ ahe lasu lɛ miiya ŋwɛi tamɔ lasu ni yɔɔ dadefeelɔi akrowa ko nɔ lɛ; ni kɛya kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ he lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni atatu diŋŋ ko, ni teɔ shi kɛ ahum kɛ nugbɔshiimɔi ni naa wa lɛ haa ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi ni miishɛɛ yɔɔ amɛmli lɛ anitsumɔ yaa oya.





YITSƆƆ 57. Yɛ Whales ni yɔɔ Penti mli lɛ he; yɛ Nyanyɔŋ mli; yɛ Wood mli; yɛ Shiti-Dade mli; yɛ Tɛ mli; yɛ Gɔji lɛ amli; yɛ ŋulamii amli.

Yɛ Pasifik Ŋshɔ lɛ mli fɛɛ, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu yɛ Nantucket, kɛ New Bedford, kɛ Sag Harbor lɛ, obaana ŋshɔŋlooi kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe mfonirii ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ, ni wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ fee yɛ Sperm Whale-nyanyɔji anɔ, loo yei akutsei ni akɛ Right Whale-wui fee lɛ, kɛ nibii krokomɛi babaoo ni tamɔ nɛkɛ. ŋaa nibii ni amɛkɛ nibii ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ feɔ lɛ jogbaŋŋ, yɛ amɛ ŋshɔ mli hejɔɔmɔ ŋmɛlɛtswai lɛ amli. Amɛteŋ mɛi komɛi yɛ adekai bibii ni akɛ nibii ni tamɔ ŋmeii tsuɔ nii, ni ato he gbɛjianɔ titri kɛha skrimshandering nitsumɔ lɛ. Nyɔŋyeli ni akɛyeɔ be kakadaŋŋ kɛjɛɔ Kristendom kɛ jeŋba kpakpa mli lɛ haa gbɔmɔ kuɔ esɛɛ kɛyaa nakai shihilɛ ni Nyɔŋmɔ kɛwo mli lɛ mli ekoŋŋ, ni ji nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ akɛ awuiyeli. Ejaakɛ, kɛtsɔ ŋshɔ mli ŋshɔŋloo ko ni ekumɔ loo ŋshɔŋloo ko ŋmei fioo ko pɛ nɔ lɛ, aná nakai naakpɛɛ tso ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ; ni ekɛ afii babaoo ni akɛtsuɔ nii daa lɛ etsu nii.

Taakɛ eji yɛ Hawaii ŋmei lɛ he lɛ, nakai nɔŋŋ eji yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ blɔfo lɛ he. Kɛ tsuishitoo ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɛɛ nɔŋŋ, kɛ shark ŋmei kome lɛ nɔŋŋ, ni ji e-jack-knife kome ni ehiii lɛ, ebaakpɛ wu he mfoniri fioo ko aha bo, jeee tamɔ nitsulɔ ko kwraa, shi moŋ ekɛ ehe ebɔ he yɛ egbɛjianɔtoo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ mli, tamɔ Hela kooloo ni yɔɔ gbeyei, Achilles gãimlibii lɛ; ni eyi obɔ kɛ yiwalɛ mumɔ kɛ susumɔi, tamɔ nakai Dutch gbɔmɔ onukpa kpakpa ni ji Albert Durer lɛ mfonirii lɛ.

Bei pii lɛ, akɛ tso mli ŋshɔŋlooi, loo ŋshɔŋlooi ni afolɔ amɛhe kɛjɛ South Sea war-wood tsei bibii ni yɔɔ ediŋŋ lɛ amli lɛ kpeɔ yɛ Amerika ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛlɔi lɛ agbɛtsɔɔmɔi amli shii abɔ. Afee amɛteŋ ekomɛi pɛpɛɛpɛ babaoo.

Yɛ maŋ lɛ mli tsũi momo komɛi ni akɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai ebɔ he lɛ amli lɛ obaana akɔɔble ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛwo amɛtsitsi nɔ kɛha mɛi ni tswaa gbɛjegbɛ lɛ naa shinaa lɛ. Kɛ́ tsɔne kudɔlɔ lɛ ewɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ni hiɛ anvil yitso lɛ baafee nɔ ni hi fe fɛɛ. Shi nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni tswaa nɛɛ efɔɔɔ kaa akɛ anɔkwa niŋmaai. Yɛ sɔlemɔtsui komɛi ni yɔɔ blema lɛ anɔ lɛ, obaana ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛ dade fee ni akɛto jɛmɛ kɛha kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔi ahe ŋshɔŋlooi; shi amɛwo amɛhe nɔ waa, ni kɛfata he lɛ, amɛji yiŋtoi kɛ yiŋtoi fɛɛ ni aŋmala nɔ akɛ “ Hands off! ” lɛ, onyɛŋ opɛi amɛmli jogbaŋŋ bɔ ni sa ni okpɛ oyiŋ yɛ amɛhe sɛɛnamɔ he.

Yɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ hei ni wuji yɔɔ mli, he ni tɛi babaoo ni egbɛ eshwã shikpɔŋ kplanaa lɛ nɔ yɛ gɔji ni nɔ kwɔlɔ ni ekumɔ lɛ ashishi lɛ, bei pii lɛ obaana mfonirii tamɔ Leviathan henɔi ni etsɔ tɛi ni fã ko efee ekome kɛfee jwɛi, ni yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ gbi ko mli lɛ, efoɔ amɛ yɛ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji amli lɛ amli.

Agbɛnɛ hu, yɛ gɔji amli maji ni gbɛfalɔ lɛ kɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai ni nɔ kwɔlɔ lɛ fɔ̃ɔ ehe be fɛɛ be lɛ amli; biɛ kɛ biɛ kɛjɛ shihilɛ ko ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ mli lɛ obaana ŋshɔŋlooi ni atsɔɔ mli yɛ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkei lɛ ahe lɛ ahe mfonirii ni hoɔ. Shi esa akɛ ofee ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nilelɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ, koni ona nɛkɛ nibii nɛɛ; ni jeee nakai pɛ, shi kɛji oosumɔ ni oku osɛɛ oyakwɛ he ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ekoŋŋ lɛ, esa akɛ oná nɔmimaa ni okɛ latitude kɛ longitude ni kɛ oklɛŋklɛŋ shidaamɔhe lɛ kpeɔ lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ atsu nii, kɛjee nakai lɛ, gɔji lɛ anɔkwɛmɔi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ tamɔ nɔ ni baa trukaa, ni oshidaamɔhe pɔtɛɛ, tsutsu shidaamɔhe lɛ baabi ni ana ekoŋŋ ni nitsumɔ wa; tamɔ Soloma Ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ, ni ji nɔ ni aleee lolo lɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ be ko ni eho lɛ Mendanna ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ŋwɛi lɛ nyiɛ amɛnɔ ni Figuera onukpa lɛ ŋma amɛhe saji.

Ni kɛ́ osaneyitso lɛ wo bo nɔ babaoo lɛ, onyɛŋ otsi ohe kɛjɛ ŋshɔŋlooi wuji ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ni ŋulamii eyi mli obɔ lɛ amli, kɛ lɛlɛi ni nyiɛɔ amɛsɛɛ lɛ ahe; tamɔ be mli ni Bokagbɛ maji lɛ eyi kɛ ta he susumɔi be kakadaŋŋ lɛ na asraafoi ni atswa amɛnaa yɛ ta mli yɛ atatui lɛ ateŋ lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ Kooyigbɛ lɛ, mitiu Leviathan kɛbɔle Pole lɛ he kɛ ŋwɛi ŋta ni kpɛɔ ni tsɔɔ lɛ shishi klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ mli tsakemɔi. Ni yɛ Antarctica ŋwɛi ni kpɛɔ lɛ shishi lɛ, mibote Argo-Navis lɛ mli, ni mikɛ mihe ewo Cetus ni ŋulamii eyi mli obɔ lɛ sɛɛtiumɔ mli kɛtee Hydrus kɛ Flying Fish lɛ sɛɛ shɔɔŋ.

Kɛ miyɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ ko ni akɛwoɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ mli ŋshɔŋlooi kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛwoɔ ŋshɔŋ lɛ ahe ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ, ani manyɛ makwɔ nakai ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔ ni mafo ŋwɛi ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi fe fɛɛ lɛ, koni mana kɛji ŋwɛi ni awieɔ he yɛ adesã mli kɛ amɛbuui ni anyɛɛɛ akane lɛ fɛɛ lɛ ebɔ nsara diɛŋtsɛ yɛ he ko ni minyɛɛɛ mana!





YITSƆƆ 58. Britania.

Beni wɔkuɔ gbɛ kɛyaa kooyi-bokagbɛ kɛjɛɔ Crozetts lɛ, wɔboteɔ brit, ni ji nɔ ko bibioo ni ji ŋmɔŋ, ni Right Whale lɛ yeɔ nii babaoo yɛ nɔ lɛ mli. Ekɛ ligii kɛ ligii shwie wɔhe, ni efee tamɔ nɔ ni wɔmiitsɔ ŋmɔji ni ŋmaa ni ekwɛ̃ ni shika tsuru yɔɔ mli ni husu bɛ mli lɛ amli.

Yɛ gbi ni ji enyɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ana Right Whales ayibɔ, ni, yɛ shweshweeshwe kɛjɛ Sperm Whaler tamɔ Pequod lɛ tutuamɔ he, kɛ amɛtsitsii ni agbele lɛ, amɛmiitsɔ brit lɛ mli blɛoo, ni, ni amɛkɛ amɛhe kpɛtɛ nakai naakpɛɛ Venetian shwilafoi lɛ ahe tsɔi ni yɔɔ amɛdaaŋ lɛ ahe lɛ, amɛji nakai gbɛ nɔ ni amɛgbala nu lɛ mli.

Taakɛ mɛi ni shwieɔ tsei leebi, ni kɛ amɛhe woɔ jwɛi kakadaŋŋ agbo ni mli efɔ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkpɔi ni nɔ ewo muji lɛ amli lɛ amli blɛoo ni amɛkɛ amɛhe woɔ mli lɛ; nakai po nɛkɛ kooloi wuji nɛɛ shwieɔ nu lɛ, ni amɛfeɔ gbee ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, ni tamɔ jwɛi, ni shwieɔ mɔ; ni amɛshiɔ bluu ni bɛ naagbee yɛ ŋshɔ ŋmɔŋ lɛ nɔ yɛ amɛsɛɛ.*

*Nakai ŋshɔ lɛ fã ni whalebii le lɛ akɛ “Brazil Banks” lɛ bɛ nakai gbɛi lɛ tamɔ Banks of Newfoundland lɛ, yɛ ŋshɔkei ni mli ekɔɔɔ kɛ gbeei ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ ahewɔ, shi moŋ yɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔkei ni tamɔ ŋmɔ ni sa kadimɔ waa nɛɛ hewɔ, ni ŋshɔkei wuji ni ji brit la night lɛ ni hoɔ yɛ jɛmɛ ni yaa nɔ yɛ nakai Rha lɛ mli lɛ kɛbaa lɛ hewɔ.

Shi gbee ni amɛfee beni amɛgbala brit lɛ mli lɛ pɛ ji nɔ ni kaiɔ mɔ ko tsɔnei ni akɛfoɔ tsei lɛ kwraa. Kɛ́ ana amɛ kɛjɛ mast-yitsei lɛ anɔ, titri lɛ beni amɛmɛ ni amɛdamɔ shi be fioo lɛ, amɛhe diji wuji lɛ tamɔ tɛi babaoo ni wala bɛ mli fe nɔ fɛɛ nɔ. Ni taakɛ eji yɛ India gbɔbimɔ maji wuji lɛ amli lɛ, gbɔ ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ baatsɔ shwuɔi ni ekã shi yɛ shikpɔŋ kplanaa lɛ nɔ lɛ anɔ bei komɛi ni eleee akɛ amɛji nakai, ni ekɛ amɛ baatsu nii akɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ hei ni nɔ bɛ ni efee ediŋŋ; nakai po, bei pii lɛ, ekɛ lɛ, ni ji klɛŋklɛŋ kwraa ni ena ŋshɔ mli leviatan henɔ nɛɛ. Ni kɛ́ ayɔse amɛ yɛ naagbee lɛ po lɛ, amɛdalɛ ni fa babaoo lɛ haa ewaa waa diɛŋtsɛ akɛ aaahe aye akɛ nɛkɛ ŋmeii babaoo ni fa fe nine nɛɛ baanyɛ afee nɔ ni jɛ fɔmɔ mli, yɛ hei fɛɛ, kɛ wala ni yɔɔ gbee loo okpɔŋɔ mli lɛ nɔŋŋ.

Lɛlɛŋ, yɛ gbɛi krokomɛi anɔ lɛ, ewaaa akɛ ooosusu ŋshɔ lɛ mli bɔɔ nii komɛi ahe kɛ henumɔi ni tamɔ nɔ ni oyɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ. Ejaakɛ eyɛ mli akɛ blema adebɔɔ he nilelɔi komɛi ekɛɛ akɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ bɔɔ nii fɛɛ ji amɛhenɔi yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli moŋ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ asusuɔ nɔ lɛ he yɛ gbɛ ni da nɔ moŋ, shi enɛ baanyɛ aba mli jogbaŋŋ; shi kɛ eba nibii krɛdɛɛi ahe lɛ, nɛgbɛ, ákɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ lɛ, ŋshɔ lɛ kɛ loo ko ni yɛ su mli lɛ haa gbee lɛ mlihilɛ ni yɔɔ ŋaa lɛ hetoo lɛ haa? Abaanyɛ akɛɛ shark ni alomɔ lɛ pɛ yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ akɛ ekɛ lɛ yeɔ egbɔ.

Shi kɛlɛ, yɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ bii lɛ fɛɛ agbɛfaŋ lɛ, abu ŋshɔi lɛ anɔ bii diɛŋtsɛ kɛ henumɔi ni anyɛɛɛ awie he ni kɛ amɛhe kpaaa gbee ni amɛshwieɔ mɔ; eyɛ mli akɛ wɔle akɛ ŋshɔ lɛ ji naanɔ terra incognita moŋ, shi no hewɔ lɛ Columbus fã gbɛ kɛho jeŋ ni aleee ni anyɛɛɛ akane lɛ nɔ koni eyana e-anaigbɛ nɔ kome ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ; shi kɛlɛ, yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ lɛ, osharai ni gbeɔ mɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ lɛ eba mɛi akpei nyɔŋmai abɔ kɛ ohai abɔ ni etee nu lɛ nɔ lɛ anɔ yɛ gbɛ ni akaii nɔ ni ajieee amɛ kpo; eyɛ mli akɛ be kukuoo ko pɛ he susumɔ baatsɔɔ, akɛ bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni gbekɛ gbɔmɔ baanyɛ ajie ehe kpo yɛ ejeŋ nilee kɛ ŋaa he, kɛ bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni, yɛ wɔsɛɛ be ni yɔɔ fɛo mli lɛ, nakai jeŋ nilee kɛ ŋaa lɛ baanyɛ aya hiɛ; shi kɛya naanɔ kɛya naanɔ, kɛyashi hiɛkpatamɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ŋshɔ lɛ baagbe lɛ he guɔ ni egbe lɛ, ni ebaafite ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ nyam fe fɛɛ, ni wa fe fɛɛ ni ebaanyɛ efee lɛ; shi kɛlɛ, kɛtsɔ nɛkɛ nibii nɛɛ diɛŋtsɛ ni atĩɔ mli daa lɛ nɔ lɛ, gbɔmɔ elaaje nakai ŋshɔ lɛ gbeyeishemɔ ni yeɔ emuu ni eji shishijee lɛ nɔ lɛ he henumɔ lɛ.

Klɛŋklɛŋ lɛlɛ ni wɔkaneɔ he sane lɛ, nyiɛ ŋshɔ ko nɔ, ni kɛ Portugal hejoomɔ lɛ efo jeŋ muu fɛɛ mli ni eshiko nɔ ko kwraa tamɔ okulafo. Nakai ŋshɔ lɛ nɔŋŋ miihoso bianɛ; nakai ŋshɔ lɛ nɔŋŋ kpata lɛlɛi ni fite yɛ afi ni ho lɛ mli lɛ ahiɛ. Hɛɛ, gbɔmɛi ni gboiɔ ni bɛ jwɛŋmɔ, Noa nu afua lɛ ebako shi lolo; jeŋ ni ja lɛ mlijaa etɛ mli enyɔ ni eha he lolo lɛ.

Mɛni ji srɔtofeemɔ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ kɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ teŋ, ni naakpɛɛ nii ni baa eko nɔ lɛ jeee naakpɛɛ nii ni baa ekroko nɔ lɛ? Gbeyeishemɔi ni tsɔ adebɔɔ hiɛ lɛ ba Hebribii lɛ anɔ, beni shikpɔŋ ni wala yɔɔ mli lɛ gbele ehe yɛ Kora kɛ ekuu lɛ naji ashishi ni emi amɛ kɛya naanɔ lɛ; shi kɛlɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ hulu ko nyɔɔɔ shi kɔkɔɔkɔ, shi moŋ yɛ gbɛ ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ nɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ ŋshɔ ni hiɛ ka lɛ miɔ lɛlɛi kɛ mɛi ni tsuɔ nii yɛ mli lɛ.

Shi jeee akɛ ŋshɔ lɛ ji henyɛlɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kɛha gbɔmɔ ni ji gbɔ kɛha lɛ kɛkɛ, shi moŋ eji henyɛlɔ kɛha lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eseshibii hu; ehiii fe Persia gbɔi ni gbe lɛ diɛŋtsɛ egbɔi lɛ; ekɛ bɔɔ nii ni lɛ diɛŋtsɛ efɔ lɛ akafee nɔ ko. Taakɛ jata yoo ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni shwieɔ ehe yɛ koo lɛ mli lɛ shwieɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ebii lɛ anɔ lɛ, nakai nɔŋŋ ŋshɔ lɛ shwieɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni he wa fe fɛɛ lɛ po kɛyaa tɛi lɛ anɔ, ni eshiɔ amɛ yɛ jɛmɛ kɛ lɛlɛi ni mli egbala lɛ ahe. Mɔbɔnalɛ ko bɛ, hewalɛ ko bɛ shi moŋ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehewalɛ kudɔɔ lɛ. Ŋshɔ ni bɛ nuŋtsɔ lɛ miihoso ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ miihoso tamɔ tawuu okpɔŋɔ ni etsɔ shwilafo ni mɔ ni ta enɔ lɛ egbo lɛ, eyi shikpɔŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ nɔ.

Susumɔ bɔ ni ŋshɔ lɛ yɔɔ ŋaa lɛ he okwɛ; bɔ ni ebɔɔ nii ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ lɛ shwieɔ nu shishi, ni anaaa yɛ fã ni fe fɛɛ mli, ni akɛ lakamɔ gbɛ etee azure sui ni yɔɔ fɛo fe fɛɛ lɛ ashishi lɛ. Susumɔ bɔ ni akutsei babaoo ni bɛ henumɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ ahe yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ kɛ amɛhefɛo ni tamɔ abonsam lɛ hu he okwɛ, tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi srɔtoi babaoo ni yɔɔ fɛo ni akɛwula amɛhe lɛ. Susumɔ jeŋ muu fɛɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli gbɔmɔyeli ni yaa nɔ lɛ he okwɛ shikome ekoŋŋ; mɛi fɛɛ ni amɛbɔɔ nii lɛ yeɔ amɛhe, ni amɛyaa nɔ amɛwuɔ naanɔ ta kɛjɛ je lɛ shishijee mli.

Susumɔ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ he okwɛ; ni no sɛɛ lɛ, otsɔ ohe kɛya shikpɔŋ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji, ni yɔɔ blɛoo, ni feɔ toiboo fe fɛɛ nɛɛ nɔ; susumɔ amɛ fɛɛ he, ŋshɔ lɛ kɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ; ni ani onaaa nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni akɛtoɔ he yɛ bo diɛŋtsɛ omli? Ejaakɛ taakɛ ŋshɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɛɛ ebɔle shikpɔŋ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji lɛ ahe lɛ, nakai nɔŋŋ Tahiti ŋshɔkpɔ kome yɛ gbɔmɔ susuma mli, ni toiŋjɔlɛ kɛ miishɛɛ eyi mli obɔ, shi shihilɛ ni ale fã lɛ mli gbeyeishemɔi fɛɛ ebɔle he. Nyɔŋmɔ abu ohe! Kaatsi ohe kɛjɛ nakai ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ, onyɛŋ oku osɛɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ!





YITSƆƆ 59. Squid.

Beni Pequod lɛ tsɔɔ brit ŋmɔji lɛ amli fiofio lɛ, ehiɛ gbɛ ni etsɔɔ nɔ kɛyaa kooyi-bokagbɛ kɛyaa Java ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ mli lolo; kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli jɔ ni tsirɛɔ e-keel lɛ, bɔni afee ni yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ ni ebɔle ehe lɛ mli lɛ, e-mast kakadaji etɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ shwieɔ shi blɛoo kɛyaa nakai kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli wa lɛ nɔ, tamɔ tsei etɛ ni yɔɔ blɛoo ni yɔɔ shikpɔŋ kplanaa nɔ lɛ. Ni kɛlɛ, yɛ be babaoo mli yɛ shika tsuru gbɛkɛ lɛ mli lɛ, abaana jet ni yɔɔ ekomefeemɔ ni gbalaa mɔ lɛ.

Shi leebi ko ni yɔɔ bluu ni mli tse, beni dioofeemɔ ko ni miihe ashɛ adebɔɔ naa nɔ lɛ gbɛ eshwa ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, dioofeemɔ ko ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ ko bɛ ni kwɛɔ nɔ; beni hulutsoo be kakadaŋŋ ni tsoɔ yɛ nu lɛ nɔ lɛ tamɔ shika tsuru nine ni akɛfɔ amɛnɔ, ni efãa teemɔŋ sane ko lɛ; beni ŋshɔkei ni shwieɔ shi lɛ feɔ amɛhe ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ beni amɛjoɔ foi blɛoo kɛyaa hiɛ lɛ; yɛ nɛkɛ ŋwɛibɔfo ni anaa lɛ mli dioofeemɔ ni mli kwɔ nɛɛ mli lɛ, Daggoo na nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ kɛjɛ ŋwɛi-mast-yitso lɛ nɔ.

Yɛ shɔŋŋ lɛ, ŋshɔkei wulu ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ te shi, ni etee ŋwɛi kɛtee ŋwɛi, ni ejie ehe kɛjɛ azure lɛ mli, ni naagbee lɛ ekpɛ yɛ wɔhiɛ tamɔ snoo-shwiemɔ, ŋshɔkei hee ni jɛ gɔji lɛ amli. No hewɔ lɛ ekpɛ be fioo, beni egbɔjɔɔ fiofio lɛ, ni eboteɔ shishi lɛ. Kɛkɛ ni ete shi shikome ekoŋŋ, ni ekpɛ dioo. Etamɔ nɔ ni jeee ŋshɔŋloo ko; ni kɛlɛ ani Moby Dick nɛ? Daggoo susu he. Ekoŋŋ lɛ, ŋmiŋmi lɛ ba shi, shi beni ejie ehe kpo ekoŋŋ lɛ, kɛ bolɔmɔ ko ni tamɔ stiletto ni haa nuu fɛɛ nuu naa kpɛɔ ehe kɛjɛɔ etsitsi nɔ lɛ, negro lɛ bolɔ—“Jɛmɛ! jɛmɛ ekoŋŋ! jɛmɛ efoɔ mli! yɛ ehiɛ tuutu! White Whale lɛ, White Whale lɛ!”

Yɛ enɛ nɔ lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi lɛ ho oya kɛtee yard-arms lɛ, taakɛ yɛ swarming-be mli lɛ, ŋmeii lɛ hoɔ oya kɛyaa tsei lɛ aniji lɛ anɔ lɛ. Ahab damɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ, ni ehiɛ eyitso yɛ hulu ni tsoɔ waa lɛ mli, ni ekɛ enine kome tsi esɛɛ shɔŋŋ yɛ klalofeemɔ mli koni ekɛ efamɔi lɛ aha lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ, ni ekɛ ehiŋmɛii ni mli wa kwɛ gbɛ ni Daggoo nine ni ekpã mli ni tsiii ehe lɛ tsɔɔ yɛ ŋwɛi lɛ nɔ.

Kɛji akɛ tsɔne kome ni yɔɔ dioo ni yɔɔ ekomefeemɔ mli lɛ ni baa oya lɛ etsu nii fiofio yɛ Ahab nɔ, bɔni afee ni esaa ehe agbɛnɛ koni ekɛ mlijɔlɛ kɛ hejɔɔmɔ he susumɔi lɛ atsa ŋshɔŋloo pɔtɛɛ ni enyiɛɔ esɛɛ lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ ni enaa lɛ nɔ; shi kɛlɛ, enɛ ji, loo kɛji akɛ ehiɛdɔɔ lɛ jie lɛ kpo; gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ ni ebaanyɛ etsɔ nɔ etsɔ lɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni eyɔse ŋmeii yɛŋ lɛ jogbaŋŋ, ni ekɛ oyaiyeli ni mli wa ha famɔ amrɔ nɔŋŋ koni ajie lɛ shi.

Etsɛɛɛ ni lɛlɛi ejwɛ lɛ bashɛ nu lɛ nɔ; Ahab nɔ lɛ etsɔ hiɛ, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛmiigba amɛhe oya kɛmiiya amɛ kooloo lɛ he. Etsɛɛɛ ni eba shi, ni beni wɔkɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔnei ni akɛfoɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ miimɛ ni ebaapue ekoŋŋ lɛ, naa! yɛ he ni efo lɛ nɔŋŋ lɛ, shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, ete shi fiofio. Shwɛ fioo ni wɔhiɛ baakpa Moby Dick he susumɔi fɛɛ anɔ yɛ be kukuoo mli, ni agbɛnɛ wɔkwɛ naakpɛɛ nii ni fe fɛɛ ni teemɔŋ ŋshɔi lɛ ejie lɛ kpo etsɔɔ adesai kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ. Nɔ ko ni da waa, ni elɛɛmɔ kɛ elɛɛmɔ ji furlongs, ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, kã nu lɛ nɔ, ni eniji kakadaji ni anyɛɛɛ akane ni miikpɛ kɛmiijɛ emligbɛ, ni amɛmiibɔle amɛhe ni amɛmiibɔle amɛhe tamɔ anacondas atsu, tamɔ nɔ ni amɛmiimɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni bɛ miishɛɛ ni amɛbaanyɛ amɛshɛ he lɛ mli shwilafoi. Ebɛ hiɛ loo hiɛ ko ni anaa; okadi ko bɛ ni abaanyɛ asusu he ni tsɔɔ henumɔ loo fɔmɔ su; shi ebɔle jɛmɛ yɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ anɔ, wala ko ni bɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ, ni bɛ su, ni tamɔ nɔ ni ba trukaa.

Taakɛ eji yɛ gbee ni baa shi ni nuɔ nu mli lɛ, elaaje fiofio ekoŋŋ, ni Starbuck miikwɛ nu ni efee basabasa lɛ lolo yɛ he ni efo mli lɛ, ni ekɛ gbee ni mli wa bolɔ akɛ—“Eshɛɔ he ko ni mana Moby Dick ni mikɛ lɛ wuɔ, fe ni mana bo, bo mumɔ yɛŋ!”

“Mɛni ji no, Nuŋtsɔ?” Flask kɛɛ.

“Squid kpeteŋkpele ni hiɛ kã lɛ, ni amɛkɛɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi fioo ko pɛ ena lɛ pɛŋ, ni amɛku amɛsɛɛ amɛtee amɛ lɛlɛdaamɔhei lɛ koni amɛyagba he sane.”

Shi Ahab ewieee nɔ ko; ni etsɔ elɛlɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, eku esɛɛ kɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ mli; mɛi krokomɛi lɛ tamɔ mɛi ni nyiɛɔ amɛsɛɛ dioo.

Nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni apasa hemɔkɛyelii ni sperm whalemen lɛ fɛɛ kɛto nɛkɛ nɔ nɛɛ naa lɛ he lɛ, eji nɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ, akɛ, kɛ́ ana lɛ fioo ko lɛ, eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ waa, nakai shihilɛ lɛ etee shɔŋŋ ni ekɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ewo mli. No hewɔ lɛ, efɔɔɔ kaa akɛ anaa, akɛ eyɛ mli akɛ amɛteŋ mɔ kome kɛ amɛ fɛɛ jajeɔ akɛ eji nɔ ni wala yɔɔ mli ni da fe fɛɛ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli moŋ, shi amɛteŋ mɛi fioo ko pɛ yɔɔ susumɔi komɛi ja susumɔi ni bɛ faŋŋ fe fɛɛ yɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ kɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ ha lɛ he; shi kɛlɛ, amɛheɔ amɛyeɔ akɛ no pɛ ji nɔ ni haa sperm whale lɛ niyenii pɛ. Ejaakɛ eyɛ mli akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi krokomɛi naa amɛ niyenii yɛ nu lɛ nɔ, ni gbɔmɔ baanyɛ ana amɛ yɛ niyenii ni amɛyeɔ lɛ mli moŋ, shi spermaceti ŋshɔŋloo lɛ naa eniyenii muu lɛ fɛɛ yɛ hei ni aleee yɛ nu lɛ shishi; ni kɛtsɔ susumɔ nɔ pɛ ji nɔ ni mɔ fɛɛ mɔ baanyɛ atsɔɔ nɔ pɔtɛɛ ni nakai niyenii lɛ yɔɔ mli. Bei komɛi lɛ, kɛ́ atiu lɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ, ebaajie nɔ ni asusuɔ akɛ eji squid lɛ niji ni etse lɛ; no hewɔ lɛ amɛteŋ mɛi komɛi jie amɛhe kpo akɛ amɛlɛɛmɔ fe ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai enyɔ kɛ nyɔŋmai etɛ. Amɛsusuɔ akɛ, bei pii lɛ, kooloo wulu ni nɛkɛ niji nɛɛ fata he lɛ kɛ amɛhe kpɛtɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ shishi; kɛ akɛ, akɛ ŋmeii haa sperm whale lɛ, ni etamɔɔɔ kooloi krokomɛi lɛ, bɔni afee ni etutua lɛ ni efo mli.

Etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ko yɛ ni baaha asusu akɛ Osɔfonukpa Pontoppodan Kraken kpeteŋkpele lɛ baanyɛ atsu ehe nii yɛ naagbee ni etsɔ Squid. Gbɛ ni Osɔfonukpa lɛ tsɔɔ mli, akɛ ehiɛɔ ehe nɔ kɛyaa shishi, kɛ saji krokomɛi ni egbaa lɛ, yɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ mli lɛ, enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ kpãa gbee. Shi abatement babaoo he miihia yɛ bulɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ babaoo ni ekɛhaa lɛ he.

Adebɔɔ he nilelɔi komɛi ni enu teemɔŋ adebɔɔ ni awieɔ he yɛ biɛ lɛ he saji ni ejaaa lɛ tsɔɔ akɛ, ekɛfata cuttle-fish kuu lɛ he, ni, yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, yɛ kponɔgbɛ nibii komɛi amli lɛ, ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni efata he, shi akɛ Akutso lɛ mli Anak pɛ.





YITSƆƆ 60. Line lɛ.

Yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni agbeɔ lɛ ahe shihilɛ ni abaatsɔɔ mli kuku lɛ he lɛ, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu koni anu shihilɛi ni tamɔ nakai fɛɛ ni ajie lɛ kpo yɛ hei krokomɛi lɛ ashishi jogbaŋŋ lɛ, miyɛ biɛ ni mawie majik, bei komɛi lɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ ahe.

Kpãa ni akɛtsu nii shishijee yɛ wuoyeli mli lɛ ji hemp ni hi fe fɛɛ, ni akɛ tar ewo mli fioo, ni akɛwooo mli, tamɔ bɔ ni afeɔ yɛ kpãai foloi amli lɛ; ejaakɛ yɛ be mli ni tar, taakɛ akɛtsuɔ nii daa lɛ, haa hemp lɛ feɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo kɛha kpãafeelɔ lɛ, ni ehaa kpãa lɛ diɛŋtsɛ hu feɔ nɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ kɛha lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ kɛha lɛlɛ ni akɛtsuɔ nii daa lɛ; shi kɛlɛ, jeee akɛ yibɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ baaha ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli awa tsɔ kɛha ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he ŋshɔŋloo ni bɛŋkɛ ni esa akɛ ekɛ ehe awo mli lɛ pɛ kɛkɛ; shi taakɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi babaoo jeɔ shishi amɛkaseɔ lɛ, tar ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ kɛ nɔ ko fataaa kpãa lɛ sɛɛfimɔ loo hewalɛ he kwraa, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni ebaaha efee mlɛo ni ekpɛ lɛ.

Yɛ afii ni eho lɛ amli lɛ, Manilla kpã lɛ eye hemp najiaŋ kwraa yɛ Amerika ŋshɔŋ wuoyeli mli akɛ nɔ ni akɛfeɔ whale-lines; ejaakɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ etsɛɛɛ tamɔ hemp moŋ, shi eyɛ hewalɛ, ni eyɛ blɛoo kwraa ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔ; ni mikɛbaafata he (akɛni æsthetics yɛ nibii fɛɛ mli hewɔ lɛ), eyɛ fɛo babaoo ni eshɛɔ lɛlɛ lɛ he, fe hemp. Hemp ji mɔ ko ni yɔɔ duŋ, ni yɔɔ duŋ, Indianyo ko; shi Manilla tamɔ Circassianyo ko ni hiɛ yitsɔi shika tsuru ni esa akɛ akwɛ.

Whale-line lɛ mlilɛɛmɔ ji inchi mlijaa etɛ mli enyɔ pɛ. Kɛ́ ona lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ, obaasusuŋ akɛ ehe wa tamɔ bɔ ni eyɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ lɛ. Kɛtsɔ kaa nɔ lɛ, etsɔnei kome kɛ nyɔŋmai enumɔ lɛ eko fɛɛ eko baaŋmɛ tsɔne ni tsiimɔ ji pound oha kɛ nyɔŋmai enyɔ; bɔni afee ni kpãa muu lɛ fɛɛ atere tsɔne ni miihe ashɛ tɔn etɛ. Yɛ elɛɛmɔ mli lɛ, sperm whale-line ni afɔɔ namɔ lɛ feɔ nɔ ko ni fe fathoms ohai enyɔ. Kɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ, abɔleɔ ehe yɛ nubu lɛ mli, jeee tamɔ ŋmei paipi ni yɔɔ ŋmei ko mli lɛ, shi moŋ bɔni afee ni efee “ŋmeii” ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ŋta ni tamɔ tsiisi, loo ŋtayaai ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ŋta ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ŋta lɛ mli, ni ŋmei ko bɛ mli shi moŋ “tsui,” loo ŋmei bibioo ko ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ lɛ. Taakɛ nɔ ni fe fɛɛ ni yɔɔ tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ baajie mɔ ko nine, enane, loo gbɔmɔtso muu lɛ fɛɛ kɛjɛ mli lɛ, akɛ hiɛdɔɔ ni fe fɛɛ tsuɔ nii yɛ tsɔne lɛ ni akɛwoɔ e-tub lɛ mli lɛ mli. Mɛi komɛi ni tswaa harpoone lɛ baaye nɔ ni miihe ashɛ leebi muu fɛɛ yɛ nitsumɔ nɛɛ mli, ni amɛbaatere tsɔne lɛ kɛya ŋwɛi shɔɔŋ ni no sɛɛ lɛ amɛbaatsɔ blɔki ko nɔ amɛfo kɛya shi kɛya nubu lɛ mli, bɔni afee ni amɛfee tamɔ nɔ ni amɛfeɔ koni amɛjie lɛ kɛjɛ ŋmeii kɛ ŋmeii fɛɛ ni baanyɛ aba lɛ amli.

Yɛ Ŋleshi lɛlɛi lɛ amli lɛ, akɛ nubui enyɔ tsuɔ nii yɛ ekome najiaŋ; line kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ ni akɛyaa nɔ abɔleɔ yɛ tsɛnsii enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ mli. Sɛɛnamɔ ko yɛ enɛ he; ejaakɛ nɛkɛ tsɔnei enyɔ nɛɛ ji bibii waa hewɔ lɛ, amɛboteɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli oya, ni amɛtsiii lɛ tsɔ; yɛ be mli ni, Amerikabii a-tub lɛ, ni elɛɛmɔ miihe ashɛ ninetalɔi etɛ ni elɛɛmɔ tamɔ bɔ ni sa lɛ, feɔ jatsu ko ni da waa kɛha lɛlɛ ko ni etsei lɛ amli lɛlɛ ji inchi kome fã pɛ; ejaakɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛlɛ lɛ shishi tamɔ ais ni he hiaa waa, ni baatere muji ni aja mli babaoo, shi jeee nɔ ni mli wa waa. Kɛji akɛ atswa kanvas ni akɛkpɛ mfoniri lɛ he lɛ yɛ Amerika line-tub lɛ nɔ lɛ, lɛlɛ lɛ feɔ tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ yookpeemɔ-keeki wulu ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ miifã gbɛ koni ekɛha ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ.

Ajieɔ line lɛ naagbeehei enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ kpo; shishigbɛ naagbee ni gbeɔ naa yɛ hiŋmɛi-splice loo loop ni jɛɔ shishi kɛbaa kɛbaa nubu lɛ sɛɛ, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ eshishigbɛ ni ekɛ ehe kpaa gbee kwraa kɛjɛɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ he. Nɛkɛ shishigbɛ naagbee lɛ he gbɛjianɔtoo nɛɛ he miihia yɛ saji enyɔ komɛi ahewɔ. Klɛŋklɛŋ: Bɔni afee ni ekɛ tsɔne kroko ni jɛ lɛlɛ ko ni bɛŋkɛ lɛ mli lɛ akpɛtɛ he lɛ afee mlɛo lɛ, kɛji akɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni atswa lɛ lɛ gbɛɛmɔ mli kwɔ waa ni ewo ehe gbeyei akɛ ebaaŋɔ tsɔne muu lɛ fɛɛ ni akɛkpɛtɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he shishijee lɛ. Yɛ shihilɛi nɛɛ amli lɛ, eji anɔkwale akɛ ajieɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ tamɔ ale kɔɔpoo, taakɛ atsɔɔ mli lɛ, kɛjɛɔ lɛlɛ kome mli kɛyaa ekroko mli; eyɛ mli akɛ klɛŋklɛŋ lɛlɛ lɛ shwieɔ ehe be fɛɛ be koni eye ebua eŋa lɛ moŋ. Nɔ ni ji enyɔ: Gbɛjianɔtoo nɛɛ he miihia waa kɛha mɛi fɛɛ ashweshweeshwefeemɔ; ejaakɛ kɛji akɛ ŋshɔ lɛ shishigbɛ naagbee lɛ kɛ ehe kpɛtɛ lɛlɛ lɛ he yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, ni kɛji akɛ no mli lɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ jo foi kɛje kpo kɛyashɛ naagbee lɛ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ minitii kome mli, ni etsuɔ ​​lasu tamɔ bɔ ni efeɔ yɛ bei komɛi amli lɛ, ekpaŋ jɛmɛ, ejaakɛ lɛlɛ ni abaakpata hiɛ lɛ baagbala lɛ kɛba shi yɛ esɛɛ kɛya ŋshɔ lɛ mli ni tɔmɔ ko bɛ he; ni yɛ nakai shihilɛ lɛ mli lɛ, maŋtsɛ ko bɛ ni baana lɛ dɔŋŋ.

Dani abaajie lɛlɛ lɛ shi kɛha sɛɛtiumɔ lɛ, ajieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ ŋwɛi naagbee lɛ kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ sɛɛ, ni etsɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ yitso lɛ he yɛ jɛmɛ, ni akɛ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ fɛɛ yaa hiɛ ekoŋŋ, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ lɛlɛ lɛ tsɔne ni akɛkpɛɔ nii loo nine lɛ nɔ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ, bɔni afee ni ekɛ ehe abɔ enine he yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli tsɔne kudɔmɔ mli; ni etsɔɔ hii lɛ ateŋ hu, beni amɛtsakeɔ amɛhe yɛ tutswaa hei ni kɛ amɛhe kpaa gbee lɛ, kɛyaa ŋmeii loo ŋmeii ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ mli, he ni tso ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli loo skewer ni dalɛ tamɔ ŋmeii ni afɔɔ namɔ lɛ tsiɔ lɛ naa koni ekaje kpo lɛ. Ejɛɔ ŋshɔkei lɛ amli ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ ŋshɔkei lɛ anɔ yɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ anɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ, atsɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli ekoŋŋ; ni aaafee fathom nyɔŋma loo nyɔŋmai enyɔ (ni atsɛɔ lɛ box-line) ni akɛwo adeka lɛ nɔ yɛ gãimlibii lɛ amli lɛ, eyaa nɔ eyaa gunwale ni yɔɔ sɛɛ fioo lolo lɛ nɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ akɛkpɛtɛɔ short-warp lɛ he—kpãa ni akɛ harpoon lɛ tsaa amrɔ nɔŋŋ lɛ; shi dani nakai tsakpaa lɛ baa lɛ, short-warp lɛ tsɔɔ teemɔŋ saji srɔtoi ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli tsɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ mli fitsofitso lɛ amli.

No hewɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ a-line lɛ shwieɔ lɛlɛ muu lɛ fɛɛ mli yɛ e-coils ni mli wawai amli, ni ebɔleɔ he ni ebɔleɔ ehe yɛ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ he fɛɛ he. Mɛi ni fãa lɛlɛ lɛ fɛɛ kɛ amɛhe woɔ etsɔnei ni yɔɔ oshara lɛ amli; bɔni afee ni shikpɔŋ nɔ gbɔmɔ ni sheɔ gbeyei lɛ hiŋmɛi lɛ, amɛfee tamɔ Indiabii ni feɔ shwɛmɔi, ni onufui ni gbeɔ mɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ kɛ amɛniji kɛ amɛhe woɔ shwɛmɔ mli. Ni yoo ni gboɔ binuu ko hu nyɛŋ, klɛŋklɛŋ kwraa lɛ, ata nakai hempen nibii ni mli wawai lɛ ateŋ, ni beni ekɛ ehewalɛ fɛɛ miitsu nii yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ, esusu akɛ yɛ be fɛɛ mli ni aleee lɛ, abaanyɛ atswa ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, ni akɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɛɛ fɛɛ awo shwɛmɔ mli tamɔ sarawagbamɔi ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ; enyɛŋ ehi shihilɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli ni ŋmɔlɔ ni haa fãji ni yɔɔ ewui amli lɛ diɛŋtsɛ hosoɔ yɛ emli tamɔ jeli ni ekpokpo lɛ bɛ mli. Shi kɛlɛ su—nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ! mɛni ji nɔ ni su nyɛŋ atsu he nii?—Gayer sallies, miishɛɛ babaoo, shwɛmɔi ni hi fe tsutsu lɛ, kɛ repartees ni mli tse fe tsutsu lɛ, onuko he pɛŋ yɛ omahogany tso lɛ nɔ, fe bɔ ni obaanu yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ lɛ cedar tso yɛŋ ni kɛlɛ ji inchi fã lɛ nɔ, beni akɛwo hangman tsɔnei amli nakai lɛ; ni, tamɔ Calais burghers enumɔ ni tsɔ Maŋtsɛ Edward hiɛ lɛ, hii enumɔ ni feɔ lɛlɛ kuu lɛ gbalaa amɛhe kɛyaa gbele tsitsii amli, ni amɛkɛ halter ebɔleɔ amɛkuɛ fɛɛ amɛkuɛ he, taakɛ obaanyɛ okɛɛ lɛ.

Ekolɛ susumɔ fioo ko pɛ baaha onyɛ obu nakai ŋshɔŋlooi ahe osharai ni baa shii abɔ lɛ he akɔntaa agbɛnɛ—ni ekomɛi fioo komɛi ji nɔ ni aŋmala ashwie shi yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ—ni ji nuu nɛɛ loo nakai nuu lɛ ni ajie lɛ kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli kɛtsɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔ, ni elaaje lɛ. Ejaakɛ, beni line lɛ miiho kɛmiijɛ kpo, ni ooota no sɛɛ lɛ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ota shi yɛ steam-engine ni miitsu nii waa lɛ mli, be mli ni tso fɛɛ tso ni foɔ, kɛ shaft, kɛ shwiili, miiye bo. Ehiii kwraa fe no; ejaakɛ onyɛŋ ota shi ni otsii ohe yɛ osharai nɛɛ amli, ejaakɛ lɛlɛ lɛ miihoso tamɔ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli, ni atswa bo kɛtee gbɛ kome kɛ gbɛ kroko nɔ, ni kɔkɔbɔɔ bibioo ko po bɛ he; ni kɛtsɔ ŋwɛi ŋtayaa ko ni tsakeɔ ehe kɛ suɔmɔ kɛ nifeemɔ ni yaa nɔ yɛ be kome mli lɛ pɛ nɔ obaanyɛ ojo Mazeppa ni afee bo lɛ naa foi, ni ojo foi kɛya he ni hulu ni naa nɔ fɛɛ nɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ nyɛŋ atsɔ bo kɛjɛ mli kɔkɔɔkɔ lɛ.

Ekoŋŋ: taakɛ toiŋjɔlɛ ni mli kwɔ ni etamɔ nɔ ni etsɔɔ ahum lɛ hiɛ pɛ ni egbaa lɛ, ekolɛ eji nɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe ahum lɛ diɛŋtsɛ; ejaakɛ, lɛɛlɛŋ, toiŋjɔlɛ lɛ ji ahum lɛ he tsɔne kɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ pɛ; ni ehiɛ mli yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ emli, taakɛ tu ni tamɔ nɔ ni ehiii kwraa lɛ hiɛɔ powda ni gbeɔ mɔ lɛ, kɛ bɔɔlu lɛ, kɛ tsɔne ni tswaa lɛ; no hewɔ lɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli hejɔɔmɔ ni yɔɔ fɛo, beni ekɛ onufu wieɔ dioo yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ ahe dani akɛbaa shwɛmɔ diɛŋtsɛ mli lɛ—enɛ ji nɔ ko ni hiɛ anɔkwa gbeyeishemɔ babaoo fe nɛkɛ sane ni yɔɔ oshara nɛɛ fã kroko fɛɛ fã. Shi mɛni hewɔ awieɔ babaoo? Hii fɛɛ hiɔ shi yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni ji whale-lines amli. Afɔ amɛ fɛɛ kɛ tsitsii ni akɛwoɔ amɛkuɛ mli; shi be mli ni gbele ni yaa oya, ni baa trukaa lɛ mli lɛ pɛ kɛkɛ ni gbɔmɛi ni gboiɔ lɛ yɔseɔ shihilɛ mli osharai ni yɔɔ dioo, ni yɔɔ mlɛo, ni yɔɔ be fɛɛ be lɛ. Ni kɛji akɛ jeŋ nilelɔ ji bo, eyɛ mli akɛ ota ŋshɔŋloo lɛlɛ lɛ mli moŋ, shi onuuu gbeyeishemɔ ko kwraa he yɛ otsui mli, fe kɛji akɛ ota ogbɛkɛ la lɛ hiɛ kɛ poker, shi jeee harpoon, yɛ omasɛi.
