YITSƆƆ 74. Sperm Whale lɛ Yitso—Sɛɛnamɔ ni Kɛ Esoro lɛ.

Naa, agbɛnɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi wuji enyɔ komɛi ni kɛ amɛyitsei ebɔ he; nyɛhaa wɔkɛ amɛ afataa he, ni wɔkɛ wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔ nɔ lɛ ato he.

Yɛ folio leviathani akuu wulu lɛ mli lɛ, Sperm Whale kɛ Right Whale ji nɔ ni sa kadimɔ waa. Amɛ pɛ ji ŋshɔŋlooi ni gbɔmɔ taoɔ amɛ daa. Kɛha Nantucketerbii lɛ, amɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi srɔtoi fɛɛ ni ale lɛ ateŋ nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ enyɔ lɛ haa. Taakɛ kponɔgbɛ srɔtofeemɔ ni yɔɔ amɛteŋ lɛ ji nɔ ni anaa yɛ amɛyitsei amli titri lɛ; ni akɛ eko fɛɛ eko yitso ji nɛkɛ be nɛɛ ni fĩɔ Pequod lɛ sɛɛ; ni taakɛ wɔbaanyɛ wɔya kɛjɛ eko mli kɛya ekroko mli yɛ heyeli mli, kɛtsɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ ni wɔɔfo kɛkɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ:—nɛgbɛ, esa akɛ male, ani obaaná hegbɛ ni hi jogbaŋŋ ni okɛkase cetology ni anyɛɔ atsuɔ he nii fe biɛ?

Yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, srɔtofeemɔ ni yɔɔ yitsei nɛɛ ateŋ lɛ haa onaa kpɛɔ ohe. Enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ dara bɔ ni sa yɛ henilee fɛɛ mli; shi akɔntaabuu mli srɔtofeemɔ ko yɛ Sperm Whale lɛ mli ni Right Whale lɛ bɛ yɛ awerɛho gbɛ nɔ. Sui babaoo yɛ Sperm Whale lɛ yitso mli. Beni onaa lɛ, okɛ nɔyeli ni fe fɛɛ lɛ haa lɛ kɛjɛɔ otsui mli, yɛ bulɛ ni egbɛ eshwa he fɛɛ he lɛ mli. Yɛ shihilɛ ni yɔɔ amrɔ nɛɛ mli hu lɛ, bulɛ nɛɛ yaa hiɛ kɛtsɔ eyitso ni yɔɔ ŋmei kɛ ŋoo su ni yɔɔ gɔŋ lɛ yiteŋ lɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ, ni enɛ haa etsɔɔ akɛ eye afii babaoo ni eyɛ niiashikpamɔ babaoo. Yɛ kukufoo mli lɛ, lɛ ji nɔ ni wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ tsɛɔ lɛ yɛ ŋaa gbɛ nɔ akɛ “ŋshɔŋloo ni hiɛ yitso ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ.”

Agbɛnɛ nyɛhaa wɔkadia nɔ ni bɛ srɔto kwraa yɛ yitsei nɛɛ amli—ni ji gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli nibii enyɔ ni he hiaa fe fɛɛ, ni ji hiŋmɛi kɛ toi lɛ. Yɛ yitso lɛ sɛɛ shɔŋŋ, kɛ shishigbɛ, ni bɛŋkɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ eko fɛɛ eko tsitsi lɛ he lɛ, kɛji otao mli fioo lɛ, naagbee lɛ obaana hiŋmɛi ni bɛ tsitsi, ni obaasusu akɛ eji teji gbekɛ hiŋmɛi; no hewɔ lɛ kɛjɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ mli lɛ, ekɛ yitso lɛ dalɛ kpãa gbee.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛjɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hiŋmɛii lɛ afã gbɛ ni yɔɔ srɔto nɛɛ nɔ lɛ, eka shi faŋŋ akɛ enyɛŋ ena nɔ ko ni yɔɔ ehiɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ, tamɔ bɔ ni enyɛŋ ena nɔ ko ni yɔɔ esɛɛgbɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ lɛ. Yɛ wiemɔ kome mli lɛ, he ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hiŋmɛii yɔɔ lɛ kɛ gbɔmɔ toii lɛ kpãa gbee; ni ekolɛ obaasusu he, kɛha bo diɛŋtsɛ ohe, bɔ ni ebaafee aha bo, ani okɛ otoii kwɛ nibii amli yɛ afãgbɛ. Obaana akɛ obaanyɛ ofã nibii ni anaa lɛ aaafee digrii nyɔŋmai etɛ pɛ kɛtsɔ hiɛ ni anaa yɛ sɛɛgbɛ ni ja lɛ nɔ; kɛ aaafee nyɔŋmai etɛ krokomɛi hu yɛ esɛɛ. Kɛji ohenyɛlɔ ni mli wa fe fɛɛ lɛ nyiɛ kɛmiiba oŋɔɔ tɛ̃ɛ, ni ewo gãimlibi yɛ hulutsoo be mli lɛ, onyɛŋ ona lɛ, tamɔ bɔ ni eji kɛji eeju bo kɛmiijɛ osɛɛ lɛ. Yɛ wiemɔ kome mli lɛ, obaaná sɛɛ enyɔ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ; shi, yɛ nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ lɛ, hiɛi enyɔ hu (sɛɛ hiɛi): ejaakɛ mɛni ji nɔ ni feɔ gbɔmɔ hiɛ—mɛɛ, lɛɛlɛŋ, ja ehiŋmɛii?

Agbɛnɛ hu, yɛ be mli ni yɛ kooloi krokomɛi babaoo ni manyɛ masusu amɛhe amrɔ nɛɛ amli lɛ, adu hiŋmɛii lɛ bɔ ni anyɛɛɛ ana koni ekɛ amɛhiŋmɛii ahewalɛ lɛ afutu, bɔni afee ni ekɛ mfoniri kome aba shi jeee enyɔ kɛha jwɛŋmɔ lɛ; he ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hiŋmɛii lɛ yɔɔ yɛ gbɛ krɛdɛɛ ko nɔ, ni yitso ni wa ni ji kubik feet babaoo, ni ŋmɛɔ amɛteŋ tamɔ gɔŋ wulu ko ni gbalaa kpaakpoi enyɔ ni yɔɔ jɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ mli eja yɛ gbɛ ni mɔɔ shi nɔ; enɛ, yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, esa akɛ egbala susumɔi ni gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli nibii ni yɔɔ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe lɛ eko fɛɛ eko kɛhaa lɛ mli kwraa. No hewɔ lɛ, esa akɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ana mfoniri kome ni yɔɔ srɔto yɛ nɛkɛ fã nɛɛ, kɛ mfoniri kroko ni yɔɔ srɔto yɛ nakai fã lɛ; yɛ be mli ni esa akɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ teŋ lɛ afee duŋ ni mli kwɔ kɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko kɛha lɛ. Yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛɛ gbɔmɔ kwɛɔ je lɛ kɛjɛɔ bulɔi adeka ko ni akɛ tsɔji enyɔ ni akɛwo esamflɛ mli lɛ ebɔ he lɛ mli. Shi yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he lɛ, akɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi enyɔ nɛɛ woɔ mli srɔtoi, ni ehaa samfɛji sɔrɔtoi enyɔ feɔ, shi awrɛho sane ji akɛ, ehaa nɔ ni anaa lɛ baa shi. Nɛkɛ su pɔtɛɛ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hiŋmɛii ahe nɛɛ ji nɔ ko ni esa akɛ ahi jwɛŋmɔ mli be fɛɛ be yɛ ŋshɔŋ loomɔ mli; ni nikanelɔ lɛ akai yɛ nibii komɛi ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ amli.

Abaanyɛ aje sanebimɔ ko ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli ni haa mɔ naa kpɛɔ ehe fe fɛɛ lɛ shishi yɛ nɛkɛ nɔ ni anaa nɛɛ he akɛ eta Leviathan he. Shi esa akɛ mitsui anyɔ mimli yɛ ŋaawoo ko he. Be abɔ ni gbɔmɔ hiŋmɛii gbeleɔ yɛ la lɛ mli lɛ, nifeemɔ ni ji nibii ni enaa lɛ ji nɔ ni jɛɛɛ mɔ diɛŋtsɛ suɔmɔ mli; ni ji akɛ, no sɛɛ lɛ enyɛŋ eye ebua kɛtsɔ tsɔne nɔ ni ena nibii fɛɛ ni yɔɔ ehiɛ lɛ. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, mɔ fɛɛ mɔ niiashikpamɔ baatsɔɔ lɛ, akɛ eyɛ mli akɛ ebaanyɛ ekɛ nibii ni tsɔɔ mɔ ko mɔ ko mli lɛ atsu nii yɛ hiŋmɛi kome mli moŋ, shi enyɛŋ eba kwraa akɛ, ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ, kɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ, baapɛi nibii enyɔ komɛi amli—bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni amɛdara loo amɛdara ha—yɛ be kome mli; ekɔɔɔ he eko kɛji amɛkã amɛhe ni amɛta amɛhe. Shi kɛji akɛ oba agbɛnɛ ni obaagbala nibii enyɔ nɛɛ ateŋ, ni okɛ duŋ ni mli kwɔ ko ni mli kwɔ ko bɔle eko fɛɛ eko he; no sɛɛ lɛ, bɔni afee ni ona amɛteŋ ekome, yɛ gbɛ ni baaha ojwɛŋmɔ ahi nɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, abaajie ekroko lɛ kwraa kɛjɛ obeaŋ jwɛŋmɔ mli. Belɛ, te eji tɛŋŋ yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he? Eji anɔkwale akɛ, esa akɛ ehiŋmɛii enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ, yɛ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe lɛ, atsu nii yɛ be kome mli; shi ani ejwɛŋmɔ lɛ mli kwɔ, ekɛ ehe feɔ ekome, ni eyɛ ŋaa fe gbɔmɔ nɔ lɛ, ni ebaanyɛ ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ akwɛ gbɛkpamɔi sɔrɔtoi enyɔ komɛi amli yɛ be kome too mli, eko yɛ efã kome, ni ekroko lɛ yɛ gbɛ ni teɔ shi woɔ enɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ? Kɛji ebaanyɛ lɛ, belɛ ani eji naakpɛɛ nɔ ko yɛ emli, tamɔ nɔ ni gbɔmɔ ko nyɛ etsɔ naagbai sɔrɔtoi enyɔ ni yɔɔ Euclid mli lɛ akpojiemɔi lɛ amli yɛ be kome mli. Ni asaŋ, kɛ akwɛ mli jogbaŋŋ lɛ, nɔ ko bɛ ni kɛ ehe kpaaa gbee yɛ nɔ ni akɛtoɔ he nɛɛ mli.

Ekolɛ eji nɔ ko ni bɛ sɛɛnamɔ ko kɛkɛ, shi etamɔ mi be fɛɛ be akɛ, bɔ ni ŋshɔŋlooi komɛi jieɔ amɛhe kpo yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ kɛ́ lɛlɛi etɛ loo ejwɛ ebɔle amɛhe lɛ; hiɛgbele kɛ gbɛnaa nii ni yɔɔ gbeyeishemɔi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ahe, ni afɔɔ namɔ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ; Misusuɔ akɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ jɛɔ suɔmɔnaa nii ni bɛ yelikɛbuamɔ mli, ni esa akɛ amɛ ninamɔ hewalɛi ni mli eja ni teɔ shi woɔ amɛhe kwraa lɛ kɛ amɛhe awo mli lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ.

Shi ŋshɔŋloo lɛ toi eyi obɔ kɛ taomɔ nii tamɔ hiŋmɛi lɛ. Kɛji akɛ gbɔ kwraa ji bo yɛ amɛhewolonɔ su lɛ mli lɛ, ekolɛ obaatao yitsei enyɔ nɛɛ anɔ ŋmɛlɛtswai babaoo, ni onaaa nakai gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli nibii lɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ. Shi nyɛhaa wɔhiɛa nɛkɛ ŋmei nɛɛ mli yɛ biɛ, ni wɔkwɛa wɔhe kɛkpeɔ he ni wɔyɔɔ lɛ. Mɛɛ naabu ni yɔɔ fɛo ni he tse diɛŋtsɛ po eji nɛkɛ! kɛjɛ shikpɔŋ kɛyashi ŋwɛi, ni akɛ tsɔne yɛŋ ni kpɛɔ, ni kpɛɔ tamɔ ayemforo atadei ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ ewo mli, loo moŋ akɛ wolo ewo mli.

YITSƆƆ 75. Ninejurɔgbɛ Ŋshɔloo lɛ Yitso—Susumɔ ni Kɛ Esoro lɛ.

Beni wɔfoɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, nyɛhaa wɔkwɛa Right Whale lɛ yitso lɛ jogbaŋŋ agbɛnɛ be kakadaŋŋ.

Taakɛ eji yɛ gbɛ ni da nɔ lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛ Sperm Whale yitso ni nɔ kwɔ lɛ ato Roma tawuu-shwiili he (titri lɛ yɛ hiɛ, he ni eyɛ ŋwɛi shɔɔŋ waa lɛ); no hewɔ lɛ, kɛ́ akwɛ lɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ, Right Whale lɛ yitso lɛ tamɔɔ tokota wulu ko ni yɔɔ galliot naneshi ni bɛ fɛo kwraa. Afii ohai enyɔ ni eho nɛ lɛ, Dutch gbɛfalɔ onukpa ko kɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ lɛ to tokotafeelɔ naagbee nɔ lɛ he. Ni yɛ naagbee loo tokota nɛɛ nɔŋŋ mli lɛ, nakai yoomo ni yɔɔ gbekɛbii ashihilɛhe lɛ sane lɛ mli lɛ, kɛ ebii ni miiho lɛ, baanyɛ ahi shi jogbaŋŋ waa, lɛ kɛ eseshibii fɛɛ.

Shi beni obɛŋkɛɔ yitso kpeteŋkpele nɛɛ he lɛ ejeɔ shishi esusuɔ nibii srɔtoi ahe, yɛ bɔ ni osusuɔ nii ahe ohaa lɛ naa.
