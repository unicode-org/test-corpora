Babaoo ni mikɛ mihe woɔ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ sane nɛɛ mli, ni mikɛ minibii amlitaomɔi lɛ yaashɛɔ eshishijee shishijee lɛ, babaoo ni bɔ ni eyɔɔ hiɛ nyam waa kɛ bɔ ni etsɛ waa lɛ haa minaa kpɛɔ mihe waa; ni titri lɛ, kɛ́ mina nyɔŋmɔi-fã kɛ henyɛlɔi wuji babaoo, gbalɔi srɔtoi fɛɛ, ni yɛ gbɛ kome loo gbɛ kroko nɔ amɛjie srɔtofeemɔ kpo amɛshwie nɔ lɛ, mikɛ susumɔ ni ji akɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ mifata nyɛmimɛi akuu ko ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ lɛ he lɛ yaa mihe.

Osɔfonukpa lɛ tsɔɔ akɛ ehe ehiaaa ni wɔbu Yona akɛ afu lɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ musu mli, shi moŋ akɛ ehi enaabu fã ko mli be kukuoo ko. Ni etamɔ nɔ ni enɛ ji nɔ ni jwɛŋmɔ yɔɔ mli bɔ ni sa yɛ Osɔfonukpa kpakpa lɛ mli. Ejaakɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, Right Whale lɛ daaŋ lɛ baanyɛ aŋɔ whist-tables enyɔ komɛi, ni ebaaha shwɛlɔi lɛ fɛɛ ahi shi jogbaŋŋ. Ekolɛ, Yona hu baanyɛ ekɛ ehe awo ŋmei ko ni mli ewo ŋmei mli; shi, kɛ asusu he shii enyɔ nɔ lɛ, Right Whale lɛ bɛ ŋmeii.

Yiŋtoo kroko hu ni Sag-Harbor (ekɛ nakai gbɛi lɛ tee) wo he hewalɛ yɛ hemɔkɛyeli ni ebɛ yɛ gbalɔ lɛ sane nɛɛ mli lɛ he lɛ, ji nɔ ko ni bɛ faŋŋ ni kɔɔ egbɔmɔtso ni awo lɛ tsuŋ lɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli nu lɛ he. Shi nɛkɛ shitee-kɛ-woo nɛɛ hu gbeeɔ shikpɔŋ, ejaakɛ German shishitsɔɔmɔ he nilelɔ ko susuɔ akɛ ekolɛ Yona yatao abobaahe yɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni egbo ni miiho lɛ mli—be mli ni French asraafoi ni yɔɔ Russia tafãa lɛ mli lɛ tsake amɛ okpɔŋɔi ni egboi lɛ amɛfee buu, ni amɛkpã mli amɛbote mli lɛ po. Kɛfata he lɛ, shikpɔŋ wulu lɛ nɔ saji ahe wielɔi krokomɛi etsɔɔ akɛ, beni ashɛrɛ Yona ashwie ŋshɔ lɛ mli kɛjɛ Yopa lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ, ejo foi kɛtee lɛlɛ kroko ni bɛŋkɛ jɛmɛ lɛ mli amrɔ nɔŋŋ, lɛlɛ ko ni ŋshɔŋloo ko yɔɔ mli kɛha mfoniri-yitso; ni, mikɛbaafata he akɛ, ekolɛ atsɛɔ lɛ “The Whale,” taakɛ abaptisiɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi komɛi ŋmɛnɛ akɛ “Shark,” “Gull,” “Eagle” lɛ. Ni asaŋ shishitsɔɔmɔ he nilelɔi ni he esa ni esusu akɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni atsĩ tã yɛ Yona wolo lɛ mli lɛ tsɔɔ wala yiwalaheremɔ ko kɛkɛ—kɔɔyɔɔ kotoku ko ni mli edɔ—ni gbalɔ ni yɔɔ oshara mli lɛ tsɔ nu kɛtee mli, ni no hewɔ lɛ ahere lɛ kɛjɛ nu mli hiɛkpatamɔ lɛ mli lɛ hu bɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni Sag-Harbor ohiafo lɛ ehiii fe fɛɛ yɛ he fɛɛ he. Shi eyɛ yiŋtoo kroko lolo hewɔ ni ebɛ hemɔkɛyeli lɛ. Enɛ ji enɛ, kɛji mikaiɔ jogbaŋŋ: ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mi Yona yɛ Mediteranea Ŋshɔ lɛ mli, ni yɛ gbii etɛ sɛɛ lɛ, ashwie lɛ yɛ he ko ni kɛ Ninive, ni ji maŋ ko ni yɔɔ Tigris lɛ he lɛ teŋ jɛkɛmɔ ji gbii etɛ gbɛfaa, ni eji nɔ ni fe gbii etɛ gbɛfaa kɛjɛ Mediteranea ŋshɔ lɛ naa he ni bɛŋkɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ kwraa. Mɛɛ gbɛ nɔ ebaa lɛ nakai?

Shi ani gbɛ kroko bɛ ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ baatsɔ nɔ ekɛ gbalɔ lɛ aba shikpɔŋ yɛ nakai gbɛ kuku ni kɛ Ninive teŋ jɛkɛmɔ yɔɔ lɛ mli? Hɛɛ. Ekolɛ ebaawo lɛ kɛbɔle ehe kɛtsɔ Hiɛnɔkamɔ Kpakpa lɛ gbɛ lɛ nɔ. Shi kɛji akɛ awieee gbɛ ni atsɔɔ nɔ atsɔɔ Mediteranea Ŋshɔ lɛ mli fɛɛ lɛ he, kɛ gbɛ kroko ni atsɔɔ nɔ kɛyaa Persia Ŋshɔ lɛ kɛ Ŋshɔ Tsuru lɛ nɔ lɛ he lɛ, susumɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ baabi ni abɔle Afrika fɛɛ he kwraa yɛ gbii etɛ mli, ni awieee Tigris nui lɛ ahe, ni bɛŋkɛ he ni Ninive yɔɔ lɛ, ni eji nɔ ko ni mli ekwɔɔɔ tsɔ. Yona ni ekɛ Cape of Good Hope lɛ shwie shi yɛ gbi kome mli mra nɛkɛ lɛ baajie nakai ŋshɔkpɔ wulu lɛ namɔ he woo lɛ kɛjɛ Bartholomew Diaz, mɔ ni ehe gbɛi waa akɛ ena lɛ lɛ dɛŋ, ni no baaha ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ yinɔsane afee amalelɔ.

Shi Sag-Harbor momo lɛ kwashiai awiemɔi nɛɛ fɛɛ jie kwashiai ajwɛŋmɔ he henɔwomɔ ni eyɔɔ lɛ kpo pɛ—nɔ ko ni esa akɛ awie ashi lolo yɛ emli, ejaakɛ ena akɛ nikasemɔ fioo pɛ eyɔɔ ja nɔ ni ejie kɛjɛ hulu lɛ kɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ pɛ. Mikɛɔ akɛ etsɔɔ kwashiai asane, henɔwomɔ ni bɛ Nyɔŋmɔjamɔ, kɛ nyaŋemɔ, abonsam atuatsemɔ ni ekɛteɔ shi ewoɔ osɔfoi ni abuɔ amɛ waa lɛ pɛ. Ejaakɛ Portugal Katolik osɔfo ko ji mɔ ni kɛ susumɔ nɛɛ diɛŋtsɛ ni kɔɔ Yona ni eya Ninive kɛtsɔ Hiɛnɔkamɔ Kpakpa Ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ he lɛ tee hiɛ akɛ okadi ni haa naakpɛɛ nii ni yaa nɔ lɛ yaa hiɛ. Ni eba lɛ nakai. Kɛfata he lɛ, kɛbashi ŋmɛnɛ lɛ, Turkeybii ni yɔɔ nilee babaoo lɛ heɔ Yona yinɔsane mli sane lɛ amɛyeɔ waa. Ni aaafee afii ohai etɛ ni eho nɛ lɛ, Ŋleshi gbɛfalɔ ko ni yɔɔ Harris Voyages wolo momo lɛ mli lɛ wie Turkey Sɔlemɔtsu ko ni ama kɛwo Yona hiɛ nyam lɛ he, ni Sɔlemɔtsu lɛ ji naakpɛɛ kane ko ni tsoɔ ni mu ko bɛ mli.





YITSƆƆ 84. Pitchpoling.

Bɔni afee ni amɛjo foi mlɛo ni amɛjo foi oya lɛ, afɔɔ tsɔnei lɛ ashishifãi lɛ ahe mu; ni yɛ yiŋtoo kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ hewɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛlɔi komɛi feɔ nitsumɔ ko ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ yɛ amɛlɛlɛ lɛ nɔ; amɛwoɔ shishigbɛ mu. Ni esaaa akɛ ŋwanejee ko bɛ he hu akɛ, akɛni gbɛjianɔtoo ni tamɔ nɛkɛ nyɛŋ aye mɔ ko awui hewɔ lɛ, ekolɛ sɛɛnamɔ ko bɛ he ni abuuu; kɛji akɛ asusu akɛ mu kɛ nu ji henyɛlɔi; akɛ mu ji nɔ ko ni shwieɔ shi, ni akɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ hiɛ ji ni eha lɛlɛ lɛ ashwie shi kɛ ekãa. Queequeg heɔ eyeɔ waa akɛ esa akɛ afɔ elɛlɛ lɛ mu, ni leebi ko beni German lɛlɛ ni ji Jungfrau lɛ laaje lɛ sɛɛ etsɛɛɛ tsɔ lɛ, ekɛ piŋmɔi ni fe kusum nifeemɔi tsu nii yɛ nakai nitsumɔ lɛ mli; ni efoɔ eshishi, he ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ esɛɛ lɛ, ni ekɛ ehe miikpa muji lɛ mli tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ miitao ni ekɛ yitsɔi ni ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ lɛlɛ lɛ shishi lɛ mli lɛ aba. Etamɔ nɔ ni eetsu nii yɛ toiboo mli eha henumɔ pɔtɛɛ ko. Ni asaŋ ehiɛɛɛ nɔ ni nifeemɔ lɛ esaaa.

Beni shɛɔ shwane mli lɛ, akwɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi; shi beni lɛlɛ lɛ ba shi kɛba amɛŋɔɔ pɛ kɛkɛ ni amɛtsɔ amɛhe ni amɛjo foi kɛ oyaiyeli; foidamɔ ni bɛ gbɛjianɔtoo mli, tamɔ Cleopatra lɛlɛi ni jɛ Actium lɛ.

Fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, lɛlɛi lɛ nyiɛ amɛsɛɛ, ni Stubb nɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni nyiɛ hiɛ. Kɛtsɔ mɔdɛŋbɔɔ babaoo nɔ lɛ, naagbee lɛ Tashtego ye omanye yɛ dade kome ni ekɛdu lɛ mli; shi ŋshɔŋloo ni etswa lɛ lɛ, ni etswaaa gbɛɛmɔ ko kwraa lɛ, tee nɔ efo foi kɛtee ŋwɛigbɛ lolo, ni ekɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ oya fata he. Esa akɛ dade ni adu lɛ nɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni sɛɛ efooo lɛ ajie lɛ mra loo sɛɛ mli ni nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ni baanyɛ ajie lɛ kpo. Ebafee nɔ ni he hiaa waa akɛ aaatswa ŋshɔŋloo ni foɔ lɛ, loo aaatsui anyɔ emli akɛ ebaafite lɛ. Shi ni ekɛgbala lɛlɛ lɛ kɛya esɛɛ lɛ nyɛŋ aba mli, efo nu lɛ oya waa ni ekɛ mlifu tsu nii. No sɛɛ lɛ, mɛni eshwɛ?

Yɛ naakpɛɛ nibii kɛ ŋaa nitsumɔi fɛɛ ateŋ, nine mli ŋaa kɛ ŋaa nibii ni anyɛɛɛ akane, ni bei pii lɛ anyɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsulɔ ni he esa waa lɛ nɔ lɛ, eko kwraa bɛ ni fe nakai nifeemɔ kpakpa ni akɛ gãimlibii ni atsɛɔ lɛ pitchpoling lɛ tsuɔ nii lɛ. Klante bibioo, loo klante ni mli kwɔ, yɛ enitsumɔi fɛɛ amli lɛ, ekɛ nɔ ko ni tamɔ lɛ fɔ̃ɔɔ ehe nɔ. Ehe miihia waa pɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni joɔ foi ni bɛ hewalɛ; nɔ ni yɔɔ mli waa kɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ mli ji naakpɛɛ gbɛ ni akɛ gãimlibii kakadaŋŋ lɛ shwieɔ lɛlɛ ko ni hosoɔ waa, ni shwieɔ shi, yɛ hiɛyaa ni fe nine shishi lɛ mli pɛpɛɛpɛ. Kɛ akɛ dade kɛ tsei fata he lɛ, akpɔlɔ muu lɛ fɛɛ kɛlɛ ji aaafee ninetalɔi nyɔŋma loo nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ; tso lɛ mli tse kwraa fe harpoon lɛ, ni akɛ nɔ ko ni tsiimɔ waaa hu fee—pine. Akɛ kpã bibioo ko ni atsɛɔ lɛ warp, ni kɛlɛ waa, ni abaanyɛ atsɔ nɔ agbala lɛ kɛku sɛɛ kɛba nine lɛ mli beni atswa dart sɛɛ lɛ ewo mli.

Shi dani wɔbaaya hiɛ lɛ, ehe miihia ni wɔtsĩ tã yɛ biɛ, akɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ abaanyɛ atswa harpoon lɛ yɛ gbɛ kome nɔ kɛ lance lɛ moŋ, shi bei pii lɛ, afeee nakai; ni kɛ́ afee lɛ, eyeee omanye shii abɔ lolo, yɛ harpoon lɛ tsiimɔ ni fa kɛ elɛɛmɔ ni baa shi kɛ akɛto lance lɛ he lɛ hewɔ, ni yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ ebafeɔ naagbai ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, taakɛ eji nɔ ni afɔɔ feemɔ lɛ, esa akɛ oya oya kɛya ŋshɔŋloo ko he klɛŋklɛŋ, dani ŋshɔŋloo ko ni akɛtswaa ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ aba mli.

Kwɛmɔ Stubb agbɛnɛ; nuu ko ni kɛjɛ eŋmɔlɔ, ejɛ etsui mli efee kpoo kɛ hejɔlɛ ni eyɔɔ yɛ oyaiyeli ni mli wa fe fɛɛ mli lɛ, he esa krɛdɛɛ koni efee nɔ ni hi fe fɛɛ yɛ pitchpoling mli. Kwɛmɔ lɛ; edamɔ shi jaŋŋ yɛ lɛlɛ ni fãa lɛ hiɛ ni atswa lɛ mli; ni akɛ kooloo yitsɔi ebɔ he lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ni gbalaa lɛ lɛ hiɛ ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai ejwɛ. Beni Stubb hiɛɔ lance kakadaŋŋ lɛ mli mlɛo, ni ekwɛɔ elɛɛmɔ lɛ mli shii enyɔ loo shii etɛ koni ena kɛji eja pɛpɛɛpɛ lɛ, ekɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa ni akɛtswaa lala lɛ buaa lala lɛ he tsɔne lɛ naa yɛ enine kome mli, bɔni afee ni ekɛ enaagbee ni yɔɔ ehe lɛ ahi enine mli, ni eshiɔ fã ni eshwɛ lɛ ni nɔ ko akatsi enaa. Kɛkɛ ni ehiɛ lance lɛ mli obɔ dani etsitsi lɛ teŋgbɛ, ni ekɛto ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he; beni, ekɛha ehe lɛ, ekɛ sɛɛgbɛ ni yɔɔ enine mli lɛ shwieɔ shi fiofio, ni no haa ewoɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ nɔ kɛyashiɔ be mli ni tawuu nii lɛ damɔ enine nɔ jogbaŋŋ, ni ji ninetalɔi nyɔŋma kɛ enumɔ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli. Esusuɔ ohe tamɔ mɔ ko ni feɔ shwɛmɔi, ni ekɛ tso kakadaŋŋ ko ni yɔɔ etsitsi nɔ lɛ ŋmɛɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ. Be ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ kɛ oyaiyeli ni bɛ gbɛi, yɛ ŋwɛi ŋta ni nɔ kwɔ waa mli lɛ, dade ni kpɛɔ lɛ tsɔɔ he ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ yɔɔ lɛ mli, ni ekpokpoɔ yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ wala he lɛ. Yɛ nu ni mli tse najiaŋ lɛ, agbɛnɛ ekɛ lá tsuru shwieɔ shi.

“No shwie spigot lɛ kɛjɛ emli!” Stubb bolɔ. "'Eji July gbi ni gbooo ni ji Ejwɛ lɛ; esa akɛ nu jɛɛhei fɛɛ atsu wain he nii ŋmɛnɛ! Ani agbɛnɛ, eji Orleans whisky momo, loo Ohio momo, loo Monongahela momo ni awieee he! No sɛɛ lɛ, Tashtego, gbekɛ nuu, masumɔ ni omɔ canakin ko mli kɛya jet lɛ he, ni wɔbaanu, be fɛɛ be, wɔbaabɔle he! choice punch yɛ e-spout-hole lɛ mligbɛ ni egbɛ eshwa jɛmɛ lɛ mli, ni kɛjɛ nakai punch-bowl ni hiɛ ka lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ nibii ni wala yɔɔ amɛmli lɛ atsu nii.”

Shii abɔ yɛ shwɛmɔ wiemɔi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ, atĩɔ gãimlibi ni yɔɔ ŋaa lɛ mli, ni akpɔlɔ lɛ kuɔ esɛɛ kɛyaa enuŋtsɔ ŋɔɔ tamɔ tsina ni akɛ ŋaa emɔ lɛ. Whale ni yeɔ piŋmɔ lɛ yaa e-flurry lɛ mli; tsɔne ni akɛgbalaa tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ egbɔjɔ, ni tsɔne kudɔlɔ lɛ shwieɔ shi kɛyaa sɛɛgbɛ, ekɛ eniji shwieɔ shi, ni ekwɛɔ bɔ ni kooloo lɛ gboɔ lɛ dioo.





YITSƆƆ 85. Nugbɔshiimɔ.

Nakai akɛ afii akpei enumɔ nɛ—ni mɔ ko mɔ ko leee afii akpekpei abɔ ni eho—kulɛ esa akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi wuji lɛ ashwie ŋshɔ lɛ fɛɛ nɔ, ni amɛshwie ŋshɔ lɛ mli abɔɔi lɛ anɔ ni amɛfee amɛ muji, tamɔ bɔ ni afeɔ yɛ tsɛnsii babaoo ni akɛshwieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli loo ni feɔ muji lɛ amli lɛ; ni akɛ afii ohai komɛi ni eho nɛ lɛ, kulɛ esa akɛ gbɔbilɔi akpei abɔ abɛŋkɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nu jɛɛhe lɛ, ni amɛkwɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni shwieɔ shi kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi nɛɛ—ni esa akɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ afee, ni kɛlɛ, akɛ kɛyashi minitii ni ajɔɔ lɛ nɛɛ mli (minitii nyɔŋma kɛ enumɔ kɛ fã ni eho ŋmɛlɛtswaa kome PM yɛ nɛkɛ December gbi ni ji nyɔŋma kɛ ekpaa nɛɛ mli lolo lɛ w5 it1, AD1 . ji, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, nu diɛŋtsɛ, loo nɔ ko nɔ ko fe lasu—enɛ ji nɔ ko ni sa kadimɔ waa diɛŋtsɛ.

No hewɔ lɛ, nyɛhaa wɔkwɛa sane nɛɛ, kɛ nibii komɛi ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni baa trukaa lɛ. Mɔ fɛɛ mɔ le akɛ kɛtsɔ amɛ ŋmeii lɛ ahe ŋaa krɛdɛɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ŋmeii akutsei lɛ fɛɛ muɔ kɔɔyɔɔ ni be fɛɛ be lɛ kɛ nɔ ni amɛfoɔ mli lɛ feɔ ekome lɛ; no hewɔ lɛ, herring loo cod baanyɛ ahi shi afii ohai kome, ni ewooo eyitso nɔ shikome po kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ. Shi yɛ emli gbɛjianɔtoo ni kadiɔ ni haa lɛ fɛ̃i daa, tamɔ gbɔmɔ nɔ lɛ hewɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ baanyɛ ahi shi kɛtsɔ kɔɔyɔɔ ni ajie lɛ kpo yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli tse lɛ mli lɛ ni eeemu lɛ nɔ pɛ. No hewɔ lɛ ehe hiaa ni eyasara ŋwɛi je lɛ yɛ be kɛ bei amli. Shi enyɛŋ emu kɛtsɔ enaabu nɔ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, ejaakɛ, yɛ esubaŋ ni eyɔɔ daa lɛ mli lɛ, kɛ hoo lɛ, afu Sperm Whale lɛ daaŋ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ shishi ninetalɔi kpaanyɔ; ni nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, ekɔɔyɔɔ mli tsɔne lɛ kɛ enaabu bɛ tsakpaa ko kwraa. Dabi, etsɔɔ e-spiracle lɛ pɛ nɔ emuɔ; ni enɛ yɛ eyitso nɔ.

Kɛ mikɛɛ, akɛ yɛ adebɔɔ fɛɛ adebɔɔ mli lɛ, mumɔ ji nitsumɔ ko pɛ ni he hiaa waa kɛha wala, yɛ bɔ ni ejieɔ nɔ ko kɛjɛɔ kɔɔyɔɔ mli, ni kɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ akɛba lá lɛ he lɛ, ehaa lá lɛ shishitoo mla ni haa mɔ wala lɛ haa lɛ, misusuuu akɛ matɔmɔ; eyɛ mli akɛ ekolɛ mikɛ jeŋ nilee mli wiemɔi komɛi ni he ehiaaa baatsu nii moŋ. Susumɔ he akɛ, ni nɔ ni nyiɛɔ sɛɛ ji akɛ, kɛji akɛ abaanyɛ atsɔ mumɔ kome nɔ ajie lá fɛɛ ni yɔɔ gbɔmɔ mli lɛ mli lɛ, no lɛ ekolɛ ebaatsi etsitsi naa ni ekɛ ekroko akaŋɔɔ be babaoo. No ji akɛ, no mli lɛ ebaahi shi ni emuuu. Eyɛ mli akɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ejaaa moŋ, shi nakai ji bɔ ni eji yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he pɛpɛɛpɛ, ni ehiɔ shi yɛ gbɛjianɔtoo naa, yɛ be kɛ bei amli, eŋmɛlɛtswaa muu lɛ fɛɛ kɛ nɔ ni fe nakai (kɛji eyɛ shishigbɛ) ni ekɛmuuu mumɔ kome po, loo bɔ ni eji yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, emuɔ kɔɔyɔɔ bibioo ko; ejaakɛ, kaimɔ akɛ, ebɛ ŋmeii.
