EISHOMO 94. Okunyiganyiga omukono.

Ekyo kinyanja kya Stubb, ekyaguzirwe munonga, kikareetwa nk’oku kihikire aha rubaju rwa Pequod, ahu emirimo egyo yoona ey’okushara n’okuhanika eyaabaire egambirweho omu bwijwire enyima, yaabaire neekira kurabamu, nangwa n’okuhingisa Heidelburgh Tun, nari Case.

Obu abamwe baabaire nibeejumbira omu murimo ogu ogw’aha muheru, abandi bakaba bakora okukurura ebiyungu ebihango, ahonaaho byaheza kwijura amaizi g’ekihama; kandi obwire obuhikire ku bwahikire, amaizi g’ekihama nigo gamwe gakaba gahindurwa n’obwegyendesereza gatakagiire omu mirimo y’okugyezesa, eya anon.

Kikaba kyakyeire kandi kyahinduka ekibiriiti kuhika aha rurengo rw’okugira ngu, n’abandi baingi, ku naashutami omu maisho g’okwoga kwa Constantine okuhango okwakyo, nkashanga omu muringo gw’okutangaaza gukozirwe sementi omu bicupuri, aha n’aha niguzengyerera omu kicweka ky’amaizi. Kikaba kiri omurimo gwaitu okunyiganyiga ebizimba ebi kugaruka omu maizi. Omurimo ogurikushemeza kandi ogutarimu amajuta! N’eky’okutangaaza kureeba ngu ira amaizi g’ekihama aga gakaba gari ekirungo ky’okweboneza ekirikukunda munonga. Ekirikushoborokyerwa gye nk’ekyo! ekirikushemeza nk’ekyo! omugoyezi nk’ogwo! ekirungo ky’okushemeza nk’ekyo! Bwanyima y’okugira emikono yangye omunda kumara edakiika nkye zonka, enkumu zangye zikahurira nk’enzigye, kandi zatandika, nk’oku kirikugambwa, kuhinduka nk’enjoka n’okuzirika.

Nk’oku naabaire nshutami aho n’obusingye bwangye, nshabire amaguru aha nkungu; bwanyima y’okukora munonga aha nshonga y’omuyaga; ahansi y’eiguru rya bururu eririmu obusingye; emeeri eri ahansi y’omuyaga ogutarikukora gye, kandi erikwetoorora omu busingye; nk’oku naabaire nyozya engaro zangye omu bihimba ebyo ebigumire kandi ebigumire eby’ebicweka by’omubiri ebitaahiremu, ebirukire haihi omu shaaha emwe; nk'oku baabaire nibahendagura enkumu zangye, bakasheesha obugaiga bwabo bwona, nk'emizaabibu ehikire omu viinyo yaabo; nk’oku naabaire ninfuka omugaju ogwo ogutaine kamogo, —omu muringo gw’amazima n’omu mazima, nk’omugaju gw’ebimuri by’omu bwire bw’enjura; Ninkumanyisa ngu kumara obwire obwo nkaba ntuura nk'omu kishaka ky'omushenyi; Nkeebwa byona ebirikukwata aha kiraganiso kyaitu ekirikutiinisa; omu nshaho egyo etarikushoboororwa, nkayozya engaro zangye n’omutima gwangye; Nkaba ndi haihi kutandika kuha ekitiinisa enyikiriza y’ebishuba eya ira eya Paracelsan ngu amaizi g’ekihama gaine omugasho ogutarikukira kureebwa omu kukyendeeza obutagatsi bw’ekiniga; obu naabaire ninyoga omu kwoga okwo, nkahurira nyine obugabe bw’obuhangwa kuruga omu bubi bwona, nari obushaarizi, nari obubi, obw’omuringo gwona.

Okunyiga! okunyiga! okunyiga! omu kasheeshe koona; Nkanyiganyiga egyo nshaho kuhitsya obu nyowe nyenka naabaire ndi haihi kugishuuma; Nkanyiganyiga amaizi ago g’ekihama kuhitsya obu obushema bw’omuringo gw’okutangaaza bwanyijaho; kandi nkashanga ndikuginyiganyiga emikono y’abakozi bagyenzi bangye ntamanyire, n’okuhabisa emikono yaabo ngu n’ebicupuri ebigumire. Enteekateeka nk’egyo ey’okukanya, ey’okukundana, ey’omukago, ey’okukundana niyo okwejumba oku kwazaire; ngu aha muheru nkaba nguma ninbanyiganyiga emikono, kandi n’okureeba ahaiguru omu maisho gaabo n’enyehurira; nk’okugira ngu,—Oh! bagyenzi bangye abakundwa, ahabwenki tushemerire kuguma nitukunda acerbities zoona ez’abantu, nari kumanya obushema bukye nari eitima! Ija; reka tunyige emikono okwehinguririza; ngaaha, twena reka twenyigirizemu; reka twenyige omu nsi yoona omu mate n’enkari z’embabazi zonka.

Nkaba ninza kuguma ninnyiganyiga amaizi ago ebiro byona! Ahabw’okugira ngu hati, ahabw’ebintu bingi ebi naareebire kumara obwire buraingwa, ebirikugarukagaruka, nshangire ngu omu mbeera zoona omuntu aine kuhika aha kugaruza ahansi, nari waaba oine kuhindura, okwetunguura kwe okw’okutunga obusingye oburikubaasa kuhikirizibwa; obutagiteeka hoona omu bwengye nari omu kuteekateeka; kwonka omu mukazi, omutima, ekitanda, emeeza, ekitebe, aha rubaju rw’omuriro, eihanga; hati nk’oku nyetegyereize ebi byona, nyeteekateekire kunyiganyiga omushango ebiro byona. Omu biteekateeko by’okworekwa kw’ekiro, nkareeba emirongooti miraingwa ya baamaraika omu paradiso, buri omwe aine emikono ye omu nsaka y’amaizi g’ekihama.

Hati, obwo turikugamba aha nshonga y’okuteerana, nikiba kihikire kugamba aha bintu ebindi ebirikushushana nabyo, omu bizinesi y’okutebeekanisa enyanja y’okuteerana ahabw’emirimo y’okugyezaho.

Okubanza nihaija embaraasi erikwera, erikwetwa, erikuruga omu kicweka ky’ekyenyanja ekirikukyendeera, kandi n’omu bicweka by’ebihaha bye ebigumire. Nikiguma n’emitsi egumire—ekibunu ky’emibiri—kwonka nikiguma kirimu amajuta. Bwanyima y’okusharaho kuruga aha nshozi y’enyanja, embaraasi erikwera neebanza yaasharaho ebicweka by’omubiri ebirikutwarwa (oblongs) etakagiire omu nkora y’okugiteera. Nibishushana n’ebibumbe by’amabaare ga Berkshire.

Plum-pudding n’ekigambo ekirikuheebwa ebicweka bimwe eby’omubiri gw’enyanja ebicwekire, aha n’aha birikuhamira aha rubaju rw’ebishaka, kandi ebirikukira kwejumba omu muringo muhango omu kutagira amajuta. Nikigaruramu amaani munonga, ekirikushemeza, kandi ekirungi munonga kureeba. Nk’oku eiziina ryakyo ririkworeka, n’ey’erangi y’omuhendo munonga, erimu ebicupuri, erimu ebicweka by’omushana n’eby’ezaabu, erimu ebicucu by’erangi erikutukura munonga n’eya papo. Ni plums z’amabaare g’omuhendo, omu bishushani bya citron. N’obu haraabe hariho enshonga, nikiguma kwezibira kugirya. Ninkihamya, ngu rimwe nkaiba enyima y’omuti gw’omumaisho kugyezaho. Kikaba nikishemeza ekintu nk’oku naabaire nyine kuteekateeka ngu ekirungo ky’obugabe kuruga omu kibero kya Louis le Gros kikaba nikibaasa kushemeza, turikuteekateeka ngu akaitwa ekiro ky’okubanza bwanyima y’obwire bw’enyama y’entare, kandi obwire obwo obw’enyama y’entare bwonka bukaba buri obw’obwire obwo n’omuzaabibu murungi ogutari gwa buriijo ogw’emisiri ya Champagne.

Hariho ekindi kintu, kandi eky’omwe munonga, ekirikuhinduka omu nkora ya bizinesi egi, kwonka eki ndikuhurira ngu nikibuzabuza munonga ekirikumara kushoboorora. Nikyetwa ekibunu ky’omubiri; eiziina ry’okubanza n’abanyabwengye, kandi n’obuhangwa bw’ekintu nikwo buri. Ni inefably oozy, stringy affair, erikukira kushangwa omu biyungu by’amaizi g’ekihama, bwanyima y’okumara obwire buraingwa garikunyiganyiga, kandi bwanyima y’okugisheenya. Ninkikwata nk’ebihimba by’ekibokisi ebikye omu muringo gw’okutangaaza, ebicwekire, birikuteerana.

Gurry, eki barikweta, n’ekigambo ekihikire eky’abajubi b’ebinyonyi ebirikwetwa right whalemen, kwonka obumwe omu muringo gw’omutaano nikikoresibwa abajubi b’enkari. Nikimanyisa ekintu ekirikwiragura, ekirikukwaata ekirikusharaho aha mugongo gwa Greenland nari right whale, kandi bingi aharibyo nibishweka ebishengye by’emyoyo egyo ey’ahansi erikuhiiga Leviathan ogwo otaine kitiinisa.

Abanyabuzaare. Strictly ekigambo eki ti ky’obuhangwa omu bigambo by’enyanja. Kwonka nk’oku kirikukoresibwa abavugi b’enyanja, nikiba kityo. Ekikwato ky’okuteera enyanja (whaleman’s nipper) n’ekicweka kigufu ekigumire eky’ebintu ebiine emitsi ebiteirwe kuruga omu kicweka ekirikukyendeera eky’omukira gwa Leviathan: nikihika ahari inci emwe omu buhango, kandi aha bindi, nikihikaana n’obuhango bw’ekicweka ky’ekyoma eky’enkoni. Edgewise neegyendera aha rubaju rw’amajuta, neekora nk’ekicupuri ky’oruhu; kandi ahabw’okushemeza ebitaine iziina, nk’eby’obufumu, nibireeta hamwe n’oburofa bwona.

Kwonka okumanya enshonga ezi zoona ez’obushema, omuringo gwawe murungi n’okushuuma omurundi gumwe omu kishengye kya blubber, okagira orubazo ruraingwa n’abasibe baakyo. Ekicweka eki kikaba kyagambirweho enyima nk’ekicweka ky’okutaho ebicweka by’ebitambaara, ku birikujurwa kandi bikaihwaho kuruga aha nkungu. Obwire obuhikire obw’okusharaho ebirimu ku burikuhika, omuzigo ogu niguba guri ogw’okutiina aba tyro boona, namunonga nyekiro. Aha rubaju rumwe, orurikwaka etabaaza etarikureebwa gye, omwanya gusigaireho gutarimu abakozi. Okutwarira hamwe nizigyenda babiri babiri, —omunya-pike-n’omunya-gaff n’omunya-spade. Ekikwato ky’okuteera enyanja (whaling-pike) nikishushana n’eky’okurwanisa ky’okutemba emeeri ekiine eiziina rimwe. Gafu n’ekintu nk’ekikwato ky’okuteera obwato. Omuvugi w’emotoka y’emigugu naakwata aha rupapura rw’ebicupuri, kandi ateeraho kukizibira kusheesheka, obwo emeeri erikweshushaniriza n’okugurukaguruka. Omu bwire obwo, omuteezi w’embaraasi ayemereire aha rupapura rwonka, arikurusharaho omu bicweka by’embaraasi ebirikutwarwa. Ekicupuri eki kiine obushaarizi nk’oku hone erikubaasa kukikora; ebigyere by’omuvugi w’emotoka y’omwika tibiine nkaito; ekintu eki ayemereireho obumwe nikiija kumusheesheka kitarikubaasa kuzibirwa, nk’ekicupuri. Ku araabe yaasharaho kimwe aha bigyere bye, nari eky’omwe aha bahwezi be, noija kutangaara munonga? Ebigyere nibibura omu bashaija abakuzire omu blubber-room.





EISHOMO RYA 95. Ekikaari.

Ku waakubaire oteire aha bwato bwa Pequod omu bwire bumwe obw’okucondooza oku bwanyima y’okufa kw’enyanja y’enyanja; kandi kuri waagyendeire omu maisho haihi n’omuyaga, ninkihamya ngu okaabaire nookyebera n’okwenda kumanya ekintu ekitari ky’okutangaaza munonga, eky’omutaano, eki waakubaire nooreeba aho, kigwejegyeire omu bureingwa omu bicupuri by’omuyaga. Ti kiina ky’okutangaaza ekiri omu mutwe muhango gw’enyanja; ti prodigy y’orubaju rwe orw’ahansi orutaine nshonga; ti ky’okutangaaza ky’omukira gwe ogurikushushana; tihariho na kimwe ahari ebi ekyakubaasa kukutangaaza munonga, nk’ekicweka ky’okureeba ekikopo ekyo ekitarikubarwa,—ekirikukiraho oburaingwa bw’omu Kentucky, haihi n’ekigyere kimwe omu bugazi ahansi, kandi kirikwiragura nk’ekya Yojo, ekishushani ky’omuti gwa ebony ekya Queequeg. Kandi ekishushani, buzima, n'ekyo; nari, okukiraho, omu bwire bwa ira, okushushana kwayo kukaba kuri. Ekishushani nk’ekyo ekyashangirwe omu bishaka by’omugabekazi Maaka omuri Buyudaaya; kandi ahabw'okuramya eki, Omugabe Asa, mutabani we, akamwihaho, yaacwekyereza ekishushani ekyo, yaakiyotsya nk'ekintu ekirikuzira aha mugyera Kedroni, nk'oku kirikworekwa omu mwirima omu shuura ya 15 ey'ekitabo ky'okubanza eky'Abagabe.

Reeba omuvugi w’enyanja, orikwetwa mincer, hati orikwija, kandi arikuhwerwa abanywani be babiri, arikugaruka enyima munonga ahabw’enyanja ya grandissimus, nk’oku abavugi b’enyanja barikugyeta, kandi aine ebibega bifukamire, arikugiteera nk’omuvugi w’emotoka y’emigugu orikutwara munywani we ofiire kuruga omu musiri. Okugitunguura aha mutwe gw’enyanja, hati naagumizamu omu muringo gw’ekibumbe ky’ekibumbe (cylindrical) kwihaho oruhu rwayo orurikwiragura, nk’oku omuhiigi w’omu Afirika arikwihaho oruhu rwa boa. Eki kikozirwe ahindura oruhu omunda n’aheeru, nk’okuguru kwa pantaloon; nikigiha okutambatambura kurungi, kugira ngu haihi kikanyise oburaingwa bwayo emirundi ebiri; kandi aha muheru akagihanika, ejanjaire gye, omu nshaho, ngu eyome. Ere long, nikitwarwa ahansi; ku arikugiihaho fuuti nka ishatu, arikuza aha rubaju oruriho enshonda zoona, reero akasharaho ebicweka bibiri by’okuteeramu ebiina by’emikono aha rubaju orundi, naaba ayeshekyerera omu mubiri omu bureingwa. Omunyabwengye hati ayemereire omu maisho gaawe akata sente omu bihandiiko by’ebiragiro ebiijwire eby’okwetwa kwe. Tikirikwijukwa ekiragiro kye kyona, okuta sente omu bizinesi egi yonka nikwija kumurinda gye, obwo arikukora emirimo y’omutaano ey’omu ofiisi ye.

EISHOMO 96. Emirimo y’okugyezaho.

Oyihireho amaato ge agarikuhanikwa, omuvugi w’enyanja owa America naataanisibwa aheeru ahabw’okukora emirimo ye ey’okugyezaho. Naayoreka okutashushana kw’okutangaaza okw’amabaare agagumire munonga agarikuteerana n’omuti gw’omukyere n’omuti gw’omukyere omu kuhanga emeeri ehweire. Ni nk’oku kuruga omu musiri gw’omutaano batwaire ekiyungu ky’amatafaari kuza aha mbaaho ze.

Emirimo y’okugyezaho neebyarwa ahagati y’omuti gw’omumaisho n’omuti muhango, ekicweka ekirikukirayo kuba eky’omwanya aha rubaju rw’enyanja. Embaaho eziri ahansi n’ez’amaani g’omutaano, zitebeekanisiibwe kubaasa kugumira oburemeezi bw’eitafaari n’eitaka ebiri haihi kuba bigumire, ebirikwingana fuuti ikumi ahari square munaana, kandi ebirikwingana fuuti itaano omu buraingwa. Omusingi tigurikutaaha omu mutwe gw’enyanja, kwonka amabaare nigahamibwa gye aha mutwe gw’enyanja ahabw’amaguru g’ekyoma agarikuremeera garikuguhamya embaju zoona, kandi gakaguteera ahansi kuhika aha mbaaho. Aha mbaju zaayo kiine ebiti, kandi ahaiguru kishwekirwe kimwe omuhanda muhango, ogurikushenda kandi ogurimu ebipande. Okwihaho ekiina eki nitushohoza ebiyungu bikuru by’okugyezesa, bibiri omu mubaro, kandi buri kimwe kiine obushoboorozi bw’ebidomora bingi. Ku zitarikukoresibwa, niziguma zishemeire omu muringo gw’okutangaaza. Obumwe nizishemezibwa n’amabaare g’esabuuni n’omushenyi, okuhitsya obu zirikujwara omunda nk’ebikopo by’efeeza eby’okuteera. Omu bwire bw’okurinda nyekiro abavugi b’enyanja abakuzire abamwe abaine ekiniga nibaija kuzigurukiramu kandi beezingireyo kuhuumuraho. Obu barikukora omu kubishemeza—omushaija omwe omuri buri nyungu, aha rubaju n’orubaju—okugaaniira kwingi okw’ekihama nikukorwa, aha minwa y’ekyoma. Kandi n’omwanya gw’okuteekateeka munonga aha bibaro. Omu mukono gwa bumosho try-pot ya Pequod, n’eibare rya sabuuni ririkwetoorora n’obweziriki, ahu naabandize kukwatwaho omu muringo ogutarikuhikaana n’amazima g’okutangaaza, ngu omu geometry emibiri yoona erikwetoorora aha cycloid, eibaare ryangye rya sabuuni nk’eky’okureeberaho, neija kuruga omu nshonga yoona omu bwire nibwo bumwe.

Okwihaho orubaaho rw’omuriro kuruga omu maisho g’emirimo y’okugyezesa, amabaare agatariho kintu kyona ag’orubaju orwo nigareebeka, nigataahamu eminwa ebiri y’ekyoma ey’ebitara, ahansi y’ebiyungu butunu. Eminwa egi erimu enyiigi z’ekyoma eziremeire. Okwotsya kw’amaani okw’omuriro nikuzibirwa kwemanyisa aha rubaju rw’enyanja, kurabira omu kiina ekitari kihango ekirikuhika ahansi y’ekicweka kyona ekikingire eky’emirimo. Ahabw’omuhanda oguteirwe enyima, ekiina eki nikiguma nikigaruka kiijwire amaizi ahonaaho nk’oku garikusheesheka. Tihariho n’eby’okurya by’aheeru; niziigura butoosha kuruga aha kisiika ky’enyima. Kandi aha reka tugarukye enyima kumara akaire kakye.

Ni shaaha nka mwenda z’ekiro obu emirimo y’okugyezaho eya Pequod yaabanza kutandika aha rugendo oru oruriho hati. Kikaba kiri ekya Stubb kureeberera bizinesi egyo.

"Byona bitebeekanisiibwe aho? Off hatch, reero, kandi omutandikye. Iwe otekye, okore emirimo." Eki kikaba kiri ekintu kyorobi, ahabw’okuba omubeizi akaba atwire naateera ebihimba bye omu muriro omu bwire bwona obu yaabaire naahinguramu. Aha kigambirwe ngu omu rugendo rw’okuroba enyanja omuriro gw’okubanza omu try-works gwine kuriisibwa kumara obwire n’enku. Bwanyima y’ekyo tihaine enku ezirikukoresibwa, okwihaho nk’omuringo gw’okurahuka kwotsya amajuta g’omutaano. Omu kigambo kimwe, bwanyima y’okugyezibwa, obujuma oburikwera kandi oburikwera, hati oburikwetwa scraps nari fritters, nibuguma burimu ebintu bingi ebitarikukora amajuta. Ebi bihimba nibigaburira omuriro. Nk’omujurizi orikwotsya munonga, nari omunyabwengye orikwekunda, ku arikuheza kukwatwa omuriro, ekinyonyi eki nikitunga amajuta ge kandi kyotsya omubiri gwe. Ku araabe yaarya omwika gwe wenka! ahakuba omwika gwe nigutiinisa kuguhuuta, kandi okuguhuuta oine kuguhuuta, kandi tikwo kwonka, kureka oine kugutuuramu kumara obwire. Nikigira ekinuuko ekitarikugambwa, eky’omu ishwa, eky’Abahindu aharikyo, nk’ekirikubaasa kwehereera haihi n’eby’okuziika. Nikinuuka nk’eibaare rya bumosho ry’ekiro ky’okucwerwa orubanja; ni enshonga ahabw’ekiina.

Ekiro kyahika ahagati y’ekiro emirimo ekaba neetandika kukora omu bwijwire. Tukaba turi gye kuruga omu mutumbi; emeeri ekaba ekozirwe; omuyaga gukaba nigugaruramu amaani; omwirima gw’enyanja y’omu ishwa gukaba guri mwingi munonga. Kwonka omwirima ogwo gukaba gushemezibwa omuriro gw’amaani, ogwabaire niguruga omu bwire bukye kuruga omu nfuuzi z’omucuucu, kandi gukamurikira buri mugoye gw’ahaiguru omu nshaho, nk’oku kyabaire kiri aha muriro gw’Abagriika ogwabaire gumanyirwe munonga. Emeeri eyaabaire neeyotsya ekagumizamu neevuga, nk’oku yaaragiirwe kukora ekikorwa ky’okuhoorera enzigu ataine kwefuuza. N’ahabw’ekyo emeeri z’omu nyanja eza Hydriote, Canaris, ezirimu ebicupuri n’ebiriisa by’ekibiriiti, zikaba niziruga omu myaaro yaazo ey’omu kasheeshe, ziine ebipande bihango by’ebirimi by’okukoresa omu kugyendesa emeeri, zikaba zitwara aha meeri z’Abaturuki, kandi zikazingazinga omu muriro.

Ekishengye ky’okushutamamu, ekyabaire kiihirweho ahaiguru y’emirimo, hati kikaba nikibaha ekikopo kihango omu maisho gaabo.
