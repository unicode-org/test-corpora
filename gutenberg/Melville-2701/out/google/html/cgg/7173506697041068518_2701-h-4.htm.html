Omu biro ebi eby’okutebeekanisa, nyowe na Queequeg tukaba tukira kutaayaayira emeeri egyo, kandi nk’oku kyabaire kiri emirundi mingi nkaba mbuuza ahari Kapiteeni Ahabu, n’oku yaabaire ari, n’obu yaabaire naaza kwija omu bwato bwe. Aha bibuuzo ebi bakaba bagarukamu, ngu akaba naayongyera kuba gye, kandi akaba nateekateekwaho kuza omu bwato buri izooba; omu bwire obwo, abakuru b’emeeri babiri, Pelegi na Biludaadi, bakaba nibabaasa kukora buri kimwe ekyabaire nikyetengyesa kubaasa kuhikaana n’emeeri ey’orugyendo. Kuri noogira ngu nkaba ndi omwesigwa ahariinye nyenka, nkaba ninza kureeba gye omu mutima gwangye ngu nkakora kwonka ekicweka ky’okuteekateeka ngu nkaba nyehaireyo omu muringo ogu aha rugendo ruraingwa nk’orwo, ntaine n’omwe orikuta amaisho gangye aha mushaija owaabaire naija kuba omutegyeki w’orugyendo rwona, ahonaaho obu emeeri yaarugayo aha nyanja erimu abantu baingi. Kwonka omushaija ku arikuteekateeka ngu hariho ekibi kyona, obumwe nikibaho ngu yaaba yaaheza kwejumba omu nshonga egyo, ateeraho kushereka ebi arikuteekateekaho nangwa n’okuruga ahariwe wenka ataine bwengye. Aha muheru kikaheebwa ngu obwire obumwe eizooba eryakuratsireho buzima emeeri neija kutandika. N’ahabw’ekyo omu kasheeshe akaakuratsireho, nyowe na Queequeg tukatandika kare munonga.

Ahabw’okukwatwaho ekibuuzo eki ekirikworeka amazima, nkagarukamu nti, “Eego, nkaba ninteekateeka ngu nkareeba abashaija bana nari bataano, kwonka kikaba kiri ekicuucu munonga kubaasa kuhamya.”

“Ronda oha?”

Nkaba ninza kubarabura ahari—kwonka mutafayo, mutafayo—byona ni kimwe, byona omu ka nabyo;—omushana gw'amaani omu kasheeshe aka, tikwo? Musiibure imwe. Timurikwija kugaruka kubareeba juba munonga, ninteekateeka; okwihaho kyaba kiri omu maisho g'Orukiiko rw'Abaramuzi Abakuru." Kandi n’ebigambo ebi ebicwekire akahendera agyenzire, yansigaho, ahabw’akaanya kakye, omu kutangaara okutari kukye ahabw’obushema bwe obw’okushoberwa.

Aha muheru, twaza aha bwato bwa Pequod, tukashanga buri kimwe kiri omu busingye bwingi, hatariho mwoyo ogurikugyendera. Omuryango gw’akaju gukaba gukingirwe omunda; ebishengye byona bikaba biriho, kandi biine ebikopo by’okuteera. Okuza omu maisho aha forecastle, tukashanga slide ya scuttle eigukire.
