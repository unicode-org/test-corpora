EISHOMO 55. Eby’ebishushani by’ebinyonyi ebihango.

I shall ere long paint to you nk’oku omuntu arikubaasa ataine kanvaasi, ekintu nk’omuringo gw’amazima gw’enyanja nk’oku buzima erikureebeka omu maisho g’omuvugi w’enyanja obu omu mubiri gwe gwonka oguhikire enyanja erikuba ekwatsirwe aha rubaju rw’emeeri y’enyanja kugira ngu ebaasa kuteebwaho omu muringo gw’oburingaaniza aho. Nikibaasa kuba kirungi, n’ahabw’ekyo, enyimaho kuranga ebishushani ebyo eby’okuteekateekaho eby’okwenda kumanya ebirikumuhakanisa n’okuhitsya n’eri izooba n’obumanzi ebirikuhakanisa okwikiriza kw’omunyaihanga. Ni bwire bw’okutereeza ensi omu nshonga egi, turikuhamya ebishushani nk’ebyo eby’enyanja yoona ebitahikire.

Nikibaasa kuba ngu entandikwa y’ebishushani ebyo byona eby’okuhabisa neija kushangwa omu bibumbe bya ira munonga eby’Abahindu, Abanyamisri, n’Abagriika. Ahabw’okuba kuruga omu bwire obwo obw’okuhangahanga kwonka obutari bw’amazima obu aha bipande by’amabaare g’omuhendo eby’amahembo, aha bikondo by’ebishushani, n’aha ngabo, emidaali, ebikopo, n’ebicweka by’esente, enyanja y’omunyanja ekaba neeshushanisibwa omu minzaani y’eby’okurwanisa by’enjegyere nk’eya Saladin, n’omutwe gw’enkofiira nk’ogwa St. kuruga obwo ekintu ky’omuringo nigwo gumwe eky’orusa kisingire, ti mu bishushani ebingi ebirikugambwaho munonga eby’enyanja y’enyanja yonka, kureka n’omu bishushani bingi ebya sayansi ebirikumugambaho.

Hati, ahabw’okugira ngu, ekishushani kya ira munonga ekiriho hati ekirikugira ngu n’eky’enyanja, nikishangwa omu kiina ky’enyanja ekimanyirwe munonga ekya Elephanta, omuri Buhindi. Aba Brahmins nibagira ngu omu bibumbe ebiri haihi kuhwaho eby’omu kikaari ekyo ekitarikwijukwa, eby’obushuubuzi byona n’okukora, buri kimwe ekirikuteekateekwaho eky’omuntu, bikaba bitebeekanisiibwe emyaka mingi kimwe aharibyo kitakabahoga buzima. Tikirikutangaaza n’ahabw’ekyo, ngu omu muringo gumwe omurimo gwaitu ogw’ekitiinisa ogw’okuroba enyanja gukaba gushemerire kuba guriho gukingirwe. Ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Hindoo whale ekirikugambwaho, nikibaho omu kitongore ky’omutaano eky’orugo, ekirikworeka okuhinduka kwa Vishnu omu muringo gwa leviathan, okwega okurikumanyirwa nka Matse Avatar. Kwonka n’obu ekibumbe eki kiraabe kiri ekicweka ky’omuntu n’ekicweka ky’enyanja, kugira ngu kihe omukira gw’eky’aha muheru gwonka, kwonka ekicweka ekyo kikye ahariwe kyona kigwire. Nikireebeka nk’omukira gw’enyanja ogurikukyendeera, okukira emikono mihango ey’ebinyonyi ebihango eby’enyanja y’amazima.

Kwonka gyenda omu Galeries eza ira, kandi oreebe hati ekishushani ky’omutambi mukuru w’Ekikristaayo eky’ekyenyanja eki; ahakuba tarikubaasa kusingura gye kukira Abahindu abaabaireho enyima y’omwegyemure. Ni ekishushani kya Guido ekya Perseus arikucungura Andromeda kuruga omu kicuncu ky’omu nyanja nari eky’enyanja. Guido akatunga nkahi eky’okureeberaho ky’ekihangirwe ekitari kya buriijo nk’ekyo? Kandi Hogarth, omu kusiiga ekishushani nikyo kimwe omu kitabo kye “Perseus Descending,” tarikubaasa kushoboorora gye ekishushani kimwe. Ekicweka kihango ky’ekinyonyi ekyo ekya Hogarthian nikigurukaguruka aha mutwe, kitarikureeta inci emwe y’amaizi. Kiine omuringo gwa howdah aha mugongo gwakyo, n’akanwa kaakyo akarimu amaino agatunguukire ahu ebicu birikwehindura, nikibaasa kutwarwa nk’Omuryango gw’Abahemu ogurikuruga omu mugyera Thames kurabira omu maizi kuza omu Munaara. Reero, hariho enyanja za Prodromus eza Scotch Sibbald eza ira, n’enyanja ya Yona, nk’oku zirikworekwa omu bihandiiko bya Baibuli eza ira n’omu bihandiiko bya ira ebyabaire bihandiikirwe omu ntandikwa. Ebi nibiija kugambwaho ki? Aha bikwatiraine n’enyanja y’omubiki w’ebitabo erikwetoorora nk’omuti gw’omuzaabibu okwehinguririza omuti gw’enkongi erikushuuma—nk’oku erikuteebwaho akabonero kandi erimu ezaabu aha migongo n’aha mpapura z’omutwe gw’ebitabo bingi ebikuru n’ebisya—ekyo n’ekihangirwe ekirikushemeza munonga kwonka eky’omutaano, ekirikutoorezibwa, ninkitwara, kuruga omu bishushani ebirikushushana n’ebyo. N’obu kiraabe kiri ngu omu nsi yoona nikyetwa dolphin, n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, ekyenyanja eki eky’omuhandiiki w’ebitabo ninkyeta okugyezaho enyanja; ahabw’okuba kikaba kigyendereirwe kityo obu ekyoma ekyo kyabanza kukoresibwa. Kikatandikwaho omuhandiiki w’omu Italy owa ira ahu kiri nk’omu kyasha kya 15, omu bwire bw’Okugarukaho kw’Okwega; kandi omu biro ebyo, nangwa n’okuhitsya omu bwire obukye munonga, abantu bakaba nibateekateeka ngu enyanja z’ebinyonyi ebirikwetwa dolphins n’ekika kya Leviathan.

Omu bishushani n’ebindi bishushani by’ebitabo ebimwe ebya ira obumwe noija kubugana okukwatwaho kw’okwenda kumanya munonga aha kinyonyi ekikuru, ahu emiringo yoona ey’ebicupuri, jets d’eau, entuura y’okwotsya n’obufuki, Saratoga na Baden-Baden, birikwija birikubumbabumba kuruga omu bwongo bwe obutaruhire. Omu rupapura rw’omutwe gw’ekitabo ky’okubanza ekya “Okutunguuka omu Kwega” noija kushangamu ebinyonyi ebimwe ebirikwenda kumanya ebirikukiraho.

Kwonka tukareka okuteeraho oku kwona okutari kw’obukugu, ka tureebe ebishushani ebyo ebya leviathan ebirikweshushaniriza nk’ebiine obusingye, ebitebeekanisiibwe ebya sayansi, ebyakozirwe abo abarikumanya. Omu kitabo kya Harris ekya ira eky’engyendo z’omu nyanja harimu ebipande bimwe eby’enyanja ebirugire omu kitabo ky’enyanja eky’Abadaaki, AD 1671, ekiine omutwe “Orugendo rw’enyanja kuza Spitzbergen omu bwato Jonas omu nyanja, Peter Peterson owa Friesland, mukama waabo.” Omuri kimwe aha bipande ebyo, enyanja z’omu nyanja, nk’ebifunjo bihango by’emiti, nizijwekyerwa zigwejegyeire ahagati y’ebizinga by’orubaare, n’edubu ezirikwera nizihunga aha migongo yaazo ezihuriire. Omu rupapura orundi, ekishushani ky’omutaano (prodigious blunder) kikozirwe omu kujwekyera enyanja y’enyanja erimu ebicweka by’omubiri ebirikushushana n’eby’enyanja.

Reero kandi, hariho quarto erikutangaaza, eyahandiikirwe omwe Captain Colnett, Post Captain omu mahe g’enyanja g’Abaingereza, erimu omutwe “Orugendo orurikwetoorora Cape Horn kuza omu nyanja z’omumashuma, n’ekigyendererwa ky’okujanjaaza eby’obuvubi bya Spermaceti Whale.” Omu kitabo eki harimu enteekateeka erikweshushaniriza nk’eri “Ekishushani ky’enyanja erikwetwa Physeter nari Spermaceti whale, ekwatsirwe aha minzaani kuruga ahari emwe eyaitsirwe aha nyanja ya Mexico, omu Kwamunaana, 1793, kandi ekateebwa aha mutwe gw’enyanja.” Tindikubanganisa ngu kapiteeni akaba aine ekishushani eki eky’amazima ekyakwatsirwe ahabw’okugasira abavugi be b’omu nyanja. Okugambaho ​​ekintu kimwe kyonka aharikyo, reka ngambe ngu kiine eriisho eririkuteebwaho, kurugiirira aha rulengo orurikuhererukayo, aha nshozi y’ekinyonyi ekikuzire omu bwijwire, kikaba nikiija kuhindura eriisho ry’enyanja egyo nk’edirisa y’obuta oburaingwa bwa fuuti nka itaano. Ah, omukuru wangye w'amaani, ahabw'enki otatuhaire Yona orikureeba omu riisho eryo!

Kandi n’ebitabo ebirikukirayo kuteerana ebyafaayo by’obuhangwa eby’omutima gw’omuntu ahabw’okugasira abato n’abato, tibiine nshobi y’obushaarizi niyo emwe. Reeba ekitabo ekyo ekirikumanyirwa munonga “Obuhangwa bw’Omuheisi w’Ezaabu.” Omu katabo ka London akahandiikirwe omu bugufu omu mwaka gwa 1807, harimu ebipande by’ekinyonyi ekirikuteekateekwaho kuba “ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa whale” na “narwhale.” Tindikwenda kureebeka nk’otaine mugasho, kwonka ekinyonyi eki ekitarikureebeka gye nikishushana n’empunu eshazirweho; kandi, aha bikwatiraine n’enyanja ya narwhale, okugireebaho kakye nikimara kutangaaza omuntu, ngu omu kyasha eki ekya ikumi n’omwenda enkongoro nk’egyo ekaba neebaasa kukwatwa nk’ey’amazima aha bantu boona ab’obwengye ab’aboojo b’amashomero.

Reero, kandi, omu mwaka gwa 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, omunyabwengye mukuru omu by’obuhangwa, akahandiika ekitabo kya sayansi ekitebeekanisiibwe gye eky’enyanja, ekirimu ebishushani bitari bimwe na bimwe eby’ebika by’enyanja ebitarikushushana. Ebi byona tibihikire byonka, kureka ekishushani ky’enyanja ya Mysticetus nari Greenland whale (nikwo kugira ngu, enyanja ya Right whale), nangwa na Scoresby, omushaija owaabaire aine obumanyirizi kumara obwire buraingwa nk’okukwata aha kika ekyo, naarangirira ngu tikiine ekirikushushana nakyo omu buhangwa.

Kwonka okutaho ekirungo ky’enkofiira aha bizinesi egi yoona ey’okushobya kukaba kuri okw’omunyasayansi Frederick Cuvier, murumuna wa Baron ruranganwa. Omu mwaka gwa 1836, akahandiika ekitabo ekirikwetwa Natural History of Whales, ahu yaaha eki arikweta ekishushani ky’enyanja erikwetwa Sperm Whale. Otakayorekire ekishushani ekyo omunya-Nantucket weena, kikaba kiri kirungi kutebeekanisa okugaruka kwawe omu bugufu kuruga Nantucket. Omu kigambo kimwe, ekinyonyi kya Frederick Cuvier ti kinyonyi ky’omukazi, kureka n’ekinyonyi ky’omukishaka. N’amazima, taratungireho mugasho gw’okugyendera aha nyanja y’enyanja (abashaija nk’abo tibarikukira kutunga), kwonka ekishushani ekyo akakiiha nkahi, n’oha orikubaasa kukimanya? Obundi akakitunga nk’oku omubandiize we omu bya sayansi omu murimo nigwo gumwe, Desmarest, yaatungire emwe aha nshonga ze z’okuiha enda ezihikire; nikwo kugira ngu, kuruga omu kishushani ky’Abachaina. Kandi shi abo Bachaina abo n’abashaija b’amagara ki abaine ekaraamu, ebikopo bingi n’ebikopo by’omutaano nibitumanyisa.

Kandi shi enyanja z'abasiigi b'ebipande ezirikureebeka omu nguuto zihanikire ahaiguru y'amaduuka g'abashuubuzi b'amajuta, niziija kugambwaho ki? Okutwarira hamwe ni Richard wa kashatu. enyanja z’omu nyanja, eziine ebihimba bya dromedary, kandi eziine obushaarizi bwingi; okunywa kyamushana aha bihimba bishatu nari bina eby’abavugi b’enyanja, nikwo kugira ngu amaato g’enyanja agaijwire abavugi b’enyanja: oburema bwabo nibusheesheka omu nyanja z’eshagama n’erangi ya bururu.

Kwonka enshobi ezi nyingi omu kushushanisa enyanja y’enyanja tizirikutangaaza munonga bwanyima y’ebyo byona. Nakyo nikikuru! Ebishushani bya sayansi ebingi birugire omu by’enyanja ebikwatsirwe; kandi ebi nibihikaana n’okuhikiirira nk’oku ekishushani ky’emeeri ecwekire, erimu omugongo ogucwekire, kyakubaire nikijwekyera gye ekinyamaishwa ky’ekitiinisa kyonka omu kwehimbisa kwakyo kwona okutaine rubaju okw’ebibunu n’ebipande. N’obu enjojo ziraabe ziyemereire ahabw’oburaingwa bwazo bwona, Leviathan ohuriire takakyemereire omu muringo gw’oburingaaniza ahabw’ekishushani kye. Ekinyonyi ekihuriire, omu bukuru bwakyo bwona n’omu makuru gaakyo, nikireebeka omu nyanja omu maizi agatarikushoboororwa kwonka; kandi aha maizi ekicweka kye kihango munonga tikirikureebwa, nk’emeeri erikutandikwa omu murongooti gw’orutaro; kandi kuruga omu kintu ekyo n’ekintu ekitarikubaasika ebiro byona omuntu orikufa kumuhanika omu mwanya omu mwanya, kugira ngu arinde ebizimba bye byona eby’amaani n’okugurukaguruka kwe. Kandi, tutarikugamba aha ntaaniso erikuteekateekwaho munonga ey’omuringo gw’okuteerana ahagati y’enyanja nkye erikuyonka n’enyanja ya Platonian Leviathan eijwire; kwonka, nangwa n’omu nshonga y’emwe ahari ezo nkoko ezito ezirikuyonka eziteirwe aha mutwe gw’emeeri, nikwo kiri obwo n’ekishushani kye eky’aheeru, ekirikushushana n’eky’enyanja, ekiine amaguru, ekirikuhindukahinduka, ngu okushoboorora kwe okuhikire sitaani we wenka kukaba kutarikubaasa kukwaata.

Kwonka nikibaasa kuteekateekwaho, ngu kuruga omu magufa g’enyanja eyaabaire erimu obusha, nihabaasa kutunga obuhabuzi obuhikire oburikukwata aha kishushani kyayo ekihikire. Ngaaha. Ahabw’okuba n’ekimwe aha bintu ebirikushemeza munonga ahari Leviathan ogu, ngu amagufa ge nigatuha ekiteekateeko kikye munonga aha nkora ye yoona. N’obu amagufa ga Jeremy Bentham, agarikuhanikwa ahabw’okutaho etabaaza omu ibiikiro ry’ebitabo ry’omwe aha bamwitsire, garikworeka gye ekiteekateeko ky’omugurusi orikukora eby’obuhwezi kandi oine ebishuba bigumire, aine emitwarize endiijo yoona eya Jeremy ey’omuntu wenka erikwebembera; kwonka tihariho kintu kyona eky’omuringo ogu ekyabaire nikibaasa kushoboororwa kuruga omu magufa g’entare yoona againe ebicweka by’omubiri. Nangwa, nk’oku Omuhiigi mukuru arikugira, amagufa g’enyanja yonka nigagira akakwate n’ekinyamaishwa ekiijwire kandi ekiine ebipande nk’oku ekinyonyi kiine akakwate n’ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa chrysalis ekirikukizingira omu muringo gw’okwehinguririza. Obutashushana obu nibureebeka omu mutwe omu muringo ogurikushemeza, nk’oku omu kicweka kimwe eky’ekitabo eki kirikwija kworekwa omu muringo gw’omutaano. Kandi nikyorekwa omu muringo gw’okutangaaza munonga omu kipapa ky’aha rubaju, amagufa gaakyo garikugarukamu haihi n’amagufa g’omukono gw’omuntu, okwihaho ekikumu ky’omukono kyonka. Ekipapaijo eki kiine enkumu ina ez’amagufa eza butoosha, orukumu orurikworeka, orwa rwagati, orurikumanyisa empeta, n’orukumu rukye. Kwonka ebi byona nibituura omu kijwaro kyabyo eky’omubiri obutahwaho, nk’oku enkumu z’omuntu ziri omu kijwaro ekikozirwe abantu. Eizooba rimwe Stubb orikushemeza akagira ati: “N’obu obumwe ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa whale kiraabe nikituheereza omu muringo gw’obutafayo, buzima tikirikubaasa kugambwa ngu naatukwata ataine mittens.”

Ahabw’enshonga ezi zoona, n’ahabw’ekyo, omu muringo gwona ogu orikubaasa kukireebamu, oine kwetenga kuhendera ngu Leviathan omukuru n’ekihangirwe ekyo kimwe omu nsi ekiine kuguma kitasiigirwe erangi kuhitsya aha muheru. N’amazima, ekishushani kimwe nikibaasa kuteera akamanyiso haihi munonga kukira ekindi, kwonka tihaine ekirikubaasa kukiteera n’obuhikire bwingi munonga. N’ahabw’ekyo tihariho muringo gw’ensi ogw’okumanya gye oku ekinyonyi eki kirikushusha buzima. Kandi omuringo gwonka ogu orikubaasa kutungamu nangwa n’ekiteekateeko ekirikugumisiriza aha nkora ye ey’amagara, n’okuza omu kuhiiga enyanja ahabwawe; kwonka waaba nookora ekyo, nooba ori omu kabi kakye k’okuguma naateerwa esigiri ebiro byona kandi akabingwa nawe. Nahabwekyo, nikireebeka ahariinye ngu kikaba nikirungi otaba ori omunyabwengye munonga omu kwenda kwawe kumanya ebirikukwata ahari Leviathan ogu.





EISHOMO 56. Eby’ebishushani by’enyanja ebitari birungi munonga, n’ebishushani by’amazima eby’ebishushani by’okuteera enyanja.

Aha bikwatiraine n’ebishushani by’ebinyonyi ebirikwetwa whales, ninshoberwa munonga aha kutaaha omu bitebyo ebyo ebirikukirayo obubi ebirikushangwa omu bitabo ebimwe, ebya ira n’ebya hati, namunonga omuri Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, n’ebindi Kwonka enshonga egyo ninhinguraho.

Nimmanya enteekateeka ina zonka ezihandiikirwe ez’enyanja mpango erikwetwa Sperm Whale; Aba Colnett, aba Huggins, aba Frederick Cuvier, na Beale. Omu shuura ehweire Colnett na Cuvier bakagambwaho. Ekya Huggins nikikira munonga ekyaabo; kwonka, ahabw’okugira ngu, eya Beale niyo erikukirayo oburungi. Ebishushani bya Beale byona eby’enyanja egi ni birungi, okwihaho ekishushani kya rwagati omu kishushani ky’enyanja ishatu omu mitwarize etari emwe n’emwe, ekirikuhendera eshuura ye eya kabiri. Ekishushani kye eky’omu maisho, amaato garikutaahirira Sperm Whales, n’obu kiraabe hatariho kubangaanisa kyabarwa kureetaho okubanganisa kw’abantu b’omu kicweka ky’abashaija abamwe ab’omu parlour, nikihikiirira munonga kandi nikishushana n’amagara omu nkora yaakyo yoona. Ebimwe aha bishushani bya Sperm Whale ebiri omu J. Ross Browne nibihikiirira gye omu nkora; kwonka zihandiikirwe omu muringo gw’obusaasi. Kwonka ogwo ti mushango gwe.

Aha kinyonyi kya Right Whale, ebishushani by’omutaano ebirungi munonga biri omu Scoresby; kwonka niziteebwa aha rulengo rukye munonga kubaasa kumanyisa ekiteekateeko eki orikwenda. Aine ekishushani kimwe kyonka eky’ebishushani by’okuhiiga enyanja, kandi eki n’ekizibu ky’obusaasi, ahabw’okuba n’ebishushani nk’ebyo byonka, byaba bikozirwe gye, orikubaasa kutunga ekintu kyona nk’ekiteekateeko ky’amazima eky’enyanja ehuriire nk’oku abahiigi be abahuriire barikugireeba.

Kwonka, twatwarwa nk’ebintu byona, n’obu biraabe biri birungi munonga, n’obu omu bintu ebimwe bitari bihikire, eby’okureeberaho by’enyanja n’eby’okuteera enyanja ebirikushangwa hoona, n’ebishushani bibiri bihango eby’Abafaransa, ebikozirwe gye, kandi ebirugire omu bishushani ebyakozirwe Garnery emwe. Okukuratana, nibijwekyera okutaahirira enyanja ya Sperm na Right Whale. Omu kishushani ky’okubanza ekinyonyi ky’ekitiinisa ekya Sperm Whale nikishushanisibwa omu bukuru bw’amaani, kiherize kwimuka ahansi y’obwato kuruga omu nkungu z’enyanja, kandi kiine ahaiguru omu mwanya aha mugongo gwe ekicweka ky’embaaho z’omuriro ekirikutiinisa. Omutwe gw’obwato tigurikuhendagura kimwe, kandi nigukururwa gurikugyeragyeranisibwa kwonka aha mugongo gw’ekinyonyi; kandi oyemereire omu rubaju orwo, ahabw’obwire obwo bumwe obutarikubarwa, nooreeba omuvugi w’enyanja, ekicweka kimwe kishwekirwe omucuucu gw’enyanja ogurikwotsya ogurikwotsya, kandi ari omu kikorwa ky’okuguruka, nk’orikuruga omu kiina. Ekikorwa ky’ekintu kyona n’eky’okutangaaza kirungi kandi eky’amazima. Ekicweka kya line-tub ekirimu busha nikizengyerera aha nyanja eyezire; emiti y’embaaho z’enkoni ezisheeshekire neeteera omunda; emitwe y’abavugi b’emotoka z’emigugu neejanjaara aha nyanja y’enyanja omu miringo etarikushushana ey’okutiina; obwo omu rugendo orurikwiragura orurimu omuyaga emeeri erikwemerera ahansi aha mwanya. Enshobi y’amaani neebaasa kushangwa omu bintu ebirikukwata aha mubiri gw’enyanja egi, kwonka reka ekyo kihingure; ahabw’okuba, ahabw’amagara gangye, nkaba ntarikubaasa kuteera ekishushani kirungi nk’ekyo.

Omu kishushani kya kabiri, obwato buri omu kikorwa ky’okuteera aha rubaju rw’enyanja mpango erikwiruka erikwetwa Right Whale, erikwehindura ekicweka kyayo ekirimu ebishaka ebirikwiragura omu nyanja nk’orukiri orurimu ebishaka kuruga omu nshozi za Patagonia. Enyonyi ze zigumire, ziijwire, kandi zirikwiragura nk’omucuucu; n’ahabw’ekyo kuruga omu mwika mwingi munonga omu nfuuzi, okateekateeka ngu haine kuba hariho ekiihuro ky’obumanzi ekirikuteeka omu mara makuru ahansi. Enkoko z’omu nyanja niziteera obunyaatsi obukye, eby’enyanja ebirikwetwa shell-fish, n’ebindi bihimba by’omu nyanja n’ebihimba ebirikwetwa maccaroni, ebi Right Whale obumwe erikutwara aha mugongo gwayo ogurimu oburwaire. Kandi obwire obwo bwona, enyamaishwa y’omukishaka ey’eminwa mihango neehunga omu maizi maingi, neesigaho tani z’ebihimba ebirikwera ebirimu akavuyo omu nshozi ze, kandi neereetera obwato obukye kutengeeta omu bicupuri nk’obwato obukwatsirwe haihi n’enziga z’enyanja z’omu nyanja. Kwonka shi ahabw’enki noogamba bingi? Abashaija boona nibatuura bazingirwe omu mirongooti y’enyanja. Boona nibazaarwa baine enkofiira omu bitsya; kwonka abantu ku barikukwatwa omu kuhinduka kw’ahonaaho, okw’ahonaaho kw’okufa, nibo barikumanya akabi k’amagara akatarikugamba, akatarikureebwa butunu, akarikugumaho. Kandi ku oraabe ori omunyabwengye, n’obu oraabe oshutami omu bwato bw’enyanja, omu mutima gwawe torikuhurira oine obwoba n’akakye, okukira oku orikushutama omu maisho g’omuriro gwawe ogw’omu mwabazyo oine ekikopo ky’okuteera, beitu kitari ky’okuteera, aha rubaju rwawe.
