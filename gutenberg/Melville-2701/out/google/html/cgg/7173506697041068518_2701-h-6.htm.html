Tindikwija kweshushaniriza nk’okushoboorora kw’ebika by’ebihangirwe ebitari bimwe na bimwe omu dakiika nkye, nari—omu mwanya ogu haakiri—okushoboorora kwingi kwona. Ekigyendererwa kyangye aha n’okukora purojekiti y’okutebeekanisa eby’obuhangwa bw’ebinyonyi (cetology). Ndi omuhi w’ebyombeko, ti mwombeki.

Kwonka n’omurimo gw’okuteekateeka munonga; tihariho omushoroozi w’amabaruha owa buriijo omu Ofiisi ya Posita orikwingana nayo. Okuhika ahansi omu nyanja kubakuratira; okugira emikono omu misingi etarikugambwa, embaju, n’ekibunu ky’ensi kyonka; eki n'ekintu ky'okutiina. Nyowe ndi ki ngu essay kukwaata enyindo y'entare egi! Okujumwa okurikutiinisa okuri omuri Yobu nikubaasa kuntinisa munonga. Mbwenu shi we (omunyabwengye) naija kukora endagaano naiwe? Reeba amatsiko ge n’aga busha! Kwonka nkayoga omu maramizo g’ebitabo kandi nkagyendera omu nyanja; Ntungire kukora n’enyanja eziine emikono egi erikureebeka; Ndi omu bwesigwa; kandi ndyagyezaho. Hariho ebimwe ebirikubanza kushoboorora.

Eky’okubanza: Embeera etarikuhamibwa, etarikwetegyerezibwa eya sayansi egi eya Cetology eri omu vestibule yonka erikuhamibwa amazima, ngu omu bicweka ebimwe n’obunaku obu nikiguma kiri enshonga erikuhakanisa yaaba ekinyonyi ekikuru (whale) kiri ekyenyanja. Omu kitabo kye ekya System of Nature, AD 1776, Linnæus akarangirira ati, “Nintaanisa enyanja n’eby’enyanja.” Kwonka ahabw’okumanya kwangye, nimmanya ngu kuhitsya omu mwaka gwa 1850, enyanja z’ebinyonyi ebirikwetwa sharks na shad, alewives na herring, ezirikuhakanisa ekiragiro kya Linnæus ekyagambirwe gye, zikaba zikishangwa nizigabanisa obugabe bw’enyanja niyo emwe na Leviathan.

Enshonga ezi Linnæus yaabaire naija kugira ngu zikaba nizibinga enyanja ezi omu maizi, naagira ati: “Ahabw’omutima gwazo ogurikwotsya ogw’amaisho abiri, amahaha gaabyo, ebishushu byabyo ebirikugyendera, amatu gaabyo agarimu ebiina, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem,” kandi aha muheru, “ex merito lege naturæ jure.” Ebi byona nkabiha banywani bangye Simeon Macey na Charley Coffin, ab’omuri Nantucket, bombi bakaba bari abanywani bangye omu rugendo rumwe, kandi bakakwatanisa omu kiteekateeko ngu enshonga ezaagambirweho zikaba zitarikumara zoona. Charley akagira ngu bakaba bari humbug.

Kimanyirwe ngu, n’okurekura okuhakana kwona, nintwara enshonga nungi eya ira ngu ekinyonyi ekikuru n’ekyenyanja, kandi nyeta Yona orikwera kunshagika. Ekintu eki eky’omutaano kyashoboororwa, enshonga erikukurataho eri, ni mu kitiinisa ki eky’omunda eki ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa whale kirikutaana n’eby’enyanja ebindi. Aharuguru, Linnæus akuhaire ebintu ebyo. Kwonka omu bugufu, ni ebi: amahaha n’eshagama erikwotsya; n’obu, eby’enyanja ebindi byona bitaine mahaha kandi biine eshagama erikwotsya.

Ekindi: nituza kushoboorora tuta ekinyonyi ekikuru, ahabw’ebintu byakyo eby’aheeru ebirikureebwa butunu, kugira ngu kibe nikireebeka gye kukihandiikaho akamanyiso k’obwire bwona oburikwija? N’ahabw’ekyo, omu bugufu, ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa whale n’ekyenyanja ekirikusheeshasheesha kandi ekiine omukira ogurikwemerera . Aho niho oine we. N’obu kiraabe kikyebeirwe, okushoboorora okwo nikuruga omu kuteekateeka okugaziibwe. Walrus neesheesha munonga nk’enyanja y’omu nyanja, kwonka walrus ti kyenyanja, ahabw’okuba neetuura omu maizi. Kwonka ekigambo ky’aha muheru eky’okushoboorora nikiguma nikihamibwa munonga, nk’oku kirikugambwaho n’eky’okubanza. Haihi buri omwe aine kuba yaareebire ngu eby’enyanja byona ebi abantu b’omu itaka barikumanya tibiine mukira ogurikwemerera, kureka ogurikwemerera, nari ogurikuza ahaiguru n’ahansi. Kwonka omu by’enyanja ebirikusheeshasheesha omukira, n’obu guraabe nigubaasa kuba guri omu muringo nigwo gumwe, niguguma nigutwara omwanya ogurikushushana.

Omu kushoboorora oku aharuguru okw’eki ekinyonyi ekikuru kiri, tindikwihaho na kakye omu buzaare bw’ab’eishe-emwe aba leviathanic ekihangirwe kyona eky’omu nyanja eki okuhitsya hati kimanyirwe n’ekinyonyi ekikuru ahabw’abantu ba Nantucket abaine okumanya okurungi; kandi, aha rubaju orundi, kugikwatanisa n’ekyenyanja kyona ekyabaire nikitwarwa nk’eky’aheeru y’eihanga okuhitsya hati omu bushoboorozi.* N’ahabw’ekyo, eby’enyanja byona ebikye, ebirikusheeshasheesha, n’ebiine emikira erikwemerera biine kuteebwa omu ntebeekanisa egi ey’ahansi eya Cetology. Hati, mbwenu, haija okubaganisamu okuhango kw’omugyenyi weena ow’enyanja.

*Ninkimanya ngu kuhisya omubwire obu, ebyenyanja ebiine omuringo gwa Lamatins na Dugongs (Pig-fish na Sow-fish eby’omu bibokisi bya Nantucket) nibitwarwa abanyabwengye baingi omu by’obuhangwa omu nshozi z’enyanja. Kwonka nk’oku eby’enyanja ebi eby’empunu birikukora oruyombo, ebirikugaya, ebingi birikwehereera omu kanwa k’emigyera, kandi birikurya obunyaatsi oburimu amaizi, kandi na munonga nk’oku bitarikusheeshasheesha, ninyehakana ebihandiiko byabyo nk’enyanja; kandi babahaire passport zaabo z’okuruga omu Bukama bwa Cetology.

EKITABO I. ( Folio ), EISHOMO I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Ekinyonyi eki, omu Bajungu ba ira ekirikumanyirwa omu muringo ogutarikwetegyerezibwa gye nk’ekinyonyi kya Trumpa, n’ekinyonyi kya Physeter, n’ekinyonyi ky’omutwe Anvil, nikyo Cachalot eya hati ey’Abafaransa, kandi Pottsfich ey’Abagirimaani eya Macroce Longphalus, n’eya Pottsfich ey’Abagirimaani eya Macroce. Hatariho kubangaanisa, niwe muntu orikukirayo obuhango omu nsi yoona; ekirikukirayo kutiinisa omu nshozi zoona ezi orikubaasa kutunga; ekirikukirayo obukuru omu nshonga; kandi eky’aha muheru, n’eky’omuhendo munonga omu by’obushuubuzi; ahabw’okuba niwe kihangirwe kyonka eki ekintu ekyo eky’omuhendo, spermaceti, kirikurugamu. Eby’omutaano bye byona, omu myanya endiijo mingi, nibiija kwongyerwamu amaani. Okukira munonga n’eiziina rye hati nyine kukora. Ku turikuteekateeka aha by’orurimi, n’ekintu ekitaine makuru. Ebyasha bimwe enyima, obu ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Sperm whale kyabaire kiri haihi kutamanyirwa kimwe omu buntu bwakyo obuhikire, kandi obu amajuta gaakyo gaabaire nigatunga omu butandu kuruga omu kyenyanja ekyabaire kikwatsirwe; omu biro ebyo spermaceti, nikireebeka ngu abantu bakaba nibateekateeka ngu neeruga omu kihangirwe ekirikushushana n’ekyo ekyabaire nikimanywa omuri Bungyereza obwire obwo nka Greenland nari Right Whale. Eki kikaba kiri ekiteekateeko kandi, ngu spermaceti niyo emwe ekaba eri egyo nshekyerero y’okurahutsya ey’enyanja ya Greenland Whale ei enyuguta y’okubanza ey’ekigambo eki erikworeka buzima. Omu bwire obwo, nabwo, spermaceti ekaba eri nkye munonga, etarikukoresibwa nk’omushana, kureka nk’amajuta n’omubazi gwonka. Kikaba kiri eky’okutunga abashuubuzi b’emibazi kwonka nk’oku ebiro ebi orikugura ounce y’omuceeri. Nk’oku ndikuteekateeka, omu bwire, obuhangwa bwenyini bwa spermaceti ku bwamanyirwe, eiziina ryayo ery’okubanza rikaba rikyagumaho abashuubuzi; tihariho kubangaanisa kwongyera aha muheendo gwayo ahabw’entekateeka erikutangaaza munonga ey’okubura kwayo. Kandi n’ahabw’ekyo eiziina eri riine kuba ryahererukire kuheebwa enyanja y’enyanja ei spermaceti egi yaarugiremu buzima.

EKITABO I. ( Folio ), EISHOMO II. ( Right Whale ).—Omu muringo gumwe egi niyo erikukirayo kuheebwa ekitiinisa omu nyamaishwa ezirikwetwa leviathans, ahabw’okuba niyo erikubanza kuhiigwa abantu butoosha. Nikirugamu ekihandiiko ekirikumanyirwa munonga nka whalebone nari baleen; n’amajuta agamanyirwe munonga nka “amajuta g’enyanja,” ekintu ky’ahansi omu by’obushuubuzi. Omu bajubi, naaheebwa ebitinisa ebi byona ataine kushoroora: The Whale; ekinyonyi ekikuru ekya Greenland; ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Whale; ekinyonyi Ekikuru; ekinyonyi ky’Amazima; ekinyonyi ky’Oburyo. Hariho obutamanya bwingi aha bikwatiraine n’okumanya ebika by’ebinyonyi ebibatiziibwe omu bwingi. Mbwenu shi ekinyonyi eki, eki ndikuta omu kika kya kabiri ekya Folios zangye, n’enki? Ni Mysticetus Mukuru ow’abanyabwengye b’obuhangwa b’Orungyereza; ekinyonyi kya Greenland eky’abanyabwengye b’Orungyereza; Baleine Ordinaire ey’abavugi b’enyanja aba Bufaransa; ekyenyanja kya Growlands eky’omu Sweden. Nikyo kinyonyi ekikuru eki kumara eby’obusingye ebirikurenga bibiri ehingwire kihiigwa Abadaaki n’Abangyereza omu nyanja za Arctic; niyo nshozi ei abajubi ba America bamazire obwire buraingwa barikukuratira omu nyanja ya India, aha nkungu za Brazil, aha nyanja ya Nor’ West Coast, n’ebindi bicweka by’ensi ebitari bimwe na bimwe, ebi baateireho Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Abamwe nibeeshushaniriza nk’abarikureeba entaaniso eri ahagati y’enyanja ya Greenland whale ey’Abangyereza n’enyanja ya right whale ey’Abamerika. Kwonka niziikirizana buzima omu mitwarize yaazo yoona ey’amaani; kandi tihaine nshonga emwe eyaabaire erikworekwa erikubaasa kugyenderaho entaaniso mpango. Ni ahabw’okubaganisamu okutaine bugarukiro okurugiirira aha ntaaniso ezirikukirayo obutahikiirira, ngu ebitongore ebimwe eby’ebyafaayo by’obuhangwa nibihinduka ebigumire munonga. Ekinyonyi ekihikire nikiija kujanjabirwa ahandi omu bwire buraingwa, kirikujuriza okushoboorora ekinyonyi ky’omukazi ekirikureeta amaizi g’ekihama.

EKITABO I. ( Folio ), EISHOMO RYA III. ( Fin-Back ).—Ahansi y’omutwe ogu ninteekateeka aha kicuncu eki, omu maziina gatari gamwe na gamwe nka Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, na Long-John, kireebirwe haihi omu nyanja yoona kandi ekirikukira kuba kiri ekinyonyi ekikuru eki enyonyi yaakyo eri hare erikukira kushoboororwa abavugi abarikuraba omu nyanja ya Atlantic, omu nshaho z’ebipapura z’omu nyanja ya New York. Omu bureingwa obu arikutunga, n’omu baleen ye, Fin-back neeshushana n’enyanja ya right whale, kwonka eine omubiri mukye, kandi eine erangi erikwera, eri haihi n’ey’omuzeituuni. Eminywa ye empango neeyoreka ekishushani ekirikushushana n’eky’ekikwato, ekirikukorwaho ebicweka by’ebihimba bihango ebirikuteerana, ebirikushendashenda. Ekintu kye ekikuru ekirikutaanisaho abantu, ekibunu, eki yaarugiremu eiziina rye, nikikira kuba kiri ekintu ekirikureebwa gye. Ekibunu eki nikiba kiine oburaingwa bwa fuuti nka ishatu nari ina, nikikura kiine oburaingwa kuruga aha rubaju rw’enyima rw’omugongo, kiine omubiri gw’enkokora, kandi kiine omuheru ogurikwotsya munonga. N’obu ekicweka ekindi kikye eky’ekihangirwe kiraabe kitarikureebeka, ekibunu eki ekirikwetaanisaho, obumwe, nikiija kureebwa gye nikiruga ahaiguru. Enyanja ku erikuba egumire omu muringo gw’oburingaaniza, kandi erimu akamanyiso kakye k’ebicu ebirikwetoorora, kandi ekibunu eki ekirikushushana n’ekya gnomon kyayemerera kandi kiteera ebicuucu aha itaka eriine ebicupuri, nikibaasa kuteekateekwaho gye ngu enzindo y’amaizi erikugyehinguririza neeshushana n’ekibumbe ky’okuteeraho, n’omuringo gwayo n’enyuguta z’eshaaha ezirimu ebicupuri. Aha rubaaho orwo orwa Ahazi ekicuucu nikikira kugaruka enyima. Fin-Back teri omu kigombe. Nareebeka nk’orikwanga enyanja, nk’oku abashaija abamwe barikwanga abantu. Enshoni nyingi; butoosha kuza wenka; okutunguuka omu maizi agari hare munonga kandi agarimu obuzibu obutarikuteekateekwaho; enyonyi ye egoroire kandi emwe ey’ahaiguru erikwimuka nk’eicumu riraingwa eritarikukunda abantu aha ruhanga orutarimu itaka; aine ekiconco ky’amaani g’okutangaaza n’obwangu omu kwoga, nk’oku arikubaasa kuhakanisa okukuratirwa kwona okuriho hati kuruga aha muntu; eki leviathan nikireebeka nka Kaini owaabingirwe kandi otarikubaasa kusingurwa ow’oruganda rwe, aine akamanyiso ke omuringo ogwo aha mugongo gwe. Kuruga omu kugira baleen omu kanwa ke, Fin-Back obumwe neetwarwa hamwe n’enyanja ya right whale, omu kika ky’ebiteekateeko ekirikwetwa Whalebone whales , nikwo kugira ngu, enyanja eziine baleen. Omuri ebinyonyi ebi ebirikwetwa Whalebone whales, nikibaasa kureebeka nk’ekirimu ebika bitari bimwe na bimwe, kwonka ebingi aharibyo tibirikumanywa munonga. Enyanja eziine enyindo mpango n’eziine emimiro; enyanja eziine omutwe gwa pike; enyanja ezirimu ebibunu; ebinyonyi ebiine amapapa g’ahansi n’ebinyonyi ebiine amaguru g’ahansi, nigo maziina g’abajubi g’ebika bikye.

Aha bikwatiraine n’eiziina eri erya “Whalebone whales,” n’eky’omugasho munonga kugamba, ngu n’obu eiziina nk’eryo riraabe niribaasa kuba rirungi omu kwongyera aha kushoboorora aha muringo gumwe gw’enyanja, kwonka n’eky’obusha kugyezaho kushoroora gye Leviathan, erikwegamira aha baleen ye nari fin, nari hump,; n’obu ebicweka ebyo ebiteirweho akamanyiso nari ebishushani biraabe nibireebeka gye munonga nk’ebitebeekanisiibwe gye kubaasa kuha omusingi gw’enkora ya Cetology eya butoosha kukira ebindi bishushani by’omubiri ebitaine kakwate, ebi ekinyonyi ekikuru, omu miringo ye, kirikworeka. Mbwenu shi? Enyamaishwa erikwetwa baleen, ekibunu, ekibunu ky’omugongo, n’ameino; ebi n’ebintu ebiine obumanyiso bwabyo obutaine kushoroora omu miringo yoona ey’enyanja, hatariho kuteekateeka aha kirikubaasa kuba kiri obuhangwa bw’omutindo gwabyo omu bintu ebindi kandi ebikuru munonga. N’ahabw’ekyo, ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa sperm whale n’ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa humpbacked whale, buri kimwe kiine ekibunu; kwonka aho okushushana nikuhwaho. Reero, ekinyonyi eki kimwe eky’omugongo n’ekinyonyi kya Greenland, buri kimwe ahari ebi kiine baleen; kwonka obwo n’okushushana nikuhwaho. Kandi nikwo kiri n’ebicweka ebindi ebi twagambaho ​​aharuguru. Omu miringo etari emwe n’emwe ey’enyanja, nizikora ebicweka nk’ebyo ebitarikushushana; nari, omu mbeera y’omwe aharibo orikwetaanisa, okwetaanisa nk’okwo okutarimu buteeka; nk’okuhakanisa kimwe enkora zoona ez’omutaano ezitebeekanisiibwe aha musingi nk’ogwo. Aha rukiri oru buri omwe omu bacondoozi b’eby’obuhangwa bw’enyanja (whale) abaabaganisiibwemu.

Kwonka nikibaasa kuteekateekwaho ngu, omu bicweka by’omunda eby’enyanja, omu nkora y’omubiri gwe—aho, haakiri, nitwija kubaasa kuteera okushoroora okuhikire. Ngaaha; nk’eky’okureeberaho, n’enki ekirimu omu mubiri gw’enyanja ya Greenland ekirikushemeza munonga kukira baleen ye? Kwonka tukareeba ngu ahabw’enyanja ye erikwetwa baleen tikirikubaasika kushoroora gye enyanja ya Greenland whale. Kandi ku oraabe nooshuuma omu mara g’ebinyonyi ebitari bimwe na bimwe, ahabw’enki okwo otarikushangayo entaaniso ekicweka kya makumi ataano nk’oku kirikubaasika aha mutebeekanisa nk’ebyo eby’aheeru ebibaire byaheza kubarwa. Mbwenu shi niki ekisigaireho? tihariho ekindi kureka okukwata enyanja z’ebinyonyi omu mubiri, omu bwingi bwazo bwona obw’obugabe, n’okuzishoroora omu muringo ogwo n’obumanzi. Kandi egi niyo nkora ya Bibliographical erikutwarwa aha; kandi niyo yonka erikubaasa kusingura, ahabw’okuba niyo yonka erikubaasa kukorwa. Okugumizamu.

( Grampus ).—N’obu ekyenyanja eki, ekiine okuhuuta kw’amaani, nari okukiraho okuhuuta, kihaire abantu b’omu itaka enfumu, nikimanywa munonga nk’ekirikutuura omu nyanja y’omunda munonga, kwonka tikirikuteebwa omu kicweka ky’enyanja ezirikwetwa whales. Kwonka ahabw’okugira ngu aine emitwarize yoona mikuru erikutaanisaho leviathan, abanyabwengye b’eby’obuhangwa abaingi bamumanyire ahabw’okugira ngu aine kimwe. Niwe aine obuhango bwa octavo obw’omutindo, arikutaana kuruga ahari fuuti ikumi n’etaano kuhika ahari makumi abiri n’etaano omu buraingwa, kandi aine obuhango oburikuhikaana okwehinguririza ekibunu. Naayoga omu masyo; tarikuhiigwa butoosha, n’obu amajuta ge garaabe gari maingi munonga, kandi gari marungi munonga ahabw’omushana. Abajubi abamwe enkora ye neetwarwa nk’erikumanyisa okutunguuka kw’enyanja mpango erikwetwa sperm whale.

EKITABO II. ( Okatavo ), EISHOMO RYA II. ( Black Fish ).—Ninha amaziina g’abajubi abarikumanywa munonga ahabw’eby’enyanja ebi byona, ahabw’okuba okutwarira hamwe nibyo birikukirayo oburungi. Eiziina ryona ahu riraabe ritarikwetegyerezibwa gye nari ritarikushoboororwa gye, ninyija kukigamba, kandi nteereho erindi. Ninkora ekyo hati, ninkwata aha Kyanyanja Ekirikwiragura, eki barikweta, ahabw’okuba oburikwiragura n’ekiragiro haihi omu nshozi zoona. Mbwenu, mwete Hyena Whale, waaba nooyenda. Obushaarizi bwe nibumanyirwa gye, kandi kuruga omu mbeera ei enkokora z’omunda z’eminwa ye zirikugoma ziza ahaiguru, aine okumwenya okutahwaho okwa Mephistophelean aha maisho ge. Ekinyonyi eki nikiba kiine oburaingwa bwa fuuti nka ikumi na mukaaga nari ikumi na munaana. Nashangwa haihi omu latitude zoona. Aine omuringo gw’omutaano gw’okworeka ekibunu kye eky’omugongo ekiine akakwato omu kwoga, ekirikushushana n’enyindo y’Abarooma. Abahiigi b’enyanja ku barikuba batarikukoresa sente nyingi, obumwe nibakwata enyanja ya Hyena whale, kubaasa kuguma baine amajuta g’obuguzi bukye g’okukoresa omu maka—nk’oku abakozi b’omu maka abamwe abarikukoresa esente nyingi, bataine kampuni, kandi bari bonka, barikwotsya amajuta agatarikushemeza omu mwanya gw’okukoresa ebishaju ebirikunuuka. N’obu blubber yaabyo eraabe eri nkye munonga, emwe aha nshozi ezi niziija kukuha amajuta garikurenga galoni makumi ashatu.

EKITABO II. ( Okatavo ), EISHOMO RYA KASHATU. ( Narwhale ) . Ekihangirwe eki kiine oburaingwa bwa fuuti nka ikumi na mukaaga, kandi eihembe ryakyo riri ahagati ya fuuti itaano, n’obu emwe eraabe neerenga ikumi, kandi nangwa neehika fuuti ikumi n’etaano. Okushoboorora gye, eihembe eri n’eihembe eririkurenga, eririkukura kuruga omu rubaju omu murongooti ogurikuteganisibwa kakye kuruga aha rubaju orurikuraba. Kwonka nikishangwa aha rubaju orubi rwonka, oruine akakwate karungi, rurikuha mukama waakyo ekintu ekirikushushana n’orubaju rw’omushaija w’omukono gwa bumosho otaine nshonga. Ni kigyendererwa ki ekihikire eki eihembe eri ery’amahembe g’entare nari eicumu eririkugarukamu, kikaba nikiija kuguma kukigamba. Tikirikureebeka ngu nikikoresibwa nk’ekyoma ky’ekyenyanja ky’ekisyo n’ekyenyanja ky’ebicupuri; n’obu abavugi b’enyanja abamwe baraabe nibangira ngu Narwhale neegikoresa nk’ekikwato ky’okuteera ahansi y’enyanja kutunga eby’okurya. Charley Coffin akagira ngu kikaba kikoresibwa omu kuteera orubaare; ahakuba Narwhale, erikwimuka ekahika aha mutwe gw’enyanja ya Polar, ekashanga erimu orubaare, neeteera eihembe rye ahaiguru, n’ahabw’ekyo neehenda. Kwonka torikubaasa kuhamya ngu kimwe aha biteekateeko ebi kihikire. Ekiteekateeko kyangye nyenka kiri ngu, n’obu eihembe eri ery’orubaju rumwe buzima riraabe niribaasa kukoresibwa Narwhale—n’obu ekyo kiraabe nikibaasa kuba kityo—buzima kikaba nikiija kumushemeza munonga ahabw’okutunga folda omu kushoma obupapura. Enyanja ya Narwhale ei mpurire neeyetwa Tusked whale, Horned whale, n’enyanja ya Unicorn. Buzima n’eky’okureeberaho ky’okwenda kumanya eky’obuhangwa bwa Unicornism oburikushangwa haihi omu bukama bwona obw’ebihangirwe ebiine amagara. Kuruga omu bahandiiki bamwe aba ira aba cloistered nshangire ngu eihembe eri ryonka erya sea-unicorn omu biro bya ira rikaba niritwarwa nk’omubazi muhango gw’okurwanisa obutwa, kandi nk’oku kyabaire kiri, okuritebeekanisa kukaba kureeta emihendo y’amaani. Kandi kikaba kihindurwa omunyugunyu ogurikuhindukahinduka ogw’abakazi abarikuzirika, omu muringo nigwo gumwe nk’oku amahembe g’entare y’ekishaija garikukorwamu amahembe g’entare. Omu kubanza kikaba kyonka nikibarwa nk’ekintu ky’okwenda kumanya munonga. Black Letter neingambira ngu Sir Martin Frobisher obu yaabaire naagaruka kuruga omu rugyendo orwo, obu Omugabekazi Bess yaakora omu muringo gw’obumanzi kumuhanika omukono gwe ogwabaire gurimu amabaare g’omuhendo kuruga omu dirisa y’ekikaari kya Greenwich, obwo emeeri ye ey’obumanzi yaabaire neegyendera omu mugyera Thames; Black Letter naagira ati: “Sir Martin ku yaagarukire kuruga omu rugyendo orwo, afukamiire akaha omukuru we eihembe riraingwa ry’omutaano erya Narwhale, eryamara obwire buraingwa bwanyima y’okuhanikwa omu nju y’obugabe omuri Windsor.” Omuhandiiki omwe kuruga Ireland naagira ngu Earl wa Leicester, afukamire, nawe akaha omukuru we eihembe erindi, eririkukwata aha kinyamaishwa ky’omu itaka eky’obuhangwa bw’enkongoro.

Narwhale eine ekishushani kirungi munonga, erikushusha nk’entare, eri ey’erangi y’ahansi erikwera nk’amate, erimu obutonyezi oburikwetoorora kandi oburaingwa oburikwiragura. Amajuta ge nigakira munonga, nigashemeza kandi nigashemeza; kwonka hariho bikye, kandi tarikukira kuhiigwa. Nakira kushangwa omu nyanja ezirikwetwa circumpolar.

( Killer ).—Aha nshonga y’enyanja egi, tihariho kihango ekimanyirwe gye omu Nantucketer, kandi tihaine na kakye aha muntu orikwegambaho ​​nk’omuhangu omu by’obuhangwa. Kurugirira aha bi namureebire ndi hare, nshemerire kugira ngu akaba ari nk’obuhango bwa grampus. Ni omunyamaishwa munonga—omuringo gw’ekyenyanja kya Feegee. Obumwe akwata enyanja mpango eza Folio aha munwa, azihanika aho nk’enzigye, okuhitsya obu ekinyamaishwa ky’amaani kirikwerarikirira kuhika aha kufa. Omwitsi tarikuhiigwa na kakye. Tikahuriraga ngu aine amajuta ki. Okwihaho nikubaasa kutwarwa eiziina eriheirwe ekinyonyi eki, ahabw’okutashoborokyerwa gye. Ahabw'okuba twena turi abaitsi, aha itaka n'omu nyanja; Bonapartes na Sharks zikaba zirimu.

EKITABO II. ( Octavo ), EISHOMO RYA V. ( Thrasher ).—Omushaija ogu omurungi naamanywa ahabw’omukira gwe, ogu arikukoresa nk’ekikwato ky’okuteera abazigu be. Naatemba omugongo gw’enyanja ya Folio whale, kandi obwo arikwoga, naakora omuhanda gwe arikugiteera enkoni; nk’oku abashomesa abamwe barikukwatanisa omu nsi kurabira omu nkora niyo emwe. Na hati hariho bikye ebirikumanywa ahari Thrasher kukira ahari Killer. Bombi n’abarikuhenda ebiragiro, nangwa n’omu nyanja ezitarimu biragiro.

DUODECIMOS.—Ebi nibitwariramu n’enyanja ezikye. I. Enkongoro ya Huzza. II. Enyamaishwa y’omuri Algeria. III. Enyamaishwa ey’omunwa gwa Mealy.

Aha bantu abatakatungire mugisha gw’omutaano gw’okwega enshonga egi, nikibaasa kureebeka nk’ekitari ky’obutoosha, ngu eby’enyanja ebitarikukira kurenga fuuti ina nari itaano biine kuteebwa omu nteerane ahagati y’EBYANYANJI—ekigambo, eki, omu nshoboorora y’abantu baingi, butoosha kirikumanyisa ekiteekateeko ky’obuhango. Kwonka ebihangirwe ebiteirweho ahaiguru nka Duodecimoes n’enyanja ezitaine nshobi, omu bigambo by’okushoboorora kwangye aha ki enyanja eri— nikwo kugira ngu ekyenyanja ekirikusheeshasheesha, ekiine omukira ogurikwemerera.

EKITABO III. ( Duodecimo ), EISHOMO 1. ( Enkongoro ya Huzza ).—Egi n’enkongoro erikushangwa haihi omu nsi yoona. Eiziina eryo n'ery'okuhayo kwangye; ahakuba hariho emiringo y’ebinyonyi ebirikukira ahari gumwe, kandi hariho ekiine kukorwa kubaasa kubitanisa. Ninmweta atyo, ahabw’okuba butoosha naayoga omu bifunjo by’okushemeza, ebiri aha nyanja mpango biguma nibyeteera omu iguru nk’enkofiira omu mutwe gw’abantu b’ebiro bina by’okwezi kwa mushanju. Okureebeka kwazo okutwarira hamwe nikusiimwa n’okushemererwa abavugi b’enyanja. Ziijwire emyoyo mirungi, zitarikuhinduka niziruga omu nkungu z’omuyaga ziza omu muyaga. Nibo bashaija abarikuguma nibatuura omu maisho g’omuyaga. Nibatwarwa nk’akamanyiso k’omugisha. Iwe wenka ku oraabe noobaasa kugumisiriza okushemererwa kashatu orikureeba eby’enyanja ebi ebiine amagara, obwo eiguru rikuhwere; omwoyo gw'okutiina Ruhanga tiguri omuriimwe. Huzza Porpoise eriisiibwe gye kandi erimu amaizi maingi neija kukuha galoni emwe nungi y’amajuta marungi. Kwonka amaizi marungi kandi agatarikwetegyerezibwa gye agarikuruga omu nshaya ze n’ag’omuhendo munonga. Nikishabwa omu bakozi b’amabaare g’omuhendo n’abakozi b’eshaaha. Abavugi b’enyanja bakagita aha hones zaabo. Enyama ya Porpoise ni nungi kurya, noomanya. Nikibaasa kuba kitakakuhikireho ngu enkongoro neesheeshasheesha. Buzima, omucuucu gwe ni mukye munonga n’ahabw’ekyo tigurikubaasa kureebwa juba. Kwonka omurundi ogundi ku oraatungye omugisha, mureebe; kandi obwo noija kureeba ekinyonyi kihango ekya Sperm whale kyonka omu kashushani kakye.

EKITABO III. ( Ekicweka kya kabiri ), EISHOMO RYA II. ( Enyamaishwa y’omu Algeria ).—Omunyabuzaare. Ekinyamaishwa munonga. Nashangwa, ninteekateeka, omu nyanja ya Pacific yonka. Naaba aine obuhango bukye kukira Huzza Porpoise, kwonka bingi aha bishushani by’omutaano. Mumunyigire, naija kukwatira aha nshozi. Nkamugarura ahansi emirundi mingi, kwonka tinkareebaga akwatsirwe.

EKITABO III. ( Ekicweka kya kabiri ), EISHOMO RYA III. ( Porpoise ey’omunwa gw’oburo ).—Omuringo gw’entare ogurikukirayo obuhango; kandi neeshangwa omu nyanja ya Pacific yonka, nk’oku kirikumanywa. Eiziina ry’Orungyereza ryonka, eri atwire naayetwa okuhitsya hati, n’ery’abajubi—Right-Whale Porpoise, kuruga omu mbeera ei arikushangwamu munonga omu bicweka bya Folio egyo. Omu bubonero, naataana omu muringo gumwe na Huzza Porpoise, ahabw’okuba aine omubiri ogutarikwetoorora kandi ogurimu okushemererwa; buzima, aine omubiri murungi kandi oguri nk’omushaija w’ekitiinisa. Tihariho bipapaijo aha mugongo (ebindi bikooko ebingi ebirikwetwa porpoises biine), aine omukira ogurikushemeza, n’amaisho g’Abahinda agarimu enyehurira z’erangi ya hazel. Kwonka akanwa ke akarimu oburo nikashiisha byona. N’obu omugongo gwe gwona kuhika aha bipapaijo bye eby’aha rubaju guraabe guri ogw’omuti gw’omusheenyi, kwonka omurongooti gw’ensharo, ogurikutaana nk’akamanyiso akari omu rubaju rw’emeeri, ogurikwetwa “ekibunu ekirikwaka gye,” omurongooti ogwo nigumuteera kuruga aha mutwe kuhika aha rubaju rw’emeeri, n’erangi ibiri zitarikushushana, ezirikwiragura ahaiguru n’ezirikwera ahansi. Ekyeru kirimu ekicweka ky’omutwe gwe, n’akanwa ke koona, ekirikumureetera kureebeka nk’owaaruga omu kutaayaayira enshaho y’eby’okurya omu muringo gw’obushaarizi. Ekicweka ekirikukirayo obubi kandi eky’oburofa! Amajuta ge nigashushana n’ag’enyanja eya buriijo.

Oyihireho DUODECIMO, enkora egi terikugumizamu, ahabw’okuba Porpoise niyo nkye munonga omu nshozi z’enyanja. Aharuguru, oine Leviyatani zoona ez’omutaano. Kwonka hariho ekibunu ky’enyanja ezitarikuhamibwa, ezirikuhunga, eziri nk’omuvugi w’enyanja omuri America, ndikumanya ahabw’eiziina, kwonka ti ahabwangye. Ndyazibara nk'oku zirikwetwa fore-castle; ahakuba nikibaasika orunyiriri nk’orwo nirubaasa kuba orw’omugasho aha bacondoozi ab’omumaisho, abarikubaasa kuheza ebi nyine aha kwonka nkatandika. Ku haraabe hariho emwe aha nshozi ezi ahaifo, bwanyima y’okukwatwa kandi ekateebwaho akamanyiso, obwo naabaasa kutaaha omu Nkora egi ahonaaho, kurugiirira aha buhango bwe bwa Folio, Octavo, nari Duodecimo:—Enshozi y’Enyindo y’Ecupa; ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Junk Whale; ekinyonyi ekiine omutwe gwa Pudding; ekinyonyi kya Cape; ekinyonyi Ekirikwebembera; ekinyonyi ky’enyanja ekya Cannon; ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Scragg; ekinyonyi ky’omuringa; ekinyonyi ky’Enjojo; ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Iceberg; ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Quog; ekinyonyi kya Bururu; etc. Kuruga omu bakuru ba Iceland, Dutch, n’Orungyereza rwa ira, nihabaasa kujurizibwa enkalala ezindi ez’enyanja ezitarikuhamibwa, eziheirwe omugisha gw’amaziina g’emiringo yoona agatari g’ekitiinisa. Kwonka ninzireka nk’ezihingwireho kimwe; kandi tikirikubaasa kuhwera kubateekateekaho ahabw’amakuru gonka, gaijwire obuleeviyaani, kwonka gataine eki garikumanyisa.

Eky’aha muheru: Kikagambwa aha kutandika, ngu enkora egi terikwija kuba eri aha, kandi omurundi gumwe, ehikire. Torikubaasa kubura kureeba gye ngu ekigambo kyangye kihikiriize. Kwonka hati ninsigaho Enkora yangye y’eby’obuhangwa (cetological System) eyemereire atyo etahweire, nk’oku Katedrali mpango eya Cologne yaabaire esigaireho, n’ekimotoka ky’okutwara ebintu (crane) kikyemereire aha mutwe gw’omunaara ogutakahweire. Ahabw’okuba ebyombeko bikye nibibaasa kuhwaho abakugu baabyo ab’okubanza; abakuru, ab’amazima, obutoosha nibasigaho eibaare ry’okukinga aha baijukuru. Ruhanga anrinde okuheza ekintu kyona.
