EISHOMO 10. Munywani w’omu kifuba.

Ku naagarukire omu Spouter-Inn kuruga omu Chapel, nkashanga Queequeg ariyo wenka munonga; we ahabw’okuruga omu Kanisa atakaheebwa omugisha obwire bukye. Akaba ashutami aha ntebe omu maisho g’omuriro, ebigyere bye biri aha ntambi y’ekitanda, kandi omu mukono gumwe akaba akwaitse haihi n’amaisho ge ekishushani kye ekyo kikye eky’omujungu; arikugireeba munonga omu maisho, kandi aine omusyo gwa jack-knife arikugiteera mpora mpora aha nyindo, obwo arikwehurira omu muringo gwe gw’obupagani.

Kwonka hati ahabw’okuteganisibwa, akataho ekishushani; kandi omu bwire bukye, akaza aha meeza, yaakwata ekitabo kihango aho, kandi akakita aha bibero bye yaatandika kubara empapura n’obutoosha obugyendereire; ahari buri rupapura rwa makumi ataano—nk’oku naabaire ninteekateeka—okwemerera akaanya kakye, kureeba busha okwehinguririize, n’okuha ebigambo aha ntambi y’okutangaara erikugurukaguruka kumara obwire buraingwa. Bwanyima akaba agaruka atandikye aha makumi ataano agarikurataho; okureebeka nk’orikutandika aha namba emwe buri murundi, nk’oku atarikubaasa kubara ebirikukira ahari makumi ataano, kandi n’omubaro muhango nk’ogwo ogw’amakumi ataano kushangwa hamwe, ngu okutangaara kwe ahabw’obungi bw’empapura kukamushemeza.

N’ekihika kingi nkashutama naamureeba. Savage n’obu araabe yaabaire ari, kandi aine obuzibu bw’okushekyerera aha maisho—haakiri nk’oku naabaire ninkunda—amaisho ge gakaba garimu ekintu ekitari ky’okuhakanisa na kakye. Torikubaasa kushereka omutima. Kurabira omu bishushani bye byona ebitari by’ensi, nkateekateeka ngu nkareeba obubonero bw’omutima gworobi ogw’amazima; kandi omu maisho ge mahango, agarimu obumanzi, garikwiragura nk’omuriro kandi garimu obumanzi, hakaba nihareebeka nk’obumanyiso bw’omwoyo ogurikubaasa kuhakanisa baadaimoni rukumi. Kandi okwihaho ebi byona, hakaba hariho emitwarize y’ahaiguru aha Mupagani, n’obutagira kitiinisa bwe ekaba etarikubaasa kuremesa kimwe. Akaba naashusha nk’omushaija otakakwatwahoga kandi otakatungireho orikumubanja. Okuba ngu n’omutwe gwe gukashara, obuso bwe bukaba bushohoziibwe omu muringo gw’obugabe kandi ogurikwaka gye, kandi bukaba nibureebeka nk’obuhango okukira oku bwabaire buri, eki tindikwija kugyezaho kucwamu; kwonka ekihamire ngu omutwe gwe gukaba guri murungi munonga omu by’obuhangwa. Nikibaasa kureebeka nk’ekishema, kwonka kikanyijutsya omutwe gwa General Washington, nk’oku kirikureebeka omu bishushani bye ebi abantu barikukunda munonga. Kikaba kiine omugyera nigwo gumwe oguraingwa ogurikugaruka enyima kuruga ahaiguru y’ebishuba, ebyabaire nibirugayo munonga, nk’ebibungo bibiri ebiraingwa ebiine emiti mingi ahaiguru. Queequeg akaba ari George Washington owaabaire naatunguuka omu by’okurya abantu.

N’obu ndaabe naabaire ninmureeba gye nk’ogwo, ndikweshushaniriza nk’orikureeba aheeru omuyaga kuruga omu kishengye, tarahurikiize okubaho kwangye, tarateireho n’okureeba n’okureebaho kumwe; kwonka akaba nareebeka nk’omwejumbire omu kubara empapura z’ekitabo eky’okutangaaza. Okuteekateeka aha ku twabaire tugwejegyeire hamwe omu kiro ekyabandize, kandi na munonga kuteekateeka aha mukono gwa rukundo ogu naabaire nshangire gukanteeraho ku naabaire nyimuka omu kasheeshe, nkateekateeka ngu obutafayo bwe obu bukaba buri obw’okutangaaza munonga. Kwonka abanyamaishwa n’ebihangirwe ebitarikumanywa; obumwe tomanya gye oku orikubaasa kubitwara. Omu kubanza nibaba nibagira ekiniga kingi; okweteerana kwabo okucureire okw’okworoba nikureebeka nk’obwengye bwa Socrates. Nkaba nshangire ngu Queequeg taragambireho na kakye, nari kureka kakye munonga, n’abavugi b’enyanja abandi omu nju y’abagyenyi. Tihaine entunguuka yoona ei yaakozire; akareebeka nk’otaine kigyendererwa ky’okutunguura ekibiina ky’abantu abu yaabaire naamanya. Ebi byona bikankwataho nk’omuntu omwe w’amaani; kwonka, ku naateekateekireho kabiri, hakaba harimu ekintu haihi n’eky’ahaiguru munonga. Hanu hakaba hariho omushaija owabaire ari mailo nka obukumi makumi abiri kuruga omuka, arikurabira omu muhanda gwa Cape Horn, nikwo kugira ngu—ogwo gukaba guri omuhanda gwonka ogu yaabaire naabaasa kuhikayo—akajugunywa omu bantu nk’abatarikumanya ahariwe nk’oku yaabaire ari omu nsi ya Jupiter; kandi obwo akaba nareebeka nk’oine obusingye bwona; okurinda obusingye oburikukirayo; okumarwa n’obunywani bwe; obutoosha arikwingana nawe. Buzima eki kikaba kiri okukwata aha filosofiya nungi; n’obu hatariho kubangaanisa yaabaire atahuriraga ngu hariho ekintu nk’ekyo. Kwonka, obundi, kuba abanyabwengye ab’amazima, itwe abafa titushemerire kuba nitumanya ngu nitutuuraho munonga nari ngu nituteeraho munonga. N’ahabw’ekyo ahonaaho ku ndikuhurira ngu omushaija nk’ogwo nari nk’ogwo naayehayo ahabw’omunyabwengye, ninhendera ngu, nk’omukaikuru orikurwara endwara y’okubura eby’okurya, aine kuba “ahenda ekirungo kye ky’okurya.”

Nk’oku naabaire nshutami aho omu kishengye ekyo hati ekyabaire kiri kyonka; omuriro ogurikwotsya ahansi, omu rurengo orwo orw’obucureezi obu, bwanyima y’amaani gaagwo ag’okubanza gaherize kuhuumuriza empeho, obwo gukaba nigujwa kwonka kureeba; ebicuucu by’omu mwabazyo n’ebishushani by’emizimu birikweteerana okwehinguririza ebishengye, kandi bikatureeba omunda itwe babiri abacumire, abari bonka; omuyaga ogurikuteera hatariho omu kuzimba kw’ekitiinisa; Nkatandika kugira enyehurira ezitarikumanywa. Nkahurira nashuuma omunda yangye. Tihariho omutima gwangye ogwabaire gucwekire n’omukono gwangye ogwabaire gushazire ogwabaire guhindukire gukahindukira ensi y’ebicuncu. Omunyamaishwa ogu orikuhuumuriza akaba agicungwire. Aho niho yaashutami, obutafayo bwe bwonka burikugamba obuhangwa obutarimu oburyarya obutunguukire n’obugobya obutaine mugasho. Akaba ari omunyamaishwa; okureeba munonga kw’okureeba; kwonka nkatandika kuhurira ngu ninkunda kumukunda omu muringo gw’amazima. Kandi ebyo bintu nibyo bimwe ebyabaire nibiija kubinga abandi abaingi, nibyo byabaire biri amagineeti agaabaire nigandeetera omu muringo ogwo. Ndyagyezaho munywani wangye omupagani, nkateekateeka, ahabw’okuba embabazi z’Ekikristaayo zihamize kwonka obucureezi obutaine rubaju. Nkakurura entebe yangye haihi nawe, kandi naakora obubonero bw’omukago n’okuhabura, ndikukora kyona ekirikubaasika kugamba nawe omu bwire obwo. Omu kubanza akaba atarikureeba gye entunguuka ezi; kwonka hati, ku naagambire aha kutaayaayira abagyenyi kwe ekiro ekyahererukireyo, akakora enteekateeka y’okumbuuza yaaba nituza kugaruka kuba ab’omu kitanda. Nkamugira ngu eego; whereat nkateekateeka ngu akaba nareebeka nk’oshemereirwe, obundi arikusiimwa kakye.

Bwanyima tukahindura ekitabo ekyo hamwe, kandi nkateeraho kumushoboororera ekigyendererwa ky’okukiteera, n’amakuru g’ebishushani bikye ebyabaire birimu. N’ahabw’ekyo nkatandika kukunda kwe juba; kandi kuruga obwo tukaza omu kugaaniira okurungi oku twabaire nitubaasa kureeba aha bintu bitari bimwe na bimwe eby’aheeru ebirikureebeka omu tauni egi emanyirwe munonga. Tihakyahingwireho obwire buraingwa nkateekateeka aha kunywa etaabe y’abantu; kandi, arikushohoza enshaho ye na tomahawk, yanshaba ekifuba omu bucureezi. Kandi obwo tukaba tushutama turikuhindurana ebicupuri kuruga omu payipu ye egyo ey’omu ishwa, kandi tukagiguma nitugihinguraho butoosha ahagati yeitu.

Ku haraabe haabaire hakiriho orubaare rwona orw’okutafayo ahariinye omu ibeere ry’Omupagani, omwika ogu ogurikushemeza, ogw’obucureezi ogu twabaire twine, gukagusheenya juba, kandi gwatusigaho abanyabuzaare. Akaba nareebeka nk’orikuntwara omu buhangwa kandi atarikuragirwa nk’oku nyowe ndikumutwara; kandi omwika gwaitu ku gwahwaire, yanyiganyiga obuso bwe aha bwangye, yankwata omu kifuba, yaagira ngu kuruga hati tukashwerwa; ekirikumanyisa, omu bigambo by’eihanga rye, ngu tukaba turi banywani b’omu kifuba; akaba naija kunfeera n'okushemererwa, kyaba nikyetengyesa. Omu muntu w’omu ihanga, omuriro ogu ogw’obunywani ogw’ahonaaho gukaba nigwija kureebeka nk’ogutakahikire munonga, ekintu ekitarikwesigwa munonga; kwonka omu kinyamaishwa eki kyorobi ebiragiro ebyo ebya ira tibirikwija kukora.

Bwanyima y’okurya kyakiro, n’okugaaniira okundi n’okunywa etaabe, tukaza omu kishengye kyaitu hamwe. Yankorera ekiconco ky'omutwe gwe ogushemeire; akaihayo akashaho ke akahango k’etaabe, kandi arikukwaata ahansi y’etaabe, yaashohoza doola nka makumi ashatu z’efeeza; reero akabijanjaaza aha meeza, kandi akabibaganisamu omu muringo gw’ebyoma omu bicweka bibiri ebirikwingana, akabihanika kimwe aharibyo, kandi akagira ngu n’ekyangye. Nkaba ninza kuhakanisa; kwonka akanhuumuriza arikuzishuka omu nsaho z’empare zangye. Nkabareka baagumaho. Bwanyima akaza omu kushaba kwe okwa mwabazyo, yaihayo ekishushani kye, kandi yaihaho ekipande ky’omuriro eky’empapura. Ahabw’obumanyiso obumwe, nkateekateeka ngu akaba nareebeka nk’oine okwerarikirira ngu nyegaiteho; kwonka ahabw’okumanya gye ekyabaire nikiija kukuratira, nkateekateekaho akaanya kakye yaaba, ku araabe yanyetsire, nkaba ninza kukuratira nari ebindi.

Nkaba ndi Omukristaayo murungi; akazaarwa kandi akazaarwa omu kifuba ky’Ekanisa ya Presbyterian etaine nshobi. Mbwenu shi nkaba ninza kwegaita nta n’omuramya w’ebishushani ogu ow’omu ishwa omu kuramya ekicweka kye ky’embaaho? Kwonka shi okuramya n’enki? thought I. Hati nooteekateeka, Ishimaeli, ngu Ruhanga omukuru w’eiguru n’ensi—abapagani na boona barimu—nabaasa kuba aine eitima ahabw’akacweka k’embaaho akatari kakuru akarikwiragura? Ekitarabasika! Kwonka shi okuramya n’enki?—okukora ebi Ruhanga akunda— okwo n’okuramya. Kandi shi okukunda kwa Ruhanga n’enki?—okukora ahari mugyenzi wangye eki ndikwenda ngu mugyenzi wangye ankore— ekyo nikyo Ruhanga akunda. Hati, Queequeg n’omuntu mugyenzi wangye. Kandi shi ninshaba ngu ogu Queequeg ankore ki? Ahabw’enki, mwegaite naanye omu muringo gwangye gw’okuramya ogwa Presbyterian ogw’omutaano. Ekirugiremu, obwo nyine kwegaita nawe omu bye; ergo, nyine kuhinduka omuramya w’ebishushani. Ntyo naayotsya ebishaju; akahwera omu kuhagira ekishushani ekikye ekitaine rubanja; akamuha bisikwiti eyokize erimu Queequeg; salamed omu maisho ge emirundi ebiri nari eshatu; akamunywegyera enyindo; kandi ekyo ku kyakozirwe, tukajumbura twaza omu kitanda, turi omu busingye n’omuntu waitu w’omunda n’ensi yoona. Kwonka titukaza kubyama tutaine kakye akarikugaaniiraho.

Oku kiri tindikumanya; kwonka tihariho mwanya nk’ekitanda ky’okushohozamu ebihama ahagati y’abanywani. Omushaija n’omukazi, nibagira ngu, niho barikwigurirana ahansi munonga y’emyoyo yaabo; kandi abakuzire abamwe nibakira kubeihabeiha n‘okugaaniira aha bwire bwa ira kuhitsya haihi omu kasheeshe. N’ahabw’ekyo, omu bwire bw’obushwere bw’emitima yaitu, tukaba tugwejegyeire nyowe na Queequeg—ababiri abarungi kandi abaine rukundo.





EISHOMO 11. Ekijwaro ky’ekiro.

Tukaba tugwejegyeire tutyo omu kitanda, turikugaaniira kandi turikugwejegyera omu bwire bukye, kandi Queequeg hati n’obunaku obu omu muringo gwa rukundo arikuteera amaguru ge aga brown agarimu tattoo aha gangye, reero akagagarura enyima; n’ahabw’ekyo tukaba turi abanywani b’abantu boona kandi abaine obugabe kandi abanguhi; obu, aha muheru, ahabw’okugaaniira kwaitu, okugwejegyera okukye okwabaire kusigaire omuriitwe kukarugaho kwona, kandi twahurira nitwenda kugaruka kwimuka, n’obu okuhuumura kw’omushana kwabaire kukiri omu bwire bw’omumaisho.

Eego, tukaba nitusisimuka munonga; n’okuhitsya obu omwanya gwaitu gw’okugwejegyera gukatandika kuruha, kandi kakye kakye tukashanga tushutami; emyenda ekaba etwehinguririize gye, eyesigamiire aha rubaaho rw’omutwe n’amaguru gaitu ana gakwatsirwe haihi, kandi enyindo zaitu ibiri zirikuzifukamaho, nk’oku amaguru gaitu gaabaire gari ebiyungu by’okuhuumuriza. Tukahurira turi gye munonga kandi turi gye, okukira munonga ahabw’okugira ngu hakaba hariho obufuki bwingi aheeru y’enju; buzima kuruga omu bijwaro by’ekitanda nabyo, ahabw’okureeba ngu omu kishengye hakaba hatariho muriro. Ekindi, ningira, ahabw’okuba buzima okushemererwa n’obutagatsi bw’omubiri, akacweka kakye kamwe kaine kuba kari akafuka, ahabw’okuba tihaine mutwarize omu nsi egi ogutari nk’oku guri ahabw’okugyeragyeranisa kwonka. Tihariho ekiriho ahabwakyo. Waaba noyehimbisa ngu oine obusingye bwona, kandi obaire oine obwire buraingwa munonga, obwo torikubaasa kugambwa ngu oine obusingye obundi. Kwonka ku oraabe, nka Queequeg nanye omu kitanda, omutwe gw’enyindo yaawe nari omutwe gw’omutwe gwawe gufukiire kakye, ahabw’enki obwo, buzima, omu kuteekateeka kwa boona noohurira oine okushemererwa okurikukirayo kandi otaine kubanganisa. Ahabw’enshonga egi omuzigo gw’okubyamamu tigushemerire kuteebwamu omuriro, ekimwe aha bintu by’okweshemeza ebi abagaiga barikuteganisa. Ahabw’okuba oburaingwa bw’okushemererwa kw’omuringo ogu n’okutagira ekindi kureka obutambaara ahagati yaawe n’okushemererwa kwawe n’obufuki bw’omuyaga gw’aheeru. Reero aho noogwejegyera nk’enku emwe erikwotsya omu mutima gw’ekibiriiti ky’omu kicweka kya arctic.

Tukaba tumazire obwire tushutami omu muringo ogu gw’okufukama, ahonaaho nkateekateeka ngu ninyija kwigura amaisho; ahakuba ku ndikuba ndi ahagati y’ebipande, yaaba omu ihangwe nari nyekiro, n’obu ndaabe ngwejegyeire nari nshutami, nyine omuringo gw’okuguma nkingire amaisho, kubaasa kwongyera kuta omutima aha kushemererwa kw’okuguma omu kitanda. Ahabw’okuba tihariho muntu orikubaasa kuhurira gye eki ari, okwihaho amaisho ge gakingire; nk’oku omwirima buzima guri ekintu ekihikire eky’obuhangwa bwaitu, n’obu omushana guraabe nigukira kushemeza ekicweka kyaitu eky’eibumba. Ku naakingire amaisho gangye obwo, kandi nkaruga omu mwirima gwangye ogurikushemeza kandi ogu naayehangire kuza omu mwirima gw’aheeru oguteirweho kandi ogugumire ogw’eshaha ikumi n’ibiri z’ekiro ezitarimu mushana, nkatunga okuhemuka okutarikushemeza. Kandi tindikuhakanisa na kakye okuhabura kuruga ahari Queequeg ngu obundi kikaba kiri kirungi kuteera etabaaza, ahabw’okureeba ngu tukaba tusisikeire munonga; kandi okwihaho ekyo akahurira aine ekyetengo ky’amaani ky’okugira ebicupuri bikye ebitarimu kugamba kuruga omu Tomahawk ye. Kigambe ngu n’obu ndaabe naabaire mpurire okuziika kw’amaani nk’okwo ahabw’okunywa etaabe ye omu kitanda ekiro ekyabandize, kwonka reeba oku okushoroora kwaitu okugumire kurikweyongyera rukundo ku erikuba neeza kugihenda. Ahabw’okuba hati nkaba ntarikwenda ekindi kyona kukira okugira Queequeg arikunywa etaabe haihi naanye, nangwa n’omu kitanda, ahabw’okuba akaba nareebeka nk’oijwire okushemererwa kw’omu ka okurimu obusingye nk’okwo obwire obwo. Tindagarukire kuhurira nyine okwerarikirira okurengire aha nkora ya inshuwarensi ya mukama wa nju. Nkaba ndiho ahabw’okushemererwa kw’ekihama okukye okw’okugaaniira aha paipu n’ekitambaara na munywani wangye buzima. Twaba twine ebijwaro byaitu eby’ebishaju ebiteirwe aha mabega gaitu, hati tukaba turaba omu Tomahawk kuruga ahari emwe kuza aha ndiijo, kuhitsya obu mpora mpora twakuzire ahaiguru yaitu ekigyezo ky’omwika ekirikuhanikwa ekya bururu, ekirikumurikwa omuriro gw’etabaaza ensya eyaabaire neeyaka.

Okuba ngu omugyezi ogu orikugurukaguruka akazingira omunyamaishwa akaza omu myanya eri hare munonga, tindikumanya, kwonka hati akaba naagamba aha kirwa eki yaazaariirwemu; kandi ahabw’okwenda kuhurira ebyafaayo bye, nkamushaba ngu agumizemu abigambire. Akabyorobera n’okushemererwa. N’obu omu bwire obwo nabaire ntarikwetegyereza gye ebigambo bye bikye, kwonka okushohozibwa okwakuratsireho, obu naabaire nmanyire gye ebigambo bye ebicwekire, hati nibinshoboororera kworeka ekigano kyona nk’oku kirikubaasa kuhamya omu nshaho yonka ei ndikuha.





EISHOMO 12. Ebihandiiko by’amagara g’omuntu.

Queequeg akaba ari enzaarwa ya Rokovoko, ekizinga ekiri hare munonga omu Burengyerwa-izooba n’Oburugwa-izooba. Tikiri ahansi omu maapu yoona; emyanya y’amazima terikubaho.

Obu ekinyonyi ky’omu ishwa ekisya kizairwe kirikwiruka omu kibira ky’obuzaarwa bwe omu kicuucu ky’obunyaatsi, kikuratirwa embuzi ezirikurya, nk’oku kiri omu kishaka ky’ebishaka; nangwa n’obwo, omu mutima gwa Queequeg ogwabaire nigukunda munonga, hakaba harimu ekyetengo ky’amaani eky’okureeba ekintu ekirikukira aha Bukristaayo okukira eky’okureeberaho ky’omuvubi w’enyanja nari ebiri. Ishe akaba ari Omukuru, Omugabe; ishento akaba ari Omunyamurwa Omukuru; kandi aha rubaju rwa nyinazaara akaba ayehimbisa ahabwa ba ssenga abaabaire bari abakazi b’abashaija b’amaani abatarikusingurwa. Omu misi ye hakaba harimu eshagama nungi munonga—ebintu by’obugabe; n’obu araabe yaashiisiibwe omu muringo gw’obusaasi, nintiina, ahabw’omuze gw’okurya abantu ogu yaabaire aine omu buto bwe obu yaabaire atashomesiibwe.

Emeeri ya Sag Harbor ekataayaayira omwaaro gwa ishe, kandi Queequeg akasherura omuhanda gw’okuza omu mahanga g’Ekikristaayo. Kwonka emeeri, ahabw'okugira ngu ekaba eine abavugi b'enyanja boona, ekayanga ekijwaro kye; kandi ti Mugabe weena obushoboorozi bwa ishe obu yaabaire naabaasa kusingura. Kwonka Queequeg akaraganisa. Akavuga ari wenka omu bwato bwe, yaaza omu nyanja eri hare munonga, ei yaabaire naamanya ngu emeeri eine kurabamu ku eraabe yaaruga aha kirwa ekyo. Aha rubaju rumwe hakaba hariho oruhanga rw’amaizi; aha rubaju orundi orurimi rw’itaka oruri ahansi, orurimu ebishaka ebyabaire nibikura bikaza omu maizi. Asherekire obwato bwe, obukiri aha maizi, omu bishaka ebi, n’omutwe gwabwo gurikureeba enyanja, yaashutama aha rubaju rw’enyima, aine ekigyere kiri ahansi omu ngaro; kandi emeeri ku yaabaire neehinguraho, yaarugayo nk'omushana; akatunga orubaju rwe; n’ekigyere kye kimwe ekyabaire kigarukire enyima kikahinduka kyabiika obwato bwe; akatemba aha minyororo; kandi yaayeshuka omu bureingwa bwona aha mutwe gw'enyanja, yaakwataho empeta y'okuteeraho, yaarahira ngu tarikwija kugireka, n'obu eraabe yaabaire eshazirwemu ebicweka.

Busha kapiteeni akamutiinatiinisa kumunaga omu nyanja; akahanika omuhoro aha biganja bye ebiri busha; Queequeg akaba ari mutabani w’Omugabe, kandi Queequeg tarahuzya. Ahabw’okukwatwaho ahabw’okubura obwoba, n’okwenda kwe okw’amaani okw’okutaayaayira amadiini agarikweyeta Amakristaayo, aha muheru omukuru w’emeeri akarekyera aho, kandi yamugira ngu naabaasa kwehindura omuka. Kwonka ogu mutsigazi murungi w’omunyamaishwa—ogu Omugabe wa Wales ow’omu nyanja, tarareebire akaju ka Kapiteeni. Bakamuta ahansi omu bavugi b'emeeri, bamuhindura omuvugi w'enyanja. Kwonka nka Czar Peter owaabaire naamarwa n’okukora munonga omu myanya y’okukoreramu emeeri z’omu ndembo z’aheeru, Queequeg taragaya ekintu kyona ekirikureebeka nk’eky’obushema, ku araabe yaabaire naabaasa kutunga amaani g’okumurikira abantu b’omu ihanga rye abatarikwegyesibwa n’okushemererwa. Ahabw’okuba ahansi—nikwo yangira—akaba naakorwaho ekyetengo ky’amaani eky’okwega omu Bakristaayo, eby’obuhangwa ebi yaabaire naabaasa kureetera abantu be kuguma baine okushemererwa okukira oku baabaire bari; kandi ekirikukira ahari ekyo, bakaba bakiri barungi kukira oku baabaire bari. Kwonka, kakye! ebikorwa by’abavugi b’enyanja bikamuhamiza juba ngu n’Abakristaayo nibabaasa kuba ab’obusaasi kandi ababi; okukira okutaine bugarukiro, okukira abanyamahanga boona aba ishe. Akahika aha muheru omu mwaaro gwa Sag ogwa ira; n'okureeba ebi abavugi b'emeeri baakozireyo; kandi bwanyima bakagumizamu Nantucket, kandi bakareeba oku baakoreise empeera zaabo omu mwanya ogwo nabwo, Queequeg omworo akagihayo ahabw’okubura. Akateekateeka ngu, n’ensi embi omu meridians zoona; Ndyafa ndi omupagani.

Kandi atyo omuramya w’ebishushani omugurusi omu mutima, akaba naakituura omu Bakristaayo aba, ajwaire emyenda yaabo, kandi agyezaho kugamba ebigambo byabo by’obushema. Nikyo emiringo y’okutangaaza erikumukwataho, n’obu hati aine obwire kuruga omuka.

By hints, nkamubuuza yaaba atarikuteekateeka kugaruka, n’okugira okuteebwaho obugabe; ahabw’okugira ngu hati akaba naabaasa kuteekateeka ngu ishe afiire kandi akagyenda, akaba akuzire munonga kandi aine amaani makye omu byafaayo by’aha muheru. Akagarukamu ngu ngaaha, tikwo; kandi yayongyeraho ngu akaba ari okutiina Obukristaayo, nari shi Abakristaayo, bakaba batamuhikireho ahabw’okutemba entebe y’obugabe eshemeire kandi etaine kamogo ey’Abagabe makumi ashatu abapagani abamubandiize. Kwonka akagira ngu bwanyima y’obwire bukye, akaba naija kugaruka, — ahonaaho ku yaabaire ahurira yaagaruka yaabatizibwa. Kwonka ahabw’obwire bukye, akateekateeka ngu agyende omu meeri, kandi abibe ebihimba bye by’omu ishwa omu nyanja ina zoona. Bakaba bamukoreire omuhoro, kandi ekyoma ekyo eky’amabaare kikaba kiri omu mwanya gw’omuhoro hati.

Nkamubuuza ekirikubaasa kuba ekigyendererwa kye eky’ahonaaho, ekirikukwata aha ntambura ze ez’omumaisho. Akagarukamu, kugaruka kuza omu nyanja, omu kwetwa kwe okwa ira. Aha kintu eki, nkamugira ngu okuhiiga enyanja z’omu nyanja n’entebeekanisa yangye nyenka, kandi naamumanyisa ekigyendererwa kyangye ky’okuruga Nantucket, nk’omwanya ogurikukirayo kuha amatsiko g’okuhiiga enyanja z’omu nyanja z’omunyabwengye kurugaho. Ahonaaho akacwamu kunhereeza aha kirwa ekyo, emeeri eri aha meeri niyo emwe, ataaha omu nshaho emwe, obwato bumwe, akavuyo kamwe nanye, omu bugufu kugaaniira ahari buri hap yangye; n’emikono yangye yombi omu ye, n’obumanzi n’okuta omu Potluck y’ensi zombi. Ebi byona nkabiikiriza n’okushemererwa; ahakuba oyihireho rukundo ei naabaire nyine ahari Queequeg hati, akaba ari omuvugi w’enkoni oine obumanyirizi, kandi nk’oku ari, akaba atarikubaasa kuremwa kuba ow’omugasho munonga aha muntu, owaabaire, nk’owangye, ataine ki arikumanya kyona aha byama by’okuhiiga enyanja, n’obu araabe yaabaire naamanya gye enyanja, nk’oku abavugi b’enyanja abashuubuzi barikumanya.

Ekigano kye kyaheza n’okufuka kw’omupiira gwe ogw’aha muheru ogwabaire nigufa, Queequeg akankwata omu kifuba, yanyiganyiga obuso bwe aha bwangye, kandi yaafuka omushana, twayehindura kuruga ahari buri omwe, omu muringo ogu n’ogu, kandi omu bwire bukye munonga tukaba tugwejegyeire.





EISHOMO 13. Ekigaari ky’enziga.

Omu kasheeshe akaakuratsireho, Orwokubanza, bwanyima y’okunaga omutwe ogwabaire guteirwe omubazi gw’okushara, ahabw’okumara bbulooka, nkashoboorora ebishare byangye n’ebya munywani wangye; n’okukoresa, kwonka, esente za munywani wangye. Mukama w’enju owaabaire namwenya, hamwe n’abashuubuzi, bakaba nibareebeka nk’abashemereirwe omu muringo gw’okutangaaza ahabw’omukago gw’ahonaaho ogwabaire gutandikire ahagati yangye na Queequeg—na munonga nk’oku ebitebyo bya Peter Coffin eby’enkoko n’ente ebirikumukwataho byabaire bintiiniise munonga enyimaho aha bikwatiraine n’omuntu wenka ou hati ndikukora nawe.

Twaguza akagaari k’ebigyere, kandi twataaha omu bintu byaitu, otwariiremu n’ensaho yangye y’omworo eya kapeti, n’ensaho ya Queequeg ey’ekipande n’ekitanda, hare tukashuuma twaza “omu Moss,” akagaari kakye aka Nantucket akabaire kakwatsire aha mwaro. Ku twabaire nitugyenda abantu bakatureeba; ti ahari Queequeg munonga—ahabw’okuba bakaba bamanyiriire kureeba abantu barikurya abantu nkawe omu nguuto zaabo,—kwonka aha kureeba nyowe nawe aha nshonga z’ekihama nk’ezo. Kwonka titukahurikiize, tukaba nitugyenda nituvuga emotoka y’ekigaari turikugihindura, kandi Queequeg hati n’obwire bukye akaba ayemerera kuhindura ekibunu aha nshonda ze z’enkoni. Nkamubuuza ahabw’enki yaabaire atwara ekintu ekirikureeta oburemeezi nk’ekyo aha nkungu, kandi yaaba emeeri zoona ezirikuhiiga enyanja z’ebinyonyi zitarikutunga ebikwato byazo. Aha kintu eki, omu bwijwire, akagarukamu ati, ngu n’obu eki naabaire ninteekateekaho kyabaire kiri eky’amazima ekirikumara, kwonka akaba aine rukundo y’omutaano aha nkoni ye, ahabw’okuba ekaba eri ey’ebintu ebihamibwe, egyeziibwe gye omu ntaro nyingi z’okufa, kandi erimu akakwate karungi n’emitima y’enyanja. Omu bugufu, nk’abagyesi baingi ab’omu itaka n’abahingi, abarikuza omu bishaka by’abahingi baine eby’okurwanisa byabo eby’okuteera—n’obu baraabe bataine bujunaanizibwa bwona bw’okubiha—n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, Queequeg, ahabw’enshonga ze ez’ekihama, akaba naakunda enkoni ye.

Akahindura akagaari kuruga omu ngaro zangye akagata omu ge, yangira ekitebyo ekirikushemeza ekirikukwata aha kagaari k’okubanza aku yaabaire areebire. Kikaba kiri omu mwaro gwa Sag. Nikireebeka bakama b’emeeri ye bakaba bamugurize emwe, ey’okutwaramu ekifuba kye ekiremeire kuza omu nju ye y’okuraaramu. Okutareebeka nk’otaine ki arikumanya aha kintu ekyo—n’obu omu mazima yaabaire ari atyo kimwe, aha bikwatiraine n’omuringo gwenyini ogw’okukoresamu akagaari—Queequeg akakitaho ekifuba kye; akagiteera juba; kandi bwanyima atwara ekigaari aha mabega kandi agyendera aha mwaro. “Ahabw’enki,” nkagira, “Queequeg, noobaasa kuba noomanya gye okukiraho, omuntu naija kuteekateeka. Abantu tibarashekire?”

Aha kintu eki, akangambira ekindi kigano. Abantu b’aha kirwa kye ekya Rokovoko, nikireebeka ngu aha bugyenyi bwabo bw’obugyenyi nibooreka amaizi g’ebihimba ebito agarimu omugaju omu kikopo kihango ekirimu amabara nk’ekikopo ky’okuteera; kandi ekikopo eki eky’okuteera ebikonde butoosha nikikora ekirungo kihango eky’ahagati aha mukyeeka ogurukire ahu obugyenyi burikubaho. Hati emeeri y’abashuubuzi emwe mpango ekakwata ahari Rokovoko, kandi omukuru waayo—kuruga omu byafaayo byona, omushaija w’ekitiinisa munonga orikukuratira obwire, haakiri ahabw’omukuru w’enyanja—omukuru ogu akeetwa aha bugyenyi bw’obugyenyi bwa munyaanya wa Queequeg, omugabekazi murungi muto owaabaire yaaheza kuhikiza emyaka 10. Kurungi; abagyenyi b’obugyenyi boona ku baabaire bateeraine omu nju y’omugore ey’emizeituuni, Kapiteeni ogu ataaha, kandi ahabw’okuheebwa omwanya gw’ekitiinisa, ayeta aha mutwe gw’ekikopo ky’okuteera ebikoni, kandi ahagati y’Omunyamurwa Omukuru n’omukuru we Omugabe, ishe Queequeg. Embabazi ku zirikugambwa,—ahabw’okuba abo bantu baine embabazi zaabo nk’oku turi—n’obu Queequeg araabe yangira ngu okutashushana naitwe, aba omu bwire nk’obwo turikureeba ahansi aha bikopo byaitu, bo, okutaana n’ekyo, barikukopa embaata, nibareeba ahaiguru aha Mugabi mukuru w’obugyenyi bwona—Embabazi, ningira, nizigambwa, Omunyamurwa Omukuru owa imme the ban the imme the; nikwo kugira ngu, okuta enkumu ze ezirikwera kandi ezirikwera omu nsaka eky’okunywa ekiine omugisha kitakagyendeire. Okwereeba ateirwe haihi n’Omunyamurwa, kandi akareeba omukoro, kandi arikweteekateekaho—okuba Kapiteeni w’emeeri—nk’oine obukuru oburikworeka Omugabe w’ekizinga kyonka, namunonga omu nju y’Omugabe wenka—Kapiteeni agumizamu n’obucureezi kunaaba engaro ze omu punchbowl fingge it;-taking a huglass I; Queequeg akagira ati: “Hati, eki orikuteera hati?—Abantu baitu tibarashekire?”

Aha muheru, twashashura orugyendo, kandi n’emigugu erimu oburinzi, twemerera aha bwato oburikwetwa schooner. Ekaba neeimutsya emeeri, ekagyendera aha mugyera Acushnet. Aha rubaju rumwe, New Bedford ekaba neetunguuka omu bishengye by’enguuto, emiti yaayo erimu orubaare yoona erikumurinkana omu mwanya ogurikwera kandi ogurikwotsya. Ebibungo bihango n’ensozi z’ebidomora aha bidomora bikaba bicumbirwe aha myanya ye y’okushutamamu, kandi aha rubaju n’orubaju emeeri z’enyanja ezirikuzengyerera omu nsi zikaba zigwejegyeire kandi zikwatsirwe gye aha muheru; obwo kuruga omu bandi hakaba niharugayo amaraka g’ababeizi n’abakozi b’ebyoma, n’amaraka g’omuriro n’ebyoma by’okusheenya ekishaayi, byona birikworeka ngu emeeri ensya zikaba nizitandika; ngu orugyendo rumwe orurikukirayo akabi kandi oruraingwa rukahwaho, nirutandika rwa kabiri rwonka; kandi ekya kabiri kyahwa, kyatandika ekya kashatu kyonka, n’ebindi, ebiro byona n’ebiro byona. Okwo nikwo okutagira bugarukiro kuri, eego, okutagumisiriza kw’okuteeraho kwona okw’omu nsi.

Okutunga amaizi agarikukirayo kwiguka, omuyaga ogurikugaruramu amaani gukaba guhinduka murungi; aka Moss kakye kakateera ekicuucu ky’ahonaaho kuruga omu buta bwe, nk’oku akaana k’endogoya kaabaire nikasheeshasheesha. Ka nkahuuta omuyaga ogwo ogw’Abatartar!—ka nkagaya ensi egyo eyabaire neehindukahinduka!—omuhanda ogwo omuhango ogwa buriijo gukaba gurimu ebifunjo byona n’obumanyiso bw’ebigyere n’ebigyere by’abahuuku; kandi yanhindura kushemererwa obuhango bw’enyanja obutarikwikiriza ebihandiiko byona.

Aha iziba niryo rimwe ery’ebicupuri, Queequeg akaba nashusha nk’orikunywa kandi arikuteerana naanye. Enyindo ze ezirimu omwirima zikaba zizimba; akooreka ameino ge agafiire kandi agashongoire. Aha, aha tukaguruka; kandi offing yaitu ekatunga, Moss ekaha ekitiinisa okuteera; akacumita kandi yaajuba obuta bwe nk’omuhuuku omu maisho ga Sultan. Twesigamiire aha rubaju, tukaba tuza aha rubaju; buri ruhu rw'omugoye niruteera nk'orukoba; emiti ebiri miraingwa erikugurukaguruka nk’emiti y’Abahinda omu nkuba z’aha itaka. Twijwire munonga ekishushani eki ekyabaire nikitetema, obu twabaire twemereire aha rubaju rw’obuta oburikugwa omu maizi, ngu kumara obwire tutarikureeba okureeba okurikushekyerera kw’abavugi, orukiiko oruri nk’orwa lubber, abaabaire nibatangaara ngu abantu babiri bagyenzi baitu baine kuba baine abanywani nk’abo; nk’oku omweru yaabaire ari ekintu kyona eky’ekitiinisa kukira omujungu owaabaire ajwairwe erangi erikwera. Kwonka hakaba hariho obuboozi n’ebihimba ebimwe aho, ebyabaire, ahabw’okugira ekibira kyabyo ekihango, biine kuba byarugire omu mutima n’ahagati y’ebibira byona. Queequeg akakwata kimwe aha bihingwa ebi ebito arikumutooreza enyima y’omugongo gwe. Nkateekateeka ngu eshaaha y’okucwekyerezibwa kw’omukishaka ekahika. Okuteera orukoba rwe, omunyamaishwa w’amaani akamukwata omu mikono ye, kandi ahabw’obumanyi n’amaani ebiri haihi n’eby’okutangaaza, yaamutuma ahaiguru omu mubiri omu mwanya; reero akateeraho kakye aha rubaju rwe rw’enyima ahagati y’enyanja ya somerset, mugyenzi we akagwa ahansi aine amahaha garikuturika aha bigyere bye, obwo Queequeg, arikumuhindura omugongo, yaamurikira omupiira gwe ogwa tomahawk kandi yaaguhitsya ahariinye ngu nfuuhe.

"Okukwaata! Okukwaata!" akagamba n’eiraka rihango, arikwirukira omu ofiisa ogwo; “Okukapu, Kapu, hanu hariho sitaani.”

"Hallo, iwe mukama wangye," Kapiteeni akarira, orubaju rw'enyanja orukye, arikukuratira Queequeg, "n'enki omu nkuba noomanyisa ekyo? Torikumanya ngu noobaasa kuba yaitsire chap ogwo?"

“Eki arikugamba?” Queequeg akagira atyo, obwo arikuhindukira nyowe omu bucureezi.

“Naagira,” nkagira, “ngu okaija haihi kwita-e ogwo mushaija okwo,” arikworeka eihembe rya kinyasi eririkutetema.

“Ita-e,” Queequeg akarira, arikuhindura omu maisho ge agaabaire garimu tattoo omu muringo ogutari gw’ensi ogw’okugaya, “ah! we bevy akakye-e kakye-e; Queequeg ta kill-e kakye munonga-e ekyenyanja-e; Queequeg aite-e ekinyonyi kihango!”

“Reeba iwe,” Kapiteeni akagamba n’eiraka rihango, “ninza kukwita -e , iwe omunya-nyama, waagyezaho obukodyo bwawe bwona aha bwato hanu; n’ahabw’ekyo teekateeka aha riisho ryawe.”

Kwonka kikabaho omu bwire obwo bwonka, ngu obwire bukahika ngu Kapiteeni ateekateekye aha riisho rye. Okugyemeserezibwa kw’amaani aha rubaju rw’enyanja orukuru kukaba kwataaniiseho ekipande ky’obwire, kandi obuhuta bw’amaani hati bukaba nibuguruka kuruga aha rubaju rumwe kuza aha rundi, burikusheenya kimwe ekicweka kyona eky’aha rubaju rw’enyanja. Omworo ou Queequeg yaabaire akwatsireho kubi munonga, akasheesheka omu nyanja; emikono yoona ekaba eri omu kutiina; kandi okugyezaho kunyaga aha boomu kugigumaho, kikaba nikireebeka nk’obushema. Kikaguruka kuruga aha rubaju rwa buryo kuza aha rwa bumosho, kandi kyagaruka enyima, haihi omu kuteera kw’eshaha kumwe, kandi buri kaanya kakaba nikareebeka nk’akarikuza kusheenyagurika kahinduka ebicweka. Tihariho ekyakozirwe, kandi tihaine ekyabaire nikireebeka nk’ekirikubaasa kukorwa; abo ababaire bari aha mutwe gw’enyanja bakairuka baaza aha buta, kandi beemerera nibareeba boomu nk’oku eri orubaju rwa hansi orw’enyanja y’enyanja eyaabaire erimu ekiniga kingi. Omu bwire bw’okushoberwa oku, Queequeg akagwa omu mavi ge, kandi yaayekurura ahansi y’omuhanda gw’ekibunu, yaakwata omurunga, yaahamya omuheru gumwe aha bigo, reero yaateera ogundi nk’omugoye, yaakikwata okwehinguririza ekibunu nk’oku kyabaire nikisheenya aha mutwe gwe gwona, kandi ogwo gukaba guri jerk yoona, origye. Ekishaayi ky’enyanja kikaba nikihunga omu muyaga, kandi obwo emikono erikwesheenya obwato bw’enyima, Queequeg, ajwaire ebijwaro kuhika omu kifuba, akahunga kuruga aha rubaju n’orubaju ruraingwa oruriho orw’okuguruka. Okumara edakiika ishatu nari n’okukiraho akaba areebeka arikwoga nk’embwa, arikuteera emikono ye miraingwa egoroire omu maisho ge, kandi arikuhindukahinduka arikworeka ebibega bye ebiine amagara kurabira omu nkungu y’obufuki. Nkareeba omunywani wangye omukuru kandi ow’ekitiinisa, kwonka tindareebire muntu weena ow’okujunwa. Eihembe rya kinyaatsi rikaba ryagyenzire ahansi. Okweteera amasasi omu muringo oguhikire kuruga omu maizi, Queequeg, hati akareeba akaanya kakye okwehinguririize, kandi arikushusha ngu naareeba oku enshonga ziri, yaaza omu maizi yaabura. Edakkika nkye ezindi, kandi akagaruka yaimuka, omukono gumwe gukiteera, kandi ogundi gukurura ekishushani ekitaine magara. Omu bwire bukye, obwato bukabatwara. Omworo ogwo gukagarurwaho. Emikono yoona ekateera akaruuru Queequeg nk’omutegyeki w’ekitiinisa; kapiteeni yaashaba okusaasirwa. Kuruga aha shaaha egyo nkaba ninkora Queequeg nk’enkongoro; eego, okuhitsya obu omworo Queequeg yaatwara omuvuyo gwe muraingwa ogw’aha muheru.

Mbwenu shi hakabaho obutamanya nk’obwo? Akaba nareebeka nk’otarikuteekateeka ngu na kakye akaba ashemerire kutunga omudaali kuruga omu bibiina by’abantu n’eby’obukuru. Akashaba amaizi gonka—amaizi marungi—ekintu ky’okusiimura omubazi gw’omubazi; ekyo kyaheza kukorwa, akajwara emyenda eyomire, yaamurikira omupiira gwe, kandi yaayesigamira aha bigo, kandi yaareeba omu bucureezi abo abamwehinguririize, akashusha nk’orikwegambira wenka—“N’ensi y’abantu boona, ey’abantu boona, omu meridians zoona. Itwe abarikurya abantu twine kuhwera Abakristaayo aba.”





EISHOMO RYA 14. Nantu.

Tihariho ekindi ekyabaho aha kicweka ekishemerire kugambwaho; n’ahabw’ekyo, bwanyima y’okwiruka gye, tukahika Nantucket gye.

Nantuketi! Ihaho maapu yaawe ogireebere. Reeba oku erikutwara enshonda y’ensi eyaabuzima; oku erikwemerera aho, hare n’enyanja, eri yonka kukira enju y’etara eya Eddystone. Reeba—orushozi rwonka, n’ekibunu ky’omushenyi; beach yoona, hatariho nshonga. Hariho omushenyi mwingi aho kukira oku waakubaasa kukoresa omu myaka makumi abiri nk’omu mwanya gw’empapura z’okusiiga. Abamwe aba gamesome wights nibaija kukugambira ngu baine kuhingayo omwata, tigurikukura omu buhangwa; ngu nibareeta ebishaka bya Canada kuruga omu ihanga; ngu baine kwohereza aheeru y’enyanja kutunga spile kuremesa okusheeshasheesha omu nshaho y’amajuta; ngu ebicweka by’embaaho omuri Nantucket nibitwarwa nk’ebicweka by’omusharaba gwenyini omuri Rooma; ngu abantu b’omuri ekyo kicweka bahinga ebishaka omu maisho g’enju zaabo, kubaasa kutunga ekicuucu omu bwire bw’omushana; ngu orubaju rumwe rw’obunyaatsi nirukora oasis, orubaju rushatu omu kugyendera kw’ekiro kimwe rukora ekishaka; ngu nibajwara enkaito z’omushenyi ogurikwetwa quicksand, ekintu nk’enkaito z’omushana eza Laplander; ngu zikingirwe munonga, zikingirwe, zikingirwe buri muringo, ziyehinguririziibwe, kandi zikozirwe ekizinga ky’omutaano aha rubaju rw’enyanja, ngu aha ntebe zaabo zonka n’aha meeza obumwe obukooko obukye nibubaasa kushangwa bukwatsire, nk’aha migongo y’enyanja z’omu nyanja. Kwonka ebikorwa ebi eby’omutaano nibyoreka kwonka ngu Nantucket ti Illinois.

Reeba hati ekitebyo ky’obuhangwa eky’okutangaaza oku ekizinga eki kyatuurwamu abashaija abatukura. Nikwo enfumu erikugyenda. Omu bwire bwa kare ekihungu kikaba kiguruka aha nyanja ya New England, kyatwara omwana muto w’omuhindi omu bigyere bye. Abazaire bakareeba omwana waabo arikutwarwa aha maizi mahango atarikureebwa. Bakacwamu kukuratira omuhanda nigwo gumwe.
