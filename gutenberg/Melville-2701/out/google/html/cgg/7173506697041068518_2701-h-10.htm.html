Hakaba nihareebeka ngu hakaba hariho bikye omu bigambo, kwonka eiraka rikaba nirimanyisa obusaasi bw’amaani obutaine buhwezi kukira oku omugurusi omushema yaabaire ayorekire. Kwonka akahindukira omuvugi w’emeeri, owaabaire kuhitsya hati akwatsire emeeri omu muyaga kukyendeeza aha kugyendera kwayo, yaagamba n’eiraka rihango omu iraka rye ry’ekicuncu erya ira ati,—“Imutsya omuvugi!

Okwetoorora ensi yoona! Hariho bingi omu iraka eryo ebirikureetaho enyehurira z’okwehimbisa; kwonka shi okwetoorora okwo kwona nikutwara nkahi? Kurabira omu kabi akatarikubarwa kwonka kuhika ahu twatandikire, ahu abo abu twatsigireho barinzire, baabaire bari omu maisho gaitu obwire bwona.

Ensi egi ku eraabe eri oruhanga orutarikuhwaho, kandi turikuvuga emeeri turikuza omu burugwa-izooba tukaba nitubaasa kuhika aha myanya mirungi ebiro byona, kandi tukazoora ebishushani ebirikushemeza kandi ebitarikumanywa kukira Cyclades zoona nari Ebizinga by’Omugabe Sulemaani, obwo hakaba hariho okuraganisa omu rugendo. Kwonka omu kukuratira ebyo byahama ebya hare ebi turikuroota, nari omu kuhiiga okubonabonesibwa kw’ekihangirwe ekyo eky’omuzimu eki, obwire bumwe nari obundi, kirikwoga omu maisho g’emitima y’abantu boona; obwo barikubinga nk’abo aha nsi egi eyehinguririize, nibatutwara omu myanya etaine nshonga nari ahagati y’omuhanda nibatusiga tushoberwa.
