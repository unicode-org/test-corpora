EISHOMO 101. Ekikopo ky’okunywa.

Ere emeeri y’Orungyereza etakahweireho, ebe eteirwe ahansi aha, ngu ekaruga London, kandi ekaheebwa eiziina ry’omugyenzi Samuel Enderby, omushuubuzi w’orurembo orwo, ow’okubanza omu nju y’okuroba enyanja emanyirwe munonga eya Enderby & Sons; enju ei omu kuteekateeka kwangye kw’omuvugi w’enyanja omworo, etari hare n’enju z’obugabe ezikwataniise eza Tudors na Bourbons, omu nshonga y’okukunda ebyafaayo buzima. Ni bwire ki, omwaka gwa Mukama waitu 1775 gutakahikire, enju egi mpango y’okuroberamu eby’enyanja ekaba eriho, ebihandiiko byangye bingi eby’eby’enyanja tibirikushoboorora gye; kwonka omu mwaka ogwo (1775) kikataho emeeri z’Abaingereza ezabandize kuhiiga Sperm Whale obutoosha; n’obu emyaka nka makumi n’ebiri enyima (kuruga 1726) aba Coffins na Maceys baitu ab’amaani aba Nantucket n’omusiri gw’emizaabibu baabaire bari omu meeri mpango barikukuratira Leviathan ogwo, kwonka omu nyanja ya Atlantic ey’omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho n’omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho bwonka: ti ahandi hoona. Kihandiikwe gye aha, ngu aba Nantucketers nibo baabandize omu bantu kuteera n’ekyoma ekitunguukire ekinyonyi kihango ekya Sperm Whale; kandi ngu kumara ekicweka ky’ekyasha nibo bonka ab’ensi yoona abaabaire bamuteera enkoni nk’ezo.

Omu mwaka gwa 1778, emeeri nungi, Amelia, ekateebwaho ahabw’ekigyendererwa ky’omutaano, kandi ahabw’okutegyekwa Enderbys yonka ey’amaani, ekeetooroora Cape Horn n’obumanzi, kandi niyo yaabandize omu mahanga kugarura ahansi emeeri y’omuringo gwona ey’enyanja omu nyanja mpango ey’omumashuma. Orugyendo orwo rukaba ruri orw’obukugu kandi orw’omugisha; kandi akagaruka omu mwanya gwe aine ekikwato kye ekikwatsire kiijwire amaizi g’omuhendo, eky’okureeberaho kya Amelia kikakuratirwa juba emeeri ezindi, ez’Orungyereza n’ez’Abamerika, kandi obwo omwanya muhango gw’enyanja ya Sperm Whale ogw’omu nyanja ya Pacific gukasheesheka. Kwonka batarikumarwa n’ekikorwa eki kirungi, enju etarikuruha ekagaruka yayetunguura: Samueli na Batabani be boona—ni bangahi, nyinabo wenka niwe arikumanya—kandi bari ahansi y’obureeberezi bwabo obw’ahonaaho, kandi ekicweka, ninteekateeka ngu, ahabw’okushashura kwabo, gavumenti ya Bungyereza ekagyemeserezibwa kwohereza emeeri y’orutaro eya Rattler omu rugyendo rw’okuroba enyanja rwa disco South rwa disco. Ekiragiirwe omukuru w’amahe g’omu nyanja Post-Captain, Rattler ekagikora orugyendo rw’okuteera akaari, kandi ekakora obuheereza obumwe; oku kiri kitarikureebeka. Kwonka eki ti kyonka. Omu mwaka gwa 1819, enju niyo emwe ekataho emeeri yaabo eya discovery whale, kuza omu rugyendo rw’okugyezesa eby’enyanja omu maizi ga Japan aga hare. Emeeri egyo—erikwetwa gye “Syren”—ekakora orugyendo rw’okugyezesa orw’ekitiinisa; kandi nikwo omwanya muhango gw’okuteereramu enyanja z’omu Japan gwabandize kumanywa omu bantu boona. Syren omu rugendo oru orumanyirwe munonga ekaba neeragirwa Kapiteeni Coffin, omunya Nantucket.

Ekitiinisa kyona ahari ba Enderbies, n’ahabw’ekyo, enju yaabo, ninteekateeka ngu eriho n’okuhitsya hati; n’obu hatariho kubangaanisa Samueli ow’okubanza aine kuba yaaherize kusheesheka ira akawaya ke kuza omu Nyanja mpango ey’Oburugwa-izooba ey’ensi endiijo.

Emeeri eyayetsirwe eiziina rye ekaba eshemerire kuheebwa ekitiinisa, ahabw’okuba ekaba neevuga juba munonga kandi eri ey’ekitiinisa omu miringo yoona. Nkamuvuga omurundi gumwe omu kasheeshe ahu ndi aha rubaju rw’enyanja ya Patagonia, kandi nanywa flip nungi ahansi omu forecastle. Ekaba eri gam nungi ei twabaire twine, kandi zoona zikaba ziri trumps—buri mwoyo ogwabaire guri omu meeri. Amagara makye aharibo, n’okufa kw’okushemererwa. Kandi egyo gam nungi ei naabaire nyine—obwire buraingwa, obwire buraingwa munonga bwanyima y’omugurusi Ahabu kukwata aha mbaaho ze n’ekigyere kye eky’amahembe g’entare—nikinyijutsya obugyenyi bw’ekitiinisa, obugumire, obw’Abasaxon obw’emeeri egyo; kandi omureeberezi wangye anyebwe, na sitaani anyijukye, ku ndaabe naabura okureeba. Okuhindura? Nkagira ngu twine flip? Eego, kandi tukaba tugihindura aha sipiidi ya galoni ikumi omu shaaha; kandi omuyaga ku gwaizire (ahabw’okuba niguba guri hare aho na Patagonia), kandi emikono yoona—abagyenyi na boona—bakeetwa kuza aha mutwe gw’enyanja, tukaba turi top-heavy munonga ngu tukaba twine kuzungurukana ahaiguru omu nshaho z’obuta; kandi tukaba tutamanya tukazingazinga ebijwaro byaitu omu nshaho z’emeeri, n’ahabw’ekyo tukaba tuhanikwa aho, turikucumita omuyaga gw’amaani, eky’okureeberaho ky’okurabura aha nshaho zoona ezaabaire zitamiire. Kwonka, emiti y’enyanja ekaba etarikurenga aha nyanja; kandi omu bwire bukye tukagurukaguruka, turi abacureezi munonga, ngu tukaba twine kugaruka kuraba aha flip, n’obu omunyugunyu gw’omu ishwa gwabaire nigusheesheka aha scuttle y’omu maisho, kureka gukaba gukyebeirwe munonga kandi gukaguteeka nk’oku ndikukunda.

Enyama y’ente ekaba eri nungi—egumire, kwonka erimu omubiri. Bagira ngu n’enyama y’ente; abandi, ngu n’enyama y’ente ey’omukishaka; kwonka tindikumanya, buzima, oku ekyo kyabaireho. Nabo bakaba baine ebihimba; obuhimba obukye, kwonka obuhango, oburi nk’ekibunu ekirikushushana, kandi obutarikubaasa kusheenyuka. Nkateekateeka ngu noobaasa kubihurira, kandi obizingire omunda yaawe bwanyima y’okubimira. Ku waakuba oinamire munonga omu maisho, okaba ota omu kabi okukuteera nk’emipiira ya billiard. Omugaati—kwonka ekyo kikaba kitarikubaasa kuhwerwa; okwihaho ekyo, kikaba kiri ekirikurwanisa obuhuta bw’omubiri; omubugufu, omugaati gukaba gurimu ebishare byonka ebisya ebi baabaire baine. Kwonka forecastle ekaba etari y’omushana munonga, kandi kikaba kyorobi munonga kuza omu rusonda orurimu omwirima waaba noogirya. Kwonka byona, okumutwara kuruga aha motoka kuza aha mutwe, orikuteekateeka aha bugabe bw’ebibokisi by’omuteeki, otwariiremu n’ebibokisi bye eby’ebipapura ebiine amagara; omu maisho n’enyima, ningira ngu Samuel Enderby ekaba eri emeeri y’okushemererwa; y’ebishare birungi n’ebintu bingi; flip nungi kandi ey’amaani; crack banywani boona, na kapita kuruga aha bigyere bya booti kuhika aha nkofiira-bandi.

Kwonka ahabw’enki, nimuteekateeka, ngu Samuel Enderby, n’abandi bavugi b’enyanja abamwe ab’Orungyereza abu ndikumanya—n’obu baraabe batari boona—bakaba bari emeeri ezimanyirwe munonga, ezirikutaayaayira abagyenyi; ekyo kikaba nikihinguraho okwehinguririza enyama y'ente, n'omugaati, n'ekikopo, n'okushemeza; kandi tibararuha juba kurya n'okunywa n'okusheka? Ndyakugambira. Okushemererwa okurungi okurikukirayo kw’abavugi b’enyanja aba ab’Orungyereza n’enshonga y’okucondooza aha byafaayo. Kandi tinkakyeireho na kakye okucondooza aha byafaayo by’enyanja, obwo kyabaire nikireebeka ngu nikyetengyesa.

Abangyereza bakabanza omu kushoha enyanja n’Abaholandi, Abazealand, na Abadani; ou baatungiremu ebigambo bingi ebikiriho omu by’obuvubi; kandi ekindi, emicwe yaabo eya ira ey’ebishaju, erikukwata aha by’okurya bingi n’okunywa. Ahabw’okuba, nk’ekintu ky’omutaano, emeeri y’abashuubuzi ey’Orungyereza neeshara abavugi baayo; kwonka tikwo kiri omunyabwengye w’Orungyereza. Nahabwekyo, omu Rungyereza, ekintu eki eky’okuteera enyanja nungi okushemererwa ti kya buriijo kandi eky’obuhangwa, kureka n’eky’omutaano kandi eky’omutaano; kandi, n’ahabw’ekyo, kiine kuba kiine obukomooko bw’omutaano, oburikworekwa aha, kandi nibwija kwongyerwa kushoboororwa gye.

Omu kucondooza kwangye omu byafaayo bya Leviathan, nkateera akagwe aha kitabo kya ira eky’Orudaaki, eki, ahabw’okunuuka kwakyo okw’omucuucu, naabaire nimmanya ngu kiine kuba nikikwata aha ba whale. Omutwe gukaba guri, “Dan Coopman,” n’ahabw’ekyo nkahendera ngu eki kiine kuba kiri ebiteekateeko by’omuhendo munonga eby’omuvugi w’emotoka y’omwika omwe omuri Amsterdam omu by’obuvubi, nk’oku buri meeri y’enyanja erikuba eine kwekorera omuvugi waayo. Nkagumibwa omu kiteekateeko eki ahabw’okureeba ngu n’okukora “Fitz Swackhammer” emwe. Kwonka munywani wangye Dr. Snodhead, omushaija omwegyesiibwe munonga, omushomesa w’Orudaaki rwa Low Dutch n’orwa High German omu yunivasite ya Santa Claus na St. “Omushuubuzi.” Omu bugufu, ekitabo eki ekya ira kandi eky’obwegyese ekya Low Dutch kikaba nikikwata aha by’obushuubuzi bya Holland; kandi, omu nshonga ezindi, hakaba harimu ebyafaayo ebirikushemeza munonga ebirikukwata aha kushoha kwayo okw’enyanja. Kandi omu shuura egi niyo, erimu omutwe, “Smeer,” nari “Fat,” ahu naashangire orunyiriri rureingwa orurimu ebicweka bingi eby’ebijwaro by’okubiikamu n’ebishengye by’okubiikamu eby’enyanja 180 eby’enyanja z’omu Budaaki; kuruga omu runyiriri orwo, nk’oku rwavunuurwa Dr. Snodhead, ninhandiika ebi:

Emeeza z’embariirira ezirikukira obwingi niziyoma omu kushoma; kwonka tikwo kiri omu nshonga eriho hati, ahu omushomi arikwijura emipiira yoona, ebidomora, ebikopo by’amaizi, n’ebihimba by’ebihimba birungi n’okushemererwa kurungi.

Omu bwire obwo, nkamara ebiro bishatu ndikwega n’okwega okunywa amaarwa aga goona, enyama y’ente, n’omugaati, omu bwire obwo ebiteekateeko bingi eby’omutaano bikampabura omu muringo gw’omutaano, ebirikubaasa kukoresibwa omu muringo gw’okukiraho kandi ogwa Plato; kandi, ekindi, nkateerana emeeza z’okwongyeraho ezangye, ezirikukwata aha bwingi oburikubaasa kuba buri obw’eby’enyanja ebirikwetwa stock-fish, n’ebindi, ebyabaire biriibwa buri muvugi w’enyanja owa Low Dutch omu kujuba okwo okwa ira okwa Greenland na Spitzbergen whale. Kandi eki nikisheesha ekikopo.





EISHOMO 102. Omunyabwengye omu Arsacides.

Okuhitsya hati, omu kushoboorora aha Sperm Whale, n’okukira munonga ntuura aha by’okutangaaza by’orubaju rwe orw’aheeru; nari omu bwijwire kandi omu bwijwire aha bintu bimwe bikye eby‘omunda eby‘omubumbi. Kwonka omu kwetegyereza okuhango kandi okuhikire okurikumusheenya, nikimpa hati kumushumurura n’okukiraho, n’okushumurura enshonga z’omuhogo gwe, okushumurura ebijwaro bye by’okujwara, n’okusheenya ebikwato n’amaisho g’enyungu z’amagufa ge ag’omunda, kumuta omu maisho gaawe omu ultimatum ye; nikwo kugira ngu omu magufa ge agataine bukwakurizo.

Kwonka shi hati, Ishimaeli? Nikibaho kita, iwe omuvugi w’enyanja kwonka omu by’obuvubi, oyeshushanirize ngu noomanya ekintu kyona aha bicweka by’enyanja ebiri ahansi y’itaka? Stubb omunyabwengye, owaabaire agyendeire aha capstan yaawe, akaha emishomo aha nkora y’omubiri gw’ebinyonyi ebirikwetwa Cetacea; kandi ahabw’obuhwezi bw’ekikwato ky’omuyaga, okukwaata orubaju rw’eky’okureeberaho kworeka? Yeshoborore, iwe Ishimaeli. Noobaasa kuteera ekinyonyi ekikuzire aha nkungu yaawe kukyebera, nk’oku omuteeki arikuteeka empunu eyokize? Buzima tikwo kiri. Iwe Ishimaeli, okuhitsya hati obaire ori omujurizi ow'amazima; kwonka yegyendesereze oku orikubaasa kukoresa omugisha gwa Yona wenka; omugisha gw’okugaaniira aha bikondo n’emiti; ebikondo, emiti y’omugoye, ebigwejegyeire, n’ebirikuteebwa ahansi, ebirikukora omutindo gwa leviathan; kandi nk'ebiyungu by'amajuta, ebishengye by'amate, amajuta, n'ebihimba omu mara ge.

Ninkihamya, ngu kuruga ahari Yona, abanyamaishwa bakye nibo bataahire hare munonga ahansi y’oruhu rw’enyanja mpango; kwonka nabwo, nkatunga omugisha gw’okumushara omu kacweka kakye. Omu bwato obu naabaire ndimu, akaana kakye aka Sperm Whale kakaba kateebwa aha mutwe gw’enyanja ahabw’okuteeraho nari enshaho ye, kukora ebikwato by’okutaho amabaare g’empi, n’emitwe y’amacumu. Noteekateeka ngu nkareka omugisha ogwo gugyende, ntakoreise enkoni yangye y’obwato n’omusyo gw’okuteera, kandi nkahenda ekipande kandi nkashoma byona ebiri omu mwana ogwo muto?

Kandi aha bikwatiraine n’okumanya kwangye okuhikire aha magufa g’entare omu kukura kwago okuhango, okuhikire, ahabw’okumanya okwo okutarikukira kureebwa nyine eibanja ahari munywani wangye w’obugabe owaafa Tranquo, omugabe wa Tranque, omwe aha ba Arsacides. Ahabw’okugira ngu nkaba ndi Tranque, emyaka ehingwireho, obu naabaire nkwataniise n’emeeri y’obushuubuzi eya Dey eya Algiers, nkeetwa kumara ekicweka ky’ebiro bikuru bya Arsacidean na mukama wa Tranque, omu nju ye ey’emizeituuni eyaabaire ahuumwire omuri Pupella; omugyera oguri aha rubaju rw’enyanja gutari hare munonga n’eki abavugi baitu baabaire nibeeta Bamboo-Town, orurembo rwe orukuru.

Omu mitwarize endiijo mingi mirungi, munywani wangye w’obugabe Tranquo, ahabw’okuheebwa ekiconco kya rukundo y’okwehayo aha nshonga zoona ez’obunyamahe, akaba areetsire hamwe omuri Pupella ebintu byona ebitarikukira kureebwa ebi abantu be abaine obwengye bwingi baabaire nibabaasa kuhanga; okukira munonga emiti eshazirwe ey’ebintu by’okutangaaza, ebigogo ebishazirwe, amacumu agateirwemu, ebikwato by’okuvuga eby’omuhendo, emeeri ezirimu omugaju; kandi ebi byona bikagabwa omu by’okutangaaza byona eby’obuhangwa, ebicu ebiine eby’okutangaaza, ebirikuha omushoro byabaire biteire aha nkungu ze.

Omukuru omuri ebi eby’aha muheru akaba ari ekinyonyi kihango ekya Sperm Whale, ekyabaire, bwanyima y’omuyaga gw’amaani ogutari gwa buriijo, kishangirwe kifiire kandi kikwatsire, omutwe gwakyo gukwatsire aha muti gwa kokoo, ogwabaire guri nk’amapapa, ogwabaire gurimu ebicucu, gukaba nigushusha nk’enyonyi ye ey’ebishaka. Omubiri muhango ku gwaherize kwihwaho ebizingo byagwo ebyabaire nibihika omunda, kandi amagufa gakahinduka omucuucu ogwomire omu izooba, obwo amagufa gakatwarwa n’obwegyendesereza kuza ahaiguru omu kiina kya Pupella, ahu hekalu mpango y’emiti y’emizeituuni hati yaabaire neegukingira.

Embaju zikaba zihanikwa n’ebikopo; vertebræ zikaba zihandiikirweho ebihandiiko by’ebyafaayo bya Arsacidean, omu bihandiiko by’orujungu ebitarikumanywa; omu kifuba, abanyamurwa bakaba baguma nibateera omuriro gw’omubazi ogutarikuzima, n’ahabw’ekyo omutwe gw’amazima gukaba gugaruka gwayohereza omucuucu gwagwo ogw’omuriro; obwo, erikuhanikwa aha itaagi, orubaju rw’ahansi orurikutiinisa rurikutengeeta aha baramya boona, nk’ekisyo ekyabaire kihanikire aha ishokye ekyatiinisa munonga Damocles.

Kikaba kiri ekintu ky’okutangaaza. Enku zikaba ziri eza kinyasi nk’ebishaka by’omu Icy Glen; emiti ekaba eyemereire ahaiguru kandi erikwetunguura, neehurira omubisi gwayo oguhuriire; ensi erikukora munonga ahansi ekaba eri nk’orukoba rw’omuruki, erimu kapeti nungi munonga, ei emiti y’omuzaabibu ey’ahansi yaabaire neekora orukoba n’orukoba, kandi ebimuri ebihuriire bikaba biri ebishushani. Emiti yoona, n'amataagi gaayo goona agaremeire; ebishaka byona, n'ebishaka, n'ebishaka; omuyaga ogurikutwara obutumwa; ebi byona bikaba bitarikuhwaho. Kurabira omu nkomo z’amababi, eizooba rikuru rikaba nirishusha nk’ekinyonyi ekirikuguruka kirikuruka ebishaka ebitarikuruha. Ruhanga-omuruki, niwe aruka; kandi ahabw'okuruka okwo kumureetera okuhurira, ngu atahurira eiraka ry'omuntu orikufa; kandi ahabw’okuhuuta okwo, naitwe, abarikureeba aha rubaaho, nituba tufiire amatu; kandi ku turaahungaho nitwija kuhurira amaraka rukumi agarikugamba kurabira omuriyo.
