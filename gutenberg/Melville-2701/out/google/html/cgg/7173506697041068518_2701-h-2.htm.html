EISHOMO 4. Ekipande ky’okubanza.

Ku naasisiimukire omu kasheeshe akaakuratsireho nk’omushana, nkashanga omukono gwa Queequeg gunshukire omu muringo ogurikukirayo rukundo n’okukundana. Okaba ori haihi kuteekateeka ngu nkaba ndi omukazi we. Ekipande ky’aha rubaju kikaba kiri eky’ebipande, kiijwire obucweka bukye obutarikushushana obw’erangi n’enkokora ishatu; kandi omukono gwe ogu gukaba guriho tattoo hoona n’ekishushani ky’omu Krete ekitarikuhwaho, hatariho bicweka bibiri ebyabaire biri eby’ekicuucu kimwe ekihikire—ahabw’okuteekateeka ngu akaguma aine omukono gwe omu nyanja omu muringo ogutari gw’obutoosha omu izooba n’omu kicuucu, emikono ye y’esaati ekaba ezingire omu bwire butari bumwe na bumwe—eki kikaba nikishushana n’omukono gwe gwona nk’ogwa, ekijwaro ky’okuteeraho. Buzima, ekicweka kigwejegyeire aharikyo nk’oku omukono gwabaire nigukora obu naabanza kusisimuka, nkaba ntarikubaasa kukimanya kuruga aha rubaaho, bakaba nibajwanzya erangi zaabo hamwe; kandi n’okuhurira oburemeezi n’okugyemeserezibwa kwonka nikyo naabaire nimbaasa kumanya ngu Queequeg akaba nannywegyera.

Ebi naabaire ninhurira bikaba biri eby’okutangaaza. Reka ngyezeho kubishoboorora. Obu naabaire ndi muto, ninyijuka gye embeera erikushushana n’egyo eyaabaireho; yaaba kyabaire kiri eky’amazima nari ekirooto, nkaba ntarikubaasa kukishoboorora kimwe. Embeera ekaba eri egi. Nkaba nsharaho ekikaari nari ekindi—ninteekateeka ngu kikaba nikigyezaho kuza ahaiguru y’omukyeeka, nk’oku naabaire nreebire akasheenya kakye karikukora ebiro bikye enyima; kandi maama wangye w’omukazi owaabaire, omu muringo gumwe nari ogundi, obwire bwona arikunkubita, nari arikunyohereza omu kitanda ntaine kyakiro,—maama akankurura amaguru kuruga omu nshaho y’omuriro kandi yanpakira kuza omu kitanda, n’obu eraabe yaabaire eri shaaha ibiri zonka z’omu mwabazyo gw’ebiro 21 by’okwezi kwa mukaaga, ekiro ekirikukirayo oburaingwa omu mwaka omu kicweka kyaitu ky’ensi. Nkahurira natiina munonga. Kwonka hakaba hatariho buhwezi bwakyo, n’ahabw’ekyo nkaza aha madaara nkaza omu kashengye kangye kakye aha gati y’ekishengye kya kashatu, nayejuura mpora mpora nk’oku kirikubaasika kugira ngu nyite obwire, kandi n’okuhuuta okubi nkaza ahagati y’ebipande.

Nkaba nshutami aho n’obusaasi ndikubarira ngu eshaaha ikumi na mukaaga zoona ziine kuhinguraho ntakatungire amatsiko g’okuzooka. Eshaaha ikumi na mukaaga omu kitanda! akakye k’omugongo gwangye kakashaasha kukiteekateekaho. Kandi kikaba kiri kikye munonga nakyo; eizooba nirijwa omu dirisa, n’okuteera akaari kwingi omu nguuto, n’okuhurira amaraka g’abashaija abarikuteerana omu nju yoona. Nkahurira naayongyera kuba kubi—aha muheru nkaimuka, naajwara, kandi naaza ahansi mpora mpora omu bigyere byangye ebiine amasitookisi, naasherura maama wangye w’omukazi, kandi ahonaaho naayeshuka aha bigyere bye, ndikumushaba nk’okusiimwa kw’omutaano ngu ampe okushereeka kurungi ahabw’emitwarize yangye mibi; ekintu kyona buzima kureka okuncwera orubanja rw’okugwejegyera obwire buraingwa nk’obwo obutarikugumisiriza. Kwonka akaba ari orikukirayo oburungi kandi orikufayo munonga ahari ba maama b’abazaire, kandi kugaruka nkaba nyine kuza omu kishengye kyangye. Kumara eshaaha nyingi nkagwejegyera aho ntaine ki ndikuhurira, ndikuhurira ndi kubi munonga kukira oku naabaire ninhurira kuruga obwo, nangwa n’okuruga omu bizibu ebikuru ebyakuratsireho. Aha muheru nyine kuba naagwa omu kirooto ky’okugwejegyera ekiine oburemeezi; kandi mpora mpora n’okukisisimuka—ekicweka kimwe kiijwire ebirooto—nkaigura amaisho gangye, kandi ekishengye ekyabaire kitarimu omushana gw’eizooba hati kikaba kizingirwe omwirima gw’aheeru. Ahonaaho nkahurira okushoberwa kurikwiruka omu fureemu yangye yoona; tihaine ekyabaire nikireebeka, n'obu kyakuba eky'okuhurirwa; kwonka omukono ogutari gw’obuhangwa gukaba nigushusha nk’oguteirwe omu gwangye. Omukono gwangye gukaba guhanikire aha rubaju rw’ekitanda, kandi ekishushani nari ekihangirwe ekitaine iziina, ekitarikuteekateekwaho, ekitarikugamba, eki omukono gwabaire guri, kikaba nikireebeka nk’ekishutami haihi aha rubaju rw’ekitanda kyangye. Ahabw’okuba ekyabaire nikireebeka nk’emyaka erikurundwa aha myaka, nkaba nshutami aho, nfukiire ahabw’okutiina okurikukirayo obubi, ntaine bumanzi bw’okukurura omukono gwangye; kwonka n’okuteekateeka ngu ku ndaabe naabaasa kugiteera inci emwe n’emwe, omubazi gw’akabi munonga gukaba nigwija kuhenda. Nkaba ntarikumanya oku okwetegyereza oku kwahererukire kunsheesheka; kwonka ku naabaire nyimuka omu kasheeshe, nkabijuka byona n’okutetema, kandi kumara ebiro n’esande n’ameezi bwanyima nkaba nfeerwa omu kugyezaho okurikuteganisa okushoboorora ekihama. Ngaaha, kuhitsya aha shaaha egi yonka, ninkira kwebuuzabuuza.

Hati, kwihaho obwooba oburikutiinisa, kandi ebiteekateeko byangye aha kuhurira omukono gw’omutaano omu gwangye bikaba nibishushana munonga, omu butamanya bwabyo, n’ebyo ebi naabaire ntunga aha kusisimuka n’okureeba omukono gwa Queequeg ogw’ekipagani gukanteera. Kwonka aha muheru ebintu byona ebyabaireho omu kiro ekyahingwire bikagaruka byagaruka omu muringo gw’obucureezi, kimwe aha kimwe, omu mazima agahamire, reero nkaba nshutami ndi ohuriire ahabw’oburemeezi oburikushemeza. Ahabw’okuba n’obu ndaabe naagyezaho kunyiganyiga omukono gwe—kwiguraho ekikwato kye ky’omugore—kwonka, agwejegyeire nk’oku yaabaire ari, akaguma annywegyera munonga, nk’oku hatariho ekindi kureka okufa ekirikubaasa kututaanisa babiri. Hati nkateeraho kumusisimura—“Queequeg!”—kwonka eky’okugarukamu kye kikaba kiri okuhuuta kwonka. Nkaheza nshutama, enshaya yangye eri nk’eri omu nkomo y’embaraasi; kandi ahonaaho akahurira aine akashara kakye. Okunaga aha rubaju rw’ekipande ky’okuteereraho, hakaba hariho tomahawk egwejegyeire aha rubaju rw’omunyamaishwa, nk’omwana w’amaisho g’enkoni. Pickle nungi, buzima, nkateekateeka; akagwejegyera hanu omu nju etari emwe n’emwe omu izooba rihango, aine omunyanyama n’enkongoro! “Omugabe!—omu iziina ry’oburungi, Omugabe, imuka!” Aha muheru, ahabw’okushoroora kwingi, n’okushoborora n’eiraka rihango kandi okutarikuhwaho aha kutahikiirira kwe okw’okunywegyera omushaija mugyenzi we omu muringo ogwo ogw’obushwere, nkabaasa kureetaho okugurukaguruka; kandi ahonaaho, akagarura enyima omukono gwe, yayezunguza hoona nk’embwa ya Newfoundland kuruga omu maizi gonka, kandi yaashutama omu kitanda, agumire nk’omuti gw’enyanja, arikundeeba, kandi arikusiiga amaisho ge nk’otarikwijuka kimwe oku naizire kuba ndiyo, n’obu ekintu kyabaire nikindeetera kumanya gye. Omu bwire obwo, nkaba nshutami n’amaisho n’obucureezi, ntaine kubanganisa kw’amaani hati, kandi nkaba nfukamiire kureeba ekihangirwe ekirikwenda kumanya munonga. Ku, aha muheru, ebiteekateeko bye byareebeka nk’ebikozirwe kukwata aha mitwarize y’ogwo ou yaabaire naagwejegyera nawe, kandi akahinduka, nk’oku kirikugambwa, kugarukana n’amazima; akaguruka yaaruga ahansi, kandi ahabw’obumanyiso n’amakuru agamwe akanha okwetegyereza ngu, kyaba nikishemeza, akaba naija kubanza yaajwara reero ansigaho kujwara bwanyima, omuzigo gwona nsigaho nyowe. Ninteekateeka nyowe, Queequeg, omu mbeera ezi, eki n’ekigambo ky’obuhangwa munonga; kwonka, amazima gari ngu, abanyamaishwa aba baine omutima gw’okuzaarwa ogw’okushemererwa, gamba eki orikwenda; nikitangaaza oku barikuba baine obucureezi omu bwijwire. Ninsiima Queequeg ahabw’okugira ngu akantwaza gye munonga kandi yanteekateekaho munonga, obwo nyowe nyine omushango gw’obushema bwingi; okumureeba ari aha kitanda, n’okureeba ebikorwa bye byona eby’omu kaabuyonjo; ahabw’obwire obwo okwetenga kwangye kuriyo nikutunguuka omu kuzaara kwangye. Nangwa n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, omushaija nka Queequeg torikureeba buri izooba, we n’emiringo ye bakaba baine omugasho gw’omutaano ogutari gwa buriijo.

Akatandika kujwara ahaiguru arikujwara enkofiira ye eya beaver, ndaingwa munonga, aha rubaju, reero—akyaine empare ze—akahiiga obutiiti bwe. Eki ahansi y’eiguru yaakikoreire, tindikubaasa kukimanya, kwonka eki yaakuratsireho kikaba kiri okwehendagura—obuti omu ngaro, n’enkofiira—ahansi y’ekitanda; obu, kuruga omu kuhuuta kw’amaani n’okuteganisibwa, nkateekateeka ngu akaba naakora munonga arikwetunguura; n’obu haraabe hatariho kiragiro kyona eky’oburingaaniza eki naahuriire, omushaija weena naashabwa kuba omu kihama yaaba naajwara obutiiti bwe. Kwonka Queequeg, nooreeba ngu akaba ari ekihangirwe omu bwire bw’okuhinduka—ti n’ekinyonyi nari ekinyonyi. Akaba aine obuhangwa oburikumara kworeka obutashushana bwe omu mitwarize etarikumanywa munonga. Obwegyese bwe bukaba butakahwaire. Akaba ari omwegi wa diguri. Kuri ataine diguri nkye y’obuhangwa, akaba nabaasa kuba atarikwerarikirira n’obuti na kakye; kwonka obwo, kuri atakabaire omunyamaishwa, akaba atarikubaasa kuroota kuza ahansi y’ekitanda kubijwara. Aha muheru, akarugayo aine enkofiira ye erimu ebicupuri bingi kandi erimu ebicupuri aha maisho ge, kandi yaatandika kucucuuka n’okugurukaguruka omu kishengye, nk’oku, atamanyiriire munonga obutiiti, obutiiti bwe oburimu amaizi, oburimu ebihimba by’ente—obundi obutakozirwe kugyendera aha kuragiira nabwo—okukira bukamunyiganyiga kandi bukamubonabonesa omu bwire bw’okubanza.

Okureeba, hati, ngu tihaine mikingo y’okuhika aha dirisa, kandi ngu omuhanda gukaba guri mukye munonga, enju eyaabaire eri aha rubaju orundi ekaragiira okureeba gye omu kishengye, kandi n’okureeba munonga ekishushani ekitaine kitiinisa eki Queequeg yaakozire, arikuteera akaari aine ekindi kikye kureka enkofiira ye n’ebijwaro bye; Nkamushaba nk’oku naabaire nimbaasa, ngu ayongyereho omu kaabuyonjo ke kakye, kandi okukira munonga ngu ataahe omu pantalo ze ahonaaho nk’oku kirikubaasika. Akaikiriza, reero yaagumizamu yaayonaaba. Omu bwire obwo omu kasheeshe Omukristaayo weena akaba anaaba omu maisho; kwonka Queequeg, ekyantangaire, akamarwa n’okuzibira okunaaba kwe omu kifuba, omu mikono n’omu ngaro. Bwanyima akajwara ekijwaro kye ky’omu kifuba, kandi yaakwata ekipande kya sabuuni egumire aha meeza y’ahagati y’ekitanda ky’okunaabiramu, yaakiteeka omu maizi yaatandika kusheeshasheesha omu maisho ge. Nkaba ninreeba kureeba ahu yaabiikire enkoni ye, obu lo and behold, aihaho enkoni kuruga omu rusonda rw’ekitanda, asheesheka omu rubaaho oruraingwa, asheeshuura omutwe, agusharaho kakye aha buti bwe, kandi agyendera aha kacweka k’endabirwamu aha kisiika, atandika kushara n’amaani, nari shi okushara ekibunu kye. Teekateeka nyowe, Queequeg, eki n’okukozesa ebikopo bya Rogers ebirungi munonga n’okuhoorera enzigu. Bwanyima nkatangaara okukye aha murimo ogu ku naamanyire ekyoma ki ekirungi eki omutwe gw’enkoni gurikukora, n’oku emikono miraingwa egoroire erikuguma eine obushaarizi bwingi munonga obwire bwona.

Ekitanda kye ekisigaireho kikahikiirizibwa juba, kandi akaruga omu kishengye n’okwehimbisa, azingire omu jaketi ye mpango ey’enkima y’omuvugi, kandi arikuzaana omuhoro gwe nk’omuhoro gw’omukuru w’amahe.





EISHOMO 5. Eky’omu kasheeshe.

Nkakuratira ahonaaho, kandi kushuuma omu kishengye ky’okunywamu nkashemeza mukama w’enju owaabaire namwenya munonga. Tihariho n’obushaarizi obu naabaire nyine ahariwe, n’obu araabe yaabaire atwire nanyeshunga omu mwanya ti kakye omu nshonga y’ogwo ou naabaire ninshutama nawe omu kitanda.

Kwonka, okusheka kurungi n’ekintu kirungi ky’amaani, kandi okukiraho n’ekintu kirungi ekirikubura munonga; oku kirikweyongyera n'eky'obusaasi. Nahabwekyo, omushaija weena, omu muntu we wenka, ku araabe naabaasa ebintu by’okushemeza omuntu weena, arekye kugaruka enyima, kureka aikirize n’okushemererwa kukozesa n’okukoresibwa omu muringo ogwo. Kandi omushaija oine ekintu kyona ekirikushekyerera munonga ahariwe, reeba ngu hariho bingi omu mushaija ogwo okukira oku orikuteekateeka.

Ekishengye ky'okunywamu hati kikaba kiijwire aba boarders ababaire nibagwa omu kiro ekyabandize, kandi abu naabaire ntakareebaga gye. Haihi boona bakaba bari abavugi b’enyanja; abakuru b'abavugi b'emotoka z'emigugu, n'abavugi b'emotoka z'emigugu abakabiri, n'abavugi b'emotoka z'emigugu abakashatu, n'ababeizi b'omu nyanja, n'abaheisi b'omu nyanja, n'abaheisi b'omu nyanja, n'abarinzi b'emeeri; ekigombe kya brown n’eky’omubiri, ekiine ebireju bya bosky; seti etaine nshonga, erimu ebishaju, boona bajwaire ebijwaro by’enkima eby’okujwara omu kasheeshe.

Okaba noobaasa kumanya gye obwire obu buri omwe yaabaire amazire aha nkungu. Etama ry’omutsigazi ogu eririmu amagara marungi nirishushana n’erya pear eyokize omu izooba omu rangi, kandi niribaasa kureebeka nk’eririkunuuka haihi nk’omushenyi; tarikubaasa kuba yaamazire ebiro bishatu aha itaka kuruga omu rugyendo rwe orw’Abahinda. Ogwo mushaija orikumukurataho nareebeka nk’oine ebicucu bikye; noobaasa kugira ngu okukwata aha mbaaho ya satin kuri omuriwe. Omu rangi y’omuntu wa kashatu hakiriho erangi ya tropic tawn, kwonka erimu erangi erikutukura kakye; hatariho kubangaanisa amazire wiiki zoona aha nkungu. Kwonka shi n’oha owaabaire naabaasa kworeka eitama nka Queequeg? eki, kiine erangi zitari zimwe na zimwe, kikaba nikireebeka nk’omugyera Andes ogw’omu burengyerwa izooba, kworeka omu nkora emwe, ebirikutaanisa embeera z’obwire, zooni aha zooni.

"Ekyokurya, ho!" hati mukama wa nju akarira, yaateera orwigi, kandi omunda twaza kurya kyamushana.

Nibagira ngu abantu abareebire ensi, obwo nibaba baine obusingye bwingi omu mitwarize, nibeekwataho munonga omu bantu. Kwonka ti buriijo: Ledyard, omugyenyi mukuru wa New England, na Mungo Park, owa Scotland; omu bantu boona, bakaba baine obuhame bukye omu kishengye ky’okuraaramu. Kwonka obundi okuraba Siberia kwonka omu motoka y’okuvuga embwa nk’oku Ledyard yaakozire, nari okugyendera orugyendo ruraingwa rw’okwehereera aha nda erimu busha, omu mutima gw’abanyamahanga omuri Afirika, ogwabaire guri omubaro gw’eby’okureeberaho bibi bya Mungo—omuringo gw’okugyenderamu ogu, ningira ngu, nigubaasa kutaba omuringo gw’ahaiguru munonga ogw’okutunga polish y’abantu. Kwonka nabwo, obwire obwingi, ekintu ky’omuringo ogwo kiine kuba kiri hoona.

Ebiteekateeko ebi aha byonka nibireetwa embeera ngu bwanyima y’okushutama twena aha meeza, kandi nkaba nintebeekanisa kuhurira ebitebyo birungi ebirikukwata aha kuhiiga enyanja; ekikantangaaza munonga, haihi buri mushaija akaguma ahunami munonga. Kandi tikyo kyonka, kureka bakaba nibareebeka nk’abashobeirwe. Eego, hanu hakaba hariho ekibinja ky’embwa z’omu nyanja, nyingi aharizo zitaine nshoni n’emwe zikaba zitembire aha nyanja mpango ezirikwetwa whales—eziabaire zitari zaabo zoona—kandi zikabateerana zifiire zitarikuzireeba; kandi nabwo, hanu bakashutama aha meeza y’okuriiraho omu kasheeshe—boona ababaire nibeetwa kimwe, boona ababaire nibakunda eby’obuzaare—barikureebana okwehinguririza nk’entaama nk’oku batakarugaho omu maisho g’entaama ezimwe ahagati y’ensozi za Green Mountains. Okureeba okurikwenda kumanya; ebi bidubu ebiine enshoni, ebi biine obwoba by’okurwanisa eby’enyanja!

Kwonka ahari Queequeg—ahabwenki, Queequeg akashutama aho omuribo—aha mutwe gw’emeeza, nabwo, kikabaho omu muringo gw’omugisha; nk’oku kirikufuka nk’orubaare. Okuhamya tindikubaasa kugamba bingi aha kuzaara kwe. Omukundwa we omukuru akaba atarikubaasa kuhakanisa n’omutima gumwe okureeta orukoba rwe omu kyamushana nawe, kandi akarukoresayo hatariho mukoro; okuhika ahaiguru y’emeeza nayo, kuhika aha kabi akaabaire kari haihi kuhika aha mitwe mingi, n’okukwaata enyama z’ente zirikumuhikaho. Kwonka ekyo buzima kikaba kikozirwe we omu muringo gw’obucureezi bwingi, kandi buri omwe naamanya ngu omu kuteekateeka kw’abantu abaingi, okukora ekintu kyona omu muringo gw’obucureezi n’okukikora omu muringo gw’obucureezi.

Titurikwija kugamba aha bintu byona eby’omutaano ebya Queequeg aha; oku yaabaire ayetantara kaawa n’ebihimba ebirikwotsya, kandi akateeka omutima gwe ogutaine rubaju aha nyama z’ente, ezirikukorwa omu bwire bukye. Ekirikumara, ngu kyamushana ku kyahwaire akarugaho nk’abandi yaaza omu kishengye ky’abantu boona, yaata omushana gwe ogwa tomahawk-pipe, kandi akaba ashutami aho ahunamu arikugaaya kandi arikunywa etaabe aine enkofiira ye etarikutaanisa, obu naaza aheeru n’okugyendera omu ruguuto.





EISHOMO 6. Omuhanda.

Kuri noogira ngu nkatangaara omu kubanza kureeba omuntu otari w’omutaano nka Queequeg arikuzengyerera omu bantu b’obucureezi ab’omu tauni etunguukire, okutangaara okwo kukarugaho juba obu naabanza kugyendera omu mushana gw’omushana omu nguuto za New Bedford.

Omu nguuto empango eziri haihi n’emyanya y’okushuutamamu, omwaaro gwona ogw’enyanja nigwija kuba nigukira kuha okureeba ebishushani ebitarikushushana kuruga omu bicweka by’aheeru. Nangwa n’omu nguuto za Broadway na Chestnut, abavugi b’enyanja obumwe nibaija kuteera abakazi abaine obwoba. Omuhanda gwa Regent tigutarikumanywa aba Lascars na Malaya; kandi omuri Bombay, omu kishaka kya Apollo, aba Yankee abahuriire nibakira kutiinisa abatuuragye. Kwonka New Bedford neesinga omuhanda gwona ogw’amaizi n’ogwa Wapping. Omu myanya egi erikuhererukayo kugambaho ​​nooreeba abavugi b’enyanja bonka; kwonka omuri New Bedford, abanyamaishwa buzima nibeemerera barikugaaniira aha nshonda z’enguuto; abanyamaishwa abahikire; baingi aharibo nibakitwara amagufa gaabo enyama etarikwera. Nikireetera omunyamahanga kureeba.

Kwonka, okwihaho Abafeegee, Abatongatobooar, Abaerromanggo, Abapannangi, na Brighggian, kandi, oyihireho ebishushani by’omu ishwa eby’enyanja ebyabaire nibigyendera omu nguuto zitarikufayo, noija kureeba ebindi ebirikushemeza munonga. Aho buri saabiiti nihahika omu tauni egi abashaija baingi munonga aba Vermont na New Hampshire abarikwiragura, boona baine eiriho ry’okutunga n’ekitiinisa omu by’obuvubi. Nizikira kuba ziri nto, z’omubiri gw’amaani; bagyenzi baabo abatemire ebibira, hati nibasherura okuteera embanda n’okunyaga eicumu ry’enyanja. Bingi biri nk’ebishaka nk’ensozi za Green ahu baarugire. Omu bintu ebimwe noija kubiteekateekaho kwonka biine eshaaha nkye. Reeba aho! chap egyo erikwetoorora enshonda. Ajwaire enkofiira y’enkongoro n’ekooti y’omukira gw’enzigye, ajwaire omusipi gw’omuvugi w’enyanja n’omusyo gw’omu kifuba.
