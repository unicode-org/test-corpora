Enshonga erikureebeka ahabwenki Ahabu atagiire omu meeri y’enyanja ei twabaire tugambireho n’egi: omuyaga n’enyanja bikaba nibimanyisa ebihunga. Kwonka n’obu eki kyakubaire kitari kityo, bwanyima y’ebyo byona, obundi, akaba atarikwija kumutemba—okurugiirira aha mitwarize ye eyakuratsireho omu bwire nk’obwo—ku kiraabe kyabaire kityo, ahabw’omuringo gw’okuhimbisa, akaba atungire eky’okugarukamu kibi aha kibuuzo eki yaabuuza. Ahabw’okuba, nk’oku kyahikire, akaba nafayo obutakwatanisa, nangwa n’okumara edakiika itaano, na kapiteeni weena ou yaabaire atamanyire, okwihaho akabaasa kuhayo bimwe aha makuru ago agu yaabaire naasherura munonga. Kwonka ebi byona nibibaasa kuguma bitarikuteberezibwa gye, ku kiraabe kitari kintu ekigambirwe aha aha nkozesa y’omutaano ey’emeeri z’okuroba enyanja z’omu nyanja ku zirikubugana omu nyanja z’aheeru, kandi na munonga aha mwanya gw’okugyenderamu ogwa buriijo.

Ku baraabe abanyamahanga babiri barikuraba omu kihanga kya Pine Barrens omu ihanga rya New York, nari omu kihanga kya Salisbury omuri Bungyereza ekirimu amatongo nk’ago; ku barikubugana omu ihamba nk’eryo eritarikushemeza, aba bombi, ahabw’amagara gaabo, tibarikubaasa kwetantara gye okuramutsyana; n’okwemerera akaanya kakye kugaaniira aha makuru; kandi, obundi, okushutama kumara akaire kakye n’okuhuumura omu kuteerana: obwo, ka n’eky’obuhangwa ngu aha mutwe gw’enyanja ogutaine bugarukiro ogwa Pine Barrens na Salisbury Plains, emeeri ibiri z’okuroba enyanja zirikujumagana aha nshonda z’ensi—aha rubaju rw’ekizinga kya Fanning kyonka, nari King’s Mills eziri hare; ka n’eky’obuhangwa, ningira ngu omu mbeera nk’ezo emeeri ezi tiziine kuhindurana orubaare rwonka, kureka ziine kwija omu nkoragana erikukirayo haihi, ey’omukago n’ey’okukwatanisa. Kandi okukira munonga eki nikibaasa kureebeka nk’ekintu ky’amazima, omu nshonga y’emeeri eziine aha mwaaro gumwe, kandi ezi abakuru baazo, abakuru baazo, n’abashaija abatari bakye barikumanyana ahabwabo; kandi ekirugiremu, baine ebintu by‘omu ka ebikundwa eby‘emiringo yoona eby‘okugambaho.

Aha bwato obumazire obwire buraingwa butariho, obundi obw’aheeru, obundi, bwine amabaruha aha bwato; omu muringo gwona, naija kuhamya ngu yaamureka aine empapura ezimwe ez’ekiro omwaka gumwe nari ebiri bwanyima y’eyahererukireyo aha fairo ze ezitarikureebwa gye kandi ezirimu ebikumu. Kandi omu kugarukamu obucureezi obwo, emeeri eyabaire neeza aheeru ekaba neija kutunga obwengye obusya obw’okuroba enyanja kuruga omu mwanya ogu yaabaire naabaasa kuzamu, ekintu ekyabaire kiri kikuru munonga ahariwe. Kandi omu diguri, ebi byona nibiija kuba eby’amazima aha bikwatiraine n’emeeri z’okuroba enyanja ezirikuraba omu muhanda gwazo aha mwanya gw’okugyenderamu gwonka, n’obu baraabe nibamara obwire buraingwa barikwingana batari omuka. Ahabw’okuba omwe aharibo naabaasa kuba yaatungire okutwarwa kw’amabaruha kuruga aha meeri emwe eya kashatu, kandi hati eri hare munonga; kandi emwe aha mabaruha ago neebaasa kuba eri ey’abantu b’omu bwato obu arikubugana hati. Oyihireho ekyo, bakaba bagaaniira aha makuru g’okuroba enyanja, kandi bagaaniira gye. Kandi n’entaaniso y’eihanga ekaba etarikubaasa kureetaho entaaniso yoona ey’omugasho munonga; nikwo kugira ngu, kasita enjuyi zombi zirikugamba orurimi rumwe, nk’oku kiri aha Bamerika n’Orungyereza. Aha bya Men-of-War, ku barikutunga omugisha gw’okubugana omu nyanja, nibabanza baraba omu murongooti gw’okufukama n’okusharaho eby’obushema, nk’okuteera ebendera, ngu nikireebeka ngu tihariho n’okwenda kurungi kw’omutima okuhikire n’okukunda kw’ab’eishe-emwe okurikukwata ahari ekyo na kakye. Nk’okukwata aha ruteerane rw’emeeri z’abahuuku, ahabw’enki, bari omu bwangu bw’amaani, nibahungagana ahonaaho nk’oku kirikubaasika. Ahabw’okuba, omu nshonga y’abanyabuzaare b’aha nyanja, tugire ngu, nkaba nyine kukunda kumanya yaaba omurimo ogwo ogwabo gwine ekitiinisa ky’omutaano aharigwo. GAM. EIZIINA— Orukiiko rw’abantu rw’eby’enyanja bibiri ( nari ebirikukiraho ) eby’enyanja, okutwarira hamwe aha mwanya gw’okugyenderamu; obu, bwanyima y’okuhindurana orubare, barikuhindurana okutaayaayira abavugi b’amaato: abakuru babiri abasigaire, kumara obwire, aha bwato bumwe, kandi abakuru babiri aha bwato obundi.

Hariho ekindi kintu kikye ekirikukwata ahari Gamming eki tutaine kwebwa aha. Emirimo yoona eine obucweka bwayo obukye obw’omutaano omu bwijwire; nikwo n’okujuba eby’enyanja (whale) kuri. Omu bwato bw’abanyabuzaare, obw’omushaija w’orutaro, nari obw’abahuuku, omuvugi w’obwato ku arikuvugwa hoona omu bwato bwe, butoosha naashutama omu bipande by’enyima aha ntebe nungi, obumwe erimu obucupa aho, kandi obwire obwingi naaba ayevuga n’akacupa kakye karungi k’omuvugi w’obwato akashemeziibwe emirunga y’abashaija n’abakazi n’ebipande. Kwonka obwato bw’enyanja tiburiho ntebe aha rubaju rw’enyima, tiburiho sofa y’omuringo ogwo gwona, kandi tiburiho n’ekikoreso kyona. Obwire bw’ahaiguru buzima, abakuru b’enyanja abaabaire nibavuga enziga aha maizi aha bimotoka nk’abakuru b’amateeka abaine obuhuta bw’omubiri omu ntebe z’obugabe. Kandi aha bikwatiraine n’omuhingi, obwato bw’enyanja tiburikwikiriza obukazi bwona nk’obwo; kandi n’ahabw’ekyo nk’oku kiri omu kuzaana emizaano abavugi b’obwato boona baine kuruga omu bwato, kandi n’ahabw’ekyo nk’oku omuvugi w’obwato nari omuvugi w’enkoni ari omwe aha mubaro, ogwo ori ahansi y’obwato niwe ari omuvugi aha mukoro ogwo, kandi omuvugi w’obwato, ahabw’okubura ahu arikushutama, naakururwa kuza omu kutaayaayira kwe boona beemereire nk’omuti gw’omupaini. Kandi obwire obwingi noija kureeba ngu ahabw’okumanya amaisho g’ensi yoona erikureebwa garikumuhuumuriraho kuruga aha mbaju z’emeeri ibiri, omuvugi ogu oyemereire weena ahuriire aha bukuru bw’okurinda ekitiinisa kye arikurinda amaguru ge. Kandi egi ti nshonga yoona eyorobi munonga; ahakuba enyima ye hariho ekigyere kihango munonga ekirikurugayo ekirikumuteera hati n’omu kakye k’omugongo gwe, ekigyere ky’enyima nikigarukamu kirikuteera amaguru ge omu maisho. N’ahabw’ekyo naaba ashwekirwe kimwe omu maisho n’enyima, kandi naabaasa kwegaita aha rubaju rwonka arikushutama aha bigyere bye ebigoroirwe; kwonka okuteera kw’obwato okwa ahonaaho, okw’obushaarizi nikukira kuza hare kumuteera, ahabw’okuba oburaingwa bw’omusingi ti kintu kyona hatariho obugazi oburikuhikaana. Kora enkokora y’okujanjaaza emiti ebiri yonka, kandi torikubaasa kugyemerera. Reero, kandi, tikikaabaireho omu maisho g’ensi agarimu ebihimba, tikikaabaireho, ningira, ngu kapiteeni ogu orikugurukaguruka areebekye arikwehamya akacweka kakye munonga arikukwaata ekintu kyona n’emikono ye; buzima, nk’akamanyiso k’okwehabura kwe kwona, okurikugurukaguruka, okutwarira hamwe naatwara emikono ye omu nsaho z’empare ze; kwonka obundi ahabw’okugira ngu okutwarira hamwe n’emikono mikuru munonga kandi erikuremera, naagitwarayo ahabw’okugiteeraho. Nangwa n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo hakabaho ebyabaireho, ebihamibwe gye nabyo, ahu omuvugi w’enyanja abaire naamanywa ahabw’akaanya kakye akatari ka buriijo akagumire nari kabiri, omu bwire bw’omuyaga gw’ahonaaho akagira ngu—okukwata eishokye ry’omuvugi w’enyanja ori haihi, kandi akagumaho nk’okufa okurikutiinisa.

“‘Nahabwekyo munyambe Iguru, kandi aha kitiinisa kyangye ekigano eki mbagambiire, bakuru, kiri omu nshonga n’ebintu byakyo ebikuru, kihikire. Nimmanya ngu n’amazima; kikabaho aha mupiira ogu; nkateera emeeri; nkamanya abavugi; nkareeba kandi naagamba na Steelkilt kuruga obu Radney yaafa.’”
