EISHOMO 22. Noiri nungi.

Aha muheru, kuhika omu ihangwe, aha kubinga kw’aha muheru kw’abavugi b’emeeri, kandi bwanyima ya Pequod kukururwa kuruga aha mwaro, kandi bwanyima ya Charity owaabaire naateekateeka obwire bwona kuruga omu bwato bw’enyanja, aine ekiconco kye eky’aha muheru—enkofiira y’ekiro ahabwa Stubb, omukundwa we owa kabiri, omuramu we ogu ow’aha rubaju—Baibuli boona ababiri, Abakuru b'amahe, Pelegi na Biludaadi, bakaruga omu kaju, kandi baahindukira omukuru w'abavugi, Pelegi yaagira ati:

"Hati, Mukama Starbuck, nohamya ngu buri kimwe kihikire? Kapiteeni Ahabu byona byeteekateekire—akagamba nawe kwonka—tihariho ekindi ekirikubaasa kutunga kuruga aha rubaju rw'enyanja, eh? Haza, yeta emikono yoona, obwo. Muster 'em aft here—blast 'em!"

Bildad akagira ati: “Tihariho kwetenga ebigambo by’obushema, n’obu waakuba noorahuka munonga, Peleg, kwonka rugaho, munywani wangye Starbuck, okore eki turikuragiira.”

Ka hati! Aha aha nshonga yonka ey’okutandikiraho orugyendo, Kapiteeni Pelegi na Kapiteeni Bildad bakaba nibarugyenda n’omukono muhango aha rubaju rwa kana, nk’oku baabaire nibaija kuba abakuru b’amahe g’aha rubaju omu nyanja, hamwe n’okureebeka kwona aha mwaaro. Kandi ahari Kapiteeni Ahabu, tihaine kamanyiso ke akaabaire katakareebekire; kwonka, bakagira ngu akaba ari omu kabini. Kwonka obwo, ekiteekateeko kikaba kiri, ngu okubaho kwe kukaba kutarikwetengyesa na kakye omu kureeta emeeri ahansi y’oburemeezi, n’okugihabura gye kuruga omu nyanja. Buzima, nk’oku ekyo kyabaire kitari kintu kye ekihikire na kakye, kureka eky’omuvugi w’enyonyi; kandi nk’oku yaabaire atakakizire kimwe—nikwo baagambire—n’ahabw’ekyo, Kapiteeni Ahabu akaguma ahansi. Kandi ebi byona bikaba nibireebeka nk’eby’obuhangwa ebirikumara; na munonga nk’oku kiri omu buheereza bw’abashuubuzi abakuru b’emeeri baingi tibarikweyoreka aha mutwe gw’emeeri kumara obwire buraingwa bwanyima y’okuhanika enkongi, kureka nibaguma ahaiguru y’emeeza y’omu kaju, baine okushemererwa kw’okusibura na banywani baabo ab’aha rubaju rw’enyanja, batakarugire omu meeri kumara obwire buraingwa n’omuvugi w’emeeri.

Kwonka hakaba hatariho mugisha muhango gw’okuteekateeka aha nshonga egyo, ahabw’okuba hati Kapiteeni Pelegi akaba ariho weena. Akaba naashusha ngu niwe yaabaire naakora bingi omu kugamba n’okuragiira, beitu ti Biludaadi.

Akagamba n’eiraka rihango ati: “Murekye hanu, imwe batabani b’abashaija abatakashweire,” obwo abavugi b’emeeri barikuguma aha mutwe gw’emeeri omukuru. "Omushaija Starbuck, bavugire enyima."

“Mutere eihema aho!”—nikwo kiragiro ekyakuratsireho. Nk’oku naagambireho enyima, ekishengye eki eky’amagufa g’enyanja tikikateirwehoga kureka aha mwaaro; kandi aha bwato bwa Pequod, kumara emyaka makumi ashatu, ekiragiro ky’okuteera eihema kikaba nikimanywa gye nk’ekintu ekyakuratsireho okuhanika enkongi.

“Omushaija omukuru w’emeeri! Eshagama n’enkuba!—guruka!”—nikyo kiragiro ekyakuratsireho, kandi abavugi b’emeeri bakaguruka basherura ebikwato by’emikono.

Hati omu kutunga ahansi y’oburemeezi, ekitebe eki omuvugi w’enyonyi arikutuuramu okutwarira hamwe n’ekicweka ky’omu maisho g’emeeri. Kandi hanu Bildad, owaabaire, na Peleg, kimanyirwe, okwihaho abakuru be abandi, akaba ari omwe aha bavugi b’enyonyi abaine orusa rw’okuvuga aha mwaaro—arikuteekateekwaho kuba yaayetungire omuvugi w’enyonyi kubaasa kucungura esente z’okuvuga enyonyi eza Nantucket aha meeri zoona ezi yaabaire naafayoho, ahabw’okuba akaba atarikuvugaho enyonyi endiijo yoona ei yaabaire naareeba, obuta ahabw’enkongoro eyaabaire neehika haihi, kandi omu bwire obumwe bakaba barikweshongora ekyabaire nikireebeka nk’omuti gw’okushoberwa ogwa zaaburi, kushemeza emikono aha muyaga, owaabaire naayomba omu muringo gw’okuteera akaari aha baishiki abaabaire bari omu Booble Alley, n’okwenda kurungi kuruga omu mutima. Kwonka nabwo, ti biro bishatu enyima, Biludadi akaba abagambiire ngu tihariho byeshongoro by’obushema ebirikwija kwikirizibwa aha bwato bwa Pequod, namunonga omu kuza ahansi y’oburemeezi; kandi Charity, munyaanya, akaba ataireho kopi nkye y’okutooranamu eya Watts omu kishengye kya buri muvugi w’enyanja.

Omu bwire obwo, arikureeberera ekicweka ekindi eky’emeeri, Kapiteeni Pelegi akasheenya kandi yarahira aha rubaju rw’enyima omu muringo ogurikutiinisa munonga. Nkaba ndi haihi kuteekateeka ngu naija kubiika emeeri ekananga etakatandikire kwimuka; omu ntandikwa nkahuumura aha nshonda zangye z’omu ngaro, kandi naagambira Queequeg ngu nawe akore nikyo kimwe, ndikuteekateeka aha kabi aku twena twabaire twiruka, omu kutandika orugyendo rw’enyanja na sitaani nk’ogwo ahabw’omuvugi w’enyonyi. Kwonka nkaba ninyehuumuriza n’ekiteekateeko ngu omuri Biludadi orikutiina Ruhanga hakaba nihabaasa kushangwamu okujunwa okumwe, n’obu araabe yaabaire ari omu kugwejegyera kwe okwa magana mushanju na makumi mushanju na mushanju; ku naahuriire okuteera kw’amaani okwa ahonaaho omu mugongo gwangye, kandi nkahinduka, nkakwatwa obwoba ahabw’okureebeka kwa Kapiteeni Peleg omu kikorwa ky’okuiha okuguru kwe omu mwanya gwangye ogwa haihi. Ogwo gukaba guri ekikopo kyangye ky’okubanza.

“Ogwo nigwo muringo ogu barikuhanikamu omu buheereza bw’abashuubuzi?” akaborooga. "Entuura, iwe omutwe gw'entaama; entuura, kandi ohende omugongo gwawe! Ahabw'enki otarikutuura, ningira, mwena—enjura! Quohog! entuura, iwe chap n'ebireju ebirikutukura; entuura aho, Scotch-cap; entuura, iwe empare ya kinyasi. Entuura, ningira, mwena, kandi otunguure amaisho gaanyu!" Kandi arikugira atyo, akagyendera aha rubaju rw’omuyaga, aha n’aha arikukoresa okuguru kwe omu bwengye bwingi, obwo Biludaadi otarikuteganisibwa arikuguma naayebembera n’ekyeshongoro kye kya zaaburi. Ninteekateeka ngu, Kapiteeni Peleg aine kuba yaabaire aine eki arikunywa erizooba.

Aha muheru enkongi ekaba eri ahaiguru, amaato gakateebwaho, kandi twarugayo twagyendera aha maizi. Ekaba eri Noiri ngufu kandi erimu obufuki; kandi ekiro kikye eky’omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho ku kyabaire nikijwanzya ekiro, tukaba tweshanga turi haihi kusheesheka aha nyanja y’obutiti, ei okufuuhirira kwayo okwabaire nikutufuuhirira omu barafu, nk’omu by’okurwanisa ebishemeziibwe. Emirongooti miraingwa y’ameino aha bigo ekaba neeshemeza omu mushana gw’okwezi; kandi nk’amaino g’enjojo emwe mpango agarikwera ag’amahembe g’entare, ebicu bihango ebirikugoma bikaba birugiirira aha buta.

Lank Bildad, nk’omuvugi w’enyonyi, akeebembera eshaaha y’okubanza, kandi ebiro byona, nk’oku emeeri eya ira yaabaire neeza omu nyanja z’ebishaka, kandi ekayohereza obufuki oburikutetema ahariyo yoona, kandi omuyaga gukaba niguteera akaari, kandi emirunga ekaba neeteera, ebigambo bye ebigumire bikahurirwa,—

      “Emisiri erikushemeza eri aheeru y’omwegyemure ogurikwezimba, .
Yemerera ojwaire ebijwaro bya kinyasi ebiriho.
Nahabwekyo aha Bayudaaya Kanaani eya ira ekaba eyemereire;
Obu Yorudani arikwehindura ahagati.”

Tihariho ebigambo ebyo ebirungi ebyabaire nibihurirwa gye ahariinye nk’obwire obwo. Bakaba baijwire amatsiko n’ebirungi. N’obu ekiro eki eky’obutiti kyabaire kirimu obufuki omu nyanja ya Atlantic erimu oruyombo, n’obu ebigyere byangye byabaire birimu amaizi n’ekijwaro kyangye kiine amaizi maingi, hakaba hakiriho, obwo kikaba nikireebeka ahariinye, emyanya mingi y’okuhungiramu erikushemeza; kandi ebishaka n’ebishaka nibiguma biri eby’omu bwire bw’omushana, n’ahabw’ekyo obunyaatsi oburikuteera omu bwire bw’enjura, butarikuteerwa, butaine nshonga, nibuguma ahagati y’omushana.

Aha muheru tukatunga offing nk’egyo, ngu abavugi b’enyonyi abo bombi bakaba batakyetengyesa. Obuato bw’amaani obwabaire butuhereize bukatandika kugyendera aha rubaju.

Kikaba kiri eky’okutangaaza kandi kitari ky’okushemeza, oku Pelegi na Biludadi baakwatsirweho omu bwire obu, namunonga Kapiteeni Biludaadi. Ahabw'okuziika okurugaho, kwonka; okuziika munonga kuruga, ahabw’oburungi, emeeri ebohirwe aha rugendo ruraingwa kandi orw’akabi munonga—okurenga aha nkungu zombi ezirimu omuyaga; emeeri ei yaateiremu enkumi n’enkumi z’empiiha ezi yaabaire akozire n’amaani; emeeri, ei omugurusi ou yaabaire naavuga nawe yaabaire agyenderamu nk’omukuru w’emeeri; omushaija haihi n’okukura nk’ogwo, omurundi ogundi akatandika kubugana okutiinisa kwona okw’orubaju orutaine saasi; okukyenga okusiibura ekintu nk’oku buri muringo gwona kiijwire buri kimwe eki arikukunda,—omworo omugurusi Bildadi akagumaho obwire buraingwa; akagyendera aha rubaju rw’enyanja n’entambwe z’okwerarikirira; akairuka yaashuuma omu kaju kugamba ekigambo ky’okusibura ekindi okwo; akagaruka yaija aha bwato, yaareeba omuyaga; akareeba aha maizi mahango kandi agatarikuhwaho, agarikugarukira aha Mahanga g’Oburugwa-izooba agatarikureebwa hare gonka; akareeba aha nsi; akareeba ahaiguru; akareeba aha rubaju rwa buryo n’orwa bumosho; akareeba buri hamwe kandi hatariho ahu ari; kandi aha muheru, omu muringo gw’ebyoma arikuzingazinga omurunga aha nshonda yaagwo, akakwata Peleg omugurusi omu ngaro, kandi akwaitse etabaaza, kumara akaanya kakye akeemerera arikumureeba omu maisho nk’obuzira, nk’oku yaabaire naagira ati, “N’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, munywani wangye Peleg, nimbaasa kukigumira; eego, nimbaasa kukigumira.”

Kandi Pelegi we wenka, akakira kukitwara nk’omunyabwengye; kwonka ahabw’obuhangwa bwe bwona, hakaba hariho amarira gaabaire nigamwenya omu maisho ge, obu etabaaza yaabaire neehika haihi munonga. Kandi nawe, tarairukire kakye kuruga omu kabini kuza aha nkungu—hati ekigambo ahansi, kandi hati ekigambo na Starbuck, omunywani mukuru.

Kwonka, aha muheru, akahindukira munywani we, n'okureeba okurikuhererukayo okurikumukwataho,—“Kapiteeni Bildad—ija, munywani we w'emeeri omukuru, twine kuzayo. Garuka omu kishaayi kikuru aho! Obwato ahoy! Yemerera aha rubaju kwija haihi aha rubaju, hati! Yegyendesereze, yegyendesereze!—ija, Bildad, ahari Luckbu—gamba yelust yaawe. Stubb—omugisha ahariimwe, Mr. Flask—musiibura kandi omugisha murungi ahariimwe mwena—kandi ekiro eki emyaka eshatu ndyagira ekiihuro ky’omu mwabazyo ekirikwotsya ndikunywa etaabe ahabwanyu omuri Hurrah eya ira kandi hare!”

“Ruhanga abahe omugisha, kandi mube omu kurinda kwe okurikwera, abashaija,” omugurusi Bilidadi akagira, haihi ataine nshonga. "Ninteekateeka ngu hati nimuza kugira embeera nungi y'obwire, ngu Kapiteeni Ahabu abone kuba naaza omugati yaanyu juba—eizooba rirungi niryo ryonka eri arikwetenga, kandi nimuza kugira bingi omu rugyendo rw'omu kicweka ky'obutiti eki murikuza. Otakyeebwa okushaba kwawe, nabwo. Stubb; kikaba nikisheeshasheesha kakye, nkateekateeka. Ku oraabe nookwata aha birwa, yegyendesereze obushambani.

“Ija, ija, Kapiteeni Bilidaadi, orekye kugaaniira,—ogyende!” kandi Pelegi yaaheza kumurahuka, yaaza aha rubaju rw'orubaju, bombi baagwa omu bwato.

Emeeri n’obwato bikataanira kimwe; omuyaga gw’ekiro ogurikwotsya kandi ogurimu amaizi gukaba gufuka ahagati; enkongoro erikuteera orwaari ekaguruka ahaiguru; ebibunu bibiri bikagurukaguruka; tukaha ebigambo bishatu by’okushemererwa eby’omutima ogurikuremeera, kandi twaza omu nyanja ya Atlantic eri yonka nk’omufu.





EISHOMO 23. Omugyera gwa Lee.

Eshuura ezimwe enyima, Bulkington omwe akagambwaho, omuvugi w’enyanja mureingwa, owaabaire ari omu itaka risya, akabugana omuri New Bedford aha nju y’abagyenyi.

Obu omu kiro ekyo ekyabaire nikitetema omu bwire bw’obutiti, Pequod ekateera obuta bwayo obw’okuhoorera enzigu omu bicu by’obufuki eby’obubi, n’oha ou nshemerire kureeba ayemereire aha mutwe gwayo kureka Bulkington! Nkareeba n’okutiina kw’okusaasirwa n’okutiina aha mushaija, owaabaire yaaheza kuruga omu rugendo rw’akabi orw’emyaka ena omu bwire bw’obutiti, akaba naabaasa kugaruka kugyendera aha nyanja ataine busingye ahabw’obwire obundi obw’omuyaga. Ensi ekaba neeshusha nk’erikwotsya kuhika aha bigyere bye. Ebintu ebirikutangaaza munonga n’ebitarikugambwaho; okwijuka okuri omunda munonga tikurikurugamu bihandiiko by’okuziika; eshuura egi eya inci mukaaga n’ekituuro kya Bulkington ekitaine mabaare. Reka ngambe ngu kikamuhikaho nk’oku kyabaire aha meeri erikuteerwa omuyaga, erikwevuga omu muringo gw’obusaasi aha itaka eririkureeba omuyaga. Omwanya gukaba nigwija fain kuha obuhwezi; omwaaro nigushaasha; omu mwaaro harimu oburinzi, okuhuumurizibwa, eibaare ry’omuriro, eky’omu mwabazyo, obutambaara oburikwotsya, banywani, byona ebyo biri eby’embabazi aha kufa kwaitu. Kwonka omu muyaga ogwo, omwaaro, ensi, n’akabi akarikukirayo obuhango aha meeri egyo; aine kuguruka obugyenyi bwona; okukora aha itaka kumwe, n’obu kwakubaire nikuriisa ekibunu, kukaba kumureetera kutetema kurabira omu itaka. N’amaani ge goona naateerana emeeri zoona kuruga aha rubaju rw’enyanja; omu kukora ekyo, naarwana ‘n’omuyaga gwonka ogu fain yaabaire naija kumuhuha omuka; asherura obutagira itaka bwona obw’enyanja eyaabaire erimu ebicupuri; ahabw’obuhungiro okuhunga omu kabi; munywani we omwe wenka omuzigu we orikukirayo obushaarizi!

Hati nimumanya, Bulkington? Nimushusha ngu nimureeba akamanyiso kakye k’amazima ago agatarikugumisiriza omu kufa; ngu okuteekateeka kwona okuri omunda, okw’obweziriki n’okuteeraho kw’omutima okutaine kutiina okurinda okwetegyeka okuri omu mubonano kw’enyanja ye; obwo omuyaga gw’amaani ogw’omu iguru n’ensi gurikukora orukwe rw’okumunaga aha rubaju rw’enyanja orurimu obugobya kandi orurimu obuhuuku?

Kwonka nk’oku omu butagira itaka bwonka burikutuura amazima g’ahaiguru, agatariho nshonda, agatahwaho nka Ruhanga—nikwo, nikirungi okuhwerekyerera omu kuborooga okwo okutaine bugarukiro, okukira okuteerwa aha rubaju rw’enyanja otaine kitiinisa, n’obu ekyo kyakubaire kiri oburinzi! Ahabw’okugira ngu nk’enzigye, obwo, oh! n’oha owaabaire naija kukunda kuza ahansi! Okutiinisa kw’abarikutiinisa! obusaasi obu bwona n'obwa busha? Mugume, mugume, iwe Bulkington! Ogumeho obusaasi, demiruhanga! Ahaiguru kuruga omu kufuuhirira kw’enyanja yaawe-okucwekyerera—okugororoka ahaiguru, okuguruka apotheosis yaawe!





EISHOMO 24. Omujurizi.

Nk’oku nyowe na Queequeg hati tutandikire omu muringo gw’oburingaaniza omu bizinesi egi ey’okuroba enyanja; kandi nk’oku bizinesi egi ey’okuroba enyanja z’omu nyanja omu muringo gumwe ebaire neetwarwa omu bantu b’omu itaka nk’ekintu ekitari ky’ebitebyo kandi ekitaine kitiinisa; n'ahabw'ekyo, nyine okwerarikirira kwona kubahamiza, imwe abanya-itaka, obutari buringaaniza obu eki kitukoreire itwe abahiigi b'enyanja.

Omu mwanya gw’okubanza, nikibaasa kuteekateekwaho nk’ekitari ky’omugasho munonga kuhamya amazima, ngu omu bantu boona, bizinesi y’okuroba enyanja terikubarwa aha rurengo n’ebi barikweta emirimo y’obugabe. Omunyamahanga ku araabe yaaza omu bantu b’omu rurembo orukuru abatari bamwe na bamwe, kikaba nikiija kutunguura kakye ekiteekateeko ky’abantu boona aha birungi bye, ku araabe yaayorekwa kampuni nk’omuteezi w’enkoni, tugire ngu; kandi ku araabe omu kugyeragyeranisa n’abakuru b’amahe g’omu nyanja aine kutaho enyuguta z’okubanza SWF (Sperm Whale Fishery) aha kaada ye y’okutaayaayira abantu, omuringo nk’ogwo nigwija kutwarwa nk’ogw’okubanza kandi ogw’okushekyerera.

Hatariho kubangaanisa enshonga emwe erikwebembera ahabw’enki ensi neeyanga kutuha ekitiinisa itwe aba whalemen, n’egi: nibateekateeka ngu, omu kurungi, okwetwa kwaitu nikuhikaana n’omuringo gw’obushuubuzi bw’okushara enyama; kandi ngu twaba nitwejumbamu n’amaani, nitwehinguririzibwa oburofa bw’emiringo yoona. Abanyanyama itwe turi, ekyo kihikire. Kwonka abashaija b’enyama, nabo, n’abashaija b’enyama abaine baagi erikukizayo eshagama boona babaire bari Abakuru b’amahe abu ensi etarikuhinduka erikushemererwa kuha ekitiinisa. Kandi aha nshonga y’obutashemeire oburikuteekateekwaho omu mirimo yaitu, juba nimwija kutandika omu mazima agamwe agatakamanyirwe gye okuhitsya hati, kandi aga, omu bwijwire, nigaija kuhinga omu businguzi emeeri y’enyanja y’ebishaka haakiri omu bintu ebishemeire munonga eby’ensi egi eshemeire. Kwonka n’okwikiriza omushango ogurikugambwaho kuba ogw’amazima; ni bishengye ki ebitebeekanisiibwe ebirikusheesheka eby’emeeri y’enyanja ebirikugyeragyeranisibwa n’ebintu ebitarikugambwa eby’omu myanya egyo ey’orutaro ahu abaserukare baingi munonga barikuruga kunywa omu kushemererwa kw’abakazi boona? Kandi ekiteekateeko ky’akabi kyaba nikiyongyera munonga aha kwetunguura oku abantu barikugira aha murimo gw’omuserukare; reka mbahamize ngu abaserukare baingi abaabaire nibagyendera omu busingye kuza aha battery, bakaba nibaija kurahuka kugaruka enyima omu kureebeka kw’omukira muhango gw’enyanja y’ebinyonyi ebirikwetwa sperm whale, barikufuka omuyaga oguri ahaiguru y’omutwe gwe. Ahabw’okuba ni bintu ki ebirikutiinisa ebirikwetegyerezibwa eby’omuntu waagyeragyeranisa n’ebirikutiinisa n’eby’okutangaaza bya Ruhanga ebiine akakwate!

Kwonka, n’obu ensi eraabe neetushwijuma abahiigi b’enyanja, kwonka neetuha ekitiinisa ekikuru munonga tutamanyire; eego, okuramya okurikukirayo! ahakuba haihi ebipande byona, etabaaza, n’amakanzu ebirikwotsya okwehinguririza ensi, nibyotsya, nk’oku byabaire biri enyima y’ebituuro bingi munonga, ahabw’ekitiinisa kyaitu!

Kwonka reeba enshonga egi omu bindi bitaara; mugipime aha minzaani y'emiringo yoona; reeba eki itwe abashaija b’enyanja turi, kandi eki twabaire turi.

Ahabw’enki Abadaaki omu bwire bwa De Witt baabaire baine abakuru b’emeeri zaabo z’okuroba enyanja? Ahabw’enki Louis XVI yaakozire. owa Bufaransa, aha sente ze, akatebeekanisa emeeri z’okuroba enyanja kuruga Dunkirk, kandi omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa yaayeta omu tauni egyo amaka nka makumi n’abiri nari abiri kuruga aha kirwa kyaitu ekya Nantucket? Ahabw’enki Bungyereza ahagati y’emyaka 1750 na 1788 yaashashura abashaija baayo abavugi b’enyanja omu nshaho ezirikurenga £1,000,000? Kandi eky’aha muheru, nikibaho kita ngu itwe abanya whale ba America hati nitukira abanya whale abasigaireho boona abaine ebipande omu nsi yoona; okuvuga emeeri ezirikurenga magana mushanju; erimu abashaija obukumi ikumi na munaana; buri mwaka n’okurya 4,000,000 z’empiiha; emeeri ezo zikaba ziri, omu bwire bw’okuzivuga, doola 20,000,000! kandi buri mwaka turikureeta omu myaaro yaitu eby’okurya ebisharuirwe gye eby’empiiha 7,000,000. Kwonka, ekigambo eki eky’omu ishwa kikaba nikireebeka nk’ekitari ky’oburyo, ahabw’eki omu Manxman w’enzirugavu yaabaire naagamba, omushaija mukuru w’omu kituuro, owaabaire atakarugire Nantucket omu meeri, akaba atakareebaga Ahabu w’omu ishwa. Nangwa n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, emigyenzo y’enyanja eya ira, enyikiriza ezitarikwijukwa, zikaba zita omu Manxman ogu omukuru amaani g’okwetegyereza agatari ga buriijo. N’ahabw’ekyo tihaine omuvugi w’enyanja omweru owaamuhakanisa munonga obu yaagira ngu ku haraabe hariho obwire obu Kapiteeni Ahabu yaaza kuteebwa omu busingye—ekirikubaasa kutabaho, n’ahabw’ekyo akagamba n’eiraka rihango—obwo, weena owaabaire naija kukora ofiisi egyo ey’aha muheru ahabw’abafu, naija kumushangaho akamanyiso k’okuzaarwa kuruga aha mutwe kuhika aha mutwe.

Amaani g’amaani gakakwata aha rubaju rwona orw’obusaasi rwa Ahabu, n’akabonero k’obusaasi akaabaire kariho, ngu omu bwire bukye obw’okubanza nkaba ntarikumanya ngu tihariho kakye aha bushaarizi obu oburikutegyeka bukaba buri ahabw’okuguru okw’obunyamahe okw’omweru oku yaabaire ayemereireho ekicweka. Kikaba kyanyizire enyima ngu okuguru oku okw’amahembe g’entare kukaba kuri omu nyanja kukozirwe kuruga omu igufa erishemeziibwe ery’orubaju rw’enyanja y’omunyanja. “Aye, akashohozibwa aha rubaju rwa Japan,” nikwo omuhindi omugurusi orikwetwa Gay-Head yaagizire rimwe; “kwonka nk’emeeri ye eyacwekyereziibwe, akatwara omuti ogundi atagarukire omuka ahabw’okugigura.

Nkakwatwaho munonga ahabw’okuguma aine omubiri nk’omwe. Aha rubaju rwona orw’ekishengye kya Pequod, kandi haihi munonga n’ebijwaro bya mizzen, hakaba hariho ekiina ky’okuteeraho (auger), ekibooroire nk’ekicweka kya inci nari nk’ekyo, omu rubaaho. Okuguru kwe okw’eigufa kukaguma omu kiina ekyo; omukono gumwe guteirwe ahaiguru, kandi gukwatsirwe ekijwaro ky'okukingira; Kapiteeni Ahabu akeemerera, arikureeba aheeru butunu aha mutwe gw’emeeri ogwabaire guguma niguteera. Hakaba hariho obutahwaho bw’obumanzi obuhamire, okumariirira, okutarikwehayo, omu kwehayo okuhamire kandi okutaine kutiina, okw’omumaisho okw’okureeba okwo. Tihariho kigambo na kimwe eki yaagambire; kandi n'abakuru be tibaamugambiire kintu kyona; n’obu baraabe baabaire nibakora obubonero bwabo bwona obukye munonga n’ebigambo byabo, bakaba nibooreka gye okutagira busingye, ku kiraabe kitari ky’obusaasi, eky’okuguma bari ahansi y’eriisho ry’omukuru eriine oburemeezi. Kandi ti ekyo kyonka, kureka Ahabu owaabaire aine omutima gw’obusaasi akeemerera omu maisho gaabo aine omusharaba omu maisho; omu kitiinisa kyona eky’obugabe ekirikutegyeka ekitaine iziina eky’obusaasi obumwe obw’amaani.

Ere long, kuruga omu kutaayaayira kwe okw’okubanza omu mwanya, akagaruka omu kaju ke. Kwonka bwanyima y’akasheeshe ako, akaba areebeka abavugi b’emeeri buri izooba; nari ayemereire omu kiina kye ky’okuteeraho, nari ashutami aha ntebe y’amahembe g’entare ei yaabaire aine; nari okugyendera aha deki munonga. Eiguru ku ryabaire niriguma nirikyeera; buzima, akatandika kukura genial kakye, akaguma naaguma ari omunyabwengye; nk’oku, emeeri ku yaabaire yaaruga omuka, hatariho ekindi kureka ekicuucu ky’enyanja ekyabaire kifiire omu bwire bw’obutiti ekyabaire kimureeteire kwehereera munonga. Kandi, ahonaaho, kikabaho ngu akaba ari haihi kuguma omu mwanya; kwonka, nk’oku kyabaire kiri hati, ahabw’ebi byona ebi yaagambire, nari ebi yaakozire nk’oku birikwetegyerezibwa, aha nkungu y’omushana eyahererukireyo, akaba nareebeka nk’otarikwetengyesa aho nk’omuti ogundi. Kwonka Pequod ekaba neekora omuhanda gwonka hati; obutaguma nibavuga emeeri; haihi byona eby’okutebeekanisa eby’enyanja ebyabaire nibyetenga kureebererwa abashwereine bakaba baine obushoboorozi bwona, n’ahabw’ekyo hakaba hariho kakye nari hatariho kintu kyona, kuruga ahariwe, eky’okukoresa nari okushemeza Ahabu, hati; kandi atyo akabinga, ahabw’obwire obwo bumwe, ebicu ebyabaire nibiteerana aha mutwe gwe bikaba biteeraine aha mutwe gwe, nk’oku ebicu byona birikutoorana enshonda ezirikukirayo oburaingwa kweteeraho.

Nangwa n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, omu bwire bukye, okuhabisa okurikwotsya, okurikuteganisa okw’embeera nungi y’obwire bw’ebiro bikuru ei twahikiremu, kukaba nikureebeka nk’okurikumushemeza mporampora kuruga omu mbeera ye. Ahabw’okuba, nk’oku abaishiki b’amatama garikutukura, abarikuzina, Apuri na Maaci, barikuza omuka omu kibira ky’obutiti, ekitari ky’abantu; nangwa n’omuti gw’omukyere ogurikukirayo obukye, ogurikukirayo obuhango, ogurikukirayo kuba ogw’enkuba, haakiri nigwija kwohereza ebihimba bikye eby’ebishaka, okwakiira abagyenyi nk’abo abaine emitima eshemereirwe; n’ahabw’ekyo Ahabu akakora, aha muheru, akagarukamu kakye okushemeza kw’okuzaana okw’omuyaga ogwo ogw’omwishiki. Emirundi erikurenga ahari gumwe akataho ekimuri ky’okureeba ekikye, ekyabaire, omu mushaija ondiijo weena, kikaba nikiija kurugayo juba omu kumwenya.
