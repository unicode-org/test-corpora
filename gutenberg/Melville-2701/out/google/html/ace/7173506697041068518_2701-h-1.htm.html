“Soe-e debel droeneuh?”—jih akhe jih jipeugah—“droeneuh hana peugah haba-e, dam-me, lon poh-e.” Dan meunan geupeugah haba tomahawk nyang ka meuterang nyan mulai meukeumang teuntang lon lam seupôt.

“Tuan tanoh, demi Allah, Petrus Peti Mati!” kheun I. “Tuan tanoh! Jaga! Peti mati! Malaikat! seulamatkan saya!”

“Peugah-e! peugah-ee lon soe-ee be, atawa dam-lon, lon poh-e!” keulai geukheun kanibal nyan, seudangkan kembang-kembang jih nyang dahsyat nibak tomahawk nyan jipeutabu abu tembakau nyang suum bak lon sampoe lon pike linen lon akan meuapui. Tapi alhamdulillah, bak saat nyan tuan rumoh teuka lam kama cahaya lam jaroe, dan ji plueng dari teumpat éh lon plueng u ateuh jih.

“Bek teumakot jinoe,” kheun jih sira jiceu-ceu lom, “Queequeg disinoe hana akan jipeurusak saboh ôk ulée gata.”

“Bek le gata ceumeucue,” kheun lon, “dan pakon gata hana tapeugah bak lon bahwa harpooneer nuraka nyan nakeuh sidroe kanibal?”

"Lon pike droeneuh ka neuteupeue nyan;—hana lon peugah bak droeneuh, jih sidroe ureueng meukat ulee-ulee di sekitar kota?—teuma meuputa-puta lom dan neujak teungeut. Queequeg, kalon keunoe—gata sabbee lon, lon sabbee—gata ureung nyoe teungeut gata—gata sabbee?”

“Me sabbee le”—geukheun Queequeg, geupupoe pipa gobnyan dan geuduek bak teumpat éh.

“Kamoe gettee in,” tambah jih, geubri isyarat keu lon ngon tomahawk jih, dan geubôh bajee nyan u saboh blah. Gobnyan beutôi-beutôi geupeubuet hai nyoe kon deungon cara sipil mantong tapi deungon cara nyang beutôi-beutôi jroh dan amal. Lon teudong lon kalon jih siat. Keu mandum tato jih, jih secara keseluruhan sidroe kanibal nyang gleh dan lagak. Peue mantong karu nyang ka lon peugot nyoe, lon pike keudroe-droe lon—ureueng nyan manusia saban lagee lon: jih na alasan nyang saban le keu teumakot keu lon, lagee lon haroh teumakot keu jih. Leubeh get teungeut ngon kanibal nyang sadar nibak ureueng Kristen nyang mabok.

"Tuan tanoh," kheun lon, "peugah bak jih beu jikeubah tomahawk jih disinan, atawa pipa, atawa peu mantong nyang droeneuh peugah; peugah bak jih beu jitheun merokok, singkat haba, dan lon akan meuputa-puta ngon jih. Tapi lon hana galak na ureung agam merokok bak teumpat éh ngon lon. Nyan bahaya. Selain nyan, lon hana asuransi."

Nyoe dipeugah bak Queequeg, jih langsong patuh, dan lom deungon sopan geubri isyarat keu lon untuk tamong lam teumpat éh-meugulong u saboh blah sebanyak geupeugah-“lon hana lon sentuh gaki droeneuh.”

“Selamat malam, tuan rumoh,” kheun lon, “jeut neujak.”

Lon meuputa-puta, dan hana tom teungeut leubeh get lam udep lon.
