DUL KWAN ME 43. Winy!

"HIST! Iwinyo koko meno, Cabaco?"

Onwongo obedo cawa me dye ceng: lero dwe maleng; lutic me yeya ni gucung i kin gii, ma oyabe ki i kom pii maleng ma tye i dyere, nio wa i kom pii ma tye cok ki taffrail. I yoo man, gi kato ki baket me pongo scuttle-butt. Cung, pi kare malac, i kom kabedo maleng me dul ma ki lwongo ni quarter-deck, onongo gigwokke pe me lok nyo ngwec ki tyengi. Cake i cing acel me cito i cing mukene, baket ni ocito i ling mot, ma ki turo ki koko pa yeya, ki koko pa yeya ma tye ka mede anyim.

I kin kare me yweyo ni, Archy, ngat acel i kin jo ma ki gwoko, ma kabedo ne onwongo tye cok ki kama ki keto iye jami ma ki lwongo ni after-hatches, oloko ki jirani ne, la Cholo, lok ma malo ni.

"Hist! iwinyo koko meno, Cabaco?"

"Kwany baket ni, in ibi waco, Archy? ngo ma imito waco?"

"Tye kany dok--i te doggola--pe iwinyo--aon--owinyo calo aona."

"Abedo ki lworo! Kwany ki baket ma ki dwoko cen ni."

"Tye kunu-kunu!-winye calo dano aryo onyo adek ma gi tye ka nino, kombedi!"

"Caramba! dong itiimo, latic me yeya, ibi timo? Obedo mugati adek ma ki lwoko ma i camo pi cam me otyeno ma tye ka loko i komi--pe gin mo mukene. Nen i baket!"

"Wac gin ma imito, latic me yeya; atye ki it maber."

"Aye, in aye lacoo ni, in aye, ma iwinyo koko pa Quakeress macon ma tye mairo pyerabic i nam ki Nantucket; in aye lacoo ni."

"Nyero woko; wabi neno ngo ma obi time. Winy, Cabaco, tye ngat mo ma tye piny i dog nam ma peya ki neno i kom yeya; ki atamo ni Mogul wa macon bene ngeyo gin mo i kome. Awinyo Stubb owaco ki Flask, i otyeno mo acel, ni tye gin mo ma kit meno i yamo."

"Tish! baket ni!"





DUL KWAN ME 44. Karatac.

Ka onongo ilubo kor ladit mony Ahab piny i ot bute ingee yamo magwar ma otimme i dyewor ma oweko kimoko tam ne ki luticce, onongo ibi neno en ka cito i ot ma ki keto iye jami ma tye i dog nam, ki kelo karatac ma nen calo yellowish sea charts, ki keto gi i nyime i kom meja ne ma ki juku. I kare ma en obedo piny i nyime, onongo ibi neno en tye ka kwano dul ma pat pat ki rangi ma nen ki wange; ki ki pencil ma mot ento ma ocung matek me coyo jami mukene i kom kabedo ma onongo tye nono. I kare mogo, en onongo loko i kom buk macon ma tye i ngete, ma ki coyo iye kare me mwaka ki kabedo ma, i wot me yeya mapol macon, rec ma ki lwongo ni sperm whales onongo ki mako onyo ki neno.

I kare ma tye ka tic kit meno, tara ma pek ma ki keto i wiye, onongo tye ka ngwec ki ngwec pa yeya, dok pi naka onongo tye ka keto mac ma ryeny ki tipu me lain i kom wiye ma opong ki ngwec, nio ka onongo nen calo en kikome onongo tye ka keto lanyut ki rwom me ngwec i kom bongo ma opong ki ngwec, onongo bene tye ka coyo rwom ki rwom me ngwec ki pencil mogo i kom karatac ma ki keto lanyut iye matut i wiye.

Ento pi obedo dyewor ni kikome ma, i kare ma en tye kene i ot bute, Ahab obedo ka tamo i kom karatac ne. Cokcok dyewor weng ki kelo gi woko; cokcok dyewor weng ki kwanyo lanyut mogo ma ki coyo i pencil, ki mukene ki loko. Pien ki karatac me nam angwen ni ma tye i nyime, Ahab onwongo tye ka wot i kin pii ma mol ki yamo ma mol, ki neno me cobo tam ma en tye kwede i cwinye.

Kombedi, bot ngat mo keken ma pe ngeyo yoo pa ler ma ki lwongo ni leviathans, romo nen calo tic ma pe ki gen iye me yenyo ler acel ma tye kene i nam ma pe ki guro i lobo kany. Lutic ma i yeya ma i nyim ki i kin piny gubino piny; dul ma ki lwongo ni line tubs ni ki keto gi kama gi tye iye; jami ma ki lwongo ni cranes ni ki cwalo gi woko; dyekal madit ni onwongo tye angec, ki yeya adek ni onwongo tye ka ngwec i wi nam calo agulu adek ma ki yubu ki got mabor. I nge cel, lutic gi ma cwiny gi tek ki cing gi acel gu moko i kom cel, kun tyengi acel onongo tye ka cung i kom gunwale. Pi meno nen dul pa lulweny mabor ma onongo gitye ka ngwec i yeya pa lumone.

Ento i kare man ma pire tek ni, lok mo owinyo ma okwanyo wang dano weny ki i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whale. Ki acaki dano weng gu neno Ahab macol, ma onwongo ki guro ki ler abic ma nen calo ki yubu ki yamo.
