DUL ME 32. Cetology.

Dong ki cwalo wa ki tek cwiny i kom pii ma tye i dye nam; ento cokcok-ki wabirweny woko i kabedone ma pe ki dog nam, ma pe ki dog nam. Ere meno otimme; ma peya Pequod's weedy hull ni odonyo tung ki barnacled hulls pa leviathan; i acaki ber me keto cwinyi i kom lok ma pire tek me niang matut i kom jami ma ki nyutu ki jami ma ki waco ma lubo.

Tye gin mo ma ki yubu me nyutu rec ma ki lwongo ni whale i dul ne mapol, ma dong amito keto i nyimi. Ento pe obedo tic ma yot. Kit ma ki poko kwede jami ma tye i kin jami ma kelo ayelayela, pe tye gin mo ma ki waco kany. Winy ngo ma lutela maber ki manyen gu keto piny.

"Pe tye dul mo me kwan i kom ler ma tye iye ma calo ma ki lwongo ni Cetology," owaco Captain Scoresby, AD 1820.

"Pe tye yub na, ka onwongo tye i teko na, me donyo i lok ma mako kit me poko ler ma ki lwongo ni cetacea i dul ki gang. * * * Apoka poka tye i kin lucoc me tekwaro pa ler eni" (sperm whale), owaco ladit Beale, AD 1839.

"Pe rwate me lubo kwan wa i pii ma pe kitwero niang iye." "Bongo ma pe kitwero donyo iye ma oumo ngec wa i kom cetacea." “Poto ma opong ki okuto.” "Gin ma ki nyutu ni pe otum ento tiyo me buru wan dano ma ngeyo lok kom ginacweya."

Pi meno lok i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, Cuvier madit, ki John Hunter, ki Lesson, jami ma ki ngeyo i kom ler ki kom ler. Kadi bed kumeno, kadi bed ni ngec me ada tye manok, ento bukke tye mapol; ki dong i rwom manok, ki cetology, onyo kwan ma mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whales. Pol pa coo, matino ki madongo, macon ki manyen, dano ma gibedo i ngom ki lutic me nam, ma gu coyo lok i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whale. Nen i kom mogo manok:—Lucoc me Baibul; Aristotle; Pliny; Aldrovandi; Ladit Thomas Browne; Gesner; Ray; Linnæus; Rondeletius; Willoughby; Rangi ma cal ki lum; Artedi; Sibbald; Brisson; Marten; Lacépède; Bonneterre; Desmarest; Baron Cuvier; Frederick Cuvier; John Hunter; Owen; Scoresby; Beale; Bennett; J. Ross Browne; ngat ma ocoyo buk pa Miriam Coffin; Olmstead; ki Rev. T. Cheever. Ento ngo ma jami eni weng gu coyo ni, jami ma ki kwanyo malo ni obi nyutu.

I kin nying ma tye i kin lucoc me rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, jo ma lubo Owen keken aye guneno rec ma ki lwongo ni whale makwo; ki ento ngat acel i kingi onwongo obedo ngat ma ngeyo tic ki jami lweny ki rec ma ki lwongo ni whales. Atye ka waco ni Captain Scoresby. I kom lok ma pat ma kwako Greenland onyo right-whale, en aye ngat ma tye ki twero maber loyo. Ento Scoresby onongo pe ngeyo gin mo ki pe owaco gin mo i kom rec madit ma ki lwongo ni sperm whale, ka kiporo ki rec ma ki lwongo ni Greenland whale ma pe opore me waco ne. Ki kany omyera ki wac, ni rec ma ki lwongo ni Greenland whale tye ka kwalo komker me nam. En pe obedo gin ma dit loyo i kin rec ma ki lwongo ni whales. Ento, pi kare malac ma en owaco ni pire tek, ki ngec manok ma, nio wa i mwaka pyerabiro mukato angec, oketo i kom ler ma ki lwongo ni sperm-whale ma onongo pe ki ngeyo, ki ma ngec ma pe ki ngeyo nio wa i kare ni pud tye ka loc i kin dano weny ento i kabedo mogo ma ki ngeyo iye jami me cayan; jemo man dong otiime maber. Lok i kom lok ma mako ler ma ki lwongo ni leviathanic allusions i lucoc madongo me kare mukato angec, obi miini yom cwiny ni rec ma ki lwongo ni Greenland whale, labongo ngat mo ma twero pyem kwede, onwongo obedo kabaka me nam bot gi. Ento kare dong oo pi lok manyen. Man aye Charing Cross; wuwiny! dano mabeco weng,—rec ma ki lwongo ni Greenland whale ki ryemo woko,—rec ma dit ma ki lwongo ni sperm whale dong tye ka loc!

Tye buk aryo keken ma tye ma gi tye ka keto ler ma kwo i nyimi, ki i kare acel, i rwom ma lamal loyo gi loyo i temo ni. Buk magi tye pa Beale ki Bennett; gin aryo ni i kare gi ludaktari ma gi tiyo i England South-Sea whale-ships, ki gin aryo ni coo ma genne. Gin ma okwongo ma mako ler ma ki lwongo ni sperm whale ma ki nongo i kom gi tye matidi; ento kit ma tye kwede, tye maber atika, kadi bed ni pol kare ki keto i lok me cayan. I kare ni, kadi bed kumeno, rec ma ki lwongo ni sperm whale, me cayan onyo me poem, kwo ne pe otum i buk mo keken. Ma kato ler mukene ma ki neko, kwo ne tye ma pe ki coyo.
