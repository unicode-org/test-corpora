DUL KWAN ME 74. Wic pa ler ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale-Neno ma ki poko.

Kany, kombedi, tye rec madongo aryo, ma gi keto wigi kacel; wek wa ribbe kwed gi, ki wa ket wa kacel.

I kin dul ler ma ki lwongo ni folio leviathans, ler ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale ki Right Whale gubedo ma ki ngeyo gii loyo. Gin aye rec ma ki lwongo ni whales ma dano maro neko gi kare ki kare. Ki bot jo Nantucketer, gi nyutu jami aryo ma okato i kin ler ma ki ngeyo i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whale. Kit ma apoka poka ma tye i kin gii nen i wigi; ki ma calo wic pa ngat acel acel tye kare ni ma tye ka cung ki tung cing Pequod; ki kit ma wa twero cito kwede ki acel me cito i mukene, kun wa kato ki i kom deck:—kan ma, amito ngeyo, in ibi nongo kare maber me pwonyo cetology ma tiyo maber loyo kany?

I acaki, ibedo ki ur i kom apokapoka ma tye i kin wic magi. Gin aryo ni weng gi dit ma oromo i cwiny dano weny; ento tye gin mo ma rwate i kin rec ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale ma Right Whale pe ki iye. Tye jami mukene ma tye i wi ler ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale. Kit ma ineno kwede, in imiyo twero madit atika bote, ma lubu deyo ma opong. I kare ni bene, deyo man ki medo ki kala ma ki lwongo ni pepper ki salt ma tye i wiye i wi got, ma miyo lanyut me mwaka ma dit ki ngec madwong. Ki lok macek, en tye gin ma lumak rec lwongo ni "rec ma wic ne col."

Kong dong wanenu gin ma pe rwate ki wic magi—ma en aye, dul kom aryo ma pigi tego loyo, wang ki it. Ka mabor i nget wic, ki piny piny, cok ki dog rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, ka iyenyo mot, i agiki ne ibi neno wang ma pe ki lak, ma in itamo ni obedo wang latin ler; pi meno ki ikom rwom weny tye ki dit pa wic.

Kombedi, ki i kit ma wang rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ni tye kwede, nen maber ni en pe twero neno gin mo ma tye anyim kikome, ma loyo kit ma en twero neno kwede gin mo ma tye anyim kikome. I lok acel, kama wang rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ni tye iye rom ki kit ma it dano tye kwede; ki in itwero tamo, piri keni, kit ma obi bedo kwede kwedi, ka ibedo ka neno jami ki i it. Ibi nongo ni in itwero keto cik ma romo diguri pyer adek me neno ma peya ineno tung cing ma tye atir; ki dano mukene ma romo pyer adek angec. Ka lakwor ni ma rac loyo ni tye ka wot tung boti, ki pala lucwan ma ki tingo malo i dye ceng, onongo pe ibi twero neno en, ma calo ka en tye ka kwalo in ki angec. I lok acel, in ibi bedo ki tyen aryo, me waco ne; ento, i kare acel, bene, anyim aryo (nyim tung cing): pi ngo ma miyo anyim pa dano-ngo, ada, ento wange?

Medo ikom meno, kadi bed ni pol pa ler mukene ma atwero tamo gii kombedi, wang gi tye ma ki pito ma pe nen me rwate ki teko gi me neno, me miyo cal acel ento pi ariiyo bot adam wic; kit ma wang rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ni tye kwede, ma ki poko gii ki futi mapol me wic, ma ocung i kin gii calo got madit ma poko kin nam aryo i kin godi; man, me ada, myero opok kin jami ma dul kom acel acel ma tye kene miyo. Rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, pi meno, myero onen cal acel ma pat i tung kany, ki cal mukene ma pat i tung meno; ma nongo jami weng ma tye i kin gii omyera obed piny macol ki gin mo keken bote. Dano ki twero waco ni en tye ka neno lobo ki i kom gin ma ki lwongo ni sentry-box ma tye ki jami aryo ma ki rubu i wang ot. Ento ki rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, dul kom aryo ni ki keto gi pat pat, ma miyo wang ot aryo ma pat pat, ento ki cwer cwiny balo neno. Kit ma wang rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ni nen kwede obedo gin ma myero ki ket i wiiyi i kare me mako rec; ki me bedo ma lakwan opo i kom jami mogo ma lubo.

Lapeny ma kelo lworo ki ma kelo lworo romo cako lok ma mako gin ma ki neno ni ma mako Leviathan. Ento myero abed ma cwinya yom ki lok mo. Ka wang dano tye ma oyabe i lero, neno ne pe obedo me miti; meno aye ni, en pe twero konyo me neno gin mo keken ma tye i nyime. Kadi bed kumeno, gin ma ngat mo keken okato ki iye obi pwonyo en, ni kadi bed ni en twero neno jami ma pe ki poko kin dano i kare acel, ento tek tutwal pi en, ki diro, ki ki diro, me neno jami aryo mo keken—kadi bed ni dit nyo tidi—i kare acel; pi ibed ki par ka gi butu kacel ki gudu kom ngat acel acel. Ento ka i oo me poko jami aryo ni, ki i guro gin acel acel ki dul macol ma opong; eka, me neno acel i kingi, i yoo ma kelo tam ni i kome, mukene ni ki bi kwanyo woko ki i tam ni me kare ni. Ki dong, tye nining ki rec ma ki lwongo ni whale? Adaa, wange weng, i kom gi, omyera gubed ka tic i kare acel; ento tika obwone tye ki jami mapol, ma rwate, ki ma nen maber loyo pa dano, ma en twero i kare acel me neno jami aryo ma pat pat, acel i tung cinge acel, ki mukene i tung cinge mukene? Ka en twero, kono obedo gin maber i kome, ma calo ngat mo twero kato ki i kin jami aryo ma pat pat ma tye i Euclid. Ki dong, ka ki kwedo maber, pe tye gin mo ma pe rwate i poro man.

Twero bedo ni obedo gin ma pe ki timo, ento kare weny nen bota, ni kit ma rec mogo ma ki lwongo ni whales giwoto kwede ka gi tye i yeya adek onyo angwen; lworo ki lworo me bedo ki lworo, ma pol pa rec ma ki lwongo ni whales; Atamo ni jami eni weng oa ki i tam ma pe ki kony, ma iye teko gi me neno ma opokke ki ma pat pat omyera obed iye.

Ento it rec ma ki lwongo ni whale opong ki miti me ngeyo jami calo wang. Ka in ibedo ngat ma pe ingeyo dul kom gi, ki in ibi bedo ka yenyo wic aryo ni pi cawa malac, ki pi inongo dul kom meno. It pe ki pot yat ma woko; ki i bur ni kikome in itwero keto gin ma ki lwongo ni quill, ki en tye matidi ata. Ki keto manok i nge wang. Ki woro i kom it gi, apoka poka ma pire tek ni omyera ki nen i kin ler ma ki lwongo ni sperm whale ki tung acuc. I kare ma it pa ngat mukwongo ni tye ki doggola ma oyabe woko, pa ngat me aryo ni ki umo ki del kom ma ki lwongo ni membrane, ma weko pe nen ki woko.

Tika pe obedo gin me aura, ni gin ma dit calo rec ma ki lwongo ni whale twero neno lobo ki wang matidi, ki winyo mor pa kot ki it ma tidi loyo pa ler ma ki lwongo ni hare? Ento ka wange onwongo lac calo lens pa Herschel's great telescope; ki ite onongo tye ma opong calo doggola me ot lega madit; meno mono obi miyo en bedo ki neno mabor, onyo winyo maber? Pe wacel.—Pingo dong itemo “medo” tammi? Lok ne i dul ma pat pat.

Wek dong ki gin mo kiken ma ki tiyo kwede ki cuma ma tiyo ki yamo ma tye i cing wa, wa lok i kom wi rec ma ki lwongo ni sperm whale, wek obed piny piny malo; lacen, cito malo ki latwo me cito i wi got, nen piny i dog; ki ka onongo pe ni kom ni dong ki poko woko ki iye, ki mac ma ki lwongo ni lantern onongo waromo cito piny i kin got madit me Kentucky Mammoth Cave me iye. Ento wek wa mako kany ki lak man, ki wanen kama wan tye iye. Man obedo dog maleng ki nen maleng atika! cake i ngom nio wa i wi ot, ki keto i kin piny, onyo ki keto i karatac ki del kom ma ryeny, ma ryeny calo bongo me nyom.

Ento bino woko kombedi, ki inen lak ma piny ni, ma nen calo umu mabor ma tidi pa bok ma ki lwongo ni snuff-box, ki cinge i agiki acel, ento pi tung acel. Ka i tingo ne malo, wek i nong malo, ki inen lak lak ne, nen calo portcullis maber atika; ki jami ma kit meno, ayelayela! nyutu bot dano mapol ni ngat ma pe tiyo maber i tic me mako rec, ma jami ma ki lwongo ni spikes ni poto i kom gi ki teko ma mako rec. Ento gin ma rac loyo tye me neno, ka itye ka cito piny i nam, ineno rec mo ma ki lwongo ni sulky whale, ma tye ka mol kunnu, ki doge ma dit, ma tye fut apar wiye abic mabor, ma tye ka cung atir ki kome, pi wilobo weny calo yeya ma tye ka ngwec. Rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ni pe otoo; en cwinye otur keken; ma pe rwate, twero bedo; hypochondriac; ki tye ka butu piny, ma oweko lak doge odoko yot, kun weko en tye i peko ma kit meno, ma obedo lok me lewic bot kaka ne weny, ma omyera, labongo akalakala mo, gu ket doge i kome.

I kare mukene lak wic ma piny ni-ma ladiro ma ngeyo tic me goyo cal-ki kwanyo ki i wi yeya me kwanyo lak ler ma ki lwongo ni ivory, ki miyo jami ma mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whalebone ma mako rec ma mako jami ma pat pat, medo ki yat, umbrella-stock, ki cing me mako rec.

Ki gin mabor, ma ool, lak wic ni ki ywayo i yeya, ma calo gin ma ki keto i kom yeya; ki ka kare ma opore oo-nino manok ingee tic mukene-Queequeg, Daggoo, ki Tashtego, ma gubedo ludaktari ma ngeyo tic maber, gubi cako goyo lak. Ki pala ma ki lwongo ni cutting-spade, Queequeg diyo lak; ki dong ki mako doge piny ki ringbolts, ki gin ma ki tiyo kwede ki malo, gi kwanyo lak gi woko, ma calo dyang me Michigan gi kwanyo yat oak macon ki i poto. Pol kare tye lak pyera angwen wiye aryo weny; i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whales, ma obale woko, ento pe obale; ki dong ki pongo i nge kit ma wa yubu kwede. Lacen ki turo doge ni me doko got, ki ki keto calo gin ma ki tiyo kwede me gedo odi.





DUL KWAN ME 75. Wi rec ma ki lwongo ni Right Whale-Neno ma ki poro.

Ka wa kato ki i wi yeya, wek dong wanenu wi rec ma ki lwongo ni Right Whale.

Kit ma ki yubu kwede wic pa ler ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale ki twero poro ki gadigadi me lweny pa Luroma (tutwale i nyime, ma tye ma opong ki dul kom); ki dong, ka ineno maber, wic pa rec ma ki lwongo ni Right Whale nen calo war ma ki lwongo ni galliot-toed shoe. Mwaki miya aryo mukato angec lawot mo ma otii me Dutch oporo kit ma kome nen kwede ki pa ngat ma yubu war me agiiki. Ki i agiki acel-lu, dako ma otii ma ki lwongo ni nursery, ki lutino ma tye ka dongo, romo bedo maber, en ki lutino ne weng.

Ento ka i nyiko cok ki wic madit ni en cako bedo ki jami ma pat pat, malube ki neno ni. Ka icung i wiye ki ineno i kom dul ma ki lwongo ni F-shaped spoutholes, ibi kwanyo wic ni weny pi bass-viol ma dit, ki spiracles eni, apertures ma tye i sounding-board ne. Ki dong, doki, ka i keto wangi i kom gin ma nen calo ler ma ki lwongo ni comb-incrustation i kom gin ma ki lwongo ni mass- gin ma ryangi ne rom ki lum, ma ki lwongo ni barnacled, ma jo Greenland gilwongo ni "lak," ki lukwat me Southern gilwongo ni "bonnet" pa rec ma ki lwongo ni Right Whale; ka iketo wangi i kom lok man keken, onongo ibitero wic me yat oak mo madit, ma tye ki ot winyo i bute. I yoo mo keken, ka ineno rec ma kwo ma gibedo kany i kom bongo man, tam ma kit meno obi bino i wiiyi; kono, adaa, miti ni dong ki moko ki lok ma ki lwongo ni "ker" bene ki miiyo i kome; i kom lok meno ibi bedo ki miti madit me tamo kit ma ler man ma tek ni tye kwede kabaka me nam ma ki ruko bongo ma ki lwongo ni diademed, ma ki keto bongo ne ma ryangi ne rom ki pot yat i yoo maber ni. Ento ka rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ni obedo rwot, en nen calo ngat ma nen marac atika ma twero bedo ki deyo. Nen leb ma piny ma tye ka guro ni! kit ma lok me akemo ki akemo madit tye kunu! gin ma ki lwongo ni sulk and pout, kit ma ki pimo kwede pa lapur, ma romo fut pyera aryo bor ki fut abic i lapiny; lok ma ki lwongo ni sulk ki pout ma obi miini 500 gallons me moo ki mukene.

Tye gin marac atika, kombedi, ni rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ma pe ki gum ni omyero ki lok lok ma mako ler ma ki lwongo ni harelipped. Ka ma opoto iye ni tye fut acel ki borre. Oromo bedo ni min latin ni i kare mo ma pire tek onwongo tye ka wot ki yeya i dog nam me Peru, i kare ma oyengyeng oweko dog nam oyabe. I kom dog man, ma calo i kom doggola ma mol, dong wa donyo i dog. I kom lok na ka onongo atye i Mackinaw, onongo myero ater man calo dul ma i ot pa jo India. Rwot maber! man aye yo ma Yona owoto kwede? Wi ot ni borre romo fut apar wiye aryo, ki tye ka ngwec i tung cinge ma lamal, ma calo tye gin ma ki lwongo ni ridge-pole kunnu; nongo jami ma ki lwongo ni ribbed, arhed, hairy sides, nyutu botwa ki jami ma nen maber, nucu ma ocung, ma nen calo scimetar ma ki lwongo ni whalebone, waci miya adek i tung acel, ma cung i kom dul kom ma malo onyo cogo wic, gi yubu jami ma ki lwongo ni Venetian blinds ma dong ki waco gii kama pat. Dog cogo magi tye ki yer, ma Right Whale tiyo kwede me kwanyo pii, ki ma en gwoko rec ma tidi, ka oyabe doge en kato ki i nam me brit i kare me cam. I kin dul kom ma ki lwongo ni cogo, kit ma gicung kwede i kit ma gi tye kwede, tye lanyut mogo ma nen maber, ma guro, ma obale, ki ma opong, ma dano mogo ma gi kwano rec ni gi kwano mwaka pa ler ni, ma calo mwaka pa yat oak ki dul ne ma rwate. Kadi bed ni lok adaa pa gin ni tye ka mabor ki nyutu, ento tye ki jami ma ki poro ki gin ma twero time. I yoo mo keken, ka wa ye, omyera wa mi mwaka ma dit bot ler ma ki lwongo ni Right Whale ma loyo ma nen calo ber.

I kare macon, nen calo onongo tye jami mapol ma mako jami ma ki lwongo ni blinds. Lawot mo acel i Purchas olwongo gi ni "yer wic" ma tye i dog rec ma ki lwongo ni whale;* ngat mukene ni, "yer wic opego"; lacoo me adek ma otii i Hackluyt tiyo ki leb maber ni: "Tye ler ma romo miya aryo ki pyerabic ma dongo i tung cinge ma malo, ma gicung i kom leb i tung cinge acel acel."

*Man poyo wiwa ni rec ma ki lwongo ni Right Whale tye ki yer wic, onyo yer wic, ma tye ki yer wic manok ma nen calo tye i dul ma malo me agiki me doge ma piny. I kare mogo dul kom eni miyo nen calo lakwo i wange ma nen calo tye ki woro.

Kit ma dano weng ngeyo kwede, "yer opego," "lak," "yer," "wang," onyo gin mo kiken ma imito, miyo bot mon bongo gi ki jami mukene ma weko gi bedo matek. Ento i lok man, miti ni obedo ka rweny woko. I kare pa Queen Anne ma cogo onongo tye i deyo ne, ler ma ki lwongo ni farthingale onongo tye i kare meno. Ki kit ma mon macon ni giwoto kwede ki yomcwiny, kadi bed ni i dog rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, kit ma itwero wacone kwede; kadi bed kumeno, i kare me lwok, ki tam ma pe ki tam, wan i kare ni wa ngweco i te lak acel-lu pi gwok; umbrella ni obedo kema ma ki keto i kom cogo acel-lu.

Ento kombedi wi myero owil i kom jami ma ki lwongo ni blinds ki whiskers pi kare manok, ki, cung i dog Right Whale, nen piny ma orumowa odoco. Ka ineno jami ma ki yubu ki cogo ni ma ki yubu maber, tika onongo pe itamo ni itye i kin jami ma ki lwongo ni Haarlem organ, ki ineno i kom paip ne ma romo alip mapol? Pi yubu kapet i kom dul kom wa tye ki kapet ma ki yubu ki Turkey ma yom loyo-leb, ma ki keto, ma calo, i ngom me dog. Tye ma pek ki yom, ki twero poko ne ka ki tingo ne i wi yeya. Leb man ma tye i nyimwa; i neno ma okato omyera awaci ni onwongo obedo gin ma ki lwongo ni six-barreler; ma en aye ni, obi miini wel moo ma rom ki meno.

Ma peya man, omyera obed ni ineno lok adaa ma acako kwede- ni rec ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale ki Right Whale ni tye ki wic ma pat pat. Me tyeko lok, ki dong: i Right Whale's pe tye pii mo madit me pii; pe ki lak ler ma ki lwongo ni ivory; pe bor, lak ma tidi ma tye piny, ma calo pa rec ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale. Ki i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale pe tye gin mo ma pe nen maber; pe ki dog piny madit; ki pe tye gin mo ki leb. Dok, rec ma ki lwongo ni Right Whale tye ki dog pii aryo, Sperm Whale tye ki acel keken.

Nen i agiki ne, kombedi, i kom wic magi ma ki lwongo ni hooded hoods, nongo gi tye ka butu kacel; pien ngat acel obi cito piny, ma pe ki coyo, i nam; mukene ni pe obi bedo pi kare malac me lubo.

In itwero neno kit ma ler ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale nen kwede kunu? En tye kit meno ma en oto kwede, ento mogo ma nen calo tye ka rweny woko. Atamo ni wic ne ma lac ni opong ki ling ma nen calo i dye tim, ma ki nywalo ki i tam ma pe paro pi too. Ento nen kit ma wic mukene ni nen kwede. Nen leb ma piny ma mwonya ni, ma ki diyo labongo miti i kom dul kom ma ki lwongo ni vessel, wek omak doge matek. Tika wic man weng pe nen calo tye ka lok i kom yub madit me tic i kom to? Rec man ma ki lwongo ni Right Whale atamo ni obedo Stoic; ler ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale, ngat ma lubo Plato, ma twero bedo ni otero Spinoza i kare ma en dong oti woko.





DUL ME 76. Laco ma ki lwongo ni Battering-Ram.

Ma peya i weko, pi kare manok, wi ler ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale, amito ni in, macalo ngat ma ngeyo lok kom kom dano, ibed ki lok ma yot- tutwalle i lok i kom dul kome ma i nyime, i kit ma ki coko kwede jami ducu. Amito ni ibed ka kwedo ne kombedi ki tam me yubu piri keni gin mo ma pe ki medo, ma ryek ma twero bedo ki teko me lweny ma twero bedo tye kunu. Kany tye gin ma pire tek; pien myero i lok lok man maber ki in kekeni, onyo ibed ngat ma pe ye i kom gin mo ma kelo lworo, ento pi gin ma pe tye ada, gwok nyo ka mo keken ma ki nongo i tekwaro ma ki coyo.

In ineno ni i kit ma ler ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale tye ka kwang kwede, wiye wiye nen calo tye ma ocung i pii; in ineno ni dul ma piny me anyim ni opoto cen cen, me miyo kabedo me dwogo cen bot dul mabor ma nongo lak ma piny ma nen calo boom; ineno ni dog tye i te wic, i yo acel-lu, ada, calo dog ni tye i te dogi. Medo ikom meno ineno ni rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ni pe ki ume ma woko; ki ni ume ma en tye kwede- bur me doge- tye i wiye; ineno ni wange ki ite tye i nget wiye, ma romo acel me adek me borre ki anyim. Pi meno, omyera ibed ni dong iniang ni nyim wic pa ler ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale ni tye cel ma otoo, ma pe nen, labongo dul kom mo kiken onyo gin mo ma nen maber. Medo ikom meno, in dong myero itam ni i kom dul ma lapiny, ma piny, ma odok cen i nyim wic, aye tye ki cogo mo manok; ki pi nio ka i oo cok ki fut pyera aryo ki i nyimi ma i oo i dongo pa wic ni weny. Pi meno dul kom ma pe ki cogo ni weng obed calo wad acel. Me agiki ne, kadi bed kumeno, ma calo ma ki bi nyute cok cok ni, jami ma tye iye tye iye moo ma goro loyo; ento, in dong myero inge kit ma gin ni tye kwede ma tye ka keto jami ma nen calo dako. I kabedo mogo mukato angec atito boti kit ma moo pa rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ni umo kwede kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, kit ma kome umo kwede nyig yat ma ki lwongo ni orange. Kit meno ki wic; ento ki apoka poka eni: i kom wic, envelope ni, kadi bed ni pe pek tutwal, tye ma tek ma pe ki cogo, ma ngat mo keken ma pe omako ne twero byeko. Gin ma ki lwongo ni harpoon ma tek loyo, lance ma bit loyo ma ki tiyo kwede ki cing dano ma tek loyo, dwogo cen ki iye. Tye calo wic pa rec ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whale ni ki yubu ki tyen aguragura. An pe atamo ni gin mo ma mako cwinya tye iye.

Tam i komi bene i kom gin mukene. Ka dano aryo madongo, ma opong ki jami me India gubi rwate ki guro i kom luwot gi i dog nam, ngo ma lutic me yeya ni timo? Pe gi cung i kin gii, i kare ma gi rwate, gin mo kiken ma tek, ma calo nyonyo onyo bawo. Ku, gi gwoko kunu gin ma dit, ma rwate ki bongo ma ki lwongo ni tow ki cork, ma ki umu ki del ler dyang ma pek ki tek loyo. En ki tek cwiny ki labongo awano omako jam ma onongo obi turo jami gi ma ki lwongo ni oaken handspikes ki iron crow-bars. Ki kene man nyutu lok ma nen ma atye ka wot iye. Ento medo i kom lok man, obino i koma, ni calo rec ma rwate ki gin ma ki lwongo ni swimming bladder i igi, ma twero, ki miti, me dongo onyo dwoko piny; ki ma calo Sperm Whale, kit ma angeyo kwede, pe ki jami ma kit meno i kome; ka kiporo, bene, kit ma en dong obolo kwede wiye piny i te pii, ki oyot oyot kwang ki wiye malo ki i pii; ka kiporo ki kit ma envelope ne twero mol kwede ma pe ki gengo; neno kit ma iye ma pat pat ma tye i wiye; obino i koma, awaco, ni jami ma ki lwongo ni mystical lung-celled honeycombs ma tye kunu ni twero bedo ki gin mo ma onongo pe ki ngeyo ki ma onongo pe ki tamo ni tye ki wat ki yamo ma woko, wek gubed ki kero me dongo ki dwoko piny pa yamo. Ka tye kit meno, tam i kom teko ma pe kitwero juku ne, ma gin ma pe kitwero mako ki ma balo jami weny gi konyo iye.

Kombedi, lanyut. Labongo bal mo, tye ka diyo cel ma otoo, ma pe kitwero turo, ma pe kitwero balo, ki gin ma tye ka mol i iye; tye ka kwang i nge ne jami mapol makwo, ma ki twero byeko calo bao ma ki keto i kom gii- ki gii; ki gin weng gi winyo miti acel, ma calo ler ma tidi loyo. Pi meno ka atye ka tito boti jami ma pat pat ki rwom me teko ma tye ka nen i kom ler man ma dit ni; ka abi nyuti jami mogo ma en otimo ma pe ki ngeyo; Ageno ni ibi bedo ka kwero jami ma pe ingeyo, ki ibi bedo atera me lubu lok man; ni kadi bed ni Sperm Whale oyabo yoo ma okato ki i Isthmus me Darien, ki orubu Atlantic ki Pacific, in pe ibi tingo yec wic ni malo. Pien ka in pe itye ki rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, in ibedo ngat ma maro lok ada. Ento lok ada ma nen maber obedo gin ma ler ma ki lwongo ni salamander giants romo rwate kwede; kit ma kare ni nok kwede pi lutela me lobe mukene? Ngo ma otimme i kom awobi ma goro ma tye ka kwanyo bongo pa lubanga ma lworo i Lais?
