DUL KWAN ME 89. Rec ma ki mako oyot ki rec ma ki mako.

Lok ma mako waif ki waif-poles i dul me agiki ento acel, mito ni ki lok i kom cik ki cik ma kwako mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, ma waif twero bedo lanyut ki lanyut madit.

Pol kare time ni ka yeya mapol tye ka wot kacel, rec ma ki lwongo ni whale twero nongo peko ki yeya acel, ki dong ongweco, ki dong neko ki yeya mukene; ki kany tye ki jami mapol ma pe rwate, ma weng gi tye ka nywako i gin acel ma pire tek ni. Me labolle,—i nge ngwec matek ki mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, kome twero gonyo ki i yeya pi yamo magwar; ki ka cito ka mabor me cito tung bot yamo, ki mako ki lakwat rec me aryo, ma, i yo ma mwol, otelo ne maber, labongo peko me kwo onyo cinge. Pi meno lok ma kelo lworo ki lweny pol kare bino bedo tye i kin ludobo rec, ka onongo pe tye cik mo ma ki coyo onyo ma pe ki coyo, ma rwate ki dano weny, ma pe ki pyem iye ma tiyo i kom lok weny.

Twero bedo ni cik acel keken me mako rec ma ki yer ki cik, onwongo tye pa Holland. Ki moko ni States-General i mwaka AD 1695. Ento kadi bed ni pe tye lobo mukene ma onongo tye ki cik mo ma ki coyo me mako rec, ento lukwat rec me Amerika gubedo lutela gi ki lupilida i lok man. Gin gu miiyo yoo ma pi rwate ma okato Justinian's Pandects ki By-laws pa Chinese Society for the Suppression of Meddling with other people's Business. Ee; cik magi ki twero coyo gi i kom cente pa Queen Anne, onyo i kom gin ma ki lwongo ni harpoon, ki ki ruko i ngut, pi meno gi tidi.

II. Rec ma ki lwongo ni Loose-Fish obedo gin maber pi ngat mo kiken ma twero mako ne oyotoyot.

Ento gin ma tye ka kelo peko i kom coc man maber ni tye kit ma en tidi kwede, ma mito lok mapol me gonyo ne.

Mukwongo: rec ma ki lwongo ni Fast-Fish obedo ngo? Rec ma kwo onyo otoo tye ka ngwec oyoto, ka ki keto i yeya ma tye iye, ki gin mo keken ma ngat ma tye iye onyo dano ma tye iye twero doro,- gin ma ki lwongo ni mast, oar, nine-inch cable, telegraph wire, onyo strand of cobweb, gin weng rom. I yo acel-lu rec bene ngweco oyotoyot ka tye ki waif, onyo lanyut mo keken ma ki ngeyo me bedo kwede; ka dul ma tye ka kwero ni nyutu teko gi i kare mo keken me tero ne kacel, kacel ki miti gi me timo ne.

Magi obedo lok me cayan; ento lok pa lumony ma ki lwongo ni whalemen ni kare mukene bedo ki lok matek ki koko matek- Coke-upon-Littleton me cing. Adaa, i kin jo ma kit gi atir ki ma ki woro gi ma ki lwongo ni whalemen, ki miyo twero pi lok ma pat pat, ma twero bedo gin marac atika pi ngat acel me waco ni en tye ki whale ma ngat mukene oryemo onyo oneko. Ento jo mukene pe gi tye ki cwiny me mwolo.

Mwaki pyerabic mukato angec onongo tye lok mo ma mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whale-trover ma ki doto i England, ma lupilida ni gu waco ni inge ngwec matek i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whale i nam me tung kumalo; ki i kare ma gin (lupilida) gunongo adwogi maber i mako rec ni; i agiki ne, pi peko me kwo gi, ki miito gi me weko pe yeya gi keken, ento yeya gi kikome. I agiki ne jo ma kidoto gi (lutic me yeya mukene) gubino ki rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, gu goyo, gu neko, gu mako, ki i agiki gu kwanyo ne i nyim wang jo ma okelo koko ni. Ki i kare ma dano ma kidoto gi ni ki juku gi, ladit gi obolo cinge i lak dano ma kidoto ni, ki omoko tam gi ni pi lok ma en otimo ni, en dong obi gwoko gii, jami lweny, ki yeya, ma onwongo odong i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whale i kare ma ki mako gi. Pi meno lupilida ni gu doto pi nongo wel rec ma ki lwongo ni whale, line, harpoons, ki yeya gi.

Ngo ma obedo cik me niyee i kom gi ma pat ki rec ma ki lwongo ni Loose-Fish? Ngo ma bot dano ma gi loko lok ma mako lok me cato wil i yoo ma pi opore obedo tam pa dano ma gi tamo ento Loose-Fish? DUL ME 92. Ambergris.

I kare ni yat ma ki lwongo ni ambergris ni obedo gin ma kelo miti, ki pire tek calo gin ma ki cato, ma i mwaka 1791 ngat mo ma ki nywalo i Nantucket ma ki lwongo ni Captain Coffin ki penyo en i bar me ot moko cik me England i kom lok meno. Pi i kare meno, ki adaa nio wa i kare ma okato, kama yat ma ki lwongo ni ambergris oa ki iye onwongo tye, calo yat ma ki lwongo ni amber kikome, ma obedo peko bot lupwonye. Kadi bed ni lok ni ambergris obedo leb French ma ki lwongo ni gray amber, ento jami aryo ni pat pat. Pi amber, kadi bed ni kare mukene ki nongo i dog nam, bene ki nongo i ngom mogo mabor, kun ambergris pe ki nongo i nam keken. Medo ikom meno, amber obedo gin ma tek, nen, goro, pe ki ngwec, ma ki tiyo kwede me yubu dog ki paip, me yubu jami me deyo ki jami me deyo; ento ambergris tye mayom, tye ki moo, ki ngwece kur ki ngwece kur, ma pol kare ki tiyo kwede i yubu moo, i pastiles, tara ma wel ne tek, jami ma ki lwongo ni hair-powders, ki pomatum. Dano me Turkey tiyo kwede me tedo, ki bene gitero i Mecca, pi gin acel ma kitero ki odok eceta i St. Peter's i Rome. Lucat kongo vino mogo gi keto nyig mogo manok i claret, me miiyo mit ne.

Nga ma obi tamo, kono, ni mon ki coo ma kit meno omyera gubed ki jami ma ki nongo i ic pa rec ma kome lit! Ento tye kit meno. Ki bot jo mogo, ambergris ki tamo ni en aye okelo, ki bot jo mukene adwogi ne, me dyspepsia i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whale. Kit me cango two ma ki lwongo ni dyspepsia ni onwongo tek me waco, kono ka ki miiyo yadi pa Brandreth ma romo adek onyo angwen, ki ingeye gi kato ki i yoo me peko, ma calo lutic ma gi tiyo i wango got.

Wiya owil me waco ni onongo kinongo i ambergris ni, jami mogo matek, ma rwate, ma tye ki cogo, ma i acaki Stubb otamo ni twero bedo bute me twer pa lutic me yeya; ento lacen ote nen ni gin onwongo pe obedo gin mo makato cogo ler ma ki lwongo ni squid ma ki yubu i yoo meno.

Kombedi ma bal pa ambergris ma ngwece kur ni omyera ki nong i cwiny gin ma obale ni; man pe gin mo? Tam kong i kom lok pa Lakwena Paulo i Jo Korint, i kom bal ki bal ma pe balo; kit ma gicoyowa kwede i lewic, ento gicerowa i deyo. Ki bene po i wi lok pa Paracelsus ma mako ngo ma miyo musk maber loyo. Ki dong pi iwek lok ada ni i kin jami ma pe mit, pii ma ki lwongo ni Cologne, i kare ma ki yubu ne, en aye rac loyo.

Amito tyeko dul lok man ki lok ma malo ni, ento pe atwero, pi lworo na me kwero lok ma ki waco i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whalemer, ki ma, i tam pa jo mogo ma dong gitye ki tam ma pe opore, ki twero tero calo gin ma ki waco i kom rec aryo pa la France. Ka mukene i buk man lok ma ki waco ni lok goba ni ki nyutu ni tic me mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whale tye i kin tic ma pe ki yubu maber. Ento tye gin mukene ma myero ki kwero. Gi waco ni rec ma ki lwongo ni whale weny gi ngwec marac. Man dong lok man ma obale ni oyabe nining?

Atamo ni, ni ki twero lubo ne i kare ma yeya ma mako rec ma ki lwongo ni Greenland obino i London, mwaki ma oo cencwari aryo mukato angec. Pien lumony ma ki lwongo ni whalemers ni pe gu temo moo gi i nam kit ma yeya me Southern ni gi timo kwede; ento poko moo ma nyen ni i dul matino tino, ki keto i bur ma ki keto i pii madongo, ki tero gang kit meno; kare manok me kare i nam ma ngiic ni, ki kot ma cwer matek ma gi nongo, ma gengo yoo mukene. Atamo bene, ni bal man marac i kom lukwat rec ma ki lwongo ni whalers ni twero bedo ni ki keto i dog nam me Greenland, i kare mukato angec, pa caro mo ma ki lwongo ni Dutch ma ki lwongo ni Schmerenburgh onyo Smeerenberg, ma nying me agiki ni obedo ma lapwony Fogo Von Slack otiyo kwede, i ticce madit i kom Smells, buk ma ki kwano i kom lok meno. Onwongo obedo ka gwoko keno, jami ma ki keto iye moo, ki jami ma ki keto iye moo; ki i kare ma tic ni tye ka wot maber onongo pe kelo ngwec mo ma mit. Ki gin ma otimme atura, yeya ni kikome olaro en; ento cake i cawa meno laco matidi ni owoto i wi yeya ma laming; ma calo meno, gin owaco ni en tye. Nam onongo tye ka gwoko kome ma lamal ni malo, ento obalo cwinye ma lamal. Pe oto woko, kadi bed kumeno. Ento ki tero piny makwo i rwom ma lamal, kan ma jami ma pat pat ma pe ki ngeyo ma tye ka ngwec i nyim wange ma pe nen; ki laco ma lamar, ryeko, onyutu jami ma en ogwoko; ki i kin jami ma yomo yic, ma cwiny gi pe,, ma pe gik, Pip oneno jami mapol, Lubanga ma tye kabedo weny, ler ma ki lwongo ni coral, ma ki i wi pii ma opong ki jami ma nen maber. En oneno tyen Lubanga i kom gin ma ki lwongo ni loom, ki oloko ne; ki pi meno lutic luwote i yeya olwonge ni laming. Pi meno, goro pa dano obedo tam me polo; ki ka aa ki i tam ma mako dano, dano bino i agiki i tam me polo, ma, me tam, obedo gin ma pe rwate ki ma kelo lworo; ki lonyo onyo peko, winyo calo pe ki juku, pe paro calo Lubanga ne.

Pi jo mukene, pe i bal i kom Stubb matek. Gin ni tye ma rwate i tic me mako rec ni; ki i dul ma lubo lok ni, ki bi neno ngo ma calo weko gin ma otimme i koma.
