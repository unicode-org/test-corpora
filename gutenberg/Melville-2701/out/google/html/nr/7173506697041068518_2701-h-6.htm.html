ISAHLUKO 32. I-Cetology.

Sele sithunyelwe ngesibindi emanzini atjhingelako; kodwana msinyana sizokulahleka ebujameni bayo obungenamkhawulo, obungenamatheku. Ngaphambi kobana lokho kwenzeke; ngaphambi kobana isikhumba se-Pequod esinamakhula sigijime eceleni kwesikhumba se-leviathan; ekuthomeni kuhle ukutjheja indaba eqakatheke khulu ekuzwisiseni ngokuthokoza okupheleleko kwezambulo ezikhethekileko ze-leviathanic kanye nama-allusions wemihlobo yoke ezakulandela.

Mbukiso ohlelekileko we-whale emihlobeni yayo ebanzi, engingathanda ukuyibeka phambi kwakho. Kodwana akusiwo umsebenzi olula. Ukuhlukaniswa kweengcenye zesiphithiphithi, akukho okuncani lapha kukhulunywa ngakho. Lalela lokho okubekwe phasi ziiphathimandla ezihle khulu nezakamuva.

"Alikho igatja le-Zoology elibandakanyeka khulu njengalelo elibizwa nge-Cetology," kutjho u-Captain Scoresby, AD 1820.

"Akusiwo umnqopho wami, ngathana bekusemandleni wami, ukungena ekuphenyweni ngendlela yamambala yokuhlukanisa i-cetacea ibe ziinqhema nemindeni. * * * Ukudideka okupheleleko kukhona hlangana nezazi-mlando zesilwana lesi" (umkhomo wesidoda), kutjho uDokotela ohlinzako u-Beale, AD 1839.

"Ukungafaneleki ukwenza irhubhululo lethu emanzini angazwisisekiko." "Iveli engenakungena evala ilwazi lethu nge-cetacea." “Insimu egcwele ameva.” "Zonke iinkomba ezingakapheleli kodwana zisiza ukuhlunguphaza thina bafundi bemvelo."

Nanyana kunjalo, nanyana ilwazi lamambala lincani, kodwana iincwadi zinengi; begodu njalo ngezinga elincani, nge-cetology, nofana isayensi yemikhomo. Manengi amadoda, amancani namakhulu, amadala namatjha, abantu bendawo nabasebenzi bemikhumbi, abatlole ngokubanzi nofana kancani, ngomkhomo. Iinzathu lapho u-Linnæus bekangakghona ukuqotjha khona imikhomo emanzini, uthi: "Ngebanga lehliziyo yayo efuthumeleko, amaphaphu wayo, amajwabu wayo atjhingako, iindlebe zawo ezinemigodi, i-penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem," begodu ekugcineni, "ex lege naturæ jure merittoque." Kodwana ngamafuphi, ngilezi: amaphaphu neengazi ezifuthumeleko; kuthi, zoke ezinye iimfesi azinamaphaphu begodu zineengazi ezibandako.

Okulandelako: sizoyihlathulula njani umkhomo, ngezinto zangaphandle ezibonakalako, ukwenzela bona siyibize ngokukhanyako isikhathi esizako? Ukufitjhani,-ke, umkhomo mfesi ophumako onomsila ovundlileko . Lapho unaye. Nanyana kunjalo, ihlathululo leyo ngumphumela wokuzindla okwandisiweko. I-walrus ikhupha umlomo njengomkhomo, kodwana i-walrus akusiyo ifesi, ngombana ihlala emanzini. Kodwana igama lokugcina lencazelo lisaqinileko, njengombana lihlanganiswe nelokuthoma. Pheze woke umuntu kufanele bona unanzelele bona yoke iimfesi ezijayelekileko ebantwini abahlala ephasini azinamsila ophaphathekileko, kodwana othe ngqo, nofana ophakama phasi. Nanyana, hlangana neemfesi eziphuphumako umsila, nanyana ungaba nobujamo obufanako, uthatha ubujamo obuvundlileko.

Ngokwehlathululo engenhla yokuthi umkhomo uyini, angikhiphi nanyana ngisiphi isidalwa saselwandle ebegade sikhonjwe ngomkhomo ngabantu be-Nantucket abanolwazi oluhle khulu; begodu, ngakelinye ihlangothi, ungahlanganisi nanyana ngiyiphi iimfesi ebegade zithathwa njengezivela eenarheni zangaphandle.* Ngalokho, zoke iinhlambi ezincani, eziphumako, nezinomsila ovundlileko kufanele zifakwe ehlelweni le-Cetology. Nje-ke, kuza ukwahlukana okukhulu kwayo yoke imikhomo.

*Ngiyazi bona bekube nje, iimfesi ezibizwa ngokuthi ma-Lamatins nama-Dugongs (i-Pig-fish kanye ne-Sow-fish of the Coffins of Nantucket) zifakwe ziingcwethi ezinengi zemvelo hlangana nemikhomo. Kodwana njengombana iimfesi zefarigi lezi zinomsindo, zideleleka, zicashe khulu emilonyeni yemilambo, begodu zidla ukudla okumanzi, begodu khulukhulu njengombana zingaphumi, ngiyakuphika ukuqinisekiswa kwazo njengemikhomo; begodu babanike amapasipoti wabo bona batjhiye uMbuso we-Cetology.

AMAFOLIYO. Hlangana nalokhu ngifaka hlangana izahluko ezilandelako:—I. I -Sperm Whale ; II. umkhomo wesidla ; III. i -Fin-Back Whale ; IV. i- Hump-backed Whale ; V. i -Razor Back Whale ; VI. i- Sulphur Bottom Whale .

INCWADI I. ( I-Folio ), ISAHLUKO I. ( I-Sperm Whale ).—Umkhomo lo, hlangana namaNgisi wekadeni owaziwa ngokuthi yi-Trumpa whale, kanye ne-Physeter whale, kanye ne-Anvil Headed whale, yi-Cachalot yamanje yama-French, kanye ne-Pottsfich yama-German, kanye ne-Macrocelus yama-Longphalus yama-Words. Ngaphandle kokungabaza, ngumuntu omkhulu kunabo boke abahlala ephasini; imikhomo enamandla khulu ongahlangana nayo; ubukhulu khulu; begodu kokugcina, okuqakatheke khulu kwezokurhweba; nguye kwaphela isidalwa lapho into eqakathekileko, i-spermaceti, itholwa kiyo. Boke ubujamo bakhe obuhlukileko, kezinye iindawo ezinengi, buzokukhuliswa. Ngokuyinhloko ngebizo lakhe engifanele ngenze. Nakuqalwa ngokwesayensi, akuzwakali. Eminyakeni emakhulu eyadlulako, lokha i-Sperm whale gade ingaziwa ngokupheleleko ngobuntu bayo, begodu lokha amafutha wayo atholakala ngephutha emfesini ebhajwe; ngalezo zinsuku i-spermaceti, bekungabonakala ngasuthi, bekuthathwa bona ithathwe esidalweni esifana naleso esaziwa e-England nge-Greenland nofana i-Right Whale. Kwaba mbono godu, bona i-spermaceti efanako bekuyihlaya elirhabisako le-Greenland Whale elivezwa lilunga lokuthoma legama. Ngaleso sikhathi, godu, i-spermaceti beyitlhogeka khulu, ingasetjenziselwa ukukhanya, kodwana njengesithambiso nemithi kwaphela. Bekufuze itholwe ebathengisi beendakamizwa njengombana namhlanjesi uthenga i-ounces ye-rhubarb. Lokha, njengombana ngibona, ngokukhamba kwesikhathi, ubujamo bamambala be-spermaceti baziwa, ibizo layo lokuthoma belisagcinwa babathengisi; ngokungangabazeki ukuthuthukisa inani layo ngombono oqakatheke khulu ngokutlhogeka kwayo. Njeke-ke ibizo kufanele bona ekugcineni lanikelwa umkhomo lapho i-spermaceti le yavela khona kwamambala.

INCWADI I. ( Ifoliyo ), ISAHLUKO II. ( Right Whale ).—Ngokuthileko lokhu kuhlonitjhwa khulu kuma-leviathan, ngombana ngiwo wokuthoma ukuzingelwa babantu. Ikhiqiza i-atikili eyaziwa ngokuthi yi-whalebone nofana i-baleen; kanye namafutha aziwa ngokukhethekileko ngokuthi "amafutha we-whale," okuyinto ephasi kwezokurhweba. Hlangana nabadobi, ubizwa ngokungakhethi ngazo zoke iinhloko ezilandelako: Umkhomo; i-Greenland Whale; umkhomo omnyama; i-Great Whale; i-True Whale; umkhomo wesidla. Kunokungacaci kuhle malungana nobunjalo bemihlobo ebhabhadiswe khulu. Yini-ke umkhomo, engiwufaka emhlobeni wesibili we-Folios yami? Yi-Great Mysticetus yezazi zemvelo zamaNgisi; i-Greenland Whale yama-whalemen we-England; i-Baleine Ordinaire yama-whalemen we-France; i-Growlands Walfish yama-Sweden. Yimkhomo eminyakeni engaphezu kwamakhulu amabili edlulileko ebegade izingelwa ma-Dutch nama-English elwandle le-Arctic; mkhomo abadobi be-America ebegade bawuphishekela elwandle le-India, e-Brazil Banks, e-Nor' West Coast, nezinye iindawo ezihlukahlukeneko zephasi, ezikhethwe ziiNdawo zokuTjhiya iimfesi ze-Right Whale.

Abanye benza kwangathi babona umehluko phakathi komkhomo we-Greenland wamaNgisi nomkhomo wesokudla wamaMelika. Kodwana bayavumelana kuhle kizo zoke iimpawu zabo ezikulu; begodu akukavezwa iqiniso elilodwa eliqinileko elingasekela umehluko omkhulu. Kungenxa yokuhlukaniswa okungapheliko okusekelwe emahlukeni ongapheliko, ukuthi eminye iminyango yomlando wemvelo iba yinkimbinkimbi. Umkhomo wesidla uzokuphathwa kwenye indawo ngobude obuthileko, ngokuya ngokucacisa umkhomo wesidoda.

INCWADI I. ( Ifoliyo ), ISAHLUKO III. ( I-Fin-Back ).—Ngaphasi kwehloko le ngicabanga bona isilwane esikhulu, ngamagama ahlukahlukeneko we-Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, no-Long-John, sele sibonwe pheze kiwo woke amalwandle begodu kuvamise ukuba mkhomo one-jet yayo ekude evamise ukuchazwa babantu abawela i-Atlantic, e-New York packet-tracks. Ngobude abufinyelelako, nange-baleen yakhe, i-Fin-back ifana nomkhomo wesokudla, kodwana inobubanzi obuncani, begodu inombala okhanyako, otjhidela emnqumeni. Iindebe zakhe ezinkulu ziveza ubujamo obufana nekheyibuli, obukhiwe ziinhluthu ezinkulu ezihlanganako, ezitjhingako. Isici sakhe esikhulu esihlukileko, i-fin, lapho athatha khona ibizo lakhe, kanengi kuba yinto ebonakalako. Isiphini lesi singamamitha amathathu nofana amane ubude, sikhula ngokujameleko ukusuka engcenyeni yangemuva yangemuva, sinejamo elifana ne-engele, begodu sinesiphetho esibukhali khulu. Ngitjho nanyana kungekho enye ingcenye encani yesidalwa ebonakalako, i-fin le ehlukanisiweko, ngezinye iinkhathi, izokubonakala iphuma phezulu. Lokha ilwandle nalithulileko, begodu litshwaywe kancani ngamagagasi ayindilinga, begodu isiphini esifana ne-gnomon sisukume siphose amathunzi phezu kwendawo eshwabeneko, kungenzeka kucatjangwe bona isiyingi samanzi esiyizungezileko sifana nedayali, ngesitayela sayo kanye namagagasi we-awa aqoshwe kiyo. Kuleyo diyali ka-Ahazi isithunzi sivame ukubuyela emuva. I-Fin-Back ayithandi abantu. Ubonakala azonda imikhomo, njengombana amanye amadoda azonda abantu. Unamahloni khulu; uhlala wedwa; ukukhuphukela phezulu ngokungakalindeleki emanzini akude khulu begodu anobujamo obumbi khulu; i-jet yakhe eqondileko neyodwa ephakemeko ephakama njengomkhonto omude ongathandi abantu phezu kwethafa elingenalutho; unesiphiwo samandla amangalisako nejubane lokubhukuda, ukujamelana nakho koke ukuphishekela kwabantu; ileviyathani le ibonakala inguKayini oqotjhiweko nongahlulwako womhlobo wakhe, othwele itshwayo lakhe isitayela leso emhlane wakhe. Ngokuba ne-baleen emlonyeni wakhe, i-Fin-Back kwesinye isikhathi ifakwa ne-whale yesokudla, hlangana nomhlobo othiyori obizwa ngokuthi yi-Whalebone whales , okutjho bona, imikhomo ene-baleen. Kule mikhomo ebizwa nge-Whalebone, kubonakala ngasuthi kunemihlobo eminengi, inengi layo, nanyana kunjalo, eyaziwa kancani. Imikhomo enempumulo ebanzi kanye nemikhomo enomlomo; imikhomo eneenhloko ze-pike; imikhomo ehlanganisiweko; imikhomo enemihlathi engaphasi kanye nemikhomo enomhlathi, ngamabizo wabathiyi beenhlambi wemihlobo embalwa.

Malungana nesibizo se-“Whalebone whales,” kuqakathekile ukutjho, bona nanyana indlela enjalo ingaba lula ekwenzeni lula ukutjho imikhomo ethileko, kodwana kulilize ukulinga ukuhlukanisa okucacileko kwe-Leviathan, esekelwe phezu kwe-baleen, namkha i-hump, namkha i-fin, namkha i-teeth; kungakhathaliseki bona iingcenye ezitshwayiweko namkha iimfanelo zibonakala zitjhugululwe ngcono ukunikela isisekelo sehlelo elijayelekileko le-Cetology kunanoma ngikuphi okunye ukuhlukaniswa komzimba, okuvezwa mkhomo, emihlobeni yawo. Kwenzeka njani-ke? I-baleen, i-hump, i-back-fin, namazinyo; lezi zizinto ezihlukileko ezisakazwe ngokungakhethi hlangana nayo yoke imihlobo yemikhomo, ngaphandle kokuqalela bona kungaba yini ubujamo besakhiwo sazo kwezinye izinto eziqakatheke khulu. Ngalokho, i-sperm whale ne-humpbacked whale, enye nenye ine-hump; kodwana lapho ukufana kuyaphela. Bese, umkhomo ofanako one-humpback kanye nomkhomo we-Greenland, ngamunye wabo une-baleen; kodwana lapho godu ukufana kuyaphela. Begodu kuyafana nezinye iingcenye ezikhulunywe ngehla. Emihlobeni ehlukileko yemikhomo, yenza iinhlanganisela ezingakajayeleki; namkha, endabeni yokuthi nanyana ngiyiphi yazo ihlukaniswe, ukuzihlukanisa okungakajayeleki; njengokuphikisana nazo zoke iindlela ezijayelekileko ezakhiwe phezu kwesisekelo esinjalo. Edwaleni leli woke umuntu ofunda ngemvelo yemikhomo uhlukene.

Kodwana kungenzeka kucatjangwe bona, eencenyeni zangaphakathi zomkhomo, emzimbeni wayo—lapho, okungenani, sizokukghona ukuthola ukuhlukaniswa okufaneleko. Awa; ngiyiphi into, ngokwesibonelo, ekhona emzimbeni we-Greenland whale edlula ukudlula i-baleen yayo? Nanyana kunjalo sibonile bona nge-baleen yakhe akukghonakali ukuhlukanisa kuhle i-Greenland whale. Begodu nangabe wehlela emathunjini wama-leviathan ahlukahlukeneko, kubayini lapho ungazukufumana umehluko ingcenye yamashumi amahlanu etholakala kumhleli njengalezo zangaphandle esele zibalwe. Yini-ke eseleko? akukho okunye ngaphandle kokubamba imikhomo ngomzimba, ngomthamo wayo woke, begodu uyihlukanise ngesibindi ngaleyo ndlela. Begodu le yindlela ye-Bhibliographic lapha eyamukelwe; begodu ngiyo kwaphela engaphumelela, ngombana ngiyo kwaphela engenzeka. Ukuragela phambili.

INCWADI I. ( Ifoliyo ) ISAHLUKO IV. ( Hump Back ).—Umkhomo lo uvamise ukubonwa eligwini le-Amerikha elisenyakatho. Bekasolo athunjwa lapho, begodu adoselwe ethekwini. Unephakethe elikhulu kuye njengomthengisi; nofana ungambiza ngokuthi yiNdlovu ne-Castle whale. Nanyana kukuphi, ibizo lakhe elidumileko alimhlukanisi ngokwaneleko, ngombana i-sperm whale nayo ine-hump nanyana incani. Amafutha wakhe awaqakatheki kangako. Une-baleen. Ngumuntu othandeka khulu begodu unenhliziyo elula kiyo yoke imikhomo, wenza i-gay foam enengi namanzi amhlophe ukudlula nanyana ngiyiphi enye.

INCWADI I. ( Ifoliyo ), ISAHLUKO V. ( I-Razor Back ).—Ngomkhomo lo kuncani okwaziwako ngaphandle kwebizo lakhe. Ngimbone kude ne-Cape Horn. Ngokwemvelo yokuthatha umhlalaphasi, ubalekela bobabili abazingeli nezazi zefilosofi. Nanyana angasi ligwala, akakaze atjengise nanyana ngiyiphi ingcenye yakhe ngaphandle komgogodla wakhe, ophakama ngomrhoba omude obukhali. Myekele akhambe. Ngiyazi okuncani ngaye, begodu akekho omunye umuntu owaziko.

INCWADI I. ( Ifoliyo ), ISAHLUKO VI. ( Sulphur Bottom ).—Omunye umnumzana othatha umhlalaphasi, onesisu esifana nesibabule, ngokungangabazeki wathola ngokukhuhla amathayili we-Tartarian kwezinye zeendlela zakhe zokutshuza ezijulileko. Akavami ukubonwa; okungenani angikaze ngimbone ngaphandle kwamalwandle aseningizimu, begodu njalo kude khulu ukufunda ubuso bakhe. Akasoze ajahwa; bekazokubaleka ngeentambo zokukhamba ngeentambo. Iimmangaliso zitjelwa ngaye. Sawubona, i-Sulphur Bottom! Angikwazi ukutjho okunye okuliqiniso ngawe, namkha umNantucketer omdala khulu.

AMA-OCTAVOES.*—Lawa ahlanganisa imikhomo yobukhulu obuphakathi, hlangana nayo ekhona ingabalwa:—I., i -Grampus ; II., iNhlanzi emnyama ; III., i -Narwhale ; IV., Umtjhayeli ; V., uMbulali .

*Kuyacaca bona kubayini incwadi le yama-whale ingabizwa ngokuthi yi-Quarto. Ngombana, nanyana imikhomo ye-oda le, nanyana incani kunaleyo ye-oda langaphambilini, nanyana kunjalo igcina ukufana okulinganako nayo emfanekisweni, kodwana ivolumu ye-Quarto yomhlanganisi weencwadi ngendlela yayo elinganisiweko ayilondolozi ubujamo bevolumu ye-Folio, kodwana ivolumu ye-Octavo iyalondoloza.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO I. ( Grampus ).—Nanyana ifesi le, ephefumula khulu, namkha ukuvunguza, yenze isaga ebantwini bendawo, yaziwa khulu njengomuntu ohlala emanzini atjhingako, kodwana ayifakwa hlangana nemikhomo. Kodwana ngokuba nazo zoke iimpawu ezihlukileko ze-leviathan, inengi lezazi zemvelo zimqaphele njengomunye. Unobukhulu obulinganiselweko be-octavo, ubude busuka emafidini alitjhumi nahlanu ukuya kwamatjhumi amabili nahlanu, begodu ubukhulu obufanako obusekhalweni. Ubhukuda ngemihlambi; akasoze azingelwa qobe, nanyana amafutha wakhe amanengi khulu, begodu alungele ukukhanya. Ngabanye abadobi indlela yakhe ithathwa njengesibonakaliso sokuragela phambili komkhomo wesidoda esikhulu.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO II. ( Iimfesi eziNzima ).—Nginikela amagama wabadobi abadumileko kizo zoke iimfesi lezi, ngombana ngokuvamileko ngizo ezihle khulu. Lapho nanyana ngiliphi ibizo lingacaci namkha lingatjho litho, ngizokutjho njalo, begodu ngiphakamise elinye. Ngiyakwenza nje, ngithinta i-Black Fish, okubizwa ngokuthi, ngombana ubumnyama buyinto ejayelekileko kiyo yoke imikhomo. Njeke, mbize nge-Hyena Whale, nangabe uyathanda. Ukuthanda kwakhe ukudla kwaziwa khulu, begodu ngebanga lokuthi ama-engeli wangaphakathi wemilebe yakhe agobekele phezulu, uphethe ukumomotheka okungapheliko kwe-Mephistophelean ebusweni bakhe. Umkhomo lo ubude bulinganiselwa emafidini alitjhumi nathandathu nofana alitjhumi nabunane. Utholakala pheze kiwo woke ama-latitude. Unendlela engakajayeleki yokutjengisa isiphiko sakhe esigoqwe ngemuva lokha nakabhukudako, esifana nekhala lamaRoma. Lokha nabangasebenzi ngendlela ezuzisako, abazingeli be-sperm whale kwesinye isikhathi babamba i-Hyena whale, ukugcina ukunikelwa kwamafutha atjhiphileko womsebenzi wangekhaya—njengombana abanye abasebenzi bezindlu abasebenzako, ngaphandle komuntu, begodu bebodwa, batjhisa i-tallow engamnandi esikhundleni se-wax enuka kamnandi. Nanyana i-blubber yazo incani khulu, eminye yemikhomo le izokukhipha amalitha angaphezu kwamatjhumi amathathu wamafutha.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO III. ( I-Narwhale ), okutjho bona, umkhomo we-Nostril .—Esinye isibonelo somkhomo obizwa ngokumangalisako, oqanjwe njalo ngebanga lophondo lwakhe olungakajayeleki ekuthomeni ebegade lithathwa njengempumulo eneenhloko. Isilwana lesi singamamitha alitjhumi nathandathu ubude, kuthi iphondo laso lilinganiselwa emafidini amahlanu, nanoma amanye adlula amafidi alitjhumi, begodu afikelela emafidini alitjhumi nahlanu. Nasikhuluma ngokuqinileko, iphondo leli lizinyo elide, elikhula liphuma emhlathini ngomugqa ophasi kancani ukusuka evundlileko. Kodwana itholakala kwaphela ngehlangothini elibi, elinomphumela omumbi, okunikela umnikazi wayo into efana nendoda esebenzisa isandla sokhohlo. Kungaba budisi ukutjho bona uphondo lwendlovu namkha umkhonto lo uphendula umnqopho onjani. Akubonakali ngasuthi isetjenziswa njenge-blade ye-sword-fish ne-bill-fish; nanyana abanye amatilosi bangitjela bona i-Narwhale iyisebenzisa njenge-rake ekuphenduleni phasi kwelwandle ukuthola ukudla. U-Charley Coffin wathi gade isetjenziselwa ukubhoboza iqhwa; ngombana i-Narwhale, ikhuphukela phezulu kweLwandle le-Polar, begodu iyithole igcwele iqhwa, iphakamisa uphondo lwayo phezulu, begodu iphule. Kodwana angeze wakghona ukufakazela nanyana ngikuphi kwalokhu kuqagela bona kulungile. Umbono wami kukuthi, nanyana uphondo olulodwa lungasetjenziswa yi-Narwhale—nanyana kungaba njani—kungaba lula kuye bona abe nefolda yokufunda amaphetjhana. I-Narwhale engiyizwe ibizwa ngokuthi yi-Tusked whale, i-Horned whale, kanye ne-Unicorn whale. Ngokuqinisekileko usibonelo esihle se-Unicornism esitholakala pheze kiyo yoke imibuso yemvelo ephilako. Kubatloli abadala abathileko ngifumene bona uphondo lwe-unicorn ye-sea-unicorn esikhathini sakade beluthathwa njengomuthi omkhulu wokulwisana nobuthi, begodu ngalokho, ukulungiselela kwayo bekuletha intengo ekulu. Kwabuye kwavunjululwa kwaba yitswayi eliguquguqukako labafazi abaqulekako, ngendlela efanako neempondo zenyamazana eduna ezenziwa ngayo zibe yi-hartshorn. Ekuthomeni bekuthathwa njengento yokufuna ukwazi okukhulu. I-Black Letter ingitjela bona u-Sir Martin Frobisher ekubuyeni kwakhe ekhambweni, lokha i-Queen Bess nayiphakamisela isandla sayo samagugu kuye ngefasitela le-Greenwich Palace, njengombana umkhumbi wakhe onesibindi ukhamba ngehla nge-Thames; "lokha uMnumzana Martin nakabuya ekhambweni," kutjho u-Black Letter, "waguqa ngamadolo wanikela ubukhosi bakhe uphondo olude khulu lwe-Narwhale, egade ilengiswe isikhathi eside esigodlweni se-Windsor." Umtloli we-Ireland uthi u-Earl we-Leicester, aguqe ngamadolo, naye wanikela ubukhosi bakhe elinye iphondo, eliphathelene nesilwane sendawo semvelo ye-unicorn.

I-Narwhale inesithombe esihle khulu, esifana nengwe, inombala omhlophe njengebisi, inamabala amnyama ayindilinga namade. Amafutha wakhe aphezulu khulu, akhanya begodu alungile; kodwana kuncani, begodu akakavami ukuzingelwa. Utholakala khulu elwandle elizungezileko.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO IV. ( Umbulali ).—Ngomkhomo lo kuncani okwaziwako e-Nantucketer, begodu akukho litho elwazini lezemvelo. Ngokuya ngalokho engikubonile ngaye kude, ngingathi bekakhulu njenge-grampus. Uyisilwane khulu—umhlobo wefesi ye-Feegee. Kwesinye isikhathi uthatha imikhomo emikhulu ye-Folio ngomlomo, bese ulenga lapho njenge-leech, bekube kulapho isilwane esinamandla sikhathazeka khulu. Umbulali akazingelwa. Angikaze ngizwe bona unamafutha anjani. Kungathathwa ngaphandle kwebizo elinikelwe umkhomo lo, ngebanga lokungacaci kwawo. Ngombana soke singababulali, ephasini nelwandle; U-Bonapartes nama-Shark afakiwe.

INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), ISAHLUKO V. ( Umtjhayeli ).—Umnumzana lo udume ngomsila wakhe, awusebenzisa njenge-ferule ekutshayeni izitha zakhe. Ukhwela umkhomo we-Folio emhlana, begodu njengombana abhukuda, wenza indlela yakhe ngokumtjhwaba; njengombana abanye abotitjhere bezikolo bazwana ephasini ngendlela efanako. Kusese kuncani okwaziwako nge-Trasher kune-Killer. Bobabili babantu abaphula umthetho, ngitjho naselwandle olungenamthetho.

Iphetha njalo INCWADI YII. ( Octavo ), begodu ithoma INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ).

I-DUODECIMOES.—Lokhu kufaka hlangana imikhomo emincani. I. I-Huzza Porpoise. II. I-Algerine Porpoise. III. I-Mealy-mouthed Porpoise.

Kilabo abangakaze bafumane ithuba lokufunda indaba le, kungenzeka kubonakala kungakajayeleki, bona iimfesi ezingakavami ukudlula amafidi amane nofana amahlanu kufanele zihlanganiswe hlangana nama-WHALES—igama, elithi, ngomqondo odumileko, lihlala lidlulisela umqondo wobukhulu. Kodwana izidalwa ezibekwe phasi njenge-Duodecimoes ziimkhomo ezingenaphutha, ngokuya ngehlathululo yami yokuthi umkhomo uyini— okutjho bona ifesi ephuphumako, enomsila ovundlileko.

INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ), ISAHLUKO 1. ( I-Huzza Porpoise ).—Le yi-porpoise ejayelekileko etholakala ephasini loke. Ibizo livela kimi; ngombana kunemihlobo eminengi yama-porpoises, begodu kufuze kwenziwe okuthileko ukuzihlukanisa. Ngimbiza njalo, ngombana uhlale abhukuda eendaweni ezihlekisako, ezihlala ziziphosela ezulwini njengeenkepisi esiqubuthwini se-Fourth-of-July. Ukubukeka kwazo kuvame ukudunyiswa ngethabo mtjhayeli wesikepe. Zizele imimoya emihle, zihlala zivela emagagasini anomoya ukuya emoyeni. Ngamajaha ahlala aphila ngaphambi komoya. Zithathwa njengesibonakaliso senhlanhla. Nangabe wena ngokwakho ungajamelana nokuthokoza kathathu ngokubona iimfesi eziphilako, khona-ke izulu lizokusiza; umoya wokuthanda uZimu awukho kini. I-Huzza Porpoise edliswe kuhle, ekhulupheleko izokunikela igaloni elilodwa elihle lamafutha amahle. Kodwana uketshezi oluhle nobuthakathaka olukhutjhwe emihlathini yakhe luqakatheke khulu. Ifunwa hlangana nabathengisi bamagugu nabenzi bamawashi. Amatilosi ayifaka eenhlotsheni zawo. Inyama ye-porpoise idliwa kuhle, uyazi. Kungenzeka bona akhenge kufike engqondweni yakho bona i-porpoise iyakhipha umlomo. Kwamambala, umlomo wakhe mncani kangangobana awubonakali lula. Kodwana ngesikhathi esilandelako nawuthola ithuba, mqale; begodu uzokubona i-Sperm whale ekulu ngokwayo incani.

INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ), ISAHLUKO II. ( Algerine Porpoise ).—Ipirate. Kumbi khulu. Utholakala kwaphela, ngicabanga bona, e-Pacific. Mkhulu kancani kune-Huzza Porpoise, kodwana mkhulu khulu. Mthukuthelise, begodu uzokubophelela abe yi-shark. Ngiyehlisele yena kanengi, kodwana angikaze ngimbone athunjiwe.

INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ), ISAHLUKO III. ( I-Porpoise enomlomo we-Mealy ).—Umhlobo omkhulu khulu we-Porpoise; begodu itholakala kwaphela e-Pacific, njengombana kwaziwa. Ibizo lesiNgisi elilodwa, ebegade abizwa ngalo bekube nje, ngelabadobi—i-Right-Whale Porpoise, ngombana utholakala khulu eduze ne-Folio. Ngokwejamo, uhlukile ngezinga elithileko ku-Huzza Porpoise, ngokuba yi-girth engasiyindilinga begodu injabulisako; kwamambala, ungumuntu omuhle begodu unjengomuntu omnumzana. Akanawo amaphiko emhlane wakhe (amanye ama-porpoise amanengi anawo), unomsila omuhle, namehlo we-India anombala we-hazel. Kodwana umlomo wakhe omnandi umosha koke. Nanyana umgogodla wakhe woke ukuya emaphepheni wakhe aseceleni une-sable etjhingako, kodwana umkhawulo, ohlukileko njengetshwayo elisesikhumbeni somkhumbi, elibizwa ngokuthi "isikhalo esikhanyako," umugqa loyo umdweba ukusuka esiqwini ukuya ngemva, ngemibala emibili ehlukileko, emnyama ngaphezulu nomhlophe ngaphansi. Okumhlophe kuhlanganisa ingcenye yekhanda lakhe, nomlomo wakhe woke, okwenza bona abonakale ngasuthi usanda kubaleka ekuvakatjheleni isikhwama sokudla. Isici esimbi khulu nesimbi khulu! Amafutha wakhe afana khulu newe-porpoise ejayelekileko.

* * * * * *

Ngale kwe-DUODECIMO, ihlelo leli alirageli phambili, ngombana i-Porpoise mncani khulu emikhomeni. Ngehla, unawo woke amaLeviyathani aqakathekileko. Kodwana kuneenqhema zemikhomo engaqinisekiko, ebalekako, engakajayeleki, leyo, njengomdosi wemikhomo we-America, engiwaziko ngedumela, kodwana ingasi mathupha. Ngizozibala ngebizo lazo le-fore-castle; ngombana mhlamunye irhelo elinjalo lingaba lisizo kubaphenyi besikhathi esizako, abangaqeda lokho enginakho lapha kodwana engikuthomileko. Nangabe enye yemikhomo elandelako, izokubanjwa begodu iphawulwe, khona-ke ingafakwa lula kileSistimu, ngokuya ngobukhulu bayo be-Folio, i-Octavo, nofana i-Duodecimo:—I-Bottle-Nose Whale; i-Junk Whale; i-Pudding-Headed Whale; i-Cape Whale; umkhomo odosa phambili; i-Cannon Whale; i-Scragg Whale; i-Coppered Whale; i-Elephant Whale; i-Iceberg Whale; i-Quog Whale; i-Blue Whale; njll. Kusukela eemphathisweni ze-Iceland, ze-Dutch, neze-English zakudala, kungenzeka kube nezinye iinhlu zemikhomo engaqinisekiko, ebusiswe ngamagama angasimahle. Kodwana ngiyazitjhiya njengombana zingasasebenzi; begodu angeze kwasiza ukuzisola njengemisindo nje, ezele ubuLeviyathani, kodwana zingatjho litho.

Ekugcineni: Kwatjhiwo ekuthomeni, bona ihlelo leli angeze lapha, begodu msinyana, liphelele. Ngeze wakghona ukubona ngokukhanyako bona ngiligcine ilizwi lami. Kodwana nje ngitjhiya ihlelo lami le-cetological lingapheli, njengombana i-Cathedral ekulu ye-Cologne yatjhiywa, ne-crane isajame phezu kombhoshongo ongakapheleli. Ngombana ukwakhiwa okuncani kungaqedwa ngabakhi bokuthoma; ezinkulu, eziliqiniso, zihlala zitjhiya ilitshe le-copestone esizukulwaneni esizako. UZimu angivikele bona ngingaqedi nanyana yini. Incwadi yoke iyidrafti—awa, kodwana idrafthi yomtlolo. O, Isikhathi, Amandla, Imali, Nesineke!
