ISAHLUKO 86. Umsila.

Ezinye iimbongi ziye zadumisa ilihlo elithambileko le-antelope, kanye namaphiko amahle wenyoni engasoze yehla; okungakaqakatheki, ngigubha umsila.

Nakuqalwa umsila we-Sperm Whale omkhulu khulu uthoma lapho uthoma khona ukufikela ebubanzini bomuntu, uhlanganisa indawo yayo engaphezulu, okungenani amafidi amatjhumi amahlanu. Umzimba omncani oyindilinga wempande yayo uyakhula ube ziintende ezimbili ezibanzi, eziqinileko, eziphaphathekileko nofana ama-fluke, kancanikancani zikhula zibe ngaphasi kwe-intjhi ngobukhulu. E-crotch nofana ekuhlanganeni, ama-fluke la ahlangana kancani, bese ahlehla eceleni njengamaphiko, atjhiye isikhala esikhulu hlangana nawo. Akukho nto ephilako lapho imigqa yobuhle ichazwe kuhle khulu ukudlula imingcele yenyanga yama-fluke la. Ekukhuleni kwawo okukhulu emkhomeni okhulileko, umsila uzakudlula amamitha amatjhumi amabili ububanzi.

Ilunga loke libonakala njengebhedi eminyeneko yemisipha ehlangeneko; kodwana usike kiwo, uzokufumana bona ama-strata amathathu ahlukileko ayakha:—phezulu, phakathi, nephasi. Iintambo ezisezingeni eliphezulu nelingaphasi, zide begodu zivundlile; lezo eziphakathi, ezimfitjhani khulu, begodu zigijima ngokuphambana hlangana namalarha wangaphandle. Isakhiwo esithathu, njengananyana yini enye, sinikela amandla emsileni. Kumfundi weendonga zakade zamaRoma, isendlalelo esiphakathi sizokunikela ukufana okumangalisako kwendlela encani yamathayili ahlala atjhuguluka ngamatje kulezo zinto ezihle khulu zezinto zakade, begodu ngokungangabazeki zifaka isandla khulu emandleni amakhulu wokwakha amatshe.

Kodwana kwangathi amandla amakhulu wendawo emsila we-tendinous bekangakaneli, ubunengi be-leviathan buhlanganiswe nge-warp ne-woof yemicu yemisipha nemicu, edlula ngapha nangapha kwe-loin bese yehlela ema-flukes, ihlangana nayo ngokungazwakaliko, begodu inesandla esikhulu emandleni wayo; ukwenzela bona emsileni amandla angalinganiswako womkhomo woke abonakala ahlanganiswe khulu. Ingabe ukubhujiswa kungenzeka endabeni, lokhu bekuyinto yokwenza.

Begodu lokhu—amandla wayo amangalisako, akuvamanga ukukhubaza ukugoba okuhle kokunyakaza kwayo; lapho ubuntwana bobulula bukhukhuleka khona ngokusebenzisa i-Titanism yamandla. Ngokuphikisako, lezo zinyathelo zithola ubuhle bazo obumbi khulu kikho. Amandla wamambala awasoze aphazamise ubuhle nofana ukuzwana, kodwana avamise ukukunikela; begodu kikho koke okuhle khulu, amandla ahlobene khulu nomlingo. Susa imisipha ebotjhiweko ebonakala iqhuma emabula ku-Hercules ebaziweko, begodu ubuhle bayo buzokuphela. Njengoba u-Eckerman ozinikeleko aphakamisa ishidi lelineni esidumbu saka-Goethe esinqunu, wagandeleleka ngesifuba esikhulu sendoda leyo, ebegade ibonakala njenge-arch yokunqoba yamaRoma. Lokha u-Angelo nakapenda ngitjho noZimu uBaba asesimweni somuntu, tjheja bona kukhona ukuqina okunjani. Nanyana yini abangayiveza ngethando laphezulu eNdodaneni, iinthombe ezithambileko, ezigoqeneko, ezifana nobufazi be-Italy, lapho umbono wakhe sele ufakwe khona ngepumelelo; iinthombe lezi, njengombana zinganamandla woke, azitjengisi litho ngamandla, kodwana ezimbi, ezingokwesifazana zokuzithoba nokukghodlhelela, okuvunywa kizo zoke izandla, zakha ubuhle obusebenzako obuhlukileko beemfundiso zakhe.

Ukuthamba okufihlakeleko kwesitho engikhuluma ngaso, kungakhathaliseki bona sisetjenziswa emidlalweni, namkha ngokuzimisela, namkha ngokuthukuthela, nanyana kungaba njani, ukugoba kwaso kuhlala kuphawulwa ngomusa owedlulako. Lapho ayikho ingalo yenganekwane engadlula kiyo.

Iinkhathi ezinhlanu ezikulu zihlukile kiyo. Kokuthoma, lokha nayisetjenziswa njengesiphephelo sokuragela phambili; Kwesibili, lokha nakusetjenziswa njenge-mace epini; Okwesithathu, ekuthanyeleni; Okwesine, e-lobtailing; Okwesihlanu, ekuphakameni okukhulu.

Kokuthoma: Njengombana ivundlile endaweni yayo, umsila weLeviyathani wenza ngendlela ehlukileko kunemisila yazo zoke ezinye iinlwana zaselwandle. Akukaze kugoqeke. Emuntwini nofana emfesini, ukutjhinga kutshwayo lokuzithoba. Kumkhomo, umsila wakhe ngiwo wodwa indlela yokuqhuba. Ngokwe-scroll-wise igoqelwe phambili ngaphasi komzimba, bese msinyana ibuyela emuva, ngilokhu okunikela ukunyakaza okukodwa kokugijima, ukugxuma esilwaneni esikhulu lokha nasibhukuda ngokuthukuthela. Iinhluthu zakhe eziseceleni zisebenzela ukutjhayela kwaphela.

Kwesibili: Kuqakathekile kancani, ukuthi ngesikhathi omunye umkhomo wesidoda ulwisana nomunye umkhomo wesidoda ngehloko nomhlathi, nanyana kunjalo, ekulwisaneni kwakhe nomuntu, usebenzisa umsila wakhe ngokudelelako. Ekubetheni isikepe, msinyana ugoba ama-fluke wakhe kiso, begodu ukubetha kwenziwa yi-recoil kwaphela. Nangabe yenziwa emoyeni ongavimbekiko, khulukhulu nangabe yehlela ephawulweni layo, ukutjhwaba akunakujamelana. Akukho zimbambo zomuntu namkha zesikepe ezingajamelana nakho. Insindiso yakho ilele ekuyibalekeni; kodwana nangabe iza ngehlangothini emanzini aphikisanako, khona-ke ngokwengcenye ngebanga lokuphakama okulula kwesikepe somkhomo, nokuqina kwezinto zaso, imbambo edabukileko nofana ipulangwe elidabukileko nofana ezimbili, umhlobo wokuthunga ehlangothini, kuvamise ukuba mphumela omumbi khulu. Ukutshaywa okutjhingela ngaphasi kwamanzi kuvamise ukutholwa eendaweni zokudoba, kangangobana kuthathwa njengokudlala kwabantwana. Omunye umuntu ukhumula isigqoko, begodu umgodi uyavalwa.

Okwesithathu: Angeze ngakghona ukukutjengisa, kodwana kubonakala kimi, bona emkhomeni umqondo wokuthinta udzimelele emsileni; ngombana malungana nalokhu kunobumnandi obulinganiswa nobuhle besiqu sendlovu. Lokhu kumnandi kubonakala khulu ngesenzo sokutjhayela, lokha ngobumnene bobuntombazana umkhomo ngokubuthakathaka okuthileko uthutha ama-flukes wawo amakhulu ukusuka ehlangothini ukuya kwelinye phezu kobuso bolwandle; begodu nangabe uzwa iindevu zomkhumbi, maye kulowo mkhumbi, iindevu nakho koke. Yeka ubumnene obukhona ekuthinteni kwangaphambilini! Ngathana umsila lo bewunamandla wokubamba, bengizakucabanga ngendlovu ka-Darmonodes ebegade ivame ukuya emakethe yamathuthumbo, begodu ngokubingelela okuphasi inikela amantombazana amakhala, bese iphulula iindawo zawo. Eendatjaneni ezinengi, kuyadabukisa bona umkhomo awunalo ubuhle bokubamba emsileni wawo; ngombana ngike ngezwa ngenye indlovu, lokha nayilimeleko epini, yagoba isiqu sayo begodu yakhipha umcibisholo.

Okwesine: Ukutshontsha umkhomo ungakaqapheli ekuphepheni okucatshangelwako phakathi kwamalwandle ahlala yedwa, umthola angakagobeki ekukhulupheleni kwesithunzi sakhe, begodu njengekati, udlala elwandle kwangathi liziko. Kodwana usabona amandlakhe emdlalweni wakhe. Iintende ezibanzi zomsila wakhe ziphakanyiswa phezulu emoyeni; bese ushaya phezulu, ukuthuthumela okukhulu kuzwakala amakhilomitha amanengi. Ungacabanga bona kukhishwe isibhamu esikhulu; begodu nangabe uqaphele umqhele omncani womhwamuko ovela e-spiracle ngakelinye ihlangothi lakhe, ungacabanga bona lokho bekuyintuthu evela emgodini wokuthinta.

Okwesihlanu: Njengombana kunjalo ngokuma okujayelekileko kwe-leviathan ama-flukes alele ngaphasi khulu kwezinga lomgogodla wayo, bese kuthi angabonakali ngaphasi kwamanzi; kodwana lokha nasele azokutjhingela ngaphakathi kwemanzini atjhingako, ama-fluke wakhe woke angamamitha angaba matjhumi amathathu womzimba wakhe aphoselwa phezulu emoyeni, begodu ahlala athuthumela isikhatjhana, bekube kulapho adubula phasi angabonakali. Ngaphandle kokuphuka okukhulu —kwenye indawo okuzokuchazwa ngayo — ukuphakama kwama-flukes womkhomo mhlamunye yinto ehle khulu engabonwa kiyo yoke imvelo ephilako. Ngaphandle kokujula okungenamkhawulo umsila omkhulu ubonakala uqhaqhazela ezulwini eliphezulu. Njeke emabhudangweni, ngike ngabona uSathana ophakemeko akhupha inzipho yakhe ekulu ehlunguphazako evela elangabini le-Baltic yeSihogo. Kodwana ngokubuka iinthombe ezinjalo, kutjho bona unjani; nangabe ku-Dantean, amadimoni azokuza kuwe; nangabe ku-Isaya, iingilozi ezikulu. Ngijame enhloko yomkhumbi wami ngesikhathi sokuphuma kwelanga okwakwenza isibhakabhaka nelwandle libe bomvu, ngake ngabona umhlambi omkhulu wemikhomo empumalanga, yoke iqale elangeni, begodu isikhatjhana ithuthumela ngokuvumelana nama-flukes aphakemeko. Njengombana bekubonakala kimi ngesikhatheso, ukukhonza onkulunkulu okukhulu kangaka bekungakaze kubonwe, ngitjho ne-Persia, ikhaya labakhulekeli bomlilo. Njengoba u-Ptolemy Philopater afakaza ngendlovu ye-Afrika, nami ngafakaza ngomkhomo, ngathi ngumuntu ozinikele khulu kunazo zoke iinlwana. Ngombana ngokuya kweKosi uJuba, iindlovu zebutho zekadeni bezivame ukuthokozela ekuseni ziphakamise amathumbu wazo ngokuthula okukhulu.

Ukumadanisa okungenzekako esahlukweni lesi, hlangana nomkhomo nendlovu, ngokuya ngezinto ezithileko zomsila womunye nesiqu somunye, akukafaneli kubeke ukubeka amalunga amabili aphikisanako ngokulingana, khulukhulu iinlwana ezivela kizo. Njengombana indlovu enamandla khulu iyi-terrier ku-Leviyathani, ngokunjalo, nayimadaniswa nomsila ka-Leviyathani, isiqu sayo siyisiqu se-lily. Ukutshaywa okumbi khulu okuvela esiqwini sendlovu bekufana nokutjhayisana kwefeni, nakuqathaniswa nokutjhayisana okungalinganisekiko kwama-flukes we-sperm whale, okuthi kanengi ngemva komunye kuphosele iinkepe zoke ngazo zoke iingwegwe nabasebenzi emoyeni, njengombana umdlali we-India uphonsa amabhola wakhe.

*Nanyana koke ukumadanisa ngendlela yobukhulu hlangana nomkhomo nendlovu kungakaqakatheki, ngombana kilokho indlovu ijame ngendlela efanako nomkhomo njengombana inja yenza endlovuni; nanyana kunjalo, akukho amaphuzu athileko afanako; hlangana nalezi kukhona umlomo. Kuyaziwa bona indlovu ivamise ukudosa amanzi nofana ithuli esiqwini sayo, bese iyiphakamisa, iyiphose emfuleni.

Nangicabanga khulu ngomsila onamandla lo, ngiyazisola khulu ngokungakghoni kwami ​​ukuwuveza. Ngezinye iinkhathi kuba neenthombe kiyo, nanyana zingathabisa isandla somuntu, zihlala zingachazeki. Emhlambini omkhulu, okumangalisako, ngezinye iinkhathi, zizenzo eziyimfihlakalo, kangangobana ngizwe abazingeli abamemezele bona zifana namatshwayo namatshwayo we-Free-Mason; ukuthi umkhomo, kwamambala, ngalezi zindlela wakhuluma ngokuhlakanipha nephasi. Begodu akukho okunye ukunyakaza komkhomo emzimbeni wayo woke, okuzele ukungaziwa, begodu okungaphenduli kumhlaseli wakhe onelwazi khulu. Mhlukanise ngendlela engingakghona ngayo, khona-ke, ngiya ngesikhumba esijulileko; Angimazi, begodu angeze ngamazi. Kodwana nangabe angazi ngitjho nomsila womkhomo lo, ngizwisisa njani ihlokwakhe? khulukhulu, ungabuzwisisa njani ubuso bakhe, lokha ubuso akanabo? Uzakubona iingcenye zami zemuva, umsila wami, kubonakala ngasuthi uthi, kodwana ubuso bami angeze babonakala. Kodwana angeze ngakghona ukubona kuhle iingcenye zakhe ezingemuva; begodu ngitjho bona ufunani ngobuso bakhe, ngithi godu akanabuso.





ISAHLUKO 87. I-Grand Armada.

I-peninsula ede nencani ye-Malacca, eya ngesewula-mpumalanga ukusuka eendaweni ze-Birmah, yenza indawo esewula khulu kunazo zoke i-Asia. Ngokulandelana okuragela phambili ukusuka esihlengeleni leso kuneenhlengele ezide ze-Sumatra, i-Java, i-Bally, ne-Timor; okuthi, nezinye ezinengi, zakha i-mole ekulu, nofana i-rampart, ngobude obuhlanganisa i-Asia ne-Australia, begodu ihlukanisa ilwandle le-India elide elingaphukileko ukusuka eenhlengeleni ze-oriental ezineenhluthu ezinengi. I-rampart le ihlatjhwe ziinkundla ezinengi ukwenzela bona kube lula emikhunjini nemikhomo; okubonakalako hlangana nazo kuneenqhema ze-Sunda ne-Malacca. Ngase-Sunda, khulukhulu, imikhumbi eya e-China ukusuka entshonalanga, ivela elwandle le-China.

Iinqhema ezincani ze-Sunda zihlukanisa i-Sumatra ne-Java; begodu ajame phakathi nendlela ekulu yeziqhingi, esekelwe siqubuthu esihlaza satjani, esaziwa babantu bemikhumbi nge-Java Head; azikhambelani kancani nesango eliphakathi elivulela embusweni omkhulu onamaboda: begodu nawutjheja umnotho ongapheliko wezinongo, namasilika, namagugu, negolide, nezinyo lendlovu, lapho iinhlengele eziyinkulungwana zelwandle le-oriental zinothiswe khona, kubonakala kuyinto eqakathekileko yemvelo, ukuthi amagugu anjalo, ngokubunjwa kwezwe, okungenani ukubukeka, nanyana kungasebenzi, kokugadwa ephasini langeTjingalanga elibamba koke. Amabhishi we-Straits of Sunda awanazo iinqabo ezilawulako ezivikela iminyango ye-Mediterranean, i-Baltic, ne-Propontis. Ngokungafani nama-Danes, ama-Oriental la awafuni ukuhlonitjhwa okukhulu kwamaseyila aphezulu avela emkhankasweni ongapheliko wemikhumbi ngaphambi komoya, ebegade idlule emakhulwini weminyaka, ebusuku nemini, hlangana neenhlengele ze-Sumatra ne-Java, ithwele imithwalo ebiza khulu yepumalanga. Kodwana ngitjho nanyana batjhiya ngokutjhaphuluka umnyanya onjengalo, abalali nakancani isibawo sabo sokubhadela umthelo oqinileko.

Isikhathi sokuphela kwengqondo ama-proas wama-Malay, acashe hlangana nama-cove aphasi anomthunzi kanye nama-slets we-Sumatra, aphume phezu kwemikhumbi ekhamba ngeenkhathi, afuna khulu ukubhadelwa ngemikhonto yawo. Nanyana ngokujeziswa okuphindaphindiweko okuneengazi abakutholileko ezandleni zama-European cruisers, isibindi sama-corsair la sele sigandelelekile muva nje; kodwana, ngitjho nanamhlanjesi, ngezinye iinkhathi sizwa ngemikhumbi yamaNgisi neye-Amerikha, leyo, emanzini lawo, ebegade igibele begodu yaphangwa ngokungazisoli.

Ngomoya omuhle, omutjha, i-Pequod besele itjhidela eendaweni ezitjhidelako; U-Ahabi ahlose ukudlula kiwo aye elwandle le-Javan, begodu ukusuka lapho, akhamba aya ngetlhagwini, phezu kwamanzi aziwako bona ahlala lapha nalaphaya yi-Sperm Whale, akhukhule egwini nge-Philippines Islands, begodu athole igu elide le-Japan, ngesikhathi sesikhathi esikhulu sokudoba imikhomo lapho. Ngalezi zindlela, i-Pequod ejikelezako beyizokutjhayela pheze yoke indawo eyaziwako ye-Sperm Whale ephasini, ngaphambi kokuthi yehle phezu kwe-Line e-Pacific; lapho u-Ahabi, nanyana kizo zoke iindawo abhalelwa kukuphishekela kwakhe, athembele ngokuqinileko ekunikeleni ipi no-Moby Dick, elwandle bekaziwa khulu ukuvakatjhela kilo; begodu ngesikhathi lapho angathathwa khona njengomuntu oyihluphako.

Kodwana njani nje? emzameni lo, ingabe u-Ahabi akathinti inarha? ingabe isiqhema sakhe sisela umoya? Kwamambala, uzokuma athole amanzi. Awa. Isikhathi eside, nje, ilanga eligijima njengesekisi beligijima ngaphakathi kwendandatho yakhe evuthako, begodu alitlhogi ukudla ngaphandle kwalokho okuphakathi kwakhe. Ngalokho u-Ahabi. Tjheja lokhu, godu, ku-whale. Lokha amanye ama-hull alayitjhwe phasi ngezinto ezivela eenarheni zangaphandle, bona zidluliselwe ema-wharf wangaphandle; umkhumbi wemikhomo okhamba ephasini loke awuthwali umthwalo ngaphandle kwakhe nabasebenzi, izikhali zabo neemfuno zabo. Unokuqukethwe kwechibi loke okufakwe emabhodleleni wakhe. Ugcwele izinto zokusebenza; ingasi ngokupheleleko ngomthofu wengulube ongasebenziko kanye ne-kentledge. Uthwele amanzi weminyaka ngaphakathi kwakhe. Hlanza amanzi amadala we-Nantucket; okuthi, lokha nasele iminyaka emithathu iphapha, i-Nantucketer, e-Pacific, ikhetha ukusela ngaphambi kokuthi kube namanzi anetswayi, kodwana izolo iphume ngama-cask, ukusuka emifuleni ye-Peruvian nofana ye-India. Kungakho-ke, ngitjho nanyana eminye imikhumbi ingahle iye e-China isuka e-New York, begodu ibuyele emuva, ithinta amatheku amanengi, umkhumbi wemikhomo, kiso soke isikhathi leso, kungenzeka bona awuzange ubone ngitjho nenhlamvu yehlabathi; isiqhema sakhe besingakaboni umuntu ngaphandle kwabasebenzi bemikhumbi abaphaphako njengabo. Nje-ke lokho kwabathwala iindaba zokuthi kufike omunye umkhukhula; bebazokuphendula kwaphela—“Kulungile, bafana, nansi umkhumbi!”

Nje, njengoba i-Sperm Whales eminengi sele ithunjiwe eduze kwe-Java, eduze ne-Straits of Sunda; kwamambala, njengombana indawo enengi, ejikelezako, beyibonwa badobi njengendawo ehle khulu yokukhamba ngesikepe; ngalokho-ke, njengombana i-Pequod ithola khulu i-Java Head, abaqaphi bebadunyiswa kanengi, begodu bayeleliswa bona bahlale baphapheme. Kodwana nanyana amawa ahlaza satjani wephasi abonakala msinyana ngaphakathi kwesikepe, begodu ngamakhala athokozako isinamoni efretjhi beyiphefumulelwa emoyeni, kodwana akukho jet eyachazwako. Pheze ukulahla yoke imicabango yokuwela nanyana ngimuphi umdlalo lapha, umkhumbi besele useduze nokungena emgodini, lokha ukukhala okujayelekileko nakuzwakala phezulu, begodu kungakabi sikhathi eside umbukiso wobuhle obungakajayeleki wasibingelela.

Kodwana lapha-ke, bona ngebanga lomsebenzi ongadinwako ebegade azingelwa ngawo phezu kwawo woke amalwandle amane, ama-Sperm Whales, kunokobana akhambe ngeenqhema ezincani ezihlukanisiweko, njengeenkhathi zangaphambilini, nje sele ahlangabezana nawo ngemihlambi emikhulu, kesinye isikhathi amukela isiqubuthu esikhulu kangangobana bekungabonakala ngasuthi amabizo amanengi wawo bekafunga iligi nesivumelwano sokusizana nokuvikela. Ekuhlanganisweni kwe-Sperm Whale ibe ziinqhema ezikulu kangaka, kungabangelwa ubujamo bokuthi ngitjho naseendaweni ezihle khulu zokukhamba ngesikepe, kesinye isikhathi ningakhamba ngesikepe amaviki neenyanga ndawonye, ​​ngaphandle kokubingelelwa mlomo owodwa; bese ubingelelwa msinyana ngilokho kwesinye isikhathi okubonakala ngasuthi ziinkulungwana ngeenkulungwana.

Ebanzi kiwo womabili amabhawu, ibanga elingaba ziimayela ezimbili nofana ezintathu, begodu yakha isiyingi esikhulu, ehlanganisa ihlangothi elilodwa lomkhathi ophakemeko, iketango eliragela phambili lama-whale-jets belidlala phezulu begodu likhazimula emoyeni wemini. Ngokungafani nama-twin-jets aqondileko we-Right Whale, ahlukana phezulu, awela ngamagatja amabili, njengamagatja we-willow, umlomo owodwa otjhingela phambili we-Sperm Whale uveza isihlahla esiqinileko esigoqeneko senkungu emhlophe, eragela phambili nokukhuphuka nokuwa.

Nawubonwa ukusuka emphemeni we-Pequod, njeke, njengombana azokuphakama entabeni ephakemeko yelwandle, iinqhema zeenqhema ezinomhwamuko, ezigoqa ngamunye emoyeni, begodu zibonwa ngomoya ohlanganisiweko wenkungu eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, bezibonakala njengeenkulungwana zezitjhimela ezithabileko zedorobha elithileko elikhulu, ezichazwe njengekuseni ekwindla, ngehhashi elithileko eliphakemeko.
