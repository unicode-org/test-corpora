"BEGODU NGAPHUNYEKE KUPHELA UKUTJHELA" UJobe.

Idrama sele iphelile. Kubayini-ke omunye umuntu aphuma phambili?—Ngombana omunye wasinda ekuphahlazekeni.

Kwenzeka, ngemva kokunyamalala kwe-Parsee, benginguye u-Fates amkhethe ukuthatha indawo yomcibisholo ka-Ahabi, lokha umcibisholo loyo nakathatha isikhundla esinganamuntu; ofanako, owathi ngelanga lokugcina amadoda amathathu aphoselwa ngaphandle kwesikepe esinyakazisako, wawisa ngemuva. Njeke, ngintanta emkhawulweni wesigcawu esilandelako, begodu ngiyibona ngokuzeleko, lokha ukudonsa okuphelileko komkhumbi otjhingileko nakufika kimi, ngaleso sikhathi, kodwana kancanikancani, ngidoselwa e-vortex yokuvala. Nangifika kiyo, besele yehlile yaba yidamu elikhilimu. Ukujikeleza nokujikeleza, bese, begodu njalo ukunciphisa ukuya ebhamuleni elimnyama elifana nebhathini e-eksisi yesiyingi esijikelezako kancanikancani, njengenye i-Ixion engiyenzako. Bekube kulapho, ukuthola isikhungo esiqakathekileko, ibhamuza elimnyama eliya phezulu laqhuma; begodu nje, itjhaphulukile ngebanga lomthombo wayo ohlakaniphileko, begodu, ngebanga lokuphakama kwayo okukhulu, iphakama ngamandla amakhulu, ibhokisi lokuphila ladutshulwa ngobude ukusuka elwandle, lawa, laphapha eceleni kwami. Ngikhuthazwe libhokisi lokugcwaba, pheze ilanga nobusuku, ngaphaphazela phezu kwe-main ethambileko nefana nokulila. Amatjhagi angalimaziko, begade atjhelela ngasuthi anamakhiya emlonyeni wawo; ama-sea-hawks ahlasela ahamba ngesikepe ngeenlomo ezigoqiweko.
