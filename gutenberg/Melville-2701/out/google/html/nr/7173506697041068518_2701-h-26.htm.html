Kwaphela iminyaka ematjhumi amane, wakhiqiza begodu wasabalalisa ama-eBook we-Project Gutenberg™ ngenethiwekhi ekhululekileko yokuzithandela.
Ama-eBook we-Project GutenbergTM avamise ukwakhiwa ngeenhlelo ezinengi ezigadangisiweko, zoke eziqinisekiswa njengezingavikelwe lilungelo lokutlola e-US ngaphandle kobana kufakwe isaziso selungelo lokutlola. Ngalokho-ke, asigcini ama-eBook ngokukhambisana nanyana ngiliphi iphepha elithileko.
Abantu abanengi bathoma kuwebhusayithi yethu enendawo ekulu yokusesha i-PG: www.gutenberg.org .
Iwebhusayithi le ifaka hlangana ilwazi nge-Project Gutenberg™, okufaka hlangana indlela yokwenza iminikelo ku-Project Gutenberg Literary Archive Foundation, indlela yokusiza ukukhiqiza ama-eBooks wethu amatjha, nokuthi ungabhalisa njani incwadi yethu yeendaba ye-imeyili ukuzwa ngama-eBooks amatjha.
