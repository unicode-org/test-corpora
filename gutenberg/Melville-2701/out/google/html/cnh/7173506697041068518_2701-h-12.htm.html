A saunak ah, a tlangpi in a ummi sperm whale-line nih fathom zakhat hnih leng a tah. Lawng hnulei lei ah cun ti kholhnak kuang chungah a tlongmi a si, a tlongmi rungrul-pipe bantuk si loin, a tlongmi, cheese bantuk a simi a tlongmi “sheaves” asiloah aa pehtlaimi tliknak tlawmpal a ser khawh nakhnga, “lungthin” asiloah cheese axis ah a ummi a hmete a dirmi tube dah ti lo cu a tlongmi a um lo. Coiling chung i a tlawm bik aa tthenmi asiloah a tlikmi nih, a dih tikah, palhnak um loin mi pakhatkhat kut, ke, asiloah pum dihlak a lak khawh caah, ralrinnak sangbik cu a hri kha a ti kholhnak kuang chungah chiahnak ah hman a si. Harpooneers cheukhat nih hi chawlehnak ah zinglei khat tluk an hman lai, hri cu sangpi ah an phorh i cu hnuah ti kholhnak lei ah block pakhat in a tanglei lei ah an hler, cucaah a si khomi tliknak le tliknak vialte in luatnak caah tliknak tuahsernak ah a si.

Mirang tilawng hna ahcun pakhat si loin tikuang pahnih an hman; line pakhat cu tikuang pahnih ah pehzulh tein aa hrolh. Hi ah hin thatnak cheukhat a um; zeicahtiah hi twin-tubs pawl hi an hmete tuk caah tilawng chungah fawi deuh in an i tlum, cun an hrem tuk lo; cu lio ah, American ti kholhnak, pe thum hrawng a kauhnak le aa tlukmi thuknak nih, a thingphel cu inch cheu tluk a thuhnak lawng a simi tilawng caah thilrit a ngan tukmi a si; zeicahtiah whale-lawng tang cu a biapi tukmi tikhal bantuk a si, cu nih cun a ritmi a phawt lai, asinain a tlawm tukmi cu a phorh lai lo. American line-tub cungah suaimi canvas khuhnak kha an bengh tikah, tilawng cu nga ngan pawl pekchanh ding in a liannganmi thitumnak changreu he a zuangmi bantuk in a lang.

Line a donghnak pahnih cu a lang; a tanglei bik cu mit-hrennak asiloah hri in a dong i a tanglei in ti kholhnak kuang lei ah a hung kai, cun a cunglei ah aa thlai i zeizong vialte he aa then dih. A tanglei bik timhtuahnak hi a ruang pahnih ah a herh. Pakhatnak: A pawngkam tilawng in a dang hri pakhat hren khawhnak ding caah, a pahmi nga ngan nih a hramthawk tein harpoon he aa pehtlaimi hri vialte a lak dih sual lai ti phan ah a aw a thuk tuk sual ahcun. Hi bantuk thil sining ah, ngapi cu tilawng pakhat in a dang pakhat ah zu hrai bantuk in aa ṭhial; tilawng hmasa bik cu a nupi bawmh awkah a pawng ah a um zungzal ko nain. Pahnihnak: Hi timhtuahnak hi mipi himnak caah a herhmi a si; zeicahtiah tilawng hri a niam bik cu zei lam hmanh in tilawng he aa pehtlaih ahcun, cun nga ngan nih a donghnak tiang a tlik ahcun, a caancaan ah a tuah tawn bantuk in minute pakhat te ah, cuka ahcun a dir lai lo, zeicahtiah a rawkralmi tilawng cu a hnulei in rili thuknak ah a hnuh lai; cun cu bantuk thil a can ahcun khua-autu pakhat hmanh nih an hmu ti lai lo.

Lawng cu dawi awkah an ṭhumh hlanah, hri cunglei donghnak cu ti kholhnak kuang in hnulei ah an lak i, cuka i a ummi thingtan cu a velchum in an kalpi i, tilawng cu a saunak dihlak in hmailei ah an kalpi ṭhan, mi pakhat cio i an hrennak hri cung ah vailamtah in an dirter, cucaah tilawng cit tikah a kutdong ah a tlik; cun pa pawl karlak zong ah a kal, annih cu aa ralchanhmi meithal kuan ah an ṭhut tikah, tilawng hmailei ah a ummi thir hrolh asiloah hrolh hna tiang, cuka ahcun thing hri asiloah skewer cu a tlangpi quill tluk a nganmi nih a chuah khawh lonak ding caah a kham. A tliknak in a thal cung ah a tlawmpal in aa thlai, cun tilawng chung ah a luhter tthan; cun fathom pahra asiloah kul hrawng (box-line tiah auhmi) cu thal chung i bawm cungah aa hren, a hnulei tlawmpal ah a ummi gunwale lei ah a kal i, cu hnuah short-warp—harpoon he aa pehtlaimi hri ah aa peh; sihmanhsehlaw cu pehtlaihnak hlan ah, short-warp nih cun a tawinak in chim awk a har tukmi thilthup phunphun a kal.

Cuticun whale-line nih tilawng dihlak cu a harmi coils ah a thleh, a pawngkam ah a tlik i a tlik. Lawng mawngtu vialte cu a ttihnungmi a tliknak ah an i tel; cucaah rammi mit ttih a nungmi hna caah cun, India jugglers bantuk in an lang, a thi khomi rul hna nih an ke le an kut kha lentecelhnak in an i hruk. Cun a thi khomi nu fapa pakhat hmanh nih, a voikhatnak caah, cu hempen thilri hna lakah a ṭhu kho lo, cun tilawng citnak ah a thazaang dihlak in aa zuam lioah, zeitik caan paoh ah harpoon cu a kah khawh lai tiah a ruah kho lai lo, cun hi ṭihnungmi thil vialte cu meikhu bantuk in an hman lai tiah a ruah kho lai lo; a ruh chung i a ummi ruhro hrimhrim kha a hninhmi jelly bantukin a hninh tertu hninhnak um loin cu bantuk thil sining cu a um kho lo. Sihmanhsehlaw ziaza—thil khuaruahhar! ziaza nih zeidah a tuah khawh lo?—Gayer sallies, nuamhnak tam deuh, hnihchuak ttha deuh, le a ceu deuhmi repartees, na mahogany cung ah na theih bal lomi, whale-lawng i a cheu tluk a sau mi cedar rang cung ah na theih dingmi nakin, cu bantuk in thahmi hna hri in an thlai tikah; cun, Siangpahrang Edward hlan i Calais khuami paruk hna bantuk in, tilawng riantuantu a dirhmi pa paruk hna cu thihnak kaa chungah an i hnuh, an hngawng kip ah hri in an hren, na ti khawh.

Ruahnak tlawmte nih a tu ah nga tlaihnak rawhralnak a chuak lengmangmi—a cheu cu a tlawmpal te lawng an si—hi ​​pa asiloah cu pa cu tilawng chungin hri nih a chuahpi i a tlau timi kong kha na chim khawh men lai. Zeicatiah, hri cu a zuang lio ah, tilawng chungah ṭhut cu, a zuangmi hri, le hri, le kedan vialte nih an in hrocer lio ah, a tlingmi steam-engine aw-an phunphun lakah ṭhut bantuk a si. A chia deuh; zeicahtiah hi ttihnung hna lakah hin cawlcang loin na ṭhu kho lai lo, zeicahtiah tilawng cu hrinnak bantukin a hninh i, ralrinnak hmete hmanh um loin khatlei khatlei ah an in thlak; cun mah le mah i remh khawhnak le duhnak le tuahsernak aa khat tein tuahnak lawnglawng in, Mazeppa ah sernak in na luat khawh lai, cun zeizong vialte a hmu khomi ni hrimhrim nih an in tuk khawh lonak hmun ah na zam khawh lai.

Cun: thlichia a hlan deuh i a chimchungmi a thukmi daihnak cu thlichia nakin a ttihnung deuh men lai; zeicahtiah, a ngaingai ti ahcun, daihnak cu thlichia nih a khuh i a khuh lawng a si; cun amah tein aa khumh, zeicahtiah fahnak a pe lomi meithal nih thihnak a chuahter khomi meithal, le ball, le puahnak kha a tlaih; cucaah hri i a nuammi dinhnak, a taktak ah luhpi a si hlanah dai tein tilawng mawngtu hna pawngkam ah a tlik—hi thil hi hi thil ttihnung thil dang vialte nakin tihnung taktak a chuahtertu thil a si. Asinain zeicahdah tam deuh in chim? Mi vialte cu whale-lines nih a khuh hna. An dihlak in an hngawng ah hri he an chuak; sihmanhsehlaw thihnak a rangmi, ruahlopi in aa thlengmi ah tlaih an si tik lawngah, a thimi hna nih dai tein, a fiang lomi, a um zungzalmi nunnak ttihnung hna kha an hngalh. Cun fimnak lei mifim na si ahcun, nga nganpi chungah na ṭhu ko nain, na pawngah hriamnam he si loin zanlei mei hmai ah na ṭhut nakin na lungthin ah ṭihphannak pakhat hmanh na ngei lai lo.
