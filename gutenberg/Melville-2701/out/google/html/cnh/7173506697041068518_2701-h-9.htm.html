BU 46. Ruahchannak.

A tinhmi mei lin nih a kangh ko nain, Ahab nih a ruahnak le a tuahsernak vialte ah Moby Dick tlaih khawhnak ding kha a rak i ruahchan bal lo; cu duhnak pakhat caah thih khawhmi thil vialte pekchanh awkah timhcia a si ko nain; sihmanhsehlaw, amah cu a sining le caan saupi a rak um cangmi a si caah a si kho men, cu nih cun tilawng khualtlawnnak kong he pehtlai in biaceihnak kha a hlawt dih. Asiloah a tlawmbik ah a dang tein a si ahcun, amah he aa pehtlaimi a dang tinhmi a um lo. A ttha tukmi a si lai, a si kho men, a monomania hmanh ruah tikah, White Whale cungah a phuhrunnak cu sperm whale vialte tiang a phan kho men, cun a thahmi saram tam deuhdeuh ah a hnu ah a tonmi whale pakhat cio cu a huatmi a si lai ti langhternak caan tha a karh deuhdeuh ti langhter cu a si kho men. Asinain cu bantuk ruahnak cu a dang tein a um taktak ahcun, a uknak duhnak he aa tlak lo nain, amah kha a hlen khawh lomi ruahnak cheuhnak dang an um rih.

Ahab nih a tinhmi tlinter awkah thilri a hman a hau; cun thlapa thladem ah hmanmi thilri vialte lakah, pa pawl cu ningcang loin chuah an thiam bik. Tahchunhnak ah, a hngalhmi cu, Starbuck cung ah a tthatnak cu a cheukhat ah a tthawng ko nain, cu tthannak nih cun thlarau lei minung tling kha a khuh lo, pumsa lei tthatnak lawng nih fimthiamnak thiamnak aa telter; zeicahtiah thlarau thianghlim, fimthiamnak lei nain pumsa pehtlaihnak phunkhat ah an dir. Starbuck pum le Starbuck duhnak cu Ahab ta a si, Ahab nih Starbuck thluak ah a magnet a chiah chung paoh cu; cu vialte caah cun a nupi bawi bik nih a thlarau chungah a ralbawipa kawlnak kha a huat ti kha a hngalh ko nain, lunglawm tein amah le amah aa ṭhen lai, asiloah a hlawh a tling lai lo ti kha a hngalh. White Whale hmuh a si hlan ah caan saupi a rau lai ti a si kho men. Cu caan saupi chung ah Starbuck cu a ralbawi hruainak dohnak ah a langhnganmi dohnak ah a tla kho men, a tlangpi in, fimkhur tein, thil sining nih a chuahpimi thil cheukhat nih a cungah an chuahpi lo ahcun. Cu lawng si loin, Ahab nih Moby Dick a upatnak a thli tein a hrutnak cu a ttha tukmi a fimthiamnak le a fimthiamnak nakin a langhter deuh, atu lio caan caah cun, saram kawlnak cu a hramthawk tein a ummi khuaruahhar ruahnak ttha lo kha lam pakhatkhat in hloh a si lai; tilawng khualtlawnnak i a ttihnungmi thil vialte kha a fiang lomi hnulei ah chiah a hau (zeicahtiah mi tlawmte ralthatnak cu tuahsernak nih a hloh lomi caan saupi thlacamnak dohnak tehte a si); zan saupi an dir lio ah, a ralbawi le a ralkap pawl nih Moby Dick nakin ruah awk thil naih deuh an ngei lai ti kha a si. Zeicatiah zeitluk in lungtho tein le fak ngai in tilawng riantuantu hna nih a kawlnak thawngthanhnak kha an rak lawmh; sihmanhsehlaw tilawng mawngtu phunkip cu a tlawm deuh le a tam deuh in an i hlen i zumhawktlak an si lo—annih cu aa dangmi lenglei nikhua ah an nung, cun a thlen khawhnak kha an hngalh—cun an kawlnak ah a hlatmi le a lawngmi thil pakhatkhat caah an tlaih hna tikah, a donghnak ah nunnak le duhnak a tthatnak a um ko zongah, caan karlak duhnak le riantuannak nih a donghnak bik ngandamnak caah a kham hna awk le a ngolter hna awk a si zuanhnah.

Cun Ahab zong nih thil dang pakhat hmanh a hngal lo. Lungthin a fah caan ah minung nih a niam deuhmi ruahnak vialte kha an huat; sihmanhsehlaw cu bantuk caan cu a tlau. Ahab nih a sermi minung phunghrampi dirhmun cu thurhnawmhnak a si tiah Ahab nih a ruah. White Whale nih hi ka mi hrokhrawl phu hna lungthin kha tling tein a thawhter hna ti kha cohlan, cun an mi hrutnak he i celh cu an chungah a ttha ngaimi knight-errantism hmanh a chuahter, cu hmanh ah, an dawtnak caah Moby Dick kha an dawi lio ah, an nifatin an duhnak caah rawl zong an ngei a hau. Zeicatiah hlanlio chan i a sangmi le raltha ngaimi Crusader pawl hmanh nih an thlan thianghlim caah raltuk awkah meng thong hnih tluk ram an tan, firnak tuah loin, tangka fir loin le a dang biaknak lei thilri hna hmuh loin an lung a tling lo. An donghnak le dawtnak thil pakhat lawng kha fek tein an rak tlaih hna ahcun—cu donghnak le dawtnak thil cu, mi tampi nih huatnak in an rak kirtak hna lai. Hi hna hi tangka ruahchannak vialte ka chuh hna lai lo, tiah Ahab nih a ruah—a si, tangka.
