Cun a tu cu a caan le hmun hmaan tein, caan saupi le kau ngai in tilawng khualtlawnnak hnu ah, Ahab,—a dang nga tlaihnak ti vialte nih an hlonh dih—a ral cu rili-inn ah a dawi, cuka ah him deuh in thah awkah; a tu cu, a fahnak hma a tuarnak latitude le longitude ah a har ngai cang; a tu cu a hlan ni ah Moby Dick he a rak i tong taktakmi tilawng pakhat kong an chim cang;—cun a tu cu tilawng phunphun he a hnu hnu in a tonmi vialte nih a sualnak a tuah zongah sualnak a tuah zongah a sa zuatmi hna kha white whale nih a thleh hnanak khuachia lungput langhter awkah aa dang tein an lung aa rual cang; a tu cu tarpa mit ah thil pakhatkhat a um, cucu a derthawmmi thlarau nih hmuh khawh a si lo. A tla lomi polar arfi bantuk in, a nungmi, arctic, thla ruk chung zan chung ah a thlehmi, a fekmi, a laifang zohnak kha a dirpi; cucaah Ahab tinhmi cu lungrethei in a ummi tilawng riantuantu hna zanlai ah a zungzal in a ceu. Cu nih cun an cungah a uk hna caah, an lunghrinhnak, lunghrinhnak, lunghrinhnak, tihnak vialte cu an thlarau tangah an i thup i fei pakhat hmanh a chuak lo.

Hi hmailei caan karlak zongah, hnihchuak vialte, hramhram in tuahmi asiloah a chuakmi, an tlau dih. Stubb nih cun mithmai panh tein um aa zuam ti lo; Starbuck nih pakhat hmanh zoh aa zuam ti lo. Cu bantuk cun, lawmhnak le ngaihchiatnak, ruahchannak le tihnak, cu a ttha bikmi leidip ah an cawh i, caan caah, Ahab thir thlarau a hrenmi vutcam chungah an cawh. Seh bantuk in, annih cu tilawng cung ah an i chawk, tarpa uknak mit nih an cungah a um ti kha an hngalh zungzal.

Asinain a thuhmi a thuhmi caan ah thukpi in na zohfel maw; a cungah pakhat lawng a um tiah a ruah tikah; cu tikah Ahab mit nih tilawng chung riantuantu hna mit a hlen hna bantuk in, hngalh khawh lomi Parsee mitthli nih a mit a hlen hna ti kha na hmuh ko lai; asiloah zeitindah, a tlawmbik ah, ramlak lam pakhatkhat in, a caan ah a hnorsuan. Cu bantuk chapmi, a tlikmi khuaruahhar thil nih a tlawmmi Fedallah cu a tu ah a luhter cang; cu bantuk ngol loin a hninhnak nih a hninh; cu pa pawl nih cun lunghrinhnak he an zoh; a cheuchum cu a fiang lo, a langhning ahcun, amah cu a thi khomi thil a si taktak maw, asiloah hmuh khawh lomi thilnung pakhatkhat pum nih tilawng cung ah a thlakmi a hninhmi thlalang a si maw. Cun cu thlalang cu cuka ah cun a zuang zungzal. Zeicatiah zan ah hmanh ah, Fedallah cu a it bal lo, asiloah a tanglei ah a kal ti cu theih a si bal lo. Suimilam tampi chung a dir peng ko lai: asinain a ṭhu bal lo i aa hngat bal lo; a mit a tlawm nain khuaruahhar nih fiang tein a chim—Kannih ralvengtu pahnih cu kan i din bal lo.

Cun, zeitik caan paoh ah, zan ah siseh, chun ah siseh, tilawng mawngtu hna nih tilawng cung ah an kai kho cang, Ahab cu an hmai ah a um lo ahcun; a pivot-hole ah a dir, asiloah aa thleng lomi ri pahnih karlak ah thingphel pawl kha a kal ttha tein a kal,—main-mast le mizen; asiloah cabin-scuttle chungah a dir kha an hmuh,—a nungmi ke cu tilawng cung ah a hung kai, a ke in a kal lai bantuk in; a lukhuh cu a mit cungah fakpi in aa hninh; cucaah zeitluk in a cawlcang lo zongah, zeitluk in ni le zan an chap zongah, a riahnak khan chungah aa hninh lo; sihmanhsehlaw cu a tlongmi lukhuh tangah aa thupmi nih, hi vialte caah, a mit cu a caan ah a khar taktak maw ti kha palhnak um loin an chim kho bal lo; asiloah fakpi in a zoh rih hna maw; zeihmanh a poi lo, suimilam pakhat chung cu tikhor chungah a dir ko nain, cun lung in suaimi angki le lukhuh cungah cun zan-nih nih a hninh hna. Zan ah a hnimmi thilthuam hna cu, a thaizing ni ceunak nih a cungah a roter; cun cuticun, nikhat hnu nikhat, zankhat hnu zankhat; thingphel tang ah a kal ti lo; cabin chungin a duhmi paoh cu thil cu a kuat.

Cu bantuk thli lakah cun a ei; cucu, a rawl pahnih lawng a ummi,—zing rawl le zan rawl: zanriah cu a tawng bal lo; a hmul zong a zun lo; cu hna cu muihnak in a tthang dih, thingkung hna i an cawhmi hram hna bantuk in, cu hna cu a cunglei thingram ah an thi ko nain, a taklawng in an ṭhang rih. Sihmanhsehlaw a nunnak dihlak cu tilawng cung i suimilam pakhat ah a cang cang ko nain; cun Parsee i a thli tein zohkhenhnak cu amah ta bantuk in dinh caan um loin a si ko nain; sihmanhsehlaw hi pahnih nih hin bia an i ruah bal lo—pakhat le pakhat—caan saupi ah a liamcia mi thil pakhatkhat nih a herhter lo ahcun. Cu bantuk a tthawngmi khuaruahhar thil cu a thli tein an pahnih fonh dingin a lang ko nain; langhngan in, le khuaruahhar in a ummi tilawng chung riantuantu hna caah cun, an i tthennak tung bantuk in an lang. Chun ah bia pakhat chim khawhnak caan an ngeih ahcun; zan ah, mi hrut pawl cu an pahnih in, bia tlawmte hmanh an i chawnh khawh. A caan ahcun, suimilam sau bik chung, rial pakhat hmanh a sur lo, arfi ceunak chungah aa hlatnak in an dir; Ahab cu a tilawng cung ah, Parsee cu tilawng nganpi pawngah; asinain pakhat le pakhat kha fek tein an i zoh rih; Parsee ah Ahab nih a thlakmi thlalang a hmuh bantuk in, Parsee Ahab ah a hlawtmi thilri a hmuh bantuk in.

Cun, zeitindah a si ti ahcun, Ahab nih—amah pumpak sining tein, nifatin, suimilam fatin le caan paoh ah, a tangah a ummi hna sinah nawlpeknak in a langhter bantukin,—Ahab cu amah tein a ummi bawi pakhat a lo; Parsee cu a sal si loin. Cun an pahnih in an i fonh tthan, cun hmuh khawh lomi uktu nih a hruai hna; a fekmi ruhro pawng ah a ummi a tlawmmi thlalang. Zeicahtiah hi Parsee hi zeibantuk a si hmanh ah, a ruh le a ke vialte cu Ahab a fekmi a si.

Chunglei a ceu hmasa bik ah, a thir aw cu a hnulei in a thang,—“Mast-heads kha minung tuah!”—cun nikhat chung vialte, ni tlak hnu le zanlei hnu tiang, suimilam fatin tein, tilawng mawngtu bell a tum tikah, cu aw cu a thang—“Zeidah na hmuh?—a rang tuk!”

Sihmanhsehlaw ni thum, ni li a rauh hnu ah, fa a kawlmi Rachel he an i ton hnu ah; cun a chuahnak pakhat hmanh hmuh a si rih lo; monomaniac tarpa nih a tilawng chung riantuantu hna zumhtlak sinak kha a zumh lo bantuk in a lang; a tlawmbik ah, Pagan harpooners ti lo cu an dihlak in; Stubb le Flask nih a kawlmi hmuhnak kha lungtho tein an philh sual lai lo ti hmanh a lunghrinh ko rua tiah a lang. Asinain hi lunghrinhnak hna hi amah ta taktak an si ahcun, a tuahsernak nih a langhter ko nain, bia in chim lo dingin fimkhur tein a hrial.

“Keimah hrimhrim nih ngapi cu keimah nih ka hmuh hmasa bik lai,”—tiah a ti. “A si! Ahab nih a ngeih ko lai!” cun amah kut hrimhrim in bawm chung i a ummi thal hri bu pakhat a ser; cun a kut pakhat kha van ah a thlah, a tthenmi lung pakhat lawng he, main-mast lu ah fek tein hren awkah, a tanglei lei ah a tlikmi hri a donghnak pahnih kha a hmuh; cun pakhat kha a bawm ah a hren i a dang pakhat caah hri pakhat a timhtuah, cucaah hri ah hren khawh a si. Cuti a tuah dih hnu ah, cu a donghnak cu a kut ah a um rih i hri pawngah a dir, a riantuantu hna kha a zoh hna, pakhat in pakhat ah a hlonh hna; Daggoo, Queequeg, Tashtego cung ah a zoh sau ngai a ngol; asinain Fedallah hrial; cun a hawipa cungah a fekmi aa bochanmi mit a chiah i,—“Hri kha la, bawipa—na kut ah kan pek, Starbuck,” tiah a ti. Cun bawm chungah a minung kha a chiah hna i, a ṭhutnak ah hler dingin bia a pek hna, Starbuck cu a donghnak ah hri a hrenmi a si; cun a hnuah a pawngah a dir. Cun cuticun, siangpahrang thirleng cung ah a kut pakhat in aa tlaih i, Ahab nih rili cu meng tampi le meng tampi tiang a zoh,—hmailei, hnulei, hi lei kap, le khi lei,—cu tluk sangpi ah nawlpekmi a kau ngaimi velchum chung ah.

Rigging chung i a sangmi hmun pakhatkhat ah a kut in rian a ttuan tikah, cu hmun cu a ke chiah khawhnak a um lo, rili cung tilawng mawngtu cu cuka hmun ah cun an hler i, cuka ahcun hri in an dirpi; hi thil sining tangah, tilawng cung i a hrenmi a donghnak cu a hleiin zohkhenhtu pakhatkhat sinah fek tein zohkhenh a si zungzal. Zeicatiah cu bantuk ramlak ah a tlikmi thilri hna cu, a cunglei ah an i pehtlaihnak phunphun cu tilawng cung ah hmuhmi nih palhnak um loin thleidan khawh a si zungzal lo; cun hi hri hna i an tilawng cung donghnak hna cu minutes tlawmpal hnu tlawmpal ah hri in an thlak hna tikah, a zungzal in ralvengtu um loin, tilawng mawngtu cu tilawng riantuantu hna ralrin lonak ruangah tilawng cung ah a tla i rili ah a tla sual ahcun, a thihnak a si ko lai. Cucaah hi kong he pehtlai in Ahab nih a tuahmi thil cu aa dang lo; an kong ah khuaruahhar pakhat lawng a ummi cu, Starbuck, biakhiahnak tuah a naih tikah amah doh aa zuammi pa pakhat lawng a si—cu hna lak ah pakhat zong, a zumhtlaknak kha a lunghrinhmi a lo;—a khuaruahhar ngai, hi pa hi a ralvengtu caah a thim awk a simi pa a si; cu bantuk zumh lomi minung kut ah a nunnak dihlak zalong tein a pek.

Atu cu, a voikhatnak Ahab cu van ah a ṭhu; cuka ah minutes pahra a um hlan ah; hi latitude hna ah hin whalemen hna i an lu pawng ah a ttha lo ngai in a zuang tawnmi a hmur a senmi savage sea-hawk hna lak i pakhat; cu vate hna lak i pakhat cu a lu pawng ah a tlik i a au i a zul kho lomi rang tukin aa mer. Cun pe thongkhat tluk van ah a zuang; cun a tanglei lei ah a tlik, cun a lu pawng ah a tlik tthan.

Asinain a mit cu a mui i a hlatnak vancung ah a chiah caah, Ahab nih hi ramlak vate hi a hmelchunh lo bantuk a si; cun, a ngaingai ti ahcun, ahohmanh nih an rak hmelchunh tuk lai lo, thil sining aa dangmi a si lo; a tu lawng ah a tlawm bik mit nih cun hmuhnak kip ah hlennak sullam pakhatkhat a hmuh.

“Na lukhuh, na lukhuh, bawipa!” ruahlo piin Sicilian tilawng mawngtu nih a au, amah cu mizen-mast-head ah chiahmi a si, Ahab hnulei ah a dir, amah nakin a niam deuh ko nain, thli thukpi nih a ṭhen hna.

Asinain sable thla cu tarpa mit hmai ah a um cang; a lu ah a saumi aa hrenmi tangka: au thawng he, black hawk cu a laksawng he a zam.

Mupi pakhat nih Tarquin lu cu a velchum ah voithum a zuang, a lukhuh kha a phoih i a thlen, cu tikah a nupi Tanaquil nih Tarquin cu Rome siangpahrang a si lai tiah a thanh. Asinain lukhuh thlennak thawng lawngin cu hmelchunhnak cu a ttha. Ahab lukhuh cu an remh ṭhan bal lo; ramlak vate cu a zuang i a zuang; hmailei ah a hlatnak ah: cun a donghnak ah a tlau; cu a tlaunak hmun in, a hmete in a senmi hmun pakhat cu a fiang lo, cu hmun sangpi in rili chungah a tla.
