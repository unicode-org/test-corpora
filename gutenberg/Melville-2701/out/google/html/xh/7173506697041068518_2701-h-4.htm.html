Kodwa ndiyibethe phantsi loo nto; kwaye kwakhona ukuphawula oleleyo, jocularly wacebisa Queequeg ukuba mhlawumbi kungcono ukuhlala phezulu kunye nomzimba; emxelela ukuba azimise ngokufanelekileyo. Wabeka isandla sakhe kumqolo wobuthongo, ngokungathi uvakalelwa ukuba uthambile ngokwaneleyo; kwaye ke, ngaphandle kwe-ado engaphezulu, wahlala ngokuzolileyo apho.

“Lubabalo! Queequeg, sukuhlala apho,” ndatsho.

“Owu! perry dood seat,” watsho uQueequeg, “indlela yelizwe lam; ayisayi kumenzakalisa ebusweni.”

“Ubuso!” Ndathi, "Ndibize ubuso bakhe? Ubuso obunobubele kakhulu ngoko; kodwa indlela aphefumla ngayo, uyaziqinisa; yehla, Queequeg, unzima, uyasila ubuso bamahlwempu. Hleka, Queequeg! Khangela, uza kukutshitshisa kungekudala. Ndiyazibuza ukuba akavuki. "

U-Queequeg wazisusa waya ngaphaya kwentloko yomntu oleleyo, waza walayita umbhobho wakhe we-tomahawk. Ndahlala ezinyaweni. Sagcina umbhobho udlula phezu komntu olalayo, ukusuka kwenye ukuya kwenye. Kusenjalo, ekumbuzeni ngendlela yakhe eyaphukileyo, Queequeg wandinika ukuqonda ukuba, kwilizwe lakhe, ngenxa yokungabikho settees kunye neesofa zonke iintlobo, ukumkani, iinkosi, kunye nabantu abakhulu ngokubanzi, babe ngokwesiko lokutyebisa ezinye zemiyalelo ephantsi yeeottomans; kwaye ukubonelela indlu ngokutofotofo kulo mba, kwakufuneka kuphela uthenge amavila asibhozo okanye alishumi, uwabeke macala onke kwiingqameko nakwiindawo zokuhlala. Ngaphandle koko, kwakulula kakhulu kuhambo; ngcono kakhulu kunezo zitulo zasegadini eziguqulwa zibe ziintonga zokuhamba; Ngasihlandlo sithile, inkosi yabiza umlindi wayo, yamcela ukuba azenzele intente phantsi komthi onabileyo, mhlawumbi kwindawo enamanzi amaninzi.

Ngelixa ebalisa ezi zinto, ngalo lonke ixesha u-Queequeg efumana i-tomahawk kum, wayechumisa i-hatchet-side yayo phezu kwentloko yolala.

"Yeyantoni leyo, Queequeg?"

“Perry easy, kill-e; oh! perry easy!”

Wayeqhubeka kunye nezinye iinkumbulo zasendle malunga tomahawk-umbhobho wakhe, leyo, kwakubonakala, ukuba ukusetyenziswa yayo ezimbini zombini ingqondo iintshaba zakhe kwaye wawuthomalalisa umphefumlo wakhe, xa satsalwa ngokuthe ngqo rigger elele. Umphunga owomeleleyo ngoku uzalise ngokupheleleyo umngxuma obekelwe isivumelwano, waqala ukuxela kuye. Waphefumla ngohlobo oluthile lobunzima; emva koko wabonakala ekhathazekile empumlweni; emva koko ijikelezwe kanye okanye kabini; wahlala ngempundu wacikica amehlo.

“Holloa!” waphefumla ekugqibeleni, "ngoobani abatshayayo?"

“Amadoda athunyelweyo,” ndaphendula mna, “uhamba nini ngesikhephe?”

"Ewe, ewe, ningena kuye, ingaba nina? Uhamba ngesikhephe namhlanje. UCaptain ungene phezolo."

“Nguwuphi uMphathi?—UAhabhi?

"Ngubani ngaphandle kwakhe?"

Bendizakumbuza eminye imibuzo eyongezelelweyo malunga no-Ahabhi, xa seva ingxolo phezu komgangatho.

“Holloa! "Uliqabane eliphambili eliphilileyo, lowo; indoda elungileyo, kwaye ilungile; kodwa bonke abaphilayo ngoku, kufuneka ndiphendukele kuye." Watsho ekhwela emkhumbini, salandela.

Ngoku ilanga laligqats' ubhobhoyi. Kwaoko abasebenzi basenqanaweni beza ngababini-bathathu; iinqwelo-mafutha ziqhubela phambili; amaqabane ayebandakanyeke ngokukhutheleyo; kwaye abantu abaninzi baselunxwemeni babexakekile bezisa izinto ezahlukeneyo zokugqibela enqanaweni. Ngelo xesha uKapteni uAhabhi wahlala engabonakali ngaphakathi kwindlu yakhe.
