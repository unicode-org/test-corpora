ISAHLUKO 45. I-Afidavithi.

Ukuza kuthi ga ngoku ukuba kukho ingxelo ekhoyo kule ncwadi; kwaye, eneneni, njengokuba ngokungangqalanga echukumisa enye okanye ezimbini iinkcukacha ezinomdla kakhulu kwaye zinomdla kwimikhwa yeminenga yamadlozi, isahluko esingaphambili, kwinxalenye yaso yangaphambili, ibaluleke kakhulu njengoko iya kufumaneka kulo mqulu; kodwa umba ophambili wawo ufuna ukuba ukhuliselwe ngakumbi nangokuqhelekiyo ngakumbi, ukuze uqondwe ngokwaneleyo, kwaye ngaphezu koko, ukususa nakuphi na ukungakholeleki okunokuthi kukunyanzelwa kukungazi okunzulu kwesifundo siphela, kwezinye iingqondo, mayela nobunyaniso bendalo beengongoma eziphambili zalo mcimbi.

Andikhathali ukwenza le nxalenye yomsebenzi wam ngendlela; kodwa uya kwaneliseka ukuvelisa imbonakalo enqwenelekayo ngokucaphula okwahlukileyo kwezinto, ngokubonakalayo okanye ngokuthembekileyo kwaziwa kum njenge-whaleman; kwaye kwezi zicatshulwa, ndiyithatha-isigqibo esijoliswe kuyo siya kuzilandela ngokwaso.

Eyokuqala: Ndiyazi ngokwam izihlandlo ezithathu apho umnenga, emva kokufumana i-harpoon, uye wabaleka ngokupheleleyo; kwaye, emva kwekhefu (kwithuba elinye leminyaka emithathu), uphinde wabethwa kwangeso sandla sinye, wabulawa; xa iintsimbi ezimbini, zombini ziphawulwe yi-cypher yabucala efanayo, zithatyathwe emzimbeni. Kwimeko apho iminyaka emithathu yangenelela phakathi kokujikwa kweeharpoons ezimbini; kwaye ndiyacinga ukuba isenokuba yinto engaphaya koko; indoda leyo darted kubo kwenzeka, kwisithuba, ukuya kwinqanawa yorhwebo kuhambo oluya eAfrika, waya elunxwemeni apho, wajoyina iqela lokufumanisa, kwaye wangena kude ngaphakathi, apho wahamba iminyaka ephantse ibe mibini, ehlala esengozini yeenyoka, ama-tigers, ama-tigers, i-miasmas enetyhefu, kunye nazo zonke ezinye iingozi eziqhelekileyo kwisiganeko esingaziwayo sommandla wokuzulazula entliziyweni. Ngelo xesha, umnenga awayewubethile umele ukuba wawuseluhambeni; ngokungathandabuzekiyo yayiwujikelezile umhlaba izihlandlo ezithathu, iwutyhutyha lonke unxweme lwaseAfrika; kodwa ngelize. Wabuya wahlanganisana lo mntu nalo mnenga, waza omnye wamoyisa omnye. Ndithi mna, ngokwam, ndiye ndazi imizekelo emithathu efana nale; oko kubini kuzo ndabona iminenga ibetha; kwaye, kuhlaselo lwesibini, wabona iintsimbi ezimbini zineempawu ezisikiweyo kuzo, emva koko zithathwe kwintlanzi efileyo. Kumzekelo weminyaka emithathu, kuye kwawa kangangokuba ndandisesikhepheni omabini amaxesha, owokuqala nowokugqibela, kwaye okokugqibela ndabona ngokucacileyo uhlobo oluthile lwemolekyuli enkulu phantsi kweliso lomnenga, endandikhe ndayibona apho kwiminyaka emithathu edlulileyo. Ndithi iminyaka emithathu, kodwa ndiqinisekile ukuba yayingaphezulu koko. Nantsi imizekelo emithathu, ngoko, endiyaziyo ngokwam ubunyani bayo; kodwa ndivile ngamanye amaxesha amaninzi kubantu abanyanisekanga kulo mba kungekho mhlaba ulungileyo wokumangalela.
