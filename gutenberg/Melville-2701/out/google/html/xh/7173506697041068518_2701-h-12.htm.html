ISAHLUKO SAMA-55. YeMifanekiso Emanyumnyezi Yeminenga.

Ndiya kupeyinta ixesha elide kuwe njengoko umntu unako ngaphandle kweseyile, into efana neyona ndlela yokwenene yomnenga njengoko ebonakala ngokwenene kwiliso le-whaleman xa kumzimba wakhe opheleleyo umnenga ubotshwe ecaleni kwenqanawa yomnenga ukuze anyathelwe ngokufanelekileyo apho. Kusenokuba luncedo, ke ngoko, ngaphambili ukubhengeza loo mizobo inomdla yokucinga ngaye ethi kude kube namhlanje icel’ umngeni ukholo lomnini-mhlaba. Lixesha lokulungisa ihlabathi kulo mbandela, ngokubonisa ukuba imifanekiso elolo hlobo yomnenga iphosakele.

Kusenokwenzeka ukuba oyena mthombo uphambili wayo yonke loo mifanekiso iqingqiweyo iya kufunyanwa phakathi kweyona mifanekiso midala yamaHindoo, amaYiputa, namaGrike. Ukususela kuloo maxesha okuvelisa kodwa angenangqiqo xa kwiipaneli zebhastile zeetempile, iziseko zemifanekiso eqingqiweyo, kunye namakhaka, iimedallions, iikomityi kunye neengqekembe, ihlengesi lazotywa kwizikali ze-chain-armor njengeSaladin, kunye nentloko enesigcina-ntloko efana neSt. ukusukela ngoko kukho into efana nelayisenisi ekhoyo, kungekuphela nje kwimifanekiso emininzi edumileyo yomnenga, kodwa kwiinkcazo ezininzi zenzululwazi ngaye.

Ngoku, ngawo onke amathuba, owona mfanekiso wakudala ukhoyo ekucingelwa ukuba ngowomnenga, ufumaneka kwicavern-pagoda yodumo yase-Elephanta, eIndiya. AmaBrahmin akholelwa kwelokuba kwimifanekiso eqingqiweyo ephantse ingapheli yaloo pagoda yamandulo, lonke urhwebo nezinto ezenziwayo, yonke into enokucingeleka yomntu, yayiyiminyaka efanekiselwa kwangaphambili ngaphambi kokuba kubekho nayiphi na kuzo. Akumangalisi ke ngoko, ukuba ngandlel’ ithile umsebenzi wethu obekekileyo wokuloba iminenga bekufanele ukuba ukho apho. Umnenga weHindoo ekubhekiselwa kuwo, uvela kwisebe elahlukileyo eludongeni, ebonisa ukuzalwa kukaVishnu ngendlela yeleviathan, eyaziwa ngokuba yiMatse Avatar. Kodwa nangona lo mzobo sisiqingatha somntu kunye nesiqingatha somnenga, ukuze nje unike umsila wokugqibela, kodwa loo nxalenye encinci yakhe ayilunganga. Ikhangeleka ngakumbi njengomsila ogobhozayo wenhlwathi, kuneentende zezandla ezibanzi zomnenga wokwenene.

Kodwa yiya kwiiGalari zakudala, ukhangele ngoku kumfanekiso womzobi omkhulu ongumKristu wale ntlanzi; kuba akaphumeleli ngcono kuneHindoo yamandulo. Ngumfanekiso kaGuido kaPerseus ehlangula uAndromeda kwisilwanyana saselwandle okanye umnenga. Wawufumana phi uGuido umzekelo wesidalwa esingaqhelekanga ngolo hlobo? Kananjalo noHogarth, ekupeyinteni umfanekiso ofanayo kweyakhe “iPerseus Eyehlayo,” akayenzi ibengcono into enye. Ukonakala okukhulu kwesi silo saseHogarthian sithe nca phezu komhlaba, kunqabile ukuzoba i-intshi enye yamanzi. Inohlobo oluthile lwe-howdah emqolo, kwaye umlomo wayo othe tye othe tyaba ekuqengqeleka kuwo amaza, unokusiwa kwiSango labangcatshi eliphuma kumlambo iThames ngamanzi ukuya kwiNqaba. Emva koko, kukho iminenga ebizwa ngokuba yiProdromus ye<em>Scotch Sibbald endala, kunye ne<em>whale kaYona, njengoko kuboniswe kwimibhalo yeeBhayibhile ezindala kunye nokusikwa kwemibhalo emidala. Kuya kuthiwani na ngezi? Ngokuphathelele umnenga we-book-binder's whale ojikelezayo onjenge-vine-stalk ejikeleza isitokhwe se-ankile ehlayo-njengoko isitampu kwaye ihonjiswe ngasemva kunye namaphepha ezihloko zeencwadi ezininzi ezindala kunye nezintsha-eso sidalwa esihle kakhulu kodwa esilungileyo, esixeliweyo, ndiyithatha, kwimizobo efana ne-antique vases. Nangona kwihlabathi jikelele ihlengesi, nangona kunjalo ndiyibiza le ntlanzi ye-book-binder's ilinge lomnenga; kuba yayicetyiwe xa isixhobo saziswa okokuqala. Yaziswa ngumvakalisi omdala waseTaliyane ndaweni ithile malunga nenkulungwane ye-15, ngexesha lokuVuselelwa kokuFunda; kwaye ngaloo mihla, kwanakwixesha lasemva kwexesha elithelekisekayo, amahlengesi ayedume ukuba luhlobo lweLeviyatan.

Kwiivignettes kunye nezinye izihombiso zezinye iincwadi zakudala ngamanye amaxesha uya kudibana nokuchukumisa okunomdla kakhulu kwi-whale, apho zonke iintlobo ze-spouts, i-jets d'eau, imithombo eshushu kunye nokubanda, uSaratoga kunye ne-Baden-Baden, beza bephuma kwingqondo yakhe engaphelelwanga. Kwiphepha lesihloko lohlelo lokuqala "Ukuqhubela phambili kokuFunda" uya kufumana iminenga enomdla.

Kodwa siyeka zonke ezi nzame zingekho ngcathu, makhe sijonge loo mifanekiso yeleviyatan ecinga ukuba inengqondo, inkcazo yenzululwazi, ngabo baziyo. Kwingqokelela yohambo lukaHarris endala kukho iipleyiti zeminenga ethatyathwe kwincwadi yesiDatshi yohambo, AD 1671, enomxholo othi “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson waseFriesland, master.” Kwelinye lala macwecwe iminenga, njengezihlenga ezinkulu zezigodo, imelwe ilele phakathi kweziqithi zomkhenkce, zineebhere ezimhlophe ezibaleka phezu komqolo ophilayo. Kwenye ipleyiti, impazamo enkulu yenziwe ngokumela umnenga oneempukane ezincanyana.

Kwakhona, kukho iquarto enomtsalane, ebhalwe ngomnye uMphathi uColnett, iPost Captain kumkhosi wasemanzini wamaNgesi, enomxholo othi “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, ngenjongo yokwandisa iSpermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Kule ncwadi kukho ulwandlalo olubizwa ngokuba "nguMfanekiso wePhyseter okanye i-Spermaceti whale, etsalwa ngokwemilinganiselo ukusuka kulowo wabulawa kunxweme lwaseMexico, ngo-Agasti, ngo-1793, kwaye wanyuselwa kumgangatho." Andithandabuzi ukuba ukhapteni uwuthathele lo mfanekiso ulungileyo ukuze kuxhamle oomatiloshe bakhe. Ukukhankanya nje into enye ngayo, mandithi ineliso elalisebenza, ngokwesikali esihamba nalo, kumnenga okhule ngokupheleleyo, elinokwenza iliso lalo mnenga libe yifestile yesaphetha emalunga neemitha ezintlanu ubude. Hayi, umthetheli wam oligorha, kwakutheni ukuze ungasiniki uYona ejonge ngelaa liso?

Kananjalo ayilulo olona qulunqo lwesazela lweMbali yeNdalo ukuze kuxhamle abancinci nabathambileyo, bakhululekile kubugwenxa bempazamo efanayo. Jonga laa ncwadi idumileyo ethi “Indalo Ephilayo Yomkhandi Wegolide.” Kuhlelo olufutshane lwaseLondon lowe-1807, kukho iipleyiti zomnenga ekuthiwa “umnenga” kunye “nomnenga.” Andinqweneli ukubonakala ndingenambeko, kodwa lo mnenga ungabukekiyo ufana kakhulu nemazi yehagu enqunyuliweyo; yaye, ngokubhekisele kumnenga, ukukroba nje okukodwa kuyo kwanele ukukhwankqisa umntu, ukuba kule nkulungwane yeshumi elinesithoba loo mvubu inokuba nesundu ngokokwenene kulo naluphi na uluntu olukrelekrele lwamakhwenkwe esikolo.

Emva koko, kwakhona, ngo-1825, uBernard Germain, u-Count de Lacépède, isazi sendalo esikhulu, wapapasha incwadi yesayensi ye-whale, apho kukho imifanekiso emininzi yeentlobo ezahlukeneyo zeLeviyatan. Zonke ezi azichanekanga nje kuphela, kodwa umfanekiso Mysticetus okanye Greenland whale (oko kukuthi, umnenga Right), nkqu Scoresby, indoda ende amava ngokuchukumisa ukuba iintlobo, uvakalisa ukuba iqabane layo kwindalo.

Kodwa ukubekwa kwe-cap-sheaf kulo lonke eli shishini eliphazamisayo laligcinelwe inzululwazi uFrederick Cuvier, umzalwana kuBaron owaziwayo. Ngomnyaka we-1836, wapapasha iMbali yeNdalo yeMinenga, apho anika into ayibiza ngokuba ngumfanekiso weSperm Whale. Phambi kokuba ubonise lo mfanekiso kuyo nayiphi na i-Nantucketer, ububonelele ngcono ngesishwankathelo sakho sokurhoxa eNantucket. Ngelizwi nje, iSperm Whale kaFrederick Cuvier ayingoMnenga weSperm, kodwa sisikwashi. Kakade ke, akazange afumane ngenelo yohambo lokuloba iminenga (abantu abanjalo abafane babe nayo), kodwa ukuba wawuvela phi loo mfanekiso, ngubani onokuxela? Mhlawumbi wayifumana njengomanduleli wakhe kwezenzululwazi kwindawo enye, uDesmarest, wafumana enye yezisu zakhe eziyinyani; oko kukuthi, ukusuka kumzobo waseTshayina. Kwaye ngabaphi abafana abadlamkileyo abanepensile la maTshayina, iikomityi ezininzi ezityebileyo neesosi ziyasazisa.

Ngokuphathelele iminenga yabazobi bemiqondiso ebonwa ezitratweni ijinga phezu kweevenkile zabathengisi beoli, kuya kutshiwo ntoni ngayo? Ngokuqhelekileyo nguRichard III. iminenga, ene-dromedary humps, kwaye iyingozi kakhulu; Isidlo sakusasa kwiitiloshe ezintathu okanye ezine, iinqanawa zomnenga ezizele ngoomatiloshe: iziphene zabo zindanda kulwandle lwegazi kunye nepeyinti ezuba.

Kodwa ezi mpazamo zininzi ekufanekiseni umnenga azimangalisi kangako emva koko. Khawuqwalasele! Uninzi lwemizobo yezenzululwazi ithathwe kwiintlanzi ezixineneyo; yaye ezi zichane kanye njengomzobo wenqanawa eyaphukileyo, enomqolo owaphukileyo, unokusimela ngokufanelekileyo isilwanyana esibekekileyo ngokwaso kulo lonke ibhongo laso elingeyonxalenye yenyama yenqanawa. Nangona iindlovu zimile ubude bazo obupheleleyo, iLeviyatan ephilayo ayizange ikwazi ukudada ngokufanelekileyo kumfanekiso wayo. Umnenga ophilayo, ngobungangamsha bakhe obupheleleyo nokubaluleka kwawo, ubonakala kuphela elwandle kumanzi angenakuqondwa; kwaye inkitha yakhe ayibonakali, njengenqanawa eya kuluhlu lwedabi; kwaye ngaphandle kweso siqalelo yinto engenakwenzeka ngonaphakade ukuba umntu ofayo amphakamisele emoyeni emoyeni, ukuze alondoloze konke ukudumba kwakhe okunamandla kunye nokuphazamiseka. Kwaye, singasathethi ke ngomahluko oqikelelwayo wecontour phakathi komnenga omncinci owanyayo kunye neLeviyatan kaPlato ekhule ngokupheleleyo; ukanti, nakwimeko yomnye waloo minenga incinci ifunxayo iphakanyiselwe kumgangatho wenqanawa, unjalo ke umilo wakhe ongaqhelekanga, onjengeeel, onamaqunube, awahlukeneyo, kangangokuba intetho yakhe echanekileyo umtyholi ngokwakhe akanakuyibamba.

Kodwa kusenokuba yinto enqwenelekayo, ukuba kumathambo ahamba ze womnenga ovalelweyo, amacebiso achanekileyo anokuthathwa achukumisa ubume bakhe bokwenyani. Hayi akunjalo. Kuba yenye yezinto ezinomdla ngakumbi ngale Leviyatan, ukuba i-skeleton yakhe inika umbono omncinci wemilo yakhe jikelele. Nangona i-skeleton kaJeremy Bentham, ejinga kwi-candelabra kwithala leencwadi lomnye wababulali bakhe, idlulisela ngokuchanekileyo ingcamango yendoda endala ye-burly-browed utilitarian, nazo zonke ezinye iimpawu eziphambili zobuntu bukaJeremy; ukanti akukho nto ilolu hlobo inokuchazwa kuwo nawaphi na amathambo eleviyatan. Ngapha koko, njengoko uMzingeli omkhulu esitsho, amathambo omnenga anobudlelwane obufanayo nesilwanyana esityalwe ngokupheleleyo kunye nesinamaqhuqhuva njengoko isinambuzane sisenza kwi-chrysalis esiyigubungele ngokusangqa. Oku kungaqhelekanga kubonakaliswe ngokumangalisayo entlokweni, njengoko kwenye inxalenye yale ncwadi iya kuboniswa ngengozi. Ikwaboniswa ngendlela emangalisayo kwifin yecala, amathambo aphantse aphendule ngokuthe ngqo kumathambo esandla somntu, thabatha ubhontsi kuphela. Eli phiko lineminwe emine eqhelekileyo yamathambo, isalathisi, umbindi, umsesane, kunye nomnwe omncinci. Kodwa zonke ezi zihleli ngokusisigxina kwisigqubuthelo sazo senyama, njengeminwe yomntu kwisigqubuthelo sokwenziwa. “Noko ke ngokungakhathali maxa wambi umnenga usenokusikhonza,” watsho njalo uStubb owayeqhula ngenye imini, “akunakuze kuthiwe uyasiphatha ngaphandle kweemittens.”

Ngenxa yazo zonke ezi zizathu, ke, nangayiphi na indlela onokuyijonga ngayo, kufuneka ugqibe kwelokuba iLeviyatan enkulu seso sidalwa esinye ehlabathini esimele sihlale singapeyintwanga de kuse ekugqibeleni. Liyinyaniso elokuba, umfanekiso omnye unokufikela uphawu kufutshane kunomnye, kodwa akukho namnye unokuwubetha ngalo naluphi na uhlobo oluchanekileyo. Ngoko akukho ndlela yasemhlabeni yokufumanisa ukuba ngokwenene unjani umnenga. Kwaye ekuphela kwendlela onokufumana ngayo ingcamango enyamezelekayo yecontour yakhe ephilayo, kukuhamba ngeminenga ngokwakho; kodwa ngokwenza njalo, awukho mngciphekweni omncinci wokuba nesitovu sanaphakade kwaye utyiswe nguye. Ke ngoko, kubonakala kum ukuba ubungaphambuki kakhulu ekufuneni kwakho leleviyatan.





ISAHLUKO SAMA-56. NgeMifanekiso eNgenempazamo yeMinenga, kunye neMifanekiso eyiNyaniso yeZiqephu zoMnenga.

Ngokudibanisa nemifanekiso eyoyikisayo yeminenga, ndihendwa kakhulu apha ukuba ndingene kula mabali amnandi ngakumbi awo afumaneka kwiincwadi ezithile, zakudala kunye nezanamhlanje, ngakumbi kwiPliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, njl.

Ndiyazi ngamagqabantshintshi amane kuphela eSperm Whale enkulu; UColnett, ekaHuggins, ekaFrederick Cuvier, kunye noBeale. Kwisahluko esidlulileyo uColnett noCuvier kubhekiselwe kubo. I-Huggins's ibhetele kakhulu kuneyabo; kodwa, ngeengxaki ezinkulu, iBeale yeyona ilungileyo. Yonke imizobo yeBeale yalo mnenga ilungile, ngaphandle komzobo ophakathi kumfanekiso wemikhomo emithathu kwiimo zengqondo ezahlukeneyo, ibamba isahluko sakhe sesibini. Umphambili wakhe, izikhephe ezihlasela iSperm Whales, nangona ngokungathandabuzekiyo zibalwa ukuvuselela ukuthandabuza kwamadoda athile ahlala kwigumbi lokuhlala, ichanekile ngokuncomekayo kwaye ifana nobomi kwisiphumo sayo ngokubanzi. Eminye imizobo yeSperm Whale eJ. Ross Browne ichanekile kakuhle kwicontour; zikrolwe ububi. Ayilotyala lakhe elo.

Kwi-Right Whale, eyona mifanekiso ibalaseleyo yolwandlalo iku-Scoresby; kodwa zitsalwa kwisikali esincinane kakhulu ukuze zidlulise ingcamango enqwenelekayo. Unomfanekiso omnye wemiboniso yeminenga, kwaye oku kukunqongophala okulusizi, kuba kungenxa yemifanekiso enjalo kuphela, xa konke kwenziwe kakuhle, unokufumana nantoni na efana nombono oyinyaniso womnenga ophilayo njengoko ubonwa ngabazingeli bakhe abaphilayo.

Kodwa, kuthathwe kubo bonke, okona kulungileyo, nangona kwezinye iinkcukacha kungezona ezona zichanekileyo, imiboniso yeminenga kunye nemiboniso yeminenga inokufumaneka naphi na, yimikrolo emibini emikhulu yesiFrentshi, eyenziwe kakuhle, kwaye ithathwe kwimizobo yeGarnery enye. Ngokulandelelana, bamele ukuhlaselwa kweSperm kunye neRight Whale. Kumkrolo wokuqala iSperm Whale ebekekileyo iboniswa ngobungangamsha obupheleleyo bamandla, ivele yaphakama nje ngaphantsi kwesikhephe ukusuka kumbindi wolwandle, kwaye ithwele phezulu emoyeni emqolo ukuqhekeka okumanyumnyezi kwamaplanga eziko. Umphambili wephenyane awophukanga ngokuyinxenye, kwaye uzotywa nje ulungelelaniswe phezu komqolo werhamncwa; uze ume kuloo mqolo, kwangelo xesha linye lingenakulinganiswa nanto, ubona umbhexi, egutyungelwe livumba lomnenga obilayo, yaye etsiba-tsiba ngokungathi usuka eweni. Umsebenzi wayo yonke le nto ulungile ngokumangalisayo kwaye uyinyani. Ibhafu yomgca enesiqingatha engenanto idada kulwandle olumhlophe; izibonda zokhuni zeehapuni ezichithekileyo zibhobhile kuyo; iintloko zabasebenzi abaqubhayo zithe saa malunga nomnenga ngeendlela ezahlukeneyo zokoyika; ngelixa kumgama omnyama osisaqhwithi inqanawa ijonge ezantsi kuloo ndawo. Impazamo enkulu inokufunyanwa kwiinkcukacha ze-anatomical zalo mnenga, kodwa makudlule oko; ekubeni, ngenxa yobomi bam, Andikwazanga ukuzoba entle kangaka.

Kumzobo wesibini, isikhephe sikwisenzo sokuzoba ecaleni kwecala elibiyelweyo loMnenga omkhulu obalekayo, oqengqa ukhula lwakhe olumnyama elwandle ngathi yi-mossy rock-slide evela kumawa ePatagonian. Iindiza zayo zithe tye, zizele, zimnyama njengothuthu; ukuze kuphuphuma umsi onjalo kwitshimini, ungacinga ukuba kukho ukupheka kwesidlo sangokuhlwa ngenkalipho kwizibilini ezinkulu ezingezantsi. Iintaka zaselwandle zigqogqa oononkala abancinane, iintlanzi zoonokrwece, nezinye iilekese zaselwandle neemaccaroni, ezithi maxa wambi zithwalwe nguMnenga oyiRight kumqolo wakhe ophilayo. Kwaye ngalo lonke elo xesha ileviyatan enemilebe engqindilili igxalathelana enzonzobileni, ishiya iitoni zamagaqa amhlophe axokozelayo ekuvukeni kwayo, kwaye ibangela isikhephe esincinci ukuba sishukume kwimibhobho efana nesikhephe esibanjiswe kufutshane namavili omkhombe wolwandle. Ngaloo ndlela, umphambili yingxokozelo; kodwa ngasemva, ngokwahlukileyo kobugcisa obuncomekayo, inqanaba leglasi lolwandle lizolile, iiseyile ezithontsizayo ezingenastarched zenqanawa engenamandla, kunye nobunzima obunzima bomnenga ofileyo, inqaba eyoyisiweyo, kunye neflegi yokubamba ixhonywe ngobuvila kwipali yomnenga efakwe kwi-spout-mngxuma yakhe.

Ngubani uGarnery umzobi, okanye wayengubani, andazi. Kodwa ubomi bam ngenxa yabo wayekwazi ukuthetha ngesifundo sakhe, okanye ngenye indlela ngokumangalisayo wayefundiswe ngumnenga onamava. AmaFrentshi ngabantwana bokwenza umsebenzi wokupeyinta. Hamba uye kujonga kuyo yonke imizobo yaseYurophu, kwaye uya kuyifumana phi igalari yokuphila kunye nesiphithiphithi sokuphefumla kwiseyile, njengakuloo holo yoloyiso eVersailles; apho obukeleyo esilwa ngendlela yakhe, upell-mell, ngamadabi amakhulu alandelelanayo eFransi; apho lonke ikrele libonakala lidanyaza kwiZibane zaseMntla, kwaye ookumkani abaxhobileyo abalandelelanayo kunye nama-Emperors atyhutyha, njengentlawulo yeecentaurs ezithweswe isithsaba? Ayifanelanga kwaphela indawo kule galari, zezi zixhobo zaselwandle zaseGarnery.

Ubuchule bendalo bamaFrentshi bokubamba umzobo wezinto bubonakala bubonakaliswa ngokukodwa kwimizobo nemizobo abanayo kwimiboniso yabo yokuloba. Ngaphandle kwesinye eshumini samava eNgilani kushishino lokuloba, kwaye ingeyiyo inxalenye yewaka yabemi baseMelika, nangona kunjalo baye banika ezi zizwe zombini imizobo ekuphela kwayo egqityiweyo kwaphela ekwaziyo ukuhambisa umoya wokwenene wokuzingela iminenga. Ubukhulu becala, abadwebi beminenga bamaNgesi nabaseMelika babonakala baneliseke ngokupheleleyo ngokubonisa ulwandlalo lwezinto, ezifana neprofayili engenamntu yomnenga; leyo, ngokumalunga nokubukeka kwesiphumo, imalunga nokuzoba iprofayile yephiramidi. Nkqu Scoresby, ngokusesikweni eyaziwayo Umnenga Right, emva kokuba esinike ubude obuqinileyo ngokupheleleyo umnenga Greenland, kunye ezintathu okanye ezine miniatures eethe-ethe of narwhale kunye porpoises, usiphatha kuthotho imikrolo classical amagwegwe iphenyane, iimela zokunqunqa, kunye grapnels; kunye nenkuthalo encinci ye-Leuwenhoeck inikezela ekuhlolweni kwehlabathi elingcangcazelayo amashumi alithoba anesithandathu afane ne-fac-similes yeekristale zekhephu ze-Arctic ezandisiweyo. Andithethi ukudelela umkhenkethi ogqwesileyo (ndiyamnika imbeko njengegqala), kodwa kumcimbi obaluleke kakhulu ngokuqinisekileyo yayikukubeka iliso kungakhange kufumaneke i-afidavithi efungelweyo ethathwe phambi koBulungisa boXolo baseGreenland.

Ukongezelela kuloo mikrolo mihle yaseGarnery, kukho eminye imikrolo emibini yesiFrentshi ekufanele iphawulwe, ebhalwe ngomnye umrhumo othi “H. Banqwenela ukuba irhamncwa ebezizezalo ezi ngalo ngokuqhelekileyo libambelela kumandlalo wolwandle; nokuba umnenga obizwa ngokuba yi<em>sperm whale, ngokungafaniyo nezinye iintlobo, unikwa amazinyo ukuze uwuhlasele uze uwukrazule.

Kubonakala ngathi umhlaba unokucinga ukuba iKraken enkulu kaBhishophu uPontoppodan inokuthi ekugqibeleni isombulule ibe yiskwidi. Indlela uBhishophu ayichaza ngayo, njengokunyuka nokutshona ngokutshintshanayo, nezinye iinkcukacha azibalisayo, kuzo zonke ezi zinto zimbini ziyahambelana. Kodwa ukuthotywa okuninzi kuyafuneka ngokubhekiselele kubuninzi obumangalisayo abunikayo.

Ngabanye abantu bendalo abathe bava amahemuhemu ngesidalwa esingaqondakaliyo, ekuthethwa ngaso apha, sibandakanyiwe kudidi lwentlanzi ekuthiwa yicuttlefish, ekuthi, eneneni, kwimiba ethile yangaphandle ibonakale ngathi yeyabo, kodwa njenge-Anaki yesizwe.





ISAHLUKO 60. Umgca.

Ngokubhekiselele kwindawo yokuloba iminenga kungekudala eza kuchazwa, kunye nokuqonda ngcono zonke izigcawu ezifanayo kwenye indawo ezithiwe thaca, ndilapha ukuthetha ngomlingo, ngamanye amaxesha umgca womnenga owoyikekayo.

Umgca owawusetyenziswa ekuqaleni kushishino lokuloba wawuyeyona hemp igqwesileyo, umphunga kancinane yitela, ungafakwanga kuyo, njengakwimeko yeentambo eziqhelekileyo; kuba ngeli lixa itela, njengoko iqhele ukusetyenziswa, yenza i-hemp ibe lula ngakumbi kumenzi wentambo, kwaye kananjalo inikezela intambo ngokwayo ilungele ngakumbi umatiloshe kusetyenziso lwenqanawa oluqhelekileyo; ukanti, isixa esiqhelekileyo asiyi kuwenza uqine kakhulu umgca womnenga ukulungiselela ukusongelwa ngokusondeleyo ekufuneka uphantsi kwawo; kodwa njengoko uninzi lwabamatiloshe beqalisa ukufunda, itela ngokubanzi ayongezi kwaphela ekomeleleni okanye ekomeleleni kwentambo, nokokuba ingayinika kangakanani na ukubumbana kunye neglosi.

Kwiminyaka yamva nje intambo yeManilla kushishino lokuloba eMelika iphantse yayisusa ngokupheleleyo ihemp njengesixhobo semigca yomnenga; kuba, nangona ingenako ixesha elide njengehemp, yomelele, kwaye ithambile ngakumbi kwaye iyalastiki; kwaye ndiya kongeza (ekubeni kukho i-æsthetics kuzo zonke izinto), ibukeka ngakumbi kwaye ifanele isikhephe, kune-hemp. I-Hemp ngumntu omnyama, obumnyama, uhlobo lwamaNdiya; kodwa uManilla ufana neCircassian eneenwele zegolide ukujonga.

I-whale-line isibini kwisithathu se-intshi ubukhulu. Ekuqaleni, ubungayi kucinga ukuba yomelele njengoko injalo ngokwenene. Ngovavanyo imisonto yayo enye kunye namashumi amahlanu iya kuxhoma ubunzima bekhulu elinamashumi amabini eeponti; ukuze yonke intambo ithwale ubunzima obuphantse bulingane neetoni ezintathu. Ngobude, i-common sperm whale-line ilinganisa into engaphezu kwamakhulu amabini eefathom. Ngasemva kwesikhephe ibhijelwe ngokujikelezayo ebhafini, hayi njengombhobho wombungu wento emileyo, kodwa ukuze yenze umjikelo omnye, ubunzima obububume obufana nesonka samasi "izithungu" ezixineneyo zebhedi, okanye iileya ze-spiralizations ezixineneyo, ngaphandle komgongxo kodwa "intliziyo," okanye ityhubhu ethe nkqo engumzuzu eyenziwe kwiaxis yetshizi. Njengoko eyona ntanjana incinane okanye iklinkca kwi-coiling inokuthi, xa ibaleka, ikhuphe ingalo yomntu, umlenze, okanye umzimba uphela, elona nyathelo lokhuseleko lisetyenziswa ekubekeni umgca ebhafini. Ezinye iiharpooneers ziya kudla phantse yonke intsasa kweli shishini, zithwele umgca phezulu phezulu kwaye emva koko ziwujongise ezantsi ngebhloko ukuya ebhafini, ukuze ijiyile ukuyikhulula kuyo yonke imibimbi kunye nokujijeka okunokwenzeka.

Kwiinqanawa zamaNgesi kusetyenziswa iibhafu ezimbini endaweni yesinye; umgca omnye ubhijelwa ngokuqhubekayo kuzo zombini iityhubhu. Kukho inxenye koku; ngenxa yokuba ezi ityhubhu zimbini zincinane kakhulu zingena lula enqanaweni, kwaye aziwuxinzeleleki kangako; kanti, ibhafu yaseMelika, ephantse ilingane neenyawo ezintathu ububanzi nobunzulu obulinganayo, isenza umthwalo omkhulu wenqanawa omaplanga aso asisiqingatha-intshi enye ubukhulu; kuba emazantsi ephenyane lomnenga kufana nomkhenkce obalulekileyo, oya kuthwala ubunzima obuninzi obabiweyo, kodwa hayi kakhulu kugxininise. Xa isigqubuthelo seseyile esipeyintiweyo siqhwatywa kwi-tub yomgca yaseMelika, isikhephe sijongeka ngathi siphuma ngeqebengwana elikhulu lomtshato lokubonisa iminenga.

Zombini iziphelo zomgca zibonakalisiwe; isiphelo esisezantsi sinqamla kwi-eye-splice okanye i-loop evela ezantsi kwicala le-tub, kwaye ijinga phezu komphetho wayo ikhutshwe ngokupheleleyo kuyo yonke into. Eli lungiselelo lesiphelo esisezantsi liyimfuneko kwiiakhawunti ezimbini. Eyokuqala: Ukuze kube lula ukuqiniswa komgca owongezelelweyo osuka kwisikhephe esingummelwane, ukuba umnenga ubethwe uvakala unzulu ngokokude ugrogrise ukukhupha wonke umgca owawuncanyathiselwe kwiharpoon. Kule mizekelo, i-whale ngokuqinisekileyo iguqulwa njengemagi ye-ale, njengokuba kunjalo, ukusuka kwisikhephe esinye ukuya kwesinye; nangona isikhephe sokuqala sisoloko sindanda ukuze sincede iqabane laso. Okwesibini: Eli lungiselelo liyimfuneko ngenxa yokhuseleko olufanayo; kuba ngaba isiphelo esisezantsi somgca nangayiphi na indlela encanyathiselwe enqanaweni, kwaye ngaba umnenga ngoko ukubaleka umgca ngaphandle ukuya ekupheleni phantse ngomzuzu omnye, ukutshaya njengoko ngamanye amaxesha esenza, wayengayi kumisa apho, kuba iphenyane eligwetyiweyo liya kurhuqelwa phantsi emva kwakhe ukuya kubunzulu bolwandle; kwaye xa kunjalo akukho mntu umemeza edolophini unokuze amfumane kwakhona.

Ngaphambi kokuba kuthotywe isikhephe ukuze sileqe, isiphelo esiphezulu somgca sithatyathwa ngasemva ebhafini, size sijikeleze i-loggerhead apho, siphinde siqhutyelwe phambili bonke ubude bephenyane, siphumle ngokunqamlezileyo phezu kwentambo yokuluka okanye umqheba wephini lokubhexa lomntu ngamnye, ukuze ligxadazele esihlahleni sakhe ekubhexeni; kananjalo edlula phakathi kwamadoda, njengoko ehlala ngokutshintshisanayo kwimipu echaseneyo, ukuya kwiichoki ezikhokelayo okanye iigrooves kumphambili otsolo ogqithileyo wephenyane, apho isikhonkwane somthi okanye i-skewer esilingana nentsinjana eqhelekileyo, sithintela ukuba singatyibiliki ngaphandle. Ukusuka kwi-chocks ijinga kwi-festoon encinci phezu kwezaphetha, kwaye emva koko idluliselwe ngaphakathi kwinqanawa kwakhona; yaye iifatom ezimalunga neshumi okanye ezingamashumi amabini (ezibizwa ngokuba ngumgca webhokisi) zibhijelwe phezu kwebhokisi ekwizaphetha, iqhubela phambili nendlela yayo ukuya kwi-gunwale isekude kancinane ngasemva, ize ke ibotshelelwe kulwakhiwo olufutshane—intambo edityaniswa ngokukhawuleza nehapuni; kodwa ngaphambi kolu nxibelelwano, i-warp emfutshane ihamba ngeendlela ezininzi ezingaqondakaliyo ezidinayo kwiinkcukacha.

Ngaloo ndlela i-whale-line isonge isikhephe sonke kwiikhoyili zaso ezintsonkothileyo, ijija kwaye ijikeleza malunga namacala onke. Bonke ababhexi babandakanyeka kwiindlela zayo eziyingozi; kangangokuba kwiliso elineentloni lomnini-mhlaba, babonakale benjengeenjubaqa zamaIndiya, ezinezona nyoka zibulalayo zithe thaa amalungu awo ngokudlalayo. Kananjalo akanako nawuphi na unyana womfazi onokufa, okwesihlandlo sokuqala, ukuba ahlale phakathi kwezo zintsonkothe ​​ze-hempen, aze ngoxa ezama kangangoko ukubheqa, acinge ukuba nangaliphi na ixesha elingaziwayo i-harpoon inokudutyulwa, kwaye zonke ezi ngqungquthela zoyikekayo zidlalwe njengemibane ekhencezayo; akanako ukujongana ngolo hlobo ngaphandle kokungcangcazela okwenza umongo emathanjeni akhe ungcangcazele kuye njengejeli eshukunyiswayo. Kodwa umkhwa-into engaqhelekanga! yintoni engenakufezwa ngumkhwa?—Ii-Gayer sallies, ulonwabo olungakumbi, iziqhulo ezilunge ngakumbi, kunye neerepartees eziqaqambileyo, akuzange uve phezu kwe-mahogany yakho, kunokuba uya kuva phezu komsedare omhlophe wesiqingatha se-intshi yesikhephe somnenga, xa ngaloo ndlela ujinga kwiintambo ze-hangman; kwaye, njengamaqhekeza amathandathu eCalais phambi koKumkani u-Edward, amadoda amathandathu aqamba abasebenzi atsalela imihlathi yokufa, ene-halter entanyeni nganye, njengoko unokuthi.

Mhlawumbi ukucinga nje okuncinane kuya kukwenza ukwazi ukuphendula ngeentlekele zokuloba iminenga ngokuphindaphindiweyo—ezinye ezimbalwa kuzo ekuthethwa ngazo ngokungakhathali—zale ndoda okanye laloo ndoda ikhutshwa enqanaweni ngomgca, yaza yalahleka. Kuba, xa umgca uphumela ngaphandle, ukuze uhlale ngoko enqanaweni, kufana nokuhlaliswa phakathi kwezandi ezininzi zokutswina kwe-injini yomphunga edlala ngokupheleleyo, xa yonke imiqadi ephaphazelayo, neshafti, nevili, ikudla ingca. Kubi ngakumbi; kuba aninakukwazi ukuhlala ningashukumi entliziyweni yezi ngozi, ngenxa yokuba isikhephe siyahexa oku kwebhedi, yaye ugxumeka ngapha nangapha, ngaphandle kwesilumkiso esincinane; kwaye kuphela nge-buoyancy ethile yokuzilungisa kunye nokudityaniswa kokuzikhethela kunye nesenzo, unokuphunyuka usenziwa iMazeppa, kwaye ubaleke apho ilanga elibona yonke into ngokwakhe lingenakuze likugqobhoze.

Kwakhona: njengokuba ukuzola okunzulu okubonakalayo kuphela kwandulela kwaye kuprofethe ngoqhwithela, mhlawumbi kubi kakhulu kunoqhwithela ngokwaso; kuba, okunene, ukuzola sisigqubuthela nemvulophu yoqhwithela; kwaye iqulethe kuyo ngokwayo, njengoko umpu obonakala ungenabungozi ubambe umgubo obulalayo, kunye nebhola, kunye nogqabhuko-dubulo; Ngoko ukuphumla okuthandekayo komgca, njengoko inyoka ngokuthe cwaka malunga nababhexi ngaphambi kokuba bangeniswe ekudlaleni okwenyani-le yinto ethwele ukoyikeka kokwenyani kunayo nayiphi na enye inkalo yalo mcimbi uyingozi. Kodwa kutheni sithetha ngakumbi? Bonke abantu bahlala begutyungelwe yimigca yomnenga. Bonke bazalwa benezikhewu ezintanyeni zabo; kodwa kuphela xa bebanjwe kukufa okukhawulezileyo, ngequbuliso, apho abantu abafayo babona iingozi ezithuleyo, ezifihlakeleyo, ezihlala zikho ebomini. Kwaye ukuba uyintanda-bulumko, nangona uhleli kwisikhephe somnenga, awuyi kuthi entliziyweni uzive usoyika ngakumbi, kunokuba uhleli phambi komlilo wakho wangokuhlwa kunye ne-poker, kwaye hayi i-harpoon, ecaleni kwakho.
