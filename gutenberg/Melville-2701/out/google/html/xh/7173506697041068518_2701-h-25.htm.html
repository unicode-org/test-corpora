Epilogue

“NDISINDE ndedwa, ukuze ndikuxelele” uYobhi.

Idrama yenziwe. Kutheni ke ukuba ubani aphume apha?—Kungenxa yokuba umntu wasinda kuloo ngozi.

Kuye kwenzeka, ukuba emva kokunyamalala kukaParsee, ndandingulowo uFates wammisela ukuba athathe indawo yesaphetha sika-Ahabhi, xa umsaphetha wayethatha isikhundla esingenamntu; okwathi, ngomhla wokugqibela xa la madoda mathathu akhutshelwa ngaphandle kwephenyane elishukumayo, wawiswa ngasemva. Ke, ndidada kumda womboniso olandelayo, kwaye ndiwubona ngokupheleleyo, xa ukufunxa isiqingatha sesikhephe esizikileyo safika kum, ngelo xesha, kodwa ngokuthe chu, nditsaleleka ukuya kwi-vortex yokuvala. Ukufika kwam kuyo, yayisele ithontsile yatsho yatshona kwidama elimnandi. Ukujikeleza kunye nokujikeleza, emva koko, kwaye uhlala ukhonkotha ukuya kwiqhosha-elifana neqamza elimnyama kwi-axis yesangqa esijikelezayo esicothayo, njengenye i-Ixion endiyijikelezileyo. Ukude, ekufumaneni elo ziko libalulekileyo, iqamza elimnyama libheke phezulu lagqabhuka; kwaye ngoku, ikhululwe ngenxa yomthombo wayo wobuqili, kwaye, ngenxa yobumnandi bayo obukhulu, iphakama ngamandla amakhulu, ibhokisi yobomi yadubula ngobude ukusuka elwandle, yawa, yadada ecaleni kwam. Ndichukunyiswe yiloo bhokisi, phantse imini yonke nobusuku bonke, ndadada phezu kwesixhobo esithambileyo nesifana nesimbonono. Ookrebe abangonzakalisiyo, babebhadula ngokungathi kukho imikhusane emilonyeni yabo; ookhetshe baselwandle abakhohlakeleyo bahamba ngesikhephe ngemilomo. Ngosuku lwesibini, kwasondela iseyile, yaza yandifunqula ekugqibeleni. YayinguRakeli owayephambukile, owathi xa wayekhangela abantwana bakhe abalahlekileyo, wafumana enye inkedama kuphela.
