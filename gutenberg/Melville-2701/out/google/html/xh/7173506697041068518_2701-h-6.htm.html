INCWADI I. ( Folio ), ISAHLUKO III. ( Fin-Back ).—Ngaphantsi kwale ntloko ndibala i-monster leyo, ngamagama ahlukeneyo kaFin-Back, uTall-Spout, noLong-John, eye yabonwa phantse kulo lonke ulwandle kwaye ngokuqhelekileyo ingumnenga omoya wayo umnenga osoloko uchazwa ngabakhweli abawela iAtlantiki, kwiipakethe-tracks zaseNew York. Ngobude abufumanayo, kunye ne-baleen yakhe, i-Fin-back ifana nomnenga ofanelekileyo, kodwa i-portly girth encinci, kunye nombala okhanyayo, usondela kumnquma. Imilebe yakhe emikhulu ibonisa inkalo efana nentambo, eyenziwe ngokudibanisa, ukugoba okugobileyo kwemibimbi emikhulu. Uphawu lwakhe olukhulu olwahlulayo, iphiko, afumana kulo igama lakhe, lidla ngokuba yinto ebonakalayo. Eli phiko limalunga neemitha ezintathu okanye ezine ubude, likhula ngokuthe nkqo ukusuka kwindawo engasemva yomva, limile okwe-engular, kwaye linencam etsolo kakhulu. Nokuba ayibonakali neyona ndawo incinci yesidalwa, eli phiko likwanti, ngamanye amaxesha, liya kubonakala ngokucacileyo liphuma phezulu. Xa ulwandle luzolile ngokuphakathi, kwaye luphawulwe kancinane ngamazambuku angqukuva, kwaye le mpumlo ifana ne-gnomon iphakama kwaye iphosa izithunzi kumphezulu oshwabeneyo, kunokucingelwa ukuba isangqa samanzi esisijikelezileyo ngandlel’ ithile sifana nokucofa, kunye nesitayile saso kunye nemigca yeyure ene-wavy igcwelwe kuyo. Kweso sidawuli sika-Ahazi isithunzi ngokufuthi sibuyela umva. I-Fin-Back ayithandeki. Ubonakala engumthiyile womnenga, njengoko abanye abantu bethiyile abantu. Uneentloni kakhulu; uhlala eyedwa; ukunyuka ngokungalindelekanga ukuya kumphezulu weyona ndawo ikude kunye nawona manzi ashushu; Inqwelo-moya yakhe ethe tye nephakamileyo inyuka njengomkhonto omde oyingozi kwithafa elingumqwebedu; uphiwe ngamandla amangalisayo nesantya sokuqubha, njengokudelela konke ukusukela umntu; leleviyatan ibonakala igxothiwe kwaye ingenakoyiswa uKayin wohlanga lwakhe, ethwele uphawu lwakhe ngolo hlobo emqolo. Ukususela ekubeni ne-baleen emlonyeni wakhe, i-Fin-Back ngamanye amaxesha ifakwe kunye ne-right whale, phakathi kweentlobo zethiyori ezibizwa ngokuba yi-Whalebone whales , oko kukuthi, iminenga ene-baleen. Kwezi zibizwa ngokuba yi-Whalebone whales, kubonakala ngathi kukho iintlobo ezininzi, uninzi lwazo, nangona kunjalo, ziyaziwa kancinci. iminenga enempumlo ebanzi kunye neminenga enemilomo; iminenga enentloko yepike; iminenga edibeneyo; iminenga engaphantsi kwemihlathi kunye neminenga erostrated, ngamagama abalobi kwiintlobo ezimbalwa.

Ngokubhekiselele kwesi sibheno se "Whalebone whales," kubaluleke kakhulu ukukhankanya, ukuba nangona kunjalo i-nomenclature enjalo ingaba lula ekuququzeleleni ukubhekisela kuhlobo oluthile lweminenga, kodwa kulilize ukuzama ukuhlelwa okucacileyo kweLeviyatan, esekelwe phezu kwe-baleen yakhe, okanye i-hump, okanye i-fin, okanye amazinyo; Nangona ezo nxalenye ziphawulweyo okanye iimpawu ngokucacileyo zibonakala zilungelelaniswe ngcono ukuhlawula isiseko senkqubo eqhelekileyo yeCetology kunayo nayiphi na enye imilinganiselo yomzimba efihliweyo, apho umnenga, ngeentlobo zakhe, ubonisa. Njani ke ngoko? I-baleen, i-hump, i-back-fin, kunye namazinyo; ezi zizinto ezikhethekileyo ezisasazwe ngokungakhethiyo phakathi kwazo zonke iintlobo zeminenga, ngaphandle kokujonga nokuba yintoni na ubume besakhiwo sabo kwezinye kunye neenkcukacha ezibaluleke ngakumbi. Ngaloo ndlela, umnenga obizwa ngokuba yi<em>sperm whale nomnenga one<em>humpbacked whale, ngamnye uneqhuma; Ke khona umzekeliso uyanqanyulwa. Kwiintlobo ngeentlobo zeminenga, yenza imidibaniso engaqhelekanga ngolo hlobo; okanye, kwimeko yawo nawuphi na omnye wabo, ukubekwa wedwa okungaqhelekanga; njengokuchasa ngokupheleleyo yonke indlela jikelele eyenziweyo ngesiseko esinjalo. Kweli litye zonke iingcali zendalo zomnenga zaqhekeka.

( Narwhale ), oko kukuthi, umnenga weNostril. —Esinye isiganeko somnenga owaziwayo owaziwayo ngegama, ndicingela ukuba kuphondo lwakhe olukhethekileyo kwakucingwa ukuba yimpumlo ephakame kakhulu. Isidalwa simalunga neemitha ezilishumi elinesithandathu ubude, ngelixa uphondo lwayo luziimitha ezintlanu, nangona ezinye zingaphezu kweshumi, kwaye zifikelela kwishumi elinesihlanu leemitha. Ayibonakali ngathi isetyenziswa njengencakuba yentlanzi yekrele kunye nebhilikhwe; nangona abanye oomatiloshe bendixelela ukuba iNarwhale iyisebenzisela iraki ekujikeni emazantsi olwandle ukuze ifumane ukutya. UCharley Coffin uthe yayisetyenziselwa ukugqobhoza umkhenkce; kuba iNarwhale, inyuka iye phezu koLwandle oluNcama, yaza yalifumana igqunywe ngumkhenkce, yaluphakamisa uphondo lwayo, yaza ke ngoko yatyhoboza. Kodwa awukwazi ukungqina nokuba enye yezi ngqikelelo ichanekile. Olwam uluvo lwelokuba, nangona olu phondo lucalanye lunokusetyenziswa ngokwenene yiNarwhale—nangona oko kusenokuba njalo—ngokuqinisekileyo kuya kuba luncedo kakhulu kuye ukuba afumane ifolda yokufunda amaphecana. INarwhale endikhe ndayiva ibizwa ngokuba yiTusked whale, iHorned whale, kunye neUnicorn whale. Ngokuqinisekileyo ungumzekelo onomdla we-Unicornism ofumaneka phantse kubo bonke ubukumkani bendalo ephilayo. Kubabhali abathile abadala ndiqokelele ukuba olu phondo lwe-unicorn yaselwandle lwaluthathwa ngeentsuku zamandulo njengeyeza lokuchasa ityhefu, kwaye ngenxa yoko, amalungiselelo alo azisa amaxabiso amakhulu. Kwakhona yagalelwa ukuba ibe neetyuwa eziguquguqukayo zamanenekazi atyhafileyo, kanye ngendlela efanayo neempondo zexhama eliduna ezenziweyo zibe yihartshorn. Ekuqaleni yayigqalwa njengento ebangela umdla omkhulu. I-Black Letter indixelela ukuba uMhlekazi uMartin Frobisher ekubuyeni kwakhe kolo hambo, xa uKumkanikazi uBess wawangawangisa ngobukroti isandla sakhe esinamatye anqabileyo kuye evela kwifestile yeGreenwich Palace, njengoko inqanawa yakhe ekhaliphileyo yayisihla ngeThames; “Xa uMhlekazi uMartin wabuya kolo hambo lwaselwandle,” utshilo uBlack Letter, “eguqe ngamadolo wazisa kuye uphondo olude lweNarwhale, olwathi ixesha elide lujinga kwinqaba yaseWindsor.” Umbhali wase-Ireland uyaphikisa ukuba i-Earl yaseLeicester, eguqe ngamadolo, yenza ngokunjalo ekuphakameni kwakhe olunye uphondo, oluphathelele kwirhamncwa elisemhlabeni lendalo ye-unicorn.

I-Narwhale inomfanekiso omhle kakhulu, inkangeleko efana nengwe, inombala womhlaba omhlophe wobisi, onamachaphaza angqukuva kunye ne-oblong emnyama. Ioli yakhe iphezulu kakhulu, icacile kwaye ilungile; kodwa incinci, kwaye akazingelwa ngokungaqhelekanga. Ufumaneka kakhulu kwiilwandle ezijikelezayo.

Ukhwelisa umqolo womnenga iFolio, yaye njengoko edada, usebenza indlela yakhe ngokumkatsa; njengoko abanye abafundisi besikolo bedibana kwihlabathi ngenkqubo efanayo. Kuncinci okwaziwayo ngeThrasher kuneyoMbulali. Omabini ngabaphuli-mthetho, nakwiilwandle ezichasene nomthetho.

Iphela njalo INCWADI II. ( Octavo ), kwaye iqala INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ).

DUODECIMOES.—Ezi ziquka iminenga emincinane. I. I-Huzza Porpoise. II. I-Algerine Porpoise. III. I-Porpoise ene-Mealy-mouthed.

Kwabo bangakhange bafumane ithuba lokufunda esi sifundo, kusenokubonakala kungaqhelekanga, ukuba iintlanzi ezingagqithiyo kwienyawo ezine okanye ezintlanu zifanele zidityaniswe phakathi kweWHALES—igama elithi, ngendlela eqhelekileyo, elisoloko lidlulisela ingcamango yobukhulu. Kodwa izidalwa ezibekwe apha ngasentla njenge-Duodecimoes ziyiminenga engenampazamo, ngokwemigaqo yengcaciso yam yokuba yintoni umnenga— okt intlanzi ephumayo, enomsila othe tye.

INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ), ISAHLUKO 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).— Le yiporpoise eqhelekileyo efumaneka phantse kuwo wonke umhlaba. Igama endilinikwe mna; kuba kukho ngaphezu kohlobo olunye lwamarhamncwa, yaye kufuneka kwenziwe okuthile ukuzehlula. Ndimbiza ngolo hlobo, kuba uhlala equbha kumanzi ashushu, athi phezu kolwandle olubanzi aqhubeke eziphosela ezulwini njengeentloko kwisihlwele se-4 kaJulayi. Ngokuqhelekileyo inkangeleko yazo inconywa ngumatiloshe. Egcwele imimoya emihle, ahlala ephuma kwimpepho ebhudlayo ukuya ngasemoyeni. Ngamakhwenkwe ahlala ehleli phambi komoya. Babalwa njengethamsanqa. Ukuba uthe waba nako ukumelana nemincili emithathu, wondela ezi ntlanzi zimbi, wokunceda izulu; umoya wobumbeko akakho kuni. I-Huzza Porpoise eyondliwe kakuhle, etyebileyo iya kukunika igaloni enye yeoli elungileyo. Kodwa ulwelo olucolekileyo noluethe-ethe olukhutshwa emihlathini yakhe luxabiseke kakhulu. Kucelwa phakathi kwabenzi bezacholo kunye nabenzi beewotshi. Oomatiloshe bawubeka elucikweni lwabo. Inyama yehagu ityiwa kakuhle, uyazi. Isenokuba ayizange ithi qatha kuwe into yokuba i-porpoise spouts. Enyanisweni, i-spout yakhe incinci kangangokuba ayibonakali ngokukhawuleza. Kodwa kwixesha elizayo xa ufumana ithuba, mjonge; kwaye emva koko uya kubona i-Sperm whale enkulu ngokwayo incinci.

INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ), ISAHLUKO II. ( Algerine Porpoise ).— Umphangi. Ukhohlakele kakhulu. Ufumaneka kuphela, ndicinga ukuba, kwiPasifiki. Mkhulu ngandlel' ithile kune-Huzza Porpoise, kodwa uninzi lwenziwe ngokubanzi. Mqumbise, kwaye uya kubambelela kukrebe. Ndiye ndamthoba izihlandlo ezininzi, kodwa andizange ndimbone ebanjwa.

INCWADI III. ( Duodecimo ), ISAHLUKO III. ( I-Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).— Olona hlobo lukhulu lwePorpoise; kwaye ifumaneka kuphela kwiPasifiki, ukuza kuthi ga ngoku njengoko yaziwa. Ekuphela kwegama lesiNgesi, athe wabizwa ngalo ukuza kuthi ga ngoku, lelo labalobi—iRight-Whale Porpoise, ngokwemeko yokuba ufunyenwe ikakhulu kufutshane naloo Folio. Ngemilo, uyahluka kwiqondo elithile ukusuka kwi-Huzza Porpoise, ukuba yi-rotund encinci kunye ne-jolly girth; ngokwenene, ungumntu ococekileyo kwaye onjengendoda. Ayinamaphiko emqolo (uninzi lwezinye iiporpoise), inomsila othandekayo, kunye namehlo anobubele aseIndiya anjenge-hazel hue. Kodwa umlomo wakhe womgubo uyonakalisa yonke into. Nangona umqolo wakhe wonke ukuya kutsho emacaleni akhe ungowesable enzulu, ukanti umgca womda, ocacileyo njengophawu olukwisiqu senqanawa, olubizwa ngokuba “sisinqe esiqaqambileyo,” loo mgca uyamhluba ukusuka kwisiqu ukuya ngasemva, ngemibala emibini eyahlukeneyo, emnyama ngaphezulu namhlophe ngaphantsi. Umhlophe uquka inxalenye yentloko yakhe, kunye nomlomo wakhe wonke, obangela ukuba abukeke ngathi usandul 'ukusinda kutyelelo olubi ukuya kwingxowa yokutya. Eyona nto imbi kwaye inomgubo! Ioli yakhe ifana nqwa naleyo yentaka eqhelekileyo.

Ngaphaya kwe-DUODECIMO, le nkqubo ayiqhubeki, njengoko i-Porpoise ingowona mncinane kwiminenga. Ngaphezulu, unazo zonke iiLeviatans eziphawulekayo. Kodwa kukho i-rabble yeminenga engaqinisekanga, ebalekayo, enesiqingatha-emangalisayo, ethi, njenge-whaleman yaseMelika, ndiyazi ngegama, kodwa kungekhona ngokobuqu. Ndiya kubabala ngokweenqama zabo zamandulo; kuba ngokunokwenzeka uluhlu olunjalo lunokuba luncedo kubaphandi bexesha elizayo, abanokugqiba oku ndinako apha kodwa sele beqalile. Ukuba nawuphina kule minenga ilandelayo, iya kubanjwa kwaye imakishwe, ngoko unokufakwa lula kule Nkqubo, ngokobukhulu bakhe be-Folio, i-Octavo, okanye i-Duodecimo:—I-Bottle-Nose Whale; iJunk Whale; umnenga onentloko yePudding; umnenga waseKapa; uMnenga oKhokelayo; iCannon Whale; iScragg Whale; iCoppered Whale; uMnenga weendlovu; umnenga womkhenkce; iQuog Whale; iBlue Whale; njl. Kwigunya lesi-Icelandic, isiDatshi, nesiNgesi sakudala, kusenokucatshulwa olunye uludwe lweminenga engaqinisekanga, esikelelwe ngazo zonke iintlobo zamagama angenamandla. Ke mna ndizishiyile ezo, ngathi ziphelelwe; kwaye akanakunceda ngokukrokra kubo ngenxa yezandi nje, ezizele yiLeviyatanism, kodwa engabonisi nto.

Ekugqibeleni: Kwaxelwa kwasekuqaleni, ukuba le nkqubo yayingayi kubakho, kwaye ngoko nangoko, igqibelele. Aniboni ngokucacileyo, ukuba ndiligcinile ilizwi lam. Kodwa ngoku ndishiya iNkqubo yam ye-cetological imile ingagqitywanga, nanjengoko iCathedral enkulu yaseCologne yashiyeka, ikhreyini isamile phezu kwenqaba engagqitywanga. Kuba ii-rections ezincinci zinokugqitywa ngabakhi bazo bokuqala; abazukileyo, abanyanisekileyo, bahlala beyishiya i-copestone kwisizukulwana. UThixo makandigcine ndingagqibelisi nto. Le ncwadi iyonke yidrafti—hayi, kodwa yidrafti yoyilo. Owu, ixesha, amandla, imali kunye nomonde!
