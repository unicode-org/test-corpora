Naw di difrɛn kayn wɛl dɛn nid sɔm kayn pɔpul kɔmprɛhɛnsif klasification, if na wan izi autlayn wan fɔ di prɛsɛn, afta dis fɔ ful-ɔp ɔl in dipatmɛnt dɛn bay di lebra dɛn we go kam afta dat. As no beta man de go bifo fo tek dis mata fo han, a de giv mi own poor endeavors. A nɔ prɔmis ɛnitin we kɔmplit; bikɔs ɛni mɔtalman tin we dɛn se kɔmplit, fɔ da rizin de fɔ mek i nɔ gɛt mistek. A nɔ go mek lɛk se a de tɔk lɛk se a de tɔk bɔt di difrɛn difrɛn animal dɛn we gɛt smɔl smɔl bɔdi, ɔ—na dis ples at ɔl—bɔku pan ɛni we aw dɛn de tɔk bɔt am. Fɔs: Di nɔ shɔ, nɔ sɛtul kɔndishɔn fɔ dis sayɛns fɔ Sɛtɔlɔji de na di very vestibule we di tru tin dɔn sho se na sɔm kwata dɛn i stil de bi wan moot pɔynt if wɛl na fish. Insay in buk we nem System of Nature, AD 1776, Linnæus bin tɔk se, “A de separet di wɛl dɛn frɔm di fish dɛn.” Bɔt frɔm mi yon no, a no se te to di ia 1850, dɛn bin stil de si shak ɛn shad, alewif ɛn ɛring, we de agens di ɛkspɛs lɔ we Linɛɔs bin mek, we de sheb di prɔpati we di sem si dɛn gɛt wit di Livaytan.

Di grɔn we Linneus go dɔn fɔdɔm fɔ dɔn drɛb di wɛl dɛn na di wata, i tɔk dis: “Bikɔs ɔf dɛn wam baylokul at, dɛn lɔng dɛn, dɛn aylid dɛn we kin muv, dɛn ol yes, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem,” ɛn fɔ dɔn, “ex lege naturæ jure meritoque.” A bin sɛn ɔl dis to mi padi dɛn we nem Simiɔn Masi ɛn Chali Kɔfin, we kɔmɔt na Nantɔkɛt, we dɛn ɔl tu na bin mi mɛsmɛt pan wan patikyula voyage, ɛn dɛn bin jɔyn an fɔ tink se di rizin dɛn we dɛn bin tɔk bɔt nɔ bin du fɔ mi atɔl. Charley profanely hinted se dɛn bin humbug.

Yu fɔ no se, we a lɛf ɔl agyumɛnt, a tek di gud ol fashin grɔn se di wɛl na fish, ɛn kɔl oli Jona fɔ bak mi. Dis fondamental tin setul, di neks point na, in wich intanal rispek di wɛl difrɛn frɔm ɔda fish. Na di ɔp, Linnæus dɔn gi yu dɛn tin dɛn de. Bɔt fɔ tɔk smɔl, na dɛn wan ya: lɔng ɛn wam blɔd; we, ɔl di ɔda fish dɛn nɔ gɛt lɔng ɛn dɛn gɛt kol blɔd.

Neks: aw wi go difayn di wɛl, bay in klia ɛksternal tin dɛm, so dat wi go klia wan fɔ lɛbul am fɔ ɔl di tɛm we de kam? So fɔ tɔk shɔt, wɛl na fish we de swɛt wit in tel we de ɔrizɔntal . Na de yu gɛt am. No mata aw dɛn kɔntrakt am, da difinishɔn de na di rizɔlt fɔ mek dɛn tink gud wan bɔt am. Walrus kin spɔt lɛk wɛl, bɔt di walrus nɔto fish, bikɔs i kin waka na wata. Bɔt di las wɔd fɔ di difinishɔn stil mɔ kɔjent, as kɔpl wit di fɔs wan. Klosap ɛnibɔdi go dɔn notis se ɔl di fish dɛn we pipul dɛn we de wok na di land sabi nɔ gɛt flat, bɔt dɛn gɛt tel we tinap, ɔ we de ɔp ɛn dɔŋ. We, pan fish dɛn we de spɔt di tel, pan ɔl we i kin shep di sem, i nɔ kin chenj fɔ tek wan ɔrizɔntal pozishɔn.

Bay di difinishɔn we de ɔp fɔ wetin wɛl bi, a nɔ de kɔmɔt pan di leviathanic brɔdaship ɛni si krichɔ we di Nantucketers dɛn we sabi bɔt di bɛst tin fɔ no te naw wit di wɛl; ɛn, na di ɔda say, fɔ link wit am ɛni fish we dɛn bin dɔn tek te naw as strenja.* So, ɔl di smɔl smɔl fish dɛn we de spɔt, ɛn we gɛt ɔrizɔntal tel fɔ de insay dis grɔn plan we dɛn mek fɔ Sɛtɔlɔji. So naw, di big big divishɔn dɛn we di wan ol wɛl ɔs de kam.

BUK I. ( Folio ), CHAPTER I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Dis wɛl, bitwin di Inglish pipul dɛn trade we dɛn nɔ bin no klia wan as di Trumpa wɛl, ɛn di Physeter wɛl, ɛn di Anvil Headed whale, na di Cachalot we di Frɛnch pipul dɛn gɛt naw, ɛn di Pottsfich we di Jamani pipul dɛn gɛt, ɛn di Macrocephalus we de na di Lɔng Wɔd dɛn. Wi nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se na in na di big wan we de na di wɔl; di wan we kin mek pɔsin fred pas ɔl di wɛl dɛn we pɔsin kin mit; di wan we gɛt pawa pas ɔlman; ɛn las wan, na di wan we valyu pas ɔl na di biznɛs; na in na di wangren tin we dɛn mek fɔ gɛt da valyu tin de, we na spermaceti. Ɔl in spɛshal tin dɛn go, na bɔku ɔda ples dɛn, big pan. Na wit in nem mɔ na in a gɛt fɔ du naw. If yu tink bɔt filɔjik, i nɔ mek sɛns. Sɔm ɔndrɛd ia bifo dis tɛm, we dɛn nɔ bin no di Spɛm wɛl ɔlmost in yon rayt wan, ɛn we dɛn bin jɔs aksidɛntli gɛt in ɔyl frɔm di fish we bin dɔn stɔp; insay dɛn tɛm dɛn de, i go tan lɛk se pipul dɛn bin de tink se spermaceti kɔmɔt frɔm wan tin we fiba di wan we dɛn bin de kɔl da tɛm de na Ingland as Grinland ɔ Rayt Wɛl. Na di aidia bak, se dis sem spermaceti na bin dat kwik-kwik kɔmɛnt fɔ di Grinland Wɛl we di fɔs silabɔd fɔ di wɔd rili ɛksprɛs. Insay dɛn tɛm dɛn de, bak, spermaceti bin rili shɔt, dɛn nɔ bin de yuz am fɔ layt, bɔt na fɔ ɔint ɛn mɛrɛsin nɔmɔ. I bin onli fo get from di druggists as yu tide bai wan ounce of rhubarb. We, as a tink, as tɛm de go, pipul dɛn kam fɔ no di tru we aw di spermaceti tan, di wan dɛn we de sɛl am bin stil gɛt in fɔs nem; i nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ mek i gɛt mɔ valyu bay wan noshɔn we so strenj we impɔtant fɔ se i nɔ bɔku. Ɛn so di nem go dɔn kam fɔ bi di wɛl we dɛn rili pul dis spermaceti frɔm.

BUK I. ( Folio ), CHAPTER II. ( Right Whale ).—Wan tin ya, dis na di wan we dɛn kin rɛspɛkt pas ɔl di ɔda wan dɛn we dɛn kɔl leviathan, bikɔs na in na di fɔs wan we mɔtalman kin fɛn ɔltɛm. I de gi di atikul we pipul dɛn kin kɔl wɛlbɔn ɔ balin; ɛn di ɔyl we dɛn spɛshal as “wɛl ɔyl,” we na wan tin we nɔ impɔtant pas ɔl na biznɛs. Pan di fishaman dɛn, dɛn nɔ de pik am wit ɔl dɛn taytul dɛn ya: Di Wɛl; di Grinland Wɛl; di Blak Wɛl; di Gret Wɛl; di Tru Wɛl; di Rayt Wɛl. Bɔku tin de we nɔ klia bɔt udat na di kayn animal we dɛn baptayz bɔku bɔku wan so. Wetin den na di wɛl, we a put insay di sɛkɔn spisis fɔ mi Folios? Na di Gret Mysticetus we di Inglish pipul dɛn we de stɔdi bɔt di tin dɛn we Gɔd mek bin rayt; di Grinland Wɛl we di Inglish wɛlman dɛn bin de mek; di Baleine Ordinaire we di Frɛnch wɛlman dɛn bin de mek; di Growlands Walfish we di pipul dɛn na Swidan bin de mek. Na di wɛl we fɔ pas tu ɔndrɛd ia we dɔn pas, di Dɔch ɛn Inglish pipul dɛn dɔn de fɛn am na di Aktik si dɛn; na di wɛl we di Amɛrikan fishaman dɛn dɔn de rɔnata fɔ lɔng tɛm na di Indian si, na di Brazil Banks, na di Nɔ’ Wɛst Kɔst, ɛn difrɛn ɔda pat dɛn na di wɔl, we dɛn dɔn pik fɔ bi Rayt Wɛl Kruiz Grɔund.

Sɔm dɛn kin mek lɛk se dɛn si difrɛns bitwin di Grinland wɛl we di Inglish pipul dɛn gɛt ɛn di rayt wɛl we di Amɛrika pipul dɛn gɛt. Bɔt dɛn gri klia wan pan ɔl dɛn big big tin dɛn; ɛn dɛn nɔ dɔn prɛzɛnt wan patikyula tin yet we dɛn go ebul fɔ mek wan rili difrɛns pan. Na bay sabdivishɔn dɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnd we de bays pan di difrɛns dɛn we nɔ gɛt wan kɔnklushɔn, na in sɔm dipatmɛnt dɛn na di natura istri kin bi so repellingly intricate. Dɛn go trit di rayt wɛl ɔdasay fɔ sɔm lɔng tɛm, wit rɛfrɛns fɔ mek di sperm wɛl klia.

BUK I. ( Folio ), CHAPTER III. ( Fin-Back ).—Ɔnda dis ed a de kɔl wan monsta we, wit di difrɛn nem dɛn we na Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, ɛn Long-John, dɛn dɔn si am klos to ɔl di si ɛn na di wɛl we pasenja dɛn we de krɔs di Atlantik, na di Nyu Yɔk paket-trak dɛn kin tɔk bɔt in jet we de fa. Insay di lɔng we i kin rich, ɛn insay in baleen, di Fin-bak tan lɛk di rayt wɛl, bɔt i nɔ gɛt bɛtɛ pɔt, ɛn i gɛt layt kɔlɔ, i kin kam nia ɔliv. In big big lip dɛn de sho wan aspek we tan lɛk kebul, we dɛn mek bay di fold dɛn we de twis ɛn slant we gɛt big big wrinkles. Bɔku tɛm, di big tin we de mek i difrɛn, we na di fin, we i gɛt in nem frɔm, na sɔntin we pɔsin kin si. Dis fin kin lɔng lɛk tri ɔ 4 fit, i kin gro vertikal frɔm di bak pat pan di bak, i gɛt wan shep we tan lɛk angul, ɛn i kin gɛt wan ɛnd we rili shap we gɛt pɔynt. Ivin if nɔto di smɔl ɔda pat pan di tin we dɛn mek, yu go si dis fin we de fa frɔm di wɔl, sɔntɛnde, i de kɔmɔt klia wan. We di si kol smɔl, ɛn dɛn mak am smɔl wit sfɛri ripɛl dɛn, ɛn dis fin we tan lɛk gnomon tinap ɛn put shado pan di say we rɔtin, dɛn kin tink gud wan se di wata sɛklɔ we de rawnd am tan lɛk dayal smɔl, wit in stayl ɛn wev awa-layn dɛn we dɛn grev pan am. Na da Eaz-dial de, bɔku tɛm di shado kin go bak. Di Fin-Back nɔto gregarious. I tan lɛk se i et wɛl, jɔs lɛk aw sɔm man dɛn et man. I kin shem bad bad wan; ɔltɛm dɛn kin go in wan; we dɛn nɔ bin de ɛkspɛkt fɔ go ɔp to di wata na di wata dɛn we de fa pas ɔl ɛn we gɛt kol at; in stret ɛn wan ay ay jet we de rayz lɛk wan lɔng misanthropik spia pan wan ples we nɔ gɛt natin; gifted wit so wɔndaful pawa ɛn velositi fɔ swim, dat fɔ defy ɔl di prɛsɛn purchɔ frɔm mɔtalman; dis liviathan tan lɛk Ken we dɛn dɔn drɛb ɛn we dɛn nɔ ebul fɔ win, we gɛt da stayl de na in bak. Frɔm we i gɛt di baleen na in mɔt, sɔntɛnde dɛn kin put di Fin-Back wit di rayt wɛl, bitwin wan tiori spɛshal we dɛn kɔl Whalebone whales , dat na wɛl wit baleen. Fɔ dɛn wan ya we dɛn kɔl Whalebone whales, i go tan lɛk se bɔku difrɛn kayn dɛn de, bɔt bɔku pan dɛn nɔ bɔku pipul dɛn no bɔt. Wɛl dɛn we gɛt brayt nos ɛn wɛl we gɛt bich; wɛl dɛn we gɛt paik ed; wɛl dɛn we gɛt bɔnch; ɔnda-jawed wɛl ɛn rostrated wɛl, na di fishaman dɛn nem fɔ sɔm kayn.

Insay kɔnekshɔn wit dis apelativ fɔ “Wɛlbɔn wɛl,” i rili impɔtant fɔ tɔk, se pan ɔl we dis kayn nem kin izi fɔ mek i izi fɔ tɔk bɔt sɔm kayn wɛl dɛn, bɔt stil i na fɔ natin fɔ tray fɔ mek di Livaytan klas klia wan, we dɛn bil pan ɛni wan pan in baleen, ɔ hump, ɔ fin, ɔ tit dɛn; pan ɔl we dɛn mak pat ɔ ficha dɛn de rili klia se i tan lɛk se dɛn bɛtɛ adap fɔ afɔd di bies fɔ wan rɛgyula sistɛm fɔ Sɛtɔlɔji pas ɛni ɔda detached bɔdi difrɛns, we di wɛl, insay in kayn dɛn, de prɛzɛnt. So aw yu go du am? Di baleen, hump, bak-fin, ɛn di tit dɛn; dis na tin dεm we dεn pekulεri dεm de skata bitwin כl kayn wεl dεm we dεn nכ de skata, witout eni rispek to wetin kin bi di nεchכr fכ dεn strכkchכ in כda εn mכr implεnt patikyula dεm. So, di sperm whale ɛn di humpbacked whale, ɛni wan pan dɛn gɛt hump; bɔt na de di sem tin nɔ de igen. Dɔn, dis sem humpbacked wɛl ɛn di Grinland wɛl, ɛni wan pan dɛn wan ya gɛt baleen; bɔt na de bak di sem tin nɔ de igen. Ɛn na di sem tin wit di ɔda pat dɛn we wi dɔn tɔk bɔt. Insay difrɛn kayn wɛl dɛn, dɛn kin mek dɛn kayn kɔmbayn dɛn de we nɔ kin apin ɔltɛm; ɔ, if ɛni wan pan dɛn detach, da kayn isolation de we nɔ de ɔltɛm; as utterly fɔ defy ɔl di jenɛral mɛtodayzeshɔn we dɛn fɔm pan dis kayn bies. Na dis rɔk ɔl wan pan di wɛl-naturalist dɛn dɔn split.

Bɔt dɛn kin tink se, insay di insay pat dɛn na di wɛl, insay in bɔdi—na de, at ɔl, wi go ebul fɔ hit di rayt klasification. Nɔ, nɔto so; wetin tin, fo egzampl, de na di Grinland wel in anatomi we strikt pas in baleen? Bɔt stil wi dɔn si se bay in baleen i nɔ pɔsibul fɔ klas di Grinland wɛl kɔrɛkt wan. Ɛn if yu go dɔŋ insay di bɔdi fɔ di difrɛn lɛviathan dɛn, wetin mek de yu nɔ go fɛn difrɛns dɛn we na fifti pat we de fɔ di sistɛm we de mek di sistɛm lɛk dɛn wan dɛn we de na do we dɛn dɔn ɔlrɛdi kɔnt. Dɔn wetin lɛf? natin pas fɔ ol di wɛl dɛn bɔdi, insay dɛn ɔl fri volyum, ɛn wit maynd fɔ sɔt dɛn da we de. Ɛn dis na di Bibliographical system we dɛn adopt ya; ɛn na in nɔmɔ go ebul fɔ win, bikɔs na in nɔmɔ pɔsin kin ebul fɔ du. Fɔ go bifo.

( Sulphur Bottom ).—Wan ɔda jentlman we bin de ritaia, we gɛt brimston bɛlɛ, nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se i bin gɛt bay we i bin de skrap along di Tartarian tayl dɛn insay sɔm pan in dip dip daiv dɛn. Dɛn nɔ kin si am bɛtɛ; at ɔl a nɔ ɛva si am pas na di rimot sawt si dɛm, ɛn afta dat ɔltɛm a de tu big distans fɔ stɔdi in fes. Dɛn nɔ de ɛva rɔnata am; i go rɔnawe wit rop-wɔk dɛn we gɛt layn. Dɛn kin tɛl prodigies bɔt am. Adieu, Sɔlfɔ Bɔt! A nɔ go ebul fɔ tɔk natin mɔ we na tru bɔt una, ɛn di oldest Nantucketer nɔ go ebul fɔ tɔk.

Na so BUK I. ( Folio ) dɔn, ɛn naw BUK II bigin. ( Ɔktavo ).

OCTAVOES.*—Dɛn wan ya embras di wɛl dɛn we gɛt middling magnitude, we dɛn kin nɔmba dɛn we de de:—I., di Grampus ; II., di Blak Fish dɛn ; III., di Narwhale ; IV., di Thrasher ; V., di pɔsin we kil .

*Wetin mek dɛn nɔ kɔl dis buk bɔt wɛl di Kwato, na sɔntin we rili klia. Bikɔs, pan ɔl we di wɛl dɛn na dis ɔda, pan ɔl we dɛn smɔl pas di wan dɛn we bin de na di fɔs ɔda, pan ɔl dat, dɛn stil gɛt wan prɔpɔshɔn we tan lɛk dɛn insay figa, yet di bukbaynda in Quarto volyum insay in dimɛnshɔn fɔm nɔ de kip di shep fɔ di Folio volyum, bɔt di Ɔktavo volyum de kip am.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER I. ( Grampus ).—Pan ɔl we dis fish we de blo lawd sawnd, ɔ we i de blo, dɔn mek pipul dɛn we de wok na di land gɛt prɔvab, pipul dɛn sabi am so as pɔsin we de na di dip dip, bɔt stil, pipul dɛn nɔ kin kɔl am pan wɛl. Bɔt bikɔs i gɛt ɔl di big big tin dɛn we di lɛviatan gɛt, bɔku pan di wan dɛn we de stɔdi bɔt di wɔl dɔn no am fɔ wan. I gɛt mɔdaret ɔktavo saiz, i de difrɛn frɔm fayvtin to twɛnti fayv fit in lɔng, ɛn i gɛt kɔrɛspɔndɛns dimɛnshɔn rawnd di wɛst. I de swim insay bɔku bɔku grup dɛn; dɛn nɔ kin ɛva fɛn am ɔltɛm, pan ɔl we in ɔyl bɔku, ɛn i rili fayn fɔ layt. Sɔm fishaman dɛn kin tek di we aw i de du tin as sɔntin we de sho se di big big sperm wɛl dɔn go bifo.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER II. ( Blak Fish ).—A gi di pɔpul fishaman dɛn nem fɔ ɔl dɛn fish ya, bikɔs jɔs lɛk ɔl ɔda fish dɛn na dɛn na di bɛst. Usay ɛni nem apin fɔ bi vague ɔ inexpressive, a go se so, ɛn gi advays fɔ ɔda wan. A de du am naw, a de tɔch di Blak Fish, we dɛn kɔl, bikɔs blaknɛs na di rul bitwin ɔlmost ɔl wɛl. So, kɔl am di Hyena Whale, if yu want. Pipul dɛn no bɔt in voracity, ɛn frɔm di sikɔstɛms we di insay angul dɛn na in lip dɛn kɔba ɔp, i de kɛr wan Mɛfistofil grin we go de sote go na in fes. Dis wɛl kin lɔng lɛk siksti ɔ ettin fit so. Dɛn kin si am na lɛk ɔl di latitud dɛn. I gɛt wan spɛshal we fɔ sho in dɔsal huk fin we i de swim, we tan lɛk Roman nos. We dɛn nɔ de yuz dɛn mɔ prɔfit, di wan dɛn we de fɛn sperm wɛl kin kech di Hyena wɛl sɔntɛnde, fɔ mek dɛn kɔntinyu fɔ gɛt ɔyl we nɔ dia fɔ wok na dɛn os—as sɔm pipul dɛn we de kia fɔ di os we nɔ de tek bɔku mɔni, we dɛn nɔ gɛt kɔmpin, ɛn we dɛn kin jɔs bɔn dɛnsɛf, kin bɔn talɔ we nɔ fayn instead fɔ waks we kin smɛl. Pan ɔl we dɛn blɔk kin rili tan, sɔm pan dɛn wɛl ya go gi yu ɔyl we pas tati galɔn.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER III. ( Narwhale ), dat na, Nostril whale .—Wan ɔda ɛgzampul bɔt wan wɛl we dɛn kɔl wɛl, we dɛn kɔl so, a tink se na frɔm in spɛshal ɔn we dɛn bin mistek fɔs fɔ se na in nos we gɛt pik. Di krichɔ lɔng lɛk siksti fit, ɛn in ɔn kin avrej fayv fit, pan ɔl we sɔm kin pas tɛn, ɛn i kin ivin rich fayvtin fit. Fɔ tɔk am strikt wan, dis ɔn na jɔs wan tusk we dɔn lɔng, we de gro kɔmɔt na di jaw insay wan layn we de dɔŋ smɔl frɔm di ɔrizɔntal. Bɔt na di bad bad sayd nɔmɔ dɛn kin si am, we gɛt bad bad tin fɔ du, we kin gi in ɔna sɔntin we fiba di aspek we pɔsin we nɔ gɛt natin fɔ du wit lɛft-an kin gɛt. Us patikyula rizin dis ayvri ɔn ɔ lans de ansa, i go at fɔ tɔk. I tan lɛk se dɛn nɔ de yuz am lɛk di bled fɔ di sɔd-fish ɛn di bil-fish; pan ɔl we sɔm pipul dɛn we de wok na di bot kin tɛl mi se di Narwhale kin yuz am fɔ rek fɔ tɔn oba di bottom na di si fɔ it. Charley Coffin bin se dɛn bin de yuz am fɔ mek ays-piercer; bikɔs di Narwhale, we de go ɔp na di say we di Polar Si de, ɛn si se i gɛt ays, i de push in ɔn ɔp, ɛn so i brok tru. Bɔt yu nɔ go ebul fɔ pruv se ɛni wan pan dɛn tin ya kɔrɛkt. Mi yon opinion na, se pan ɔl we dis wan-sayd ɔn kin rili yuz di Narwhale—ilɛksɛf dat kin bi—i go mɔs bi se i go rili izi fɔ am fɔ wan fɔlda we i de rid pamphlet dɛn. Di Narwhale we a dɔn yɛri dɛn kɔl am Tusked whale, di Horned whale, ɛn di Unicorn whale. I rili na wan ɛgzampul we pɔsin kin want fɔ no mɔ bɔt di Yunikɔnizm we dɛn kin si na ɔlmost ɔl di kiŋdɔm dɛn we gɛt layf. Frɔm sɔm ol ɔda pipul dɛn we de rayt buk we dɛn dɔn klos, a dɔn gɛda se dis sem si-yunikɔn in ɔn bin de trade trade, dɛn bin de tek am as di big mɛrɛsin fɔ fɛt pɔyzin, ɛn as a so, di pripiamɛnt dɛn we dɛn bin de pripia am bin de briŋ bɔku bɔku prayz dɛn. Dɛn bin de distil am bak to wan volatile salts fɔ faint ladies, di sem we aw dɛn kin mek di man dia dɛn ɔn fɔ mek hartshorn. Fɔs, dɛn bin de kɔl am insɛf sɔntin we pipul dɛn kin rili want fɔ no mɔ. Blak Lɛta tɛl mi se Shɛf Matin Frɔbisha we i bin de kam bak frɔm da voyage de, we Kwin Bɛs bin rili gɛt maynd fɔ wev in an we gɛt jɔlɔs to am frɔm wan winda na Grinwich Palɛs, as in bold ship bin de go dɔŋ di Temz; Blak Lɛta bin tɔk se: “we Shɛf Matin bin kam bak frɔm da travul de, i bin bɛn in ni i bin gi am wan wɔndaful lɔng ɔn we dɛn kɔl Narwhale, we fɔ lɔng tɛm afta i bin dɔn hang na di kast na Winsɔ.” Wan Ayrish rayta avers se di Earl of Leicester, we bin bɛn in ni, bin du semweso prɛzɛnt to in aynɛs ɔda ɔn, we gɛt fɔ du wit wan land animal we tan lɛk yunikɔn.

Di Narwhale gɛt wan luk we rili fayn, we tan lɛk lɛpad, i gɛt milk-wayt grɔn kɔlɔ, i gɛt rawnd ɛn ɔblɔng spat dɛn we blak. In ɔyl rili bɛtɛ, i klia ɛn fayn; bɔt na smɔl tin nɔmɔ de, ɛn dɛn nɔ kin fɛn am ɔltɛm. Dɛn kin si am mɔ na di si dɛn we de rawnd di si.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER IV. ( Killer ).—Di Nantucketer nɔ no bɛtɛ bɔt dis wɛl, ɛn di pɔsin we se i sabi bɔt di tin dɛn we Gɔd mek nɔ no natin. Frɔm wetin a dɔn si bɔt am fa, a fɔ se i bin de bɔt di big big grampus. I rili savayj—na wan kayn Feegee fish. Sɔntɛnde i kin tek di big big Folio wɛl dɛn na in lip, ɛn hang de lɛk lich, te di pawaful brut wɔri te i day. Dɛn nɔ de ɛva ɔnt di Kila. A nɔ ɛva yɛri us kayn ɔyl i gɛt. Dɛn kin tek ɛksɛpshɔn to di nem we dɛn gi dis wɛl, bikɔs i nɔ klia. Bikɔs wi ɔl na pipul dɛn we de kil, na land ɛn na si; Bonapartes ɛn Sharks inklud.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER V. ( Thrasher ).—Dis jentlman gɛt nem fɔ in tel, we i de yuz fɔ ferule fɔ bit in ɛnimi dɛn. I de rayd di Folio wɛl in bak, ɛn as i de swim, i de wok in pasej bay we i de bit am; as sɔm skul masta dɛn kin gɛt wanwɔd na di wɔl bay di sem kayn we. Stil smɔl pipul dɛn no bɔt di Thrasher pas di Killer. Dɛn ɔl tu na pipul dɛn we nɔ de fala lɔ, ivin na di si we nɔ gɛt lɔ.

Na so BUK II dɔn. ( Octavo ), ɛn bigin BUK III. ( Duodecimo ) we dɛn kɔl Duodecimo.

DUODECIMOES.—Dɛn wan ya na di smɔl smɔl wɛl dɛn. I. Di Huzza Pɔpɔyz. II. Di Aljerin Pɔpɔyz. III. Di Mɛli-mɔt Pɔpɔyz.

Fɔ di wan dɛn we nɔ gɛt chans fɔ stɔdi di tɔpik spɛshal wan, i go tan lɛk se i strenj, se fish dɛn we nɔ kɔmɔn fɔ pas 4 ɔ fayv fit fɔ mared bitwin WƐL dɛn—na wɔd, we, insay di pɔpul sɛns, kin mek pɔsin tink ɔltɛm se dɛn big. Bɔt di krichɔ dɛm we dɛn sɛt dɔŋ ɔp as Duodecimoes na wɛl we nɔ de mek mistek, bay di wɔd dɛm we a de difinishɔn fɔ wetin wɛl bi— dat na fish we de spɔt, wit ɔrizɔntal tel.

BUK III. ( Duodecimo ), CHAPTER 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Dis na di kɔmɔn pɔp we dɛn kin si klos to ɔlsay na di wɔl. Di nem na mi yon we; bikɔs i pas wan kayn pɔp, ɛn dɛn fɔ du sɔntin fɔ mek dɛn difrɛn. A kɔl am so, bikɔs i kin swim ɔltɛm na hilarious shoals, we pan di brayt si kin kip de tos dɛnsɛf to ɛvin lɛk kap insay wan krawd we de na di Fɔs Julay. Bɔku tɛm, di pɔsin we de wok na di si kin gladi fɔ di we aw dɛn luk. Dɛn ful-ɔp wit fayn fayn spirit dɛn, ɛn ɔltɛm dɛn kin kɔmɔt na di briz we de blo ɛn go na di say we di briz de blo. Na di bɔbɔ dɛn we de liv ɔltɛm bifo di briz. Dɛn kin tek dɛn as laki laki. If yusɛf ebul fɔ bia wit tri gladi at we yu si dɛn fish dɛn ya we gɛt layf, dat min se ɛvin ɛp una; di spirit fɔ ple gem we lɛk Gɔd nɔ de insay una. Wan Huzza Porpoise we dɛn it fayn ɛn we gɛt bɔku bɔku it, go gi yu wan gud galɔn gud ɔyl. Bɔt di fayn ɛn dilik wata we dɛn kin pul na in jaw gɛt valyu pasmak. Na di wan dɛn we de aks fɔ am fɔ di wan dɛn we de mek jɔlɔs ɛn di wan dɛn we de mek wach. Di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot kin put am pan dɛn ɔnɛs. Porpoise mit na gud tin fɔ it, yu no. Sɔntɛm i nɔ go ɛva kam na yu maynd se pɔpɔyz de swɛt. Fɔ tru, in spɔt so smɔl dat i nɔ kin izi fɔ no am. Bɔt di nɛks tɛm we yu gɛt chans, wach am; ɛn yu go si di big Spɛm wɛl insɛf insay smɔl smɔl.

BUK III. ( Duodecimo ), CHAPTER II. ( Algerine Porpoise ).—Na pɔsin we de tif na di si. Rili savayj. Na di Pasifik nɔmɔ dɛn kin fɛn am, a tink se. I big smɔl pas di Huzza Porpoise, bɔt bɔku pan di sem jenɛral mek. Mek i vɛks, ɛn i go bɔkul to wan shak. A dɔn lɔs fɔ am bɔku tɛm, bɔt a nɔ ɛva si am yet we dɛn kech am.

BUK III. ( Duodecimo ), CHAPTER III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Di kayn Pɔpɔyz we big pas ɔl; ɛn na di Pasifik nɔmɔ dɛn kin si am, so fa as dɛn no am. Di wangren Inglish nem, we dɛn dɔn kɔl am te naw, na di fishaman dɛn—Right-Whale Porpoise, frɔm di sikɔstɛms we dɛn kin si am mɔ na di say we de nia da Folio de. In shep, i difrɛn pan sɔm digri frɔm di Huzza Porpoise, bikɔs i nɔ gɛt bɛtɛ rotund ɛn jolly girth; fɔ tru, i rili fayn ɛn tan lɛk jentlman. I nɔ gɛt fin na in bak (mɔs ɔda pɔpɔyz dɛn gɛt), i gɛt fayn tel, ɛn i gɛt sɛntimɛnt Indian yay dɛn we gɛt hazel hue. Bɔt in mil-mɔt de pwɛl ɔltin. Pan ɔl we in wan ol bak dɔŋ to in sayd fins dɛn na dip sable, bɔt stil gɛt bɔda layn, we difrɛn lɛk di mak we de na ship in hul, we dɛn kɔl “brayt wes,” da layn de de strek am frɔm stem to stern, wit tu difrɛn kɔlɔ dɛn, blak ɔp ɛn wayt dɔŋ. Di wayt kɔmpɔz pat pan in ed, ɛn di wan ol mɔt, we de mek i luk lɛk se i jɔs rɔnawe frɔm wan bad bad visit to wan it-bag. Wan aspek we rili mean ɛn mealy! In ɔyl tan lɛk di wan we kɔmɔn pɔp.

Bifo di DUODECIMO, dis sistɛm nɔ de go bifo, bikɔs di Porpoise na di smɔl wan pan di wɛl dɛn. Na ɔp, yu gɛt ɔl di Livaytan dɛn we yu notis. Bɔt bɔku bɔku wɛl dɛn de we nɔ shɔ, we de rɔnawe, we de af-af fayn fayn wan, we, as Amɛrikin wɛlman, a no bay gud nem, bɔt nɔto mi yon. A go kɔnt dɛn bay dɛn fɔs-kast apileshɔn dɛn; bikɔs sɔntɛm dis kayn list kin valyu to di wan dɛn we go du investayshɔn tumara bambay, we go dɔn wetin a gɛt ya bɔt a dɔn bigin. If ɛni wan pan dɛn wɛl ya, dɛn go kech am ɛn mak am afta dis, den dɛn kin put am wantɛm wantɛm insay dis Sistɛm, akɔdin to in Folio, Octavo, ɔ Duodecimo magnitud:—Di Bɔtul-Nos Wɛl; di Junk Whale we dɛn kɔl Junk Whale; di Wɛl we gɛt Pudin-Hɛd; di Kep Wɛl; di Lidin Wɛl; di Kanɔn Wɛl; di Skrag Wɛl; di Kɔpa Wɛl; di Ɛlifant Wɛl; di Aysbɔg Wɛl; di Quog Whale we dɛn kɔl Quog Whale; di Blu Wɛl; etc. Frɔm di Ayland, Dɔch, ɛn ol Inglish ɔtoriti dɛn, dɛn kin kot ɔda list dɛn fɔ wɛl dɛn we nɔ shɔ, we dɛn blɛs wit ɔl kayn nem dɛn we nɔ fayn. Bɔt a de pul dɛn as tin dɛn we nɔ de igen; ɛn i nɔ go ebul fɔ ɛp fɔ sɔprayz dɛn fɔ jɔs sawnd dɛn, we ful-ɔp wit Livaytan rilijɔn, bɔt we nɔ min natin.

Fɔ dɔn: Dɛn bin tɔk na di biginin, se dis sistɛm nɔ go de ya, ɛn wan tɛm, i go pafɛkt. Yu nɔ go ebul fɔ si klia wan se a dɔn kip mi wɔd. Bɔt naw a de lɛf mi sɛtɔlɔjik Sistɛm tinap so we i nɔ dɔn, ivin as dɛn bin lɛf di big Katidral na Kɔlɔn, wit di kren we stil tinap ɔp di tawa we nɔ dɔn dɔn. Fɔ smɔl erekshɔn dɛn kin dɔn bay dɛn fɔs akitɔk dɛn; grand wan dɛn, tru wan dɛn, kin ɛva lɛf di kɔpston to di pikin dɛn we go kam afta dɛn. Gɔd de mek a nɔ ɛva kɔmplit ɛnitin. Dis wan ol buk na jɔs drɔt—nɔ, bɔt na drɔg. Oh, Taym, Strɔng, Kɛsh, ɛn Peshɛnt!
