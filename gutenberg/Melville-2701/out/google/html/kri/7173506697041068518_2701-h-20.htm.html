CHAPTER 101. Di Dikanta.

Ere di Inglish ship fade frɔm di yay, ilɛksɛf i sɛt am dɔŋ ya, se i kɔmɔt na Lɔndɔn, ɛn dɛn gi am in nem to di layt Samiɛl Ɛndabi, we na bin biznɛsman na da siti de, we na di ɔrijinal fɔ di fambul wɛl os we dɛn kɔl Ɛndabi ɛn in pikin dɛn; wan os we insay mi po wɛlman in opinion, kam nɔ fa biɛn di wanwɔd kiŋ os dɛm fɔ di Tudors ɛn Bourbons, in pɔynt fɔ rial istri intres. Aw lɔng, bifo wi Masta in ia 1775, dis big wɛl os bin de, mi bɔku bɔku fish-dɔkyumɛnt dɛn nɔ mek am klia; bɔt insay da ia de (1775) i bin fit di fɔs Inglish ship dɛn we bin ɛva de fɛn di Spɛm Wɛl ɔltɛm; pan ɔl we fɔ sɔm skɔ ia bifo dat (sins 1726) wi valiant Kɔfin ɛn Masi dɛn we kɔmɔt na Nantucket ɛn di Vayn Gad bin dɔn insay big big bot dɛn we bin de rɔnata da Livaytan de, bɔt na di Nɔt ɛn Sawt Atlantik nɔmɔ: nɔto ɔdasay. I bi se dɛn rayt am klia wan ya, se di Nantucketers na bin di fɔs wan pan mɔtalman fɔ harpoon wit sivilizayt stɛl di big Sperm Whale; ɛn fɔ af sɛnti ia, na dɛn wangren pipul dɛn na di wan ol wɔl we bin de harpoon am so.

Insay 1778, wan fayn ship we dɛn kɔl Amelia, bin fit fɔ di ɛkspɛs rizin, ɛn na di wan dɛn nɔmɔ we di Ɛndabi dɛn we bin gɛt trɛnk bin de chaj am, i bin gɛt maynd fɔ rawnd Kep Hɔn, ɛn na in na bin di fɔs wan pan di neshɔn dɛn fɔ put ɛni kayn wɛl-bɔt dɔŋ na di big Sawt Si. Di voyage na bin skilful ɛn laki wan; ɛn we i go bak na in ples wit in hold we ful-ɔp wit di valyu sperm, i nɔ tu te, ɔda ship dɛn, Inglish ɛn Amɛrikin, fala di Amelia in ɛgzampul, ɛn na so dɛn trowe di big big Sperm Whale grɔn na di Pasifik opin. Bɔt bikɔs i nɔ bin satisfay wit dis gud tin, di os we nɔ bin de taya bin mek i gladi bak: Samiɛl ɛn ɔl in Pikin dɛn—ɔmɔs, na dɛn mama nɔmɔ no—ɛn ɔnda dɛn wantɛm wantɛm, ɛn sɔm pat pan am, a tink se, pan dɛn spɛns, dɛn bin mek di British gɔvmɛnt sɛn di sloop-of-war Rattler fɔ wan wɛl we dɛn bin de travul fɔ fɛn tin dɛn na di Sawt Si. Na wan nevl Post-Captain bin de kɔmand di Rattler, i bin mek wan ratling voyage pan am, ɛn i bin du sɔm savis; aw bɔku nɔ de apia. Bɔt nɔto ɔl dis. Insay 1819, di sem os bin fit fɔ mek dɛn yon ship we dɛn kɔl diskvayri wɛl ship, fɔ go test kruiz na di rimot wata dɛn na Jepan. Da ship de—we dɛn kɔl gud gud wan “Syren”—bin mek wan fayn ɛkspirimɛnt kruz; ɛn na so di big Japan Whaling Ground fɔs pipul dɛn bin kam fɔ no. Di Sayrin we bin de insay dis fayn fayn trip, na wan Kpɛntin Kɔfin, we na bin Nantucketer, bin de kɔmand am.

Ɔl di ɔna to di Ɛndabi dɛm, so, we dɛn os, a tink se, de te tide; pan ɔl we i nɔ gɛt wan dawt se di fɔs Samiɛl go dɔn slip in kebul fɔ di big Sawt Si na di ɔda wɔl fɔ lɔng tɛm.

Di ship we dɛn gi in nem bin fit fɔ gɛt di ɔnɔ, bikɔs i bin de travul kwik kwik wan ɛn i bin de wok fayn ɔlsay. A bin bod am wan tɛm na midnayt sɔmsay nia di Patagonia kɔst, ɛn drink gud flip dɔŋ na di fɔkas. Na bin fayn gam we wi bin gɛt, ɛn dɛn ɔl na bin trɔmp—ɛvri sol we bin de insay di bot. Wan shɔt layf to dɛn, ɛn wan gladi gladi day. Ɛn da fayn fayn gam we a bin gɛt—lɔng, rili lɔng afta we ol Eab tɔch in plang dɛn wit in ayvri il—i de mɛmba mi bɔt di fayn fayn, sɔlid, Sakson ɔspitul we da ship de bin gɛt; ɛn mek mi pasɔn fɔgɛt mi, ɛn di dɛbul mɛmba mi, if a ɛva lɔs am. Tɔn oba? A bin se wi bin gɛt flip? Yɛs, ɛn wi bin de flip am wit di rit we na tɛn galɔn insay di awa; ɛn we di squall kam (bikɔs i squally off de bay Patagonia), ɛn ɔl di an dɛn—visita dɛn ɛn ɔlman—dɛn kɔl dɛn fɔ rif topsails, wi bin so top-hevi dat wi bin gɛt fɔ swing wisɛf ɔp insay bowlines; ɛn wi nɔ bin no natin, wi bin de ful-ɔp di skit dɛn na wi jakit dɛn insay di sel dɛn, so dat wi bin de hang de, rif fast insay di big big briz we bin de ala, we na wɔnin ɛgzampul fɔ ɔl di tar dɛn we dɔn drɔnk. Bɔt di mast dɛn nɔ bin go ova di wata; ɛn bay ɛn bay wi bin de skram dɔŋ, so soba, dat wi bin gɛt fɔ pas di flip bak, pan ɔl we di savayj sɔl sprɛy we bin de bɔs dɔŋ di fɔkastɔl skata, bin rili diluted am tumɔs ɛn pik am to mi teist.

Di bif bin fayn—i bin tranga, bɔt i bin gɛt bɔdi insay. Dɛn se na bul-bif; ɔda wan dɛn, se na dromedary bif; bɔt a nɔ no, fɔ tru, aw dat bin bi. Dɛn bin gɛt dɔmpling bak; sכm sכm, bכt sכbstanshכl, simetrikal globulכr, εn indestructible dumplings. A bin fansi se yu kin fil dɛn, ɛn rol dɛn rawnd insay yu afta dɛn dɔn swɛla dɛn. If yu butu tumɔs fɔ go bifo, yu kin risk fɔ mek dɛn pitch kɔmɔt na yu lɛk biliard-bɔl. Di bred—bɔt dat nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɛp; apat frɔm dat, i bin de agens skɔbutik; in shot, di bred bin gɛt di wangren fresh fare we dɛn bin gɛt. Bɔt di fɔkas nɔ bin rili layt, ɛn i bin rili izi fɔ step ova na dak kɔna we yu it am. Bɔt ɔl togɛda, i de kɛr am frɔm wan trak to di ɔda wan, we i tink bɔt di dimɛnshɔn dɛn fɔ di kuk in bɔyl dɛn, inklud in yon layf pachmɛnt bɔyl dɛn; bifo ɛn biɛn, a se, di Samiɛl Ɛndabi na bin wan ship we bin de gladi; fɔ gud fare ɛn bɔku tin; fayn flip ɛn strɔng; krak felo ɔl, ɛn kapital frɔm but il to hat-band.

Bɔt wetin mek, una tink, se di Samiɛl Ɛndabi, ɛn sɔm ɔda Inglish wɛl hala dɛn we a sabi—nɔto ɔl—na bin so fambul, ɔspitabl ship dɛn; we pas rawnd di bif, ɛn di bred, ɛn di tin, ɛn di jok; ɛn i nɔ tu te, dɛn nɔ bin taya fɔ it, drink, ɛn laf? A go tɛl yu. Di bɔku bɔku gud gladi at we dɛn Inglish wɛl pipul ya gɛt na impɔtant tin fɔ mek dɛn du risach bɔt istri. A nɔ bin dɔn lɛf fɔ du risach bak bɔt wɛl dɛn we dɛn bin dɔn rayt bɔt istri, we i bin tan lɛk se dɛn nid am.

Di pipul dɛn we kɔmɔt na Ɔland, di pipul dɛn na Ziland, ɛn di pipul dɛn na Denmak bin de bifo di Inglish dɛn we dɛn bin de kech wɛl; frɔm we dɛn pul bɔku wɔd dɛn we stil de na di fisha biznɛs; ɛn wetin pas dat, na dɛn fat ol fashɔn, we de tɔch bɔku tin fɔ it ɛn drink. Bikɔs, as jɔnaral tin, di Inglish biznɛs-ship de skrim in kru; bɔt nɔto so di Inglish wɛl man. So, insay di Inglish, dis tin fɔ wɛl-at gud gladi nɔto nɔmal ɛn natura, bɔt na insidɛntal ɛn patikyula; ɛn, bikɔs ɔf dat, i fɔ gɛt sɔm spɛshal ɔrijin, we dɛn dɔn pɔynt ya, ɛn dɛn go stil ɛksplen am mɔ.

We a bin de du risach bɔt di Livaytan istri dɛn, a bin stɔp pan wan ol Dutch volyum, we, bay di smel we i bin de smɛl fɔ kech wɛl, a bin no se i go mɔs bi bɔt pipul dɛn we de kech wɛl. Di taytul na, “Dan Coopman,” so a bin dɔn tɔk se dis fɔ bi di impɔtant mɛmori dɛn we sɔm Amstadam kɔpa dɛn we bin de na di fisha wok bin dɔn rayt, bikɔs ɛvri wɛl ship fɔ kɛr in kɔpa. A bin gɛt mɔ trɛnk fɔ dis opinion bay we a si se na di prodakshɔn fɔ wan “Fitz Swackhammer.” Bɔt mi padi Dɔktɔ Snɔd, we na man we rili lan buk, we na prɔfɛsɔ fɔ Lɔw Dɔch ɛn Ay Jaman na di kɔleji na Santa Klɔs ɛn Sɛnt Pɔt, we a gi am di wok fɔ translet, ɛn gi am wan bɔks we gɛt sperm kandul fɔ in trɔbul—dis sem Dɔktɔ Snɔd, jɔs afta i spay di buk, i mek a no se “Dan Kupman” nɔ min “Di Kupa,” bɔt “Di Biznɛsman." Fɔ tɔk smɔl, dis ol ɛn lan Lɔw Dɔch buk bin tɔk bɔt di biznɛs we dɛn bin de du na Ɔland; ɛn, pan ɔda tɔpik dɛn, i bin gɛt wan rili intrestin stori bɔt aw i bin de fishin wɛl. Ɛn insay dis chapta na, we gɛt di taytul, “Smeer,” ɔ “Fat,” na in a fɛn wan lɔng ditayli list fɔ di klos dɛn fɔ di lard ɛn sɛla fɔ 180 sel dɛn fɔ Dutch wɛlman dɛn; frɔm da list de, as Dɔktɔ Snodhead translet am, a transkrib dɛn tin ya:

400,000 lbs we yu de yuz. fɔ bif fɔ bif. 60,000 lbs we yu de yuz. Frisland bɔd fɔ it. 150,000 lbs we yu de yuz. di stok fish dɛn. 550,000 lbs we yu de yuz. na biskit. 72,000 lbs we yu de yuz. fɔ bred we saf saf. 2,800 firkins fɔ bɔta. 20,000 lbs we yu de yuz. Teksel & Leyden chiz. 144,000 lbs we yu de yuz. chiz (i go bi se na atikul we nɔ impɔtant). 550 ankers na Jɛnɛva. 10,800 barɛl bia.

Mɔs pan di statystik tebul dɛn kin dray we dɛn de rid; bɔt nɔto so insay di kes we de naw, usay di pɔsin we de rid kin ful-ɔp wit wan ol paip, barɛl, kwata, ɛn gil dɛn we gɛt gud gin ɛn gud gladi at.

Da tɛm de, a bin de spɛn tri dez fɔ stɔdi ɔl dis bia, bif, ɛn bred, ɛn insay da tɛm de dɛn bin de tink bɔt bɔku dip dip tin dɛn to mi, we go ebul fɔ yuz am we pas ɔl ɛn we Plato; ɛn, pan ɔl dat, a bin kɔmpilayt sɔpɔtmɛnt tebul dɛn fɔ mi yon, we bin tɔch di prɔbabl kwantiti fɔ stok-fish, ɛn ɔda tin dɛn, we ɛvri Lɔw Dɔch harpuoneer bin de it na da ol Grinland ɛn Spitzbergen wɛl fishery de. Fɔs, i tan lɛk se di bɔku bɔta, ɛn di Texel ɛn Leyden chiz we dɛn it, rili wɔndaful. Bɔt a impute am to dɛn naturally unctuous natures, we dɛn de rɛnd stil mɔ unctuous bay di nature of dɛn vocation, ɛn mɔ bay we dɛn de pursue dɛn gem na dɛn frigid Polar Seas, na di very coasts of dat Esquimaux kɔntri usay di convivial natives de prɔmis dɛnsɛf insay bampa dɛn we gɛt tren ɔyl.

Di kwantiti fɔ bia, sɛf, rili big, 10,800 barɛl. Naw, as dɛn bin jɔs ebul fɔ kɛr dɛn polar fisheries dɛn de go na kɔt insay di shɔt sɔmma we da klaymat de bin de, so dat di wan ol kruz we wan pan dɛn Dutch wɛlman ya bin de waka, inklud di shɔt trip fɔ go ɛn kam bak na di Spitzbergen si, nɔ bin pas tri mɔnt bɛtɛ, lɛ wi se, ɛn we wi kɔl 30 man dɛn to ɛni wan pan dɛn flet we gɛt 180 sel, wi gɛt 5,400 Low Dutch siman dɛn ɔl togɛda; so, a se, wi gɛt prɛsishɔn tu barɛl bia fɔ wan man, fɔ twɛlv wik alawɛns, we nɔ gɛt in fayn prɔpɔshɔn pan da 550 ankers gin de. Naw, ilɛksɛf dɛn gin ɛn bia harpuoneers ya, so fuddled as pɔsin kin tink se dɛn bin bi, na bin di rayt kayn man fɔ tinap na bot in ed, ɛn tek gud aim fɔ flay wɛl; dis go tan lɛk se i nɔ pɔsibul smɔl. Bɔt stil dɛn bin aim dɛn, ɛn nak dɛnsɛf. Bɔt dis bin rili fa Nɔt, mek dɛn mɛmba am, usay bia gri fayn wit di kɔnstityushɔn; na di Ikweta, na wi fisha biznɛs na di sawt, bia go fayn fɔ mek di harpuoneer slip na di mast-hɛd ɛn boozy insay in bot; ɛn bad bad lɔs kin apin to Nantucket ɛn Nyu Bɛdfɔd.

Bɔt nɔto so igen; dɛn dɔn tɔk inof fɔ sho se di ol Dutch wɛl dɛn we bin de tu ɔ tri ɔndrɛd ia bifo dis tɛm, na bin ay liva; ɛn se di Inglish pipul dɛn we de kech wɛl nɔ fɔgɛt bɔt wan ɛgzampul we rili fayn. Bikɔs, dɛn se, we yu de krɔs na ɛmti ship, if yu nɔ ebul fɔ gɛt natin bɛtɛ kɔmɔt na di wɔl, gɛt gud dina kɔmɔt pan am, at ɔl. Ɛn dis kin mek di dekanta ɛmti.





CHAPTER 102. Wan Bower in di Arsacides.

Te naw, we a de tɔk bɔt di Spɛm Wɛl, a dɔn tɔk mɔ bɔt di wɔndaful tin dɛn we i de du we de na do; ɔ separet ɛn ditayli pan sɔm smɔl intɛriɔ strɔkchɔral ficha dɛn. Bɔt fɔ wan big ɛn gud swip kɔmprɛhɛnshɔn bɔt am, i fayn fɔ mi naw fɔ opin in bɔtin stil mɔ, ɛn fɔ pul di pɔynt dɛn na in hos, fɔ pul in garta dɛn, ɛn fɔ trowe di huk dɛn ɛn di yay dɛn fɔ di jɔyn dɛn na in bon dɛn we de insay in bɔdi, fɔ put am bifo yu insay in ɔltimatum; dat min se, insay in skel we nɔ gɛt kɔndishɔn.

Bɔt aw naw, Ishmayl? Aw i bi, we yu, we na jɔs pɔsin we de rayd ɔs na di fisha wok, de mek lɛk se yu no natin bɔt di say dɛn we di wɛl de ɔnda grɔn? Did erudite Stubb, mounted pan yu capstan, bin gi lekchɔ bɔt di anatomi fɔ di Cetacea; ɛn bay ɛp fɔ di windlas, ol wan spɛshal rib fɔ ɛgzibit? Ɛksplen yusɛf, Ishmayl. Yu kin land wan wɛl we dɔn ful-ɔp na yu dɛk fɔ mek dɛn chɛk yu, lɛk aw pɔsin we de kuk de it rost-pig? Fɔ tru, nɔto so. Yu dɔn bi tru tru witnɛs te naw, Ishmayl; bɔt gɛt kia aw yu de tek di ɔnɔ we Jona nɔmɔ gɛt; di ɔnɔ fɔ tɔk bɔt di joist ɛn bim dɛn; di raf dɛn, di rij-pol, di slip dɛn, ɛn di ɔnda-pin dɛn, we mek di freym we dɛn mek wit lɛviathan; ɛn lɛk di talɔ-vat, dairy-rum, bɔta, ɛn chiz we de na in bɔdi.

A kɔnfɛs, se frɔm Jona, na smɔl wɛlman dɛn nɔmɔ dɔn go fa fawe ɔnda di big wɛl in skin; pan ɔl dat, dɛn dɔn blɛs mi fɔ gɛt chans fɔ kɔt am smɔl smɔl. Insay wan ship we a bin de, wan tɛm dɛn bin de es wan smɔl pikin we nem Spɛm Wɛl in bɔdi na di dɛk fɔ mek i pok ɔ bag, fɔ mek shɔp fɔ di barb dɛn na di harpun dɛn, ɛn fɔ di ed dɛn fɔ di lans dɛn. Tink yu a let dat chans go, witout yus mi bot-hatchet en jack-naif, en brok di sil en rid ol di tin dem fo dat yong pikin?

Ɛn as fɔ mi ɛksaktɔl no bɔt di bon dɛn na di leviathan insay dɛn big big, ful grow divɛlɔpmɛnt, fɔ da rare no de a gɛt dɛt to mi let kiŋ padi Tranquo, kiŋ na Tranque, wan pan di Arsacides. Fɔ we a bin de na Tranque, ia bifo, we a bin de atak di tredin-ship we nem Dey na Algiers, dɛn bin invayt mi fɔ spɛn pat pan di Arsacidean ɔlide dɛn wit di masta na Tranque, na in pam vilej we i dɔn ritaia na Pupella; wan glen we de nia di si we nɔ de fa frɔm di say we wi selɔ dɛn bin de kɔl Bambu-Taun, we na in kapital siti.

Pan bɔku ɔda fayn kwaliti dɛn, mi kiŋ padi Tranquo, we bin gɛt gift fɔ lɛk ɔl di tin dɛn we gɛt fɔ du wit barbaric vertu, bin dɔn briŋ togɛda na Pupella ɛnitin we nɔ bɔku we di pipul dɛn we gɛt mɔ sɛns kin mek; mɔ na wud dɛn we dɛn kɔt wit wɔndaful tin dɛn, shɔɛl dɛn we dɛn kɔt kɔt, spia dɛn we dɛn put insay, padul dɛn we dia, kenu dɛn we gɛt fayn fayn sɛnt; ɛn ɔl dɛn tin ya bin sheb bitwin ɛni wɔndaful tin dɛn we de na di wɔl, di wef dɛn we dɛn bin de kɛr go na in shore dɛn we dɛn bin de kɛr go na di wɔndaful tin dɛn we dɛn bin dɔn kɛr go.

Di men wan pan dɛn las wan ya na bin wan big Spɛm Wɛl, we, afta wan big big briz we bin de blo fɔ lɔng tɛm we nɔ bin kɔmɔn, dɛn bin dɔn fɛn am day ɛn stɔp, wit in ed agens wan kakao-nat tik, we in flawa dɛn we tan lɛk tufted droopings bin tan lɛk in verdant jet. We dɛn dɔn pul di big bɔdi las las fɔ pul in fatɔm-dip ɛnfold dɛn, ɛn di bon dɛn tɔn dɔst dray na di san, den dɛn tek tɛm kɛr di skel go ɔp di Pupella glen, usay wan big tɛmpul we gɛt lordly palm dɛn de shel am naw.

Dɛn bin de hang di rib dɛn wit trofi dɛn; dɛn bin kɔt di vertebræ dɛn wit Arsacidean annals, insay strenj hayroglifik dɛn; insay di skel, di prist dɛn bin de kip wan fayn fayn faya we nɔ bin dɔn ɔt, so dat di mistik ed bin sɛn in vaypa spɔt bak; we, we dɛn bin de hang am pan wan tif, di wɔndaful ɔda jaw we bin de dɔŋ bin de shek shek oba ɔl di wan dɛn we bin de wɔship Gɔd, lɛk di sɔd we dɛn hang pan ia we bin mek Damɔklis fred so.

Na bin wɔndaful tin fɔ si. Di wud bin grin lɛk mɔs na di Aysi Glɛn; di tik dɛn bin tinap ay ɛn prawd, dɛn bin de fil dɛn sap we gɛt layf; di grɔn we bin de wok tranga wan we bin de ɔnda bin tan lɛk mashin we pɔsin we de mek klos, wit wan fayn fayn kap pan am, we di grɔn-vayn tendril dɛn bin de mek di wɔp ɛn wuf, ɛn di flawa dɛn we gɛt layf bin de mek di figa dɛn. Ɔl di tik dɛn, wit ɔl dɛn branch dɛn we gɛt lod; ɔl di tik dɛn, ɛn di fɛn dɛn, ɛn di gras dɛn; di briz we de kɛr mɛsej; ɔl dɛn tin ya bin de wok ɔltɛm. Di slev dɛn we bin de nia de we nɔ biliv Gɔd ɛn we bin rili fred, bin tek am as wan pan di enjɛl dɛn we bin dɔn fɔdɔm in bon. Di Alabama dɔktɔ dɛn bin se na big big reptayl, ɛn dɛn gi am di nem Basilosaurus. Bɔt sɔm spɛshal bon dɛn we dɛn kɛr go krɔs di si to Owen, di Inglish Anatomist, i kam fɔ no se dis alɛgd reptayl na wɛl, pan ɔl we na wan spɛshal wan we dɔn kɔmɔt. We a tinap midul dɛn pawaful Livaytan skel, skel, tut, jaw, rib, ɛn vertebræ, ɔl dɛn tin ya gɛt sɔm pat dɛn we fiba di si-mɔnsta dɛn we bin dɔn de; bɔt di sem tɛm de bia na di ɔda say di sem kayn afiniti to di annihilated antichronical Leviathans, dɛn incalculable seniors; Mi na, bay wan flɔd, bɔn bak to da wɔndaful tɛm de, ere tɛm sɛf kin se i dɔn bigin; bikɔs tɛm bigin wit mɔtalman. Na ya Satɔn in grey chaos de rol oba mi, ɛn a kin gɛt dim, shuddering glimpses insay dɛn Polar eternities de; we ays we dɛn mek wit wed bin de prɛs tranga wan pan di say dɛn we dɛn kɔl naw di Tropik; ɛn insay ɔl di 25,000 mayl dɛn we dis wɔl bin de rawnd, dɛn nɔ bin si wan land we brayt lɛk an we pɔsin kin ebul fɔ liv. Dɔn di wan ol wɔl na bin di wɛl in yon; ɛn, we na kiŋ fɔ di tin dɛn we Gɔd mek, i lɛf in wek along di layn dɛn we de naw na di Andes ɛn di Himmalehs. Udat go sho wan pedigree lɛk Liviathan? Eab in harpun bin dɔn shed blɔd we dɔn ol pas Fɛro in yon. I tan lɛk se Mɛtusela na skul-bɔy. A de luk rawnd fɔ shek an wit Shɛm. A de fred bad bad wan pan dis antemosaic, unsourced existence of di unspeakable terrors of di whale, we, bikɔs i bin dɔn de bifo ɔl tɛm, i fɔ nid fɔ de afta ɔl di uman ej dɛn dɔn dɔn.

Bɔt nɔto in wan dis Liviathan dɔn lɛf in trays bifo Adamit insay di stereotype plet dɛn we di nature, ɛn insay laymston ɛn marl i lɛf in ol bust; bɔt pan Ijipshian tablɛt dɛn, we i tan lɛk se di tɛm we dɛn bin de trade trade de tɔk se dɛn gɛt sɔntin fɔ du wit tin dɛn we dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ dɛn mek am, wi si di print we nɔ gɛt wan mistek pan in fin. Insay wan apatmɛnt na di big tɛmpul na Denderah, lɛk fifti ia so bifo dis tɛm, dɛn bin fɛn wan planisfya we dɛn mek ɛn pent pan di granit siling, we gɛt bɔku bɔku sɛnta, grifin, ɛn dɔlfin, we fiba di grotesk figa dɛn we de na di sɛlestial glob fɔ di pipul dɛn we de naw. We ol Liviathan bin de glid midul dɛn, i bin de swim lɛk aw i bin de trade; bin de swim de na da planisfya de, bɔku bɔku ia bifo dɛn kray Sɔlɔmɔn.

Nɔto fɔ lɛf ɔda strenj atɛsteshɔn fɔ di ol we di wɛl bin de, insay in yon ɔs post-diluvian rialiti, as di venɛrabl Jɔn Lio, di ol Barbary travulman, bin put am dɔŋ.

Bɔt di tru tin bɔt di Mata na dat, na di tu say dɛn na di Tempul, Rɔk dɛn de we de shot tu Mayl insay di Si, ɛn wund di Wɛl dɛn we dɛn layt pan dɛn Dɛn kin kip wan Wɛl in Rib we lɔng pasmak fɔ wan Mirakul, we de ledɔm na di Grɔn wit in kɔnvɛks pat we de ɔp pas ɔl, de mek wan Ak, we dɛn se wan Man pan wan Kamɛl in Bak nɔ go ebul fɔ rich di ed wan ɔndrɛd Ia bifo a si am Dɛn Istriyan dɛn de afɛm, se wan Prɔfɛt we bin dɔn tɔk bɔt Mahomet, kɔmɔt na dis Tɛmpl, ɛn sɔm nɔ tinap fɔ asɛf, se na di Wɛl bin trowe di Prɔfɛt Jonas na di Bays na di Tɛmpl.”

CHAPTER 105. Yu tink se di Wɛl In Magnitude De Smɔl?—I Go Day?

So, as dis Liviathan de kam floundering down pan wi frɔm di ed-wata dɛm fɔ di Eternities, i kin fit fɔ aks, if, insay di lɔng kɔs fɔ in jɛnɛreshɔn dɛm, i nɔ dɔn degenerate frɔm di ɔrijinal bɔku pan in sires.

Bɔt we wi du invɛstigeshɔn wi si, se nɔto jɔs di wɛl dɛn we de naw, dɛn bɛtɛ pas di wan dɛn we dɛn fɔs fɔstɛm tin dɛn we dɛn dɔn fɛn na di Tɛshari sistɛm (we embras wan difrɛn jiolojikal tɛm bifo mɔtalman), bɔt pan di wɛl dɛn we dɛn fɛn na da Tɛshari sistɛm de, di wan dɛn we de pan in las fɔmɛshɔn dɛn pas di wan dɛn we bin de trade.

Pan ɔl di wɛl dɛn we dɛn bin dɔn dig yet bifo di adamit, di wan we big pas ɔl na di Alabama wan we wi bin tɔk bɔt na di las chapta, ɛn dat nɔ bin lɔng pas sɛvinti fit insay di skel. We, wi dɔn si, se di tep-mɛzhɔ de gi sɛvinti tu fit fɔ di skel fɔ wan big saiz mɔdan wɛl. Ɛn a dɔn yɛri, pan wɛlman dɛn ɔtoriti, se dɛn dɔn kech Spɛm Wɛl nia wan ɔndrɛd fit lɔng di tɛm we dɛn kech dɛn.

Bɔt lɛ i nɔ bi, dat pan ɔl we di wɛl dɛn we de naw na advans pan magnitude pan ɔl di wan dɛn we bin de insay di jiolojikal tɛm dɛn we bin dɔn de bifo; yu nɔ tink se frɔm Adam in tɛm, dɛn dɔn pwɛl?

Fɔ tru, wi fɔ dɔn so, if wi want fɔ gi kredit to di stori dɛn we man dɛn lɛk Plini, ɛn di wan dɛn we bin de stɔdi bɔt di tin dɛn we Gɔd mek trade trade bin rayt. Bikɔs Plini tɛl wi bɔt wɛl dɛn we bin gɛt bɔku bɔku ak dɛn we gɛt layf, ɛn Aldrovandus bɔt ɔda wan dɛn we lɔng et ɔndrɛd fit—Rope Walks ɛn Thames Tunnels of Whales! Ɛn ivin insay di tɛm we Banks ɛn Solander, we na Kuk in naturalist dɛn, wi si wan Danish mɛmba na di Akademi fɔ Sayns we de put sɔm Ayland Wɛl dɛn (reydan-siskur, ɔ Wrinkled Bellies) dɔŋ na wan ɔndrɛd ɛn twɛnti yad; dat na tri ɔndrɛd ɛn siksti fit. Ɛn Lacépède, di Frɛnch naturalist, insay in ɛlaborate istri bɔt wɛl, we i bigin in wok (pej 3), i put di Rayt Wɛl dɔŋ wan ɔndrɛd mita, tri ɔndrɛd ɛn twɛnti-ɛit fit. Ɛn dɛn bin pul dis wok so let as AD 1825.

Bɔt yu tink se ɛnibɔdi we de fɛt wɛl biliv dɛn stori ya? Nɔ, di wɛl we de tide big lɛk in gret gret granpa dɛn we bin de insay Plini in tɛm. Ɛn if ɛva a go usay Plini de, mi, we na wɛlman (mɔ pas aw i bin de), go mek bold tɛl am so. Bikɔs a nɔ ebul fɔ ɔndastand aw i bi, se pan ɔl we di Ijipshian mami dɛn we dɛn bin bɛr bɔku bɔku ia bifo ivin Plini bɔn, nɔ de mɛzhɔ bɔku tin na dɛn kɔfin lɛk wan mɔdan Kentuckian insay in sɔks; ɛn pan ɔl we di kaw ɛn ɔda animal dɛn we dɛn skapul pan di ol Ijipshian ɛn Ninivɛ tablɛt dɛn, bay di rilitiv prɔpɔshɔn we dɛn drɔ dɛn insay, jɔs lɛk aw dɛn pruv klia wan se di ay-brɛd, stɔl-fed, prayz kaw dɛn na Smitfild, nɔto jɔs ikwal, bɔt dɛn pas fa fawe pan di wan dɛn we fat pas ɔl di fat kaw dɛn we Fɛro bin gɛt; pan ɔl dis, a nɔ go gri se pan ɔl di animal dɛn, di wɛl nɔmɔ fɔ dɔn pwɛl.

Bɔt stil ɔda kwɛstyɔn stil de; wan we di Nantucketers we gɛt mɔ rɛkɔndit kin mek pipul dɛn vɛks bɔku tɛm. Ilɛksɛf na bikɔs ɔf di we aw pipul dɛn de luk ɔlmost ɔltin na di mast-hɛd dɛn na di wɛl-ship dɛn, we naw de go insay ivin tru di wata we de kɔmɔt na Behring, ɛn go insay di sikrit drɔwa ɛn lɔka dɛn we de fa pas ɔl na di wɔl; ɛn di tawzin hapun ɛn lans dɛn bin de rɔn rawnd ɔl di kɔntinɛntal kɔst dɛn; di tin we dɛn kin tɔk bɔt na, if Livaytan kin ebul fɔ bia fɔ lɔng tɛm we dɛn de rɔnata am so, ɛn we i nɔ fil bad bɔt am; if dɛn nɔ fɔ dɔnawe wit am las las na di wata, ɛn di las wɛl, lɛk di las man, smok in las paip, dɔn insɛf ivapɔret insay di las puf.

We yu kɔmpia di wɛl grup dɛn we gɛt hump wit di bɔfa dɛn we gɛt hump, we, nɔto fɔti ia bifo naw, bin skata bay tɛn tawzin pipul dɛn na di prɛri na Ilinois ɛn Misɔri, ɛn shek dɛn ayɛn manɛs ɛn skɔl wit dɛn brɔw we gɛt tɛnda pan di sayt dɛn we gɛt bɔku pipul dɛn na di riva-kapital dɛn, usay naw di pɔlitiks brɔka de sɛl yu land fɔ wan dɔla fɔ wan inch; insay dis kayn kɔmpiashɔn, i go tan lɛk se dɛn dɔn gi wan agyumɛnt we nɔ go ebul fɔ stɔp, fɔ sho se di wɛl we dɛn de fɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ rɔnawe naw fɔ dɔnawe wit am kwik kwik wan.

Bɔt yu fɔ luk dis tin wit ɔl yu at. Pan ɔl we shɔt tɛm dɔn pas—nɔto gud layf—di sɛns fɔ di bɔfa dɛn na Ilinois bin pas di sɛns we dɛn bin de kɔnt man dɛn naw na Lɔndɔn, ɛn pan ɔl we tide, wan ɔn ɔ fut nɔ stil de na ɔl da rijyɔn de; ɛn pan ɔl we di tin we mek dɛn kil dis wɔndaful tin na mɔtalman in spia; yet di difrɛn we aw dɛn bin de fɛn wɛl, nɔ gri fɔ mek di Livaytan dɔn so we nɔ gɛt glori. Fɔti man dɛn we de na wan ship we de fɛn di Spɛm Wɛl fɔ fɔti-ɛit mɔnt tink se dɛn dɔn du rili fayn, ɛn tɛl Gɔd tɛnki, if las las dɛn kɛr di ɔyl fɔ fɔti fish go os. We, insay di tɛm we di ol Kanada ɛn Indian ɔntin ɛn trap pipul dɛn na di Wɛst bin de, we di fa wɛst (we di san we de go dɔŋ di san stil de kɔmɔt) na bin wildanɛs ɛn vajin, di sem nɔmba fɔ man dɛn we gɛt mɔkasin, fɔ di sem nɔmba fɔ mɔnt, we bin de rayd ɔs instead fɔ go na ship, nɔ bin fɔ dɔn kil fɔti, bɔt fɔti tawzin ɛn mɔ bɔfa dɛn; wan tru tin we, if nid de, dɛn kin tɔk bɔt am wit sɔm statystik.

Nɔto, if dɛn tek am se na tru, i tan lɛk se ɛni argumɛnt de fɔ mek di Spɛm Wɛl dɔn smɔl smɔl, fɔ ɛgzampul, se insay di ia dɛn we bin de trade (di las pat pan di las sɛntiwɔd, lɛ wi se) dɛn bin de mit dɛn Livaytan ya, we bin de insay smɔl smɔl pɔd dɛn, bɔku tɛm pas aw dɛn de naw, ɛn, bikɔs ɔf dat, dɛn nɔ bin de travul fɔ lɔng tɛm so, ɛn dɛn bin de pe bɔku mɔni bak. Na bikɔs, lɛk aw dɛn dɔn notis ɔdasay dɛn, dɛn wɛl dɛn de, we sɔm we dɛn kin si fɔ sef, kin swim naw na di si insay big big karavan dɛn, so dat to big digri di solitary, yok, ɛn pɔd, ɛn skul dɛn we bin de ɔda tɛm we bin skata, dɔn gɛda naw to big big ami dɛn bɔt we nɔ gɛt wanwɔd ɔlsay, we nɔ kin de ɔltɛm. Na dat nɔmɔ. Ɛn ikwal fallacious seems di conceit, se bikɔs di wan dɛn we dɛn kɔl wɛl-bɔn wɛl nɔ de haunt bɔku grɔn igen insay di fɔs ia dɛn we bɔku wit dɛn, na dat mek da spisis de bak de go dɔŋ. Bikɔs dɛn jɔs de drɛb dɛn frɔm promontɔri to kap; ɛn if wan kɔst nɔ gɛt layf igen wit dɛn jet dɛn, den, mek shɔ se, sɔm ɔda ɛn rimot strɛch dɔn sɔprayz rili dis biɛn tɛm wit di spɛktakl we dɛn nɔ sabi.

Dɔn bak: bɔt dɛn las Livaytan dɛn ya we wi bin tɔk bɔt, dɛn gɛt tu strɔng fɔt dɛn, we, pan ɔl we mɔtalman go ebul fɔ win, dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ win dɛn sote go. Ɛn jɔs lɛk aw dɛn atak dɛn vali dɛn, di Swizaland dɛn we gɛt frɔst dɔn tɔn bak na dɛn mawnten dɛn; so, we dɛn de fɛn dɛn frɔm di savana ɛn glad dɛn na di midul si, di wɛl-bɔn wɛl dɛn kin las las fɔ go na dɛn Polar citadel dɛn, ɛn we dɛn de dayv ɔnda di ɔltimat glas barɛri ɛn wɔl dɛn de, kam ɔp bitwin ays fil ɛn flo dɛn; ɛn insay wan charmed sɛklɔ fɔ Disɛmba we go de sote go, bid defiance to all pursuit from man.

Bɔt as sɔntɛm fifti pan dɛn wɛl-bɔn wɛl ya na harpoon fɔ wan cachalot, sɔm filɔsofa dɛn na di fɔkas dɔn kam fɔ no se dis fayn bad bad tin we dɔn apin dɔn ɔlrɛdi rili siriɔs fɔ mek dɛn batalyɔn dɛn nɔ bɔku. Bɔt pan ɔl we fɔ sɔm tɛm pas, bɔku pan dɛn wɛl ya, we nɔ pas 13,000, na di Amɛrika pipul dɛn nɔmɔ dɔn kil dɛn ɛvri ia na di nɔt’ wɛst kɔst; yet tin dɛn de we dɛn kin tink bɔt we kin mek ivin dis sikɔstɛms nɔ gɛt bɛtɛ ɔ nɔ gɛt ɛnitin fɔ du wit am as argumɛnt we de agens dis.

Natural as it is to be somewhat incredulous concerning the populousness of the more enormous creatures of the globe, yet wetin wi go se to Harto, di istri man fɔ Goa, we i tɛl wi se wan tɛm we dɛn bin de ɔntin di Kiŋ na Siam, i tek 4,000 ɛlifant dɛn; dat na dɛn rijyɔn dɛn de, ɛlifant dɛn bɔku lɛk bɔku bɔku kaw dɛn we de na di say dɛn we kol. Ɛn i tan lɛk se no rizin nɔ de fɔ dawt se if dɛn ɛlifant dɛn ya, we Semiramis, Porus, Anibal, ɛn ɔl di kiŋ dɛn we bin kam afta dɛnsɛf na di Is dɔn de fɛn dɛn fɔ lɔng tɛm naw—if dɛn stil de liv de bɔku bɔku wan, i go mɔs bi se di big wɛl go lɔng pas ɔl di wan dɛn we dɛn de ɔntin, bikɔs i gɛt ples fɔ it paste fɔ go de, we rili tu tɛm pas ɔl Eshia, ɔl tu na Amɛrika, Yurop ɛn Afrika, Nyu Ɔland, ɛn ɔl di Ayland dɛn na di si we dɛn jɔyn.

Pantap dat: wi fɔ tink bɔt, se frɔm di presumed big lɔng layf we wɛl, dɛn probably attaining di ej fɔ wan sɛnti ia ɛn mɔ, so pan ɛni wan tɛm, sɔm difrɛn big jɛnɛreshɔn dɛn fɔ bi kɔntempɔral. Ɛn wetin dat na, i nɔ go te igen wi go gɛt sɔm aidia bɔt, bay we wi imajin ɔl di grev-yad, simɛstri, ɛn famili vault dɛn we Gɔd mek de gi ɔl di man, uman, ɛn pikin dɛn we bin de alayv sɛvinti fayv ia bifo; ɛn ad dis bɔku bɔku ɔs to di mɔtalman we de naw na di wɔl.

So, fɔ ɔl dɛn tin ya, wi kin tek di wɛl se i nɔ de day pan in spɛshal, ilɛksɛf i kin pwɛl pan in wan wan. I bin de swim di si bifo di kɔntinɛnt dɛn brok wata; wan tɛm i bin swim oba di say we di Tuileries, ɛn Windsor Castle, ɛn di Kremlin bin de. We Noa bin rɔf, i nɔ bin lɛk Noa in Ak; ɛn if ɛva wata go kam bak na di wɔl, lɛk di Netherlands, fɔ kil in rat dɛn, dat min se di wɛl we go de sote go stil de, ɛn we i de mɛn pan di ɔp pat pan di kres we di wata we de rɔn na di ekwetorial, i go tɔk in frothed defiance to di skay.





CHAPTER 106. Eab in Leg.

Di we aw Kpɛntin Eab bin kɔmɔt na di Samiɛl Ɛndabi we kɔmɔt na Lɔndɔn kwik kwik wan, nɔ bin dɔn gɛt sɔm smɔl smɔl fɛt-fɛt to in yon pɔsin. I bin dɔn layt wit so ɛnaji pan wan thwart na in bot dat in ayvri leg bin gɛt wan shok we af-af. Ɛn we afta i dɔn gɛt in yon dɛk, ɛn in yon pivot-hol de, i so vehemently wil rawnd wit wan urgent kɔmand to di stiarman (i bin, as ɛvride, sɔntin bɔt in nɔ stiarin inflexibly inof); dɔn, di ayvri we bin dɔn shek bin gɛt wan ɔda twist ɛn rench, dat pan ɔl we i bin stil kɔntinyu fɔ de ɔlsay, ɛn to ɔl di we aw i tan lɛk se i want fɔ du sɔntin, Eab nɔ bin si am se i fayn fɔ abop pan am atɔl.

Ɛn fɔ tru, i bin tan lɛk se na smɔl tin fɔ wɔnda, se pan ɔl di we aw Eab bin de tek tɛm du tin, sɔntɛnde i bin tek tɛm pe atɛnshɔn to di we aw da day bon we i bin tinap sɔm pat pan, bin de. Bikɔs i nɔ bin tu te bifo di Pequod kɔmɔt na Nantucket, dɛn bin fɛn am wan nɛt de ledɔm na grɔn, ɛn i nɔ bin gɛt sɛns; bay sɔm pɔsin we dɛn nɔ no, ɛn we i tan lɛk se i nɔ ebul fɔ ɛksplen, we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ imajin, in ayvri limb bin so vaylɛntli displace, dat i bin dɔn slap am-ways, ɛn ɔltin bin jɔs chuk in groin; ɛn i nɔ bin rili at fɔ mek di wund we bin de mek i fil pen, wɛl ɔl.

Nɔto, da tɛm de, i nɔ bin dɔn ebul fɔ go insay in maynd we bin de mek i gɛt wan prɔblɛm, se ɔl di pen we da sɔfa de bin de sɔfa da tɛm de, na bin jɔs di dairekt kwɛstyɔn bɔt wan prɔblɛm we bin de trade; ɛn i bin tan lɛk se insɛf si klia wan, se as di reptayl we gɛt pɔyzin pas ɔl na di mawnten de mek in kayn kɔntinyu fɔ de sote go lɛk di siŋman we swit pas ɔl na di grɔn; so, ikwal wit ɛvri felicity, ɔl miserable events do naturally beget dɛn lɛk. Yɛs, pas di sem tin, Eab bin tink; bikɔs ɔl tu di gret gret granpa ɛn di pikin dɛn we de kam afta Gladi de go fa pas di gret gret granpa ɛn di pikin dɛn we de kam afta Gladi. Fɔ, nɔto fɔ hint dis: se na infɔmeshɔn frɔm sɔm kanonic tichin dɛm, se pan ɔl we sɔm natura ɛnjɔymɛnt dɛm ya nɔ go gɛt pikin we dɛn bɔn fɔ di ɔda wɔl, bɔt, difrɛn frɔm dat, dɛn go fala di gladi-pikin we ɔl ɛl in pwɛl at; we, sɔm gilti mɔtalman misɛf dɛn go stil bɔn pikin dɛn we go de go bifo sote go we gɛt sɔri-at we pas di grev; nɔto at ɔl fɔ hint dis, i stil tan lɛk se i nɔ ikwal na di dip analisis fɔ di tin. Bikɔs, Eab bin tink, pan ɔl we ivin di ay ay gladi gladi we dɛn kin gɛt na dis wɔl kin ɛva gɛt wan patikyula smɔl smɔl tin we nɔ gɛt wan minin we de ayd insay dɛn, bɔt, na di bɔt, ɔl di at-wɔri, wan minin we nɔ izi fɔ ɔndastand, ɛn, insay sɔm man dɛn, wan big big enjɛl; so du dɛn diligent tracings-out nɔ belie di obvious deduction. Fɔ tray di jɛnɛral dɛn fɔ dɛn ay mɔtal misɛf ya, de kɛr wi las las bitwin di gɔd dɛn primogenitures we nɔ gɛt sɔs; so dat, pan ɔl di san dɛn we gladi, we de mek hay, ɛn di saf saf simbal, rawnd avɛst-mun, wi fɔ nid fɔ giv-ɔp to dis: fɔ mek di gɔd dɛnsɛf nɔ gladi sote go. Di bɔn-mak we nɔ go ɛva kɔmɔt, we de mek pɔsin fil bad we de na mɔtalman in brɔda, na jɔs di stamp fɔ sɔri we de na di wan dɛn we de sayn.

We dɛn nɔ no se na ya, dɛn dɔn tɔk bɔt wan sikrit, we sɔntɛm i go mɔs bi se, insay wan we we dɛn dɔn sɛt, dɛn dɔn tɔk bɔt am bifo. Wit bɔku ɔda patikyula tin dɛn we gɛt fɔ du wit Eab, ɔltɛm i bin dɔn de bi mistɛri to sɔm, wetin mek i bin bi, se fɔ wan patikyula tɛm, bifo ɛn afta di Pequod sel, i bin dɔn ayd insɛf fa wit da kayn Grɔn-Lama-layk ɛksklusivnɛs de; ɛn, fɔ da wan intaval de, i bin de fɛn say fɔ rɔnawe we i nɔ ebul fɔ tɔk, lɛk se, bitwin di mabul sɛnat fɔ di wan dɛn we dɔn day. Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg in brut rizin fɔ dis tin nɔ bin tan lɛk se i nɔ fit; bɔt fɔ tru, as i tɔch ɔl Eab in dip pat, ɛni rivyu bin tek mɔ pan impɔtant daknɛs pas layt we de ɛksplen. Bɔt, na di ɛnd, ɔltin kɔmɔt na do; dis wan mata bin du, at least. Da bad bad mishap de na di bottom fɔ in fɔ sɔm tɛm we i nɔ bin de nia ɔda pipul dɛn. Ɛn nɔto dis nɔmɔ, bɔt to da sɛklɔ we de ɔltɛm kɔntrakt, we de drɔp na di si, we, fɔ ɛni rizin, bin gɛt di ɔnɔ fɔ mek i kam nia am we dɛn nɔ ban bɛtɛ; to dat shemful sɛklɔ di ɔp hinted casualty—remain, as it did, moodily unaccounted for Ehab—invest insɛf wit terrors, nɔto ɔl ɔndaived frɔm di land fɔ spirit ɛn fɔ kray. So dat, tru di zil we dɛn bin gɛt fɔ am, dɛn ɔl bin dɔn plan, so fa as insay dɛn bin de, fɔ mek ɔda pipul dɛn nɔ no bɔt dis tin; ɛn na dat mek i bi, se nɔto te wan kɔnsidabul intaval dɔn pas, i transpire pan di Pequod in dɛk dɛn.

Bɔt bi ɔl dis as i bi; mek di sinɔd we dɛn nɔ de si, we nɔ klia na di ays, ɔ di prins dɛn ɛn di wan dɛn we gɛt pawa fɔ pe bak, gɛt fɔ du ɔ nɔ gɛt fɔ du wit Eab we de na dis wɔl, bɔt stil, insay dis tin we de apin naw bɔt in leg, i tek klia prɛktikal we dɛn fɔ du;—i kɔl di kapɛnta.

Ɛn we da funkshɔnal de apia bifo am, i tɛl am witout delay fɔ bigin fɔ mek nyu leg, ɛn dayrɛkt di mates fɔ si am saplae wit ɔl di stɔd ɛn joist dɛn fɔ jaw-ivory (Sperm Whale) we dɛn dɔn gɛda te naw na di voyage, so dat dɛn go tek tɛm pik di tin dɛn we strɔng pas ɔl, we gɛt klia gren. We dɛn dɔn du dis, di kapɛnta bin gɛt ɔda lɔ fɔ mek dɛn dɔn di leg da nɛt de; ɛn fɔ gi ɔl di fitin dɛn fɔ am, indipɛndɛnt frɔm di wan dɛn we gɛt fɔ du wit di wan we dɛn nɔ trɔst we dɛn de yuz. Pantap dat, dɛn bin tɛl dɛn fɔ es di ship in fɔj kɔmɔt na di say we dɛn bin de kip am fɔ sɔm tɛm; ɛn fɔ mek di wok go bifo kwik kwik wan, dɛn bin tɛl di blaksmit fɔ go wantɛm wantɛm fɔ mek ɛnitin we dɛn nid fɔ mek wit ayɛn.
