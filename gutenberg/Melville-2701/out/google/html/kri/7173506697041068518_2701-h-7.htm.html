CHAPTER 33. Di Spɛksnayda.

We i kam pan di ɔfisa dɛn we de na di wɛl-kraft, dis tan lɛk se na gud ples lɛk ɛni wan fɔ sɛt smɔl domestik pekyuliti pan ship-bɔd, we kɔmɔt frɔm di ɛgzistens fɔ di harpooneer klas ɔf ɔfisa dɛn, wan klas we dɛn nɔ no fɔ tru insay ɛni ɔda marin pas di wɛl-flit.

Di big impɔtants we dɛn gi di harpooneer in wok de sho bay di tru tin, se fɔs na di ol Dutch Fishery, tu sɛnti ia ɛn mɔ bifo, di kɔmand fɔ wan wɛl ship nɔ bin de ɔlsay na di pɔsin we dɛn de kɔl naw di kapten, bɔt dɛn bin sheb am bitwin am ɛn wan ɔfisa we dɛn kɔl di Specksnyder. Literally dis wɔd min Fat-Cutter; bɔt aw dɛn bin de yuz am, as tɛm de go, i bin mek i ikwal to Chif Harpuoneer. Dɛn tɛm dɛn de, di kapten in pawa bin jɔs de fɔ di we aw di bot de go ɛn di jenɛral manejmɛnt fɔ di bot; we i bin de oba di dipatmɛnt fɔ fɛn wɛl ɛn ɔl di tin dɛn we bin de mɔna am, di Spɛksnayda ɔ Chif Hapun bin de rul. Insay di British Grinland Fishery, ɔnda di kɔrɔpt taytul Specksioneer, dɛn stil kip dis ol Dutch ɔfisa, bɔt i sɔri fɔ no se dɛn dɔn shɔt di rɛspɛkt we i bin gɛt trade. Naw i rank jɔs as sinia Harpooneer; ɛn as a so, na jɔs wan pan di kapten in mɔ infɛriɔ subalterns. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, as pan di gud kɔndɔkt we di harpuoneers de du di sakses fɔ wan wɛl voyage de dipen mɔ, ɛn bikɔs insay di American Fishery i nɔ jɔs bi impɔtant ɔfisa na di bot, bɔt ɔnda sɔm sikɔstɛms (nayt wach na wan wɛl ples) di kɔmand fɔ di ship in dɛk na in bak; so di big big pɔlitikal maksim fɔ di si de aks fɔ, se i fɔ nominally liv apat frɔm di man dɛn bifo di mast, ɛn bi in sɔm we difrɛns as dɛn prɔfɛshɔnal supia; pan ɔl we ɔltɛm, bay dɛn, dɛn kin tek am as dɛn soshal ikwal.

Naw, di big difrɛns we dɛn drɔ bitwin ɔfisa ɛn man na si, na dis—di fɔs wan de liv biɛn, di las wan de go bifo. So, insay wɛl-ship ɛn biznɛsman dɛn, di mared pipul dɛn kin gɛt dɛn kwata wit di kapten; ɛn so, bak, insay bɔku pan di Amɛrikan pipul dɛn we de kech wɛl di harpuoneers dɛn kin lod na di afta pat na di ship. Dat min se, dɛn kin tek dɛn it na di kapten in kabin, ɛn slip na ples we dɛn de tɔk to am indaykt wan.

Pan ɔl we di lɔng tɛm we pipul dɛn kin travul fɔ go kech wɛl na di Sawt (we na di lɔng pas ɔl di waka dɛn we mɔtalman dɔn travul naw ɔ we i dɔn ɛva waka), di spɛshal prɔblɛm dɛn we kin apin to am, ɛn di kɔmyuniti we gɛt intres we de bitwin wan kɔmni, we dɛn ɔl, we ay ɔ we smɔl, nɔ kin dipen fɔ dɛn prɔfit, nɔto pan di pe we dɛn kin pe dɛn, bɔt dɛn kin dipen pan dɛn kɔmɔn lɔk, togɛda wit dɛn kɔmɔn we aw dɛn kin wach, we nɔ gɛt maynd, ɛn we dɛn kin wok tranga wan; pan ɔl we ɔl dɛn tin ya kin du insay sɔm kes dɛn kin bɔn wan less rigorous disiplin pas in biznɛsman dɛn jɔs; yet, nɔ ɛva tink bɔt aw lɛk wan ol Mɛsopotemia famili, dɛn wɛlman ya kin, insay sɔm fɔstɛm instans dɛn, liv togɛda; fɔ ɔl dat, di punctilious externals, at least, of di kwata-dɛk nɔ kin rili rilaks wit matirial, ɛn in no instans dɛn kin dɔn am. Fɔ tru, bɔku pan dɛn na di Nantucket ship dɛn we yu go si di skipa de parade in kwata-dɛk wit wan elated grandeur we nɔ pas ɛni soja nevi; nay, extorting almost as much outward homage as if i wɛr di imperial pepul, ɛn nɔto di shabbiest of pilot-cloth.

Ɛn pan ɔl we pan ɔl di man dɛn di kapten we bin de mud di Pequod na bin di lɛst giv to da kayn shalɔs asɔmpshɔn de; ɛn pan ɔl we di wangren ɔmajɛ we i ɛva aks fɔ, na fɔ obe am wantɛm wantɛm; pan ɔl we i nɔ bin nid ɛnibɔdi fɔ pul di sus na in fut bifo i step pan di kwata-dɛk; ɛn pan ɔl we tɛm dɛn bin de we, bikɔs ɔf spɛshal tin dɛn we gɛt fɔ du wit di tin dɛn we go apin afta dis we dɛn go tɔk bɔt, i bin de tɔk to dɛn wit wɔd dɛn we nɔ kɔmɔn, ilɛksɛf na fɔ put insɛf dɔŋ ɔ fɔ mek pipul dɛn fred , ɔ ɔda we; yet ivin Kpɛntin Eab nɔ bin de si di impɔtant we dɛn ɛn di we aw dɛn de yuz di si.

Sɔntɛm, dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ no bak se biɛn dɛn fɔm ɛn yus dɛn de, lɛk se, sɔntɛnde i bin de mask insɛf; incidentally mek yus of dem for oda en mo prayvet ends dan dem legitimately intend to subserve. Dat sɔm sultanism na in bren, we bin dɔn ɔdasay in gud digri nɔ bin de; tru dɛn fɔm dɛn de da sem sultanism bin kam fɔ bi bɔdi insay wan diktetaship we nɔ go ebul fɔ stɔp. Fɔ bi man in intɛlektual supiriɔriti wetin i go, i nɔ go ɛva ebul fɔ tek di prɛktikal, avaylabl suprimi pas ɔda man dɛm, if i nɔ gɛt di ɛp fɔ sɔm kayn ɛksternal art ɛn entrenchmɛnt dɛm, ɔltɛm, insay dɛnsɛf, mɔ ɔ lɛs paltry ɛn bays. Dis na, we fɔ sote go kip Gɔd in tru tru prins dɛn na di Ɛmpaya frɔm di wɔl in hustings; ɛn lɛf di ays ɔna we dis ɛya kin gi, to dɛn man dɛn we kin bi fambul mɔ tru dɛn infinite inferiority to di choice hiden handful of di Divine Inert, pas tru dɛn undoubted superiority ova di ded level of di mass. Dis kayn big gud kwaliti de lurk insay dɛn smɔl tin ya we ekstrim pɔlitikal lay lay biliv dɛn de invɛst dɛn, dat insay sɔm kiŋ instans dɛn ivin to idiot imbecility dɛn dɔn gi pawa. Bɔt we, lɛk Nikolas di Zar in kes, di krawn we gɛt ring fɔ di jografik ɛmpaya de rawnd wan impyɔrian bren; den, di plebeian herds crouch abased bifo di tremendous sentralization. Nɔto, di trayjik dramatis we go sho mɔtalman indomitableness in in ful swip ɛn dairekt swing, nɔ go ɛva fɔgɛt wan hint, incidentally so impɔtant insay in art, as di wan we dɛn de tɔk bɔt naw.

Bɔt Eab, mi Kaptɛn, stil de muf bifo mi wit ɔl in Nantucket grimness ɛn shagginess; ɛn insay dis episɔd we de tɔch Emparɔ ɛn Kiŋ dɛn, a nɔ fɔ ayd se a jɔs gɛt fɔ du wit wan po ol wɛl-ɔntin lɛk am; ɛn, bikɔs ɔf dat, dɛn nɔ de gi mi ɔl di ɔda fayn fayn trap dɛn ɛn os dɛn we de na do. Oh, Eab! wetin go bi grand insay yu, i fɔ nid fɔ pul am frɔm di skay, ɛn dayv fɔ am na di dip, ɛn sho am na di briz we nɔ gɛt bɔdi!





CHAPTER 34. Di Kabin-Tebul.

Na midulnɛt; ɛn Dough-Boy, di stiwɔd, we de push in pale bred-brɛd fes frɔm di kabin-skata, de anawns dina to in masta ɛn masta; we, we sidɔm na di lee kwata-bɔt, jɔs dɔn de tek wan ɔbshɔbishɔn fɔ di san; ɛn naw i de kɔl di latitud we de na di smɔl smɔl tablɛt we tan lɛk mɛdal, we dɛn kip fɔ da tin de ɛvride na di ɔp pat pan in ayvri leg. Frɔm di we aw i nɔ bin pe atɛnshɔn to di nyus atɔl, yu go tink se Eab we bin de fil bad nɔ bin yɛri in ɔmbul. Bɔt naw, we i ol di mizen shroud dɛn, i swing insɛf go na di dɛk, ɛn wit wan ivin, nɔ gladi vɔys, se, “Dina, Mista Stabɔk,” i lɔs insay di kabin.

We di las ɛko fɔ in sultan in stɛp dɔn day, ɛn Starbuck, di fɔs Emir, gɛt ɔl di rizin fɔ tink se i sidɔm, dɔn Starbuck wek frɔm in kwayɛt, tek sɔm tɔn dɛn along di plank dɛn, ɛn, afta wan grev peep insay di binnacle, se, wit sɔm tɔch fɔ gladi, “Dinner, Mr. Stubb,” ɛn dɔŋ di scuttle. Di sɛkɔn Emir lounge bɔt di rigging sɔm tɛm, ɛn afta dat i shek di men bres smɔl, fɔ si if i go fayn wit da impɔtant rop de, insɛf tek di ol lod, ɛn wit wan kwik “Dinner, Mr. Flask,” de fala afta di wan dɛn we bin de bifo am.

Bɔt di tɔd Emir, we naw de si insɛf ɔl in wan na di kwata-dɛk, i tan lɛk se i fil fri frɔm sɔm kɔriɔs kɔntrol; bikɔs, we i de tip ɔlkayn wink dɛn we i no na ɔlkayn say, ɛn kik in sus, i de slap insay wan shap bɔt nɔys swɛlin we de mek wan ɔnpaip rayt oba di Grɛnd Turk in ed; ɛn afta dat, bay wan dexterous sleight, we i de pitch in kap ɔp insay di mizentop fɔ wan shelf, i go dɔŋ rollicking so fa at least as i de kɔntinyu fɔ si am frɔm di dɛk, rivɛns ɔl di ɔda prushɔn dɛn, bay we i de briŋ ɔp di riya wit myuzik. Bɔt bifo i step insay di kabin domɔt we de dɔŋ, i stɔp smɔl, i ship nyu fes ɔltogɛda, ɛn, afta dat, indipɛndɛnt, hilarious smɔl Flask go insay Kiŋ Eab in fes, insay di karakta we Abjectus, ɔ di Slev gɛt.

I nɔto di smɔl wan pan di strenj tin dɛn we di intens artificialness of si-usages brid, se we dɛn de na di opin ɛya na di dɛk sɔm ɔfisa dɛn, we dɛn provokeshɔn, bia dɛnsɛf wit maynd ɛn defyingly inof to dɛn kɔmanda; yet, tɛn to wan, lɛ dɛn ɔfisa dɛn de di nɛks mɔnt go dɔŋ to dɛn kɔstɔm dina na da sem kɔmanda in kebin de, ɛn wantɛm wantɛm dɛn inofɛns, nɔ fɔ se deprecatory ɛn ɔmbul ɛya to am, as i sidɔm na di ed fɔ di tebul; dis na wɔndaful tin, sɔntɛnde i kin mek pɔsin kɔmɛnt pas ɔl. Wetin mek dis difrɛns? Prɔblɛm? Sɔntɛm nɔto so. Fɔ bi Bɛlshaza, we na bin Kiŋ na Babilɔn; ɛn fɔ bi Bɛlshaza, nɔto fɔ prawd bɔt fɔ sho rɛspɛkt, fɔ tru, i go mɔs bi se i bin gɛt sɔm tin dɛn we gɛt fɔ du wit di wɔl. Bɔt di wan we insay di rayt rigal ɛn intɛligent spirit de bifo in yon prayvet dina-tebul fɔ invayt gɔst dɛn, da man de in pawa we nɔ gɛt chalenj ɛn dominɔn fɔ wan wan inflɔɛns fɔ di tɛm; dat man in kiŋdɔm pas Bɛlshaza in yon, bikɔs Bɛlshaza nɔto bin di bigman. We dɔn it in padi dɛn wan tɛm nɔmɔ, dɔn test wetin i bi fɔ bi Siza. Na witchery of soshal czarship we no withstanding nɔ de. Naw, if to dis kɔnsidareshɔn yu superadd di ɔfishal suprimi fɔ wan ship-masta, den, bay infɔmeshɔn, yu go derive di kɔz fɔ da peculiarity of si-layf we wi jɔs tɔk bɔt.

Ova in tebul we dɛn mek wit ayvri, Eab bin de bifo lɛk si layɔn we nɔ de tɔk ɛn we gɛt bɔdi na di wayt kɔral bich, ɛn in pikin dɛn we lɛk fɛt-fɛt bɔt we stil de rɛspɛkt bin de rawnd am. Insay in yon rayt tɔn, ɛni ɔfisa bin de wet fɔ mek dɛn sav am. Dɛn bin tan lɛk smɔl pikin dɛn bifo Eab; ɛn pan ɔl dat, insay Eab, i bin tan lɛk se di smɔl smɔl soshal prawd nɔ bin de. Wit wan maynd, dɛn intent yay dɛn ɔl bin tay pan di ol man in nɛf, as i de kɔt di chif dish bifo am. A nɔ tink se fɔ di wɔl dɛn bin fɔ dɔn dɔti da tɛm de wit di smɔl ɔbshɔbishɔn, ivin pan tɔpik we nɔ gɛt wan bɔt lɛk di wɛda. Nɔ! Ɛn we Eab es in nɛf ɛn fɔk, we dɛn lɔk di slais bif bitwin dɛn, dat mek Eab mek sayn to Stabɔk in plet to am, di pɔsin we i mared gɛt in mit lɛk se i de gɛt alms; ɛn kɔt am fayn fayn wan; ɛn smɔl bigin if, sɔntɛm, di nɛf gras agens di plet; ɛn chew am we nɔ gɛt nɔys; ɛn swɛla am, nɔto we i nɔ tek tɛm. Bikɔs, lɛk di Koroneshɔn bankwet na Frankfɔt, usay di Jaman Emparɔ de it dip dip wan wit di sɛvin Impɛrial Ilɛktɔ dɛn, so dɛn kebin it ya na bin sɔm kayn we fɔ it dɛn we dɛn bin de it, we dɛn bin de it insay bad bad silɛns; ɛn yet na tebul ol Eab nɔ bin ban fɔ tɔk; na insɛf nɔmɔ bin mumu. I bin rili fil fri fɔ chok Stub, we wan rat mek wan rɛkɛt wantɛm wantɛm na di hold we de dɔŋ. Ɛn po smɔl Flask, na in na bin di yɔŋ bɔy pikin, ɛn smɔl bɔbɔ fɔ dis famili pati we bin taya. In yon na bin di shinbon dɛn fɔ di salin bif; in yon fɔ dɔn bi di drɔmstik dɛn. Fɔ mek Flask tink se i de ɛp insɛf, dis go dɔn tan lɛk se i tan lɛk tif insay di fɔs digri. If i bin ɛp insɛf na da tebul de, wi nɔ gɛt wan dawt, i nɔ bin fɔ dɔn ɛva ebul fɔ ol in ed ɔp na dis ɔnɛs wɔl; pan ɔl dat, i strenj fɔ se, Eab nɔ ɛva ban am. Ɛn if Flask bin ɛp insɛf, di chans bin de fɔ se Eab nɔ bin dɔn ɛva notis am. Di tin we nɔ pas ɔl, na dat Flask bin tink se i ɛp insɛf fɔ mek bɔta. If i bin tink se di wan dɛn we gɛt di ship nɔ gri fɔ gi am am, bikɔs i bin de klɔt in klia, san san; ɔ if i tink se, we i de travul fɔ lɔng tɛm na dɛn kayn wata dɛn de we nɔ gɛt makit, bɔta de na prɛmiɔm, ɛn dat mek i nɔto fɔ am, na subaltern; pan ɔl we i bin bi, Flask, alas! na bin man we nɔ gɛt bɔta!

Wan ɔda tin. Flask na bin di las pɔsin we bin de dɔŋ na di dina, ɛn Flask na di fɔs man we bin kam ɔp. Tink bɔt! Fɔ dis, Flask in dina bin bad bad wan jam insay pɔynt fɔ tɛm. Starbuck ɛn Stubb ɔl tu bin gɛt di stat fɔ am; ɛn pan ɔl dat, dɛn gɛt di ɔnɔ bak fɔ slip na di bak. If Stubb ivin, we na jɔs wan peg ay pas Flask, apin fɔ gɛt bɔt smɔl apɛtit, ɛn i nɔ tu te i sho simptom dɛn fɔ dɔn in it, den Flask fɔ bestir insɛf, i nɔ go gɛt pas tri mɔtful da de de; bikɔs i agens oli yus fɔ mek Stubb bifo Flask to di dɛk. So na dat Flask bin admit wan tɛm insay prayvet, se frɔm we i dɔn rayz to di rɛspɛkt fɔ ɔfisa, frɔm da tɛm de i nɔ ɛva no wetin i bi fɔ bi ɔda we pas angri, mɔ ɔ smɔl. Bikɔs wetin i it nɔ bin mek i nɔ angri, bɔt i bin mek i nɔ day insay am. Flask bin tink se pis ɛn satisfayshɔn dɔn kɔmɔt na mi bɛlɛ fɔ ɔltɛm. Mi na ɔfisa; bɔt, aw a wish se a kin fish smɔl ol-fashin bif na di fɔkas, lɛk aw a bin de du we a bin de bifo di mast. Na di frut dɛm fɔ promoshɔn de naw; na di vaniti fɔ glori de: na di krayse na layf de! Apat frɔm dat, if i bin so dat ɛni jɔs selɔ na di Pequod bin gɛt grɔj agens Flask insay Flask in ɔfishal kapasiti, ɔl wetin da selɔ de bin gɛt fɔ du, fɔ mek i go gɛt bɔku blɛsin, na fɔ go aft na dina-taym, ɛn gɛt wan peep pan Flask tru di kabin skay-layt, sidɔm sili ɛn dufounded bifo awful Ahab.

Naw, Eab ɛn in tri kɔmpin dɛn bin mek wetin dɛn go kɔl di fɔs tebul na di Pequod in kebin. Afta dɛn kɔmɔt, we bin de apin insay invayt ɔda te dɛn kam, dɛn bin de klia di kanvas klos, ɔ fɔ se, di pallid stiwɔd bin mek am bak to sɔm ɔda we aw dɛn bin de mek am kwik kwik wan. Ɛn afta dat, dɛn bin kɔl di tri pipul dɛn we de wok wit harpun fɔ go na di fɛstival, ɛn na dɛn na bin di wan dɛn we lɛf na di fɛstival. Dɛn mek wan kayn tɛmporari savant dɛn ɔl fɔ di ay ɛn pawaful kebin.

In strenj kɔntrast to di hardly tolerable constraint ɛn nameless invisible domineerings of di kapten in tebul, na di ɔl kia-fri laysens ɛn izi, di ɔlmost frantic dimokrasi fɔ dɛn inferior felo dɛn di harpooneers. Pan ɔl we i bin tan lɛk se dɛn masta dɛn, we na di man ɛn uman dɛn, bin de fred di sawnd we di hinj dɛn na dɛn yon jaw de mek, di wan dɛn we de mek harpoon bin de it dɛn it wit gladi at so dat ripɔt bin de to am. Dɛn bin de it lɛk masta dɛn; dɛn bin de ful-ɔp dɛn bɛlɛ lɛk Indian ship dɛn ɔl di de we de lod wit spays. Dɛn kayn portentous apɛtit dɛn ya bin gɛt Queequeg ɛn Tashtego, dat fɔ ful-ɔp di vakɛns dɛn we di fɔs it we dɛn bin dɔn it bin mek, bɔku tɛm di pale Dough-Boy bin fain fɔ briŋ wan big baron fɔ sɔl-jɔnk, we i tan lɛk se dɛn dɔn kɔt am kɔmɔt na di sɔlid ɔks. Ɛn if i nɔ bin gɛt layf bɔt am, if i nɔ bin go wit wan nimbul hop-skip-ɛn-jɔmp, den Tashtego bin gɛt wan we we nɔ gɛt sɛns fɔ aksɛleret am bay we i de dart wan fɔk na in bak, we i de yuz harpun. Ɛn wan tɛm, Daggoo, we dɛn bin seiz wit wan sɔdɛn kɔmɛnt, ɛp Dɔg-bɔy in mɛmori bay we i snɛp am ɔp bɔdi, ɛn push in ed insay wan big ɛmti wud trencha, we Tashtego, we ol nɛf na in an, bigin fɔ layt di sɛklɔ fɔs fɔ skel am. Na insɛf na bin smɔl pɔsin we bin rili de fred, we bin de shek shek, dis stiwɔd we gɛt fes we tan lɛk bred; di pikin dɛn we wan bankrupt baker ɛn wan ɔspitul nɔs bɔn. Ɛn wetin wit di tinap spɛktakl we di blak wɔndaful Eab bin de si, ɛn di tɛm we dɛn tri savayj dɛn ya bin de kam fɛn am wit bɔku bɔku pipul dɛn, Dɔ-bɔy in wan ol layf na bin wan kɔntinyu lip-kwɛv. Bɔku tɛm, afta we i si di harpuoneers dɛn ful-ɔp wit ɔl di tin dɛn we dɛn de aks fɔ, i kin rɔnawe kɔmɔt na dɛn an ɛn go insay in smɔl pantri we de nia am, ɛn i kin fred fɔ luk dɛn tru di blaynd dɛn na in domɔt, te ɔltin dɔn.

Na bin fayn tin fɔ si Kwik sidɔm oba agens Tashtego, de agens in fayl tit dɛn to di Indian in yon: krɔs to dɛn, Daggoo sidɔm na grɔn, bikɔs wan bɛnch fɔ dɔn briŋ in ed we gɛt bɔdi-plum to di lɔw karlayn dɛn; pan ɛvri muvmɛnt we in big big an dɛn de muv, we de mek di lɔw kabin fremwɔk shek, lɛk we Afrikan ɛlifant de go pasɛnja na ship. Bɔt fɔ ɔl dis, di big nigro bin rili abstemious, nɔto fɔ se dainty. I bin tan lɛk se i nɔ pɔsibul fɔ mek bay dɛn kayn smɔl smɔl mɔt dɛn de, i go ebul fɔ kip di vitality we dɛn de skata tru so brayt, baronial, ɛn superb pɔsin. Bɔt, wi nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se dis nobul savayj bin de it strɔng ɛn drink dip dip pan di bɔku bɔku tin dɛn we de na di briz; ɛn tru in dilayt nos dɛn we i snuf insay di sublaym layf fɔ di wɔl dɛn. Nɔto bay bif ɔ bay bred, dɛn mek jayant dɛn ɔ dɛn de gi dɛn tin fɔ it. Bɔt Kwik, i bin gɛt wan mɔtalman, barbaric smack na di lip we i de it—wan ɔgly sawnd inof—sote di Dough-Boy we bin de shek shek bin lɛf smɔl fɔ luk fɔ si if ɛni mak pan tit de lurk insay in yon slim an dɛn. Ɛn we i go yɛri Tashtego de siŋ fɔ mek i prodyuz insɛf, so dat dɛn go pik in bon dɛn, di stiwɔd we gɛt simpul sɛns bin jɔs brok brok di krok we bin de hang rawnd am na di pantri, bay di we aw i bin gɛt di palsi we i bin de fil wantɛm wantɛm. Nɔto di wɛtston we di wan dɛn we de mek hapun bin de kɛr na dɛn poket, fɔ dɛn lans ɛn ɔda wɛpɔn dɛn; ɛn we dɛn kin yuz wetston dɛn, we dɛn de it dina, dɛn kin shap dɛn nɛf dɛn fɔ sho se dɛn de shap dɛn nɛf dɛn; dat gratin sawnd nɔ bin atɔl fɔ mek po Dɔg-Bɔy kwayɛt. Aw i go fɔgɛt se insay in Ayland tɛm, Kwik, fɔ wan, go mɔs dɔn gilti fɔ sɔm kil, kɔnvivial indiskrɛshɔn dɛn. Alas! Dɔti-Bɔy! hard fares di wayt weita we de wet pan kanibal. Nɔto napkin i fɔ kɛr na in an, bɔt na bukla. Bɔt as tɛm de go, i bin rili gladi we di tri sɔl-si wɔman dɛn go grap ɛn kɔmɔt; to in yes dɛn we nɔ biliv ɛn we de tɔk lay lay stori dɛn, ɔl dɛn mared bon dɛn de jingl insay dɛn ɛvri stɛp, lɛk Moorish scimetars we de insay skab.

Bɔt, pan ɔl we dɛn barbarian ya bin de it na di kebin, ɛn nominally bin de liv de; stil, bikɔs dɛn nɔ bin de sidɔm na dɛn abit, dɛn nɔ bin de ɛva de insay am pas we dɛn de it, ɛn jɔs bifo dɛn slip, we dɛn pas de go na dɛn yon spɛshal say.

Insay dis wan tin, i bin tan lɛk se Eab nɔ bin de pan bɔku pan di Amɛrikan wɛl kapten dɛn, we, as sɛt, dɛn kin rili tink se bay rayt di ship in kabin na dɛn yon; ɛn na bay kɔtisti nɔmɔ, ɛni ɔda pɔsin, ɛni tɛm, dɛn kin alaw am fɔ de de. So dat, fɔ tru, di mates ɛn harpooneers fɔ di Pequod go mɔs se dɛn bin de liv na do na di kebin pas insay de. Bikɔs we dɛn go insay de, na sɔntin we tan lɛk strit domɔt we de go insay os; tɔn insay fɔ smɔl tɛm, na fɔ tɔn aut di nɛks wan; ɛn, as tin we go de sote go, fɔ de na di opin ples. Dɛn nɔ bin lɔs bɔku tin bak pan dis; insay di kebin, nɔbɔdi nɔ bin de wit am; soshal, Eab nɔ bin ebul fɔ go de. Pan ɔl we dɛn bin de kɔl am insay di sɛns we dɛn bin de kɔnt Kristian Kristian dɛn, i bin stil bi strenja to di Kristian rilijɔn. I bin de liv na di wɔl, lɛk aw di las wan pan di Grisly Bears bin de liv na Misɔri we dɛn bin dɔn sɛtul. Ɛn lɛk we Spring ɛn Sɔma dɔn go, da wayl Lɔgan we de na di bush, we de bɛr insɛf na di ol na wan tik, bin de liv aut di winta de, de sok in yon fut; so, insay in ol ej we nɔ bin fayn ɛn we bin de ala, Eab in sol, bin lɔk insay di kev trɔnk na in bɔdi, de it di sullen paw dɛn we i dak!





CHAPTER 35. Di Mast-Hɛd.

Na di tɛm we di wɛda bin fayn mɔ, na in we a bin de rɔtin di rayt we wit di ɔda siman dɛn, mi fɔs mast-hɛd bin kam rawnd.

Insay bɔku pan di Amɛrikin wɛlman dɛn di mast-hɛd dɛn kin gɛt pipul dɛn klos wan tɛm wit di bot we de kɔmɔt na in pɔt; ivin if i kin gɛt fayvtin tawzin mayl, ɛn mɔ, fɔ sel bifo i rich na in rayt kruiz grɔn. Ɛn if, afta tri, 4, ɔ fayv ia travul, i de kam nia os wit ɛnitin we ɛmti insay am—lɛ wi se, ɛmti vayl ivin—dɛn, dɛn kin kip in mast-hed dɛn te to di las wan; ɛn nɔto te in skay-sɛl-pol dɛn sel insay midul di spia dɛn na di pɔt, i kin lɛf di op ɔltogɛda fɔ kech wan wɛl mɔ.

Naw, as di biznɛs fɔ tinap mast-hɛd, na di sho ɔ na wata, na wan we rili ol ɛn intrestin, lɛ wi in sɔm mɛzhɔ expatiate ya. A tek am, se di fɔs pipul dɛn we bin tinap fɔ mast-hɛd na di ol Ijipshian dɛn; bikɔs, insay ɔl mi risach dɛn, a nɔ si ɛni wan bifo dɛn. Bikɔs pan ɔl we dɛn gret gret granpa dɛn, we na di wan dɛn we bil Bebɛl, go mɔs dɔn, bay dɛn tawa, bin dɔn mekɔp dɛn maynd fɔ mek di mast-ed we ay pas ɔl na ɔl Eshia, ɔ Afrika sɛf; yet (ere dɛn put di las trak pan am) as dɛn kin se da big ston mast we dɛn gɛt bin go nia di bod, insay di bad bad briz we Gɔd in wamat bin de blo; so, wi nɔ go ebul fɔ gi dɛn Bebɛl bilda dɛn ya fɔs pas di Ijipshian dɛn. Ɛn se di Ijipshian dɛn na bin neshɔn we tinap wit mast-ed, na wan asɛsmɛnt we dɛn bays pan di jenɛral biliv we di wan dɛn we de stɔdi bɔt aw pipul dɛn de du tin trade trade bin biliv, se dɛn bin bil di fɔs piramid dɛn fɔ astronomik rizin dɛn: wan tiori we dɛn sɔpɔt wan wan bay di pekul we tan lɛk stɛp fɔ ɔl di 4 say dɛn na dɛn bildin dɛn de; we, wit prodigious long upliftings of their legs, dem ol astronomers bin yus fɔ go ɔp to di apex, ɛn siŋ fɔ nyu sta dɛm; ivin lɛk aw di say dɛn we pipul dɛn kin luk fɔ wan ship we de naw kin siŋ fɔ mek dɛn ebul fɔ go sel, ɔ wɛl we jɔs de na di say we dɛn de si. Insay Sɛnt Stayl, di Kristian ɛmit we bin gɛt nem trade trade, we bil am wan ay ay ston pila na di dɛzat ɛn spɛn di wan ol las pat pan in layf na in mawnten, ɛn i bin de es in it frɔm di grɔn wit takɛl; insay am wi gɛt wan wɔndaful ɛgzampul bɔt wan pɔsin we nɔ de fred fɔ tinap fɔ mast-ed; we nɔ fɔ mek fɔg ɔ frɔst, ren, ays blɔk, ɔ slit drɛb kɔmɔt na in ples; bɔt valiantly fes ɔltin aut to di las, rili day na in post. Fɔ di mɔdan standers-of-mast-heads wi gɛt bɔt wan sɛt we nɔ gɛt layf; na jɔs ston, ayɛn, ɛn brɔnz man dɛn; we pan ɔl we dɛn ebul fɔ fes wan big big briz, dɛn stil nɔ ebul fɔ du di biznɛs fɔ siŋ we dɛn fɛn ɛni strenj tin we dɛn de si. Napoleɔn de de; we, pan di tap pan di kɔlɔn na Vendome, tinap wit in an dɛn we dɛn fold, lɛk wan ɔndrɛd ɛn fifti fit na di ays; careless, naw, udat de rul di dɛk dɛn we de dɔŋ; ilɛksɛf na Luis Filip, Luis Blank, ɔ Luis di Dɛbul. Gret Washinton, insɛf, tinap ay ay wan pan in ay ay men-mast na Baltimo, ɛn lɛk wan pan Hɛkyul in pila dɛn, in kɔlɔm de mak da pɔynt de we mɔtalman gɛt big big wan we na smɔl mɔtalman dɛn nɔmɔ go go pas. Admiral Nelson, insɛf, pan wan kapstan we dɛn mek wit gɔn-mɛtal, tinap in mast-ed na Trafalgar Square; ɛn ɛva we dɛn mɔs ɔbskɔr bay da Lɔndɔn smok de, dɛn de gi token yet se wan hiro we ayd de de; bikɔs usay smok de, faya fɔ de. Bɔt nɔto big Washinton, ɔ Nɛpɔlyɔn, ɔ Nɛlson, nɔ go ansa wan singl ays blɔk frɔm dɔŋ, ilɛksɛf dɛn advays dɛn kɔl dɛn fɔ mek padi wit di dɛk dɛn we de ambɔg dɛn we dɛn de luk pan; ɔltogɛda dɛn kin tink se dɛn spirit kin go insay di tik tik ays we go kam, ɛn diskray us shoal ɛn us rɔk dɛn fɔ avɔyd.

I kin tan lɛk se i nɔ mek sɛns fɔ kɔpl di wan dɛn we de tinap na di land we gɛt mast-hed wit di wan dɛn we de na di si pan ɛni we; bɔt se fɔ tru, i nɔ so, de sho klia wan bay wan tin we Obed Macy, we na di wangren istri man bɔt Nantucket, tinap fɔ ansa fɔ. Di worthy Obed tɛl wi, se insay di fɔs tɛm we dɛn bin de fishin wɛl, ere ship dɛn bin de lanch ɔltɛm fɔ rɔnata di gem, di pipul dɛn na da ayland de bin de bil ay ay spars along di si-kɔst, usay di luk-ɔut dɛn bin de go ɔp bay we dɛn yuz nel klit, sɔntin we fɔl dɛn de go ɔp stej na wan hεn-haus. Sɔm ia bifo dis tɛm, di Bay wɛlman dɛn na Nyu Ziland bin tek dis sem plan, ɛn we dɛn tɔk bɔt di gem, dɛn gi notis to di bot dɛn we rɛdi fɔ pipul dɛn we de nia di bich. Bɔt dis kɔstɔm dɔn nɔ de igen naw; tɔn wi den to di wan prɔpa mast-hɛd, we na wɛl-ship we de na si. Dɛn kin kip pipul dɛn na di tri mast-hɛd dɛn frɔm we di san kɔmɔt te di san go dɔŋ; di siman dɛn de tek dɛn rɛgyula tɔn (lɛk na di edman), ɛn rili fri dɛnsɛf ɛvri tu awa. Insay di serene wɛda na di tropiks i kin rili fayn di mast-hɛd; nɔto so, to man we de drim we de tink gud wan, i kin mek pɔsin gladi. Na de yu tinap, wan ɔndrɛd fit ɔp di dɛk dɛn we nɔ gɛt wan vɔys, de strit along di dip, lɛk se di mast dɛn na big big stik dɛn, we ɔnda yu ɛn bitwin yu leg dɛn, lɛk se, yu de swim di big big monsta dɛn na di si, ivin lɛk aw ship dɛn bin de travul bitwin di but dɛn fɔ di fambul Kɔlɔs na ol Rɔds. Na de yu tinap, yu lɔs insay di siriɔs si we nɔ gɛt ɛnd, wit natin we rɔf pas di wef dɛn. Di ship we dɔn trance de rol indolently; di tred briz we de slip de blo; ɔltin de sɔlv yu insay languor. Fɔ di mɔs pat, insay dis tropik wɛl layf, wan sublaym uneventfulness de invɛst yu; yu nɔ de yɛri ɛni nyuz; nɔ rid ɛni gazɛt; ekstra dɛn wit sɔprayz akɔn dɛn bɔt kɔmɔn tin dɛn nɔ de ɛva ful yu fɔ ɛnjɔy yusɛf we yu nɔ nid; yu nɔ de yɛri bɔt ɛnitin we de mek yu sɔfa na os; bankrupt sikyɔriti dɛn; di stok dɛn we de fɔdɔm; nɔ de ɛva wɔri wit di tink bɔt wetin yu go it fɔ dina—bikɔs ɔl di it dɛn we yu de it fɔ tri ia ɛn mɔ, dɛn kin kip dɛn fayn fayn wan insay bɔtul dɛn, ɛn yu bil fɔ pe nɔ kin chenj.

Insay wan pan dɛn wɛl man dɛn de we de na di sawt, we yu de travul fɔ lɔng tɛm fɔ tri ɔ 4 ia, lɛk aw i kin apin bɔku tɛm, di sɔm pan di difrɛn awa dɛn we yu kin spɛn na di mast-hɛd go rich sɔm wan ol mɔnt. Ɛn i rili sɔri fɔ no se di ples we yu de devote so bɔku pat pan di ɔl tɛm na yu natura layf, fɔ so sadly destitute fɔ ɛnitin we de kam nia wan kɔzy inhabitiveness, ɔ adapt fɔ brid wan kɔmfyut lokalness fɔ filin, lɛk pertains to a bed, a hamack, a hearse, a sentry box, a pulpit, a coach, or any other of those small and snug contrivances we man dɛn kin put dɛnsɛf ayd fɔ sɔm tɛm. Di say we yu kin sidɔm pas ɔl na di ed fɔ di t’ gallant-mast, usay yu tinap pan tu tin paralel stik dɛn (we na ɔlmost pekul to wɛlman dɛn) we dɛn kɔl di t’ gallant krɔs-tri dɛn. Na ya, we di si de tos rawnd, di pɔsin we de bigin fɔ fil kin fil fayn lɛk aw i kin fil fayn we i tinap pan kaw in ɔn. Fɔ tru, we kol weta yu kin kɛr yu os go ɔp wit yu, we tan lɛk wach-kot; bɔt fɔ tɔk di rayt we, di wach-kot we tik pas ɔl nɔto os pas di bɔdi we nɔ gɛt klos; bikɔs lɛk aw dɛn glu di sol insay in tabanakul we gɛt bɔdi, ɛn i nɔ ebul fɔ muv fri wan insay de, ɔ ivin muf kɔmɔt de, we i nɔ de rɔn big risk fɔ day (lɛk pilgrim we nɔ no natin we de krɔs di Alps we gɛt sno insay winta); so wach-kot nɔto so mɔ na os bɔt na jɔs ɛnvilɔp, ɔ ɔda skin we de kɔba yu. Yu nɔ go ebul fɔ put shelf ɔ bɔks fɔ drɔwa na yu bɔdi, ɛn yu nɔ go ebul fɔ mek fayn kloz fɔ yu wach-kot igen.

We i kam pan ɔl dis, i rili sɔri fɔ no se di mast-hɛd dɛn na wan wɛl ship we de na di sawt nɔ gɛt dɛn smɔl tɛnt ɔ pulpit dɛn de we pɔsin kin jɛlɔs, we dɛn kɔl kraw-nɛst , usay dɛn kin protɛkt di say dɛn we pɔsin we de kech wɛl na Grinland kin luk fɔ frɔm di bad bad wɛda we di si we dɔn frɔz kin gɛt. Insay di fayasayd stori we Kpɛntin Slit bin rayt, we gɛt di taytul “A Voyage among the Icebergs, in quest of the Greenland Whale, ɛn i apin fɔ mek dɛn fɛn bak di Lost Icelandic Colonies of Old Greenland;” insay dis volyum we wi kin admaya, ɔl di wan dɛn we tinap fɔ mast-hɛd dɛn dɔn ful-ɔp wit wan fayn fayn sikɔstɛms akɔdin to di kraw-nɛst we dɛn jɔs mek da tɛm de na di Gleshia, we na bin di nem fɔ Kpɛntin Slit in gud kraft. I kɔl am di Sleet in krawd-nɛst , fɔ ɔnɔ insɛf; i bi di ɔrijinal invintɔ ɛn patɛnt, ɛn fri frɔm ɔl ridikul lay lay delikasi, ɛn ol se if wi kɔl wi yon pikin dɛn afta wi yon nem (wi papa dɛn bi di ɔrijinal invintɔ ɛn patɛnt), so semweso wi fɔ denominate afta wisɛf ɛni ɔda aprɛshɔn we wi go bɔn. We i shep, di Slit in kraw-nɛst na sɔntin lɛk big tierce ɔ paip; bɔt i opin ɔp usay dɛn dɔn mek am wit sayd-skrin we yu kin muv fɔ mek yu nɔ ebul fɔ blo na yu ed we big big briz de blo. Bikɔs yu fiks na di mawnten fɔ di mast, yu kin go insay de tru wan smɔl trap-hatch we de dɔŋ. Na di afta sayd, ɔ sayd we de nia di stɛp pat na di ship, dɛn gɛt fayn sidɔm ples, ɛn dɛn gɛt lɔka ɔnda fɔ put ambɔla, kɔmfɔta, ɛn kot. Insay di frɔnt, dɛn put lɛda rɛk, usay yu kin kip yu tɔk trɔmpɛt, paip, tɛliskɔp, ɛn ɔda tin dɛn we yu kin yuz na di si. We Kpɛntin Slit insɛf tinap in mast-ed insay dis kraw-nɛst we i gɛt, i tɛl wi se i bin gɛt rayf wit am ɔltɛm (we dɛn kin fiks bak na di rɛk), togɛda wit paoda flask ɛn shot, fɔ di rizin fɔ pop di stray narwɛl dɛn, ɔ vagrant si yunikɔn dɛn we de infest dɛn wata dɛn de; bikɔs yu nɔ go ebul fɔ shot dɛn fayn fayn wan frɔm di dɛk bikɔs di wata de tinap tranga wan, bɔt fɔ shot dɛn dɔŋ na sɔntin we rili difrɛn. Naw, i klia se na wok we Kpɛtɛn Slit bin de du fɔ lɛk fɔ tɔk bɔt, lɛk aw i de du, ɔl di smɔl smɔl tin dɛn we de mek i izi fɔ in krawd-nɛst; bɔt pan ɔl we i so big pan bɔku pan dɛn tin ya, ɛn pan ɔl we i de trit wi wit wan rili sayɛns akɔdin to in ɛkspiriɛns dɛn na dis kraw-nɛst, wit wan smɔl kɔmpas we i kip de fɔ di rizin fɔ kɔba di mistek dɛn we de kɔmɔt frɔm wetin dɛn kɔl di “lɔkal atrakshɔn” fɔ ɔl di binakl magnet dɛn; wan mistek we dɛn kin se na di ɔrizɔntal say we di ayɛn bin de na di ship in plank dɛn, ɛn insay di Gleshia in kes, sɔntɛm, bikɔs bɔku bɔku blaksmit dɛn we dɔn brok brok bin de bitwin in kru dɛn; A se, se pan ɔl we di Kpɛntin rili gɛt sɛns ɛn sayɛnsman ya, yet, fɔ ɔl in lanin “binnacle deviations,” “azimuth compass observations,” ɛn “approximate errors,” i no gud gud wan, Kpɛntin Slit, se i nɔ bin so imerged insay dɛn dip magnɛtik meditashɔn dɛn de, as fɔ fel fɔ atrak wan wan tɛm to da wɛl replenished smɔl kes-bɔtul, so fayn fayn wan we i tuck insay wan say na in krawd in nɛst, we i izi fɔ rich na in an. Dɔn i de rɔn dɛn bifo am nia di kapstan, wit dɛn harpun dɛn na dɛn an, we in tri kɔmpin dɛn tinap na in sayd wit dɛn lans, ɛn di ɔda pipul dɛn we de na di ship mek wan sɛklɔ rawnd di grup; i tinap fɔ smɔl tɛm de luk fɔ ɔl di man dɛn we de na in kru. Bɔt dɛn wayl yay dɛn de mit in yon, lɛk aw di prɛri wulf dɛn yay dɛn we gɛt blɔd mit dɛn lida in yay, bifo i rɔsh go na dɛn ed na di trayla fɔ di bayson; bɔt, alas! na fɔ fɔdɔm insay di ayd trap we di Indian gɛt.

“Drink ɛn pas!” i kray, ɛn gi di ebi ebi chaj flagon to di siman we de nia am. "Di kru nɔmɔ naw de drink. Raun wit am, rawnd! Shɔt drɔt—lɔng swɛla, man; 'tis hot as Setan in fut. So, so; i de go rawnd fayn fayn wan. I spiralize in ye; fɔk aut na di snek-snap yay. Wɛl du; ɔlmost dreyn. Dat we i go, dis we i kam. Hand am—here’s a hollow! Men, ye seem di ia so brimming layf dɔn gulp ɛn dɔn go.

"Una atɛnd naw, mi brayt pipul dɛm. A dɔn gɛda una ɔlsay rawnd dis kapstan; ɛn una mates, flank mi wit una lans dɛm; ɛn una harpooneers, tinap de wit una ayɛn dɛm; ɛn una, stout mariners, ring mi insay, so dat a go in sɔm kayn we fɔ rivaiv wan nobul kɔstɔm fɔ mi fishaman papa dɛm bifo mi. O man dɛm, una go si yet se—Ha! bɔy, kam bak? bad pennies come not sooner. Hand it mi.

"Advans, una mates! Krɔs una lans ful bifo mi. Wɛl du! Mek a tɔch di aks." So we i se, wit in an we i dɔn ɛkstɛnd, i ol di tri lɛvul, we de rayt lans dɛn na dɛn sɛnt we dɛn krɔs; we dɛn bin de du dat, i bin de twitch dɛn wantɛm wantɛm ɛn wit fred; da tɛm de, i de luk gud gud wan frɔm Starbuck to Stubb; frɔm Stubb to Flask. I bin tan lɛk se, bay sɔm nemlɛs, intɛriɔ volishɔn, i go fain fɔ dɔn shɔk insay dɛn di sem faya imɔshɔn we dɔn gɛda insay di Leyden jɔg fɔ in yon magnɛtik layf. Di tri mates kwail bifo in strɔng, sustained, ɛn mistik aspek. Stubb ɛn Flask bin luk saydway frɔm am; di ɔnɛs yay fɔ Starbuck bin fɔdɔm dɔŋ.

“Na fɔ natin!” Eab bin kray; “bɔt, sɔntɛm, ‘tis wɛl. Bikɔs una tri bɔt wan tɛm tek di ful-fɔs shɔk, dɔn mi yon ilɛktrik tin, we bin dɔn sɔntɛm dɔn frɔm aut mi. Sɔntɛm, bak, i bin fɔ dɔn drɔp una day. Sɔntɛm una nɔ nid am. Daun lans! Ɛn naw, una mates, a dɔn pik una tri kɔba dɛn to mi tri pegan fambul dɛn de—yon tri mɔs ɔnaful jentlman dɛn ɛn nobul dɛm, mi valiant harpooneers Disdain the task? Wetin , we di big Pop de was in tiara fɔ ewer?

We dɛn bin de obe di ɔda we dɛn nɔ tɔk natin, di tri hapun dɛn bin tinap naw wit di ayɛn pat we dɔn kɔmɔt na dɛn harpun, we lɔng lɛk tri fit, we dɛn ol, barb ɔp, bifo am.

“No stab mi wit dat keen steel! Cant dem; cant dem ova! yu no sabi di goblet end? Turn up di socket! So, so; now, you cup-bears, advance. Di ayɛn! tek dɛn; ol dɛn we a de ful-ɔp!” Wantɛm wantɛm, i bin de go frɔm wan ɔfisa to di ɔda wan smɔl smɔl, ɛn i bin ful-ɔp di hapun sɔkɛt dɛn wit di faya wata we de kɔmɔt na di piuta.

“Naw, tri to tri, una tinap. Kɔmɛnt di kil-kil klip dɛn! Gi dɛn, una we dɛn dɔn mek pati naw to dis lig we nɔ go ebul fɔ sɔlv. Ha! Starbuck! bɔt di deed dɔn! Yon ratifying san naw de wet fɔ sidɔm pan am. Drink, una harpooneers! drink ɛn swɛ, una man dɛn we de mek di dayful whaleboat’s bow—Death to Moby Dik!Gɔd de fɛn wi ɔl, if wi nɔ de fɛn Mobi Dik te i day!” Dɛn bin es di lɔng stɛl kɔpɛl dɛn we gɛt barb; ɛn fɔ kray ɛn kɔndɛm dɛn agens di wayt wɛl, dɛn bin de kray di spirit dɛn wan tɛm wit wan his. Starbuck pal, ɛn tɔn, ɛn shek. Wan tɛm bak, ɛn las las, di piɔta we dɛn dɔn ful-ɔp bak bin go rawnd bitwin di kru we bin de fred; we i de wev in fri an to dɛn, dɛn ɔl skata; ɛn Eab bin ritaia insay in os.
