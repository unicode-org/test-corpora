CHAPTER 107. Di Kapɛnta.

Sidɔm yusɛf sultanically bitwin di mun dɛn na Satɔn, ɛn tek ay abstrakt man wan; ɛn i tan lɛk se i wɔndaful, i big, ɛn i de sɔfa. Bɔt frɔm di sem pɔynt, tek mɔtalman in mas, ɛn fɔ di bɔku pat, dɛn tan lɛk wan grup we nɔ nid fɔ dupliket, ɔl tu di sem tɛm ɛn frɔm dɛn gret gret granpa dɛn. Bɔt pan ɔl we i bin ɔmbul pas ɔlman, ɛn i bin fa frɔm fɔ gi ɛgzampul bɔt di ay, uman abstrakshɔn; di Pequod in kapɛnta nɔto bin dupliket; so, naw i de kam insɛf na dis stej.

Lɛk ɔl di ship kapɛnta dɛn we de go na si, ɛn mɔ di wan dɛn we de pan wɛl ship dɛn, i bin, to sɔm ɔf-han, prɛktikal mak, i bin gɛt ɛkspiriɛns pan bɔku wok dɛn ɛn kɔl kolataral to in yon; di kapɛnta in pursuit bi di ol ɛn ɔtbranch trɔnk fɔ ɔl dɛn bɔku bɔku hankraft dɛn we mɔ ɔ lɛs gɛt fɔ du wit wud as ɔgzilari matirial. Bɔt, apat frɔm di aplikeshɔn to am fɔ di jenɛrik tɔk we de ɔp, dis kapɛnta na di Pɛkud bin singul efyushɔn pan dɛn tawzin mɛkanikal imejensi dɛn we nɔ gɛt nem we bin de kɔntinyu fɔ kam bak na big ship, we i bin de travul fɔ tri ɔ 4 ia, na si dɛn we nɔ sivilizayt ɛn we de fa fawe. Fɔ mek wi nɔ tɔk bɔt aw i rɛdi fɔ du ɔdinari wok dɛn:—i de mek stov bot dɛn, sprung spays, fɔ mek di shep fɔ di ɔs dɛn we gɛt klɔm blad, fɔ put bul in yay dɛn na di dɛk, ɔ fɔ put nyu tik-nɛl dɛn na di sayd plank dɛn, ɛn ɔda difrɛn tin dɛn we gɛt fɔ du wit in spɛshal biznɛs mɔ dairekt wan; pan ɔl dat, i bin sabi fɔ du ɔl kayn tin dɛn we nɔ gri wit dɛnsɛf, we yusful ɛn we i nɔ bin want fɔ du.

Di wan big stej usay i bin akt ɔl in difrɛn pat dɛn so manifold, na in vays-bɛnch; wan lɔng rud ponderous tebul we gɛt bɔku bad bad tin dɛn, we gɛt difrɛn saiz, ɛn we dɛn mek wit ayɛn ɛn wud. Ɔltɛm pas we wɛl dɛn bin de nia, dɛn bin de lash dis bɛnch fayn fayn wan athwartships agens di riya fɔ di Try-works.

Dɛn kin si se wan belaying pin tu big fɔ mek i izi fɔ put insay in ol: di kapɛnta kin klap am insay wan pan in bad bad tin dɛn we i kin rɛdi ɔltɛm, ɛn i kin fayl am smɔl wantɛm wantɛm. Wan land-bɔd we dɔn lɔs we gɛt strenj flay, de kɔmɔt na di bot, ɛn dɛn mek am kapchɔ: wit klin shev stik dɛn we dɛn mek wit rayt-wɛl bon, ɛn krɔs-bim dɛn we dɛn mek wit sperm wɛl ayvri, di kapɛnta mek wan kech we tan lɛk pagoda fɔ am. Wan pɔsin we de rayd ɔs kin swɛt in an: di kapɛnta kin mek wan loshan we kin mek i fil fayn. Stub bin rili want fɔ mek dɛn peint vermilion sta dɛn pan di bled fɔ in ɛni ɔs; we i de skru ɛni ɔs insay in big vays wud, di kapɛnta de saplae di kɔnstɛlɛshɔn simɛtrik wan. Wan man we de wok na di bot kin tek wan fansi fɔ wɛr shak-bon ia-ring: di kapɛnta kin drɔ in yes. Wan ɔda wan gɛt di tut we de at: di kapɛnta de pul pinsa, ɛn klap wan an pan in bɛnch de tɛl am fɔ sidɔm de; bɔt di po pɔsin we nɔ ebul fɔ manej de wins ɔnda di ɔpreshɔn we dɛn nɔ dɔn dɔn; we i de rɔn rawnd di handel fɔ in wud vays, di kapɛnta sayn am fɔ klap in jaw insay dat, if i go mek i drɔ di tut.

So, dis kapɛnta bin rɛdi ɔltɛm, ɛn i nɔ bin bisin bɔt am ɛn i nɔ bin gɛt ɛni rɛspɛkt pan ɔl. Tit i bin akɔntayn bit dɛn pan ayvri; ed dɛn we i bin si se na tɔp-blɔk dɛn; man dɛnsɛf i bin layt fɔ ol fɔ kapstan. Bɔt pan ɔl we naw i de pan so big fild so difrɛn we dɛn dɔn akɔmplit ɛn wit so layfliness of expertness in am, tu; ɔl dis go tan lɛk se i de agyu bɔt sɔm nɔ kɔmɔn vivacity of intelligence. Bɔt nɔto jɔs so. Bikɔs natin nɔ bin dis man we bin rili wɔndaful, pas fɔ wan patikyula tin we nɔ gɛt pɔsin in yon we lɛk se i nɔ gɛt wan bɔt; impersonal, a se; bikɔs i so shed ɔf insay di tin dɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnd we de rawnd am, dat i bin tan lɛk wan wit di jenɛral stoliditi we pɔsin kin si insay di wan ol wɔl we wi kin si; we pan ɔl we i nɔ de stɔp fɔ du sɔntin we dɛn nɔ ebul fɔ kɔnt, stil de ol in pis sote go, ɛn nɔ de pe atɛnshɔn to yu, pan ɔl we yu de dig fawndeshɔn fɔ katidral dɛn. Yet dis haf-ɔribul stoliditi bin de insay am, we involv, tu, as i bin tan lɛk, wan ɔl-ramifying at-lɛsnɛs;—yet i bin oddly dash sɔm tɛm, wit wan ol, krach-layk, antediluvian, wheezing humorousness, nɔt unstreaked naw ɛn den wit wan patikyula grizzled wittiness; lɛk di wan dɛn we go dɔn sav fɔ pas di tɛm we dɛn de wach midulnɛt na di fɔs ples we gɛt biad fɔ Noa in ak. Yu tink se dis ol kapɛnta bin dɔn de waka waka fɔ ɔl in layf, we in bɔku rɔl, go ɛn kam, nɔto jɔs nɔ bin dɔn gɛda ɛni mɔs; bɔt wetin pas dat, i bin dɔn rɔb ɛni smɔl smɔl tin dɛn we bin de na do we go dɔn gɛt fɔ du wit am fɔs? I bin bi wan stript abstrakt; wan intagral we nɔ gɛt frakshɔn; nɔ kɔmprɔmis as pikin we dɛn jɔs bɔn; liv we dɛn nɔ bin dɔn plan fɔ tɔk bɔt dis wɔl ɔ di nɛks wɔl. Yu go dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ se, dis strenj we i nɔ bin ebul fɔ kɔmprɔmis, i bin gɛt fɔ du wit wan kayn we aw i nɔ bin gɛt sɛns; bikɔs insay in bɔku bɔku wok dɛn, i nɔ bin tan lɛk se i de wok so bikɔs ɔf rizin ɔ bay instinkt, ɔ jɔs bikɔs dɛn dɔn tich am to am, ɔ bay ɛnitin we dɛn miks ɔl dɛn tin ya, ivin ɔ i nɔ ivin; bɔt na jɔs bay wan kayn dɛf ɛn mumu, spontan rial prɔses. I bin klin manipuleshɔn; in bren, if i bin dɔn ɛva gɛt wan, i fɔ dɔn ɔz kwik kwik wan insay di mɔsul dɛn na in finga dɛn. I bin tan lɛk wan pan dɛn wan dɛn we nɔ gɛt sɛns bɔt stil rili yusful, multum in parvo , Sheffield contrivances, we i tek am se di ɛkstirian—pan ɔl we i swel smɔl—fɔ wan kɔmɔn pɔkit naif; bɔt i nɔ jɔs gɛt bled dɛn we gɛt difrɛn saiz dɛn, bɔt i gɛt bak skru-drayv, kɔk-skru, twiza, awl, pen, rula, nel-fayla, kɔwntasink. So, if in supia dɛn want fɔ yuz di kapɛnta fɔ skru-drayv, ɔl wetin dɛn fɔ du na fɔ opin da pat de pan am, ɛn di skru fast: ɔ if fɔ twiza, tek am ɔp bay in leg, ɛn na de dɛn de.

Bɔt stil, as wi bin dɔn tɔk bifo tɛm, dis kapɛnta we gɛt ɔl di tul dɛn, we opin ɛn shɔt, nɔto bin jɔs mashin we ɔtomɛtik de mek. If i nɔ bin gɛt wan kɔmɔn sol insay am, i bin gɛt sɔntin we nɔ klia ɛn we sɔm kayn we de du in wok atɔl wan. Wetin dat bin bi, ilɛksɛf na ɛsin we dɛn mek wit kwik silva, ɔ sɔm drop dɛn we dɛn mek wit hatshɔn, nɔbɔdi nɔ go ebul fɔ tɔk bɔt am. Bɔt na de i bin de; ɛn na de i bin dɔn de fɔ lɛk siksti ia ɔ mɔ. Ɛn dis na bin, dis sem layf-prinsipul we nɔ gɛt ɛnitin fɔ du wit am, we gɛt kɔni kɔni we insay am; dis na bin, we bin de kip am wan big pat pan di tɛm fɔ tɔk in wan; bɔt jɔs lɛk wan wil we nɔ gɛt rizin, we de tɔk in wan bak wit ɔmbul; ɔ fɔ se, in bɔdi na bin sɛntry-bɔks ɛn dis soliloquizer bin de gayd de, ɛn i bin de tɔk ɔltɛm fɔ mek i wek.





CHAPTER 108. Eab ɛn di Kapɛnta.

Di Dɛk—Fɔs Nayt Wach.

( Kapɛnta tinap bifo in vays-bɛnch, ɛn bay di layt we tu lantan dɛn de gi bizi de fayl di ayvri joist fɔ di leg, we na di joist we dɛn tay fayn fayn wan na di vays. Slab dɛn we gɛt ayvri, lɛda strap, pad, skru, ɛn difrɛn tul dɛn we gɛt ɔl kayn tin de ledɔm rawnd di bɛnch. Fɔ go bifo, dɛn si di rɛd flawa na di fɔj, usay di blaksmit de wok. ) )

Drat di fayl, ɛn drat di bon! Dat na had we fɔ saf, ɛn dat na saf we fɔ at. So wi go, we de fayl ol jaw ɛn shinbon. Lɛ wi tray ɔda wan. Aye, naw, dis de wok beta ( sneezes ). Halloa, dis bon dɔst na ( sneezes )—wetin mek i ( sneezes )—yes na ( sneezes )—bles mi sol, i nɔ go mek a tɔk! Dis na wetin wan ol pɔsin kin gɛt naw fɔ wok na day lɔmba. Saw wan layf tik, ɛn yu nɔ gɛt dis dɔst; kɔt layf bon, ɛn yu nɔ kin gɛt am ( sniz ). Kam, kam, yu ol Smut, de, bia wan an, ɛn lɛ wi gɛt da ferule ɛn bɔkul-skru de; A go redi fɔ dɛn naw. Laki naw ( sneezes ) no knee-joint de fo mek; dat kin mek pɔsin kɔnfyus smɔl; bɔt na jɔs shinbon—wetin mek i izi lɛk fɔ mek hop-pol; na mi nɔmɔ fɔ lɛk fɔ put gud finish pan. Taym, tɛm; if a bot onli geht di taim, a kin ton am aut lek neat leg naw lek eva ( sneezes ) skrap to wan ledi na wan parlor. Dɛn bɔkskin leg ɛn kaw pikin dɛn we a dɔn si na shɔp winda dɛn nɔ go kɔmpia atɔl. Dɛn kin sok wata, dɛn kin sok; ɛn fɔ tru, yu kin gɛt rɔmatik, ɛn dɛn kin gɛt fɔ dɔktɔ ( sniz ) wit was ɛn loshan, jɔs lɛk layf leg. De; bifo a si am ɔf, naw, a fɔ kɔl in ol Mogulship, ɛn si if di lɔng go fayn; tu shɔt, if ɛnitin, a gɛs. Ha! dat na di il; wi de na laki; na ya i kam, ɔ na ɔda pɔsin, dat na sɔntin we shɔ.

Dɛn mɛzhɔ am fɔ wan leg! gud. Wɛl, nɔto di fɔs tɛm dis. Bɔt am! De; kip yu finga pan am. Dis na kɔjent vays we yu gɛt ya, kapɛnta; mek a fil in grip wan tɛm. So, so, so; i kin pinch sɔm.

Oh, sa, i go brok bon—wam, tek tɛm!

Nɔ fred; A lɛk fɔ grip gud gud wan; A lɛk fɔ fil sɔntin na dis slip wɔl we kin ol, man. Wetin Prometheus de tɔk bɔt de?—di blaksmit, a min—wetin i de tɔk bɔt?

I go mɔs bi se i de fɔj di bɔkul-skru, sa, naw.

Rayt. Na patnaship; i de gi di mɔsul pat. I mek wan faya faya rɛd faya de!

Aye, sa, yu se; i fɔ gɛt di wayt ɔt fɔ dis kayn fayn wok.

Um-m. So i fɔ du dat. A de si am naw as tin we min pas ɔl, se da ol Grik, Prɔmitiɔs, we mek man, dɛn se, fɔ dɔn bi blaksmit, ɛn mek dɛn gɛt layf wit faya; bikɔs wetin dɛn mek wit faya fɔ bi faya in yon fayn fayn wan; ɛn so ɛl na prɔbabl. Aw di sɔt kin flay! Dis go mɔs bi di ɔda pat we di Grik mek di Afrikan dɛn. Kapɛnta, we i dɔn wit da bɔkul de, tɛl am fɔ mek wan pe fɔ stɛl sɔldɔm; na wan pedlar de aboard wit wan krosh pak.

Sa?

Ol; we Prometheus de boht am, a go oda wan komplit man afta wan desirable patna. Imprimis, we ay fifti fit insay in sɔks; dɔn, dɛn mek di chɛst we dɛn mek lɛk di Temz Tɛnal; den, leg wit rut to 'em, fɔ de na wan ples; dɔn, an dɛn tri fit tru di an; nɔ gɛt at atɔl, bras fɔɛd, ɛn lɛk wan kwata eka fayn fayn bren; ɛn mek a si—a go ɔda yay fɔ si na do? Nɔ, bɔt put skay-layt pan in ed fɔ mek i shayn insay. Na de, tek di ɔda, ɛn go.

Naw, wetin i de tɔk bɔt, ɛn udat i de tɔk to, a fɔ lɛk fɔ no? A go kɔntinyu fɔ tinap ya? ( na sayd ).

'Tis bɔt indipɛndɛnt akitɛkɛt fɔ mek wan blaynd dome; na wan ya. Nɔ, nɔ, nɔ; A go mɔs gɛt lantan.

Ho, ho! Na dat, hey? Na tu ya, sa; wan go sav mi turn.

Kapɛnta? wetin mek dat na—bɔt nɔ;—wan rili tidi, ɛn, a kin se, wan kayn biznɛs we rili lɛk jɛnɛral we yu de insay ya, kapɛnta;—ɔ yu go lɛk fɔ wok na kle?

KAPƐNTA ( i bigin fɔ du in wok bak ).

Wɛl, wɛl, wɛl! Stubb sabi am pas ɔl, ɛn Stubb kin se ɔltɛm i na queer; nɔ tɔk natin pas se wan inof smɔl wɔd queer; i na queer, na so Stubb se; i na queer—kwik, queer; ɛn kɔntinyu fɔ it am insay Mista Stabɔk ɔltɛm—kwik—sa—kwik, kwɛr, rili kwɛr. Ɛn na in leg ya! Yɛs, naw we a tink bɔt am, na in bedfɛlɔ de ya! gɛt stik we dɛn mek wit wɛl in jaw-bon fɔ uman! Ɛn dis na in leg; i go tinap pan dis. Wetin na bin dat naw bɔt wan leg we tinap na tri ples, ɛn ɔl di tri ples dɛn we tinap na wan ɛl—aw dat bin bi? O! A nɔ wɔnda i bin de luk mi so swɛ! A na wan kayn pɔsin we kin tink bɔt strenj sɔm tɛm, dɛn kin se; bɔt dat na jɔs haphazard-layk. Dɔn, shɔt, smɔl ol bɔdi lɛk mi, nɔ fɔ ɛva tek am fɔ waka go na dip wata wit lɔng lɔng kapten dɛn we dɛn bil wit ɛrɔn; di wata chuck yu anda di chin priti kwik, en big kray de fo layf-bot. Ɛn na di ɛrɔn in leg ya! lɔng ɛn slim, shɔ se i go du! Naw, fɔ bɔku pipul dɛn wan pe leg de las fɔ dɛn layf, ɛn dat fɔ bi bikɔs dɛn de yuz dɛn wit sɔri-at, lɛk aw wan ol uman we gɛt sɔri-at de yuz in roly-poly ol kɔch-ɔs dɛn. Bɔt Eab; oh i na had drayva. Luk, drɛb wan leg te i day, ɛn spavin di ɔda wan fɔ layf, ɛn naw i dɔn west bon leg dɛn bay di kɔd. Halloa, de, yu Smut! bia wan an de wit dɛn skru dɛn de, ɛn lɛ wi dɔn am bifo di pɔsin we gɛt layf bak kam a-kɔl wit in ɔn fɔ ɔl di leg dɛn, tru ɔ lay, as briba-man dɛn de go rawnd de gɛda ol bia barɛl dɛn, fɔ ful-ɔp dɛn bak. CHAPTER 110. Kwik insay In Kɔfin.

We dɛn bin de luk fɔ am, dɛn kam fɔ no se di bɔtul dɛn we dɛn las slap insay di ol, bin fayn fayn wan, ɛn di lik fɔ dɔn de fa fawe. CHAPTER 111. Di Pasifik.

We wi bin de glid nia di Bashee ayland dɛn wi bin kɔmɔt las las pan di big Sawt Si; if nɔto fɔ ɔda tin dɛn, a bin fɔ dɔn grit mi dia Pasifik wit tɛnki we a nɔ ebul fɔ kɔnt, bikɔs naw dɛn dɔn ansa di lɔng beg we a bin de beg we a bin yɔŋ; dat serene ocean bin rol go ist frɔm mi wan tawzin lig blu.

Na, pɔsin nɔ no us swit mistɛri bɔt dis si, we in saful saful saful stirring dɛn tan lɛk se i de tɔk bɔt sɔm ayd sol ɔnda; lɛk dɛn fabled undulations of di Ephesian sod oba di bɛri Ivanjelis Sɛnt Jɔn. Di sem wef dɛn de was di mol dɛn na di nyu tɔŋ dɛn we dɛn bil na Kalifonia, bɔt yestede dɛn plant dɛn bay di rays we man dɛn jɔs dɔn plant, ɛn lave di faded bɔt stil fayn fayn skit dɛn na Eshia land dɛn, we ol pas Ebraam; we ɔl bitwin di flot milky-ways na kɔral ayland dɛm, ɛn di low-lay, endless, unknown Archipelagoes, ɛn Japans we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ pas. CHAPTER 112. Di Blaksmit.

Availing himself of the mild, sommer-cool weather that now reigned in these latitudes, ɛn fɔ pripia fɔ di peculiarly active pursuits shortly to be anticipated, Perth, di begrimed, blistered old blaksmit, nɔ bin dɔn pul in pɔtabl fɔj to di hold bak, afta i dɔn dɔn in kɔntribyushɔn wok fɔ Eab in leg, bɔt i stil kip am na di dɛk, fast lash to ringbolt bay di fɔrɛst; bikɔs naw di wan dɛn we de oba di ed, ɛn di wan dɛn we de mek harpun, ɛn di wan dɛn we de bɔw, kin kɔl dɛn ɔltɛm fɔ mek dɛn du sɔm smɔl wok fɔ dɛn; chenj, ɔ ripɛnt, ɔ nyu shep dɛn difrɛn wɛpɔn dɛn ɛn bot fɔnicha. Naw, fɔ rizin dɛn we gɛt sɛns, we gɛt sɛns pas ɔl, ɛn we gɛt mɔni, di blaksmit in shɔp bin de na di bɛsin usay i bin de, bɔt i bin gɛt wan ɔda say fɔ go insay; so dat ɔltɛm di yɔŋ ɛn lɔv wɛlbɔdi wɛf bin de lisin wit ɛni nervɔs we nɔ gladi, bɔt wit strɔng gladi at, to di strɔng ring we in yɔŋ man we ol man in hama de ring; we in rivabreshɔn, we dɛn bin de muf bay we i de pas na di flɔ ɛn di wɔl, bin kam to am, nɔto we nɔ swit, na in nɔsri; ɛn so, fɔ mek Leba in ayɛn lullaby strɔng, dɛn bin de rɔk di blaksmit in bebi dɛn fɔ slip.

Oh, woe on woe! Oh, Day, wetin mek yu nɔ kin ebul fɔ bi di rayt tɛm sɔm tɛm? If yu bin dɔn tek dis ol blaksmit to yusɛf bifo in ful ruin kam pan am, den di yɔŋ uman we in man dɔn day bin gɛt wan fayn fayn sɔri-at, ɛn in ɔfɛn dɛn bin gɛt wan tru tru venerable, legendary sire fɔ drim bɔt insay dɛn afta ia; ɛn dɛn ɔl na kɔmpɛtɛnshɔn fɔ kil kia. Bɔt Day bin pul sɔm big brɔda we gɛt gud kwaliti dɛn, we pan in wispal ɛvride wok nɔmɔ bin de hang di rispɔnsibiliti fɔ sɔm ɔda famili, ɛn lɛf di ol man we wɔs pas yuslɛs tinap, te di hideous rot of life fɔ mek am izi fɔ avɛst.

Wetin mek yu fɔ tɛl di wan ol tin? Di blo dɛn we di bɛsin hama bin de blo ɛvride bin de gro mɔ ɛn mɔ bitwin; ɛn ɛni blo ɛvride bin de gro smɔl pas di las wan; di wɛf bin sidɔm frɔz na di winda, wit yay we nɔ de kray, ɛn i bin de shayn shayn pan in pikin dɛn fes we de kray; di bɛlɔ dɛn bin fɔdɔm; di fɔj bin chok wit sinda; dɛn bin dɔn sɛl di os; di mama dayv dɔŋ insay di lɔng gras we de na di chɔch yad; in pikin dɛn bin fala am de tu tɛm; ɛn di ol man we nɔ gɛt os, we nɔ gɛt famili, bin de rɔn kɔmɔt na wan vagabond insay krep; in ɛni sɔri-at we nɔ gɛt rɛspɛkt; in grey ed na skɔn to flaks kɔl!

I tan lɛk se day na di wangren tin we pɔsin kin want fɔ du fɔ mek pɔsin gɛt wok lɛk dis; bɔt Day na jɔs wan lanch insay di rijyɔn fɔ di strenj Untried; na jɔs di fɔs salut to di pɔsibul dɛn fɔ di big big Rimot, di Wail, di Wata, di Unshored; so, to di yay dɛn we dɛn kayn man dɛn de we stil want fɔ day, we stil lɛf sɔm intɛriɔ kɔmpɔnkshɔn dɛn insay dɛn agens fɔ kil dɛnsɛf, di si we de kɔntribyut ɛn we de tek ɔltin, de mek i want fɔ skata in wan ol ples we nɔ pɔsibul fɔ imajin, we de tek fred, ɛn wɔndaful, nyu layf advantej dɛn; ɛn frɔm di at fɔ di Pasifik dɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnd, di tawzin mɛrmɛyd dɛn de siŋ to dɛn—“Kam ya, yu at brok; na ya ɔda layf de we nɔ gɛt di gilti fɔ day we de bitwin dɛn; na ya wɔndaful tin dɛn we pas mɔtalman, we nɔ de day fɔ dɛn. Kam ya! bɛr yusɛf insay layf we, to yu naw ikwal et ɛn et, landed wɔl, na mɔ fɔgɛt pas day. Kam ya! yu grev ston sɛf, insay di chɔch yad, ɛn kam ya, te wi mared yu!”

We i yɛri dɛn vɔys ya, Is ɛn Wɛst, bay we di san de kɔmɔt ali, ɛn bay we di ivintɛm fɔdɔm, di blaksmit in sol ansa se, Aye, a kam! En so Perth bin go a-whaling.





CHAPTER 113. Di Fɔj.

Wit mat biad, ɛn we dɛn kɔba am wit wan aprɔn we gɛt shak skin we gɛt brist, lɛk midul de, Pɛt bin tinap bitwin in fɔj ɛn anvil, di las wan bin put am pan wan ayɛn wud tik, wit wan an we ol wan paik-ed insay di kol, ɛn wit di ɔda wan na in fɔj in lɔng, we Kpɛntin Eab kam, i ol wan smɔl lɛda bag we tan lɛk rɔsti na in an. We Eab bin stil de fa smɔl frɔm di fɔj, Eab we bin de fil bad, bin stɔp smɔl; te las las, Pɛt, we i pul in ayɛn kɔmɔt na di faya, bigin fɔ hama am pan di anvil—di rɛd mas bin de sɛn di spak dɛn insay tik tik flay dɛn we de flay, sɔm pan dɛn bin de flay nia Eab.

“Na yu Mama Keri in kɔk dɛn ya, Pɛt? dɛn de flay ɔltɛm na yu wek; bɔd dɛn we de sho gud tin, bak, bɔt nɔto to ɔlman;—luk ya, dɛn de bɔn; bɔt yu—yu de liv wit dɛn witout a scorch.”

Pɛt bin rɛst fɔ smɔl tɛm pan in hama se: “Bikɔs a dɔn bɔn ɔlsay, Kpɛntin Eab; “A dɔn pas fɔ bɔn, nɔto izi wan yu nɔ go ebul fɔ bɔn wan skata.”

"Wɛl, wɛl; nɔ mɔ. Yu shrunk vɔys de sawnd tu kol, sanely woeful to mi. In no Paradays misɛf, a nɔ de peshɛnt fɔ ɔl misɛf na ɔda pipul dɛn we nɔ de mek pɔsin kray. Yu fɔ go mad, blaksmit; se, wetin mek yu nɔ go mad? Aw yu go ebul fɔ bia we yu nɔ mad? Di ɛvin stil et yu, we yu ebul'st nɔ go kray?—Wetin yu bin de mek de?”

“Wɛldin wan ol paik-hɛd, sa, sim ɛn dent bin de insay.”

“Ɛn yu kin mek ɔltin smol bak, blaksmit, afta yu dɔn yuz am tranga wan lɛk aw i bin dɔn yuz am?”

“A tink se na so, sa.”

“Ɛn a tink se yu go ebul fɔ smol klos to ɛni sim ɛn dent, nɔ ɛva tink bɔt aw di mɛtal at, blaksmit?”

“Aye, sa, a tink se a kin;ɔl simpul ɛn dent bɔt wan.”

Eab bin ala wit zil fɔ go bifo, ɛn ledɔm wit in tu an dɛn na Pɛt in sholda dɛn se: “Una luk ya.” “luk yu ya— ya —yu kin smol wan sim lɛk dis, blaksmit,” i de swip wan an akɔdin to in rib brɔw; “if yu bin ebul, blaksmit, gladi fɔ lɛ a le mi ed pan yu anvil, ɛn fil yu hama we ebi pas ɔl bitwin mi yay. Ansa! Yu go ebul fɔ mek dis sim smol?”

“O! na di wan dat, sa! Said I nɔto ɔl sim ɛn dent bɔt wan?”

“Aye, blaksmit, na di wan; ye, man, i nɔ smoothable; bikɔs pan ɔl we yu jɔs si am ya na mi bɔdi, i dɔn wok dɔŋ insay di bon na mi skel— dat na ɔl wrinkles! Bɔt, away wit pikin in ple; no mɔ gaffs ɛn pikes tide. Luk yu ya!” jingling di lɛda bag, lɛk se i ful-ɔp wit gold kɔyn dɛn. "Mi sɛf want mek dɛn mek wan hapun; wan we wan tawzin yok we gɛt dɛbul dɛn nɔ bin ebul fɔ sheb, Pɛt; sɔntin we go stik insay wɛl lɛk in yon fin-bɔn. Di tin de de," i de trowe di paus pan di anvil. “Luk yu, blaksmit, dis na di nel-stɔb dɛn we dɛn gɛda na di stɛl sus dɛn we ɔs dɛn we de rɔn kin gɛt.”

“Ɔs-shu stɔb, sa? Wetin mek, Kpɛntin Eab, yu gɛt ya, so, di bɛst ɛn stɛp tin we wi blaksmit dɛn dɔn ɛva wok.”

"A no am, ol man; dɛn stɔb ya go wɛldɔm togɛda lɛk glu frɔm di bon dɛn we dɔn mɛlt fɔ kilman. Kwik! fɔj mi di harpun. Ɛn fɔj mi fɔs, twɛlv stik fɔ in shank; dɔn briz, ɛn twist, ɛn hama dɛn twɛlv ya togɛda lɛk di yan ɛn strɛch dɛn na to-layn. Kwik! A go blo di faya."

We dɛn dɔn mek di twɛlv stik dɛn, Eab bin tray dɛn wan bay wan bay we i bin de spiral dɛn, wit in yon an, rawnd wan lɔng ɛn ebi ayɛn bolt. “Na fɔlt!” fɔ rijek di las wan. “Wɔk dat bak, Pɛt.”

Dis dɔn, Pɛt bin de go bigin fɔ wɛldɔm di twɛlv to wan, we Eab stɔp in an, ɛn se i go wɛld in yon ayɛn. As, den, wit rɛgyula, gasping hems, i hama pan di anvil, Pɛt pas to am di glowing rod dɛm, wan afta di ɔda, ɛn di had prɛs fɔj shot ɔp in intens stret flawa, di Parsee pas kwayɛt wan, ɛn butu oba in ed to di faya, i bin tan lɛk se i de kɔl sɔm swɛ ɔ sɔm blɛsin pan di wok. Bɔt, as Eab luk ɔp, i slip na sayd.

“Wetin da bɔnch lucifers de dodj bɔt de fɔ?” Stubb bin de muf, i de luk frɔm di fɔkas. “Dat Parsee de smɛl faya lɛk fiuzi;ɛn i de smɛl am insɛf, lɛk ɔt mɔsket in paoda-pan.”

Las las di shank, insay wan kɔmplit stik, bin gɛt in las ɔt; ɛn as Pɛt, fɔ mek i vɛks, i put am ɔl di his insay di bɔtul we gɛt wata we bin de nia de, di stim we bin de bɔn bin shot ɔp to Eab in fes we bin bɛn.

“Yu go brand mi, Pɛt?” wins fɔ smɔl tɛm wit di pen; “a dɔn de bɔt fɔ fɔj mi yon branding-ayɔn, den?”

“Pre Gɔd, nɔto dat; yet a de fred sɔntin, Kpɛntin Eab. Dis harpun nɔto fɔ di Wait Wɛl?”

“Fɔ di wayt dɛbul! Bɔt naw fɔ di barb dɛn; yu fɔ mek dɛn yusɛf, man. Na mi reza dɛn de ya—di bɛst we dɛn mek wit stɛl; na ya, ɛn mek di barb dɛn shap lɛk di nidul-slit na di Aysi Si.”

Fɔ smɔl tɛm, di ol blaksmit bin de yay di reza dɛn lɛk se i go fain nɔ yuz dɛn.

“Tek dɛn, man, a nɔ nid dɛn, bikɔs naw a nɔ de sheb, it, ɛn pre te—bɔt na ya—fɔ wok!”

Di stɛl we dɛn mek las las fɔ mek i tan lɛk aro, ɛn Pɛt bin wɛl am to di shank, i nɔ tu te, i pɔynt di ayɛn in ɛnd; ɛn as di blaksmit bin de kam fɔ gi di barb dɛn di las ɔt, bifo i mek dɛn kol, i kray to Eab fɔ put di wata-bɔks nia.

“Nɔ, nɔ—nɔ wata fɔ dat; a want am fɔ di tru tru day-tɛm. Ahoy, de! Tashtego, Queequeg, Daggoo! Wetin una se, pegan dɛn! Una go gi mi bɔku blɔd we go kɔba dis barb?” ol am ay ay wan. Wan grup we gɛt dak dak nɔd dɛn ansa se, Yɛs. Dɛn bin mek tri pankchɔ dɛn na di hitin bɔdi, ɛn afta dat dɛn bin de mek di Wait Wɛl in barb dɛn tem.

“Ego non baptizo te in nomine patris, sed in nomine diaboli!” Eab bin ala lawd wan, as di bad bad ayɛn bin de it di blɔd we i bin de baptayz.

Naw, we Eab bin gɛda di spay tik dɛn frɔm dɔŋ, ɛn pik wan pan di hikɔ, wit di bark we stil de put am insay, Eab bin fit di ɛnd to di ayɛn in sɔkɛtɛ. Dɔn dɛn kin pul wan kɔyl we gɛt nyu tow-layn, ɛn dɛn kin kɛr sɔm pat pan am go na di windlas, ɛn strɛch to big tɛnsiɔn. We Eab bin de pres in fut pan am, te di rop de hum lɛk ap-string, dɔn i bin rili want fɔ bɛn oba am, ɛn i nɔ si ɛni strɛch, i ala se, “Gud! ɛn naw fɔ di seiz dɛn.”

Na wan say we di rop nɔ bin tay, ɛn dɛn bin de breyd di sɛpret yan dɛn ɔl ɛn wev rawnd di sɔkɛt fɔ di hapun; dɔn dɛn bin de drɛb di pol tranga wan go ɔp insay di sɔkɛtɛ; frɔm di ɛnd we de dɔŋ dɛn bin de tray di rop af-af along di pol in lɔng, ɛn dɛn bin de tay am fayn fayn wan so, wit twin dɛn we de twistin. Dis we dɛn dɔn du, pol, ayɛn, ɛn rop—lɛk di Tri Fates—nɔ bin stil ebul fɔ separet, ɛn Eab bin de rɔn wit di wɛpɔn wit maynd; di sawnd we in ayvri leg de mek, ɛn di sawnd we di hikɔ pol de mek, dɛn ɔl tu de ring ol wan along ɛni plang. Bɔt bifo i go insay in kebin, layt, nɔ natura, af-af-bantering, yet di mɔs piteous sawnd dɛn bin yɛri. Oh, Pip, yu de! yu laf we yu de laf bad bad wan, yu yay we nɔ de du natin bɔt we nɔ de rɛst; ɔl yu strenj mami dɛn nɔ bin miks wit di blak bad bad tin we di ship we gɛt pwɛl at bin gɛt, ɛn provok am!
