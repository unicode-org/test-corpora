CHAPTER 53. Di Gam.

Di rizin we mek i tan lɛk se Eab nɔ bin go insay di wɛl we wi bin dɔn tɔk bɔt na dis: di briz ɛn di si bin de sho se big big briz de blo. Bɔt ivin if dis nɔ bin bi, afta ɔl, sɔntɛm, i nɔ bin fɔ dɔn go insay am—if yu jɔj am bay di we aw i bin biev afta dat pan di sem kayn tɛm dɛn—if na so i bin bi se, bay di we aw dɛn bin de prez am, i bin dɔn gɛt bad ansa to di kwɛstyɔn we i bin aks. Bikɔs, as i bi leta, i bin bisin fɔ nɔ mared, ivin fɔ fayv minit, wit ɛni strenja kapten, pas nɔmɔ i ebul fɔ gi sɔm pan da infɔmeshɔn de we i bin de luk fɔ wit ɔl in at. Bɔt ɔl dis kin stil nɔ ebul fɔ ɛstimat, if nɔto sɔntin we dɛn bin tɔk ya bɔt di spɛshal we aw dɛn kin yuz di bot dɛn we dɛn kin yuz fɔ kech wɛl we dɛn kin mit dɛnsɛf na fɔrina si, ɛn mɔ na wan kɔmɔn kruiz-grɔn.

If tu strenja dɛn krɔs di Pine Barrens na Nyu Yɔk Stet, ɔ di ikwal say we nɔ gɛt pipul dɛn na Salisbury Plain na Ingland; if dɛn kin mit dɛnsɛf na dɛn kayn wayl ples dɛn de we nɔ kin izi fɔ dɛn, dɛn tu pipul ya, fɔ dɛn layf, nɔ go ebul fɔ avɔyd fɔ salut dɛnsɛf; ɛn stɔp fɔ smɔl tɛm fɔ chenj di nyus; ɛn, sɔntɛm, sidɔm fɔ sɔm tɛm ɛn rɛst insay kɔnsɛt: den, i rili natura pas di si we nɔ gɛt limit we dɛn kɔl Pine Barrens ɛn Salisbury Plains, tu bot dɛn we de kech wɛl we de tɔk bɔt dɛnsɛf na di ɛnd dɛn na di wɔl—we de nia di wan dɛn we de na Fanning’s Ayland, ɔ di King’s Mills we de fa fawe; aw mɔ natura, a se, se ɔnda dɛn kayn tin ya, dɛn ship ya nɔ fɔ jɔs chenj ays blɔk, bɔt dɛn fɔ kam insay stil klos, mɔ padi ɛn soshal kɔntakt. Ɛn mɔ, i go tan lɛk se dis na tin we go apin, we i kam pan bot dɛn we dɛn gɛt na wan si pɔt, ɛn we dɛn kapten dɛn, ɔfisa dɛn, ɛn nɔto sɔm pan di man dɛn, dɛnsɛf sabi dɛnsɛf; ɛn bikɔs ɔf dat, gɛt ɔl kayn dia domestik tin fɔ tɔk bɔt.

Fɔ di ship we nɔ de fɔ lɔng tɛm, di wan we de go na do, sɔntɛm, gɛt lɛta dɛn na di bot; pan ɔl we i bi, i go mek shɔ se i gɛt sɔm pepa dɛn bɔt wan deti wan ɔ tu ia leta pas di las wan pan in fayl dɛn we nɔ klia ɛn we dɛn dɔn wɛr wit in an. Ɛn fɔ pe bak fɔ da kɔtisti de, di ship we de go na do go gɛt di laytst intɛlijɛns fɔ kech wɛl frɔm di kruiz-grɔn usay i go dɔn go, we na tin we rili impɔtant to am. Ɛn insay di digri, ɔl dis go bi tru bɔt di bot dɛn we de kech wɛl we de krɔs dɛnsɛf in trak na di kruiz-grɔn sɛf, pan ɔl we dɛn nɔ de na os fɔ lɔng tɛm di sem we. Bikɔs wan pan dɛn go dɔn gɛt transfa lɛta frɔm sɔm tɔd, ɛn naw fa fa fa bot; ɛn sɔm pan dɛn lɛta dɛn de kin bi fɔ di pipul dɛn na di ship we i de mit naw. Apat frɔm dat, dɛn bin de chenj di nyus bɔt di wɛl, ɛn dɛn bin de tɔk to dɛnsɛf fayn fayn wan. Bikɔs nɔto jɔs dɛn go mit wit ɔl di sɔri-at we di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot gɛt, bɔt dɛn go mit wit ɔl di spɛshal fayn fayn tin dɛn we kin kɔmɔt frɔm wan kɔmɔn tin we dɛn kin du ɛn di tin dɛn we dɛn ɔl tu kin gɛt ɛn di prɔblɛm dɛn we dɛn kin gɛt.

Nɔto difrɛns bitwin kɔntri dɛn nɔ go mek ɛni rili impɔtant difrɛns; dat min se, as lɔng as ɔl tu di pati dɛn de tɔk wan langwej, lɛk aw i kin bi wit Amɛrikin ɛn Inglish. Pan ɔl we, fɔ tru, frɔm di smɔl nɔmba fɔ di Inglish pipul dɛn we de kech wɛl, dɛn kayn mitin ya nɔ kin apin bɔku tɛm, ɛn we dɛn kin apin, i kin tu fit fɔ mek sɔm kayn shem de bitwin dɛn; bikɔs yu Inglishman na rili rizɔv, ɛn yu Yanki, i nɔ de fansi da kayn tin de pan ɛnibɔdi pas insɛf. Apat frɔm dat, sɔntɛnde di Inglish pipul dɛn we de kech wɛl kin afɛkt wan kayn mɛtropɔliti we pas di Amɛrikan pipul dɛn we de kech wɛl; bɔt di lɔng, slim Nantucketer, wit in nondescript provincialisms, as wan kayn si-peasant. Bɔt usay dis supiriɔriti pan di Inglish wɛlman dɛn rili de, i go at fɔ tɔk, we wi si se di Yanki dɛn insay wan de, togɛda, kil mɔ wɛl pas ɔl di Inglish pipul dɛn, togɛda, insay tɛn ia. Bɔt dis na smɔl fɔbul we nɔ gɛt wan prɔblɛm na di Inglish pipul dɛn we de fɛn wɛl, we di Nantucketer nɔ de tek bɔku tin na dɛn at; sɔntɛm, bikɔs i no se insɛf gɛt sɔm tin dɛn we i nɔ ebul fɔ du.

So, so, wi si se pan ɔl di ship dɛn we de go na di si apat frɔm dɛnsɛf, di wan dɛn we de kech wɛl gɛt mɔ rizin fɔ mek dɛn lɛk pipul dɛn—ɛn na so dɛn bi. We, sɔm biznɛs ship dɛn we de krɔs dɛnsɛf in wek na di mid-Atlantik, bɔku tɛm go pas we dɛn nɔ gɛt wan wɔd fɔ no, dɛn go kɔt dɛnsɛf na di ay si, lɛk wan bres ɔf dandi na Brodway; ɛn ɔl di tɛm indulging, sɔntɛm, in finical kritik pan dɛnsɛf in rig. As fɔ Man-of-War, we dɛn chans fɔ mit na si, dɛn kin fɔs go tru so string of sili bowings and scrapings, so such ducking of ensigns, dat i tan lɛk se nɔto bɔku rayt-dɔwn hearty hearty ɛn brɔda lɔv de bɔt am atɔl. As touching Slave-ships meeting, wai, dem de in so prodigious hurry, dem de ron away from each oda as soon as possible. Ɛn fɔ Pirates, we dɛn chans fɔ krɔs dɛnsɛf dɛn krɔs-bon, di fɔs ays blɔk na—“Ɔmɔs skel?”—di sem we aw pipul dɛn we de kech wɛl kin ays—“Ɔmɔs barɛl?” Ɛn we dɛn ansa da kwɛstyɔn de, pairati dɛn kin stiar apat wantɛm wantɛm, bikɔs dɛn na infɛnal wikɛd pipul dɛn na ɔl tu di say dɛn, ɛn dɛn nɔ lɛk fɔ si bɔku pan dɛn bad pipul dɛn we tan lɛk dɛnsɛf.

Bɔt luk di pɔsin we lɛk Gɔd, we ɔnɛs, we nɔ de mek lɛk se i gɛt ɔda pipul dɛn, we lɛk fɔ wɛlkɔm pipul dɛn, we lɛk fɔ spɛn tɛm wit ɔda pipul dɛn, we fri ɛn izi fɔ kech wɛl! Wetin di pɔsin we de kech wɛl kin du we i mit ɔda pɔsin we de kech wɛl na ɛni kayn fayn wɛda? I gɛt wan “ Gam ,” sɔntin we ɔl ɔda ship dɛn nɔ no atɔl dat dɛn nɔ ɛva yɛri bɔt di nem ivin; ɛn if bay chans dɛn fɔ yɛri bɔt am, dɛn kin jɔs grin pan am, ɛn ripit gem sɔm tin dɛn bɔt “spouters” ɛn “blubber-boilers,” ɛn dɛn kayn tin dɛn de lɛk fayn fayn ɛksklamashɔn dɛn. Wetin mek ɔl di Machɛn-siman dɛn, ɛn bak ɔl di Pirates ɛn Man-of-War in man dɛn, ɛn Slev-ship selɔ dɛn, valyu dis kayn skɔnful filin to Wɛl-ship dɛn; dis na kweshon we i go tranga fo ansa. Bikɔs, insay di kes fɔ pairati, se, a fɔ lɛk fɔ no if da wok de we dɛn de du gɛt ɛni pekul glori bɔt am. Sɔntɛnde i kin dɔn wit ɛleveshɔn we nɔ kɔmɔn, fɔ tru; bɔt na di gallows nɔmɔ. Ɛn apat frɔm dat, we dɛn es man ɔp da kayn we de, i nɔ gɛt di rayt fawndeshɔn fɔ mek i ay pasmak. So, a dɔn tɔk se we i de bost se i ay ay ay wan pas wɛlman, insay da asɛsmɛnt de di pairati nɔ gɛt ɛni strɔng bɛnifit fɔ tinap pan.

Bɔt wetin na Gam? Yu kin taya yu indeks finga we yu de rɔn ɔp ɛn dɔŋ di kɔlɔn dɛn na dikshɔnari dɛn, ɛn yu nɔ go ɛva fɛn di wɔd. Dɔktɔ Jɔnsin nɔ bin ɛva gɛt da sɛns de; Noa Webster in ak nɔ de ol am. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, dis sem wɔd we de sho aw pɔsin de tɔk, naw fɔ lɔng tɛm naw, dɛn dɔn de yuz am ɔltɛm bitwin lɛk fayvtin tawzin tru tru Yanki dɛn we dɛn bɔn. Fɔ tru, i nid fɔ gɛt difinishɔn, ɛn dɛn fɔ put am insay di Lɛksikɔn. Wit dat view, mek a lan difain am.

Na wan ɔda smɔl tin de bɔt Gamming we wi nɔ fɔ fɔgɛt ya. Ɔl di wok dɛn we dɛn de du gɛt dɛn yon smɔl smɔl tin dɛn we dɛn kin tɔk bɔt; na so di we aw dɛn de fishin wit wɛl dɔn du. Insay pairati, man-ɔf-wɔ, ɔ slev ship, we dɛn de row di kapten ɛnisay na in bot, i kin sidɔm ɔltɛm na di stɛp sheet dɛn na wan fayn sidɔm ples we kin gɛt kusɛn de, ɛn bɔku tɛm i kin stiar insɛf wit wan fayn smɔl milina tila we dɛn dɔn drɛs wit gay kɔd ɛn ribin. Bɔt di wɛl-bɔt nɔ gɛt sidɔm ples biɛn, i nɔ gɛt sofa we tan lɛk dat, ɛn i nɔ gɛt tila atɔl. Ay tɛm fɔ tru, if dɛn bin de wil di kapten dɛn we de kech wɛl bɔt di wata pan kastɔ lɛk gouti ol ɔldaman dɛn we de na patɛnt chia dɛn. Ɛn as fɔ pɔsin we de til, di wɛl-bɔt nɔ de ɛva admit ɛni kayn uman we tan lɛk dis; ɛn so lɛk aw dɛn de ple gem, wan kɔmplit bot in kru fɔ kɔmɔt na di ship, ɛn na dat mek as di pɔsin we de stiar di bot ɔ di pɔsin we de kip di bot na di nɔmba, da pɔsin de we de ɔnda am na di pɔsin we de stiar di tɛm, ɛn di kapten, we nɔ gɛt ples fɔ sidɔm, dɛn kin pul am go na in visit ɔl tinap lɛk pain tik. Ɛn bɔku tɛm yu go notis se bikɔs dis kapten we tinap de no di yay dɛn fɔ di wan ol wɔl we wi kin si we de rɛst pan am frɔm di sayd dɛn na di tu ship dɛn, ɔltin de alayv to di impɔtant tin fɔ sɔpɔt in rɛspɛkt bay we i de kip in leg dɛn. Dis nɔto tin we rili izi bak; bikɔs na in biɛn, di big big stiarin ɔa we ​​de projɛkt de nak am naw ɛn naw na di smɔl pat na in bak, di afta-ɔr de tɔn bak bay we i de rap in ni dɛn bifo. So i kin wed ɔlsay bifo ɛn biɛn, ɛn i kin jɔs ebul fɔ ɛkspɛn insɛf na sayd bay we i sidɔm pan in leg dɛn we i strɛch; bɔt bɔku tɛm we di bot de blo wantɛm wantɛm ɛn we gɛt fɛt-fɛt, i go go fa fɔ mek i fɔdɔm, bikɔs di lɔng we di fawndeshɔn gɛt nɔto natin if i nɔ gɛt di sem brayt. Jɔs mek tu pol dɛn spred angle, ɛn yu nɔ go ebul fɔ tinap fɔ dɛn. Dɔn, bak, i nɔ go ɛva du insay klia yay fɔ di wɔl in rivɛt yay, i nɔ go ɛva du, a se, fɔ mek dɛn si dis kapten we de strɛch de stedi insɛf di smɔl smɔl patikyula bay we i de ol ɛnitin wit in an; fɔ tru, as token fɔ in ɔl, buoyant sɛlf-kɔmand, i jɔs de kɛr in an dɛn na in trowza in poket; bɔt sɔntɛm bikɔs i kin jɔs bi an dɛn we rili big ɛn ebi, i kin kɛr dɛn go de fɔ mek dɛn mek balast. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, sɔm tin dɛn dɔn apin, we dɛn dɔn ɔtɛnɛt fayn fayn wan bak, usay dɛn dɔn no di kapten fɔ wan ɔ tu tɛm we nɔ kɔmɔn, insay wan swɛlin wantɛm wantɛm se—fɔ ol di pɔsin we de nia am in ia, ɛn ol de lɛk bad bad day.





CHAPTER 54. Di Taun-Ho in Stori.

( As dɛn bin tɛl am na di Golden Inn. )

Di Kep ɔf Gud Op, ɛn ɔl di wata rijyɔn we de rawnd de, tan lɛk sɔm notis 4 kɔna dɛn na wan big aywe, usay yu kin mit bɔku pipul dɛn we de travul pas ɛni ɔda pat.

I nɔ tu te afta dɛn dɔn tɔk di Goney, dɛn mit wan ɔda wɛlman we de go na os, we nem di Tawn-Ho, *. Klosap ɔl di pipul dɛn we kɔmɔt na Polinesia bin de wok fɔ am. In di shot gam we ensued she giv wi strong nyus of Moby Dick. To sɔm di jenɛral intres pan di Wait Wɛl bin dɔn wayl naw bikɔs ɔf wan sikɔstɛms na di Tawn-Ho in stori, we bin tan lɛk se i nɔ klia fɔ involv wit di wɛl wan patikyula wɔndaful, invayt visit fɔ wan pan dɛn so kɔl jɔjmɛnt dɛn fɔ Gɔd we sɔntɛnde dɛn kin se i kin pas sɔm man dɛn. Dis las tin we apin, wit in yon patikyula akkompanimɛnt dɛn, we mek wetin dɛn kin kɔl di sikrit pat pan di bad bad tin we dɛn go tɔk bɔt, nɔ ɛva rich na Kpɛntin Eab ɔ in kɔmpin dɛn yes. Bikɔs da sikrit pat de pan di stori nɔ bin no to di kapten fɔ di Tawn-Ho insɛf. Na bin di prayvet prɔpati fɔ tri kɔnfɛdɛrat wayt siman dɛn na da ship de, i tan lɛk se wan pan dɛn bin kɔmyuniket am to Tashtego wit Romish injɔkshɔn fɔ sikrit, bɔt di nɛks nɛt Tashtego bin de rɔn na in slip, ɛn sho bɔku pan am da we de, dat we dɛn wek am i nɔ bin ebul fɔ kip di ɔda wan dɛn fayn fayn wan. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, dis tin bin gɛt so pawaful inflɔwɛns pan dɛn siman dɛn na di Pequod we kam fɔ no am ful wan, ɛn bay so wan strenj delikasi, fɔ kɔl am so, if dɛn bin de rul dɛn pan dis mata, dat dɛn bin kip di sikrit bitwin dɛnsɛf so dat i nɔ ɛva transpire abaft di Pequod in men-mast. We a de intawev insay in rayt ples dis dak trɛd wit di stori as dɛn tɔk am na pɔblik na di ship, di wan ol dis strenj afɛkt naw a de go bifo fɔ put pan las rɛkɔd.

*Di ol wɛl-kray we dɛn fɔs si wɛl frɔm di mast-hɛd, we wɛlman dɛn stil de yuz fɔ fɛn di fambul Gallipagos tɛrapin.

Fɔ mek a laf, a go kip di stayl we a bin de tɔk bɔt am wan tɛm na Lima, to wan lounging sɛklɔ fɔ mi Spanish padi dɛn, wan sɛnt in ivintɛm, we de smok pan di tik-gilt tayl piazza na di Golden Inn. Pan dɛn fayn fayn kavaliɔ dɛn de, di yɔŋ Dons dɛn, we na Pita ɛn Sɛbastian, bin de tayt padi biznɛs wit mi; ɛn na dat mek dɛn kin aks kwɛstyɔn dɛn we de bitwin dɛn wan wan tɛm, ɛn we dɛn kin ansa fayn fayn wan da tɛm de.

In truth, well nigh the whole of this passage being attended by very prosperous breezes, di Town-Ho bin dɔn ɔl bɔt shɔ se i rich in pafɛkt sef na in pɔt witout di apinɛns fɔ di least fatality, if nɔto fɔ di brutal ɔvabearing of Radney, di mate, wan Vineyarder, ɛn di bita provoked revengeance of Steelkilt, wan Lakeman ɛn desperado frɔm Buffalo.

"Dɛn tɛm de, we di fɔs tap na di bot in bɔt, di Lekman bin dɔn slak di layn, so dat i go drɔp biɛn frɔm di whirlpool; i bin de luk am wit kol at, i bin de tink bɔt in yon tink. Bɔt wan wantɛm, wɔndaful, we di bot bin de jɔk dɔŋ, kwik kwik wan, i briŋ in nɛf kam na di layn. I kɔt am; ɛn di wɛl bin fri. Bɔt, na sɔm distans, Mobi Dik bin rayz bak, wit sɔm tatters pan Radney in rɛd wul shit, we dɛn kech insay di tit dɛn we bin dɔn pwɛl am Ɔl di 4 bot dɛn gi chase bak bɔt di wɛl bin rɔnawe pan dɛn, ɛn leta i nɔ de igen.

“In gud tɛm, di Tawn-Ho bin rich na in pɔt—na wan savayj ples, we de in wan—we no sivilizayt krichɔ nɔ bin de.Da, we di Lekman bin de bifo, ɔl di fɔs mastaman dɛn bin de bay wilful fɔ lɛf midul di pam tik dɛn pas fayv ɔ siks;

“Bikɔs di ship in kɔmni bin ridyus to jɔs wan anful, di kapten kɔl di Ayland pipul dɛn fɔ ɛp am fɔ di tranga biznɛs fɔ hev dɔŋ di ship fɔ stɔp di lik. Bɔt to dis kayn we we nɔ bin de rɛst fɔ wach dɛn denja padi dɛn, dis smɔl grup we na wayt pipul dɛn bin nid fɔ du, na nɛt ɛn de, ɛn di tranga wok we dɛn bin de du bin rili bad, dat we di ship bin rɛdi bak fɔ si, dɛn bin de insay so so wan wik kɔndishɔn we di kapten nɔ bin gɛt maynd fɔ put ɔf wit dɛn insay wan bot we ebi so Afta i tek advays wit in ɔfisa dɛn, i lod ɛn rɔn in tu kanɔn dɛn kɔmɔt na di bɔw dɛn ɛn wɔn di pipul dɛn na di Ayland fɔ mek dɛn nɔ go nia di ship we dɛn de pan di bɛst, ɛn i tek wan man wit am wɛl-bɔt, we dɛn bin de stiar stret bifo di briz fɔ go na Tahiti, we de fayv ɔndrɛd mayl fa, fɔ mek i go gɛt wan trɛnk fɔ in kru.

“Di de we mek 4 fɔ di sel, dɛn bin tɔk bɔt wan big kenu, we i bin tan lɛk se i tɔch wan ayland we gɛt kɔral Lekman laf am fɔ mek i provok am se if di pistɔl sote i klik na di lɔk, i go bɛr am insay bɔbul ɛn fom.

"'Krɔs yu an, sa; trowe yu ed bak. Naw, ripit afta mi. As Stilfilt lɛf mi so, a swɛ fɔ bich dis bot na yanda ayland, ɛn de de siks dez. If a nɔ du am, mek laytin kam mi!'

“Na wan fayn masta sabi bukman,’ di Lekman laf. ‘Adios, Senor!’ ɛn jomp insay di si, i swim bak to in kɔmpin dɛn.

"We i de wach di bot te i fayn fayn wan na di bich, ɛn drɔ am to di rut dɛm fɔ di kakao-nat tik dɛm, Stilfilt mek sel bak, ɛn insay di rayt tɛm i rich na Tahiti, in yon ples we i de go. Na de, lɔk bin mek padi wit am; tu ship dɛn bin de kam fɔ go na Frans, ɛn dɛn bin providentially in want of precisely that number of men which the sailor headed. ɛn so fɔ ɛva gɛt di stat fɔ dɛn fɔma kapten, if i bin dɔn at ɔl maynd fɔ wok dɛn ligal ritribushɔn.

“Lɛk tɛn dez afta di Frɛnch ship dɛn dɔn go, di wɛl-bɔt kam, ɛn dɛn fos di kapten fɔ mek i tek sɔm pan di pipul dɛn na Tahiti we gɛt mɔ sivilizayt, we bin dɔn yus to di si smɔl.

“Usay Steelkilt de naw, jentlman dɛn, nɔbɔdi nɔ no; bɔt na di ayland we nem Nantucket, di uman we in man dɔn day na Radney stil de tɔn to di si we nɔ gri fɔ giv ɔp in dayman dɛn; stil na drim i de si di bad bad wayt wɛl we bin pwɛl am. * * * *
