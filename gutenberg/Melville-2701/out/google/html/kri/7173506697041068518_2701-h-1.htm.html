CHAPTER 1. Di tin dɛn we de apin.

Una kɔl mi Ismaɛl. Sɔm ia bifo naw—nɔ ɛva tink bɔt aw lɔng prɛsishɔn—bikɔs a nɔ bin gɛt bɛtɛ mɔni ɔ a nɔ bin gɛt ɛni mɔni na mi pɔs, ɛn a nɔ bin gɛt ɛni patikyula tin fɔ intres mi na di sho, a bin tink se a go sel lɛk smɔl ɛn si di wata pat na di wɔl. Na we we a gɛt fɔ drɛb kɔmɔt na di splin ɛn fɔ mek di blɔd go ɔlsay. Ɛnitɛm we a si se a de gro grim bɔt di mɔt; ɛnitɛm we na damp, drizzly Novɛmba na mi sol; ɛnitɛm we a si se a de stɔp bifo kɔfin we dɛn de kip tin dɛn we a nɔ want, ɛn briŋ di riya fɔ ɛni bɛrin we a mit; ɛn mɔ ɛnitɛm we mi hypos gɛt so ɔpa han pan mi, we i nid fɔ gɛt strɔng moral prinsipul fɔ mek a nɔ step insay di strit bay wilful, ɛn fɔ nak pipul dɛn hat bay we a de du am—dɛn, a de akɔntayn am se na ay tɛm fɔ rich na si as soon as a ebul. Dis na mi substitute fɔ pistol ɛn bɔl. Wit wan filɔsofik flourish Keto trowe insɛf pan in sɔd; A kwayɛt wan tek go na di ship. Natin nɔ de we de mek wi sɔprayz pan dis. If dɛn bɔt bin no am, ɔlmost ɔl man dɛn we de na dɛn digri, sɔm tɛm ɔ ɔda tɛm, kin cherish rili klos to di sem filin dɛn to di si wit mi.

Naw, yu insular siti we nem Manhattoes de, we dɛn dɔn bɛlt rawnd di watawɛl dɛn lɛk di Indian ayland dɛn wit kɔral rif dɛn—kɔmishɔn de rawnd am wit in sɔf. Rayt ɛn lɛft, di strit dɛn de kɛr yu go na wata. I ekstrim daun tawn na di batri, usay da nobul mol de was wev, ɛn kol wit briz, we sɔm awa bifo dat nɔ bin de na land. Luk di krawd we de wach wata de.

Sakumbul di siti fɔ wan drim Sabat aftanun. Go frɔm Corlears Hook to Coenties Slip, ɛn frɔm de, bay Whitehall, go na di nɔt. Wetin yu si?—Dɛn post dɛn lɛk sɛntinɛl dɛn we nɔ de tɔk ɔlsay na di tɔŋ, dɛn tinap bɔku bɔku pipul dɛn we de day ɛn dɛn tinap tranga wan we dɛn de tink bɔt di si. Sɔm dɛn de ledɔm pan di spayl dɛn; sɔm sidɔm na di pier-heads; sɔm de luk oba di bulwark dɛn fɔ ship dɛn we kɔmɔt na Chaena; sɔm ay ay ay wan na di rig, lɛk se dɛn de tray fɔ gɛt wan stil bɛtɛ peep we de go na di si. Bɔt dɛn ɔl na landman dɛn; fɔ wik de dɛn we dɛn kin kip insay lat ɛn plasta—dɛn tay dɛn na kɔnta dɛn, dɛn nel dɛn na bɛnch dɛn, dɛn kin tay dɛn na dɛsk dɛn. So aw dis kin bi? Yu tink se di grɔn fil dɛn nɔ de igen? Wetin dɛn de du na ya?

Bɔt luk! na ya mɔ krawd de kam, we de waka stret fɔ di wata, ɛn i tan lɛk se dɛn de go dayv. Strenj! Natin nɔ go satisfay dɛn pas di limit we pas ɔl na di land; loitering anda di shady lee of yonder warehouses nɔ go du fɔ yu. Nɔ, dɛn fɔ kam nia di wata jɔs lɛk aw dɛn go ebul fɔ kam nia di wata we dɛn nɔ fɔdɔm insay.Ɛn na de dɛn tinap—mayl dɛn frɔm dɛn—lig dɛn. Inlanders ɔl, dɛn kɔmɔt frɔm len ɛn ali, strit ɛn avɛnyu—nɔt, ist, sawt, ɛn wɛst. Bɔt stil, na ya dɛn ɔl gɛt wanwɔd. Tɛl mi, yu tink se di magnɛtik gud we di nidul dɛn we de na di kɔmpas dɛn na ɔl dɛn ship dɛn de, kin mek dɛn kam de?

Wan tɛm bak. Se yu de na di kɔntri; na sɔm ay ay land dɛn we gɛt lek dɛn. Tek klos to ɛni rod we yu want, ɛn tɛn to wan i de kɛr yu go dɔŋ na wan dal, ɛn lɛf yu de nia wan pul na di strim. Majik de insay de. Mek di man dɛn we nɔ de tink gud wan, go insay in dip dip tin dɛn we i de tink bɔt—stand da man de na in leg, put in fut a-go, ɛn i go lid yu to wata we nɔ go mek mistek, if wata de na ɔl da rijyɔn de. If yu fɔ ɛva tɔsti na di big Amɛrikan dɛzat, tray dis ɛkspiriɛns, if yu karavan apin fɔ saplae wit mɛtafizik prɔfɛsɔ. Yɛs, as ɔlman no, fɔ tink gud wan ɛn wata kin mared sote go.

Bɔt na wan atis de ya. I want fɔ peint yu di drimest, shadiest, kwayɛt, mɔs ɛnchantin bit fɔ romantik land skay na ɔl di vali na di Sako. Wetin na di men tin we i de yuz? Na de in tik dɛn tinap, ɛn ɛni wan pan dɛn gɛt wan ol tik, lɛk se wan ɛmit ɛn wan krɔs de insay; ɛn na ya in mɛdɔ de slip, ɛn na de in kaw dɛn de slip; ɛn ɔp frɔm de kɔtij, wan smok we de slip de go. Dip insay fa fa wudlan dɛn, briz kin blo wan mazi we, ɛn i kin rich to di mawnten dɛn we de ɔvalap we dɛn was wit dɛn blu we de nia di il. Bɔt pan ɔl we di pikchɔ de so tran, ɛn pan ɔl we dis pain tik de shek in sigrɛt lɛk lif na dis shɛpad in ed, ɔltin na fɔ natin, pas nɔmɔ di shɛpad in yay bin de pan di majik strim we bin de bifo am. Go visit di Prairies insay Jun, we fɔ bɔku bɔku mayl dɛn yu kin waka dip yu ni-dip bitwin Tayga-lili dɛn—wetin na di wan charm we yu want?—Wata—nɔto wan drop wata de de! Yu tink se Niagara na bin katarakt we gɛt san, yu go travul fɔ yu tawzin mayl fɔ si am? Wetin mek di po pɔyt na Tɛnisi, we i gɛt tu anful silva wantɛm wantɛm, bin tink if i fɔ bay kot fɔ am, we i sɔri fɔ no se i nid, ɔ fɔ put in mɔni fɔ travul fɔ waka na Rɔkway Bich? Wetin mek ɔlmost ɛvri strɔng wɛlbɔdi bɔbɔ wit strɔng wɛlbɔdi sol insay am, na sɔm tɛm ɔ ɔda krayzi fɔ go na si? Wetin mek we yu bin de travul fɔ di fɔstɛm as pasenja, yusɛf bin fil dis kayn mistik shek, we dɛn bin tɛl yu fɔ di fɔstɛm se yu ɛn yu ship nɔ de si yu ɛn yu ship naw? Wetin mek di ol pipul dɛn na Pashia bin de kip di si oli? Wetin mek di Grik dɛn gi am wan sɛpret gɔd, ɛn in yon brɔda fɔ Jove? Fɔ tru, ɔl dis nɔto tin we nɔ gɛt wan minin. Ɛn stil dip di minin fɔ da stori de bɔt Nasis, we bikɔs i nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɔndastand di imej we bin de mek i sɔfa ɛn we nɔ gɛt wan bɔt we i bin si na di watawɛl, i bin go insay de ɛn drawn am. Bɔt da sem imej de, wisɛf de si na ɔl di riva ɛn si. Na di imej fɔ di phantom fɔ layf we pɔsin nɔ ebul fɔ ɔndastand; ɛn dis na di ki fɔ ɔl dis.

Naw, we a se a gɛt di abit fɔ go na si ɛnitɛm we a bigin fɔ gro hazy bɔt di yay, ɛn bigin fɔ ɔva kɔnshɛns pan mi lɔng, a nɔ min fɔ mek dɛn tink se a kin ɛva go na si as pasenja. Fɔ mek yu go as pasenja yu nid fɔ gɛt pɔs, ɛn pɔs na jɔs rag pas yu gɛt sɔntin insay. Apat frɔm dat, pasenja dɛn kin sik na di si—dɛn kin gro fɔ fɛt—nɔ kin slip na nɛt—nɔ kin ɛnjɔy dɛnsɛf bɔku, as jɔnaral tin;—nɔ, a nɔ de ɛva go as pasenja; ɛn, pan ɔl we a na sɔntin we gɛt sɔl, a nɔ kin ɛva go na si as Kɔmwɔd, ɔ Kpɛntin, ɔ Kuk. A de lɛf di glori ɛn difrɛns we dɛn kayn ɔfis dɛn de gɛt to di wan dɛn we lɛk dɛn. Fɔ mi pat, a et ɔl di ɔnaful rɛspɛktful wok, prɔblɛm dɛn, ɛn ɔlkayn trɔbul dɛn. I rili tan lɛk aw a kin du fɔ kia fɔ misɛf, we a nɔ tek kia ɔf ship, bark, brig, skun, ɛn wetin nɔ de. Ɛn as fɔ go as kuk,—pan ɔl we a kɔnfɛs se bɔku glori de insay dat, kuk na wan kayn ɔfisa we de na ship-bɔd—bɔt, sɔm kayn we, a nɔ bin ɛva fansi fɔ broyl fɔl dɛn;—pan ɔl we wans dɛn dɔn brok am, dɛn dɔn mek bɔta wit sɛns, ɛn dɛn dɔn jɔj am wit sɔl ɛn pepa, nɔbɔdi nɔ de we go tɔk mɔ wit rɛspɛkt, nɔto fɔ se wit rɛspɛkt, bɔt pɔsin we dɔn brok fowl pas aw a go du am. Na frɔm di aydɔl dotings we di ol Ijipshian dɛn bin de du pan broiled ibis ɛn roast riva ɔs, na in yu si di mami dɛn fɔ dɛn krichɔ dɛn de insay dɛn big big bek-haus dɛn we na di pyramid dɛn.

Nɔ, we a go na si, a kin go lɛk simpul selɔ, rayt bifo di mast, plumb dɔŋ insay di fɔkas, ɔp de to di kiŋ mast-hɛd. Na tru se dɛn kin ɔda mi bɔt sɔm, ɛn mek a jomp frɔm wan spar to di ɔda wan, lɛk grasshopa na May mɛdɔ. Ɛn fɔs, dis kayn tin nɔ kin fayn fɔ yu. I kin tɔch pɔsin in sɛns fɔ ɔnɔ, mɔ if yu kɔmɔt na wan ol famili we dɔn establish na di land, di Van Rensselaers, ɔ Randolphs, ɔ Hardicanutes. Ɛn pas ɔl, if jɔs bifo yu put yu an insay di tar-pɔt, yu dɔn de lod am as kɔntri skulmasta, we de mek di bɔbɔ dɛn we lɔng pas ɔl tinap fɔ fred yu. Di chenj na wan we rili, a de ashu yu, frɔm skulmasta to selɔ, ɛn i nid fɔ gɛt strɔng dɛkɔkshɔn fɔ Sɛnika ɛn di Stoik dɛn fɔ mek yu ebul fɔ grin ɛn bia am. Bɔt ivin dis kin west as tɛm de go.

Wetin bɔt am, if sɔm ol hunks dɛn na si-kapten ɔda mi fɔ tek brum ɛn swip dɔŋ di dɛk dɛn? Wetin da indignity de amount to, weighed, I min, in di skel na di Nyu Tɛstamɛnt? Yu tink se di akɛnjɛl Gebrɛl tink ɛnitin di smɔl bɔt mi, bikɔs a de obe da ol hunks de wantɛm wantɛm ɛn wit rɛspɛkt insay da patikyula instans de? Udat nɔto slev? Tel mi dat. Wɛl, so, aw di ol si-kapten dɛn kin ɔda mi fɔ tɔk bɔt—ilɛk aw dɛn kin thump ɛn pan mi bɔt, a kin satisfay fɔ no se i fayn; dat ɔlman na wan we ɔ ɔda we dɛn de sav di sem we—ilɛksɛf na fizik ɔ mɛtafizikal pɔynt fɔ si, dat na; ɛn so di yunivasal thump de pas rawnd, ɛn ɔl di an dɛn fɔ rɔb dɛnsɛf in sholda-bled, ɛn satisfay.

Agen, a kin go na si ɔltɛm as selɔ, bikɔs dɛn kin mek pɔynt fɔ pe mi fɔ mi trɔbul, bɔt dɛn nɔ kin ɛva pe pasenja dɛn wan peni we a ɛva yɛri bɔt. Bifo dat, di pasenja dɛnsɛf fɔ pe. Ɛn ɔl di difrɛns de na di wɔl bitwin fɔ pe ɛn fɔ pe. Sɔntɛm di akt fɔ pe na di tin we nɔ kɔmfyut pas ɔl we di tu tifman dɛn we bin de tif di frut gadin dɛn bin du to wi. Bɔt we dɛn de pe am ,—wetin go kɔmpia wit am? Di wok we pɔsin kin du na di siti we pɔsin kin gɛt mɔni kin rili wɔndaful, we wi tink bɔt se wi rili biliv se mɔni na di rut fɔ ɔl di bad tin dɛn we de apin na dis wɔl, ɛn nɔto so pɔsin we gɛt mɔni nɔ go ebul fɔ go na ɛvin. Ah! wi kin gladi fɔ kɔnsayn wisɛf fɔ day!

Fɔ dɔn, a kin go na si ɔltɛm as selɔ, bikɔs ɔf di fayn fayn ɛksesaiz ɛn di klin briz we de na di fɔs-kast dɛk. Fɔ lɛk dis wɔl, ed briz de fa fawe pas briz we de kɔmɔt biɛn (dat na, if yu nɔ ɛva pwɛl di Paytagorian maksim), so fɔ di mɔs pat di Kɔmwɔd we de na di kwata-dɛk kin gɛt in atmosfɛs na sɛkɔn han frɔm di selɔ dɛn we de na di fɔskast. I tink se na in de blo am fɔs; bɔt nɔto so. Na di sem we aw di kɔmɔn pipul dɛn kin lid dɛn lida dɛn pan bɔku ɔda tin dɛn, di sem tɛm we di lida dɛn nɔ kin sɔprayz bɛtɛ. Bɔt na dat mek afta we a dɔn smɛl di si bɔku tɛm lɛk biznɛsman we de wok na di si, naw a fɔ tek am na mi ed fɔ go waka fɔ kech wɛl; dis di polis ɔfisa we wi nɔ de si na di Fates, we de wach mi ɔltɛm, ɛn sikrit wan de dɔg mi, ɛn inflɔws mi insay sɔm we we nɔ gɛt akɔntayn—i kin ansa bɛtɛ pas ɛni ɔda pɔsin. Ɛn, wi nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se, di we aw a bin de go dis we fɔ kech wɛl, bin bi pat pan di big program na Prɔvidɛns we dɛn bin dɔn mek lɔng tɛm bifo. I kam in as wan kayn shɔt intalɔd ɛn solo bitwin mɔ bɔku pɔfɔmɛnshɔn dɛn. A tek am se dis pat pan di bil fɔ dɔn rɔn sɔntin lɛk dis:

“ Grɛnd Kɔntest Ilɛkshɔn fɔ di Prɛsidɛnshi na Amɛrika. “WHALING VOYAGE BY ONE ISHMAEL. “BLƆDI FƐTUL NA AFGHANISTAN.”

Pan ɔl we a nɔ ebul fɔ tɛl wetin mek na dat dɛn stej manija dɛn, di Fates, bin put mi dɔŋ fɔ dis shabby pat pan wan wɛl voyage, we ɔda pipul dɛn bin de sɛt dɔŋ fɔ magnificent pat dɛn na ay trajedi, ɛn shɔt ɛn izi pat dɛn na genteel kɔmɛdi, ɛn jolly pat dɛn na farces—pan ɔl we a nɔ ebul fɔ tɛl wetin mek dis na bin ɛksaktɔli; yet, naw we a mɛmba ɔl di sikɔstɛms, a tink se a kin si smɔl insay di spring ɛn motives we dɛn bin de prizent mi wit kɔni kɔni ɔnda difrɛn disgiz, indyuz mi fɔ sɛt fɔ du di pat we a bin du, apat frɔm cajoling mi insay di delusion se na choice rizults frɔm mi own unbiased friwill ɛn diskriminating judgment.

Di men tin we mek dɛn du dɛn tin ya na di aidia we bin rili bɔku bɔt di big wɛl insɛf. Dis kayn big big animal we bin de sho se a nɔ izi fɔ ɔndastand ɛn we nɔ izi fɔ ɔndastand bin mek a want fɔ no mɔ. Dɔn di wayl ɛn fa fa si dɛn usay i bin de rol in ayland bɔku bɔku wan; di prɔblɛm dɛn we di wɛl kin gɛt we dɛn nɔ ebul fɔ gi, we nɔ gɛt nem; dɛn tin ya, wit ɔl di wɔndaful tin dɛn we bin atɛnd fɔ wan tawzin Patagonia tin dɛn we a bin de si ɛn sawnd, bin ɛp fɔ mek a swɛla fɔ wetin a want. Sɔntɛm, wit ɔda man dɛn, dɛn kayn tin ya nɔ bin fɔ dɔn bi tin we de mek pɔsin want fɔ du sɔntin; bɔt as fɔ mi, a de sɔfa wit wan it we go de sote go fɔ tin dɛn we de fa. A lɛk fɔ sel na si dɛn we dɛn nɔ fɔ du, ɛn land na barbarous kɔst dɛn. We a nɔ de pe atɛnshɔn to wetin gud, a kin no kwik kwik wan se sɔntin de mek a fred, ɛn a kin stil de wit am—dɛn go alaw mi—bikɔs i fayn fɔ mek a gɛt padi biznɛs wit ɔl di prizina dɛn na di ples we pɔsin de lod.

So bikɔs ɔf dɛn tin ya, dɛn bin gladi fɔ di waka we dɛn bin de travul fɔ kech wɛl; di big big flod-get dɛm na di wɔnda-wɔl bin swing opin, ɛn insay di wayl kɔnsɛt dɛm we bin de swɛ mi to mi purpose, tu ɛn tu de de flot insay mi inmost sol, ɛndlɛs prɔseshɔn dɛm fɔ di wɛl, ɛn, midul bɔku pan dɛn ɔl, wan big hud phantom, lɛk sno il na di ays.





CHAPTER 2. Di Kapɛt-Bag.

A put wan ɔ tu shit insay mi ol kap-bag, put am ɔnda mi an, ɛn bigin fɔ go na Kep Hɔn ɛn di Pasifik. We a lɛf di gud siti we nem ol Manhatto, a rich na Nyu Bɛdfɔd di rayt we. Na bin Satide nɛt insay Disɛmba. A bin rili fil bad we a kam fɔ no se di smɔl paket fɔ Nantucket dɔn ɔlrɛdi sel, ɛn no we fɔ rich da ples de nɔ go gi, te di nɛks Mɔnde.

As bɔku pan di yɔŋ kandidet dɛn fɔ di pen ɛn penalty dɛn we dɛn kin gɛt we dɛn de kech wɛl kin stɔp na dis sem Nyu Bɛdfɔd, frɔm de fɔ bigin dɛn voyage, i kin bi bak se mi, fɔ wan, nɔ bin gɛt ɛni aydia fɔ du dat. Bikɔs dɛn bin mek mi maynd fɔ sel insay nɔto ɔda wan pas wan Nantucket kraft, bikɔs wan fayn, bɔku bɔku tin bin de bɔt ɔltin we gɛt fɔ du wit da fambul ol ayland de, we bin rili mek a gladi. Apat frɔm dat, pan ɔl we Nyu Bɛdfɔd dɔn de smɔl smɔl fɔ tek di biznɛs fɔ kech wɛl, ɛn pan ɔl we insay dis mata, po ol Nantucket naw de biɛn am fa fawe, yet Nantucket na bin in big ɔrijinal—di Taya fɔ dis Katej;—di ples usay di fɔs Amɛrikan wɛl we dɔn day bin stɔp. Usay ɔdasay pas frɔm Nantucket, dɛn aborijinial wɛlman dɛn, di Rɛd-Man dɛn, bin fɔs sally aut insay kenu fɔ gi chase to di Leviathan? Ɛn usay pas frɔm Nantucket, bak, da fɔs advantej smɔl sloop, we pat pan am lod wit imported cobblestones—so di stori go—fɔ trowe pan di wɛl dɛn, fɔ mek dɛn go diskɔba we dɛn dɔn nia fɔ risk wan harpun frɔm di bowsprit?

Naw we a gɛt wan nɛt, wan de, ɛn stil ɔda nɛt we de fala mi na Nyu Bɛdfɔd, bifo a ebul fɔ go na di pɔt we a want fɔ go, i bin bi sɔntin we a fɔ wɔri bɔt usay a fɔ it ɛn slip da tɛm de. Na bin wan nɛt we bin rili dawt, nɔto so, na bin wan nɛt we bin rili dak ɛn we bin rili bad, we bin kol bad bad wan ɛn we nɔ bin gɛt gladi at. A nɔ bin no ɛnibɔdi na di ples. Wit wɔri grapnel a bin dɔn saund mi poket, ɛn jɔs briŋ sɔm ​​silva pis,—So, ɛnisay we yu go, Ishmayl, a se to misɛf, as a tinap na di midul pan wan dray strit de sholda mi bag, ɛn kɔmpia di daknɛs we de go na di nɔt wit di daknɛs we de go na di sawt—ɛnisay we yu sɛns yu go kɔnklɔd fɔ lod fɔ di nɛt, mi dia Ismayl, mek shɔ se yu aks di prayz, ɛn nɔ tu patikyula.

Wit stɛp dɛn we de stɔp a waka na di strit dɛn, ɛn pas di sayn we se “The Crossed Harpoons”—bɔt i bin luk tu dia ɛn gladi de. Fɔ go bifo, frɔm di brayt rɛd winda dɛn na di “Swɔd-Fish Inn,” so fayaful rayt dɛn bin de kam, dat i bin tan lɛk se i dɔn mɛlt di pak sno ɛn ays frɔm bifo di os, bikɔs ɔdasay dɛn di frɔst we dɔn kol bin ledɔm tɛn inch tik na wan had, asfaltik rod,—rather weary for mi, wen a struck my foot against the flinty projections, because from hard, remorseless service the mi but dɛn in fut bin de pan wan bad bad prɔblɛm. Tu dia ɛn gladi, bak a tink, a stɔp wan mɔnt fɔ wach di brayt brayt glare na di strit, ɛn yɛri di sawnd dɛn fɔ di tinklin glas dɛn we de insay. Bɔt go bifo, Ishmayl, a se las las; yu noh yehri? kɔmɔt bifo di domɔt; yu patch but dem de stop di we. So on ai go. Naw bay instinkt a bin de fala di strit dɛn we bin de kɛr mi go na wata, bikɔs na de, i nɔ gɛt wan dawt se, na de di inn dɛn we nɔ dia pas ɔl, if nɔto di ples dɛn we pipul dɛn kin gladi fɔ.

Dɛn kayn strit dɛn de we nɔ fayn! blɔk dɛn we blak, nɔto os, na ɔl tu an, ɛn ya ɛn de wan kandul, lɛk kandul we de muv rawnd na grev. Na dis awa na nɛt, we na di las de insay di wik, da kwata de na di tɔŋ bin pruv se i nɔ gɛt pipul dɛn. Bɔt naw a kam nia wan layt we gɛt smok we de kɔmɔt na wan bildin we lɔŋ ɛn wayd, ɛn di domɔt bin tinap opin wan we de invayt pipul dɛn. I bin gɛt wan luk we dɛn nɔ tek tɛm luk, lɛk se dɛn mek am fɔ di we aw pipul dɛn de yuz am; so, we a de go insay, di fɔs tin we a du na fɔ stɔp pan wan ashis bɔks we de na di wɔl. Ha! a bin tink se, ha, as di tin dɛn we de flay bin lɛf smɔl fɔ mek a chok mi, dɛn ashis ya kɔmɔt na da siti we dɔn pwɛl, Gɔmɔra? Bɔt “The Crossed Harpoons,” ɛn “The Sword-Fish?”—so dis, so i go mɔs bi se na di sayn fɔ “Di Trap.” Bɔt, a pik misɛf ɛn yɛri wan lawd vɔys insay, a push go bifo ɛn opin wan sɛkɔn, insay domɔt.

I bin tan lɛk se na di big Blak Palimɛnt sidɔm na Tɔfɛt. Wan ɔndrɛd blak fes dɛn bin de tɔn rawnd na dɛn row fɔ luk; ɛn biɛn, wan blak Enjɛl fɔ Dum bin de bit wan buk na wan pulpit. Na bin wan nigro chɔch; ɛn di pricha in tɛks bin de bɔt di blak we daknɛs de, ɛn di kray ɛn kray ɛn tit-gnɛsh we de de. Ha, Ismaɛl, muf mi, bak aut, Wretched ɛnjɔymɛnt na di sayn fɔ ‘Di Trap!’.

We a de go bifo, a las kam to wan kayn layt we nɔ de fa frɔm di dok dɛn, ɛn yɛri wan pɔsin we nɔ gɛt layf de krak krak na di ays; ɛn we a luk ɔp, a si wan sayn we de swing oba di domɔt we gɛt wan wayt peint pan am, we de sho smɔl smɔl wan lɔng stret jet we gɛt misti sprɛy, ɛn dɛn wɔd ya ɔnda de—“Di Spouter Inn:—Pita Kɔfin.”

Kɔfin?—Spouter?—Rather ominous in that particular connexion, thought I. Bɔt na kɔmɔn nem na Nantucket, dɛn se, ɛn a tink se dis Pita we de ya na pɔsin we kɔmɔt de. As di layt bin luk so dak, ɛn di ples, fɔ di tɛm, bin luk kwayɛt inof, ɛn di smɔl wud os we dɔn pwɛl insɛf bin luk lɛk se dɛn go dɔn kɛr am kat ya frɔm di ruins na sɔm distrikt we dɔn bɔn, ɛn as di sayn we de swing bin gɛt wan kayn krik we po pipul dɛn de krik to am, a bin tink se na ya na di sem ples fɔ slip we nɔ dia, ɛn di bɛst we fɔ kɔfi we dɛn kɔl pia kɔfi.

Na bin wan kayn ples we nɔ bin izi fɔ ɔndastand—na wan ol os we gɛt gebul ɛnd, wan say bin tan lɛk se i nɔ ebul fɔ waka, ɛn i bin de ledɔm wit sɔri-at. I bin tinap na wan shap blak kɔna, usay da big big briz de we bin de blo Yuroklidɔn bin de kɔntinyu fɔ ala pas aw i bin de ala bɔt di po Pɔl in kraft we i bin de tos. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, Yuroklidɔn na pawaful plezant zephyr to ɛnibɔdi we de insay os, wit in fut dɛn na di hob kwayɛt wan de tost fɔ bed. Wan ol man we de rayt buk se: “We yu jɔj da big big briz de we dɛn kɔl Yuroklidon—we a gɛt di wangren kɔpi we a gɛt bɔt in wok dɛn—i kin mek wɔndaful difrɛns, ilɛksɛf yu luk am na glas winda usay di frɔst ɔl de na do, ɔ if yu si am frɔm da winda de we nɔ gɛt sash, usay di frɔst de na ɔl tu di say dɛn, ɛn we di wayt Day na di wangren say glazier.” Na tru, a bin tink, as dis pat de kam na mi maynd—ol blak-lɛta, yu de tink gud wan. Yɛs, dɛn yay ya na winda, ɛn dis bɔdi we a gɛt na di os. Wetin a pity dem no stop op di chinks en di crannies though, en thrust in likl lint ya en dea. Bɔt i dɔn let fɔ mek ɛni impɔtant tin naw. Di wan ol wɔl dɔn dɔn; di kɔpston de pan, ɛn dɛn bin kɛr di chips dɛn go wit kat wan milyɔn ia bifo dis tɛm. Po Lazarɔs de de, we de chat in tit dɛn agens di kɔba ston fɔ in pilo, ɛn shek in tatters wit in shiverings, i kin plɔg ɔl tu in yes wit rag, ɛn put kɔn-kɔb insay in mɔt, ɛn yet dat nɔ go mek di big big Yuroklaydɔn nɔ kɔmɔt. Yuroklaydɔn! se ol Dives, insay in rɛd silk wrap—(i bin gɛt wan we rɛd wan afta dat) pooh, pooh! Dis na fayn fayn nɛt we frɔsti; aw Ɔriɔn de shayn; wetin na di nɔt layt dɛn! Mek dɛn tɔk bɔt dɛn oriental sɔma klaym dɛn we gɛt kɔnsavatori dɛn we de sote go; gi mi di ɔnɔ fɔ mek mi yon sɔmda wit mi yon kol.

Bɔt wetin Lazarɔs tink? I go ebul fɔ wam in blu an dɛn bay we i ol dɛn ɔp to di big big layt dɛn we de na di nɔt? Yu nɔ tink se Lazarɔs go lɛk fɔ de na Sumatra pas fɔ de ya? Yu nɔ tink se i go lɛk fɔ le am lɔng lɔng wan along di layn we di ekweta de; yes, una gɔd dɛn! go dɔŋ na di ol we gɛt faya insɛf, fɔ mek dis frɔst nɔ kam?

Naw, we Lazarɔs fɔ ledɔm de na di kɔba ston bifo di domɔt fɔ Dayv, dis na wɔndaful tin pas fɔ mek dɛn tay wan ays blɔk pan wan pan di Mɔluka dɛn. Yet Dives insɛf, insɛf de liv lɛk Czar na ays pales we dɛn mek wit frɔzɛn sighs, ɛn bikɔs na prɛsidɛnt fɔ wan temperance sosayti, i jɔs de drink di tepid tears of orphans.

Bɔt nɔ mɔ pan dis blub naw, wi de go a-whaling, ɛn bɔku pan dat de fɔ kam yet. Lɛ wi skrap di ays frɔm wi fut dɛn we dɔn frɔst, ɛn si us kayn ples dis “Spouter” go bi.





CHAPTER 3. Di Spouter-Inn we de na di wɔl.

We yu go insay da Spouter-Inn we gɛt gable, yu si yusɛf insay wan wayd, lɔw, strɛch ɛntrɛ wit ol-fashin wainskɔt, we de mɛmba wan pan di bulwark dɛn fɔ sɔm ol kraft we dɛn dɔn kɔndɛm. Na wan say, dɛn bin hang wan rili big ɔyl peint we dɛn bin dɔn smok so gud gud wan, ɛn ɔl di we dɛn bin dɔn pwɛl, dat na di krɔslayt dɛn we nɔ ikwal we yu bin de yuz fɔ wach am, na bay we yu de stɔdi am gud gud wan ɛn we yu go fɛn am ɔltɛm, ɛn we yu tek tɛm aks di neba dɛn gud gud wan, na in yu go ebul fɔ ɔndastand di rizin we mek i du am ɛni we. Such unaccountable masses of shades and shadows, dat fɔs yu bin lɛf smɔl fɔ tink se sɔm ambishɔs yɔŋ atis, insay di tɛm fɔ di Nyu Ingland hags, bin tray fɔ delineate chaos bewitched. Bɔt bay we yu tink gud wan ɛn wit ɔl yu at, ɛn tink gud wan bɔku tɛm, ɛn mɔ bay we yu trowe di smɔl winda opin to di bak pat pan di ɛntrɛ, yu kin dɔn kam fɔ no se dis kayn aidia, ilɛksɛf i wayl, nɔ go bi tin we nɔ rayt atɔl.

Bɔt wetin bin mek yu kɔnfyus ɛn kɔnfyus pas ɔl na wan lɔng, limber, portentous, blak mas we gɛt sɔntin we de flay na di sɛntrɔm pan di pikchɔ oba tri blu, dim, perpendicular layn dɛn we de flɔt insay wan yist we nɔ gɛt nem. Wan boggy, soggy, squitchy pikchɔ tru tru, inaf fɔ drɛb man we de fred fɔ distɛkt. Yet bin de wan kayn indefinite, haf-attained, unimaginable sublimity bɔt am we fayn fɔ frɔz yu to am, te yu involuntarily tek wan oth wit yusɛf fɔ fɛn ɔut wetin da wɔndaful peint de min. Ever and anon a brayt, but, alas, deceptive idea would dart you through.—Na di Blak Si insay wan midnayt gale.—Na di unnatural combat of di fo primal elements.—Na wan blasted heath.—Na wan Hyperborean winta scene.—Na di brok-up of di aisbound stream of Time. Bɔt las las ɔl dɛn fansi ya bin gri fɔ da wan portentous sɔntin de we bin de midul di pikchɔ. Dat bin kam fɔ no wan tɛm, ɛn ɔl di ɔda wan dɛn bin klia. Bɔt stɔp; yu nɔ tink se i tan lɛk big big fish? ivin di big big livaytan insɛf?

Infakt, di we aw di atis bin mek am bin tan lɛk dis: na mi yon las tiori, we sɔm pat pan am bin kɔmɔt frɔm di tin dɛn we bɔku ol pipul dɛn we a bin de tɔk to bɔt di tɔpik bin dɔn gɛda. Di pikchɔ de sho wan Kep-Hɔna we de insay wan big big briz; di ship we gɛt af-af fawndeshɔn we bin de welt de wit in tri mast dɛn we dɛn dɔn brok nɔmɔ we pɔsin kin si; ɛn wan wɛl we vɛks, we i want fɔ spring klin oba di kraft, de du di big big akt fɔ tik insɛf pan di tri mast-ed dɛn.

Dɛn bin hang di ɔda wɔl na dis ɛntrɛ ɔlsay wit wan big big klɔb ɛn spia dɛn we tan lɛk het. Sɔm dɛn bin tik tik wit tit dɛn we de shayn we tan lɛk ayvri saw; ɔda wan dɛn bin gɛt knot dɛn we gɛt mɔtalman ia; ɛn wan bin tan lɛk sikl, ɛn wan big handel bin de swip rawnd lɛk di pat we wan mashin we gɛt lɔng an dɛn bin mek na di nyu gras we dɛn dɔn kɔt. Yu bin de shek shek as yu de luk, ɛn de wɔnda us monstrous kanibal ɛn savayj go ɛva go day-harvesting wit dis kayn hacking, horrifying implement. Dɛn bin miks wit dɛn tin ya, dɛn bin rɔsti ol wɛl lans ɛn hapun dɛn we ɔl bin brok ɛn nɔ fayn. Sɔm pan dɛn na bin wɛpɔn dɛn we dɛn bin de tɔk bɔt. Wit dis lans we bin de lɔng wan tɛm, we naw gɛt wayl ɛlbow, fifti ia bifo naw, Netan Swain bin kil fayvtin wɛl bitwin wan san we de kɔmɔt ɛn we di san de go dɔŋ. Ɛn da hapun de—so lɛk kɔkskru naw—dɛn bin trowe am na Javan si, ɛn wan wɛl rɔnawe wit am, sɔm ia afta dat dɛn kil am na di Kep ɔf Blankɔ. Di ɔrijinal ayɛn bin go insay nia di tel, ɛn, lɛk nidul we nɔ de rɛst we de de na man in bɔdi, i travul ful fɔti fit, ɛn las las dɛn fɛn am insay di hump.

We yu krɔs dis dak ɛntrɛ, ɛn go tru yon lɔw-arch we—we yu kɔt tru wetin insay ol tɛm go dɔn bi wan big sɛntral chimini wit faya ples ɔlsay—yu go insay di pɔblik rum. Wan ples we stil dak pas ɔl na dis, wit so lɔw ponderous bim dɛn ɔp, ɛn so ol wrinkled plank dɛn ɔnda, dat yu go ɔlmost fansi se yu trod sɔm ol kraft in kɔkpit, mɔ fɔ dis kayn ala ala nɛt, we dis ol ak we dɛn ankɔ na kɔna bin de rɔk so vɛks. Na wan say, wan lɔng tebul we smɔl, we tan lɛk shelf, we dɛn kɔba wit krak krak glas kes dɛn, we ful-ɔp wit dɔti rari tin dɛn we dɛn gɛda frɔm dis big wɔl in fa fa say dɛn. We i de projɛkt frɔm di fa angle na di rum, wan den we tan lɛk dak tin tinap—di ba—na rud we fɔ tray fɔ kɔt wan rayt wɛl in ed. I bi so, na de di big big arch bon na di wɛl in jaw tinap, so wayd, i kin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ kɔch drayv ɔnda am. Insay de, dɛn gɛt shelf dɛn we nɔ fayn, we dɛn rɛnj rawnd wit ol dikanta, bɔtul, flask; ɛn insay dɛn jaw dɛn de we de pwɛl kwik kwik wan, lɛk wan ɔda Jona we dɛn swɛ (we dɛn kɔl am fɔ tru), wan smɔl ol man we dɔn dray de mek pipul dɛn de fred, we, fɔ dɛn mɔni, de sɛl di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot wit dia delirium ɛn day.

Di tumbul dɛn we i de tɔn in pɔyzin insay, na tin dɛn we rili bad. Pan ɔl we tru tru silinda dɛn we nɔ gɛt—insay, di bad bad grɔn gɔglin glas dɛn bin ful-ɔp fɔ ful-ɔp dɔŋ to wan bɔt we de tif. Paralel meridian dɛn we dɛn rud wan pik insay di glas, de rawnd dɛn futpad dɛn kɔp dɛn ya. Fil to dis mak, ɛn yu chaj na jɔs wan peni; to dis wan peni mɔ; ɛn so go to di ful glas—di Kep Hɔn mɛzhɔ, we yu kin gulp fɔ wan shilin.

We a go insay di ples a si bɔku yɔŋ pipul dɛn we de wok na di si gɛda rawnd wan tebul, ɛn wan dim layt we de dayvɔs de chɛk di spɛshal wan dɛn we dɛn kɔl skrimshander . A bin luk fɔ di pɔsin we gɛt di os, ɛn we a tɛl am se a want fɔ mek dɛn gɛt rum fɔ de, a bin gɛt fɔ ansa se in os ful-ɔp—nɔto bed we nɔbɔdi nɔ de de. “Bɔt avast,” i ad, tap in fɔɛd, “yu haint no objɛshɔn fɔ sheb wan harpooneer in blankit, nɔto so? I s’pose yu de go a-whalin’, so yu bɛtɛ fɔ yus to da kayn tin de.”

A tɛl am se a nɔ ɛva lɛk fɔ slip tu pipul dɛn na bed; dat if a fɔ ɛva du dat, i go dipen pan udat di pɔsin we de mek hapun go bi, ɛn if in (di pɔsin we gɛt di land) rili nɔ gɛt ɔda ples fɔ mi, ɛn di pɔsin we de mek hapun nɔ bin disayd fɔ se i nɔ gri wit am, wetin mek pas fɔ waka waka go fa fawe na wan strenj tɔŋ na nɛt we bita so, a go bia wit di af pan ɛni fayn man in blankit.

"A bin tink so. I fayn; tek sidɔm ples. Sɔpa?—yu want sɔpa? Sɔpa go rɛdi dairekt wan."

A sidɔm pan wan ol wud sɛtul, we dɛn kɔt ɔlsay lɛk bɛnch na di Batri. Na wan ɛnd wan tar we bin de luk fɔ tin dɛn bin stil de mek am fayn mɔ wit in jak-naif, i bin de butu ɛn wok tranga wan fɔ go fa say we de bitwin in leg dɛn. I bin de tray in an pan wan ship ɔnda ful sel, bɔt i nɔ bin mek bɔku edway, a bin tink.

Las las, dɛn kɔl wi lɛk 4 ɔ 5 pan wi fɔ kam it na wan rum we de nia de. I bin kol as Ayland—nɔ gɛt faya atɔl—di pɔsin we gɛt di land bin se i nɔ go ebul fɔ pe fɔ am. Natin nɔ de pas tu talɔ kandul dɛn we nɔ fayn, ɛn ɛni wan pan dɛn de insay wan sheet we de waynd. Wi bin fain fɔ bɔt wi mɔnki jakit dɛn, ɛn ol to wi lip kɔp dɛn we gɛt ti we de bɔn wit wi finga dɛn we dɔn frɔz af. Bɔt di prays na bin di kayn we we rili impɔtant—nɔto mit ɛn pɔt nɔmɔ, bɔt dɔmplin; gud ɛvin dɛn! dumplings fɔ it di ivintɛm it! Wan yɔŋ man we wɛr grɔn bɔks kot, adrɛs insɛf to dɛn dɔmpling ya insay wan we we rili bad.

Di pɔsin we gɛt di land se: “Mi bɔy pikin, yu go gɛt di bad bad drim fɔ wan day sartainty.”

A wispa se: “Landlɔd, dat nɔto di harpooneer?”

I se, “O, nɔ,” i luk wan kayn dɛbul fani, “di harpooneer na dak kɔmpleks chap. I nɔ de ɛva it dɔmpling, i nɔ de it—i nɔ de it natin pas stik, ɛn i lɛk ’em rare.”

Mi se: “Di dɛbul we i de du, usay da harpoone de de? I de ya?”

Di ansa na bin se: “I go de ya fɔ lɔng tɛm.”

A nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɛp, bɔt a bigin fɔ fil se a de dawt dis “dak kɔlɔ” we de mek harpun. Ɛni we aw i bi, a bin disayd se if i kam fɔ no se wi fɔ slip togɛda, i fɔ pul in klos ɛn go na bed bifo a du dat.

Sɔpa dɔn, di kɔmni go bak na di ba-rum, we, bikɔs a nɔ no wetin ɔda fɔ du wit misɛf, a disayd fɔ spɛn di ɔda pat na di ivintɛm as pɔsin we de luk pan.

Naw dɛn yɛri wan nɔys we de mek pipul dɛn nɔ gɛt wanwɔd witout. Start op, di landlord kray, "Na di Grampus in kru. A sid am ripɔt in di offing dis mɔnin; wan tri ia voyage, ɛn wan ful ship. Hurrah, bɔy dɛm; naw wi go gɛt di layt nyus frɔm di Feegees."

Dɛn yɛri wan tramp fɔ si but dɛn na di ɛntrɛ; dɛn bin trowe di domɔt opin, ɛn wan wayl sɛt fɔ pipul dɛn we de wok na di si bin rol insay inof. Dɛn bin kɔba dɛn wit dɛn shaggy wach kot, ɛn wit dɛn ed we dɛn muf wit wul kɔmfɔta, ɔl bedarn ɛn ragged, ɛn dɛn biad stif wit ayskul, dɛn bin tan lɛk se na bea dɛn we kɔmɔt na Labrador de bɔn. Dɛn bin jɔs dɔn land frɔm dɛn bot, ɛn dis na di fɔs os we dɛn go insay. So, i nɔ wɔnda we dɛn mek wan stret wek fɔ di wɛl in mɔt—di ba—we i nɔ tu te, di smɔl ol Jona we bin rɔtin, we bin de du di ɔfisa de, tɔn dɛn ɔlsay. Wan pan dɛn bin kɔmplen bɔt bad kol na in ed, we Jona miks am wan mɛrɛsin we tan lɛk pitch we gɛt gin ɛn molas, we i swɛ se na sovayb mɛrɛsin fɔ ɔl di kol ɛn kata, i nɔ ɛva tink bɔt aw lɔng i dɔn de, ɔ if dɛn kech am na di si na Labradɔ, ɔ na di wɛda say na wan ays-ayland.

I nɔ tu te, di rɔm bin go ɔp insay dɛn ed, lɛk aw i kin jɔs du ivin wit di arrantest topers we jɔs land frɔm si, ɛn dɛn bigin fɔ caper bɔt mɔs obstreperously.

Bɔt a bin si se wan pan dɛn bin de ol smɔl, ɛn pan ɔl we i bin tan lɛk se i want fɔ nɔ pwɛl di we aw in kɔmpin dɛn na di ship bin de laf wit in yon fes we bin soba, bɔt pan ɔl dat, i nɔ bin de mek bɔku nɔys lɛk di ɔda wan dɛn. Dis man bin intres mi wantɛm wantɛm; ɛn bikɔs di si-gɔd dɛn bin dɔn ɔda se i nɔ go te igen i go bi mi shipmet (pan ɔl we na pɔsin we de slip, so fa as dis stori de), a go ya fɔ tɔk smɔl bɔt am. I bin tinap ful siks fit ayt, wit nobul sholda, ɛn bɔks we tan lɛk kɔfa-dam. A nɔ kin si da kayn brawn de pan man. In fes bin rili brawn ɛn bɔn, ɛn dis bin mek in wayt tit dɛn de shayn bikɔs ɔf di difrɛns; we insay di dip shado dɛn na in yay, sɔm tin dɛn we i bin de mɛmba bin de flɔt we i bin tan lɛk se i nɔ bin de gi am bɔku gladi at. In vɔys bin anawns wantɛm wantɛm se in na Sawt, ɛn frɔm in fayn fayn ayt, a bin tink se i go mɔs bi wan pan dɛn lɔng lɔng pipul dɛn we de klaym mawnten dɛn we kɔmɔt na di Alleghanian Ridge na Virginia. We di gladi gladi we in kɔmpin dɛn bin de mek bin dɔn go ɔp, dis man slip go we nɔbɔdi nɔ si am, ɛn a nɔ si am igen te i bi mi kɔmpin na di si. Bɔt insay sɔm minit, in kɔmpin dɛn we bin de na di ship bin mis am, ɛn bikɔs, i tan lɛk se fɔ sɔm rizin dɛn na bin big big fevrit wit dɛn, dɛn bin ala se “Bulkington! Bulkington! usay Bulkington de?” ɛn rɔn kɔmɔt na di os fɔ rɔnata am.

Naw na bin lɛk nayn oklɔk, ɛn i bin tan lɛk se di rum bin kwayɛt pasmak afta dɛn ɔrgi dɛn ya, a bigin fɔ kɔle misɛf fɔ wan smɔl plan we bin dɔn kam na mi maynd jɔs bifo di siman dɛn kam insay.

No man nɔ lɛk fɔ slip tu pipul dɛn na bed. Infakt, yu go bɛtɛ fɔ lɛ yu nɔ slip wit yu yon brɔda. a noh sabi aw i bi, boht pipul laik foh de prayvet we dem de slip. Ɛn we i kam pan fɔ slip wit strenja we yu nɔ no, na strenj in, na strenj tɔŋ, ɛn da strenja de na harpuoneer, den yu objɛshɔn dɛn kin bɔku sote go. Nɔto ɛni rizin nɔ bin de na dis wɔl we mek mi as pɔsin we de wok na di bot fɔ slip tu pipul dɛn na bed, pas ɛni ɔda pɔsin; bikɔs di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot nɔ kin slip tu pipul dɛn na bed na si igen, lɛk aw Kiŋ dɛn we nɔ mared kin slip na di si. Fɔ mek shɔ se dɛn ɔl de slip togɛda na wan apatmɛnt, bɔt yu gɛt yu yon hamak, ɛn kɔba yusɛf wit yu yon blankit, ɛn slip na yu yon skin.

Di mɔ we a bin de tink bɔt dis harpuoneer, na di mɔ a bin de et fɔ tink bɔt fɔ slip wit am. I bin fayn fɔ tink se bikɔs i bi hapun, in linin ɔ wul, as i bi, nɔ go bi di wan we klin pas ɔl, fɔ tru, i nɔ go bi di bɛst wan. A bigin fɔ twitch ɔlsay. Apat frɔm dat, i bin de let, ɛn mi fayn harpuoneer fɔ de na os ɛn go bed. Lɛ wi se naw, i fɔ fɔdɔm pan mi na midulnɛt—aw a go no frɔm us wikɛd ol i bin dɔn de kam?

“Landlord! A dɔn chenj mi maynd bɔt da harpooneer de.—A nɔ go slip wit am. A go tray di bɛnch ya.”

“Jɔs lɛk aw yu want; a sɔri se a nɔ ebul fɔ spay una tebul klos fɔ matres, ɛn na plaguy rɔf bod ya”—filin fɔ di knot ɛn notch dɛn. “Bɔt wet smɔl, Skrimshander; a dɔn gɛt kapɛnta plen de na di ba—wet, a se, ɛn a go mek una snug inof.” So se na in prokyur di plen; ɛn wit in ol silk hankɛf fɔs fɔ dɔst di bɛnch, wit trɛnk fɔ plan fɔ go fa nia mi bed, di tɛm de grin lɛk apis. Di shavings bin de flay rayt ɛn lɛft; til las las di plen-ayɔn kam bamp agens wan knot we nɔ go pwɛl. Di pɔsin we gɛt di land bin dɔn nia fɔ swɛt in an, ɛn a tɛl am fɔ lɛ i lɛf fɔ wok fɔ ɛvin—di bed bin saf fɔ fit mi, ɛn a nɔ bin no aw ɔl di planin we de na di wɔl go mek ayda dɔŋ pan pain plank. So i gɛda di sheb dɛn wit ɔda grin, ɛn trowe dɛn na di big stov we de na di midul pan di rum, i go du in biznɛs, ɛn lɛf mi na wan brawn stɔdi.

A tek di mɛzhɔ fɔ di bɛnch naw, ɛn a si se i tu shɔt wan fut; bɔt dɛn kin mek dat fayn wit chia. Bɔt i bin tu smɔl wan fut, ɛn di ɔda bɛnch we bin de na di rum bin ay lɛk 4 inch pas di wan we dɛn bin dɔn plan—so dɛn nɔ bin gɛt yok fɔ dɛn. Dɔn a put di fɔs bɛnch lɔng lɔng wan along di wangren klia ples agens di wɔl, lɛf smɔl intaval bitwin, fɔ mek mi bak setul insay. Bɔt i nɔ tu te a kam fɔ no se kol briz de kam oba mi frɔm ɔnda di sil na di winda, we dis plan nɔ go ɛva du atɔl, mɔ as ɔda kɔrɛnt frɔm di rikɛt domɔt mit di wan we kɔmɔt na di winda, ɛn dɛn ɔl tu togɛda fɔm wan siriɔs smɔl smɔl briz we de nia di winda we de nia mi di ples we a bin dɔn tink fɔ slip di nɛt.

Di dɛbul kam wit da harpuoneer de, a tink, bɔt stɔp, a nɔ go ebul fɔ tif wan mach pan am—bolt in domɔt insay, ɛn jomp insay in bed, fɔ mek di nak dɛn we gɛt pawa pas ɔl wek mi? I bin tan lɛk se nɔto bad aidia; bɔt we a tink bɔt am bak, a nɔ gri wit am. Bikɔs udat go no bɔt wetin di nɛks mɔnin, so jɔs afta a kɔmɔt na di rum, di pɔsin we de ple di harpun go dɔn tinap na di ɛntrɛ, ɔl rɛdi fɔ nak mi dɔŋ!

Bɔt stil, we a luk rawnd mi bak, ɛn we a nɔ si ɛni chans fɔ spɛn wan nɛt we a go sɔfa pas nɔmɔ a de na ɔda pɔsin in bed, a bigin fɔ tink se afta ɔl, sɔntɛm a go valyu di bad we aw pipul dɛn de tink bɔt dis man we a nɔ no bɔt we nɔ mek sɛns. Tink se a, a go wet fɔ sɔm tɛm; i go mɔs dɔn de drɔp insay bifo tɛm. A go luk am gud gud wan da tɛm de, ɛn sɔntɛm wi kin bi jolly gud bedfellows afta ɔl—natin nɔ de fɔ tɛl.

Bɔt pan ɔl we di ɔda bɔda dɛn bin de kɔntinyu fɔ kam insay wan, tu, ɛn tri, ɛn go slip, yet no sayn nɔ de fɔ mi harpuoneer.

“Landlɔd!” a se, “us kayn chap i bi—i kin kip dɛn kayn let awa dɛn de ɔltɛm?” I bin at naw pan twɛlv oklɔk.

Di pɔsin we gɛt di land bin laf bak wit in smɔl smɔl laf, ɛn i bin tan lɛk se i bin rili tikl pan sɔntin we a nɔ ebul fɔ ɔndastand. “Nɔ,” i ansa, “jɛnɔral wan na ali bɔd—airley to bed ɛn airley fɔ rayz—yes, na di bɔd we de kech di wom. Bɔt tinayt i go aut fɔ ped, yu si, ɛn a nɔ si wetin na airth de kip am so let, pas nɔmɔ, i kin bi, i nɔ ebul fɔ sɛl in ed.”

“I nɔ ebul fɔ sɛl in ed?—Us kayn bambuz stori dis yu de tɛl mi?” fɔ gɛt wan big big wamat. “Yu de mek lɛk se yu de se, landlord, se dis harpooneer rili ɛnjɔy dis blɛsin Satide nɛt, ɔ fɔ se Sɔnde mɔnin, fɔ pedl in ed rawnd dis tɔŋ?”

Di pɔsin we gɛt di land se: “Na dat, ɛn a tɛl am se i nɔ go ebul fɔ sɛl am ya, di makit dɔn ful-ɔp wit tin dɛn.”

“Wit wetin?” na in a bin ala.

“Wit ed fɔ bi shɔ;nɔto bɔku ed de na di wɔl?”

A tɔk wit kol at se: “A de tɛl yu wetin i bi, landlɔd, i bɛtɛ lɛ yu stɔp fɔ spin da yan de to mi—a nɔ grin.”

“May be not,” i tek wan stik ɛn whittling wan tutpik, “bɔt a raytther guess yu go dɔn brawn if dat ere harpooneer yɛri yu a slanderin’ in ed.”

“A go brok am fɔ am,” a se, naw a de flay insay wan pawa bak na dis unakɔntabl farrago we di landlɔd in yon.

I se: “I dɔn brok a’rɛdi.”

“Brɔk,” a se—“ brok , yu min?”

“Satain, ɛn na di sem rizin we mek i nɔ ebul fɔ sɛl am, a tink se.”

“Landlord,” a se, as a kul lɛk Mawnt Ɛkla insay wan sno-storm—“landlord, stɔp fɔ whittling. Mi ɛn yu fɔ ɔndastand wisɛf, ɛn dat bak witout delay. A kam na yu os ɛn want bed; yu tɛl mi se yu kin jɔs gi mi af wan;se di ɔda haf na wan patikyula harpooneer in yon. Ɛn bɔt dis harpooneer, we a nɔ si yet, yu kɔntinyu fɔ tɛl mi di mɔs mistif ɛn exasperating stori dɛn we de tɛnd fɔ bɔn insay mi wan filin we nɔ kɔmfyut to di man we yu de disayn fɔ mi bed-fello—wan kayn kɔnekshɔn, landlɔd, we na wan intimate ɛn kɔnfidɛns wan insay di ay digri a de aks mi naw fɔ tɔk ɛn tɛl mi udat ɛn wetin dis harpooneer bi, ɛn if a go bi in ɔl rispek sef fɔ spɛn di nɛt wit am. yu go so gud fɔ unsay da stori de bɔt fɔ sɛl in ed, we if na tru a tek am se na gud pruf se dis harpooneer na stark mad, ɛn a nɔ gɛt ɛni aydia fɔ slip wit madman ;

“Wɔl,” di landlɔd se, i de tek lɔng briz, “dat na purty lɔng sarmon fɔ chap we de rip smɔl naw ɛn da tɛm de. Bɔt bi izi, bi izi, dis ya harpooneer we a dɔn de tɛl yu bɔt dɔn jɔs kam frɔm di sawt si, usay i bay bɔku ‘balmed Nyu Ziland ed dɛn (great curios, yu no), ɛn i dɔn sɛl ɔl pan dɛn bɔt wan, ɛn dat wan i de tray fɔ sɛl tinayt, kɔz tumara Sɔnde, ɛn i nɔ go du fɔ de sɛl mɔtalman ed bɔt di strit dɛn we pipul dɛn de go na chɔch I bin want fɔ, las Sɔnde, bɔt a bin stɔp am jɔs lɛk aw i bin de go na do wit 4 ed dɛn we dɛn strɛch pan wan string, fɔ ɔl di ays lɛk wan string ɔf inɔn.”

Dis stori bin klia di sikrit we nɔ bin gɛt fɔ ansa, ɛn sho se di pɔsin we gɛt di land, afta ɔl, nɔ bin gɛt ɛni aidia fɔ ful mi—bɔt di sem tɛm wetin a go tink bɔt wan harpuoneer we bin de kɔmɔt na Satide nɛt klin insay di oli Sabat, we bin de du dis kayn biznɛs we de it pipul dɛn lɛk fɔ sɛl di ed fɔ pipul dɛn we de wɔship aydɔl we dɔn day?

“Dipen pan am, landlord, dat harpooneer na denja man.”

“I de pe reg’lar,” na bin di rijoinder. "Bɔt kam, i de kam dreadful let, yu bin fɔ bɛtɛ fɔ de tɔn flukes—na fayn bed; mi ɛn Sal bin slip na da ere bed de di nɛt we dɛn splais. Plɛnti rum de fɔ tu fɔ kik arawnd na da bed de; na ɔlmayti big bed dat. Wetin mek, bifo wi giv am, Sal bin de put wi Sam ɛn smɔl Jɔni insay di fut fɔ am. Bɔt A bin gɛt wan drim ɛn sprawl lɛk wan nɛt, ɛn sɔm kayn we, Sam bin gɛt pitch na di flɔ, ɛn kam nia fɔ brok in an we, Sal se i nɔ go du, Kam along ya, a go gi una wan glim insay wan jiffy;” ɛn so i se i layt kandul ɛn ol am to mi, ɛn se i go lid di rod. Bɔt a bin tinap we a nɔ bin dɔn mekɔp mi maynd; we i de luk wan klok na di kɔna, i ala se “I vum it’s Sunday—yu nɔ go si da harpooneer de tinayt; i kam fɔ ankɔ sɔmsay—kam along den; do kam; yu nɔ go kam?”

A tink bɔt di tin fɔ smɔl tɛm, dɔn wi go ɔp stej, ɛn dɛn kɛr mi go insay wan smɔl rum, we kol lɛk klam, ɛn we gɛt tin dɛn, fɔ tru, wit wan wɔndaful bed, we bin rili big fɔ ɛni 4 pipul dɛn we de wok wit harpun fɔ slip nia dɛnsɛf.

Di pɔsin we gɛt di land se: “Na de,” ɛn i put di kandul pan wan krayzi ol si chɛst we de du dɛbul duty as was-stand ɛn sɛnta tebul; “de, mek yu fil fayn naw, ɛn gud nɛt fɔ una.” A tɔn rawnd fɔ mek a nɔ luk di bed, bɔt i bin dɔn lɔs.

Fol bak di kɔntapen, a butu oba di bed. Pan ɔl we nɔto ɛni wan pan di wan dɛn we fayn pas ɔl, i bin stil tinap fɔ di skrutiny we dɛn bin ebul fɔ bia. Dɔn a glan rawnd di rum; ɛn apat frɔm di bedstead ɛn di sɛnt tebul, dɛn nɔ bin ebul fɔ si ɛni ɔda fɔnicha we na di ples in yon, pas wan rud shelf, di 4 wɔl dɛn, ɛn wan fayabɔd we gɛt pepa we tinap fɔ wan man we de nak wɛl. Fɔ tin dɛn we nɔto di rum in yon fayn, wan hamak bin de we dɛn tay, ɛn trowe am na grɔn na wan kɔna; bak wan big siman bag, we gɛt di harpuoneer in wodrɔb, we wi nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se insay di ples fɔ wan land trɔnk. Semweso, wan pak we gɛt ɔtlandish bon fish huk dɛn bin de na di shelf oba di faya-ples, ɛn wan lɔng harpun bin tinap na di ed fɔ di bed.

Bɔt wetin na dis na di chɛst? A tek am, ɛn ol am nia di layt, ɛn fil am, ɛn smɛl am, ɛn tray ɔl we a ebul fɔ rich sɔm satisfay kɔnklushɔn bɔt am. A kin kɔmpia am to natin pas wan big domɔt mat, we dɛn mek fayn fayn wan na di ed dɛn wit smɔl smɔl tin dɛn we de mek tin dɛn we tan lɛk di stɛyn pɔkupin kwil dɛn we de rawnd wan Indian mɔkasin. Wan ol ɔ slit bin de na di midul pan dis mat, lɛk aw yu si di sem tin na Sawt Amɛrika poncho dɛn. Bɔt yu tink se i pɔsibul fɔ mek ɛnibɔdi we de wok wit harpoon we dɔn soba go go insay wan domɔt mat, ɛn parade na ɛni Kristian tɔŋ na strit dɛn wit da kayn klos de? A put am, fɔ tray am, ɛn i wet mi dɔŋ lɛk hampa, i nɔ kɔmɔn fɔ shaggy ɛn tik, ɛn a tink se i damp smɔl, lɛk se dis mistiriɔs harpooneer bin dɔn wɛr am fɔ ren de. A bin go ɔp insay de to wan smɔl glas we bin stɔp na di wɔl, ɛn a nɔ ɛva si dis kayn tin na mi layf. A tear misɛf kɔmɔt insay am wit so rɔsh dat a gi misɛf wan kink na mi nɛk.

A sidɔm na di sayd we di bed de, ɛn bigin fɔ tink bɔt dis harpuoneer we de sɛl ed, ɛn in domɔt mat. Afta a tink sɔm tɛm na di bed-sayd, a grap ɛn pul mi mɔnki jakit, dɔn a tinap na di midul na di rum de tink. A den pul mi kot, ɛn tink smɔl mɔ na mi shit sliv. Bɔt we a bigin fɔ fil kol bad bad wan naw, a nɔ drɛs af lɛk aw a bin drɛs, ɛn mɛmba wetin di pɔsin we gɛt di land bin tɔk bɔt di pɔsin we de mek di harpun nɔ kam bak na os atɔl da nɛt de, bikɔs i bin so rili let, a nɔ mek ɛni ado igen, bɔt a jomp kɔmɔt na mi pantalɔn ɛn but, ɛn afta dat a blo di layt fɔdɔm na bed, ɛn kɔmɛnt misɛf to di kia we ɛvin de kia fɔ mi.

If dat matres bin ful-ɔp wit kɔn-kɔb ɔ brok brok krok, notin nɔ de fɔ tɛl, bɔt a bin rol bɔt wan gud tin, ɛn a nɔ bin ebul fɔ slip fɔ lɔng tɛm. Las las a slid go insay wan layt doz, ɛn a bin dɔn rili nia fɔ mek gud ɔf go na di land na Nɔd, we a yɛri wan ebi ebi fut fɔdɔm na di pasej, ɛn si wan layt we de shayn kam insay di rum frɔm ɔnda di domɔt.

Masta sev mi, a tink se, dat fɔ bi di harpooneer, di infernal ed-peddler. Bɔt a bin ledɔm pafɛkt wan, ɛn a bin disayd nɔ fɔ tɔk wan wɔd te dɛn tɔk to mi. We di strenja ol layt na wan an, ɛn da sem Nyu Ziland ed de na di ɔda an, i go insay di rum, ɛn i nɔ luk to di bed, i put in kandul fayn fayn wan frɔm mi na di flɔ na wan kɔna, dɔn i bigin fɔ wok fa fawe pan di knot kɔd dɛn na di big bag we a bin dɔn tɔk bɔt bifo se i de na di rum. A bin rili want fɔ si in fes, bɔt i bin kip am avɔyd fɔ sɔm tɛm we i bin de wok fɔ pul di bag in mɔt. Bɔt dis akɔmplit, i tɔn rawnd—we, gud ɛvin! na big big tin fɔ si! Na da kayn fes de! I bin gɛt dak, pepul, yɔlɔ kɔlɔ, ya ɛn de i bin stik oba wit big blak luk skwea dɛn. Yes, na jɔs lɛk aw a bin de tink, na bad bad bedfelɔ; i dɔn de fɛt, dɛn kɔt am bad bad wan, ɛn na ya i de, jɔs frɔm di dɔktɔ we de du di ɔpreshɔn. Bɔt da tɛm de i chans fɔ tɔn in fes so to di layt, dat a si klia wan se dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ bi stik-plasta atɔl, dɛn blak skwea dɛn de na in chɛst. Dɛn na bin sɔm kayn dɔti dɛn. Fɔs, a nɔ bin no wetin fɔ mek wit dis; bɔt i nɔ tu te, wan tin we a tink bɔt di trut kam na mi maynd. A mɛmba wan stori bɔt wan wayt man—we na bin wɛlman bak—we, we i fɔdɔm wit di pipul dɛn we de it pipul dɛn, dɛn bin dɔn tatu am. A bin dɔn tɔk se dis man we de wok wit harpu, we i bin de travul fɔ go fa fa say, go mɔs dɔn mit wit wan kayn advantej we tan lɛk dis. Ɛn wetin na am, a bin tink, afta ɔl! Na in nɔmɔ de na do; man kin ɔnɛs pan ɛni kayn skin. Bɔt afta dat, wetin fɔ mek pan in kɔmpleks we nɔ de na di wɔl, da pat de, a min, we de ledɔm rawnd, ɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnitin fɔ du wit di skwea dɛn we dɛn de tatu. Fɔ tru, i nɔ go bi natin pas gud kot fɔ tropik tan; bɔt a nɔ ɛva yɛri se hot san de tan wan wayt man to wan pepul yɔlɔ wan. Bɔt, a nɔ bin ɛva go na di Sawt Si; ɛn sɔntɛm di san we bin de de bin mek dɛn kayn wɔndaful tin ya apin to di skin. Naw, we ɔl dɛn aidia dɛn ya bin de pas tru mi lɛk laytin, dis man we de ple hapun nɔ bin ɛva notis mi atɔl. Bɔt, afta sɔm prɔblɛm we i dɔn opin in bag, i bigin fɔ fumbling insay, ɛn naw i pul wan kayn tomahawk, ɛn wan walet we tan lɛk sil skin wit di ia. We i put dɛn tin ya na di ol chɛst we de midul di rum, dɔn i tek di Nyu Ziland ed—na tin we rili bad—ɛn i kram am dɔŋ insay di bag. Naw i pul in hat—na nyu bif hat—we a kam nia fɔ siŋ wit fresh sɔprayz. No ia nɔ bin de na in ed—nɔbɔdi nɔ bin de fɔ tɔk bɔt at ɔl—natin nɔ bin de pas wan smɔl skel-knot we bin twist ɔp na in fɔɛd. In bold pepul ed naw bin de luk fɔ ɔl di wɔl lɛk mildewed skull. If di strenja nɔ bin tinap bitwin mi ɛn di domɔt, a fɔ dɔn bolt kɔmɔt de kwik pas aw a bin fɔ dɔn bolt dina.

Ivin as i bin de, a bin tink se sɔntin de slip kɔmɔt na di winda, bɔt na di sɛkɔn flɔ bak. Mi nɔto kɔvayd, bɔt wetin fɔ mek pan dis pepul raskal we de sɛl ed ɔltogɛda pas mi kɔmprɛhɛnshɔn. Ignorance na di mama ɔ papa we de fred, ɛn bikɔs a nɔ gɛt plus ɛn kɔnfyus atɔl bɔt di strenja, a kɔnfɛs se a bin de fred am naw lɛk se na di dɛbul insɛf bin dɔn brok insay mi rum so na nɛt. Infakt, a bin so fred am dat a nɔ bin gem inof jɔs da tɛm de fɔ adrɛs am, ɛn aks fɔ ansa we satisfay bɔt wetin bin tan lɛk se i nɔ go ebul fɔ ɛksplen insay am.

Dis tɛm ya, i kɔntinyu fɔ du di biznɛs fɔ pul in klos, ɛn las las i sho in chɛst ɛn in an dɛn. As a de liv, dɛn bin de chɛk dɛn pat dɛn ya we dɛn kɔba wit di sem skwea dɛn we in fes gɛt; in bak sɛf bin de ɔlsay na di sem dak skwea dɛn; i bin tan lɛk se i bin de insay wan Tati Ia Wɔ, ɛn jɔs rɔnawe pan am wit wan stik-plasta shit. Stil mɔ, in yon leg dɛn bin gɛt mak, lɛk se wan pak we gɛt dak grin frog dɛn de rɔn go ɔp di yɔŋ pam tik dɛn trɔnk. Naw i bin rili klia se i go mɔs bi sɔm bad bad savayj ɔ ɔda wan we dɛn ship pan wan wɛlman na di Sawt Si, ɛn so i land na dis Kristian kɔntri. A bin shek we a tink bɔt am. Wan pɔsin we de sɛl ed bak—sɔntɛm na in yon brɔda dɛn ed. I kin tek wan fansi to mi yon—ɛvin! luk da tomahawk de!

Bɔt tɛm nɔ bin de fɔ shek shek, bikɔs naw di savayj bin de du sɔntin we bin rili kɔle mi atɛnshɔn, ɛn mek a biliv se fɔ tru, i fɔ bi hitin. We i go to in ebi ebi grego, ɔ wrapall, ɔ dreadnaught, we i bin dɔn hang pan chia bifo, i fumbl insay di poket dɛn, ɛn prodyuz fɔ lɔng tɛm wan kɔriɔs smɔl difɔm imej wit wan hunch na in bak, ɛn jɔs di kɔlɔ we wan tri dez ol Kongo bebi gɛt. We a mɛmba di ed we dɛn bin dɔn ɛmbas, fɔs i bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ a tink se dis blak manikin na rial bebi we dɛn kip di sem we. Bɔt we a si se i nɔ limber atɔl, ɛn i bin de shayn fayn fayn wan lɛk polish ebony, a kam fɔ no se i fɔ bi natin pas wud aydɔl, we fɔ tru, i pruv se na so i bi. Fɔ naw di savayj go ɔp to di ɛmti faya-ples, ɛn pul di pepa faya-bɔd, sɛt dis smɔl hunch-bak imej, lɛk tɛnpin, bitwin di andirons. Di chimini jamb ɛn ɔl di brik dɛn we bin de insay bin rili sooty, so a bin tink se dis faya-ples mek wan smɔl shrine ɔ chapel we rili fit fɔ in Kongo aydɔl.

Naw a skru mi yay tranga wan to di af-af ayd imej, a fil bɔt sik at izi da tɛm de—fɔ si wetin de nɛks fɔ fala. Fɔs i kin pul lɛk tu anful shavings na in grego poket, ɛn put dɛn fayn fayn wan bifo di aydɔl; dɔn i le smɔl ship biskit pan am ɛn put di faya we de kɔmɔt na di lamp, ɛn layt di shavings fɔ mek sakrifays faya. Naw, afta we i dɔn snɛp bɔku tɛm kwik kwik wan insay di faya, ɛn i stil pul in finga dɛn kwik kwik wan (we i bin tan lɛk se i de bɔn dɛn bad bad wan), i bin dɔn ebul fɔ pul di biskit; dɔn i blo di ɔt ɛn ashis smɔl, i mek wan pɔlitikal ɔf fɔ am to di smɔl nigro. Bɔt i bin tan lɛk se di smɔl dɛbul nɔ bin lɛk dis kayn dray kayn fare atɔl; i nɔ bin ɛva muf in lip dɛn. Ɔl dɛn strenj antics ya bin de wit stil strenja guttural noises frɔm di devotee, we i bin tan lɛk se i de pre insay wan sing-song ɔ ɔdasay de siŋ sɔm ​​pegan sam ɔ ɔda wan, we in fes bin de twitch rawnd di we we nɔ natin. Las las we i dɔn ɔt di faya, i tek di aydɔl ɔp we i nɔ rili gɛt sɛrimɔni, ɛn bag am bak na in grego pɔkit as i nɔ tek tɛm lɛk se na spɔtman we de bag wan day wudkɔk.

Ɔl dɛn kwik prosiding ya bin mek a nɔ fil fayn mɔ ɛn mɔ, ɛn we a si am naw de sho strɔng simptom dɛn fɔ dɔn in biznɛs ɔpreshɔn, ɛn jomp insay bed wit mi, a bin tink se na ay tɛm, naw ɔ nɔ ɛva, bifo dɛn ɔt di layt, fɔ brok di spɛl we a bin dɔn tay mi fɔ lɔng tɛm.

Bɔt di intaval we a spɛn fɔ tink bɔt wetin fɔ tɔk, na bin wan we de kil pɔsin. I tek in tomahawk na di tebul, i chɛk di ed fɔ am fɔ smɔl tɛm, dɔn i ol am to di layt, wit in mɔt na di handel, i pul big big klawd dɛn we gɛt tabak smok. Di nɛks tɛm di layt dɔn ɔt, ɛn dis wayl kanibal, tomahawk bitwin in tit, spring insay bed wit mi. A bin de siŋ aut, a nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɛp am naw; ɛn gi wan grɔmbul wantɛm wantɛm fɔ sɔprayz i bigin fɔ fil mi.

We a de stɔp sɔntin, a nɔ no wetin, a rol kɔmɔt nia am agens di wɔl, dɔn a kɔl am, ilɛk udat ɔ ɛnitin we i bi, fɔ kwayɛt, ɛn mek a grap ɛn layt di lamp bak. Bɔt in guttural rispɔns dɛn bin satisfay mi wantɛm wantɛm se i bɔt i nɔ ɔndastand wetin a min.

“Udat-e debel yu?”—i las se—“yu nɔ tɔk-e, dam-mi, a kil-e.” Ɛn so we a de tɔk di tomahawk we gɛt layt bigin fɔ gro bɔt mi na dak.

“Landlord, fɔ Gɔd in sek, Pita Kɔfin!” I ala se: “Landlɔd! Wach! Kɔfin! Enjɛl dɛn! sev mi!”

“Tɔk-e! tɛl-ee mi udat-ee bi, ɔ dam-mi, a kil-e!” bak di kanibal grɔmbul, we in horrid flourishings of di tomahawk skata di hot tabak ashis bɔt mi te a tink se mi linin go gɛt faya. Bɔt tɛnki ɛvin, na da tɛm de di landlɔd kam insay di rum layt in an, ɛn jomp frɔm di bed a rɔn go nia am.

I grin bak se: “Nɔ fred naw, Kwik ya nɔ go ambɔg wan ia na yu ed.”

A ala se: “Stɔp yu grin, ɛn wetin mek yu nɔ tɛl mi se da infernal harpooneer de na pɔsin we de it pipul dɛn?”

“A bin tink se una no am;—a nɔ bin tɛl una, i bin de peddlin’ ed rawnd tɔŋ?—bɔt tɔn flukes bak ɛn go slip. Queequeg, luk ya—yu sabbee mi, a sabbee—yu dis man slip yu—yu sabbee?”

“Me sabbee plenty”—Kwik bin grɔmbul, i bin de blo in paip ɛn sidɔm ɔp na bed.

I ad se: “Yu gettee in,” i mek sayn to mi wit in tomahawk, ɛn trowe di klos dɛn na wan say. I nɔ bin jɔs du dis insay sivil, bɔt i bin rili du am fayn ɛn i bin de ɛp pipul dɛn fɔ ɛp ɔda pipul dɛn. A tinap de luk am fɔ smɔl tɛm. Fɔ ɔl in tatu dɛn i bin on di ɔl wan klin, kɔmɛli luk kanibal. Wetin na ɔl dis fus we a dɔn de mek bɔt, a tink to misɛf—di man na mɔtalman jɔs lɛk mi: i gɛt jɔs di sem rizin fɔ fred mi, jɔs lɛk aw a fɔ fred am. I bɛtɛ fɔ slip wit pɔsin we de it pɔsin we gɛt sɛns pas Kristian we dɔn chak.

“Landlord,” a se, “tɛl am fɔ stash in tomahawk de, ɔ paip, ɔ ɛnitin we yu kɔl am; tɛl am fɔ stɔp fɔ smok, in shot, ɛn a go tɔn insay wit am. Bɔt a nɔ fansi fɔ gɛt man we de smok na bed wit mi. I denja. Apat frɔm dat, a nɔ gɛt inshɔrans.”

We dɛn tɛl Kwik dis, i gri wantɛm wantɛm, ɛn i mek sayn bak wit pɔlitiks fɔ mek a go insay bed—i rol ova to wan say lɛk aw i se—“A nɔ go tɔch wan leg pan una.”

A se: “Gud nɛt, landlɔd, yu kin go.”

A tɔn insay, ɛn a nɔ ɛva slip bɛtɛ na mi layf.
