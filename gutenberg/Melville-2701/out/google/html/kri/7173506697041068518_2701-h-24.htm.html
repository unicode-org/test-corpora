CHAPTER 131. Di Pequod Mit Di Gladi.

Di intens Pequod bin de sel go bifo; di wef dɛn we bin de rɔl ɛn di de dɛn bin de go; di layf-buoy-kɔfin stil de swing layt wan; ɛn dɛn bin tɔk bɔt wan ɔda ship, we dɛn nɔ bin kɔl am di rɔŋ nem di Delayt. As i de kam nia, ɔl in yay dɛn bin de pan in brayt bim dɛn, we dɛn kɔl shia, we, insay sɔm wɛl-ship dɛn, kin krɔs di kwata-dɛk we ay et ɔ nayn fit; we de sav fɔ kɛr di spay, we nɔ gɛt rig, ɔ we nɔ ebul fɔ waka.

Na di strenja in shia dɛn bin si di wayt rib dɛn we bin brok brok, ɛn sɔm smɔl plank dɛn we bin skata, we bin dɔn bi wɛl-bot trade; bɔt naw yu si tru dis ship we dɔn pwɛl, klia wan lɛk aw yu de si tru di skel we dɔn pul, we nɔ gɛt af-af, ɛn we de blech ɔs.

“Yu dɔn si di Wait Wɛl?”

"Luk!" di kapten we gɛt olo chɛk ansa frɔm in tafrail; ɛn wit in trɔmpɛt i sho di bot we dɔn pwɛl.

“Yu dɔn kil am?”

Di ɔda wan ansa wit sɔri-at, ɛn i bin de luk wan rawnd hamak we bin de na di dɛk, we sɔm pipul dɛn we nɔ bin gɛt nɔys bin bizi fɔ siŋ togɛda, ɛn i bin tɔk se: “Di harpun nɔ fɔj yet we go ɛva du dat.”

“Nɔto fɔj!” ɛn we i de snɛp Pɛt in lɛvɛl ayɛn frɔm in kroch, Eab ol am, ɛn ala se—“Luk una, Nantucketer; na ya na dis an a de ol in day! Dɛn barb ya we blɔd dɔn vɛks, ɛn laytin dɔn mek dɛn vɛks; ɛn a swɛ se a go vɛks dɛn triplɛ na da hot ples de biɛn di fin, usay di Wait Wɛl kin fil in layf we i dɔn aks mɔ!”

“Dɔn Gɔd kip yu, ol man—si dat”—i de pɔynt to di hamak—“A de bɛr bɔt wan pan fayv strɔng man dɛn, we bin de alayv yestede nɔmɔ; bɔt dɛn bin dɔn day bifo nɛt. Na da wan de nɔmɔ a de bɛr; di ɔda wan dɛn bin bɛr bifo dɛn day; yu de sel pan dɛn grev.” Dɔn i tɔn to in kru—“Una rɛdi de? put di plank da tɛm de na di rel, ɛn es di bɔdi; so, so—O!Gɔd”—advans go to di hamak wit an dɛn we dɛn es ɔp—“mek di layf bak ɛn di layf——”

“Bres fɔ go bifo! Up helm!” Eab kray lɛk laytin to in man dɛn.

Bɔt di Pequod we bin stat wantɛm wantɛm nɔ bin kwik fɔ rɔnawe pan di sawnd fɔ di splash we di bɔdi bin mek i nɔ tu te we i de nak di si; nɔto so kwik, fɔ tru, bɔt dat sɔm pan di bɔbul dɛn we de flay go dɔn sprinkl in bɔdi wit dɛn gɔst baptizim.

As Eab de glid naw frɔm di Dilayt we in at bin pwɛl, di strenj layf-buoy we bin de hang na di Pequod in stern bin kam insay klia rilif.

“Ha!ya!, luk ya, man dɛn!” wan vɔys we de sho wetin go apin bifo tɛm bin kray we i bin de wek. “Na fɔ natin, una strenja dɛn, una de flay wi sɔri bɛrin, una bɔt tɔn wi una tafrɛl fɔ sho wi una kɔfin!”

Bɔt pan ɔl we dɛn difrɛn so insay, di difrɛns bin jɔs de insay shed ɛn shado dɛn we nɔ de; i bin tan lɛk se dɛn tu pipul dɛn de na wan; na di sɛks nɔmɔ, lɛk se, na in bin mek dɛn difrɛn.

We yu bin de ɔp, lɛk kiŋ zaza ɛn kiŋ, i bin tan lɛk se di san de gi dis saful briz to dis si we gɛt maynd ɛn we de rɔl; ivin as yawo to ɔkɔ. Ɛn na di layn we de girdling na di ɔrayzin, wan saf ɛn shek shek muvmɛnt—we dɛn kin si mɔ ya na di ekweta—bin de sho di lɔv, throbbing trɔst, di lɔv alam, we di po yawo gi in bɔdi wit.

Dɛn tay am ɛn twist am; gnarled ɛn knot wit wrinkles; haggardly firm ɛn nɔ de chenj; in yay dɛn de shayn lɛk kol, we stil de shayn na di ashis we dɔn pwɛl; Eab we nɔ bin de shek shek bin tinap na di klin ples na mɔnin; i es in ɛlmɛt we skata we gɛt brɔw to di fayn gyal in fɔɛd na ɛvin.

Oh, imɔtal infancy, ɛn inosɛns fɔ di azure! Pipul dɛn we gɛt wing we wi nɔ de si we de ple ple ɔlsay rawnd wi! Swit pikin tɛm we di briz ɛn skay de! aw una nɔ bin no bɔt di bad tin we bin de apin to Eab we bin dɔn lɔs! Bɔt na so a dɔn si smɔl Miriam ɛn Mata, ɛlf dɛn we gɛt laf yay, de gambol rawnd dɛn ol sire we nɔ de pe atɛnshɔn; spɔt wit di sɛklɔ we gɛt singed lɔk dɛn we bin de gro na di mared fɔ da krata we dɔn bɔn na in bren.

We Eab bin de krɔs di dɛk smɔl smɔl frɔm di skata, i bin ledɔm oba di sayd ɛn wach aw in shado we bin de na di wata bin de sink ɛn sink to in yay, di mɔ ɛn mɔ i bin de tray fɔ chuk di dip. Bɔt di fayn fayn smel dɛn we bin de na da enchanted air de bin rili tan lɛk se dɛn dɔn pul di kansa tin we bin de na in sol fɔ smɔl tɛm. Dat gladi, gladi gladi briz, da winsome skay, bin du las strok ɛn kɔt kɔt am; di stɛp-mama wɔl, we bin so lɔng kruk—we bin de protɛkt—naw bin de trowe in an dɛn we gɛt lɔv rawnd in stɛp-an nɛk, ɛn i bin tan lɛk se i bin de gladi fɔ kray oba am, lɛk se i de kray pan wan, se ilɛksɛf i bin want fɔ du am ɛn mek mistek, i bin stil ebul fɔ fɛn am na in at fɔ sev ɛn fɔ blɛs. Frɔm ɔnda in hat we bin slɔp, Eab drɔp wan kray wata na di si; ɛn ɔl di Pasifik nɔ bin gɛt bɔku jɛntri lɛk da wan smɔl drɔp de.

Starbuck bin si di ol man; si am, aw i bin ledɔm bad bad wan oba di sayd; ɛn i bin tan lɛk se i yɛri insay in yon tru at di kray we nɔ gɛt wan mɛzhɔ we de tif kɔmɔt na di sɛntrɔm fɔ di kol at we bin de rawnd. I tek tɛm mek i nɔ tɔch am, ɔ mek i notis am, i stil kam nia am, ɛn tinap de.

Eab bin tɔn.

“Stabɔk!”

"Sa."

“O, Starbuck! na wan mild, mild briz, ɛn wan mild lukin skay. Na da kayn de de—we rili swit lɛk dis—a bin bit mi fɔs wɛl—wan bɔy-harpuoneer we ol ettin ia!Fɔti—fɔti—fɔti ia bifo!—fɔti ia we a bin de kɔntinyu fɔ kech wɛl! fɔti ia we a nɔ bin gɛt wanwɔd, ɛn denja, ɛn storm-taym fɔti ia we a nɔ bin gɛt sɔri-at! si! ɛni sɔri-at frɔm di grɔn kɔntri we nɔ gɛt—o, taya! an, ɛn brok di wɔl in fresh bred to mi mold krɔkrɔs—fa, ful oceans away, frɔm da yɔŋ gyal-wɛf we a mared pas fifti, ɛn lɛf bɔt wan dent na mi mared pilo—wɛf?—rather a widow with her husband alive! dɔn, di krayzi, di fred, di blɔd we de bɔl ɛn di smok we de smok, we ol Eab dɔn vɛks bad bad wan fɔ rɔnata in animal—we na dɛbul pas man!—aye, aye! oar, ɛn di lans? ol, so rili, rili ol, Starbuck? Intolerably old? Kloz! Stand klos to mi, Starbuck; Mek a luk insay mɔtalman yay; I bɛtɛ pas fɔ luk insay si ɔ skay; I bɛtɛ pas fɔ luk Gɔd. bay di grɔn land; Na di brayt faya-stɔŋ-ston! Dis na di majik glas, man; A si mi wɛf ɛn mi pikin na yu yay. Nɔ, nɔ; Stay on board, on board!—Lɔwa nɔto we a du dat; Wen branded Ahab giv chase tu Moby Dik.Dat hazad nɔ go bi yu yon, nɔto wit di fa fa os we a de si na da yay de!”

“O, mi Kpɛtɛn! mi Kpɛtɛn! nobul sol! grand ol at, afta ɔl! wetin mek ɛnibɔdi fɔ gi chase to da fish we dɛn et! Away wit mi! lɛ wi flay dɛn wata ya we de kil! lɛ wi go na os! Wɛf ɛn pikin, sɛf, na Starbuck in—wɛf ɛn pikin fɔ in brɔda, sista, ple-felɔ yɔŋ; ivin lɛk aw yu, sa, na di wɛf ɛn pikin fɔ yu lov, longing, paternal old age let us away!—dis instant mek a chenj di kɔs, aw i go laf, O mi Kaptɛn, a tink se, sa, dɛn gɛt sɔm kayn mild blu dez, ivin lɛk dis, na Nantucket.”

“Dɛn dɔn, dɛn dɔn si dɛn. A dɔn si dɛn—sɔm sɔm dez dɛn na mɔnin. Na lɛk dis tɛm—yes, na in noon nap naw—di bɔbɔ de wek wit layf;i sidɔm na bed;ɛn in mama tɛl am bɔt mi, fɔ kanibal ol mi; aw a de ɔdasay na di dip, bɔt a go kam bak yet fɔ dans am bak.”

"'Na mi Meri, mi Meri insɛf! I bin prɔmis se dɛn fɔ kɛr mi bɔy pikin, ɛvri mɔnin, go na di il fɔ kech di fɔs luk pan in papa in sel! Yɛs, yes! nɔ mɔ! i dɔn! wi de go na Nantucket! Kam, mi Kpɛntin, stɔdi aut di kɔs, ɛn lɛ wi go! Si, si! di bɔbɔ in fes frɔm di winda! di bɔbɔ in an de pan di il!”

Bɔt Eab nɔ bin ebul fɔ si am; lɛk frut tik we dɔn pwɛl i shek, ɛn trowe in las apul we dɔn rɔtin na di grɔn.

“Wetin na am, wetin na tin we nɔ gɛt nem, we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ ɔndastand, we nɔ de na di wɔl; us cozening, hidden lord and master, ɛn cruel, remorseless emperor de kɔmand mi;dat agens ɔl di natural lovings and longings, a so kɔntinyu fɔ push, ɛn krawd, ɛn jam misɛf pan ɔl di tɛm; recklessly mek mi rɛdi fɔ du wetin insay mi yon proper, natural heart, I durst not so much as dare? Is Eab, Eab?Na mi, Gɔd, ɔ udat, de es dis an? are turned round and round in dis world, and Fate is the handspike, ɛn dis si we nɔ gɛt sawnd Luk! briz we nɔ gɛt bɛtɛ trɛnk, ɛn skay we de mek i fil lɛk se i de smɛl naw, lɛk se i de blo frɔm wan ples we de fa fawe, ɛn di wan dɛn we de kɔt gras de slip midul di hay we dɛn dɔn kɔt nyu wan, wi ɔl de slip na di fil. as las ia swɛt dɛn bin de trowe dɔŋ, ɛn lɛf na di say dɛn we dɛn kɔt af-af—Stabɔk!”

Bɔt blanched to a corpse’s hue wit despair, di Mate bin dɔn tif go.

Eab krɔs di dɛk fɔ luk di ɔda say; bɔt i bigin wit tu yay dɛn we de sho, we nɔ de chenj na di wata we de de. Fedallah bin de ledɔm oba di sem rel we nɔ de muv.





CHAPTER 133. Di Chase—Fɔs De.

Da nɛt de, insay di mid-watch, we di ol man—as i kin du insay intaval dɛn—stɛp kɔmɔt na di skata we i bin de ledɔm insay, ɛn go na in pivot-ol, wantɛm wantɛm i push in fes faya wan, i snuf di si ɛya lɛk aw wan sɛnsful ship in dɔg go want, fɔ kam nia sɔm barbarous ayland. I bin tɔk se wan wɛl fɔ dɔn de nia. I nɔ tu te, ɔl di wachman dɛn bin de si da spɛshal smel de, we sɔntɛnde di sperm wɛl we de alayv kin gi am fa fawe; ɛn ɛni man we de wok na di bot nɔ bin sɔprayz we, afta we i dɔn inspɛkt di kɔmpas, ɛn afta dat di dɔg-vɛn, ɛn afta dat i dɔn no di rayt we aw di smel de nia as i pɔsibul, Eab bin tɛl kwik kwik wan fɔ chenj di rod we di ship de go smɔl, ɛn fɔ mek di sel shɔt.

Di akyu polisi we de dikte dɛn muvmɛnt ya bin inof vindikɛt we de brek, bay we dɛn si wan lɔng slip na di si dairekt ɛn lɔng bifo, we smol lɛk ɔyl, ɛn we tan lɛk di plet wata wrinkles we de nia am, di polish mɛtal-layk mak dɛn fɔ sɔm swift tayd-rip, na di mɔt fɔ wan dip, rɔpid strim.

“Man di mast-hed dɛm! Kɔl ɔl an!”

Tɛnda wit di bɔt dɛn fɔ tri klab hanspayk dɛn na di fɔkastɔl dɛk, Daggoo wek di wan dɛn we de slip wit dɛn kayn jɔjmɛnt klap dɛn de we i tan lɛk se dɛn de pul briz frɔm di skata, so wantɛm wantɛm dɛn apia wit dɛn klos na dɛn an.

“Wetin d’ye si?” Eab kray, i flat in fes na di skay.

“Natin, natin sa!” na di sawnd we bin de hail dɔŋ fɔ ansa.

“T’gallant sails!—stunsails!, dɔŋ ɛn ɔp, ɛn na ɔl tu di say dɛn!”

Ɔl di sel we dɛn dɔn sɛt, naw i trowe di layf-layn, we dɛn bin dɔn kip fɔ shek am to di men kiŋ-mast ed; ɛn insay sɔm tɛm dɛn bin de es am go de, we, we, we i bin de jɔs tu-tɛd pan di rod ɔp, ɛn we i bin de luk bifo tru di ɔrizɔntal vakɛns bitwin di men-tɔp-sɛl ɛn di tɔp-galant-sɛl, i rayz wan kray we tan lɛk gɔl na di ays. “Na de i de blo!—na de i de blo! Na hump lɛk sno-hil! Na Mobi Dik!”

Faya di kray we i bin tan lɛk se di tri luk-ɔut dɛn tek am wan tɛm, di man dɛn we bin de na di dɛk bin rɔsh go na di rig fɔ si di fambul wɛl we dɛn bin dɔn de rɔnata fɔ lɔng tɛm. Eab bin dɔn gɛt in las ples naw, sɔm fit ɔp di ɔda luk-ɔut dɛn, Tashtego bin tinap jɔs ɔnda am pan di kap pan di tɔp-gallant-mast, so dat di Indian in ed bin dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ Eab in il. Frɔm dis ayt, dɛn bin de si di wɛl naw sɔm mayl ɔ so bifo, pan ɛvri rol na di si i de sho in ay spak spak hump, ɛn ɔltɛm i de jet in saylɛn spɔt na di ays. Fɔ di wan dɛn we nɔ biliv pan di bot, i bin tan lɛk di sem saylɛns spɔt we dɛn bin dɔn si fɔ lɔng tɛm na di Atlantik ɛn Indian Oshɔn dɛn we di mun bin de shayn.

“Ɛn yu tink se nɔbɔdi nɔ si am bifo?” Eab bin kray, ɛn i bin de prez di man dɛn we bin sidɔm rawnd am.

Tashtego bin tɔk se: “A si am klos to da sem tɛm de, sa, we Kpɛntin Eab bin si am, ɛn a kray.”

“Nɔto di sem instant; nɔto di sem—nɔ, di dablɔn na mi yon, Fate bin kip di dablɔn fɔ mi. Na mi nɔmɔ; nɔbɔdi pan una nɔ bin fɔ dɔn ebul fɔ rayz di Wait Wɛl fɔs. Na de i de blo!—na de i de blo!—na de i de blo! Na de bak!—na de bak!” i kray, wit lɔng lɔng, lɔng tɛm, mɛtɔdik tɔyn dɛn, we i gri wit di we aw di wɛl in jet dɛn we pɔsin kin si kin lɔng smɔl smɔl. “I go saund! In stunsails! Daun top-gallant-sails! Stand by tri bot. Mista Starbuck, memba, stay on board, en kip di ship. Helm de! Luff, luff a point! So; stedi, man, stedi! Dere go flukes! No, no; onli blak wata! Ol redi di bot dem de? Stand by, stand by! Lower mi, Mr. Starbuck lower, lower,—kwik, kwik!” ɛn i slid tru di ays go na di dɛk.

Stubb kray se: “I de go stret na di ples we di briz de blo, sa, i de kɔmɔt nia wi, i nɔ ebul fɔ si di ship yet.”

“Bi mumu, man! Stand nia di bres! Had dɔŋ di ɛlm!—brɛs ɔp! Shiv am!—shiv am!—So; wɛl dat! Bot, bot!”

I nɔ tu te, dɛn drɔp ɔl di bot dɛn pas Starbuck in yon; ɔl di bot-sɛl dɛn we dɛn dɔn sɛt—ɔl di padul dɛn de flay; wit rippling swiftness, shot to leeward; ɛn Eab bin de bifo di biginin. Wan pale, day-glimmer bin layt Fedallah in yay dɛn we bin dɔn sink; wan bad bad muvmɛnt bin de swɛt in mɔt.

Lɛk nautilus shel dɛn we nɔ gɛt nɔys, dɛn layt prow dɛn bin de spid pas na di si; bɔt na smɔl smɔl nɔmɔ dɛn bin de nia di ɛnimi. As dɛn de kam nia am, di si bin de gro mɔ ɛn mɔ; i bin tan lɛk se i de drɔ kap oba in wef dɛn; i bin tan lɛk se na midulnɛt-mɛdɔ, so i bin de skata wit kol at. Afta lɔng tɛm, di ɔntinman we nɔ bin de blo bin kam so nia in animal we i bin tan lɛk se i nɔ bin de tink, dat in wan ol hump we bin de shayn bin de klia wan, i bin de slayv along di si lɛk se na tin we nɔ de nia ɔda pipul dɛn, ɛn i bin de kɔntinyu fɔ put am insay wan ring we de rɔn we gɛt fayn fayn, we gɛt wul, we gɛt grin fom. I si di big big, involv wrinkles na di ed we bin de projɛkt smɔl biɛn. Bifo am, fa fawe na di saf saf Turkish-rugged wata, di wayt shado we de shayn frɔm in brayt, milk fɔɛd, wan myuzik ripl we bin de ple ple wit di shed; ɛn biɛn, di blu wata dɛn bin de flɔd wan bay wan insay di vali we bin de muv we i bin de wek we i nɔ bin de chenj; ɛn na di tu an dɛn, brayt bɔbul dɛn bin de grap ɛn dans nia in sayd. Bɔt dɛn tin ya bin brok bak bay di layt fut finga dɛn fɔ bɔku bɔku gay fɔl dɛn we bin de flay saful saful na di si, we bin de chenj chenj wit dɛn fitful flay; ɛn lɛk sɔm flag-staf dɛn we de rayz frɔm di pent hul fɔ wan argosy, di lɔng bɔt brok brok pol fɔ wan lans we dɛn jɔs dɔn projɛkt frɔm di wayt wɛl in bak; ɛn wan wan tɛm wan pan di klawd we gɛt sɔft fut fɔl dɛn we de flay, ɛn de skim go ɛn kam lɛk kanopi oba di fish, sidɔm kwayɛt wan ɛn rɔk pan dis tik, di lɔng tel fɛda dɛn de rɔn lɛk penɔn.

We di de bigin fɔ blo, dɛn bin de put pipul dɛn bak na di tri mast-hɛd dɛn di rayt tɛm.

“Yu si am?” Eab kray afta i alaw smɔl ples fɔ mek di layt skata.

“Nɔ si natin, sa.”

“Tɔn ɔl yu an dɛn ɔp ɛn mek sel! i de travul fast pas aw a bin tink;—di top-gallant sel dɛn!—aye, dɛn fɔ dɔn kip dɛn pan am ɔl nɛt. Bɔt i nɔ mata—’tis bɔt i de rɛst fɔ di rɔsh.”

Na ya dɛn se, dis pertinacious pursuit fɔ wan patikyula wɛl, kɔntinyu tru de to nɛt, ɛn tru nɛt to de, na tin we nɔbɔdi nɔ si yet na di Sawt si fisha. Bikɔs na so di wɔndaful skil, prescience of experience, ɛn kɔnfidɛns we nɔbɔdi nɔ go ebul fɔ win we sɔm big big natura genius dɛn bitwin di Nantucket kɔmanda dɛn gɛt; dat frɔm di simpul ɔbshɔbishɔn fɔ wan wɛl we dɛn las diskrayb am, dɛn go, ɔnda sɔm givɛn sikɔstɛms, prɛti kɔrɛkt wan fɔ tɔk ɔl tu di dairekshɔn we i go kɔntinyu fɔ swim fɔ sɔm tɛm, we i nɔ de si am, ɛn bak di prɔbabl rit we i go go bifo insay da tɛm de. Ɛn, insay dɛn kes ya, sɔm kayn we as payɔt, we i de bɔt fɔ lɔs si wan kɔst, we in jenɛral trenin i no gud gud wan, ɛn we i want fɔ go bak to bak kwik kwik wan bak, bɔt na sɔm ɔda tɛm; lɛk as dis payɔt tinap nia in kɔmpas, ɛn tek di prɛsis biɛrin fɔ di kap we yu de si naw, fɔ mek i go mɔs hit rayt di rimot, edland we pɔsin nɔ de si, we dɛn go dɔn go fɛn: na so di fishaman, na in kɔmpas, wit di wɛl; bikɔs afta dɛn dɔn rɔnata am, ɛn mak am wit ɔl dɛn at, tru sɔm awa dɛn we di de layt, dɔn, we nɛt de mek di fish nɔ klia, di we aw di krichɔ go wek tumara bambay tru di daknɛs, i kin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ di pɔsin we de fɛn di animal in maynd gɛt sɛns, jɔs lɛk aw di payɔnia in kɔst de to am. So dat to dis ɔntinman in wɔndaful skil, di prɔvab evanescence fɔ wan tin we dɛn rayt insay wata, wan wek, na to ɔl di tin dɛn we dɛn want, i nia as pɔsin kin abop pan am lɛk di land we tinap tranga wan. Ɛn as pipul dɛn sabi di pawaful ayɛn Livaytan we de na di mɔdan relwe pan in ɛvri paz, dat, wit wach na dɛn an, man dɛn kin taym in rit as dɔktɔ di wan we bebi in puls de du; ɛn layt wan se, di tren we de go ɔp ɔ di tren we de dɔŋ go rich na da kayn ples de, na da kayn awa de; ivin so, klos, sɔm tɛm dɛn de we dɛn Nantucketers ya tɛm dat ɔda Leviathan of di dip, akɔdin to di observed humor of his speed; ɛn se to dɛnsɛf, so bɔku awa naw dis wɛl go dɔn go tu ɔndrɛd mayl, go dɔn rich dis ɔ da digri de fɔ latitud ɔ lɔnjitud. Bɔt fɔ mek dis akyu at ɔl saksesful na di ɛnd, di briz ɛn di si fɔ bi di wɛlman in padi dɛn; bikɔs wetin prɛzɛnt avail to di becalmed ɔ windbound mariner na di skil we de mek i no se i de ɛksaktɔli nayntin tri lig ɛn wan kwata frɔm in pɔt? Inferable frɔm dɛn stetmɛnt ya, na bɔku kolataral subtil tin dɛn we de tɔch di chase fɔ wɛl.

Di ship bin rɔtin; lɛf dis kayn fɔrɔ na di si lɛk we kanɔn-bɔl, we nɔ de, bi plɔg-shɛri ɛn tɔn ɔp di lɛvul fil.

“Na sɔl ɛn hεmp!” Stubb kray, “bɔt dis swift muvmɛnt we di dɛk de muv de krip ɔp in leg ɛn tingl na di at. Mi ɛn dis ship na tu brayt kɔmpin dɛn!—Ha, ha! Sɔm wan tek mi ɔp, ɛn lans mi, spayna-ways, na di si,—bikɔs bay layf-ɔk! mi spayna na kil. Ha, ha! wi de go di waka we nɔ de lɛf ɛni dɔst biɛn!”

“Na de i de blo—i de blo!—i de blo!—rayt bifo!” na bin naw di mast-hed kray.

“Aye, aye!” Stubb kray se, “A bin no am—yu nɔ go ebul fɔ rɔnawe—blɔd ɛn split yu spɔt, O wɛl! di mad dɛbul insɛf de afta yu! blo yu trɔmp—blis yu lɔng!—Eab go dam ɔf yu blɔd, lɛk aw milman de lɔk in wata get na di strim!”

Ɛn Stubb bin du bɔt i bin tɔk fɔ wɛl nia ɔl da kru de. Di frenzies of di chase bin don by dis taim wok dem bubblingly up, laik ol wayn wok nyu. Ilɛk us pale fred ɛn forebodings sɔm pan dɛn go dɔn fil bifo; dɛn nɔ bin jɔs de mek dɛn nɔ si dɛn naw bikɔs Eab bin de fred mɔ ɛn mɔ, bɔt dɛn bin brok, ɛn dɛn bin de win dɛn ɔlsay, lɛk prɛri hare dɛn we de shem ɛn we de skata bifo di bayson dɛn we de bɔnd. Fate in an bin dɔn tek ɔl dɛn sol; ɛn bay di prɔblɛm dɛn we bin de mek pɔsin stɔp di de bifo; di rek fɔ di pas nɛt in sɔspɛns; di fiks, nɔ fred, blaynd, rɛklɛs we dɛn wayl kraft bin de go plɔng to in flay mak; bay ɔl dɛn tin ya, dɛn at bin de bow. Di briz we bin de mek big big bɛlɛ dɛn na dɛn sel dɛn, ɛn rɔsh di bot go bifo wit an dɛn we dɛn nɔ de si lɛk se dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ stɔp; dis bin tan lɛk di sayn fɔ da ɛjɛnshi we dɛn nɔ bin de si we bin so mek dɛn bi slev to di rays.

Dɛn na bin wan man, nɔto tati man. Bikɔs i tan lɛk di wan ship we bin de ol dɛn ɔl; pan ɔl we dɛn bin put am togɛda wit ɔl di tin dɛn we difrɛn—ɔk, ɛn maple, ɛn pain wud; ayɛn, ɛn pitch, ɛn hεmp—bɔt ɔl dɛn tin ya bin de rɔn insay di wan kɔnkrit hul, we bin de shot we i de go, ɔl tu balans ɛn di lɔng sɛntral kil bin de dayrɛkt am; ivin so, ɔl di individyualiti dɛn fɔ di kru, dis man in maynd, da man de in fred; gilti ɛn gilti, ɔl di kayn we dɛn bin weld insay wan, ɛn dɛn ɔl bin de dayrɛkt to da fatal gol we Eab dɛn wan masta ɛn kil bin rili pɔynt to.

Di rigging bin liv. Di mast-hed dɛn, lɛk di tɔp dɛn pan lɔng lɔng an dɛn, bin gɛt bɔku bɔku an ɛn fut dɛn. Sɔm dɛn bin de ol wan spar wit wan an, ɛn dɛn bin de es di ɔda wan wit wef we dɛn nɔ gɛt peshɛnt; ɔda wan dɛn, we bin de shed dɛn yay frɔm di layt we di san bin de shayn, bin sidɔm fa fawe na di yad dɛn we bin de rɔk; ɔl di spars in ful bearing of mɔtalman, rɛdi ɛn rayp fɔ dɛn fate. Ah! aw dɛn stil de tray tranga wan fɔ pas da blu we nɔ gɛt ɛnd fɔ fɛn di tin we go pwɛl dɛn!

“Wetin mek una nɔ de siŋ fɔ am, if una si am?” Eab kray, we, afta sɔm minit dɛn pas frɔm di fɔs kray, dɛn nɔ yɛri igen. “Sway mi, man dɛm; dɛn dɔn ful una; nɔto Mobi Dik de trowe wan od jet da we de, dɔn i lɔs.”

I bin ivin bi so; insay dɛn ed we dɛn bin want fɔ du sɔntin, di man dɛn bin dɔn mistek sɔm ɔda tin fɔ di wɛl-spɔt, as di tin we apin insɛf bin pruv i nɔ tu te; bikɔs Eab nɔ bin dɔn rich usay i sidɔm; i nɔ bin izi fɔ mek dɛn put di rop to in pin na dɛk, we i de nak di ki-nɔt to wan ɔkestra, we de mek di briz de shek shek lɛk di kɔmbayn dischaj dɛn we rayf dɛn de pul. Dɛn yɛri di triɔmph halloo fɔ tati bɔkskin lɔng dɛn, as—we bin de fa fawe nia di ship pas di ples we di imajinari jet bin de, we nɔ bin rich wan mayl bifo—Mobi Dik bin bɔs in bɔdi wit in yay! Bikɔs nɔto bay ɛni kol at ɛn indolent spouting; nɔto bay di pisful gush we da mistik watawɛl de na in ed, di Wait Wɛl naw sho di say we i de; bɔt bay di tin we rili wɔndaful we na fɔ brok di lɔ. We di Spɛm Wɛl de rayz wit in pawa pas ɔl frɔm di dip ples dɛn we de fa pas ɔl, so i de bɔm in wan ol bɔl insay di klin ɛlimɛnt na di briz, ɛn we i gɛda wan mawnten we gɛt fom we de shayn, i de sho in ples to di distans we na sɛvin mayl ɛn mɔ. Insay dɛn tɛm dɛn de, di wef dɛn we rɔtin, we vɛks we i de shek, kin tan lɛk in mane; insay sɔm kes dɛn, dis brech na in akt fɔ defy.

“Na de i de brok! na de i de brok!” na bin di kray, lɛk aw insay in bravado dɛn we nɔ pɔsibul fɔ mɛzhɔ di Wait Wɛl bin de tos insɛf lɛk salmɔn to Ɛvin. So wantɛm wantɛm we dɛn si am na di blu ples we de na di si, ɛn we i rili fil fri agens di say we stil blu na di skay, di sprɛy we i es ɔp, fɔ di tɛm, bin de shayn ɛn i bin de shayn lɛk glasia we i nɔ ebul fɔ bia; ɛn tinap de smɔl smɔl de fade ɛn fade away frɔm in fɔs spak spak intensiti, to di dim mistiness fɔ wan shawa we de go bifo na wan vale.

“Aye, brech yu las to di san, Mobi Dik!” Eab ala se, “yu awa ɛn yu harpun dɔn nia!—Dɔn!dɔn ɔl una, bɔt wan man de bifo. Di bot dɛn!—tinap nia!”

Bikɔs di man dɛn nɔ bin de tink bɔt di rop-lada dɛn we bin de mek dɛn taya na di shroud dɛn, lɛk sta dɛn we de shot, bin slid go na di dɛk, nia di bakstay ɛn halyard dɛn we bin de fa frɔm ɔda pipul dɛn; we Eab, we nɔ bin de dart, bɔt stil kwik kwik wan, dɛn bin drɔp am frɔm in sidɔm ples.

I kray se: “Lɔw fa,” so jɔs afta i rich na in bot—na spay wan, we dɛn rig di aftanun bifo. “Mista Stabɔk, di ship na yu yon—kip fa frɔm di bot dɛn, bɔt kip nia dɛn. Lɔwa, ɔl!”

I tan lɛk se i want fɔ mek dɛn fred kwik kwik wan, bay dis tɛm we na di fɔs pɔsin we atak insɛf, Mobi Dik bin dɔn tɔn, ɛn naw i bin de kam fɔ di tri kru dɛn. Eab in bot bin de na di midul; ɛn we i de gladi fɔ in man dɛn, i tɛl dɛn se i go tek di wɛl in ed ɛn ed,—dat na fɔ pul am stret to in fɔɛd,—na tin we nɔ kɔmɔn; bikɔs we i de insay wan patikyula limit, da kayn kɔs de nɔ de mek di wɛl bigin fɔ si am na di sayd. Bɔt bifo dɛn gɛt da klos limit de, ɛn pan ɔl we ɔl di tri bot dɛn bin klin lɛk di ship in tri mast dɛn to in yay; di Wait Wɛl we bin de rɔn insɛf fɔ spid we i vɛks bad bad wan, lɛk se i bin de rɔsh bitwin di bot dɛn wit opin jaw, ɛn wan tel we de lash, bin de gi bad bad fɛt ɔlsay; ɛn i nɔ bin pe atɛnshɔn to di ayɛn dɛn we bin de rɔn pan am frɔm ɛni bot, i bin tan lɛk se dɛn jɔs want fɔ dɔnawe wit ɛni difrɛn plank we dɛn mek dɛn bot dɛn de wit. Bɔt i bin sabi fɔ manej, i bin de wil ɔltɛm lɛk chaja dɛn we dɛn dɔn tren na di fil; di bot dɛn bin de rɔnawe pan am fɔ sɔm tɛm; bɔt sɔntɛnde, bɔt i kin brayt lɛk plang; pan ɔl we ɔltɛm, Eab in wɔd we nɔ de na di wɔl bin de kɔt ɔl di ɔda kray dɛn bɔt in yon kray.

Bɔt las las insay in evolushɔn dɛn we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ tray fɔ no, di Wait Wɛl so krɔs ɛn krɔs bak, ɛn insay wan tawzin we dɛn i ɛnta di slak we di tri layn dɛn we de fast naw to am, dat dɛn fɔs shɔt, ɛn, fɔ dɛnsɛf, wɔp di devoted bot dɛn to di ayɛn dɛn we dɛn dɔn plant insay am; pan ɔl we naw fɔ smɔl tɛm di wɛl drɔ asay smɔl, lɛk se i de gɛda fɔ wan mɔ big chaj. We Eab bin tek da chans de, i bin fɔs pe mɔ layn: dɔn i bin de pul ɛn jɔk am bak kwik kwik wan—i bin de op se da we de go mek i nɔ gɛt sɔm snarl dɛn—we lo!—na tin we i bin si we bin rili bad pas di shak dɛn tit we dɛn bin de fɛt!

Dɛn kech ɛn twist—kɔkskru insay di mazi dɛn na di layn, lɔs harpun ɛn lans, wit ɔl dɛn bristling barb ɛn pɔynt dɛn, kam flash ɛn drip te to di chɔk dɛn na di bɔw dɛn na Eab in bot. Na wan tin nɔmɔ dɛn bin ebul fɔ du. We i ol di bot-naif, i bin rili rich insay—tru—ɛn afta dat, i nɔ bin de—di rayt we stɛl de mek; drɛg insay di layn we de biɛn, pas am, insay di bot, to di pɔsin we de bɔw, ɛn afta dat, tu tɛm, i skata di rop nia di chɔk dɛn—i drɔp di stɛl fagɔt we dɛn bin dɔn intasept insay di si; ɛn ɔltin bin fast bak. Da tɛm de, di Wait Wɛl mek wan rɔsh wantɛm wantɛm bitwin di ɔda tangle dɛn we lɛf na di ɔda layn dɛn; bay we i du dat, i nɔ ebul fɔ stɔp di bot dɛn we involv mɔ na Stubb ɛn Flask to in flukes; dash dɛn togɛda lɛk tu rɔlin husks na wan bich we sɔf dɔn bit, dɔn, we i de dayv dɔŋ insay di si, i lɔs insay wan bɔyl maelstrom, usay, fɔ sɔm tɛm, di sida chips dɛn we de smɛl fayn na di ship dɛn we dɔn pwɛl bin de dans rawnd ɛn rawnd, lɛk di natmɛk we dɛn dɔn grat insay wan bol we dɛn dɔn stir kwik kwik wan.

We di tu kru dɛn bin stil de rawnd na di wata, dɛn bin de es dɛn an afta di layn-tɔb dɛn we de rɔn, di ɔs dɛn, ɛn ɔda fɔnicha dɛn we de flɔt, we smɔl Flask we bin de aslɔp bin de bob ɔp ɛn dɔŋ lɛk ɛmti vayl, i bin de twit in leg dɛn ɔp fɔ rɔnawe pan di jaw dɛn we di shak dɛn bin de fred; ɛn Stubb bin de siŋ wit lɔv fɔ mek sɔmbɔdi lad am; ɛn we di ol man in layn—we de skata naw—admit se i pul insay di krim pul fɔ sev udat i ebul;—insay da wayl simultaneousness of wan tawzin kɔnkrit perils,—I bin tan lɛk se Eab in bot we nɔ bin dɔn bit yet dɔn drɔ am go ɔp to Ɛvin bay waya dɛn we wi nɔ de si,—as, i tan lɛk aro, i de shot perpendicularly frɔm di si, di Wait Whale dash in brayt fɔɛd agens in bɔt, ɛn sɛn am, tɔn oba ɛn ɔva, na di ɛj; te i fɔdɔm bak—gunwale dɔŋ—ɛn Eab ɛn in man dɛn strɛch fɔ kɔmɔt ɔnda am, lɛk sil dɛn we kɔmɔt na wan kev we de nia di si.

Di fɔs tɛm we di wɛl bin de tɔn agens—we i bin de chenj di we aw i de go as i de nak di wata—nɔ bin want am fɔ lans am along am, to smɔl distans frɔm di sɛntrɔm fɔ di pwɛl pwɛl we i bin dɔn mek; ɛn wit in bak to am, naw i ledɔm fɔ smɔl tɛm de fil wit in fluk frɔm wan say to di ɔda say; ɛn ɛnitɛm we wan ɔs we nɔ de waka, wan smɔl plank, di smɔl smɔl chip ɔ krɔm pan di bot dɛn tɔch in skin, in tel kin drɔ bak kwik kwik wan, ɛn kam na sayd de bit di si. Bɔt i nɔ tu te, lɛk se i satisfay se in wok fɔ da tɛm de dɔn, i push in fɔɛd we dɛn plet tru di si, ɛn we i de fala am di layn dɛn we miks togɛda, kɔntinyu fɔ waka we di briz de blo wit di we aw pɔsin we de travul kin du am.

Jɔs lɛk bifo, di ship we bin de pe atɛnshɔn dɔn tɔk bɔt di wan ol fɛt, i kam bak fɔ bia dɔŋ fɔ sev, ɛn drɔp wan bot, pik di marina dɛn we bin de flɔt, di tub dɛn, di ɔs dɛn, ɛn ɛni ɔda tin we dɛn kin kech pan, ɛn land dɛn sef wan na in dɛk. Sɔm dɛn sholda, an, ɛn anklɛ dɛn bin swɛt; livid kɔntushɔn dɛn; harpun ɛn lans dɛn we gɛt wrench; intricacies of rop we nɔ go ebul fɔ kɔmɔt; ɔs ɛn plang dɛn we dɔn brok brok; ɔl dɛn tin ya bin de de; bɔt i bin tan lɛk se no sik nɔ bin apin we go kil ɛnibɔdi ɔ ivin siriɔs. Jɔs lɛk Fɛdala di de bifo, na so dɛn bin fɛn Eab naw de tay pan in bot in af we bin brok, we bin mek i izi fɔ flɔt; ɛn i nɔ bin mek i taya so lɛk di bad tin we bin apin di de bifo.

Bɔt we dɛn ɛp am fɔ go na di dɛk, ɔl di yay dɛn bin de pan am; as instead fɔ tinap in wan i stil haf-af-hang pan di sholda fɔ Starbuck, we te naw na di fɔs pɔsin fɔ ɛp am. Dɛn bin dɔn kɔt in ayvri leg, ɛn na wan shɔt shap swɛlin nɔmɔ lɛf.

“Aye, aye, Starbuck, 'tis swit fɔ slip sɔmtɛm, bi di slim udat i go; ɛn go ol Eab bin dɔn slip bɔku tɛm pas aw i dɔn le.”

Di kapɛnta we de kam ɔp naw se: “Di fɛrɔ nɔ tinap, sa; “A put gud wok insay da leg de.”

Stubb bin tɔk wit rial wɔri se: “Bɔt nɔ bon brok, sa, a op.”

“Aye! ɛn ɔl splintered to pieces, Stubb!—d’ye see it.—Bɔt ivin wit wan brok bon bon, ol Eab nɔ tɔch;ɛn a kɔnt no layf bon fɔ mi wan jot mɔ mi, pas dis day wan we dɔn lɔs. Nɔ wayt wɛl, ɔ man, ɔr fiend, nɔ kin so mɔ as gras ol Eab insay in yon prɔpa ɛn inaccessible biin. Eni lid kin ebul fɔ tɔch yonder floor, eni mast skrap yonder ruf?—Aloft dea wich way?”

“Dɛd to leeward, sa.”

“Up helm, den; pail pan di sel bak, ship kipa dɛm!dɔwn di ɔda spay bot dɛn ɛn rig dɛn—Mista Stabɔk go, ɛn gɛda di bot in kru dɛm.”

“Lɛ a ɛp yu fɔs fɔ go na di wɔl dɛn, sa.”

“O, o, oh! aw dis splinter de chuk mi naw! Accursed fate! dat di kapten we nɔ ebul fɔ win insay di sol fɔ gɛt da kayn krayv mate de!”

"Sa?"

"Mi bɔdi, man, nɔto yu. Gi mi sɔntin fɔ wan ken—na de, da lans we de shek shek go du. Musta di man dɛn. Fɔ tru, a nɔ si am yet. Bay ɛvin i nɔ go ebul fɔ bi!—mis?—kwik! kɔl dɛn ɔl.”

Di ol man in hinted tinkin na tru. We dɛn gɛda di kɔmni, di Parsee nɔ bin de de.
