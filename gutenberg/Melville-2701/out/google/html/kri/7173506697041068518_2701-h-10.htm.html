CHAPTER 48. Di Fɔs Lɔs.

Di phantoms, bikɔs na so dɛn bin tan lɛk da tɛm de, bin de flit na di ɔda say na di dɛk, ɛn, wit wan sɛri we nɔ gɛt nɔys, dɛn bin de trowe di takɛl ɛn band dɛn na di bot we bin de swing de. Dɛn bin de tek dis bot ɔltɛm as wan pan di spay bot dɛn, pan ɔl we dɛn bin de kɔl am di kapten in yon, bikɔs i bin de hang na di stabɔd kwata. Di figa we bin tinap naw nia in bɔw dɛn bin lɔng ɛn swɛt, ɛn wan wayt tut bin de kɔmɔt bad bad wan frɔm in lip dɛn we tan lɛk stɛl. Wan rumpled Chaynish jakit we dɛn mek wit blak kɔtɔn bin inves am, wit wayd blak trayz dɛn we gɛt di sem dak tin dɛn. Bɔt strenj wan we bin krawn dis ɛbon na wan wayt plet turban we de shayn, di ia we de alayv bin breyd ɛn kɔyl rawnd ɛn rawnd pan in ed. Les swart in aspect, di kompin dɛm fɔ dis figa bin gɛt da vivid, tayga-yɔlɔ kɔmpleks we spɛshal to sɔm pan di aborijini netiv dɛm na di Manillas;—wan rays we gɛt badnem fɔ wan patikyula diabolism of subtilty, ɛn bay sɔm ɔnɛs wayt mariner dɛm we dɛn se na di pe spay ɛn sikrit kɔnfidɛns ɛjɛn dɛn na di wata fɔ di dɛbul, dɛn masta, we dɛn kɔnt-rum lɛ wi se i de ɔdasay.

We di ship in kɔmpin we bin de wɔnda bin de luk dɛn strenja dɛn ya, Eab ala to di ol man we gɛt wayt tɔban we bin de na dɛn ed se, “Ɔl rɛdi de, Fɛdala?”

Di ansa we dɛn bin ansa af-af af-af se: “Rɛdi.”

“Lower away den;d’ye yɛri?” we de ala krɔs di dɛk. “Lɔw away de, a de se.”

Na so in vɔys bin de mek tɛnda, dat pan ɔl we dɛn bin de sɔprayz, di man dɛn bin rɔn oba di rel; di shiv dɛn bin de rɔn rawnd insay di blɔk dɛn; wit wan wallow, di tri bot dɛn drɔp insay di si; we, wit wan dexterous, off-handed daring, we dɛn nɔ no bɔt ɛni ɔda wok, di selɔ dɛn, we tan lɛk got, bin jomp dɔŋ di ship we de rɔl in sayd insay di bot dɛn we dɛn bin dɔn tos dɔŋ.

I nɔ bin izi fɔ mek dɛn pul kɔmɔt ɔnda di ship in lee, we wan nɔmba 4 kil, we bin de kɔmɔt na di say we di briz de blo, pul rawnd ɔnda di stɛp, ɛn sho di fayv strenja dɛn we bin de row Eab, we, we bin tinap stret na di ship, bin de prez Stabɔk, Stɔb, ɛn Flask lawd wan, fɔ mek dɛn spre dɛnsɛf ɔlsay, so dat i go kɔba wan big big wata. Bɔt wit ɔl dɛn yay bak we dɛn rivet pan di swart Fɛdala ɛn in kru, di prizina dɛn na di ɔda bot dɛn nɔ obe di kɔmand.

Starbuck bin tɔk se: “Kpɛntin Eab?—.”

Eab bin ala se: “Una skata; “gi we, ɔl di 4 bot dɛn.Yu, Flask, pul mɔ to leeward!”

“Aye, aye, sa,” smɔl King-Post kray wit gladi at, we i de swip rawnd in big stiarin ɔa. “Lay bak!” we i de adrɛs in kru. “Na de!—na de!—de de bak! Na de i blo rayt bifo, bɔy pikin dɛn!—le bak!”

“Nɔ ɛva lisin to yɔlɔ bɔbɔ dɛn, Achi.”

Aki se: “O, a nɔ de tink bɔt dɛn, sa; "A bin no ɔltin bifo naw. A nɔ yɛri dɛn na di hold? Ɛn a nɔ tɛl Kabako ya bɔt am? Wetin una se, Kabako? Dɛn na stowaway, Mista Flask."

“Pul, pul, mi fayn at-layv; pul, mi pikin dɛm; pul, mi smɔl wan dɛm,” Stɔb bin swɛt wit drɔl ɛn sooth to in kru, sɔm pan dɛn stil sho sayn dɛm fɔ nɔ fil fayn. “Wetin mek una nɔ de brok una bakbon, mi bɔy pikin dɛn? Wetin na di tin we una de luk? Dɛn chap dɛn de we de na yanda bot? Tut! Dɛn na fayv mɔ an nɔmɔ de kam fɔ ɛp wi—nɔ ɛva tink frɔm usay—di mɔ di mɔ de mek wi gladi. Pul, den, du pul; nɔ ɛva tink bɔt di brimston—dɛbul dɛn na gud kɔmpin dɛn inof. So, so; na de una de naw; dat na di strɔk fɔ wan tawzin paund; Na di strok fɔ swip di stej dɛn! Hurrah fɔ di gold kɔp fɔ sperm ɔyl, mi hiro dɛn! Tri gladi gladi, man dɛn—ɔl dɛn at de alayv! I izi, i izi; Nɔ rɔsh—nɔ de rɔsh. Wai yu no snap yu oars, yu rascals? Bit somtin, yu dog dem! So, so, so, den:—sɔft wan, saful wan! Dat na . it—dat na am lɔng ɛn strɔng! pul, ɛn bigin yu yay kɔmɔt na ya!” we i de wip di shap naif kɔmɔt na in gird; “ɛvri mama in pikin fɔ una pul in nɛf, ɛn pul wit di bled bitwin in tit. Na dat—na dat. Naw una de du sɔntin; dat tan lɛk am, mi stɛl-bit dɛn. Start am—start am, mi silva-spɔnj dɛn! Start am, marling-spikes!”

Stubb in exordium to in kru dɛn gi am ya at laaj, bikɔs i bin gɛt bɛtɛ wan pekul we fɔ tɔk to dɛn in jɔnal, ɛn mɔ fɔ inkulkate di rilijɔn fɔ row. Bɔt yu nɔ fɔ tink frɔm dis spɛshal we aw i bin de prich se i ɛva flay insay downright passion wit in kɔngrigeshɔn. Atɔl; ɛn na de in men spɛshal tin bin de. I go se di tin dɛn we rili wɔndaful to in kru, insay wan tɔyn we so strenj kɔmpawnd wit ɛnjɔymɛnt ɛn vɛks, ɛn di wamat bin tan lɛk se dɛn kɔlkul am jɔs lɛk spays to di ɛnjɔymɛnt, dat no ɔsman nɔ go ebul fɔ yɛri dɛn kayn kwik invokeshɔn dɛn de we i nɔ pul fɔ dia layf, ɛn yet i pul fɔ di jɔs jok fɔ di tin. Apat frɔm dat, ɔltɛm i bin de luk so izi ɛn indolent insɛf, so loungingly manej in stiarin-oar, ɛn so brayt opin—open-mɔt sɔntɛnde—dat fɔ jɔs si dis kayn kɔmanda we de yawn, bay sheer force of contrast, akt lɛk charm pan di kru. Dɔn bak, Stubb na bin wan pan dɛn odd sort of humorists, we in jollity kin so curiously ambiguous sɔntɛnde, dat i kin put ɔl inferiors pan dɛn gad pan di mata fɔ obe dɛn.

Fɔ obe wan sayn we Eab bin gi, Starbuck bin de pul naw obliquely akɔdin to Stubb in bow; ɛn we fɔ wan minit ɔ so di tu bot dɛn bin rili nia dɛnsɛf, Stɔb bin prez di pɔsin we dɛn mared to.

“Mista Starbuck! larboard bot de, ahoy! wan wɔd wit yu, sa, if yu duya!”

“Halloa!” Starbuck kam bak, i tɔn rawnd nɔto wan inch as i de tɔk; stil de ɛnkɔrej in kru wit ɔl in at bɔt i de wispa; in fes bin set lɛk flint we kɔmɔt na Stubb in yon.

“Wetin una tink bɔt dɛn yɔlɔ bɔbɔ dɛn de, sa!”

"Smɔgl pan bot, sɔm kayn we, bifo di ship sel. (Strɔng, strɔng, bɔy dɛm!)" insay wan wispa to in kru, dɔn i tɔk lawd wan bak: "A sad biznɛs, Mista Stubb! (seethe her, seethe her, my lads!) bɔt nɔ ɛva tink, Mista Stubb, ɔl fɔ di bɛst. Lɛ ɔl yu kru pul strɔng, kam wetin go. (Spring, mi man dɛm, spring!) Der's hogsheads of sperm ahead, Mr. Stubb, ɛn na dat una kam fɔ (Pul, mi bɔy pikin dɛn!) Sperm, sperm’s the play Dis at least is duty and profit hand in hand.”

"Aye, aye, a bin tink as moch," soliloquised Stubb, wen di bot dem diverge, "as soon as a klap ai pan 'em, a tink so. Aye, en na dat i bin go in di afta hol fo, so often, as Dough-Boy long saspek. Dem bin ayd daun de. Di Wait Whale de na di bottom of it. Wel, wel, so bi am! Can't be helped! All rayt!

Naw we dɛn ɔtlandish strenja dɛn ya kam na wan impɔtant tɛm lɛk we dɛn de put di bot dɛn dɔŋ frɔm di dɛk, dis nɔ bin dɔn wek sɔm pan di ship in kɔmpin dɛn we nɔ mek sɛns; bɔt Archy in fansi diskvayri we bin gɛt sɔm tɛm bifo bin kam na ɔda kɔntri bitwin dɛn, pan ɔl we fɔ tru, dɛn nɔ bin gi dɛn di kredit da tɛm de, dis bin dɔn in sɔm smɔl mɛzhɔ rɛdi dɛn fɔ di ivin. I pul di ekstrim ed fɔ dɛn wɔnda; ɛn so wetin wit ɔl dis ɛn Stubb in kɔnfidɛns we fɔ akɔntayn fɔ dɛn apia, dɛn bin fri fɔ di tɛm frɔm lay lay tin dɛn we dɛn bin de tink bɔt; pan ɔl we di afɛkt stil lɛf bɔku ples fɔ ɔl kayn wayl kɔnjɛkshɔn as fɔ dak Eab in prɛsis ɛjɛnshi pan di mata frɔm di biginin. Fɔ mi, a bin de mɛmba kwayɛt wan di mistiriɔs shado dɛn we a bin dɔn si de krip na di Pequod di tɛm we di Nantucket bin de shayn dak, ɛn bak di enigmatical hintings of the unaccountable Ilayja.

Insay da tɛm de, Eab nɔ bin yɛri bɔt in ɔfisa dɛn, bikɔs i bin dɔn de fa fawe fɔ go na di say we di briz de blo, i bin stil de rɔn bifo di ɔda bot dɛn; wan sikɔstɛms we de tɔk aw pawaful wan kru bin de pul am. Dɛn tayga yɔlɔ krichɔ dɛn de we i bin gɛt bin tan lɛk ɔl stɛl ɛn wɛl bon; lɛk fayv trip-hama dɛn bin de rayz ɛn fɔdɔm wit rɛgyula strɔk dɛn we gɛt trɛnk, we bin de stat di bot wan wan tɛm along di wata lɛk ɔrizɔntal bɔrst bɔyla we kɔmɔt na Misisipi stiama. As fɔ Fedallah, we dɛn si de pul di harpooneer oar, i bin dɔn trowe in blak jakit na sayd, ɛn sho in nekɛd chɛst wit di wan ol pat pan in bɔdi ɔp di gɔnwal, we dɛn kɔt klia wan agens di ɔltɛm dipreshɔn dɛn na di wata ɔrayzin; we na di ɔda ɛnd na di bot Eab, wit wan an, lɛk pɔsin we de fɛn fɛns, we dɛn trowe af bak na di ɛj, lɛk se i de kɔndɛm ɛnitin we i want fɔ trip; Dɛn bin si Eab de manej in stiarin ɔa stedi as insay wan tawzin bot we dɛn de put dɔŋ bifo di Wait Wɛl dɔn kɔt am. Wan tɛm di an we dɛn stret bin de muv wan patikyula we ɛn afta dat i bin de kɔntinyu fɔ de, ɛn dɛn bin de si di bot in fayv ɔs dɛn de ɔp wan tɛm. Bot ɛn di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot bin sidɔm na di si we nɔ de muv. Wantɛm wantɛm di tri bot dɛn we bin spred we bin de biɛn bin stɔp we dɛn de go. Di wɛl dɛn bin dɔn sidɔm ɔltɛm na dɛn bɔdi dɔŋ insay di blu, so dat nɔ bin de sho se dɛn de muv fa fawe, pan ɔl we Eab bin dɔn si am frɔm di say we i bin de nia am.

“Ɔlman de luk na in ɔs!” Starbuck bin kray. “Yu, Kwik, tinap!”

Di savayj bin de spring wit sɛns pan di triangul rayz bɔks we de na di bɔw, i tinap stret de, ɛn wit in yay dɛn we bin rili want fɔ luk, i bin de luk di say we dɛn bin dɔn tɔk bɔt di las tɛm we dɛn bin de rɔnata am. Semweso pan di ekstrim stern na di bot usay i bin tu triangul pletfɔm lɛvɛl wit di gɔnwal, dɛn bin si Starbuck insɛf kol ɛn adroitly balans insɛf to di jɔk tossings fɔ in chip fɔ wan kraft, ɛn silently eyeing di big blu yay na di si.

Nɔto fa fawe Flask in bot sɛf bin de ledɔm we i nɔ de blo; in kɔmanda we nɔ tek tɛm tinap pan di tap pan di loggerhead, wan strɔng kayn post we gɛt rut na di kil, ɛn we de go ɔp lɛk tu fit so ɔp di lɛvul na di stern pletfɔm. Dɛn kin yuz am fɔ kech tɔn wit di wɛl layn. I tɔp nɔ spays pas man in an, ɛn we i tinap pan da kayn bays de, i bin tan lɛk se Flask sidɔm na di mast-ed fɔ sɔm ship we bin dɔn sink to ɔlman pas in trak dɛn. Bɔt smɔl King-Post bin smɔl ɛn shɔt, ɛn di sem tɛm smɔl King-Post bin ful-ɔp wit big ɛn lɔng ambishɔn, so dis loggerhead stand-point we i bin gɛt nɔ bin satisfay King-Post atɔl.

“A nɔ de si tri si dɛn de, tip wi wan ɔs de, ɛn lɛ a go to dat.”

Pan dis, Daggoo, wit ɛni an pan di gɔnwal fɔ mek i stedi, i slid kwik kwik wan biɛn, dɔn we i tinap insɛf, i volontia in ay ay sholda dɛn fɔ wan pedestal.

“Gud wan mast-hed as eni wan, sa. Yu go mount?”

“Dat a want, ɛn tɛl una tɛnki bad bad wan, mi fayn kɔmpin, na in nɔmɔ a want mek una lɔng fɔ fifti fit.”

We di big big nigro plant in fut dɛn tranga wan agens tu ɔpɔzit plank dɛn na di bot, i butu smɔl, prɛzɛnt in flat pal to Flask in fut, dɔn i put Flask in an pan in ed we gɛt bɔdi-plum ɛn tɛl am fɔ spring as insɛf fɔ tos, wit wan dexterous fling land di smɔl man ay ɛn dray na in sholda. Ɛn na ya Flask bin tinap naw, Daggoo wit wan lif an de furnish am wit brestband fɔ ledɔm pan ɛn stedi insɛf bay.

Ɛni tɛm i kin strenj fɔ si di tayro fɔ si wit us wɔndaful abit fɔ skil we i nɔ no natin di wɛlman go kɔntinyu fɔ tinap stret na in bot, ivin we di si dɛn we de rɔn pas ɔl ɛn we de rɔn krɔs, de rɔn rawnd am. I stil strenj fɔ si am giddily sidɔm pan di loggerhead insɛf, ɔnda dɛn kayn tin dɛn de. Bɔt we yu si smɔl Flask we dɛn rayd pan big big Daggoo, i bin mek yu want fɔ no mɔ; fɔ sɔpɔt insɛf wit wan kol, indipɛndɛnt, izi, nɔ tink bɔt, barbaric majesty, di nobul nigro to ɛvri rol na di si harmoniously rol in fayn fɔm. Na in brayt bak, Flask we gɛt flaks ia bin tan lɛk se i tan lɛk sno. Di pɔsin we de kɛr am bin luk fayn pas di pɔsin we de rayd am. Though truly vivacious, tumultuous, ostentatious likl Flask go naw en den stamp wit impatience; bɔt nɔto wan ad heave i bin gi am so to di nigro in lordly chest. So a dɔn si Passion ɛn Vanity de stamp di liv magnanimous wɔl, bɔt di wɔl nɔ chenj in tayd ɛn in sizin fɔ dat.

Insay da tɛm de, Stubb, we na di tɔd patna, nɔ bin betray ɛni solicitudes we de luk fa. Sɔntɛm di wɛl dɛn bin fɔ dɔn mek wan pan di sawnd dɛn we dɛn kin mek ɔltɛm, nɔto fɔ dayv fɔ sɔm tɛm bikɔs dɛn jɔs fred; ɛn if na so i bi, i tan lɛk se Stubb, as i kin du na dɛn kayn kes dɛn de, i bin dɔn mekɔp in maynd fɔ kɔrej di languish intaval wit in paip. I pul am na in hatband, usay i bin de wɛr am ɔltɛm aslant lɛk fɛda. I lod am, ɛn ram di lod go os wit in thumb-end; bɔt i nɔ bin izi fɔ mek i layt in mach akɔdin to di rɔf sandpepa na in an, we Tashtego, we na in harpuoneer, we in yay bin dɔn de sɛt to briz lɛk tu sta dɛn we nɔ de chenj, bin drɔp wantɛm wantɛm lɛk layt frɔm in strɛch abit to in sidɔm ples, ɛn ala wit kwik phrensy fɔ rɔsh se, “Dɔn, dɔŋ ɔl, ɛn gi we!—na dɛn de!”

To pɔsin we de wok na di land, no wɛl, ɔ ɛni sayn fɔ ɛring, nɔ bin fɔ dɔn si da tɛm de; natin nɔ de pas wan trɔbul bit we gɛt grin wayt wata, ɛn tint skata puf dɛn we gɛt vapour we de flay oba am, ɛn we de blo suffusingly go to leeward, lɛk di kɔnfyus skud frɔm wayt rɔlin billows. Di briz we bin de rawnd bin de shek shek ɛn tingul wantɛm wantɛm, lɛk se i tan lɛk di briz we de oba plet dɛn we gɛt ayɛn we dɔn wam bad bad wan. Ɔnda dis atmosfɛrik wev ɛn kɔl, ɛn sɔm pat ɔnda wan tin layt wata, bak, di wɛl dɛn bin de swim. We dɛn si am bifo tɛm pas ɔl di ɔda tin dɛn we de sho se di vapour dɛn we dɛn bin de pul, i bin tan lɛk se na dɛn kɔriɔ dɛn we de rɔn bifo ɛn dɛn detached flay outriders.

Naw ɔl di 4 bot dɛn bin de tray tranga wan fɔ fɛn da wan ples de we gɛt wata ɛn briz we gɛt prɔblɛm. Bɔt i bin fayn fɔ pas dɛn; i bin de flay go bifo ɛn go bifo, as bɔku bɔku bɔbul dɛn we de miks miks de kɛr go dɔŋ wan wata we de rɔn kwik kwik wan frɔm di il dɛn.

“Pul, pul, mi gud bɔy pikin dɛm,” na so Starbuck tɔk, wit di lɔs we pɔsibul bɔt we gɛt strɔng kɔnsɛntret wispa to in man dɛm; we di shap fiks glans frɔm in yay bin de dart stret bifo di bɔw, i bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ i tan lɛk tu nidul dɛn we pɔsin kin si insay tu binnakul kɔmpas dɛn we nɔ de mek mistek. Bɔt i nɔ bin tɔk bɔku tin to in kru, ɛn in kru nɔ bin tɔk ɛnitin to am. Na di bot nɔmɔ bin de kwayɛt wan wan tɛm, wan pan in spɛshal wispa dɛn bin de mek i sɔprayz, we naw i harsh wit kɔmand, we naw saf wit beg.

Aw difrɛn di lawd smɔl King-Post. "Sing aut ɛn se sɔntin, mi at. Roar ɛn pul, mi tɛnda! Bich mi, bich mi pan dɛn blak bak, bɔy pikin dɛm; na dat nɔmɔ du fɔ mi, ɛn a go sayn ova to una mi Mata in Vayn Gad plantɛshɔn, bɔy pikin dɛm; inklud mi wɛf ɛn pikin dɛm, bɔy pikin dɛm. Le mi pan—lay mi pan! O Masta, Masta! bɔt a go go stark, staring mad! Si! si da wayt wata de!” Ɛn so ala, i pul in hat na in ed, ɛn stamp ɔp ɛn dɔŋ pan am; dɔn i pik am, i de ple ple wit am fa fawe na di si; ɛn leta i fɔdɔm fɔ mɛn ɛn fɔdɔm na di bot in stɛp lɛk krayzi kɔlt we kɔmɔt na di prɛri.

“Luk da chap de naw,” filɔsofik drɔ Stubb, we, wit in shɔt paip we nɔ gɛt layt, we i mɛkanikal fɔ kip bitwin in tit, na shɔt distans, fala afta—“I gɛt fit, we Flask gɛt. Fit? yes, gi am fit—dat na di wɔd sɛf—pitch fit insay ’em. Merrily, merrily, hearts-alive. Pudding for supper, you no;—merry’s the word Pul, sucklings—pull, ɔl wetin di dɛbul de rɔsh bɔt? ɔl yu liva ɛn lɔng!”

Bɔt wetin na dat Eab we nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɔndastand bin tɔk to da tayga-yɔlɔ kru we i bin de wok wit—dɛn wɔd ya na bin di bɛst we fɔ lɛf na ya; bikɔs yu de liv ɔnda di blɛsin layt na di ivanjelikal land. Te to di diskɔvri we Kabako bin dɔn pablish, di selɔ dɛn nɔ bin dɔn si am bifo tɛm, pan ɔl we fɔ shɔ we, afta dɛn dɔn kɔmɔt na di pɔt fɔ smɔl tɛm, ɔl an dɔn dɔn di kɔstɔm biznɛs fɔ fit di wɛl bot dɛn fɔ savis; we sɔm tɛm afta dis, dɛn bin de si Eab naw ɛn naw de bestir insɛf pan di mata fɔ mek thole-pin wit in yon an fɔ wetin dɛn bin tink se na wan pan di spay bot dɛn, ɛn ivin solicitously kɔt di smɔl wud skewer dɛn, we we di layn de dɔn, dɛn kin pin oba di groove na di bo: we ɔl dis dɛn bin observ insay am, ɛn patikyula in solicitude fɔ gɛt ɛkstra kot fɔ sheathing insay di bottom na di bot, lɛk se i go mek i ebul fɔ bia di pɔynt prɛshɔn we in ayvri an gɛt; ɛn bak di wɔri we i bin de wɔri we i bin de shep di shɔl bod, ɔ klɔmsi klit, as dɛn kin kɔl am sɔm tɛm, di ɔrizɔntal pat na di bot in bɔ fɔ mek i brayt di ni agens we i de dart ɔ chuk di wɛl; we dɛn si aw ɔltɛm i kin tinap na da bot de wit in wan ni we i fiks na di sɛmi-sɛkɔral dipreshɔn na di klit, ɛn wit di kapɛnta in chisel i kin gog aut smɔl ya ɛn stret am smɔl de; ɔl dɛn tin ya, a se, bin dɔn wek bɔku pipul dɛn intres ɛn want fɔ no mɔ da tɛm de. Bɔt ɔlmost ɔlman bin tink se dis patikyula prɛparativ heedfulnɛs insay Eab fɔ jɔs bi wit di atɛnshɔn fɔ di ɔltimat chase of Moby Dick; bikɔs i bin dɔn ɔlrɛdi sho se i want fɔ fɛn da monsta de we de day insɛf. Bɔt dis kayn tin we dɛn bin de tink bɔt nɔ bin gɛt fɔ du wit di fa fa say we dɛn bin de tink bɔt ɛni bot in kru we dɛn sɛn fɔ wok na da bot de.

Naw, wit di subordinate phantoms, i nɔ tu te, di wɔndaful tin we bin lɛf bin dɔn; bikɔs insay pɔsin we de kech wɛl, i nɔ kin te, wɔndaful tin dɛn kin dɔn. Apat frɔm dat, naw ɛn naw dɛn kayn tin dɛn ya we nɔ gɛt ɛni akɔntabl ɛn ɛnd dɛn we strenj neshɔn dɛn kin gɛt kin kɔmɔt na di say dɛn we dɛn nɔ no ɛn di ashis-ol dɛn na di wɔl fɔ mek dɛn man dɛn ya we de flɔt we nɔ de fala di lɔ we de kech wɛl; ɛn bɔku tɛm di ship dɛnsɛf kin pik dɛn kayn tin dɛn de we dɛn kin si we dɛn kin si de tos rawnd di opin si pan plang dɛn, smɔl smɔl bot dɛn we dɔn pwɛl, ɔa, wɛl bot, kenu, Japan dɔti tin dɛn we dɛn dɔn blo, ɛn ɔda tin dɛn; dat Bilzibɔb insɛf go klaym ɔp di sayd ɛn step dɔŋ insay di kabin fɔ tɔk wit di kapten, ɛn i nɔ go mek ɛni gladi gladi we nɔ go ebul fɔ stɔp na di fɔkas.

Bɔt bi ɔl dis as i bi, shɔ se pan ɔl we di sɔbdɔnayt phantoms nɔ tu te dɛn fɛn dɛn ples bitwin di kru, pan ɔl we stil as i bin sɔm kayn we difrɛn frɔm dɛn, yet da Fɛdala we gɛt ia-tɔban bin kɔntinyu fɔ bi mistɛri we dɛn nɔ muf te to di las. Usay i kɔmɔt na wan wɔl we gɛt abit lɛk dis, bay us kayn tay we i nɔ gɛt ɛni akɔntabl i nɔ tu te, i sho se i gɛt sɔntin fɔ du wit Eab in spɛshal prɔpati dɛn; nɔ, so fa as fɔ gɛt sɔm kayn af-af inflɔɛns; Ɛvin no, bɔt sɔntɛm i bin ivin gɛt pawa oba am; ɔl dis nɔbɔdi nɔ bin no. Bɔt pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ sɔpɔt wan indipɛndɛnt ɛya we gɛt fɔ du wit Fɛdala. I bin tan lɛk pɔsin we sivilizayt, pipul dɛn we de na os we de na di say we kol kin jɔs de si na dɛn drim, ɛn dat bɔt i nɔ klia; bɔt di wan dɛn we tan lɛk dɛn naw ɛn naw de glid bitwin di Eshia kɔmyuniti dɛn we nɔ de chenj, mɔ di Iriɛnt ayland dɛn we de na di ist pat na di kɔntinɛnt—dɛn kɔntri dɛn de we gɛt insuleshɔn, we nɔ de mɛmba, we nɔ de chenj, we ivin insay dɛn tɛm ya stil de kip bɔku pan di gɔst aborijinial we di wɔl in fɔs jɛnɛreshɔn dɛn bin gɛt, we di mɛmori fɔ di fɔs man na bin difrɛn tin we dɛn mɛmba, ɛn ɔl di man dɛn we kɔmɔt na in pikin dɛn, i nɔ bin no usay i kɔmɔt, dɛn bin de yay dɛnsɛf as rial phantom, ɛn aks di san ɛn di mun wetin mek dɛn mek dɛn ɛn wetin mek dɛn mek dɛn; we pan ɔl we, akɔdin to Jɛnɛsis, di enjɛl dɛn bin rili jɔyn wit mɔtalman gyal pikin dɛn, di dɛbul dɛnsɛf, ad di Rabin dɛn we nɔ de na di Baybul, we bin de du tin dɛn we pipul dɛn kin du na di wɔl.





CHAPTER 51. Di Spirit-Spout.

Dez, wiks pas, ɛn ɔnda izi sel, di ayvri Pequod bin dɔn swip smɔl smɔl akɔdin to 4 difrɛn kruiz-grɔn dɛn; dat we de nia di Azores; ɔf di Kep di Vɛd; na di Plɛt (we dɛn kɔl so), we de kɔmɔt na di Rio de la Plata in mɔt; ɛn di Carrol Ground, we na wan ples we nɔ gɛt stak, we gɛt wata, we de na di sawt pat frɔm Sɛnt Ɛlɛna.

Na we dɛn bin de glid tru dɛn las wata ya, wan nɛt we bin gɛt kol at ɛn we di mun bin de shayn, we ɔl di wef dɛn bin de rɔl pas lɛk skrol dɛn we dɛn mek wit silva; ɛn, bay dɛn saf, suffusing seethings, mek wetin tan lɛk silva silɛns, nɔto fɔ de wansay; na da kayn nɛt de we nɔbɔdi nɔ bin de tɔk, dɛn bin si wan silva jet fa bifo di wayt bɔbul dɛn we bin de na di bɔw. Di mun bin layt am, i bin tan lɛk se na ɛvin; i bin tan lɛk se sɔm gɔd dɛn we gɛt plum ɛn we de shayn bin de tɔn agens di si. Fedallah bin fɔs diskrayb dis jet. Bikɔs pan dɛn nɛt ya we di mun kin shayn, na in i bin de yus fɔ go ɔp di men-mast ed, ɛn tinap fɔ luk-ɔut de, wit di sem prɛsishɔn lɛk se na de. Ɛn pan ɔl dat, pan ɔl we dɛn bin de si bɔku bɔku wɛl dɛn na nɛt, nɔto wan wɛl man pan wan ɔndrɛd pipul dɛn go tray fɔ put dɛn dɔŋ. So, yu go tink bɔt aw di pipul dɛn we de wok na di si bin si dis ol pɔsin we kɔmɔt na di Ist de sidɔm ɔp na dis kayn awa dɛn we nɔ kɔmɔn; in turban ɛn di mun, we na kɔmpin dɛn na wan skay. Bɔt we, afta i dɔn spɛn in yunifom intaval de fɔ sɔm nɛt dɛn we de afta dɛnsɛf we i nɔ tɔk wan sawnd; we, afta ɔl dis kwayɛt, dɛn yɛri in vɔys we nɔ de na di wɔl de anawns da silva, mun layt jet de, ɛvri marina we de ledɔm bigin fɔ tinap lɛk se sɔm spirit we gɛt wing dɔn layt na di rig, ɛn prez di kru we de day. “Na de i de blo!” If dɛn bin dɔn blo di trɔmp fɔ jɔj, dɛn nɔ bin fɔ dɔn shek pas dat; yet stil dɛn nɔ bin fil ɛni fred; bifo dat, na fɔ ɛnjɔy yusɛf. Bikɔs pan ɔl we na bin awa we dɛn nɔ bin dɔn yus fɔ du, bɔt di kray bin rili impɔtant, ɛn i bin so de mek pɔsin gladi, dat na lɛk ɔl di sol dɛn we bin de insay di bot bin want fɔ lɛ dɛn put am dɔŋ.

We Eab bin de waka na di dɛk wit kwik, sayd-lunging strid, i kɔmand fɔ mek dɛn sɛt di t’gallant sel dɛn ɛn di kiŋ dɛn, ɛn ɛni stunsel bin skata. Di bɛst man na di ship fɔ tek di edman. Dɔn, wit ɛni mast-hɛd we gɛt pipul dɛn, di kraft we dɛn bin dɔn gɛda bin de rol dɔŋ bifo di briz. Di strenj, upheaving, lifting tendency of di taffrail briz we de ful di hol dɛm fɔ bɔku bɔku sel dɛm, mek di buoyant, hovering deck fil lɛk ɛya ɔnda di fut dɛm; we stil i bin de rɔsh go, lɛk se tu tin dɛn we de agens am de tray tranga wan insay am—wan fɔ go ɔp dairekt na ɛvin, di ɔda wan fɔ drayv yawingly to sɔm ɔrizɔntal gol. Ɛn if yu bin dɔn wach Eab in fes da nɛt de, yu fɔ dɔn tink se insay insɛf tu difrɛn tin dɛn de fɛt. Pan ɔl we in wan layf leg bin de mek layf layf ikɔ dɛn along di dɛk, ɛni strok pan in day an ɛn fut bin de sawnd lɛk kɔfin-tap. Na layf ɛn day dis ol man bin waka. Bɔt pan ɔl we di ship bin de spid so kwik, ɛn pan ɔl we frɔm ɔl di yay dɛn, lɛk aro, di glans dɛn we dɛn bin want fɔ luk bin de shot, bɔt stil dɛn nɔ bin si di silva jet igen da nɛt de. Ɛni man we de wok na di bot bin swɛ se i si am wan tɛm, bɔt nɔto di sɛkɔn tɛm.

Dis midnayt-spɔt bin dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ mek i bi tin we dɛn dɔn fɔgɛt, we, sɔm dez afta dat, lo! na di sem awa we pipul dɛn nɔ bin de tɔk, dɛn bin anawns am bak: ɔlman bin tɔk bɔt am bak; bɔt we i mek sel fɔ ɔvatak am, wan tɛm bak i lɔs lɛk se i nɔ ɛva lɔs. Ɛn so i bin de sav wi nɛt afta nɛt, te nɔbɔdi nɔ lisin to am pas fɔ wɔnda am. Misteriously jetted in di klia mun layt, ɔ sta layt, as i kin bi; fɔ lɔs bak fɔ wan ol de, ɔ tu dez, ɔ tri dez; ɛn sɔm kayn we i bin tan lɛk se na ɛvri difrɛn ripitishɔn de go bifo mɔ ɛn mɔ na wi vɛn, dis solitary jet bin tan lɛk se i de mek wi want fɔ go sote go.

Nɔto wit di lay lay biliv we dɛn nɔ bin de mɛmba bɔt dɛn rays, ɛn akɔdin to di prɛtɛnaturalnɛs, as i bin tan lɛk, we pan bɔku tin dɛn bin invɛst di Pequod, dɛn nɔ bin de want sɔm pan di siman dɛn we bin swɛ dat ɛnitɛm ɛn ɛnisay we dɛn tɔk bɔt; pan ɔl we rimot tɛm dɛn, ɔ pan ɔl we di latitud ɛn lɔnjitud dɛn we de fa frɔm dɛnsɛf, na wan wɛl we insɛf sɛf bin de trowe da spɔt we nɔ de nia; ɛn da wɛl de, we nem Mobi Dik. Fɔ sɔm tɛm, wan spɛshal fred bin de rul bak pan dis flitting aparishin, lɛk se i de tret wi fɔ go bifo ɛn go bifo, so dat di monsta go tɔn rawnd pan wi, ɛn rend wi las las na di si dɛn we de fa ɛn we gɛt savayj pas ɔl.

Dɛn tɛmporari fred ya, we so vayg bɔt so bad bad, bin gɛt wan wɔndaful pawa frɔm di kɔntrast sereniti fɔ di wɛda, we, ɔnda ɔl in blu blandnɛs, sɔm bin tink se wan dɛbul charm de lurk, as fɔ de ɛn dez wi bin de travul along, tru si so taya, lonesomely mild, dat ɔl di spes, in repugnance to wi vengeful errand, bin tan lɛk se i de vacating insɛf fɔ layf bifo wi prow we tan lɛk urn.

Bɔt, las las, we wi tɔn to di ist, di Kep briz bigin fɔ ala rawnd wi, ɛn wi grap ɛn fɔdɔm pan di lɔng, trɔbul si dɛn we de de; we di Pequod we gɛt ayvri-tusk shap shap wan butu to di blast, ɛn chuk di dak wev dɛn insay in madnɛs, te, lɛk shawa we gɛt silva chips, di fom-flɛks dɛn flay oba in bulwark dɛn; dɔn ɔl dis layf we nɔ gɛt natin nɔ bin de igen, bɔt i bin gi ples fɔ si tin dɛn we bin rili bad pas aw i bin de bifo.

Klostu to wi bo, strenj fɔm dɛn na di wata bin de rɔn ya ɛn de bifo wi; we tik na wi biɛn bin de flay di si-rɛvɛn dɛn we wi nɔ ebul fɔ ɔndastand. Ɛn ɛvri mɔnin, we wi sidɔm na di say dɛn we wi de, dɛn bin de si dɛn bɔd dɛn ya row dɛn; ɛn pan ɔl we wi bin de ala, fɔ lɔng tɛm, wi bin de tay tranga wan pan di hεmp, lɛk se dɛn tek wi ship as kraft we de drɛf, we nɔbɔdi nɔ de de; wan tin we dɛn dɔn pik fɔ pwɛl, ɛn dat mek i fit fɔ mek dɛn slip fɔ dɛnsɛf we nɔ gɛt os. Ɛn i bin de hev ɛn hev, stil de hev di blak si we nɔ de rɛst, lɛk se in big big tayd dɛn na kɔnshɛns; ɛn di big mundane sol bin de insay pen ɛn rigrɛt fɔ di lɔng sin ɛn sɔfa we i bin dɔn briŋ.

Cape of Good Hope, dɛn de kɔl una? Rather Cape Tormentoso, as dɛn bin de kɔl am trade; fɔ lɔng tɛm we wi bin dɔn mek wi gladi fɔ di lay lay silɛns dɛn we bin dɔn atɛnd wi bifo, wi si se dɛn dɔn lanch wi na dis si we dɛn bin de sɔfa, usay pipul dɛn we gilti bin chenj to dɛn bɔd dɛn de ɛn dɛn fish ya, i bin tan lɛk se dɛn dɔn kɔndɛm dɛn fɔ swim sote go we dɛn nɔ gɛt ɛni say fɔ ayd, ɔ bit da blak briz de we nɔ gɛt ɛni ɔrayzin. Bɔt i kol, i wayt lɛk sno, ɛn i nɔ de chenj; stil de dayrɛkt in watawɛl we gɛt fɛda dɛn to di skay; stil de mek sayn fɔ wi fɔ go bifo, sɔntɛnde dɛn go tɔk bɔt di jet we de in wan.

Insay ɔl dis blak we di tin dɛn we bin de apin na di wɔl bin blak, Eab, pan ɔl we i bin de tek di tɛm fɔ di tɛm we i bin de kɔmand di dɛk we bin dɔn dɔti ɛn we bin denja, i bin sho se i bin de na di say we di ples bin rili dak; ɛn i nɔ bin de tɔk to in kɔmpin dɛn pas ɛni ɔda tɛm. Insay tɛm dɛn we big big briz kin blo lɛk dis, afta dɛn dɔn mek ɔltin we de ɔp ɛn ɔp, dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ du natin pas fɔ wet fɔ di prɔblɛm we di big big briz de blo. Dɔn Kpɛntin ɛn kru kin bi prɛktikal fatalist dɛn. So, wit in ayvri leg we dɛn put insay di ol we i dɔn yus fɔ, ɛn wit wan an we ol wan shroud tranga wan, Eab go tinap fɔ lɔng lɔng tɛm ɛn de luk day to di briz, ɛn wan wan tɛm we swɛlin ɔ sno kin blo, i go jɔs tan lɛk se in yon aylash dɛn jɔyn togɛda. Insay da tɛm de, di wan dɛn we de wok na di ship we di bad bad si dɛn we bin de brok brok oba in bɔw dɛn bin de drɛb frɔm di fɔs pat na di ship, bin tinap na wan layn nia di wɔl dɛn we de na di wes; ɛn di mɔ we i bɛtɛ fɔ gayd di wef dɛn we de jomp, ɛni man bin dɔn slip insɛf insay wan kayn bolayn we dɛn tay pan di rel, we i bin de swing lɛk se i de insay bɛlt we dɔn lus. Na smɔl wɔd dɛn nɔmɔ dɛn bin tɔk ɔ dɛn nɔ bin tɔk; ɛn di ship we nɔ bin de tɔk, lɛk se na pipul dɛn we de wok na di si we dɛn dɔn pent wit waks de wok, de afta de, bin de kɔt ɔl di kwik kwik wan we di dɛbul wef dɛn bin de mek ɛn gladi at. Na nɛt di sem mumu we mɔtalman bin de muf bifo di kray we di si bin de ala bin de win; stil insay kwayɛt di man dɛn bin de swing na di bowlayn dɛn; stil Eab we nɔ gɛt wɔd bin tinap fɔ di bɔm. Ivin we i bin tan lɛk se nature we taya de aks fɔ rɛst i nɔ go fɛn da rɛst de na in hamak. Starbuck nɔ go ɛva fɔgɛt di ol man in aspek, we wan nɛt we i de go dɔŋ insay di kabin fɔ mak aw di baromita tinap, i si am wit yay we lɔk sidɔm stret na in chia we dɛn skru na di flɔ; di ren ɛn di slit we bin dɔn af-af we di big big briz we i bin dɔn gɛt sɔm tɛm bifo bin de kɔmɔt, bin stil de drɔp smɔl smɔl frɔm di hat ɛn kot we dɛn nɔ pul. Na di tebul we bin de nia am, dɛn bin dɔn opin wan pan dɛn chɛt dɛn we gɛt tayd ɛn kɔrɛnt we dɛn bin dɔn tɔk bɔt bifo tɛm. In lantan bin de swing frɔm in an we bin tayt tayt wan. Pan ɔl we di bɔdi bin tinap, dɛn bin de trowe di ed bak so dat di yay dɛn we dɛn lɔk go pɔynt to di nidul fɔ di tɛl-tɛl we bin de swing frɔm wan bim we de na di siling.*

*Dɛn kɔl di kabin-kɔmpas di tɛl-tɛl, bikɔs if i nɔ go to di kɔmpas we de na di ed, di Kpɛntin, we i de dɔŋ, i kin tɛl insɛf bɔt di kɔs we di ship de go.

Terrible ol man! tink Starbuck wit wan shudder, slip in dis gale, stil yu stedi de ayst yu purpose.





CHAPTER 52. Di Albatross.

Sawt-ist frɔm di Kep, ɔf di fa fa Krozɛt, wan gud kruiz grɔn fɔ Rayt Wɛlman, wan sel bin de kam bifo, di Goney (Albatross) in nem. As i de kam nia am smɔl smɔl, frɔm mi ay ay ples we a sidɔm na di fɔs-mast-hɛd, a bin si gud gud wan fɔ si da tin de we rili wɔndaful to wan tayro we de fishin na di fa fa si—wan man we de kech wɛl na si, ɛn i nɔ bin de na os fɔ lɔng tɛm.

I tan lɛk se di wef dɛn dɔn ful-ɔp, dɛn bin blich dis kraft lɛk di skel we wan walrus we dɔn stɔp. Ɔl dɔŋ in sayd dɛn, dis spɛktral apinans bin tray wit lɔng chanɛl dɛn we gɛt rɔst we dɔn rɛd, we ɔl in spars ɛn in rig dɛn bin tan lɛk di tik tik branch dɛn na tik dɛn we ful-ɔp wit hoar-frost. Na in sel dɛn we bin de dɔŋ nɔmɔ dɛn bin put. Wan wayl sight na fɔ si in lɔng-biad luk-ɔut pan dɛn tri mast-hɛd dɛn de. I bin tan lɛk se dɛn wɛr animal dɛn skin, so dɛn bin kɔt ɛn kɔt di klos we bin dɔn sev fɔ lɛk 4 ia so we dɛn bin de travul wit bot. We dɛn tinap wit ayɛn hoop dɛn we dɛn nel na di mast, dɛn bin de swɛla ɛn swing oba wan si we nɔ ebul fɔ ɔndastand; ɛn pan ɔl we, we di ship bin de glid smɔl smɔl nia wi stɛp, wi siks man dɛn we bin de na di ays bin kam nia wisɛf dat i go lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ wi jomp frɔm di mast-ed dɛn na wan ship to di wan dɛn we de na di ɔda wan; bɔt stil, dɛn fishaman dɛn de we bin de luk lɛk se dɛn nɔ gɛt natin, we bin de luk wi saful wan as dɛn de pas, nɔ bin tɔk wan wɔd to wi yon pipul dɛn we bin de luk wi, we dɛn bin de yɛri di ays blɔk we bin de na di kwata dɛk frɔm dɔŋ.

“Ship ahoy! Yu dɔn si di Wait Wɛl?”

Bɔt as di strenj kapten, we bin de ledɔm oba di pallid bulwark dɛn, bin de du di akt fɔ put in trɔmpɛt na in mɔt, sɔm kayn we i fɔdɔm frɔm in an insay di si; ɛn di briz we bin de blo naw, i bin tray fɔ natin fɔ mek pipul dɛn yɛri insɛf if i nɔ gɛt am. Meantime in ship bin stil de inkrisayz di distans bitwin. We di siman dɛn na di Pɛkud bin de sho difrɛn we dɛn we dɛn nɔ bin de si dis bad bad tin we apin we dɛn jɔs tɔk bɔt di Wait Wɛl in nem to ɔda ship, Eab bin stɔp fɔ smɔl tɛm; i bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ i tan lɛk se i fɔ dɔn put bot dɔŋ fɔ go insay di strenja, if di briz we bin de trɛtin am nɔ bin dɔn ban am. Bɔt we i tek advantej pan in pozishɔn we di briz de blo, i ol in trɔmpɛt bak, ɛn we i no bay in aspek se di strenja bot na Nantucketer ɛn i nɔ tu te, i de go na os, i ala lawd wan—“Ahoy de! Dis na di Pequod, we dɛn dɔn tay rawnd di wɔl! Tɛl dɛn fɔ adrɛs ɔl di lɛta dɛn we go kam to di Pasifik si! ɛn dis tɛm fɔ tri ia, if a nɔ de na os, tɛl dɛn fɔ adrɛs dɛn to ——”

Na da tɛm de di tu wek dɛn bin krɔs fayn fayn wan, ɛn wantɛm wantɛm, akɔdin to dɛn wan wan we, smɔl smɔl fish dɛn we nɔ de du bad, we fɔ sɔm dez bifo dat, bin de swim kwayɛt wan nia wi sayd, bin rɔn go wit wetin bin tan lɛk se dɛn de shek shek, ɛn dɛn bin de rɔn bifo ɛn biɛn wit di strenja in flanks. Pan ɔl we insay di kɔs fɔ in kɔntinyu fɔ travul, bɔku tɛm bifo dat, Eab go dɔn notis wan sem kayn tin bifo, bɔt, to ɛni man we de tink wan man, di smɔl smɔl tin dɛn we nɔ impɔtant kin gɛt minin.

“Una swim go nia mi, nɔto so?” Eab bin de grɔmbul, i bin de luk oba di wata. I bin tan lɛk se na smɔl tin nɔmɔ bin de insay di wɔd dɛn, bɔt di tɔyn bin sho mɔ dip sɔri-at we nɔ ebul fɔ ɛp pas aw di ol man we bin dɔn kray bin dɔn ɛva sho. Bɔt we i tɔn to di stiarman, we te naw dɔn de ol di ship na di briz fɔ mek i nɔ go bifo, i ala wit in ol layɔn vɔys se,—“Up helm! Kip am fɔ go rawnd di wɔl!”

Raun di wɔl! Bɔku tin de insay da sawnd de fɔ mek pɔsin fil prawd; bɔt usay ɔl da say we de rawnd di wɔl de du? Na tru bɔku bɔku prɔblɛm dɛn nɔmɔ te to di say we wi bigin, usay di wan dɛn we wi lɛf biɛn sikrit, bin de bifo wi ɔltɛm.

If dis wɔl bin bi ples we nɔ gɛt ɛnd, ɛn bay we wi bin de sel go na di ist wi go ebul fɔ rich nyu distans dɛn sote go, ɛn fɛn tin dɛn we swit ɛn strenj pas ɛni Sayklɔd ɔ Ayland we Kiŋ Sɔlɔmɔn bin de, dat min se prɔmis bin de insay di trip. Bɔt fɔ fɛn dɛn fa fa mistɛri dɛn de we wi de drim bɔt, ɔ fɔ rɔnata da dɛbul phantom de we, sɔm tɛm ɔ ɔda tɛm, de swim bifo ɔl mɔtalman at wit sɔfa; we dɛn de rɔnata dɛn kayn pipul dɛn de oba dis rawnd glob, dɛn kin lid wi na mazi we nɔ gɛt natin ɔ dɛn kin lɛf wi midul.
