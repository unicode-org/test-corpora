CHAPTER 89. Fast-Fish ɛn Lus-Fish.

Di allusion to di waif ɛn waif-poles na di las chapta bɔt wan, nid sɔm akɔdin to di lɔ ɛn rigyuleshɔn dɛn fɔ di wɛl fisha, we dɛn kin tek di waif as di big simbul ɛn badge.

Bɔku tɛm i kin apin se we bɔku ship dɛn de krɔs wit kɔmpin, wan bot kin bit wan wɛl, dɔn i kin rɔnawe, ɛn leta ɔda bot kin kil am ɛn kech am; ɛn na ya dɛn indaykt wan kɔmprɛs bɔku smɔl smɔl kɔntinjɛns dɛn, ɔl de tek pat pan dis wan big ficha. Fɔ ɛgzampul,—afta dɛn dɔn taya ɛn denja we dɛn dɔn rɔnata ɛn kech wɛl, di bɔdi kin kɔmɔt na di ship bikɔs ɔf wan big big briz; ɛn we i de drɛf fa fawe go na di say we di briz de blo, sɛkɔn wɛlman go tek am bak, we, we i kol, i de pul am nia nia, ɛn i nɔ de put layf ɔ layn pan denja. So di agyumɛnt dɛn we de mɔna ɛn we gɛt fɛt-fɛt pas ɔl go de bitwin di fishaman dɛn, if nɔto sɔm lɔ we dɛn rayt ɔ we dɛn nɔ rayt, we de ɔlsay na di wɔl, we nɔ gɛt wan agyumɛnt, we dɛn go yuz fɔ ɔl di kes dɛn.

Sɔntɛm di wangren fɔmal kɔd we di lɔ bin alaw fɔ kil wɛl, na di wan we di lɔ bin mek, na di wan we bin de na Ɔland. Na di Stet-Jɛnɛral bin disayd am insay AD 1695. Bɔt pan ɔl we no ɔda neshɔn nɔ ɛva gɛt ɛni lɔ we dɛn rayt bɔt fɔ kil wɛl, stil di Amɛrikan fishaman dɛn dɔn bi dɛn yon lɔya ɛn lɔya dɛn pan dis. Dɛn dɔn gi wan sistɛm we fɔ shɔt kɔmprɛhnsivnɛs pas Jastinian in Pandekt ɛn di Bay-lɔ dɛn fɔ di Chaynish Sɔsayti fɔ di Sɔpreshɔn fɔ Meddling wit ɔda Pipul dɛn Biznɛs. Yɛs; dɛn lɔ ya kin rayt pan Kwin An in farthing, ɔ di barb fɔ wan harpun, ɛn wɛr dɛn rawnd di nɛk, so dɛn smɔl.

I. Wan Fast-Fish na di pati fast to am.

II. Wan Loose-Fish na fayn gem fɔ ɛnibɔdi we go ebul fɔ kech am kwik kwik wan.

Bɔt wetin de ple di mischief wit dis masta kɔd na di admirable brevity of it, we nid fɔ gɛt bɔku bɔku kɔmɛnt fɔ ɛksplen am.

Fɔs: Wetin na Fast-Fish? Fish we de alayv ɔ we dɔn day, i kin fast pan tɛknikal wan, we dɛn kɔnɛkt am wit ship ɔ bot we pipul dɛn dɔn de, bay ɛni mɛdiɔm we di pɔsin we de insay ɔ di wan dɛn we de insay kin kɔntrol atɔl,—mast, ɔa, nayn inch kebul, tɛligraf waya, ɔ wan strɛch we gɛt kɔba, ɔltin na di sem. Semweso fish teknikli fast we i de bia waif, ɔ ɛni ɔda sayn we dɛn no se i gɛt; so lɔng as di pati we de waif am de sho klia wan se dɛn ebul fɔ tek am nia ɛni tɛm, ɛn bak se dɛn want fɔ du dat.

Dis na sayɛns kɔmɛnt dɛn; bɔt di kɔmɛnt dɛn we di wɛlman dɛnsɛf kin tɔk sɔntɛnde kin gɛt wɔd dɛn we at ɛn nak we at pas dat—di Kɔk-ɔp-Litltɔn we dɛn kin yuz fɔ mek di fist. Na tru se, pan di wɛlman dɛn we de du tin tret ɛn we gɛt ɔnɔ, dɛn kin alaw ɔltɛm fɔ pekul kes dɛn, usay i go bi wan bad bad injɔstis fɔ gud gud wan fɔ mek wan pati se i gɛt wɛl we ɔda pati bin dɔn rɔnata ɔ kil bifo. Bɔt ɔda pipul dɛn nɔ de tek tɛm so atɔl.

Sɔm fifti ia so dɔn pas, wan kɔriɔs kes bin de we dɛn bin de kɛr go na Ingland bɔt wɛl-trova, usay di wan dɛn we bin de ple di kes bin tɔk se afta dɛn dɔn rɔnata wɛl tranga wan na di Nɔtan si dɛn; ɛn we fɔ tru, dɛn (di wan dɛn we de ple di kes) bin dɔn ebul fɔ harpuon di fish; dɛn bin dɔn las, bikɔs dɛn layf bin de pan denja, dɛn nɔ bin jɔs fɔ lɛf dɛn layn dɛn, bɔt dɛn bot sɛf. Dɔn di wan dɛn we dɛn bin de fɛt (di wan dɛn we de wok na ɔda ship) bin kam wit di wɛl, dɛn bin bit am, kil am, tek am, ɛn leta dɛn tek am bifo di wan dɛn we bin de ple di kes in yay. Ɛn we dɛn bin de tɔk bad bɔt dɛn difendant dɛn de, dɛn kapten bin snɛp in finga dɛn na di wan dɛn we de ple di kes dɛn tit, ɛn mek dɛn no se bay we i de du doksɔlɔji to di dɛd we i dɔn du, naw i go kip dɛn layn, hapun, ɛn bot, we bin dɔn kɔntinyu fɔ tay pan di wɛl di tɛm we dɛn tek am. So di wan dɛn we de ple di kes naw bin kɛr dɛn go kɔt fɔ mek dɛn gɛt bak di valyu fɔ dɛn wɛl, layn, harpun, ɛn bot.

Mista Erskine na bin advaysa fɔ di wan dɛn we dɛn bin de fɛt; Na Lɔd Ɛlinbɔro na bin di jɔj. We dɛn bin de fɛt, di witty Erskine bin kɔntinyu fɔ sho in pozishɔn, bay we i tɔk bɔt wan kraym we i jɔs du. kɔn. kes, we wan jentlman, afta we i tray fɔ kɔntrol in wɛf in bad bad tin dɛn fɔ natin, dɔn lɛf am las las na di si dɛn we gɛt layf; bɔt as di ia dɛn de go, we i ripɛnt fɔ da step de, i bigin fɔ du sɔntin fɔ mek i gɛt am bak. Erskine bin de na di ɔda say; ɛn afta dat i sɔpɔt am bay we i se, pan ɔl we di jentlman bin dɔn yuz harpoon di uman fɔs, ɛn wan tɛm i bin dɔn fast, ɛn na bikɔs ɔf di big strɛs we i bin gɛt we i bin de du bad bad tin dɛn we bin de go dɔŋ, i bin dɔn lɛf am las las; yet lɛf am i du, so dat i bi fish we nɔ gɛt natin; ɛn na dat mek we wan jentlman we bin kam afta dat bin ri-harpuon am, di uman bin bi da jentlman we bin kam afta dat in prɔpati, wit ɛni hapun we dɛn bin fɔ dɔn fɛn we de stik insay am.

Naw insay di kes we wi de naw, Erskine bin tɔk se di ɛgzampul dɛn bɔt di wɛl ɛn di uman bin de sho dɛnsɛf.

Dɛn pled ya, ɛn di kɔwnta pled, we dɛn yɛri di rayt we, di jɔj we rili lan buk insay sɛt tɛm dɛn disayd, fɔ wit,—Se as fɔ di bot, i gi am to di wan dɛn we de ple di kes, bikɔs dɛn jɔs dɔn lɛf am fɔ sev dɛn layf; bɔt se we i kam pan di wɛl, hapun, ɛn layn we dɛn bin de agyu bɔt, na di wan dɛn we dɛn bin de fɛt; di wɛl, bikɔs na bin Luz-Fish di tɛm we dɛn bin kech am fɔ di las tɛm; ɛn di harpun ɛn layn bikɔs we di fish mek ɔf wit dɛn, i (di fish) kin gɛt prɔpati na dɛn atikul dɛn de; ɛn na dat mek ɛnibɔdi we tek di fish afta dat bin gɛt rayt fɔ gɛt dɛn. Naw di difendant dɛn afta dat tek di fish; ergo, di atikul dɛn we wi bin dɔn tɔk bɔt na dɛn yon.

Wan kɔmɔn man we de luk dis disizhɔn we di Jɔj we rili lan buk, kin pɔsibul fɔ agens am. Bɔt we dɛn plow am to di praymar rɔk fɔ di mata, di tu big prinsipul dɛn we dɛn put dɔŋ insay di twin wɛl wɛl lɔ dɛn we wi bin dɔn kot, ɛn we Lɔd Ɛlinbɔro bin yuz ɛn ɛksplen am klia wan insay di kes we wi dɔn tɔk bɔt ɔp; dɛn tu lɔ ya we de tɔch Fast-Fish ɛn Loose-Fish, a se, we yu tink bɔt am, dɛn go si am di fawndeshɔnal tin dɛn fɔ ɔl mɔtalman lɔ; bikɔs pan ɔl we di Tɛmpl we dɛn mek wit skapul gɛt kɔmplikt, di Lɔ Tɛmpl, lɛk di Filistin dɛn Tɛmpl, gɛt tu tin dɛn nɔmɔ fɔ tinap pan.

Yu nɔ tink se na wɔd we de na ɔlman in mɔt se, Posɛshɔn na af pan di lɔ: dat min se, ilɛk aw di tin kam insay? Bɔt bɔku tɛm, fɔ gɛt sɔntin na di wan ol lɔ. Wetin na di sinew ɛn sol fɔ Rɔshian slev ɛn Ripɔblikan slev dɛn pas Fast-Fish, we posɛshɔn na di wan ol lɔ? Wetin to di rapacious landlord na di widow in las mit bɔt na Fast-Fish? Wetin na yonder undetected villain’s marble mansion wit wan doa-plet fɔ wan waif; wetin na dat pas wan Fast-Fish? Wetin na di ruinous discount we Mordecai, di brɔka, kin gɛt frɔm po Woebegone, di bankrupt, pan lɔn fɔ mek Woebegone in famili nɔ angri; wetin na dat ruinous diskont bot na Fast-Fish? Wetin na di Akbishɔp fɔ Savesoul in inkɔm we na £100,000 we dɛn seiz frɔm di smɔl bred ɛn chiz fɔ ɔndrɛd tawzin brok-bak lebra dɛn (ɔl shɔ se ɛvin nɔ gɛt ɛni wan pan Savesoul in ɛp) wetin na da globular £100,000 de bɔt na Fast-Fish? Wetin na di Duke of Dunder in hereditary taun en hamlet dem bot Fast-Fish? Wetin to da redoubted harpooneer, John Bull, na po Ayriland, bɔt na Fast-Fish? Wetin to da apɔsul lansa, Brɔda Jonathan, na Tɛksas bɔt na Fast-Fish? Ɛn bɔt ɔl dɛn tin ya, yu nɔ tink se Pɔsin na di wan ol lɔ?

Bɔt if di tichin bɔt Fast-Fish bi prɛti jɔs aplay, di kayn tichin bɔt Luz-Fish stil de mɔ. Dat na tin we dɛn kin yuz ɔlsay na di wɔl ɛn ɔlsay na di wɔl.

Wetin na bin Amɛrika insay 1492 pas wan Luz-Fish, we Kɔlɔmbɔs bin slap di Spanish standad bay we i bin de waif am fɔ in kiŋ in masta ɛn in masta? Wetin na bin Polan to di Czar? Wetin Gris to di Turk? Wetin India to Ingland? Wetin las las Mɛksiko go bi to Amɛrika? Ɔl di Lus-Fish dɛn.

Wetin na di Rayt dɛm fɔ Mɔtalman ɛn di Fridɔm dɛm fɔ di Wɔl bɔt Lus-Fish? Wetin ɔl man dɛn maynd ɛn opinion bɔt Luz-Fish? Wetin na di prinsipul fɔ biliv pan rilijɔn pan dɛn pas wan Lus-Fish? Wetin to di ostentatious smuggling verbalists na di tinkin of tinka bot Loose-Fish? Wetin na di big glob insɛf pas wan Lɔs-Fish? Ɛn wetin yu bi, rida, bɔt na Luz-Fish ɛn Fast-Fish, bak?

Na jɔs sɔm dez afta dɛn mit di Frɛnchman, na in wan tin we impɔtant pas ɔl bin apin to di wan dɛn we nɔ bin impɔtant pas ɔlman pan di wan dɛn we bin de wok na di Pequod; wan tin we apin we kin mek pɔsin fil bad pas ɔl; ɛn we dɔn ɛnd fɔ gi di sɔmtɛm madly merry ɛn predestinated kraft wit wan liv ɛn ɛva akɔmpan prɔfɛsi fɔ ɛni shattered sikwil go pruv in yon.
