CHAPTER 41. Mobi Dik.

Mi, Ismayl, na bin wan pan da kru de; mi ala ala bin dɔn go ɔp wit di ɔda wan dɛn; dɛn bin dɔn wɛl mi swɛ wit dɛn yon; ɛn a bin de ala strɔng mɔ, ɛn a bin de hama ɛn ol mi swɛ mɔ, bikɔs ɔf di fred we bin de na mi sol. Wan wayl, mistik, sɔri-at filin bin de insay mi; I bin tan lɛk se Eab in fɛt-fɛt we nɔ bin ebul fɔ kil am. Wit gridi yes a lan di istri bɔt da monsta we bin de kil pipul dɛn we mi ɛn ɔl di ɔda wan dɛn bin dɔn tek wi swɛ fɔ fɛt fɛt ɛn fɔ revaŋg agens.

Fɔ sɔm tɛm we dɔn pas, pan ɔl we na intaval dɛn nɔmɔ, di Wait Wɛl we nɔ gɛt ɛnibɔdi, we nɔ de nia ɔda pipul dɛn, bin dɔn de mɔna dɛn si dɛn we nɔ sivilizayt we di Spɛm Wɛl fishaman dɛn kin go mɔs. Bɔt nɔto dɛn ɔl bin no se i de; na wan wan pan dɛn nɔmɔ, we yu kɔmpia am, bin dɔn si am we dɛn no; we di nɔmba we as yet dɔn rili ɛn no se dɛn dɔn gi am fɛt, bin smɔl fɔ tru. Bikɔs, bikɔs ɔf di bɔku bɔku pipul dɛn we de kruz wɛl; di disɔda we dɛn bin de sprink dɛn oba di wan ol wata rawnd, bɔku pan dɛn bin de advantej fɔ push dɛn fɛn along solitary latitudes, so as i nɔ kin izi ɔ nɔ ɛva fɔ wan ol twɛlv mɔnt ɔ mɔ pan wan strɛch, fɔ mit wan singl nyus-telling sel ɔf ɛni kayn; di lɔng we pasmak fɔ ɛni difrɛn trip; di irɛgyulariti fɔ di tɛm dɛn we dɛn kin tek bot frɔm os; ɔl dɛn tin ya, wit ɔda sikɔstɛms, dairekt ɛn indayrɛkt, fɔ lɔng tɛm bin de ambɔg di spred tru di wan ol wɔl-wayd wɛl-flit fɔ di spɛshal individyualizing nyus bɔt Mobi Dik. I nɔ bin izi fɔ dawt, se sɔm bot dɛn we dɛn ripɔt se dɛn dɔn mit, na da kayn tɛm de, ɔ na da kayn mɛridian de, wan Spɛm Wɛl we nɔ kɔmɔn magnitud ɛn bad bad wan, we wɛl, afta i dɔn du big bad bad tin to di wan dɛn we atak am, dɔn rɔnawe kɔmɔt pan dɛn kpatakpata; to sɔm maynd i nɔ bin bi wan prɛzumshɔn we nɔ rayt, a se, se di wɛl we dɛn de tɔk bɔt fɔ dɔn bi nɔto ɔda pɔsin pas Mobi Dik. Yet as of let di Sperm Whale fishery bin dɔn mak wit difrɛn ɛn nɔto unfrequent instances of big ferocity, cunning, ɛn malice in di monsta we dɛn atak; na dat mek i bin bi, se di wan dɛn we bay aksidɛnt bin gi fɛt to Mobi Dik we dɛn nɔ no; dɛn kayn ɔntinman dɛn de, sɔntɛm, fɔ di bɔku pat, bin satisfay fɔ se di spɛshal fred we i bin de mek, mɔ, lɛk se, na di prɔblɛm dɛn we di Spɛm Wɛl fishaman dɛn gɛt, pas di wan wan tin we mek dɛn de fishin. Na da we de, bɔku tɛm, pipul dɛn bin dɔn de tink bɔt di bad bad tin we Eab ɛn di wɛl bin mit te naw.

Ɛn as fɔ di wan dɛn we, we dɛn bin dɔn yɛri bɔt di Wait Wɛl bifo, bay chans dɛn si am; insay di biginin fɔ di tin dɛn bin gɛt ɛvri wan pan dɛn, ɔlmost, lɛk aw dɛn bin gɛt maynd ɛn nɔ fred fɔ put am dɔŋ fɔ am, lɛk ɛni ɔda wɛl we gɛt da kayn animal de. Bɔt as tɛm de go, dɛn kayn bad bad tin dɛn ya bin apin we dɛn bin de atak dɛn—nɔto jɔs we dɛn bin de atak dɛn an ɛn anklɛ dɛn we bin brok, dɛn an ɛn fut dɛn bin brok, ɔ we dɛn kɔt dɛn bɔdi we bin de it—bɔt dɛn bin kil dɛn te to di las digri we dɛn bin de kil; dɛn ripit disastrous repulses, ɔl de akumulet ɛn pila dɛn terrors pan Moby Dick; dɛn tin dɛn de bin dɔn go fa fɔ shek di trɛnk we bɔku brayt ɔntinman dɛn gɛt, we di stori bɔt di Wait Wɛl bin dɔn kam to dɛn.

Ɛn ɔlkayn wayl rɔmɔr dɛn nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɛgzajarayt, ɛn stil di tru istri dɛn bɔt dɛn tin dɛn ya we bin de kil pipul dɛn, bin de mek pipul dɛn fred mɔ. Bikɔs nɔto jɔs fayn fayn rɔmɔr dɛn kin gro kɔmɔt na di bɔdi fɔ ɔl di bad bad tin dɛn we kin apin we kin mek pɔsin sɔprayz,—as di tik we dɛn dɔn bit de bɔn in fɛns dɛn; bɔt, insay maritaym layf, fa fawe pas di wan we de na terra firma, wayl rɔmɔr dɛn bɔku, ɛnisay we ɛni adekwayt rialiti de fɔ dɛn fɔ klem to. Ɛn as di si pas di land pan dis tin, na so di wɛl fisha pas ɔl ɔda kayn maritaym layf, insay di wɔndaful ɛn fred we di rɔmɔr dɛn we kin skata sɔm tɛm dɛn de. Bikɔs nɔto jɔs wɛlman dɛn lɛk bɔdi we nɔ gɛt da ignorance ɛn lay lay biliv de we ɔl di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot gɛt; bɔt pan ɔl di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot, i nɔ izi fɔ mek dɛn kam nia ɛnitin we rili wɔndaful na di si; fes to fes dɛn nɔ jɔs de yay in big big wɔndaful tin dɛn, bɔt, an to jaw, de gi dɛn fɛt. Yu wan, insay wata dɛn we de fa fawe, dat pan ɔl we yu sel wan tawzin mayl, ɛn pas wan tawzin shore, yu nɔ go kam na ɛni faya-ston we dɛn kɔt kɔt, ɔ ɛnitin we go wɛlkɔm pipul dɛn ɔnda da pat de na di san; insay dɛn kayn latitud ɛn lɔnjitud dɛn de, we i de fala tumɔs da kayn kɔl de lɛk aw i de du, di wɛlman de rap wit inflɔɛns dɛn ɔl we de mek in fansi bɛlɛ wit bɔku wan pawaful bɔn.

So, i nɔ wɔnda we ɛva gɛda volyum frɔm di jɔs transit oba di wayd wata spɛs, di ɔtblɔwn rɔmɔr dɛm bɔt di Wait Wɛl bin du in di ɛnd inkɔrpɔret wit dɛnsɛf ɔl kayn morbid hint, ɛn af-fɔm fœtal sɔgzhɛshɔn dɛm fɔ supɛnachɔral ɛjɛnshi dɛm, we bin dɔn invɛst Mobi Dik wit nyu terorizim we dɛn nɔ borrow frɔm ɛnitin we de apia we pɔsin kin si. So dat bɔku tɛm i bin dɔn mek dis kayn panik, dat na smɔl pipul dɛn we bay dɛn rɔmɔr dɛn de, at ɔl, bin dɔn yɛri bɔt di Wait Wɛl, na smɔl pan dɛn ɔntinman dɛn de bin rɛdi fɔ mit di prɔblɛm dɛn we de na in jaw.

Bɔt ɔda ɛn impɔtant tin dɛn bin stil de we dɛn bin de du. Ivin naw, di ɔrijinal prestij we di Spɛm Wɛl bin gɛt, we dɛn bin de fred fɔ difrɛn frɔm ɔl di ɔda kayn leviathan, dɔn day kɔmɔt na di wɛlman dɛn maynd as bɔdi. Sɔm pipul dɛn de dis de bitwin dɛn, we pan ɔl we dɛn gɛt sɛns ɛn gɛt maynd fɔ gi fɛt to di Grinland ɔ Rayt wɛl, sɔntɛm—ilɛksɛf na bikɔs dɛn nɔ gɛt ɛkspiriɛns pan dɛn wok, ɔ bikɔs dɛn nɔ ebul fɔ du am, ɔ dɛn de shem, dɛn nɔ go gri fɔ tek wan kɔntes wit di Spɛm Wɛl; pan ɔl we i bi, bɔku pipul dɛn de we de fɛt wɛl, mɔ pan dɛn neshɔn dɛn we de kech wɛl we nɔ de sel ɔnda di Amɛrikan flag, we nɔ ɛva mit di Spɛm Wɛl wit ɛnimi, bɔt we dɛn wangren no bɔt di lɛviathan na fɔ di ignoble monsta we dɛn bin de rɔnata fɔs na di Nɔt; we dɛn sidɔm na dɛn hatch, dɛn man ya go yɛri wit pikin intres ɛn fred we de nia faya, to di wayl, strenj stori dɛn bɔt Sawt wɛl. Nor is di pre-eminent tremendousness of di big Sperm Whale eniwe mo filingly comprehended, pas on board of dem prows we stem am.

Ɛn lɛk se di rial tin we dɛn dɔn tɛst naw bɔt in pawa bin dɔn trowe in shado bifo am trade trade trade; wi de si sɔm buk dɛn we de stɔdi bɔt di tin dɛn we Gɔd mek—Olassen ɛn Povelson—we de tɔk se di Spɛm Wɛl nɔto jɔs sɔntin we de mek ɔl ɔda tin dɛn we de na di si kin sɔprayz, bɔt i kin rili bad lɛk aw i kin kɔntinyu fɔ tɔsti fɔ mɔtalman blɔd. Nɔto ivin te to so let tɛm lɛk Cuvier in yon, dɛn nɔ bin dɔn pul dɛn tin ya ɔ ɔlmost di sem tin dɛn we dɛn bin de tink bɔt. Bikɔs insay in Natural History, di Barɔn insɛf tɔk se we dɛn si di Sperm Whale, ɔl di fish dɛn (inklud di shak dɛn) “dɛn kin bit dɛn wit di fred we gɛt layf pas ɔl,” ɛn “bɔku tɛm we dɛn de flay kwik kwik wan, dɛn kin rɔn dɛnsɛf agens di rɔk dɛn wit da kayn fɛt-fɛt so dat kin mek dɛn day wantɛm wantɛm.” Ɛn ɔltogɛda di jenɛral ɛkspiriɛns dɛn na di fisha biznɛs kin chenj dɛn kayn ripɔt dɛn ya; yet insay dɛn ful terribleness, ivin to di bloodthirsty item of Povelson, di superstitious biliv pan dɛm, in sɔm vicissitudes of dɛn vocation, rivaiv insay di maynd dɛm fɔ di hunters.

So dat we dɛn bin fred pasmak wit di rɔmɔr ɛn tin dɛn we bin de tɔk bɔt am, nɔto sɔm pan di fishaman dɛn bin mɛmba, we dɛn bin de tɔk bɔt Mobi Dik, di fɔs tɛm dɛn we dɛn bin de fishin di Spɛm Wɛl, we bɔku tɛm i bin at fɔ mek pipul dɛn we dɔn de praktis fɔ lɔng tɛm na di Rayt wɛl, bigin fɔ fɛt di prɔblɛm dɛn we dis nyu ɛn we nɔ gɛt maynd kin gɛt; dɛn kayn man dɛn de we de protɛst se pan ɔl we dɛn go op se dɛn go rɔnata ɔda leviathan dɛn, bɔt stil fɔ rɔnata ɛn pɔynt lans pan dis kayn aparishin lɛk di Spɛm Wɛl nɔto fɔ mɔtalman we de day. Dat fɔ tray am, i nɔ go ebul fɔ avɔyd fɔ torn insay wan kwik sote go. Na dis ed, sɔm wɔndaful dɔkyumɛnt dɛn de we dɛn kin luk fɔ.

Bɔt pan ɔl dat, sɔm bin de, we ivin pan dɛn tin ya bin rɛdi fɔ gi chase to Moby Dick; ɛn wan nɔmba we stil bɔku pas dat, we dɛn bin chans fɔ yɛri bɔt am fa ɛn we nɔ klia, we nɔ bin gɛt di patikyula tin dɛn bɔt ɛni patikyula bad bad tin, ɛn we nɔ bin gɛt lay lay tin dɛn we bin de wit dɛn, bin rili strɔng fɔ mek dɛn nɔ rɔnawe pan di fɛt if dɛn tɛl dɛn fɔ du dat.

Wan pan di wayl advays dɛn we dɛn tɔk bɔt, as las las kam fɔ gɛt sɔntin fɔ du wit di Wait Wɛl na di maynd fɔ di wan dɛn we lɛk fɔ biliv lay lay tin dɛn, na di kɔnsɛpt we nɔ de na di wɔl se Mobi Dik de ɔlsay; dat dɛn bin rili mit am na difrɛn latitud dɛn wan ɛn di sem tɛm.

Nɔto, pan ɔl we dɛn kayn maynd dɛn de fɔ dɔn biliv, dis prawd nɔ bin gɛt sɔm smɔl sho se i pɔsibul fɔ biliv lay lay tin dɛn. Fɔ lɛk aw dɛn nɔ ɛva tɔk bɔt di sikrit dɛn bɔt di kɔrɛnt dɛn we de na di si, ivin to di risach we gɛt sɛns pas ɔl; so di we dɛn we di Spɛm Wɛl de ayd we i de ɔnda di wata, kin stil bi, bɔku pan dɛn, we nɔ gɛt ɛnitin fɔ du wit di wan dɛn we de rɔnata am; ɛn wan wan tɛm dɛn dɔn ɔriginate di mɔs kɔriɔs ɛn kɔntradiktɔri spɛkulayshɔn dɛn bɔt dɛn, mɔ bɔt di mistik mɔd dɛn usay, afta i dɔn sawnd to wan big dip, i de transpɔt insɛf wit da kayn big big swiftness de to di say dɛn we de fa pas ɔl.

Na tin we Amɛrika ɛn Inglish wɛl-ship dɛn sabi gud gud wan, ɛn bak na tin we Skɔresbi bin put pan pawaful rɛkɔd ia bifo, se dɛn dɔn kech sɔm wɛl dɛn fa fawe na di nɔt pat na di Pasifik, we dɛn dɔn fɛn di barb dɛn we dɛn kɔl harpun dɛn we dɛn kin dart na di Grinland si dɛn na dɛn bɔdi. I nɔ fɔ tɔk bak se insay sɔm pan dɛn tin ya, dɛn dɔn tɔk se di tɛm bitwin di tu asɔlt dɛn nɔ bin fɔ dɔn pas bɔku dez. So, bay inference, sɔm wɛlman dɛn dɔn biliv se di Nor’ West Passage, we bin dɔn bi prɔblɛm fɔ mɔtalman fɔ lɔng tɛm, nɔ bin ɛva bi prɔblɛm to di wɛl. So dat na ya, insay di rial layf ɛkspiriɛns we pipul dɛn we de alayv gɛt, di wɔndaful tin dɛn we dɛn bin de tɔk bɔt trade trade bɔt di inland Strello mawnten na Pɔtyugal (we dɛn bin se nia in tɔp, wan lek de usay di ship dɛn we dɔn pwɛl bin de flɔt ɔp to di wata); ɛn da stori de we stil rili wɔndaful bɔt di Arethusa watawɛl nia Sirakyu (we dɛn bin biliv se in wata kɔmɔt na di Oli Land bay wan rod we de ɔnda grɔn); dɛn fayn fayn stori ya dɔn ɔlmost ikwal to di rial tin dɛn we di wɛlman dɛn de du.

So, dɛn fos am fɔ no bɔt dɛn kayn wɔndaful tin dɛn ya lɛk dɛn wan ya; ɛn we i no se afta we dɛn dɔn atak di Wait Wɛl bɔku tɛm ɛn we nɔ gɛt maynd, i dɔn rɔnawe layf wan; i nɔ go rili sɔprayz fɔ si se sɔm pipul dɛn we de fɛt wɛl fɔ go fa fawe pan dɛn lay lay biliv dɛn; fɔ deklare se Mobi Dik nɔto jɔs de ɔlsay, bɔt i nɔ de day (bikɔs i nɔ de day na bɔt i de ɔlsay insay tɛm); dat pan ɔl we dɛn fɔ plant tik dɛn we gɛt spia na in flank, i go stil swim go ɛn nɔbɔdi nɔ du am; ɔ if fɔ tru, dɛn fɔ ɛva mek i swɛt tik tik blɔd, fɔ si da kayn tin de go bi jɔs fɔ ful pɔsin we rili bad; bikɔs bak insay big big briz dɛn we nɔ gɛt wan blɔd we de fa ɔndrɛd lig dɛn, dɛn go si in jet we nɔ dɔti wan tɛm bak.

Bɔt ivin we dɛn pul dɛn tin ya we dɛn kin tink bɔt we pas mɔtalman, inof bin de insay di wɔl mek ɛn in kɔntestable character of di monsta fɔ strik di imajineshɔn wit pawa we i nɔ bin dɔn yus. Bikɔs, nɔto so in bɔku bɔku wan we nɔ kɔmɔn bin mek i difrɛn frɔm ɔda sperm wɛl dɛn, bɔt, lɛk aw dɛn bin de trowe am ɔdasay—na wan spɛshal fɔrɛst we wayt lɛk sno we gɛt wrinkled, ɛn wan ay, wayt hump we tan lɛk piramid. Dɛn tin ya na bin in impɔtant tin dɛn; di token dɛn we, ivin insay di si dɛn we nɔ gɛt limit, we dɛn nɔ no, i bin de sho in aydentiti, fa fawe, to di wan dɛn we sabi am.

Di ɔda pat pan in bɔdi bin so strɛch, ɛn spat, ɛn mabul wit di sem shrouded hue, dat, na di ɛnd, i bin dɔn gɛt in difrɛn nem fɔ di Wait Wɛl; wan nem, fɔ tru, we rili jɔstifay bay in klia aspek, we dɛn si am de glid na ay noon tru wan dak blu si, we de lɛf wan milky-way wek we gɛt krim fom, ɔl spangled wit gold gleamings.

Nɔto in magnitude we i nɔ bin dɔn yus, ɔ in wɔndaful hue, ɔ yet in deformed lower jaw, bin so inves di wɛl wit natura l terror, as dat unexampled, intelligent malignity we, akɔdin to patikyula akɔdin dɛn, i bin dɔn evins ɔva ɛn ɔva insay in assaults. Mɔ pas ɔl, di we aw i bin de rɔn bak wit trɛtin bin mek pipul dɛn at pwɛl pas sɔntɛm ɛni ɔda tin. Bikɔs, we i bin de swim bifo di wan dɛn we bin de rɔnata am we bin de gladi, wit ɛni sayn we i bin de sho se i de ala, bɔku tɛm dɛn bin dɔn no am se i kin tɔn rawnd wantɛm wantɛm, ɛn we i de bia dɛn, i kin mek dɛn bot dɛn skata, ɔ i kin drɛb dɛn bak wit sɔprayz fɔ go na dɛn ship.

Ɔlrɛdi, bɔku pipul dɛn bin dɔn day we dɛn bin de rɔnata am. Bɔt pan ɔl we dɛn kayn disasta dɛn ya, ilɛksɛf dɛn nɔ bin bɔku na di si, dɛn nɔ bin kɔmɔn atɔl na di fisha biznɛs; yet, bɔku tɛm, i bin tan lɛk se di Wait Wɛl in infɛnal tin we i bin dɔn tink bɔt fɔ du bad bad tin, dat ɛni we we i kɔt in bɔdi ɔ day we i mek, dɛn nɔ bin de tek am ɔl se na wan ɛjɛn we nɔ gɛt sɛns dɔn du am.

So, Jɔj, to us pitch dɛn fɔ inflamed, distracted fury di maynd fɔ in mɔ desperate hunters bin impelled, we midul di chips of chewed bots, ɛn di sink limbs of the torn comrades, dɛn swim kɔmɔt na di wayt curds of di wɛl in bad bad wamat insay di serene, exasperating sanlight, we de smayl on, lɛk se na birth ɔ a a bridal we dɛn kin yuz fɔ mared.

In tri bot dɛn bin de stov rawnd am, ɛn ɔa ɛn man dɛn ɔl tu bin de rɔn rawnd di ed; wan kapten, we tek di layn-naif frɔm in brok brok prow, bin dɔn rɔsh pan di wɛl, as wan Akansas duellist pan in ɛnimi, blaynd wan de luk fɔ wit siks inch bled fɔ rich di fathom-dip layf fɔ di wɛl. Da kapten de na Eab. Ɛn afta dat, i bin swip in ɔda jaw we tan lɛk sikl ɔnda am wantɛm wantɛm, Mobi Dik bin dɔn ripɛnt Eab in leg, lɛk pɔsin we de kɔt gras na di fil. No Turk we gɛt turban, no Venetian ɔ Malay we dɛn haya, nɔ bin fɔ dɔn bit am wit mɔ we i tan lɛk se i gɛt bad at. CHAPTER 42. Di Wait we di Wɛl gɛt.

Wetin di wayt wɛl bin bi to Eab, dɛn dɔn hint am; wetin, sɔntɛnde, i bin bi to mi, as dɛn nɔ tɔk yet.

Apat frɔm dɛn mɔ klia kɔnsidareshɔn dɛn we de tɔch Mobi Dik, we nɔ bin ebul bɔt wan wan tɛm wek insay ɛni man in sol sɔm alam, ɔda tinkin bin de, ɔ rayt we nɔ klia, we nɔ gɛt nem, we bin de fred bɔt am, we sɔntɛnde bay in intensiti bin pas ɔl di ɔda wan dɛn kpatakpata; ɛn yet i bin so mistik ɛn i nɔ bin izi fɔ tɔk bɔt, dat i bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ a nɔ gɛt op fɔ put am insay wan we we pɔsin kin ɔndastand. Na di wayt we di wɛl bin wayt bin mek a fred pas ɔl. Bɔt aw a go op fɔ ɛksplen misɛf ya; ɛn yet, insay sɔm dim, random we, ɛksplen misɛf se a fɔ, ɔdasay ɔl dɛn chapta ya kin bi natin.

Pan ɔl we insay bɔku natura tin dɛn, wayt kin mek pɔsin fayn mɔ ɛn mɔ, lɛk se i de gi sɔm spɛshal gud tin dɛn fɔ insɛf, lɛk mabul, japonika, ɛn pal; ɛn pan ɔl we difrɛn neshɔn dɛn dɔn no sɔm we dɛn se wan patikyula kiŋ de bifo pan dis kɔlɔ; ivin di barbaric, grand old kings na Pegu we put di taytul “Lord of the White Elephants” pas ɔl dɛn ɔda fayn fayn askripshɔn dɛn fɔ rul; ɛn di kiŋ dɛn na Saym tide we de opin di sem sno-wayt 4 fut we de na di kiŋ in standad; ɛn di Hanoverian flag we gɛt di wan figa we gɛt chaja we wayt lɛk sno; ɛn di big Ɔstrian Ɛmpaya, we na Sizarian, we gɛt di rayt fɔ rul di Rom, we gɛt fɔ di kiŋ in kɔlɔ di sem impyɔrian kɔlɔ; ɛn pan ɔl we dis prɛ-ɛminɛns insay de aplay to di mɔtalman rays sɛf, we de gi di wayt man fayn mastaship oba ɛvri dak trayb; ɛn pan ɔl we, apat frɔm ɔl dis, dɛn dɔn ivin mek wayt pɔsin impɔtant fɔ gladi, bikɔs pan di Roman dɛn wan wayt ston bin de mak wan de we pipul dɛn kin gladi; ɛn pan ɔl we insay ɔda sɔri-at ɛn tin dɛn we de sho se pɔsin de day, dɛn kin mek dis sem kɔlɔ bi di sayn fɔ bɔku tin dɛn we kin tɔch pɔsin in at, we gɛt ay pozishɔn—di inosɛns we di yawo dɛn gɛt, di fayn we aw dɛn ol; pan ɔl we pan di Rɛd Man dɛn na Amɛrika fɔ gi di wayt bɛlt we dɛn kɔl wampum na bin di dip prɔmis fɔ ɔnɔ; pan ɔl we na bɔku klaym dɛn, waytnɛs de sho di majesty fɔ Jɔstis insay di ermine of di Jɔj, ɛn i de kɔntribyut to di ɛvride stet fɔ kiŋ ɛn kwin dɛn we milk-wayt stɛd dɛn de drɔ; pan ɔl we ivin insay di ay ay sikrit dɛn na di rilijɔn dɛn we rili ɔganayz, dɛn dɔn mek am di sayn fɔ di divayn we nɔ gɛt wan dɔti ɛn pawa; na di pipul dɛn we de wɔship faya na Pashia, dɛn bin de ol di wayt fɔk faya we oli pas ɔl na di ɔlta; ɛn insay di Grik lay lay stori dɛn, dɛn mek Gret Jove insɛf bi bɔdi insay wan kaw we wayt lɛk sno; ɛn pan ɔl we to di nobul Iroquois, di midwinter sakrifays fɔ di oli Wait Dɔg na bin di oliest festival fɔ dɛn tioloji, dat krichɔ we nɔ gɛt dɔti, fetful krichɔ bin de ol di klin envaysɔ we dɛn kin sɛn to di Gret Spirit wit di nyus ɛvri ia bɔt dɛn yon fetful; ɛn pan ɔl we dɛn kɔmɔt dairekt wan frɔm di Latin wɔd fɔ wayt, ɔl Kristian prist dɛn gɛt di nem fɔ wan pat pan dɛn oli klos, we na di alb ɔ tunik, we dɛn kin wɛr ɔnda di kasɔk; ɛn pan ɔl we pan di oli pomp dɛn we di Romish fet gɛt, dɛn kin yuz wayt spɛshal wan fɔ sɛlibret di Pashɔn we wi Masta gɛt; Dis kwaliti we nɔ izi fɔ si, we de mek di tink bɔt wayt, we dɛn dayvɔs frɔm mɔ kayn padi biznɛs, ɛn kɔpl wit ɛni tin we rili bad insɛf, fɔ mek da fred de go ɔp to di fa fa say. Witnɛs di wayt bea we de na di pol dɛn, ɛn di wayt shak we de na di tropiks; wetin pas dɛn smol, flaky waytnɛs de mek dɛn bi di transcendent horrors we dɛn bi? Dat ghastly whiteness it is which imparts such an abhorrent mildness, ivin mo loathsome dan terrific, to di mumu gloating of dem aspek. So dat nɔto di tayga we gɛt faya faya we wɛr in heraldik kot go mek i gɛt maynd lɛk di bea ɔ shak we gɛt wayt kɔba.*

*Wit rεfrεns to di Polar bear, i kin bi se di wan we go fain go stil dip insay dis mata, se nɔto di wayt, we dɛn tek am separet, we de ayt di intolerable hideousness of dat brut; fɔ, we dɛn analayz, dat ayt hideousness, dɛn kin se, jɔs rayz frɔm di sikɔstɛms, se di irɛspɔnsibul ferociousness fɔ di krichɔ tinap invɛst insay di fleece of celestial inosence ɛn lɔv; ɛn na dat mek, bay we di Polar bea de briŋ tu kayn difrɛn filin dɛn ya togɛda na wi maynd, i de mek wi fred wit wan difrɛns we nɔto fɔ natin. As fɔ di wayt shak, di wayt gliding ghostliness of repose in dat krichɔ, we dɛn si am insay in ɔdinari mud, strenj wan tallies wit di sem kwaliti in di Polar quadruped. Dis peculiarity na di French pipul dem de hit am klia wan fo di nem we dem giv dat fish. Di Romish mis fɔ di wan dɛn we dɔn day bigin wit “Requiem eternam” (rɛst we go de sote go), usay Requiem de kɔl di mis insɛf, ɛn ɛni ɔda myuzik we dɛn kin yuz fɔ bɛr pɔsin. Naw, fɔ tɔk bɔt di wayt, kwayɛt stilnɛs we day de insay dis shak, ɛn di saful day we in abit dɛn de kil, di Frɛnch dɛn kɔl am Requin .

Tink bɔt di albatros, usay dɛn klawd dɛn de we de mek pɔsin wɔnda na Gɔd in yay ɛn we de mek pɔsin fred bad bad wan, we da wayt phantom de de go insay ɔl di imajineshɔn dɛn? Nɔto Coleridge bin fɔs trowe da spɛl de; bɔt Gɔd in big, we nɔ de mek pɔsin gladi, we na Nature.*

*A mɛmba di fɔs albatros we a ɛva si. Na di tɛm we big big briz bin de blo fɔ lɔng tɛm, insay wata we bin at fɔ si na di Antaktik si dɛn. Frɔm mi fɔstɛm wach we de dɔŋ, a go ɔp na di dɛk we gɛt klawd; ɛn de, a rɔsh pan di men hatch dɛn, a si wan rigal, fɛda tin we nɔ gɛt wan spat wayt, ɛn wit wan huk, Roman bil we sublaym. Wan wan tɛm, i kin arch in big big enjɛl dɛn wing dɛn, lɛk se i de ɔg sɔm oli ak. Wonderous flutterings ɛn throbbings bin shek am. Pan ɔl we i nɔ bin gɛt ɛni bad bad tin na in bɔdi, i bin de kray, lɛk sɔm kiŋ in gɔst we de sɔfa pasmak. Tru in inexpressible, strenj yay, methought a peep to sikrit we tek hol of God. As Ebraam bifo di enjɛl dɛn, a butu misɛf; di wayt tin bin so wayt, in wing dɛn bin so wayd, ɛn insay dɛn wata dɛn de we dɛn bin dɔn kɛr go na ɔda kɔntri fɔ ɔltɛm, a bin dɔn lɔs di bad bad wɔp mɛmori dɛn bɔt tradishɔn ɛn tɔŋ dɛn. Long a bin de luk da prodigy of plumage. A nɔ ebul fɔ tɛl, a kin jɔs hint, di tin dɛn we bin dart tru mi da tɛm de. Bɔt las las a wek; ɛn tɔn, aks wan man we de wok na di bot us bɔd dis. A goney, i ansa am. Goney goney! nɔ ɛva yɛri da nem de bifo; yu tink se dis glori tin na tin we pipul dɛn we de na di si nɔ no atɔl! nɔ ɛva! Bɔt sɔm tɛm afta dat, a kam fɔ no se goney na sɔm siman dɛn nem fɔ albatros. So dat bay no possibiliti nɔ bin fɔ dɔn gɛt ɛnitin fɔ du wit dɛn mistik impreshɔn dɛn we na bin mi yon, we a si da bɔd de na wi dɛk. Bikɔs a nɔ bin dɔn rid di Raym da tɛm de, ɛn a nɔ bin no se di bɔd na albatros. Yet, we a de se dis, a de du bɔt indaykt wan de burnish smɔl brayt di nobul mɛrit fɔ di poem ɛn di poet.

So, a de asɛf se insay di wɔndaful bɔdi wayt we di bɔd gɛt, di sikrit we di bɔd gɛt, na in mɔ de ayd; wan trut di mɔ we dɛn de sho pan dis, se bay wan solecism ɔf wɔd dɛn, bɔd dɛn de we dɛn kɔl grey albatross; ɛn dɛn tin ya a dɔn si bɔku tɛm, bɔt a nɔ ɛva si dɛn wit dɛn kayn filin dɛn ya lɛk we a si di Antaktik fɔl.

Bɔt aw dɛn bin dɔn kech di mistik tin? Nɔ wispa am, ɛn a go tɛl am; wit wan trɛtin huk ɛn layn, as di fɔl de flɔt na di si. Las las di Kpɛntin mek wan postman pan am; tay wan lɛta we dɛn mek wit lɛda rawnd in nɛk, wit di tɛm ɛn ples we di ship de; ɛn afta dat, lɛf am fɔ rɔnawe. Bɔt a nɔ dawt, dɛn pul da lɛda tally de, we dɛn min fɔ mɔtalman, na Ɛvin, we di wayt fɔl flay fɔ jɔyn di wing-folding, di invoking, ɛn adoring chɛrubin dɛn!

Di wan we pipul dɛn sabi pas ɔl na wi Wɛstinian anal ɛn Indian tradishɔn na di Wait Sti na di Prɛri; wan fayn fayn chaja we wayt lɛk milk, big yay, smɔl ed, bluf chɛst, ɛn wit di rɛspɛkt we wan tawzin kiŋ dɛn gɛt insay in ay ay kat we de oba. Na in na bin di Xerxes we dɛn bin pik fɔ bɔku bɔku wayl ɔs dɛn, we dɛn paste dɛn da tɛm de, na di Rɔki Mawnt dɛn ɛn di Aleghanies nɔmɔ bin de fɛns dɛn. Na dɛn ed we de bɔn faya i trop am go na di wɛst lɛk da sta we dɛn dɔn pik we ɛvri ivintɛm de lid pan di bɔku bɔku layt dɛn. Di flashing kaskad fɔ in mane, di kɔba kɔmɛt na in tel, inves am wit os dɛn we shayn pas aw gold ɛn silva-bit dɛn bin fɔ dɔn gi am. Wan mɔs impyɔrial ɛn akɛnjɛl aparishin fɔ da wɔl we nɔ bin fɔdɔm, we de na di wɛst, we to di yay dɛn fɔ di ol trap ɛn ɔntinman dɛn bin mek di glori dɛn bak fɔ dɛn fɔstɛm tɛm dɛn de we Adam bin de waka fayn fayn wan lɛk gɔd, blɔf-brɔw ɛn nɔ bin de fred lɛk dis pawaful stɛd. Ilɛksɛf i de mach midul in ɛdman dɛn ɛn mashɛl dɛn na di vɛn we bɔku bɔku grup dɛn bin de we bin de strim am oba di ples dɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnd, lɛk wan Oayo; ɔ if wit in sɔbjɛkt dɛn we de rawnd am we de browz ɔlsay na di ɔrayzin, di Wait Stid bin de galɔp fɔ rivyu dɛn wit wam nos dɛn we de rɛd tru in kol milk; pan ɛni aspek we i bin de prɛzɛnt insɛf, ɔltɛm to di Indian dɛn we gɛt maynd pas ɔlman, dɛn bin de rɛspɛkt am ɛn fred am we dɛn bin de shek shek. Wi nɔ go ebul fɔ aks kwɛstyɔn bak frɔm wetin de na di lay lay rɛkɔd bɔt dis fayn ɔs, se na in spiritual waytnɛs mɔ, we bin so klos am wit divaynship; ɛn se dis divaynship bin gɛt di wan we, pan ɔl we i bin de kɔmand fɔ wɔship, di sem tɛm i bin de mek pipul dɛn fred we nɔ gɛt nem.

Bɔt ɔda instans dɛn de usay dis waytnɛs de lɔs ɔl da aksesɔri ɛn strenj glori we de invɛst am na di Wait Sti ɛn Albatros.

Wetin na di tin we insay di Albino man so pekul we de ripel ɛn bɔku tɛm i de shɔk di yay, as dat sɔntɛnde in yon fambul ɛn fambul dɛn kin et am! Na da wayt we de inves am, tin we dɛn de sho wit di nem we i gɛt. Di Albino mek am fayn lɛk ɔda man dɛn—i nɔ gɛt ɛni bad bad tin we nɔ fayn—ɛn pan ɔl dat, dis jɔs tin we de sho se i wayt ɔlsay na di wɔl, de mek i rili strenj we i rili bad pas di we aw dɛn kin pwɛl bɛlɛ we nɔ fayn pas ɔl. Wetin mek dis fɔ bi so?

Nor, in kwik oda aspek, Nature in her least palpable bot not di less malicious ejensi, fail fo enlist bitwin in fos dis krawn atribyut of di terrible. Frɔm di we aw i gɛt sno, dɛn dɔn kɔl di gɔst we gɛt gauntlet na di Sawt Si dɛn di Wait Skwal. Nɔto bak, insay sɔm istri instans dɛn, dɛn nɔ dɔn pul di art fɔ mek mɔtalman gɛt bad at fɔ ɛp am so pawaful. I rili mek di pawa we da pasej de gɛt na Froissart, we dɛn mask insay di sno simbul fɔ dɛn grup, di Wait Hud dɛn na Gɛnt we nɔ gɛt op igen, kil dɛn beilif na di makit!

Ɛn, pan sɔm tin dɛn, di kɔmɔn, ɛriti ɛkspiriɛns we ɔl mɔtalman gɛt nɔ de sho se dis kɔlɔ pas mɔtalman. I nɔ go ebul fɔ dawt gud gud wan, se di wan kwaliti we pɔsin kin si na di aspek fɔ di wan dɛn we dɔn day we kin mek di pɔsin we de wach am fil bad pas ɔl, na di mabul pallor we de ling de; lɛk se fɔ tru, da pallor de tan lɛk di badge we de mek pɔsin fred na di ɔda wɔl, jɔs lɛk aw pɔsin kin fred we pɔsin kin day ya. Ɛn frɔm da pallor we di wan dɛn we dɔn day gɛt, wi kin borrow di expressive hue of di shroud we wi rap dɛn insay. Ɛn ivin insay wi lay lay biliv dɛn wi nɔ de fel fɔ trowe di sem mantle we gɛt sno rawnd wi phantoms; ɔl di gɔst dɛn we de rayz insay wan fɔg we wayt lɛk milk—Yɛs, we dɛn fred ya de kech wi, lɛ wi ad, se ivin di kiŋ we de fred, we di ivanjelis mek pɔsin insɛf, i de rayd in ɔs we gɛt pallid.

So, insay in ɔda mud dɛm, we de sho ɛni big ɔ gras tin we i want bay waytnɛs, nɔbɔdi nɔ go dinay se insay in profoundest idealized signifyans i kɔl up wan peculiar apparition to di sol.

Bɔt pan ɔl we dɛn nɔ gri wit dis pɔynt, aw mɔtalman fɔ ansa fɔ am? Fɔ analayz am, go tan lɛk se i nɔ pɔsibul. So, wi kin bay di sayt we sɔm pan dɛn instans dɛn de usay dis tin we wayt—pan ɔl we fɔ di tɛm ɔl ɔ in big pat dɔn pul ɔl di dairekt asosieshɔn dɛn we dɛn kɔl fɔ gi am sɔntin we de mek i fred, bɔt pan ɔl dat, dɛn kin si se i de du di sem majik pan wi, ilɛksɛf dɛn chenj am;—so wi kin op fɔ layt pan sɔm chans klu fɔ kɔndɔkt wi to di hiden kɔz we wi de luk fɔ?

Lɛ wi tray. Bɔt pan tin lɛk dis, subtil tin kin apil to subtlety, ɛn if i nɔ imajin, nɔbɔdi nɔ go ebul fɔ fala ɔda pɔsin fɔ go insay dɛn ɔl ya. Ɛn pan ɔl we, wi nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se sɔm pan di tin dɛn we dɛn bin de tink bɔt we dɛn bin de tink bɔt, bɔku man dɛn go dɔn sheb dɛn, bɔt stil sɔntɛm na smɔl pipul dɛn nɔmɔ bin no bɔt dɛn ɔl da tɛm de, ɛn dat mek dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ mɛmba dɛn naw.

Wetin mek to di man we nɔ gɛt tich, we apin fɔ no bɔt di pekul karakta we bin de da tɛm de, di bare menshɔn bɔt Whitsuntide marshal insay di fansi dis kayn lɔng, dray, nɔ tɔk-lɛs prɔseshɔn dɛn we pipul dɛn we de go waka sloslo, we dɛn dɔn put dɔŋ ɛn we dɛn dɔn hud wit nyu sno we dɔn fɔdɔm? Ɔ, to di Protɛstant we nɔ rid, we nɔ gɛt sɛns na di Midul Amɛrika Stet dɛn, wetin mek we dɛn tɔk bɔt wan Wait Fria ɔ wan Wait Nɔn we de pas, de mek pɔsin mɛmba dis kayn statu we nɔ gɛt yay na in sol?

Ɔ wetin de apat frɔm di tradishɔn dɛn fɔ di wɔriɔ ɛn kiŋ dɛn we de na jel (we nɔ go rili akɔn fɔ am) we de mek di Wait Tawa na Lɔndɔn de tɛl so bɔku mɔ strɔng wan pan di imajineshɔn fɔ wan Amɛrikin we nɔ travul, pas dɛn ɔda stori strɔkchɔ dɛn de, in neba dɛn—di Baywɔd Tawa, ɔ ivin di Blɔdi? Ɛn dɛn sublimer tawa dɛn de, di Wait Mawnt dɛn na Nyu Amsha, usay, insay spɛshal mud, da big big ghostliness de kɔmɔt oba di sol we dɛn tɔk bɔt da nem de nɔmɔ, we di tink bɔt Virginia’s Blue Ridge ful-ɔp wit wan saf, dewy, fa fa drim? Ɔ wetin mek, ilɛksɛf na ɔl di latitud ɛn lɔnjitud, di nem fɔ di Wait Si de sho da kayn spɛktralnɛs de oba di fansi, we di wan fɔ di Yɔlɔ Si de mek wi fil bad wit mɔtalman tink bɔt lɔng lɔng aftanun dɛn we dɛn dɔn lak wit mild aftanun dɛn na di wef, we di san we de go dɔŋ pas ɔl ɛn we de slip pas ɔl? Ɔ, fɔ pik wan instans we nɔ impɔtant atɔl, we dɛn jɔs adrɛs to di fansi, wetin mek, we yu de rid di ol fayn fayn stori dɛn na Sɛntral Yurop, “di lɔng pale man” na di Hartz fɔrɛst dɛn, we in pallor we nɔ de chenj nɔ de chenj de glid tru di grɔn na di grov dɛn—wetin mek dis phantom rili bad pas ɔl di whooping imps na di Blocksburg?

Ɛn nɔto, ɔltogɛda, fɔ mɛmba in atkwek dɛn we bin pwɛl di katidral; ɔ di stampedo dɛn we in si dɛn we de mek i fred; ɔ di dray skay we nɔ de ɛva ren we nɔ de kray; nɔto fɔ si in big fil we gɛt spia dɛn we de ledɔm, kɔp-ston dɛn we gɛt wrench, ɛn krɔs dɛn ɔlsay (lɛk kant yad dɛn we gɛt ankɔ ship dɛn); ɛn in avɛnyu dɛn we de nia di siti we gɛt os-wɔl dɛn we de ledɔm pan dɛnsɛf, lɛk wan pak we dɛn dɔn tos wit kad;—nɔto dɛn tin ya nɔmɔ de mek Lima we nɔ gɛt kray wata, di siti we strenj pas ɔl, we sɔri pas ɔl we yu nɔ go si. Bikɔs Lima dɔn tek di wayt vel; ɛn wan ay fred de insay dis wayt we in sɔri-at de. Ol as Pizarro, dis waytnɛs de kip in ruins fɔ sote go nyu; admit nɔto di gladi gladi grɛn we de rɔtin kɔmplit wan; spred oba in brok brok ramparts di rigid pallor fɔ wan apoplexy we de fiks in yon distortions.

A no se, to di kɔmɔn aprɛshɔn, dis fenomen fɔ waytnɛs nɔ kɔnfɛs se na di praymɛnt ɛjɛn fɔ ɛgzajarayt di terror fɔ tin dɛn we ɔdasay terrible; ɛn to di maynd we nɔ de imajin, natin nɔ de we de mek pipul dɛn fred insay dɛn apia dɛn we dɛn bad bad tin to ɔda maynd ɔlmost jɔs de pan dis wan tin we de apin, mɔ we dɛn sho am ɔnda ɛni we atɔl we de kam nia fɔ mumu ɔ fɔ de ɔlsay na di wɔl. Wetin a min wit dɛn tu tin ya, sɔntɛm di ɛgzampul dɛn we de kam biɛn dis kin mek wi no klia wan.

Fɔs: Di pɔsin we de wok na di si, we i de kam nia di kɔst dɛn na ɔda kɔntri dɛn, if na nɛt i yɛri di kray we di wan dɛn we de brok de mek, i kin bigin fɔ wach, ɛn i kin fil jɔs lɛk se i de fred fɔ mek i shap ɔl in pawa; bɔt ɔnda di sem kayn tin, lɛ dɛn kɔl am frɔm in hamak fɔ si in ship we de travul tru wan si we gɛt milk wayt midnayt—lɛk se frɔm di edland dɛn we de rawnd am, bɔku bɔku wayt bea dɛn we dɛn dɔn kɔm de swim rawnd am, dɔn i fil se i de fred we i nɔ tɔk natin, we i biliv lay lay tin dɛn; di phantom we dɛn kɔba di wata we dɔn wayt, rili bad to am lɛk rial gɔst; na fɔ natin di lid de mek i biliv se i stil de ɔf sawnd; at ɛn helm dɛn ɔl tu de go dɔŋ; i nɔ de ɛva rɛst te blu wata de ɔnda am igen. Bɔt stil, usay di man we de wok na di bot de we go tɛl yu se, “Masta, nɔto di fred we a bin de fred fɔ nak rɔk dɛn we ayd, na di fred we a bin de fred fɔ da bad bad wayt we bin mek a fil bad?”

Sɛkɔn: Fɔ di Indian we kɔmɔt na Pɛru, di we aw i de si di Andes we sno de mek i fred ɔltɛm, i nɔ de mek i fred, pas nɔmɔ, sɔntɛm, i jɔs de tink bɔt di frɔst we nɔ de dɔn sote go we de rul na dɛn kayn big ples dɛn de, ɛn di natura l kɔnsɛpt fɔ se i go mek pɔsin fred fɔ lɔs insɛf na dɛn kayn ples dɛn de we nɔ gɛt mɔtalman. Na di sem tin kin apin to di bakwudman na di Wɛst, we wit kɔmparativ indipɛndɛns de si wan prɛri we nɔ gɛt bɔda we gɛt sno we dɛn dɔn drɛb, we nɔ gɛt shado fɔ tik ɔ tik fɔ brok di fiks trans we wayt. Nɔto so di man we de wok na di bot, we i de si di fayn fayn tin dɛn we de na di Antaktik si dɛn; usay sɔntɛnde, bay sɔm infɛnal trik we legerdemain in di pawa dɛn we frɔst ɛn ɛya gɛt, i, we de shek shek ɛn af ship brok, instead fɔ renbo dɛn de tɔk op ɛn kɔrej to in misɛf, de si wetin tan lɛk chɔch yad we nɔ gɛt bɔda de grin pan am wit in smɔl ays monumɛnt dɛn ɛn krɔs dɛn we skata.

Bɔt yu se, methinks se wayt-lid chapta bɔt waytnɛs na jɔs wan wayt flag we dɛn hang aut frɔm wan krayv sol; yu de sɔrɛnda to wan hypo, Ismaɛl.

Tɛl mi, wetin mek dis strɔng yɔŋ kɔlt, we bɔn pikin na sɔm pisful vali na Vɛmɔnt, we de fa frɔm ɔl di animal dɛn we de it animal dɛn—wetin mek na di de we di san de shayn pas ɔl, if yu jɔs shek wan fresh bɔfa klos biɛn am, so dat i nɔ go ivin si am, bɔt i jɔs de smɛl in wayl animal muskiness—wetin mek i go bigin, snor, ɛn wit yay we de bɔs, i go paw di grɔn insay frensies fɔ fred? No mɛmba nɔ de insay am fɔ ɛni gorings fɔ wayl animal dɛn na in grɔn nɔt os, so di strenj muskiness we i de smɛl nɔ go ebul fɔ mɛmba to am ɛnitin we gɛt fɔ du wit di ɛkspiriɛns we di prɔblɛm dɛn we i bin gɛt trade; bikɔs wetin no in, dis Nyu Ingland kɔlt, fɔ di blak bayson dɛn we de fa fawe na Ɔrigɔn?

Nɔ: bɔt na ya yu de si ivin insay wan mumu brut, di instinkt fɔ no di dɛbul biznɛs na di wɔl. Pan ɔl we i de bɔku bɔku mayl frɔm Ɔrigɔn, stil we i de smɛl da savayj mɔsk de, di bayson grup dɛn we de rɔtin, we de rɔtin, de de lɛk di wayl pikin we nɔ gɛt pipul dɛn na di prɛri, we dis wantɛm wantɛm dɛn go de tramp to dɔst.

So, so, di muf rɔlin dɛn we de na wan si we gɛt milk; di blak rɔsh we di frɔst we gɛt fayn fayn mawnten dɛn kin mek; di say dɛn we nɔ gɛt pipul dɛn we de chenj di sno dɛn we de blo na di ples dɛn we dɛn kɔl prɛri; ɔl dɛn tin ya, to Ismayl, tan lɛk we da bɔfa klos de shek to di kɔlt we de fred!

Pan ɔl we nɔbɔdi nɔ no usay di tin dɛn we nɔ gɛt nem de we di mistik sayn de gi dɛn kayn hint dɛn de; yet wit mi, lɛk di kɔlt, sɔmsay dɛn tin dɛn de fɔ de. Pan ɔl we insay bɔku pan in aspek dɛn, i tan lɛk se dɛn mek dis wɔl we wi de si wit lɔv, di sfɛra dɛn we wi nɔ de si, dɛn mek am wit fred.

Bɔt nɔto yet wi dɔn sɔlv di inkantashɔn fɔ dis waytnɛs, ɛn lan wetin mek i de apil wit da kayn pawa de to di sol; ɛn mɔ strenj ɛn fa fawe fɔ sho se i gɛt sɔntin fɔ du wit am—wetin mek, as wi dɔn si, na wan tɛm di tin we min di tin dɛn we gɛt fɔ du wit Gɔd biznɛs we min pas ɔl, nɔto di veil we di Kristian in Gɔd de mek; ɛn yet i fɔ bi lɛk aw i de, di tin we de mek tin dɛn we de mek mɔtalman fred pas ɔl.

Yu tink se bay we i nɔ de dɔn, i de shado di ples dɛn we nɔ gɛt at ɛn di big big tin dɛn we de na di wan ol wɔl, ɛn so i de chuk wi frɔm biɛn wit di tink bɔt fɔ dɔnawe wit am, we wi de si di wayt dip ples dɛn na di milky we? Ɔ na, dat as in essence waytnɛs nɔto so mɔ wan kɔlɔ as di visible absence of colour; ɛn di sem tɛm di kɔnkrit we gɛt ɔl di kɔlɔ dɛn; na fɔ dɛn rizin ya mek dis kayn mumu blank, we ful-ɔp wit minin, de na wan big land we gɛt sno—wan kɔlɔ we nɔ gɛt kɔlɔ, we gɛt ɔl di kɔlɔ we nɔ biliv se Gɔd de, we wi de shrink frɔm? Ɛn we wi tink bɔt da ɔda tiori de we di natura filɔsofa dɛn tɔk, se ɔl di ɔda kɔlɔ dɛn we de na di wɔl—ɛvri stet ɔ fayn fayn emblazoning—na di swit swit tin dɛn we di san de go dɔŋ skay ɛn wud dɛn; yes, ɛn di velvet dɛn we gɛt gold we bɔtaflay dɛn mek, ɛn di bɔtaflay chɛk dɛn we yɔŋ gyal pikin dɛn gɛt; ɔl dɛn tin ya na jɔs lay lay tin dɛn we nɔ klia, we nɔto rili tin dɛn we de insay tin dɛn, bɔt dɛn jɔs de pan am frɔm do; so dat ɔl di Nature we dɛn dɔn mek gɔd de peint absoliutli lɛk di raregal, we in allurements nɔ kɔba natin pas di charnel-haus insay; ɛn we wi go bifo, ɛn tink se di mistik kɔsmɛtik we de mek ɛni wan pan in kɔlɔ, di big prinsipul fɔ layt, fɔ ɔltɛm de kɔntinyu fɔ wayt ɔ nɔ gɛt kɔlɔ insɛf, ɛn if i de wok witout mɛdiɔm pan matirial, go tɔch ɔl di tin dɛn, ivin tulip ɛn ros, wit in yon blank tinj—we wi de tink bɔt ɔl dis, di yunivas we gɛt palsi de bifo wi we gɛt lɛprɔsi; ɛn lɛk pipul dɛn we de travul bay wilful na Lapland, we nɔ gri fɔ wɛr glas we gɛt kɔlɔ ɛn kɔlɔ na dɛn yay, na so di bad bad infidel de luk insɛf blaynd pan di big big wayt shroud we de rap ɔl di prɔspɛkt rawnd am. Ɛn pan ɔl dɛn tin ya, di Albino wɛl na bin di sayn. So una de wɔnda di faya ɔnt?
