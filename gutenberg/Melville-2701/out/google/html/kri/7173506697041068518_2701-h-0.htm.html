EKSTRƐKT DƐN. (Na wan Sab-Sab-Laybriyan de gi am).

Wi go si se dis jɔs pen-staking burrower ɛn grub-worm fɔ wan po dɛbul na wan Sab-Sub tan lɛk se i dɔn go tru di lɔng Vatikan ɛn strit-stɔl dɛn na di wɔl, pik ɛni random allusions to wɛl dɛn we i go ebul fɔ fɛn ɛni we insay ɛni buk we i bi, oli ɔ profane. So yu nɔ fɔ, in ɛvri kes at ɔl, tek di higgledy-piggledy wɛl stetmɛnt dɛn, ilɛksɛf dɛn ɔtɛnɛtik, insay dɛn ɛkstrakt ya, fɔ tru tru gospel sɛtɔlɔji. Far frɔm am. As i tɔch di wan dɛn we rayt di Baybul trade trade jɔs, ɛn bak di poet dɛn we de ya, dɛn pat ya na valyu ɔ ɛnjɔymɛnt nɔmɔ, as dɛn de gi yu wan glancing bird’s eye view fɔ wetin dɛn dɔn tɔk promiscuously, tink, fansi, ɛn siŋ bɔt Liviathan, bay bɔku neshɔn ɛn jɛnɛreshɔn dɛn, inklud wi yon.

So fare thee well, poor devil of a Sub-Sub, we a bi in kɔmɛntatɔ. Yu de pan da trayb we nɔ gɛt op ɛn we nɔ gɛt wan wayn na dis wɔl we nɔ go ɛva wam; ɛn fɔ udat ivin Pale Sherry go tu rosy-strong; bɔt udat pɔsin kin lɛk fɔ sidɔm wit sɔntɛnde, ɛn fil se i po-dɛbul, bak; ɛn gro wit kɔnvivial pan kray wata; ɛn tɛl dɛn klia wan, wit ful yay ɛn ɛmti glas, ɛn insay nɔt ɔltin we nɔ fayn fɔ sɔri—Give it up, Sub-Subs! Bikɔs di mɔ we una de fil pen fɔ mek di wɔl gladi, na di mɔ una go de tɛl tɛnki sote go! Would dat a kin klia ɔut Hampton Court ɛn di Tuileries fɔ una! Bɔt gulp dɔŋ una kray wata ɛn hie aloft to di royal-mast wit una at; bikɔs yu padi dɛn we dɔn go bifo de klin di ɛvin we gɛt sɛvin stori, ɛn mek Gebrɛl, Maykɛl, ɛn Rafaɛl we dɔn de pamper fɔ lɔng tɛm, rɛfyuji dɛn, agens yu kam. Na ya una de bit bɔt at dɛn we skata togɛda—na de, una go bit glas dɛn we nɔ go brok!
