MUTU 32. Cetology.

Ife tawululidwa kale molimba mtima pa kuya; koma posachedwapa tidzatayika mu kukula kwake kopanda malire, kopanda malire. Zisanachitike izo; Pamaso pa udzu wa Pequod umagudubuza mbali ndi mbali ndi ziboliboli za barnacles za Leviathan; poyambirira ndikwabwino kuyang'anira nkhani yomwe ili yofunikira kwambiri pakumvetsetsa koyamika kwa mavumbulutsidwe apadera a Leviathanic ndi zonena zamitundu yonse zomwe ziyenera kutsatira.

Ndi ena mwadongosolo chionetsero cha chinsomba mu genera wake yotakata, kuti ine tsopano kulephera kuika pamaso panu. Komabe si ntchito yophweka. Gulu la zigawo za chipwirikiti, palibe zochepa zomwe zafotokozedwa apa. Mvetserani zomwe akuluakulu aboma abwino komanso aposachedwa alemba.

"Palibe nthambi ya Zoology yomwe imakhudzidwa kwambiri ngati yomwe imatchedwa Cetology," akutero Captain Scoresby, AD 1820.

“Sichili cholinga changa, chikadakhala m’mphamvu yanga, kuloŵa m’kafukufuku wokhudza njira yeniyeni yogaŵira cetacea m’magulu ndi mabanja.” * * * Kusokonezeka kwakukulu kulipo pakati pa akatswiri a mbiri ya nyama imeneyi” ( sperm whale ), anatero Surgeon Beale, AD 1839.

"Kusayenerera kuchita kafukufuku wathu m'madzi osawerengeka." "Chophimba chosatheka kuphimba chidziwitso chathu cha cetacea." “Munda wodzala minga.” "Zizindikiro zonse zosakwanira izi koma zimatizunza ife akatswiri achilengedwe."

Motero amalankhula za namgumi, Cuvier wamkulu, ndi John Hunter, ndi Phunziro, zounikira izo za zoology ndi anatomy. Ngakhale zili choncho, ngakhale chidziwitso chenicheni ndi chochepa, komabe mabuku alipo ochuluka; ndipo kotero mu digiri yaying'ono, ndi cetology, kapena sayansi ya anamgumi. Ambiri ali amuna, aang’ono ndi aakulu, akale ndi atsopano, apamtunda ndi apanyanja, amene mokulira kapena pang’ono, olembedwa za namgumi. Thamangani angapo:—The Authors of the Bible; Aristotle; Pliny; Aldrovandi; Sir Thomas Browne; Gesner; Ray; Linnæus; Rondeletius; Willoughby; Wobiriwira; Artedi; Sibbald; Brisson; Marten; Lacépède; Bonneterre; Desmarest; Baron Cuvier; Frederick Cuvier; John Hunter; Owen; Scoresby; Beale; Bennett; J. Ross Browne; Mlembi wa Miriam Coffin; Olmstead; ndi Rev. T. Cheever. Koma zomwe zidalembedwa pamwambapa, zomwe tafotokozazi zikuwonetsa.

Pa mayina amene ali m’ndandanda wa olemba anamgumiwa, ndi okhawo amene amatsatira Owen amene anaonapo anamgumi amoyo; ndipo koma mmodzi wa iwo anali katswiri weniweni wa harpooneer ndi whaleman. Ndikutanthauza Captain Scoresby. Pankhani yosiyana ya Greenland kapena right-whale, iye ndiye ulamuliro wabwino kwambiri womwe ulipo. Koma Scoresby sankadziwa kalikonse ndipo sanena kanthu za chinsomba chachikulu cha sperm whale, poyerekeza ndi chomwe chinsomba cha Greenland chili pafupi kutchulidwa mosayenera. Ndipo apa zinenedwa, kuti Greenland whale ndi wolanda pampando wachifumu wa nyanja. Iye sali ngakhale m’njira iriyonse wamkulu wa anamgumi. Komabe, chifukwa cha nthawi yayitali ya zonena zake, ndi kusadziwa kwakukulu komwe, mpaka zaka makumi asanu ndi awiri zapitazo, adayikapo umuna wodabwitsa kwambiri kapena wosadziwika bwino, ndipo umbuli umene mpaka lero ukulamulirabe m'madera onse a sayansi ndi madoko a whale; kulanda uku kwachitika mwanjira iliyonse. Kutchula pafupifupi mawu onse a Leviathanic mu ndakatulo zazikulu zamasiku apitawa, adzakukhutiritsani kuti chinsomba cha Greenland, popanda mdani mmodzi, chinali kwa iwo mfumu ya m'nyanja. Koma tsopano yafika nthawi yolengeza uthenga watsopano. Ichi ndi Charing Cross; imvani inu! anthu abwino onse,—nangumi wa ku Greenland akuchotsedwa, — namgumi wamkulu wa umuna tsopano akulamulira!

Pali mabuku awiri okha omwe amayesa kuyika umuna wamoyo pamaso panu, ndipo panthawi imodzimodziyo, pamlingo wakutali amapambana pakuyesa. Mabuku amenewo ndi a Beale ndi a Bennett; onse mu nthawi yawo madokotala kwa English South-Sea whale-zombo, ndi amuna enieni ndi odalirika. Nkhani yapachiyambi yokhudza umuna namgumi kuti ipezeke m’mavoliyumu awo imakhala yaying’ono; koma mpaka pomwe ikupita, ndi yabwino kwambiri, ngakhale nthawi zambiri imangokhala kulongosola kwasayansi. Pakadali pano, chinsomba cha sperm whale, chasayansi kapena ndakatulo, chimakhala chosakwanira m'mabuku aliwonse. Kuposa anamgumi ena onse amene amasakidwa, moyo wake ndi wosalembedwa.

Tsopano mitundu yosiyanasiyana ya anamgumi ikufunika mtundu wina wa gulu lodziwika bwino lodziwika bwino, ngati ndandanda yosavuta yamasiku ano, yoti idzazidwe m'madipatimenti ake onse ndi antchito otsatira. Popeza palibe munthu wabwinoko amene angafune kutenga nkhaniyi m'manja, pano ndikupereka zoyesayesa zanga zosauka. Sindilonjeza kanthu kokwanira; chifukwa chinthu chilichonse chaumunthu chiyenera kukhala chokwanira, pachifukwa chimenecho chiyenera kukhala cholakwa mosalephera. Sindidzayerekeza kulongosola pang'ono kwamitundu yosiyanasiyana ya zamoyo, kapena - m'malo ano osachepera - kulongosola kulikonse. Cholinga changa apa ndikungopanga zolemba za systematization ya cetology. Ine ndine mmisiri wa zomangamanga, osati womanga.

Koma ndi ntchito yolemetsa; palibe cholembera makalata wamba mu Post-Ofesi chofanana nacho. Kufufuza pansi panyanja pambuyo pawo; kukhala ndi manja pakati pa maziko osaneneka, nthiti, ndi chiuno kwambiri cha dziko; Ichi ndi chinthu choopsa. Ndine chiyani kuti ndigwire mphuno ya Leviathan! Zotonza za Yobu zikhoza kundikhumudwitsa ine. Kodi apangana nawe pangano? Taonani, chiyembekezo cha iye chili chabe! Koma ndasambira m’mabuku osungira mabuku, ndipo ndadutsa panyanja; Ndakhala ndikuchita ndi anamgumi ndi manja owoneka awa; Ine ndiri mu mtima; ndipo ndidzayesa. Pali zina zoyambira kukonzekera.

Choyamba: Mkhalidwe wosatsimikizirika, wosakhazikika wa sayansi ya Cetology ili m’bwalo lomwe limatsimikiziridwa ndi mfundo yakuti, m’madera ena akadalibe mfundo yakuti ngati namgumi akhale nsomba. M’buku lake lakuti System of Nature, AD 1776, Linnæus anati, “Motere ndimalekanitsa anamgumi ndi nsomba.” Koma mwachidziŵitso changa, ndikudziwa kuti kufikira m’chaka cha 1850, nsomba za shaki ndi shad, alewives ndi hering’i, zotsutsana ndi lamulo lachindunji la Linnæus, zinapezedwabe zikugaŵanitsa kukhala ndi nyanja imodzi ndi Leviathan.

Zifukwa zimene Linnæus akafuna kuchita zikanathamangitsa anamgumiwo m’madzi, iye akunena motere: “Chifukwa cha mtima wawo wofunda, mapapo awo, zikope zawo zosunthika, makutu awo otsika, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem,” ndipo pomalizira pake, “ex lege naturæ jure meritoque.” Ndinapereka zonsezi kwa anzanga a Simeon Macey ndi Charley Coffin, a ku Nantucket, onse anzanga amene ndinali nawo paulendo wina wapamadzi, ndipo anagwirizana poganiza kuti zifukwa zimene zafotokozedwazo zinali zosakwanira. Charley ananena monyoza kuti iwo anali opusa.

Zidziwike kuti, posiya mkangano wonse, ine ndikutenga malo achikale abwino akuti chinsomba ndi nsomba, ndipo ndinamuyitana Yona woyera kuti andithandize. Chofunikira ichi chinakhazikika, mfundo yotsatira ndi yakuti, momwe namgumi amasiyana ndi nsomba zina zamkati. Pamwambapa, Linnæus wakupatsani zinthuzo. Koma mwachidule, ndi awa: mapapo ndi magazi otentha; pamene, nsomba zina zonse zilibe mapapo ndi magazi ozizira.

Kenako: Kodi tingafotokoze bwanji chinsomba, mwa mawonekedwe ake odziwika bwino, kuti titchule momveka bwino kuti nthawi zonse zikubwera? Kunena mwachidule, chinsomba ndi nsomba yotuluka mchira yokhala ndi mchira wopingasa . Ndi inu apo. Ngakhale atagwirizana, kutanthauzira kumeneko ndi zotsatira za kusinkhasinkha kowonjezereka. Walrus amatuluka ngati chinsomba, koma walrus si nsomba, chifukwa ndi amphibious. Koma nthawi yomaliza ya tanthauzoli imakhala yomveka bwino, monga yophatikizidwa ndi yoyamba. Pafupifupi aliyense ayenera kuti anazindikira kuti nsomba zonse zomwe anthu akumtunda amazidziwa zilibe mchira wophwanthira, koma woyima, kapena wokwera ndi pansi. Pamene, pakati pa nsomba zouluka mchira, ngakhale kuti ungakhale woumbidwa mofananamo, nthaŵi zonse umakhala wopingasa.

Mwa tanthawuzo lomwe lili pamwambapa la zomwe namgumi ali, sindimapatula ku ubale wa leviathanic chilichonse chodziwika ndi namgumi mpaka pano ndi Nantucketers odziwa bwino; kapenanso, kumbali ina, sichikugwirizana ndi nsomba iliyonse yomwe imadziwika kuti ndi yachilendo. Chifukwa chake, nsomba zonse zazing'ono, zotuluka, ndi zopingasa ziyenera kuphatikizidwa mu dongosolo la Cetology. Tsopano, ndiye, pakubwera magawano aakulu a gulu lonse la anamgumi.

*Ndikudziwa kuti mpaka pano, nsomba zomwe zimatchedwa Lamatins ndi Dugongs (Nsomba za nkhumba ndi Sow-nsomba za Coffins za Nantucket) zimaphatikizidwa ndi akatswiri ambiri a zachilengedwe pakati pa anamgumi. Koma monga momwe nkhumba-nsombazi zili phokoso, zonyansa, zomwe zimabisala m'kamwa mwa mitsinje, ndikudya udzu wonyowa, ndipo makamaka ngati sizimatuluka, ndimakana zizindikiro zawo monga anamgumi; ndipo awapatsa mapasipoti awo kuti asiye Ufumu wa Cetology.

Choyamba: Malingana ndi kukula kwake ndikugawa anamgumiwo kukhala MABUKU atatu oyambirira (ogawanika kukhala MITU), ndipo awa adzawamvetsetsa onse, ang'onoang'ono ndi aakulu.

I. NYAMULI WA FOLIO; II. OCTAVO WALE; III. DUODECIMO WALE.

Monga mtundu wa FOLIO ndikupereka Umuna Whale ; a OCTAVO, Grampus ; DUODECIMO, Porpoise .

FOLIOS. Pamitu imeneyi ndikuphatikizapo mitu yotsatirayi:—I. Nangumi wa Umuna ; II. Chinsomba Choyenera ; III. chinsomba cha Fin-Back ; IV. nangumi wochirikiza Hump ; V. Razor Back Whale ; VI. nangumi wa Sulfur Under Whale .

BUKU I. ( Folio ), MUTU 1. ( Sperm Whale ).— Nangumi ameneyu, pakati pa Chingelezi chakale chodziŵika mosadziwika bwino monga namgumi wotchedwa Trumpa whale, ndi Physeter whale, ndi Anvil Headed whale, ndi Cachalot wamakono wa French, ndi Pottsfich of the Germans, ndi Macrocephalus of the Long Words. Iye, mosakayikira, ndiye wokhalamo wamkulu kwambiri padziko lapansi; zowopsya kwambiri mwa zida zonse zomwe mungakumane nazo; mawonekedwe apamwamba kwambiri; ndipo potsirizira pake, pazamalonda zamtengo wapatali kwambiri; iye pokhala cholengedwa chokhacho chomwe chinthu chamtengo wapatalicho, spermaceti, chimachokera. Zodabwitsa zake zonse, m'malo ena ambiri, zidzakulitsidwa. Ndiyenera kuchita ndi dzina lake makamaka. Philologically amaganiziridwa, ndizosamveka. Zaka mazana angapo zapitazo, pamene Nangumi wa Umuna anali wosadziŵika kotheratu mwa umunthu wake woyenerera, ndi pamene mafuta ake anangopezedwa mwangozi kuchokera ku nsomba yotsekeredwa; m’masiku amenewo spermaceti, zingaonekere, mofala ankayenera kuti anachokera ku cholengedwa chofanana ndi chimene panthaŵiyo chinkatchedwa ku England monga Greenland kapena Right Whale. Linalinso lingaliro, kuti spermaceti yemweyo anali nthabwala yofulumizitsa ya Greenland Whale yomwe syllable yoyamba ya liwu limafotokoza kwenikweni. Munthawi imeneyo, komanso, spermaceti inali yosowa kwambiri, osagwiritsidwa ntchito ngati kuwala, koma ngati mafuta odzola ndi mankhwala. Zinangoyenera kuperekedwa kwa ogulitsa mankhwala monga masiku ano mukugula rhubarb imodzi. Pamene, monga momwe ndimaonera, m'kupita kwa nthawi, chikhalidwe chenicheni cha spermaceti chinadziwika, dzina lake loyambirira lidasungidwabe ndi ogulitsa; mosakayikira kukulitsa mtengo wake ndi lingaliro lofunikira modabwitsa la kusowa kwake. Ndipo kotero kuti dzinali liyenera kuti linaperekedwa kwa chinsomba chomwe spermaceti iyi idachokeradi.

BUKU I. ( Folio ), MUTU II. ( Right Whale ).—M’mbali ina iyi ndiyo yolemekezeka koposa ya namwali, pokhala imene imasakidwa mokhazikika ndi munthu. Imapereka nkhani yomwe imadziwika kuti whalebone kapena baleen; ndi mafuta otchedwa “mafuta a whale,” omwe ndi otsika kwambiri pazamalonda. Pakati pa asodzi, iye amasankhidwa mosasankha ndi maudindo otsatirawa: Whale; chinsomba cha Greenland; Black Whale; Whale Wamkulu; Nangumi Woona; Chinsomba Choyenera. Pali chinsinsi chokhudza kudziwika kwa mitundu yomwe imabatizidwa mochuluka. Nanga chinsomba ndi chiyani, chomwe ndimaphatikiza mumtundu wachiwiri wa Folios wanga? Ndi Great Mysticetus of the English naturalists; Nangumi waku Greenland wa namgumi achingelezi; Baleine Ordinaire wa French whalemen; ndi Growlands Walfish of the Sweden. Ndiwo namgumi amene kwa zaka zoposa mazana aŵiri zapitazo anasakidwa ndi Dutch ndi English m’nyanja za Arctic; ndi anamgumi amene asodzi a ku America akhala akulondola kwanthaŵi yaitali m’nyanja ya Indian, ku Brazil Banks, ku Nor’ West Coast, ndi madera ena osiyanasiyana a dziko lapansi, osankhidwa ndi iwo kuti Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Ena amayerekezera kuti akuwona kusiyana pakati pa namgumi wa Greenland wa ku England ndi namgumi wakumanja wa Amereka. Koma amavomereza ndendende m’mbali zawo zonse zazikulu; komanso sipanapereke umboni umodzi wotsimikizika womwe ungapangitse kusiyana kwakukulu. Ndi mwa kugawikana kosalekeza kozikidwa pa kusiyana kosadziwika bwino, m’pamene madipatimenti ena a mbiri ya chilengedwe amakhala ovuta kwambiri. Nangumi woyenerera adzapatsidwa chithandizo kwinakwake motalika, ponena za kufotokoza za sperm whale.

BUKU I. ( Folio ), MUTU III. ( Fin-Back ).— Pansi pa mutu umenewu ndimalingalira chilombo chimene, mwa mayina osiyanasiyana a Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, ndi Long-John, chawonedwa pafupifupi m’nyanja iliyonse ndipo kaŵirikaŵiri ndi namgumi amene ndege yake yakutali imanenedwa kaŵirikaŵiri ndi apaulendo awoloka Atlantic, mu New York packet tracks. M'litali mwake amapeza, ndipo mu baleen wake, Fin-back amafanana ndi chinsomba choyenera, koma ndi chaching'ono chocheperako, ndi mtundu wopepuka, akuyandikira ku azitona. Milomo yake yayikulu imapereka mawonekedwe ngati chingwe, opangidwa ndi zopindika, zopindika za makwinya akulu. Mbali yake yayikulu yosiyanitsa, chipsepse, chomwe amatengera dzina lake, nthawi zambiri chimakhala chinthu chowonekera. Zipsepsezi zimakhala zazitali pafupifupi mapazi atatu kapena anayi, zimakula molunjika kuchokera kuseri kwa kumbuyo, zooneka ngati makona, komanso zosongoka kwambiri. Ngakhale kuti mbali ina ya nyamayo sioneka, chipsepse chakutalichi nthawi zina chimaoneka moonekeratu chikutuluka pamwamba. Nyanja ikakhala yabata pang'ono, komanso yodziwika pang'ono ndi mafunde ozungulira, ndipo chipsepse chonga ngati gnomon ichi chikuyimilira ndikuyika mithunzi pamalo okhwinyata, titha kuganiza kuti madzi ozungulira ozungulira amafanana ndi dial, ndi kalembedwe kake ndi mizere yozungulira ya ola lojambulidwapo. Pankhani ya Ahazi imeneyo mthunzi umabwerera m’mbuyo. The Fin-Back simasewera. Iye amawoneka wodana ndi anamgumi, monga amuna ena amadana ndi anthu. Wamanyazi kwambiri; nthawi zonse amakhala yekha; kukwera pamwamba mosayembekezereka m'madzi akutali kwambiri ndi owawa kwambiri; ndege yake yowongoka ndi imodzi yokwezeka yokwera ngati mkondo wamtali wonyansa kwambiri pa chigwa chouma; wopatsidwa mphamvu yodabwitsa yoteroyo ndi liwiro la kusambira, monga kupeputsa zonse zimene munthu ali nazo; Leviatani uyu akuwoneka Kaini wothamangitsidwa ndi wosagonjetseka wa fuko lake, atanyamula chizindikiro chake kalembedwe kameneko pa nsana wake. Kuchokera kukhala ndi baleen m'kamwa mwake, Fin-Back nthawi zina amaphatikizidwa ndi chinsomba cholondola, pakati pa mitundu yodziwika bwino yotchedwa Whalebone whales , ndiko kuti, anamgumi okhala ndi baleen. Mwa izi zotchedwa Whalebone whales, zikuwoneka kuti pali mitundu ingapo, yomwe yambiri, komabe, sadziwika. Anangumi amphuno yotakata ndi anamgumi amilomo; anamgumi amutu wa pike; anamgumi agulu; anamgumi omwe ali pansi pa nsagwada ndi anangumi ozungulira, ndi mayina a asodzi a mitundu ingapo.

Pokhudzana ndi kudandaula kwa "whalebone whales," ndizofunika kwambiri kutchula, kuti ngakhale dzina loterolo likhoza kukhala losavuta kuthandizira kufotokozera zamtundu wina wa nsomba, komabe n'kopanda pake kuyesa gulu lodziwika bwino la Leviathan, lomwe linakhazikitsidwa pa baleen, kapena hump, kapena fin, kapena mano; ngakhale kuti mbali zolembedwazo zimawoneka bwino kuti zigwirizane ndi maziko a dongosolo la Cetology kusiyana ndi kusiyana kwina kulikonse kwa thupi, komwe namgumi, mwa mitundu yake, amapereka. Nanga bwanji? Nsalu, hump, back-fin, ndi mano; Izi ndi zinthu zomwe mawonekedwe ake amamwazikana mopanda tsankho pakati pa mitundu yonse ya anamgumi, mosasamala kanthu za zomwe zingakhale mawonekedwe awo pazinthu zina komanso zofunika kwambiri. Chotero, sperm whale ndi humpbacked whale, aliyense ali ndi hump; koma pamenepo fanizo lileka. Ndiye, namgumi yemweyu wa humpbacked whale ndi Greenland whale, aliyense wa awa ali ndi baleen; koma pameneponso fanizo lileka. Ndipo ndi chimodzimodzi ndi mbali zina zomwe tazitchulazi. M'mitundu yosiyanasiyana ya anamgumi, amapanga mikangano yosakhazikika; kapena, ngati aliyense wa iwo adzipatula, kudzipatula kotere; monga kunyoza njira zonse zokhazikitsidwa pamaziko otere. Pamwala uwu, akatswiri onse odziwa zachilengedwe anagawanika.

Koma zikhoza kuganiziridwa kuti, m'zigawo zamkati za whale, mu thupi lake - kumeneko, titha kugunda gulu loyenera. Ayi; mwachitsanzo, ndi chiyani chomwe chili mu thupi la namgumi wa Greenland chodabwitsa kwambiri kuposa baleen wake? Komabe tawona kuti ndi baleen wake ndizosatheka kuyika bwino chinsomba cha Greenland. Ndipo ngati mutsikira m'matumbo a leviathan osiyanasiyana, bwanji kumeneko simudzapeza kusiyanitsa gawo la makumi asanu lomwe likupezeka kwa systematizer monga akunja aja adalembedwa kale. Nanga chatsala chiyani? palibe kanthu koma kugwira anamgumi mwathupi, mu voliyumu yawo yonse yowolowa manja, ndi kuwasandutsa molimba mtima mwanjira imeneyo. Ndipo iyi ndi kachitidwe ka mabuku kamene kamatengedwa pano; ndipo ndi imodzi yokha yomwe ingapambane, chifukwa yokha ndi yotheka. Kuti tipitirize.

BUKU I. ( Folio ) MUTU IV. ( Hump Back ).— Nangumi ameneyu amaoneka kaŵirikaŵiri kugombe la kumpoto kwa America. Nthawi zambiri amagwidwa kumeneko, ndipo amakokeredwa ku doko. Ali ndi paketi yayikulu pa iye ngati wogulitsa; kapena mungamutche Nyangumi wa Njovu ndi Castle. Mulimonse mmene zingakhalire, dzina lotchuka la iye silimusiyanitsa mokwanira, popeza kuti namgumi wa sperm whale nayenso ali ndi hump ngakhale kakang’ono. Mafuta ake sali amtengo wapatali. Ali ndi baleen. Iye ndiye wochititsa chidwi kwambiri komanso wopepuka kwambiri pa anamgumi onse, akupanga thovu lachiwerewere komanso madzi oyera nthawi zambiri kuposa ena aliwonse.

BUKU I. ( Folio ), MUTU V. ( Razor Back ).— Za namgumi ameneyu zimadziwika pang’ono koma dzina lake. Ndamuwona patali ndi Cape Horn. Mwa chikhalidwe chopuma, iye amazemba alenje ndi afilosofi. Ngakhale kuti sanali wamantha, sanasonyezepo mbali ina iliyonse ya iye koma nsana wake, womwe umatuluka m’phiri lalitali lakuthwa. Msiyeni apite. Sindikudziwa zambiri za iye, ngakhalenso wina aliyense.

BUKU I. ( Folio ), MUTU VI. ( Sulfur Bottom ).—Bambo wina wopuma pantchito, yemwe anali ndi mimba ya sulufule, mosakayikira anakolopa m’mataipi a Chitartaria m’mabwalo ake ena ake ozama m’madzi. Iye samawoneka kawirikawiri; osachepera sindinamuwonepo kupatula kunyanja zakutali zakummwera, ndiyeno nthawi zonse patali kwambiri kuti ndiphunzire nkhope yake. Sathamangitsidwa; ankathawa ndi zingwe zoyendamo. Prodigies amauzidwa za iye. Adieu, Sulphur Pansi! Sindinganenenso zomwe zili zoona kwa inu, komanso Nantucketer wakale kwambiri.

Apa akutha BUKU I. ( Folio ), ndipo tsopano akuyamba BUKU II. ( Octavo ).

OCTAVOES.*—Amenewa amakumbatira anamgumi a ukulu wapakati, amene pakati pawo angakhalepo:—I., Grampus ; II., Nsomba Zakuda ; III., Narwhale ; IV., Thrasher ; V., Wakupha .

*Chifukwa chiyani buku ili la anamgumi silinatchulidwe kuti Quarto ndi lomveka bwino. Chifukwa, ngakhale anamgumi a dongosolo ili, ngakhale ang'onoang'ono kuposa a dongosolo lakale, komabe amakhalabe ndi mawonekedwe ofanana nawo pachithunzi, komabe buku la Quarto la omanga mabuku mu mawonekedwe ake silimasunga mawonekedwe a voliyumu ya Folio, koma voliyumu ya Octavo imatero.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), MUTU 1. ( Grampus ).—Ngakhale kuti nsomba imeneyi, imene kupuma kwake mokweza, kapena m’malo mwake kuwomba, kwapereka mwambi kwa anthu akumtunda, imadziŵika bwino kwambiri kuti ili m’malo akuya, komabe siinatchulidwe m’gulu la anamgumi. Koma pokhala ndi mbali zonse zazikulu zapadera za leviathan, akatswiri ambiri a zachilengedwe amamuzindikira chifukwa chimodzi. Iye ndi wamtali wa octavo, wosiyana kuchokera pa mapazi khumi ndi asanu mpaka makumi awiri ndi asanu m'litali, ndi miyeso yofanana kuzungulira m'chiuno. Amasambira m'magulu; sasakidwa nthawi zonse, ngakhale kuti mafuta ake ndi ochuluka, komanso abwino kwambiri pakuwala. Ndi asodzi ena njira yake imawonedwa ngati chisonyezero cha kupita patsogolo kwa chinsomba chachikulu cha sperm whale.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), MUTU Wachiwiri. ( Nsomba Zakuda ).—Ndimapereka mayina a asodzi otchuka a nsomba zonsezi, pakuti nthaŵi zambiri ndizo zabwino koposa. Pomwe dzina lililonse limakhala losamveka bwino kapena losavuta kutchula, nditero, ndikuwonetsa lina. Ndimachita izi tsopano, ndikukhudza Nsomba Zakuda, zomwe zimatchedwa, chifukwa chakuda ndi lamulo pakati pa pafupifupi anamgumi onse. Ndiye mutchuleni Nangumi wa Fisi ngati mukufuna. Voracity yake imadziwika bwino, ndipo kuyambira pomwe milomo yake imakhala yokhotakhota m'mwamba, amanyamula nkhope yake yosatha ya Mephistophelean. Nangumiyu amatalika pafupifupi mapazi khumi ndi asanu ndi limodzi kapena khumi ndi asanu ndi atatu. Amapezeka pafupifupi m'madera onse. Ali ndi njira yachilendo yowonetsera zipsepse zake zakumbuyo zokokera posambira, zomwe zimawoneka ngati mphuno yachiroma. Akapanda kugwira ntchito yopeza phindu, osaka ma sperm whale nthawi zina amagwira nangumi wa Fisi, kuti azipeza mafuta otsika mtengo pantchito zapakhomo —monga momwe osamalira nyumba osamala, popanda kampani, komanso okha okha, amawotcha phula losakoma m’malo mwa sera wonunkha. Ngakhale kuti buluu wawo ndi woonda kwambiri, ena mwa anamgumiwa amakupatsirani mafuta opitirira magaloni makumi atatu.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), MUTU III. ( Narwhale ), ndiko kuti, Nangumi wa M’mphuno . Cholengedwacho chimakhala ndi utali wa mapazi khumi ndi asanu ndi limodzi, pamene nyanga yake imatalika mamita asanu, ngakhale ena amapitirira khumi, ndipo amafika mamita khumi ndi asanu. Kunena zowona, nyanga imeneyi ndi nyanga yotalika, yotuluka kuchokera kunsagwada mumzere wopsinjika pang'ono kuchokera kumtunda. Koma zimangopezeka kumbali yoyipa, yomwe ili ndi zotsatira zoyipa, zomwe zimapatsa mwiniwake chinthu chofananira ndi gawo la munthu wamanzere wopusa. Kodi nyanga ya njovu kapena mikondo imeneyi imayankha kuti cholinga chake n'chiyani, zingakhale zovuta kunena. Sichikuwoneka kuti chikugwiritsidwa ntchito ngati tsamba la nsomba ya lupanga ndi nsomba za bilu; ngakhale amalinyero ena amandiuza kuti Narwhale amawagwiritsa ntchito ngati chowotcha potembenuza pansi panyanja kuti apeze chakudya. Charley Coffin adati idagwiritsidwa ntchito poboola ayezi; pakuti Narwhale, ikukwera pamwamba pa Nyanja ya Polar, ndikupeza kuti ili ndi madzi oundana, inakweza nyanga yake mmwamba, ndipo motero inathyola. Koma simungatsimikize kuti imodzi mwa mfundozi ndi yolondola. Lingaliro langa ndiloti, ngakhale nyanga ya mbali imodziyi ingagwiritsidwe ntchito ndi Narwhale-ngakhale zingakhale choncho-zingakhale zophweka kwa iye kupeza chikwatu chowerengera timapepala. Nangumi amene ndimamumva akutchedwa Nangumi wa Tusked, Nangumi wa Horned, ndi Nangumi wa Unicorn. Iye ndi chitsanzo chodziwika bwino cha Unicornism chomwe chimapezeka pafupifupi mu ufumu uliwonse wa chilengedwe chamoyo. Kuchokera kwa olemba ena akale odziwika bwino ndapeza kuti nyanga ya unicorn ya m'nyanja yomweyi inali m'masiku akale omwe amawonedwa ngati mankhwala othana ndi chiphe, ndipo chifukwa chake, kukonzekera kwake kunabweretsa mitengo yayikulu. Ankathiridwanso ku mchere wosasinthasintha wa madona omwe akukomoka, monga momwe nyanga za gwape amapangidwa kukhala hartshorn. Poyambirira izo mwazokha zinkawerengedwa ngati chinthu chochititsa chidwi kwambiri. Black Letter imandiuza kuti Sir Martin Frobisher pobwerera kuchokera ku ulendo umenewo, pamene Mfumukazi Bess anagwedeza molimba mtima dzanja lake la miyala yamtengo wapatali kwa iye kuchokera pawindo la Greenwich Palace, pamene ngalawa yake yolimba mtima inkayenda pamtsinje wa Thames; “Pamene Sir Martin anabwerera kuchokera ku ulendo umenewo,” akutero Black Letter, “atagwada mawondo anapereka kwa mkulu wake nyanga ya Narwhale, imene kwa nthawi yaitali inapachikidwa m’nyumba yachifumu ya ku Windsor.” Wolemba waku Ireland amatsutsa kuti Earl wa Leicester, atagwada, adaperekanso kwa mkulu wake nyanga ina, yokhudzana ndi chilombo chamtundu wa unicorn.

Narwhale ili ndi mawonekedwe okongola kwambiri, ngati nyalugwe, okhala ndi mtundu wa nthaka yoyera ngati mkaka, wokhala ndi mawanga ozungulira ndi oblong akuda. Mafuta ake ndi apamwamba kwambiri, omveka bwino komanso abwino; koma ndi pang’ono, ndipo sasakasaka. Amapezeka makamaka m'nyanja zozungulira.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), MUTU IV. ( Killer ).— Za namgumiyu pang’ono amadziŵika ndendende ndi Nantucketer, ndipo palibe nkomwe kwa wodzinenera kuti ndi wachilengedwe. Kuchokera pa zomwe ndamuwona patali, ndiyenera kunena kuti anali za kukula kwa grampu. Iye ndi wankhanza kwambiri—mtundu wa nsomba za Feegee. Nthawi zina amatenga anangumi akulu akulu a Folio pa milomo, napachikidwa pamenepo ngati chimbalangondo, mpaka chinyama champhamvu chikuda nkhawa cha kufa. Wakupha sasakidwa. Sindinamvepo kuti ali ndi mafuta otani. Kupatulapo kungatengedwe ku dzina lomwe linaperekedwa pa chinsomba ichi, chifukwa cha kusazindikira kwake. Pakuti ife tonse ndife akupha, pamtunda ndi panyanja; Bonapartes ndi Shark akuphatikizidwa.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAPITALA V. ( Thrasher ).— Njonda imeneyi ndi yotchuka chifukwa cha mchira wake, umene amaugwiritsira ntchito pomenya adani ake. Amakwera pamsana wa namgumi wa Folio, ndipo pamene akusambira, akugwira ntchito yake mwa kumkwapula; monga aphunzitsi ena akusukulu amapezana m'dziko mwa njira yofanana. Komabe zochepa zomwe zimadziwika za Thrasher kuposa za Killer. Onse ndi ophwanya malamulo, ngakhale m'nyanja zopanda malamulo.

Izi zikutha BUKU II. ( Octavo ), ndipo akuyamba BUKU III. ( Duodecimo ).

DUODECIMOES.—Izi zikuphatikizapo anamgumi ang’onoang’ono. I. The Huzza Porpoise. II. Nyama ya Algeria. III. Nyama ya Nanguluwe ya Mealy-mouthed.

Kwa iwo omwe sanaphunzirepo mwamwayi phunziroli, zingawoneke zachilendo, kuti nsomba zosapitirira mamita anayi kapena asanu ziyenera kuphatikizidwa pakati pa WHALES - liwu, lomwe, m'lingaliro lodziwika, nthawi zonse limapereka lingaliro la kukula kwake. Koma zolengedwa zomwe zili pamwambapo monga ma Duodecimoes ndi anamgumi osalephera, malinga ndi tanthauzo langa la chinsomba - mwachitsanzo, nsomba yotuluka, yokhala ndi mchira wopingasa.

BUKU III. ( Duodecimo ), MUTU 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).— Ili ndilo kalulu wamba wopezeka pafupifupi padziko lonse lapansi. Dzinalo ndi la zopereka zanga; pakuti pali akalulu ambiri, ndipo payenera kuchitidwa kanthu kuwasiyanitsa; Ndimamutcha motero, chifukwa nthawi zonse amasambira m'mabwalo osangalatsa, omwe panyanja yotakata amangodziponyera okha kumwamba ngati zisoti pagulu la anthu lachinayi la Julayi. Maonekedwe awo kaŵirikaŵiri amayamikiridwa ndi woyendetsa sitimayo. Zodzaza ndi mizimu yabwino, nthawi zonse zimachokera ku mphepo yamkuntho kupita kumphepo yamkuntho. Ndiwo anyamata omwe amakhala nthawi zonse pamaso pa mphepo. Amawerengedwa ngati mwayi. Ngati inu nokha mungathe kupirira kukondwera katatu pakuwona nsomba zowopsya izi, kumwamba kukuthandizani; mzimu wachikondwerero chaumulungu mulibe mwa inu. Huzza Porpoise wodyetsedwa bwino adzakupatsirani galoni imodzi yamafuta abwino. Koma madzi abwino komanso osalimba otengedwa m’nsagwada zake ndi ofunika kwambiri. Ndi pempho pakati pa miyala yamtengo wapatali ndi mawotchi. Amalinyero amachiyika pamanyowa awo. Nyama ya nkhumba imadya bwino, mukudziwa. N’kutheka kuti simunayambe mwaganizapo kuti kalulu amawombera. Zowonadi, chopondera chake ndi chaching'ono kwambiri kotero kuti sichidziwika bwino. Koma nthawi ina mukapeza mwayi, muzimuyang'ana; ndipo pamenepo mudzawona chinsomba chachikulu cha Sperm mwiniyo pang'ono.

BUKU III. ( Duodecimo ), MUTU Wachiwiri. ( Algerine Porpoise ).— Wachifwamba. Zoyipa kwambiri. Amangopezeka, ndikuganiza, ku Pacific. Iye ndi wamkulu pang'ono kuposa Huzza Porpoise, koma ambiri omwewo amapanga. Muumirize, ndipo adzamanga shaki. Ndamutsitsa nthawi zambiri, koma sindinamuone atagwidwa.

BUKU III. ( Duodecimo ), MUTU III. ( Nyama ya Nambala ya Mealy-mouthed ).— Mtundu waukulu kwambiri wa Nanguluwe; ndipo amapezeka ku Pacific kokha, monga momwe amadziwika. Dzina lokhalo lachingerezi, lomwe adadziwika nalo mpaka pano, ndi la asodzi-Right-Whale Porpoise, chifukwa chakuti amapezeka kwambiri pafupi ndi Folioyo. M'mawonekedwe ake, amasiyana pang'ono ndi Huzza Porpoise, pokhala wozungulira komanso wosangalatsa; ndithu, iye ndi wowoneka bwino komanso wofanana ndi njonda. Alibe zipsepse pamsana pake (akalulu ena ambiri ali nawo), ali ndi mchira wokondeka, ndi maso achimwenye achifundo a mtundu wa hazel. Koma m'kamwa mwake ufa uwononga zonse. Ngakhale kuti msana wake wonse mpaka m'mbali mwa zipsepse zake ndi wozama kwambiri, komabe mzere wamalire, wodziwika bwino ngati chizindikiro mu chombo cha ngalawa, chotchedwa "chiuno chowala," mzerewo umamusuntha kuchokera ku tsinde kupita kumbuyo, ndi mitundu iwiri yosiyana, yakuda pamwamba ndi yoyera pansi. Choyeracho chimakhala ndi mbali ya mutu wake, ndi mkamwa mwake wonse, zomwe zimamupangitsa kuti aziwoneka ngati wathawa ulendo woipa wopita ku thumba la chakudya. Mtundu wosavuta komanso wosavuta! Mafuta ake amafanana kwambiri ndi akalulu wamba.

Kupitilira DUODECIMO, dongosololi silimapitilira, popeza Porpoise ndiye ang'ono kwambiri amnsomba. Pamwambapa, muli ndi ma Leviatans onse odziwika. Koma pali zinsomba zosatsimikizika, zothawathawa, zowoneka bwino kwambiri, zomwe, monga American whaleman, ndikudziwa ndi mbiri, koma osati ndekha. Ndidzawawerenga m'matchulidwe awo achitetezo; chifukwa mwina mndandanda wotere ukhoza kukhala wofunikira kwa ofufuza amtsogolo, omwe atha kumaliza zomwe ndili nazo pano koma ayamba. Ngati anamgumi otsatirawa, pambuyo pake adzagwidwa ndi kuikidwa chizindikiro, ndiye kuti akhoza kuphatikizidwa mosavuta mu Dongosololi, malinga ndi kukula kwake kwa Folio, Octavo, kapena Duodecimo:—The Bottle-Nose Whale; chinsomba cha Junk Whale; Nangumi Wamutu Wa Pudding; chinsomba cha Cape; Nangumi Wotsogolera; Cannon Whale; chinsomba cha Scragg; Nangumi Wamkuwa; Nangumi wa Njovu; Nangumi wa Iceberg; Quog Whale; Blue Whale; ndi zina zotero. Kuchokera ku maulamuliro achi Icelandic, Chidatchi, ndi Chingelezi chakale, pangakhale ndandanda ina ya anamgumi osadziwika bwino, odalitsidwa ndi mayina amtundu uliwonse. Koma ndiwasiya monga zachikale; ndipo sangachitire mwina kukayikira iwo ndi mawu chabe, odzaza ndi Leviathaniism, koma osatanthauza kanthu.

Pomaliza: Zinanenedwa poyambirira, kuti dongosolo lino silidzakhala pano, ndipo nthawi yomweyo, lidzakhala langwiro. Simungathe kuona kuti ndasunga mawu anga. Koma tsopano ndikusiya cetological System yanga itayimilira osamalizidwa, ngakhale tchalitchi chachikulu cha Cologne chidasiyidwa, crane ikuyimabe pamwamba pa nsanja yosamalizidwa. Kwa erections ang'onoang'ono akhoza kumalizidwa ndi omanga awo oyambirira; olemekezeka, owona, amasiya mwala wamtengo wapatali ku mbadwa. Mulungu andisunge kuti ndisamalizitse kalikonse. Bukhu lonseli ndi losavuta - ayi, koma ndondomeko ya ndondomeko. O, Nthawi, Mphamvu, Ndalama, ndi Kuleza Mtima!
