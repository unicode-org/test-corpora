MUTU 92. Ambergris.

Tsopano ambergris ndi chinthu chodabwitsa kwambiri, komanso chofunikira kwambiri ngati nkhani yazamalonda, kotero kuti mu 1791 Kaputeni wina wobadwa ku Nantucket Coffin adayesedwa pa bala la English House of Commons pankhaniyi. Pakuti panthawiyo, ndipo mpaka tsiku lakumapeto, chiyambi chenicheni cha ambergris chinakhalabe, monga amber mwiniwake, vuto kwa ophunzira. Ngakhale kuti mawu akuti ambergris ndi opangidwa ndi Chifalansa oti amber imvi, komabe zinthu ziwirizi ndizosiyana kwambiri. Amber, ngakhale kuti nthawi zina amapezeka m'mphepete mwa nyanja, amakumbidwanso m'madera akutali, pamene ambergris sapezeka m'mphepete mwa nyanja. Kupatula apo, amber ndi chinthu cholimba, chowonekera, chosasunthika, chosanunkhiza, chomwe chimagwiritsidwa ntchito popanga pakamwa ndi mapaipi, kupanga mikanda ndi zokongoletsera; koma ambergris ndi ofewa, phula, ndi onunkhira kwambiri ndi zokometsera, kotero kuti amagwiritsidwa ntchito kwambiri mu zonunkhira, mu pastiles, makandulo amtengo wapatali, tsitsi-ufa, ndi pomatum. Anthu a ku Turkey amaugwiritsa ntchito pophika, komanso amapita nawo ku Mecca, n’cholinga chofanana ndi chakuti lubani atengere ku St. Peter’s ku Rome. Amalonda ena avinyo amaponya njere zingapo mu claret, kuti ziwonjezeke.

Ndiye ndani angaganize kuti madona ndi njonda zabwino zotere ziyenera kudzikongoletsa ndi zinthu zomwe zimapezeka m'matumbo olemetsa a nangumi wodwala! Komabe zili choncho. Ndi ena, ambergris akuyenera kukhala chifukwa, ndi ena zotsatira za dyspepsia mu whale. Momwe mungachiritsire dyspepsia yotereyi zinali zovuta kunena, pokhapokha popereka mabwato atatu kapena anayi a mapiritsi a Brandreth, ndiyeno akuthawa, monga momwe ogwira ntchito amachitira pophulika miyala.

Ndayiwala kunena kuti munapezeka mu ambergris, mbale zina zolimba, zozungulira, za mafupa, zomwe poyamba Stubb ankaganiza kuti zingakhale mabatani a thalauza amalinyero; koma pambuyo pake zinapezeka kuti iwo anali chabe zidutswa za mafupa ang'onoang'ono a nyamakazi oumitsidwa mwanjira imeneyo.

Tsopano kuti kusavunda kwa ambergris onunkhira kwambiri akuyenera kupezeka mu mtima wa kuvunda kotero; kodi ichi sichinthu? Ine opine, kuti ndi momveka traceable kufika koyamba kwa Greenland whaling zombo ku London, zoposa zaka ziwiri zapitazo. Chifukwa chakuti asodzi amenewo sanatero, ndipo satero tsopano, kuyesa mafuta awo panyanja monga zombo za Kummwera zachitira nthawizonse; koma duleni tinthu tating'onoting'ono tonunkhira tomwe timatulutsa timadzi tating'ono ting'ono, tiwaponye m'mabowo a mitsuko ikuluikulu, ndi kupita nawo kunyumba motere; kufupika kwa nyengo m’Nyanja Youndana imeneyo, ndi mikuntho yadzidzidzi ndi yamphamvu imene imaonekerako, kuletsa njira ina iriyonse. Ndipo pakutsikira kwachiwiri, ngalawayo inapalasa namgumi; ndipo pamene nsombayo inalandira chitsulo chachitsulo, inapereka rap yake yachizolowezi, yomwe inachitika, panthawiyi, kukhala pansi pa mpando wosauka wa Pip. Kudzidzimuka kodziwikiratu kwa nthawiyo kunamupangitsa kuti adumphe, kupalasa m'manja, kutuluka m'ngalawamo; ndipo m’njira yotere, mbali ina ya chinsomba choterecho ikubwera pa chifuwa chake, anainyamula m’madzi ndi iye, kuti akoledwe nayo, pamene potsirizira pake agwera m’madzi. Nthawi yomweyo chinsomba chokanthidwacho chinayamba kuthamanga kwambiri, mzerewo unawongoka; ndi presto! Pip wosauka adabwera akutuluka thovu mpaka kumabwato, akukokedwa mopanda chisoni ndi mzere, womwe unasintha kangapo pachifuwa ndi khosi.

Tashtego anaima mu mauta. Anali wodzaza ndi moto wakusaka. Iye ankadana Pip chifukwa cha poltroon. Atalanda mpeni wa ngalawayo m’chimake, anaimitsa nsonga yake yakuthwa pamwamba pa mzerewo, n’kutembenukira ku Stubb, ndipo anafuula mofunsa mafunso, “Dulani?” Panthawiyi, nkhope ya Pip yabuluu, yotsamwitsidwa idawoneka bwino, Chitani, chifukwa cha Mulungu! Zonse zidadutsa mwaphazi. Pasanathe theka la miniti, zonsezi zinachitika.

“Mwamwayi, cheka!” anabangula Stubb; ndipo kotero chinsombacho chinatayika ndipo Pip anapulumutsidwa.

Choncho atangochira, munthu wakuda wosaukayo anamenyedwa ndi mapokoso ndi kunyozedwa ndi ogwira ntchito. Mwabata kulola matemberero osakhazikikawa kuti asungunuke, Stubb ndiye m'chigwa, ngati bizinesi, koma moseketsa, adatemberera Pip mwalamulo; ndipo atachita izi, adamupatsa upangiri wabwino kwambiri. Zomwe zidalipo, Osadumphira m'boti, Pip, kupatula - koma zina zonse zinali zamuyaya, monga upangiri wabwino kwambiri. Tsopano, ambiri, Amamatirani ku bwato , ndi mwambi wanu weniweni mu whaling; koma nthawi zina zidzachitika pamene Kudumpha kuchokera bwato , akadali bwino. Komanso, ngati kuti azindikira kuti ngati angapatse Pip upangiri wopanda chikumbumtima, ndiye kuti akumusiyira malire oti azitha kulumphira mtsogolo; Stubb mwadzidzidzi anasiya uphungu wonse, ndipo anamaliza ndi lamulo la peremptory, "Matirani ku ngalawa, Pip, kapena mwa Ambuye, sindidzakunyamulani ngati mulumpha; samalani zimenezo. Sitingakwanitse kutaya anamgumi monga inu; namgumi angagulitse maulendo makumi atatu zomwe mungakonde, Pip, ku Alabama. Kumbukirani zimenezo, ndipo musalumphenso. " Apa mwina Stubb ananena mosalunjika, kuti ngakhale munthu amakonda mnzake, komabe munthu ndi nyama yopanga ndalama, zomwe nthawi zambiri zimasokoneza ubwino wake.

Koma ife tonse tiri mmanja mwa Amulungu; ndipo Pip adalumphanso. Zinali pamikhalidwe yofanana kwambiri ndi ntchito yoyamba; koma nthawi iyi sadatukula chingwe; ndipo chifukwa chake, chinsombacho chikayamba kuthamanga, Pip adasiyidwa m'nyanja, ngati thunthu la woyenda mwachangu. Kalanga! Stubb anali wowona kwambiri ku mawu ake. Linali tsiku lokongola, lochuluka, labuluu; nyanja yopindika yadekha ndi yozizira, ndipo ikutambasula mozungulira, mozungulira, mpaka m'chizimezime, ngati chikopa chagolide chopunthidwa mopambanitsa. Kukwera ndi kutsika m'nyanjayi, mutu wa ebon wa Pip unkawoneka ngati mutu wa cloves. Palibe mpeni wa ngalawa womwe unamukweza pamene anagwa mofulumira chonchi. Msana wosasunthika wa Stubb unatembenuzidwira pa iye; ndipo chinsombacho chinali nacho mapiko. M'mphindi zitatu, nyanja yopanda nyanja inali pakati pa Pip ndi Stubb. Kuchokera pakatikati pa nyanja, Pip wosauka adatembenuza mutu wake wonyezimira, wopindika, wakuda kudzuwa, malo ena osungulumwa, ngakhale kuti anali apamwamba kwambiri komanso owala kwambiri.

Masiku ano, kunja kukakhala bata, kusambira panyanja n’kosavuta kwa munthu wozolowera kusambira mofanana ndi kukwera ngolo yopita kumtunda. Koma kusungulumwa kowopsyako sikungatheke. Kudziika mtima kwambiri pakati pa ukulu wopanda chifundo, Mulungu wanga! ndani anganene? Mark, mmene amalinyero akusamba m’nyanja mopanda bata—onani mmene akukumbatira kwambiri sitima yawo ndi gombe lokha m’mbali mwake.

Koma kodi Stubb anamusiyadi munthu wakuda wosaukayo kuti akumane ndi tsoka lake? Ayi; iye sanatanthauze kutero, osachepera. Chifukwa panali mabwato awiri mu kudzuka kwake, ndipo iye ankaganiza, mosakayika, kuti iwo ndithudi adzafika kwa Pip mofulumira kwambiri, ndi kumunyamula iye; ngakhale, ndithudi, malingaliro oterowo kwa opalasa omwe ali pachiwopsezo chifukwa chamanyazi awo, sinthawi zonse amawonekera ndi alenje muzochitika zonse zofanana; ndipo zochitika zoterezi sizichitika kawirikawiri; pafupifupi nthawi zonse mu usodzi, wamantha, omwe amatchedwanso, amadziwika ndi kunyansidwa kopanda chifundo komwe kumachitika kwa asitikali apamadzi ndi magulu ankhondo.

Koma zinachitika, kuti ngalawa, popanda kuona Pip, mwadzidzidzi akazitape anamgumi pafupi ndi iwo mbali imodzi, anatembenuka, ndipo anathamangitsa; ndipo bwato la Stubb linali tsopano kutali kwambiri, ndipo iye ndi antchito ake onse anali ndi chidwi kwambiri ndi nsomba zake, kuti Pip's ringed hood anayamba kufalikira momvetsa chisoni mozungulira iye. Mwamwayi chombocho chinam’pulumutsa; koma kuyambira ora lomwelo wachikudayo adayenda pabwalo ngati chitsiru; kotero, osachepera, iwo anati iye anali. Nyanja idasunga thupi lake monyodola, koma idamira moyo wake wopanda malire. Komabe, sanamizidwe konse. M'malo mwake ananyamulidwa ali wamoyo kupita ku kuya modabwitsa, kumene mipangidwe yachilendo ya dziko losasunthika la nyama zakutchire zinkayenda uku ndi uku pamaso pake; Ndipo wachinyengo, Nzeru adavumbulutsa milu yake; ndi pakati pa chisangalalo, opanda mtima, muyaya achichepere, Pip adawona unyinji, wopezeka ponseponse wa Mulungu, tizilombo ta korali, kuti kuchokera mumlengalenga wa madzi kuwulutsa orbs zazikulu. Iye anawona phazi la Mulungu pa chingwe chopondera, ndipo analankhula ilo; ndipo chifukwa chake amnzake amamutcha wamisala. Chotero misala ya munthu ndi nzeru zakumwamba; ndi kuyendayenda kuchokera ku kulingalira konse kwa chivundi, munthu amabwera potsirizira pake ku ganizo lakumwamba lija, limene, kuganiza, nzopanda pake ndi lonjenjemera; ndipo tsoka kapena tsoka, amamva kuti ndiye wosanyengerera, wosayanjanitsika ngati Mulungu wake.

Kwa ena onse, musamadzudzule Stubb kwambiri. Chinthucho ndi chofala mu usodzi umenewo; ndipo motsatizana ndi nkhaniyo, zidzawoneka ngati kudzisiya komwe kunandigwera.
