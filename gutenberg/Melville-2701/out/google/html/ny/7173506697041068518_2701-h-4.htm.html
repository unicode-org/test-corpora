"Ndani koma iye?"

Ine ndimati ndimufunse iye mafunso ena owonjezera okhudza Ahabu, pamene ife tinamva phokoso pa sitima.

"Holloa! Starbuck a astir," anatero wowomberayo. “Iye ndi mnzake wamkulu wamoyo, ameneyo; munthu wabwino, ndi wopembedza; koma onse amoyo tsopano, ndiyenera kutembenukirako.” Ndipo kunena kotero iye anapita pa sitima, ndipo ife tinatsatira.

Tsopano kunja kunali kutacha bwino. Posakhalitsa anthu oyendetsa ngalawa anafika awiriawiri ndi atatu; owomberawo anangodziyendetsa okha; okwatiranawo anali otanganidwa kwambiri; ndipo anthu angapo a m'mphepete mwa nyanja anali otanganidwa kubweretsa zinthu zosiyanasiyana zomaliza. Panthawiyi Kaputeni Ahabu anakhalabe wosaoneka m’nyumba yake.
