Pakuti, chifukwa cha kuchuluka kwa oyenda anamgumi; njira yosalongosoka imene anakonkhedwa pa madzi onse, ambiri a iwo modzidzimutsa akukankhira kufunafuna kwawo m’malo otalikirana, kotero kuti kawirikawiri kapena konse kwa miyezi khumi ndi iwiri kapena kuposerapo motambasuka, kukumana ndi ngalawa imodzi yolengeza nkhani yamtundu uliwonse; kutalika kwakukulu kwa ulendo uliwonse wosiyana; kusakhazikika kwa nthawi yoyenda panyanja kuchokera kunyumba; zonsezi, ndi zochitika zina, zachindunji ndi zosalunjika, zalepheretsa kufalikira padziko lonse lapansi pagulu lapadziko lonse lapansi la uthenga wapadera wokhudza Moby Dick. Sizinali zokayikitsa, kuti ziwiya zingapo linanena kuti anakumana, pa nthawi yoteroyo, kapena pa meridian woteroyo, ndi Umuna Whale wa zachilendo ukulu ndi zoipa, amene chinsomba, pambuyo kuchita zoipa kwambiri kwa adani ake, anathawa kwathunthu; kwa malingaliro ena sikunali kulingalira mopanda chilungamo, ndikunena, kuti chinsomba chomwe chikufunsidwa chiyenera kukhala china koma Moby Dick. Komabe pofika posachedwapa nsomba za Sperm Whale zinali zitadziwika ndi zochitika zosiyanasiyana osati zachilendo zankhanza, chinyengo, ndi njiru mu chilombocho; chotero izo zinali, kuti iwo amene mwangozi mosadziwa anamenyana ndi Moby Dick; alenje oterowo, mwina, kwa gawo lalikulu, anali okhutira kufotokoza zoopsa zachilendo zomwe adazipanga, mochuluka, titero, ku zoopsa za usodzi wa Sperm Whale, kuposa chifukwa cha munthu payekha. Mwanjira imeneyo, makamaka, kukumana kowopsa pakati pa Ahabu ndi namgumi mpaka pano kunali kofala kwambiri.

Ndipo iwo amene adamva kale za White Whale, mwangozi adamuwona; mu chiyambi cha chinthu iwo anali aliyense wa iwo, pafupifupi, monga molimba mtima ndi mopanda mantha adatsitsidwa kwa iye, monga chinsomba china chirichonse cha mtundu umenewo. Koma m’kupita kwa nthaŵi, masoka oterowo anadza m’ziukiro zimenezi—osati zongotyoka manja ndi akakolo, zothyoka miyendo, kapena kudula ziŵalo zomeza—koma zakupha kufikira mlingo womalizira wakupha; zilakolako zobwerezabwereza zowononga, zonse zikuwunjika ndikuunjikira zoopsa zawo pa Moby Dick; Zinthu zimenezo zinali zitapita kutali kuti zigwedeze mphamvu ya alenje olimba mtima ambiri, kwa amene nkhani ya Chinsomba Choyera inafika pomalizira pake.

Komanso mphekesera zamitundumitundu sizinalephere kukokomeza, ndipo m'pamenenso mbiri yowona ya kukumana koopsa kumeneku ndi yowopsa kwambiri. Sikuti mphekesera zochititsa mantha zimatuluka mwachibadwa kuchokera m'thupi la zochitika zonse zoopsa, monga mtengo wokanthidwa umabala mafangasi ake; koma, m’moyo wa m’nyanja, zochulukiratu kuposa zija za terra firma, mphekesera zakuthengo zimachuluka, kulikonse kumene kuli chenicheni chirichonse chokwanira kaamba ka iwo kukakamirako. Ndipo monga momwe nyanja imaposa nthaka pankhaniyi, momwemonso nsomba za namgumi zimaposa zamoyo zapanyanja zamtundu uliwonse, modabwitsa komanso mwamantha a mphekesera zomwe nthawi zina zimafalikira kumeneko. Pakuti si anthu a m'madzi monga gulu losamasulidwa ku umbuli ndi kukhulupirira malaulo zomwe zimatengera amalinyero onse; koma mwa amalinyero onse, iwo ndi omwe ali ndi mwayi wokumana nawo mwachindunji chilichonse chomwe chili chodabwitsa m'nyanja; maso ndi maso iwo samangoyang'ana zozizwitsa zake zazikulu, koma, dzanja ndi nsagwada, zimamenyana nawo. Iwe wekha, m’madzi akutali chotero, kuti ngakhale unayenda mailosi chikwi, ndi kudutsa magombe chikwi, simunafika ku mwala uliwonse wonyezimira, kapena wochereza pansi pa mbali imeneyo ya dzuwa; m'madera oterowo ndi ma longitude, kufunafunanso kuitana kotere monga momwe amachitira, msodzi amazunguliridwa ndi zisonkhezero zonse zomwe zimachititsa kuti malingaliro ake akhale ndi pakati pa kubadwa kwamphamvu.

Choncho, n’zosadabwitsa kuti nthaŵi zonse kusonkhanitsa kuchuluka kwa mafunde a m’madzi otakasuka kwambiri, mphekesera zomveka za White Whale pamapeto pake zinaphatikizirapo mitundu yonse ya malingaliro oipa, ndi malingaliro ang’onoang’ono a mphamvu zauzimu, amene m’kupita kwanthaŵi anaika Moby Dick ndi zoopsa zatsopano zosabwerekedwa ku chirichonse chimene chikuwonekera. Kotero kuti nthawi zambiri mantha oterowo adafika potsiriza, kuti ochepa omwe ndi mphekesera zimenezo, osachepera, adamva za White Whale, ochepa mwa alenje amenewo anali okonzeka kukumana ndi zoopsa za nsagwada zake.

Koma panalinso zinthu zina zothandiza kwambiri pa ntchito. Ngakhale masiku ano palibe kutchuka koyambirira kwa Nangumi wa Umuna, yemwe amasiyanitsidwa mochititsa mantha ndi mitundu ina yonse ya leviathan, yomwe inafa m'maganizo mwa a whalemen monga thupi. Pali ena lero pakati pawo, amene, ngakhale kuti anali anzeru ndi olimba mtima mokwanira popereka nkhondo ku Greenland kapena Right whale, mwina—kaya chifukwa cha kusadziŵa bwino ntchito, kapena kusakhoza, kapena mantha, kukana mpikisano ndi Nangumi wa Umuna; Mulimonsemo, pali ochuluka a whalemen, makamaka pakati pa mayiko whaling osayenda pansi pa mbendera American, amene sanayambe mwaudani anakumana ndi Nangumi Umuna, koma amene chidziwitso cha leviathan ndi okhawo chilombo chonyozeka primitively anatsatira kumpoto; Atakhala pazipewa zawo, amuna awa amamvetsera mwachidwi komanso mwachidwi, ku nthano zakuthengo, zachilendo zaku Southern whaling. Komanso kuchulukira kwamphamvu kwa Nangumi wamkulu wa Sperm Whale kulikonse komwe kumamveka bwino, kuposa m'mphepete mwa nsonga zomwe zimamulepheretsa.

Ndipo monga ngati kuti mphamvu zake zoyesedwa tsopano zidaika mthunzi wake patsogolo pake; timapeza ena odziwa zachilengedwe - Olassen ndi Povelson - akulengeza kuti Mtsinje wa Umuna sikuti ungokhala wodabwitsa kwa cholengedwa china chilichonse cha m'nyanja, komanso kukhala wowopsa kwambiri monga kukhala ndi ludzu la magazi a anthu mosalekeza. Ngakhalenso mpaka mochedwa kwambiri ngati Cuvier's, izi kapena zofananira zomwe zidawoneka. Pakuti m’kabuku kake ka Natural History, Baron mwiniwakeyo akutsimikizira kuti poona Nangumi wa Umuna, nsomba zonse (nsomba za shaki) “zimakanthidwa ndi zoopsa zowopsa kwambiri,” ndipo “kaŵirikaŵiri m’kuthaŵa kwawo zimathamangira kumiyala ndi chiwawa choterocho mpaka kuchititsa imfa yanthaŵi yomweyo.” Ndipo komabe zochitika muusodzi zitha kusintha malipoti ngati awa; komabe mu kuopsa kwawo kotheratu, ngakhale ku chinthu chokhetsa magazi cha Povelson, chikhulupiriro chamatsenga mwa iwo, m'njira zina za ntchito yawo, chimatsitsimutsidwa m'malingaliro a osaka.

Kotero kuti mantha ndi mphekesera ndi zodabwitsa zokhudza iye, si ochepa a asodzi anakumbukira, ponena za Moby Dick, masiku oyambirira a Umuna Whale nsomba, pamene nthawi zambiri zinali zovuta kukopa yaitali anachita Ufulu whalemen kuti ayambe mu zoopsa za nkhondo yatsopanoyi ndi wolimba mtima; amuna oterowo akutsutsa kuti ngakhale alevi ena akhoza kutsatiridwa, komabe kuthamangitsa ndi kuloza kuwoneka ngati Chinsomba cha Sperm Whale sichinali cha munthu. Kuti kuyesa izo, kudzakhala mosapeŵeka kung'ambika mu muyaya mwamsanga. Pamutu uwu, pali zolemba zina zochititsa chidwi zomwe zingafufuzidwe.

Komabe, ena analipo, omwe ngakhale pamaso pa zinthu izi anali okonzeka kuthamangitsa Moby Dick; ndipo enanso okulirapo amene, kungozimva za iye motalikirana ndi momvekera bwino, popanda tsatanetsatane wachindunji wa tsoka linalake, ndipo popanda kutsagana ndi zikhulupiriro, anali olimba mokwanira kuti asathawe kunkhondo ngati ataperekedwa.

Limodzi mwa malingaliro akutchire omwe akutchulidwa, monga potsirizira pake akubwera kuti agwirizane ndi White Whale m'maganizo a okhulupirira zamatsenga, anali kudzikuza kuti Moby Dick anali ponseponse; kuti adakumanapo m'malo osiyanasiyana panthawi imodzi.

Komanso, maganizo oterewo ayenera kuti anali ongotengeka maganizo, sikunali kudzikuza kopanda chionetsero chochepa chabe cha kukhulupirira malodza. Pakuti monga zinsinsi za mafunde a m'nyanja sizinaululidwe, ngakhale kufufuza kozama kwambiri; kotero njira zobisika za Umuna Whale pamene pansi pa nthaka zimakhalabe, mbali yaikulu, zosawerengeka kwa omulondola; ndipo nthawi ndi nthawi ayambitsa zongopeka zachidwi kwambiri ndi zotsutsana ponena za iwo, makamaka okhudza mitundu yachinsinsi yomwe, pambuyo pa kumveka mozama kwambiri, amadziyendetsa yekha ndi kufulumira kwakukulu koteroko kupita kumadera akutali kwambiri.

Ndi chinthu chodziwika bwino kwa zombo zonse za ku America ndi Chingerezi, komanso chinthu chomwe chinayikidwa pa mbiri yakale zaka zapitazo ndi Scoresby, kuti anamgumi ena agwidwa kutali kumpoto kwa Pacific, omwe matupi awo apezeka mipiringidzo ya ma harpoons akuyenda mu nyanja ya Greenland. Komanso siziyenera kutsutsidwa, kuti nthawi zina izi zanenedwa kuti nthawi yapakati pa ziwawa ziwirizi sizingadutse masiku ambiri. Motero, mongoyerekeza, asayansi ena amakhulupirira kuti Nor' West Passage, yomwe inali vuto kwa nthawi yaitali kwa munthu, silinali vuto kwa namgumi. Kotero kuti pano, muzochitika zenizeni zamoyo za amuna amoyo, zochititsa chidwi zakale za phiri la Strello ku Portugal (pafupi ndi pamwamba pake pankanenedwa kuti pali nyanja yomwe zosweka za zombo zinayandama pamwamba); ndi nkhani yodabwitsa kwambiri ya kasupe wa Arethusa pafupi ndi Surakusa (amene madzi ake ankakhulupirira kuti anachokera ku Dziko Lopatulika ndi ndime ya pansi pa nthaka); nkhani zochititsa chidwi izi pafupifupi mofanana mokwanira ndi zenizeni za whalemen.

Kukakamizika kuzolowerana, ndiye, ndi prodigies ngati izi; ndipo podziwa kuti pambuyo poukiridwa mobwerezabwereza, mopanda mantha, White Whale inathawa wamoyo; sichingakhale chodabwitsa kwambiri kuti asodzi ena ayenera kupitabe patsogolo m’zikhulupiriro zawo; kulengeza Moby Dick osati ponseponse, koma wosafa (pakuti moyo wosakhoza kufa uli koma ubiquity mu nthawi); kuti ngakhale zitabzalidwa mikondo m’mbali mwace, iye adzasambirabe osavulazidwa; kapena ngati alavulidwa magazi okhuthara, kuoneka koteroko sikungangokhala chinyengo choipitsitsa; pakuti kachiwiri mu mafunde osasunthika mazana a ligi kutali, ndege yake yosawonongeka idzawonekanso.

Koma ngakhale atalandidwa zongopeka zauzimu izi, panali zokwanira mu kapangidwe ka dziko lapansi ndi khalidwe losatsutsika la chilombocho kuti ligonjetse malingaliro ndi mphamvu zosadziwika. Pakuti, sikunali kochuluka kwake kosazolowereka komwe kumamusiyanitsa kwambiri ndi anamgumi ena a umuna, koma, monga momwe anatayira kwina kulikonse - mphumi yachilendo yoyera ngati chipale chofewa, ndi hump yoyera ya piramidi. Izi zinali mawonekedwe ake odziwika; zizindikiro zomwe, ngakhale m'nyanja zopanda malire, zosazindikirika, adadziwonetsera yekha, patali, kwa iwo omwe amamudziwa.

Thupi lake lonse linali la mikwingwirima, ndi mawanga, ndi miyala ya nsangalabwi ndi mtundu womwewo wophimbidwa, kotero kuti, pamapeto pake, adapeza kutchuka kwake kwa White Whale; dzina, ndithudi, lolungamitsidwa kwenikweni ndi mawonekedwe ake owoneka bwino, pamene likuwoneka likuyenda masana masana kupyola mu nyanja yakuda yabuluu, kusiya chithovu chosalala, chonse chodzaza ndi zonyezimira zagolide.

Komanso sikunali kukula kwake kosadziwika, kapena mawonekedwe ake odabwitsa, kapena nsagwada zake zapansi zopunduka, zomwe zinachititsa kuti chinsombacho chiwopsyezeke, monga nkhanza zosayerekezeka, zaluntha zomwe, malinga ndi nkhani zina, adaziwonetsa mobwerezabwereza m'mawu ake. Koma ngakhale masoka otere, ngakhale avulazidwa pang'ono kumtunda, sizinali zachilendo mu usodzi; komabe, muzochitika zambiri, izi zinkawoneka ngati White Whale ndi infernal infernal aforethought of infercity, kuti kudula kulikonse kapena imfa yomwe iye anayambitsa, sikunali kuonedwa ngati kuchitidwa ndi wothandizira wosazindikira.

Woweruza, ndiye, ku mabwalo owopsa, osokonezedwa ndi mkwiyo wa alenje ake osimidwa kwambiri adasonkhezeredwa, pamene pakati pa zipsera za mabwato omwe amatafunidwa, ndi miyendo yakumira ya ma comrades ong'ambika, adasambira kuchokera m'miyendo yoyera ya mkwiyo wowopsa wa nangumi kulowa mumtendere, ngati kuwala kwa dzuwa, ngati kusekerera kwa mkwatibwi pa kubadwa.

Ngalawa zake zitatu zinamuzungulira iye, ndi zopalasira ndi amuna onse akuyendayenda m’mphepete mwa nyanja; woyendetsa m'modzi, atatenga mpeni wakutsogolo kwake wosweka, adathamangira chinsombacho, monga womenyana ndi mdani wake ku Arkansas, kufunafuna mwachimbulimbuli ndi tsamba la mainchesi sikisi kuti afikire moyo wakuzama wa chinsombacho.
