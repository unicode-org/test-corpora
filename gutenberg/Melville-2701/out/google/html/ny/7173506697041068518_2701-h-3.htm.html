MUTU 10. Bwenzi lapachifuwa.

Kubwerera ku Spouter-Inn kuchokera ku Chapel, ndinapeza Queequeg kumeneko yekha; adachoka ku Chapel nthawi isanaperekedwe. Iye anali atakhala pa benchi patsogolo pa moto, ndi mapazi ake pa mbaula mbaula, ndipo mu dzanja limodzi anali atagwirizira chapafupi ku nkhope yake fano lake lachikuda; akuyang'anitsitsa m'maso mwake, ndipo mpeni ukugwedezeka pang'onopang'ono pamphuno pake, uku akung'ung'udza yekha mwachikunja.

Koma atasokonezedwa tsopano, naimika fanolo; ndipo posakhalitsa, akupita ku gome, anatenga bukhu lalikulu kumeneko, naliyika pa chifuwa chake anayamba kuwerenga masamba mwadala; Patsamba lililonse la makumi asanu—momwe ndimayembekezera—nditaima kamphindi, ndikuyang’ana mopanda kanthu momuzungulira, ndi kuyankhula ku mluzu wodabwitsa. Adzayambanso pa makumi asanu otsatira; akuwoneka kuti akuyamba pa nambala wani nthawi iliyonse, ngati kuti sangawerenge kupitirira makumi asanu, ndipo zinali kokha mwa kuchuluka kwa makumi asanu omwe anapezeka palimodzi, kuti kudabwa kwake pa unyinji wa masamba kunakondwera.

Mwachidwi kwambiri ndinakhala ndikumuyang'ana. Ngakhale anali wankhanza, komanso wodetsedwa kwambiri pankhope - makamaka mwa kukoma kwanga - nkhope yake inali ndi china chake chomwe sichinali chosagwirizana. Simungathe kubisa mzimu. Kupyolera mu zolemba zake zonse zosakhala pansi, ndinaganiza kuti ndinawona zizindikiro za mtima wosavuta woona mtima; ndipo m'maso mwake akulu, akuya, akuda amoto ndi olimba mtima, munawoneka zizindikiro za mzimu umene ungayese ziwanda chikwi. Ndipo pambali pa zonsezi, panali kunyada kwina kwa Akunja, kumene ngakhale chigololo chake sichinathe konse. Ankaoneka ngati munthu amene anali asanakhalepo n’komwe ndiponso anali asanakhalepo ndi ngongole. Kaya kunalinso, kuti mutu wake ukumetedwa, mphumi yake inakokedwa mu mpumulo womasuka ndi wowala, ndipo inkawoneka yotambasula kuposa momwe izo zikanakhalira, izi sindidzayesa kusankha; koma ndithu kuti mutu wake unali wabwino kwambiri. Zingawoneke ngati zopusa, koma zidandikumbutsa mutu wa General Washington, monga tawonera m'mabasi otchuka a iye. Inali ndi malo otsetsereka aatali omwe nthawi zambiri amabwerera kuchokera pamwamba pa zinkhokwe, zomwenso zinali zowoneka bwino, ngati zipinda ziwiri zazitali zokhala ndi matabwa pamwamba. Queequeg anali George Washington wodya anthu.

Pamene ndinali kumufufuza mosamalitsa, ndikunamizira kuti ndikuyang'ana mphepo yamkuntho kuchokera m'bwalo, sanamvere kukhalapo kwanga, sanadzivutitse ngakhale kungoyang'ana kumodzi; koma anaoneka wotanganitsidwa kotheratu ndi kuŵerenga masamba a bukhu lochititsa chidwilo. Poganizira momwe tinali kugona limodzi usiku watha, makamaka poganizira mkono wachikondi womwe ndinaupeza utaponyedwa pa ine podzuka m'mawa, ndinaganiza kuti kusayanjanitsika kwake kunali kodabwitsa kwambiri. Koma achifwamba ndi zolengedwa zachilendo; nthawi zina simudziwa momwe mungawatengere. Poyamba, iwo ndi ochuluka; kudzisonkhanitsa kwawo modekha kwa kuphweka kumawoneka ngati nzeru za Sokerasi. Ndinazindikiranso kuti Queequeg sanagwirizane konse, kapena pang'ono kwambiri, ndi apanyanja ena m'nyumba ya alendo. Sadatukule chilichonse; adawoneka kuti alibe chikhumbo chokulitsa gulu la omwe amawadziwa. Zonsezi zinandikhudza ine ngati wamphamvu mmodzi; komabe, pamalingaliro achiwiri, munali chinachake pafupifupi chapamwamba mmenemo. Pano panali munthu wa mailosi zikwi makumi awiri kuchokera kwawo, njira ya ku Cape Horn, ndiko kuti—imene inali njira yokhayo imene akanatha kukafikako—woponyedwa pakati pa anthu odabwitsa kwa iye monga ngati anali m’dziko la Jupiter; ndipo komabe iye adawoneka momasuka; kusunga bata kwambiri; wokhutira ndi bwenzi lake; nthawi zonse wofanana naye. Ndithudi uku kunali kukhudza kwa filosofi yabwino; ngakhale mosakayikira anali asanamvepo kuti pali chinthu choterocho. Koma, mwina, kuti tikhale anthanthi owona, anthufe sitiyenera kukhala ozindikira za kukhala ndi moyo kapena kulimbikira. Ndikangomva kuti munthu woteroyo wadzipereka yekha kukhala wanzeru, ndimaganiza kuti, monga mayi wachikulire, ayenera kuti "adathyola chimbudzi chake."

Pamene ine ndinakhala mmenemo m'chipindamo chopanda anthu; moto woyaka pang'onopang'ono, mu siteji yofatsa pamene, pambuyo pa mphamvu yake yoyamba yatenthetsa mpweya, ndiye umangowala kuti uwoneke; mithunzi yamadzulo ndi zozizwitsa zikusonkhanira m'mipando, ndikuyang'ana mkati mwathu chete, awiri okha; mvula yamkuntho ikukula popanda kuphulika kwakukulu; Ndinayamba kukhala wanzeru ndi malingaliro achilendo. Ndinamva kusungunuka mwa ine. Palibenso mtima wanga wosweka ndi dzanja langa lopenga lomwe silinatembenuzidwe motsutsana ndi dziko la mimbulu. Wachipongwe ameneyu anali atawombola. Kumeneko anakhala, kusayanjanitsika kwake kumalankhula chikhalidwe chomwe munalibe chinyengo chotukuka ndi chinyengo chopanda pake. Iye anali wakuthengo; mawonekedwe owoneka bwino kwambiri; komabe ndinayamba kudzimva kuti ndikukopeka modabwitsa kwa iye. Ndipo zinthu zomwezo zomwe zikanabweza ena ambiri, anali maginito omwe amandikoka. Ndiyesa mnzanga wachikunja, ndimaganiza ine, popeza kukoma mtima kwachikhristu kwatsimikizira ulemu wopanda pake. Ndinayakira benchi yanga pafupi ndi iye, ndipo ndinapanga zizindikiro zaubwenzi ndi malangizo, kuyesetsa kuti ndilankhule naye panthawiyi. Poyamba sanazindikire zotukuka izi; koma pakali pano, ndikunena za alendo omwe adalandira usiku watha, adaganiza zondifunsa ngati tidzakhalanso ogona. Ndinamuuza kuti inde; pomwe ndimaganiza kuti akuwoneka wokondwa, mwina woyamikira pang'ono.

Kenako tinatsegula limodzi bukhulo, ndipo ndinayesetsa kumufotokozera cholinga cha kusindikiza, ndi tanthauzo la zithunzi zochepa zimene zinali mmenemo. Motero posakhalitsa ndinachita chidwi chake; ndipo kuchokera pamenepo tidapita kukacheza bwino kwambiri momwe tingathere zamitundu yosiyanasiyana yakunja yomwe tingawone mtawuni yotchukayi. Posakhalitsa ndinapempha kuti tizisuta; ndipo, akupanga thumba lake ndi tomahawk, iye mwakachetechete anandipatsa mpweya. Ndiyeno ife tinakhala kusinthanitsa zofunkha kuchokera ku chitoliro chake chakuthengocho, ndi kuchisunga mokhazikika chikudutsa pakati pathu.

Ngati mu chifuwa cha Akunja munandibisalirabe madzi oundana, utsi wosangalatsa uwu umene tinali nawo, posakhalitsa unasungunula, ndi kutisiyira ife abwenzi. Iye ankawoneka kuti amanditengera kwa ine mwachibadwa ndi mosadziletsa monga ine kwa iye; ndipo pamene utsi wathu unatha, anakanikizira mphumi yake pa yanga, nandigwira m’chiuno, nati kuyambira tsopano tinakwatirana; kutanthauza, m’mawu a dziko lake, kuti tinali mabwenzi apamtima; akanandifera mokondwera, ngati pangafunike kutero. Kwa munthu wakumudzi, lawi ladzidzidzi laubwenzi limeneli likadawoneka kukhala losadalitsika kwambiri, chinthu choyenera kusadaliridwa; koma m’zomvetsa chisoni izi malamulo akale sakanagwira ntchito.

Titadya chakudya chamadzulo, ndi kucheza kwinanso ndikusuta, tinapita kuchipinda chathu pamodzi. Anandipangira mphatso ya pamutu pake; anatulutsa chikwama chake chachikulu cha fodya, ndikupapasa pansi pa fodya, natulutsamo madola makumi atatu asiliva; kenako kuwayala patebulo, ndi kumawagawa iwo mu magawo awiri ofanana, anakankhira mmodzi wa iwo kwa ine, ndipo anati icho chinali changa. Ine ndimati ndiwonetsere; koma adanditontholetsa pondithira m'matumba a thalauza langa. Ndinawalola kukhala. Kenako anayamba kuchita mapemphero ake amadzulo, n’kutulutsa fano lake, n’kuchotsa bolodi lamoto. Ndi zizindikiro ndi zizindikiro zina, ndinaganiza kuti akuwoneka wofunitsitsa kuti ndigwirizane naye; koma podziwa zimene zidzachitike, ndinalingalira kaye kaye ngati atandiitana, ndimvera kapena ayi.

Ndinali Mkhristu wabwino; kubadwa ndi kuleredwa mu chifuwa cha Mpingo wosalephera wa Presbateria. Nanga ndingathe bwanji kugwirizana ndi wopembedza mafano wakuthengoyu polambira mtengo wake? Koma kodi kupembedza ndi chiyani? ndinaganiza I. Kodi mukuganiza tsopano, Ishmaeli, kuti Mulungu wamkulu wakumwamba ndi dziko lapansi—akunja ndi onse—akhoza kuchitira nsanje kamtengo kakang’ono ka mtengo wakuda? N'zosatheka! Koma kodi kulambira nchiyani?—kuchita chifuniro cha Mulungu— kumeneko ndiko kulambira. Ndipo chifuniro cha Mulungu nchiyani?—kuti ndichitire mnzanga chimene ndingafune kuti mnzanga andichitire ine, ndicho chifuniro cha Mulungu. Tsopano, Queequeg ndi mnzanga. Ndipo ndikufuna kuti Queequeg uyu andichite chiyani? Bwanji, lumikizanani nane mu mtundu wanga wa kupembedza kwa Presbateria. Chifukwa chake ndiyenera kuyanjana naye mwa iye; chifukwa chake, ndiyenera kukhala wopembedza mafano. Chotero ndinayatsa mimeteyo; anathandizira kulimbikitsa fano laling'ono losalakwa; anamupatsa masikono opsereza ndi Queequeg; kupemphera kawiri kapena katatu pamaso pake; napsyopsyona mphuno yake; ndipo zitatero, tinavula ndi kukagona, pamtendere ndi chikumbumtima chathu komanso dziko lonse lapansi. Koma sitinagone popanda kucheza pang’ono.

Ziri bwanji sindikudziwa; koma palibe malo ngati bedi oulula mwachinsinsi pakati pa mabwenzi. Mwamuna ndi mkazi, iwo amati, pamenepo amatsegula pansi pa miyoyo yawo kwa wina ndi mzake; ndipo okwatirana ena okalamba nthawi zambiri amanama ndi kucheza nthawi zakale mpaka m'mawa. Chotero, m’mitima yathu yaukwati, ndinagona ine ndi Queequeg—awiri omasuka, okondana.





MUTU 11. Chovala chausiku.

Tinali atagona motero pabedi, kucheza ndi kugona pakapita nthawi yochepa, ndipo Queequeg tsopano ndiyeno mwachikondi kuponya miyendo yake bulauni mphini pa anga, ndiyeno kuwakokera iwo mmbuyo; ochezeka kotheratu ndi omasuka ndi osavuta tinali ife; pamene, potsirizira pake, chifukwa cha zokangana zathu, tulo tating’ono tomwe tinatsalira mwa ife tinachoka, ndipo tinamva ngati tidzukenso, ngakhale kuti kadzutsa kanali m’tsogolo.

Inde, tinakhala maso kwambiri; kotero kuti malo athu otsalirawo anayamba kutopa, ndipo pang’ono ndi pang’ono tinadzipeza titakhala; zovalazo zinatizinga bwino, tikutsamira pa thabwa lamutu ndi mawondo athu anayi atakokedwa moyandikana, ndipo mphuno zathu ziŵiri zikuweramira pamwamba pa izo, monga ngati ziwiya za mawondo athu zinali zotenthetsera. Tinamva kukhala abwino kwambiri komanso odekha, makamaka popeza kunja kunali kozizira kwambiri; anatulukanso m'zovala poona kuti m'chipindamo mulibe moto. Ndikunena choncho kwambiri, chifukwa kuti musangalale ndi kutentha kwa thupi, mbali ina yaing'ono ya inu iyenera kukhala yozizira, chifukwa palibe khalidwe m'dzikoli lomwe silili momwe liri mosiyana. Palibe chomwe chilipo chokha. Ngati mumadzikweza kuti ndinu omasuka, ndipo mwakhala kwa nthawi yayitali, ndiye kuti simunganenedwenso kukhala omasuka. Koma ngati, monga Queequeg ndi ine pabedi, nsonga ya mphuno yanu kapena mutu wamutu mwazizira pang'ono, bwanji ndiye, ndithudi, mu chidziwitso chonse mumamva mosangalala komanso kutentha mosakayika. Pachifukwa ichi, nyumba yogona siyenera kutenthedwa ndi moto, womwe ndi umodzi mwazovuta za anthu olemera. Pakuti kutalika kwa mtundu uwu wa zokoma ndi kukhala wopanda kanthu koma bulangeti pakati pa inu ndi snugness wanu ndi kuzizira kwa mpweya wakunja. Ndiye pamenepo mukugona ngati kuwala kotentha komwe kuli mkati mwa kristalo wa kumtunda.

Tinali titakhala mogwada kwa nthawi ndithu, pamene nthawi yomweyo ndinaganiza kuti nditsegule maso anga; pakuti pamene pakati pa mapepala, kaya ndi masana kapena usiku, ndipo kaya ali m'tulo kapena maso, ndimakhala ndi njira yotsekereza maso anga nthawi zonse, kuti ndiwonetsetse kwambiri kugona kwa kukhala pabedi. Chifukwa palibe munthu amene angadzimve bwino momwe alili pokhapokha maso ake atatsekedwa; monga ngati kuti mdima ndiwo chinthu choyenera cha zinthu zathu, ngakhale kuwala kumakhala kogwirizana kwambiri ndi gawo lathu ladongo. Nditatsegula maso anga pamenepo, ndikutuluka mumdima wanga wosangalatsa komanso wodzipangira ndekha mumdima wonyezimira wakunja wa 12 koloko usiku, ndidakumana ndi mkwiyo wosavomerezeka. Ndiponso sindinatsutsane ndi lingaliro lochokera ku Queequeg kuti mwina kunali kwabwino kumenya kuwala, powona kuti tinali maso kwambiri; ndipo pambali pake adamva chikhumbo champhamvu chokhala ndi matontho ochepa ochepa kuchokera ku Tomahawk yake. Ziri choncho, kuti ngakhale ndinali nditamva kuipidwa kwambiri ndi kusuta kwake pabedi usiku watha, koma onani momwe tsankho lathu lolimba limakulirakulira pamene chikondi chikafika kuti chizikhotetsa. Pakuti tsopano ine ndinkakonda kanthu kuposa kukhala Queequeg kusuta ndi ine, ngakhale pabedi, chifukwa ankaoneka kuti wodzaza ndi chimwemwe chotere cha banja ndiye. Sindinayambenso kuda nkhaŵa ndi ndondomeko ya inshuwalansi ya mwininyumba. Ndinkangokhalira kugawana chitoliro komanso bulangeti ndi mnzanga weniweni. Ndi ma jekete athu onyezimira atakokedwa pamapewa athu, tsopano tidadutsa Tomahawk kuchokera kumodzi kupita ku imzake, mpaka pang'onopang'ono utsi woyezera utsi wabuluu unakula pamwamba pathu, wowunikiridwa ndi lawi la nyali yoyaka.

Kaya anali kuti woyesa wovutitsayu adagubuduza woyipayo kupita kutali, sindikudziwa, koma tsopano adalankhula za chisumbu chakwawo; ndipo, mofunitsitsa kumva mbiri yake, ndinamupempha kuti apitirize kunena. Anamvera mosangalala. Ngakhale panthawiyo sindimamvetsetsa mawu ake ochepa, komabe zowulula zomwe zidachitika pambuyo pake, nditazolowerana ndi mawu ake osweka, tsopano zimandithandizira kufotokoza nkhani yonse monga momwe ingasonyezere m'mafupa omwe ndimapereka.





MUTU 12. Zambiri.

Queequeg anali mbadwa ya Rokovoko, chilumba chakutali kumadzulo ndi Kumwera. Sili pansi pamapu aliwonse; malo enieni sali konse.

Pamene latsopano-aswa wankhanza akuthamanga kuthengo za kwawo nkhalango mu udzu clout, kutsatiridwa ndi nibbling mbuzi, ngati kuti anali wobiriwira sapling; ngakhale pamenepo, m’moyo wofuna kutchuka wa Queequeg, munabisala chikhumbo champhamvu chofuna kuwona china chake cha Matchalitchi Achikristu kuposa chitsanzo cha namgumi kapena ziŵiri. Atate ake anali Mkulu Wamkulu, Mfumu; amalume ake Mkulu wa Ansembe; ndipo kumbali ya amayi adanyadira azakhali omwe anali akazi ankhondo osagonjetseka. Munali magazi abwino kwambiri m'mitsempha yake - zinthu zachifumu; ngakhale adakhumudwa momvetsa chisoni, ndikuwopa, chifukwa cha kudya anthu komwe adadya muunyamata wake wosaphunzitsidwa.

Sitima yapamadzi ya Sag Harbor inapita kudoko la atate wake, ndipo Queequeg anafuna njira yopitira kumaiko achikristu. Koma ngalawa, pokhala nao amalinyero, inakana malaya ace; ndipo si mphamvu zonse za Mfumu atate wake zikanapambana. Koma Queequeg analumbira. Ali yekha m’ngalawa yake, anapalasa kupita ku njira yakutali, imene ankadziwa kuti ngalawayo iyenera kudutsa pamene inkatuluka pachilumbacho. Kumbali ina kunali matanthwe a korali; kwinakwake, lilime laling'ono lamtunda, lokutidwa ndi nkhalango za mangrove zomwe zinamera m'madzi. Iye anabisa ngalawa yake yoyandama pakati pa nkhalangozi, kutsogolo kwake kunka kunyanja, iye anakhala pansi kumbuyo kwa ngalawayo, yopalasa m’manja mwake; ndipo m’mene chombo chidalikuyandama, chinatuluka ngati mphezi; adapeza mbali yake; ndi kugunda kumodzi kwa phazi lake lakumbuyo ndi kumiza bwato lake; anakwera mmwamba unyolo; ndipo anadzigwetsera pamwamba pa dengapo, nagwira chingwe cha mphete pamenepo, nalumbira kuti sadzachisiya, chingakhale chinaduladula.

M’pake kuti kapitawoyo anaopseza kuti amponya m’madzi; anapachika chodulidwa pamwamba pa manja ake maliseche; Queequeg anali mwana wa Mfumu, ndipo Queequeg sanasinthe. Atagwidwa ndi kulimba mtima kwake, ndi chikhumbo chake chadzaoneni chochezera Matchalitchi Achikristu, kapitawoyo pomalizira pake analeka, namuuza kuti akhoza kukhala kunyumba. Koma mnyamata wabwino wankhalwe ameneyu—Kalonga wa ku Wales wa m’nyanjayi, sanaonepo kanyumba ka Captain. Iwo anamuyika iye pansi pakati pa amalinyero, ndipo anamupanga iye munthu wa nsomba. Koma mofanana ndi Czar Peter wokhutiritsidwa kugwira ntchito m’mabwalo a zombo za m’mizinda yakunja, Queequeg sananyoze chilichonse chooneka ngati chonyozeka, ngati mwakutero akanapeza mwachimwemwe mphamvu yakuunikira anthu a m’dziko lake osaphunzitsidwa. Pakuti pansi—chotero anandiuza—anasonkhezeredwa ndi chikhumbo chachikulu chofuna kuphunzira pakati pa Akristu, luso lopangitsa anthu ake kukhala achimwemwebe kuposa mmene analili; ndipo koposa izo, akadali bwino kuposa iwo. Koma, kalanga! machitachita a asodzi anam’tsimikizira kuti ngakhale Akristu angakhale atsoka ndi oipa; mopanda malire, kuposa achikunja onse a atate wake. Anafika potsiriza ku Sag Harbor yakale; ndi kuona zimene amalinyero anachita kumeneko; ndiyeno kupita ku Nantucket, ndi kuwona momwe iwo amathera malipiro awo mu malo nawonso, Queequeg osauka anapereka izo chifukwa chotayika. Anaganiza kuti, ndi dziko loipa mu meridians onse; Ndidzafa wachikunja.

Ndipo chotero, wopembedza mafano wakale mu mtima, iye ankakhalabe pakati pa Akhristu awa, ankavala zovala zawo, ndipo ankayesera kulankhula zamwano zawo. Chifukwa chake njira zamakedzana za iye, ngakhale tsopano kwakanthawi kuchokera kunyumba.

Ndi malingaliro, ndinamufunsa ngati sakufuna kubwerera, ndikukhala ndi korona; popeza akadaona atate wace atafa, napita, popeza anali wokalamba ndithu ndi wofooka. Iye anayankha kuti ayi, koma ayi; ndipo anawonjezera kuti iye anali Chikhristu choopsa, kapena kuti Akhristu, sanamuyenerere kukwera pampando wachifumu woyera ndi wosadetsedwa wa mafumu makumi atatu achikunja patsogolo pake. Koma posapita nthaŵi, iye anati adzabwerera, —atangomva kuti wabatizidwanso. Koma kalelo, iye anaganiza zoyenda uku ndi uku, ndi kufesa mphesa zake zakuthengo m’nyanja zonse zinayi. Iwo anali atamupanga iye chomuwuzira iye, ndipo chitsulo chonga minga chinali mmalo mwa ndodo tsopano.

Ndinamufunsa chomwe chingakhale cholinga chake, chokhudza mayendedwe ake amtsogolo. Iye anayankha, kuti apitenso kunyanja, mu ntchito yake yakale. Zitatha izi, ndinamuuza kuti kupha nsomba kunali kwanga ndekha, ndipo ndinamuuza za cholinga changa chochoka ku Nantucket, monga doko lodalirika kwambiri la whaleman wothamanga kuti ayambepo. Nthaŵi yomweyo iye anatsimikiza kutsagana nane ku chisumbu chimenecho, kukwera m’chombo chomwecho, kuloŵa ulonda womwewo, bwato lomwelo, chisokonezo chofanana ndi ine, mwachidule kugawana zanga zonse; ndi manja anga onse m'manja mwake, molimba mtima ndilowerere mu Potluck wa maiko onse awiri. Izi zonse ndinavomereza mokondwera; pakuti pambali pa chikondi chimene ine tsopano ndinachimva kwa Queequeg, iye anali wodziwa harpooneer, ndipo chotero, sakanakhoza kulephera kukhala wothandiza kwambiri kwa munthu, yemwe, monga ine, ndinali wosadziwa kwathunthu zinsinsi za whaling, ngakhale kuti ankadziwa bwino nyanja, monga amadziwika kwa apanyanja amalonda.

Nkhani yake ikutha ndi chitoliro chake chomaliza kufa, Queequeg adandikumbatira, adakankhira mphumi yake motsutsana ndi yanga, ndikuwulutsa kuwala, tidagubuduza wina ndi mnzake, uku ndi uko, ndipo posakhalitsa tinali kugona.
