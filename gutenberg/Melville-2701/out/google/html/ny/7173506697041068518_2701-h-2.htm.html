MUTU 4. The Counterpane.

Nditadzuka m'mawa kutacha, ndinapeza mkono wa Queequeg utandigwera mwachikondi komanso mwachikondi. Unali pafupi kuganiza kuti ndinali mkazi wake. Chophimbacho chinali cha zigamba, zodzaza ndi mabwalo ang'onoang'ono amitundu yocheperako ndi makona atatu; ndipo dzanja lake lojambulidwa monsemo ndi labyrinth yosalekeza yaku Krete, palibe magawo awiri omwe anali a mthunzi wolondola-chifukwa ndikuganiza kuti adasunga mkono wake panyanja mopanda mthunzi padzuwa ndi mthunzi, malaya ake amapindika mosadukiza nthawi zosiyanasiyana - mkono womwewu, ndimati, adayang'ana chinsalu cha dziko lomwelo. Zowonadi, pang'onopang'ono atagona pa izo monga mkono unachitira pamene ine ndinadzuka koyamba, ine sindikanatha kuzidziwa izo kuchokera pa quilt, iwo kotero blended mitundu yawo palimodzi; ndipo zinali kokha chifukwa cha kulemera ndi kukakamizidwa kuti ine ndikhoza kudziwa kuti Queequeg anali kundikumbatira.

Zomverera zanga zinali zachilendo. Ndiloleni ndiyese kuwafotokozera. Pamene ndinali mwana, ndimakumbukira bwino lomwe mkhalidwe wofananawo umene unandichitikira; kaya zinali zenizeni kapena maloto, sindikanatha kukhazikika. Mkhalidwe unali uwu. Ine ndinali ndikudula kapezi wina kapena wina—ine ndikuganiza kunali kuyesera kukwawira pa chumuni, monga ine ndinawonapo ndikusesa pang’ono masiku angapo apitawo; ndi mayi anga ondipeza amene, mwanjira ina kapena ina, anali kundikwapula nthaŵi zonse, kapena kunditumiza kukagona kopanda chakudya chamadzulo, —amayi anandikokera ndi miyendo m’chumuni ndi kundinyamula kukagona, ngakhale kuti inali 2 koloko masana a June 21, tsiku lalitali kwambiri m’chaka cha dziko lathu lapansi. Ndinamva chisoni kwambiri. Koma panalibe chithandizo kaamba ka icho, chotero ndinakwera masitepe m’kachipinda kanga kakang’ono m’chipinda chachitatu, ndikudzivula mwapang’onopang’ono kuti ndiwononge nthaŵi, ndipo ndi kuusa moyo koŵaŵa ndinafika pakati pa zofundazo.

Ndinagona pamenepo mokhumudwa ndikuwerengera kuti maola khumi ndi asanu ndi limodzi athunthu adutse ndisanayembekezere chiukiriro. Maola khumi ndi asanu ndi limodzi pabedi! kamsana kanga kakang'ono kanandiwawa poganiza. Ndipo kunali kopepukanso kwambiri; dzuŵa likuŵala pa zenera, ndi phokoso lalikulu la makochi m’makwalala, ndi mkokomo wa mawu achiwerewere m’nyumba monse. Ndinadzimva kukhala woipitsitsa—potsirizira pake ndinadzuka, kuvala, ndi kutsika mwapang’onopang’ono m’mapazi anga onyamula masitonkeni, ndinafunafuna amayi anga ondipeza, ndipo mwadzidzidzi ndinadzigwetsera pamapazi awo, ndikuwachonderera monga chiyanjo chapadera kuti andipatse ine kuterera kwabwino chifukwa cha khalidwe langa loipa; chilichonse koma kundidzudzula kuti ndigone nthawi yayitali yosapirira. Koma anali mayi wabwino kwambiri komanso wosamala kwambiri za amayi opeza, ndipo pobwerera ndinayenera kupita kuchipinda changa. Kwa maola angapo ndinagona pamenepo, ndikudzimva kukhala woipitsitsa kuposa momwe ndakhala ndikuchitirapo kuyambira pamenepo, ngakhale chifukwa cha masoka aakulu amene ananditsatira. Pomaliza ndiyenera kuti ndinagwa m'maloto owopsa a kugona; ndi kudzuka pang'onopang'ono kuchokera m'malotowo - ndikulowa m'maloto - ndinatsegula maso anga, ndipo chipinda chomwe chinali ndi dzuwa tsopano chinali mdima wakunja. Nthawi yomweyo ndinamva kugwedezeka kumayenda mu chimango changa chonse; palibe chimene chinali kuonekera, ndipo palibe chimene chinamveka; koma dzanja lauzimu linkawoneka kuti layikidwa mwa ine. Dzanja langa lidapachikidwa pamtanda, ndipo wopanda dzina, wosayerekezeka, mawonekedwe opanda phokoso kapena phantom, yomwe dzanja lake linali, limawoneka ngati lili pafupi ndi bedi langa. Pakuti mibadwo yowoneka ngati yaunjikana mibadwo, ndinagona pamenepo, wowuma ndi mantha owopsa, osalimba mtima kuukoka dzanja langa; komabe ndimaganiza kuti ngati ndingathe kuyigwedeza inchi imodzi yokha, matsenga owopsa atha kusweka. Sindinadziwe momwe chidziwitsochi chidandithamangira; koma nditadzuka m'mawa, ndinakumbukira zonse monjenjemera, ndipo kwa masiku ndi masabata ndi miyezi pambuyo pake ndidadzitaya poyesa kufotokoza chinsinsicho. Ayi, mpaka ora lomweli, nthawi zambiri ndimadzidodometsa.

Tsopano, chotsani mantha owopsya, ndipo zomverera zanga pa kumverera dzanja lauzimu mwa ine zinali zofanana kwambiri, mwachilendo, ndi zomwe ndinakumana nazo pa kudzuka ndikuwona mkono wachikunja wa Queequeg utaponyedwa mozungulira ine. Koma pamapeto pake zonse zomwe zidachitika usiku wapitawu zidachitikanso mwachidwi, chimodzi ndi chimodzi, mwatsatanetsatane, kenako ndidagona wamoyo ku vuto loseketsa. Pakuti ngakhale ndidayesa kusuntha mkono wake, ndikutsegula chomangira chamkwati wake, komabe, akugona momwe adagonera, adandikumbatirabe mwamphamvu, ngati kuti palibe koma imfa iyenera kutilekanitsa. Tsopano ndinayesetsa kumudzutsa—“Queequeg!”—koma yankho lake lokha linali nkhonono. Kenako ndinagudubuzika, khosi langa likumva ngati lili mu kolala ya kavalo; ndipo mwadzidzidzi ndinamva kukanda pang'ono. Atataya pambali kansaluyo, pali kamwana kakang'ono kamene kanagona m'mbali mwa munthu wankhanzayo, ngati kuti ndi kamwana kankhope. Nkhaka wokongola, moona, ndinaganiza ine; abed pano m'nyumba yachilendo masana, ndi munthu wodya anthu komanso tomahawk! "Queequeg!—m'dzina la ubwino, Queequeg, dzukani! M'kupita kwa nthawi, chifukwa cha kugwedezeka kwakukulu, ndi kufotokoza mokweza ndi kosalekeza pa kusayenerera kwa iye kukumbatira mwamuna mnzanga mu chikhalidwe chaukwati umenewo, ndinakwanitsa kutulutsa kung'ung'udza; ndipo pakadali pano, adabweza mkono wake, adadzigwedeza yekha ngati galu wa Newfoundland atangotuluka m'madzi, ndipo adakhala pabedi, wowuma ngati ndodo ya pike, akundiyang'ana, ndikusisita maso ake ngati sanakumbukire momwe ndidakhalira komweko, ngakhale chidziwitso chochepa chodziwa china chake chokhudza ine chidawoneka pang'onopang'ono. Panthawiyi, ndidagona ndikuyang'ana mwakachetechete, osakayikira ngakhale pang'ono, ndikungoyang'ana mwachidwi cholengedwa chodabwitsa chotere. Pamene, potsirizira pake, malingaliro ake anawoneka ngati apangidwa kukhudza khalidwe la mnzake wa pabedi, ndipo iye anakhala, titero, kuyanjanitsidwa ndi chenichenicho; iye analumphira pansi, ndipo mwa zizindikiro zina ndi mawu zinandipatsa ine kumvetsa kuti, ngati izo zindikomera ine, iye akanayamba kuvala ndiyeno kundisiya ine kuvala pambuyo pake, kusiya nyumba yonse kwa ine ndekha. Ndikuganiza ine, Queequeg, pansi pazimenezi, ichi ndi chiwopsezo chotukuka kwambiri; koma zoona zake n’zakuti, anthu oipawa ali ndi maganizo obadwa nawo a kukoma, nenani zomwe mungafune; n’zodabwitsa kuti ali aulemu. Ndimalipira kuyamikira kwa Queequeg, chifukwa ankandichitira ulemu komanso kuganizira, pamene ndinali ndi mlandu wamwano waukulu; kumuyang’ana iye ali pakama, ndi kupenyerera mayendedwe ake onse achimbudzi; kwa nthawi yomwe chidwi changa chikufikira pakubereka kwanga. Komabe, munthu ngati Queequeg simumuona tsiku lililonse, iye ndi njira zake zinali zachilendo kwa iye.

Anayamba kuvala pamwamba ndi kuvala chipewa chake cha beaver, chachitali kwambiri, cham'mphepete mwake, kenako - kusiya mathalauza ake - adasaka nsapato zake. Zomwe adazichitira pansi pamiyamba, sindingadziwe, koma kuyenda kwake kotsatira kunali kudziphwanya yekha - nsapato m'manja, ndi chipewa - pansi pa kama; pamene, chifukwa cha chiwawa chambiri ndi kupsinjika maganizo, ndinaganiza kuti anali wolimbikira ntchito yodziwombera yekha; ngakhale kuti palibe lamulo loyenera lomwe ndidamvapo, munthu aliyense amafunikira kukhala payekha povala nsapato zake. Koma mukuona kuti Queequeg anali cholengedwa chimene chinasintha—osati mbozi kapena gulugufe. Iye anali wotukuka mokwanira kusonyeza kusadziŵika kwake m’makhalidwe odabwitsa kwambiri. Maphunziro ake anali asanamalize. Iye anali undergraduate. Akadapanda kukhala wotukuka pang'ono, mwina sakanadzivutitsa ndi nsapato konse; koma ndiye, akadapanda kukhala wankhanza, sakadalota kuti alowe pansi pa kama kuti avale. Pomalizira pake, adatulukira chipewa chake chili chopindika kwambiri ndikugwa pansi m'maso mwake, ndikuyamba kunjenjemera ndikudumphadumpha mchipindacho, ngati kuti sanazolowerane ndi nsapato, zikopa zake zonyowa, zokwinya - mwinanso sizinapangitsenso kuyitanitsa - m'malo mwake adamutsina ndikumuzunza m'mawa woyamba kuchokera kuzizira kowawa.

Powona, tsopano, kuti panalibe makatani pa zenera, ndi kuti msewu kukhala yopapatiza kwambiri, nyumba moyang'anizana analamula mbalambanda mu chipinda, ndi kuona mochulukira indecorous chithunzi kuti Queequeg anapanga, staving za ndi pang'ono china koma chipewa chake ndi nsapato pa; Ndinamupempha momwe ndingathere, kuti afulumizitse chimbudzi chake, makamaka kuti alowe m'mapantilo ake mwamsanga. Anamvera, kenako nkuyamba kusamba. Pa nthawiyo m’maŵa Mkristu aliyense akanasamba nkhope yake; koma Queequeg, modabwitsidwa kwanga, adakhutira ndi kuletsa zodzola zake pachifuwa, mikono, ndi manja. Kenako anavala chijasi chake, ndi kutenga sopo wolimba pa tebulo lapakati pa choyikapo chochapira, n’kuuviika m’madzi ndikuyamba kupukuta nkhope yake. Ndinkayang'ana kuti ndiwone kumene amasunga lumo lake, pamene taonani, akutenga chulu kuchokera pakona ya bedi, amachotsa matabwa aatali, amamasula mutu, amawombera pang'ono pa nsapato yake, ndikukwera mpaka pagalasi pakhoma, akuyamba kukwapula mwamphamvu, kapena m'malo mwake kugwedeza masaya ake. Ndikuganiza ine, Queequeg, ndikugwiritsa ntchito zodulira zabwino kwambiri za Rogers ndi kubwezera. Pambuyo pake ndinadzifunsa mocheperapo pa opareshoni imeneyi pamene ndinadziŵa za chitsulo chabwino chomwe mutu wa harpoon umapangidwa, ndi momwe mbali zowongoka zazitali zimasungidwira mwakuthwa kwambiri.

Posakhalitsa chimbudzi chake chinapezeka, ndipo monyadira anatuluka m’chipindacho, atadzikulunga mu jekete lake lalikulu la nyani, ndikuseŵera kapu yake ngati ndodo ya marshal.





MUTU 5. Chakudya cham'mawa.

Ndidatsata zomwezo mwachangu, ndikutsikira mchipinda chodyeramo ndidakumana ndi eni nyumbayo mosangalala kwambiri. Sindinkakonda kumuchitira nkhanza, ngakhale kuti anali atakhala ndi ine pang'ono pa nkhani ya mnzanga.

Komabe, kuseka kwabwino ndi chinthu chabwino kwambiri, komanso chosowa kwambiri; zambiri ndiye chisoni. Chifukwa chake, ngati munthu wina mwa iye yekha achita nthabwala yabwino kwa wina aliyense, asakhale wambuyo, koma alole mokondwera kuwononga ndi kuwonongedwa kutero. Ndipo munthu yemwe ali ndi chirichonse choseketsa kwambiri za iye, onetsetsani kuti muli zambiri mwa munthu ameneyo kuposa momwe inu mwina mukuganizira.

Chipinda chodyeramo chinali chodzaza ndi anthu ogona omwe anali atatsika usiku wapitawo, ndipo omwe ndinali ndisanawawone bwino. Iwo anali pafupifupi onse anamgumi; anzako akulu, ndi anzako achiwiri, ndi anzao achitatu, ndi akalipentala am’nyanja, ndi amisiri a panyanja, ndi osula zitsulo m’nyanja, ndi amisiri, ndi osunga zombo; kampani yabulauni ndi yakuda, yokhala ndi ndevu za bosky; osameta, osameta, onse atavala majekete a nyani a mikanjo yammawa.

Mutha kudziwa bwino lomwe nthawi yomwe aliyense adakhala kumtunda. Tsaya lathanzi la mnyamata uyu lili ngati peyala yowotchedwa ndi dzuwa, ndipo imaoneka ngati inunkhiza ngati musky; sangakhale masiku atatu atatsika kuchokera paulendo wake waku India. Munthu ameneyo pafupi naye amawoneka mithunzi yochepa yopepuka; mukhoza kunena kuti kukhudza kwa mtengo wa satin kuli mwa iye. Mu complexion wachitatu akadali kuzizira kotentha, koma pang'ono bleached withal; mosakayikira wakhala kumtunda masabata athunthu. Koma ndani angasonyeze tsaya ngati Queequeg? zomwe, zotchingidwa ndi tint tosiyanasiyana, zinkawoneka ngati malo otsetsereka a kumadzulo kwa mapiri a Andes, kuwonekera motsatizana, nyengo zosiyanasiyana, madera ndi madera.

"Grub, ho!" tsopano mwininyumbayo anafuula, akutsegula chitseko, ndipo tinalowa m’kadzutsa.

Iwo amanena kuti anthu amene aona dziko lapansi, motero amakhala omasuka m’makhalidwe awo, odzikhala okha pokhala nawo. Osati nthawi zonse, ngakhale: Ledyard, mlendo wamkulu wa New England, ndi Mungo Park, Scotch one; mwa anthu onse, anali ndi chitsimikizo chochepa m'chipindamo. Koma mwina kungowoloka ku Siberia pamwambo wokokedwa ndi agalu monga Ledyard anachitira, kapena kuyenda ulendo wautali wopanda kanthu, mu mtima wakuda wa Africa, womwe unali kuchuluka kwa machitidwe osauka a Mungo - ulendo woterewu, ndikunena, sungakhale njira yabwino kwambiri yopezera ulemu wapamwamba. Komabe, kwa mbali zambiri, chinthu choterocho chiyenera kupezeka kulikonse.

Kusinkhasinkha uku kumabwera chifukwa cha zomwe tidakhala tonse patebulo, ndipo ndinali kukonzekera kuti ndimve nkhani zabwino zokhudzana ndi nsomba; modabwitsa, pafupifupi mwamuna aliyense anakhala chete chete. Ndipo osati izo zokha, komanso iwo ankawoneka manyazi. Inde, apa panali gulu la agalu a m’nyanja, amene ambiri a iwo mosachita manyazi ngakhale pang’ono anakwera anamgumi aakulu m’nyanja zazikulu—anthu osawadziŵa kotheratu—ndikuwapha akufa osapenyerera; ndipo komabe, adakhala pagome la chakudya cham'mawa - onse a kuyitana kofanana, zokonda zapachibale - akuyang'anana wina ndi mzake ngati kuti anali asanawonekerepo pakati pa mapiri a Green. Kuwona mwachidwi; zimbalangondo zamanyazi izi, ankhondo amanyazi a whalemen!

Koma Queequeg - chifukwa, Queequeg anakhala pamenepo pakati pawo - pamutu pa tebulo, nayenso, izo mwangozi; ozizira ngati icicle. Kunena zoona sindingathe kunena zambiri za kuswana kwake. Wosilira wake wamkulu sakadalungamitsa mwachikondi kubweretsa nkhokwe yake m'mawa ndi iye, ndikugwiritsa ntchito pamenepo popanda mwambo; kufika pa gome ndi izo, kwa chiwopsezo chayandikira cha mitu yambiri, ndi kulimbana ndi ng'ombe steaks kwa iye. Koma izo ndithudi anachita bwino kwambiri ndi iye, ndipo aliyense akudziwa kuti mu kulingalira kwa anthu ambiri, kuchita chirichonse mwaulemu ndiko kuchita mwaulemu.

Sitilankhula za mawonekedwe a Queequeg pano; mmene ankapeŵa khofi ndi masikono otentha, ndi kuika chidwi chake chonse pa nyama ya beefsteak, zomwe anachita kawirikawiri. Zokwanira, kuti chakudya cham'mawa chitatha adachoka ngati ena onse mchipinda cha anthu onse, adayatsa chitoliro chake, ndipo adakhala chete akugaya ndikusuta atavala chipewa chake chosalekanitsidwa, pomwe ndidatuluka kukayenda.





MUTU 6. Msewu.

Ndikanadabwitsidwa poyamba kuwona munthu wachilendo ngati Queequeg akuzungulira pakati pa anthu aulemu a tauni yotukuka, kudabwa kumeneko kunasiya posakhalitsa ndikuyenda masana m'misewu ya New Bedford.

M'misewu yomwe ili pafupi ndi madoko, doko lililonse lalikulu limapereka nthawi zambiri kuti muwone zolemba zachilendo zochokera kumadera akunja. Ngakhale m'misewu ya Broadway ndi Chestnut, oyendetsa ngalawa aku Mediterranean nthawi zina amathamangitsa azimayi omwe ali ndi mantha. Regent Street sichidziwika kwa Lascars ndi Malay; ndipo ku Bombay, ku Apollo Green, a Yankee amakhala nthawi zambiri amawopsyeza mbadwa. Koma New Bedford imagonjetsa Water Street ndi Wapping. M'malo otchulidwa omalizawa mukuwona amalinyero okha; koma ku New Bedford, odya anthu enieni amaima kumacheza m’ngodya za misewu; zolusa kwathunthu; ambiri a iwo akali ndi mafupa a nyama yonyansa. Zimapangitsa mlendo kuyang'ana.

Koma, kuwonjezera pa a Feegeeans, Tongatobooarrs, Erromanggoans, Pannangian, ndi Brighggians, komanso, kuwonjezera pa zitsanzo zakuthengo zaumisiri wa whaling womwe sunamvedwe m'misewu, mudzawona zowoneka bwino kwambiri, zoseketsa kwambiri. M'tauniyi mumafika mlungu uliwonse amuna ambiri obiriwira a Vermonters ndi New Hampshire, omwe ali ndi ludzu lofuna kupindula ndi ulemerero mu usodzi. Ambiri ndi aang'ono, a mafelemu olimba; anthu amene agwetsa nkhalango, ndipo tsopano akufuna kugwetsa nkhwangwa ndi kuthyola namgumi. Ambiri ndi obiriwira ngati Mapiri Obiriwira kumene anachokera. Muzinthu zina mungaganize koma zatha maola ochepa. Taonani apo! mutu uja akungozungulira pakona. Amavala chipewa cha beaver ndi malaya amchira womeza, amamanga lamba wapamadzi ndi mpeni wa mchira. Apa pakubwera wina ali ndi sou'-wester ndi chovala cha bombazine.

Palibe dandy woberekedwa mtawuni yemwe angafanane ndi wowetedwa kudziko - ndikutanthauza munthu wamba - munthu yemwe, m'masiku agalu, amatchetcha maekala ake awiri m'magolovesi achikopa kuopa kupukuta manja ake. Tsopano dziko lakuda ngati ili likulowa m'mutu mwake kuti likhale lodziwika bwino, ndikulowa nawo msodzi waukulu wa whale, muyenera kuwona zinthu zoseketsa zomwe amachita atafika padoko. Polankhula chovala chake chapanyanja, akuyitanitsa mabatani a belu kumajasi ake; zomangirira ku thalauza lake. Ah, osauka Hay-Mbewu! adzasweka kowawa bwanji zingwezo pa chimphepo choyamba cholira, pamene muthamangitsidwa, zomangira, mabatani, ndi zonse, kukhosi kwa namondwe.

Koma musaganize kuti tauni yotchukayi ili ndi anthu odya nyama, odya nyama, ndi ma bumpkins kuti asonyeze alendo ake. Ayi konse. Komabe New Bedford ndi malo abwino kwambiri. Pakadapanda ife ansomba, malo amenewo mwina lero akanakhala ali mumkhalidwe wolira ngati gombe la Labrador. Zili choncho, mbali zina za dziko lake n'zokwanira kuopseza munthu, zimaoneka ngati zachiwembu. Tawuniyo mwina ndiye malo okondedwa kwambiri kukhalamo, ku New England konse. Ndilo dziko la mafuta, zoonadi: koma si monga Kanani; ndi dziko la tirigu ndi vinyo. Makwalala sayenda mkaka; kapena m'nyengo ya masika sawapaka mazira atsopano. Komabe, mosasamala kanthu za izi, palibe paliponse ku America konse komwe mungapeze nyumba zambiri zapatrician; mapaki ndi minda yokongola kwambiri, kuposa ku New Bedford. Anachokera kuti? zidabzalidwa bwanji pa dziko lomwe kale linali lonyowa kwambiri?

Pitani mukayang'ane pazingwe zophiphiritsa zachitsulo zozungulira nyumba yokwezeka kutaliko, ndipo funso lanu lidzayankhidwa. Inde; nyumba zolimba mtima zonsezi ndi minda yamaluwa yamaluwa zidachokera kunyanja za Atlantic, Pacific, ndi Indian. Mmodzi ndi onse, iwo anasonkhanitsidwa pamodzi ndi kukokedwa pamwamba pano kuchokera pansi pa nyanja. Kodi Herr Alexander angachite zinthu ngati izi?

Ku New Bedford, atate, amati, amapereka anamgumi kuti apereke ndalama kwa ana awo aakazi, ndikugawa adzukulu awo ndi ma porpoises pang'ono. Muyenera kupita ku New Bedford kuti mukawone ukwati wopambana; pakuti, amati, ali ndi mosungiramo mafuta m'nyumba iliyonse, ndipo usiku uliwonse mosasamala amawotcha utali wawo mu makandulo a spermaceti.

M'nyengo yachilimwe, tauniyi imakhala yokoma kuwona; zodzala ndi mapulo abwino—njira zazitali zobiriwira ndi golide. Ndipo mu Ogasiti, m'mwamba, mtedza wokongola komanso wochuluka wa mahatchi, wanzeru za candelabra, umapatsa anthu odutsa m'mphepete mwa milomo yawo yowongoka ya maluwa osonkhana. Choncho wamphamvuzonse ndi luso; amene m'zigawo zambiri za New Bedford adakulitsa maluwa owala kwambiri pamiyala yopanda kanthu yomwe idatayidwa pambali patsiku lomaliza la chilengedwe.

Ndipo akazi aku New Bedford, amaphuka ngati maluwa awo omwe ofiira. Koma maluwa amangophuka m’chilimwe; pamene kumera kwabwino kwa masaya awo kumakhala kosatha ngati kuwala kwa dzuwa m’miyamba yachisanu ndi chiwiri. Chifuwa chake chakuya chinagwedezeka ngati ndi kutupa pansi; manja ake oponyedwa adawoneka ngati zinthu zolimbana zikugwira ntchito; ndi mabingu amene anagudubuzika pamphumi pake, ndi kuunika kudaturuka m’diso lake, kunapangitsa akumva ake onse opepuka kuyang’ana pa iye ndi mantha ofulumira amene anali achilendo kwa iwo.

Ndipo kudadza bata m’maso mwake, pamene adatembenuza mwakachetechetenso masamba a Buku; ndipo, potsirizira pake, atayima osasunthika, ndi maso otsekedwa, kwa mphindi, ankawoneka akulankhulana ndi Mulungu ndi iyemwini.
