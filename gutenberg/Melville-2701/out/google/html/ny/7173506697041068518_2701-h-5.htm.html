MUTU 22. Khrisimasi yosangalatsa.

Pomaliza, chakumasana, pakuthamangitsidwa komaliza kwa oyendetsa sitimayo, ndipo Pequod atatulutsidwa m'bwalo, ndipo pambuyo poti Charity woganiza bwino adakwera m'bwato la namgumi, ndi mphatso yake yomaliza - chipewa chausiku cha Stubb, mnzake wachiwiri, mlamu wake, ndi Baibulo lopatula kwa onsewa, Captain ndi Peleg - natembenukira kwa mkulu wa mabwenzi, Pelege anati:

Ndinali kudzitonthoza ndekha, komabe, ndi ganizo lakuti mwa Bilidadi wopembedzayo angapezeke chipulumutso, ngakhale mazana asanu ndi awiri mphambu makumi asanu ndi awiri mphambu zisanu ndi ziwiri; pamene ndinamva phokoso lakuthwa mwadzidzidzi kumbuyo kwanga, ndikutembenuka, ndinachita mantha ndi maonekedwe a Captain Peleg pochotsa mwendo wake pafupi ndi ine. Uku kunali kumenya kwanga koyamba.

Mizere italiitali ya mano ya m’malingapo inkanyezimira ndi kuwala kwa mwezi; ndipo mofanana ndi minyanga yoyera ya njovu ina yaikulu, timizere tokhotakhota tinkadalira mautawo.

Lank Bildad, monga woyendetsa ndege, adatsogolera ulonda woyamba, ndipo nthawi zonse, pamene chombo chakale chinamira mu nyanja zobiriwira, ndikutumiza chisanu chonjenjemera ponseponse pa iye, ndipo mphepo inalira, ndipo chingwe chinamveka, zolemba zake zokhazikika zinamveka,

      “Minda yokoma kupitirira madzi osefukira,

Mawu okoma amenewo sanamveke mokoma kwa ine kuposa pamenepo. Iwo anali odzaza ndi chiyembekezo ndi zotsatira. Ngakhale kuti kunali kozizira kwambiri usiku wachisanu mu nyanja ya Atlantic, ngakhale kuti mapazi anga anyowa ndi jekete yonyowa, panalibe, ndiye zinkawoneka kwa ine, malo abwino osungira; ndi meads ndi glades kotero kwamuyaya vernal, kuti udzu wowomberedwa ndi kasupe, wosaponderezedwa, wosasunthika, umakhalabe pakati pa chirimwe.

Pomalizira pake tinapeza mwayi wotero, kuti oyendetsa ndege awiriwa sankafunikanso. Boti lamphamvu lomwe linatiperekeza linayamba kuyenda motsatira.

Zinali zosangalatsa komanso zosasangalatsa, momwe Pelegi ndi Bilidadi adakhudzidwira panthawiyi, makamaka Captain Bilidadi. Kuti kunyansidwa kuchoke, komabe; wonyansidwa kwambiri kuchoka, mwabwino, chombo choyenda ulendo wautali ndi wowopsa—kudutsa Cape onse amphepo; chombo chimene zikwi za madola ake anazipeza movutikira anaikamo; chombo, m'mene woyendetsa sitima wakale anayenda monga woyendetsa; munthu pafupifupi wachikulire monga iye, kamodzinso kuyamba kukumana ndi zoopsa zonse za nsagwada chifundo; kunyansidwa kutsazikana ndi chinthu chomwe chili chonse chopanda chidwi kwa iye, - Bilidadi wokalamba wosauka adachedwa; kukwera pamwamba ndi masitepe oda nkhawa; anathamangira mu kanyumba kukayankhula mawu ena otsanzikana kumeneko; anadzanso pamtunda, nayang’ana chakumphepo; anayang'ana ku madzi otakata ndi osatha, ongomangidwa ndi maiko akutali osawoneka a Kum'mawa; ndinayang'ana cha ku dziko; anayang'ana mmwamba; kuyang'ana kumanja ndi kumanzere; anayang'ana paliponse ndipo palibe; ndipo potsirizira pake, akumangirira chingwe pachipini chake, nagwira dzanja la Pelegi wolimba, ndikunyamula nyali, kwa kamphindi anayimirira akuyang'ana nkhope yake molimba mtima, mpaka kunena kuti, "Komabe, bwenzi la Pelegi, ndingathe kupirira; inde, ndingathe."

Ponena za Pelegi mwiniwakeyo, adazitenga ngati wanthanthi; koma pa filosofi yake yonse, misozi inali kuphethira m’diso lake, pamene nyaliyo inayandikira kwambiri. Ndipo iyenso, sanathamangire pang'ono kuchokera panyumba kupita kumtunda - tsopano mawu pansipa, ndipo tsopano mawu ndi Starbuck, mnzake wamkulu.

Koma, potsirizira pake, anatembenukira kwa mnzake, ndi maonekedwe omalizira ponena za iye, “Kaputeni Bilidadi—bwerani, woyendetsa sitima wakale, tiyenera kupita. Kubwerera ku bwalo lalikulu kumeneko! Botolo—tsazikanani ndi zabwino zonse kwa nonse—ndipo lero kwa zaka zitatu ndidzakudyerani chakudya chamadzulo chotentha ku Nantucket yakale.”

“Mulungu akudalitseni inu, ndipo akhale nanu m’kusunga Kwake kopatulika, amuna,” anang’ung’udza Bilidadi wokalamba, pafupifupi mosagwirizana. "Ndikuyembekeza kuti mudzakhala ndi nyengo yabwino tsopano, kotero kuti Kaputeni Ahabu posachedwapa akuyenda pakati panu-dzuwa lokoma ndilo zonse zomwe amafunikira, ndipo mudzakhala nawo ambiri paulendo wotentha womwe mukupita. Samalani posaka, okwatirana. “Bwera, bwera, Kaputeni Bilidadi; siya kuyendayenda,—chokapo!” ndipo pamenepo, Pelegi anafulumira kuoloka m’mbali, nagwa onse awiri m’ngalawa.

Sitima ndi ngalawa zinapatukana; kunkawomba mphepo yausiku yozizira, yonyowa; mbalame yofuula inawulukira pamwamba pake; mitundu yambiri yamitundu yosiyanasiyana; tinasangalala kwambiri, ndipo mwakhungu tinagwera m’nyanja ya Atlantic yokhayo.





MUTU 23. The Lee Shore.

Mitu ina mmbuyo, m'modzi wa Bulkington adanenedwa, woyenda panyanja wamtali, yemwe adakumana naye ku New Bedford kunyumba ya alendo.

Pausiku wanyengo yozizira uja, a Pequod adaponya mauta ake obwezera m'mafunde ozizira oyipa, ndiyenera kumuwona ndani atayima pachitsogozo chake koma Bulkington! Ndinayang'ana mwachifundo ndi mwamantha pa mwamunayo, yemwe mkatikati mwa nyengo yachisanu wangotsika kumene kuchokera paulendo wowopsa wazaka zinayi, adatha kukankhiranso mopanda mpumulo kwa nthawi inanso yamkuntho. Dzikolo linkawoneka ngati likutentha pamapazi ake. Zinthu zodabwitsa kwambiri zomwe sizingatchulidwe; zokumbukira zozama sizipereka ma epitaphs; mutu uwu wa inchi zisanu ndi chimodzi ndi manda opanda mwala a Bulkington. Ndiloleni ndingonena kuti zinamuyendera bwino ngati ngalawa yokankhidwa ndi namondwe, yomwe imayenda momvetsa chisoni kumtunda wa leeward. Doko likalephera kupereka chithandizo; doko ndi lomvetsa chisoni; padoko pali chitetezo, chitonthozo, mwala wamoto, chakudya chamadzulo, zofunda zofunda, abwenzi, zonse zomwe zili zachifundo ku imfa zathu. Koma mu chiwopsezo chimenecho, doko, pamtunda, ndi pachiwopsezo chambiri cha ngalawayo; ayenera kukhala wochereza alendo; Kukhudza kumodzi kwa nthaka, ngakhale kumadyetsera msipu, kumamupangitsa kunjenjemera uku ndi uku. Ndi mphamvu zake zonse amaunjikira onse akuyenda panyanja; potero, amalimbana 'molimbana ndi mphepo zomwezo zomwe zimakanika kumuwomba kupita kwawo; amafunafuna kusowa kwa nthaka kwa nyanja yonse; chifukwa cha kuthawirako, kuthamangira m'mavuto; bwenzi lake lokhalo mdani wake wowawa kwambiri!

Kodi mukudziwa tsopano, Bulkington? Kungoyang'ana kumene mukuwoneka kuti mukuwona za chowonadi chosaloleka; kuti kulingalira konse kozama, kowona ndi kuyesayesa kopanda mantha kwa moyo kusunga ufulu womasuka wa nyanja yake; pamene mphepo zakuthengo za kumwamba ndi dziko lapansi zipangana zomponya pa gombe lonyenga, laukapolo?

Koma monga momwe kusowa malo kokha kumakhala chowonadi chapamwamba kwambiri, chopanda malire, chopanda malire ngati Mulungu—kotero, kuli kwabwino kufa mu kufuula kosatha kumeneko, kusiyana ndi kuphwanyidwa mopanda ulemu, ngakhale kukanakhala chitetezo! Kwa ngati nyongolotsi, ndiye, o! amene akanakwawa kumtunda! Zowopsa zowopsa! Kodi ululu wonsewu ndi wopanda pake? Limbani mtima, limbikani mtima, O Bulkington! Pitirizani inu momvetsa chisoni, mulungu! M'mwamba kuchokera kumadzi akuwonongeka kwa nyanja-molunjika mmwamba, kudumpha apotheosis!





MUTU 24. Woyimira mlandu.

Monga Queequeg ndi ine tsopano mwachilungamo anayamba bizinesi ya whaling; ndipo popeza kuti ntchito yoweta anamgumi imeneyi mwa njira inayake yafikira pa kuwonedwa pakati pa anthu a m’dziko monga chinthu chosakambitsirana ndi chonyozeka; chifukwa chake, ndili ndi nkhawa zonse kuti ndikutsimikizireni, inu anthu akumtunda, za kusalungama komwe kuchitiridwa kwa ife asaka nyama.

Poyamba, zikhoza kuonedwa ngati zosafunikira kutsimikizira mfundo yakuti, pakati pa anthu ambiri, malonda a nsomba samawerengedwa pamlingo ndi zomwe zimatchedwa ntchito za ufulu. Ngati mlendo atadziwitsidwa mumtundu wina uliwonse wosiyana, zingapititse patsogolo pang'ono malingaliro ake onse, ngati akanaperekedwa kwa kampaniyo ngati wotsutsa, nenani; ndipo ngati motsanzira asitikali apanyanja ayenera kuwonjezera zilembo zoyamba za SWF (Sperm Whale Fishery) ku khadi yake yochezera, njira yoteroyo ingaonedwe ngati yongopeka komanso yopusa.

Mosakayikira chifukwa chimodzi chachikulu chimene dziko likulepherera kupereka ulemu kwa anangumi, n’chakuti iwo amaganiza kuti ntchito yathu ndi ya mtundu wa bizinezi yophera nyama; ndi kuti tikachita khama m’menemo, timazingidwa ndi zodetsa zamtundu uliwonse. Ndife opha nyama, izo nzoona. Koma ogula nyama, nawonso, ndi opha nyama omwe ali ndi baji yokhetsa magazi kwambiri onse akhala ankhondo ankhondo omwe dziko lapansi limakondwera nthawi zonse kuwalemekeza. Ndipo ponena za nkhani ya kuipitsidwa kwa bizinesi yathu, posachedwa mudzayambitsidwira kuzinthu zina zomwe sizidziwika bwino mpaka pano, zomwe, ponseponse, zidzabzala zombo za sperm whale mwachipambano pakati pa zinthu zoyera kwambiri padziko lapansi pano. Koma ngakhale kuvomereza mlanduwo kukhala woona; Kodi ndi malo oterera otere amtundu wanji omwe angafanane ndi zofa zosaneneka za m'mabwalo omenyera nkhondo omwe asitikali ambiri amabwerera kudzamwa mwachisangalalo cha azimayi onse? Ndipo ngati lingaliro la ngozi kwambiri limakulitsa kudzitukumula kotchuka kwa ntchito ya msilikali; ndiloleni ndikutsimikizireni kuti omenyera nkhondo ambiri omwe adakwera momasuka kupita ku batri, amatha kubweza msanga mchira wawukulu wa sperm whale, ndikuwuluka mumlengalenga pamutu pake. Pakuti zoopsa zomvetsa chisoni za anthu n’zotani poziyerekezera ndi zoopsa ndi zodabwitsa za Mulungu!

Koma, ngakhale dziko litiyang'ana ife osaka anamgumi, komabe mosadziŵa ilo limatipatsa ife ulemu wozama; inde, kupembedza kochuluka! pakuti pafupifupi zotchinga zonse, nyali, ndi makandulo amene amayaka padziko lonse lapansi, amayaka, monga momwe zimachitira tiakachisi ambiri, ku ulemerero wathu!

Koma yang'anani pa nkhaniyi m'zinthu zina; muyeseni m’miyeso yamitundumitundu; onani zomwe ife ansomba tiri, ndipo takhala tiri.

N’chifukwa chiyani anthu a ku Dutch m’nthawi ya De Witt anali ndi asilikali oyendetsa zombo zawo zopha anamgumi? Chifukwa chiyani Louis XVI. wa ku France, ndi ndalama zake zake, analinganiza zombo zopha nsomba zochokera ku Dunkirk, ndikuyitanitsa mwaulemu ku tauniyo mabanja angapo kapena awiri ochokera pachilumba chathu cha Nantucket? Kodi nchifukwa ninji dziko la Britain pakati pa zaka za 1750 ndi 1788 linapereka ndalama zopitirira £1,000,000 kwa asodzi ake? Ndipo potsiriza, zinatheka bwanji kuti ife ansomba a ku Amereka tsopano tikuchuluka kuposa ena onse ankhondo ogwidwa m’manja padziko lapansi; yenda zombo zokwera zombo mazana asanu ndi awiri; okhala ndi amuna zikwi khumi ndi zisanu ndi zitatu; chaka chilichonse kudya madola 4,000,000; zombo zamtengo wapatali, pa nthawi yoyenda, $20,000,000! ndipo chaka chilichonse kuitanitsa ku madoko athu zokolola zabwino za $7,000,000. Kodi zonsezi zimatheka bwanji, ngati palibe chovuta pakuwopa nsomba?

Koma ili si theka; yang'ananinso.

Ndikunena momasuka, kuti wafilosofi wa cosmopolite, pa moyo wake, sangasonyeze chikoka chimodzi chamtendere, chomwe mkati mwa zaka makumi asanu ndi limodzi zapitazi chakhala chikugwira ntchito kwambiri padziko lonse lapansi, chotengedwa pamodzi, kusiyana ndi malonda apamwamba komanso amphamvu a nsomba. Mwanjira ina ndi inzake, wabala zochitika zochititsa chidwi kwambiri mwa iwo okha, ndipo ndi zofunika kwambiri mosalekeza m’nkhani zawo zotsatizanatsatizana, kotero kuti kupha anamgumi kungaonedwe ngati mayi wa ku Aigupto uja, amene anabala ana okhawo oyembekezera kuchokera m’mimba mwake. Ingakhale ntchito yopanda chiyembekezo, yosatha kutchula zinthu zonsezi. Lolani dzanja lokwanira. Kwa zaka zambiri, sitima ya anangumi yakhala ikuyendetsa madera akutali kwambiri padziko lapansi. Adasanthula nyanja ndi zisumbu zomwe zinalibe tchati, pomwe palibe Cook kapena Vancouver adayendapo. Ngati amuna ankhondo aku America ndi a ku Europe tsopano akukwera mwamtendere m'madoko amodzi owopsa, aloleni iwo apereke moni ku ulemu ndi ulemerero wa ngalawa ya whale, yomwe poyamba idawawonetsa njira, ndipo idatanthauziridwa koyamba pakati pawo ndi achifwamba. Akhoza kukondwerera monga momwe angachitire ngwazi za Kufufuza Maulendo, Ma Cooks anu, ma Krusenstern anu; koma ndikunena kuti Akaputeni ambiri osadziwika adachoka ku Nantucket, zomwe zinali zazikulu, komanso zazikulu kuposa Cook wanu ndi Krusenstern wanu. Pakuti m'zopanda kanthu zawo zopanda kanthu, iwo, m'madzi ogwedezeka achikunja, ndi m'mphepete mwa nyanja za zisumbu zosawerengeka, zamkuntho, adamenyana ndi zodabwitsa za namwali ndi zoopsa zomwe Cook ndi amadzi ake onse ndi muskets sakanatha kulimba mtima. Zonse zomwe zimapangidwira bwino mu maulendo akale a ku South Sea, zinthuzo zinali chabe malo wamba a Nantucketers athu otchuka. Nthawi zambiri, zochitika zomwe Vancouver amapatulira mitu itatu, amuna awa amawona kuti ndi osayenera kuyikidwa mu chipika wamba wa sitimayo. Ah, dziko! O, dziko!

Mpaka pomwe usodzi wa anamgumi unafika ku Cape Horn, palibe malonda koma atsamunda, ngakhale kugonana kulikonse koma atsamunda, kunkachitika pakati pa Ulaya ndi mzere wautali wa zigawo zotchuka za ku Spain pamphepete mwa nyanja ya Pacific. Anali whaleman amene anayamba kuswa ndondomeko yansanje ya korona wa ku Spain, kukhudza madera amenewo; ndipo, ngati danga liloledwa, zikhoza kuwonetsedwa momveka bwino momwe kuchokera ku nsombazi pamapeto pake zinachititsa kuti dziko la Peru, Chili, ndi Bolivia limasulidwe ku goli la Old Spain, ndi kukhazikitsidwa kwa demokalase yamuyaya m'madera amenewo.

Amereka wamkulu uja ku mbali ina ya chigawocho, Australia, anapatsidwa kwa dziko lowunikiridwa ndi whaleman. Atapezedwa koyamba ndi munthu wina wachidatchi mosadziwa, zombo zina zonse zinazemba kwanthaŵi yaitali magombe amenewo monga owopsa kwambiri; koma ngalawa ya namgumi inakhudza pamenepo. Chombo cha nangumi ndi mayi weniweni wa gulu lamphamvu lomwe tsopano lino. Komanso, atangoyamba kumene kukakhala ku Australia, osamukira kumayiko ena anapulumutsidwa kangapo ku njala ndi masikono abwino a sitima ya nangumi mwamwayi kuponya nangula m'madzi awo. Zisumbu zosawerengeka za Polynesia yonse zimavomereza chowonadi chofanana, ndikuchita ulemu wamalonda ku sitima ya anamgumi, imene inatsegula njira ya amishonale ndi amalonda, ndipo m’zochitika zambiri ananyamula amishonale akale kupita kumalo awo oyamba. Ngati dziko la Japan lokhala ndi mipiringidzo iwiri lidzakhala lochereza alendo, ndiye kuti kutamandidwa kudzakhala koyenera kwa sitima ya anangumi yokha; pakuti ali kale pakhomo.

Koma ngati, pamaso pa zonsezi, mukunenabe kuti nsonga za nsomba zilibe mayanjano olemekezeka ogwirizana nawo, ndiye kuti ndine wokonzeka kugwedeza mikondo makumi asanu ndi inu kumeneko, ndikukumasulani ndi chisoti chogawanika nthawi zonse.

MUTU 28. Ahabu.

Kwa masiku angapo atachoka ku Nantucket, palibe chomwe chidawoneka cha Captain Ahabu. Okwatiranawo nthawi zonse ankatonthozana wina ndi mzake pa ulonda, ndipo pa chilichonse chomwe chikanawoneka chosiyana, iwo ankawoneka ngati olamulira okha a ngalawayo; koma nthawi zina amatuluka m'nyumbamo ndi kulamula modzidzimutsa komanso modzidzimutsa, kuti pambuyo pake zidali zomveka koma adalamulira mwamphamvu. Inde, mbuye wawo wamkulu ndi wolamulira wankhanza anali komweko, ngakhale mpaka pano sanawonedwe ndi maso aliwonse osaloledwa kulowa mumsasa wopatulika wa kanyumbako.

Nthawi zonse ndikakwera pamawotchi anga pansi, nthawi yomweyo ndimayang'ana m'mwamba kuti ndizindikire ngati pali nkhope yachilendo yomwe imawoneka; chifukwa chakusautsika kwanga koyamba kosamveka kukhudza kapitawo wosadziwika, tsopano m'mphepete mwa nyanja, kunakhala pafupifupi kusokoneza. Izi zidakulitsidwa modabwitsa nthawi zina chifukwa cha kusakhazikika kwaudyerekezi kwa Eliya mosaitanidwa mobwerezabwereza kwa ine, ndi mphamvu zobisika zomwe sindikanatha kuziganizira. Koma sindikanatha kupirira nazo, monga momwe ndinalili m'mikhalidwe ina ndidatsala pang'ono kumwetulira chifukwa cha zomwe mneneri wodabwitsa uja wapamadzi. Koma kaya kunali kuopa kapena kusamasuka—kuchitcha chomwecho—chimene ndinachimva, komabe pamene ndinabwera kudzayang’ana za ine m’sitimayo, zinkawoneka mopanda mphamvu konse kuyamikira malingaliro oterowo. Chifukwa ngakhale ma harpooneers, ndi gulu lalikulu la ogwira nawo ntchito, anali ankhanza kwambiri, achikunja, komanso amtundu wowoneka bwino kuposa makampani onse ochita zamalonda omwe adandidziwitsa, komabe ndidanena izi - ndikuzinena moyenerera - kupadera kowopsa kwa chikhalidwe cha kuthengo chaku Scandinavia komwe ndidasiya. Koma makamaka chinali mbali ya akuluakulu atatu a sitima yapamadzi, okwatirana, omwe anawerengeredwa mokakamiza kwambiri kuti athetse kukayikira kopanda mtundu kumeneku, ndi kuchititsa chidaliro ndi chisangalalo m'chiwonetsero chilichonse cha ulendo. Atatu abwinoko, othekera kwambiri ogwira ntchito panyanja ndi amuna, aliyense mwa njira yakeyake, sakanakhoza kupezeka mosavuta, ndipo iwo anali aliyense wa iwo Achimereka; munthu wa ku Nantucketer, wa mpesa, munthu wa ku Cape. Tsopano, inali Khrisimasi pamene ngalawa inawombera kuchokera ku doko lake, kwa danga ife tinali ndi nyengo yowawa ya Polar, ngakhale nthawi zonse kuthawa kuchokera kumeneko kupita kumwera; ndipo mwa mlingo uliwonse ndi mphindi ya latitude yomwe tinayenda panyanja, pang'onopang'ono kusiya nyengo yozizira yopanda chifundo ija, ndi nyengo yake yonse yosapiririka kumbuyo kwathu. Inali imodzi mwazotsika pang'ono, komabe imvi komanso yachisoni m'mawa wa kusinthako, pomwe ndi mphepo yamkuntho sitimayo inkathamanga m'madzi ndikudumpha mwachangu komanso mwachangu, kuti nditakwera pamwamba pa kuyitanira kwa ulonda wam'tsogolo, nditangoyang'ana molunjika kumtunda wa taffrail. Zowona zimaposa mantha; Kapitao Ahabu anaima pa bwalo lake.

Pankawoneka kuti palibe chizindikiro cha matenda wamba okhudza iye, kapena kuchira kwa aliyense. Ankawoneka ngati munthu wodulidwa pamtengo, pamene moto wawononga kwambiri ziwalo zonse popanda kuzipsereza, kapena kuchotsa kachinthu kamodzi pa kulimba kwake kokalamba. Maonekedwe ake onse apamwamba, otakata, ankawoneka ngati opangidwa ndi mkuwa wolimba, ndipo amapangidwa mu nkhungu yosasinthika, monga Cellini's cast Perseus. Kutuluka pakati pa imvi zake, ndikupitirira mbali imodzi ya nkhope yake yopyapyala ndi khosi, mpaka itasowa m'zovala zake, mudawona chizindikiro chowonda ngati ndodo, choyera moyera. Zinali ngati msoko wa perpendicular nthawi zina umapangidwa mumtengo wowongoka, wokwezeka wamtengo waukulu, pomwe mphezi yakumtunda imagwetsera pansi, ndipo popanda kugwetsa nthambi imodzi, imasenda ndi kutulutsa khungwa kuchokera pamwamba mpaka pansi, ndikuthamangira m'nthaka, ndikusiya mtengowo udakali wamoyo, koma wodziwika. Kaya chizindikirocho chinabadwa naye, kapena ngati chinali chilonda chotsala ndi bala, palibe amene akanatha kunena. Mwa kuvomerezana mwakachetechete, m’ulendo wonsewo panalibe zonena za izo, makamaka kwa okwatirana. Koma mkulu wina wa Tashtego, Mmwenye wokalamba wa Gay-Head pakati pa gulu la ogwira ntchito, ananena mwachikhulupiriro kuti Ahabu sanadziwike motere mpaka atakwanitsa zaka makumi anayi, ndipo zinamugwera, osati chifukwa cha nkhondo iliyonse, koma mkangano wapanyanja. Komabe, malingaliro akuthengowa adawoneka ngati osagwirizana, ndi zomwe Manxman imvi adanenera, bambo wakale wakumanda, yemwe anali asanatulukepo kuchokera ku Nantucket, anali asanamuwonepo Ahabu wamtchire. Komabe, miyambo yakale yapanyanja, zokhulupirira zakale, zidatchuka kwambiri ndi Manxman wakale uyu ndi mphamvu zakuzindikira. Kotero kuti palibe woyendetsa ngalawa woyera amene anamutsutsa kwambiri pamene ananena kuti ngati Kaputeni Ahabu atayikidwa mwabata - zomwe sizingachitike, kotero iye anadandaula - ndiye kuti, aliyense amene angachite udindo wotsiriza wa akufa, adzapeza chizindikiro cha kubadwa kwa iye kuchokera ku korona mpaka kumutu.

Zoyipa zonse za Ahabu zidandikhudza mwamphamvu, komanso mtundu wowoneka bwino womwe udawulula, kotero kuti kwakanthawi kochepa sindidazindikire kuti kukhumudwa kwakukuluku kudachitika chifukwa cha mwendo woyera wankhanza womwe adayimilirapo. Ndinali nditazindikira kale kuti mwendo wa mnyanga wa njovu umenewu uli panyanja unapangidwa kuchokera ku fupa lopukutidwa la nsagwada za namgumi. “Inde, anaphwanyidwa ku Japan,” anatero Indian Gay-Head wakale kamodzi; "koma mofanana ndi chombo chake chophwanyidwa, anatumiza mlongoti wina osabwera kunyumba kuti adzautenge.

Ndinachita chidwi ndi kaimidwe kake komwe iye anakhalabe. Pambali iliyonse ya doko la Pequod, ndipo pafupi ndi mizzen shrouds, panali dzenje, lobowoleredwa pafupifupi theka la inchi kapena apo, mu thabwa. Mwendo wake wa fupa unakhazikika mu dzenjelo; mkono umodzi wokwezeka, ndi wogwirizira ndi nsaru; Kaputeni Ahabu anaima chilili, akuyang'ana kutsogolo kwa ngalawayo. Panali kulimba kolimba kopanda malire, kufuna kotsimikizika, kosagonja, pakudzipereka kokhazikika ndi kopanda mantha, kutsogolo kwa kuyang'ana kumeneko. Osati mawu omwe iye analankhula; kapena anyamata ake sananena kanthu kwa iye; ngakhale ndi manja awo ang'onoang'ono ndi mawu awo, adawonetsa momveka bwino kusakhazikika, kapena kuti sikunali kowawa, kuzindikira kukhala pansi pamavuto. Ndipo osati izo zokha, koma Ahabu wosweka mtima anaima pamaso pawo ndi kupachikidwa pa nkhope yake; mu ulamuliro wonse wopanda dzina wolemetsa ulemu wa tsoka lalikulu.

Pasanapite nthawi yaitali, kuyambira ulendo wake woyamba mumlengalenga, adachoka m'nyumba yake. Koma pambuyo pa m’maŵa umenewo, tsiku lililonse anali kuonekera kwa ogwira ntchito; kaya atayima mu dzenje lake, kapena atakhala pa chopondapo cha minyanga ya njovu chimene iye anali nacho; kapena kukwera pamwamba kwambiri. Pamene thambo linayamba kuchepa; ndithudi, anayamba kukula pang'ono genial, iye anakhalabe pang'onopang'ono wodzipatula; monga ngati ngalawayo idachoka kunyumba, palibe kanthu koma mdima wandiweyani wa nyanja womwe unamutsekereza. Ndipo kunali, kuti iye anali pafupifupi mowirikiza mu mlengalenga; koma, pakali pano, pa zonse zomwe adanena, kapena zomwe adachita momveka bwino, pamtunda womaliza wadzuwa, adawoneka ngati wosafunikira pamenepo ngati mlongoti wina. Koma Pequod anali kungopanga ndime tsopano; osati kuyenda pafupipafupi; pafupifupi kukonzekera nsomba zonse zofunika kuyang'anira okwatirana anali mokwanira woyenera, kotero kuti panalibe pang'ono kapena kanthu, mwa iye yekha, ntchito kapena kusangalala Ahabu, tsopano; ndipo motero, kuthamangitsa, kwa mphindi imodzi yokha, mitambo yomwe ili pamwamba pake inaunjika pamphumi pake, pamene nthawi zonse mitambo yonse imasankha nsonga zazitali kwambiri kuti ziwunjikepo.

Komabe, patangopita nthaŵi yaitali, kukopa kosangalatsa, kokangana kwa nyengo yosangalatsa ya tchuthi imene tinafikako, kunkaoneka kuti kumam'sangalatsa pang'onopang'ono chifukwa cha mmene ankamvera. Pakuti, monga pamene atsikana ofiira, akuvina, April ndi May, amapita kwawo ku nkhalango zozizira, zosautsa; ngakhale mtengo wobiriwira wobiriwira, wobiriwira kwambiri, wonyezimira kwambiri udzatulutsa mphukira zingapo zobiriwira, kuti zilandire alendo amitima yokondwa otere; kotero Ahabu anachita, pamapeto pake, kuyankha pang'ono ku zokopa zamasewera a atsikanawo. Koposa kamodzi adatulutsa duwa lofowoka la mawonekedwe, lomwe, mwa munthu wina aliyense, posakhalitsa likanatulutsa maluwa ndikumwetulira.
