Nupwen a aporausa ekkei mettoch, iteitan fansoun Queequeg a angei ewe tomahawk seniei, a suki ewe pekin hatchet won mokuren ewe chon annut.

“Met ena ren, Kuwe?”

“Peri mecheres, nieno-e; oh!peri mecheres!”

A sopweno ren ekkoch achechemeni mi ngaw usun an we tomahawk-pipe, minne, a usun ita, a fen non an kewe ruu aea me a ekieki chon koum ngeni me a asoso ngunun, nupwen aua chok fiti ewe rigger mi annut. Ewe pwichikar mi pochokun iei a unusen aurano ewe chuk mi kon, a poputa ne apasa won i. A ngunungun ren eu sokkun ngunungun; iwe a usun ita osukosuk non ewe noun; mwirin a rokopwanieno fan ew ika fan ruu; mwirin a mwot me a suki mesan kewe.

“Olo!” a asoso non soponon, “ion ami chon sakaw?”

Ua pölüweni, “Ekkewe mwän mi fiti sepelin, inet epwe sai?”

“Ewer, ewer, oupwe tonong non ewe wa, ika met? A sai ikenai. Ewe Kapten a feito won ewe wa nesosor.”

“Met Kapten?—Ahap?”

“Iö esap i wesewesen?”

Ua mochen eisini i ekkoch kapas eis usun Ahab, nupwen aua rongorong ew mongungu won deck.

Ewe chon atake a apasa, “Kilisou! A astir an Starbuck.” “I emon chienach mi manaman, ena; mwan mi murinno, me emon chon fel; nge meinisin mi manau iei, upwe siwin ngeni.” Iwe a apasa iei usun a feino won ewe deck, me am aua tapweno mwirin.

Iei a ffat pwe ewe akkar a tuputiu. Ekiselo chok ekkewe chon angang ra feito won ewe wa lon ruu me ulumon; ekkewe chon rigger ra pwisin fitikoko; ekkewe pupulu ra achocho le fiti; me ekkoch ekkewe aramasen ewe sat ra angang weires ne uwei sokopaten pisekin soponon won ewe wa. Non ena fansoun Kapten Ahap a chok nomw non an we imw ese tongeni kuna.
