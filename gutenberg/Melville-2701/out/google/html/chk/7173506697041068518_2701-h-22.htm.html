SOPUN 115. Ewe Pequod a Chuuri Ewe Mwan.

Iwe, a fokkun apwapwa ekkewe mettoch ra kuna me ekkewe mongungu ra feito me mwen ewe asepwal, fitu wiik mwirin an Ahap we harpoon a weld.

Nupwen ei sipw mi pwapwa ren feioch a tori ewe Pequod mi mokutukut, ewe mongungun barbarian ren ekkewe watten drum a feito seni an we forata; me a chuen arap ngeni, ew mwichen noun kewe mwan ra kuna pwe ra uta unukun an kewe watten try-pot, minne, ra uren ewe poke mi usun parchment ika inisin ewe ik mi pwech, a fori ew mongungu mi pochokun ngeni iteiten stroke ren poun ekkewe chon angang mi fitikoko. Lon ewe leenien äppirü, ekkewe chon fiti ewe fönüfan me ekkewe chon fiti ewe föün äsefich ra fiti ekkewe nengngin mi üföüf üf mi lingöch mi fitiir le sü seni ekkewe fönüen Polynesia; atun ra chék chéúfetál lón eú wa mi ling, mi péchékkúl lefilen ewe chénúpupu me ewe chénúpupu mi lap, úlúmén chón Long Island, mi wor ar kewe chénúpupu mi lingéch mi ffér seni únúchen man mi ling, ra wisen emmweni ewe jig mi fókkun apwapwa. Lon ena atun, ekkoch chon ewe mwichen sipw ra fitikoko ren ewe angangen forata ekkewe try-works, ikewe ekkewe watten sepi ra fen katowu seni ie. Kopwe fen ekieki pwe ra kan ataielo ewe Bastille mi anumamau, ekkena sokkun puchor mi fokkun ngau ra efisata, lupwen ekkewe brick me mortar rese chuen wor lamoter iei ra aturalong lon ewe matau.

Samon me masta won ei neni meinisin, ewe kaptin a uta wenechar won ewe nenien sipw mi tekia, pwe unusen ewe drama mi pwapwa a uren me mwen mesan, me a usun ita a chok foruta fan iten pwisin an emon me emon epwe apwapwa.

Iwe Ahap, i a pwan uta won an we kukkunun puun, a uren uren uren uren uren uren, ren ew rochopwak mi pochokun; me nupwen ekkewe ruu sipw ra pwerelong non ar kewe ran —ew meinisin pwapwa ren mettoch ra wesino, ewe emon meinisin esisin usun mettoch repwe feito —nour kewe ruemon kaptin non pwisin ir ra appiru unusen ewe sokofesen mi amwarar non ewe neni.

“Kopwe feito won ewe wa, kopwe feito won ewe wa!” a puchor ewe chon nemeni Bachelor mi gay, a angei ew kap me ew puun non ewe asepwan.

“Ka fen kuna ewe Waimw mi pwechepwech?” a ngungures Ahap ren an polueni.

Iwe kopwe feino won om kewe aan, me ngang upwe ai. Feino ikewe! Kopwe sai meinisin, me tumunu i ngeni ewe asepwan!"

SOPUN 116. Ewe Waimw mi Malo.

Ese kan fis non ei manau, nupwen, non ewe pekin pung, ekkewe chon fortune ra sani ra sai arap ngeni kich, kich, ina mwo ika meinisin ra turutiw me mwen, sia angei ekis ewe asepwan mi pwichikar, me pwapwa mefi ach kewe sai mi bagging ra uren. Namotongaw, oh whale, ka kutta iotek ren ewe akkar mi amanaua meinisin, ewe a chok kori manau, nge kosap ngeniei sefan, ka awora sefan. ew nuku mi rochopwak meinisin om kewe imminglings rese tongeni iteni ra fitikoko fan ikei, ua uren ngunungun ren ekkewe mettoch mi manau me nom, ra tou usun asepwan, nge konik iei;

SOPUN 117. Ewe Mwichen Souneng.

Ekkewe fomon whale mi ninnino non ena pwinin ra fen mano non ew neni mi towaw; emon, towaw ngeni ewe asepwan; eu, ese kon towau, ngeni lesen; emon me mwen; emon mwirin. Ekkei ulumon mwirin ra uwei ngenir ere pwin; nge ewe emon mi asepwan ese tongeni tori tori nesosor; iwe ewe wa minne a fen nieno a kouno unukun unusen ewe pwinin; iwe ena wa an Ahap.

Ewe waif-pole a chék chéúfetál lón ewe chénúpupu mi chéúfetál lón ewe whale mi máló; me ewe lantern mi suki seni won, a aturanong ew saram mi osukosuk won ewe puun mi pwechepwech, mi saramaram, me towaw seni ekkewe wave non ewe pwinin, minne a mosonoson ne chafeini ewe watten pechen ewe whale, usun ew surf mi mosonoson won ew sat.

Ahap me meinisin chon an we wa ra usun ita ra annut nge ewe Parsee; ekkewe ra chappetiw non ewe bow, ra mwot ne nenengeni ekkewe shark, ekkewe ra fiti urumot won ewe whale, me ra tapwei ekkewe ira setar mi saramaram ren ar kewe ttit. Ew mongungu usun ewe mongungu non ekkewe mwichen sounfiu won Asphaltites ren ekkewe ngunun Komora rese omusano, ra ran me ngungures non ewe asepwan.

Poputa seni an kewe annut, Ahap, mesan ngeni mesan, a kuna ewe Parsee; me ra hooped ren ewe rochopwak non ewe pwinin ra usun ekkewe amuchunon mwan non ew fonufan mi uren konik. A apasa: “Ua fen rong sefan.”

“Ren ekkewe waimw? Use apasa, chinnap mwan, pwe ese wor waimw are pworun peias epwe tongeni om?”

“Nge io kewe ra rongorong pwe ra malo won matau?”

“Nge ua apasa, chinnap mwan, pwe me mwen kopwe tongeni mano non ei sai, ruemon chon mano repwe wesewesen kuna me reom won ewe sat; ewe aewin ese for ren poun aramas; me ewe ira mi pwa ren ewe amuchunon epwe mar non Merika.”

“Ewer, ewe! ew neni mi amwarar pwe, Parsee: —ew sepenin me an kewe puun ra fitikoko won ewe sat ren ekkewe wa fan iten ekkewe chon uwei pall. Ha! Ena sokkun neni sisap mwitir kuna."

“Ina mwo ika epwe tori ewe amuchunon, upwe chuen feino mwen mesom noum we pilot.”

“Iwe nupwen ka fen fokkun feino me mwen —ika epwe fis ena —iwe me mwen ai upwe tongeni tapweno mwirin, kopwe chuen pwato ngeniei, pwe kopwe pilot ngeniei chuen?—Esap ina usun? Iwe, iwe, ua nuku meinisin met ka apasa, oh nei pilot! Mi wor ai ruu pwon pwe upwe chuen nieno Moby Dick me manau seni.”

“Kopwe angei pwan ew pwon, mwan chinnap,” ewe Parsee a apasa, nupwen mesan a saramaram usun ekkewe fitikoko non ewe rochopwak —“Hemp chok epwe tongeni nieno en.”

“Ewe ira, oua wewe ngeni.—Ngang use tongeni mano iwe, won fonu me won sat,” Ahap a puchor, ren ew ngingiin turunufas;—“Use tongeni mano won fonu me won sat!”

Ir me ruemon ra fanafaneno sefan, usun chok emon mwan. Ewe pwinin mi pwichikar a feito, me ekkewe chon angang mi annut ra uta seni ewe puun wa, me mwen lekuniol ewe whale mi mano a uwei ngeni ewe wa.





SOPUN 118. Ewe Kuu.

Ewe fansoun fan iten ewe Line a arapoto; me iteiten ran nupwen Ahap, a feito seni an we imw, a suki mesan ngeni nang, ewe chon uwei ewe sipw mi mammasa epwe pwarata ne amwochu an kewe spoke, me ekkewe chon sai mi mochen repwe mwitir su ngeni ekkewe braces, me repwe uta ikewe me meinisin meser ra nenengeni ewe doubloon mi nail; ese mosonoson ngeni ewe alluk pwe epwe aiti ngeni ewe equator ewe sipw. Non fansoun mi och ewe annuk a feito. A weires won ewe pwinin mi tekia; me Ahap, a mwot lon ekkewe ttit won waimw we mi tekia, a mochen angei an we fofforun nennengeni ewe akkar iteiten ran pwe epwe silei ia a latitude ie.

Iei, lon ena matauen Japan, ekkewe ran lon summer ra ussun chok ekkewe ran mi saramaram. Ena akkar mi saramaram lon Japan ese tongeni saramaram a ussun ita pwe ina ewe leeni mi saramaram lon ewe matau mi lingoch mi lingoch mi ngetenget ese tongeni aukuuk. Ewe lang a nikinikin lak; kuchu ese wor; ewe horizon a fitikoko; me ei uren saram ese tongeni awesi a usun chok ekkewe saram rese tongeni soposopono non an Kot we nenien mwottun king. Iwe, ena quadrant an Ahap a wor ekkewe glass mi lingoch, ren ar repwe tongeni kuna ena ekkei mi saramaram. Ina minne, a suki an we leenien mot ngeni ewe roong lon ewe sipw, me ren an we pisekin angang mi ussun chok eu pisekin angang mi fiti ekkewe fuu a nom won mesan, a nonnom lon ena lapalap ren ekkoch fansoun pwe epwe tongeni kuna ewe fansoun mi pwung lupwen ewe akkar epwe angei an we meridian mi pwung. Non ena fansoun nupwen unusen an ekiek a tori, ewe Parsee a chapetiw fan itan won ewe puun sipw, me ren mesan a suki usun chok mesen Ahab, a nenengeni ewe chok akkar ren; chok ekkewe puun mesan kewe ra arapakan hood ar kewe orbs, me mesan kewe mi uren fitikoko a nom fan nemenien ew mochenin fonufan. Non fansoun nangatam ewe osukosuk mi mochen a fis; me ren an pencil won pechen we mi for seni ivory, Ahap a muttir ekieki ika epwe ifa ukukun an latitude lon ena chok fansoun. Mwirin a turutiw non ew fansoun ekiek, a nenengeni sefani ngeni ewe akkar me a ngungures ngeni pwisin i: “En esisinen sat! En Pilot mi tekia me pochokun! Ka ereni ei ennetin ia ua nom ia —nge ka tongeni aturanong ew kukkun esisin ia upwe nom ia? Are ka tongeni apasa ia ei pwan ew mettoch a nom ia? epwe chok nenengeni i Ekkei mesei ra nenengeni ewe chok mesen aramas mi pwan mwo iei a nenengeni i, ewer, me non ewe mesen aramas mi pwan mwo iei a nenengeni ekkewe pisek won ewe neni rese sinei, ikewe ie, en akkar!”

Iwe, a nenengeni an we quadrant, me a anganga, eu mwirin eu, an kewe chommong pisekin maun, a ekieki sefan, me a ngungures: “Ewe minen urumot mi umwes! Pisekin urumot an ekkewe semirit mi lamalam tekia, ekkewe Admiral, me Commodores, me Kapten; ewe fonufan a mwareiti en, ren om tipatchem me pochokkul; pwisin en ka nom won ei fonufan mi watte, me ewe poun mi amwochu en: esap wor eu chok jot epwe nom ia lesor lekuniol; manamanen manau nge a keni i, usun ekkei mesen chinnap ra mwo iei keni ren om saram, O akkar! a suki ngeni ewe deck, “usap chuen emmweni ai we aan won fonufan ren en; ewe kampas mi tekia won ewe sipw, me ewe tettenin ekkewe mi mano, ren ira me ren line; ekkei repwe emweniei, me pwari ngeniei neniei won ewe sat. kinikinfesen me ataielo en!”

Nupwen ewe chinnap mi uren netipengaw a ina usun kapas me ina usun a atai ren pechen kewe mi manau me mano, ew win mi sikesik mi usun ita a fis ngeni Ahab, me ew netipengaw mi fatafatoch mi usun ita a fis ngeni pwisin i —ekkei ra fetan won mesen ewe Parsee mi mongungu, ese mwokut. Ese wor emon a kuna pwe a uta me a glide ngeni; atun, pokiten ar niuokkus ren lapalapen nour we meilapen sounfiu, ekkewe chon sai ra chufengen won ewe sepenin, tori Ahap, a osukosuk ren an fetal won ewe sepenin, a puchor —“Ngeni ekkewe brace! Non an aneasochisi ewe annukun noun we souemwen —pwe epwe mwitir repotei, me non ew me nein ekkewe rue me fomon awa, och siwin mi finata non ekkewe mettochun ewe deck, —Starbuck ese mwo mwitir ne suki ekkewe yard ngeni ewe asepwan —ina mwo ika ese mochen me netipengaw, —nap seni an epwe mecha ngeni ewe Caprium we.

Ere a fichifich non an we state-room, ese mochen a mwokut me mwen ena ekis fansoun. Ewe lamp non ewe cabin —a angei ekkewe swing mi towaw ngeni ei me ena —a saramaram non fich, me a aturanong saram mi fich won ewe asam mi bolt an ewe chinnap, —ew mi ititiw, mi wor ekkewe blind mi fich ra tonong, non nenien ekkewe panel asan.
