Nge ekkewe monun nom mi nom asan pwe Duodecimoes ir ekkewe waimw rese tongeni mwaken, ren ekkewe kapasen ai awewei met ew waimw —wewen emon ik mi puchor, mi wor ew chuk mi ititiw.

PUK III. ( Duodecimo ), SOPUN 1. ( Ewe Popun Huzza ).—Iei ewe poon mi kan nom arapakan won unusen fonufan. Ewe it a feito seni pwisin ai awora; pun a wor lap seni eu sokkun por, iwe, epwe wor och mettoch epwe fis pwe epwe aimufeseniir. Ua eita ngeni i ina usun, pokiten a kan fiti ekkewe chuk mi hilarious, minne won ewe sat mi watte ra chok suki ngeni nang usun chok ekkewe kap non ew mwichen aramas non ewe Foun July. Ewe chon sai a kan mwareiti ar pwarata ren pwapwa. Ra ur ren ngun mi murinno, ra kan feito seni ekkewe ttit mi pwichikar ngeni ekkewe ttit mi pwichikar. Ir ekkewe at mi manau fansoun meinisin mwen ewe asepwal. Ra ekieki pwe ir eu esissin mi lucky. Ika en pwisin ka tongeni pworacho ngeni ülüngät cheer ren om küna ekkei ik mi manaman, iwe, läng epwe älisuk; ewe ngunun pwapwa mi fich ngeni Kot ese nom remi. Eú Huzza Porpoise mi múrinné me chéúfetál epwe ngenikemi eú kalon mi múrinné seni ewe oil mi múrinné. Nge ewe konik mi lingoch me lingoch mi katowu seni uwan ​​kewe a fokkun auchea. A wor tingor lein ekkewe chon fori pisek auchea me ekkewe chon fori watch. Ekkewe chon sai ra kan äeä woon nöür kewe hone. Futuken porpoise mi murinno ne mongo, ka sinei. Eli ese mwo tori ekiekum pwe emon porpoise epwe ssa. Pwungun pwe an we spout a fokkun kukkun pwe ese kon lien mecheres an aramas kuna. Nge ewe fansoun mwirin a wor om tufich, nengeni i; iwe kopwe mwitir kuna ewe watten Sperm whale pwisin non kukkun.

PUK III. ( Ruemon ), SOPUN II. ( Porpos seni Algeria ).—Emon chon atai alluk. A fokkun safei. A chok nom, ai ekieki, non ewe Pacific. A ekis watte seni ewe Huzza Porpoise, nge chommongun ekkewe chok sokkun man mi fiti ewe mwichen sounfiu. Kopwe song ngeni, iwe, epwe ässäwa eman shark. Ua fen lower fan itan fan chommong, nge use mwo kuna an a katurunong.

PUK III. ( Ruemon ), SOPUN III. ( Porpoise mi wor únúchen man mi chéúfetál ).—Ewe sókkun Porpose mi watte seni meinisin; me a chok nom non ewe Pacific, ukukun met sia sinei. Ewe chok it non fosun England, ewe a fen iteni ren tori ikenai, ina iten ekkewe chon attaw —Right-Whale Porpoise, seni ewe napanap pwe a kon nom non ewe nenien ena Folio. Lon lapalapan, a sokkofesen lon ekkoch lapalap seni ewe Huzza Porpoise, pun ese kon lien lingoch me pwapwa; enletin, i emon mi fokkun neat me usun chok emon mwan mi murinno. Ese wor föün äsefich woon pechen (lap ngeni ekkewe ekkoch föün äsefich ra wor), a wor föün äsefich mi lingöch, me mesen chon India mi lingöch mi lingöch. Nge an kapas mi möngö a atai meinisin. Inaamwo ika unusen pechen tori pechen kewe a for seni efoch sable mi alollol, nge eu line mi sokkolo, mi sokkolo ussun chok ewe esissin lon eu puun sipw, mi iteni “ewe waist mi saramaram,” ena line a suki ngeni seni ewe puun ngeni ewe puun, nge a wor ruu sokkun colour mi sokkolo, mi pwechepwech asan me mi pwechepwech fan. Ewe pwechepwech a kapachelong kinikinin mokuran, me unusen awan, minne a fori pwe epwe ussun ita pwe a keran chok su seni eu fofforun kirikiringau ngeni eu meal-bag. Eú kinikin mi fókkun menemenéch me menemenéch! An we oil a fokkun ussun chok ewe oil mi nom ren ewe porpoise.

Pwan nukun ewe DUODECIMO, ei kokkot ese fis, pun ewe Porpoise a kukkun seni ekkewe waimw. Me asan, a wor meinisin ekkewe Lefiatan mi itefoulo. Nge mi wor ew mwichen ekkewe whale rese nukuchar, ra su, ra kinikinin famini, minne, usun emon chon whale non Merika, ua sinei ren itefouno, nge esapw pwisin ngang. Ngang upwe anneani ir ren ar kewe it mi itefouno; pun eni ena sokkun list epwe tongeni auchea ngeni ekkewe chon kutta poraus non fansoun mwach, ekkewe repwe tongeni awesi met mi wor rei ikei nge ua poputa. Ika emon me nein ekkei waimw, epwe mwirin ei epwe tori me makketiw, iwe, epwe tongeni mwitir ne tonong non ei Sistem, anongonong won an Folio, Octavo, ika Duodecimo watte:—Ewe Waimw mi Noun Noun; ewe Walin Junk; ewe Whael mi Puding-Mokuran; ewe Kap; ewe Waimw mi Emweni; ewe Kan Kan; ewe Waimw itan Scragg; ewe Kop mi Kop; ewe Elefant; ewe Waimw mi Ice; ewe Waimw itan Quog; ewe Wail mi Pulu; etc. Seni ekkewe sou nemenem lon Iceland, Dutch, me England lom lom, eli epwe wor pwal ekkoch list mi mak ussun ekkewe waimw rese lukuchar, mi feioch ren sokkopaten it mi mwaal meinisin. Nge ua likitalo ir pwe ra unusen morolo; me ese tongeni an epwe anisi an epwe ekieki pwe ir mi chok ngingi, mi ur ren Lefiathan, nge ese wewe ngeni och mettoch.

Ren enletin: A fen mak le poputaan, pwe ei kokkot esap nom ikei, me fan eu chok, epwe unusochulo. Ouse tongeni nge oupwe kuna pwe ua fen apwonueta ai kapas. Nge iei ua likiti ai we Sistem ren cetology a uta ina usun ese wes, usun chok ewe watten imwen fel non Cologne a nom, ren ewe crane a chuen uta won ewe puun non ewe imw tekia ese wes. Pun ekkewe kukkun erection mei tongeni wes ren ar kewe aewin chon forata; ekkewe mi watte, ekkewe mi enlet, ra kan likitalo ewe koput ngeni mwirimwirir kewe. Kot epwe tumunuei pwe usap tongeni awesi och mettoch. Unusen ei puk a chok eu minen atakirikir —ewer, nge eu minen asoso mi fiti eu minen asoso. O, Fansoun, Pochokun, Kas, me Mosonoson!
