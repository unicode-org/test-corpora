SOPUN 1. Ekkewe lusun.

Oupwe aita ngeniei Ismael. Ekkoch ier a no —ese nifinifin ifa ukukun fansoun mi ennet —pokiten ese wor ai moni non ai we pwor, me ese wor och mettoch mi monomon epwe amwarar ngeniei won sat, ua ekieki pwe upwe sai ekis me kuna ewe kinikinin fonufan mi uren konik. Ina eu mettoch mi wor rei ai upwe angei ewe spleen me nemeni ewe fitikoko. Iteiten fansoun ua kuna pwe ua watteno grim usun ewe mongungu; iteitan fansoun a ew November mi pwichikar, pwichikar non nguni; iteitan fansoun ua kuna pwe use mochen upwe asoso me mwen ekkewe nenien iseis kofin mano, me uwei ewe mwirin iteiten peias ua churi; me akkaewin iteitan nupwen ai hypos a angei ena sokkun pochokun won ngang, pwe a namwot ew nongonongen manau mi pochokun pwe epwe eppeti ai upwe ekiekin tonong non ewe aan, me methoch ne atai ekkewe hat an aramas —iwe, ua ekieki pwe a tori fansoun ai upwe feino ngeni sat non ewe fansoun ua tongeni. Iei ai siwinin pistol me ball. Ren eu ekiek mi murinno Cato a pwisin aturalong won an we ketilas; Ua ekis angei ngeni ewe sipw. Ese wor och mettoch mi amwarar lon ei mettoch. Ika ra chok sinei, arapakan meinisin mwan non ar degree, ekkoch fansoun ika ekkoch, ra aucheani fokkun arapakan chok ekkewe chok memef ngeni ewe sat ren ngang.

Iei a wor om we telinimw mi nom unukkun ewe Manhatto, mi pwellifeili ren ekkewe leenien waimw ussun chok ekkewe fonuen India ren ekkewe chuk mi poon —ekkewe business ra pwellifeili ren an kewe matau. Pwungun me pekin, ekkewe al ra uwei ngonuk ngeni ewe konik. An we leeni mi fokkun tekia ina ewe battery, ikewe ena molumol mi tekia a tori ren ekkewe wa, me a pwichikar ren ekkewe asepwal, ekkewe fitu awa me mwan rese tongeni kuna fonu. Nengeni ekkewe mwichen aramas mi fiti konik ikewe ie.

Kopwe pwellifeili ewe telinimw lon eu pwinin Sapat mi ling. Kopwe feilo seni Corlears Hook ngeni Coenties Slip, me seni ikewe ie, ren Whitehall, ngeni notou. Met ka kuna?—Ra nom ussun chok chon mas mi monomon unukkun ewe telinimw, ra uta fite puku aramas mi tongeni malo mi chok ekieki ussun ewe matau. Ekkoch ra lukuluk won ekkewe spile; ekkoch ra mwot won ekkewe pier; ekkoch ra nenengeni ekkewe tittin sipw seni China; ekkoch ra tekia lon ewe rigging, ussun ita ra achocho le angei eu peep mi chuen murinno ngeni ewe matau. Nge ekkei meinisin ir chon fonu; ekkewe ranin wiik ra pentiu lon lath me plaster —ra riri ngeni ekkewe kaunter, ra riri ngeni ekkewe pet, ra riri ngeni ekkewe chepel. Iwe, ifa usun ei? Ekkewe fönü mi chöüfetäl ra fen morolo? Met ra fori ikei?

Nge nengeni! ikei a feito chommong mwichen aramas, ra fetan wenechar ngeni ewe konik, me usun ita repwene fiti ew dive. Sokkono! Esap wor och mettoch epwe apwapwa ngeniir nge ewe leni mi fokkun watte lon ewe fanü; fitikoko fan ewe shady lee lon ekkewe leenien iseis pisek ikewe ie esap naf. Aapw, repwe chok arap ngeni ewe konik usun chok ar tongeni nge resap turutiw non. Iwe ikewe ra uta —fite mail senir —fite league. Chon fonufan meinisin, ra feito seni ekkewe al me ekkewe al mi poon, ekkewe al me ekkewe al mi poon —eor, otiu, otiu, me otiu. Nge ikei ir meinisin ra tipeeufengen. Kopwe erenikem, ewe manamanen magnet mi nom ren ekkewe niit lon ekkewe compass lon ekkena sipw meinisin a lukiir ngeni ikewe ie?

Pwal eu fansoun. Kopwe apasa pwe ka nom non ewe fonu; non ekkoch fonu mi tekia ren ekkewe konik. Kopwe angei arapakan meinisin aan ka mochen, me engon ngeni ew a uwei ngonuk ngeni fan non ew dale, me likituk ikewe ren ew konik non ewe chanpupu. A wor magic lon. Mut ngeni ewe aramas mi kon towaw seni ekiek epwe tonong non an kewe ekiek mi kon watte —kopwe uta ena mwan won pechen kewe, kopwe isetiw pechen kewe a-fetan, iwe epwe emwenikemi ngeni konik non tipemwaramwar, ika konik mi wor non unusen ena kinikin. Ika pwe kopwe tongeni kaka non ewe watten fonu pon non Merika, sotuni ei sossot, ika pwe om karavan a fis pwe epwe kawor ren emon sensen metaphysics. Ewer, usun meinisin ra sinei, ekiek me konik ra ririfengen feinfeino chok.

Nge iei emon chon fori. A mochen peinit ngonuk ewe leeni mi kon ling, mi ling, mi kinamwe, me mi amwarar lon unusen ewe leeni mi poon lon ewe Saco. Ifa ewe mettoch mi lamot a aea? Ikenai ra uta an kewe ira, emon me emon a wor eu puun mi poon, ussun ita emon hermit me eu irapenges ra nom lon; iwe ikei a annut an we chuk, me ikewe ra annut noun kewe man; me asan seni ena imw a feilo eu pwichikar mi ling. Lon ekkewe leenien ira mi towau, a wor eu asepwal mi mazy, a tori ekkewe chuk mi pwellifeili ekkewe chuuk mi lingoch lon ar kewe blue mi lingoch. Nge ina mwo ika ewe sasing a nom non ena napanap mi uren fitikoko, me ina mwo ika ei ira pine a suki an kewe ngungu usun chok foun ira won mokuren ei chon masen siip, nge meinisin ra chok namotongaw, ika pwe mesen ewe chon masen siip ese nenengeni ewe chanpupu mi magic me mwen mesan. Kopwe feilo churi ekkewe Prairies lon June, lupwen ren fitepuku mwail ka fetal tori pecheom lein ekkewe Tiger-lilies —met ewe emon mi lingoch a mochen? —Konik —ese wor eu ttikin konik ikewe ie! Ika Niagara a chök eü chönüttu mi chöüfetäl, kopwe säi ükükün fite ngeröü mwail pwe kopwe küna? Pwata ewe soumak mi woungaw seni Tennessee, nupwen a mwitir angei ruu puun silifer, a ekieki ika epwe moni ngeni ew kout, minne a fokkun osupwang ren, ika epwe isoni noun moni non ew sai ngeni Rockaway Beach? Pwata arapakan meinisin at mi pochokun mi pochokun mi wor ew ngun mi pochokun mi pochokun non i, non ekkoch fansoun ika ekkoch fansoun ra mwaken ar repwe feino ngeni sat? Pwata non om aewin sai usun emon chon sai, ka pwisin mefi ena sokkun fitikoko mi monomon, nupwen ka akkomw erenikemi pwe en me noum we sipw oua fen tou seni fonu iei? Pwata ekkewe chón Persia lóóm ra ekieki pwe ewe matau a pin? Pwata ekkewe chon Kriis ra ngeni eu kot mi sokkolo, me pwisin pwiin Jove? Pwungun pwe ekkena mettoch meinisin rese poon. Iwe a pwan kon watte wewen ena porausen Narcissus, ewe pokiten ese tongeni weweiti ewe sasing mi eriaffou, mosonoson a kuna non ewe chanpupu, a turutiw non me a turutiw. Nge ena chok lios, sia pwisin kuna lon ekkewe chanpupu me matau meinisin. Ina ewe sasingin ewe ngunun manau ese tongeni weweiti; iwe iei ewe kii ngeni meinisin.

Iei, nupwen ua apasa pwe ua nom non ewe napanap ai upwe feino won sat iteitan fansoun ua poputa ne uren netipengaw usun mesen aramas, me poputa ne kon menemenoch ren ai kewe ngungu, use mochen epwe wor ekiek pwe ua kan feino won sat usun emon chon sai. Pun ren om kopwe feilo ussun emon chon sai epwe lamot epwe wor noum pwor, me eu pwor a chok eu ppwul ika ese wor och mettoch lon. Pwan eu, ekkewe chon sai ra semwen ren sea —ra kan anini —rese annut non pwin —rese kon pwapwa, usun ew mettoch mi itefouno; —esapw, use kan feino usun emon chon sai; ese pwan, ina mwo ika ngang och mettoch mi salt, use kan feino ngeni sat usun emon Komodor, ika emon Kapten, ika emon Kok. Ua likitalo lingen me sokkofesenin ekkena ofes ngeni ekkewe mi saniir. Ren ai kinikin, ua oput meinisin angang weires mi itefouno, sossot, me riaffou ren sokopaten mettoch meinisin. A fokkun watte met ua tongeni fori pwe upwe tumunu pwisin ngang, nge use tumunu ekkewe sipw, barque, brig, schooner, me met chok. Iwe, ren ai upwe feilo ussun chon kuk, —inaamwo ika ua pwarata pwe a wor watte ling lon ena, emon chon kuk a eu sokkun ofes won sipw —nge, ekkoch, use mwo mochen kuk man; broiled foun ira nap seni ai upwe fori. Ina seni ekkewe fofforun fel ngeni uluulun ekkewe chon Isip loom ra kan fori won ekkewe ibis mi keni me ekkewe oris mi fen keni, pwe ka kuna ekkewe mummies an ekkena man lon ar kewe watten imwen bake ekkewe pyramid.

Aapw, nupwen ua feino ngeni sat, ua feino usun emon chon sai mi mecheres, me mwen ewe mast, ua plumb ngeni ewe forecastle, won ikewe ie ngeni ewe mast-mokuren ewe king. Pwungun pwe ra kan alluk ngeniei ussun ekkoch, me fori ai upwe su seni spar ngeni spar, ussun chok emon manmwacho lon eu leenien May. Iwe, akkom, ei sokkun mettoch ese kon apwapwa. A kku an emon meefi sufol, akkaewin ika ka feito seni eu famili mi fen foruta lon ewe fonu, ekkewe famili Van Renssela, are Randolph, are Hardicanut. Iwe nap seni meinisin, ika me mwen chok om kopwe wanong poum non ewe tar-pot, ka fen nemeni usun emon chon sukun non ew fonu, ka fori ekkewe at mi tekia repwe uta ren niuokus ngonuk. Ewe siwin mi kechiw, ua anukunuku ngonuk, seni emon sensen sukun ngeni emon chon sai, me a namwot ew pochokunen decoction seni Seneca me ekkewe Stoic pwe epwe atufichikemi ne ngingioch me nikitu ngeni. Nge pwal mwo nge ei a morolo mwirin och fansoun.

Met ren, ika ekkoch hunks mi chinnap seni emon kaptinin sea ra annuku ngeniei ai upwe angei ew broom me suki ekkewe deck? Ka ekieki pwe ewe chonlang mi nap Kapriel a ekieki och mettoch mi kisikis seniei, pokiten ua mwitir me sufoliiti ena hunks mi chinnap non ena chok fansoun? Iö esap emön chon angang? Kopwe ereni ei ena. Iwe, iwe, ina mwo ika ekkewe kaptin sea mi chinnap repwe tongeni annuku ngeniei usun —ina mwo ika repwe tongeni thump me punch ngeniei usun, mi wor ai pwapwa ne sinei pwe mi och; pwe meinisin ekkewe ekkoch ra ew napanap ika ekkoch ra angang non ukukun chok napanap —ika non ew ekiekin inis ika metafisik, wewen; iwe ina usun ewe thump universal a fetan won, me poun meinisin repwe suki emon me emon won pecher, me repwe pwapwa.

Pwal och, ua kan feilo won matau fansoun meinisin ussun chok emon chon sai, pun ra kan fori eu kokkot pwe repwe moniei fan iten ai osukosuk, nge rese kan moni ekkewe chon sai eu chok moni ua fen rongorong ussun. Nge, ekkewe chon sai repwe pwisin moni. Iwe, a wor meinisin sokkofesenin fonufan lefilen ach sipwe moni me ach sipwe angei moni. Ewe fofforun moni, neman ina ewe mettoch mi kon aletipechou ekkewe ruuemon chon sola lon ewe atake ra efisata ngenikich. Nge an epwe kamo , —met epwe awewe ngeni? Ren enletin, ua kan ló wóón matau usun emén chón sái, pokiten ewe minen apwapwa mi múrinné me ewe ásepwál mi liméch lón ewe leenien sái wóón ewe fore-castle.
