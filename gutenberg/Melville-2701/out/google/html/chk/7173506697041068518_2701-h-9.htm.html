SOPUN 46. Ekkewe ekiek.

Inaamwo ika, a kkuf ren ewe ekkei mi pwichikar an we kokkot, nge Ahap lon an ekiek me foffor meinisin a fen ekieki ussun ewe sopwoloon an epwe angei Moby Dick; ina mwo ika a usun ita a mmonleta ne asoresini meinisin mochenin fonufan ngeni ena ew mochen; nge inaamwo ika ina, nge eli epwe fen fis pwe ren napanapan me an fansoun langattam a fokkun fiti fofforun emon man mi fiti ekkei, iwe, esap unusen likitalo ewe angangen apwonueta ewe sai. Are at least ika pwe ei ese ina, ese wor mochen ekkoch popun mi kon watte an pochokun ren i. Epwe kon watte an epwe alimelima, eni, pwal mwo nge ika sipwe ekieki ussun an monomon, pwe epwe tongeni apasa pwe an liwinimang ngeni ewe White Whale epwe tongeni fen watteta lon ekkoch lapalap ngeni meinisin ekkewe sperm whale, me pwe ewe chommongun ekkewe manmwacho a nielo ren chommongun, epwe pwal chommongolo ekkewe tufich pwe emon me emon leir epwe tongeni kuna ekkewe manmwacho mwirin. Nge ika pwe ena sokkun ekiek epwe wesewesen sokkolo, a chuen wor pwal ekkoch ekiek mi kapachelong, inaamwo ika rese kon lien tipeeu ngeni ewe fofforun mwanesol an mochen nemenem, nge rese fokkun tongeni amwokutu i.

Ren an epwe apwonueta an kokkot Ahap epwe aea pisekin angang; me seni ekkewe pisekin angang meinisin ra aea lon ewe saram, mwan ra kon tipeeu ngeni ar repwe tou seni kokkot. A sinei, awewe chok, pwe ina mwo ika a magnetic an nemenem non ekkoch napanap a nap seni Starbuck, nge ena nemenem ese kku ewe unusen mwan non pekin ngun usun chok ewe chok nemenem non pekin inis a kapachelong ewe nemenem non mirit; pun ngeni ekkewe mi chok ngun, ekkewe mi tipatchem nge ra uta non ew sokkun ririin inis. Inisin Starbuck me an Starbuck mochenin koput ra nom ren Ahap, ukukun chok Ahap a tumunu an we magnet non ekiekin Starbuck; nge a chok sinei pwe ren ekkei meinisin ewe chienan mi nap, non ngunun, a oput an we kaptin kutta, me epwe tongeni, epwe pwapwa ne aimufeseni inisin seni, ika pwan mwo asonga. Eli epwe fis pwe epwe fis eu fansoun mi langattam me mwen an epwe kuna ewe White Whale. Non ena fansoun nangatam Starbuck epwe tongeni turutiw non ekkewe niwinin u ngeni an noun we kaptin emwenin, ika pwe ekkoch napanap mi monomon, mirit, me napanap mi fis ngeni. Esap chok ina, nge ewe tiparoch mi monomon an Ahap sufoniti Moby Dick a pwano iei nap seni an ekiek mi nap me tipachem ne kuna pwe, ren ei fansoun, ewe hunt epwe non ekkoch napanap epwe pout seni ena ekiek mi amwarar mi fis non inisin; pwe unusen ewe niuokkus lon ewe sai epwe chok nonnom lon ewe leeni mi rochopwak (pun ekkoch chok mwan pwora ina pisekin pwarata ngeni ekiek mi langattam nge ese ngaselo ren foffor); pwe nupwen ra uta ar kewe fansoun mas mi langattam non pwin, noun kewe ofes me mwan epwe wor ekkoch mettoch mi arap ar repwe ekieki nap seni Moby Dick. Pun ina mwo ika ekkewe chon angang mi ngaw ra fokkun mochen me tipepos ra mwareiti ewe esinesin usun an kutta; nge meinisin chon sai won sepenin meinisin ra kon are kisikis ar mochen me rese tongeni lukuluk —ra manau lon ewe fansoun lukun mi sokkofesen, me ra asoso ren an fiffis —me lupwen ra nom fan iten och mettoch mi towau me poon lon ewe kutta, inaamwo ika epwe wor pwonen manau me mochen lon sopwoloon, nge a lap seni meinisin mettoch mi lamot ngeniir pwe epwe fis ngeniir och mettoch mi lamot ngeniir. a fis fan iten ewe amuchunon dash.

Ahap ese pwal ekieki usun pwal eú mettóch. Non fansoun pochokunen memef aramas ra turunufasei meinisin ekiek mi monomon; nge ekkena fansoun ra chok morolo. Ewe lapalap mi nomofoch fan iten ewe mwan mi foruta, Ahap a ekieki, ina ewe lapalap mi limengau. Ach sipwe apasa pwe ewe White Whale a unusen amwokutu letipen ei mwichen chon angang mi ngau, me ar fiti ar fofforun mwanesol a pwal mwo nge efisata eu sokkun mocheniaingau mi kirokiroch lon ir, nge, nge pokiten ar tongei ena ra ngeni Moby Dick, epwe pwal wor ar mongo fan iten ar kewe mocheniaingau iteiten ran. Pun pwal mwo nge ekkewe sounfiun Crusader mi tekia me tipepwora lon fansoun lom lom rese pwapwaiti ar repwe pwellifeili ruu ngerou mwail lon fonu pwe repwe maun fan iten ar peias mi pin, nge rese fori fofforun sola, angei pocket, me angei pwal ekkoch pisekin fel ren ewe al. Ika pwe ra fen fokkun amwochu ar we eu chok mettoch mi amuchunon me tong —ena mettoch mi amuchunon me tong, chommong repwe fen kul seni ren ar oput. Ngang usapw angei seni ekkei mwan, ekieki Ahap, meinisin apinukunuk ren moni —ewer, moni. Ra tongeni turunufasei cash iei; nge mut ngeni ekkoch maram repwe lo, me ese wor pwonen ekiek usun ena ngenir, iwe mwirin ei chok moni mi kinamwe meinisin non ew chok fansoun a mutin non ir, ei chok moni epwe mwitir ne cashier Ahab.

Ese pwal wor pwal eu popun tumun mi kon riri ngeni pwisin Ahap. Pokiten a fen fis fan mosonoson, a tongeni fis, me eli epwe ekis mwen an epwe pwarata ewe popun mi akkom nge mi monomon fan iten an ewe Pequod sai, Ahap a unusen miritiiti iei pwe, ren an fori ena, a fen pwisin suki ngeni ewe tipis ese tongeni polueni pwe epwe angei nemenem; me ren unusen an ese wor liwinin, lon pekin foffor me alluk, noun kewe chon angang ika ra mochen, me ren ena popun ra tufich, repwe tongeni u ngeni meinisin ar repwe alleasochisi i, me pwal mwo nge angei seni i ewe alluk ren fofforun mwanesol.
