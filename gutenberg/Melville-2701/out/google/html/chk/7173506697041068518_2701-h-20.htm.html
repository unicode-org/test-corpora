Fopuku mwan non ew sipw ra kutta ekkewe Sperm Whales ren fopuku walu maram ra ekieki pwe ra fori mi fokkun murinno, me kinisou ngeni Kot, ika pwe soposopono ra uwei ngeni imwer ewe oil seni fopuku ik. Pwal och, ika sipwe ekieki pwe mi pwung, ese ussun ita pwe a wor och kapasen anini fan iten an epwe morolo ekis me ekis ewe Sperm Whale, awewe chok, pwe lon ekkewe ier lom (ewe sopwoloon kinikinin ewe senturi a lo, apasa) ekkena Leviathan, lon ekkewe kukkun pod, ra chuuriir fan chommong lap seni ikenai, me, pokiten ena, ekkewe sai me ekkewe sai rese pwal kon langattam. Pokiten, ussun a fen kuna lon pwal ekkoch leeni, ekkena whale, mi kkuf ren ekkoch ekiek ussun tumunun aramas, iei ra kan ssafetal lon ekkewe matau lon ekkewe mwichen sounfiu mi watte, ina minne, ren eu ukukun ekkewe solitary, yoke, me pod, me sukul mi toropasfeil lon ekkewe ekkoch ran ra kan chufengen iei lon ekkewe armi mi watte nge mi sokkofesen, nge rese kan fiti. Ina chok. Iwe, a ussun ita pwe a pwal mwaal ewe ekiek, pwe pokiten ekkewe whale mi iteni whale-bone rese chuen nom lon chommong leeni lon ekkewe ier lom mi ur ren ir, ina popun ena sokkun man a pwal kisikisilo. Pun ra chök ässäwa seni ewe chuk mi tekia ngeni ewe chuk mi tekia; me ika eu konik ese chiwen manau ren ar kewe jet, iwe, kopwe nuku pwe ekkoch pwan ekkoch me towaw seni ekkewe konik ra fokkun keran chok niuokus ren ewe minen apwapwa rese sinei.

Pwal och: ussun ekkena Leviathan mi mak porauser mwirin, a wor ruu imwen maun mi pochokkul, iwe, lon meinisin ekiekin aramas, repwe chok nonnom tori feilfeilo chok. Iwe usun chok nupwen ra tonong non ar kewe fonu pon, ekkewe chon Switzerland mi pwichikar ra niwin ngeni ar kewe chuuk; ina minne, ra kkuf seni ekkewe savanna me ekkewe leenien ppwul lon ekkewe matau lefilen ekkewe matau, ekkewe whale mi wor futuken whale ra tongeni mwittir feilo ngeni ar kewe leenien maun lon ewe Polar, me ra kkuf fan ekkewe minen eppet me tit mi fokkun glass ikewe ie, ra feito lein ekkewe leenien ppwul mi ais me ekkewe ppwul mi poon; me non ew char mi uren fitikoko non December ese much, a ungeni meinisin kutta seni aramas.

SOPUN 106. Ewe Pechen Ahap.

Ewe napanap mi mwitir an Kapten Ahap a tou seni ewe Samuel Enderby seni London, ese fen fis nge ese wor emon a tumunu ren ekkoch kukkun fofforun mwanesol ngeni pwisin i. A fen saramaram ren ena sokkun pochokun won ew thwart an we wa pwe pechen we ivory a angei ew kinikinin fitikoko. Iwe nupwen mwirin an angei pwisin an we deck, me pwisin an we pivot-hole ikewe, a fokkun pochokun ne wiil ren ew annuk mi mwitir ngeni ewe chon uwei (a, usun chok me nom, ew mettoch usun an ese naf an epwe uwei); iwe, ewe ivory mi fen chechech a angei ena sokkun fitikoko me fitikoko, pwe inaamwo ika a chuen unus, me ngeni meinisin lapalap mi lukulukoch, nge Ahap ese ekieki pwe a unusen lukuchar.

Iwe, ennetin, a usun ita ese wor mettoch mi amwarar, pwe ren meinisin an kewe fofforun tipemwaramwar mi choufetal, Ahap a kan tumunuoch ngeni lapalapen ena futuk mi malo minne a ekis uta won. Pun ese kon fansoun nangatam me mwen an ewe Pequod sai seni Nantucket, pwe a kuna non ew pwinin a annut won ewe puun, me ese wor an ekiek; ren ekkoch rese sinei, me usun ita rese tongeni awewei, rese tongeni ekieki, pechen we mi for seni ivory a fokkun pochokun ne siwin, pwe a fen stake-wise tori, me meinisin ra chok tori pechen; ese pwal fis pwe ese fokkun weires pwe ewe ppwul mi eriaffou a unusen chikar.

Pwal och, lon ena atun, ese fen tolong lon an ekiek mi monomon, pwe meinisin ewe riaffoun ena riaffou mi nom lon ena fansoun, ina chok ewe poraus mi weneiti eu riaffou me lom; me i a pwan fatafatoch usun ita a kuna, pwe usun ewe man mi poison nap seni meinisin non ewe marsh a apochokuna an sokkun usun chok ese tongeni siwin usun ewe chon kon mi kon ngingioch seni meinisin non ewe ira; ina minne, usun chok meinisin felicity, meinisin mettoch mi ngaw ra kan fis non napanap mi fis usun chok. Ewer, nap seni ew chok, a ekieki Ahap; pokiten ekkewe popun me mwirimwirin Grief ra feilo towau seni ekkewe popun me mwirimwirin Joy. Pun, esapw ai upwe apasa ei: pwe ew inference seni ekkoch afanafan non canon, pwe ina mwo ika ekkoch pwapwaan napanap ikei esap wor nour repwe uputiw ngenir fan iten ewe pwan ew fonufan, nge, non ewe napanap, epwe fiti ewe pwapwa-ese wor semirit ren meinisin hell netipengaw; nge, ekkoch riaffoun mano mi tipis repwe chuen nounou ngeni pwisin ir ew mwirimwirin netipengaw mi fefeita feinfeino chok nap seni ewe peias; ese fokkun hint ren ei, mi chuen usun ew inequality non ewe anapanapen alollol usun ewe mettoch. Pun, Ahap a ekieki, inaamwo ika pwal mwo nge ekkewe feioch mi tekia seni meinisin won fonufan ra fen eani eu sokkun mettoch mi kisikis mi monomon mi monomon lon ir, nge, lon pekin ngun, meinisin osukosuken letip, eu wewe mi monomon, me, lon ekkoch mwan, eu lapalap mi lapalap seni ekkewe chonlang; ina usun ar kewe angangen kutta poraus mi tinikken rese luku ewe poraus mi ffat. Ren ach sipwe fiti ekkewe uruon mwirimwirin ekkei riaffoun mano mi tekia, a uwei kich non soponon nein ekkewe popun uruwo ese wor popun an ekkewe kot; ina minne, mwen meinisin ekkewe akkar mi pwapwa, mi fori fetil, me ekkewe cymbal mi mosonoson, ekkewe maram mi rokopwanieno, mi namwot sipwe fang ngeni ei: pwe ekkewe kot pwisin rese pwapwa feinfeino chok. Ewe esisinen uputiw ese tongeni morono, mi netipengaw non mesen aramas, ina chok ewe esisinen netipengaw non ekkewe chon sain.

Ese wor emon a sinei ikei ew monomon a fen pwano, minne eni epwe tongeni murino, non napanap mi kefinita, a fen pwano me nom. Ren chommong pwan ekkoch poraus usun Ahab, fansoun meinisin a chok nom non ew monomon ngeni ekkoch, pwata a ina usun, pwe ren ew fansoun, me mwen me mwirin ewe sai won ewe Pequod, a fen opano inisin ren ena sokkun ekiek mi monomon usun chok ewe Grand-Lama; me, ren ena ew fansoun, a kutta nenien tumun ese tongeni kapas, usun ita, nein ekkewe senapen ekkewe mi mano mi marble. Ewe popun mi ngaw an Kapten Peleg ren ei mettoch a pwarata pwe ese fokkun naf; nge, ennetin, usun a tori meinisin kinikinin Ahab mi kon watte, iteiten pwarata meinisin a angei nap seni rochopwak mi auchea nap seni saram mi awewei. Nge, non soponon, meinisin ra tou; ei ew mettoch a fori, ese nifinifin. Ena feiengau mi fokkun ngau a nom lon ewe sopwoloon an nonnom chok ren och fansoun. Iwe esap chok ei, nge ngeni ena circle mi kan kon, mi turutiw won sat, ewe, ren och popun, a wor ren ewe tufichin an epwe arap ngeni i ese kon pinepin; ngeni ena mwichen aramas mi niuokkus ewe feiengau mi fen mak asan —a chuen nom, ussun chok a fis, nge Ahap ese ekieki usun —a pwisin uren niuokkus, ese unusen kkuf seni ewe fonuen ngun me kechiu. Ina minne, ren ar tinikken fan itan, ir meinisin ra fen kokkot, ukukun ar nom non ir, pwe repwe amoielo ewe sinei usun ei mettoch seni ekkoch; iwe ina popun a fis, pwe ese tori ew fansoun mi watte a fen no, a fis won ekkewe deck an ewe Pequod.

Nge epwe iei usun meinisin; mut ngeni ewe synod ese kuna, ese fat non ewe asepwan, ika ekkewe samol mi niwinsefanito me ekkewe pochokunen ekkei, repwe fori ika rese fori ngeni Ahap won fonufan, nge, non ei mettoch ikenai usun pechen, a angei ekkewe napanap mi fatafatoch mi auchea; —a kori ewe chon for ira.

Iwe, lupwen ena chon angang a pwa mwen mesan, a ereni pwe esap muttir poputa le fori eu minefoon pechen, me a ereni ekkewe chon angang pwe repwe kuna pwe epwe wor meinisin ekkewe chuk me chuk mi for seni ewe chuk (Sperm Whale) mi fen ioifengen lon ewe sai, pwe epwe tongeni fis eu kefil mi murinno seni meinisin. Ei a fis, ewe chon for pisek a angei annuk pwe epwe fori ewe pechen epwe wes non ena pwinin; me an epwe awora meinisin ekkewe pisekin angang fan itan, ese nifinifin ekkewe mi weneiti ewe emon ese lukuluk won mi aea. Pwal och, a allúkú pwe ewe leenien ffér sipw epwe chék chéúfetál seni an chék chéúfetál lón ewe leenien ffér sipw ren och fansoun; me, ren an epwe mwitir ewe angang, ewe chon for pisek a alluk pwe epwe muttir poputa le forata met sokkun pisekin angang seni mecha epwe tongeni lamot.
