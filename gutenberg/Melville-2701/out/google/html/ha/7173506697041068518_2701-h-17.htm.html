Babin da ya gabata ya ba da labarin wani babban jiki ko garken maniyyi Whales, sannan kuma an ba da dalilin da zai iya haifar da tarin tarin.

Yanzu, ko da yake a wasu lokuta ana cin karo da irin waɗannan manyan jigogi, amma, kamar yadda aka gani, ko da a halin yanzu, a wasu lokatai ana lura da ƴan ƙungiyar mawaƙa, waɗanda ke rungumar mutane ashirin zuwa hamsin kowanne. A hakikanin gaskiya, wannan mutumi dan Ottoman ne na marmari, yana yin iyo a cikin duniyar ruwa, kewaye da shi tare da duk abin ta'aziyya da sha'awar haram. Idan wani matashi Leviathan ya zo ta wannan hanya ba tare da dalili ba, ya ɗauka ya kusance ɗaya daga cikin matan a asirce, da irin tsananin fushin Bashaw ya kai masa hari, ya kore shi! Yanzu, kamar yadda masunta ke kiran harem na whales makaranta, haka kuma ubangidan wannan makarantar a fasahance ake kira da malamin makaranta. Don haka ba a cikin hali mai tsauri ba, duk da abin sha'awa, cewa bayan ya tafi makaranta da kansa, sai ya fita waje yana koyar da ba abin da ya koya a can ba, amma wautarsa. Sunansa, malamin makaranta, zai zama kamar an samo shi daga sunan da aka ba wa haramun, amma wasu sun ɗauka cewa mutumin da ya fara ba da irin wannan nau'in whale na Ottoman, dole ne ya karanta abubuwan tunawa na Vidocq, kuma ya sanar da kansa ko wane irin malamin makaranta ne wanda shahararren Bafaranshe ya kasance a cikin ƙananansa, da kuma abin da ya koya a cikin wasu darussa.

Irin wannan keɓancewa da keɓantawa wanda babban malamin makaranta whale ya kai kansa a cikin shekarunsa masu tasowa, gaskiya ne ga duk tsofaffin maniyyi Whales. Makarantun da ba kowa ba sai matasa da ƙwararrun maza, waɗanda aka ambata a baya, suna ba da bambanci mai ƙarfi ga makarantun haram. Domin yayin da waɗancan kifin kifayen mata suka kasance masu jin kunya, samarin maza, ko bijimai arba'in, kamar yadda ake kiran su, sun fi kowa ƙanƙanta a cikin dukan Leviathans, kuma a cikin karin magana sun fi hatsarin saduwa; ban da masu launin toka masu ban sha'awa, masu kifin kifi, wasu lokuta suna haduwa, kuma waɗannan za su yi yaƙi da ku kamar mugun fizge masu fusata da gout.

Makarantun ganga arba'in sun fi na harami girma. Kamar gungun matasa ’yan makaranta, suna cike da faɗa, nishaɗi, da mugunta, suna faɗuwa a duniya a cikin irin wannan halin rashin hankali, da jujjuyawa, ta yadda babu wani mawallafi mai hankali da zai tabbatar da su fiye da yadda zai yi wani ɗan tarzoma a Yale ko Harvard. Ba da daɗewa ba suka bar wannan hargitsi ko da yake, kuma lokacin da kusan kashi uku cikin huɗu suka girma, suka watse, suka tafi daban don neman ƙauyuka, wato, haramun.
