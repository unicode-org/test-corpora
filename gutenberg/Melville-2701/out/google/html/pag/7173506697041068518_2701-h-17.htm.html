KAPITULO 86. Say Ikol.

Saray arum a manag-anlong so angidayew ed malambot a mata na antelope, tan say marakep a bago na manok ya agbalot onleleksab; aliwan tanton singa tawen, manselebra ak na ikol.

No isipen ya say sankabalgan ya ikol na Sperm Whale et onggapo ed saman a punto na pueg to ya onkukurang ya singa kabaleg na sakey a too, diad tapew to labat et walaan na kalawakan ya ag-onkulang ed limamplon piye kuadrado. Say masimpit a malimpek a laman na lamot to et onlaknab ed duaran malapar, malet, patatdang a palma odino flukes, a kalkalnan onkupas ya anggad kulang a sakey pulgada so kapal to. Diad pagew odino pansasangsangan, sarayan flukes et medyo manpapaspas, insan ira onaarawi ed balang sakey a singa saray payak, ya mangitilak na malaknab a bakante ed baetan. Anggapoy mabilay a bengatla ya mas marakep so pakapatnagan na saray linya na karakpan nen say singa-bulan iran ketegan na sarayan fluke. Diad sankagrabian ya ilalaknab to ed balyena ya ombaleg la, say ikol to et onlampas ed duamplon mitro so karukey to.

Say interon membro et singa makapal a webbed bed na saray welded sinews; balet ta putoten mo itan, tan nanengneng mo ya taloran nanduruman strata so manitibukel ed satan:—tagey, pegley, tan leksab. Saray lanot ed tagey tan leksab a layer et andukey tan pabitay; saramay walad pegley, antikey a tuloy, tan manbabatik ed baetan na saray paway a layer. Sayan taloran istruktura, singa met ed antokaman, et mangiiter na pakayari ed ikol. Parad saray manaaral na daan iran padir na Roma, say pegley a layer et mangiter na makapainteres a mipara ed manipis a kurso na saray baldosa a naynay a mansasalet ed bato ed saraman a makapakelkelaw iran relikya na antigo, tan andi-duaruwan makatulong a maong ed baleg a biskeg na masonry.

Balet singa ag magenap iyan baleg a lokal a pakayari ed tendinous tail, say interon karaklan na leviathan et akapeket ed sakey a warp tan woof na saray masel a lanot tan filament, ya ondadalan ed balang dapag na saray pagew tan onlelereg ed saray flukes, ya ag nalilikna a manlalaok ed sikara, tan baleg so nitulong to ed biskeg da; kanian diad ikol et singa akasentro ed sakey a punto so mankakasakey ya agnasukat a puersa na interon balyena. No nayarin nagawa so inkaandi, saya so nepeg ya gawaen.

Nisay saya—say makapakelkelaw a biskeg to, so aglan balot makapakapuy ed marakep a panag-ipit na saray galaw to; no iner say inka-ugaw na inka-mainomay et onkukurang ed panamegley na sakey a Titanism na pakayari. Diad kasunian, saratan a galaw so makagamor na sankagrabian a karakpan da manlapud satan. Say tuan biskeg et agbalot makaderal ed karakpan odino pantutunosan, balet mabetbet ya iter to itan; tan diad amin a makapadinayew a karakpan, say biskeg so walaan na baleg a pisiglaotan ed mahiko. Ekalen iray akagaton a tendon ya singa onsasabog ed marmol ed inukit ya Hercules, tan naandi so anting-anting to. Nen intagey na debosyonadon si Eckerman so linen sheet manlapud lakseb ya bangkay nen Goethe, sikatoy abigla ed baleg a pagew na laki, ya singa arko na pananalo na Roma. No ipinta nen Angelo so Dios ya Ama ed porma na too, tandaan no antoy inkabiskeg to. Tan antokaman so nayarin iparungtal da nipaakar ed madibinon panangaro ed Anak, saray anlemew, kulot, hermaphroditical iran litrato na Italya, a ditan et matalonan nipatnag so ideya to; sarayan litrato, a singa sikara ed amin a panagpaatagey, so mangipapabitar na anggapoy antokaman a pakayari, noagta say negatibo labat, bii na panagpasakop tan panagsungdo, a diad amin a lima et inkonpesal to itan, a manporma ed saray nikadkaduman praktikal a birtud na saray bangat to.

Ontan so ag-amta ya inka-elastiko na organo ya tutukoyen ko, ya balanglan uusaren ed isport, odino diad inkasimoon, odino diad sanok, antokaman so kipapasen na nonot to, saray panag-ipit to et naynay a natandaan na alablabas a grasya. Anggapoy taklay na engkanto ya makalampas ed satan.

Limaran baleg a galaw so nikadkaduma ed satan. Unona, no usaren bilang palikpik parad iyaaligwas; Kumadwa, no usaren bilang mace ed bakal; Komatlo, diad panaglinis; Komapat, diad panag-lobtailing; Komalima, diad peaking flukes.

Unona: Lapud akaalagey so ikol na Leviatan, duma so ikiwas to ed ikol na amin ya pinalsa ed dayat. Agton balot onkukurong. Diad too odino sira, say pan-wriggle et tanda na inkaabeba. Parad balyena, say ikol to so saksakey a paraan pian makagalaw. Say scroll-wise ya inmarap ed silong na laman, insan maples ya inmabeba, saya so mangiiter na nikadkaduman panag-dart, panaglukso na galaw ed ulsa legan ya manlalangoy a maong. Saray side-fins to et manseserbi labat ya mangimaneho.

Kumadwa: Medyo makabkabaliksan, ya bangta say sakey a sperm whale et milalaban labat ed sananey a sperm whale panamegley na ulo tan panga to, anggaman ontan, diad saray panlalaban to ed too, sikatoy manunan tan mapangta ya uusaren toy ikol to. Diad pansasagpot to ed sakey a baloto, maples ton iyaarawi iray flukes to ed satan, tan say suntok to et lapud recoil. No ginawa itan ed anggapoy makaamper ya dagem, lalo la no onleksab itan ed marka to, say stroke et agla naresistian. Anggapoy taglang na too odino baloto ya makasungdo ed satan. Say kilalaban mo labat et diad pangipaliis ed satan; balet no onsabi itan ed gilig na danum, sirin lapud anlemew ya itatagey na baloto na balyena, tan say inka-elastiko na saray materyales to, say sakey a apilat a taglang odino sakey odino duaran putol-putol a tabla, sakey a klase na tahi ed gilig, so kaslakan a sankagrabian a resulta. Sarayan linmereg a suntok ed gilig na danum so mabetbet a naawat ed panagsigay, kanian ipapasen iratan a panag-awit labat na ugaw. Walay sakey ya mangilulukas na kawes to, tan say abut et natunda.

Komatlo: Agko nayarian ya ipanengneng itan, balet parad siak, diad balyena say pakalikna na pan-diwit et akasentro ed ikol to; ta diad sayan pamaakaran et walay inka-delikado to ya mipara labat ed inka-marakep na pukel na elepante. Sayan inka-delikado so manunan napatnagan ed kiwas na panag-sweep, sano diad inka-uyamo na inka-marikit et say balyena tekep na pigaran anlemew a inka-maalwar et igagalaw to so angkakabaleg iran ulpo to manlapud sakey a diking ya anggad sananey a diking diad tapew na dayat; tan no nalilikna to labat so balbas na marino, pakaskasi itan a marino, balbas tan amin. Agaylan panangasi so walad satan ya unonan pan-diwit! No walay pakayari na sayan ikol ya mangala, nepeg kon tampol ya isipen so elepante nen Darmonodes ya mabetbet ya onla ed tindaan na saray rosas, tan diad abeban panangibano et mangiiter na saray nosegay ed saray bibii, insan to inaro iray sona ra. Diad mas dakel a salaysay nen say sakey, makapaermen ta say balyena et walaan na sayan makapasagyat a birtud ed ikol to; ta walay nadngelan ko nipaakar ed sananey nin elepante, ya nen asugatan ed laban, inkurba toy pueg to tan inala toy pana.

Komapat: Diad ag-iilaloan ya panagtakew ed balyena diad kaligenan na pegley na saray dayat ya ag-onkukurang, tan sikatoy singa kuting ya manlalaro ed dayat a singa sakey a pugon. Balet siansia nin nanengneng mo so pakayari to ed panag-play to. Saray malapar a palad na ikol to so inmawet ed tagey; insan to ipelag so tapew, say maksil a konkusyon so ontanol ed loob na dakel a milya. Ngalngali isipen mon walay baleg ya paltog ya pinaltog; tan no naimano yo so anlemew a korona na singaw manlapud spiracle ed biek a sampot to, isipen yo a satan so asewek manlapud touch-hole.

Komalima: Singa ed ordinaryon onleletaw a postura na leviathan saray flukes et walad leksab na beneg to, insan agla ra nanengneng ed silong na tapew; balet nen sikatoy onlelereg lad kaaralman, say interon flukes to ya ag-onkulang ed talomplon mitro so karukey na laman to et nibantak ya akaalagey ed dagem, tan mansiansian onyegyeg ed sakey a bekta, anggad ag-ira nanengneng. Likud ed makapadinayew a panag-apag —diad arum a pasen a nayarin deskribien —say pantok na saray ngipen na balyena so nayarin sankarakpan a nanengneng ed amin a mabilay a palsa. Manlapud andi-dalem a kaaralem say angkakabaleg ya ikol et singa manpapasnok ed sankatageyan a katawenan. Kanian diad kugip ko, anengneng ko so makapadinayew a Satanas ya mangibubuga na makapairap a baleg a kuko to manlapud ngalab na Baltic na Impierno. Balet diad pakanengneng ed saratan ya eksena, amin ed amin et no antoy mood mo; no diad Dantean, saray demonyo so onsabi ed sika; no diad samay nen Isaias, saray arkanghel. Nen akaalagey ak ed uloan na palo na barko legan ya onsisinag so agew ya manpapaspas ed tawen tan dayat, aminsan et anengneng ko so baleg a pulok na balyena ed bukig, ya amin da et mamaarap ed banwa, tan diad sakey a bekta et manpapasnok ira a mankakasakey ed saray mansasnit a balyena. Diad pakanengneng ko ed saman a panaon, say ontan ya engranden panangipatnag na panagdayew ed saray dirios et agnin balot anengneng, anggan diad Persia, say ayaman na saray managdayew ed apoy. Singa ed impaneknek nen Ptolemy Philopater nipaakar ed elepante ed Aprika, inpaneknek ko met so nipaakar ed balyena, ya inkuan kon sikato so sankadebotoan ed amin a pinalsa. Ta unong ed si Arin Juba, saray elepante na sundalo nensaman et mabetbet ya onkukurang ed kabuasan ya akatagey so pueg da ed sankarakelan a kareenan.

Say panangikompara ed sayan kapitulo, ed baetan na balyena tan say elepante, no nipaakar ed pigaran aspekto na ikol na sakey tan say pukel na sananey, et ag nepeg ya ipasen ya parepareho iratan a duaran mankontraan ya organo, lalo la no saray pinalsa ya kawalaan da. Ta no panon ya say sankabiskegan ya elepante et sakey labat a terrier ed Leviathan, ontan met, no ikompara ed ikol na Leviathan, say pueg to et singa labat sanga na lirio. Say sankagrabian ya impakasuntok na pueg na elepante et say makapaliket ya pan-tap na bentilador, no ikompara ed agnasukat ya pan-crash tan pan-crash na saray ambelat ya flukes na sperm whale, ya diad paulit-ulit ya inkagawa et nantutumbokan ya mangibabantak na interon baloto ya amin so sagwan tan tripulante ra ed tagey, a singa met ed sakey ya Indian juggler ya mangibabantak na saray bola to.

*Anggaman amin a panangikompara ed paraan na inkalapagan a karakel na balyena tan say elepante et andiay kakanaan, lapud diad satan a partikular et say elepante so manalalagey ed parehon respeto ed balyena a singa say gagawaen na aso ed elepante; anggaman ontan, wala ray pigaran punton makapainteres ya mipara; kaiba ed saraya so spout. Kabkabat ya say elepante et mabetbet ya mangaala na danum odino dabok ed pueg to, insan to itagey itan, ipelag tod ilog.

No lalon iisipen ko iyan mabiskeg ya ikol, lalon panermenan ko so agko pakayarin mangibalikas ed satan. No maminsan et wala ray galaw ed satan, ya anggaman makapaliket ed lima na too, mansiansian agnapaliwawa. Diad malaknab a pulok, makapadinayew, sagpaminsan, irayan mistikon galaw, kanian wala ray nadngel kon manag-anop ya angibaga ya mipara iratan ed saray tanda tan simbolo na Free-Mason; ya say balyena, diad tua, diad sarayan paraan et marundunong ya akitongtong ed mundo. Anggapo met so arum ya galaw na balyena ed inkalapagan a laman to, a napno na nikadkaduman bengatla, tan ag-itan naebatan na sankaeksperiensyaan ya manag-atake ed sikato. I-dissect mo no panon kon nayarian, sirin, onloob ak labat ed aralem a baog; Agko kabat, tan ag ko lan balot kabat. Balet no agko amta so anggan say ikol na sayan balyena, panon kon natalosan so ulo to? lalo la, panon ya natalosan so lupa to, anta anggapoy lupa to? Nanengneng mo so beneg ko, say ikol ko, singa ibabaga to, balet ag nanengneng so lupak. Balet agko sigpot a nanengneng iray parte na beneg to; tan ipanengneng no antoy labay to nipaakar ed lupa to, ibagak lamet ya anggapoy lupa to.





KAPITULO 87. Say Baleg ya Armada.

Say andukey tan mainget a peninsula na Malacca, ya onnatnat ed abalaten-bukig manlapud teritorya na Birmah, so manitibukel ed sankaabalaten a pasen ed interon Asia. Diad nantutumbokan a linya manlapud satan a peninsula et onnatnat iray andukey ya isla na Sumatra, Java, Bally, tan Timor; ya, kaiba so dakel niran arum, et manporma na baleg a mole, odino rampart, ya mangikokonekta ed Asia ed Australia, tan mangibiig ed andukey ya ag-apagpag ya Indian ocean manlapud saray makapal a oriental archipelago. Sayan padir et walay pigaran sally-port parad kombeniensya na saray barko tan balyena; say mapatnag ed saratan et say straits na Sunda tan Malacca. Diad gilig na Sunda, nagkalalo la, saray bapor ya mamaarap ed China manlapud sagur, et ompapaway ed dayat na China.

Saraman ya mainget ya straits na Sunda so mangibiig ed Sumatra manlapud Java; tan akaalagey ed pegley na satan a malaknab a padir na saray isla, ya akasuportaan na satan a maksil a berdin propontory, ya kabkabat na saray marino bilang Java Head; ag ira tanton mipara ed sentro na puerta ya onlulukas ed sakey a malaknab ya apadir ya empiryo: tan no ikonsidera so agnaupot a kayamanan na saray rekado, tan seda, tan alahas, tan balitok, tan garing, ya diad panamegley to et pinayaman na sanlibon isla na saman a dayat ed bukig, ompatnag a sakey a makabkabaliksan a probisyon na kalikasan, a saratan a kayamanan, diad mismon inkaporma na dalin, so nepeg a magmaliw a sankaabebaan say itsura, anggan panon lay inka-epektibo to, et nabantayan ed amin-amin a mundon mamasagur. Saray gilig na Straits of Sunda et anggapoy suplay na saray makapadinayew a kuta ya manbabantay ed saray lolooban na Mediterranean, Baltic, tan Propontis. Aliwan singa saray Danes, sarayan Oriental et ag da labay so makapadinayew ya panangigalang ed saray inyabeban top-sail manlapud agnaparaan a prosesyon na saray barko ed arap na dagem, ya diad apalabas iran siglo, diad labi tan agew, et ondadalan ed baetan na saray isla na Sumatra tan Java, ya akarga na sankamablian iran kargamento ed bukig. Balet bangta bulos dan itunda so sakey a seremonya a singa saya, ag da balot ipupulisay so ibabaga ran mas malet a buis.

Agla nanonotan iray piratiko ya proas na saray Malay, ya manaamot ed saray abeban malimpek a baybay tan saray isla na Sumatra, so linma ed saray bapor a manlalayag ed saray gilig na dayat, a mankekerew na buis ed punta na saray gayang da. Anggaman lapud paulit-ulit iran makapadimlan dusa ya naawat da ed lima na saray European cruisers, say inkakpel na sarayan corsairs et medyo napugeran; balet, anggan diad kaplesan, sagpaminsan et narerengel tayo so nipaakar ed saray bapor na Ingles tan Amerikano, ya diad saratan a danum et andi-panangasi ya linmugan tan sinasamsam.

Lapud marakep tan preskon dagem, say Pequod et asingger la natan ed sarayan gilig; Si Ahab et manlabay ya ondalan ed sikara ya anggad dayat na Javan, insan diman, manbiabiahe ya amaarap ed amianen, diad tapew na saray danum ya kabkabat ya mabetbet ya dadalanen na Sperm Whale, ondalan ed gilig na dayat na Philippine Islands, tan makasabi ed arawin baybay na Japan, diad panaon na baleg a panaon na panag-ala na balyena diman. Diad sarayan paraan, say manliber-liber a Pequod et nalampasan to so ngalngalin amin a kabkabat a pasen a panagbiahean na Sperm Whale ed mundo, sakbay ya onleksab ed Line diad Pasipiko; diman si Ahab, anggaman ag-asumpal so pananap to ed amin a pasen, so mapekder a nanilalo ed pilalaban to ed si Moby Dick, diad dayat a sikatoy kabkabat a mabetbet a lalaen to; tan diad panaon ya nayarin sikatoy makatunongan ya ipasen ya mansasakit ed satan.

Balet panon natan? diad sayan sonadon panag-anap, ag kasi nadiwit nen Ahab so dalin? oniinum na dagem iray tripulante to? Seguradon sikatoy ontunda pian mananap na danum. Andi. Diad andukey a panaon, natan, say agew ya manbabatik ed sirko et manbabatik ed loob na manliob a singsing to, tan agto la kaukolan so naakan no ag say walad sikato. Kanian si Ahab. Markaan mo met iya ed panag-ayam na balyena. Bangta saray arum ya kasko et kargaan na saray alien ya kagawaan, pian iyalis ed saray pantalan na arum a bansa; say barkon balyena ya manliliberliber ed mundo et anggapoy awit ton kargamento no ag say sikato tan saray tripulante to, saray armas da tan saray labay da. Walay karga na interon lawak ya akabotelya ed malaknab ya pananginan to. Sikatoy napno na saray utilities; aliwan amin ya ag nausar ya pig-lead tan kentledge. Walay awit ton dakel a taon ya danum. Malinew ya daan ya manunan danum na Nantucket; ya, no taloy taon ya onleletaw, say Nantucketer, diad Pasipiko, et mas labay toy oninum antis na maasin a pluido, balet ta kaplesan et inmalis ed saray bariles, manlapud saray ilog na Peru odino India. Kanian, bangta saray arum a barko et nayarin linma ed China manlapud New York, tan pinmawil lamet, ya akasabi ed pigaran daongan, say barkon balyena, ed amin na saman a panaon, et nayarin ag akanengneng na anggan sakey a bukel na dalin; saray tripulante to lapud anggapoy laki balet ta onleletaw a singa sikara a singa sikara. Kanian diad saman et inawit mo ed sikara so balita ya sinmabi so sananey a delap; ebat da labat—“Bueno, ugugaw, nia so biong!”

Natan, lapud dakel lan Sperm Whales so narel ed mamasagur a baybay na Java, diad asingger ed Straits of Sunda; tua, lapud maslak a dalin, say roundabout, et kaslakan ya bibidbiren na saray managsigay bilang ekselentin pasen parad panaglayag; kanian, legan ya lalon ondarakel so Pequod ed Java Head, saray managbantay et paulit-ulit ya indayew, tan binagaan ya mansiansian aliing. Balet anggaman saray berdin pukdol na dalin so ag-abayag et pinmatnag ed bandad kawanan, tan diad maliket iran eleng et nasingsing so preskon kanela ed dagem, balet anggapo ni anggan sakey a jet so adeskribe. Ngalngali la intunda so amin a kanonotan ya onloob ed antokaman a galaw dia, say barko so ngalngali la onloob ed saray gilig na dayat, sanen say niyugali lan panliket a manliket so nadngel manlapud tagey, tan ag abayag et sakey a palabas na nikadkaduman karakpan so sinmabi ed sikami.

Balet dia so pangibaga, a lapud ag-ontutundan kimey ya inusar da ed kaplesan ya panag-anop ed amin ya apatiran dayat, saray Sperm Whale, imbes ya ngalngalin lanang ya manlayag ed melag iran grupo, a singa nensaman, et mabetbet la ran nanengneng ed malaknab iran pulok, a no maminsan et mangaabrasa na baleg a karaklan, a singa singa dakel so ngaran da ya nansamba liga tan sipanan parad pantutulongan tan proteksion. Diad sayan pantitipon na Sperm Whale ed angkakabaleg iran caravan, nayarin ipasen so sirkumstansya ya anggan diad sankaabigan iran pasen a panaglayag, nayarin manlayag kayo natan ed loob na pigaran simba tan bulan a saniiba, ya anggapoy anggan sakey a spout; insan biglan nasaludoan na no maminsan et singa nilibo ed nilibo.

Say kalapar na duaran pana, diad kaarawi na manga dua o taloran milya, tan manporma na baleg a semicircle, ya mangaakop ed kapaldua na patatdang a abot, sakey a nantutumbokan a kadena na saray balyena-jet so ontatagey tan mankirlap ed dagem ed pegley-agew. Aliwan singa saray diretso ya perpendicular twin-jets na Right Whale, ya, no manbibiigan ed tagey, et onlelereg ed duaran sanga, a singa saray sanga na willow ya akapeket, say saksakey ya akaarap ya sanga na Sperm Whale et mangipapanengneng na makapal ya akakulong a kakiewan na amputin alimbusabos, ya tuloytuloy ya ontatagey tan onleleksab ya anggad dagem.

No nanengneng ed kubyerta na Pequod, sirin, legan ya ontatagey ed atagey a pukdol na dayat, sayan grupo na saray singa-singa, ya onkukurong ed dagem, tan nanengneng ed manlalaok ya atmospera na bluish haze, et singa sanlibon maliket iran tsimenea na sakey a makapal a metropolis, ya indeskribe na sakey a balmy ya kabuasan na otonyo, ya akalugan ed sakey a kabayo.

Legan ya onaasingger iray armada ya onaasingger ed agmakakaaro ed kapalandeyan, papelesen day martsa ra, amin da et labalabay dan ipuesto itan a mapeligron dalanan ed beneg da, tan onlaknab lamet ira ed maligen a pasen ed patar; anggan ontan met so ginawa na sayan baleg a grupo na balyena a singa manaapura la ed unaan panamegley na saray kairapan; kalkalnan onkukurang iray payak na semicircle da, tan onlangoy ira, diad sakey a malet, balet ta siansia nin singa bulan.

Say Pequod et tinmumbok ed amin ya layag; saray harpooneer ya mangaawit na armas da, tan maksil iran manpapasnok manlapud uloan na saray akabitin nin baloto ra. No say dagem et nansiansia labat, anggapoy panduaruwa no sikara, ya linmabas ed sarayan Straits of Sunda, say baleg a sundalo et onla labat ed saray dayat ed Bukig pian nanengneng so pakadakep na aliwan pigara ed bilang da. Tan siopay makapangibaga no kasin, diad saman ya nantitipon a caravan, si Moby Dick a mismo so ag temporaryon manlangoy, a singa say dadayewen ya amputin elepante ed prosesyon na koronasyon na saray Siamese! Kanian diad panamegley na stun-sail ya akatambak ed stun-sail, nanlayag kami, ya ipapaarap mi irayan leviathan ed unaan mi; nen biglan nadngel so boses nen Tashtego, ya maksil ya angipaarap ed imano ed sakey a bengatla ya walad benegan mi.

Mipara ed krescent ed van mi, walay anengneng min sananey ed beneg mi. Singa itan ginawa na saray amputin singaw ya ontatagey tan onleleksab a singa saray sangi na saray balyena; ag ira labat sigpot ya linma tan linma; ta sikaray naynay ya onleletaw, ya ag ira naandi ed kaunoran. Say resulta na sayan impaabeba et medyo mangiyilustra ed satan a makabat a balikas ed Fishery,—no mas dakel so balyena et daiset so sira. Diad amin ya balyena ya in-drug, sakey labat so narel. Saray arum et nanplano pian makatakas ed saman a panaon, balet ta inala ra labat, a singa nanengneng ed saginonor, na arum a barko nen say Pequod.





KAPITULO 88. Saray Eskuelaan tan saray Managbangat na Eskuelaan.

Say akaunan kapitulo so angiter na salaysay nipaakar ed baleg a laman odino pulok na saray Sperm Whale, tan diman met so angiter na posiblin sengegan na saratan a angkakabaleg a panagtipon.

Natan, anggaman no maminsan et wala ray ontan ya angkakabaleg a laman, balet, singa nanengneng, anggan diad kaplesan, wala ray angkekelag ya grupo ya naimano, ya manlalapud duamplo anggad limamplon indibidual kada sakey. Saratan a banda et kabkabat bilang eskuelaan. Kaslakan et duaran klase iratan; saramay ngalngalin bibii so tugyopen da, tan saramay ag mantitipon noag saray malangwer nin mabiskeg a kalakian, odino baka, unong ed kabkabat a tawag ed sikara.

Diad iyaatendi na saray kabayo ed eskuelaan na bibii, nanengneng mo so sakey a laki ya baleg la, balet aliwan matatken; ya, no walay alarma to, ipapanengneng toy inka-galante to diad ipelag tod beneg tan insakbong to so itakas na saray bibii to. Diad tua, sayan laki et sakey a magarbon Ottoman, ya manlalangoy ed tapew na danum, ya kaiba toy amin a ligliwa tan panangaro na harem. Makapadinayew so pandumaan na sayan Ottoman tan saray asawa to; lapud, bangta sikatoy lanang ya sankabalgan ya leviathanic proporsyon, saray bibii, anggano sigpot lan ombaleg, et ag-onsulok ed kakatlo na karaklan na sakey a lalakin kaslakan so kabaleg to. Sikaray medyo malimpek, tua; Ibagak ya ag onlampas ed kapalduan dosena na yarda ed kaliberliber na pagew. Anggaman ontan, ag nayarin iburi ya diad inkalapagan et walaan ira na kanepegan ed en bon point .

Makapainteres so pakanengneng ed sayan harem tan say katawan to ed saray andiay-kakanaan a panag-awit-apag da. Singa saray uso, sikaray lawas onkukurang ed panag-anap na nanduruman klase. Nabet mo ra ed Line diad panaon na sigpot a rosas na Equatorial feeding season, ya pinmawil ka la, nayarin, manlapud impanayam mo ed tiagew diad saray dayat ed Bukig, tan diad ontan et manpalikdo ed tiagew na amin ya agmakapaliket a kapagaan tan petang. Diad panaon ya akapan-lounge la ra ed tagey tan leksab na promenade na Equator, onla ra ed Oriental waters diad panaalagar ed ambetel a panaon diman, kanian napaliisan da so arum nin alablabas a temperatura ed taon.

No mareen ya onaarap ed sakey ed sarayan biahe, no walay nanengneng ton nikadkaduman makapasuspetsa, say lord whale et maalwar ya manbabantay ed makapainteres ya pamilya to. No walay agmakatunongan ya mangibaga ed malangwer nin Leviathan ya onsasabi ed satan a dalan, ipasen mon kompidensyal ya onasingger ed sakey ed saray bibii, tekep na makapadinayew a sanok ya inataki na Bashaw ed sikato, tan sikatoy pinataynan to! Atagey a panaon, diad tua, no saray andiay prinsipyon kalangweran a singa sikato so naabuloyan ya onloob ed inkasanto na liket ed abung; anggano gawaen toy labay na Bashaw, agto nayarian ya ag-onla ed dukolan to so sankabantog ya Lothario; ta, abagey! amin ya sira et pareho. Diad gilig na dayat, saray bibii so mabetbet a pansengegan na sankagrabian a panlalaban ed limog na saray karibal dan mandinayew; ontan met ed saray balyena, ya no maminsan et onsasabi ed makapatey a bakal, tan amin et lapud panangaro. Manbakor ira panamegley na andukey iran panga ra, no maminsan et mankakasakey ira, kanian mansasagpot iran magmaliw a sankatageyan a singa saray elk ya manlalaban ya mankakasakey ed saray saklor da. Aliwan pigara so naala ya walaan na aralem iran peklat ed sarayan inkaapag-apag,—saray ulo ya apaldua, ngipen ya apilat, palikpik ya apaldua; tan diad arum a kipapasen, saray sangi ya apaldua tan apaldua.

Balet no ipasen tayo so panlusob na liket na ayayep ed bilay ya onarawi ed inmunan impan-apura na katawan na harem, sirin kasin talagan makapaligsay-puso so panbantay ed satan a katawan. Maalwar ton ipaparungtal lamet so baleg a karaklan to ed limog da tan manliliket diman na antikey a panaon, a siansia nin walad makapasagyat a kaliberliber na malangwer nin Lothario, a singa say maridios a Solomon a debosyonadon mandadayew ed limog na sanlibon asawa to. Lapud wala ray arum ya balyena ya nanengneng, saray managsigay et matalag ya manguusil ed sakey ed sarayan Grand Turks; ta sarayan Grand Turks et masyadon dakel so biskeg da, kanian melag so inka-unctuous da. Nipaakar ed saray anak a lalaki tan bibii ya iyanak da, akin, saratan ya anak a lalaki tan bibii so nepeg ya mangasikaso ed inkasikara; anggan panon, diad tulong labat na ina. Ta singa arum ya omnivorous roving lovers ya nayarin tawagen, say Lord Whale ko et anggapoy panlabayan tod nursery, anggan panon lay panlabayan tod bower; tan kanian, lapud sakey a maong a managbiahe, tinaynan to ray ag to kabat ya anak to ed interon mundo; balang ugaw et eksotiko. Diad maabig a panaon, anggaman ontan, legan ya onkakapuy so seseg na kalangweran; legan ya ondarakel so taon tan saray basuraan; legan ya say panagdalepdep so mangiiter ed sikato na seryoson panagtunda; diad antikey, singa say inkalapagan a inka-lassitude so onsasabi ed napgaan a Turko; insan say panangaro ed inkainawaan tan birtud so onsalat ed panangaro ed saray bibii; say Ottoman tayo et linmoob ed andiay pakayari, magbabawi, makapangiter na simbawa ed bilay, mansamba, mangibuyak ed harem, tan binmaleg ya magmaliw ya napanuliranan, maermen ya masiken a kamarerwa, linma a bukor to ed limog na saray meridian tan parallel ya ibabaga to ray pikakasi to, tan papasakbayan to so kada malangwer nin Leviathan manlapud saray lingo to ed panangaro.

Natan, no panon ya say harem na saray balyena et tatawagen na saray managsigay ya eskuelaan, ontan met so katawan tan amo na satan ya eskuelaan ya kabkabat ya schoolmaster. Kanian aliwan diad istrikton ugali, anggan panon lay makapadinayew a panag-ayew, ya kayari ton maneskuela, sikatoy onla ed arum a bansa ya ag to ipasal so naaralan to diman, noagta say inkakulangkulang na satan. Say titulo to, schoolmaster, et singa natural ya nanlapud ngaran ya inter ed mismon harem, balet wala ray mangibabagan say toon inmuna ya angiter na ngaran ed sayan klase na Ottoman whale, et seguradon akabasa ed saray memoir nen Vidocq, tan impaamta tod sarili to no antoy klase na country-schoolmaster nen ugaw ni, tan no antoy okultura na saraman ya leksion insipan to ed pigaran estudyante to.

Say parehon inkaapag-apag tan inkaapag-apag ya nalilikna na schoolmaster whale legan ya ontatakken la, et tua met ed amin ya maedad lan Sperm Whale. Diad ngalngalin amin a pasen, say sakey a balyena ya manbukbukor—a singa tawag ed manbukbukor a Leviathan—et napaneknekan ya sakey a balyena nensaman. Singa say mapagalang a si Daniel Boone ya walay balbas ton lumot, anggapoy arum ya asingger ed sikato no ag say Kalikasan a mismo; tan sikato so inala ton asawa ed kalawakan na danum, tan sikato so sankaabigan ed saray asawan bii, anggaman sikatoy walaan na dakel a makapadimlan sekreto.

Saray eskuelaan ya walaan na arum noag saray kalangweran tan mabiskeg a lalaki, ya asalambit la, so mangiyoopresi na mabiskeg a pandumaan ed saray eskuelaan na harem. Ta bangta saratan a bibiin balyena et mabaing, saray malangwer nin lalaki, odino apataplon-barrel-bulls, unong ed tawag da ed sikara, so sankagrabian ed amin a Leviathan, tan sankapeligroan a naabet; likud ed saramay makapakelkelaw ya abuek so ulo ra, tan saray balyena ya abuek, ya no maminsan et nabet, tan saraya so milaban ed sika a singa saray makapataktakot a demonyo ya napapagaan lapud penal gout.

Say Forty-barrel-bull ya eskuelaan et mas baleg nen saray eskuelaan na harem. Singa sakey a grupo na saray kalangweran ya manaaral ed kolehiyo, napno ira na panlalaban, panliliket, tan kaugsan, ya onlelereg ed interon mundo ed ontan lan kapeles, ya anggapoy maalwar ya underwriter ya mangiseguro ed sikara a singa say pangiseguro to ed sakey a magulon ugaw ed Yale odino Harvard. Balet ta ag-abayag et tinaynan da la iyan gulo, tan no manga talora-kapat la ra et ombaleg la ra, mansian ira, tan manbibiigan ira pian mananap na panaayaman, salanti, saray harem.

Say sananey nin punto na pandumaan na saray eskuelaan na lalaki tan bibii et mas lalon pakabidbiran ed saray bibii. Ibagam ya tinamaan mo so sakey a Forty-barrel-bull—pobrin demonyo! tinaynan na amin ya kakaiba to. Balet ta sugaten so sakey a membro na eskuelaan na harem, tan saray kakaiba to so onlangoy ed kaliberliber to tekep na amin a tanda na kapagaan, a no maminsan et mansiansian asingger ed sikato tan andukey a panaon, a singa ra mismon nabiktima.
