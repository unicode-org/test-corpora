Natan, legan ya pantotongtongan so nipaakar ed semilya, nepeg ya pantongtongan so arum nin bengatla a mipara ed satan, diad negosyo na pangiparaan ed semilya parad saray try-works.

Walay taway to ya singa ed isipen ko ya nayarin natawayan na sakey a royal cutlet manlapud pueg nen Louis le Gros, no ipasen kon sikatoy pinatey ed inmunan agew kayari na panaon na karne na usa, tan saman a partikular a panaon na karne na usa et mipara ed nikadkaduman marakep a panag-ani na saray kaubasan na Champagne.

Sayan pasen so akauna lan abitla bilang pangikargaan na saray piraso na kubrekama, no naekal tan naala ed balyena. Diad sakey a diking, ya sinag na sakey a malimpek a parol, walay pasen ya atilak parad saray trabahador. Kaslakan et manparis ira,—sakey a pike-and-gaffman tan sakey a spade-man. Kaleganan to, say spade-man et akaalagey ed mismon sheet, ya akapeket itan ed saray portable horse-pieces. Sayan pala et matarem a singa nagawaan na dugpa; saray sali na managpala et andiay sapatos; say bengatlan akaalageyan to et no maminsan et agnasuppiat ya onarawi ed sikato, a singa sakey a sledge. Saray gamegamet na sali et matalag ed saray beteranon lalakin manlalapud blubber-room.

No linmugan ka ed Pequod diad sakey a punto na sayan post-mortemizing na balyena; tan no nanakar ka ed unaan ya asingger ed windlass, segurado ak ya na-scan mo komon ya anggapoy daiset ya inkausisa so sakey a nikadkaduman tuloy, makapabiglan bengatla, ya anengneng mo komon diman, ya akayurong ed karukey na lee scuppers. Aliwan say makapakelkelaw a bobon ed baleg ya ulo na balyena; aliwan say prodigy na ag-akabitin ya abeban panga to; aliwan say milagro na simetrikon ikol to; anggapo ed saraya so makapabigla ed sika, singa say kapaldua na pakanengneng ed satan ya ag nabilang a cone,—mas andukey nen say katagey na sakey a Kentuckian, ngalngalin sakey a mitro so diametro to ed leksab, tan jet-black a singa si Yojo, say ebony idolo na Queequeg. KAPITULO 96. Say Try-Works.

Likud ed saray aka-hoisted ya baloto to, say sakey ya American whaler et nikadkaduma ed saray try-works to. Ipaparungtal to so makapainteres ya anomaliya na sanka-solid a masonry ya inlaok ed oak tan hemp pian nagawa so asumpal a barko. Singa manlapud lawak so sakey a hurnoan na laryo ya inawit ed saray tabla to.

Saray try-works et akatanem ed baetan na foremast tan mainmast, say sankaawang a parte na deck. Saray tabla ed leksab et nikadkaduman mabiskeg, ya akaparaan pian nasungdoan so belat na ngalngalin malet a laryo tan mortar, manga samploran mitro ed waloran kuadrado, tan limaran mitro ed katagey. Say pundasyon et ag makaloob ed deck, balet say masonry et malet ya akapeket ed tapew na saray ambelat a pueg ya balatyang ya akapeket ed amin a diking, tan akapeket ed saray tabla. Diad saray gilig to et walay tabla to, tan diad tagey et sigpot ya asakbongan na baleg, akapeket, tan walay batten ya hatchway. No ekalen tayo iyan hatch et nanengneng tayo ray angkakabaleg ya try-pot, duara so bilang da, tan kada sakey et walay kapasidad na pigaran bariles. No ag-iratan uusaren, mansiansian malinis iratan. No maminsan et papakintab iratan na soapstone tan buer, anggad onsinag ira ed loob a singa saray pilak a punch-bowl. Legan na panagbantay ed labi et wala ray makulangkulang ya masiken a marino ya onkurong ed saratan tan onkurong ira diman pian manpainawa. Legan ya manpapasnok ed saratan—sakey a too ed kada kaldero, a mankakasakey—dakel so kompidensyal a komunikasyon ya gagawaen, diad saray bibil a bakal. Saya met so pasen parad aralem a panagdalepdep ed matematika. Diad kawigi ya try-pot na Pequod, ya say soapstone et maseet ya onliliber ed siak, so inmunan agdirektan impakabigla ko ed makapadinayew a katuaan, ya diad geometry et amin a laman ya onlelereg ed cycloid, say soapstone ko alimbawa, et onleksab manlapud anggan iner a punto diad eksakton parehon panaon.

No ekalen so fire-board ed arap na try-works, say anggapoy kawes ton masonry ed satan a diking et napatnagan, ya akalooban na duaran balatyang a sangi na saray hurno, ya walad silong na saray kaldero. Sarayan sangi et walaan na ambelat iran puertan balatyang. Say mabiskeg a petang na apoy et ag makasabi ed kubyerta, diad panamegley na malimpek a reservoir ya onnatnat ed silong na interon akasara a tapew na saray kimey. Diad panamegley na sakey a tanel ya inyan ed beneg, sayan reservoir et mansiansian napno na danum legan ya onsingaw itan. Anggapoy tsimenea ed paway; direkta iran manlukas manlapud beneg a padir. Tan dia et ompawil tayo ed sakey a bekta.

Manga alas nuebe na labi nen ginmapo so panag-try-work na Pequod ed sayan biahe natan. Kien nen Stubb so mangasikaso ed negosyo.

“Akaparaan la diman? Off, insan, tan igapom. Manluto, mamaputok ed saray kimey.” Mainomay iya, ta say karpintero et mangitutulak na saray kiskisan to ed hurno legan ya ondalan. Nia et nibaga ya diad panaglayag na balyena say unonan apoy ed saray try-works et kaukolan ya pakanen na kiew ed loob na sakey a panaon. Kayari na satan et anggapo lay nausar a kiew, likud ed paraan pian maples ya onsinag so manunan gasolina. Diad sakey a salita, kayari na impanubok, say malimpek tan linmeksab ya taba, ya tatawagen natan ya scraps o fritters, et walaan ni na dakel ya ag-ontutumbok iran kablian to. Sarayan fritters so mamapakan ed ngalab. Singa say sakey a martir ya manpopoolan, odino sakey a misanthrope ya manguupot ed sarili to, no napoolan la, say balyena et mansuplay na dilin gasolina to tan manpopoolan ed mismon laman to. Inupot to komon so mismon asewek to! ta say asewek to et makapataktakot ya ingen, tan nepeg mon ingen itan, tan aliwan satan labat, no ag ingen nepeg kan manbilay ed satan ed saman a panaon. Walay agnabalikas, atap, tan Hindoo ya angob to, a singa nayarin walad asingger na saray ponponan. Say angob to et singa kawigin payak na agew na panangukom; sakey itan ya argumento parad abot.

Nen pegley-labi et sigpot lan mankikimey iray kimey. Malinew kami ed bangkay; ginawa la so layag; say dagem et manrepresko; say kabilungetan ed dayat et grabe. Balet satan a kabilungetan so napno na saray masebeg a ngalab, a diad panapanaon et manlalapud saray sooty flues, tan manliwawa ed kada atagey a lubir ed rigging, a singa ed bantog ya apoy na Griego. Say manpopoolan a barko so nantultuloy, a singa anggapoy pagbabawian ton ginawa so sakey a panagbales. Kanian saray pitch tan sulfur-freighted brigs na matapang a Hydriote, Canaris, ya ompapaway manlapud saray pantalan da ed pegley-labi, a walaan na malapar iran sheet na ngalab parad saray layag, so inmabeba ed saray Turkish frigates, tan intiklop da iratan ed saray conflagrations.

Say abot, ya inekal ed tagey na saray kimey, et walaan la natan na malapar a pugon ed arap da. Diad tapew na saya et wala ray Tartarean ya kurti na saray paganon manag-harpoon, ya lanang ya man-stoker na barkon balyena. Diad panamegley na angkakabaleg iran poste et ipelag da ray mansisingsing a masa na taba ed saray mansasnit a kaldero, odino papawalaen da ray apoy ed leksab, anggad saray singa uleg ya ngalab et pinmaway ed saray puerta pian erelen ira ed sali ra. Say asewek et linmeksab ed makapaermen iran bunton. Diad kada pitch na barko et walay pitch na manpabukal a larak, ya singa labay dan onlukso ed lupa ra. Diad arap na sangi na saray kimey, diad biek a diking na malapar a pugon a gawad kiew, et walay windlass. Saya so nanserbi parad sea-sofa. Diad saya et akayurong so relo, no ag-inusar, ya onnenengneng ed ambalbalangan petang na apoy, anggad saray mata ra et singa apoolan ed ulo ra. Say kolor-balatyang ya itsura ra, ya natan et aderal lapud asewek tan linget, saray balbas dan mankibalatar, tan say mandumaan ya barbaro ya inkasilew na ngipen da, amin na saraya so nikadkaduman niparungtal ed saray makapainteres ya dekorasyon na saray kimey. Legan dan isasalaysay ed balang sakey iray agmasanton agawaan da, saray salaysay da nipaakar ed takot et ibabaga ra ed saray salita na liket; legan ya onlelereg ed sikara so ag-sibilisadon taway da, a singa saray ngalab manlapud hurnoan; diad arap da, saray manag-harpooneer et mabiskeg ya mansesenyas panamegley na angkakabaleg iran tinidor tan dippers da; legan ya onkukurang so dagem, tan say dayat so onlukso, tan say barko so onkukurong tan onlelereg, balet ta malet ton inpaltog so ambalanga ya impierno to ed kabilungetan na dayat tan labi, tan mapangta ton pinaltog so amputin pukel ed sangi to, tan maruksan inluwa to ed kaliberliber to ed amin a dapag; insan say onkukurang a Pequod, a kargaan na saray masebeg, tan kargaan na apoy, tan manpopoolan na bangkay, tan onlelereg ed satan a kabilungetan, so singa materyal a kapara na kamarerwa na monomaniac a komandante to.

Ontan so inisip ko, legan kon akaalagey ed timon to, tan diad andukey ya oras et mareen kon igigiya so dalan na sayan fire-ship diad dayat. Diad saman a panaon, diad kabilungetan ko, balet mas anengneng ko so inka-ambalanga, say inkabaliwala, say inka-makapataktakot na arum. Say tuloytuloy ya pakanengneng ed saray porma na demonyo ed arap ko, ya kapaldua et walad asewek tan kapaldua et apoy, saraya so angitarok na mipadparan pasingawey ed kamarerwak, nen ginmapo ak lan onsuko ed satan ya ag naisip ya inkaugip ya onsabi ed siak diad pegley-labi.

Balet diad saman a labi, nagkalalo la, walay nikadkaduman (tan agnapaliwawa) a bengatla ya sinmabi ed nonot ko. Manlapud antikey ya ugip ko, walay makapataktakot ya lingo ed siak. Say panga-pukel tiller so tinamaan na diking ko, ya akayurong ed satan; diad layag ko et walay abeban ungol na saray layag, ya onggapo lan onyegyeg ed dagem; Inisip ko ya lukas iray matak; Kapaldua ak lan makalikna ya iiyan ko ray gamegamet ko ed saray taklay tan mekanikal ya iyunat ko iratan ya mas arawi ni. Balet, anggaman ed amin na saya, anggapoy anengneng kon kompas ed arap ko pian makadalan; anggaman singa sakey minuto labat manlapu nen anengneng ko so kard, lapud say malet ya binnacle lamp ya manliliwawa ed satan. Anggapoy nanengneng ko no ag say jet gloom, ya panapanaon et nagagawaan na makapataktakot lapud saray kirmat na ambalbalanga. Say sankaimportantian et say impresyon, ya antokaman a maples, maples a bengatla ya akaalageyan ko et aliwan tanton akapeket ed anggan dinan a daongan ed unaan noagta manaapura manlapud amin a daongan ed beneg. Sakey a mabiskeg, makapabiglan liknaan, a singa patey, so sinmabi ed siak. Manpapasnok ya inegnaan na limak so tiller, balet ta walay makapabiglan panagpaatagey ya say tiller et, anggan panon, diad sakey a makapasagyat a paraan, akabaliktad. Dios ko! antoy problemak? inisip ko. Nia! diad antikey ya ugip ko et inmarap ak, tan akaarap ak ed beneg na barko, ya say beneg ko et akaarap ed arap to tan say kompas. Diad sakey a bekta et inmarap ak ed beneg, diad dugan panaon pian ag-ontikyab so bapor ed dagem, tan nayarin ontumba. Agaylan liket tan agaylan pisasalamat so pakainawaan manlapud sayan ag-inkatural a halusinasyon ed labi, tan say makapatey ya ag-inilaloan ya inawit na lee!

Ag ka onnenengneng na andukey ed arap na apoy, O too! Ag ka balot mankukugip ya say limam et walad timon!
