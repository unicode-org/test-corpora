KAPITULO 53. Say Gam.

Say mapatnag a rason no akin ya ag linmugan si Ahab ed balyena ya asalita tayo et saya: say dagem tan dayat so mangipapanengneng na bagyo. Balet anggano aliwan ontan so agawa, agto komon inlugan ed sikato—base ed ginawa to ed mipadparan inkagawa—no ontan komon ta, diad proseso na panag-hailing, sikatoy akagamor na negatibon ebat ed tepet ya intepet to. Balet amin na saya et nayarin mansiansian kulang so kalkulasyon, no anggapoy nibaga dia nipaakar ed nikadkaduman uusaren na saray bapor a panag-ayam na balyena no mankakasakey ira ed dayat na arum a bansa, tan nagkalalo la ed sakey a pasen a panaglayag.

No walay duaran sankaili ya ombabaliw ed Pine Barrens diad New York State, odino say parehon nisulinek ya Salisbury Plain ed England; no kaslakan ya mankakasakey ira ed ontan ya agmakapaliket iran katakelan, sarayan duara, parad bilay da, et ag da napaliisan so pansaludoan; tan ontunda na daiset pian mansalita na balita; tan, nayarin, manyurong na daiset tan manpainawa ed konsierto: sirin, agaylan natural nen say diad agnaparaan a Pine Barrens tan Salisbury Plains na dayat, duaran barkon manag-ala na balyena ya manbabaroy ed balang sakey diad sampot na dalin—diad Fanning's Island, odino diad arawin King's Mills; mas natural, kuan ko, ya diad ontan iran sirkumstansya et sarayan barko et nepeg ya aglabat mansasalatan na uran a yelo, no ag ingen mas maapit, mas makaaro tan makapikaaro. Tan nagkalalo la no saya so ompatnag a sakey a bengatla, diad kaso na saray bapor a kayarian na sakey a daongan, tan saray kapitan, opisyal, tan aliwan pigara ed saray lalaki so personal a kabat na balang sakey; tan lapud satan, nawalaan na amin a klase na pinabpabli iran bengatla ed abung a pantongtongan.

Parad abayag lan anggapoy barko, say outward-bounder, nayarin, et walaan na saray sulat; antokaman so nagawa, seguradon nawalaan na pigaran papel na sakey a petsa sakey odino duaran taon ya onlabas nen say unor ed saray blurred tan thumb-worn files to. Tan diad laknab, amin na saya so mansiansian tua nipaakar ed saray bapor a panag-ayam na balyena ya manbabaliw ed balang sakey diad mismon cruising-ground, anggaman parehon abayag iran anggapo ed abung da. Ta sakey ed sikara so nayarin akaawat na panangiyalis na saray sulat manlapud sakey a komatlo, tan natan et arawin barko; tan arum ed saratan a sulat et nayarin parad saray totoo ed barko ya nakakatongtong to natan. Likud ni, manpapasaliw ira na balita nipaakar ed panag-balyena, tan mantongtong iran maong. Ta aliwa labat ya naabot da so amin a simpatya na saray marino, no ag ingen ontan met ed amin a nikadkaduman pankaabigan a manlalapud sakey a pankakasakey a pansasagpotan tan pankakasakeyan na saray kakulangan tan kapeligroan.

GAM. NOUN— Sakey a sosyal a pantitipon na duaran ( odino masulok ni ) a Whaleship, a kaslakan et diad sakey a panaglayag; nen, kayari na impansaliwan da na saray graniso, nansaliwan ira na saray tripulante na baloto: say duaran kapitan so atilak, ed saman a panaon, ed loob na sakey a barko, tan say duaran manunan kapitan ed sananey.

Walay sananey nin melag ya bengatla nipaakar ed Gamming ya ag nepeg ya lingwanan dia. Amin a propesyon et walaan na dilin detalye; ontan met ed panagsigay na balyena. Diad sakey a barkon pirata, man-of-war, odino aripen, no say kapitan et akagaud ed anggan iner ed baloto to, sikatoy lanang ya akayurong ed beneg na baloto diad komportable, no maminsan et walay unan ya yurongan diman, tan mabetbet ya imamaneho toy sarili to panamegley na melag a milliner's tiller ya adekoraan na saray lubir tan laso. Balet say whale-boat et anggapoy yurongan tod beneg, anggapoy sopa to, tan anggapoy tiller to. Talagan atagey so panaon, no saray kapitan na panag-ayam na balyena et ipapaliber ed danum ya akalugan ed saray castor a singa saray gouty ya masiken ya alderman ya aka-patent chairs. Tan nipaakar ed sakey a tiller, say whale-boat et agbalot mangawat na antokaman ya ontan ya inka-babai; tan kanian singa diad panag-games et kaukolan ya ontaynan so kompleton tripulante na baloto ed barko, tan lapud say managmaneho na baloto odino manag-harpooneer et kaiba, satan ya sakop so manibela ed saman ya okasyon, tan say kapitan, lapud anggapoy pasen ton yurongan, et iyakar ed bisita to ya akaalagey ya amin a singa sakey a kiew a pino. Tan mabetbet ya naimano yo ya lapud naimano to ray mata na interon nanengneng a mundo ya akabantay ed sikato manlapud gilig na duaran barko, sayan akaalagey a kapitan et mabilay ed kaimportantian na pamasiansia ed dignidad to diad pamasiansia ed saray sali to. Nisay saya so mainomay a bengatla; ta diad beneg to et say baleg ya ontutubo a panagmanibela ya onsasabi ed sikato natan tan insan diad melag a parte na beneg to, say ontumbok ed beneg to et manbabales diad pangipakpak tod saray pueg to ed arap. Kanian sikatoy sigpot ya akapeket ed unaan tan beneg, tan nayarian to labat ya palaknaben so inkasikato ed gilig diad pangiyurong to ed saray akaunat a sali to; balet say biglaan tan maksil ya itatagey na baloto et mabetbet ya ontumba ed sikato, lapud say karukey na pundasyon et anggapoy kakanaan to no anggapoy mipara ed kalapar to.
