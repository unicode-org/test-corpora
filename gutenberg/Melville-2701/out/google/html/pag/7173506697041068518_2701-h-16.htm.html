KAPITULO 81. Say Pequod et Anengneng toy Birhen.

Sinmabi so nigana lan agew, tan akabat mi so barkon Jungfrau, si Derick De Deer, kapitan, na Bremen.

Diad sakey a panaon, saray Dutch tan German so sankamaongan a manag-ayam na balyena ed mundo, natan et kabiangan la ra ed saray sankaabebaan; balet dia tan diman ed malaknab iran interval na latitude tan longitude, sagpaminsan ka nin mankakasakey ed bandera ra diad Pasipiko.

Diad ag-amta a rason, singa labalabay na Jungfrau so mangiter na respeto ed sikato. Nen arawi ni ed Pequod, sikatoy linma, tan inpelag toy sakey a baloto, say kapitan to so inmarap ed sikami, ya agmakaanos ya akaalagey ed saray proa imbes ya diad beneg.

"Antoy walad lima to diman?" inmeyag si Starbuck, ya inturo toy sakey a bengatla ya akaawit na Aleman. "Imposible!—sakey a lamparaan!"

"Aliwan ontan," inkuan nen Stubb, "andi, andi, sakey itan a kaldero na kape, Mr. Starbuck; sikatoy onla pian manggawa na kape tayo, sikato so Yarman; agmo nanengneng so baleg a lata diman ed abay to?—saman so manpabukal a danum to. Oh! sikatoy maong, sikato so Yarman."

"Mila ka," inkelyaw nen Flask, "sakey itan a panagpakan na lamparaan tan sakey a lata na larak. Naupotan lay larak to, tan sikatoy linma a manpapalimos."

Anggano singa makapainteres so sakey a barko na langis ya manguutang na langis ed dalin na balyena, tan anggano ag-itan onkontra ed daan ya proverbio nipaakar ed pangiyakar na baga ed Newcastle, balet no maminsan et talagan nagagawa itan; tan diad kaplesan a kaso, si Kapitan Derick De Deer so talagan nanggawa na lamp-feeder unong ya indeklara nen Flask.

Nen sikatoy onlulugan ed kubyerta, biglan inasinggeran nen Ahab, ya agton balot inimano so walad lima to; balet diad abuyak a salita to, say Aleman so agnambayag et angipatnag na sigpot ya agto pikakabat ed White Whale; tampol ton inyarap so tongtongan ed lamp-feeder tan lata na larak to, tan wala ray imbaga ton nipaakar ed kaukolan ton onloob ed duyan to no labi legan ya ambilunget—say unor lan tedter na larak na Bremen et naupot la, tan anggapo ni anggan sakey a flying-fish ya narel to pian nasuplayan so kakulangan to; diad impangibaga to ya say barko to et talagan tatawagen ya malinis (salanti, anggapoy karga), ya makanepegan na ngaran ya Jungfrau odino Birhen.

Kayari na saray nakaukolan to, tinmaynan si Derick; balet agto naala so gilig na barko to, nen ngalngalin mankakasakey ya inmatagey iray balyena manlapud ulo na palo na duaran barko; tan lapud labalabay nen Derick so panguusil, kanian ag tinmunda pian ilugan so oil-can tan lamp-feeder to, sikatoy linmabas ed baloto to tan tinumbok to ray leviathan lamp-feeder.

Natan, lapud say galaw et inmatagey ed dagem, sikato tan saray arum nin taloran baloton Aleman ya tinmumbok ed sikato, so walaan na baleg a gapoan na saray kilya na Pequod. Walay waloran balyena, sakey a kaslakan a grupo. Lapud amta ra so kapeligroan da, sikaray amin so onkukurang a maples a diretso ed arap na dagem, ya ikukuskos da so saray gilig da a singa karakel na saray kabayon aka-harness. Angitilak ira na baleg tan malaknab a panagbantayan, a singa ra tuloytuloy ya mangibubuskag na baleg a malapar a pergamino ed dayat.

Diad sayan maples ya panagbangon, tan dakel a fathoms ed beneg, et manlangoy so sakey a baleg tan aka-hump ya masiken a baka, ya lapud matantan ya iyaaligwas to, tan lapud saray nikadkaduman duyaw ya inkrustations ya onlelereg ed sikato, et singa walay jaundice to, odino arum nin sakit. No kasin sayan balyena et kabiangan na pod nensaman, singa kuestionable; ta aliwan niyugali parad saratan a gagalangen a leviathan so magmaliw a sosyal. Anggaman ontan, sikatoy nansiansia ed panagbantay da, anggaman talagan say danum ed beneg da so seguradon amatunda ed sikato, lapud say amputin pukel odino ulpo ed malapar a sangi to et sakey a putol-putol, a singa say ulpo a naporma sano mansasabat so duaran manlalaban ya agos. Say sangi to et antikey, matantan, tan mairap; ya ompaway ya walay makapataktakot a klase na uran, tan onlelereg ed saray apilat a piraso, ya tinumbokan na nikadkaduman gulo ed silong na dalin ed sikato, ya singa walay pinmaway ed sananey a niponpon a sampot to, a sengegan na inmepas ed danum ed benegan to.

“Siopa so walaan na paregoric?” inkuan nen Stubb, "walay sakit na eges to, natatakot ak. Katawan, isipen mo pa so pakawalaan na kapalduan acre na sakit na eges to! Saray mauges a dagem so mamapaermen ed sikato, ugugaw. Saya so sankaunaan ya mauges a dagem ya amtak ya onsiplog manlapud astern; balet nengneng yo, wala kasi so whale yaw nensaman? seguradon naandi so tiller to."

Bilang sakey ya Indiaman ya onlelereg ed baybay na Hindostan ya walay napno ton kabayo ya natatakot, saray careens, buries, rolls, tan wallows ed dalan to; ontan met so ginawa na sayan masiken a balyena ya angitagey ed matakken lan laman to, tan no maminsan et manbabaliw ed saray mairap a sampot na taglang to, tan ipaparungtal to so sengegan na inka-malikdo to ed ag-inkatoo na tuod na palikpik to ed kawanan. Mairap ya ibaga no kasin abalang to itan a palikpik ed bakal, odino nianak ya anggapoy ontan.

"Manalagar ka labat na daiset, masiken, tan ikdan ta kay sling parad satan ya asugatan a taklay," inmeyag so maruksan Flask, ya inturo toy whale-line ya asingger ed sikato.

"Isip mo ya ag to ka ipelag ed satan," inmeyag si Starbuck. "Itunda yo, odino sikatoy naala na Aleman."

Diad sakey a getma et amin ya nankakasakey a manbabakal a baloto so akaarap ed sayan sakey a sira, lapud aliwa labat a sikato so sankabalgan, tan sirin say sankamablian a balyena, noagta sikato so sankaasinggeran ed sikara, tan saray arum a balyena so onkukurang a maples, nagkalalo la, a ngalngali la ag-ira manguusil ed saman a panaon. Diad sayan bekta et saray kilya na Pequod so apaltog na taloran baloton Aleman ya unor ya inleksab; balet manlapud marakep ya gapoan to, say baloto nen Derick so siansia nin angidaulo ed panguusil, anggaman kada bekta et onaasingger iray karibal ton dayo. Say tatakotan da labat, et, lapud asingger lad marka to, sikatoy makapan-dart na bakal to antis dan sigpot ya nalampasan tan nalampasan. No nipaakar ed si Derick, singa sikatoy matalek ya ontan so nagawa, tan sagpaminsan ya walay makapabaing ya galaw to et yugyog toy lamp-feeder to ed arum ya baloto.

"Say aso ya andiay panangasi tan andiay pisasalamat!" inmeyag si Starbuck; "inelek-elekan tan papasooten to ak ed samay mairap-bilay a kahon ya inkarga ko parad sikato nen ag limaran minuto so apalabas!"—insan diad daan ya mabiskeg a bulong to—“Itunda yo, greyhound! Aso ed satan!”

"Ibagak ed sikayo no anto itan, lalaki"—inmeyag si Stubb ed saray kakaiba to—"kontra ed relihyon ko so onsyodot; balet labay kon kanen itan a kontrabida a Yarman—Pull—agta ontan? Abuloyan yo kasi itan a maruksa a manalo ed sikayo? Labalabay yo kasi so brandy? Sakey a hogshead na brandy, sirin, ed sankaabigan a too Gala kayo, akin et agkayo manpapasnok? Siopa so angipelag na angkla ed dayat—ag kami onkukurang anggan sakey a pulgada—akapakalma kami Halloo, nia so dika ya ontutubo ed dalem na baloto—tan diad Katawan, say palo diman et ontutubo.

"O! nengnengen yo ray singa gagawaen to!" inmeyag si Flask, ya mansasayaw ed tagey tan leksab—“Agaylan hump—O, do pile ed karne na baka—singa sakey a troso! Oh! saray anak ko, gawaen yo so spring—slap-jacks tan quahogs parad pangdem, amta yo, saray anak ko—luton clams tan muffins—oh, do , do , spring,—sikatoy sanlasus a barreller natan—agyo—ag yo, oh! —nengnengen yo itan ya Yarman—O, ag yo kasi labay so duff!

Diad sayan bekta et si Derick et manpapaspas na lamp-feeder to ed saray onsasabin baloto, tan ontan met ed oil-can to; nayarin walay duaran panmoria ton patundaen so dalan na saray karibal to, tan diad saman met a panaon et napapeles to so ekonomya to diad panamegley na magano lan pangibantak ed beneg.

"Say agmakabat ya Dutch dogger!" inmeyag si Stubb. "Iguyor yo natan, lalaki, so singa limamplon libon linya na barkon panlalaban a karga na saray ambalangan buek a demonyo. Antoy ibaga yo, Tashtego; sika kasi so toon man-snap na duamplo tan duamplon piraso na beklew yo parad kagalangan nen masiken a Gayhead? Antoy ibaga yo?"

"Ibagak, guyoren mo a singa dios-dam,"—inmeyag so Indian.

Lapud masebeg, balet ta parepareho so inkaapag-apag na saray panag-ayew na saray Aleman, say taloran baloto na Pequod so ginmapon nan-rate na ngalngalin mankakasakey; tan, lapud ontan lay inka-disposisyon to, inmasingger ed sikato. Diad saman ya marakep, malukak, tan singa kabalyero ya awawey na pangulo nen onaasingger ed biktima to, saray taloran kapareha so inmalagey a mapaatagey, a sagpaminsan et insuportaan da so ontumbok a managsagwan tekep na makapalikliket ya eyag na, "Diad sikato so onlelereg, natan! Hurrah parad amputin-dapol a dagem! Onleksab kayo ed Yarman! Manlayag kayo ed tapew to!"

Balet ta talagan nandesisyon so orihinal ya gapoan no si Derick so walaan, ya anggaman ed amin ya inka-galante ra, sikatoy apaneknekan komon ya analo ed sayan lumba, no anggapoy matunong a panangukom ya sinmabi ed sikato diad sakey a alimbusabos ya akasabi ed espada na midship oarsman to. Legan ya sayan makulangkulang a lubber et mansasagpot ya ibulos so white-ash to, tan legan ya, bilang resulta, say baloto nen Derick et ngalngali la ontumba, tan sikatoy onkukurong ed saray totoo to diad mabiskeg a sanok;—saman so maabig a panaon parad Starbuck, Stubb, tan Flask. Diad sakey ya inmeyag, sikaray angigapo na mortal ya inyabante, tan inmarap ira ed kuarter na German. Sakey a bekta ni, tan amin ya apatiran baloto et akaarap ed balyena, legan ya onnatnat ed sikara, diad duaran dapag, so manbubula ya daluyon ya ginawa to.

Makapataktakot, makapaermen, tan makapaermen itan a pasingawey. Say balyena et onla lad paway, tan ipapaarap toy sangi to ed unaan to diad tuloytuloy ya mansasakit ya jet; legan ya say sakey a makapuy a palikpik to so manpapaknol ed diking to lapud ot-ot na takot. Natan ed sayan lima, natan ed satan, sikatoy inmawet ed onkukurang a panagbiahe to, tan diad kada daluyon ya onlelereg ed dayat, odino onlelereg ed tawen so sakey a manpapaknol a palikpik to. Kanian anengneng ko so sakey a manok ya apaldua so payak to ya manggagawa na makapataktakot iran apaldua a sirkulo ed dagem, ya andi-kakanaan a mansasagpot a makaliktar ed saray piratan hawk. Balet say manok et walay boses to, tan diad makapaermen iran eyag et ipaamta toy takot to; balet say takot ed sayan baleg ya andi-kakanaan ya ayep ed dayat, so akadena tan aka-enkantado ed sikato; anggapoy boses to, likud ed samay makapakebbiew ya panlinawa to diad panamegley na spiracle to, tan saya so nanggawa ed pakanengneng ed sikato ya makapaermen; legan ya, diad makapakelkelaw ya kabaleg to, portcullis panga to, tan makapanyarin-amin ya ikol to, walay magenap ya makapabigla ed sankabiskegan a too ya makapaermen.

Lapud anengneng to natan ya pigaran bekta labat so mangiter na bentaha ed saray baloto na Pequod, tan imbes ya naandi so galaw to, pinili nen Derick ya i-risk so parad sikato et singa ag-onkulang ya andukey a pana, antis ya makaliktar so unor a pankanawnawa.

Balet ag-abayag et inmalagey so manag-harpooneer to parad stroke, amin ya taloran tigre—Queequeg, Tashtego, Daggoo—so instinct ya inmalagey, tan inmalagey ed sakey a diagonal a hilera, ya inturo da ray barbs da; tan linmoob ed ulo na German harpooneer, say taloran Nantucket irons da et linmoob ed balyena. Makabulag iran singa bula tan amputin apoy! Saray taloran baloto, diad inmunan sanok na impan-apura na balyena, so akabunggo ed saray Aleman ya mabiskeg, kanian si Derick tan say abigla ton manag-harpooneer so atumba, tan linmabas ira ed taloran mantitikyab a kilya.

“Ag kayo natatakot, saray kahon na mantikilya ko,” so inkelyaw nen Stubb, ya onnenengneng ed sikara legan ya onlalabas; "naala kayo la natan—sige—akanengneng ak na pigaran pating ed beneg—saray aso nen St. Bernard, amta yo—manpapaabig ed saray nagogonigon a manbiabiahe. Hurrah! saya so dalan na panaglayag natan. Kada kilya et sakey a sinag na banwa! Hurrah!—Nia so onla tayo a singa taloran lata a kaldero ed ikol na sakey a madgar ed nonot ko! elepante ed sakey a tilbury ed sakey a patar—manggawa na saray wheel-spokes ya ontitikyab ed satan a paraan;

Balet antikey labat so impanbatik na ulsa. Biglan inmepas so panngalngali to, tan sikatoy maingal a nan-ingal. Diad maksil a panag-apura, say taloran linya so tinmakdang ed kaliberliber na saray ulo na troso tekep na ontanlan puersa ya akagawa na aralem iran abot ed saratan; bangta natatakot iray manag-harpoon ya say maples a panag-arpo et mamaupot ed saray linya, kanian diad impangusar da ed amin a biskeg da, et narel da so paulit-ulit a panagsigarilyo ya akabemben ed lubir; anggad diad kaunoran—lapud say perpendicular strain manlapud saray lead-lined chocks na saray baloto, ya diretso so taloran lubir ya linmeksab ed asul—saray gunwales na saray proa et ngalngalin parepareho ed danum, bangta say taloran beneg et akakiling ed tagey. Tan ag-abayag et tinmunda so tanol na balyena, diad loob na pigaran panaon et nansiansia ra ed ontan ya awawey, ta natatakot iran manggastos na mas dakel a linya, anggaman say posisyon da et medyo makapataktakot. Balet anggaman saray baloto so atumba tan naandi ed onian paraan, balet saya so “panbemben,” unong ed tawag ed satan; say inkabit to ed saray matadem a saksak na mabilay a laman to manlapud beneg; saya so mabetbet a mamapairap ed Leviatan pian ontalindeg lamet pian labanan so matadem a gayang na saray kalaban to. Balet agtayo pantongtongan so kapeligroan na bengatla, nepeg ya panduaruwaan no kasin sayan kurso so lawas sankaabigan; ta makatunongan labat ya isipen ya no mas andukey so panaon na panaon na danum ed silong na danum, mas naknaksawan. Lapud, lapud baleg a tapew to—diad sakey a matatken lan sperm whale ya kulang na 2000 square feet—say pressure na danum et baleg. Amta tayon amin no antoy makapakelkelaw ya belat na atmospera ya aarapen tayo; anggan dia, diad tagey na dalin, diad dagem; agaylan kabaleg, sirin, so pabelat na sakey a balyena, ya awit to ed beneg to so sakey a lusek a duaran lasus a fathom na dayat! Nepeg ya ag-onkulang ed belat na limamplon atmospera. Inkalkula na sakey a manag-balyena ya manbelat itan na duamplon barkon panlalaban, a walaan na amin a paltog, tan tindaan, tan lalakin akalugan.

Legan ya akarukol diman so taloran baloto ed satan a kalmadon onkukurang a dayat, a manbabantay ed andi-anggaan ya asul a pegley to; tan singa anggapoy anggan sakey ya ungol odino eyag na antokaman a klase, andi, anggan sakey a daluyon odino bula so pinmaway manlapud kaaraleman to; antoy isipen na too, ya diad silong na amin ya kareenan tan kareenan, say sankagrabian ya ulsa ed dayat et manpapasnok tan manpapasnok ed ot-ot! Anggapoy waloran pulgada na akaalagey ya lubir ya nanengneng ed saray proa. Ompatnag a napanmatalkan a diad panamegley na taloran manipis a sinulid et say baleg a Leviatan so akabitin a singa say baleg a belat ed waloran agew a relo. Sinuspendi? tan ed anto? Diad taloran piraso na tabla. Saya kasi so pinalsa ya aminsan et matalona ya inkuan—“Nayarian mo kasi ya panoen so baog to na saray balatyang a balatyang? odino say ulo to na saray gayang a sira? Say kampilan na samay onsasabi ed sikato et ag makabemben, say gayang, say pana, odino say balkonahe: ipapasen toy balatyang a singa dayami, say pana agto nayarian a gawaen a singa dayami so balatyang; diad panyegyeg na gayang!” Saya so pinalsa? saya sikato? Oh! ya saray ag-inkayadyari et nepeg ya ontumbok ed saray propeta. Ta diad biskeg na sanlibon pueg ed ikol to, inyamot nen Leviatan so ulo to ed silong na palandey na dayat, pian iyamot ed saray gayang na sira na Pequod!

Diad saman ya onlelereg ya sinag na agew ed ngarem, saray anino ya impaway na taloran baloto ed dalem na danum, et seguradon andukey tan malapar pian nasalimbengan so kapaldua na armada nen Xerxes. Siopay makapangibaga no panon kataktakot ed samay asugatan a balyena so angkakabaleg iran multo ya onlelereg ed tagey na ulo to!

"Manalagar kayo, lalaki; sikatoy onkikiwas," inmeyag si Starbuck, legan ya biglan onyeyegyeg so taloran linya ed danum, ya malinew ya mangipapaarap ed sikara, a singa panamegley na saray magnetic wires, na saray tiktik na bilay tan patey na balyena, kanian nalilikna iratan na balang sakey a managsagwan diad yurongan to. Diad tinmumbok a bekta, a nainawaan a maong lapud onlelereg iran proa, saray baloto so biglan tinmunda ed tagey, a singa gagawaen na melag a yelo, sanen say makapal a pulok na amputin oso so tinmakot manlapud satan ya amaarap ed dayat.

"Iloob mo! Iloob mo!" inmeyag lamet si Starbuck; “sikatoy ombangon.”

Saray linya, ya, ag-abayag et ag-onkulang so kalapar na sakey a lima, et wala la natan ed andukey tan maples iran coil ya inbantak ya amin et onlelereg ed saray baloto, tan agnambayag et say balyena so akapeket na danum ed loob na duaran barko na karukey na saray manag-anop.

Say galaw to et malinew ya mangipapanengneng na alablabas ya inka-upot to. Diad maslak ya ayayep ed dalin et wala ray pigaran balbula odino flood-gate ed dakel ed saray ugat da, ya no nasugatan, say dala et tampol ya onsara ed pigaran direksion. Aliwan ontan so balyena; sakey ed saray nikadkaduma to et say pakawalaan na interon non-valvular ya istruktura na saray ugat, kanian no natusok na anggan melag labat a punta a singa say harpoon, say makapatey a panag-agus so tampol ya onggapo ed interon arterial system to; tan no saya so napaatagey na ekstraordinaryon presyon na danum ed arawi ed leksab na tapew na dalin, say bilay to so nayarin nibaga ya onlelereg manlapud sikato diad ag-ontutundan ilog. Balet ta ontan lay karakel na dala ed sikato, tan ontan lay kaarawi tan karakel na saray subol to ed loob, kanian sikatoy mantultuloy a manpadugo tan manpadugo ed loob na andukey a panaon; singa diad kelang et onagus so sakey ya ilog, a say lapuan to et diad saray subol na ararawi tan agnanengneng iran pukdol. Anggad natan, nen saray baloto so angiguyor ed sayan balyena, tan mapeligron inmamot ed saray onkukurong a balyena to, tan saray gayang so inmamot ed sikato, sikara so tinumbokan na saray malet a jet manlapud balon ginawan sugat, a tuloytuloy a manpapatogtog, bangta say natural a spout-hole ed ulo to so wala labat ed saray panaon, anggan panon lay kapeles to, ya mangibabaki ed makapataktakot a danum to ed dagem. Manlapud sayan unor ya abot et anggapo ni so pinmaway a dala, lapud anggapo ni so importantin kabiangan to ya atumba. Say bilay to, unong ed tawag da ed satan, et ag-apektado.

Nen mas asingger lan apaliberan na saray baloto, say interon bandad tagey na laman to, ya maslak ed satan et kaslakan ya onlelereg, et malinew ya napatnagan. Saray mata to, odino saray pasen a kawalaan na saray mata to, so anengneng. No panon ya mantitipon iray nikadkaduman ag-inmabobon a karaklan ed saray abut na sankamaongan ya oak no manpapasnok ira, ontan met ed saray pasen ya datin kawalaan na mata na balyena, natan et wala ray bulag ya bombilya, ya makapaermen ya nanengneng. Balet anggapoy panangasi. Diad amin ya inkatatken to, tan say sakey a taklay to, tan saray bulag a mata to, sikatoy nepeg ya ompatey tan pateyen, pian naliwawaan iray bakla a nobya tan arum niran panliliket na lalaki, tan pian naliwawaan met iray solemnen simbaan ya mangipupulong na andi-kondisyon ya ag-inkasakit na amin ed amin. Manpapasnok ni ed dala to, diad kaunoran et anengneng to so sakey a nikadkaduman kolor na bunton odino protuberance, a singa kabaleg na sakey a bushel, ya abeba ed gilig.

"Maong a pasen," inmeyag si Flask; "abuloyan mo ak labat ya tusok diman na maminsan."

“Avast!” inmeyag si Starbuck, "agla kaukolan itan!"

Balet say makatoon Starbuck et atrasado la. Diad bektan inpaltog na dart et sakey a ulcerous jet so pinmaway manlapud sayan maruksan sugat, tan sikatoy angitonton ed satan ed mas ni nen say ag-onkulang ya ot-ot, say balyena natan et manbubuga na makapal a dala, tekep na maples a sanok ya bulag ya inmamot ed barko, ya amawala ed sikara tan ed saray makapadinayew a tripulante ra ed amin a pasen na saray uran na dala, ya amatumba ed baloto nen Flask tan amawala na saray pana. Saya so impatey ton stroke. Ta, diad sayan panaon, sikatoy naupot la lapud inkaandi na dala, kanian sikatoy andiay nagawaan ya inmalis manlapud aderal ya ginawa to; akarukol ya manpapasnok ed diking to, ya agmakayarin manpapaknol ed ag-inkayadyari na palikpik to, insan paulit-ulit ya kalkalnan onliliber a singa onkakapuy a mundo; inwalwal to ray amputin sekreto na eges to; akarukol a singa troso, tan inatey. Makapaermen itan, samay unor ya onsasaew ya spout. Singa no saray ag nanengneng a lima et kalkalnan naala so danum manlapud sakey a mabiskeg a subol, tan tekep na kapalduan ag-onkikiwas a maermen a panag-ungol say spray-column so onleleksab tan onleleksab ed dalin—ontan so unor ya andukey lan ompatey a sangi na balyena.

KAPITULO 82. Say Galang tan Gloria na Panag-balyena.

Wala ray pigaran negosyo ya say maalwar ya ag-inkayadyari so peteg a paraan.

No lalo ak ya ontutumbok ed sayan pamaakaran na panag-ayam na balyena, tan itulak ko ray panagsukimat ko anggad gapoan to, lalo ak ya mandinayew ed baleg a kagalangan tan inkadaanan to; tan lalo la no makaromog ak na dakel a baleg a demi-dirios tan bayani, propeta na amin a klase, ya diad sakey a paraan odino sananey et angipatnag na inkadumaan ed satan, siak so napapalikdo ed panagnonot a siak a mismo so kabiangan, anggaman balet ta sakop, na ontan lan inka-emblazoned a fraternity.

Say matapang a si Perseus, ya anak nen Jupiter, so sankaunaan ya manag-awit na balyena; tan diad andi-anggaan a kagalangan na tawag tayo et nibaga, ya say inmunan balyena ya inataki na kaagian tayo et ag pinatey ed antokaman a marutak a getma. Saraman so panaon na kabalyero ed propesyon mi, nen man-awit kami labat na armas pian tulongan iray nagogonigon, tan aliwan pian panoen iray lamparaan na totoo. Amta na balang sakey so marakep ya istorya nen Perseus tan Andromeda; no panon ya say marakep ya Andromeda, ya anak na sakey ya ari, so akagaton ed sakey a bato diad gilig na dayat, tan legan ya sikatoy awiten nen Leviathan, si Perseus, say prinsipe na saray manag-awit na balyena, so makpel ya inmabante, tan inala to so ulsa, tan inyabuloy tan inasawa to so katulong. Sakey itan a makapadinayew ya artistikon agawaan, ya matalag a nagawaan na saray sankamaongan a manag-harpooner natan; lapud sayan Leviatan et inatey ed sankaunaan a pana. Nen inala na saray Romano so Joppe, satan met lanlamang ya pukel so inawit ed Italya bilang pananalo. Niarum ni, mas baleg so nabawasan to ed gloria na impansamantala no si St. George et akaabet na sakey a mankukurongkurong ya ayep ed dalin, imbes ya milaban ed baleg ya ulsa ed aralem a dayat. Siopaman a too et nayarin mamatey na uleg, balet say Perseus labat, say St. George, say Coffin, so walaan na puso ya makpel ya onla ed sakey a balyena.

Agtayo komon palikdoen na saray modernon painting ed sayan eksena; ta anggaman say pinalsa ya abet na saman a matapang a balyena nensaman et singa-griffin so itsura to, tan anggaman say bakal et nipanengneng ed dalin tan say santo ya akalugan ed kabayo, balet no isipen so baleg ya ag-inkaamta ed saraman a panaon, sanen say tuan itsura na balyena et ag-amta na saray artist; tan no isipen ya singa ed kaso nen Perseus, say balyena nen St. George et nayarin kinmamang ed dayat diad gilig na dayat; tan no isipen ya say ayep ya luluganan nen San Jorge et nayarin sakey labat a baleg a seal, odino kabayo ed dayat; no nonoten irayan amin, ag-itan ompatnag ya agmitunosan ed sagradon alamat tan saray sankadaanan ya draft na eksena, so pangibemben ed sayan tatawagen a dragon ya anggapoy arum noag say baleg a Leviathan a mismo. KAPITULO 83. Si Jonas ya Ipapasen ed Kasaysayan.

Diad akaunan kapitulo et tinukoy so makatantandan istorya nen Jonas tan say balyena. Natan et wala ray taga-Nantucket ya agmanmamatalek ed sayan makatantandan istorya nen Jonas tan say balyena. KAPITULO 84. Pitchpoling.

Pian mainomay tan maples iran ombatik, saray ehe na karwahe et nilanaan; tan parad parehon gagala, wala ray manag-awit na balyena ya manggagawa na mipadparan operasyon ed baloto ra; mangrasa ira ed leksab. Si Queequeg so manisia a maong ed panaglana ed baloto to, tan sakey a kabuasan ya ag abayag kayari na inkaandi na barkon Aleman a Jungfrau, sikatoy nansagpot a maong nen say kaslakan ed satan a trabaho; onkukurong ed silong na leksab to, ya akabitin ed gilig, tan mankukuskos ed ag-inka-mantika a singa maseet ya manaanap na buek manlapud kalbo na bapor. Sikatoy singa mankikimey bilang katutulok ed sakey a partikular a panmoria. Agmet itan nansiansian ag-inkatunongan na ebento.

Nen bandad ugto et pinabaleg iray balyena; balet nen linmeksab so barko ed sikara, sikara so linmingaw tan binmatik a maples; sakey a magulon panagbiahe, a singa saray barko nen Cleopatra manlapud Actium.

Anggaman ontan, intuloy na saray baloto, tan say baloto nen Stubb so sankaunaan. Lapud baleg a sagpot, tinmalona si Tashtego ed kaunoran ya angitanem na sakey a bakal; balet say atumba ya balyena, ya ag-onkikiwas, et nantultuloy nin siansia ed pabitay to, ya walay kaaruman ya inka-apura to. Say ontan ya ag-ontutundan panag-ipit ed intanem a bakal et nepeg ya diad magano odino mabayag et agnapaliisan ya naekal itan. Nagmaliw ya importante so pangibantak ed ontitikyab a balyena, odino mankontento ed pakaandi to. Balet ta agto nayarian ya iguygoy so baloto ed gilig to, sikatoy maples tan mapasnok ya linmangoy. Anto sirin so atilak?

Diad amin ya makapakelkelaw iran kagawaan tan inkadunong, saray inkadunong na lima tan agnabilang iran masilib a bengatla, ya mabetbet a napilitan na beteranon manag-balyena, anggapo so onlampas ed satan a maabig a panag-obra ed gayang a tatawagen a pitchpoling.
