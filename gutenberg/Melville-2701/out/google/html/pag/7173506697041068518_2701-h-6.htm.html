Balet lapud sarayan baboy-fish et maingal, makapadimla, a maslak et mankukurongkurong ed sangi na ilog, tan mamangan na basa-basa a dayami, tan lalo la ta ag-ira man-sput, iburi ko so kredensyal da bilang balyena; tan inter da ed sikara iray pasaporte ra pian makaalis ira ed Kingdom of Cetology.

FOLIOS. Kaiba ed saraya so ilaktip ko dia iray onggendan a kapitulo:—I. Say Sperm Whale ; II. say Kawanan a Balyena ; III. say balyena na palikpik ; IV. say Balyena ya walay ulpo to ; V. say Razor Back Whale ; VI. say Sulphur Bottom Whale .

LIBRO I. ( Folio ), KAPITULO I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Sayan balyena, ed limog na saray Ingles nensaman ya aliwan malinew ya kabkabat bilang say Trumpa whale, tan say Physeter whale, tan say Anvil Headed whale, so kaplesan ya Cachalot na saray Pranses, tan say Pottsfich na saray Aleman, tan say Longphalus na saray Words. Sikato so, andi-duaruwan, sankarakelan a manaayam ed mundo; say sankagrabian ed amin ya balyena ya nanengneng; say sankatalonggaringan ed aspekto; tan unor, say sankamablian ed komersyo; sikato labat so pinalsa ya nanlapuan na satan ya mablin substansia, say spermaceti. Amin ya nikadkaduma to et, diad dakel niran pasen, et napalaknab. Say ngaran to so kaukolan kon gawaen natan. No ikonsidera ed pilolohiko, anggapoy kakanaan to. Pigaran siglo lay apalabas, nen say Sperm whale et ngalngali ag ni kabat ed dilin inkatoo to, tan nen say larak to et aksidentin naala ed samay aka-stranded ya sira; nen saman ya panaon, say spermaceti et singa nanlapud sakey a pinalsa ya mipara ed samay kabkabat ed England bilang say Greenland odino Right Whale. Saya met so ideya, ya sayan parehon spermaceti so makapapeles ya humor na Greenland Whale ya literal ya ibabalikas na unonan silaba na salita. Diad saraman a panaon, say spermaceti et matalag met, ya ag-itan uusaren parad liwawa, no ag ingen bilang ointment tan tambal labat. Naala labat itan ed saray druggist ta natan et mansasaliw kay sakey onsa na rhubarb. Nen, unong ed opinyon ko, diad ilalabas na panaon, naamtaan so tuan inkatoo na spermaceti, say orihinal a ngaran to et nansiansia ni ed saray managlako; andi-duaruwan mamaaligwas ed kablian to diad panamegley na sakey a kanonotan a nikadkaduman makabkabaliksan ed inkakulang to. Kanian say ngaran et nepeg ya niiter ed balyena ya talagan nanlapuan na sayan spermaceti.

LIBRO I. ( Folio ), KAPITULO II. ( Right Whale ).—Diad sakey a paraan, saya so sankagalangan ed saray leviathan, ta sikato so sankaunaan ya regular ya aanapen na too. Mangiter itan na artikulo ya kaslakan ya tatawagen ya whalebone o baleen; tan say larak ya kabkabat bilang “larak na balyena,” sakey ya abeban bengatla ed komersyo. Diad saray managsigay, sikatoy tatawagen na sarayan titulo: Say Balyena; say Greenland Whale; say Black Whale; say Baleg a Balyena; say Tuan Balyena; say Dugan Balyena. Walay daiset ya ag-amta nipaakar ed pakabidbiran na saray klase na ayayep ya amayamay ya abautismoan. Anto sirin so balyena, ya inlaktip ko ed komaduan klase na balyena ko? Saya so Great Mysticetus na saray English naturalists; say Greenland Whale na saray English whaleman; say Baleine Ordinaire na saray French whalemen; say Growlands Walfish na saray Swedes. Saya so balyena ya masulok lan duaran siglo so apalabas ya aanapen na saray Olandes tan Ingles diad dayat na Arctic; saya so balyena ya abayag lan aanapen na saray managsigay ya Amerikano diad Indian ocean, diad Brazil Banks, diad Nor' West Coast, tan diad nanduruman pasen ed mundo, ya tatawagen dan Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Wala ray mankunkunwari ya walay nanenengneng dan pandumaan na Greenland whale na saray Ingles tan say right whale na saray Amerikano. Balet ta eksakton mankakasakey ira ed amin ya angkakabaleg iran kabiangan da; anggapo ni so nipresenta ya sakey a determinadon katuaan ya pangibasiyan na radikal a pandumaan. Diad panamegley na andi-anggaan iran subdibisyon base ed saray sankagrabian a pandumaan, so pigaran departamento na natural ya awaran so nagmaliw a makapadimlan komplikado. Say kawanan a balyena et pantongtongan ed arum a pasen ed pigaran karukey, a manutukoy ed pangipaliwawa ed balyena na semilya.

LIBRO I. ( Folio ), KAPITULO III. ( Fin-Back ).—Diad sayan ulo et ipapasen ko so sakey a ulsa ya, diad nanduruman ngaran a Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, tan Long-John, et anengneng la ed ngalngalin amin a dayat tan kaslakan et say balyena ya say arawin jet to et mabetbet ya dedeskribien na saray pasahero ya ombabaliw ed Atlantiko, diad saray packet-track na New York. Diad karukey ya naabot to, tan diad baleen to, say Fin-back et mipara ed kawanan a balyena, balet ta aliwan tanton ambelat so girth to, tan mas malimpek so kolor to, ya asingger ed olibo. Say angkakabaleg iran bibil to et singa kable ya itsura, ya aporma na saray mankakasakey tan manpapaspas a tiklop na angkakabaleg iran kulubot. Say makapadinayew a kabiangan to, say palikpik, a nanlapuan na ngaran to, so mabetbet a mapatnag a bengatla. Sayan palikpik et manga talora o apatiran mitro so karukey to, ya ombabaleg ya akaalagey manlapud beneg na beneg, singa anggulo so kurti to, tan walay matalimbeng ya sampot to. Anggano anggapoy anggan melag a parte na pinalsa ya nanengneng, sayan nisulinek a palikpik et no maminsan et nanengneng ya onlelereg manlapud tapew na dalin. No say dayat et medyo kalmado, tan medyo walay marka ton malimpek iran daluyon, tan sayan singa-gnomon a palikpik so onalagey tan mangipelag na anino ed malimpek a tapew, nayarin ipasen a say danum a sirkulo ya akapaliber ed satan et singa dial, a walaan na estilo tan maalon iran linya na oras ya inukit ed satan. Diad satan ya Ahaz-dial et mabetbet ya ompawil so anino. Say Fin-Back et aliwan gregarious. Sikatoy singa mamubusol ed balyena, ta arum a lalaki et mamubusol ed too. Mabaing a tuloy; lanang ya manbukbukor; ag-iilaloan ya ontatdang ed tapew na danum diad sankaarawian tan sankaermenan a danum; say diretso tan saksakey ya atagey a jet to et ontatagey a singa atagey a misanthropic a gayang ed sakey ya anggapoy tanaman a patar; walaan na makapakelkelaw a pakayari tan kapeles ed panaglangoy, ya agto nalabanan so amin a kaplesan a pananap na too; sayan leviathan et singa samay pinataynan tan agnatalo ya Cain na rasa to, ya walay marka to ed satan ya estilo ed beneg to. Lapud walad sangi to so baleen, say Fin-Back et no maminsan et kaiba na right whale, kaiba ed sakey a teoretikon klase na ayayep ya tatawagen ya Whalebone whales , salanti, saray balyena ya walay baleen da. Diad sarayan tatawagen ya Whalebone whales, singa wala ray pigaran klase, balet maslak ed saratan et daiset so kabat. Saray balyena ya malapar so eleng da tan balyena ya walay sangi ra; balyena ya walay ulo na pike; nankakasakey iran balyena; saray balyena ya walad silong na panga tan saray balyena ya walay luko, so ngaran na saray managsigay parad pigaran klase.

Nipaakar ed sayan apelasyon na “Whalebone whales,” importante ya bitlaen, ya anggano kombeniente so ontan a nomenklatura pian napagaan so pangibaga ed arum a klase na balyena, balet ta andi-kakanaan so panggunaet a malinew a klasipikasyonen so Leviathan, a nibase ed baleen, odino hump, odino palikpik, odino ngipen to; anggaman saratan a markaan a parte odino tampok so mapatnag a mas mapatnag a mas akaadapta pian nawalaan na basiyan parad regular a sistema na Cetology nen say antokaman niran nikadkaduman pandumaan na laman, ya ipaparungtal na balyena, diad saray klase to. Panon sirin? Say baleen, hump, back-fin, tan ngipen; saraya so bengatla ya saray nikadkaduman nikadkaduma ra et nitaytayak ed amin a klase na balyena, ya anggapoy pibabali ra no antoy nayarin inkagawa na istruktura ra ed arum tan mas importantin detalye. Kanian, say sperm whale tan say humpbacked whale, et balang sakey et walay ulpo to; balet diman et ontunda so panangipara. Insan, say parehon humpbacked whale tan say Greenland whale, balang sakey ed saraya et walay baleen to; balet diman lamet et tinmunda so panangiparaan. Tan pareho met ed arum ya parte ya asalambit ed tagey. Diad nanduruman klase na balyena, manggagawa ira na ontan iran ag-regular a kombinasyon; odino, no walay sakey ed sikara ya nisian, ontan ya ag-regular ya inkaapag-apag; bilang sigpot ya onsusumpa ed amin ya inkalapagan a metodisasyon ya ginawa ed ontan a basiyan. Diad sayan bato et apagpag so balang sakey ed saray whale-naturalists.

Balet nayarin isipen ya, diad loob na balyena, diad anatomy to—diman, anggan panon et naala tayo so dugan klasipikasyon. Andi; antoy bengatla, alimbawa, ed anatomy na Greenland whale ya mas makapadinayew nen say baleen to? Balet anengneng tayo ya diad panamegley na baleen to et imposiblin susto so pangiklasipika ed Greenland whale. Tan no onlasur ka ed saray pait na nanduruman leviathan, akin et agmo naromog diman iray pandumaan na koma-limamplon kabiangan ya walad sistematizer a singa saramay walad paway ya abitla la. Anto sirin so natilak? anggapoy arum no ag say pangala ed saray balyena, diad interon liberal a karakel da, tan makpel ya i-sort iratan ed ontan a paraan. Tan saya so sistema na Bibliograpiya ya inadapta dia; tan satan labat so posiblin ontalona, ​​ta satan labat so nagawaan. Pian makapantultuloy.

LIBRO I. ( Folio ) KAPITULO IV. ( Hump Back ).—Sayan balyena et mabetbet ya nanengneng ed mamabukig ya baybay na Amerika. Sikatoy mabetbet ya narel diman, tan iguguyor ed daongan. Sikatoy walaan na baleg a pakete a singa sakey a managlako; odino nayarin tawagen mo ya Elepante tan Castle whale. Antokaman so nagawa, say popular a ngaran to et agmakapangibiig ed sikato, lapud say sperm whale et walaan met na ulpo anggaman mas melag. Aliwan tanton mabmabli so larak to. Walay baleen to. Sikato so sanka-games tan sanka-light-hearted ed amin ya balyena, ya mas dakel so gagawaen ton gay foam tan white water nen say arum ed sikara.

LIBRO I. ( Folio ), KAPITULO V. ( Razor Back ).—Nipaakar ed sayan balyena et daiset so amta noag say ngaran to. Anengneng ko ed arawi ed Cape Horn. Lapud sikatoy manretiro, sikatoy agmakaliktar ed saray manag-anop tan saray pilosopo. Anggaman aliwan matakot, agto nin balot impanengneng so antokaman a kabiangan to noag say beneg to, ya ontatagey ed andukey tan matarem a pukdol. Paulyanan mo la. Daiset labat so amtak nipaakar ed sikato, tan anggapo met so amtak na arum.

LIBRO I. ( Folio ), KAPITULO VI. ( Sulphur Bottom ).—Sakey nin manretiro lan laki, ya singa asupre so eges to, so seguradon akagamor na panag-scrape ed saray baldosa na Tartarian diad pigaran mas aralem iran panag-dive to. Matalag ya nanengneng; anggan panon et agko nin balot anengneng likud ed arawin dayat ed abalaten, insan lanang ya arawi pian aralen so lupa to. Sikatoy agbalot nausil; sikatoy ombatik ya walay lubir ton linya. Saray prodigy so nibaga nipaakar ed sikato. Adieu, Sulphur Bottom! Anggapo lay nibagak ya tua nipaakar ed sika, tan agmet nayarin ibaga na sankatatkenan ya taga-Nantucketer.

( Grampus ).—Anggaman sayan sira, ya say maksil ya panlinawa to, odino say panlinawa to, et nagmaliw ya proverbio ed saray taga-dalin, et kabkabat a manaayam ed aralem a dayat, balet ta ag-itan ipapasen na saray balyena. LIBRO II. ( Octavo ), KAPITULO II. ( Black Fish ).—Iiter ko so ngaran na saray sikat a managsigay parad amin na sarayan sira, ta diad inkalapagan et sikara so sankaabigan. No walay ngaran ya aliwan malinew odino agmakapangibalikas, ibagak itan, tan mangisuheri ak na sananey. Gagawaen ko itan natan, ya diwiten so Black Fish, tatawagen da, lapud say inkaandi so totontonen ed ngalngalin amin ya balyena. Kanian, tawagen mo ya Hyena Whale, no labay mo. Kabkabat so inka-boracity to, tan lapud saray anggulo na bibil to et akakurba ed tagey, sikatoy walaan na andi-anggaan ya Mephistophelean ya ngisi ed lupa to. Sayan balyena et manpromedio na manga labinanem odino labinwalon mitro so karukey to. Sikatoy naromog ed ngalngalin amin a latitud. Walay nikadkaduman paraan to ya ipanengneng so dorsal hooked fin to legan ya manlangoy, ya singa eleng na Romano. No ag-ira mas makagunggona ed trabaho, saray manag-anop na sperm whale et no maminsan et mangaala na Hyena whale, pian napansiansia so suplay na baraton larak parad trabaho ed abung—lapud pigaran matipid a manag-asikaso na abung, ya anggapoy kaiba ra, tan bukbukor da, so manpopoolan na agmakapaliket a taba imbes ya mabanglon wax. Anggaman manipis so taba ra, arum ed sarayan balyena et makapangiter na masulok ya talomplon galon na larak.

LIBRO II. ( Octavo ), KAPITULO III. ( Narwhale ), salanti, Nostril whale .—Sakey nin alimbawa na sakey a balyena ya makapainteres so ngaran to, ya ontan so inngaran to lapud say nikadkaduman saklor to et inmuna ya inisip ya walay toktok to. Say pinalsa et manga labin-anem a mitro so karukey to, bangta say saklor to et manpromedio na limaran mitro, anggaman arum so onlampas ed samplora, tan onabot ni ingen ed labinliman mitro. Diad estrikton panangibaga, sayan saklor et sakey labat ya pinarukey a ngipen, ya ombabaleg manlapud panga diad sakey a linya ya medyo akapeket manlapud pabitay. Balet ta naromog labat itan ed makapadimlan dapag, ya walay mauges ya epekto to, ya mangiiter ed akankayarian to na bengatlan mipara ed itsura na sakey a makulangkulang a kawigin lima. Mairap ya ibaga no antoy eksakton gagala na sayan saklor odino lansa ya gawad garing. Ompatnag ya ag-itan nausar a singa say espada na sira tan bill-fish; anggaman ibabaga na arum a marino ed siak ya uusaren itan na Narwhale bilang rake diad pangibalikas tod dalem na dayat parad naakan. Inkuan nen Charley Coffin ya inusar itan parad ice-piercer; ta say Narwhale, ya ontatdang ed tapew na Polar Sea, tan aromog ton napno na yelo, intagey toy sungay to, kanian sikatoy onloob. Balet agmo napaneknekan ya duga so anggan dinan ed sarayan panag-isip. Say opinyon ko et, anggan panon et talagan usaren na Narwhale iyan one-sided horn—anggan panon itan—seguradon kombeniente itan ed sikato parad sakey a folder ed panagbasa na saray pamphlet. Say Narwhale et nadngel kon tatawagen dan Tusked whale, Horned whale, tan Unicorn whale. Sikatoy talagan sakey a makapainteres ya alimbawa na Unicornism ya naromog ed ngalngalin amin a panarian na saray mabilay a palsa. Manlapud pigaran daan ya autor et naala ko ya sayan saklor na sea-unicorn et nen saman et ipapasen ya sankaabigan ya tambal sumpad samal, tan lapud satan, say panagparaan ed satan et baleg so bili to. In-distill met itan pian magmaliw ya ag-onkupas ya asin parad saray bibiin manpapasnok, a singa met ed panggawa na saray saklor na kalakian ya usa pian magmaliw ya hartshorn. Diad gapo et saya so ipapasen a sakey a bengatla ya pan-usisaan a maong. Ibabaga na Black Letter ed siak ya nen pinmawil si Sir Martin Frobisher manlapud saman ya biahe, nen si Queen Bess so matapang ya angiwagayway ed sikato na alahas ya lima to manlapud bintana na Greenwich Palace, legan ya manlalayag so makpel a barko to ed Thames; "nen pinmawil si Sir Martin manlapud saman a biahe," inkuan nen Black Letter, "ya akayurong so pueg to et inter to ed katageyan to so sakey a makapadinayew ya andukey a saklor na Narwhale, ya diad andukey a panaon et akasabit ed kastilyo ed Windsor." Inkuan na sakey ya autor ya taga-Ireland ya say Earl of Leicester, ya akayurong, et angiter met ed inkaatagey to na sananey a saklor, a nipaakar ed sakey ya ayep ed dalin ya singa unicorn.

Say Narwhale et walaan na marakep a itsura, singa leopardo, a singa gatas ya amputin kolor to, a walaan na malimpek tan andukey iran kolor ya andeket. Say larak to et mas atagey, malinew tan pino; balet daiset labat itan, tan sikatoy matalag ya aanapen. Sikatoy maslak ya naromog ed saray circumpolar ya dayat.

DUODECIMOES.—Kaiba ed saraya iray angkekelag a balyena. I. Say Huzza Porpoise. II. Say Algerine Porpoise. III. Say Mealy-mouthed Porpoise.

Parad saramay ag ni akapanaral ed sayan tema, nayarin singa nikadkaduma, ya saray sira ya ag-onlampas ed apatiran odino limaran mitro et nepeg ya i-marshal ed limog na saray BALYENA—sakey a salita, ya, diad popular a pantalos, et lanang ya mangipapasabi na ideya na kabaleg. LIBRO III. ( Duodecimo ), KAPITULO 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Saya so kaslakan ya porpoise ya naromog ed ngalngalin interon mundo. Say ngaran et nanlapud dilin inter ko; ta wala ray masulok ya sakey a klase na porpoise, tan walay kaukolan ya gawaen pian nibiig iratan. Ontan so tawag ko ed sikato, lapud sikatoy lanang ya manlangoy ed makapaliket iran gilig na dayat, ya diad malapar a dayat et mantutultuloy ya onlelereg ed tawen a singa saray kalo ed sakey a karaklan ed Fourth-of-July. Say itsura ra et kaslakan ya panliliketan na saray marino. Lapud napno ira na maong ya espiritu, sikaray naynay ya manlalapud maples iran daluyon ya anggad dagem. Sikara so ugugaw a lalaki ya lanang ya manbibilay ed arap na dagem. Ipapasen iran masuwerten tanda. No sika a mismo so makasungdo ed taloran panliket ed pakanengneng ed sarayan mabilabilay a sira, sirin say tawen so ontulong ed sika; say espiritu na maridios a panag-ayew anggapo ed sikayo. Say naakan tan mataba ya Huzza Porpoise et mangiter ed sika na sakey a galon na maong a larak. Balet say maong tan malimpek a pluido ya naala ed saray panga to et alablabas so kablian to. Saya so kekerewen na saray alahas tan managgaway relo. Iiyan itan na saray marino ed saray hone da. Say karne na porpoise et maong ya kanen, antam. Nayarin agmo nin balot inisip ya walay porpoise ya man-south. Tua, say sangi to et melag kanian ag tanton nanengneng. Balet diad ontumbok ya pankanawnawam, bantayan mo; tan nanengneng mo so baleg ya Sperm whale ya mismon melag.

LIBRO III. ( Duodecimo ), KAPITULO II. ( Algerine Porpoise ).—Sakey a pirata. Maruksa. Sikatoy naromog labat, iisipen ko, diad Pasipiko. Mas baleg nen say Huzza Porpoise, balet parehoy ginawa to. Papasnok mo, tan sikatoy onkurong ed sakey a pating. Aminpigak lan inmepas parad sikato, balet agko nin balot anengneng ya sikatoy narel.

LIBRO III. ( Duodecimo ), KAPITULO III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Say sankabalgan a klase na Porpoise; tan naromog labat ed Pasipiko, anggad amta. Say ngaran to labat ed Ingles, ya anggad natan et say ngaran na saray managsigay—Right-Whale Porpoise, lapud sikatoy manunan naromog ed kaliberliber na satan a Folio. Diad itsura to, sikatoy midumaan ed Huzza Porpoise, ta aliwan tanton malimpek tan maliket so laman to; tua, sikatoy medyo mauksoy tan singa ginoo. Anggapoy palikpik to ed beneg to (maslak ed arum ya porpoise et walaan), marakep so ikol to, tan sentimental iran mata na Indian ya kolor hazel. Balet say mealy-sangi to et maneral ed amin. Anggaman say interon beneg to ya anggad gilig na palikpik to et singa aralem a sable, balet walay linya na ketegan to, ya midumaan ed marka ed kasko na barko, ya tatawagen ya “bright waist,” satan a linya so mangigugulis ed sikato manlapud sengeg ya anggad beneg, ya walaan na duaran mandumaan a kolor, andeket ed tagey tan amputi ed leksab. Say amputi et manlalapud kabiangan na ulo to, tan say interon sangi to, ya singa sikatoy akatakas la manlapud sakey a mauges ya imbisita ed sakey a meal-bag. Sakey a sankagrabian tan sankagrabian ya aspekto! Say larak to et mipara ed ordinaryon porpoise.

Diad labas na DUODECIMO, sayan sistema et ag ontuloy, lapud say Porpoise so sankamelagan ed saray balyena. Diad tagey, walad sika so amin ya Leviathan ya kabkabat. Balet wala ray ag segurado, takas, kapalduay makapadinayew iran balyena, ya, bilang sakey ya Amerikanon balyena, kabat ko ed reputasyon, balet aliwan personal. Ibilang ko ra unong ed saray tawag da ed arap na kastilyo; ta posiblin say ontan a listaan ​​et nayarin makana ed saray arapen ya imbestigador, ya nayarin makasumpal ed walad siak dia balet ta inggapo ko la. No siopaman ed sarayan balyena, so narel tan markaan, sirin et sikatoy mainomay ya ilaktip ed sayan Sistema, unong ed Folio, Octavo, odino Duodecimo magnitude to:—Say Bottle-Nose Whale; say Junk Whale; say Pudding-Headed Whale; say Cape Whale; say Manunan Balyena; say Cannon Whale; say Scragg Whale; say Coppered Whale; say Balyena ya Elepante; say Iceberg Whale; say Quog Whale; say Blue Whale; tan arum nira. Manlapud saray autoridad na Icelandic, Dutch, tan old English, nayarin wala ray inaon ya arum nin listaan ​​na saray ag seguradon balyena, ya abendisyonan na amin a klase na ag-inkahustisya iran ngaran. Balet ta inekal ko iratan bilang ag la nausar; tan ag naiwasan ya suspetsaen iratan ya singa labat tanol, napno na Leviathanism, balet anggapoy kabaliksan da.

Kaunoran: Nibaga la ed gapo, ya sayan sistema et ag-onsabi, tan tampol ya naperpekto. Agmo nayarian ya ag nanengneng a malinew ya tinoor ko so salitak. Balet natan et itilak ko so sistemak na setolohiko ya akaalagey ya agni asumpal, a singa say baleg a Katedral na Cologne ya atilak, a say crane so akaalagey ni ed toktok na agni asumpal a tori. Ta saray angkekelag ya impaalagey et nayarin nasumpal na saray inmunan arkitekto ra; saray angkakabaleg, saray tua, et ag da la itaynan so copestone ed saray kailalakan.
