Ta, lapud karakel na saray balyena; say ag-inkauksoy a paraan na inkaapag-apag da ed interon kaliberliber na danum, a dakel ed sikara so magunaet a mangitutulak ed panaanap da ed saray latitud, kanian matalag odino agbalot diad loob na labinduan bulan odino onsulok ni, so makaabet na anggan sakey a layag a mangibabaga na balita na antokaman a klase; say alablabas a karukey na kada nanduruman biahe; say ag-inkaregular na panaon na panaglayag manlapud abung; amin na saraya, tan arum niran sirkumstansya, direkta tan agdirekta, so abayag ya mangaamper ed ikakayat na saray espisyal ya balita nipaakar ed Moby Dick ed interon mundon manag-ayam na balyena. Anggapoy panduaruwaan ya pigaran barko so nireport ya akaabet, ed ontan odino ontan a panaon, odino diad ontan odino ontan a meridian, na sakey a Sperm Whale ya aliwan kaslakan a kabaleg tan inkagrabe to, a say balyena, kayari ton nanggawa na baleg a kaugsan ed saray manag-atake ed sikato, so sigpot a makaliktar ed sikara; parad arum ya kanonotan et aliwan agmakatunongan ya panmoria, kuan ko, ya say balyena ya tutukoyen et seguradon aliwan si Moby Dick. Balet diad kaplesan et say panagsigay na Sperm Whale et atandaan na nanduruman tan aliwan matalag ya inkagawa na baleg a inka-agum, inka-masilib, tan inka-maruksa ed samay ulsa ya inataki; kanian, saramay ag-akaamta ya akilaban ed si Moby Dick; saratan a manag-ayam, nayarin, diad maslak a kipapasen, so kontento lan mangibaga ed nikadkaduman takot ya inyanak to, a singa panangibaga, ed saray kapeligroan na panagsigay na Sperm Whale ed inkalapagan, nen say indibidual a sengegan. Diad ontan, maslak, say makadesyang ya impitongtong nen Ahab tan say balyena et ipapasen na karaklan.

Tan nipaakar ed saramay, akauna lan akadngel nipaakar ed White Whale, et ag-inilaloan ya akanengneng ed sikato; diad gapo na bengatla et walaan ira na balang sakey ed sikara, ngalngali, singa makpel tan andi-takot ya inyabeba parad sikato, a singa parad antokaman nin balyena ed satan a klase. Balet diad kaunoran, ontan iran kalamidad so agawa ed sarayan panangataki—aliwa labat ed saray apilat a pulso tan bikking, apilat iran sali, odino makapataktakot iran imputasyon—noag ingen makapatey anggad sampot a grado na ipapatey; saraman ya paulit-ulit ya makadesyang ya inkaapag-apag, ya amin da et mantitipon tan mantitipon na takot da ed si Moby Dick; saratan a bengatla so amaknol ed biskeg na dakel a makpel a manag-anop, a diad kaunoran et akasabian da so istorya na White Whale.

Nisay saray makapataktakot a tsismis na amin a klase so ag-inmabobon, tan lalon makapataktakot so peteg iran awaran na sarayan makapatey ya engkwentro. Ta aliwa labat ya saray makapadinayew a tsismis so natural ya ontubo manlapud mismon laman na amin a makapabiglan makapataktakot iran ebento, —singa say kiew ya atumba et mangiyanak na saray fungi to; balet, diad bilay ed dayat, mas ni nen say diad terra firma, dakel so makapataktakot iran tsismis, inerman so kawalaan na antokaman a magenap a katuaan parad sikara. Tan no panon ya say dayat et onlampas ed dalin ed sayan pamaakaran, ontan met so panagsigay na balyena ya onlampas ed amin a klase na bilay ed dayat, diad makapakelkelaw tan makapataktakot iran tsismis ya no maminsan et onlelereg diman. Ta aliwan saray whalemen labat ya singa sakey a laman so ag naliklikas ed satan ya inka-ignorante tan superstisyon ya atawir ed amin a marino; balet diad amin a marino, sikara so sankadirektan akapitongtong ed antokaman a makapataktakot ed dayat; arap-arapan et aglabat da nanenengneng iray sankabalgan a pakelaw to, noagta, lima ed panga, milaban ira ed sikara. Bukor mo, diad sankaarawian iran danum, ya anggano manlayag ka na sanlibon milya, tan linmabas ka ed sanlibon gilig na dayat, ag ka makasabi ed antokaman a pinaltog a bato na pugon, odino antokaman a makapasagyat ed silong na satan a parte na banwa; diad ontan iran latitude tan longitude, ya ontutumbok ed ontan a tawag a singa sikato, say manag-awit na balyena et abalotan na saray impluensya ya amin so onkikiwas a mamalukon ed panlabayan to na dakel a mabiskeg a kianak.

Agla pankelawan, sirin, ya lapud lanang ya mantitipon na karakel manlapud basta labat panagbiahe ed sankalaknaban iran danum, saray ag-inmabobon a tsismis nipaakar ed White Whale so diad kaunoran et angilaktip ed inkasikara na amin a klase na makapadimlan pakapatnagan, tan kapalduan-porman fœtal iran suhestion na supernatural iran ahensia, ya diad kaunoran et angiter ed Moby Dick na balon agnanengneng iran makapataktakot a nanenengneng manlapud antokaman a nanengneng. Kanian diad dakel a kaso et ontan so inkataktakot to, ya pigpigara ed saratan a tsismis, anggan panon et akadngel la nipaakar ed White Whale, pigpigara ed saraman a manag-anop so mabulos a mangarap ed saray kapeligroan na panga to.

Balet wala ni ray arum tan mas importantin praktikal ya impluensya ya onkikiwas. Anggan diad kaplesan et say orihinal a kagalangan na Sperm Whale, a makapataktakot a nibiig ed amin ya arum a klase na leviathan, et anggapo ni ed kanonotan na saray whalemen bilang sakey a laman. Wala ray totoo natan ed limog da, ya anggaman matalino tan makpel iran mibakal ed Greenland odino Right whale, nayarin—lapud ag-inkaeksperiensya ed propesyon, odino ag-inkayadyari, odino inka-matakot, et ag-ira milaban ed Sperm Whale; antokaman so nagawa, dakel so manag-awit na balyena, lalo la ed saramay bansan manag-awit na balyena ya agmanlalayag ed silong na bandera na Amerika, ya agnin balot akaabet na Sperm Whale, balet ta say pikakabat da labat ed leviathan et say agmakatunongan ya ulsa ya inusilan da ed Bukig; ya akayurong ed saray abot da, sarayan lalaki et ondengel tekep na singa ugaw ya interes tan pankelaw ed abay na apoy, ed saray atap, nikadkaduman salaysay nipaakar ed panag-ayam na balyena ed Abalaten. Say sankatalonggaringan ya inkabaleg na baleg a Sperm Whale et anggapo met so mas nalilikna nen say walad loob na saray prow ya mamapaarap ed sikato.

Tan singa say natan et nasubok lan katuaan na biskeg to so diad datin panaon na alamat et angibuntok na anino to ed arap to; wala ray naturalist ed libro—si Olassen tan Povelson—ya mangibabagan say Sperm Whale et aglabat makapabigla ed amin ya pinalsa ed dayat, no ag ingen sikatoy makapasinagem ya masebeg ya tuloytuloy ya napgaan ed dala na too. Anggan anggad panaon nen Cuvier, saraya odino ngalngalin mipadparan impresyon et agla naandi. Ta diad Natural History to, inkuan na Baron a mismo ya diad pakanengneng ed Sperm Whale, amin a sira (kaiba la ray pating) et “napapagaan na sankabiskegan iran takot,” tan “mabetbet a diad inkapeles na itatikya ra et ontupak ira ed saray batobato a singa ra tampol ya ompatey.” Tan anggaman ontan, saray inkalapagan ya eksperiensya ed panagsigay so nayarin manguman ed saray report a singa saraya; balet diad sigpot a inkataktakot da, anggan diad samay makapataktakot a bengatla nen Povelson, say superstitious ya sisiaen ed sikara et, diad pigaran pananguman na bokasyon da, et nabilay lamet ed kanonotan na saray manag-anop.

Kanian lapud abigla ed saray tsismis tan pakapatnagan nipaakar ed sikato, pigara ed saray managsigay so nannonot, nipaakar ed Moby Dick, ed saray inmunan agew na panagsigay na Sperm Whale, sanen mabetbet a mairap so pangisugsog ed saray abayag lan mansasagpot a Right whalemen pian onloob ed saray kapeligroan na sayan balon tan makpel a bakal; saratan a lalaki so manproprotesta ya anggaman wala ray arum a leviathan ya nailaloan ya nausil, balet say panguusil tan pangituro na pana ed ontan ya aparisyon a singa say Sperm Whale et aliwan parad mortal a too. No salien itan, et seguradon nabuyak ed maples ya andi-anggaan. Diad sayan uloan, wala ray pigaran makatantandan dokumento ya nayarin nengnengen.

Anggaman ontan, wala ray arum, ya anggan diad arap na sarayan bengatla et akaparaan iran mangusil ed si Moby Dick; tan mas dakel ni so bilang da, ya ag da labay ya narengel so nipaakar ed sikato diad arawi tan ag malinew, ya anggapoy espisipikon detalye na antokaman a kalamidad, tan anggapoy saray superstitiyoson kaiba, et magenap ya ag makabatik ed bakal no iyapay.

Sakey ed saray makapataktakot ya suhestion ya tinukoy, ya diad kaunoran et nisiglaot ed White Whale diad kanonotan na saray totoon walaan na superstisyon, et say agmakatunongan ya panagpaatagey ya say Moby Dick et walad amin a pasen; ya sikatoy talagan akabat ed nanduruman latitude ed sakey tan parehon bekta na panaon.

Nisay, anggano makapanisia iratan a kanonotan, sayan panagpaatagey so sigpot ya anggapoy pigaran makapuy a pakapatnagan na superstisyoson posibilidad. Ta singa saray sekreto na saray agos ed dayat et agnin balot niparungtal, anggan diad sankatalonggaringan a panagsukimat; kanian saray akaamot a dalan na Sperm Whale no walad silong na tapew et mansiansia, diad baleg a kabiangan, ya ag-ebatan na saray manguusil ed sikato; tan panapanaon et nanlapuan iray sanka-interesan tan sankakontraan iran espekulasyon nipaakar ed sikara, lalo la nipaakar ed saray mistikon paraan a ditan, kayari na panag-awit to ed baleg a kaaralem, sikatoy mangiyakar ed inkasikato tekep na ontan lan kapeles ed sankaarawian iran pasen.

Saya so kabkabat na saray barkon balyena na Amerika tan Ingles, tan ontan met ed sakey a bengatla ya inyan ed rekord nen Scoresby pigaran taon lay apalabas, ya wala ray balyena ya narel ed arawin amianen na Pasipiko, ya diad saray bangkay da et wala ray anengneng ya harpoon ya inbantak ed dayat na Greenland. Agmet nepeg ya ibaliwala ya diad arum ed sarayan inkagawa et indeklara ya say baetan na duaran panangataki et ag nayarin onlampas ed dakel ya agew. Kanian, diad konklusyon, panisiaan na arum a manag-awit na balyena, a say Nor' West Passage, ya abayag lan problema ed too, et agbalot nagmaliw a problema ed balyena. Kanian dia, diad peteg ya eksperiensya na mabilay a totoo, saray prodigies ya insalaysay nensaman ed loob na palandey Strello ed Portugal (ya asingger ed toktok to et walay sakey a lawak ya ditan et onleletaw iray aderal a barko ya onleletaw ed tapew na danum); tan say mas makapakelkelaw nin istorya na subol na Arethusa ya asingger ed Syracuse (ya say danum to et papanisiaan ya nanlapud Masanton Dalin diad sakey a dalan ed silong na dalin); sarayan makapadinayew a salaysay so ngalngalin sigpot a mipara ed saray katuaan na saray balyena.

Napilitan ya mikabat, sirin, ed saray prodigy a singa saraya; tan amta ton kayari na paulit-ulit tan makpel ya impangataki, say White Whale et akatakas a mabilay; ag la pankelawan no akin ya wala ray manag-balyena ya onla ni ed saray superstisyon da; ibabaga ton say Moby Dick et aliwan amin a pasen, no ag ingen ag ompatey (ta say ag ipapatey et walad amin a pasen ed panaon); ya anggano saray kakiewan na gayang so nitanem ed saray gilig to, sikatoy onlangoy nin siansia ya anggapoy sakit to; odino no talagan sikatoy napapaway na makapal a dala, say ontan a pakanengnengan et sakey labat a makapataktakot a panagpalikdo; ta diad saray agnaparaan a daluyon a nilasus a liga so kaarawi to, say ag-abalaten ya jet to so nanengneng lamet.

Balet anggano naandi la irayan supernatural ya panag-isip, wala so magenap ed mangaraldalin a inkagawa tan agnasuppiat a karakter na ulsa pian nasabi so imahinasyon na agnaparaan a pakayari. Ta, aliwan say nikadkaduman kabaleg to so mangibiig ed sikato manlapud arum iran sperm whale, noagta, singa ed arum a pasen ya imbantak—sakey a nikadkaduman singa-niebe ya amputin kulot a moling, tan sakey ya atagey, singa-piramid ya amputin ulpo. Saraya so manunan kabiangan to; saray token ya anggan diad anggapoy limitasyon tan agnaamtaan iran dayat, imparungtal toy inkatoo to, diad arawi, ed saramay mikabat ed sikato.

Say arum nin parte na laman to et walaan na saray gulis-gulis, tan mantsa, tan marmol a walaan na parehon kolor ya akatapew, kanian, diad kaunoran, sikatoy akagamor na nikadkaduman ngaran ton White Whale; sakey a ngaran, a tua, so literal a makatunongan lapud mabilabilay ya itsura to, sanen anengneng a manpapaspas ed atagey a pegley diad maimpis ya asul a dayat, a mangitilak na milky-way wake na creamy foam, ya amin et mankirlap na balitok iran kinmiwas.

Aliwan say agnaparaan a kabaleg to, odino say makapadinayew a kolor to, odino say depormadon abeban panga to, so amawala na natural a takot ed balyena, noagta samay agnaparaan, intelihentin inkauges ya, unong ed espisipikon salaysay, et paulit-ulit ton impanengneng ed saray panag-atake to. Mas ni nen say amin, saray makapalikdon impan-atras to so mas amaermen nen say antokaman. Ta, legan ya manlangoy ed arap na saray manliliket ya manguusil ed sikato, tekep na amin ya ompatnag a sintomas na alarma, sikatoy aminpiga lan kabkabat a biglan onliliber, tan, diad ipelag to ed sikara, balanglan nabuyak iray baloto ra, odino ipawil to ra ed barko ra a natatakot.

Pigaran inatey la so inmatendi ed panguusil ed sikato. Balet anggaman saray mipadparan kalamidad, anggan panon lay daiset ya agawa ed gilig na dayat, et aliwan nikadkaduma ed panagsigay; balet, diad maslak a kipapasen, singa ontan so impierno ya inisip na White Whale nipaakar ed inka-maruksa, ya kada panag-apag o panagpatey to et ag-ipapasen ya ginawa na sakey ya agmakabat ya ahente.

Ukomen, sirin, no antoy inkaapag-apag na saray mas desperadon manag-anop to, nen diad pegley na saray tipak na saray baloton nan-chew, tan saray onlelereg a sali na saray apilat a kakaiba, sikaray linmugan manlapud saray amputin curds na makapataktakot a sanok na balyena ya amaarap ed mareen, makapasinagem a sinag na banwa, ya inmimis ed sakey a nobya, a singa ed sakey a nobya.

Say taloran baloto to so onkukurang ed kaliberliber to, tan saray sagwan tan lalaki so parehon onkukurong ed saray alimbusabos; sakey a kapitan, ya inala toy line-knife ed aperdin prow to, so binmatik ed balyena, bilang sakey ya Arkansas duellist ed kalaban to, ya bulag ya manaanap na anemiran pulgadan espada pian naabot so bilay na balyena. Saman ya kapitan et si Ahab. Insan to biglan inawit nen Moby Dick so singa karit ya panga to ed silong to, tan inani toy sali nen Ahab, a singa sakey a manag-ani na dika ed uma. Anggapoy turbante ya Turko, anggapoy upaan ya Venetian o Malay, ya makapatey ed sikato na mas singa kaugsan. Kanian, anggapoy rason pian panduaruwaan ya manlapu lad saman ya ngalngali la makapatey ya impitongtong to, si Ahab et nanliket ed samay balyena, lalo la ta sikatoy akapibiang ed sikato, aliwan amin ya sakit to labat ed laman, noagta amin ya intelektual tan espiritual ya inkaaburido to. Say White Whale so linmugan ed arap to bilang say monomaniac ya inkarnasyon na amin na saraman ya mauges ya ahensia ya nalilikna na arum ya aralem a totoo ya kakanen da, anggad sikaray natilak a manbibilay a walaan na kapalduan puso tan kapalduan baga. Say ag nanengneng ya kaugsan ya wala la manlapud gapo; ya anggan saray modernon Kristiano et mangiiter na kapaldua na mundo ed pananguley to; ya saray kadaanan ya Ophite ed bukig et igagalang da ed estatua ran demonyo;—Ag-inmabeba si Ahab tan nandayew ed satan a singa sikara; balet ta inyalis toy ideya to ed samay makapadimlan white whale, inyarap toy sarili to, ya amin et apaldua, sumpad satan. Amin ya makapaermen tan makapairap; amin a mamapakiwas ed saray bengatla; amin a katuaan a walaan na kaugsan; amin ya mamapaermen ed saray ugat tan mamapaermen ed utek; amin ya masilib ya demonismo na bilay tan kanonotan; amin ya kaugsan, parad buanges ya si Ahab, et mapatnag ya inpersona, tan ginawan praktikal ya naataki ed Moby Dick. Insan, diad impan-dart to ed samay ulsa, ya sankabembenan toy kutsilyo, sikatoy bigla labat ya inmabobon ed sakey a too; tan nen sikatoy na-stroke ya amasakit ed sikato, nayarin alikna to labat so makapasinagem a sugat ed laman to, balet anggapo lay mas ni. Say report na agnasuppiat ya inka-delirium to ed dayat et inkuan met na karaklan ya mipara ed satan. Tan ontan met, amin ya kaaruman ya inka-moodi ya naynay kayari to, anggad mismon agew na panaglayag to ed Pequod ed kaplesan ya biahe, et akayurong ya mannononot ed moling to. Aliwan tanton posibli, ya arawi ed agpanmatalek ed inkasusto to parad sananey a panaglayag na balyena, lapud ontan iran ambilunget a sintomas, saray mankuenta a totoo ed satan a maalwar ya isla so walaan na tendensia a manpasirayew, a lapud saratan a mismon rason et sikatoy mas kualipikado tan akapeket, parad sakey a pananap a napno na sanok tan inka-wild a singa say panag-anop na saray balyena. Nagatin-gatin ed loob tan napoolan ed paway, tekep na saray ag-ontutundan ngipen na pigaran agnatambal ya ideya; say ontan, no sikatoy naromog, et singa sikatoy mismon too ya mangipaltog na balatyang to tan mangitagey na gayang to sumpad sankagrabian ed amin ya ayayep. Odino, no diad antokaman a rason et isipen ya sikatoy agmakayari ed laman parad satan, balet ta say ontan a too et singa superlatibon makayarin manliket tan man-ungol ed saray sakop to ed panangataki. Balet antokaman so nagawa, seguradon lapud say sekreto na ag-ontutundan sanok to et akapeket tan akapeket ed sikato, ginagala nen Ahab so nanlayag ed kaplesan a biahe tekep na saksakey tan makapasagyat a gagala a say pananap ed White Whale. No walay sakey ed saray datin kabat to ya walad gilig na dayat balet ta kapaldua labat so mankukugip ed no antoy manaamot ed sikato nen saman, panon kalaknab ya naala na saray makapataktakot tan matunong iran kamarerwa so barko manlapud ontan a makapataktakot a too! Sikaray akasentro ed saray makagunggona iran biahe, say tubo et bilangen ed dolyar manlapud mint. Sikatoy malabay a manggawa na makpel, agnaparaan, tan supernatural a panagbales.

Nia, sirin, so uban, agmaridios a masiken, ya manguusil ed sakey a balyena nen Job ed intiron mundo, diad ulo na sakey a tripulante, met, a manunan tugyopen na saray mongrel renegades, tan saray abastos, tan saray kanibal —a kinmapuy met ed moral, lapud ag-inkayadyari na birtud ya anggapoy tulong odino ag-inkahustisya na Starll, say ag-inka-interes tan ag-inkamaalwar ed Stubb, tan say kasmak ya inka-ordinaryo ed Flask. Say ontan ya tripulante, ya opisyal, et singa espisyal ya pinili tan inkarga na pigaran makapataktakot ya ipapatey pian natulongan to ed monomaniac ya panagbales to. No panon ya inmebat ira ed sanok na masiken—diad antokaman a mauges a mahiko so inkaapag-apag na kamarerwa ra, a no maminsan et singa ngalngali la ra manbubusol ed sikato; say White Whale et singa met ed agnasungdoan ya kalaban da; no panon ya agawa irayan amin—no antoy kabaliksan na White Whale ed sikara, odino no panon ed saray ag da naimano ya pakatalos da, ontan met, diad sakey a malimpek, ag-iisipen a paraan, sikatoy nayarin singa onlelereg a baleg a demonyo na dayat na bilay, — no panon ya ipaliwawa irayan amin, et singa onlelereg ed mas aralem nen say nayarian nen Ishmael. Say minero ed silong na dalin ya mankikimey ed sikatayo ya amin, panon ya naamtaan no iner so laen na baras to diad pirmin manguuman tan ag-onkukurang ya tanol na pick to? Siopa so agmakalikna na ag-onsuppiat a panag-awit na taklay? Anton baloto ya aka-tow na pitomplo tan apat so makaalagey ya ag-onkikiwas? Sakey, inyawat koy inkasiak ed inkaandi na panaon tan pasen; balet legan ya amin et manaapura pian naabet so balyena, anggapoy anengneng da ed satan ya ayep no ag say sankagrabian ya sakit.





KAPITULO 42. Say Kaputi na Balyena.

No antoy kabaliksan na balyena ya amputi ed si Ahab, et nibaga la; no anto, no maminsan, so inkatoo to ed siak, anggad natan et agni nibaga.

Likud ed saray mas mapatnag ya konsiderasyon nipaakar ed si Moby Dick, ya ag nayarin panapanaon ya mamawala na alarma ed kamarerwa na siopaman a too, wala ni sananey a kanonotan, odino aliwan malinew, andiay ngaran a takot nipaakar ed sikato, a no maminsan lapud inkagrabe to et sigpot ton natalonaan so amin ya arum; balet ta ontanlan misteryoso tan ngalngali agnasabi itan, kanian ngalngali ak la nadismaya ya mangiyan ed satan ed natalosan a porma. Say inkaputi na balyena so amabigla ed siak. Balet panon kon nailaloan ya nipaliwawa so inkasiak dia; balet, diad sakey a malimpek, random a paraan, ipaliwawak so inkasiak a kaukolan ko, ta no andi et nayarin andi-kakanaan irayan amin a kapitulo.

Anggaman diad dakel a natural a bengatla, say inkaputi so mamaaligwas ed karakpan, a singa mangiiter na nikadkaduman birtud to, a singa saray marmol, japonica, tan perlas; tan anggaman nanduruman bansa so akabidbir ed sakey a klase na inka-ari ed sayan kolor; anggan saray barbaro, angkakabaleg ya arari na Pegu ya mangiiter na titulon “Katawan na saray Amputin Elepante” nen say amin ya arum nin makapadinayew ya panangibaga ra na pananguley; tan saray modernon arari na Siam ya mangibubuntok na parehon amputin singa niebe ya apatiran sali ed estandarte na ari; tan say bandera na Hanoverian ya walay sakey a pigura na singa-niebe ya amputin karga; tan say baleg ya Empiryo na Austria, si Cæsarian, ya mananawir ed Roma, ya walaan na parehon kolor na imperyo; tan anggaman sayan inkatalonggaring ed satan et onaplika ed mismon rasa na too, ya mangiiter ed amputin too na ideyal a pakauley ed kada makapuy a tribu; tan anggaman, nilikud ed amin na saya, say inka-amputi et nagmaliw ya makabkabaliksan ed liket, ta diad limog na saray Romano et sakey ya amputin bato so mamaneknek ed maliket ya agew; tan anggaman diad arum a mortal a simpatya tan simbolo, sayan parehon kolor so ginawan simbolo na dakel a makapasagyat, noble iran bengatla—say inka-inosente na saray nobya, say inka-mapangasi na edad; anggaman diad limog na saray Red Men of America say pangiter na amputin sinturon na wampum so sankarakpan a sipan na kagalangan; anggaman diad dakel a klima, say inkaputi so mangipapanengneng na inkabantog na Hustisya ed ermine na Ukom, tan ontutulong ed inagew-agew a kipapasen na saray arari tan reyna ya iguguyor na saray amputin kabayo a singa gatas; anggaman anggan diad saray atagey a misteryo na sankatageyan a relihyon et ginawa itan a simbolo na madibinon inkaandi-mantsa tan pakayari; na saray Persianon managdayew na apoy, say amputin tinidor ya ngalab so sankasantosan ed altar; tan diad saray mitolohiya na Griego, say Baleg a Jove a mismo so nagmaliw a too ed sakey a toro ya amputin singa niebe; tan anggaman parad saray noble ya Iroquois, say bagat ed kapegleyan na panaoy ambetel na sagradon White Dog so sankasantosan a piesta na teolohiya ra, satan ya andiay mantsa, matoor a pinalsa so ipapasen a sankapuroan ya ibabaki ra ed Baleg ya Espiritu tekep na tinaon a balita na dilin katooran da; tan anggaman direktan nanlapud Latin a salita parad amputi, amin a Kristianon pari et nanlapud ngaran na sakey a kabiangan na sagradon kawes da, say alb odino tunika, ya isulong ed silong na cassock; tan anggaman kaiba ed saray masanton kagalangan na pananisia na saray Romano, say amputi so espisyal ya uusaren ed selebrasyon na Pasyon na Katawan tayo; anggaman diad pasingawey nen San Juan, saray amputin kawes so niiter ed saray dinondon, tan saray duamplo tan apat a mamasiken so akaalagey ya akakawes na amputi ed arap na baleg ya amputin trono, tan say Masanto ya akayurong diman ya amputi a singa bagoy karnero; balet diad amin na sarayan atipon ya asosasyon, ed antokaman a masamit, tan mapagalang, tan makapadinayew, wala ni so sakey a bengatla ya ag natalosan ed sankaaraleman ya ideya na sayan kolor, a mas makapataktakot ed kamarerwa nen say ambalanga ya makapataktakot ed dala.

Sayan ag natalosan a kalidad so sengegan na kanonotan na inka-amputi, no nisian ed mas maomaong iran kaulop, tan itipon ed antokaman a makapataktakot ed inkasikato, pian napaaligwas itan a takot anggad sankaarawian a ketegan. Nanengneng so amputin oso ed saray poste, tan say amputin pating ed saray tropiko; anto no ag say makinis, mankibalatar ya kaputi ra so manggagawa ed sikaran makapataktakot a singa ra? Say makapataktakot ya inka-amputi so mangiiter na makapadimlan inkaulimek, a mas makapadimla nen say makapataktakot, ed ag-inkayadyari na itsura ra. Pian aliwan say masebeg a tigre ya aka-heraldic coat to so makapangiter na pakpel a singa say white-shrouded ya oso odino pating.*

*Nipaakar ed Polar bear, nayarin ipaseseg na samay malabay ya onloob ni ed sayan pamaakaran, ya aliwan say inka-amputi, ya ipapasen ya nikadkaduma, so mamapaatagey ed ag-itepel ya inka-maruksa na satan ya ayayep; ta, no analisaen, say inmatagey ya inka-makapaermen, nayarin ibaga, et manlalapud sirkumstansya, ya say iresponsablen inka-maruksa na pinalsa et akaalagey ed bago na inka-inosente tan aro; tan lapud satan, diad pangitipon na duaran mankontraan ya emosyon ed kanonotan tayo, say Polar bear et mamataktakot ed sikatayo na ag-inkaugalian a pandumaan. Balet anggano ipasen tayon tua irayan amin; balet, no aliwan lapud inka-amputi, agmo nawalaan na ontan ya lalon takot.

Nipaakar ed amputin pating, say amputin manpapaspas ya aswang na satan a pinalsa, no nanengneng ed ordinaryon kipapasen to, et nikadkaduman mipara ed parehon kalidad na Polar quadruped. Sayan nikadkaduma so sankapatnagan ya anengneng na saray Pranses diad ngaran ya inter da ed satan a sira. Say misa na saray inaatey ed saray Romish et onggapo ed “Requiem eternam” (andi-anggaan a painawa), a ditan et say Requiem et mankabaliksan na misa a mismo, tan antokaman nin musika ed ponpon. Natan, diad pangipabitar ed amputi, mareen ya kareenan na ipapatey na sayan pating, tan say ag-inkayadyari na saray ugali to, sikatoy tatawagen na saray Pranses ya Requin .

Isipen pa so albatross, iner so nanlapuan na saraman a lurem na espiritual a pankelaw tan amputin takot, a ditan et manlalayag itan ya amputin aswang ed amin ya imahinasyon? Aliwan si Coleridge so inmunan angibantak ed satan ya anting-anting; balet say baleg, agmakapaliket a premyo na Dios, say Kalikasan.*

*Nanonotan ko ni so sankaunaan ya anengneng kon albatros. Legan na andukey a bagyo, diad saray danum ya asingger ed dayat na Antarctic. Manlapud panagbantay ko ed ngarem ed leksab, tinmatdang ak ed alablabas a kubyerta; tan diman, ya akasabi ed saray manunan puerta, anengneng ko so sakey a marakep, singa bago ya anggapoy mantsa ya amputi, tan walay akasabit, Romanon sangi ya makapadinayew. Diad panapanaon, inarko to so angkakabaleg iran payak na arkanghel to, a singa mangaabrasa ed sakey a masanton kaban. Saray makapakelkelaw a panag-ayew tan panag-uyog so amayegyeg ed satan. Anggano ag-asakitan so laman to, sikatoy angipaway na eyag, a singa sakey ya aswang na ari ya walad supernatural ya gonigon. Diad panamegley na agnabalikas, nikadkaduman mata to, inisip ko ya akanengneng ak na saray sekreto ya akasabi ed Dios. Singa si Abraham ed arap na saray anghel, dinmakmomo ak; say amputin bengatla et amputi, saray payak to et malapar, tan diad saraman ya andi-anggaan a danum, naandi so makapaermen iran pakanodnonotan ed saray tradisyon tan saray baley. Abayag kon ninengneng itan ya makapadinayew ya buek. Agko nayarian ya ibaga, nayarian ko labat ya ipanengneng, iray bengatlan linmabas ed siak nen saman. Balet diad kaunoran et binmangon ak; tan inmarap, tinepetan toy sakey a marino no anton manok iya. A goney, ebat to. Naandi la! ag ko nin balot nadngel itan a ngaran nensaman; naisip kasi ya sayan makapadinayew a bengatla et ag kabat na totoo ed gilig na dayat! agbalot! Balet kayari na pigaran panaon, naamtaan ko ya say goney et ngaran na sakey a marino parad albatross. Kanian anggapoy posibilidad ya say wild Rhyme nen Coleridge et walay koneksion to ed saraman ya mistikon impresyon ko, nen anengneng ko iman ya manok ed kubyerta mi. Ta agko ni abasa so Rhyme ed saman, tan agko amta ya say manok et sakey ya albatross. Balet, diad pangibagak ed saya, agko direktan ipapaliwawa so marakep a merito na anlong tan say manag-anlong.

Ibabagak sirin ya diad makapakelkelaw ya inkaputi na laman na manok et manunan akaamot so sekreto na anting-anting; sakey a katuaan ya lalon napatnagan ed saya, ya diad panamegley na solecism na saray termino et wala ray manok ya tatawagen ya grey albatrosses; tan saraya so mabetbet kon anengneng, balet agbalot walaan na ontan iran emosyon a singa nen anengneng ko so manok ed Antarctic.

Balet panon ya narel so mistikon bengatla? Ag mo ibubulong, tan ibagak; walay makapalikdon kawit tan linya, legan ya onleletaw so manok ed dayat. Diad kaunoran et ginawa itan na Kapitan a kartero; mangigaton na letra, katat a tally ed beklew to, a walaan na oras tan pasen na barko; insan to paulyanan ya ontakas. Balet agko panduaruwaan, ya say katat ya tally, ya parad too, et naekal ed tawen, nen tinmekkiab so amputin manok pian mila ed saray manpapaknol na payak, manpipikasi, tan mandayew ed saray kerubin!

Say sankabantog ed saray Western annals tan tradisyon na Indian et samay White Steed of the Prairies; sakey a marakdakep a singa gatas ya amputin kargamento, angkakabaleg so mata to, melag so ulo to, singa say pagew to, tan walay dignidad na sanlibon ari ed atagey tan makapabaing a karwahe to. Sikato so pinilin Xerxes na dakel a pulok na saray atap a kabayo, a saray panagpastolan da ed saraman a panaon et aliber labat na Rocky Mountains tan Alleghanies. Diad mandarlang ya ulo ra et inyarap to itan ed sagur a singa samay pinilin bitewen ya kada labi et mangitonton ed saray armada na liwawa. Say mankirlap a kaskad na buek to, say kurbadon kometa na ikol to, so angiter ed sikato na saray ayaman a mas mankirlap nen say nayarin iter ed sikato na saray manag-awit na balitok tan pilak. Sakey a sanka-imperiyal tan sanka-arkanghelikal ya impanengneng na saman ya ag-ontumba, mamasagur a mundo, ya diad pakanengneng na saray daan a manag-ayam tan manag-anop et amabilay lamet ed saray kagalangan na saraman ya inmunan panaon sanen si Adan so nanakar a makapadinayew a singa sakey a dios, a singa say mabiskeg a kabayo tan anggapoy takot to. Balanglan manmartsa ed pegley na saray katulongan tan marshal to diad van na agnabilang iran grupo ya ag-ontutundan onaagus ed saray patar, a singa sakey ya Ohio; odino no kasin diad saray manliber-liber ya sakop to ya manbabantay ed kaliberliber diad abot, say White Steed et maples ya manrerepaso ed sikara ya saray ambetel ya eleng to et manpapasnok lapud ambetel ya inka-gatas to; antokaman ya aspekto so pangipapanengneng tod sarili to, sikatoy lanang ed saray sankamakpel ya Indian ya pangigalangan tan pankelawan. Agmet nayarin ikuestion manlapud no antoy walad maawaran a rekord na sayan noble a kabayo, ya say espiritual ya inka-amputi to so manunan angitarok ed sikato na inka-Dios; tan sayan inka-Dios so walaan na satan ya anggaman mangigaganggan na panagdayew, diad saman met a panaon et mangipapaakseb na sakey a takot ya anggapoy ngaran to.

Balet wala ray arum ya inkagawa ya sayan inka-amputi et naandi so amin ya accessory tan nikadkaduman gloria ya angiter ed satan ed White Steed tan Albatross.

Antoy walad sakey a too ya Albino ya makapabigla tan mabetbet ya makapabigla ed mata, ya no maminsan et sikatoy kabusol na saray kakanayon to! Saya so inka-amputi ya mangiiter ed sikato, sakey a bengatla ya ibalikas na ngaran ya awit to. Say Albino et singa met ed arum a lalaki—anggapoy baleg a depekto to—balet sayan aspekto na amin ya inka-amputi so manggagawa ed sikaton mas makapadimla nen say sankagrabian ya aborsion. Akin et nepeg ya ontan?

Nisay, diad arum niran aspekto, say Kalikasan ed agtanton naimano to balet aliwan saray agtanton mauges ya ahensia to, so agmakapangilista ed limog na saray puersa to ed sayan sankatalonggaringan ya ugali na makapataktakot. Lapud niebe so itsura to, say gauntleted ghost na Southern Seas et tatawagen ya White Squall. Nisay, diad pigaran makatantandan inkagawa, say arte na inkauges na too so angipaway na mabiskeg a katulongan. Agaylan lalon inmawet so epekto na satan a bersikulo ed Froissart, sanen, akamaskara ed singa niebe a simbolo na grupo ra, saray desperadon White Hoods na Ghent so amatey ed bailiff da diad tindaan!

Nisay, diad arum a bengatla, say kaslakan, atawir ya eksperiensya na amin a katooan so agmakatasi ed supernaturalismo na sayan kolor. Agnayarin panduaruwaan a say sakey a nanengneng a kalidad ed aspekto na inaatey a sankabiglaan na managbantay, et say marmol a kolor a mansiasiansia diman; singa peteg ya say inkaputi to et singa tanda na takot ed sananey a mundo, a singa met ed mortal a takot dia. Tan manlapud satan a kolor na inaatey, naala tayo so makapabalikas a kolor na kawes ya inbalkot tayo ed sikara. Anggan diad saray superstisyon tayo et agtayo nibantak so parehon snowy mantle ed kaliberliber na saray phantom tayo; amin ya aswang ya ontatagey ed singa gatas ya amputin lurem—On, legan ya onsasabi ed sikatayo irayan makapataktakot, iyarum tayo, ya anggan say ari na saray makapataktakot, no inrepresenta na ebanghelista, et akalugan ed amputin kabayo to.

Kanian, diad arum ya ugali to, ya isisimbolo toy antokaman ya engranden odino marakep a bengatla ya labay to diad panamegley na inka-amputi, anggapoy toon makapangiburi ya diad sankarakpan ya kabaliksan to et walay nikadkaduman nengneng to ed kamarerwa.

Balet anggaman anggapoy agpantatalosan ya naresolbe iyan punto, panon ya ikuenta na mortal a too itan? Say pangusisa ed satan et singa imposibli. Nayarian tayo sirin, diad panamegley na pangibaga ed pigara ed saratan ya inkagawa a sayan bengatla nipaakar ed inka-amputi—anggaman diad panaon et sigpot odino diad baleg a kabiangan et naekal ed amin a direktan asosasyon ya akaisip ya mangiter ed satan na antokaman a makapataktakot, balet ta anggaman ontan, aromog a mangipapaakseb ed sikatayo na parehon panagsalamangka, anggan panon lay inkauman to;—sarag tayo sirin so manilalo a nawalaan na liwawa ed pigaran pakapatnagan pian anapen tayo so akaamot a kondukta?

Salien tayo. Balet diad ontan a pamaakaran, say inkadunong et makapasagyat ed inkadunong, tan no anggapoy imahinasyon et anggapoy toon makatumbok ed sananey diad sarayan hall. Tan anggaman, andi-duaruwan, pigara ed saray imahinasyon ya ipresenta so nayarin inabang na karaklan a totoo, balet pigpigara so nayarin sigpot a makaamta ed saratan ed saman a panaon, kanian nayarin ag da nanonotan iratan natan.

Akin ed samay toon ag-akaaral ya ideyal, ya ag tanton kabat to so nikadkaduman karakter ed saman a panaon, et say basta pangibaga ed Whitsuntide marshal ed andukey, makapaermen, agmakasalitan prosesyon na saray matantan ya peregrino, ya akapeket tan aka-hood na balon niebe? Odino, parad saray ag-akabasa, ag-sopistikadon Protestante na Middle American States, akin et say onlabas a panangibaga ed sakey ya White Friar odino sakey ya White Nun et mamapawala na ontan ya andiay mata ya estatua ed kamarerwa?

Odino anto ni so wala nilikud ed saray tradisyon na saray akapriso a mibabakal tan arari (ya ag-itan sigpot ya ipaliwawa) a manggagawa ed White Tower na London a mas mabiskeg a mangibabaga ed imahinasyon na sakey ya ag-akapasyar ya Amerikano, nen saray arum nin istruktura, saray kakaabay to—say Byward Tower, odino anggan say Bloody? Tan saraman ya mas atagey a tori, say White Mountains na New Hampshire, a ditan, diad nikadkaduman kipapasen, so panlalapuan na baleg a inka-aswang ed kamarerwa diad basta pangibaga ed satan a ngaran, bangta say kanonotan ed Blue Ridge na Virginia et napno na anlemew, ambetel, arawin kugip? Odino akin, antokaman so latitude tan longitude, say ngaran na White Sea et walaan na ontan ya spectralness ed panlabayan, bangta say Yellow Sea et mangiiter ed sikatayo na saray mortal a kanonotan na andukey tan malimpek iran ngarem ed saray daluyon, ya tumbokan na sanka-gaudi tan sanka-ugipan iran panselek na banwa? Odino, pian pilien so ag-inkayadyari ya alimbawa, ya puron nipaarap ed panlabayan, akin, diad pambasa ed saray daan ya engkanto na Central Europe, et say “atagey ya amputin too” na saray katakelan na Hartz, ya say agmanguuman ya kolor to et ag-onkukurang ed berde na saray kakiewan—akin et sayan aswang et mas makapataktakot nen say amin ya mansasnit ed Blocksburg?

Nisay, diad inkalapagan, so pakanodnonotan ed saray yegyeg to ya amawala na katedral; nisay saray onlelereg a dayat to; ni say ag-onlulua na amagan katawenan ya agbalot onuran; nisay say pakanengneng ed malaknab a lawak to na saray akayurong ya espiho, saray akapeket a bato, tan saray krus ya amin et akapeket (singa saray akapeket a sular na saray akaangkla iran barko); tan saray suburban avenue to na saray dingding na abung ya akapeket ed balang sakey, a singa sakey ya inbantak a pakete na baraha;—aliwan sarayan bengatla labat so manggagawa ed Lima ya andiay lua, say sanka-nikadkaduman, sankaermenan a syudad ya agmo nanengneng. Ta inala nen Lima so amputin belo; tan wala so mas atagey a takot ed sayan inkaputi na kairapan to. Singa si Pizarro, say inkaputi to et mansiansian balo ed saray geray to; agto aawaten so maliket ya inkaberde na sigpot ya inkabulok; onkakayat ed tapew na saray aderal a padir to so malet a kolor na sakey ya apoplexy ya mangaapiger ed saray dilin distorsyon to.

Amtak ya, diad karaklan ya pakatalos, sayan ebento na inka-amputi et ag-ipapasen ya manunan sengegan na pangipaatagey ed takot na saray bengatla ya makapataktakot; nisay ed agmakaisip ya kanonotan et walay antokaman a takot ed saratan ya itsura ya say makapataktakot ed sananey a kanonotan et ngalngalin manlalapud sayan sakey a bengatla, lalo la no nipanengneng ed silong na antokaman a nengneng ya asingger ed inka-emel odino inka-unibersal. Say labay kon ibaga ed sarayan duaran balikas et nayarin napaliwawa ed saray onggendan ya alimbawa.
