KAPITULO 114. Say Gilder.

Diad lalon inloob to ed sentro na panaglayag na Hapon, say Pequod et ag-abayag et nagmaliw ya amin ya ag-onkikiwas ed panagsigay. Mabetbet, diad malimpek tan marakep a panaon, diad loob na labindua, labinliman, labinwalo, tan duamplon oras ed panagbiahe, sikaray mibibiang ed saray baloto, a tuloytuloy a manguguyor, odino manlalayag, odino mansasagwan ed saray balyena, odino diad loob na anemaplo odino pitomplon minuto a kalmadon manaalagar ed iyaalsa ra; anggaman walaan na daiset a itatalona parad saray ot-ot da.

Diad ontan iran panaon, diad silong na onlelereg ya agew; manleletaw ed interon agew ed makinis, matantan ya onleletaw iran daluyon; akayurong ed baloto to, ya anlemew a singa birch a baloto; tan ontanlan makapikaaro ed saray mismon anlemew a daluyon, a singa saray pusa a singa bato ed pugon a man-ungol ira sumpad gunwale; saraya so panaon na makapalikdon kareenan, no nanenengneng so mareen a karakpan tan kinmiwas na katat na dayat, nalingwanan so puso na tigre ya onkukurang ed silong to; tan agto labay ya tandaan, ya sayan velvet paw et mangiyaamot na andiay-panangasin ngipen.

Saraya so panaon, sano diad whale-boat to et say rover et maalwar a makalikna na sakey a klase na filial, matalek, singa-dalin a liknaan ed dayat; ya ipapasen to itan bilang dakel a mabulaklak a dalin; tan say arawin barko ya nanengneng labat so toktok na saray palo to, et singa mansasagpot ya onabante, aliwan diad panamegley na atagey iran daluyon, noagta diad atagey a dika na sakey a manbabaroy a katakelan: a singa no saray kabayo na saray emigrante ed sagur so mangipapatnag labat na akaalagey iran layag da, bangta saray akaamot a laman da so malaknab a manlalako ed makapakelkelaw a berde.

Saray abayag lan birhen a lawak; saray malimpek ya asul a gilig na pukdol; singa diad tapew na saraya et walay mantakew na kareenan, say ungol; ngalngali ka la mansamba ya saray ugugaw ya naknaksawan ed panag-awit et naugip ed sarayan pasen, diad sakey a maliket a panaon na Mayo, no saray rosas na katakelan et napuputer. Tan amin na saya et manlalaok ed sanka-mistiko ya liknaan mo; kanian say katuaan tan say karakpan, kapalduan mankakasakey, mankakasakey, tan manporma na sakey ya anggapoy gulo.

Nisay saratan a makarelaks ya eksena, anggaman temporaryo labat, so andian na anggan temporaryon epekto ed si Ahab. Balet no sarayan sekreton balitok a tombok so singa mangilukas ed sikato na dilin sekreton balitok a kayamanan to, balet say panlinawa to ed saratan so apaneknekan a makapadimla.

O, saray dika! oh, pirmin andi-anggaan iran tanamanan ed kamarerwa; diad sikayo,—anggaman abayag lan amagaan lapud inatey a kelang na bilay ed dalin,—diad sikayo, saray totoo so nayarin onkurong, a singa saray malangwer nin kabayo ed balon kabuasan; tan diad pigaran ag-onlabas a bekta, nalikna so ambetel a linaew na imortal a bilay ed sikara. Komon ta mansiansia irayan mapalar a kalmado. Balet saray nanlalaok, nanlalaok iran lubir na bilay et inabel na warp tan woof: saray kalmadon binawit na saray bagyo, sakey a bagyo parad kada kalmado. Anggapoy tuloytuloy ya ag-ontumbok ya iyaaligwas ed sayan bilay; agtayo onabante ed saray pihon grado, tan diad unor et ontunda:—diad panamegley na ag-amtan anting-anting na inkaugaw, ag-inisip a pananisia na inkaugaw, panduaruwa na inkatobonbalo (say kaslakan a kadederal), insan panagduaruwa, insan agpanisia, a manpapainawa ed kaunoran diad panagnonot na inkatoo nipaakar ed No. Balet no asumpal la, tumboken tayo lamet so bilog; tan saray ugugaw, ugugaw a lalaki, tan lalaki, tan Ifs ed andi-anggaan. Iner so kawalaan na unor a pantalan, ya ag tayo la ontunda? Diad anton rapt ether so panlayag na mundo, ya agbalot naksawan so sankakapuyan? Iner so akaamotan ya ama na ugaw? Saray kamarerwa tayo et singa saray ulila ya saray ina ra ya ag-akasal et inatey legan dan inyanak: say sekreto na inka-ama tayo et walad lubok da, tan kaukolan tayo diman pian naaralan itan.

Tan diad saman met lanlamang ya agew, ya onnenengneng ed leksab manlapud gilig na baloto to ed saman met lanlamang a balitok a dayat, si Starbuck so mapaabeban nannguronguro:—

"Say karakpan ya agnatalosan, a singa anengneng na anggan sakey a manaaro ed mata na malangwer nin nobya to!—Ag mo ak ibabaga nipaakar ed saray pating mo ya singa ngipen, tan saray panagkidnap mon kanibal a paraan. Say pananisia komon so mamaandi ed katuaan; say panlabayan komon so mamawala na pakanodnonotan; onnenengneng ak komon ed aralem tan manisia."

Tan si Stubb, a singa sira, a walaan na mankirlap iran siksik, so linmukso ed saman met a balitok a liwawa:—

"Siak si Stubb, tan walay history nen Stubb; balet dia et nansamba si Stubb ya sikatoy lanang ya maliket!"





KAPITULO 115. Say Pequod et Akabat toy Bachelor.

Tan makapaliket iray nanenengneng tan saray tanol ya onlelereg antis na dagem, pigaran simba kayari na impan-weld ed harpoon nen Ahab.

Sakey itan a barko na Nantucket, say Bachelor, ya akasabi la ed unor a bariles na larak to, tan insebel to ray mansasabog ya abot to; tan natan, ya akakawes na maliket a piesta opisyal, et maliket, anggaman medyo andi-kakanaan-magloria, a manlalayag ed limog na saray barko ya arawi ed dalin, sakbay ton inturo so proa to paonlad abung to.

Saray taloran lalaki ed uloan na palo to et akasulong na andukey iran streamers na maimpis ya ambalangan bunting ed saray sombrero ra; manlapud beneg, walay baloton balyena ya akabitin, manlapud leksab ya anggad leksab; tan akabitin ed bowsprit so anengneng ya andukey ya abeban panga na unor ya balyena ya pinatey da. Saray signal, ensign, tan jack na amin a kolor so ontitikyab manlapud rigging to, ed amin a diking. Diad gilig na balang sakey ed taloran basket to et walay duaran bariles na semilya; diad tagey to, diad saray cross-tree to ed tagey na palo, anengneng mo ray malimpek a breaker na parehon mablin pluido; tan akapasak ed manunan trak to so sakey a lamparaan a gansa.

Unong ed naamtaan ed saginonor, say Bachelor so akaawat na sankabiglaan ya itatalona; lalon makapakelkelaw, ta legan ya manbiabiahe ed parehon dayat et dakel niran barko so linma ed loob na pigaran bulan ya anggapoy anggan sakey a sira. Aliwa labat ya saray bariles na karne na baka tan tinapay so inter pian nawalaan na pasen so mas mablin semilya, no ag ingen wala met iray kaaruman a kaaruman a bariles ya insaliwan, manlapud saray barkon akabat to; tan saraya so inyan ed gilig na kubyerta, tan diad saray kuarto na kapitan tan saray opisyal. Anggan say lamisaan ed cabin et atumba ed kinling-wood; tan say cabin mess et angan ed malapar ya ulo na oil-butt, ya insakbit ed datal parad sakey a sentro. Diad forecastle, saray marino so talagan aka-caulk tan aka-pitch ed saray pagew da, tan pinano da iratan; makapaliket ya inyarum, ya say kusinero et pinalakpak toy ulo to ed sankabalgan ya boiler to, tan pinano to itan; ya insaksak na mayordomo so ekstran kape to tan pinano to; ya saray manag-arpoon so angitonton ed saray patoonan na saray bakal da tan pinano da iratan; ya talagan amin et napno na semilya, likud ed saray bulsa na pantaloon na kapitan, tan saramay inreserba ton pangisulong na lima to, bilang paneknek ed sarili ton inkapnek.

Nen onlelereg iyan maliket a barko na kapalaran ed maermen a Pequod, say barbaro ya tanol na angkakabaleg iran tambol so nanlapud forecastle to; tan diad iyasingger to, anengneng so sakey a grupo na saray sundalo to ya akaalagey ed kaliberliber na angkakabaleg iran try-pot to, ya asakbongan na singa pergamino ya katat na eges na andeket a sira, tan mangiiter na maksil ya ungol ed kada pan-ipit na lima na saray tripulante. Diad quarter-deck, saray mates tan harpooneers et mansasayaw a kaiba iray olive-hued ya bibii ya akabatik ed sikara manlapud Polynesian Isles; legan ya akabitin ed sakey ya dekorasyon ya baloto, ya malet ya akapeket ed baetan na foremast tan mainmast, taloran Long Island negroes, ya walaan na mankirlap iran fiddle-bow ya gawad garing na balyena, so mangidadaulo ed makapaliket ya jig. Kaleganan to, arum ed kompanya na barko so magulon okupado ed panagpaalagey na saray try-works, a ditan et inekal iray angkakabaleg a kaldero. Ngalngali mo komon inisip ya ibabatik da so nisamban Bastille, singa saray makapataktakot ya eyag da, legan ya saray andiay kakanaan lan laryo tan semento so ibabantak ed dayat.

Katawan tan katawan ed amin na sayan eksena, say kapitan so inmalagey a maptek ed atagey a quarter-deck na barko, kanian say amin a makapaliket a drama so napno ed arap to, tan singa ginawa labat parad dilin indibidual a panagpalikdo to.

Tan si Ahab, sikato met so akaalagey ed quarter-deck to, a balkes tan andeket, a walaan na makapasinagem a ermen; tan legan ya manbabaliw so duaran barko ed balang sakey—say sakey et manliliket ed saray bengatlan linmabas, say sananey et manpapalikdo ed saray bengatlan onsabi—say duaran kapitan da so angipatnag na makapadinayew a pandumaan na eksena.

“Onlugan kayo, onlugan kayo!” inmeyag so bakla ya komandante na Bachelor, ya intagey toy sakey a baso tan sakey a bote ed tagey.

"Anengneng mo lay White Whale?" inmebat si Ahab.

“Andi, nadngel ko labat so nipaakar ed sikato, balet ag ka manisia ed sikato,” inkuan na samay sakey ya maliket. “Onlugan kayo!”

"Masyado kan maliket. Manlayag ka. Wala ray inateyan mo?"

"Aliwan magenap so pansalita—duaran taga-isla, satan labat;—balet onlugan ka, masiken, onla ka. Ag mabayag et ekalen ko la itan ya andeket ed moling mo. Onla ka, labay mo (maliket so palabas); sakey a napnon barko tan pawil la."

"Agaylan makapakelkelaw so pamilyar ed sakey a makulangkulang!" inkuan nen Ahab; insan maksil, "Sika so sakey a napnon barko tan onsesempet ed abung mo, kuan mo; bueno, sirin, tawagen mo ak a sakey a barko ya anggapoy karga to, tan onla ed paway. Kanian onla ka la, tan siak so onla ed siak. Onarap ka diman! Iparaan mo so amin a layag, tan iyagel mo ed dagem!"

Kanian, legan ya say sakey a barko et maliket ya onkukurang ed arap na dagem, say sananey et masebeg ya akilaban ed satan; tan dia ed ontan nansiansia so duaran banga; saray tripulante na Pequod ya onnenengneng ed samay onaarawi ya Bachelor; balet saray totoo na Bachelor et ag da inimano so pakanengneng da ed mabilabilay ya panliliket da. Tan nen si Ahab, ya akayurong ed tapew na taffrail, et onnenengneng ed bapor ya onsesempet, sikatoy angala ed bulsa to na melag a bote na buer, insan to ninmengneng manlapud barko ya anggad bote, a singa to la pankakasakeyen so duaran arawin asosasyon, ta satan a vial et napno na tanol na Natuck.





KAPITULO 116. Say Ompapatey a Balyena.

Aliwan matalag ed sayan bilay, no, diad kawanan a dapag, saray paborito na kapalaran so manlalayag ya asingger ed sikatayo, sikatayo, anggaman amin so akapeket la, makaala na daiset ya onkukurang a dagem, tan maliket a nalilikna so panpano na saray layag tayo. Ontan met so ginawa na Pequod. Ta diad tinmumbok ya agew kayari ton akabat so bakla ya Bachelor, wala ray anengneng dan balyena tan apatira so inatey; tan sakey ed sikara et ginawa nen Ahab.

Arawrawi lad ngarem; tan nen asumpal so amin a panagpaltog na ambalanga a laban: tan manleletaw ed marakep a panselek na agew a dayat tan tawen, say agew tan balyena so parehon inatey a saniiba; insan, ontan lan masamit tan ontan lan inka-maermen, ontan lan inka-inwreating orisons ya akakulong ed saman ya rosas ya dagem, ya singa singa arawi ed aralem a berde iran lawak na kumbento na saray isla na Manilla, say Spanish land-breeze, wantonly turned sailor, so linma ed dayat, ya awit na sarayan himno na visperas.

Nainawa lamet, balet ta nainawa labat ed mas aralem a ermen, si Ahab, ya inmarawi ed balyena, so akayurong a maalwar a manbabantay ed unor iran inkaandi to manlapud mareen lan baloto. Ta saman ya nikadkaduman pasingawey ya nanenengneng ed amin ya sperm whale ya ompapatey—say iyaarap na ulo ed agew, tan ontan lay ipatey da—saman ya nikadkaduman pasingawey, ya anengneng ed ontan ya mareen a labi, et anggan panon et angipasabi ed si Ahab na makapakelkelaw ya ag to ni amta nensaman.

“Sikatoy onliliber tan ontalineng ed satan,—agaylan kalmado, balet agaylan mapekder, so pangiter na galang tan pangiter na tawag ed kiew to, diad unor iran galaw to nen ompapatey. Sikato met so mandadayew ed apoy; sankamatooran, malapar, baronial a vassal na banwa!—O komon ta nanengneng na sarayan masyadon makapadinayew a mata irayan masyadon makapasagyat a nengneng! labas ni nen say amin ya ungol na kayamanan odino kairapan na too; say patey so onliliber ed bangkay, tan onla itan ed sananey a direksion.

“O, sika ya maimpis a Hindoo a kapaldua na kalikasan, a manlapud saray nalner a pukel so angipaalagey na nikadkaduman tronom ed sakey a pasen ed pegley na sarayan ag-onkukurang a dayat; sika so sakey ya infidel, sika a reyna, tan peteg a mansasalita ka ed siak diad malaknab a panagpatey a Bagyo, tan say mareen a ponpon to kayari na sayan impatey nen Northy so angipawil ed impatey to. ulo, insan linma lamet, ya anggapoy leksion ed siak.

"O, trebly hooped tan welded hip of power! Oh, atagey ya pilalek, rainbowed jet!—ya sakey so mansasagpot, sayan sakey et mangipelag na amin ya andi-kakanaan! Anggapoy kakanaan, oh balyena, manaanap kay pikakasi ed amin a makapabilay ya agew, ya manatawag labat na bilay, balet agto itan iter lamet. Balet sika, takotan mo ak, no mangiter ka na kapaldua, sakey a bato, no walay kapalduan mo, mangiter ed siak na kapaldua mas ambilunget a pananisia. Amin ya agnasabin ngaran mo et onleletaw ed silong ko dia;

“Sirin, uran a yelo, anggad angga, O dayat, a diad andi-anggaan a panagbantak to et makaromog na saksakey a painawaan na saray manok ed katakelan. Nianak ed dalin, balet ta sinususo na dayat; anggaman say pukdol tan lawak so angiyanak ed siak, sikayo ran daluyon so angiyanak ed siak!”





KAPITULO 117. Say Panagbantay na Balyena.

Saray apatiran balyena ya inatey ed saman a labi et inatey ya ag-ontutumbok; sakey, arawi ed dagem; sakey, ya agtanton arawi, ed bandad dagem; sakey ed unaan; sakey ed beneg. Sarayan unor a talora so inawit ed abay sakbay na labi; balet say walad dagem et ag nasabi anggad kabuasan; tan say baloto ya amatey ed sikato et akaamot ed gilig to ed sanlabi; tan saman a baloto et kayarian nen Ahab.

Say waif-pole et insulong ya diretso ed abot na inatey a balyena; tan say parol ya akasabit ed toktok to, et mangiiter na makapagonigon a mankirlap a liwawa ed andeket, masileng a beneg, tan arawi ed saray daluyon ed pegley-labi, a kalmadon manpapaspas ed malapar a gilig na balyena, a singa anlemew a daluyon ed gilig na dayat.

Si Ahab tan amin ya tripulante na baloto to et singa naugip balet say Parsee; ya akayurong ed proa, ya akayurong ya manbabantay ed saray pating, ya makapadinayew ya manlalaro ed kaliberliber na balyena, tan tatapiken da ray anlemew a tabla na sedro panamegley na ikol da. Say tanol a singa say panngalngali na saray iskwadron ed tapew na saray Asphaltite na saray ag-aperdonan aswang na Gomorrah, so onkukurang a manggiwgiw ed dagem.

Diad gapo na ugip to, anengneng nen Ahab so Parsee; tan akapaliber ed kabilungetan na labi et singa ra unor a totoo ed mundon adelap. "Akugip ko lamet itan," inkuanto.

"Nipaakar ed saray luluganan na bangkay? Agko ta imbaga, masiken, ya anggan say luluganan na bangkay odino lungon et ag nayarin kayarian mo?"

“Tan siopa ray narerengel ya ompatey ed dayat?”

"Balet inkuan ko, masiken, ya antis kan ompatey ed sayan biahe, duaran bangkay so nepeg a nanengneng mo ed dayat; say unaan et ag ginawa na too; tan say nanengneng a kiew na unor et nepeg ya itanem ed Amerika."

"Aye, aye! sakey a nikadkaduman pasingawey ya, Parsee:—sakey a luluganan tan saray bago to ya onleletaw ed tapew na dayat a kaiba iray daluyon parad saray manag-awit na bangkay. Ha! Say ontan a pasingawey et agtayo la nanengneng."

"Manisia ka o andi, ag ka ompatey anggad ag mo nanengneng, masiken."

"Tan antoy ibabaga na satan nipaakar ed sika?"

"Anggano onsabi itan ed sampot, siak so onla ed unaan mo ed piloto mo."

"Tan no wala ka la nensaman—no ontan so nagawa—insan antis ak ya ontumbok, kaukolan mo nin siansia so ompatnag ed siak, pian pilotoen mo ak ni?—Agta ontan? Bueno, sirin, anisia ak ed amin ya ibabaga yo, oh piloto ko! Walay duaran sipan ko dia ya pateyen ko ni si Moby Dick tan makaliktar ak ed satan."

"Mangala ka lamet na prenda, masiken," inkuan na Parsee, legan ya onsisinag iray mata to a singa saray langa ed kabilungetan—"Hemp labat so makapatey ed sika."

"Say bitayan, say labay mon ibaga.—Siak so ag-ompatey, diad dalin tan diad dayat," so inkelyaw nen Ahab, tekep na tawa na panagludlurey;—"Ag-ompatey ed dalin tan diad dayat!"

Sikaran dua so ag-inmesel lamet, a singa sakey a too. Sinmabi so kabuasan, tan saray naugip a tripulante so inmalagey manlapud dalem na baloto, tan sakbay na pegley-agew et inawit ed barko so inatey a balyena.





KAPITULO 118. Say Quadrant.

Say panaon parad Linya et asingger la; tan inagew-agew no si Ahab, ya manlalapud cabin to, et onnengneng ed tagey, say maalwar a timon et manpapasirayew ed saray rayos to, tan saray maseseg a marino et maples ya ombatik ed saray braces, tan onalagey ira diman ya akaarap so amin a mata ra ed akapeket a doubloon; agmakaanos ed ganggan ya ituro so arap na barko ed ekwador. Diad dugan panaon et sinmabi so order. Mairap itan nen pegley-agew; tan si Ahab, ya akayurong ed saray proa na atagey a baloto to, et ngalngali la man-obserba ed agew pian naamtaan so latitude to.

Natan, diad satan a dayat na Hapon, saray agew ed tiagew et singa saray balon liwawa. Say ag-onkirlap a mabilabilay ya agew na Hapon so singa mankirlap a pokus na agnasukat a manliob a salming na singa salming a dayat. Say tawen et singa abel; saray lurem et anggapo; say abot so onleletaw; tan sayan inkalakseb na ag naekal a liwawa et singa ag natepel a karakpan na trono na Dios. Bueno, say quadrant nen Ahab et walaan na de-kolor iran salming, ya diad panamegley to et nanengneng so solar fire. Kanian, ya inyurong toy akayurong a porma to ed rolyo na barko, tan say instrumento ton singa astrolohiko so inyan to ed mata to, sikatoy nansiansia ed satan a postura ed loob na pigaran bekta pian nanengneng so eksakton bekta no kapigan so eksakton meridian to. Kaleganan to, legan ya akasentro so amin ya imano to, say Parsee et akatalimukor ed silong to diad kubyerta na barko, tan say lupa to et singa nen Ahab, et manbabantay ed parehon agew a kaiba to; saray mata to labat so kapaldua ya akasakbong ed saray orbs da, tan say atap a lupa to so ag-inmabobon ed sakey a mangaraldalin ya inkaandi-panangaro. Diad kaunoran et naala so labay ya obserbasyon; tan diad panamegley na lapis to ed sali ton garing, agnambayag et kinalkula nen Ahab no antoy latitude to ed saman ya eksakton bekta. Insan nen sikatoy tinmangay na sakey a bekta, sikatoy tinmangay lamet ed banwa tan inkuanto ed inkasikato: “Sika a marka na dayat! sika atagey tan makapanyarin Piloto! Ibagam ed siak so tua no iner so kawalaan ko —balet kasin nayarian mon ibaga no iner so kawalaan ko? Odino nayarian mo kasi so mangibaga no iner so kawalaan na sananey a bengatla nilikud ed siak a manbibilay ed sayan bekta? Nepeg a wadtan ka ed sayan bekta? onnenengneng ed sikato. Sarayan matak et onnenengneng ed mismon mata ya anggan natan et makakanengneng ed sikato;

Insan nen ninmengneng ed kuadrante to, tan nantutumbokan ya inasikaso to so dakel a kabalistikon kagawaan to, sikatoy nannonot lamet, tan inkuanto: “Makulangkulang ya ay-ayam! ay-ayam na ugugaw na saray mapaatagey ya Admiral, tan Commodores, tan Kapitan; say mundo so manpapasirayew ed sika, ed inkadunong tan biskeg mo; balet anto so nagawaan mo no ag say mairap-bilay, no iner so pangibagaan mo, no iner so pakanengnengan mo ed mairap-bilay sika so walad sayan malaknab a planeta, tan say lima ya akabemben ed sika et anggapo ni! say inkapalsa to balet et mansasnit ed sikato, ta sarayan daan a matam et natan et apoolan la na liwawa mo, O banwa! ya ipapaarap tod kubyerta, "agko la igiya so mangaraldalin a dalan ko ed panamegley mo; say patatdang a kompas na barko, tan say patatdang a panagkuenta, diad panamegley na troso tan diad panamegley na lubir; saraya so mangitonton ed siak, tan mangipanengneng ed siak na pasen ko ed dayat. Aye," ya onsisinag manlapud baloto ya amaarap ed kubyerta, "ontan ta ka nataktak, sika a sankatageyan a bengatla , tan singa ak ontuturo ed talora; deralen ta ka!”

Legan ya mansasalita so mansasakit ya masiken tan sikatoy natatapak na saray mabilay tan inatey a sali to, sakey a makapataktakot a pananalo ya singa parad si Ahab, tan sakey a makapatey ya inkaandi-ilalo ya singa parad sikaton dili—saratan so linmabas ed lupa na emel tan ag-onkikiwas a Parsee. Anggapoy naimano to ya inmalagey tan inmalis; bangta, abigla ed itsura na komandante ra, saray marino so nankakasakey ed forecastle, anggad si Ahab, a manpapaga ed kubyerta, so inmeyag—“To the braces! Up helm!—square in!”
