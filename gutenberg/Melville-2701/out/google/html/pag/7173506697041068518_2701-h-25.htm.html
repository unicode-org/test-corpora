Diad inlusob to ed silong na barkon onlelereg, say balyena so binmatik a manggiwgiw ed kilya to; balet nen linmingaw ed silong na danum, maples ya inmawet lamet ed tapew, ya arawi ed biek a proa, balet ta pigaran yarda labat so kaarawi to ed baloto nen Ahab, ya diman, ed loob na pigaran panaon, et sikatoy akaalagey ya ag-onkikiwas.

"Ipaarap ko so laman ko ed banwa. Antoy ho, Tashtego! parengel mo ak ed martilyo mo. Oh! sika ran taloran ag-insuko ya espiho ko; sika ya ag-apaltog a kilya; tan say dios-bully labat a kasko; sika a malet a kubyerta, tan mapaatagey a timon, tan Pole-pointed prow,--makapapatey a barko, sirin et naandi ak la? Naandi ak la ed unor a naandi ed siak! Sirin et naandi ak ed unor a panagputot ed siak! say inkamapaatagey na saray kapitan ya aderal? balyena; anggad sampot et mansasaksak ed sika;

Say harpoon so pinaltog; say atumba ya balyena et tinmakdang ed unaan; diad kapeles na panag-apoy et linmabas so linya ed saray kanal;—linmabas a foul. Inmabeba si Ahab pian linisan itan; inekal to itan; balet say flying turn et akasabi ed beklew to, tan anggapoy boses to legan ya ipapana na saray emel ya Turkish so biktima ra, sikatoy pinaltog ed paway na baloto, antis ya naamtaan na saray tripulante ya sikatoy anggapo la. Diad tinmumbok a bekta, say ambelat ya eye-splice ed unor a sampot na lubir so tinmekkiab manlapud anggapoy karga ton tub, ya atumba so sakey a managsagwan, tan inmabeba ed dayat, tan naandi ed kaaralem to.

Diad sakey a bekta, saray tripulante na baloto ya ag-onkikiwas et tinmalindeg; insan inmarap. "Say barko? Baleg a Dios, iner so kawalaan na barko?" Agnambayag et anengneng da ed saray malimpek tan makapawetwet iran medium so onkukurang a phantom to, a singa say gas ya Fata Morgana; saray sankatageyan labat a palo so walad danum; bangta akapeket ed inka-infatuation, odino katooran, odino kapalaran, ed saray datin atagey a yurongan da, saray paganon manag-harpooner so siansia nin mansiasiansia ed onlelereg iran panagbantay da ed dayat. Tan natan, saray konsentrikon sirkulo so angala ed saksakey a baloto, tan amin a tripulante to, tan kada onleletaw a sagwan, tan kada lance-pole, tan manpapawil-pawil, mabilay tan andi-bilay, amin a manliber tan manliber ed sakey a vortex, so angiyakar ed sankamelagan a chip na Pequod ya ag nanengneng.

Balet nen saray unor ya whelmings et manlalaok ed tapew na linmereg ya ulo na Indian diad mainmast, ya angitilak na pigaran pulgada na akaalagey ya spar ya anengneng ni, kaiba so andukey ya onlelereg ya yarda na bandera, ya kalmadon onlelereg, tekep na makapaermen iran pantutumbokan, ed tapew na saray makaderal a daluyon ya ngalngali da la nadiwit—diad saman ya ambalanga ya taklay, tampol ya inmepas so martilyo; ya intagey ed beneg ed paway na dagem, diad gawa na pangipako ed bandera a mas maples tan mas maples ni ed onkakapuy a spar. Sakey a sky-hawk ya makapabaing ya tinmumbok ed manunan trak ya onleleksab manlapud natural ya ayaman to ed limog na saray bitewen, ya manpapasnok ed bandera, tan manpapasnok ed si Tashtego diman; sayan manok so akasabi natan ed malapar a payak to ed baetan na martilyo tan say kiew; tan diad parehon pakalikna to ed satan ya etherial thrill, say linmereg ya atap ya ayep ed leksab, diad death-gasp to, et pinansiansia toy martilyo to ya akayelo diman; tan ontan so manok ed tawen, a walaan na saray eyag na arkanghel, tan say imperyal a sangi to ya akaarap ed tagey, tan say interon inkapriso to ya akatiklop ed bandera nen Ahab, so linmeksab a kaiba toy barko to, a singa si Satanas, ya ag-onlelereg ed impierno anggad agto ni awit so mabilay a kabiangan na tawen a kaiba to, tan insulong to so inkasikato.

Natan et saray angkekelag a manok so ontitikyab a mankelyaw ed tapew na manpapasnok nin gulpo; sakey a makapaermen ya amputin daluyon so onlelereg ed saray mapitek a gilig to; insan amin et atumba, tan say baleg a sakob na dayat so linmukso a singa linmukso to nen limaran libon taon lay apalabas.





Epilogue

“TAN AK LABAT AK YA AKALIKAS PIAN IBABAGA ED SIKA” Job.

Asumpal lay drama. Akin sirin et walay onkukurang?—Lapud walay akaliktar ed inkagba na barko.

Agnagawa, kayari na inkaandi na Parsee, siak so inordinaan na Fates ya onsalat ed managpana nen Ahab, nen inawat na satan a managpana so bakantin posisyon; sikato met, ya, nen diad unor ya agew et inbantak so taloran lalaki manlapud paway na manyuyugyog a baloto, sikatoy atumba ed beneg. Kanian, manleletaw ed gilig na ontumbok ya eksena, tan diad sigpot a pakanengneng ed satan, sanen say kapaldua lan naupot a panagsipsip na linmereg a barko so akasabi ed siak, siak so insan, balet ta kalkalna, ya inyasingger ed mansara a vortex. Nen akasabi ak ed satan, linmeksab la tan nagmaliw ya creamy pool. Manbilog tan manbilog, sirin, tan lanang ya onkukurong ed singa buton ya andeket a bula diad axis na satan ya matantan ya onkukurong a sirkulo, a singa sananey ya Ixion ya inpawil ko. Anggad, diad pakagamor ed satan a importantin sentro, say andeket a bula ya ontatdang so binmetag; tan natan, nibulos lapud masilib a subol to, tan, lapud baleg a panag-alsa to, ontatagey a mabiskeg, say life-buoy na lungon so pinmaway ed karukey manlapud dayat, atumba, tan linmugan ed abay ko. Lapud pinabiskeg na satan a lungon, diad loob na ngalngalin sakey ya agew tan labi, linmugan ak ed malambot tan singa-ermen a main. Saray ag makaderal ya pating, et linmabas iran singa walay kandado ed sangi ra; saray masebeg ya sea-hawk et manlalayag ya walay balkot dan sangi. Diad komaduan agew, walay layag ya inmasingger, inmasingger, tan inala to ak ed kaunoran. Si Rachel ya makulangkulang ya manbiabiahe, ya diad pananap to lamet ed saray naandi lan anak to et akaromog labat na sananey ya ulila.
