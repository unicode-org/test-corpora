SHAPITUA 4. Tshilamba tshia kubandila.

Ngakajuka mu dinda diakalonda mu munya, ngakapeta tshianza tshia Queequeg tshiela pa mutu panyi mu mushindu wa dinanga ne dinanga dia bungi. Uvua pabuipi ne kuela meji ne: mvua mukajende. Tshilamba tshia ku mutu tshivua tshienza ne bipese bia bintu, tshiuwule tente ne tu-mabala tukese tua patupu ne tua triangle; ne diboko diende edi divua ne tshimfuanyi tshia tshimfuanyi tshia mu Kreta tshidi katshiyi ne ndekelu, kakuyi bitupa bibidi biatshi bivua ne mundidimbi umue menemene—ndi ngela meji ne: uvua mulame diboko diende mu mbuu mu mushindu mubi mu munya ne mundidimbi, maboko ende avua mavunguluke misangu mishilangane—diboko diende dimue adi, ndi ngamba ne: divua dimueneka bu tshitupa tshimue tshia buloba tshilamba. Bushuwa, meme mulale pa mutu patshi anu muvua diboko dienze pamvua mubike bua musangu wa kumpala, tshivua mua kutshimanya ku tshilamba tshia kujingila to, bavua basangisha misombelu yabu pamue; ne anu ku lungenyi lua bujitu ne tshienzedi ke mumvua mua kumanya ne: Queequeg uvua unkuata mu tshianza.

Malu amvua mumvue avua a dikema. Lekela ntete bua kubumvuija. Pantshivua muana, ndi mvuluka bimpe bualu kampanda bua muomumue buvua bumfikile; nansha biobi bikale bualu bulelela anyi tshilota, tshivua mua kuitaba to. Nsombelu uvua nunku. Mvua nkosa ndambu wa tshiamu tshia mitaku anyi tshikuabu—ndi ngela meji ne: tshivua tshikeba kubanda mu tshiibi tshia kapia, anu mumvua mumone tshikomba tshikese tshienza matuku makese kumpala; ne mamuanyi wa bakaji uvua, mu mushindu kampanda anyi kansanga, unkuma misangu yonso, anyi untuma ku bulalu tshiyi ne tshiakudia tshia dilolo,—mamuanyi uvua unkoka ku maulu mu tshiibi tshia kapia ne unkoka bua kuya kulala, nansha muvuaku anu dîba dibidi dia mu mapingaja dia matuku 21 a ngondo wa 6, dituku dinene dia tshidimu tshijima mu tshitupa tshietu tshia buloba. Ngakumvua buôwa. Kadi kakuvua diambuluisha nansha dimue, nunku meme kubanda pa tshibandilu ngakaya mu kazubu kanyi kakese ka mu tshisasa tshisatu, meme kudivula bilamba bitekete bitekete bua kujimija dîba, ne meme kuela mbila mikole meme kubuela pankatshi pa bilamba.

Mvua mulale apu ne kanyinganyinga kakole ngela meji ne: mêba dikumi ne asambombo avua ne bua kupita kumpala kua meme kutekemena dibika dia ku lufu. Mêba dikumi ne asambombo mu bulalu! tshitupa tshia mukolo tshianyi tshivua tshisama pamvua ngela meji bua bualu ebu. Ne bivua bipepele kabidi; Dîba didi dikenkesha ku dididishi, ne mutoyi munene wa mashinyi mu misesu, ne mutoyi wa mêyi a bantu badi baselangane mu nzubu mujima. Ngakumvua bibi menemene—ndekelu wa bionso ngakajuka, meme kuvuala, ne meme kupuekela bitekete bitekete mu makasa anyi avua ne tshilamba tshia panshi, meme kukeba mamuanyi wa bakaji, ne mu tshimpitshimpi meme kudiela ku makasa ende, kumusengelela bu dianyisha dia pa buadi bua kumpesha dikasa dimpe bua tshikadilu tshianyi tshibi; tshintu tshionso bulelela kadi anu kunkosela tshibawu tshia kulala pa bulalu mutantshi mule menemene. Kadi uvua mamu mulela mutambe buimpe ne uvua ne kondo ka muoyo kimpe, ne mvua ne bua kuya mu nzubu wanyi. Munkatshi mua mêba a bungi mvua mulale muaba au mutabale, ndiumvua bibi menemene kupita mumvua muanji kumvua katshia anu pinapu, nansha ku makenga manene avua malonda. Ndekelu wa bionso ndi ne tshia kuikala mukuluke mu tshilota tshibi tshia tulu; ne pandi njuka bitekete bitekete—mubuela mu bilota—ngakakangula mêsu anyi, ne nzubu uvua kumpala kua dîba uvua mpindieu mubuikila kudi mîdima ya pambelu. Diakamue ngakumvua tshimvundu tshipita mu mubidi wanyi mujima; kakuvua tshintu tshivua tshimueneka, ne kakuvua tshivua tshiumvuika; kadi tshianza tshia pa mutu tshivua tshimueneka tshiteka mu tshianyi. Diboko dianyi divua dilengeja pa tshilamba, ne tshintu tshidi katshiyi ne dina, tshidi katshiyi mua kuela meji, tshidi katshiyi ne dîyi, tshidi tshianza tshikale tshiatshi, tshivua tshimueneka tshisombe pabuipi ne bulalu buanyi. Bua bidimu bivua bimueneka bu bidimu bia bungi, mvua mulale apu, mutshiokeshibue ne buôwa bua bungi, tshiyi ne dikima dia kukoka tshianza tshianyi kule; kadi ndi ngela meji ne: meme mua kutshinyungulula anu santimetre umue, tshishima tshibi atshi tshivua mua kunyanguka. Tshivua mumanye mushindu uvua lungenyi elu luenda lunyema kundi to; kadi pamvua njuka mu dinda, ngakavuluka bionso ne buôwa buonso, ne munkatshi mua matuku ne mbingu ne ngondo pashishe ngakadijimija mu mateta a dipanga dia lungenyi a kumvuija bualu busokoka. Tòo, too ne pa dîba edi, ntu misangu ya bungi ndiela mpata ne bualu ebu.

Mpindieu, umbusha buôwa bubi, ne lungenyi luanyi lua kumvua tshianza tshia pa mutu mu tshianyi luvua lufuanangane, mu bumuntu buabu, ne lumvua mumvue pangakajuka ne kumona tshianza tshia bampanga tshia Queequeg tshinyunguluke. Ino ku mfulo bintu byonso bya bufuku byapita byadi bya monka, umo ku umo, mu bulelela, ne pashishe ngakalala anu ne muoyo ku lutatu lua tuseku. Bualu nansha mumvua mutete bua kunyunguluja diboko diende—kubulula tshilamba tshiende tshia muena dibaka—nansha nanku, mulale bu muvuaye, uvua anu unkuatshila bikole, bienze anu bu ne: kakuena tshintu tshikuabu to, anu lufu ke ludi mua kututapulula tuetu babidi. Mpindieu ngakadienzeja bua kumujula—“Queequeg!”—kadi diandamuna diende dimuepele divua dia kuela mbila. Pashishe ngakakuluka, nshingu wanyi umvua bu uvua mu tshilamba tshia kabalu; ne mu tshimpitshimpi ngakumvua dikosa dikese. Patudi tuela tshilamba tshia ku tshiibi, kuvua tomahawk mulale ku luseke lua tshisuku, bienze anu bu ne: uvua muana wa mpala wa tshingoma. Tshintu tshilenga, bulelela, ngakela meji; mulale apa mu nzubu wa bende mu munya, ne muntu udi udia bantu ne tomahawk! "Queequeg!—mu dina dia buimpe, Queequeg, juka!" Ndekelu wa bionso, ku diambuluisha dia dikokangana dia bungi, ne diakula dia dîyi dikole ne dia kashidi pa bidi bitangila dipanga diakane dia kukuata mulume nende mu mushindu wa dibaka, ngakafika ku dipatula mutoyi; ne diakamue, wakapingaja diboko diende, kudinyunguluka bu mbwa wa mu Newfoundland uvua mupatuke mu mayi, ne wakasomba pa bulalu, mukole bu mutshi wa pike, untangila, ne unkoka mêsu ende bienze anu bu ne: kavua muvuluke bimpe mushindu umvua mufike kuntu aku to, nansha muvua lungenyi lutekete lua kumanya bualu kampanda buanyi lumueneka luenda lumueneka bitekete bitekete. Mu tshikondo atshi, mvua mulale mutangile, tshiyi ne dielakana dikole mpindieu, ne mvua mudisuike bua kutangila tshifukibua tshivua ne dijinga dia kumanya malu a bungi. Pavua, ndekelu wa bionso, lungenyi luende lumueneka bu luvua luenza bua kulenga ngikadilu wa muena bulalu nende, ne wakalua, bu mudibi, mulengejangana ne bualu abu; wakatuta panshi, ne ku diambuluisha dia bimanyinu kampanda ne mitoyi kampanda wakampesha lungenyi lua ne: bikalabi binsankisha, uvua mua kuanji kuvuala ne pashishe kunshiya bua kuvuala pashishe, kushiya nzubu mujima nkayanyi. Meme, Queequeg, ndi ngela meji ne: mu nsombelu udiku, etshi ntshilele tshiakane; kadi, bulelela budi ne, bena tshinyangu aba badi ne lungenyi lua ku tshilelelu lua bulenga, amba tshiudi musue; bidi bikemesha mudibu ne kanemu. Ndi ngela Queequeg kalumbandi aka, bualu uvua mungenzele malu ne kanemu ne kanemu ka bungi, eku meme muikale ne tshibawu tshia tshikisu tshikole; kumutangila ku bulalu, ne kutangila ngendu yende yonso ya tshina tshia mâyi; bua tshikondo atshi dijinga dianyi dia kumanya malu dienda dikola mu dilela dianyi. Nansha nanku, muntu bu mudi Queequeg kuena umona dituku dionso to, yeye ne bienzedi biende bivua bia mushinga mukole.

Wakatuadija kuvuala bilamba biende bia pa mutu pa kuvuala tshilamba tshiende tshia beaver, tshile menemene, ku luseke, ne pashishe—katshiyi ne tshilamba tshiende tshia pantalo—wakakeba bisabata biende. Tshiena mua kuamba bua tshinyi uvua muenze bualu ebu muinshi mua diulu, kadi tshivuaye muenze tshivua tshia kuditshibula—mu bisabata mu tshianza, ne tshilamba tshia ku mutu—muinshi mua bulalu; pavua, ku diambuluisha dia dipuekesha dia mbila ne dikankamika dia mishindu ne mishindu, ngakamona ne: uvua wenza mudimu mukole wa kudikosa; nansha mudiku kakuyi mukenji wa bulongame undi mumvue, muntu yonso udi ne tshia kuikala nkayende padiye uvuala bisabata biende. Kadi Queequeg, udi mumone, uvua tshifukibua tshivua mu tshikondo tshia dishintuluka—katshivua tshipupu anyi tshipupu to. Uvua anu ne tshibidilu tshia kuleja bumuntu buende bua pa buabu mu mishindu ya dikema. Dilongesha diende kadivua dianji kujika to. Uvua mulongi wa kalasa ka kumpala. Bu yeye kayi muikale ne lungenyi lukese, kavua mua kuikala muditatshishe ne bisabata nansha kakese; kadi pashishe, bu yeye kayi anu muena tshinyangu, kavua mua kuikala muele meji bua kubuela muinshi mua bulalu bua kubivuala to. Ndekelu wa bionso, wakapatuka ne tshifulu tshiende tshinyanguke bikole ne tshinyanguke pa mêsu ende, ne kutuadijaye kuela mbila ne kuendakana mu nzubu, bienze anu bu ne: kavua muibidilangane ne bisabata, bilamba biende bibidi bia dikoba dia ngombe bivua binyanguke ne bivua binyanguke—pamuapa kabiyi bienza bilondeshile dilomba—bivua bimutapa ne bimukengesha ku ntuadijilu kua dinda dia mashika makole.

Bu muvuaye mumone, mpindieu, ne: kakuvua rido ku dididishi to, ne muvua njila mukese menemene, nzubu uvua mutangilangane nende uvua ufila tshimuenenu tshimpe tshia mu nzubu, ne uvua umona bikole tshimfuanyi tshibi tshivua Queequeg muenze, wenda unyunguluka kayi ne tshintu tshikuabu to, anu tshifulu tshiende ne bisabata biende; Ngakamusengelela mu mushindu mumvua mua kukokesha, bua kuendesha tshiibi tshiende tshia kuowa mâyi ndambu, ne nangananga bua kubuela mu bilamba biende bia pantaloon lukasa. Wakatumikila, ne pashishe kutuadijaye kuowa mâyi. Dîba adi mu dinda muena Kristo yonso uvua mua kuowa mâyi ku mpala; kadi Queequeg, ku dikema dianyi, wakasanka anu bua kuowa mâyi ku tshiadi, ku maboko ne ku bianza. Pashishe wakavuala kazaku kende, ne kuangataye tshitupa tshia nsabanga mukole pa mesa a munkatshi mua tshilamba tshia kuowa mâyi, kutshielaye mu mayi ne kutuadijaye kuela butoke ku mpala wende. Mvua mutangile bua kumona muaba uvuaye mulame tshingoma tshiende, pavuaye wangata tshingoma tshia mu ditumba dia bulalu, upatula mutshi mule, upatula mutu, utshikosa kakese pa tshilamba tshiende, ne wenda uya too ne ku tshitupa tshia lumuenu tshivua tshitangile ku tshimanu, utuadija kukosa bikole, anyi kuela tshingoma ku matama ende. Meme, Queequeg, ndi ngela meji ne, udi wenza mudimu ne bia kudia bitambe buimpe bia Rogers ne disombuela. Pashishe ngakadiebeja kakese bua mudimu eu pangakafika ku dimanya tshiamu tshimpe tshidibu benze natshi mutu wa tshingoma, ne mudi mishiku mile milulame misangu yonso mikole menemene.

Tshiamu tshienda tshia kuowela tshiakajika diakamue, ne wakapatuka mu nzubu ne lutambishi luonso, muvuale tshilamba tshiende tshinene tshia nkima, ne wenda ne tshingoma tshiende bu tshingoma tshia mfumu wa basalayi.





SHAPITA 5. Biakudia bia mu dinda.

Ngakalonda lukasa, ne meme kupuekela mu nzubu wa kunua maluvu meme kuakidila muena nzubu uvua useka bimpe menemene. Tshivua ne lungenyi lubi nansha lumue kudiye to, nansha muvuaye muenze malu mabi nanyi nansha kakese mu tshilumbu tshia mulunda wanyi wa bulalu.

Ino, kuseka kiyampe i kintu kiyampe kikatampe, kadi i kintu kiyampe kikatampe; luse ludi luvula. Nunku, bikala muntu nansha umue, mu bumuntu buende, mua kufila bintu bia kusekesha nabi muntu mukuabu, kikadi muena nyima to, kadi adilekelele ne disanka dionso bua kupitshisha makuta ne kupitshisha makuta mu mushindu au. Ne muntu udi ne tshintu tshia kusekesha natshi, ikala mujadike ne kudi malu a bungi mu muntu au kupita muudi mua kuela meji.

Nzubu wa maluvu uvua mpindieu muwule tente ne bantu bavua balue butuku bua kumpala, ne bamvua tshiyi muanji kubatangila bimpe to. Bavua pabuipi ne bonso bena baleine; bena mudimu banene, ne bena mudimu babidi, ne bena mudimu basatu, ne benji ba mabaya, ne benji ba mitaku ya mu mayi, ne benji ba tshiamu tshia mitaku, ne benji ba nzembu, ne balami ba mazuwa; tshisumbu tshia bantu bafike ne ba mubidi mukole, ne ndevu mikole; tshisumbu tshia bantu badi kabayi bakose nsuki, badi ne nsuki mikole, bonso bavuale bilamba bia nkima bua bilamba bia mu dinda.

Uvua mua kuamba patoke bungi bua matuku avua yonso wa kudibu muikale ku muelelu wa mâyi. Ditama dia nsongalume eu didi ne makanda a mubidi didi anu bu tshimuma tshia poire tshiosha kudi munya, ne didi mua kumueneka bu didi diunvuika bu musky; kena mua kuikala mufika matuku asatu ku luendu luende lua mu Inde to. Muntu udi ku luseke luende udi umueneka mutoke kakese; udi mua kuamba ne tshitupa tshia mutshi wa satin tshidi munda muende. Mu dikoba dia tshitupa tshisatu mudi anu dikoba dia tropique, kadi dilengeja kakese; kakuyi mpata uvua mushale mbingu mijima ku muelelu wa mâyi. Kadi nnganyi uvua mua kuleja ditama bu dia Queequeg? udi, mubuikila ne misombelu mishilashilangane, uvua umueneka bu tshibandabanda tshia ku ouest tshia Andes, bua kuleja mu mulongo umue, mishilangane ya luya, tshitupa ne tshitupa.

"Grub, ho!" Mpindieu muena nzubu wakadila, wakakangula tshiibi, ne tuetu kubuela bua kudia mu dinda.

Badi bamba ne: bantu badi bamone malu a pa buloba, badi balua kuikala ne bupole mu nsombelu, ne badi ne budikadidi padibu mu tshisumbu. Kadi ki mmisangu yonso to: Ledyard, muenji wa ngendu munene wa mu Nouvelle-Angleterre, ne Mungo Park, wa mu Écosse; pa bantu bonso, badi ne dishindika dikese mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi. Ino pamua kusabuka patupu Sibérie mu ditempu dikoka kudi mbwa bu muvua Ledyard muenze, anyi kuendakana mutantshi mule nkayende ne tshifu tshitupu, mu mutshima wa bena Afrike, tshivua tshisangisha tshia bienzedi bia Mungo mupele—ndi ngamba ne: luendu lua mushindu eu kaluena mua kuikala mushindu muimpe wa kupeta lumu lua bungi to. Nansha nanku, mu tshitupa tshinene, tshintu tshia mushindu eu tshidi mua kuikala muaba wonso.

Malu atudi tuela meji aa adi afumina ku nsombelu wa se: tuetu bonso bamane kusomba ku mêsa, ne mvua ndilongolola bua kumvua miyuki mimpe ya diloba dia baleine; ku dikema dianyi, pabuipi ne muntu yonso wakashala mupuwe bikole. Ne ki nganu ke bualu abu to, kadi bavua bamueneka bu bavua ne bundu. Eyowa, apa muvua tshisumbu tshia mbua ya mu mayi, ba bungi ba kudibu kabayi ne bundu nansha bukese bavua babande mu mazuwa a baleine manene mu mbuu minene—benyi bonso kudibu—ne babaluisha bafue kabayi banyingalala; ne nansha nanku, apa bavua basombe ku mêsa a biakudia bia mu dinda—bonso bavua ne dibikila dia muomumue, bonso bavua ne majinga a muomumue—batangilangana ne bundu bu muvuabu kabayi banji kumona tshikumbi kampanda tshia mikoko munkatshi mua Mikuna ya Vert to. Tshintu tshia kumona tshia dikema; urse eyi ya bundu, baleine ya mvita ya buôwa!

Kadi bua Queequeg—bua tshinyi, Queequeg uvua musombe munkatshi muabu—ku mutu kua mesa, kabidi, bivua bienzeke anu ku mpukapuka; bu mudi mashika. Bua kuamba bulelela, tshiena mua kuamba malu a bungi bua dilela diende to. Muntu uvua umuanyisha bikole kavua mua kuikala mubingishe ne muoyo mujima bua muvuaye mulue ne tshingoma tshiende mu tshiakudia tshia mu dinda, ne muenze natshi mudimu kuntu aku kakuyi tshilele to; kufikaye pa mutu pa mesa natshi, ku njiwu idi pabuipi ya mitu ya bungi, ne kukuata nyama ya ngombe kudiye. Kadi bualu abu buvua buenzeke ne bupole buonso kudiye, ne muntu yonso mmumanye ne: mu lungenyi lua bantu ba bungi, kuenza bualu buonso ne bupole nkubuenza ne bupole.

Katuakula bua malu onso a pa buawu a Queequeg apa to; mushindu kayi uvuaye wepuka kafe ne mampa a kapia, ne uvua uteka ntema yende yonso ku nyama ya ngombe, ivua yenzeka misangu mikese. Bikumbane, bua se: pavua biakudia bia mu dinda bijika, wakapatuka bu bakuabu mu nzubu wa bantu bonso, kutemeshaye tshiamu tshiende tshia tomahawk, ne uvua musombe muaba au mupuwe wenda udia ne unua makanya ne tshilamba tshiende tshivua katshiyi mua kutapuluka, pamvua mupatuke bua kuendakana.





SHAPITA 6. Njila.

Bu meme mukeme kumpala pamvua mumone muntu wa pa buende bu mudi Queequeg wenda unyunguluka munkatshi mua bantu ba mu tshimenga tshimpe, dikema adi diakajika diakamue pamvua muenze luendu luanyi lua kumpala lua munya mu misesu ya mu New Bedford.

Mu njila minene idi pabuipi ne tshitudilu tshia mazuwa, tshitudilu tshionso tshinene tshia ku mbuu tshidi mua kufila misangu ya bungi mushindu wa kumona bintu bia dikema bia mu bitupa bia ku babende. Nansha mu misesu ya Broadway ne ya Chestnut, bena mazuwa ba mu Mediterane batu imue misangu batuta bakaji badi ne buôwa. Njila wa Regent ki mmumanyike kudi bena Lascar ne bena Malaisie to; ne ku Bombay, mu Apollo Green, bena Yankees badi ne muoyo batu misangu ya bungi batshinyisha bena ditunga. Ino New Bedford udi upita Water Street ne Wapping. Mu miaba ya ndekelu eyi udi umona anu bena mazuwa; kadi mu New Bedford, bantu badi badia bantu badi bimana bayukila ku nseke ya njila; nyama ya luonji; ba bungi ba kudibu batshidi anu ne mifuba yabu ne mubidi udi kauyi wa tshijila. Bidi bienza bua muntu udi kayi mumanye atangile.

Ino, pa kumbusha Feegeeans, Tongatobooarrs, Erromanggoans, Pannangians, ne Brighggians, ne, pa kumbusha bifukibua bia muitu bia mazuwa a baleine adi endakana mu misesu, neumone bintu bikuabu bidi bitamba kusankisha, ne bia kusekesha. Lumingu luonso batu bafika mu tshimenga etshi bantu ba bungi ba mu Vermont ne ba mu New Hampshire, bonso badi ne nyota ya kupeta bintu ne butumbi mu mudimu wa diloba. Badi batamba kuikala bansonga, badi ne mibidi mikole; bantu bakuabu badi bakose mitshi, ne mpindieu badi bakeba bua kuela tshingoma panshi ne kukuata tshingoma tshia baleine. Mivule idi ne matamba bu mudi mikuna ya matamba muvuabu bafumine. Mu amue malu udi mua kuela meji ne: mmapite mêba makese. Tangila kuntu aku! muntu au udi wenda unyunguluka mu ditumba. Udi muvuale tshilamba tshia beaver ne tshilamba tshia mukila wa nyunyi wa nkutshi, muvuale mukaba wa mazuwa ne muele wa mvita. Apa udi ulua mukuabu ne tshilamba tshia sou'-wester ne tshilamba tshia bombazine.

Kakuena dandy wa mu tshimenga udi mua kufuanyikija ne wa mu mpata—ndi musue kuamba dandy wa bumpkin wa bushuwa—muntu udi, mu matuku a mbua, mua kukosa madimi ende abidi ne bianza bia mbuji bua buôwa bua kunyanguka bianza biende. Mpindieu padi muntu wa mu mpata bu mudi eu wangata bualu ebu mu mutu bua kuenza lumu luimpe, ne kubuela mu tshisumbu tshinene tshia diloba dia baleine, udi ne bua kumona malu a tuseku adiye wenza padiye ufika ku tshitudilu tshia mazuwa. Padiye wakula bua tshilamba tshienda tshia pa mbuu, udi ulomba bua kuela butoyi ku bilamba biende bia ku makasa; mikaba ku pantalo yende ya tshilamba. Ah, Hay-Seed mupele! mushindu kayi wikala mikaba ayi mua kupatuka mu tshipepele tshikole tshia kumpala, paudi muendeshibue, mikaba, butonde, ne bionso, panshi mu nshingu wa tshipepele tshikole.

Kadi kuela meji ne: tshimenga etshi tshiende lumu tshidi anu ne bantu badi ne mpungi, bantu badi badia bantu ne nyama ya nsengu bua kuleja benyi bende to. Nansha kakese. Nansha nanku New Bedford mmuaba wa bantu ba tshianana. Bu tuetu katuyi bena baleine, tshitupa tshia buloba atshi tshivua mua kuikala lelu mu nsombelu wa mbila anu bu mudi ku muelelu wa mâyi wa Labrador. Bu mudibi, bitupa bia ditunga diabu dia panyima bidi bikumbane bua kutshinyisha muntu, bidi bimueneka bu bia mifuba.
