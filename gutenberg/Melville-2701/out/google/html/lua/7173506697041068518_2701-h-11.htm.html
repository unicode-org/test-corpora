SHAPITA 53. Gam.

Bualu buvua bumueneka buvua bufikishe Ahaba ku dibenga kubuela mu mazuwa a baleine atuvua batele buvua ne: lupepele ne mbuu bivua bileja bipepele bikole. Kadi nansha biobi kabiyi nanku, kavua mua kuikala mumubande to, pamuapa, bilondeshile bienzedi biende biakalonda mu bikondo bia muomumue, bikalabi nanku, ku diambuluisha dia dibikila, uvua mupete diandamuna dibi ku lukonko luvuaye muele. Bualu, bu mudibi bimueneka, kavua uditatshisha bua kusomba, nansha bua minite itanu, ne kapitene nansha umue uvuaye kayi mumanye to, anu yeye mua kufila amue malu avuaye ukeba ne muoyo mujima. Kadi bionso ebi bidi mua kushala kabiyi bikumbane, bu kabiyi bualu kampanda budibu bambe apa bua mishindu ya pa buayi idibu benza mudimu ne mazuwa a baleine padiwu asambakena mu mbuu mikuabu, ne nangananga pa muaba umue wa ngendu.

Shi benyi babidi basabuka Pine Barrens mu New York State, nansha Salisbury Plain mu Angleterre; Shi badi batuilangana mu mpata ya nunku idi kayiyi mimpe, aba babidi, bua muoyo wabu, kabena mua kuepuka dianyisha dia muntu ne muntu to; ne kuimana bua tshitupa tshîpi bua kushintakaja ngumu; ne, pamuapa, kusomba panshi bua tshitupa tshîpi ne kuikisha mu tshisumbu: pashishe, mmunyi mudibi bitamba kuikala bia pa tshibidilu kupita pa Pine Barrens ne Salisbury Plains ya mu mbuu, mazuwa abidi a baleine adi alejangana ku ndekelu kua buloba—ku tshidila tshia Fanning nkayatshi, anyi ku King’s Mills udi kule; Ndi ngamba ne, bidi bia pa tshibidilu bua se: mu nsombelu ya nunku, mazuwa aa kaena anu ne bua kushintakaja mvula wa mabue to, kadi adi ne bua kulua mu malanda mimpe, a bulunda ne a bulunda. Ne nangananga bualu ebu budi mua kumueneka bu bualu bua pa tshibidilu, mu tshilumbu tshia mazuwa adi mu tshitudilu tshimue tshia mazuwa, ne adi bamfumu bawu, banene ba basalayi, ne ki mbantu bakese ba kudibu bamanyangana; ne pashishe, kuikala ne bintu bia mu nzubu bia mishindu yonso bia kuakula.

Bua mazuwa adi kaayiku mutantshi mule, muena mudimu wa pambelu, pamuapa, udi ne mikanda mu mazuwa; nansha biobi bikale mushindu kayi, neamulekele bua ikale ne mabeji a dituku kampanda tshidimu tshimue anyi bibidi panyima pa tshia ndekelu pa dosie yende idi kayiyi mimanyike bimpe ne idi ne tshianza tshinene. Ne bua kupingaja bulenga abu, mazuwa avua aya pambelu avua ne bua kupeta ngumu ya ndekelu ya diloba dia baleine ivua mifumine muaba uvuabu mua kuya, bualu bua mushinga mukole kudiye. Ne mu tshitupa, bionso ebi nebikale bilelela pa bidi bitangila mazuwa a baleine adi asabuka njila wa umue ne mukuabu pa tshipalu tshia ngendu nkayatshi, nansha mudiwu kaayi kumbelu mutantshi mule. Bualu umue wa kudibu uvua mua kuikala mupete dituma dia mikanda kudi muntu mukuabu muisatu, ne mpindieu udi kule menemene; ne imue ya ku mikanda ayi idi mua kuikala ya bantu ba mu mazuwa adiye upetangana nende mpindieu. Kabidi, bavua mua kuyukila lumu lua diloba dia baleine, ne kuyukila bimpe. Bualu kabavua anu mua kusambakena ne luse luonso lua bena matu to, kadi kabidi ne malu onso a pa buawu avua afumina ku dikeba dia muomumue ne ku dipanga dia bintu ne njiwu bivuabu babanyangana.

Kabidi dishilangana dia ditunga kadiena mua kuenza dishilangana dia mushinga mukole to; mbuena kuamba ne, pikala bantu bonso babidi bakula muakulu umue, anu mudibi bua bena Amerika ne bena Anglais. Nansha mudibi nanku, bua bungi bukese bua bena Angleterre badi baloba baleine, bisangilu bia nunku kabitu bienzeka misangu ya bungi to, ne padibi bienzeka bidi mua kuikala ne bundu pankatshi pabu; bualu bua muena Angleterre webe udi ne budimu, ne muena Yankee webe, kena musue bualu bua mushindu eu kudi muntu mukuabu to, anu yeye nkayende. Kabidi, balobi ba baleine ba mu Angleterre batu imue misangu ne bukole bua mu tshimenga tshinene kupita balobi ba baleine ba mu Amerike; pa bidi bitangila Nantucketer mule, mutekete, ne malu ende a mu provense adi kaayi umvuika, bu muena madimi wa mu mbuu. Kadi muaba udi bunene ebu bua baleine ba mu Angleterre, bidi mua kuikala bikole bua kuamba, bualu bena Yankees mu dituku dimue, mu tshisumbu, badi bashipa baleine ba bungi kupita bena Angleterre bonso, mu tshisumbu, mu bidimu dikumi. Kadi etshi ntshilema tshikese tshidi katshiyi ne bualu mu bena Angleterre badi baloba baleine, tshidi muena Nantucketer kayi wangata ne mushinga to; pamuapa, bualu mmumanye ne: yeye pende udi ne bilema bikese.

Nunku, tudi tumona ne: pa mazuwa onso adi enda pa nkayawu mu mbuu, balobi ba baleine ke badi ne bualu bua bungi bua kuikala ne malanda ne bantu—ne badi nanku. Padi, mazuwa makuabu a bangenda mushinga adi asabuka mu tshitupa tshia munkatshi mua Mbuu wa Atlantique, misangu ya bungi adi apita kaayi nansha muaku nansha umue wa dianyisha, adi ashipangana mu mbuu munene, bu tshisumbu tshia bantu badi mu Broadway; ne dîba dionso didifila, pamuapa, mu dipenda dia ndekelu pa tshintu tshia muntu ne muntu. Pa bidi bitangila bantu ba mvita, padibu bapeta mushindu wa kusambakena mu mbuu, badi banji kupitshila mu milongo ya malu a bupote ne dikosa dia mishiku, dibunda dia bendela, bidi bimueneka ne: kakuena dinanga dia muoyo muimpe ne dinanga dia bana betu nansha dikese to. Pa bidi bitangila tshisangilu tshia mazuwa a bapika, bua tshinyi, badi ne lukasa lua bungi, badi banyemangana lukasa. Kadi pa bidi bitangila ba-Pirate, padibu bapeta mushindu wa kusabuka mifuba ya muntu ne muntu, mvula wa kumpala udi ne: “Mifuba bungi munyi?”—mu mushindu wa muomumue ne udi balobi ba baleine bamba ne: “Milondo bungi munyi?” Ne lukonku elu lumane kuandamuna, bena tshinyangu badi bapatuka diakamue, bualu badi bantu babi ba mu inferno ku nseke yonso ibidi, ne kabatu basue kumona bantu babi ba bungi to.

Kadi tangila muena nzambi, muena bululame, kayi udileja, muena diakidila benyi, muena malu a bantu, muena budikadidi ne mupepele! Ntshinyi tshidi muena baleine wenza padiye upetangana ne muena baleine mukuabu mu tshikondo kayi tshionso tshia luya luimpe? Udi ne " Gam ," tshintu tshidi katshiyi tshimanyike kudi mazuwa makuabu onso adibu kabayi banji kumvua dîna edi to; ne pikalabu bumvue bualu ebu ku mpukapuka, badi anu baseka, ne bapingaja malu a bundu adi atangila “spouters” ne “blubber-boilers,” ne malu makuabu bu mudi: mbila milenga. Bua tshinyi bena ngendu bonso ba mu mayi, ne kabidi bena tshinyangu ne bena mvita bonso, ne bena mazuwa a bapika, badi ne lungenyi lua dipepeja dia nunku kudi mazuwa a baleine; elu i lukonku ludi mua kuikala lukole bua kuandamuna. Bualu, mu tshilumbu tshia bena tshinyangu, tuambe ne, ndi musue kumanya ni mudimu wabu udi ne butumbi bua pa buabu. Imue misangu bitu bijika mu dibandila didi kadiyi dia pa tshibidilu, bulelela; kadi anu ku mutshi. Ne pa kumbusha, padi muntu mubandishibua mu mushindu wa pa buawu, kena ne tshishimikidi tshimpe tshia bule buende bua pa mutu to. Nunku, ndi mfika ku diamba ne: mu diditumbisha bua mudiye mubandishibue ku mutu kua baleine, mu diamba edi muena tshinyangu kena ne tshishimikidi tshikole tshia kuimanapu to.

Kadi Gam ntshinyi? Udi mua kutshiokesha munu webe wa tshianza paudi wenda ubanda ne upueka mu milongo ya nkudimuinu, ne kuyi upeta muaku nansha kakese. Dr. Johnson kakafika nansha kakese ku dimanya edi to; Mazuwa a Noa Webster kaena ne bualu ebu to. Nansha nanku, muaku wa muomumue eu udi mpindieu wenza mudimu munkatshi mua bidimu bia bungi munkatshi mua bena Yankees baledibue binunu dikumi ne bitanu. Bushuwa, bidi bikengela diumvuija, ne bidi ne tshia kubuejibua mu mukanda wa miaku. Ne mmuenenu eu, lekelayi mumvuija ne lungenyi luonso.

GAM. NOUN— Tshisangilu tshia bantu babidi ( anyi ba bungi ) ba baleine, pa tshibidilu pa tshipalu tshia ngendu; padibu, kunyima kua bamane kushintakaja mvula wa mabue, badi bashintakaja dikumbula dia bena mudimu ba mu mazuwa: ba kapitene babidi bashala, bua tshikondo atshi, mu mazuwa amue, ne bamfumu babidi ba mu mazuwa makuabu.

Kudi bualu bukuabu bukese budi butangila manaya a mfranga budi kabuyi ne bua kupua muoyo apa. Midimu yonso idi ne bintu biayi bikese bia pa buabi; ke mudibi kabidi bua diloba dia baleine. Mu mazuwa a bena tshinyangu, a mvita, anyi a bapika, padibu bambula kapitene muaba wonso mu buatu buende, utu anu usomba mu mabeji a kunyima kua mazuwa pa nkuasa muimpe, imue misangu udi ne miosa, ne misangu ya bungi udi udilombola ne tshiamu tshikese tshilenga tshia mudimu tshilengeja ne mionji ne mionji. Kadi buatu bua baleine kabuena ne muaba wa kusomba kunyima, kabuena ne tshibasa tshia mushindu eu nansha kakese, ne kabuena ne tshiamu tshia kuendesha natshi nansha kakese. Misangu ya bungi bulelela, bu bakapitene ba baleine bavua benda mu mayi pa makalu bu mudi balombodi ba kale ba gouty mu nkuasa ya patent. Ne pa bidi bitangila tshiamu tshia mudimu, buatu bua baleine kabuena buitaba nansha kakese bumuntu bua bakaji bua nunku to; ne nunku bu mudibi mu manaya, bena mudimu ba buatu bonso badi ne bua kumbuka mu buatu, ne nunku bu mudi mulombodi wa buatu anyi muena tshingoma muikale wa bungi, muena mudimu au ke udi mulombodi wa buatu pa tshikondo atshi, ne kapitene, kayi ne muaba wa kusomba, udi ukoka bua kuya kumutangila bonso bimane bu mutshi wa pine. Ne misangu ya bungi neumone ne: bu mudiye mumanye ne: mêsu a buloba bujima adi amueneka adi amutangila ku nseke ya mazuwa abidi, kapitene eu udi muimane udi mumanye mushinga wa kulama bunême buende pa kulama maulu ende. Kabidi ki mbualu bupepele to; bualu kunyima kuende kudi tshiamu tshinene tshia kuendesha natshi mashinyi tshidi tshimukuma mpindieu ne pashishe mu tshitupa tshikese tshia mukolo wende, tshiamu tshia panyima tshidi tshimupingaja pa kumukuma binu kumpala. Nunku udi mubuikila kumpala ne panyima, ne udi anu mua kudiundisha ku luseke padiye usomba pa maulu ende maolola; kadi dikuma dia buatu dia tshimpitshimpi ne dia tshikisu didi mua kuya kule bua kumupuekesha, bualu bule bua tshishimikidi kabuena ne mushinga padiku kakuyi bulampe budi bupetangana to. Enza anu tshitupa tshia mitshi ibidi, ne kuena mua kuimana to. Pashishe, kabidi, kabivua mua kuenza mu mêsu a bantu ba pa buloba to, kabivua mua kuenza nansha kakese, ndi ngamba, bua kapitene eu uvua mubandile pa mutu bua kumumonaye udijadika nansha kakese pa kukuata tshintu tshionso ne bianza biende; bulelela, bu tshimanyishilu tshia bukokeshi buende buonso, udi utamba kuambula bianza biende mu mifuku ya bilamba biende; kadi pamuapa bu mudiye ne bianza binene ne bilema, udi ubimbula kuntu aku bua kubiteka nabi. Nansha nanku kudi misangu mikuabu, idi mijadikibue bimpe kabidi, mudi kapitene mumanyibue bua tshikondo kampanda tshia ntatu tshidi katshiyi tshia pa tshibidilu anyi bibidi, mu tshipepele tshikole tshia tshimpitshimpi tshidi tshiamba—bua kukuata nsuki ya muendeshi wa buatu udi pabuipi, ne kulamamu bu lufu lukole.





SHAPITA 54. Muyuki wa Town-Ho.

( Anu mudibu bambe ku Golden Inn. )

Cape of Good Hope, ne bitupa bionso bia mayi bidi bimunyunguluke, bidi anu bu bitupa binayi bia njila munene, muaba uudi mua kupetangana ne benyi ba bungi kupita muaba mukuabu wonso.

Kabivua bipite matuku a bungi kunyima kua yeye mumane kuakula muakulu wa Goney to, ke pakatuilanganaye ne muena baleine mukuabu uvua uya kumbelu, Town-Ho,*. Uvua mulombodibua pabuipi ne mujima kudi bena Polynésie. Mu manaya makese avua malonda, wakatupesha lumu lukole lua Moby Dick. Bua bamue bantu, dijinga dia bantu bonso dia baleine mutoke divua mpindieu dikoleshibue bikole kudi bualu kampanda bua muyuki wa Town-Ho, buvua bumueneka bu buvua butangila baleine bualu kampanda bua dikema, buvua buenzeke mu mushindu kampanda uvuabu babikila ne: malumbuluisha a Nzambi adibu bamba ne: avua apita bamue bantu. Bualu ebu bua ndekelu, ne bintu biabu bia pa buabi, bidi bienza tshitudi mua kubikila ne: tshitupa tshia mu musokoko tshia njiwu idibu ne bua kulonda, katshivua tshifike ku matshi a Kapitene Ahaba anyi a balunda bende to. Bualu bua tshitupa tshia musokoko tshia muyuki katshivua tshimanyike kudi kapitene wa Town-Ho nkayende to. Bivua bintu bia pa nkaya bia bena mazuwa batoka basatu bavua mu tshisumbu tshia mazuwa au, umue wa kudibu, udi umueneka, wakamanyisha Tashtego bualu ebu ne mikenji ya bena Lomo ya tshisokomenu, kadi butuku buakalonda Tashtego wakatambakana mu tulu tuende, ne wakasokolola malu a bungi mu mushindu au, ne pakamujula kavua mua kulama malu makuabu to. Nansha nanku, bualu ebu buvua ne bukole bua bungi pa bena mazuwa ba mu Pequod bavua balue kubumanya bimpe, ne ku diambuluisha dia bulenga bua dikema, bua kububikila nanku, bavua babalombola mu bualu ebu, ke bualu kayi bakalama bualu busokoka munkatshi muabu bua kabuenzeki nansha kakese kunyima kua tshitekelu tshinene tshia mazuwa a Pequod. Kulamakaja mu muaba watshi muakane tshintu etshi tshifiike ne muyuki bu mudiwu mumanyishibua patoke mu mazuwa, bualu ebu buonso bua dikema ndi mpindieu ntungunuka ne kubuteka mu mukanda wa kashidi.

*Muadi wa baleine wa kale pa kumona baleine bua musangu wa kumpala ku mutu kua tshiamu tshia mashinyi, utshidibu benza nende mudimu kudi bena baleine mu diloba dia terrapin wa mu Gallipagos.

Bua bualu buanyi bua kusekesha, nenlame mushindu umvua mulondeshe bualu ebu ku Lima, kudi balunda banyi bena Espagne bavua basomba, dituku dikuabu dia tshijila, bavua banua makanya pa tshipalu tshia nzubu wa tshilala benyi wa Golden Inn. Mu basalayi ba pa tubalu aba, bansonga bena Don, Pedro ne Sebastian, bavua mu malanda mimpe nanyi; ne ke bualu kayi nkonko ya pa tshibidilu idibu bela imue misangu, ne idibu bandamuna bimpe tshikondo atshi.

“Bidimu bibidi kumpala kua meme kumanya malu andi musue kunulongesha, bana betu, Town-Ho, Sperm Whaler wa mu Nantucket, uvua wenda mu mazuwa mu Pacifique wenu apa, kayi matuku a bungi a luendu lua ku est kumbukila ku tshiibi tshia nzubu muimpe wa Golden Inn eu.
