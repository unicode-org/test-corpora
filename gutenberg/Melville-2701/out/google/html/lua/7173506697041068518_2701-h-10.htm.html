Uvua tshifukibua tshia mushindu eu tshidi bantu ba mu nzubu ba mu bitupa bia mashika bamona anu mu bilota biabu, ne bualu ebu anu mu mushindu mubi; kadi badi bafuanangane nabu mpindieu ne mpindieu badi benda munkatshi mua bisumbu bia bena Asia bidi kabiyi bishintuluka, nangananga bidiila bia ku Est bidi ku est kua tshitupa tshia buloba—matunga adi kaayi mua kushintuluka, adi kaayi mua kushintuluka, adi nansha mu matuku etu aa atshidi anu alama malu a bungi a buena-muntu bua mu nyuma a bipungu bia kumpala bia pa buloba, pavua tshivulukilu tshia muntu wa kumpala tshikale tshintu tshisunguluke, tshivulukilu tshia ndelanganyi yende yonso, kayiyi mimanyibue kuvuaye mufumine, batangilangana bu bintu bia bushuwa bia mu nyuma, ne bakebeja dîba ne ngondo bua tshinyi bavua babafuke ne bua tshinyi; padi, bilondeshile Genese, banjelu bavua bushuwa badisangishe ne bana ba bakaji ba bantu, bademon kabidi, badi basakidila balabi badi kabayi ba mu mikenji, bavua baditue mu malu a patupu.





SHAPITA 51. Nyuma-Mupuya.

Matuku, mbingu biakapita, ne muinshi mua buatu bupepele, Pequod wa meno a ntambue uvua mupitshile bitekete bitekete mu miaba inayi ya bungi ya ngendu; udi pabuipi ne Azores; ku mpenga kua Cape de Verdes; pa Plate (udibu babikila nunku), muikale ku mushimi wa Rio de la Plata; ne Carrol Ground, muaba udi kayi ne mitshi, udi ne mâyi, ku Sud kua St. Helena.

Patshivuaye wenda upitshila mu mayi a ndekelu aa, butuku kampanda bua ditalala ne bua ngondo, pavua mavuala onso apita bu mivungu ya arjan; ne, ku diambuluisha dia luya luabu lutekete, lukole, bakenza tshivua tshimueneka bu ditalala dia arjan, kadi ki mbua nkaya to; mu butuku bua mutoyi nunku, ndeke wa arjan uvua umueneka kule kumpala kua bipepele bitoke bia ku mutu. Mukenkesha kudi ngondo, uvua umueneka bu wa mu diulu; bivua bimueneka ne: nzambi kampanda uvua ne miosa ne uvua ukenka uvua ujuka mu mbuu. Fedallah ke uvua muanji kumvuija ndeke eu. Bualu bua butuku ebu bua ngondo, uvua ne tshibidilu tshia kubanda ku mutu kua tshibandilu tshinene, ne kuimana bua kutangila kuntu aku, ne bujalame bua muomumue bu muvua munya. Nansha nanku, nansha muvuabu bamona bisumbu bia baleine butuku, kakuvua muena baleine nansha umue pa lukama uvua mua kubapuekesha to. Udi mua kuela meji ne lungenyi kayi, nunku, bena mazuwa bavua bamone muena Orient wa kale eu musombe muulu mu mêba a pa buawu nunku; tshilamba tshiende ne ngondo, balunda mu diulu dimue. Ino padi, kunyima kua yeye mumane kupitshisha tshikondo tshiende tshia mudimu kuntu aku butuku buvule bulondangane kayi mupatule nansha dîyi dimue; pavua, kunyima kua ditalala dionso edi, dîyi diende dia pa buloba diumvuika dimanyisha ndeke wa arjan, mutokesha wa ngondo, muena mazuwa yonso uvua mulale wakabanga kujuka bienze anu bu ne: nyuma kampanda wa mapuapua uvua mutoke mu tshiamu tshia mazuwa, ne kuela bena mudimu ba pa buloba tuasakidila. "Ke udi ufuma!" Bu mpungi wa dilumbuluisha muele, kabavua mua kuikala bateketa kabidi to; nansha nanku kabavua bumvua buôwa nansha bumue to; kadi disanka. Bualu nansha muvua dîba divua kadiyi dia pa tshibidilu, kadi kudila kuvua kukemesha bikole, ne kuvua kusankisha bikole, bienza bua ne: muntu yonso uvua mu buatu au uvua musue kupuekeshibua panshi.

Pavuaye wenda pa mazuwa ne lubilu lua bungi, Ahaba wakatuma dîyi bua kuteka mazuwa a mâyi ne makalu a bukalenge, ne mazuwa onso a stunsail avua matangalake. Muntu mutambe buimpe mu buatu ke udi ne bua kuangata tshifulu tshia mazuwa. Pashishe, ne mutu wa mazuwa onso muikale ne bantu, mazuwa avua mabuikila avua akulukila panshi kumpala kua lupepele. Mushindu wa dikema, wa tshinyangu, wa dibandisha dia lupepele lua taffrail luwuja miaba ya mazuwa a bungi, uvua wenza bua tshibandilu tshivua tshienda tshibandila tshiumvuike bu kapepe muinshi mua makasa; patshivuaye anu wenda lubilu, bienze anu bu ne: malu abidi avua amuluisha avua amuluisha—umue bua kubanda buludiludi mu diulu, mukuabu bua kuendesha lubilu lukole too ne ku tshipatshila kampanda tshivua tshitangile ku luseke. Ne bu wewe mutangile mpala wa Ahaba butuku abu, uvua mua kuikala wela meji ne: kudiye pende kudi malu abidi mashilangane avua aluangana. Pavua dikasa diende dimue dia muoyo dienza mutoyi wa bukole pa tshibandilu, dikuma dionso dia tshiadi tshiende tshifue divua diumvuika bu dikuma dia sanduku wa lufu. Pa muoyo ne lufu muntu mununu eu uvua wenda. Kadi nansha muvua buatu buenda lubilu lukole, ne nansha muvua mêsu onso, bu mikete, mêsu avua amueneka ne dijinga dikole, nansha nanku ndeke wa arjan kavua umueneka kabidi butuku abu to. Muendeshi wa buatu yonso uvua utshipa ne: uvua mumone bualu ebu musangu umue, kadi ki mmusangu muibidi to.

Tshintu etshi tshia munkatshi mua butuku tshivua pabuipi ne kukola tshintu tshivuabu bapue muoyo, pavua, matuku makese pashishe, monayi! mu diba dimua dia mutoyi, diakamanyishibua kabidi: kabidi diakamanyishibua kudi bonso; kadi pavuaye muenze buatu bua kumupita, wakajimina kabidi bienze anu bu ne: kavua muanji kujimina to. Ne nunku bivua bituenzela mudimu butuku ne butuku, too ne pavua muntu nansha umue kayi uteleja anu bua kudikema. Mu mushindu wa dikema mu butoke bua ngondo, anyi butoke bua mitoto, bilondeshile mudibi mua kuikala; kujimina kabidi bua dituku dijima, anyi matuku abidi, anyi asatu; ne mu mushindu kampanda umueneka ku dipingaja dionso dishilangane bu uvua wenda uya kumpala ne kumpala mu mashinyi etu, ndeke eu uvua nkayende uvua umueneka utukoka kashidi.

Nansha ne ditabuja dia kale dia tshisamba tshiabu, ne bilondeshile malu a patupu, bu muvuabi bimueneka, avua mu malu a bungi avua matekeshe Pequod, kabavuaku dijinga dia bamue ba ku bena mazuwa bavua baditshipa ne: dîba dionso ne muaba wonso uvuabu bumvuija; nansha mu bikondo bia kule, anyi mu latitude ne longitude ya kule, tshina atshi tshivua katshiyi mua kusemena tshivua tshiela kudi baleine umuepele; ne baleine au, Moby Dick. Bua tshikondo kampanda, kuvua kabidi buôwa bua pa buabu bua tshimuenekelu etshi tshivua tshienda tshipita, bienze anu bu ne: tshivua tshitubikila mu mushindu wa dishima, bua se: tshintu tshinene atshi tshitutangile, ne tshitutshibule ndekelu wa bionso mu mbuu wa kule ne wa tshikisu.

Malu aa a tshitupa tshîpi, adi kaayi umvuika bimpe kadi adi mabi menemene, avua mafumine ku ditalala dia tshikondo tshishilangane, muvua, muinshi mua butoke buatshi buonso bua bule, bamue bela meji ne: muvua tshintu tshia diabolo tshisokome, bu muvua matuku ne matuku atuvua tuenda, mu mbuu ne ditshioka dia bungi, dia bupole, dia mu tshibuashibuashi tshionso, bua kudisombuela, mu tshilema tshietu uvua umueneka bu uvua ulekela muoyo kumpala kua tshitudilu tshietu tshivua tshienze bu tshibombi.

Ino, ndekelu wa bionso, patudi tuya ku est, lupepele lua ku Cape luakatuadija kutunyunguluka, ne tuakajuka ne tukulukila pa mbuu mile, mikole idiku; Pavua Pequod uvua ne meno a meno a ntambue muiname bikole ku tshipupu, ne mushipe mavuala a mîdima mu bupote buende, too ne pavua, bu mvula ya bipese bia arjan, bipese bia butoke bivua bibuuka pa mutu pa bimanu biende; pashishe bintu bionso ebi bia munda mutupu bia muoyo biakajika, kadi biakashiya muaba wa bintu bivua bimueneka bibi kupita kumpala.

Pabuipi ne mikete yetu, bintu bia dikema mu mayi bivua bienda bipatuka kumpala kuetu; pavua nyunyi ya mbuu ivua kayiyi mua kujingulula ivua ipueka kunyima kuetu. Ne mu dinda dionso, patudi tusomba pa miaba yetu, milongo ya nyunyi eyi ivua imueneka; ne nansha mutuvua tuela mbila, mutantshi mule tuakalamata ku tshisuku, bienze anu bu ne: bavua bamona mazuwa etu bu mazuwa avua enda apita, kaayi ne muntu; tshintu tshisungula bua kubutula, ne ke bualu kayi tshikumbane bua kuikala muaba wa tshilala benyi bua bobu bine badi kabayi ne nzubu. Ne uvua mubandishibue ne mubandishibue, uvua anu mubandishe mbuu mufike kayi ne ditalala, bienze anu bu ne: mavuala ende manene avua kondo ka muoyo; ne anyima munene wa pa buloba uvua mu kanyinganyinga ne dinyingalala bua mpekatu mule ne makenga avuaye mupatule.

Cape wa Ditekemena Dimpe, badi bakubikila anyi? Kadi Cape Tormentoso, muvuabu babikila kale; bua mutantshi mule mukokibua kudi ditalala dia tshinyangu divua ditufikile kumpala, tuakadimona batue mu mbuu eu uvua mukengeshibue, muvua bantu bavua ne tshibawu bakudimune nyunyi ne mishipa eyi, bavua bamueneka bu bavua ne bua kuowa mâyi kashidi kakuyi muaba wa kusokomena, anyi kukuma lupepele lufiike alu kakuyi muaba nansha umue. Kadi ditalala, ditoke bu neje, ne kadiyi dishintuluka; utshidi anu ulombola tshina tshiende tshia mapuapua muulu; utshidi anu utubikila kumpala, ndeke wa nkayende uvua misangu mikuabu mua kumvuija.

Mu tshikondo tshionso etshi tshia mîdima ya bintu, Ahaba, nansha muvuaye wela meji bua tshikondo atshi bua kuikala ne bukokeshi bua tshiendelele pa tshibandilu tshivua ne mâyi ne tshivua ne njiwu, wakaleja muvuaye ne tshiji tshikole; ne uvua wakula ne bena dibaka nende misangu mikese kupita kumpala. Mu bikondo bia tshipepele tshikole bu mudi ebi, kunyima kua bionso bia muulu ne bia muulu bimane kuikala bijadikibue, kakuena tshikuabu tshidi mua kuenzeka to, anu kuindila tshilumbu tshia tshipepele tshikole. Pashishe Kapitene ne bena diende badi balua bena lufu. Nunku, ne dikasa diende dia meno a ntambue dibueja mu tshina tshiadi, ne tshianza tshimue tshikuate tshilamba tshia kujika natshi, Ahaba uvua wimana mêba a bungi mutangile ku lupepele, eku tshipepele tshikole tshia mashika anyi tshia neje tshivua anu tshikuata nsuki yende pamue. Mu kitatyi'kya, bena mudimu ba pa buatu bavua bafuma ku tshitupa tshia kumpala tshia mazuwa kudi mbuu wa njiwu uvua mubutuka pa mutu pa mazuwa, bavua bimane mu mulongo ku luseke lua bimanu bia mu tshiadi; ne bua kudilama bimpe ku mavuala avua atuluka, muntu yonso uvua muditue mu mushindu kampanda wa tshingoma tshilamakaja ku tshibandilu, muvuaye wenda unyunguluka bu mu mukaba musunguluke. Mêyi makese anyi nansha amue avua maamba; ne buatu buvua kabuyi ne dîyi, bu buvua buenzeja kudi bendeshi ba mazuwa bavua ne bilamba bia nsongo, dituku dionso divua dienda dipita mu bupote buonso bua lukasa ne disanka dia mavuala a bademon. Butuku bupote bua muomumue bua bantu kumpala kua mbila ya mbuu buvua bupita; anu mu ditalala bantu bavua benda banyunguluka mu mionji ya mikete; kayi ne tshia kuamba, Ahaba wakimana kumpala kua tshiona. Nansha pavua bumuntu butshioke bumueneka bu bulomba dikisha, kavua mua kukeba dikisha adi mu tshitanda tshiende to. Starbuck kavua mua kupua muoyo tshimuenekelu tshia mununu eu to, pavua butuku bukuabu pavuaye upuekela mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi bua kuleja muvua baromètre muimane, wakamumona ne mêsu makanga musombe mululame mu nkuasa wende uvuabu basuike panshi; mvula ne mvula wa tshipepele tshikole uvuaye mupatukemu matuku makese kumpala bivua bipatuka, bitshidi anu bipueka bitekete bitekete ku tshilamba ne ku tshilamba tshivua katshiyi tshiumbushibue. SHAPITA 52. Mushipa wa Albatros.

Ku Sud-Est kua Cape, pabuipi ne Crozetts wa kule, muaba muimpe wa kuendakana bua Right Whalemen, mazuwa avua alua kumpala, Goney (Albatross) mu dina diende. Pavuaye wenda usemena bitekete bitekete, ku tshibandilu tshianyi tshia kumpala ku mutu kua mazuwa, mvua mmona bimpe tshintu atshi tshivua tshikemesha bikole kudi tyro mu mudimu wa diloba wa mu mbuu wa kule—mulobi wa baleine mu mbuu, ne uvua kayi kumbelu mutantshi mule.

Bu muvua mavuala mapite bungi, buatu ebu buvua butoke bu mufuba wa nyunyi wa walrus uvua mubuele mu mâyi. Mu nseke yende yonso, tshimuenekelu etshi tshia dikema tshivua tshilonda kudi njila mile ya tshisuku tshikunze, eku biamu biende bionso bia mvita ne bintu biende bivua bu matamba manene a mitshi mibuikila kudi mashika makole. Anu mazuwa ende a kuinshi ke avua mateka. Bivua bualu bua dikema bua kumona ndevu yende mile itangila mitu isatu ya mazuwa. Bavua bamueneka bavuale biseba bia nyama, nunku bavua bapanda ne bapanda bilamba bivua bipanduke bidimu bitue ku binayi bia luendu lua pa mâyi. Baimane mu bipese bia tshiamu bikuate ku mutshi wa tshikokedi, bavua banyunguluka ne banyunguluka pa mutu pa mbuu uvua kauyi ne ndekelu; ne nansha, pavua buatu buenda bupueka bitekete bitekete pabuipi ne kunyima kuetu, tuetu bantu basambombo mu lupepele tuakasemena pabuipi menemene ne tuvua mua kuikala pabuipi ne kutuluka ku mitu ya mazuwa amue kuya ku makuabu; kadi, balobi abu bavua bamueneka bu badi kabayi ne bualu, batutangila ne bupole pavuabu bapita, kabavua bambe dîyi nansha dimue kudi bantu bavua batutangila to, pavua mvula wa mabwe uvua umueneka kuinshi.

"Mazuwa ahoy! Ukadiku mumone baleine mutoke anyi?"

Kadi bu muvua kapitene wa muenyi, muiname pa mutu pa tshibumba tshitoke, mu tshienzedi tshia kuteka mpungi wende ku mishiku yende, mu mushindu kampanda wakakuluka ku tshianza tshiende mu mbuu; ne lupepele luvua lubanda mpindieu, wakadienzeja tshianana bua kumvuika kayi nalu. Mu tshikondo atshi buatu buende buvua anu buenda buvula ntanta pankatshi pabu. Pavua mu mishindu mishilashilangane bena mudimu ba mu mazuwa a Pequod baleja muvuabu bamone bualu bubi ebu pavuabu batela dîna dia Baleine Mutoke ku mazuwa makuabu, Ahaba wakimana bua tshitupa tshîpi; bivua bimueneka bu ne: uvua mua kuikala mupuekeshe buatu bua kubuela mu muenyi au, bu tshipepele tshivua tshimutshinyisha katshiyi tshimupangishe. Ino kukwata mushindo wandi wa luvula, wakwata kabidi mpungi wandi, ne mumanye ku tshimuenekelu tshiende ne: mazuwa a benyi avua a mu Nantucketer ne avua mua kuya kumbelu mu katupa kîpi emu, wakabikila ne dîyi dikole ne: “Ahoy, eu ke Pequod, musuika pa buloba bujima! ku ——”

Dîba adi, mishipa ibidi yakasabuka, ne diakamue, bilondeshile mishindu yabu ya pa buayi, tusumbu tua mishipa mikese ivua kayiyi ne bualu, ivua matuku makese kumpala ivua mienda mu mâyi mu ditalala ku luseke luetu, yakanyema ne mapapa avua amueneka bu avua ateketa, ne kuditeka kumpala ne panyima ne maboko a muenyi au. Nansha mudi mu ngendu yende ya pa mâyi Ahaba ne bua kuikala mumone bualu bua muomumue misangu ya bungi, kadi, kudi muntu yonso udi ne lungenyi lua patupu, malu makese menemene adi ne diumvuija dia patupu.

"Udi unyema kule nanyi, ki mmuomu anyi?" ke muvua Ahaba muele mbila, utangila mu mâyi. Kuvua kumueneka anu kakese mu mêyi, kadi dîyi divua dileja kanyinganyinga kakole kavua kayi ne tshia kuenza kupita muvua muntu mununu uvua mutshimbakane muanji kuleja. Kadi wakakudimuka kudi mulombodi wa buatu, uvua too ne mpindieu mukuate buatu mu lupepele bua kukepesha dienda diabu kumpala, wakadila ne dîyi diende dia kale dia ntambue ne: “Ubandishe ku mutu! Umupangishe bua kuendakana pa buloba bujima!”

Pa buloba bujima! Kudi bia bungi mu mutoyi au bua kusaka bantu ku dikala ne lutambishi; kadi luendu luonso elu ludi lufika penyi? Anu kupitshila ku njiwu bungi kabuyi kubala too ne muaba utuvua batuadije, muaba utuvua bashiye panyima mu bukubi, bavua misangu yonso kumpala kuetu.

Bu buloba ebu buikale tshibandabanda tshidi katshiyi ne ndekelu, ne patuvua tuenda mu mazuwa batangile ku est tuvua mua kufika kashidi ku ntanta mipiamipia, ne kupeta bintu bilenga ne bia dikema kupita Cyclades anyi bidiila bia Mukalenge Solomo, dîba adi kuvua dilaya mu luendu elu. Kadi mu dilonda dia malu masokoka a kule atudi tuela meji, anyi mu dilonda dia tshishima tshia bademon tshidi, tshikondo kampanda anyi kansanga, tshienda kumpala kua mitshima ya bantu bonso; patudi tulondakaja bintu ebi pa mutu pa buloba ebu bu tshijengu, badi batulombola mu njila idi kayiyi ne tshintu anyi batushiya munkatshi mua njila batekeshibue mu mikolo.
