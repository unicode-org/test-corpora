SHAPITA 86. Mukila.

Bafundi bakuabu ba tusala mbatumbishe dîsu dimpe dia nyunyi wa nkutshi, ne mapapa mimpe a nyunyi udi kayi upueka nansha kakese; kakese ka mu diulu, ndi nsekelela mukila.

Patudi tuela meji ne: mukila wa Sperm Whale udi ne bunene bua bungi menemene udi utuadija pa tshitupa tshia mubidi tshidi tshienda tshikepela too ne ku bule bua muntu, udi ne tshitupa tshia pa mutu patshi nkayatshi, bunene bua metre makumi atanu. Mubidi wa tshijengu wa miji yende udi wenda ukola mu mitshi ibidi mialabale, mikole, mibalakane anyi bipese bia mitshi, bienda binyanguka ku kakese ku kakese too ne ku bunene bua santimetre umue. Ku tshiadi anyi ku disangisha, bipese ebi bidi bipetangana kakese, pashishe bipatuka ku luseke bu mapuapua, bishiya muaba munene pankatshi pabi. Kakuena tshintu tshidi ne muoyo tshidi milongo ya bulengele mimanyike bimpe kupita mu mikalu ya ngondo ya tshijengu ya bintu ebi to. Ku ditangalaka diandi dia bungi mu baleine mukole, mukila udi mua kupita bule bua metre makumi abidi.

Tshitupa tshijima tshidi tshimueneka bu bulalu bua mishipa mikole; kadi utshikose, ne udi umona ne: bitupa bisatu bishilangane bidi bienza:—tshia kuulu, tshia pankatshi ne tshia kuinshi. Mionji idi mu tshitupa tshia kuulu ne tshia kuinshi, idi mile ne mishindame; adi a munkatshi, makese menemene, ne adi enda mu nkuruse pankatshi pa bitupa bia pambelu. Bulongolodi ebu bua bisatu, anu bu tshintu tshikuabu tshionso, budi bupesha mukila bukole. Bua mulongi wa bimanu bia kale bia bena Lomo, tshitupa tshia pankatshi netshikale tshifuanangane ne tshitupa tshikese tshia mabue tshidi tshishintuluka misangu yonso ne dibue mu bintu bia kale bia dikema, ne tshidi katshiyi mpata tshifila bukole bua bungi bua mabue.

Ino bwa se bukomo bukatampe ebu mu mukila wa nsongo kabuvua bukumbane, tshitupa tshijima tshia leviathan tshidi tshienza ne tshilamba ne tshilamba tshia mishipa ne mionji, idi ipita ku luseke luonso lua tshiadi ne ipuekela mu bipese bia mubidi, idi isambakana nayi mu mushindu udi kauyi umvuika, ne idi yambuluisha bikole bua kuikala ne bukole; nunku mu mukila bukole budi kabuyi bupima bua baleine mujima budi bumueneka bu busangisha too ne muaba kampanda. Shi dibutuka didi mua kuenzeka, etshi ke tshintu tshia kuenza.

Kabidi bualu ebu—bukole buende bua dikema, budi mua kunyangakaja dienda diawu dia bulenga; muaba udi buana bua bupepele buenda buenda buenda buenda buenda buenda buenda buenda buenda buenda buenda bupita mu Titanisme wa bukole. Kadi, ngendu ayi idi ipeta bulengele buayi bua dikema kudiyi. Bukole bulelela kabuena bunyanga bulengele anyi buobumue to, kadi budi bufila misangu ya bungi; ne mu tshintu tshionso tshilenga tshia dikema, bukole budi ne malu a bungi a kuenza ne majimbu. Umbusha mionji mikanga idi imueneka bu idi ipatuka ku mabue a marbre mu Hercules musonga, ne bulengele buende nebujimine. Pavua Eckerman muena nzambi mubandishe tshilamba tshia lin tshivua pa tshitalu tshia Goethe tshivua butaka, uvua mubungame bikole bua tshifu tshinene tshia muntu au tshivua tshimueneka bu tshifulu tshia ditshimuna tshia bena Lomo. Padi Angelo uleja nansha Nzambi Tatu mu mubidi wa muntu, ela meji bua bukole budiku. Ne nansha bobu mua kuleja tshinyi tshia dinanga dia Nzambi mu Muana, bimfuanyi bia bena Italie bia bupole, bia tshijengu, bia bumuntu bua bakaji, mudibu baleje lungenyi luende bimpe menemene; bimfuanyi ebi, nansha mudibi kabiyi ne bukole buonso, kabiena bileja bukole nansha bumue, kadi bidi anu bibi, bia bakaji bia dikokela ne dinanukila, bidi ku bianza bionso bitabijibua, bienza ngikadilu mimpe ya pa buayi ya malongesha ende.

Ke mudi tshitupa tshia mubidi tshindi ngakula natshi tshikole, nansha biobi bienza mudimu mu manaya, anyi mu tshisumi, anyi mu tshiji, nansha biobi bikale mu mushindu kayi, dikokangana diatshi didi anu dimanyishibua kudi ngasa mupite bungi. Mu bualu ebu kakuena diboko dia nsumuinu didi mua kutshipita to.

Bienzedi bitanu binene bidi bia pa buabi. Tshia kumpala, padibu benza mudimu bu tshintu tshia kulonda; Tshibidi, padibu benza mudimu bu tshingoma mu mvita; Tshisatu, mu dikomba; Tshinayi, mu lobtailing; Tshitanu, mu dikola dia bipupu.

Tshia kumpala: Bu mudi mukila wa Leviyatane muikale mutangile muaba wende, udi wenza malu mu mushindu mushilangane ne wa bifukibua bikuabu bionso bia mu mâyi. Katshiena tshinyunguluka nansha kakese. Mu muntu anyi mu mushipa, dikokangana didi tshimanyishilu tshia bupuekele. Bua baleine, mukila wende ke tshintu tshimuepele tshidiye wenza natshi mudimu. Muvungu-wise udi unyunguluka kumpala muinshi mua mubidi, ne pashishe upingana lubilu lubilu panyima, ke tshidi tshipesha tshintu tshibi tshidi tshienda lubilu, tshienda tshituta paditshi tshienda tshiowa mâyi ne tshiji tshikole. Mapapa ende a ku luseke adi anu ambuluisha bua kulombola.

Tshibidi: Bidi ne mushinga mukese, ne: nansha mudi baleine umue uluangana anu ne baleine mukuabu ne mutu wende ne mpala wende, nansha nanku, mu mvita yende ne muntu, udi nangananga wenza mudimu ne mukila wende ne tshikisu. Padiye ukuma buatu, udi unyunguluka ne lukasa luonso, ne tshipupu tshidi anu tshienzeka ku dipingana dia buatu. Bikalabi bienza mu lupepele ludi kaluyi lupangisha, nangananga bikalabi bipueka too ne ku tshimanyinu tshiabi, tshipupu tshidi anu tshikole bua kutshikanda. Kakuena mbadi ya muntu anyi ya buatu idi mua kuyitantamena to. Lupandu luebe lumuepele ludi mu kuepuka; kadi pikalaye ulua ku luseke mu mayi adi mashilangane, dîba adi bua dipepele dia buatu bua baleine, ne bukole bua bintu bidibu benze nabi mudimu, tshitupa tshia mubidi tshidi tshipanduluke anyi dibaya didi dinyanguke anyi abidi, mushindu kampanda wa disonga ku luseke, ke tshipeta tshitambe bubi. Bino bingoma bya mu mayi bitamba kupetekibua mu mudimu wa diloba, bienza bua se bidi bimueneka bu dinaya dia bana. Muntu mukuabu udi umbusha tshilamba, ne tshina tshidi tshijika.

Tshisatu: Tshiena mua kuleja to, kadi bidi bimueneka kundi ne: mu baleine lungenyi lua kulenga ludi lutangalake mu mukila; bualu bua mu bualu ebu mudi buimpe budi anu mua kufuanangana ne bulenga bua mubidi wa ntambue. Buimpe ebu budi bumueneka nangananga mu tshienzedi tshia dikomba, padi mu bupole bua nsongakaji baleine ne bupole kampanda butekete wenda unyunguluka mishiku yende minene ku luseke ne ku luseke pa mutu pa mbuu; ne yeye mumvue anu nsuki ya muendeshi wa mazuwa, diakabi kudi muena mazuwa au, nsuki ne bionso. Bulenga kayipu budiku mu ditenga dia kumpala adi! Bu mukila eu muikale ne bukole bua kukuata, mvua mua kuela meji diakamue bua ntambue wa Darmonodes uvua uya misangu ya bungi mu tshisalu tshia maluba, ne uvua upesha bana ba bakaji bintu bia ku mishiku, ne pashishe ulenga miaba yabu. Mu malu a bungi, bidi bibungamija bua se: baleine kena ne ngikadilu muimpe eu mu mukila wende to; bualu bua ndi mumvue kabidi bua ntambue mukuabu, uvua pavuaye mutapike mu mvita, uvua unyunguluka mubidi wende ne mupatule muele.

Tshinayi: Paudi wiba mu tshimpitshimpi pa baleine mu bukubi bua mu lungenyi lua munkatshi mua mbuu udi nkayende, udi umupeta kayi mutshioke bua bunene bua bunême buende, ne bu muana wa mpushi, udi unaya pa mbuu bienze anu bu ne: udi tshioshelu. Kadi nansha nanku udi umona bukole buende mu dinaya diende. Maboko manene a mukila wandi adi apueka mulu mu lupepele; pashishe kukuma pa mutu, tshiona tshia makubakuba tshidi tshiumvuika mutantshi wa kilometre ya bungi. Udi mua kuela meji ne tshingoma tshinene tshivua tshipatuka; ne shi uvua mumone tshifulu tshia lupepele tshikole tshia mu spiracle ku muelelu wende mukuabu, uvua mua kuela meji ne: uvua muishi wa mu tshina tshia kulenga.

Tshitanu: Anu mudibi mu mushindu wa pa tshibidilu wa leviathan udi wenda pa mâyi, bipese bia mubidi bidi muinshi mua mukolo wende, pashishe kabiena bimueneka muinshi mua mâyi to; kadi padiye pabuipi ne kuditua mu mâyi manene, bipese biende bionso bidi ne bule bua metre makumi asatu a mubidi wende bidi bibuelakana mu lupepele, ne bidi bishala binyunguluka tshitupa tshîpi, too ne padibi bipueka panshi kabiyi bimueneka. Pa kumbusha dibutuka dia mushinga mukole —muaba mukuabu udibu mua kumvuija—dibandila edi dia mishiku ya baleine didi pamuapa tshintu tshinene tshidi mua kumueneka mu bintu bionso bidi ne muoyo. Mu buondoke budi kabuyi ne ndekelu, mukila munene udi umueneka wenda unyema mu diulu dia muulu menemene. Nunku mu bilota, mvuaku mumone Satana wa bunene upatula dikasa diende dinene divua dikengeshibua difumina mu kapia ka Baltique ka Inferno anyi? Ino mu kutangila bintu bya uno muswelo, bidi bilombola mu muswelo umo; shi mu Dantean, bademon nebalue kuudi; bikala mu wa Yeshaya, banjelu banene. Pamvua muimane ku mutu kua mazuwa anyi pavua dîba dipatuka divua dilengeja diulu ne mbuu, musangu mukuabu ngakamona tshisumbu tshinene tshia baleine ku est, bonso baya batangile ku dîba, ne bua tshitupa tshîpi bavua banyunguluka pamue ne mitoyi mikole. Anu mumvua mmona tshikondo atshi, tshimfuanyi tshinene tshia ditendelela nzambi katshivua tshimueneka nansha mu Pelasa, muaba uvua batendeledi ba kapia. Anu muvua Ptolémée Philopater mufile bujadiki bua ntambue wa mu Afrike, meme panyi ngakafila bujadiki bua baleine, mmuamba ne: uvua mutambe kutendelela Nzambi kupita bifukibua bionso. Bualu bilondeshile Mukalenge Juba, ntambue ya basalayi ya kale ivua misangu ya bungi isaka mu dinda ne mikolo yayi mibandisha mu ditalala dikole.

Difuanyikija dia mpukapuka mu nshapita eu, pankatshi pa baleine ne ntambue, bilondeshile amue malu a mukila wa umue ne mubidi wa mukuabu, kadiena ne bua kuikala ne tshibidilu tshia kuteka bitupa bibidi ebi bidi bishilangane pa muaba wa muomumue, nansha bifukibua bidibi bipetangana. Bualu bu mudi ntambue wa bukole menemene muikale anu nyama wa ntambue kudi Leviyatane, nunku, patudi tumufuanyikija ne mukila wa Leviyatane, mutshi wende udi anu bu mutshi wa lily. Ku muelelu kua tshitupa tshia mâyi tshia Sunda katshiena ne bimanu bia bukole bidi bilama biibi bia ku Mbuu wa Mediterane, wa Baltique ne wa Propontis to. Mu kitatyi kimo, patuvua balala mu tshimpitshimpi, bintu bia tshimpitshimpi bivua bimueneka ku bule bivua bileja mudimu wa mazuwa makuabu, avua anu afila manga kudi baleine ku mikalu ya tshitudilu; anyi pamuapa kutungunuka ne mvita mu tshijengu tshia kumpala, muaba uvuabu babapesha muaba wa bungi ne miaba mimpe ya kunyemena. Imue misangu batu ne tshibidilu tshia kukeba bua kumukosa lubilu kudi baleine udi ne bukole ne budimu, bua kukeba bua kumutapa ku tshiadi, mu mushindu kampanda, pa kutshibula anyi kunyanga tshitupa tshiende tshinene tshia mukila. Bidi bienzeka pa kuela tshiamu tshia kukosa natshi tshianza tshipi, tshidibu balamakaja tshingoma bua kutshikoka kabidi. Mushipa wa baleine uvua mutapike (bu mutudi balonge pashishe) mu tshitupa etshi, kadi ki mmukumbane to, bu muvuabi bimueneka, uvua mupatuke mu buatu, mumbule tshitupa tshia tshingoma tshia bingoma; ne mu kanyinganyinga kakole ka mputa, uvua mpindieu wenda unyema munkatshi mua tshijengu tshivua tshienda tshinyunguluka bu Arnold uvua mubande nkayende mu mvita ya Saratoga, muikale ne kanyinganyinga muaba wonso uvuaye uya.

Kadi bivua bibungamija bu muvua mputa wa baleine eu, ne tshikena kumona tshibi tshikumbane, mu mushindu kayi wonso; kadi buôwa bua pa buabu buvuaye umueneka bu uvua usaka tshisumbu tshikuabu tshia nyama, buvua bufumina ku bualu buvua kumpala ntanta uvua pankatshi pabu mutusokoke. Ino ku mfulo tuakajingulula amba ku mwanda umo wa ku mpukapuka ya mu mudimu wa diloba, baleine eu uvua mubuele mu tshingoma tshivuaye ukoka; uvua kabidi munyeme ne tshiamu tshia kukosa natshi munda muende; ne pavua ndekelu wa tshingoma tshivua tshilamakaja ku tshingoma atshi, tshikuate kashidi mu nshinga ya tshingoma tshivua tshinyunguluke mukila wende, tshingoma tshia kukosa natshi tshivua tshipatuke mu mubidi wende. Nunku uvua mukengeshibue too ne ku bupote, uvua mpindieu wenda unyunguluka mu mayi, wenda unyunguluka ne tshikisu ne mukila wende mupepele, ne wela tshingoma tshikole, utapa ne ushipa balunda bende.

Tshintu etshi tshia dikema tshivua tshimueneka bu tshivua tshivuluija tshisumbu tshijima tshia nyama ku buôwa buabu bua dishindama. Tshia kumpala, baleine idi yenza muelelu wa dijiba dietu yakatuadija kudisangisha kakese, ne kukulukangana, bienze anu bu ne: ivua mibandishibue kudi mavuala avua mafume ku bule; pashishe dijiba nkayadi diakatuadija kukola ne kuvula; nzubu ya dibaka ya muinshi mua mâyi ne nzubu ya dikoleshila bana biakajimina; mu njila ya bungi idi ikola, ba baleine badi mu nseke ya munkatshi bakatuadija kuowa mu bisumbu bidi bienda bikola. Eyowa, ditalala dia mutantshi mule divua dienda dijika. Mutoyi mukese uvua wenda uya kumpala wakateleja diakamue; ne pashishe bu mudi mabue a mashika adi ne tshimvundu padi musulu munene wa Hudson unyanguka mu muvu wa printemps, tshisumbu tshijima tshia baleine tshiakalua tshikuluka pa tshitupa tshiabu tshia munda, bienze anu bu ne: tshidi tshidisangisha mu mukuna umue. Muipate, wewe Queequeg—baleine udiku!—mutute!—mukuate! Imanayi—imanayi, ne nushale nanku! Muvu wa printemps, bantu—kokayi, bantu; kanuyi nuela meji bua mikolo yabu—bakuate!

CITUPA 88. Tulasa ne balombodi ba tulasa.

Nshapita mupita udi ufila muyuki wa tshisumbu tshinene anyi tshisumbu tshia baleine ya sperm, ne pashishe bavua bafila kabidi tshidi mua kuikala tshikebeshe disangisha dinene adi.

Mpindieu, nansha mudibu batuilangana ne mibidi minene ya nunku imue misangu, kadi, anu mudibi ne bua kuikala bimueneka, nansha mu matuku etu aa, tusumbu tukese tua pa nkaya tudi tumueneka misangu mikuabu, tuangata bantu kubangila ku makumi abidi too ne ku makumi atanu ku tshisumbu tshionso. Bisumbu bia misambu bia nunku mbimanyike bu tulasa. Pa tshibidilu bitu bia mishindu ibidi; aba badi pabuipi ne bonso bakaji, ne aba badi kabayi basangisha nansha umue to, anu bana ba balume ba bukole, anyi ngombe, bu mudibu bababikila.

Mu dibuela dia mu tshilongelu tshia bakaji, udi umona misangu yonso mulume mukole, kadi kayi mukulakaje to; udi, ku tshinyongopelu tshionso, uleja dikima diende pa kukuluka kunyima ne kubuikila dinyema dia bakaji bende. Mu bulelela, muntu eu udi muena Ottoman wa bubanji, wenda unyunguluka pa mutu pa mâyi, munyunguluka ne bintu bionso bia busambi ne dinanga dia harem. Dishilangana didi pankatshi pa muena Ottoman eu ne bakaji bende ba bakaji didi dikemesha; bualu, nansha mudiye misangu yonso ne bunene bua leviathanic, bakaji, nansha padibu bakola bimpe, kabena bapita tshia bisatu tshia bunene bua mulume wa bunene bua munkatshi to. Badi ne butekete, bulelela; Ndi ngamba ne, ki mbua kupita tshitupa tshia metre dikumi ne abidi mu tshiadi to. Nansha nanku, katuena mua kubenga ne: mu tshibungi badi ne bukenji bua kupeta en bon point ku bumpianyi to.

Bidi bikemesha bua kumona nzubu wa tshilala benyi eu ne mfumuende mu ngendu yabu ya tshianana. Anu bu bintu bia mu tshikondo, badi anu mu luendu mu dikeba dia mishindu ne mishindu. Udi ubapeta pa mulongo mu tshikondo tshia maluba onso a tshikondo tshia kudia tshia mu Equatorial, mumane kupingana, pamuapa, ku dipitshisha muvu wa été mu mbuu ya ku Nord, ne nunku udi ushima muvu wa été ne ditshioka ne luya luonso lubi. Dîba didibu bamane kusomba mu tshibandabanda tshia Equateur bua tshitupa tshîpi, badi batuadija kuya mu mâyi a ku Est bua kuindila tshikondo tshia mashika kuntu aku, ne nunku badi bepuka mashika makuabu a bungi a tshidimu.

Padibu benda baya mu ditalala mu umue wa ku ngendu eyi, bikalabu bamone tshintu kampanda tshia dikema tshidi katshiyi tshimueneka, baleine wanyi udi utangila bimpe dîku diende didi disankisha. Shi muntu nansha umo udi mua kuenzeja nkasampe Leviathan ulua mu njila au, udi mua kusemena pabuipi ne umue wa ku bakaji abu mu musokoko, ne tshiji tshikole tshidi Bashaw umuluisha, ne umuipata! Bikondo bikola, bulelela, shi bansonga badi kabayi ne mikenji bu yeye badi ne tshia kuanyishibua bua kubuela mu tshijila tshia disanka dia mu nzubu; nansha mudiye wenza tshidi Bashaw musue, kena mua kulama Lothario udi ne lumu lubi menemene pambelu pa bulalu buende to; bualu, diakabi! mishipa yonso idi mu tshitupa tshimue. Padibu ku muelelu wa mâyi, bakaji batu misangu ya bungi benza mvita mikole menemene munkatshi mua bantu badi babananga; ke mudibi kabidi bua baleine, idi imue misangu ilua mu mvita ya lufu, ne bionso ebi bua dinanga. Badi benza lupangu ne mishiku yabu mile ya kuinshi, imue misangu bayisuika pamue, ne nunku badienzeja bua kuikala ne bumfumu bu mudi nyama wa ntambue udi ulama nsengu yawu mu mvita. Kabena bakese badibu bakuate ne mputa mikole ya malu aa to,—mitu minyanguke, meno matshibuka, mapapa manyanguke; ne mu imue misangu, mishiku mikosa ne mikosolola.

Kadi tuetu tuela meji ne: muena tshinyangu wa disanka dia mu nzubu udi mua kudinyema ku lubilu lua kumpala lua mfumu wa nzubu wa tshilala benyi, dîba adi bidi bikemesha bikole bua kutangila mfumu au. Ne bupole buonso udi ubueja tshitupa tshiende tshinene munkatshi muabu kabidi ne udi usanka muaba au bua tshitupa tshîpi, utshidi anu pabuipi ne nsongalume Lothario, bu mudi Solomo muena nzambi utendelela ne muoyo mujima munkatshi mua bakaji bende tshinunu. Bu mudi baleine mikuabu imueneka, balobi kabatu batamba kulonda umue wa ku Grand Turks eyi to; bualu bua bena Turquie banene aba badi ne bukole bua bungi, ke bualu kayi dipanga diabu dia mafuta ndikese. Bua bana ba balume ne bana ba bakaji badibu balela, bana ba balume ne ba bakaji abu badi ne bua kuditabalela; nansha kakese, anu ne diambuluisha dia mamu. Bualu anu bu bamue bananga bakuabu badi badia bintu bionso badi mua kuikala ne dîna, Mfumu wanyi Whale kena ne dinanga dia nzubu wa bana to, nansha yeye muikale ne dinanga dia bower; ne nunku, bu mudiye muena ngendu munene, udi ushiya bana bende badi kabayi bamanyike pa buloba bujima; muana yonso udi wa pa buende. Mu tshikondo tshimpe, nansha nanku, bu mudi tshisumi tshia bunsonga tshikepela; bu mudi bidimu ne bintu bia bukoya bienda bivula; bu mudi diela meji dimupesha dikisha dia tshijila; mu tshikoso, bu mudi dibungama dia bungi dipita muena Turquie mupuwe; pashishe dinanga dia bupole ne buimpe didi dipingana pa muaba wa dinanga dia bana ba bakaji; Ottoman wetu udi ubuela mu tshitupa tshia muoyo tshidi katshiyi ne bukole, udi unyingalala, udi ufila mibelu, udi uditshipa, udi ushipa nzubu wa tshilala benyi, ne udi ukola bua kulua muntu wa kale wa tshilejilu, udi ne tshiji, udi wenda nkayende munkatshi mua meridien ne paralele wenza masambila ende, ne udimuija nsongalume yonso wa Leviathan ku bilema biende bia dinanga.

Mpindieu, bu mudi tshisumbu tshia baleine tshibikila kudi balobi ne: tshilongelu, ke mudi mfumu ne mfumu wa tshilongelu atshi mumanyike bu mulongeshi wa tshilongelu. Ke bualu kayi ki mbimpe, nansha biobi bikale bia dikema, ne: yeye mumane kuya mu kalasa nkayende, udi ne bua kuya ku babende kayi ulongesha malu avuaye mulonge kuntu aku to, kadi bupote bua kalasa. Dina dienda dia mulongeshi, didi mua kuikala difumina ku dina divuabu bapeshe nzubu wa tshilala benyi, kadi bamue bantu badi bela meji ne: muntu uvua muanji kubikila baleine wa bena Ottoman eu, uvua ne bua kuikala mubale mikanda ya Vidocq, ne mudimanyishe muvuaye mulongeshi wa mu tshilongelu tshia mu mpata au mu matuku ende a bunsonga, ne tshivua tshilongelu tshia malu a mîdima atshi wakalongesha bamue balongi bende.

Ditapuluka ne ditapuluka dia muomumue didi baleine wa mu kalasa udipeta mu bidimu biende bia bukulakaje, didi bulelela bua baleine yonso ya Sperm idi mikulakaje. Pabuipi ne pa buloba bujima, baleine udi nkayende—mudibu babikila Leviathan udi nkayende—udi uleja ne: uvua wa kale. Anu bu Daniel Boone udi ne ndevu ya moss, kakuikala ne muntu mukuabu pabuipi nende to, anu Bufuki nkayabu; ne yeye udi wangata bu mukajende mu tshipela tshia mayi, ne udi mukaji mutambe buimpe, nansha mudiye ulama malu masokoka a bungi a tshikisu.

Tulasa tudi katuyi ne muntu nansha umue to, anu bansongalume ne ba bukole, tudibu batele kumpala eku, tudi tufila dishilangana dikole ne tulasa tua harem. Bualu nansha mudi baleine bakaji aba bikale ne buowa, bana ba balume anyi ngombe ya 40-barrel, mudibu bababikila, ke badi ne tshikisu tshikole kupita baleine yonso, ne badi ne njiwu ya bungi ya kusambakena nayi; pa kumbusha baleine idi ne mitu mitoke, imue misangu ituilangana nayi, ne eyi neyikuluishe bu bademon babi badi ne tshiji tshia gout.

Tulasa tua 40-barrel-bull tudi tunene kupita tulasa tua harem. Anu bu tshisumbu tshia bansonga badi mu kalasa, mbawule tente ne mvita, masanka ne malu mabi, benda banyunguluka pa buloba bujima ne lubilu lua tshikisu, lua se: kakuena muena mudimu wa budimu udi mua kubakuba bu mudiye mua kuenza kudi nsongalume wa tshimvundu wa mu Yale anyi wa mu Harvard to. Badi balekela tshimvundu etshi mu katupa kîpi, ne padi bantu batue ku bisatu bia binayi bakola, badi batapuluka, ne baya pa nkayabu bakeba miaba ya kusombela, mmumue ne: nzubu ya tshilala benyi.

Bualu bukuabu budi bushilangane pankatshi pa tulasa tua balume ne tua bakaji budi anu bumanyine pa bantu balume ne bakaji. Tuambe ne: udi ukuma ngombe wa tshibombi tshia makumi anayi—diabolo mupele! balunda bende bonso bakamulekela. Kadi ukuma muena mu tshilongelu tshia harem, ne balunda bende badi bamunyunguluka ne tshimanyinu tshionso tshia dibungama, imue misangu bashala pabuipi nende ne mutantshi mule, bu bobu bine bua kukuluka.
