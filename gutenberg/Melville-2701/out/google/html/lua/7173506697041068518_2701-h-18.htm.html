SHAPITUA 89. Mushipa wa lukasa ne mushipa wa tshianana.

Ditela dia waif ne waif-poles mu nshapita wa ndekelu kadi umue, didi dilomba amue malu adi atangila mikenji ne bilele bia diloba dia baleine, mudi waif mua kuikala tshimanyinu tshinene ne tshimanyishilu.

Misangu ya bungi bitu bienzeka ne: padi mazuwa a bungi enda mu tshisumbu, baleine udi mua kukuatshibua kudi mazuwa amue, pashishe kunyema, ne ndekelu wa bionso kumushipa ne kumukuata kudi mazuwa makuabu; ne muaba eu mudi malu makese a bungi adi kaayi malelela, onso adi ne tshitupa tshia bualu bunene ebu. Tshilejilu,—panyima pa dilondangana dikole ne dia njiwu ne dikuata dia baleine, tshitalu tshidi mua kupatuka mu mazuwa bua tshipepele tshikole; ne wenda uya kule mutangile ku lupepele, udi mua kukuata kabidi kudi mulobi wa baleine muibidi, udi, mu ditalala, mu mushindu muimpe, umukoka ku luseke, kayi ne njiwu ya muoyo anyi ya nshinga. Nunku bilumbu binene ne bia tshikisu bivua mua kujuka misangu ya bungi pankatshi pa balobi, bu muvuaku kakuyi mikenji mifunda anyi kayiyi mifunda, ya pa buloba bujima, kayiyi ne mpata idi mua kuenzela bilumbu bionso.

Pamuapa mukenji umuapele wa diloba dia baleine uvua muanyishibue kudi mikenji, uvua wa mu Hollande. Bivua bimanyishibua kudi States-General mu AD 1695. Kadi nansha mudi ditunga dikuabu kadiyi dianji kuikala ne mikenji mifunda ya diloba dia baleine, nansha nanku balobi ba mu Amerike mbalue bafidi ba mikenji ne bamanyi ba mikenji mu bualu ebu. Badi bafila ndongoluelu udi mu bule ne mu butshiama udi upita Pandects ya Justinien ne Mikenji ya Société chinoise bua dipangisha dia dibuela mu malu a bantu bakuabu. Eyowa; mikenji eyi idi mua kuikala mifunda pa tshibombi tshia mukalenge mukaji Anne, anyi pa tshingoma tshia tshingoma, ne mivuale mu nshingu, bualu idi mikese.

I. Fast-Fish udi wa tshisumbu tshia lukasa kudiye.

II. Mushipa wa Loose-Fish mmushindu muimpe wa muntu yonso udi mua kumukuata lukasa.

Ino tshidi tshienza bubi mu meyi aa a meji i buipi buende bua dikema, budi bulomba mukanda munene wa diumvuija bua kuumvuija.

Tshia kumpala: Fast-Fish ntshinyi? Mushipa wa mushipa udi ne muoyo anyi mufue udi ne lubilu lua bungi, padiye mulamakaja ne mazuwa anyi buatu budi ne bantu, ku diambuluisha dia tshintu kayi tshionso tshidi mua kulombodibua kudi muntu udimu anyi bantu badimu,—tshiamu, tshiamu tshia kuendesha natshi buatu, tshiamu tshia 9 inch, tshiamu tshia telegrafe, anyi tshilamba tshia nsese, bionso bidi anu bia muomumue. Bia muomumue, mushipa udi wenda lubilu padiye ne waif, anyi tshimanyinu tshikuabu tshionso tshia dikala natshi; pikala tshisumbu tshidi tshibenga tshileja patoke bukokeshi buatshi dîba kayi dionso bua kutshiangata ku luseke, ne kabidi dijinga diatshi dia kuenza nanku.

Eyi i mifundu ya malu a sianse; kadi meyi a bena baleine nkayabu atu imue misangu ne mêyi makole ne bikuma bikole menemene—Coke-upon-Littleton wa tshianza. Bushuwa, munkatshi mua bena baleine badi ne bululame ne ba kanemu batu misangu yonso bafila makuta bua bilumbu bia pa buabi, mudibi mua kuikala bibi menemene bua muntu umue kuamba ne: udi ne baleine uvuabu balonda anyi bashipe kudi muntu mukuabu. Kadi bakuabu kabena ne budimu nansha kakese to.

Kukadi bidimu bitue ku makumi atanu, kuvua tshilumbu tshia dikema tshia baleine-trover tshivua tshilumbuluishibue mu Angleterre, muvua bafundi ba tshilumbu etshi baleje ne: kunyima kua dilondangana dikole dia baleine mu mbuu ya ku Nord; ne pavuabu bushuwa bobu (bafundi) bafike ku dikuma mushipa; ndekelu wa bionso, ku diambuluisha dia njiwu ya mioyo yabu, bavua ne bua kulekela ki nganu milongo yabu to, kadi buatu buabu nkayabu. Ndekelu wa bionso bafundi (bena mudimu ba mu mazuwa makuabu) bakalua ne baleine, kumukuma, kumushipa, kumukuata, ne ndekelu wa bionso kumuangata ku mêsu kua bafundi. Ne pavuabu babanda bafundi aba, kapitene wabu wakakuma minu yende mu meno a bafundi, ne wakabajadikila ne: ku diambuluisha dia doxology ku tshienzedi tshivuaye muenze, uvua mpindieu ne bua kulama muonji wabu, bingoma ne buatu, bivua bishale bilamakaja ku baleine tshikondo tshivuabu bamukuate. Ke bualu kayi bafundi bavua mpindieu balomba bua kubapingajila mushinga wa baleine wabu, muonji wabu, bingoma bia mvita ne buatu buabu.

Muanetu Erskine uvua mufidi wa mibelu wa bantu bavuabu babanda; Lord Ellenborough uvua mulumbuluishi. Mu tshikondo tshia dilumbuluisha, Erskine wa meji wakatungunuka ne kuleja muaba wende, pa kuakula bua tshibawu tshivua tshienzeke matuku mashale aa. con. tshilumbu, muvua muntu mukuabu, kunyima kua yeye mumane kuteta bua kukandamena tshikisu tshia mukajende, ndekelu wa bionso mumulekele pa mbuu ya muoyo; kadi mu bule bua bidimu, munyingalala bua tshienzedi atshi, wakatuadija tshienzedi tshia kumupingaja. Erskine uvua ku luseke lukuabu; ne pashishe wakajadika bualu ebu wamba ne: nansha muvua muntu au muele mamu au mvita ku ntuadijilu, ne uvua mumujile biakudia, ne anu bua kanyinganyinga kakole ka tshikisu tshiende tshivua tshienda tshikepela, wakamulekela ndekelu wa bionso; kadi wakamulekela, nunku wakalua mushipa wa tshianana; ne nunku pavua muntu mukuabu uvua ulonda mumupingaje tshingoma, mukaji au wakalua tshintu tshia muntu au, pamue ne tshingoma tshionso tshivuabu mua kuikala bamupete tshimulamakaja.

Mpindieu mu tshilumbu etshi Erskine wakamba ne: bilejilu bia baleine ne mukaji bivua bilejangana.

Malomba aa, ne malomba makuabu, pavuabu bateleje bimpe, mulumbuluishi mupiluke mu miaku misunguluke wakapangadija, mmumue ne: bua buatu, wakabupesha bafundi, bualu bavua anu babulekele bua kusungila mioyo yabu; kadi pa bidi bitangila baleine, bingoma ne nshinga, bivua bia bantu bavuabu babanda; baleine, bualu uvua Loose-Fish mu tshikondo tshia dikuata dia ndekelu; ne bingoma ne nshinga bualu pavua mushipa wenda nabi, yeye (mushipa) uvua upeta muaba mu bintu abi; ne ke bualu kayi muntu yonso uvua muangate mushipa uvua ne bukenji bua kuyipeta. Mpindieu bafundi bavua bangate mushipa; nunku, biena-bualu bidibu batele ebi bivua biabu.

Muntu wa patupu udi utangila dipangadika edi dia mulumbuluishi mulonge bikole, udi mua kuikala mua kudibenga. Ino byafika ku dibue dia kumpala dia bualu ebu, meyi manene abidi adibu bafunde mu mikenji ya diloba dia baleine ya mapasa adibu batele kumpala, ne adibu benze nawu mudimu ne adibu bumvuije kudi Lord Ellenborough mu tshilumbu tshidibu batele kulu eku; mikenji ibidi eyi idi itangila Fast-Fish ne Loose-Fish, ndi ngamba ne, neyikale, pa kuelangana meji, mikale nshindamenu wa mikenji yonso ya bantu; bualu nansha mudibu ne bintu bia bungi bia kuenza nabi mudimu, ntempelo wa mikenji, anu bu ntempelo wa bena Peleshete, udi anu ne bintu bibidi bia kuimana nabi.

Kabienaku muaku udi mukana mua muntu yonso ne, Dikala ne tshintu ntshitupa tshia mikenji: mbuena kuamba ne, nansha tshintu atshi tshilue kuikala ne tshintu mushindu kayi anyi? Kadi misangu ya bungi kuikala ne bintu kudi mikenji yonso. Ntshinyi tshidi mishipa ne mioyo ya bapika ba mu Russie ne bapika ba mu République pa kumbusha Fast-Fish, udi ne bumpianyi buikale mu mikenji yonso? Ntshinyi kudi muena nzubu wa tshikisu tshidi tshintu tshia ndekelu tshia mukaji mukamba pa kumbusha Fast-Fish? Ntshinyi tshidi kuntu aku nzubu munene wa mabue a marbre wa muntu mubi udi kayi mumanyike udi ne tshilamba tshia ku tshiibi bua waif; ntshinyi etshi pa kumbusha Fast-Fish? Ntshinyi tshidi Môdekai, muena mudimu, upeta kudi Woebegone mupele, muena tshinyangu, pa dibanza dia kulama dîku dia Woebegone ku nzala; ntshinyi tshidi tshipuekesha tshinyangu etshi pa kumbusha Fast-Fish? Mmakuta kayi a muepiskopo munene wa Savesoul a £100,000 adibu bangate ku mampa makese ne ku fromaje ya binunu nkama ne binunu bia bena mudimu bavua ne mikolo mitshibuka (bonso badi bajadike ne: nebaye mu diulu kakuyi diambuluisha dia Savesoul) ntshinyi tshidi £100,000 wa tshijengu atshi kadi anu Fast-Fish? Mmisoko ne misoko ya bumpianyi ya Duke wa Dunder mminyi pa kumbusha Fast-Fish? Ntshinyi kudi muena tshingoma, John Bull, tshidi Irlande mupele, kadi Fast-Fish? Bua muena mudimu wa bupostolo au, muanetu Jonathan, Texas udi tshinyi anu bu mushipa wa lubilu? Ne pa bidi bitangila bionso ebi, bumpianyi ki mbua mikenji yonso anyi?

Ino shi dilongesha dia Fast-Fish didi mua kukumbana, dilongesha dia Loose-Fish didi anu dikumbane. Bidi bikumbanyine pa buloba bujima ne pa buloba bujima.

Amerika uvua tshinyi mu 1492 pa kumbusha Loose-Fish, muvua Colomb mushipe tshipiminu tshia bena Espagne pa kutshilekela kudi mfumuende wa bukalenge ne mukajende? Pologne uvua tshinyi kudi Czar? Grèce kayi kudi bena Turquie? Inde ne Angleterre kayi? Ndekelu wa bionso Mexique neikale tshinyi kudi États-Unis? Mishipa yonso.

Mmalu kayi adi makokeshi a muntu ne budikadidi bua pa buloba pa kumbusha Loose-Fish? Lungenyi lua bantu bonso ne mmuenenu kayi pa kumbusha Loose-Fish? Ntshinyi tshidi tshileja ditabuja dia tshitendelelu kudibu pa kumbusha Mushipa wa Loose-Fish? Ntshinyi tshidi meji a bantu badi bela meji pa kumbusha Loose-Fish? Buloba bunene nkayabu budi tshinyi pa kumbusha Mushipa wa Loose? Ne udi tshinyi, mubadi, pa kumbusha Mushipa wa Luse ne Mushipa wa Lubilu, pebe?





SHAPITA 90. Mitu anyi mikila.

"De balena vero sufficit, si rex habeat caput, et regina caudam." Bracton, l. 3, c. 3.

Latin wa mu mikanda ya mikenji ya mu Angleterre, udi muangatshila pamue ne tshidiye wamba, udi umvuija ne: pa baleine yonso idibu bakuate kudi muntu yonso ku muelelu wa mâyi a buloba abu, Mukalenge, bu muena mvita munene wa kanemu, udi ne bua kuikala ne mutu, ne mukalenge mukaji udi ne bua kuikala ne mukila ne kanemu. Ditapuluka didi, mu baleine, difuanangane anu bu ditapulula dia pome; kakuena tshidi tshishala tshia pankatshi to. Mpindieu bu mudi mukenji eu, mu mushindu mushintulula, muikale ne bukole too ne lelu mu Angleterre; ne bu mudibi bifila mu mishindu mishilashilangane bualu budi kabuyi bumanyike budi butangila mikenji ya Fast and Loose-Fish, bidi biumvuija muaba eu mu nshapita mukuabu, pa dîyi dimue dia bulenga didi disaka mashinyi a mu Angleterre bua kuikala ku njiwu ya mashinyi makuabu, adibu balamina nangananga bua bantu ba mu bukalenge. Mu muaba wa kumpala, bua kujadika ne: mikenji idibu batele kuulu eku itshidi anu ne bukole, ndi ntungunuka ne kunuleja bualu buvua buenzeke mu bidimu bibidi bishale ebi.

Bidi bimueneka ne: bamue bena mazuwa ba bululame ba mu Dover, anyi ba mu Sandwich, anyi ba mu Cinque Ports, bavua bafike ku dishipa ne kuela baleine muimpe uvuabu batele kule ne muelelu wa mâyi kunyima kua dilondangana dikole. Mpindieu Cinque Ports idi mu tshitupa anyi mu mushindu kampanda muinshi mua bukokeshi bua mushindu kampanda wa mpulushi anyi beadle, udibu babikila ne: Lord Warden. Kulama mudimu buludiludi kudi mukalenge, ndi ngitabuja ne, makuta onso a bukalenge adi afika ku bitupa bia Cinque Port adi alua ende. Kudi bamue bafundi, mudimu eu udi ubikidibua ne: sinecure. Kadi ki mmuomu to. Bualu bua Lord Warden udi ne mudimu wa bungi imue misangu mu dilongolola dia bintu biende; idi yende nangananga ku diambuluisha dia fobbing umue umue au.

Mpindieu pavua bena matu bapele aba bavua bafume ku dîba, kabayi ne makasa, ne bilamba biabu bivua bivunguluke muulu pa maulu abu a eely, bavua bakoka mishipa yabu ya mafuta muulu ne muume, badilaya bua kupeta £150 mimpe ya mafuta ne mifuba ya mushinga mukole; ne mu lungenyi badi banua tshiayi tshia tshianana ne bakaji babu, ne maluvu mimpe ne balunda babu, ku bukole bua bitupa biabu; Muntu mulonge bikole ne muena kilisto ne muena luse udi ubanda, ne mukanda wa Blackstone muinshi mua diboko diende; ne udi umuteka pa mutu pa baleine, udi wamba ne: "Bamfumu banyi, mushipa eu udi mushipa wa lukasa. Ndi muwukuate bu wa Mukalenge wa mulami." Pa bualu ebu, bena mazuwa bapele mu tshimvundu tshiabu tshia kanemu—mu Anglais mulelela—kabayi bamanye tshia kuamba, badi bakuluka mu dikosa mitu yabu ne bukole buonso; mu tshikondo atshi uvua utangila ne kanyinganyinga kumbukila ku baleine too ne ku muenyi. Kadi bualu ebu kabuakalongolola bualu nansha bumue, anyi kutekesha mutshima mukole wa muntu mulonge ne mukanda wa Blackstone to. Ndekelu wa bionso umue wa kudibu, kunyima kua yeye mumane kukeba ngenyi yende mutantshi mule, wakalua kuakula ne dikima,

"Ndi nkusengelela, mfumu, Mfumu wa tshilaminu udi nganyi?"

"Mukalenge."

"Kadi mfumu kavua ne bualu nansha bumue bua kuangata mushipa eu anyi?"

"I wende."

"Tudi mu ntatu mikole, ne njiwu, ne makuta kampanda, ne bionso ebi bidi mua kuya ku diakalenga dia Duke; katuena tupeta tshintu nansha tshimue bua kanyinganyinga ketu anu bipumbishi bietu anyi?"

"I wende."

"Mfumu udi mupele bikole menemene bua kumuenzeja ku bukole bua kupeta tshia kudia anyi?"

"I wende."

"Mvua ngela meji bua kuambuluisha mamuanyi mukulakaje uvua mulale pa bulalu ne tshitupa tshia tshitupa tshianyi tshia baleine eu."

"I wende."

"Mukalenge kakusanka ne tshia binayi anyi tshitupa anyi?"

"I wende."

Mu dîyi dimue, baleine uvua mukuatshibue ne mupanyibue, ne Grace wende Duke wa Wellington wakapeta makuta. Pa kumona bualu ebu mu mushindu kampanda, tshilumbu etshi tshidi mua kuikala tshiangatshibua bu bualu bukole, mu nsombelu kampanda, mfumu wa tshitendelelu wa bululame wa mu tshimenga wakafundila Grace wende mukanda ne kanemu konso, umusengelela bua kuangata tshilumbu tshia bena mazuwa aba bavua kabayi ne diakalenga mu lungenyi luonso. Ku bualu ebu Mukalenge wanyi Duke wakandamuna (mikanda yonso ibidi ivua mipatuke) ne: uvua mumane kuenza nanku, ne uvua mupete makuta, ne uvua ne bua kuikala ne dibanza kudi mfumu wa tshitendelelu bikalaye (muntu wa tshijila) mua kubenga kubuelakana mu malu a bantu bakuabu mu matuku atshilualua. Eu ke muntu mukulakaje utshidi anu ne mvita, muimane ku matumba a makalenge asatu, ku bianza bionso wenzeja balomba bintu bua kufila bintu bia luse anyi?

Nebimueneke bipepele ne mu bualu ebu bukenji budibu bamba ne: budi nabu Duke bua kupeta baleine buvua bufila kudi Mukalenge. Tudi ne tshia kukonka dîba adi bua tshinyi Mukalenge udi ne bukokeshi ebu ku ntuadijilu. Mikenji nkayayi ikadi mimane kutekibua. Ino Plowdon udi utupesha bualu bua bualu ebu. Plowdon udi wamba ne: baleine udibu bakuate nunku udi wa Mukalenge ne Mukalenge mukaji, “bua mudiye mutambe buimpe.” Ne kudi bafidi ba ngenyi bapiluke, bualu ebu budi bujadikibue bu tshilumbu tshikole mu malu a mushindu eu.

Kadi bua tshinyi Mukalenge udi ne bua kuikala ne mutu, ne Mukalenge mukaji ne mukila? Bualu bua bualu ebu, nuenu bamanyi ba mikenji!

Mu mukanda wandi wa "Queen-Gold," anyi Queen-pinmoney, mufundi wa kale wa King's Bench, William Prynne, udi wamba nunku ne: "Ye tail is ye Queen's, bua yenu Queen's wardroy ikale ne yenu wa mufuba wa baleine." Mpindieu bualu ebu buvua bufunda tshikondo tshivua mufuba mufiike wa baleine wa Greenland anyi wa baleine wa ku dia balume wenza mudimu bikole mu bilamba bia bakaji. Kadi mufuba eu kawena mu mukila to; udi mu mutu, tshidi tshilema tshia dibungama kudi muena mikenji wa meji bu mudi Prynne. Ino mukalenge mukaji i nsongakaji, bua kumupesha mukila anyi? Diumvuija dia mu tshimfuanyi didi mua kuikala muaba eu.

Kudi mishipa ibidi ya bukalenge milongolola kudi bafundi ba mikenji ba mu Angleterre—baleine ne sturgeon; bionso bibidi bintu bia bukalenge muinshi mua mikalu kampanda, ne bidi bifila tshitupa tshia dikumi tshia makuta a bukalenge. Tshiena mumanye ne: mufundi mukuabu mmuleje bualu ebu to; kadi ku diambuluisha dia lungenyi, bidi bimueneka kundi ne: mushipa wa sturgeon udi ne bua kuikala muabanya mu mushindu wa muomumue ne baleine, Mukalenge udi upeta mutu mukole ne mukole udi wa pa buawu wa mushipa au, udi, mu tshimfuanyi, mua kuikala mushindamene pa bualu kampanda budibu bela meji ne: budi bumusankisha. Ne nunku kudi bualu budi bumueneka mu malu onso, nansha mu mikenji.





SHAPITA 91. Pequod udi usambakena ne Rose-Bud.

"Bivua bia tshianana bua kukuata Ambergriese mu tshiadi tshia Leviathan eu, fetor udi kayi mua kutantamena kayi ubenga kukonka." Sir T. Browne, VE

Bivua lumingu lumue anyi ibidi kunyima kua tshikena kumona tshia ndekelu tshia diloba dia baleine, ne patuvua tuenda tuenda bitekete bitekete pa mbuu wa tulu, wa luya, wa munkatshi mua munya, ne: mishiku ya bungi ivua pa tshibandilu tshia mazuwa a Pequod yakaleja ne: bavua ne budimu bua bungi kupita mêsu asatu avua muulu. Dinunkila dia pa buadi ne kadiyi dimpe menemene divua diunvuika mu mbuu.

"Ndi ngela meji a bualu kampanda mpindieu," ke muakamba Stubb, "ne: muaba kampanda muaba eu kudi bamue ba ku baleine bavua ne manga atuvua bashipe dituku dikuabu. Mvua ngela meji ne: nebafike mu katupa kîpi emu."

Mu tshikondo etshi, lupepele lua kumpala luakaya ku luseke; ne mutantshi mule muvua buatu, ne mazuwa avua malengeja avua aleja ne: baleine kampanda uvua ne bua kuikala ku luseke. Patuvua tusemena pabuipi, muenyi au wakaleja mekala a mu Mfualansa ku mutu kuende; ne ku diambuluisha dia ditutu dia nyunyi ya mu mbuu ivua inyunguluka, ne itambakana, ne imunyunguluka, bivua bimueneka patoke ne: baleine uvua ku luseke luende uvua ne bua kuikala tshidi balobi babikila ne: baleine uvuabu batute, mbuena kuamba ne: baleine uvua mufue kayi munyanguke pa mbuu, ne nunku uvua muele tshitalu tshivua katshiyi tshiakanyine. Bidi mua kuikala biumvuika, mupuya kayi mubi udi tshisumbu tshia nunku ne bua kupatula; bibi kupita tshimenga tshia bena Ashû mu tshipupu, padi badi ne muoyo kabayi ne bukole bua kujika bafue. Bamue bantu badi bamona bualu ebu bu kabuyi mua kuitaba to, nunku kakuena lukuka ludi mua kubasaka bua kuimana ku luseke luabu to. Kadi kudi bantu badi mua kuenza nanku; nansha mudi mafuta adibu bapeta kudi bantu ba mushindu eu adi a mushinga mukese menemene, ne kaena nansha kakese a mushindu wa attar-of-rose to.

Patuvua tusemena pabuipi ne lupepele luvua luenda lujika, tuakamona ne: muena France uvua ne baleine muibidi ku luseke luende; ne baleine muibidi eu uvua umueneka bu wa mpala kupita wa kumpala. Mu bulelela, biakalua umue wa ku baleine idi ne ntatu idi imueneka bu idi yuma ne ifua ne disama dia dyspepsia anyi dipanga dia biakudia; kushiya mibidi yabu mifue pabuipi ne kayiyi ne tshintu tshionso bu mudi mafuta. Nansha nanku, mu muaba muakane netumone ne: kakuena mulobi udi mumanye malu udi mua kuela mpala wende kumpala kua baleine bu eu, nansha yeye mua kuepuka baleine mishipa mu tshibungi.

Pequod uvua mpindieu musemene pabuipi menemene ne muenyi au, ke bualu kayi Stubb wakaditshipa ne: uvua mumanye mutshi wende wa tshikokedi uvua mubuikila mu milongo ivua misuika ku mukila wa umue wa ku baleine eyi.

"Kudi muntu muimpe, mpindieu," wakaseka, muimane kumpala kua mazuwa, "kudi nyama wa ntambue bua nuenu! Ndi mumanye bimpe ne: bena France aba badi anu bademon bapele mu mudimu wa diloba; imue misangu badi bapuekesha buatu buabu bu bashipi ba mishipa, babuangata bu mishiku ya Sperm Whale, ne imue misangu badi bumbusha mazuwa abu onso; bibombi bia buji bua mafuta, ne bibombi bia bintu bia kuowa nabi kapia, kumona ne: mafuta onso adibu mua kupeta kaakumbana bua kuela kapia ka Kapitene to; Diabolo!
