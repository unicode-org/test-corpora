SHAPITA 10. Mulunda wa pa muoyo.

Pamvua mpingana ku Spouter-Inn ufumina ku Nzubu wa Ntendelelu, ngakapeta Queequeg nkayende; yeye mumane kumbuka mu Nzubu wa Ntendelelu kumpala kua dibenesha tshikondo kampanda. Uvua musombe pa tshibasa kumpala kua kapia, ne makasa ende pa tshioshelu, ne ku tshianza tshimue uvua mukuate pabuipi ne mpala wende lupingu luende lukese lua muena tshitungu; udi utangila bikole mu mpala wende, ne muele wa jack-knife udi umutapa bitekete bitekete ku mishiku yende, mu tshikondo atshi udi wela mbila mu mushindu wende wa bumpangano.

Kadi bu muvuaye mpindieu mupangile, wakateka lupingu; ne mutantshi mukese, uya ku mesa, wakangata mukanda munene kuntu aku, ne kuuteka pa tshiadi tshiende kutuadijaye kubala mabeji ne tshibidilu tshia budisuile; ku dibeji dionso dia makumi atanu—anu mumvua ngela meji—wimana tshitupa tshîpi, utangila munda mutupu, ne ufila dîyi dia tshiona tshia dikema tshia mutantshi mule. Uvua mua kutuadija kabidi ku makumi atanu avua alonda; uvua umueneka bu uvua utuadija ku nomba umue misangu yonso, bienze anu bu ne: kavua mua kubala kupita makumi atanu to, ne anu pavua bungi bunene bua makumi atanu buvuabu bapeta pamue, ke bualu kayi wakakema bua bungi bua mabeji.

Ne disanka dia bungi ngakasomba mutangile. Nansha muvuaye muena tshinyangu, ne munyanguke bikole ku mpala—nansha mundi musue—mpala wende uvua ne tshintu tshivua katshiyi tshimpe nansha kakese. Kuena mua kusokoka anyima to. Mu binjinjimbi biende bionso bidi kabiyi bia pa buloba, mvua ngela meji ne: mvua mumone bilejilu bia mutshima mupepele wa bululame; ne mu mêsu ende manene, makole, mafike ne a dikima, muvua bimanyinu bia nyuma uvua mua kutshimuna bademon tshinunu. Ne pa kumbusha bionso ebi, kuvua bualu kampanda bua mushinga mukole buvua butangila Mumpangano, buvua nansha bubi buende kabuyi mua kunyangakaja nansha kakese. Uvua umueneka bu muntu uvua kayi muanji kuela mbila ne kayi muanji kuikala ne muena dibanza. Nansha bikalabi ne: mutu wende uvua mukosa, mpala wende uvua mupatuke mu mushindu wa budikadidi ne mutoke, ne uvua umueneka mualabale kupita muvuaye mua kuikala, bualu ebu tshiena mua kudienzeja bua kupangadija to; kadi bushuwa mutu wende uvua muimpe be. Bidi mua kumueneka bu bualu bua bupote, kadi bivua bimvuluija mutu wa mfumu wa basalayi Washington, bu mudibi bimueneka mu bimfuanyi biende bia bantu ba bungi. Uvua ne tshibandilu tshile tshia muomumue tshivua tshienda tshipingana pa mutu pa mpala, tshivua kabidi tshipatuka bikole, bu mikuna ibidi mile ivua ne mitshi mikole pa mutu. Queequeg uvua George Washington mulongolola mu mushindu wa kudia bantu.

Pamvua mmutangila bimpe, ngenza bu ne: mvua mutangile tshipepele tshikole tshivua tshifumina ku tshikuku, kavua muteleje dikalaku dianyi nansha kakese, kavua muditatshishe nansha kakese nansha kakese; kadi uvua umueneka muikale ne mudimu wonso wa kubala mabeji a mukanda wa dikema. Patudi tuela meji bua mushindu utuvua balale pamue butuku bua kumpala, ne nangananga patudi tuela meji bua diboko dia dinanga dimvua mupete dianyisha pamvua njuka mu dinda, ngakamona ne: dipanga dia dianyisha diende edi divua dia dikema. Kadi nyama ya luonji i bifukibua bia dikema; imue misangu kuena mumanye menemene mua kubiangata to. Kumpala badi bapitakana; didisangisha diabu dia malu mapepele didi dimueneka bu meji a Socrate. Ngakamona kabidi ne: Queequeg kavua muanji kudisangisha nansha kakese, anyi kakese menemene, ne bena mazuwa bakuabu ba mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi to. Kavua muenze bualu nansha bumue; uvua umueneka kayi ne dijinga dia kuvudija tshisumbu tshia bantu bavuaye mumanye to. Bionso ebi biakantonda bu tshintu tshia bukole; kadi, pa kuela meji musangu muibidi, kuvua bualu kampanda bua dikema munda muawu. Muntu eu uvua mutantshi wa kilometre binunu makumi abidi ne nzubu, mu njila wa Cape Horn, mmumue ne:—uvua mushindu umuepele uvuaye mua kufikamu—muela munkatshi mua bantu bavuaye kayi mumanye bienze anu bu uvua mu buloba bua Jupiter; ne nansha nanku uvua umueneka mupole; kulama ditalala dia bungi; usanka ne bulunda buende nkayende; misangu yonso muomumue ne yeye nkayende. Bushuwa, bualu ebu buvua tshitupa tshia lungenyi luimpe; nansha muvuaye kayi muanji kumvua ne: kudi bualu bua nunku. Kadi, pamuapa, bua kuikala bena meji balelela, tuetu bantu katuena ne bua kuikala ne lungenyi lua kuikala ne muoyo anyi kudienzeja nunku to. Diakamue pamvua ngumvua ne: muntu eu udi udifila bua kuikala muena meji, ndi mfika ku diamba ne: anu bu mamu mukulakaje uvua ne disama dia tshiadi, uvua ne bua kuikala “mutshibule tshiamu tshiende tshia kudia.”

Pamvua musombe mu nzubu au uvua mpindieu nkayanyi; kapia kadi katema kakese, mu tshitupa atshi tshia bupole padi, kunyima kua bukole buatshi bua kumpala bumane kukaja kapepe, pashishe kadi kakenka anu bua kukatangila; mundidimbi wa dilolo ne bintu bia mu nyuma bivua bibuela mu nzubu ya tshilala benyi, ne bitutangila tuetu babidi bapuwe, nkayetu; tshipepele tshikole tshidi tshienda tshikola; Ngakatuadija kuikala ne lungenyi lua dikema. Ngakumvua dinyanguka munda muanyi. Kakuvua kabidi mutshima wanyi mupanduluke ne tshianza tshianyi tshinyanguke bivua bikudimune bua kuluisha buloba bua mbua to. Muntu wa luonji eu uvua muwupikula. Uvua musombe kuntu aku, dipanga diende dia dianyisha divua dileja ngikadilu uvua kayi ne lubombo lua tshididi ne dishima dia patupu. Uvua wa tshisuku; tshikena kumona tshia kumona; kadi ngakatuadija kudiumvua mukokibue kudiye mu mushindu udi kauyi umvuika. Ne bintu bia muomumue abi bivua mua kuipata bakuabu ba bungi, ke bivua binkoka bivua binkoka nunku. Nsa kuteta mulunda wa bampangano, ngakela meji, bualu bulenga bua bena kilisto budi buleja anu bulenga bua patupu. Ngakateka tshibasa tshianyi pabuipi nende, ne meme kuenza bimanyinu bia bulunda ne mibelu, ngenza muanyi muonso bua kuyukila nende mu tshikondo atshi. Ku ntuadijilu kavua mumone malu aa to; kadi mpindieu, pamvua ngakula bua diakidila diende dia butuku bua ndekelu, wakatuadija kungebeja ni tuvua mua kuikala kabidi balala pamue. Ngakamuambila ne: eyowa; pamvua ngela meji ne: uvua umueneka musankishibue, pamuapa muele kalumbandi kakese.

Pashishe tuakabulula mukanda pamue, meme kudienzeja bua kumumvuija tshipatshila tshia dipatula mukanda au, ne diumvuija dia bimfuanyi bikese bivuamu. Nunku ngakapeta dijinga diende; ne kumbukila apu tuakaya kuakula bimpe menemene bua bintu bishilashilangane bia pambelu bivuabu mua kumona mu tshimenga etshi tshiende lumu. Mu katupa kîpi ngakafila lungenyi lua kunua makanya; ne, mupatule tshibombi tshienda ne tomahawk, wakampesha tshintu tshia kunua. Ne pashishe tuakasomba tushintakaja mupuya wa mupuya wende wa tshisuku, ne tuenza bua upita pa tshibidilu pankatshi petu.

Bikalaku kabidi tshintu tshia dipanga dia dianyisha tshivua tshisokome kundi mu tshiadi tshia Pagan, muishi eu muimpe ne wa bulenga utuvua nende, wakawujimija mu katupa kîpi, ne kutushiya balunda. Uvua umueneka bu uvua wangata malu a pa tshibidilu ne kayi mubikidibue anu bu mundi meme kumuangata; ne pavua muishi wetu mujike, wakafina mpala wende ku wanyi, kunkuataye mu tshiadi, ne kungambilaye ne: kubangila anu mpindieu tudi baselangane; mbuena kuamba ne, mu tshiambilu tshia ditunga diabu, ne tuvua balunda ba pa muoyo; uvua mua kufua bua bualu buanyi ne disanka dionso, bikalabi bikengela. Mu muena ditunga, lubimbi lua bulunda lua tshimpitshimpi elu luvua mua kuikala lumueneka bu lua kumpala kua dîba, bualu budi kabuyi mua kueyemenyibua bikole; kadi mu tshisuku tshipepele etshi mikenji ya kale ayi kayivua mua kukumbana to.

Panyima pa didia dia dilolo, ne diyukidilangana dikuabu ne dinua dia makanya, tuakaya mu nzubu wetu pamue. Yeye wakampesha dipa dia mutu wende mulengeja; wakapatula tshibombi tshienda tshinene tshia makanya, ne wenda ulenga muinshi mua makanya, kupatulaye dolare mitue ku makumi asatu ya arjan; pashishe kubitangalaja pa mesa, ne kubitapulula mu bitupa bibidi bia muomumue, kuela tshimue tshia kudibi kundi, ne kuamba ne: tshivua tshianyi. Mvua nya kuela mpata; kadi wakanpuekesha dîyi pa kubipongolola mu mifuba ya pantalo yanyi. Ngakabalekela bashala. Pashishe wakaya kuenza masambila ende a dilolo, kupatula lupingu luende, ne kumbusha tshilamba tshia kapia tshia dibeji. Ku diambuluisha dia bimanyinu kampanda, mvua ngela meji ne: uvua umueneka musue bua meme kumusambakena nende; kadi bu mumvua mumanye bimpe tshivua ne bua kulonda, ngakela meji bua tshitupa tshîpi bua kumanya ni, yeye mumbikile, mvua mua kutumikila anyi kubenga.

Mvua muena Kristo muimpe; muledibua ne mukolele mu tshiadi tshia Ekeleziya wa Presbytérien udi kayi ne bilema. Mmunyi mumvua mua kudisangisha ne mutendeledi wa mpingu wa tshisuku eu bua kutendelela tshitupa tshiende tshia mutshi? Kadi ntendelelu ntshinyi? Meme kuela meji. Udi wela meji mpindieu, Yishemaele, ne Nzambi wa diulu ne buloba wa luse—bampangano ne bonso—udi mua kuikala ne mukawu bua tshitupa tshia mutshi mufiike tshidi katshiyi ne mushinga anyi? Kabiena mua kuenzeka to! Kadi ntendelelu ntshinyi?—kuenza disua dia Nzambi— ke ntendelelu. Ne disua dia Nzambi ntshinyi?—kuenzela muntu nanyi tshindi musue bua muntu nanyi angenzele— ke disua dia Nzambi. Mpindieu, Queequeg udi muntu nanyi. Ne ntshinyi tshindi musue bua Queequeg eu angenzele? Bua tshinyi, ikala mu buobumue nanyi mu mushindu wanyi wa ntendelelu wa bena Presbytérien. Nunku, ndi ne tshia kuikala nende mu buobumue; nunku, ndi ne tshia kulua mutendeledi wa mpingu. Nunku ngakatemesha bipese; wakambuluisha bua kukolesha lupingu lukese luvua kaluyi ne bualu; wakamupesha biscuit muosha ne Queequeg; uvua muela mbila kumpala kuende misangu ibidi anyi isatu; kumutua mishiku; ne kunyima kua bualu ebu, tuakavula bilamba ne tuetu kuya kulala, mu ditalala ne kondo ketu ka muoyo ne ba pa buloba bujima. Kadi katuvua tuya kulala katuyi tuyukila kakese to.

Tshiena mumanye mudibi to; kadi kakuena muaba udi bu bulalu bua dimanyisha dia malu masokoka pankatshi pa balunda to. Badi bamba ne: mulume ne mukaji badi banzulula mutshima wabu kudi muntu ne muntu; ne bamue bena dibaka bakulakaje batu batamba kushima ne kuyukila malu a kale too ne mu dinda. Nunku, mu luendu lua mu mitshima yetu, meme ne Queequeg tuvua tulala—bantu babidi ba dinanga ne ba dinanga.





SHAPITA 11. Tshilamba tshia butuku.

Tuvua balale nunku mu bulalu, tuyukila ne tulala tulu mu bikondo bikese, ne Queequeg misangu ne misangu uvua wela maulu ende a nsongo avua ne tatuaje pa mutu pa anyi ne dinanga dionso, ne pashishe uwakoka panyima; nunku tuvua bantu ba bungi ne badikadile ne bapepele; pavua, ndekelu wa bionso, bua diyukidilangana dietu, bualu bukese bua tulu buvua bushale munda muetu buakajika, ne tuetu kumvua dijinga dia kujuka kabidi, nansha muvua dikisha dia munya dikale anu mutantshi mule.

Eyowa, tuakalua batabale bikole; bia bungi menemene, muaba wetu wa kulala wakatuadija kututshiokesha, ne ku kakese ku kakese tuvua tudimona basombe; bilamba bivua bitunyunguluke bimpe, bishindamene pa tshilamba tshia ku mutu ne binu bietu binayi bikuate pabuipi, ne mishiku yetu ibidi mikoka pa mutu pabi, bienze anu bu ne: bibombi bietu bivua bia kuowa nabi kapia. Tuvua tudiumvua bimpe ne mu bupole, nangananga bualu kuvua mashika a bungi pambelu; bushuwa, mu bilamba bia bulalu kabidi, mumone ne: kakuvua kapia mu nzubu to. Ndi ngamba nanku, bualu bua bushuwa bua kusanka ne luya lua mubidi, tshitupa tshikese tshia mubidi webe tshidi ne bua kuikala ne mashika, bualu kakuena ngikadilu nansha umue pa buloba ebu udi kayi tshidiye anu ku dishilangana to. Kakuena tshintu tshidiku nkayatshi to. Shi udi uditumbisha ne udi mupole, ne udi mutantshi mule, dîba adi kabena mua kuamba ne udi mupole kabidi to. Ino shi, pamo bwa ami ne Queequeg mu bulalu, nsongo ya mulu obe nansha nsongo ya mutwe obe mikale ne mashika, mwanda waka, mu bulelela, mu lungenyi luebe luonso udi umvua disanka dia bungi ne luya lukole. Bua bualu ebu, nzubu wa kulala kena ne tshia kuikala ne kapia to, bualu ebu budi bumue bua ku malu a mushinga mukole adi babanji bapeta. Bualu bule bua mushindu ewu wa bulenga budi bua kuikala ne tshintu tshikuabu to, anu tshilamba tshidi pankatshi pebe ne bupole buebe ne mashika a lupepele lua pambelu. Pashishe udi ulala bu tshintu tshimue tshidi tshifila kapia mu mutshima wa dibue dia arctique.

Tuvua basombe mu mushindu eu wa kuinama mutantshi mule, pamvua ngela meji ne: mvua mua kukangula mêsu anyi; bualu pandi pankatshi pa bilamba, nansha mu munya anyi butuku, ne nansha mulale anyi mubike, ndi ne mushindu wa kulama mêsu anyi makanga misangu yonso, bua kumona mua kuimanyina pa bupole bua kuikala mu bulalu. Bualu kakuena muntu udi mua kumvua bumuntu buende bimpe to, anu padiye kayi mukanga mêsu ende; bu ne: mîdima ivua bushuwa tshintu tshimpe tshia bumuntu buetu, nansha mudi butoke buikale buimpe ku tshitupa tshietu tshia dilongo. Pamvua mubulule mêsu anyi, ne mupatuke mu mîdima yanyi mimpe ne ya meme nkayanyi mu mîdima ya pambelu mikole ne mikole ya dîba dia dikumi ne dibidi dia butuku divua kadiyi ditoke, ngakapeta tshiji tshikole. Kabidi tshivua mubenge nansha kakese lungenyi lua kudi Queequeg lua se: pamuapa bivua bimpe kuela muendu, bualu tuvua batabale bikole; ne pa kumbusha apu uvua ne dijinga dikole dia kuikala ne bintu bikese bia mutoyi bia mu Tomahawk wende. Tuambe ne: nansha mumvua mumvue tshiji tshikole bua dinua diende dia makanya mu bulalu butuku bua kumpala, kadi mona mudi kansungansunga ketu kakole kakola padi dinanga dilua kudikosa. Bua mpindieu tshiena musue tshintu tshikuabu tshimpe kupita kuikala ne Queequeg unua makanya pabuipi nanyi, nansha mu bulalu, bualu uvua umueneka muwule tente ne disanka dia mu nzubu dia ditalala tshikondo atshi. Tshivua kabidi muditatshisha bikole bua mikenji ya tshishimbi ya muena nzubu to. Mvua anu ne muoyo bua kuikala ne disulakana dia tshisokomenu dia kuabanya pipe ne tshilamba ne mulunda wanyi mulelela. Ne bilamba bietu bia nsuki mikoka pa makaya etu, mpindieu tuakapita Tomahawk ku umue too ne ku mukuabu, too ne pavua tshintu tshia bule tshivua tshilengeja muishi tshikola pa mutu petu, tshitokeshibua kudi lubimbi lua muendu mupiamupia.

Nansha biobi bikale ne: mutetshi eu uvua muele tshisuku mu miaba ya kule, tshiena mumanye to, kadi mpindieu uvua wakula bua tshidila tshiende tshia diledibua; ne, mu dijinga dia kumvua muyuki wende, ngakamusengelela bua kutungunuka ne kuakula. Wakatumikila ne disanka dionso. Nansha mumvua tshikondo atshi tshiyi mumvue bimpe mêyi ende makese, kadi malu avuaye muambe pashishe, pamvua mumanye bimpe miaku yende minyanguke, mpindieu adi angambuluisha bua kuleja muyuki mujima mudiwu mua kuikala mu tshimfuanyi tshindi mfila.





SHAPITA 12. Biographie.

Queequeg uvua muledibue mu Rokovoko, tshidila tshidi kule menemene ku Ouest ne ku Sud. Kabiena panshi pa karte nansha kamue; miaba milelela kayena nansha kakese.

Padi nyama wa luonji mupiamupia wenda unyema mu mpata wende wa diledibua mu bisonsa, ulonda kudi mbuji idi idia, bienze anu bu mutshi wa tshisuku; nansha nanku, mu mutshima wa Queequeg wa dijinga dia kupeta bintu bia bungi, uvua ne dijinga dikole dia kumona tshintu tshikuabu tshia Bukua-Buena-Kristo kupita muena baleine umue anyi ibidi. Tatuende uvua Mfumu Munene, Mukalenge; kakuende uvua Muakuidi Munene; ne ku luseke lua mamu uvua uditumbisha ne bana ba bakaji bavua bakaji ba baluanganyi ba mvita bavua kabayi mua kutshimuna. Kuvua mashi mimpe mu mijilu yende—bintu bia bukalenge; nansha muvuaye munyanguke bikole, ndi ntshina, bua tshilele tshia kudia bantu tshivuaye mukoleshe mu bunsonga buende kayi mulongeshibue.

Mazuwa a Sag Harbor akaya kutangila tshitudilu tshia tatuende, ne Queequeg wakakeba njila wa kuya mu matunga a bena Kristo. Kadi buatu, buvua ne bantu ba bungi bavua benza mudimu mu mazuwa, buakabenga tshilamba tshiende; ne bukole buonso bua tatuende bua Mukalenge kabuvua mua kutshimuna to. Kadi Queequeg wakaditshipa. Yeye nkayende mu buatu buende, wakaya ku tshibandabanda tshia kule, tshivuaye mumanye ne: mazuwa avua ne bua kupitshilamu pavuaye umbuka mu tshidila. Ku luseke lumue kuvua dibue dia koray; ku luseke lukuabu ludimi lua buloba lupuekele, lubuikila kudi bisonsa bia mangrove bivua bikola mu mâyi. Wakasokoka buatu buende, butshidi pa mayi, munkatshi mua bisonsa ebi, ne tshiadi tshiabu tshitangile ku mbuu, wakasomba ku nyima kua buatu, ne tshiamu tshia kuendesha natshi mu tshianza; ne pavua buatu buenda buenda buenda, wakapatuka bu munya; wakapeta luseke luenda; ne dikasa diende dimue dia panyima wakakuluka ne kuina buatu buende; wakabanda ku minyololo; ne wakadiela mu bule buonso pa tshibandilu, wakakuata tshingoma kuntu aku, ne wakaditshipa bua kubenga kutshilekela, nansha muvuaye mutshibule bitupa bitupa.

Kapitene wakatshinyisha bua kumuela mu buatu tshianana; uvua muele tshingoma pa bianza biende bia butaka; Queequeg uvua muana wa mukalenge, ne Queequeg kavua mushintuluke to. Bu muvuaye mukemeshibue kudi dipanga dia buôwa diende, ne dijinga diende dikole dia kuya kutangila Bukua-Buena-Kristo, ndekelu wa bionso kapitene wakalekela, ne kumuambilaye ne: uvua mua kuikala kumbelu. Kadi nsongalume muimpe eu—Mukalenge wa mu mbuu wa mu ditunga dia Pays de Galles, kavua muanji kumona nzubu wa Kapitene to. Bakamuteka panshi munkatshi mua bendeshi ba mazuwa, ne kumuvuija muena baleine. Ino pamo bwa Czar Peter udi musue kuenza mudimu mu nzubu ya mazuwa ya mu bimenga bia ku babende, Queequeg kavua ubenga bundu buvua bumueneka to, bikalaye mua kupeta ne disanka bukole bua kutokesha bena ditunga diende bavua kabayi balongeshibue. Bualu ku ndekelu—ke muvuaye mungambile—uvua muenzejibue kudi dijinga dikole dia kulonga munkatshi mua bena kilisto, mishindu ya kuenza bua bantu bende bikale ne disanka dia bungi kupita muvuabu; ne kupita apu, bidi anu bimpa kupita muvuabu. Kadi, diakabi! bienzedi bia baleine biakamujadikila mu katupa kîpi ne: nansha bena Kristo badi mua kuikala bena dikenga ne babi; kupita bampangano bonso ba tatuende. Ndekelu wa bionso tuakafika ku tshitudilu tshia kale tshia Sag; ne wakamona malu avua bendeshi ba mazuwa benza kuntu aku; ne pashishe kuya ku Nantucket, ne kumona muvuabu bapitshisha makuta abu muaba au kabidi, Queequeg mupele wakawulekela bu tshintu tshivuaye mujimije. Uvua wela meji ne: mbuloba bubi mu mishindu yonso; Nsa kufua mumpangano.

Ne nunku mutendeledi wa mpingu wa kale mu mutshima, uvua anu musombele munkatshi mua bena kilisto aba, uvuala bilamba biabu, ne uvua uteta kuakula malu abu a tshianana. Ke bualu kayi mishindu ya dikema idi itangila yeye, nansha mudiye mpindieu mutantshi mule kumbelu.

Ku diambuluisha dia ngenyi, ngakamuebeja ni kavua musue kupingana, ne kuenza tshibilu tshia ku mutu kua bukalenge to; bualu uvua mua kumona tatuende bu mufue ne mufue, bualu uvua mukulakaje bikole ne mutekete mu mianu ya ndekelu. Yeye kuandamuna ne: tòo, ki mmuanji to; ne wakasakidila ne: uvua ne buôwa bua se: bena kilisto, anyi bena kilisto, kabavua bamukumbane bua kubanda mu nkuasa wa bukalenge mukezuke ne kayi munyanguke wa bakalenge bampangano makumi asatu bavua kumpala kuende to. Kadi ku kakese ku kakese, wakamba ne: uvua ne bua kupingana,—diakamue pavuaye udiumvua mubatijibue kabidi. Bua tshitupa tshîpi, nansha nanku, wakapangadija bua kuya mu mazuwa, ne kukuna nkunde yende ya muitu mu mbuu yonso inayi. Bavua bamuvuije muena lungoma, ne tshiamu tshia mitaku atshi tshivua pa muaba wa dikombo dia bukalenge mpindieu.
