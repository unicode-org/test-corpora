SHAPITA 24. Muakuidi.

Bu mudi meme ne Queequeg mpindieu batuadije mudimu eu wa diloba dia baleine; ne bu mudi mudimu eu wa diloba dia baleine mu mushindu kampanda mufike ku diangatshibua kudi bena pa buloba bu mudimu udi kauyi wa tusala ne udi kayi ne lumu luimpe; nunku, ndi ne dijinga dikole dia kunujadikila, nuenu bena buloba, bua malu mabi adibu batuenzela tuetu balobi ba baleine.

SHAPITA 28. Ahaba.

Matuku a bungi kunyima kua yeye mumane kumbuka mu Nantucket, kakuvua tshintu nansha tshimue tshivua tshimueneka tshia Kapitene Ahab to. Bena dibaka bavua bambuluishangana pa tshibidilu mu bikondo bia mudimu, ne bua tshionso tshivua mua kumueneka tshishilangane, bavua bamueneka bu bobu nkayabu bavua balombola mazuwa; anu bobu bavua imue misangu bapatuka mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi ne mêyi a tshimpitshimpi ne a tshimpitshimpi, ne kunyima kua bionso bivua bimueneka patoke ne: bavua anu batuma dîyi pa muaba wa bakuabu. Eyowa, mfumuabu mutambe bunene ne mfumu wa tshikisu uvuaku, nansha muvuaye kayi muanji kumona kudi mêsu nansha amue avua kaayi maanyishibue bua kubuela mu nzubu wa tshijila wa nzubu wa tshilala benyi.

Dîba dionso dimvua mbanda pa tshibandilu tshia mazuwa ku diambuluisha dia nsa yanyi ivua kuinshi, mvua ntangila diakamue panyima bua kumona ni mpala kampanda wa dikema uvua umueneka; bualu bua dibungama dianyi dia kumpala dia patupu dia kulenga kapitene udi kayi mumanyike, mpindieu mu tshisokomenu tshia mbuu, diakalua pabuipi ne tshinyangu. Bualu ebu buvua buvula bikole imue misangu kudi malu a diabolo a Eliya uvua munyanguke avua alua kundi tshiyi mubikidibue, ne bukole budi kabuyi bumueneka bumvua tshiyi muanji kuela meji to. Kadi tshivua mua kubitantamena to, anu bu muvuabi mu bikondo bikuabu mvua pabuipi ne kuseka bua malu a tshijila a muprofete au wa pa buende wa ku mazuwa. Kadi nansha biobi bikale bia buôwa anyi bia dipanga dia ditalala—bua kubikila nanku—tshimvua ngumvua, kadi dîba dionso dimvua mlua kutangila muaba umvua mu buatu, bivua bimueneka bu bivua kabiyi biakane bua kuangata lungenyi lua nunku ne mushinga. Bualu nansha muvua bena tshingoma, ne tshisumbu tshinene tshia bena mudimu, bikale bantu ba tshikisu, ba bisamba bia bende ne ba mishindu ne mishindu kupita kumpanyi yonso ya mazuwa a ngendu avua malu amvua mumone kumpala manfikishe ku dimanya, nansha nanku mvua ngela meji ne: bualu ebu budi bufumina ku bumuntu bua pa buabu bua tshisuku tshia bena Scandina tshivua tshienze bua meme kumbuka. Kadi bivua nangananga bualu bua bamfumu basatu ba mazuwa, bena mudimu nende, buvua buenzejibue bikole bua kujikija dielakana edi dia tshianana, ne kufikisha bantu ku dieyemena ne ku disanka mu luendu luonso luvuabu benza. Bantu basatu bampe, badi mua kuikala bamfumu ba mazuwa ne bantu, yonso mu mushindu wende, kabavua mua kubapeta bipepele to, ne yonso wa kudibu uvua bena Amerike; muena Nantucketer, muena madimi a tumuma tua mvinyo, muntu wa mu Cape. Mpindieu, uvua Nowele pavua mazuwa mapatuke ku tshitudilu tshiawu, bua muaba utuvua ne luya lua Polar lukole, nansha mutuvua tunyema misangu yonso batangile ku Sud; ne ku degre yonso ne ku minite yonso ya latitude ituvua tuenda, ku kakese ku kakese tushiya muvu wa mashika au uvua kauyi ne luse, ne tshikondo tshiende tshionso tshivua katshiyi tshimpe panyima petu. Bivua bimue bia ku matuku avua kaayi apuekela, kadi avua anu makole ne avua ne mîdima mu dinda dia dishintuluka, pavua ne lupepele luimpe buatu buenda lubilu mu mâyi ne lubilu lua dibutuka ne lua kanyinganyinga, ne pamvua mubande pa tshibandilu tshia mazuwa ku dibikila dia tshiluilu tshia mu dinda, diakamue pangakatangila ku tabora, buatu buvua buenda bupueka bua kukankamika. Bulelela buvua bupita buôwa; Kapitene Ahaba uvua muimane pa tshibandilu tshiende.

Kakuvua tshimanyishilu nansha tshimue tshia disama dia mubidi dia pa tshibidilu kudiye, nansha dia diondapibua dia disama nansha dimue. Uvua umueneka bu muntu uvuabu bakose ku mutshi, pavua kapia kanyangakaja bitupa bionso bia mubidi kabiyi bibutula, anyi bimbusha tshitupa tshimue tshia bukole buabi bua kale. Mubidi wende mujima mule, mualabale, uvua umueneka muenza ne tshiamu tshia mitaku tshikole, ne muenza mu mushindu uvua kauyi mua kushintuluka, bu mudi Perseus muenza kudi Cellini. Pavuaye wenda upatuka munkatshi mua nsuki yende mitoke, ne wenda upuekela ku luseke lumue lua mpala wende ne nshingu wende bivua bikuate kapia, too ne pavuaye mujimine mu bilamba biende, uvua mumone tshimanyinu tshitekete tshivua tshienze bu dikombo, tshitoke bikole. Bivua bifuanangana ne tshilamba tshivua tshienza imue misangu mu mutshi mululame ne mubandile, pavua mupenyi wa kuulu uwututa panshi, ne kayi utshibula ditamba nansha dimue, ukosa ne ukosolola tshikoba kumbukila kuulu too ne kuinshi, kumpala kua kuyawu mu buloba, ushiya mutshi utshidi anu ne muoyo, kadi muikale ne tshimanyinu. Nansha tshimanyishilu atshi tshivua tshiledibue nende, anyi tshivua tshilonda tshivua tshishiye kudi mputa kampanda mukole, muntu nansha umue kavua mua kuamba to. Ku diambuluisha dia dianyisha dia patupu, mu luendu luonso kabavua bakula nansha kakese bua bualu ebu to, nangananga kudi bena dibaka. Ino musangu umo mukulumpe wa Tashtego, muena Inde mukulakaje wa Gay-Head munkatshi mua bena mudimu, wakamba ne ditabuja dionso ne: ki mpavuaye ne bidimu makumi anayi ke pakalua Ahaba kuikala ne tshimanyinu tshia nunku to, ne pashishe biakamufikila, ki mbua tshiji tshia mvita ya lufu to, kadi mu mvita ya bintu bia mu mbuu. Ino, kintu kikatampe kino kyadi kimweke bu kya bubi, ku kintu kya Manxman mufike, muntu mukulakaje wa mu lukita, wadi, kayi muanji kupatuka mu Nantucket, kayi muanji kumona dîsu edi pa Ahaba wa tshisuku to. Nansha nanku, bilele bia kale bia mbuu, bilele bia kale, biakapesha muntu wa kale eu bukole bua kujingulula malu. Nunku kakuena muendeshi wa mazuwa mutoke uvua mua kumubenga bikole pavuaye muambe ne: bikalabu mua kulala Kapitene Ahaba mu ditalala—tshivua mua kuikala tshikole bua kuenzeka, ke muvuaye muambe ne: dîba adi, muntu yonso uvua ne bua kuenza mudimu wa ndekelu au bua bafue, uvua mua kupeta tshimanyinu tshia diledibua kudiye kumbukila ku tshifulu too ne ku tshiadi.

Bukole buonso bua tshimuenekelu tshibi tshia Ahaba tshiakantonda, ne tshimanyishilu tshibi tshivua tshimueneka, ke bualu kayi mu bikondo bikese bia kumpala tshivua mumone ne: nansha kakese ka tshiji etshi tshivua tshifumina ku dikasa ditoke dia tshinyangu divuaye muimane. Mvua mufike ku dimona ne: dikasa edi dia meno a ntambue divua mu mbuu dienza ne mufuba mulengeja wa mpala wa baleine wa sperme. "Eyowa, uvua mutshiokeshibue ku Japon," ke muvua muena Inde wa kale wa Gay-Head muambe umue musangu; "kadi anu bu buatu buende buvua bunyanguke, wakatuma tshiamu tshikuabu tshia mazuwa kayi mupingane kumbelu bua kutshipeta to. Udi ne tshiamu tshia mvita tshia 'em."

Ngakakema bua mushindu uvuaye muimane. Ku luseke luonso lua tshisasa tshia Pequod, ne pabuipi ne tshilamba tshia mizzen, kuvua tshina tshia auger, tshibueja bu tshitupa tshia inch anyi kupita apu, mu dibaya. Dikasa diende dia mufuba diakashindama mu tshina atshi; diboko dimue dibandisha, ne dikuate ku tshilamba; Kapitene Ahaba uvua muimane mululame, mutangile ku luseke lukuabu lua mazuwa avua anu apueka. Kuvua bukole budi kabuyi ne ndekelu, dipangadika dikole, didi kadiyi mua kulekela, mu didifila dishindame ne didi kadiyi ne buôwa, dia kumpala dia ditangila adi. Kavua muambe dîyi nansha dimue; banene bende kabavua bamuambile bualu nansha bumue to; nansha mudibi ne bilejilu biabu bikese ne bilejilu biabu, bavua baleja patoke mudibu kabayi ne ditalala, nansha biobi kabiyi bienza kanyinganyinga, bua kuikala muinshi mua dîsu dia mfumu udi ne lutatu. Ne ki nganu ke bualu abu to, kadi Ahaba uvua ne tshiji tshikole uvua muimane kumpala kuabu ne mutshi wa tshikokedi ku mpala; mu bunême buonso bua bukalenge budi kabuyi ne dina bua dikenga kampanda dinene.

Katupa kîpi, ku dikumbula diende dia kumpala mu lupepele, wakapingana mu nzubu wende wa tshilala benyi. Kadi kunyima kua dinda adi, uvua umueneka dituku dionso kudi bena mudimu; nansha muimane mu tshina tshienda, anyi musombe pa tshibasa tshia meno a ntambue tshivuaye natshi; anyi kuendakana bikole pa tshibandilu. Pavua diulu dienda dikepela; bulelela, wakatuadija kukola kakese, wakalua anu muena budisuile; bienze anu bu ne: pavua mazuwa mafume kumbelu, kakuvua tshintu tshikuabu to, anu luya lukole lua mu mbuu luvua lumulame nkayende. Ne, mu katupa kîpi, biakenzeka ne, uvua pabuipi ne kuikala mu lupepele misangu yonso; kadi, too ne mpindieu, bua bionso bivuaye muambe, anyi muenze, pa tshibandilu tshia ndekelu tshivua ne munya, uvua umueneka kayi ne mushinga kuntu aku anu bu tshitekelu tshikuabu tshia mazuwa. Kadi Pequod uvua anu wenza tshitupa mpindieu; kuyi wenda pa tshibidilu; pabuipi ne mishindu yonso ya diloba dia baleine ivua dijinga ne dilombola kudi bena dibaka bavua bamanye bimpe, nunku kakuvua tshintu nansha tshimue, tshia kudiye nkayende, tshia kuenza anyi kusankisha Ahaba, mpindieu; ne nunku kuipata, bua tshitupa tshimue atshi, matutu avua mabuikila pa matutu avua mabuikila pa mpala wende, anu mudi matutu onso asungula mitu mitumbuke bua kudibuikila.

Nansha nanku, mu katupa kîpi emu, luya, luya lua dishima lua tshikondo tshimpe tshia dikisha tshituvua bafike, luakamueneka bu luvua lumusankisha ku kakese ku kakese. Bualu, bu mudi bana ba bakaji ba matama makunze, ba maja, ngondo muinayi ne ngondo muitanu, baya kumbelu mu mitshi ya mashika, ya luse; nansha mutshi wa chêne wa kale udi kayi ne tshintu, mukole, udi ne makubakuba udi mua kutuma tumuma tukese tua matamba, bua kuakidila benyi badi ne disanka dia nunku; nunku Ahaba wakenza, ku ndekelu, diandamuna dikese ku dikokangana dia luya lua nsongakaji alu. Misangu mipite pa umua uvua muleje tshimuenekelu tshitekete tshia mêsu, tshivua, mu muntu mukuabu yonso, mua kupatuka mu katupa kîpi emu mu diseka.
