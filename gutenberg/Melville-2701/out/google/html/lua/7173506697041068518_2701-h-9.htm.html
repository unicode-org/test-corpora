SHAPITA 43. Teleja!

"HIST! Udi mumvue mutoyi au anyi, Cabaco?"

Bivua tshikondo tshia munkatshi: butoke bua ngondo; bena mazuwa bavua bimane mu tshijengu, kumbukila ku tshimue tshia ku bimanu bia mâyi mimpe mu tshiadi, too ne ku tshibandilu tshia scuttle pabuipi ne taffrail. Mu mushindu ewu, bavua bapitshisha bibombi bua kuwuja scuttle-butt. Pavuabu bimane, mu tshitupa tshinene, pa muaba wa tshijila wa tshisasa, bavua badimuke bua kubenga kuakula anyi kuenza tshiona tshia makasa abu. Ku tshianza ne ku tshianza, bibombi bivua bienda mu ditalala dikole, anu bitshibuka kudi dikuma dia tshiamu tshia mazuwa, ne mutoyi wa tshiamu tshia mazuwa tshivua tshienda tshienda tshikola.

Munkatshi mua dikisha edi, ke muvua Archy, umue wa ku ba-cordon, uvua ne tshitekelu tshiende pabuipi ne tshiibi tshia kunyima, muele muena mutumba nende, muena Cholo, mêyi adi kulu eku.

"Hist! udi mumvue mutoyi au anyi, Cabaco?"

"Angata tshibombi, udi musue, Archy? Mmutoyi kayi uudi musue kuamba?"

"Ke tshidi kabidi—muinshi mua tshiibi—kuena umvua—tshifu—tshivua tshiumvuika bu tshifu."

"Tshifu tshikale tshibi! Pitshisha tshibombi tshia kupingana natshi."

"Aku kabidi—aku!—bidi biumvuika bu bantu babidi anyi basatu badi balala badi bakudimuka, mpindieu!"

"Caramba! udi muenze, muena mazuwa, udi mua kuenza anyi? I biscuit bisatu bitoka binudi nudia mu dilolo bidi bienda bishintuluka munda muenu—kakuyi tshikuabu. Tangila mu tshibombi!"

"Amba tshiudi musue, muena mazuwa, ndi ne matshi a buitshi."

"Eyowa, udi muntu, ki mmuomu, uvua mumvue mutoyi wa tshiamu tshia mudimu tshia Quakeress tshia kale mutantshi wa kilometre makumi atanu ku mbuu kumbukila ku Nantucket; udi muntu."

"Seka; netumone tshikala mua kuenzeka. Teleja, Cabaco, kudi muntu kampanda mu tshitudilu tshia panyima pa mazuwa udi kayi muanji kumueneka pa mazuwa; ne ndi ngela meji ne: Mogul wetu wa kale mmumanye bualu kampanda pa bualu ebu pende. Ngakumvua Stubb wambila Flask, dimue dituku mu dinda, ne: kuvua bualu kampanda bua mushindu eu mu lupepele."

“Tish! tshibombi!”





SHAPITA 44. Tshibumba.

Bu wewe mulonde Kapitene Ahaba mu nzubu wende wa tshilala benyi kunyima kua tshipepele tshikole tshivua tshienzeke butuku buakalonda dianyisha dia tshimpitshimpi dia tshipatshila tshiende ne bena mudimu bende, uvua mua kumumona uya mu tshibuta tshia bintu tshia mu tshibuelelu tshia nzubu, ne upatula mukanda munene wa karte ka mbuu ka mubidi wa jaune, uwutangalaja kumpala kuende pa mesa ende avua manyanguke. Pashishe yeye musombe kumpala kuatshi, uvua mua kumumona wenda ulonga milongo mishilashilangane ne mitoto ivuaye umona; ne ne crayon mutekete kadi mushindame, funda mishindu mikuabu pa miaba ivua kumpala kayiyi ne tshintu. Pa tshibidilu, uvua utangila milongo ya mikanda ya kale ivua ku luseke luende, muvuabu bafunde bikondo ne miaba ivuabu bakuate anyi bamone baleine ya kale mu ngendu ya kale ya mazuwa mashilangane.

Patshivuaye wenza mudimu nunku, muendu munene wa tshiamu tshia mitaku uvua muele mu nkanu pa mutu pende, uvua anu wenda unyunguluka ne dienda dia mazuwa, ne uvua wela kashidi nsese ne mundidimbi wa milongo pa mpala wende uvua munyanguke, too ne pavuaye umueneka bu ne: pavuaye yeye nkayende wenda ufunda milongo ne njila pa makala avua manyanguke, uvua kabidi ufunda milongo ne milongo mikuabu ivua mimueneka ne crayon pa kazubu ka mpala wende kadi ne tshimanyinu tshikole.

Kadi ki mbutuku ebu menemene buvua, mu nkayende mu nzubu wende wa tshilala benyi, Ahaba wela meji nunku bua karte kende to. Pabuipi ne butuku buonso bavua babapatula; pabuipi ne butuku buonso bimue bimanyinu bia crayon bivua biumbushibua, ne bikuabu bivua bipingajibua. Bualu ne ma karte a mbuu yonso inayi kumpala kuende, Ahaba uvua wenda mu tshibungubungu tshia misulu ne tshipepele tshikole, ne tshipatshila tshia kukumbaja lungenyi lua muntu nkayende lua anyima wende.

Mpindieu, kudi muntu yonso udi kayi mumanye bimpe njila ya ba-leviathan, bidi mua kumueneka bu mudimu udi kauyi ne ditekemena nunku bua kukeba tshifukibua tshimue tshidi nkayatshi mu mbuu idi kayiyi mibuikila pa buloba ebu. Kadi kabivua bimueneka nanku kudi Ahaba to, bualu uvua mumanye mishindu yonso ya mavuala ne misulu; ne nunku kubala dienda dia biakudia bia baleine wa mabele; ne, kabidi, kuvuluka bikondo bia pa tshibidilu, bijadika bia kumuloba mu latitude kampanda; uvua mua kufika ku meji mimpe, pabuipi ne ku malu malelela, adi atangila dituku dimpe dia kuikala pa buloba ebu anyi abu bua kukeba nyama wende.

Bushuwa, bualu budi butangila mushindu udi baleine wa sperme uya mu mâyi kampanda budi bujadikibue bikole, ke bualu kayi bapote ba bungi badi bela meji ne: badi mua kumutangila bimpe ne kumulonga pa buloba bujima anyi; bikala mikanda ya luendu lumue lua mazuwa onso a baleine misangisha bimpe, dîba adi ngendu ya baleine ya sperm ivua mua kuikala mipetangana ne ya herring-shoals anyi ya nyunyi ya swallows. Pa mwanda utala bualu ebu, mbatete bua kuenza karte milenga ya dienda dia baleine wa mabele.*

*Bu mudi malu adi kulu aa mafunda, meyi aa adi ne disanka dia bungi
mu mukanda wa mbulamatadi, mupatula kudi Lieutenant Maury, wa
nzubu wa tshikebelu wa ditunga, mu Washington, mu dia matuku 16 ngondo muinayi 1851. Kudi

ya degre itanu ya latitude ne degre itanu ya longitude;
mu tshitupa tshionso tshidi ne bitupa dikumi ne bibidi

wa matuku adibu bapitshishe mu ngondo yonso mu
distrike, ne mikuabu ibidi bua kuleja bungi bua matuku mu
baleine, mabele anyi dia balume, badi bamone.”

Kabidi, padibu benza luendu kumbukila muaba kampanda wa kudiila batangile ku mukuabu, baleine ya mabele, balombodibua kudi lungenyi kampanda ludi kaluyi mua kunyanguka—tuambe ne: meji masokoka a kudi Nzambi—atu atamba kuowa mâyi mu mijilu , mudibu bababikila; batungunuka ne njila wabu mu mulongo wa mbuu kampanda ne bujalame budi kabuyi bushintuluka, bua se: kakuvua buatu nansha bumue buvua buende mu njila wabu, bilondeshile karte nansha kamue, ne tshia dikumi tshia bujalame bua dikema nunku to. Nansha mudi, mu nsombelu eyi, njila udi baleine umue wangata muikale mululame bu mudi muena mudimu wa tshipiminu, ne nansha mudi mulongo wa kuya kumpala muikale mushikila anu ku tshibandilu tshiende tshidi katshiyi mua kuepuka, tshilulame, kadi mushipa wa tshianana udibu bamba ne: udi wela mâyi mu bikondo ebi, utu utamba kuangata kilometre mikese mu butshiama (more anyi mikese, bu mudibu bela meji ne: mushipa udi mua kualabaja anyi mualabale); kadi kena upita tshimuenekelu tshia ku mitu ya mazuwa a baleine, padiye wenda unyunguluka mu tshitupa etshi tshia majimbu to. Mu tshikoso, mu bikondo kampanda bia mu bulampe abu ne mu njila au, badi mua kukeba ne dishindika dionso baleine badi bendakana.

Ne nunku ki nganu mu bikondo bijadika, pa miaba ya kudiila misunguluke mimanyike bimpe, ke muvua Ahaba mua kutekemena bua kusambakena ne nyama wende to; kadi mu kusabuka miaba ya bungi ya mayi pankatshi pa maloba au uvua mua, ku diambuluisha dia mudimu wende, muaba wende ne dîba diende mu njila wende, bua nansha nanku kayi mua kuikala kayi ne ditekemena dia dipetangana to.

Kuvua bualu buvua bumueneka ku mêsu bu buvua bumupangisha bua kuenza malu avua kaayi makane kadi avua anu malelela. Kadi ki mmuomu mu bulelela to, pamuapa. Nansha mudi baleine ya sperm gregarious ne bikondo biayi bia pa tshibidilu bia miaba kampanda, kadi mu tshibungi kuena mua kuamba ne: bisumbu bia baleine bivua bienda mu tshitupa tshia latitude anyi tshia longitude tshidimu etshi, tuambe ne: nebilue bia muomumue ne bivuabu bapete muaba au tshikondo tshishale to; nansha mudiku bilejilu bia pa buabi ne bidi kabiyi mua kuela mpata mudi malu adi mashilangane ne aa malelela. Mu tshikoso, mêyi a muomumue, anu mu tshitupa tshikese, adi atangila bantu badi nkayabu ne badi kabayi basombela munkatshi mua baleine ya sperm mikole ne mikulakaje. Nunku nansha muvua Moby Dick mu tshidimu tshishale mumoneke, tshilejilu, pa tshidibu babikila ne: buloba bua Seychelle mu mbuu wa Inde, anyi Volcano Bay ku muelelu wa mâyi wa Japon; kadi kabivua bilonda, ne: bu Pequod mua kuya kutangila umue wa ku miaba ayi mu tshikondo tshionso tshivua tshilonda, uvua mua kumusambakenamu kakuyi mpata. Ke mudibi kabidi bua miaba mikuabu ya kudiila, muvuaye imue misangu mudisokolole. Kadi bionso ebi bivua bimueneka anu bu miaba yende ya kuimana ne nzubu ya tshilala benyi ya mu mbuu, mu mushindu kampanda, ki mmiaba yende ya kusombela mutantshi mule to. Ne muaba udibu bakula bua mushindu uvua Ahaba mua kukumbaja tshipatshila tshiende too ne katataka, mbakule anu bua mushindu kayi wonso uvuaye mua kuenza, kumpala kua dîba anyi muaba kampanda, pavua malu onso avua mua kuenzeka mua kulua malu avua mua kuenzeka, ne, anu muvua Ahaba wela meji ne disanka dionso, malu onso avua mua kuenzeka avua mua kulua tshintu tshivua tshilonda panyima pa bualu kampanda buvua mua kuenzeka. Dîba ne muaba au bivua bisangisha mu tshiambilu tshimue tshia malu a tekiniki—tshikondo tshia pa mulongo. Bua kuntu aku ne pashishe, munkatshi mua bidimu bia bungi bilondangane, bavua bakula bua Moby Dick misangu ne misangu, ushala mu mâyi au bua tshikondo kampanda, bu mudi dîba, mu tshijengu tshiadi tshia tshidimu tshionso, dienda dienda mu tshikondo kampanda tshidibu bamanyishe mu tshimanyinu tshionso tshia Zodiac. Muaba au ke muvua kabidi malu a bungi a lufu avua menzeke ne baleine mutoke; kuntu aku mavuala avua mafunda ne bienzedi biende; kuvua kabidi muaba wa kanyinganyinga uvua muntu mununu wa monomaniac mupete tshipatshila tshibi tshia kudisombuela. Kadi mu budimu ne budimu budi Ahaba muele anyima wende udi wela meji mu diloba edi didi kadiyi dinyanguka, kavua mua kudilekela bua kuimanyika ditekemena diende dionso pa bualu bumuepele budibu batele kulu eku, nansha biobi mua kuikala bilenga ku ditekemena adi; nansha mu dipanga tulu dia mutshipu wende kavua mua kutalaja mutshima wende uvua kauyi mupole bua kupingaja dikeba dionso divuaye mua kuenza to.

Mpindieu, Pequod uvua mupatuke mu Nantucket ku ntuadijilu kua tshikondo tshia mudimu. Kakuena bualu nansha bumue budi mua kuenzeka buvua mua kuambuluisha komanda wende bua kuenza luendu lunene lua ku Sud, kupingana misangu ibidi ku Cape Horn, ne pashishe kunyema panshi makumi asambombo a degre ya latitude bua kufika mu Pacifique wa ku equateur mu tshikondo tshia kuya kuntu aku. Nunku, udi ne tshia kuindila tshikondo tshidi tshilonda. Kadi dîba dia kumpala dia dienda dia mazuwa a Pequod divua, pamuapa, disungudibua bimpe kudi Ahaba, ne lungenyi lua tshimuenekelu tshia malu etshi. Bualu bua, tshitupa tshia matuku ne munya nkama isatu ne makumi asambombo ne atanu tshivua kumpala kuende; tshikondo tshivuaye, pamutu pa kuikala ne lutulu luonso ku muelelu wa mâyi, uvua mua kupitshisha mu diloba dia mishindu ne mishindu; Shi ku mpukapuka, baleine mutoke, mupitshishe dikisha diende mu mbuu kule ne miaba yende ya kudiila, udi mua kuleja mpala wende munyanguke ku Golfe wa Perse, anyi mu Mbuu wa Bengal, anyi mu Mbuu wa Chine, anyi mu mâyi makuabu adibu bakengesha kudi tshisamba tshiende. Nunku, Monsoons, Pampas, Nor'-Westers, Harmattans, Trades; lupepele luonso pa kumbusha lua Levanter ne Simoon, ludi mua kuela Moby Dick mu tshijengu tshia buloba tshia zig-zag tshia tshijengu tshia Pequod tshidi tshienda tshinyunguluka.

Kadi kuanyisha bionso ebi; kadi, kutangila ne budimu ne bupole, bidi bimueneka ne: ki mbualu bua bupote to, bualu ebu; ne mu mbuu mualabale udi kayi ne mikalu, baleine umue udi nkayende, nansha bobu bamupeta, udi ne bua kuela meji ne: udi mua kumanyibua kudi mulobi wende, nansha bu Mufti wa ndevu mitoke mu njila minene ya mu Constantinople idi miule tente ne bantu anyi? Eyowa. Bualu mpala mutoke bu neje wa Moby Dick, ne tshitupa tshiende tshitoke bu neje, kabivua mua kuikala kabiyi biumvuika to. Ne tshiena mubale baleine anyi, Ahaba uvua wela mbila munda muende, bu muvuaye mumane kubala mikanda yende too ne mutantshi mule panyima pa minuit uvua udiela tshianyima mu bilota—mubala, ne uvuaku mua kunyema anyi? Mapapa ende manene mmanyanguke, ne mmapatuke bu ditshi dia mukoko mujimine! Ne apa, lungenyi luende lua bupote luvua mua kunyema mu lubilu lukole; too ne pavua ditshioka ne dipanga dia lungenyi biakamukuata; ne mu lupepele lua pa mutu pa mazuwa uvua ukeba bua kupetulula bukole buende. Ah, Nzambi! ntshinyi tshia makenga tshidi muntu au utantamena udi muwule tente ne dijinga dimue dia kudisombuela didi kadiyi dikumbane. Udi ulala ne bianza bikanga; ne udi ujuka ne misumadi yende ya mashi mu bianza biende.

Misangu ya bungi, pavuaye muenzejibue bua kupatuka mu tshitanda tshiende kudi bilota bia butuku bivua bitshiokesha ne bivua kabiyi mua kuitaba, bivua, bipingaja meji ende makole mu munya, bivua bienda nabi munkatshi mua dikokangana dia phrensies, ne bivua bienda binyunguluka mu buongo buende buvua buenza kapia, too ne pavua dikuma dia muaba wende wa muoyo dilua tshiji tshikole; ne pavua, bu muvuabi bienzeka imue misangu, ntatu ya mu nyuma eyi ivua munda muende mimubandishe ku tshishimikidi tshiayi, ne tshina tshivua tshimueneka tshiunzuluka munda muende, muvua lubimbi lua kapia ne mipenyi bipatuka, ne bademon bavua ne mulawu bavua bamulomba bua kutuluka munkatshi muabu; pavua inferno eu munda muende muele mbila muinshi muende, muadi wa tshisuku uvua mua kumvuika mu buatu; ne mêsu avua akenka Ahaba uvua upatuka mu nzubu wende wa tshilala benyi, bienze anu bu uvua unyema ku bulalu buvua buosha kapia. Kadi ebi, pamuapa, pamutu pa kuikala bimanyinu bidi kabiyi mua kukanda bia butekete kampanda busokoka, anyi buôwa bua dipangadika diende nkayende, bivua anu bimanyinu bia bukole buabu. Bualu, mu bikondo bia nunku, Ahaba mutshimbakane, muena tshinyangu, mulobi wa baleine mutoke; Ahaba eu uvua muye ku hamac wende, kavua muena mudimu uvua mumufikishe ku dipatuka kabidi ne buôwa to. Tshia ndekelu tshivua tshilele tshia tshiendelele, tshidi ne muoyo anyi anyima munda muende; ne mu tulu, muikale bua tshikondo kampanda mutapuluke ne lungenyi lua ngikadilu, luvua misangu mikuabu luenza mudimu bu tshintu tshia pambelu anyi muena mudimu, uvua ukeba kunyema ku mpukapuka ku dipetangana dikole dia tshintu tshia tshikisu, tshivua, bua tshikondo atshi, katshiyi kabidi tshitupa tshiatshi to. Kadi bu mudi lungenyi kaluyiku padilu kaluyi lupetangana ne anyima, nunku bidi ne bua kuikala nanku, mu tshilumbu tshia Ahaba, uvua mufile meji ende onso ne majinga ende onso ku tshipatshila tshiende tshimuepele tshinene; tshipatshila atshi, ku diambuluisha dia disua diatshi nkayatshi, tshiakadienzeja bua kuluisha nzambi ne bademon mu mushindu wa muntu udi udiangata nkayende, udi udikokesha nkayende. Tòo, uvua mua kuikala ne muoyo ne kuosha, eku bukole bua bantu bonso buvuaye musangishe nabu, bunyema ne buôwa bua diledibua divua kadiyi dilomba ne kadiyi ne tatu. Nunku, nyuma mukengeshibue uvua umueneka mu mêsu a mubidi, pavua tshivua tshimueneka bu Ahaba mupatuke lubilu mu nzubu muende, tshivua bua tshikondo atshi anu tshintu tshivua katshiyi ne tshintu, tshintu tshivua katshiyi ne tshimuenekelu tshivua tshilala tulu, mutoyi wa butoke bua muoyo, bua kuikala bushuwa, kadi kayi ne tshintu tshia kuela mekala, ne ke bualu kayi tshivua tshitupu munda muende. Nzambi akuambuluishe, muntu mununu, meji ebe mmafuke tshifukibua munda muebe; ne yeye udi lungenyi luende lukole lumuvuija nunku Promethée; nyunyi wa nkutshi udi udia mutshima au kashidi; tshifukibua atshi ntshifukibua tshidiye ufuka.





SHAPITA 45. Mukanda wa tshipungidi.

Pa bidi bitangila tshidi mua kuikala tshia muyuki mu mukanda ewu; ne, bushuwa, bu mudibi bilenga mu mushindu udi kauyi mujalame bualu bumue anyi bibidi bia dikema ne bia dikema mu bibidilu bia baleine ya sperme, nshapita udi kumpala eku, mu tshitupa tshiende tshia kumpala, udi ne mushinga wa bungi bu mudibi mua kupeteka mu mukanda eu; kadi bualu bua kumpala budi bulomba bua kuikalabu bavudijibue bikole ne bumanyike bimpe, bua kubumvua bimpe, ne kabidi bua kumbusha dipanga dia kuitabuja dionso didi dipanga dia lungenyi lua bualu buonso mua kufikisha ku lungenyi lua bamue, pa bidi bitangila bulelela bua tshilelela bua malu manene a bualu ebu.

Ndi musue kubenga kuenza tshitupa etshi tshia mudimu wanyi mu mushindu mulongolola; kadi neikale ne disanka dia kupatula lungenyi ludibu basue ku diambuluisha dia bilejilu bia pa nkaya bia bintu, bidi bimanyike anyi bia kueyemena kudi meme bu muena baleine; ne ku bilejilu ebi, ndi ngangata—dijikija dia bualu nedilonde nkayadi.

Tshia kumpala: Meme nkayanyi ndi mumanye misangu isatu idi baleine, kunyima kua yeye mumane kupeta tshingoma, mumane kunyema; ne, kunyima kua tshikondo kampanda (mu tshikondo tshimue tshia bidimu bisatu), mmukuate kabidi ku tshianza tshimue atshi, ne mmushipe; padibu bumbusha biamu bibidi, bionso bibidi bifunda ne tshimanyishilu tshimue tshia pa nkaya, mu mubidi. Mu tshilumbu tshivua bidimu bisatu bipita pankatshi pa diela dia bingoma bibidi; ne ndi ngela meji ne bidi mua kuikala bualu bukuabu bupite apu; muntu uvua mubatute, mu tshikondo atshi, bua kuya mu mazuwa a ngendu mu luendu lua kuya mu Afrike, wakaya ku muelelu wa mâyi kuntu aku, wakabuela mu tshisumbu tshia bantu bavua bapeta bintu, ne wakabuela kule menemene munda mua ditunga, muvuaye muenze luendu munkatshi mua bidimu bitue ku bibidi, misangu ya bungi uvua mu njiwu kudi nyoka, nyama ya luonji, ntambue, miasmason ya lufu, ne malu makuabu onso avua menzeke mu mutshima wa diendakana bitupa bidi kabiyi bimanyike. Mu tshikondo atshi, baleine uvuaye mukuate uvua ne bua kuikala pende mu luendu luende; kakuyi mpata, uvua munyunguluke buloba bujima misangu isatu, wenda unyunguluka ne nseke yonso ya mu Afrike; kadi tshianana. Muntu eu ne baleine eu bakalua kabidi pamue, ne umue wakatshimuna mukuabu. Ndi ngamba ne, meme nkayanyi, ndi mumanye bilejilu bisatu bia muomumue ne ebi; ke mu bibidi bia kudibi mvua mumone baleine mikuma; ne, pa dibunda dibidi, wakamona biamu bibidi bivua ne bimanyinu bivuabu bakose munda muabi, pashishe bivuabu bangate ku mushipa uvua mufue. Mu tshikondu tshia bidimu bisatu, biakamueneka bu ne: mvua mu buatu misangu yonso ibidi, wa kumpala ne wa ndekelu, ne musangu wa ndekelu ngakajingulula bimpe tshintu tshinene tshia pa buatshi tshivua muinshi mua dîsu dia baleine, tshimvua mumone kuntu aku bidimu bisatu bishale ebi. Ndi ngamba ne: bidimu bisatu, kadi ndi mujadike ne: bivua bipite apu. Mona bilejilu bisatu, nunku, bindi meme nkayanyi mumanye bulelela buabi; kadi ndi mumvue bilejilu bikuabu bia bungi kudi bantu badi bulelela buabu mu bualu ebu kabuyi ne tshijadiki tshimpe tshia kubafunda.
