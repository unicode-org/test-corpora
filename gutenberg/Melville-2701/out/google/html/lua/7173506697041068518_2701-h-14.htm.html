SHAPITA 67. Kukosa.

Bivua mu Disambombo butuku, ne Nsabatu wa nunku wakalonda! Balongeshi ba ex officio ba dishipa dia Nsabatu bonso badi baleine. Pequod wa meno a ntambue wakalua tshintu tshivua tshimueneka bu tshinyangu; muena mazuwa yonso mushipianganyi. Uvua mua kuikala wela meji ne: tuvua tufila ngombe mikunze binunu dikumi kudi nzambi ya mbuu.

Mu muaba wa kumpala, bintu binene bia kutema nabi, munkatshi mua bintu bikuabu bia bujitu bidi ne tshisumbu tshia mabue adibu batamba kuenza ne mvinyo wa vert, ne adi muntu nansha umue kayi mua kubandisha—tshisumbu tshinene tshia tumuma tua mvinyo etshi tshivua tshibandishibua too ne ku mutu kua tshibandilu tshinene ne tshilamakaja bikole ku mutu wa tshibandilu tshia kuinshi, muaba wa bukole menemene pa mutu pa mazuwa. Ku ndekelu kua tshingoma tshia hawser tshidi tshienda tshipita mu bintu ebi, pashishe tshivua tshilombodibua ku windlass, ne tshitupa tshinene tshia kuinshi tshia biamu bia mvita tshivua tshinyunguluka pa mutu pa baleine; ku tshibumba etshi bavua balamakaja tshingoma tshinene tshia blubber, tshivua ne bujitu bua kilo lukama. Ne mpindieu bavua balengeja mu bitupa bitupa ku luseke, Starbuck ne Stubb, balunda, bikale ne bingoma biabu bile, bakatuadija kukosa tshina mu mubidi bua kubueja tshingoma anu pa mutu pa mapapa abidi adi pabuipi. Padibu bamane kuenza nanku, mulongo mualabale wa tshitupa tshia tshijengu udi unyunguluka tshina, tshingoma tshidi tshibueja, ne tshisumbu tshinene tshia bena mudimu tshidi tshienza mutoyi wa tshikisu, mpindieu tshidi tshituadija kuela mbila mu tshisumbu tshimue tshia bantu ku tshiamu tshia nzembu. Diakamue, mazuwa onso adi apingana ku luseke luende; tshintu tshionso tshidi munda muende tshidi tshituadija bu misonso ya nzubu wa kale mu tshikondo tshia mashika; udi uteketa, unyunguluka, ne utua mitu yende ya buôwa muulu. Udi wenda unyemena kudi baleine, eku tshiona tshionso tshia windlass tshiandamuna kudi tshiona tshia diambuluisha tshidi tshifumina ku mavuala a mâyi; too ne ku ndekelu, dîyi dia lukasa, dia dikema didi diumvuika; ne tshipepele tshikole buatu budi buenda buenda bubanda ne bupingana panyima pa baleine, ne tshiamu tshia mvita tshidi tshijuka mu tshimuenekelu tshikoka panyima patshi tshitupa tshia tshijengu tshia tshitupa tshia kumpala tshia blubber. Mpindieu bu mudi mafuta a baleine abuikila baleine anu bu mudi tshikoba tshibuikila orange, ke mudibi biumbushibua ku mubidi anu bu mudi orange imue misangu umbushibua padibu bamuvuija mu tshijengu. Bualu bukole budibu balama kudi tshiamu tshia nzembu budi buenza bua baleine ikale anu wenda unyunguluka mu mayi, ne bu mudi mafuta adi mu tshitupa tshimue apatuka mu mushindu umue bilondeshile mulongo udibu babikila ne: “tshilamba,” tshikosa musangu umue kudi bipese bia Starbuck ne Stubb, bena dibaka; ne anu bu mudibi bipatuka lukasa, ne bushuwa ku tshienzedi atshi nkayatshi, bidi anu bibandishibua muulu ne muulu too ne padi ndekelu wende wa kuulu udisha tshitupa tshinene; bantu badi ku tshiamu tshia nzembu badi balekela kuela mbila, ne bua tshitupa tshîpi anyi bibidi tshintu tshinene tshidi tshitoka mashi tshidi tshienda tshinyunguluka bu tshidi tshipueka mu diulu, ne muntu yonso udiku udi ne bua kuikala ne budimu bua kutshiepuka paditshi tshienda tshinyunguluka, bikalabi kabiyi nanku tshidi mua kumukuma ku matshi ne kumuela mutu panshi mu mâyi.

Umue wa ku bena tshingoma udi uya kumpala ne tshingoma tshile ne tshikole tshidibu babikila ne: boarding-sword, ne udi utangila mushindu wende udi ukosa ne budimu buonso tshina tshinene mu tshitupa tshia kuinshi tshia tshintu tshidi tshienda tshinyunguluka. Mu tshina etshi, ndekelu wa tshintu tshibidi tshidi tshishintuluka tshidi pashishe tshikuata bua kulama tshintu tshidi tshikuata mafuta, bua kudilongolola bua tshidi tshilonda. Pashishe, muena mvita eu mupiluke, udimuija bianza bionso bua kuimana, udi wenza kabidi lubilu lua malu a sianse ku tshibumba, ne ne bipese bikese bia ku luseke, bia dipanga ditekemena, bia dikuma, udi utshikosolola mu bitupa bibidi; nunku padi tshitupa tshipi tshia kuinshi tshitshidi tshienda lubilu, tshitupa tshile tshia kuulu, tshidibu babikila ne: tshitupa tshia tshilamba, tshidi tshinyunguluka bimpe, ne tshidi tshilongolola bua kupueka. Bantu badi baya kumpala badi mpindieu batuadija kuimba misambu yabu, ne padi tshintu tshimue tshienda tshikosa ne tshibandisha tshitupa tshibidi tshia baleine, tshikuabu tshidi tshipueka bitekete bitekete, ne tshitupa tshia kumpala tshidi tshipueka kupitshila ku tshibuelelu tshinene tshidi anu kuinshi, mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi udi kayi ne bintu bidibu babikila ne: blubber-room. Mu nzubu ewu wa mu dilolo, bianza bia mishindu ne mishindu bidi bitungunuka ne kuela tshilamba tshile tshia kulala bienze anu bu ne: ntshisumbu tshinene tshia nyoka miluka. Ne nunku mudimu udi wenda; bintu bibidi bidi bibandisha ne bipuekesha musangu umua; baleine ne windlass bidi bienda bikola, ba heavers bimba, bantu ba mu nzubu wa blubber badi banyunguluka, bena mudimu badi badila, buatu budi buenda bukola, ne bianza bionso bidi bitshipa imue misangu, bua kujikija bilumbu bia bantu bonso.





SHAPITA 68. Tshilamba.

Ndi mufile ntema mikese ku tshiena bualu etshi tshidi katshiyi tshitamba kunyingalaja, dikoba dia baleine. Ndi mupeta bilumbu pa bidi bitangila bualu ebu ne bamanyi ba baleine badi pa mâyi, ne bamanyi ba malu a bufuki badi ku muelelu wa mâyi. Lungenyi luanyi lua ntuadijilu kaluena lushintuluke to; kadi mmuenenu patupu.

Lukonku ludi ne, dikoba dia baleine didi tshinyi ne penyi? Ukadi mumanye tshidi mafuta ende. Mafuta aa adi amueneka bu nyama wa ngombe mukole, udi ne ntete mikole, kadi udi mukole, udi ne bukole bua bungi ne udi mukole, ne udi ne bunene bua santimetre muanda mukulu anyi dikumi too ne ku dikumi ne ibidi ne dikumi ne itanu.

Mpindieu, nansha mudibi mua kumueneka bu bualu bua tshianana bua kuakula bua dikoba dia tshifukibua kayi tshionso bu didi ne bujalame ne bunene bua mushindu eu, kadi mu bulelela ebi kabiena bijadiki bidi bibengangana ne lungenyi lua nunku to; bualu kuena mua kubandisha tshitupa tshikuabu tshikole tshidi tshibuikila mubidi wa baleine to, anu mafuta a muomumue; ne tshitupa tshia pambelu tshidi tshibuikila nyama yonso, bikalatshi tshikole, tshidi mua kuikala tshinyi pa kumbusha dikoba? Bushuwa, ku tshitalu tshia baleine tshidi katshiyi tshinyanguke, udi mua kukosa ne tshianza tshiebe tshintu tshitekete tshidi katshiyi ne ndekelu, tshidi tshimueneka patoke, tshidi tshifuanangane ne bitupa bitekete bia isinglass, anu tshidi anu tshipepele ne tshitekete bu satin; mbuena kuamba ne, kumpala kua kuyumibua, padibi kabiyi anu bikola ne bikola, kadi bilua bikole ne binyanguka. Ndi ne bintu biume bia bungi, bindi ngenza nabi mudimu bua kuela bimanyinu mu mikanda yanyi ya baleine. Bidi bimueneka patoke, anu mumvua muambe kumpala; ne bu mudibu bateka pa dibeji difunda, imue misangu mvua ndisankisha meme nkayanyi ne kuela meji ne: bivua ne bukole bua bungi. Nansha biobikala mushindu kayi, bidi bisankisha bua kubala malu a baleine ku diambuluisha dia bintu biabu bia kumona nabi, anu muudi mua kuamba. Kadi tshindi ngamba apa ntshia etshi. Tshintu tshimue tshimue tshidi katshiyi ne ndekelu, tshia isinglass, tshidi, ndi ngitaba, tshibueja mubidi mujima wa baleine, katshiena tshiangatshibua bu dikoba dia tshifukibua, bu dikoba dia dikoba, mu mushindu kampanda; bualu bivua anu bia bupote bua kuamba ne: dikoba diakane dia baleine munene didi ditekete ne dimpe kupita dikoba dia muana utshidi muledibue. Kadi kakuyi kabidi bualu ebu to.

Kuela meji ne: mafuta adi dikoba dia baleine; pashishe, padi kikoba etshi, bu mudibi bua baleine munene menemene, netshipatule tshitupa tshinene tshia milondo lukama ya mafuta; ne, padibu bamona ne, mu bungi, anyi mu bujitu, mafuta au, mu nsombelu wende udibu baleje, adi anu bitupa bisatu bia binayi, kadi ki ntshintu tshijima tshia tshilamba to; ke bualu kayi tudi mua kuikala ne lungenyi kampanda lua bunene bua tshintu tshidi ne muoyo atshi, tshitupa tshikese tshia mubidi watshi tshidi tshipatula dijiba dia mâyi bu mudi adi. Pa kubala milondo dikumi ku tone, udi ne tone dikumi bua bujitu bua tshitupa tshisatu tshia bintu bia dikoba dia baleine.

Mu nsombelu, tshitupa tshidi tshimueneka tshia Baleine wa Sperm ki ntshikese munkatshi mua bintu bia dikema bidiye uleja to. Pabuipi ne misangu yonso, udi ne bimanyinu bia bungi bia bululame mu mulongolongo munene, tshintu tshimue tshimue tshidi mu milongo milenga ya mu Italie. Kadi bimanyinu ebi kabiena bimueneka bifunda pa tshintu tshia isinglass tshitudi batele kulu eku to, kadi bidi bimueneka bu bidi bimueneka kupitshila kutshi, bienze anu bu ne: mbifunda pa mubidi nkayawu. Kabidi ki mbualu ebu buonso to. Mu imue misangu, kudi dîsu dia lukasa, dia kutangila, bimanyinu abi bia mulongolongo, bu mudibi mu difunda dilelela, kadi bidi bifila nshindamenu wa mifundu mikuabu ya bungi. Bino bidi na hieroglyphes; mbuena kuamba ne, shi udi ubikila maleta adi kaayi umvuika adi pa bimanu bia bimanu ne: hieroglyphes, dîba adi ke muaku muimpe wa kuenza nawu mudimu mu dipetangana dia mpindieu. Ku diambuluisha dia tshivulukilu tshianyi tshia maleta a hieroglyphes pa Sperm Whale kampanda, ngakakema bikole bua dibeji divua dileja maleta a kale a bena Inde avuabu bafunde pa bimanu bia hieroglyphes bia lumu bivua ku muelelu wa Upper Mississippi. Anu bu mabue aa a tshishima, kabidi, baleine udi ne tshimanyinu tshia tshishima udi ushala kayi mua kumvuika. Diumvuija edi dia mabue a mu Inde didi dimvuluija bualu bukuabu. Pa kumbusha bintu bikuabu bionso bidi pambelu pa Sperm Whale uleja, katu utamba kuleja nyima, ne nangananga nseke yende, mijimine mu tshitupa tshinene tshia tshimuenekelu tshia mulongolongo wa pa tshibidilu, bua mputa ya bungi mibi, yonso ya tshitupa tshidi katshiyi tshia pa tshibidilu, tshia mpukapuka. Ndi ne tshia kuamba ne: mabue a mu Nouvelle-Angleterre adi ku muelelu wa mbuu, adi Agassiz wela meji ne: adi ne bimanyinu bia dikuma dia tshikisu ne mabue a mashika manene adi apueka pa mâyi—ndi ne bua kuamba ne: mabue au kaena ne bua kufuanangana ne Sperm Whale mu bualu ebu to. Bidi bimueneka kabidi kundi ne: milonda ya nunku idi mu baleine idi mua kuikala mienzeke bua dipetangana dia lukuna ne baleine mikuabu; bualu ndi mubamone bikole mu ngombe minene, mikole ya mishindu eyi.

Dîyi dimue anyi abidi makuabu adi atangila bualu ebu bua dikoba anyi mafuta a baleine. Bakadi bamba ne, badi bamumbusha mu bitupa bile, bidibu babikila ne: bitupa bia tshilamba. Anu bu miaku ya bungi ya mu mbuu, eu udi ne disanka dia bungi ne udi ne mushinga. Bualu baleine udi bushuwa mubuikila mu mafuta ende bu mu tshilamba tshia bushuwa anyi mu tshilamba tshia pa mutu; anyi, bitambe buimpe, poncho wa bena Inde uvua mubuele pa mutu pende, ne mubuele ku ndekelu kuende. Bua dibuikila dia mubidi wende edi, ke bualu kayi baleine udi mua kudilama bimpe mu bikondo bionso, mu mbuu yonso, mu bikondo bionso ne mu mavuala onso. Ntshinyi tshidi mua kuenzeka bua baleine wa mu Greenland, tuambe ne: mu mbuu ya mashika ya ku Nord, yeye kayi ne tshiakudia tshiende tshimpe? Bushuwa, mishipa mikuabu idi mipeta lubilu lua bungi mu mayi a Hyperborean; kadi aba, ikala mumone, i mishipa yebe ya mashi mashika, kayiyi ne mifuba, idi mifuba yayi mikale bu frigo; bifukibua, bidi bidiosha muinshi mua tshibandabanda tshia mabue a mashika, bu mudi ngenda-mushinga mu muvu wa mashika mua kuowa kumpala kua kapia ka mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi; kadi, anu bu muntu, baleine udi ne mifuba ne mashi a kapia. Ela mashi ende mu mashika, ne udi ufua. Mbimpe be nunku—pa kumbusha kunyima kua diumvuija—ne tshintu tshinene etshi, tshidi ne luya lua mubidi ludi ne mushinga wa bungi anu bu mudilu kudi muntu; mbimpe be bua yeye kumupeta kumbelu, muina ku mishiku yende bua kashidi mu mayi a mu Arctique! muaba udi, padi bena mazuwa bakuluka mu buatu, batu babapeta imue misangu, ngondo ya bungi pashishe, bamane kuikala mu mashika mu mitshima ya madimi a neje, anu mudibu bapeta nyunyi mulamakaja mu ambre. Kadi bualu bua dikema budi ne: kumanya, anu mudibi bileja kudi mateta, ne: mashi a baleine wa mu Polaire adi ne kapia kupita a baleine wa mu Bornéo mu muvu wa été.

Bidi bimueneka kundi, ne muaba eu tudi tumona buimpe bua patupu bua bukole bua muntu nkayende, ne buimpe bua patupu bua bimanu binene, ne buimpe bua patupu bua bunene bua munda. Oh, muntu! tuanyisha ne wikale tshilejilu tshia baleine! Wewe pebe, ikala ne luya munkatshi mua mabue a mashika. Wewe pebe, udi ne muoyo pa buloba ebu kuyi wa pa buloba ebu. Ikala mupole ku equateur; ikala ne mashi eba ku Pole. Bu tshibumba tshinene tshia Saint Peter, ne bu baleine munene, ikala, muntu! mu bikondo bionso luya luebe nkayebe.

Kadi mbipepele be bua kulongesha malu mimpe aa ne kakuena ditekemena nansha dikese! Pa bidi bitangila dibaka, mmunyi mudibi bikese bidi ne tshibumba bu tshia Saint-Pierre! bia bifukibua, bidi bikese menemene bu baleine!





SHAPITA 69. Dijika dia mufue.

“Kokayi nkanu, lekelayi tshitalu tshiye ku nyima!”

Ba tackles banene mbafike mpindieu ku dienza mudimu wabu. Mubidi mutoke wa baleine mukosa mutu udi ukenka bu lukita lua mabue a marbre; nansha mudiye mushintuluke mu dikala, ki mmujimije tshintu nansha tshimue mu bungi to. Utshidi anu munene. Bitekete bitekete udi wenda unyema kule, mayi adi mamunyunguluke adi matapuluka ne apuekeshibua kudi nyunyi ya requin idi kayiyi mikute, ne luya lua mulu ludi lubungamija kudi nyunyi ya nyunyi idi wela mbila, idi mishiku yayi mikale anu bu nyunyi ya bungi idi ipepeja mu baleine. Tshintu tshinene tshitoka tshidi katshiyi ne mutu tshidi tshienda tshienda tshienda tshipita kule ne mazuwa, ne mutshi wonso uditshi tshienda tshienda, tshidi tshimueneka bu tshia nsongo ya requin ne tshia nsongo ya nyunyi, tshidi tshivudija mutoyi wa lufu. Bua mêba ne mêba a bungi ku buatu budi pabuipi ne kuimana, tshimuenekelu tshibi etshi tshidi tshimueneka. Muinshi mua diulu dia azure didi kadiyi ne matutu ne dimpe, pa mpala muimpe wa mbuu muimpe, mupuekesha kudi lupepele lua disanka, tshisumbu tshinene tshia lufu tshidi tshienda tshienda tshienda, too ne paditshi tshijimina mu miaba idi kayiyi ne ndekelu.

Kudi lufu lua kanyinganyinga ne lua kuseka lua bungi! Nyunyi ya mu mbuu yonso mu madilu a tshitendelelu, nyunyi ya muulu yonso mu bilamba bifiike anyi bia mabala. Mu nsombelu kadi bakese ba kudibu bavua mua kuambuluisha baleine, meme ween, bu yeye mua kuikala nabi dijinga; kadi pa tshibilu tshia madilu ende badi balua ne lukunukunu luonso. Oh, tshinyangu tshibi tshia pa buloba! kudi baleine mutambe bukole udi kayi ne budikadidi.

Kabidi, etshi ki nshikidilu to. Nansha mudi mubidi munyanguke, nyuma wa disombuela udi upanduka ne udi ubuela pa mutu pawu bua kutshinyisha. Mutangidibue kudi muntu kampanda wa mvita udi ne buôwa anyi mazuwa a dikebulula adi enda anyanguka ku bule, padi ntanta udi usokoka nyunyi idi mibumbakane, nansha nanku udi anu uleja tshintu tshitoke tshidi tshienda tshibuela mu dîba, ne tshintu tshitoke tshidi tshienda tshibandila; diakamue tshitalu tshia baleine tshidi katshiyi tshienza bibi, ne minu idi iteketa tshidi tshiteka panshi mu tshisuku— mikuna, mabue, ne bikunyibua bidi pabuipi apu: nudimuke! Ne bidimu bia bungi pashishe, pamuapa, mazuwa adi apueka muaba au; kutuluka pa mutu pawu bu mudi mikoko ya bupote ituluka pa mutu pa tshintu tshidi katshiyi ne tshintu, bualu mulombodi wabu uvua ututa kuntu aku ku ntuadijilu pavuabu bakuate mutshi. Kudi mikenji yebe ya malu a kale; kudi mudimu webe wa bilele; kudi muyuki wa dipanduka diebe dia malu a kale adi kaayi mafike pa buloba, ne mpindieu kaayi nansha apueka mu lupepele to! Kudi orthodoxie!

Nunku, nansha mudi mubidi wa baleine munene mua kuikala tshintu tshia buôwa kudi baluishi bende, mu lufu luende nyuma wende udi ulua buôwa budi kabuyi ne bukole kudi ba pa buloba.

Udi witabuja nyuma anyi, mulunda wanyi? Kudi nyuma mikuabu pa kumbusha ya Cock-Lane, ne bantu ba muinshi mua buloba kupita Docteur Johnson badi bayitaba.





SHAPITA 70. Sphynx.

Kabivua ne bua kuikala bipua muoyo ne: kumpala kua kumbusha tshitalu tshia leviyatana tshijima, bakamukosa mutu to. Mpindieu, dikosa dia mutu dia baleine wa mabele ntshienzedi tshia malu a sianse, tshidi baminganga ba baleine bapiluke baditumbisha nabu bikole: ne ki mbua bualu to.

Ela meji ne: baleine kena ne tshintu tshidi mua kubikila bimpe ne: nshingu to; kadi, muaba udi mutu wende ne mubidi wende bimueneka bipetangana, ke muaba au ke mudi tshitupa tshiende tshinene. Vuluka kabidi ne: munganga udi ne bua kuenza mudimu kuulu, bule bua metre matue ku muanda mukulu anyi dikumi pankatshi pende ne muntu udiye upanda, ne muntu au udi pabuipi ne kusokoma mu mbuu udi munyanguke, udi wenda unyunguluka, ne misangu ya bungi udi ne tshimvundu ne udi ututa. Vuluka kabidi ne: mu nsombelu mibi eyi udi ne bua kupanda makasa a bungi mu mubidi; ne mu mushindu wa muinshi mua buloba, kayi nansha umona nansha kakese mu tshitupa tshidi tshienda tshinyanguka tshienza nunku, udi ne bua kuepuka ne budimu buonso bitupa bionso bidi pabuipi, bidibu bakandike, ne kutapulula menemene mukolo wa mukolo muaba wa mushinga mukole pa kuwubueja mu tshifu. Nunku, kuenaku ukema bua diditumbisha dia Stubb dia se: uvua mulombe anu minite dikumi bua kukosa baleine wa mabele anyi?

Padibu banji kumukosa, mutu udi upuekeshibua ku nyima ne ulamakaja kuntu aku ne tshingoma too ne padi mubidi upatuka. Bionso ebi bimane kuenzeka, bikalabi bia baleine mukese, badi babibandisha pa mazuwa bua kubibutula ku budisuile. Ino, na leviathan mukola, kabiena mua kuenzeka to; bualu mutu wa baleine udi ukuata pabuipi ne tshia bisatu tshia bujitu buende buonso, ne bua kuimanyika bujitu bua nunku, nansha ku diambuluisha dia bintu binene bia muena baleine, bualu ebu buvua bualu bua tshianana anu bu kuteta kupima tshikumbi tshia bena Pays-Bas mu tshipiminu tshia bena mabwe a mushinga mukole.

Pavuabu bakose mutu wa baleine wa Pequod ne bambula mubidi wende, mutu wende uvua mubandishibua ku luseke lua mazuwa—pabuipi ne tshitupa tshia njila wa kupatuka mu mbuu, bua se: uvua mua kuikala anu mukoleshibue kudi tshintu tshiende tshia ku dibele. Ne kuntu aku ne buatu buvua buenda buenda buenda bupueka kudibu, bua dikokangana dinene dia panshi difumina ku mutu wa tshibumba tshia kuinshi, ne diboko dionso dia lupangu ku luseke alu dipatuka bu tshiamu tshia nzembu pa mutu pa mavuala a mâyi; kuntu aku, mutu au uvua utoka mashi uvua mulenge ku tshiadi tshia Pequod bu tshia Holofernes munene uvua mu mukaba wa Yudita.

Pakajika mudimu wa ndekelu eu, kuvua midi, ne bena mazuwa bakaya kuinshi bua kudia. Ditalala diakakokesha pa tshibandilu tshia kumpala tshivua ne tshimvundu kadi mpindieu tshishale tshitupu. Ditalala dia tshiamu tshia mitaku dikole, bu mutshi wa lotus wa jaune wa pa buloba bujima, divua dienda divula mabeji ende avua kaayi ne mutoyi pa mbuu.

Katupa kakese kakapita, ne mu muaba uvua kawuyi mutoyi au wakalua Ahaba nkayende mu nzubu wende wa tshilala benyi. Mu mudimu wa tshimvundu wa dikosa ne dilama dia baleine, kudi lubilu lua bungi lua kupingana ne kupingana munkatshi mua bena mudimu. Tshivua tshibuejamu kudi mulunda wanyi Queequeg, uvua mudimu wende, bu muena tshingoma, wa kupueka pa mukolo wa tshintu tshinene etshi bua tshipatshila tshia pa buatshi tshidibu bambe. Kadi misangu ya bungi, nsombelu udi ulomba ne: muena tshingoma ashale pa baleine too ne padibu bajikija mudimu wonso wa diumusha anyi diumusha. Nnganyi udi umutshina, pa kumbusha nguvena mukulakaje udi kayi ne dikima dia kumukuata ne kumuteka mu bilamba bibidi, bu mudiye mukumbanyine, kadi udi umulekela uya kukuata bantu ku bukole;

"Udi wela meji ne: Fedallah mmusue kukuata Kapitene Ahaba anyi?"

"Ndi ngela meji anyi? Neumanye mu katupa kîpi emu, Flask. Kadi ndi nya mpindieu bua kumutangila; ne meme mumone bualu kampanda budi buenza buôwa, nenmukuate anu ku nshingu wende, ne ngambe ne: Tangila, Beelzebula, kuena wenza nanku to; ne yeye muenze tshimvundu, kudi Mukalenge, nenkuate mu tshibombi tshiende, bua kumuangata kudi muendeshi wa mazuwa, ne umupeshe tshisumi ne tshikole, bua se: mukila wende neupatuke ku tshisuku—udi mumone anyi;

"Ne newenze tshinyi ne mukila, Stubb?"

"Ntshienza natshi? Netshisumbishe ku tshingoma tshia ngombe patudi tufika kumbelu;—tshinyi tshikuabu?"

"Mpindieu, udi musue kuamba tshiudi wamba, ne tshiudi wamba katshia ku kale anyi, Stubb?"

"Mubi nansha mubi, apa tudi ku buatu."

Mazuwa avua mabikila muaba eu, bua kukoka baleine ku luseke lua larboard, muaba uvuabu balongolole nkanu ya fluke ne bintu bikuabu bia mushinga bua kumulama.

"Tshiena mukuambile nanku anyi?" Flask unena; "eyowa, neumone mu katupa kîpi emu mutu wa baleine wa ku dia balume eu mubandishibue kumpala kua wa parmacetti au."

Mu tshikondo tshimpe, mêyi a Flask akalua malelela. Anu bu kumpala, Pequod wakasemena bikole mutangile ku mutu kua baleine wa mabele, mpindieu, ku diambuluisha dia mitu yonso ibidi, wakapeta kabidi tshifulu tshiende tshimpe; nansha mudibi bikutonda bikole, udi mua kuitaba. Nunku, paudi ku luseke lumue ubandisha mutu wa Locke, udi uya ku luseke alu; kadi mpindieu, ku luseke lukuabu, bandisha mu Kant ne udi upingana kabidi; kadi mu nsombelu mubi menemene. Nunku, bamue bantu batu anu batungunuka ne kulongolola buatu. Aa, nuenu batshimbakane! Ela mitu yonso eyi ya makubakuba mu mayi, ne pashishe neulue pa mâyi bipepele ne dia balume.

Padibu bumbusha tshitalu tshia baleine wa ku dia balume, padibu balua nende ku luseke lua mazuwa, malu a muomumue adi enzeka anu bu mudiwu enzeka mu tshilumbu tshia baleine wa sperme; anu, mu tshikondu tshia ndekelu, mutu udi ukoshibua mujima, kadi mu tshikondu tshia kumpala mishiku ne ludimi bidi bipatuka pa nkayabi ne bibandishibua pa tshibandilu, ne mifuba yonso mifike mimanyike bimpe milamakaja ku tshidibu babikila ne: tshitupa tshia tshifulu tshia butumbi. Kadi kakuena bualu bua mushindu eu, mu tshilumbu etshi, buvua buenzeke to. Bitalu bia baleine yonso ibidi ivua mikuluke ku nyima; ne buatu buvua ne bintu bia bungi kabuvua bufuanangane ne mpunda uvua muambule bintu bibidi bia bujitu bua bungi to.

Mu tshikondo atshi, Fedallah uvua utangila mutu wa baleine wa ku dia balume ne ditalala, ne misangu yonso uvua utangila ku milonda mikole ivuaku too ne ku milongo ivua mu tshianza tshiende. Ne Ahaba wakimana mu mpukapuka, ne muena Parsee wakashala mu mundidimbi wende; kadi, shi mundidimbi wa Parsee uvuaku nansha kakese uvua umueneka anu bu uvua mua kusambakena ne, ne kulepesha wa Ahaba. Bu muvua bena mudimu benda batungunuka ne mudimu, ngenyi ya bena Lapland ivua mitangalake munkatshi muabu, pa bidi bitangila bintu bionso ebi bivua bipita.
