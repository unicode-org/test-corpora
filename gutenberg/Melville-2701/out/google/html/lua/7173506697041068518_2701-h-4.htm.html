Pamvua nya, mvua muwule tente ne meji; tshivuabu bandongeshe ku mpukapuka bua Kapitene Ahaba, tshiakanwuja ne kanyinganyinga kampanda ka tshinyangu kavua katangila bualu buende. Ne mu mushindu kampanda, tshikondo atshi, ngakumvua luse ne kanyinganyinga bua bualu buende, kadi bua tshiena mumanye tshinyi, pa kumbusha anu dijimija dia dikasa diende dia tshikisu. Kadi nansha nanku mvua kabidi mumvue buôwa bua dikema kudiye; kadi buôwa bua mushindu eu, budi tshiyi mua kumvuija nansha kakese, kabuvua buôwa menemene to; Tshiena mumanye tshivuatshi to. Kadi ngakumvua; ne kabivua bintonda kudiye to; nansha mumvua ndiumvua tshiyi ne lutulu bua tshivua tshimueneka bu bualu busokoka munda muende, mu mushindu mubi bu mumvua mumanyike kudi meme tshikondo atshi. Ino, meji anyi akaya mu njila mikuabo, nunku bua tshikondo etshi tshia mîdima Ahaba wakajimija lungenyi luanyi.





SHAPITA 17. Ramadan.

Bu muvua Ramadan wa Queequeg, anyi Dijila dia biakudia ne Dipuekeshibua milongo, ne bua kutungunuka dituku dijima, tshivua musungule bua kumutatshisha too ne butuku to; bualu ndi nnemeka kanemu kakole kudi muntu yonso udi ne bua kuenza malu a ntendelelu, nansha biobi bikale bia kusekesha, ne tshivua mua kumona mu mutshima wanyi bua kupepeja nansha tshisumbu tshia mbua tshidi tshitendelela tshitanda tshia nkutshi to; anyi bifukibua bikuabu bia mu bitupa kampanda bia buloba buetu, bidi ne tshipiminu tshia dienda dia makasa tshidi katshiyi tshianji kuenzeka mu mitoto mikuabu, binama kumpala kua tshitalu tshia muena buloba udi mufue anu bua bintu bia bungi bidibu nabi ne bifutshila mu dîna diende.

Ndi ngamba ne, tuetu bena kilisto ba Presbytérien bimpe tudi ne tshia kuikala ne luse mu malu aa, ne katuena tudimona bapite bantu bakuabu ba pa buloba, bampangano ne bikuabu, bua lungenyi luabu lua tshitupa tshia bupote pa malu aa. Kuvua Queequeg, mpindieu, uvua bushuwa wela meji a Yojo ne Ramadan wende;—kadi netuambe tshinyi bua bualu abu? Queequeg uvua wela meji ne uvua mumanye tshivuaye wamba, ndi ngela meji; uvua umueneka musanke; ne uikishe kuntu aku. Dikokangana dietu dionso nende kadivua mua kutuambuluisha to; ikale, ndi ngamba: ne Diulu ditufuile luse tuetu bonso—bena Presbytérien ne bampangano bonso—bualu tuetu bonso tudi mu mushindu kampanda mutshibuka ku mutu, ne tudi dijinga ne dilongolola.

Ku dilolo, pamvua mudiumvue mujadike ne: manaya ende onso ne mikiya yende ivua ne bua kujika, ngakaya mu nzubu wende ne meme kukokola ku tshiibi; kadi kakuena diandamuna to. Ngakateta bua kutshiunzulula, kadi tshivua tshisuika munda. "Queequeg," ngakamba bitekete bitekete kupitshila mu tshina tshia tshifungu:—bionso bipuwe. "Ndi ngamba, Queequeg! bua tshinyi kuena wakula? Ndi meme—Yishmael." Kadi bionso biakashala anu bu kumpala. Ngakatuadija kuikala ne buôwa. Mvua mumupeshe dîba dia bungi nunku; Ngakela meji ne: uvua mua kuikala ne disama dia apoplexie. Ngakatangila mu tshina tshia tshifungu; kadi tshiibi tshivua tshiunzuluka mu ditumba dia pa buadi dia nzubu, tshiibi tshia tshiibi tshivua anu tshinyanguke ne tshibi. Mvua mmona anu tshitupa tshia tshilamba tshia ku makasa a bulalu ne mulongo wa tshimanu, kadi tshivua mmona tshikuabu to. Ngakakema pangakamona mutshi wa tshingoma tshia Queequeg, uvua muena nzubu mumuangatshile dilolo dia kumpala, kumpala kua tuetu kuya mu nzubu. Bidi bikemesha, meme kuela meji; kadi nansha biobi bikale mushindu kayi, bu mudi tshingoma tshiimane kuntu aku, ne katu uya misangu mikese anyi kayi uya pambelu kayi natshi to, ke bualu kayi udi ne bua kuikala munda apa, ne kakuena tshilema tshidi mua kuenzeka to.

"Queequeg!—Queequeg!"—bionso bidi anu nanku. Bualu kampanda buvua ne bua kuikala buenzeke. Apoplexie! Ngakateta bua kunzulula tshiibi; kadi wakakandamena ne mutu mukole. Pamvua ntuluka mu tshibandilu, ngakambila muntu wa kumpala umvua mupetangane naye malu amvua ngela meji ne lukasa luonso, mmumue ne: muena mudimu wa mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi. "La! la!" wakadila ne: “Mvua ngela meji ne: kuvua bualu kampanda buvua buenzeke. Ngakaya bua kulongolola bulalu panyima pa didia dia mu dinda, ne tshiibi tshivua tshikanga; ne kakuvua mpuku uvua umvua to; ne katshia anu pinapu tshivua tshishale anu mupuwe. Kadi mvua ngela meji ne: pamuapa, nuenu bonso babidi nuvua baye ne nukanga bintu bienu bua kubilama bimpe. La! la, mamu! apoplexie!”—ne ne miadi eyi, wakanyema mutangile ku tshikuku, meme kumulonda.

Mukaji Hussey wakamueneka mutantshi mukese, ne tshilongo tshia mustarde ku tshianza tshimue ne vinegre-cruet ku tshikuabu, mumane kumbuka ku mudimu wa kutangila ba-castor, ne wela muanende wa balume mufike mpata mu tshikondo atshi.

"Nzubu wa nkunyi!" Ngakela mbila ne: “Njila kayi wa kuya nende? Nyema bua bualu bua Nzambi, ne wangate tshintu tshia kunzulula tshiibi—tshingoma!—tshingoma! mmupete disama dia tshiadi; wimanyine patshi!”—ne ngamba nunku mvua ntuluka lubilu kabidi ku tshibandilu tshianza tshia tshianana, pavua mamu Hussey mubueje tshibombi tshia mustarde ne vinegre, tshibuelelu tshia nzubu wende wa tshilala benyi.

"Ntshinyi tshidi tshikuenzekele, nsongalume?"

“Angata tshingoma, bua bualu bua Nzambi, ndaku lubilu bua kuya kudi munganga pandi ntshibulula!”

"Tangila apa," ke muakamba muena nzubu, wela lukasa tshintu tshia vinegre panshi, bua kuikala ne tshianza tshimue; "Tangila apa; udi wakula bua kunzulula tshiibi tshianyi nansha tshimue anyi?"—ne yeye kunkuata ku tshianza. "Ntshilumbu kayi tshiudi natshi? Tshilumbu kayi tshiudi natshi, muena mazuwa?"

Mu mushindu mupole, kadi ne lukasa, ngakamupesha bua kumvua tshilumbu tshijima. Wakakuma vinegre-cruet ku luseke lumue lua muulu wende kayi mumanye, wakaela meji bua tshitupa tshîpi; pashishe kuambaye ne: “Tòo, tshitu muanji kutshimona katshia ngatshiteka muaba au to.” Wakaya lubilu mu kazubu kakese kavua muinshi mua tshibandilu, wakatangila munda, ne pakapinganaye, kungambilaye ne: tshingoma tshia Queequeg katshivuaku to. Wakadila wamba ne: “Mmudishipe.” "Mbibi bua Stiggs kuenzaye kabidi—kudi tshikandakanda tshikuabu tshidi tshienda—Nzambi umvuile mamuende mupele luse!—nebikale dibutuka dia nzubu wanyi. Nsongalume mupele eu udi ne muanabu wa bakaji anyi? Nsongakaji au udi penyi?—aku, Betty, ndaku kudi Snarles muena mudimu wa dizola, ne umuambile bua kungenzele tshimanyinu,—ne dianyisha dia kudishipa, ne kuela mbila apa Parlor;”—udi mua kushipa nyunyi yonso ibidi musangu umue. Kushipa? Mukelenge afuile nyuma wende luse! Mutoyi kayi udiku? Wewe, nsongalume, udiku!”

Ne wenda lubilu panyima panyi, wakankuata pamvua kabidi nteta bua kunzulula tshiibi ku bukole.

"Tshiena ngitaba to; tshiena mua kunyangakaja nzubu wanyi to. Ndaku kudi muenji wa makunji, kudi umue mutantshi wa kilometre umue ne apa. Kadi avast!" wela tshianza tshienda mu tshibombi tshia ku luseke, "mona tshifungu tshidi mua kukumbana, ndi ngela meji; tumonayi." Ne ne bualu ebu, wakatshikudimuna mu tshiibi; kadi, diakabi! Tshiamu tshia pa mutu tshia Queequeg tshiakashala katshiyi tshipatuka munda.

"Ndi ne tshia kutshibulula," ngakamba, ne mvua ntuluka mu tshibuelelu kakese, bua kutuadija bimpe, pavua muena nzubu mumkuate, untshipa kabidi ne: tshivua ne bua kunyanga nzubu wende to; kadi ngakamutapulula, ne ne lubilu lua tshimpitshimpi lua mubidi meme kuditua mu tshimanyishilu.

Ne mutoyi wa dikema, tshiibi tshiakabuluka, ne tshikunyibua tshiakakuma ku tshimanu, kutuma tshiamu tshia nzembu ku plafond; ne kuntu aku, diulu dimpe! Queequeg uvua musombe, mupole ne mudisangishe nkayende; munkatshi mua nzubu; udi musombe pa mishiku yende, ne mukuate Yojo pa mutu pende. Kavua utangila luseke lumue anyi lukuabu to, kadi uvua musombe bu lupingu lusonga ne tshimanyinu tshikese tshia muoyo wa mudimu.

"Queequeg," ngakamba, nya kudiye, "Queequeg, udi ne bualu kayi?"

"Kavua musombe nunku dituku dijima to, ki mmuomu anyi?" ke muakamba muena nzubu.

Kadi bionso bituvua bambe, katuvua mua kumukoka dîyi nansha dimue; Mvua pabuipi ne kumvua dijinga dia kumusaka, bua kushintulula muaba wende, bualu bivua pabuipi ne kuikala kabiyi mua kuitaba, bivua bimueneka bikole ne bivua kabiyi bia pa tshibidilu; nangananga, bu mudibi bimueneka ne: uvua musombe nunku munkatshi mua mêba mapite pa muanda mukulu anyi dikumi, wenda pende kayi udia biakudia biende bia pa tshibidilu.

Meme kuamba ne: “Mamu Hussey, udi ne muoyo nansha kakese, nunku utushiye, wewe musue, ne meme nkayanyi nentungunuke ne kuenza bualu ebu bua dikema.”

Meme kukanga tshiibi kudi muena nzubu, meme kudienzeja bua kuenzeja Queequeg bua kuangata nkuasa; kadi tshianana. Wakasombamu; ne tshionso tshivuaye mua kuenza—bua malu anyi onso a buneme ne a bupole—kavua mua kunyungisha tshintu nansha tshimue, anyi kuamba dîyi nansha dimue, nansha kuntangila, anyi kumona dikalaku dianyi nansha kakese.

Ndi ndiebeja, meme kuela meji, ni bualu ebu budi mua kuikala tshitupa tshia Ramadan wende; badi bajila biakudia biabu mushindu au mu tshidila tshiende tshia diledibua. Bidi ne tshia kuikala nunku; eyowa, i tshitupa tshia ditabuja diende, ndi ngela meji; biyampe, nunku, alekele; neajuke mu katupa kîpi anyi pashishe, kakuyi mpata. Kabiena mua kushala kashidi to, tuasakidila Nzambi, ne Ramadan wende utu ulua anu musangu umue ku tshidimu; ne tshiena ngitaba ne: bidi bikumbaja dîba dia bungi dîba adi to.

Ngapueka bua kudia. Kunyima kua bamane kusomba mutantshi mule bateleja miyuki mile ya bamue bena matu bavua bafuma ku difumina ku luendu lua plum-pudding, muvuabu balubikila (mmumue ne: luendu lupi lua diloba dia baleine mu mazuwa a mâyi anyi mu mazuwa a mâyi, avua makanga ku nord kua mulongo, anu mu Mbuu wa Atlantique); kunyima kua meme mumane kuteleja bintu ebi too ne pa dîba dia dikumi ne dimue, ngakabanda ku tshibandilu bua kuya kulala, ndiumvua mujadike ne: dîba adi Queequeg uvua ne bua kuikala mujikije Ramadan wende. Kadi tòo; uvuaku anu muaba umvua mumushiye; kavua muele nansha santimetre nansha umue to. Ngakatuadija kumufikila munda; bivua bimueneka bu bia tshianana ne bia bupote bua kuikala musombe muaba au dituku dijima ne tshitupa tshia butuku pa bilamba biende mu nzubu wa mashika, mukuate tshitupa tshia nkunyi ku mutu kuende.

“Bua bualu bua diulu, Queequeg, juka, udinyuke, juka udie biakudia bia dilolo, neufue nzala, neudishipe, Queequeg.” Kadi kavua muandamune dîyi nansha dimue to.

Bu mumvua mupangile ditekemena kudiye, nunku, ngakangata dipangadika dia kuya kulala ne kulala; ne kakuyi mpata, kumpala kua tshikondo tshile, uvua mua kundonda. Kadi kumpala kua meme kubuela, ngakangata tshilamba tshianyi tshile tshia dikoba dia urse, meme kutshiela pa mutu pende, bualu tshivua tshilaya bua kuikala butuku bua mashika makole; ne kavua ne tshintu tshikuabu to, anu tshilamba tshiende tshia patupu tshia tshijengu. Bua tshikondo kampanda, ngenza bionso bimvua musue, tshivua mua kubuela mu tulu tukese to. Mvua mujime muendu; ne lungenyi lua patupu lua Queequeg—ki ntanta wa metre anayi to—musombe muaba au mu mushindu mubi, nkayende mu mashika ne mu mîdima; bualu ebu buakantonda bikole. Elabi meji; kulala butuku bujima mu kazubu kamue ne mumpangano uvua mutabale bikole pa tshiadi tshiende mu Ramadan eu wa kanyinganyinga ne kawuyi mua kuandamuna!

Kadi mu mushindu kampanda ngakalekela ndekelu wa bionso, ne tshivua mumanye bualu bukuabu too ne mu dinda; Pavuaye utangila ku luseke lua bulalu, Queequeg uvua musombe, bienze anu bu ne: bavua bamukuate panshi. Kadi diakamue pavua dîba dia kumpala dibuela mu dididishi, wakajuka, ne mifuba mikole ne mikole, kadi ne mêsu a disanka; uvua unyema ulua kundi muaba umvua mulale; wakafina kabidi mpala wende ku wanyi; ne wakamba ne: Ramadan wende ukavua mujike.

Mpindieu, bu mumvua muambe kumpala, tshiena ne bualu nansha bumue bua ntendelelu wa muntu nansha umue, nansha biobi bikale mushindu kayi, anu padi muntu au kayi ushipa anyi utuka muntu mukuabu, bualu muntu au pende kena witabuja to. Kadi padi tshitendelelu tshia muntu tshilua tshikole menemene; padibi bimukengesha bimpe; ne, ku ndekelu, udi wenza bua buloba buetu ebu buikale nzubu wa tshilala benyi udi kauyi muimpe wa kulala; pashishe ndi ngela meji ne: ntshikondo tshia kuangata muntu au ku luseke ne kuelangana nende mpata.

Ne ke mumvua muenze mpindieu ne Queequeg. "Queequeg," meme kuamba, "lala mpindieu, ne ulale ne unteleje." Pashishe ngakatungunuka, kutuadija ne dikola ne dikola dia bitendelelu bia kale, ne kufika too ne ku bitendelelu bishilashilangane bia tshikondo etshi, mu tshikondo atshi ngakadienzeja bua kuleja Queequeg ne: bionso ebi bia Karême, bia Ramadan, ne bia mutantshi mule bia mu nzubu ya mashika, kayiyi ne disanka bivua bia tshianana; bibi bua makanda a mubidi; tshianana bua anyima; mu tshikoso, udi ubengangana ne mikenji idi imueneka patoke ya bukezuke ne lungenyi luimpe. Ngakamuambila kabidi ne: bu mudiye mu malu makuabu muena tshinyangu wa lungenyi lua bungi ne wa meji, biakantonda, biakantonda bikole menemene, bua kumumona mpindieu muikale mutshimbakane bikole bua Ramadan wende wa bupote eu. Kabidi, ngakamba ne, dijila dia biakudia didi dienza bua mubidi unyanguke; ke bualu kayi nyuma udi ubuela; ne meji onso adi afumina ku dijila dia biakudia adi ne bua kuikala ne nzala ya tshitupa. Etshi ke tshidi tshifikisha bena bitendelelu ba bungi badi ne disama dia dyspeptic ku dinanga lungenyi lua kanyinganyinga lua nunku lua malu abu a kunyima kua lufu. Mu muaku umua, Queequeg, ngakamba, mu mushindu mubi; inferno i lungenyi ludi lufumina ku tshintu tshia pome tshidi katshiyi tshijika; ne katshia anu pinapu bidi bienda bikola ku diambuluisha dia disama dia tshiadi tshia ku bumpianyi didi dikolesha kudi Ramadan.

Pashishe ngakebeja Queequeg ni yeye muine uvua ne lutatu lua disama dia tshiadi; kuleja lungenyi elu patoke, bua yeye kumona mua kuluangata. Wakamba ne: tòo; anu pa tshikondo tshimue tshia kuvuluka. Bivua kunyima kua difesto dinene difila kudi tatuende mukalenge, pa ditshimuna dia mvita minene muvua baluishi makumi atanu bashipe pabuipi ne pa dîba dibidi dia mu mapingaja, ne bonso bavua bateka ne badia anu dilolo adi.

"Kakuena kabidi, Queequeg," ngakamba, ndi ngelakana; "nebienze;" bualu mvua mumanye malu avua menzeke kayi muanji kuakula kabidi to. Mvua mumone muendeshi wa mazuwa uvua muye kutangila tshidila atshi, ne wakangambila ne: pavua mvita minene mienzekemu, bivua ne tshibidilu tshia kulamba bantu bonso bavuabu bashipe mu lupangu anyi mu budimi bua mutshimuna; ne pashishe, umue ku umue, bavua babateka mu milonda minene ya mabaya, ne babalengeja bu pilau, ne mampa ne nkunde; ne ndambu wa persil mu mishiku yabu, bavua babatuma ne mianu ya mutshimuna kudi balunda bende bonso, anu bu ne: mapa aa avua a nkutshi ya bungi ya Nowele.

Bualu bukuabu, tshiena ngela meji ne: mêyi anyi avua atangila malu a ntendelelu avua ne bukole bua bungi kudi Queequeg to. Bualu, mu muaba wa kumpala, uvua umueneka mu mushindu kampanda kayi umvua bimpe pa bualu bua mushinga mukole abu, pa kumbusha yeye mumane kumutangila bilondeshile mmuenenu wende; ne, pa muaba muibidi, kavua mumvue bipite pa tshia bisatu to, uvua muele meji anyi anu mumvua musue; ne ndekelu wa bionso, kakuyi mpata uvua wela meji ne: uvua mumanye malu a bungi a ntendelelu mulelela kumpita meme. Wakantangila ne kanyinganyinga kakole ne luse, bienze anu bu ne: uvua wela meji ne: bivua bualu bua luse lua bungi bua nsongalume wa meji nunku kujimina mu ditabuja dia bampangano dia evanjeliyo.

Ndekelu wa bionso tuakajuka ne kuvuala bilamba; ne Queequeg, udia biakudia bia mu dinda bia mishindu yonso, bua se: muena nzubu kapeta makuta a bungi bua Ramadan wende, tuakapatuka bua kubuela mu mazuwa a Pequod, tuendakana, ne tuela meno etu ne mifuba ya halibut.





SHAPITA 18. Mâko andi.

Patuvua tuenda tupueka ku ndekelu kua tshitudilu tshia mazuwa batangile ku mazuwa, Queequeg muambule tshingoma tshiende, Kapitene Peleg ne dîyi diende dia tshikisu wakatuakidila ne dîyi dikole ku nzubu wende wa tshilala benyi, wamba ne: kavua wela meji ne: mulunda wanyi uvua mudia bantu to, ne kabidi wakamanyisha ne: kavua mulekele bantu bavua badia bantu mu buatu abu to, anu bobu kabayi bapatule mabeji abu kumpala.

"Udi musue kuamba tshinyi ne bualu ebu, Kapitene Peleg?" Ngakamba meme, mpindieu ntuluka pa bimanu, ne nshiya mulunda wanyi muimane pa tshitudilu tshia mazuwa.

Wakandamuna ne: “Ndi musue kuamba ne: udi ne bua kuleja mikanda yende.”

"Eyowa," ke muakamba Kapitene Bildad ne dîyi diende dia patupu, wela mutu wende kunyima kua Peleg, pambelu pa nzubu wa tshilala benyi. "Udi ne tshia kuleja mudiye mukudimune mutshima. Muana wa mîdima," wakasakidila, utangila Queequeg, "udi mpindieu mu buobumue ne ekeleziya kampanda wa bena kilisto anyi?"

Ngakamba ne: “Bua tshinyi, udi muena mu Ekeleziya wa kumpala wa Tshisumbu.” Tudi mua kuamba ne, bantu ba bungi badi ne mputa badi benda mu mazuwa a Nantucket ndekelu wa bionso badi balua kukudimuna mu ekeleziya.

Bildad wakadila wamba ne: “Ekeleziya wa kumpala wa tshisumbu, ntshinyi tshidi tshitendelela mu nzubu wa bisangilu wa Diakono Deuteronomy Coleman?” ne yeye kuamba nunku, kupatula manyanu ende, wakaasukula ne tshilamba tshiende tshinene tshia bandana tshia jaune, ne kutshivualaye bimpe menemene, kupatukaye mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi, ne kuimanaye bikole pa bimanu, kutangilaye Queequeg bimpe mutantshi mule.

"Mmutantshi kayi udiye muikale muena mu tshisumbu?" wakamba pashishe, unkudimuka kundi; "ki mutantshi mule, ndi ngela meji, nsongalume."

"Tòo," ke muakamba Peleg, "ne yeye pende ki mmubatijibue bimpe to, anyi bivua mua kumusukula ndambu wa bule bua diabolo au ku mpala kuende."

Biledada wakadila wamba ne: “Uambilaku, muena Peleshete eu udi ubuela mu tshisangilu tshia Diakono Dutelonome anyi?

"Tshiena mumanye bualu nansha bumue bua Diakono Dutelonome anyi tshisangilu tshiende to," ngakamba; "Tshindi mumanye ntshia se, Queequeg udi muledibue mu Ekeleziya wa kumpala wa tshisumbu. Yeye nkayende udi diakon, Queequeg udi."

"Nsongalume," Biladada unena na bukomo, "udi unyunguluka nami—dileja, wewe nsongalume muena Hiti. Udi usua kuamba ekeleziya kayi? Ngandamune."

Bu mumvua mudimone muenzejibue bikole nunku, ngakandamuna. "Ndi musue kuamba, mfumu, Ekeleziya umue wa kale wa bena Katolike udi wewe ne meme, ne Kapitene Peleg kuntu aku, ne Queequeg apa, ne tuetu bonso, ne muana wa mamu yonso ne anyima wetu tudi; Tshisumbu tshinene tshia kumpala tshia kashidi tshia buloba bujima bua ntendelelu; tuetu bonso tudi batshi; anu bamue ba kutudi badi banange amue malu adi kaayi malelela mu tshianza tshinene tshitudi tuenza."

"Splice, udi musue kuamba splice hands," ke muvua Peleg wela lubila, wenda usemena pabuipi. "Nsongalume, mbimpe utuma mazuwa bua misionere, pamutu pa tshianza tshia kumpala; tshitu muanji kumvua muyuki muimpe kupita eu to. "Udi mutshioke, Queequeg," meme kuamba, "lua."

Lukonko elu luvua lunyunguluke, ngakakema, ngakandamuna ne: “Eyowa, mvua ngela meji ne: mvua mumone bantu banayi anyi batanu, kadi katshivua tshishiki to.”

Ne meyi aa avua manyanguke, ndekelu wa bionso wakaya, kunshiya, bua tshitupa tshîpi, mu dikema dikese bua bupote buende bua tshimpitshimpi.

Ndekelu wa bionso, patuakabuela mu mazuwa a Pequod, tuakapeta bintu bionso mu ditalala dia bungi, kakuyi muntu nansha umue uvua unyunguluka to. Tshiibi tshia nzubu wa tshilala benyi tshivua tshikanga munda; biibi bionso bivua bimane, ne bivua biuwule tente ne bipese bia tshiamu tshia mitaku. Patuvua tuya kumpala ku tshitudilu tshia kumpala, tuakasangana tshibandilu tshia scuttle tshiunzulula. Patuakamona butoke, tuakapueka, ne kusangana anu muena mudimu wa kale, mubuikila mu tshilamba tshia nkunde tshivua tshinyanguke. Bakamuela mu bule buonso pa bifuka bibidi, mpala wende mutangile panshi ne mubuikila mu maboko ende makuate. Tulu tukole menemene tuakalala pa mutu pende.

"Bena matu aba batudi bamone, Queequeg, badi mua kuikala baye penyi?" Ngakamba meme, mutangile muntu uvua mulale ne dielakana. Kadi bivua bimueneka ne, pavuaye ku tshitudilu tshia mazuwa, Queequeg kavua mumone nansha kakese tshimvua ngamba mpindieu to; ke bualu kayi mvua mua kuikala muele meji ne: mvua mushimibue mu bualu abu, bu kabiyi bua lukonko lua Eliya luvua kaluyi mua kumvuika to. Kadi ngakakuma tshintu panshi; ne kuela kabidi tshimanyinu pa muntu uvua mulale, kuambila Queequeg ne tuseku ne: pamuapa mbimpe tuetu kusomba ne tshitalu; umuambila bua kudijadika bilondeshile bualu abu. Wakateka tshianza tshienda pa tshiadi tshia muntu uvua mulale, bienze anu bu uvua umvua ni tshivua tshitekete bikumbane; ne pashishe, kakuyi bualu bukuabu, wakasomba panshi apu.

"Ngasa! Queequeg, kosomba kuntu aku," ngakamba.

"Oh! nkuasa wa perry dood," ke muakamba Queequeg, "njila wanyi wa mu mpata; kawena mua kumuenzela bibi ku mpala to."

"Mpala!" Ngakamba ne, "ubikila mpala wandi anyi? mpala muimpe be; kadi mmunyi mudiye upuyakana, udi udibandisha; upueka, Queequeg, udi ne bujitu, udi utapa mpala wa bapele. Upueka, Queequeg! Tangila, neakukose mu katupa kîpi emu. Ndi ndiebeja ne: kena ujuka to."

Queequeg wakadipatula anu ku mutu kua muntu uvua mulale, ne wakatemesha tshiamu tshiende tshia tomahawk. Ngakasomba ku makasa. Tuvua tulama pipe upita pa mutu pa tshilamba tshia kulala, kumbukila ku umue too ne ku mukuabu. Mu tshikondo atshi, pavuaye mumuele nkonko mu mushindu wende munyanguke, Queequeg wakampesha lungenyi lua se: mu buloba buende, bua dipanga dia nkuasa ne nkuasa ya mishindu yonso, mukalenge, bamfumu ne bantu banene mu tshibungi, bavua ne tshibidilu tshia kukolesha bamue ba ku bantu ba muinshi mua buloba bua ba-ottoman; ne bua kulongolola nzubu bimpe mu bualu ebu, uvua anu ne bua kusumba bantu batshimbakane muanda mukulu anyi dikumi, ne kubateka mu nseke yonso ya tshiibi ne mu nzubu ya tshilala benyi. Kabidi, bivua bimpa mu luendu; biyampe kupita nkuasa ya mu budimi idibu mua kukudimuna mitshi ya kuenda nayi; imue misangu, mfumu kampanda uvua ubikila muena mudimu wende, ne umulomba bua kudienzela tshibasa muinshi mua mutshi uvua mutangalake, pamuapa muaba kampanda wa mâyi a mu tshisuku.

Patshivuaye ulonda malu aa, misangu yonso ivua Queequeg wangata tomahawk kudi meme, uvua utua tshiadi pa mutu pa muntu uvua mulale.

"Bua tshinyi, Queequeg?"

"Perry easy, kill-e; oh! perry easy!"

Uvua wenda uvuluka amue malu a tshinyangu bua tshiombelu tshiende tshia tomahawk, tshivua, bimueneka bu ne: tshivua ne midimu ibidi tshivua tshikolesha baluishi bende ne tshilengeja anyima wende, patuvua bakokibue buludiludi kudi tshintu tshivua tshilala tulu. Lupepele lukole ludi mpindieu luwuja tshina tshivua tshinyanguke, luakatuadija kumuambila. Uvua upuyakana ne mushindu kampanda wa dipanga dia dîyi; pashishe uvua umueneka mubungame mu mishiku; pashishe uvua unyunguluka musangu umua anyi ibidi; pashishe wakasomba ne kuelaye mêsu ende.

Dîba divua mpindieu dipatuka patoke. Mu katupa kîpi, bena mudimu bakabuela mu buatu babidi babidi ne basatu; bena mudimu bavua badienzeja; bena dibaka bavua badifila bikole; ne bantu ba bungi ba ku muelelu wa mâyi bavua ne mudimu wa bungi wa kulua ne bintu bia ndekelu bishilangane mu mazuwa.
