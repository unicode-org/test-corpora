SHAPITA 55. Wa Bimfuanyi Bibi bia Baleine.

Nenkuleje mutantshi mule bu mudi muntu mua kuenza kayi ne tshilamba, tshintu tshidi tshifuanangane ne tshimuenekelu tshilelela tshia baleine bu mudiye umueneka ku mêsu kua baleine padi mu mubidi wende mujima baleine muimanyine ku luseke lua mazuwa a baleine bua se: badi mua kumutua bimpe kuntu aku. Bidi mua kuikala bimpa, nunku, kumpala bua kumanyisha bimfuanyi biende bia mu lungenyi bia dikema bidi nansha too ne lelu bibengangana ne ditabuja dia muena buloba eu ne dishindika dionso. Tshikondo ntshia kulongolola malu a pa buloba mu bualu ebu, pa kujadika ne: bimfuanyi bia baleine ebi mbibi.

Bidi mua kuikala ne: tshintu tshia kumpala tshia dishima dia bimfuanyi etshi netshikale munkatshi mua bimfuanyi bia kale bia bena Hindu, bena Ejipitu ne bena Gelika. Katshia ku bikondo abi bia dienza dia malu kadi kabiyi ne bululame pavua pa mabue a ntempelo, pa bibandilu bia mpingu, ne pa ngabu, medaille, makopo ne makuta a tshiamu, bavua bakosa dolphin mu bipese bia bia mvita bia nkanu bu bia Saladin, ne mutu uvua ne tshifulu bu tshia St. George; Katshia anu pinapu, tshintu tshia muomumue tshidi tshitamba kuikalaku, ki nganu mu bimfuanyi bia baleine bidi bitamba kumanyika to, kadi mu mishindu ya bungi ya malu a sianse idi ileja bualu buende.

Mpindieu, ku mishindu yonso, tshimfuanyi tshia kale tshidiku tshidibu bamba ne: ntshia baleine, tshidi mu tshina tshia mabue tshia Elephanta, mu Inde. Ba-Brahmin badi bamba ne: mu bimfuanyi bidi kabiyi ne ndekelu bia nzubu wa tshitendelelu wa kale au, midimu yonso ne malu onso adibu benza, malu onso adi muntu mua kuenza, bivua bimanyishibue bidimu bia bungi kumpala kua umue wa kudibu kuluaye. Kabiena bikemesha bua se: mu mushindu kampanda mudimu wetu muimpe wa diloba dia baleine uvua ne bua kuikala mubuikila. Baleine wa bena Hindu udibu batela, udi mu tshitupa tshikuabu tshia tshimanu, udi uleja dilua dia Vishnu mu tshimfuanyi tshia leviathan, udibu babikila ne: Matse Avatar. Kadi nansha mudi tshimfuanyi etshi tshikale tshitupa tshia muntu ne tshitupa tshia baleine, bua kufila anu mukila wa wa ndekelu, nansha nanku tshitupa tshikese tshia yeye tshidi tshibi. Bidi bimueneka bu mukila wa anaconda, kupita bianza bia baleine mulelela.

Kadi ndaku ku nzubu ya kale ya binjinjimbi, ne tangila mpindieu tshimfuanyi tshia muena kilisto munene tshia mushipa eu; bualu kena ukumbaja bimpe kupita muena Hindu wa kumpala kua mvula wa kabutu to. I tshimfuanyi tshia Guido tshia Perseus usungila Andromeda kudi nyama wa mu mbuu anyi baleine. Guido uvua mupete penyi tshimfuanyi tshia tshifukibua tshia dikema tshia mushindu eu? Nansha Hogarth, mu dizola dia tshikena kumona tshia muomumue mu “Perseus Descending” wende, kena wenza bualu nansha bumue buimpe to. Bunene bunene bua tshintu tshinene tshia Hogarthian tshidi tshienda tshinyunguluka pa mutu pa buloba, tshikoka nansha santimetre umue wa mayi. Udi ne mushindu kampanda wa howdah ku nyima, ne mukana muende udi ne menu mulabale mudi mavuala a mâyi apita, udi mua kuikala muangatshila ku Tshiibi tshia Bashipianganyi tshidi tshifumina ku Thames ku mâyi too ne ku Tshibumba. Pashishe, kudi baleine ya Prodromus ya mu Scotch Sibbald wa kale, ne baleine ya Jonah, bu mudibi bileja mu bimfuanyi bia Bible ya kale ne mu bitupa bia mikanda ya kale ya ntuadijilu. Nebambe tshinyi bua malu aa? Pa bidi bitangila baleine wa mufundi wa mikanda udi unyunguluka bu muonji wa tumuma tua mvinyo munyunguluke tshitekelu tshia mazuwa tshidi tshipueka—mudibu bafunda ne balengeja ne or ku nyima ne ku mabeji a ntuadijilu a mikanda ya bungi ya kale ne mipiamipia—etshi ntshifukibua tshilenga menemene kadi tshia dikema, tshifuanyikija, ndi ngangata, ku bimfuanyi bia muomumue bidi pa bintu bia kale. Nansha mudibu babikila mu buloba bujima ne: dolphin, ndi mbikila mushipa wa mukanda eu ne: diteta dia baleine; bualu bua se bavua balongolole nanku pavuabu bapatule tshiamu etshi bua musangu wa kumpala. Bivua bipatuka kudi mupatudi wa kale wa mu Italie mu bidimu bia 1400, mu tshikondo tshia Dibika dia Dilonga; ne mu matuku au, ne too ne mu tshikondo tshia ndekelu, bantu bavua bela meji ne: nyama wa dolphin uvua mushindu wa nyama wa Leviathan.

Mu bimfuanyi ne mu bintu bikuabu bia kulengeja nabi mikanda ya kale, imue misangu neupete malu a dikema a baleine, muaba udi mishindu yonso ya mâyi, mâyi a kapia ne mashika, Saratoga ne Baden-Baden, bipatuka mu buongo buende budi kabuyi butshioke. Mu dibeji dia mutu wa tshibejibeji tshia kumpala tshia "Dikola dia dilonga" neupete bamue baleine badi basue kumanya malu a bungi.

Ino patudi tulekela mateta onso aa adi kaayi a mudimu, tuanji kutangila bimfuanyi bia leviathan bidi bileja ne: mbimpe, bilejilu bia malu a sianse, kudi aba badi bamanye. Mu tshibutshilu tshia ngendu ya kale ya Harris mudi bipese bia baleine biangatshila mu mukanda wa ngendu wa mu Pays-Bas, mu 1671 panyima pa Yezu, udi ne tshiena-bualu tshia ne: “Luendu lua kuloba baleine lua kuya ku Spitzbergen mu mazuwa a Jonas mu baleine, Peter Peterson wa mu Friesland, mfumu.” Mu tshimue tshia ku bipese abi, baleine, bu mazuwa manene a mitshi, badi baleja balale munkatshi mua mabue a mashika, ne urse mitoke idi yenda lubilu pa mikolo yayi idi ne muoyo. Mu dibeji dikuabu, tshilema tshinene tshidi tshienza bua kuleja baleine ne mputa mishindame.

Pashishe kabidi, kudi quarto wa dikema, mufunda kudi Kapitene Colnett, Kapitene wa Poste mu mazuwa a mâyi a mu Angleterre, udi ne tshiena bualu tshia ne: “Luendu lua kunyunguluka Cape Horn mu Mbuu wa ku Sud, bua tshipatshila tshia kualabaja mudimu wa diloba dia baleine ya Spermaceti.” Mu mukanda ewu mudi tshimfuanyi tshidi tshileja bu "tshimfuanyi tshia baleine wa Physeter anyi Spermaceti, muangatshila ku tshipiminu tshia umue uvuabu bashipe ku muelelu wa mâyi a mu Mexique, mu ngondo wa muanda mukulu, mu 1793, ne mubandisha pa mazuwa." Ndi ngela mpata ne: kapitene ke uvua muangate tshimfuanyi etshi tshia bushuwa bua diakalenga dia basalayi bende ba mu mayi to. Bua kuamba anu bualu bumue pa bidi bitangila bualu ebu, lekela ngambe ne: udi ne dîsu didi ditangila, bilondeshile tshipiminu tshidi tshilonda, kudi baleine wa mabele mukole, didi mua kuenza bua dîsu dia baleine au dikale dididishi dia nsongo dia bule bua metre matue ku atanu. Ah, kapitene wanyi wa dikima, bua tshinyi kuena mutupeshe Yona utangila ku dîsu adi!

Kabidi, mikanda ya malu a kale a bufuki idi ne budimu bua diakalenga dia bansonga ne batekete, kayena kayiyi ne bilema bia muomumue to. Tangila mukanda muende lumu wa "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." Mu mukanda muipi wa mu Londres wa mu 1807, mudi mabeji a "baleine" ne "narwhale." Tshiena musue kumueneka bu muntu udi kayi ne lumu to, kadi baleine eu udi kayi umueneka bimpe udi umueneka anu bu ngulube mukosa; ne, pa bidi bitangila narwhale, kumutangila anu kakese kudi kukumbana bua kukemesha muntu, bualu mu bidimu bia 1800 ebi, hippogriff wa nunku uvua mua kuikala mulelela kudi bantu bonso ba meji ba mu kalasa.

Pashishe, kabidi, mu 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, mumanyi munene wa malu a bufuki, wakapatula mukanda wa malu a sianse wa baleine, mudi bimfuanyi bia bungi bia mishindu mishilangane ya Leviathan. Bionso ebi kabiena anu bibi to, kadi tshimfuanyi tshia baleine wa Mysticetus anyi wa Greenland (mmumue ne: baleine wa ku dia balume), nansha Scoresby, muntu udi ne dimanya dia bungi dia kulenga mushindu eu, udi wamba ne: kena ne tshintu tshidi tshifuanangane nende mu bufuki to.

Ino diteka dia tshifulu tshia mudimu mu malu onso aa avua kaayi malelela divua anu bua mumanyi wa malu a sianse Frederick Cuvier, muanabu ne Baron muende lumu. Mu 1836, wakapatula mukanda wa Histoire naturelle des baleine, muvuaye mufile tshidiye ubikila ne: tshimfuanyi tshia baleine wa mabele. Kumpala kua kuleja tshimfuanyi etshi kudi muntu yonso wa mu Nantucket, mbimpe udilongolole bua kujikija lutetuku luebe lua mu Nantucket. Mu meyi makese, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale ki Sperm Whale to, kadi i squash. Bushuwa, kavua muanji kupeta diakalenga dia luendu lua kuloba baleine to (bantu ba nunku kabatu batamba kupeta to), kadi mmuaba kayi uvuaye mupete tshimfuanyi atshi, nnganyi udi mua kuamba? Pamuapa uvua mupeta bu mudi mumanyi wende wa malu a sianse mu mudimu umue umue au, Desmarest, mupete dimue dia ku mafu ende malelela; mmumue ne: ku tshimfuanyi tshia bena Chine. Ne mmunyi mudi bana ba balume ba muoyo ne crayon bena Chine aba, makopo ne bibombi bia bungi bidi bitumanyisha.

Bua baleine ya bafundi ba bimanyinu idibu bamona mu misesu milengeja pa mutu pa nzubu ya dipanyishila mafuta, ntshinyi tshidibu mua kuamba bua bualu buayi? Pa tshibidilu badi Richard III. baleine, ne nsongo ya dromedary, ne nyama ya luonji; kudia mu dinda pa mampa asatu anyi anayi a bena mazuwa, mbuena kuamba ne: mazuwa a baleine avua mawule tente ne bena mazuwa: bulema buabu buvua buenda buenda buenda mu mbuu ya mashi ne ya dipembu dia bule.

Kadi bilema bia bungi ebi mu dileja dia baleine kabiena bitukemesha to. Elabi meji! Bimfuanyi bia bungi bia malu a sianse mbiangatshila ku mushipa uvua mubuele mu mâyi; ne bintu ebi bidi anu bu mudi tshimfuanyi tshia mazuwa manyanguke, ne mukolo mutshibuka, tshidi mua kuleja bimpe nyama wa lumu nkayende mu lutambishi luende luonso lua mubidi ne biamu bia mvita. Nansha mudi ntambue mimane bua bule buayi buonso, Leviathan udi ne muoyo ki mmuanji kudibandisha bimpe bua tshimfuanyi tshiende to. Baleine udi ne muoyo, mu bunene buende buonso ne mu mushinga wende, udi anu mua kumueneka mu mbuu mu mâyi adi kaayi mua kujinguludibua; ne pa mayi tshitupa tshiende tshinene katshiena tshimueneka to, bu mazuwa a mvita adibu batume; ne mu tshintu atshi mbualu budi kabuyi mua kuenzeka kashidi bua muntu udi ufua kumubandisha mubidi wende mu lupepele, bua kulama mavuala ende onso ne mavuala ende onso. Kadi, katuyi tuakula bua dishilangana dinene didi pankatshi pa baleine udi unyunguluka ne Leviathan wa Platon mukole; kadi, nansha mu tshilumbu tshia umue wa ku bana ba baleine badi banyunguluka badibu babandishe ku tshibandilu tshia mazuwa, udi ne tshimuenekelu tshia pa buadi, tshidi tshifuanangane ne ngulube, tshidi ne mubidi mutekete, tshidi tshishilangane, tshidi diabolo nkayende kayi mua kukuata tshimuenekelu tshiende tshijalame to.

Ino bidi mua kuikala biumvuika, ne, ku mufuba wa baleine uvua mubuele mu tshina, tudi mua kupeta ngenyi milelela idi itangila tshimuenekelu tshiende tshilelela. Nansha kakese. Bualu budi bumue bua ku malu adi atamba kusankisha adi atangila Leviatana eu, ne: mufuba wende udi ufila lungenyi lukese lua mushindu udiye. Nansha mudi mufuba wa Jeremy Bentham, udi mu tshilaminu tshia mikanda tshia umue wa ku bashipi bende, udi uleja bimpe lungenyi lua muntu mukulakaje wa mpala mukole udi wenza mudimu ne bintu bia bungi, ne ngikadilu mikuabu yonso ya Jeremy; kadi kakuena bualu nansha bumue bua mushindu eu budibu mua kuela meji ku mifuba ya leviathan nansha umue to. Mu bulelela, anu mudi Mulobi munene wamba, mufuba wa baleine udi ne malanda a muomumue ne nyama udi ne bintu bionso ne udi ne bintu bia bungi anu mudi tshipupu tshienza ne chrysalis udi mumubuikile bimpe menemene. Bualu ebu budi bumueneka patoke mu mutu, anu mudibi bileja mu tshitupa kampanda tshia mukanda eu. Udi kabidi muleja mu mushindu wa dikema mu dikasa dia ku luseke, mifuba yadi idi mua kuandamuna anu ku mifuba ya tshianza tshia muntu, pa kumbusha anu tshianza tshinene. Dikasa edi didi ne minu inayi ya mifuba, munu wa tshianza, wa pankatshi, wa tshianza, ne munu mukese. Kadi bionso ebi mbilame kashidi mu tshibuikidiji tshiabu tshia mubidi, bu mudi minu ya muntu mu tshibuikidiji tshia bantu. "Nansha mudi baleine mua kutuenzela mudimu ne budimu buonso," ke muakamba Stubb uvua usekesha dimue dituku, "katuena mua kuamba ne: udi mua kutuenzela mudimu kayi ne miele ya bianza to."

Bua malu onso aa, nunku, mushindu kayi wonso uudi mua kumona, udi ne bua kufika ku diamba ne: Leviyatana munene ke tshifukibua tshimuepele tshia pa buloba tshidi ne bua kushala katshiyi tshilengeja too ne ku ndekelu. Bushuwa, tshimfuanyi tshimue tshidi mua kufika pabuipi menemene ne tshikuabu, kadi nansha umue kena mua kutshipeta ne bujalame bua bungi to. Nunku kakuena mushindu wa pa buloba wa kumanya menemene mudi baleine umueneka to. Ne mushindu umuepele uudi mua kupeta nansha lungenyi luakane lua nsombelu wende, nkuya wewe nkayebe ku diloba dia baleine; kadi paudi wenza nunku, udi mu njiwu mikese ya kuikala mu tshikuku tshia kashidi ne muina kudiye. Nunku, bidi bimueneka kundi ne: mbimpe wewe kubenga kuikala ne tshiji tshikole mu dijinga diebe dia kumanya malu adi atangila Leviatana eu.





SHAPITA 56. Wa bimfuanyi bia baleine bidi kabiyi ne bilema bia bungi, ne bimfuanyi bilelela bia malu a diloba dia baleine.

Mu diumvuangana ne bimfuanyi bia tshinyangu bia baleine, ndi mutetshibue bikole apa bua kubuela mu miyuki ya tshinyangu ya bungi ya baleine idibu mua kupeta mu mikanda kampanda, ya kale ne ya lelu, nangananga mu Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, ne bikuabu. Kadi ndi mpita bualu ebu.

Ndi mumanye anu mikanda inayi mipatula ya baleine munene wa Sperm; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, ne Beale's. Mu nshapita mupita Colnett ne Cuvier mbatele. Tshia Huggins ntshimpe kupita tshiabu; kadi, mu mishindu mikole, wa Beale ke udi mutambe buimpe. Bimfuanyi bionso bia Beale bia baleine eu mbimpe, pa kumbusha tshimfuanyi tshia munkatshi mu tshimfuanyi tshia baleine isatu idi ne ngikadilu mishilangane, idi ijikija nshapita wende muibidi. Tshimfuanyi tshienda tshia kumpala, mazuwa adi aluisha baleine ya sperm, nansha mudibi kabiyi mpata bua kujula dielakana dia bamue bantu ba mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi, tshidi tshijalame bikole ne tshidi tshifuanangane ne tshia muoyo mu tshienzedi tshiatshi tshijima. Bimue bia ku bimfuanyi bia Sperm Whale mu J. Ross Browne bidi bimpa mu tshimfuanyi; kadi badi bafunda mu mushindu mubi. Kadi ki mbualu buende to.

Pa baleine wa ku dia balume, binjinjimbi bitambe buimpe bidi mu Scoresby; kadi mbabifunde pa tshipiminu tshikese menemene bua kufila lungenyi luimpe. Udi anu ne tshimfuanyi tshimue tshia malu a diloba dia baleine, ne etshi ntshilema tshia dibungama, bualu anu ku bimfuanyi bia nunku, padibi bienza bimpe, ke muudi mua kupeta tshintu tshionso tshidi bu lungenyi lua bushuwa lua baleine udi ne muoyo bu mudibi bimueneka kudi balobi bende badi ne muoyo.

Ino, patudi tuangata bionso, bidi bitambe buimpe, nansha mudibi kabiyi biakane, bilejilu bia baleine ne bimfuanyi bia diloba dia baleine bidi mua kuikala muaba wonso, mbimfuanyi bibidi binene bia mu Mfualansa, bienza bimpe, ne biangatshila ku bimfuanyi bia Garnery kampanda. Mu bulongame, badi baleja dibunda dia Sperm ne Right Whale. Mu tshimfuanyi tshia kumpala, mbaleine wa butumbi udi muleja ne bukole buonso, mupatuke muinshi mua buatu mu mbuu, ne mumbule mu lupepele pa mukolo wende tshinyangu tshikole tshia mabaya a mu tshikuku. Luseke lua buatu kaluena lutshibuke to, ne ludi lukoka anu pa mukolo wa tshintu tshibi; ne muimane mu tshibandilu atshi, bua tshikondo tshimuepele tshidi katshiyi mua kubala, udi umona muendeshi wa buatu, tshitupa tshiende tshibuikila kudi mupuya wa baleine udi utema, ne mu tshienzedi tshia kutuluka, bienze anu bu udi ufumina mu tshibandabanda. Bienzedi bia bualu buonso bidi bilenga ne bilelela bia dikema. Tshina tshia mâyi tshidi katshiyi ne tshintu tshidi tshienda pa mbuu mutoke; mitshi ya bingoma bia bingoma bivua bibutuka bivua bienda binyanguka munda muawu; mitu ya bena mudimu ba kuowa mâyi idi mitangalake pa baleine mu mishindu mishilangane ya buôwa; padi mu bule bua tshipepele tshikole buatu buenda bupueka pa muaba au. Bilema binene bidi mua kupetekibua mu malu a mubidi wa baleine eu, kadi lekela bualu abu bupite; bualu, bua muoyo wanyi, tshivua mua kuzola tshimfuanyi tshimpe nunku to.

Mu tshimfuanyi tshibidi, buatu budi mu tshienzedi tshia kukoka ku luseke lua baleine munene wa ku dia balume udi unyema, udi wenda unyunguluka tshitupa tshiende tshifiike tshia lupela mu mbuu bu dibue dia nsongo didi difumina ku mabue a mu Patagonie. Ma jet ende adi mamane, maule tente, ne mafike bu lupuishi; nunku bua muishi wa bungi mu tshiibi tshia kapia, udi mua kuela meji ne: kudi ne bua kuikala biakudia bia dilolo bia dikima bidi biteka mu mala manene adi kuinshi. Nyunyi ya mu mbuu idi ilenga tu-crabe tukese, mushipa wa mbuji, ne bonbon mikuabu ya mu mbuu ne maccaroni, idi Baleine wa ku dia balume imue misangu umbula pa mukolo wende udi ne disama. Ne mu kitatyi kyonso leviathan udi ne mishiku minene udi wenda unyema mu mayi, ushiya tone ya mafuta matoke adi ne tshimvundu mu tshiadi tshiende, ne wenza bua buatu bukese buenda buenda mu mavuala bu buatu budibu bakuate pabuipi ne nkata ya buatu bua mu mbuu. Nunku, kumpala kudi tshimvundu tshikole; kadi kunyima, mu dishilangana dia dikema, mudi tshibandilu tshia mbuu tshidi tshitalala, mazuwa adi kaayi ne amidon adi apueka a mazuwa adi kaayi ne bukole, ne tshisumbu tshia baleine mufue, tshibumba tshidibu batshimune, ne dibendele dia dikuata didi dilengeja ku mutshi wa baleine mubueja mu tshina tshiende tshia mâyi.

Garnery mufundi wa bintu udi nganyi, anyi uvua nganyi, tshiena mumanye to. Kadi mu nsombelu wanyi uvua mumanye bimpe tshiena bualu tshiende, anyi uvua mulongeshibue bimpe kudi muena baleine kampanda uvua ne dimanya dia bungi. Bena France i bana ba balume ba malu a dizola. Ndaku utangile bimfuanyi bionso bia ku Mputu, ne mmuaba kayi uudi mua kupeta tshimfuanyi tshia bintu bidi ne muoyo ne bidi bipuyakana pa tshilamba, bu mudi mu nzubu wa ditshimuna wa ku Versailles; muaba udi mutangidi uluangana njila wende, pell-mell, mu mvita minene milondangane ya mu France; muaba udi muele wonso umueneka bu munya wa butoke bua ku Nord, ne bakalenge ne Bamfumu badi ne bia mvita badi balondangana badi benda lubilu, bu tshiluilu tshia centaurs badi ne tshifulu tshia butumbi anyi? Kabiena bikumbanyine muaba mu tshilaminu atshi to, bidi bintu bia mvita bia mu mbuu bia Garnery.

Bukole bua pa tshibidilu bua bena France bua kuangata bintu bilenga budi bumueneka bu bumueneka patoke mu bimfuanyi ne mu bimfuanyi bidibu nabi bia miaba yabu ya diloba dia baleine. Bu mudiku kakuyi tshia dikumi tshia dimanya dia bena Angleterre mu malu a diloba, ne kakuyi tshia tshinunu tshia bena Amerike, nansha nanku mbapeshe matunga onso abidi tshimfuanyi tshimuepele tshidibu bajikije kuenza tshidi mua kuleja lungenyi lulelela lua diloba dia baleine. Mu tshitupa tshinene, bafundi ba baleine ba mu Angleterre ne ba mu Amerike badi bamueneka basanka bikole bua kuleja tshimfuanyi tshia bintu, bu mudi tshimfuanyi tshia baleine tshidi katshiyi ne muntu; tshidi, pa bidi bitangila bulengele bua tshienzedi, tshidi tshifuanangane ne dizola dia tshimfuanyi tshia piramide. Nansha Scoresby, muena baleine wa ku dia balume, kunyima kua yeye mumane kutupesha bule bujima bua baleine wa mu Greenland, ne bimfuanyi bikese bisatu anyi binayi bia narwhales ne porpoises, udi utupesha mulongolongo wa bimfuanyi bia kale bia nshinga ya buatu, miele ya kukosa nabi, ne grapnels; ne na tshisumi tshia mu tshimfuanyi tshia Leuwenhoeck udi ufila ku dikonkonona dia buloba budi buenda buteketa, bimfuanyi makumi asambombo ne bisambombo bia mabue a neje a mu Arctique adibu bavudije. Nkikadi musue kupepeja muendeshi wa ngendu muimpe to (ndi mmunemeka bu muena mvita wa kale), kadi mu bualu bua mushinga mukole, bivua bushuwa bualu bubi bua kubenga kupeta bua dibue dionso mukanda wa ditshipa uvuabu bangate kumpala kua mulumbuluishi wa ditalala wa mu Greenland.

Nansha mudi bena sperm whalemen bikale ne malu kayi adi atangila dimona dia tshintu etshi, bidi bijadika ne: dimona dia tshintu etshi didi dia pa buadi, bualu ebu budi buenda bufika kule bua kutshibueja mu malu a dikema. Katuena tutamba kumueneka to, nansha mudi umue wa kudibu wamba ne: ntshintu tshinene tshidi ne muoyo mu mbuu, kadi bakese menemene badi ne lungenyi lua patupu pa bidi bitangila bumuntu ne mushindu wende mulelela; nansha nanku, badi bela meji ne: udi upesha baleine wa sperme biakudia biende tshimuepele. Bualu nansha mudi mishindu mikuabu ya baleine ipeta biakudia biayi pa mutu pa mâyi, ne idi mua kumueneka kudi muntu padiye udia, baleine wa spermaceti udi upeta biakudia biende bionso mu miaba idi kayiyi mimanyike muinshi mua mâyi; ne anu ku diambuluisha dia lungenyi ke mudi muntu yonso mua kuamba tshidi, menemene, biakudia abi. Misangu mikuabu, padibu bamulonda pabuipi, udi mua kupatula tshidibu bela meji ne: ntshia maboko a mushipa wa calmar; bamue ba kudibu bavua baleja bule bua metre mapite pa makumi abidi ne makumi asatu. Badi bela meji ne: tshintu tshinene tshivua ne maboko aa tshidi anu tshilamakaja ku tshina tshia mbuu; ne se: baleine wa mabele, bishilangane ne mishindu mikuabu, udi ne meno bua kumuluisha ne kumutapa.

Bidi bimueneka ne kudi bualu kampanda bua kuela meji ne: Kraken munene wa Muepiskopo Pontoppodan udi mua kulua Squid. Mushindu udi Muepiskopo umvuija, bu udi wenda ubanda ne upueka, ne amue malu adiye ulonda, mu bionso ebi bidi bipetangana. Kadi dipuekesha dia bungi didi dikengedibua bua bungi bua dikema budiye ufila.

Kudi bamue bamanyi ba malu a bufuki badi bumvue lumu lua tshifukibua tshia dikema, tshidibu bakula apa, tshidi tshibueja mu tshisumbu tshia mushipa wa cuttle-fish, udi, mu bulelela, mu malu kampanda a pambelu mua kumueneka bu udi watshi, kadi anu bu Anak wa tshisa.





SHAPITA 60. Mulongo.

Pa bidi bitangila tshikena kumona tshia diloba dia baleine tshidibu ne bua kumvuija mu katupa kîpi emu, ne kabidi bua kumvua bimpe malu onso a muomumue adibu baleje miaba mikuabu, ndi ne bua kuakula apa bua mulongo wa baleine wa majimbu, imue misangu wa buôwa.

Mulongo uvuabu benze nende mudimu ku ntuadijilu mu mudimu wa diloba uvua wa tshisuku tshimpe menemene, muenza ne goudron kakese, kayi muwuja, bu mudibi bua mionji ya patupu; bualu nansha mudibu benza mudimu ne tshiamu tshia goudron, tshidi tshienza bua tshisuku tshikale tshipepele kudi muenji wa mionji, ne tshidi kabidi tshienza bua tshingoma tshikale tshimpe kudi muendeshi wa buatu bua kuenza natshi mudimu mu mazuwa a pa tshibidilu; kadi, ki nganu bungi bua pa tshibidilu budi mua kukolesha mulongo wa baleine bua dikuata dia pabuipi didibu ne bua kuikala nadi to; kadi anu mudi bena mazuwa ba bungi batuadija kulonga, tshiamu tshia goudron katshiena tshivudija bukole anyi bukole bua nkamba to, nansha muditshi mua kutshipesha bukole ne butoke.

Mu bidimu bishale ebi, tshingoma tshia Manilla mu mudimu wa diloba wa mu Amerike ntshipingane pa muaba wa hemp bu tshintu tshia kuenza natshi mishipa ya baleine; bualu, nansha mudibi kabiyi bikole bu mudi tshisuku, tshidi tshikole, ne tshidi tshitamba butekete ne tshikole; ne nensakidile (bu mudiku æsthetics mu bintu bionso), udi mutambe buimpe ne udi ukumbaja buatu, kupita hemp. Hemp i tshintu tshifiike, tshifiike, mushindu wa muena Inde; kadi Manilla udi bu muena Circassia wa nsuki ya or bua kumumona.

Mulongo wa baleine udi anu ne bunene bua bitupa bibidi bia bisatu bia inch. Ku dimona dia kumpala, kuena mua kuela meji ne: udi ne bukole bu mudiye menemene to. Ku diambuluisha dia diteta, nshinga yende umue ne makumi atanu neyikale ne bujitu bua kilo lukama ne makumi abidi; bua tshingoma tshijima tshikale ne bujitu bua pabuipi ne tone isatu. Mu bule, mulongo wa baleine wa sperme udi ne bule bua metre nkama ibidi. Ku nyima kua buatu, udi unyunguluka mu tshina tshia mâyi, ki mbua tshintu tshidi tshienda tshinyunguluka to, kadi bua kuenza tshintu tshimue tshidi tshienza bu fromaje tshia “bipese” bia bungi, anyi bitupa bia bintu bidi bienza bu fromaje, kabiyi ne muaba nansha umue to, kadi anu “mutshima,” anyi tshintu tshikese tshidi tshienza ku tshitupa tshia fromaje. Bu mudi dikokangana dikese anyi dikoseka dia mu tshilamba mua kujika, mu dijika, mua kumbusha diboko dia muntu, dikasa anyi mubidi mujima, budimu bua bungi budi buenzeka padibu balama muonji mu tshina tshiawu. Bamue ba-harpoon nebapitshishe pabuipi ne dinda dijima mu mudimu eu, bambula muonji muulu ne pashishe bawukoka panshi kupitshila ku tshibumba tshitangile ku tshina tshia mâyi, nunku mu tshienzedi tshia kutshikosa bua kutshiumusha ku nsongo ne ku nsongo yonso idi mua kuikalaku.

Mu mazuwa a bena Angleterre batu benza mudimu ne bibombi bibidi pamutu pa tshimue; mulongo umua udi wenda unyunguluka mu bibombi bionso bibidi. Kudi diakalenga kampanda mu bualu ebu; bualu bua bipese ebi bidi bikese bidi mua kubuela mu buatu bipepele, ne kabitu bitamba kutshienzeja to; kadi, tshina tshia mâyi tshia bena Amerike, tshidi pabuipi ne metre asatu mu diamètre ne tshidi ne bunene bua muomumue, tshidi tshienza bujitu bua bungi bua mazuwa adi ne mabaya adi anu ne tshitupa tshia inch mu bunene; bualu bua kuinshi kua buatu bua baleine kudi bu neje mikole, idi mua kuambula bujitu bua bungi, kadi ki mbua bujitu bua bungi to. Padi tshibuikidiji tshia tshilamba tshifunda pa tshilamba tshia mu Amerike, buatu budi bumueneka bu budi buenda bumbuka ne mukate wa dibaka munene wa kupesha baleine.

Nsongo yonso ibidi ya mulongo idi mipatuke; tshitupa tshia kuinshi tshidi tshijika mu tshitupa tshia mêsu anyi tshilamba tshidi tshifumina kuinshi tshitangile ku luseke lua tshina tshia mâyi, ne tshidi tshilengeja ku muelelu watshi tshitapuluke ne tshintu tshionso. Dilongolola edi dia ndekelu wa kuinshi didi dikengedibua bua malu abidi. Tshia kumpala: Bua kupepeja dilamakaja dia muonji mukuabu wa buatu budi pabuipi, pikala baleine mushipa udi mua kuikala ne mutoyi wa muinshi mua buloba udi mua kutshinyisha bua kuangata muonji mujima uvua mulamakaja ku ntuadijilu ku tshingoma. Mu bikondo ebi, baleine udi bushuwa ushintuluka bu dikopo dia maluvu, bu mudibi, kumbukila ku buatu bumue too ne ku bukuabu; nansha mudi buatu bua kumpala buikala anu pabuipi bua kuambuluisha mukajende. Tshibidi: Dilongolola edi didi ne mushinga wa bungi bua bukubi bua bantu bonso; bualu bikala tshitupa tshia kuinshi tshia mulongo tshilamakaja ku buatu mu mushindu kayi wonso, ne bikala baleine mua kunyema mulongo too ne ku ndekelu pabuipi ne mu minite umue, udi ufuma muishi bu mudiye wenza imue misangu, kavua mua kuimana apu to, bualu buatu buvua ne bua kufua buvua mua kukoka panyima pende mu buondoke bua mbuu; ne mu bualu ebu kakuena muena musoko uvua mua kumupeta kabidi to.

Kumpala kua kupuekesha buatu bua kulonda, tshitupa tshia kuulu tshia mulongo tshidi tshiangatshibua kunyima kua tshina tshia mâyi, ne tshipita kunyunguluka tshibandilu tshia buatu, tshidi kabidi tshienda tshiya kumpala mu bule buonso bua buatu, tshiikisha mu nkuruse pa tshiamu tshia kukosa natshi buatu anyi pa tshianza tshia buatu bua muntu yonso, bua tshilue ku tshianza tshiende padiye wela buatu; ne kabidi kupitshila pankatshi pa bantu balume, padibu basombela ku bimanu bia bingoma bidi bishilangane, too ne ku bimanu bia plomb anyi ku milonda idi ku mutu kua buatu, muaba udi tshintu tshia mabaya anyi tshia tshiamu tshia mitaku tshia bunene bua tshintu tshia pa tshibidilu, tshidi tshipangisha bua kupatuka. Ku tshikokedi tshidi tshilengeja mu tshilamba tshikese pa mutu pa miele ya mvita, ne pashishe tshidi tshipita kabidi munda mua buatu; ne bule bua metre dikumi anyi makumi abidi (budibu babikila ne: box-line) budibu basuika pa kazubu mu mikete, budi butungunuka ne njila wabu too ne ku gunwale udi anu mutantshi mukese kunyima, ne pashishe budi bulamakaja ku short-warp—nshinga udibu balamakaja diakamue ne harpoon; kadi kumpala kua dipetangana edi, tshitupa tshîpi tshidi tshipita mu malu mashilangane adi kaayi umvuika adi kaayi umvuika to.

Nunku mulongo wa baleine udi unyunguluka buatu bujima mu mishindu yabu mikole, wenda unyunguluka ne unyunguluka pabuipi ne mu nseke yonso. Bantu bonso badi benda buatu badi baditua mu malu ende adi ne njiwu; nunku ku dîsu dia buôwa dia muena buloba, badi bamueneka bu bena India badi benza manaya a mvita, ne nyoka ya lufu idi milengeja bitupa biabu bia mubidi. Kabidi muana nansha umue wa mukaji udi ufua, bua musangu wa kumpala, kena mua kusomba munkatshi mua bintu bia nsongo ebi, ne padiye wenza mudimu mukole bua kuela buatu, kuela meji ne: mu tshikondo kayi tshionso tshidi katshiyi tshimanyike, tshingoma tshidi mua kuikala tshituta, ne malu onso aa mabi adi mua kuenzeka bu mipenyi idi ne tshijengu; kena mua kuikala mu nsombelu wa nunku kayi ne dikankamika didi dienza bua mufuba wa mu mifuba yende uteketa munda muende bu jeli mukankamijibue. Kadi tshibidilu—tshintu tshia dikema! ntshinyi tshidi tshilele katshiyi mua kukumbaja?—Malu a disanka, masanka a bungi, tuseku tuimpe, ne diandamuna dimpe, kuyi muanji kumvua pa mutu pa mutshi webe wa mahogany, kupita muudi mua kumvua pa mutu pa mutshi wa sedre mutoke wa tshitupa tshia santimetre wa buatu bua baleine, padibu bamulengeja nunku mu mionji ya muena tshibawu; ne, pamo bwa bena mu Calais basambombo kumpala kua Mukalenge Edward, bantu basambombo badi mu tshisumbu tshia mazuwa badi bakoka mu mishiku ya lufu, ne tshilamba tshia ku nshingu yonso, anu muudi mua kuamba.

Pamuapa lungenyi lukese ludi mua kukuambuluisha bua kufila tshijadiki tshia bipupu bia diloba dia baleine misangu ne misangu—bimue bia kudibi mbifunda mu tshikoso—bia muntu eu anyi muntu au uvuabu bumbushe mu buatu kudi muonji, ne mujimine. Bualu, padi mulongo wenda upatuka, bua kuikala musombe mu buatu, bidi anu bu kuikala musombe munkatshi mua mutoyi wa mashinyi a muishi udi wenza mudimu bimpe, padi tshiamu tshionso tshidi tshienda tshibuuka, ne tshiamu tshionso, ne nkata, bidi bikudia. Bidi bibi menemene; bualu bua kuena mua kusomba kayi unyema mu mutshima wa njiwu eyi, bualu bua buatu budi buenda buenda bu tshitanda, ne udi muteka mu njila umue ne mukuabu, kakuyi didimuija nansha dikese; ne anu ku diambuluisha dia tshisumi kampanda tshia kudilongolola ne dienza dia malu musangu umue, udi mua kuepuka bua kuikala Mazeppa, ne kunyema muaba udi dîba didi dimona bionso nkayadi kadiyi mua kukutapa nansha kakese.

Musangu eu kabidi: bu mudi ditalala dikole didi anu dimueneka kumpala kua ne dimanyisha dia tshipepele tshikole, didi pamuapa dibi kupita tshipepele tshikole nkayatshi; bualu, bushuwa, ditalala didi anu tshibuikidiji ne tshibuikidiji tshia tshipepele tshikole; ne udi munda muende, bu mudi tshingoma tshidi tshimueneka katshiyi ne bualu tshidi tshikuata lupuishi lua lufu, ne mupimbu, ne dibutuka; nunku dikisha dimpe dia mulongo, bu mudiwu wenda unyunguluka bena buatu kumpala kua kuwubueja mu dinaya dilelela—etshi ntshintu tshidi ne buôwa bua bungi kupita bualu bukuabu buonso bua njiwu ebu. Kadi bua tshinyi kuamba bia bungi? Bantu bonso badi basombele mu milongo ya baleine. Bantu bonso badi baledibue ne bilamba mu nshingu yabu; kadi anu padibu bakuatshibue mu lufu lua lukasa, lua tshimpitshimpi, ke padi bantu bafua bajingulula njiwu ya muoyo idi kayiyi ne dîyi, idi kayiyi imueneka patoke ne idiku misangu yonso. Ne shi udi mumanyi wa malu a nkindi, nansha muudi musombe mu buatu bua baleine, kuena mua kumvua buôwa nansha bumue, kupita muudi musombe kumpala kua kapia kebe ka dilolo ne tshingoma, kadi ki ntshingoma to, ku luseke luebe.
