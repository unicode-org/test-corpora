SHAPITA 130. Tshifulu.

Ne mpindieu mu tshikondo ne muaba muimpe, kunyima kua luendu lua pa mâyi lule ne lua bungi, Ahaba,—mâyi makuabu onso a diloba dia baleine—uvua umueneka bu uvua muipate muena lukuna wende mu tshikumbi tshia mbuu, bua kumushipa bimpe menemene kuntu aku; mpindieu, uvua mudimone mukole ku latitude ne ku longitude muvuabu bamuenzele mputa wende wa makenga; Mpindieu bu muvuabu bakula bua buatu buvua bushuwa bupetangana ne Moby Dick dituku dia kumpala;—ne mpindieu bu muvua bisangilu biende bionso bivua bilondangana ne mazuwa mashilangane bivua biumvuangane bua kuleja dipanga dia lungenyi dia bademon divua baleine mutoke utapa bapote bende, nansha yeye muenze mpekatu anyi muenze mpekatu kudiye; Mpindieu bivua ne: kuvua tshintu kampanda tshivua tshisokome mu mêsu a mununu, tshivua tshikole bua mioyo mitekete kumona. Bu mudi mutoto wa polaire udi kauyi ujika, udi mu tshibuashibuashi tshia buloba bujima, butuku bua ngondo isambombo ulama mêsu ende adi atapa, adi kaayi ashintuluka, adi pankatshi; nunku tshipatshila tshia Ahaba tshiakamueneka patoke pavua bena mudimu ba mu buatu bavua ne kanyinganyinga benza mudimu wa munkatshi mua butuku. Bivua bikokesha pa mutu pabu nunku, ne lungenyi luabu luonso, dielakana diabu, dielakana diabu, buôwa buabu, bivua mua kusokoma muinshi mua mioyo yabu, ne kabiyi bipatuka difuma dimue anyi dibeji dimue.

Mu tshikondo etshi tshia tshilejelu kabidi, tuseku tuonso, tua kuenzeja anyi tua ku tshilelelu, tuakajimina. Muomumue, disanka ne kanyinganyinga, ditekemena ne buôwa, bivua bimueneka bu bivua bibuikila too ne ku lupuishi lunene, ne lupuishi, bua tshikondo atshi, mu tshibumba tshikole tshia anyima wa tshiamu wa Ahaba. Anu bu mashinyi, bavua bendakana pa tshibandilu, bamanye ne: dîsu dia tshikisu dia mununu divua dibatangila.

Kadi uvuaku mumukonkonone bikole mu mêba ende a tshisokomenu anyi; pavuaye wela meji ne: kakuvua tshimuenenu to, anu tshimue tshivua tshimutangile; pashishe uvua mua kuikala mumone ne: nansha muvua mêsu a Ahaba akemesha bena mudimu ba mu buatu, mêsu a muena Parsee uvua kayi mua kumvua avua akemesha ende; anyi mu mushindu kampanda, nansha, mu mushindu kampanda wa tshinyangu, imue misangu uvua umulenga.
