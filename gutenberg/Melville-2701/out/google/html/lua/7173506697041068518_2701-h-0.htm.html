Nunku, kuena ne bua kuangata, nansha mu nsombelu yonso, mêyi a baleine a higgledy-piggledy, nansha wowu malelela mushindu kayi, mu bitupa ebi, bua cetologie wa lumu luimpe mulelela. Kule ne bualu abu. Pa bidi bitangila bafundi ba kale mu tshibungi, ne kabidi bafundi ba tusala badi bamueneka muaba eu, bitupa ebi bidi anu ne mushinga anyi bidi bisankisha, bu mudibi bifila lungenyi lua nyunyi lua malu adibu bambe, bela meji, bafuanyikije ne bimba bua Leviathan, kudi bisamba bia bungi ne bipungu bia bungi, nansha bietu.

Nunku wikale bimpe, diabolo mupele wa Sub-Sub, undi mumanyishi wende. Udi wa mu tshisa tshidi katshiyi ne ditekemena, tshitoke tshidi mvinyo nansha umue wa pa buloba ebu mua kuikala ne luya; ne nansha Pale Sherry uvua mua kuikala ne bukole bua bungi; kadi muntu utu misangu mikuabu musue kusomba nende, ne kudiumvua bu mupele-diabolo, kabidi; ne kukola mu buobumue pa binsonji; ne kubambila patoke, ne mêsu maule tente ne makalashi a tshianana, ne mu dibungama didi kadiyi dimpe menemene ne: Lekelayi, Sub-Subs! Bualu bua bungi bua malu anudi nuenza bua kusankisha ba pa buloba, nenuikale batamba kuikala kabayi ne dianyisha kashidi! Bu meme mua kujikija Hampton Court ne Tuileries bua nuenu! Kadi nupuekeshe binsonji bienu ne nubandile ku tshibumba tshia bukalenge ne mitshima yenu; bualu bua balunda bebe bakadi baye kumpala badi basukula diulu dia bisasa muanda mutekete, ne benza bena tshimuangi ba Gabaliele, Mikaele ne Rafaele badibu banange matuku a bungi, bua kuluisha dilua diebe. Apa nudi nukuma anu mitshima mipanduluke pamue—kuoku aku, nenukuate makalashi adi kaayi mua kutshibuka!
