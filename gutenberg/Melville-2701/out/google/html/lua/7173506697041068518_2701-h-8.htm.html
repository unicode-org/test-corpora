Ne, pavuaye unyema mu latitude mikole, buatu, ne mishipa mipepele mitangalake, buakapita mu matunga a mashika, ne, ku tshimuenekelu tshionso, dipanga dia lungenyi dia muntu mununu divua dimueneka bu divua dishale panyima pende ne bipupu bia Cape Horn, ne wakapatuka mu tshina tshiende tshia mîdima mu butoke ne mu lupepele lua dibenesha; nansha dîba adi, pavuaye muambule tshintu atshi tshikole, musangishe kumpala, nansha muvuatshi tshitoke, ne mupatule kabidi mêyi ende a ditalala; ne balunda bende bakela Nzambi tuasakidila bualu bupote bubi buvua mpindieu bujike; nansha dîba adi, Ahaba, mu bumuntu buende busokoka, wakatungunuka ne kuela mbila. Bupote bua bantu budi misangu ya bungi bualu bua budimu ne bua mpuku. Paudi wela meji ne: uvua munyeme, uvua mua kuikala anu mushintuluke mu mushindu kampanda uvua kauyi umueneka patoke to. Bupote buonso bua Ahaba kabuakajika to, kadi buakakola bikole; bu mudi Hudson udi kayi mupuekele, padi muntu wa ku Nord wa lumu eu upitshila mu tshibandabanda tshia Highland mu mushindu mutekete, kadi mu mushindu udi kauyi mua kumvuika. Ino, pamo bwa mu monomanie wandi wa tshitupa tshîpi, kakuvua tshitupa nansha tshimue tshia bupote bunene bua Ahaba tshivua tshishale panyima to; nunku mu bupote bunene abu, kakuvua tshitupa nansha tshimue tshia meji ende manene a ku tshilelelu tshivua tshijimine to. Tshintu atshi kumpala kua tshintu tshidi ne muoyo, mpindieu ntshilue tshiamu tshia mudimu tshidi ne muoyo. Shi tshishima tshia nunku tshidi mua kuimana, bupote buende bua pa buabu buakanyanga lungenyi luenda, ne buakambula, ne buakakudimuna bingoma biatshi bionso pa tshimanyinu tshiatshi tshia bupote; nunku kule ne kujimija bukole buende, Ahaba, ku tshipatshila tshimue atshi, uvua mpindieu ne bukole misangu tshinunu kupita muvuaye mua kuikala nabu ne lungenyi luonso pa tshintu nansha tshimue tshia meji.

Etshi ntshivule; kadi tshitupa tshinene, tshifiike, tshikole tshia Ahaba katshiena tshileja to. Kadi mbia tshianana bua kumanyisha malu a muinshi mua buloba, ne bulelela buonso budi ne mushinga wa bungi. Kupueka kule menemene munda mua nzubu wa tshilala benyi wa Cluny mutudi bimane apa—nansha biobi bikale binene ne bia dikema, mpindieu lekelayi;—ne wangate njila wenu, nuenu bantu banene, badi ne kanyinganyinga, mu nzubu minene ya bena Lomo ya Thermes; muaba udi kule muinshi mua bimanu bia dikema bia buloba bua mulu bua muntu, muji wende wa bunene, bumuntu buende buonso bubi budi busombe mu nsombelu wa ndevu; tshintu tshia kale tshijika muinshi mua bintu bia kale, ne tshiteka pa mifuba! Nunku ne nkuasa wa bukalenge mutshibuka, nzambi minene idi iseka mukalenge au mupika; nunku bu mudi Caryatid, mubedi udi musombe, mukuate pa mpala wende udi ne mashika bintu bia bungi bia kale. Nupuekele kuntu aku, nuenu bantu badi ne lutambishi ne badi ne kanyinganyinga! lukonku lua mukalenge wa lutambishi, wa kanyinganyinga! Difuanangana dia dîku! Eyowa, wakanulela, nuenu bansonga badibu bakuate mu bupika; ne anu kudi tatuebe mubi ke kudi mua kulua bualu busokoka bua kale bua ditunga.

Mpindieu, mu mutshima wende, Ahaba uvua ne ndambu wa lungenyi lua bualu ebu, mbuena kuamba ne: bintu bianyi bionso bidi bimpe, tshipatshila tshianyi ne tshintu tshianyi ntshia bupote. Kadi kayi ne bukole bua kushipa, anyi kushintulula, anyi kuepuka bualu ebu; yeye pende uvua mumanye ne: uvua mudishima kudi bantu katshia ku kale; mu mushindu kampanda, uvua anu muenze. Kadi bualu bua dishima diende buvua anu bumanyine pa dijingulula diende dia malu, ki mbua disua diende to. Nansha nanku, wakakumbaja bimpe menemene mu dishima adi, ke bualu kayi pakafikaye ku muelelu wa mâyi ne dikasa dia meno a ntambue, muntu nansha umue wa mu Nantucketer kavua wela meji ne: uvua anu ne kanyinganyinga ka pa tshibidilu, ne bualu ebu bua lukasa, ne njiwu mikole ivua mimukuate.

Luapolo lua dipanga dia lungenyi luende mu mbuu luvua kabidi luamba kudi bantu ba bungi bua bualu bukuabu. Ne nanku kabidi, malu onso avua mavudijibue avua misangu yonso pashishe, too ne ku dituku dia diendesha mazuwa mu Pequod mu luendu lua mpindieu, avua masombe wela meji pa mpala wende. Kabiena kabidi mua kuikala nanku to, bua se: kule ne kubenga kueyemena dikumbana diende bua luendu lukuabu lua diloba dia baleine, bua bimanyinu bia mîdima bia nunku, bantu bavua ne lungenyi lua bungi ba mu tshidila atshi tshia budimu bavua basue kuikala ne lutambishi, ne: bua malu au mene uvua mukumbane bimpe ne uvua muteka pa muaba, bua mudimu uvua muwule tente ne tshiji ne tshikisu bu mudi diloba dia baleine dia mashi. Kudia munda ne kuosha pambelu, ne meno adi kaayi ajika a lungenyi kampanda ludi kaluyi mua kuondopibua; muntu wa nunku, yeye mua kuikala mupeta, uvua mua kumueneka bu muntu uvua mua kuela tshiamu tshiende tshia mitaku ne kubandisha muele wende wa mvita bua kuluisha nyama mibi menemene. Peshi, shi bua bualu kampanda badi bela meji ne: kena ne makanda a mubidi bua bualu abu, kadi muntu wa nunku udi mua kumueneka muikale ne bukole bua bungi bua kusankisha ne kuela mbila kudi bena mudimu bende bua kumuluisha. Kadi nansha biobi bikale bionso ebi, bidi bimueneka patoke ne: ne tshisokomenu tshia tshiji tshiende tshidi katshiyi tshijika tshivuaye mubuikile, Ahaba uvua muenze luendu elu ne tshipatshila tshimuepele tshia kuloba baleine mutoke. Bu muntu nansha umue wa ku bamanyangana nende ba kale uvua ku muelelu wa mâyi mulote tshitupa tshia tshivua tshimusokome tshikondo atshi, mmunyi muvua mioyo yabu mikole ne milulame mua kunyanga mazuwa kudi muntu wa tshikisu nunku! Bavua badisuike bua kuenza ngendu ya pa mâyi ivua ifila makuta, makuta avua ne bua kubadibua mu dolare ya mu tshisalu tshia makuta. Uvua ne lungenyi lua kudisombuela ne dikima, dia tshianana, ne dia pa mutu pa buloba.

Nunku, apa ke muvua muntu mukulakaje eu uvua ne mpala mitoke, uvua kayi utshina Nzambi, wenda ulonda ne milawu baleine wa Yobo pa buloba bujima, ku mutu kua tshisumbu tshia bena mudimu, kabidi, tshienza nangananga ne bena tshinyangu, ne bena tshinyangu, ne bantu bavua badia bantu—bavua kabidi batekete mu bikadilu, bua dipanga dia bumuntu bua patupu anyi bua dipanga dia lungenyi lua Starll, dipanga dia lungenyi ne dipanga dia lungenyi mu Stubb, ne bupote budi butangalake mu Flask. Tshisumbu tshia nunku, tshilombola nunku, tshivua tshimueneka tshisungula ne tshiuwule tente ne lufu kampanda lua inferno bua kumuambuluisha ku disombuela diende dia monomaniac. Mushindu kayi uvuabu bandamuna bikole ku tshiji tshia mununu au—ku diambuluisha dia majimbu mabi kayi avua mioyo yabu mikuate, ne imue misangu lukuna luende luvua lumueneka pabuipi ne luabu; Baleine Mutoke udi muluishi wabu udi kayi mua kutantamena bu wende; mushindu kayi uvua bionso ebi bilue—tshivua Baleine Mutoke kudibu, anyi mushindu kayi uvuabu bumvue malu abu kabayi bamanye, kabidi, mu mushindu kampanda mutoke, uvuabu kabayi bamanye, uvua mua kuikala umueneka bu demon munene uvua wenda unyunguluka wa mu mbuu ya muoyo,—bionso ebi bua kumvuija, bivua mua kuikala bua kuditua muinshi menemene kupita muvua Ishmael mua kuya. Muena mudimu wa muinshi mua buloba udi wenza mudimu munda muetu tuetu bonso, mmunyi mudi muntu mua kumanya kudiye ulombola tshingoma tshiende ku mutoyi wa tshintu tshiende tshidi tshienda tshishintuluka ne tshidi katshiyi tshiumvuika? Nnganyi udi kayi umvua dikoka dia diboko didi kadiyi mua kukandika? Ntshinyi tshia buatu tshidi tshikoka makumi muanda mutekete ne inayi tshidi mua kuimana? Tshia kumpala, ngakadifila ku dilekela dia tshikondo ne muaba; kadi patshivua bantu bonso mu lubilu bua kusambakena ne baleine, kabavua bamone tshintu nansha tshimue mu nyama au to, anu disama dia lufu.





SHAPITA 42. Butoke bua baleine.

Tshivua baleine mutoke kudi Ahaba, ntshileja; tshivuaye, imue misangu, kundi, katshiena tshianji kuamba to.

Pa kumbusha malu adi amueneka patoke adi atangila Moby Dick, adi kaayi mua kujula misangu mikuabu buôwa mu anyima wa muntu nansha umue, kuvua lungenyi lukuabu, anyi buôwa budi kabuyi bumueneka patoke, budi kabuyi ne dîna buvua bumutangila, buvua imue misangu ku bukole buabu bupita bakuabu bonso bikole; kadi nansha nanku bivua bia tshishima ne bivua pabuipi ne kuikala kabiyi mua kuamba, ke bualu kayi ndi pabuipi ne kujimija ditekemena dia kubiteka mu mushindu udi mua kumvuika. Butoke bua baleine ke buvua buntonda bikole. Kadi mmunyi mundi mua kutekemena bua kudiumvuija apa; ne nansha nanku, mu mushindu kampanda mubi, wa mpukapuka, ndimvuije ndi ne tshia, tshianana nshapita yonso eyi idi mua kuikala tshianana.

Nansha mudibi mu bintu bia bungi bia ku bufuki, butoke budi bulengeja bulengele, bienze anu bu budi bufila buimpe kampanda bua pa buabu, bu mudi mu mabue a marbre, a japonica ne a mabue a busanga; ne nansha mudi bisamba bishilangane bitaba mu mushindu kampanda bumfumu kampanda bua bukalenge mu mushindu eu; nansha bakalenge ba kale ba tshinyangu, banene ba mu Pegu bavua bateka muanzu wa “Mfumu wa Nzolo Mitoke” ku mutu kua miaku yabu mikuabu yonso ya bumfumu; ne bakalenge ba matuku etu aa ba mu Siam bavua bambula tshintu tshimue tshimue tshitoka tshia neje mu tshibandilu tshia bukalenge; ne dibendele dia Hanover didi ne tshimfuanyi tshimue tshia tshiamu tshitoke tshia neje; ne bukalenge bunene bua Autriche, Cæsarian, mupianyi wa bukalenge bua Lomo, uvua ne dikala dia bukalenge dia muomumue; ne nansha mudi bunene ebu butangila tshisamba tshia bantu nkayabu, budi bupesha muena mutoke bumfumu buimpe pa tshisa tshionso tshia mîdima; ne nansha mudibi, pa kumbusha bionso ebi, butoke budi mene buvuijibue ne mushinga wa disanka, bualu munkatshi mua bena Lomo dibue ditoke divua dileja dituku dia disanka; ne nansha mudibi mu luse lukuabu lua bantu badi bafua ne mu bilejilu, tshimuenekelu tshimue etshi tshidi tshivuijibua tshimanyishilu tshia bintu bia bungi bidi bilenga ku muoyo, bia butumbi—dibenga kuikala ne bualu dia bakaji badi basela, bulenga bua bidimu; nansha muvua munkatshi mua bantu bafike ba mu Amerike difila dia mukaba mutoke wa wampum divua tshijadiki tshinene tshia buneme; nansha mudibi mu miaba ya bungi, butoke budi buleja bunene bua Buakane mu ermine wa Mulumbuluishi, ne budi bufila diambuluisha ku nsombelu wa ku dituku ne ku dituku wa bakalenge ne bakalenge bakaji bakoka kudi tubalu tutoke tua mabele; nansha mu malu masokoka manene a bitendelelu bia mushinga mukole, mbavuije tshimanyishilu tshia dipanga ditoba ne bukole bua Nzambi; kudi batendeledi ba kapia ba bena Pelasa, lubimbi lua kapia lutoke luvuabu bangata bu lua tshijila pa tshioshelu; ne mu mianu ya bena Gelika, Jove Munene nkayende uvua muvuijibue mu mubidi wa ngombe mutoke bu neje; ne nansha muvua kudi bena Iroquois banene, mulambu wa tshijila wa Mbwa Mutoke uvua tshibilu tshitambe tshijila tshia teoloji wabu, tshifukibua atshi tshivua katshiyi ne kalema, tshia lulamatu tshivua tshiangatshibua bu mutumibue mutambe buimpe uvuabu mua kutuma kudi Nyuma Munene ne lumu lua tshidimu tshionso lua lulamatu luabu nkayabu; ne nansha mudibi bifumina buludiludi ku muaku wa mu Latin udi umvuija mutoke, bakuidi bonso bena Kristo badi bapeta dina dia tshitupa tshimue tshia bilamba biabu bia tshijila, alb anyi tunic, bivuala muinshi mua tshilamba tshia ku makasa; ne nansha mudibi munkatshi mua bintu bia tshijila bia ditabuja dia bena Lomo, tshitoke tshidi tshienza mudimu nangananga mu difesto dia dikenga dia Mukelenge wetu; nansha mudi mu tshikena kumona tshia Mukelenge Yone, bilamba bitoke bidi bipeshibua kudi bapikudibue, ne bakulu makumi abidi ne banayi badi bimane bavuale bilamba bitoke kumpala kua nkuasa munene mutoke, ne Wa Tshijila udi musombe muaba au mutoke bu miosa ya mikoko; kadi nansha mudi malanda onso aa masangisha, ne tshionso tshidi tshimpe, ne tshia kanemu, ne tshia mushinga mukole, kudi anu tshintu tshidi katshiyi tshiumvuika mu lungenyi lua munda lua dikala edi, tshidi tshienza buôwa bikole ku anyima kupita dikala dikunze didi dienza buôwa mu mashi.

Ngikadilu ewu udi kayi umueneka, udi wenza bua lungenyi lua butoke, padilu lutapuluke ne balunda ba bulenga, ne lusangisha ne tshintu tshionso tshidi tshibi menemene, bua kubandisha buôwa abu too ne ku mikalu ya kule menemene. Tangila urse mutoke wa ku mikuna, ne requin mutoke wa mu matunga a mashika; ntshinyi tshidi tshibavuija bantu ba buôwa badibu? Butoke bua dikema abu ke budi bufila bupole bubi menemene, budi mene bupite bubi menemene, ku dianyisha dia tshianana dia tshimuenekelu tshiabu. Bua ntambue wa mênu makole udi muvuale bilamba biende bia tshijila kayi mua kutekesha dikima bu urse anyi requin udi ne tshilamba tshitoke to.*

*Pa bidi bitangila urse wa polaire, bidi mua kuikala bilomba kudi muntu udi musue kuya munda menemene mu bualu ebu, ne: ki mbutoke, budibu bangata pa nkayabu, budi buvudija bubi budi kabuyi mua kuitaba bua nyama wa luonji au to; bualu, mu dikonkonona, bualu bua bubi budi buvula, tudi mua kuamba ne, budi anu bufumina ku nsombelu, ne: tshikisu tshia tshifukibua tshidi katshiyi ne bujitu tshidi tshiimane mu miosa ya dipanga dia bubi ne dinanga dia mu diulu; ne nunku, pa kusangisha ngenyi ibidi mishilangane mu lungenyi luetu, urse wa polaire udi ututshinyisha ne dishilangana didi kadiyi dia pa tshibidilu. Kadi nansha tuetu tuela meji ne: bionso ebi mbilelela; kadi, bu kabiyi bua butoke, kuvua mua kuikala ne buôwa bukole abu to.

Pa bidi bitangila requin mutoke, tshimuenekelu tshitoka tshia dikisha mu tshifukibua atshi, paditshi tshimueneka mu nsombelu wende wa pa tshibidilu, tshidi tshipetangana ne ngikadilu wa muomumue ne wa Polar quadruped. Bualu ebu budi bumueneka patoke kudi bena France mu dina didibu bapesha mushipa au. Misa wa bena Lomo bua bafue udi utuadija ne "Requiem eternam" (dikisha dia kashidi), ke mudi Requiem uleja misa nkayayi, ne mijiki mikuabu yonso ya madilu. Mpindieu, mu dimanyisha dia lufu lutoke, lua mupuwe lua mu requin eu, ne lufu lutekete lua bibidilu biende, bena France badi bamubikila ne: Requin .

Ela meji bua nyunyi wa albatros, mmuaba kayi udi matutu a dikema a mu nyuma ne buôwa bua patupu afumina, mudi tshishima tshitoke atshi tshienda mu lungenyi luonso? Coleridge ke uvua muanji kuela tshishima atshi to; kadi tshipedi tshinene tshia Nzambi, tshidi katshiyi ne kanemu, Bufuki.*

*Ndi mvuluka albatros wa kumpala umvua mumone. Bivua mu tshikondo tshia tshipepele tshikole, mu mayi makole pa mbuu ya Antarctique. Kumbukila ku tshikebelu tshianyi tshia kumpala kua midi kuinshi, ngakabanda ku tshibandilu tshivua ne matutu; ne kuntu aku, muele pa biibi binene, ngakamona tshintu tshia bukalenge, tshia mapuapua tshia butoke kabuyi ne ditoba, ne tshivua ne mpala mulengeja, wa bena Lomo muimpe be. Imue misangu, uvua wela mapuapua ende manene a banjelu munene, bienze anu bu uvua musue kuambula mushete kampanda wa tshijila. Dikuma dia dikema ne dikuma diakatshinyunguluka. Nansha muvuaye kayi mutapike ku mubidi, uvua wela mbila, bu nyuma wa mukalenge kampanda uvua mu dikenga dia pa buadi. Ku diambuluisha dia mêsu ende adi kaayi mua kuleja, a dikema, meme kuela meji ne: mvua mutangile malu masokoka avua makuate Nzambi. Bu muvua Abalahama kumpala kua banjelu, ngakatua binu panshi; tshintu tshitoke tshivua tshitoke bikole, mapuapua atshi avua maalabale bikole, ne mu mâyi au avua manyanguke kashidi, mvua mujimije malu mabi a bilele ne a misoko. Mutantshi mule ngakatangila tshintu tshia dikema atshi tshia mapapa. Nkibwanyapo kuamba, ndi mua kufila anu lungenyi, bintu bivua bipita munda muanyi tshikondo atshi. Kadi ndekelu wa bionso ngakajuka; ne wakakudimuka, kuebeja muendeshi wa buatu ne: nyunyi kayi eu. A goney, yeye kuandamuna. Uye! tshivua muanji kumvua dina adi to; bidi mua kuenzeka ne: tshintu tshia butumbi etshi katshiena tshimanyike kudi bantu badi ku muelelu wa mâyi to! nansha kakese! Kadi matuku makese pashishe, ngakamanya ne: goney uvua dîna dia albatros dia muena mazuwa kampanda. Nunku, kakuena mushindu nansha umue wa Coleridge's wild Rhyme uvua mua kuikala ne bualu nansha bumue ne malu a tshishima avua anyi, pamvua mumone nyunyi au pa tshibandilu tshietu. Bualu nansha meme tshivua muanji kubala Rhyme, anyi mumanye ne: nyunyi au uvua albatross to. Ino, mu ngamba bino, ndi ngenza anu mu mushindu udi kauyi mujalame bua kutokesha kakese diakalengele dia tusala ne dia mufundi wa tusala.

Ndi ngamba, nunku, ne mu butoke bua mubidi wa nyunyi mudi nangananga tshisokomenu tshia tshishima; bulelela budi bumueneka patoke mu bualu ebu, ne: ku diambuluisha dia miaku misunguluke kudi nyunyi idibu babikila ne: albatrosses gris; ne bintu ebi ndi mubimone misangu ya bungi, kadi tshitu muanji kumona ne lungenyi lua nunku pamvua mumone nyunyi wa mu Antarctique to.

Kadi mmunyi muvuabu bakuate tshintu tshia tshishima atshi? Kuedi mutoyi to, nennuambile; ne tshingoma tshia dishima ne muonji, bu mudi nyunyi wenda pa mbuu. Ndekelu wa bionso Kapitene wakatshienza bu muena poste; kusuika maleta a tshiseba mu nshingu wende, ne dîba ne muaba wa mazuwa; ne pashishe kumulekela bua kunyema. Ino nkidipo mpata amba, kintu kya kikoba, kya bantu, kyabalwa mu Diulu, padi nyunyi mutoke mubuuka mwanda wa kwisambakena na mapuapua, a kulomba, ne a kutendelela bakeluba!

Tshidi tshimanyike bikole mu mikanda yetu ya ku Ouest ne mu bilele bia bena Inde ntshia kabalu ka White Steed ka mu mpata; tshitekelu tshilenga tshitoke bu mabele, tshia mesu manene, tshia mutu mukese, tshiadi tshikole, ne tshidi ne bunême bua bakalenge tshinunu mu ditempu diende dinene ne dia tshikisu. Uvua Xerxes musungula wa bisumbu binene bia tubalu tua muitu, bivua madimi abu mu matuku au avua anu makuba kudi mikuna ya mabue ne mikuna ya Alleghanies. Ku mutu wabu wa kapia wakaya ku ouest bu mutoto musungula udi ulombola dilolo dionso pa biluilu bia butoke. Dibue dia nsuki yende didi dikenka, mukila wende udi unyunguluka, biakamupesha nzubu mimpe kupita muvua ba-beateur ba or ne arjan mua kumupesha. Tshimuenekelu tshia bumfumu ne tshia banjelu banene tshia buloba bua ku ouest budi kabuyi bukuluke, buvua ku mêsu kua bapote ne balobi ba kale bupingaje butumbi bua bikondo bia kale abi pavua Adama wenda ne butumbi bu nzambi, ne mpala mubi ne kayi ne buôwa bu kabalu aka ka bukole.
