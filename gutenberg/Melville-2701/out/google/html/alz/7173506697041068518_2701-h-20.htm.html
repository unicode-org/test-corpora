CHAPTER 101. Decanter.

Ma podi meli pa England utho woko, kadi eketho piny kany, nia eai kud i London, man julwonge ni Samuel Enderby ma tho, jara lworo mi adhura meno, ma en e jatel wi ot ma julwongo ni Enderby & Sons; ot ma i paru pa jaracik para, bino bor ungo ku ot pa rwoth pa Tudors ku Bourbons, i lembe mi lembe mukadho. Kare adi, mapodi oro pa Rwoth mwa 1775, ot madit mi mako rec ma dit eni ubedu tiye, waraga para madwong mi rec nyuthu ngo; ento i oro eca (1775) eketho meli mir acel mi England ma ubedu ka dwar Sperm Whale; kadi pi oro madwong mukadho (nicaku i 1726) Coffins man Maceys mwa ma tek pa Nantucket man Vineyard ubedu ka lubo tok Leviathan eca, ento i North man South Atlantic kende: kama mange de epe. Bed ma jugoro maleng kany, nia Nantucketers ubedu ju ma kwong mir acel ikind dhanu ni goyo Sperm Whale madit ku cuma maber; man nia pi nusu mi oro miya acel, gin kende re ma gibedo dhanu mi ng'om zoo ma gicaku timo lembe marac i kume.

I 1778, yei maber, Amelia, juyike pi tic ma pire tek, man ku telowic pa Enderbys ma tek, ukadhu Cape Horn ku tego cwiny, man ebedo mir acel ikind thek mi ketho yei ma dit mi South Sea. Wotho ne ubedo maber man maber; man edwogo i kabedu pare ku cinge ma upong ku ic ma pire tek, lanyuth pa Amelia ugam ulubo tok yei mange, mi England man mi Amerika, man kumeno kabedo malac mi Sperm Whale mi Pacific ugam unyai. Ento ma cwinye yom ku timo maber eni, ot ma rwinyo eca udok ucungo kendu: Samuel ku awiya pare zo—adi, min gi kende re ma ngeyo—man ithe loc migi, man manok, aparu, ku sente migi, gavumente mi Britain ugam ucwalu meli ma julwongo ni sloop-of-war Rattler i wotho mi maku bale i nam ma yo piny. Ku telowic pa jadwong mi nam, Rattler utimo wotho ma tek, man etimo tic moko; adi ma nen ungo. Ento eni kende ngo. I 1819, ot marom eno uketho yei migi mi nwangu rec ma julwongo ni discovery whale, pi ni cidho i nam mabor mi Japan. Meli eca-ma julwongo niya "Syren"-utimo wotho maber mi temo lembe; man ubedu kumeno ma kabedo madit mi Japanese Whaling Ground ugam ungeyere. Syren i wotho ma pire tek eni ubedu judongo pa Captain Coffin, Nantucketer.

Woro zoo ni Enderbies, dong, ma ot migi, aparu, utiye nitundu kawoni; kadi nang nya Samuel macon ucikere ni edaru weko waya pare pi nam madit mi South Sea mi ng'om mange.

Meli ma julwongo ku nyinge ubedo ber pi woro, pi bedo yei ma ringo pio pio man yei maber yore zo. Abedo i iye wang acel i diewor moko i dhu nam mi Patagonian, man amadhu kongo maber i forecastle. Ubedo tuko maber ma wabedu kudu, man gin zo ubedu trumps—dhanu ceke ma ubedu i yei. Kwo ma nok igi, man tho ma kud anyonga. Man gam maber ma abino kudu—kare, kare malac ingeye ma Ahab muti mulo yen pare ku tyende macol—epoi ira pi berocwiny, ma tek, pa Saxon mi welo pa yei ne; man wek rwoth para uwil ira, man jajok upoi ira, kan aweko wange. Loko? Awacu niya wabedo ku flip? Eyo, man wa loko ku rwom pa gallon apar pi sawa acel; man kinde ma koth madit ubinu (pien koth ucwir ku Patagonia), man cing dhanu ceke—ju ma bino man dhanu ceke—julwongo gi ni reef topsails, wabedo ku top-heavy ma umitu wabed ka wawotho iwi ngati acel acel malu i bowlines; man waweko cing jaket mwa i yei, kara wabed keca, ma wacungo pio pio i yamu ma tek, lanyuth mi poyo wic ni tars ceke ma mer. Ento, yei ne ucidho ngo iwi nam; man ndhundhu ne wapodho piny, ma wabedo ma wangeyo ngo, ma umitu wadok i flip kendu, kadi nang kadu ma nyai ma ucwalu piny i forecastle scuttle, ubedu ma nyai man ma nyai ikum paru para.

Ringu dyang ne ber—tek, ento ku adila i iye. Giwacu niya ubedu ring dyang; jumoko, nia ubedu ringu dhiang ma dromedary; ento angeyo ngo, andha, kite ma eca ubedu kudu. Gibedu ku dumplings de; dumplings ma nok, ento ma dit, ma nen calu globular, man ma copo nyothre ngo. Abedo ku paru niya icopo winjo gi, man i royo gi i ii ingeye ma ju mwonyo gi. Ka igoro piny mabor akeca anyim, icopo ketho cing gi woko kud i kumi calu mupira mi billiard. Mugati—ento eca copo konyu ngo; man de, ubedu yath ma jwigo two ma nyai; i lembe ma nok, mugati ne ubedo ku cam ma nyen kende ma gibedo kudu. Ento forecastle ubedu ma ler ungo, man ubedu yot ni kadho i kabedo macol ka icamu. Ento i lembe zo, tingo kude kud i mutukari nituc i wi mutukari, ka waneno dit pa boilers pa jatedo, ikinde ku boilers pare ma kwo; kore man ngeye, awacu, Samuel Enderby ubedu yei ma kud anyonga; pa cam maber man madwong; flip maber man matek; crack fellows zo, man lim nicaku i boot heels nitundu i hat-band.

Ento pirango ma, iparu, nia Samuel Enderby, man jumoko ma gi julonyo rec mi England ma angeyo—kadi bende ngo—gibedu yei ma nying gi uyik man ma jolo welo; ma ukadhu ikum ringu dyang, man mugati, man can, man jok; man giol ungo pio pio ku camu, man madhu, man ngyeru? Abiwecu iri. Cwiny maber pa ju ma mako rec mi England ubedo piny ma pire tek pi timo sayusac ikum lembe mukadho. Man de abedo ka weko timo sayusac ikum rec ma ukadhu, kadi nangu nen ni mitire.

Ju England ugam utelo wigi i tic mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whale ku ju Hollanders, Zealanders, man Danes; ma gicaku kudu wec madwong ma podi utiye i tic mi mako rec; man ango ma umedire, kura migi macon, ma mulo cam man madhu madwong. Pi, calu piny ma rwinyo, meli mi kuloka mi England nyayu dhanu pare; ento epe ku whaleer mi England. Pieno, i dhok mi Ingresa, lembe maeni mi mako rec maber epe piny ma rwinyo man ma rwinyo, ento piny ma rwinyo man ma rwinyo; man, kumeno, umitu obed ku acaki moku ma pire tek, ma juweco pire keni, man jubi medo nyang ikume.

I kare ma abetimo sayusac ikum lembe mukadho pa Leviathanic, anwango buku moko macon mi Dutch, ma, pi nguce pa rec ma julwongo ni whaling, angeyu niya ubedu ikum ju ma mako rec. Nyinge ubedo, "Dan Coopman," uketho agam agam nia eni ucikere ni bedu lembe ma pire tek pa jurutic moko mi Amsterdam i tic mi maku rec, calu yei moko ci ma mako rec ma julwongo ni whale ucikere ni tingo cooper pare. Abedo ku tego pa paru eni ikare ma aneno nya ebedo tic pa "Fitz Swackhammer" acel. Ento jarimba Dr. Snodhead, ngati ma somo lee, japonji mi Low Dutch man High German i kolej pa Santa Claus man St. Pott's, ma amiyo ire tic mi loko dhok, amiye sanduku pa sperm candles pi peko pare—Dr. Snodhead eni, ndhundhu ma eneno buku ne, ewacu ira niya “D Coop” umito wacu ngo “The Merchant.” I lembe ma nok, buku macon man ma juponjo mi Low Dutch uweco ikum kuloka mi Holland; man, ikind lembe mukende, ubedu ku lembe ma nyangu dhanu ikum mako rec ma julwongo ni whale. Man i dul maeni, ma wiye ubedo, "Smeer," nyo "Fat," ma anwangu iye karatasi ma lac pa jam piny ma jubi ketho i ot gwoko piny man i ot piny pa 180 sail pa Dutch whalemen; kud i lembe ma jugoro, calu ma Dr. Snodhead uloko, agoro lembe ma e:

400,000 lbs. pa ring dyang. 60,000 lbs. Friesland ring upegu. 150,000 lbs. pa rec. 550,000 lbs. pa biscuit. 72,000 lbs. pa mugati mayom. 2,800 firkins pa moo. 20,000 lbs. Texel & Leyden cheese. 144,000 lbs. cheese (copu bedu piny ma ber ungo). 550 ankers pa Geneva. 10,800 barrels pa kongo.

Pol pa tables mi wel gibedo muthwo in kwanu; epe i lembe ma kawoni, kadi ango, kama jakwan upong ku pipe, barrel, quarts, man gills pa kongo maber man anyonga maber.

I kare meno, atiyo ku nindu adek pi nyang ikum kongo, ringu dyang, man mugati, ma i kare meno paru madwong ma pire tek jubedu ka paru ira, ma copo bedu ku tic ma malu man pa Platonic; man, de, acwio tables mange para gira, ma mako wel pa rec, man mange, ma jucamu ku ju Low Dutch harpooneers i kare macon mi Greenland man Spitzbergen whale fishery. I acaki, wel pa moo, man Texel ku Leyden cheese ma jucamu, nen ni ber mandha. Aparu, kadi, pi kura migi ma leng, ma judwoko gi ma leng uloyu pi kit tic migi, man asaga pi lubo tok migi i nam ma ngic ma julwongo ni Polar Seas, i dhu nam mi ngom pa Esquimaux kama dhanu ma gibedo i ngom meno gikwo i iye i bumpers mi moo mi gari.

Wel pa kongo de, utiye malac mandha, 10,800 barrels. Kawoni, calu polar fisheries eca copo bedu kende kende i kare mi cwir ma nok mi kite ma koth ku ceng ryeny kudu, kara wotho pa acel ikin ju Dutch whalemen, ikinde wotho ma nok mi cidho man kud i nam mi Spitzbergen, ukadhu ngo dwi adek, waci, man ka wakwanu dhanu 30 i acel acel mi yei migi 180, wabe ku dhanu 550, 40, wabe nwangu dhanu mi Dutch ma piny zo; kumeno, awacu, watiye ku pipa ario pa kongo pi ngati acel acel, pi yenga apar wi aryo, muweko wel pare maber pa 550 ankers pa gin. Kawoni, kadi ju ma gi tiyo ku gin man beer harpooneers, ma gibedo ku lworo ma lee dit, gibedo dhanu maber ma copo cungu iwi yei, man mako rec ma mol iwi yei; eni nen calu piny ma copo timere ngo. Ento gi ketho cing gi ikum gi, man gi goyo gi de. Ento eni ubedu bor mandha yo malu, jupoi, kama kongo urombo maber ku cik madit; iwi Equator, in kabedo mwa mi mako rec mi yo piny, kongo copo bedu maber mi ketho jaring yei nindo i wi yei man madhu kongo i yei pare; man tho madit copo wok i Nantucket man New Bedford.

Ento dong epe; lembe madwong juweco pire ni nyutho nia ju macon mi Dutch whalers mi oro miya aryo nyo adek mukadho gibedo ku adunde ma malo; man nia ju ma mako rec mi England giweko ngo lanyuth maber akeca. Pi, giwacu, ka ibewotho i yei ma epe, tek icopo nwangu piny moko maber uloyu kud i ng'om, nwang cam maber kud i iye, kadi nyanok. Man eni weko decanter doko piny.





THEK 102. Bower i Arsacides.

Nitundu kawoni, i lembe ma jukoro ikum Sperm Whale, abedo ka weco ikum berocwiny pa kite ma woko; nyo ke acel acel man i yo matuc iwi lembe moko manok mi ot. Ento ni nyang ikume, eber ira kawoni ni gonyo bute pare, man ni gonyo theng hose pare, ni gonyo garters pare, man ni gonyo cinge man wange pa cogo pare ma iye, ni kethe i nyimi i ultimatum pare; eni nyutho, i cogo pare ma epe ku cik.

Ento kawoni nenedi, Ishmael? Ere nenedi, in, ngati ma tiyo ku yei i tic mi mako rec, itimo nen calu ingeyo lembe moko ikum thenge ma piny pa rec ma julwongo ni whale? Stubb ma jagor, ma ucungu iwi capstan peri, umiyu ponji ikum kit ma Cetacea utiye kudu; man ku kony pa windlass, tingo cing cing moko pi nyuthu? Tit lembe peri giri, Ishmael. Icopo ketho rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale iwi yei peri pi nineno, calu jatedo ubecamu opego ma juwango? Andha epe. Ibedo janeno mandha nitundu kawoni, Ishmael; ento bed ma ling ikum kite ma ibe tiyo kudu ku twero pa Yona kende; twero mi weco iwi joists man beams; rafters, ridge-pole, sleepers, man under-pinnings, ma timo frame-work pa leviathan; man maru tallow-vats, dairy-rooms, butteries, man cheeseries i ic pare.

Aloko, nia nicaku i kare pa Jonah, ju ma nok kende re ma gimondo bor piny i the adila pa rec madit; kadi kudu, anwangu gum mi poko kume i ayi ma nok. I yei ma abedo i iye, nyathin ma nok ma julwongo ni Sperm Whale jutingo iwi yei pi poke nyo bag pare, pi timo sheaths pi barbs pa harpoons, man pi wi lances. Iparu niya aweko kare eni ucidh, manangu atiyu ngo ku boat-hatchet man jack-knife para, man aturo seal man kwanu lembe ceke ma nwang i nyathin eca?

Man pi ngec para mandha ikum cogo pa leviathan i dongo migi madit, pi ngec ma nok eno atiye ku banja ni jarimba ma dit Tranquo, rwoth pa Tranque, acel ikin Arsacides. Pi bedo i Tranque, oro mukadho, kinde ma abedo i meli mi kuloka Dey mi Algiers, julwonga ni bedo i ceng yom pa Arsacidean ku rwoth pa Tranque, i ot pare ma juweko tic i Pupella; got moko ma yo piny mabor ungo ku kama juruwil mwa gilwongo ni Bamboo-Town, adhura madit pare.

Ikind kit kura mange mapol, jarimba ma dit Tranquo, ma juyike ku mar ma lee pi lembe ceke mi barbaric vertu, ukelo karacelu i Pupella piny moko ci ma nen ungo ma dhanu pare ma rieko copo yubu; yen ma juyubu ku jam tic maber, adila ma juyubu, pala ma juyubu, yei ma wel migi tek, yei ma leng; man lembe eni zo jupoko ikind piny ma nyayu anyonga, piny ma nyayu anyonga, ma pii ma juculo kudu musoro uketho iwi dhu nam pare.

Ma dit ikind ju ma tok cen ubedo Sperm Whale madit, ma, ingey yamu ma tek ma tek, junwange utho man ucungo, ku wiye ikum yen mi cocoa-nut, ma yuk pare nen calu yei pare macol. Kinde ma kum madit ne udaru wok woko kud i iye, man cogo ne doko lum ma muthwo i ceng, eca jutero cogo ne maber iwi Pupella glen, kama ot pa Mungu madit mi lum ma dit ubedo ka gwoke.

Ribs juyike ku trophies; vertebræ jugoro ku Arsacidean annals, i hieroglyphics ma nen ungo; i wic, jukwenda gi gwoko mac ma leng ma rwinyo ngo, kara wic ma rwinyo kendo ucwalo pii pare ma lyeth; ma, ucungo iwi jang yen, lak cinge ma piny ubedo ka yeng iwi ju ma woro Mungu, calu pala ma juketho iwi yuk wic ma nyayu lworo i Damocles.

Ubedo neno maber akeca. Yen ne ubedo macol calu lum mi Icy Glen; yen ne ucungu malu man ku cwiny ma tek, ma giwinjo pii migi ma kwo; ngom ma piny ubedu calu jam tic pa jayub bongo, ku carpet ma leng iwie, ma lum pa mzabibu iwi ngom ubedo the warp man woof, man thiwe ma kwo ubedo cal. Yen zo, ku jang yen migi zo; lum zo, man ferns, man lum; yamo ma tingo kwenda; lembe eni zo ubedu katiyo. Ikind lacings pa ith, ceng madit nen calu shuttle ma ucungo iwi lum macol. Oh, jayub bongo ma tic! jabodho ma nen ungo!—cung!—wec acel!—kani ma bongo ne mol kudu? ot pa rwoth ango ma ecopu bedu kudu? pirango ma timo tic ma nja eni? Wec, jabodho!—wek cingi!—ento wec acel kende kudi! Ungo—shuttle utingo—piny ucidhi kud i wang loom; carpet ma nyen-rushing pi naka rwinyo. Mungu-jabodho, eyubu; man ku timo meno edoko ith, ma ewinjo ngo dwal pa dhanu; man ku wer meno, wan de, ma waneno loom wabedo ith; man kende kende ka wawok kud i iye, wabiwinjo dwal elufu acel ma weco kud i iye. Pien kadi etiye i factory ceke mi piny. Wec ma juweco ma winjere ngo ikind ju ma giwotho; wec marom eno winjere maleng woko kud i kor ot, ma wok kud i ot ma oyabere. Kumeno ju ma rac ungiyure. Ah, dhanu! dong, bed moth; kum kumeno, i kind lembe maeni mi ng'om ma dit, paru peri ma nyawang copo winjere bor.

Kawoni, ikind lum macol, ma kwo ope i iye pa yen mi Arsacidean, cogo madit, ma tar, ma juworo ubedo ka ling—ngati ma timo tic ma dit! Ento, calu ma rangi macol man woof ma nja ubedo ka rwinyo man ucungu ikume, ngati ma timo tic ma tek nen calu jayub ma rieko; en gire juyike ku lum; dwi ku dwi ma wabekwo ku lum macol man macol; ento en gire ubedo cogo. Kwo uketho tho; Tho uketho kwo; mungu ma rac ugamu kwo ma nyathin, man unyole ku dwong pa wic ma nyap.

Kawoni, kinde ma ku Tranquo pa rwoth alimo rec ma dit eni, man aneno wic pa wic calu keno, man iro ma juyubu ubewok kud kama jet mandha uwok kud i iye, cwinya utal nia rwoth ucikere ni neno ot lega ni piny maber. Enyero. Ento ma sagu nenedi nya jukwenda gicikiri ni kwero nia jet pare ma lyeth ubedo mandha. I ngeye man i ngeye awotho i wang cogo eni—abolo yen mi mzabibu woko—aturu cinge—man ku mupira pa Arsacidean twine, awotho, awotho pi kare malac ikind colonnades man arbours mapol ma ucungo. Ento ndhundhu line para uthum; man lubo tok cen, awok kud i dhugola ma amondo i iye. Aneno ngo piny moko makwo iiye; piny moko ubedu nuti ento cogo kende.

Ka abolo ira lum ma rangi pa lum, adok kendo i kind cogo. Kud i cing gi ma ucungo i wic, julam gineno nia atiye ka maku bor pa cinge ma tok cen, "Kawoni nenedi!" gikok; "Icopo pimu eni mungu mwa! Eni piwa." “Eyo, julam—ber, wubikethe pi kare ma romo adi?” Ento i ngeye lwiny matek ucaku ikind gi, ikum tyend man inci; gi turo sconces pa ngati acel acel ku yard-sticks migi—wic madit ne uwinjere—man maku kare maber eno, agam atimo aloka loka para giri pio pio.

Pim eni kawoni amito aket inyim peri. Ento mir acel, jukyewo, nia, i lembe eni, atiye agonya ngo ni weco lembe moku ci ma amito. Pien tiye judongo pa skeleton ma icopo lwongo gi, mi temo bedo para. Leviathanic Museum utiye, giwacu ira, i Hull, England, acel ikin kabedo mi maku rec ma dit mi ngom meno, kama gitiye ku lei moko maber pa rec ma julwongo ni fin-backs man rec mange. Kumeno bende, awinjo nia i ot gwoko piny macon mi Manchester, i New Hampshire, gitiye ku piny ma wegi piny lwongo ni "piny acel kende maber pa Greenland nyo River Whale i ngom mi Amerika." Bende, ikabedu moku i Yorkshire, England, Burton Constable ku nyinge, Sir Clifford Constable moko utiye ku cogo pa Sperm Whale, ento dit pare rwinyo, ma rom ku pa jarimba King Tranquo.

I lembe ario eno, rec ma julwongo ni stranded whales ma cogo ario eni gibedo i iye, jular jukwayu gi ni wegi pi the lembe marom. Rwoth Tranquo umaku piny pare pi en emito; man Sir Clifford, kum ebedo rwoth pa judongo mi kabedo eca. Sir Clifford's whale juweco pire i iye zo; kara, calu sanduku madit mi kawono, icopo yabe man cego, i iye zo ma cogo—nyayu cinge calu jamb cinge madit—man iwoth ceng zo iwi lak cinge ma piny. Jubiketho cing iwi dhugola pare moko man dhugola pare; man jatel wi tyend dhanu bi nyuthu welo ma bino i anyim ku dul pa cwiyu i ngete. Sir Clifford paru mi culu twopence pi neno kabedo ma juweco i iye i ng'et ng'ut; threepence ni winjo dwol i iye pa cerebellum pare; man sixpence pi neno maber akeca kud i wiye.

Dimension pa cogo ma kawoni abe medo ni ketho piny ju goyo wec acel acel kud i cinga ma yor acam, kama atimo igi tattoo; calu i wotho para mi lum i kare meno, yore mukende maber mi gwoko wel piny ma pire tek epe. Ento calu ma cwinya upong pi kabedo, man amito nia dul kum para mukende ubed karatasi ma oling pi poem ma abedo ka kyewo—kadi bende, dul ango ma untatooed copo bedu—abedo ngo ku peko ikum inches ma tung tung; kadi, andha, inches ucikere ngo ni mondo i pima maber pa rec ma julwongo ni whale.





CHAPTER 103. Pim pa cogo pa rec ma julwongo ni Whale.

I acaki, amito aketh inyimwu lembe moko ma yot, ma mako kwo pa leviathan eni, ma cogo pare wabe nyutho nyanok. Lembe ma kumeno copo bedu ber kany.

Nimakere ku wel ma atimo maber, man ma atimo ikum wel pa Captain Scoresby, pa ton pyer abiro pi rec madit mi Greenland ma bor pare romo futi pyer abusiel; nimakere ku kwan para maber, awacu, Sperm Whale ma dit uloyu, ikind futi pyer abora ku abic ku pyer abungwen i bor, man piny moko ma piny pa futi pyer angwen i rwom pare ma lageng, rec ma kumeno bibedo ku pek ma romo ton pyer abungwen; kara, ka ikwanu dhanu apar wi adek i ton acel, ecopo bedu tek uloyu wel dhanu mi caru acel ma dhanu elufu acel ku dak acel gikwo i iye.

Iparu ngo niya wic, calu dhyeng ma jukubo, umaku juketh i kum leviathan eni, mi ketho edok elok paru pa ngati moko ci?

I ngei ma aketho i yo matung tung wic pare, wange, lak, lak, yibe, wiye, lak, man thenge mange mapol, kawoni abeci nyuthu piny ma pire tek uloyu i dul pa cogo pare ma unyai ungo. Ento calu colossal skull utingo dul madit pa dul pa cogo; calu ma etiye thenge ma tek uloyu; man calu lembe moko ope ma jubi dwoko wange ikume i dul maeni, umitu kud iwek ni tingo i wii peri, nyo i the cingi, kani wabe medere, tek epe icopo nwangu ngo ngec malong ikum kit ma wabe neno kudu.

I bor, cogo pa Sperm Whale i Tranque ubedo futi pyer abiro wi aryo; kara ka dong edaru timo man edaru kwo, ecibedo bor pa futi pyer abungwen; kum i rec ma dit, cogo pa cogo rwenyo bor pa acel mi abic ka juporo ku cogo ma kwo. Ikind futi pyer abiro wi aryo eni, wic pare ku lak pare ubedo futi pyer aryo, man eweko futi pyer abic mi cogo ngeye. Ma juketho i kum cogo ng'ete eni, pi piny moko ma nok uloyu acel mi adek pa bor pare, ubedo jamb cinge ma dit ma ling ling ma con ubedo ku cinge ma pire tek.

I banga, sanduku ma dit ma rangi pa ivory-ribbed, ku ng'ut ma bor, ma rwinyo ngo, ma ucungo bor kude i yo ma atira, nen ungo nyanok calu kor meli madit ma juketho iwi yei, ma kende kende 20 pa bow-ribs pare ma nen ungo re ma juketho i iye, man keel ubedo, pi kare malac, ento ma bor, ma jupoko, yen.

Ribs ne ubedo apar iwi ngete acel. Mir acel, nicaku kud i ngute, bor pare romo futi abusiel; mir aryo, mir adek, man mir angwen gibedo bor, nitundu i thum pa mir abic, nyo acel ikin cinge ma ikin, ma romo futi abora ku inci moko. Nicaku i thenge eca, cinge ma udong udok piny, nitundu mir apar man mir ajiki ubedo futi abic ku inci moko kende. I rwom ma malu, gin ceke gibedo ku rwom ma rom ku bor migi. Thenge ma ikin ubedo ma ucungo uloyu. I kind Arsacides moko jutiyu kudu pi ketho lum ma juwotho i iye iwi kulo ma nok.

Ka wabeparu pi ribs eni, acopo ngo ni bedo ma cwinya utur ku lembe ma utimere, ma judwoko wang mapol i buku eni, nia cogo pa rec ma julwongo ni whale epe ku kite ma etiye kudu. Ma dit uloyu kud i Tranque ribs, acel ikin ma ikin, utingo thenge pa rec ma, i kwo, utiye malac uloyu i thum pa rec. Kawoni, thum madit pa kum pa rec ma julwongo ni whale eni ucikere ni bedu futi apar wiye abusiel; man, cinge ma rom ku ne upimu ento ma kadhu futi abora. Pieno rib eni unyutho nusu pa paru mandha pa kwo pa dul ne. Mi mede, pi yore moku, kama kawoni aneno kende kende ng'ut ng'ut, piny ceke ma con ubedo ma umu ku ring'o, ler, rimo, man ic. Kadok kumeno de, pi ng’ut ma dit, aneno kende kende ng’et ng’ut ma nwang’u ubedo ngo maleng’; man i kabedu pa piny ma pek man ma dit, ento ma epe ku cogo, piny ma oling!

Ebedo lembe ma kony man mi mingo nenedi, dong, aparu, pi dhanu ma lworo ma podi wotho ungo ni temo ni nyang ikum rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale, ni neno kende iwi cogo pare ma tho, ma ucungo i lum ma ling eni. Ungo. I adunde pa peko ma nyap kende; kende kende kani i kind eddyings pa lworo pare; iwi nam ma thuc kende, ecopo nwangu rec ma dit man ma kwo.

Ento ng'ut. Pi meno, yore maber uloyu ma wacopo paru kudu utiye, ku crane, ni ketho cogo pare malu. Tic ma timo pio pio epe. Ento kawoni edaru, nen calu Pompey's Pillar.

Jutiye ku vertebræ pyer angwen man ma tung tung, ma i skeleton gicokiri ngo karacelu. Pol kare gibedo calu kidi madongo ma jugoro iwi got ma julwongo ni Gothic, ma gicungo iwi kidi ma pek. Ma dit uloyu, acel ma ikin, utiye ku lac piny ma kadhu futi adek, man ku thum ma kadho futi angwen. Ma nok uloyu, kama ng'ut cinge ucungo i iye, utiye inci aryo kende i lac, man nen calu billiard-ball matar. Juwacu ira niya podi moko nuti ma nok, ento jular junyotho gi ku ju moko ma camu dhanu, awiya pa padri, ma gikwalo gi pi tuko kudu marbles. Omiyo waneno kaka ng’ut kata gik madongo ma ngima ng’eny ng’eny e gikone e tugo mayot mar nyithindo.





CHAPTER 104. The Fossil Whale.

Nicaku i pek pare madit, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale mio the lembe maber akeca ma juromo medo, medo dit, man medo dit. Icopo, icopo ngo ni diyo cwinye. Ku twero maber, jubi timo ire kende kende i imperial folio. Kud ni wacu kendu furlongs pare nicaku i spiracle nituc i tail, man yards ma epimo ikum kore; paru kende ikum rwom ma malu pa ic pare, kama gibedo i iye calu waya madongo man hawsers ma jukubotho i thenge ma piny pa yei mi lwiny.

Calu ma adaru timo tic ku Leviathan eni, eber ira ni yio nia atiye ngati ma ngeyo lembe zo i tic eni; ma eweko ngo ikum thwol ma nok pa rimo pare, man ewotho woko nitundu ikum ic pare. I ngei ma judaru tito pire i pol pa kwo pare ma kawoni man kite ma kume utiye kudu, kawoni dong udong ni medo rwom pare i yore mi archæological, fossiliferous, man antediluvion. Ka jutiyo kudu ikum lei moko ci ma epe ku Leviathan—i kum thwol nyo thwol—wec ma kumeno copo bedu ni lembe ma pire tek. Ento ka Leviathan ubedo lembe ma jugoro, lembe ne juloko. Abedo ku copo mi ngwec i rwom maeni i the wec ma pek pa diksonari. Man kawoni jubi wacu, nia kare ku kare ebedo ber ni penjo ngati moko ikind lembe maeni, atiyo ku quarto edition madit pa Johnson, ma juwilo pi lembe eno; pi en lexicographer ma nyinge uyik ma rwom pare nen ungo uketho ebedo ber ni ketho lexicon ma jubi tiyo kudu ni jagor ma calu an.

Ng'atu moko winjo pol kare pi jugor ma gicungo man gimedo ku wi lembe migi, kadi nya ecopo nen ni lembe ma rwinyo. Ka dong, ku an, kyewo pa Leviathan eni nenedi? Ku ngeyo ngo, chirography para umedire i coc madongo. Mi ira yuk wic pa condor! Mii ira vur pa Vesuvius pi inkstand! Jurimo, mako cinga! Pi timo kende mi kyewo paru para ikum Leviathan eni, gi nyayu lworo i iya, man gi ketho abedo ku lworo ku ngec migi ma dongo dongo, calu ve gi ketho i iye dul mi ngec, man thek pa rec madongo, man dhanu, man mastodons, mukadho, kawoni, man ma bibino, ku panorama ceke mi ng'om, man iwi ngom zoo, ungo muweko suburbs pare. Ma kumeno, man ma dit, utiye ber pa wi lembe madit man ma rwinyo! Wa medo rwom pare. Mi wodhu buku maber, umitu iyer wi lembe maber. Buku moko mbe ma dit man ma rii ma jucopu kyewo iwi ler, kadi nang dhanu mapol gitemo.

Ma podi amondo ngo iwi lembe pa Fossil Whales, anyutho rwom para calu jagor mi ngom, ni wacu nia i kare para matung tung abedo jabodho mi goyo kidi, man bende jabodho madit mi vur, pii man vur, ka gwoko kongo, cellars, man cisterns mi kit zo. Kumeno bende, ni acaki, amitu poyo wi jakwan, nia kadi i acaki pa geological strata ju nwango dul pa lei ma dit ma kawoni dong utho; piny ma lubo ma junwango i gin ma julwongo ni Tertiary formations nen calu piny ma mako, nyo ma mako, ikind lei ma kwo ma rwinyo, man ju ma nyithindho migi mabor juwacu nia gimondo i yei; Fossil Whales ceke ma junwango kawoni gi ubedo pa Tertiary period, ma en e kare ma tok cen ma ukadhu i wang yik pa piny. Man kadi nya ngati acel de mbe ma dwoko wang lembe ikum kit lei moko ci ma kawoni, podi gi rom ku gi i lembe matung tung, mi nyutho rwom migi calu Cetacean fossils.

Piny ma nyai pa pre-adamite whales, dul pa cogo man cogo migi, junwango ikind oro pyer adek mukadho, i kare matung tung, i thenge mi Alps, i Lombardy, i France, i England, i Scotland, man i ngom mi Louisiana, Mississippi, man Alabama. Ikind piny ma pire tek uloyu utiye thenge moko pa wic, ma i oro 1779 juyike i Rue Dauphine i Paris, yo ma nok ma oyabere iwi ot ker pa Tuileries; man cogo ma juwodho i kare ma jugoyo i dhu nam madongo mi Antwerp, i rundi pa Napoleon. Cuvier uwacu nia jamb kum eni ubedu pa lei moko ma julwongo ngo Leviathanic.

Ento piny maber uloyu kud ikind piny pa Cetacean ubedo cogo pa lei moko ma tho, ma junwango i oro 1842, i podho pa Judge Creagh, i Alabama. Jutic ma gibedo ku lworo ma lee ma gini i kabedu ne gi gamu ni cogo pa malaika acel m'utho. Daktari mi Alabama giwacu niya etiye lei madit ma mol iwi pii, man gimiye nying ni Basilosaurus. Ento cogo moko ma juwodho kud i nam ni Owen, jagor pa dhanu mi England, edoko nya lei ma mol iwi nam eca ubedo rec madit, kadi nang ecaku tho. Lanyuth ma pire tek pa lemandha, ma judwoko wang mapol i buku eni, nia cogo pa rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale unyutho ngo kite ma kume ubedo kudu. Owen udwoko nying lei ma dit Zeuglodon; man i waraga pare ma esomo i wang London Geological Society, ewacu, i yore moku, acel ikin lei ma tung tung ma aloka loka mi ng'om uketho kwo pare uthum.

Kinde ma acungu ikind cogo pa Leviathan, wic, lak, lak, cing, man cogo, zo nenre ku kit lei moko ma nen calu lei makwiny mi nam; ento i kare marom, gibedo ku winjiri marom ku Leviathans ma junyotho, judongo migi ma dhanu copo kwanu ngo; An, ku pii ma dit, judwoko cen i kare maber eca, ma podi kare eca ucaku ngo; kum kare ucaku ku dhanu. Kae Saturn's grey chaos ucungo iwi wiya, man anwangu nen macol, ma nyayu lworo i Polar eternities; kinde ma lum ma nyai ucungo iwi ngom ma kawoni julwongo ni Tropics; man ikind 25,000 miles mi ng'om eni, ngom moko ope ma dhanu copo kwo i iye. I ngeye ng'om zoo ubedo pa rec ma dit; man, rwoth mi cwic, eweko kwo pare i thenge mi Andes man Himmalehs. Nga ma copo nyutho thekwaru calu Leviathan? Harpoon pa Ahab ugam ucwalu rimo ma dit uloyu pa Farao. Methuselah nen calu nyathin mi somo. Aneno piny pi mako cing ku Shem. Abedo ku lworo madit ikum kwo pa antemosaic, ma ungiyu ngo ikum lworo pa rec ma julwongo ni whale, ma, ubedu nuti mapodi kare ceke ukadhu, umitu obed nuti ingey oro pa dhanu udaru kadhu.

Ento epe kende re ma Leviathan eni uweko lanyuth pare ma podi Adamite ukwo i iye, man i limestone man marl eweko cal pare macon; ento iwi kidi mi Misiri, ma lembe migi macon nen calu nyutho nia gitiye ku kidi ma nen calu fossiliferous, wanwango lanyuth pa cinge. I ot moko mi hekalu madit mi Denderah, oro pyer abic mukadho, junwango iwi ot ma dit ma juyubu man jugoro rangi, ma upong ku centaurs, griffins, man dolphins, ma rom ku cal ma nen ungo iwi polo mi kare eni. Ka giwotho ikind gi, Leviathan ma jadit ubedo ka dhuny calu macon; ubedu ka dhuny i planisphere, oro madwong ma podi Solomon unyai ungo.

Man de umitu kud juwek lembe mange ma nyang ma nyutho nia whale ubedo con, i kwo pare ma rwinyo i ngei pii ma dit, calu ma John Leo, jawoth macon mi Barbary uketho piny.

“Kabor ungo ku dhu nam, gitiye ku ot pa Mungu, ma cinge man lum pare juyike ku cogo pa rec ma dit; kum rec ma dit ma dit pol kare jubolo gi tho i dhu nam. Dhanu mapol giparu, nia ku tego ma Mungu umio i ot pa Mungu, winyo moko mbe ma copo kadhu kud i iye manangu etho ngo pio pio ento andha pa Math utiye. ku thenge ario mi ot pa Mungu, kidi moko nuti ma ucwalu Miles ario i nam, man ucwalu Whales ka gipodho iwi gi. Gigwoko Whale's Rib ma bor pare tek pi Miracle, ma ucungo iwi ngom ku thenge pare ma malu, ma timo Arch, ma Wi Bael copo tundo ngo iwie. (John Leo uwacu) juwacu niya ebedo keca oro dak acel mapodi anene ngo. Ju ma gisomo lembe mukadho giwacu, nia jabila moko ma ukoro lembe pa Mahomet, uai kud i ot pa Mungu eni, man jumoko giwacu ngo, nia jabila Jonas jubolo woko ku rec ma julwongo ni Whale i thenge mi ot pa Mungu.

I Afric Temple of the Whale eni aweku in, jakwan, man tek ibedo Nantucketer, man whaleman, ibeworo keca ling ling.





CHAPTER 105. Nyo dit pa rec ma julwongo ni Whale udok piny?—Ebi tho?

Ka dong, calu Leviathan eni bino iwiwa kud i wi pii mi Eternities, ecopu bedu ber ni penjo, ka, i kind oro pare, edaru rwinyo ngo kud i acaki pa kwaro pare.

Ento i ngei lubo tok lembe, wanwango, nya epe kende nia rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whales ma tin eni gi dit uloyu ma sagu ma ju nwango i Tertiary system (ma uketho i iye kare ma tung tung ma podi dhanu ukwo ngo), ento i kind whale ma nwangire i Tertiary system, ju ma gibedo i dul pare ma tok cen gi dit uloyo ju ma con.

Ikind rec ma julwongo ni pre-adamite whales ma podi juwodho, madit uloyu utiye Alabama ma juweco pire i dul mir ajiki, man ma bor pare ubedu piny ma romo futi pyer abiro. Ento, dong waneno, nia tape-measure miyo futi pyer abiro wi aryo pi cogo pa rec madit ma dit ma kawoni. Man awinjo, iwi twero pa ju ma gwoko rec, nia rec ma julwongo ni Sperm Whales jumake bor migi romo futi miya acel i kare ma jumake.

Ento kud ebed, nia kadi bende rec ma dit ma kawoni utiye ma dit uloyu ma sagu ma con; copo bedu ngo, nia nicaku i rundi pa Adamu gi doko rac?

Andha, umitu wabed kumeno, tek wabemito wabed ku foyofoc ikum lembe pa dhanu macalo Pliny, man ju ma gisomo lembe ikum piny ma con. Pi Pliny uwacu iwa pi rec ma dit ma utingo ngom ma dit ma kwo, man Aldrovandus pa mange ma romo fut 800 i bor—Rope Walks man Thames Tunnels pa Whales! Man kadi i ceng pa Banks ku Solander, ju ma gisomo lembe pa piny pa Cooke, wanwango ngati moko mi Denmark ma ubedo i Academy of Sciences ma uketho Iceland Whales moko (reydan-siskur, nyo Wrinkled Bellies) i yard miya acel ku pyer aryo; niwacu, futi miya adek ku pyer abusiel. Man Lacépède, jagor mi ngom mi France, i lembe pare maber pa rec ma julwongo ni whales, i acaki pa tic pare (pot karatasi mir 3), eketho Right Whale piny i mita dak acel, futi dak adek ku pyer aryo wi abora. Man tic eni juwodho i thum pa AD 1825.

Ento kodi ngati moku ma maru rec ma julwongo ni whaleman biyio lembe eni? Ungo. Whale ma kawoni dit calu kwaro pare i rundi pa Pliny. Man tek adok kama Pliny utiye i iye, an, jakwath rec (ma sagu en), abiketho cwinya tek ni wacu ire. Pien acopo nyang kudu ngo kite ma etiye kudu, nia kadok ju m'utho pa dhanu mi Misiri ma juyiko oro elifu madwong ma podi Pliny unyol ungo, gipimo ngo piny madwong i sanduku migi calu dhanu mi Kentucky ma kawoni i soksi pare; man podi dhyeng man lei mange ma jugoro iwi kidi ma con pa Misiri man Nineveh, ku rwom ma jugoro kudu, nyutho kamaleng ni dhyeng ma juyubu maber, ma jucamu i ot, ma juyike i Smithfield, rom ungo kende, ento ukadhu lee dit ma sagu dyang ma dit pa Pharaoh; I wang lembe eni zo, acopo yio ngo nia ikind lei zo, rec ma dit kende re ma umitu obed ma rac.

Ento podi penji mange podi utiye; acel ma pol kare jubedu ka nyayu lworo i iye ku ju Nantucketers ma rwinyo. Kadi bedu ni kum dhanu ma ngeyo lembe zo ikum wi meli ma dit, ma kawoni gibemondo kadi kud i Behring's straits, man i kabedo mabor man kama jugwoku iye piny; man harpoons ku lances elifu acel gi ringo i dhu nam zo; lembe ma pire tek utiye, ka Leviathan copo ciro lwiny ma lee, man ret ma rwinyo; ka nyo ecikere ngo ni jwigere kud i pii, man rec ma dit ma tok cen, calu dhanu ma tok cen, madhu pipe pare ma tok cen, man en gire etho i pii ma tok cen.

Ka juporo dul pa rec ma julwongo ni whales ku dul pa buffalo, ma, oro pyer angwen mukadho, unyai ku dhanu mapol i lum mi Illinois ku Missouri, man utingo yuk wic migi man ucungu ku wic migi ma upong ku polo iwi kabedo ma dit mi kulo, kama kawoni jumalworo ngom i wel piny acel inch; I poro ma kumeno, lembe ma dhanu copo kwero ngo copo bedu, ni nyutho nia rec ma jucako copo rwinyo ngo pio pio.

Ento umitu inen lembe eni ku yore moku ci. Kadi bende kare manok mukadho—kwo maber ungo—kwan pa buffalo i Illinois ukadhu wel pa coo ma kawoni utiye i London, man kadi nya tin eni tung acel kadi tyend gi udong ungo i ngom eca; man kadi bende the lembe ma nyayu nek ma rac eni ubedo pala pa dhanu; ento kit ma tung tung pa whale-hunt ukwero thum pa Leviathan. Dhanu pyer angwen i yei acel ma gibe dwar Sperm Whales pi dwi pyer angwen wi abora giparu niya gitimo maber akeca, man foc Mungu, tek i ajiki gi tingo moo pa rec pyer angwen i pacu. Ento, i ceng pa ju ma con mi Canada ku India ma gi judwar man ju ma gi kwalo lei mi West, kinde ma far west (ma ceng wok i iye podi wok) ubedo lum man nyaku, wel marom pa coo ma gibedo ku moccasined, pi dweche marom, ma gibedo iwi agura kakare ni wotho i yei, gicopo nego dhanu mapol, ento pi elufu mapol; lemandha ma, ka mitire, jucopo tito ku wel.

Man de, ka waneno maber, nen ungo lembe moko ma nyayu rweny pa Sperm Whale, calu, nia i oro mukadho (i thum pa oro elifu mukadho, waci) Leviathans eni, i dul ma nok, jubedo ka rombu kudu pol kare uloyu kawoni, man, pi the lembe ne, wotho migi ubedu mabor ungo, man ubedu ku bero madwong. Pien, calu ma jular juneno kaka mange, rec ma julwongo ni whales, ma nen pa lembe moko ikum bedo maber, kawoni giwotho i nam i caravans madongo, ma uketho i rwom ma malu ju ma gibedo kendgi, yokes, man pods, man schools mi ceng mukende dong gicokiri i dul madongo ento ma gipokere, man ma pol ungo. Eni kende. Man bende nen ni lembe ma rac, nia pilembe ju ma julwongo gi ni whale-bone whales dong gibedo ngo i kabedo madwong i oro mukadho ma gipong kugi, eno uketho kit lei eni de ube rwinyo. Pien jubewotho gi kende kud i promontory ni cape; man ka dhu nam moko dong ukwo ngo ku ndege migi, ci, bed ma ingeyo, cula mange man mabor ugam unyai ikum lembe ma jung'eyo ongo.

Man de: ikum Leviathans ma juweco pigi mir ajiki, gitiye ku udi ario ma tek, ma, i paru pa dhanu, bibedo ma dhanu copo turo ngo. Man calu i kare ma gimondo i lum migi, ju Swiss ma ngic gi dok cen i got migi; dong, ma jucako gi kud i savannas man glades mi nam ma idyere, whale-bone whales copo cidho i Polar citadels migi, man gipodho piny i thenge mi gilasi man cel, bino ikind podho ma ngic man got; man i kind dwi mir apar wi ario ma rondo ku rondo, kwero lembe ceke ma dhanu timo.

Ento calu ma romo pyer abic pa rec ma julwongo ni whale-bone whales jubecayu gi pi cachalot acel, jumoko ma gisomo lembe mi forecastle giwacu nia peko maber eni udaru dwoko piny dul mony migi. Ento kadi nang pi kare moko mukadho, wel pa rec ma julwongo ni whales, ma romo 13,000, jubed junego gi kubang oro i dhu nam ma yo piny ku ju America kende; ento lembe moko nuti ma ketho kadi lembe maeni bedu nok nyo epe calu lembe ma rwate ku lembe eni.

Kadok ebedo lembe ma rwinyore ni bedo ku yioyic moko ikum wel pa lei madongo mi ng'om, ento wabewecu ang'o ni Harto, jagor mi lembe mukadho mi Goa, kinde ma ewacu iwa nia i lwiny acel rwoth mi Siam umaku lyec 4,000; nia i kabedu eca lyec utiye madwong calu dhyeng i kabedu ma ngic. Man nen calu the lembe moko mbe ma copo ketho wabedo ku jiji nia ka lyec eni, ma kawoni jubecamu pi oro elifu madwong, ku Semiramis, ku Porus, ku Hannibal, man ku rwoth mi yo piny—ka gikwo keca i wel madwong, copo bedu nya rec madit ma dit copo kwo ma sagu ju ma jucayu, pi en etiye ku podho mi ngom mi Asia, ma rom ku ngom mi Amerika. Europe ku Afrika, New Holland, man cula mi nam zo karacelu.

Bende: umitu wapar, nia kud i kwo ma lee pa rec ma julwongo ni whales, nyalo bedo ni giromo oro miya acel man ma kadhu, pieno i kare moko ci, rwom ma tung tung mi dhanu madongo ucikere ni bedu i kare marom. Man ango ma eni, wacopo nwangu paru moku ikume, ka wabe paru ikum liel, liel, man udi pa juru ot ma cwio ma ukelo kum pa coo, mon, man awiya ma gikwo oro pyer abiro wiye abic mukadho; man medu dhanu mapol eni ikum wel dhanu ma kawoni iwi ngom.

Pieno, pi lembe eni zo, wakwanu rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ni piny ma tho ngo i kit kwo pare, kadi nang etho i kwo pare gire. Egam ewotho i nam mapodi ngom eca unyai pii; edaru dhuny iwi kabedo pa Tuileries, man Windsor Castle, man Kremlin. I pii ma dit pa Noa edaru yei pa Noa; man tek ng'om udok upong ku pii kendu, calu Netherlands, mi nego oyo pare, ci rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ma nja bikwo, man ebidongo iwi got ma malu pa pii ma dit mi equatorial, ebiwodho lembe pare ma rac i polo.





THE 106. Tyend Ahab.

Kite ma Captain Ahab uweko kudu Samuel Enderby mi London, ubedu ngo ma dhanu moko ungiyu ngo ku timo marac ikum dhanu pare gire. Ebedo ku tego ma lee iwi yei pare ma tyende ma rangi pa ivory unwangu ret ma romo nusu. Man kinde ma edaru nwangu deck pare gire, man pivot-hole pare gire keca, edaru ngwec ku tego cwiny ku cik ma pire tek ni steersman (ebedo, calu nja, piny moku ikum steering pare ma rwinyo ngo); i ngeye, ivory ma dong unyai ugam unwangu aloka loka man wrench, kadi nang edoko maleng, man nen calu etiye ku miti, ento Ahab uneno ngo nia etiye piny maber.

Man, andha, nen calu lembe ma nok mi lworo, nia pi timo pare ma rac, Ahab ubedu ka ketho cwinye ikum kite ma cogo ma tho ma en ucungu kudu. Pien ubedu kare malac ungo ma podi Pequod ucaku wotho kud i Nantucket, ma junwange dyewor acel ma evuto piny, man ma engeyo ngo; ku lembe moko ma jungeye ngo, man ma nen ni lembe ma dhanu copo nyang kudu ngo, ma dhanu copo paru ngo, cinge macol ubedo ka wok matek, ma edaru goyo ku yen, man edaru turo cinge; man de ubedu labongo peko ma lee dit ma ret ma ubedu ka nyayu can ne uthum.

Bende, i kare meno, epodho ngo i paru pare ma monomaniac, nia can ceke mi can ma kawoni ubedu kende kende ikum can ma con; man en de nen kamaleng ni eneno, nia calu lei ma mol ma rac uloyu mi lum eketho kwo pare doko tek calu jawer ma leng uloyu mi lum; dong, marom ku berocwiny moko ci, lembe marac ceke nyayu kwo migi. Eyo, makadhu marom, Ahab uparu; pi kite ma kwaro man nyithindho pa Grief cidho anyim uloyu kwaro man nyithindho pa Joy. Pi, ungo ni nyuthu lembe eni: nya etiye lembe ma juwodho kud i ponji moko mi cik, nia kadi bende lembe moko ma nyayu anyonga ma dhanu timo kawoni epe ku nyithindho ma junyolo igi pi ng'om mange, ento, i yo ma tung tung, ebilubo ku anyonga-ma epe ku nyithindho pa lworo mi liel; ento, can moko ma rac ma copo tho copo nyodo igi gire nyithindho ma rwinyo ma nja ma kadhu liel; epe ni nyuthu lembe eni, podi nen calu apoka poka utiye ikind nyang ikum lembe eni. Pi, Ahab uparu, kadi bende piny ma malu mi ng'om de utiye ku piny moko ma pire tek ungo ma ucungo i igi, ento, piny, can ceke mi cwiny, the lembe ma pire tek, man, i dhanu moko, dit pa malaika madit; kumeno timo tic migi mi lubo tok lembe kud nyayu lembe ma nen kamaleng. Ni lubu thek pa dhanu ma can ma lee eni, tero wa i ajikine ikind thek ma rwinyo pa mungu; kara, i wang ceng ma mor, ma nyayu lum, man dwi ma mol, dwi mi kac, umitu wawek lembe eni: nia mungu gire gibedo ngo kud anyonga rondo ku rondo. Lanyuth mi nyol ma nyayu can i wang dhanu, utiye lanyuth pa can i ju ma gicoyo cing gi.

Ma ungeye ngo, lembe moko ma pire tek unyayu, ma copo bedu ber, i yo ma juketho, jular unyuthu con. Ku lembe mange madwong ikum Ahab, kare ku kare ubedu lembe ma dhanu moko ungiyu ngo, pirango ma ubedu, nia pi kare moko, mapodi man ingey wotho pa Pequod, ebed ekwo ku nen pa Grand-Lama; man, pi kare acel kende, esayu kabedo ma epe ku wec, calu, ikind judongo pa ju m'utho. Thelembe ma rac pa Captain Peleg pi lembe eni nen ni epe; kadi, andha, calu ma mako thenge ma thuc pa Ahab, nyuthu moko ci ubedo ku mudho ma pire tek uloyu ma sagu lero ma gonyo. Ento, i ajikine, lembe zo wok woko; lembe acel eni utimere, kadi nok. Peko ma rac eca ubedo i thenge mi bedo kende kende pi kare manok. Man ungo eni kende, ento ni ju ma gibedo ka timo tic, ma gipodho i dhu nam, ma, pi the lembe moku ci, gibedo ku twero mi bedo i bange ma jukwero ngo; ni dul ma lworo eca, lembe ma juweco pire malu—ma udong, calu ma etimo, ma Ahab ungiyu ngo—ubedo ku lworo, ma juweko ngo kud i ngom pa tipo man ywak. Pieno, ni kadhu kud i amora migi pi en, gin ceke gicokiri, nitundu ikumgi, mi ketho ngec pa lembe eni kud ibang jumange; man kumeno ebedo, nia ungo nitundu kare malac ukadhu, edaru wok iwi Pequod's decks.

Ento bed lembe eni zo kite ma ecopu bedu kudu; wek lembe ma nen ungo, ma nyang ikume tek i polo, nyo rwoth man judongo pa mac, gibed ku lembe nyo epe ku Ahab mi ng'om, ento, i lembe maeni mi tyende, etimo lembe ma yot;—elwongo jabodho.

Man kinde ma jatic ne uwok i wange, ewacu ire nya ecaku timo tyende manyen, man ewacu ni juwadi ni gineno ku jam piny ma jukubo ku cinge (Sperm Whale) ma jucoko i wotho ne, kara juyer maber pa piny ma tek man ma leng. Ka dong jutimo eni, jabodho ne onwango cik ni juyik tyende ne uthieno eca; man mi mio piny ceke ma mitere, labongo jeni ikum piny ma jugeno ngo ma jutiyo kudu. Bende, juyero nia juyik meli ne woko kud i kabedo pare ma timo tic pi kare manok i hold; man, mi medo tic ne pio pio, juoro jabodho mi cuma ni caku tic ku cuma moko ci ma mitire.
