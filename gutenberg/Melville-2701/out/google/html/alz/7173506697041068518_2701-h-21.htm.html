THEK 107. Jabodho.

Bed piny maber ikind dwi pa Saturn, man igam ngati ma nen ungo kende; man enen ni piny ma nyayu anyonga, dit, man can. Ento kud i lembe marom, kwany dhanu mapol, man pi pol kare, nen calu dul pa dhanu ma pigi tek ungo, ma kawoni man ma wok kud i thek. Ento kadi nya ebedo ngati ma mwol, man ebedo bor ku miyo lanyuth pa lembe ma malu, ma dhanu timo; jabodho pa Pequod ubedu ngo marom; pieno, kawoni ebino i wange iwi kabedo eni.

Calu ju ma timo tic mi yei ma wotho i nam, man asaga zoo ju ma timo yei ma mako rec, ebedo, i rwom moku ma timo tic, ebedo ku ngec ma lee i tic mapol man lwongu ma pire tek; tic pa jabodho mi yen ubedo piny macon man ma dit pa tic mi cing ma pol nyo manok ma mako yen calu piny ma konyo. Ento, muweko tic ma juweco pire malu, jabodho mi Pequod eni ubedu ngati ma timo tic maber akeca i lembe ma dongo dongo ma wok i yei madit, i wotho mi oro adek nyo angwen, i nam mabor man ma rwinyo ngo. Ka wabeweco ngo ikum bedo moth pare i tic ma yot:—yiko yei ma tiyo ku mac, sprung spars, loko kit yei ma cinge tek, ketho wang dhiang i deck, nyo tree-nails manyen i side planks, man lembe mange matung tung ma mako tic pare ma pire tek; ebedo ngati ma ngeyo lembe ma rwinyo lembe ceke, ma konyo man ma rwinyo.

Kabedo acel madit ma etimo i iye lembe pare matung tung, ubedu vice-bench pare; meza ma bor man ma pek ma juketho i iye piny ma rac matung tung, ma dit migi tung tung, man ma juyubu ku cuma man yen. Saa ceke mako ka rec ma julwongo ni whales ubedu inget, kom eni ubedu ma jukubo maber ikum ngeye pa Try-works.

Belaying pin junwange malac akeca ma juromo ketho i vur pare: jabodho ucwale i acel pa vices pare ma etiye ayika, man edwoko piny ma nok. Winyo moko ma rwinyo ma rangi pare nen ungo rwinyo iwi yei, man edoko jabodho: ku cinge maleng ma juyiko ku cogo pa right-whale, man cross-beams pa sperm whale ivory, jabodho timo ot ma nen calu pagoda pi winyo ne. Jabodho yei uturo cinge: jabodho mi bao uketho moo ma kweyo cwiny. Stubb umitu nia lakala ma rangi migi nya nya nya jubedu ka goyo iwi thwon yei pare acel acel; ka ebolo oar acel acel i vice pare madit mi yen, jabodho mi bao ucwalu dul pa lakalatwe. Jara tic mi yei maru ketho cinge iwi ithe: jabodho mi yei timo ithe. Ng'atu mange utiye ku remo lak: jabodho mi bau utingo pincers, man egoyo cinge acel iwi kom pare ewacu ni ebed keca; ento ngati ma can unyayu lworo i the tic ma podi uthum ungo; ka loko cinge ikum cinge mi bao, jabodho mi bao unyutho ire nia egoy lak cinge i iye, tek emito egoy lak ne.

Pieno, jabodho eni ubedo ayika i lembe ceke, man ebedo ngati ma paro ngo man ma woro ngo i lembe ceke. Lak ma en uketho i ivory; wiye ma eneno ento top-blocks; coo gire egam etingo gi yot ni capstans. Ento podi kawoni iwi ngom malac ma jutimo ku yore matung tung man ku ngec ma lee i iye, de; lembe eni zo nen calu nyayu lembe ikum ryeko moko ma nen ungo. Ento epe andha. Ento ebedo ku cwiny ma rac mandha i iye, ma uketho i iye, de, calu ma nen, bedo ma cwinye nyai;—ento ebedo lembe ma nyayu lworo i saa moko, ku lembe macon, ma calu twal, ma con, ma nyayu ngyeru, ma rwinyo ngo kawoni man i ngeye ku janeno moko ma nen ungo; calu ma copo bedu ni tiyo pi kadhu sawa i kare mi gwoko diewor iwi yei pa Noa. Ubedo nya jabodho ma jadit eni ubedo jawoth pi kwo pare zo, ma wotho pare madwong, cidho ku ngeye, edaru ngo kende kende enwango lum; ento ango ma umedire, eweko piny moko ci ma nok ma nen kamaleng ma copo bedu ikume? Ebedo ngati ma nen ungo; integral ma unfractioned; ma rwinyo ngo calu nyathin ma fodi junyolu ngo; kwo manangu iparu ngo pi ng'om maeni nyo ng'om ma lubo. Icopo wacu, nia bedo moth ma iyere eni uketho i iye bedo ku ryeko moko ope; kum i tic pare madwong, enen ungo nia etiyo lee ku paru nyo ku paru pare, nyo kende kende pi en eponje, nyo ku rwom pa lembe eni zo, marom nyo marac; ento kende kende ku kit lembe moko ma ithe winjo man ma ling, ma timo kende kende. Ebedo jara timo lembe marac; obwongo pare, tek ebedo ku acel, ucikere ni edaru wok i ler pa cinge. Ebedo calu ngat acel m'ikind ju ma paru migi ope ento podi gitiye ku kony madit, multum in parvo , Sheffield contrivances, ma uketho nen pa woko—kadi nya edongo nyanok—pa pala ma dhanu ceke tiyo kudu; ento uketho, ungo kende kende pala ma dit matung tung, ento bende screw-drivers, cork-screws, tweezers, awls, pens, rulers, nail-filers, countersinkers. Ento, calu ma jukoro con, jabodho maeni ma timo tic ku jam tic ceke, ma timo tic ku bau, ubedu, i ngei lembe ceke, ubedu ngo cuma ma timo tic kende kende. Piny utiye," ebolo cinge iwi anvil. “Nen in, jabodho, eni ubedo lum ma jucoko pa wara pa agura ma ngwec.”

"Lembe mi tyend agura, jadit? Pirango, Captain Ahab, itiye kany, dong, piny maber man ma tek ma wan ju ma timo tic ku cuma watiyo kudu."

"Angeyo, jadit; lum eni bibedo karacelu calu glue kud i cogo pa junek. Pio pio! yub ira harpoon. Man yub ira mir acel, thwon apar wi aryo pi tyende; i ngeye ilok, man ilok, man i goyo apar wi aryo eni karacelu calu thwol man thwol mi tow-line. Pio pio! Abigoyo mac."

Kinde ma juyubu lum apar wi aryo, Ahab utemo gi, acel acel, ku loko gi, ku cinge gire, ikum cinge mabor man ma pek. “Lembe ma rac!” kwero ma tok cen. “Tim kendu, Perth.”

Ka dong jutimo eni, Perth ubedu ka caku ketho apar wi ario ni acel, kinde ma Ahab ucungo cinge, man ewacu niya ebiketho cuma pare gire. Calu, ku cinge ma rwinyo, egoyo iwi anvil, Perth umio ire lum ma rieny, acel ingey acel, man forge ma tek ucungo iwi mac pare ma tek, Parsee ukadhu ling ling, man ebolo wiye piny ikum mac, nen calu ekwayu rac moko nyo mugisa moko ikum toil. Ento, kinde ma Ahab uneno malu, edaru cidho yo piny.

“Ango ma dul pa lucifers ma gibe ringo keca?” Stubb uwacu, ma eneno kud i forecastle. "Parsee eca ngwic mac calu fusee; man ngwic pare gire, calu musket's powder-pan ma lyeth."

I ajiki, shank, i rod acel ma pong, onwango lyethu pare mir ajiki; man calu Perth, mi jwigo, ebolo piny ne zo i pii ma ceng ceng, lyethu ma lyeth ucwale i wang Ahab ma utur.

"Icopo lwongo ira, Perth?" lworo pi kare manok ku remu; "Abedo ka yubu branding-iron para gira, dong?"

"Ker Mungu, ungo eno; ento alworu piny moko, Kapitan Ahab. Eni epe ku jam lwinyu pi White Whale?"

"Pi jajok ma rangine tar! Ento kawoni pi lum; umitu itim giri giri, ngati. Kaeni razors para--maber pa cuma; keni, man tim barbs ma bit calu pii ma ngic mi nam ma ngic."

Pi kare manok, jabodho ma jadit ne uneno razor ne calu nya ebemito eti kudgi.

"Ting gi, ngati, amito gi ngo, kum kawoni agoyo yuk wic, acama, man alego ngo nitundu—ento keni-pi tic!"

Juyike i ajikine i ayi pa thwol, man juyike ku Perth i shank, steel ne ucungo iwi thum pa iron; man ikare ma jabodho mi cuma ubedu ka mio lyethu migi mir ajiki, mapodi eketho gi, ekok ibang Ahab nia eketh agulu mi pii ceng.

"Ungo, ungo—pii epe pi eno; amito nia ebed ku ngei cwiny mandha. Ahoy, keca! Tashtego, Queequeg, Daggoo! Wuwacu ango, juyic! Wubimiyo ira rimo madwong ma copo umo barb eni?" mako malo. Dhanu mapol ma wic migi col gi dwoko, Eyo. Jutimo turo adek i ring pa ju ma gi juyic, man judwoko cing pa White Whale.

“Ego non baptizo te in nomine patris, sed in nomine diaboli!” Ahab ukok ku lworo, calu cuma ma rac ecamu rimo pa batizo.

Kawoni, ma etingo yen ma juketho piny, man eyero acel pa yen mi hickory, ku lum ma podi ucungo iwie, Ahab uketho ajikine i dhugola pa cuma. I ngeye juwodho coil pa tow-line manyen, man juwodho fathoms moku i windlass, man juwodho ni tension madit. Ediyo tyende iwie, nitundo thung ne ucungu calu thung wer, man edham egoro iwie, man eneno ngo nia ngati moku ucungo iwie, Ahab uwacu, "Ber! man kawoni pi mako."

I thenge acel, thol ne ubedu ma jukubo ngo, man thwol ma jupoko eca ubedu ma juyubu man juyubu i thenge pa harpoon; yen eca judham ucungo matek i socket; kud i thenge ma piny, thwol ne ucungo nusu pa bor pa yen ne, man ucungo matek, ku cinge ma ucungo iwi cinge. Ka dong jutimo eni, yen, cuma, man thung—macalu Three Fates—ubedo ma jupoko ngo, man Ahab uringo ku jam lwiny ne; dwol pa tyende macol, man dwand pa hickory pole, zo gi winjere iwi yen moko ci. Ento mapodi emondo ngo i ot pare, dwand ma yot, ma rwinyo ngo, ma rwinyo, ento ma rac uloyu uwinjere. Oh, Pip! ngyeru peri ma rac, wang peri ma timo tic ungo ento ma ling; mummeries peri ceke ma dhanu ungiyo ngo ma rwinyo ngo ku can macol pa yei ma rac, man enyayu ikume!
