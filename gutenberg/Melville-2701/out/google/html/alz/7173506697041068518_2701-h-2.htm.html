CHAPTER 4. The Counterpane.

I kare ma acer uthieno ma lubo, anwango cing Queequeg ucungo iwi koma i yo ma ber man ma ber. Icopo paru niya abedo dhaku pare. Counterpane ubedo pa patchwork, ma upong ku rangi matung tung ma nen ungo man adek; man cinge eni ma jugoro iwie ku lanyuth pa Cretan labyrinth ma rwinyo, ma thenge ario ope ma rangi pa rangi acel—pi the lembe ma eketho cinge i nam i ceng man i tipo, cinge ma cinge ucungo iwi kare matung tung—cinge eni, awacu, nen calu piny acel acel lewic. Andha, ka abedo iwiye calu cinga utimo ikare ma acer, nangu acopo ngo ni ngeyo kud i bongo, gi loko rangi migi karacelu; man ubedu kende kende ku nen pa pek man tego ma acopo wacu niya Queequeg ubedu ka mako an.

Lembe ma awinjo ubedu ma ukoc. Wek atem ni gonyo gi. Kinde ma abedo nyathin, apoi maber ikum lembe moko ma rom ku maeni ma utime ira; kadi nangu ubedu lemandha nyo lek, nangu acopo yio ngo. Lembe ne ubedo eni. Abedo ka poko caper moko nyo mange—aparu niya ebedu ka temo cidho malu i chimney, calu ma aneno sweep ma nok timo ceng moko mukadho; man min ot para ma, yo moko nyo mange, ubedu ka goyo ira kare ku kare, nyo cwalu an i kavutu ma cam mi diewor ope,—minwa utingo an ku tyenda kudi i chimney man etingo an i kavutu, kadi nang ubedu sawa aryo kende mi diewor mi nindo 21 mi dwi mir abusiel, ceng mabor uloyu i oro i ng'om mwa. Awinjo marac akeca. Ento kony moko ubedu pee, ci adok malu i ot para ma nok i wi ot mir adek, awoko kendi para moth moth pi ni jwigo sawa, man ku yuyu ma rac amondo ikind bongo.

Abedo keca ma cwinya lith ka aparu nia sawa apar wiye abusiel ucikere ni kadhu ma podi acopo bedo ku genogen mi cer. Sawa apar wiye abusiel i kavutu! ng'ete ma nok ugam urem ka aparu pire. Man ebedo yot de; ceng ube ryeny kud i wang dirisa, man dwand mutukari madongo i ngudu, man dwand pa ju ma maru timo ribiri i ot zo. Awinjo marac man marac—i ajiki a ay malu, akendo bongo, man adok piny moth i tyenda para ma ucungo, ayenyo min ot para, man apodho iwi tyende, akwaye nya emiya kony maber pi kura para marac; piny moko cii andha ento nyayu ira nia abed iwi kavutu pi kare malac. Ento ebedo min ot maber man ma timo tic maber uloyu, man i ngeye nangu adok i ot para. Pi sawa madwong abedo keca ma anyang, awinjo marac akeca ma sagu ma awinjo con, kadi kud i peko madit ma ulubo. I ajiki ne, abino apodho i lek marac mi nindo; man ka acer moth moth kud i iye—ma nusu upong ku lek—ayabu wanga, man ot ma ceng ucwale kudu kawoni dong upong ku mudho ma woko. Ndhu ndhu awinjo lworo moko ubekadhu kud i koma zo; piny moko epe ma nen, man piny moko epe ma winje; ento cing moko ma tek nen calu juketho i cinga. Cinta ucungo iwi counterpane, man ngati ma nyinge mbe, ma dhanu copo paru ngo, ma ling, ma cinga ubedo pare, nen calu ebedo inget kavutu para. Pi lembe ma nen calu oro madwong, abedo keca, lworo ma rac uloyu, ma cwinya utur ungo; ento nangu aparu niya ka nangu acopo yengo inch acel kende, jok marac eca copo tur. Angeyu ngo kite ma ngec eni ukadhu kud i iya; ento kinde ma acer uthieno, apoi ikum lembe eca zo, man pi nindu man yenga man dwi mapol i ngeye abedo ka rwenyo gira i lembe ma nyayu lworo mi gonyo lembe ma nyang ikume. Ungo, nitundu kawoni, abedo ka paru paru para giri.

Kawoni, kwany lworo ma rac, man paru para mi winjo cing ma tek i cinga ubedo marom, i bedo migi ma ukoc, ku ma awinjo kinde ma acer man aneno cing pa Queequeg ma ucungo iwi cinga. Ento i ajikine, lembe ceke ma utimere dyewor mukadho udok kendo, acel acel, i lemandha, man i ngeye abedo kwo kende pi peko ma nyayu ngyeru. Pi kadi nangu atemo loko cinge—yabo cinge mi nyom—ento, ka enindu, egam emako cinge matek, calu tho kende re ma copo poko wan ario. Atimo kero mi cwale—“Queequeg!”—ento lagam pare acel kende ubedu nyai. I ngeye aloko wiya, nguta uwinjere calu ngati ma ucungo iwi kola pa agura; man ndhundhu awinjo ret moko manok. Ka wabolo counterpane woko, tomahawk ubedo ka nindu inget savage, calu nyathin ma wange nen calu hatchet. Pickle maber, andha, aparu; abedo kaneni i ot moko ma dhanu ngeyo ongo i diewor, ku jacam dhanu man tomahawk! "Queequeg!—i nying berocwiny, Queequeg, cer!" I ajiki, ku timo lembe madwong, man wec ma tek man ma rwinyo ngo ikum bedo pare ma mako ngati wadi i yore mi nyom, agam awok ku koko; man kawoni, edwoko cinge cen, etingo kume zo calu gwok mi Newfoundland ma wok kud i pii, man ebedo iwi kavutu, ma tek calu pike-staff, eneno an, man eyubu wange calu nya epoi ungo kite ma abino kudu keca, kadi nya ngeyo lembe moko ikume ubedu ka wok nyathotho. Ikinde marom, abedo moth man anene, manangu atie ku lworo moko ope, man abedo ka neno lei moko ma mitire. Kinde ma, i ajikine, paru pare nen calu edaru mako kura pa wadi, man edoko, calu, edaru ku lembe ne; epodho woko iwi diere, man ku lanyuth moko man dwand moko emiyo anyang nia, ka eber ira, ecopo ruko kendi ma kwong man ebiweko an aruk kendi i ngeye, man ebiweko ot zoo pira kena. Aparu an, Queequeg, ithe lembe maeni, eni ubedo lembe maber mandha; ento, lemandha utiye, ju ma kwiny eni gitiye ku paru mi bedo maber, wec ango ma imito; eber kite ma gibedo ku woro. Amio foc maeni ni Queequeg, kum etimo ira ku kura maber man ku paru, ma an abedo ku dubo mi timo marac; neno kude kud iwi kavutu, man neno timo pare mi lwoko cing; pi kare ma mitu para mi ngeyo lembe ubedo ber pa dongo para. Ento, ngati ma calu Queequeg ma ineno ngo kubang ceng, en ku yore pare ubedu ber mandha.

E ucaku kendi maber ku ketho bongo pare ma dit, ma bor mandha, inget, man i ngeye—ma podi eweko trousers pare—egam enwango wara pare. Ango ma etimo i polo, acopu ngo ni wacu, ento timo pare mukende ubedu mi turo kume gire—wara i cinge, man kofi—i the kavutu; kinde ma, kud i ngwec ma tek man lworo, anwango nya ebedo ka timo tic matek; kadi nang epe ku cik mi bedo ber ma awinjo pire, dhanu moko ci ucikere ni bedo kende ka eketho wara pare. Ento Queequeg, ineno, ubedu lei moko ma ubedu i rwom mi loko kwo pare—kadi caterpillar kadi butterfly. Ebedo ngati ma rwom pare lapiny ma romo nyuthu bedo pare ma woko i yore ma nen ungo. Somo pare podi uthum ungo. Ebedo nyathin musomo i rwom ma malu. Ka nangu ebedo ngo ku ngec ma nok, nwango ebedo ngo ku peko mi wara; ento i ngeye, ka nangu ebedo ngo ngati ma rac, nangu ecopo paru ngo mi mondo i the kavutu pi ketho gi. I ajiki, ewok ku kofi pare ma nyai man ma nyai iwi wange, man ecaku ngur man nyai i ot, calu, ma engeyo ngo wara, wara pare ma pii, ma nyai ma juyubu ku adila pa dhyeng—ma copo bedu ngo ma juyike nimakere ku cik—egam enyayu man enyayu can i kume i acaki pa ceng ma ngic ma ngic.

Ka waneno, kawoni, nia epe ku curtains i wang dirisa, man nia yo ubedo nyanok, ot ma yo tung eca umiyu neno maber i ot, man waneno kite ma Queequeg utimo ko, ma ubewotho ku piny moko manok kende ento bongo pare man wara pare kende; Akwaye kite ma acopo, ni dwoko ot lwoko cinge pio pio, man ni mondo i pantaloons pare pio pio. Egam eyio, man edham ecaku lwoko kume. I saa maeca i uthieno, Jakristu moko ci copo lwoko wange; ento Queequeg, ma uketho cwinya utal, egam eketho cinge iwi kore, cinge, man cinge. Egam eketho koti pare, man etingo sabun ma tek iwi meza mi lwoko bongo, ebolo i pii man ecaku lwoko wange. Abedo ka neno kama egwoko iye razor pare, eka nen, etingo harpoon kud i kor kavutu, ewotho ku yen ma bor, ewotho wiye, ewotho nyanok iwi wara pare, man ewotho malu ikum kiyo ma ucungo ikum ot, ecaku nyayo lak cinge matek, nyo harpooning ikum wange. Aparo ni an, Queequeg, eni tiyo ku jam tedo maber pa Rogers ku lworo. I ngeye, abedo ku jiji manok ikum tic eni kinde ma angeyo ikum cuma maber ango ma juyubu ku wiye pa harpoon, man kite ma cinge ma bor man ma rwinyore kudu.

Toilet pare mukende ugam uthum oyot, man ewok kud i ot kud anyonga, ma eruko jaket pare madit mi pilot monkey, man etingo harpoon pare calu baton pa marshal.





CHAPTER 5. Cam miwikugweno.

Alubo kore pio pio, man ka adok piny i ot amata, agam anwango won ot ma ube ngiero maber akeca. Abedo ku paru marac ikume ngo, kadi nang ebedu ka skylarking kuda nyanok ikum lembe pa wadi.

Ento, ngyeru maber ubedo piny maber mandha, man epe piny maber; kisa umedire. Ka dong, ka ngati moko, i kura pare gire, umiyu piny moko maber ni ngati moko ci, kud ebed ngati ma ngeye, ento wek ebed ku mutoro ni tiyo ku sente man ju tiyo kudu i yore meno. Man ngati ma etiye ku piny moko ma nyayu ngyeru ikume, bed ma ingeyo nia etiye ku piny madwong i ngati meno ma sagu ma iparu.

Ot mi madhu kongo dong upong ku ju ma gibedo ka bino i dyewor mukadho, man ma podi aneno ngo maber. Gibedu ju ma gimaru rec ma julwongo ni whalemen; judongo, man judongo mir aryo, man judongo mir adek, man ju ma timo tic mi gedo, man ju ma timo tic mi nam, man ju ma timo tic mi gedo, man ju ma timo tic mi harpoon, man ju ma gwoko yei; kampuni macol man ma tek, ku yuk wic ma tek; dul ma ungwe, ma yuk wic, zo uketho jaket pa monkey pi gowns miwikugweno.

Icopo wacu maleng nia ngati acel acel ubedo i dhu nam pi kare ma romo adi. Ng'atu ma nyathin eni ma kume yot ubedo calu pear ma juwango i ceng, man nen calu nguce pa musky; ecopo bedu ngo nia edaru wok ceng adek kud i wotho pare mi India. Jadit eca ma lubo kore nen maleng nyanok; icopo wacu nia yen moko mi satin utiye i iye. I adila pa ngati mir adek podi utiye ku rangi macol, ento edoko macol nyanok; edaru bedo pi yenga mapol i dhu nam. Ento nga ma copo nyutho lak calu Queequeg? ma, ma jugwoko ku rangi matung tung, nen calu Andes' western slope, ni nyutho i rwom acel, ma tung tung pa kite ma koth ku ceng ryeny kudu, kabedo ku kabedo.

“Grub, ho!” kawoni won ot owacu, eyabu dhugola, man wamondo i cam miwikugweno.

Giwacu nia coo ma gineno ng'om, gibedo ku kura maber, man gibedo ku kura maber ikind ju. Ento kare ku kare ngo: Ledyard, jawoth madit mi New England, man Mungo Park, ja Scotland acel; Ikind coo zo, gibedo ku gen ma nok uloyu i ot. Ento nyalo bedo ni kadho Siberia kende ku sledge ma gwok tingo calu ma Ledyard utimo, nyo wotho mabor kende kende ku ic ma thwol, i kind dhanu macol mi Afrika, ma ubedo dul pa tic pa Mungo ma jacan—wotho maeni, awacu, copo bedu ngo yore maber loyo mi nwangu rwom ma malu. Ento, pol kare, piny ma kumeno ucikere ni bedu kama moku ci.

Lembe ma wabeparu kawoni eca utimiri pi lembe ma utimere ma ingeye ma wabedo piny i meza, man nangu abe yiko winju lembe moko maber ikum mako rec; ku lworo ma nyanok, dhanu ceke gibedo ling. Man ungo kende, ento gineno lewic. Eyo, kan eca ubedu dul pa thwol mi nam, mapol ikum gi manangu gibedo ku lewic manok kende ma gimondo i nam madit—ma gin dhanu ma gingeyo ongo—man githo kugi manangu giweko wang gi; man podi, keca gibedo piny i meza mi cam miwikugweno-gi zo gilwongo acel, gi zo gitiye ku paru marom-gibedo ka gineno ngati acel acel ku lewic calu nya gilar gibedo woko kud i wang rombe moko ikind got macol. Neno piny ma nyayu lworo; lei ma lewic eni, ju ma lworo lworo eni!

Ento pi Queequeg—pirango, Queequeg ubedo keca ikind gi—i wi meza, de, ebedo lembe ma rwinyo; ma ngic calu icicle. Mi bedo andha, acopo ngo wacu lembe madwong ikum dongo pare. Ngati ma maru pare madit copo ngo ni ketho cinge ikum kelo harpoon pare i cam miwikugweno kude, man etiyo kudu keca manangu etimo ngo foc; ucwalu cinge iwi mesa kude, ni lembe ma copo wok ikum wi dhanu mapol, man utingo ringu ringo i bange. Ento eni ubedu timo maber akeca kude, man dhanu ceke ung'eyo niya i paru pa dhanu mapol, timo piny moku maber utiye timo ku mwolo.

Wabeweco ngo ikum kit pa Queequeg keni; kite ma ekwero kofi man hot rolls, man eketho cwinye ikum beefsteaks, ma jutimo nyanok. Eromo, nia kinde ma cam miwikugweno udaru, edok cen calu ju mukende i ot pa lwak, ewango tomahawk-pipe pare, man ebedo keca ling ling ka camu man madhu taba ku koto pare ma jupoko ngo, ikare ma awok woko pi wotho.





THE 6. Yo.

Ka nangu abedo ku lworo ikare ma aneno ngati moko ma nen ungo calu Queequeg ma ubewothu ikind dhanu ma kite ber mi adhura ma dongo dongo, lworo ne ugam uthum pio pio ikare ma awotho i yo mi New Bedford.

I yore madongo ma ceng ku dhu nam, dhu nam moko ci ma dit bibedo ku kare mi neno piny ma nen ungo ma wok kud i ngom mawoko. Kadi bende i Broadway man Chestnut streets, ju ma giwotho i nam mi Mediterranean gicopo nyayu mon ma lworo unego gi. Regent Street ungeyere ngo ni Lascars man Malays; man i Bombay, i Apollo Green, Yankees ma kwo uketho lworo i dhanu mi kabedu ne. Ento New Bedford loyo Water Street ku Wapping zo. I kabedo maeni ma juweco pigi mir ajiki, ineno ju ma giwotho ku yei kende; ento i New Bedford, ju ma camu dhanu gicungu kaweco i dhu ngundu; savages outright; pol migi podi gitingo cogo migi ma leng ungo. Etimo ngati ma jungeyo uneno.

Ento, muweko Feegeeans, Tongatobooarrs, Erromanggoans, Pannangians, man Brighggians, man, muweko lei makwiny ma rwinyo iwi ngudu, ibineno piny mukende ma nyayu ngyeru, man ma nyayu ngyeru. Kuca yenga ku yenga bino i adhura eni dhanu mapol mi Vermont man New Hampshire, ma gi mito nwangu bero man dwong i tic mi maku rec. Pol migi gitiye aradu, ku kum ma tek; juwadi ma gi turo lum, man kawoni gibemito gibolo axe man gikwalu pala pa rec ma julwongo ni whale-lance. Pol migi ubedo macol calu got macol ma giai kud i iye. I lembe moko icopo paru nia gitiye pi sawa manok kende. Nen keca! ngati eca ubewotho i kor ot. Ekendo kofira pa beaver man koti ma yuk wic pa swallow, ma eketho cinge i kum sailor-belt man sheath-knife. Kae bino mukende ku sou'-wester man bombazine cloak.

Dandy moko mbe ma junyolo i adhura ma copo poro ku ngati ma junyolo i adhura—abeweco ni dandy ma jamutoro—ngati ma, i ceng pa gwok, ecopu poko ngom pare aryo ku gloves pa buckskin pi lworo mi nyayu rangi pa cinge. Kawoni ka ngati ma maru ngom ma kumeno utingo i wiye pi timo nying maber, man emondo i tic mi mako rec madit, umitu inen lembe ma nyayu ngyeru ma etimo ikare ma etuc i dhu nam. I wecu ikum kendi pare mi nam, ekwayu bell-buttons i waistcoats pare; cinge ikum kor bongo pare. Ah, Hay-Seed ma can! kite ma cinge eca bi nyai kudu i yamu ma tek mir acel, kani juwodhe, cinge, cinge, man zo, piny i dhu yamo madit.

Ento kud ipar nia adhura ma pire tek eni utiye ku ju ma tiyo ku harpoon, ju ma camu dhanu, man ju ma camu dhanu kende ma gi nyuthu ni welo pare. Ungo. New Bedford podi utiye kabedu ma dhanu ma timo lembe ma tung tung. Ka nwang epe pi wan ju ma gwoko rec, ngom eca tin eni copo bedu i lembe ma rac calu dhu nam mi Labrador. Kaka etiye, thenge moko mi ng'om pare ma ngeye romo lworo ngati, nen calu cogo. Kabedo eni gire ubedo kabedo maber uloyu mi bedu i iye, i New England zo. Etiye ngom pa moo, andha: ento epe calu Kanaan; ngom, de, pa kodhi man kongo. Yore wok ku cak ungo; kadi i kare mi cwir gi ketho tong' ma nyen i wigi. Ento, kadok kumeno de, kabedo moko mbe i Amerika ceke ma ibinwango i iye ot ma calu pa patrician; kabedo mi yom cwiny man podho ma leng, uloyu i New Bedford. Gi ay kud kani? nenedi ma jupodho iwi scoria ma con ubedo macol pa ngom?

Cidhi inen harpoons ma juyubu ku cuma ma ucungo iwi ot madit ma malu, man penji peri jubi gamu. Eyo; udi eni zo man podho ma dongo dongo uai kud i nam Atlantic, Pacific, man Indian oceans. acel acel, ju goyo gi ku jam lwiny man ju tingo gi malu keca kud i the nam. Herr Alexander copo timo lembe ma kumeno?

I New Bedford, wego, giwacu, gimiyo rec ma dit ni nyithindho migi ma mon, man gipoko nyithindho migi ma nyaku ku porpoises manok. Umitu icidh i New Bedford ni neno nyom maber; kum, giwacu, gitiye ku ka gwoko moo i ot acel acel, man dyewor ku diewor gi wango piny migi i tara pa spermaceti.

I kare mi cwir, adhura ne leng ni neno; upong ku yen mabecu—yore mabor macol man macol. Man i Dwi mir Abora, malu iwi yamu, lum ma leng man madwong ma julwongo ni horse-chestnuts, candelabra-wise, mio ​​ni ju ma ukadhu dhugola migi. Omnipotent ubedo art; ma i adhura mapol mi New Bedford uketho rangi ma leng pa thiwe iwi kidi macol ma jubolo woko i ceng mir ajiki pa cwic.

Man mon mi New Bedford, gi dongo calu thiwe migi makwar. Ento roses dongo kende i kare mi cwir; ento carnation maber pa wang gi ubedo ma rii pi ceng i polo mir abiro. Kaka mange rombu ku thwol migi, icopo ngo, mako i Salem, kama giwacu ira nya anyira ma fodi gitie aradu yuyu musk ma kumeno, ju ma gimaru gi ma giwotho ku yei giwinjo nguce migi mabor ku dhu nam, calu nya gibedu ka tuc ceng ku Moluccas ma ngucere kakare pa Puritanic sands.





THEK 7. Ka lega.

I New Bedford eni, ot pa Whaleman's Chapel ucungu, man ju ma mako rec manok kende, ma gibecidho i nam madit mi India nyo Pacific, ma giweko timo limo mi ceng yenga i kabedu ne. Angeyu niya atimo ngo.

Ka adok kud i wotho para mir acel mi uthieno, adok kendo i tic ma pire tek eni. Polo ulokore kud i ngico ma leng, man ma ngic, ni koth man koth. Abedo ku jaket para ma nyap ma julwongo ni bearskin, alwenyo ku yamu ma tek. I kare ma amondo, anwango dul pa ju ma timo tic i yei, man mon pa ju ma timo tic i yei man mon ma coggi tho. Ling moko ma ling ubedu nuti, ma sawa moko juturo ku koko pa yamu matek. Jawor acel acel ma ling nen calu ebedu kende kende ku wadi, calu nya can moko ci ma ling ubedu kende man ma jucopu tito ngo. Jatel wi kanisa podi utuc ungo; man keca cula ma ling pa coo ku mon gibedo piny ma gineno kidi macol, ku wange macol, ma jugoro i kor ot ku thenge ario mi kanica. Adek ikind gi gi timo lembe moko calu ma e, ento an atimo ngo nya aloko:—

PIPOYO JOHN TALBOT, ma, i oro apar wi abora, etho i nam, ceng ku cula mi Desolation, i Patagonia, nindo 1, mi Dwi mir Aparacel , 1836. TABLET ENI juyike pi poyo wic pare KU nyamin.

PI POYO PA ROBERT LONG, WILLIS ELLERY, NATHAN COLEMAN, WALTER CANNY, SETH MACY, MAN SAMUEL GLEIG, ma gibedo acel ikin jutic mi yei mi yei ELIZA Ma juwodhe woko kud i wang dhanu ku Whale, I Ofisi, i December mi Pace 31 st , 1839. MARBLE ENI juketho kany ni ju ma gikwo kugi ma gikwo.

PIPOYO NI POYO PA JAPTEN EZEKIEL HARDY, ma i the yei pare junego ku Sperm Whale i dhu nam mi Japan, nindo 3 mi dwi mir abora , 1833. TABLET ENI juyike pi poyo wic pare KU dhaku pare ma cware tho.

Ka awodho koth kud i koto para man koti para ma ice-glazed, abedo piny inget dhugola, man aloka yo piny man abedo iworo nineno Queequeg inget kuda. Ku kite ma lembe ne nen kudu, nen pa wange ma nyayu lworo i wange. Jaracik eni ubedo ngati acel kende ma ubedu nuti ma nen calu eneno dhumo para; kum en kende re ma copo kwanu ngo, man, kumeno, ebedo ka kwanu ngo lembe ma jugoro iwi ot. Ka nyo jupagi moko pa ju ma giwotho i nam ma nying gi uwok keca kawoni gitiye ikind jucokiri, nangu angeyu ngo; ento pol pa ret ma jugoro ngo i tic mi mako rec, man nen maleng nia mon mapol ma gini keca gi ketho wang gi ka epe ku jam lworo moko ma rwinyo ngo, ma awinjo andha nia i wanga eca jucokiri, ma i adunde migi ma rwinyo nen pa karatasi macol eca uketho ret macon ucwalu remo manyen.

Ai! wun ma ju m'utho peri juyik ithe lum macol; ngati ma ucungu ikind thiwe copo wacu—kani, kani jara para; wung'eyo ngo nyoth ma wok i kor dhanu ma calu maeni. Lembe ma rac nenedi i marbles ma rangi migi col ma umu ngo buru! Lembe ma nyayu can i lembe ma jugoro ma rwinyo ngo! Lembe ma rac ma nyayu tho man timo ma rac ma dhanu ungiyo ngo i lembe ma nen calu ubenyayu yioyic zo, man kwero cer pa dhanu ma githo manangu liel ope. Man bende copu bedu ni karatasi eni ucungu i vur pa Elephanta calu keni.
