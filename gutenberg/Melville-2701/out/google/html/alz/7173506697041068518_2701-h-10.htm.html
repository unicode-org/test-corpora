Man pi peko ma nok man lworo, genogen mi peko ma wok nyathotho, peko mi kwo man cing; lembe eni zo, man tho kende, nen ire kende kende lembe ma nyayu anyonga, ma ber, man ma nyayu anyonga i cinge ma jumiyo ni jadhum ma nen ungo man ma dhanu copo dwoko wange ngo. Kit kura ma rac ma abeweco pire, bino iwi dhanu kende i saa moko mi can madit; ebino i kind bedo moth pare, kara gin ma con nen ire ni piny ma pire tek, kawoni nen calu dul pa tuko. Piny moko mbe ma rom ku peku mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whaling pi nyayu lembe maeni ma yot man ma yot man ma ber man ma rwinyo dhanu; man kude kawoni aneno wotho pa Pequod, man White Whale madit ni piny pare.

"Queequeg," awacu, kinde ma gi tingo an, jadit mir ajiki, i wi yei, man podi abedo ka yengo koti para pi rwinyo kud i pii; "Queequeg, jarimba maber, lembe ma kumeno timere pol kare?" Labongo paru madwong, kadi nya edaru nen calu an, emiya nyang nia lembe ma kumeno timere pol kare.

"Mr. Stubb," awacu, aloko wanga ibang ngati ma pire tek, ma, ma utingo jaket pare mi moo, kawoni ubedu ka madhu taba pare i koth; "Mr. Stubb, aparo ni awinjo ka iwacu nia ikind ju ma gwoko rec ma julwongo ni whaleman, Mr. Starbuck, utiye ngati ma gwoko man ma rieko uloyu. Aparu nia, ni cidho iwi rec ma mol ku yei peri ma juketho i yamo ma tek utiye rwom ma malu pa ju ma gwoko rec?"

“Eyo. Adok piny pi rec ma dit ma wok kud i yei ma mol i yamo ma tek i Cape Horn.”

"Mr. Flask," awacu, aloko wanga ibang King-Post ma nyathin, ma ucungu inget; "Ibedo ku ngec ikum lembe eni, man an epe. Icopo wacu ira ka etiye cik ma copo loke ngo i tic mi mako rec eni, Mr. Flask, pi jaring yei ni turo ngeye gire man edwoko kume cen i lak tho?"

“Icopo loko ngo piny ma nok?” Flask uwacu. "Eyo, eni cik. Amito anen jutic mi yei ma gibe dwoko pii ikum wang rec ma dit. Ha, ha! rec ma dit eca copo miyo gi wang ma nen ungo, poi ikume!"

Kae, kud i bang juruneno adek ma timo lembe marac, abedo ku lembe ma jukoro ikum kesi ne zo. Ka waparu, dong, nia yamu ma tek man ma nyai i pii man bedo i pii ma thuc, ubedu lembe ma timere pol kare i kwo ma kumeno; ka waneno nia i saa ma pire tek mi cidho i nam ma julwongo ni whale, umitu awek kwo para i cing ngati ma utelo wi yei—pol kare ngati ma i saa maeca ebedo i rwom ma malu mi turo yei ku cinge gire; ka waparu nia peko ma wok ikum yei mwa eca ubedu pi Starbuck ma ubedu ka ringo ku yei pare ma dit, man ka waparu nia Starbuck, kadi kudu, ubedu ngati ma ungeyere pi bedo moth pare i tic mi maku rec; ka iparu niya abedo ikind yei pa Starbuck ma ber mandha; man i ajiki ka paru ikum timo pa jajok ma ubedu ka lubo kora, mulo White Whale: ka akwanu piny ceke karacelu, awacu, aparu niya acopo cidho piny man atim waraga mi paru para. "Queequeg," awacu, "bin, ibibedo janen wi cik para, jatel wi tic, man jatel wi tic para."

Ecopu nen ni lembe ma nyang nia ikind dhanu ceke, ju ma giwotho i yei umaku gibed ka timo aloka loka i kum lembe ma gimito man waraga mi lembariba migi, ento dhanu moko mbe i ng'om ma gimaru timo eno. Eni ubedo wang mir angwen i kwo para mi nam ma atimo lembe marom. I ngei thum pa foc ikum lembe ma kawoni, awinjo yot; kidi moko juwodho kud i cwinya. Bende, nindu ceke ma umitu akwo kawoni bibedo maber calu nindu ma Lazaru ukwo i ngei cer pare; adwogi maber pa dwi madwong nyo yenga madwong. Abedo kwo pira kena; tho para man yik para ubedu ma jugwoko i koma. Aneno piny ma ling man kud anyonga, calu tipo ma ling man ma cwinye leng ma bedo i ot ma jugwoku i iye pa juru ot.

Kawoni, aparu, ma angeyo ngo ka abolo cinga iwi bongo para, eni ubecidho pi pii ma ngic, ma jucoko i tho man nyoth, man jajok utingo ma tok cen.





THEK 50. Yei pa Ahab ku jutic pare. Fedallah.

"Nga ma copo paru, Flask!" Stubb owacu; "Ka nangu atiye ku tyend acel kende icopo maku ngo i yei, kawang nya i juku vur ku tyend cinga. Oh! etiye jadit maber akeca!"

"Aneno ngo nya etiye lembe ma nyang ikume, i ngei lembe ceke, ikum lembe meno," Flask uwacu. "Ka tyende ubedo iwi kore, kawoni, nwango ubedo piny mange. Eni copo ketho ebedo goro; ento etiye ku tyende acel, man thenge mukende maber udong, ingeyo."

“Angeyo ngo eni, nyathin para ma nok; podi anene ngo ka epodho piny.”

Ikind dhanu ma ngeyo lembe pa rec ma dit, dhanu mapol giwacu nia, ka waneno beru pa kwo pare pi bero pa wotho pare, eber ni jatel wi rec ma dit ni ketho kwo pare i ariti i kare ma etiye ka lubo tokgi. Pieno, askari pa Tamerlane gibed giweco ku ywak i wang gi, ka nyo kwo pare ma pire tek eca umaku juter i lwiny.

Ento ku Ahab penji ne ugam ulokore. Ka waparu nia ku tyende ario dhanu ubedo kende kende jabodho i saa ceke mi peku; ka iparu nia lubo tok rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whales ubedo i peko madit man ma lee dit; nia saa moko ci, andha, uketho i iye peko; I thenge maeni, eber ni ngati moko ci ma goro ni mondo i yei mi whale-boat pi dwar? Calu piny ma rwinyo, wegi piny pa Pequod umaku gibed giparu nya epe.

Ahab ung'eyo maber niya kadi bende jurimo pare mi pacu giparu nya emondo i yei i lembe moko ma rac ungo, pi bedu kago ku kabedu mi lwiny man miyo cik pare giri, ento pi Captain Ahab ni bedu ku yei ma jupoko ire calu jatel wi tic mi dwar—mandha pa Captain Ahab ni bedo ku dhanu abic mukende, jutic mi yei marom, engeyo maber niya paru maber ma kumeno ugam umondo ngo i wic pa wegi yei mi Pequod. Pieno, egam ekwayu ngo jutic mi yei kud i bang gi, man de ebed enyutho ngo paru pare ikumgi. Ento edaru timo lembe pare gire ikum lembe ceke ma pire tek. I kare ma Cabaco unwangu ngo, ju ma giwotho i yei gilar gineno ngo, kadi nya, ingey bedo woko kud i nam, dhanu ceke gi daru tic migi mi ketho yei ma dit pi tic; kinde moko i ngeye Ahab ugam unwange ka timo thole-pins ku cinge gire pi gin ma juparu niya ubedo acel mi yei ma juweko, man kadi ku kero mi turo skewers ma nok ma juyubu ku yen, ma ka cinge uthum jubedu ka ketho iwi ng'et yei: kinde ma lembe eni zo nen i ie man bedo ku mer ma lee eketho piny i the yei, calu nya eketho ecungo maber i cinge macol pa ivory; man bende lworo ma enyuthu i yiku thigh board, nyo clumsy cleat, kite ma julwonge kudu kare moko, piny ma ucungo iwi yei pi ketho tyende i kum darting nyo turo whale; kinde ma juneno kite ma en ucungu kudu i yei ku tyende acel kende ma ucungo i thenge ma ling ling i cleat, man ku cing jabodho ma jugoro nyanok keca man edwoko piny nyanok keca; lembe eni zo, awacu, ukelo miti man mitu mi ngeyo lembe madwong i kare ne. Ento dhanu mapol giparu niya bedo moth pa Ahab ucikere ni bedu kende kende pi lubo tok Moby Dick; pi en edaru nyutho paru pare mi dwar lei ma tho eca i wange. Ento paru ma kumeno uketho ngo lworo moko ikum jurutic mi yei moko ci ma juketho i yei meno.

Kawoni, ku phantoms ma piny, ango ma udong uthum pio pio; kum i whaler, lemandha rwinyo nyathotho. Mi mede, kawoni ku kawoni lembe ma rac man ajiki pa thek ma dhanu ngeyo ongo bino kud i kabedo ma dhanu ngeyo ongo man i vur mi ng'om ni dhanu ma gikwo i pii; man yei gire pol kare gitingo lei ma nen ungo ma nwangire ka ringo iwi nam iwi bao, jamb yei, yei, yei, yei, jamb tic pa Japan, man piny mukende mapol; nia Beelzebub en gire copo tingo iwi ngete man edok piny i cabin pi wecu ku jatel wi meli, man ecopo kelo ngo anyonga moko ci i forecastle.

Ento kadi bed ni lembe eni zo, lemandha utiye nia kadi bende ju ma gikwo i the loc ne ginwangu kabedo migi ikind jutic, kadi nang edoko tung tung kudgi, ento Fedallah ma wic pare udong ubedo lembe ma dhanu ung'eyo ongo. Ka ma ewok kud i iye i ng'om ma ku kura ma kumeno, ku kit cing ango ma enyutho kudu nia etiye ku winjiri ku lonyo pa Ahab; epe, nitundu ikum bedo ku rwom moko ma nen ungo; Polo ngeyo, ento copo bedu nya ebedo ku twero iwie; lembe eni zo ngati moko mbe ma ngeyo. Ento ngati moko copo bedu ngo ku paru ma rac ikum Fedallah. Ebedo lei moko ma rwom pare lapiny, dhanu mi ot ma gikwo i kabedo ma ngic gineno kende i lek migi, man eno nen ungo; ento ju ma calu gi kawoni man kawoni giwotho ikind thek mi Asia ma lokore ngo, asaga cula mi Oriental ma yo piny pa ngom—piny ma jugwoko, ma rwinyo, ma juloko ngo, ma kadi i nindo ma kawoni podi gigwoko kwo pa dhanu macon mi ng'om, kinde ma poi pa dhanu ma kwong'a ubedo piny ma pire tek man dhanu pare ceke ungiyo ngo kama en uai i iye, gineno ngati acel acel calu piny ma nen ungo, man gipenjo ceng ku dwe pirango ma jucwio gi man pi ango; kadok, nimakere ku Thiwiwec, malaika gibedo karacelu ku nyithindho ma mon pa dhanu, jok ma rac de, medu ku Rabbins ma uncanonical, ma gibedo ku mer ma rwinyo.





CHAPTER 51. Tipo-Spout.

Nindu, yenga ukadhu, man i the yei ma yot, Pequod ma rangi pa ivory ukadhu moth moth iwi kabedo angwen matung tung; ma nwang i Azores; i dhu nam mi Cape de Verdes; iwi Plate (ma julwongo kumeno), ma nwangre i dhu Rio de la Plata; man Carrol Ground, kabedo ma pii ope i iye, yo piny pa St. Helena.

Ubedo ikare ma wabewotho ikind pii ma tok cen eni, dyewor moko ma ler man ma ler pa dwe ucwale, ma pii eca ubekadhu calu karatasi mi silva; man, ku lyethu migi mayom, ma nyai, gi ketho piny ma nen calu ling, ento ngo bedo kendgi; I dyewor ma ling ma kumeno, jet ma rangi pa silva juneno mabor ku pii ma tar ma nen i wange. Lero pa dwe, nen calu polo; nen calu nya mungu moko ma mol man ma rieny ucungo kud i nam. Fedallah ugam unyutho pi jet eni. Pi dyewor maeni ma dwe ryeny, ubedu kura pare ni cidho iwi wi meli madit, man ni cungo ka neno woko, ku kite maber marom ku ma ceng ubedu ceng. Ento, kadi bende dul pa rec ma julwongo ni whales juneno dyewor, ngati acel de mbe ikind dak acel ma copo ketho gi piny. Icopo paru ku paru ango, dong, ju ma giwotho i nam gineno Oriental macon ma ucungo iwi polo i sawa ma nen ungo; turban pare man dwe, jurimo i polo acel. Ento kinde ma, ingey bedo i kare pare mi yunifom keca pi dyewor madwong ma eweco ngo dwal acel de; kinde ma, ingey ling eni zo, dwond pare ma nen ungo iwi ngom uwinje ka tito jet ma rangi pa silva, dwe, jalweny moko ci ma ucungo iwi tyende ucaku cungu iwi tyende calu tipo moko ma tiye ku bwome uwok i yei, man efoyo jutic mi yei. “Eni efoyo!” Ka nwango thum pa pokolembe ugoy, nangu gicopo yeng ungo; ento podi giwinjo ngo lworo; ento anyonga. Pi kadi nya eca ubedo sawa ma rwinyo, ento koko ne ubedu maber akeca, man ubedu kud anyonga madit, ma dhanu ceke ma ubedu i yei ne ubedu mitu juwodhe piny.

Woth iwi yei ku ngwec ma nyap, Ahab ungolo t'gallant sails man royals ni juyik, man stunsail acel acel jupoko. Ng'atu maber uloyu i yei ucikere ni telo wi yei. I ngeye, ku mast-head acel acel ma juketho i iye, yei ma jucoko ubedo piny i wang yamu. Lembe ma nyang ikume tek, ma nyayu lworo, man ma tingo piny pa yamu pa taffrail ma pongu vur pa yei madwong, uketho deck ma ucungo iwi yei nen calu yamu ithe tyend dhanu; podi edaru ringo, calu nya piny ario ma rac ubedu ka nyayu lwiny i iye— acel ni cidho i polo, acel ni cidho i polo. Man kani ineno wang Ahab uthieno eca, nwang iparu niya i iye de piny ario matung tung ube lwiny. I kare ma tyende acel makwo ubedo ka winjo dwand ma tek i wi yei, cinge acel acel ma tho ubedo ka winjore calu sanduku mi tho. I kwo man tho jadwong eni uwotho. Ento kadi nangu yei ne uringo pio pio, man kadi nang dhanu ceke gineno, calu adungu, nen pa dhanu ma cwinygi tek, ento jet ma rangi pa silva ne nen ungo uthieno eca. Jatel wi yei moko ci ukwoni nia eneno wang acel, ento ungo wang mir aryo.

Piny ma wok kud i diewor eca ubedo piny ma juwilo, ikare ma, inindu moko i ngeye, nen! i sawa marom ma ling, judwoko kendo: kendo juweco pire ni dhanu ceke; ento ikare ma etimo yei mi kadho, edok etho kendu calu nangu ebedo ungo. Man kumeno etimo tic iwa dyewor ku dyewor, nitundu ngati moko ope ma winjo lembe pare kende kende ni bedo ku wur ikume. I yore ma dhanu ungiyu ngo i lero pa dwe, nyo lero pa lakalatwe, calu ma copo bedu; rwinyo kendu pi ceng acel, nyo ceng aryo, nyo adek; man nen calu i timo moko ci ma nen ni ube medere anyim man anyim i van mwa, ndege ma kende eni nen calu ube ywayo wa.

Kadi ku yioyic ma con pa thek migi, man nimakere ku preternaturalness, calu ma nen, ma i lembe madwong uketho Pequod, ubedu nuti ma mitu jumoko mi ju ma giwotho i nam ma gikwo ni saa moko ci man kama moko ci ma jukoro; kadi i kare mabor, nyo i latitude man longitude mabor, pii ma dhanu copo nwangu ngo eca jubolo ku rec acel kende; man rec ma dit, Moby Dick. Pi kare moko, lworo moko ubedu nuti ikum piny ma ubetimere eni, calu nya ebedo ka lwongu wa, kara lei eca ulok ikumwa, man utur wa i nam mabor man ma rac.

Lembe ma nyayu lworo eni, ma nen ungo ento ma rac, unwangu tego ma lee dit kud i kwiocwiny pa koth, ma i iye, i thenge mi blue blandness pare, dhanu moko giparu niya piny moko ma rac utiye, calu pi nindu man nindu ma wawotho i iye, ikind nam ma oling, ma yot, ma kabedo ceke, pi culo kwor, pi culo kwor mwa nen calu eweko kwo pare i wang urn-like prow mwa.

Ento, i ajikine, kinde ma wadok yo piny, yamu mi Cape ucaku koko ikumwa, man wa ay malu man wapodho iwi nam mabor man ma nyai ma nuti keca; kinde ma Pequod ma lak thwol ucungo iwi yamo, man edaru pii macol i ngei pare, nitundo, calu koth pa silver chips, foam-flakes utingo iwi bulwarks pare; i ngeye, kwo ma ling eca ukadhu, ento eweko kabedo ni neno ma rac uloyu macon.

Kachiel ku thwol mwa, piny ma nen ungo i pii uringo kuca man kuca i wangwa; kinde ma i ngeye mwa ubedo ka ngwec iwi nam ma julwongo ni sea-ravens. Man kugweno kugweno, ka wabedo iwi kabedo mwa, nen dul pa winy eni; man kadi bende wabe koko, pi kare malac wabedo ka mako hemp, calu ma gineno yei mwa ni yei ma mol, ma dhanu ope i iye; piny ma juyike ni jwigo, man kumeno eromo kabedo mi bedo pa ju ma gipe ku pacu. Man utingo man utingo, podi utingo nam macol, calu ka pii pare ma dit ubedo paru; man tipo madit mi kwo eca ubedu i can man can pi dubo man can ma enyayu.

Cape of Good Hope, gilwongo wu? Ento Cape Tormentoso, calu ma julwongo con; pi kare malac ma wabedo ku lworo ikum ling ma ukadhu ikumwa, wanwango wabecidho i nam ma rac eni, kama dhanu ma timo dubo gilokore ni winy man rec eni, nen calu jupoko lembe ikumgi ni dhuny iwi pii rondo ku rondo manangu epe ku kabedo moku ci, nyo ni goyo yamo macol ma epe ku kabedo moku ci. Ento ling, tar calu pee, man loko ngo; podi ubetelo wi pii pare mi yuk iwi polo; podi ube lwongo wa nicaku con, ndege ma kende kende jubiweco pire.

I kind piny macol eni, Ahab, kadi nya ebedo ku twero mi telo wi yei ma pii man ma rac, enyutho bedo macol uloyu; man ebed eweco ku juwadi nyanok nyanok. I saa mi koth ma tek calu maeni, i ngei ma piny ceke ma malu man malu udaru bedo maber, piny mukende epe ma jucopo timo ento kende kende ni kuro lembe pa yamu ma tek. I ngeye Captain ku jutic pare gidoko ju ma timo tic mi tho. Pieno, ku tyende macol ma juketho i vur pare, man ku cinge acel ma utingo bongo, Ahab pi sawa madwong ucungu ma eneno yamu, ma yamo ma tek nyo pee ucwalu yuk wange. Ikinde marom, jurutic ma juwodho kud i kore pa yei ku nam ma rac ma ucwalu wi yei, ucungu i lain i dhu kor yei; man ber pi gwoko ikum pii ma ucungo, ngati acel acel ugam ucungo i bowline moko ma juketho i rail, ma ebed ewotho i iye calu i belt ma juwodho. Wec manok nyo epe ma juweco; man yei ma ling, calu ju ma giwotho ku rangi mi wax, ceng ku ceng ubekadhu ikind lworo man anyonga pa pii ma rac. I diewor, bedo ling marom pa dhanu mapodi koko pa nam ucungo; podi i ling coo ne giwotho i bowlines; podi Ahab ucungu iwi dhuny. Kadi bende ka kwo ma olo nen ni mitu yom cwiny, ebed eyenyo ngo yom meno i kom ot pare. Starbuck copo weko ngo kite ma jadit ne nen kudu, ikare ma diewor moko ecidho piny i ot madit ni neno kite ma barometer ucungu kudu, enene ku wange ma umu ma ebedo atira i kom kom pare ma juketho iwi ngom; koth man koth ma nyai pa yamu ma tek ma en ugam uwok kud i iye, podi ubepodho moth moth kud i koto man koti ma juwodho ngo. I wi meza ma ceng kude ubedo acel pa chart pa pii man pii ma juweco pire con. Lantern pare ucungo kud i cinge ma ucungo matek. Kadi bende kume ubedo ucungu, wiye jubolo ngeye kara wange ma umu nen ikum sindhani pa tell-tale ma ucungo iwi yen ma ucungo iwi ot.*

*Cabin-compass julwonge ni tell-tale, kum manangu ibecidho ngo i compass ma ni i the helm, Captain, podi etiye piny, ecopu ngeyo kite ma yei ubecidho kudu.

Jadit marac! Starbuck upenu ku lworo, enindu i yamo madit eni, podi ibedo ka neno paru peri.





THEK 52. Albatross.

Yo piny yo reto kud i Cape, iwi Crozetts mabor, kabedu maber mi wotho pi Right Whalemen, yei moko ucungu anyim, Goney (Albatross) ku nyinge. Kinde ma ebino dhugola moth moth, kud iwi kom para ma malu i wi yei, abedo ku neno maber pa piny ma nen ma ber mandha ni tyro i nam mabor—ngati ma mako rec i nam, man ma ukadhu kud i pacu pi kare malac.

Calu nya pii upong, yei eni ubedo macol calu cogo pa walrus ma ucungo. I thenge pare zo, kite ma nen pa piny eni nen ku rangi macol macol, ma spars pare man rigging pare ubedu calu jang yen ma gwai ma upong ku hoar-frost. Kende kende yei pare ma piny re ma juketho. Neno piny ma nyayu lworo ubedo nineno yuk pare ma bor ma neno wi yei adek eca. Ginen calu girwako adila pa lei, uketho gipoko man gipoko kendi ma ukadhu pi oro angwen mi wotho i yei. Gicungu iwi cing cing ma jugoyo iwi yei, giwotho man giwotho iwi nam ma dit; man kadi, kinde ma yei ne ucungo moth moth i the ngete mwa, wan dhanu abusiel ma wabewotho i polo wabino kago ku wadi ma wacopo rwinyo kud i wi yei acel ni wi meli mange; ento, ju ma mako rec ma nen calu ju ma gibedo ling, ma gineno wa moth moth kinde ma gibe kadho, giweco ngo lembe moko acel de ni ju ma gineno wa, podi koth ma ucungo iwi yei ubedo ka winjere kud piny.

"Meli ahoy! Ilar ineno White Whale?"

Ento kinde ma jatel wi mony, ma ucungo iwi got macol, ubedu ka ketho thum pare i dhoge, epodho kud i cinge i nam; man yamu kawoni ube medere malu, etimo kero mi winjere manangu epe. Ikind kare eni, yei pare ubedu ka medu bor pa kindgi. Kinde ma i yore matung tung ma ling ju ma giwotho i nam mi Pequod gibedu ka nyuthu nen pa lembe ma rac eni ikare ma juweco nying White Whale i yei mange, Ahab oling pi kare manok; nen calu ecopo dwoko yei piny pi emondo i jal ma engeyo ngo, tek yamu ma lworo ugam ukwero ngo. Ento ni tiyo ku rwom pare mi yamo, egam etingo tum pare, man engeyo nia yei ma engeyo ngo ubedo Nantucketer man kawoni edok i pacu, ewacu ku dwal ma tek—"Ahoy kuno! Eni ubedo Pequod, ma ecidho i ng'om zoo! Wac igi nia gi cwal waraga ceke mi anyim i nam madit mi Pacific man oro adek, ka atie ngo i pacu, abewecu igi! I --"

I saa maeca, rec ario ne gicokiri, man ndhundhu, nimakere ku yore migi, dul rec ma nok ma rac ungo, ma pi nindo moko mukadho ubedu ka dhuny i ngete mwa, uringo ku cinge ma nen calu ube yeng yeng, man ucungu anyim ku ngeye ku cinge pa jadwong ne. Kadok ikind wotho pare ma nja Ahab ucikere ni neno lembe marom, ento, ni ngati moko ci ma maru timo lembe acel kende, lembe ma nok nok tiye ku the lembe.

“Wuwok kud i banga, kodi?” Ahab uwacu, ma eneno i pii. Nen calu lembe manok kende i wec, ento dwal ne unyutho can ma lee dit ma jadongo ma jamutoro ne unyutho con. Ento eloko wange ni jatel wi yei, ma kawoni ubedu ka mako yei i yamu pi jwigo rwom pare, ekok ku dwal pare macon calu lamvor,—“Up helm! Wek en kud i ng'om zoo!”

I ng'om zoo! Lembe madwong utiye i dwand meno ma nyayu anyonga; ento kani ma circumnavigation eca timo kudu? Kende kende nikadhu kud i peku mapol nitundo ikare ma wacaku kudu, kama ju ma waweko i ngeye gibedo maber, ubedu kare ku kare i nyimwa.

Ka ng'om eni ubedo lum ma jik ungo, man ka wawotho yo piny wacopo tuc kamabor manyen, man nwangu piny ma leng man ma nen ungo ma sagu Cyclades nyo cula pa rwoth Solomon, nwango genogen ubedu nuti i wotho ne. Ento i lubo tok lembe ma dhanu ung'eyo ngo ma wabeparu, nyo i lubo tok timo pa jok ma rac ma, saa moko nyo mukende, ucungo i nyim cwiny dhanu ceke; ka wabe lubo tok piny ma kumeno iwi ngom ma ling eni, gi telo wiwa i yo ma ling ling nyo gi weko wa ma cwinya lith.
