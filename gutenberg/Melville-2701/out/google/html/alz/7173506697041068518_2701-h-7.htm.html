CHAPTER 33. The Specksnyder.

Pi lembe pa judongo pa yei, eni nen calu kabedo maber mi ketho piny moko ma pire tek i wi yei, ma wok kud i bedo pa judongo pa harpooneer, rwom ma jungeye ngo i nam mange ma mako yei pa rec.

Ber pa tic pa harpooneer nenre kamaleng, nia i acaki i Dutch Fishery macon, oro miya aryo man makadhu mukadho, telowic pa yei ma dit ma julwongo ni captain ubedu ngo iwi ngati ma kawoni julwongo ni captain, ento jupoko ikind en ku jadwong moko ma julwongo ni Specksnyder. Lembe eni the lembe eni thee Fat-Cutter; tic kudu, kadi ango, i kare moko uketho edoko rom ku Chief Harpooneer. I ceng eca, twero pa jatel wi yei ubedu kende ikum telo wi yei man telo wi yei; podi iwi dipatmen mi dwar pa rec madit man lembe pare zo, Specksnyder nyoke Chief Harpooneer re ma ubedo rwoth madit. I British Greenland Fishery, i the nying ma rac pa Specksioneer, jadit macon mi Dutch podi jugwoko, ento woro pare macon judwoko piny. I kare eni etiye i rwom ma malu pa Harpooneer; man calu ma kumeno, etiye acel ikin jutic ma piny pa jatel wi mony. Ento, calu ikum kura maber pa ju ma mako rec, bedo ber pa wotho mi maku rec ma dit ucungo iwi, man pi en i American Fishery etiye ngo jatel wi yei kende, ento i thenge moko (gwok dyewor i kabedu mi maku rec) telo wi yei de utiye pare; kumeno cik madit mi tela mi nam kwayu, nia ebed ekwo kende kende ku coo ma nen i wang yei, man ebed ku rwom moku ma pire tek calu jatel wi tic migi; kadi nya kare ku kare, ku gin, gineno gi ni dhanu marom ku migi.

Kawoni, tung tung madit ma nen ikind jadwong tic ku dhanu i nam, utiye eni—kwo mir acel kwo ngeye, mir ajiki anyim. Pieno, i yei ma dit ma julwongo ni whale man ju ma timo kuloka, ju wadi gibedo ku kabedo migi ku jadit meli; man kumeno, bende, i pol pa ju America ma mako rec, ju ma mako rec gibedo i ngeye pa yei. Eni nyutho nya, gi camu cam migi i ot pa jatel wi meli, man gi nindo i kabedu ma giweco kude.

Kadi bende kare malac mi wotho mi maku rec ma julwongo ni Southern whaling voyage (ma ubedo wotho mabor uloyu ma dhanu timo kawoni nyo con), peko ma wok kud i iye, man dul pa dhanu ma gimaru ikind kampuni, ma gin ceke, malu nyo piny, gicungo pi bero migi, ungo iwi cul ma juketho, ento iwi gum migi, karacelu ku bedo moth, bedo moth, man tic matek; kadi bende lembe eni zo timo i kare moko nyayu cik ma tek ungo ni dhanu ma timo kuloka; ento, kud ipar kite ma dhanu mi Mesopotamia macon gikwo kudu karacelu; pi lembe eni zo, piny ma woko ma pire tek, ma nok, pa quarter-deck epe nya yot, man edaru timo ngo. Andha, pol pa yei mi Nantucket ma ibineno jatel wi meli ne ubewotho ku rwom ma malu ma rwom pare nen ungo i meli moko ci mi mony; epe, eketho foyofoc ma nen kamaleng calu kani ebedo ku bongo macol pa rwoth, man epe ku bongo macol pa jaring mutukari.

Man kadi bende ikind dhanu ceke, jatel wi meli mi Pequod ma cwinye lith ubedu ngati ma ubedu ku paru ma nok uloyu; man kadi bende foc acel kende ma etimo, ubedu winjo lembe ma rwinyo, ma rwinyo; kadi nang emito ngo nia ngati moku uwodh wara kud i tyende mapodi emondo iwi yei; man kadi nya kare moko ubedu nuti ma, pi lembe moko ma ukoc ma mako lembe ma bitimere i ngeye, eweco kudgi i wec ma ukoc, kadi i lembe mi lworo nyo in terrorem , nyo mange; ento kadi Captain Ahab de ubedu ngati ma ungiyu ngo ikum kite man tic pa nam.

Bende, nyalo bedo ni, ok bi bedo ni ng’ato ng’eyo bang’e, ni bang’ yore ma ng’ato tiyogo, kaka ne en, kinde moko ne oumo wiye; timo tic kudgi pi lembe mange man ma pire tek uloyu ma dhanu copo timo ngo. Sultanism moko mi wic pare, ma ubedu i rwom maber ubedu ma unyuthire ngo; nikadhu kud i kit lembe eni, sultanism marom eno udoko i tela ma tek ma dhanu copo kwero ngo. Pi bedo ngati ma rwom pare malu, ecopu ngo ni bedo ku twero madit ikum dhanu mukende, manangu epe ku kony pa ngec moko ma woko man rwom ma malu, kare ku kare, i igi gigi, ma rwinyo man ma rwinyo. Eni re, ma rondo ku rondo gwoko rwoth pa Mungu mandha mi ker kud i dhuny pa ng'om; man eweko woro ma malu ma yamu eni copo mio, ni dhanu ma doko ngati ma nying gi ungiru lee nikadhu kud i bedo migi ma piny ma rwinyo pa Divine Inert, makadhu kud i bedo migi ma malu ma rwinyo dhanu mapol. Ber ma dit eca ucungo i piny ma nok eni ka yioyic ma tek mi tela uketho i iye, ma i lembe moko pa rwoth kadi ni ju ma mingo gi timo kero. Ento ka, calu i lembe pa Nicholas the Czar, wic mi ker mi ng'om ucungo iwi wic pa rwoth; i ngeye, dul pa plebeian ucungo piny i wang poko piny madit. Bende, jagor mi drama ma nyayu can ma copo nyuthu kwo ma dhanu copo tho kudu i rwom pare ma malu man i rwom ma malu, ebiweko ngo lembe moko, ma pire tek i tic pare mi goyo cal, calu ma kawoni juweco pire.

Ento Ahab, Captain para, podi ubewothu i nyima i Nantucket pare ma rac man ma nyap; man i lembe maeni ma mako Emperors ku Kings, umitu kud abed aling nya atiye ku tic kende ku jadwar rec ma dit ma jacan calu en; man, pi the lembe meno, piny ceke ma nen woko man ot jukwero ira. Oh, Ahab! ango ma bibedo madit i ii, umitu juwodhe kud i polo, man juwodhe i pii ma thuc, man juwodhe i yamu ma dhanu ope i iye!





CHAPTER 34. The Cabin-Table.

Ubedo dyeceng; man Dough-Boy, jatel wi tic, ucwalu wange macol ma nen calu mugati kud i cabin-scuttle, unyutho cam mi diewor ni rwoth pare man rwoth pare; ma, bedo i lee quarter-boat, ubedu ka neno ceng; man kawoni etiye ka kwano latitude iwi tablet ma leng, ma nen calu medallion, ma jugwoko pi tic ma kubang ceng iwi thenge ma malu pa tyende ma rangi pa ivory. Ni kadhu kud i bedo pare ma rwinyo lembe, icopo paru niya Ahab ma cwinye lith uwinjo ngo lembe pare. Ento kawoni, maku mizen shrouds, eloko kume iwi deck, man ku dwal ma rwinyo, ewacu, “Cam mi diewor, Mr. Starbuck,” eweko i cabin.

Kinde ma dwand pa sultan pare uthum, man Starbuck, Emir mir acel, utiye ku tyen lok ceke mi paru niya ebedo piny, eka Starbuck ucungu kud i ling pare, ewotho iwi yen, man, ingey neno i binnacle, ewacu, ku mutoro moku, “Cam mi diewor, Mr, stubbs man scuttles. Emir mir aryo ubedo ka ling ikum rigging pi kare manok, man edham yengo brace madit nyanok, mi neno ka nyo ebibedo maber ku thung ma pire tek, en bende etingo tic macon, man ku wec ma nyap "Dinner, Mr. Flask," elubo tok ju m'utelo wigi.

Ento Emir mir adek, kawoni enenre kende iwi yei mir angwen, nen calu ewinjo agonya kud i lembe moku ma jukethe; pi, ma eketho wange ma ngeyo lembe i yore matung tung, man ebolo wara pare woko, epodho i dwand ma tek ento ma epe ku dwand pa hornpipe iwi wic pa Grand Turk; man i ngeye, ku ryeko, ebolo kofira pare malu i mizentop pi shelf, ecidho piny ma ucungo mabor ma nen ungo kud i wi deck, eloko ngwec mange ceke, ku kelo wer i ngeye ku wer. Ento ma podi emondo ngo i dhugola mi ot ma piny, ecungu, enyutho wange manyen, man, i ngeye, Flask ma nok ma jamutoro man ma nyayu ngyeru umondo i wang rwoth Ahab, i kite pa Abjectus, nyo opii.

Epe piny ma nok ikind piny ma dhanu ungiyu ma wok kud i tic mi nam, nia podi gitiye i polo ma yot pa deck, judongo moko, ka jubenyayu lembe i wigi, gibikwo ku tego cwiny man ku lworo i kum jatel wigi; ento, apar ku acel, wek judongo eca i saa mukende gicidh piny i cam migi mi diewor i ot pa jatel wi mony acel, man ndhundhu ne gi nen ku wang gi ma rac, man gi nen marac i kume, ma en ebedo i wi meza; eni ber mandha, kadi sawa moko nyayu ngyeru. Pirango ma tung tung eni? Peko? Nyo epe. Nibedo Belshazar, rwoth pa Babeli; man ni bedu Belshazar, ungo ku kwiny ento ku woro, andha ucikere ni bedu ku rwom moko ma pire tek. Ento ngati ma ku cwiny ma rwinyo man ma rieko telo wi meza pare mi cam pa welo ma julwongo, tego pa ngati meno man tego pa ngati acel acel pi kare meno; rwoth pa jadhum eca uloyo pa Belshazar, kum Belshazar ubedu ngo jadit. Ng'atu ma udaru camu jurimu pare wang acel kende, udaru nwangu ango ma bedu Cæsar. Etiye timo pa rwoth pa dhanu ma dhanu copo juku ngo. Kawoni, tek i lembe maeni imedo rwom pa jatel wi yei, ci, ku paru, ibinwango the lembe ma nyayu kwo mi nam ma juweco pire.

I wi meza pare ma juyubu ku lum, Ahab ubedo calu lamvor mi nam ma ling, ma yiku wiye iwi dhu nam ma rangine tar, ma juguro ku nyithindhe ma gimaru lwiny ento ma podi gibedo ku woro. I kare pare gire, jadwong tic acel acel ubedo kakuru ni jukony. Gibedu calu awiya ma thindho i wang Ahab; man podi, i Ahab, nen calu epe ku rwom ma nok mi kwo pa dhanu. Ku paru acel, wang gi zo nen ikum pala pa jadit ne, ma en eyubu ajeni madit i nyime. Aparu ngo niya pi ng'om zoo gicopo nyayu lembe marac ikum kare meno ku neno manok kende, kadi ikum lembe ma pire tek calu kite ma koth ku ceng ryeny kudu. Ungo! Man kinde ma etingo pala pare ku fork pare, ma ikind gi ubedo ringu dyang, Ahab unyutho Starbuck i cinge, jadhoge ugamu ringo pare calu ngati ma ugamu kony; man thume maber; man ecaku nyanok tek, nyalo bedo, pala ne ucungo iwi sahan; man ecamu ma epe ku koko; man umwonyo, ungo manangu engeyo ngo. Pi, calu foc mi yik pa rwoth i Frankfort, kama rwoth mi Germany camu ku judongo abiro mi rwoth, kumeno cam eni ubedo cam ma pire tek, ma jucamu i ling ma rac; man podi i meza Ahab ma jadit ukwero ngo wec; kende en gire re ma ubedu ling. Ebedo ber mandha ni juku Stubb, ikare ma oyo moko utimo racket iwi hold piny. Man Flask ma nyathin ma jacan, ebedo nyathin ma tokcen, man nyathin ma nyathin mi foc mi pacu ma ool. Mukene ubedo cogo pa ring dyang ma saline; pare ubedo jamb tedu. Pi Flask ni bedo ku paru mi konye gire, eni ucikere ni nen ire calu kwalu piny i rwom mir acel. Ka nwang ekonyo gire i meza eca, m'umbe jiji, nwang ecopo bedu ngo ku copo mi tingo wiye malo i ng'om maeni ma atira; kadi kudu, lembe ma ukoc ni wacu, Ahab ukwero ngo. Man tek Flask ukonye gire, Ahab copo bedu nya ungiyu ngo. Ma nok uloyu, Flask uparu nya ecopo konye gire ni butter. Ka nyo eparu nia wegi yei ne gikwero ire, pi lembe ma nyayu remo i kume maleng man ma ceng; nyo ka eneno nia, i wotho mabor ma kumeno i pii ma jumaku ngo, moo ubedo piny ma pire tek, man kumeno ubedu ngo ire, piny ma pire tek; kadi kudu, Flask, alas! ubedo jadhum ma epe ku moo!

Piny mange. Flask ubedo ngati mir ajiki piny i cam mi diewor, man Flask ubedo ngati mir acel ma ucungu malu. Kethi iparo peri! Pi eni cam pa Flask ubedo ma upong marac i kare ma mitire. Starbuck ku Stubb zo gicaku kude; man podi gitiye ku gum mi bedo i ngeye. Ka Stubb kadi, ma ubedo peg acel kende ma malu uloyu Flask, etiye ku mitu mi cam manok, man kawoni enyutho lanyuth mi thum pa cam pare, ci Flask umitu etim kero pare gire, ecopo nwangu ngo cam ma kadhu adek i ceng eca; kum etiye ikum tic maleng ni Stubb ni telo Flask i deck. Pieno, Flask ugam uyio i cinge kende, nia nicaku ma enwangu rwom pa jadit, nicaku i kare ne engeyo ngo ango ma bedu kec, madwong nyo manok. Pi gin ma ecamu uketho kec pare uthum ungo, ento eweko kec pare bedo ma tho ungo i iye. Kuc man bedo kud anyonga, Flask uparu, uweko ic para rondo ku rondo. Atiye jadit tic; ento, kite ma amito kudu nya acopo mako rec ma con i forecastle, calu ma abed atimo ikare ma abino i wang mast. Nitie nying yen mi rwom ma malu kawoni; nuti bedo ma rwinyo pa dwong: nuti bedo ma rwinyo pa kwo! Mi mede, tek ebedo kumeno nia jaring yei moko ci mi Pequod ubedu ku ngei ikum Flask i tic pa Flask, gin ma jaring yei umitu etim, pi nwangu cul ma lee, ubedu ni cidho i ngeye i sawa mi cam mi diewor, man ni neno Flask kud i lero mi polo mi cabin, m'ubedo m'umbe lworo man m'ubedo ku lworo i wang' Awabhab.

Kawoni, Ahab ku juwadi adek gicaku gin ma jucopo lwonge ni meza mir acel i ot pa Pequod. I ngei wotho migi, ma utimiri i ayi ma juloko ni tuc migi, bongo ma juyiko ku cing juyike, nyo judwoko i ayi moko ma rwinyo pa jatel wi tic. Man i ngeye ju lwongo juwer adek ni bino i cam, gin re ma gibedo ju m'udong. Gitimo kit ot pa jurutic ma rii pi kare manok mi ot ma malu man ma tek.

I tung tung ma ukoc ku rwom ma tek man rwom ma nen ungo pa meza pa jatel wi meli, ubedu bedo agonya man bedo yot, democracy ma nyai pa ju ma rwom gi piny ma julwongo ni harpooneers. Kinde ma rwoth migi, juwadi, nen calu lworo ikum dwand cing gi, ju ma gi goyo harpoonees gicamu cam migi ku mit ma lee dit ma uketho jubedu ka lwongo. Gi camu calu rwoth; gipong ic migi calu yei pa ju India ceng' zoo ma upong ku jamb tedu. Queequeg ku Tashtego gibedo ku miti ma lee dit, ma pi nipongo kabedu ma ukadhu, pol kare Dough-Boy macol ubedo ku miti mi kelo baron madit mi salt-junk, ma nen calu juwodho kud i dhyeng ma tek. Man ka ebedo ngo ku kwo ikum lembene, ka ebedo ngo ku ngwec ma yot, ci Tashtego ubedu ku yore ma rac mi medo kero pare ku goyo fork i ngeye, calu harpoon. Man wang acel Daggoo, ma ucungo ku ngyeru, ukonyo Dough-Boy ni poyo wic pare ku mako kume, man ebolo wiye i vur madit ma yen, ma Tashtego, ku pala i cinge, ucaku ketho cinge ikume. Ebedo ngati ma lworo lworo, ma lworo, jara tic ma nen calu mugati; nyithindho pa jatel mugati ma rwinyo man jaratwo mi ot yat. Man ango ku nen pa Ahab macol, man limo pa ju adek ma rac eni, kwo pa Dough-Boy ubedu acel ma nja. Pol kare, ingey nenu ju ma tiyo ku harpooneers ma gitiye ku piny ceke ma gimito, ebed ering kud i cing gi man ecidho i pantry pare ma nok ma ceng kudu, man ebed eneno gi ku lworo kud i dhugola pare, nitundo piny ceke uthum.

Ubedo lembe maber nineno Queequeg mubedo inget Tashtego, ma ucungo ikum lak pare ma ucungo ikum pa ju India: ikum gi, Daggoo mubedo iwi ngom, pi kom ma ubedu ka kelo wiye ma ucungo ikum mutukari mi tho i carlines ma piny; i timo moko ci pa tyende madongo, ma weko ot ma piny yeng yeng, calu ka lyec mi Afrika ucidho i yei. Ento pi lembe eni zo, negro madit ne ubedo ngati ma kwero cam, ungo ni wacu ni dainty. Nen calu nya ecopo bedu ngo nya ku dhoge ma nok ecopu gwoko tego ma unyai ikind dhanu ma lac, ma rwom gi malu, man ma rwom gi malu. Ento, m'umbe jiji, jajok ma pire tek eni camu matek man emadhu pii ma lee; man nikadhu kud i ume ma lac enwango kwo maber pa ng'om. Ungo ku ringu dyang nyo ku mugati, dhanu madongo juyike nyo jumiyo igi cam. Ento Queequeg, ekadhu kud i cinge ma rac, ma rac akeca—ma Dough-Boy ma ubedu ka myel ubedu ka neno ka nyo lanyuth moko mi lak ubedu i cinge ma nyap. Man kinde ma ewinjo Tashtego ubewero wer pi en gire, kara juwodh cogo pare, jatel wi tic ma rieko ne uturo jam tedo ma ucungo i ngete i ot cam, ku ret pare ma wok nyathotho. Man de whetstone ma ju ma gi goyo harpoonees gi tingo i cing gi, pi pala migi man jam lwiny mange; man ku kidi ma jwigo, i cam mi diewor, gibed gi tiyo ku pala migi; dwand ma rac ne uketho Dough-Boy ma can ubedo ku kwiocwiny. Ecopo wil nenedi nia i ceng pare mi cula, Queequeg, pi acel, ucikere ni bedu ku dubo mi neko dhanu, man timo lembe marac. Alas! Dough-Boy! tic matek pa jatic ma rangi pa del kom pare tar ma kuro ju ma camu dhanu. Ecikere ngo ni tingo napkin iwi cinge, ento buckler. I kare maber, kadi, pi anyonga pare madit, jukwenda adek mi nam ma kadu gi ay malu man giwok; ni ithe pare ma yioyic, ma loko lembe mi ndra, cogo migi mi lwiny zo ucungo i igi i rwom acel acel, calu Moorish scimetars i scabbards.

Ento, kadi bende ju ma rangi migi col gi camu i ot, man gikwo keca; kadi bende, ni bedo piny moko ci ento gibedo ling i kura migi, gibed gibedo ngo i iye kende kende i saa mi cam, man i wang nindo, kinde ma giwotho i iye ni kabedo migi gigi.

I lembe acel eni, Ahab nen ungo ni ngati ma ukoc ku judongo mapol mi Amerika, ma, calu dul, gibedo ku paru niya ot pa yei ubedo migi; man nya etiye pi berocwiny kende ma ngati moko ci, i saa moko ci, juyiyu kudu keca. Pieno, i lemandha, juwadi man juwer pa Pequod jucopo wacu nia gikwo woko kud i ot ma dit ento ungo i iye. Pien kinde ma gimondo i iye, ubedu piny moko calu dhugola mi yo ma mondo i ot; lokore i iye pi kare manok, kende kende ni dwoko woko i ngeye; man, calu piny ma nja, bedo i polo. Man de gi rwenyo piny madwong ungo; i cabin ne onge ng’at ma ne dhi bedo gi osiepe; ikind dhanu, Ahab ubedu ngati ma dhanu copo nwangu ngo. Kadi bende jumedo nyinge i wel mi dhanu mi kanisa mi Jukristu, podi ebedo ngati ma wok kud i iye. Ekwo i ng'om, calu ma tokcen pa Grisly Bears ukwo i Missouri. Man calu ma Spring ku Summer udaru kadhu, Logan ma jakwo mi lum, m'ubedo i vur pa yen, ukwo i kare mi ngico keca, ma madhu tyende gire; dong, i oro pare ma rac, ma ukoc, tipo pa Ahab, ma jukubo i iye, ecaku camu iwi tyende macol pa kume!





CHAPTER 35. Mast-Head.

Ubedo ikare ma koth ubedu ber, ma i kare ma aloko ku jutic mukende mi yei, wi yei para mir acel ugam wok.

I kind ju America mapol ma gicamu rec ma julwongo ni whalemen, mast-heads jutiyo kudu i saa marom ku ma yei uwok kud i dhu nam; kadi nya ecopo bedu ku mayils elifu apar abic, man madwong, mi wotho i yei ma podi etuc ungo kama ecidho i iye. Man ka, ingey wotho mi oro adek, angwen, nyo abic, ebecidho i pacu ku piny moko ci ma utu i iye—wacu, agulu ma utu—eca, wi meli pare jugwoko ku dhanu nitundo i ajikine; man ungo nitundu skysail-poles pare wok ikind spires mi port, eweko gen mi maku whale acel.

Kawoni, calu tic mi cungo wi meli, i dhu nam nyoke i pii, utiye tic macon man maber, wek wawec ikume. Agam, nia ju ma kwong ma gicungo iwi mast-heads ubedu ju Misiri macon; kum, i lembe para mi sayu lembe, anwango ngo ma ukadhu iwang gi. Pi kadi bende kwaro migi, ju ma gigero Babel, umitu, ku tower migi, gibed giparu mi ketho mast-head ma malu uloyu kud i Asia zo, nyo Afrika de; ento (kare ma mutukari mir ajiki juketho i iye) calu ma kidi madit migi copo wacu nia edaru kadhu kud i wi bao, i yamu ma tek pa ngei pa Mungu; kumeno, wacopo ngo ni mio ni ju ma gi goyo ot Babeli ni rwom ma malu uloyu ju Misiri. Man nia ju Misiri gibedo thek ma gicungo iwi meli, utiye lembe ma ju ma gisomo lembe mi rundi ma con giyio, nia pyramid ma kwong eca juyike pi lembe mi ngiyo kor polo: paru ma juyike ku kit ma juyiko kudo i thenge angwen mi ot eca; ma kumeno, ku tingo tyend gi mabor, ju ma gisomo lembe iwi polo macon eca gibedo ku kura mi cidho iwi got, man giwero wer pi lakala manyen; kadi bende nen pa yei mi kare eni wer pi sail, nyo whale ma nen kende. I Saint Stylites, jakristu ma nyinge uyik i rundi ma con, ma eyiku ire kidi ma malu i lum man ekwo kwo pare zo iwi wiye, etingo cam pare kud i ngom ku jam tic; i iye watiye ku lanyuth maber pa jatel wi yei; ma jubi ryemu kud i kabedu pare ku koth, koth, koth, nyo koth; ento ku tego cwiny ma ucungo ikum piny ceke nitundo i ajikine, etho i kabedo pare. I kind standers-of-mast-heads mi kare eni, watiye ku set ma kwo ope; dhanu ma nen calu kidi, cuma, man bronze; ma, kadi nya gicopo wok ku yamu matek, podi gicopo ngo ni wero wer ka ginwangu piny moko ma nen ungo. Napoleon utiye; ma, iwi wi dul pa Vendome, ucungu ku cinge ma ucungo, futi acel ku pyer abic iwi polo; ling, kawoni, ngati ma bitelo wi decks piny; kadi Louis Philippe, Louis Blanc, nyo Louis the Devil. Great Washington, de, ucungu malo iwi yei pare ma dit i Baltimore, man calu acel pa Hercules' pillars, column pare nyutho rwom pa dhanu ma dhanu manok kende re ma copo kadho. Admiral Nelson, de, iwi capstan pa gun-metal, ucungu iwi mast-head pare i Trafalgar Square; man kare ku kare ma dhanu mapol ung'eyo ngo ku lyethu mi London, lanyuth podi jumiyo nya ngati moko ma pire tek utiye keca; kum kama iro utiye, mac umaku obed nuti. Ento kadi Washington madit, kadi Napoleon, kadi Nelson, bi dwoko ngo koth acel kud piny, kadi nang ecaku lwongo gi ni bedo jurimo ku juk migi ku deck ma nen ungo ma gineno; ento ecopu bedu, nia tipo migi bino i kind lyethu ma tek mi anyim, man giweco ikum got man kidi ma umitu juwek.

Ecopu nen ni epe ku rwom mi ketho mast-head standers mi ngom ku mi nam; ento i lemandha epe, nen kamaleng ku piny moko ma Obed Macy, jagor acel kende mi Nantucket, ucungu pire. Obed ma ber uwacu iwa, nia i rundi ma kwong mi mako rec, ma podi meli ubedu ka wok nja pi lubo tok rec, dhanu mi cula meno gigero cula ma malu i dhu nam, ma dhanu ma neno wigi giwotho ku cleats ma juketho i wi ot, piny moko calu winy ma cidho malu i ot ma malu. Oru manok mukadho, yub marom eni ugam utingo ku ju ma gikwo i Bay whalemen mi New Zealand, ma, ikare ma gi loko lembe ikum lei ne, gi miyo lwongo ni yei ma dhanu tiyu kudu ceng ku dhu nam.
