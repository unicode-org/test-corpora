THEK 81. Pequod urombo ku nyaku ma nyaku.

Nindo ma jular juyero utuc, man waromo ku meli Jungfrau, Derick De Deer, jadit, pa Bremen.

I kare moko dhanu ma dit uloyu kud i ng'om zoo, ju Dutch ku ju Germany dong gitiye ikind ju ma nok uloyu; ento keca ku keca i kind latitude ku longitude, podi iromo ku bendera migi i Pacific.

Pi the lembe moku, Jungfrau nen calu ebemito emi woro pare. Kinde ma podi bor ku Pequod, edham edaru, man ebolo yei, jatel wi yei ugam ucwale i bangwa, ma ucungu iwi yei kakare pa ngeye.

"Etie ku ango i cinge keca?" Starbuck owacu, ma enyutho piny moko ma ja German utingo ku cinge. "Ecopu bedu ngo!--lamp-feeder!"

"Eni epe," Stubb uwacu, "ungo, epe, etiye agulu mi chai, Mr. Starbuck; ebebino ka yubu iwa kawa mwa, utiye Yarman; ineno ngo tin ma dit eca ingete?—eni pii pare ma lyel. Oh! etiye maber, utiye Yarman."

"Cidh kudi," Flask owacu, "etiye piny mi mio mac man pii mi moo. Moo pare uthum, man ebino ka kwayu piny."

Kadi bende ecopu nen ni meli mi oil ubedo ka kwalo oil iwi ngom mi whale-ground, man kadi nang ecopo bedu tung tung ku wec macon ikum tero makaa i Newcastle, ento kare mukende lembe ma kumeno timere andha; man i lembe ma kawoni, Captain Derick De Deer utimo tic mi mio mac ni mac calu ma Flask uwacu.

Kinde ma ecidho iwi yei, Ahab ugam ucungo ire pio pio, manangu eparu ngo ikum gin ma etingo i cinge; ento i dhok pare ma nyai, Ja-Alemania unyutho nya engeyo ngo ikum White Whale; ndhundhu eloko wec i kum lamp-feeder pare man oil can, ku wec moko ma mako bedo pare mi loko i hammock pare i dyewor i mudho ma thuc—piny pare mir ajiki pa Bremen oil uthum, man rec moko acel de mbe ma jumako pi pongu peko ne; edaru ku wecu niya yei pare ubedo gin ma julwongo i Fishery ni yei maleng (niwacu, yei ma utu), ma uporre ku nying Jungfrau nyo Virgin.

Piny ma mitere ugam unwangu, Derick ucidho; ento edaru nwangu ngo thenge pa yei pare, kinde ma rec ma julwongo ni whales ugam ucungo iwi wi yei ario zoo; man Derick ubedu ku miti madit mi lubo tok jeni ne, ma eweko ngo ni ketho oil-can pare man lamp-feeder pare i yei, ewotho iwi yei pare man edaru lubo lamp-feeders pa leviathan.

Kawoni, tuko ne udok malu, en ku yei adek mukende pa Germany ma ulubo tokgi, ugam ucaku tic ku Pequod's keels. Ubedo ku rec abora, pod acel. Ngeyo peko migi, gibedu kawothu ku ngwec ma tek i nyim yamu, ka giwotho cing gi ikum cing gi calu thwol pa agura madwong. Giweko yik madit, ma lac, calu ju ma gibe loko waraga ma lac iwi nam.

Ma upong i ngwec ma nyap eni, man fathoms madwong in ngeye, ungwec ku dhyang madit, ma dit, ma ku wotho pare ma nyathoth, man ku rangi ma nya nya ma ucungo iwie, nen calu etiye ku jaundice, nyo two mange. Ka nyo rec ma julwongo ni whale eni ubedo pa pod eca, nen calu lembe ma nyayu penji; kum epe kura pa leviathans ma juworo ni bedo ku dhanu mange. Ento, ecungo ikumgi, kadi nang pii migi mi ngeye ucikere ni bedu nya eketho ebedo ling, kum cogo ma tar nyoke pii ma ucungo iwi dhoge ma lac ubedo ma nyai, calu pii ma wok ka pii ario ma rac urombo. Dwol pare ubedo nyanok, moth, man tic matek; wok ku yamo ma tek, man edaru wok i dul ma nyai, ma ulubre ku koko ma nen ungo i iye, ma nen calu ewok kud i thenge mange ma juyike, ma weko pii ma ngeye ucwale.

“Nga ma utiye ku paregoric moko?” Stubb uwacu, "etiye ku remo ic, lworo unyai. Rwoth, paru pi bedo ku nusu acre mi remo ic! Yamu ma rac ubegwoko Christmas i iye, awobi. Etiye yamu marac mir acel ma angeyo ni ucwalu kud i yo piny; ento nen, nyo whale yaw ubedo kumeno con? Ecikere nibedo tiller pare."

Calu Indiaman ma ucungo iwi dhu nam mi Hindostan ku dul pa agura ma lworo, ucungo, ucungo, ucungo, man ucungo iwi yo pare; kumeno bende rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale utingo piny pare ma dit, man kawoni man kawoni eloko wiye iwi cinge ma nyai, enyutho the lembe ma nyayu timo pare ma rac i cinge ma yor acam. Ka nyo edaru rwenyo cinge i lwiny, nyo junyole ma epe ku cinge, ubedu tek ni wacu.

"Kur nyanok kende, nyathin muti, man abimiyi sling pi cing ma ret," owacu Flask ma rac, ma enyutho cinge ikum dul pa rec ma julwongo ni whale-line ma ceng kudu.

"Get iri nia kud enyai kudu," Starbuck owacu. "Wek yo, nyo ja German bibedo kude."

Ku lembakeca acel, yei ceke ma gicungo karacelu gicungo pi rec acel eni, kum epe kende nia ebedo rec madit uloyu, man ebedo rec ma pire tek uloyu, ento ebedo ceng kugi, man rec mange de ubedu kawothu ku ngwec ma tek, man, ubedu ka kwero lubo tokgi pi kare ne. I kare eni, Pequod's keels ugam ucungo ikum yei adek pa Germany ma judwokogi piny; ento nicaku i acaki maber ma enwango, yei pa Derick podi utelo wi ngwec, kadi nang kare ku kare ju m'uai kud i ng'om mange gibedu ceng. Piny acel kende ma gi bedo ku lworo, ubedu, nya ebedo ceng ku rwom pare, ecopu bedu ku copo mi tiyo ku cuma pare mapodi gi kadhu ngo man gi kadhu. Pi Derick, enen ni etiye ku genogen nia ebibedo kumeno, man kare ku kare ku cinge ma nyayu ngyeru eyubu lamp-feeder pare i yei mange.

"Gwok ma jamutoro man ma foyofoc ungo!" Starbuck owacu; "Enyayu man enyayu ira ku sanduku ma rac ma apong ire dakika abic mukadho!"—man i wec pare ma tek—"Wek yo, greyhounds! Dog to it!"

"Awecu iri ango ma etiye, dhanu" - Stubb owacu ni jutic pare - "etiye ikum dini para ni bedo ku ngei cwiny; ento amito camu Yarman ma rac eca—Pull—icopo ngo? Wubiweko ngati ma rac eca ugoywu? Wumaru brandy? Adhura pa brandy, dong, ni dhanu maber uloyu, pirango ma wuwangu rimo moko? Nga ma ubedu ka bolo meli iwi nam—wabedo moth ungo—wabedo moth.

"Oh! nen suds ma etimo!" Flask owacu, m'ubedo ka myel malu man piny—“Ubedo ango—Oh, timo pile iwi ring dyang—ubedo calu yen! Oh! awiya para, tim spring—slap-jacks man quahogs pi cam mi diewor, ingeyo, awiya para—baked clams man muffins—oh, tim , tim , spring,—etiye barreller miya acel kawoni—kud ibe lose! —nen nia Yarman—Oh , wucopo ngo ni timo lembe ma rac !

I saa maeca Derick ubedu ka ketho lamp-feeder pare i yei ma ubebino, man bende oil-can pare; nyalo bedu ku paru ario mi dwoko piny yo pa ju wadi, man i sawa marom mi medo kero pare gire ku kero mi dwoko ngeye.

“Gwok ma rac pa Dutch!” Stubb owacu. "Wuwodh kawoni, coo, calu yei mi lwiny elufu pyer abic ma upong ku jok ma wic migi makwar. Wuwacu ango, Tashtego; in ibedo ngati ma copo turu ng'ut peri i dul pyer aryo pi woro pa Gayhead ma jadit? Wuwacu ango?"

"Awacu, ywal calu mungu-dam,"—Ja India owacu.

Ku tego cwiny, ento ku lworo pa ju German, yei adek pa Pequod ucaku ngwec karacelu; man, ma cwinye tek, edaru ceng i bange pi kare manok. I kura maber, ma oling, man ma jatel wi mony nen kudu ka ebecidho kago ku lei pare, ju adek eca ucungu malu ku anyonga, kare ku kare gicungo ikum jatel wi yei ku koko ma nyangu dhanu ma ewacu, "Eni epodho, kawoni! Hurray pi yamo macol! Piny ku Yarman! Woth iwi yei!"

Ento edham ecaku acaki tek Derick ubedu ku tego cwiny, kadi nang ubedu ku tego cwiny, ecopo nyutho nia etiye jalonyo i ngwec eni, tek ubedu ngo pokolembe ma atira iwie i rec ma mako pala pa jaring yei pare. Kinde ma lubber ma jamutoro eni ube tembo ni gonyo white-ash pare, man kinde ma, pi adwogi ne, yei pa Derick ubedu kapodho, man ebed epodho iwi dhanu pare ku ngei madit;—eca ubedu kare maber ni Starbuck, Stubb, man Flask. Ku koko, gicaku ngwec maber, man gicaku ngwec iwi thenge pa German. I ngeye nyanok, man yei angwen ne zo gibedo i dhu nam ma dit, ma ucungo kud i bang gi, ku thenge ario zoo, ubedo pii ma mol ma etimo.

Ubedo piny ma rac, ma nyayu can lee, man ma nyayu anyonga. Whale ne kawoni ubecidho woko, man ubecwalo dhuny pare i nyime i jet ma jubenyayu can i iye; ma cinge acel ma rac ube goyo ngete ku lworo. Kawoni ni cinge eni, kawoni ni cing eca, egam ewotho i ngwec pare ma rwinyo, man podi i pii moko ci ma eturo, epodho i nam, nyo epodho i polo ku cinge acel ma ubegoyo. Dong aneno winy ma tiye ku bwome ma jukure ma timo dul ma nyayu lworo i polo, ma ubetimo kero ni ringo kud i cing jok ma gikwo i nam. Ento winyo utiye ku dwal, man ku ywak ma tek ebi nyuthu lworo pare; ento lworo pa lei madit ma ling pa nam, jutwio ku nyor man juyike i iye; ebedo ungo ku dwal, mako yweyo ma ucungo iwi spiracle pare, man eni umiyu nen pa ngati ma rac mandha; podi, i pek pare maber, lak ma portcullis, man yibe ma tek, ubedu lembe madwong ma copo nyayu lworo i dhanu ma tek uloyu.

Nen kawoni nia kende kende kare manok kende re ma bi miyo yei pa Pequod bedo ku twero, man kakare ni bedo ma juweko tuko pare, Derick oyero ni ketho piny ma nen calu dart ma bor mandha, mapodi kare ma tok cen copo ringo.

Ento kawoni kende ma jatel wi harpooneer pare ucungu pi mako cinge, nyathi thwol adek eni zo—Queequeg, Tashtego, Daggoo—ucungu iwi tyend gi, man ucungu i rwom ma lac, man ucungu iwi tyend gi; man gi ringo iwi wic pa German harpooneer, Nantucket irons migi adek gimondo i whale. Yamu ma nyayu wang man mac ma tar! Yei adek, i ngei mir acel pa ngwec pa rec ma dit, ugoyo yei pa German ku tego madit, ma Derick ku harpooneer pare ma ungwel gipodho woko, man giwotho iwi yei adek ma ucungo iwi yei.

"Kud ilwor, dhanu para mi moo," Stubb owacu, ma eneno gi ku wange ma ukadhu; "Jubi tingowu kawoni--ber-aneno rec moko ma julwongo ni sharks yo piny--gwok pa St. Bernard, ingeyo-gikonyo juwotho ma cwinygi lith. Hurrah! eni yore mi wotho kawoni. Keel acel acel ubedo lero pa ceng! Hurrah!-Eni wabecidho calu kettle adek i ngeye pa madgar uketho i wiya! elephant in tilbury iwi ngom—ma timo wheel-spokes utingo, awobi, kani ibecidho i kume kumeno;

Ento ngwec pa leya ne ubedo ma nok. Emiyo yweyo ma nyawang, ewinjo dwand ma tek. Ku ngwec ma tek, laini adek ne uringo iwi loggerheads ku tego ma dit ma uketho gi goyo vur ma thuc i igi; kadi bende ju ma gi goyo harpooneers gibedo ku lworo madit nia dwand ma tek eni copo jwigo cing gi, ma gitiyo ku tego migi mi rieko, gi maku ngwec mapol ku thung ma gi mako; nitundu i ajikine—pi tego pa yei ma ucungo iwi yei, ma thwol adek ne cidho piny i blue—gunwales pa bows ubedo marom ku pii, ma sterns adek ubedo malu iwi polo. Man rec ma julwongo ni whale ugam uweko dwand, pi kare moko gibedo i kura meno, lworo mi tiyo ku cinge, kadi nang kabedo ne ubedu tek. Ento kadi yei juwodhe piny man rwinyo i yo maeni, ento etiye "gwoko," calu ma julwonge; eni mako ku cinge ma nyap pa ring kume makwo kud i ngeye; eni re ma nyayu can i Leviathan pio pio ni ay malu kendu pi rombu ku pala pa judhoge. Ento ka waweco ngo ikum peku pa piny ne, ukwayu wabed ku jiji ka nyo yore eni re ma ber uloyu; kum etiye lembe maber ni paru, nia ka rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ubedo i the pii pi kare malac, ebedo ku olo madwong. Pien, pi dit pa ng'om pare—i sperm whale ma dit ma romo 2000 square feet—peko pa pii utiye malac. Wan ceke wangeyo peko ma lee dit ma wabecungu i iye; kadi keni, iwi ngom, i polo; dong, pek pa rec ma dit, ma tingo iwi ngete lum ma romo futi miya aryo mi nam! Umitu obed marom ku pek pa atmosphere pyer abic. Jalwor acel uketho pek pa yei pyer aryo, ku jam lwiny migi zo, man piny migi, man dhanu ma gibedo i yei.

Calu yei adek ne gibedo keca iwi nam ma mol moth, ma gineno piny i diewor pare macol ma nja; man calu epe koko acel nyo ywak moko cii, epe, kadi pii ma mol nyo pii ma wok kud i iye; ango ma dhanu mi ngom copo paru, nya i the ling man ling eca, lei madit mi nam ubedu ka yeng man ka nyayu can! Ungo inches abora pa thwol ma ucungo iwi cinge nen ungo i kor cinge. Nen calu lemandha nia ku thwol adek ma thindho Leviathan madit ne ucungo calu pek madit ikum sawa mi nindu abora. Ryemo? man ni ango? Ni dul adek pa board. Eni re ma juweco pire ku tego cwiny—“Icopo pongu adila pare ku thwol, nyo wiye ku tong rec? Pala pa ngati ma ucungo i kume copo mako ngo, thung, pala, kadi cinge: eneno chuma calu lum, thwol copo ketho epe calu thwol; ku yeng pa pala!” Eni lei? eni en? Ai! nia lembe ma copo timere ngo ucikere ni lubo tok jukwenda. Pien ku tego pa tyende elifu acel i yibe, Leviathan uringo wiye piny i the got mi nam, mi kane kud i cing rec pa Pequod!

I lero pa ceng ma piny eca, tipo ma yei adek ne ucwalo piny, ucikere ni bedu bor man lac ma copo ketho tipo pa dul mony pa Xerxes. Ng'a ma copo wacu kite ma lworo pa rec ma julwongo ni whale ma remo ne nen kudu nen calu tipo madongo ma ucungo iwi wiye!

"Cung, coo; ewotho," Starbuck owacu, ma dul adek ne ucwale i pii, ma ucwalu malu ikumgi, calu ku waya magnetic, kwo man tho pa rec ma dit, kara jaring yei acel acel uwinj gi i kombe pare. I saa mukende, ma judwokogi piny kud i cing yei, yei ne ugam ucungo malo, calu ma icefield ma nok bitimo, kinde ma dul pa winyo ma rangi migi tar ugam lworo kud i iye man gimondo i nam.

"Kel i iye! Keth i iye!" Starbuck owacu kendu; “ebe ay malu.”

Laini, ma, nyanok kende, ma cing acel kende copo nwangu ngo, dong ubedu i coils mabor ma jubolo cen ma ubepodho i yei, man pio pio rec ma julwongo ni whale ugam uturo pii i kind bor pa yei ario pa ju ma gicayu lei.

Motions pare unyutho kamaleng nia ebedo ku olo ma lee. I kind lei mapol mi ngom, valve moko nyo flood-gates utiye i ler migi mapol, ma ka jutwoyo, rimo ne ucungo iwi yore moku. Epe kumeno ku rec madit; acel ikin lembe ma pire tek utiye ni bedu ku non-valvular structure pa ler mi rimo, kara ka juturo kadi ku cing moko ma nok calu harpoon, remo ma rac ucaku iwi ler mi rimo pare zo; man ka eni umedire ku tego pa pii mabor ku piny, kwo pare jucopo wacu nia ubewok kud i bange i kulo ma rwinyo ngo. Ento rimo madwong akeca i iye, man bor man pol pa pii ma iye, ma ebi medo rimo man rimo pi kare malac; kadi bende i kare mi kec, kulo bimol, ma acaki pare utiye i vur mi godi mabor man ma dhanu copo ngeyo ngo. Kadi kawoni de, kinde ma yei ucungo iwi rec ma dit eni, man ucungo iwi yei pare ma ube yeng, man lances ucungo i iye, julubo tokgi ku jet ma tek kud i ret ma nyen, ma umediri ka tuko, ma spout-hole ma nwang i wiye ubedo kende kende i kare moko, kadi nya pio pio, ucwalu pii pare ma lworo i polo. Kud i vur mir ajiki eni rimo moko ope ma uwok, kum kum moko ope ma pire tek i kume ma podi jutwo. Kwo pare, kite ma gilwonge kudu, ubedu ma rwinyo ngo.

Calu yei dong ucungu ikume, thenge ma malu pa kite pare, ku pol pare ma rwinyo i pii, unyuthire maleng. Wange, nyo kabedo ma wange ubedo i iye, juneno. Calu dhanu mapol ma dongo marac gicokiri i vur pa yen madongo ma julwongo ni oaks ka gicungo piny, kumeno bende kud i kabedo ma wang rec ma julwongo ni whale ubedo i iye con, kawoni dong uwok woko bulbs ma wang gi utho, ma rac ni neno. Ento kisa ubedu ungo. Pi oro pare zo, man cinge acel, man wange ma otho, umitu etho tho man junege, kara ebed ku ler i kum nyom man foc mange mi coo, man bende pi eketh ler i kanisa ma pire tek ma rweyo lembanyong'a ma rwinyo dhanu ceke ni dhanu ceke. Podi ube mol i rimo pare, i ajikine enyuthu dul moko ma rangi pare uloko nyo protuberance, dit pa bushel, piny i thenge.

"Kabedo maber," Flask uwacu; "wek kende abed ka goye keca wang acel."

“Avast!” Starbuck owacu, "pe ku yeny pa lembe eni!"

Ento Starbuck ma ber ubedo cen. I saa mi dart, jet ma rac ugam uwok kud i ret ma rac eni, man ugam ucwale i can ma lee dit, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale kawoni ubewodho rimo mapek, ku ngei ma tek ugam ucungo iwi yei, ma uketho gi man jutic migi ma gicungo iwi yei ne ucwalu rimo, ma uketho yei pa Flask utur man uketho bows udoko tek. Ubedo tho pare. Pi, i kare eni, ebedo ku can lee dit pi rwenyo rimo, ma egam epodho cen kud i yey ma etimo; ebedo ka yweyo iwi ngete, ebedo ka goyo ku cinge ma ucungo, man ebedo ka lokore moth moth calu ng'om ma rwinyo; udwoko wang lembe ma tar pa ic pare; ebedo calu yen, man etho. Ubedo lembe ma rac mandha, ma tok cen ma ube thum. Calu ma dhanu ma gineno ngo pii juwodho kud i pii moko ma dit, man ku gurglings mi can ma jukuboth, spray-column udok piny man udok piny i ngom—kum kumeno pii ma tok cen ma tho pa rec ma dit.

Ndhu ndhu, podi jurutic mi yei gibekuru tuc pa yei, avu ne unyutho lanyuth mi pii ku piny pare zo ma jugwoko ngo. Ndhu, nimakere ku cik pa Starbuck, juyike cinge i kabedu matung tung, kara pi kare malac yei moko ci ubed buoy; rec ma julwongo ni sunken whale ucungo iwi inches manok piny migi ku thung. Ku gwoko maber, kinde ma yei udhingo, juwodhe rec ma julwongo ni whale i thenge pare, man jugwoke matek keca ku cinge ma tek, kum ubedu lemandha niya tek juyike ngo, avu ne copo podho piny.

Ebedo lembe ma nya nya ikare ma jucaku kudu ku spade, bor pa harpoon ma unyai unwange i ringe, i thenge ma piny pa dul ma jukoro con. Ento calu ma dul pa harpoons nwangire pol kare i kom pa rec ma jucaku, ku ringo ma juyiko maber i ngete, man piny moko ci ma nyutho kabedo migi; kumeno, ucikere ni bedu ku the lembe mange ma jungeye ngo i lembe maeni ma copo ketho ulceration ma juweco pire. Ento piny ma pire tek uloyu ubedu lemandha pa lance-head kidi ma junwango i kume, bor ungo ku cuma ma juyike, ring kume ucungo ikume. Nga ma utingo kidi eca? Man awene? Ecopu bedu ni dhanu moko mi Nor' West Indian re ma ucwale con mapodi America unwangu ngo.

Lembe mange ma nyayu anyonga ma copo bedu ni juwodho kud i cabinet ma dit eni epe. Ento juku ikum lembe mukende ma juwok unwangu, pi meli ma jubedu ka tingo iwi nam, pi kite ma kume ubedu ka rwinyo kudu. Ento, Starbuck, ma ubedu ku twero mi timo lembe, ucungo ikume nitundu; ucungo iwie matek, andha, nia ka i ngeye yei ne copo tur, tek podi emediri ku mako cinge ku kum; i ngeye, kinde ma jumiyo cik ni juwodh kud i iye, edoko tek ma tek ikum wi yen ma juketho i iye fluke-chains man cables, ma uketho ebedo tek ni bologi woko. Ikind kare eni piny ceke i Pequod ubedu maber. Ni kadho yo mukende pa deck ubedo calu wotho iwi wi ot ma ucungo iwi got. Meli ne ukoc man uywak. Pol pa ivory inlayings pa bulwarks man cabins pare ucaku kud i kabedu migi, ku dislocation ma unnatural. I nono, jukelo cing man ungwal iwi cing cing ma rwinyo ngo, mi kwanyo gi kud i wi yen; man piny ma dit ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ubedo piny ma jucopo tundo ngo i dhu pii, ma sawa ceke nen calu pek madwong umedire i kum pii ma ubepodho, man yei ne nen ni ubecidho i pii.

“Kur, kur, icopo ngo?” Stubb owacu ni kum, "kud ibed i timo marac ma rwinyo! Ku polo, coo, umitu watim piny moku nyo wacidh i iye. Ber ungo ni yenyo piny; avast, awacu ku cingi peri, man iring ngati acel ikindwu pi buku mi rwo man pala mi pen, man i thum nyoru madongo."

"Pala? Eyo, eyo," Queequeg owacu, man etingo pala ma pek pa jabodho, ewok kud i porthole, man steel ni iron, ecaku turo fluke-chains madongo. Ento stroke manok, ma upong ku lyethu, jumiyo, kinde ma kero ma lee uketho ju mukende. Ku ngwec ma tek, piny moko ci ma jukubo ucidho adrift; meli ne ucungo, avu ne utho.

Kawoni, rwinyo pa Sperm Whale ma junego kawoni eni ubedo piny ma nyayu lworo; kadi jadwar rec moko ope ma podi udwoko wange maber. Pol kare Sperm Whale ma tho bedo iwi pii ku tego madit, ku ngete nyo iye ma malu iwi pii. Tek whales kende ma gipodho i pii ubedo lei ma dit, ma nyap, man ma cwiny gi tur, lard migi ubedo nok man cogo migi zo ubedo pek man rheumatic; I ngeye icopo yero ku the lembe moku nia rwinyo eni utimiri pi pek pa rec ma rwinyo, ma wok kud i bedo mbe pa piny ma mol i iye. Ento epe kumeno. Pi nyithindho pa rec ma julwongo ni whales, ma gitiye ku yot kum maber, man ma gikwo ku paru maber, ma junego gi ma podi kare migi rwinyo ngo i ceng ma mor man dwi mir abic mi kwo, ku moo migi mi yweyo ikum gi; kadi ju ma tek man ma tek eni de kare moko gipodho.

Ento, juweco nia Sperm Whale utiye ku peko ma nok uloyu ikum lei moko ci. Ka acel m'i kind gi ucidhi piny, Right Whales pyer aryo timo. Tung tung eni ikind lei eni epe ku jiji ikum wel pa cogo pa Right Whale; Venetian blinds pare kende ma sawa moko pek pare kadhu ton acel; kud i peko eni, Sperm Whale utiye agonya. Ento kare moko nuti ma, ingey sawa madwong nyo nindu madwong, rec ma julwongo ni sunken whale ucer kendo, ma tek uloyu ma sagu kwo pare. Ento the lembe eni nen kamaleng. Yamu wok i iye; epong ku rwom ma malu; doko kit balloon pa lei. Meli mi lwiny copo bedu tek ni gwoko en piny i kare meno. I Shore Whale, i soundings, ikind Bays of New Zealand, ka Right Whale umio lanyuth mi pii, gi ketho buoys i kume, ku thung madwong; kara ka kum udok piny, gingeyo ka ma jubiyenye i iye ka ebiidho malu kendo.

Udong kare manok ingey tho pa kum ne, juwinje koko kud i Pequod's mast-heads, ma unyutho nia Jungfrau ubedwoko yei pare piny; kadi nya pii acel kende ma nen ubedo pa Fin-Back, ma ubedo ikind lei ma julwongo ni whales ma dhanu copo mako ngo, pi tego pare madit mi dhuny. Ento, Fin-Back's spout urombo ku pa Sperm Whale, ma ju ma mako rec ma gingeyo ngo giparu niya etiye. Man kumeno Derick ku juru ot pare zo gibedo ka lubo tok lei ma rac eni. Nyaku ma nyaku ma nyaku upong ku yei, utingo yei angwen ma fodi gitie aradu, man kumeno gin ceke gi rwenyo bor ku yamo, podi gi lubo tok yei ku tego cwiny.

Ai! pol migi ubedo Fin-Backs, man pol migi ubedo Dericks, jahera para.





CHAPTER 82. Woro man dwong pa mako rec.

Nitie kuloka moko ma timo lembe ma rwinyore maber ubedo yore mandha.

Kaka amedo donjo i lembe mi mako rec, man amedo timo sayusac para nitundo i acaki pare, ecaku bedo kud anyonga lee dit pi woro pare man kite ma etiye kudu con; man asaga ka anwango demi-gods man heroes madwong, jubila mi kit zo, ma yore moko nyo mange gi ketho tung tung ikume, abedo ku paru niya an gira abedo, kadi nang nya atie piny, i dul pa juwadi.

Perseus ma jalwor, wod pa Jupiter, ubedu jatel wi rec ma kwong; man pi woro ma lau pa lwongu mwa jubi wacu, nia rec ma dit ma kwong ma junego ku umego mwa junego ngo ku paru moko marac. Eno ubedu nindo mi tic mwa, ma wabedu ku jam lwiny kende pi konyo ju ma remo migi lith, man ungo pi pongu kabedo mi cam pa coo. Dhanu ceke ngeyo lembe maber pa Perseus ku Andromeda; kite ma Andromeda ma leng, nyaku pa rwoth, jutwio kudu i kidi i dhu nam, man calu Leviathan ubedu ka tingo, Perseus, rwoth pa ju ma gwoko rec, ucidho anyim ku tego cwiny, utingo leya madit, man enyayu man egamu nyaku ne. Ebedo tic maber mi goyo cal, ma juwer maber mi kare eni timo ngo; pi kite ma Leviathan eni junego i thwol mir acel. Man kud iwek ngati moko ubed ku jiji ikum lembe pa Arkite eni; kum i Joppa macon, ma kawoni Jaffa, i dhu nam mi Syria, i ot pa Mungu acel mi Pagan, ucungu pi oro madwong cogo madit pa rec ma dit, ma lembe mi adhura man dhanu ceke ma bedo i iye giwacu niya cogo pa lei ma dit ma Perseus unego. Kinde ma Jurumi umaku Joppa, jutero cogo marom eno i Italy pi loyo lwiny. Gin ma nen ni piny ma pire tek man ma pire tek uloyu i lembe eni, utiye eni: ubedu kud i Joppa kama Yona ucaku wotho i yei.

Calu lembe pa Perseus ku Andromeda—andha, dhanu moko giwacu niya giwok kud i iye—ubedo lembe ma pire tek pa St. George ku Dragon; dragon ma ageno ni ubedo whale; kum i lembe madwong macon, rec ma julwongo ni whales ku dragons gibedo karacelu, man pol kare gicungo pi juwothgi. "Ibedo calu lamvor mi pii, man calu thwol mi nam," Ezekiel uwacu; maeni, the lembe ma pire tek utiye rec ma julwongo ni whale; andha, Biblia moko tiyo ku wec eno gire. Mi mede, ecopu kwanyu piny madwong kud i dwong pa tic pa St. George ento enwango lei moko ma mol iwi ngom, kakare ni lwiny ku lei madit mi nam. Ngati moko ci copo nego thwol, ento Perseus kende, St. George, Coffin, re ma gitiye ku cwiny mi wotho ku tego cwiny ikum rec madit.

Kud iwek cal ma kawoni mi kabedo eni unyai wa; pi kadi bende lei ma jalwor ma tek eca urombo kudu nen calu griffin, man kadi lwiny ne nenre iwi ngom man jakwenda iwi kana, ento ka waparu pi ngec ma lee mi kare eni, ma kite pa rec madit eca ungeyere ngo ni ju ma timo cal; man ka waparu niya calu i lembe pa Perseus, St. George's whale copo bedu nya ewok kud i nam i dhu nam; man ka waparu niya lei ma St. George ubedo iwie copo bedu kende kende seal madit, nyo sea-horse; ka waparu lembe eni zo, ecopo nen ungo nya eromo ngo ku lembe maleng man lembe macon mi kabedo ne, ni mako ngati ma julwongo ni dragon eni kende kende Leviathan madit en gire. I andha, ka juketho i wang lemandha ma tek man ma nyayu can, lembe eni zo bibedo calu rec, ringu, man winy pa ju Filistia, ma nyinge Dagon; ma juyike i wang sanduku pa Israel, wi kana pare man cinge ario zoo upodho kud i kume, man kende re ma udong dul pa rec pare. Pieno, dong, ngati acel ikindwa ma pire tek, kadi wa whaleman, utiye jatel wi ngom mi England; man ku twero maber, wan juwer mi Nantucket umaku wabed i dul ma pire tek pa St. George. Man kumeno, kud iwek judongo mi dul ma pire tek (ma ngati moko mbe, acopo wacu, ma ubedu ku winjiri ku rec ma dit calu jatel wigi), wek kud gi nen Nantucketer ku lewic, pi kadi i bongo mwa ma juyubu ku yier man bongo mwa ma juyubu ku tar, wabedo ku twero maber mi nwangu deko pa St. Georges ma sagu gin.

Ka wabe yio Hercules ikindwa nyo epe, ikum lembe eni abedo ku jiji pi kare malac: pi kadi nangu nimakere ku lembe mi thek pa Jugiriki, Crockett ku Kit Carson macon-ju ma timo tic maber ma nyayu mutoro, jutwoyo piny man jubolo gi malu ku rec madit; podi, kadi nang eketho ebedo whaleman, eno copo bedu ma juweco pire. Kamoro moko nen ungo nya ebed egoyo rec pare ku harpoon, kawang, andha, kud i iye. Ento, ecopu bedu ni ngati ma timo tic pare ku ngec pare kende; kadok kumeno de, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale umake, tek egam emake ngo. Aloko nia etiye acel ikin thek mwa.

Ento, ku judongo ma ber, lembe pa ju Greece pa Hercules ku rec madit nen ni uai kud i lembe pa jo Hebrew macon pa Jonah ku rec madit; man vice versâ; andha gi rom. Ka awacu nia atiye demi-god, pirango ngo jakwenda?

Man de judongo, ju maleng, mungu moko, man jubila kende ngo re ma gi ketho dul pa dul mwa. Jadit mwa madit podi jubi nyuthu nyinge; kum calu rwoth mi rundi ma con, wanwango pii ma dit mi bedo wadi ikind mungu madongo gire. Lembe maber mi yo piny kawoni jubiponjo kud i Shaster, ma umiyu iwa lworo pa Vishnoo, acel ikin dhanu adek ma gini Mungu pa Hindoos; emiyu iwa Vishnoo ma leng eni gire pi Rwoth mwa;—Vishnoo, ma, ku kwo pare mir acel ikind apar mi ng'om, eketho man edwoko rec maleng. Kinde ma Brahma, nyo Mungu pa Mungu, ewacu Shaster, ungolo cwinya mi cweyo ng'om kendu ingey rwinyo pare, enyolo Vishnoo, ni telo wi tic; ento Vedas, nyo buku mi lembe ma dhanu ungiyu ngo, ma kwan migi nen calu ubedu piny ma pire tek ni Vishnoo mapodi ecaku cwio piny, man ma umitu ubedu ku piny moko ma copo konyo ju ma timo ot, Vedas eni ubedu i the pii; pieno Vishnoo udoko ngati ma julwongo ni whale, man edaru winjo i iye nitundo i thum pa pii, ekonyo buku maleng. Nyo Vishnoo eni ubedu ngo jadwar rec, dong? kadok calu ngati ma ringo thur julwonge jaring thur?

Perseus, St. George, Hercules, Jonah, man Vishnoo! dul pa ju moko nuti piri! Kabedo makani ma mako pa whaleman ma copo timo kumeno?





CHAPTER 83. Jonah ma nen i lembe mukadho.

Juweco ikum lembe mukadho pa Jonah ku rec madit i thenge mukadho. Kawoni dhanu moko mi Nantucket giyio ngo lembe mukadho pa Jonah ku rec madit. Ento i ngeye jumoko ma gibedo ku jiji i kum Jugiriki ku Juroma, ma, ma gicungo tung tung kud juyic mi rundi migi, gibedo ku jiji ikum lembe pa Hercules ku rec madit, man Arion ku dolphin; man podi bedo migi ku jiji ikum kura eca uketho ngo kura eno doko lembe ma nok, pi lembe eni zo.

The lembe madit pa jadwong moko mi Sag-Harbor ma uketho epenju lembe pa Hebrew ubedu eni:—Ebedo ku acel ikin Biblia macon ma leng, ma juyubu ku lembe ma nyayu anyonga, ma epe ku lembe mi ngec; acel m'ikindgi ucungo pi Jonah's whale ku lak ario i wiye—lembe ma ukoc kende kende i kum kit Leviathan (Right Whale, man kit rec ma tung' tung'), ma ju ma mako rec giwacu kumae, “A penny roll would choke him”; swallow pare utiye matidi akeca. Ento, ikum lembe eni, lagam pa Bishop Jebb ma ucungo ikume utiye atera. Etie ngo piny ma pire tek, Bishop uwacu, nia wanenu Jonah calu ngati ma juyike i ic pa rec madit, ento calu ngati ma juyike pi kare manok i thenge moko mi dhoge. Man eni nen ni ber mandha i Bishop maber. Pi andha, dhok pa Right Whale copo bedu ku meza moko, man bedu maber pa jurutugo zo. Ecopu bedu, bende, Jonah copo bedu nya ebedo ku lak ma oling; ento, i paru mir aryo, Right Whale epe ku lak.

The lembe mange ma Sag-Harbor (ecidho ku nying eca) ukwayu pi yeny pare mi yioyic i lembe pa jabila, ubedu piny moko ma nen ungo ikum kume ma juketho i mabusi man pii pa ic pa rec. Ento lembe maeni de upodhu piny, kum ja Germany exegetist moko paru niya Jonah umaku ubedu ka kwo i kum rec ma tho ma ucungo iwi pii—kadi bende askari pa France i lwiny pa Russia giloko agura migi ma tho ni hema, man gimondo i iye. Mi mede, jumoko ma giweco ikum ngom eca giwacu nia kinde ma Jonah jubolo woko kud i yei mi Joppa, edaru ringo i yei mange ma ceng ceng, yei moko ku rec madit ma julwongo ni whale; man, acopo medu, copu bedu julwonge niya "The Whale," calu jumoko ma kawoni julwongo gi niya "Shark," "Gull," "Eagle." Bende epe ku ju ma gisomo lembe ma giwacu nia rec ma julwongo ni whale ma juweco pire i buku pa Yona ubedo kende kende piny ma gwoko kwo—sanduku ma yamo upong kudu—ma jabila ma ubedu i ariti ne uringo i iye, man edaru lare kud i tho pa pii. Sag-Harbor ma rac, dong, nen ni rac zo. Ento ebino ku the lembe mange ma umitu ebed ungo ku yioyic. Ebedo eni, kan apoi maber: Yona ugam umwonyo ku rec madit i nam Mediterranean, man ingey nindu adek juwodhe kama moku ikind wotho mi nindu adek kud i Nineveh, adhura moko ma ni i Tigris, ma sagu wotho mi nindu adek kud i dhu nam Mediterranean. Eni nenedi?

Ento kodi yore mange ubedu nuti ma rec ma julwongo ni whale copo nwangu ko jakwenda i kabedo ma nok ku Nineveh? Eyo. Ecopu bedu etingo kude i yo mi Cape of Good Hope. Ento ka waweco ngo ikum wotho ikind nam Mediterranean, man wotho mange ma malu i nam Persian Gulf man nam makwar, paru ma kumeno copo kwayu wotho mi ng'om zoo mi Afrika i nindu adek, ka waweco ngo pi pii mi Tigris, ceng'ini ku kabedo pa Nineveh, ma tiye piny akeca ma rec ma julwongo ni whale copo dhuny i dhu nam pa Jonah's Beath. pa Gen maber i ceng ma con eca copo kwalu rwom mi nwangu ngom madit eca kud ibang Bartholomew Diaz, jadhum ma julwongo, man eketho lembe mi kare eni doko ndra.

Ento lembe maeni mi mingo pa Sag-Harbor macon unyutho kende kende rwom pare mi mingo mi paru—lembe ma podi etiye rac i iye, pi enen nia etiye ku ponji manok kende kende ma enwango kud i ceng man nam. Awacu nya enyutho kende kende cwinye mi mingo, cwiny ma rac, man lwiny pare ma rac man ma rac ikum judongo pa dini. Pien pa rwoth pa dini mi Katholiki mi Portugal, paru pa Jonah mi cidho i Nineveh nikadhu kud i Cape of Good Hope uketho iwange calu lanyuth pa timo thangu madit. Man ebedo kumeno. Mi mede, nitundu kawoni, ju Turkey ma gisomo lee giyio lembe pa Yona. Man oro miya adek mukadho, jawoth moko mi England i buku pa Harris's Voyages, uweco ikum Mosque pa Turkey ma jugiero pi woro Jonah, ma i iye Mosque ubedo tara ma wango ma wang ma moo ope.





CHAPTER 84. Pitchpoling.

Mi weko gi ringo yot man pio pio, juwiro axles pa gari; man pi lembe marom, jumoko ma mako rec madongo timo tic marom ku yei migi; gi ketho moo piny. Bende epe ku jiji nia calu yore ma kumeno copo timo ngo rac, ecopu bedu ngo ku bero moko ma rac; ka iparu nia moo ku pii gi rac; nia moo ubedo piny ma mol, man nia piny ma nen utiye mi ketho yei mol maber. Queequeg uyio matek ikum ketho moo i yei pare, man uthieno moko ma ndhundhu ingeye ma meli pa Germany Jungfrau utho, egam etimo tic ma lee dit; ng’ielo piny i the ng’ete, kama ebedo ka ng’ielo i ng’ete, man ebedo ka ng’iel i unctuousness calu ng’atu m’utimo kero ni ketho yuk wic ma ng’ic kud i ng’ete ma ng’ic. Nen calu ebetimo tic ni lubu paru moko ma pire tek. Bende edong ungo ma rwinyo ku lembe ne.

I diewor, rec ma julwongo ni whales jupito; ento ndhundhu ma meli ucidho i bang gi, gilokore man gi ringo ku yamo ma tek; ngwec ma rwinyo, calu yei pa Cleopatra kud i Actium.

Ento, yei ne ulubo tokgi, man pa Stubb ubedo ma kwong. Ku kero madit, Tashtego udaru timo kero mi ketho cuma acel; ento rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale, manangu ewinjo ngo dwand, podi umediri ku ngwec pare, ku kero ma lee. Peko ma kumeno ma rwinyo ngo ikum cuma ma jupodho ucikere ni wok woko pio pio nyo i ngeye. Edoku piny ma pire tek ni goyo flying whale, nyo bedo moth ni rwenyo. Ento ni tingo yei i thenge pare ubedu tek, ewotho pio pio man ku ngei cwiny. Ango ma udong?

Ikind jam tic mabecu man ryeko, diro mi cing man diru madwong, ma jaracik ma con ubedo ka timo, acel de mbe ma kadhu timo maber ku pala ma julwongo ni pitchpoling. Pala ma nok, nyo pala ma lac, i tic pare zo epe ku piny moko ma rom kudu. Etie piny ma pire tek kende ku rec ma julwongo ni running whale; lembe pare madit man kite pare utiye bor maber ma lance mabor jubolo kudu maber kud i yei ma ube yeng matek, ma ube yeng yei, i the telowic ma tek. Cuma man yen uketho i iye, pala eca zoo bor pare romo futi apar nyo apar wi aryo; cinge utiye nyanok uloyu pa harpoon, man de piny ma yot—pine. Juyike ku thung moko ma nok ma julwongo ni warp, mabor mandha, ma jucopo tingo kudu i cing ingey ngwec.

Ento mapodi wacidho anyim, piretek ni wacu kany, kadi nang harpoon copo bedu ma jutiyo kudu i yo marom ku lance, ento epe; man ka jutimo, podi epe ku adwogi maber, pi pek madit man bor pa harpoon ka juporo ku lance, ma edoko peko madit. Calu piny ma rwinyo, dong, umitu icak wok pio pio ikum rec ma dit, mapodi pitchpoling moko bino i tic.

Nen kawoni Stubb; jadit moko ma kud i kura pare mi ngyeru, bedo moth man bedo moth i saa mi peku, ubedu ku diru mi timo maber i pitchpoling. Nen ire; ecungu maber i wang yei ma ucungo; ma jukubo ku foam ma leng, towing whale utiye futi pyer angwen anyim. Maku lance ma bor ne yot, neno wang aryo nyo adek i bor pare ni neno ka etiye atira, Stubb utingo coil pa warp ku cinge acel, kara ebed ku cinge ma ucungo, man eweko mukende ma epe. I ngeye etingo lance ma upong i wang cinge, edwoko i kum rec ma dit; ka, eumo kude, edwoko cinge piny, man etingo cinge malu nitundo ma jam lwiny ucungu maber iwi cinge, fut apar wiye abic malu. Eneno iri calu jabodho, ma eketho cinge mabor iwi tike. I saa mukende ku ngwec ma nyap, ma nyinge mbe, i arch ma malu mandha, cuma ma rieny ucungo iwi bor ma nyai, man ungwel i kabedu pa kwo pa rec ma julwongo ni whale. Kakare mi pii ma lyel, kawoni ewodho rimo makwar.

"Eni ucwalu spigot woko kud i iye!" Stubb owacu. "'Eni nindo mir angwen mi dwi mir abiro ma tho ngo; pii ceke umaku ubed ku kongo tin! Ka kawoni, ubedu kongo macon mi Orleans, nyo Ohio macon, nyo Monongahela macon ma juweco pire ngo! Kadong, Tashtego, awobi, abiketho iketh canakin i jet, man wabimadhu kongo i iye! Eyo, kongo ma kwo mandha, wabecamu kongo ma kwo i adunde. nyai pa vur pare keca, man kud i punch-bowl ma kwo eca madhu piny makwo.

Kare ku kare i wec ma tek ma kumeno, thwol ma tek judwoko wange, tong eca dwogo ibang rwoth pare calu greyhound ma jugwoko ku cinge maber. Rec ma julwongo ni whale ma cwinye lit ucidho i ngwec pare; tow-line ucungo, man pitchpoler upoto yo ngeye, ugoyo cinge, man eneno ka leya ne tho.





CHAPTER 85. Fountain.

Ni pi oro elufu abusiel—man ngati moko mbe ma ngeyo oro miliyon adi ma ukadhu—rec madit ma dit ucikere ni bedo ka wok iwi nam, man ka lwoko man lwoko podho mi nam ma thuc, calu ma timo ku pii madwong; man nia pi oro mapol mukadho, ju ma gicayu lei elufu mapol gibedu ceng ku pii pa rec ma dit, gineno pii ma mol man ma mol—nya eni zo ucikere ni bedu, man podi, nia nitundo i dakika maber eni (dakika apar wi abic ku nusu ingey sawa acel mi diewor mi ceng apar wiye abusiel mi dwi mir apar wi ario, AD 185), ka nyo pii eni podi utiye peko utiye, i ngei lembe zo, pii mandha, nyo piny moko cii ento yamo—eni ubedo piny ma pire tek.

Ka dong, wek wanenu lembe eni, karacelu ku piny moko ma ber. Ng'ato ka ng'ato ong'eyo ni kuom rieko makende mar gills maggi, dhoudi mag finy e wi duto yuyo yamo ma kinde duto riwre gi gik ma giyudo e iye; kumeno, rec ma julwongo ni herring nyoke cod copo kwo pi oro miya acel, man ecopo tingo wiye malu iwi pii. Ento pi kite ma etiye ku iye ma miyo etiye ku ombo ma rwinyo, calu pa dhanu, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale copo kwo kende kende ni yweyo yamo ma wok kud i polo. Pieno, mitu pa wotho pare ma nja i ng'om ma malu. Ento ecopu ngo yweyo kud i dhoge, pi, i kura pare ma rwinyo, dhoge pa Sperm Whale juyiko fut abora piny; man ango ma podi utiye, yamo pare epe ku winjiri ku dhoge. Ungo, eyuyu kud i spiracle pare kende; man eni utiye iwi wiye.

Ka awacu, nia i lei moko ci yuyu ubedo tic ma pire tek pi kwo, calu ma ewodho kud i yamu piny moko, ma ka jukelo i rimo emiyo rimo bedo kwo, aparu ngo niya abitimo dubo; kadi nya acopo tiyo ku wec moko mi ngec ma pire tek ungo. Keth i wii, man elubo nia ka rimo ceke i dhanu copo wok ku yweyo acel, ecopu cego ume man ecopo kelo ngo mange pi kare malac. Eni nyutho nya, ecopo kwo labongo yweyo. Kadok ecopu nen ni lembe ma nen ungo, eni utiye lembe pa rec ma julwongo ni whale, ma kwo, ku kare, pi sawa pare zo man makadhu (ka etiye piny) manangu eyuyu ngo, nyo eyuyu yamo moko cii; kum, poi, epe ku gills. Eni nenedi? Ikind cinge man thenge acel acel mi ng'ete, juyike ku labyrinth mi Cretan ma calu vermicelli, ma ler ne, ka ewok kud iwi ngom, upong ku rimo ma oxygen. Pi sawa acel nyo makadhu, fathoms elufu acel i nam, etingo kero ma lee i iye, calu ngamia ma ukadhu i lum ma pii epe i iye tingo pii ma lee pi tic kudu i anyim i iye angwen. Lembe pa kum pa labyrinth eni epe ku laro lembe; man nia paru ma juketho iwie utiye lemandha man lemandha, nen ni lembe ma pire tek ira, ka aneno tek cwiny pa leviathan eca ma copo gonyo ngo, calu ju ma mako rec giwacu. Eni re ma amito wacu. Ka junyayu ngo, i ngei wok iwi pii, Sperm Whale bimediri keca pi kare ma rom rom ku ju mukende ma junyayu ngo. Wek ebed pi dakika apar wiye acel, man ewotho wang pyer abiro, niwacu, eyuyu yamo pyer abiro; man ka ecer kendu, ebibedo ku genogen mi yweyo pare pyer abiro kendu, ni dakika acel. Kawoni, ka ingeye ma eyuyu yamo manok ibe lworo, kara ewinj, ebibedo ka ringo kendo pi timo yamo maber. Man ungo nitundu yuyu pyer abiro eca juweco, ecopo cidho piny ni bedo woko pi kare pare zo piny. Nen, kadi ango, nia ikind dhanu matung tung wel eni gi tung tung; ento i acel acel gi rom. Kawoni, pirango ma rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ucikere ni ketho cinge woko, kawang epe pi pongu yamo pare, mapodi epodho piny pi ber? Eni nen kamaleng, bende, nia yeny pa rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ucwale i peko mi tho pa ngwec. Pien ungo ku thwol nyo ku ret ma jucopo mako leviathan madit eni, kinde ma ebewotho i yei mabor ma romo futi elifu acel i the lero pa ceng. Ungo diru peri kende, O jadwar, calu piny ma pire tek ma kelo loc iri!

I dhanu, yweyo ubetimere kare ku kare—yuyo acel tiyo pi pulsations ario nyo adek kende; kara tic moko ci mange ma umitu etim, ka ecere nyo enindu, yweyo ecikere, nyo etho. Ento Sperm Whale yuyu kende kende acel mi abiro nyo ceng yenga mi kare pare.

Juweco ni rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale yweyo kende kende kud i vur pare; tek ecopu bedu andha ni dhu dhu pare ucungo ku pii, ci an aparu niya omyero wabed ku the lembe ma omiyo ngiyo pare mi ngwic nen ni rwinyo i iye; pi piny acel kende ikume ma dwoko wang ikum ume utiye vur ma rom; man pi bedu ma upong ku piny aryo, jucopo paru ngo nya ecopo bedu ku tego mi ngwic. Ento pi lembe ma nyang ikum pii-kadi bedu pii nyo yamo--pe ku genogen ma rwinyo ikum wic eni. Eyo, kadi kudu, nia Sperm Whale epe ku kabedo maber mi ngwec. Ento ango ma emito kudgi? Piny macol epe, violet epe, pii mi Cologne epe i nam.

Bende, calu windpipe pare kende kende oyabere i tube pa spouting canal pare, man calu canal ma bor ne—calu grand Erie Canal—juyike ku kit lock moko (ma yabere man cego) pi gwoko yamu piny nyo pii ma malu, kumeno rec ma julwongo ni whale epe ku dwal; kawang ibe nyayu lembe marac ikume ku wecu niya, ka ewacu ikume, eweco ku ume. Ento kendu, ango ma rec ma julwongo ni whale uwacu?
