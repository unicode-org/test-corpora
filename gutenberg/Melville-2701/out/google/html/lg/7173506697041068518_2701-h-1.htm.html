Mu kiseera kino, oluvannyuma lw’okunyaga engalo nnyingi mu bwangu mu muliro, n’okuggyayo engalo ze mu bwangu (nga alabika ng’aziyokya nnyo), ku nkomerero yasobola okufulumya bisikiiti; Oluvannyuma ng’afuuwa ebbugumu n’evvu katono, n’akola eky’okugiwaayo mu ngeri ey’empisa eri omuddugavu omuto. Naye sitaani omutono teyalabika ng’afaayo ku kika ky’ebisale ebikalu bwe bityo n’akatono; teyatambuzangako mimwa gye. Ebikolwa bino byonna eby’ekyewuunyo byali biwerekerwako amaloboozi g’omu lubuto agaali gakyatamanyiddwa okuva eri omugoberezi, eyalabika ng’asaba mu kuyimba-oluyimba oba si ekyo ng’ayimba zabbuli ezimu ez’ekikaafiiri oba endala, mu kiseera ekyo ffeesi ye n’ewuubaala mu ngeri esinga obutaba ya butonde. At last okuzikiza omuliro, yakwata ekifaananyi waggulu mu ngeri etaali ya mukolo nnyo, n’addamu okukisiba mu nsawo ye eya grego mu ngeri ey’obutafaayo ng’alinga omuzannyi assa mu nsawo y’enkoko enfu.

Emisango gino gyonna egy’ekyewuunyo gyayongera obutabeera bulungi, era okumulaba kati ng’ayoleka obubonero obw’amaanyi obw’okumaliriza emirimu gye egya bizinensi, n’okubuuka mu buliri nange, nnalowooza nti ekiseera kituuse, kati oba nedda, ng’ettaala tennazikizibwa, okumenya ekirooto kye nnali mmaze ebbanga nga nsibiddwa.

Naye interval gye namala mu kuteesa ku by’okwogera, yali ya kufa. Ng’asitula tomahawk ye ku mmeeza, n’agikebera omutwe okumala akaseera katono, n’oluvannyuma n’agikwata ku kitangaala, ng’akamwa ke kali ku mukono, n’afuuwa ebire ebinene eby’omukka gwa taaba. Akaseera akaddako ekitangaala kyazikizibwa, era kino eky’omu nsiko, Tomahawk wakati w’amannyo ge, yabuuka mu buliri nange. Nayimba out, saasobola butasobola kukikola kati; Era okuwa okuwuuma okw’amangu okw’okwewuunya n’atandika okunwulira.

Nga nnyiganyiga ekintu, nga simanyi kiki, namwekulukuunya okuva ku ye ne mmukuba ku bbugwe, olwo ne mmuloga, ani oba kyonna ky’ayinza okuba, okusirika, era ka nsiike n’okuddamu okukoleeza ettaala. Naye eby’okuddamu bye eby’omu lubuto byanmatiza omulundi gumu nti naye bubi n’ategeera amakulu gange.

“Ani-e debel you?”—Yasembayo okugamba nti—“You no speak-e, dam-me, I kill-e.” era bwe kityo ne bagamba nti Tomahawk eyakoleezebwa yatandika okukulaakulana ku nze mu nzikiza.

Naye eggulu lyebazibwe, mu kaseera ako landiroodi yajja mu kitangaala ky’ekisenge mu ngalo, n’abuuka okuva ku kitanda nadduka okutuuka gy’ali.

“Totya kati,” bwe yagamba ng’addamu okunyeenya, “Queequeg here wouldn’t dears a hair of your head.”

“Mukomye okunyeenya,” bwe nnaleekaana nti, “era lwaki tewangamba nti omusumba oyo ow’omu geyeena yali muntu alya abantu?”

“Nnalowooza nti mukimanyi;—saamugamba, yali peddlin’ heads okwetoloola ekibuga?—naye kyusa flukes nate ogende okwebaka.Quequeg, laba wano—o sabbee nze, nze sabbee—gwe omusajja ono akwebaka ggwe—gwe sabbee?”

“Me Sabbee Plenty” —okugolola Queequeg, okufuuwa ku payipu ye n’atuula waggulu ku kitanda.

“Ggwe gette mu,” bwe yagasseeko ng’ankola akabonero ne Tomahawk we, n’okusuula engoye ku ludda olumu. Kino ddala yakikola mu ngeri ya civil yokka naye nga ya kisa ddala era nga ya muzirakisa. Nayimirira nga mmutunuulira akaseera katono. Ku ttatu ze zonna okutwalira awamu yali a clean, comely looking cannibal. Kiki kino kyonna akajagalalo ke mbadde nkola, bwe nnalowooza mu mutima gwange — omusajja oyo muntu nga nange: alina ensonga nnyingi nnyo okuntiya, nga bwe nnina okumutya.
