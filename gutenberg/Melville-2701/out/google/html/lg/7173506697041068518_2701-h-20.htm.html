ESSUULA 101. Omuwandiisi w’ebitabo.

Ere emmeeri y’Olungereza egenda okuggwaawo okuva mu maaso, kabeere ng’eteekeddwa wansi wano, nti yava e London, era n’etuumibwa erinnya ly’omugenzi Samuel Enderby, omusuubuzi w’ekibuga ekyo, eyasooka ey’ennyumba emanyiddwa ennyo ey’okuvuba envubu eya Enderby & Sons; ennyumba nga mu ndowooza y’omuvuzi wange omwavu whale, ejja si wala mabega wa nnyumba z’obwakabaka ezigatta ez’Abatudor ne Bourbon, mu nsonga ey’ebyafaayo ebya nnamaddala. Ebbanga lye, nga omwaka gwa Mukama waffe 1775 tegunnatuuka, ennyumba eno ennene ey’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale yaliwo, ebiwandiiko byange ebingi eby’ebyennyanja tebitegeeza bulungi; naye mu mwaka ogwo (1775) yateekawo emmeeri z’Abangereza ezaasooka okuyigga bulijjo Sperm Whale; newankubadde okumala emyaka egiwerako emabega (okuva mu 1726) abazira baffe aba Coffins ne Maceys ab’e Nantucket ne Vineyard baali mu bibinja ebinene nga bagoberera Leviathan eyo, naye mu North ne South Atlantic yokka: si awalala. Kibeere nga kiwandiikiddwa mu ngeri ey’enjawulo wano, nti aba Nantucketers be baasooka mu bantu okukuba enkoba n’ekyuma eky’empuku The great Sperm Whale; era nti okumala ekitundu ky’ekyasa be bantu bokka mu nsi yonna abaamukuba ensambaggere bwe batyo.

Mu 1778, emmeeri ennungi, Amelia, yategekebwa ekigendererwa eky’amangu, era ku buvunaanyizibwa bwokka obw’aba Enderby ab’amaanyi, n’obuvumu yeetooloola Cape Horn, era ye yasooka mu mawanga okukka wansi eryato erya whale-eryato ery’engeri yonna mu Nnyanja ennene ey’obugwanjuba. Olugendo luno lwabadde lwa bukugu era lwa mukisa; era ng’adda mu kifo we yali asimba ng’akutte ejjudde ensigo ez’omuwendo, ekyokulabirako kya Amelia mu bbanga ttono kyagobererwa emmeeri endala, ez’Olungereza n’ez’Abamerika, era bwe kityo ebibangirizi ebinene ebya Sperm Whale eby’omu Pacific ne bisuulibwa. Naye nga tematidde na kikolwa kino ekirungi, ennyumba etakoowa yaddamu okwecamula: Samuel ne Batabani be bonna —bameka, maama waabwe yekka y’amanyi —era wansi w’obukulembeze bwabwe obw’amangu, era ekitundu, ndowooza, ku ssente zaabwe, gavumenti ya Bungereza yasendebwasendebwa okusindika emmeeri ya Rattler eyali eyitibwa sloop-of-war Rattler ku lugendo lw’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale olw’okuzuula mu Nnyanja ey’Ebugwanjuba. Nga eduumirwa Post-Captain y’amagye g’oku mazzi, Rattler yakola olugendo oluwuuma, era n’ekola obuweereza obumu; bwe bungi obutalabika. Naye kino si kye kyokka. Mu 1819, ennyumba y’emu yassaawo emmeeri yaabwe eya discovery whale, okugenda ku lugendo lw’okuwooma okugenda mu mazzi ag’ewala mu Japan. Emmeeri eyo —eyitibwa obulungi “Syren” —yakola olugendo olw’ekitiibwa olw’okugezesa; era bwe kityo ekifo ekinene eky’okuvuba envubu ekya Japan kyasooka okumanyibwa okutwalira awamu. Syren mu lugendo luno olw’ettutumu yali eduumirwa Captain Coffin, eyali Nantucketer.

Ekitiibwa kyonna eri aba Enderbies, n’olwekyo, ennyumba yaabwe, ndowooza, eriwo n’okutuusa kati; wadde nga awatali kubuusabuusa Samwiri ow’olubereberye ateekwa okuba nga yaseerera edda waya ye olw’Ennyanja ennene ey’Obugwanjuba ey’ensi endala.

Emmeeri eyatuumibwa erinnya lye yali esaanira ekitiibwa ekyo, ng’evuga mangu nnyo era nga ya kitiibwa buli kkubo. Namulinnya omulundi gumu mu ttumbi awalala ku lubalama lw’ennyanja Patagonia, ne nnywa flip ennungi wansi mu forecastle. Yali gam nnungi gye twalina, era bonna baali ba trumps —buli mwoyo ogwali ku lyato. Obulamu obumpi gye bali, n’okufa okw’essanyu. Era gam eyo ennungi gye nnalina — ebbanga ddene nnyo nga Akabu omukadde amaze okukwata ku bipande bye n’ekisinziiro kye eky’amasanga — kinnuma ku kusembeza abagenyi okw’ekitiibwa, okunywevu, okw’Abasaksoni okw’emmeeri eyo; era omusumba wange anneerabire, era sitaani anzijukire, singa nkibulako. Okukyuusa? Nze nnagambye nti tulina flip? Yee, era twagifuumuula ku sipiidi ya ggaloni kkumi buli ssaawa; era omuyaga bwe gwajja (kubanga guli squally off there by Patagonia), era emikono gyonna —abagenyi na bonna —nga bayitibwa ku reef topsails, twali top-heavy nnyo ne tulina okuwuuba buli omu waggulu mu bowlines; era mu butamanya ne tufuumuula sikaati z’obukooti bwaffe mu mazzi, ne tuwanika awo, nga tusiba ebiyiriro mu muyaga ogwali guwowoggana, ekyokulabirako eky’okulabula eri ttaala zonna ezitamidde. Kyokka, ebikondo tebyasukkiridde ku nnyanja; era mu kaseera katono twafubutuka wansi, nga tuli balongoofu nnyo, ne tulina okuddamu okuyita ku flip, wadde ng’ekifuuyira ky’omunnyo eky’obukambwe nga kibutuka wansi mu forecastle scuttle, rather too much diluted and pickled it to my taste.

Ennyama y’ente yali nnungi —nga nkalu, naye ng’omubiri gulimu. Bagamba nti yali nnyama ya nte; abalala, nti yali nnyama ya nte eya dromedary; naye simanyi, mu butuufu, engeri ekyo gye kyali. Nabo baali balina dumplings; obuwunga obutono, naye obunene, obw’enkulungo mu ngeri ya symmetrically, era nga tebusaanawo. Nalowooza nti osobola okuziwulira, n’okuziyiringisiza mu ggwe nga zimaze okumira. Bw’ofukamira wala nnyo mu maaso, oteeka mu kabi okukuba kwabwe okuva mu ggwe ng’emipira gya bbiiru. Omugaati —naye ekyo tekyasobola kuyambibwa; ng’oggyeeko ekyo, yali ddagala eriziyiza scorbutic; mu bufunze, omugaati gwali gulimu ebisale byokka ebipya bye baalina. Naye forecastle teyali kitangaala nnyo, era kyali kyangu nnyo okulinnya mu nsonda enzikivu ng’ogirya. Naye byonna awamu, okumutwala okuva ku loole okudda ku mulala, ng’alowooza ku bipimo by’ebiyumba by’omufumbi, nga mw’otwalidde n’ebyuma bye eby’amaliba ebiramu; mu maaso n’emabega, ngamba, emmeeri ya Samuel Enderby yali mmeeri ya ssanyu; eby’ebisale ebirungi n’ebingi; fine flip ate nga ya maanyi; crack fellows bonna, ne capital okuva ku boot heels okutuuka ku hat-band.

Naye lwaki kyali, mulowooze, nti Samuel Enderby, n’abavubi abalala Abazungu be mmanyi — si bonna wadde — baali mmeeri ez’ettutumu bwe zityo, ezisembeza abagenyi; ekyayita okwetooloola ennyama y’ente, n’omugaati, n’ekibbo, n’okusaaga; era tebaakoowa mangu okulya, n'okunywa, n'okuseka? Nja kukubuulira. Essanyu eringi ery’abavubi bano Abangereza, nsonga ya kunoonyereza ku byafaayo. Era sifuddeeyo n’akatono okunoonyereza ku byafaayo ku nnyanja ekika kya whale, so nga kirabika kyetaagisa.

Abangereza baakulemberwa mu nvuba y’ennyanja ekika kya whale aba Holland, Aba Zealand, n’Abadan; mwe baaggya ebigambo bingi ebikyaliyo mu by’obuvubi; n’ekisingawo, emisono gyabwe egy’amasavu egy’edda, nga gikwata ku bingi eby’okulya n’okunywa. Kubanga, ng’ekintu eky’awamu, emmeeri y’abasuubuzi ey’Olungereza ekuba abakozi baayo; naye si bwe kiri omuvubi w’ennyanja Omuzungu. N’olwekyo, mu Lungereza, ekintu kino eky’okuvuba envubu si kya bulijjo era kya butonde, wabula kya butanwa era kya njawulo; era, n’olwekyo, erina okuba n’ensibuko ey’enjawulo, wano eragiddwa, era ejja kwongera okunnyonnyolwa.

Mu kunoonyereza kwange mu byafaayo by’Abaleviathanic, nnasanga omuzingo ogw’edda ogw’Oludaaki, nga, olw’akawoowo kaago akaali kavuba, nnamanya nti guteekwa okuba nga gukwata ku bavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale. Omutwe gwali, “Dan Coopman,” n’olwekyo nnamaliriza nti kino kiteekwa okuba nga kye bijjukizo eby’omuwendo ennyo eby’emmeeri ezimu eza Amsterdam cooper mu by’obuvubi, nga buli mmeeri ya whale erina okutwala cooper yaayo. Nnanywezebwa mu ndowooza eno bwe nnalaba nti yali okufulumya “Fitz Swackhammer” emu. Naye mukwano gwange Dr. Snodhead, omusajja omuyivu ennyo, pulofeesa w’Oludaaki olwa Low Dutch ne High German mu ttendekero lya Santa Claus ne St. Pott’s, gwe nnakwasa omulimu ogwo okuvvuunula, nga mmuwa bbokisi y’emimuli gy’ensigo olw’obuzibu bwe —D Mutunzi." Mu bufunze, ekitabo kino eky’edda era eky’okuyiga ekya Low Dutch kyakwata ku by’obusuubuzi bya Holland; era, mu nsonga endala, yalimu emboozi ennyuvu ennyo ku nvuba yaayo ey’ennyanja ekika kya whale. Era mu ssuula eno, omutwe, “Smeer,” oba “Fat,” mwe nnasanga olukalala oluwanvu olukwata ku bintu eby’enjawulo eby’engoye ez’okutereka n’ebiyumba by’abavubi 180 abavuba ennyanja ekika kya Sail of Dutch whalemen; okuva ku lukalala olwo, nga bwe lwavvuunulwa Dr. Snodhead, mpandiika bino wammanga:

Firkins 2,800 eza butto. 20,000 lbs. Cheese ya Texel & Leyden. 144,000 lbs. kkeeki (oyinza okuba nga kiwandiiko kya wansi). Ankers 550 eza Geneva. Ebipipa bya bbiya 10,800.

Emmeeza z’ebibalo ezisinga zikalu mu kusoma; naye si bwe kiri mu mbeera eriwo kati, omusomi gy’abooga payipu enzijuvu, ebipipa, lita, ne gills za gin ennungi n’essanyu eddungi.

Mu kiseera ekyo, nnamala ennaku ssatu nga nfuba okugaaya bbiya ono yenna, ennyama y’ente, n’omugaati mu ngeri ey’okunoonyereza, mu kiseera ekyo ebirowoozo bingi eby’amaanyi ne binteesebwako mu butanwa, nga bisobola okukozesebwa mu ngeri esukkulumye ku ya bulijjo era eya Plato; era, n’ekirala, nakuŋŋaanya emmeeza ez’okugatta ezange, nga nkwata ku bungi obuyinza okuba obw’ebyennyanja ebiyitibwa stock-fish, n’ebirala, ebyaliibwa buli muvubi wa low Dutch harpooneer mu nvuba eyo ey’edda eya Greenland ne Spitzbergen whale. Mu kusooka, obungi bwa butto, ne kkeeki ya Texel ne Leyden ezikozesebwa, kirabika kyewuunyisa. Kyokka, nkiteeka ku butonde bwabwe obw’obutonde obutali bwa bbugumu, okufuulibwa n’obutabeera bwa bbugumu olw’obutonde bw’omulimu gwabwe, n’okusingira ddala olw’okugoberera omuzannyo gwabwe mu Nnyanja ezo eza Polar ezinnyogovu, ku lubalama lw’ennyanja zennyini ez’ensi eyo ey’e Esquimaux enzaalwa z’abantu ab’omukwano gye beeyama buli omu mu bumpers z’amafuta g’eggaali y’omukka.

Omuwendo gwa bbiya nagwo munene nnyo, ebipipa 10,800. Kati, ng’obuvubi obwo obw’oku njuba bwe bwali busobola okuvunaanibwa mu kyeya ekitono eky’embeera eyo, bwe kityo olugendo lwonna olw’omu ku bavubi bano aba Budaaki, nga mw’otwalidde n’olugendo olumpi olw’okugenda n’okudda ku nnyanja Spitzbergen, terwasukka nnyo myezi esatu, tugambe, era nga tubalirira abasajja 30 ku buli kibinja kyabwe eky’amaato 180, tulina abavubi b’ennyanja 5,400 aba Low Dutch bonna awamu; n’olwekyo, ngamba, tulina ebipipa bibiri ddala ebya bbiya buli muntu, olw’ensako ya wiiki kkumi na bbiri, nga tobaliddeemu kitundu kye ekituufu ku ankers eyo 550 eza gin. Kati, oba bano abavuzi ba gin ne bbiya, abafubutuka ennyo ng’omuntu bw’ayinza okubalowooza nti baali, baali basajja ba kika kituufu okuyimirira mu mutwe gw’eryato, ne batwala ekigendererwa ekirungi ku nnyanja ekika kya whale ezibuuka; kino kyandirabise ng’ekitali kya bulijjo. Naye ne babagenderera, era nabo ne babakuba. Naye kino kyali wala nnyo North, kijjukirwe, bbiya gy’akkiriziganya bulungi ne ssemateeka; ku Equator, mu nvuba yaffe ey’obugwanjuba, bbiya yandibadde asaanira okufuula omuvubi wa harpoone otulo ku mutwe gw’omuti n’okunywa boozy mu lyato lye; era okufiirwa okw’amaanyi kuyinza okuddirira Nantucket ne New Bedford.

Naye tewakyaliwo; ekimala kyogerwa okulaga nti abavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya Dutch abakadde abaaliwo ebyasa bibiri oba bisatu emabega baali ba kibumba kiwanvu; era nti abavubi b’ennyanja Abangereza tebasuulirira kyakulabirako kirungi nnyo bwe kityo. ESSUULA 102. Omu Bower mu Arsacides.

N’okutuusa kati, mu kujjanjaba mu ngeri ey’ennyonnyola ku Sperm Whale, okusinga ntunuulidde ebyewuunyo by’oludda lwe olw’ebweru; oba okwawukana era mu bujjuvu ku bimu ku bifaananyi ebitono eby’enzimba y’omunda. Naye okutuuka ku kumutegeera okunene era okujjuvu, kinsaana kati okwongera okumusumulula, n’okusumulula ensonga za hoosi ye, okusumulula ebisiba bye, n’okusumulula enkoba n’amaaso g’ennyondo z’amagumba ge ag’omunda ennyo, okumuteeka mu maaso go mu ultimatum ye; kwe kugamba, mu magumba ge agataliiko bukwakkulizo.

Naye kati otya Isimayiri? Kiba kitya, ggwe, omuvubi omukka mu by’obuvubi, weefuula nti olina ky’omanyi ku bitundu by’ennyanja ekika kya whale ebiri wansi w’ettaka? Stubb omuyivu, eyasimbye ku capstan yo, yawa emisomo ku anatomy ya Cetacea; era nga oyambibwako ekyuma ekikuba empewo, kwata waggulu olubavu olw’ekyokulabirako olw’okwolesebwa? Weennyonnyole, Isimayiri. Osobola okussa ennyanja ekika kya whale ekuze mu bujjuvu ku ddeeke yo okwekebejjebwa, ng’omufumbi bw’afumba embizzi eyokeddwa? Mazima ddala si bwe kiri. Okutuusa kati obadde mujulirwa wa mazima, Isimayiri; naye beera n’okufaayo engeri gy’okwatamu enkizo ya Yona yekka; enkizo ey’okwogera ku bikondo n’ebikondo; ebikondo, ebikondo by’omugongo, ebisula, n’ebipima wansi, nga bikola fuleemu ya leviathan; era ng’alinga ebibya eby’omuti, ebisenge by’amata, butto, ne kkeeki mu byenda bye.

Njatula, nti okuva ku Yona, abavubi ba whale batono abayingidde wala nnyo wansi w’olususu lw’ennyanja ekika kya whale enkuze; wadde kiri kityo, nfunye omukisa okumusalasala mu butonotono. Mu mmeeri gye nnali, lumu akaana akatono akayitibwa Sperm Whale kaasitulwa mu mubiri okutuuka ku ddeeke okusobola okumukuba oba ensawo ye, okukola ebikoola by’emiggo gy’engoye, n’emitwe gy’amafumu. Olowooza omukisa ogwo naguleka, nga sikozesezza boat-hatchet yange ne jack-knife yange, ne nmenya seal ne nsoma byonna ebiri mu mwana oyo omuto?

Era ku ky’okumanya kwange okutuufu ku magumba ga leviathan mu nkulaakulana yaago ennene ennyo, ekuze mu bujjuvu, olw’okumanya okwo okutatera kubaawo mbanja mukwano gwange ow’obwakabaka omugenzi Tranquo, kabaka wa Tranque, omu ku ba Arsacides. Olw’okuba nnali e Tranque, emyaka egiyise, bwe nnali nkwatagana n’emmeeri y’ebyobusuubuzi Dey ey’e Algiers, nnayitibwa okumala ekitundu ky’ennaku enkulu ez’e Arsacidean ne mukama wa Tranque, mu nnyumba ye ey’enkindu eyawummula e Pupella; ekiwonvu ekiri ku mabbali g’ennyanja ekitali wala nnyo okuva ku ekyo abalunnyanja baffe kye baayita Bamboo-Town, ekibuga kye ekikulu.

Mu ngeri endala nnyingi ennungi, mukwano gwange ow’obwakabaka Tranquo, olw’okuba aweereddwa ekirabo ky’okwagala okw’amaanyi eri ensonga zonna ez’obukambwe, yali agatta mu Pupella ebintu byonna ebitali bimu abantu be abaali abagezi ennyo bye baali basobola okuyiiya; okusinga embaawo eziyooleddwa ez’ebyuma ebyewuunyisa, ebisusunku ebitemeddwa, amafumu agateekeddwamu, ebiwujjo eby’ebbeeyi, amaato ag’akawoowo; era bino byonna ne bigabibwa mu byewuunyo byonna eby’obutonde, amayengo agaali gatikkiddwa mu byewuunyo, agasasula omusolo ge gaali gasudde ku lubalama lwe.

Ekikulu mu bino eby’oluvannyuma yali Sperm Whale ennene, oluvannyuma lw’omuyaga ogw’amaanyi ogw’ekiseera ekitali kya bulijjo, yali esangiddwa ng’efudde era ng’esibye, ng’omutwe gwe gutunudde ku muti gwa cocoa-nut, ng’ebiwujjo byagwo ebiringa ebyoya, ebirimu ebikuta byali birabika ng’ennyonyi ye eya kiragala. Omulambo omunene bwe gwamala okuggyibwako ebizibiti byagwo ebiwanvu okutuuka ku ssa, era amagumba ne gakala enfuufu mu musana, olwo amagumba ne gatambuzibwa n’obwegendereza okulinnya mu kiwonvu kya Pupella, kati yeekaalu ennene ey’enkindu ez’obwakabaka gye yali egakuumibwa.

Embavu zaali ziwanikiddwako ebikopo; vertebræ zaali ziyooleddwa n’ebitabo by’ebyafaayo eby’ekika kya Arsacidean, mu biwandiiko eby’enjawulo eby’ennukuta; mu kiwanga, bakabona baakuuma ennimi z’omuliro ez’akawoowo ezitazikiddwa, omutwe ogw’ekyama ne guddamu okusindika omudumu gwagwo ogw’omukka; ate nga, nga kiwanikiddwa ku ttabi, akawanga aka wansi ak’entiisa kakankana ku bagoberezi bonna, ng’ekitala ekyali kiwaniridde enviiri ekyatiisa ennyo Damocles.

Kyali kifaananyi kya kitalo. Enku zaali za kiragala ng’ebiwuka ebiyitibwa mosses of Icy Glen; emiti gyayimirira waggulu era nga gya maanyi, nga giwulira omubisi gwagyo omulamu; ettaka eryali likola ennyo wansi lyali ng’engoye z’omuluka, nga kuliko kapeti ennungi ennyo, nga ku yo emisinde gy’emizabbibu egy’oku ttaka gyakola ekiwujjo n’ebyoya, ate ebimuli ebiramu ne bikola ebifaananyi. Emiti gyonna, n'amatabi gaago gonna agatikkiddwa; ebisaka byonna, n'ebikoola, n'omuddo; empewo etambuza obubaka; bino byonna awatali kuddirira byali bikola. Okuyita mu miguwa gy’ebikoola, enjuba ennene yalabika ng’ennyonyi ebuuka ng’eluka ebimera ebibisi ebitakooye. Yogera, omuluka!—sigala omukono gwo!—naye ekigambo kimu naawe! Nedda — ekyuma ekikuba engoye kibuuka —ebifaananyi bitengejja okuva mu maaso g’ekyuma ekiluka engoye; kapeti efubutuka emirembe emirembe gyonna esereba. Kati, wakati mu lugoye olwa kiragala, olutawummuza bulamu olw’omuti ogwo ogw’e Arsacidean, amagumba amanene, amazungu, agasinzibwa gaali gagalamidde nga gawummudde —omusajja omunene ennyo ataliiko kye gukola! Naye, ng’ebiwujjo n’ebyoya ebibisi ebilukibwa bulijjo bitabula era nga biwuuma okumwetooloola, omuzira ow’amaanyi yalabika ng’omuluka omukuusa; ye kennyini yenna alukibwa n'emizabbibu; buli mwezi nga tufudde nti kiragala, ebibisi ebibisi; naye ye kennyini magumba. Kati, bwe nnakyalira ne Tranquo ow’obwakabaka okukyalira ennyanja eno ey’ekitalo, ne ndaba ekiwanga ekyoto, n’omukka ogw’obutonde nga gulinnya okuva ennyonyi entuufu we yali efulumye, neewuunya nti kabaka yandibadde atwala ekifo eky’okusinzizaamu ng’ekintu eky’obutonde. Yaseka. Naye ekisingawo nakyewuunya nti bakabona balayira nti omukka gwe ogw’omukka gwali gwa nnamaddala. Nga ngenda n’okudda mu maaso g’amagumba gano —nasika emizabbibu ku bbali —ne gamenya embiriizi —era n’omupiira gw’omuguwa gwa Arsacidean, ne ntambulatambula, nga nnyiganyiga okumala ebbanga wakati mu mpagi zaago ennyingi eziriko ebisiikirize n’emiti. Naye mu bbanga ttono layini yange yali evuddewo; era nga ngoberera okuddayo, ne nva mu kifo we nnayingira. Munda saalaba kintu kyonna kiramu; tewali kyaliwo okuggyako amagumba.

Nga nsala omuggo ogwa kiragala ogw’okupima, nnaddamu okubbira munda mu magumba. Okuva ku musaale gwabwe ogw’obusaale mu kiwanga, bakabona baategeera nga nkwata obugulumivu bw’olubavu olusembayo, “Kati kitya!” ne baleekaana nti; “Dar’st ggwe opimira katonda waffe ono!Oyo yaffe.” “Aye, bakabona — kale, mumufuula bbanga ki?” Naye awo okuvuganya okw’amaanyi ne kusituka wakati waabwe, ku bigere ne yinsi; baayatika buli omu ebikondo bya munne n’emiggo gyabwe egy’omu luggya — ekiwanga ekinene kyawuuma —era nga nkwata omukisa ogwo ogw’omukisa, nnamaliriza mangu ebipimo byange.

Ebipimo bino kati nteesa okuteeka mu maaso gammwe. Naye okusooka, kiwandiike, nti, mu nsonga eno, siri wa ddembe kwogera kipimo kyonna eky’okwegomba kye njagala. Kubanga waliwo abakulu b’amagumba b’osobola okujuliza, okugezesa obutuufu bwange. Waliwo Leviathanic Museum, bang’amba, mu Hull, Bungereza, ogumu ku myalo gy’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale mu nsi eyo, gye balina ebimu ku bifaananyi ebirungi eby’ennyonyi eziyitibwa fin-backs n’ennyanja endala. Mu ngeri y’emu, mpulidde nti mu myuziyamu y’e Manchester, mu New Hampshire, balina ekyo bannannyini byo kye bayita “ekifaananyi kyokka ekituukiridde eky’ensolo ekika kya Greenland oba River Whale mu Amerika.” Ekirala, mu kifo ekimu mu Yorkshire, Bungereza, Burton Constable erinnya lye, Sir Clifford Constable omu alina mu ngalo ze amagumba g’ennyonyi ekika kya Sperm Whale, naye nga ga sayizi ya kigero, mu ngeri yonna nga tegasinga bunene bwa mukwano gwange King Tranquo.

Mu mbeera zombi, ennyanja ekika kya whale ezaali zisibye amagumba gano abiri mwe gaali, mu kusooka bannannyini zo be baali bazigamba ku nsonga ezifaanagana. Kabaka Tranquo ng’awamba eyiye olw’okuba yali ayagala; ne Sir Clifford, kubanga yali mukama wa seignories z’ebitundu ebyo. Envubu ya Sir Clifford ebadde etegeerekeka mu kitundu kyonna; olwo, ng’ekifuba ekinene eky’ebintu, osobole okumuggulawo n’okumuggala, mu bifo bye byonna eby’amagumba —n’oyanjuluza embiriizi ze ng’omuwagizi omunene ennyo —n’owuubaala olunaku lwonna ku bbwa lye erya wansi. Ebizibiti birina okuteekebwa ku bimu ku nzigi ze ez’omutego n’ebizibiti bye; era omuzannyi w’ebigere ajja kulaga round future visitors nga alina ekibinja ky’ebisumuluzo ku mabbali ge. Sir Clifford alowooza ku kusasula ssente bbiri olw’okutunula mu galagi ewunyiriza mu mugongo; threepence okuwulira echo mu kinnya ky’obwongo bwe; ne sixpence olw’okulaba okutaliiko kuvuganya okuva mu kyenyi kye.

Ebipimo by’amagumba kati bye nja okugenda mu maaso n’okuteeka wansi bikoppololwa kigambo okuva ku mukono gwange ogwa ddyo, gye nnabiteekako ttatu; nga bwe kyali mu kutaayaaya kwange okw’omu nsiko mu kiseera ekyo, tewaaliwo ngeri ndala ya bukuumi ey’okukuuma ebibalo eby’omuwendo bwe bityo. Naye nga bwe nnali njjudde abantu olw’ekifo, era nga njagala ebitundu by’omubiri gwange ebirala bisigadde nga lupapula njereere olw’ekitontome kye nnali nyiiya mu kiseera ekyo — waakiri, ebitundu ki ebitaliiko ttatu ebiyinza okusigala — saatawaanya yinsi ezitali za bulijjo; era, ddala, yinsi tezirina kuyingira n’akatono mu kupima okulungi okw’ennyanja ekika kya whale.





ESSUULA 103. Okupima Amagumba ga The Whale.

Mu kifo ekisooka, njagala okuteeka mu maaso gammwe ekigambo ekitongole, ekitegeerekeka obulungi, ekikwata ku kitundu ekinene ekiramu ekya leviathan eno, amagumba gaayo ge tugenda okwolesa mu bufunze. Ekigambo ng’ekyo kiyinza okuba eky’omugaso wano.

Okusinziira ku kubalirira okw’obwegendereza kwe nkoze, era nga ekitundu kye nsinziira ku kuteebereza kwa Kapiteeni Scoresby, okwa ttani nsanvu ku nnyanja Greenland whale esinga obunene ey’obuwanvu bwa ffuuti nkaaga; okusinziira ku kubalirira kwange okw’obwegendereza, ngamba nti, Ennyama y’Ensigo ey’obunene obusinga obunene, wakati wa ffuuti kinaana mu ttaano n’ekyenda mu buwanvu, n’ekintu ekitakka wansi wa ffuuti amakumi ana mu kwetooloola kwayo okujjuvu, ennyanja ekika kya whale ng’eyo ejja kuzitowa waakiri ttani mwenda; bwe kityo, bw’abala abasajja kkumi na basatu ku ttani, yandibadde asinga nnyo omuwendo gw’abantu bonna awamu ogw’ekyalo kyonna eky’abantu lukumi mu kikumi.

Olwo tolowooza nti obwongo, ng’ente eziriko ekikoligo, bulina okuteekebwa ku leviathan ono, okumuleetera okuwuguka n’akatono ku ndowooza y’omuntu yenna ow’ettaka?

Nga mmaze mu ngeri ez’enjawulo okuteeka mu maaso go ekiwanga kye, ekituli ky’omusulo, akawanga, amannyo, omukira, ekyenyi, ebiwaawaatiro, n’abavubi ebitundu ebirala, kati nja kumala kulaga ekisinga okunyumira mu kitundu ekinene eky’amagumba ge agatali gaziyiziddwa. Naye ng’ekiwanga ekinene ennyo bwe kikwata ekitundu ekinene ennyo eky’obunene bwonna obw’amagumba; nga bwe kiri ekitundu ekisinga okukaluba; era nga bwe watali kintu kyonna kiddibwamu ku kyo mu ssuula eno, tolina kulemererwa kukitwala mu birowoozo byo, oba wansi w’omukono gwo, nga bwe tugenda mu maaso, bwe kitaba ekyo tojja kufuna ndowooza enzijuvu ku nsengeka ey’awamu gye tunaatera okulaba.

Mu buwanvu, amagumba ga Sperm Whale e Tranque gaali ga ffuuti nsanvu mu bbiri; bwe kityo bwe yateekebwamu ssente mu bujjuvu era n’agaziwa mu bulamu, ateekwa okuba nga yalina obuwanvu bwa ffuuti kyenda; kubanga mu nnyanja ekika kya whale, amagumba gafiirwa nga kimu kya kutaano mu buwanvu bw’ogeraageranya n’omubiri omulamu. Ku ffuuti zino nsanvu mu bbiri, ekiwanga kye n’akawanga ke byali bikola ffuuti nga amakumi abiri, ne bisigalawo ffuuti nga ataano ez’eggumba ly’omugongo erya bulijjo. Ku ggumba lino ery’omugongo, okumala ekintu ekitakka wansi wa kimu kya kusatu eky’obuwanvu bwagwo, kwaliko ekibbo eky’amaanyi ekyekulungirivu eky’embavu edda ekyali kizingiramu ebitundu bye ebikulu.

Eri nze ekifuba kino ekinene ennyo eky’embavu z’amasanga, nga kiriko omugongo omuwanvu, ogutawummudde, nga guwanvuye wala okuva ku kyo mu layini engolokofu, nga tekifaanana katono ku kisenge ky’emmeeri ennene eyateekebwa ku sitokisi, ng’embavu zaayo ez’obutaasa nga amakumi abiri zokka ze ziyingizibwa, era nga n’omugongo guba mu ngeri endala, okumala ekiseera, naye embaawo empanvu, ezikutuse.

Embavu zaali kkumi ku ludda olumu. Esooka, okutandika okuva mu bulago, yali kumpi ffuuti mukaaga obuwanvu; ekyokubiri, eky’okusatu, n’eky’okuna buli kimu kyali kiwanvuwa mu lunyiriri, okutuusa lwe watuuka ku ntikko y’ekyokutaano, oba emu ku mbavu ez’omu makkati, ezaali zipima ffuuti munaana ne yinsi ezimu. Okuva ku kitundu ekyo, embiriizi ezaali zisigaddewo zakendeera, okutuusa ez’ekkumi n’ezisembayo lwe zaawanvuwa ffuuti ttaano zokka ne yinsi ezimu. Mu bugumu okutwalira awamu, zonna zaali zirina ekintu ekirabika ng’ekikwatagana n’obuwanvu bwazo. Embiriizi eza wakati ze zaali zisinga okubeera nga zirimu ebikonde. Mu bimu ku Arsacides zikozesebwa okukola ebikondo kwe bateeka ebibanda by’amakubo g’ebigere ku migga emitonotono.

Mu kulowooza ku mbavu zino, saasobola buta kukubwa buto n’embeera, eddiddwamu mu ngeri ez’enjawulo ennyo mu kitabo kino, ne kiba nti amagumba g’ennyanja ekika kya whale si kibumbe kya ffoomu ye gye yateekamu ssente. Embavu ezisinga obunene ku za Tranque, emu ku za wakati, zaali zikwata ekitundu ekyo eky’ebyennyanja nga mu bulamu, ekisinga obunene mu buziba. Kati, obuziba obusinga obunene obw’omubiri ogwateekebwamu ssente ogw’ennyanja eno entongole buteekwa okuba nga bwali waakiri ffuuti kkumi na mukaaga; so nga, olubavu olukwatagana lwapima naye nga lusukka ffuuti munaana. Olwo olubavu luno ne lutuusa kitundu kyokka eky’endowooza entuufu ey’obunene obulamu obw’ekitundu ekyo. Ng’oggyeeko ekyo, okumala engeri emu, kati gye nnalaba naye omugongo ogw’obwereere, byonna ebyali bizingiddwako ttani z’obungi obwongezeddwa mu nnyama, ebinywa, omusaayi, n’ekyenda. Ekirala, olw’ebiwaawaatiro ebingi, wano nnalaba ennyondo ntono ezitategekeddwa bulungi; era mu kifo ky’ebiwuka ebizitowa era eby’ekitiibwa, naye nga tebirina magumba, ekintu ekitaliimu kintu kyonna!

N’olwekyo, nga bwe nnalowooza nti kya bwereere era kya busirusiru, omuntu ow’ensonyi atatambula okugezaako okutegeera obulungi ennyanja eno ey’ekitalo, ng’agenda akuba enduulu yokka ku magumba gaayo agafudde agakendedde, agagoloddwa mu muti guno ogw’emirembe. Nedda.Mu mutima gwokka ogw’akabi akasinga amangu; nga munda mu biwujjo by’ebiwujjo bye eby’obusungu byokka; ku nnyanja enzito etaliiko nsalo yokka, ennyanja ekika kya whale essiddwamu ssente mu bujjuvu y’esobola okuzuulibwa mu butuufu era mu bulamu.

Naye omugongo. Ku lw’ekyo, engeri esinga obulungi gye tuyinza okukirowoozaamu, nga tulina crane, okutuuma amagumba gaayo waggulu ku nkomerero. Tewali bizinensi ya mangu. Naye kati kiwedde, kifaanana nnyo Empagi ya Pompey.

Mu byonna mulimu vertebræ amakumi ana ne odd, nga mu magumba tezisibiddwa wamu. Zisinga kugalamira nga bulooka ennene eziriko enkokola ku mwaliiro ogw’ekika kya Gothic, nga zikola emisomo emigumu egy’amayinja amazito. Ekisinga obunene, eky’omu makkati, mu bugazi kiba kintu ekitakka wansi wa ffuuti ssatu, ate mu buziba kisukka ffuuti nnya. Ekisinga obutono, omugongo we gukendeera okutuuka mu mukira, gubeera gwa yinsi bbiri zokka mu bugazi, era gufaanana ekintu ekiringa omupiira gwa bbiiru omweru. Nategeezebwa nti wakyaliwo obutonotono, naye nga bufiiriddwa obuwundo obumu obutono obulya abantu, abaana ba kabona, obwali bubibba okuzannya nabyo amayinja amabajje. Bwe tutyo tulaba engeri omugongo gw’ebiramu n’ebiramu ebisinga obunene gye gukendeera ku nkomerero ne gufuuka omuzannyo gw’omwana omungu.





ESSUULA 104. Ennyama y’ennyanja Fossil Whale.

Okuva ku bungi bwe obw’amaanyi ennyanja ekika kya whale etuwa omulamwa ogusinga okusanyusa kw’eyinza okugaziya, okugaziya, n’okutwalira awamu okugaziya. Would you, toyinza kumunyigiriza. Ku ddembe eddungi alina okuyisibwa mu imperial folio yokka. Obutaddamu kwogera ku ffuuti ze okuva ku spiracle okutuuka ku mukira, n’oluggya lw’apima ku kiwato; lowooza ku biwujjo ebinene ennyo eby’ebyenda bye byokka, gye bimugalamira ng’obuguwa obunene n’ebiwujjo ebikulukutiddwa mu ddeeke eri wansi w’ettaka erya orlop-deck ey’emmeeri ey’olutalo.

Okuva bwe kiri nti neeyamye okukwata Leviathan ono, kinsaanidde okwesiima nga mmanyi byonna mu mulimu guno; obutabuusa maaso buwuka obutonotono obw’ensigo obw’omusaayi gwe, n’okumuwuuta okutuuka ku koyilo ey’enkomeredde ey’ekyenda kye. Olw’okuba yamwogerako dda mu bintu ebisinga obungi eby’enjawulo by’abeera n’ensengekera y’omubiri ebiriwo kati, kati kisigadde okumugulumiza mu ndowooza y’eby’edda, eby’ebintu eby’edda, n’eby’amataba nga tegannabaawo. Bwe zikozesebwa ku kitonde ekirala kyonna okuggyako Leviathan —ku nseenene oba enseenene —ebigambo ng’ebyo ebiyitibwa portly ebigambo biyinza okutwalibwa mu bwenkanya ng’eby’obukulu mu ngeri etasaana. Naye Leviyasani bwe kiba kiwandiiko, omusango gukyusibwa. Fain am I to stagger to this emprise wansi w’ebigambo ebisinga obuzito mu nkuluze. Era wano kigambibwa, nti buli lwe kiba kyangu okwebuuza ku omu mu nkola ya dissertations zino, bulijjo nkozesezza ekitabo ekinene ennyo ekya quarto ekya Johnson, ekyagulibwa mu bulambulukufu olw’ekigendererwa ekyo; kubanga omuwandiisi w’enkuluze oyo omututumufu’s uncommon personal bulk more fitted him okukung’aanya enzivuunula egenda okukozesebwa omuwandiisi w’ennyanja whale nga nze.

Omuntu atera okuwulira ku bawandiisi abasituka ne bazimba n’ensonga yaabwe, wadde nga eyinza okulabika ng’eya bulijjo. Kale ntya nange, nga mpandiika ku Leeviyasaani ono? Mu butamanya chirography yange egaziwa mu placard capitals. Mpa ekipande kya condor! Mpa ekinnya kya Vesuvius eky’okuteeka yinki! Mikwano mukwate emikono gyange! Kubanga mu kikolwa kyokka eky’okuwandiika ebirowoozo byange ku Leviathan ono, bankooya, ne banzirika olw’obujjuvu bwabwe obw’okusenya obubunye, nga balinga abazingiramu enzirugavu yonna eya ssaayansi, n’emilembe gyonna egy’ennyanja ekika kya whale, n’abantu, ne mastodons, eby’emabega, ebiriwo kati, n’ebigenda okujja, n’ebifaananyi byonna ebizitowa eby’obwakabaka ku nsi, ne mu bwengula bwonna, si nga tobaliddeemu bitundu byayo ebiriraanyewo. Ng’ekyo, era ekigulumiza ennyo, kye mpisa ennungi ey’omulamwa omunene era ogw’eddembe! Tugaziya okutuuka ku kitundu kyayo ekinene. Okusobola okufulumya ekitabo eky’amaanyi, olina okulonda omulamwa ogw’amaanyi. Tewali muzingo munene era guwangaala guyinza kuwandiikibwa ku nkwa, wadde nga waliwo bangi abagugezezzaako.

Ere okuyingira ku nsonga ya Fossil Whales, nnyanjula ebiwandiiko byange ng’omukugu mu by’ettaka, nga ntegeeza nti mu biseera byange eby’enjawulo mbadde muyimbi wa mayinja, era era nga ndi musima nnyo ow’emyala, emyala n’enzizi, ebifo eby’omwenge, ebiyumba, n’ebiyumba eby’engeri zonna. Mu ngeri y’emu, mu ngeri y’okusooka, njagala okujjukiza omusomi, nti wadde mu bitundu by’eby’ettaka eby’edda musangibwamu ebifo eby’edda eby’ebisolo ebikambwe kati kumpi bisaanyeewo ddala; ebisigalira ebyaddirira ebyazuulibwa mu biyitibwa ebitonde bya Tertiary birabika nga bye bikwatagana, oba mu ngeri yonna ebikwatibwa, wakati w’ebitonde ebitali bya bulijjo, n’ebyo abazzukulu baabwe ab’ewala abagambibwa nti baayingira mu Ssanduuko; Fossil Whales zonna ezizuuliddwa okutuusa kati za kiseera kya Tertiary, nga kino kye kiseera ekisembayo okukulembera ebitonde eby’okungulu. Era newankubadde nga tewali n’emu ku zo eddamu bulungi kika kyonna ekimanyiddwa eky’omulembe guno, zikyafaanagana ekimala nazo mu bintu okutwalira awamu, okusobola okulaga obutuufu bw’okutwala ekifo kyazo ng’ebintu eby’edda eby’e Cetacean.

Ebintu ebimenyese ebyawuddwamu eby’ennyanja ekika kya whales ezaaliwo nga tezinnabaawo, ebitundutundu by’amagumba gazo n’amagumba gazo, mu myaka amakumi asatu egiyise, mu biseera eby’enjawulo, bizuuliddwa ku musingi gw’ensozi za Alps, mu Lombardy, mu Bufalansa, mu Bungereza, mu Scotland, ne mu States of Louisiana, Mississippi, ne Alabama. Mu bimu ku bisinga okwewuunyisa ku bisigalira ng’ebyo mulimu ekitundu ky’ekiwanga, mu mwaka gwa 1779 ekyaziikibwa mu Rue Dauphine mu Paris, oluguudo olumpi oluggukira kumpi butereevu ku lubiri lw’Abatuileries; n’amagumba agaaziikibwa mu kusima emyalo eminene egy’e Antwerp, mu kiseera kya Napoleon. Cuvier yagamba nti ebitundutundu bino byali bya bika bya Leviathanic ebimu ebitali bimanyiddwa nnyo.

Naye ekisinga okwewuunyisa mu bisigalira byonna eby’omu Cetacean byali magumba amanene kumpi agajjuvu ag’ekisolo ekizibu ekyasaanawo, ekyasangibwa mu mwaka gwa 1842, ku nnimiro y’omulamuzi Creagh, mu Alabama. Abaddu abaali bakkiririza mu kitundu ekyo abaali batiisibwatiisibwa ennyo baakitwala ng’amagumba g’omu ku bamalayika abaali bagudde. Abasawo b’e Alabama baakirangirira nti kyali kisolo kinene nnyo ekyekulula, era ne bakiwa erinnya lya Basilosaurus. Naye amagumba agamu ag’ekyokulabirako kyayo nga gatwalibwa emitala w’ennyanja eri Owen, Omuzungu omukugu mu by’omubiri, kyazuulibwa nti ekisolo kino ekigambibwa nti kyali kikulukuta kyali kiwuka kya nnyanja, wadde nga kyali kya kika kya nsolo. Ekyokulabirako eky’amaanyi eky’ensonga, enfunda n’enfunda eddibwamu mu kitabo kino, nti amagumba g’ennyanja ekika kya whale gawa naye kitono ekiraga enkula y’omubiri gwe ogwateekebwamu ssente mu bujjuvu. Bwatyo Owen n’addamu okubatiza ekisolo ekiyitibwa Zeuglodon; era mu lupapula lwe olwasomebwa mu maaso g’ekibiina kya London Geological Society, yakilangirira, mu nsonga, ekimu ku bitonde ebisinga okuba eby’enjawulo enkyukakyuka z’ensi yonna bye zisanguddewo.

Bwe nnyimiridde wakati w’amagumba gano ag’amaanyi aga Leviathan, ebiwanga, amanyo, ensaya, embiriizi, n’omugongo, byonna nga bifaanagana ekitundu n’ebika by’ebisolo ebikambwe eby’omu nnyanja ebiriwo; naye mu kiseera kye kimu nga ku ludda olulala alina enkolagana efaananako n’Abaleeviyaani abaali bazikirizibwa abaali baziyiza emirembe, abakadde baabwe abatabalibwa; Nze, amataba, nzuulwa okuddayo mu kiseera ekyo ekyewuunyisa, ere ekiseera kyennyini kiyinza okugambibwa nti kyatandika; kubanga ebiseera byatandika n’omuntu. Wano akavuyo aka enzirugavu aka Saturn kanneekulukuunyako, era nfuna amaaso agazibu, agakankana mu biseera ebyo eby’emirembe gyonna ebya Polar; ebigo ebya bbalaafu ebyali bikutte bwe byanyiga nnyo ku bitundu kati ebiyitibwa Tropics; era mu mayiro zonna 25,000 ez’okwetooloola ensi eno, tewaaliwo mukono gwonna ogw’obugazi bw’omukono oguyinza okubeerawo. Awo ensi yonna yali ya whale; era, kabaka w’ebitonde, yaleka okuzuukuka kwe ng’ayita mu nnyiriri eziriwo kati ez’ensozi Andes ne Himmalehs. Ani ayinza okulaga olulyo nga Leviathan? Ekyuma kya Akabu kyali kiyiise omusaayi omukadde okusinga ogwa Falaawo. Mesusela alabika nga mulenzi wa ssomero. Ntunula okwetoloola okukwatagana ne Seemu mu ngalo. Ntidde nnyo olw’okubeerawo kuno okw’ekika kya antemosaic, okutali kwa nsibuko okw’entiisa ezitayogerwako ez’ennyanja ekika kya whale, nga, olw’okuba zaaliwo nga tezinnabaawo, zirina okwetaaga okubaawo oluvannyuma lw’emyaka gyonna egy’obuntu okuggwa.

Naye si ye Leviathan ono yekka alese ebiwandiiko bye eby’edda nga Adamiti tennabaawo mu bipande by’obutonde eby’ekika kya stereotype, era mu limestone ne marl yasikira ekifaananyi kye eky’edda; naye ku bipande by’e Misiri, ng’obukadde bwabyo bulabika ng’obugamba nti kumpi kwali kwa mpisa ya bifo eby’edda, tusanga ekiwandiiko ekitaliimu kubuusabuusa eky’ekiwaawaatiro kye. Mu muzigo gwa yeekaalu ennene eya Denderah, emyaka nga ataano egiyise, ku kisenge kya granite kyazuulibwa ekisenge ekibumbe era ekyasiigibwa langi, nga kijjudde ennyonyi eziyitibwa centaurs, griffins, ne dolphins, ezifaananako n’ebifaananyi eby’ekitalo ku nsi ey’omu ggulu ey’abantu ab’omulembe guno. Nga yeekulukuunya mu bo, Leeviyasani omukadde yawuga ng’edda; yaliwo ng’awuga mu planisphere eyo, ebyasa bingi nga Sulemaani tannakwatibwa.

Era tewalina kulekebwawo kukakasa kulala okwewuunyisa okw’obukadde bw’ennyanja ekika kya whale, mu magumba ge ag’amagumba ag’oluvannyuma lw’amataba, nga bwe kyateekebwawo omutembeeyi ow’ekitiibwa John Leo, omutambuze omukadde owa Barbary.

“Tebali wala nnyo okuva ku ludda lw’Ennyanja, balina Yeekaalu, Ebikondo n’Ebikondo byayo bikoleddwa mu Magumba ga Whale-Bones; kubanga Whale ez’obunene obw’ekitalo emirundi mingi zisuulibwa waggulu nga zifudde ku lubalama olwo.Abantu aba bulijjo balowooza, nti olw’Amaanyi ag’ekyama Katonda ge yawa Yeekaalu, tewali Whale eyinza kugiyita awatali kufa mangu.Naye amazima g’Ensonga gali, nti ku buli ludda olwa Temple, waliwo Amayinja agakuba Maayilo bbiri mu Nnyanja, ne galumya Whale bwe zimulisizaako Zikuuma Olubavu lwa Whale olw’obuwanvu obutasuubirwa olw’Ekyamagero, nga kigalamidde ku Ttaka ng’ekitundu kyayo ekikonvu waggulu, kikola Arch, Omutwe gwayo teguyinza kutuukirirwa Musajja ku Mugongo gwa Ngamiya Emyaka kikumi nga sinnakiraba Bannabyafaayo baabwe bakakasa, nti Nabbi eyalagula ku Mahomet, yava mu Yeekaalu eno, era abamu tebayimiridde kukakasa, nti Nabbi Yona yasuulibwa Whale ku Musingi gwa Yeekaalu.”

ESSUULA 105. Obunene bwa Whale Bukendeera? — Anaazikirizibwa?

N’olwekyo, olw’okuba Leviyasani ono bw’ajja ng’atuwuuba okuva mu mazzi ag’Emirembe n’Emirembe, kiyinza okubuuzibwa mu ngeri esaanira, oba, mu lugendo oluwanvu olw’emirembe gye, teyavunda okuva ku kitundu ekinene ekyasooka ekya bakitaabe.

Naye bwe tunoonyereza tusanga, nti si whales zokka ez’omulembe guno ze zisinga mu bunene okusinga ezo ezisangibwa mu nkola ya Tertiary (ezizingiramu ekiseera eky’enjawulo eky’eby’ettaka nga omuntu tannabaawo), naye ku whale ezisangibwa mu nkola eyo eya Tertiary, ezo ezibeera mu bitonde byayo eby’oluvannyuma zisukka mu bunene ezo ez’edda.

Ku nnyanja ekika kya whale zonna ezaali tezinnaba kusima, esinga obunene ye Alabama eyogerwako mu ssuula esembayo, era ng’obuwanvu tebuwera ffuuti nsanvu mu magumba. So nga, twalaba dda, nti ekipimo ky’olutambi kiwa ffuuti nsanvu mu bbiri ku magumba g’ennyanja ennene ey’omulembe. Era mpulidde, ku buyinza bw’abavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale, nti Sperm Whales zaawambibwa kumpi ffuuti kikumi obuwanvu mu kiseera we zaakwatibwa.

Naye ka kibeere, nti wadde nga ennyanja ekika kya whale ez’essaawa eno zigenda mu maaso mu bunene ku ezo ez’ebiseera byonna eby’eby’ettaka eby’emabega; kiyinza obutaba, nti okuva mu kiseera kya Adamu zaavunda?

Mazima ddala, tulina okumaliriza bwe tutyo, bwe tuba ab’okusiba ebiwandiiko by’abaami nga Pliny, n’abakugu mu by’obutonde ab’edda okutwalira awamu. Kubanga Pliny atubuulira ku nnyanja ekika kya whale ezaakwata yiika z’ebitundu ebiramu, ne Aldrovandus w’endala ezaali zipima ffuuti ebikumi munaana mu buwanvu —Rope Walks ne Thames Tunnels of Whales! Era ne mu nnaku za Banks ne Solander, abakugu mu by’obutonde ba Cooke, tusanga omuntu Omudan ow’ekibiina kya Academy of Sciences ng’ateeka wansi envubu ezimu eza Iceland (reydan-siskur, oba Wrinkled Bellies) ku yaadi kikumi mu abiri; kwe kugamba, ffuuti ebikumi bisatu mu nkaaga. Era Lacépède, omukugu mu by’obutonde Omufaransa, mu byafaayo bye ebizibu ennyo eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale, ku ntandikwa yennyini ey’omulimu gwe (olupapula 3), assa wansi envubu eya Right Whale ku mita kikumi, ffuuti ebikumi bisatu mu abiri mu munaana. Era omulimu guno gwafulumizibwa kikeerezi nnyo nga AD 1825.

Naye waliwo omuvuzi w’ennyanja whale yenna anaakkiriza emboozi zino? Nedda, ennyanja ekika kya whale ey’ennaku zino nnene nga bajjajjaabe mu kiseera kya Pliny. Era singa ngenda Pliny gy’ali, nze, omuvuzi w’ennyanja whale (okusinga bwe yali), nja kukola obuvumu okumugamba bw’atyo. Kubanga sisobola kutegeera ngeri gye kiri, nti wadde nga mummy z’Abamisiri ezaaziikibwa emyaka enkumi n’enkumi nga ne Pliny tannazaalibwa, tezipima nnyo mu ssanduuko zazo ng’Omukentuck ow’omulembe guno mu masokisi ge; era nga ente n’ebisolo ebirala ebibumbe ku bipande eby’edda eby’e Misiri n’e Nineeve, olw’ebipimo ebituufu mwe bikubiddwa, nga bwe bikakasa bulungi nti ente ez’olulyo oluwanvu, eziriisibwa mu ddundiro, ez’ebirabo eza Smithfield, tezenkanankana zokka, naye zisukka wala mu bunene ezisinga okugejja mu nte za Falaawo ezigejja; mu maaso ga bino byonna, sijja kukkiriza nti mu bisolo byonna ennyanja ekika kya whale yokka yandibadde evunda.

Naye okubuuliriza okulala kukyaliwo; omu atera okutabulwa aba Nantucketers abasinga okubeera abatali balongoofu. Oba olw’okutunula kumpi okumanyi byonna ku mitwe gy’emmeeri z’ennyanja ekika kya whale, kati eziyingira ne mu biwonvu bya Behring, ne ziyingira mu ddulaaya n’ebisenge eby’ekyama ebisinga ewala mu nsi yonna; era enkumi n’engoye n’amafumu ne bidduka ku lubalama lw’ennyanja zonna eza ssemazinga; ensonga enkulu eri nti, oba Leviyasani asobola okumala ebbanga eddene okugumira okuyigga okugazi bwe kuti, n’okusaanyaawo okutaliimu kwejjusa; oba ku nkomerero tasaanidde kuzikirizibwa mu mazzi, era ennyanja ekika kya whale esembayo, okufaananako n’omusajja asembayo, n’efuuwa payipu ye esembayo, n’oluvannyuma ye kennyini n’efuumuuka mu kufuuwa okusembayo.

Nga tugeraageranya ebisibo by’ennyanja ekika kya whale eziriko enkovu n’ebisibo by’enyanja ebiriko ebikonde, nga, si myaka amakumi ana egiyise, byabuna enkumi n’enkumi mu bibira eby’e Illinois ne Missouri, ne bikankanya enviiri zaabyo ez’ekyuma ne bikuba ennyindo n’ebibatu byabwe ebikutte okubwatuka ku bifo by’ebibuga ebikulu eby’emigga ebirimu abantu abangi, kati omusuubuzi ow’empisa gy’akuguza ettaka ku ddoola emu buli yinsi; mu kugeraageranya ng’okwo ensonga etayinza kuziyizibwa yandirabise ng’eweereddwa, okulaga nti kati ennyanja ekika kya whale eyiggibwa tesobola kusimattuka kusaanawo mangu.

Naye ensonga eno olina okugitunuulira mu buli ngeri. Wadde nga ekiseera kitono nnyo emabega —si bulamu birungi —okubala abantu kw’enyanja mu Illinois kwasukka okubala kw’abasajja kati mu London, era newankubadde nga mu kiseera kino tewali jjembe wadde ekigere ku zo ekisigadde mu kitundu ekyo kyonna; era newankubadde nga ekyavaako okuzikirizibwa kuno okw’ekitalo kwali ffumu ly’omuntu; naye obutonde obw’enjawulo ennyo obw’okuyigga ennyanja ekika kya whale bugaana nnyo enkomerero etali ya kitiibwa nnyo eri Leviathan. Abasajja amakumi ana mu mmeeri emu nga bayigga Sperm Whales okumala emyezi amakumi ana mu munaana balowooza nti bakoze bulungi nnyo, era Katonda yeebazibwe, singa ku nkomerero batwala awaka amafuta g’ebyennyanja amakumi ana. So nga, mu biseera by’abayizzi n’abatego b’amawanga g’obugwanjuba abakadde ab’e Canada n’Abayindi, ng’amaserengeta ag’ewala (enjuba yaago ng’egwa n’okutuusa kati) yali ddungu era nga mbeerera, omuwendo gwe gumu ogw’abasajja aba moccasined, okumala emyezi gye gimu, nga beebagadde embalaasi mu kifo ky’okusaabala mu mmeeri, bandibadde batta enyanja si makumi ana, wabula emitwalo amakumi ana n’okusingawo; ensonga nti, singa wabaawo obwetaavu, eyinza okulagibwa mu bibalo.

Era, bwe kitwalibwa ng’ekituufu, tekirabika nga nsonga yonna ewagira okusaanawo mpolampola kwa Sperm Whale, okugeza, nti mu myaka egyayita (ekitundu eky’enkomerero eky’ekyasa ekyayita, tugambe) Leviathans zino, mu bikoola ebitonotono, zaasanga emirundi mingi nnyo okusinga mu kiseera kino, era, ekivaamu, engendo tezaali mpanvu nnyo, era era zaali za musaala nnyo. Kubanga, nga bwe kibadde kyalabiddwa awalala, ennyanja ekika kya whale ezo, nga zikwatibwako endowooza ezimu ez’obukuumi, kati ziwuga ennyanja mu kabangali ennene ennyo, ne kiba nti ku kigero ekinene ebifo eby’obwerufu ebisaasaanidde, ebikoligo, n’ebikoola, n’amasomero ag’ennaku endala kati bikuŋŋaanyiziddwa mu magye amanene naye nga gaawuddwamu nnyo, agatatera kubaawo. Ekyo kyokka. Era kyenkanyi ekikyamu kirabika okwegulumiza, nti olw’okuba ennyanja eziyitibwa whale-bone whale tezikyalina nsonga nnyingi mu myaka egyayita nga zijjudde, kye kiva kivaako ekika ekyo nakyo kikendeera. Kubanga zigobebwa okuva ku nsozi okutuuka ku mbeba yokka; era singa olubalama lw’ennyanja olumu terukyanywezebwa nnyo n’ennyonyi zaabwe ez’ennyonyi, olwo, kakasa nti, omuguwa omulala era ogw’ewala gubadde guwuniikirira gye buvuddeko ennyo olw’ekyewuunyo ekitamanyiddwa.

Ate era: ku bikwata ku Baleeviyasani bano abasembayo okwogerwako, balina ebigo bibiri ebinywevu, nga, mu byonna ebiyinza okubaawo mu bantu, bijja kusigala nga tebiyinza kuwambibwa emirembe gyonna. Era nga bwe kiri ku kulumba ebiwonvu byabwe, Abaswisi abajjudde omuzira bazze emabega ne bagenda mu nsozi zaabwe; kale, nga ziyiggibwa okuva mu savannas ne glades ez’ennyanja eza wakati, whale-bone whale ku nkomerero zisobola okuddukira mu bigo byabwe ebya Polar, era nga zibbira wansi w’ebiziyiza eby’endabirwamu ebisembayo ne bbugwe eyo, ne zijja waggulu wakati mu nnimiro ezirimu omuzira n’ebiwonvu; era mu nkulungo ewunyiriza eya Ddesemba ow’olubeerera, bid defiance eri byonna ebigobererwa okuva eri omuntu.

Naye nga oboolyawo amakumi ataano ku nnyanja zino ezirina amagumba g’ennyanja ekika kya whale bwe zikubiddwa enkuufiira ku cachalot emu, abamu ku bafirosoofo ab’omu forecastle bamalirizza nti akatyabaga kano akalungi kakendeeza dda nnyo nnyo ebibinja byabwe. Naye newankubadde nga okumala ekiseera ekiyise omuwendo gw’ennyanja ekika kya whale zino, ezitakka wansi wa 13,000, buli mwaka zibadde zittibwa ku lubalama lw’ennyanja nor’ west coast Abamerika bokka; naye waliwo okulowoozebwako okufuula n’embeera eno okuba entono oba nga tewali nsonga ng’ensonga ewakanya mu nsonga eno.

Wadde nga kya butonde okubeera nga tetukkiriza ku bikwata ku bungi bw’ebitonde ebisinga obunene ennyo mu nsi yonna, naye kiki kye tunaagamba Harto, munnabyafaayo wa Goa, bw’atugamba nti mu kuyigga okumu Kabaka wa Siam yakwata enjovu 4,000; nti mu bitundu ebyo enjovu nnyingi ng’ebibinja by’ente mu mbeera ey’obutiti. Era kirabika tewali nsonga yonna lwaki tubuusabuusa nti singa enjovu zino, kati ezibadde ziyigganyizibwa okumala enkumi n’enkumi z’emyaka, Semiramis, Porus, Hannibal, ne bakabaka bonna ab’Ebuvanjuba abaddiring’ana —bwe ziba nga zikyawangaala eyo mu bungi, nnyo nnyo envubu ennene eyinza okuwangaala okusinga okuyigga kwonna, okuva bwe kiri nti erina amalundiro g’agenda okugaziwa, nga gasinga ddala emirundi ebiri okusinga Asiya yonna, Amerika, Bulaaya ne Afirika, New Holland, n’ebizinga byonna eby’ennyanja bwe bigattiddwa.

Ate era: tulina okulowooza, nti okuva ku bulamu obuwanvu obulowoozebwa okuba obw’amaanyi obw’ennyanja ekika kya whale, oboolyawo okutuuka ku myaka gyazo egy’ekyasa n’okusingawo, n’olwekyo mu kiseera kyonna ekimu, emirembe gy’abantu abakulu egy’enjawulo egiwerako giteekwa okuba egy’omulembe. Era ekyo kye kiri, mu bbanga ttono tuyinza okufuna ekirowoozo ekimu, nga tukuba akafaananyi ku biggya byonna eby’amalaalo, amasabo, n’ebifo eby’amaka eby’obutonzi nga bivaamu emirambo emiramu egy’abasajja, abakazi, n’abaana bonna abaali balamu emyaka nsanvu mu etaano egiyise; era n’okwongerako ekifo kino ekitabalika ku muwendo gw’abantu abaliwo kati mu nsi yonna.

N’olwekyo, olw’ebintu bino byonna, tutwala ennyanja ekika kya whale ng’etafa mu kika kyayo, wadde nga esaanawo mu butonde bwayo. Yawuga ennyanja nga ssemazinga tezinnaba kumenya mazzi; lumu yawuga ku kifo awaali Tuileries, n’olubiri lwa Windsor, ne Kremlin. Mu mataba ga Nuuwa yanyooma Eryato lya Nuuwa; era singa ensi egenda kuddamu okubooga, okufaananako ne Budaaki, okutta envunza zaayo, olwo ennyanja ekika kya whale ey’olubeerera ejja kukyawona, era ng’ekulidde ku ntikko ey’oku ntikko ey’amataba ga ekyengulu, efuuwa okujeemera kwe okufuukuuse mu bbanga.





ESSUULA 106. Ekigere kya Akabu.

Engeri ey’amangu Kapiteeni Akabu gye yali avuddemu emmeeri ya Samuel Enderby ey’e London, yali tebadde ya butafaayo n’obutabanguko obutonotono eri omuntu we yennyini. Yali ayaka n’amaanyi mangi nnyo ku kugwa kw’eryato lye ne kiba nti okugulu kwe okw’amasanga kwali kufunye ensisi eyawukana ekitundu. Era bwe oluvannyuma lw’okufuna ddeeke ye, n’ekinnya kye eky’okuvuga awo, n’avuga n’amaanyi ennyo n’ekiragiro eky’amangu eri omuvuzi (yali, nga bulijjo, ekintu ku butavuga kwe mu ngeri etakyukakyuka kimala); awo, amasanga agaali gakankanyizibwa edda ne gafuna okukyusibwakyusibwa n’okusumululwa okw’enjawulo, ne kiba nti wadde nga gaasigala nga gonna, era mu kulabika kwonna nga ga kwegomba, naye Akabu teyakiraba nti kyesigika ddala.

Era, ddala, kyalabika ng’ekintu ekitono ekyewuunyisa, nti olw’obutafaayo bwe bwonna obwali bubunye, obw’eddalu, Akabu oluusi yafaayo nnyo ku mbeera y’eggumba eryo eryafu lye yayimiriddeko ekitundu. Kubanga tewaali bbanga ddene nnyo nga Pequod tennasimbula okuva e Nantucket, nti yali asangiddwa ekiro kimu ng’agalamidde ku ttaka, era nga tategeera; olw’omuntu omu atamanyiddwa, era alabika ng’atayinza kunnyonnyolwa, atayinza kulowoozebwako, ekitundu kye eky’amasanga nga kisenguddwa nnyo, ne kiba nti kyali kikubiddwa mu ngeri ey’omuggo, era nga byonna bimufumita ekisambi; era tewali buzibu buyitiridde nti ekiwundu ekyali kiruma ennyo kyawona ddala.

Era, mu kiseera ekyo, tekyalemererwa kuyingira mu birowoozo bye eby’omuntu omu, nti ennaku yonna ey’okubonaabona okwo okwaliwo mu kiseera ekyo kwali nsonga butereevu yokka ey’ekibonyoobonyo eky’edda; era naye yalabika bulungi ng’alaba, nti ng’ekisolo ekyekulula ekisinga obutwa mu lutobazzi bwe kiwangaaza ekika kyakyo nga tekyewalika ng’omuyimbi asinga okuwooma mu kibira; kale, kyenkanyi ne buli felicity, ebintu byonna eby’ennaku mu butonde bizaala ebifaanana byabwe. Weewaawo, okusinga kyenkanyi, Akabu bwe yalowooza; okuva obuzaale n’ezzadde ly’Enaku bwe bigenda wala okusinga obuzaale n’ezzadde ly’Essanyu. Kubanga, si kulaga kino: nti kwe kuteebereza okuva mu njigiriza ezimu ez’ennono, nti wadde ng’ebimu ku binyumirwa eby’obutonde wano tebijja kuba na baana gye babazaaliddwa olw’ensi endala, naye, okwawukana ku ekyo, bijja kugobererwa essanyu-obutaba na mwana mu kuggwaamu essuubi kwonna okwa ggeyeena; so nga, ennaku ezimu ezifa ezirimu omusango zijja kuba zikyazaala mu ngeri ey’obuzaale ezzadde ery’ennaku ezigenda mu maaso emirembe gyonna egy’ennaku ezisukka entaana; si n’akatono okulaga kino, wakyaliwo obutenkanankana mu kwekenneenya okw’obuziba okw’ekintu ekyo. Kubanga, Akabu yalowooza, wadde nga n’essanyu ery’oku nsi ery’oku ntikko lirina obutono obumu obutategeeza obukwekwese, naye, wansi, ebizibu byonna eby’omu mutima, amakulu ag’ekyama, era, mu basajja abamu, obukulu bwa bamalayika abakulu; bwe batyo n’okulondoola kwabwe okunyiikivu tekulimba kuggyibwako okweyoleka. Okulondoola ennyiriri z’obuzaale bw’ennaku zino ez’okufa ez’oku ntikko, kitutwala ku nkomerero mu nsibuko ezitaliiko nsibuko za bakatonda; bwe kityo, mu maaso g’enjuba zonna ezisanyusa, ezikola omuddo, n’okukuba ebikonde ebigonvu, emyezi egy’amakungula egy’ekyekulungirivu, tulina okwetaaga okwewaayo mu kino: nti bakatonda bennyini tebasanyuka emirembe gyonna. Akabonero k’amazaalibwa akatazikirizibwa, ak’ennaku mu kyenyi ky’omuntu, sitampu yokka ey’ennaku mu bassa emikono.

Mu butamanya wano ekyama kifulumiziddwa, mpozzi ekiyinza okusinga okutuufu, mu ngeri eteereddwawo, ekyabikkulwa emabegako. Nga waliwo ebintu ebirala bingi ebikwata ku Akabu, bulijjo kyali kisigadde nga kyama eri abamu, lwaki kyali, nti okumala ekiseera ekigere, nga Pequod tannavuga n’oluvannyuma lw’okusaabala, yali yeekwese wala n’okwetongola ng’okwo okufaanana okwa Grand-Lama; era, olw’ekiseera ekyo ekimu, yanoonya obuddukiro nga tasobola kwogera, nga bwe tuyinza okugamba, wakati mu lukiiko lw’abafu olwa mabbaale. Ensonga ya Kapiteeni Peleg eyali ewunya ennyo ku kintu kino yalabika ng’emala n’akatono; wadde, ddala, nga bwe kyakwata ku kitundu kyonna ekya Akabu ekizito, buli kubikkulirwa kwalya nnyo mu kizikiza eky’amaanyi okusinga ekitangaala ekinnyonnyola. Naye, ku nkomerero, byonna byavaayo; ensonga eno emu yakola, waakiri. Akabenje ako ak’entiisa kaali wansi w’okwekweka kwe okw’akaseera obuseera. Era si kino kyokka, naye eri enzirugavu eyo eyali ekola endagaano buli kiseera, egwa ku lukalu, olw’ensonga yonna, eyalina enkizo ey’enkola etali ya kuwereddwa nnyo gy’ali; eri enzirugavu eyo ey’ensonyi waggulu yalaga nti omuntu afiiriddwa —nga yasigala, nga bwe yakola, nga Akabu yali tabaliriddwa mu ngeri ey’omutima —yeteeka ssente mu ntiisa, nga teyali wansi ddala okuva mu nsi y’emyoyo n’okukaaba. Kale, olw’obunyiikivu bwabwe gy’ali, bonna baali beekobaana, okutuuka mu bo, okuziba okumanya kw’ekintu kino okuva eri abalala; era kye kyava, nti okutuusa nga wayiseewo ekiseera ekinene, kyatuuka ku ddeeke za Pequod.

Naye bino byonna bibeere nga bwe biyinza; leka sinodi etalabika, etaliimu makulu mu bbanga, oba abalangira abeesasuza n’abafuzi b’omuliro, balina akakwate oba nedda ne Akabu ow’oku nsi, naye, mu nsonga eno eriwo kati ey’okugulu kwe, yakwata emitendera egy’omugaso egy’olwatu;—yayita omubazzi.

Era omukozi oyo bwe yalabika mu maaso ge, n’amulagira awatali kulwa n’atandika okukola ekigere ekipya, n’alagira banne okumulaba ng’aweereddwa ebisiba byonna n’ebikondo eby’amasanga g’amasanga (Sperm Whale) ebyali bikuŋŋaanyiziddwa okutuusa kati ku lugendo, okusobola okulonda n’obwegendereza ebintu ebisinga obugumu, ebisinga okubeera ebitangaavu. Kino bwe kyakolebwa, omubazzi yafuna ebiragiro okumaliriza okugulu ekiro ekyo; era n’okugiwa ebikozesebwa byonna, nga tebyesigamye ku ebyo ebikwata ku ekyo ekiteesigika ekikozesebwa. Ate era, ekifo eky’emmeeri ekyo kyalagirwa okusitulwa okuva mu kifo we kyali tekikola okumala akaseera; era, okusobola okwanguya ensonga, omuweesi yalagirwa okugenda mu maaso omulundi gumu n’okujingirira ebyuma byonna eby’ekyuma ebyandibadde byetaagisa.
