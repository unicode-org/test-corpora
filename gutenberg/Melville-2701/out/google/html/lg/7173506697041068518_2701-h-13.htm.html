Singa ku Starbuck okwolesebwa kwa squid kyali kintu kya portents, oku queequeg kyali kintu kya njawulo nnyo.

Mu bbanga ttono ng’atuuka ku ludda lwe, Stubb, ng’anyweza okugulu kwe mu cleat eyali efuuse enfuufu, yaddusa omusota oluvannyuma lw’omusota mu byennyanja ebibuuka; Ku kigambo ekiduumira, eryato likyukakyuka nga liva mu kkubo lya whale’s horrible wallow, ate oluvannyuma ne lituuka n’okudduka okudduka okulala.

ESSUULA 62. Omusipi.

Ekigambo ekikwata ku kintu ekyaliwo mu ssuula esembayo.

Okusinziira ku nkozesa etakyukakyuka ey’okuvuba, eryato eriyitibwa whale-boat lisika okuva ku mmeeri, ng’omukulu oba omutemu whale ye muvuzi ow’ekiseera, ate omuvubi wa harpooneer oba whale-fastener y’asika ekyuma ekisinga okuba eky’omu maaso, ekyo ekimanyiddwa nga harpooneer-oar. Kati kyetaaga omukono ogw’amaanyi era ogw’obunkenke okukuba ekyuma ekisooka mu byennyanja; Kubanga emirundi mingi, mu kye bayita omusota omuwanvu, ekintu ekizito kirina okusuulibwa okutuuka ku bbanga lya ffuuti amakumi abiri oba amakumi asatu. Naye wadde nga yawangaaza era ng’ekooya okuyigga, Harpooneer asuubirwa okusikayo ekyuma kye mu kiseera kino okutuuka ku kiyitirivu; mazima ddala, asuubirwa okuteekawo ekyokulabirako ky’emirimu egisukkulumye ku muntu eri abalala, si mu kuvugira mu ngeri etasuubirwa kwokka, naye n’okuleekaana okw’amaanyi era okw’obuzira enfunda n’enfunda; era kye kitegeeza okusigala ng’oleekaana waggulu ku kkampasi y’omuntu, ng’ate ebinywa ebirala byonna binywezeddwa era nga kitundu kitandise —ekyo tewali akimanyi wabula abo abakigezezzaako. Ku lw’omu, sisobola kuwuubaala nnyo era nkola mu ngeri ey’obulagajjavu ennyo mu kiseera kimu era mu kiseera kye kimu. N’olwekyo, mu mbeera eno ey’okunyigirizibwa, ey’okuwoggana, ng’omugongo gwe gutunudde mu byennyanja, omulundi gumu omukubi w’ennanga akooye awulira okukaaba okusanyusa —“Yimiridde, muwe!” Kati alina okugwa n’okunyweza ekiwato kye, okwetooloola wakati we wakati mu kkubo, n’akwata ekyuma kye okuva mu kisambi, era n’amaanyi matono ge gayinza okusigala, awandiika emboozi okukisuula mu ngeri emu oba endala mu nnyanja ekika kya whale. Tekyewunyisa, okutwala ekibinja kyonna eky’abavuzi b’ennyanja mu mubiri, nti ku mikisa egy’obwenkanya egy’amakumi ataano egy’omusota, so si etaano giba gya buwanguzi; Tekyewuunyisa nti aba Harpooner bangi abatalina mukisa bakolimiddwa ddalu era banakuzibwa; Tekyewuunyisa nti abamu ku bo mu butuufu baakutula emisuwa gyabwe egy’omusaayi mu lyato; Tekyewuunyisa nti ennyonyi ezimu eziyitibwa sperm whalemen teziriiwo emyaka ena nga zirina ebipipa bina; Tekyewuunyisa nti eri bannannyini mmeeri bangi, okuvuba envubu kifiirwa nnyo; Kubanga ye harpooneer ekola olugendo, era bw’oggya omukka mu mubiri gwe oyinza otya okusuubira okugusanga awo ng’abasinga baagala!

Nate, singa omusipi guba guwangudde, olwo mu kaseera ak’okubiri akakulu, kwe kugamba, ennyanja ekika kya whale bw’etandika okudduka, omuvuzi w’amaato n’omuvuzi w’amaato mu ngeri y’emu batandika okudduka mu maaso n’emabega, mu kabi akali kumpi eri bo bennyini ne buli muntu omulala. Olwo lwe bakyusa ebifo; Era omukulu w’essomero, akulira eby’emikono omuto, akwata siteegi ye entuufu mu busaale bw’eryato.

Kati, sifaayo ani akuuma ekikontana n’ekyo, naye bino byonna bya busirusiru era tebyetaagisa. Omukulu w’omutwe alina okusigala mu busaale okuva ku kusooka okutuuka ku nkomerero; bombi yandibadde akuba ensambaggere n’effumu, era tewali kuvugira kwonna kulina kumusuubirwamu, okuggyako mu mbeera eyeeyolese eri omuvubi yenna. Nkimanyi nti kino oluusi kyandibadde kizingiramu okufiirwa sipiidi entono mu kuyigga; naye obumanyirivu obw’ekiseera ekiwanvu mu bavubi ab’enjawulo ab’amawanga agasukka mu limu bunkakasizza nti mu kulemererwa okusinga obungi mu nvuba, mu ngeri yonna tekubadde nnyo sipiidi ya whale ng’okukoowa okwayogerwako emabegako okw’omuvubi w’ennyanja ekika kya harpooneer kwe kubaleetedde.

Okusobola okukakasa obulungi obusinga obunene mu musinde, abavuzi b’engoye z’ensi eno balina okutandika okuyimirira okuva mu butakola, so si okuva mu kukola ennyo.





ESSUULA 63. Ensigo.

Okuva mu kikolo, amatabi gakula; okuva mu byo, amatabi. Kale, mu masomo agavaamu ebibala, kuza essuula.

Crotch eyogerwako ku lupapula oluyise esaana okwogerwako nga yeetongodde. Guno muggo oguliko enkokola ogw’engeri ey’enjawulo, nga guweza ffuuti nga bbiri, nga guyingizibwa mu ngeri eyeesimbye mu kisenge ky’emmundu eky’oku ddyo okumpi n’obutaasa, n’ekigendererwa eky’okufunira ekifo eky’okuwummuliramu enkomerero y’embaawo ey’engo, enkomerero yaayo endala ey’obwereere, ey’emisumaali efuluma mu ngeri eserengese okuva ku maaso. Mu ngeri eyo eky’okulwanyisa ekyo kiba kituuse mangu eri oyo akisuula, n’akinyagulula amangu okuva mu kifo we kiwummudde ng’omusajja ow’emabega w’emmundu bw’akuba emmundu ye okuva ku bbugwe. Kiba kya mpisa okubeera n’ennanga bbiri eziwummuzibwa mu kisambi, nga ziyitibwa ekyuma ekisooka n’ekyokubiri.

Naye ensukusa zino ebbiri, buli emu ku kaguwa kaayo, zombi zikwatagana ne layini; Ekintu nga kino kiri bwe kiti: okubaddusa bombi, bwe kiba kisoboka, ekimu mu kaseera ako oluvannyuma lw’omulala mu whale y’emu; bwe kityo singa, mu kusika okujja, omuntu alina okuggyayo, omulala ayinza okuba ng’akyasigaza okukwata. Kwe kubisaamu emirundi ebiri emikisa. Naye kitera nnyo okubaawo nti olw’okudduka okw’amangu, okw’amaanyi, okw’okukonziba kw’ennyanja ekika kya whale ng’afunye ekyuma ekisooka, kifuuka ekitasobokera ddala omukubi wa harpoone, wadde ng’alinga omulabe mu ntambula ze, okumusuula ekyuma ekyokubiri mu ye.
