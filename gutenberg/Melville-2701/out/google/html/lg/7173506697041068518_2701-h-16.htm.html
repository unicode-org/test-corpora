ESSUULA 81. Pequod Asisinkana Bikira Maria.

Olunaku olwali lutegekeddwa lwatuuka, era mu ngeri entuufu twasisinkana emmeeri Jungfrau, Derick De Deer, master, ow’e Bremen.

Mu kiseera ekimu abantu abasinga okuvuba envubu mu nsi yonna, Abadaaki n’Abagirimaani kati be bamu ku basinga obutono; naye wano ne wali mu bbanga erigazi ennyo erya latitude ne longitude, oluusi n’oluusi okyasisinkana ne bendera yaabwe mu Pacific.

Olw’ensonga ezimu, Jungfrau yalabika ng’ayagala nnyo okumuwa ekitiibwa. Nga akyali wala okuva ku Pequod, yeetooloola okutuuka, n’asuula eryato, kapiteeni we yasindikibwa okututuukako, ng’ayimiridde mu maaso mu kifo ky’emabega w’eryato nga talina bugumiikiriza.

“Alina ki mu ngalo ze eyo?” Starbuck bwe yakaaba ng’asonga ku kintu Omugirimaani gwe yali akutte mu ngeri ey’okuwuuba. “Tekisoboka! —ekintu ekigabula ettaala!”

“Si ekyo,” Stubb bwe yagamba, “nedda, nedda, kiyungu kya kaawa, Mwami Starbuck; ava kutukolera kaawa waffe, ye Yarman; tolaba ekibbo ekyo ekinene eky’ebbaati awo ku mabbali ge?—ago ge mazzi ge agabuguma. Oh! ali bulungi, ye Yarman.”

“Genda naawe,” Flask bwe yakaaba, “kiliisa ttaala n’ekibbo ky’amafuta. Amafuta gamuweddeko, era azze a-sabiriza.”

Wadde nga kiyinza okulabika ng’ekyewuunyisa emmeeri ey’amafuta okwewola amafuta ku ttaka ly’ennyanja ekika kya whale, era ne bwe kiyinza okukontana mu ngeri ey’ekifuulannenge olugero olukadde olukwata ku kutwala amanda e Newcastle, naye oluusi ekintu ng’ekyo ddala kibaawo; era mu musango guno Captain Derick De Deer ddala yakola ekyuma ekigabula ettaala nga Flask bwe yalangirira.

Bwe yali alinnya ku ddeeke, Akabu n’amutuukako mu bwangu, nga tafuddeeyo n’akatono ku bye yalina mu ngalo ze; naye mu lingo ye eyamenyese, mu bbanga ttono Omugirimaani yalaga obutamanya bwe bwonna ku White Whale; amangu ago ng’akyusa emboozi n’agiteeka ku kifo kye eky’okuliisa ettaala n’ekibbo kye eky’amafuta, ng’ebigambo ebimu bikwata ku kuba nti alina okukyuka mu hammock ye ekiro mu kizikiza ekinene —ettondo lye erisembayo ery’amafuta ga Bremen nga liweddewo, era nga tewali n’ekyennyanja ekibuuka n’ekimu ekyakwatibwa okusobola okukola ku bbula; ng’amaliriza ng’alaga nti ddala emmeeri ye yali ekyo mu Buvubi kye kiyitibwa mu by’ekikugu ekiyonjo (kwe kugamba, ekyerere), nga kisaanidde bulungi erinnya lya Jungfrau oba Bikira Maria.

Ebyetaago bye bwe byamuweebwa, Derick n’agenda; naye yali tannafuna ludda lwa mmeeri ye, nga kumpi ennyanja ekika kya whale zisitulwa okuva ku mitwe gy’amaato gombi; era Derick yali ayagala nnyo okuyigga, ne kiba nti awatali kuyimirira kuteeka kibbo kye eky’amafuta n’ekirya ettaala ku lyato, n’asala okwetooloola eryato lye n’akola ng’agoberera eby’okuliisa ettaala ya leviathan.

Kati, omuzannyo bwe gwali gulinnye okutuuka ku bbugumu, ye n’amaato amalala asatu ag’Abagirimaani agaamugoberera mu bbanga ttono, baalina entandikwa nnyo ey’ebisenge bya Pequod. Waaliwo ennyanja ekika kya whale munaana, nga zino za kikuta kya wakati. Olw’okuba baali bamanyi akabi kaabwe, bonna baali bagenda nga bakwatagana ku sipiidi ey’amaanyi butereevu ng’empewo tennatandika, nga basikasika ku mabbali gaabwe okumpi ng’ebiwanvu by’embalaasi bingi bwe bityo ebiri mu bbugumu. Baalekawo okuzuukuka okunene, okugazi, nga balinga abasumulula buli kiseera olugoye olunene olugazi ku nnyanja.

Nga ejjudde mu kuzuukuka kuno okw’amangu, era nga mu bbanga eritali ddene emabega, yawuga ente ennume enkadde ennene ennyo, eyali efuukuuse, nga olw’okukulaakulana kwayo okwali kugeraageranyizibwa, awamu n’ebikuta ebya kyenvu ebitali bya bulijjo ebyali bimukula, yalabika ng’etawaanyizibwa obulwadde bwa jaundice, oba obunafu obulala. Oba whale eno yali ya pod nga bukyali, kyalabika nga kibuusibwabuusibwa; kubanga si kya mpisa nti ba leviathans ab’ekitiibwa ng’abo okubeera n’abantu n’akatono. Wadde kyali kityo, yanywerera ku kuzuukuka kwabwe, wadde nga ddala amazzi gaabwe ag’emabega gateekwa okuba nga gaamuddirira, kubanga eggumba eryeru oba okuzimba ku mumwa gwe omugazi kwali kwa bbugumu, ng’okuzimba okwakolebwa ng’emigga ebiri egy’obulabe gisisinkanye. Omusulo gwe gwali mumpi, nga gugenda mpola era nga gukola nnyo; okuvaayo n’ekika ky’amazzi agaziyira, ne yeemala mu bitundutundu ebikutuse, nga kigobererwa akajagalalo ak’ekyewuunyo ak’omu ttaka mu ye, akaali kalabika ng’akafuluma ku nkomerero ye endala eyaziikibwa, ne kireetera amazzi emabega we okuwuuma.

“Ani alina paregoric?” Stubb bwe yagamba, “alina obulumi bw’olubuto, ntya. Mukama, lowooza ku ky’okulumwa olubuto ekitundu kya yiika! Empewo embi zimukutte Ssekukkulu ey’eddalu, abalenzi. Ye mpewo envundu esoose gye nnali mmanyi okufuuwa okuva emabega; naye laba, did ever whale yaw so before? it must be, he’s lost his tiller.”

Ng’Omuyindi azitoowereddwa ng’asituka wansi ku lubalama lw’ennyanja Hindostan ng’alina omugugu gw’embalaasi ezitidde ku ddeeke, careens, buries, rolls, and wallows in her way; bwe kityo n’envubu eno enkadde n’esitula ekitundu kyayo ekinene ekyakaddiye, era kati n’oluvannyuma ekitundu n’ekyuka ku mbavu zaayo ezizibu, ne kibikkula ekivaako okuzuukuka kwe okw’obulimba mu kikonge ekitali kya butonde eky’ekiwaawaatiro kyayo eky’oku ddyo. Oba yali afiiriddwa ekiwaawaatiro ekyo mu lutalo, oba yazaalibwa nga tekirina, kyali kizibu okwogera.

“Lindako katono, chap omukadde, era nja kukuwa sling olw’omukono ogwo ogwali gufunye ebisago,” Flask omukambwe bwe yakaaba, ng’asonga ku layini y’ennyanja ekika kya whale okumpi naye.

“Mind takukuba na kyo,” Starbuck bwe yakaaba. “Muwe ekkubo, oba Omugirimaani ajja kumufuna.”

Mu kigendererwa kimu amaato gonna agaali gavuganya agagatta gaali gasonga ku kyennyanja kino ekimu, kubanga teyakoma ku kuba nti ye yali asinga obunene, n’olwekyo nga ye whale esinga okuba ey’omuwendo, naye yali asinga kubeera kumpi nazo, era whales endala zaali zigenda n’embiro ennene nnyo, n’ekirala, nga kumpi zisomooza okuyigga olw’ekiseera ekyo. Mu kiseera kino ebikondo bya Pequod byali bikubiddwa amasasi amaato asatu ag’e Girimaani agaasembayo okukka; naye okuva ku ntandikwa ennene gye yali afunye, eryato lya Derick lyali likyakulembera okuyigga, wadde nga buli kaseera kasembereranga abavuganya be ab’ebweru. Ekintu kyokka kye baali batya, kyali nti, okuva ku kuba nti yali amaze okumpi nnyo n’akabonero ke, yandisobozeseddwa okukuba ekyuma kye nga tebannasobola kumutuukira ddala n’okumuyita. Ate ye Derick, yalabika nga mukakafu nnyo nti kino kyandibadde bwe kityo, era oluusi n’oluusi n’akabonero ak’okusekerera yakankanya ekyuma kye eky’ettaala ku maato amalala.

“Embwa etali ya kisa era etasiima!” Starbuck bwe yakaaba; “ansekerera era angumiikiriza n’ekibokisi eky’obwavu ennyo kye nnamujjuza si ddakiika ttaano eziyise!” —olwo mu kuwuubaala kwe okw’amaanyi okw’edda —“Muwe ekkubo, ennyonyi ekika kya greyhounds! Embwa ku kyo!”

“Mbuulira kye kiri, abasajja” — Stubb bwe yakaaba eri abakozi be —“kimenya eddiini yange okugwa eddalu; naye nandyagadde okulya Yarman oyo omubi—Sisika—temuli? Mugenda kuleka omusajja oyo omufere okubakuba? Oyagala brandy? A hogshead of brandy, then, to the best man. Jjangu, lwaki abamu ku mmwe tebatulika ekibya ky’omusaayi? nti ebadde esuula ennanga ku nnyanja —tetuwuguka wadde yinsi yonna —tukkakkana, wuuno omuddo ogumera wansi w’eryato —era ku lwa Mukama, ekikondo ekyo tekijja kukola, abalenzi, Mutunuulire Yarman oyo, abasajja, munaafuuwa omuliro.

“O! laba ebisulo by’akola!” yakaaba Flask, ng’azina waggulu ne wansi —“Nga kiwujjo—Oh, kola entuumu ku nnyama y’ente—egalamira ng’omuti! Oh! ba lads bange, kola spring—slap-jacks ne quahogs for supper, omanyi, ba lads bange—baked clams and muffins—oh, do , do , spring,—he’s a hundred barreller—don’t lose him now—don’t oh, don’t! —laba ekyo Yarman—Oo, temugenda kusika duff yammwe, sogger bwetyo Temwagala nnyo ensigo!—bbanka yonna!—Oh, do , do , do!

Mu kaseera kano Derick yali mu kikolwa eky’okusuula ekintu kye eky’okuliisa ettaala ku maato agaali gagenda mu maaso, era n’ekibbo kye eky’amafuta; mpozzi n’endowooza ey’emirundi ebiri ey’okuzza emabega ekkubo ly’abavuganya be, ate mu kiseera kye kimu mu by’enfuna n’ayanguyiza eyiye olw’okukubirizibwa okw’akaseera katono okw’okusuula emabega.

“Omuyimbi Omudaaki atalina mpisa!” Stubb bwe yakaaba. “Sika kati, abasajja, ng’emigugu emitwalo ataano egy’ennyiriri z’emmeeri z’olutalo eza sitaani ez’enviiri emmyuufu. Kiki d’ye ky’oyogera, Tashtego; oli musajja okukutula omugongo gwo mu bitundu bibiri n’amakumi abiri olw’ekitiibwa kya Gayhead omukadde? What d’ye say?”

“Nze ngamba, sika nga katonda-dam,” — Omuyindi bwe yakaaba.

Mu bukambwe, naye nga zikubirizibwa kyenkanyi olw’okusekererwa kw’Omugirimaani, amaato asatu aga Pequod kati gaatandika okutambula kumpi nga gakwatagana; era, olw’okwagala bwe kityo, n’amusemberera akaseera katono. Mu ndowooza eyo ennungi, etali nnywevu, ey’embalaasi ey’omukulu w’omutwe bwe yali asemberera omuyiggo gwe, bannaabwe abasatu baayimirira n’amalala, oluusi ne bawagira omuvuzi w’amaato oluvannyuma n’okukaaba okusanyusa nti, “Eyo gy’aseerera, kati! Yanguwa olw’empewo ey’evvu eryeru! Wansi ne Yarman! Sail over him!”

Naye bwe kityo yasalawo entandikwa ey’olubereberye singa Derick yalina, nti wadde nga balina obuzira bwabwe bwonna, yandibadde akakasa nti ye muwanguzi mu mpaka zino, singa tewaali musango mutuukirivu gwamukka mu crab eyakwata ekiso ky’omuvuzi we ow’omu makkati g’emmeeri. Nga lubber eno etali nnungi efuba okusumulula evvu lye eryeru, era nga, mu kivaamu, eryato lya Derick lyali linaatera okugwa, era n’abwatuka n’agenda ku basajja be mu busungu obw’amaanyi;—ekyo kyali kiseera kirungi eri Starbuck, Stubb, ne Flask. Nga baleekaana, baatutte entandikwa ey’okufa mu maaso, era mu ngeri ey’okwekulukuunya ne bagenda waggulu ku kwata y’Omugirimaani. Akaseera akalala, era amaato gonna ana gaali mu diagonically mu whale’s immediate wake, ate nga gagoloddwa okuva ku go, ku njuyi zombi, we waali okuzimba okufuumuuka kwe yakola.

Kyali kifaananyi kya ntiisa, ekisinga okusaasira, era eddalu. Whale kati yali egenda omutwe, era ng’esindika ekifuba kyayo mu maaso ge mu nnyonyi eyali etulugunyizibwa buli kiseera; ate ekiwaawaatiro kye ekimu ekyavu kyakuba oludda lwe mu bulumi obw’okutya. Kati ku mukono guno, kati ku ogwo, yawuubaala mu nnyonyi ye eyali ewuguka, era nga akyali ku buli kiwujjo kye yamenya, mu ngeri ey’okusannyalala n’abbira mu nnyanja, oba ku mabbali n’ayiringisibwa ng’ayolekera eggulu ekiwaawaatiro kye ekikuba. Bwentyo ndabye ekinyonyi ekirina ekiwawaatiro ekisaliddwa nga kikola enzirugavu ezimenyese mu ngeri ey’entiisa mu bbanga, nga kifuba bwereere okutoloka mu nnyonyi z’ababbi. Naye ekinyonyi kirina eddoboozi, era n’okukaaba okw’okwemulugunya kijja kumanyisa okutya kwakyo; naye okutya kw’ensolo eno ennene ennyo ensirifu ey’ennyanja, kwasibibwa enjegere ne kulogebwa mu ye; teyalina ddoboozi, okuggyako okussa okwo okuziyira okuyita mu spiracle ye, era kino kyamufuula okusaasira mu ngeri etayinza kwogerwa; ate nga akyali, mu bungi bwe obw’ekyewuunyo, akawanga ke aka portcullis, n’omukira ogw’amaanyi gonna, waaliwo ebimala okutiisa omusajja asinga obugumu eyasaasira ennyo.

Okulaba kati nti naye akaseera katono nnyo okusingawo kwandiwadde amaato ga Pequod enkizo, era mu kifo ky’okulemesebwa bw’atyo omuzannyo gwe, Derick yasalawo okuteeka mu kabi ekyo gy’ali ekiteekwa okuba nga kyalabika ng’omuggo omuwanvu ennyo mu ngeri etaali ya bulijjo, ere omukisa ogusembayo tegunnabaawo emirembe gyonna.

Naye amangu ddala ng’omukubi w’ennanga ye yayimirira olw’okukubwa, enjovu zonsatule —Queequeg, Tashtego, Daggoo —mu butonde ne zibuuka ne ziyimirira, era nga ziyimiridde mu lunyiriri oluwanvu, mu kiseera kye kimu ne zisonga emiggo gyazo; era ne badduka ku mutwe gw’omuvuzi w’ennyonyi Omugirimaani, ebyuma byabwe ebisatu ebya Nantucket ne biyingira mu nnyanja ekika kya whale. Omukka oguziba amaaso ogw’ekifuumuuka n’omuliro ogweru! Amaato ago asatu, mu busungu obwasooka obw’okudduka kw’ennyanja ekika kya whale n’omutwe, gaakuba ag’Omugirimaani ku bbali n’amaanyi mangi nnyo, ne kiba nti bombi Derick n’omuvuzi we eyali asobeddwa ne bayiwa ebweru, ne basaabala okuyita ku biwujjo ebisatu ebyabuuka.

“Temutya, bbokisi zange eza butto,” Stubb bwe yakaaba, ng’abasuula amaaso agayitawo ng’akuba amasasi; “mwe’ll be picked up presently—all right—I saw some sharks astern—St. Bernard’s dogs, you know—relieve distressed travellers. Hurrah! eno y’engeri y’okusaabala kati. Buli keel a sunbeam! Hurrah!—Wano tugenda ng’ebibbo by’amabaati bisatu ku mukira gwa cougar eddalu!Kino kinteeka mu birowoozo by’okusiba enjovu mu a tilbury ku lusenyi —efuula emisinde gya nnamuziga okubuuka, abalenzi, bwe mumusiba mu ngeri eyo era nga waliwo akabi ak’okusuulibwa ebweru, eno y’engeri munno gy’awuliramu ng’agenda ewa Davy Jones —byona bya kudduka mu nnyonyi egenda okusereba!

Naye emisinde gy’ekisolo ekyo gyali gya kaseera katono. Ng’awa omukka ogw’amangu, yavuga mu ngeri ey’akajagalalo. Nga balina okudduka okw’okusiba, layini essatu zaabuuka okwetooloola emitwe gy’emiggo n’amaanyi mangi nnyo ne zikuba emiwaatwa emiwanvu mu zo; wadde ng’abavuzi b’engoye baali batya nnyo ne kiba nti okuwulikika kuno okw’amangu kwandikooye mu bbanga ttono layini, ne kiba nti nga bakozesa amaanyi gaabwe gonna ag’obukugu, baakwata enkyukakyuka eziddiŋŋana nga bafuuwa sigala n’omuguwa gwe baali bagenda okunywerera; okutuusa ku nkomerero —olw’okunyigirizibwa okw’okwesimbye okuva ku bikoola by’amaato ebyali biyimiriddeko omusulo, emiguwa esatu gye gyava wansi butereevu mu bbululu —ebikondo by’emmundu eby’obutaasa kumpi byali bikwatagana n’amazzi, ate ng’amagye asatu ag’emabega galengejja waggulu mu bbanga. Era mu bbanga ttono ennyanja ekika kya whale bwe yalekera awo okuwulikika, okumala ekiseera yasigala mu ndowooza eyo, nga batya okusaasaanya layini endala, wadde ng’ekifo kyali kiwunya katono. Naye newankubadde nga amaato gaggyiddwa wansi ne gabula mu ngeri eno, naye kino kye “kukwata,” nga bwe kiyitibwa; kino ng’akwatagana n’ebiwujjo ebisongovu eby’ennyama ye enamu okuva emabega; kino kye kitera okubonyaabonya Leviyasani mu bbanga ttono n’asituka nate okusisinkana effumu ery’amaanyi ery’abalabe be. Naye nga tetulina kwogera ku kabi k’ekintu ekyo, kibuusibwabuusibwa oba bulijjo ekkubo lino lye lisinga obulungi; kubanga kya magezi okulowooza, nti whale ekubiddwa gy’ekoma okumala wansi w’amazzi, gy’ekoma okukoowa. Kubanga, olw’obugulumivu bwe —mu sperm whale ekuze mu bujjuvu ekintu ekitasukka square feet 2000 — puleesa y’amazzi nnene nnyo. Ffenna tumanyi obuzito bw’empewo obw’ekyewuunyo ffe kennyini bwe tuyimiridde wansi; ne wano, waggulu w’ettaka, mu bbanga; n’olwekyo, omugugu gw’ennyanja ekika kya whale nga munene nnyo, ng’etisse ku mugongo gwe empagi ya ffuuti ebikumi bibiri ez’ennyanja! Kiteekwa waakiri okwenkana obuzito bw’empewo amakumi ataano. Omuvuzi w’ennyanja ekika kya whale omu akibalirira obuzito bw’emmeeri amakumi abiri ezitambula mu layini y’olutalo, nga zirina emmundu zazo zonna, n’amaduuka, n’abasajja abali ku mmeeri.

Nga amaato asatu gagalamidde awo ku nnyanja eyo eyali yeekulukuunya mpola, nga gatunudde wansi mu misana gyayo egya bbululu egy’olubeerera; era nga tewali kusinda wadde okukaaba okw’engeri yonna, nedda, nga n’amayengo oba ekiwujjo bwe kyava mu buziba bwakyo; nga omukugu mu by’ettaka yandirowoozezza, nti wansi w’okusirika okwo kwonna n’obutebenkevu, ekisolo ekisinga obunene eky’ennyanja kyali kiwuuma era nga kiwunya olw’obulumi! Tewali yinsi munaana ez’omuguwa ogw’okwesimbye ogwalabika ku butaasa. Kirabika kyesigika nti olw’obuwuzi busatu obugonvu bwe butyo Leviathan omukulu yali awaniriddwa ng’obuzito obunene ku ssaawa ey’ennaku munaana. Okuyimiriza? era ku ki? Ku bitundutundu bisatu eby’olubaawo. Kino kye kitonde kye kyayogerwako edda n’obuwanguzi bwe butyo —“Oyinza okujjuza olususu lwe ebyuma eby’emiguwa? oba omutwe gwe n’amafumu g’ebyennyanja? Ekitala ky’oyo amugalamidde tekiyinza kukwata, effumu, effumu, newakubadde enkovu: atwala ekyuma ng’essubi, akasaale tekayinza kumudduka, emisinde gibalibwa ng’ebisubi, ye aseka olw’okukankana kw’effumu!” Kino ekitonde? ono ye? Oh! nti obutatuukirira kugoberera bannabbi. Kubanga olw’amaanyi g’ebisambi lukumi mu mukira gwe, Leviathan yali adduse omutwe gwe wansi w’ensozi z’ennyanja, okumukweka amafumu g’ebyennyanja aga Pequod!

Mu musana ogwo ogw’emisana ogwali guserengese, ebisiikirize amaato asatu bye gaasindika wansi wansi w’amazzi, biteekwa okuba nga byali biwanvu ekimala era nga bigazi ekimala okusiikiriza ekitundu ky’eggye lya Zakisi. Ani ayinza okutegeera engeri ennyanja ekika kya whale eyali efunye ebisago gye yateekwa okuba nga yali ewunyiriza ennene ennyo bwe zityo ezaali ziwuguka ku mutwe gwe!

“Muyimirire awo, abasajja; asikasika,” Starbuck bwe yakaaba, nga layini essatu zikankana mu mazzi mu bwangu, nga ziyita mu ngeri ey’enjawulo waggulu gye ziri, nga bwe ziyita mu waya za magineeti, okukuba kw’obulamu n’okufa kw’ennyanja ekika kya whale, buli muvuzi w’amaato n’abiwulira mu ntebe ye. Akaseera akaddako, nga gawummudde ekitundu ekinene olw’okunyigirizibwa okukka wansi ku butaasa, amaato gaawa okubuuka okw’amangu waggulu, ng’ekifo ekitono eky’omuzira bwe kinaakola, ekisibo ekinene eky’eddubu eryeru bwe litiisibwa okuva mu kyo ne liyingira mu nnyanja.

“Haul in! Haul in!” Starbuck bwe yazzeemu okukaaba; “asituka.”

Ennyiriri, nga, mu kaseera katono emabega, nga tewali bugazi bwa mukono gumu, kati zaali mu koyilo empanvu ez’amangu ezaasuulibwa emabega zonna nga zitonnya mu maato, era mu bbanga ttono ennyanja ekika kya whale yamenya amazzi mu buwanvu bw’emmeeri bbiri okuva ku bayizzi.

Ebiteeso bye byali biraga bulungi nti yali akooye nnyo. Mu bisolo ebisinga obungi eby’oku lukalu wabaawo obuuma obumu oba emiryango gy’amataba mu misuwa gyabyo mingi, nga bwe buba bufunye ebisago, omusaayi mu ddaala eritali limu waakiri guggalwa mangu mu njuyi ezimu. Si bwe kiri ku nnyanja ekika kya whale; ekimu ku bintu eby’enjawulo kwe kuba n’ensengekera yonna etali ya valvuar ey’emisuwa, bwe kityo bwe kifumita wadde akatundu akatono ennyo ng’akawuka, okufulumya amazzi ag’okutta omulundi gumu kutandikibwawo ku nkola ye yonna ey’emisuwa; era kino bwe kyongera olw’okunyigirizibwa okw’ekitalo okw’amazzi agali ewala ennyo wansi w’amazzi, obulamu bwe buyinza okugambibwa nti bumuyiwa mu migga egitasalako. Naye obungi bw’omusaayi mu ye bunene nnyo, era ensulo zaayo ez’omunda ziri wala nnyo era nnyingi, ne kiba nti ajja kusigala ng’avaamu omusaayi n’okuvaamu omusaayi okumala ekiseera ekinene; ne mu kyeya omugga bwe gunaakulukuta, ng’ensibuko yaagwo eri mu nzizi z’obusozi obw’ewala era obutategeerekeka. Ne kati, amaato bwe gaasika ku nnyanja eno ey’ekika kya whale, ne gasika mu ngeri ey’akabi ku biwujjo bye ebiwuubaala, era n’amafumu ne gamufubutuka, ne gagobererwa ebiwujjo ebitali binywevu okuva mu kiwundu ekipya ekyakolebwa, ebyasigala nga bizannya buli kiseera, ate ng’ekinnya eky’obutonde eky’okufuuwa amazzi mu mutwe gwe kyali mu bbanga lyokka, wadde nga kya mangu, nga gasindika obunnyogovu bwayo obwali butiisa mu bbanga. Mu kifo kino ekisembayo tewaaliwo musaayi gwajja, kubanga n’okutuusa kati tewali kitundu kikulu kye kyali kikubiddwa. Obulamu bwe, nga bwe babuyita mu ngeri ey’amakulu, tebwakwatibwako.

Nga kati amaato geeyongera okumwetooloola, ekitundu kyonna eky’okungulu eky’ekifaananyi kye, nga kiriko ekitundu ekinene ekitera okunnyika mu mazzi, kyabikkulwa bulungi. Amaaso ge, oba okusingawo ebifo amaaso ge we gaali gatunuuliddwa. Ng’ebibinja ebyewuunyisa ebikula obubi bwe bikuŋŋaana mu binnya by’amafundo g’emiti gy’emivule egy’ekitiibwa ennyo nga givunnama, bwe kityo okuva ku nsonga amaaso g’ennyanja whale gye gaali gatudde, kati bbaatule ezifulumye ezizibe, ezisaasira mu ngeri ey’entiisa okulaba. Naye okusaasira tewaaliwo. Olw’obukadde bwe bwonna, n’omukono gwe ogumu, n’amaaso ge agazibe, alina okufa okufa n’attibwa, asobole okumulisiza abagole abagaala ebisiyaga n’ebintu ebirala ebisanyusa abasajja, era n’okumulisiza amakanisa ag’ekitiibwa agabuulira obutaliiko buzibu obutaliiko bukwakkulizo bwonna eri bonna. Nga akyayiringisibwa mu musaayi gwe, ku nkomerero yalaga ekitundu ku kibinja oba ekifulukwa ekyakyuse langi mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa, ekinene ng’ekibbo, wansi wansi ku mabbali.

“Ekifo ekirungi,” Flask bwe yakaaba; “muleke mmufumire awo omulundi gumu.”

“Avast!” Starbuck bwe yakaaba nti, “ekyo tekyetaagisa!”

Naye Starbuck ow’obuntubulamu yalwawo nnyo. Mu kaseera k’omusaale ennyonyi eyali efuuse amabwa yakuba amabwa okuva mu kiwundu kino eky’obukambwe, era n’egigoba mu bulumi obusukka mu kubonaabona, kati whale ng’efuuwa omusaayi omunene, n’obusungu obw’amangu bwadduka mu ngeri ey’amaaso ku nnyonyi eyo, n’ebafuuwa n’abakozi baabwe abaali bagulumiza wonna n’enkuba ey’amaanyi, n’ekuba eryato lya Flask n’okwonoona obutaasa. Yali stroke ye ey’okufa. Kubanga, mu kiseera kino, yali amaze nnyo olw’okufiirwa omusaayi, ne yeekulukuunya okuva ku mmeeri gye yali akoze nga talina ky’asobola kukola; yali agalamidde ng’assa omukka ku ludda lwe, ng’akuba enduulu mu ngeri etali ya maanyi n’ekiwawaatiro kye ekyali kikutte, olwo n’azingulula mpola mpola ng’ensi egenda ekendeera; yakyusa ebyama ebyeru eby’olubuto lwe; agalamidde ng’ekikondo, n’afa. Kyasinga kusaasira, ekifuba ekyo ekyasembayo okuggwaako. Nga amazzi bwe gaggyibwako mpolampola okuva mu nsulo ezimu ez’amaanyi, era n’okuwuuma okw’ennaku okuziyiziddwa ekitundu, empagi efuuyira ekka era n’ekka wansi —bwe kityo n’omusulo ogusembayo ogw’ennyanja ekika kya whale ogufa okumala ebbanga eddene.

Mu bbanga ttono, ng’abakozi bano balindirira okutuuka kw’emmeeri, omulambo gwalaga obubonero obw’okubbira ng’eby’obugagga byagwo byonna tebiriiko mmundu. Amangu ago, olw’ebiragiro bya Starbuck, layini zaaginyweza mu bifo eby’enjawulo, bwe kityo ere long buli lyato ne libeera buoy; ennyanja ekika kya whale eyabbira ng’ewaniriddwa yinsi ntono wansi waabwe olw’emiguwa. Mu nzirukanya ey’obwegendereza ennyo, emmeeri bwe yasemberera, ennyanja ekika kya whale yakyusibwa n’etwalibwa ku ludda lwayo, era n’enyweza nnyo eyo enjegere za fluke ezisinga okukaluba, kubanga kyali kyeyoleka lwatu nti okuggyako ng’ewaniriddwa mu ngeri ey’ekikugu, omulambo gwali gugenda kubbira omulundi gumu wansi.

Kyatuuka mu butanwa nnyo ne kiba nti kumpi bwe yasooka okumusala n’ekiso, obuwanvu bwonna obw’engoye eyali efuuse enfuufu bwasangibwa nga buyingidde mu nnyama ye, ku kitundu ekya wansi eky’ekibinja ekyayogerwako emabegako. Naye ng’ebikonge by’engo bwe bitera okusangibwa mu mirambo gy’ennyanja ekika kya whale eziwambibwa, ng’ennyama ewonye bulungi okugyetoolodde, era nga tewali kumanyika kwa ngeri yonna kulaga kifo kyazo; n’olwekyo, wateekwa okuba nga waaliwo ensonga endala etamanyiddwa mu musango guno okusobola okutegeeza mu bujjuvu amabwa agoogeddwako. Naye ekyasinga okwewuunyisa kwe kuba nti waliwo omutwe gw’effumu ogw’ejjinja ogwasangibwa mu ye, nga si wala nnyo n’ekyuma ekyali kiziikiddwa, ng’ennyama enywevu ddala ku kyo. Ani yali afubutuse effumu eryo ery’amayinja? Era ddi? Kiyinza okuba nga kyaddukiddwa abamu ku Nor' West Indian edda nga Amerika tennazuulibwa.

Ebyewuunyo ebirala ki ebiyinza okuba nga byanoonyezebwa okuva mu kabineti eno ey’ekitalo tewali kwogera. Naye okuyimirira okw’amangu kwateekebwawo ku bintu ebirala ebyazuulibwa, olw’emmeeri okusimbulwa mu ngeri etabangawo n’egenda ku mabbali okutuuka ku nnyanja, olw’omulambo okweyongera ennyo okubbira. Kyokka Starbuck eyalina enteekateeka y’emirimu, yagiwanirira okutuuka ku nkomerero; yagiwanirira n’obumalirivu nnyo, ddala, ne kiba nti mu bbanga eggwanvu emmeeri yandibadde ebbidde, singa yali ekyanywerera mu kusiba emikono n’omulambo; awo, ekiragiro bwe kyaweebwa okumenya okuva ku kyo, okunyigirizibwa okutatambula ku mitwe gy’embaawo kwe kwasibirwako enjegere za fluke n’emiguwa, ne kiba nti tekisoboka kuzisuula. Mu kiseera kino buli kimu mu Pequod kyali kiserengese. Okusomoka okutuuka ku ludda olulala olwa ddeeke kyali ng’okulinnya akasolya k’ennyumba akawanvu akaliko ebikondo. Emmeeri yasiinda era n’efuuwa omukka. Ebintu bingi ebyassibwako amasanga mu bigo bye n’ebiyumba bye byatandikibwa okuva mu bifo byabyo, olw’okusengulwa okutali kwa butonde. Mu bwereere emisinde gy’emikono n’enkookooma byaleetebwa okusitula ku njegere za fluke ezitatambula, okuzisengula okuva ku mitwe gy’embaawo; era nga kati ennyanja ekika kya whale yali etuuse wansi nnyo ne kiba nti enkomerero ezaali zinyweredde mu mazzi tezaasobola kusemberera n’akatono, so nga buli kaseera ttani zonna ez’obuzito zaali zirabika nga zeeyongera ku kitundu ekinene ekyali kibbira, era emmeeri yalabika ng’egenda kugenda waggulu.

“Mukwate, mukwate, si bwe kiri?” Stubb yakaaba eri omulambo, “temubeera mu sitaani ow’amangu bw’atyo okubbira! Olw’okubwatuka, abasajja, tulina okukola ekintu oba okugenda ku lw’ekyo. No use prying there; avast, I say with your handspikes, and run one of you for a prayer book and a pen-knife, and cut the big chains.”

“Ekiso? Aye, aye,” Queequeg bwe yakaaba, era bwe yakwata ekiso ekizito eky’omubazzi, yeesigamye okuva mu mulyango, era ekyuma okutuuka ku kyuma, n’atandika okusala enjegere za fluke ezisinga obunene. Naye emiggo mitono, egyajjudde ennimi z’omuliro, gyaweebwa, ng’okunyigirizibwa okuyitiridde kukosa ebisigadde. With a terrific snap, buli kisiba kyagenda nga kiwuguka; emmeeri yatereezebwa, omulambo ne gubbira.

Kati, okubbira kuno oluusi n’oluusi okuteewalika okw’ennyonyi ekika kya Sperm Whale eyaakattibwa kintu kya kwewuunya nnyo; era n’omuvubi yenna tannaba kugibalirira bulungi. Ebiseera ebisinga Sperm Whale enfu etengejja n’amaanyi amangi, ng’oludda oba olubuto lwayo luli waggulu nnyo waggulu w’amazzi. Singa envubu zokka ezabbira bwe zityo zaali bitonde bikadde, bitono, era eby’omutima ogumenyese, paadi zazo ez’amasavu gakendeera era amagumba gazo gonna ne gazitowa era nga galina endwadde z’enkizi; awo oyinza n’ensonga ezimu okukakasa nti okubbira kuno kuva ku maanyi ag’ekisikirize agatali ga bulijjo mu byennyanja ebibbira bwe bityo, ekiva ku butabeerawo buno obw’ekintu ekibuuka mu ye. Naye si bwe kiri. Ku whales ento, mu bulamu obw’oku ntikko, era nga zizimba n’ebirowoozo eby’ekitiibwa, nga zisaliddwako nga tezinnabaawo mu bbugumu ery’ebbugumu ne May w’obulamu, nga zirina amasavu gazo gonna agafuuwa amasavu agazikwatako; n’abazira bano ab’amaanyi era abayiiya oluusi babbira.

Kyokka, kigambibwa nti ensolo ekika kya Sperm Whale tetera nnyo kugwa ku kabenje kano okusinga ebika ebirala byonna. Awali ekimu eky’ekika ekyo ekikka, Right Whales amakumi abiri we zikola. Enjawulo eno mu kika kino awatali kubuusabuusa eyinza okuteekebwa mu ddaala eritali ntono ku bungi bw’amagumba mu Right Whale; blind ze ez’e Venetian zokka oluusi nga zizitowa ttani ezisukka mu emu; okuva ku buzito buno Sperm Whale ya ddembe ddala. Naye waliwo ebiseera, oluvannyuma lw’okumala essaawa nnyingi oba ennaku eziwera, ennyanja ekika kya whale eyabbira n’eddamu okusituka, ng’efuumuuka okusinga mu bulamu. Naye ensonga lwaki kino kikoleddwa yeeyoleka bulungi. Gaasi zikolebwa mu ye; azimba okutuuka ku bunene obw’ekitalo; afuuka ekika kya bbaatule y’ebisolo. Emmeeri eyali egenda mu layini y’olutalo yali tesobola kumukuuma wansi mu kiseera ekyo. Mu Shore Whaling, ku soundings, mu Bays of New Zealand, Right Whale bw’ewa akabonero k’okubbira, bamusiba buoys, nga balina emiguwa mingi; bwe batyo omulambo bwe guba guserengese, ne bamanya we bagunoonya nga gunaaba gulinnye nate.

Tewaayita bbanga ddene ng’omulambo gubbira, enduulu yawulirwa okuva ku mitwe gy’amaato ga Pequod, ng’elangirira nti Jungfrau yali ezzeemu okukka amaato gaayo; wadde ng’omusulo gwokka ogwalabika gwali gwa Fin-Back, ogw’ekika ky’ennyanja ekika kya whale ezitakwatibwa, olw’amaanyi gaayo agatali ga bulijjo ag’okuwuga. Wadde kiri kityo, ekifuba kya Fin-Back kifaanagana nnyo n’ekya Sperm Whale, ne kiba nti abavubi abatalina bukugu batera okukikyamulwa. Era ekivaamu Derick n’omugenyi we yenna kati baali mu kugoba n’obuzira ensolo eno etasemberera. Bikira Maria ng’ajjula byonna n’asaabala, n’agoberera ebiwujjo bye bina ebito, era bwe bityo byonna ne bibula wala okutuuka mu bbanga, nga bakyayigga mu buvumu, n’essuubi.

Oh! bangi ba Fin-Backs, ate bangi ba Dericks mukwano gwange.





ESSUULA 82. Ekitiibwa n’Ekitiibwa ky’Okuvuba Envubu.

Waliwo ebitongole ebimu nga mu bino okutabangula n’obwegendereza y’enkola entuufu.

Gy’okoma okubbira mu nsonga eno ey’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale, n’okusika okunoonyereza kwange okutuuka ku mutwe gwennyini ogw’ensulo yaayo gye nkoma okukwatibwako nnyo olw’ekitiibwa kyayo ekinene n’obukadde bwayo; era naddala bwe nsanga bakatonda abakulu bangi nnyo n’abazira, bannabbi ab’engeri zonna, mu ngeri emu oba endala abayiwako enjawulo, ntambuzibwa n’okufumiitiriza nti nze kennyini ndi wa, wadde naye nga wansi, mu kibiina ky’obwasseruganda ekimanyiddwa ennyo.

Omuzira Perseus, mutabani wa Jupiter, ye yali omuvuzi w’ennyanja ekika kya whale eyasooka; era eri ekitiibwa eky’olubeerera eky’okuyitibwa kwaffe kigambibwa, nti ennyanja ekika kya whale eyasooka okulumbibwa obwasseruganda bwaffe teyattibwa na kigendererwa kyonna kibi. Ezo zaali nnaku za knightly ez’omulimu gwaffe, nga twetikka emmundu zokka okuyamba abaali mu nnaku, so si kujjuza biyumba bya basajja. Buli omu amanyi emboozi ennungi eya Perseus ne Andromeda; engeri Andromeda omwagalwa, muwala wa kabaka, gye yasibirwa ku lwazi ku lubalama lw’ennyanja, era nga Leviathan ali mu kikolwa kyennyini eky’okumutwala, Perseus, omulangira w’abavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale, ng’agenda mu maaso n’obuvumu, n’akuba enkoba y’ekisolo ekyo, n’azaala n’awasa omuzaana oyo. Yali mulimu gwa by’emikono ogw’okusiimibwa, nga tegutera kutuukibwako bavubi ba harpoone abasinga obulungi mu kiseera kino; kubanga Leeviyasani ono yattibwa ku musaale ogwasooka ennyo. Era tewali muntu yenna abuusabuusa emboozi eno ey’Abaarki; kubanga mu Yopa ey’edda, kati Yaffa, ku lubalama lw’ennyanja Busuuli, mu emu ku yeekaalu z’Abakaafiiri, waaliwo okumala emyaka mingi amagumba amanene ennyo ag’ennyanja ekika kya whale, enfumo z’ekibuga n’abatuuze bonna ge baagamba nti ge magumba agafaanagana n’ekisolo ekinene Perseus kye yatta. Abaruumi bwe baatwala Yopa, amagumba ge gamu gaatwalibwa e Yitale mu buwanguzi. Ekisinga okulabika ng’ekisinga obumu era ekikulu mu mboozi eno, kiri nti: Yona mwe yasimbula okuva e Yopa.

Okufaananako n’akabenje ka Perseus ne Andromeda —mazima ddala, abamu bwe balowooza nti kaava mu ngeri etali butereevu —ye mboozi eyo emanyiddwa ennyo eya St. George n’Omusota; ekisota ki kye nkuuma nti kyali kiwuka ekiyitibwa whale; kubanga mu bitabo by’ebyafaayo bingi eby’edda ennyanja ekika kya whale n’ebisota bitabuddwatabuddwa wamu mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa, era emirundi mingi biyimiriddewo. Ezeekyeri agamba nti: “Oli ng’empologoma ey’amazzi, era ng’ekisota eky’omu nnyanja; wano, kitegeeza mu bulambulukufu ennyanja ekika kya whale; mu mazima, enkyusa za Baibuli ezimu zikozesa ekigambo ekyo kyennyini. Ng’oggyeeko ekyo, kyandiggye nnyo ku kitiibwa ky’okukola ennyo yalina St. George naye n’asisinkana ekisolo ekiseeyeeya eky’ensi, mu kifo ky’okukola olutalo n’ekisolo ekinene eky’omu buziba. Omusajja yenna ayinza okutta omusota, naye Perseus, St. George, Coffin bokka be balina omutima mu bo okutambula n’obuvumu okutuuka ku whale.

Ebifaananyi eby’omulembe eby’ekifo kino bireme kutubuzaabuza; kubanga newankubadde ekitonde ekyasisinkana omusajja oyo omuzira w’ennyanja ow’edda kikiikirirwa mu ngeri etategeerekeka nga kiringa griffin, era newankubadde ng’olutalo lulagibwa ku lukalu ate omutuukirivu ng’ali ku mbalaasi, naye nga tulowooza ku butamanya obunene obw’ebiseera ebyo, ng’engeri entuufu ey’ennyanja ekika kya whale temanyiddwa bayiiya; era bw’olowooza nti nga bwe kyali mu mbeera ya Perseus, ennyanja ekika kya St. George’s whale yandibadde yeekulukuunya okuva mu nnyanja ku bbiici; era nga tulowooza nti ensolo eyali yeebagalwa St. George yandiba nga yali seal ennene yokka, oba embalaasi y’ennyanja; nga bino byonna bikutte mu birowoozo, tekijja kulabika nga tekikwatagana n’akatono n’olugero olutukuvu n’ebifaananyi eby’edda eby’ekifo ekyo, okukwata kino kye bayita ekisota nga si mulala okuggyako Leviathan omukulu yennyini. Mu butuufu, bwe kiteekeddwa mu maaso g’amazima amakakali era agafumita, emboozi eno yonna ejja kutambula ng’ekifaananyi ekyo eky’ebyennyanja, ennyama, n’ebinyonyi eky’Abafirisuuti, erinnya lya Dagoni; eyasimbibwa mu maaso g’essanduuko ya Isiraeri, omutwe gw’embalaasi ye n’engalo ze zombi ne bimugwako, era ekikonge oba ekitundu kye eky’ebyennyanja kyokka ne kisigalawo. Bwe kityo, n’olwekyo, omu ku sitampu yaffe ey’ekitiibwa, wadde omuvuzi w’ennyanja whale, ye mukuumi wa Bungereza omukuumi; era olw’eddembe eddungi, ffe aba harpooneers ba Nantucket tusaanidde okuwandiisibwa mu order esinga ekitiibwa eya St. George. Era n’olwekyo, baleme kubaawo ba knights ba kampuni eyo ey’ekitiibwa (tewali n’omu ku bo, nnyiikirira okugamba, abaddeko na kakwate na whale ng’omuyambi waabwe omukulu), baleme kutunuulira Nantucketer n’okunyooma, okuva bwe kiri nti ne mu frocks zaffe ez’ebyoya by’endiga n’empale eziriko kolaasi tulina obuyinza nnyo okuyooyoota St. George okusinga bo.

Oba okukkiriza Hercules mu ffe oba nedda, ku nsonga eno nnasigala nga nbuusabuusa: kubanga wadde okusinziira ku nfumo z’Abayonaani, Crockett oyo ow’edda ne Kit Carson —omukozi oyo ow’amaanyi ow’okusanyuka ebikolwa ebirungi, yamira wansi n’asuulibwa waggulu ennyanja ekika kya whale; still, oba ekyo strictly kimufuula whaleman, ekyo kiyinza okuba nga kibuusibwabuusibwa. Tewali we kirabika nti mu butuufu yakubye ebyennyanja bye, okuggyako, ddala, okuva munda. Wadde kiri kityo, ayinza okutwalibwa ng’ekika ky’omuvuzi w’ennyanja ekika kya whale atali kyeyagalire; mu ngeri yonna whale yamukwata, singa teyakwata whale. Nze mmuwozaako lwa kimu ku bika byaffe.

Naye, ab’obuyinza abasinga okukontana, emboozi eno ey’Oluyonaani eya Hercules n’ennyanja ekika kya whale etwalibwa ng’evudde mu mboozi y’Olwebbulaniya ekyali ey’edda ennyo eya Yona n’ennyanja ekika kya whale; ne vice versâ; mazima ddala bafaanagana nnyo. Bwemba nga nneewozaako nti demi-god olwo, lwaki si nnabbi?

Era n’abazira, abatukuvu, bakatonda abatonotono, ne bannabbi bokka si be bakola omuzingo gwonna ogw’ekibiina kyaffe. Grand master waffe akyalina okutuumibwa erinnya; kubanga okufaananako bakabaka ab’obwakabaka ab’edda, tusanga amazzi g’omutwe ag’obwasseruganda bwaffe mu kintu kyonna ekitali kitono okusinga bakatonda abakulu bennyini. Emboozi eyo ey’ekitalo ey’omu buvanjuba kati ya kwegezaamu okuva mu Shaster, etuwa Vishnoo ow’entiisa, omu ku bantu abasatu mu bwakatonda bw’Abahindu; atuwa Vishnoo ono ow’obwakatonda yennyini ku lwa Mukama waffe;—Vishnoo, olw’okufuuka omubiri gwe ekkumi ogw’oku nsi, eyawula emirembe gyonna era n’atukuza ennyanja ekika kya whale. Brahma, oba Katonda wa Bakatonda, bw’agamba Shaster, bwe yasalawo okuddamu okutonda ensi oluvannyuma lw’ekimu ku kusasika kwayo buli luvannyuma lwa kiseera, yazaala Vishnoo, okukubiriza omulimu; naye Vedas, oba ebitabo eby’ekyama, okusoma kwabyo kwe kwandirabise nga tekyetaagisa nnyo eri Vishnoo nga tannatandika kutonda, era n’olwekyo nga kiteekwa okuba nga kyalimu ekintu mu ngeri y’okubuulirira okw’omugaso eri abavubuka abakubi b’ebifaananyi, Vedas zino zaali zigalamidde wansi mu mazzi; bwe kityo Vishnoo n’afuuka omubiri mu nnyanja ekika kya whale, era ng’awulikika wansi mu ye okutuuka mu buziba obw’enkomeredde, n’ataasa ebitabo ebitukuvu. Kati olwo ono Vishnoo teyabadde muvuzi wa nnyanja? n’omusajja eyeebagadde embalaasi bw’ayitibwa omuvuzi w’embalaasi?

Perseus, George Omutukuvu, Hercules, Yona, ne Vishnoo! waliwo member-roll gy'oli! Kiraabu ki okuggyako eya whaleman eyinza okugenda bwetyo?





ESSUULA 83. Yona Atwalibwa Mu byafaayo.

Yayogerwako ku mboozi ey’ebyafaayo eya Yona n’ennyanja ekika kya whale mu ssuula eyasooka. Kati abamu ku ba Nantucketers basinga buteesiga mboozi eno ey’ebyafaayo eya Yona ne whale. Naye ate waaliwo Abayonaani n’Abaruumi abamu abaali babuusabuusa, nga baawukana ku bakaafiiri abasodokisi ab’omulembe gwabwe, kyenkanyi baabuusabuusa emboozi ya Hercules ne whale, ne Arion ne dolphin; era naye okubuusabuusa kwabwe obulombolombo obwo tekyafuula nnono ezo one whit the less facts, for all that.

Ensonga enkulu eyavaako omuvubi omu omukadde ow’e Sag-Harbor okubuusabuusa emboozi y’Olwebbulaniya yali eno: —Yali alina emu ku Bayibuli ezo ez’omulembe ez’omulembe, nga ziyooyooteddwa n’ebipande eby’okwewuunya, ebitali bya ssaayansi; ekimu ku byo kyali kikiikirira envubu ya Yona ng’erina ebiwujjo bibiri mu mutwe gwe —ekintu eky’enjawulo ekituufu kyokka ku kika kya Leviathan (Right Whale, n’ebika by’omutendera ogwo), abavubi kwe balina enjogera eno nti, “Omuzingo gwa ssente gwandimuzibye”; okumira kwe kale katono nnyo. Naye, ku kino, eky’okuddamu kya Bishop Jebb eky’okusuubira kyetegefu. Tekyetaagisa, Omulabirizi bw’alaga nti Yona tumutwala ng’aziikiddwa mu lubuto lw’ennyanja ekika kya whale, wabula ng’asula mu kitundu ekimu eky’akamwa ke okumala akaseera. Era kino kirabika nga kya magezi ekimala mu Bishop omulungi. Kubanga ddala, akamwa ka Right Whale kandibadde kasuza emmeeza za whist-tables bbiri, era nga zituuza bulungi abazannyi bonna. Era kiyinzika okuba nga Yona yandiba nga yeesibye mu linnyo eririmu ekituli; naye, bw’olowooza omulundi ogw’okubiri, Right Whale terina mannyo.

Ensonga endala Sag-Harbor (yagenda n’erinnya eryo) gye yakubiriza olw’obutaba na kukkiriza mu nsonga eno eya nnabbi, yali kintu ekizibu ennyo nga kijuliza omulambo gwe ogwasibirwa n’omubisi gw’omu lubuto gwa whale. Naye okuwakanya kuno mu ngeri y’emu kugwa wansi, kubanga omukugu mu by’ennyonyola Omugirimaani alowooza nti Yona ateekwa okuba nga yaddukira mu mulambo ogwatengejja ogw’ennyanja ekika kya whale eyafudde —ne bwe baali abaserikale b’Abafaransa mu kampeyini y’e Russia bwe baafuula embalaasi zaabwe ezaafa weema, ne baziseeyeeya mu zo. Ng’oggyeeko ekyo, kiraguddwa abanyonyola abalala ku ssemazinga, nti Yona bwe yasuulibwa ku nnyanja okuva ku mmeeri ya Yopa, amangu ago yaddukira mu lyato eddala eryali okumpi awo, eryato erimu eryalina ennyanja ekika kya whale olw’omutwe gw’ekifaananyi; era, nnyinza okugattako, oboolyawo eyitibwa “The Whale,” ng’emirimu egimu egy’emikono bwe gibatizibwa ennaku zino “Shark,” “Gull,” “Eagle.” Era tewabaddewo baagala bavvuunuzi abayivu ababadde balowooza nti ennyanja ekika kya whale eyogerwako mu kitabo kya Yona yali etegeeza kyokka ekikuuma obulamu —ensawo y’empewo eyali efuukuuse —nnabbi oyo eyali mu katyabaga k’okusaanawo gye yawugirako, era bwe kityo n’awonyezebwa okuva mu kuzikirizibwa okw’amazzi. N’olwekyo, omwavu Sag-Harbor alabika nga worsted all round. Naye yalina ensonga endala eyamuleetera obutaba na kukkiriza. Kyali kino, bwe mba nzijukira bulungi: Yona yamira ennyanja ekika kya whale mu Nnyanja Meditereniyani, era oluvannyuma lw’ennaku ssatu n’asesebwa awalala mu lugendo lw’ennaku ssatu okuva e Nineeve, ekibuga ekiri ku mugga Tiguli, olugendo olusukka nnyo olw’ennaku ssatu okusomoka okuva ku kifo ekisinga okumpi ku lubalama lw’ennyanja Meditereniyani. Ekyo kiri kitya?

Naye tewaaliwo ngeri ndala ennyanja ekika kya whale gye yayinza okukka ku nnabbi mu bbanga eryo ettono okuva e Nineeve? Yee. Yandiba nga yamutambuza okwetoloola mu kkubo eriyita mu Cape of Good Hope. Naye obutayogera ku kuyita mu buwanvu bwonna obwa Mediterranean, n’okuyita okulala okulinnya mu Persian Gulf ne Red Sea, okuteebereza ng’okwo kwandibaddemu okwetooloola ddala Afirika yonna mu nnaku ssatu, nga tetulina kwogera ku mazzi ga Tigris, okumpi n’ekifo kya Nineeve, nga gaali mawanvu nnyo nga whale yonna eyinza okuwugamu olunaku nga bukyali kyandiggye ekitiibwa ky’okuzuula omutwe ogwo omunene okuva ku Bartholomew Diaz, eyakizuula erinnya, era bwe kityo ne kifuula ebyafaayo eby’omulembe guno okuba eby’obulimba.

Naye ensonga zino zonna ez’obusirusiru eza Sag-Harbor omukadde zaayoleka kwokka okwenyumiriza kwe okw’obusirusiru okw’ensonga —ekintu ekyasinga okunenya mu ye, ng’alaba nti yalina okuyiga okutono okuggyako bye yali alonze okuva mu njuba ne mu nnyanja. Nze ngamba nti kiraga amalala ge ag’obusirusiru, ag’obutatya Katonda, n’obujeemu bwe obw’omuzizo, obw’omusitaani eri bannaddiini abaweebwa ekitiibwa. Kubanga kabona w’Ekikatoliki Omupotugali, endowooza eno yennyini ey’okugenda kwa Yona e Nineeve ng’ayita mu Cape of Good Hope yaleetebwa mu maaso ng’akabonero ak’okukuza ekyamagero eky’awamu. Era bwe kityo bwe kyali. Ng’oggyeeko ekyo, n’okutuusa leero, Abatuluuki abamanyiddwa ennyo bakkiririza nnyo mu mboozi ya Yona ey’ebyafaayo. Era ebyasa nga bisatu emabega, omutambuze Omuzungu mu kitabo ekikadde ekya Harris’s Voyages, ayogera ku Muzikiti gw’Abatuluuki ogwazimbibwa okussa ekitiibwa mu Yona, Omuzikiti mwe gwali ettaala ey’ekyamagero eyayaka nga tewali mafuta gonna.





ESSUULA 84. Okukuba ebikonde (Pitchpoling).

Okusobola okuzidduka amangu era mu bwangu, ebisiki by’ebigaali bifukibwako amafuta; era olw’ekigendererwa kye kimu, abavubi abamu bakola omulimu ogufaananako n’ogwo ku lyato lyabwe; zisiiga amafuta wansi. Era tekibuusibwabuusibwa nti engeri enkola ng’eyo gy’etayinza kukola bulabe bwonna, eyinza obutaba na mugaso gwonna ogunyoomebwa; okulowooza nti amafuta n’amazzi bya bulabe; nti amafuta kintu ekisereba, era nti ekintu ekitunuulirwa kwe kuleetera eryato okusereba n’obuvumu. Queequeg yali akkiririza nnyo mu kufuka amafuta ku lyato lye, era lumu ku makya nga wayiseewo akaseera katono ng’emmeeri ya Girimaani eyitibwa Jungfrau ebula, yafuna obulumi obusukka ku bwa bulijjo mu mulimu ogwo; nga yeekulukuunya wansi wa wansi waayo, we yali ewaniridde ku mabbali, era ng’esika mu butabeera bulungi ng’alinga anoonya n’obunyiikivu okukakasa ekirime ky’enviiri okuva mu kiwalaata ky’ekyuma ekyo. Yalabika ng’akola mu kugondera presentiment emu entongole. Era tekyasigala nga tekituufu olw’ekintu ekyo.

Nga tunaatera okutuuka emisana ennyanja ekika kya whale zaakuzibwa; naye amangu ddala ng’emmeeri ekka gye bali, ne bakyuka ne badduka n’enkuba ey’amangu; ennyonyi etategekeddwa bulungi, nga ku bbaasi za Cleopatra okuva e Actium.

Wadde kyali kityo, amaato gaagoberedde, era eya Stubb ye yasinga. Olw’okufuba ennyo, Tashtego ku nkomerero yasobola okusimba ekyuma kimu; naye ennyanja ekika kya whale eyakubwa, nga tewulikika n’akatono, yakyagenda mu maaso n’okubuuka kwayo okw’okwebungulula, ng’eyongera okubuuka. Okunyigirizibwa ng’okwo okutasalako ku kyuma ekisimbibwa kulina okukiggyamu amangu oba oluvannyuma awatali kwewalika. Kyafuuka kikulu nnyo okukuba flying whale, oba okubeera omumativu okumufiirwa. Naye okusika eryato okutuuka ku mabbali ge kyali tekisoboka, yawuga mangu nnyo era n’obusungu. Olwo kiki ekyasigalawo?

Ku byuma byonna ebyewuunyisa n’obukodyo, ebyuma ebikozesebwa mu ngalo n’obukodyo obutabalika, omuvuzi w’ennyanja omukadde bw’atera okuwalirizibwa, tewali kisukka kakodyo ako akalungi n’effumu eriyitibwa pitchpoling. Ekitala ekitono, oba ekitala ekigazi, mu dduyiro waakyo yenna tekyewaanira ku kintu kyonna kiringa ekyo. Tekyetaagisa nnyo ng’olina ennyanja ekika kya whale edduka ennyo; ensonga yaayo enkulu n’ekintu ekigikwatako y’ebanga ery’ekitalo effumu empanvu lye liddukira mu butuufu okuva ku lyato erikankana n’amaanyi, erikankana, wansi w’okugenda mu maaso okuyitiridde. Ekyuma n’embaawo nga biriko, effumu lyonna lirina obuwanvu bwa ffuuti nga kkumi oba kkumi na bbiri; omuggo mutono nnyo okusinga ogw’ekyuma ekiyitibwa harpoon, era era gwa kintu ekiweweevu —omuti gwa payini. Kiteekebwako akaguwa akatono akayitibwa warp, akawanvu ennyo, nga kano kasobola okusimbulwa okudda ku mukono oluvannyuma lw’okudduka.

Naye nga tetunnagenda mu maaso, kikulu okwogera wano, nti wadde ng’engoye eyinza okukubwa mu ngeri y’emu n’emmundu, naye tekitera kukolebwa; era bwe kikolebwa, kikyali kitono nnyo okutuuka ku buwanguzi, olw’obuzito obunene n’obuwanvu obutono obw’engoye bw’ogeraageranya n’embazzi, mu butuufu ne bifuuka ebizibu eby’amaanyi. N’olwekyo, ng’ekintu eky’awamu, olina okusooka okutuuka ku sipiidi ku nnyanja ekika kya whale, nga tebannaba kujja mu nkola ya pitchpoling yonna.

Laba kati ku Stubb; omusajja okuva ku bunnyogovu bwe obw’okusesa, obugenderere n’okwenkanankana mu mbeera ez’amangu ennyo, yalina ebisaanyizo eby’enjawulo okusukkuluma mu kukuba ebikonde. Mutunuulire; ayimiridde nga yeegolodde mu bbuutu y’eryato eribuuka erisuuliddwa; ng’ezingiddwa mu bbugumu ery’ebyoya by’endiga, ennyanja ekika kya whale esika eri mu maaso ffuuti amakumi ana. Ng’akwata effumu empanvu mu ngeri etali ya maanyi, ng’atunula emirundi ebiri oba esatu mu buwanvu bwayo okulaba oba nga ligoloddwa ddala, Stubb mu ngeri ey’okufuuwa akuŋŋaanya koyilo ya warp mu mukono gumu, asobole okunyweza enkomerero yaayo ey’eddembe mu kukwata kwe, n’aleka endala nga teziziyiziddwa. Olwo ng’akutte effumu nga lijjudde mu maaso g’omusipi gwe ogw’ekiwato wakati, n’agutereeza ku nnyanja ekika kya whale; bwe, ng’amubikkako, n’anyigiriza buli kiseera enkomerero y’akabina mu ngalo ze, bw’atyo n’asitula ensonga okutuusa ng’ekyokulwanyisa kiyimiridde bulungi ku ngalo ze, ffuuti kkumi na ttaano mu bbanga. Akulowoozaako ng’omuntu akola ‘juggler’, ng’agerageranya omuggo omuwanvu ku kalevu ke. Akaseera akaddako n’okusikirizibwa okw’amangu, okutaliiko linnya, mu kisenge ekigulumivu eky’ekitalo ekyuma ekimasamasa kibuna ebanga erifuumuuka, era ne kikankana mu kifo ky’obulamu bw’ennyanja ekika kya whale. Mu kifo ky’amazzi agayakaayakana, kati afuuwa omusaayi omumyufu.

“Ekyo kyamugoba essuuka!” Stubb bwe yakaaba. "'Tis July's immortal Fourth; ensulo zonna zirina okudduka omwenge leero! Wandibadde kati, yali wiisiki ya Orleans enkadde, oba Ohio enkadde, oba Monongahela omukadde atayogerwako! Olwo, Tashtego, lad, I'd have you hold a canakin to the jet, and we'd drink round it! Yea, verily, hearts alive, we'd brew choice in the spread of his spout-hole eyo, era okuva mu punch-bowl eyo enamu quaff ebintu ebiramu.”

Enfunda n’enfunda ku mboozi ey’omuzannyo ng’eyo, omusasi ogw’ekikugu guddibwamu, effumu ne lidda eri mukama waalyo ng’ensowera enzirugavu ekwatiddwa mu kaguwa ak’obukugu. Whale eno erumwa ennyo egenda mu bbugumu lye; layini y’okusika ekendedde, era omukubi w’ensambaggere n’agwa emabega, n’azinga emikono gye, era mu kasirise atunuulira ekisolo ekyo ng’efa.





ESSUULA 85. Ensulo.

Nti okumala emyaka enkumi mukaaga —era tewali amanyi bukadde bwa myaka emeka emabega — envubu ennene zandibadde zifuuwa amazzi mu nnyanja yonna, era nga zimansira era nga zifuumuuka ensuku z’obuziba, nga bwe kiri ku biyungu bingi nnyo ebimansira oba ebifuuwa enfuufu; era nti okumala ebyasa ebimu emabega, enkumi n’enkumi z’abayizzi baali bandibadde kumpi n’ensulo y’ennyanja ekika kya whale, nga balaba bino ebimansira n’ebifuuwa —nti bino byonna birina okuba, era naye, nti okukka ku ddakiika eno ey’omukisa (eddakiika kkumi na ttaano ne kwata eziyise ku ssaawa emu ey’ekiro PM ey’olunaku luno olw’ekkumi n’omukaaga mu December, AD 1851), kyandibadde kikyali kizibu, oba okufuuwa kuno, oluvannyuma lw’ebyo byonna, ddala amazzi, oba tewali kirala okuggyako omukka —mazima ddala kino kintu kya kwetegereza.

N’olwekyo, katutunuulire ensonga eno, awamu n’ebintu ebimu ebinyuvu ebikwatagana. Buli omu akimanyi nti olw’obukuusa obw’enjawulo obw’ebinywa byabwe, ebika by’ebiwujjo okutwaliza awamu bissa empewo ekiseera kyonna egattibwa wamu n’ekintu mwe biwugira; n’olwekyo, ensolo ekika kya herring oba cod eyinza okuwangaala ekyasa, era nga teyimusa mutwe gwayo na mulundi gumu waggulu w’amazzi. Naye olw’ensengekera ye ey’omunda eriko akabonero emuwa amawuggwe aga bulijjo, okufaananako n’ag’omuntu, ennyanja ekika kya whale esobola okuwangaala ng’essa empewo etaliimu mu bbanga eriggule. Nolwekyo obwetaavu bw’okukyala kwe buli luvannyuma lwa kiseera mu nsi eya waggulu. Naye tasobola mu ddaala lyonna kussa mu kamwa ke, kubanga, mu ndowooza ye eya bulijjo, akamwa ka Sperm Whale kaziikibwa waakiri ffuuti munaana wansi w’amazzi; era ekisingawo, omudumu gwe ogw’empewo tegulina kakwate konna na kamwa ke. Nedda, assa ng’ayita mu spiracle ye yokka; era kino kiri waggulu ku mutwe gwe.

Bwemba ngamba, nti mu kitonde kyonna okussa mulimu gwokka oguteetaagisa mu bulamu, okutuusa lwe guggya mu mpewo ekintu ekimu, oluvannyuma bwe kiyingizibwa mu musaayi ne kigabira omusaayi omusingi gwagwo ogulamu, sirowooza nti nja kusobya; wadde nga nnyinza okukozesa ebigambo ebimu ebya ssaayansi ebisukkiridde. Kitwale, era kivaamu nti singa omusaayi gwonna mu musajja gusobola okufuuwa empewo n’omukka gumu, olwo ayinza okusiba ennyindo ze n’ataleeta ndala okumala ekiseera ekiwanvu. Kwe kugamba, olwo yandibadde mulamu nga tassa. Wadde nga kiyinza okulabika ng’ekitali kya bulijjo, kino bwe kiri ddala ku nnyanja ekika kya whale, ewangaala mu ngeri entegeke, mu biseera ebitali bimu, essaawa ye enzijuvu n’okusingawo (bwe kiba wansi) nga tassa mukka wadde, oba nnyo nga mu ngeri yonna essa akatundu k’empewo; kubanga, jjukira nti talina gills. Kino kiri kitya? Wakati w’embavu ze ne ku buli ludda lw’omugongo gwe aweebwa ekiwujjo ekyewuunyisa eky’e Kuleete ekizingirwamu eky’emisuwa egy’ekika kya vermicelli, emisuwa egyo, bw’ava ku ngulu, gigaziwa ddala n’omusaayi ogulimu omukka gwa oxygen. N’olwekyo okumala essaawa emu oba okusingawo, ffuuti lukumi mu nnyanja, atambuza amaanyi agasukkiridde mu ye, ng’engamiya esala eddungu eritaliiko mazzi bw’etwala ekyokunywa ekisukkiridde okukozesebwa mu biseera eby’omu maaso mu lubuto lwayo oluna olw’okugatta. Ensonga y’ensengekera y’omubiri (anatomical fact) ya labyrinth eno tewali kuwakana; era nti endowooza ekisimbiddwako ya magezi era ya mazima, kirabika gye nsinga okumatiza, bwe ndowooza ku bukakanyavu obutannyonnyolwa obwa leviathan oyo mu kuba n’ebiwujjo bye ebifuluma , ng’abavubi bwe babigamba. Kino kye ntegeeza. Singa tetulugunyizibwa, bw’eneesituka ku mazzi, Sperm Whale ejja kusigala eyo okumala ekiseera nga yeefaananyirizaako ddala n’okusituka kwe okulala kwonna okutatulugunyizibwa. Gamba nti asigala eddakiika kkumi n’emu, n’akuba enduulu emirundi nsanvu, kwe kugamba, n’assa omukka nsanvu; olwo buli lw’anaddamu okusituka, ajja kukakasa nti azzeemu omukka gwe ensanvu, okutuuka ku ddakiika emu. Kati, singa oluvannyuma lw’okuleeta omukka mitono n’omulabula, n’akuba enduulu, bulijjo ajja kuba azzeemu okwewala okutereeza empewo ye eya bulijjo. Era si okutuusa ng’omukka ogwo nsanvu gutegeezeddwa, ajja kusembayo okukka okusigala ebweru ekisanja kye ekijjuvu wansi. Kyokka weetegereze nti mu bantu ab’enjawulo emiwendo gino gya njawulo; naye mu muntu yenna bafaanagana. Kati, lwaki bwe kityo ennyanja ekika kya whale yandibadde enywerera ku kuba n’ebiwujjo byayo bifulumye, okuggyako nga yali ya kujjuza tterekero lyayo ery’empewo, nga tennakka olw’obulungi? Era nga kyeyoleka bulungi nti obwetaavu buno obw’okusituka kw’ennyanja ekika kya whale bumuteeka mu bulabe bwonna obw’okufa olw’okuyigga. Kubanga si mu kkoolaasi oba katimba nti leviathan eno ennene yali esobola kukwatibwa, bwe yali esaabala ffuuti lukumi wansi w’omusana. Si nnyo bukugu bwo, n’olwekyo, ggwe omuyizzi, ng’ebyetaago ebinene ebikukuba obuwanguzi!

Mu muntu, okussa kugenda mu maaso obutasalako —omukka gumu guweereza okukuba emirundi ebiri oba esatu gyokka; bwe kityo bizinensi endala yonna gy’alina okukolako, ng’azuukuse oba ng’asula, okussa alina, oba okufa ajja. Naye Sperm Whale essa nga kimu kya musanvu oba Ssande yokka mu kiseera kye.

Kigambibwa nti ennyanja eno essa omukka ng’eyita mu kinnya kyayo ekiyitibwa spout-hole yokka; singa mu mazima kiyinza okugattibwako nti ebifulukwa bye bitabuddwamu amazzi, olwo ndowooza nti tusaanidde okuweebwayo ensonga lwaki okuwunyiriza kwe kulabika nga kuzikirizibwa mu ye; kubanga ekintu kyokka ekimukwatako ekiddamu n’akatono ennyindo ye, kye kinnya ekyo ekifaanagana; era olw’okuba kyali kizibiddwa nnyo ebintu bibiri, tekyayinza kusuubirwa kuba na maanyi ga kuwunyiriza. Naye olw’ekyama ky’ekifuba —ka kibeere mazzi oba omukka —tewali bukakafu bwonna buyinza kutuukirizibwa ku mutwe guno. Wadde kiri kityo, kikakafu nti Sperm Whale terina biwunyiriza bituufu. Naye kiki ky’ayagala ku bo? Tewali roses, tewali violets, tewali Cologne-amazzi mu nnyanja.

Ate era, ng’omudumu gwe ogw’empewo bwe guggukira gwokka mu ttanka y’omwala gwe ogufuuwa amazzi, era ng’omwala ogwo omuwanvu —nga omwala omunene ogwa Erie —guteekeddwamu ekika ky’ebizibiti (ebigguka n’okuggalawo) okusobola okukuuma empewo wansi oba okuggya amazzi waggulu, n’olwekyo ennyanja ekika kya whale terina ddoboozi; okuggyako ng’omuvuma ng’ogamba nti, bw’awuuma mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa bw’atyo, ayogera ng’ayita mu nnyindo. Naye ate, kiki ekiyitibwa whale ky’egamba? Sitera kumanya kitonde kyonna kizito ekyalina ky’ayogera eri ensi eno, okuggyako nga kiwaliriziddwa okusiba ekintu mu ngeri y’okubeezaawo obulamu. Oh! musanyufu nti ensi muwuliriza mulungi nnyo!

Kati, omukutu ogufulumya amazzi ogwa Sperm Whale, okusinga nga gugendereddwamu nga bwe guli ogw’okutambuza empewo, era nga gwa ffuuti eziwerako nga gugalamiddwa, mu bbanga, wansi ddala w’omutwe gwe ogwa waggulu, era katono ku ludda olumu; omwala guno ogw’okwewuunya gufaananako nnyo payipu ya ggaasi eteekeddwa wansi mu kibuga ku ludda olumu olw’oluguudo. Naye ekibuuzo kidda oba payipu ya ggaasi eno nayo payipu ya mazzi; mu ngeri endala, oba omukka ogufuluma mu Sperm Whale gwe mukka gwokka ogw’omukka ogufulumiziddwa, oba omukka ogwo ogufulumiziddwa gutabulwa n’amazzi agayingizibwa mu kamwa, ne gafulumizibwa nga gayita mu spiracle. Kikakafu nti akamwa kawuliziganya mu ngeri etali butereevu n’omukutu ogufulumya amazzi; naye tekiyinza kukakasibwa nti kino kigendereddwamu kufulumya mazzi nga gayita mu spiracle. Kubanga obwetaavu obusinga obunene obw’okukola ekyo bwandirabise nti, so nga mu kuliisa mu butanwa anywa amazzi. Naye emmere ya Sperm Whale eri wala wansi w’amazzi, era eyo tasobola kufuuwa ne bw’aba yandifudde. Ng’oggyeeko ekyo, bw’omutunuulira ennyo, n’omussaako essaawa n’essaawa yo, ojja kukizuula nti bw’atatulugunyizibwa, waliwo ennyiriri ezitakyuka wakati w’ebiseera by’ennyonyi ze n’ebiseera ebya bulijjo eby’okussa.
