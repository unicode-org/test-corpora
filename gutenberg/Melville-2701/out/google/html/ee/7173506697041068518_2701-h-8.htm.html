Eye menye nusi mebɔ kura hã o, be le esi teƒe be yemaka ɖe eƒe dzedze na tɔmelãwo léle ƒe mɔzɔzɔ bubu dzi o, le dzesi doviviti mawo ta la, ƒukpo ma si me nunya le dzi me tɔwo bua akɔnta la dina be yewoalé dadaa ɖe te, ale be le susu mawo tututu ta la, edze nyuie wu eye wòɖoe ɖe ƒua dzi, hena nusi yɔ fũ kple dziku kple gbemelãwo abe tɔmelãwo didi si me ʋu le ene. Woƒoe le ememe eye wotɔ dzoe le egodo, kple susu aɖe si womate ŋu ada o ƒe asike siwo me mekɔ o, siwo medzudzɔna o; ame ma tɔgbe, ne woate ŋu ake ɖe eŋu la, adze abe ŋutsua ŋutɔe atsɔ eƒe gayibɔ aƒu gbe ahakɔ eƒe akplɔ ɖe lã wɔadã siwo dzi ŋɔ wu ɖesiaɖe ŋu ene. Alo, ne le susu aɖe ta wosusu be ŋutilã me ŋutete mele eŋu na ema o la, ke hã adze abe ame ma tɔgbe si de ŋgɔ wu be wòado dzidzɔ na eƒe teviwo ahado ɣli ɖe amedzidzedzea ta ene. Gake aleke kee esiawo katã ɖale o, kakaɖedzi li be esi eƒe dziku si nu metsina o ƒe nya ɣaɣla si nye tagbɔsesẽ la tsɔ ga ƒoe eye wòtsɔ safui de eme ta la, Axab ɖoe koŋ ɖo tɔdziʋu yi mɔzɔzɔ si li fifia dzi kple nu ɖeka kolia si xɔa nusianu si nye White Whale la didi. Ne ɖe eƒe ame nyanyɛ xoxoawo dometɔ aɖe nɔ ƒuta gake afã ku drɔ̃e le nusi nɔ bebem ɖe eme ɣemaɣi ŋu la, anye ne eteƒe madidi o woƒe luʋɔ siwo ƒe mo wɔ yaa eye wole dzɔdzɔe la axɔ melia le gbɔgbɔ vɔ̃ ma tɔgbe si si! Woɖoe kplikpaa be yewoazɔ ƒudzimɔzɔzɔ siwo me viɖe le, eye woaxlẽ viɖe si woakpɔ la ɖe anyi le dɔlar me tso gakuƒea. Eɖoe be yeabia hlɔ̃ dzinɔameƒotɔe, si womate ŋu atrɔ asi le o, eye wògbɔ dzɔdzɔmetɔ ŋu.

Eyata afisiae ame tsitsi sia si ƒe ta biã, mavɔ̃mawu, nɔ Hiob ƒe tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale yome kple fiƒodewo le xexeame godoo, le ʋua me dɔwɔlawo ƒe ta me, hã, si ƒe akpa gãtɔ nye mongrel aglãdzelawo, kple ame siwo ƒu gbe, kple ameɖulawo—siwo gbɔdzɔ le agbenyuinɔnɔ gome hã, le nɔnɔme nyui alo susu nyui dzro aɖe ko ƒe ŋutetemanɔamesi le Starbuck, amesi ŋu womate ŋu awɔ nuvevii o ta ɖekematsɔleme kple ŋumaɖɔɖo ƒe dzidzɔkpɔkpɔ le Stubb me, kple ame tsɛ si xɔ aƒe ɖe Flask me. Edze abe dzomavɔ me ku aɖewo tia ʋua me dɔwɔla ma tɔgbe, si ƒe kpovitɔwo alea gbegbe, etɔxɛe eye wobla wo nu ƒu be woakpe ɖe eŋu wòabia hlɔ̃e si nye ame ɖeka kolia ene. Aleke wòdzɔe nye si wònye be wowɔ nu ɖe ​​ame tsitsia ƒe dziku ŋu ale gbegbe—akunya vɔ̃ɖi ka gbegbee nye si woƒe luʋɔwo xɔ, ale gbegbe be ɣeaɖewoɣi la, eƒe fuléle dzena abe wo tɔ kloe ene; White Whale la abe woƒe futɔ si womate ŋu ado dzi atsɔ o ene abe etɔ ene; alesi nusiawo katã va dzɔe—nusi White Whale nye na wo, alo alesi woƒe gɔmesese siwo womenya o nu, hã, le mɔ aɖe si me mekɔ o, si ŋu womesusui o nu la, ate ŋu adze abe agbe ƒe atsiaƒuwo ƒe gbɔgbɔ vɔ̃ gã si le tsatsam ene,—ne esiawo katã me ɖeɖe la, anye be wòage ɖe tsi me ayi goglo wu alesi Ismael ate ŋu ayi. Tomenukula si le anyigba te si wɔa dɔ le mí katã me la, aleke ame ate ŋu anya afisi wòkplɔa eƒe ʋuƒoa yina to eƒe pick ƒe gbeɖiɖi si le tɔtrɔm ɣesiaɣi, si me mekɔ o me? Amekae mesea alɔnu si womate ŋu axe mɔ na o la ƒe hehe o? Tɔdziʋu kae le ʋu blaadre vɔ ene hem ate ŋu anɔ tsitrenu? Le ɖeka gome la, metsɔ ɖokuinye na be magblẽ ɣeyiɣia kple teƒea ɖi; gake togbɔ be wo katã wole du dzi be yewoado go tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale hã la, womete ŋu kpɔ naneke le lã wɔadã ma me o negbe dɔnɔ si wua ame wu.





TA 42. Whale ƒe Yevunyenye.

Nusi ƒumelã ɣi la nye na Axab, woɖee fia; nusi wònye nam ɣeaɖewoɣi, gake womegblɔe haɖe o.

Le susu mawo siwo dze ƒã wu siwo ka Moby Dick ŋu, siwo mete ŋu nyɔa ŋɔdzinu aɖe le ŋutsu aɖeke ƒe luʋɔ me ɣeaɖewoɣi o gome la, susu bubu aɖe, alo ne míagblɔe tututu la, ŋɔdzinu bubu aɖe si me mekɔ o, si ŋkɔ mele o, nɔ eŋu, si ɣeaɖewoɣi to eƒe sesẽme ɖua ame mamlɛawo katã dzi keŋkeŋ; eye ke hã enye nya ɣaɣla eye womate ŋu agblɔe o kloe, ale gbegbe be mɔkpɔkpɔ bu ɖem kloe be matsɔe ade nɔnɔme si gɔme woate ŋu ase me. Whale ƒe ɣiee ƒo nuwo katã ta la, ewɔ nuku nam ŋutɔ. Gake aleke makpɔ mɔ be maɖe ɖokuinye me le afisia; eye ke hã, le mɔ aɖe si me mekɔ o, si le vome nu la, ɖe eme na ɖokuinye be ele be maɖe eme, ne menye nenema o la, ta siawo katã ate ŋu anye dzodzro.

Togbɔ be le dzɔdzɔmenu geɖe me la, ɣie doa atsyɔ̃ɖoɖo ɖe ŋgɔ le mɔ si kɔa nu ŋu, abe ɖe wònaa nɔnɔme nyui tɔxɛ aɖe si le eɖokui si ene, abe alesi wòle le kpe xɔasi siwo nye marble, japonica, kple dzonu me ene; eye togbɔ be dukɔ vovovowo kpɔ fia ƒe ŋgɔyiyi aɖe dze sii le mɔ aɖe nu le amadede sia me hã la; Pegu-fia xoxo gã siwo nye ame manyanuwo gɔ̃ hã tsɔ dzesideŋkɔ si nye “Akpɔkplɔ Yeyewo ƒe Aƒetɔ” ɖo woƒe dziɖuɖu ŋuti nya bubu siwo katã wogblɔna le mɔ wɔnuku aɖe nu la ta; eye egbegbe Siam-fiawo le afɔ ene ma ke si le abe sno ene la ɖem ɖe go le fia ƒe aflaga me; kple Hanovertɔwo ƒe aflaga si dzi charger si ƒe amadede nye sno ƒe nɔnɔmetata ɖeka le; kple Austria Fiaɖuƒe gã, Cæsarian, si nye Roma si ɖu dzi ƒe domenyila, si ƒe amadede nye fiaɖuƒe ƒe amadede ma ke; eye togbɔ be ŋgɔyiyi sia si le eme la ku ɖe ameƒomea ŋutɔ ŋu hã la, si na yevu ɖua aƒetɔ nyuitɔ kekeake ɖe to ɖesiaɖe si do viviti dzi; eye togbɔ be, hekpe ɖe esiawo katã ŋu hã la, woɖe dzidzɔkpɔkpɔ gɔ̃ hã ɖe yevute ŋu, elabena le Romatɔwo dome la, kpe ɣi aɖe de dzesi dzidzɔŋkeke; eye togbɔ be le veveseseɖeamenu kple kpɔɖeŋunyagbɔgblɔ bubu siwo kuna me la, wowɔa amadede sia ke wònyea nu geɖe siwo wɔa dɔ ɖe ame dzi, siwo ŋu bubu le ƒe dzesi hã—ŋugbetɔsrɔ̃wo ƒe fɔmaɖimaɖi, tsitsi ƒe dɔmenyonyo; togbɔ be le Amerika Ŋutsu Dzĩwo dome la, wampum ƒe alidziblanu ɣi nanae nye bubu ƒe adzɔgbeɖeɖe detotɔ kekeake hã; togbɔ be le yame ƒe nɔnɔme geɖe me la, yevunyenye nyea Dzɔdzɔenyenye ƒe gãnyenye ƒe kpɔɖeŋu le Ʋɔnudrɔ̃la ƒe ermine me hã, eye wòkpena ɖe fiawo kple fianyɔnu siwo sɔ siwo le abe notsi ene hea wo gbesiagbe ƒe nɔnɔme me hã; togbɔ be le subɔsubɔha siwo ŋu bubu le wu ƒe nya ɣaɣla kɔkɔwo gɔ̃ hã me la, wowɔe wònye Mawu ƒe ɖiƒoƒo kple ŋusẽ ƒe dzesi hã; Persiatɔwo ƒe dzosubɔlawoe léa dzo ɣi si wotsɔ fɔkpa wɔe la ɖe kɔkɔetɔ kekeake le vɔsamlekpuia dzi; eye le Helatɔwo ƒe gliwo me la, wowɔ Jove Gãtɔ ŋutɔ wòzu ŋutilã le nyitsu si ƒe amadede nye sno me; eye togbɔ be le Iroquoitɔ bubumewo gome la, Avũ Yeye kɔkɔe la ƒe dzomeŋɔli domedome vɔsa nye woƒe mawunyaŋununya ƒe azã kɔkɔetɔ kekeake sã hã la, esi wolé nuwɔwɔ ma si ŋu ɖiƒoƒo aɖeke mele o, si nye nuteƒewɔla la ɖe dɔla dzadzɛtɔ kekeake si woate ŋu aɖo ɖe Gbɔgbɔ Gã la gbɔ kple woawo ŋutɔ ƒe nuteƒewɔwɔ ŋuti ƒe sia ƒe nyadzɔdzɔwo ta; eye togbɔ be Latingbe me nya si gɔmee nye yevu me tso tẽ hã la, Kristotɔ nunɔlawo katã xɔ ŋkɔ na woƒe awu kɔkɔe la ƒe akpa ɖeka, si nye alb alo awudziwui, si wodona le cassock te; eye togbɔ be le Romatɔwo ƒe xɔse ƒe atsyɔ̃ɖoɖo kɔkɔewo dome hã la, wozãa yevu etɔxɛe le mía Aƒetɔ ƒe Fukpekpe ƒe azãɖuɖu me; togbɔ be le Yohanes Kɔkɔe ƒe Ŋutega me la, wotsɔ awu ɣiwo na amesiwo woɖe la, eye hamemegã blaeve vɔ eneawo tsi tre ɖe awu ɣiwo me le fiazikpui ɣi gã la ŋkume, kple Kɔkɔetɔ si bɔbɔ nɔ afima ɣi abe alẽfu ene; ke hã le hadede siawo katã siwo woƒo ƒu ta, kple nusianu si vivina, eye bubu le eŋu, eye wòkɔ wu la, nane si womate ŋu akpɔ o gakpɔtɔ le amadede sia ƒe susu ememetɔ me, si naa vɔvɔ̃ ɖoa luʋɔ la wu dzodzro ma si doa vɔvɔ̃ na ame le ʋu me.

Nɔnɔme sia si womate ŋu akpɔ o ye wònye, si nana yevuwo ƒe susu, ne woɖe wo nɔewo ɖa tso hadede siwo me dɔmenyonyo le wu gbɔ, eye wotsɔe kpe ɖe nu ɖesiaɖe si dzi ŋɔ le eɖokui si ŋu la, doa ŋɔdzinu ma ɖe ŋgɔ wòdea didiƒe wu. Kpɔ dzata ɣi si le atikpowo dzi, kple ƒumelã ɣi si le teƒe xɔdzowo la ɖa; nukae negbe woƒe ɣie si le gbadzaa eye wòle ƒuƒuie la na wozu ŋɔdzinu siwo de ŋgɔ wu ɖesiaɖe si wonye? Yevunyenye dziŋɔ mae naa tufafa nyɔŋu sia tɔgbe, si nyɔ ŋu wu dziŋɔ gɔ̃ hã, naa woƒe akpa ƒe dzidzɔkpɔkpɔ mumu la. Ale be menye amegãxi si ƒe asike sẽ le eƒe heraldic-wu me ate ŋu aʋuʋu dzinɔameƒo abe dzata alo ƒumelã ɣi si wotsɔ atsyɔ nu ɣie ene o.*

*Le Polar bear ŋu la, ɖewohĩ amesi alɔ̃ be yeayi nya sia me ayi ŋgɔe la aƒoe ɖe enu, be menye yevunyenye, si wobu ɖe vovo, ye doa lã wɔadã ma ƒe ŋunyɔnu si womate ŋu ado dzi atsɔ o la ɖe dzi o; elabena ne wodzro eme la, woate ŋu agblɔ be ɖeko ŋunyɔnu ma si dzi ɖe edzi la dona tso nɔnɔmea me, be nuwɔwɔa ƒe ŋutasẽnuwɔwɔ si me agbanɔamedzi mele o la tsi tre ɖe dziƒo fɔmaɖimaɖi kple lɔlɔ̃ ƒe lãgbalẽ me; eye le esia ta, to seselelãme eve siwo tsi tre ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu alea ƒoƒo ƒu ɖe míaƒe susu me me la, Polar-dzata la doa vɔvɔ̃ na mí kple vovototo si mele dzɔdzɔme nu o ale gbegbe. Gake ne míetsɔe be nusiawo katã nye nyateƒe gɔ̃ hã; ke hã, ne menye yevuwo tae o la, anye ne ŋɔdzinu ma si nu sẽ ɖe edzi manɔ asiwò o.

Le ƒumelã ɣia gome la, gbɔɖeme ƒe gbɔgbɔ ɣi si le dzodzom le nuwɔwɔ ma me, ne wokpɔe le eƒe seselelãme dzrowo me la, ewɔa nuku ŋutɔ be esɔ kple nɔnɔme ma ke le Polar afɔ ene me. Franseawo ƒe nɔnɔme tɔxɛ sia dzena nyuie wu le ŋkɔ si wotsɔ na tɔmelã ma me. Romishtɔwo ƒe misa si wowɔna na ame kukuwo dzea egɔme kple “Requiem eternam” (gbɔɖeme mavɔ), afisi Requiem tsɔ ŋkɔ na misa la ŋutɔ, kple kunuha bubu ɖesiaɖe tsoe. Fifia, le nuƒoƒo tso ku ƒe ɖoɖoezizi ɣi si le ƒumelã gã sia me, kple eƒe numamewo ƒe ku fafɛ ŋu la, Franseawo yɔe be Requin .

Bu albatross ŋu kpɔ, afikae gbɔgbɔ me nukunu kple vɔvɔ̃ si me kɔ o ƒe alilikpo mawo, siwo me gbɔgbɔ vɔ̃ ɣi ma zɔa mɔ le le susuwo katã me la tso? Menye Coleridge ye tsɔ afakaka ma ƒu gbe gbã o; ke Mawu ƒe kafukafuxɔla gã, si medzea ame ŋu o, Dzɔdzɔme.*

*Meɖo ŋku albatross gbãtɔ si mekpɔ kpɔ dzi. Enye le ahom sesẽ aɖe si nɔ tutum ɣeyiɣi didi aɖe me, le tsi sesẽ siwo le Antarctica-ƒua dzi me. Metso nye ŋdɔ gaƒoɖokui si le ete la dzi yi ʋua ƒe akpa si alilikpowo yɔ fũu la dzi; eye le afima, esi meƒu du yi ʋɔtru gãwo dzi la, mekpɔ fia ƒe nu aɖe si le abe fu ene si ƒe amadede nye ɣie si ŋu ɖi aɖeke mele o, eye Romatɔwo ƒe akɔtaɖonu si wotsɔ kɔsɔkɔsɔ wɔe si de ŋgɔ ŋutɔ. Le dometsotso aɖewo me la, etsɔa eƒe mawudɔla gã ƒe aʋala gãwo doa goe, abe ɖe wòxɔ aɖaka kɔkɔe aɖe ene. Ʋuʋudedi kple ʋuʋudedi wɔnukuwo ʋuʋui. Togbɔ be mewɔ nuvevii le ŋutilã me o hã la, edo ɣli, abe fia aɖe ƒe gbɔgbɔ si le xaxa si gbɔ dzɔdzɔmetɔ ŋu me ene. To eƒe ŋku siwo womate ŋu agblɔ o, siwo wɔ nuku me la, mesusu be meɖe ŋku ɖe nya ɣaɣla siwo lé Mawu ŋu. Abe Abraham le mawudɔlawo ŋkume ene la, mebɔbɔ ta; nu ɣia ɣi ale gbegbe, eƒe aʋalawo keke ale gbegbe, eye le tsi mawo siwo woɖe aboyoe tegbee me la, kɔnyinyiwo kple duwo ƒe ŋkuɖodzinya wɔnublanui siwo trɔna la bu. Melé ŋku ɖe fu wɔnuku ma ŋu ɣeyiɣi didi aɖe. Nyemate ŋu agblɔ, ɖeko mate ŋu aƒo nu tso wo ŋu, nusiwo ƒu du to menye ɣemaɣi o. Gake mlɔeba la, menyɔ; eye wòtrɔ, bia ƒudzidela aɖe be xevi kae nye esia. A goney, eɖo eŋu be. Goney! nyemese ŋkɔ ma kpɔ o; ɖe wòanya wɔ be ŋutikɔkɔe nu sia nye nusi amegbetɔ siwo le ƒuta menya kura oa! gbɛɖɛ! Gake le ɣeyiɣi aɖe megbe la, mese be goney nye ƒudzidela aɖewo ƒe ŋkɔ na albatross. Ale be le mɔ aɖeke nu la, Coleridge ƒe gbemegbe me Rhyme mate ŋu awɔ naneke kple nu ɣaɣla mawo siwo nye tɔnye o, esi mekpɔ xevi ma le míaƒe ʋua ƒe ʋuƒo dzi. Elabena nyemexlẽ Rhyme la ɣemaɣi hã o, eye nyemenya xevi la hã be enye albatross o. Ke hã le nya sia gbɔgblɔ me la, le mɔ si mele tẽ o nu la, meɖea hakpanya la kple hakpanyaŋlɔla la ƒe dzedzeme bubume la dzi kpɔtɔna vie.

Eyata mete gbe ɖe edzi be xevi la ƒe ŋutilã ƒe ɣie wɔnuku la mee afakaka la ƒe nya ɣaɣla koŋ le; nyateƒe aɖe si dze le esia me wu, be to nyawo ƒe vovototodedeameme me la, xevi siwo woyɔna be albatross ɣiwo li; kple esiawo mekpɔ enuenu, gake menye kple seselelãmewo abe esime mekpɔ Antarctica xevi ene gbeɖe o.

Gake aleke wowɔ lé nu ɣaɣla la? Mègagblɔe le ɣlidodo me o, eye magblɔe; kple kɔsɔkɔsɔ kple ka si nye alakpatɔ, esime xeviawo nɔ tsia dzi le atsiaƒu dzi. Mlɔeba la, Kapitan la tsɔe wɔ posudɔwɔla; lãgbalẽ ƒe xexlẽdzesi si woŋlɔ ɖe kɔ dzi, kple melia ƒe ɣeyiɣi kple teƒe si wòle; eye emegbe woaɖe asi le eŋu wòasi. Gake nyemeke ɖi o, woɖe lãgbalẽ xexlẽdzesi ma, si woɖo na amegbetɔ, le Dziƒo, esime xe ɣia dzo be yeawɔ ɖeka kple kerubi siwo le aʋala ƒom, yɔyɔ, kple tadedeagu!

Nusi xɔ ŋkɔ wu le míaƒe Ɣetoɖoƒetɔwo ƒe ŋutinyagbalẽwo kple Indiatɔwo ƒe kɔnyinyiwo mee nye White Steed of the Prairies tɔ; charger wɔnuku aɖe si ƒe amadede nye notsi, si ƒe ŋkuwo lolo, eƒe ta le sue, eƒe akɔta le blɔ, eye bubu le eŋu abe fia akpe ɖeka ene le eƒe tasiaɖam kɔkɔ si me wodoa vloe la me. Eyae nye sɔ gbemelãha gã siwo ƒe lãnyiƒewo le ŋkeke mawo me la, Rocky Towo kple Alleghanies-towo koe ƒo xlã wo ƒe Xerxes tiatia. Le woƒe ta si le dzo dam la, etsɔe ɖo ɣetoɖoƒe gome abe ɣletivi tiatia ma si kplɔa kekeli ƒe aʋakɔwo fiẽ sia fiẽ ene. Eƒe ɖa ƒe tsidzadza si le keklẽm, eƒe asike ƒe ɣletivimefakaka si le goglo la na wòkpɔ xɔ siwo klẽna wu esiwo sika kple klosaloƒomɔ̃wo ate ŋu anae hafi. Ðe wònye be to eƒe ɣeyiɣi mavɔmavɔ me doa vɔvɔli ɖe xexeame ƒe dzimaɖitsitsi kple nu gãwo dzi, eye wòtoa esia me tsɔa tsɔtsrɔ̃ ƒe susu tsɔa yi ƒoa mí tso megbe, ne míele notsimɔa ƒe gogloƒe ɣiwo kpɔma? Alo ɖe wònye, be abe alesi le nyateƒe me ene la, ɣie menye amadede boo o ke boŋ amadede ƒe anyimanɔmanɔ si wokpɔna; eye le ɣeyiɣi ma ke me la, kɔkrit si ƒe amadede le vovovo; susu siawo tae ƒuƒlu mumu sia tɔgbe, si gɔmesese yɔ fũ, le sno ƒe nɔnɔme gbadza aɖe me—mawudzimaxɔse ƒe amadede ɖesiaɖe si me amadede mele o, si me míeɖea mía ɖokui ɖa le? Eye ne míebu dzɔdzɔmeŋutinunyalawo ƒe nufiafia bubu ma ŋu la, be anyigba dzi amadede bubuawo katã—wo dometɔ ɖesiaɖe si ƒe amadede nye gã alo lɔlɔ̃—si nye ɣeɖoto ƒe dziŋgɔli kple avewo ƒe amadede viviwo; ẽ, kple kpakpaluʋuiwo ƒe velvet siwo wotsɔ sika wɔe, kple nyɔnuvi suewo ƒe kpakpaluʋui ƒe ŋgonu; esiawo katã nye amebeble siwo menya kpɔna dzea sii bɔbɔe o, siwo menye nusiwo wotsɔ dea nu me ŋutɔŋutɔ o, ke boŋ wotsɔa wo ɖoa wo dzi tso egodo ko; ale be Dzɔdzɔme siwo katã wotsɔ mawuwo wɔe la taa nu keŋkeŋ abe gbolo ene, amesi ƒe nu lédzinamewo metsyɔ naneke dzi o negbe charnel-xɔ si le eme ko; eye ne míeyi ŋgɔ, eye míebu eŋu be atsyɔ̃ɖomi ɣaɣla si naa eƒe amadede ɖesiaɖe, si nye kekeli ƒe gɔmeɖose gã la, nɔa ɣie alo amadede aɖeke manɔmee le eɖokui si tegbee, eye ne ele dɔ wɔm mawɔmawɔe ɖe nu dzi la, aka asi nuwo katã ŋu, tulips kple roses gɔ̃ hã, kple eya ŋutɔ ƒe amadede ƒuƒlu—ne míede ŋugble le esiawo katã ŋu la, xexeame katã si ƒe lãme gblẽ la mlɔ mía ŋkume abe anyidɔléla ene; eye abe mɔzɔla siwo ɖoe koŋ zɔa mɔ le Lapland, siwo gbea ŋkumeɖobolo siwo le amadede vovovowo me kple esiwo le amadede vovovowo me dodo ɖe woƒe ŋkuwo dzi ene la, nenema ke dzimaxɔsetɔ wɔnublanui la kpɔa eɖokui ŋkumaʋumaʋui le avɔ ɣi ŋkuɖodzinu si xatsa mɔkpɔkpɔwo katã ŋu. Eye le nu siawo katã dome la, Albino tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale ye nye dzesi. Ekema ɖe miewɔ nuku na mi le dzobibi ƒe adedada ŋua?
