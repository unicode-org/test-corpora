Ðetugbi Nɔaƒe si ƒo ƒu ɖe wo katã ŋu la, ewɔ eƒe ƒumelã ene siwo nye lãvi sue la yome, eye to esia me la, wo katã wobu yi didiƒe ʋĩ va ɖo yaƒoƒoa gbɔ, wogakpɔtɔ nɔ wo yome tim dzinɔameƒotɔe kple mɔkpɔkpɔ.

O! wo dometɔ geɖe nye Fin-Backs, eye geɖe nye Dericks, xɔ̃nye.





TA 82. Bubu kple Ŋutikɔkɔe na tɔmelãwo léle.

Dɔwɔƒe aɖewo li siwo me ɖoɖomawɔmawɔ nyuie nye mɔnu vavãtɔ le.

Zi alesi megena ɖe tɔmelãwo léle ƒe nya sia me, eye metutua nye numekukuwo yia eƒe kele ƒe ta ŋutɔŋutɔ dzi la, zi nenemae bubu gã si le eŋu kple blematɔnyenye wɔa dɔ ɖe dzinyee; eye vevietɔ ne meke ɖe mawu afã kple kalẽtɔ gã geɖe, nyagblɔɖila ƒomevi ɖesiaɖe, siwo to mɔ aɖe nu alo bubu dzi kɔ vovototodedeameme ɖe edzi la, wotsɔa ŋugble si nye be nye ŋutɔ menye nɔviwo ƒe habɔbɔ si woɖo atsyɔ̃ na alea la tɔ, togbɔ be gake le ete hã.

Perseus kalẽtɔ, si nye Yupiter viŋutsu, ye nye tɔmelã gbãtɔ si tsɔa tɔmelãwo ƒua gbe; eye woagblɔ na bubu mavɔ si le míaƒe yɔyɔ ŋu be, womewu tɔmelã gbãtɔ si dzi míaƒe nɔviwo ƒe habɔbɔa dze la kple tameɖoɖo gbegblẽ aɖeke o. Emawoe nye míaƒe dɔa ƒe sɔdolawo ƒe ŋkekewo, esime aʋawɔnuwo koe míetsɔna tsɔ kpena ɖe amesiwo le xaxa ŋu, eye menye be míatsɔ ayɔ ŋutsuwo ƒe akaɖi-nuɖuƒewo o. Amesiame nya Perseus kple Andromeda ƒe ŋutinya nyui la; alesi wobla Andromeda lɔlɔ̃tɔ, si nye fia aɖe vinyɔnu, ɖe agakpe aɖe ŋu le ƒuta, eye esi Leviatan nɔ ekɔm la, Perseus, si nye tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whalemen ƒe amegã, nɔ ŋgɔ yim dzinɔameƒotɔe, tsɔ saŋku ƒo lã wɔadã la, eye wòɖe dɔlanyɔnua heɖee. Enye aɖaŋudɔ si dze kafukafu, si ƒã hafi egbegbe saŋkuƒola nyuitɔwo te ŋu wɔna; esi wònye be wowu Leviatan sia le aŋutrɔ gbãtɔ tututu me ta. Eye ame aɖeke megake ɖi Arkitɔwo ƒe ŋutinya sia o; elabena le blema Yopa, si nye Yafa fifia, si le Siria-ƒuta, le Trɔ̃subɔlawo ƒe gbedoxɔwo dometɔ ɖeka me la, ƒumelã gã aɖe si woyɔna be whale, si dua ƒe xotutuwo kple emenɔlawo katã gblɔ be enye lã wɔadã si Perseus wu la ƒe ƒu siwo sɔ la nɔ tsitrenu hena ƒe geɖe. Esi Romatɔwo xɔ Yopa la, wotsɔ ƒunukpeƒe ma ke yi Italy le aʋadziɖuɖu me. Nusi dze abe ɖe wòle vevie wu eye wòfia be ele vevie wu le ŋutinya sia me ene, enye esi: Yona ye Yona ɖo tɔdziʋu tso.

George Kɔkɔe kple Ʋɔ driba ƒe ŋutinya xɔŋkɔ ma si sɔ kple Perseus kple Andromeda ƒe afɔku—le nyateƒe me la, ame aɖewo susu be edzɔ tso eme le mɔ si mele tẽ o nu—la; ʋɔ driba si meléna ɖe asi be enye tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale; elabena le ŋutinyagbalẽ xoxo geɖe me la, tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale kple ʋɔ dribawo tsaka ɖekae le mɔ wɔnuku aɖe nu, eye zi geɖe la, wotsia tre ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu. Ezekiel gblɔ be: “Èle abe tsidzidzata kple atsiaƒu me ʋɔ driba ene; le afisia, si gɔmee nye tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale kɔte; le nyateƒe me la, Biblia gɔmeɖeɖe aɖewo zã nya ma ŋutɔ. Gakpe ɖe eŋu la, aɖe ŋutikɔkɔe si le aʋadziɖuɖu si George Kɔkɔe nɔ ŋu la ɖa ŋutɔ gake edo go lã si tana le anyigba dzi si le tsatsam, le esi teƒe be wòawɔ aʋa kple lã wɔadã gã si le gogloƒe la. Ŋutsu ɖesiaɖe ate ŋu awu da, gake Perseus, George Kɔkɔe, Aɖaka, koe dzi le wo me be woazɔ dzideƒotɔe ayi tɔmelã aɖe gbɔ.

Migana nukpɔkpɔ sia ƒe egbegbe nutatawo naflu mí o; elabena togbɔ be woɖe nuwɔwɔ si tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale kalẽtɔ ma do goe le blema la fia le mɔ si me mekɔ o nu le nɔnɔme si le abe griffin ene me, eye togbɔ be woɖe aʋawɔwɔa fia le anyigba dzi eye woɖe ame kɔkɔe la fia le sɔ dzi hã la, evɔ ne míebu ɣeyiɣi mawo ƒe manyamanya gã la ŋu, esime nutalawo menya tɔmelã ƒe nɔnɔme ŋutɔŋutɔ o; eye ne míebu eŋu be abe alesi wònɔ le Perseus gome ene la, ɖewohĩ St. George ƒe tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale la ƒu du tso atsiaƒua me le ƒuta; eye ne míebu eŋu be ɖewohĩ nutrenu gã aɖe, alo ƒumelã aɖe koe lã si George Kɔkɔe do la nye; ne nusiawo katã le susu me na mí la, madze abe ɖe mesɔ kura kple xotutu kɔkɔea kple nukpɔkpɔa ƒe blema nɔnɔmetatawo o ene, be míalé amesi woyɔna be ʋɔ driba sia ɖe asi be menye ame bubu aɖeke o negbe Lewiatan gã la ŋutɔ ko. Le nyateƒe me la, ne wotsɔ ŋutinya sia katã da ɖe nyateƒe si me kɔ eye wòƒoa nu la ŋkume la, adze abe Filistitɔwo ƒe tɔmelã, lã, kple xeviwo ƒe legba ma, si ŋkɔe nye Dagon ene; ame si wodo ɖe Israel ƒe aɖaka la ŋgɔ ta la, eƒe sɔ ƒe ta kple eƒe asibidɛ eveawo ge le eŋu, eye eƒe ke alo tɔmelã ƒe akpa aɖe koe susɔ. Eyata mía ŋutɔwo ƒe stamp bubumewo dometɔ ɖeka, si nye tɔmelã si woyɔna be whaleman gɔ̃ hã, nye England ƒe dzɔla si kpɔa wo dzi; eye to gomenɔamesi nyuiwo dzi la, ele be woaŋlɔ mí Nantucket ƒe harpooneers ŋkɔ ɖe St. George ƒe ɖoɖo si me bubu le wu la me. Eye le esia ta, megana bubumeha ma ƒe sɔdolawo (amesiwo dometɔ aɖeke, metsɔ dzideƒo magblɔ be, mewɔ ɖeka kple tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale abe woƒe ametakpɔla gã ene kpɔ o), megatsɔ vlododo ŋkume na Nantucketer gbeɖe o, elabena le míaƒe awudziwui siwo wotsɔ lãfu wɔe kple afɔkpa siwo wotsɔ aŋɔ wɔe gɔ̃ hã me la, míedze na George Kɔkɔe ƒe atsyɔ̃ɖoɖo nyuie wu wo sã.

Nenye be maxɔ Hercules ɖe mía dome loo alo nyemaxɔe o, ɖikeke nɔ ŋunye le esia ŋu ɣeyiɣi didi aɖe: elabena togbɔ be le Helatɔwo ƒe gliwo nu la, blema Crockett kple Kit Carson ma—si nye dzidzɔkpɔkpɔ ƒe dɔ nyui wɔwɔ ƒe ame sesẽ ma, la, tɔmelã aɖe mi eye wòtsɔe ƒu gbe; ke hã, ne ema na wòzu tɔmelã si woyɔna be whaleman keŋkeŋ hã la, woate ŋu aʋli nya le ema ŋu. Edze le afi aɖeke be etsɔ aŋe ƒo eƒe tɔmelãa ŋutɔŋutɔ kpɔ o, negbe ɖe wòtso ememe vavã hafi. Ke hã, woate ŋu abui be enye tɔmelã siwo melɔ̃na o ƒomevi aɖe; aleke kee wòɖale o, tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale la lée, ne menye tɔmelã lae lée o. Mebiaa eyama ɖe míaƒe ƒomea me tɔ ɖeka ta.

Gake, le dziɖuɖumegã nyuitɔ kekeake siwo tsi tre ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu nu la, wobu Helatɔwo ƒe ŋutinya sia si ku ɖe Hercules kple tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale ŋu be etso Hebrigbe me ŋutinya si gakpɔtɔ nye blematɔ si ku ɖe Yona kple tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale ŋu la me; eye wòtrɔ ɖe eŋuâ; kakaɖedzitɔe la, woɖi wo nɔewo ŋutɔ. Ne megblɔ be mawu afãe nye ema la, ke nukatae nyemagblɔ nyagblɔɖila la o?

Eye kalẽtɔwo, ame kɔkɔewo, mawu afãwo, kple nyagblɔɖilawo ɖeɖe hã menye míaƒe ha la ƒe agbalẽ xatsaxatsa bliboa o. Míaƒe aƒetɔ gãtɔ ƒe ŋkɔ gakpɔtɔ li; elabena abe fiafia siwo nɔ anyi le blema ene la, míekpɔa míaƒe nɔviwo ƒe habɔbɔa ƒe tatsiwo le naneke me wu mawu gãwo ŋutɔ o. Fifia ele be woasrɔ̃ ɣedzeƒe ŋutinya wɔnuku ma tso Shaster gbɔ, si na míekpɔ Vishnoo dziŋɔ, si nye ame etɔ̃ siwo le Hindutɔwo ƒe mawunyenye me dometɔ ɖeka; tsɔ Vishnoo sia si tso dziƒo la ŋutɔ na mí ɖe míaƒe Aƒetɔ ta;—Vishnoo, amesi to eƒe anyigbadzi ŋutilã ewoawo dometɔ gbãtɔ dzi la, eɖe tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale ɖe vovo eye wòkɔ eŋu tegbee. Shaster gblɔ be esi Brahma, alo Mawuwo ƒe Mawu, ɖoe be yeagbugbɔ xexeame awɔ le eƒe ʋuʋu ɣeaɖewoɣi dometɔ ɖeka megbe la, edzi Vishnoo, be wòakpɔ dɔa dzi; gake Veda, alo agbalẽ ɣaɣlawo, siwo me dzodzro adze abe ɖe wòhiã vevie na Vishnoo hafi dze nuwɔwɔ gɔme ene, eye le esia ta anya nane si le abe aɖaŋuɖoɖo ŋutɔŋutɔ siwo fia sɔhɛ xɔtuɖaŋunyalawo ene la, Veda siawo mlɔ tsiawo gɔme; eyata Vishnoo va zu ŋutilã le tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale me, eye esi wòɖi le eme vaseɖe gogloƒe ke la, eɖe babla kɔkɔeawo. Ekema ɖe menye tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale-ƒolae Vishnoo sia nye oa? abe alesi woyɔa sɔdola be sɔdola ene?

Perseus, George Kɔkɔe, Hercules, Yona, kple Vishnoo! member-roll aɖe li na wò! Kluvi kae ate ŋu aɖo ta nenema negbe tɔmelã si woyɔna be whaleman tɔ ko?





TA 83. Wobu Yona le Ŋutinya me.

Woƒo nu tso ŋutinya me ŋutinya si ku ɖe Yona kple tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale ŋu le ta si do ŋgɔ me. Fifia Nantuckettɔ aɖewo meka ɖe Yona kple tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale ƒe ŋutinya me ŋutinya sia dzi boŋ o. Gake Hela kple Romatɔ ɖikela aɖewo hã nɔ anyi emegbe, amesiwo to vovo tso trɔ̃subɔla orthodɔks siwo nɔ anyi le woƒe ɣeyiɣia me gbɔ ta la, woke ɖi Hercules kple tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale, kple Arion kple dolphin ƒe ŋutinya sɔsɔe; eye ke hã woƒe ɖikeke le kɔnyinyi mawo ŋu mena kɔnyinyi mawo mezu nyateƒenya sue aɖeke o, le emawo katã ta.

Susu vevitɔ si tae Sag-Harbor tɔmelã xoxo aɖe ke ɖi Hebritɔwo ƒe ŋutinyaae nye esi:—Biblia xoxo mawo siwo mebɔ o la dometɔ ɖeka nɔ esi, eye wotsɔ agbalẽvi siwo me wodi be yewoanya nu tsoe, siwo mesɔ ɖe dzɔdzɔmeŋutinunya nu o, ɖo atsyɔ̃ nɛ; wo dometɔ ɖeka tsi tre ɖi na Yona ƒe tɔmelã si me tsi eve le le ta—si nye nusi to vovo le Leviathan ƒomevi aɖe (Right Whale, kple hatsotso ma me tɔ) gome ko, si ŋu tɔƒodelawo gblɔna le be, “Penny roll ɖeka aƒoe”; eƒe nu mimi le sue ŋutɔ ale gbegbe. Gake, le esia ŋu la, Bisiɔp Jebb ƒe ŋuɖoɖo si wòkpɔ mɔ na la sɔ gbe. Bisiɔp la de dzesii be mehiã be míabu Yona be woɖii ɖe tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale ƒe ƒodo me o, ke boŋ abe ɖe wòtsi eƒe nu ƒe akpa aɖe hena ɣeyiɣi aɖe ene. Eye edze abe esia sɔ le Bisiɔp nyui la gome ene. Elabena le nyateƒe me la, Right Whale ƒe nu axɔ whist-table eve aɖewo, eye wòana fefewɔlawo katã nanɔ anyi bɔbɔe. Anɔ eme hã be Yona anya ƒo eɖokui ɖe aɖu aɖe si me do le me; gake ne èbu eŋu zi evelia la, aɖu mele Right Whale la ŋu o.

Susu bubu si Sag-Harbor (etsɔ ŋkɔ ma yi) ƒoe ɖe enu le eƒe xɔse ƒe anyimanɔmanɔ le nyagblɔɖila la ƒe nya sia me ta, enye nane si me mekɔ o le eƒe ŋutilã si wode gaxɔ me kple tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale ƒe dɔgbo me tsiwo ŋu. Gake tsitretsiɖeŋu sia hã dze anyigba nenema ke, elabena Germanytɔ numeɖela aɖe susui be Yona anya sitsoƒe le tɔmelã kuku aɖe ƒe kuku si le tsia dzi me—abe alesi Franse-srafo siwo nɔ Russiatɔwo ƒe aʋawɔɣia trɔ woƒe sɔ kukuawo wozu agbadɔwo, eye wotso wo me ene gɔ̃ hã. Gakpe ɖe eŋu la, anyigbagã dzi numeɖela bubuwo gblɔe ɖi be esi wotsɔ Yona ƒu gbe tso Yopa-melia me yi ƒua me la, ewɔ eƒe sisi enumake yi tɔdziʋu bubu si te ɖe afima ŋu, tɔdziʋu aɖe si me tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale le hena nɔnɔmetata-ta; eye, magblɔ kpee be, ɖewohĩ woayɔe be “The Whale,” abe alesi woyɔa asinudɔwɔwɔ aɖewo egbea be “Shark,” “Gull,” “Hɔ̃.” Eye nugɔmeɖela agbalẽnyala siwo gblɔ be tɔmelã si ŋu woƒo nu tsoe le Yona ƒe agbalẽa me la fia agbeɖenu ko—ya kotoku si me ya le—si me nyagblɔɖila si le afɔku me la ƒu tsi yi, eye eyata woɖee tso tsɔtsrɔ̃ si me tsi le me hã mehiã o. Eyata edze abe Sag-Harbor dahe la vɔ̃ɖi wu le goawo katã me ene. Gake susu bubu aɖe ganɔ esi si tae xɔse mele esi o. Esiae nye esi, ne meɖo ŋku edzi nyuie: Tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale la mi Yona le Mediterranea Ƒua me, eye le ŋkeke etɔ̃ megbe la, eɖe aɖuɖɔ le afi aɖe le ŋkeke etɔ̃ ƒe mɔzɔzɔ tso Niniwe, si nye du aɖe si le Tigris, si didi wu ŋkeke etɔ̃ ƒe mɔzɔzɔ tso Mediterranea-ƒuta ƒe teƒe si te ɖe eŋu wu la me. Aleke wòle?

Gake ɖe mɔ bubu aɖeke meli si dzi tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale la ato aɖi nyagblɔɖila la le Niniwe gbɔ kpuie ma oa? Ɛ̃. Ðewohĩ etsɔe ƒo xlãe to Mɔkpɔkpɔ Nyui ƒe Ta la dzi hafi. Gake ne míaƒo nu tso mɔzɔzɔ to Mediterranea-ƒua ƒe didime bliboa me, kple mɔ bubu si to Persia-ƒuta kple Ƒudzĩa dzi ŋu o la, nususugblɔ ma abia be woaƒo xlã Afrika katã keŋkeŋ le ŋkeke etɔ̃ me, ne míaƒo nu tso Tigris-tsi siwo te ɖe Niniwe teƒe ŋu, si me goglo akpa be tɔmelã aɖeke mate ŋu aƒu tsi le eme ŋu o ŋkeke ɖeka kaba axɔ bubu si le ta gã ma si ŋu woke ɖo la le Bartholomew Diaz, si xɔ ŋkɔ be eke ɖe eŋu la si, eye ale wòana egbegbe ŋutinya nanye alakpatɔ.

Gake ɖeko Sag-Harbor tsitsia ƒe bometsinya siawo katã ɖe eƒe susu ƒe dada bometsitsi fia—si nye nusi gabu fɔe wu le esi, esi wòkpɔe be nu sue aɖe koe yesrɔ̃ negbe nusiwo yefɔ tso ɣe kple atsiaƒu me ko. Megblɔ be ɖeko wòɖe eƒe dada bometsitsi, mawumavɔ̃mavɔ̃, kple ŋunyɔnu, abosam ƒe aglãdzedze ɖe nunɔla bubumewo ŋu fia. Elabena Portugaltɔ Katoliko nunɔla aɖee do susu sia tututu si nye be Yona nayi Niniwe to Mɔkpɔkpɔ Nyui ƒe Ta la dzi la ɖe ŋgɔ be wòanye dzesi si woatsɔ adzi nukunu bliboa ɖe edzi. Eye nenemae wònɔ. Gakpe ɖe eŋu la, va se ɖe egbea la, Turkeytɔ siwo si nunya le ŋutɔ la xɔ Yona ƒe ŋutinya me ŋutinya dzi se vevie. Eye anɔ ƒe alafa etɔ̃ enye sia la, Eŋlisi mɔzɔla aɖe si le Harris ƒe Mɔzɔzɔ xoxowo me ƒo nu tso Turkeytɔwo ƒe Mosque aɖe si wotu tsɔ de bubu Yona ŋu, si me Mosque nye akaɖi wɔnuku aɖe si biã ami aɖeke manɔmee le.





TA 84. Gbeɖiɖi ƒe gbeɖiɖi.

Be woate ŋu aƒu du bɔbɔe eye woaƒu du kabakaba la, wosi ami na tasiaɖamwo ƒe asikewo; eye le taɖodzinu ma ke ta la, tɔmelãɖela aɖewo wɔa dɔ ma tɔgbe le woƒe tɔdziʋua me; wodea ami ɖe ete. Eye mele be woake ɖi hã be esi wònye be ɖoɖo ma tɔgbe mate ŋu agblẽ nu le ame ŋu o ta la, ɖewohĩ viɖe aɖeke manɔ eŋu si woado vloe o; ne míebu eŋu be ami kple tsi nye futɔwo; be ami nye nusi ƒua du, eye be nusi ŋu wole ŋku lém ɖoe nye be wòana tɔdziʋua naƒu du dzinɔameƒotɔe. Queequeg xɔ amisisi na eƒe tɔdziʋua dzi se vevie, eye gbeɖeka ŋdi esi Germanytɔwo ƒe meli si nye Jungfrau bu megbe kpuie la, ewɔ nu geɖe wu vevesese siwo wowɔna le kɔnu nu le dɔ ma wɔwɔ me; nɔa tsatsam le eƒe gɔme te, afisi wòtsia axadzi le, eye wònɔa asi tutum le nusi me kɔ o la me abe ɖe wòle didim vevie be yeawɔ nugblẽfexeɖoɖo na taɖa aɖe si tso aɖaŋudɔa ƒe taɖa si le ɖaa me ene. Edze abe enɔ dɔ wɔm le toɖoɖo presentiment aɖe koŋ me ene. Eye megakpɔtɔ nye nusi mesɔ o le nudzɔdzɔa ta o.

Ke hã, míate ŋu asusui, ne míate ŋu aɖo kpe edzi ahaɖo kpe edzi o gɔ̃ hã. Nye nukpɔsusue nye esi: be ʋuʋudedi la menye naneke wu kuɖiɖi o. Enye ame si bua nuwo ŋu eye wòdea to hã. Esi menɔ agbalẽ sue aɖe ŋlɔm tso Mavɔmavɔ ŋu la, didi vevie nɔ menye be matsɔ ahuhɔ̃e aɖe aɖo ŋkunyeme; eye ere long saw reflected there, didi be yeanya nu tso nudzodzoewo kple ƒutsotsoewo ƒe ʋuʋu le yame le nye ta dzi ɖe eme. Elabena, d'ye kpɔe, dziŋgɔliwo meɖia tsa yia ya si me kɔ la gbɔ o; ɖeko wodoa keklẽ na yaƒoƒo. Eye ale, to ɖikeke siwo me mekɔ o siwo le nye susu me ƒe kuɖiɖi ɣiwo katã me la, mawume nukpɔsusuwo daa tu ɣeaɖewoɣi, eye wotsɔa dziƒo ƒe keklẽ aɖe doa dzo nye afu la me.
