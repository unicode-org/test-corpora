Ẽ, tomefafa si nɔ anyi ɣeyiɣi didi la nɔ dzodzom. Eteƒe medidi o wose gbeɖiɖi aɖe si nɔ ŋgɔ yim bɔbɔe; eye emegbe abe tsikpe gbogbo siwo me zitɔtɔ le ne tɔsisi gã Hudson gbã le Dzomeŋɔli ene la, tɔmelã gbogboawo va nɔ gegem ɖe woƒe titina ememetɔ dzi, abe ɖe wole wo ɖokui ƒom ƒu ɖe to ɖeka si bɔ dzi ene. Gake to agbagbadzedze vevie me la, míeda tu ɖe ɣeyiɣi kpui aɖe ƒe ʋuʋu aɖe me mlɔeba; emegbe woaɖe asi le wo ɖokui ŋu kabakaba, eye le ɣeyiɣi ma ke me la, woanɔ ŋku lém ɖe asitsaƒe bubu ŋu vevie. Le sisi geɖe siwo keke abe taɖa ene megbe la, míeƒu du kabakaba yi afisi nye gotagome nutomeawo dometɔ ɖeka teti koe nye ema, gake fifia tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whales le vome la tsoe, eye wo katã míewɔ ŋutasesẽtɔe be míazu titina ɖeka. Woƒle dzɔgbenyui ƒe ɖeɖekpɔkpɔ sia le asi bɔbɔe me to Queequeg ƒe kposɔ ƒe bu me, amesi esime wònɔ tsitre ɖe datiawo me be yeaƒo tɔmelã siwo sina la, ya si le ʋuʋum to fluke gbadza eve siwo te ɖe afima ŋu ƒe ʋuʋu kpata me la tsɔ eƒe kposɔa le ta me.

Togbɔ be xexeame katã ƒe zitɔtɔa nye zitɔtɔ kple ɖoɖomawɔmawɔ fifia hã la, eteƒe medidi o ekpɔ eɖokui gbɔ va zu nusi dze abe habɔbɔ si wowɔ ɖe ɖoɖo nu ene; elabena esi woƒo ƒu mlɔeba ɖe ŋutilã ɖeka si me ʋuʋudedi le me ta la, emegbe wogawɔ woƒe yameʋuɖoɖo yi ŋgɔe yeyee kple ʋuʋudedi si dzi ɖe edzi. Viɖe aɖeke menɔ wo yometiti yi ŋgɔe ŋu o; gake tɔdziʋuawo gakpɔtɔ nɔ woƒe ŋudzɔ be yewoafɔ tɔmelã siwo ŋu atike muamewo le siwo woate ŋu atsɔ aƒu gbe ɖe megbe, eye nenema ke woakpɔ ɖeka si Flask wu heɖe asi le eŋu la ta. Nusi do tso ɖiɖi sia me nye nya ma si wogblɔ si me nunya le le Tɔƒodeƒea ƒe kpɔɖeŋu vie,—zi alesi tɔmelãwo sɔ gbɔe la, zi nenemae tɔmelãwo hã mesɔ gbɔ o. Le tɔmelã siwo katã wozã atike vɔ̃ɖiwo dome la, ɖeka koe wolé. Susu mamlɛawo ɖo tae be yewoasi hena ɣeyiɣia, gake ɖeko wotsɔ aɖaŋu bubu aɖe si menye Pequod o la xɔ wo, abe alesi woakpɔe le afisia ene.





TA 88. Sukuwo kple Sukudzikpɔlawo.

Ta si do ŋgɔ ƒo nu tso Sperm Whale ƒe ha gã aɖe alo ha gã aɖe ŋu, eye emegbe wogblɔ nusi ate ŋu ahe nusiwo gbɔ wòtso be woƒo ƒu gã mawo tso hã.

Fifia togbɔ be wodoa go ŋutilã gã mawo ɣeaɖewoɣi hã la, abe alesi wòanya kpɔe ene la, le egbeŋkekea me gɔ̃ hã la, wokpɔa ha sue siwo ɖe wo ɖokui ɖa ɣeaɖewoɣi, eye wo dometɔ ɖesiaɖe xɔa ame ɖekaɖeka siwo tso blaeve va ɖo blaatɔ̃. Woyɔa haƒoha mawo be sukuwo. Zi geɖe la, wonyea ƒomevi eve; esiwo me nyɔnuwo koe le kloe, kple esiwo meƒoa ɖeke nu ƒu o negbe atsuvi sesẽwo, alo nyitsuvi, abe alesi woyɔa wo nyuie ene.

Le sɔdolawo ƒe nyɔnuwo ƒe sukua dede me la, èkpɔa ŋutsu si ƒe lolome de blibo, gake metsi o ɣesiaɣi; amesi, le ɣlidodo ɖesiaɖe me la, eɖea eƒe kalẽwɔwɔ fiana to gege ɖe megbe eye wòtsyɔa eƒe nyɔnuwo ƒe dzodzo dzi. Le nyateƒe me la, aƒetɔ sia nye Ottomantɔ atsyɔ̃ɖola, si le tsi ƒum le xexe si me tsi le la dzi, eye akɔfafa kple lɔlɔ̃ siwo katã le harem la me kpe ɖe eŋu. Vovototo si le Ottoman sia kple eƒe ahiãviwo dome la ɖe dzesi ŋutɔ; elabena togbɔ be enye leviathanic ƒe agbɔsɔsɔ gãtɔ kekeake ɣesiaɣi hã la, nyɔnuawo, ne wotsi bliboe gɔ̃ hã la, womewu ŋutsu si ƒe lolome le mama dedie nu ƒe akpa gãtɔ ƒe akpa etɔ̃lia o. Wole ʋeʋẽm ne wotsɔ wo sɔ kple wo nɔewo, nyateƒee; Metsɔ dzideƒo gblɔ be, nyemagawu meta blaeve vɔ afã le akɔta o. Ke hã, womate ŋu agbe o, be le nusianu me la, wodze le domenyinu me be woaxɔ en bon point .

Enyea didi vevie be woakpɔ harem sia kple eƒe aƒetɔ le woƒe kuviawɔwɔ ƒe tsatsam. Abe atsyãwo ene la, wole ʋuʋum tegbee le nu vovovowo dim le dziɖeɖi me. Èdoa go wo le Line la dzi le ɣeyiɣi nyuitɔ dzi na Equatorial ƒe nuɖuɖuɣia ƒe seƒoƒo bliboa, esi nètrɔ gbɔ teti koe nye ema, ɖewohĩ, tso dzomeŋɔli nɔnɔ le Dziehe atsiaƒuwo me, eye wòblea dzomeŋɔli alea le ɖeɖiteameŋu kple dzoxɔxɔ si medzea ame ŋu o katã ta. Kaka woaɖi ɖe eme le Equator ƒe tsaɖiƒea hena ɣeyiɣi aɖe la, wodzea egɔme yia Ɣedzeƒetɔwo ƒe tsiwo gbɔ le mɔkpɔkpɔ me na fafaɣi si anɔ afima, eye ale woƒoa asa na dzoxɔxɔ si gbɔ eme bubu si le ƒea me.

Ne ele ŋgɔ yim le tomefafa me le mɔzɔzɔ siawo dometɔ ɖeka me la, ne wokpɔ nu wɔnuku aɖewo siwo ŋu ɖikeke le la, nye aƒetɔ tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale léa ŋku ɖe eƒe ƒome dodzidzɔnamea ŋu nyuie. Ne ɖetugbui Leviatan aɖe si mesɔ o naɖe fu na ɖekakpui Leviatan si gbɔna mɔ ma dzi la, tsɔe be yeate ɖe nyɔnuawo dometɔ ɖeka ŋu le adzame, eye dziku wɔnuku ka gbegbee nye si Bashaw la tsɔ dze edzi, eye wònyae ɖa! Nyateƒee, ɣeyiɣi kɔkɔwo, ne woaɖe mɔ na sɔhɛ rake siwo si gɔmeɖose mele o abe eya ene be woadze aƒeme dzidzɔkpɔkpɔ ƒe kɔkɔenyenye dzi; togbɔ be ewɔ nusi Bashaw la awɔ hã la, mate ŋu ana Lothario si xɔ ŋkɔ wu la natsi eƒe aba dzi o; elabena, baba! tɔmelãwo katã ƒe aba le ɖeka. Esi wole ƒuta la, zi geɖe la, nyɔnuawo hea aʋawɔwɔ dziŋɔtɔwo kekeake vɛ le woƒe hoʋlila siwo doa alɔ wo nɔewo dome; nenema kee wòle le tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale hã gome, amesiwo vaa aʋa si wua ame ɣeaɖewoɣi, eye wo katã wowɔna le lɔlɔ̃ ta. Wotsɔa woƒe aɖu legbe siwo le ete la ƒoa ʋɔtru, ɣeaɖewoɣi la, woblaa wo ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu, eye alea wodzea agbagba be yewoaxɔ ŋgɔ abe ƒumelã siwo tsɔa woƒe asikewo blaa wo nɔewo le aʋawɔwɔ me ene. Womelé ame ʋɛ aɖewo siwo ƒe abi goglo siwo le gododo siawo me o,—ta siwo me ʋuʋudedi le, aɖu siwo gbã, asike siwo le ʋuʋu; eye le go aɖewo me la, nu siwo wotsɔ safuiwo ƒo ƒui eye woɖe wo ɖa.

Gake ne míetsɔe be aƒeme dzidzɔkpɔkpɔ dzi dzela la tsɔ eɖokui dzoe le harem ƒe aƒetɔ ƒe duƒuƒu gbãtɔ me la, ekema ɖe wòhea susu ɖa ŋutɔ be woalé ŋku ɖe aƒetɔ ma ŋua. Egaƒoa nu tso eƒe akpa gãtɔ ŋu blewuu le wo dome ake eye wògakpɔa dzidzɔ le afima hena ɣeyiɣi aɖe, eye wògakpɔtɔ le nuto si nyɔa gbɔdɔdɔ ƒe dzodzro ɖe ame me na ɖekakpui Lothario, abe mawuvɔ̃la Salomo si le ta dem agu na veviedodotɔe le eƒe ahiã akpeawo dome ene. Esi woɖe mɔ be tɔmelã bubuwo nanɔ dzedzem ta la, ƒã hafi tɔƒodelawo ati Turkeytɔ Gã siawo dometɔ ɖeka yome; elabena Turkey Gã siawo tsɔa woƒe ŋusẽ ƒua gbe akpa, eye le esia ta woƒe makɔmakɔnyenye le sue. Le viŋutsu kple nyɔnuvi siwo wodzi gome la, nukatae, ele be viŋutsu kple vinyɔnu mawo nalé be na wo ɖokui; ne mede ɖeke o la, vidadaa ƒe kpekpeɖeŋu koe. Elabena abe lɔlɔ̃tɔ bubu aɖewo siwo ɖua nusianu siwo le tsa ɖim siwo ƒe ŋkɔ woate ŋu ayɔ ene la, nye Aƒetɔ Whale melɔ̃a vidzikpɔƒea o, aleke kee wòɖalɔ̃a bower o; eye ale esi wònye mɔzɔla gã aɖe ta la, egblẽa eƒe vidzĩ siwo ƒe ŋkɔ womeyɔ o la ɖe xexeame katã; vidzĩ ɖesiaɖe nye nusi to vovo. Ke hã, le ɣeyiɣi nyuitɔ dzi la, ne sɔhɛmenɔɣi ƒe dzonɔameme dzi le ɖeɖem kpɔtɔ; esi ƒewo kple gbeɖuɖɔwo le dzidzim ɖe edzi; abe alesi ŋugbledede doa eƒe ɖiɖiɖeme veviwoe ene; kpuie ko la, abe alesi kuviawɔwɔ si bɔ ɖe Turkeytɔ si yɔ fũu la ta ene; emegbe lɔlɔ̃ na bɔbɔɖeanyi kple nɔnɔme nyui xɔa ɖe lɔlɔ̃ na ɖetugbuiwo teƒe; míaƒe Ottoman gena ɖe agbe ƒe akpa si me ŋusẽ mele o, si trɔ dzime, si nye nuxlɔ̃ame, ka atam, gbã harem la, eye wòtsi va zu luʋɔ tsitsi si nye kpɔɖeŋu, si ƒe dzi gbãna, eya ɖeka nɔa tsa ɖim le meridians kple parallels dome nɔa eƒe gbedodoɖawo gblɔm, eye wòxlɔ̃a nu ɖekakpui Leviathan ɖesiaɖe tso eƒe lɔlɔ̃ ƒe vodadawo me.

Fifia, abe alesi tɔƒodelawo yɔa tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale ƒe nɔƒe be suku ene la, nenema kee suku ma ƒe aƒetɔ kple aƒetɔ si woyɔna le mɔ̃ɖaŋununya me be sukudzikpɔla hã le. Eyata menye le nɔnɔme sesẽ me o, aleke kee wòɖanye fewuɖunya si dze kafukafu o, be ne eya ŋutɔ de suku vɔ la, emegbe wòayi duta aƒã nusi wòsrɔ̃ le afima ɖe ame me menye nusi wòsrɔ̃ le afima o, ke boŋ eƒe bometsitsi. Eƒe dzesideŋkɔ si nye sukutatɔ, adze abe ɖe wòtso ŋkɔ si wotsɔ na sɔlemexɔa ŋutɔ me le dzɔdzɔme nu ene, gake ame aɖewo susui be ŋutsu si tsɔ ŋkɔ na Ottoman tɔmelã sia ƒomevi gbã la, anya xlẽ Vidocq ƒe ŋkuɖodzinyawo, eye wòna eɖokui nya be dukɔa me sukufiala ka ƒomevie Fransetɔ xɔŋkɔ ma nye le eƒe ɖevime, kple alesi vivimenusɔsrɔ̃ mawo nɔ si woƒã ɖe eƒe sukuvi aɖewo me.

Ameɖekaɖeka kple ame ɖokui ɖeɖe ɖe aga si me sukutatɔ tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale betakes eɖokui le le eƒe tsitsime la ke nye nyateƒe le Sperm Whale tsitsiwo katã hã gome. Le xexeame katã kloe la, tɔmelã si tsi akogo—abe alesi woyɔa Leviathan ɖeka kolia ene—ɖo kpe blema tɔ dzi. Abe Daniel Boone si ƒe ɖa le abe ʋuʋudedi ene si ŋu bubu le ene la, ame aɖeke magate ɖe eŋu o negbe Dzɔdzɔme ŋutɔ ko; eye wòɖenɛ le tsiwo ƒe gbedadaƒo, eye wònye srɔ̃nyɔnu nyuitɔ kekeake, togbɔ be eɣlaa nya ɣaɣla geɖe siwo me seselelãme le hã.

Suku siwo me haƒoha aɖeke mele o negbe ŋutsu sɔhɛwo kple ŋusẽtɔwo ko, siwo ŋu míeƒo nu tsoe va yi la, na vovototo gã aɖe le harem sukuwo ŋu. Elabena togbɔ be tɔmelã nyɔnu mawo nye esiwo vɔ̃na le nɔnɔme nu hã la, nyitsuvi siwo ƒe nɔnɔme le abe tɔmelã blaene ene, abe alesi woyɔnɛ ene la, nye esiwo wɔa avu wu Leviathantɔwo katã, eye le lododo me la, afɔku le wo me wu be woado go wo; negbe tɔmelã wɔnuku mawo siwo ƒe ta le ɣie, siwo ƒe amadede nye aŋutiɖiɖi, siwo doa go ɣeaɖewoɣi ko, eye amesiawo awɔ avu kpli wò abe gbɔgbɔ vɔ̃ dziŋɔ siwo do dziku le tohehe ƒe gout ta ene.

Suku siwo me nyitsu blaene le la lolo wu harem sukuwo. Abe sɔhɛ kɔledziviwo ƒe ameha ene la, dzre, modzakaɖeɖe, kple vɔ̃ɖinyenye yɔ wo me fũ, wole gegem ɖe xexeame katã le ŋumaɖɔɖo, fefenya me ale gbegbe be nugblẽfexeha si me nunya le aɖeke mawɔ nugblẽfexeɖoɖo na wo abe alesi wòawɔ nugblẽfexeɖoɖo na ŋutsuvi zitɔtɔ le Yale alo Harvard ene o. Gake eteƒe medidina hafi woɖea asi le zitɔtɔ sia ŋu o, eye ne wo dometɔ siwo ade etɔ̃ le ene ɖesiaɖe me tsi la, wogbãna, eye woɖea wo ɖokui ɖa le teƒe siwo woanɔ, si nye harems, didi.

Vovototo bubu si le ŋutsuwo kple nyɔnuwo ƒe sukuwo dome gakpɔtɔ nye ŋutsuwo kple nyɔnuwo ƒe nɔnɔme wu. Gblɔ be yeƒo nyitsu si ƒe agba blaene—abosam dahe! eƒe zɔhɛwo katã ɖe asi le eŋu. Gake ƒo harem sukua me tɔ aɖe, eye eƒe zɔhɛwo ƒua tsi ƒoa xlãe kple dzimaɖitsitsi ƒe dzesi ɖesiaɖe, ɣeaɖewoɣi nɔa egbɔ ale gbegbe eye wonɔa anyi didi ale gbegbe, abe woawo ŋutɔwo ene be yewoadze lã aɖe dzi.
