TA 115. Pequod Do Do Go Bachelor.

Eye nukpɔkpɔwo kple gbeɖiɖi siwo va nɔ ɖiɖim hafi ya ƒo, si nye kwasiɖa ʋɛ aɖewo le esi wotsɔ ga ƒo Axab ƒe akɔtaɖonua vɔ megbe la, do dzidzɔ na ame ŋutɔ.

Enye Nantucket meli aɖe, si nye Bachelor, si ƒo eɖokui ɖe eƒe amigo mamlɛtɔ me teti koe nye ema, eye wòtsɔ ga ƒo eƒe ʋɔtru siwo nɔ gbagbãm la; eye fifia, le dzidzɔkpɔkpɔ ƒe ŋkekenyuiwu me la, enɔ tsatsam le meli siwo dome didi ŋutɔ le anyigba la dome dzidzɔtɔe, togbɔ be dzodzro aɖee wònye hã, hafi fia asi eƒe ŋgɔgbe yi aƒeme.

Ŋutsu etɔ̃ siwo le eƒe mast-ta ŋu la doa afɔkpa dzĩ siwo le gbadzaa ƒe ʋuƒo legbewo ɖe woƒe kposɔwo ŋu; tso tɔdziʋua ƒe megbe la, wotsɔ tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale-tɔdziʋu aɖe tsi eƒe anyime; eye wokpɔ tɔmelã mamlɛtɔ si wowu la ƒe aɖu legbe si le ete la wònɔ xɔxlɔ̃m le atikplɔa ŋu. Dzesiwo, aflagawo, kple jack siwo ƒe amadede le vovovo la nɔ dzodzom tso eƒe rigging la me, le akpa sia akpa. Tsinyenye ƒe tsigoe eve nɔ eƒe kusi etɔ̃ siwo wotsɔ blae la dometɔ ɖesiaɖe ŋu le axadzi; si tame, le eƒe atitsoga-ti siwo le eƒe tame la, èkpɔ tsi xɔasi ma ke ƒe gbagbãƒe tsɛwo; eye wotsɔ akɔbli akaɖi aɖe klã ɖe eƒe agbatsɔʋu gã la ŋu.

Menye ɖeko wotsɔ nyilã kple abolo bablawo na be woatsɔ ana teƒe na tsinyenye si xɔ asi wu sã o, ke woɖɔli tsigoe bubu siwo wotsɔ kpe ɖe eŋu, tso meli siwo wòdo goe gbɔ; eye wodzra esiawo ɖo ɖe ʋua ƒe akpa si woɖoe ɖo la to, kple aʋafiagã kple asrafomegãwo ƒe dziɖuɖuxɔwo me. Woƒo xɔa ƒe kplɔ̃a ŋutɔ gɔ̃ hã wòzu dzodoƒe; eye ʋua me tɔtɔ la ɖu ami ƒe ta gbadzaa, si wotsɔ ƒo ɖe anyigba hena titinanu aɖe. Le ŋgɔgbexɔa me la, ƒudzidelawo tsɔ caulk kple woƒe akɔta ƒu gbe ŋutɔŋutɔ, eye woyɔ wo me fũ; wotsɔ nukokoedoname gblɔ kpee, be nuɖala la ƒo ta ɖe eƒe dzodoƒe gãtɔ kekeake dzi, eye wòyɔe fũ; be aƒedzikpɔla la do eƒe kɔfize si susɔ la hede eme; be saŋkuƒolawo tsɔ ta ɖo woƒe gayibɔwo ƒe afɔtiwo eye woyɔ wo me fũ; be le nyateƒe me la, tsinyenye yɔ nusianu me fũ, negbe ʋumefia la ƒe pantaloons kotokuwo, kple esiwo wòdzra ɖo ɖi be yeatsɔ yeƒe asiwo ade eme, le eƒe dzidzeme bliboa ƒe ɖaseɖiɖi si me dziɖeleameƒo le me.

Esi dzɔgbenyui ƒe meli sia si me dzidzɔ le la nɔ gegem ɖe Pequod si ƒe dzi gbã la, kpẽ gãwo ƒe ɖiɖi si nye amedzrowɔwɔ la nɔ ɖiɖim tso eƒe ŋgɔgbekpa dzi; eye esi wogagogoe la, wokpɔ eƒe ŋutsuwo ƒe ha aɖe wotsi tre ƒo xlã eƒe try-ze gã siwo wotsɔ tɔmelã yibɔa ƒe poke alo ƒodo ƒe ŋutigbalẽ si le abe lãgbalẽ ene tsyɔ la, doa ɣli sesĩe le ʋua me dɔwɔlawo ƒe asi siwo wobla ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu ƒe ƒoƒo ɖesiaɖe me. TA 116. Whale si le Kukum.

Menye ƒã hafi le agbe sia me o, ne, le ɖusime la, dzɔgbenyui ƒe lã siwo wòlɔ̃na wu la zɔa tɔdziʋu le mía gbɔ la, togbɔ be mí katã míeɖiɖi do ŋgɔ hã la, míeléa ya si ƒona sesĩe la ƒe akpa aɖe, eye míesenɛ le mía ɖokui me dzidzɔtɔe be míaƒe kotoku me tɔdziʋuwo yɔ fũ. Ŋdɔ me ʋĩ ke; eye esi wowu aʋawɔwɔ dzĩ la ƒe akplɔwo katã nu: eye wonɔ tsia dzi le ɣeɖoto atsiaƒu kple dziŋgɔli lɔlɔ̃a me la, ɣe kple tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale siaa ku ɖekae; emegbe, vivi kple nuxaxa ale gbegbe, orison siwo ƒo xlã wo ale gbegbe, siwo ƒo xlãe le ya ma si me dzo le me, ale gbegbe be edze abe ɖe Spaintɔwo ƒe anyigbadziya si trɔ zu ƒudzidela le vome la yi ƒua dzi, eye wotsɔ vesper-ha siawo tsɔ agbae ene kloe.

“Etrɔna hetrɔnɛ ɖe eŋu,—aleke gbegbe wòwɔa nu blewui, gake aleke gbegbe wòwɔa nu maʋãmaʋãe nye si, eƒe bubudedeameŋu kple yɔyɔ ƒe mo, kple eƒe ʋuʋu mamlɛtɔ siwo le kukum. “Oo, trebly hooped and welded hip of power! Oo, high aspiring, rainbowed jet!—ame ma dzea agbagba, amesia jettest nusianu dzodzro! TA 117. Whale Dzɔla.
