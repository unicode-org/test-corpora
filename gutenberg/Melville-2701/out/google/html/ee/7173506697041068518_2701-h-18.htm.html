TA 89. Tɔmelã kabakaba kple Tɔmelã si me woɖea lã le.

Nya si wogblɔ tso waif kple waif-tiwo ŋu le ta mamlɛtɔ me gake ɖeka ko, bia be woagblɔ tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale lãɖeɖe ƒe sewo kple ɖoɖo aɖewo, siwo ƒe dzesi kple akɔtagbalẽvi gãtɔ ate ŋu abu.

Edzɔna enuenu be ne meli geɖe le zɔzɔm ɖekae la, tɔdziʋu ɖeka ate ŋu aƒo tɔmelã aɖe, emegbe wòasi, eye tɔdziʋu bubu awui mlɔeba ahalée; eye afisiae wowɔ nudzɔdzɔ suesuesue geɖe siwo ate ŋu adzɔ le mɔ si mele tẽ o nu, siwo katã kpɔa gome le nɔnɔme gã ɖeka sia me. Le kpɔɖeŋu me,—le tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale yometiti kple eléle si ŋu ɖeɖi te eye afɔku le eme megbe la, ame kukua ate ŋu aɖe asi le melia ŋu le ahom sesẽ aɖe ta; eye ne wole dzodzom le didiƒe ʋĩ yina yame la, tɔmelãléla evelia agbugbɔe axɔ, amesi, le tomefafa me, heae ɖe axadzi nyuie, evɔ agbe alo fli aɖeke manɔ eme o. Eyata nyaʋiʋli siwo doa dziku na ame wu eye ŋutasẽnuwɔwɔ le wu la ado mo ɖa le tɔƒodelawo dome zi geɖe, ne menye se aɖe si woŋlɔ alo si womeŋlɔ o, si nye xexeame katã tɔ, si ŋu womeʋlia nya le o, si ku ɖe nyawo katã ŋu meli o.

Ðewohĩ tɔmelãwo léle ŋuti se ɖeka kolia si ŋu sedede ɖe mɔ ɖo le se nu, enye Holland tɔ. Dukɔ Gãwoe de se nɛ le ƒe 1695 M.Ŋ. II. Loose-Fish nye fefe si me dzɔdzɔenyenye le na amesiame si ate ŋu alée kaba wu.

Gake nusi wɔa nu gbegblẽ si le aɖaŋudzedze ƒe se sia ŋue nye eƒe kpuiƒe si dze kafukafu, si hiã be woaɖe numeɖeɖe gbogbo aɖewo atsɔ aɖe eme.

Gbãtɔ: Nukae Nye Tɔmelã si Wotsɔna Klɔa Nu Kabakaba? Tɔmelã si le agbe alo esi ku la zɔna kabakaba le mɔ̃ɖaŋununya nu, ne wotsɔe do ka kple meli alo tɔdziʋu si me amewo le, to mɔnu ɖesiaɖe si dzi emela alo emenɔlawo ate ŋu akpɔ ŋusẽ ɖo kura dzi,—akɔta, tɔdziʋu, ka si ƒe didime nye sentimeta enyi, telegraf ka, alo ʋetsuvi ƒe ka, wo katã wole ɖeka. Nenema ke tɔmelã hã ƒua du le mɔ̃ɖaŋununya gome ne etsɔ waif, alo dzesi bubu ɖesiaɖe si wonya be ele ame si; zi alesi akpa si le asi ɖem le eŋu la ɖe woƒe ŋutete fia kɔte be yewoatsɔe akpe ɖe eŋu ɣesiaɣi, kpakple woƒe tameɖoɖo be yewoawɔe nenema.

Esiawo nye dzɔdzɔmeŋutinunya me numeɖeɖewo; gake ɣeaɖewoɣi la, tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whalemen ŋutɔ ƒe numeɖeɖewo nyea nya sesẽwo kple ƒoƒo sesẽwo—si nye Coke-upon-Littleton of the fist. Nyateƒee, le tɔmelã siwo le dzɔdzɔe wu eye bubu le wo ŋu dome la, wonaa gakpekpeɖeŋu ɣesiaɣi ɖe nya tɔxɛwo ta, afisi anye agbenyuinɔnɔ ƒe madzɔmadzɔnyenye dzikutɔe be akpa ɖeka nagblɔ be tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale si akpa bubu ti yome alo wu tsã la le ye si. Gake ame bubuwo ya meɖɔa ŋu ɖo nenema kura o.

Anɔ abe ƒe blaatɔ̃ enye sia la, wotsɔ nya ɖe tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale-trover ŋu ​​ƒe nya aɖe si me wodi be yewoanya nu tsoe ŋu le England, si me nyadrɔ̃lawo gblɔe le be le tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale yome titi sesĩe le Dziehe atsiaƒuwo me megbe; eye esime woawo (nyadrɔ̃lawo) kpɔ dzidzedze le tɔmelãawo ƒoƒo me vavã; wova hiã mlɔeba, to woƒe agbe ƒe afɔku me, be menye woƒe fliwo ɖeɖekoe wogblẽ ɖi o, ke boŋ woƒe tɔdziʋua ŋutɔ hã. Mlɔeba la, amesiwo ŋu wotsɔ nya ɖo (meli bubu me dɔwɔlawo) va to tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale vɛ, woƒoe, wui, xɔe, eye mlɔeba woxɔe le amesi tsɔ nya ɖe eŋu la ŋkume. Eye esi wotsi tre ɖe amesiwo ŋu wotsɔ nya ɖo mawo ŋu la, woƒe ʋumefia tsɔ eƒe asibidɛwo ƒo ɖe nyadrɔ̃lawo ƒe aɖuwo me, eye wòka ɖe edzi na wo be to doxology me na agbalẽ si yewɔ la, yealé woƒe ka, akplɔwo, kple tɔdziʋu, siwo gakpɔtɔ lé ɖe tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale ŋu le ɣeyiɣi si me woxɔe la ɖe asi azɔ. Eyata nyadrɔ̃lawo tsɔ nya ɖe wo ŋu azɔ be woaxɔ yewoƒe tɔmelã, ka, harpoon, kple tɔdziʋu ƒe asixɔxɔ.

Aƒetɔ Erskine nye amesiwo ŋu wotsɔ nya ɖo ƒe senyala; Lord Ellenborough ye nye ʋɔnudrɔ̃la la. Le nyaʋiʋlia me la, Erskine si nye nukokoedonamela la yi edzi wɔ eƒe nukpɔsusu ƒe kpɔɖeŋu, esi wòƒo nu tso nuvlowɔwɔ aɖe si dzɔ nyitsɔ laa ŋu. con. nya, si me aƒetɔ aɖe, esi wòdze agbagba dzodzro be yeaxe mɔ ɖe srɔ̃a ƒe ŋutasẽnuwɔwɔ nu vɔ megbe la, egblẽe ɖi mlɔeba le agbe ƒe atsiaƒuwo dzi; gake le ƒeawo me la, esi wòtrɔ dzime tso afɔɖeɖe ma me la, eɖo afɔɖeɖe aɖe anyi be yeagaxɔe. Erskine nɔ akpa kemɛ; eye emegbe eɖo kpe edzi esi wògblɔ be, togbɔ be aƒetɔa tsɔ akplɔ ƒo aƒenɔa le gɔmedzedzea me, eye wòtsi nu dɔ ɣeaɖeɣi, eye le eƒe vɔ̃ɖinyenye si nɔ gegem ɖe eme ƒe nuteɖeamedzi gã ta ko hã la, egblẽe ɖi mlɔeba; ke hã egblẽe ɖi, ale be wòzu tɔmelã si me woɖea asi le; eye eyata esi aƒetɔ aɖe si kplɔe ɖo gatsɔ aŋe ƒoe la, aƒenɔa va zua aƒetɔ ma si kplɔe ɖo ƒe nunɔamesi emegbe, tsɔ kpe ɖe akplɔ ɖesiaɖe si woate ŋu akpɔ be elé ɖe eŋu la ŋu.

Fifia le nya si me míele fifia me la, Erskine ʋli nya be tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale kple aƒenɔa ƒe kpɔɖeŋuwo nye wo nɔewo ƒe kpɔɖeŋu siwo sɔ kple wo nɔewo.

Esi wose nya siawo, kple nya siwo wotsɔ tsi tre ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu, esi wose ʋɔnudrɔ̃la agbalẽnyala ŋutɔ la le nya siwo woɖo ɖi me tso nya me, si nye,—Be le tɔdziʋua gome la, yetsɔe de asi na nyadrɔ̃lawo, elabena ɖeko wogblẽe ɖi be yewoaɖe yewoƒe agbe; gake le tɔmelã si ŋu nyaʋiʋli le, harpoon, kple line gome la, wonye amesiwo ŋu wotsɔ nya ɖo la tɔ; tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale, elabena Loose-Fish ye wònye le ɣeyiɣi si me wolée zi mamlɛtɔ; kple harpoons kple line elabena ne tɔmelãa dzo kpli wo la, eya (tɔmelãa) xɔa nunɔamesi aɖe le nu mawo me; eye eyata gome le amesiame si axɔ tɔmelãawo emegbe la si ɖe wo ŋu. Fifia amesiwo ŋu wotsɔ nya ɖo la xɔ tɔmelãawo emegbe; ergo, nyati siwo míeyɔ va yi la nye wo tɔ.

Ame tsɛ aɖe si le ŋku lém ɖe Ʋɔnudrɔ̃la agbalẽnyala ŋutɔ ƒe nyametsotso sia ŋu, ate ŋu atsi tre ɖe eŋu. Gake ne woƒãe va ɖo nyaa ƒe kpe vevitɔ gbɔ la, gɔmeɖose gã eve siwo woɖo ɖe tɔmelãwo léle ƒe se vevi siwo míeyɔ va yi me, eye Lord Ellenborough zã wo heɖe eme le nya si míeyɔ ɖe etame me; megblɔ be se eve siawo siwo ku ɖe Fast-Fish kple Loose-Fish ŋu la, ne wobu eŋu la, woakpɔe be wonye amegbetɔ ƒe senyawo katã ƒe gɔmeɖoanyiwo; elabena togbɔ be kpememe ƒe nɔnɔmetata siwo gɔme sese sesẽ hã la, nu eve koe le Se Gbedoxɔa ŋu abe Filistitɔwo ƒe Gbedoxɔa ene si dzi wòatsi tre ɖo.

Ðe menye nya si wogblɔna le ame sia ame ƒe nu me be, Nutɔnyenye nye se la ƒe afã o: si fia be, eɖanye aleke kee nu la va xɔe oa? Gake zi geɖe la, nunɔamesiwo ƒe amesinɔnɔ nyea se bliboa. Nukae nye asiɖeɖe le nu gbegblẽ si Mardoxai, si nye asitsala la, xɔna tso Woebegone dahe, si nye gaxɔmenɔla, gbɔ le gadodo aɖe me be yeatsɔ ana dɔ nagawu Woebegone ƒe ƒomea o; nukae nye asiɖeɖe le nu gbegblẽ ma ŋu negbe Fast-Fish?
