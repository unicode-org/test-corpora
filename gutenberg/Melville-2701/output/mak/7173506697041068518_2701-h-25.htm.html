Anjo dramaya lebaki. Angngapa na anrinni rie' tau a'dakka mange ri dallekang?—Nasaba' rie' tau salama' battu ri ancuru'na.

Kebetulan, ribokoangna Parsee tappela, inakke iami antu tau ni tunjukkan ri Fates untu angngalle tampa pana Ahab, ri wattu anjo pana angngallei posisi kosong; ia to'ji, ia, ri wattu allo kala'busang anjo tallua tau nipela' battu ri kappala' a'goyang, nipela' ri boko. Jari, a'layang ri biring adegan ribokoangna, siagang ri pa'cinikang sukku'na, ri wattu se'reang pa'se'reang kappala' ancuru' battu ri nakke, inakke anjo wattua, mingka lambat-lambat, nitari' mange ri pusaran pa'tutu'. Ri wattungku battu mange anjoreng, anjo tampaka a'jari kolam krim.
