Kinde ma awotho, cwinya upong ku paru; gin ma jular jukoro ira ikum Captain Ahab, upongu ira ku lembe moko ma nyayu can ikume. THE 17. Ramadan.

Calu Queequeg's Ramadan, nyoke kec man lewic, umitu umediri ceng' zoo, agam ayero ngo ni nyayu peko i kume nitundu uthieno; pi an amaro woro madit ikum tic pa dhanu ceke mi dini, kadi bed ni lembe ma nyayu ngyeru, man acopo nwangu ngo i cwinya ni ketho piny ma nok kadi dul pa thwol ma woro toad-stool; nyo lei mange ma gikwo i kabedu moko mi ng'om mwa, ma ku rwom ma malu mi timo lembe ma rwinyo ngo i ng'om mange, guro piny i wang' ngati ma tho ma won ngom pi piny ma lee ma gitiye kudu man gipango i nyinge.

Awacu, wan Jukristu maber mi Presbyterian umaku wabed ju ma timo berocwiny i lembe eni, man kud wabed ku nen pa dhanu mange, ju ma gi juyic man mange, pi paru migi ma rwinyo ikum lembe eni. Queequeg ubedu nuti, kawoni, ubedu ka ketho paru ma rac ikum Yojo man Ramadan pare;—ento ango ikum eno? Queequeg uparu niya engeyo ango ma ebedu ka weco pire, aparu; nen calu ebedo kud anyonga; man keca wek eyom. Pyem mwa kude zo bibedo ber ungo; wek ebed, awacu: man polo ubed ku kisa ikumwa zo—Presbyterians man Pagans marom—pien wan ceke wabedo ku tur ma rac ikum wiwa, man wabemito juyik.

Cwinya utal ni neno ka ucungo iwi ot cinge pa Queequeg's harpoon, ma won ot utingo kud i bange uthieno mukadho, mapodi wamondo ngo i ot. Eni lembe manyen, aparu; ento kadok kumeno de, calu ma harpoon ucungu keca, man ecidho ngo iwi ngom mange manangu epe kude, ci umitu ebed i ot keni, man epe ku lembe moku ma copo timere.

Ka aringo piny kud i wi ot, aloko pio pio paru para ni ngati mir acel ma aromo kudu—jatic mi ot. “La! la!” ekok kumae, “Aparu nia lembe moko nuti ma utimere. Acidho ni yiko kavutu i ng’ei cam mi diewor, man dhugola ubedo ma juketho; man ng’atu moko mbe ma copo winjo; man ebedo ling’ ling’ niai i saa maeca. Ento aparu, copo bedo, wun ario zoo wucidho man wuketho piny wu i ot pi gwokogi maber. La! la, ma’am!—Murs! apoplexy!”—man ku ywak eni, eringo i ot tedo, an alube.

Adok piny pi cam mi diewor. Acaku bedo ku ngei ikume; nen calu lembe ma rwinyo man ma rwinyo ni bedo keca ceng' man nusu dyewor iwi kore i ot ma ngic, maku yen iwi wiye.

"Pi polo, Queequeg, ay malu man yeng giri; ay malu man icam cam mi diewor. Ibi tho ku kec; ibe nek giri, Queequeg." Ento egam edwoko ngo lembe moko.

Pieno, pi bedo ku gen ikume, amoko paru mi cidho i kavutu man nindu; man labongo jiji, mapodi kare malac ukadhu, ecopo lubo tokgi. Par ikume; nindo dyewor zo i ot acel ku ngati mu'a ma ungiyu iwi cinge i Ramadan ma rac man ma dhanu copo dwoko wange ngo!

Ento yore moku aweko tic, man angeyu piny moko ope nitundu ceng wok; kinde ma, ka waneno iwi kavutu, Queequeg ubedo piny, calu ka juwodhe piny i diere. Ento ndhundhu ma ceng umondo i wang dirisa, e ay malu, ku cinge ma tek man ma nyai, ento ku wang ma kud anyonga; edaru bino ira kama abedo i iye; edwogo wiye kendu ikum para; man ewacu niya Ramadan pare uthum.

I ngeye apenju Queequeg ka en gire de ebedo ku peko mi camu ic; nyuthu paru ne maleng, kara ecopu jolo. Ewecu ungo; kende kende i saa acel ma pire tek. Ubedo ingey foc madit ma won ot pare ubimo umio, i ngei lwiny madit ma dhanu pyer abic utho i saa ario mi diewor, man jutedo man jucamu uthieno eca.

I ngei lembe zo, aparu ngo niya wec para ikum dini ukelo aloka loka madit ikum Queequeg. Pien, i acaki, en nen calu ewinjo ngo lembe ma pire tek eno, kawang ka juporo ku paru pare gire; man, i kabedu mir aryo, engeyu ngo ikum acel ikum adek nyang ikum lembe para, eketho paru para yot kite ma amito; man, i ajiki, ebed eparu niya engeyo lembe madwong ikum dini mandha makadhu an. Enena ku lworo man kisa, calu nya eparu niya etiye lembe mi can lee dit nia awobi ma riek ma kumeno ucikere ni rwenyo kwo pa evangelical pagan piety.

I ajiki wa ay malu man wa ruke; man Queequeg, ma ucamu cam miwikugweno ma leng pa chowders matung tung, kara won ot kud unwang bero madwong pi Ramadan pare, wawok woko pi mondo i Pequod, wawothu, man wakwanyo lak mwa ku cogo pa halibut.

CHAPTER 19. Jakwenda.

“Ju ma wabewotho kugi i yei, wuoro i yei meno?”

Queequeg ku an wawok kud i Pequod, man wabedu kawothu cen kud i pii, pi kare ma ngati acel acel ubedo ku paru pare gire, kinde ma wec ma malu eca juweco iwa ku ngati moko ma wangeyo ongo, ma, ucungu i nyimwa, eketho cinge madit ikum yei ma juweco pire. “Iseoro i iye?” edwoko kendo.

"Ibeweco ikum yei ma julwongo ni Pequod, aparu," awacu, ka temo ni nwangu kare manok mi nenu kude.

"Eyo, Pequod-meli eca," ewacu, edwoko cinge zo cen, man edham ewotho woko pio pio kud i bange, ku bayonet ma ucungo ku cinge ma ucungo ma ucungo iwi piny ne.

“Lembe moko nuti piny keca ikum tipo peri?”
