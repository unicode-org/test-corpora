CHAPTER 10. Jahera ma cwinye tek.

Ka adok i Spouter-Inn kud i Chapel, anwango Queequeg keca kende; edaru weko ot lega ma podi foc ungo pi kare moko. Ebedo iwi kom i wang mac, ku tyende iwi mac, man ku cinge acel egam emaku wange nyathigi macol pare; eneno matek i wange, man ku jack-knife ma ucungo iwi ume, man ebedo ka wero ire gire i yo pare ma rwinyo.

Ento kawoni dong jupoko lembe pare, eketho cal ne malu; man ndhundhu, ecidho i meza, etingo buku madit keca, man ekethe iwi tyende ecaku kwanu karatasi ku rwom ma malu; i mbaya acel acel mi pyerabic—calu ma aparu—cungu nyanok, aneno piny ma nen ungo i ngete, man emiyo wec ni dwand ma ucungo pi kare malac. Ecopo caku kendu i sawa pyer abic mukende; nen calu ecaku ku namba acel kare ku kare, calu nya ecopo kwanu ngo makadhu pyer abic, man ubedu kende kende pi wel ma dit pa pyer abic ma ju nwango karacelu, ma uketho lworo pare ikum wel pa karatasi ubedo kud anyonga.

Ku miti madit abedo ka nenu. Kadi bende ebedo ngati ma kwiny, man wange nen marac akeca—i paru para—wange de ubedu ku piny moko ma nyayu can i iye. Icopo kano ngo tipo. Ikind cal pare ma nen ungo, aparu niya aneno lanyuth pa cwiny ma atira; man i wange malac, ma thuc, macol man ma tek, nen calu lanyuth pa tipo ma copo ketho ju ma rac elufu acel. Man ikum lembe eni zo, ubedu ku paru moko ma malu ikum Pagan, ma kadi bedu pare marac de copo nyayu peko ikume. Enen ni ngati ma podi epe ku lworo man ma podi epe ku jumaku banja. Ka nyo ebedo, bende, nia wiye juyike, wiye juwodhe woko i yo ma yot man ma ler, man nen ma lac uloyu ma sagu kite ma ecopo bedu kudu, eni abeciro ngo; ento andha ubedu wic pare maber akeca. Ecopu nen ni lembe mi ming, ento epoi wiya wic pa General Washington, calu ma nen i cal pare ma dhanu zo maru. Ebedo ku rwom marom ma bor ma ucungo iwi wic, ma de ubedu malu, calu got ario ma bor ma upong ku yen iwi wiye. Queequeg ubedo George Washington ma juyiko ku cing dhanu.

Kinde ma abedo ka ngiyo kude, man atimo nya ebedu ka neno yamu kud i ot, ewinjo ngo bedo para, ebed eparu ngo ikume kende kende; ento nen calu etiye ka kwanu karatasi mi buku maber. Ka waparu kite ma wabedu ka nindu kudu karacelu dyewor mukadho, man ka waparu ikum cing ma ber ma anwango ka jubolo iwiya ikare ma acer uthieno, aparu niya bedo pare ma epe ku paru eni ubedu piny ma nen ungo. Ento ju ma kwiny gi dhanu ma nen ungo; saa moko ingeyo ngo kite mi tingo gi. I acaki gibedo ku lworo; bedo migi ma ling man ma yot nen calu ryeko pa Socratic. Abedo ka neno bende nia Queequeg ubedu ngo ku winjiri moko ci, nyo manok kende, ku jutic mi nam mukende i ot welo. Etimo ngo lembe moku cii; nen calu epe ku miti mi medo wel pa ju ma engeyo. Lembe eni zo uketho cwinya tek; ento, i paru mir aryo, ubedu piny moko ma pire tek i iye. Eni ubedo jadhum moko ma bor ku pacu pare pi mayils 20,000, i yo mi Cape Horn, ma eni—ma ubedo yo acel kende ma ecopu tuc kudu—jubolo ikind dhanu ma engeyo ngo ire calu ka nwango ebedo i ngom mi Jupiter; man podi enen ni ebedo agonya; gwoko kuc ma dit; bedo moth ku jurimo pare gire; kare ku kare rom ku en gire. Andha eni ubedu lembe mi ryeko maber; kadi nangu epe ku jiji nya ewinjo ngo nia lembe ma kumeno utiye. Ento, nyalo bedu, ni bedo ju ma rieko mandha, wan dhanu ucikere ngo ni bedo ku ngec mi kwo nyo mi kero. Ka dong awinjo nia ngati moko ci miyo kwo pare ni jafilosofi, agam anwango nya, calu dhaku ma muti ma remo pare nyai, ecicopere nia ebed "uturo digester pare."

Kinde ma abedo keca i ot ma dong ling; mac ma wango piny, i rwom ma yot, ingey tego pare mir acel udaru lyethu yamu, ecaku wang kende kende ni neno; ler mi diewor man tipo ma gicokiri i dhu cula, man gineno iwa wan ario ma ling man ma wabedo kendgi; yamu ma tek ma ucwir ungo; Acaku bedo ku paru ma tung tung. Awinjo lyethu i iya. Adunde para ma nyai man cinga ma nyai ugam udok ikum ng'om ma nen calu thwol. Savage ma kweyo cwiny eni ugam ugoye. Ebedo keca, bedo pare ma nyang ikum lembe moko ubeweco ikum kura ma epe ku timo mi ndra man ndra ma rwinyo. Ebedo jalwor; nen pa piny maber mi nenu; ento acaku nwangu nia atie ku mer ikume. Man piny marom ma copo nyayu lworo i dhanu mange, eca re ma ubedu magnet ma uketho abedo ku lworo. Abitemo jarimba moko ma japagani, aparu, pi berocwiny pa Jukristu unyutho bedo ber mandha. Aketo kom para ceng kude, man atimo lanyuth moko maber, atimo kero para mi wecu kude ikind kare meno. I acaki, eneno ngo lembe maeni; ento kawoni, ikare ma aloko ikum rwoth pare mi diewor ma ukadhu, egam epenja ka nyo wabecidho kendu ni bedo karacelu. Awacu ire ee; kama aparu nya ebedo kud anyonga, nyo jufoye nyanok.

I ngeye waloko buku ne karacelu, man atembu ni tito ire the lembe ma uketho juwodho buku ne, man the lembe pa cal manok ma ubedu i iye. Pieno, aloko paru pare pio pio; man kud i iye wacidho ka weco maber akeca ikum piny ma woko ma nen i adhura ma pire tek eni. I ngeye nyanok, adham timo lembe moko; man, ewodho cinge man tomahawk, emiyo ira kongo moth moth. Man i ngeye wabedo piny ka loko yamo kud i pipe pare macol, man wabedo ka kadhu ikindwa kare ku kare.

Ka podi ice moko ma nyayu lworo ikuma ubedu nuti i iye pa Pagan, iro maber man ma ber ma wabedu kudu, edaru wok woko, man eweko wabedo juwadi. Ebedo nen calu egam egamu an maber man m'umbe lwongo calu an bende amaru en; man kinde ma iro mwa uthum, edham eketho wiye ikum para, egam etingo kora i kore, man ewacu niya nicaku kawoni wabe nyom; the lembe, i wec pa ngom pare, nia wabedo jurimo ma ceng; ecopo tho pi an kud anyonga, tek mitire. I kind dhanu mi ngom, mac ma nyai pa jurimo eni nen calu piny ma podi rwinyo ngo, piny ma dhanu copo yio ngo; ento i savage ma yot eni cik macon eca copo tiyo ngo.

I ngei cam mi diewor, man wec mange man madhu taba, wacidho i ot mwa karacelu. Etimo ira mic pa wiye ma juyiko; utingo osau pare madit mi taba, man edaru mako piny pa taba, ewodho sente ma romo dola pyer adek mi silva; man epoko gi iwi mesa, man epoko gi i dul aryo marom, ecwalu acel ikin gi ira, man ewacu niya etiye para. Abedu ka timo lembe marac; ento edwoko ling ira ku cwalo gi i cinga. Aweko gibedo. Egam ecidho i rwo pare mi diewor, ewodho cal pare, man ewodho karatasi mi mac. Ku lanyuth moko man lanyuth moko, aparu niya ebedo ku lworo nia adok i bange; ento ngeyo maber ango ma ubetimere, aparu pi kare manok ka, tek elwonga, abitimo nyo epe.

Abedo Jakristu maber; junyole man judongo i kor kanisa mi Presbyterian ma rwinyo ngo. Dong acopo bedo karacelu nenedi ku jajok maeni mi woro yen pare? Ento woro ubedo ango? aparu. Iparu kawoni, Ishmael, nia Mungu ma dit mi polo ku ng'om—ju ma gi juyic man dhanu ceke—copo bedu ku lworo ikum yen macol ma nok? Larire ngo! Ento woro ubedo ango?—timo yeny pa Mungu— eno ubedo woro. Man yeny pa Mungu utiye ango?—ni timo ni dhanu wadwa gin ma amito dhanu wadwa gitim ira— eno ubedo yeny pa Mungu. Kawoni, Queequeg ubedo wadi. Man ango ma amito nya Queequeg eni utim ira? Pirango, bed karacelu ku an i kit woro para mi Presbyterian. Kumeno, umitu abed acel kude i pare; ergo, umitu adok ja woro mungu. Ci awango shavings; ukonyo ni ketho jamb timo moko ma rac ungo; umiye mugati ma juwango ku Queequeg; juyero ire wang aryo nyo adek; edham ume; man ka dong jutimo eno, waweko bongo man wacidho i kavutu, ku kuc ku paru mwa man ku ng'om zoo. Ento wacidho ngo nindu manangu wabeweco ngo.

Angeyo ngo kite ma etiye kudu; ento kabedu moko epe ma calu kavutu pi nyuthu lembe ma pire tek ikind jurimo. Co ku dhaku, giwacu, giyabu cwiny migi ni ngati acel acel; man juru ot moko ma dit pol kare gi goyo ndra man giweco ikum lembe macon nitundu uthieno. Pieno, i nindo mi nyom mwa, an ku Queequeg wabedu—ju aryo ma ber man ma maru dhanu.





CHAPTER 11. Nightgown.

Wabed wabedo i kavutu, wabeweco man wabedu ka nindu i kare manok, man Queequeg kawoni ku ngeye ebolo tyende macol ma jugoro iwi para, man edwogo gi cen; wabedo dhanu ma maru bedo karacelu man ma bedo agonya man ma yot; kinde ma, i ajikine, pi lembe mi wec mwa, nappishness ma nok ma udong i iwa uthum, man wawinjo nia wabe ay malu kendu, kadi nang ceng ubedu piny i anyim.

Eyo, wabedo ma wangwa twolo; ma lee dit ma kabedo mwa mi vutu ucaku doko tek, man nyanok nyanok wanwango nia wabedo malo; bongo ne ucungo ikumwa, ucungo ikum wi-board ku tyendwa angwen ma ucungo ikumgi, man um mwa ario ucungo iwi gi, calu ka kneepans mwa ubedo warming-pans. Wawinjere maber man ma ling, mandha ne ikum piny ma ngic woko; andha kud i bongo mi kavutu de, nineno nia mac ubedu pee i ot. Man de, awacu, kum andha pi nwangu lyethu mi kum, thenge moko ma nok ikum peri umaku obed ma ngic, kum epe kura moko i ng'om maeni ma epe ku kite ma etiye kudu. Piny moko mbe ma nwangre kende. Ka ibedo kud anyonga ikum giri nia ibedo maber, man ibedo maber pi kare malac, eca dong jucopo yero ngo niya ibedo maber kendu. Ento ka, calu Queequeg man an i kavutu, wi um peri nyo wic peri ubedo ma ngic nyanok, pirango, andha, i paru peri zo iwinjo maber man ma mor. Pi the lembe eni ot ma dhanu bedo i iye ucikere ngo ni bedu ku mac, ma en e acel ikin piny ma dhanu ma lonyo gi bedo kudu. Pi rwom ma malu pa kit leng pa piny eni utiye ni bedu ku piny moko cii ento bongo peri man ngico pa yamu ma woko. I ngeye ibedo calu mac acel ma lyeth i adunde pa arctic crystal.

Wabedo piny i yo maeni pi kare moko, ma kawoni aparu niya abe yabo wanga; kum ka abedo i kind bongo, kadi dieceng nyo dyewor, man kadi anindu nyo acer, atiye ku yore mi gwoko wanga nja, kara abed ku kero mi bedo i kavutu. Pien ngati moko ope ma copo winjo kite pare gire maber kende kende wange ung'eyere ngo; calu nya mudho ubedo piny ma pire tek i kwo mwa, kadi nya lero ubedo piny maber uloyu ikum piny mwa macol. I kare ma ayabo wanga, man awok kud i mudho para ma ber man ma atimo pira kena i mudho ma woko ma juketho man ma rac pa sawa apar wi aryo mi diewor, anwango lworo ma rac. Man de agam akwero ngo lembe ma Queequeg uwacu nia ecopo bedu ber ni ketho ler, pi nineno nia wabedu ma wangeyo; man ikum eca, ewinjo miti madit mi bedo ku yamo manok ma ling kud i Tomahawk pare. Bed nya, kadi nangu awinjo marac ikum madhu taba pare i kavutu dyewor ma ukadhu, ento nen kite ma paru mwa ma tek udongu kudu ka mar bino ni jwigo gi. Kawoni dong amaru ngo piny moko maber uloyu ma sagu bedu ku Queequeg ma ube madhu taba, kadi i kavutu, kum enen ni epong ku mutoro mi ot ma ling. Abedo ngo ku lworo ma lee pi cik pa won ot mi insurance. Abedo kwo kende kende pi bedo maber pa poko paip man blanket ku jarimba mandha. Ku koti mwa ma nyap ma ucungo iwi gok mwa, kawoni wakadhu Tomahawk kud acel ni mukende, nitundo moth moth piny moko macol ma ucungu iwiwa, ma ucwale ku mac pa tara ma nyen.

Ka nyo ebedo nya jatem eca utingo jajok ne cen i kabedu mabor, angeyo ongo, ento kawoni eweco ikum cula pare ma junyole i iye; man, ku miti madit mi winjo lembe pare, akwaye nya emediri ku wecu. En eyio ku mutoro. Kadok i kare meno nangu ang'eyo ngo lembe pare manok, ento lembe ma enyutho i ngeye, kinde ma dong' ang'eyo lembe pare ma rwinyo, kawoni konyowa ni nyuthu lembe ceke calu ma ecopu nyutho i skeleton ma amiyo.





THEK 12. Lembe mi kwo pa dhanu.

Queequeg ubedo jadongo pa Rokovoko, cula moko mabor ku yo reto man yo piny. Epe piny i map moko ci; kabedo mandha epe.

Kinde ma lei moko ma nyen ube ringo iwi lum pare i lum, man diegi ma ube camu, calu nya etiye yen macol; kadi bende, i cwiny pa Queequeg ma cwinye tek, ubedu ku miti madit mi neno piny moko ma pire tek pa Jukristu ma sagu jadwar moko nyo aryo. Won ot pare ubedo jadit madit, rwoth; nera pare ubedo jalam madit; man i thenge pa min ot, ebed enyayu aunts ma gibedo mon pa jukwenda ma jucopo loyo ngo. Rimo maber ubedu i ler pare—piny pa rwoth; kadi nya edoko rac, lworo, pi kura mi camu dhanu ma enwango i kare ma fodi etiye aradu.

Meli moko mi Sag Harbor ucidho i nam pa wone, man Queequeg uyenyo yo mi cidho i ngom pa Jukristu. Ento yei, ma ubedu ku dhanu mapol ma giwotho i nam, ugam ukwero bongo pare; man rwoth pa won ot pare ceke copo loyo ngo. Ento Queequeg utimo lembariba. En kende i yei pare, ecidho i nam mabor, ma engeyo nia yei ucikere kadhu kud i iye ka eweko cula. I thenge acel ubedu lum ma julwongo ni coral reef; I thenge mange, ng’om ma piny, ma juketho i ie lum ma dongo dongo ma dongo i pii. E kano yei pare, ma podi ucungo iwi pii, ikind lum eni, ku kore yo nam, ebedo piny i ngeye, ku yei piny i cinge; man kinde ma yei ubedo ka ringo, calu ler ewok woko; unwangu thenge pare; ku ngwec acel ma ngeye pa tyende epodho man epodho yei pare; utingo iwi nyoru; man ebolo kume iwi yei, etingo ring-bolt keca, man ekwonge nia ecopo weko ngo ecidh, kadi nya jutwoye.

M'umbe kony, jatel wi yei ugam ukwayu nya ebibole i nam; eketho cutlass iwi cinge ma ling; Queequeg ubedo wod pa rwoth, man Queequeg ugam ucungo ngo. Ku lworo pa lworo pare, man mitu pare mi limo dini mi Jukristu, jatel wi meli ne ugam ukwero, man ewacu ire nya ecopu bedo i pacu. Ento nyathin ma jal maber eni—Rwoth mi nam mi Wales, eneno ngo ot pa Captain. Gikethe piny ikind ju ma giwotho i yei, man giloko ire ni jaracik. Ento calu Czar Peter ma cwinye yom ni tic i kabedo mi yiku yei mi adhura mawoko, Queequeg ukwero lewic moko cii, kan ecopo nwangu tego mi ponji dhanu pare ma gisomo ngo. Pi the lembe ma piny—ewacu ira—ebedo ku miti madit mi ponjo ikind Jukristu, diru ma copo ketho dhanu pare bedo kud anyonga ma sagu ma gibedo kudu; man makadhu eca, podi ber uloyu ma con. Ento, alas! timo pa ju ma gwoko rec ma dit uketho enyang nia kadi Jukristu de gicopo bedo ju ma can man ju ma rac; madwong uloyu, makadhu dhanu pa wone zo. Utuc i Sag Harbor macon; man gineno ango ma ju ma giwotho i yei gitimo keca; man i ngeye gicidho i Nantucket, man gineno kite ma gitiyo kudu ku cul migi i kabedu ne de, Queequeg ma jacan uweko pi rwinyo. Eparu niya, etiye ngom marac i yore zo; Abitho calu pagan.

Man kumeno jarawor macon i cwinye, podi ekwo ikind Jukristu eni, eruke kendi migi, man etemo weco lembe migi mi ndra. Pieno lembe ma nyayu paru ikume, kadi nya kawoni dong etiye kud i pacu.

Ku kony, apenje ka ebemito ngo edok cen, man ebed ku rwoth; pi en ecopo paru nia won ot pare utho man etho, en ebedo muti man goro i lembe ma tok cen. Edwoko ungo, podi epe; man emedo nia ebedo ku lworo nia dini pa Jukristu, nyo Jukristu, uketho ebedo ngo ku twero mi bedo i kom ker maleng man ma leng pa rwoth pyer adek ma juyic ungo i wange. Ento ndhundhu, ewacu, ebidwogo,—ka ewinjo nia edaru batizo kendu. Pi kare manok, kadi ango, eparu mi wotho ku yei, man epodho oats pare i nam angwen zo. Gitimo ire harpooneer, man cuma ma cinge eca ubedo kabedo pa scepter kawoni.

Apenje nya ango ma copo bedu paru pare kawoni, ikum wotho pare mi anyim. Edwoko, ni cidho i nam kendu, i tic pare macon. Ikum lembe eni, awacu ire nia mako rec ma dit ubedo yub para gira, man awacu ire nia amito awok kud i Nantucket, calu kabedo maber loyo pi ju ma mako rec ma dit ni wok kud i iye. En ndhundhu eca emoko tam pare ni lubo koda i cula meno, meli i yei marom, mondo i saa acel, yei marom, lembe marom ku an, i lembe ma nok ni poko lembe para zo; ku cinga ario zoo i cinge, ku tego cwiny i Potluck mi ng'om ario zoo. I kum lembe eni zo, ayiyu kud anyonga; kum muweko mar ma kawoni abedo kudu pi Queequeg, ebedo jabodho ma ngeyo tic ku harpoone, man calu an, ecopo bedu ngo ku kony madit ni ngati acel, ma, calu an, engeyo ngo lembe ma pire tek mi mako rec, kadi nangu engeyo nam maber, calu ma juruwil mi nam gingeyo.

Lembe pare uthum ku yuk pa pipe pare ma tok cen, Queequeg utingo an, ucwalu wiye ikum wiya, man ucwalu ler, wapodho kud ikind wadi, yo ku yo, man ndhundhu ne wabedo ka nindu.





CHAPTER 13. Wheelbarrow.

Idiwor mukende, ceng kasi acel, ingey ketho wic ma juyiko ni jabodho, pi block acel, adwoko cul para man pa wadi; tiyo ku, kadi ango, sente pa wadi. Won ot ma ube ngier, man ju ma gibedo i ot, nen calu gibedo kud anyonga lee dit pi bedo jurimo ma wok ikind an ku Queequeg—mandha ne, lembe pa Peter Coffin ikume ugam unyayu lworo i iya ikum ngati ma kawoni abedo kude.

Wa gamu gari, man wa ketho piny mwa, ikinde ubedo sanduku para ma rac, man sanduku pa Queequeg man hammock, wacidho piny i “the Moss,” meli ma nok mi Nantucket ma juketho i dhu nam. Kinde ma wabewothu dhanu ubedu ka nenu; ungo i Queequeg akeca—pien gibedu ka neno ju ma camu dhanu calu en i ngudu migi,—ento gineno en man an i lembe ma pire tek. Ento wawinjo ngo lembe migi, wabewotho ku mutukari ku cinge, man Queequeg kawoni ku kare ucungu pi yiko cinge ma ucungo iwi harpoon barbs pare. Apenje pirango ma etingo piny ma nyayu peko i dhu nam, man ka yei ceke ma mako rec ma julwongo ni whale ginwangu ngo jam lwiny migi gigi. I kum lembe eni, i lembe ma pire tek, edwoko, nia kadi nang ango ma awacu ubedo lemandha, ento ebedo ku mar madit ikum harpoon pare gire, kum ebedo piny ma pire tek, ma jutemo maber i lwiny madwong, man ma ceng ikum adunde pa rec ma julwongo ni whales. I lembe ma nok, calu jufur man jupur mapol, ma gicidho i podho pa jurufur ku pala migi gigi—kadi bende epe ku twero mi mio gi—kadi bende, Queequeg, pi lembe pare gire, emaru harpoon pare gire.

Eloko gari kud i cinga ni pare, ewacu ira lembe moko ma nyayu ngyeru ikum gari ma kwong ma eneno. Ubedo i Sag Harbor. Wegi yei pare, nen calu, giculo ire acel, ma ecopo tingo iye sanduku pare ma pek i ot pare. Ni nen ni ngati ma ngeyo ngo lembe ne—kadi bende i lemandha ebedo kumeno, ikum kite ma jubi tiyo kudu ku barrow—Queequeg uketho cwinye ikume; goye pio pio; man etingo gari man ecidho malu iwi yei. "Pirango," awacu, "Queequeg, icopo bedu ingeyo maber uloyu, ngati moko copo paru. Dhanu gi ngiero ngo?"

I kum lembe eni, ewacu ira lembe mange. Dhanu mi cula pare mi Rokovoko, nen calu, i foc mi nyom migi giwodho pii ma leng pa nying yen ma nyen i calabash malac ma rangire calu punchbowl; man punchbowl eni kare ku kare bedo piny maber mi deyo iwi kom ma juyiko ku cing kama jutimo i iye foc. Kawoni meli moko madit mi kuloka ugam utuc i Rokovoko, man jadit pare—ku lembe zo, jadit ma kite ber mandha, kadi pi jatel wi nam—jatel wi tic eni julwonge i nyom pa nyamin Queequeg, nyam pa rwoth ma nyathin ma oro apar. Ber; kinde ma welo mi nyom zo gicokiri i ot pa nyaku ma juyubu ku bamboo, jatel wi mony eni umondo, man jumiye kabedo mi woro, eketho kume i dhu punchbowl, man ikind rwoth madit ku rwoth pare rwoth, won Queequeg. Kisa ma juweco,-pi dhanu eno gitiye ku berocwiny migi calu wan-kadi bende Queequeg uwacu ira nia epe ku wan, ma i saa ma kumeno waneno piny i sahan mwa, gin, i yo ma tung tung, gilubo tok ducks, gineno malu i bang' Jami madit mi foc ceke-Ber, awacu, juwacu, jalam madit ucaku cam ku foc mi poyo wic mi ng'om; niwacu, ketho cinge ma juyiko man ma juyiko i basen ma podi kongo ma juyiko ne uwok ungo. Nenre gire ma juketho inget pa rwoth, man eneno kwer, man eparu nia etiye jatel wi yei—calu ngati ma pire tek uloyu rwoth mi cula, asaga i ot pa rwoth gire—jatel wi meli ucaku lwoko cinge i punchbowl;—egam etingo ni finger-glass madit. "Kawoni," Queequeg uwacu, "ibe paru ango kawoni?—Dhanu mwa gi ngiero ngo?"
