THEK 95. Cassock.

Ka nwang ibemondo i yei mi Pequod i kare moko mi tho pa rec ma dit; man ka nwang ibe wotho anyim ceng ku windlass, angeyu niya nwang ibe neno ku miti ma nok piny moko ma nen ungo, ma nyang ikume tek, ma nwang ibe neno keca, ma ucungo iwi bor i lee scuppers. Man ayi gin m'acwia, andha, etiye; nyo, i rundi ma con, cal pare ubedo. Mungu ma kumeno ma nwangire i lum pa Daker Maakah i Yudea; man pi woro ma, Rwoth Asa, wode, ugoye, man enyotho ayi gin m'acwia, man ewange ni piny ma rac i kulo mi Kedron, calu ma jukoro i thenge mir 15 mi buku mir acel pa rwoth.

Ka dong etimo eni, eloko adila ne woko, calu tyend pantaloon; emiyo edongo maber, ma romo medu diameter pare wang aryo; man i ajikine eketho, enyai maber, i rigging, kara etho. CHAPTER 96. The Try-Works.

Kakare pa yei pare ma jutingo, jadwar rec mi Amerika ungeyere woko pi tic pare ma etimo. Enyuthu lembe ma nyayu lworo pa kidi ma tek uloyu ma ucungo ku oak man hemp pi yiku yei ma judaru timo. Ebedo calu nia kud i podho ma oling jutingo brick-kiln i planks pare.

Try-works juyike ikind foremast ku mainmast, thenge ma dit pa deck. Bath ma piny utiye ku tego ma ukoc, juyike pi tingo pek pa dul ma tek pa brik man mortar, ma romo futi apar ku bor abora, man abic i bor. Foundation ne ok donjo e deck, to masonry ne oketo matek e wi lowo gi tiende mapek ma ne oting’o chuma ma ne oting’o e yore duto, kendo ma ne oting’o piny e wi yien. I thenge ma yo piny juyike ku yen, man iwi wiye juyike ku dhugola malac, ma ucungo, ma ucungo. Ka juwodho hatch eni wabe nyutho try-pots madongo, aryo i wel, man acel acel ku kero pa barrels madwong. Ka jutiyu kudu ngo, jugwoko gi maleng mandha. Saa moko juyike ku soapstone man lum, nitundo ma gi ler i iye calu punch-bowls mi silva. I saa mi gwoko piny mi diewor, jumoko ma dongo ma timo tic mi meli ma timo lembe marac gibimondo i igi man gicokiri keca pi nindu. I kare ma jutiyo kudu pi yiko gi—ngati acel i agulu acel acel, inget ku cing—wec mapol ma pire tek jutimo, iwi dhoge mi cuma. Etiye kabedu mi ponji mi math. Ubedo i cing cing ma yor acam pa Pequod, ku soapstone ma ucungo iwi koma, ma acaku wok ku lembe ma pire tek, nia i geometry piny ceke ma ucungo iwi cycloid, soapstone para pi lapor, biwok kud i kabedo moku ci i sawa marom.

Ka juwodho fire-board kud i kore pa try-works, masonry ma nen ungo i thenge meno nen kamaleng, ma jumondo ku dhugola ario mi furnaces, piny pa agulu. Dhoge eni juyike ku dhugola ma pek ma juyubu ku cuma. Lyethu ma tek pa mac jukwero ni cidho i deck, ni kadhu kud i reservoir ma nyap ma ucungo piny pa kabedo mi tic. Ku tunnel ma juketho i ngeye, reservoir eni bedo ma pong ku pii pio pio ma edoko pii. Onge chimneys ma woko; gi yabo direct kud i kor ot ma ngeye. Man kawoni wek wadok cen pi kare manok.

Ubedo saa abungwen mi diewor ma Pequod's try-works ucaku i wotho maeni. Ubedo pa Stubb ni neno wi tic ne.

"Gin ceke dong utimbre keca? Wek dhugola, dong, man icak. I tedo, icak tic." Eni ubedu piny ma yot, kum jabodho ubedo ka ketho lum pare i mac i kare ceke. Kae jubi wacu nia i wotho mi mako rec ma dit, mac mir acel i try-works umaku jucam pi kare moko ku yen. I ngeye, jutiyo ku yen moko ope, mako kende ni tiyo ku mac pio pio. I wec acel, ingey temo, moo ma nyap, ma nyap, ma kawoni julwongo ni scraps nyo fritters, podi utiye ku piny madwong ma nyayu moo. Fritters eni camu mac. Calu tho pa yioyic ma wango, nyo ngati ma timo lembe marac, ka dong juwango, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale mio moo pare gire man wango ku kume gire. Ka nwang edaru tiyo ku sigare pare gire! kum iro pare rac ni yuyu, man iyuye umitu, man ungo kende, ento umitu ibed i iye pi kare ne. Etiye ku nguce ma dhanu copo weco ngo, ma rac, ma Hindoo, calu ma copo bedu inget mac mi yik. Ngwece calu cinge ma lacwic mi ceng pokolembe; etiye laro lembe pi vur.

I diewor, tic ne ucaku tic. Wabedu maleng kud i kum ju m'utho; sail ubedo ka timere; yamu ubedu ka lyel; mudho mi nam macol ubedo tek. Ento mudho eca juwango ku mac ma tek, ma kare ku kare uwok kud i mac ma nyai, man ucwalu thwol moko ci ma malu i rigging, calu ku mac pa Greek. Meli ma ubewang ne umediri, calu ngati ma juoro ire timo lembe moko mi culo kwor. Pieno, yei ma tingo pii man sulphur pa Hydriote, Canaris, ma wok kud i dhu nam migi mi diewor, ku mac ma lac pi yei, ucwalu piny iwi meli pa Turkey, man ugoyo gi i mac.

Hatch, ma juwodho kud iwi tic, dong uketho mac malac i nyimgi. Ucungu iwi eca ubedo cal pa Tartarean pa ju ma timo harpooneers, ma kare ku kare gibedo ju ma gwoko yei pa rec ma julwongo ni whale. Ku lum madongo ma ucungo iwi cing gi gibolo moo madwong i pii ma lyeth, nyo gi wango mac piny, nitundo ma mac ma calu thwol ucwale, ucungo, woko kud i dhugola ni mako gi ku tyend gi. Iro ne ucwale woko i dul macol. I rwom acel acel mi yei, rwom pa moo ma lyethu ubedu nuti, ma nen calu ubedu mitu mi tuc i wang gi. I dhugola pa tic, yo malu pa mac ma lac ma juyubu ku yen, ubedu windlass. Eni ubedo sofa mi nam. Kan ecaku sawa, kani epe ku tic mange, gineno i lyethu makwar pa mac, nitundu wang gi ungwel i wigi. Kite migi macol, ma kawoni dong upong ku iro man lyethu, yuk wigi ma nyai, man lak gi ma leng, eni zo unyuthire i ayi ma nen ungo i tic migi. Kinde ma gibekoro ni juwothgi lembe migi ma rac, lembe migi mi lworo juweco ku wec mi anyonga; calu ngyeru migi ma rwinyo uwok kud i igi, calu mac ma wok kud i mac; kadok ku ngeye, i wang gi, ju ma gi goyo harpooneers gibedu ka nyuthu cing gi ku cing gi ma dit man dippers; calu yamu ubekok, man nam ucungo, man yei ucungo man ucungo, man podi ebolo red hell pare mabor man mabor i mudho nam man dyewor, man ebolo cogo ma tar i dhoge ku lewic, man ebolo lak i kume ku yore zo; i ngeye Pequod ma ube ringo, ma utingo ku ju ma rac, man ma upong ku mac, man uwango avu, man umondo i mudho macol, nen calu piny ma rwate ku tipo pa jatel wi mony pare.

Ebedo nen ira, ma acungu i kore, man pi sawa madwong ling ling atelo yo pa yei ma mac iwi nam. Abedo, pi kare meno, i mudho an gira, an de aneno makwar, lworo, man lworo pa jumange. Neno pa jajok ma nen i wanga, ma ucungo nusu i iro man nusu i mac, eni ugam unyayu neno ma rom i cwinya, kawoni kende ma acaku yio nindo ma copo wok ira i diewor.

Ento dyewor eca, lembe moko ma wangeyo ngo (man ma dhanu copo gonyo ngo) wok i wiya. Nicaku i nindu ma nok, nangu abedo ku lworo ma lee dit ikum piny moko marac. The jaw-bone tiller ugoyo cinga, ma ucungu ikume; i it para ubedu dwand ma piny pa yei, ma ucaku yeng yeng i yamu; Aparu niya wanga oyabere; Abedo ku nen ma nok mi ketho cinga iwi cinge man mi tingo gi mabor ku cinga. Ento, kadi bende lembe eni zo, aneno ngo compass i nyima ma acopo telo kudu; kadi nya nen calu dakika acel kende nicaku ma aneno kad ne, ku tara ma ucungo ma ucungo iwie. Piny moko mbe ma nen i wanga ento jet gloom, kawoni man i ngeye ma edoko rac ku ler pa rangi makwar. Lembe ma pire tek uloyu ubedu paru, nya piny moko ci ma ringo ma acungu iwie ubedu ngo ma ucungo iwi kabedo moku ci ma ubedu anyim calu ngwec kud i kabedo ceke ma yo ngeye. Awinjiri ma tek, ma nyayu lworo, calu tho, bino iwia. Ku lworo cinga umaku tiller, ento ku paru ma rac nia tiller ne, yo moko, i yo moko ma nyayu anyonga, utur. Mungu para! ango marac ikuma? paru para. Lo! I nindo para manok, aloko koma, man abedo ka neno ngeye pa yei, ku ngeye para i ngeye man compass. I saa moko, adok cen, i kare ma mitire pi juku yei kud ucidhi malo i yamu, man nyalo bedo ni epodho. Anyonga nenedi man foyofoc nenedi pi bedo agonya kud i nen pa piny ma nen ungo mi dyewor, man tho ma bino pi bedo ma lee ukelo!

Kud inen kare malac i wang mac, O dhanu! Kud ipar ku cingi iwi yei! Kud ilok ngeye peri i kum kompas; jolo lembe ma kwong mir acel pa tiller; kud iyii mac ma juyubu, ma rangi pare makwar weko piny ceke nen marac. Urwo, i ceng ma leng, polo bibedo maleng; ju ma nen calu pajogi i mac ma lyel, uthieno bi nyuthu yore mukende, kadi nya yot, kony; ceng ma leng, macol, ma kud anyonga, tara acel kende ma lemandha—ju mukende ento ju ndra kende!

Ento ceng umo ngo Virginia's Dismal Swamp, kadi Rome's recursed Campagna, kadi Sahara malac, kadi miles miliyon madwong mi lum man can ma piny pa dwe. Ceng umo ngo nam, ma en e thenge macol pa ng'om eni, man ma en e dul aryo iwi adek pa ng'om eni. Dong, dong, dhanu ma tho ma utiye ku mutoro madwong uloyu can i iye, dhanu ma tho copo bedu ngo mandha—mandha ngo, nyo ma dongo ngo. Ku buku marom. Buku ma lemandha uloyu kud ikind dhanu ceke ubedo Ng'atu pa can, man buku mandha uloyu uloyu ubedo pa Solomon, man Ecclesiastes ubedo cuma maber pa can. "Gin ceke utiye mananu." CEKE. Ng'om maeni ma timo lembe ma mitire kawoni podi unwangu ngo ryeko pa Solomon ma jakristu ngo. Ento ngati ma jwigo ot yath man mabusi, man wotho pio pio ka kadho liel, man emaru weco ikum opera ma sagu mac; lwongo Cowper, Young, Pascal, Rousseau, ju jok ma can, dhanu ma remo; man i kwo pare ma epe ku lworo, ekwonge ni Rabelais ni ngati ma riek, man ma kud anyonga;—ngo nia dhanu copo bedu piny iwi kidi mi liel, man turo lum macol ku Solomon ma ber mandha.

Ento kadi Solomon, ewacu, "ng'atu ma wok kud i yo mi nyang ebidong" ( niwacu , kadi podi ekwo) "i kind ju m'utho." Kud imiiri giri ni mac, kara kud elok peri, man etho; pi kare ma etimo ira. Nitie rieko ma en woe; ento can moko nuti ma utiye mic. Man etiye Catskill eagle i tipo moko ma copo rwinyo piny i got macol, man ewok kud i iye kendu man edoko piny ma nen ungo i kabedo ma ceng ryeny. Man kadi nang edaru ngwec i kind got, got ne utiye i got; kara kadi i ngwec pare ma piny, ungwal mi got podi utiye malo uloyu winy mukende iwi lum, kadi nang gi ringo malu.

Ka nwang iwok kud i Pequod's try-works ni Pequod's forecastle, kama ju ma timo tic ungo gibedu ka nindu i iye, pi kare manok kende nwang ibeparu niya icungu i kabedo moko maleng pa rwoth man jukony. Ewango, de, moo maleng loyo, i kite ma juyubu ngo, man, kumeno, ma juyubu ngo; pii ma ceng, dwe, nyo astral ungeye ngo i dhu nam. Ebedo mit calu moo lum macon i dwi mir angwen.
