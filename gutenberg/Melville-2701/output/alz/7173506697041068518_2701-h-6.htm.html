CHAPTER 32. Cetology.

Dong wabecidho iwi pii ma thuc; ento kawoni wabi rwenyo i ngom pare ma epe ku dhu nam, man ma epe ku dhu nam. Ere ma bino timere; mapodi Pequod's weedy hull ucwalu cing ku barnacled hulls pa leviathan; I acaki ber ni bedo i kum lembe ma pire tek ni nyang maber pa leviathanic revelations man allusions ma tung tung ma bino lubo.

Etiye nyuthu moko maber pa rec ma julwongo ni whale i thek pare malac, ma kawoni amito aket i nyimi. Ento epe tic ma yot. Poko pa piny ma nyayu lwiny, piny moko epe ma juweco pire keni. Winj lembe ma judongo maber man manyen uketho piny.

"Ungo dul moko mi Zoology ma tiye ku tic ma lee calu ma julwongo ni Cetology," Captain Scoresby uwacu, AD 1820.

"Eni epe paru para, tek ebedo i tego para, ni mondo i penji ikum yore mandha mi poko cetacea i ungu man ju pacu. * * * Lembe ma nyayu anyonga utiye ikind ju ma goro lembe mukadho pa lei eni" (sperm whale), ewacu Surgeon Beale, AD 1839.

"Unfitness ni lubo tok lembe mwa i pii ma dhanu copo nyang kudu ngo." “Vel ma copo wok ungo ma umo ngec mwa ikum cetacea.” “Podho ma opong ku kuthe.” “Lembe maeni zo ma rwinyo ngo ento konyowa ni nyayu can iwa wan ju ma gisomo lembe mi piny.”

Pieno, wec ikum rec madit, Cuvier madit, man John Hunter, man Lesson, lero pa lei man kit kum. Kata kamano, kata obedo ni ng’eyo madier nok, to buge mang’eny; man kumeno i rwom moku manok, ku cetology, nyo ngec pa rec ma dit. Dhanu mapol, ma nok man madongo, ma dit man ma nyen, dhanu mi ngom man ju ma giwotho i nam, ma gicoyo lembe ikum rec madit nyo manok. Nen iwi moko:—Jugor mi Biblia; Aristotle; Pliny; Aldrovandi; Sir Thomas Browne; Gesner; Ray; Linnæus; Rondeletius; Willoughby; Alum alum; Artedi; Sibbald; Brisson; Marten; Lacépède; Bonneterre; Desmarest; Baron Cuvier; Frederick Cuvier; John Hunter; Owen; Scoresby; Beale; Bennett; J. Ross Browne; jagor pa Miriam Coffin; Olmstead; man Rev. T. Cheever. Ento pi lembe ma pire tek ma lembe eni zo ukyewu, lembe ma jugoro malu eno binyuthu.

Ikind nying ma nwangire i karatasi pa jugor pa rec ma julwongo ni whale, ju ma gilubu Owen kende re ma gineno whale makwo; man ento acel ikin gi ubedo jabodho mi goyo harpoone man whaleman mandha. Abeweco ikum Captain Scoresby. I kum lembe matung tung pa Greenland nyo right-whale, etiye ngati ma pire tek uloyu. Ento Scoresby ung'eyo ngo lembe moko man ewacu ngo lembe moko ikum sperm whale madit, ka juporo ku Greenland whale ma rwinyo ngo. Man kawoni jubi wacu, nia rec ma julwongo ni Greenland whale ubedo jakwo iwi komker mi nam. Etiye ngo kadi acel de ma dit uloyu ikind rec madongo. Ento, pi bedo ma pire tek pa lembe pare, man ngec ma lee ma, nitundu oro pyer abiro mukadho, uketho sperm-whale ma dhanu ngeyo ongo, man ngec ma rwinyo nitundu kawoni podi utiye i kabedo ceke ento kabedo moko manok mi ngec mi ngec man whale-ports; usurpation eni udaru thum. Ka iporo ku leviathanic allusions i juwer madongo mi kare mukadho, ibiketho ibedo kud anyonga ni Greenland whale, manangu epe ku jadhoge, ubedu igi rwoth mi nam. Ento kare dong utuc pi lwongo manyen. Eni ubedo Charing Cross; winj! dhanu maber zo,—rec madit mi Greenland juwodhe woko,—rec madit mi sperm dong ubimo!

Buku ario kende utiye ma nen calu giketho sperm whale makwo i nyimi, man i kare marom, i rwom ma malu gi loyo tembe ne. Buku eni ubedo pa Beale ku Bennett; gin ario zoo i kare migi ju ma gitimo yath i meli mi England South-Sea, man gin ario zoo dhanu ma gen man ma gen. Piny ma kwong ma mako sperm whale ma nwangre i volumes migi utiye nok; ento kadok kawoni, etiye maber akeca, kadi nya etiye kende kende i lembe mi ryeko. Ento podi, sperm whale, mi ryeko nyo mi poem, kwo pare uthum ungo i giragora moko ci. Ma malu uloyu ikum rec ma jucako, kwo pare utiye kwo ma jugoro ngo.

Kawoni kit lei matung tung mi whales mitu kit poko moko ma dhanu zo maru, kadi nya yot pi kare eni, ma jubi pongu i dipatment pare zo ku jurutic mukende. Calu ngati moko mbe maber uloyu pi maku lembe eni i cinge, kawoni amiyo kero para marac. Aciko ngo piny moko ci ma romo; kum piny moko ci pa dhanu ma jukwanu ni bedu malong, umitu pi the lembe meno bedu ma rac. Abecitimo ngo lembe ma nok ikum kit kum pa lei matung tung, nyo—i kabedu eni—ni lembe mapol. Lembe para kany utiye ni ketho yub pa yub pa cetology. An a jayub ot, ento jayub ot epe.

Ento etiye tic ma tek; ngati moko mbe ma poko waraga i Post-Office ma rom kude. Ni lubo tokgi piny i the nam; ni bedu ku cingi ikind thek ma dhanu copo weco ngo, cinge, man cinge pa ng'om; eni ubedo piny ma lworo. An ango ma umitu atim pi mako um pa leviathan eni! Lembe marac ma juyero i Job copo nyayu lworo ira. Ecopo (leviathan) timo lembariba kudi? Nen gen pare utiye nono! Ento adaru kadhu kud i ot kwan man awotho i nam; Abedo ku tic ku rec ma julwongo ni whales ku cing gi ma nen; Atiye ku cwiny ma tek; man abitemo. Lembe moko nuti ma umitu jutim.

Mir acel: Lembe ma ungeyere ngo, ma ungeyere ngo pa ngec mi Cetology utiye i vestibule ma jukwanu ku lemandha, nia i kabedo moko podi utiye lembe ma pire tek ka nyo rec ma julwongo ni whale utiye rec. I System of Nature pare, AD 1776, Linnæus uwacu, "Apoko kind rec ma dit ku rec." Ento ku ngec para gira, angeyo niya nitundu i oro 1850, rec sharks ku shad, alewives man herring, ikum cik pa Linnæus, jubedu ka nwangu gi ka poko ngom mi nam acel ku Leviathan.

The lembe ma Linnæus copo timo nya edaru ryemu rec madongo kud i pii, ewacu kumae: "Pi adunde migi ma mor, ombo migi, wang gi ma mol, it migi ma oling, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem," man i ajiki, "ex lege naturæ jure meritoque." Aoro lembe eni zo ni jurimo para Simeon Macey man Charley Coffin, mi Nantucket, ju ario zoo wadi wadi i wotho moko, man gicokiri i paru niya the lembe ma jukoro eca ubedu piny ma romo ngo. Charley uwacu ku lewic nia gitiye ju ma rac.

Bed ma ung'eyo nia, weko lembe moko ci, agam paru macon nia rec ma julwongo ni whale ubedo rec, man alwongo Jonah maleng ni kony ira. Lembe ma pire tek eni uthum, lembe mukende utiye, i yo ango ma rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale opokiri ku rec mukende. Malu, Linnæus umiyu iri piny eni. Ento i lembe ma nok, gitiye: ombo man rimo ma mor; ento, rec mukende zo gipe ku ombo man rimo migi ngic.

Mukende: wacopo gonyo whale nenedi, ku piny pare ma woko, kara wabed ku nying pi kare ceke ma ubebinu? Mi bedu ma nok, dong, rec ma julwongo ni whale ubedo rec ma mol ku yibe ma ucungo . Kan ibedo kude. Kadi bende judwoko piny, gonyo lembe eca ubedo adwogi pa meditation ma jumedo. Walrus weco calu whale, ento walrus epe rec, kum ekwo i pii. Ento wec mir ajiki mi the lembe podi utiye ma pire tek, calu ma juporo ku mir acel. Ng'atu moko ci ucikere ni ngeyo nia rec ceke ma dhanu mi ngom gingeyo gipe ku yibe ma lat, ento gitiye ku yibe ma ucungo malu, nyo malu man piny. Ento, ikind rec ma mol, yibe, kadi nya ecopu bedu marom, edoko piny ma ucungo.

Ku gonyo ma malu pa gin ma rec ma julwongo ni whale, an aweko ngo kud ikind ju ma gikwo i nam ma julwongo ni whale ni ju ma gingeyo lembe maber mi Nantucketers; kadi, yo mukende, mako kudu rec moko ci ma dhanu moko gineno ni lei ma woko.* Pieno, rec ceke ma nok, ma mol, man ma yibe migi ucungo ucikere ni bedu i yub maeni mi Cetology. Lembe madwong utiye ma dhanu ungeye ngo ikum kit lei ma dhanu mapol ungiyo. Dong ango ma julwongo ni whale, ma aketho ikind lei mir aryo mi Folios para? Etiye Mysticetus madit pa ju ma gisomo lembe iwi ngom mi England; rec ma julwongo ni Greenland Whale pa ju England; Baleine Ordinaire pa French whalemen; Growlands Walfish pa Swedes. Etiye rec ma julwongo ni whale ma pi oro ma kadhu miya aryo mukadho jubedu ka nego ku ju Dutch ku ju England i nam mi Arctic; etiye rec ma julwongo ni whale ma judwar rec mi Amerika gibedo ka lubo tokgi pi kare malac i nam mi India, i Brazil Banks, i Nor' West Coast, man i kabedo mange matung tung mi ng'om, ma julwongo ni Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Jumoko timo calu gineno tung tung ikum rec ma julwongo ni Greenland whale pa ju England ku right whale pa ju America. Ento giyio lembe migi zo; man de podi jukoro ngo lembe moko ma pire tek ma copo ketho apoka poka madit. Ubedo ku poko ma rwinyo ma ucungo iwi tung tung ma rwinyo ngo, ma weko thenge moko mi lembe mi kwo doko tek mandha. Whale ma yor acam jubi weco ikume kaka mange, ku nen pa sperm whale.

BUKU I. ( Folio ), CHAPTER III. ( Fin-Back ).—I thenge mi wi lembe eni, aparu nia lei moko ma dit ma, ku nying matung tung Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, man Long-John, juneno i nam ceke man ubedo rec ma dit ma juwotho i iye ma giwotho i nam Atlantic, i New York packet-tracks. I bor ma enwangu, man i baleen pare, Fin-back nen calu whale ma cinge yor acam, ento etiye ku bor ma nok, man rangi ma ler, ma romo olive. Dhoge madongo nyuthu kit ma calu cable, ma juyubu ku cinge ma ucungo, ma ucungo iwi cinge madongo. Kite ma pire tek ma poko kudu, fin, ma enwango nyinge kud i iye, pol kare ubedo piny ma nen kamaleng. Fin eni bor pare romo futi adek nyo angwen, edongo malu kud i ngeye pa kore, ma nen calu angular, man ku thum ma lac. Kadi bende ka dul moko ma nok pa lei ne nen ungo, cinge ma juketho kende eni, saa moko, nen kamaleng ka ewok kud iwi ngom. Ka nam ubedo moth, man edongo ku pii ma nyai, man gnomon-like fin eni ucungu man eketho tipo iwi ngom ma nyai, ecopu bedu ni pii ma ucungu kudu nen calu dial, ku kit pare man wavy hour-lines ma jugoro iwie. I kum Ahaz-dial eca, tipo udok cen. Fin-Back epe ku dhanu mapol. Enen ni ngati ma maru rec ma dit, calu coo moko ma maru dhanu. Lewic lee; nja cidho kende; ma jukuro ngo iwi pii i pii mabor man macol; jet pare ma lageng man acel ma malu ma ucungo calu pala ma bor ma dhanu timo ngo i wi ngom ma lum; juyike ku tego man ngwec ma lee dit i dhuny, ma copo kwero lembe ceke ma dhanu timo kawoni; leviathan eni nen calu Kain ma juwodho man ma jucopo loyo ngo, ma utingo lanyuth pare iwi ngeye. Nicaku i bedo ku baleen i dhoge, Fin-Back pol kare ju medo ku rec ma yor acam, ikind lei moko ma julwongo ni Whalebone whales , niwacu, rec ma ku baleen. Ikind rec ma julwongo ni Whalebone whales, nen calu utiye ku kitgi madwong, pol migi, kadi ango, ungeyere manok. Rec ma julwongo ni broad-nosed whales man beaked whales; pike-headed whales; rec ma julwongo ni bunched whales; rec ma julwongo ni under-jawed whales man rostrated whales, gitiye nying ju ma mako rec pi kit rec moko.

I lembe maeni ma julwongo ni "Whalebone whales," piretek ni wacu, kadi bende nying ma kumeno copo bedu ber pi ketho lembe ikum kit rec moko, ento epe ku kony ni temo poko Leviathan, ma ucungo iwi baleen pare, nyo hump, nyo fin, nyo teeth; kadi bende, thenge ma juketho i iye lanyuth eca nen kamaleng ni ber uloyu pi bedo the cik mi Cetology ma sagu kit kum mange ma tung tung, ma rec ma julwongo ni whale, i kit pare, nyuthu. Dong nenedi? Baleen, hump, back-fin, man lak; eni gi piny ma tung tung migi unyai ikind kit rec ma tung tung, manangu giparu ngo ikum kite ma gi nen kudu i lembe mange man ma pire tek. Pieno, sperm whale man humpbacked whale, acel acel utiye ku hump; ento keca lembe ma rom eca uthum. I ngeye, rec ma julwongo ni humpbacked whale man Greenland whale, acel acel utiye ku baleen; ento keca kendu, rom pa lembe uthum. Man etiye marom ku thenge mange ma juweco pigi malu. I kind kit rec matung tung, gi bedo ku dul ma tung tung; nyo, i lembe pa ngati moko ci m'ikindgi ma jupoko, bedo kende ma rwinyo ngo; calu ma rwinyo lembe ceke ma juyiko iwi the lembe ma kumeno. Iwi kidi eni ngati acel acel ma ngeyo lembe pa rec ma julwongo ni whale upokere.

Ento ecopu bedu nya, i thenge ma iye pa rec ma dit, i kum pare—kuca, kadi nok, wacopo nwangu poko maber. Ungo; piny ango, calu labol, ma utiye i kum pa rec ma julwongo ni Greenland whale ma nen maleng uloyu ma sagu baleen pare? Ento waneno nia ku baleen pare ecopu bedu ngo maber ni poko Greenland whale. Man ka ipodho i ic pa leviathans matung tung, pirango ma keca ibinwangu ngo tung tung mi thenge mir abic ma nwangre ni systematizer calu ju ma woko ma judaru kwanu. Ango dong udong? piny moko epe ento mako rec ma julwongo ni whales ku kum, i rwom migi mi bedo agonya, man poko gi ku tego cwiny. Man eni ubedo yub mi Bibliographic ma jutiyo kudu; man etiye acel kende ma copo bedu maber, kum en kende re ma copo timere. Mi mediri.

BUKU I. ( Folio ) THE IV. ( Hump Back ).—Rec ma julwongo ni whale eni pol kare juneno i dhu nam mi Amerika ma yo malu. Jumake kare ku kare keca, man jutere i dhu nam. Etiye ku piny madit ikume calu jalwor; nyo icopo lwonge ni Elephant and Castle whale. Ku yore moku ci, nying ma dhanu zo maru pi en epe, pi en sperm whale de utiye ku yuk kadi nang nya nok. Umo pare pire tek ungo. Etiye ku baleen. Etiye ngati ma maru tuko man ma cwinye yot uloyu ikind rec ma julwongo ni whales, ma etimo gay foam man white water mapol uloyu.

BUKU I. ( Folio ), CHAPTER V. ( Razor Back ).—Pi rec ma dit eni ungeyere manok ento nyinge kende. Anene ku bor pa Cape Horn. Ku kura mi weko tic, eweko ju ma dwar lei man ju ma rieko. Kadok ebedo ngo jalworo, podi enyutho ngo thenge moku cii ikume ento ngeye, ma ucungu iwi got ma bor. Wek ecidhi. Angeyo lembe manok ikume, kadi ngati moko ope.

BUKU I. ( Folio ), CHAPTER VI. ( Sulphur Bottom ).—Ng'atu mange ma ube weko tic, ku ic ma nyaleng, edaru nwangu tic ku cinge i Tartarian tiles i kind lembe pare ma pire tek. Juneno nyanok; at least anene ngo kende kende i nam mabor mi yo piny, man kare ku kare i kabedo mabor akeca mi ngiyo wange. Jube lubo tok pare ngo; ecopo ringo ku thung-wotho mi cing. Prodigies juweco pire. Adieu, Sulphur Bottom! Acopo yero ngo lembe mange ma utiye lemandha ikumwu, kadi Nantucketer ma dit uloyu de ecopu ngo.

Kumeno uthum BUKU I. ( Folio ), man kawoni BUKU II ucaku. ( Octavo ).

OCTAVOES.*—Maeni mako rec ma dit ma nyai, ma ikindgi jucopo kwanu:—I., Grampus ; II., rec macol ; III., the Narwhale ; IV., Thrasher ; V., Janek .

*Pirango ma buku pa rec ma julwongo ni whales jukwanu ngo ni Quarto utiye lembe ma yot. Pien, kadi nya rec ma dit mi dul eni, kadi nya gi nok uloyu mi dul macon, re gikwo ku kit ma rom ku migi i cal, ento buku pa Quarto pa jagor buku i kite pare ma dit ugwoko ngo kite pa buku pa Folio, ento buku pa Octavo egwoko.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER I. ( Grampus ).—Kadi rec eni, ma yuyu pare ma tek, nyo yuyu pare, uketho dhanu mi ngom gi bedo ku wec, ungeyere maber ni ngati ma bedo i nam ma thuc, ento dhanu mapol ungeye ngo ikind rec ma dit. Ento pi bedo ku kit ma tung tung pa leviathan, ju ma gisomo lembe ikum piny ma kwo ungiyu kudu. Etiye ku dit pa octavo ma rwinyo, bor pare ucaku ikum futi apar wiye abic nitundo futi pyer aryo wi abic, man bor pare rom ku kore. En ewotho i dul; jubeyenyo ngo kare ku kare, kadi nya moo pare utiye madwong, man ber pi lero. Ku ju ma mako rec moko gineno yore pare ni lanyuth pa anyim pa sperm whale madit.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER II. ( Rec macol ).—Amiyo nying ju ma maru rec eni zo, kum pol kare gi re maber uloyu. Ka nying moko utiye ma nyang ungo nyo ma nyang ikume ngo, abewecu, man abimiyu paru mange. Atimo kumeno kawoni, mulo rec macol, ma julwongo, kum rangi macol ubedo cik ikind rec ma julwongo ni whales. Dong, lwonge ni Hyena Whale, kan imito. Bedo ku ngec pare mi camu piny ungeyere, man nimakere ku kite ma dhoge ucungo malu, etiye ku ngier pa Mephistophelean i wange. Whale eni bor pare romo futi apar wiye abusiel nyo apar wi abora. Enwange i latitudes zo. Etiye ku yore moko ma ukoc mi nyuthu cinge ma ucungo i ngeye i dhuny, ma nen calu um pa ju Roma. Ka jutiyo kudu ngo maber, ju ma gi dwar sperm whale kare moko gimaku Hyena whale, pi ni gwoko moo ma yot pi tic mi ot—calu jumoko ma gwoko ot ma timo tic maber, ma dhanu moko ope i iye, man kendgi kendgi, wango tallow ma rac kakare pa wax ma nguc. Kadi bende moo migi utiye ma nyap, moko pa rec ma julwongo ni whales copo miyo iri gallons ma kadhu pyer adek pa moo.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER III. ( Narwhale ), niwacu, Nostril whale .—Lapor mange pa winyo ma julwongo ni whale, ma julwongo kumeno pi tunge ma juporo ku ume ma ucungo. Lei ne bor pare romo futi apar wiye abusiel, ento tunge romo futi abic, kadi moko kadhu futi apar, man romo futi apar wiye abic. Ka waweco lemandha, tunge eni utiye lak ma bor, ma dongo kud i lak i rwom ma piny nyanok kud i thenge ma lageng. Ento nwange kende i thenge ma rac, ma etiye ku adwogi marac, miyo won piny moko ma rom ku pa ngati ma tiyo ku cinge ma lacwic. Lembe ango ma ivory horn nyo lance edwoko, ecopo bedu tek ni wacu. Nen ungo ni jutiyo kudu calu pala rec ma julwongo ni sword-fish man bill-fish; kadi bende ju moko ma giwotho i yei giwacu ira niya Narwhale tiyo kudu pi loko piny i nam pi cam. Charley Coffin uwaco ni jutiyo kudu pi timo ice-piercer; pi Narwhale, ma ucungo iwi nam mi Polar, man enwango nia upong ku pee, etingo tunge malu, man epodho. Ento icopo nyutho ngo nia acel ikin lembe maeni utiye lemandha. Paru para giri utiye, kadi nang tung acel eni copo bedu tic pa Narwhale—kadi bende ecopu bedu nenedi—ecopo bedu ber ire pi karatasi mi kwanu waraga. Narwhale ma awinjo julwonge ni Tusked whale, Horned whale, man Unicorn whale. En ubedo lanyuth maber pa Unicornism ma nwangre i ker ceke mi piny ma kwo. Kud i bang jugor moko ma con, agam anwango nia tung pa sea-unicorn eni i ceng macon jubedu ka kwanu ni yath madit pa thwol, man kumeno, yubu ne ukelo bei madit. Juyike de ni kadu ma nyai pi mon ma rwinyo, yo marom ku tung pa deer ma co juyike ni hartshorn. I acaki, ebedo piny ma dhanu mitu ngeyo lee. Waraga macol uwacu ira nia Sir Martin Frobisher i dwogo pare kud i wotho pare, kinde ma Queen Bess utingo cinge ma leng i wang dirisa mi Greenwich Palace, ma yei pare ma tek ubewotho piny i Thames; "Kinde ma Sir Martin udwogo kud i wotho pare," Black Letter uwacu, "i wi tyende ma ucungo, enyutho ni rwoth pare tung mabor ma dit pa Narwhale, ma pi kare malac i ngeye juketho i ot pa rwoth i Windsor." Jagor moko mi Ireland uwacu ni Earl pa Leicester, ma ucungo iwi tyende, emiyo ni rwoth pare tung mange, ma mako lei mi ngom ma nen calu unicorn.

Narwhale utiye ku rangi maber mandha, nen calu leopard, ma rangi pare tar calu cak, ma upong ku rangi macol man macol. Umo pare ber mandha, maleng man maber; ento etiye manok, man jubedu ka dware nyanok. Enwangre pol kare i nam ma ling ling.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER IV. ( Killer ).—I kum rec ma julwongo ni whale eni, Nantucketer ung'eyo lembe manok kende, man ju ma giwacu nia gi ngeyo lembe iwi piny ma kwo. Ku gin ma aneno i kume ku bor, acopo wacu niya ebedo dit pa nyathin ma dit. Etiye ngati ma rac mandha—kit rec ma Feegee. Saa moko emaku Folio whales madongo ku dhoge, man ecungo keca calu leech, nitundu lei ma tek eca lworo tho. Janeko dhanu jubeyenyo ngo. Awinjo ngo kit moo ma etiye kudu. Nying ma jumiyo ni rec ma julwongo ni whale eni copo bedu tung tung, pi the lembe ma epe. Pien wan ceke wabedo junek, iwi ngom man iwi nam; Bonapartes ku Sharks uketho i iye.

BUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER V. ( Thrasher ).—Jadit eni ungeyere pi yibe pare, ma etiyo kudu pi goyo judhoge. Ebedo iwi ngete pa Folio whale, man kani ebedu ka dhuny, etimo tic pare ku goye; calu juruponj moko gikwo maber i ng'om ku yore marom. Podi lembe manok kende ungeye ikum Thrasher ikum Killer. Gin ario zoo gitiye ju ma turo cik, kadi i nam ma cik ope i iye.

Kumeno uthum BUKU II. ( Octavo ), man ecaku BUKU mir adek. ( Duodecimo ).

DUODECIMOES.—Maeni uketho i iye rec ma nok. I. The Huzza Porpoise. II. Algerine Porpoise. III. Mealy-mouthed Porpoise.

Ni ju ma gi nwango ngo kare mi ponjo lembe eni, ecopu nen ni lembe manyen, nia rec ma kadhu futi angwen nyo abic ucikere ni bedo ikind WHALES—wec, ma, i yore ma dhanu mapol ngeyo, kare ku kare nyuthu paru mi dit. Ento lei ma juketho malu calu Duodecimoes gibedo rec ma julwongo ni whale, nimakere ku the lembe ma aloko ikum gin ma whale utiye— niwacu rec ma weco, ku yibe ma ucungo.

BUKU mir adek. ( Duodecimo ), CHAPTER 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Eni ubedo porpoise ma nwangire i ng'om zoo. Nying ne ubedo pa an gira; kum tiye kit porpoises madwong, man piny moku umitu jutim pi poko kind gi. Alwonge kumeno, kum ebedo ka dhuny i pii ma nyayu ngyeru, ma iwi nam ma lac mediri ka cidho i polo calu wic i dul pa dhanu mi ceng angwen mi dwi mir abiro. Kite ma gineno kudu jufoyo ku anyonga pa ju ma giwotho i nam. Gipong ku tipo maber, giwok kud i yamo ma tek ni windward. Gitiye awiya ma nja kwo iwang yamu. Ju kwanu gi ni lanyuth pa gum. Ka in giri icopo ciro anyonga adek ka ineno rec ma leng eni, polo bikonyi; tipo mi tuko pa Mungu epe i iwu. Huzza Porpoise ma camu maber man ma pek bi miyo iri gallon acel maber pa moo maber. Ento pii maber man ma nyap ma juwodho kud i lak pare piretek mandha. Etiye piny ma jukwayu ikind ju ma yiku jam piny mabecu man ju ma yiku sawa. Jurutic mi yei giketho iwi cing gi. Ringu pa porpoise ber mi camu, ingeyo. Ecopu bedu nya epe i wii nia porpoise copo wok. Andha, dhoge utiye nyanok ma dhanu copo nyang kudu yot ungo. Ento sawa mange ma inwango kare, nen en; man ibi neno Sperm whale madit en gire i ayi ma nok.

BUKU mir adek. ( Duodecimo ), CHAPTER II. ( Algerine Porpoise ).—A pirate. Marac mandha. E nwange kende, aparu, i Pacific. Etiye dit uloyu Huzza Porpoise, ento etiye ku rwom marom. Keth cwinye, man ebidoko rec shark. Abedo piny pi en wang mapol, ento podi aneno ngo ka jumake.

BUKU mir adek. ( Duodecimo ), CHAPTER III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Kit Porpoise madit uloyu; man nwangire kende i Pacific, nitundu kawoni. Nying acel kende mi dhok mi Ingresa, ma jubed julwonge kudu nitundu kawoni, utiye pa ju ma mako rec—Right-Whale Porpoise, nicaku ikum lembe ma nwangire i dhu Folio meno. I kite kume, epokere ku Huzza Porpoise, pi bedo ku kume ma rwinyo man ma rwinyo ngo; andha, etiye ngati ma leng man ma kite ber. Epe ku lwithe iwi ngete (pol pa porpoises mukende gitiye kudu), etiye ku yibe ma leng, man wange ma rangi pa India ma rangi pa hazel. Ento dhoge ma leng nyayu piny zo. Kadi bende ngeye pare zo nitundu ikum cinge utiye pa sable ma thuc, ento cinge ma ucungo, ma tung tung calu lanyuth ma nwangre i kor yei, ma julwongo ni "bright waist," cinge eca ucungo iwie nicaku i kor yei nitundu i ngeye, ku rangi ario matung tung, macol malu man matar piny. Matar uketho i iye thenge moko mi wiye, man thenge moko mi dhoge, ma weko enen calu ngati m'uwok kud i lim ma rac ma jutimo i sanduku mi cam. Lembe ma rac man ma rac! Umo pare nen calu pa porpoise.

* * * * * *

I ngei DUODECIMO, lembe eni umedire ngo, pi kite ma Porpoise utiye ma nok uloyu ikind rec madongo. Malu, itiye ku Leviathans zo ma pire tek. Ento tiye dul pa rec ma dhanu moko ungeye ngo, ma ringo, ma nen ungo, ma, calu ja America ma gwoko rec, angeyo ku nying gi, ento ungo pira kena. Abikwanu gi ku nying migi mi fore-castle; pi lembe ma kumeno copo bedu ber ni ju ma gi lubo tok lembe i anyim, ma copo daru gin ma atiye kudu kany ento acaku. Ka ngati acel ikin rec ma e, jubi mako man jubi ketho lanyuth iwie, eca jubi ketho i dul eni, nimakere ku rwom pare mi Folio, Octavo, nyo Duodecimo:—The Bottle-Nose Whale; Junk Whale; the Pudding-Headed Whale; Cape Whale; the Leading Whale; Cannon Whale; Scragg Whale; Coppered Whale; Elephant Whale; Iceberg Whale; Quog Whale; Blue Whale; man mukende mapol. Kud i bang judongo mi Iceland, Dutch, man England macon, jucopu bedu ku lembe mukende ma jukoro ikum rec ma julwongo ni whales, ma juyiko ku nying ma rac. Ento aweko gi calu piny ma rwinyo; man ecopu konyo ngo ni paru nia gitiye dwand kende, ma upong ku Leviathanism, ento ma nyutho piny moko ope.

I ajiki: Juyero i acaki, nia lembe eni bibedo nuti ngo, man ndhundhu, ebibedo maleng. Icopo ngo ni neno kamaleng nia adaru gwoko lembe para. Ento kawoni aweko cetological System para ucungu ma rwinyo ngo, kadi nangu Cathedral madit mi Cologne udong, ku crane ma podi ucungu iwi wi ot ma rwinyo ngo. Pi gedo ma nok copo bedu ma juyiko ku ju ma kwong mi gedo; madongo, mandha, giweko copestone ni nyithindho. Mungu gwoka kud atim piny moko ci. Buku eni zo utiye kende kende lembe ma jugoro—ungo, ento lembe ma jugoro. Oh, sawa, tego, sente, man bedo moth!
