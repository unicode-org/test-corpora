CHAPTER 22. Natale maber.

I ngeye, i diewor, i ngei weko tic pa ju ma timo tic i yei, man i ngei ma juwodho Pequod kud i dhu nam, man i ngei ma Charity ma paru pare nja uwok kud i yei ma dit, ku mic pare mir ajiki—koto mi diewor pi Stubb, wadi mir aryo, omin cware, man jatel wi meli—pi Captain aryo, eni zo Peleg ku Bildad, giwok kud i ot, man giloko wang gi ibang jatel wi tic, Peleg uwacu:

"Kawoni, Mr. Starbuck, ingeyo nia lembe zo utiye maber? Jatel wi mony Ahab utiye ayika—aweco kude kende—piny moko mbe ma jubi nwangu kud i dhu nam, eh? Ber, lwong dhanu ceke, dong. Cok gi i ngeye keni—wang gi!"

"Umito ngo wec mi lewic, kadi bed ni timo pio pio nenedi, Peleg," Bildad uwacu, "ento cidhi kudi, jarimba Starbuck, man itim lembe ma wamito."

Kawoni nenedi! Kae i acaki mi wotho, Captain Peleg ku Captain Bildad gibedu kawothu ku cing gi malo iwi quarter-deck, calu nangu gibedu jutel wi mony i nam, man bende i kabedo ceke mi nam. Man, ikum Captain Ahab, lanyuth moko ope ma podi juneno; ento, giwacu niya ebedo i ot. Ento i ngeye, paru ubedo, nya bedo pare ubedu piny ma pire tek ungo pi ketho yei ubed piny ma pek, man telo ne maber i nam. Andha, calu ma eca ubedu ngo tic pare, ento pa jaring mutukari; man calu ma podi edaru ngo maber—giwacu—pi meno, Captain Ahab udong piny. Man lembe eni zo nen ni piny ma rwinyo; asaga mandha i tic mi lworo, judongo mapol mi yei gi nyuthiri ngo iwi yei pi kare malac ingey tingo meli, ento gibedo iwi meza mi cabin, gibedo ku foc mi weko yei ku jurimo migi mi dhu nam, mapodi giweko yei pi bero ku jaring meli.

Ento ubedu ungo ku kare madwong mi paru ikum lembe ne, kum Captain Peleg dong ubedu kwo. Nen calu etimo pol pa wec man cik, ento Bildad epe.

"I ngeye, wun awiya pa coo," ewacu, ma jururing yei gibedo i wi yei madit. “Mr. Starbuck, ring gi ngeye.”

“Gwok hema keca!”—ubedo cik mukende. Calu ma alar awacu, whalebone marquee eni juyike ngo kende kende i port; man iwi yei mi Pequod, pi oro pyer adek, cik mi goyo hema ungeyere ni piny mukende ma lubo tingo meli.

"Man the capstan! Rimo man polo!—juk!"-ubedo cik mukende, man jurutic gi ringo pi cing gi.

Kawoni pi bedo piny ma pek, kabedo ma jararing meli bedo i iye utiye thenge ma kore pa yei. Man keni Bildad, ma, ku Peleg, jubed jung'eyo, ikum judongo pare mukende, ubedu acel ikin ju ma giwotho ku ndege i dhu nam—jubeparu niya edaru doko jatel wi yei pi gwoko cul pa Nantucket ni meli ceke ma en ubedu ka timo tic i iye, kum ebed etelo wi yei mange ngo—Bildad, awacu, copo bedu nia kawoni etiye ka timo tic mi telo wi yei ma dit wok ikum anchor, man ikind kare moko wer ma nen calu psalmody ma rac, mi cwalu cing windlass, ma ukwayu wer moko ikum anyira i Booble Alley, ku cwiny maber. Ento, nindo adek mukadho ngo, Bildad uwacu igi niya wer moko ci ma rac jubi yiyu ngo i yei mi Pequod, asaga mandha i kare ma pek migi tiye piny; man Charity, nyamin, uketho kopi ma nok pa Watts i kabedo pa jadwar acel acel.

Ikinde marom, ma eneno wi dul mange mi yei, Captain Peleg ugam ucungo man ugam ucungo yo piny i yo ma lworo. Abedo paru nya ecopu nyayu yei i pii mapodi anchor utingo ngo; m'umbe paru para acungu iwi cinga, man awacu ni Queequeg ni etim kumeno, ka paru pi peku ma wan ario zoo wawok kud i iye, i caku wotho ku jajok ma kumeno pi jaring mutukari. Abedo ka kweyo cwinya gira, kadi ango, ku paru niya i Bildad ma jamutoro copo nwangu both moko, kadi nang ebedo oro pare miya abiro ku pyer abiro wi abiro; kinde ma awinjo cinga ma tek i ngeye, man aloko wanga, cwinya unyai ikum nen pa Captain Peleg ma utingo tyende kud i dhugola para. Eni ubedu tuko para mir acel.

"Eni yore ma gi timo kudu i tic mi juwil piny?" ekok. "Cwic, in wi rombe; cwir, man itur ng'ut peri! Pirango ngo icwir, awacu, wun ceke—cwir! Quohog! cwir, in chap ku yuk wic makwar; cwir keca, Scotch-cap; cwir, in pants macol. Cwir, awacu, wun ceke, man cwir wangwu woko!" Man ewacu kumeno, ewotho ku windlass, keca ku keca etiyo ku tyende ma yot, ma Bildad ma rwinyo ngo umediri ku telo ku psalmody pare. Aparo ni, Captain Peleg umaku ubedu ka madhu piny moko tin.

I ajiki ne, meli ne ucungo, yei ne ucungo, man wacidho. Ubedo nindo mi tho pa Kristu ma nyanok man ma ngic; man calu ceng ma nyanok mi yo malu udoko dyewor, wanwango nia wabedo mabor iwi nam ma ngic, ma pii ma ngic pare uketho wabedo i pee, calu i jam lwiny ma leng. Laini mabor pa lak iwi bulwarks ubedo ka rieny i lero pa dwe; man calu lak lyec moko ma dit, icicles madongo ma ucungo ucungo iwi thwol.

Lank Bildad, calu jaring mutukari, utelo wi dul ma kwong mir acel, man nja ku nja, calu yei macon ne ucwale i nam macol, man ucwalu koyo ma ngic ikume, man yamu ukok, man thwol ugoy, wer pare ma ucungo uwinjere,—

      “Podho ma leng ma kadho pii ma pong,
Cung ku kendi macol ma kwo.
Dong ni Juyahudi Kanaan ma con ucungo,
Kinde ma Jordan ubedo ka ngwec ikind gi.

Wec ma leng eca ugam uwinjiri ira maber uloyu. Gibedu ku gen man nyak. Kadi bende dyewor ma ngic mi ngico i nam Atlantic, kadi bed tyend para man jaket para ma pii, podi, eca nen ira, kabedo maber madwong ma bino; man meads man glades ma gibedo vernal ma rondo ku rondo, ma lum ma dongo i spring, ma juwotho ngo, ma juweko ngo, udong i kind cwir.

I ajiki ne wanwango rwom ma malu, ma jururing ndege ario ne dong jumito ngo. Yei ma tek ma ubedu kudwa ucaku ngwec ku yei.

Ubedo lembe ma nyayu anyonga man epe, kite ma Peleg ku Bildad gi nwango kudu i kare eni, asaga Captain Bildad. Pi lworo mi weko, podi; ebedo ku lworo madit mi weko, pi ber, yei ma ucidho i wotho mabor man ma rac—i ng'om mi Capes ma koth ucwir; yei ma jubolo i iye sente pare mapol ma enwangu matek; meli, ma ngati moko ma con ubedo jatel wi meli; jadit moko ma oro pare rom ku pare, ecaku wok kendo ku lworo pa lak ma timo kisa; emaru ngo ni yero foyofoc ni piny moko ma upong ku miti pare,—Bildad ma jacan ubedo pi kare malac; uwotho iwi deck ku wotho ma nyayu lworo; ringo piny i cabin ni wecu wec mi foyofoc mange keca; kendo ebino iwi yei, man eneno yo piny; uneno yo piny ma lac man ma jik ungo, ma ucungo kende ku ngom mabor ma nen ungo ma yo piny; uneno yo piny; uneno malo; uneno yo cinge man yo cinge; uneno kabedo ceke man kama moko mbe; man i ajiki, ma etingo thung iwi cinge, etingo Peleg ma tek ku cinge, man etingo ler, pi kare manok ecungu man eneno wange ku tego cwiny, calu ma ewacu kumae, "Kadi kumeno, jarimba Peleg, acopo ciro; ee, acopo."

Pi Peleg en gire, egam etingo kudu calu jafilosofi; ento pi lembe pare mi ryeko, pii wange ubedu ka mol i wange, kinde ma ler ne ubinu kago. Man en de, eringo ngo nyanok kud i cabin ni deck—kawoni wec piny, man kawoni wec ku Starbuck, jatel wi tic.

Ento, i ajikine, eloko wange ibang wadi, ku neno ma tok cen ikume,—“Captain Bildad—bin, wadi meli macon, umitu wacidh. Dwog i kabedo madit keca! Yei ahoy! Cung ka bino kago, kawoni! Bed ma ling, ma ling!—bin, Bildad, awobi—wac lembe peri mir ajiki. Bed ku gum, Starbu——Mr. wu, Mr. Flask—ber man gum maber iri—man tin eni oro adek abibedo ku cam mi diewor ma lyet piwu i Nantucket macon man cen!”

"Mungu umii mugisa iwu, man wubed i gwok pare maleng, dhanu," Bildad ma jadit uwacu, ma nen ungo. "Ageno nia koth ubibedo maber kawoni, kara Captain Ahab ubed ku ceng maber kende ma emito, man wubibedo kugi madwong i wotho mi ng'om ma ngic ma wubecidho i iye. Bed ma ling i dwar, wu juwadi. Kud wuketh yei m'umbe yeny, wu ju ma timo harpooners; cedar maber ma rangine tar unyai ungo ku sente adek kubang oro. wilo rwo peri de. Stubb; ecaku pii nyanok, aparu. Ka wumulo cula, Mr. Flask, kud iketh cheese eca piny pi kare malac, Mr. Starbuck ebinyotho.

"Bin, bin, Kapitan Bildad; wek wec,-cidhi!" man kumeno, Peleg utingo kude pio pio, man gin ario zoo gipodho i yei.

Meli ku yei gipokere; yamo ma ngic man ma ngic mi diewor ucwir ikindgi; gull ma kok ucungo iwi wiya; meli ario ne ucungo matek; wamiyo foc adek ma tek, man wapodho i nam Atlantic kende.





THEK 23. The Lee Shore.

I dul moko mukadho, Bulkington acel juweco pire, jaracik mabor, ma wok kud i nam manyen, ma junwango i New Bedford i ot welo.

Kinde ma i dyewor mi ngico ma ngic, Pequod ubolo thwol pare mi culo kwor i pii ma ngic ma rac, nga ma acopo neno ucungu i kore ento Bulkington! Aneno ku lworo man lworo i kum jadhum, ma i kind kare mi ngico edaru wok kud i wotho mi oro angwen ma rac, ma copo cidho kendu pi kare mange ma tek. Ngom ne nen ni lyethu i tyende. Piny maber uloyu utiye piny ma juweco pire ngo; poyo wic ma thuc nyayu ngo epitaphs; dul mir abusiel eni utiye liel pa Bulkington ma epe ku kidi. Wek awac kende nya etimo ire calu meli ma yamu ucwale, ma ube ringo marac iwi ngom ma yamo ucwale. Port ne copo miyo kony; port utiye marac; I dhu nam utiye bedo maber, bedo maber, kidi mi mac, cam mi diewor, bongo ma mor, jurimo, piny ceke ma ber ni tho mwa. Ento i yamu ma tek, dhu nam, ngom, ubedo ariti madit pa yei; umitu ebed ku jolo welo zo; mulo ngom acel kende, kadi nya edaru camu keel, ecopu ketho ebedo ka yeng yeng. Ku tego pare zo epongu yei ceke kud i dhu nam; i timo kumeno, elweny 'ku yamu ma fain copo cwale i pacu; yenyo ngom mi nam ma nyai kendu; pi kabedo mi gwok ma rwinyo i ariti; jarimbe acel kende jadhoge ma rac!

Ingeyo kawoni, Bulkington? Nen nya ibe neno lemandha ma dhanu copo yio ngo; nia paru ma thuc man ma iye tek utiye kende kende kero pa tipo mi gwoko bedo agonya pa nam pare; podi yamu ma tek mi polo ku mi ng'om ubetimo yik mi cwale i dhu nam ma rac man ma dhanu timo i iye opii?

Ento calu ma i ngom kende re ma bedo lemandha ma malu, ma dhu dhu, ma rwinyo calu Mungu—kum kumeno, ber ni tho i ngom ma rwinyo ma rwinyo, ma sagu ni rwinyo iwi pii, kadi nangu eca ubedo ber! Pi worm-like, dong, oh! ngati makani ma copo cidho i ngom! Lworo pa piny ma rac! can eni zo tiye mananu? Bed ku cwiny, bed ku cwiny, O Bulkington! Bed ku lworo, demigod! I malu kud i pii peri mi nam-ma rwinyo—i malu, ucungo apotheosis peri!





THEK 24. Jatel wi lembe.

Calu Queequeg man an dong wacaku tic mi mako rec ma dit; man calu tic mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whale udoko ni lembe ma dhanu mi ngom gineno ni tic ma rac man ma rac; kumeno, atiye ku lworo mi ketho wu, wu dhanu mi ngom, ungiyo lembe ma rac ma jutimo iwa wan ju ma gi dwar rec.

I acaki, ecopu bedu piny ma pire tek ungo ni ketho lemandha, nia ikind dhanu mapol, tic mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whale jukwanu ngo i rwom marom ku ma julwongo ni liberal professions. Ka ngati moko ma jung'eyo ngo jukelo i kind dhanu mi adhura madit, ecopu medo paru pa dhanu ikum ber pare, tek ju nyuthe i kampuni calu jabodho, waci; man tek ni lubre ku judongo mi nam eketho nying SWF (Sperm Whale Fishery) i kad pare mi limo, timo ma kumeno jubikethe ni lembe ma nyayu lworo man ma nyayu lworo.

Labongo jiji, the lembe acel madit m'uketho ng'om ukwero miyowa woro iwa ju ma gikwo i nam, utiye eni: giparu niya, ka ber, tic mwa utiye tic mi neko ringu; man nia ka wabe timo tic eno, wabedo ikind piny ma rac matung tung. Wabedo ju ma poko ringu, eni lemandha. Ento ju ma juku ringu, de, man ju ma juku ringu ku badge ma rimo ucwir ubedo Martial Commanders ma ng'om zoo maru ni mio igi deyo. Man pi lembe mi bedo maleng pa tic mwa, kawoni dong ibi nwangu lembe moko ma podi jungeyo ngo, man ma, ikum lembe ceke, bi ketho sperm whale-ship i kind piny maleng mi ng'om maeni. Ento kadi yio lembe ma juweco pire ni bedu lemandha; ango ma rwinyo ma rwinyo pa yei ma julwongo ni whale-ship ma juporo ku ringo ma rwinyo ma rwinyo pa lwiny ma askari madwong dwogo kud i iye pi madhu i foc pa mon? Man ka paru mi lworo umedire paru pa dhanu ikum tic pa askari; wek aketh cwinyi nia dhanu mapol ma gikwo i lwiny ma giwotho iwi battery, gicopo dwoko cen pio pio ka gineno yibe ma dit pa sperm whale, ma nyayu yamo iwi wiye. Pien lworo pa dhanu ango ka juporo ku lworo man timo pa Mungu!

Ento, kadi nang ngom ube lubo tok wan ju ma gi dwar rec madongo, ento edaru mio iwa woro madit uloyu; ee, woro ma dit! kum tapers, lamps, man candles ceke ma wango iwi ngom, wango, calu macon i kabedo mi woro madwong, pi dwong mwa!

Ento nen lembe eni i yore mukende; pim ku kilo matung tung; nen ango ma wan whalemen wabedo, man wabedo.

Pirango ma Dutch i rundi pa De Witt ubedu ku judongo pa yei migi mi mako rec? Pirango ma Louis XVI. pa France, ku sente pare gire, yiko yei mi maku rec ma dit kud i Dunkirk, man lwongo ku mwolo i adhura meno juru ot moko nyo ario kud i cula mwa mi Nantucket? Pirango ma Britain ikind oro 1750 ku 1788 uculo ni ju ma gwoko rec pare ma kadhu £1,000,000? Man mir ajiki, ecopo bedu nenedi nia wan whalemen mi Amerika dong wabedo ku wel ma sagu whalemen mukende mi ng'om zoo; wotho ku yei ma kadhu dak abiro; judongo ku dhanu elifu apar wi abora; kubang oro tiyo ku 4,000,000 pa dola; yei ma wel migi, i kare ma giwotho, $20,000,000! man kubang oro wakelo i dhu nam mwa piny maber pa $7,000,000. Lembe eni zo bino nenedi, kan epe ku piny moko ma tek i mako rec?

Ento eni epe nusu; nen kendu.

Aloko ku cwinya ceke, nia jagor ma ngeyo lembe mi ng'om zoo copo ngo, pi kwo pare, nyuthu piny acel kende ma kelo kuc, ma ikind oro pyer abusiel mukadho ubedo ku copo madit iwi ng'om zoo, ka jukwanu i dul acel, ma sagu tic ma malu man ma tek mi mako rec. I yo acel ku mange, edaru nyayu lembe ma pire tek i igi gigi, man ma pire tek i lembe migi ma lubo, ma ketho rec ma julwongo ni whaling copo bedu ni min Misiri, ma unyolo awiya gire ma ic kud i ic pare. Ecopo bedu tic ma gen ope, ma thum ungo ni ketho piny eni i karatasi. Wek cing moko kende uromo. Pi oro madwong mukadho, yei ma julwongo ni whale-ship ubedo jatel wi tic mi tero rec i kabedo mabor man ma dhanu ngeyo ngo iwi ngom. En edaru ngiyo nam man cula ma ubedu ungo ku chart, kama Cook nyo Vancouver ubedu kawothu i iye. Ka jurulwiny mi Amerika ku mi Europe kawoni gibewotho ku kuc i dhu nam ma con ubedo marac, wek gi foc pi woro man dwong pa yei ma julwongo ni whale-ship, ma ugam unyutho igi yo, man ma ugam unyutho ikindgi ku dhanu ma rac. Gicopo kwero calu ma gibitimo ni judongo mi Exploring Expeditions, ju tedo peri, ju Krusensterns peri; ento awacu nia judongo mapol ma nying gi ungeye ngo giwok kud i Nantucket, ma gibedo judongo, man madongo uloyu Cook peri man Krusenstern peri. Pien i bedo migi ma kony ungo, gin, i pii ma nyai ma nyai, man i dhu nam mi cula mi javelin, gi lwiny ku lembe ma nyayu lworo man lworo ma Cook ku jurucik pare mi nam man muskets pare gi copo timo ngo. Lembe ceke ma jutimo i South Sea Voyages macon, piny eni ubedu kende kende piny ma dhanu mi Nantucketers mwa ma tek gikwo kudu. Pol kare, wotho ma Vancouver umiyu dul adek, coo eni gineno nia giromo ngo ni bedo piny i karatasi mi yei. Ah, ng'om! Oh, ng'om!

Ma podi tic mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whale ugam uthum i Cape Horn, tic mi kuloka ubedu ngo ento mi colonial, man ubedu ku timo mi buto ento mi colonial, ubedu katimere ikind Europe ku theng ngom mi Spain ma ubedu i dhu nam mi Pacific. Ubedo jadwar rec ma kwong ma uturo cik mi jealousy pa rwoth pa Spain, ma umulo thek eca; man, ka kabedo uyio, ecopu nyuthu kamaleng kite ma ju ma gikwo i nam eca gicaku gonyo ko Peru, Chili, man Bolivia kud i cing Old Spain, man yik pa democracy ma nja i kabedu eca.

America madit ma ni yo mukende mi ng'om, Australia, jumiyo ni ng'om ma dhanu ungiyu ku lemandha. I ngei nwangu pare mir acel ma dhanu mi Netherlands unwangu, yei mange ceke ugam uweko dhu nam eca pi kare malac; ento yei ma julwongo ni whale ugam utuc keca. Meli ma julwongo ni whale-ship ubedo min mandha pa dul pa rec ma kawoni utiye ku tego. Bende, i acaki pa bedo pa ju Australia mir acel, ju ma gi ay kud i ngom mange juboth gi wang mapol kud i kec ku biscuit maber pa yei ma julwongo ni whale-ship ma uketho cinge i pii migi. Cula ma jukwanu ngo i Polynesia zoo giyio lemandha marom, man gitimo foc mi kuloka ni meli ma julwongo ni whale-ship, ma uketho yo ni jukwenda man juwil, man i kare mapol utero jukwenda macon i kabedu migi mir acel. Ka ngom ma juketho i iye cing ario, Japan, ubedo ber, eca kende re ma jubi miyo ire foc; kum dong etiye i dhugola.

Ento ka, i wang lembe eni zo, podi iwacu nia dwar pa rec ma julwongo ni whaling epe ku winjiri maber kude, eca dong atiye ayika ni tiyo ku pala ma romo pyerabic ku in keca, man niwodho iri ku helmet ma jupoko kare ku kare.

Whale epe ku jagor ma nyinge uyik, man whale epe ku jagor ma nyinge uyik, icopo wacu.

Whale epe jagor ma nyinge uyik, man whale epe jagor ma nyinge uyik? Nga ma ukyewu lapor mir acel pa Leviathan mwa? Ng'a ento Job ma tek! Man ngati makani ma ukyewu lembe mir acel ikum wotho mi mako rec ma dit? Nga, ento rwoth ma nok uloyu Alfred the Great, ma, ku kyeu pare mi rwoth, utingo wec pa Other, jadwar rec mi Norway mi kare eni! Man ngati makani ma uwacu foc mwa maber i ot loc? Nga, ento Edmund Burke!

Andha, ento whalemen gire gibedo jok ma rac; gipe ku rimo maber i ler migi.

Rimo maber ope i ler migi? Gitiye ku piny moko maber uloyu rimo pa rwoth keca. Min Benjamin Franklin ubedo Mary Morrel; i ngeye, ku nyom, Mary Folger, acel ikin ju ma con ma gikwo i Nantucket, man kwaro pa dul pa Folgers man ju ma tiyo ku harpooneers—gi zo gibedo juwadi man jupagi pa Benjamin—i ceng'ni gibewotho ku cuma ma nyai kud i thenge acel mi ng'om nitundo i thenge mange.

Ber kendu; ento i ngeye dhanu ceke giyio nia mako rec ma julwongo ni whaling epe ku woro.

Whaling epe ku woro? Whaling ubedo piny madit! Nimakere ku cik pa England macon, rec ma julwongo ni whale julwonge ni "rec pa rwoth." *

Oh, eni kende kende! Rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale en gire de ungiyure ngo i yore moku madit.

Whale ne ungiyu ngo i yore moku madit? Ikind loc madit ma jumiyo ni jatel wi mony mi Roma ikare ma emondo i adhura madit mi ng'om, cogo pa rec ma dit, ma jukelo kud i dhu nam mi Syria, ubedu piny ma pire tek uloyu i wotho ma jubedu ka goyo.*

*Nen dul ma lubo pi lembe mukende ikum wi lembe eni.

Yeye, pi en ibe cite; ento, wec ango ma imito, epe ku woro mandha i mako rec.

Bedo mbe ku woro i mako rec? Ber pa lwongu mwa polo kende nyutho. Cetus ubedo dul pa lakalatwe in theng ngom ma yo piny! Dong epe! Dwok kofi peri piny i wang Czar, man iter i Queequeg! Dong epe! Angeyo ngati moko ma, i kwo pare, edaru mako rec ma dit miya adek ku pyer abic. Aneno jadhum eca ni ngati ma pire tek uloyu ma sagu jatel wi mony macon ma ugam ukwo pi maku adhura madwong ma jugwoko ku cel.

Man, pi an, ka, ku yore moku ci, tiye piny moku ma podi ungiyu ngo i iya; tek acopo nwangu nying maber i ng'om ma nok ento ma ling ma acopo bedu ngo ku miti ma rwinyo; tek i ngeye abitimo piny moko ma, iwi lembe ceke, ngati copo timo maber uloyu ma eweko timo ngo; ka, i tho para, ju ma timo tic para, nyo ju ma giculo banja para, ginwangu MSS moko ma pire tek. i kom mesa para, man keni amiyo woro man dwong pa dwar pa rec; pi whale-ship ubedo Yale College para man Harvard para.





CHAPTER 25. Postscript.

Pi bero pa whhaling, acopo ketho lembe moko cii ento lembe ma jukwanu. Ento ingey ketho lembe pare, jawang lembe ma umitu ejuk paru moko ma rwinyo ngo, ma copo weco maber ikum lembe pare—jawang lembe ma kumeno, ecopo bedo ngo jatel wi lembe?

Jung'eyo maber nia i saa mi ketho rwoth ku dhaku pa rwoth, kadi wa ma kawoni, lembe moko ma nyayu anyonga ikum tic migi ukadhu. Utiye saltcellar mi ng'om, ma julwong'o kumeno, man ecopu bedu castor mi ng'om. Kite ma gitiyo kudu ku kadu, andha—nga ma ngeyo? Angeyu andha, kadi ango, nia wic pa rwoth juwiro ku moo i saa mi rwoth pare, kadi nya wic pa salad. Ecopu bedu, kadi kudu, nya giwiro moo ikume ku lembakeca mi ketho iiye timo tic maber, calu ma giwiro moo ikum cuma? Lembe madwong copo bedu ma jubeparu kudu keni, ikum woro ma pire tek pa yub eni, kum i kwo ma dhanu zo waworo ento marac man ma nyayu lewic ikum ngati ma wiro wic pare, man ewinjo nguc pa wic meno. I andha, jadit ma dit ma tiyo ku moo mi yuk wic, kawang ikum yath, jadhum eca copo bedu ku kabedo moku i iye. Calu cik, ecopu bedu ngo piny madwong i rwom pare zo.

Ento piny acel kende ma umitu juketh iwie kany, utiye eni—moo ango ma jutiyo kudu i kare mi yik pa rwoth? Andha ecopu bedu ngo moo mi olive, nyo moo mi macassar, nyo moo pa castor, nyo moo pa bear, nyo moo pa train, nyo moo pa cod-liver. Dong ango ma ecopu bedu, ento moo pa ic i kite pare ma juyubu ngo, ma epe ku ler, ma leng loyo moo zo?

Par ikum lembe eni, wu dhanu mi Britain ma gen! wan whalemen wamiyo rwoth peri ku rwoth peri piny mi rwoth!





CHAPTER 26. Knights ku Squires.

Jatel wi Pequod ubedo Starbuck, ma ubedo ja Nantucket, man Quaker ku thek pare. Ebedo jadhum ma bor, man ma cwinye tek, man kadi nya junyole i dhu nam ma ngic, enen ni ecopo ciro kabedo ma ngic, kume ubedo matek calu biskuit ma jutedo wang aryo. Ka jutere i Indies, rimo pare ma kwo copo nyothre ngo calu kongo ma juketho i agulu. Umitu ebed junyole i kare moko mi kec man kec, nyo i ceng moko mi kec ma ngom pare ungeyere pire. Kende kende oro pyer adek kende ma eneno; kare mi cwir eca uketho piny pare zo ma pire tek ungo. Ento eni, bedo pare ma nyap, nen calu lanyuth pa nyayu lworo man lworo, ento nen calu lanyuth pa two moku cii. Ebedo kende kende lworo pa jal ne. Ebedo ngo ngati ma nen marac; ma tung tung. Adila pare maleng man ma tek ubedo ber mandha; man ma ucungo i iye, man ma juyiko ku yot kum man tego, calu ja Misiri ma judwoko kwo, Starbuck eni nen calu etiye ayika ni ciro pi oro madwong ma ubebinu, man ni ciro kare ku kare, calu kawoni; kadi bed ni Polar snow nyo ceng ma lyeth, calu patent chronometer, tego pare mi iye ubedu ber i kabedo zo. Ka ineno i wange, nen calu ineno cal ma podi udong pa peko ma romo elufu acel ma enwang kudu i kwo pare. Ng'atu ma ling, ma ling, ma kwo pare pol kare ubedo pantomime mi timo, man epe chapter mi dwal. Ento, pi bedo moth pare man tego cwiny pare, ubedu ku kura moko i iye ma saa moko ubedu ka loko, man i kare moko ubedu ka nen ni edaru kadhu kura mange ceke. Ku nen pa ngati ma timo tic i nam, man ku woro madit, bedo kende ma pii i kwo pare uketho ebedo ku yioyic ma tek; ento ni kit yioyic ma kumeno, ma i dilo moko nen ni wok kud i ryeko ento kud i ngec ma rwinyo. Lembe ma nen woko man lembe ma iye ubedu pare. Man ka saa moko piny eni nyayu cwinye, poyo wic pare mabor pa dhaku pare man nyathin pare mi Cape, uketho cwinye utur lee dit kud i kura pare macon, man uketho edoko tek ni lembe ma nyayu lworo ma, i dhanu moko ma cwiny migi leng, juku timo pa dhanu ma rac, ma nyayu lworo i dhanu mange aloka loka pa tic mi mako rec. "Abi bedu ku ngati moko ope i yei para," Starbuck uwacu, "ma lworo ngo rec madit." Ku lembe eni, nen calu emito wacu, ungo kende kende nia tego cwiny ma gen man ma pire tek uloyu utiye ma wok kud i rwom maber pa peko ma juwok kudu, ento nya ngati ma lworo ope i iye utiye wadi ma rac uloyu ma sagu ngati ma lworo.

"Eyo, eyo," Stubb uwacu, wadi mir aryo, "Starbuck, keca, utiye ngati ma gwoko lembe maber akeca ma icopo nwangu kama moku ci i kabedo mi mako rec eni." Ento wabeci neno ango ma wec "gwok" thee kudu ka jutiyo kudu ni ngati moko calu Stubb, nyo jadwar moko ci.

Starbuck ubedu ngo jatel wi lwiny ingey peko; i iye tego cwiny ubedu ngo paru; ento piny moko ma pire tek ire, man kare ku kare i cinge i lembe ceke ma pire tek. Mi mede, eparu, nyalo bedo, nia i tic mi mako rec, tego cwiny ubedo piny acel madit ma pire tek i yei, calu ringu dyang man mugati pare, man kud juwodhe marac. Pieno ebedo ungo ku miti mi cidho piny pi rec ma julwongo ni whales ingey ceng podho; kadi pi mediri ni lwiny ku rec ma ubedu ka lwiny kude. Pi, Starbuck upenu, an atiye kany i nam madit eni mi nego rec ma dit pi kwo para, man ungo ni nego gi pi kwo migi; man nia dhanu dak ku dak acel junego Starbuck ung'eyo maber. Peko ango ma ubedo pa wone? Kan, i pii ma thuc, ecopo nwangu tyend umego pare ma utur?

Ku ponji ma kumeno i iye, man, ma sagu, jumiyo ire lembe moko ma nyayu yioyic, calu ma juweco pire; tego cwiny pa Starbuck eni ma copo, kadi kudu, podi udongo, umitu ubedu tek mandha. Ento ubedu ngo i kura maber ma dhanu moko ubedu ku yik maber, man ku lembe ma rac ma enwang kudu man poyo wic pare; ubedu ngo i kite ma piny eni copo ketho ko piny moko i iye, ma, i thenge maber, copo wok kud i cinge, man wango tego cwinye zo. Man ku tego cwinye kadi nang epe, ebedo tego cwiny ma nenre i dhanu moko ma cwinygi tek, ma, kadi nang gicungo matek i lwiny ku nam, nyo yamu, nyo rec madongo, nyo lembe moko ci ma nyayu lworo mi ng'om, ento gicopo ngo ni cung matek i kum lembe ma nyayu lworo, kum lworo mi tipo, ma sawa moko nyayu lworo i dhanu man copo nyayu lworo i dhanu.

Ento ka lembe ma bino bino nyuthu i lembe moku ci, rwinyo pa tego pa Starbuck ma jacan, acopo bedu tek ni kyewo; kum etiye piny ma nyayu can lee, ungo ma nyayu lworo, ni nyuthu rwinyo pa tego cwiny. Dhanu copo nen ni dhanu ma rac calu joint stock-companies man thek; ju ma timo lembe marac, ju ma mingo, man ju ma neko dhanu copo bedu nuti; coo copo bedu ku wang ma rac man ma nyap; ento dhanu, i paru pare, etiye ngati ma pire tek man ma rieny, piny ma dit man ma rieny, ma iwi goro moku ci ma nyayu lewic i iye dhanu wadi ceke gi ringo ni bolo kendi migi ma wel migi tek. Kit coo ma leng ma wawinjo i iwa giri, mabor akeca i iwa, ma udong maber kadi nang kite ma woko nen ni rwinyo; rimo mol ku can madit ikum neno pa ngati ma tek pare unyothere. Bende, bedo moth kende, i neno ma rac ma kumeno, copo juku ngo lembe pare ikum lakalatwe ma yio. Ento woro ma dit maeni ma abeweco pire, epe woro pa rwoth man bongo, ento woro ma dit ma epe ku bongo. Ibineno ka ler i cing ma tingo jamb tic nyo ma tingo spike; rwom pa democracy ma, ku cing dhanu ceke, wok ma rwinyo kud i bang Mungu; En gire! Mungu madit mandha! Ikind man i ngete pa democracy zo! Bedo pare i kabedo ceke, rom pa Mungu mwa!

Ka, dong, ni ju ma giwotho i nam, man ju ma gikwo i nam man ju ma giweko lembe, abidwoko igi kit ma malu, kadi nang edoko col; keth ikum gi berocwiny ma rac; tek kadi ngati ma cwinye lith, nyalo bedo ngati ma piny uloyu, ikind gi zo, saa moko bi tingo kume iwi got ma malu; ka abimulo cing jara tic meno ku ler moko ma leng; tek abiketho rangi iwi ceng pare ma rac; man i kum ju ma kwedo lembe ceke ma dhanu copo timo, nyuth ira i iye, in i Tipo mi bedo marom, ma ipoko bongo acel mi ker pa dhanu iwi dhanu para zo! Bed moth ira i iye, in Mungu madit mi democracy! ma ukwetu ngo ni jadubu macol, Bunyan, macol, ma jamutoro; In ma irwako ku ith macol ma juyubu wang ario, cing macol man ma can pa Cervantes macon; In ma itingo Andrew Jackson kud i kind got; ma ubolo en iwi kana mi lwiny; ma uketho edoko malu uloyu komker! In ma, i wotho peri mi tego, mi ng'om, ibedo ka kwanyo judongo peri ma juyeru kud i kind judongo pa rwoth; ciro ira i iye, O Mungu!





CHAPTER 27. Knights ku Squires.

Stubb ubedo wadi mir aryo. Ebedo nyathin mi Cape Cod; man kumeno, nimakere ku kite ma dhanu mi kabedu ne tiyo kudu, julwonge ni Cape-Cod-man. Anyonga-cidho-ber; kadi craven kadi valiant; maku peko ma bino ku nen pa lembe; man podi etiye i peko madit mi ngwec, etiye ka timo tic matek, ebedo moth man ecokiri calu journeyman joiner ma timo tic pi oro. Ng'atu ma timo lembe maber, yot, man ma ling, etelo wi yei pare ma julwongo ni whale-boat calu ve lembe ma rac uloyu ubedo cam mi diewor kende, man jurutic pare zo gilwongo welo. Ebedo ngati ma pire tek ikum kit ma juyubu kudu dul pare mi yei, calu jararing mutukari macon ikum bedo maber pa sanduku pare. Kinde ma ubedu kago ku rec ma dit, i kare ma lwiny ubekadhu, egam etingo lance pare ma ber man ma timo lembe marac, calu ngati ma ube tiyo ku nyundo pare. Ebed ewero wer pare macon mi rigadig ma ebed ewotho ku lei ma rac uloyu. Tiyu kude pi kare malac, pi Stubb eni, uloko lak tho ni kom ma yot. Gin ma eparu ikum tho kende, epe. Ka nyo ebed eparu ikum lembene, ecopu bedu penji; ento, tek ebedo ku gum mi ketho paru pare i yo ma kumeno i ngei cam mi diewor maber, labongo jiji, calu jaring yei maber, egam etingo ni bedo lwongo pa jurucik ni rwinyo malu, man ni bedo keca, ikum piny moko ma ecopo nwangu ka elubo cik, man ungo pio pio.

Ango, nyalo bedu, ku piny mukende, ma uketho Stubb ubedo ngati ma yot, ma lworo mbe, ma ubewothu ku mutoro ku peko mi kwo i ng'om ma upong ku juwil liel, ma gicokiri piny ku piny migi; ango ma ukonye ni kelo kura pare maber; piny eca ucikere ni bedu pipe pare. Pi, calu ume, pipe pare ma nok, macol ubedo acel ikin kit wange. Icopo bedu nya ecopo wok woko kudi kavutu pare manangu epe ku ume calu manangu epe ku pipe pare. Ebedo ku rwom pa paip mapol keca ma juketho i iye piny, ma juketho i rack, kama yot ni cinge; man, kare ku kare ma edok i iye, ebed ewotho gi zo iwi rwom acel, ecaku wango acel acel nitundu i thum pa dul; man idwoko gi kendu kara gibed ayika kendu. Pi, kinde ma Stubb uketho bongo, kakare ni ketho tyende i trosers pare, eketho pipe pare i dhoge.

Awacu ni madhu taba ma nja eca ucikere ni bedu the lembe acel, kadi nya, pa kura pare ma ukoc; kum dhanu ceke ung'eyo nia yamu mi ng'om eni, kadi i dhu nam nyo i pii, utiye ku peko ma lee dit pa dhanu mapol ma githo ka giwotho; man calu i kare pa two cholera, dhanu moko wotho ku bongo ma juyiko ku camphora i dhoggi; kumeno, kumeno bende, ikum peko mi tho, iro pa taba pa Stubb copo bedu ni tiyo calu piny moko ma jwigo two.

Jarimo mir adek ubedo Flask, ma ubedo ja Tisbury, i Martha's Vineyard. Awobi ma nyanok, ma tek, ma rangi pare nya nya, ma maru lwiny ikum rec ma julwongo ni whales, ma nen calu eparu niya leviathans madongo eca gi timo lembe marac ikume; man kumeno ubedu lembe ma pire tek ire, ni nyotho gi kare ku kare ma girombo kudu. Ebedo rwenyo ikum paru pare mi woro pi lembe madwong ma nyayu anyonga pa dit migi man yore migi mi timo lembe ma nyang ikum dhanu; man tho ni piny moko ci calu lworo pa peko moku ci ma copo wok kud i rwate kudgi; nia i paru pare marac, wondrous whale ubedo kende kende kit oyo ma dit, nyo oyo ma pii, ma mitu kende kende timo lembe manok man tiyo ku sawa manok man peko pi nego man lwoko. Bedo ma engeyo ngo, lworo pare ma engeyo ngo uketho edoko ngati ma timo lembe marac ikum rec ma julwongo ni whales; elubu rec eni pi bero migi; man wotho mi oro adek i nam mi Cape Horn ubedu kende kende tuko maber ma rii pi kare malac. Calu musuma pa jabodho jupoko i musuma ma juyubu man musuma ma juturo; dong dhanu copo bedu ku apoka poka marom. Little Flask ubedo acel ikin ju ma juyubu; juyike ni mako matek man ni rii pi kare malac. Gilwonge ni King-Post iwi yei mi Pequod; kum, i kite, ecopu porre ku yen ma nyap, ma rwom pare lac ma julwongo ku nying eni ikind ju ma mako rec mi Arctic; man ma ku yore pa yen madwong ma ucungo i iye, konyowa ni gwoko yei i kum pii ma ngic ma wok kud i nam.

Kawoni ju adek eni—Starbuck, Stubb, man Flask, ubedu coo ma pire tek. Gin re ma gitelo wi yei adek pa Pequod calu judongo. I lwiny madit ma Captain Ahab copo ketho dul mony pare ni rwinyo iwi rec madongo, judongo adek eni gibedo judongo mi dul mony. Nyo, ma gibedo ku jam lwiny migi mabor, gibedo calu ju adek ma juyeru; kadi bende ju ma gi goyo harpoonees gibedo ju ma gi goyo javelins.

Man calu i tic mi mako rec ma julwongo eni, ngati acel acel nyo jatel wi tic, calu Gothic Knight macon, ebedo ku jatel wi yei pare nyo jatel wi yei pare, ma i kare moko emiyo ire pala manyen, ka ma kwong eca jutwoyo marac, nyo jutwoyo cinge i lwiny; man de, calu ma pol kare bedo ikind ju aryo eno, bedo karacelu man bedo jurimo; dong eber, nia i kabedu eni waketh piny ju ma gicoyo wer pa Pequod, man rwoth ango ma ngati acel acel ubedu kudu.

Mir acel ubedu Queequeg, ma Starbuck, jatel wi tic, eyiku pi bedo squire pare. Ento Queequeg ungeyere con.

I ngeye ubedo Tashtego, ja India ma ungiyo ngo kud i Gay Head, kabedo ma yo reto pa Martha's Vineyard, kama podi utiye iye dul pa dhanu ma rangi migi col, ma ubedu ka mio cula mi Nantucket ku jurutugo pare mapol ma tek. I tic mi mako rec, pol kare giwotho ku nying ma julwongo ni Gay-Headers. Yuk wic pa Tashtego ma bor, ma nyap, macol, cogo pare ma malu, man wange macol macol—pi ja India, Oriental i dit migi, ento Antarctic i nen migi ma rieny—maeni zo unyutho nia etiye rwoth pa rimo pa jukwenda ma cwinygi tek, ma, pi sayu New England moose madit, ugam ucwalu cing gi lum madongo. Ento dong edaru ngo lubo tok lei mi lum mi lum, Tashtego dong ubedo ka dwar i ngeye pa rec madongo mi nam; harpoon ma rwinyo ngo pa wode ma rwinyo maber pa thwol ma rwinyo pa won ot. Ka ineno rangi macol pa tyende ma nen calu thwol, icopo bedu nya iparu ikum yioyic pa jumoko ma con mi Puritan, man icopo yio nia ja India ma jamutoro eni ubedo wod pa rwoth pa tego pa polo. Tashtego ubedo Stubb jatel wi wadi mir aryo.

Mir adek ikind ju ma gi goyo harpoone ubedo Daggoo, ngati ma dit, macol calu makaa, ku tyende ma calu lamvor—Ahasuerus ma nen. I ithe ubedo ku cing ario macol, ma dit ma ju ma giwotho i yei gilwongo gi ni ring-bolts, man giweco ikum ketho top-sail halyards ikum gi. I kare ma fodi etie aradu, Daggoo ugam ucungo iwi yei ma julwongo ni whaler, m'ubedo i nam ma oling i dhu nam pare. Man podi abedo ngo kaka moko ci i ng'om ento i Afrika, Nantucket, man i cula mi pagan ma ju ma gicamu rec madongo giwotho i iye; man dong edaru kwo pi oro madwong kwo mi maku rec i yei pa wegi ma giparu ngo ikum kit dhanu ma gicwalo; Daggoo ukwo ku kura pare mi timo lembe ma rac, man ucungo calu giraffe, ewotho iwi yei ku rwom ma malu mi futi abusiel ku abic i soksi pare. Ubedo ku mwolo ma nen i wange; man jadit moku ma tar ucungu i nyime nen calu bendera ma tar ma bino pi kwayo lwiny mi dhugola. Mitu mi ngeyo, jara pa rwoth eni, Ahasuerus Daggoo, ubedu Squire pa Flask ma nok, ma nen calu jatugo mi chess ingete. Pi lembe ma udong pa kampuni pa Pequod, jubi wacu, nia tin eni ngati acel de ikind dhanu elufu mapol ma gitiye i wang mast ma jutiyo kudu i tic mi mako rec madit mi Amerika, utiye dhanu mi Amerika ma junyolu, kadi nang judongo ceke utiye. I lembe maeni, etiye marom ku rec mi Amerika ma julwongo ni whale fishery calu ku dul mony pa Amerika man dul mony man juruwil, man dul mony ma jutiyo kudu pi gieru pa American Canals man Railroads. Lembe marom, awacu, kum i lembe eni zo, dhanu mi Amerika ma gibedo i ngom mi Amerika gi miyo obwongo, dhanu mi ng'om zoo gi miyo ler. Wel ma nok pa ju ma mako rec ma julwongo ni whaling gi ubedo pa Azores, kama ju ma mako rec ma julwongo ni Nantucket whalers ma giwotho woko gibed giwotho i iye pi medo dhanu migi kud i kind ju ma gikwo i nam ma tek. I yo marom, ju ma gi mako yei mi Greenland ma giwok kud i Hull nyoke London, gimondo i cula mi Shetland, pi nwangu jutic migi. I kare ma gibecidho i pacu, gibolo gi keca kendu. Kite ma etiye kudu, ngati moko mbe ma copo wacu, ento dhanu mi cula nen calu gibedo ju ma loyo rec ma julwongo ni whalemen. Gibedu dhanu mi cula mi Pequod, Isolatoes de, alwongo gi kumeno, manangu angeyo ngo ngom pa dhanu, ento Isolato acel acel ukwo i ngom pare gire. Ento kawoni, ma gicungo iwi keel acel, nen pa Isolatoes eni! Anacharsis Clootz ma wok kud i cula mi nam zo, man kud i ng'om zo, ucidho ku Old Ahab i Pequod ni ketho lembe mi ng'om i wang' bar ma dhanu mapol kud i iye gidok kud i iye. Black Little Pip—etimo ngo—oh, ungo! ecidho i wange. Awobi mi Alabama ma can! I wi Pequod's forecastle macol, wubinene pi kare malac, ka goyo tambourine pare; i wang kare ma rondo ku rondo, kinde ma juore, i quarter-deck madit ma malu, juoro ire nia ebed ku malaika, man egoy tambourine pare i dwong; julwongo jalwor keni, julwongo jaratela keca!





THE 28. Ahab.

Pi nindu madwong ingey weko Nantucket, piny moko ope ma nen pa Captain Ahab. Juruot gibed gikonyo juwothgi kare ku kare i sawa, man pi lembe moko ci ma nen ungo, nen calu gin kende re ma gitelo wi yei; kende kende kare moko giwok kud i cabin ku cik ma nyawang man ma tek, ma i ngei lembe zo nen kamaleng ni gin kende re ma gimiyo cik. Eyo, rwoth migi madit man jatel wi dhanu ubedu keca, kadi nangu podi juneno ngo ku wang dhanu moko ci ma juyiyu ngo ni mondo i ot maleng mi ot.

Kare ceke ma adok malu iwi yei kud i sawa para ma piny, aneno ngeye pi nineno ka wang moko ma nen ungo; pi lworo para mir acel ikum jatel wi meli ma jungeye ngo, ma kawoni utiye i nam, udoko piny ma nyayu lworo. Eni ugam umedire i saa moko ku timo pa Elijah ma jajok ma ubedu ka dwogo i iya, ku tego ma nya wang ma nangu acopo paru ngo con. Ento acopo ciro gi marac, calu i lembe mange ma abino ayika ni ngiero pi timo ma rac pa jabila ma nen ungo pa nam. Ento kadi ango ma ubedu lworo nyo lworo—ni lwonge kumeno—ma awinjo, ento kare ku kare ma abino nineno i yei, nen calu epe ku twero mi gwoko paru ma kumeno. Pi kadi bende ju ma tiyo ku harpooneers, ku dul madit pa jurutic, gibedo ju ma timo lembe ma rac, ma timo lembe ma rac, man ma timo lembe matung tung ma sagu ju ma timo tic mi kuloka ma lembe ma ukadhu kud i iye uketho abedo ku ngec ikum gi, re asu aloko lembe eni—man aloko lembe eni—ni kit ma dhanu mi Scandina ma gikwo i iye giweko. Ento ubedu lembe pa judongo adek mi yei, juwadi, ma juyike ku tego mi jwigo paru ma rac eni, man mi kelo gen man anyonga i lembe ceke mi wotho. Ju adek maber, ma copo bedu judongo mi nam man coo, acel acel ku yore pare matung tung, jucopo nwangu ngo, man gin ceke ubedu dhanu mi Amerika; Nantucketer, Vineyarder, Cape man. Kawoni, ubedo nindo mi tho pa Kristu ma yei ne ucwalu kud i dhu nam pare, pi kabedo moko ma wabedo ku koth ma tek mi Polar, kadi nang kare ceke wabe ringo kud i iye ni yo piny; man ku digri acel acel man dakika moko ci mi latitude ma wawotho, moth moku waweko ngicu ma rac, man koth pare ma rac. Ubedo acel ikin ceng ma rwinyo ngo, ento podi ubedo macol man macol ma ukethu i kare mi loko, kinde ma ku yamu maber yei ubedo ka ringo i pii ku ngwec ma tek man ma nyayu can, ma kani adok iwi yei i lwong pa jurugwok mi diewor, ndhundhu ma aloko wanga ikum tabora, yei ugam unyai ikuma pi lworo. Lembe mandha ukadhu lworo; Jadit Ahab ucungu iwi meli pare.

Nen ni epe ku lanyuth moko mi two ma rwinyo ikume, nyo mi cango kud i iye. Enen calu jadhum ma jungolo kud i yen, kinde ma mac unyotho cinge zo man ecaku wango gi ngo, nyo etingo piny acel kud i tego migi ma con. Kite pare ma malu man ma lac, nen calu juyike ku bronze ma tek, man juyike i ayi ma copo loko ngo, calu Cellini's cast Perseus. Ka ewok kud i kind yuk wic pare macol, man emediri piny i thenge acel mi wange man ngute ma wange uwang, nitundo edaru rwinyo i kendi pare, ineno lanyuth moko ma nyap, macol. Ebedo calu jamb tedu ma juyike i kor yen madit, ka ler ma malu ucwale piny, man manangu eturo jang yen acel de ngo, epodho man ebolo yen ne kud i wi yen ni piny, ma podi epodho i ngom, eweko yen ne podi ubedo macol, ento ekwo. Ka nyo lanyuth ne unyolere kude, nyo ka nyo ebedo ret ma juweko ku ret moko ma tek, ngati moko ope ma copo wacu. Ku yioyic moko ma ling, ikind wotho ne zoo, lembe manok nyo epe ma juweco pire, asaga pa juwadi. Ento nindo moko jadit pa Tashtego, Gay-Head Indian moko ma dit ikind jutic, uwacu ku yioyic nia ungo nitundu edaru oro pyerangwen ma Ahab udoko ku nying ma kumeno, man ecaku wok i kume, ungo i ngei pa lwiny moko ci, ento i lwiny mi nam. Ento, lembe ma rac eni nen ni epe, nimakere ku gin ma Manxman macol uwacu, jadit moko ma jamutoro, ma, ma podi ewok kud i Nantucket, nangu eneno ngo Ahab ma jamutoro. Ento, kura macon mi nam, yioyic ma con, uketho Manxman ma con eni ku tego mi nyang ikum piny. Kara ngati moko mbe ma rangi pare tar ma ugam ukwero lembe pare ikare ma ewacu niya ka Captain Ahab jubikethe piny ku kwiocwiny—ma copo bedu tek, ento ewacu ku mwolo—e, ngati moko ci ma timo tic mir ajiki pi ju m'utho, binwangu lanyuth mi nyol i kume nicaku i wiye nitundo i tyende.

Kite ma rac pa Ahab uketho abedo ku tego ma lee, man rangi ma rac ma ucungo iwie, ma pi kare manok ma kwong, aneno ngo nia lembe ma rac eni ubedu pi tyende ma tar ma en ucungu iwie. I kare mukadho ebino ira nia tyend ivory eni ubedu i nam ma juyike ku cogo ma juyubu maber pa lak sperm whale. "Eyo, juwodhe woko kud i Japan," ewacu Gay-Head Indian acel; "ento calu yei pare ma unyai, eoro yei mange ma edok ungo i pacu. Etiye ku quiver pa 'em."

Abedo kud anyonga lee dit pi kite ma en ucungu kudu. I thenge acel acel pa Pequod's quarter deck, man kago ku mizzen shrouds, ubedu ku vur mi auger, ma jubolo romo nusu inch nyo marom, i plank. Tyende ma cogo ucungo i vur meno; cing acel ucungo malu, man ucungo ku bongo; Jatel wi meli Ahab ucungu moth, eneno woko kud i wang yei ma ucungo iwi yei. Ubedo ku tego cwiny ma rwinyo, cwiny ma tek, ma rwinyo ngo, i yik ma ucungo man ma lworo mbe, ma ucungo anyim pa neno meno. Eweco ngo lembe moko; man judongo pare de giwacu ngo ire lembe moko; kadi bende ku timo migi ma nok nok man wec migi, ginyutho kamaleng bedo moth, ka epe lit, bedo i the wang jatel wi dhanu. Man kende ngo, ento Ahab ma cwinye lith ucungu inyim gi ku cinge i wange; i rwom pa rwoth ma rwom pare lapiny ma rwom pare lapiny.

I ngeye, nicaku i rombo pare mir acel i polo, edok i ot pare. Ento ingey uthieno eca, ebedo ka nenre kubang ceng ni jurutic; nyo ucungu i vur pare mi pivot, nyo ebedo iwi kom mi ivory ma etiye kudu; nyo wotho matek iwi yei. Kaka polo udoko piny macol; andha, ucaku doko ngati ma kite ber, edoko ngati ma rwinyo; calu, kinde ma yei uwok kud i pacu, piny moko epe ento ngico ma tho pa nam re ma uketho ebedo kende. Man, nyanok nyanok, edoko nya ebedo iwi yamu kare ku kare; ento, podi, pi lembe ceke ma ewacu, nyo etimo, iwi yei ma ceng ucwir, enen ni epe ku yeny keca calu meli mange. Ento Pequod ubedu kawothu kende kawoni; wotho ngo i yei kare ku kare; pol pa yub mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whaling ma mitu jubedu ka neno wigi, ju wadi gi ubedu ku copo mi timo, uketho ubedu piny manok nyo piny moko epe, kud i bange gire, mi tiyo nyo nyayu anyonga i Ahab, kawoni; man kumeno ryemo cen, pi kare acel kende, pol ma ucungo iwi dul acel ubedo iwi wiye, calu ma pol ceke yero wi got ma malu uloyu.

Ento, pi kare malac, lyethu, ma nyayu anyonga pa piny maber, mi ceng yom ma wabino i iye, nen calu ube nyayu anyonga i iye. Pi, calu kinde ma anyira ma rangi migi makwar, ma gibe myel, April ku May, gi dok i pacu i lum ma ngic man ma jumaru ngo; kadi yen macol, ma nyap, macol macol macol bi cwalu lum moko macol, pi jolo welo ma cwinygi yom; ci Ahab utimo, i ajikine, nyanok ni dwoko wang lembe ma nyayu anyonga pa nyaku. Pol kare eketho wange ma nen ungo, ma, i ngati moko ci, nangu ecopo wok woko i vunju.
