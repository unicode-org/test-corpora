CHAPTER 89. Fast-Fish man Loose-Fish.

Lembe ma juweco ikum waif ku waif-poles i dul mir ajiki ento acel kende, mitu lembe moko ikum cik man cik mi mako rec, ma waif copo bedu ni lanyuth man badge madit.

Ku lapor,—ingeye ngwec ma tek man ma rac man maku rec ma dit, avu copo wok kud i yei pi yamo ma tek; man ka ringo bor ku yamo, jubi mako kendu ku jadwar mir aryo, ma, i yo ma ling, ma tingo ku cinge, manangu epe ku peko mi kwo nyo rwom. Omiyo, ywak mager kendo mager ne dhi bedo e kind jolupo, ka ne onge chik moro ma ondiki kata ma ok ondiki, ma ng’ato ka ng’ato nyalo ng’ado, ma ok nyal ng’ado bura ma tiyo e weche duto.

Copere cik acel kende mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whaling ma juyiyu ku cik, ubedu pa Holland. Juyike ku cik pa States-General i AD 1695. Ento kadi ngom mange ope ku cik moko ma jugoro ikum mako rec, ento ju ma mako rec mi Amerika gibedo ju ma kiewo cik man ju ma kiewo cik i lembe eni. Eyo; cik eni copo bedu ma jukyewo iwi sente pa Queen Anne, nyo iwi cing harpoon, man jukethe iwi ngute, pi meno gi nok.

I. Fast-Fish ubedo pa ju ma timo tic pio pio.

II. Loose-Fish ubedo rec maber ni ngati moko ci ma copo make pio pio.

Ento gin ma nyayu peko i kum cik maeni utiye bedo ma nok pare, ma mitu lembe madwong ma jukoro pi ni gonyo.

Mir acel: Fast-Fish utiye ango? Rec ma kwo nyo mutho timo ngwec pio pio, ka juporo ku yei ma dhanu bedo i iye, ku yore moku ci ma dhanu ma bedo i iye nyo ju ma bedo i iye copo doro,—mast, oar, waya ma romo inci abungwen, waya mi telegraph, nyo thwol pa cobweb, zo gi rom. Kumeno bende rec bedo pio pio kani etingo waif, nyo lanyuth moku ci ma nyutho nia etiye ku rec; tek kende dul ma weko timo ne nyutho kero migi i saa moko ci mi tingo piny ne, man paru migi mi timo kumeno.

Eni ubedo lembe ma jukoro i lembe mi ngec; ento wec pa ju whalemen gire kare moko gibedo ku wec matek man goyo matek—Coke-upon-Littleton pa cing. Andha, ikind ju ma timo lembe ma atira man ma juworo, juyike kare ku kare pi lembe moko ma ukoc, kama ecopo bedu rac pa kura pa ngati acel ni yero nia etiye ku rec ma dhanu mukende uringu nyo unego. Ento jumoko gibedo ngo ku cwiny ma tek.

Oru ma romo pyer abic mukadho, lembe moko ma nyayu lworo ikum rec ma julwongo ni whale-trover ma jupoko i kot i England, kama ju ma gikelo lembe ne giwacu niya ingey lubo tok rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale i nam ma yo malu; man kinde ma andha gin (ju ma gi ketho lembe) gi nwango adwogi maber i mako rec; i ajikine, pi kwo migi ma rac, umitu giwek yei migi kende ngo, ento yei migi gire de. I ajiki, ju ma jucwiny gi (jutic mi yei mange) giwok ku rec ma julwongo ni whale, gi goyo, gi nego, gi mako, man i ajiki gi gamu i wang ju ma gi doto. Man kinde ma ju ma judoto gi juyero igi, jadit migi ugoyo cinge i lak ju ma judoto gi, man eketho cwinygi nia ni kadhu kud i lembe ma etimo, dong ecopo gwoko thwol migi, harpoons, man yei, ma ubedu ikum rec ma dit i kare ma jumake. Pieno ju ma gicaku lembe gicaku ketho lembe i kot pi dwoko wel pa rec ma julwongo ni whale, line, harpoons, man yei migi.

Ento ka ponji pa Fast-Fish utiye piny ma tiyo kudu, ponji pa Loose-Fish podi utiye piny ma tiyo kudu. Eni tiyo i ng'om zoo man i ng'om zoo.

America ubedo ango i 1492 ento Loose-Fish, ma i iye Columbus uketho cik pa Spain ni weko rwoth pare man dhaku pare? Poland ubedo ango ni Czar? Greece ango ni Turk? India ni England ango? Mexico bibedo ango ni ngom mi Amerika? Rec ma oling zo.

Ango ma twero pa dhanu man bedo agonya pa ng'om ento Loose-Fish? Ango ma dhanu zo paru man paru migi ento Loose-Fish? Ango ma ubedo cik mi yioyic igi ento Loose-Fish? Ango ma ju ma timo lembe mi nyayu lworo i dhanu gi paru pa ju ma paru ento Loose-Fish? Globe madit en ango ento Loose-Fish? Man in ango, jakwan, ento Loose-Fish man Fast-Fish, de?

Ni lubre ku yub migi ma nok eni, Guernsey-man, ithe loc pa jagor, umitu ewacu ni Captain ango ma emaru, ento calu ma eai kud i Stubb; man pi Stubb, ecikere ni weco lembe moko ci ma rwinyo ma bino i wiye i saa mi wec.

I saa maeca, ngati ma juyero igi uwok kud i ot pare. Ebedo ngati ma nok man macol, ento nen calu jadit mi nam, ku yuk wic madongo man yuk wic, kadi kudu; man eketho koti makwar ma rangi pa pamba ma juketho i cinge. Ni jadwong eni, Stubb dong ju nyuthe ku woro ni Guernsey-man, ma ndhundhu ecaku ketho lembe mi gonyo lembe ikind gi.

“Abiwacu ire ango mir acel?” ewacu.

"Pirango," Stubb uwacu, ma eneno velvet vest man saa man seals, "icopo caku ku wacu ire niya enen ni nyathin, kadi nangu atimo ngo calu japoklembe."

"Ewacu, Monsieur," Guernsey-man uwacu, i dhok mi French, ma eloko wange ni jatel wi meli pare, "nya ceng kawoni kende yei pare uweco ku meli, ma jatel wi meli man jatel wi meli, ku jutic abusiel, githo ku lyethu ma jucaku kud i rec ma julwongo ni blasted whale ma gikelo kugi."

I kum lembe eni, jatel wi yei ucaku, man emito ngeyo lembe madwong.

“Kawoni ango?” ewacu jadhum pa Guernsey ni Stubb.

"Pirango, calu ma etingo lembe yot, wac ire nia kawoni dong anene maber, angeyu niya ecopo ngo ni telo wi yei ma dit ma julwongo ni whale-ship ma sagu St. Jago monkey. I andha, wac ire nia etiye baboon."

"Ekwongiri man ewacu, Monsieur, nia rec mange ma julwongo ni whale, ma muthwo, utiye rac uloyu ma sagu ma juwango; i ajikine, Monsieur, ekwayu wa, calu ma wabekwo ku kwo mwa ma pire tek, ni weko rec eni."

Ndhu ndhu jatel wi yei uringo anyim, man ku dwal ma tek ewacu ni jurutic pare ni juku tingo jam lwiny, man ndhundhu eca giweko waya man nyor ma ucungo i yei.

“Kawoni ango?” ewacu jadhum pa Guernsey, kinde ma jatel wi mony udwogo i bang gi.

"Pirango, wek anen; ee, icopo wacu ire kawoni nia—nya—i andha, wacu ire nia atimo lembe marac i kume, man (ku en gire) nyo ngati mange."

"Ewacu, Monsieur, nia etiye kud anyonga madit ni bedo ku kony moku ci iwa."

Kinde ma ewinjo lembe eni, jatel wi yei ukwoni nia gi ubedo ju ma foyofoc (niwacu en gire man wadi) man edaru ku lwongo Stubb piny i ot pare ni madhu kongo Bordeaux.

"Emito iter kopo mi kongo kude," jalok dhok uwacu.

"Foyo ire kud adundeni, ento wac ire nia etiye ikum cik para ni madhu kongo ku jadhum ma atimo kudu. Andha, wac ire nia umitu acidhi."

"Ewacu, Monsieur, nia cik pare biyio ngo nia ebed emadhu kongo; ento ka Monsieur umito ebed kwo ceng' mange pi madhu kongo, ci Monsieur ucikere ni weko yei angwen zo, man ewotho yei cen kud i bang rec madongo eni, kum etiye ling ma gicopo ringo ngo."

I saa maeca Stubb ubedo iwi dhu nam, man emondo i yei pare, elwongo Guernsey-man ni lembe maeni,- nia ka etiye ku tow-line mabor i yei pare, ebitimo lembe ceke ma ecopu pi konyogi, ni kwanyo rec ma yot pa ju aryo kud i ngete yei. I kare ma yei pa ja France, eca, ubedu ka ywayo yei yo acel, Stubb utingo yei pare yo mange, man eweko yei ma bor mandha.

Kawoni yamu moko uwok; Stubb utimo nen calu ebecidho cen kud i rec ma dit; ma etingo yei pare, ja France umedire bor pare, ma Pequod ucungo ikind en ku Stubb's whale. I ngeye Stubb utingo cinge pio pio ikum kum ma ucungo iwi pii, man elwongo Pequod ni miyo ngec ikum paru pare, ndhundhu ecaku kadho nying pa rieko pare ma atira ngo. Etingo boat-spade pare ma bith, ecaku timo tic i kum, nyanok in ngeye pa side fin. Icopo paru niya ebedu ka goyo ot ma piny i nam; man ikare ma spade pare ugoyo ikum cinge ma nyap, ubedu calu loko tiles macon pa Roman man potters ma juyiko i loam ma pek pa England. Jurutic mi yei pare zo ubedu kud anyonga madit, gibekonyo jadit migi ku miti madit, man nen calu ju ma gibe sayu mola macol.

Man kare ceke winy mapol ubedu ka mol, man ucungo, man ukok, man ukok, man ube lwiny ikumgi. Stubb ucaku nen ni cwinya cwer, asaga ma nosegay marac umedire, ikare ma wok kud i adunde pa two eni, pii moko ma nyai ma mol kud i iye, ma ucwale kud i kind nguce marac ma edaru mako ngo, calu pii acel bimol i iye man edaru lubo ku mange, manangu edaru rwinyo ngo pi kare moko.

"Atiye kude, atiye kude," Stubb owacu, ku mutoro, ma eturo piny moko i theng ngom, "sandu! sanduku!"

Ebolo spade pare piny, ebolo cinge ario zoo i iye, man ewodho piny moko ma nen calu sabun mi Windsor ma ucek, nyo cheese macon ma rangire; ma unctuous man ma leng kude. Icopo turo yot yot ku cingi madit; etiye ku rangi ikind rangi ma yellow ku ash. Man eni, jurimo maber, utiye ambergris, ma wel pare romo gold guinea acel ni jara yath moko ci. Ju nwango cing abicel; ento mukende mapol rwinyo i nam, man mukende mapol, nyo, copo bedu ma jugwoko tek ebedo ngo pi cik ma tek pa Ahab ma cwinye tek ni Stubb nia ewek, man emond i yei, ka epe yei ne copo yero igi nia ebed maber.





CHAPTER 92. Ambergris.

Kawoni ambergris eni ubedo piny ma pire tek, man piretek calu piny mi kuloka, ma i 1791 Captain Coffin ma junyolu i Nantucket jupenje i bar mi ot pa judongo pa England ikum lembe eni. Pi kare meno, man andha nitundu kawoni, acaki pa ambergris ubedo, calu amber kende, peko ni ju ma gisomo. Kadi bende wec ambergris ubedo kende kende lembe mi French pi grey amber, ento piny aryo eni gi tung tung. Pi amber, kadi nya kare moko nwangire i dhu nam, juyubu de i ngom moko mabor, ento ambergris nwangre ngo kende iwi nam. Mi mede, amber ubedo piny matek, maleng, ma nyap, ma epe ku nguce, ma jutiyo kudu pi tiyo ku dhudhu, pi goyo man jam deyo; ento ambergris utiye mayom, ma nyap, man ma ngucere man ma ngic, ma jutiyo kudu lee i yubu pafum, i pastiles, candles mabec, hair-powders, man pomatum. Ju Turks gitiyo kudu pi tedo, man de gitero i Mecca, pi lembe marom ku ma frankincense jutero i St. Peter's i Rome. Juwil moko mi kongo gibolo kodhi manok i claret, mi medo leng.

Nga ma copo paru, dong, nia mon ku coo ma ber kumeno umaku gi camu piny ma nwangre i ic pa rec ma lith! Ento etiye kumeno. Ku dhanu moko, ambergris juparu niya etiye the lembe, man ju mukende etiye adwogi, pa dyspepsia i rec ma julwongo ni whale. Kite mi cango two ma kumeno ubedu tek ni wacu, kawang ikum miyo yath pa Brandreth adek nyo angwen, man ingeye wok kud i yo marac, calu jurutic timo i wango kidi.

Wiya wil ni wacu nia ju nwango i ambergris eni, piny moko ma tek, ma ling, ma ku cogo, ma Stubb uparu niya copo bedu buttons pa jolweny; ento i ngeye edoko nya gibedo ngo piny moko cii kende kende cogo pa squid ma juyiko i yo ma kumeno.

Kawoni ma rwinyo pa ambergris ma ngweje tek eni jubinwange i adunde pa rwinyo ma kumeno; eni piny moko epe? Kepar pi lembe ma St. Paul uwacu i Jukorinto, ikum nyoth man bedo ma rwinyo ngo; nia jucoyowa i lewic, ento jucerwa i dwong'. Man bende poi ikum wec pa Paracelsus ikum ango ma weko musk bedo maber loyo. Man kud iwil lembe ma nyayu lworo nia ikind piny ceke ma rac, pii mi Cologne, i acaki pa yiku pare, utiye marac uloyu.

Amito daru wi lembe ku lembe ma malu, ento acopo ngo, pi lworo para mi dwoko lembe ma juyero ikum ju ma gikwo i rec, man ma, i paru pa dhanu moko ma gibedo ku paru ma tung tung, jucopo kwanu ni lembe ma jukoro ikum rec aryo pa ja France. Kaka mange i buku eni, lembe ma juwacu ma rac eca judwokogi nya epe, nia tic mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whale utiye tic ma nyayu lworo man ma rwinyo ngo. Ento lembe mange nuti ma umitu jukwer. Giwacu nia rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whales nja nguce marac. Dong lewic ma rac eni ucaku nenedi?

Aparu, nya ecopu bedu ni acaki pa meli ma mako rec ma julwongo ni Greenland i London, oro ma kadhu miya aryo mukadho. Pien ju ma gwoko rec ma julwongo ni whalemen ne gi temo ngo, man kawoni de, gi temo moo migi i nam calu ma meli mi Southern gibed gitimo kare ku kare; ento poko moo ma nyen i dul ma nok nok, ibolo i vur mi pii madongo, man itere i pacu i yo ma kumeno; bedo ma nok pa kare i nam ma ngic, man yamu ma tek man ma tek ma gi bedo i iye, ma kwero yo mange. Adwogi ne utiye, nia kinde ma juturo i hold, man juwodho piny acel ikin liel pa rec ma julwongo ni whale, i Greenland dock, juwok ku ngec ma rom ku ma juwok kud i goyo liel mi adhura macon, pi thek pa Lying-in Hospital.

Aparu bende, nia lembe marac maeni ikum ju ma mako rec ma julwongo ni whalers copo bedu ni lembe ma jutimo i dhu nam mi Greenland, i kare mukadho, pa caru moko mi Dutch ma julwongo ni Schmerenburgh nyo Smeerenberg, ma nyinge ma tokcen ubedo ma jagor Fogo Von Slack utiyo kudu, i tic pare madit ikum Smells, buku mi somo ikum lembe meno. Calu ma nyinge nyutho (smeer, fat; berg, to put up), caru eni juyike pi bedo ku kabedu pa blubber pa Dutch whale fleet ni temo, manangu jutere ngo i pacu i Holland pi lembe eno. Ubedo dul pa furnace, fat-kettles, man oil sheds; man kinde ma tic ubedo katimere, egam enyayu ngo nguce maber. Ento lembe eni zo tung tung ku South Sea Sperm Whaler; ma i wotho mi oro angwen, ingey pongu pii pare ku moo, epe, nyo, tero nindu pyer abic i tic mi lwoko; man i kite ma juketho kudu i pii, moo ne nen ungo ku nguce. Lembe mandha utiye, nia kwo nyo tho, tek juyike maber, rec ma julwongo ni whales calu lei moko ope ku nguce marac; man ju ma gikwo i rec ma julwongo ni whalemen gicopo ngo ni ngeyo, calu dhanu mi rundi ma ukadhu ma jutimo igi tic mi nwangu Jew i dul pa dhanu, ku um. Bende, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale copo bedu ngo ku nguce, kadi nang, calu piny ma rwinyo, etiye ku yot kum maber; timo tic mi yot kum madwong; kare ku kare woko; kadi, etiye lemandha, pol kare ungo i polo. Awacu, nia wotho pa Sperm Whale's flukes iwi pii nyayu nguce, calu ka dhaku ma nguce pa musk ucwalu kendi pare i ot ma mor. Dong aporo Sperm Whale ku ango pi nguce, ka waneno dit pare? Nyo ecibedo ngo ni lyec ma nyinge uyik, ku lak ma juyubu ku jam piny mabec, man ma leng ku myrrh, ma juwodho kud i adhura mi India pi ni mio deyo ni Alexander the Great?





CHAPTER 93. The Castaway.

Ubedo ceng manok kende ingey rombu ku ja France, ma lembe moko ma pire tek utimere ikum ngati ma pire tek ungo ikind jutic pa Pequod; lembe ma rac mandha; man ma jik i miyo tic ma nya wange tek man ma juyiko con ku lembila ma kwo man ma lubo tok lembe moko ci ma copo bedu pare gire.

Kawoni, i yei ma dit, epe dhanu ceke ma cidho i yei. Jumoko manok kende re ma jugwoko ma julwongo gi ni ship-keepers, ma province migi utiye ni tic ku yei podi yei ube lubo tok rec ma dit. Calu lembe ma rwinyo, ju ma gwoko yei eni gitiye dhanu ma tek calu coo ma gitiye i yei. Ento ka ngati moku ma nyap, ma jamutoro, nyo ma lworo utiye i yei, ngati meno jubikethe ni jatel wi yei. Ubedo kumeno i Pequod ku nyathin macol ma nyinge Pippin ma julwonge, Pip ma julwongo kudu. Pip ma can! wuwinjo pire con; umitu ipoi ikum tambourine pare i diewor ma tek, ma cwinye lith.

I yore ma woko, Pip ku Dough-Boy gi timo lembe marom, calu pony macol ku matar, ma dongo marom, kadi nang rangi migi ukoc, ma giwotho i rwom acel. Ento kadi bende Dough-Boy ubedu ngati ma timo lembe marac man ma timo lembe marac, Pip, kadi nang ebedo ku cwiny ma mwol, ebedo ku ryeko mandha, ku ler maber, ma ber, man ma kud anyonga ma nen pa thek pare; thek, ma gibedo kud anyonga i nindu mi yom man kwir ku mutoro man maber uloyu thek moko ci. Pi ju ma rangi migi col, calendar mi oro ucikere ni nyuthu piny moko kende ento nindo miya adek ku pyer abusiel wi abic mi dwi mir angwen man nindo mi oro manyen. Man kud ivunju, podi akyewo nia rangi macol ma nok eni ubedo maleng, kum kadi rangi macol de utiye ku ryeko pare; nen yon ebony ma ler, ma juketho i cabinet pa rwoth. Ento Pip umaru kwo, man kwo maber; kara tic ma nyayu lworo ma en ebedo i iye, uketho ler pare doko rac; kadi, calu ma podi jubi neno ngo, gin ma jubedu ka jwigo i iye pi kare manok, i ajikine jubedu ka lyel ku mac ma nyawang, ma unyutho ire rwom ma malu wang apar ma i ngom pare mi Tolland County i Connecticut, edaru ketho kwo pa juwer mapol iwi greenfrolic; man i rwom maber, ku ha-ha pare! uloko ng'om ma ling ling ni tambourine acel ma lakala. Pieno, kadi nang i yamu maleng mi dieceng, ma ucungo iwi ngute ma rangi pa polo, pii maleng pa diamond bi ler maber; ento, kinde ma jaradiri ma rieko unyutho iri diamond i ler pare ma ber, eketho i ngom macol, man edham ewange, ungo ku ceng, ento ku yamu moko ma nen ungo. I ngeye wok woko ku mac ma lyel, maber akeca; man diamond ma lyeth marac, ma con ubedo lanyuth pa polo ma leng, nen calu jam piny moko ma jukwalo kud ibang Rwoth mi Gehena. Ento wek wadok i lembe.

Etimere, nia i lembe mi ambergris, Stubb's after-oarsman ugam unwangu cinge, ma pi kare moko edoko goro; man, pi kare manok, Pip juketho i kabedu pare.

I kare ma Stubb udok piny kude, Pip unyutho lworo madit; ento kud anyonga, pi kare meno, eweko bedo ceng ku rec ma dit; man kumeno ewok ungo ku lworo; kadi bende Stubb ubedu ka nene, etimo kero, i ngeye, ni cwale ni ebed ku tego cwiny pare maber akeca, kum ecopo nwangu nya eber.

Kawoni i kare ma piny mir aryo, yei ugam ucungo iwi rec madit; man calu rec ne ugamu cuma ma jucwalo, emiyo rap pare ma rwinyo, ma utimere, i lembe maeni, ni bedu ithe kom pa Pip ma jacan. Lembe ma nyayu lworo i kare meno uketho epodho, ku cinge, woko kud i yei; man i yo ma kumeno, thenge moko pa dul pa rec ma julwongo ni slack whale line ma bino ikum kore, enyaye iwi nam kude, kara edoko i iye, ikare ma epodho i pii. I saa maeca, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ucaku ringo matek, cinge ucungo pio pio; man presto! Pip ma jacan bino ku yuk ikum yei, ma jutere keca ku cinge, ma ugam utinge wang mapol i kore man i wiye.

Tashtego ucungu i cinge. Ebedo ku mac mi dwar. Edagu Pip pi poltroon. Etingo pala mi yei kud i cinge, ebolo cinge ma lac iwi cinge, man eloko wange ibang Stubb, ewacu ku penji kumae, “Cut?” Ikinde marom, wang Pip macol, ma ucungo nen kamaleng, Do, pi bero pa Mungu! Lembe zo ukadhu nyathotho. Ikind nusu dakika, lembe eni zo utimere.

“Damn him, cut!” owacu Stubb; man kumeno rec ma julwongo ni whale utho man Pip utho.

Kakare ma edoko yot, nyathin macol ma jacan ne ugam unyay ku koko man nyayu can pa jurutic. Ku ling ling weko lembe ma rac eni uthum, Stubb i ngeye i yo ma yot, calu mi kuloka, ento podi nya yo ma nyayu ngyeru, ewacu lembe marac ikum Pip; man timo eno, ungiyo ire paru maber. Lembe ubedo, kud ipodho kud i yei, Pip, ento—ento lembe mukende eca ubedu ma rwinyo ngo, calu ma juk maber uloyu. Kawoni, i rwom ma malu, Bed i yei , ubedo lembe peri mandha i mako rec; ento lembe moko copo timere ka Leap from the boat , podi ber. Bende, calu ma enyang nia ka emiyo paru maber ni Pip, ecopo weko ire kabedo malac ma ecopo timo ngo pi anyim; Stubb uweko paru pare zo iwi yei, man edaru ku cik ma tek, "Bed i yei, Pip, nyo ku Rwoth, abitingi ngo ka ipodho; poi ikum lembe eni. Wacopo ngo ni weko rec ma dit ma calu in; rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale copo wilu pi wang pyer adek ma sagu ma icopo, Pip, i Alabama Bed i wii, man kud ipodho." I lembe maeni, Stubb copo nyutho nya kadi nang dhanu maru wadi, dhanu utiye lei ma timo sente, ma timo pare pol kare nyayu peko i berocwiny pare.

Ento wan ceke watiye i cing Mungu; man Pip ucungo kendu. Ebedo i thenge ma rom ku tuko mir acel; ento i kare eni eweko ngo cinge woko; man kumeno, kinde ma rec ma julwongo ni whale ucaku ringo, Pip udong i nam, calu jamb wotho ma ringo pio pio. Alas! Stubb ubedu mandha ikum lembe pare. Ubedo ceng maber, maber, man macol; nam ma ling man ma ngic, man ma ucungo iwi ngom, iwi ngom, calu adila pa jabodho mi gold ma jugoyo woko. Ka ucungo malo man piny i nam meno, wic pa Pip ma ebon nen calu wic pa cloves. Boat-knife moko ope ma jutingo ikare ma epodho pio pio yo ngeye. Ngec pa Stubb ma rwinyo lokore i kume; man rec madit ne ubedo ku bwome. Ikind dakika adek, mayil acel mi nam ma dhu nam ubedu ikind Pip ku Stubb. Kud i dye nam, Pip ma jacan uloko wiye macol, macol ni ceng, ngati mange ma ling, kadi nya ebedo ma malu man ma ler.

Kawoni, i kare ma piny oling, dhuny i nam ma oling yot ni ju ma ngeyo dhuny calu ringo i spring-carriage i dhu nam. Ento bedo kend ma rac eca epe. Kethu cwinya ikum ngati gire ikind piny ma cwinye tek, Mungu para! nga ma copo wacu? Mark, kite ma ju ma giwotho i yei gi lwoko ko pii i nam—nen kite ma gi maku kudu yei migi man gi bedo i dhu nam kende.

Ento nyo Stubb uweko nyathin macol ma jacan ni kwo pare? Ungo; ebed emito ngo, kadi nyanok. Pien yei ario ubedu i ngeye, man eparu, labongo jiji, nia gibibino i bang Pip pio pio, man gitinge; kadi, andha, paru ma kumeno ikum ju ma giwotho ku yei ma juketho i ariti pi lworo migi gigi, epe kare ku kare ni ju ma gikwo i yei i lembe marom rom; man lembe ma kumeno timere pol kare ngo; pol kare i tic mi mako rec, ngati ma lworo, ma julwonge kumeno, jubedu ka nyuthu ku ngei ma rac marom ku pa judongo pa lumony man judongo pa lumony.

Ento etimere, nya yei eca, manangu gineno ngo Pip, ma ndhundhu gineno rec ma julwongo ni whales ceng kugi yo acel, giloko, man giringo; man yei pa Stubb dong ubedu bor akeca, man en ku jutic pare zo ubedu ka maru rec pare, ma Pip's ringed horizon ucaku nyai ikume marac. Ku rwom ma lac, yei ne gire edaru lare; ento nicaku i sawa eca nyathin macol ne ucidho iwi yei ma jamutoro; kumeno, kadi nok, giwacu niya ebedo. Nam ugam utingo kume ma rwinyo malu, ento etho tipo pare ma rwinyo. Ento etho ngo i pii zo. Ento jutere piny makwo i theng ngom ma thuc, kama kit piny ma nen ungo pa piny ma kwong ma unyai unyai i wange ma nen ungo; man ngati ma jamutoro, Rieko, unyutho piny pare ma egwoko; man ikind kwo ma kud anyonga, ma cwinye pe, ma rwinyo, Pip uneno lei madwong, Mungu-ma nwangire, ma wok kud i pii ma dit. Eneno tyend Mungu iwi tyend jamb tedu, man eweco; man pi the lembe ma juwothe ku meli gilwonge ni jamutoro. Dong wic pa dhanu utiye ngec mi polo; man wotho kud i paru ceke, dhanu bino i ajikine i paru mi polo, ma, ni paru, epe ku the lembe man ma nyayu lworo; man lonyo nyo can, ewinjo nia epe ku lworo, epe ku paru pare calu Mungu pare.

Pi ju mukende, kud iket lembe iwi Stubb matek akeca. Lembe ne nenre i tic mi mako rec; man i ngeye pa lapor, jubi neno ango ma calu weko lembe utimere ira giri.
