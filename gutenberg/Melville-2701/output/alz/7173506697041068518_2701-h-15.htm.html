CHAPTER 74. Wic pa Sperm Whale—Neno ma tung tung.

Kany, kawoni, utiye rec madongo ario, ma gi ketho wigi karacelu; wek wabed kugi, man wabed kugi karacelu.

Ikind dul madit pa folio leviathans, Sperm Whale man Right Whale gi re ma pire tek uloyu. Gin kende re ma gitiye rec ma julwongo ni whales ma dhanu maru. Ni Nantucketer, gi nyuthu thenge aryo ma kadho pa kit rec ma julwongo ni whale. Calu tung tung ma woko ikind gi nenre i wigi; man calu wic pa ngati acel acel utiye kare eni ma ucungu kud i thenge pa Pequod; man calu ma wacopo wotho agonya kud i acel ni mukende, ku wotho kende iwi deck:—kani, amito ngeyo, ibinwangu kare maber mi somo cetology ma tiyo maber uloyu keni?

I acaki, ibedo kud anyonga ikum tung tung ma nuti ikind wi lembe eni. Gin ario zoo gi dit ma romo i paru migi zo; ento tiye ku rwom moko ma rom pa Sperm Whale ma Right Whale epe kudu. Tye kit kura mange i wic pa Sperm Whale. Ka ineno, ibe miyo rwom ma malu ire, i yore mi woro. I lembe ma kawoni de, woro eni umedire ku rangi pa pepper man salt pa wiye i wi got, ma mio lanyuth pa oro ma dit man ngec ma lee. I lembe ma nok, etiye gin ma ju ma mako rec lwongo ni "grey-headed whale."

Wek wanenu piny ma nok ma tung tung ikind wic eni—niwacu, dul ario ma pigi tek, wang man it. Bor ngeye inget wiye, man piny, kago ku lak wic acel acel, ka iyenyo maber, ibineno wang ma lashless, ma icopo paru nya ubedo wang nyathin ma nyathin; dong ikum rwom zo utiye ikum dit pa wic.

Kawoni, kud i kabedo ma tung tung pa wang whale, nen kele niya ecopu neno ngo piny moku ma ni i wange, kadi nang eneno ngo piny ma ni yo ngeye. I wec acel, kabedo pa wang rec ma dit urombo ku kabedo pa it pa dhanu; man icopo paru, piri keni, kite ma ecopu bedu kudu iri, tek ibedo ka ngiyo piny ku it peri. Ibinwangu niya icopo tiyo ku digri pyer adek kende mi neno piny i wang neno piny ma atira; man dhanu mukende pyer adek ingeye. Ka jadubu peri ma rac uloyu ubewotho i bangi, ku pala ma jutingo malo i ceng, nangu icopo nene ngo, calu ka ebedu ka kwalo iwi ngeye. I wec acel, icopo bedu ku ngeye ario, ka wabeweco; ento, i sawa marom, de, wang ario (wang wang): pi ango ma weko wang dhanu—ango, andha, ento wange?

Bende, podi i lei mange mapol ma kawoni acopo paru, wang gi juyike maber akeca mi rombu tego migi mi neno piny, kara giwodh cal acel man ario ungo i wic; kabedo ma ukoc pa wang whale, ma jupoko ku fut cubic mapol pa wiye ma tek, ma ucungu ikind gi calu got madit ma poko nam ario i lum; eni, andha, umitu upok lembe ma dul acel acel ma timo tic pare gire mio. Whale, dong, umitu onen cal acel ma tung tung yo maeni, man cal mange ma tung tung yo eca; man piny ceke ikind gi ucikere ni bedu mudho ma thuc man piny moko ope ire. Dhanu copo, i andha, juyero ni gineno ng'om kud i sentry-box ku sashes ario ma gicokiri pi dirisa pare. Ento ku rec ma julwongo ni whale, dul ario eni ju ketho gi tung tung, ma weko dirisa ario ma tung tung, ento nyayu peko i neno. Kite ma wang rec ma julwongo ni whale nen kudu ubedo piny ma umitu juketh i wiya i tic mi mako rec; man ni ju ma kwano gi poi i lembe moko ma lubo.

Penji ma nyayu lworo man ma nya wange tek copo caku ikum lembe ma nen calu mulo Leviathan. Ento umitu abed ma cwinya yom ku kony moko. Tek wang dhanu oyabere i lero, timo mi neno ubedo piny ma dhanu timo ngo; niwacu, ecopo konyo ngo ni neno piny moko ci ma ni i wange. Ento, lembe ma ngati moko ci ukadhu kud i iye biponje, nia kadi nang ecopu nwangu piny ma tung tung i wang acel, ecopu bedu tek ire, ku diro, man ku kero, ni ngiyo piny ario moko ci—kadi bed ni dit nyo ma nok—i saa acel; kud ipar kani gi vutu karacelu man gimulo ngati acel acel. Ento tek kawoni ibe poko piny aryo eni, man iguro acel acel ku dul macol ma thuc; ka dong, pi nineno acel ikin gi, i yo ma copo kelo paru peri ikume, mukende bibedo woko kud i paru peri mi kare eni. Dong, etiye nenedi ku rec madit? Andha, wange ario zoo, igi gigi, umitu gitim tic i saa acel; ento kodi obwongo pare utiye ku ngec madwong, ma rwate, man ma nyang ikum dhanu, ma ecopo i saa marom ni ngiyo lembe ario matung tung, acel i thenge acel pare, man mukende i thenge mange? Ka ecopu, eca ubedo piny maber i iye, calu ka ngati copo kadhu kud i lanyuth pa peko ario matung tung i Euclid. Nyo, ka jutimo sayusac maber, epe ku lembe moku ma rwinyo ngo i poro eni.

Ecopu bedu kende kende paru ma kony ope, ento nja nen ira, nya ngwec ma nen ungo pa rec moko ma julwongo ni whales kinde ma yei adek nyo angwen ucungo iwi gi; lworo man lworo pa queer frights, ma nenre ni whales ma kumeno; Aparo ni lembe eni zo wok kud i lembe ma nyayu anyonga pa miti, ma i iye tego migi mi neno ma opokiri man ma tung tung ucikere ni bedu i iye.

Ento it pa rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale upong ku mitu mi ngeyo lembe calu wang. Ka ibedo ngati ma ingeyo ngo ikum thek migi, icopo sayu iwi wi ario eni pi sawa madwong, man icopo nwangu ngo dul kom meno. It epe ku ith ma woko; man i vur eca kende icopo ketho quill ungo, eber mandha. Ebedo nyanok ingey wang. Ku woro ikum it gi, tung tung madit eni ucikere ni nen ikind sperm whale ku right. Ith pa ma kwong eca utiye ku dhugola ma woko, ma ithe mir aryo umu ku membrane, kara ebed ma nen ungo kud woko.

Ebedo ngo lembe mi ngeyo, nia piny madit calu rec madit ma julwongo ni whale copo neno ng'om ku wang ma nok, man winjo polo ku it ma nok uloyu pa hare? Ento ka wange ubedo lac calu lens pa Herschel's telescope madit; man ithe ubedo malac calu dhugola mi kanisa madit; nyo eno biketho ebedo ku neno ma bor, nyo winjo maber? Ungo kadi nyanok.—Pirango ma itemo "medo" paru peri? Lok kudu.

Wek kawoni ku levers man steam-engines ma watiye kudu, wok iwi wic pa sperm whale, kara ebed piny malu; kadong, ka iidho ku yende ni wi got, nen piny i dhoge; man tek ebedo ngo nia kum dong jupoko kud i iye, ku tara wacopo lor piny i Kentucky Mammoth Cave madit mi iye. Ento wek wabed ku lak maeni, man wanenu kama watiye i iye. Ebedo dhok maleng man maleng mandha! nicaku i the ot nitundo i wi ot, ma jugoro, nyo jugoro ku karatasi ku rangi matar ma ler, ma ler calu satin mi nyom.

Ento wok woko kawoni, man inen lak ma piny eni, ma nen calu cing mabor ma nyap pa sanduku madit mi kongo, ku cinge i thenge acel, ento ungo thenge acel. Ka itingo malu, kara ebed malu, man inyuth lak pare, nen calu portcullis maber akeca; man kumeno, alas! enyutho ni dhanu mapol nia etie wight ma ber ungo i tic mi mako rec, ma ng’om maeni upodho i wie ku tego ma tek. Ku pala ma tek, Queequeg ucwalu lak; i ngeye jutwoyo lak cinge piny ku ringbolts, man piny moko ma jucwalo kud malo, giwodho lak gi woko, calu dhyeng mi Michigan tingo lum macol kud i ngom mi lum. Pol kare lak pyer angwen wi aryo zo; i rec ma dit, ma rwinyo, ento ma rwinyo ngo; kadi jupongo ngo ingey kit mwa ma dhanu timo. I ngeye jupoko lak ne ni slabs, man jupoke woko calu joists pi gier ot.





CHAPTER 75. Wic pa rec ma yor acam—nen ma tung tung.

Ka wakadhu iwi yei, wek dong wanen wi Right Whale maber.

Calu ma nen kudu wic pa Sperm Whale juporo ku gari mi lwiny pa ju Roma (mandha ne i kore, kama etiye ma ling ling); dong, ka ineno maber, wi Right Whale nen calu wara ma dit ma tiye ku tyend galliot. Oru miya aryo mukadho jadwong moko mi Dutch ma wotho i nam uporo kite pare ku pa jabodho pa wara. Man i thum pa wara eni, dhaku ma jadit mi lembe mi nursery, ku nyithindhe ma ubepong, copo bedu maber, en ku nyithindhe zo.

Ento ka ibecidho kago ku wic madit eni ecaku bedo ku paru matung tung, nimakere ku paru peri. Ka icungu iwi kore man inenu dhugola aryo ma nen calu F, icopo kwanyo wiye zo pi bass-viol madit, man spiracles eni, dhugola mi sounding-board pare. I ngeye, kendu, kan iketho wang peri iwi piny ma nen ungo, ma nen calu yuk wic ma ucungo iwi lum-piny macol, macol, ma dhanu mi Greenland gilwongo ni "korona," man ju ma mako rec mi Southern gilwongo ni "bonnet" pa Right Whale; ka iketho wang peri iwi lembe eni kende, icopo kwanyo wic pa yen moko madit, ku ot pa winy i iye. I yore moku ci, ka ineno rec ma kwo ma gibedo kany iwi bonet eni, paru ma kumeno bibedo i wii; kawang, andha, miti peri juyike ku wec mi ngec ma julwongo ni "korona" ma juyike i kume; I lembe maeni ibi bedo ku miti madit mi paru kite ma lei ma tek eni ubedo rwoth mi nam ma juketho ire diadem, ma wic pare macol juyike ire i yo maber eni. Ento ka rec ma julwongo ni whale eni ubedo rwoth, etiye ngati ma nen ma rac mandha ma copo bedu rwoth. Nen lak ma piny ma ucungo! ango ma rac man pout utiye keca! a sulk and pout, nimakere ku pima pa jabodho, ma romo futi pyer aryo bor man futi abic thum; a sulk man pout ma bi miyo iri 500 gallons pa moo man mukende mapol.

Lembe mi can madit, kawoni, nia rec ma rac eni ucikere ni bedu hare-lipped. Fissure ne romo futi acel. Nyalo bedo ni min ot i kare moko ma pire tek ubedu kawothu i nam mi Peru, ikare ma yenge unyayu dhu nam. Iwi lak eni, calu iwi dhugola ma nyap, dong wamondo i dhoge. I kum lembe para kani abedo i Mackinaw, nangu acopo kwanu eni ni bedu i ot pa ju India. Rwoth maber! en e yo ma Yona ucidho i iye? Wi ot bor pare romo futi apar wi aryo, man ecidho i dhugola ma lac, calu ka nyo ridge-pole moko nuti keca; man thenge maeni ma nyai, ma nyai, ma yuk wic, nyuthu iwa lembe mabecu, nusu ma ucungu, ma nen calu scimetar, calu 300 i thenge acel, ma lubere ku thenge ma malu pa wic nyo cogo wic, gi ketho Venetian blinds ma juweco pigi kaka mange. Thenge pa cogo eni utiye ku yuk wic, ma Right Whale tiyo kudu pi pii, man i iye egwoko rec ma nok, ka oyabe dhoge ewotho i nam pa brit i kare mi camu. I kind cogo, ma gicungo i rwom migi, otiye ku lanyuth moko ma nyayu anyonga, ma guro, ma ucungu, man ma ucungu, ma jumoko ma gimaru rec ma julwongo ni whalemen gikwanu oro pa lei ne, calu oro pa yen oak ku cinge ma ling ling. Kadok nwango lemandha pa lembe eni utiye bor ku lembe ma juromo nyuthu, ento etiye ku rwom pa analogical probability. I rundi ma con, nen calu dhanu mapol ubedu ku paru madwong ikum blinds eni. Calu ma dhanu ceke ung'eyo, "ngwec pa opego," "ngwec," "yic," "wic," nyo gin moko ci ma imito, gi miyo ni mon bongo migi man piny mukende ma weko gi bedo matek. CHAPTER 77. The Great Heidelburgh Tun.

Kawoni bino Baling pa lembe. Ento mi nyang ikume maber, umitu ingei lembe moko ikum kit ma piny ma jutiyo kudu tiye kudu.

Ni lubre ku wic pa Sperm Whale calu oblong ma tek, icopo, iwi rwom ma ucungo, poko i thenge ario,* ma piny ubedo dul pa cogo, ma ketho wic man lak, man ma malu ubedo piny ma cogo ope i iye; thenge pare ma lac ma anyim ma uketho wic pa rec ma julwongo ni whale.
