Nitundu, nwangu kabedo ma pire tek, bubble macol ma malu upoto; man kawoni, ma jugonyo pi pii pare ma rieko, man, pi rwom pare madit mi pii, ma ucungo ku tego madit, sanduku mi kwo pa dhanu m'utho ucwale kud i nam, upodho piny, man ucungo ingeta. Ma ucungo iwi sanduku eca, pi ceng acel ku dyewor acel, awotho iwi yei ma mol man ma calu wer. Rec ma julwongo ni sharks ma timo ngo rac, giwotho iwi dhugola migi; winyo ma julwongo ni sea-hawks ma kwiny giwotho ku dhoge ma jugwoko. I ceng mir aryo, yei moko ubinu kago, kago, man etingo an i ajikine. Ubedo Rachel ma timo lembe marac, ma i sayu pare mi lubo tok awiya pare ma rwinyo, enwangu nyathin mange ma won ot.
