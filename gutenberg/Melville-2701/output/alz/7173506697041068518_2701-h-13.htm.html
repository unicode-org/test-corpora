THEK 61. Stubb unego rec ma dit.

Ka ni Starbuck nen pa Squid ubedo piny ma nyutho, ni Queequeg ubedo piny matung tung.

"Ka inene 'quid," ewacu jajok, ma ucungo ku harpoon pare i kore pa yei pare, "i ngeye ibineno 'parm whale' pio pio."

Nindu mukende ubedo ma ling man ma ngic, man ku piny moko ma pire tek ma copo ketho gi timo tic kugi, jurutic pa Pequod gicopo kwetu ngo nindu ma nam ma ling eca ukelo. Pi theng ngom mi Indian Ocean ma wabedu kawothu i iye eca epe ma ju ma gilwongo ni whalemen lwongo ni ngom ma kwo; niwacu, eketho nen pa porpoises, dolphins, flying-fish, man lei mukende ma kwo i pii ma mol, ma sagu ma nwang i Rio de la Plata, nyo i dhu nam mi Peru.

Ubedo kare para mi cungo i wang yei; man ku goka ma ucungo iwi bongo pa rwoth ma jukuboth, ku ngeye man ku ngeye abedo ka ngwec i piny ma nen calu yamo ma nyayu lworo. Lembe moko ope ma copo cungo; I paru mi lek ma rwenyo ngec zo, i ajikine tipo para uwok kud i koma; kadi nangu koma podi umediri ka yeng calu pendulum, kare malac ingey tego ma ugam ucwale ugam uwok woko.

Ma podi wilo wil wok iwiya, aneno nia jutic mi nam ma dit man mizzen-mast-heads ubedu ka nindu. Pieno, i ajikine, wan adek zoo wawok kud i spars, man pi swing moko ci ma watimo, jubedo ku yeng ma piny kud i bang jatel wi yei ma ubedu ka nindu. Waves, de, utingo wigi ma rwinyo; man i dhu nam ma lac, yo piny ucungo yo reto, man ceng ucungo iwi piny ceke.

I ngeye nyanok, pii ucwale ithe wanga ma umu; calu timo marac cinga umaku bongo; moko ma nen ungo, ma timo ber ugam ugwoko an; ku lworo adok i kwo. Man nen! kago piny mwa, mabor ungo ku fathoms pyer angwen, Sperm Whale madit ubedo ka ngwec i pii calu meli ma utur, ngeye ma lac, ma ler, ma rangi pa Ethiopia, ma ryeny i lero pa ceng calu kiyo. Ento ma ubewotho i nam, man ma ubewotho ku pii ma mol, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale nen calu burgher ma ube madhu pipe pare mi diewor ma mor. Ento pipe eca, rec ma can, ubedu peri mir ajiki. Calu ma jutwo ku cing jajok moko, yei ma nindo man dhanu ceke ma gibedo i iye gicaku nindo acel; man dwand madwong kud i thenge zo mi yei, karacelu ku dwand adek ma malu, gikok ku koko ma rwinyo, ma rec madit ne ucwalu pii ma ryeny i polo moth moth man kare ku kare.

"Wek yei woko! Luff!" Ahab owacu. Man ni lubu cik pare gire, ebolo yei piny ma podi jatel wi yei utingo ngo cinge.

Koko ma nyawang pa jurutic mi yei ucikere ni nyayu lworo i rec madit; man ma podi yei utuc piny, elokore maber, egam ewotho yo piny, ento ku kwiocwiny ma tek, man etimo pii manok kende, ma eparu nia ecopo bedo ngo ku lworo, Ahab umio cik nia kud jutii ku yei, man ngati moko kud uwec ento ku wec ma yot. Ka wabedo calu Ontario Indians iwi gunwales mi yei, wawotho pio pio ento ling ling; bedo moth ma yio ngo pi meli ma timo ngo dwand ma jube ketho. Kawoni, ma wabe ringo i ngeye, lei makwiny ne utingo yibe pare futi pyer angwen i polo, man epodho woko kud i wang dhanu calu ot ma malu ma jucamu.

“Lembe moko ubetimere!” ubedo koko, lembe ma jukoro ndhundhu ku Stubb's ma ucwalu mac pare man ucwalu pipe pare, pi kare eni juyike. I ngei ma kare pare mi sayu dwand ukadhu, rec madit ne ucungu kendu, man kawoni ebedo i wang yei pa ja madhu taba, man ceng ceng ku yei eca, Stubb ukwanu rwom mi make. Ebedo lemandha, kawoni, nia rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ugam unyang ikum ju ma lubo tok pare. Bedo ling ceke mi bedo moth ubedu piny ma pire tek ungo. Paddles jubolo piny, man oars bino matek i tuko. Man podi epodho i pipe pare, Stubb ukwayu jutic pare pi lwiny ne.

Eyo, aloka loka madit bino iwi rec. Makwo i ariti pare, ebecidho "wotho woko"; thenge eca uwok kud i mad yeast ma en eyubu.*

*Ebi nen ikabedu mange ikum piny ma yot mandha ma iye pa wic madit pa sperm whale. Kadi bende nen ni etiye dit uloyu, etiye piny ma pire tek uloyu ikume. Pi meno ku yot yot etingo malu iwi polo, man etimo kumeno kani ewotho ku ngwec pare madit. Mi mede, lac pa thenge ma malu pa wiye, man kite ma pii ma nyai pa thenge ma piny, ma ka etingo wiye malo, jucopo wacu niya elokore kud i bluff-bowed sluggish galliot ni sharppointed New York pilot-boat.

"Cak kude, caku kude, coo para! Kud wubed ku lworo; kwany sawa madwong-ento caku kude; caku kude calu polo, eno kende," Stubb owacu, ma ucwalu iro kani ebeweco. "Cak kude, kawoni; mi igi cing mabor man matek, Tashtego. Cak kude, Tash, nyathin para—caku kude, zo; ento bed moth, bed moth—cucumbers ubedo wec—yot, yot—cak kude kende calu tho ma rac man jok jok ma ngyeru, man cer ju m'utho ma juyiko maber kud i liel migi—cak awobi pare zo!

"Woo-hoo! Wa-hee!" Gay-Header owacu koko, ma utingo wer moko ma con i polo; calu ngati acel acel ma ucungo i yei ma tek ucungo anyim ku ngwec acel ma tek ma ja India ma cwinye tek umio.

Ento koko pare ma rac judwoko wange ku dhanu mukende ma rac. “Kee-hee! Kee-hee!” Daggoo owacu, ma ucungo anyim man ngeye iwi kom pare, calu lamvor ma ubewothu i ot pare.

“Ka-la! Koo-loo!” Queequeg ukok, calu ma eturo dhoge iwi ringo pa Grenadier. Man kumeno ku oars man yells keels poko nam. Ikinde marom, Stubb ubedo ku kabedu pare i mutukari, podi eketho cwiny dhanu pare ni caku, man ecaku yuyu iro kud i dhoge. Calu ju ma cwinygi lith gi tingo man gi tego, nitundo ma koko mi jolo welo uwinjiri—“Cung, Tashtego!—mi ire!” Harpoon ne jubolo. “Stern all!” Ju ma giwotho ku yei gi dwoko pii cen; i saa marom piny moko ucaku lyel man ucwale iwi cing gi acel acel. Ubedo dul pa jok. I kare ma ukadhu, Stubb ugam umaku ngwec ario mukende kude iwi loggerhead, kama, pi rwom ma malu pa ngwec pare, iro ma rangi pa hempen blue ugam uwok malu man ugam ucungo ku iro ma ucungo iwi paip pare. Calu laini ukadhu ikum loggerhead; kumeno bende, ma podi etuc ungo i kabedu ne, edaru kadhu kud i cing Stubb ario, kama bongo mi cing, nyo square mi quilted canvas ma jubed juruko i kare eni, upoto piny. Ubedo calu maku pala lucwic pa jadhogi ma cinge ario, man jadhogi ne ube tembo kare ku kare ni kwanye kud i cingi.

"Wek laini! jwigo laini!" Stubb owacu ni jatel wi pii (en ebedo inget pii) ma, utingo kofi pare, ebolo pii nam i iye.* Jutimo loko mange, kara dul ne ucaku mako kabedo pare. Yey ne dong utingo i pii ma lyet calu rec shark ma cinge zo. Stubb ku Tashtego keni giloko kabedo—stem pi stern—tic ma nyayu lworo mandha i lembe ma nyayu lworo.

*Pi nyuthu beru pa timo eni, jucopo wacu, nia, i tic mi mako rec macon mi Dutch, mop jubed jutiyo kudu pi jwigo pii ku pii; I yei mange madwong, piggin ma juyubu ku baw, nyo bailer, juketho pi tic eno. Kofi peri, kadi ango, utiye maber uloyu.

Nicaku i cing yei ma ucungo iwi bor pa wi yei, man nicaku i rwom ma malu pa yei, icopo paru niya yei ne utiye ku keel aryo— acel ma poko pii, acel ma nyayu yamo—ma yei ubekadhu kud i kind piny ario ma tung tung i saa acel. Cacade ma mediri ubetimere i bows; eddy ma jik ungo i wange; man, ku ngwec ma nok kud i iye, kadi nang cing ma nok, yei ma yeng yeng, ma nyai ucungo iwi gunwale pare ma ucungo i nam. Kumeno gi ringo; ngati acel acel ku tego man ku tego ma ucungo iwi kom pare, kara kud juwodhe i foam; man kite mabor pa Tashtego ma ucungo iwi steering oar ma ucungu piny wang aryo, pi ni dwoko piny rwom pa piny pare. Atlantics ku Pacifics zo nen calu ukadhu ikare ma gibe ringo i yo migi, nitundu i ajikine rec ma julwongo ni whale udwoko ngwec pare piny.

“Kel i—kel i!” Stubb owacu ni jabodho! man, nen ikum rec madit, cinge zo ucaku ywayo yei ikume, podi yei ne podi jube tingo. I ngeye nyanok, Stubb, ma eketho tyende i cleat ma nya wange tek, ucwalu dart acel acel i rec ma ucungo; i lembe pa cik, yei ne lokore ka wok kud i yo pa rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale, man edok ecidho malu pi timo fling mange.

Pii makwar kawoni ucwir kudi thenge zo pa lei makwiny calu kulo ma mol piny kud iwi got. Kume ma remo ne unyai ungo i pii ma pii ento i rimo, ma ucwir man ucwir pi furlongs ingeye. Ceng ma ucungo iwi nam macol eni i nam, udwoko ler pare i wang dhanu ceke, kara gin ceke gi nenre ikindgi calu dhanu makwar. Man i kare eni zo, jet ku jet pa iro matar ubedo ka wok kud i spiracle pa whale, man puff ku puff ma tek kud i dhoge pa jadwong ma ubedu kud anyonga; calu i dart moko ci, ma etingo lance pare ma goro (ku cinge ma juketho i kume), Stubb udwoko wange kendo kendo, ku goyo ma nyap nyanok ikum gunwale, man edwoko wange kendo kendo i whale.

“Yer malo—ting malo!” koro ekok ni jatel wi adungu, ma rec ma julwongo ni whale ma ubepodho ubedo moth i ngei pare. “Ci malo!—kago ku!” man yei ne ucungo inget rec. Kinde ma etuc mabor iwi yei, Stubb utingo thwol pare mabor ma bit i rec, man egwoko keca, ma ucwale man ucwale maber, calu ngati ma ubeyenyo ni winjo i ngei saa moko mi gold ma rec ma julwongo ni whale copo umwonyo, man ma ebedo ku lworo mi turo ma podi ecopo mako ngo. Ento sawa mi mola macol ma en ubeyenyo ubedo kwo ma iye pa rec. Man kawoni jutwo; kum, nicaku kud i lworo pare i piny ma juweco pire ngo ma julwongo ni "flurry" pare, lei makwiny ne ubedo ka mol i rimo pare, epong ku pii ma nyayu lworo, ma lyethu, ma uketho yei ma ubedu i ariti, ma ugam upoto yo ngeye, ubedu ku lworo madwong mi wok kud i ceng ma ceng eca.

Man kawoni ma edaru rwinyo i ngwec pare, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale udok kendo i wang dhanu; wok kud i thenge ni thenge; spasmodically dilating and contracting his spout-hole, with sharp, cracking, agonized respirations. I ajiki, pii ma nyai ma nyai, calu ve nya ubedo nya macol pa kongo makwar, ucwale i yamu ma lworo; man epodho cen kendu, eringo ma mol piny ku cinge ma rwinyo ngo i nam. Cwinye utur!

"Etho, Mr. Stubb," Daggoo uwacu.

“Eyo; paip ario zoo uthum!” man ewodho pare kud i dhoge, Stubb ubolo buru ma tho iwi pii; man, pi kare manok, ucungu ku paru man eneno avu madit ma etimo.





THEK 62. The Dart.

Lembe moko ikum lembe moko ma utimere i dul mir ajiki.

Ni lubre ku tic ma rwinyore mi mako rec, yei ma dit ma julwongo ni whale-boat ucungo iwi yei, ku jatel wi yei nyoke janek rec ma dit calu jatel wi yei pi kare manok, man harpooneer nyo whale-fastener ma tingo oar ma kwong, ma julwongo ni harpooneer-oar. Kawoni emito cing matek man ma lworo pi goyo cuma mir acel i rec; pi kare ku kare, i gin ma julwongo ni long dart, jam tic ma pek umitu jubol bor pa futi pyer aryo nyo pyer adek. Ento kadi bed ni ngwec eca ubedo pi kare malac man ebedo tek, jatel wi harpooneer jubekuro ni etingo yei pare nitundo i ajikine; andha, jubekuro ni ebed lanyuth pa tic ma malu pa dhanu ni ju mukende, ungo kende ku ngwec maber akeca, ento ku wec ma tek man ma tek; man ango ma etiye ni mediri ka koko i wi kompas peri, podi ler mange zo ucungo man nusu ucaku tic—gin ango ma ngati moko mbe ma ngeyo ento ju ma gitemo. Pi acel, acopu ngo koko ku cwinya zo man atim tic marac akeca i sawa acel. I lembe maeni mi lworo, man mi koko, dong, ku ngeye i kum rec, ndhundhu ne jabodho mi harpooner ma ool uwinjo koko ma nyayu anyonga—“Cung, man imi ire!” Kawoni umitu epodh piny man eketh yei pare, elokre i dye yo, eting harpoon pare kud i kore, man ku tego manok ma copo bedu, etembo ni cwale i whale. Ebedo lembe mi ziazia ngo, ka wakwanu dul pa ju ma gikwo i nam acel, nia kud i kind 50 ma ber pa dart, abic kende re ma timo maber; epe lembe mi ziazia nia ju ma timo tic ku harpooneers mapol ma gi timo rac gi bedo ku lworo man gi timo rac; epe lembe mi ziazia nia jumoko ikum gi gi turo ler mi rimo migi i yei; epe ku lworo nia sperm whalemen moko gibedo mbe pi oro angwen ku pipa angwen; epe lembe mi ziazia nia ni wegi yei madwong, mako rec ma julwongo ni whale ubedo lembe ma rwinyo; kum en e harpooneer ma timo wotho, man ka itingo yweyo kud i kume, icopo bedo ku gen mi nwangu nenedi ka dhanu mapol mitu!

Bende, ka dart ubedo maber, eca i saa mir aryo ma pire tek, niwacu, ka rec ma dit ucaku ringo, boatheader ku harpooneer de gicaku ringo anyim man ngeye, ni lworo ma copo wok ikumgi man ikum dhanu eca. I ngeye giloko kabedo; man jatel wi yei, jatel wi yei ma nok, utingo kabedo pare i thenge mi yei.

Kawoni, angeyu ngo ikum ngati ma ucungo ikum lembe ma tung tung, ento lembe eni zo utiye mi mingo man ma pire tek ungo. Jatel wi tic umaku ubed i cinge nicaku i acaki nituc i ajiki; ecikere ni goyo harpoon man lance, man kud ebed ku rowing moko ci ma jukuro kud i bange, mako kende i lembe ma nen kamaleng ni jadwar rec. Angeyo niya eni copo bedu ku rwenyo ngwec manok i ngwec; ento ngec ma lee i kum rec ma tung tung mi thek acel uketho abedo ku yioyic nia i pol pa lembe ma rac ma jutimo i tic mi mako rec, ebedo ngo ngwec pa rec ma julwongo ni whale calu ma jular jukoro con nia olo pa harpooneer re ma ukelo gi.

Mi bedo ku tic maber uloyu i dart, ju ma tiyo ku harpooneers mi ng'om eni umitu gicak cungu iwi tyend gi kud i bedo ling, ento ungo kud i tic matek.





CHAPTER 63. The Crotch.

I kind lum, jang yen dongo; kud ikind gi, jang yen. Dong, i lembe ma kelo adwogi maber, med chapter.

Crotch ma juweco pire i mbaya mukadho ukwayu juwec pire kende. Etiye yen ma jukubo ku rangi matung tung, ma bor pare romo futi aryo, ma juketho i starboard gunwale ceng ku bow, pi bedo ku kabedo mi yom pi ngete pa harpoon, ma cinge mange macol, ma jukuboth uwok kud i prow. Kumeno, jam lwiny ne bedo ceng ikum jatel wi cinge, ma etingo ne yot yot kud i cinge calu jal ma timo tic i lum maku cinge kud i kor ot. Ubedo kura ni bedu ku harpoons ario ma juketho i cing, ma julwongo ni irons mir acel ku mir aryo.

Ento harpoons ario eni, acel acel ku thung pare gire, gi zo gi rwate ku cing; lembe ubedo eni: ni cwalu gi ario zoo, ka copu bedu, acel ingeye acel i rec acel; kara ka, i kare ma bino, acel utingo woko, wadi copo bedu asu ku twero. Etiye medu pa gum wang aryo. Ento pol kare etimere nia pi ngwec ma nyawang, ma tek, ma nyai pa rec ma julwongo ni whale ikare ma enwangu cuma mir acel, edoko tek ni harpooneer, kadi bed ni etiye calu lyethu i wotho pare, ni cwalu cuma mir aryo i iye. Ento, calu ma cuma mir aryo dong ucungo ku cinge, man cinge ube ringo, ci jam lwiny eca umaku, i lembe ceke, juyik woko kud i yei, yore moku man kama moku; ka epe, peko madit uloyu copo bedu cing dhanu zo. Ka jupodho i pii, etiye i lembe ma kumeno; spare coils pa box line (ma juweco pire i dul mukadho) ma weko tic eni, pol kare, bedu yot. Ento timo ma pire tek eni epe kare ku kare ma dhanu moko ungiyo ngo ku dhanu ma rac man ma tho.

Bende: umitu ingei niya ka jubolo cuma mir aryo iwi nam, edoko piny ma lworo ma ucungo, ma ucungo iwi yei man rec ma dit, ma ucungo iwi yei, nyo ungolo gi, man eketho lworo i yore zo. Bende, i ayi ma rwinyo, ecopu bedu ngo ni gwoko kendu nitundu ju mako rec ma julwongo ni whale man etho.

Nen, kawoni, kite ma umitu obed kudu i lembe pa yei angwen ma gi tiyo ku yei acel ma tek, ma timo tic, man ma ngeyo lembe; ka pi kura eni i iye, man bende pi ret ma romo elufu acel mi tic ma tek, abora nyo apar ma rwinyo copo bedu ka ucungo ikume. Pi, andha, yei acel acel juyike ku harpoons madwong ma jubi goyo iwi cinge tek yei mir acel jubolo maber man edwoko ngo. Lembe eni zo jukoro gi maber kany, pi eno gibiweko ngo ni nyuthu lembe mapol ma pire tek, kadi nya edoko tek, i lembe ma jubi goyo i ngeye.





THE 64. Cam mi diewor pa Stubb.

Rec madit pa Stubb junego bor ku yei. Ubedo ling; dong, wabe timo tandem pa yei adek, wacaku tic ma nyathoth mi tingo trophy ni Pequod. Man kawoni, calu wan dhanu apar wi abora ku cing mwa pyer adek wi abusiel, man cingwa miya acel ku pyer abora, watiyo moth moth sawa ku sawa iwi kum ma timo ngo tic man ma timo tic i nam; man nen calu ecopo loko ngo, mako i kare ma bor; lanyuth maber jubedu ka nyuthu ikum dit pa dhanu ma wawotho. Pi, iwi pii madit mi Hang-Ho, nyo gin moko ci ma gilwonge, i China, jurutic angwen nyo abic iwi yo mi tyend dhanu bi tingo piny ma pek ma jucwalo ku rwom pa mairo acel pi sawa acel; ento argosy madit eni ma watingo ma tek, calu ma jupong ku pig-lead madwong.

Mudho ubino; ento ler adek ma malu man piny i Pequod's main-rigging unyutho yo mwa; ikare ma wabecidho ceng, waneno Ahab ubebolo acel ikin mac mapol iwi got. Eneno rec ma dit ma ucungo iwi yei pi kare manok, emio cik ma nja pi gwoko ne pi dyewor, man emiyo ler pare ni jara tic mi nam, emondo i ot, man edok ungo iwange kendu nitundo uthieno.

Kadi bende, i neno wi lubo tok rec madit eni, Captain Ahab unyutho tic pare macon, ni lwonge kumeno; ento kawoni ma lei ne utho, lembe moko ma nyayu anyonga, nyo bedo moth, nyo bedo moth, nen calu ubetimere i iye; calu nen pa ngati mutho eca upoyo wiye nia Moby Dick podi jubi nego ngo; man kadi bende jukelo rec mange elifu acel i yei pare, lembe eni zo copo ketho piny pare madit man ma timo lembe acel kende ucidhi anyim. I ngeye nyanok, icopo paru nia kud i dwand ma iwi yei pa Pequod, nia cing dhanu ceke ubedu ka yube ka ketho meli i nam ma thuc; pi nyoru ma pek jube tingo iwi yei, man jube cwalu woko kud i vur mi yei. Ento ku cinge ma nyai, avu madit ne gire, ento yei epe, re ma umitu juketh i yei. Jutwi ku wiye i ngeye, man yibe i theng yei, rec ma dit kawoni uvuto ku adila pare macol ceng ku meli man juneno kud i mudho pa dyewor, ma umu spars man rigging malu, gin ario—meli ku whale, nen calu gicokiri karacelu calu thwol madongo, ma acel udong ucungu.

*Piny moko ma nok copo bedu maber kany. Gima tek man ma gen ma meli utiye kudu ikum rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ka juyike ingete, utiye ku cinge nyo yibe; man pi rwom pare madit, dul ne pek uloyu mukende (muweko side-fins), bedo pare ma yot kadi i tho, miyo epodho piny piny; kara ku cingi kud ibed ku copo mi mako kudu kud i yei, kara iket nyor ikume. Ento peko eni judaru ku ryeko: juyike dul ma nok man ma tek ku bao i thenge ma woko, man piny ma pek i dyere, ma thenge mange jugwoko i yei. Ku telowic maber, juyike float ma juyubu ku yen ni medere iwi thenge mange pa mass, kara kawoni dong jutwoyo whale, chain juyike yot ni lubo kore; man ka juwotho i kum, i ajikine jugwoko matek i thenge ma nok pa yibe, kama girombo ku iye ma lac nyoke lobes.

Ka Ahab ma cwinye lith kawoni ubedo moth, kadi nang ikum deck, Stubb, wadi mir aryo, ma cwinye lith, unyutho anyonga ma nen ungo ento podi ber. Ebedo ku tic ma lee dit ma Starbuck, jatel wi tic pare, uweko tic pare pi kare ma en kende re ma telo wi lembe. Lembe acel ma nok, ma konyo pi kwo maeni i Stubb, unyuthire nyathotho. Stubb ubedo ngati ma cwinye tek; ebedo ku mar ma lee dit ikum rec ma julwongo ni whale calu piny ma leng i dhoge.

"Ringu, ringu, ma podi anindu ngo! In, Daggoo! icidho iwi nam, man ipoko ira acel kud i cinge!"

Kan ung'ei, nia kadi ju ma mako rec ma gikwo i lum gi timo ngo, calu piny ma rwinyo, man nimakere ku cik madit mi judongo, gi ketho jurudegi gi culu pi lwiny (ma podi gi nwango ngo lim mi wotho), ento kawoni man i ngeye ibinwangu jumoko mi Nantucketers ma gitiye ku miti madit pi thenge moko mi Sperm Whabble ma Stubble uketho; ma uketho i iye thenge ma nyai pa kum.

I kind diewor, ringu eca jupoko man jutedo; man juwango ku ler ario pa moo pa ic, Stubb ucungu matek pi cam pare mi diewor i capstan-head, calu capstan ne ubedo sideboard. Stubb de ubedu ngo ngati acel kende ma ucamu ring'o rec ma julwongo ni whale i dyewor eca. Ku loko dwandgi ku dwand pare gire, rec sharks elufu ku elufu, ma ucungu ikum leviathan ma tho, ma ucamu moth pare. Ju ma nok ma gibedo piny i kavutu migi gibedo ku lworo pi goyo yibe migi ikum yei, i kind inches manok ku adunde pa ju ma gibe nindo. Ka ineno yo piny icopo neno gi (calu ma podi iwinjo gi ngo) ka giwotho i pii macol man macol, man giloko wigi iwi ngeye ka giwodho dul madongo ma rom ku wic pa dhanu. THEK 65. Whale calu cam.

Dhanu ma tho ucikere ni camu lei ma camu tara pare, man, calu Stubb, camu ku lero pare gire, calu ma icopo wacu; eni nen ni piny ma nen ungo ma ngati ucikere ni cidho nyanok i lembe mukadho man paru pare.

Ubedo lembe ma jugoro, nia oro miya adek mukadho leb pa Right Whale jubedu ka nen ni piny ma leng i France, man jubedu ka wilu bei madit keca. Bende, i rundi pa Henry VIIIth, jatedo moko mi ker unwangu foc maber pi yubu jamb tedu maber mi camu ku rec ma juyiko ku mac, ma, poi, utiye kit rec moko ma julwongo ni whale. Porpoises, andha, podi tin eni gineno gi ni cam maber. Ringu ne juyike ni opira ma rom ku opira mi billiard, man ka juyike maber man juyike maber jucopo kwanu ni turtle-balls nyo veal balls. Lembe mandha utiye, ikind ju ma gi dwar, rec ma julwongo ni whale jubi kwanu ni cam maber, kan epe; ento ka ibino piny i wang ringo ma bor pare romo futi dak acel, ekwanyo mitu peri mi cam. Man eni poyo wiya nia dhanu moko mi England, ma con juweko gi i Greenland ku yei mi maku rec—nya coo gi gikwo pi dwi madwong iwi lum macol pa rec ma juweko i dhu nam ingey temo pii. Ikind ju Dutch whalemen, piny eni julwongo gi niya "fritters"; ma, andha, girombo lee, ma gibedo macol man ma leng, man ma ngwic migi nen ve dough-nuts pa mon mi ot macon mi Amsterdam nyo oly-cooks, ka gi nyen.
