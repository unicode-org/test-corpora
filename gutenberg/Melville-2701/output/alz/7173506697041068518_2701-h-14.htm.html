CHAPTER 67. Cutting In.

Ubedo dyewor mi ceng kas acel, man ceng yenga ma lubo! Juponj ma timo tic mi turo ceng yenga gi zo gi ju ma timo tic mi turo cik. Pequod ma rangi pa ivory udoko piny ma nen calu piny ma rac; jalweny moko ci ubedo jawang ringu. Icopo paru niya wabe mio thwon dyang makwar elifu apar ni mungu nam.

I acaki, jam tic ma dit mi poko piny, ikind piny mukende ma pek ma uketho i iye dul pa got ma rangi pa lum, man ma ngati acel de copo tingo ngo—dul madit pa mzabibu eni juyike malu ikum wi got man jutwoyo matek ikum wi yei ma piny, kama tek uloyu iwi yei. Ajiki pa thwol ma calu hawser ma ucungo ikind piny ma nya wang eca, jutero i windlass, man dul madit ma piny pa tackles juwodho iwi rec; ikum block eni juyike blubber hook madit, ma pek pare romo paun miya acel. Man kawoni ma jucungo iwi ngete, Starbuck ku Stubb, juwadi, ku jam lwiny migi ma bor, ucaku ketho vur i kum dhanu pi ketho cing gi iwi cing cing ario. Ka dong jutimo eni, jupoko dul ma lac, ma ucungo iwi vur, jubolo cinge, man dul madit pa jurutic ube goyo wer ma tek, kawoni ucaku wecu ikind dul pa dhanu mapol i windlass. Kinde ma ndhundhu, yei zoo ucungo iwi ngete; bolt moko ci i iye ucaku calu wic mi ot macon i kare ma ngic; yeng yeng, yeng yeng, man etingo wiye ma lworo i polo. Kakare, edwoko wange ikum rec ma dit, ma windlass moko ci ma wok kud i windlass judwoko wange ku kony pa windlass; nitundu i ajikine, dwand moko ma nyap man ma nyayu lworo uwinjere; ku yei madit yei ucungo malu man ngeye kud i whale, man triumpful tackle ucungu in wange ma utingo ingeye thenge ma rwinyo pa strip mir acel pa blubber. Kawoni calu blubber umu whale calu ma rind umo orange, kumeno juwodhe woko kud i kum calu orange juwodhe woko ku spiralizing it. Pi rwom ma windlass ucungo kudu uketho whale ubedo ka ngwec iwi pii, man calu ma blubber i strip acel ucungo iwi rwom ma julwongo ni "scarf," ma jupoko ku spades pa Starbuck ku Stubb, ju ma gicokiri; man pio pio calu ma juwodhe woko, man andha ku timo eno gire, jube tingo malu man malu malu nitundu ikume ma malu ucamu wi got madit; dhanu ma gibedo i windlass giweko tingo, man pi kare manok nyo aryo rimo ma mol ne ucwale iwi polo, man ngati acel acel ma nuti ucikere ni bedo moth pi jwigo ka ewotho, tek epe ecopu nyayu ithe man epodho iwi nam.

Ng'atu acel m'ikind ju ma bino i coko ne kawoni ubecidho ku jam lwiny mabor man ma tek ma julwongo ni boarding-sword, man ka eneno kare pare, ebolo vur madit i thenge ma piny pa piny ma ucungo. I kind vur eni, ajiki pa tuko mir aryo ma dit ma juloko juyike kara ebed ku cing ikum blubber, kara ebed ayika pi gin ma bilubo. I ngeye, jabodho ma jabodho eni, eketho cing dhanu ceke ucungu, kendo etimo ngwec mi ngec ikum piny ne, man ku cinge manok, ma nyayu lworo, ma nyayu lworo, eturo piny ne i ario; kara podi thenge ma piny ma nok podi utiye pio pio, thenge ma malu ma bor, ma julwongo ni blanket-piece, ucungo maleng, man dong etiye atera pi dwoko piny. Ju ma tingo piny kawoni gicaku wer migi, man podi tackle acel ubewodho man ubetingo strip mir aryo kud i whale, mange ubedo ka dwoko piny moth moth, man strip mir acel ucidho piny kud i dhugola madit ma piny, i ot ma juketho iye piny ma julwongo ni blubber-room. I ot maeni mi diewor, cing dhanu mapol ma timo tic maber umediri ka tingo bongo ma bor ma calu thwol ma kwo. Man kumeno tic umediri; jamb tic ario ne tingo man dwoko piny i sawa acel; whale ku windlass zo ube tingo, heavers ube wer, blubber-room gentlemen ube coiling, mates ube scarfing, meli ube tek, man cing dhanu ceke ube kwero kare ku kare, pi jwigo lwiny.





THEK 68. Jam umo.

Amio nen ma nok ikum lembe ma dhanu ungiyo ngo, adila pa rec ma julwongo ni whale. Abedo ku lembe ma nya wange ikume ku ju ma ngeyo tic ikum rec ma julwongo ni whalemen, man ju ma gisomo ikum piny ma kwo i nam. Paru para macon udong marom; ento etiye paru kende.

Penji utiye, adila pa whale utiye ango man kan kani? Dong ing'eyo nia blubber pare utiye ango. Blubber eca ubedo piny moko ma nen calu ringu dyang ma tek, ma nyai, ento ma tek, ma nyap man ma nyap, man edongo niai i inci abora nyo apar nitundo apar wi aryo ku apar wiye abic.

Kawoni, kadi nang ecaku nen ni lembe mi mingo ni weco ikum adila pa lei moko ci ni bedu ku kit ma rom man ma gwai, ento i lemandha eni epe ku lembe ma nyayu paru ma kumeno; kum icopo tingo ngo piny moko cii ma ucungo iwi kom pa rec ma dit ento blubber marom; man dul ma woko ma ucungo iwi lei moku ci, tek ebedo ma pek, ango ma copo bedu ento adila? Andha, kud i adila pa rec ma julwongo ni whale, icopo kwanyo ku cingi piny moko ma nyap, ma nen maleng, ma nen calu isinglass ma nyap, ento etiye yot man mayom calu satin; eni, ma podi jutwo ngo, ka edaru nyai man edoko tek, ento edoko tek man ma nyap. Atiye ku piny moko ma muthwo, ma atiyo kudu pi ketho lanyuth i buku para mi whale. Etie maleng, calu ma awacu con; man ka juketho iwi karatasi ma jugoro, saa moko abedo kud anyonga ku nen nia etiye ku tego madit. Ku yore moku ci, eber ni kwanu lembe ikum rec ma julwongo ni whales nikadhu kud i wang gi, calu ma icopo wacu. Ento gin ma atye ka weco pire kany utiye eni. Piny marom ma nyap, isinglass, ma, ayiyu, mako kum pa rec ma dit, epe ni nen calu adila pa lei, calu adila pa adila, ni wecu; kum ubedu lembe mi ming kende ni wacu, nia adila pa rec madit ma julwongo ni tremendous whale utiye mayom man mayom uloyu adila pa nyathin ma fodi junyolu ngo. Ento dong epe.

Ka waparu nia blubber ubedo adila pa rec ma dit; kadong, ka adila eni, calu i lembe pa Sperm Whale madit, ebi mio pii ma romo barel miya acel pa moo; man, ka juporo nia, i wel, nyo i pek, moo meno, i kite pare ma juweco pire, utiye acel mi angwen kende, man epe piny ceke mi koti; paru moko copo bedu nuti ikum dit pa piny ma kwo, ma dul acel kende pa iye ma nyayu nam ma pii ma rom ku meno. Ka ikwanu pipa apar pi ton acel, ibedo ku ton apar pi pek pa piny adek kende pa adila pa rec ma dit.

I kwo, piny ma nen pa Sperm Whale epe ikind piny madwong ma enyutho. Pol kare edoko piny ma juporo man judwoko kendo ku lanyuth ma rwinyo ma romo wel, piny moko calu ma nwangire i cal maber mi Italy. Ento lanyuth eni nen ungo ni jukyewo iwi piny ma julwongo ni isinglass ma juweco pire malu, ento nen calu jukyewo kud i iye, calu ka jukyewo iwi kum gire. Bende epe eni kende. I kare moko, ni wang ma neno piny pio pio, lanyuth ma rwom pare lapiny, calu i goyo cal mandha, ento eketho the lembe mange. Eni gi hieroglyphics; eni, ka ilwongo cyphers ma dhanu ungeye ngo iwi cel pa pyramids ni hieroglyphics, eno re ma ubedo wec maber mi tiyo kudu i lembe ma kawoni. Ku ponji para maber pa hieroglyphics iwi Sperm Whale acel, cwinya unyai lee dit pi plate moko ma nyutho coc macon pa ju India ma jugoro iwi hieroglyphics palisades ma pire tek i dhu nam mi Upper Mississippi. Calu kidi ma nyayu lworo, de, rec ma julwongo ni mystic-marked whale udong ma dhanu copo nyang kudu ngo. Lembe maeni ikum godi pa India poyo wiya ikum piny mange. Kakare pa lembe mukende ma woko pa Sperm Whale nyuthu, en enyutho ngo ngeye, man asaga thenge pare, ma juwodho i thenge madit pa nen pa linear, pi the lembe mapol ma rac, ma nen ungo, ma nen ungo. Acopo wacu nia kidi mi New England ma nwang'ere i dhu nam, ma Agassiz paru nya etiye ku lanyuth mi mako cing ku icebergs madongo ma ucungo iwi pii—Acopo wacu, nia kidi eni ucikere ngo ni bedu calu Sperm Whale i lembe eni. Man de nen ira nya ret ma kumeno i rec ma julwongo ni whale copo bedu ni piny ma wok kud i cing rec ma julwongo ni whale mange; kum aneno gi lee dit i thwol madongo, ma dongo ma romo.

Wec acel nyo aryo mukende ikum lembe eni ikum adila nyo moo pa rec ma dit. Juweco pire con, nia juwodho kud i bange i dul mabor, ma julwongo ni blanket-pieces. Calu sea-terms mapol, eni utiye kud anyonga man piretek. Pien rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ucungo iwi moo pare calu i blanket mandha nyo counterpane; nyo, ber muloyo, poncho pa India ucwale iwi wiye, man ucungo iwi kore. Ubedo pi bedo maber pa kume, ma whale copo bedu maber i koth zo, i nam zo, i sawa zo, man i pii zo. Ango ma copo timere ikum winyo ma julwongo ni Greenland whale, i nam ma ngic man ma ngic mi yo malu, ka epe ku piny pare ma leng? Andha, rec mukende nwangire ma mol matek akeca i pii mi Hyperborean; ento eni, nen, gi ubedo rec peri ma rimo ngic, ma ic migi utiye friji; lei, ma lyel ithe got ma ngic, calu jawothu i kare mi ngico ma lyel i wang mac mi ot welo; ento, calu dhanu, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale utiye ku ombo man rimo ma mor. Gwok rimo pare, man etho. Ebedo ber nenedi—muweko i ngei gonyo—nya lei madit eni, ma lyethu pa kum ubedo piny ma pire tek ni dhanu; ber nenedi nia jubinwange i pacu, ma ucungo iwi dhoge pi kwo pare zo i pii mi Arctic! kama, ka ju ma giwotho i nam gipodho i nam, jubed junwangu gi, dweche mapol i ngeye, ma gicungo i dye nam, calu winyo ma junwangu ma jukubo i amber. Ento piny ma nyayu awura uloyu utiye ni ngeyo, calu ma judaru nyutho ku lembe ma jutimo, nia rimo pa rec ma julwongo ni Polar whale ubedo ma mor uloyu rimo pa Borneo negro i kare mi cwir.

Nen ira, nia i kae waneno bero ma nok pa kero pa ngati acel acel, man bero ma nok pa cel ma gwai, man bero ma nok pa kabedo ma iye. Oh, ngati! nen man ibed calu rec ma julwongo ni whale! In de, bed ma mor ikind pee. In de, kwo i ng'om eni manangu ibedo ngo i iye. Bed moth i equator; gwok pii peri mi rimo i Pole. Calu dome madit pa St. Peter's, man calu whale madit, gwok, O dhanu! i kare ceke lyethu peri giri.

Ento ebedo yot man epe ku genogen ni ponji lembe mabecu eni! Ikind gedo, manok kende re ma dome calu St. Peter's! pa lei, nen calu rec ma dit ma nok!





THEK 69. Liel.

"Ting nyoru! Wek avu ne ucidh yo ngeye!"

Jutugo madongo dong gitimo tic migi. Kum pa rec ma julwongo ni whale ma jungolo wic pare ucwer calu liel ma kidi macol; kadi nya eloko rangi pare, edaru rwinyo piny moko cii. podi etiye madit. Moth moth ewotho cen, pii ma ucungo iwie ungwel man ucwale ku rec sharks, man yamu ma malu upong ku ngwec ma tek pa winy ma kok, ma dhoge ubedo calu poniards mapol ma rac i rec whale. Phantom ma dit ma tar ma wiye epe ubewotho bor man bor ku yei, man dul moko ci ma ewotho kudu, ma nen calu roods square pa sharks man cubic roods pa winy, medu dwand ma rac. Pi sawa madwong kud i yei ma ucungu, piny ma rac nenre. Ithere piny pa polo macol man macol, iwi wang nam ma leng, ma yamo ma kud anyonga ucwale, tho madit eca ubewotho iwi pii, nitundo rwinyu i kabedo ma rwinyo.

Nitie yik ma rac man ma nyayu ngyeru! Sea-vultures zo gibedo i ywak madit, air-sharks zo gibedo i rangi macol nyo macol. I kwo ento manok kende re ma copo konyo rec ma dit, I ween, ka nyo ebedo ku yenyne; ento i saa mi cam mi yik pare, giwotho ku woro madit. Oh, vultureism marac mi ng'om! ma epe whale ma tek uloyu kud i iye.

Man eni epe ajiki. Kud kum dhanu ma rac, tipo moko ma timo lembe marac ukwo man ucungo iwie pi lworo. Juneno ku ngati moko ma lworo lworo nyo yei ma timo lembe ma rac kud kamabor, ka borne umu winyi ma ucungo, kadi bende podi nyuthu piny ma tar ma ucungo iwi ceng, man pii ma tar ma ucungo malu ikume; ndhundhu kum winyo ma julwongo ni whale, ku cinge ma myel, juketho piny i lum— shoals, kidi, man breakers i kabedo maeni: bed ma ling! Man pi oro madwong i ngeye, nyo, yei uweko kabedo ne; gipodho iwie calu rombe ma mingo gipodho iwi vacuum, kum jatel wigi ugam upodho keca kinde ma jumaku yen. Cik peri mi lembe mukadho nuti; tiye tic peri mi kura macon; utiye lembe pa kwo peri ma tek pa yioyic peri macon ma podi rwinyo ngo iwi ngom, man kawoni podi rwinyo ngo iwi yamu! Orthodoxy utiye!

Pieno, kadi nang i kwo, kum pa rec madit copo bedu lworo mandha ni judhoge, i tho pare tipo pare doko lworo ma tek ungo ni ng'om zoo.

Ibedo jayic pa tipo, jawodha? Tiye ku tipo mukende ma unyai pa Cock-Lane, man dhanu ma iye tek uloyu pa Daktari Johnson ma giyio gi.





THEK 70. Sphynx.

Umitu kud juwek nia ma podi juwodho ngo kum pa leviathan, junego wiye. Kawoni, turo wiye pa Sperm Whale ubedo tic ma jutimo i kum dhanu, ma ju ma timo yath pa winyo ma julwongo ni whale gibedo kud anyonga ikum gi gigi: man epe labongo tyen lok.

Keth i wii nia rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale epe ku piny moko ma jucopo lwonge ni ngute; ento, kama wiye ku kume nen calu gicokiri, keca, ikabedu eca, utiye thenge ma gwai. Poi, bende, nia daktari umaku etim operation kud malu, fut abora nyo apar ikind en ku jatic pare, man jadhum eca ung'eyere i nam ma rangi pare lokore, ma nyai, man ma pol kare nyai man ma nyai. Keth i wii, bende, nia i thenge mi lembe ma rac eni, umitu etur tyende madwong i ring; man i yore ma piny eno, manangu enen ungo kadi acel de i wang ret ma nyai, umitu ebed ku ryeko mi jwigo piny ceke ma ceng kudu, man epok ng'et ng'ut i kabedo ma pire tek ku ketho i wic. Dong ibeworo ngo, dong, pi nyang pa Stubb, nia ekwayu dakika apar kende mi turo wi rec ma julwongo ni sperm whale?

Ka jupoko wiye mir acel, wic jubolo yo ngeye man jugwoko keca ku waya nitundo ma juwodhe kume. Ka jutimo kumeno, ka etiye pa rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale, jutingo iwi yei pi jwigere woko. Ento, ku leviathan ma dit eni ecopu bedu ngo; pi wic pa sperm whale utingo acel mi adek pa pek pare, man ni juku tic ma kumeno, kadi ku cing ma dit pa jadwar, eni ubedu piny ma kony ungo calu temo pimo ot pa Dutch ku pimo pa juwil piny.

Pequod's whale ma jupoko wiye man juwodho kume, wiye jutingo i thenge pa yei—i kind nusu pa nam, kara ebed ma juyike ku piny pare ma wok kud i iye. Man keca ku yei ma tek ma ucungo iwi yei, pi rwom ma malu ma ucungo piny kud i wi yei ma piny, man yard-arm acel acel i thenge eca ucungo calu crane iwi pii; keca, wic ma rimo mol eca ucungo iwi kore pa Pequod calu pa Holofernes ma dit ma ucungo iwi cinge pa Judith.

Kinde ma tic maeni mir ajiki uthum, eca ubedo dyeceng, man ju ma giwotho i nam gicidho piny pi camu cam migi mi diewor. Liling ubedo iwi deck ma con ubedo ku lworo ento kawoni dong udong. Copper calm ma tek, calu universal yellow lotus, ubedu ka nyai iwi nam.

Kare manok ukadhu, man i kind dwand eca Ahab wok kende kud i ot pare. I kare ma ewotho iwi yei mir angwen, ecungu pi neno yo piny, man edham emondo moth moth i cinge ma dit egam etingo pala ma bor pa Stubb—ma podi udong keca i ngei tho pa rec ma dit—man egoye i thenge ma piny pa piny ma ucungo iwi yei, eketho cinge mange i thenge acel, man ecungo iwi wange nen maber iwi wic eni.

Ubedo wiye macol man ma umu wic; man ucungo keca ikind piny ma ling, nen calu Sphynx utiye i thim. "Wec, in wic ma dit man ma pire tek," Ahab uwacu ku mwolo, "ma, kadi nang epe ku yuk wic, ento kawoni ku keca nen macol ku lum; wec, wic ma tek, man iwac iwa lembe ma pire tek ma ni i ii. I kind wic ceke, in ipodho piny ma thuc. nying ma jugoro ngo man dul mony mi nam, man gen ma juweco ngo i iye man jamb tedu ma jukubotho; kwo mi ketho gi piny. Ineno jurumer ma jukubotho kud i yei migi ma lyel; ucidhi iwi yei ma epe ku ret—i kare ma lyeth ma tek ube yeng yei ma ceng kudu ma copo tingo cware ma atira ni cinge ma ucungo man ma mitu.

WI 71. Lembe pa Jeroboam.

Cing i cing, yei man yamu ucwir; ento yamu bino pio pio uloyu yei, man pio pio Pequod ucaku yeng.

I ngeye nyanok, nikadhu kud i gilasi yei pa jadwong ne man wi yei pa dhanu unyutho nia etiye yei pa rec ma julwongo ni whale. Ento calu ma ebedo bor ku yamu, man ebedo ka ringo, nen calu ebecidho i ngom mange, Pequod copo bedo ku genogen mi tuc i bange. Ci signal ne juyike ni neno adwogi ango ma jubi dwoko.

Kae jubi wacu, calu yei pa judongo pa lumony, yei pa American Whale Fleet gitiye ku lanyuth migi gigi; lembe ceke ma nyutho ma jucoko i buku ku nying yei acel acel, jatel wi yei acel acel jumiyo igi. Kumeno, judongo pa rec ma julwongo ni whale gibedo ku copo mi ngiyo ngati acel acel iwi nam, kadi nang bor man ku kabedo ma nok.

Lembe pa Pequod ugam udwoko wange ku ngati ma engeyo ngo ma uketho piny pare gire; ma unyutho nia yei ne ubedo pa Jeroboam pa Nantucket. Ka eketho yards pare, epodho piny, epodho abeam i the Pequod's lee, man edwoko yei piny; edoko ceng nyanok; ento, calu ma dul pa yei ubedo ka yike ku cik pa Starbuck ni ketho jatel wi yei, jadwong ma juweco pire utingo cinge kud i ngeye pa yei pare pi nyutho nia timo ne ubedu piny ma pire tek ungo. Ewok nya Jeroboam ubedu ku two marac iwi yei, man Mayhew, jadit pare, ubedu ku lworo mi nyayu two i dul pa Pequod. Pi, kadi nang en ku jutic mi yei gibedo maleng, man kadi yei pare ubedu bor ku nusu pa rifle-shot, man nam ma rac man yamu ubedu ka mol man ube mol ikind gi; ento ku cwinye ceke lubu cik mi bedo kendgi mi ngom, egam ekwero ni bino i rwate ku Pequod.

Ento eni ukwero ngo wec zo. Nigwoko bor pa yadi manok ikind gi ku yei, yei pa Jeroboam ku tic ku yei pare kare ku kare uketho ebedo parallel ku Pequod, ma en ewotho matek i nam (pien i kare eni yamo ubecwir), ku yei pare madit ma ucungo; kadi, andha, saa moko ku acaki pa pii madit ma ucungo, yei ne jucopo cwalu yo moku anyim; ento ndhundhu nica ecopo kelo i rwom pare maber kendu. Nimakere ku lembe eni, man lembe mange ma rom ku meno, wec ubedo ikind ju aryo eno; ento i kind kare moko epe manangu epe ku turo lembe mange ma tung tung.

Tingo yei i yei pa Jeroboam, ubedu ngati ma kite nen ungo, kadi i kwo mi mako rec ma dit ma dhanu ma pire tek ubedo piny ceke. Ebedo awobi ma nok, ma nyanok, ma nyathin, ma ungwel iwi wange zo ku rangi macol, man eketho yuk wic ma nya aceng. Koti ma bor, ma jukoyo maber ma rangi pa walnut ma unyai umuke; cinge ma ucungo iwi cinge ma ucungo iwi cinge. Delirium ma thuc, ma ling, ma jik ungo ubedu i wange.

I kare ma dhanu eca jular jukoro pire, Stubb uwacu—“Eni! Eni!—ng'atu ma cinge bor ma Town-Ho's company uwacu iwa!” Stubb keni uketho lembe moko ma nyang ikum Jeroboam, man ngati moko ikind jutic pare, kare moko mukadho ikare ma Pequod uweco Town-Ho. Ni lubre ku lembe maeni man lembe ma juponjo i ngeye, nen calu scaramouch ma juweco pire ubedo ku twero madit ikum dhanu ceke i Jeroboam. Lembe pare ubedo eni:

Egam epodho ikind ju ma wic pa Neskyeuna Shakers, kama ebedo jabila madit; I coko migi ma nyai, ma ling ma giwok piny wang mapol kud i polo ku yo mi dhugola, ma giwacu nia jubi yabu pio pio pa vial mir abiro, ma etingo i cinge; ento, ma, kakare ni bedu ku gunpowder, umitu juketh ku laudanum. Lembe moko ma nyang ikume, mitu pa jukwenda ugam umake, eweko Neskyeuna ecidho i Nantucket, kama, ku rieko ma nen ungo, egam etimo lembe ma rwinyo, man emiyore gire ni ngati ma copo yio pi wotho pa Jeroboam mi maku rec. Gi ketho cing gi i kume; ento ndhundhu kinde ma meli uwok kud i wang ngom, wic pare ucaku wok manyen. Enyutho kudu nia etiye malaika madit Gabriel, man ekwayu jatel wi yei ni ucung iwi yei. Egoro manifesto pare, kama enyutho kudu nia etiye jalar pa cula mi nam man jatel wi ngom mi Oceanica. Kite ma enyutho ko lembe eni;—tugo macol, ma tek pa paru pare ma epe ku nindo, man lworo ma lee pa delirium mandha, uketho Gabriel eni i wiya pa dhanu mapol ma gingeyo ngo, ku kite ma leng. Bende, gibed gilwore. I kare maber, lembe ma Flask uwacu ubedo lemandha. Calu macon, Pequod ucungo iwi wiye pa sperm whale, kawoni, ku rwom pa wiye ario zoo, edwoko rwom pare marom; kadi nang ebe cwinya lith, icopo yio. Dong, kani i thenge acel itingo wic pa Locke, icidho iwi yo meno; ento kawoni, yo mukende, tingo Kant's man idok kendu; ento i lembe ma rac akeca. Pieno, paru moko nja mediri ka timo yei. Oh, wun ju ma ming! both wi polo eni zo iwi nam, man i ngeye ibecidho i pii ma yot man yor acam.

I ketho kum rec ma julwongo ni right whale, ka jukelo i ngete meli, lembe marom ma jutimo ku sperm whale; ento, i kare ma tok cen, wic jupoko zo, ento i kare ma kwong, lak ku leb juwodhe woko man jutingo iwi yei, ku cogo macol ma julwongo ni crown-piece. Ento piny ma kumeno, i lembe ma kawoni, epe ma jutimo. Avu pa rec ma julwongo ni whales ario zoo upoto yo piny; man yei ma utingo wic ne nen ungo nyanok nyanok ku mule ma utingo panniers ma pek uloyu.

Ikinde marom, Fedallah ubedu ka neno wiye pa rec ma yor acam, man nja ubedu ka neno kud i cinge ma thuc.
