CHAPTER 1. Loomings.

Lwonga Ishmael. Oru moko mukadho- kud ipar pi kare ma rom kani--ma sente manok nyo epe i sanduku para, man piny moko ope ma nyayu anyonga i dhu nam, aparu niya abiwotho ku yei nyanok man anen thenge mi pii mi ng'om. Etiye yore moko ma atiye kudu mi kwanyu adunde man mi yiko rimo. Ka nwango abedo ku lworo ikum dhoga; kinde ceke ma ubedo dwi mir apar wi acel ma ngic, ma ngic i cwinya; kinde ceke ma anwangu nia acungu i wang ot ma jugwoku i iye sanduku mi tho, man akelo lembe mi ngeye pa yik moko ci ma aromo kudu; man asaga mandha ka hypos para utingo cinga, ma kwayu cik ma tek mi juku an kud adok i ngudu, man nyayu wic pa dhanu woko—i ngeye, aneno nya eca ubedo kare mi tuc i nam pio pio. Eni ubedo piny ma aloko pi pistol ku mupira. Ku ryeko mi ryeko Cato ubolo kume iwi pala pare; Adok i yei ling ling. Piny moko mbe ma nyayu wur i lembe eni. Ka nwango gingeyo, coo zo i digri migi, kare moko nyo mukende, gimaru paru marom ku para ikum nam.

Kawoni utiye adhura peri mi Manhattoes, ma ucungu ku cula mi pii calu cula mi India ku got ma dit ma julwongo ni coral reefs—wil ugoye ku nam. Ku cing cing ku cing cing, ngudu tero in i pii. Kabedo pare ma yo piny utiye battery, kama lum ma leng eca lwoko ku pii, man juyike ku yamu, ma sawa manok mukadho ubedu woko kud i ngom. Nen dul pa dhanu ma gineno pii keca.

Woth i adhura mi ceng yenga mi ceng yenga ma leng. Woth kud i Corlears Hook nitundo i Coenties Slip, man kud i keca, i Whitehall, yo malu. Ineno ango?—Juyike calu juwang ma ling i adhura, ucungu dhanu elifu ku elifu ma nen i nam. Moko ucungo ikum spiles; moko ubedo iwi wi yei; moko gineno wi yei ma wok kud i China; moko malu malu i rigging, calu kani gibe tembo ni neno nam maber. Ento ju eni zo gi dhanu mi ngom; pa nindu mi yenga ma jugwoko i lath man plaster—ma jutwio iwi kom, jutwio iwi kom, jutwio iwi kom. Dong eni utiye nenedi? Nyo podho macol uthum? Gitimo ang'o keni?

Ento nen! keni bino udul dhanu madwong, ma giwotho iwi pii, man nen calu gibecidho i pii. Tung! Piny moko mbe ma bibedo kud anyonga igi ento ng'om ma dit uloyu; bedo i the lum macol pa ot moko ma jugwoku i iye piny eca copo bedu ngo. Ungo. Umitu gi bed kago ku pii ma gicopo ngo ma gipodho i iye. Man gicungu keca—mayils mapol—leagues. Dhanu mi ngom zo, gi ai kud i yo man yo, yo man yo—yo malo, yo piny, yo piny, man yo reto. Ento keni gi zo gibedo karacelu. Wec ira, nyo tego pa magnetic pa sindo pa compass pa yei eca zo ucwalu gi keca?

Wang acel kendu. Wec nia itiye i ngom; i ngom moko ma malu pa nam. Woth yo moku ci ma imito, man apar ku acel ebiteri piny i got, man eweki keca inget pii i kulo. Tiye ku jok i iye. Wek dhanu ma paru migi mbe udok i paru pare ma thuc—cung iwi tyende, keth tyende, man ebi teri i pii, tek pii utiye i kabedu eca zo. Ka ibedo ku pii i lum madit mi Amerika, tem lembe maeni, ka karavan peri utiye ku japonji mi lembe mi kwo. Eyo, calu dhanu ceke ngeyo, meditation ku pii gibedo karacelu rondo ku rondo.

Ento eni jaradiri. Emito egoyi cal maber loyo, macol, ma ling, man ma nyayu anyonga i kind ngom mi Saco. Ango ma ubedo piny madit ma etiyo kudu? Kuca ucungu yen pare, acel acel ku cinge ma oling, calu nya hermit ku crucifix gibedo iiye; man keni ubedo lum pare, man keca ubedo lei pare; man malu kud i ot moko eca cidho iro ma nyayu nindo. I kind lum mabor, yamu wok ku yore ma nyawang, ma tuc i godi ma ucungo iwi got ma rangi migi bulu. Ento kadi bende cal ne ubedo ma nya wange, man kadi bende yen mi pine eni yengo yuyu pare calu ith wic pa jakwath eni, ento lembe eca zo ubedu nono, tek wang jakwath ne ubedu ka neno pii mi tango ma ubedu i wange. Cidhi i Prairies i Dwi mir Abusiel, kinde ma pi mayils madwong iwotho i kind Tiger-lilies—ango ma charm acel mitu?—Pi—pii moko ope keca! Ka Niagara ubedo cataract mi lum, icopo wotho mairo elifu acel pi nenu? Pirango ma jagor wer ma jacan mi Tennessee, ikare ma enwangu cing ario mi fedha, eparu ka nyo ewil ire koti, ma emito, nyo eketh sente pare i wotho mi wotho i Rockaway Beach? Pirango ma awobi moko ci ma kume yot ku tipo ma kume yot i igi, i saa moko kadi mukende gibedo ku lworo mi cidho i nam? Pirango i wotho peri mir acel calu jawoth, in giri iwinjo yeng yeng ma nyayu lworo, kinde ma jular juyero iri nia in ku yei peri dong wube woko kud i ngom? Pirango ma dhanu mi Persia macon gineno nam maleng? Pirango ma Jugiriki gimiye mungu mange, man umego pa Jove? Andha, lembe eni zo epe ku the lembe. Man podi the lembe ma pire tek pa Narcissus, ma pi en ecopo nyang ikum cal ma nyayu can man ma mwol ma eneno i pii, epodho i iye man etho. Ento cal marom eno, wan giwa waneno i kulo man nam zo. Etiye cal pa piny ma kwo ma dhanu copo mako ngo; man eni ubedo acaki pa lembe eni zo.

Kawoni, ka awacu niya atiye ku kura mi cidho i nam kare ku kare ka acaku bedo macol ikum wanga, man acaku bedo ku lworo ikum ombo para, amito ngo nya abed acidho i nam calu jawoth. Pi ni cidho calu jawoth umitu ibed ku sanduku, man sanduku ubedo bongo kende tek ibedo ku piny moko i iye. Mi mede, juruwotho gibedo ku two mi nam—gibedo ku lworo—gi nindo ngo dyewor—gibedo ngo kud anyonga lee, calu piny ma rwinyo;—ungo, acidho ngo calu jawotho; kadi, kadi nangu atiye ngati ma rwinyo, adok i nam calu Commodore, nyo Captain, nyo Cook. Aweko dwong pa tic ma kumeno ni ju ma gimaru gi. Pi thenge para, akwedo tic ceke maber, tembe, man can mi kit moko ci. Ebedo piny ma acopo timo ni gwoko kwo para, labongo gwoko yei, barques, brigs, schooners, man mange de. Man pi cidho calu jatedo,—kadi bende ayiyu nya etiye ku dwong pa jatedo i iye, jatedo ubedo calu jatel wi yei—ento, yo moko, nangu abedo kud anyonga mi tedo winy;—kadi bende ka dong jutedo, juyike ku moo maber, man juyike ku kadu maber, ngati moko mbe ma copo weco ku woro ma sagu, ku woro ma rac, winyo ma sagu ma amito. Ubedo kud i woro pa dhanu macon mi Misiri iwi ibis ma juwango man agura ma juwango i pii, ma ineno mumies pa lei eni i ot migi madit mi tedo ma julwongo ni pyramids.

Ungo, ka adok i nam, acidho calu jaring yei, i wang yei, adok piny i forecastle, malo keca nitundo i wi meli pa rwoth. Andha, gimaru lwongo ira lembe moku, man giketho abedo ka ngwec kud i spar ni spar, calu thwol i lum mi dwi mir abic. Man mir acel, lembe ma kumeno ubedu marac akeca. Emulo paru pa ngati pi woro, asaga ka i ai kud i pacu ma con i ngom, Van Rensselaers, nyo Randolphs, nyo Hardicanutes. Man makadhu ikum lembe ceke, tek podi ibe ketho cingi i tar-pot, ibedo ka timo tic calu japonji mi somo mi caru, ma weko awiya coo madongo ucungu iwi lworo peri. Lokruok ubedo lembe ma pire tek, ageno iri, niai kud i japonji mi somo ni jatel wi yei, man mitu rwom ma tek pa Seneca man Stoics kara ibed ku copo mi ngier man mi ciro. Ento kadi eni de rwinyo i kare.

Ango ma copo timere, ka ngati moko ma dit pa jatel wi nam ungolo ira nia agam jamb lwoko man acwal piny iwi yei? Lembakeca eca nyutho ango, ka jupimo, amito wacu, i pek mi lembanyong'a manyen? Iparu niya malaika madit Gabriel paru lembe moko marac ikum an, kum awinjo lembe pa ngati macon eca pio pio man ku woro? Nga ma epe opii? Wec ira lembe eni. Ber, dong, kadi bende judongo mi nam ma con gicopo ciko ira—kadi bende gicopo goyo ira man gi goyo ira, atiye ku mutoro mi ngeyo nya etiye maber; nia dhanu ceke utiye yo acel nyo mange ma jutiyo kudu i yo marom—kadi i nen pa piny nyo i nen pa piny, niwacu; man kumeno universal thump ukadhu, man cing dhanu ceke umaku ucwal cing jumange, man gibed ma cwinygi yom.

Bende, kare ku kare acidho i nam calu jaring yei, kum gi ketho yub mi culu ira pi peko para, ento gi culu ngo ju ma wotho ku yei kadi acel de ma awinjo pire. Ento, juruwotho gire re ma umitu gicul. Man tung tung utiye i ng'om ikind culu man bedo juculu. Tic mi culu ubedo lembe ma rac uloyu ma jukwo ario mi podho nying gi timo ikumwa. Ento bedo ma juculo ,—ango ma bipore kude? Tic mi adhura ma dhanu nwango kudu sente utiye ber mandha, ka waparu niya sente utiye thek pa peko mi ng'om, man nia ngati ma sente copo mondo i polo. Ah! kite ma wabedo kud anyonga ni weko kwo mwa ni tho!

I ajiki, nja adok i nam calu jaring yei, pi tic maber man yamu maleng pa fore-castle deck. Pi kite ma i ng'om eni, yamu ma wok kud i wi yei utiye madwong uloyu ma sagu yamu ma wok kud i yo ma yo piny (niwacu, tek i turo ngo cik pa Pythagore), kumeno pol kare Commodore ma ni i quarter-deck nwangu yamo pare i cinge mir arionde kud i bang ju ma giwotho i yei ma ni i forecastle. Ebe paru niya eyuye wang ma kwong; ento epe. I yore marom, dhanu mapol telo wi judongo migi i lembe mange madwong, i saa marom ma judongo giparu ngo ikume. Ento pirango ma uketho ingeye ma abedo ka ngiyo nam wang mapol calu jara tic mi yei, dong acopo ketho i wiya ni cidho i wotho mi maku rec; eni ubedo japolisi ma nen ungo pa Fates, ma bedo ku wang ikuma kare ku kare, man ma mako ira i mung, man eketho cwinya i yore moku ma dhanu copo dwoko ngo—ecopo dwoko lembe maber uloyu ngati moko ci. Man, labongo jiji, wotho para i wotho mi maku rec ma dit, ubedo thenge mi yub madit pa Providence ma jugoro con. Ebino calu wec ma nyanok man kende ikind wer madongo. Abedo ku paru niya dul pa cik eni ucikere ni bedu ku lembe ma kumeno:

“ Yero madit mi telowic mi ng'om mir Amerika. “WOTH MI WAHAL PA ISHMAEL MOKO. "LWINY MI RIMO I AFFGHANISTAN."

Kadok acopu ngo ni wacu pirango ma judongo pa stage, Fates, giketho an piny pi thenge ma rac mi wotho mi maku rec, ma jumoko juketho pi thenge ma ber i tragedies madongo, man thenge ma nok man ma yot i gentel comedies, man thenge ma jolly i farces—kadi bende acopo ngo ni wacu pirango ma eni andha; ento, kawoni ma apoi i kum lembe ceke, aparo ni acopo neno nyanok i lembe ma wok man paru ma jubedu ka nyuthu ira ku yore matung tung, uketho acaku timo tic ma atimo, man eketho abedo ku paru niya eca ubedo yero ma wok kud i paru para man ngolo kop ma poko kind dhanu.

Ma pire tek ikind lembe eni ubedu paru ma lee pa rec madit ma julwongo ni whale en gire. Ley ma rac man ma dhanu ungiyu ngo uketho cwinya mi ngeyo lembe zo. I ngeye nam macol man mabor kama enyayu iye cula pare; lembe ma copo wok ungo, ma julwongo ngo, ma rac pa rec ma julwongo ni whale; eni, ku piny maber pa Patagonian ma dhanu mapol gineno man ma giwinjo, ukonya ni ketho cwinya ikum paru para. Ku coo mukende, nyo, lembe ma kumeno copo bedu ngo lembe ma nyayu anyonga; ento pira kena, cwinya lith ku lworo ma nja pi piny mabor. Amaru wotho i nam ma jukwero, man podho i dhu nam ma rac. Ka aweko ngo gin maber, abedo nyap pio pio ni neno lworo, man acopo bedo asu kude—gicopo yio—pien ber ni bedo ku winjiri maber ku ju ma jutwio mi kabedu ma ngati ubedo i iye.

Pi the lembe eni, dong, wotho mi mako rec ma dit ubedo ber; dhugola madit mi ng'om mi lewic ugam oyabere, man i paru ma nyayu anyonga ma uketho abedo ku paru para, ario ku ario ugam umondo i cwinya, wotho ma rwinyo pa rec ma dit, man, ikindgi zo, ngati acel ma dit ma ucungo iwi got, calu got macol i polo.





CHAPTER 2. The Carpet-Bag.

Abolo kor bongu acel nyo aryo i sanduku para macon, aketo i the cinga, man acaku cidho i Cape Horn man Pacific. Ka aweko adhura maber mi Manhatto macon, awok i New Bedford. Ubedo dyewor mi ceng kas acel mi dwi mir apar wi ario. Cwinya utur lee ikare ma anwango nia jeni ma nok mi Nantucket udaru kadhu, man nia yore moko ope mi tuc kama eca, nitundu ceng kasi acel ma lubo.

Calu ju mapol ma fodi gitie aradu ma gibemito nwangu can man peko mi mako rec madongo gicungo i New Bedford, man gicaku wotho migi, ecopu bedu ni an, pi acel, nangu ango paru ngo mi timo kumeno. Pi paru para ubedu mi wotho i yei mi Nantucket, kum ubedu ku piny moko maber man ma nya wang ikum piny moko ci ma mako cula macon, ma unyayu anyonga i iya. Kadok nwang’u New Bedford ubedo ka ketho cing’e i kum tic mi ng’iewo rec ma julwong’o whale, man kadok nwang’u i lembe maeni Nantucket ma con ma jacan dong’ udong’ i ng’eye, ento Nantucket ubedo kabedo pare ma pire tek ma kwong’a—Tire mi Carthage maeni;—kabedo ma rec ma kwong’a ma tho mi Amerika ubedo i ie. Kaka mange ento kud i Nantucket ma ju ma gikwo i nam, Red-Men, gicaku wok ku yei pi lubo tok Leviathan? Man kani ento kud i Nantucket, bende, ma meli ma nok ma kwong ma jamutoro eca uwok, ma upong ku kidi ma jucwalu kud i ngom mange—eca ma lembe ubetimere—pi ni bolo i kum rec ma julwongo ni whales, pi ni ngeyo kani ma gicokiri ni ketho harpoon kud i bowsprit?

Kawoni dong atiye ku dyewor acel, ceng acel, man dyewor mange mukende ma ulubo i wanga i New Bedford, mapodi acopo cidho i dhu nam, edoko lembe ma nyayu lworo kama acopo camu man nindu i iye. Ubedo dyewor ma nen nya epe ku jiji, ungo, dyewor macol man ma rac, ma ngic man ma kud anyonga. Angeyu ngati moko ope ikabedu ne. Ku lworo ma lee, agam awotho i jeba para, man akelo sente manok kende mi mola ma kwar,—Ento, kaka moko ci ma icidho i iye, Ishmael, awacu i iya gira, ma acungu i dye yo macol ma tingo osau para, man aporo mudho ma yo malu ku mudho ma yo piny—kabedo moku ci i diewor peri, icopo daru nindo para, Ishmael ma amaro, bed ma ingeyo wel piny, man kud ibed ngati ma pire tek.

Ku ngwec ma ucungo, awotho i ngudu, man akadhu iwi lanyuth pa “The Crossed Harpoons”—ento nen ni piny ma wel pare tek man ma kud anyonga keca. I ngeye, kud i dirisa makwar ma ler pa "Sword-Fish Inn," ler ma lyeth uwok, ma nen calu edaru loko pee man pee ma ucungo i wang ot, pi kama moko ci pee ma ucungo ubedo inches apar i yo ma tek, ma nen calu asphaltic,--ebedo ma olo ira, ka agoyo tyenda i kum got, pi tic ma tek tic ma mbe ku kisa, tyend wara para ubedu i lembe marac mandha. Bei pare tek man ma kud anyonga, kendu aparu, acungu pi kare manok mi nenu lero ma lee i ngudu, man awinjo dwand glasi ma ucungo iiye. Ento mediri, Ishmael, awacu i ajikine; iwinjo ngo? wok cen kud i wang dhugola; wara peri ma juyubu ucungo yo. Dong adok. An dong alubo yo ma tero an i pii, kum keca, m'umbe jiji, ubedu kabedo ma wel migi yot, ka epe ot ma leng.

Yore macol ma kumeno! dul macol, ot ungo, ku cing ario zoo, man keca ku keca tara, calu tara ma wotho i liel. I sawa maeni mi diewor, mi ceng mir ajiki mi yenga, thenge mi adhura eca ubedo dhanu ma oling. Ento kawoni abino i mac ma lyel ma wok kud i ot moko ma piny, ma lac, ma dhugola pare ucungu oyabere. Ebedo ku nen ma rwinyo, calu nya juyike pi tic pa lwak; dong, ikare ma amondo, piny mir acel ma atimo ubedu ni podho iwi ash-box i baraza. Ha! aparu, ha, calu ma piny ma ucungo iwi polo ubemito etho, kodi buru eni uai kud i adhura ma jucwinyo, Gomorrah? Ento "The Crossed Harpoons," man "The Sword-Fish?"—eni, eca ucikere ni bedu lanyuth pa "The Trap." Ento, atingo wiya man awinjo dwand ma tek i ot, adham man ayabu dhugola mir aryo, ma iye.

Nen calu Parliament madit pa ju ma rangi migi col ubedo i Tophet. Wang dhanu macol dak acel ulokore i rwom migi ni neno; man ingeye, malaika macol pa tho ubedu ka goyo buku iwi kabedo mi lega. Ubedo kanisa pa ju macol; man lembe pa jakwenda ubedo ikum mudho macol, man ywak man ywak man nyayo lak keca. Ha, Ishmael, awacu, adok cen, anyonga ma rac i lanyuth pa 'The Trap!'

Ka wabewothu anyim, awok i lero moko ma nya yot ungo bor ku dog nam, man awinjo dwand moko ma ling iwi yamu; man eneno malu, eneno lanyuth ma ucungo iwi dhugola ku cal matar iwiye, ma nyutho pii mabor ma ucungo iwi pii, man wec maeni piny—“The Spouter Inn:—Peter Coffin.”

Sanduku?—Spouter?—Ebedo rac in lembe meno, aparu. Ento etiye nying ma dhanu mapol ungiyo i Nantucket, giwacu, man aparu nia Peter eni utiye jadwong kud i ngom mange. Calu ler ne nen nya nya nya, man kabedo ne, pi kare ne, nen ling, man ot ma nok ma juyubu ku yen ne nen calu juwodhe kud i kabedo moko ma juwango, man calu lanyuth ma ucungo iwi cinge ubedo ku dwand ma tek, aparu niya eni re ma ubedo kabedo maber pi kabedo ma yot, kawa man kawa maber loyo.

Ubedo kabedo moko ma nen ungo—ot macon ma juketho i iye gable, thenge acel ubedo palsied, man ucungo piny ku can. Ecungu iwi dhu kor ot macol, kama yamu ma tek ma julwongo ni Euroclydon ubedo ka koko marac uloyu ikum meli pa Paul ma jacan. Euroclydon, kadi kudu, utiye zephyr maber mandha ni ngati moko ci ma ni i ot, ku tyende iwi hob ma ling ling ma ube tedo pi kavutu. "I ngolo kop pa yamu ma tek ma julwongo ni Euroclydon," ewacu jagor moko macon-ma tic pare atiye ku kopi acel kende ma podi utiye--"etimo tung tung madit, kadi ineno woko kud i wang dirisa ma ngic utiye woko, nyo kadi ineno kud i wang dirisa ma epe ku cing, ma ngic man tho utiye i thenge ario zoo ubedo glazier acel kende. Andha, aparu, calu ma lembe eni wok i wiya—waraga macol macon, iparu maber. Eyo, wang eni utiye dirisa, man koma eni utiye ot. Lembe mi can nenedi nia gi juku ngo cinge man cinge, man gi ketho lum manok keca ku keca. Ento kawoni dong ukadhu kare mi timo aloka loka moku ci. Wi ngom uthum; copestone utiye iwi, man chips juwodhe woko oro miliyon acel mukadho. Lazarus ma jacan keca, ubeweco ku lak pare ikum kidi ma juketho pi pilo pare, man ebewotho ku yeng cinge ku yeng yeng pare, ecopu umu ithe ario ku bongo, man eketho lum i dhoge, ento eno copo ketho ngo Euroclydon ma tek. Euroclydon! Dives ma muti uwacu, i bongo pare makwar—(ebedo ku makwar i ngeye) pooh, pooh! Ubedo dyewor maber man ma ngic; kite ma Orion rieny kudu; ler ma yo malu nen ango! Wek giwec ikum kite ma piny migi nen kudu i kare mi cwir ma rii pi kare malac; miyo ira gum mi timo summer para ku makaa para gira.

Ento Lazaru paru ango? Ecopu lyero cinge macol ku tingo gi malu ikum ler madit mi yo malu? Nyo Lazarus copo bedu i Sumatra ma sagu keni? Nyo ecopo ketho piny mabor ku bor pa equator; eyo, wu mungu! cidhi piny i vur mi mac kende, pi gwoko ngico eni?

Kawoni, nia Lazarus ubedo iwi kidi ma ucungo i wang dhugola pa Dives, eni ber uloyu ma sagu nia iceberg ubedo i Moluccas acel. Ento Dives en gire, en de ekwo calu Czar i ot ma dit ma juyubu ku yuyu ma ngic, man calu jaratela mi kanisa mi bedo moth, emadhu pii wang ma ngic pa awiya ma junyodo gi utho kende.

Ento kawoni dong wabeweco ngo, wabecidho i a-whaling, man lembe madwong podi ubebinu. Wek wawodh pee kud i tyendwa ma ngic, man wanenu kabedo ango ma "Spouter" eni copo bedu.





CHAPTER 3. The Spouter-Inn.

Ka imondo i Spouter-Inn, inwango giri i dhugola ma lac, ma piny, ma ucungo ku wainscots ma con, ma poyo wic acel ikin bulwarks pa tic moko ma con. I thenge acel, cal moko ma dit ma jugoro ku moo ma juyiko maber akeca, man ma juyiko ku moo, ma ineno ku rangi ma tung tung, ubedu kende kende ku somo maber man wotho i iye, man penjo judhugola, ma icopo nwangu nyang ikum tic pare. Dhanu mapol ma nen ungo, ma i acaki icopo paru niya jabodho moko ma nyathin, i rundi pa New England hags, utemo nyuthu lembe ma rac. Ento ku paru madwong man ma iye tek, man paru ma judwoko wang mapol, man asaga ku yabo wang dirisa ma nok yo ngeye pa dhugola, i ajikine iwok i paru niya paru ma kumeno, kadi nang epe, copo bedu ngo ma rwinyo.

Ento gin ma uketho ibedo ku jiji man uketho ibedo ku jiji ubedo piny moko ma bor, ma nyap, ma nen maleng, macol ma ucungo i dye cal iwi rangi adek macol, macol, ma ucungu iwi thobi ma nyinge epe. Cal macol, ma pii pii, ma pii pii mandha, ma romo nyayu lworo i dhanu. Ento ubedu ku kit piny moko ma rwinyo, ma rwinyo ungo, ma rwinyo ngo ikume ma uketho ibedo ling ikume, nitundu ikare ma itimo lembariba peri giri ni ngeyo ango ma cal maber eno thee. Pol kare paru moko ma ber, ento, marac, ma nyayu ndra copo wok iri.—Eni nam macol i yamu ma tek mi diewor.—Ebedo lwiny ma rwinyo pa piny angwen ma pire tek.—Ebedo lum ma jucwinyo.—Ebedo kabedo mi ngico ma tek.—Ebedo turo pa pii ma ngic mi Time. Agam anwango nya jatel wi harpooneer eni, ikind wotho pare mabor, umaku ebed enwango lembe marom. Man ango, aparu, i ngei lembe zo! Etiye woko pare kende; dhanu copo bedo m'umbe ndra i kit adila moko ci. Ento dong, ango ma wacikara ni timo ku rangi pare ma nen ungo, thenge meno, amito wacu, ma evuto, man ma epe ku rangi pa tattoo. Mi bedu andha, ecopu bedu piny moko kende ento bongo maber mi rangi macol; ento awinjo ngo pi ceng ma lyet ma loko ngati ma tar doko jal ma nya aceng. Ento, nangu abedo ngo i nam mi South Seas; man nyalo bedu ceng keca ukelo adwogi maber ikum adila. Kawoni, podi paru eni zo ubedu ka kadhu kud i iya calu lyethu, jatel wi harpooneer eni uneno ngo ira kadi nyanok de. Ento, ingey bedo ku peko moku mi yabo osau pare, ecaku timo lembe marac i iye, man kawoni ewodho tomahawk moko, man osau ma juyubu ku adila pa seal ku yuk wic. Eketho piny eni iwi sanduku macon i dye ot, egam etingo wiye pa New Zealand—piny ma rac akeca—man ebolo piny i sanduku. Egam ewotho koto pare—koto manyen pa beaver—kani abino ka wer ku lworo manyen. Ubedo ungo ku yuk wic iwi wiye—ma wacopo weco pire—piny moko mbe ento yuk wic ma nok ma ucungo iwi wiye. Wiye macol macol kawoni nen iwi ngom zoo calu wic macol. Tek ngati ma angeyo ongo ucungu ngo ikindwa ku dhugola, nangu awok woko pio pio ma sagu ma awok kudi dhugola.

Kadi bende ubedu kumeno, aparu nia piny moku copo wok kud i wang dirisa, ento ubedu wi ot mir aryo ngeye. An abedo ngo jalworo, ento ango ma amito atim ikum jaracik ma rangi pa rangi macol eni ukadhu ikum nyang para. Ng'eyo ngo ubedo wego pa lworo, man bedo ma cwinya unyai man ma nyang ikum ngati ma angeyo ongo, aloko nia kawoni dong alwore lee dit calu jajok en gire ma umondo i ot para i dyewor. I andha, lworo ubedu i kume lee dit ma uketho abedo ngo ku kero mi weco kude, man mi penjo lagam maber ikum gin ma nen ni epe i iye.

Ikinde marom, emediri ku tic mi wodhu bongo, man i ajiki enyutho kore man cinge. Kaka akwo, thenge ma umo kume eni juyike ku square marom ku wange; ngeye, de, ubedu iwi kabedo macol marom; nen calu ebedo i lwiny mi oro pyer adek, man ewok kud i iye ku kor bongu ma juketho i iye plaster. Man de, tyende ubedo ku lanyuth, calu nya dul pa ungwal ma rangi pa lum macol ubedo ka ringo iwi lum ma nyathin. Dong eca nen maleng niya ecibedo ngati moko ma rac nyo ngati mange ma jucwalo iwi yei ma dit i nam ma yo piny, man epodho i ngom pa Jukristu eni. Abedo ka yeng yeng ka aparu pire. Jalwor wic de—copu bedu wic pa umego pare gire. Ecopu bedu ku miti ikum para—polo! nen tomahawk eca!

Ento kare mi lworo ubedu pee, pi kawoni jajok ne utimo lembe moku ma unyayu wiya, man uketho ageno nia andha ecopo bedu jadhum. Ecidho i grego pare ma pek, nyo wrapall, nyo dreadnaught, ma con ebed eketho iwi kom, enyayu i cinge, man ewok ku cal moko ma nen ungo ma nen ungo ma nen ungo i ngeye, man rangi pa nyathin mi Congo ma oro pare adek. Ka apoi iwi wic ma juyiko, i acaki aparu niya manikin macol eni ubedo nyathin mandha ma jugwoko i yo marom. Ento nineno nya ebedo ngo ma nyap, man nya ebedo maleng calu ebony ma juyubu maber, adham nia ecibedo piny moko ci ento cal ma juyubu ku yen, ma andha ebedo. Pi kare eni, jajok ne ucidho malu i kabedo mi mac ma utu, man ewotho mac ma jugoro ku karatasi, eketho cal ma nok ma ucungo iwi ngeye, calu tenpin, ikind andirons. Jambs mi chimney man bricks ceke ma iiye ubedo ku lum madwong, uketho aparu nia kabedo mi mac eni ubedo kabedo ma nok maber pa Congo idol pare.

Kawoni aloko wanga matek ikum cal ma ungwe, awinjo marac ikum kare eni—pi neno ango ma ulubo. Mir acel etingo cinge ma romo ario kud i jeba pare mi grego, man eketho gi maber i wang cal; man eketho biscuit moko iwi meli man eketho mac kud i tara, ewango shavings ni mac mi lam. Kawoni, ingey mako piny mapol i mac, man wodhu cinge woko pio pio (ma nen calu ebedo ka wango gi marac), i ajikine edaru wodhu biscuit; man eweko lyethu man buru nyanok, etimo mic maber ni nyathin macol. Ento jajok ma nok ne nen ungo nya emaru wil ma kumeno; eloko ngo dhoge. Lembe ma nyayu lworo eni zo ubedu ku dwand ma dhanu ungiyo ngo kud i bang ju ma woro Mungu, ma nen calu ebe lega i wer nyoke ebe wer psalmody moko mi pagan nyo wer mange, ma wange ubedo ka yeng i yo ma rwinyo ngo. I ngei nego mac, etingo ayi gin m'acwia ne malu m'umbe timo lembe moko, man edwoko kendo i jeba pare mi grego maber akeca calu ngati m'utimo tuko ma utingo woodcock ma tho.

Lembe eni zo umedire lworo para, man ninene kawoni enyutho lanyuth ma tek mi daru tic pare mi kuloka, man epodho i kavutu ku an, aparu niya eca ubedo kare, kawoni nyo podi ungo, ma podi ler uthum ungo, mi turo timo ma nangu abedo i iye pi kare malac.

Ento kare ma abedo ka paru ikum ango ma abewecu, ubedu kare ma rac. Etingo tomahawk pare kud iwi meza, eneno wiye pi kare manok, man edham etingo i mac, ku dhoge i cinge, ewodho polo madit pa iro pa taba. I saa ma lubo, ler ne utho, man jacam dhanu ma kwiny, tomahawk ikind lake, upoto i kavutu ku an. Awero wer, nangu acopo timo ngo kawoni; man ecaku winjo ira.

Ka aloko lembe moku, angeyu ngo ango, aloko cen kud i bange ikum ot, man akwaye, ngati moko ci nyo ango ma ecopu bedu, ebed ling, man wek a ay malu man awang tara kendu. Ento dwokowang lembe pare ma nya wange uketho cwinya yom pio pio nia engeyo ngo the lembe para.

"Nga-e debel in?"—ewacu i ajikine—"icopo weco ngo, dam-an, anego-e." Man wecu kumeno tomahawk ma ler ucaku wok ikuma i mudho.

“Won ot, pi bero pa Mungu, Peter Coffin!” akok. "Won ot! Nen! Sanduku! Malaika! laru an!"

"Wec-e! wec-ee ira anga-ee be, nyo dam-a, anego-e!" kendo jal ma camu dhanu ugam ukok, ma tomahawk pare ma rac uketho buru pa taba ma lyet i koma nitundo ma aparu nia bongo para bino wang. Ento foc ni polo, i saa maeca won ot umondo i ot ku ler ku cinge, man awok kudi kavutu aringo i bange.

"Kud ilwor kawoni," ewacu, ku ngier kendu, "Queequeg keni copo nyayu peko ikum yuk wic peri."

"Wek ngyeru peri," akok, "man pirango ma iwecu ira ngo niya jadubo ma jamutoro eca ubedo jadubo?"

"Aparu niya ing'eyo;—abe awecu iri ngo, ebedo jara lworo wic i adhura?—ento lok kendo man icid nindo. Queequeg, nen kany—i sabbee an, an sabbee—in jadhum eni unindu—in sabbee?”

"A sabbee madwong"- Queequeg uwacu, ma eyubu pipe pare man ebedo iwi kavutu.

"Imond i iye," emedu, enyutho ira ku tomahawk pare, man ebolo bongo ne yo acel. Etimo eni i yore maber kende ngo ento i yo maber man mi berocwiny. Acungu ka nene pi kare manok. Pi cal pare zo, ebedo ngati ma leng, ma nen maleng man ma camu dhanu. Ango ma ubedo lembe ma nyayu lworo ma abedo ka timo, aparu i iya gira—ng'atu ne ubedo dhanu calu an: etiye ku tyen lok madwong mi lworo ira, calu an bende umitu abed lworo i kume. Ber nindu ku ngati ma camu dhanu ma madhu kongo ma sagu jakristu ma mer.

"Won ot," awacu, "wac ire eketh tomahawk pare keca, nyo pipe, nyo gin moko ci ma ilwonge; wac ire ewek madhu taba, i lembe ma nok, man abidok kude. Ento amito ngo ni bedu ku jadhum ma madhu taba i kavutu kuda. Etiye rac. Bende, atie ku insurance ungo."

Maeni ma juweco ni Queequeg, egam eyio, man edwoko cinge kendo ku mwolo nia adok i kavutu—eloko cinge i thenge acel calu ma ewacu—“Abimulo tyendi peri acel de ngo.”

"Utwor maber, won ot," awacu, "icopo cidhu."

Adok cen, man anindo ngo maber uloyu i kwo para.
