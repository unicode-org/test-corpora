Calu, m'utho man m'uwinjo ngo, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale udham ucungo anyim, calu ku tego pa ngwec pare pi ni kwanyu thwol ma ucungo i kume; calu ma wabenyayu ret ma tar i nam, ku thenge ceke ma wabe lworo ka wabe ringo, ku lei ma wic ma ube ringo ikumwa; yei mwa ma jukuboth ubedo calu yei ma jukubo ku ice-isles i yamo madit, man ubetimo kero niwotho ikind yore migi ma nya wange tek, ma engeyo ngo nia ecopo bedu ma juketho i iye man jutur.

Ento m'umbe lworo, Queequeg utelo wiwa maber; kawoni wabe weko lei moko ma rac eni iwi yo mwa ma ukadhu; kawoni wabecidho cen kud i iye, ma lum pare madongo ucungo iwi wiye, ma saa ceke, Starbuck ucungu iwi thwol, ku lance i cinge, ma ucwalu kud i yo mwa whales moko ci ma ecopu tuc ku darts ma nok, kum kare ubedu pee mi timo whales ma bor. Yie ma dit ma julwongo ni whaleboats tingo piny moko ma nyayu lworo, ma jular juyubu ku Nantucket Indians, ma julwongo ni druggs. Ento sperm whales ungiyure ngo kubang ceng; podi icopo, dong, umitu ineg dhanu ceke ma icopo. Man tek icopo nego gi zo wang acel, umitu iket gi iwi cing gi, kara gicopu nego gi i kare ma imito. Pieno, i saa ma kumeno yath, bino i penji. Yey mwa ubedo ku piny adek. Mir acel ku mir aryo juwodho gi maber, man waneno rec ma julwongo ni whales ube ringo ku lworo, ma ucungo iwi kero madit pa towing drugg. Gibedu matek calu ju ma timo lembe marac ku nyor man mupira. Ento ikare ma ebolo mir adek, i timo mi bolo iwi nam, egam epodho piny acel mi kombe mi yei, man i saa moko ewotho woko man etere cen, man ebolo jaring yei piny i the yei ma kombe ne ucwale kud i there. I thenge ario zoo nam umondo i kum yen ma ret, ento wapongo i sanduku ario nyo adek man bongo, man wajuku pii ma mol pi kare manok.

Ubedo lembe ma yot ungo ni tiyo ku yath ma mero dhanu, tek epe nia ka wabecidho i kind dul pa lei, yo pa rec ma julwongo ni whale udoko nok akeca; man de, nia ka wabecidho anyim man mabor ku kabedo mi lwiny, peko ma rac ne nen ni ube rwinyo. Pieno, ka i ajikine, harpoon ma ucungo wok woko, man rec ma julwongo ni towing whale ugam unyai; i ngeye, ku tego ma rwinyo pa rwom pare mi poko, wawotho ikind rec ario ma julwongo ni whales i iye pa nam, calu ve kud i got moku wapodho i nam ma ling. Kae yamu ma tek i kind lum ma dit ma julwongo ni whales, juwinjo ento juwinjo ngo. I kind ng'om maeni, nam ubedo ku ng'om ma leng' ma calu satin, ma julwong'o sleek, ma wok kud i pii ma nok ma rec ma julwong'o whale ubolo woko i kare ma ebedo ling'. Eyo, kawoni dong wabedu i kwiocwiny ma giwacu nia etiye i cwiny pa lembe ceke. Man podi i bor ma nen ungo, waneno koko pa dul ma woko, man waneno dul pa rec ma julwongo ni whales, abora nyo apar i acel acel, ma ubewothu nyap nyap, calu dul pa agura mapol i ring; man gibedo kago ku cing gi, ma jaring mutukari mi Titanic copo bedu yot ni ukadhu ikum ju ma ikin gi, man gicopo cidho iwi ngeye. Ni kadhu pa dul pa rec ma julwongo ni whales, ma ucungo ikum embayed axis pa rombo, yore moko ope ma wacopo ringo kudu. Umitu wabed ma wanenu pi tur pa cel ma kwo ma ucungo iwa; cel ma ugam ujolo wa kende pi juku wa. Ka wabedo i dye nam, wabedu ka limowa ku dhyeng ma nok man nyithindho; mon ku awiya pa rwoth maeni.

Kawoni, ka iketo i iye kare ma lac ikind dul ma woko ma lokore, man ka imedo kabedo ma ni ikin pods matung tung i kind dul eca, kabedo ceke i kare eni, ma dhanu ceke ujolo, umaku obed ku square miles ario nyo adek. I yore moku ci—kadi bende lembe ma kumeno i saa ma kumeno copo bedu mi ndra—jucopo nwangu lembe ma wok kud i yei mwa ma piny ma nen calu ube tuko malu kud i dhu kor polo. Aloko pi lembe eni, kum, calu nya dhyeng ku nyithind dhiang jubedu ka ketho gi i ot ma iye; man calu nya rwom ma lac pa lei ne uketho gi ngeyo ngo the lembe ma uketho gicungo; nyo, copu bedu, bedu nyathin, ma ngeyo ngo, man ma timo dubo man ma ngeyo ngo; kadi nangu ecopu bedu, rec ma nok eni—ma kawoni man i ngeye ma gibecidho i yei mwa ma oling kud i dhu nam—ginyutho lworo man gen ma lee, nyo lworo ma podi unyai ma ubedu tek ni bedo kud anyonga ikume. Calu gwok mi ot, gibino ka ngiyo wa, nitundo i gunwales mwa, man gimulo gi; nitundu nen calu jok moko uketho gi doko lei. Queequeg ugoyo wigi; Starbuck ugoyo ngeye ku pala pare; ento lworo pa adwogi ne, pi kare ne ucungo ikume.

Ento piny mabor ku ng'om maber eni iwi ngom, ng'om mange man ma podi wangeyo ungo urombo ku wangwa ka waneno yo piny. Pi, ma juketho i pii, nen pa min ot pa rec ma julwongo ni whales, man ju ma nen calu girth migi ma dit ma nen calu gi doko min ot. Nam, calu ma alar awacu, ubedu i thenge ma thuc man ma nen maleng; man calu awiya ma fodi gitiye nyathin ma gibe cak cak, gibe neno cen kud i cak, calu ma gikwo kwo ario matung tung i kare meno; man podi podi wabe nwangu cam ma tho, bed podi wabe camu piny moko ma nen ungo iwi ngom;—kadi bende awiya pa rec ma julwongo ni whales eni nen calu gineno malu iwa, ento ungo iwa, calu ka wabedo kende kende Gulfweed ma nok i wang gi ma fodi junyolu ngo. Ka giwotho iwi ngete, min ot de nen calu gibeneno wa ling. Ngati acel ikin awiya ma fodi gitiye aradu, ma nen calu ecaku ceng acel kende, copo bedu futi apar wi angwen i bor, man fut abusiel i lac. Ebedo ku lworo manok; kadi nang edoko nen ni piny ma nok ento edoko maber kud i kabedo ma rac ma ebedo i iye i reticule pa min ot; kama, yibe ku wiye, man zo ayika pi kare mi ngico ma tok cen, rec ma julwongo ni whale ma podi junyolu ngo ubedo m'utur calu thwol pa Tartar. Side-fins ma yot, man palms pa flukes pare, podi utingo nen pa it pa nyathin ma wok kud i ngom mawoko.

“Lain! lain!” Queequeg owacu, ma eneno wi gunwale; "en pio pio! en pio pio!—Nga ma ucungo ire! Nga ma ugoyo?—Rec aryo; acel madit, acel ma nok!"

“Ango ma nyayu can iri, ngati?” Starbuck owacu.

"Nen-e kan," Queequeg uwacu, ma enyutho piny.

Calu ma rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale, ma kud i tub utingo thwol ma romo fathoms miya mapol; calu, ingey winjo dwand ma thuc, epodho malu kendu, man enyutho curling line ma jukuboth ma ucungo malu man ucungo iwi polo; dong kawoni, Starbuck uneno dul ma bor pa ic pa Madame Leviathan, ma nyathin ne nen calu podi jutwio i dam pare. Ebedo nya yot ungo i ngwec ma nyai, dul maeni, ku thenge pa min ot ma gony, edoko mako ku hempen acel, kara nyathin ne ucungo. Moko kuom lembe ma pire tek pa nam nen calu jukoro iwa i pii ma leng eni. Waneno Leviathan amours ma nyathin i nam ma thuc.*

*Sperm whale, calu rec mange mi Leviathan, ento upore ku rec mange mapol, nyodo ma rwinyo i kare ceke; ingey nyodo ma copo bedu dwi abungwen, ma nyayu nyodo acel acel; kadi bende i lembe moko manok ma jung'eyo ma junyolo Esau ku Yakobo:—lembe ma jutimo i kare mi nyolo cak ku cak ario, ma juketho i yo ma nyayu anyonga, acel acel i thenge acel acel mi cak; ento thwon gire ucungo malo kud i iye. Ka piny ma pigi tek eni i rec ma julwongo ni nursing whale jupoko ku pala pa jadwar, cak man rimo pa min ot loko rangi pa nam pi rods. Cak ne leng man leng; dhanu udaru temo; ecopu bedu maber ku strawberries. Ka gipong ku woro ikindgi, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whales foc hominum .

Man kumeno, kadi bende juguro gi ku dul ma tung tung mi lworo man lworo, lei maeni ma dhanu copo nyang kudu ngo ma gini i dyere gi bedo agonya man lworo ungo i lembe mi kuc; ee, wabedo kud anyonga man kud anyonga. Ento kadok kumeno de, i kind yamo ma tek mi Atlantic mi kwo para, nyo an gira fodi asu abedo i kind dhanu ma ling’ling’; man podi piny ma pek pa can ma rwinyo ubewothu i koma, i thenge ma piny man i thenge ma piny keca podi abedo ka lwoko cwinya i anyonga ma rondo ku rondo.

Ikinde marom, ma wabedo ku lworo, lembe ma nen ungo ma nen kama bor unyutho tic pa yei mange, ma podi gibetimo tic mi mio yath ni rec ma julwongo ni whales i wang nam; nyo copo bedu ni mediri ku lwiny i kind dul mir acel, kama kabedo madwong man kabedo moko maber mi ling eca juyike igi. Ento neno pa rec ma julwongo ni whales ma juyiko ku yath ma mero dhanu kawoni man i ngeye ma gibe ringo ku wang gi iwi dul, ubedu piny moko ope ma nen ungo i wangwa. Saa moko etiye kura ka jube ringo pio pio ni rec ma dit ma tek man ma ling, ni yenyo yore mi turo tyende, calu ma juweco pire, ku turo nyo turo ler madit mi yibe. Jutimo ku darting ku cutting-spade ma cinge nyanok, ma juketho i iye thung pi dwoko cen kendu. Rec moko ma julwongo ni whale ma ret (calu ma waponjo i ngeye) i thenge eni, ento epe maber, calu ma nen, edaru wok kud i yei, etingo kude nusu pa cinge; man i can madit mi ret, kawoni ebedo ka ringo ikind ju ma giwotho calu Arnold ma ubedo kende kende, i lwiny mi Saratoga, ma etingo lworo kama moku ci ma ecidho i iye.

Ento lith calu ret pa rec ma dit, man nen ma nyayu lworo, yore moku ci; ento lworo ma ukoc ma enen ni eketho cwiny dhanu mukende, ubedu pi the lembe moko ma i acaki bor pa dhanu eca unyayu ngo iwa. Ento i ngeye wa nwango nia ku acel ikin ret ma dhanu copo paru ngo i tic mi mako rec, rec ma julwongo ni whale eni ugam ucungo i cing harpoon-line ma etingo; en de eringo ku pala ma i iye; man podi thum pa thung ma juketho i kum jam lwiny ne, ugam ucungo iwi cinge ma ucungo iwi yebe, pala ne kende ugam uwok woko kud i kume. Pieno, edaru nyayu can i iye, kawoni ebed ewotho i pii, ebed ewotho ku yebe pare ma nyap, man ebed ebolo pala ma tek i kume, man ebed enyayu ret man ebenego juwadi.

Piny maber eni nen calu epoi wi lei ceke kud i lworo migi ma ucungo. Mir acel, rec ma julwongo ni whales ma ucungo i dhu nam mwa ucaku pong nyanok, man gipodho ikumgi gigi, calu jutingo gi ku nusu pa nam mabor; man nam ne gire ucaku yeng man upong; ot mi nyom man ot mi nyom ma pii pii utho; i orbit mapol man mapol ma nyai, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whales ma ni i circle ma dit ucaku dhuny i dul ma dit. Eyo, kwiocwiny ma rii ubedu ka wok. Dwol ma piny uwinjire pio pio; man i ngeye calu pii ma dit ma julwongo ni block-ice kinde ma kulo madit Hudson utur i kare mi ceng yenga, dul pa rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whales bino ka podho iwi dyere migi, calu ma gicokiri i got acel. I ngeye Starbuck ku Queequeg giloko kabedo; Starbuck utingo ngeye.

“Oars! Oars!” ewacu matek, maku yei—“maku yei peri, man mako tipo peri, kawoni! Mungu para, coo, cung! Wuye woko, in Queequeg—rec madit ma nuti!—goye! Cung—cungu malu, man bed kumeno! Spring, coo—ywayu, coo; kud ipar ikum ngeye migi—scrape gi!

Yey ne dong ucungo ikind yei ario madongo macol, ma weko Dardanelles ma nyap ikind yei migi mabor. Ento ku kero ma lee, i ajikine wamondo i dhugola ma rii pi kare manok; man eweko yo pio pio, man i sawa marom ebedo ka neno yo mange. I ngei ngwec madwong ma rom ku yuk wic, i ajikine wamondo pio pio i gin ma ubedu acel ikin dul ma woko, ento kawoni jukadho ku rec ma julwongo ni random whales, ma gi zo gi timo kabedo acel. Lar maber eni juwilo ku wel ma lapiny pi rwenyo kofi pa Queequeg, ma, ikare ma ucungu iwi yei pi goyo rec ma rwinyo, koto pare ugam utingo woko kud i wiye pi yamo ma wok kud i cinge.

Lembe ma nyayu lwiny man ma nyayu lworo calu lembe ma dhanu zo timo kawoni, edoko lembe ma nen calu lembe ma rwinyore; pi bedo karacelu i ajikine i dul acel ma pek, gi dwoko ngwec migi manyen ku kero ma lee. Lubu tok lembe mukende ubedu piny ma kony epe; ento yei ne podi ucungu iwi yei migi ni kwalo rec ma julwongo ni drug whales ma copo bedu ma jubolo yo ngeye, man bende ni gwoko acel ma Flask unego man eweko. Waif ubedo yen ma juketho i iye pennoned, ma ario nyo adek tingo ku yei acel acel; man ma, ka lei moko nuti, ju ketho gi i kum rec ma tho, pi nyuthu kabedu pare iwi nam, man bende pi lanyuth pa bedo ku piny eca, tek yei pa yei mange bino ceng.

Adwogi pa dwoko piny eni ubedo lanyuth pa wec ma ber ma juwacu i Fishery,—ka whales madwong rec nok. Ikind rec ma julwongo ni whales, acel kende re ma jumake. Ju mukende gi timo kero mi ringo pi kare manok, ento kende kende ni tero gi, calu ma wabineno i ngeye, ku yei mange ma epe ku Pequod.





CHAPTER 88. Sukul man judongo mi sukul.

Wi lembe mukadho umiyu lapor pa dul madit pa Sperm Whales, man de juweco ikum the lembe ma nyayu dul pa Sperm Whales.

Kawoni, kadi nang dhanu madongo ma kumeno nwangire kare ku kare, ento, calu ma umitu jubed juneno, kadi i nindo ma kawoni, dul ma nok ma poko kindgi nenre kare ku kare, ma romo dhanu pyer aryo ku pyerabic acel acel. Juruwer ma kumeno julwongo gi ni sukul. Pol kare gibedo kit ario; ju ma gicokiri ku mon, man ju ma gicokiri ngo ento co ma fodi gitie aradu ma tek, nyo thwol, calu ma julwongo gi.

I cavalier ma bino i sukul pa mon, ineno coo ma dit, ento eti ngo; ma, ikum lworo moko ci, nyutho tego cwinye ni podho in ngeye man umo ngwec pa mon pare. I andha, jadit eni ubedo Ottoman ma ber, ma ubewotho iwi pii, ma ucungo ku kony pa harem. Tung tung ma ni ikin Ottoman eni ku mon pare ma mon pare nen ungo; kum, kadi nang etiye ngati ma dit uloyu, mon, kadi nang edongo, gi kadhu acel ikum adek pa coo ma dit. Gitiye ma yot, andha; Acopo wacu, kud ikadhu nusu pa yadi apar wi ario i kor cing. Ento, ecopu ngo ni kwero, nia ikum lembe ceke gitiye ku twero mi nwangu en bon point .

Ebedo lembe ma nyayu anyonga lee ni neno harem eni ku rwoth pare i wotho migi ma rwinyo. Calu piny ma rwinyo, gibedo nja i wotho pi sayu piny ma tung tung. Iromo kugi i Line i kare mi lum ma pong pa kare mi cam mi Equatorial, ma dong idok cen, nyalo bedo, kud i bedo i kare mi cwir i nam mi yo malu, man ibecamu cwir ku olo man lyethu ma rac. I kare ma gibedo malu man piny i Equator pi kare manok, gicaku cidho i pii mi Oriental pi kuro kare mi ngico keca, man kumeno giweko lyethu ma lee mi oro.

Ka wabewotho ku kwiocwiny i acel ikin wotho eni, ka nen piny moko ma nyayu lworo, rwoth rec ma julwongo ni lord whale bedo ku wange ikum juru ot pare ma ber. Ka ngati moku copo ketho Leviathan ma nyathin bino yo eca, nen nia ecopo cidho ceng ku mon acel, ku ngei madit ma Bashaw ugoye, man eryeme woko! Saa ma malu, andha, ka ju ma fodi gitie aradu ma gikwo ngo ku cik calu en juyio igi ni mondo i ot maleng pa mutoro mi ot; kadi timo ango ma Bashaw mitu, ecopu ketho ngo Lothario ma nyinge rac uloyu kudi kavutu pare; pi, alas! kabedo pa rec zo i acel. Kaka i dhu nam, mon pol kare gi kelo lwiny marac ikind ju ma gimaru gi; kumeno bende ku rec ma julwongo ni whales, ma saa moko bino i lwiny ma rac, man zo pi mar. Gi ketho cing gi ku lak gi mabor ma piny, kare mukende gi ketho gi karacelu, man gi tembo ni bedo malu calu elks ma gi ketho cing gi ikindgi. Dhanu manok kende re ma jumako gi ku ret ma thuc mi rombo eni,—wic ma nyai, lak ma nyai, lak ma nyai; man i kare moko, dhoge ma nyai man ma nyai.

Ento ka waparu niya jadhum pa mutoro mi ot utingo kume gire i ngwec mir acel pa rwoth pa harem, eca nyutho nia etiye lembe ma nyayu anyonga lee ni neno rwoth meno. Moth moth edwoko rwom pare madit ikind gi kendu man ebedo keca pi kare manok, podi ebedo ceng ku Lothario ma nyathin, calu Solomon ma jamutoro ma ubeworo ikind mon pare elifu acel. Ka waweko rec mange ma julwongo ni whales ni nen, ju ma mako rec gicopo lubo ngo acel ikin Grand Turks eni; kum Grand Turks eni gibedo ku tego madwong akeca, man kumeno unctuousness migi nok. Pi awiya ma co ku ma mon ma ginyolo, pirango, awiya ma co ku ma mon eca umitu gi gwok gi gigi; kadi, ku kony pa min ot kende. Pi calu jumoko ma gimaru cam ma tung tung ma copo bedu nying gi, Rwoth Whale para epe ku mitu pa nursery, kadi nangu etiye ku mitu pa bower; man kumeno, calu jawoth madit, eweko awiya pare ma julwongo ngo iwi ngom zo; nyathin moko ci ubedo piny ma nen ungo. I kare maber, kadi kudu, calu miti pa aradu ube rwinyo; calu oro man dumps umedire; calu paru pare miyo ebedo moth; I lembe ma nok, calu lassitude ma dit utingo Turk ma oling; i ngeye mar pa yot kum man ber loko mar pa anyira; Ottoman mwa umondo i kwo ma rwinyo, ma loko cwiny, ma timo lembe maber, ekwero, eturo harem, man edongo ni ngati ma ber, ma jamutoro, ewotho kende ikind meridians man parallels ma ewacu lega pare, man eponjo Leviathan acel acel kud i kum lembe pare ma rac.

Kawoni, calu harem pa rec ma julwongo ni whales ju ma mako rec julwongo ni sukul, kumeno rwoth man jatel wi sukul eca julwonge ni jatel wi somu. Pieno epe ku kura ma tek, kadi nang etiye lembe ma nyayu lworo, nia ingey cidho i somo en gire, ecidho i ngom mawoko ma eponjo ngo lembe ma eponjo keca, ento mi mingo pare. Nying pare, jatel wi somu, nen calu uai kud i nying ma jumiyo ni harem kende, ento dhanu moko giparu niya jadhum ma kwong'a umiyu nying pa Ottoman whale eni, umaku ebed esomo lembe pa Vidocq, man enyang kudu nia jatel wi somu mi ng'om ma ja France ma nyinge uyik ne ubedo nenedi i ceng pare ma fodi etie aradu, man ponji mi kura pa dhanu eca ubedu ango eponjo ikind awiya pare moko.

Bedo kendgi man bedo kendgi ma nyathin ma dit mi somo bedo kudu i oro pare mi dongo, utiye lemandha pi Sperm Whales ceke ma oro migi ukadhu. I ng'om zoo, lone whale—calu ma julwongo Leviathan ma kende—unyutho nia ubedo rec ma con. Calu Daniel Boone ma nyinge moss-bearded, ebibedo ku ngati moko mbe ceng kude ento Nature kende; man egam egamu ni dhaku pare i lum mi pii, man etiye dhaku maber uloyu, kadi nang egwoko lembe madwong ma nyayu can i iye.

Sukuru ma dhanu moko mbe i iye ento coo ma fodi gitie aradu man ma tek, ma juweco pigi con, gimio tung tung madit ku sukul mi harem. Pi kadi nya rec ma mon eca gibedo ku lworo, co ma co, nyo forty-barrel-bulls, kite ma gilwongo gi kudu, gitiye ma tek uloyu ikind Leviathans zo, man gibedo ma rac uloyu; muweko rec ma wic migi nya nya, ma wic migi nya nya, ma saa moko irombo kudu, man eni bi lwiny kudi calu jok ma rac ma remo migi lith.

Sukuru mi Forty-barrel-bull gi dit uloyo sukul mi harem. Calu udul dhanu ma fodi gitie aradu, gipong ku lwiny, anyonga, man timo marac, ma giwotho i ng'om zoo ku rwom ma lac, ma dhanu moko mbe ma copo gwoko gi calu ma etimo ni awobi ma timo lembe marac i Yale nyo Harvard. Giweko lembe eni nyathotho, man ka dhanu adek ikum angwen gidongo, gipokere, man giwotho kendgi pi yenyo kabedo, niwacu, harems.

Lembe mange ma tung tung ikum sukul pa coo ku mon utiye ikum kit pa coo ku mon. Wec nia igoyo thwol ma romo 40-barrel-jajok ma jacan! juwadi zo giweko tic pare. Ento goyo ngati moko mi sukul mi harem, man juwadi giwotho i ngete ku lworo, saa moko gibedo ceng kude man pi kare malac, calu gin gigi gipodho i lei.
