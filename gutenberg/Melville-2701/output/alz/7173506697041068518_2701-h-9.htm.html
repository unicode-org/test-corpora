"Hist! iwinjo koko eca, Cabaco?"

"Ting agulu, icopo, Archy? Ibemito wacu ango?"

"Eni kendu—eni etiye!—ewinjiri calu dhanu ario nyo adek ma gibe nindo gibe loko wigi, kawoni!"

"Caramba! itimo, wadi mi yei, ibecitimo? Ebedo biskuit adek ma jucamu pi cam mi diewor ma loko iiye-piny mange epe. Nen i bakuli!"

“Tish! the bucket!”





CHAPTER 44. The Chart.

Ka nwang ilubo tok Captain Ahab piny i ot pare ingey yamu ma tek ma utime i dyewor ma ukadhu ikum yub pare ku jutic pare, nwang ibe neno ka ecidho i locker i transom, man ewodho karatasi madit mi nam ma nya aceng, epoko gi i wange iwi meza pare ma jukuboth. Ka dong ebedo piny i wange, icopo nenu ka eponjo lembe matung tung man rangi ma tung tung ma wange unwangu; man ku pencil ma moth ento ma ling, kyewo lembe mukende iwi kabedu ma con ubedu nono. I kind kare moko, ebed edwoko wang buku macon ma juketho ingete, kama jugoro i iye kare mi oro man kabedo ma, i wotho matung tung macon pa yei matung tung, sperm whales ma jucaku nyo juneno.

Kinde ma etiye ka timo kumeno, tara ma pek ma jukubo ku nyoru iwi wiye, ube yeng yeng ku wotho pa yei, man ebed ebolo ler man tipo pa laini iwi wange ma nyai, nitundu nen calu nya en gire ebe ketho lanyuth man yore iwi charts ma nyai, de ebedo ka goyo laini man yore moku iwi chart ma jugoro maber iwi wiye.

Ento ubedu ngo dyewor eni kende ma, i ot pare kende, Ahab ubedu ka paru paru pare. I dyewor ceke jubed juwodho gi woko; ceng ku diewor moko jubed juwodho lanyuth pa pencil, man mukende jubed juloko. Pien ku karatasi pa nam angwen ma nen i wange, Ahab ubedu kawothu i nam ma mol man ma mol, ku paru mi timo lembe ma ber pa paru pare.

Kawoni, ni ngati moko ci ma ung'eyo ngo yore pa leviathans, ecopu nen ni tic ma epe ku genogen, ento ni sayu lei acel kende i nam madit mi ng'om eni. Ento epe ni Ahab, ma engeyo rwom pa pii man pii; man kumeno kwanu rwom pa cam pa sperm whale; man, bende, poyo wic ikum kare ma rwinyo, ma juyiko pi dwar pare i latitudes moko; copo tuc i lembe ma ber, ma copo wok i lembe ma rwinyo, ikum ceng maber loyo mi bedo iwi ngom eni nyo eca pi sayu lei pare.

Mandha, andha, utiye lemandha ikum bedo pa sperm whale i pii moko, ma juru dwar mapol giyio nia, jucopo nenu man juponje i ng'om zoo; ka lembe ma jugoro pi wotho acel pa meli pa rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale jucoko maber, ci wotho pa sperm whale bino nwangire ni rwate ku pa herring-shoals nyo flights pa swallows. I kum lembe maeni, jutemo ni ketho karatasi mi wotho pa sperm whale.*

CHAPTER 45. The Affidavit.

Nimakere ku lembe ma copo bedu i buku eni; man, andha, calu ma wabeweco iwi lembe acel nyo ario ma pire tek man ma nyayu anyonga i kura pa sperm whales, dul ma ukadhu, i thenge pare ma kwong, piretek calu ma jubinwangu i buku eni; ento lembe ma pire tek ukwayu ju medu man ju medo ikume, kara ju nyang ikume maber, man ni kwanyu lworo moko ci ma ngec ma thuc ikum lembe eca copo kelo i paru pa dhanu moko, ikum lemandha pa lembe ma pire tek mi lembe eni.

Amito ngo atim tic para eni maber; ento bibedo moth ni kelo paru ma mitere ku wec matung tung pa piny, ma dhanu ngeyo ni whaleman; man kud i lembe ma jugoro eni, agam—ajiki ma juyenyo bino lubo kende kende.

Mir acel: An gira angeyu lembe adek ma rec ma julwongo ni whale, ingey nwangu harpoon, uringo woko; man, ingey kare moko (i kare acel mi oro adek), jutwoye kendu ku cing acel, man junege; kinde ma cuma ario, ma jugoro ku cipher acel, juwodho kud i kum. I kare ma oro adek ukadhu ikind cwalo harpoons aryo; man aparu niya ecopu bedu piny moko makadhu kudu; jadhum ma utingo gi, i kind kare, ni cidho i yei mi kuloka i wotho mi Afrika, ucidho i dhu nam keca, emondo i dul pa ju ma giyenyo lembe manyen, man emondo bor i kind ngom, kama ewotho i iye pi oro ma romo aryo, ma pol kare ebedo i ariti pa thwol, lei makwiny, lamvor, thwol ma rac, ku lembe mange ma rac ma copo wok i adunde kabedo ma jungeye ngo. Ikinde marom, rec ma julwongo ni whale ma en etho de ucikere ni bedu i wotho pare; m'umbe jiji nya edaru wotho iwi ngom wang adek, edaru wotho ku cinge i dhu nam mi Afrika; ento pi bero moko ope. Jadit eni ku rec ma dit eni gi dok karacelu, man acel uturo wadi. Awacu niya an, an gira, angeyo lembe adek ma rom ku maeni; eno ubedo ikind ario ma aneno rec ma julwongo ni whales; man, i lwiny mir aryo, eneno cuma ario ku lanyuth ma jupoko i igi, i ngeye juwodho kud i rec ma tho. I kind oro adek, edoko tek nia abedo i yei wang ario zoo, mir acel man mir ajiki, man mir ajiki ang'eyo kit moko ma dit ma nen ungo i wang rec, ma aneno keca oro adek mukadho. Awacu oro adek, ento angeyu niya ubedu madwong uloyu. Kae utiye lembe adek, ma an gira angeyo lemandha migi; ento awinjo pi lembe mange madwong kud ibang dhanu ma lemandha migi i lembe ne epe ku the lembe maber mi ketho cing gi ikumgi.

Mir aryo: Ung'eyere maber i Sperm Whale Fishery, kadi dhanu mi ng'om ma yo piny copo bedu ma gingeyo ngo, nia ubedu ku lembe madwong ma jupoi i iye ma rec moko ma julwongo ni whale i nam ubedo i kare mabor man kabedo ma dhanu ungiyu. Pirango ma rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale udoko ku lanyuth ma kumeno ubedu ngo pi kit kume pare ma tung tung ma tung tung ku whale mange; pi kadi bed ni chance whale moko ci copo bedu ma ukoc, gi daru timo pare ma ukoc ku nege, man gi lwoke piny ni moo ma pire tek. Ungo: the lembe ubedo eni: nia kud i lembe ma rac ma ukadhu kud i tic mi mako rec, ubedu ku rwom ma malu pa rec ma julwongo ni whale calu ma ubedu ikum Rinaldo Rinaldini, ma uketho ju ma mako rec mapol gibedo kud anyonga mi ngeyo en ku mulo tarpaulins migi kende kende kinde ma jubinwange ka ebedo i dhu nam, manangu giyenyo ngo dhanu mukende ma gingeyo. Calu jok jok moko ma can i dhu nam ma gingeyo ngati madit ma cwinye lith, gitimo foyofoc mabor ma dhanu ngeyo ngo i yo, kara tek gi lubo tok ngati ne, gicopo nwangu foyofoc pi paru migi.

Ento ungo kende kende nia acel acel pa rec ma julwongo ni whales ubedo ku rwom ma malu—Ungo, icopo lwonge ni rwom ma malu i nam; epe kende nia ebedo ngati ma pire tek i kwo man kawoni etiye ngati ma tho ngo i lembe mi anyim ingey tho, ento juyio ire i twero, twero, man tung tung mi nying; ubedu ku nying marom ku pa Cambyses nyo Cæsar. Ebedo ngo kumeno, O Timor Tom! in leviathan ma nyinge uyik, ma remo pare nen ve iceberg, ma ubedu ka ling i Oriental straits ma nyinge eca, ma pii pare nenre pol kare kud i dhu nam mi Ombay? Ebedo ngo kumeno, O New Zealand Jack! I lworo pa ju ma giwotho i nam ma giwotho i ng'om mi Tattoo Land? Ebedo ngo kumeno, O Morquan! Rwoth mi Japan, ma ndege pare ma malu giwacu nia saa moko nen calu musalaba ma tar pa pee iwi polo? Ebedo ngo kumeno, O Don Miguel! in Chilian whale, ma jugoro calu thwol ma dit ku hieroglyphics iwi ngeye! I lembe ma yot, nen rec angwen ma jung'eyo maber ni ju ma gisomo lembe mi Cetacean History calu Marius nyo Sylla ni ju ma gisomo lembe macon.

Ento eni kende ngo. New Zealand Tom ku Don Miguel, ingey timo lembe marac ikind yei mi yei matung tung, i ajikine gicidho ka sayu, jubedu ka yenyo gi, jubedu ka lubo tok gi man jubedu ka nego gi ku judongo ma tek, ma utingo meli migi ku piny ma nen kamaleng, calu ma jubedu kawothu kud i Narragan Woods, Butler ubedo ku lembe pare macon paru mi mako janek ma nyinge Annawon, jalweny madit pa rwoth pa India Philip.

Angeyo ngo kama acopo nwangu iye kabedo maber uloyu keni, mi weco ikum piny acel nyo aryo mange, ma ira nen ni piny ma pire tek, calu ma jugoro ma nyutho i yore ceke bedo ber pa lembe pa White Whale, asaga pa ret madit. Pi eno ubedo acel ikin lembe ma nyayu can ma lemandha mito kony ma lee calu ma rac. Dhanu mapol ma gikwo i ng'om gi ngeyo ngo lembe moko ma nen kamaleng man ma nen ungo i ng'om, ma manangu gipe ku lembe moko ma nen maleng, mi lembe mukadho man mange, mi mako rec, gicopo ngiyo Moby Dick calu lembe ma rac, nyo ma rac man ma rac uloyu, lapor ma rac man ma dhanu copo yio ngo.

Mir acel: Kadi bende coo mapol gitiye ku paru moko ma nyang ikum peku ma dit mi mako rec, ento gipe ku paru moku ma nen maleng pa peku eno, man kite ma gi dwoko kudu wang mapol. The lembe acel copo bedu, nia ngati acel de ikind pyer abic pa peko man tho pa dhanu ma tho i tic mi mako rec, nwango rekod pa lwak i pacu, kadi nang edaru man juwilo rekod ne. Iparu niya ngati ma can eca, ma kawoni jumake ku rec ma julwongo ni whale-line i dhu nam mi New Guinea, jube tingo piny i the nam ku leviathan—iparu niya nying ngati ma can eca biwok i gazeti ma ibisomo urwo i cam peri miwikugweno? Ungo: kum waraga ne utiye marac akeca ikind keni ku New Guinea. I andha, iwinjo lembe ma jucopo lwonge ni lembe ma nja ma wok kud i New Guinea? Ento abewecu iri nia i wotho acel ma atimo i Pacific, ikind mange mapol waweco ikum yei pyer adek matung tung, acel acel utho ku rec madit, mukende utho acel, man adek acel acel utho ku jurutic mi yei acel acel. Pi Mungu, bed maber ku tara peri man tara peri! epe gallon acel ma iwango, ento rimo pa dhanu acel kende re ma ucwale.

Mir aryo: Dhanu ma ni i dhu nam gitiye ku paru moko ma rwinyo nia rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ubedo lei madit ma tek mandha; ento anwango nia ka abeweco igi lapor moko ma pire tek pa dit pa piny ario eni, gifoya lee dit pi bedo para ma timo lembe marac; kinde ma, awacu iwi cwinya, nangu angeyu ngo paru mi bedo ngati ma timo lembe marac ma sagu Musa, kinde ma egoro lembe mi masendi mi Misiri.
