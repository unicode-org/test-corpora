CHAPTER 42. Whiteness pa Whale.

Gin ma white whale ubedo ni Ahab, jukoro; ango, saa moko, ebedo ira, kawoni podi juweco ngo.

Kakare pa lembe ma nen kamaleng ma mako Moby Dick, ma copo ketho lworo i cwiny pa ngati moko cii, paru mange de ubedu tiye, nyo lworo ma nyang ikume, ma saa moko ku tego pare edaru loyo dhanu mukende zo; man podi ebedo lembe ma nyang ikume tek man ma dhanu copo weco pire ngo, ma uketho abedo ku lworo mi kethe i ayi ma dhanu copo nyang kudu. Ubedo tar pa rec ma dit ma uketho cwinya utal. Ento acopo bedo ku genogen nenedi mi gonyo lembe para keni; man podi, i yore moku ma nya wange tek, aloko ira giri nia umitu, tek epe, dul eni zo copo bedu piny ma kony ope.

Kadi bende i piny madwong ma cwio, rangi ma tar medo leng, calu nya emiyo ber moko ma pire tek, calu i marbles, japonicas, man pearls; man kadi nang thek matung tung ungiyu rwom moko pa rwoth i rangi eni; kadi rwoth macon mi Pegu ma giketho nying "Rwoth pa lyec ma rangi migi tar" iwi rwom migi mi telo; man rwoth mi kare eni mi Siam ma ucungo iwi rwom pa rwoth; man bendera pa Hanoverian ma utingo cal acel pa charger ma tar calu pee; man ker madit mi Austria, Cæsarian, jalonyo pa Rome, ma rangi pa ker pare ubedo ku rangi marom; man kadi bende bedo ma pire tek eni tiye ikum thek pa dhanu gire, miyo dhanu ma rangi migi tar bedo ku twero ikum thek moko ci macol; man kadi, ikum lembe eni zo, rangi matar ubedo piny ma pire tek ni anyonga, kum ikind ju Roma kidi matar ubedo lanyuth pa ceng mi anyonga; man kadi bende i lembe mange mi kisa man lanyuth, rangi marom eni juyike ni lanyuth pa piny madwong ma mulo cwiny, ma ber—bedo maleng pa mon, berocwiny pa oro; kadi bende i lembe ma pire tek mi dini ma malu juyike ni lanyuth pa bedo maleng man tego; ku ju ma giworo mac mi Persia, mac ma rangine tar ma juketho iwi keno; man i dhok mi Jugiriki ma julwong’o Inthologies mi dhu Jugiriki, jubed jung’olo nia etie ng’atu ma pire tek i ng’om ma tung’ tung’; Kite maeni ma dhanu ungiyu ngo, ma ketho paru pa bedo tar, ka jupoko ku juwadi ma ber, man juporo ku piny moko ci ma rac i kume gire, mi medo lworo meno nitundo i kajikne. Nen winyo makwar mi polo, man rec shark makwar mi tropics; ango ento rangi migi ma leng man ma nyap ma weko gibedo ju ma lworo ma dit ma gitiye?
