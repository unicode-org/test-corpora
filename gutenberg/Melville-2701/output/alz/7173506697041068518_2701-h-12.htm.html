CHAPTER 55. Mi cal ma rac pa rec ma julwongo ni Whales.

Abeci goro iri pi kare malac calu ngati moko ma epe ku canvas, piny moko calu kit pa rec ma dit ma enenre i wang whaleman kani i kume ma whale ucungo inget meli pa whale kara jucopu tingo iwie maber. Ecopu bedu ber, dong, con ni ketho lembe ikume ma dhanu moko paru ikume ma kadok tin eni ube nyayu lembe ikum yioyic pa jadwong ngom. Saa utimiri mi ketho ng'om ubed maber i lembe eni, ni nyutho nia cal ma kumeno pa rec ma julwongo ni whale utiye marac.

Ecopu bedu nya acaki pa lembe ma nyayu paru ma rac eca nwangre ikind cal macon pa Hindoo, Egypt, man Grecian. Nicaku i kare ma juyubu piny manyen ento ma dhanu timo ngo lembe maber ikare ma iwi got macol pa hekalu, iwi kom pa cal, man iwi jam lwiny, medallion, kopo, man sente, rec ma julwongo ni dolphin jubedu ka goyo ku cinge ma calu pa Saladin, man wic ma juketho i wiye calu pa St. George; nicaku eca, piny moko ma rom ku maeni ubedo nuti, ungo kende i cal mapol pa rec ma julwongo ni whale, ento i lembe mapol ma jukoro ikume.

Kawoni, ku lembe ceke, cal macon uloyu ma podi utiye pa rec ma julwongo ni whale, nwangire i cavern-pagoda ma julwongo ni Elephanta, i India. Ju Brahmins giwacu nia i kind cal ma juyiko ma romo thum ungo mi pagoda ma con, tic ceke man lembe ceke ma jutimo, lembe ceke ma dhanu timo, jular juyiko con ma fodi acel de uwok ungo. Ebedo lembe mi ziazia ngo, nia i yo moko tic mwa maber mi maku rec ma julwongo ni whale ucikere ni bedu nuti. Hindoo whale ma juweco pire, nwangire i thenge mange mi cel, ma nyutho bedo pa Vishnu i ayi pa leviathan, ma julwongo ni Matse Avatar. Ento kadi nya cal eni utiye nusu pa dhanu man nusu pa rec ma dit, kara emii yibe pa rec ma tokcen, ento dul ma nok eni utiye marac. Nen calu yibe ma rwinyo pa anaconda, ma sagu cing ma lac pa ler ma dit pa rec ma julwongo ni true whale.

Ento cidhi i Galleries macon, man nen kawoni cal pa jagor madit pa Jukristu pa rec eni; kum etimo maber uloyu ma sagu Hindoo ma con. Etiye cal pa Guido pa Perseus ma laru Andromeda kud i cing lei makwiny mi nam nyo rec madit. Guido unwangu lanyuth pa lei ma nen ungo kumeno kan kani? Bende Hogarth, i goyo cal marom i "Perseus Descending" pare gire, timo ngo piny maber uloyu. Peko madit pa leya madit pa Hogarthian eca ucungo iwi ngom, ma utingo pii acel de ngo. Etiye ku howdah moko iwi ngeye, man dhoge ma lac ma pii ucungo i iye, jucopo tingo pi Traitors' Gate ma wok kud i Thames ku pii i Tower. I ngeye, tiye Prodromus whales pa Scotch Sibbald macon, man Jonah's whale, calu ma ju nyuthu i cal pa Biblia macon man i acaki pa buku macon. Ango ma jubi wacu ikum gi? Pi lembe pa book-binder's whale ma ucungo calu yen mi mzabibu ma ucungo iwi yei ma rwinyo piny—calu ma juketho i ngeye man i wi karatasi mi buku mapol macon man manyen—eni ubedo piny ma leng man ma ber mandha, ma juporo, agam, kud i cal ma rom ku pa antique vases. Kadi bende julwonge ni dolphin, an alwongo rec maeni ma mako buku ni tembe pa rec ma julwongo ni whale; kum ubedu lembe ma juyike ikare ma jucaku nyuthu jam tic ne. Jukelo ku jagor moko macon mi Italy ikare moko i rundi mir 15, i kare mi Revival of Learning; man i ceng eca, man kadi i kare ma ukadhu, dhanu mapol paru niya dolphins ubedo lei moko pa Leviathan.

I kind lembe ma jugoro man piny mukende ma juyubu i buku moko macon, icopo nwangu lembe ma nyayu anyonga lee i kum rec ma julwongo ni whale, kama pii ma ngic, pii ma ngic man ma ngic, Saratoga man Baden-Baden, wok kud i wic pare ma rwinyo ngo. I mbaya ma pire tek pa "Advancement of Learning" ibinwangu winyo moko ma nyang ikum lembe.

Ento ka waweko lembe maeni ma timo ngo tic, wek wanenu cal pa leviathan ma nyutho nia etiye ma rwinyo, ma jutimo ku ngec mi ryeko, pa ju ma gingeyo. I kind lembe pa Harris ma con mi wotho, otiye dul pa rec moko ma juwodho kud i buku pa Dutch mi wotho, AD 1671, ma nyinge "Wotho mi mako rec ma dit ni Spitzbergen i yei Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson pa Friesland, jatel wi tic." Ikind acel ikin dul eca, rec ma julwongo ni whales, calu yei madongo pa yen, ju nyuthu gi ma gi vutu ikind cula mi pee, ku winyo ma rangi migi tar ma gi ringo iwi ngeye migi. I plate mange, blunder ma dit jutimo ni nyuthu rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ku flukes ma ucungo.

Man kendu, tiye quarto ma dit, ma jugoro ku Captain Colnett acel, Captain mi Post i navy mi England, ma wiye ubedo "Wotho ma ucungo iwi Cape Horn i nam ma yo piny, pi medo Spermaceti Whale Fisheries." I buku eni utiye cal pa Physeter nyo Spermaceti whale, maju goyo ku rwom pa acel ma junego i dhu nam mi Mexico, Dwi mir Abora, 1793, man jutingo iwi yei.” Angeyu ngo nia jatel wi meli ne uketho cal ma ber eni utingo pi bero pa judongo pare mi nam. Mi wacu lembe acel kende ikume, wek awac nya etiye ku wang ma jutiyo kudu, nimakere ku rwom ma lubo, ikum sperm whale ma dit, ma copo ketho wang whale meno doko bow-window ma bor pare romo futi abic. Ah, jatel wi mony para, pirango ma imiyo ngo iwa Jonah ma neno kud i wange!

Bende epe lembe ma jugoro maber pa Natural History pi bero pa juma nyathin man ma fodi gitie aradu, ma epe ku timo marac marom. Nen tic ma dhanu zo maru "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." I lembe ma jugoro ma nok mi London mi 1807, jubedu ku sahan pa "whale" man "narwhale." Amito ngo nen ni ngati ma timo ngo tic maber, ento rec ma leng eni nen calu rec ma jukubo; man, pi narwhale, neno acel kende copo ketho ngati ung'eyo, nia i rundi mir apar wi abungwen eni, hippogriff ma kumeno copo bedu piny ma pire tek ikum dhanu moko ci ma riek pa awiya mi somo.

I ngeye, kendu, i 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, jabodho madit mi ngec mi piny, ugoro buku mi ngec ikum rec ma julwongo ni whale, ma i iye cal mapol pa lei matung tung pa Leviathan. Lembe eni zo epe kende, ento cal pa Mysticetus nyo Greenland whale (niwacu, Right whale), kadi Scoresby, ngati mu'a ngeyo lembe ikum kit lei eni, uwaco ni epe ku wadi pare i ngom.

Ento ketho cap-sheaf i tic maeni ma rac ubedo kende kende ni Frederick Cuvier, umego pa Baron ma nyinge uyik. I 1836, egoro buku pare ma julwongo ni Natural History of Whales, ma i iye emio cal pa Sperm Whale. Mapodi inyutho cal eni ni ngati moko ci mi Nantucketer, ber ka ibemito idok cen kud i Nantucket. I wec acel, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale epe Sperm Whale, ento squash. Eyo, ebedo ungo ku bero mi wotho mi mako rec (dhanu ma kumeno gibedo ungo), ento kama enwangu kudu cal eni, nga ma copo wacu? Nyo enwangu kudu calu ngati mutelo pare mi ngec i tic marom, Desmarest, unwangu acel ikin abortion pare mandha; eni, kud i cal pa ju China. Man kit awobi ma kwo ku pencil ma ju China gi, pol pa kopo man saucers giwacu iwa.

Pi lembe pa ju ma goyo cal ma nen i ngudu ma ucungo iwi duka pa juwil moo, jubi wacu ango ikumgi? Pol kare gibedo Richard III. rec ma dit, ku dromedary humps, man ma rac mandha; cam miwikugweno iwi sailor tarts adek nyo angwen, ma en e yei ma dit ma upong ku ju ma timo tic i nam: goro migi upong i nam mi rimo man rangi macol.

Ento lembe ma rac ma tung tung ma jutimo i nyuthu rec ma julwongo ni whale epe lembe mi ziazia. Kethi iparo peri! Pol pa cal pa ju ma gisomo lembe mi ryeko juwodho kud i rec ma jucwinyo; man eni gi nen maleng calu cal pa yei ma unyothere, ku ngeye ma otur, copo nyuthu leya ma pire tek eni gire i rwom pare mi yei man spars. Kadi bende lyec ucungu pi bor migi zo, Leviathan makwo podi ucungu ngo pi cal pare. Rec ma kwo, i rwom pare man rwom pare, nen kende i nam i pii ma dhanu copo nyang kudu ngo; man iwi pii, dul madit pare nen ungo, calu yei ma jucwalo pi lwiny; man kud i piny eno etiye piny ma copo bedu ngo ni dhanu ni tingo kume i polo, kara ebed ku tego pare man rwom pare. Man, ka waweco ngo ikum tung tung madit mi nen pa rec ma nyathin man Leviathan ma dit pa Plato; ento, kadi i lembe pa acel ikin rec ma nyathin ma ucungo iwi yei, kite ma etiye kudu nen calu eel, limbered, ma tung tung, ma jajok gire copo nwangu ngo.

Ento ecopu bedu, nia kud i cogo pa rec ma julwongo ni whale ma jukuboth, lembe ma atira copo wok ikum kite pare mandha. Ungo. Pi en e piny acel ma nyayu lworo ikum Leviathan eni, nya cogo pare miyo ngec manok ikum kite ma en utiye kudu. Kadi bende cogo pa Jeremy Bentham, ma juketho pi candelabra i ot buku pa ngati acel m'ikind ju m'utimo tic pare, nyutho paru pa jadit ma dit ma wic pare tek, ku kit pa Jeremy mange ma pire tek; ento piny moko mbe ma kumeno ma jucopo nwangu kud i cogo pa leviathan. I andha, calu ma jadwar madit uwacu, cogo pa rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale utiye ku winjiri marom ku lei ma juketho i iye man ma juketho i iye, calu ma winyo timo ku chrysalis ma umu wiye. Kite ma ukoc eni nen kamaleng i wic, calu ma i thenge moko mi buku eni jubi nyuthu. Man de nenre maber akeca i cinge ma yo piny, ma cogo pare rwate ku cogo pa cing dhanu, man eweko cinge madit kende. Find eni utiye ku cing angwen ma nen calu cogo, cing ma lageng, cing ma dyere, cing cing ma nok, man cing ma nok. Ento piny eni zo gibedo nja i adila migi, calu cing dhanu i adila ma juyubu. "Kadi nya rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale copo timo tic iwa marac nenedi," Stubb uwacu ceng moko, "jucopo ngo ni wacu nia ecopo timo tic iwa manangu epe ku mittens."

Pi the lembe eni zo, dong, yore moku ci ma icopo nenu kudu, umitu ibed ku paru niya Leviathan madit utiye lei acel kende i ng'om ma umitu obed ma juyubu ngo nitundu. Andha, cal acel copo wok ceng ceng ku mukende, ento ngati moko ope ma copo wok ku rwom ma lac. Pieno yore moko epe mi ng'eyo kite ma rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale nen kudu. Man yore acel kende ma icopo nwangu kudu paru moko ikum kwo pare, utiye ni cidho i mako rec; ento ka itimo kumeno, ibecidho i ariti ma nok mi bedo i mac ma nja kude. Pieno, nen ira nia ber ka kud ibed ngati ma timo lembe ma nyayu lworo ikum Leviathan eni.





CHAPTER 56. Mi cal ma rac pa rec ma julwongo ni Whales, man cal ma andha pa kabedo mi mako rec.

I lembe ma mako cal pa rec ma dit, cwinya ubedo tek ni mondo i lembe ma rac pa gi ma nwangre i buku moko, macon man ma kawoni, asaga i Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, man mange. Ento aweko lembe eni woko.

Angeyo lembe angwen kende ma jugoro ikum Sperm Whale madit; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, man Beale's. I dul mukadho Colnett ku Cuvier juweco pigi. pa Huggins ber uloyu migi; ento, ku rwom ma malu, pa Beale ubedo maber uloyu. Cal pa Beale pa rec madit eni ber, muweko cal ma ikin i cal pa rec adek ma gitiye ku kura matung tung, ma uketho thenge pare mir aryo. Lembe pare ma kwong, yei ma ucungo ikum Sperm Whales, kadi nang epe ku jiji nya juyike pi nyayu jiji pa dhanu moko, utiye lemandha man kwo i adwogi pare. Moko pa cal pa Sperm Whale i J. Ross Browne utiye maber akeca; ento jugoro gi marac. Ento eni epe dubo pare.

Pa Right Whale, cal maber loyo utiye i Scoresby; ento ju goyo gi i rwom ma nok akeca ma copo mio paru maber. Etiye ku cal acel kende mi kabedo mi mako rec, man eni ubedo goro ma nyayu can, kum etiye ku cal ma kumeno kende, ka jutimo maber, ma icopo nwangu kudu paru mandha pa rec madit ma kwo calu ju ma gicayu rec pare ma kwo gineno.

Ento, ka jukwanu pi lembe ceke, maber uloyu, kadi nang i lembe moko epe, nyuthu pa rec ma dit man kabedo mi mako rec ma nwangre kama moku ci, utiye cal aryo madongo mi France, ma juyubu maber, man majukwanyo kud i cal pa Garnery acel. Ku yore migi, gi nyuthu lwiny ikum Sperm man Right Whale. I cal mir acel, Sperm Whale ma pire tek ju nyuthu ku tego pare, ma ucungu iwi yei kud i nam ma thuc, man utingo malo i polo iwi ngeye piny ma rac pa stoven planks. Theng yei ne unyai ungo, man juwodhe kende kende iwi ng'et yei ne; man ucungu i dhu nam, pi kare acel kende ma dhanu copo kwanu ngo, ineno jaring yei, ma nusu pare umu ku pii ma lyeth pa rec ma dit, man etiye ka ngwec, calu ngati m'uai kud i got. Tic pa piny eca zo ber man lemandha. Line-tub ma ungweyo nusu ucungo iwi nam macol; lum ma juyubu ku harpoons ma jucwalo ucungo i iye; wic pa ju ma giwotho i pii gi nyai iwi rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ku wec ma tung tung mi lworo; podi i bor pa yamu macol, yei ubedo ka wok piny iwi kabedo ne. Lembe marac copo nwangire i kum kit kum pa rec ma julwongo ni whale eni, ento wek eca ukadhu; kum, pi kwo para, nangu acopo ngo ni goyo cal maber akeca.

I cal mir aryo, yei utiye ka goyo i thenge mi barnacled flank pa Right Whale madit ma ringo, ma ubewotho ku lum pare macol i nam calu kidi moko ma mol kud i Patagonian cliffs. Jet pare ucungo, upong, man macol calu soot; kara kud i lyethu madwong i chimney, icopo paru niya cam mi diewor ma tek ube tedo i iye piny. Winy mi nam gibe camu rec ma nok, rec ma nyap, man cam mi nam mukende man maccaroni, ma Right Whale tingo iwi ngeye ma rac. Man i kare eni zo, leviathan ma dhoge ma gwai ube ringo i nam ma thuc, eweko ton pa curds ma tar ma nyai i ngeye, man eketho yei ma nok ubedo ka yeng i pii calu yei ma jumaku inget paddle-wheels pa ocean steamer. Pieno, piny ma iwang eca ubedo lembe ma nyayu lworo; ento ngeye, i ayi maber mi goyo cal, utiye rwom pa nam ma oling, yei ma rwinyo ma rwinyo pa yei ma tek ungo, man dul pa rec ma tho, ot ma juloyo, ku bendera mi mako ma ucungo iwi yen pa rec ma juketho i vur pare.

Garnery jagor cal ubedo anga, nyo ubedu, angeyo ongo. Ento kwo para pi en ebedo ngati ma ngeyo lembe pare maber, nyo eponjo maber akeca ku ngati moko ma ngeyo tic pare maber. Ju France gibedo coo pi goyo cal. Cidhi inen cal zo mi Europe, man kan ma ibi nwango iye cal ma kwo man ma yuyu iwi canvas, calu i ot madit mi Versailles; kama janeno lwinyu iye, pell-mell, ikind lwiny madongo mi France; kama pala moko ci nen calu mac pa Northern Lights, man rwoth man rwoth ma ucungo ku jam lwiny ube ringo, calu dul pa centaurs? Epe kabedo ma ber ungo i ot ma jugwoku i iye piny, utiye piny ma jucaku i nam mi Garnery.

Ngec pa ju France mi maku piny ma nen maleng nen ni nen kamaleng i cal man cal ma gi goyo iwi kabedo migi mi mako rec. Ku rwom acel mi apar pa ngec pa England i tic mi mako rec, man rwom acel acel pa ngec pa ju America, gi miyo thek ario eno cal acel kende ma judaru timo ma copo nyutho tipo mandha pa dwar pa rec. Pol kare, ju ma goyo cal pa rec ma julwongo ni whale ma julwongo ni whale ma julwongo ni whale, gineno nia gibedo kud anyonga ikum nyuthu kit ma piny nen kudu, calu ve kit ma whale nen kudu; ma, nimakere ku nen pa piny, nen calu goyo cal pa pyramid. Kadi bende Scoresby, Right whaleman ma nyinge ungiru, ingey miyo iwa nen pa Greenland whale, man narwhales man porpoises adek nyo angwen, emiyo iwa cal macon pa boat hooks, chopping knives, man grapnels; man ku diligence mi microscope pa Leuwenhoeck eketho cinge iwi ngom ma ube yeng wic, fac-similes pyer abungwen wi abusiel pa snow crystals mi Arctic. Amito ngo ni nyayu lewic i kum jawoth maber (amiyo ire deyo pi ngati ma con), ento i lembe ma pire tek eca ubedo lembe ma rac ni bedo ngo ku waraga mi kwo ma jukubo i wang japoklembe mi kuc mi Greenland.

Mi medo ikum cal maber pa Garnery, otiye cal mukende ario mi France ma ber mi poyo wigi, pa ngati moko ma nyinge "H. Durand." acel ikin gi, kadi nang epe maber pi tic mwa ma kawoni, ento eber ni juweco pire i lembe mange. Ubedo kabedo ma ling ikind cula mi Pacific; jaracik mi French whaler ucungo iwi yei, i dhu nam, i kabedo ma ling, man etingo pii iwi yei; yuk yei ma ucungo iwi yei, man ith lum ma bor pa yen ma yor i ngeye, zo gi ucungo karacelu i yamu ma yamu epe. Adwogi ne ber mandha, ka juporo ku nyuthu pa ju ma mako rec ma tek i the acel ikin yore migi manok mi yom cwiny mi yo piny. Lembe mange ma jugoro ubedo lembe matung tung: yei ubedo iwi nam ma oling, man i adunde pa kwo pa Leviathanic, ku Right Whale ingete; yei (i timo mi turo-i) ucungo iwi lei ma dit calu ma ucungo iwi nam; man yei, ma ucungo pio pio kud i kabedu mi tic eni, ubedu ka lubo tok rec ma dit mabor. Harpoons ku lances gibedo maber pi tic; ju adek ma giwotho ku yei gibe ketho yei i vur pare; man kud i dhuny pa nam, yei ma nok ucungu iwi pii, calu agura ma ube dongo. Kud i yei, iro pa can pa rec ma mol ubedo ka medere malu calu iro iwi caru pa ju ma yiku cuma; man yo piny, polo macol, ma ucungo malu ku yamo ma tek man koth, nen calu ube medo tic pa ju ma giwotho i nam ma cwinygi yom.





CHAPTER 57. Pa Whales in Paint; i Lak; i Wood; i Sheet-Iron; i kidi; i got; i Stars.

I Tower-hill, kani ibecidho piny i London docks, icopo bedu ineno jakwath moko ma goro (nyoke kedger , calu ju ma giwotho i yei giwacu) ma utingo bao ma jugoro i wange, ma nyutho lembe ma rac ma etimo ma eweko tyende. Utiye rec adek man yei adek; man acel ikin yei (ma juparu niya utiye ku tyende ma rwinyo i kite pare macon) jubenyayu ku lak rec ma dit. Saa moko ci pi oro apar eni, giwacu ira, jadhum eca utingo cal ne, man enyutho piny ne ni dhanu ma yio ngo. Ento kare mi bedo ber pare dong utuc. Rec pare adek gibedo rec maber akeca ma jugoro i Wapping, kadi ango; man lum pare ubedo lum ma dhanu moko cii ma ibinwangu i theng ngom ma yo reto. Ento, kadi nang edaru bedo iwi lum eca, wec moko ope ma jaracik ma jacan timo; ento, ku wange ma piny, ucungu ku can man eparu pi turo cinge.

I kind nam madit mi Pacific, man i Nantucket, man New Bedford, man Sag Harbor, ibinwangu cal pa rec ma julwongo ni whales man kabedo mi mako rec, ma ju ma mako rec gi goyo iwi lak rec ma julwongo ni Sperm Whale-teeth, nyo ladies' busks ma juyubu ku Right Whale-bone, man artic mange ma rom ku meno, ma julwongo ni whale ma nok ma julwongo gi contrivances ma gi goyo maber akeca kud i piny ma nyap, i sawa migi mi yom cwiny i nam. Moko kuomgi gitiye ku sanduku ma nok pa jam tic mi lak, ma juyike pi tic mi skrimshandering. Ento, i ayi ma rwinyo, gitimo tic ku jack-knives migi kende; man, ku jam tic ma tek pa jaring yei, gibidwoko iri piny moko ci ma imito, i yo ma jaring yei maru.

Bedo cen kud i dini pa Jukristu man kura maber dwoko dhanu i kite ma Mungu uketho i iye, niwacu gin ma julwongo ni savagery. Jadwar peri mandha utiye ngati ma rac calu Iroquois. An kukuma atiye ngati ma kwiny, ma atiye ku yioyic moko ci ento ni Rwoth pa ju ma camu dhanu; man ayika saa moko ci mi turo cik pare.

Kawoni, acel ikin kit pa ngati ma rac i saa pare mi tic, utiye bedo moth pare maber mi tic. Jam lwiny macon mi Hawaii nyo spear-paddle, i rwom pare man rwom pare mi goyo cal, utiye lanyuth madit pa bedo moth pa dhanu calu lexicon mi Latin. Pi, ku nyathi moko ma nyai pa rec ma jukunyo nyo lak rec shark, tic ma nyayu lworo pa yen udaru timere; man edaru tero oro madwong mi tic maber.

Calu ma ku Hawaiian savage, kumeno bende ku white sailor-savage. Ku bedo moth marom, man ku lak rec shark acel, pa jack-knife pare acel ma rac, ebi goyo iri cal moko ma juyubu ku cogo, ma epe calu jatic, ento ma ceng ikum kit ma juyubu kudu, calu Greek savage, Achilles's shield; man ma upong ku tipo mi timo lembe marac man mi paru, calu cal pa ngati moko maber mi Dutch, Albert Durer.

Whales ma julwongo ni wooden whales, nyo whales ma jupoko kud i lum macol ma nok mi South Sea war-wood, junwango gi pol kare i forecastles pa ju America ma gicayu rec. Moko ikum gi jutimo ku diru mandha.

I ot moko ma con ma juketho i wigi gable, icopo neno rec ma julwongo ni brass whales ma juguro ku yibe pi ju ma goyo dhugola mi dhugola. Ka jatel piny ubedo ka nindu, rec ma julwongo ni anvil-headed whale ubedo ber. Ento knocking whales eni gi nen ungo maber akeca calu essays ma gen. Iwi wi got pa kanisa moko macon ibineno rec ma julwongo ni sheet-iron whales ma juketho keca pi weather-cocks; ento gitiye malo, man ikum lembe eca gitiye ku lembe ceke ma jugoro ku " Ci cingi woko! " icopo ngo ni ngiyo gi maber akeca mi ngiyo bero migi.

I thenge mi ng'om ma ku cogo, kama i the godi mabor ma juturo, kidi madwong ucungo iwi lum, icopo nwangu cal pa Leviathan ma ucungo i lum, ma i ceng ma yamu ubecwalu, epodho ikum gi i pii macol.

I ngeye, kendu, i ngom ma got ma juwotho i iye nja ucungo iwi got ma malu; keca ku keca kud i wang lembe moko ma ber, ibinwangu nen pa kit pa rec ma julwongo ni whales ma nen i dhu got. CHAPTER 60. The Line.

Ku lapor pa kabedo mi mako rec ma dit ma jubi weco pire, man pi ni nyang maber pa lembe ma rom ku meno ma jukoro kama mange, atiye ka weco ikum rec ma julwongo ni whale-line.
