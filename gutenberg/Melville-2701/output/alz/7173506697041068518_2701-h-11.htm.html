THEK 53. Gam.

The lembe ma uketho Ahab umondo ngo i yei ma wabeweco pire ubedu eni: yamu man nam unyutho yamu matek. Ento kadi nangu eca ubedu ngo lembe, ecopo bedu ngo, nyalo bedu, ebed emondo i iye—ni lubre ku kura pare i kare marom—ka nwang eber, ku yore mi lwongo, enwango lagam marac ikum penji ma epenju. Pi, calu ma edoko, ebed eparo ngo ni bedo karacelu, kadi pi dakika abic, ku jadit moku ci ma engeyo ngo, kawang ecopo mio lembe moko ma emito. Ento lembe eni zo copo bedu ma juporo ngo, tek epe lembe moku ma juweco pire ikum tic ma tung tung pa yei ma mako rec ma julwongo ni whaling-vessels ka girombo ku juwothgi i nam ma woko, man asaga ikum kabedo ma juwotho i iye.

Ka dhanu ario ma jungeyo ongo ukadhu Pine Barrens in New York State, nyo Salisbury Plain in England; ka girombo ikindgi i lum ma rac, ju aryo eni, pi kwo migi, gicopo kwetu ngo foc ikindgi; man ucung pi kare manok mi loko lembe manyen; man, nyalo bedu, bedo piny pi kare manok man yom i rwom acel: dong, kite ma ber loyo iwi Pine Barrens man Salisbury Plains mi nam, yei ario ma mako rec ma julwongo ni whale ma gibecidho i dhu nam—i cula mi Fanning's Island, nyo King's Mills mabor; kite ma ber, awacu, nia i thenge ma kumeno, yei eni umaku ngo gi lok kende kende, ento gi bino i winjiri ma ceng, man ma ber man ma ber. Man asaga eca nen calu lembe ma rwinyo, i lembe pa yei ma juwilo i nam acel, man ma judongo, judongo, man dhanu manok kende re ma gingeyo giri; man kumeno, bedu ku piny matung tung mi ot ma wabeweco ikumgi.

Pi yei ma ubedu ungo pi kare malac, jatel wi yei, nyo, etiye ku waraga iwi yei; kadok kumeno de, ebibedo ku genogen mi weko ebed ku karatasi moko mi nindo moko oro acel nyo aryo i ngeye pa ma tok cen i karatasi pare ma nen ungo man ma cinge ucungo. Man pi dwoko berocwiny meno, yei ma ubecidho woko binwangu ngec manyen mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whale kud i kabedo mi ngwec ma ecopo bedu i iye, piny ma pire tek uloyu ire. Man i kadhiri, lembe eni zo bibedo lemandha ikum yei ma mako rec ma dit ma kadho yo pa ngati acel acel i kabedo mi wotho, kadi nya gibedo cen kud i pacu pi kare malac. Pi acel ikin gi copo bedu ni enwango waraga kud ibang ngati mir adek, man dong kawoni bor; man waraga moko copo bedu pi dhanu mi yei ma enwang kudu kawoni. Mi mede, gicopo loko lembe mi mako rec, man gibedo ku wec maber. Pien ungo kende kende nia gicopo nwangu kisa pa ju ma giwotho i yei, ento bende ku berocwiny ma tung tung ma wok kud i timo lembe acel man peko man peko ma gipoko.

Bende tung tung mi ngom copo ketho tung tung moku ma pire tek; eni, tek dhanu ario zoo giweco dhok acel, calu ma dhanu mi Amerika ku mi dhok mi Ingresa giweco. Kadi bende, ni bedo andha, kud i wel ma nok pa ju ma mako rec mi England, rombo ma kumeno timre ngo pol kare, man ka timere, eromo bedu lewic ikind gi; pi ja England peri utiye ngati ma ling, man Yankee peri, emaru ngo lembe ma kumeno i ngati moko ento en gire. Mi mede, ju ma mako rec mi England kare moko gibedo ku rwom ma malu ikum ju ma mako rec mi Amerika; ikum Nantucketer ma bor man ma nyap, ku lembe pare ma rwinyo ngo, calu jafur mi nam. Ento kama ber pa ju ma gwoko rec mi England utiye andha, ecopo bedu tek ni wacu, nineno nia Yankees i ceng acel, karacelu, unego rec madwong uloyu England zo, karacelu, i oro apar. Ento eni ubedo goro ma nok ma rac ungo ikind ju ma dwar rec ma julwongo ni England, ma Nantucketer uketho ngo ikume; nyalo bedo ni, nikech ong'eyo ni en bende en gi ketho moko manok.

Dong, waneno nia ikind yei ceke ma wotho i nam, ju ma mako rec madongo gitiye ku tyen lok madwong mi bedo ku mer—man gitiye kumeno. Kadok kumeno de, meli moko mi kuloka ma kadho i kind nam mi Atlantic, pol kare giwotho manangu giweco ngo lembe moko cii, gipodho ikindgi i nam madit, calu dul pa ju ma timo tic i Broadway; man kare ku kare gibedo, nyalo bedo, i timo lembe ma rac ikum ngati acel acel. Pi Men-of-War, ka girombo i nam, gicaku wok kud i kind lembe ma nyayu lworo man timo lembe marac, timo lembe ma nyayu lworo, ma nen ungo nia etiye ku mar maber man mar pa umego. I kum rombo pa Slave-ships, pirango, gitiye i rwom ma malu, gi ringo cen kudi juwothgi pio pio. Man pi Pirates, ka gi nwango kare mi kadhu i kind cogo pa ngati acel acel, koth ma kwong ubedo—“Wiye adi?”—yore marom ku ju ma mako rec ma julwongo ni—“Barrel adi?” Man ka judwoko wang penji eni, ju ma gikwo i nam giweko gi, kum gitiye ju ma rac i thenge ario zoo, man gimaru ngo ni neno kit pa ju ma rac pa ngati acel acel.

Man kadok kumeno de, ka dhanu jutingo malu i yo ma tung tung, epe ku the lembe maber pi rwom pare ma malu. Pieno, adaru, nia i nyuthu nia etiye ngati ma malu uloyu iwi whaleman, i lembe meno jaracik ne epe ku the lembe moku ma ecopo cungu iwie.

Ento Gam ubedo ango? Icopo tiyo ku cingi peri ma lageng ka ibe ringo malu man piny ikind buku mi gonyo lembe, man icopo nwangu ngo wec ne. Daktari Johnson unwangu ngo ngec eno; Noah Webster's ark epe. Ento, wec maeni ma nyuthu lembe eni kawoni pi oro madwong jubedu katiyo kudu ikind dhanu ma romo elifu apar wiye abic ma junyolu i Yankees. Andha, emito gony, man umitu juketh i Lexicon. Ku paru eno, wek alok the lembe.

Lembe mange ma nok utiye ikum Gamming ma umitu kud juwil keni. Tic ceke gitiye ku lembe migi ma nok ma tung tung; kumeno bende tic mi mako rec ma julwongo ni whale. Man ikum tiller, whale-boat eyio ngo lembe moko ma kumeno; man kumeno calu i tuko, jurutic mi yei ceke umaku giwek yei, man kumeno calu jatel wi yei nyo jatel wi yei utiye wel, jatel wi yei eca ubedo jatel wi yei, man jatel wi yei, ma epe ku kabedu mi bedu i iye, juwodhe woko pi limo pare ma gicungu calu yen mi pine. En etiye ma ucungo iwange man i ngeye, man ecopu nyai kende kende ku bedo piny iwi tyende ma ucungo; ento ngwec ma nyai man ma tek pa yei copo cidho bor mi turo yei, kum bor pa the cen epe ka lac pa yei epe. Tim kende spread angle pa poles aryo, man icopo cungo gi ngo. I ngeye, kendu, ecopu bedu ngo i wang dhanu mi ng'om, ecopu bedu ngo, awacu, pi jatel wi mony maeni ma ucungo iwi cinge nen ka eketho cinge ikum piny moko ci ku cinge; andha, calu lanyuth pa bedo ku twero pare gire, etingo cinge i cinge; ento nyalo bedo ni cinge dit man ma pek, etingo gi keca pi ballast. ( Calu ma jukoro i Golden Inn. )
