CHAPTER 132. The Symphony.

Ubedo ceng maleng macol. Pol pa yamu ku nam ubedu tek ni poko kindgi i azure ma upong ku piny ceke; kende, yamu ma dhanu paru ubedo maleng man mayom, ku nen pa dhaku, man nam ma tek man ma calu pa coo ubedo ku yamo mabor, matek, man ma rii, calu kore pa Samson i nindo pare.

Kae, man keca, malu, ucungo iwi bwom ma rangi pa pee pa winy ma nok, ma rangi migi upe; eni ubedu paru maber pa yamo pa mon; ento i nam ma thuc, piny mabor i nam macol, leviathans ma tek, rec ma julwongo ni sword-fish, man sharks; man eni ubedu paru ma tek, ma nyayu lworo, man ma nyayu nek pa nam ma coo.

Ento kadi nya ebedo tung tung i iye, tung tung ne ubedo kende i tipo man tipo woko; gin ario ne nen calu acel; ubedu kende kende nico ku dhaku, calu ma juweco pire, re ma poko kind gi.

I polo, calu rwoth man rwoth, ceng nen calu emiyo yamu ma mwol ni nam ma tek man ma mol; kadi bende calu dhaku ni dhaku. Man i thenge mi kor polo, ngwec mayom man ma yeng yeng—ma nen pol kare i equator—unyutho gen ma ber, ma nyayu lworo, lworo ma ber, ma nyaku ma jacan umiyu kore.

Jutweyo man juwiro; gnarled man knotted ku wrinkles; ma tek man ma rwinyo ngo; wange ma rieny calu makaa, ma podi rieny i buru pa nyoth; Ahab ma ling ucungu iwi ceng maleng; tingo wic pare ma nyai ma nyaku ma nyaku ma leng i polo.

Oh, nyathin ma tho ngo, man bedo maleng pa azure! Lei ma nen ungo ma tye ku bwome ma giwotho i ngete mwa! Nyathin maber pa yamu man polo! kite ma wubedo ku wic ikum peko pa Ahab macon! Ento aneno Miriam ku Martha ma nyathin, elves ma wang gi ngiero, ma giwotho ku won ot migi ma con; tuko ku circle pa singed locks ma udongo iwi dhugola pa obwongo pare.

Ka edaru kadho iwi yei moth moth kud i scuttle, Ahab ucungo iwi ngete man eneno kite ma tipo pare i pii upoto kudu man upoto i wange, kite ma etimo kero ni turo pii. Ento nguce maber i yamu ma leng eca nen calu eweko, pi kare manok, piny ma rac ma i cwinye. Yamu ma kud anyonga, ma kud anyonga, polo ma leng, ugam umuke man umuke; ng'om pa min ot, ma ubedu ku kura ma rac—ma jukwero—dong uketho cinge ma ku mer i wiye, man nen calu ebekok kud anyonga iwie, calu iwi ngati acel, nia kadi nang ebedo ku paru man timo marac, ecopo nwangu i adunde pare ni laru man ni mio mugisa. Kud ithe kofi pare ma ucungo, Ahab ubolo pi wange i nam; kadi bende ngom mi Pacific zoo ubedu ku lonyo ma rom ku ma nok eca.

Starbuck uneno jadit ne; nene, kite ma ecungo kudu iwi ngete; man nen calu ewinjo i adunde pare mandha ywak ma rwinyo ma wok kud i kind kwiocwiny ma ubedu i ngete. Ku bedo moth kara kud emul kude, nyo kud enen, edok ecidho i bange, man ecungu keca.

Ahab uloko wange.

“Starbuck!”

"Jadit."

"Oh, Starbuck! etiye yamu ma mol, man polo ma nen maleng. I ceng ma kumeno-ma leng akeca calu eni-anego rec ma julwongo ni whale para mir acel--awobi-harpooneer mi oro apar wi abora! Oru pyer angwen—pier angwen—pier angwen mukadho!—ma ukadhu! Oru pyer angwen mi mako rec ma rwinyo! oro pyerangwen mi bedo kende, man pi oro madwong! nam ma ling ling! kisa kud i ngom macol ma epe—oh, olo! cinge ma kubang ceng, man eturo mugati manyen mi ng'om ni kor, nam mabor, kud i bang' dhaku ma nyaku ma anyomo i ngei oro pyerabic, man awotho i yei pi Cape Horn ceng' ma lubo, man aweko kende kende ng'et cing'a—dhaku ma cware tho? en, Starbuck; man i ngeye, lworo, lworo, rimo ma lyel man wange ma lyel, ma, pi nyai elufu acel, jadwong Ahab ubedo ka lubo tok lei pare—ma rac uloyu dhanu!—aye, aye ango ma jamutoro—ma jamutoro—pirango, pirango! cing ikum yei, man cuma, man lance? Nen, Starbuck ubedo tek nenedi, nia ku tic ma tek ma atingo, tyend acel ma jacan ucikere ni bedu ma jukwanyu kud i thenge para? ma dit, ma dit, Starbuck? ng’atu m’utii kude? Cul! cungo ceng’ini i bang’a, Starbuck; wek akenen wang’ dhanu; eber ma sagu yungu i nam; ber m’usagu zoo pi nigamu Mungu. Ku ng’om; ku wang’ ma wang’u ijo! eno tie glasi mi magic, dhanu; Ne aneno chiega gi nyathina e wang’a. Ungo ngo; bedo i board, i board!’—ma nwang’u fodi atimo ngo; saa ku jumange Ahab ucungo ikum Moby Dick. Peko eca bibedo peri ngo. Ungo, ungo ku pacu mabor ma aneno i wange!

“Oh, Kapitan para! Kapitan para! tipo maber! cwinya ma dit, i ngei lembe zo! pirango ma ngati moko copo lubo tok rec ma jukwero! Wek wadok i pii ma rac eni! wek wadok i pacu! Dhaku man nyathin de, gitiye pa Starbuck—dhaku man nyathin pa umego pare, nyamin, juma wadi, calu dhaku man nyathin ma jumaru; mitu, oro pa won ot!

"Gitiye, gitiye. Aneno gi-i ceng moko mi cwir i uthieno. I saa maeni-ee, etiye nindo pare mi diewor kawoni-awobi ne ucere ku tego cwiny; ebedo piny i kavutu; man min ot pare uwacu ire pira, pi ngati ma camu dhanu; kite ma atiye woko iwi pii ma thuc, ento abidok kendo ni myel kude kendu."

"'Eni Mary para, Mary para en gire! Eciko nia nyathin para, kugweno kugweno, jubi tingo iwi got ni neno yei pa wone! Eyo, eyo! dong epe! edaru! wabecidho i Nantucket! Bi, Kapten para, som woko, man wek wacidhi cen! Nen, nen! wang awobi kud i wang dirisa!"

“Eni ango, piny ango ma nyinge mbe, ma nyang ikume ngo, piny ma rwinyo ngo, rwoth man rwoth ma jamutoro, man rwoth ma rac man ma timo lembe marac ung'olo ira; nia i kum mar man yeny ceke mi dhanu, abedo ka diyo cwinya, man abedo ka pongu, man abedo ayika ni timo lembe ma ber i cwinya, kadi nang Ahab, Ahab? Ento ka ceng madit epe ku tic pare gire; Ku polo, ngati, wabe lokore i ng'om eni, calu windlass, man kare ceke, nen polo ma ngieru, man nam ma winjere ngo! juwothe i bar? CHAPTER 133. The Chase—First Day.

I dyewor eca, i dye ceng, kinde ma jadit-calu ma etimo kare ku kare-uwok kud i scuttle ma ebedo i iye, man ecidho i pivot-hole pare, edham ewotho wange woko ku tego, ma eyubu yamu mi nam calu gwok pa yei ma rieko timo, i kare ma ebedu kago ku cula moko ma rac. En uwacu ni rec madit ucikere ni bedu ceng. I ngeye nyanok, nguce ma ukoc, saa moko mabor ma juwodho kud i sperm whale makwo, ugam unyai ni dhanu ceke; man ngati moko mbe ma ung'eyo nia, ingey neno compass, man i ngeye dog-vane, man ingeye nwangu ka ma nguce ucwale kudu, Ahab ungolo nia julok yo pa yei nyanok, man juyik sail.

Cik ma tek ma ucungo iwi wotho eni ubedo maleng i diewor, ku neno pii mabor ma ucungo iwi nam ma ucungo iwange, ma leng calu moo, man ma nen calu pii ma ucungo i ngete, lanyuth ma nen calu cuma ma ucungo iwi pii ma mol, i dhu pii ma thuc.

"Man the mast-heads! Lwong cing dhanu zo!"

Ku dwand ma tek ku cing adek ma juketho iwi forecastle deck, Daggoo ucwalu ju ma gibe nindo ku cing gi ma tek ma nen calu gibewok kud i scuttle, ci ndhundhu ne giwok ku kendi migi i cing gi.

“Ineno ango?” Ahab owacu, ma etingo wange malo.

“Gin moko epe, epe rwoth!” ubedo dwand ma ucungo piny i dwoko.

“T'gallant sails!—stunsails! piny man malo, man ku thenge ario zoo!”

Ka yei zo ucung, edaru weko cinge mi kwo, ma jugwoku pi ywaye i wi meli madit pa rwoth; man i kare manok kende gibedu ka tingo kude, ikare ma, podi etiye kende kende ario iwi adek pa yo malo, man podi eneno anyim kud i kind yei madit man yei ma malu, etingo koko ma calu gull i polo. "Eni eywak!—eni eywak! Ebedo calu got macol! Etiye Moby Dick!"

Ku koko ma nen calu dhanu adek ma gineno woko giwinjo, coo ma gini iwi yei gi ringo i rigging ni neno rec ma julwongo ni whale ma gibedu ka lubo tokgi pi kare malac. Ahab dong edaru nwangu kabedo pare mir ajiki, tyende moko malu pa ju mukende, Tashtego ucungu piny pare iwi wi yei ma malu, kara wic pa ja India ne ubedu marom ku tyende pa Ahab. Nicaku iwi bor eni, rec ma julwongo ni whale dong nen mairo moko nyo ma kadhu iwange, i dhu nam moko ci ma enyuthu kore ma malu ma rieny, man kare ku kare ebolo dhoge ma ling i polo. Ni ju ma giwotho i nam ma yio ngo, nen calu pii ma ling ma gineno con i nam Atlantic man Indian Oceans.

“Man ngati moko ope ma uneno con?” Ahab owacu, ma ukwayu dhanu ma gibedo i ngete.

"Anene i saa marom, jadit, ma Captain Ahab uneno, man akok," Tashtego uwacu.

"Ungo i saa marom; epe marom—ungo, doubloon ubedo para, Fate uketho doubloon ira. An kende; ngati moko mbe ikindwu ma copo tingo White Whale mir acel. Efoyo!—ewoyo!—ewoyo! Kuca kendu!—kuca kendu!" ekok, ku dwal ma bor, ma rii, ma rwinyo, ma rwate ku rwom ma malu pa jets ma nen pa whale. "Ebeci goyo! I stunsails! Piny iwi-galant-sails! Cung inget yei adek. Mr. Starbuck, poi, bed iwi yei, man igwok yei. Helm keca! Luff, luff a point! So; steady, man, steady! There go flukes! Ungo, ungo; pii macol kende! Yei macol zo utiye keca? Cung inget, Mr. Starbuck. Starbuck; piny, piny,—pio pio, pio pio! man epodho kud i polo nitundo i wi yei.

"Ebecidho yo malu, jadit," Stubb owacu, "bor kudwa; podi eneno ngo yei."

"Bed ngati ma ling, ngati! Cung inget cinge! Cung piny matek!—cung matek! Yer!—Yer!—Ento; ber eno! Yei, yei!"

Ndhu ndhu yei zo ento pa Starbuck juweko; yei mi yei zo ucungu—yei yei zo ubewothu; ku ngwec ma tek, ma ucungo yo piny; man Ahab utelo wi acaki. Lero macol, ma nen pa tho uketho wang Fedallah ma rwinyo; timo marac ugam unyai dhoge.

Calu winyo ma julwongo ni nautilus shells, ler migi ma nyap ugam uringo i nam; ento moth moth kende re ma gicokiri ceng ku judhogi. Kinde ma gicokiri i bange, nam ubedo ka medere mayom; nen calu ebe goyo carpet iwi pii; nen calu podho mi dyeceng, uketho enyai. I ngeye, jadwar ma ling eca bino kago ku lei pare ma engeyo ngo, ma dul pare ma nen maleng nen maleng, ubewotho iwi nam calu piny moko kende, man ubemedere ni bedo i dul mabecu, macol, macol. Eneno wic ma dit, ma ucungo iwi wic ma ucungo woko. I wange, mabor iwi pii ma mol ma Turkey-rugged, tipo ma tar ma ler uwok kud i wiye ma lac, ma calu cak, wer ma mol ma lubo tipo ne; man ingeye, pii macol ubedo ka mol iwi got ma mol pa wake pare ma ucungo; man ku cinge ario zoo, pii ma ryeny ucungo man umel ingete. Ento eni jutur kendu ku tyend winyi ma yot pa winy mapol ma giwotho iwi nam, ma giloko ku ngwec migi maber; man calu flag-staff moko ma ucungu iwi wi yei ma jugoro ku rangi mi argosy, yen mabor ento ma utur pa lance ma nyen ma ucungo iwi ngeye pa white whale; man ikind kare moko acel ikin pol pa winy ma tyende mayom ucungo, man ucungo iwi rec, ucungo ling man ucungo iwi yen eni, yuk wigi ma bor ucungo calu pennons.

Anyonga ma mwol—mwol ma dit mi yom cwiny i ngwec, uketho gliding whale. Ungo nyathi thwol ma tar ma nyinge Jupiter ma ubewotho ku Europa ma ucungo iwi tunge ma leng; wange ma leng, ma nen yo piny ma neno nyaku; ku yei ma leng ma nyayu anyonga, ma ucungo iwi yei mi nyom i Crete; epe Jove, epe rwoth madit ma malu! ukadhu White Whale ma juyike maber akeca.

I thenge acel acel ma yot-ma rwate ku pii ma jupoko, ma kende kende eweko en, man edaru mol cen-i thenge acel acel ma ler, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ubolo piny ma nyayu anyonga. Ubedo lembe mi nyang nia dhanu moko ikind ju ma gicayu lei ma gitero man giworo ku kwiocwiny eni, gicaku lwiny ikume; ento edaru nwangu ling eca ento epe pa yamo madit. Ento ling, ling maber, oh, whale! Iwotho iwi, ni dhanu ceke ma gineno pi wang mir acel, kadi nang dhanu adi ma ibenyayu man inyotho con.

Man kumeno, ikind kwiocwiny ma ling pa nam ma ngic, ikind pii ma cing gi ucungu ku anyonga madit, Moby Dick umediri anyim, podi egwoko lworo pa kore pare ma pii, ma egwoko lworo pa dhoge. Ento ndhundhu nica, dul kome mi kore ucungu kud i pii; pi kare manok, kume zo macol ubedo arch ma malu, calu Virginia's Natural Bridge, man ku lworo ma etingo bendera pare i polo, mungu madit unyuthore gire, ukok, man ewok woko kud i wang dhanu. Ka ucungu, man ucungu iwi kor yei, winy nam ma rangi migi tar gibedo iwi nam ma eweko.

Ku oars apeak, man paddles piny, sheets mi sails migi ubedo adrift, yei adek dong ubedo ka ling, ma gibekuru Moby Dick ni dwogo.

"Saa acel," Ahab uwacu, ucungu iwi yei pare; man eneno woko pa kabedu pa whale, yo piny ma rangi pa polo man kabedo ma lac ma nen ungo. Ebedo nyanok kende; kum kendu wange nen calu ubewotho i wiye ma en ewotho i pii. Yamo dong udoko maleng; nam ucaku pong.

"Winy!—winy!" Tashtego owacu.

I lembe ma bor pa ju India, calu ka winyo ma julwongo ni herons utingo cinge, winy ma rangi migi tar kawoni gibe ringo ikum yei pa Ahab; man ikare ma romo yards manok ecaku ngwec iwi pii keca, ka ngwec iwi pii, ku koko ma kud anyonga, ma ku genogen. Neno migi ubedo maleng uloyu pa dhanu; Ahab unwangu ngo lanyuth moko i nam. Ento ndhundhu kinde ma eneno piny man piny i iye, eneno piny moko makwar ma dit ungo ma sagu weasel makwar, ku ngwec ma tek ma ucungo, man ma ube medere, nitundo ma elokore, man ecaku nen kamaleng dul aryo ma bor ma rangi migi tar, ma rieny, ma ucungo malu kud i thenge ma piny ma nen ungo. Ubedo Moby Dick ma dhoge oyabere man dhoge ma oyabere; dul pare malac, macol podi ube rwate ku blue pa nam. Dhoge ma rieny ubedo piny i the yei calu liel ma dhugola ma oyabere; man emiyo yei acel ku steering oar pare, Ahab uloko yei ne cen kud i nen pa piny ma dit eni. I ngeye, elwongo Fedallah ni loko kabedo kude, ecidho anyim i dhu yei, man etingo harpoon pa Perth, ewacu ni jutic pare ni mako yei migi man gicung inget yei.

Kawoni, pi the lembe maeni mi loko yei iwi thenge pare, yei, ku genogen, juyike ni nen wi yei ma podi etiye i pii. Ento calu ma enyang ikum lembe eni, Moby Dick, ku ryeko marac ma juyero i kume, eloko kume gire, calu, i saa moko, ebolo wiye ma ucungo piny i the yei.

Ikind man ikind; kud i kind yen acel acel man i cinge acel acel, ebedo kud anyonga pi kare manok, rec ma dit ma julwongo ni whale ubedo iwi ngete, calu rec shark ma camu, moth moth man ma winjo maber etingo thwol pare i dhoge, kara lak ma bor, ma nyap, ma piny ucungo malo i polo, man acel ikin lak ne ucungo i cinge. Piny macol ma rangi pa iye ubedo ikind inches abusiel ku wiye pa Ahab, man utuc malo uloyu. I kura eni, White Whale dong utingo cedar ma nok calu pusi ma rac mandha. Ku wang ma ungiyo ngo Fedallah uneno, man utingo cinge; ento ju ma tiger-yellow gibed gipodho iwi wigi giri pi nwangu stern.

Man kawoni, podi elastic gunwales ario zoo gibe wok man gibe wok woko, calu whale ubedo ka tuko ku yei ma rac i yo ma rac eni; man kud i kume ma ubedu piny i the yei, jucopo ngo ni goye kud i yei, kum yei ne ubedu i iye, calu ma ubedu; man podi yei mukende ucungu, calu ma podi peko moko utimiri ngo, eca ubedo Ahab ma cwinye utur, ma cwinye lith pi bedo ceng pa jadubu pare, ma uketho ebedo kwo man epe ku kony i cinge ma edagu; ku lworo ikum lembe eni zo, emaku cogo mabor ku cinge ma ling, man etembo matek ni kwanyu kud i cinge. Kaka kawoni etimo kero ma kony ope, lak ucwale kud i bange; gunwales ma nyap ugam ucungo, upoto, man utur, calu lak ario zoo, calu shears madit, ucwale anyim, ugam unyotho yei ne i dul aryo, man ugam ucungo kendu i nam, ikind yei ario ma ucungo iwi pii. Ju maeni giwotho yo piny, thenge ma tur gipodho piny, jutic mi yei ma yo ngeye gicungo i kum gunwales, man gitemo matek ni mako oars pi goyo gi.

I kare ma kwong, ma podi yei utur ungo, Ahab, ngati mir acel ni nyang ikum paru pa rec madit, ku tingo wiye malo, wotho ma eweko yei pi kare ne; i saa maeca cinge utimo kero acel mir ajiki mi cwalu yei woko kud i cinge. Ento kende kende ka epodho i dog rec ma dit, man epodho yo piny ka epodho, yei ne ugam utingo cinge ikum lak; ewotho woko kud i iye, ma en ucungo iwi cinge; man kumeno epodho piny ku wange iwi nam.

Moby Dick ubewotho cen kud i kum lei pare, kawoni ebedo bor nyanok, man ebecwalu wiye ma tar malu man piny ikind pii; man i sawa marom eloko moth moth kume zo; kara kinde ma wiye ma dit ma nyai ucungo—ma romo futi pyer aryo nyo makadhu kud i pii—pii ma kawoni ubedo ka medere, ku pii migi ma gicokiri, uturo iwi pii; ku lworo, gibolo pii migi ma ucungo malu iwi polo.* Pieno, i yamo ma tek, pii ma ucungo iwi pii ma julwongo ni Channel billows udok cen kud i the Eddystone, ku tego cwiny mi turo wi got ku cing gi.

*Timo eni utiye kende kende ni sperm whale. Enwangu nying pare (pitchpoling) ni kum jupore ku rwom ma malu man piny pa whale-lance, i tic ma julwongo pitchpoling, ma juloko con. Ku timo eni, rec madit ma julwongo ni whale umaku onen maber man maleng piny moko ci ma copo bedu ikume.

Ento ndhundhu nicaku dwoko kura pare ma rwinyo, Moby Dick uringo pio pio i dhu yei ma rwinyo; ku cinge ma ucungo iwi pii i ngei pare mi culo kwor, calu nya ebemito etim lembe mange man ma rac uloyu. Neno yei ma utur ne nen calu eketho cwinye lith, calu rimo pa mzabibu man mulberry ma jubolo i wang lyec pa Antiochus i buku pa Maccabees. Ikinde marom, Ahab ugam unyothere i pii pa yibe pa rec ma dit, man ebedo ngati ma goro ma copo dhuny,—kadi bende ecopo bedu iwi pii, kadi i kind pii ma mol ma kumeno; wic pa Ahab ma kony ope nen, calu bubble ma jubolo ma ka lworo moko copo wok. Kud i ngeye pa yei, Fedallah unene ku wange ma lworo man ma mwol; jutic ma ucungo, i thenge mange ma ucungo, gicopo konye ngo; ma sagu ma romo igi ni neno gi gigi. CHAPTER 134. The Chase—Nindu mir aryo.

I diewor, wi yei adek ne juyike kendu.
