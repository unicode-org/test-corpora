SAPTA 43. Hark!

"HIST! Yu harim dispela nois, Cabaco?"

Em i namel was: gutpela mun lait; ol siman i sanap insait long wanpela 'cordon', stat long wanpela 'fresh-water butt' long wasket, i go inap long 'scuttle-butt' klostu long 'taffrail'. Long dispela rot, ol i pasim ol baket long pulapim 'scuttle-butt'. Ol i sanap, long bikpela hap, long ol ples holi bilong 'quarter-deck', na ol i was gut long noken toktok o mekim lek bilong ol i pairap. Long han i go long han, ol baket i go insait long dippela kwaet, tasol ol i brukim long wanwan taim pairap bilong sel, na stedi hum bilong kil i wok long go het oltaim.

Em i stap namel long dispela malolo, we Archy, wanpela bilong ol 'cordon', husat pos bilong em i stap klostu long ol 'after-hatches', i bin tok isi long neiba bilong em, wanpela Cholo, ol toktok antap.

"Hist! yu harim dispela nois, Cabaco?"

"Yu ken kisim baket, Archy? Wanem nois yu minim?"

"Em i stap gen - aninit long ol dua - yu no harim - wanpela kus - em i krai olsem wanpela kus."

"Kus i bagarap! Givim dispela baket i go bek."

"Em i stap gen - em i stap! - i luk olsem tupela o tripela man i slip i tanim tanim, nau!"

"Caramba! yu pinisim, poroman bilong sip, bai yu mekim? Em tripela bisket we yu kaikai long apinun i tanim tanim insait long yu - nogat narapela samting. Lukluk long baket!"

"Yu ken tok wanem samting yu laik mekim, poroman bilong sip; mi gat sap yau."

"Yes, yu dispela man, a?

"Smail i go; bai yumi lukim wanem samting bai kamap. Hark ye, Cabaco, i gat wanpela man i stap daunbilo long 'after-hold' we ol i no bin lukim yet long dek; na mi ting lapun Mogul bilong mipela i save long dispela tu. Mi harim Stubb i tokim Flask, long wanpela moning was, olsem i gat kain samting olsem i stap long win."

"Tish! baket!"





SAPTA 44. Jat.

Sapos yu bin bihainim Kepten Ahab i go daun insait long haus bilong em bihain long bikpela win na ren i bin kamap long nait bihain long dispela bikpela tok orait bilong em wantaim ol kru bilong em, bai yu lukim em i go long wanpela 'locker' insait long 'transom', na kisim wanpela bikpela 'wrinkled roll' bilong ol 'yellowish sea charts', na putim long fran bilong em antap long tebol bilong em. Orait taim em i sindaun long fran bilong en, yu bai lukim em i wok long stadim gut ol kain kain lain na sedo we ai bilong em i bungim; na wantaim isi tasol strongpela pensil trasim ol narapela kos antap long ol spes we bipo i bin stap nating. Long sampela taim, em i save lukluk long ol hip bilong olpela log-buk i stap klostu long em, we ol i raitim ol sisen na ples we, long ol kain kain raun bilong ol kain kain sip, ol i bin kisim o lukim ol 'sperm whale'.

Taim em i wok olsem, hevi 'pewter lamp' we i hangamap long sen antap long het bilong em, i save seksek oltaim wantaim muvmen bilong sip, na oltaim i save tromoi ol lait na sedo bilong ol lain long 'brow' brow bilong em, inap long em i luk olsem taim em yet i wok long makim ol lain na rot long ol 'wrinkled charts', sampela lain na pensil tu i wok long raitim antap long jat bilong pes bilong em we i gat bikpela mak long en.

Tasol i no dispela nait stret we, taim em i stap wanpis insait long haus sel bilong em, Ahab i tingting planti long ol jat bilong em. Klostu olgeta nait ol i save bringim ol i kam ausait; klostu olgeta nait ol i save rausim sampela mak bilong pensil, na ol i save senisim ol narapela. Long wanem, wantaim ol jat bilong olgeta 4-pela solwara i stap long ai bilong em, Ahab i wok long raun long wanpela 'maze' bilong ol 'currents' na 'eddies', wantaim tingting long kamapim moa yet dispela tingting bilong solwara bilong em.

Nau, long husat manmeri i no save gut long pasin bilong ol 'leviathan', em i ken luk olsem em i no gat as bilong en long painim wanpela animal tasol insait long solwara bilong dispela graun. Tasol i no olsem long Ahab, husat i save long ol set bilong olgeta tait na karent; na long dispela rot ol i ken skelim ol 'drifting' bilong kaikai bilong 'sperm whale'; na, tu, tingim ol taim bilong painim em long ol spesel latitude; inap long kamapim ol gutpela tingting, klostu long ol samting we i tru, long wanem taim em i ken stap long dispela o dispela graun long painim abus bilong em.

Tru tumas, em i tru olsem ol 'sperm whale' i save go long ol wara long olgeta taim, olsem na planti man bilong painim abus i ting olsem, ol i ken lukluk gut long em na stadim em long olgeta hap bilong wol; sapos ol i bungim gut ol log bilong wanpela raun bilong olgeta lain weil, orait ol bai painim olsem ol 'migration' bilong 'sperm whale' i wankain olsem bilong ol 'herring-shoals' o 'flights' bilong ol 'swallow'. Long dispela tingting, ol i bin traim long wokim ol gutpela 'migratory charts' bilong 'sperm whale'.*

Na tu, taim ol i wokabaut long wanpela ples bilong kaikai i go long narapela ples, ol 'sperm whale', we sampela 'infallible instinct' i stiaim ol - olsem, sikret save bilong God - planti taim ol i save swim insait long ol 'veins' , olsem ol i kolim ol; ol i go het long rot bilong ol long wanpela solwara wantaim gutpela mak tru, olsem na i no gat wanpela sip i bin ron long rot bilong em, long wanpela jat, wantaim wanpela tait bilong dispela kain gutpela mak tru. Maski, long ol dispela taim, rot we wanpela weil i bihainim i mas stret olsem wanpela 'surveyor', na maski lain bilong go het i mas stap tasol long 'wake' bilong em yet we em i no inap abrusim, tasol 'arbitrary vein' we long ol dispela taim ol i tok em i save swim long en, i save kisim sampela mail longpela (more o less, olsem ol i ting rop i go bikpela o i go bikpela); tasol i no save winim lukluk bilong ol het bilong 'whale-ship', taim em i ron gut long dispela 'magic zone'. Samting bilong dispela em olsem, long ol sampela sisen insait long dispela bikpela hap na long dispela rot, ol i ken painim ol weil we i save raun wantaim bikpela bilip.

Na long dispela as, i no long ol taim tasol, long ol ples we ol man i save gut long en, Ahab inap long bungim abus bilong em; tasol long brukim ol bikpela wara namel long ol dispela graun em inap, long rot bilong art bilong em, putim na taimim em yet long rot bilong em, olsem maski long dispela taim em i no inap stap nating long wanpela bung.

I gat wanpela samting i kamap we long namba wan lukluk i luk olsem i pasim tingting bilong em long tingting kranki tasol em i wok long bihainim gutpela tingting. Tasol ating i no olsem long trupela samting. Maski ol 'gregarious sperm whales' i gat ol sisen bilong ol long sampela hap graun, tasol long jeneral yu no inap tok olsem ol lain we i bin stap long dispela kain 'latitude' o 'longitude' long dispela yia, bai kamap wankain olsem ol lain we ol i bin painim long dispela hap long sisen i go pinis; maski i gat ol narakain na i no gat askim long en we ol samting i no wankain olsem dispela i bin kamap tru. Long olgeta taim, wankain tok, insait long liklik mak tasol, i stret long ol 'solitary' na 'hermits' namel long ol 'sperm whale' we i bikpela pinis. Olsem na maski ol i bin lukim Moby Dick long wanpela yia i go pinis, olsem, long hap ol i kolim Seychelle graun long solwara India, o Volcano Bay long nambis bilong Japan; tasol em i no bihainim, olsem sapos Pequod i go long wanpela bilong ol dispela hap long narapela sisen, em bai bungim em long dispela hap. Olsem tasol, wantaim sampela narapela ples bilong kaikai, we sampela taim em i bin soim em yet. Tasol olgeta dispela i luk olsem ol ples bilong em tasol na ol ples bilong em long solwara, olsem yumi ken tok, i no ol ples bilong em long stap longpela taim. Na we ol i bin toktok long sans bilong Ahab long inapim wok bilong em, ol i bin tok tasol long wanem kain rot, samting i bin kamap pastaim, na ol narapela samting em i bin mekim, pastaim long wanpela taim o ples i kamap, taim olgeta samting inap kamap bai kamap olsem samting inap kamap, na, olsem Ahab i bin tingim, olgeta samting bai kamap bihain long wanpela samting. Dispela taim na ples i bin stap insait long wanpela teknikel toktok - Sisen-long-Lain. Long dispela taim na bihain, long sampela yia bihain long narapela, ol i bin stori long Moby Dick, i save stap long ol dispela wara inap liklik taim, taim san, long raun bilong en long olgeta yia, i save stap nating long wanpela haptaim insait long wanpela sain bilong Zodiac. Long dispela hap tu, planti bilong ol bagarap i bin kamap wantaim waitpela weil; long dispela hap ol weiv i bin stori long ol wok bilong em; i gat tu dispela ples nogut we dispela lapun man i save laikim wanpela samting tasol i bin painim as tingting nogut bilong bekim rong bilong em. Tasol long gutpela tingting na was gut we Ahab i bin tromoi tingting bilong em long dispela wok painim, em i no inap larim em yet long putim olgeta hop bilong em long dispela wanpela bikpela samting we mipela i stori long en antap, maski em i gutpela long ol dispela hop; na tu, long slip bilong em long tok promis bilong em, em i no inap long mekim bel bilong em i stap isi na surukim olgeta wok bilong em.

Nau, Pequod i bin lusim Nantucket long stat bilong Sisen-long-Lain. Nogat wanpela samting inap long mekim na komanda bilong em i ken mekim bikpela rot i go long saut, tupela taim long Cape Horn, na bihain ran i go daun long 60 digri bilong latitud na kamap long ekwetorial Pasifik long taim bilong raun long sip i go long hap. Olsem na, em i mas wet long neks sisen. Tasol ating Ahab i bin makim stret dispela aua bilong Pequod long raun, long tingting bilong dispela kain kala bilong ol samting. Long wanem, wanpela haptaim bilong tripela handet na sikisti-faiv de na nait i stap long ai bilong em; wanpela taim we, em i no save stap isi long nambis, em bai spendim long painim abus; sapos long sans Waitpela Weil, i spendim holide bilong em long ol solwara we i stap longwe long ol ples we em i save kaikai long en, i soim 'wrinkled brow' bilong em long Persian Gulf, o long Bengal Bay, o China Seas, o long ol narapela solwara we ol lain bilong em i save stap long en. Olsem na ol Monsun, Pampas, Nor'-Westers, Harmattans, Trades; olgeta win tasol Levanter na Simoon, inap long sakim Moby Dick i go insait long 'zig-zag' wol-sekol bilong Pequod's 'circumnavigating wake'.

Tasol givim olgeta dispela; tasol, sapos yu lukluk long en long pasin hait na isi, i luk olsem em i no wanpela kranki tingting tasol, dispela; olsem insait long bikpela solwara we i no gat arere, wanpela weil i stap wanpis, maski ol i bungim em, ol i mas ting olsem em inap long luksave long man bilong painim em, maski em i wanpela waitpela mausgras Mufti long ol bikpela rot bilong Constantinople? Yes. Long wanem, waitpela bros bilong Moby Dick, na sno-waitpela 'hump' bilong em, i no inap long stap olsem wanpela samting we i no inap long paulim tingting bilong em. Na mi no bin kaunim weil, Ahab i save tok isi long em yet, olsem bihain long em i lukluk long ol jat bilong em inap longpela taim bihain long namel nait em bai tromoi em yet i go bek long ol driman - kaunim em, na em bai ranawe? Ol bikpela fin bilong em i les, na i bruk olsem yau bilong sipsip i lus! Na hia, longlong tingting bilong em bai ran i go long wanpela resis we i no inap pulim win; inap long taim em i les na tingting planti i kamap long em; na long ples klia bilong dek em bai painim rot bilong kisim bek strong bilong em. Ah, God! wanem kain pen na hevi dispela man i save karim taim em i tingting planti long bekim rong em i no winim. Em i slip wantaim han i pas strong; na em i kirap wantaim ol nil bilong em yet i gat blut long han bilong em.

Planti taim, taim em i lusim 'hammock' bilong em long ol driman bilong nait we i save mekim em i les na i no inap long karim, we, i save kirapim gen ol strongpela tingting bilong em yet long de, na i save karim ol dispela tingting i go het namel long ol 'phrensies' i pait, na i save tanim ol i go i kam insait long 'brain' bilong em, inap long 'throbbing' bilong 'life-pot' bilong em i no inap long karim belhat bilong em; na taim, olsem sampela taim i save kamap, ol dispela hevi bilong spirit insait long em i mekim bodi bilong em i go antap long as bilong en, na wanpela hul i luk olsem i op insait long em, we ol paia na laitning i sut i go antap, na ol spirit nogut i singautim em long kalap i go daun namel long ol; taim dispela hel insait long em yet i yawn aninit long em, wanpela wail krai bai kamap long sip; na wantaim ai i lait strong, Ahab i save lusim rum bilong em, olsem em i ranawe long bet we paia i kukim. Tasol ol dispela, ating, i no olsem ol mak bilong sampela hait wiknes, o pret long tingting bilong em yet, ol i ol klia mak bilong strong bilong en. Long wanem, long kain taim olsem, longlong Ahab, em i man bilong painim waitpela weil we i save tingting planti na i no save bel isi; dispela Ahab husat i bin go long 'hammock' bilong em, em i no dispela man we i bin mekim em i kirap nogut gen. Laspela em i stap oltaim, laip prinsipel o sol insait long em; na long taim bilong slip, em i stap longwe long tingting bilong em, we long ol narapela taim i save yusim em olsem ausait kar o ejen bilong em, em i wok long painim rot bilong ranawe long dispela samting i hat tru, we, long dispela taim, em i no moa stap insait long en. Tasol olsem tingting i no stap sapos em i no wok bung wantaim sol, olsem na em i mas olsem, long sait bilong Ahab, em i givim olgeta tingting na tingting bilong em long wanpela bikpela wok bilong em; dispela as tingting, long rot bilong 'inveteracy' bilong em yet, i fosim em yet long pait long ol god na tewel na kamap olsem wanpela kain samting we em i tingim em yet, na i stap indipenden long em yet. Nogat, inap long stap laip na kukim, taim strong bilong bodi we em i bin stap wantaim, i ranawe wantaim bikpela pret long taim mama i karim pikinini. Olsem na, dispela spirit i gat pen we i kamap long ai bilong bodi, taim wanem samting i luk olsem Ahab i ran i kam aut long rum bilong em, em i wanpela samting i stap nating, em i wanpela samting i no gat fom na em i slip, em i wanpela lait i gat laip, tasol em i no gat wanpela samting bilong kalarim, na olsem na em i wanpela samting i stap nating long em yet. God i helpim yu, lapun man, tingting bilong yu i kamapim wanpela kriesen insait long yu; na em husat i gat strongpela tingting i mekim em i kamap wanpela Prometheus; wanpela 'vulture' i save kaikaim dispela bel oltaim oltaim; dispela tarangau em i wanpela samting em i wokim.





SAPTA 45. Affidavit.

Long wanem samting i stap insait long dispela buk; na, tru tumas, olsem yumi toktok long wanpela o tupela gutpela na gutpela samting long pasin bilong ol 'sperm whale', sapta i go pas, long namba wan hap bilong en, em i bikpela samting olsem yumi bai painim insait long dispela volium; tasol bikpela samting bilong en i mas i gat moa yet na moa save long en, bai ol i ken kliagut long en, na tu, rausim olgeta tingting nogut we ol i no save gut long dispela samting inap kamapim long tingting bilong sampela, long sait bilong trupela samting bilong ol bikpela samting bilong dispela samting.

Mi no laik mekim dispela hap wok bilong mi long gutpela pasin; tasol bai em i amamas long kamapim tingting we mi laikim long rot bilong kolim ol narapela narapela samting, we mi save long en olsem wanpela 'whaleman'; na long ol dispela tok, mi kisim dispela - pinis bilong tingting bai kamap long em yet.

Namba wan: Mi yet i bin save long tripela taim we wanpela weil, bihain long em i kisim wanpela 'harpun', i bin ranawe olgeta; na, bihain long wanpela taim (long wanpela taim bilong tripela yia), ol i bin paitim em gen long wankain han, na kilim em i dai; taim tupela ain, tupela wantaim i gat wankain praivet saifa, ol i bin rausim long bodi. Long taim we tripela yia i bin stap namel long taim bilong tromoi tupela 'harpun'; na mi ting ating em i bin wanpela samting moa long dispela; man husat i bin ronim ol i go long wanpela treding sip long raun i go long Afrika, i go long nambis long hap, joinim wanpela lain bilong painim ol samting, na i go insait tru long kantri, we em i bin raun inap long tupela yia, na planti taim ol snek, ol wel animal, ol taiga, ol poisin miasmas, na olgeta arapela hevi i save bagarapim em ol rijen we yumi no save long en. Long wankain taim, weil em i bin paitim i mas i bin stap long raun bilong em tu; i no gat tupela tingting olsem em i bin raun long wol tripela taim, na i raun long olgeta nambis bilong Afrika; tasol i no gat as bilong en. Dispela man na dispela weil i kam bung gen, na wanpela i winim narapela. Mi tok mi yet, mi bin save long tripela samting i wankain olsem dispela; em long tupela bilong ol mi lukim ol weil i kisim bagarap; na, long namba tu taim ol i paitim em, em i lukim tupela ain wantaim ol mak ol i bin katim long en, na bihain ol i kisim long ol pis i dai pinis. Insait long tripela yia, em i kamap olsem mi bin stap long bot tupela taim, namba wan na laspela taim, na laspela taim mi luksave klia long wanpela kain bikpela 'mole' aninit long ai bilong weil, we mi bin lukim long dispela hap tripela yia i go pinis. Mi tok tripela yia, tasol mi save tru olsem em i winim dispela. Orait, hia i gat tripela samting we mi yet i save long en; tasol mi harim planti ol narapela samting i kamap long ol manmeri husat i no gat gutpela as long rausim tok tru bilong ol long dispela samting.

Namba tu: Em i save gut insait long Sperm Whale Fishery, maski ol manmeri long nambis i no save long en, olsem i bin gat sampela gutpela historikal instans we wanpela kain wel long solwara i bin stap long ol longwe taim na ples we planti manmeri i save long en. Bilong wanem dispela kain weil i kisim dispela mak em i no bikos bodi bilong em i narakain long ol narapela weil; long wanem, maski wanpela 'chance whale' i narakain long dispela samting, ol i pinisim ol narakain pasin bilong em long kilim em, na boilim em i go daun long wanpela wel we i gat bikpela pe tru. Nogat: as bilong dispela em olsem: long ol bikpela ekspiriens bilong wok fiseri i gat wanpela bikpela nem bilong dispela kain weil olsem i bin kamap long Rinaldo Rinaldini, olsem na planti pisman i amamas long luksave long em long rot bilong tatsim tasol ol 'tarpaulin' bilong ol taim ol i painim em i slip long solwara, na ol i no painim wanpela moa saveman. Olsem sampela rabis tewel long nambis we i save long wanpela bikpela man i save belhat, ol i save mekim ol 'salutation' longwe long em long strit, nogut sapos ol i bihainim dispela man i go moa, ol i ken kisim wanpela 'summari thump' long tingting bilong ol.

Tasol i no olsem olgeta wan wan bilong ol dispela biknem weil i bin kisim biknem tasol - Nogat, yu ken kolim olsem em i wanpela biknem long solwara; em i no bin gat nem tasol long laip bilong em na nau em i no inap i dai long ol stori bilong 'forecastle' bihain long em i dai, tasol em i bin kisim olgeta raits, privilej, na distinksen bilong wanpela nem; i gat wankain nem olsem Cambyses o Cæsar. Em i no bin olsem, O Timor Tom! yu biknem leviathan, i gat mak olsem aisbeg, husat i bin hait longpela taim long ol Oriental straits bilong dispela nem, husat ol i save lukim maus bilong em long nambis bilong Ombay? Em i no bin olsem, O New Zealand Jack! yu pret long olgeta krusa we i bin brukim ol 'wake' bilong ol klostu long Tattoo Land? Em i no bin olsem, O Morquan! King bilong Japan, husat i gat bikpela balus ol i tok sampela taim i luk olsem wanpela waitpela diwai kros i sanap antap long skai? Em i no bin olsem, O Don Miguel! yu Chilian whale, i gat mak olsem wanpela lapun bikbel wantaim ol 'hieroglyphics' long baksait bilong en! Long tok klia, hia i gat 4-pela weil we ol sumatin bilong Cetacean History i save gut long en olsem Marius o Sylla long ol saveman bilong bipo.

Tasol dispela i no olgeta. New Zealand Tom na Don Miguel, bihain long ol i bin kamapim bikpela bagarap namel long ol bot bilong ol narapela narapela sip, ol i bin go painim ol, ol i bin painim ol, ol strongpela kepten bilong painim weil i bin ranim ol na kilim ol, na ol i bin apim ol anka bilong ol wantaim dispela samting i stap long ai bilong ol, olsem taim ol i bin go aut long Narragan Woods, Kepten Butler i bin gat olpela tingting bilong em tingting long holim dispela nogut man bilong kilim man Annawon, em i nambawan soldia bilong King Philip bilong India.

Mi no save we mi ken painim wanpela gutpela ples moa long hia tasol, long kolim wanpela o tupela narapela samting, we long mi i luk olsem em i bikpela samting, olsem long ol pepa we ol i prinim i kamapim long olgeta samting olsem stori bilong Waitpela Weil i stret tru, moa yet long bagarap i bin kamap. Long wanem, dispela em i wanpela bilong ol dispela taim we tok tru i nidim bikpela strong olsem rong. Planti manmeri bilong graun i no save long sampela bilong ol gutpela samting bilong wol, olsem na sapos ol i no gat sampela toksave long ol samting bilong bipo na ol narapela samting, long wok bilong kisim pis, ol i ken tingim Moby Dick olsem wanpela bikpela stori nating, o nogut moa na i nogut moa, em i wanpela tok piksa nogut tru na i no inap long karim.

SAPTA 46. Ol tingting.

Maski, em i kukim em long hatpela paia bilong tingting bilong em, Ahab long olgeta tingting na pasin bilong em i bin tingting long kisim Moby Dick; maski em i luk olsem em i redi long lusim olgeta samting bilong em long dispela wanpela laik tasol; maski i olsem, ating em i bin stap longpela taim na em i bin stap longpela taim na em i bin marit tumas long pasin bilong wanpela 'fiery whaleman', na em i no bin lusim wok bilong em long raun long sip. O sapos dispela i no olsem, ol i no laikim ol narapela tingting we i gat bikpela strong moa long em. Ating, sapos yumi tingim 'monomania' bilong em, sapos yumi tok olsem pasin bilong em long bekim rong bilong Waitpela Weil i bin go inap long olgeta 'sperm whale', na sapos em i kilim planti monster moa, sans bilong em i go antap olsem olgeta wan wan 'whale' em i bungim bihain em i no laikim em. Tasol sapos dispela kain tingting i tru tru, i gat sampela moa tingting i stap yet we, maski i no stret tumas wantaim pasin bilong em long bosim, tasol i no inap long kirapim em.

Long inapim tingting bilong em Ahab i mas yusim ol tul; na long olgeta tul we ol i save yusim long sedo bilong mun, ol man i save lusim oda. Em i save, olsem eksampel, olsem maski strong bilong em long sampela samting i winim Starbuck, tasol dispela strong i no karamapim ful man bilong spirit moa olsem pasin bilong bodi tasol i involvim save bilong em; long wanem, long ol manmeri i gat spirit tasol, ol i gat save tasol ol i sanap insait long wanpela kain rilesensip bilong bodi. Bodi bilong Starbuck na laik bilong Starbuck em i bilong Ahab, inap long taim Ahab i holim strong tingting bilong em long tingting bilong Starbuck; yet em i save olsem maski long olgeta dispela samting, nambawan poroman, insait long sol bilong em, i nolaik tru long wok bilong kepten bilong em, na sapos em inap, em bai amamas long rausim em yet long dispela, o bagarapim dispela wok. Ating bai i gat longpela taim pastaim long ol i lukim Waitpela Weil. Long dispela longpela taim Starbuck bai inap long pundaun gen long ol bikhet pasin bilong em long lidasip bilong kepten bilong em, sapos ol i no kamapim sampela samting nating, gutpela tingting, na ol samting i kamap long em. I no dispela tasol, tasol pasin kranki bilong Ahab long rispektim Moby Dick i no bin kamap ples klia moa long bikpela tingting na gutpela tingting bilong em long luksave olsem, long nau, dispela wok painim i mas lusim dispela narakain tingting nogut we i bin putim em; olsem olgeta pret bilong raun i mas stap hait i go insait long ples we i no klia tumas (long wanem, liklik lain man tasol i no gat strongpela tingting na dispela i soim olsem ol i no inap tingim gut ol samting longpela taim na ol i no inap mekim wanpela samting); olsem taim ol i sanap longpela na was long nait, ol opisa na ol man bilong em i mas i gat sampela samting i stap klostu long tingim moa long Moby Dick. Long wanem, maski ol dispela wel kru i bin amamas na strong long tokaut long wok painim bilong em; tasol olgeta seila bilong olgeta kain i save tingting planti na ol i no inap long trastim ol - ol i save stap long kain kain weta ausait, na ol i save pulim win bilong en - na taim ol i holim ol long wanpela samting i stap longwe na i no gat samting long en, maski em i gutpela long laip na laik long pinis bilong en, em i antap long olgeta samting olsem ol wok na wok bilong sotpela taim i mas stopim ol na stopim helt bilong ol des.

Na tu, Ahab i no tingim narapela samting. Long taim bilong strongpela filings ol manmeri i save les long olgeta tingting nogut; tasol ol dispela kain taim i save pinis. Ahab i ting olsem 'permanent constitutional condition' bilong man ol i wokim em i pasin nogut. Tru tumas, White Whale i save kirapim bel bilong ol dispela wel animal bilong mi, na pilai raun long pasin nogut bilong ol i save kamapim wanpela kain pasin bilong givim bel long ol nait, tasol, taim ol i laikim Moby Dick, ol i mas gat kaikai tu bilong ol samting ol i save laikim long olgeta de. Long wanem, ol biknem na strongpela Kruseda bilong bipo tu i no amamas long raun long tupela tausen mail long graun bilong pait long kisim holi matmat bilong ol, na ol i no stilim ol samting, kisim mani, na kisim ol narapela gutpela samting long rot. Sapos ol i bin holim strong wanpela laspela na romantik samting bilong ol - dispela laspela na romantik samting, planti tumas bai i lusim wantaim bel nogut. Mi no inap rausim ol dispela man, Ahab i ting, olgeta hop bilong kisim mani - yes, mani. Ol i ken tok nogut long mani nau; tasol larim sampela mun i go pinis, na nogat promis bilong en long ol, na bihain dispela wankain mani i stap isi olgeta i wok long bikhet long ol, dispela wankain mani bai i no longtaim na Ahab i kisim mani.

Na tu, i no gat narapela tingting bilong lukautim em yet we i stret long Ahab yet. Ahab i bin tokaut hariap long as tingting bilong raun bilong Pequod, na nau em i luksave olsem, taim em i mekim olsem, em i bin opim em yet long dispela sas bilong kisim ol samting bilong graun; na wantaim gutpela pasin bilong no kisim strafe, long sait bilong pasin na lo, ol wokman bilong em sapos ol i laik mekim olsem, na ol i gat save long mekim dispela wok, ol i ken sakim olgeta samting moa long bihainim tok bilong em, na tu, ol i ken kisim dispela tok long em. Maski ol i tok olsem ol i bin kisim ol samting bilong ol narapela, na ol samting we inap kamap sapos dispela tingting i kamap, ating Ahab i bin tingting planti long lukautim em yet. Dispela proteksen i ken stap tasol long 'brain' na 'heart' na han bilong em yet, na em i save lukluk gut long olgeta liklik samting we i save kamap long win we ol wokman bilong em i ken kisim.

Long olgeta dispela as, na ol narapela ating i no inap long toktok long en, Ahab i lukim klia olsem em i mas bihainim yet as tingting bilong raun bilong Pequod; bihainim olgeta pasin bilong yusim; na i no dispela tasol, tasol fosim em yet long soim olgeta gutpela laik bilong em long mekim wok bilong em.

Maski i olsem, planti taim ol i save harim nek bilong em i singaut long tripela 'mast-heads' na tokim ol long lukluk gut, na noken lusim ripot bilong wanpela 'porpoise'. Dispela was i no bin stap longpela taim nating.





SAPTA 47. Man bilong wokim mat.

Em i wanpela klaut na hatpela apinun; ol siman i les long sindaun long ol dek, o lukluk i go insait long wara i gat kala lid. Queequeg na mi i bin wok liklik long samapim samting ol i kolim 'sword-mat', bilong pasim bot bilong mipela moa. Olgeta samting i stap isi na i stap isi na i no gat as bilong en, na i gat planti tingting i stap long win, olsem na olgeta wan wan seila i stap isi i luk olsem ol i laik i stap olsem ol yet we ol i no inap lukim.

Mi bin wokman o pes bilong Queequeg, taim mi bisi long mat.
