SAPTA 32. Cetology.

Mipela i sanap pinis long solwara i go daun tru; tasol klostu nau bai yumi lus long ol bikpela ples bilong en we i no gat nambis na i no gat ples hait. Pastaim long dispela i kamap; pastaim long 'weedy hull' bilong Pequod i rol klostu wantaim ol 'barnacled hull' bilong 'leviathan'; long stat em i gutpela long lukluk long wanpela samting we i klostu tru long kisim gutpela luksave long ol spesel revelesen na ol tok piksa bilong olgeta kain samting we bai kamap bihain.

Em i sampela 'systematized exhibition' bilong ol 'whale' long ol bikpela lain bilong en, we nau mi laik putim long ai bilong yu. Tasol em i no isi wok. Klasifikesen bilong ol samting insait long wanpela 'chaos', i no liklik samting, em i stap hia. Harim wanem samting ol gutpela na nupela atoriti i bin putim daun.

"I no gat wanpela brens bilong Zoology i stap insait long en olsem dispela we ol i kolim Cetology," Kepten Scoresby, AD 1820 i tok.

"Em i no tingting bilong mi, sapos mi gat strong, long go insait long wok painimaut long trupela rot bilong tilim ol 'cetacea' i go long ol grup na famili. * * * Ol man bilong raitim stori bilong dispela animal i paul tru" (sperm whale), Surgeon Beale, AD 1839 i tok.

"No inap long mekim wok painimaut bilong mipela long ol wara we i no inap long kliagut long en." "Vel we i no inap long brukim i karamapim save bilong yumi long ol 'cetacea'." "Wanpela gaden we i gat planti nil i stap long en." "Olgeta dispela inkomplit indikesen tasol i givim pen long mipela ol 'naturalists'."

Olsem na toktok long weil, bikpela Cuvier, na John Hunter, na Lesson, ol dispela lait bilong 'zoology' na 'anatomy'. Tasol, maski i gat liklik save tru, tasol i gat planti buk; na olsem long liklik mak, wantaim 'cetology', o saiens bilong ol weil. Planti man, liklik na bikpela, lapun na nupela, ol man bilong graun na ol man bilong solwara, husat i bin raitim planti o liklik, long dispela weil. Lukluk long sampela:—Ol Man i Raitim Baibel; Aristotle; Pliny; Aldrovandi; Sir Thomas Browne; Gesner; Ray; Linnæus; Rondeletius; Willoughby; Grin; Artedi; Sibbald; Brisson; Marten; Lacépède; Bonneterre; Desmarest; Baron Cuvier; Frederick Cuvier; John Hunter; Owen; Scoresby; Beale; Bennett; J. Ross Browne; man i raitim buk bilong Miriam Coffin; Olmstead; na Rev. T. Cheever. Tasol wanem as tingting olgeta dispela i bin raitim, ol dispela hap tok antap bai soim.

Long ol nem insait long dispela lista bilong ol man i raitim ol wel, ol lain tasol i bihainim Owen i bin lukim ol wel animal i stap laip; na tasol wanpela bilong ol em i wanpela gutpela man bilong paitim 'harpun' na 'whaleman'. Mi minim Kepten Scoresby. Long narapela samting bilong Greenland o rait-wel, em i nambawan atoriti i stap. Tasol Scoresby i no save long wanpela samting na i no tok wanpela samting long bikpela 'sperm whale', sapos yumi skelim wantaim 'Greenland whale' em i klostu i no gutpela long kolim nem bilong em. Na hia yumi ken tok, olsem Greenland whale em i wanpela man i save kisim sia king bilong ol solwara. Em i no bikpela tru long ol weil. Tasol, bikos long longpela taim ol tok bilong em i bikpela samting, na bikpela pasin bilong no save we, inap long 70 yia i go pinis, i bin putim dispela 'sperm-whale' we i bin gutpela o ol i no save long en, na dispela pasin bilong no save i stap yet long olgeta hap tasol sampela liklik 'scientific retreat' na 'whale-port'; dispela 'usurpation' i bin pinis olgeta. Sapos yu tingim klostu olgeta tok bilong 'leviathanic' long ol bikpela man bilong raitim ol poem bilong bipo, bai yu inap save olsem 'Greenland whale', i no gat wanpela birua bilong em, em i king bilong ol solwara. Tasol taim i kamap pinis long kamapim wanpela nupela toksave. Dispela em Charing Cross; harim! gutpela manmeri olgeta,—ol i rausim weil bilong Grinlan,—bikpela sperm weil nau i stap king!

I gat tupela buk tasol i stap we i giaman olsem ol i putim 'sperm whale' i stap laip long ai bilong yu, na long wankain taim, long longwe tru ol i winim dispela traim. Ol dispela buk em bilong Beale na Bennett; tupela long taim bilong ol em ol dokta bilong katim man long ol Inglis Saut-Si weil-sip, na tupela i stretpela na gutpela man. As samting we i tatsim 'sperm whale' we yu ken painim insait long ol volium bilong ol em i liklik tru; tasol inap long mak bilong en, em i gutpela tru, maski planti taim em i stap long tok klia bilong saiens tasol. Tasol, nau yet, ol 'sperm whale', maski ol i bilong saiens o bilong ol poet, ol i no stap gut insait long wanpela buk na nius. Antap long olgeta narapela weil we ol i save painim, laip bilong em i no gat rait long en.

Nau ol kain kain weil i nidim wanpela kain 'comprehensive classification' we planti manmeri i save laikim, sapos em i wanpela isipela 'outline' tasol bilong nau, bihain ol wokman bilong bihain bai pulapim olgeta dipatmen bilong en. Long wanem, i no gat wanpela man i gutpela moa long mekim dispela samting, olsem na mi givim ol wok bilong mi yet. Mi no inap promis long mekim wanpela samting i gutpela olgeta; bikos olgeta samting bilong man we ol i ting em i gutpela olgeta, em i mas i gat asua long dispela as tasol. Mi no inap giaman long mekim wanpela liklik tok klia long bodi bilong ol kain kain animal, o - long dispela hap - long planti tok klia. As tingting bilong mi long hia em long projektim draf bilong wanpela sistematisesen bilong 'cetology'. Mi akitek, i no man bilong wokim haus.

Tasol em i wanpela hatpela wok; nogat wanpela nomol man bilong skelim ol pas insait long Pos-Opis i wankain olsem dispela. Long painim ol i go daun long as bilong solwara; long gat han bilong yu namel long ol bikpela samting tru, ol 'ribs', na 'pelvis' bilong dispela graun; dispela em i wanpela samting bilong pret. Mi wanem samting na mi mas traim long pulim nus bilong dispela 'leviathan'! Ol tok bilas nogut insait long Jop i ken mekim mi pret. Em bai mekim wanpela kontrak wantaim yu? Lukim hop bilong em i samting nating! Tasol mi bin swim long ol laibreri na sel long solwara; Mi bin wok wantaim ol weil wantaim ol dispela han we yu ken lukim; Mi tok tru; na bai mi traim. I gat sampela samting bilong stretim.

Namba wan: Dispela samting we i no klia na i no stap gut long dispela saiens bilong Cetology i stap long 'vestibule' we i soim olsem, long sampela hap em i stap yet olsem wanpela samting we i no klia tumas sapos wanpela wel em i wanpela pis. Insait long buk bilong em System of Nature, AD 1776, Linnæus i tok, "Mi save separetim ol wel long ol pis." Tasol long save bilong mi yet, mi save olsem i go inap long yia 1850, ol sak na ol 'shad', ol 'alewives' na ol 'herring', egensim tok bilong Linnæus, ol i bin painim yet ol i brukim graun bilong ol wankain solwara wantaim Leviathan.

Ol as tingting bilong Linnæus long rausim ol weil long solwara, em i tok olsem: "Long wanem hat bilong ol i gat tupela 'bilocular', lewa bilong ol, ai bilong ol i save muv, yau bilong ol i gat hul, 'penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem'," na las tru, "ex lege naturæ jure merittoque." Mi bin givim olgeta dispela samting long ol pren bilong mi Simeon Macey na Charley Coffin, bilong Nantucket, tupela i bin poroman bilong mi long wanpela raun, na tupela i wanbel long tingting olsem ol as tingting i no inap tru. Charley i tok nogut olsem ol i 'humbug'.

Yu mas save olsem, mi lusim olgeta tok pait, mi kisim gutpela tingting bilong bipo olsem weil em i wanpela pis, na mi singautim holi Jona long sapotim mi. Dispela bikpela samting i kamap pinis, narapela poin em, long wanem samting weil i narakain long ol narapela pis. Antap, Linnæus i givim yu ol dispela samting. Tasol long sotpela tok, em ol dispela: lewa na hatpela blut; tasol, olgeta narapela pis i no gat lewa na i gat kol blut.

Neks: hau bai yumi makim weil, long ol samting bilong em i stap ausait, na bai yumi ken kolim em long olgeta taim bihain? Long sotpela tok, orait, wanpela weil em i wanpela pis we i gat maus na tel bilong en i stap stret . Em i stap. Maski ol i sotim, dispela mining em i kamap long rot bilong mediteisen i go bikpela. Tasol laspela tok bilong dispela mining i strong moa yet, wankain olsem namba wan tok. Klostu olgeta manmeri i mas luksave olsem olgeta pis we ol man bilong ples i save long en i no gat wanpela flatpela, tasol wanpela 'vertical' o 'up-and-down' tel. Tasol, namel long ol pis we i save 'spouting' tel bilong en, maski em i gat wankain seip, em i save stap longpela taim.

Long dispela mining antap bilong wanem samting em weil, mi no inap rausim wanpela animal bilong solwara we ol saveman bilong Nantucket i bin luksave long en olsem weil; na tu, long narapela sait, noken bungim wantaim em wanpela pis we ol i bin tingim olsem ol narapela pis.* Olsem na, olgeta liklik, spit, na horisontel tel pis i mas stap insait long dispela graun-plan bilong Cetology. Nau, ol bikpela lain bilong olgeta weil i kam.

*Mi save olsem i kam inap nau, ol pis ol i kolim Lamatins na Dugongs (Pig-pis na Sow-pis bilong ol Bokis bilong Nantucket) em planti saveman bilong ol samting bilong ples i save putim ol insait long ol weil. Tasol bikos ol dispela pik-pis i save mekim nois, na ol i no laikim tumas, planti taim ol i save hait long maus bilong ol wara, na kaikai gras we i wet, na moa yet bikos ol i no save maus, mi tok nogat long ol olsem ol weil; na ol i soim ol paspot bilong ol long lusim Kingdom bilong Cetology.

Namba wan: Long bikpela bilong ol, mi brukim ol weil i go long tripela bikpela BUK (yu ken brukim i go long ol SAPT), na ol dispela bai kliagut long olgeta, liklik na bikpela.

I. FOLIO WEIL; II. OCTAVO WHALE; III. DUODECIMO WHALE.

Olsem kain bilong FOLIO mi soim Sperm Whale ; bilong OCTAVO, Grampus ; bilong DUODECIMO, Porpoise .

OL FOLIOS. Namel long ol dispela mi putim ol dispela sapta:—I. Sperm Whale ; II. Rait Weil ; III. Fin-Back Whale ; IV. Hump-backed Whale ; V. Razor Back Whale ; VI. Salfa Bottom Whale .

BUK I. ( Folio ), SAPTA I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Dispela weil, namel long ol Inglis bilong bipo ol i save kolim olsem Trumpa weil, na Physeter weil, na Anvil Headed weil, em i Cachalot bilong nau bilong ol Frans, na Pottsfich bilong ol Jeman, na Macrocelus bilong ol Words. Em i, i no gat tupela tingting, bikpela man tru long wol; em i nambawan strongpela weil long bungim; em i nambawan tru; na laspela, em i nambawan samting tru insait long wok bisnis; em i wanpela tasol we ol i kisim dispela gutpela samting, 'spermaceti', long en. Olgeta samting bilong em bai, long planti ol narapela hap, ol i ken tok moa long en. Em i bikpela samting wantaim nem bilong em we nau mi mas mekim. Sapos yumi tingim long sait bilong tok ples, em i no gat as bilong en. Sampela handet yia i go pinis, taim ol i no save long 'Sperm whale' long pasin bilong em yet, na taim ol i kisim oil bilong em long pis we i stap long solwara; long dispela taim, i luk olsem ol i ting olsem 'spermaceti' i kam long wanpela animal we i wankain olsem wanpela animal we ol i save kolim long Inglan olsem Greenland o Right Whale. Em i tingting tu, olsem dispela wankain 'spermaceti' em i dispela 'quickening humor' bilong Greenland Whale we namba wan silabel bilong dispela wod i makim tru. Long dispela taim, tu, spermaceti i sot tru, ol i no yusim bilong givim lait, tasol ol i yusim olsem wanpela marasin na marasin tasol. Em i bilong kisim tasol long ol dokta bikos nau yu save baim wanpela auns bilong 'rhubarb'. Taim, olsem mi ting, bihain long taim, ol i save long trupela pasin bilong 'spermaceti', ol dil i holim yet nem bilong en pastaim; i no gat tupela tingting long apim veliu bilong en long rot bilong wanpela tingting we i narakain tru long sot bilong en. Na olsem dispela nem i mas kamap long weil we dispela 'spermaceti' i bin kamap long en.

BUK I. ( Folio ), SAPTA II. ( Right Whale ).—Long wanpela samting, dispela em i nambawan 'leviathan', long wanem, em i namba wan 'leviathan' we ol man i save painim em. Em i kamapim wanpela samting we ol i save kolim 'whalebone' o 'baleen'; na oil we ol i save kolim olsem "whale oil," em i wanpela samting we i no gutpela tumas long wok bisnis. Namel long ol man bilong kisim pis, ol i save kolim em long olgeta dispela nem: The Whale; wel bilong Grinlan; Blakpela Weil; Bikpela Weil; Trupela Weil; Rait Weil. I gat planti samting i no klia long aidentiti bilong ol dispela kain animal we planti manmeri i bin kisim baptais long en. Orait, wanem samting em i weil, we mi putim insait long namba tu kain bilong ol Folios bilong mi? Em i Bikpela Mysticetus bilong ol saveman bilong Inglan; Greenland Whale bilong ol Inglan welman; Baleine Ordinaire bilong ol French whalemen; ol Growlands Walfish bilong ol Sweden. Em i dispela weil we long moa long tupela handet yia i go pinis ol Netelan na Inglan i bin wok long painim em long ol solwara Aktik; em i weil we ol pisman bilong Amerika i bin painim longpela taim long solwara India, long Brazil Banks, long Nor' Wes Kos, na long ol narapela hap bilong wol, we ol i kolim olsem Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Sampela i giaman olsem ol i lukim wanpela samting i narapela long 'Greenland whale' bilong ol Inglan na 'right whale' bilong ol Amerika. Tasol ol i wanbel tru long olgeta bikpela samting bilong ol; na tu, i no gat wanpela samting i kamap yet we ol i ken kamapim bikpela luksave long en. Em i long rot bilong ol liklik hap we i no gat pinis bilong en we i kamap long ol samting we i no klia tumas, na sampela dipatmen bilong 'natural history' i kamap hat tru. Ol bai toktok long 'right whale' long sampela taim, wantaim tingting long painimaut 'sperm whale'.

BUK I. ( Folio ), SAPTA III. ( Fin-Back ).—Aninit long dispela het mi tingim wanpela bikpela animal we, long ol kain kain nem olsem Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, na Long-John, ol i bin lukim long klostu olgeta solwara na em i wanpela weil we ol pasindia i save tok long longwe balus bilong em we ol pasindia i save brukim solwara Atlantik, long ol 'packet-tracks' bilong New York. Long longpela bilong em, na long 'baleen' bilong em, Fin-back i luk olsem 'right whale', tasol em i no bikpela tumas, na kala bilong en i lait moa, klostu long oliv. Bikpela lips bilong em i soim olsem wanpela 'cable', we i kamap long ol bikpela 'wrinkles' we i tanim tanim na i slek. Bikpela samting bilong em, em 'fin', we em i kisim nem bilong em long en, planti taim em i wanpela samting we ol manmeri i save lukim. Dispela 'fin' em i olsem tripela o foapela fit longpela, na i gro stret long baksait bilong baksait, i gat wanpela 'angular' seip, na i gat sap tru arere bilong en. Maski sapos i no gat liklik hap bilong dispela animal i stap ples klia, dispela 'fin' we i stap wanpis bai, sampela taim, yu ken lukim i kamap ples klia long antap bilong wara. Taim solwara i stap isi liklik, na i gat liklik mak bilong ol 'spherical ripples', na dispela 'gnomon-like fin' i sanap na tromoi ol sedo antap long ol liklik hap graun, yumi ken ting olsem wara i raunim em i luk olsem wanpela 'dial', wantaim stail bilong en na ol 'wavy hour-lines' i stap long en. Long dispela 'Ahaz-dial' planti taim sedo i save go bek. Fin-Back i no save bung wantaim ol narapela. Em i luk olsem em i save heitim ol weil, olsem sampela man i save heitim ol man. Em i sem tru; oltaim yu save stap wanpis; i go antap long wara we i no tingim na i kamap long ol wara we i stap longwe tru na i no gat lait; stretpela na wanpela bikpela jet bilong em i go antap olsem wanpela longpela spia bilong ol man i no laikim man antap long wanpela ples daun; i gat gutpela strong na spit tru long swim, na em i no inap long sakim olgeta samting we man i laik mekim; dispela 'leviathan' i luk olsem em i wanpela 'cain' bilong lain bilong em we ol i bin rausim na i no inap long winim em, na em i gat dispela stail long baksait bilong em. Long wanem em i gat 'baleen' insait long maus bilong em, sampela taim ol i save putim 'Fin-Back' wantaim 'right whale', namel long wanpela 'theoretic species' ol i kolim 'Whalebone whales' , em ol 'whale' wantaim 'baleen'. Long ol dispela weil we ol i kolim 'Whalebone', i luk olsem i gat sampela kain, tasol planti bilong ol i no save tumas long ol. Ol weil i gat bikpela nus na ol weil i gat maus; ol weil i gat het bilong paik; ol weil i bung wantaim; ol 'under-jawed whales' na 'rostrated whales', em ol nem bilong sampela kain weil bilong ol man bilong kisim pis.

Long sait bilong dispela nem bilong "Whalebone whales," em i bikpela samting long tok, olsem maski kain nem olsem i ken helpim yumi long kolim sampela kain weil, tasol em i nating long traim long makim klia Leviathan, we i kamap long baleen, o hump, o fin, o teeth bilong em; maski ol dispela hap o samting i gat mak i luk olsem ol i gutpela long kamapim as bilong wanpela sistem bilong Cetology winim ol narapela bodi i stap narapela narapela, we weil, long kain bilong em, i save soim. Orait olsem wanem? Ol 'baleen', 'hump', 'back-fin', na ol tit; ol dispela em ol samting we ol narakain bilong ol i stap namel long olgeta kain weil, na i no gat tingting long wanem kain straksa bilong ol i stap long ol narapela na bikpela samting moa. Olsem na, 'sperm whale' na 'humpbacked whale', wanwan i gat wanpela 'hump'; tasol long dispela hap pasin bilong wankain i pinis. Orait, dispela wankain 'humpbacked whale' na 'Greenland whale', olgeta wan wan bilong ol i gat 'baleen'; tasol long dispela hap gen wankain pasin i pinis. Na em i wankain tasol long ol narapela hap we mipela i stori long en antap. Long ol kain kain weil, ol i save kamapim ol dispela kain kombinesen we i no stret; o, sapos wanpela bilong ol i stap wanpis, dispela kain pasin bilong stap wanpis i no stret; olsem tru tru long sakim olgeta jeneral metodisasen we i kamap long dispela kain as. Long dispela ston olgeta wan wan bilong ol 'whale-naturalists' i bruk.

Tasol ating yumi ken ting olsem, insait long ol hap insait long weil, insait long bodi bilong em - long dispela hap, yumi ken painim stretpela klasifikesen. Nogat; wanem samting, eksampel, i stap insait long bodi bilong Greenland whale we i luk nais moa long baleen bilong em? Tasol mipela i lukim olsem long 'baleen' bilong em em i no inap long makim stret ol 'Greenland whale'. Na sapos yu go daun insait long bel bilong ol kain kain 'leviathan', bilong wanem yu no inap painim ol 'distinctions' long namba 50 hap we i stap long 'systematizer' olsem ol dispela 'external ones' we yumi kolim pinis. Orait wanem samting i stap yet? nogat narapela samting tasol long holim ol weil long bodi bilong ol, long olgeta liberal volium bilong ol, na strongim bel long makim ol long dispela rot. Na dispela em i 'Bibliographical system' we ol i adoptim; na em wanpela tasol inap long kamap gut, long wanem, em tasol inap long kamap. Long go het.

BUK I. ( Folio ) SAPTA IV. ( Hump Back ).—Planti taim ol i save lukim dispela weil long nambis bilong Amerika long hap not. Planti taim ol i save holim em long dispela hap, na pulim em i go insait long haba. Em i gat bikpela paket long em olsem wanpela man bilong salim ol samting; o yu ken kolim em Elefan na Kasel weil. Maski wanem samting i kamap, nem bilong em i no inap long makim em, long wanem, 'sperm whale' tu i gat wanpela 'hump' maski em i liklik. Oil bilong em i no gat bikpela pe tumas. Em i gat baleen. Em i wanpela weil we i gat gutpela pasin na em i no gat planti hevi, na em i save wokim planti 'gay foam' na waitpela wara moa long ol narapela weil.

BUK I. ( Folio ), SAPTA V. ( Razor Back ).—Ol i no save tumas long dispela weil, nem bilong em tasol. Mi bin lukim em longwe long Cape Horn. Em i save ritaia, na em i save ranawe long ol man bilong painim abus na ol saveman. Maski em i no pret, em i no soim yet wanpela hap bilong em tasol baksait bilong em, we i go antap long wanpela longpela sap rip. Larim em i go. Mi no save tumas long em, na tu ol narapela i no save.

BUK I. ( Folio ), SAPTA VI. ( Sulphur Bottom ).—Wanpela narapela man husat i ritaia, wantaim bel bilong em olsem salfa, i no gat tupela tingting olsem em i bin skrapim ol tail bilong Tartarian long sampela bilong ol dip daiv bilong em. Ol i no save lukim em planti taim; at least mi no bin lukim em long ol longwe solwara long saut, na oltaim mi stap longwe tumas long lukluk long pes bilong em. Ol i no save ranim em; em bai ranawe wantaim ol rop-wok bilong lain. Ol i save tokim ol prodigies long em. Adieu, Sulphur Bottom! Mi no inap tok moa long yu, na tu, olpela man bilong Nantucketer tu i no inap.

Olsem na BUK I i pinis. ( Folio ), na nau BUK II i stat. ( Octavo ).

OCTAVOES.*—Ol dispela i holim ol weil we i gat namel bikpela bilong en, namel long ol weil i stap:—I., ol Grampus ; II., Blakpela Pis ; III., Narwhale ; IV., Thrasher ; V., man bilong kilim man .

*Bilong wanem dispela buk bilong ol weil i no gat nem Quarto em i klia tru. Long wanem, maski ol weil bilong dispela oda, maski ol i liklik moa long ol weil bilong bipo, ol i holim wankain piksa olsem ol, tasol Quarto volium bilong bukbinder long dimensen fom bilong en i no holim seip bilong Folio volium, tasol Octavo volium i gat.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), SAPTA I. ( Grampus ).—Maski dispela pis, we em i save pulim win strong, o moa gutpela, i bin givim wanpela savetok long ol manmeri bilong ples, em i wanpela man bilong ples we planti man i save long en, tasol ol man i no save kolim em olsem wanpela welfis. Tasol bikos em i gat olgeta bikpela samting bilong 'leviathan', planti saveman bilong ol samting bilong ples i luksave long em olsem wanpela bilong ol. Em i gat liklik 'octavo' sais, longpela bilong em i save senis namel long 15 i go inap long 25 fit, na i gat wankain sais long wasket bilong em. Em i save swim long ol lain; ol i no save painim em oltaim, maski oil bilong em i planti tru, na i gutpela long lait. Sampela man bilong kisim pis i ting dispela rot bilong em i soim olsem dispela bikpela 'sperm whale' bai kam klostu.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), SAPTA II. ( Black Fish ).—Mi givim nem bilong ol man bilong kisim pis long olgeta dispela pis, long wanem, planti taim ol i nambawan tru. Sapos wanpela nem i no klia o i no gat as bilong en, bai mi tok olsem, na givim narapela nem. Mi mekim olsem nau, mi tatsim Blakpela Pis, ol i kolim, bikos blakpela em i rul namel long klostu olgeta weil. Olsem na, sapos yu laik, kolim em Hyena Whale. Planti manmeri i save long pasin bilong em long kaikai planti, na long wanem ol insait bilong lips bilong em i ben i go antap, em i gat wanpela Mephistophelean grin long pes bilong em. Dispela weil i save longpela olsem 16 o 18 fit. Em i stap long klostu olgeta 'latitude'. Em i gat wanpela narakain pasin bilong soim 'dorsal hooked fin' bilong em taim em i swim, we i luk olsem nus bilong ol Rom. Taim ol i no kisim gutpela wok, ol man bilong painim ol 'sperm whale' i save holim 'Hyena whale' sampela taim, long holim saplai bilong oil we i no gat bikpela pe bilong mekim wok long haus - olsem sampela haus kipa i save lukautim gut mani, taim ol i no gat kampani, na ol i stap wanpis, ol i save kukim 'tallow' we i no gutpela na i no kukim 'wax' we i gat gutpela smel. Maski gris bilong ol i liklik tru, sampela bilong ol dispela weil bai givim yu moa long 30 lita oil.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), SAPTA III. ( Narwhale ), em, Nostril whale .—Narapela eksampel bilong wanpela weil we ol i kolim dispela kain nem, mi ting ol i kolim dispela nem bikos long kom bilong em we ol i bin ting em i wanpela nus i gat antap. Longpela bilong dispela animal em inap 16 fit, na kom bilong en i gat 5 fit, tasol sampela i winim 10 fit, na i go inap 15 fit. Long tok stret, dispela kom em i wanpela longpela tit tasol, i gro aut long mausgras long wanpela lain we i go daun liklik long horisontal. Tasol em i stap tasol long sait nogut, we i gat nogut samting, na i givim papa bilong en samting i wankain olsem wanpela lephan man i no gat gutpela tingting. Em i hat long tok wanem as tru dispela kom o spia bilong tit bilong elefan i bekim. I luk olsem ol i no save yusim olsem naip bilong bainat-pis na 'bill-fish'; maski sampela seila i tokim mi olsem Narwhale i save yusim dispela samting olsem wanpela 'rake' long tanim as bilong solwara bilong painim kaikai. Charley Coffin i tok ol i bin yusim dispela masin bilong sutim ais; long wanem, Narwhale, i go antap long solwara Polar, na painim olsem ais i karamapim, em i putim kom bilong em i go antap, na em i brukim solwara. Tasol yu no inap pruvim olsem wanpela bilong ol dispela tingting i stret. Tingting bilong mi yet em olsem, maski dispela wanpela sait kom i ken tru tru Narwhale i yusim—maski em i olsem wanem—em bai gutpela tru long em long putim wanpela folda bilong ritim ol liklik nius. Mi harim ol i kolim Narwhale olsem Tusked whale, Horned whale, na Unicorn whale. Em i wanpela gutpela eksampel bilong 'Unicornism' we yu ken painim long klostu olgeta kingdom bilong ol samting i gat laip. Long sampela olpela man bilong raitim ol buk mi bin kisim save olsem dispela wankain kom bilong si-yunikon long bipo ol i bin tingim olsem em i gutpela marasin bilong daunim marasin nogut, na olsem, wok bilong redim em i save bringim bikpela pe tru. Ol i bin tanim tu i go long ol sol we i save pairap hariap long ol meri i save lus tingting, wankain olsem ol i save wokim kom bilong ol man dia i kamap 'hartshorn'. Pastaim ol i ting em i wanpela samting we planti man i laik save long en. Black Letter i tokim mi olsem Sir Martin Frobisher taim em i kam bek long dispela raun bilong em, taim Kwin Bess i bin soim han bilong em long windo bilong Greenwich Palace, taim sip bilong em i ron i go daun long wara Thames; "taim Sir Martin i kam bek long dispela raun," Black Letter i tok, "em i brukim skru na givim long haines wanpela bikpela longpela kom bilong Narwhale, we i bin hangamap longpela taim long haus king long Windsor." Wanpela man bilong raitim buk bilong Aialan i tok olsem Earl bilong Leicester, husat i brukim skru, i givim narapela kom long biknem bilong em, em i bilong wanpela animal bilong graun we i gat kain kain 'unicorn'.

Narwhale i luk naispela tru, i luk olsem lepat, em i gat waitpela kala olsem milk, na i gat ol raunpela na longpela mak bilong blakpela kala. Oil bilong em i gutpela tru, klia na gutpela; tasol i no gat planti, na ol i no save painim em planti taim. Em i save stap planti long ol solwara we i stap klostu long ol 'cirampolar'.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), SAPTA IV. ( Killer ).—Ol manmeri bilong Nantucketer i no save gut long dispela weil, na ol man i save tok ol i save long ol samting bilong ples i no save liklik long en. Long wanem samting mi bin lukim long em longwe, mi ken tok olsem em i bikpela olsem wanpela 'grampus'. Em i wel animal tru - em i wanpela kain pis Feegee. Sampela taim em i save holim maus bilong ol bikpela Folio weil, na hangamap i stap olsem wanpela 'leech', inap long dispela strongpela animal i wari nogut tru. Ol i no save painim dispela man bilong kilim man. Mi no bin harim wanem kain oil em i gat. Ol i ken tingim dispela nem we ol i givim long dispela weil, long wanem, em i no klia tumas. Long wanem, yumi olgeta i save kilim man, long graun na long solwara; Bonapartes na ol sak i stap insait.

BUK II. ( Octavo ), SAPTA V. ( Thrasher ).—Dispela man i gat nem long tel bilong em, we em i save yusim olsem wanpela 'ferule' long paitim ol birua bilong em. Em i kalap long baksait bilong Folio weil, na taim em i swim, em i wokim rot bilong em long wipim em; olsem sampela skulmasta i save wok gut wantaim long wol long wankain pasin. I no gat planti save long Thrasher olsem long Killer. Tupela wantaim i ol 'outlaw', maski long ol solwara we i no gat lo.

Olsem na BUK II i pinis. ( Octavo ), na i stat BUK III. ( Duodecimo ).

DUODECIMOES.—Dispela em ol liklik weil. I. Huzza Porpoise. II. Algerine Porpoise. III. Mealy-mouthed Porpoise.

Long ol lain husat i no bin kisim sans long stadim dispela samting, ating em i luk narakain, olsem ol pis we i no save winim 4 o 5 fit ol i mas putim namel long ol 'WHALES' - wanpela tok, we, long tingting bilong planti manmeri, i save soim olsem ol i bikpela tru. Tasol ol animal we i stap antap olsem Duodecimoes em ol weil tru, long mining bilong mi long wanem samting em weil - olsem wanpela pis i save maus, wantaim tel i stap stret.

BUK III. ( Duodecimo ), SAPTA 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Dispela em i wanpela 'porpoise' we ol i save painim long olgeta hap bilong wol. Nem em i bilong mi yet; long wanem i gat planti kain 'porpoises', na ol i mas mekim sampela samting long luksave long ol. Mi kolim em olsem, long wanem, em i save swim oltaim long ol naispela solwara, we antap long bikpela solwara i save tromoi ol yet i go antap long heven olsem ol kep insait long wanpela lain bilong Fourth-of-July. Ol man bilong solwara i save amamas long lukluk bilong ol. Ol i pulap long ol gutpela spirit, na oltaim ol i save kam long ol 'breezy billows' i go long 'windward'. Ol i ol mangi we i save stap oltaim long ai bilong win. Ol i ting ol i olsem laki mak. Sapos yu yet inap long karim tripela amamas taim yu lukim ol dispela strongpela pis, orait heven i helpim yu; spirit bilong pasin bilong pilai olsem God i no stap insait long yupela. Wanpela Huzza Porpoise we i gat gutpela kaikai na i patpela bai givim yu wanpela gutpela galon bilong gutpela oil. Tasol gutpela na liklik wara ol i kisim long maus bilong em i gat bikpela pe tru. Em i samting we ol man bilong wokim ol bilas na ol klok i save askim long en. Ol seila i save putim long ol 'hones' bilong ol. Mit bilong 'porpoise' em i gutpela long kaikai, yu save. Ating yu no bin tingim olsem wanpela 'porpoise' i save maus. Tru tumas, maus bilong em i liklik tru na yu no inap luksave long en. Tasol long narapela taim yu gat sans, lukluk long em; na bihain bai yu lukim bikpela Sperm whale yet i liklik tru.

BUK III. ( Duodecimo ), SAPTA II. ( Algerine Porpoise ).—Wanpela pairet. Em i nogut tru. Mi ting em i stap tasol long Pasifik. Em i bikpela liklik long Huzza Porpoise, tasol em i wankain jenerel. Yu mas kros long em, na em bai i go long wanpela sak. Mi bin daunim em planti taim, tasol mi no bin lukim ol i holim em yet.

BUK III. ( Duodecimo ), SAPTA III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Em i bikpela kain Porpoise; na ol i save painim tasol long Pasifik, inap long mak ol i save long en. Wanpela Inglis nem tasol, we ol i save kolim em long en, em nem bilong ol man bilong kisim pis—Right-Whale Porpoise, long wanem, em i save stap klostu long dispela Folio. Long sait bilong seip, em i narakain long Huzza Porpoise, em i no raunpela tumas na em i no gat gutpela bodi; tru tumas, em i gat gutpela bodi na i luk olsem wanpela man. Em i no gat ol 'fin' long baksait bilong em (planti ol narapela 'porpoise' i gat), em i gat naispela tel, na ai bilong em i gat kala olsem 'hazel'. Tasol maus bilong em i save bagarapim olgeta samting. Maski olgeta baksait bilong em i go daun long sait fin bilong em i olsem wanpela dip 'sable', tasol i gat wanpela 'baundri line', i luk olsem mak insait long 'hull' bilong sip, ol i kolim "bright waist," dispela lain i soim em long 'stem' i go long 'stern', wantaim tupela kala, blak antap na wait daunbilo. Waitpela kala i karamapim hap bilong het bilong em, na olgeta hap bilong maus bilong em, we i mekim em i luk olsem em i bin ranawe long wanpela bikpela visit i go long wanpela 'meal-bag'. Em i wanpela samting nogut tru! Oil bilong em i wankain olsem oil bilong 'common porpoise'.

* * * * * *

Bihain long DUODECIMO, dispela sistem i no save go het, long wanem, Porpoise em i liklik tru long ol narapela weil. Antap, yu gat olgeta Leviatan we i gat nem. Tasol i gat planti weil we i no klia, i save ranawe, na i no gutpela tumas, we, olsem wanpela 'whaleman' bilong Amerika, mi save long nem bilong ol, tasol mi yet i no save long ol. Bai mi kolim ol long nem bilong ol long 'fore-castle'; long wanem, ating dispela kain lista i ken helpim ol investigeta bilong bihain, husat i ken pinisim wanem samting mi gat hia tasol mi bin statim. Sapos ol i holim wanpela bilong ol dispela weil na putim mak long en, orait ol i ken putim em insait long dispela Sistem, bihainim Folio, Octavo, o Duodecimo mak bilong em:—The Bottle-Nose Whale; Junk Whale; 'Pudding-Headed Whale'; Cape Whale; 'Leading Whale'; Cannon Whale; Scragg Whale; Coppered Whale; Elefan Weil; 'Iceberg Whale'; Quog Whale; Blupela Weil; na ol narapela. Long ol atoriti bilong Iceland, Dutch, na olpela Inglan, ating ol i ken kolim ol narapela lista bilong ol weil we i no klia tumas, we ol i gat kain kain nem nogut. Tasol mi rausim ol olsem ol i no wok moa; na i hatwok long helpim long ting olsem ol i nois tasol, i pulap long Leviathanism, tasol i no makim wanpela samting.

Laspela: Ol i bin tok long stat, olsem dispela sistem bai no inap kamap, na wantu tasol, bai kamap gutpela olgeta. Yupela i no inap long lukim klia olsem mi bin bihainim tok bilong mi. Tasol nau mi lusim 'cetological System' bilong mi i stap yet na i no pinis yet, wankain olsem bikpela Katedral bilong Cologne i stap yet, wantaim 'crane' i sanap yet antap long taua we i no pinis yet. Long wanem, ol liklik haus ol i ken pinisim long han bilong ol namba wan akitek bilong ol; ol bikpela, ol trupela, i save lusim 'copestone' i go long ol pikinini bihain. God i no ken pinisim wanpela samting. Dispela buk em i wanpela 'draft' tasol - nogat, em i 'draft' bilong wanpela 'draft'. Oh, Taim, Strong, Mani, na Pasin bilong wet!
