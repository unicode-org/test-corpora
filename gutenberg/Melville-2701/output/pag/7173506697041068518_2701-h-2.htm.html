KAPITULO 4. Say Counterpane.

Nen binmangon ak ed tinmumbok ya kabuasan nen manliwawa lay agew, anengneng kon akapeket so taklay nen Queequeg ed siak diad sankamaaroan tan sankamaaroan a paraan. Ngalngali mo la inisip ya siak so asawa to. Say counterpane et gawad patchwork, napno na nikadkaduman melag iran parti-coloured ya kuadrado tan trianggulo; tan sayan taklay to et aka-tattoo ed amin a laman na sakey ya agnaparaan a Cretan labyrinth na sakey a pigura, ya anggapoy duaran parte to ya saksakey so kolor to—lapud iisipen ko ya ag-inmabobon so taklay to ed dayat diad agew tan liwawa, saray manggas na kamiseta to et ag-regular ya akalukot ed nanduruman panaon—sayan parehon taklay to, ibabagak ya singa parehon strip na mundo ules. Tua, singa ak akarukol ed satan a singa ginawa na taklay nen inmunan binmangon ak, agko nanengneng itan manlapud kubrekama, ta nankakasakey so kolor da; tan diad pakaliknak labat na belat tan pressure et naamtaan ko ya aalagaren ak nen Queequeg.

Nikadkaduman naliliknak. Salien kon ipaliwawa iratan. Nen ugaw ak ni, nanonotan ko ni so medyo mipadparan sirkumstansya ya agawa ed siak; balanglan tua itan odino kugip, agkon balot sigpot a naresolbe. Say sirkumstansya et saya. Manpuputer ak na caper odino arum ni—iisipen kon mansasagpot itan ya onkurong ed tsimenea, a singa anengneng ko ed sakey a melag a panag-alis pigaran agew lay apalabas; tan say stepmother ko ya, anggan panon et lanang to ak ya latilaken, odino ipapaugip to ak ya anggapoy pangdem,—inguygoy ak nen nanay ko ed saray sali ko paway ed tsimenea tan inyakar to ak ed dukolan, anggaman alas dos labat na ngarem na Hunyo 21, say sankaaruman ya agew ed sakey taon diad hemisphere tayo. Makapataktakot so liknaan ko. Balet anggapoy makatulong ed satan, kanian diad tagey na takayan et linma ak ed melag a kuartok ed komatlon grado, inukbaran koy inkasiak a magano pian ag-onkulang so panaon, tan tekep na ampait a pan-ingas et linma ak ed baetan na saray sapin.

Akarukol ak diman ya maer-ermen ya mankuenta ya labinanem ya interon oras so onlabas antis ak ya manilalo ed kioli. Labin-anem ya oras ed dukolan! say melag a beklew ko et ansakisakit no isipen ko itan. Tan anlemew met itan; say agew ya onsisinag ed bintana, tan say maksil ya ungol na saray kotse ed saray karsada, tan say tanol na saray boses na bakla ed interon abung. Lalon onloor so liknaan ko—diad kaunoran et inmalagey ak, nankawes, tan maalwar ya inmepas ed saray sali ko ya aka-medyas, inanap koy stepmother ko, tan biglan inmabeba ak ed salian to, ya akikasi ak ed sikato bilang sakey a partikular a pabor ya ikdan to ak na maabig a slippering lapud ag-inkayadyari ko; antokaman a tua noag say pangikondena ed siak a mandukdukol ed dukolan ed ontan lan karukey na panaon. Balet sikato so sankamaongan tan sankakonsiensiaan ed saray stepmother, tan pawil ko et kaukolan ko lay onla ed kuartok. Diad loob na pigaran oras et akarukol ak diman ya aliing, ya mas grabe so liknaan ko nen say kapiganman, anggan diad saray sankagrabian ya desyang ya sinmublay. Diad kaunoran et seguradon naplag ak ed sakey a makapagonigon a bangungot na inkaugip; tan kalkalnan binmangon ed satan—a kapaldua et napno na kugip—inlukas ko ray matak, tan say kuarto ya antis ya naliwawaan na agew et abalkot la natan na kabilungetan ed paway. Tampol kon alikna so inkabigla ya ondadalan ed interon laman ko; anggapoy nanengneng, tan anggapoy narengel; balet singa sakey a lima ya ag-inkayadyari so inyan ed limak. Say taklay ko et akabitin ed tapew na counterpane, tan say andiay ngaran, agnaisip, mareen a porma odino phantom, a kawalaan na lima, so singa akayurong ed abay na dukolan ko. Ta singa saray panaon ya atipon ed saray panaon, akarukol ak diman, a nanyelo ed saray sankagrabian a takot, ya agko labay ya ekalen so limak; balet agko la iisipen ya no nayarian ko labat ya igalaw itan na sakey a pulgada, naandi so makapataktakot ya anting-anting. Agko amta no panon ya inmarawi ed siak iyan pakatebek; balet nen binmangon ak ed kabuasan, manpapasnok ak ya anonotan so amin, tan diad loob na pigaran agew tan simba tan bulan kayari na satan et abalang ko so inkasiak ed makapawetwet iran sagpot a mangipaliwawa ed misteryo. Andi, anggad sayan oras, mabetbet kon iisipen so inkasiak ed satan.

Natan, ekalen mo so makapataktakot a takot, tan saray liknaan ko ed pakaliknak ed supernatural a limak et mipara, diad inka-nikadkaduma ra, ed saramay naeksperiensyak nen binmangon ak tan anengneng ko so paganon taklay nen Queequeg ya akapeket ed siak. Balet diad kaunoran et amin ya agawa nen imbeneg a labi so pinmawil, sakey-sakey, ed ag-onkukurang a katuaan, insan ak la nambilay ed makapaliket a kipapasen. Ta anggano sinali kon igalaw so taklay to—ilukasan so kapot na nobyo to—balet, legan ya naugip, sikatoy siansia nin malet ya angalakap ed siak, a singa anggapo lay arum noag say patey so makapangibiig ed sikami a dua. Nansagpotan ko natan ya liingen—“Queequeg!”—balet say ebat to labat et sakey a panngalngal. Insan ak inmawet, say beklew ko et singa walad kulintas na kabayo; tan biglan walay alikna ton daiset ya gasgas. Inbantak toy counterpane, wadman so tomahawk ya akaugip ed abay na savage, a singa sakey ya ugaw ya singa sundang so lupa to. Sakey a marakep ya atsara, tua, inisip ko; akaalagey dia ed sakey ya abung ya ag ni kabat ed pegley-agew, a kaiba so sakey a kanibal tan sakey a tomahawk! “Queequeg!—diad ngaran na kamaongan, Queequeg, ombangon ka!” Diad kaunoran, lapud dakel a pan-wriggle, tan maksil tan ag-ontutundan panangibalikas ed ag-inkayadyari na panangabrasa to ed kapara ton laki ed ontan ya estilo na panangasawa, tinmalona ak ya akapangipaway na sakey a ungol; tan diad kaplesan, inatras toy taklay to, inyegyeg toy laman to a singa sakey ya aso na Newfoundland ya nanlapud danum, tan inmirung ed dukolan, a malet a singa sakey a pike-staff, ya onnenengneng ed siak, tan mankukuskos ed saray mata to a singa agto nanonotan no panon ak ya akasabi diman, anggaman say malimpek a pakatebek to ed sakey a bengatla nipaakar ed siak so singa kalkalnan onsisinag. Kaleganan to, akarukol ak ya mareen ya onnenengneng ed sikato, ya anggapoy seryoson panduaruwa ko natan, tan akaparaan ak ya nengnengen a maong so sakey a pinalsa ya makapainteres. Nen, diad kaunoran, singa akapandesisyon la so ugali na kaiba ton dukolan, tan sikatoy nagmaliw, a singa panangibaga, a mipakna ed katuaan; sikatoy linmukso ed datal, tan diad panamegley na pigaran tanda tan tanol et atalosan to ak ya, no labay ko, sikatoy mankawes nin unona insan to ak paulyanan a mankawes kayari na satan, ya itilak ko so interon apartment parad siak. Isipen ko, Queequeg, diad silong na saray sirkumstansya, saya so sakey a sibilisadon tuloy ya panangibalikas; balet, say tua, sarayan masebeg et walaan na nisipor a pakalikna na delikado, ibagam so labay mo; makapakelkelaw no panon so inka-magalang da. Iiter ko iyan partikular a komendasyon ed si Queequeg, lapud sikatoy angipatnag ed siak na masimpit tan konsiderasyon, bangta siak so nankasalanan na baleg a inkabastos; manbabantay ed sikato manlapud dukolan, tan manbabantay ed amin ya galaw to ed kasilyas; parad panaon ya say pan-usisa ko et onkakapuy ed panag-anak ko. Anggaman ontan, say sakey a too a singa si Queequeg ya agmo nanengneng ed inagew-agew, sikato tan saray paraan to so makanakana ed nikadkaduman panmoria.

Ginmapo ya nan-kawes ed tagey diad impangisulong toy sombrero ton beaver, sakey ya atagey a tuloy, diad gilig, insan—ya anggapo ni so pantalon to—inanap to ray botas to. Agko amta no antoy ginawa to ed silong na tawen, balet say ontumbok ya ginawa to et say pangimekmek tod sarili to—awit toy botas, tan akasulong na sombrero—diad silong na dukolan; nen, manlapud nanduruman bayolentin panngalngali tan pan-stress, inisip ko ya sikatoy mansasagpot a maong; anggaman anggapoy ganggan na inkasusto ya nadngel ko, ya siopaman a laki et kaukolan ya magmaliw ya pribado no mansusulong na sapatos to. Balet si Queequeg, nanengneng mo, et sakey a pinalsa ya walad yugtan na pananguman—aliwan ulod odino kulibangbang. Sikatoy sibilisado la pian nipanengneng toy inka-outlandish to diad sanka-estrangheron paraan. Agni asumpal so edukasyon to. Sikatoy sakey ya undergraduate. No sikatoy aliwan sibilisado na daiset, seguradon agto komon inisip so inkasikato ed saray botas; balet ta, no sikatoy ag ni nagmaliw ya masebeg, agton balot inisip ya onloob ed silong na dukolan pian isulong iratan. Diad kaunoran, sikatoy pinmaway ya say sombrero to et aderal tan akapeket ed saray mata to, tan ginmapon nankigtot tan nankibalatar ed loob na kuarto, a singa, lapud agto tanton niyugali so botas, say paris ton ambetel tan akukurong a katat na baka—ya nayarin ag met ginawa ed order—so angipit tan amairap ed sikato diad primeron impaway na sakey a ampait a kabuasan ya ambetel.

Lapud anengneng, natan, ya anggapoy kurtina ed bintana, tan say karsada et mainget a tuloy, say abung ed kasumpalan to so mangigaganggan na malinew a nengnengen ed kuarto, tan lalon naimano so agmakanepegan a pigura nen Queequeg, a manpapasyar ya anggapoy arum a kawes to noag say sombrero tan botas to; Kinerew ko ed sikato anggad nayarian ko, ya papelesen toy kasilyas to, tan nagkalalo la ya onloob ed pantaloon to diad sankasakbayan a paraan. Tinmulok, insan intuloy toy nan-uras. Diad saman a panaon ed kabuasan et siopaman a Kristiano so man-uras na lupa to; balet si Queequeg, diad pankelaw ko, et nankontento lan man-ablusyon ed pagew, taklay, tan lima to. Insan to insulong so waistcoat to, tan inala toy sakey a piraso na malet a sabon ed sentro na lamisaan, insawsaw to itan ed danum tan ginapoan ton inbura so lupa to. Manbabantay ak pian nanengneng ko no iner so pangisimpenan to na labaha to, nen inala toy harpoon ed suyok na dukolan, inekal to so andukey a tabla, inukbaran toy ulo to, in-whet to na daiset ed botas to, tan linma ed samay espiho ya akapeket ed dingding, tan ginapoan toy mabiskeg a panag-scrape, odino panag-harpoon ed saray aping to. Isipen ko, Queequeg, ya saya so panguusar ed sankaabigan ya kubyertos nen Rogers ya walay panangibales. Kayari to et agak la nankelaw ed sayan operasyon nen naamtaan ko no antoy maong ya asero ya ginawa ed ulo na harpoon, tan no panon katadem so andukey tan diretso iran gilig.

Say arum nin kasilyas to et ag-abayag et agawaan to, tan sikatoy mapaatagey ya pinmaway ed kuarto, ya akabalot ed baleg ya pilot monkey jacket to, tan aka-awit na harpoon to a singa baton na marshal.





KAPITULO 5. Almusal.

Tinmumbok ak a tampol, tan nen linmeksab ak ed bar-room et maliket kon inarap so manngingimit ya akan-abung. Anggapoy nalilikna kon mauges ed sikato, anggaman sikatoy nan-skylarking ed siak ya aliwan daiset ed pamaakaran na kaibak ed dukolan.

Balet, say maong ya tawa et sakey a mabiskeg a bengatla, tan maslak et matalag a maong a bengatla; mas lalon makapaermen. Kanian, no siopaman a too, diad dilin inkatoo to, so makayarin mangiter na bengatla parad maong a panag-ayew ed siopaman, sikatoy ag komon magmaliw ya atrasado, noagta sikatoy maliket ya abuloyan toy inkasikato a manggastos tan nagastos ed ontan a paraan. Tan say toon walay antokaman ya makapaliket ed sikato, seguroen ya walay mas dakel ed satan a too nen say nayarin iisipen mo.

Say bar-room et napno la natan na saray boarders ya linma nen imbeneg a labi, tan agko ni nanengneng a maong. Ngalngalin amin da et manag-awit na balyena; saray manunan kaaro, tan saray komadua, tan saray komatlon kaaro, tan saray karpintero ed dayat, tan saray managgaway gansa ed dayat, tan saray panday ed dayat, tan saray manag-arpa, tan saray managbantay na barko; sakey a brown tan mabiskeg a grupo, a walaan na bosky a balbas; sakey ya ag-akaputot, balbas ya set, ya amin et akasulong na monkey jacket parad morning gowns.

Malinew mon nanengneng no panon kabayag so kada sakey ed gilig na dayat. Say mabunigas ya aping na sayan kalangweran et singa kolor na peras ya inluto ed agew, tan singa singa musky so angob to; agto nayarin taloy agew ya akasabi ed dayat manlapud biahe to ed India. Say laki ya asingger ed sikato et singa mas maliwawa; nayarin ibagam ya walay satin wood ed sikato. Diad baog na komatlo et wala ni so kolor na tropiko, balet ta medyo amputi; seguradon sikatoy nansiansia ed gilig na dayat na pigaran simba. Balet siopay makapangipanengneng na aping a singa si Queequeg? ya, ya walaan na nanduruman kolor, et singa western slope na Andes, ya ompatnag ed sakey a array, mandumaan so klima, kada sona.

“Grub, ho!” natan et inmeyag so akankayarian na abung, ya inlukas toy puerta, tan linmoob kami pian man-almusal.

Ibabaga ran saray totoon akanengneng ed mundo, et magmamaliw iran mainomay so ugali ra, tan magmaliw iran mabiskeg ed sarili da no kaiba ra. Balet aliwan naynay: si Ledyard, say maong ya managbiahe ed New England, tan say Mungo Park, say Scotch; ed amin a totoo, sikara so walaan na sankaabebaan ya kaseguroan ed parlor. Balet nayarin say basta labat ibaliw ed Siberia ya akalugan ed sakey a sledge ya iguguyor na aso a singa ginawa nen Ledyard, odino say andukey a panakar ya bukor ya anggapoy naakan, diad puso na saray negro ed Aprika, ya saya so nantipon na saray agawaan nen mairap-bilay a si Mungo—sayan klase na biahe, kuan ko, et nayarin aliwan sankaabigan a paraan pian nawalaan na atagey a posisyon ed sosyedad. Balet, diad maslak a kipapasen, ontan a klase na bengatla et naromog ed anggan iner.

Sarayan pannonot dia et nanlapud sirkumstansya ya kayari min amin ya akayurong ed lamisaan, tan manpaparaan ak ya ondengel na pigaran mararakep ya istorya nipaakar ed panag-balyena; diad agko melag a pankelawan, ngalngalin amin a lalaki so nansiansian ag-inmesel. Tan aliwan satan labat, noagta singa ra nababaingan. On, wadia so sakey a grupo na saray aso ed dayat, a dakel ed sikara so andian na anggan daiset a kababaingan ya linmugan ed angkakabaleg iran balyena diad dayat—ya ag da kabat—tan inatey da ra ya ag-ira kinmiwas; balet, dia ira akayurong ed sakey a lamisaan na almusal—ya amin da et parehoy tawag da, amin da et parehoy panlabayan da—ya manbabantay ira ya singa ag ira balot anengneng ed sakey a pukokan na karnero ed limog na Green Mountains. Sakey a makapainteres a nengnengen; sarayan mabaing ya oso, sarayan mabaing ya balyena!

Balet nipaakar ed si Queequeg—akin, si Queequeg so akayurong diman ed limog da—diad uloan na lamisaan, met, ontan met so inkagawa; singa ambetel na yelo. Pian segurado, agko nibaga so dakel nipaakar ed panag-anak to. Say sankabalgan ya mandinayew ed sikato et agmakatunongan ya inawit toy harpoon to ed almusal to, tan inusar to diman ya anggapoy seremonya; inabot to itan ed lamisaan, ya onsasabin napeligro so dakel ya ulo, tan inawit to ray beefsteaks ya amaarap ed sikato. Balet talagan makapaliket itan ya ginawa to, tan amta na balang sakey ya diad panmoria na karaklan, say panggawa na antokaman ya makapaliket et singa panggawa ed satan ya maong.

Agtayo pantongtongan dia so amin ya nikadkaduman ugali na Queequeg; no panon ton ag-oninum na kape tan hot rolls, tan inyaplika toy amin ya imano to ed saray beefsteak, ya matalag ya gagawaen. Saya, nen asumpal lay almusal et sikatoy inmarawi a singa arum ed publikon kuarto, insindian toy tomahawk-pipe to, tan akayurong diman ya mareen ya mantutunaw tan mansisigarilyo ya akasulong na agnaparaan ya sombrero to, nen pinmaway ak pian manpasyar.





KAPITULO 6. Say Karsada.

No siak so abigla nen inmuna nen anengneng ko so sakey a makapadinayew ya indibidual a singa si Queequeg ya onliliber ed limog na magalang a sosyedad na sakey a sibilisadon baley, satan a pankelaw so agnambayag et naandi sanen linma ak ed primeron panagpasyar ko ed saray karsada na New Bedford.

Diad saray karsada ya asingger ed saray pantalan, antokaman a baleg a pantalan so mabetbet a mangiyoopresi na pakanengnengan ed saray sanka-queer ya itsura ya ag-ontutukoy ed arum a pasen. Anggan diad Broadway tan Chestnut streets, saray marino ed Mediterranean et no maminsan et manpapasnok ed saray bibiin natatakot. Say Regent Street et ag kabat na saray Lascar tan Malay; tan diad Bombay, diad Apollo Green, saray mabilay a Yankees so mabetbet a mamapataktakot ed saray katutubo. Balet ta taloen na New Bedford so amin ya Water Street tan Wapping. Diad sarayan unor ya abitlan pasen et saray marino labat so nanengneng mo; balet diad New Bedford, saray totoon kanibal so mansasalita ed saray suyok na karsada; saray masebeg a totoo; dakel ed sikara so mangaawit ni na saray pukel da ya ag masanto a laman. Makapanengneng itan na sakey ya ag kabat.

Balet, nilikud ed saray Feegeeans, Tongatobooarrs, Erromanggoans, Pannangians, tan Brighggians, tan, nilikud ed saray atap ya ayayep na balyena ya ag-ontutundan manliliber ed saray karsada, nanengneng mo ni ray arum ya mas makapainteres, seguradon mas makapaliket. Kada simba et onsasabi ed sayan baley so dakel a berdin taga-Vermont tan taga-New Hampshire, ya amin da et napgaan ed pakagunggonaan tan gloria ed panagsigay. Maslak ed sikara et malangwer ni, tan malet so laman da; saray kaparak ya nanputot na katakelan, tan natan et labay dan ipelag so wasay tan alaen so gayang na balyena. Dakel so singa berde na Green Mountains ya nanlapuan da. Diad arum a bengatla et isipen mo ya pigaran oras ni ra. Nengneng mo diman! saman ya laki ya manpapasyar ed suyok. Sikatoy akasulong na beaver hat tan swallow-tailed coat, ya akabalkes na sailor-belt tan sheath-knife. Nia so sananey ya walay sou'-wester tan bombazine ya kawes to.

Anggapoy town-bred dandy ya makapipara ed sakey ya country-bred dandy—say labay kon ibaga et sakey a bumpkin dandy—sakey a too ya diad panaon na aso et man-mow na duaran acre to ya aka-buckskin gloves lapud takot ton na-tann so lima to. Natan no say sakey a country dandy a singa saya et manisip ya manggawa na nikadkaduman reputasyon, tan mibiang ed baleg a panagsigay na balyena, nepeg mon nanengneng iray makapaliket a gagawaen to no onsabi ed daongan. Diad pangibaga tod kawes ton parad dayat, sikatoy man-order na saray butones na kampanilya ed saray waistcoat to; saray tali ed canvas ya pantalon to. Ah, pobrin Hay-Seed! no panon kagrabe so pakasabog na saratan a tali ed unonan maksil a dagem, sano sika so natulak, saray tali, butones, tan amin, ed eleng na bagyo.

Balet agmo isipen ya sayan bantog a baley et walaan labat na saray harpooneer, kanibal, tan bumpkins ya ipanengneng tod saray bisita to. Andi. Say New Bedford et sakey nin queer a pasen. No aliwan lapud sikami ran manag-balyena, satan a dalin so nayarin walad sayan agew a singa say baybay na Labrador. Diad tua, saray parte na bansa to et magenap la pian nataktakot so sakey, singa ra makalmo na pukel. Say mismon baley so nayarin sankaabigan a panayaman, diad interon New England. Sakey itan a dalin na larak, tua: balet aliwan singa say Canaan; sakey a dalin, met, na mais tan alak. Saray karsada et ag-onkukurang na gatas; nisay diad panaoy primabera et papasementoan da iratan na balon itlog. Balet, anggaman ontan, anggapo lay arum a pasen ed interon America ya pakaromogan mo na mas dakel nin kaabungan a singa patrician; mas mayaman iray parke tan hardin nen say diad New Bedford. Iner so nanlapuan da? panon ya intanem ed sayan datin mapitek a scoria na sakey a bansa?

Onla ka tan nengnengen mo ray bakal ya simbolo na harpoon ed kaliberliber na atagey a mansion, tan naebatan so tepet mo. On; amin na sarayan matapang ya kaabungan tan tanamanan na saray rosas et nanlapud Atlantic, Pacific, tan Indian oceans. Sakey tan amin, sikaray pinaltog tan inguygoy dia manlapud dalem na dayat. Sarag kasi nen Herr Alexander so manggawa na ontan?

Diad New Bedford, saray ama, inkuan da, et mangiiter na balyena ed saray anak dan bibii, tan mangiiter ira na pigaran porpoises ed saray akulaw da. Kaukolan moy onla ed New Bedford pian makanengneng na marakep a kasal; ta, ibabaga ra, wala ray reservoir na larak ed kada abung, tan kada labi et ag da labay ya popoolan iray karukey da ed saray kandila ya spermaceti.

No panaoy tiagew, say baley et marakep ya nengnengen; napno na mararakep iran maple—andukey iran dalan na berde tan balitok. Tan no Agosto, diad tagey na dagem, saray mararakep tan daakan a horse-chestnuts, a singa kandelabra, so mangiiter ed saray ondadalan a manpapaspas iran maptek a kono na nankakasakey iran rosas. Kanian makapanyarin-amin so arte; ya diad dakel a distrito na New Bedford et walaan na maliwliwawan terrace na saray rosas ed tapew na saray ag-ontutubo iran bato ya inbantak ed unor ya agew na impamalsa.

Tan saray bibii ed New Bedford, manbubulaklak ira a singa saray dilin ambalangan rosas da. Balet saray rosas et manrosas labat no tiagew; bangbalet say marakep a kolor na saray aping da et mansiansian singa sinag na banwa ed komapiton katawenan. Diad arum a pasen et makapipara ed satan a rosas da, nilikud ed Salem, a ditan et ibabaga ra ed siak a saray malangwer a bibii so manlinawa na ontan a musk, saray marino ran inararo ra so manangob ed sikara ed arawi ed gilig na dayat, a singa ra onaasingger ed mabanglon Moluccas imbes a diad Puritanic sands.





KAPITULO 7. Say Kapilya.

Diad sayan mismon New Bedford et walay Whaleman's Chapel, tan pigpigara labat so maermen ya managsigay ya onla ed Indian Ocean odino Pacific, ya ag makabisita ed satan a pasen no Simba. Segurado ak ya agko ginawa itan.

Nen pinmawil ak manlapud inmunan impanakar ko ed kabuasan, linma ak lamet ed sayan espisyal a kimey. Say tawen so anguman manlapud malinew, maaw-awang ya ambetel, ya anggad mansasnit ya uran tan alimbusabos.
