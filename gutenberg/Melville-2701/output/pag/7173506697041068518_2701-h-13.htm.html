KAPITULO 61. Pinatey nen Stubb so sakey a Balyena.

No parad si Starbuck et sakey a tanda so inkipatnag na Squid, parad si Queequeg et duman tuloy itan.

"No nanengneng mo ya 'quid," inkuan na masebeg, ya ipapasakbay toy harpoon to ed proa na akatagey ya baloto to, "insan mo nanengneng ya 'parm whale."

Say tinmumbok ya agew et mareen tan ambetel, tan lapud anggapoy espisyal ya makapilaban ed sikara, saray tripulante na Pequod et ag da nalabanan so ugip ya sengegan na ontan ya bakantin dayat. Ta sayan parte na Indian Ocean ya dadalanen mi nen saman et aliwan tatawagen na saray balyena ya mabilabilay a dalin; salanti, mas daiset so nanengneng ton porpoise, dolphin, flying-fish, tan arum niran mabilabilay ya manaayam ed mas makapasagyat iran danum, nen saray walad Rio de la Plata, odino diad gilig na dayat ed Peru.

Turno ko la so onalagey ed unaan na palo; tan diad saray abagak ya akapeket ed saray amaga ya kawes na ari, siak so onkukurang ed singa makapasagyat ya dagem. Anggapoy resolusyon ya makasungdo ed satan; diad saman ya singa kugip ya naandi so amin ya pakalikna, diad kaunoran et pinmaway so kamarerwak ed laman ko; anggaman say laman ko et mantutultuloy ya onkukurong a singa sakey a pendulum, abayag kayari na inkaandi na pakayari ya inmunan angigalaw ed satan.

Antis ya sinmabi ed siak so inkalingwanan, naimano ko ya saray marino ed manunan tan mizzen-mast-heads et naugip la. Kanian diad kaunoran et amin min talo so andiay bilay ya inmawet manlapud saray spars, tan kada inmawet ya gagawaen mi et walay ungol manlapud leksab manlapud naugip a timon. Saray daluyon, met, et inmawet ed saray ag-inkayadyari na toktok da; tan diad biek na malaknab a liwawa na dayat, say bukig et ontumbok ed sagur, tan say banwa et walad tapew na amin.

Biglan singa pinmaway iray bula ed silong na saray akasara ya matak; singa saray bisyo saray limak so angegnaan ed saray kawes; walay agnanengneng, mapangasin ahensia ya angipreserba ed siak; diad inkabigla ko et pinmawil ak ed bilay. Tan nia! asingger ed silong na lee mi, aliwan apataplon fathoms so kaarawi to, sakey a higanten Sperm Whale so manpapaspas ed danum a singa say atumba a kasko na frigate, say malapar tan makintab a beneg to, a kolor na Ethiopian, a mankirlap ed sinag na banwa a singa espiho. Balet ta matamoy ya onlelereg ed gilig na dayat, tan lanang ya mareen ya ipapaway toy singaw ya jet to, say balyena et singa sakey a portly burgher ya mansisigarilyo na pipe to ed sakey a ambetel ya ngarem. Balet saman ya tubo, pobrin balyena, so unor mo. Singa tinampal na baston na sakey ya enkanto, say naugip a barko tan balang sakey ya naugip ed loob to et aminpigan ginmapon aliing; tan masulok ya samploran boses manlapud amin a parte na bapor, a kaiba so taloran nota manlapud tagey, so angipaway na niyugali lan eyag, legan a say baleg a sira so kalkalna tan naynay a mangibubuga na mankirlap a asin ed dagem.

"Ekal yo ray baloto! Luff!" inmeyag si Ahab. Tan lapud inunor toy ganggan to, intumba to so timon antis ya naala na timon iray rayos.

Say biglan inkelyaw na saray tripulante so seguradon amaalarma ed balyena; tan sakbay ya inmepas iray baloto, a makapadinayew ya inmarap, sikatoy linmangoy ya amaarap ed dagem, balet ta walaan na ontan lan kalmadon kareenan, tan manggagawa na daiset iran daluyon legan ton manlangoy, kanian inisip ton sikatoy ag ni natatakot, si Ahab so angiter na ganggan ya ag la mangusar na sagwan, tan anggapoy mansalita noag diad panagbulong. Kanian akayurong kamin singa saray Indian na Ontario ed saray gunwale na baloto, maples kami balet ta mareen ya nansagwan; say kalmadon ag pangawat ed saray anggapoy ungol ya layag ya ipapasen. Diad kaplesan, legan min onkukurang ya manguusil, say ulsa et inmawet so ikol to na apataplon mitro ed tagey, insan linmeksab ya ag nanengneng a singa sakey a tori ya inakmon.

"Wala ray nagagawa!" so inkelyaw, sakey ya anunsyo ya tinmumbok a tampol ed impamawala nen Stubb na posporo to tan impamawala na tubo to, ta natan et niiter lay painawa. Kayari na interon panaon na panagsukimat to, inmalagey lamet so balyena, tan lapud akauna la natan ed baloto na managsigarilyo, tan mas asingger ed satan nen say arum ed arum, nanmatalek si Stubb ed kagalangan na pakarel to. Mapatnag la natan ya say balyena et akabatan to la ray manguusil ed sikato. Kanian anggapo lay kakanaan na amin ya kareenan na panag-alwar. Saray sagwan so nipelag, tan saray sagwan so maksil ya inmepas. Tan manpapaspas nin siansia ed tubo to, inliket nen Stubb iray tripulante to ed panangataki.

On, walay baleg ya pananguman ya sinmabi ed sira. Mabilay ya amin ed kapeligroan to, sikatoy onla ed paway; satan a parte ya ompapaway manlapud lebadura ya ginawa to.*

*Nanengneng ed arum a pasen no antoy anlemew a bengatla ed interon loob na baleg ya ulo na sperm whale. Anggaman mapatnag ya sankabalgan, saya so sankaliketan a parte nipaakar ed sikato. Kanian mainomay ton itagey itan ed tagey, tan naynay ton gagawaen itan no onla ed sankapelesan a kapeles to. Nilikud ni, ontan lay kalapar na tagey a parte na arap na ulo to, tan ontan lay onkakapuy a cut-water formation na leksab a parte, kanian diad pangitagey tod ulo to, nayarin ibaga ya sikatoy manguman ed inkasikato manlapud sakey a bluff-bowed sluggish galliot ya anggad sakey a matalimbeng a New York pilot-boat.

"Igapo yo, igapo yo, totook! Ag kayo manaapura; mangigana kayo na dakel a panaon—balet igapo yo; igapo yo a singa saray karol, satan labat," inmeyag si Stubb, ya ipapaway toy asewek legan ton mansasalita. "Igapo mo la natan; ikdan mo ra na andukey tan mabiskeg a panag-ipit, Tashtego. Igapo mo ra, Tash, anak ko—igapo mo la, amin; balet mansiansian kalmado, mansiansian ambetel—pipino so salita—mainomay, mainomay—igapo mo labat a singa makapasinagem ya patey tan saray manngingimit a demonyo, tan paolien mo ray niponpon ya inaatey a pabitay manlapud lubok da—igapo to iratan ya amin!”

"Woo-hoo! Wa-hee!" inmeyag so Gay-Header bilang ebat, ya intagey toy pigaran daan ya war-whoop ed katawenan; legan ya balang sakey a managsagwan ed naiirapan a baloto et agto labay ya inmarap ed unaan tekep na sakey a makapadinayew a panangidaulo ya inter na maseseg ya Indian.

Balet saray makapasinagem ya eyag to et inebatan na arum ya singa makapataktakot. "Kee-hee! Kee-hee!" inmeyag si Daggoo, ya onkukurang ed unaan tan beneg ed yurongan to, a singa sakey a manaakar a tigre ed kulongan to.

"Ka-la! Koo-loo!" inmeyag si Queequeg, ya singa to ipapasaksak so bibil to ed sakey a sangi na Grenadier's steak. Kanian diad panamegley na saray sagwan tan eyag et pinuter na saray kilya so dayat. Kaleganan to, si Stubb ya nansiansia ed pasen to ed van, et siansia nin pinaseseg to ray totoo to ya onla, legan ya ipupuyok toy asewek ed sangi to. Singa saray desperado et nan-guyod ira tan nan-strain ira, anggad nadngel so panangabrasa ya eyag—“Alagey ka, Tashtego!—iter mo ed sikato!” Inbantak so harpoon. “Mapeget ya amin!” Saray managsagwan et inmarap ed danum; diad saman met lanlamang a bekta et walay linmeksab tan inmepas ed balang sakey ed saray pulso ra. Saya so mahikon linya. Sakey a bekta antis na satan, si Stubb so maples ya akaala na duaran kaaruman a liko ed kaliberliber na loggerhead, a diman, lapud maples a panagliber to, sakey a hempen blue ya asewek so pinmaway tan nanlaok ed saray tuloytuloy ya asewek manlapud tubo to. Legan ya ondadalan so linya ed kaliberliber na loggerhead; ontan met, antis ya akasabi ed saman a punto, sikatoy linmabas tan linmabas ed duaran lima nen Stubb, ya ditan et aksidentin atumba iray hand-cloths, odino saray kuadrado na quilted canvas ya no maminsan et isulong ed sarayan panaon. Singa itan pangibemben ed matarem ya espada na kalaban ya duaran tarem, tan satan ya kalaban et lanang ton pansasagpotan ya ekalen itan ed limam.

"Basaen so linya! basaen so linya!" inmeyag si Stubb ed managsagwan na tub (sikatoy akayurong ed abay na tub) ya inekal toy sombrero to tan inyan toy danum na dayat ed satan.* Mas dakel so inliko to, kanian say linya et ginmapon akaalagey ed pasen to. Say baloto et tinmakdang natan ed manpabukal a danum a singa pating ya amin so palikpik to. Si Stubb tan Tashtego dia et nansalat na pasen—stem parad stern—sakey a makapabiglan negosyo ya talagan walad saman ya makapabiglan gulo.

*Pian nipanengneng so inkaimportante na sayan gawa, nayarin ibaga dia, ya, diad daan a panagsigay na saray Olandes, sakey a mop so inusar pian napelag so linya na danum; diad dakel niran barko, walay gawad kiew ya piggin, odino bailer, ya nibiig parad satan a gagala. Balet, say sombrero yo so sankaabigan.

Manlapud manyeyegyeg a linya ya onnatnat ed interon karukey na tagey a parte na baloto, tan lapud mas masimpit la natan nen say lubir na arpa, isipen mo komon ya say baloto et walaan na duaran kilya—sakey so manbibiig ed danum, say sananey et manbibiig ed dagem—legan ya onkukurang so baloto ed duaran mankontraan ya elemento a mankakasakey. Sakey a tuloytuloy a kaskad so manpapatogtog ed saray pana; sakey ya ag-ontutundan onkukurang ya alimbusabos ed beneg to; tan, diad daiset a galaw manlapud loob, anggan diad melag labat a gamegamet, say mankirlap, mankibalatar a bapor so onlelereg ed tapew na manpapasnok a gunwale to ya amaarap ed dayat. Kanian sikaray binmatik; balang sakey a too a walaan na biskeg tan manunan akapeket ed yurongan to, pian ag-ontumba ed bula; tan say atagey ya itsura nen Tashtego ed manibela ya akayurong ya ngalngalin doble, pian napaabeba so sentro na grabidad to. Say interon Atlantiko tan Pasipiko et singa linmabas legan iran manpapaputok ed dalan da, anggad diad kaunoran et medyo intunda na balyena so biahe to.

“Awiten—awiten!” inmeyag si Stubb ed managpana! tan, ya akaarap ed balyena, amin a lima so ginmapon angiguyor ed baloto ya amaarap ed sikato, legan ya iguguyor ni so baloto. Ag-abayag et inmarap ed gilig to, si Stubb, ya malet ya inyan toy pueg to ed makulangkulang a cleat, so nan-dart na kada dart ed ontitikyab a sira; diad salita na ganggan, say baloto so mansasalatan ya onarawi ed dalan na makapataktakot a panag-awit na balyena, insan onla ed tagey parad sananey nin panag-awit.

Say ambalangan delap so onlelereg natan manlapud amin a dapag na ulsa a singa saray ilog ya onleleksab ed pukdol. Say apairap a laman to et ag-inmawet ed asin no ag ingen diad dala, ya inmawet tan inmawet ed loob na pigaran estadio ed benegan da. Say mankirlap ya agew ya onlelereg ed sayan ambalbalangan lawak ed dayat, et angipawil na repleksion to ed kada lupa, kanian amin da et mankirlap ed balang sakey a singa saray ambalangan lalaki. Tan diad saman a panaon, nantutumbokan ya amputin asewek so makapasinagem ya pinmaway manlapud spiracle na balyena, tan maksil a puff kayari na puff manlapud sangi na eksayted ya headman; singa ed kada pana, ya uusaren toy pikewet a gayang to (diad linya ya akapeket ed satan), paulit-ulit ya inpetek nen Stubb, diad pigaran maples ya impakasuntok tod gunwale, insan to lamet impawil ed balyena.

“Man-pull on—man-pull up!” inmeyag natan ed managpana, legan ya onlelereg so onkukurang a balyena ed sanok to. "Onalagey ka!—asingger la!" tan say baloto et linma ed gilig na sira. Nen akasabi lad arawi ed pana, kalkalnan inyan nen Stubb so andukey tan matadem a gayang to ed sira, tan inyan to diman, a maalwar ton igagalaw tan igagalaw, a singa to maalwar ya aanapen ya nalikna kayari na pigaran balitok a relo ya nayarin inakmon na balyena, tan tatakotan ton nabuyak antis ton nasabit. Balet samay balitok ya relo ya aanapen to et say sankaaraleman a bilay na sira. Tan natan et abagbag la; ta, manlapud inka-trance to ed saman ya agnasabi a bengatla ya tatawagen ya “flurry” to, say ulsa et makapataktakot ya inmawet ed dala to, ya abalkot toy inkasikato na agnalooban, mansasakit, manpabukal a spray, kanian say napepeligron bapor, ya tampol ya inmepas ed beneg, et walaan na dakel ya ado ya bulag pian makaalis manlapud saman ya mansirunget ya dagem ya anggad malinew ya dagem na agew.

Tan natan ya onkakapuy lad panag-apura to, say balyena et pinmaway lamet ed pakanengnengan; onlelereg manlapud sakey a diking ya anggad sananey; manpapasnok tan manpapasnok so spout-hole to, ya walay matalimbeng, manbabatak, tan makapasinagem ya panlinawa to. Diad kaunoran, nantutumbokan ya onlelereg ya ambalanga ya dala, a singa saray purpura ya alak, ya pinmaway ed takot ya dagem; tan inmabeba lamet, tan binmatik ya onlelereg ed saray ag-onkikiwas a diking to ya amaarap ed dayat. Binmetag so puso to!

"Inatey la, Mr. Stubb," inkuan nen Daggoo.

"On; parehon naandi so asewek na tubo!" tan inekal toy sarili to ed sangi to, intaytayak nen Stubb so inatey a dapol ed tapew na danum; tan, diad sakey a bekta, inmalagey ya mannononot ya onnenengneng ed baleg a bangkay ya ginawa to.





KAPITULO 62. Say Dart.

Sakey a salita nipaakar ed sakey ya inkagawa ed unor a kapitulo.

Unong ed ag manguuman ya uusaren na panagsigay, say balyena-boat et ontulak manlapud barko, ya say headsman odino whale-killer so temporaryon steersman, tan say harpooneer odino whale-fastener so manguguyor ed sankaunaan ya oar, samay tatawagen ya harpooneer-oar. Natan et kaukolan toy mabiskeg tan mannerbiyos a taklay pian nasabi so unonan bakal ed sira; ta mabetbet, diad tatawagen ya andukey a pana, say ambelat a kagawaan et kaukolan ya ipelag ed distansyan duamplo odino talomplon mitro. Balet anggan panon lay karukey tan makapakesaw na panguusil, say manag-arpooner et iilaloan ya manguyos ed sagwan to anggad sampot; tua, sikatoy iilaloan ya mangipatnag na ehemplo na superhuman ya aktibidad ed arum, aliwan diad makapadinayew labat a panaggaud, noagta diad paulit-ulit a maksil tan ag-ontakot iran inkelyaw; tan no antoy kabaliksan na mantultuloy ya onkelyaw ed tagey na kompas, legan ya amin ya arum nin masel et napapagaan tan kapaldua lan onkikiwas—no antoy amta na satan noag saramay akasali ed satan. Sakey ni, agko nayarian so onakis a maong tan mankimey ya andi-alwar ed sakey tan parehon panaon. Diad sayan makapakebbiew, manpapasnok a kipapasen, sirin, ya akaarap ed sira, a tampol ya nadngel na naknaksawan lan manag-arpa so makapalikliket ya eyag—“Alagey ka, tan iter mo ed sikato!” Kaukolan to la natan ya ipelag tan iseguro so sagwan to, onliko ed pegley to, alaen toy harpoon to manlapud ulpo, tan diad anggan daiset a biskeg to, salien ton ipelag itan ed balyena. Agpankelawan, no alaen so interon armada na saray balyena ed sakey a laman, a diad limamplon patas a tsansa parad sakey a pana, aliwan lima so ontalona; ag la pankelawan no akin ya dakel so maermen ya manag-harpoon ya nababalaw tan nababaingan; ag la pankelawan ya arum ed sikara so talagan pinmaway ed saray ugat da diad baloto; agla pankelawan ya wala ray sperm whalemen ya anggapoy apatiran taon ya walay apatiran bariles da; agla pankelawan ya parad dakel ya akankayarian na barko, say panag-balyena et sakey labat ya ag-onkulang a kapagaan; ta say manag-harpooneer so manggagawa na biahe, tan no ekalen mo so panlinawa ed laman to panon mon nailaloan a naromog mo itan diman sano labalabay na karaklan!

No ontalona lamet so dart, diad komaduan kritikal a bekta, salanti, no onggapo lan ombatik so balyena, say boatheader tan harpooneer et onggapo met ya ombatik ed unaan tan beneg, diad asingger lan kapeligroan na inkasikara tan amin. Insan ira manguuman na pasen; tan say headman, say manunan opisyal na melag a barko, et mangala na manepeg ya posisyon to ed saray proa na baloto.

Natan, agko la pibabalian no siopay mangibabagan onkontra, balet amin na saya et parehon makulangkulang tan agmet kaukolan. Say pangulo et nepeg ya mansiansia ed saray pana manlapud gapo anggad sampot; nepeg ton ipaltog so harpoon tan lansa, tan anggapoy antokaman ya panaggaud ya nepeg ya ilaloan ed sikato, likud ed saray sirkumstansya ya amta na siopaman a managsigay. Amtak ya no maminsan et manresulta itan ed daiset ya pakawalaan na kapeles ed panguusil; balet say andukey ya eksperiensya ed nanduruman manag-awit na balyena ed masulok ya sakey a bansa so angikombinse ed siak ya diad karaklan na saray ag-inmabobon ed panagsigay, aliwan say kapeles na balyena so sengegan na saratan.

Pian naseguro so sankaabigan ya inkaepektibo ed panagpaltog, saray manag-harpooneer ed sayan mundo et nepeg ya onalagey lapud inka-abala, tan aliwan lapud pansasagpot.





KAPITULO 63. Say Pueg.

Diad paway na puon, ombaleg iray sanga; manlapud sikara, saray sanga. Kanian, diad saray produktibon tema, pabalegen iray kapitulo.

Say crotch ya abitla ed akaunan pahina et makanepegan na independentin bitlaen. Saya so sakey a stick ya walaan na nikadkaduman kurti, manga duaran mitro so karukey to, ya akapeket ed starboard gunwale ya asingger ed proa, pian nawalaan na pasen a painawaan so tablan sampot na harpoon, ya say sananey ya lakseb tan walay salot to et onlelereg manlapud proa. Diad ontan say armas et tampol ya asingger ed samay mangibabantak ed satan, ya alaen to itan a tampol a singa pangiyugyog na sakey a manag-awit na katakelan ed paltog to manlapud padir. Kaugalian so pakawalaan na duaran harpoon ya akapeket ed ulpo, ya tatawagen ya unaan tan komaduan bakal.

Balet sarayan duaran harpoon, ya balang sakey et walay dilin lubir to, et parehon akakonekta ed linya; say gagala et saya: pian ipelag iran dua, no posible, sakey a tampol ed sakey tan sakey ed parehon balyena; pian no, diad onsabin panag-awit, say sakey et ontaynan, say sananey et mansiansian makabemben. Saya so pandoble na tsansa. Balet ta mabetbet ya nagagawa ya lapud tampol, mabiskeg, tan makapataktakot ya pambabatik na balyena no naawat to so inmunan bakal, agla nayarin ipelag na manag-harpooneer, anggan panon lay kirmat na galaw to, so pangipelag ed sikato na komaduan bakal. Anggaman ontan, lapud say komaduan bakal et akakonekta la ed linya, tan say linya et ombabatik, kanian satan ya armas et nepeg ya, diad amin ya ebento, et ipelag ed paway na baloto, anggan panon tan inerman; ta no andi say sankagrabian a kapeligroan et mangilaktip ed amin a lima. No napelag ed danum, ontan met ed ontan iran kaso; saray ekstran coil na box line (ya asalambit ed akaunan kapitulo) ya manggagawa ed sayan agawaan, diad maslak a kipapasen, a maalwar a nagawaan. Balet sayan kritikal a gawa et aliwan naynay ya ag-aasikasoen a walaan na sankaermenan tan sankagrabian iran inatey.

Niarum ni: nepeg mon amtaen ya no say komaduan bakal et nibantak ed dayat, magmaliw itan ya akabitin, matarem ya makapataktakot, ya onkukurang ed baloto tan balyena, ya onkukurang ed saray lubir, odino manpuputer ed saratan, tan manggawa na makapadinayew a liknaan ed amin a direksion. Nisay, diad inkalapagan, posiblin naseguro lamet itan anggad narel so balyena tan sakey a bangkay.

Isipen pa natan no panon so nagawa ed kaso na apatiran baloto ya amin et manbabaroy ed sakey a nikadkaduman mabiskeg, aktibo, tan makaamta a balyena; no lapud sarayan kalidad to, ontan met ed sanlibon aksidente ya mankakasakey ed ontan a makpel a kimey, walora odino samploran malukak a segundon balatyang so nayarin mankakasakey a manbibitin ed sikato. Ta, siempre, kada baloto et walaan na pigaran harpoon ya onyuko ed linya no say unaan et ag-epektibo ya na-dart ya ag-onbawi. Amin na sarayan detalye et matoor ya insalaysay dia, ta ag ira onsaew ya mangipaliwawa ed pigaran sankaimportantian, anggan panon lay inkakomplikado na saray bersikulo, diad saray eksena ya ipinta ed saginonor.





KAPITULO 64. Pangdem nen Stubb.

Say balyena nen Stubb et inatey ya arawi ed barko. Kalmado itan; kanian, nankakasakey kamin taloran baloto, inggapo mi so matantan ya panagguyod na tropeo ed Pequod. Tan natan, legan kamin labinwalo a lalaki a walaan na talomplo tan anem a taklay mi, tan sanlasus tan walomplon thumb tan gamegamet mi, a kalkalnan mansasagpot ed kada oras ed saman ya andi-kakanaan, matamoy a bangkay ed dayat; tan singa ag-itan onkukurang, likud ed andukey iran panaon; niiter so maong ya ebidensya ed saya nipaakar ed inkagrabe na karakel na totoon inyalis mi. Ta, diad baleg a kanal na Hang-Ho, odino antokaman so tawag da ed satan, diad China, apatira odino limaran trabahador ed dalan so mangawit na angkakabaleg a kargamento ed kapeles na sakey milya kada oras; balet sayan baleg ya argosy ya inguygoy mi et ambelat ya ginawa, a singa kargaan na baboy-lead ya dakel.

Sinmabi so kabilungetan; balet taloran silew ya ontatagey tan onleleksab ed manunan rigging na Pequod so ag-angigiya ed dalan mi; anggad inmasingger kami et anengneng mi si Ahab ya angipelag na sakey ed pigaran parol ed tapew na saray padir. Diad sakey a bekta ya inenengneng to so onkukurang a balyena, insan to inyawat so parol to ed sakey a marino, linmoob ed cabin, tan agla inmarap lamet anggad kabuasan.

Nayarin aliwan lapud say balyena et alablabas so inka-unctuate to kanian singa ipapasen na saray taga-dalin so pangan ed sikato tekep na kabusol; ompatnag a manresulta, diad pigaran paraan, manlapud konsiderasyon ya asalambit la: salanti , say sakey a too et nepeg a mangan na balon pinatey a bengatla ed dayat, tan kanen to met itan diad dilin liwawa to. Balet andi-duaruwan say sankaunaan a toon akapatey na baka et impasen a managpatey; nayarin sikatoy binitay; tan no sikato so inyan ed subok to panamegley na saray baka, sikato komon so segurado; tan seguradon kanepegan to itan no walay managpatey ya makanepegan. Onla kad tindaan na karne nen Sabado na labi tan nanengneng mo ray dakel a mabilay ya ayayep ya duaran sali ya onnenengneng ed andukey iran pila na inaatey ya apatiran sali. Agta satan a pakanengnengan so mamawala na ngipen ed panga na kanibal? Kanibal? siopay aliwan kanibal? Ibagak ed sika ya mas mainomay itan nen Fejee ya angipatey ed sakey a makulangkulang a misionaryo diad silong to sumpad onsabin eras; mas mainomay itan ya itepel na satan ya provident Fejee, kuan ko, diad agew na panangukom, nen say sika, sibilisadon tan naliwawaan ya gourmand, ya mangipapako ed saray ganso ed dalin tan manpipiesta ed saray mansasnit ya altey da diad paté-de-foie-gras mo.

Balet si Stubb, kakanen to so balyena ed dilin liwawa to, agta ontan? tan satan so mangaarum na insulto ed sakit, agta ontan? Nengnengen so panangawit na kutsilyo yo, diman, say sibilisado tan naliwawan gourmand kon mangan ed saman ya inihaw a baka, antoy ginawa ed satan a panangawit?—anto ni no ag saray pukel na agi na mismon baka ya kakanen yo? Tan antoy uusaren mon panag-ala na ngipen mo, kayari mon inakmon itan ya mataba ya gansa? Walay bago na parehon manok. Tan anton pluma so inusar na Secretary na Society for the Suppression of Cruelty ed si Ganders ya pormal ya angipatnag ed saray circular to? Diad loob labat na apalabas a sakey odino duaran bulan et akapangipasa na resolusyon so sosyedad ya ag la kaukolan so man-patron na arum nilikud ed saray bakal a bolpen.





KAPITULO 66. Say Panagpatey na Pating.

No diad Southern Fishery, say narel ya Sperm Whale, kayari na andukey tan makapakesaw ya sagpot, et inawit ed labi, aliwan singa kaslakan a bengatla so kustombre ya ituloy a tampol so panag-awit ed sikato. Ta satan a negosyo et mairap a tuloy; ag ni nasumpal a magano; tan kaukolan ya amin a lima so manggawa ed satan. Kanian, say kaslakan ya uusaren et say pangala ed amin a layag; lash so timon a'lee; insan ibaki so balang sakey ed leksab ed duyan to anggad liwawa na agew, tekep na reserba ya, anggad satan a panaon, saray panagbantay ed angkla so nepeg a pansiansiaen; salanti, duara tan duara ed loob na sakey oras, kada sanasawa, saray tripulante ya manpapawil-pawil so onlugan ed kubyerta pian nanengneng dan maong so amin.

Balet no maminsan, lalo la ed Line ed Pasipiko, sayan plano et ag makaebat ed amin; lapud saray agnabilang a grupo na pating so mantitipon ed kaliberliber na akasabit a bangkay, a no sikatoy paulyanan ed loob na anem oras, ibaga tayo, diad sakey a panaon, daiset labat so nanengneng ed pukel no kabuasan. Diad karaklan ed arum a parte na dayat, balet, ya ag tanton dakel irayan sira, say makapakelkelaw ya inka-aburido ra et nayarin nabawasan a maong no maminsan, diad mabiskeg a pangigalaw ed sikara panamegley na saray matalimbeng a pala na balyena, sakey a pamaakaran ya anggaman ontan, a diad arum a kipapasen et singa lalon mamakiwas ed sikara. Balet aliwan ontan so agawa ed kaplesan ya kaso na saray pating na Pequod; balet, pian naseguro, siopaman a laki ya ag akaugalian to so ontan iran pasingawey, ya akanengneng ed diking to ed saman a labi, et ngalngali to isipen a say interon malimpek a dayat et sakey a baleg a keso, tan saraman a pating so ulod ed satan.

Anggaman ontan, nen inyan nen Stubb so angkla kayari na pangdem to; tan nen, unong ed satan, si Queequeg tan sakey a marino ed forecastle so sinmabi ed kubyerta, anggapoy melag ya eksaytment ya agawa ed limog na saray pating; ta tampol ya insabit da ray panagputot ed gilig, tan inyabeban da ray taloran parol, pian makapangiter ira na andukey a sinag na liwawa ed tapew na malimpek a dayat, sarayan duaran marino, ya inusar da so andukey iran pala na panag-ala na balyena, so nantultuloy a mamapatey ed saray pating,* diad impangipaltog da na matarem ya asero ed aralem na bungo ra, ya anengneng labat so mabilabilay a parte na bungo ra. Balet diad makapabiglan inkagulo na saray nanlalaok tan mansasagpot ya sundalo ra, saray managpana et ag lanang ya makasabi ed marka ra; tan saya so angitarok na balon panangiparungtal na agmakapanisia ya inkaruksa na kalaban. Sikaray maruksan nan-snap, aliwan diad pan-disembowels labat na balang sakey, no ag ingen singa saray nababalang a pana, ya inmabeba, tan nankagat ed dilin pana ra; anggad saratan ya eges et singa paulit-ulit ya inakmon na saksakey a sangi, pian naekal ed kabaliktaran na manngangatngat a sugat. Nisay saya labat. Aliwan maligen so pibabali ed saray bangkay tan aswang na sarayan pinalsa. Walay klase na generic o Pantheistic ya biskeg ya singa walad saray mismon kasukasuan tan pukel da, kayari na inkaandi na nayarin tatawagen ya indibidual a bilay. Pinatey tan insakbit ed kubyerta parad pankaabigan na baog to, sakey ed sarayan pating so ngalngali to la naekal so lima na pobrin Queequeg, nen sinali ton isara so inatey a taklay na makapatey a panga to.

*Say pala ya uusaren ed panag-ala na balyena et gawad sankaabigan ya asero; et nipaakar ed kabaleg na akaunat a lima na sakey a too; tan diad inkalapagan ya kurti, et mipara ed kagawaan ed hardin ya inngaran to; saray gilig to labat so perpekton patatdang, tan say tagey a sampot to et mas mainget nen say leksab. Sayan armas et lanang ya matarem anggad posible; tan no uusaren et sagpaminsan ya papaaligwasen, a singa labaha. Diad socket to, sakey a malet a poste, manlapud duamplo anggad talomplon mitro so karukey to, so niiyan parad sakey a panangawit.

"Anggapoy pibabali nen Queequeg no antoy dios ya nanggawa ed sikaton pating," inkuan na masebeg, ya makapasinagem ya intagey tan inleksab toy lima to; "kasal si Fejee god o Nantucket god; balet say dios ya nanggawa na pating et nepeg ya sakey a dam Ingin."
