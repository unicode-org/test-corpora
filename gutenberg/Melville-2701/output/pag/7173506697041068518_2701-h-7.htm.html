KAPITULO 33. Say Specksnyder.

Nipaakar ed saray opisyal na whale-craft, saya so singa maong a pasen a pangiyan na daiset a domestic peculiarity ed barko, a nanlapud kiwawala na harpooneer a klase na opisyal, sakey a klase ya ag kabat ed anggan dinan a marine nilikud ed whale-fleet.

Say baleg ya kaimportantian ya iiter ed bokasyon na manag-harpooneer et napatnagan ed katuaan, ya diad gapo et diad daan ya Dutch Fishery, duaran siglo tan onsulok ni so apalabas, say panangidaulo ed sakey a barkon balyena et aliwan sigpot ya walad samay toon tatawagen natan ya kapitan, noagta naapag ed baetan to tan sakey ya opisyal ya tatawagen ya Specksnyder. Say literal ya kabaliksan na sayan salita et Fat-Cutter; say pangusar, balet, diad ilalabas na panaon et ginawa ton mipara itan ed Chief Harpooneer. Diad saraman ya panaon, say autoridad na kapitan et limitado labat ed panaglayag tan panangasikaso ed barko; bangta say departamento na panag-ayam na balyena tan amin a kapagaan to, say Specksnyder odino Chief Harpooneer so manuley. Diad British Greenland Fishery, diad silong na aderal a titulo na Specksioneer, sayan daan ya opisyal na Dutch et siansia nin mansiasiansia, balet say datin dignidad to et makapaermen ya apatikeyan. Diad kaplesan et sikatoy senior Harpooneer; tan bilang ontan, sakey labat ed saray mas abeban subaltern na kapitan. Anggaman ontan, say itatalona na panaglayag na panagsigay ed balyena et mandedepende ed maong a kondukta na saray balyena, tan lapud diad American Fishery et sikatoy aliwa labat ya importantin opisyal ed baloto, noagta diad pigaran sirkumstansya (panbantay ed labi diad panagsigay na balyena) sikato met so mangidaulo ed kubyerta na barko; kanian say baleg a politikal a ganggan na dayat et mankaukolan, a sikatoy nepeg a manbilay a nibiig ed saray lalakin walad arap na palo, tan diad pigaran paraan et nibiig bilang superyor da ed propesyon; anggaman lanang, ed sikara, ya ipapasen dan kapara ra ed sosyedad.

Natan, say baleg a pandumaan na opisyal tan too ed dayat et saya—say unonan manbibilay ed beneg, say unor ya onarap. Kanian, diad saray barkon balyena tan saray negosyante, saray kapareha et walad kapitan so panaayaman da; tan ontan met, diad karaklan ed saray manag-awit na balyena ed Amerika et saray manag-awit na balyena et walad beneg na barko. Labay ton ibaga, mangan ira ed kabin na kapitan, tan onugip ira ed pasen ya agdirektan mitongtong ed satan.

Anggaman say andukey a panaon na panaglayag na balyena ed Abalaten (say sankabayagan ed amin a panaglayag natan odino kapiganman a ginawa na too), saray nikadkaduman kapeligroan na satan, tan say komunidad na interes a walad limog na sakey a kompanya, ya amin da, atagey man odino abeba, so mandedependi parad ganansia ra, aliwan diad niletneg a sueldo, noagta diad kaslakan a kapalaran da, kaiba so inkaalerto, inkamaalwar tan inkakuli ra; anggaman amin na sarayan bengatla et diad arum a kaso et mangiter na agtanton malet a disiplina nen saray negosyante ed inkalapagan; balet, agmo la nononoten no panon karakel so singa sakey a daan a pamilya ed Mesopotamia irayan balyena, diad pigaran primitibon inkagawa, a manbibilay a saniiba; parad amin na satan, saray makapadinayew ya paway, anggan panon, na quarter-deck et matalag ya materyal ya relaks, tan anggan panon et naandi. Tua, dakel so barkon Nantucket ya nanengneng mo so kapitan ya manpaparada ed quarter-deck to ya walay malikeliket ya inkabantog ya agnaparaan na antokaman ya military navy; andi, mangaala na ngalngalin singa karakel na galang a singa sikatoy akasulong na imperial purple, tan aliwan say sankagrabian ya pilot-cloth.

Tan anggaman ed amin a totoo say makulangkulang a kapitan na Pequod so sankaabebaan ya inter ed ontan a klase na sankaabebaan a panag-isip; tan anggaman say alenleneg a panangigalang ya inkerew to, et say ag-impatnag, tampol a katutulok; anggaman agto labay ya ekalen na sapatos ed sali to antis ya onloob ed quarter-deck; tan anggaman wala ray panaon ya, lapud nikadkaduman sirkumstansya ya misiglaotan ed saray ebento kayari na saya ya detalyadon detalyado, intongtong to iratan ed nikadkaduman termino, balanglan diad panagpaabeba odino diad terrorem , odino arum ni; balet anggan si Kapitan Ahab et agto naimano so sankaimportantian iran porma tan uusaren na dayat.

Nisay, nayarin, ag-itan natebek ed kaunoran, a diad benegan na saratan a porma tan panguusar, a singa panangibaga, sikatoy no maminsan et manpapaskara ed inkasikato; ag-inilaloan ya uusaren iratan parad arum tan mas pribadon gagala nen say lehitimon gagala ra. Say pigaran sultanismo na utek to, ya diad maabig a grado et nansiansian ag-apatnagan; diad panamegley na saratan a porma et say sultanismo so nagmaliw ya inkatoo ed sakey ya agnasuppiat a diktadura. Ta antokaman so intelektual ya inkasuperyor na sakey a too, agton balot nayarian ya ipasen so praktikal, walan inkasupremo ed arum a totoo, no anggapoy tulong na sakey a klase na arts tan entrenchments ed paway, a lawas, diad inkasikara, mas o kulang a paltry tan abeba. Saya so, ya andi-anggaan ya mangiyagel ed saray tuan prinsipe na Dios ed Empiryo manlapud saray panag-ayew na mundo; tan mangitilak na sankatageyan a kagalangan ya nayarin iter na sayan dagem, ed saramay totoon nagmaliw a bantog lapud andi-anggaan ya inkaabeba ra ed pinilin akaamot a lima na Divine Inert, nen say lapud andi-duaruwan inkasuperyor da ed inatey a lebel na karaklan. Say baleg a birtud so walad sarayan angkekelag a bengatla no saray alablabas a politikal a superstisyon so mangipuonan ed saratan, a diad arum a inkagawa na ari et anggan diad makulangkulang ya inka-makulangkulang et naitdan iratan na pakayari. Balet no, singa ed kaso nen Nicholas the Czar, say singsing a korona na geograpikon empiryo so manliber ed utek na empiryo; insan, saray plebeian a pulok so onkukurong ed arap na baleg a sentralisasyon. Nisay, say makapasinagem a dramatista ya mangipapatnag na mortal ya ag-inkayadyari ed sankagrabian tan direktan panag-uyog to, so agbalot makalingwan ed sakey a pakapatnagan, ya importantin tuloy ed arte to, a singa samay tutukoyen natan.

Balet si Ahab, say Kapitan ko, et siansia nin onkukurang ed arap ko ed amin a Nantucket ya inka-grim tan shaggig to; tan diad sayan episode nipaakar ed saray Emperador tan Arari, agko nepeg ya iyamot ya walay pibabali ko labat ed sakey a mairap-bilay a masiken a manag-anop na balyena a singa sikato; tan, sirin, amin ya makapadinayew iran kagawaan tan kaabungan et ag ko naawat. O, Ahab! no antoy magmaliw ya baleg ed sika, kaukolan ya alaen ed katawenan, tan isawsaw ed aralem, tan iparungtal ed anggapoy laman ya dagem!





KAPITULO 34. Say Cabin-Table.

Ugto la natan; tan si Dough-Boy, say mayordomo, ya inyan toy amputin tinapay ya lupa to manlapud cabin-scuttle, et inyabawag toy pangdem ed katawan tan amo to; ya akayurong ed lee quarter-boat, et manbabantay labat ed banwa; tan natan et ag-onsasalitan mankuenta ed latitude ed makinis, singa medalyon a tabla, ya inreserba parad satan ya inagew-agew a gagala diad tagey a parte na garing a sali to. Lapud ag to inimano so balita, isipen mo ya ag nadngel na maermen ya si Ahab so inkaabeba to. Balet diad kaplesan, nen inegnaan to ray mizen shrouds, inyurong toy sarili to ed deck, tan diad parepareho tan ag-inkaliketan a boses, ya inkuan ton, “Dinner, Mr. Starbuck,” et naandi ed loob na cabin.

Nen say unor ya echo na kundang na sultan to et naandi la, tan si Starbuck, say inmunan Emir, et walaan na amin a rason pian ipasen ya sikatoy akayurong la, insan binmangon si Starbuck manlapud kareenan to, linmingaw na pigaran tabla, tan, kayari na sakey a mabiskeg a pannengneng ed binnacle, inkuan to, tekep na pigaran panliket, “Say pangdem, linmeksab si Mr. Say komaduan Emir et nan-lounge ed rigging na daiset, insan to inyegyeg na daiset so manunan brace, pian nanengneng to no kasin ayos labat itan ya importantin lubir, sikato met so mangawit ed daan a pabelat, tan diad maples ya “Dinner, Mr. Flask,” et tinmumbok ed saray akauna ed sikato.

Balet say komatlon Emir, ya natan et nanenengneng ton bukbukor to labat ed quarter-deck, et singa nainawaan ed pigaran makapainteres ya panangipuera; ta, ya ipapaarap toy amin a klase na makaamta iran wink ed amin a klase na direksion, tan inekal to ray sapatos to, sikatoy ontupak ed sakey a maksil balet anggapoy ungol ya ungol na hornpipe ed tapew na ulo na Grand Turk; insan, diad panamegley na sakey a marundunong a panag-isip, ya inyan to so kalo to ed tagey na mizentop parad sakey ya estante, sikatoy onleksab a manpapaspas anggad ag-onkulang a sikatoy nanengneng manlapud kubyerta, ya inbalikas to so amin ya arum nin prosesyon, diad pangiyakar to ed beneg tekep na musika. Balet antis ya onloob ed puerta na cabin ed leksab, sikatoy tinmunda, angipatnag na balon lupa, insan, say independentin, makapaliket a melag a Flask so linmoob ed arap nen Arin Ahab, diad karakter nen Abjectus, odino say Aripen.

Aliwan saya so sankaabebaan ed saray nikadkaduman bengatla ya pinawala na graben inka-artipisyal na saray panag-usar ed dayat, a legan ya walad paway na kubyerta et arum ya opisyal so, no naproboka, et makpel tan ag-onsusumpa ed komandante ra; balet, samplora ed sakey, paulyanan iramay mismon opisyal ed ontumbok a bekta ya onla ed niyugali dan pangdem ed satan met lanlamang a kabin na komandante, tan tampol so ag-ira makapasakit, agla pigan makapabaing tan mapaabeba ya awawey da ed sikato, legan ya akayurong ed uloan na lamisaan; makapakelkelaw iya, no maminsan et makapaliket. Akin et ontan so pandumaan? Sakey a problema? Nayarin andi. Pian si Belsasar, Ari na Babilonia; tan pian sikatoy si Belsasar, aliwan mapaatagey noagta magalang, seguradon walay pigaran liknaan na ordinaryon inkabantog. Balet samay walaan na manepeg ya ari tan intelihentin espiritu so mangidadaulo ed dilin pribadon lamisaan to na saray imbitaan a bisita, say agnasuppiat a pakayari tan pananguley na indibidual ya impluensya na satan a too ed saman a panaon; say inkaari na satan a too et onlampas ed si Belsasar, ta aliwan si Belsasar so sankatalonggaringan. Siopaman ya aminsan labat ya angan ed saray kakaaro to, et alikna to no antoy kabaliksan na pagmaliw ya Cæsar. Saya so sakey a panag-anito na inka-zar na sosyedad ya anggapoy makalaban. Natan, no diad sayan konsiderasyon et idagdag mo so opisyal ya inkasupremo na sakey a kapitan na barko, sirin, diad panamegley na konklusyon, naala yo so sengegan na satan a nikadkaduman bilay ed dayat ya asalambit la.

Diad tapew na lamisaan ton gawad garing, si Ahab so angidaulo a singa sakey ya emel, maned a sea-lion diad gilig na amputin korales, ya apaliberan na saray mahilig ed guerra balet ta siansia nin magalang iran kilaw to. Diad dugan turno to, kada opisyal et manaalagar ya naserbian. Sikaray angkekelag nin ugugaw antis nen Acab; balet, diad si Ahab, singa anggapoy anggan melag ya inka-arogante ed sosyedad. Diad saksakey a kanonotan, amin da et akanengneng ed kutsilyo na masiken, legan ton uukiten so manunan putahe ed arap to. Agko iisipen ya parad mundo et pinabanday da itan a bekta anggano daiset labat so obserbasyon da, anggan diad neutral a tema a singa say panaon. Andi! Tan nen inunat toy kutsilyo tan tinidor to, ya diad baetan to et akasara so piraso na karne na baka, insinyas nen Ahab so plato nen Starbuck ed sikato, say asawa to et inawat toy karne to a singa mangaawat na limos; tan putoten a maong; tan medyo ginmapo no, ompan, say kutsilyo et akapeket ed plato; tan inngalngal to itan ya anggapoy ungol; tan inakmon to itan, aliwan andiay panag-alwar. Ta, singa say ponsia na Coronation ed Frankfort, ya diman so German Emperor et mangan a kaiba so pitoran Imperial Electors, ontan met irayan cabin meals et singa solemnidon panangan, ya kakanen ed makapataktakot a kareenan; balet diad lamisaan et ag to insebel nen masiken ya Ahab so pantotongtong; sikato labat so emel. Agaylan pakainawaan nen nalner si Stubb, nen biglan nan-raket so sakey ya ikol ed hold ed leksab. Tan say pobrin melag a Flask, sikato so sankayugtanan ya anak, tan melag ya ugaw ed sayan makapakesaw a party na pamilya. Saray pukel to et saray pukel na karne na baka; say kayarian to et saray tambol. Parad si Flask ya inisip ton tulongan toy sarili to, saya et singa to itan panagtakew ed primeron grado. No tinulongan toy sarili to ed saman a lamisaan, andi-duaruwan ag to la komon nayarian ya itagey so ulo to ed sayan matuan mundo; anggaman ontan, makapakelaw ya ibaga, ag to balot insebel nen Ahab. Tan no tinulongan nen Flask so sarili to, posiblin ag itan naimano nen Ahab. Say sankaabebaan ed amin, kasin inisip nen Flask ya tulongan toy sarili to ya mantikilya. No kasin inisip ton ag-itan inawat na saray akankayarian na barko ed sikato, lapud inka-clot na satan ed malinew tan maaw-awang a kolor na baog to; odino no kasin inisip ton, diad andukey a panaglayag to ed ontan ya anggapoy tindaan a danum, say mantikilya et walad sankatageyan a kantidad, tan sirin et aliwan parad sikato, sakey a subaltern; anggaman ontan, Flask, alas! et sakey a toon andiay mantikilya!

Sakey ni. Si Flask so unor ya linmeksab ed pangdem, tan si Flask so sankaunaan ya inmalagey. Nonoten! Ta diad saya et say pangdem nen Flask et ag-aka-jam ed punto na panaon. Si Starbuck tan Stubb so parehon angigapo ed sikato; balet ta walaan met ira na pribilehyo ya manpainawa ed beneg. No si Stubb, ya mas atagey labat nen say Flask, et walaan na daiset a ganas, tan agmanbayag et mangipanengneng na sintomas na pansampot na panangan to, sirin si Flask et kaukolan ton pabiskegen so inkasikato, agto naala so onsulok ed taloran sangi ed satan ya agew; ta sumlang ed masanton panangusar nen Stubb so unaan nen Flask ed deck. Kanian, aminsan et inamin nen Flask ed pribado, ya manlapu la nen inmalagey ed dignidad na sakey ya opisyal, manlapud saman a bekta et agto la amta no antoy kabaliksan na pagmaliw ya narasan, mas o kulang. Ta say kinan to et agto tanton naekal so eras to, noagta pinansiansia to itan ya ag-ompatey ed sikato. Say kareenan tan inkapnek, inisip nen Flask, et naandi lad eges ko. Sakey ak ya opisyal; balet, labay ko komon ya makasigay na daiset ya daan ya karne na baka diad forecastle, a singa gagawaen ko nensaman nen wala ak ni ed arap na palo. Wala ray bunga na promosyon natan; wadtan so inkaandi-kakanaan na gloria: wadtan so inkabaliwala na bilay! Nilikud ni, no bilang ta walay sakey a marino na Pequod ya walay pasnok tod si Flask diad opisyal a kapasidad nen Flask, say kaukolan labat ya gawaen na marino, pian makagamor na magenap a panagbales, et onla ed beneg legan na panangan, tan nengnengen toy Flask diad sky-light na cabin, ya akayurong ya makulangkulang tan mankelaw ed arap nen Aabh.

Natan, si Ahab tan say taloran kakaaro to so nanggawa na nayarin tatawagen ya unonan lamisaan ed abung na Pequod. Kayari na impaway da, ya agawa ed inbaliktad ya inkauksoy na insabi ra, say abel ya canvas et inekal, odino impawil ed pigaran apuraan ya inkauksoy na pallid steward. Insan saray taloran manag-arpa so inimbitaan ed ponsia, sikara so nakekeraan ya legate to. Nanggawa ra na sakey a klase na temporaryon kuarto na saray lingkor ed atagey tan mabiskeg a kabin.

Diad nikadkaduman pandumaan ed ag naanosan ya panameget tan andiay ngaran ya agnanengneng ya panagdominar na lamisaan na kapitan, et say interon andiay-panangasin lisensya tan inkamainomay, say ngalngalin makapataktakot a demokrasya na saraman ya abebbeban kaparan manag-arpo. Bangta saray amo ra, saray kapareha, et singa natatakot ed tanol na saray bisagra na panga ra, saray manag-harpooner et manngalngal na kakanen da tekep na panliket kanian walay balita ed satan. Angan ira a singa saray katawan; pinano da ray eges da a singa saray barko na India ed interon agew ya mankarga na saray rekado. Ontan lay makapadinayew iran ganas nen Queequeg tan Tashtego, ya pian napano iray bakantin ginawa na akaunan pananganan, mabetbet a say amputin Dough-Boy et labalabay toy mangawit na sakey a baleg a baron na salt-junk, a singa inala ed solidon baka. Tan no sikatoy aliwan mabilabilay ed satan, no sikatoy ag-onla ed maples a hop-skip-and-jump, si Tashtego so walaan na ag-gentleman a paraan pian napapeles to diad pangipaltog to na tinidor ed beneg to, a singa harpoon. Tan aminsan, si Daggoo, ya biglan nan-humor, et tinmulong ed memorya nen Dough-Boy diad impan-agaw tod laman to, tan insulong toy ulo to ed sakey a baleg ya anggapoy karga ton trencher, bangta si Tashtego, ya sankabembenan toy kutsilyo, et ginmapon angiparaan na sirkulo pian sikatoy na-scalp. Sikatoy natural ya mannerbiyos, manggiwgiw a klase na melag a too, sayan mayordomo ya singa tinapay so lupa to; say ilalak na sakey a bangkrap a panadero tan sakey a nars ed ospital. Tan antoy agawa ed say akaalagey ya palabas na makapataktakot ya maimpis ya si Ahab, tan say panapanaon ya magulon ibisita na sarayan taloran masebeg, say interon bilay nen Dough-Boy et sakey a tuloytuloy ya panngalngali na bibil. Kaslakan, kayari ton anengneng iray harpooneers ya akaparaan na amin a bengatla ya kaukolan da, sikatoy ontaynan ed saray lima ra ya onloob ed melag a pantry to ya asingger, tan natatakot ya onnengneng ed sikara diad panamegley na saray kurtina na puerta to, anggad nasumpal so amin.

Makapadinayew so pakanengneng ed si Queequeg ya akayurong ed arap nen Tashtego, ya akaarap ed saray ngipen to ed Indian: diad krus ed sikara, si Daggoo ya akayurong ed datal, ta say sakey a bangko et mangiyakar komon ed ulo to ya aka-plumed ed abeban carlines; diad kada galaw na angkakabaleg iran sali to, ya manggagawa ed abeban framework na cabin ya onyegyeg, a singa no say sakey ya elepante ed Aprika et manpapasahero ed sakey a barko. Balet anggaman ed amin na saya, say baleg ya negro et makapakelkelaw ya ag-onkakakan, agko labay ya ibagan marakep. Singa ag posiblin diad panamegley na ontan iran melag a sangi et napansiansia to so biskeg ya inkayat ed ontanlan malaknab, baronial, tan makapadinayew a too. Balet, andi-duaruwan sayan noble ya atap ya ayep et nankan na mabiskeg tan oniinum na aralem ed daakan ya elemento na dagem; tan diad panamegley na saray pinalaknab ya eleng to et nasingsing to so makapadinayew a bilay na saray mundo. Aliwan diad karne na baka o tinapay, saray higante so nagawa odino napapakan. Balet si Queequeg, sikatoy walaan na mortal, barbarian smack na bibil legan ya mangan—sakey a makapadimlan tanol—ya ontan lan karakel, kanian say manggiwgiw a Dough-Boy et ngalngali la onnengneng no wala ray marka na ngipen ya akaamot ed saray mismon malimpek a taklay to. Tan nen narengel toy Tashtego ya mangankanta parad sikato pian naala toy sarili to, pian naala iray pukel to, say simplin mayordomo et ngalngali la nabuyak iray plato ya akabitin ed kaliberliber to diad pantry, lapud biglan inmabig to. Nisay say bato ya awiten na saray manag-arpa ed bulsa ra, parad saray gayang tan arum niran armas da; tan diad panamegley na saratan a bato, diad pangdem, et mapasirayew dan patadem iray kutsilyo ra; satan ya makapaermen ya tanol et aglan balot makapareen ed mairap-bilay ya Dough-Boy. Panon ton nalingwanan ya nen wala nid Isla to, si Queequeg, parad sakey, et seguradon nankasalanan na pigaran makapapatey, makapaliket ya ag-inkahustisya. Abagey! Dough-Boy! mairap so bilay na amputin serbidor ya manaalagar ed saray kanibal. Aliwan napkin so nepeg ton awiten ed taklay to, no ag ingen sakey a panyo. Balet, diad maabig a panaon, diad baleg a liket to, saray taloran mibabakal ed dayat na asin so ontalindeg tan ontaynan; diad saray layag to ya agmakapanisia tan manbabaroy na istorya, amin ya martial bones da et onkukurong ed kada kundang da, a singa saray Moorish scimetars ed saray scabbards.

Balet, anggaman sarayan barbaro et mangan ed cabin, tan diman ira manaayam; balet, lapud ag-ira mankikimey ed saray ugali ra, ag ira balot onloob ed satan likud ed oras na panangan, tan sakbay na oras na ugip, no ondalan ira ed satan ya anggad dilin panaayaman da.

Diad sayan sakey a pamaakaran, si Ahab et singa aliwan eksepsion ed karaklan ed saray Amerikanon kapitan na balyena, ya, bilang sakey a grupo, et mas labay dan iisipen ya unong ed kanepegan da et kayarian da so kabin na barko; tan diad panamegley labat na panangasi et siopaman ya arum et, anggan kapigan, naabuloyan diman. Kanian, diad tua, saray kapareha tan saray harpooneer na Pequod et nayarin mas suston nibaga ya manaayam ira ed paway na cabin nen say diad loob to. Ta nen linmoob ira diman, singa itan puerta na karsada ya onloob ed abung; onliko ed loob ed sakey a bekta, insan labat onliko ed ontumbok; tan, bilang permanentin bengatla, manaayam ed paway na dagem. Anggapo met so naandi ed sikara lapud saya; diad kabin et anggapoy kaiba; diad sosyal, ag-onsabi si Ahab. Anggaman sikatoy inlaktip ed sensus na Kakristianoan, sikatoy sankaili ed satan. Sikatoy nambilay ed mundo, a singa say unor ed saray Grisly Bears ya nambilay ed abayag lan Missouri. Tan nen linma lay Primabera tan Tiagew, saman ya atap ya Logan na katakelan, ya inkotkot toy sarili to ed abot na kiew, et nanayam diman ed panaoy ambetel, ya mansususo ed saray sali to; kanian, diad inkatatken to, say kamarerwa nen Ahab, ya akasara ed ungib a puon na laman to, et diman so naakan ed saray makapaermen a sali na kabilungetan to!





KAPITULO 35. Say Ulo na Palo.

Diad panaon na mas marakep a panaon, a diad manepeg a panag-ikot ko ed arum a marino et sinmabi so inmunan ulo na palo ko.

Diad maslak ya American whalemen, saray mast-heads et walay totoon ngalngalin mankakasakey ed itaynan na barko ed daongan to; anggano nayarin wala ni labinliman libon milya, tan onsulok ni, ya kaukolan ton layag antis ya makasabi ed dugan panaglayag to. Tan no, kayari na talora, apatiran, odino limay taon a panaglayag et sikatoy onsasabi lad abung to ya walay antokaman ya anggapoy karga to—singa bilang, sakey ya anggapoy karga a banga—sirin, saray ulo na palo to et mansiansian walay totoo anggad sampot; tan anggad ag ni onloob iray skysail-pole to ed pegley na saray spire na daongan, et sikatoy sigpot ya ontunda ed ilalo ton makala na sakey nin balyena.

Natan, lapud say negosyo na saray akaalagey ya ulo na palo, diad gilig na dayat odino diad lugan, et kadaanan tan makapainteres, manpapaliwawa tayo dia. Ipapasen ko ya saray sankaunaan ya angipaalagey na ulo na palo et saray daan ya Ehipsio; lapud, diad amin ya impansukimat ko, anggapoy naromog kon akauna nen sikara. Ta anggaman saray inmuunan atateng da, saray managpaalagey na Babel, et seguradon, diad panamegley na tori ra, et ginagala dan ipaalagey so sankatageyan ya ulo na palo ed interon Asia, odino diad Aprika; balet (sakbay ya inyan ed satan so unor a trak) singa samay baleg a palo ra a bato so nayarin nibaga ya linmabas ed tabla, diad makapataktakot a dagem na sanok na Dios; kanian, agtayo nayarin iyuna irayan managpaalagey na Babel nen saray Ehipsio. Tan say saray Ehipsio et sakey a bansan walaan na mast-head standers, et sakey ya ibabaga ya nibase ed kaslakan a sisiaen na saray arkeologo, ya saray inmunan piramid et inletneg parad gagala na astronomiya: sakey a teorya ya susuportaan na nikadkaduman singa takayan ya inkaporma na amin ya apatiran gilig na saratan ya bilding; diad ontan, diad makapadinayew ya andukey ya pangitagey na sali ra, saraman ya daan ya astronomo et niyugali dan ontatdang ed toktok, tan mangansion parad saray balon bitewen; anggano saray nengnengen na modernon barko et mankanta parad layag, odino balyena ya onkukurang la. Diad Saint Stylites, say bantog ya Kristianon ermitanyo nensaman, ya angipaalagey ed sikato na atagey a lusek a bato diad desierto tan inusar toy interon unor a parte na bilay to ed toktok na satan, ya ipapaatagey toy naakan to manlapud dalin panamegley na sakey a tackle; diad sikato et walaan itayo na makapadinayew ya alimbawa na ag-ontakot a manag-alagey na saray ulo na palo; ya ag nepeg ya napaekal ed pasen to lapud saray lurem odino yelo, uran, yelo, odino yelo; balet ta makpel ton inarap so amin a bengatla anggad sampot, literal ya inatey ed posisyon to. Saray modernon standers-of-mast-heads et walaan itayo labat na andiay-bilay a set; saray totoon gawad bato, balatyang, tan gansa labat; ya anggaman sarag dan arapen so maksil a dagem, sikaray siansia nin ag makayarin mankanta no makadiskobre ira na antokaman ya nikadkaduman pasingawey. Walay Napoleon; ya, diad toktok na kolum na Vendome, et akaalagey ya akatiklop iray taklay to, manga sanlasus tan limamplon mitro ed tagey; ag-inmabobon, natan, no siopay manuley ed saray kubyerta ed leksab; balanglan si Louis Philippe, Louis Blanc, odino si Louis the Devil. Say Great Washington, met, et akaalagey ed tagey na atagey a main-mast to ed Baltimore, tan singa sakey ed saray lusek nen Hercules, say lusek to so mamaneknek ed satan a punto na inkabantog na too ya pigpigara labat a totoo so makalampas. Si Admiral Nelson, ya akaalagey met ed sakey a capstan ya gawad gun-metal, et akaalagey so mast-head to diad Trafalgar Square; tan kapiganman ya masyadon natatakot ed satan ya asewek na London, wala ni so tanda ya walay akaamot ya bayani diman; ta no iner so kawalaan na asewek, nepeg a walay apoy. Balet anggan say baleg a Washington, odino si Napoleon, odino si Nelson, et ag makaebat ed anggan sakey a uran a yelo manlapud leksab, anggan panon lay inkabaliw da pian mikaaro ed saray simbawa ra ed saray ag-onkulang a kubyerta ya sankanengneng da; anggaman ontan et nayarin isipen, ya saray espiritu ra et onloob ed makapal a liwawa na arapen, tan mangibaga no anto ran shoals tan no anto ran batobato so nepeg ya paliisan.

Nayarin singa ag manepeg ya ipasen ed antokaman a pamaakaran iray mast-head standers na dalin tan saramay walad dayat; balet ta diad tua et aliwan ontan, et malinew ya napaneknekan na sakey a bengatla ya si Obed Macy, say saksakey ya historyador na Nantucket, so walaan na eebatan. Ibabaga na makanepegan ya Obed ed sikatayo, ya nen inmunan panaon na panagsigay na balyena, antis ya naynay ya ipapalabas iray barko pian anapen so ayayep, saray totoo ed satan ya isla et angipaalagey na atagey iran spars ed gilig na dayat, ya ditan et ontatdang iray managbantay panamegley na saray akapasak a cleats, a singa saray manok ya ontatdang ed tagey na kaabungan. Pigaran taon lay apalabas sayan parehon plano so inadapta na saray Bay whalemen na New Zealand, ya nen inekal da so ayayep, angiter ira na abiso ed saray akaparaan lan baloto ya asingger ed gilig na dayat. Balet sayan kustombre et nagmaliw lan daan; insan tayo onla ed sakey a dugan ulo na palo, samay sakey a barkon balyena ed dayat. Say taloran ulo na palo et walay totoo to manlapud isabi na banwa anggad iselek na banwa; saray marino ya mansusublayan (singa diad timon), tan manrerelieve ed balang sakey kada duay oras. Diad mareen a panaon na tropiko et makapaliket a tuloy so ulo na palo; andi, parad sakey a toon mankukugip a mannononot et makapaliket itan. Diman ka akaalagey, sanlasus a mitro ed tagey na saray mareen a kubyerta, a manpapaarap ed kaaralman, a singa saray palo et angkakabaleg iran palo, bangta diad silong mo tan diad baetan na saray salim, a singa panangibaga, et manlalangoy iray sankabalgan a ulsa ed dayat, a singa saray barko a datin manlalayag ed baetan na saray botas na bantog a Colossus diad daan a Rhodes. Manalalagey ka diman, ya abalang ed andi-anggaan a serye na dayat, ya anggapoy onkukurang noag saray daluyon. Say barko ya ag-onkukurang et ag-onkukurang; onsiplog so makapaugip a dagem na negosyo; amin a bengatla et mamapaermen ed sika. Diad karaklan, diad sayan bilay na panag-ayam na balyena ed tropiko, sakey a makapadinayew ya ag-inkayadyari so mangipuonan ed sika; anggapoy narengel mon balita; ag manbasa na saray gazette; saray ekstra ya walaan na makapabiglan salaysay na saray kaslakan a bengatla et agbalot mamalikdo ed sika ed saray agnakaukolan ya eksayting; wala ray narerengel mon kairapan ed pamilya; bangkrap iran seguridad; inkagba na saray stock; ag ka balot napapagaan no antoy kanen mo ed pangdem—ta amin ya kakanen mo ed loob na taloy taon tan onsulok ni et akaamot ed saray bariles, tan say bayar mo ed pamasahe et agnauman.

Diad sakey ed saratan a manag-awit na balyena ed abalaten, diad andukey a talora odino apatiran taon a biahe, a singa kaslakan a nagagawa, say dagup na nanduruman oras ya inusar mo ed uloan na palo et mankantidad na pigaran bulan. Tan makapaermen ta say pasen ya pangiteran mo na baleg a kabiangan na interon panaon na natural a bilay mo, et nepeg ya makapaermen ya andian na antokaman ya makaasingger ed sakey a maabig a panaayaman, odino inadapta pian mamawala na komportablen lokalidad na liknaan, a singa say nipaakar ed sakey a dukolan, sakey a duyan, sakey a luluganan na bangkay, sakey a kahon na bantay, sakey a pulpito, sakey a sugn, sakey a sugn o antokaman niran melag tan arum nira saray kagawaan ya ditan et temporaryon iyaamot na totoo so inkasikara. Say sankabalegan ya pasen ya panyurongan mo et say ulo na t' gallant-mast, ya diman ka akaalagey ed duaran manipis ya manparaan a stick (ya ngalngalin nikadkaduma ed saray balyena) ya tatawagen ya t' gallant cross-trees. Dia, ya ibabatik ed dayat, say manangigapo et singa ka-komportable no sikatoy akaalagey ed saray saklor na baka. Pian segurado, no ambetel so panaon et nayarin awiten mo so abung mo ed tagey, a singa kurti na relo; balet diad suston panangibaga say sankakapal ya kawes na panagbantay et aliwan sakey ya abung nen say ag-akasulong a laman; ta no panon ya say kamarerwa et akapeket ed loob na laman ton tabernakulo, tan ag makagalaw ya bulos ed loob to, odino makaalis ni ingen ed satan, ya ag narisgo ya ompatey (singa sakey ya ignorante ya peregrino ya ombabaliw ed niebe iran Alps no panaoy ambetel); kanian say watch-coat et aliwan singa abung no ag ingen sakey labat ya sobre, odino kaaruman ya katat ya akasakbong ed sika. Agmo nayarian so mangiyan na estante odino kahon ed laman mo, tan agmo la nayarin gawaen ya kombenientin aparador na relo mo.

Nipaakar ed amin na saya, makapaermen ta saray mast-head na sakey a barkon balyena ed abalaten et anggapoy naitdan na saray makapaimon ya angkekelag a tolda odino pulpito, a tatawagen ya crow's-nest , a ditan et protektado iray panagbantay na sakey a manag-ayam na balyena ed Greenland manlapud mauges a panaon na ayelo iran dayat. Diad salaysay nen Kapitan Sleet ed abay na apoy, ya apauloan na “Sakey a Pambiahe ed limog na saray Iceberg, diad pananap ed Greenland Whale, tan diad tua et parad pakadiskobre lamet ed saray Abalang ya Kolonya na Iceland ed Daan a Greenland;” diad sayan makapadinayew a tomo, amin ya akaalagey na mast-heads et walaan na makapasagyat a sirkumstansyal a salaysay nipaakar ed agano nin naimbento ya crow's-nest na Glacier, a satan so ngaran na maong a barko nen Kapitan Sleet. Tinawag to itan ya Sleet's crow's-nest , bilang panangigalang ed sarili to; sikato so orihinal ya imbentor tan patentee, tan bulos ed amin ya makapabaing ya palson delikado, tan ibabaga ton no tawagen tayo ray anak tayo ed ngaran tayo (sikatayo ran ama so orihinal ya imbentor tan patentee), ontan met so nepeg tayon pangiter na ngaran ed sarili tayo ed antokaman ya arum nin kagawaan ya nayarin nawalaan tayo. Diad kurti, say Sleet's crow's-nest et singa baleg a tierce odino tubo; lukas itan ed tagey, balet, diman et walay nagalaw ya side-screen pian ag-onkukurang ed dagem no walay maksil a bagyo. Lapud akapeket ka ed toktok na palo, ontatdang ka ed satan panamegley na melag a trap-hatch ed leksab. Diad beneg na barko, odino asingger ed beneg na barko, et walay komportablen yurongan, ya walay locker ed leksab parad saray payong, comforter, tan amerikana. Diad arap et walay katat ya rack, ya pangiyanan na trumpeta, tubo, teleskopyo, tan arum niran kagawaan ed dayat. Nen si Kapitan Sleet so mismon angialagey ed mast-head to ed sayan crow's-nest to, imbaga tod sikatayo ya sikatoy naynay ya walay awit ton paltog (ya akapeket met ed rack), kaiba so powder flask tan paltog, pian naekal iray stray narwhales, odino saray vagrant sea unicorns ya onloloob ed saratan a danum; ta agmo ra napaltog manlapud kubyerta lapud resistensya na danum, balet say panpaltog ed sikara et duman tuloy a bengatla. Natan, malinew ya kimey na panangaro nen Kapitan Sleet so pangideskribe, a singa gagawaen to, ed amin ya angkekelag a detalyadon kombeniensya na ubong na uwak to; balet anggaman pinalaknab to so dakel ed saraya, tan anggaman ipapasen to itayo ed sakey a sientipikon salaysay nipaakar ed saray eksperimento to ed sayan crow's-nest, walay melag a kompas ya inyan to diman pian nasumpal iray lingo ya resulta na tatawagen ya “local attraction” na amin ya binnacle magnets; sakey a lingo ya nayarin ipasen ed pabitay na bakal ed saray tabla na barko, tan diad kaso na Glacier, nayarin lapud dakel so aderal a panday ed limog na saray tripulante to; Ibagak, ya anggaman say Kapitan et masilib tan sientipiko dia, balet, parad amin ya naaralan ton “binnacle deviations,” “azimuth compass observations,” tan “approximate errors,” amta ton maong, Kapitan Sleet, ya sikatoy ag-ontan lan inmabobon ed saraman ya aralem a magnetic meditations, kanian agto naakit no maminsan ed saray daiset a maong a napano, kanian akaamot ed sakey a diking na ubong na uwak to, ya mainomay ya naabot na lima to. Balet, diad inkalapagan, pandinayewan ko tan inaro ko ni ingen so makpel, matua, tan de-aral a Kapitan; balet ta ag ko labay ya ibaliwala to itan ya case-bottle, lapud anengneng kon matoor itan ya kaaro tan managligliwa, legan ya saray gamet to et aka-wantes tan aka-hood so ulo to et aaralen toy matematika ed tagey diman ed satan ya ubong na manok ya walad loob na talora odino apatiran yurongan na poste.

Balet no sikatayo ran managsigay na balyena ed Abalaten et aliwan ontan ka-snugly ya akaalagey ed tagey a singa si Kapitan Sleet tan saray taga-Greenland to; balet satan ya disbentaha et nababalang na baleg a pandumaan na kareenan na saraman ya makapasagyat a dayat ya ditan kami ran managsigay ed Abalaten et maslak ya onleletaw. Sakey, manpapainawa ak ed rigging ya mainomay, manpapainawa ak ed tagey pian mitongtong ed si Queequeg, odino siopaman ya walad duty ya nayarin naromog ko diman; insan ontatdang ak na daiset, tan mangibantak ak na matamoy a sali ed tapew na layag, nengnengen ko nin unona iray danum a panagpastolan, tan diad kaunoran et onla ak ed sankatageyan a destinasyon ko.

Gawaen kon malinis so suso to dia, tan prangkan aksobien ko ya nansiansia ak balet so pasensya ed panagbantay. Diad problema na uniberso ya manpapalikdo ed siak, panon ak ya—ya sigpot ak ya atilak ed sarilik ed ontan ya makapasagyat ya katagey—panon ko labat ya mainomay ya bembenan iray obligasyon ko ya unoren iray ganggan na amin ya barkon balyena, “Pansiansiaen mon lukas so weather eye mo, tan mangansion ka ed kada oras.”

Tan abuloyan yo ak ed sayan pasen a mamakiwas a mamakiwas ed sikayo, sikayo ran akan-barko ed Nantucket! Manalwar ed pangilistam ed saray maalwar a panagsigay mo ed siopaman ya ugaw a laki ya malimpek so kilay to tan anggapoy mata to; niiter ed agmanepeg a panagdalepdep; tan siopay mangiyoopresi ya mangipawit na Phædon imbes ya si Bowditch ed uloan to. Manalwar kayo ed ontan a too, kuan ko; kaukolan ya nanengneng iray balyena yo antis iran napatey; tan sayan sunken-eyed ya kalangweran ya Platonist et mangiguyor ed sika na samploran panagbantay ed interon mundo, tan agton balot gawaen ya mas mayaman so anggan sakey a pint na semilya. Aliwa met ya agmet kaukolan irayan mosyon. Ta natan, say panagsigay na balyena et mangiiter na ayaman parad dakel a romantiko, maermen, tan anggapoy nonot a kalangweran a lalaki, ya adismaya ed saray makapataktakot a kapagaan na dalin, tan manaanap na liknaan ed alkitran tan taba. Si Childe Harold et mabetbet ya onyurong ed ulo na palo na sakey a dismayadon barkon balyena, tan diad makapaermen a balikas et manpapasnok:—

“Man-roll ka, sika ya aralem tan maimpis ya asul a dayat, man-roll ka!
Samplon libon manag-anop na taba so onkukurang ed sika ya andi-kakanaan.”

Mabetbet ya papairapen na saray kapitan na saratan a barko iramay anggapoy nonot dan kalangweran ya pilosopo, ya babalawen da ra ya ag da nalilikna so magenap ya “interes” ed biahe; ag-ipapabitar ya sikaray andiay ilalo ed amin a kagalangan ya ambisyon, a singa diad sekreton kamarerwa da et mas labay dan ag nanengneng iray balyena nen say arum. Balet amin et andi-kakanaan; saraman ya kalangweran ya Platonist et walaan na ideya ya aliwan perpekto so pakanengneng da; sikaray antikey so pakanengneng da; antoy usar to sirin pian na-stress so nerbiyos na pakanengneng? Intilak da ed abung da so opera-glasses da.

"Akin, sika ya unggoy," inkuan na sakey a manag-arpa ed sakey ed sarayan ugugaw, "taloy taon tayo lan manbiabiahe, balet ag ka ni akapangipastol na balyena. Saray balyena et singa ngipen na manok no wala ka dia." Nayarin ontan ira; odino nayarin wala ray dakel ed sikara diad arawin abot; balet ta niluto ed singa opium ya inkaandi-kakanaan na bakantin, agmakaliknan panagnonot so sayan andiay kanonotan a kalangweran lapud panlalaok na saray daluyon ed saray kanonotan, a diad kaunoran et naandi so inkatoo to; inala to so mistikon dayat ed salian to parad nanengneng ya imahen na satan ya aralem, asul, andi-dalem a kamarerwa, ya onkakayat ed katooan tan kalikasan; tan amin ya nikadkaduman, ag-anengneng, onlelereg, marakep a bengatla ya agto nanengneng; balang sakey ya ag tanton nadiskobre, onlelereg a palikpik na ag nanengneng a nengneng, et singa ed sikato so pakapatnagan na saraman ya ag natalosan a kanonotan ya say kamarerwa labat et manlalapud tuloytuloy ya ipapaarap to. Diad sayan makapasagyat a kipapasen, say espiritum et onkukurang ed nanlapuan to; magmaliw ya onkakayat ed panaon tan liwang; singa saray dapol na Pantheistic ya inwalsik nen Cranmer, ya nagmaliw lan kabiangan na kada gilig na dayat ed interon globo.

Anggapoy bilay ed sika, natan, likud ed satan a mankibalatar a bilay ya iter na maalwar a barko; ed sikato, ya inala ed dayat; diad gilig na dayat, manlapud saray agnatalosan a daluyon na Dios. Balet legan ya naugip ka, sayan kugip mo, igalaw moy sali o limam na sakey a pulgada; ag mo la naala; tan say pakabidbiran mo et ompawil ed takot. Diad tapew na Descartian vortices et man-hover ka. Tan nayarin, diad pegley-agew, diad sankaabigan a panaon, diad sakey a kapalduan--throttled ya eyag et onleksab ka ed satan a malinew ya dagem ya onloob ed dayat ed tiagew, ya agla ontagey ed andi-anggaan. Talineng yo ya maong, sikayo ran Panteista!





KAPITULO 36. Say Quarter-Deck.

( Linmoob si Ahab: Insan, amin. )

Ag-abayag kayari na agawa ed tubo, nen sakey a kabuasan kayari na almusal, si Ahab, a singa gagawaen to, et tinmatdang ed cabin-gangway ya amaarap ed kubyerta. Ditan et kaslakan ya panaakar na maslak a sea-captains ed satan ya oras, ta saray lalaki ed uma, kayari na parehon panangan, et manpapawil-pawil ed hardin.

Agnambayag et nadngel so malet, singa garing a galaw to, legan ton panaakar iray daan a bala to, diad saray tabla a kabkabat to, kanian amin da et aderal, a singa saray bato ed geolohiya, a walaan na nikadkaduman marka na panakar to. Kasin ninmengneng ka met ed saman ya abel tan abel a kiew; diman met, nanengneng mo ni ray mas nikadkaduman bakat na sali—saray bakat na sakey ya ag-ontutundan kanonotan to.

Balet diad samay inkagawa ya pantotongtongan, mas aralem so itsura na saratan ya abel, anggano say nerbiyos ya inkiwas to nen saman ya kabuasan et angitilak na mas aralem a marka. Tan, napno na kanonotan si Ahab, kanian diad kada unipormin panagliko to, natan diad manunan palo tan natan diad binnacle, ngalngali mo nanengneng ya onliliber so kanonotan to legan ya onliliber, tan onkukurang ed sikato legan ya onkukurang; sikatoy sigpot ya walad sikato, diad tua, ya amin et singa walad loob a molde na kada paway a galaw.

“Markaan mo kasi, Flask?” inkuan nen Stubb; "say sisiw ya walad sikato et manpapasnok ed shell. 'Agmanbayag et ompaway la."

Linmabas so oras;—Si Ahab et akasara la natan ed loob na kabin to; anon, ya onkukurang ed deck, ya walaan na parehon graben panagpalikdo ed gagala ed itsura to.

Asingger lan manampot so agew. Biglan sikatoy tinmunda ed abay na saray kuta, tan inloob toy pukel ton sali ed auger-hole diman, tan diad sakey a lima to ya akabemben na sakey a shroud, ingganggan toy Starbuck ya ibaki so amin ed beneg.

"Sir!" inkuan na kapareha, ya abigla ed sakey ya ganggan ya matalag odino agbalot niiter ed barko likud ed pigaran ekstraordinaryon kaso.

“Ibaki yo so amin ed beneg,” so inulit nen Ahab. "Mast-heads, diman! onleksab kayo!"

Nen nantitipon so interon grupo na barko, tan walaan na mausisa tan aliwan sigpot ya ag-onsaew a lupa, a manbabantay ed sikato, ta sikatoy aliwan singa say itsura na panaon sano onsasabi so bagyo, si Ahab, kayari ton maples ya inmarap ed saray kuta, insan to inmarap ed limog na saray tripulante, so ginmapo manlapud punto de bista to; tan singa anggapoy kamarerwa ya asingger ed sikato et intuloy to lamet so ambelat iran turno to ed kubyerta. Diad akayurong ya ulo tan kapalduan akayurong so sombrero to et intuloy toy nanakar, ya agto naimano so mankelaw ya panbubulong na saray lalaki; anggad maalwar ya inkuan nen Stubb ed si Flask, ya seguradon impatawag ira nen Ahab diman pian nanengneng so sakey a pedestrian feat. Balet ag-itan abayag. Diad mabiskeg ya impantunda to, sikatoy inmeyag:—

"Antoy gagawaen yo no makanengneng kayo na balyena, lalaki?"

“Mankanta kayo parad sikato!” so ag-inisip ya ebat na dakel a boses ya nan-club.

"Maong!" inmeyag si Ahab, ya walay makapataktakot ya panangabobon to; nanenengneng ed makapaliket ya animasyon ya say ag-iilaloan ya tepet to so amabigla ed sikara.

"Tan antoy ontumbok ya gawaen yo, lalaki?"

“Onleksab ka, tan tumboken mo!”

"Tan antoy togtog ya togtogen yo, lalaki?"

“Sakey ya inatey a balyena odino sakey a baloton stove!”

Mas lalon nikadkaduma tan masebeg so liket tan panangabobon na lupa na masiken ed kada eyag; legan ya saray marino et nan-usisa ed balang sakey, a singa ra mankelaw no panon ya sikaran mismo so nagmaliw ya eksayted ed saratan ya singa anggapoy gagala na tepet.

Balet, amin da et nanliket lamet, nen si Ahab, ya natan et kapaldua lan manpapalikdo ed pivot-hole to, ya say sakey a lima to et akaabot ed tagey na sakey a shroud, tan malet, ngalngalin manpapasnok ya akabemben ed satan, so angibaga ed sikara:—

"Amin yon mast-headers et akadngel lad siak ya mangiiter na ganggan nipaakar ed sakey ya balyena ya amputi. Nengneng yo! Anengneng yo kasi iyan onsa na balitok ya Español?"—ya intagey toy malapar ya sinsilyo ed agew—"sakey itan a labin-anem a dolyar, lalaki. Anengneng yo? Mr. Starbuck, iter yod siak so top-maul yo."
