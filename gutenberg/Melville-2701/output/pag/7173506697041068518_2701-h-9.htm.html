KAPITULO 43. Dengel mo!

"HIST! Nadngel mo itan ya ungol, Cabaco?"

Pegley-bantayan itan: marakep so liwawa na bulan; saray marino et akaalagey ed sakey a kurdon, manlapud sakey ed saray preskon danum ed pagew, ya anggad scuttle-butt ya asingger ed taffrail. Diad onian paraan, inpasa da ray balde pian panoen so scuttle-butt. Manalalagey ira, diad maslak a pasen, diad masanton pasen na quarter-deck, tan manalwar iran ag-ira mansalita odino ag-onkukurang iray sali ra. Manlapud sakey a lima ya anggad lima, saray balde so linma ed sankarakelan a kareenan, a nabuyak labat lapud sagpaminsan a panagpaltog na layag, tan say tuloytuloy a ungol na ag-ontutundan onaabante a kilya.

Diad pegley na sayan painawa, si Archy, sakey ed saray cordon, ya say poste to et asingger ed saray after-hatches, so angibaga ed kaabay to, sakey a Cholo, na saray salita ed tagey.

"Hist! nadngel mo itan ya ungol, Cabaco?"

"Alaen mo so balde, labay mo, Archy? antoy labay mon ibaga?"

“Wala lamet—diad silong na saray abot—agmo ta narerengel—sakey ya ugip—singa ugip so tanol to.”

"Ubo ka! Ipasa mo yan balde ya ipawil."

"Wala lamet—wala la!—singa duara odino taloran naugip so onliliber, natan!"

"Caramba! agawaan mo la, shipmate, agta ontan? Say taloran basa-basa ya biskwit ya kakanen mo ed pangdem so ontutunda ed loob mo—anggapo lay arum. Nengnengen mo so balde!"

"Ibagam so labay mo, kaiba ed barko; matalimbeng so layag ko."

"On, sika so laki, andi, ya akadngel ed ungol na saray dagum na masiken a Quakeress ya limamplon milya ed dayat manlapud Nantucket; sika so laki."

"Manngisi ka; nengnengen tayo no antoy nagawa. Dengel mo, Cabaco, walay sakey a too ed leksab na after-hold ya agni anengneng ed kubyerta; tan iisipen kon walay amta met na samay daan ya Mogul tayo. Nadngel kon imbaga nen Stubb ed si Flask, sakey a panagbantay ed kabuasan, ya walay ontan ya klase na dagem."

"Tish! so balde!"





KAPITULO 44. Say Tsart.

No tinumbok mo si Kapitan Ahab ya linmoob ed cabin to kayari na bagyo ya agawa nen labi ya tinmumbok ed saman ya maruksan panangipatunay ed gagala to kaiba iray tripulante to, anengneng mo komon ya linma ed sakey a locker diad transom, tan angipaway na baleg ya amaga ya lukot na saray yellowish sea charts, ya inyan to iratan ed arap to diad aka-screw-down ya lamisaan to. Insan to inyurong ed arap na satan, anengneng mo komon ya sikatoy maalwar ya manaaral ed nanduruman linya tan anino ya anengneng to; tan diad matantan balet ta ag-onkukurang a lapis et i-trace so kaaruman iran kurso ed saray espasyo ya datin blangko. Diad panapanaon, sikatoy manutukoy ed saray bunton na daan iran log-book ed abay to, a ditan et akasulat iray panaon tan pasen a diad nanduruman datin biahe na nanduruman barko et walaray narel odino anengneng a balyena.

Legan ya ontan so gagawaen to, say ambelat a lamparaan a gawad tinta ya akasabit ed kadena ed tagey na ulo to, et tuloytuloy ya onkukurang ed galaw na barko, tan lawas mangibubuntok na manbabaroy iran liwawa tan anino na saray linya ed akukurongkurong a kiew to, anggad ngalngali singa legan a sikaton mismo so mamarka na saray linya tan kurso ed saray akukurongkurong a tsart, wala met iray nanengneng a linya tan kurso na lapis diad tapew na aralem a marka na tsart na moling to.

Balet aliwan diad sayan labi et, diad inkabukbukor to ed kabin to, inisip nen Ahab iray tsart to. Ngalngalin kada labi et ipapaway ira; ngalngalin kada labi et wala ray marka na lapis ya nabura, tan arum et nasasalatan. Ta diad saray tsart na amin ya apatiran dayat ya akaarap ed sikato, si Ahab et manbabaroy ed sakey a maze na saray agos tan alimbusabos, diad panmoria to ed mas seguradon pakasumpal na satan a monomaniac a kanonotan na kamarerwa to.

Natan, parad siopaman ya ag sigpot a makaamta ed saray paraan na saray leviathan, nayarin singa anggapoy ilalo so pananap na sakey a manbukbukor a pinalsa ed saray dayat na sayan planeta. Balet aliwan ontan so inisip nen Ahab, ta amta to so amin ya delap tan agos; tan diad ontan et nakalkula so iyaagus na kakanen na sperm whale; tan, ontan met, nononoten iray regular, seguradon panaon na panag-anop ed sikato diad partikular iran latitud; makasabi ed makatunongan iran panag-isip, ya ngalngali la onasingger ed saray kaseguroan, nipaakar ed sankapanpanaon ya agew pian onla ed saya odino satan a dalin pian mananap na biktimaen to.

Talagan segurado so katuaan nipaakar ed panapanaon ya iyaakar na sperm whale ed sakey a danum, kanian manisia so dakel a manag-anop ya sarag to kasin naobserbaan tan naaralan a maong ed interon mundo; no saray log parad sakey a biahe na interon armada na balyena et maalwar ya intipon, sirin say iyalis na sperm whale et naromog ya mipara ed saray herring-shoals odino saray biahe na saray lamok. Diad sayan pakapatnagan, nanggunaetan so manggawa na malaknab iran tsart na panag-alis na sperm whale.*

Niarum ni, no manbiabiahe ira manlapud sakey a panag-alaan na pananganan ya anggad sananey, saray sperm whale, ya igigiya na pigaran ag-onkulang ya instinct—singa bilang, sekreton inkadunong manlapud Dios—et maslak et manlangoy ed saray ugat , a singa tawag ed sikara; mantutultuloy ed dalan da ed sakey a linya na dayat tekep na ag-onkupas ya inkaeksakto, ya anggapoy barko ya akasabi ed kurso to, diad antokaman a tsart, a walaan na sakey a kakaplo na ontan a makapakelkelaw a inkasusto. Anggaman, diad sarayan kaso, say direksion ya alaen na anggan dinan a balyena et diretso a singa say parallel na surveyor, tan anggaman say linya na iyaabante et malet ya limitado ed dilin agnapaliisan, diretso a panag-awit to, balet say arbitraryon ugat ya diad sarayan panaon et ibabaga ran sikatoy manlangoy, et kaslakan ya mangaawit na pigaran milya ed kalapar (more o mas daiset, unong ya ipapasen ya onlaknab o onlaknab so ugat); balet agbalot onlampas ed pakanengnengan manlapud saray ulo na palo na barkon balyena, no maalwar ya onlelereg ed sayan magic zone. Say sumaryo et, diad partikular iran panaon ed loob na satan a kalapar tan diad satan a dalan, saray onkukurang a balyena so nayarin walaan na baleg a kompiyansa ya anapen.

Kanian aliwan diad saray panaon labat, diad saray kabkabat a nikadkaduman panag-alaan, so nailaloan nen Ahab a makaabet ed biktima to; balet diad ibaliw to ed sankalaknaban iran danum ed baetan na saratan a dalin et nayarian to, diad panamegley na arte to, so pangiyan tan pangipanaon ed inkasikato diad dalan to, pian anggan diad ontan et agto sigpot ya andian na ilalo a makapinengnengan.

Walay sakey a sirkumstansya ya diad primeron pakanengneng et singa makasbel ed makapalikdo balet ta siansia nin metodikon plano to. Balet aliwan ontan ed tua, nayarin ontan. Anggaman saray gregarious sperm whale et walaan na regular iran panaon parad partikular iran pasen, balet diad inkalapagan et agmo nayarin konklusyonan a saray pulok ya nan-aswang ed ontan tan ontan a latitude odino longitude ed sayan taon, singa bilang, et magmaliw a pareho ed saramay aromog diman nen imbeneg a panaon; anggaman wala ray nikadkaduman tan agnasuppiat ya inkagawa ya say kasunian to et apaneknekan ya tua. Diad inkalapagan, say parehon balikas, diad loob labat na agtanton malaknab a limitasyon, et onaplika ed saray manbukbukor tan ermitanyo ed limog na saray matatken tan maedad lan sperm whale. Kanian anggano anengneng lay Moby Dick nen imbeneg a taon, alimbawa, diad tatawagen ya Seychelle ground diad Indian ocean, odino Volcano Bay diad Japanese Coast; balet ag-itan tinmumbok, ya no say Pequod et ombisita ed anggan dinan ed saratan a pasen ed antokaman ya ontumbok a mipetekan a panaon, sikatoy ag-onkulang a nabet to diman. Ontan met ed arum nin panag-alaan, ya no maminsan et imparungtal toy inkasikato. Balet amin na saraya et singa labat kaslakan a panag-ontundaan to tan saray panaayaman to ed dayat, a singa panangibaga, aliwan saray pasen a panaayaman to ed andukey a panaon. Tan no iner so nipaakar ed saray tsansa nen Ahab a nasumpal so gagala to anggad natan, say tinukoy et ginawa labat ed antokaman a dalan, antecedent, ekstran ilalo to, sakbay a nadampot so sakey a partikular a panaon odino pasen, sano amin a posibilidad so magmaliw a posibilidad, tan, unong ed inisip nen Ahab, balang posibilidad so ontumbok a bengatla ed sakey a seguradon bengatla. Say partikular ya oras tan pasen et intipon ed sakey a teknikal a balikas—say Season-on-the-Line. Ta diman tan insan, diad loob na pigaran nantutumbokan a taon, say Moby Dick so panapanaon ya adeskribe, a mansiansia ed saratan a danum ed loob na pigaran panaon, legan a say banwa, diad tinaon a panag-ikot to, so manpapainawa ed sakey a nipropesiya a panaon ed anggan dinan a tanda na Zodiac. Diman met so kawalaan na karaklan ed saray makapatey ya impitongtong ed amputin balyena; diman et insalaysay iray daluyon ed saray gawa to; wadman met so makapasinagem a pasen ya aromog na monomaniac ya masiken so makapataktakot a motibo na ibales to. Balet diad maalwar ya inkakomprehensibo tan ag-ontutundan inkaalwar ya inbantak nen Ahab so manpapasnok a kamarerwa to ed sayan ag-ontutundan panag-anop, agto inabuloyan so inkasikaton mangiyan ed amin ya ilalo to ed sakey a sankaimportantian a katuaan ya asalambit ed tagey, anggan panon lay inka-makapaliket to ed saratan ya ilalo; nisay diad ag-inka-ugip na sipan to et agto la nareen so ag-inkareen na puso to pian itunda to so amin ya onsasaew a panag-anap.

Natan, say Pequod et nanlapud Nantucket diad gapo na Season-on-the-Line. Anggapoy posiblin sagpot ya makapangiter na pakayari ed komandante to pian makagawa na baleg a dalan ya mamaarap ed abalaten, doble so Cape Horn, insan ombatik ed 60 degrees na latitude ya makasabi ed equatorial Pacific diad panaon pian makabiahe diman. Kanian, kaukolan ton alagaren so ontumbok ya panaon. Balet say masakbay ya oras na panaglayag na Pequod et nayarin suston pinili nen Ahab, diad panmoria ed sayan mismon itsura na bengatla. Lapud, sakey a baetan na talon lasus tan anemaplo tan limaran agew tan labi so walad arap to; sakey a panaon ya imbes ya agmakaanos ya mansungdo ed gilig na dayat, usaren to ed nanduruman panag-anop; no ag-iilaloan ya say White Whale, ya manbabakasyon ed saray dayat ya arawi ed saray panapanaon ya panag-akan to, et onnengneng ed malimpek a kiew to diad Persian Gulf, odino diad Bengal Bay, odino China Seas, odino diad antokaman nin danum ya uusaren na rasa to. Kanian saray Monsoons, Pampas, Nor'-Westers, Harmattans, Trades; antokaman a dagem nilikud ed Levanter tan Simoon, so nayarin mangipelag ed Moby Dick ed makapalikdo a zig-zag a mundo-sirkulo na Pequod’s circumnavigating wake.

Balet diad pangiter na amin na saya; balet, no ipasen ya maalwar tan ag-onkikiwas, singa aliwan sakey a balibalin ideya, saya; ya diad malaknab ya dayat ya anggapoy anggaan to, say sakey a balyena ya manbukbukor, anggano naabet, et nepeg ya isipen ya sarag ton nabidbir so manag-anop to, anggano sikatoy sakey ya amputin balbas ya Mufti diad saray napno na totoon karsada ed Constantinople? On. Ta say nikadkaduman singa niebe ya amputin kilay nen Moby Dick, tan say singa niebe ya amputin ulpo to, et agnayarin ag nalingwanan. Tan agko ta binilang so balyena, inkuan nen Ahab ed sarili to, ta kayari ton inusisa iray tsart to anggad abayag lan pegley-labi et sikatoy ompawil ed saray kugip to—inbilang to, tan makaliktar kasi? Saray malapar a palikpik to et aburido, tan akapeket a singa abalang a layag na karnero! Tan dia, say balibalin kanonotan to et manbabatik ed sakey a lumba ya anggapoy panlinawa; anggad sakey a kapagaan tan inkakapuy na panagdalepdep so sinmabi ed sikato; tan diad paway na kubyerta et labay ton nabawi so biskeg to. Ah, Dios! anto ray irap ya susungdoan na samay too ya nauupot ed sakey ya ag-agawaan ya pilalek ya ombales. Sikatoy naugip ya akapeket iray lima to; tan ombangon ya wala ray dilin kuko to ed saray palad to.

Mabetbet, no sikatoy napilitan ya ontaynan ed duyan to lapud makapakesaw tan agnaanosan iran mabilabilay a kugip to ed labi, ya, diad pangipawil tod saray dilin mabiskeg a kanonotan to ed loob na agew, et intuloy to iratan ed pegley na panlalaban na saray phrensies, tan ipapawil-pawil to iratan ed manliob ya utek to, anggad say mismon pantiktik na bilay-pot to et agnasungdoan a sanok; tan nen, singa no maminsan, sarayan espiritual a gulo ed sikato so amabiskeg ed laman to manlapud leksab to, tan sakey a abot so singa alukasan ed sikato, a nanlapud satan et pinmaway iray nanbiig a ngalab tan kirmat, tan saray nisamban demonyo so angisenyas ed sikaton onlukso ed limog da; nen sayan impierno ed inkasikato so onyurong ed silong to, sakey a makapasinagem ya eyag so narengel ed barko; tan diad saray mankirlap a mata et ompaway si Ahab manlapud kuarto to, a singa sikatoy ontatakas ed sakey a dukolan ya apoolan. Balet saraya, nayarin, imbes ya ag naekal iran sintomas na pigaran akaamot a kakapuyan, odino takot ed dilin determinasyon to, so sankapatnagan labat a tanda na inkagrabe na satan. Ta, diad ontan iran panaon, say buanges ya si Ahab, say mapangta, agmakapaliket ya mapekder ya manag-anop na amputin balyena; sayan Ahab ya linma ed duyan to, aliwan sikato so ahente ya sengegan na inkataktakot to lamet. Say unor et say andi-anggaan, mabilay a prinsipyo odino kamarerwa ed sikato; tan diad ugip, lapud diad sakey a panaon et nisian ed kanonotan, ya diad arum a panaon et manguusar ed satan bilang paway a luluganan odino ahente to, sikatoy biglan nananap na pakaliktaran manlapud makapasinagem a pankakasakey na makapataktakot a bengatla, a diad saman a panaon et aliwa lan kabiangan na satan. Balet lapud say kanonotan et ag-onkikiwas likud no ag-akapibiang ed kamarerwa, sirin et seguradon ontan, diad kaso nen Ahab, ya iter to so amin a kanonotan tan pilalek to ed saksakey a sankatageyan a gagala to; satan a gagala, lapud dilin ag-inkayadyari na linawa to, so amasoot ed inkasikato sumpad saray dirios tan demonyo ed sakey a klase na dilin-panagpaga, independentin inkatoo. Andi, nayarin makapaermen a manbilay tan manpoolan, bangta say kaslakan a biskeg a kawalaan to, so tinmakas a natatakot manlapud ag-imbitaan tan ag-inka-ama ya inkianak. Kanian, say napapairap ya espiritu ya onsisinag ed saray mata na laman, nen say singa si Ahab et binmatik a nanlapud kuarto to, et diad saman et sakey labat a bengatla ya anggapoy porma, sakey ya andiay porma ya naugip, sakey a sinag na mabilay a liwawa, pian naseguro, balet ta anggapoy bengatlan koloran, tan sirin et sakey a blangko ed inkasikato. Tulongan ka na Dios, masiken, saray kanonotan mo so nanggawa na sakey a pinalsa ed sika; tan samay say mabiskeg a panagnonot to et manggagawa ed sikaton sakey a Prometheus; sakey a buwitre so mangan ed satan a puso ed andi-anggaan; satan a buwitre so mismon pinalsa to.





KAPITULO 45. Say Affidavit.

No antoy nayarin walad salaysay ed sayan libro; tan, diad tua, bilang agdirektan pantotongtong ed sakey odino duaran makapainteres tan makapainteres iran detalye ed saray ugali na saray sperm whale, say akaunan kapitulo, diad inmunan parte to, et singa kaimportantian na sakey a naromog ed sayan tomo; balet say manunan pamaakaran to et kaukolan ya lalon napalaknab, pian natalosan a maong, tan nagkalalo la pian naekal so antokaman ya agpanisia ya nayarin pansengegan na aralem ya agpikakabat ed interon tema ed arum a kanonotan, nipaakar ed natural a katuaan na saray manunan punto na sayan pamaakaran.

Agko labay ya gawaen iyan parte na kimey ko ya metodiko; balet ta kontento lan makapawala na labay ya impresyon diad panamegley na nanduruman sitasyon na saray bengatla, ya praktikal o napanmatalkan ya kabat ko bilang sakey a manag-balyena; tan manlapud sarayan sitasyon, alaen ko itan—say konklusyon ya gagalaen et natural ya ontumbok ed inkasikato.

Unona: Walay kabat kon mismon taloran inkagawa ya say sakey a balyena, kayari ton akaawat na harpoon, et sigpot ya akatakas; tan, kayari na sakey a panaon (diad sakey a pankanawnawa na taloy taon), sikatoy tinampal lamet na parehon lima, tan pinatey; no say duaran bakal, ya parehon akamarkaan na parehon pribadon cypher, et naala ed laman. Diad inkagawa ya taloy taon so linmabas ed baetan na impangibantak na duaran harpoon; tan iisipen ko ya nayarin mas ni nen say satan; say laki ya angitarok ed sikara ya agawa, diad baetan, pian onla ed sakey a barkon panaglakoan diad sakey a biahe ed Aprika, so linma ed gilig na dayat diman, akibiang ed sakey a grupo na panagdiskobre, tan linmoob ed arawi ed loob na bansa, a diman so impanbiahe to ed loob na ngalngalin duaran taon, a mabetbet a napepeligro lapud saray uleg, masebeg, tigre, makasamal a miasmas, tan amin ya arum niran kapeligroan ed puso na panagliber-liber ag kabat iran rehyon. Kaleganan to, say balyena ya tinamaan to et seguradon wala met ed biahe to; seguradon amitlo lan niliber to so interon mundo, ya inyakar to ed amin a gilig na Aprika; balet anggapoy gagala. Sayan too tan sayan balyena so nankakasakey lamet, tan say sakey et tinalo toy sananey. Ibagak ya siak, a mismo, so walaan na taloran inkagawa a mipara ed saya; diad duara ed sikara et anengneng ko ray balyena ya atumba; tan, diad komaduan impangataki, anengneng to so duaran bakal ya walaan na saray marka ya apaldua, insan inala ed inatey a sira. Diad loob na taloy taon, agawa ya siak so walad baloto ed parehon pankanawnawa, unona tan unor, tan diad unor et malinew kon abidbir so sakey a nikadkaduman klase na baleg a mole ed silong na mata na balyena, ya anengneng ko diman taloy taon lay apalabas. Ibagak ya taloy taon, balet segurado ak ya mas ni nen say satan. Nia so taloran inkagawa, sirin, ya amtak a mismo so katuaan; balet dakel ni so nadngel kon inkagawa manlapud totoon say inkapeteg da ed pamaakaran et anggapoy maabig a basiyan pian na-impeach.

Kumadwa: Kabkabat ed Sperm Whale Fishery, anggan panon lay ag-amta na mundo ed gilig na dayat, ya wala ray pigaran agnalinglingwanan ya inkagawa ed awaran ya say sakey a partikular a balyena ed dayat et walad arawin panaon tan pasen ya kabkabat na totoo. No akin ya ontan so inkamarka na ontan ya balyena et aliwan lapud saray nikadkaduman laman to ya midumaan ed arum ya balyena; ta anggan panon lay nikadkaduma ed satan a pamaakaran na antokaman a chance whale, agnambayag et pinatunda da la ray nikadkaduman ugali to diad impamatey da ed sikato, tan impabukal da pian magmaliw a nikadkaduman mabmablin larak. Andi: say rason et saya: ta manlapud saray makapatey ya eksperiensya ed panagsigay et walay akasabit a makapataktakot a prestihiyo na inkapeligro nipaakar ed sakey a balyena a singa ed si Rinaldo Rinaldini, kanian maslak a managsigay so kontento lan mamidbir ed sikato diad pangidiwit da labat ed saray tarpaulin da sano sikatoy nadiskobre dan manpapainawa ed dayat, ya ag da labay so mananap na kaaruman a kabat. Singa saray mairap-bilay ya demonyo ed gilig na dayat ya kabat da so sakey a mapaspasnok a too, sikatoy gagawaen da ed karsada ya ag-onkulang so panangibano ra, ta ompan no lalon ituloy da so pikakabat da, nayarin makaawat ira na summary thump lapud panagpaatagey da.

Balet aliwan balang sakey ed sarayan bantog a balyena so walaan na baleg ya indibidual a inkabantog—Andi, nayarin tawagen itan a kabkabat ed interon dayat; aliwa labat ya sikatoy bantog ed bilay tan natan et imortal la ed saray istorya na forecastle kayari na impatey to, no ag ingen sikatoy inawat ed amin a kanepegan, pribilehyo, tan pandumaan na sakey a ngaran; walaan na ngaran a singa si Cambyses odino Cæsar. Agta ontan, O Timor Tom! sika ya bantog ya leviathan, ya walay peklat to ya singa iceberg, ya abayag lan mankukurongkurong ed Oriental straits ya ontan so ngaran to, ya say sangi to et mabetbet ya nanengneng manlapud palmy beach na Ombay? Agta ontan, O New Zealand Jack! sika so takot na amin ya cruiser ya binmaliw ed saray wake da diad kaliberliber na Tattoo Land? Agta ontan, O Morquan! Ari na Hapon, ya say atagey ya jet to et ibabaga ran no maminsan et singa amputin krus ya singa niebe ed tawen? Agta ontan, O Don Miguel! sika ya Chilian whale, ya akamarka na singa daan ya pawikan ya walay mistikon hieroglyphics ed beneg to! Diad simplin prosa, nia so apatiran balyena ya kabkabat na saray estudyante na Cetacean History a singa si Marius o Sylla na klasikon iskolar.

KAPITULO 46. Saray panag-isip.

Anggaman, lapud nauupot ed ambetel ya apoy na gagala to, si Ahab ed amin ya kanonotan tan kiwas to et akaisip ed sankagrabian ya pakaerel ed si Moby Dick; anggaman singa akaparaan ya isakripisyo so amin ya pankaabigan to ed satan a sakey a pilalek; anggaman ontan et nayarin sikatoy lapud inkapalsa tan andukey lan kagagawa to et masyadon aka-asawa ed saray paraan na sakey a mansasnit a manag-awit na balyena, kanian agto la intunda so pansumpalan to ed panaglayag. Odino no aliwan ontan, wala ray arum ya motibo ya mas maimpluensya ed sikato. Nayarin alablabas so panagpino to, anganko, anggano ikonsidera so inka-monomania to, pian ipanengneng ya say inka-mabales to ed White Whale et nayarin onnatnat ed amin a sperm whale, tan no mas dakel so napatey ton ulsa et mas dakel so posibilidad ya kada balyena ya naabet to et sikato so nabusol to. Balet no talagan nikadkaduma so ontan ya hypothesis, wala ni ray kaaruman ya konsiderasyon ya anggaman aliwan tanton estrikto ya mitunosan ed inka-wildly na manuuley ton pilalek, balet ta ag-ira makayarin mamakiwas ed sikato.

Pian nasumpal nen Ahab so gagala to et kaukolan toy mangusar na kagawaan; tan ed amin ya kagawaan ya uusaren ed anino na bulan, saray totoo so mas mainomay ya ontunda. Amta to, alimbawa, ya anggan panon lay inka-magnetic na inka-asenso to ed pigaran aspekto nen say Starbuck, balet ta satan a inka-atagey so agmakasakbong ed kompleton espiritual a too a singa say inkasuperyor na laman ya mangilalanor ed intelektual a inka-master; ta parad saray puron espiritual, say intelektual balet ta manalalagey ira ed sakey a klase na relasyon na laman. Say laman nen Starbuck tan say pinilit nen Starbuck et kayarian nen Ahab, basta si Ahab et mansiansian magnet ed utek nen Starbuck; amta to nin siansia ya parad amin na saya say manunan kapareha, diad kamarerwa to, et kabusol to so panaanap na kapitan to, tan no nayarian to, et maliket ton itunda so inkasikato manlapud satan, odino itunda to ni ingen itan. Nayarin onlabas so andukey a panaon antis ya nanengneng so White Whale. Legan na satan ya andukey a panaon, si Starbuck et nayarin ompawil ed panangirebelde sumpad liderato na kapitan to, likud no wala ray ordinaryon, maalwar, sirkumstansyal ya impluensya ya nagawa ed sikato. Aliwan satan labat, no ag ingen say ag-amtan inkabaliwala nen Ahab nipaakar ed si Moby Dick et mas napatnagan nen say diad superlatibon pakatebek tan inkadunong to ed impakanengneng to ya, diad kaplesan, say panag-anop et nepeg ya naekal ed satan a nikadkaduman imahinasyon ya ag-inka-madios ya natural ya angitonton ed satan; ya say sigpot a takot ed biahe et nepeg ya ag-amta ed ag-amta a pasen (ta say pakpel na pigpigara labat a totoo so paneknek sumpad andukey a panagdalepdep ya ag-nainawaan na kiwas); ya nen manbabantay ira ed andukey a labi, saray opisyal tan sundalo to et seguradon wala ray mas asingger ya naisip da nen say Moby Dick. Ta anggan panon lay inkaseseg tan inkaseseg na saray masebeg a tripulante ya angigalang ed anunsyo na panaanap to; balet amin a klase na marino et mas odino kulang ya kapritso tan agmakapanmatalkan—manbibilay ira ed nanduruman panaon ed paway, tan nalilikna ra so inka-fickle na satan—tan no nansiansia ira parad antokaman a bengatla ya arawi tan andi-kakanaan ed pananap, anggan panon lay pankaabigan na bilay tan pilalek ed sampot, manunan nakaukolan a saray temporaryon interes tan trabaho so nepeg ya onsalet ed sikara tan mansiansian bunigas parad unor a panaon sabuyan.

Anggapo met so ag-inisip nen Ahab ed sananey a bengatla. Diad panaon na mabiskeg ya emosyon, aglabay na too so amin ya ag-inkayadyari; balet saratan a panaon et onkukurang. Say permanentin konstitusyonal a kondisyon na ginawan too, inisip nen Ahab, et say inkaduka. Lapud say White Whale et sigpot ya mamapakiwas ed kapusoan na sayan masebeg ya grupok, tan say pangibaliwala ed inka-maruksa ra et mamawala ni ingen na sakey a mabunlok a knight-errantism ed sikara, balet, bangta lapud panangaro ra ed satan et uusilan da si Moby Dick, nepeg met a walaan ira na naakan parad mas kaslakan, inagew-agew iran panlabayan da. Ta anggan saray atagey so kipapasen da tan saray kabalyero ya Crusaders nensaman et ag ira kontento ya manbiahe na duaran libon milya na dalin pian milaban parad masanton lubok da, ya ag ira manggagawa na panagtakew, panag-ala na bulsa, tan pakagamor na arum niran maridios iran bengatla. No sikara so malet ya akapeket ed sakey ya unor tan romantikon gagala ra—saman ya unor tan romantikon gagala, dakel komon so tinmaynan lapud inkadismaya. Agko ekalen ed sarayan lalaki, inisip nen Ahab, so amin ya ilalo ra ed kuarta—on, kuarta. Nayarin ag da labay so kuarta natan; balet ta onlabas so pigaran bulan, tan anggapoy sipan ed sikara, insan sayan parehon ag-onkikiwas a kuarta so manrebelde ed sikara, sayan parehon kuarta so magano lan man-cashier ed si Ahab.

Anggapo met so labay ya arum nin motibo na panag-alwar ya mas misiglaotan ed si Ahab a mismo. Lapud sikatoy ag-inisip, posiblin, tan nayarin medyo masakbay ya angiparungtal ed manunan balet pribadon gagala na biahe na Pequod, si Ahab so sigpot lan makaamta natan a diad panggawa to ed satan, sikatoy agdirektan angilukas ed inkasikato ed agnaebatan ya akusasyon na panagsamsam; tan tekep na perpekton ag-inkadusa, moral tan legal, saray tripulante to no ontan so disposisyon da, tan diad satan a gagala et makayari ra, et nayarin ipulisay so amin ya kaaruman ya katutulok ed sikato, tan maruksan alaen da ni ingen ed sikato so ganggan. Manlapud anggan say ag-ipapabitar ya imputation na panagsamsam, tan saray posiblin pansumpalan na ontan ya ag-inmabobon ya impresyon ya ondarakel, seguradon si Ahab so talagan magunaet a manprotekta ed inkasikato. Say proteksion et nayarin walad mismon utek tan puso tan lima to, ya susuportaan na maalwar, maalwar ya imano ed kada minuton impluensya na atmospera ya posiblin naeksperiensya na saray tripulante to.

Lapud sarayan amin a rason, tan arum niran nayarin masyadon analitikal pian nibaga dia, malinew ya anengneng nen Ahab ya kaukolan to nin siansia so mantultuloy ed natural, nominal a gagala na biahe na Pequod; unoren so amin ya kaugalian ya uusaren; tan aliwan satan labat, no ag ingen piliten toy inkasikato ya ipanengneng so amin a kabkabat a maseseg ya interes to ed inkalapagan a pananap na propesyon to.

Anggano ontan so nagawa, say boses to et mabetbet lan narerengel ya mangibabaing ed taloran ulo na palo tan mamapaseseg ed sikara ya mansiansian maong ya manbantay, tan ag ira lingwanan ya mangireport na anggan sakey a porpoise. Sayan panagbantay et ag-abayag ya anggapoy gunggona.





KAPITULO 47. Say Managgawa na Banig.

Sakey itan a malimpek tan ambetel a ngarem; saray marino et matamoy ya manpapainawa ed saray kubyerta, odino manbabantay ed saray danum ya kolor tingga. Sikami nen Queequeg et medyo nantrabaho ed panag-abel na tatawagen ya sword-mat, parad kaaruman ya panag-ipit ed baloto mi. Ontanlan mareen tan ag-onkulang tan anggan panon et manpapasnok so amin ya eksena, tan ontan lan inka-enkantasyon na panag-isip so mankukurongkurong ed dagem, kanian balang mareen a marino et singa akadetermina ed dilin agnanengneng ton inkasikato.

Siak so attendant odino page na Queequeg, legan ya okupado ed banig. Nen tuloytuloy kon dadalanen tan ibabalikas so palaman odino tela na marline ed baetan na andukey iran sinulid warp, ya uusaren koy dilin limak parad shuttle, tan legan ya si Queequeg, ya akaalagey ed gilig, et lanang tan anon ya ipapaarap toy ambelat ya oak ya espada to ed baetan na saray sinulid, tan ag-inmabobon ya onnenengneng ed danum, ya ag-inmabobon tan ag-inisip ya inpawil ko ed abung so kada sinulid: Diad saman et akagawa ak na nikadkaduman kugip diman. manuley ed interon barko tan ed amin a dayat, ya aderal labat lapud ag-ontutundan tanol na espada, ya singa saya so Loom of Time, tan siak a mismo et sakey a shuttle ya mekanikal ya man-weave tan man-weave ya onarawi ed saray Kapalaran. Diman so kawalaan na saray akapeket a sinulid na warp ya sakop na sakey labat, lawas ompapawil, agmanguman a yegyeg, tan satan a yegyeg so magenap labat pian awaten so crosswise interblending na arum a sinulid ed dilin sinulid to. Sayan warp et singa nakaukolan; tan dia, inisip ko, diad dilin limak et ipaarap ko so dilin shuttle ko tan i-weave ko so dilin kapalaran ko ed sarayan agnauman a lubir. Kaleganan to, say ag-inmabobon tan andiay-interes ya espada nen Queequeg, ya no maminsan et ontupak ed tela ya ag-onkikiwas, odino ag-onkukurang, odino mabiskeg, odino makapuy, unong ed nagawa; tan diad sayan pandumaan ed sampot a sugat et mamawala na mipetekan a pandumaan ed sampot ya aspekto na asumpal a tela; say espada na sayan masebeg, inisip ko, ya diad kaunoran et manporma tan manporma na parehon warp tan woof; sayan mainomay, andiay-interes ya espada et nepeg ya tsansa—on, tsansa, bulos a linawa, tan pankaukolan—ya agmet mankakasakey—ya amin et mankikimey a mankakasakey. Say diretso ya kurso na pankaukolan, ya ag nepeg ya iyarawi ed unor a kurso to—say kada mansasalet a yegyeg to, diad tua, et onkukurang labat ed satan; say libren panlabayan et bulos nin siansia ya man-ply na shuttle to ed baetan na saray inter ya thread; tan say tsansa, anggaman napepegetan ed panag-awit to ed loob na suston linya na pankaukolan, tan ag-onkukurang ed saray galaw to ya ipapaarap na kawayangan a linawa, anggaman ontan so ingganggan na saratan a dua, say tsansa so manuley ed balang sakey, tan walaan na unor a pakasugat ed saray ebento.

Kanian man-weave kami tan man-weave kami nen ginmapo ak ed sakey a tanol ya nikadkaduman tuloy, abayag lan in-drawing, tan musikal ya makapataktakot tan agmakatunongan, kanian say bola na libren linawa et inmepas ed limak, tan inmalagey ak ya onnenengneng ed saray lurem ya nanlapuan na satan a boses a singa payak. Diad tagey na saray cross-tree et samay baliw ya Gay-Header, si Tashtego. Say laman to et magunaet ya onaarap ed unaan, say lima to et akaunat a singa baston, tan diad antikey iran biglan panaon et intuloy toy onakis. Pian naseguro ya say parehon tanol et nayarin narerengel ed amin a dayat, manlapud nilasus a managbantay na balyena ya akayurong ed tagey na dagem; balet manlapud pigpigara ed saratan a baga et nayarin nanlapuan na makapakelkelaw ya eyag so singa nen Tashtego ya Indian.
