KAPITULO 110. Queequeg ed Luong To.

Diad impananap, aromog ya saray bariles ya unor ya inloob ed panagkargaan et perpekton maong, tan say itatagey to et nepeg ya mas arawi. Diad antikey, diad limog na saray manag-awit na balyena, saray manag-awit na balyena et saray manag-awit, ontan so tatawagen da.

Pobren Queequeg! nen ngalngali la kapaldua so inkaandi na barko, nepeg mo komon ya inyurong so puerta, tan sikatoy nengnengen mo diman; diman, ya akakawes ed saray drawer ton gawad bagoy karnero, say walay tattoo ya atap ya ayep et onkukurongkurong ed pegley na satan ya ambetel tan lutak, a singa sakey a berdin spotted lizard ed dalem na bobon. Tan sakey a bobon, odino sakey a yelo-abung, so anggan panon et apaneknekan ed sikato, mairap-bilay a pagano; diman, nikadkaduman ibaga, lapud amin a petang na panpapasnok to, sikatoy awalaan na makapataktakot a betel ya nagmaliw a petang; tan diad kaunoran, kayari na pigaran agew ya impanirap to, sikatoy inyan tod duyan to, ya asingger ed mismon arap na puerta na patey. Balet legan ya onkakapuy so amin a bengatla ed sikato, tan onkakasil so aping to, saray mata to, anggaman ontan, et singa onkakasil tan onkakasil; nagmaliw iran nikadkaduman anlemek na liwawa; tan mauyamo balet aralem ya inmarap ed sika diman manlapud sakit to, sakey a makapakelkelaw a paneknek ed satan ya ag-ompatey a bunigas ed sikato ya ag nayarin ompatey, odino onkapuy. Tan singa saray sirkulo ed danum, ya legan iran onkakapuy, onlalaknab; kanian saray mata to et singa manpapaspas tan manpapaspas, a singa saray singsing na Andi-anggaan. Say takot ya ag nayarin ngaranan so onloob ed sika legan kan akayurong ed abay na sayan onkakapuy ya atap ya ayep, tan anengneng mo ray nikadkaduman bengatla ed lupa to, a singa anengneng na siopaman ya akabantay nen inatey si Zoroaster. Ta antokaman a peteg a makapakelkelaw tan makapataktakot ed too, et ag nin balot nisulat ed salita odino libro. Tan say iyasingger na Patey, ya parehon mamapaarap ed amin, et parehon mamapabigla ed amin diad unor a panangiparungtal, ya sakey labat ya autor a nanlapud inaatey so makapangibaga. Kanian—ibaga tayo lamet—anggapoy ompatey lan Caldee o Griego ya walaan na mas atagey tan mas masanto iran kanonotan nen saramay misteryoson anino ra ya anengneng mon onkukurong ed lupa na mairap-bilay a Queequeg, legan ya sikatoy mareen ya akayurong ed onkukurang a duyan to, tan say onkukurang a dayat so singa kalmadon mangiyuyugyog ed sikato anggad sampot a painawa to, tan say agnanengneng a delap na dayat so angitagey ed sikato tan mamaatagey tan mamaatagey ed sikato nigana ed tawen.

Anggapoy sakey a too ed saray tripulante balet sikatoy insuko to; tan, nipaakar ed si Queequeg a mismo, say inisip to ed kaso to et mapilit ya impanengneng na sakey a pabor ya kinerew to. Tinawag toy sakey ed sikato nen manbabantay ed kabuasan, nen onsisinag la, tan inala toy lima to, inkuan ton legan ya walad Nantucket et akanengneng na pigaran angkekelag a baloto ya gawad maimpis a kiew, a singa say mayaman a war-wood na binalegan ton isla; tan diad impangusisa to, naamtaan to ya amin ya inatey ed Nantucket et saray balyena et akapeket ed saratan met lanlamang ya ambilunget a baloto, tan say panlabayan ton ontan so pangipasen ed sikato et amaliket ed sikato; ta ag-itan midumaan ed kustombre na mismon rasa to, ya, kayari ton imbalsamo so inatey a mibabakal, sikatoy inunat to ed baloto to, tan diad ontan et tinaynan to pian ilugan ed saray mabitewen ya arkipelago; ta ag da labat panisiaan ya saray bitewen et isla, no ag ingen ya arawi ed amin ya nanengneng ya abot, saray mismon mauyamo, anggapoy kontinente iran dayat da et onaagus ed asul a katawenan; tan ontan so panporma na saray amputin breaker na milky way. Inyarum to ni, ya sikatoy manggiwgiw ed pannonot ton sikatoy niponpon ed duyan to, unong ed kaslakan a kustombre ed dayat, ya inbantak a singa sakey a makapadimla ed saray makapapatey a pating. Andi: labay toy sakey a baloto a singa saramay walad Nantucket, lalon makapaliket ed sikato, lapud sikatoy sakey a manag-balyena, a singa sakey a baloto na balyena irayan baloto na lungon ya anggapoy kilya to; anggaman ag segurado so panagmaneho, tan dakel so kawayangan ed saray panaon.

"Ah! pobrin too! ompatey la natan," inkuan na marino ed Long Island.

Linma ed vice-bench to, say karpintero parad kombeniensya tan inkalapagan a reperensya, natan et insukat to ed satan so eksakton karukey na lungon, insan to ginawan permanente so pangiyalis diad impangupot to na duaran notch ed saray sampot to. Kayari to, intipon to ray tabla tan saray kagawaan to, tan nankimey la.

Nen asumpal lay unor a pako, tan say taklay so akaplano tan aka-fit la, sikatoy magano ya inyamot to so lungon tan intuloy to itan, ya tinepetan to no kasin akaparaan la ra ed satan a direksion.

Say ulo to et inbalikas na bisagra na katat, tan wadman so Queequeg ed lungon to ya anggapoy nanenengneng to no ag say kalmadon lupa to. “Rarmai” (gawaen to; mainomay itan), so innguronguro to ed kaunoran, tan nanpirma ya isalet ed duyan to.

KAPITULO 111. Say Pasipiko.

Nen manpapasyar kami ed saray isla na Bashee, akasabi kami ed baleg ya South Sea; no aliwan lapud arum a bengatla, nayarian ko komon ya ibano so pinabpabli kon Pasipiko tekep na agnabilang a pisasalamat, ta natan et naebatan so andukey a kerew ko nen ugaw ak ni; satan a mareen a dayat so linmukso ed bukig manlapud siak na sanlibon liga na asul.

Wala, ag-amta na sakey no antoy masamit a misteryo nipaakar ed sayan dayat, a say makapataktakot a panag-awit to et singa mansasalita nipaakar ed sakey ya akaamot a kamarerwa ed leksab; singa saramay alamat ya inyalis na dalin na Efeso ed tapew na akaponpon ya Ebanghelista a si San Juan. Tan nasumpal itan, ya diad tapew na sarayan panagpastolan ed dayat, malaknab iran danum a katakelan tan Potters' Fields ed amin ya apatiran kontinente, saray daluyon so ontagey tan onleksab, tan onkupas tan onagus ya andi-tunda; ta dia, minilyon a nanduruman anino tan anino, nalner iran kugip, panag-ugip, panagnonot; amin ya tatawagen tayon bilay tan kamarerwa, et mankukugip, mankukugip, ag ni; manpapasnok a singa saray naugip ed dukolan da; saray pirmin onkukurang a daluyon balet ta ginawa itan lapud ag-ira makainawa.

Parad siopaman ya Magian rover ya mannononot, sayan mareen ya Pasipiko, ya anengneng to la, et nepeg ya magmaliw ya dayat na pangampon to. Ibabatik to so sankapegleyan a danum na mundo, say Indian ocean tan Atlantic et saray taklay to labat. Saray parehon daluyon so manguuras ed saray mole na saray balon nipaalagey a baley ed California, balet nen kapegleyan et intanem na sankabaloan a rasa na totoo, tan manguuras ed saray kinmupas balet ta siansia nin mararakep iran palda na saray dalin ed Asia, a mas matatken nen si Abraham; bangta amin ed baetan na saray onleletaw a milky-way na saray isla na korales, tan saray abeban, andi-anggaan, ag-amta iran Archipelago, tan agnalooban iran Hapon. Kanian sayan misteryoso, madibinon Pasipiko so manpapaliber ed interon mundo; gawaen ton sakey a baybay so amin a baybay; singa say manpapaknol a puso na dalin. Diad inkaatagey na saratan ya andi-anggaan ya onlelereg, kaukolan mon kayarianen so makapasagyat a dios, ya iyuksoy moy ulom ed si Pan.

Balet daiset labat so kanonotan nen Pan ya amakiwas ed utek nen Ahab, ya akaalagey a singa sakey ya estatua a balatyang ed akaugalian ton pasen ed abay na mizen rigging, ya diad sakey a eleng to et agto inisip ya inekal to so asukar a musk manlapud saray isla na Bashee (ya diad saray masamit a katakelan to et seguradon manaakar iray mauyamo a manaaroan), tan diad sananey et maalwar ed maasin a panlinawa na balon aromog a dayat; satan a dayat ya ditan et seguradon manlangoy so kabusol ya White Whale. Diad andukey a panaon ya inlunsad ed sarayan ngalngali la unor lan danum, tan inmalis ya amaarap ed Japanese cruising-ground, say gagala na masiken so lalon inmawet. Saray malet a bibil to so nankakasakey a singa saray bibil na sakey a bisyo; say Delta na saray ugat na moling to et linmeksab a singa saray ilog ya napno; diad mismon ugip to, say maksil ya eyag to et linmabas ed abel na kasko, "Ipasen yo! say White Whale et manbubulos na makapal a dala!"





KAPITULO 112. Say Panday.

Diad impangusar to ed malimpek, ambetel a panaon ed tiagew ya walad sarayan latitud, tan bilang preparasyon parad saray nikadkaduman aktibon kimey ya magano lan nailaloan, si Perth, say aburido tan manpapaspas ya masiken a panday, et agto la inekal lamet so portable forge to ed hold, kayari ton asumpal so makatulong a kimey to parad Ahab, balet ta nansiansia nin maples itan akapeket ed saray ringbolt na foremast; ya natan et ngalngali ag-ontutundan tatawagen na saray pangulo, tan saray manag-arpa, tan saray managpana pian manggawa na daiset a kimey parad sikara; manguuman, o mangaapiger, odino manguuman ed nanduruman armas tan muebles na baloto. Mabetbet ya sikatoy napapaliberan na maseseg a grupo, ya amin da et manaalagar ya naserbian; sankaegnaan toy boat-spades, pike-heads, harpoon, tan lances, tan maimon ton babantayan so kada galaw to, legan ya mansasagpot.
