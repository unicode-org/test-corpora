Nen onaarawi ak, napno ak na kanonotan; say ag-iilaloan ya niparungtal ed siak nipaakar ed si Kapitan Ahab, so amano ed siak na sakey a makapasinagem ya ag-inkaseguro na ot-ot nipaakar ed sikato. Tan anggan panon, diad saman a panaon, aliknak so simpatya tan ermen ed sikato, balet ta agko amta no anto, likud no lapud maruksan inkaandi na sali to. Balet ta aliknak met so nikadkaduman takot ed sikato; balet satan a klase na takot, ya agko nayarian ya deskribien, et aliwan eksakton takot; Agko amta no anto itan. Balet aliknak itan; tan agto ak pinalikdo ed sikato; anggaman agko la naanos so singa misteryo ed sikato, ya ag-inkayadyari ta sikatoy kabat ko nensaman. Balet, saray kanonotan ko et diad kaunoran et inyakar ed arum a direksion, kanian diad kaplesan ya ambilunget et agla nanonotan si Ahab.





KAPITULO 17. Say Ramadan.

Lapud say Ramadan nen Queequeg, odino panag-ayunal tan panagpaabeba, et mantultuloy ed interon agew, agko pinili ya guloen anggad labi; ta papablien ko so sankabalgan a respeto ed relihyoson obligasyon na balang sakey, anggan panon lay inka-komiko, tan agko naromog ed pusok ya ibaliwala so anggan sakey a kongregasyon na saray ulsa ya mandadayew ed sakey a toad-stool; odino saramay arum nin pinalsa ed pigaran parte na dalin tayo, ya diad sakey a grado na inka-footmanism ya agnaparaan ed arum a planeta, et ontuek ed arap na laman na inatey ya akankayarian na dalin lapud saray agnaparaan a kayarian ya kayarian tan inabang ed ngaran to.

Ibagak, sikatayo ran maong ya Presbyterian Christians et nepeg ya magmaliw ya mapangasi ed sarayan bengatla, tan agtayo ipasen ya mas atagey itayo nen say arum ya mortal, pagano tan arum ni, lapud saray kapalduan balibalin panagpaatagey da ed sarayan bengatla. Walay Queequeg, natan, ya seguradon manliliket ed saray sanka-absurdon kanonotan nipaakar ed si Yojo tan say Ramadan to;—balet anto itan? Inisip nen Queequeg ya amta to no antoy labay ton ibaga, iisipen ko; singa sikatoy kontento; tan diman so pampainawaan to. Ag-ontulong so amin ya pansasangsangan mi ed sikato; paulyanan mo, kuan ko: tan kasian itayo na Langit ya amin—saray Presbyterian tan Pagano—ta amin tayo et walay makapataktakot ya apaldua ed ulo, tan makapaermen ta kaukolan tayon apigeren.

Nen mansirunget la, nen alikna kon segurado ak ya amin ya palabas tan ritual to et kaukolan ya asumpal la, linma ak ed kuarto to tan tinmoktok ak ed puerta; balet anggapoy ebat. Sinalik ya lukasan, balet ta akapeket ed loob. "Queequeg," inkuan ko ya maalwar diad panamegley na key-hole:—amin et mareen. "Ibagak, Queequeg! akin et ag ka mansalita? Siak iya—Ishmael." Balet amin et nansiansian singa nensaman. Ginmapo ak ya naalarma. Inabuloyan ko ed sikato so dakel a panaon; Inisip ko ya nayarin walay apoplectic fit to. Ninengneng ko so key-hole; balet say puerta ya onlulukas ed nikadkaduman suyok na kuarto, say key-hole prospect et sakey labat a pikewet tan makapadimla. Nanengneng ko labat so kabiangan na foot-board na dukolan tan sakey a linya na padir, balet anggapo lay arum. Abigla ak nen anengneng kon akapeket ed dingding so gawad tabla na harpoon nen Queequeg, ya inala ed sikato na akankayarian na abung nen imbeneg a labi, antis kamin linmugan ed kuarto. Makapakelaw itan, inisip ko; balet antokaman so nagawa, lapud say harpoon et akaalagey diman, tan sikatoy matalag odino agbalot onla ed arum a bansa no anggapo itan, kanian sikatoy nepeg ya walad loob dia, tan anggapoy posiblin lingo.

“Queequeg!—Queequeg!”—ya amin et ag-onkukurang. Seguradon walay agawa. Apoplexy! Sinalik ya lukasan so puerta; balet ta masebeg ya sinmumpa. Nen binmatik ak ed takayan, imbagak a tampol iray suspetsa ko ed samay inmunan akabat ko—say chamber-maid. "La! la!" inmeyag, "Inisip ko ya walay nagagawa. Linma ak pian manparaan na dukolan kayari na almusal, tan akakandado so puerta; tan anggapoy narengel ya utot; tan ag la nareen manlapu lad saman. Balet inisip ko, nayarin, linma kayo lan dua tan inkandado yo ray bagahe yo pian naseguro. La! la, ma'am!— Mr. Hur! apoplexy!”—tan diad sarayan eyag, sikatoy binmatik ya amaarap ed kusina, siak so tinmumbok.

Ag-abayag et pinmatnag si Mrs. Hussey, ya walay mustard-pot ed sakey a lima to tan sakey ya vinegar-cruet ed sananey a lima to, ya intunda to labat so trabaho ton mangasikaso ed saray castor, tan babalawen toy melag ya andeket ya anak to legan na satan.

“Wood-house!” inmeyag ak, "iner so laen to? Batik ka parad Dios, tan mangala ka na bengatla pian lukasan so puerta—say wasay!—say wasay! sikatoy na-stroke; mandependi ka ed satan!"—tan diad pangibaga ko ed satan et ag-inmabobon ak ya ontatdang lamet ed takayan ya anggapoy awit ko, nen inloob nen Mrs. Hussey so mustard-pot tan suka, tan say lupa na cabinet to.

“Antoy problemam, kalangweran a laki?”

"Ala moy wasay! Parad Dios, batik kad doktor, legan kon lukasan!"

"Nengneng mo dia," inkuan na akan-abung, ya inyan ton tampol so suka-cruet, pian nawalaan na bulos so sakey a lima to; "nengneng mo dia; say tutukoyen mo et say pangilukas ed anggan dinan ed saray puertak?"—tan diad saman et binembenan toy taklay ko. "Antoy problemam? Antoy problemam, shipmate?"

Diad kalmado, balet ta maples a paraan, inikdan ko na talosan so interon kaso. Agto naimano ya inpakpak to so suka-cruet ed sakey a diking na eleng to, tan nannonot na sakey a bekta; insan to inkelyaw—“Andi! Agko ni anengneng manlapu la nen inyan ko diman.” Binmatik ed melag ya aparador ed silong na takayan, tan linmingaw, tan pinmawil, imbaga tod siak ya anggapo so harpoon nen Queequeg. “Pinatey toy sarili to,” so inkelyaw to. “Makapaermen ta ginawa lamet nen Stiggs—walay sananey a counterpane—kasian na Dios so mairap-bilay ya ina to!—makaderal itan ed abung ko. Wala kasi so agi ton bii na mairap-bilay ya ugaw? Iner so kawalaan na satan a bii?—diman, Betty, onla kad si Snarles ya Pintor, tan ibagam ed sikato ya mangipinta ed siak na karatula,—ya walay inabuloyan ton manpapatey, tan mansigarilyo dia parlor;”—nayarin pateyen so duaran manok a maminpinsan. Pateyen? Kasian komon na Katawan so aswang to! Antoy ungol diman? Sika, kalangweran a laki, avast diman!”

Tan binmatik ed siak, inerel to ak legan kon sasalien lamet ya piliten ya lukasan so puerta.

"Agko itan aabuloyan; agko naderal so pasen ko. Onla kad locksmith, walay sakey a manga sakey milya manlapud dia. Balet avast!" inyan toy lima to ed bulsa to, "nia so tombok ya onkana, anganko; nengnengen tayo." Tan diad saman, inpawil to itan ed kandado; balet, abagey! Say supplemental bolt nen Queequeg et ag inekal ed loob.

"Kaukolan kon lukasan itan," inkuan ko, tan ombabatik ak na daiset ed lolooban, parad maabig a gapoan, sanen say akan-abung so akasabi ed siak, ya insipan to lamet ya agko deralen so abung to; balet inmekal ak ed sikato, tan diad biglan inmabeba na laman ko et inmabeba ak ed marka.

Diad makapakelkelaw ya ungol et alukasan so puerta, tan say knob ya inmawet ed dingding, et angibaki na plaster ed kisame; tan diman, maong a katawenan! diman akayurong si Queequeg, ya talagan kalmado tan mankokolekta ed sarili to; diad pegley na kuarto; akayurong ed saray hams to, tan akabemben ed si Yojo ed tapew na ulo to. Sikatoy ag-onnengneng ed sakey a direksion odino sananey a direksion, noagta akayurong a singa sakey ya inukit ya imahen ya anggapoy pakapatnagan na aktibon bilay.

"Queequeg," inkuan ko, ya inmasingger ed sikato, "Queequeg, antoy problemam?"

“Agto la akayurong ed interon agew, agta ontan?” inkuan na akan-abung.

Balet amin ya imbaga mi, anggapoy anggan sakey a salita ya naala mi ed sikato; Ngalngali ko la labay ya itulak, pian nauman so posisyon to, ta ngalngali ag ko la naanosan, singa ansakisakit tan aliwan natural so inkaapag-apag to; lalo la, ta diad amin a posibilidad et sikatoy akayurong la ed loob na masulok a walora odino samploran oras, ya agmet la mangan na regular a panangan to.

"Mrs. Hussey," inkuan ko, "sikatoy mabilay ed amin a bengatla; kanian taynanan mo kami, no labay mo, tan siak so mangasikaso ed sayan nikadkaduman pamaakaran."

Insan ko insara so puerta ed akankayarian na abung, tan nanggunaetan kon talonaan si Queequeg ya onyurong ed yurongan; balet andi-kakanaan. Diman so inyurong to; tan say nagawaan to labat—diad amin ya magalang ya arte tan panagpalikdo ko—agto labay ya igalaw so anggan sakey a patok, odino mangibaga na anggan sakey a salita, odino anggan nengnengen to ak, odino imanoen so kiwawala ko anggan panon.

Inisip ko, no kasin saya so nayarin kabiangan na Ramadan to; manaayunal ira ed saray ham da ed ontan a paraan diad binalegan ton isla. Nepeg ya ontan; on, kabiangan itan na sisiaen to, iisipen ko; bueno, sirin, sikatoy manpainawa; sikatoy ombangon ya magano o mabayag, anggapoy panduaruwa. Ag-itan mansiansia ed andi-anggaan, salamat ed Dios, tan say Ramadan to et onsabi labat a maminsan ed sakey taon; tan agko panisiaan ya duga itan ed saman.

Linma ak pian mangan na pangdem. Kayari na andukey ya inyurong ya ondedengel ed saray andukey ya istorya na pigaran marino ya nanlapud sakey a plum-pudding voyage, unong ed tawag da ed satan (salanti, antikey a panaglayag na balyena diad sakey a schooner odino brig, ya akakulong ed bokig na linya, diad Atlantic Ocean labat); kayari na impandengel ko ed sarayan plum-puddingers anggad ngalngalin alas-onse, linma ak ed takayan pian onugip, ya segurado ak ya diad sayan panaon et seguradon asumpal la nen Queequeg so Ramadan to. Balet andi; wadman lad samay angitilak ko ed sikato; agto ni ingalngalin anggan sakey a pulgada. Ginmapo ak ya onsyodot ed sikato; singa anggapoy kakanaan tan agmakatunongan so akayurong diman ed interon agew tan kapalduan labi ya akayurong ed saray ham to diad ambetel a kuarto, ya akabemben na sakey a piraso na kiew ed ulo to.

"Lapud tawen, Queequeg, bangon ka tan yugyog moy sarilim; bangon ka tan mangan ka. Naerasan ka; manpatey ka, Queequeg." Balet anggapoy anggan sakey a salita ya inmebat.

Lapud naandiay ilalo ak ed sikato, kanian, nandesisyon ak ya onugip tan onugip; tan andi duaruwa, sakbay na andukey a panaon, sikato so ontumbok ed siak. Balet antis ak ya linmoob, inalak so ambelat ya dyaket kon katat na oso, tan inbantak ko ed sikato, ta insipan kon ambetel a labi; tan anggapoy arum ya isulong to no ag say ordinaryon malimpek a dyaket to. Diad loob na pigaran panaon, gawaen ko so amin a labay ko, agko nayarian so onugip ed sankaabebaan a panag-ugip. Inerep ko so kandila; tan say pannonot labat ed si Queequeg—ya ag-apatiran mitro so kaarawi to—ya akayurong diman ed satan ya agmakapaliket a posisyon, ya bukbukor to ed ambetel tan ambilunget; saya so amaermen ed siak. Isipen pa; naugip ed interon labi ed saksakey a kuarto a kaiba so sakey a pagano ya aliing ed sayan makapaermen tan agnaebatan ya Ramadan!

Balet anggan panon et tinmunda ak la, tan anggapo lay amtak anggad kabuasan; nen, nen ninmengneng ed abay na dukolan, wadman so akayurong ya si Queequeg, a singa sikatoy atumba ed datal. Balet nen inmunan linmoob so agew ed bintana, sikatoy inmalagey, a walaan na malet tan mansasakit iran kasukasuan, balet ta maliket so itsura to; linmabas ya amaarap ed siak no iner so kawalaan ko; inpeket to lamet so moling to ed moling ko; tan inkuan ton asumpal lay Ramadan to.

Natan, singa imbagak la, anggapoy kontraen ko ed relihyon na siopaman a too, antokaman itan, basta ag to pinatey odino insultoen na satan a too so arum a too, lapud agmet itan manisia ed satan a too. Balet no say relihyon na sakey a too et magmaliw ya talagan makapabigla; no saya so positibon panamairap ed sikato; tan, diad sampot, gagawaen ton agkomportablen panayaman iyan dalin tayo; insan ko isipen ya panaon la pian ipulisay itan ya indibidual tan mitongtong ed sikato.

Tan ontan met so ginawak natan ed Queequeg. "Queequeg," inkuan ko, "onugip ka la natan, tan mantila ka tan dengelen mo ak." Insan ko intuloy, ginmapo ed inyaligwas tan inyaligwas na saray primitibon relihyon, tan linmeksab ed nanduruman relihyon ed kaplesan a panaon, legan na satan a panaon et nansagpotan kon ipanengneng ed si Queequeg ya amin na sarayan Kuaresma, Ramadan, tan andukey a pan-ham-squatting ed ambetel tan andiay liket iran kuarto et talagan andi-kakanaan; makapuy ed bunigas; andi-kakanaan parad kamarerwa; onsusumpa, diad antikey, ed saray mapatnag a ganggan na Kalinisan tan sentido komon. Imbagak met ed sikato ya lapud sikatoy sakey a makabat tan makabat a too ed arum a bengatla, ansakisakit ak, ansakisakit ak a maong, ya anengneng kon sikatoy natan et makapaermen a makulangkulang nipaakar ed sayan makapabaing a Ramadan to. Niarum ni, inrason ko, say panag-ayunal et mamapaermen ed laman; kanian say espiritu et onloloob; tan amin a kanonotan ya nianak ed panag-ayunal et nepeg ya kapaldua labat so naerasan. Saya so rason no akin ya maslak ed saray dyspeptic ya relihyonista et papablien da so makapaermen iran kanonotan nipaakar ed bilay da. Diad sakey a salita, Queequeg, inkuan ko, a medyo ag-ontutukoy; say impierno et sakey ya ideya ya inmuna ya inyanak ed ag-atunaw ya apple-dumpling; tan manlapu lad saman et nantultuloy itan panamegley na saray atawir ya dyspepsias ya pinabaleg na Ramadan.

Insan ko tinepetan si Queequeg no kasin sikaton mismo et walay dyspepsia to; ya ibalikas to so ideya ed malinew a paraan, pian naawat to itan. Inkuan ton andi; diad sakey labat ya ag nalingwanan ya inkagawa. Kayari na baleg a ponsia ya inter nen ama ton ari, diad impanalo to ed baleg a bakal ya ditan et limamplo ed saray kalaban so inatey nen manga alas dos na ngarem, tan amin da et aluto tan kinan ed saman a mismon labi.

"Anggapo la, Queequeg," inkuan ko, ya manggiwgiw; "ya gawaen to;" ta amtak iray konklusyon ya agto la ra insinyas. Walay anengneng kon marino ya binmisita ed saman ya mismon isla, tan imbaga tod siak ya ugali da, no walay baleg a bakal diman, ya i-barbecue so amin ya inatey diad sular odino hardin na analo; insan, sakey-sakey, inyan iratan ed angkakabaleg iran kanal a gawad kiew, tan inadornoan da a singa pilau, a walaan na breadfruit tan niyog; tan wala ray parsley ed sangi ra, ya impawil da ray komendasyon na mananalo ed amin ya kakaaro to, a singa sarayan regalo et dakel a Christmas turkeys.

Ta, agko iisipen ya saray imbagak nipaakar ed relihyon et walaan na baleg ya epekto ed si Queequeg. Lapud, diad gapo, singa sikatoy ag makadngel ed satan ya importantin tema, likud no ikonsidera ed mismon punto de bista to; tan, diad komaduan pasen, agto ak natalosan ya onsulok ed kakatlo, inyarum to ray ideyak a singa labay ko; tan, diad kaunoran, seguradon inisip ton mas dakel so amta to nipaakar ed tuan relihyon nen say siak. Ninengneng to ak tekep na sakey a klase na makapaabeba a kapagaan tan panangabagey, a singa to inisip a makapaermen a maong a say ontan a makatunongan a kalangweran a laki so naandi ed ilalo na ebanghelikal a paganon inka-dios.

Diad kaunoran et inmalagey kami tan nankawes kami; tan si Queequeg, ya mangaala na makapadinayew ya almusal na amin a klase na chowders, pian say akankayarian na abung et agmakagamor na baleg a tubo lapud Ramadan to, linmugan kamin linmugan ed Pequod, a manpapasyar, tan manpapaspas kami na saray pukel na halibut.





KAPITULO 18. Say Marcos To.

Nen manaakar kami ed sampot na pantalan ya amaarap ed barko, si Queequeg ya sankaawit toy harpoon to, si Kapitan Peleg ed maruksan boses to et maksil ya angibano ed sikami manlapud wigwam to, ya inkuan ton agto inisip ya say kaaro ko et sakey a kanibal, tan inyabawag to met ya agto abuloyan iray kanibal ya onlugan ed satan a barko, likud no sikaray akagawa na papel da nensaman.

"Antoy labay mon ibaga ed satan, Kapitan Peleg?" inkuan ko, ya onlulukso ak la natan ed saray kuta, tan tinaynan koy kaibak ya akaalagey ed pantalan.

“Labay kon ibaga,” ebat to, “kaukolan ton ipanengneng iray papeles to.”

"On," inkuan nen Kapitan Bildad ed boses ton anggapoy kakanaan, ya inyamot toy ulo to manlapud benegan nen Peleg, diad paway na wigwam. "Kaukolan ton ipanengneng ya sikatoy akomberte. Anak na kabilungetan," inyarum to, ya inmarap ed si Queequeg, "kasi ka natan et mibibiang ed anggan dinan a simbaan na Kristiano?"

“Akin,” inkuan ko, “sikatoy membro na inmunan Congregational Church.” Nia so nibaga, a dakel a walaay tattoo iran masebeg a manlalayag ed saray barko na Nantucket so diad kaunoran et nagmaliw a simbaan.

“Say Unonan Simbaan na Kongregasyon,” so inkelyaw nen Bildad, “anto! ya mandadayew ed pantitiponan nen Diakono Deuteronomio Coleman?” tan diad impangibaga to, inekal to ray salming to, kinuskos to iratan panamegley na baleg ya duyaw ya bandana ton panyo, tan insulong to iratan a maalwar, pinmaway ed wigwam, tan inmarap a malet ed tapew na saray kuta, tan ninengneng ton maong so Queequeg.

"Kapigan lan sikatoy membro?" insan to inkuan, ya inmarap ed siak; "ag tanton mabayag, iisipen ko, kalangweran a laki."

"Andi," inkuan nen Peleg, "tan ag met sikatoy abautismoan a susto, ta no andi et naurasan to komon so pigara ed saray asul na demonyo ed lupa to."

“Ibagam natan,” inkelyaw nen Bildad, “say Filisteo et sakey a regular a membro na pantitipon nen Diakono Deuteronomio? Ag ko nin balot anengneng ya onla diman, tan ipapalabas ko itan ed kada agew na Katawan.”

“Anggapoy amtak nipaakar ed si Deacon Deuteronomy odino say miting to,” inkuan ko; "Say amtak labat, si Queequeg dia et nianak ya membro na First Congregational Church. Sikatoy sakey a deacon, si Queequeg et sakey."

"Kalangweran a laki," inkuan nen Bildad a mapekder, "manpapasnok ka ed siak—ipaliwawam so inkasika, sika kalangweran a Heteo. Anton simbaan so labay mon ibaga? ebatan mo ak."

Lapud aromog kon mabiskeg so inkasiak, inmebat ak. "Say labay kon ibaga, sir, say parehon kadaanan a Simbaan na Katoliko ya sika tan siak, tan si Kapitan Peleg diman, tan si Queequeg dia, tan amin tayo, tan say anak tan kamarerwa na balang sakey ya ina et kabiangan tayo; say baleg tan andi-anggaan ya Inmunan Kongregasyon na sayan interon mundon mandadayew; amin tayo et kabiangan na satan; pigara labat ed sikatayo so mamapabli ed pigaran nikadkaduman panag-awit ed baleg a panisia ya amin tayo et mankakasakey."

“Splice, say labay mon ibaga et man-splice na lima,” so inkelyaw nen Peleg, ya onaasingger. “Kalangweran a laki, mas maabig no manbarko ka parad sakey a misionaryo, imbes a sakey a lima na arap na palo; agko nin balot nadngel so mas maabig a sermon. Si Diakono Deuteronomio—akin et agto itan natalo nen Father Mapple, tan walay inisip to. Onlugan ka, onlugan ka; agmo la nononoten iray papeles. Ibagak, ibagam ditan ed Quog—anto so tawag ed sikato? diad panamegley na baleg ya angkla, agaylan maong so itsura to; tan duga so pangasikaso to ed satan, inkuan ko, Quohog, odino antokaman so ngaran mo, akaalagey ka la kasi na sira?

Anggapoy imbaga to, si Queequeg, diad makapataktakot a paraan to, et linmukso ed saray kuta, manlapud saman et linmukso ed saray proa na sakey ed saray baloton balyena ya akabitin ed gilig; tan insan to inyan so kawigin pueg to, tan inyan toy harpoon to, tan inmeyag ed singa saya:—

"Cap'ain, anengneng mo so melag a tedter na alkitran ed danum diman? Nanengneng mo? bueno, isipen mo so sakey a mata na balyena, bueno, den!" tan diad panamegley na maksil ya puntirya to, inpaltog to so bakal ed tapew na malapar a gilig nen masiken a Bildad, a malinis ed saray kubyerta na barko, tan pinaltog to so mankirlap a alkitran ya ag nanengneng.

"Natan," inkuan nen Queequeg, ya mareen ton iguguyor so linya, "spos-ee him whale-e eye; akin, inatey so tatay balyena."

"Maples, Bildad," inkuan nen Peleg, say kapareha to, ya, abigla ed asingger na ontitikyab a harpoon, et inmarawi ed dalan na cabin. "Maples ka, kuan ko, sika Bildad, tan alaen mo ray papeles na barko. Nepeg ya walay Hedgehog diman, say labay kon ibaga et Quohog, ed sakey ed saray baloto tayo. Nengneng mo, Quohog, iter mi ed sika so koma-siamaplon lay, tan satan so mas ni nen say kapiganman ya niiter ed sakey a harpooneer ya nanlapud Nantucket."

Kanian linmoob kami ed cabin, tan diad baleg a liket ko et si Queequeg et ag-abayag et akalista ed parehon grupo na barko ya kawalaan ko met.

Nen asumpal lay amin ya preliminaries tan akaparaan lay Peleg ya amin parad pirma, sikatoy inmarap ed siak tan inkuanto, "Siguro, si Quohog diman et ag to amtay mansulat, agta ontan? Ibagak, Quohog, blast ye! manpirma ka na ngaran mo odino manggawa kay marka?"

Inmarawi si Queequeg ed labas na ulo na manag-ugip, tan sinindian toy tomahawk pipe to. Inyurong ak ed salian. Pinansiansia min onlabas so tubo ed tapew na sleeper, manlapud sakey ya anggad sananey. Kaleganan to, nen tinepetan to ak ed ag-inkayadyari to, impatalos ed siak nen Queequeg ya, diad dalin to, lapud anggapoy klase na saray sopa tan sopa, say ari, saray pangulo, tan saray totoon maong ed inkalapagan, et walad kustombren mamataba ed arum ed saray abeban klase na totoo parad saray ottoman; tan pian nawalaan na komportablen abung ed satan a pamaakaran, kaukolan mo labat so mangaliw na walora odino samploran matamoy a totoo, tan iyan mo ra ed saray pier tan alcove. Likud ni, kombeniente itan ed sakey ya ekskursion; mas maong nen saray yurongan ed hardin ya nayarin gawaen ya baston; diad sakey a pankanawnawa, sakey a hepe so anawag ed lingkor to, tan labay ton manggawa na settee to ed silong na sakey a kiew, nayarin diad sakey a ambetel a pasen a mapitek.

Legan ton isasalaysay irayan bengatla, kada no naawat nen Queequeg so tomahawk ed siak, ipapaarap toy hatchet-side to ed ulo na samay naugip.

“Antoy usar to, Queequeg?”

"Perry mainomay, kill-e; oh! perry mainomay!"

Sikatoy mantutultuloy ed pigaran makapabiglan pakanodnonotan nipaakar ed tomahawk-pipe to, ya singa diad duaran usar to et parehon amabiskeg ed saray kalaban to tan amapainawa ed kamarerwa to, nen direktan kamin naakit ed samay naugip a rigger. Say mabiskeg ya singaw ya sigpot lan manpapano ed kinmiwas ya abut, so ginmapon angibaga ed sikato. Sikatoy nanlinawa ya singa ag-onsasabi; insan singa agonigon ed eleng; insan inmarap na maminsan odino mamidua; insan inmalagey tan kinuskos toy mata to.

“Holloa!” sikatoy nanlinawa ed kaunoran, "siopa kayo ran mansisigarilyo?"

“Saray lalakin akabarko,” ebat ko, “kapigan so panlayag to?”

"On, on, onloob kayo ed sikato, andi? Manlayag natan. Linmugan so Kapitan nen imbeneg a labi."

"Antoy Kapitan?—Ahab?"

"Siopa ni no ag sikato?"

Mantepet ak ni komon ed sikato nipaakar ed si Ahab, nen walay nadngel min ungol ed kubyerta.

"Holloa! Starbuck's astir," inkuan na rigger. "Sikatoy sakey a mabilabilay a pangulo, satan; maong a too, tan sakey a maridios; balet amin a mabilay natan, nepeg kon nengnengen." Tan nen imbaga to itan, sikatoy linma ed kubyerta, tan tinmumbok kami.

Malinew la natan so isabi na banwa. Agnambayag et linmugan iray tripulante a duara tan talora; saray rigger so nan-best ed inkasikara; saray kapareha et aktibon mibibiang; tan pigara ed saray totoo ed gilig na dayat so okupadon mangaawit na nanduruman unor iran bengatla ed barko. Kaleganan to, si Kapitan Ahab et ag nanengneng ya akaamot ed loob na cabin to.
