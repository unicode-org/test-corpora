KAPITULO 89. Maples ya Sira tan Lukas ya Sira.

Say pangibaga ed saray waif tan waif-poles ed unor a kapitulo balet sakey, so mankaukolan na pigaran salaysay nipaakar ed saray ganggan tan totontonen na panagsigay na balyena, a say waif so nayarin ipasen a sankagrabian a simbolo tan badge.

Mabetbet ya nagagawa ya no pigaran barko so manbiabiahe a mankakasakey, say sakey a balyena et nayarin natamaan na sakey a barko, insan makatakas, tan diad kaunoran et napatey tan narel na sananey a barko; tan dia et agdirektan tugyopen na dakel a melag iran ag-iilaloan, ya amin da et mibibiang ed sayan sakey a baleg a kabiangan. Singa bilang,—kayari na makapakesaw tan mapeligron panguusil tan pakarel ed sakey a balyena, say bangkay et nayarin ontaynan ed barko lapud maksil a bagyo; tan onarawi ed dagem, naala lamet na komaduan manag-balyena, ya, diad kalmado, maalwar ya iguyor to itan ed abay, ya anggapoy peligro na bilay odino linya. Kanian saray sankagrabian tan sankagrabian a sangsangan so mabetbet ya onlesa ed baetan na saray managsigay, no anggapoy nisulat odino ag-insulat, sankamundoan, ag-asuppiatan a ganggan ya onaplika ed amin a kaso.

Nayarin say pormal labat ya totontonen ed panag-ayam na balyena ya inabuloyan na ley, et samay walad Holland. Ingganggan iya na States-General nen AD 1695. Balet anggaman anggapo lay arum a bansa ya walaan na nisulat a ganggan ed panagsigay na balyena, saray managsigay ya Amerikano so mismon managgaway ley tan abogado ra ed sayan pamaakaran. Angiter ira na sistema ya parad antikey ya inkakomprehensibo et onlampas ed saray Pandects nen Justinian tan saray By-laws na Chinese Society parad pangisebel ed pibabali ed negosyo na arum a totoo. On; sarayan ganggan et nayarin niukit ed sakey a sinsilyo nen Reyna Anne, odino say sakbong na harpoon, tan isulong ed beklew, kanian melag iratan.

I. Say Fast-Fish et kabiangan na grupon maples ed sikato.

II. Say Loose-Fish et duga parad siopaman ya makarel ed satan.

Balet say makapaermen ed sayan masterly code et say makapadinayew ya inkaantikey to, ya mankaukolan na dakel a komentaryo pian nipaliwawa itan.

Unona: Anto so Fast-Fish? Say mabilay o inatey a sira et maples, no ikonekta itan ed sakey a barko odino baloto ya walad loob, diad antokaman a paraan ya kontrolado na samay akalugan odino saray akalugan,—sakey a palo, sakey a sagwan, sakey a siam-pulgadan kable, sakey a telegraph wire, odino sakey a higos na cobweb, pareho labat iratan. Ontan met, say sira et maples no walay waif to, odino antokaman ya simbolo na kayarian to; basta say partido ya mangibabaliwala ed satan et malinew ya mangipatnag na abilidad da ed anggan anton oras ya awaten itan, ontan met ed getma ran gawaen itan.

Saraya so komentaryo na siensia; balet saray komentaryo na saray balyena a mismo et no maminsan et walaan na mairap iran salita tan mas mabiskeg iran panagtoktok—say Coke-upon-Littleton na kamao. Tua, diad limog na saray mas maptek tan mapagalang a manag-awit na balyena et naynay ya iikdan na allowance parad nikadkaduman kaso, a ditan et makapasinagem ya ag-inkahustisya ed moral parad sakey a partido ya ibaga ton sikatoy walaan na balyena ya datin inusilan odino pinatey na sananey a partido. Balet arum et aliwan tanton maalwar.

Manga limamplon taon lay apalabas et walay makapainteres ya kaso na whale-trover ya indemanda ed England, ya ditan et inkuan na saray nanreklamo ya kayari na mairap ya panguusil ed sakey a balyena diad dayat ed Bukig; tan nen talagan sikara (saray nanreklamo) so tinmalona ed panag-harpoon ed sira; diad kaunoran, lapud kapeligroan na bilay da, et kinaukolan dan taynanan so aliwan saray linya ra labat, noagta say mismon baloto ra. Diad kaunoran et saray akusado (saray tripulante na sananey a barko) so angala na balyena, tinamaan da, pinatey da, inala ra, tan diad kaunoran et inala da itan ed arap na saray nanreklamo. Tan nen saratan ya akusado so ag-inmabobon, say kapitan da et insaksak to ray gamegamet to ed ngipen na saray nanreklamo, tan impaseguro tod sikara ya diad panamegley na doxology ed ginawa to, pansiansiaen to la natan so linya, harpoon, tan baloto ra, ya akapeket ed balyena nen panaon na impakasamsam. Kanian saray nanreklamo et nandemanda natan parad pakabawi na balor na balyena, linya, harpoon, tan baloto ra.

Si Mr. Erskine so abogado na saray akusado; Si Lord Ellenborough so huis. Diad loob na depensa, intuloy na matalino ya Erskine so pangiyilustra ed posisyon to, diad impanukoy to ed sakey ya agano nin krimen. con. kaso, ya sakey a laki, kayari na andi-kakanaan ya impansagpot ton kontrolen so inkaruksa na asawa to, et diad kaunoran et sikatoy tinaynan to ed saray dayat na bilay; balet diad inlabas na saray taon, diad impanbabawi to ed satan a kundang, sikatoy angigapo na sakey a kiwas pian nabawi so kayarian to. Si Erskine et walad biek a dapag; tan insan to sinuportaan itan diad impangikuan to, ya anggaman say laki so inmuna lan nan-arpoon ed samay bii, tan aminsan et sikatoy inayuno to, tan lapud baleg a stress na inkaruksa to, diad kaunoran et sikatoy tinaynan to; balet sikatoy tinaynan to, kanian sikatoy nagmaliw a sakey a sira; kanian nen sikatoy in-harpoon lamet na sakey a laki, say bii so nagmaliw a kayarian na samay ontumbok a laki, kaiba so antokaman a harpoon ya nayarin aromog ya akapeket ed sikato.

Natan diad kaplesan ya kaso, inkuan nen Erskine ya saray alimbawa na balyena tan say bii et mankakasakey.

Sarayan kerew, tan saray kontran kerew, ya nadngel a maong, say marundunong a huis ed niletneg iran termino so nandesisyon, salanti,—Ya nipaakar ed baloto, inter to itan ed saray nanreklamo, lapud tinaynan da labat itan pian nisalba so bilay da; balet nipaakar ed kontrobersyal ya balyena, harpoon, tan linya, kayarian iratan na saray akusado; say balyena, lapud sakey itan a Loose-Fish nen panaon na unor a panag-aresto; tan saray harpoon tan linya lapud nen say sira et linma ed sikara, sikato (say sira) so akagamor na kayarian ed saratan ya artikulo; kanian siopaman ya mangala na sira et walay kanepegan tod saratan. Natan saray akusado kayari na satan et inala ra so sira; kanian, saray abitlan artikulo et kayarian da.

Say ordinaryon too ya onnenengneng ed sayan desisyon na marundunong a Ukom, et nayarin onkontra ed satan. Balet no aradoen so manunan bato na pamaakaran, say duaran angkabaleg a prinsipyo ya inletneg ed saray ganggan ed panag-ayam na kambal ya balyena ya akauna lan inaon, tan inyaplika tan impaliwawa nen Lord Ellenborough ed abitlan kaso; sarayan duaran ganggan nipaakar ed Fast-Fish tan Loose-Fish, kuan ko, et, no dalepdepen, et naromog ya manunan kabiangan na amin a hurisprudensya na too; ta anggaman ed komplikadon panag-iskultura to, say Templo na Ganggan, a singa say Templo na saray Filisteo, et walaan labat na duaran props ya akaalageyan.

Agta walay balikas ya walad sangi na balang sakey, Say pakawalaan et kapaldua na ganggan: salanti, anggan panon ya akaawat na bengatla? Balet mabetbet a say pakawalaan et say interon ganggan. Anto ray ugat tan kamarerwa na saray Russian serfs tan Republican slaves no ag say Fast-Fish, ya say pakawalaan to et say interon ganggan? Anto parad samay masebeg ya akankayarian na dalin so unor a bengatla na balo no ag sakey a Fast-Fish? Antoy walad saman ya ag-anengneng ya marmol ya mansion na kontrabida ya walay door-plate to parad sakey a waif; anto itan no ag say Fast-Fish? Antoy makaderal ya diskwento ya naawat nen Mordecai, say broker, ed mairap-bilay ya Woebegone, say bangkrap, diad sakey ya utang pian ag-onpatey ed eras so pamilya nen Woebegone; antoy makaderal ya diskwento no ag say Fast-Fish? Antoy sueldo na Arsobispo na Savesoul ya £100,000 ya inala tod daiset a tinapay tan keso na nilasus nilibon trabahador ya aperdi so beklew da (segurado ran onla ed tawen ya anggapoy tulong na Savesoul) antoy globular ya £100,000 no ag sakey a Fast-Fish? Anto ray baley tan baryo ya natawir na Duke of Dunder no ag say Fast-Fish? Anto parad satan ya ag-ontutundan manag-harpooneer, si John Bull, so mairap-bilay ya Ireland, no ag say sakey a Fast-Fish? Parad saman ya apostolic lancer, si Brother Jonathan, et antoy Texas no ag say Fast-Fish? Tan nipaakar ed saraya ya amin, agta say Panagkayarian so interon ganggan?

Balet no say doktrina na Fast-Fish et nayarin onaplika ed karaklan, say doktrina na Loose-Fish et mas malaknab ni. Saya so onaplika ed interon mundo tan diad interon mundo.

Anto so Amerika nen 1492 no ag say sakey a Loose-Fish, ya ditan et si Columbus so analo ed estandarte na saray Kastila diad panamegley na impangibaliwala to ed satan parad katawan tan amo ton bii? Antoy Poland parad Czar? Antoy Gresya ed saray Turko? Antoy India ed England? Antoy magmaliw ya Mexico ed United States? Amin ya Luwag-Sira.

Anto ray Kanepegan na Too tan Saray Kawayangan na Mundo no ag saray Sira? Antoy kanonotan tan opinyon na amin a totoo no ag say Loose-Fish? Antoy prinsipyo na relihyoson sisiaen ed sikara no ag say Loose-Fish? Antoy parad saray mapasirayew ya manag-smuggle verbalist so kanonotan na saray manag-isip nilikud ed si Loose-Fish? Anto so baleg a globo a mismo no ag sakey a Loose-Fish? Tan anto ka, managbasa, no ag say sakey a Loose-Fish tan sakey a Fast-Fish met?





KAPITULO 90. Saray Ulo odino Ikol.

"Magenap la, si rex et naandi la, tan say uley to." Bracton, l. 3, c. 3.

Say Latin ya nanlapud saray libro na Laws of England, ya no ikompara ed konteksto, et mankabaliksan ya, diad amin ya balyena ya narel na siopaman ed baybay na satan a dalin, say Ari, bilang Honorary Grand Harpooneer, so nepeg ya walaan na ulo, tan say Reyna so marespeton iter ed ikol. Say inkaapag-apag ya, diad balyena, et singa kapaldua na mansanas; anggapoy nakekeraan ed baetan. Natan ta sayan ganggan, diad silong na inuman a porma, et anggad natan et manpapaandar ed England; tan lapud mangiyoopresi itan ed nanduruman aspekto na nikadkaduman anomalya nipaakar ed inkalapagan a ganggan na Fast and Loose-Fish, saya so nabasa dia ed sananey a kapitulo, unong ed parehon magalang a prinsipyo ya mamapakiwas ed saray riles na Ingles a mansakripisyo ed sananey a kotse, a nireserba parad panayaman na saray ari. Diad primeron pasen, bilang makapainteres a paneknek ed katuaan ya say asalambit ed tagey a ganggan so siansia nin epektibo, ituloy ko so pangiyarap ed sikayo na sakey a sirkumstansya ya agawa ed loob na apalabas a duaran taon.

Ompatnag a wala ray matuan marino na Dover, odino Sandwich, odino arum ed saray Cinque Ports, ya kayari na mairap ya panguusil et tinmalona iran amatey tan akasabi ed gilig na sakey a marakep a balyena ya inmuna dan indeskribe ya arawi ed gilig na dayat. Natan say Cinque Ports et walad silong na hurisdiksyon na sakey a klase na pulis odino beadle, ya tatawagen ya Lord Warden. Lapud say opisina et direktan nanlapud korona, manisia ak ya amin ya royal emoluments ya nagagawa ed saray teritorya na Cinque Port et magmaliw ya asainmin to. Diad arum a managsulat, sayan opisina et tatawagen ya sinecure. Balet aliwan ontan. Lapud say Lord Warden et okupadon mantratrabaho no maminsan ed panag-fob na saray pankaukolan to; ya sikato so manunan lapud satan met lanlamang ya panag-fobbing ed sikara.

Natan nen sarayan mairap-bilay a marino ya apoolan na agew, ya anggapoy sandalyas da, tan saray pantalon da et akalukot ed tagey na saray sali ra, et naksawan dan inyakar so mataba iran sira ra ed tagey tan amaga, ya insipan da ed inkasikara so £150 manlapud mablin larak tan pukel; tan diad pantasya et oniinum na matalag a tsa a kaiba iray kaasawaan da, tan maong ya alak a kaiba iray kakaaro ra, diad biskeg na nanduruman aksion da; ontatdang so sakey a de-aral tan sanka-Kristianoan tan mapangasin laki, ya walay kopya na Blackstone ed silong na taklay to; tan inyan tod ulo na balyena, inkuanto—“Aliwa kayo! sayan sira, katawan ko, et Fast-Fish. Inala ko bilang kayarian na Katawan a Managbantay.” Diad saya et saray mairap-bilay a marino ed marespeton inkabigla ra—a peteg ya Ingles—ya ag da amta no antoy ibaga ra, so onlelereg ed mabiskeg a panagkalkal na ulo ra ed amin a pasen; legan tonya et maer-ermen ya onnenengneng manlapud balyena ya anggad samay sankaili. Balet ag-itan amaabig ed pamaakaran, odino ag-itan amalemew ed malet a puso na de-aral a laki diad kopya na Blackstone. Diad kaunoran et sakey ed sikara, kayari na andukey a panag-usisa ed saray ideya to, so nagmaliw a makpel a mansalita,

"Ipangasim, amo, siopa so Lord Warden?"

"Say Duke."

"Balet anggapoy pibabali na duke ed impangala tod sayan sira?"

"Kayarian to itan."

"Agaylay kairapan mi, tan kapeligroan, tan pigaran gastos, tan kasin amin iratan et parad pankaabigan na Duke; anggapoy nagamoran mi parad saray ot-ot mi no ag saray paltos mi?"

"Kayarian to itan."

"Kasin say Duke et mairap-bilay kanian napilitan ed sayan desperadon paraan na pananap na pambilay?"

"Kayarian to itan."

"Inisip kon painawaen so masiken kon nanay ya ag-akadukolan diad panamegley na kabiangan ko ed sayan balyena."

"Kayarian to itan."

"Ag kasi kontento so Duke ed sakey o kapaldua?"

"Kayarian to itan."

Diad sakey a salita, say balyena et inala tan inlako, tan say Grace to ya Duke na Wellington so akaawat na kuarta. No isipen ya no nengnengen ed pigaran partikular a paraan, say kaso et nayarin diad melag a posibilidad et ipasen, diad silong na saray sirkumstansya, a medyo mairap, sakey a matuan klerigo na baley so marespeton angitarok na sakey a sulat ed Grasya to, ya akikasi ed sikato ya ikonsidera ton maong so kaso na saraman ya maermen a marino. Diad saya et inmebat so Katawan kon Duke (parehon nipalapag so sulat) ya ginawa to la itan, tan naawat to so kuarta, tan sikatoy obligado ed reverend gentleman no diad arapen et sikato (say reverend gentleman) so ag mibabali ed negosyo na arum a totoo. Saya kasi so siansia nin militanten masiken, ya akaalagey ed suyok na taloran panarian, ya amin a lima to et manpipilit na limos ed saray manpapalimos?

Nanengneng ya diad sayan kaso say ibabaga ran kanepegan na Duke ed balyena et inter na Soberano. Kaukolan tayon usisaen no antoy prinsipyo na Soberano ya inmunan akapangiter ed satan a kanepegan. Say mismon ganggan et niletneg la. Balet inter nen Plowdon so rason ed satan. Inkuan nen Plowdon, say balyena ya narel et kayarian na Ari tan Reyna, "lapud superyor so inkaekselente to." Tan diad panamegley na saray sankamaongan a komentarista et saya so nagmaliw a mabiskeg ya argumento ed ontan iran pamaakaran.

Balet akin et say Ari so walaan na ulo, tan say Reyna so walaan na ikol? Sakey a rason ed satan, sikayo ran abogado!

Diad treatise to nipaakar ed “Queen-Gold,” odino Queen-pinmoney, sakey a daan ya autor na King’s Bench, sakey a si William Prynne, so angikuan: “Say ikol yo et kayarian yo na Reyna, pian say kawes yo na Reyna et nasuplayan na pukel na balyena.” Natan et insulat iya nen panaon ya say black limber bone na Greenland o Right whale et kaslakan ya uusaren ed saray laman na bibii. Balet sayan pukel et walad ikol; wala itan ed ulo, ya makapaermen ya lingo parad sakey a marundunong ya abogado a singa si Prynne. Balet kasin say Reyna et sakey a sirena, ya naitdan na ikol? Nayarin walay kabaliksan ya akaamot dia.

Walay duaran royal fish ya in-istilo na saray managsulat na ganggan ed Ingles—say balyena tan say sturgeon; parehon kayarian na ari diad silong na pigaran limitasyon, tan diad ngaran et mansusuplay na koma-samplon sanga na ordinaryon kita na korona. Anggapoy amtak ya wala ni arum ya autor ya angibaga ed satan; balet diad panmoria ko et singa say sturgeon et nepeg ya naapag ed parehon paraan a singa say balyena, say Ari so makaawat na makapal tan malet ya ulo a nikadkaduma ed satan a sira, a, no ipasen ed simbolikon paraan, et nayarin makapaliket a nibase ed pigaran ipapasen a pankaabigan. Kanian singa walay rason ed amin a bengatla, anggan diad ganggan.

KAPITULO 92. Ambergris.

Natan sayan ambergris et sakey a makapainteres a substansia, tan importante bilang sakey ya artikulo na komersyo, kanian nen 1791 et sakey a Captain Coffin ya nianak ed Nantucket so ineksamin ed bar na English House of Commons nipaakar ed satan a tema. Ta diad saman a panaon, tan diad tua anggad medyo atrasado lan agew, say eksakton nanlapuan na ambergris et nansiansian, singa say amber a mismo, sakey a problema ed saray de-aral. Anggaman say salitan ambergris et French compound parad gray amber, balet ta mandumaan so duaran substansia. Ta say amber, anggaman no maminsan et naromog ed gilig na dayat, et nakotkot met ed pigaran arawin dalin, bangbalet say ambergris et agbalot naromog likud ed dayat. Nilikud ni, say amber et malet, malinew, malimpek, anggapoy angob ya substansia, ya uusaren parad sangi na saray tubo, parad saray kuwintas tan alahas; balet say ambergris et malambot, singa wax, tan ambalingit tan maanghang, kanian maslak ya uusaren ed panagpabalingit, pastiles, mablin kandila, hair-powder, tan pomatum. Uusaren iya na saray Turko ed panagluto, tan awiten da met ed Mecca, parad parehon gagala a singa say pangiyakar ed insenso ed St. Peter's diad Rome. Wala ray managlako na alak ya mangipelag na pigaran bukel ed claret, pian napalaok itan.

Siopa sirin so manisip ya saratan a maong a bibii tan lalaki so nepeg a manliket ed inkasikara na sakey ya esensya a naromog ed agmakanepegan iran bituka na mansasakit a balyena! Balet ontan so tua. Diad arum, say ambergris so sengegan, tan diad arum et say epekto, na dyspepsia na balyena. Mairap ya ibaga no panon ya tambalen so ontan ya dyspepsia, likud no mangiter na talora odino apatiran boat loads na Brandreth's pills, insan ontaynan ed peligro, a singa gagawaen na saray trabahador ya mansasabog na bato.

Alingwanan kon ibaga ya wala ray aromog ed sayan ambergris, pigaran malet, malimpek, tan pukel iran plato, ya nen inmuna et inisip nen Stubb ya nayarin butones na pantalon na saray marino; balet ta diad saginonor et pinmatnag ya anggapo lay arum noag saray piraso na angkekelag a pukel na pusit ya inbalsamo ed ontan a paraan.

Natan ta say ag-inkabulok na sayan sanka-angoban ya ambergris et naromog ed puso na ontan ya inkabulok; anggapo kasi iya? Isipen mo so imbaga nen San Pablo ed Corinto, nipaakar ed kabulok tan ag-kabulok; no panon a nitanem itayo ed kababaingan, balet pinaoli itayo ed gloria. Tan tandaan mo met so imbaga nen Paracelsus nipaakar ed no antoy manggagawa na sankaabigan ya musk. Agmo met lilingwanan so nikadkaduman katuaan ya diad amin a bengatla ya ag-onkakasil, say Cologne-water, diad saray manunan yugtan na panaggawa to, so sankagrabian.

Labay kon sampotan so kapitulo ed samay apela ed tagey, balet agko nayarian, lapud kapagaan kon ipulisay so akusasyon ya mabetbet ya gagawaen sumpad saray balyena, tan saya, diad kalkulasyon na pigaran wala lay panangidumaduma, et nayarin ipasen ya agdirektan napaneknekan na samay imbaga nipaakar ed duaran balyena na Pranses. Diad arum a pasen ed sayan tomo et apaneknekan ya ag tua so makapadimlan panagpauges, ya say bokasyon na panag-ayam na balyena et diad sakey a mairap tan agmauksoy a negosyo. Balet wala ni sakey a bengatla ya nepeg ya isuppiat. Ipapabitar da ya amin ya balyena et lanang ya man-angob. Natan panon ya nanlapuan iyan makapadimlan stigma?

Diad opinyon ko, et malinew ya nasubaybayan itan ed inmunan insabi na saray barkon panag-ala na balyena ed Greenland diad London, masulok lan duaran siglo so apalabas. Lapud saraman a manag-balyena et ag da nensaman, tan ag da met natan, sinubok so larak da ed dayat a singa gagawaen na saray barko ed Abalaten; balet putoten so preskon taba ed angkekelag a piraso, isuksok itan ed saray abot na angkakabaleg iran bariles, tan awiten itan ed abung ed ontan a paraan; say inkaantikey na panaon ed saraman ya Icy Seas, tan say bigla tan maksil iran bagyo ya napapaarap ed sikara, ya mangisesebel ed antokaman ya arum a dalan. Say pansumpalan et, diad panloob ed hold, tan pangidiskarga ed sakey ed sarayan sementeryo na balyena, diad pantalan na Greenland, sakey a larak so niiter a medyo mipara ed samay linmesa ed panagkotkot ed daan a ponponan ed syudad, parad pundasyon na Lying-in Hospital.

Iisipen ko met ya sayan mauges ya akusasyon sumpad saray manag-awit na balyena et nayarin ipasen met ed kiwawala na sakey a baryo na Dutch ya tatawagen ya Schmerenburgh odino Smeerenberg diad gilig na Greenland, nensaman, ya say unor a ngaran to et samay inusar na makabat a si Fogo Von Slack, diad baleg a kimey to nipaakar ed Smells, sakey a libron-panaralan nipaakar ed satan a tema. Unong ed ngaran to (smeer, fat; berg, to put up), sayan baryo so niletneg pian nawalaan na pasen parad blubber na Dutch whale fleet ya nasubok, ya ag-ira awiten ed Holland parad satan a gagala. Sakey itan a koleksion na saray hurnoan, panag-alaan na taba, tan panag-alaan na larak; tan sanen saray kimey so sigpot lan mankikimey et seguradon ag-ira angipaway na makapaliket a tuloy ya angob. Balet amin na saya et midumaan ed sakey a South Sea Sperm Whaler; ya diad loob na apatiran taon ya biahe, nayarin, kayari ton sigpot ya panoen na larak so kargaan to, et ag, nayarin, manguusar na limamplon agew ed negosyo na panagpabukal; tan diad kipapasen ya aka-cask, say larak et ngalngali anggapoy angob to. Say tua et, saray balyena ya mabilay man o inatey, no desente so pantrato ed sikara, et aliwan pinalsa ya anggapoy angob to; nisay nabidbir iray balyena, a singa saray totoo nen kapegleyan iran panaon ya apektado pian nanengneng so sakey a Judio ed grupo, diad panamegley na eleng. Nisay peteg a say balyena et posiblin magmaliw ya arum nen say ambalingit, anta, bilang inkalapagan a bengatla, sikatoy manggagayaga ed ontan ya atagey a bunigas; dakel so ehersisyos; lanang ya walad paway na abung; balet, tua itan, matalag ed paway. Ibagak, ya say galaw na saray ngipen na Sperm Whale ed tagey na danum et mangiiter na pabalingit, a singa no say sakey a biin walay angob na musk et manpapaspas ed kawes to diad sakey a makapal a parlor. Anto sirin so pangikomparaan ko ed Sperm Whale parad angob, no isipen so kabaleg to? Agta diad saman a bantog ya elepante, a walaan na saray alahas a ngipen, tan ambalingit na mira, ya inyakar ed paway na sakey a baley na India pian igalang si Alejandro a Baleg?

Inala to so kutsilyo ed baloto manlapud sakob to, insabit to so matadem a tarem to ed tapew na linya, tan inmarap ed si Stubb, tan inmeyag a mantetepet, “Putot?” Kaleganan na satan, say asul tan akapeket a lupa nen Pip et malinew ya akanengneng, Do, parad Dios! Amin et linmabas ed sakey a kilaw. Diad kulang a kapalduan minuto, agawa iyan amin.

"Sumpian mo, putoten!" inmeyag si Stubb; kanian abalang so balyena tan nisalba si Pip.

Nen inmabig la, say pobrin melag ya negro et inataki na saray eyag tan panagbalaw na saray tripulante. Diad mareen ya inyabuloy nen Stubb ya ontaynan irayan ag-regular ya panagsumpa, insan diad simplin, singa negosyo, balet ta siansia nin kapalduay makapaliket a paraan, insumpa toy Pip ya opisyal; tan agawa itan, aliwan opisyal ya angiter ed sikato na dakel a maabig a simbawa. Say kabaliksan to et, Ag ka lan balot onlukso manlapud baloto, Pip, likud ed—balet amin ya arum et andi-anggaan, a singa say sankaabigan ya simbawa ed kapiganman. Natan, diad inkalapagan, say Stick to the boat , so peteg ya motto yo ed panag-whale; balet no maminsan et wala ray nagagawa no say Leap from the boat et mas maong ni. Sakey ni, singa to la naimano ya no sikatoy mangiter na ag-inmabobon a konsensiyon a simbawa ed si Pip, sikatoy mangitilak na malaknab a pasen pian makaloob parad arapen; Biglan intunda nen Stubb so amin ya simbawa to, tan anampot ed sakey a mapilit a ganggan, "Mansiansia ka ed baloto, Pip, odino diad Katawan, ag ta ka alaen no onlukso ka; tandaan mo itan. Agtayo nayarian ya naandi iray balyena ed saray singa sika; say sakey a balyena et manlako na talomplon danay nen say labay mo, Pip, diad Alabama, tan ag ka la onlukso." Diad saya et nayarin agdirektan impanengneng nen Stubb, ya anggaman inararo na too so kapara ton too, balet say too et sakey ya ayep ya mankukuarta, ya say tendensia to et mabetbet ya mangaamper ed inkamapangasi to.

Balet amin tayo et walad lima na saray Dios; tan linmukso lamet si Pip. Mipadparan tuloy so sirkumstansya to ed samay inmunan palabas; balet diad sayan bekta et agto inekal so linya; kanian, nen ginmapon binmatik so balyena, si Pip so atilak ed dayat, a singa sakey ya apuradon baul na managbiahe. Abagey! Si Stubb et masyadon matoor ed salita to. Marakep, daakan, tan asul ya agew itan; say mankirlap a dayat so kalmado tan ambetel, tan patatdang ya onnatnat, ed amin a kaliberliber, anggad abot, a singa katat na manag-awit na balitok ya pinaltog anggad sankatageyan. Manpapaatagey tan onleleksab ed saman a dayat, say ulo nen Pip ya ebon et singa ulo na cloves. Anggapoy naala ya kutsilyo nen sikatoy maples ya atumba ed beneg. Say agnaparaan ya beneg nen Stubb so inmarap ed sikato; tan say balyena et walay payak to. Diad loob na taloy minuto, sakey a milya na dayat ya anggapoy gilig so walad baetan nen Pip tan Stubb. Diad paway na sentro na dayat, say pobrin Pip et inyarap toy malimpek, mankukurongkurong, tan andeket ya ulo to ed banwa, sakey lamet ya manbukbukor ya abalang, anggaman sankatageyan tan sankaliwawaan.

Natan, no kalmado so panaon, say panlangoy ed dayat et mainomay parad saray akapraktis lan manlangoy a singa say panlugan ed spring-carriage diad gilig na dayat. Balet say makapataktakot a panbukbukor et agnaanosan. Say mabiskeg a konsentrasyon na sarili ed pegley na ontan ya andiay-puson kabaleg, Dios ko! siopay makapangibaga ed satan? Mark, no panon no saray marino ed kalmadon pasen et manaames ed maawang a dayat—tandaan no panon so inkaapit dan mangaakap ed barko ra tan onkukurang labat ed gilig to.

Balet talaga kasin intunda nen Stubb so mairap-bilay ya melag ya negro ed kapalaran to? Andi; agto labay, anggan panon. Lapud walay duaran baloto ya akaarap ed sikato, tan inisip to, ya anggapoy panduaruwa, ya sikaray onsabin tampol ed si Pip, tan sikatoy alaen da; anggaman, diad tua, saratan a konsiderasyon ed saray managsagwan ya napepeligro lapud dilin inka-matakot da, so aglanlamang napatnagan na saray manag-anop ed amin a mipadparan inkagawa; tan saratan ya inkagawa et aliwan mabetbet ya nagagawa; ngalngalin lanang ya diad panagsigay, say sakey a matakot, ya ontan so tawag da, et walaan na parehon maruksan panagbusol a nikadkaduma ed saray armada tan armada na militar.

Balet agawa, ya saraman ya baloto, ya ag da anengneng si Pip, biglan akanengneng ed saray balyena ya asingger ed sikara diad sakey a diking, inmarap tan inusilan da; tan say baloto nen Stubb et arawi la natan, tan sikato tan amin ya tripulante to et labalabay dan alaen so sira to, kanian say singsing ya abot nen Pip et ginmapon linmaknab ed kaliberliber to. Diad ag-inilaloan et sikatoy inliktar na mismon barko; balet manlapud saman ya oras say melag ya negro et linma ed kubyerta ya singa makulangkulang; ontan, anggan panon, inkuan dan sikato. Say dayat so nan-ayew ed laman to, balet ta linmereg so andi-anggaan a kamarerwa to. Balet, ag sigpot ya nalner. Imbes ya inawit a mabilay ed makapakelkelaw iran kaaralem, a ditan et saray nikadkaduman kurti na ag-auman ya inmunan mundo so onlelereg ed arap na saray ag-onkikiwas a mata to; tan say miser-merman, Kakabatan, so angiparungtal ed saray tinipon ton bunton; tan diad limog na saray maliket, andiay-puso, pirmin-ugaw ya andi-anggaan, anengneng nen Pip so dakel, walad amin a pasen, saray insekto ya korales, ya manlapud katawenan na danum et ontatagey iray angkakabaleg ya bola. Anengneng to so sali na Dios ed tapew na panag-abel, tan imbaga to itan; kanian sikatoy tinawag na saray kakaiba to ed barko ya buanges. Kanian say inkabaliwala na too et pakalikna na tawen; tan manlapud amin a mortal a rason, say too et onsabi ed kaunoran ed satan a celestial a kanonotan, a, diad panagkatunongan, et andi-kakanaan tan makapataktakot; tan kayamanan man odino pakaskasi, et nalilikna to ya ag-inmabobon, anggapoy pibabali to bilang Dios to.

Parad arum, ag yo si Stubb ya sisiaen ya maong. Say bengatla et kaslakan ed satan a panagsigay; tan diad ontumbok a salaysay, nanengneng no antoy singa inkaandiay agawa ed siak.
