KAPITULO 55. Na Saray Makapadinayew a Litrato na Saray Balyena.

Ipinta ko ed sika so andukey a panaon a singa met ed sakey a lata ya anggapoy canvas, sakey a bengatla a singa say tuan itsura na balyena a singa sikato so talagan ompatnag ed mata na balyena sano diad dilin laman to so balyena so akasabit ed abay na balyena-barko pian sikatoy makatapak diman. Kanian, nayarin makanakana so pangipakabat ed saraman ya makapainteres ya imahinasyon ya litrato to ya anggad natan et matalek ya mangaabet ed pananisia na taga-dalin. Panaon la pian ipetek so mundo ed sayan pamaakaran, diad paneknek ya aliwan tua irayan litrato na balyena.

Nayarin say manunan lapuan na amin na saratan ya pictorial delusions et naromog ed saray sankadaanan ya eskultura na Hindoo, Egyptian, tan Grecian. Manlapu la nen saraman ya panaon na saray imbension balet ta anggapoy konsiensia ra nen diad saray marmol a panel na templo, saray pedestal na estatua, tan diad saray kalasag, medalyon, kopa, tan sinsilyo, say dolphin et akadrowing ed saray timbangan na kadena a singa nen Saladin, tan walay helmet ya ulo to a singa nen St. George; manlapu lad saman et wala lay ontan ya klase na lisensya, aliwan diad maslak a sikat iran litrato na balyena, noagta diad dakel a sientipikon presentasyon nipaakar ed sikato.

Natan, diad amin a posibilidad, say sankadaanan ya litrato ya walad balyena et naromog ed bantog ya cavern-pagoda na Elephanta, diad India. Ibabaga na saray Brahmin ya diad ngalngalin andi-anggaan iran eskultura na satan ya agnaparaan ya pagoda, amin ya trabaho tan gagawaen, amin ya naisip ya avokasyon na too, et akauna lan akauna antis ya talagan linmesa so anggan dinan ed saratan. Agpankelawan sirin, ya diad sakey a klase na noble a propesyon tayo ed panag-ayam na balyena et nepeg ya ag-inmabobon. Say Hindoo whale ya tutukoyen et walad sananey a departamento na padir, ya mangipapatnag na inkatoo nen Vishnu ya singa leviathan, ya kabkabat bilang Matse Avatar. Balet anggaman sayan eskultura et kapalduan too tan kapalduan balyena, pian niiter labat so ikol na unor, balet satan a melag a kabiangan to et aliwan duga. Mas mipara itan ed manpapaspas ya ikol na anaconda, nen say malapar a palad na saray makapadinayew a ngipen na tuan balyena.

Balet onla kayod saray daan ya Galeriya, tan nengnengen yo natan so litrato na sakey a maong a Kristianon pintor na sayan sira; ta sikatoy ontalona ya ag mas maong nen say antediluvian Hindoo. Saya so litrato nen Guido nen Perseus ya mangililiktar ed si Andromeda manlapud ulsa ed dayat odino balyena. Iner so angalaan nen Guido na modelo na ontan ya nikadkaduman pinalsa? Nisay si Hogarth, diad impangipinta to ed parehon eksena ed mismon “Perseus Descending” to, et agto la napatnagan so anggan sakey a mas maong. Say baleg a katat na Hogarthian ya ulsa et onkukurong ed tapew, ya ngalngali ag makaala na anggan sakey a pulgada na danum. Walay klase na howdah to ed beneg to, tan say malaknab ya sangi to ya ditan et onlelereg iray daluyon, et nayarin alaen parad Traitors' Gate ya manlapud Thames ya mamaarap ed danum ya anggad Tore. Insan, wadtan iray balyena na Prodromus nen daan ya Scotch Sibbald, tan say balyena nen Jonah, a singa nanengneng ed saray kopya na daan iran Biblia tan saray pinuter na daan iran primer. Antoy nibaga ed saraya? Nipaakar ed balyena na book-binder ya manpapalikdo a singa sakey a sanga na ubas ed kaliberliber na sakey ya onlelereg ya angkla—a singa akatatakan tan aka-gild ed beneg tan titulo na dakel a libron daan tan balo—sakey itan a makapadinayew balet ta puron makapadinayew a pinalsa, ya inalig, inala ko, manlapud saray mipadparan pigura ed saray antikey a vases. Anggaman tatawagen ed interon mundo ya dolphin, tatawagen ko nin siansia ya sayan sira ya manag-imprinta na libro et sakey a sagpot ed balyena; lapud ontan so ginagala nen inmunan inloob so kagawaan. Inloob iya na sakey a daan ya Italyanon manangipalapag nen manga koma-15 siglo, legan na Revival of Learning; tan nen saraman ya panaon, tan anggan diad medyo masakbay a panaon, saray dolphin et ipapasen na karaklan ya sakey a klase na Leviathan.

Diad saray vignette tan arum niran dekorasyon na pigaran kadaanan a libro et no maminsan et nanengneng mo iray makapainteres a diwit ed balyena, a ditan et amin a klase na spout, jets d’eau, hot springs tan ambetel, Saratoga tan Baden-Baden, so onlelereg manlapud ag-onkupas ya utek to. Diad titulo na pahina na orihinal ya edisyon na “Advancement of Learning” et nanengneng mo so pigaran makapainteres ya balyena.

Balet no itunda tayo la irayan aliwan propesyonal a sagpot, nengnengen tayo iramay litrato na leviathan ya ibabaga ran maalwar, scientific delineations, na saramay makaamta. Diad koleksion na saray biahe nen daan lan Harris et wala ray pigaran plato na balyena ya inala ed sakey a libro na saray Dutch ya biahe, AD 1671, ya apauloan na “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen diad barkon Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson na Friesland, master.” Diad sakey ed saratan a plato et saray balyena, a singa angkakabaleg iran balsa a gawad troso, so nirepresenta ya akarukol ed limog na saray isla na yelo, a saray amputin oso so ombabatik ed saray mabilay a beneg da. Diad sananey a plato, say makapadinayew ya lingo et say pangirepresenta ed balyena ya walaan na perpendicular flukes.

Insan lamet, walay makapadinayew ya quarto, ya insulat na sakey a Captain Colnett, sakey a Post Captain ed English navy, ya say titulo to et “Sakey a Pambiahe ed kaliberliber na Cape Horn ya anggad South Seas, parad gagala ya napalaknab so Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Diad sayan libro et walay outline ya ipapasen ya “Litrato na Physeter o Spermaceti whale, ya indrowing base ed sukat na sakey ya inatey ed baybay na Mexico, Agosto, 1793, tan insakbit ed kubyerta.” Agko panduaruwaan ya say kapitan so angala na sayan tua ya litrato parad pankaabigan na saray marines to. No bitlaen ko labat so sakey a bengatla nipaakar ed saya, ibagak ya walay mata to ya no iyaplika, unong ed kaiba ton sukat, ed sakey a matatken lan sperm whale, et magmaliw a mata na satan a balyena a singa bow-window ya manga limaran mitro so karukey to. Ah, say makpel a kapitan ko, akin et ag mo kami inter ed si Jonas ya onnengneng ed satan a mata!

Nisay saray sankamaalwar a panagtipon na Natural History parad pankaabigan na saray kalangweran tan saray malangwer, so bulos ed parehon makapadimlan lingo. Nengnengen iyan popular ya kimey ya “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature.” Diad pinatikey ya edisyon na London nen 1807, wala ray plato na sakey ya ibabaga ran “whale” tan sakey ya “narwhale.” Agko labay ya ompatnag ya aliwan elegante, balet sayan makapadimlan balyena et singa labat sakey ya pinuteran a baboy; tan, nipaakar ed narwhale, say sakey a pakanengneng ed satan et magenap la pian pankelawan so sakey, ya diad sayan koma-labinsiam a siglo et say ontan ya hippogriff et nayarin ipasen ya peteg ed siopaman ya intelihentin publiko na saray maneeskuela.

Insan, lamet, nen 1825, si Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, sakey a maong a naturalista, so angipalapag na sakey a libro nipaakar ed saray balyena ya sistematiko, a ditan et walaray pigaran litrato na nanduruman klase na Leviathan. Aliwa labat ya aliwan tua irayan amin, noagta say litrato na Mysticetus odino Greenland whale (salanti, say Right whale), anggan si Scoresby, sakey ya abayag lan eksperiensyadon too ya mangaawit ed satan a klase na ayep, et angibaga ya anggapoy kapara to ed kalikasan.

Balet say pangiyan na cap-sheaf ed amin na sayan ag-inkayadyari na negosyo et para labat ed sientipikon si Frederick Cuvier, ya agi na bantog ya Baron. Nen 1836, sikatoy angipalapag na Natural History of Whales, ya ditan et inter to so tatawagen ton litrato na Sperm Whale. Antis mon ipanengneng itan ya litrato ed siopaman ya taga-Nantucket, mas maabig no iparaan mo ni so summary retreat mo manlapud Nantucket. Diad sakey a salita, say Sperm Whale nen Frederick Cuvier et aliwan Sperm Whale, no ag ingen sakey a kalabasa. Siempre, agbalot sikatoy agunggonaan ed panaglayag na balyena (matalag a nagamoran iratan a totoo), balet no iner so angalaan to ed satan a litrato, siopay makapangibaga? Nayarin naawat to itan bilang say akauna ed sikato ed siensia ed parehon lawak, si Desmarest, so akaawat na sakey ed saray autentikon aborsion to; salanti, manlapud drowing na saray Intsik. Tan no antoy klase na mabilabilay ya lalakin walay lapis na saratan ya Intsik, dakel ya queer ya tasa tan platito so mangibabaga ed sikatayo.

Nipaakar ed saray balyena na saray managpinta na karatula ya anengneng ed saray karsada ya akabitin ed tapew na saray tindaan na saray managlako na larak, antoy nibaga nipaakar ed sikara? Kaslakan et si Richard III. saray balyena, ya walay dromedary humps da, tan masebeg iran maong; man-almusal ed talora o apatiran sailor tarts, salanti, saray balyena ya napno na saray marino: saray depekto ra et onlelereg ed dayat na dala tan asul a pintura.

Balet sarayan nanduruman lingo ed pangipatnag ed balyena et aliwan makapabigla. Nonoten! Maslak ed saray drowing na siensia et inala ed saray sira ya akapeket; tan saraya et singa susto na drowing na aderal a barko, ya aputer so beneg to, ya suston mangirerepresenta ed mismon noble ya ayep diad amin ya ag-onkupas ya inkamapaatagey na kasko tan spars to. Anggaman saray elepante et akaalagey parad interon karukey da, say mabilay a Leviathan et agnin balot akaalagey na sarili to parad litrato to. Say mabilay a balyena, diad sigpot a kagalangan tan kabaliksan to, et nanengneng labat ed dayat diad agnatalosan a danum; tan onleletaw so baleg a kabiangan to ya ag nanengneng, a singa sakey ya inlunsad a barkon panlalaban; tan manlapud satan ya elemento et sakey a bengatla ya andi-anggaan ya imposible parad mortal a too ya itagey to ed dagem, pian napansiansia so amin a mabiskeg a daluyon tan daluyon to. Tan, agtayo la ibaga so posiblin pandumaan na kurti na sakey a malangwer nin mansususo a balyena tan sakey a matatken lan Platonian Leviathan; balet, anggan diad kaso na sakey ed saraman ya malangwer nin mansususo ya balyena ya insakbit ed kubyerta na barko, ontan so makapakelaw, singa eel, limbered, tan nanduruma so itsura to, ya ag naala na demonyo so eksakton itsura to.

Balet nayarin isipen ya manlapud lakseb ya pukel na aka-stranded ya balyena, et nayarin naala iray suston pakapatnagan nipaakar ed tuan itsura to. Andi. Ta sakey ed saray mas makapainteres a bengatla nipaakar ed sayan Leviatan, a say pukel to so mangiiter na daiset ya ideya ed inkalapagan a kurti to. Anggaman say pukel nen Jeremy Bentham, ya akabitin bilang kandila ed librarya na sakey ed saray angipatey ed sikato, et suston mangipapasabi na ideya na sakey a matatken a mabiskeg tan utilitarian, a walaan na amin ya arum nin manunan personal a katangian nen Jeremy; balet anggapoy ontan ya klase na bengatla ya nayarin ipasen ed saray pukel na anggan dinan a leviathan. Diad tua, singa ibabaga na baleg ya Manag-anop, say pukel na balyena et walaan na parehon relasyon ed ayep ya sigpot ya aka-invest tan aka-padd a singa say insekto ed chrysalis ya akapaliber ed satan. Sayan nikadkaduma et napatnagan ed ulo, a singa ed pigaran parte na sayan libro ya ag-iilaloan ya nipanengneng. Saya met so makapainteres ya nanengneng ed gilig na palikpik, ya saray pukel to et ngalngalin eksakton onkiwas ed saray pukel na lima na too, ya aglabat say thumb. Sayan palikpik et walaan na apatiran regular a gamet na pukel, say index, pegley, singsing, tan melag a gamet. Balet amin na saraya et permanentin akaamot ed laman dan sakbong, a singa saray gamegamet na too ed sakey ya artipisyal a sakbong. "Anggan panon lay inka-maalwar na balyena ed sikatayo," inkuan na makapaliket a Stubb ed sakey ya agew, "agton balot nibaga ya sikatoy mangasikaso ed sikatayo ya anggapoy mittens to."

Lapud sarayan amin a rason, sirin, antokaman so panmoria yo ed satan, nepeg yon isipen a say baleg a Leviatan et samay sakey a pinalsa ed mundo ya nepeg ya mansiansian ag-apinturaan anggad sampot. Tua, say sakey a litrato et nayarin mas asingger ed marka nen say sananey, balet anggapo so makasabi ed satan tekep na antokaman a baleg a grado na inkaeksakto. Kanian anggapoy paraan ed dalin pian naamtaan no antoy eksakton itsura na balyena. Tan say paraan labat pian makagamor kay anggan sakey ya ideya nipaakar ed bilay to, et no man-whale ka lanlamang; balet diad panggawam ed satan, walay melag a peligro ya magmaliw kan andi-anggaan a kalan tan nalunod ed sikato. Kanian, ompatnag ed siak ya ag ka masyadon mapilit ed pan-usisa mo nipaakar ed sayan Leviatan.





KAPITULO 56. Na saray Agtanton Lingon Litrato na Saray Balyena, tan Saray Tuan Litrato na Saray Eksena na Panag-balyena.

Nipaakar ed saray makapataktakot a litrato na saray balyena, siak so mabiskeg a natukso dia ya onloob ed saramay mas makapataktakot nin istorya nipaakar ed sikara a naromog ed pigaran libro, parehon kadaanan tan moderno, nagkalalo la ed si Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, tan arum nira. Balet agko la itan imanoen.

Amtak labat so apatiran nipalapag ya outline na baleg ya Sperm Whale; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, tan Beale's. Diad akaunan kapitulo et abitla si Colnett tan Cuvier. Mas maong so kayarian nen Huggins nen say kayarian da; balet, diad baleg a posibilidad, say Beale's so sankaabigan. Maong so amin ya drowing nen Beale ed sayan balyena, likud ed pegley ya pigura ed litrato na taloran balyena ya nanduruma so itsura da, ya mansampot ed komaduan kapitulo to. Say frontispiece to, saray baloto ya onaataki ed saray Sperm Whales, anggaman seguradon kinalkula pian nasagyat so sibilyan a panduaruwa na pigaran lalaki ed parlor, et makapadinayew a susto tan singa bilay ed inkalapagan ya epekto to. Arum ed saray drowing na Sperm Whale ed J. Ross Browne et susto so itsura da; balet ta makapaermen so inkaukit da. Balet aliwan kasalanan to itan.

Diad Right Whale, saray sankaabigan ya litrato na outline et walad Scoresby; balet ta indrowing iratan ed melag a sukat pian makapangiter na makapaliket ya impresyon. Sakey labat so litrato to na saray eksena na panag-ayam na balyena, tan saya so makapaermen a kakulangan, lapud diad panamegley labat na saratan a litrato, no maong so inkagawa to, et makagamor kay antokaman a singa tua ya ideya na mabilay a balyena a singa anengneng na saray mabilay a manag-anop to.

Balet, no alaen so amin, say sankaabigan, anggaman diad pigaran detalye et aliwan sankasusto, iran presentasyon na saray balyena tan saray eksena na panag-balyena ya naromog ed anggan iner, et duaran angkakabaleg ya French engravings, ya maong so inkagawa ra, tan inala ed saray painting na sakey a Garnery. Diad inkauksoy da, irerepresenta da ray panangataki ed Sperm tan Right Whale. Diad inmunan inukit et sakey a noble ya Sperm Whale so nilitrato ed sigpot a pakayari, ya inmalagey la ed silong na baloto manlapud kaaraleman na dayat, tan sankaawit to ed tagey so makapataktakot a inkagba na saray tabla. Say arap na baloto et ag-abaliwala, tan iguguyor ya akabalanse labat ed dukolan na ulsa; tan akaalagey ka ed satan a proa, parad satan a sakey ya agnabilang a kirmat na panaon, anengneng mo so sakey a managsagwan, a kapaldua so asakbongan na makapasinagem a manpabukal a sangi na balyena, tan diad gawa na panaglukso, a singa manlapud sakey a abot. Say kiwas na amin a bengatla et makapakelkelaw a maong tan tua. Say kapalduan anggapoy karga ton line-tub et onleletaw ed amputin dayat; saray poste na kiew na saray atumba ya harpoon et manpapaspas ed satan; saray ulo na saray manlalangoy et nitaytayak ed kaliberliber na balyena diad nanduruman balikas na takot; legan ya diad arawi tan singa bagyo et onlelereg so barko ed eksena. Nayarin walay seryoson lingo ed saray detalye na laman na sayan balyena, balet paulyanan tayo labat itan; lapud, parad bilay ko, agko nayarian so mangidrowing na ontan karakep.

Diad komaduan inukit, say baloto et man-drawing ed abay na barnacled flank na baleg ya ombabatik ya Right Whale, ya manpapalikdo ed andeket a dika to ed dayat a singa sakey a lumot a rock-slide manlapud saray pukdol na Patagonian. Saray jet to et akaalagey, napno, tan maimpis a singa soot; kanian lapud karakel na asewek ed tsimenea, isipen mo ya walay makpel a pangdem ya manluluto ed baleg a bituka ed leksab. Saray sea fowl et manpapasnok ed saray angkekelag ya alimbusabos, shell-fish, tan arum niran sea candies tan maccaroni, ya no maminsan et awiten na Right Whale ed mansasakit ya beklew to. Tan legan ya say makapal a bibil a leviathan et ombabatik ed aralem a dayat, a mangitilak na dakel a magulon amputin curds ed beneg to, tan pansengegan na onyugyog ed melag a baloto ed saray daluyon a singa sakey a baloto ya akapeket ed asingger na saray piligoy na sakey a bapor ed dayat. Kanian, say walad unaan et amin a makapasinagem a gulo; balet diad benegan, diad makapadinayew ya artistikon pandumaan, et say singa salming a lebel na dayat ya inmepas, saray onlelereg ya anggapoy almirol ya layag na andiay pakayarin barko, tan say ag-onkikiwas a masa na inatey a balyena, sakey a tinalo a kuta, a say bandera na panagdakep so matamoy ya akabitin ed poste na balyena ya insulong ed abot na sangi to.

Agko amta no siopa si Garnery ya pintor, odino sikato nensaman. Balet say bilay ko parad satan et sikatoy praktikal a makaamta ed tema to, odino makapakelkelaw so imbangat na sakey ya eksperiensyadon manag-awit na balyena. Saray Pranses so saray lalaki parad panagpinta. Onla ka tan nengnengen mo ray amin ya painting ed Europa, tan iner so pakaromogan mo na ontan ya gallery na mabilay tan manpapasnok ya gulo ed canvas, a singa diad saman ya triumphal hall ed Versailles; no iner say manbabantay et milalaban ed dalan to, a tuloytuloy, diad nantutumbokan a angkabaleg iran bakal ed France; no iner et singa sakey a kirmat na Northern Lights so kada espada, tan saray nantutumbokan ya armadon arari tan Emperador so onkukurang, a singa saray akakorona iran centaur? Aliwan sigpot ya agmakanepegan na pasen ed satan a gallery, irayan sea battle-pieces na Garnery.

Say natural ya abilidad na saray Pranses ya mangala na karakpan na saray bengatla et singa nikadkaduman napatnagan ed saray painting tan inukit da nipaakar ed saray eksena na panag-balyena ra. Lapud anggapoy anggan kakaplo na eksperiensya na England ed panagsigay, tan anggapoy kakaplo na eksperiensya na saray Amerikano, anggaman ontan et niiter da ed parehon bansa so saksakey ya asumpal ya sketch ya makapangipasabi ed peteg ya espiritu na panag-ayam na balyena. Diad maslak a kipapasen, saray Ingles tan Amerikanon managdrowing na balyena et singa kontento lan mangipresenta na mekanikal ya outline na saray bengatla, a singa say bakantin profile na balyena; ya, no nipaakar ed inka-marakep na epekto, et mipara ed pangi-sketch na profile na sakey a pyramid. Anggan si Scoresby, say bantog a Right whaleman, kayari ton inter ed sikatayo so malet ya interon karukey na Greenland whale, tan talora odino apatiran malimpek a melag iran narwhale tan porpoises, et inikdan to itayo na nantutumbokan a klasikal ya inukit na saray kawit na baloto, kutsilyo ed panag-abel, tan grapnels; tan diad mikroskopikon inkakuli na sakey a Leuwenhoeck et manpapasakop ed inspeksion na manggiwgiw a mundo na siamaplo tan anem a fac-similes na pinalaknab iran kristal na niebe ed Arctic. Anggapoy labay kon ibaga ya panagmudmora ed samay ekselentin managbiahe (igalang ko bilang beterano), balet diad ontan lan kaimportantian a pamaakaran et seguradon sakey ya ag-inkayadyari so ag-inkayadyari parad kada kristal na sakey a sinambaan ya affidavit ya inala ed arap na sakey a Greenland Justice of the Peace.

Nilikud ed saraman ya mararakep ya inukit nen Garnery, wala ni duaran French ya inukit ya makanakanan imanoen, ya ginawa na sakey ya mansususkribi ed sarili ton “H. Durand.” Sakey ed saratan, anggaman ag-eksakton inadapta ed kaplesan a gagala tayo, so makanepegan nin siansia a bitlaen ed arum a salaysay. Saya so mareen ya eksena ed pegley-agew diad limog na saray isla na Pasipiko; sakey a French whaler ya akaangkla, gilig na dayat, diad kalmado, tan matamoy ya mangaala na danum ed barko; saray akalukas a layag na barko, tan saray andukey a bulong na palma ed beneg, a parehon akapeket ed anggapoy dagem. Say epekto to et marakep a tuloy, no ikonsidera unong ed pangipaparungtal to ed saray mabiskeg a managsigay ed silong na sakey ed saray pigaran aspekto na oriental repose da. Say sananey ya inukit et duman tuloy: say barko et onkukurang ed dayat, tan diad mismon puso na bilay na Leviathanic, ya walay Right Whale ed abay to; say bapor (diad gawa na panag-cut-in) et onleletaw ed ulsa a singa diad pantalan; tan sakey a baloto, ya manaapura ya onaarawi ed sayan eksena na aktibidad, et singa manguusil ed saray balyena ed arawi. Saray harpoon tan gayang et akapeket pian nausar; taloran managsagwan so mangiiyan labat na palo ed abot to; bangta manlapud biglan inmawet na dayat, say melag a bapor et akaalagey ya kapalduan akaalagey ed paway na danum, a singa kabayon manpapabaleg. Manlapud barko, say asewek na saray irap na manpabukal a balyena et ontatagey a singa asewek ed tapew na sakey a baryo na saray pandayan; tan diad dagem, sakey ya andeket a lurem, ya ontatagey tekep na maksil iran bagyo tan uran, so singa mamapeles ed aktibidad na saray eksayted a marino.





KAPITULO 57. Na saray Balyena ed Pinta; diad Ngipen; diad Kiew; diad Sheet-Iron; diad Bato; diad Palandey; diad Bitewen.

Diad Tower-hill, legan kan onlasur ed saray pantalan na London, nayarin anengneng mo so sakey a baldado ya manpapalimos (odino kedger , unong ed ibabaga na saray marino) ya sankabembenan toy pinintaan a tabla ed arap to, ya mangirerepresenta ed makapasinagem ya eksena ya sikatoy abalang so sali to. Walay taloran balyena tan taloran baloto; tan sakey ed saray baloto (ya ipapasen ya walaan na abalang a sali ed amin ya orihinal ya integridad to) et napapagaan na saray panga na sankaunaan a balyena. Anggan kapigan ed loob na samploran taon, inkuan da ed siak, et inyatagey na satan a laki itan a litrato, tan impanengneng to itan a tuod ed agmakapanisia a mundo. Balet sinmabi la natan so panaon na pakapatunong to. Arum ed sikara et walaan na melag iran kahon na singa dentistika iran kagawaan, ya espisyal ya parad negosyo na panag-skrimshandering. Say andukey ya inkadestiero manlapud Kakristianoan tan sibilisasyon so agnapaliisan a mangipawil ed sakey a too ed saman a kipapasen ya angiyan ed sikato na Dios, salanti, say tatawagen ya inka-savagery. Say kadaanan ya Hawaiian war-club odino spear-paddle, diad sigpot a karakel tan inka-elaborate na inukit to, et singa kabaleg a tropeo na panagsungdo na too a singa say Latin lexicon. Diad parehon makapakelkelaw ya anos, tan diad parehon ngipen na pating, na sakey a mairap-bilay ya jack-knife to, sikatoy mangukit ed sika na sakey a pukel ya iskultura, ya aliwan singa sakey a trabahador, balet singa asingger ed inka-maziness na desinyo to, a singa say Griegon masebeg, say kalasag nen Achilles; tan napno na barbaro ya espiritu tan inka-suheri, a singa saray imprinta na samay marakep ya Dutch ya masebeg, si Albert Durer.

Diad pigaran daan ya abung ed uma ya walay atep ton gable et nanengneng mo ray balyena ya gansa ya akabitin ed ikol da parad saray mantoktok ed puerta ed gilig na karsada. Nisay no onlalaknab so pangiyaatagey na tema yo, ag yo met nanengneng iray angkakabaleg a balyena ed mabitewen a katawenan, tan saray baloto ya manguusil ed sikara; singa nen abayag lan napno na kanonotan na bakal saray bansa ed Bukig et anengneng da ray armada ya akasara ed bakal diad limog na saray lurem. Kanian diad Bukig et inusilan ko si Leviathan ed kaliberliber tan kaliberliber na Pole tekep na saray rebolusyon na saray maliwliwawan punto ya inmuna ya angipatnag ed sikato ed siak. Tan diad silong na mankirlap a katawenan na Antartika et linmugan ak ed Argo-Navis, tan akibiang ak ed panguusil sumpad mabitewen a Cetus ya arawi ed sankaarawian na Hydrus tan say Flying Fish.

KAPITULO 58. Brit.

Diad impamaarap mi ed amianen-bukig manlapud Crozetts, akasabi kami ed malaknab iran katakelan na brit, say melag, duyaw a substansia, a maslak a kakanen na Right Whale. Diad komaduan agew, anengneng so dakel a Right Whales, ya ag ira naataki na sakey a Sperm Whaler a singa say Pequod, ya akalukas so panga ra et matantan ya onlangoy ed brit, ya, akapeket ed saray fringing fibers na satan a makapakelkelaw a Venetian blind ed sangi ra, et diad ontan a paraan et nisian ed danum ya pinmaway ed bibil.

Singa saray manag-ani ed kabuasan, ya mankakasakey ya kalkalna tan manpapasnok ya ipapaarap da ray panag-ani ra ed andukey ya ambetel a dika ed saray katakelan; anggan ontan sarayan ulsa so nanlangoy, a manggagawa na nikadkaduman, singa dika, tan makapataktakot a tanol; tan itilak da ray andi-anggaan ya asul a kolor ed tapew na duyaw a dayat.*

*Saman a parte na dayat ya kabkabat na saray manag-awit na balyena bilang “Brazil Banks” et ag-ontan so ngaran to a singa saray Banks of Newfoundland, lapud wala ray mababaw a danum tan saray soundings diman, noagta lapud sayan makapadinayew a singa katakelan ya itsura to, a sengegan na angkakabaleg iran danum na brit ya tuloytuloy ya onleletaw ed saratan a latitud, a ditan et say Right Whale so papairapen na saray balyena.

Balet say tanol labat ya ginawa ra legan dan ibibiig so brit so makapanonot ed sakey a manag-ani. No nanengneng ed saray ulo na palo, lalo la no tinmunda ira tan ag-onkikiwas ed antikey a panaon, say angkakabaleg ya andeket a porma ra et singa anggapoy bilay a bato nen say antokaman. Tan singa ed saray angkabaleg a bansa ed India ya panag-anop, say sankaili ya walad arawi et no maminsan et onlabas ed saray patar ya akayurong ya elepante ya agto amta no ontan ira, ya ipapasen to ran anggapoy kawes, maimpis a pasen na dalin; anggan ontan, mabetbet, ed sikato, ya diad primeron pankanawnawa et akanengneng ed sayan klase na leviathan ed dayat. Tan anggano nabidbir la ed kaunoran, say baleg a kabaleg da et mamapairap a panisiaan a saratan a angkakabaleg a karakel na alablabas a tanaman so posiblin instinct, diad amin a parte, a walaan na parehon klase na bilay a manbibilay ed sakey ya aso odino kabayo.

Tua, diad arum a aspekto, ngalngali agmo nanengneng so anggan dinan a pinalsa ed aralem a dayat a walaan na parehon liknaan a singa ed saray pinalsa ed gilig na dayat. Ta anggaman wala ray daan ya naturalista ya mangibabagan amin ya pinalsa ed dalin et singa ra walad dayat; tan anggaman nengnengen so bengatla, saya so nayarin tua; balet no nipaakar ed saray espisyalidad, iner, alimbawa, so pangiter na dayat na antokaman a sira ya diad disposisyon to et onebat ed makabat a kaabigan na aso? Say sinumpa a pating labat so nayarin ibaga a mipara ed sikato.

Balet, parad saray taga-dalin ed inkalapagan, saray katutubon manaayam ed dayat so abayag lan ipapasen a walaan na emosyon ya agnabalikas ya agmakapibiang ed sosyedad tan makapadimla; anggaman amta tayon say dayat et sakey ya andi-anggaan a terra incognita, kanian si Columbus so nanlayag ed agnabilang iran ag kabat a mundo pian nadiskobre so sakey a maawang a mundo to ed sagur; anggaman, diad baleg a posibilidad, say sankataktakotan ed amin a mortal a desyang so agnalingwanan tan andiay panangidumaduma ya agawa ed samplora tan nilasus nilibo ed saramay linma ed tapew na danum; anggaman say sakey a bekta labat a konsiderasyon so mangibangat, ya anggan panon lay pangipasirayew na ugaw a laki ed siensia tan dunong to, tan anggan panon karakel, diad marakep ya arapen, say siensia tan dunong so nayarin ondarakel; balet diad andi-anggaan, anggad kapegleyan na kadederal, sikatoy insultoen tan pateyen na dayat, tan deralen to so sankamaongan, sanka-malet a fregata a nayarian ton gawaen; anggaman ontan, diad tuloytuloy ya pangulit ed sarayan impresyon, naandi so pakalikna na too ed inkagrabe na dayat ya kayarian to.

Say parehon dayat so onkukurang natan; satan met lanlamang a dayat so aneral ed saray aderal a barko nen imbeneg a taon. On, makulangkulang a totoo, say delap nen Noe so ag ni tinmunda; duaran kakatlo na patas a mundo ya nasasakopan to ni.

Antoy pandumaan na dayat tan dalin, ya say milagro ed sakey et aliwan milagro ed sananey? Saray ag-inkayadyari na takot so sinmabi ed saray Hebreo, nen diad silong na sali nen Kora tan saray kakaiba to et alukasan so mabilay a dalin tan inakmon to ra ed andi-anggaan; balet anggapoy modernon agew ya onseselek, balet diad parehon paraan ya akmonen na mabilay a dayat iray barko tan saray tripulante.

Imanoen so inkasilib na dayat; no panon ya onlelereg iray sankatatakotan a pinalsa to ed silong na danum, ya ag nanengneng ed karaklan, tan makapalikdo ya akaamot ed silong na sankarakpan iran kolor na azure. Isipen yo yan amin; insan ontalineng ed sayan berde, mauyamo, tan sankamauyamoan a dalin; isipen mo ra a dua, say dayat tan say dalin; tan ag mo kasi naromog so nikadkaduman panangikompara ed sakey a bengatla ed inkasika? Ta singa say makapataktakot a dayat ya akapaliber ed mararakep a dalin, ontan met ed kamarerwa na too et walay sakey ya insular a Tahiti, a napno na kareenan tan liket, balet ta asakbongan na amin a makapataktakot a kapalduan kabkabat a bilay. Iyagel ka na Dios! Ag ka ontaynan ed satan ya isla, ag ka lan balot makapawil!





KAPITULO 59. Pusit.

Diad kalkalnan ondadalan ed saray katakelan na brit, say Pequod et siansia nin akaarap ed amianen-bukig ya amaarap ed isla na Java; sakey a maamot ya dagem ya mangipapaarap ed kilya to, kanian diad kaliberliber a kareenan et say taloran atagey a manpapaspas a palo to so kalmadon onkukurong ed satan a makulangkulang a dagem, a singa taloran anlemew a palma ed patar. Tan siansia nin, diad andukey iran panaon ed singa pilak a labi, nanengneng so manbukbukor tan makapasagyat ya jet.

Balet sakey a malinew ya asul a kabuasan, sanen say kareenan ya ngalngali ag-inkayadyari so kinmayat ed dayat, anggaman anggapoy mangasikaso ed antokaman a kalmado; nen say andukey ya pinasileng ya sun-glade ed tapew na danum et singa sakey a balitok a gamet ya inyan ed tapew da, ya mangigaganggan na pigaran sekreto; nen manbubulong iray onlelereg a daluyon legan iran kalmadon ombabatik; diad sayan aralem a kareenan na nanengneng a globo et sakey a nikadkaduman anito so anengneng nen Daggoo manlapud manunan-ulo na palo.

Diad arawi, sakey a baleg ya amputin masa so matamoy ya ontatagey, tan ontatagey tan ontatagey, tan onsisinag ed azure, diad kaunoran et onsisinag ed arap mi a singa sakey a snow-slide, balon slide manlapud pukdol. Kanian mankirlap ed sakey a bekta, legan ya kalkalnan onkupas, tan onlelereg. Insan inmalagey lamet, tan mareen ya kinmilap. Singa aliwan balyena itan; balet ta saya kasi so Moby Dick? inisip nen Daggoo. Linmeksab lamet so phantom, balet nen pinmatnag lamet, tekep na singa stiletto ya eyag ya amabigla ed balang laki manlapud inyurong to, inmeyag so negro—“Diman! diman lamet! diman so insabi to! diad unaan! Say White Whale, say White Whale!”

Diad saya, saray marino so binmatik ed saray yard-arms, a singa diad panaon na panag-awit na saray buyog et binmatik ed saray sanga. Anggapoy kawes to ed mansasnit ya agew, si Ahab so inmalagey ed bowsprit, tan say sakey a lima to et intulak to ed benegan pian akaparaan lan mangiwagayway na saray ganggan to ed timon, inmarap ed direksion ya ipapanengneng na akaunat ya ag-onkikiwas a taklay nen Daggoo.

No kasin say onkukurang ya inyakar na sakey ya ag-onkikiwas tan manbukbukor ya jet so kalkalnan nankimey ed si Ahab, kanian sikatoy akaparaan lan mangikonekta ed saray ideya na kauyamoan tan painawa ed primeron pakanengneng ed partikular a balyena ya inusilan to; antokaman iya, odino no kasin say inkaseseg to so angitarok ed sikato; antokaman so nagawa, ag to ni anengneng so amputin masa, et sikatoy tampol ya angiter na ganggan ya iyabeba.

Say apatiran baloto et akasabi lad danum; Si Ahab et walad unaan, tan amin da et maples ya onaarap ed biktimaen da. Agnambayag et linmeksab itan, tan legan ya akabitin iray sagwan, aalagaren mi so ipawil to, nia! diad parehon pasen ya linmereg, maminsan lamet et kalkalnan inmalagey. Ngalngali nalingwanan mi ed saman a bekta so amin a kanonotan nipaakar ed Moby Dick, natan et ninmengneng kami ed sankakelkelawan a bengatla ya anggad natan et imparungtal na saray sekreton dayat ed katooan. Sakey a baleg a pulpy mass, a pigaran estadio so karukey tan kalapar to, a mankirlap a kolor-krema, so onleletaw ed danum, agnabilang so andukey iran taklay to a manlalapud sentro to, tan mankukurongkurong tan mankukurongkurong a singa ubong na anaconda, a singa bulag a mangawit ed antokaman a makapaermen a bengatla a naabot to. Anggapoy nanengneng ya lupa odino arap to; anggapoy naisip ya tanda na liknaan odino inkatoo; balet ta manpapaspas diman ed saray daluyon, sakey ya aliwan-dalin, andiay-porma, singa-kanepegan ya itsura na bilay.

Diad sakey ya abeban tanol na panagsusop et kalkalnan naandi lamet, si Starbuck et manbabantay nin siansia ed saray magulon danum ya kawalaan to, tekep na makapataktakot a boses ya inmeyag—“Mas labay ko komon ya anengneng si Moby Dick tan milaban ed sikato, nen say anengneng ta ka, sika ya amputin aswang!”

"Anto itan, Sir?" inkuan nen Flask.

"Say baleg a mabilay a pusit, ya, inkuan da, pigpigara labat a barkon balyena so akanengneng, tan pinmawil ed saray daongan da pian ibaga itan."

Balet anggapoy imbaga nen Acab; inpawil toy baloto to, tan sikatoy pinmawil ed baloto; say arum et singa mareen ya ontutumbok.

Antokaman so superstisyon na saray sperm whalemen ed inkalapagan nipaakar ed pakanengneng ed sayan bengatla, seguradon say pakanengneng ed satan et nikadkaduman tuloy, tan satan a sirkumstansya so linma ed arawi pian ipuonan itan na makapataktakot. Matalag itan ya nanengneng, ya anggaman sakey ed sikara so mangibabagan saya so sankabalgan ya mabilay a bengatla ed dayat, balet pigpigara ed sikara so walaan na antokaman nilikud ed sanka-malinew iran ideya nipaakar ed peteg a inkatoo tan itsura to; anggaman ontan, manisia ran saya so mangiter ed sperm whale na saksakey a naakan to. Ta anggaman arum a klase na balyena et makakaromog na naakan da ed tagey na danum, tan nayarin nanengneng na too legan iran mamangan, say spermaceti whale et makakagamor na amin a naakan to diad ag kabat iran lugar ed leksab na danum; tan diad panamegley labat na panag-isip et naamtaan na siopaman no antoy eksakton kabiangan na satan a kakanen. No maminsan, no maapit ya uusilan, sikatoy mangibulos ed samay ipapasen ya akapeket iran taklay na pusit; arum ed sikara so angipatnag na masulok a duamplo tan talomplon mitro so karukey da. Iisipen da ya say ulsa ya kawalaan na sarayan taklay et kaslakan ya akapeket ed dalem na dayat; tan say sperm whale, aliwan singa arum ya klase na ayayep, et walaan na ngipen pian naataki tan nabuyak to.

Ompatnag a walay rason pian isipen a say baleg a Kraken nen Bishop Pontoppodan so nayarin magmaliw a Squid. Say paraan na impaneskribe na Obispo ed satan, bilang mansasalatan ya ontatagey tan onlelereg, tan arum niran detalye ya insalaysay to, diad amin na saya et mantutunosan irayan dua. Balet kaukolan so dakel a panangibawas nipaakar ed agmakapanisiaan a karakel ya iter to ed satan.

Diad pigaran naturalista ya akadngel na saray tsismis nipaakar ed misteryoson pinalsa, ya abitla dia, et kaiba itan ed klase na cuttle-fish, ya diad tua, diad pigaran aspekto et singa kabiangan itan, balet bilang Anak labat na tribu.





KAPITULO 60. Say Linya.

Diad panutukoy ed eksena na panag-ayam na balyena ya magano lan deskribien, tan pian mas natalosan so amin ya mipadparan eksena ya nipresenta ed arum a pasen, kaukolan kon pantongtongan so nipaakar ed mahiko, no maminsan et makapataktakot a linya na balyena.

Say lubir ya inmunan inusar ed panagsigay et sankaabigan ya abaka, ya medyo insingaw na alkitran, ya ag-inmamot ed satan, a singa ed kaso na ordinaryon lubir; ta bangta say alkitran, a kaslakan ya uusaren, et manggagawa ed abaka ya mas mainomay ya usaren na managgaway lubir, tan manggagawa met ed lubir ya mas kombeniente ed marino parad kaslakan ya pangusar ed barko; balet, aliwa labat ya say ordinaryon karakel so mamabiskeg ed linya na balyena parad maapit a panag-ikot ya kaukolan ya ipaarap ed satan; balet singa onggapo lan naaralan na karaklan a marino, say alkitran ed inkalapagan et agbalot makaarum ed inkadukey odino biskeg na lubir, anggan panon lay pangiter to ed satan na inka-kompakto tan inka-gloss.

Diad apalabas iran taon say lubir na Manilla ed panagsigay na Amerika et ngalngalin amin lan sinmalat ed abaka bilang materyales parad saray linya na balyena; ta, anggaman aliwan tanton matibay a singa say abaka, mas mabiskeg itan, tan mas malambot tan mas nababanat; tan iyarum ko (lapud walay æsthetics ed amin a bengatla), et mas guapo tan matukoy ed baloto, nen say hemp. Si Hemp et sakey a maimpis, maimpis a too, sakey a klase na Indian; balet si Manilla et singa balitok so buek ton Circassian ya nengnengen.

Say kapal na balyena et duaran-kakatlo labat na sakey pulgada. Diad primeron pakanengneng, agmo isipen ya mabiskeg itan a singa ed tua. Diad panamegley na eksperimento, say sakey tan limamplon sinulid to et makabitin na sanlasus tan duamplon libra so belat to; pian say interon lubir et makaawit na belat ya ngalngalin mipara ed taloran tonelada. Diad karukey, say kaslakan ya sperm whale-line et mansukat na masulok ya duanlasus a fathom. Diad bandad beneg na baloto et manpapalikdo itan ed tub, aliwan singa say worm-pipe na still though, noagta pian manporma na sakey a malimpek, singa keso a masa na makapal a “sheaves,” odino saray layer na mankakasakey iran spiralisation, ya anggapoy antokaman a abot noag say “puso,” odino melag a bertikal a tubo ya aporma ed ehe na keso. Lapud say sankaabebaan ya tangle odino kink ed coiling et, diad pakaupot, et agnalingo ya ekalen so taklay, sali, odino interon laman na sakey a too, say sankatageyan a panag-alwar so usaren ed pangiyan ed linya ed tub to. Arum a manag-harpooneer so manguusar na ngalngalin interon kabuasan ed sayan negosyo, ya awiten da so linya ed tagey insan da ipelag ed leksab panamegley na sakey a bloke ya amaarap ed tub, pian diad gawa na panag-coil pian naekal itan ed amin a posiblin kulubot tan kurot.

Diad saray baloton Ingles et duaran tub so uusaren imbes ya sakey; say parehon linya so tuloytuloy ya akakulong ed duaran tub. Walay pigaran bentaha ed saya; lapud melmelag irayan twin-tub ya mas mainomay iran onloob ed baloto, tan ag ira masyadon naiirapan; bangbalet, say American tub, ya ngalngalin taloran mitro so diametro to tan mipara so kaaralem to, et manggagawa na medyo baleg a kargamento parad sakey a bapor ya saray tabla to et sakey labat a kapalduan pulgada so kapal to; ta say leksab na balyena-baloto et singa kritikal a yelo, ya mangawit na baleg a nibunog a belat, balet aliwan masyadon mankakasakey. No say pinintaan ya canvas ya apis et ipalakpak ed American line-tub, say baloto et singa onkukurang ya walay balibalin wedding-cake ya iter ed saray balyena.

Say duaran sampot na linya et akalukas; say abeban sampot ya mansampot ed eye-splice odino loop ya manlalapud leksab ya onsumpad gilig na tub, tan akasabit ed gilig to ya sigpot ya ag-akakonekta ed amin a bengatla. Sayan uksoyan na abeban sampot et nakaukolan ed duaran rason. Unona: Pian napagaan so pangipeket ed satan na kaaruman a lubir manlapud kabangibang a baloto, no bilang ta say atumba a balyena et ontanlan aralem so tanol to ya nayarin mangawit ed interon lubir ya inmuna lan akapeket ed harpoon. Diad sarayan inkagawa, say balyena et singa sakey a tasa na ale, a singa panangibaga, manlapud sakey a baloto ya anggad sananey; anggaman say unonan baloto et lanang ya onkukurang pian tulongan so kapareha to. Kumadwa: Sayan uksoyan et nakaukolan parad kaligenan na amin; ta no say abeban sampot na linya et akapeket ed baloto, tan no say balyena et ondalan ed linya ya anggad sampot ed ngalngalin sakey, mansisigarilyo a minuto a singa gagawaen to no maminsan, sikatoy ag ontunda diman, ta say naderal a baloto et ag nalingo ya ontumbok ed sikato diad kaaralem na dayat; tan no ontan et anggapo lamet so makaromog ed sikato na manag-awit na baley.

Antis ya iyabeba so baloto parad panguusil, say bandad tagey a sampot na linya et alaen ed beneg manlapud tub, tan diad kaliberliber na loggerhead diman, et iyabante lamet so interon karukey na baloto, ya akapeket ed tapew na loom odino panangawit na balang sakey a too, pian ontumbok itan ed pulso to legan ya manpapagaud; tan ondalan met ed baetan na saray lalaki, legan iran mansasalatan ya akayurong ed nantupag a gunwales, ya anggad saray leaded chocks odino grooves ed sankatageyan a mapitek a prow na baloto, a ditan et say sakey a pin odino skewer a gawad kiew a singa kabaleg na sakey a common quill, so mangamper ed satan pian ag-itan onlelereg. Manlapud saray pana et akabitin itan ed melag a festoon ed tapew na saray pana, insan ipasa lamet ed loob na baloto; tan manga samploran o duamplon fathom (tatawagen ya box-line) ya akapeket ed kahon diad saray pana, mantultuloy itan ya onlad gunwale ya mas arawi ni ed beneg, insan ipeket ed short-warp—say lubir ya tampol ya akakonekta ed harpoon; balet antis na satan a koneksion, say short-warp et ondalan ed nanduruman mistipikasyon ya makapakesaw ya idetalye.

Kanian say whale-line et mantitiklop ed interon baloto diad komplikadon coil to, ya manpapaspas tan manpapaspas ed kaliberliber to ed ngalngalin amin a direksion. Amin ya managsagwan et nalalanor ed mapeligron panag-ikot to; kanian diad matakot ya mata na taga-dalin, singa ra Indian jugglers, ya saray sankagrabian ya uleg et manpapaspas ed saray sali ra. Nisay siopaman ya anak na mortal a bii, diad primeron pankanawnawa, so makapanyurong ed pegley na saratan a hempen intricacies, tan legan ton ipapasoot so anggaay nayarian to ed panagsagwan, isipen ton diad antokaman ya ag-amta a bekta et nayarin napelag so harpoon, tan amin na sarayan makapataktakot a panag-apag-apag so niiyan a singa saray singsing a kirmat; agto nayarin ontan so kipapasen to no anggapoy panngalngali to ya manggawa ed mismon utek to ya manngalngali singa sakey a jelly ya jelly. Balet ugali—nikadkaduman bengatla! antoy ag nagawaan na ugali?—Saray mas maliket a sallies, mas maliket a liket, mas marakep iran jokes, tan mas maliwawa iran repartees, ya agmo nin balot nadngel ed tapew na mahogany mo, nen say narengel mo ed tapew na kapalduan pulgadan amputin sedro na balyena-boat, sano akasabit ed saray lubir na managbitay; tan, singa saray anemiran burgers na Calais sakbay nen King Edward, saray anemiran lalakin kabiangan na tripulante so onkukurang ed panga na patey, a walaan na halter ed kada beklew, a singa nayarin ibaga yo.

Nayarin say daiset a kanonotan so makatulong ed sika natan pian napaliwawa iray paulit-ulit a kalamidad ed panag-ayam na balyena—a pigara ed saratan et kaslakan labat ya insulat—ya sayan too odino saman a too so inala ed baloto lapud linya, tan abalang. Ta, no say linya et onkukurang, say pakayurong ed baloto, et singa pakayurong ed pegley na nanduruman ungol na steam-engine ya manpapatogtog, sano kada ontitikyab a sinag, tan baras, tan pilid, so manpapastol ed sika. Mas grabe ni; ta ag ka makayurong ya ag-onkikiwas ed pegley na sarayan kapeligroan, lapud say baloto et onyuyurong a singa dukolan, tan sika so akapuesto ed sakey a direksion tan sananey, ya anggapoy anggan melag a pasakbay; tan diad panamegley labat na sakey a self-adjusting buoyancy tan pankakasakey na boluntaryo tan kiwas, et makaliktar ka ed panggawa na Mazeppa na, tan ombatik ka ed pasen ya ag ka la nasaksak na mismon agew ya makanengneng ed amin.

Lamet: lapud say aralem a kareenan ya ompatnag labat ya akauna tan mangipropesiya na bagyo, et nayarin mas makapataktakot nen say bagyo a mismo; ta, diad tua, say kalmado et singa labat balot tan sakob na bagyo; tan walaan itan na inkasikato, a singa say singa agmakaderal a paltog a walaan na makapatey a pulbos, tan say bola, tan say insabog; kanian say marakep ya painawa na linya, legan ya mareen ya onkukurang ed saray managsagwan antis ya iyakar ed aktual ya galaw—saya so sakey a bengatla ya mas makapataktakot nen say antokaman ya aspekto na sayan mapeligron agawa. Balet akin et ibaga ni? Amin a totoo et manbibilay ya abalonan na saray balyena. Amin da et nianak ya walay halter ed beklew da; balet no naerel labat ira ed maples tan biglan ipatey, et namoria na saray totoo so mareen, ag-amta, tan pirmin kapeligroan na bilay. Tan no sakey kan pilosopo, anggaman akayurong ka ed baloton balyena, agmo nalikna so anggan sakey a takot, nen say no akayurong ka ed arap na apoy mo ed labi ya walay poker mo, tan aliwan harpoon, ed abay mo.
