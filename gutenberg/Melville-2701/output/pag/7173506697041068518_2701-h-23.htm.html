KAPITULO 124. Say Dagum.

Diad tinmumbok ya kabuasan say agni onkupas a dayat so linmukso ed andukey tan matantan iran daluyon na mabiskeg a laman, tan diad pansasagpot to ed onkukurang a dalan na Pequod, so angitulak ed sikato a singa saray lima na higante ya akaunat. Say mabiskeg tan ag-onkikiwas a dagem so dinmaak kanian say tawen tan dagem et singa angkakabaleg iran layag; say interon mundo so onlelereg ed arap na dagem. Lapud narerengel ed liwawa na kabuasan, say agnanengneng ya agew et naamtaan labat ed inkalaknab na pasen to; diman et onkukurang iray sinag na bayoneta to. Saray dekorasyon, a singa saray akoronaan ya arari tan reyna na Babilonia, so manuuley ed amin a bengatla. Say dayat et singa sakey a banga ya tinunaw a balitok, ya onlelereg ed liwawa tan petang.

Abayag lan ag-inmesel, si Ahab so inmalagey a bukor; tan kada no say onkukurang a barko et onlelereg ed bowsprit to, sikatoy ontalineng pian nengnengen so maliwliwawan sinag na banwa ya ompaway ed unaan; tan nen sikatoy akayurong lan maong ed beneg na barko, sikatoy linmingaw, tan anengneng toy beneg na banwa, tan no panon ya manlalaok so parehon duyaw iran sinag ed ag-onkupas ya ligliwa to.

"Ha, ha, barko ko! nayarin sika so naala natan bilang karwahe na banwa ed dayat. Ho, ho! amin yon nasyon ed arap na proa ko, iyakar ko so banwa ed sikayo! Pako ed saray kaaruman a daluyon; hallo! sakey a tandem, ipapaarap ko so dayat!"

Balet ta biglan ag-inmabobon lapud pigaran kontran kanonotan, sikatoy nanapura ya amaarap ed timon, ya maalwar ya intepet to no panon so laen na barko.

"Bukig-sagur-bukig, amo," inkuan na ataktakot a managmaneho.

"Mantitila ka!" sikatoy tinampal to na akapeket a kamao to. “Manpaarap ed Bukig ed sayan oras na kabuasan, tan say agew ed bokig?”

Diad saya et amin a kamarerwa so awetwet; ta say ebento ya anengneng nen Ahab ed saman et agnaebatan na amin; balet say makapabulag a tuloy a pakalikna to so seguradon sengegan.

Insulong toy ulo to ed kapegleyan na binnacle, anengneng nen Ahab iray kompas; say intagey ton taklay to et kalkalnan atumba; diad sakey a bekta et singa sikatoy onkiling. Diad benegan to et ninmengneng si Starbuck, tan nia! say duaran kompas et akaarap ed Bukig, tan say Pequod et singa ag-onsaew ya mamaarap ed Sagur.

Balet antis ya onsabi so sankaunaan ya alarma ed arum a bansa ed limog na saray tripulante, inkuan na masiken ya walay malet ya tawa to, "Walaan ko la! Agawa la itan nensaman. Mr. Starbuck, say karol nen imbeneg a labi so amalikdo ed saray kompas mi—saman labat. Nadngel mo la natan so ontan a bengatla, awaten ko la."

"On; balet agnin balot agawa itan ed siak, sir," inkuan na amputin asawa, ya maer-ermen.

Ginagala ton inmalagey ed arap na binnacle, tan sankanengneng to ray transpointed compass, say masiken, ya walay matarem ya inyunat ton lima, natan et anengneng toy eksakton direksion na banwa, tan lapud napnek ya eksakton akabaliktad iray dagum, inyeyag to ray ganggan to ya umanen so kurso na barko unong ed satan. Saray sular et malet; tan aminsan lamet et intulak na Pequod iray ag-ontakot ya pana to ed onsusumpan dagem, ta say ipapasen a marakep so man-juggle labat ed sikato.

Kaleganan to, antokaman so sekreton kanonotan to, anggapoy imbaga nen Starbuck, balet mareen ton impaway so amin ya ganggan; bangta si Stubb tan Flask—ya diad daiset a laknab et singa ra mibabali ed nalilikna to—et ag-inmabuloy met ya inmabobon. Nipaakar ed saray lalaki, anggaman arum ed sikara so manpapasnok, say takot da ed si Ahab et mas baleg nen say takot da ed Kapalaran. Balet singa nensaman, saray paganon manag-harpooner et ngalngali ag-akapanliket; odino no mandinayew ira, wala labat so sakey a klase na magnetismo ya inloob ed saray maomaong a kapusoan da manlapud ag-ontutundan kapusoan nen Ahab.

Diad sakey a panaon et nanakar so masiken ed kubyerta ya manpapasnok. Balet nen akalusot so ivory heel to, anengneng to ray apilat ya copper sight-tubes na quadrant ya walad sikato nen imbeneg ya agew ya binmatik ed deck.

"Sika, mairap-bilay, mapaatagey a manag-imaton ed tawen tan piloto na banwa! nen kaplesan et dineral ta ka, tan natan et dineral ak komon na saray kompas. Kanian, ontan. Anganko et ontutumbok ed impulse ya mangididikta ed bengatlan gawaen to natan, so pigaran prudensyal a motibo, a say getma to et nayarin mamaoli ed espiritu na saray tripulante to diad panamegley na sakey a panag-awit na masilib a dunong to, diad sakey a bengatla a makapakelkelaw a singa samay akabaliktad a kompas. Niarum ni, amta na masiken ya say pangimaneho panamegley na saray dagum ya aka-transpoint, anggaman ag-itan nagawaan, et aliwan nepeg ya lingwanan na saray superstisyoson marino, ya anggapoy pigaran panngalngali tan mauges iran tanda.

"Lalaki," inkuanto, ya inmarap ed saray tripulante, legan ya iiter na kapareha ed sikato iray bengatlan kinerew to, "lalaki ko, say karol so amasimbalo ed saray dagum nen masiken nen Ahab; balet manlapud sayan piraso na asero et makapanggawa si Ahab na sakey ed saray dilin dagum to, a singa tua ed antokaman."

Saray marino et mankelaw ed saray mababaing ya nengneng da, legan ya ibabaga iya; tan diad saray mankelaw iran mata et aalagaren da so antokaman a mahiko ya ontumbok. Balet inmarawi so nengneng nen Starbuck.

Diad sakey a suntok manlapud top-maul et atumba nen Ahab so bakal ya ulo na gayang, insan to inter ed mate so andukey a balatyang a baston ya atilak, tan ingganggan ton bembenan itan a maptek, ya ag-onsabi ed kubyerta. Insan, panamegley na maul, kayari ton aminpigan pinaltog so tagey a sampot na sayan balatyang a baras, inyan to so ag-ontutundan dagum ed tapew to, tan agtanton mabiskeg a pinaltog to itan, pigaran danay, say mate so akabemben nin siansia ed baras a singa nensaman. Insan diad impanggawa to na pigaran angkekelag ya nikadkaduman galaw ed satan—no kasin nakaukolan ed pan-magnetize na asero, odino say getma to labat pian napaaligwas so takot na saray tripulante, et ag segurado—sikatoy tinmawag na linen linen; tan inmalis ed binnacle, inlusot to so duaran akabaliktad a dagum diman, tan inbitay to so sail-needle ed pegley to, diad tapew na sakey ed saray compass-card. Diad gapo, say asero et manliber-liber, a manggiwgiw tan manggiwgiw ed balang sampot; balet diad kaunoran et inmalagey itan ed pasen to, nen si Ahab, ya abayag lan manbabantay ed sayan resulta, so prangkan inmarawi manlapud binnacle, tan inturo toy akaunat a taklay to ed satan, ya inkelyaw to,—“Nengnengen yo, parad sikayon dili, no si Ahab et aliwan katawan na patatdang a bato! Say banwa et Bukig, tan mansasamba itan!”

Nantutumbokan iran linmingaw, ta anggapo lay arum noag say mismon mata ra so makapangikombinse ed ontan ya inka-ignorante a singa sikara, tan nantutumbokan iran inmarawi.

Diad manliob iran mata to na panagmudmora tan pananalo, anengneng mo si Ahab ed amin a makapatey a inkamapaatagey to.





KAPITULO 125. Say Log tan Line.

Bangta natan say kapalaran Pequod et abayag lan manbabaroy ed sayan biahe, say troso tan linya et matalag lan nausar. Lapud matalek ya manmamatalek ed arum a paraan pian naamtaan so kawalaan na barko, arum a negosyante, tan dakel a manag-balyena, lalo la no manbiabiahe, so ag da labay ya itagey so troso; anggaman diad saman met a panaon, tan mabetbet a mas parad porma nen say antokaman, a naynay ya ipapasen ed niyugali lan slate so kurso ya igigiya na barko, ontan met ed ipapasen a average a peles na iyaaligwas ed kada oras. Ontan so agawa ed saray Pequod. Say tablan reel tan say angular a troso ya akapeket et akabitin, ya abayag lan ag-apektado, diad silong na barandilya na saray bulwark. Say uran tan spray so amawala ed satan; say agew tan dagem so amalikdo ed satan; amin ya elemento so nankakasakey pian nabulok so sakey a bengatla ya ag-onkikiwas. Balet anggano agto inimano irayan amin, say mood to so akasabi ed si Ahab, nen sikatoy akanengneng ed reel, ag-abayag kayari na eksena na magnet, tan anonotan to no panon ya anggapo lay quadrant to, tan anonotan to so makapataktakot a samba to nipaakar ed level log tan line. Say barko et onkukurang a manlalayag; diad beneg et saray daluyon so nan-gulo.

"Arap, diman! Itagey moy troso!"

Sinmabi so duaran marino. Say golden-hued Tahitian tan say grizzly ya Manxman. "Alaen mo so reel, sakey ed sikayo, itagey ko."

Linma ra ed bandad beneg na barko, diad gilig na barko, a ditan et say kubyerta, tekep na ag-inkayadyari na dagem, so ngalngali la onlelereg ed malimpek, manpapaspas a dayat.

Inala na Manxman so reel, tan inegnaan to itan ed tagey, diad panamegley na saray onlelereg a sampot na spindle, ya akapaliber so spool na linya, kanian inmalagey ya akabitin so angular a troso ed leksab, anggad inmasingger ed sikato si Ahab.

Inmalagey si Ahab ed arap to, tan manpapaspas na manga talomplo odino apataplon liko pian makagawa na unonan hand-coil ya ipelag ed dayat, nen say masiken ya Manxman, ya manbabantay a maong ed sikato tan ed linya, so nansalita.

"Katawan, agko itan panmamatalkan; sayan linya et singa arawi la, aderal itan na andukey a petang tan ambetel."

"'Mansiansia ak, masiken. Say andukey a petang tan ambetel, kasin aderal da ka? Singa ka makabemben. Odino, mas tua siguro, say bilay so makabemben ed sika; aliwan sika itan."

"Agko amta, amo, balet diman ak nianak."

"Diad Isle of Man, hey? Bueno, diad sananey a dalan, maabig itan. Nia so sakey a too a nanlapud Man; sakey a too ya inyanak ed datin independentin Too, tan natan et anggapoy too na Too; ya inisip na anto? Diad tagey na reel! Say inatey, bulag a padir so ontupak ed amin ya mantetepet ya ulo. Diad tagey! Kanian."

Say troso so atagey. Saray malukak a coil so maples ya inmalagey ed andukey a linya ed beneg, insan, tampol, say reel so ginmapon nan-ikot. Diad saginonor, lapud inmatagey tan inyabeba na saray onkukurang a daluyon, say resistensya na panagguyod na troso so sengegan na ag-inkayadyari na masiken a reelman.

"Mansiansia ka!"

Biglaan! say alablabas ya linya et inmepas ed sakey ya andukey a festoon; naandi so tugging log.

"Ibabalang ko so kuadrante, say karol et mamapaliber ed saray dagum, tan natan et say dayat ya balibali so manbibiig ed linya na troso. Balet sarag nen Ahab ya apigeren so amin. Iguyor mo dia, Tahitian; man-reel up ka, Manxman. Tan nengneng mo, paulyanan mo so karpintero ya manggawa na sananey a troso, tan apigeren mo so linya. Seguroen mo."

"Diad sikato la natan; parad sikato et anggapoy agawa; balet parad siak, say skewer et singa onlulukas ed pegley na mundo. Onloob, onloob, Tahitian! Sarayan linya et ondadalan ya intero, tan onkukurang: onloob ya abuyak, tan onkukurang a matantan. Ha, Pip? Onla ka pian ontulong; eh, Pip?"

Iyugyog yo, Tahiti! Iyugyog yo; anggapoy matakot ya awiten mi dia. Say taklay to! Manpapaspas labat so taklay to! putoten mo itan—anggapoy naala tayon matakot dia, sir, nia si Pip, mansasagpot ya onlugan lamet.”

"Kareenan, sika ya buanges," inkelyaw na Manxman, ya inegnaan toy taklay to. "Arawi ed quarter-deck!"

"Astern diman, katawan, astern! Nia! Nia!"

Dia, ugaw; say abung nen Ahab so magmaliw ya ayaman nen Pip manlapud natan, legan a mabilay si Ahab. Diwiten mo so sankaarawian a sentrok, say anak ko; saray lubir na puso. Gala, onleksab tayo.”

“O, ugaw, ag ta ka met labay, likud no iguygoy ta ka ed mas graben makapataktakot nen say wala dia. Gala sirin, ed abung ko. KAPITULO 126. Say Life-Buoy.

Manpapaarap natan ed abalaten-bukig panamegley na pinatas ya asero nen Ahab, tan say iyaaligwas to et nadetermina labat ed lebel na troso tan linya nen Ahab; say Pequod so akaalagey ed dalan to ya amaarap ed Equator. Say panggawa na andukey a dalan ed saratan ya ag-onsabin danum, ya anggapoy barko, tan diad andukey a panaon, ya onkukurang ed ag-onkukurang a dagem, diad tapew na saray daluyon ya ag-onkukurang; amin na saraya et singa nikadkaduman kalmadon bengatla ya mangigapo na pigaran magulo tan desperado ya eksena.

Diad kaunoran, nen asingger lay barko ed paway, a singa panangibaga, na Equatorial fishing-ground, tan diad aralem a kabilungetan ya onsasabi antis na kabuasan, et manlalayag ed abay na sakey a grupo na saray mabaton isla; say relo—ya indaulo nen Flask—so abigla ed sakey ya eyag ya makapaermen tan agmakatunongan—singa kapalduan-artikuladon eyag na saray multo na amin ya pinatey ya Inosente nen Herodes—ya sakey tan amin, et ginmapo ira ed saray panagnonot da, tan diad loob na pigaran bekta et inmalagey, odino akayurong, odino akayurong, ya amin et ondedengel ed luluganan legan ya aripen na Roma say atap ya eyag so nansiansia ed loob na pakadngel. Inkuan na saray Kristiano odino sibilisadon kabiangan na saray tripulante ya sirena iratan, tan kinmiwas ira; balet saray paganon manag-arpa et ag-abigla. Balet say gray ya Manxman—say sankatatkenan a marino ed amin—so angibaga ya saray makapalikliket ya tanol ya nadngel et boses na saray balon linmereg ed dayat.

Diad leksab na duyan to, ag-itan nadngel nen Ahab anggad kabuasan, nen sinmabi ed kubyerta; insan to insalaysay nen Flask ed sikato, ya anggapoy kaiba ton ambilunget iran kabaliksan. Sikatoy tinmawa ya andi-kakanaan, tan diad ontan et impaliwawa to so pankelawan.

Saraman ya mabaton isla ya dinalan na barko so panaayaman na dakel a seal, tan pigaran malangwer nin seal ya naandiay anak da, odino pigaran dam ya naandiay anak da, et seguradon inmasingger ed barko tan akiulop ed sikato, ya onaakis tan mannangis ed singa too. Balet saya so lalon apektado ed arum ed sikara, lapud maslak ed saray marino et walaan na superstisyoson liknaan nipaakar ed saray seal, ya aglabat manlalapud nikadkaduman tono ra no nagogonigon ira, noagta lapud itsura na too na saray malimpek ya ulo ra tan medyo intelihentin lupa ra, a nanengneng ya ontatalindeg manlapud danum a kaiba ra. Diad dayat, diad pigaran sirkumstansya, saray seal et aminpiga lan nalilingo ed totoo.

Balet say naisip na saray tripulante et makaawat na makatunongan a kumpirmasyon ed kapalaran na sakey ed sikara ed saman a kabuasan. Diad isabi na banwa, sayan laki et linma ed duyan to ya amaarap ed ulo na palo to diad arap; tan no kasin sikatoy agni kapalduan aliing ed ugip to (ta saray marino no maminsan et onla ed tagey diad transisyon a kipapasen), no kasin ontan so agawa ed samay laki, anggapo lay nibaga natan; balet, antokaman so nagawa, sikatoy ag-abayag ed yurongan to, nen walay nadngel ya eyag—sakey ya eyag tan sakey a panag-apura—tan nen tinmangay ira, anengneng da so onlelereg ya aswang ed dagem; tan no onnengneng ed leksab, walay melag ya inbantak ya bunton na amputin bula ed asul na dayat.

Say life-buoy—sakey ya andukey tan maimpis a bariles—so inpelag manlapud beneg, ya diman et lanang ya akabitin ya ontutulok ed sakey a masilib a spring; balet anggapoy lima ya inmalagey pian mangala ed satan, tan say agew ya abayag lan manpapaknol ed sayan bariles et kinmepa, kanian kalkalnan napno, tan saman ya amagan kiew et napno met ed kada buer to; tan say akapeket a balatyang a bariles so tinmumbok ed marino anggad leksab, a singa to iter ed sikato so unan to, anggaman malimpek balet ta malet.

Kanian say inmunan too na Pequod ya linmugan ed palo pian bantayan so White Whale, diad mismon nikadkaduman dalin na White Whale; saman a too et inakmon ed kaaralman. Balet pigpigara, nayarin, so nannonot ed satan ed saman a panaon. Tua, diad arum a paraan, ag ira naermenan ed sayan ebento, anggan panon et sakey a tanda; ta impasen da itan, aliwan bilang anino na kaugsan ed arapen, noagta bilang kasumpalan na sakey a kaugsan ya impasakbay la. Inkuan da ya amta da la natan so rason na saraman ya makapasinagem ya eyag ya nadngel da nen imbeneg a labi. Balet inkuan lamet na masiken ya Manxman ya andi.

Say abalang ya life-buoy et kaukolan lan salatan; Say Starbuck so niganggan ya mangasikaso ed satan; balet lapud anggapoy naromog a bariles na magenap a kagaan, tan singa ed makapasinagem a panlabay ed singa onsasabin krisis na biahe, amin a lima so agmakaanos ed antokaman a sagpot nilikud ed no anto so direktan misiglaotan ed unor a pansumpalan to, antokaman so nayarin paneknekan na satan; kanian, taynanan da la so beneg na barko ya anggapoy boya to, nen diad panamegley na pigaran nikadkaduman tanda tan inuendos et impanengneng nen Queequeg so nipaakar ed lungon to.

“Sakey a life-buoy na lungon!” inmeyag si Starbuck, ya ginmapo.

"Maslak et makapakelaw itan, nepeg kon ibaga," inkuan nen Stubb.

"Maong itan," inkuan nen Flask, "say karpintero dia et mainomay ton iyuksoy."

"Iparungtal mo; anggapo lay arum a parad satan," inkuan nen Starbuck, kayari na makapaermen ya intunda. "Iparaan mo, karpintero; agmo ak nengnengen—say lungon, labay kon ibaga. Narerengel mo ak? Iparaan mo."

"Tan ipako ko kasi so taklay, amo?" igagalaw toy lima to a singa martilyo.

“On.”

"Tan i-caulk ko ray tahi, ginoo?" igagalaw toy lima to a singa panag-caulking-iron.

“On.”

"Tan bayaran ko kasi itan na pitch, sir?" igagalaw toy lima to a singa ed sakey a pitch-pot.

"Arawi! antoy mamapaarap ed sika ed saya? Manggawa kay life-buoy ed lungon, tan anggapo lay arum.—Mr. Stubb, Mr. Flask, mila kad siak."

Tan satan ya anino et lanang ya onkukurang diman. Ta aliwan diad labi, anggan, et si Fedallah et talagan kabkabat ya naugip, odino onlelereg. Sikatoy onalagey ya ag-onkikiwas ed loob na pigaran oras: balet agbalot akayurong odino akayurong; saray makulangkulang balet makapakelkelaw a mata to so malinew ya angibaga—Sikamin duaran managbantay so agbalot manpapainawa.

Nisay, anggan kapigan, diad labi odino agew et saray marino et nayarin ontatdang ed kubyerta, likud no walad unaan da si Ahab; balanglan akaalagey ed pivot-hole to, odino eksakton manpapasyar ed saray tabla ed baetan na duaran ag-onkukurang a limitasyon,—say main-mast tan say mizen; odino anengneng da ya akaalagey ed cabin-scuttle,—say mabilay a sali to so inmabante ed kubyerta, a singa ontaynan; say sombrero to so akapeket a maong ed saray mata to; kanian anggan panon lay inka-ag-onkikiwas to, anggan panon lay kaaruman na agew tan labi, ya ag-inmamot ed duyan to; balet ta akaamot ira ed silong na satan ya akapeket a sombrero, ag dan balot naimano no kasin, diad amin na saya, saray mata to et talagan akasara no maminsan; odino no kasin sikatoy manbabantay ni ed sikara; anggapoy pibabali to, anggaman sikatoy akaalagey ed scuttle ed loob na sakey oras, tan say ag-inimano a danum ed labi so nantitipon ed saray beads na linaew ed saman ya inukit ed bato ya amerikana tan sombrero. Saray kawes ya abasa nen labi, et amagaan ed sinag na agew ed tinmumbok ya agew; tan ontan, agew ed sanagew, tan labi ed labi; ag la linma ed silong na saray tabla; antokaman so labay to ed kabin et satan so ipawit to.

Angan ed parehon lawak; salanti, say duaran panangan to labat,—almusal tan pangdem: pangdem ya agton balot diniwit; nisay inani to so balbas to; ya diad ambilunget et binmaleg ya amin et nan-gnarled, a singa saray nakotkot a lamot na kiew ya inmawet, ya siansia nin ombabaleg ya andi-kakanaan ed lakseb a leksab, anggaman naandi ed tagey a berdura. Balet anggaman say interon bilay to et nagmaliw lan sakey a relo ed kubyerta; tan anggaman say mistikon panagbantay na Parsee et anggapoy tunda to a singa sikato; balet sarayan duara et singa ag balot mansasalita—sakey a too ed sananey—likud no diad andukey iran panaon et walay onlabas ya ag-importantin bengatla ya sengegan na pankaukolan da. Anggaman say ontan ya mabiskeg ya anting-anting et singa masekreton mangikasakey ed sikaran dua; maparungtal, tan parad saray mankelkelaw a tripulante, singa ra singa poste ya akasian. No diad agew et makapansalita ra na sakey a salita; diad labi, saray emel a lalaki so pareho, no nipaakar ed anggan daiset a pansasalitaan. No maminsan, diad sankabayagan ya oras, ya anggapoy anggan sakey a uran a kristal, sikaray akaalagey ya arawi ed liwawa na bitewen; Si Ahab ed scuttle to, say Parsee ed abay na mainmast; balet ta siansia nin manbabantay ed balang sakey; singa diad Parsee et anengneng nen Ahab so anino to, diad si Ahab ya Parsee et anengneng toy tinaynan ton kayarian.

Balet, anggan panon et ginawa nen Ahab—diad mismon inkatoo to, ya inagew-agew, kada oras, tan kada bekta, ya ipaparungtal to ed saray sakop to,—si Ahab et singa sakey ya independentin katawan; say Parsee balet say aripen to. Balet parehon singa ra nankakasakey, tan sakey ya agnanengneng a managpairap so mamapaarap ed sikara; say malimpek ya anino ya akaarap ed malet a taglang. Ta antokaman so nagawa ed sayan Parsee, amin a taglang tan kilya et malet ya Ahab.

Diad inmunan sankaabebaan ya liwawa na kabuasan, say boses ton balatyang so nadngel manlapud beneg,—“Man the mast-heads!”—tan diad interon agew, anggad kaselek na banwa tan kayari na pansirunget, say parehon boses ed kada oras, diad pangipaknol na kampanilya na timon, so nadngel—“Antoy nanenengneng mo?—matarem!!”

Balet nen linmabas so talora odino apatiran agew, kayari ton akabat si Raquel ya manaanap na ananak; tan anggapo ni so anengneng ya sangi; say monomaniac ya masiken et singa agmanmamatalek ed katooran na saray tripulante to; anggan panon, ed ngalngalin amin likud ed saray Pagan harpooneers; singa sikatoy manduaruwa, anggan, no kasin si Stubb tan Flask et ag da labay ya lingwanan so nanengneng ton aanapen. Balet no talagan sikato irayan suspetsa, sikatoy maalwar ya ag-angibalikas ed saratan, anggan panon et ompatnag ya ipapatnag iratan na saray kiwas to.

“Siak so unonan makanengneng ed balyena,”—inkuan to. "On, seguradon si Ahab so walaan na doubloon!" tan diad saray dilin lima to et sikatoy nanggawa na ubong na saray aka-basket a bowline; tan angibaki na lima to ed tagey, a walaan na sakey a bloke ya akapeket, pian ipeket ed ulo na manunan palo, inawat to so duaran sampot na lubir ya akapeket ed leksab; tan diad pangikabit to ed sakey ed basket to et angiparaan na pin parad sananey a sampot, pian nipeket itan ed riles. Asumpal to iya, ya walad lima to ni itan ya sampot tan akaalagey ed abay na pin, ninmengneng ed saray tripulante to, ya manpapaspas manlapud sakey ya anggad sananey; intunda toy nengneng to ed si Daggoo, Queequeg, Tashtego; balet ta papaliisan toy Fedallah; insan to inyan so malet ya mata to ed manunan kapareha, inkuanto,—“Alaen mo so lubir, katawan—iter ko ed limam, Starbuck.” Insan to inyuksoy so laman to ed basket, tan inbilin tod sikara ya itagey da ed yurongan to, si Starbuck so angiseguro ed lubir ed kaunoran; tan kayari na satan et inmalagey ed asingger to. Kanian, diad sakey a lima to ya akapeket ed palo na ari, si Ahab et ninmengneng ed dayat ed loob na pigaran milya tan milya, — diad unaan, diad beneg, diad sayan dapag, tan diad saman, — diad loob na malaknab a sirkulo ya akaganggan ed ontanlan kaatagey.

No mankikimey iray lima to ed sakey a atagey a ngalngali nisulinek a pasen ed rigging, ya posiblin anggapoy pakaalageyan to, say marino ed dayat et itagey ed satan a pasen, tan sustinien diman na lubir; diad silong na sarayan sirkumstansya, say akapeket a sampot to ed kubyerta et naynay ya iiter ed sakey a too ya walaan na espisyal a panagbantay ed satan. Lapud diad ontan ya kalawakan na panag-awit na rigging, ya saray nanduruman relasyon da ed tagey et aglawas nanengneng ed no antoy nanengneng ed sikara diad kubyerta; tan no saray sampot na kubyerta na sarayan lubir et kada pigaran minuto et ipelag manlapud saray akapeket, magmaliw labat a natural a pakapatey, no, no anggapoy pirmin managbantay, say akaalagey a marino et lapud ag-inkamaalwar na saray tripulante et napelag ed dayat. Kanian aliwan nikadkaduma so ginawa nen Ahab ed sayan pamaakaran; say nikadkaduman bengatla ed sikara et singa, si Starbuck, ngalngalin sikato labat so toon nansagpot ya onkontra ed sikato ed antokaman ya antokaman ya asingger ed desisyon to—sakey met ed saratan, ya say katooran to ed panagbantay et singa to panduaruwaan;—makapabigla, ta saya so mismon toon nepeg ton pilien parad relo to; bulos ton iter so interon bilay to ed lima na sakey ya agto panmamatalkan.

Natan, diad primeron pankanawnawa et akayurong ed tagey si Ahab; antis ya sikatoy wadman na samploran minuto; sakey ed saramay red-billed savage sea-hawk ya mabetbet ya ontitikyab ya asingger ed saray manned mast-heads na saray whalemen ed sarayan latitud; sakey ed sarayan manok so sinmabin manpapaspas tan manpapasnok ed kaliberliber na ulo to diad sakey a maze na agnasubaybayan a maples iran panagliber. Insan inmalagey itan na sanlibon mitro a diretso ed tagey; insan inmabeba, tan inmawet lamet ed kaliberliber na ulo to.

Balet lapud akasentro so pakanengneng to ed malimpek tan arawin liwang, singa agto naimano nen Ahab iyan atap a manok; nisay, diad tua, anggapo komon so arum a mamarka ed satan a maong, lapud aliwan nikadkaduman sirkumstansya; natan labat et ngalngalin say mata ya ag tanton makaimano et singa makanengneng na sakey a klase na masilib a kabaliksan ed ngalngalin amin a pakanengnengan.

"Say sombrero yo, say sombrero yo, sir!" biglan inmeyag so Sicilian seaman, ya akapuesto ed mizen-mast-head, ya akaalagey ed benegan nen Ahab, anggaman medyo abeba nen say lebel to, tan walay aralem a dagem ya mangaapag ed sikara.

Balet say payak na sable et wala lad arap na mata na masiken; say andukey ya akasabit ya ulo to: diad sakey a eyag, say black hawk et binmatik ya awit toy premyo to.

Sakey ya agila so tinmekkiab a mamitlo ed kaliberliber na ulo nen Tarquin, ya inekal toy kalo to pian isalet itan, tan diad saman et indeklara nen Tanaquil, ya asawa to, ya si Tarquin so magmaliw ya ari na Roma. Balet diad impangisalat labat na kapa et nagmaliw ya maong so tanda. Aglan balot nipawil so sombrero nen Ahab; say atap ya hawk et tinmagey tan tinmagey a kaiba to; arawi ed unaan na proa: tan diad kaunoran et naandi; bangta manlapud punto na inkaandi to, sakey a melag ya andeket a pasen so ag nanengneng, ya onlelereg manlapud satan a baleg a kaatagey ya amaarap ed dayat.
