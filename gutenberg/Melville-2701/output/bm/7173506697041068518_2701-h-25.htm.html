Sisan, kɔnɔ misɛnninw tun bɛ pan ka pɛrɛn kɔgɔjida la min tun bɛ ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ hali bi; jikuru finman dɔ min bɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ, o bɛ gosi a kɛrɛw la minnu ka jan; o kɔfɛ, bɛɛ binna, kɔgɔji ka finiba in wulila ka taa a fɛ i n’a fɔ a bɛ girin cogo min na a bɛ san ba duuru bɔ.





Epilogue (dakun laban) (Epilogue).

“ANI NE DƆRƆN BƆRA K’A FƆ I YE” Job.

Drama in kɛra. O tuma na fɛ, mun na yan, mɔgɔ kelen bɛ sennateliya?—K’a masɔrɔ mɔgɔ dɔ ye a yɛrɛ kisi kurunba tiɲɛnen ma.

A kɛra cogo min na, ko Parsee tununnen kɔfɛ, ne de tun ye Fatew ye min sigi ka Akabu ka nɛgɛsotigi nɔ bila, tuma min na o bɛlɛkisɛtigi ye jɔyɔrɔ lankolon ta o kelen in, min, ni laban don na, cɛ saba ninnu gɛnna ka bɔ kurun yɛrɛyɛrɛ kɔnɔ, a binna kɔfɛ. O la, ka panpan ɲɛnajɛ min tugura o kɔ, o da la, ani k’a ɲɛ bɛɛ lajɛlen na, kurun min jiginna, o sumaya tilancɛ musaka sera n ma tuma min na, o waati la, nka dɔɔnin dɔɔnin, n samara ka taa dadon-yɔrɔ la. N sera a la tuma min na, a tun jiginna ka kɛ jidagayɔrɔ ye min bɛ kɛ ni krema ye. O la, a bɛ lamini ni lamini ye, wa a bɛ to ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ ka taa butɔni-i n’a fɔ bulonba nɛrɛma fan fɛ o sɛrɛkili min bɛ wotoro dɔɔnin dɔɔnin, i n’a fɔ Ixion wɛrɛ n ye min lamini. Fo, ka o cɛmancɛ nafama sɔrɔ, bulon nɛrɛma in bɛ sanfɛ ka pɛrɛn; ani sisan, a hɔrɔnyara a ka jibɔyɔrɔ namara kosɔn, wa, a ka wuliba kosɔn, a bɛ wuli ni fanga belebele ye, sanubɔlan ɲɛnamaya-bɔn ye marifa ci ka janya ka bɔ kɔgɔji la, ka bin, ka panpan ne kɛrɛ fɛ. O sanubɔlan ye ne dɛmɛ, a bɛ se ka fɔ ko tile ni tile kelen bɛɛ, ne tun bɛ panpan kurunba dɔ kan min ka nɔgɔn ani min bɛ i n’a fɔ dirge. Shark minnu ma kojugu kɛ, u tun bɛ gɛrɛntɛ ka tɛmɛ i n’a fɔ ni fɛn dɔ bɛ u da la; kɔgɔji-kɔnɔ-kɔnɔ-kɔnɔ-kɔnɔ-kɔnɔ-wuluw tun bɛ kurun kɔnɔ ni dawolo sirilenw ye. Tile filanan na, kurunba dɔ gɛrɛla, ka surunya, ka laban ka n ta. Rasɛl min tun bɛ kurunba kɔnɔ, o de tun ye yatɔ wɛrɛ dɔrɔn de sɔrɔ, a ka ɲinini kɔfɛ a den tununnenw nɔfɛ, a ye denmisɛnnin wɛrɛ dɔrɔn de sɔrɔ.
