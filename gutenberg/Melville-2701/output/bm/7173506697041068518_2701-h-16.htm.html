SABABU 81. Pequod bɛ Npogotiginin kunbɛn.

Don min tun latigɛra ka kɔn o ɲɛ, o sera, wa an ye kurunba Jungfrau kunbɛn cogo bɛnnen na, n’o ye Derick De Deer ye, min ye kuruntigi ye, Bremen dugu kɔnɔ.

Waati dɔ la, balema-minɛbaga ŋanaw tun bɛ diɲɛ kɔnɔ, Holandekaw ni Alimanw bɛ mɔgɔ dɔgɔmanninw cɛma sisan; nka yan ni yan, latitude ni longitude cɛmancɛbaw la, hali bi i bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ tuma dɔw la n’u ka darapo ye Pasifiki kɔgɔjida la.

Kun dɔw kosɔn, a tun bɛ iko Jungfrau tun b’a fɛ kosɛbɛ k’a bonya. K’a to Pequod ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ yɔrɔ dɔ la hali bi, a y’a lamini ka taa kurun dɔ la, ka kurun dɔ fili, a ka kapitɛni wulila ka taa an fan fɛ, a jɔlen tora kurun ɲɛfɛla la ni muɲubaliya ye kurun kɔkanna nɔ na.

“Mun b’a bolo yen?” Starbuck kulela, k’a bolo sin fɛn dɔ ma min minɛna Alemaɲi fɛ ni a bolo kɔrɔtalen ye. “A tɛ se ka kɛ!— lanpan-dumunikɛla dɔ!”

Stubb ko: “O tɛ, ayi, ayi, kafe-minɛn don, Mr. Starbuck; a bɛ bɔ ka na an ka kafe dilan an ye, Yarman don; i t’a ye ko o tintin belebeleba bɛ yen a kɛrɛfɛ wa?—o ye a ka ji tobilen ye. Oh! a bɛ ka ɲɛ, yala Yarman don.”.

Flask kulela ko: “Taa n’i ye, lanpan-dumunikɛla ni tulubɔlan don, tulu banna a la, a nana a-delili la.”

A bɛ se ka kɛ cogo o cogo, a bɛ se ka kɛ ko ɲininkali ye petoroli-kurun dɔ ka tulu juru ta balema-dugukolo kan, wa hali n’a bɛ se ka kɛ ko a bɛ ntalen kɔrɔ sɔsɔ cogo o cogo, n’o ye ka kɔɔri ta ka taa Newcastle, o bɛɛ n’a ta, tuma dɔw la, o ɲɔgɔnna bɛ kɛ tiɲɛ na ani nin ko in na, siga t’a la ko Kapiteni Derick De Deer ye lanpan-dumunikɛla dɔ ɲɛminɛ i n’a fɔ Flask y’a jira cogo min na.

A jigintɔ kurunba kan tuma min na, Akabu y’a gɛrɛ a la yɔrɔnin kelen, k’a sɔrɔ a ma a janto a bolo fewu; nka a ka lingo karilen na, a ma mɛn, Alemaɲi y’a jira ko a tɛ balema finman dɔn pewu; o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ ka baro wuli a ka lanpan-dumunikɛla n’a ka tulubɔlan kan, ni kuma dɔw ye minnu bɛ maga a la ko a ka kan ka wuli ka kɛ a ka hamakɛ ye su fɛ dibiba kɔnɔ — a ka Bremen tulu jiginɛ laban ma ban, wa jɛgɛ wulilen kelen ma minɛ fɔlɔ walasa ka dɛsɛ in ɲɛnabɔ k’a kuncɛ k’a jira ko tiɲɛ na, a ka kurun tun ye fɛn ye min bɛ wele ko Jɛgɛko la fɛɛrɛko siratigɛ la ko saniyalen (o kɔrɔ ye ko kurun lankolon), min ka kan ni Jungfrau walima Npogotiginin tɔgɔ ye kosɛbɛ.

A magow y’a lase, Derick taara; nka a tun ma a ka kurun fan sɔrɔ, tuma min na balemaw tun bɛ ɲini ka wuli waati kelen na ka bɔ kurun fila bɛɛ ka kurunba kunw na; ani Derick tun b’a fɛ kosɛbɛ ka tugu o kɔ, fo k’a sɔrɔ a ma jɔ dɔɔnin k’a ka tulubɔlan ni lanpan balolan bila kurun kɔnɔ, a y’a ka kurun lamini, ka leviyatan lanpanw balobagaw nɔfɛ.

Sisan, tulon in wulila ka se fiɲɛ ma, ale ni Alemaɲi kurun saba tɔ minnu tugura a kɔ joona, olu ye Pequod ka kurunba daminɛ kosɛbɛ. Balema 8 tun bɛ yen, o tun ye podo cɛmancɛ ye. U tun b’u ka farati dɔn, u tun bɛ taa ɲɔgɔn fɛ ni teliyaba ye ka ɲɛsin fiɲɛ ɲɛfɛ, k’u kɛrɛfɛlaw magaya kosɛbɛ i n’a fɔ sow sen caman minnu sirilen bɛ ɲɔgɔn na. U ye wulikanba dɔ to yen min ka bon, i n’a fɔ u bɛ to ka sɛbɛnfura belebeleba dɔ wuli kɔgɔji kan.

O kununni teliya in falen, ani kɔfɛla caman, misi kɔrɔba dɔ ye nɛgɛso min, a ka ɲɛtaa dɔɔni dɔɔni fɛ ni a bɛ tali kɛ a la, ani a ka jɛman jɛmanw fɛ minnu tun tɛ deli ka kɛ, minnu tun bɛ ka bonya a kan, a kɛra i n’a fɔ a tɔɔrɔla ni jaŋɔyin ye, walima bana wɛrɛ. Yala nin balema in tun ye podo ta ye ka kɔn o ɲɛ, a tun bɛ iko ɲininkali bɛ o la; sabu a tɛ laada ye ko o leviatan bonyalen suguw ka kɛ mɔgɔw fɛ fewu. O bɛɛ n’a ta, a nɔrɔla u kunun na, hali n’a y’a sɔrɔ tiɲɛ na, u kɔji y’a kɔtigɛ, bawo kolo finman walima funu min bɛ a dawolo jan na, o tun ye funu tigɛlen ye, i n’a fɔ funu min bɛ sɔrɔ ni jikuru jugu fila bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ. A ka dawolo tun ka surun, a tun bɛ kɛ dɔɔni dɔɔni, wa a tun bɛ baara kɛ kosɛbɛ; ka bɔ ni jikuru sugu dɔ ye min bɛ mɔgɔ sɔgɔsɔgɔ, k’a yɛrɛ kɛ fɛn tigɛlenw na, ka tugu dugukolo jukɔrɔla mankan kabakomaw kɔ a kɔnɔ, minnu tun bɛ i n’a fɔ bɔli bɛ a dancɛ suturalen tɔ la, ka kɛ sababu ye ka ji minnu bɛ a kɔfɛ, olu ka wuli.

“Jɔn ye paregoric dɔw sɔrɔ?” Stubb ko, "kɔnɔdimi b'a la, ne sirannen don. Matigi, miiri ka kɔnɔdimi kɛ acre tilancɛ la! Fiɲɛ juguw bɛ ka Noɛli dibi minɛ a kɔnɔ, cɛdenw. O ye fiɲɛ jugu fɔlɔ ye ne ye min dɔn ka bɔ kɔfɛ; nka a filɛ, yala a delila ka balema yaw kɛ ten fɔlɔ wa? a ka kan ka kɛ, a ye a ka tiller tunun."(Alimankan na)

I n’a fɔ Ɛndujamana min tun bɛ ka fɛnw ta ka jigin Hindostan kɔgɔjida la ni sow sirannenw ye, ka careens, buries, rolls, ani wallows deck a ka sira kan; o cogo kelen na, nin balema kɔrɔ in y’a ka fɛn kɔrɔba kɔrɔta, ka tila k’a yɔrɔ dɔw wuli a kɔkili gɛlɛnw kan, k’a ka kununni namara sababu bɔ kɛnɛ kan a kininbolo fan fɛ jirisunba kɔnɔ min tɛ a yɛrɛ ta ye. Ni a bɔnɛna o fin na kɛlɛ la, walima a wolola ni o fin tɛ, a fɔli tun ka gɛlɛn.

“I ka kɔnɔni kɛ dɔɔnin dɔrɔn, cɛkɔrɔba, n bɛna sɔgɔlilan di i ma o bolo joginlen in na,” Flask jugu kulela, k’a bolo sin balema-sira ma a kɛrɛfɛ.

Starbuck kulela ko: “Hakili k’a to a t’i sɔgɔ n’a ye.” “I ka sira di, n’o tɛ Alemaɲi bɛna a sɔrɔ.”

Ni laɲini kelen ye, kurun minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔsɔ, olu bɛɛ tun ɲɛsinnen bɛ nin jɛgɛ kelen in ma, bawo a ma kɛ dɔrɔn ko ale de tun ye balema belebeleba ye, wa o la, a tun ka surun u la kosɛbɛ, wa balema tɔw tun bɛ taa ni teliyaba ye, ka fara o kan, fo a tun bɛ ɲini ka ban ka tugu ɲɔgɔn kɔ o waati la. O waati in na, Pequod ka kurunba tun ye marifa ci Alemaɲi kurun saba fɛ minnu jigira laban na; nka kabini a ye daminɛba min kɛ, Derick ka kurun tun bɛ tugu-tugu-ɲɔgɔn kɔ hali bi, hali ni waati kelen-kelen bɛɛ tun bɛ gɛrɛ a ka jamana wɛrɛw ka ɲɔgɔndankɛlaw fɛ. U tùn bɛ siran fɛn kelen min ɲɛ, o ye, k'a sababu Kɛ a surunyalen ye a taamaʃyɛn na kaban, a bɛ Se k'a ka nɛgɛ Dòn sani u ka Se k'a Tɛmɛ pewu k'a Tɛmɛ. Derick ta fan fɛ, a tun bɛ iko a dalen b’a la kosɛbɛ ko o bɛna kɛ, wa tuma dɔw la, a tun bɛ a ka lanpan-dumunikɛla lamaga kurun tɔw la ni tulonko ye.

“Usi min tɛ nɛɛma kɛ ani min tɛ waleɲumandɔn jira!” Starbuck ye kulekan bɔ; “a bɛ n tulo geren ani ka n ja gɛlɛya ni faantan-kɛsu ye, n ye min fa a ye a ma miniti duuru bɔ!”—o kɔfɛ, a ka kumakan kɔrɔba kɔnɔ—“Aw ka sira di, wuluw! Dog to it!”

“Ne b’a fɔ aw ye a ye min ye, cɛw”—Stubb kulela a ka kurun kɔnɔ—“a bɛ ne ka diinɛ sɔsɔ ka dimi; nka ne b’a fɛ ka o Yarman jugu dun—Pull—aw tɛna o kɛ wa? Yala aw bɛna a to o jatigɛwalekɛla ka aw bugɔ wa? Yala aw bɛ brandi kanu wa? A hogshead of brandy, then, to the best man. Come, why don’t some of yerst a blood-vessel burst? Jɔn ta min bɛ ka kurunba dɔ fili kɔgɔji kan—an tɛ wuli ni santimɛtɛrɛ kelen ye—an hakili sigilen don, bin bɛ ka falen kurun jukɔrɔ—wa Matigi fɛ, kurunba bɛ ka falen Nin tɛna kɛ, cɛdenw, a surunyalen ni a janya ye, cɛw, yala aw bɛna tasuma daji bɔ wa?

“O!, a bɛ suw lajɛ!” Flask kulela, ka dɔn kɛ san fɛ ni duguma—“O ye hump ye dɛ—Oh, do pile on the beef—lays like a log! Oh! my lads, do spring—slap-jacks and quahogs for supper, aw b’a dɔn, n cɛdenw—ka clams ni muffins tobi—oh, do , do , spring,—he’s a hundred barreller—don’t lose him now—don’t lose him now—don’t oh, don’t! —a’ y’o lajɛ Yarman—E, yala aw tɛna sama ka taa aw ka duff la, n’o ye sog ye, yala aw tɛ sperm kanu!—banki kuuru!—Oh, do , do , do!

Nin waati in na, Derick tun bɛ wale la k’a ka lanpan-dumunikɛla bila kurunw na minnu bɛ taa ɲɛ, ani fana a ka tulubɔlan; laala ni hakilina fila ye k’a ɲɔgɔndankɛlaw ka sira bila kɔfɛ, wa o waati kelen na, k’a yɛrɛ ta teliya sɔrɔko siratigɛ la ni kɔfɛ-boli ye waati dɔɔnin kɔnɔ.

“Olandekaw ka wulukɛla min tɛ jogoɲumanya kɛ!” Stubb kulela. "A' ye sama sisan, cɛw, i n'a fɔ jinɛw kunsigi bilenmanw doni ba bi duuru. Aw bɛ mun fɔ, Tashtego; yala aw ye cɛ ye min bɛna aw kɔkolo tigɛ ka kɛ fila ni mugan ye Gayhead kɔrɔ bonya kama wa? Aw bɛ mun fɔ wa?

“Ne ko, sama i n’a fɔ ala-dama,”—Endiyɛn kulela.

Alemaɲi ka tulo gerenw fɛ ni farinya ye, nka u bilala cogo kelen na, Pequod ka kurun saba y’a daminɛ sisan ka surunya ɲɔgɔn na; ani, o cogo la, a gɛrɛla a la waati dɔɔnin kɔnɔ. O kuntigi ka o cogo ɲuman na, a lafiyalen, a ka sotigiya la, n’a bɛ gɛrɛ a ka fɛn minɛta la, furuɲɔgɔn saba tun bɛ jɔ ni yɛrɛbonya ye, tuma dɔw la, u tun bɛ kurunba kɔfɛla dɛmɛ ni kulekan ye min bɛ mɔgɔ nisɔndiya ko: “A bɛ sɛgɛn yen, sisan! I teliya ka fiɲɛ finman-ciyɛn! Jigin ni Yarman ye! Sail over him!”

Nka o cogo la, a latigɛra daminɛ fɔlɔ la ni Derick tun ye, ko hali n’u ka jagɛlɛya bɛɛ tun bɛ yen, a tun bɛna a jira ko a ye se sɔrɔ nin boli in na, ni kiritigɛ tilennen tun ma jigin a kan kansɛri dɔ kɔnɔ min y’a ka kurun cɛmancɛla kurunbatigi ka nɛgɛ minɛ. K’a sɔrɔ nin tulubɔlan nɔgɔlen in tun b’a ɲini k’a ka ciyɛn finman hɔrɔnya, wa k’a sɔrɔ, o nɔfɛ, Derick ka kurun tun bɛ ɲini ka wuli, wa a tun bɛ ka sanpɛrɛn ka taa a ka cɛw la ni dimiba ye;—o kɛra waati ɲuman ye Starbuck, Stubb ani Flask bolo. Ni kulekan ye, u ye daminɛ fagalen dɔ ta ka taa ɲɛfɛ, ka slantingly range up on the German’s quarter. Yɔrɔnin kelen wɛrɛ, ani kurun naani bɛɛ tun bɛ balema in teliya la, k’a sɔrɔ u tun bɛ ka wuli ka bɔ u la, fan fila bɛɛ la, a ye funu fomu min kɛ.

O tun ye yelifɛn ye min tun ka bon kosɛbɛ, min tun bɛ mɔgɔ hinɛ kosɛbɛ, ani min tun bɛ mɔgɔ bila ka dimi. Balema tun bɛ taa a kunkolo bɔ sisan, ka a ka dawolo ci a ɲɛfɛ jet tɔɔrɔlen dɔ kɔnɔ min bɛ to ka kɛ; k'a sɔrɔ a ka fin faantan kelen y'a kɛrɛ gosi siran tɔɔrɔ fɛ. Sisan ka se nin bolo in ma, sisan ka se o ma, a ye wuluwulu kɛ a ka wuluwulu wulilen na, wa hali bi a ye bɔgɔbɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ kari, a bɛ jigin kɔgɔji la ni sɔgɔsɔgɔ ye, walima a bɛ girin kɛrɛ fɛ ka taa sankolo fan fɛ a ka wuluwulu kelen min bɛ gosi. O cogo la, yala ne ye kɔnɔ dɔ ye min kaman tigɛlen don, a bɛ siran ka sɛrɛkili karilenw kɛ fiɲɛ na, k’a cɛsiri fu ka boli jagokɛlaw ka wuluw ɲɛ. Nka kɔnɔ in bɛ ni kan ye, a bɛna a ka siran jira ni kulekan ye; nka nin kɔgɔji waraba boboba in siran, o sirilen bɛ a la ka a lafili; kan tun t’a la, fo o ninakili sɔgɔlen min bɛ a ka sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ fɛ, wa o y’a to a yecogo kɛra makari ye min tɛ se ka fɔ; k’a sɔrɔ hali bi, a ka fɛnba kabakoma na, a ka portcullis jaw, ani a ka sebaaya bɛɛ la, fɛn caman tun bɛ yen min bɛ se ka cɛ barikamaba lasiran min ye hinɛ ten.

K’a ye sisan ko nka waati damadɔ dɔrɔn ka tɛmɛn o kan, o bɛna nafa di Pequod ka kurunw ma, wa sanni a ka kɛ sababu ye ka a ka tulon tiɲɛ o cogo la, Derick y’a sugandi ka farati lase fɛn ma min tun ka kan ka kɛ a bolo, n’a sɔrɔ o tun bɛ iko nɛgɛso janba min tɛ deli ka kɛ, sanni sababu laban ka boli fo abada.

Nka a ka harpooneer jɔlen dɔrɔn o sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ kama, jara saba bɛɛ — Queequeg, Tashtego, Daggoo — u ye u senw wuli u yɛrɛ la, ka jɔ ɲɔgɔn kan, u y’u ka barbw jira waati kelen na ani ka boli ka tɛmɛ Alemaɲi harpooneer kunkolo kan, u ka Nantucket nɛgɛ saba donna balema kɔnɔ. Fomu ni tasuma finmanw ka fiyentɔw! Kurun saba ninnu, balema in kunkolo boli dimi fɔlɔ la, u ye Alemaɲi ta gosi kɛrɛfɛ ni fanga ye, fo Derick n’a ka harpooneer hakili ɲagaminen fila bɛɛ bɔnna ka bɔ, ka tɛmɛn kurunba saba fɛ minnu bɛ pan.

“Aw kana siran, ne ka tulu-kɛsuw,” Stubb kulela, k’a ɲɛw tɛmɛn u kan k’a to marifa cilen na; “aw bɛna ta sisan—a bɛ ɲɛ—ne ye jɛgɛ dɔw ye kɔfɛ—Bernard senuma wuluw, i b’a dɔn—u bɛ taamakɛla tɔɔrɔlenw lafiya. Hurrah! nin ye kurun bolicogo ye sisan. Keel kelen-kelen bɛɛ ye tile yeelen ye! Hurrah!—An bɛ taa i n’a fɔ tintin tintin saba bɛ kuga dibi dɔ kɔ la!O bɛ ne bila hakili la ka teliya ka taa waraba dɔ la min bɛ a tilbury kɛnɛba dɔ kan—a bɛ wotoro-spokew kɛ ka pan, cɛdenw, n’aw bɛ siri a la o cogo la, n’aw ye kulu dɔ gosi, nin ye mɔgɔ dɔ dusukunnata ye min bɛ taa Davy Jones fɛ—o bɛɛ ye kɔrɔtɔ ye ka jigin awiyɔn kɔnɔ min bɛ wuli tuma bɛɛ!

Nka o jinɛ in ka boli kɛra waati kunkurunnin ye. A ye sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ barikama di, a ye mankan bɔ ni ɲagami ye. Ni grating rush ye, layini saba tun bɛ pan ka loggerheads lamini ni fanga ye fo ka gouge deep grooves in u; k’a sɔrɔ harponkɛlaw tun sirannen don kosɛbɛ fo o mankan teliya in tun bɛna layiniw sɛgɛn sɔɔni, fo n’u y’u ka seko seko bɛɛ kɛ, u ye sigarɛti wulicogo minɛ siɲɛ caman ni juru ye walasa k’u minɛ fo laban na — k’a sababu kɛ kurunw ka sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ ye min tun bɛ ni plɔmu ye, juru saba tun bɛ jigin yɔrɔ min na ka ɲɛsin bulu ma — kurunw ka marifakisɛw tun bɛ ɲini ka kɛ hali ni ji ye, k’a sɔrɔ kurun saba kɔfɛla saba tun bɛ kɔrɔta sanfɛ fiɲɛ na. Wa a ma mɛn, balema mankan dabila, waati dɔ kɔnɔ u tora o cogo la, u sirannen ka layini caman musaka, hali ni jɔyɔrɔ tun bɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ dɔɔnin. Nka hali ni kurunw jigira ka tunun o cogo la, o bɛɛ n’a ta, nin “minɛ” in de don, i n’a fɔ a bɛ fɔ cogo min na; nin sirili in na a sogo ɲɛnamaw ka bɔgɔdaga nɛrɛmugumaw fɛ ka bɔ kɔ la; nin de bɛ to ka Leviatan tɔɔrɔ walasa a ka wuli joona ka taa a juguw ka nɛgɛbere nɔgɔlen kunbɛn. O bɛɛ n’a ta, an kana kuma o fɛn in farati kan, siga ka kan ka kɛ ni nin sira in de ka fisa tuma bɛɛ; sabu a bɛnnen don k’a miiri ko ni balema min gosira, o tora ji jukɔrɔ ka mɛn, a bɛ sɛgɛn ka taa a fɛ. Parce que (bari) k’a sababu Kɛ a ka yɔrɔba ye — balema sperme (balema sperm) min falen don dafalen na, o la, fɛn min ka dɔgɔ ni mɛtɛrɛ kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen 2000 ye — ji degun ka bon kosɛbɛ. An bɛɛ b’a dɔn ko an yɛrɛ bɛ jɔ fiɲɛ girinya kabakoma min kɔrɔ; hali yan, dugukolo sanfɛ, fiɲɛ kɔnɔ; o la sa, balema doni ka bon cogo min na, a bɛ kɔgɔji jɔlan dɔ ta a kɔ kan, min janya ye mɛtɛrɛ kɛmɛ fila ye! Nin ɲɛnajɛ in bi ja minnu bɛ yen, olu kana an fili; sabu hali ni o balemakɛla jagɛlɛn in ye danfɛn min sɔrɔ fɔlɔ, o jiralen don cogo jɛlen na min cogoya bɛ i n’a fɔ grifin, ani hali ni kɛlɛ jiralen bɛ dugukolo kan ani mɔgɔ senuma so kan, o bɛɛ n’a ta, n’i y’o waatiw ka dɔnbaliyaba jateminɛ, waati min na balema suguya lakika tun tɛ dɔn dɔnkotigiw fɛ ani k’a jateminɛ ko i n’a fɔ a kɛra cogo min na Perseus ta la, n’a sɔrɔla George senuma ka balema tun bɛ se ka wuli ka bɔ kɔgɔji la kɔgɔjida la; ani k’a jateminɛ ko bagan min tun bɛ St George kan, n’a sɔrɔla o tun ye sigilan belebele dɔrɔn de ye, walima kɔgɔji so; n’an y’o bɛɛ to an hakili la, a tɛna kɛ i n’a fɔ a tɛ bɛn pewu ni nsiirin senuma ni ɲɛnajɛ in sɛbɛn kɔrɔw ye, ka nin sa min bɛ wele ko sa in minɛ, o tɛ mɔgɔ wɛrɛ ye ni Leviatanba yɛrɛ tɛ. Tiɲɛ na, n’a bilala tiɲɛ gɛlɛn ni min bɛ mɔgɔ sɔgɔ, o maana bɛɛ bɛna kɛ i n’a fɔ o Filisitikaw ka jɛgɛ, sogo ani kɔnɔ boli, Dagon tɔgɔ la; min tun sigilen bɛ Israɛl ka kurunba ɲɛfɛ, a ka so kunkolo n'a bolokɔfɛfɛn fila bɛɛ binna a kan, a kuru walima a jɛgɛ yɔrɔ dɔrɔn tora. O cogo la, o la, an yɛrɛ ka timbre noble dɔ, hali balemakɛla, o ye Angletɛri ka kɔlɔsilikɛla ye; ani jo ɲumanw fɛ, anw Nantucket ka harpooneers ka kan ka tɔgɔ sɛbɛn St George ka sigiyɔrɔba la min ka bon kosɛbɛ. Wa o de kama, o tɔnba bonyalen in ka kɛlɛcɛw kana (n b’a fɛ k’a fɔ ko u si ma deli ka kɛ ni balema ye i n’a fɔ u ka patɔrɔnba), u kana Nantucketer ɲɛ da abada ni danbe ye, bawo hali an ka finiw ni wuluwuluw ni pantalonw kɔnɔ minnu dilalen don ni gofɛrɛnaman ye, an ka kan kosɛbɛ ka tɛmɛn u kan.

Ni ne bɛna Hɛrɛki don an cɛma walima ni ne tɛna don an cɛma, o ko la, ne tora sigasiga la kabini tuma jan: bawo hali ni Gɛrɛkiw ka nsiirinw kɔnɔ, o Crockett ni Kit Carson kɔrɔba in — o kɛwale ɲumanw nisɔndiyalen kɛbaga barikama, balema dɔ ye a munumunu ka a fili san fɛ hali bi, n’o b’a kɛ balematigi ye kosɛbɛ, o bɛ se ka kɛ sɔsɔli ye. A ma Kɛ yɔrɔ si la ko a y’a ka jɛgɛ Kɛ ni harpoon ye tiɲɛ na, fo ni, tiɲɛ na, a Bɔra kɔnɔna na. O bɛɛ n’a ta, a bɛ se ka jate balemakɛla sugu dɔ ye min tɛ a yɛrɛ sago kɛ; a kɛra cogo o cogo, balema y'a minɛ, n'a ma balema minɛ. N b'a Fɔ an ka jamu dɔ ye.

Nka, faamaw fɛ minnu ka fisa ni sɔsɔli ye, nin Gɛrɛkikan maana in min bɛ Hɛrɛki ni balema ko la, a jatera ko a bɔra Heburukan maana la min kɔrɔlen don hali bi, n’o ye Jonasi ni balema ye; ani a kɔfɛta; tiɲɛ na, u bɛ ɲɔgɔn bɔ kosɛbɛ. Ni ne bɛ demi-ala de fɔ o tuma na, mun na kira tɛ?

Jatigɛwalekɛlaw, mɔgɔ senumaw, ala tilancɛw ani kiraw dɔrɔn fana tɛ an ka jɛkulu sɛbɛnfura bɛɛ lajɛlen kɛ. An ka matigiba tɔgɔ ma fɔ hali bi; sabu i n'a fɔ waati kɔrɔ masakɛw, an bɛ an ka balimaya kunkolo ji sɔrɔ foyi la ni alabaw yɛrɛ ye. O kɔrɔnfɛla maana kabakoma in ka kan ka labɛn sisan ka bɔ Shaster fɛ, min bɛ siran Vishnoo di an ma, min ye mɔgɔ saba dɔ ye min bɛ Ɛnduw ka alaɲɛsiran na; bɛ nin alaɲɛsiran Vishnoo in di an ma a yɛrɛ ye an Matigi ye;—Vishnoo, min, a ka dugukolo kan farikolo tan fɔlɔ fɛ, a ye balema bɔ ɲɔgɔn na fo abada. Tuma min na Brahma, walima Ala ka Ala, Shaster ko, a y’a latigɛ ka diɲɛ da kokura a waati ni waati tiɲɛni dɔ kɔfɛ, a ye Vishnoo bange, ka baara ɲɛminɛ nka Vedas, walima gundo gafew, minnu kalanni tun bɛna kɛ i n’a fɔ a nafa ka bon kosɛbɛ Vishnoo bolo sanni a ka danni daminɛ, wa o de kama, n’a sɔrɔla fɛn dɔ tun bɛ minnu kɔnɔ min bɛ i n’a fɔ hakilina nafamaw ka ɲɛsin fɛn dilannikɛla kamalenninw ma, o Veda ninnu tun dalen bɛ ji jukɔrɔ o la, Vishnoo kɛra farikolo ye balema dɔ kɔnɔ, ka mankan bɔ a kɔnɔ fo ka se a jugumanba ma, ka gafe senumaw kisi. O tuma na fɛ, yala nin Vishnoo tun tɛ balemakɛla ye wa? hali i ko mɔgɔ min bɛ so boli, o bɛ wele cogo min na ko sotigi ?

Perseus, George senuma, Hercules, Jonas ani Vishnoo! membre-roll dɔ bɛ yen i ye ! Kulu jumɛn bɛ se ka taa o cogo la ni balemakɛla ta tɛ?





SABABU 83. Jonasi min jatera tariku kɔnɔ.

Jonasi ni balema tariku maana fɔra sapitiri tɛmɛnen kɔnɔ. Sisan, Nantucketer dɔw b’a fɛ ka da nin tariku maana in na min bɛ Jonasi ni balema ko la. Nka o kɔfɛ, Gɛrɛkiw ni Romɛkaw dɔw tun bɛ yen minnu tun bɛ sigasiga, olu minnu bɔra u ka waati kafiri orthodoxew la, u tun bɛ sigasiga cogo kelen na Hɛrɛki ni balema, ani Arion ni delfin ka maana na o bɛɛ n’a ta, u ka sigasiga o laadalakow la, o ma o laadalakow kɛ tiɲɛw ye minnu ka dɔgɔ, o bɛɛ kosɔn.

Sag-Harbor balemakɛla kɔrɔ dɔ ka kunba min ye ɲininkali kɛ Heburukan maana na, o tun ye nin ye:—O Bibulu kɔrɔ cɛɲiw dɔ tun b’a bolo, min tun masirilen don ni tabali ye minnu tun bɛ ɲininkali kɛ, minnu tɛ dɔnniya sɔrɔ o dɔ tun bɛ Jonasi ka balema jira ni bɔgɔdaga fila ye a kun na — danfara min bɛ tiɲɛ dɔrɔn ka ɲɛsin Leviatan suguya dɔ ma (Balema kinin fɛ, ani o sigicogo suguyaw), mɔnikɛlaw bɛ nin kuma fɔ min ko la ko: “Peni rollo tun bɛna a dawolo” a munumugu ka dɔgɔ kosɛbɛ ten. Nka, nin ko in na, Episkɔpu Jebb ka jaabi kɔrɔlen labɛnnen don. Episkɔpu b’a jira ko a man kan, k’an ka Jonasi jate i n’a fɔ a su donna balema kɔnɔbara la, nka i n’a fɔ a su donna a da yɔrɔ dɔ la waati dɔɔnin kɔnɔ. Wa nin bɛ iko a bɛnnen don kosɛbɛ Episkɔpu ɲuman fɛ. Tiɲɛ na, Balema kinin fɛ da tun bɛna whist-table fila-fila ladon, ka tulonkɛlaw bɛɛ sigi cogo la min bɛ mɔgɔ lafiya. N’a sɔrɔ fana, Zonasi tun bɛ se k’a yɛrɛ dogo ɲin dingɛ dɔ kɔnɔ; nka, ni i ye miiri filanan kɛ, balema kinin fɛ, ɲinw tɛ a la.

Kun wɛrɛ min Sag-Harbor (a taara o tɔgɔ la) ye min Laɲini a ka dannaya dɛsɛ la kira ka ko in na, o ye fɛn ye min ma jɛya ka ɲɛsin a farikolo kasolen ma ani balema kɔnɔbara jiw ma. Nka o sɔsɔli in fana bɛ bin duguma, bawo Alemaɲi ɲɛfɔlikɛla dɔ b’a miiri ko n’a sɔrɔla Jonas y’a dogo balema salen dɔ su la min bɛ panpan — hali k’a sɔrɔ Faransi sɔrɔdasi minnu tun bɛ Irisi kanpaɲi na, olu y’u ka so salenw tigɛli kɛ fanibuguw ye, ka wuluwulu don u kɔnɔ. O tɛmɛnen kɔ, a fɔra farafinna ɲɛfɔlikɛla wɛrɛw fɛ, ko tuma min na Jonasi filila kɔgɔjida la ka bɔ Jope kurun kɔnɔ, a y’a ka boli kɛ o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ ka taa kurun wɛrɛ la min bɛ a kɛrɛfɛ, kurun dɔ kɔnɔ, balema bɛ min bolo ka kɛ ja-kunkolo ye ani, n b’a fara a kan, n’a sɔrɔla a bɛ wele ko “Baleine”, i n’a fɔ bololabaara dɔw bɛ batise cogo min na bi ko “Requin”, “Gull”, “Eagle.” Ɲɛfɔlikɛla ŋanaw fana ma ɲini minnu y’a fɔ ko balema min kofɔlen bɛ Jonasi ka kitabu kɔnɔ, o kɔrɔ tun ye ɲɛnamaya kisibaga dɔrɔn de ye—fiɲɛ bɔrɔ min bɛ fiyɛ—kira min tun bɛ farati la, o taara min na, wa o cogo la, a kisira ka bɔ halakili ji la. O la sa, a bɛ iko Sag-Harbor faantan juguyara a fan bɛɛ la. Nka kun wɛrɛ tun b’a fɛ a ka dannaya dɛsɛ la. Nin tun ye nin ye, ni ne hakili bɛ tiɲɛ na: balema ye Jonasi munumunu Mediterane Kɔgɔji la, tile saba tɛmɛnen kɔfɛ, a fɔɔnɔ yɔrɔ dɔ la, tile saba taama kɔnɔ ka bɔ Ninivɛ dugu la, dugu min bɛ Tigris baji kan, o ka ca ni tile saba taama ye kosɛbɛ ka tɛmɛ Mediterane kɔgɔjida yɔrɔ min ka surun a la. O bɛ kɛ cogo di?

Nka, yala cogo wɛrɛ tun tɛ yen balema ka se ka kira jigin o yɔrɔ surun na Ninivi wa? Awɔ. A tun bɛ se k’a ta ka yaala-yaala Jigiya ɲuman Kapi sira fɛ. Nka an kana kuma tɛmɛsira kan min bɛ tɛmɛ Mediterane kɔgɔji janya bɛɛ fɛ, ani tɛmɛsira wɛrɛ kan ka wuli Pɛrɛsi kɔgɔjida la ani Kɔgɔji Bilen na, o miiri sugu bɛna kɛ ni Afiriki bɛɛ laminini ye pewu tile saba kɔnɔ, an kana kuma Tigris ji kan, Ninivi yɔrɔ kɛrɛfɛ, min man dun kojugu balema si tɛ se ka jigin ka don a kɔnɔ tile fɔlɔ bɛna o kunkoloba sɔrɔli bonya bɔ Bartolome Diaz bolo, n’o ye a sɔrɔbaga tɔgɔba ye, wa o cogo la, ka bi tariku kɛ nkalontigɛla ye.

Nka nin Sag-Harbor kɔrɔ ka nalomanya sɔsɔli ninnu bɛɛ y’a ka hakilintan yɛrɛbonya jira dɔrɔn — fɛn min tun bɛ jalaki a la hali bi, k’a ye ko kalan dɔɔnin dɔrɔn de tun b’a bolo fo a ye min ta tile ni kɔgɔji la. Ne b’a fɔ ko a b’a ka kuncɛbaya hakilintan, alaɲɛsiranbali, ani a ka murutili haramulen, jinɛya dɔrɔn de jira diinɛ ɲɛmɔgɔ bonyalenw ma. Sabu Pɔrtigal Katoliki sarakalasebaa dɔ fɛ, o hakilina yɛrɛ min tun ye ko Jonasi bɛna taa Ninivɛ Jigiya ɲuman Kapi fɛ, o tun bɛ taa ɲɛ i n’a fɔ kabako bɛɛ lajɛlen bonya taamasyɛn. Wa a kɛra ten. O tɛmɛnen kɔ, fo ka na se bi ma, Turki minnu ye yeelen sɔrɔ kosɛbɛ, olu dalen bɛ Jonasi ka tariku maana na ni timinandiya ye. Wa a bɛ san kɛmɛ saba ɲɔgɔn bɔ, Angilɛ taamakɛla dɔ min bɛ Harris ka taama kɔrɔw kɔnɔ, o bɛ kuma Turki misiri dɔ kan min jɔra ka bonya da Jonasi kan, Misiri tun ye kabako lanpan ye min tun bɛ jeni k’a sɔrɔ tulu foyi ma sɔrɔ.





SABABU 84. Pitchpoling (fɔlikan) kɛli.

Walasa u ka boli nɔgɔya la ani teliya la, wotoroninw ka aksidanw bɛ mun; ani o kun kelen de kama, balemakɛla dɔw bɛ baara kɛ o cogo kelen na u ka kurun kan; u bɛ tulu kɛ duguma. Siga t’a la fana ko ikomi o kɛcogo sugu tɛ se ka kojugu si kɛ, a bɛ se ka kɛ ko nafa foyi tɛ a la; k' a jateminɛ ko tulu ni ji ye juguya ye ; ko tulu ye fɛn ye min bɛ sɛgɛn , wa ko fɛn min bɛ ye , o ye ka kurun sɛgɛn ni jagɛlɛya ye . Queequeg tun dalen bɛ kosɛbɛ a ka kurun munni na, wa don dɔ sɔgɔma, Alemaɲi kurunba Jungfrau tununnen kɔfɛ, a ma mɛn, a ye dimi caman kɛ o baara la ka tɛmɛ laadala dimi kan; ka wuluwulu a duguma jukɔrɔ, a tun bɛ dulon yɔrɔ min na kɛrɛ fɛ, ka a magaya o kɔnɔnafili la i n’a fɔ a b’a ɲini ni timinandiya ye ka kunsigi sɛnɛfɛn dɔ asiransi ka bɔ bololabaarakɛla ka wuluwulu kunsigi la. A tun bɛ iko a tun bɛ baara kɛ ka kanminɛli kɛ presentiment kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen dɔw la. A ma to fana ko min ma kɛ ni dalilu ye o ko kɛlen fɛ.

Midi waati fan fɛ, balemaw tun bɛ lamɔ; nka kurun jiginna u fɛ dɔrɔn, u y’u kɔ tigɛli daminɛ ka boli ni teliya ye; wuluwulu min ma labɛn, i n’a fɔ Cleopatra ka kurunba minnu bɔra Actium.

O bɛɛ n’a ta, kurunw tugura o kɔ, wa Stubb ta de tun bɛ ɲɛfɛ. Tashtego ye cɛsiriba kɛ, a laban na, a sera ka nɛgɛ kelen turu; nka balema min gosira, k’a sɔrɔ mankan ma bɔ fewu, o tora a ka panni tilennen na hali bi, ni kurun bolicogo dɔ farala a kan. O sɛgɛn sugu minnu bɛ kɛ nɛgɛ turulen kan, k’a sɔrɔ u ma dabila, olu ka kan ka a bɔ joona walima kɔfɛ, o tɛ se ka bali. A kɛra wajibi ye ka balema pantalen in lance, walima ka wasa ka bɔnɛ a la. Nka ka kurun sama ka taa a kɛrɛfɛ, o tun tɛ se ka kɛ, a ye jigin joona kosɛbɛ ani a dimina kosɛbɛ. O tuma na fɛ, mun tora?

Kabako minɛnw ni seko ni dɔnko bɛɛ la, bololabaarakɛminɛnw ni fɛn nɔgɔlen jatebaliya, balemakɛla kɔrɔ bɛ wajibiya ka minnu kɛ tuma caman na, u si tɛ tɛmɛ o manɛ ɲuman kan ni nɛgɛbere ye min bɛ wele ko pitchpoling. Npan fitinin, walima npan belebeleba, a ka degeliw bɛɛ la, a tɛ waso o ɲɔgɔn na. A nafa ka bon dɔrɔn ni balema bolila kɔrɔlen dɔ ye; a tiɲɛba n’a cogoya ye yɔrɔ kabakoma ye, nɛgɛ jan bɛ boli ka taa yɔrɔ min na tigitigi ka bɔ kurunba dɔ la min bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ ni fanga ye, min bɛ girin, ɲɛtaa jugumanba kɔnɔ. Nɛgɛ ni jiri fana sen bɛ o la, tama bɛɛ janya bɛ se mɛtɛrɛ tan walima tan ni fila ɲɔgɔn ma; bere ka dɔgɔ kosɛbɛ ka tɛmɛ harpoon ta kan, ani fana fɛn dɔ ye min ka nɔgɔn, n’o ye pinin ye. A bɛ labɛn ni juru fitinin ye min bɛ wele ko warp, a janya ka bon kosɛbɛ, a bɛ se ka sama ka segin bolo la min fɛ, a sɔgɔlen kɔfɛ.

Nka sanni an ka Taa ‘ɲɛ, a nafa ka bon k’a Fɔ yan, ko hali n’a bɛ Se ka Kɛ ko nɛgɛso bɛ Se ka Blà cogo kelen na ni nɛgɛbere ye, o bɛɛ n’a ta, a man teli ka Kɛ; ani n’a kɛra, hali bi a tɛ ɲɛ sɔrɔ kosɛbɛ, k’a sababu kɛ harpoon girinya ka bon ani a janya ka dɔgɔn ni i y’a suma ni nɛgɛbere ye, tiɲɛ na, o bɛ kɛ dɛsɛba ye. O la, i n’a fɔ fɛn bɛɛ lajɛlen, i ka kan ka teliya ka se balema ma fɔlɔ, sanni bɔgɔdaga si ka na baara la.

Aw ye Stubb lajɛ sisan; cɛ min ka bɔ a ka nisɔndiya la, a ka nɛnɛ laɲininen na ani a ka bɛnkan na kɔrɔtɔko jugubaw la, o tun bɛ se kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la ka tɛmɛn pitchpoling kan. A filɛ a la; a jɔlen bɛ kurunba wulilen ɲɛfɛ; a sirilen ni wuluwulu ye, balema min bɛ sama, o bɛ se mɛtɛrɛ binaani ɲɛfɛ. Stubb bɛ nɛgɛbere jan minɛ nɔgɔya la, k’a ɲɛw wuli siɲɛ fila walima siɲɛ saba a janya la walasa k’a dɔn n’a tilennen don tigitigi, a bɛ wuluwulu ka bobine lajɛ ni sɔgɔsɔgɔ ye a bolo kelen na, walasa k’a kun hɔrɔnyalen siri a bolo la, k’a tɔ to a ma bali. O kɔ fɛ, a bɛ nɛgɛso minɛ ka fa ka kɔn a cɛsirilan cɛmancɛ ɲɛ, a b’a nivoya balema na; n'a y'a datugu n'o ye, a bɛ a bolo butiki dakun digidigi ka taa a fɛ, o cogo la, a bɛ a kun kɔrɔta fo marifa ka jɔ ka bɛn a bolokɔnincinin ma, sen tan ni duuru fiɲɛ na. A b’i hakili to a la dɔɔnin i n’a fɔ jugler, a bɛ bere jan balansi a dawolo kan. Waati nata la ni wulikan teliyalen ye, tɔgɔ tɛ min na, jirisunba kɔrɔtalenba dɔ kɔnɔ, nɛgɛ jɛman bɛ fomu yɔrɔ janya, ka yɛrɛyɛrɛ balema ɲɛnamaya yɔrɔ la. Sanni a ka ji kɛnɛ, a bɛ joli bilenman bɔ sisan.

“O ye spigot gɛn ka bɔ a kɔnɔ!” Stubb kulela. "'Tis July's immortal Fourth; all fountains must run wine today! Would now, it were old Orleans whisky, or old Ohio, or unspeakable old Monongahela! O kɔfɛ, Tashtego, cɛnin, ne tun bɛna a to aw ka kanakin dɔ minɛ ka taa jet la, ani an tun bɛna min a lamini na! Ɔwɔ, tiɲɛ na, dusukunw ɲɛnama, an tun bɛna sugandili kɛ a ka punch jɛnsɛnni na spout-hole yen, ka bɔ o punch-bowl ɲɛnama in na, ka fɛn ɲɛnamaw quaff.”

Siɲɛ caman na o tulonko kuma suguw la, nɛgɛso setigi bɛ segin a kan, tama bɛ segin a makɛ ma i n’a fɔ wuluwulu min sirilen bɛ ni siri setigi ye. Balema tɔɔrɔlen bɛ taa a ka wuluwulu kɔnɔ; tow-line bɛ lafiya, ani pitchpoler bɛ bin kɔfɛ, k’a bolow da, ka bobotɔ ka waraba in ka saya lajɛ.





SABABU 85. Jiboli-yɔrɔ.

Ko san ba wɔɔrɔ kɔnɔ — wa mɔgɔ si t’a dɔn san miliyɔn joli ka kɔn o ɲɛ — balema belebelebaw tun ka kan ka wuli kɔgɔji fan bɛɛ la, ka nakɔ juguw seri ani k’u ɲagami, i n’a fɔ a bɛ kɛ cogo min na daga caman na minnu bɛ seri walima minnu bɛ ɲagami ani ko san kɛmɛ damadɔ tɛmɛnen kɔfɛ, kungokɔnɔfɛnw ɲininikɛla ba caman tun ka kan ka gɛrɛ balema jibolisira kɛrɛfɛ, ka nin jikuruw ni jibɔw kɔlɔsi — ko nin bɛɛ ka kan ka kɛ, wa o bɛɛ n’a ta, fo ka se nin miniti dugawu in ma (miniti tan ni duuru ni tila ka tɛmɛ nin Desanburukalo tile tan ni wɔɔrɔnan in na, san 1851 Krisita tile kɔfɛ), nɛgɛ kanɲɛ kelen PM waati, a ka kan ka to gɛlɛya ye hali bi, n’a y’a sɔrɔ o jirisunw ye, a bɛɛ kɔfɛ, tiɲɛ na ji, walima foyi tɛ ni lafiɲɛbɔ tɛ—siga t’a la ko nin ye ko ye min ka kan ka kɔlɔsi.
