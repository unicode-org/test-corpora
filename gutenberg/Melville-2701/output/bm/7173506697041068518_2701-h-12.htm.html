J. Ross Browne ka baleine sperme ja dɔw bɛ tiɲɛ kosɛbɛ u cogoya la; nka u jalen don cogo jugu la. Nka, o tɛ a ka hakɛ ye.

Ja fɔlɔ la, Balema Sɛrɛma bonyalen dɔ jiralen bɛ ni fanga bonya bɛɛ ye, a wulila dɔrɔn kurun jukɔrɔ ka bɔ kɔgɔji jugumanba la, ka tasumamuguw ka pankurunw tiɲɛnen kabakoma dɔ ta sanfɛ fiɲɛ na a kɔ kan. Layini-tub tilancɛ lankolon bɛ pan kɔgɔji finman kan; jiribolo minnu bɛ bɔn, olu bɛ bob obliquement a kɔnɔ; najibɔlaw kunkolow jɛnsɛnnen don balema in kan siran jiracogo danfaralenw na; k’a sɔrɔ sanfiɲɛba nɛrɛma yɔrɔjan na, kurun bɛ ka jigin o yɔrɔ la. Fɔlɔ: Walasa ka layini wɛrɛ sirili nɔgɔya a la ka bɔ kurun kɛrɛfɛ, n’a sɔrɔla balema min gosira, o mankan ka kan ka juguya fo ka bagabaga ka layini bɛɛ ta min tun sirilen bɛ harpoon na fɔlɔ. O ko ninnu na, balema in bɛ wuli tiɲɛ na i n’a fɔ ale mugu, i n’a fɔ a bɛ fɔ cogo min na, ka bɔ kurun kelen na ka taa tɔ kelen na; hali ni kurun fɔlɔ bɛ wuli tuma bɛɛ bolo la walasa k’a muso dɛmɛ. Filanan: Nin labɛn in nafa ka bon kosɛbɛ jamanadenw bɛɛ ka lakana kosɔn; sabu ni layini duguma dakun tun sirilen bɛ kurun la cogo o cogo, ni o tuma na, balema tun bɛ boli layini in na ka bɔ a laban na, a bɛ se ka kɛ miniti kelen kɔnɔ, sisi bɛ kɛ i n’a fɔ a bɛ kɛ cogo min na tuma dɔw la, a tun tɛna jɔ yen, bawo kurun halakilen tun bɛna sama ka jigin a nɔfɛ, fili tɛ min na ka don kɔgɔji jugumanba kɔnɔ wa o cogo la, dugu-kulebaga si tun tɛna a sɔrɔ tugun.

Sani kurun ka jigin ka taa a nɔfɛ, layini sanfɛla bɛ ta kɔfɛ ka bɔ jidagayɔrɔ la, ka tɛmɛ jirisunba lamini na yen, ka segin ka taa ɲɛfɛ kurun janya bɛɛ la, ka lafiɲɛ cɛ kelen-kelen bɛɛ ka kurunbonkarilan walima a bololanɛgɛ kan, walasa a ka boli a bolokɔni kan kurunbokari la ani fana ka tɛmɛ cɛw ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ, n’u sigilen bɛ ɲɔgɔn falen-falen marifakisɛw la minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn kɛrɛfɛ, ka taa kurun ɲɛfɛla nɛrɛmuguma dantɛmɛnenw na minnu bɛ ni plɔmu ye, yɔrɔ min na, jirisunba dɔ walima jirisunba dɔ min bonya bɛ se jirisunba kelen ma, o b’a bali ka sɛgɛn ka bɔ. Ka bɔ chocks la a bɛ jɛngɛ festoon dɔɔnin na bow kan, ka sɔrɔ ka tɛmɛ kurun kɔnɔ tugun; ani fasokan tan walima mugan ɲɔgɔn (min bɛ wele ko box-line) ni a sirilen bɛ kɛsu kan bow kɔnɔ, a bɛ taa a fɛ ka taa marifada la ka taa a fɛ dɔɔnin hali bi kɔfɛ, ka sɔrɔ ka nɔrɔ short-warp la — juru min bɛ siri o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ ni harpoon ye nka ka kɔn o jɛɲɔgɔnya ɲɛ, surun-kurun bɛ tɛmɛ gundo suguya caman fɛ minnu bɛ sɛgɛn kojugu fo ka se ka ɲɛfɔli kɛ.

O cogo la, balema-sira bɛ kurun bɛɛ da a ka bobine gɛlɛnw kɔnɔ, k’a firifiri ani k’a lamini a bɛ se ka fɔ ko a fan bɛɛ fɛ. Kurun bolilaw bɛɛ sen bɛ a ka jɛgɛn faratilenw na; walasa dugukolotigi maloya ɲɛ na, u bɛ i n’a fɔ Ɛnduw ka wuluwulukɛlaw, sa fagalenw bɛ u farikolo yɔrɔw masiri farikoloɲɛnajɛ la. Muso saya denkɛ si fana tɛ se, a siɲɛ fɔlɔ la, k’a yɛrɛ sigi o kansɛri gɛlɛyaw cɛma, k’a to a seko bɛɛ la kurunba la, k’a miiri ko waati dɔnbali o waati la, nɛgɛso bɛ se ka wuli, wa nin tigɛli jugu ninnu bɛɛ bɛ se ka don tulon na i n’a fɔ sanpɛrɛn bololanɛgɛw a tɛ se ka kɛ o cogo la ni yɛrɛyɛrɛ tɛ min b’a to a kolotugudaw yɛrɛ bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ a kɔnɔ i n’a fɔ jeli yɛrɛyɛrɛlen. O bɛɛ n’a ta, delina—fɛn kabakoma! delina tɛ se ka mun kɛ?—Gayer sallies, merry mirth more, better jokes, and brighter repartees, i ma deli ka mɛn i ka mahogany kan, ka tɛmɛ i bɛna min mɛn balema-kurun ka sɛdiri finman insitiri tilancɛ kan, n’i dulonna o cogo la hangman’s nooses kɔnɔ ani, i n’a fɔ Calais burgher wɔɔrɔ minnu tun bɛ masakɛ Edward ɲɛkɔrɔ, cɛ wɔɔrɔ minnu bɛ kurun kɔnɔ baarakɛlaw labɛn, olu bɛ sama ka don saya dawolo la, ni bɔgɔlan ye u kɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ la, i n’a fɔ i bɛ se k’a fɔ cogo min na.

Laala, ni i ye miiri dɔɔnin dɔrɔn de kɛ, o bɛna a to i ka se sisan ka jatebɔ kɛ o balawu minnu bɛ segin-ka-bɔ balemafɛnw ɲinini na — minnu damadɔw bɛ sɛbɛn k’a sɔrɔ u ma kɛ cogo si la — n’o ye nin cɛ in walima o cɛ bɔlen ye kurun kɔnɔ layini fɛ, ka tunun. Sabu, ni layini bɛ ka boli ka bɔ, ka sigi o tuma na kurun kɔnɔ, o bɛ i n’a fɔ i sigilen bɛ waji-motɛri ka mankan caman cɛma, tulon dafalen na, k’a sɔrɔ jirisunba bɛɛ, ani jirisunba, ani wotoro bɛɛ bɛ ka i balo. A ka jugu ka tɛmɛ o kan; sabu i tɛ se ka sigi i ma lamaga o faratiw dusukun na, bawo kurun bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ i n’a fɔ denso, wa i bɛ jɔ sira kelen fɛ ka taa fan wɛrɛ fɛ, k’a sɔrɔ i ma lasɔmi hali dɔɔnin ani ni yɛrɛlabɛnni dɔ dɔrɔn de ye ani ni ŋaniya ni wale kɛli ye waati kelen na, i bɛ se ka boli ka kɛ Mazeppa ye, ka boli ni yɔrɔ ye, tile min bɛ fɛn bɛɛ ye, o yɛrɛ tun tɛ se k’i sɔgɔ abada.

Nin sen in fana na: i n’a fɔ hakilisigi jugu min bɛ kɔn ka kɔn fɔɲɔba ɲɛ dɔrɔn ani ka kiraya kɛ, n’a sɔrɔ o ka jugu ka tɛmɛ fɔɲɔba yɛrɛ kan; sabu, tiɲɛ na, hakilisigi ye fɔɲɔba ka finimugu ni a datugulan dɔrɔn de ye; ani a b’a kɔnɔ a yɛrɛ la, i n’a fɔ marifa min bɛ i n’a fɔ kojugu tɛ min na, o bɛ mugu fagalen minɛ, ani bololanɛgɛ, ani binkanni; o la, layini ka lafiɲɛcogo ɲuman, k’a sɔrɔ a bɛ sa kɛ kurunbolilaw ko la ni makun ye sanni a ka na ni a ye tulon yɛrɛ la — nin ye fɛn ye min bɛ siran lakika ta ka tɛmɛ nin ko faratilen in fan tɔw bɛɛ kan. Nka mun na an bɛ dɔ fara o kan? Cɛw bɛɛ bɛ balo u sirilen bɛ balema-yɔrɔw la. Bɛɛ bɛ bange ni bɔrɔw ye u kɔ la; nka ni mɔgɔ sataw minɛna saya jiginni teliya la, o dɔrɔn de la, mɔgɔ sataw bɛ ɲɛnamaya faratiw dɔn minnu tɛ kuma, minnu tɛ ye, minnu bɛ yen tuma bɛɛ. Wa n’i ye filozofi ye, hali n’i sigilen bɛ balema-kurun kɔnɔ, i dusukun tɛna siran hali dɔɔnin, ka tɛmɛ n’i sigilen bɛ i ka wulada tasuma ɲɛfɛ ni poker ye, ani ni harpoon tɛ, i kɛrɛfɛ.
