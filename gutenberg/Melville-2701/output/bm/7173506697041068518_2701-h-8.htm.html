SABABU 41. Moby Dick.

Ne Ismaɛl tun ye o kurunba kɔnɔmɔgɔw dɔ ye; ne ka kulekanw tun wulila ni tɔw ye; ne ka kalili tun donna u ta la; ne ye kulekanba bɔ ka taa a fɛ, ne ye n ka kalili kɛ ka tɛmɛn fɔlɔ kan, k’a sababu kɛ siran ye min tun bɛ ne ni kɔnɔ. Kungo, gundo, hinɛ dusukunnata dɔ tun bɛ ne kɔnɔ; Akabu ka kɛlɛ min tun tɛ ban, o tun bɛ iko ne ta. N ye o waraba fagalen in tariku dɔn ni tulo namaratɔw ye, ne ni tɔw bɛɛ tun ye kalifa min kɛ fariyako ni waleɲumandɔn na.

Waati dɔw tɛmɛnen kɔ, hali ni waati dɔw dɔrɔn de la, Baleine blanche min tun tɛ taa ni a ye, n’a yɔrɔ ka jan, o tun bɛ o kɔgɔjiw la minnu tɛ siwili ye, Balema Sɛrɛma mɔnikɛlaw tun bɛ taa minnu fanba la. Nka u bɛɛ tun t’a ka kɛta dɔn; u damadɔ dɔrɔn, n’i y’a suma, u tun y’a ye k’a dɔn; k'a sɔrɔ mɔgɔ minnu tun ye kɛlɛ di a ma tiɲɛ na ani k'a dɔn fɔlɔ, olu hakɛ tun ka dɔgɔ tiɲɛ na. Bari, k’a sababu kɛ balema-kurunw caya ye; u serilen don cogo labɛnbali la ji lamini bɛɛ kan, u caman tun bɛ u ka ɲininiw gɛlɛya ni ŋaniya ye latitude kelenw kan, fo a man teli walima abada kalo tan ni fila kuuru walima ka tɛmɛ o kan, u ka se ka kibaruya fɔli kurun kelen sɔrɔ cogo si la taama danfaralen kelen-kelen bɛɛ janya dantɛmɛnen; kurunbokari waatiw labɛnbaliya ka bɔ so; ninnu bɛɛ, ni ko wɛrɛw, tilennenw ni tilennenbaliw, ye kibaru kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw jɛnsɛnni bali diɲɛ kɔnɔ balema-kurunba bɛɛ kɔnɔ, minnu ɲɛsinnen bɛ Mobi Diki ma. Siga tun tɛ a la, ko kurun damadɔw y’a fɔ k’u ye ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ, o waati sugu la, walima o waati sugu la, walima o ɲɔgɔnna meridiyɛn kan, Balema Sɛrɛma min bonya ni a juguya tɛ kelen ye, balema min, a kɛlen kɔ ka kojuguba kɛ a binkannikɛlaw la, o ye a yɛrɛ kisi pewu u ma dɔw hakili la, o tun tɛ hakilina tilenbali ye, n b’a fɔ ko balema min kofɔlen don, n’a sɔrɔ o tun tɛ mɔgɔ wɛrɛ ye ni Mobi Diki tɛ. O bɛɛ n’a ta, kabini kɔsa in na, Baleine Spérimée mɔni tun bɛ taamasiyɛn kɛ ni ko suguya caman ye, wa a tun tɛ kɛ tuma caman na, minnu kɛra farinyaba, nanbarako ani juguyaba ye, waraba min binna o kan; o de kama a kɛra, ko minnu ye kɛlɛ di Mobi Diki ma kasaara la k’a sɔrɔ u ma dɔn; o sogo ɲininikɛlaw, n’a sɔrɔla, u fanba la, u tun wasalen don k’a ka siranfɛn kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen min lamɔ, n’a fɔra cogo min na, o sababu bɔra Balema Sɛrɛmu mɔni na a bɛɛ lajɛlen ka faratiw la, ka tɛmɛ u kelen-kelen bɛɛ ka ko kan. O cogo la, a fanba la, Akabu ni balema in ka ɲɔgɔnye jugu min kɛra, o tun bɛ jate mɔgɔw fɛ fɔlɔ.

Wa minnu ye Balema Blanc ko mɛn ka kɔrɔ, k’a sɔrɔ u y’a ɲɛ minɛ k’a sɔrɔ u ma kɛ cogo si la; fɛn in daminɛ na u y'u kelen-kelen bɛɛ Sɔrɔ, a bɛ Se ka Kɛ, ni jagɛlɛya ni siranbaliya ye ka Jigin a ye, i n'a fɔ o suguya balema tɔw bɛɛ. Nka a laban na, o balawu suguw kɛra o binkanniw na tiɲɛ na — u ma dan bolokɔniw ni senkɔniw sɔgɔsɔgɔli ma, bolokɔniw karilen, walima farikolo yɔrɔw tigɛcogo minnu bɛ mɔgɔ dun — nka u ye mɔgɔ faga fo ka se mɔgɔ faga hakɛ laban ma o bɔnɛ juguw segin-segin, u bɛɛ bɛ u ka siranfɛnw dalajɛ ani k’u dalajɛ Mobi Diki kan; o kow tun taara yɔrɔ jan ka sogo ɲininikɛla jagɛlɛn caman ka fanga yɛrɛyɛrɛ, balema finman maana labanna ka se minnu ma.

Kungo kumakan suguya bɛɛ fana ma dɛsɛ ka damatɛmɛ, wa hali bi, o ɲɔgɔnye fagalenw tariku lakikaw bɛ siran ka t’a fɛ. Sabu kumakan kabakomaw tɛ bɔ kabako ko juguw bɛɛ farikolo yɛrɛ la u yɛrɛ la dɔrɔn,—i n’a fɔ jiri gosilen bɛ a ka ɲɛgɛnw bange cogo min na; nka, kɔgɔjida la ɲɛnamaya kɔnɔ, ka tɛmɛ terra firma ta kan kosɛbɛ, kungo kɔnɔ kumakanw ka ca, yɔrɔ o yɔrɔ, tiɲɛ bɛrɛbɛrɛ bɛ yen, u bɛ se ka nɔrɔ min na. Wa i n’a fɔ kɔgɔji bɛ tɛmɛ dugukolo kan nin ko in na cogo min na, balema mɔni fana bɛ tɛmɛ kɔgɔjida la ɲɛnamaya suguya tɔw bɛɛ kan, kumakanw kabako n’u siran na minnu bɛ yaala yen tuma dɔw la. Sabu, balemakɛlaw dɔrɔn tɛ i n’a fɔ farikolo, minnu tɛ bɔ o dɔnbaliya ni dannakow la, minnu ye kurunbolilaw bɛɛ ka ciyɛn ye; nka kurunbolilaw bɛɛ la, a tɛ se ka kɛ, u de bɛ tali kɛ fɛn o fɛn na min bɛ mɔgɔ kabakoya kosɛbɛ kɔgɔji kɔnɔ; ɲɛda ni ɲɛda u tɛ a kabakobaw ɲɛ dɔrɔn, nka, bolo ni dawolo, kɛlɛ di u ma. I kelen na, ji yɔrɔjanw na, fo hali n’i ye kurun ta kilomɛtɛrɛ ba kelen kɔnɔ, ka tɛmɛ kɔgɔjida ba kelen kan, i tɛna se tasuma-fanga-kabakurun sɔgɔlen si la, walima jatigila fɛn si la o tile yɔrɔ jukɔrɔ o latitude ni longitudes suguw la, ka tugu weleli sugu kɔ kojugu i n’a fɔ ale, balemakɛla bɛ siri ni nɔw ye minnu bɛɛ b’a fɛ k’a ka fancy kɔnɔmaya ni bange barikama caman ye.

O la, kabako t’a la ko tuma bɛɛ ka hakɛ lajɛ ka bɔ tɛmɛsira dɔrɔn na ji yɔrɔw kan minnu ka bon kosɛbɛ, Balema Blanc ka kumakan minnu fɔra ka bɔ kɛnɛ kan, olu labanna ka banako hakilina suguya bɛɛ don u yɛrɛ la, ani fœtal hakilina tilancɛ-fɔlenw minnu bɛ bɔ baaradaw fɛ minnu ka bon ni hadamadenw ta ye, minnu labanna ka Mobi Dick bila siranfɛn kuraw la minnu ma juru ta fɛn o fɛn na min bɛ ye ka ye. O la, ko caman na, o siran sugu labanna ka Kɛ, ko mɔgɔ damadɔ minnu tùn ye Balema Blanc ko mɛn o kumakanw fɛ, a dɔgɔyalenba la, o ɲininikɛlaw damadɔ tùn bɛ Sɔn k’a dawolo faratiw Sɔrɔ.

Nka, nɔ ɲuman wɛrɛw tun bɛ baara la hali bi ani minnu nafa ka bon kosɛbɛ. Hali bi, balema min bɛ wele ko Sperm Whale, o bonya fɔlɔ, i n’a fɔ a bɛ danfara don cogo min na ni siran ye ni leviathan suguya tɔw bɛɛ ye, o ma sa ka bɔ balemakɛlaw hakili la i n’a fɔ farikolo. Mɔgɔ dɔw bɛ u cɛma bi, hali n’u hakili ka bon ani u jagɛlɛyalen don kosɛbɛ ka kɛlɛ di Gronland walima Right whale ma, laala — k’a sababu kɛ baarakɛlaw ka ko dɔnbaliya ye, walima seko dɛsɛ fɛ, walima maloya fɛ, u bɛna ban ɲɔgɔndan dɔ la ni baleine sperme ye a mana kɛ cogo o cogo, balemakɛla caman bɛ yen, kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la, balemakɛla jamana minnu tɛ kurun ta Ameriki darapo kɔrɔ, minnu ma deli ka balema Sɛrɛma kunbɛn ni juguya ye, nka u ka dɔnniya kelenpe bɛ leviathan kan, o dan ye waraba bonyabali ye min tun bɛ tugu fɔlɔ Worodugu fɛ sigilen u ka wuluwuluw kan, nin cɛ ninnu bɛna u lamɛn ni denmisɛnninw ka tasuma kɛrɛfɛ nafa ni siran ye, kungo kɔnɔ, Saheli balemafɛnw ɲinini maana kabakomaw la. Balema-banakisɛba ka bonyaba min bɛ ɲɛfɛ, o fana tɛ faamuya yɔrɔ si la ni dusukunnata ye, ka tɛmɛ o jirisunw kan minnu b’a bali.

Wa i n’a fɔ a ka sebaaya tiɲɛni min kɔrɔbɔra sisan, o y’a sumaya fili a ɲɛ kɔrɔ, nsiirin waati kɔrɔw la; an bɛ gafe nafamafɛnw dɔnbaga dɔw sɔrɔ — Olassen ni Povelson — minnu b’a fɔ ko baleine sperme tɛ danfɛn tɔw bɛɛ kabakoya dɔrɔn kɔgɔji kɔnɔ, nka a ka jugu fana ka jugu kosɛbɛ fo ka to ka hadamaden joli minnɔgɔ sɔrɔ. Hali fo ka se waati laban ma i n’a fɔ Cuvier ta, o hakilinaw walima o ɲɔgɔnna hakilinaw ma ban. Sabula a ka Natural History kɔnɔ, Baron yɛrɛ b’a jira ko ni u ye Balema Spérimée ye, jɛgɛ bɛɛ (sɛgɛsɛgɛliw fana sen bɛ o la) “bɛ gosi ni siranfɛn ɲɛnamabaw ye,” wa “tuma caman na, u ka panni teliya la, u bɛ u yɛrɛ boli faraw kan ni fariya ye fo ka se ka mɔgɔ faga yɔrɔnin kelen.”. Wa hali o bɛɛ n’a ta, ko kɛlenw bɛɛ lajɛlen minnu bɛ kɛ jɛgɛko la, olu bɛ se ka nin kunnafoniw ɲɔgɔnna caman sɛmɛntiya; o bɛɛ n’a ta, u ka siran dafalen na, hali ka se Povelson ka joli-minɛ-fɛn ma, dannayabaliya min bɛ u la, o bɛ lakunu, u ka weleli jiginni dɔw la, kungokɔnɔna ɲininikɛlaw hakili la.

O la, k’a sababu kɛ kumakanw ni taamasiyɛnw ye minnu tun bɛ a ko la, mɔnikɛla damadɔw hakili ma jigin, ka ɲɛsin Moby Dick ma, Sperm Whale mɔni daminɛ donw na, waati min na a tun ka gɛlɛn tuma caman na ka balema kininfɛla minnu tun bɛ dege kabini tuma jan, olu bila ka don nin kɛlɛ kura ni jagɛlɛya in faratiw la o cɛ suguw bɛ ka sɔsɔli kɛ ko hali ni jigiya bɛ se ka tugu leviatan wɛrɛw nɔfɛ, o bɛɛ n’a ta, ka tugu ani ka nɛgɛso jira o jirali sugu la i n’a fɔ baleine sperme tun tɛ mɔgɔ salen ta ye. Ko k’a ɲini, o bɛna kɛ sababu ye ka tigɛtigɛ ka kɛ banbali teliya ye. O kun na, sɛbɛn kabakoma dɔw bɛ yen minnu bɛ se ka lajɛ.

O bɛɛ n’a ta, dɔw tun bɛ yen, minnu hali nin ko ninnu ɲɛkɔrɔ, u tun labɛnnen don ka Mobi Diki nɔfɛ; ani mɔgɔ minnu ka ca hali bi, minnu, n’u y’a mɛn dɔrɔn yɔrɔjan ani cogo jɛlen na, k’a sɔrɔ u ma balawu dɔ kunnafoni kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw sɔrɔ, ani k’a sɔrɔ dannakow ma fara u kan, u tun ka gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ walasa u kana boli kɛlɛ la ni u dira u ma.

Kungo laadilikan minnu kofɔra, i n’a fɔ laban na, u ni Balema Blanc bɛ tali kɛ ɲɔgɔn na dannakow hakili la, o tun ye yɛrɛbonya ye min tɛ dugukolo kan, n’o ye ko Moby Dick bɛ yɔrɔ bɛɛ ko a ye ɲɔgɔn Sɔrɔ tiɲɛ na latitude (latitude) kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw na waati kelen na.

O hakili suguw tun ka kan ka kɛ dannayabaliya ye fana, o yɛrɛbonya tun tɛ yen pewu, k’a sɔrɔ dannakow bɛ se ka kɛ cogo min na, o jirali dɔɔnin dɔɔnin. Sabu i n’a fɔ kɔgɔjiw kɔnɔ jikuruw gundo ma fɔ fɔlɔ cogo min na, hali ɲininikɛla ŋanaw fɛ; o la, balema-sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ ka sira dogolenw n’a bɛ dugukolo jukɔrɔ, u fanba bɛ to, u tɛ jate a nɔfɛmɔgɔw ye; ani waati ni waati, hakilina minnu bɛ ɲininkali kɛ kosɛbɛ ani minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔsɔ u ko la, kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la, gundola cogoyaw kan, minnu kɔnɔ, a mankan bɔlen kɔ fo ka se yɔrɔ janba ma, a b’a yɛrɛ ta ni o teliyaba in ye ka taa yɔrɔw la minnu ka jan kosɛbɛ.

O ye fɛn ye min dɔnna kosɛbɛ Ameriki ni Angilɛw ka balema-kurunw fɛ, wa o fana ye fɛn ye min bilala sɛbɛn fangatigiw kan a san caman ye nin ye Scoresby fɛ, ko balema dɔw minɛna worodugu fan fɛ Pasifiki kɔgɔjida la, minnu farikolow la, harpoonw ka barbw sɔrɔla minnu bɛ boli Gronland kɔgɔjiw kɔnɔ. A man kan fana k’a Fɔ ko nin ko ninnu dɔw la, a Fɔra ko waati min bɛ binkanni fla ninnu ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ, o tùn tɛ Se ka Tɛmɛ tile caman kan kosɛbɛ. O la, ka kɛɲɛ ni jateminɛ ye, balemakɛla dɔw dalen b’a la ko Nor’ West Passage, min tun ye gɛlɛya ye hadamaden bolo kabini tuma jan, o tun tɛ gɛlɛya ye balema ma abada. Walasa yan, cɛ ɲɛnamaw ka ɲɛnamaya lakika kɔnɔ, kabako minnu tun bɛ lakali waati kɔrɔw la, Strello kulu kɔnɔna na Pɔritigali (a fɔra ko baji dɔ bɛ yen min kuncɛ kɛrɛfɛ, kurunw tiɲɛnenw bɛ pan ka taa sanfɛ) ani o maana min ka di ka tɛmɛ o kan hali bi, n’o ye Arethusa jibolisira ye Sirakuse kɛrɛfɛ (a tun dalen b’a la ko a ji bɔra Jamana Senuman na dugujukɔrɔ sira fɛ); o lakalifɛn kabakomaw bɛ ɲini ka bɛn ɲɔgɔn ma ni balemakɛlaw ka tiɲɛkow ye.

O la sa, a jagoyalen don ka dɔn ni nin ɲɔgɔnna kabako suguw ye; ani k’a dɔn ko binkanni minnu kɛra siɲɛ caman, ni jagɛlɛya ye, Balema Blanc bolila ɲɛnama; a tɛ se ka kɛ kabako ye kosɛbɛ ko balemakɛla dɔw ka kan ka taa ɲɛ hali bi u ka dannakow la; k’a fɔ ko Moby Dick tɛ yɔrɔ bɛɛ dɔrɔn na, nka a tɛ sa (k’a masɔrɔ saya tɛ yɔrɔ bɛɛ la waati kɔnɔ); ko hali ni tamaforow tun bɛna turu a kɛrɛfɛ, a bɛna jigin ka taa hali bi, foyi ma kɛ a la; walima ni tiɲɛ na, a tun bɛna kɛ sababu ye ka joli belebele bɔ don dɔ, o yecogo sugu bɛna kɛ nanbara juguba dɔrɔn de ye; sabu tuguni, jikuruw kɔnɔ minnu tɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ, n’u bɛ kilomɛtɛrɛ kɛmɛ caman bɔ, a ka jet nɔgɔlen tun bɛna ye siɲɛ kelen kokura.

Nka hali n’a bɔra o hakilinaw la minnu ka bon ni hadamaden ta ye, fɛn caman tun bɛ o fɛnɲɛnama in ka dugukolo kan dilancogo n’a jogo la min tɛ se ka sɔsɔ walasa ka miirili gosi ni fanga ye min tun tɛ deli ka kɛ. Sabula, a ka fɛn camanba min tɛ deli ka kɛ, o de y’a danfara kosɛbɛ ni balema cɛya tɔw ye, nka, i n’a fɔ a filila yɔrɔ wɛrɛw la cogo min na—a ɲɛda kurukurulen kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen min bɛ i n’a fɔ nɛnɛ, ani wuluwulu finman sanfɛla, min bɛ i n’a fɔ piramidi. Olu tun ye a ka fɛn ɲɛnamaw ye; taamasiyɛn minnu fɛ, hali kɔgɔji dan tɛ minnu na, minnu ma dɔn, a y’a yɛrɛ jira, yɔrɔ jan, a dɔnbagaw la.

A farikolo tɔ tun bɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ, ani nɔgɔ, ani marbre ni o ɲɛda datugulen kelen ye, fo, a laban na, a y’a ka tɔgɔ danfaralen sɔrɔ min ye Balema Blanc ye tɔgɔ min, tiɲɛ na, min bɛnnen don tiɲɛ na a ɲɛda ɲɛnama fɛ, n’a y’a ye a bɛ ka panpan tilelafana fɛ kɔgɔji bulu dibiman dɔ kɔnɔ, ka nɔnɔ sira wulilen to yen, n’o ye fomu kreman ye, o bɛɛ lajɛlen bɛ ni sanu yeelenw ye.

A ka bonya min tun ma deli ka kɛ, a ɲɛda cogoya kabakoma fana tun tɛ, wa hali bi a duguma dawolo nɔgɔlen, o de ye balema in bila siran dabɔlen na kosɛbɛ, i n’a fɔ o juguya hakilitigi min misali tɛ, ka kɛɲɛ ni jatebɔ kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw ye, a tun y’o jira siɲɛ caman a ka binkanniw na. Ka tɛmɛ o bɛɛ kan, a ka kɔsegin janfalenw ye mɔgɔw dusu tiɲɛ ka tɛmɛ laala fɛn wɛrɛ kan. Sabu, n’a tun bɛ ka jigin a nɔfɛmɔgɔw ɲɛkɔrɔ minnu tun bɛ nisɔndiya la, ni siran taamasiɲɛ bɛɛ ye, a tun dɔnna siɲɛ caman ko a bɛ a yɛrɛ wuli yɔrɔnin kelen, wa, k’a jigin u kan, k’u ka kurunw siri ka tigɛtigɛ, walima k’u gɛn ka segin u ka kurun kɔnɔ ni kabako ye.

Kaban, mɔgɔ caman fagara a nɔfɛ. Nka hali ni o balawu suguw, hali ni u tun man ca kɔgɔjida la cogo o cogo, u tun tɛ ko ye min tɛ deli ka kɛ jɛgɛko la; o bɛɛ n’a ta, a fanba la, a tun bɛ iko Balema Blanc ka jahanama miirili ye farinyako la, fo a ye farikolo yɔrɔw tigɛtigɛ walima saya o saya lase a ma, o tun tɛ jate pewu ko a kɛra mɔgɔ hakilintan dɔ fɛ.

O la sa, a ka kungokɔnɔna minnu jigi tigɛlen don kosɛbɛ, olu hakili tun bɛ dimi min wulilen don, min hakili ɲagamina, o jateminɛ kɛ, tuma min na kurun sɔgɔlenw ka sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛw cɛma, ani u jɛɲɔgɔn tigɛlenw farikolo yɔrɔw minnu bɛ ka jigin, u ye u yɛrɛ jigin ka bɔ balema ka dimi juguba ka kuru finmanw na ka taa tile yeelen lafiyalen na, min bɛ mɔgɔ dimi, min bɛ nisɔndiya ka taa a fɛ, i n’a fɔ u bɛ ka wuli walima ka kɛ kɔɲɔmuso.

A ka kurun saba b’a lamini, ani kurunba ni cɛw fila bɛɛ bɛ ka girin jikuruw kɔnɔ; kapitɛni kelen, min ye layini-muru minɛ a ɲɛfɛla karilen na, o tun ye boli kɛ balema in kan, i n’a fɔ Arkansas kɛlɛcɛ dɔ a jugu fɛ, k’a ɲini fiyentɔya la ni nɛgɛ ye min janya ye santimɛtɛrɛ wɔɔrɔ ye walasa ka se balema in ka ɲɛnamaya ma min ka dun fo ka se a faso ma. O kapitɛni tun ye Akabu ye. Wa o kɔfɛ, a kɛra sababu ye k’a duguma dawolo min bɛ i n’a fɔ sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ, o barika la ka a jukɔrɔ, Mobi Diki tun ye Akabu sen tigɛ ka bɔ yen, i n’a fɔ bin tigɛbaga foro kɔnɔ. Turki si min kunsigi bɛ a la, Venetian walima Malayka baarakɛla si tun tɛ se k’a bugɔ ni juguya ye ka tɛmɛ o kan. O la sa, kun fitinin tun bɛ yen ka sigasiga ko kabini o ɲɔgɔnye min tun bɛ ɲini ka mɔgɔ faga, Akabu tun bɛ kungo kɔnɔ waleɲumandɔnba dɔ lase balema ma, a binna ka tɛmɛn fɔlɔ kan, o de kosɔn a ka bana juguba kɔnɔ, a laban na, a labanna k’a yɛrɛ dɔn a fɛ, a farikolo tɔɔrɔw bɛɛ dɔrɔn tɛ, nka a ka hakili ni hakili ta fan fɛ dimi bɛɛ. Balema finman ye 'sèn Sɔrɔ a ɲɛ i n'a fɔ o baarada juguw bɛɛ farikolo-ɲɛnajɛ kelen-kelen, cɛ jugu dɔw b'a Dɔn k'u bɛ dumuni Kɛ u kɔnɔ, fo k'u To balo la ni dusukun tilancɛ ni fogonfogon tilancɛ ye. O juguya min tɛ se ka ye, o min bɛ yen kabini daminɛ na; hali bi kerecɛnw bɛ diɲɛw tilancɛ kelen di min ka mara ma; kɔrɔnfɛla Ofite kɔrɔw tun bɛ min bonya u ka ja jinɛ la;—Ahab ma bin k’a bato i n’a fɔ olu; nka k’a hakilina bila balema finman haramulen in na ni hakilijagabɔ ye, a y’a yɛrɛ bila a kɛlɛ la, a farikolo bɛɛ tigɛlen don. O bɛɛ bɛ dibi ni tɔɔrɔba lase mɔgɔ ma; fɛn o fɛn bɛ fɛnw sɔgɔsɔgɔ; tiɲɛ bɛɛ ni juguya bɛ a kɔnɔ; o bɛɛ bɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ kari ani ka hakili gato; ɲɛnamaya ni miirili jinɛ nɔgɔlenw bɛɛ; juguya bɛɛ, ka se Akabu dibi la, u kɛra mɔgɔ ye cogo yelen na, wa u kɛra binkannikɛla ye tiɲɛ na Moby Dick kɔnɔ. A ye dimi ni koniya bɛɛ lajɛlen bɛɛ lajɛlen dalajɛ balema ka wuluwulu finman kan, a siya bɛɛ ye min sɔrɔ k’a ta Hadama la ka jigin; o kɔfɛ, i n'a fɔ a disi kɛra bɔgɔdaga ye, a y'a dusukun funteni bɔgɔlan pɛrɛn a kan.

A tɛ Se ka Kɛ ko nin monomania min bɛ a la, o y’a ka wuli o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ la a farikolo-yɔrɔ-ko-tigɛ waati tigitigi la. O kɔfɛ, a kɛlen ka bɔgɔbɔgɔ kɛ waraba in na, muru b’a bolo, a tun y’a yɛrɛ di nka juguya barikama ma, min tun bɛ kɛ ni diyanye ye, farikolo ta fan fɛ; ani tuma min na a ye sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ sɔrɔ min y’a tigɛ, n’a sɔrɔ a ye farikolo sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ tɔɔrɔlen ye, nka foyi ma tɛmɛ o kan. O bɛɛ n’a ta, tuma min na o ɲɔgɔnkanu in y’a wajibiya ka wuli ka taa so fan fɛ, ani kalo janw kɔnɔ tile ni dɔgɔkun caman kɔnɔ, Akabu ni tɔɔrɔ dalen tun bɛ ɲɔgɔn na hamakɛ kelen kɔnɔ, ka o Patagonie Cape dusukasilen ni kulekanba lamini nɛnɛ cɛmancɛ la o kɔfɛ, a farikolo tigɛlen ni a ni tigɛlen joli bɔra ɲɔgɔn na; ani o cogo la, a ye a sen don a la, o y’a kɛ dibi ye. Ko o waati dɔrɔn de la, so taama na, ɲɔgɔnye kɔfɛ, ko monomania laban y’a minɛ, a bɛ iko a bɛɛ jɛlen don k’a sababu kɛ, waati dɔw la, tɛmɛsira waati la, a tun ye hakilintan ye min bɛ wuli kosɛbɛ ani hali n’a sen tun tɛ a la, o bɛɛ n’a ta, fanga nafamaba tun bɛ a ka Misira disi la hali bi, wa ka fara o kan, a ka hakiliɲagami juguyara, fo a furuɲɔgɔnw wajibiyara k’a siri kosɛbɛ, hali yen, k’a to kurun kɔnɔ, k’a ka hamakɛ kɔnɔ. Jaki strait-jacket kɔnɔ, a y’a yɛrɛ wuli ka taa fiɲɛba dibi la. Wa, ni a bolila ka don latitudew la minnu bɛ tɔɔrɔ kosɛbɛ, kurun, ni stun’sails nɔgɔmanw jɛnsɛnnen don, a tun bɛ pan ka tɛmɛn yɔrɔ duman lafiyalenw kan, wa, a yecogo bɛɛ la, a kɛra i n’a fɔ cɛkɔrɔba in ka delirium tora a kɔfɛ ni Cape Horn funuw ye, wa a bɔra a ka dibi dingɛ kɔnɔ ka don yeelen ni fiɲɛ dugawulen kɔnɔ hali o waati la, n’a y’o jirisun in ta, ka ɲɛfɛla lajɛ, hali n’a ɲɛ jɛlen don cogo o cogo, k’a ka yamaruyaw di ni hakilisigi ye siɲɛ kelen kokura; a furuɲɔgɔnw ye barika da Ala ye dibi juguba in banna sisan; hali o waati la, Akabu, a yɛrɛ dogolen kɔnɔ, a tun bɛ ka wuli ka taa a fɛ. Hadamadenw ka dibi ye tuma caman na namara ye ani min ka ca ni wulu ye. Ni i b’a miiri ko a bolila, n’a sɔrɔ a jiginna ka kɛ cogo dɔ la min ka nɔgɔn hali bi. Akabu ka hakilintan dafalen ma nɔgɔya, nka a juguyara ka taa a fɛ; i n’a fɔ Hudson min ma jɔ, tuma min na o Worodugu mɔgɔba in bɛ woyo dɔɔnin dɔɔnin, nka a tɛ se ka faamuya ka tɛmɛ Highland kɔdingɛ kɔnɔ. Nka, i n’a fɔ a ka monomania min bɛ woyo dɔɔnin dɔɔnin, Akabu ka dibi belebeleba dɔ tun ma to kɔfɛ; o la, o dibiba in na, a ka dacogo hakiliba dɔ tun ma halaki. O ka kɔn fɛn ɲɛnama ɲɛ, sisan a kɛra minɛn ɲɛnama ye. Ni o trope diminnen sugu bɛ se ka jɔ, a ka dibi kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen ye a ka hakili bɛɛ lajɛlen minɛ, k’a ta, k’a ka kanon bɛɛ lajɛlen wuli a yɛrɛ ka dibi taamasiyɛn kan; o la sa, a yɔrɔ ka jan a ka fanga la, Akabu, ka se o kun kelen ma, fanga tun bɛ a la sisan siɲɛ ba kelen ka tɛmɛ fɔlɔ kan, a tun ye min lase ni hakilitigiya ye ka fɛn kelen min bɛ se ka kɛ hakilitigi ye.

Nin ye caman ye; o bɛɛ n’a ta, Akabu yɔrɔ min ka bon, a dibi ka bon, a ka dun, o yɔrɔ ma dɔn. Nka fu ye ka ko juguw kɛ mɔgɔw ye, wa tiɲɛ bɛɛ ka dun. An jɔlen bɛ yɔrɔ min na yan, n’i bɛ girin ka bɔ nin Hotel de Cluny min falen bɛ jirisunw na, o dusukun yɛrɛ kɔnɔ — hali n’a ka bon cogo o cogo, a kɛra kabako ye cogo o cogo, aw k’a dabila sisan;—ka aw ka sira ta, aw niw minnu ka bon, aw minnu ka dusukasi ka bon, ka taa o Romɛkaw ka sobaw la, minnu bɛ wele ko Thermes yɔrɔ min na hadamaden ka dugukolo sanfɛla sankanso kabakomaw jukɔrɔ yɔrɔ jan, a ka bonya ju, a kɔnɔko jugu bɛɛ sigilen bɛ kunsigi cogo la; fɛn kɔrɔ dɔ min suturalen don fɛn kɔrɔw jukɔrɔ, ka sigi masasigilan kan farikolo yɔrɔw kan! O la, ni masasigilan karilen ye, ala belebelebaw bɛ o masakɛ minɛlen tulo geren; o la i n’a fɔ Caryatid, a muɲuman bɛ sigi, ka san caman ka entablatures kululenw sabati a ɲɛda nɛnɛlen kan. Aw ka fiɲɛ jigin yen, aw niw kuncɛbaw, ni dusukasilenw! ɲininkali kɛ o masakɛ kuncɛba, dusukasilen in na! Denbaya bɔɲɔgɔnko dɔ! aye, a ye aw bange, aw masaladen minnu tun bɛ jɔnya la; ani ka bɔ i fa jugu dɔrɔn de la, jamana gundo kɔrɔ bɛna bɔ.

Sisan, a dusukun na, Akabu ye nin ko dɔw ye, n’o ye ko: ne ka fɛɛrɛw bɛɛ hakili ka di, ne ka ŋaniya ni ne ka fɛn ye dibi ye. O bɛɛ n’a ta, fanga t’a la ka mɔgɔ faga, walima ka fɛn caman Changer, walima ka i yɛrɛ tanga tiɲɛ ma; a tun b’a dɔn o cogo kelen na ko hadamadenw fɛ a tun bɛ a yɛrɛ kɛ fɛn ye kabini tuma jan; cogo dɔ la, a kɛra hali bi. Nka o fɛn min Kɛra a ka fɛnɲɛnɛma-ko la, o tùn bɛ a ka faamuyali dɔrɔn de kɔrɔ, a ma Kɛ a sago latigɛlen ye. O bɛɛ n’a ta, a sera o ɲɛnajɛ in na kosɛbɛ, fo tuma min na a ye senna-tɛgɛrɛ kɛ ni nɛgɛbere ye laban na, Nantucketer si ma miiri a la cogo wɛrɛ la ni nka a dusu tiɲɛna a yɛrɛ la, wa o kɛra teliya la, ni bɔnɛba ye min y’a sɔrɔ.

A ka hakiliɲagami min tɛ se ka sɔsɔ kɔgɔji la, o kofɔra o cogo kelen na, mɔgɔw tun b’a fɔ ko a ɲɔgɔnna dɔ de don. Wa o cogo kelen na fana, dusukunnataw bɛɛ faralen ɲɔgɔn kan minnu tun bɛ kɛ tuma bɛɛ o kɔfɛ, fo ka se Pequod kurunbokari don yɛrɛ ma sisan taama in na, o sigilen tun bɛ ka miiri a ɲɛda la. A tɛ se ka kɛ fana kosɛbɛ, ko a yɔrɔ ka jan a ka seko la balema ɲinini wɛrɛ la, o dibi taamasiɲɛ suguw kosɔn, o gun hakilitigi ka jatebɔlaw tun b’a fɛ ka kuncɛbaya in mara, fo o kun yɛrɛ de kosɔn a tun bɛ se kosɛbɛ, wa a tun bɛ se ka da a kan, ka ɲɛsin ɲinini ma min falen bɛ dimi ni kungo la i n’a fɔ balema ɲinini jolilen. Gnawed within and scorched out, ni hakilina furakɛbali dɔw ka sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛw ye minnu sirilen don, minnu tɛ ban; o ɲɔgɔnna, n’a tun bɛ se ka sɔrɔ, a bɛna kɛ i n’a fɔ cɛ yɛrɛ de b’a ka nɛgɛ sɔgɔ k’a ka nɛgɛbere kɔrɔta ka ɲɛsin waraba bɛɛ ma min ka jugu kosɛbɛ. Walima, ni kun dɔ kosɔn a tun b’a miiri ko a farikolo tɛ se o la, o bɛɛ n’a ta, o tigi bɛna kɛ i n’a fɔ a bɛ se kosɛbɛ ka tɛmɛn a dankanw kan ka dusu don a kɔnɔ ani ka kulekan bɔ binkanni in na. Nka nin bɛɛ ka kɛ cogo o cogo, a dalen b’a la ko ni a ka dimi min ma ban, o gundo dibiman min tun bɛ a bolo, k’a don a kɔnɔ, Akab tun ye kurun ta ni laɲini ye sisan taama in na ni fɛn kelenpe ye min bɛ fɛn bɛɛ minɛ, n’o ye ka balema finman ɲini. Ni a dɔnbaga kɔrɔ dɔ tun bɛ kɔgɔjida la, nka a tilancɛ tun ye siko kɛ min tun bɛ a kɔnɔ o waati la, u niw kabakoyara ani u ni tilennenw tun bɛna kurun bɔ o cɛ jinɛ sugu la joona dɛ! U tun jɔlen bɛ kurunbaw kan minnu bɛ nafa sɔrɔ, tɔnɔ min tun ka kan ka jate dɔrɔmɛ la ka bɔ minɛnko la. A tun b’a fɛ ka waleɲumandɔn kɛ ni jagɛlɛya ye, min tɛ se ka wuli, ani min ka bon ni hadamaden ta ye.

O la sa, nin cɛkɔrɔba kunkolo jɛman, Ala ɲɛsiranbali in tun bɛ yan, min tun bɛ Job ka balema dɔ nɔfɛ ni dangaw ye diɲɛ fan bɛɛ la, kurunba kɔnɔ baarakɛlaw kun na, ale fana, minnu tun bɛ kɛ kosɛbɛ ni mongrel murutilenw ye, ani castaways, ani mɔgɔfagalaw — minnu barika dɔgɔyara jogo ta fan fɛ fana, ni seko dɛsɛ ye min bɛ kɛ ni jogo ɲuman dɔrɔn ye min tɛ dɛmɛ sɔrɔ walima min ye hakili ɲuman ye Starbuck kɔnɔ, mɔgɔ min tɛ se ka tiɲɛ nisɔndiya min bɛ kɛ ni ɲɛmajɔbaliya ni hakilintanya ye Stubb kɔnɔ, ani mediocrité min bɛ yɔrɔ bɛɛ Flask kɔnɔ. O kurunba sugu, min tun ye sɔrɔdasi ye ten, a tun bɛ iko a sugandira kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la ani k’a fa jahanama fagalen dɔw fɛ walasa k’a dɛmɛ k’a ka waleɲumandɔn kɛ min ye mɔgɔ kelen ta ye. A kɛra cogo min na, u ye cɛkɔrɔba ka dimi jaabi kosɛbɛ—mago jugu min tun bɛ u niw la, fo tuma dɔw la, a ka koniya tun bɛ i n’a fɔ u ta bɛ ɲini ka kɛ u ta ye; Baleine Blanche u jugu min tɛ se ka muɲu, i n’a fɔ ale ta; nin bɛɛ kɛra cogo min na — Baleine Blanc tun ye min ye u bolo, walima cogo min na u ka faamuyaliw la minnu tɛ u hakili la, fana, cogo dɔ la min tɛ ye, cogo min tɛ siga la, a bɛ se ka kɛ i n’a fɔ ɲɛnamaya kɔgɔjiw ka jinɛba min bɛ panpan, — nin bɛɛ ɲɛfɔli, o bɛna kɛ ka jigin ka taa a fɛ ka tɛmɛ Ismaɛl bɛ se ka taa cogo min na. Dugukolo jukɔrɔla minɛnkokɛla min bɛ baara kɛ an bɛɛ kɔnɔ, mɔgɔ bɛ se k’a dɔn cogo di k’a ka jirisunba ɲɛminɛ yɔrɔ min na a ka pick mankan fɛ min bɛ wuli tuma bɛɛ, min bɛ muɲu? Jɔn t’a ye ko bololabaara min tɛ se ka bali? Skiff jumɛn bɛ tow of a bi wolonwula ni naani kɔnɔ, o bɛ se ka jɔ yɔrɔ kelen na? Kelen kama, ne y’a di waati ni yɔrɔ bilali ma; nka k’a sɔrɔ hali bi u bɛɛ tun bɛ kɔrɔtɔ ka balema in kunbɛn, u ma se ka foyi ye o waraba in na fo banabagatɔ min bɛ mɔgɔ faga kosɛbɛ.





SABABU 42. Balema ka fini jɛman.

Balema finman tun ye min ye Akabu fɛ, o jirala; a tun ye min ye, tuma dɔw la, ne fɛ, hali bi, o ma fɔ fɔlɔ.

O jateminɛ minnu jɛlen don kosɛbɛ, n’olu bɛ maga Mobi Diki la, n’olu tun tɛ se ka mɔgɔ si ni lasɔmi tuma dɔw la, miirili wɛrɛ tun bɛ yen, walima n’a fɔra ka ɲɛ, siranfɛn tɔgɔ tɛ min na, o tun bɛ a ko la, waati dɔw la, a fanga fɛ, a tun bɛ se sɔrɔ tɔw bɛɛ kan pewu o bɛɛ n’a ta, a tun ye gundo ye ani a tun bɛ ɲini ka fɔ kosɛbɛ, fo a bɛ ɲini ka ne jigi tigɛ k’a bila cogo la min bɛ se ka faamuya. Balema in finman de ye ne lasiran ka tɛmɛ fɛn bɛɛ kan. Nka ne bɛ se ka jigiya sɔrɔ cogo di ka n yɛrɛ ɲɛfɔ yan; ani o bɛɛ n’a ta, cogo dɔ la min tɛ jɛya, cogo la min tɛ kɛ cogo si la, n k’a ɲɛfɔ n yɛrɛ ye ko n ka kan, n’o tɛ, nin sapitiri ninnu bɛɛ bɛ se ka kɛ foyi ye.

Hali ni fɛn caman na minnu bɛ bɔ fɛnɲɛnamaw la, finman bɛ cɛɲi bonya ka ɲɛ, i n’a fɔ a bɛ jogo kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen dɔw di a yɛrɛ ma, i n’a fɔ marifaw, japonikaw ani perlew la; ani hali ni jamana suguya caman ye masakɛ ka ɲɛtaa dɔ dɔn cogo dɔ la o kulɛri in na; hali Pegu masakɛ kɔrɔ barbariw, belebelebaw, minnu ye “Wara finmanw Matigi” tɔgɔ bila u ka fanga fɔcogo cɛɲi tɔw bɛɛ sanfɛ; ani Siam bi masakɛw bɛ o sen naani kelen in dabɔ masakɛ ka pankurun kɔnɔ; ani Hanoverian darapo min bɛ ni chargeur finman ja kelen ye; ani Ɔtirisi Mansamaraba, Cæsarian, min ye Romɛ fangatigi ciyɛntabaga ye, min tun bɛ ni masakɛ kulɛri kelen ye masakɛ kulɛri kelen na; ani hali n’o ɲɛtaa min bɛ a kɔnɔ, o bɛ tali kɛ hadamaden siya yɛrɛ la, ka setigiya ɲuman di cɛ finman ma kabila dibi bɛɛ kan; ani hali ni, ka fara nin bɛɛ kan, finman yɛrɛ kɛra nisɔndiya nafama ye, bawo Romɛkaw cɛma, kabakurun finman dɔ tun bɛ nisɔndiya don taamasiyɛn; ani hali ni mɔgɔ sataw ka hinɛ ni taamasyɛn wɛrɛw la, o kulɛri kelen in bɛ kɛ fɛn caman taamasyɛn ye minnu bɛ mɔgɔ dusu minɛ, fɛnba minnu ka bon—kɔɲɔmuso ka jalakibaliya, kɔrɔya ɲumanya; hali ni Ameriki cɛ bilenmanw cɛma, wampum cɛsirilan finman dili tun ye bonya layidu juguba ye; hali ni waati caman na, nɛrɛmuguma bɛ Tilenbaliya bonya jira Kiiritigɛla ka ermine kɔnɔ, wa a bɛ kɛ sababu ye ka masakɛw ni masamusow ka don o don cogoya la, sow bɛ minnu sama i n’a fɔ nɔnɔ; hali ni hali diinɛ minnu ka bon kosɛbɛ, olu ka gundo sanfɛtaw la, a kɛra Ala ka nɔgɔbaliya ni a ka fanga taamasyɛn ye; Pɛrɛsi tasuma batobagaw fɛ, tasuma finman min bɛ foroko la, o tun bɛ minɛ yɔrɔ senuma bɛɛ la sarakabɔlan kan; ani Gɛrɛkiw ka nsiirinw kɔnɔ, Jove belebele yɛrɛ kɛra farikolo ye misiden dɔ la min finman bɛ i n’a fɔ nɛnɛ; ani hali ni Iroquois bonyalenw fɛ, wulu finman senuma ka nɛnɛ cɛmancɛ saraka tun ye u ka diinɛko seli senumanba ye ka tɛmɛ tɔw bɛɛ kan, o danfɛn nɔgɔlen, kantigi min tun bɛ ciden saniyalenba minɛ, u tun bɛ se ka min ci Niba ma ni u yɛrɛ ka kantigiya kibaruya ye san o san ani hali ni a bɔra latigɛkan daɲɛ na min kɔrɔ ye fini jɛman ye, kerecɛn sarakalasebagaw bɛɛ bɛ u ka fini senuma yɔrɔ kelen tɔgɔ sɔrɔ, n’o ye alb walima fini ye, min bɛ don cassock jukɔrɔ ani hali ni Romɛkaw ka dannaya ka bonya senumaw cɛma, fini jɛman bɛ baara kɛ kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la an Matigi ka tɔɔrɔ seli la; hali ni Yuhana senuma ka yeli la, fini jɛmanw bɛ di kunmabɔlenw ma, ani maakɔrɔ mugan ni naani bɛ jɔ ni fini jɛman ye masasigilan finmanba ɲɛkɔrɔ, ani Senuma min sigilen bɛ yen ka fin i n'a fɔ wuluwulu o bɛɛ n’a ta, nin jɛɲɔgɔnya dalajɛlen ninnu bɛɛ la, ni fɛn o fɛn ye min ka di, ani min bonyalen don, ani min ka bon, fɛn dɔ bɛ yen hali bi min tɛ se ka sɔrɔ nin kulɛri in kɔnɔna na, min bɛ siran kosɛbɛ ni la ka tɛmɛ o bilenni kan min bɛ siran joli la.

O jogo min tɛ se ka sɔrɔ, o de bɛ kɛ sababu ye ka mɔgɔ finman miirili bila, n’a farala jɛkulu ɲumanw kan, ka fara fɛn o fɛn kan min ka jugu a yɛrɛ ma, o bɛ o siranba bonya fo ka se a dan na. Aw ye seereya kɛ ursi finman ye jirisunw na, ani jɛgɛ finman min bɛ yɔrɔw la minnu ka timi; mun tɛ u ka finman nɔgɔlen, nɛrɛmuguma, min b’u kɛ siranfɛnw ye minnu ka bon kosɛbɛ? O finman sirannin de bɛ o dususuma haramulen sugu lase u fan dɔ ka nisɔndiya bobo ma, hali min ka jugu ka tɛmɛn siranfɛn kan. Walasa jara fariman min bɛ a ka heraldik fini na, o kana se ka jagɛlɛya wuli i n’a fɔ ursi walima jɛgɛba min datugulen don ni fini jɛman ye.*

*Ka ɲɛsin ursi Polar ma, a bɛ se ka kɛ ko a bɛ se ka dusu don a kɔnɔ min bɛna fain ka taa a fɛ hali bi nin ko in na, ko finman tɛ, min bɛ jate danfara la, min bɛ o waraba ka dogoya muɲunbali bonya sabu, n’a sɛgɛsɛgɛra, o dogolenba min bonyalen don, a bɛ se k’a fɔ ko a bɛ wuli dɔrɔn ka bɔ ko kɛlen na, ko danfɛn ka farinya kunkanko tɛ min na, o jɔlen bɛ sankolo jalakibaliya ni kanuya wuluwulu la o de kama, n’a ye o dusukunnata fila ɲɔgɔn sɔsɔ an hakili la, ursi Polar b’an lasiran ni danfara ye min tɛ danbe ye ten. Nka hali k’a miiri ko nin bɛɛ ye tiɲɛ ye; o bɛɛ n’a ta, ni finman tun tɛ, o siranba in tun tɛna kɛ i la.

O danfɛn in na, lafiɲɛbɔ jinɛ finman min bɛ jɛgɛn, n’a y’a ye a dusukunnata gansanw na, o bɛ jate cogo kabakoma na ni o jogo kelen ye Polar senna naani na. O danfara in bɛ Sɔrɔ kosɛbɛ Faransikaw fɛ u bɛ tɔgɔ min Di o jɛgɛ in ma. Romɛkaw ka misiri min bɛ kɛ mɔgɔ salenw ye, o bɛ daminɛ ni “Requiem eternam” (lafiɲɛbɔ banbali) ye, o yɔrɔ la, Requiem bɛ misiri yɛrɛ tɔgɔ da, ani sudon dɔnkili wɛrɛw. Sisan, k’a ɲɛsin saya makun finman ma, min bɛ nin jɛgɛba in kɔnɔ, ani a ka delinankow ka mɔgɔfaga nɔgɔman, Faransikaw b’a wele ko Requin .

I ka miiri albatross la, hakili ta fan fɛ kabako ni siran jɛman sankaba bɛ bɔ min, o jinɛ finman bɛ kurunba kɔnɔ min kɔnɔ miirili bɛɛ la? Coleridge ma o siginiden in fili fɔlɔ; nka Ala ka lauratigiba, min tɛ mɔgɔ ɲɛnajɛ, n’o ye Nature.* ye.

*Ne hakili bɛ albatross fɔlɔ la n ye min ye. O kɛra sanfiɲɛba dɔ waati jan kɔnɔ, ji gɛlɛnw na Antartiki kɔgɔjiw kan. Ka bɔ n ka wulada kɔlɔsili la duguma, n jiginna ka taa deki la min tun bɛ sankaba la; ani yen, n’ ye n yɛrɛ boli dawolo belebelebaw kan, n ye masakɛ fɛn dɔ ye, min ɲɛ ye nɛrɛ ye, nɔgɔ tɛ min na, ani min bɛ ni jɛgɛ ye, Romɛkaw ka bill sublime. Tuma dɔw la, a tun b’a ka mɛlɛkɛba kamanw kɔrɔta, i n’a fɔ a bɛ kurun senuma dɔw minɛ. Kabako wulikanw ni sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛw y’a lamaga. Hali ni farikolo ma tɔɔrɔ, a ye kulekan bɔ, i n’a fɔ masakɛ dɔw ka jinɛ min bɛ tɔɔrɔ la min ka bon ni hadamaden ta ye. A ɲɛw fɛ minnu tɛ se ka fɔ, minnu ye kabako ye, methought ne ye peeped to gundo minnu ye Ala minɛ. I ko Ibrahima mɛlɛkɛw ɲɛ kɔrɔ, ne ye ne biri. fɛn finman tun fin kosɛbɛ, a kamanw tun ka bon kosɛbɛ, wa o ji minnu tun bɛ jɔnya la fo abada, ne tun bɔnɛna laadalakow ni duguw hakilijigin juguw la. Waati jan ne ye o prodigy of plumage filɛ. N tɛ se k’a fɔ, n bɛ se k’a jira dɔrɔn, fɛn minnu ye n tɛmɛ n fɛ o waati la. Nka a laban na, ne kununna; a y'i kɔsegin, ka kuruntigi dɔ ɲininka nin ye kɔnɔ jumɛn ye. A goney, a y'a jaabi. Goney ye! a tun ma deli ka o tɔgɔ mɛn fɔlɔ; yala a bɛ se ka miiri ko nin nɔɔrɔko in tɛ dɔn fewu cɛw fɛ kɔgɔjida la wa! abada! Nka waati dɔ o kɔfɛ, n y’a dɔn ko goney ye kɔgɔjila mɔgɔ dɔw tɔgɔ ye min bɛ fɔ albatross ma. Walasa cogo si la, Coleridge ka kungo-kɔnɔ-Rhyme tun tɛ se ka foyi kɛ ni o gundolafɛnw ye minnu tun ye ne ta ye, tuma min na ne ye o kɔnɔnin ye an ka kurunba kan. Sabu ne fana tun ma Rhyme kalan o waati la, wa ne tun t’a dɔn fana ko kɔnɔ in ye albatross ye. O bɛɛ n’a ta, nin fɔli la, n b’a Kɛ nka n bɛ poyi ni poyikɛla ka nafa bonyalen yeelen dɔɔnin dɔɔnin cogo la min tɛ ɲɛ.

O la sa, n b’a Jira ko kɔnɔ in farikolo-ɲɛnajɛ kabakoma de la, o bɛ fɛnɲɛnɛma gundo dogo kosɛbɛ; tiɲɛ dɔ bɛ ye ka caya nin ko in na, ko daɲɛw kelen-kelen bɛɛ fɛ, kɔnɔw bɛ yen minnu bɛ wele ko albatros gris; ani ninnu ne ye minnu ye tuma caman, nka ne ma deli ka ye ni dusukunnataw ye i n’a fɔ ne ye Antartiki kɔnɔw ye tuma min na.

Nka, o gundolafɛn tun minɛna cogo di? Aw kana a sɔgɔsɔgɔ, ne bɛna o fɔ; ni janfa juru ni juru ye, i n'a fɔ kɔnɔw tun bɛ pan kɔgɔji kan cogo min na. A laban na, Kapiteni ye positakɛla dɔ kɛ a la; ka jatebla sɛbɛnnen dɔ siri a kɔ la, ni kurunba waati ni a yɔrɔ ye; ka tila k’a to a ka boli. Nka ne tɛ sigasiga, o jateden farilajɛlen, min kɔrɔ tun ye hadamaden ye, o bɔra Sankolo la, tuma min na kɔnɔ finmanw wulila ka fara serubɛnw kan, minnu bɛ u wele, ani minnu bɛ u bato!

Min tɔgɔ bɔra kosɛbɛ an ka Tlebi jamanaw ka tarikusɛbɛnw kɔnɔ ani Ɛnduw ka laadalakow la, o ye White Steed of the Prairies ta ye; chargeur cɛɲi dɔ, n’a ɲɛ ka bon i n’a fɔ nɔnɔ, a kunkolo ka dɔgɔ, a disi bɛ bluff, ani ni masakɛ ba kelen ka bonya ye a ka wotoro sanfɛla la, min bɛ mɔgɔ mafiɲɛya. Ale tun ye Zerxes sugandilen ye kungo sow kulubaw la, minnu ka baganmarayɔrɔw tun bɛ kogo o waatiw la Rocky Mountains ni Alleghanies dɔrɔn de fɛ. U kunkolo tasumaman na a y'a Sɔrɔ tilebin fɛ i n'a fɔ o dolow sugandilen min bɛ Taa wulada o wulada yeelenba-kuluw kan. A wuluwulu jikuruba min bɛ manamana, a kɔkili kurulen, o y’a bila ni sow ye minnu bɛ manamana ka tɛmɛ sanu ni warijɛ-bɔlaw tun bɛ se ka minnu di a ma. O diɲɛ min ma bin, tilebin yanfan fɛ, o jiracogo min ye masakɛ ye kosɛbɛ ani min ye mɛlɛkɛba ye, min ye o waati fɔlɔw nɔɔrɔw lakunu, n’o ye jatigɛwalekɛlaw ni kungokɔnɔfɛnw ɲininikɛlaw ye, waati min na Hadama tun bɛ taama ni bonya ye i n’a fɔ ala, a ɲɛda tun bɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ ani a tun tɛ siran i n’a fɔ nin so barikama in. A kɛra taama ye a dɛmɛbagaw ni marifatigiw cɛma, kulu jatebaliw ka wotoro kɔnɔ, minnu tun bɛ a labɔ kɛnɛbaw kan dan tɛ min na, i n’a fɔ Ohio; walima n’a ka lamini mɔgɔw bɛ ka u lamini bɛɛ lajɛ sanfɛla la, So finman y’u lajɛ ni boli ye ni nugu sumalenw ye minnu bɛ bilen a ka nɔnɔ nɛnɛman fɛ; a y’a yɛrɛ jira fan o fan fɛ, tuma bɛɛ Ɛndu jagɛlɛnw fɛ, a tun ye bonya ni siranfɛn ye min bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ. A tɛ se ka ɲininkali kɛ fana ka bɔ fɛn na min bɛ nin soba in ka nsiirinw kɔnɔ, ko a ka hakilimaya finman de tun don kosɛbɛ, o de y’a don alaɲɛsiran na ten; ani ko o alaɲɛsiran in tun bɛ ni min ye, hali ni a tun bɛ bato yamaruya, o waati kelen na, a tun bɛ siranfɛn dɔ wajibiya min tɔgɔ tɛ.

Nka ko wɛrɛw bɛ yen nin nɛrɛmuguma in bɛ bɔnɛ o fɛnɲɛnɛma ni nɔɔrɔ kabakoma bɛɛ la min b’a bila So jɛman ni Albatross la.

Mun de bɛ Albino la cɛ bɛ ɲɛ gɛn cogo kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen na ani ka to ka a kabakoya, i n’a fɔ tuma dɔw la a yɛrɛ somɔgɔw b’a kɔniya! O nɛrɛmuguma de b’a bila, fɛn min bɛ Fɔ a tɔgɔ fɛ. Albino dilannen don ka ɲɛ i n’a fɔ cɛ tɔw — a tɛ ni farikolo tiɲɛnen ye kosɛbɛ — o bɛɛ n’a ta, nin finman min bɛ yɔrɔ bɛɛ, o fan dɔrɔn de b’a kɛ mɔgɔ dogolen ye cogo kabakoma na ka tɛmɛ kɔnɔtiɲɛ juguba kan. Mun na o ka kan ka kɛ ten?

Ani fana, fan wɛrɛw la, Nature in a ka baarakɛda minnu tɛ se ka ye kosɛbɛ nka a tɛ kɛ ni juguya dɔgɔman ye, olu tɛ dɛsɛ k’a ka fanga cɛma nin jogo juguba in sɛbɛn a ka fanga cɛma. Ka bɔ a ka nɛnɛ fan fɛ, Saheli Kɔgɔjiw ka jinɛ gauntleted tɔgɔ dara ko White Squall. Tariku ko dɔw la, hadamadenw ka juguya seko fana ma bɔ dɛmɛnan barikama ye ten. A bɛ o tɛmɛsira nɔ bonya cogo min na Froissart dugu kɔnɔ, tuma min na, u ka kulu taamasiyɛn nɛnɛma min bɛ u masiri, Ghent ka wuluwulu jɛmanw jigitigɛlenw b’u ka kafo faga sugu la!

Fɛn dɔw fana na, hadamadenw bɛɛ ka ko kɛlen minnu bɛ kɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ, minnu bɛ bɔ ciyɛn na, olu tɛ se ka seereya kɛ o kulɛri in ka tɛmɛn hadamadenw ka seko kan. Siga tɛ se ka kɛ kosɛbɛ, ko jogo kelen min bɛ ye suw fan fɛ min bɛ filɛlikɛla lasiran kosɛbɛ, o ye marifa jɛman ye min bɛ to yen; i n’a fɔ tiɲɛ na, o jɛman in bɛ i n’a fɔ diɲɛ tɔ kelen kɔnɔ, i n’a fɔ siranba min bɛ mɔgɔ faga yan. Wa ka bɔ o suw ka jɛman in na, an bɛ juru ta an bɛ u siri fini min na, o ɲɛda cogoya jiracogo la. Hali an ka dannakow la fana, an tɛ dɛsɛ ka o nɛnɛ fini kelen in fili an ka fantow lamini na; jinɛw bɛɛ bɛ wuli nɔnɔ finman sisi kɔnɔ—Ɔwɔ, k’a sɔrɔ o siranfɛnw b’an minɛ, an k’a fara a kan, ko hali siranfɛnw masakɛ, n’a kɛra mɔgɔ ye kibaru duman fɔbaga fɛ, a bɛ boli a ka so jɛman kan.

O la, a ka dusukunnata tɔw la, a b’a fɛ fɛn belebeleba walima nɛɛma fɛn o fɛn taamasyɛn ye ni fini jɛman ye, cɛ si tɛ se ka ban ko a nafa juguba la, a bɛ jiracogo kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnen dɔ wele ni ma.

Nka hali ni sɔsɔli tɛ nin ko in na, hadamaden salen ka kan ka jaabi di o ma cogo di? Ka a sɛgɛsɛgɛ, a bɛna kɛ i n’a fɔ a tɛ se ka kɛ. O la sa, yala an bɛ se ka o ko dɔw fɔ, n’o ye ko nin fɛn finman in — hali ni waati dɔ kɔnɔ, a bɛɛ lajɛlen walima a fanba bɔra jɛɲɔgɔnya tilennenw bɛɛ la minnu jatera walasa ka siranfɛn dɔ lase a ma, nka o bɛɛ n’a ta, a bɛ sɔrɔ ko a bɛ o jinɛweleli kelen in kɛ an kan, hali n’a b’a sɛmɛntiya cogo o cogo

An k’a ɲini. Nka nin ko in ɲɔgɔn na, nɔgɔlenya bɛ fɛn nɔgɔlen de diya, wa ni miirili tɛ, cɛ si tɛ se ka tugu mɔgɔ wɛrɛ kɔ ka don o boonw kɔnɔ. Wa hali ni siga t’a la, hakilina minnu tun bɛna jira, olu dɔw dɔgɔyalenba la, cɛ fanba tun bɛ se ka minnu fɔ, o bɛɛ n’a ta, n’a sɔrɔ mɔgɔ damadɔw tun bɛ u hakili bɛɛ dɔn o waati la, o de kama, n’a sɔrɔ u tun tɛ se k’u hakili jigin sisan.

Mun na mɔgɔ min ye hakilina ɲuman kalanbali ye, n’a kɛra cogo o cogo, a bɛ o don jogo danfaralen dɔn kosɛbɛ, Whitsuntide marshal kofɔli gansan bɛ kɛ o cogo la, o taama janw, minnu bɛ mɔgɔ dusu kasi, minnu tɛ se ka kuma, minnu bɛ taa dɔɔnin dɔɔnin, minnu bɛ jigin ani minnu datugulen don ni nɛnɛ kura binna ye? Walima, cɛmancɛ-Amerika jamanaw ka Protɛstan min ma kalan, n’a ma se ka fɛn caman dɔn, mun na, n’a fɔra ka tɛmɛn, n’o ye Friar White walima Nun finman dɔ ye, o bɛ o ja ɲɛntan sugu lawuli ni kɔnɔ?

Walima mun bɛ yen n’a bɔra kɛlɛcɛw ni masakɛw ka laadalakow la minnu bɛ kasobon kɔnɔ (minnu tɛna a ɲɛfɔ pewu) min b’a to Lɔnduru sankanso jɛman bɛ fɛn caman fɔ kosɛbɛ Ameriki mɔgɔ dɔ ka miirili kan min ma taama, ka tɛmɛ o maana jɔli tɔw kan, a sigiɲɔgɔnw kan — Byward sankanso, walima hali Bloody? Wa o sankansobaw minnu ka bon kosɛbɛ, n’olu ye New Hampshire kulu jɛmanw ye, o jinɛba in bɛ bɔ yɔrɔ min na, ni dusukunnata kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw ye, ni kan o tɔgɔ fɔli gansan na, k’a sɔrɔ Virginia ka Blue Ridge miirili falen bɛ sugo nɔgɔlen dɔ la, nɛnɛ bɛ min na, min yɔrɔ ka jan? Walima mun na, latitude ni longitude bɛɛ mana kɛ min o min ye, Kɔgɔji Finman tɔgɔ bɛ o ɲɔgɔnna spectralness kɛ fancy kan, k’a sɔrɔ Kɔgɔji jɛman tɔgɔ bɛ an lasun ni miirili sataw ye, minnu bɛ kɛ wulada jan nɔgɔlenw na minnu bɛ kɛ ni lacquered ye jikuruw kan, o kɔfɛ, tilebin minnu ka bon kosɛbɛ, nka minnu bɛ sunɔgɔ kosɛbɛ? Walima, n’an bɛ misali dɔ sugandi min tɛ fɛnba ye fewu, min ɲɛsinnen bɛ fɛnɲɛnɛmaw dɔrɔn ma, mun na, Cɛma Erɔpu jamanaw ka nsiirin kɔrɔw kalanni na, Hartz kungow ka “cɛ janyalen jɛman” bɛ Hartz kungow kɔnɔ, min ɲɛda jɛman min tɛ Changé, o bɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ ni kɔnɔnafili ye kungow ka jiriw cɛma — mun na nin jinɛ in ka jugu ka tɛmɛ Blocksburg ka wuluwuluw bɛɛ kan?

A bɛɛ lajɛlen fana tɛ, a ka dugukolo yɛrɛyɛrɛw hakilijigin ye minnu ye katedrali tiɲɛ; a ka kɔgɔjiw ka sɛgɛnw fana tɛ; Sankolo jalen ɲɛjibɔbaliya fana tɛ sanji min na abada; a ka foro belebeleba fana yeli ye, n’o ye jirisunw ye minnu sirilen don, n’o ye kope-kabakurunw ye minnu sirilen don, ani jirisunw bɛɛ sɔgɔlen (i n’a fɔ kurunba minnu sirilen bɛ kurunba jɔlenw ka yards canted); ani a ka dugu kɔkan siraw, so kogow dalen bɛ ɲɔgɔn kan, i n’a fɔ kariti pake wulilen;—o fɛnw dɔrɔn tɛ Lima kɛ ɲɛji tɛ min na, dugu kabakoma, dusukasilenba i tɛ se ka min ye. Sabu Lima ye fini jɛman ta; ani siranba dɔ bɛ a ka bɔnɛ finman in na. Kɔrɔlen i n’a fɔ Pizarro, o finman in b’a ka tiɲɛniw mara fo abada; a tɛ sɔn a ma ko a bɛ toli dafalen na ni nisɔndiya ye; a bɛ a ka kogo karilenw kan, apoplexie (banakisɛfagalan) dɔ ka jɛman gɛlɛn min b’a yɛrɛ ka fɛn tiɲɛnenw labɛn.

N b’a dɔn ko, ka kɛɲɛ ni mɔgɔw bɛɛ ka siran ye, nin finmanko in tɛ sɔn a ma ko a ye fɛn fɔlɔ ye min bɛ fɛnw siran damatɛmɛ, n’o tɛ, minnu ka jugu kosɛbɛ; hakili min tɛ miirili kɛ fana, siran foyi tɛ o yecogo ninnu na minnu ka juguya hakili wɛrɛ ma, a bɛ se ka fɔ ko o bɛ kɛ nin ko kelen in dɔrɔn de la, kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenya la n’u jirala cogo si la hali dɔɔnin min bɛ gɛrɛ boboya la walima diɲɛ bɛɛ ta la. N b’a fɛ ka min Fɔ nin kuma fla ninnu na, n’a sɔrɔla o bɛ Se ka ɲɛfɔ u kelen-kelen bɛɛ la nin misali nataw fɛ.

Fɔlɔ : Kɔgɔjida la, n’a bɛ gɛrɛ jamana wɛrɛw ka jamanaw kɔgɔjida la, ni su fɛ, a ye karibagaw ka mankan mɛn, a bɛ daminɛ ka kɔlɔsili kɛ, wa a bɛ siran fo ka se k’a seko bɛɛ nɔgɔya nka o cogo kelen na tigitigi, a ka wele ka bɔ a ka hamakɛ la walasa k’a ka kurun lajɛ a bɛ ka tɛmɛ su tilancɛ kɔgɔji kɔnɔ min falen bɛ nɔnɔ finman na — i n’a fɔ ka bɔ kunkolow lamini na, ursi finmanw sɔgɔlenw ka kuluw bɛ ka wuli k’a lamini, o kɔfɛ, a bɛ siran dɔ sɔrɔ min ma kuma, dannayabaliya ji finmanw ka fantom min datugulen don, o ka jugu a ma i n’a fɔ jinɛ lakika; fu la, ɲɛmɔgɔya b'a hakili sigi ko a bɛ mankanw la hali bi; dusukun ni kurunba u fila bɛɛ bɛ jigin; a tɛ lafiɲɛ abada fo ji bulu ka don a kɔrɔ tugun. O bɛɛ n’a ta, kuruntigi min bɛna a fɔ i ye ko: “Matigi, fara dogolenw gosili siranya tun tɛ, nka o finman dogolen siran de ye ne lamaga kosɛbɛ?”

Filanan: Peru Ɛndiyɛn jamanaden fɛ, Andes bajiw yecogo min bɛ kɛ tuma bɛɛ, nɛnɛ bɛ min na, o tɛ siran foyi jira, fo, laala, nɛnɛ banbali lakolon min bɛ fanga la o yɔrɔ janbaw la, o miirili dɔrɔn na, ani yɛrɛbonya dabɔlen min b’a jira ko siranba bɛna kɛ siran ye cogo min na ka i yɛrɛ tunun o kelennasigi suguw la minnu tɛ hadamadenya ye. O cogo kelen de bɛ Tlebi yanfan kɔfɛjirila fana na, min bɛ ni ɲɛmajɔbaliya ye ni a bɛ dan tɛ min na, nɛnɛ min bɛ boli, jiri walima jiribolo biɲɛ tɛ yen walasa ka finmanw ka sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ jɔlen tiɲɛ. O tɛ, kurunbolila, a bɛ Antartiki kɔgɔjiw cogoya lajɛ; yɔrɔ min na tuma dɔw la, legerdemain ka jahanama namara dɔw fɛ nɛnɛ ni fiɲɛ fanga la, ale, min bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ ani kurun tilancɛ tiɲɛnen, sanni sanjiba ka jigiya ni dususalo kuma a ka tɔɔrɔ la, a bɛ fɛn ye min bɛ i n’a fɔ egilisi dan tɛ min na, o bɛ ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ a kan n’a ka jikuru kabakomaw n’a ka kuruwa tigɛlenw ye.

Nka i b'a fɔ ko, methinks ko finman-plɔmu sapitiri min bɛ kuma finman kan, o ye darapo finman dɔrɔn de ye min sirilen bɛ ni craven dɔ la; i b'i yɛrɛ di hypo dɔ ma, Ismaɛl.

A fɔ n ye, mun na nin faliden barikama in, min ye den sɔrɔ Vermont kɔdingɛ hɛrɛma dɔ la, min yɔrɔ ka jan baganw bɛɛ la — mun na o bɛ kɛ ten don min na tile ka bon kosɛbɛ, n’i ye misi fini kura yɛrɛyɛrɛ a kɔfɛ, fo a yɛrɛ kana se k’a ye, nka a bɛ a kungosogo muski kasa mɛn dɔrɔn — mun na a bɛna daminɛ, ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ, ani ni ɲɛw pɛrɛnnaw ye ka dugukolo sen don a kɔnɔ phrensies de siran? Hakili tɛ kungo kɔnɔ fɛnɲɛnamaw ka goring si la a ka worodugu so jɛman kɔnɔ, fo a bɛ muskiness kabakoma min kasa mɛn, o tɛ se ka hakili jigin fɛn si la min bɛ tali kɛ farati kɔrɔw sɔrɔli la sabu mun b’a dɔn, nin Angletɛri kura faliden in, Oregon yɔrɔjan bison nɛrɛmaw la?

Ayi: nka yan, i bɛ hali waraba bobo dɔ la, jinɛ dɔnniya hakilina ye diɲɛ kɔnɔ. Hali ni a ni Oregon cɛ ye kilomɛtɛrɛ ba caman ye, hali bi n’a ye o musomannin kungosogo kasa mɛn, bison kuluw bɛ yen minnu bɛ tigɛtigɛ, minnu bɛ sɔgɔsɔgɔ, olu bɛ yen i n’a fɔ kungosogo lakolonw bɛ yen i n’a fɔ kungosogow kɔnɔ, n’a sɔrɔla u bɛ ka minnu sennatan nin yɔrɔnin bɛɛ la ka kɛ buguri ye.

O cogo la, o tuma na, kɔgɔji nɔnɔma dɔ ka wulikan mugulenw; kuluw nɛnɛba cɛɲiw ka mankan dibimanw; kungo-kɔnɔ-kɛnɛw nɛnɛ fiyɛlenw ka jiginni lakolonw; ninnu bɛɛ , Ismaɛl fɛ , u bɛ i n' a fɔ o misiden fini yɛrɛyɛrɛli faliden sirannen ma !

Hali ni u fila si t’a dɔn fɛn tɔgɔ tɛ minnu na, gundo taamasiyɛn bɛ o hakilina suguw di minnu kan; o bɛɛ n'a ta, ne fɛ, i n'a fɔ faliden, yɔrɔ dɔ la, o fɛnw ka kan ka kɛ yen. Hali n’a fan caman na, a bɛ iko nin diɲɛ yelen in dabɔra kanuya de la, yɔrɔ yebaliw dabɔra siran fɛ.

Nka fɔlɔ an ma nin finman in ka lafilili ɲɛnabɔ, k’a dɔn mun na a bɛ diya ni o fanga ye; ani min ka kabako ka bon ani min ka bon kosɛbɛ—mun na, i n’a fɔ an y’a ye cogo min na, o ye hakili ta fan fɛ fɛnw taamasyɛn ye min kɔrɔ ka bon kosɛbɛ yɔrɔnin kelen na, a tɛ, Krecɛn ka Ala ka finimugu yɛrɛ; ani o bɛɛ n’a ta, a ka kan ka kɛ i n’a fɔ a bɛ cogo min na, fɛn min bɛ fanga di mɔgɔw ma fɛnw na minnu bɛ hadamadenw ka siran kosɛbɛ.

Yala a ka dan tɛ min na, a bɛ diɲɛ kɔnɔna yɔrɔ lankolonw ni a bonyabaliw sumaya, ka an sɔgɔ o cogo la ka bɔ kɔfɛ ni halakili miirili ye, n’an bɛ nɔnɔ sira juguman finmanw lajɛ wa? Walima, ko i n’a fɔ a yɛrɛ la, finman tɛ kulɛri ye kosɛbɛ, nka kulɛri tɛ yelen ye; ani o waati kelen na, bɛton min bɛ kɛ ni kulɛri bɛɛ ye; yala o kunw de kosɔn, o yɔrɔ lankolon bobo sugu dɔ bɛ yen, min falen bɛ kɔrɔ la, nɛnɛba yɔrɔba dɔ la — Ala dɔnbaliya min tɛ kulɛri ye, min ɲɛ bɛɛ ye kulɛri bɛɛ ye, an bɛ dɔgɔya ka bɔ min na wa? Wa n’an y’o miiriya wɛrɛ jateminɛ, min bɛ sɔrɔ fɛnɲɛnamaw ka dɔnnikɛlaw fɛ, ko dugukolo kan ɲɛgɛn tɔw bɛɛ — u bɛɛ lajɛlen ye fɛn ye min bɛ mɔgɔ ɲɛnajɛ walima min ka di — tilebin sankolo ni kungow ka dumuni dumanw ɔwɔ, ani wuluw ka velours sanulenw, ani npogotigininw ka wuluwulu ɲɛda; ninnu bɛɛ ye nanbara nɔgɔlenw dɔrɔn de ye, minnu tɛ fɛnw kɔnɔ tiɲɛ na, nka u bɛ bila kɛnɛma dɔrɔn; walasa Sigida alaɲɛsiranbali bɛɛ bɛ penti tigitigi i n’a fɔ jatɔmuso, min ka fɛnɲɛnɛmaw tɛ foyi datugu ni charnel-so tɛ a kɔnɔ; ani n’an taara ɲɛ, k’a jateminɛ ko gundola ɲɛgɛn min bɛ a ɲɛda kelen-kelen bɛɛ bɔ, yeelen sariyakoloba, fo abada, o bɛ to finman walima kulɛri tɛ min na a yɛrɛ la, wa ni a bɛ baara kɛ ni fɛn ye min tɛ fɛn ye, o bɛna maga fɛn bɛɛ la, hali tulipw ni rosow, n’a yɛrɛ ka ɲɛgɛn lankolon ye—ka miiri nin bɛɛ la, diɲɛ farigantɔ bɛ an ɲɛfɛ kunatɔ ani i n’a fɔ taamakɛlaw minnu b’a fɛ ka taama kɛ Lapland, minnu bɛ ban ka lunɛti kulɛrilenw ni kulɛrilenw don u ɲɛ na, o cogo kelen na, kafiri jugu in b’a yɛrɛ filɛ fiyentɔya la, o ye fini jɛman kabakoma ye min bɛ jigiya bɛɛ siri a la. Wa o fɛnw bɛɛ la, baleine Albino de tun ye taamasyɛn ye. Aw bɛ kabakoya tasuma ɲinini na wa?
