SABABU 33. Specksnyder ka mɔgɔw.

Balema-baarakɛlaw ko la, a bɛ iko nin ye yɔrɔ ɲuman ye i n’a fɔ yɔrɔ tɔw bɛɛ, walasa ka sokɔnɔna danfara dɔɔnin sigi kurun kɔnɔ, min bɛ bɔ sɔrɔdasi kulu harpooneer (harpooneer) ka sɔrɔ la, kulu min tɛ dɔn tiɲɛ na kɔgɔjida la ni balema-kurun tɛ.

Nafaba min bɛ harpooneer weleli la, o bɛ jira ni a ye, ko a daminɛ na Hollande mɔni kɔrɔ la, a bɛ san kɛmɛ fila ni kɔ bɔ, balema kurunba dɔ ɲɛmɔgɔya tun ma don pewu mɔgɔ la min bɛ wele sisan ko kapitɛni, nka a tun tilalen don a ni sɔrɔdasi dɔ cɛ min tɔgɔ ye ko Specksnyder. Daɲɛ kɔrɔ la, nin daɲɛ in kɔrɔ ye Fasa-Tigɛbaga; baarakɛcogo kɔni, waati tɛmɛnen kɔfɛ, a y’a kɛ ka bɛn Harpooneer kuntigi ma. O waatiw la, kapitɛni ka fanga tun bɛ dan kurun bolicogo n’a ɲɛnabɔli bɛɛ ma; k’a sɔrɔ balema ɲinini baarada n’a haminankow bɛɛ kan, Specksnyder walima Chief Harpooneer de tun bɛ fanga la. Angletɛri Gronland jɛgɛko la, Specksioneer tɔgɔ tiɲɛnen kɔnɔ, nin Hollande jamanatigi kɔrɔ in tora hali bi, nka a ka danbe kɔrɔ bɛ surunya dusukasi la. Sisan a bɛ jɔyɔrɔ la dɔrɔn i n’a fɔ Harpooneer kɔrɔba; ani o cogo la, a ye kapitɛni ka subalterns dɔgɔmanninw dɔ dɔrɔn de ye. O bɛɛ n’a ta, i n’a fɔ harponkɛlaw ka taamacogo ɲuman de kan, balema-ɲini taama ɲɛtaa bɛ bɔ kosɛbɛ, wa ikomi Ameriki jɛgɛko la, a tɛ sɔrɔdasi nafama dɔrɔn ye kurun kɔnɔ, nka ko dɔw la (su kɔlɔsili balema-ɲiniyɔrɔ la) kurunba kɔnɔko ɲɛmɔgɔya fana ye ale ta ye o de kama, kɔgɔji ka politiki kumasenba b’a ɲini, ko a ka kan ka sigi tɔgɔ la ka bɔ cɛw la minnu bɛ masɔrɔ ɲɛfɛ, ani ka danfara don cogo dɔ la i n’a fɔ u ka baarakɛlaw ɲɛmɔgɔ hali n’a y’a sɔrɔ tuma bɛɛ, u fɛ, u bɛ jate u ka sigida bɛnkan ye cogo min na.

Sisan, danfaraba min bɛ sɔrɔdasi ni cɛ cɛ kɔgɔji la, o ye nin ye — fɔlɔ bɛ balo kɔfɛ, laban bɛ ɲɛnamaya ɲɛfɛ. O la, balema-kurunw ni jagokɛlaw bɛɛ la, furuɲɔgɔnmaw bɛ u sigiyɔrɔ sɔrɔ kapitɛni fɛ; ani o cogo kelen na fana, Ameriki balemakɛla fanba la, harpooneerw bɛ sigi kurun kɔfɛyɔrɔ la. O kɔrɔ ye k’u b’u ka dumuni ta kapitɛni ka kabini kɔnɔ, ka sunɔgɔ yɔrɔ la k’u bɛ kumaɲɔgɔnya kɛ n’a ye cogo la min tɛ ɲɛ.

Hali ni Saheli balema ɲinini waati jan (a ka jan ni taama tɔw bɛɛ ye sisan walima hadamaden ye minnu kɛ fɔlɔ), a farati kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw, ani nafa jɛkulu min bɛ sɔrɔ tɔn dɔ cɛma, minnu bɛɛ, u ka bon walima minnu ka dɔgɔ, olu bɛɛ bɛ bɔ u ka tɔnɔw la, sara latigɛlen tɛ, nka u ka jɛkafɔ de kan, ka fara u ka kɔrɔsili jɛlen kan, u ka jagɛlɛya, ani u ka baara gɛlɛnw kan hali ni nin ko ninnu bɛɛ bɛ kɛ tuma dɔw la, u ka teli ka kololi dɔ bange min man gɛlɛn ka tɛmɛ jagokɛlaw kan caman na; o bɛɛ n’a ta, a kana kɛ abada ko nin balemakɛlaw bɛ se ka sigi ɲɔgɔn fɛ cogo min na i n’a fɔ Mezopotami denbaya kɔrɔ dɔ; o bɛɛ kama, kɛnɛmafɛnw, a dɔgɔyalenba la, quarter-deck (dakun naaninan) man teli ka lafiya farikolo ta fan fɛ, wa u tɛ ban cogo si la. Tiɲɛ na, u caman ye Nantucket kurunw ye, i bɛna kurunba ye minnu kɔnɔ, a bɛ ka a ka quarter-deck parade ni nisɔndiyaba ye min tɛ tɛmɛ sɔrɔdasi kɔgɔjidala si kan; ayi, ka kɛnɛma bonya caman ɲini i n’a fɔ a bɛ masakɛ ka fini jɛman don, wa a tɛ piloti fini nɔgɔlenba ye.

Wa hali ni cɛw bɛɛ la, Pequod kapitɛni min tun bɛ miiri kosɛbɛ, o de tun ka dɔgɔ o miirili sugu la min tun ka dun kosɛbɛ; ani hali ni bonya kelenpe min tun bɛ a la, o tun ye kanminɛli ye min bɛ kɛ cogo jɛlen na, min bɛ kɛ yɔrɔnin kelen; hali n'a tun t'a ɲini mɔgɔ si fɛ a ka sanbara bɔ a sen na sanni a ka senna-tɛgɛrɛ kɛ quarter-deck kan; ani hali ni waati dɔw tun bɛ yen, k’a sababu kɛ ko kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw ye minnu bɛ tali kɛ ko kɛlenw na kɔfɛ minnu bɛna ɲɛfɔ ka ɲɛ, a y’u ɲɛfɔ ni kumasenw ye minnu tɛ deli ka kɛ, a kɛra majigilenya ye wo, a kɛra terrorem ye wo, walima cogo wɛrɛ la o bɛɛ n’a ta, hali Kapiteni Akabu tun tɛ kɔgɔji cogoyaw n’a baarakɛcogo bɛɛ la belebeleba kɔlɔsi cogo si la.

Laala, a tɛna laban fana k’a dɔn, ko o cogoyaw ni baarakɛcogo ninnu kɔfɛ, n’a bɛ fɔ cogo min na, a y’a yɛrɛ masike tuma dɔw la; a kɛra cogo o cogo, ka baara kɛ n’u ye kuntilenna wɛrɛw kama ani kuntilenna wɛrɛw kama minnu ka ca ni u tun laɲinin ye ka baara kɛ ni u ye sariya siratigɛ la. O a hakili sultanism dɔ, min tun bɛ cogo wɛrɛ la cogo ɲuman na, o ma jira; o cogoyaw sababu fɛ o sultanisme kelen in kɛra farikolo ye dictature (jamanatigiya) kɔnɔ min tɛ se ka bali. Bari ka kɛ cɛ ka hakili ta fan fɛ sanfɛla ye a bɛna min kɛ, a tɛ se ka kɛ abada ka fanga ta min bɛ se ka kɛ, min bɛ sɔrɔ cɛ tɔw kan, ni kɛnɛma seko ni dɔnko sugu dɔw dɛmɛni tɛ, tuma bɛɛ, u yɛrɛ la, ka caya walima ka dɔgɔ, ka kɛ fɛn fitinin ye ani ka kɛ basigi ye. Nin de ye, min bɛ Ala ka Mansamara kuntigi lakikaw tanga diɲɛ ka bɔnɛw ma fo abada; ani ka bonyaba to o fiɲɛ bɛ se ka min di, cɛ minnu bɛ tɔgɔ bɔ kosɛbɛ u ka dɔgɔya danma fɛ ka tɛmɛn Ala ka Inert bolo dogolen sugandilen kan, ka tɛmɛ u ka sanfɛla salen kan siga t’a la ka tɛmɛ jama hakɛ salen kan. O jogo ɲumanba suguw bɛ dogo o fɛn misɛnnin ninnu na ni politiki dannayabaliya juguw bɛ u bila, fo masakɛ ka ko dɔw la hali hakilintan nalonmaw la, u ye fanga di u ma. Nka tuma min na, i n’a fɔ Nikola Zar ta, dugukoloko mansamara masafugulan bololanɛgɛ bɛ mansamara hakili dɔ lamini; o kɔfɛ, plebeian sagakuluw bɛ u kunbiri gwan ka kɔn cɛmancɛlafanga belebeleba ɲɛ. Dramakɛla dusukasibaga fana min tun bɛna mɔgɔ salenw ka sebaliya jira a ka sɛgɛsɛgɛli dafalen na ani a ka wulicogo tilennen na, o tɛna ɲinɛ abada hakilina dɔ kɔ, min nafa ka bon kosɛbɛ a ka seko la, i n’a fɔ a bɛ fɔ min kan sisan.

Nka Akab, n ka Kapiteni, bɛ lamaga hali bi ne ɲɛfɛ a ka Nantucket grimness ni shagginess bɛɛ la; ani nin yɔrɔ in na min bɛ maga Mansamaraw ni Masakɛw la, ne man kan k’a dogo ko ne bɛ tali kɛ ale bɔɲɔgɔnko balema-ɲinina kɔrɔ faantan dɔ dɔrɔn de la; o de kosɔn, kɛnɛma bonya jaanw ni sow bɛɛ bɛ ban ne la. Oh, Akabu! min bɛna kɛ fɛnba ye i kɔnɔ, o ka kan ka bɔ sankolo la, ka jigin ka taa a fɛ dugujukɔrɔ, ka jira fiɲɛ farikolontan na!





SABABU 34. Kabini-Tabali.

Tilelafana don; ani Dough-Boy, n’o ye sotigi ye, k’a ɲɛda buruburu jɛman bɔ kabini-sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ la, ka sufɛdumuni laseli a matigi n’a makɛ ma; min sigilen bɛ lee quarter-boat kɔnɔ, a bɛ ka tile kɔlɔsili kɛ sisan; ani sisan a bɛ ka latitude jate ni bobo ye tablɛti nɔgɔlen kan, min bɛ i n’a fɔ medayi, min maralen bɛ o don o don baara kama a sen sanfɛla la. K’a sababu kɛ a ma a janto o kibaruyaw la pewu, i bɛna a miiri ko Akab min dusu tiɲɛna, o ma a ka dɔgɔyali mɛn. Nka sisan, a kɛlen ka mizen ka finiw minɛ, a b’a yɛrɛ wuli ka taa deki la, ka kan kelen, min tɛ nisɔndiya, k’a fɔ ko “Sufɛdumuni, Mr. Starbuck,” a tununna kabini kɔnɔ.

Ni a ka sultan senna-tɛgɛnkan laban sara, ni sababu bɛɛ bɛ Starbuck, n’o ye Emir fɔlɔ ye, k’a miiri ko a sigilen bɛ, o tuma na, Starbuck bɛ kunun ka bɔ a ka lafiya la, ka wuli damadɔw kɛ pankurunw kan, kaburu dɔ filɛli kɔfɛ ka don binkɛnɛ kɔnɔ, k’a fɔ, ni magali duman dɔ ye, ko: “Sufɛdumuni, Mr. Stubb,” ka jigin scuttle kɔnɔ. Emir filanan bɛ lafiɲɛ dɔɔnin rigging ko la, ka sɔrɔ ka brace kunba lamaga dɔɔnin, k’a lajɛ n’a bɛna ɲɛ ni o juru nafama in ye, a fana bɛ doni kɔrɔ ta, wa ni teliya ye « Sufɛdumuni, Mr Flask », o bɛ tugu a ɲɛfɛmɔgɔw nɔfɛ.

Nka Emir sabanan, n’a y’a yɛrɛ ye sisan a kelen na quarter-deck kan, a bɛ iko a lafiyalen don ka bɔ ɲininkali dɔw la minnu bɛ a yɛrɛ minɛ; sabu, a bɛ dɔnniya ɲɛjibɔ sugu bɛɛ wuli fan bɛɛ fɛ, k’a ka sanbaraw bɔ, a bɛ burufiyɛkan dɔ gosi ka don Turkiba kunkolo sanfɛ yɛrɛ, nka mankan tɛ min na o kɔfɛ, ni sɛgɛnlafiɲɛbɔlan ŋana dɔ ye, k’a ka fini wuli san fɛ ka don mizentop kɔnɔ walasa ka tabali dɔ kɛ, a bɛ jigin ka wuli ka taa fo yɔrɔ jan a dɔgɔyalenba la, n’a bɛ to ka ye ka bɔ kurunba kɔnɔ, ka taama tɔw bɛɛ kɔsegin, k’a kɛ kɔfɛla lase san fɛ ni dɔnkili ye. Nka sanni a ka sen don kabini da la min bɛ duguma, a bɛ jɔ dɔɔnin, ka ɲɛda kura bila kurun kɔnɔ pewu, o kɔfɛ, Flask fitinin yɛrɛmahɔrɔnyalen, nisɔndiyalen bɛ don masakɛ Akab ɲɛkɔrɔ, Abjectus, walima Jɔn jogo la.

Kɔgɔjida-baarakɛcogo kɛlenw ka kɛwalew kɛcogo jugu ye minnu lamɔ, o tɛ fɛn fitinin ye, k’a sɔrɔ k’u to kurunba kɔnɔ, sɔrɔdasi dɔw bɛna u yɛrɛ muɲu ni jagɛlɛya ye ani ni sɔsɔli ye u ka komandan fɛ o bɛɛ n’a ta, tan ni kelen, o sɔrɔdasi yɛrɛw ka jigin waati wɛrɛ la u ka laadala dumuni na o komandan kelen in ka so kɔnɔ, ka o yɔrɔnin bɛɛ u ka juguya kɛ, k’a fɔ ko fiɲɛ min bɛ mɔgɔ mafilɛ ni majigin ye a fan fɛ, k’a sigilen to tabali kun na nin ye kabako ye, tuma dɔw la, a bɛ kɛ nisɔndiyako ye kosɛbɛ. Mun na nin danfara in bɛ yen? Gɛlɛya dɔ don wa? Laala, o tɛ. Ka kɛ Babilonɛ masakɛ Bɛlsazar ye; ani ka kɛ Bɛlsazar ye, a ma kɛ kuncɛbaya ye, nka ni bonya ye, siga t’a la ko a ka kan ka kɛ ko diɲɛ bonya dɔ tun bɛ o kɔnɔ. Nka min b’a yɛrɛ ka dunan welelenw ka dumunikɛtabali ɲɛminɛ ni masaya ni hakilitigiya hakili ɲuman ye, o cɛ ka fanga ni a ka fanga min tɛ sɔsɔ, n’o ye mɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ ka fanga ye o waati kɔnɔ o cɛ ka masaya bɛ tɛmɛ Bɛlsazar ta kan, bawo Bɛlsazar tun tɛ mɔgɔba ye. Min ye a teriw dun siɲɛ kelen dɔrɔn, o ye Sesar ye min ye, o dege. O ye sosiyali czarship ka jatɔya ye min tɛ se ka muɲu. Sisan, n’i ye kuruntigi dɔ ka fanga ofisiyali fara o jateminɛ kan, o tuma na, ni jateminɛ ye, i bɛna o kɔgɔji-fɛnw danfaralen sababu sɔrɔ min kofɔra sisan.

A ka tabali kan, nɛgɛfinw tun bɛ min kan, Akabu tun bɛ ɲɛmɔgɔya ta i n’a fɔ kɔgɔji waraba bobo, min kunsigi tun bɛ a la, korali finman bajida la, a denw tun bɛ kɛlɛ la, nka u tun bɛ bonya da u kan hali bi. A yɛrɛ ka waati bɛnnen na, sɔrɔdasi kelen-kelen bɛɛ tun bɛ makɔnɔni kɛ walasa ka baara kɛ u ye. U tun bɛ iko denmisɛnninw ka kɔn Akabu ɲɛ. o bɛɛ n’a ta, Akabu kɔnɔ, a tun bɛ iko sigida kuncɛbaya fitinin tun tɛ yen. Ni hakili kelen ye, u ɲɛw laɲinitaw bɛɛ sirilen bɛ cɛkɔrɔba ka muru la, k’a sɔrɔ a bɛ minɛnba in ja a ɲɛfɛ. N t’a miiri ko diɲɛ ta fan fɛ, u tun bɛna o waati nɔgɔ ni kɔlɔsili fitinin ye, hali ni barokun ye min tɛ mɔgɔ si ta ye i n’a fɔ waati cogoya. Ayi! Wa tuma min na a y’a bolo bɔ a ka muru ni foroko la, misisogo tigɛlen tun sirilen bɛ minnu ni ɲɔgɔn cɛ, Akabu ye Starbuck ka tasa jira a fan fɛ o cogo la, furuɲɔgɔn y’a ka sogo sɔrɔ i n’a fɔ a bɛ saraka sɔrɔ ka a tigɛ ni dususuma ye; ani dɔɔnin daminɛna ni, n’a sɔrɔla, muru ye baganmara kɛ tasa la; k'a ɲimi mankan tɛ min na; a y'a munumunu, a ma kɛ k'a sɔrɔ a ma a janto a yɛrɛ la. Sabula, i n’a fɔ Masakɛya seli min kɛra Frankfort, Alemaɲi masakɛ bɛ dumuni kɛ yɔrɔ min na kosɛbɛ ni Mansamara Sugandilikɛla wolonwula ye, o cogo la, o kabini dumuniw tun ye dumuni ye cogo dɔ la, minnu tun bɛ dun makun juguba kɔnɔ o bɛɛ n'a ta, tabali kan, cɛkɔrɔba Akabu ma baro bali; ale yɛrɛ dɔrɔn de tun ye bobo ye. O kɛra lafiya ye dɛ Stubb sɔgɔli la, tuma min na soso dɔ ye bɔgɔdaga barikama kɛ minɛyɔrɔ la min bɛ duguma. Ani Flask fitinin faantan, ale tun ye denkɛ fitinin ye, ani cɛnin fitinin nin denbaya ka ɲɛnajɛ sɛgɛnnen in na. A ta tun ye misisogo kɔgɔlen senkɔniw ye; a ta tun bɛna kɛ dununw ye. Walasa Flask tun b’a miiri ko a b’a yɛrɛ dɛmɛ, n’a sɔrɔ o tun bɛ iko a bɛ bɛn sonyali ma degere fɔlɔ la. Ni a tun y’a yɛrɛ dɛmɛ o tabali kan, siga t’a la, a tun tɛna se k’a kun kɔrɔta ka tɛmɛ o kan abada nin diɲɛ lakika in kɔnɔ; o bɛɛ n'a ta, kabako don k'a fɔ ko Akabu ma a bali abada. Wa ni Flask tun y’a yɛrɛ dɛmɛ, a tun bɛ se ka kɛ ko Akabu tun ma deli k’o kɔlɔsi fo ka se a dan na. Min ka dɔgɔ ni o bɛɛ ye, yala Flask y’a yɛrɛ jate k’a yɛrɛ dɛmɛ ka tulu kɛ wa. Yala a tun b’a miiri ko kuruntigiw banna o la a ma, k’a sababu kɛ a ɲɛda jɛlen, tile bɛ min na, o sɔgɔli kosɔn; walima n’a y’a jate ko, taama jan in na o ji suguw la, tulu bɛ sɔngɔba la, o de kosɔn a tɛ a bolo, subaltern ye; o bɛɛ n'a ta, Flask, bɔnɛ ye a sɔrɔ! tun ye mɔgɔ ye min tɛ tulu sɔrɔ!

Fɛn wɛrɛ. Flask de tun ye mɔgɔ laban ye min jiginna sufɛdumuni na, ani Flask ye cɛ fɔlɔ ye min wulila. Ka jate! Sabu nin sababu fɛ Flask ka sufɛdumuni tun bɛ jam kosɛbɛ waati dɔ la. Starbuck ni Stubb fila bɛɛ de y’a daminɛ; o bɛɛ n’a ta, nɛɛma fana b’u bolo ka lafiɲɛ kɔfɛ. Ni Stubb yɛrɛ, min ka bon ni Flask ye peg kelen dɔrɔn, a kɛra cogo o cogo, dumuni nege fitinin dɔrɔn de bɛ a la, wa a ma mɛn, a ye a ka dumuni kuncɛ taamasiɲɛw jira, o tuma na, Flask ka kan k’a yɛrɛ wuli, a tɛna tɛmɛ da saba kan o don sabu a bɛ baara senuma kɛlɛ Stubb ka kɔn Flask ɲɛ ka taa deki la. O de kama a kɛra ko Flask sɔnna a ma siɲɛ kelen a dan na, ko kabini a wulila ka kɛ sɔrɔdasi danbe ye, kabini o waati, a ma deli k’a dɔn a ye min ye n’o tɛ kɔngɔ tɛ, ka caya walima ka dɔgɔya. Sabu a ye min dun, o ma kɔngɔ lajɔ kosɛbɛ, fo k'a to a la sa. Hɛrɛ ni wasa, Flask y’a miiri, o bɔra ne kɔnɔ fo abada. Ne ye sɔrɔdasi ɲɛmɔgɔ ye; nka, n b’a fɛ cogo min na n ka se ka misisogo kɔrɔ dɔɔni minɛ foro kɔnɔ, i n’a fɔ n tun b’a kɛ cogo min na n tun bɛ masɔrɔ ɲɛfɛ. Promotion denw bɛ yen sisan; nɔɔrɔ nafantan bɛ yen : ɲɛnamaya hakilintan bɛ yen ! O tɛmɛnen kɔ, n’a kɛra ko Pequod kurunbolila gansan o kuruntigi gansan tun bɛ Flask dimi Flask ka fanga la, o kuruntigi tun ka kan ka min kɛ, walasa ka waleɲumandɔn caman sɔrɔ, o tun ye ka taa kɔfɛ sufɛdumuni waati la, ka Flask lajɛ kabini sankolola yeelen fɛ, ka sigi hakilintan ni dusukasi la Akab jugu ɲɛkɔrɔ.

Sisan, Akabu n’a furuɲɔgɔn saba ye fɛn dɔ sigi sen kan min bɛ se ka wele ko tabali fɔlɔ Pequod ka so kɔnɔ. U bɔlen kɔ, k’a kɛ cogo la min bɛ wuli ka taa u nali la, finimugu min tun bɛ kɛ ni fini ye, o tun bɛ saniya, walima n’a fɔra ka ɲɛ, a seginna cogo kɔrɔtɔlen dɔ la, sotigi fariman fɛ. Wa o kɔfɛ, u ye harpooneer saba wele ka na seli in na, u kɛra a residuary legatees ye. U ye waati dɔɔnin baarakɛlaw ka so sugu dɔ kɛ kabini sanfɛla ni barikama in na.

O danfara kabakoma ni kapitɛni ka tabali ka gɛlɛya yebali ni tɔgɔ tɛ min na, o tun ye lase ni nɔgɔya ye min tɛ hami, o tun ye demokarasi ye min tun bɛ ɲini ka kɛ jɔrɔnanko ye, o mɔgɔ fitininw ka demokarasi min tun ye harpooneers ye. K’a sɔrɔ u makɛw, furuɲɔgɔnmaw, tun bɛ i n’a fɔ u bɛ siran u yɛrɛ ka dawolo sɔgɔlenw mankan ɲɛ, harpoonkɛlaw tun b’u ka dumuni ɲimi ni diyanye ye fo kunnafoni dɔ tun bɛ o kan. U ye dumuni kɛ i ko matigiw; u y’u kɔnɔbara fa i n’a fɔ Ɛndu kurunw tile bɛɛ ka doni kɛ ni tulumafɛnw ye. SABABU 35. Mast-Kunkolo.

Waati min tun ka di kosɛbɛ, o waati de la, n ka ɲɔgɔn falen-falen cogo bɛnnen na ni kɔgɔjidala tɔw ye, n ka masɔrɔ-kun fɔlɔ nana lamini.

Ameriki balemakɛla fanba la, masti-kunkolow bɛ baara kɛ waati kelen na ni kurun bɔli ye a ka kurunbonkarila la; hali ni a bɛ se ka kilomɛtɛrɛ ba tan ni duuru sɔrɔ, ani ka tɛmɛ o kan, ka kurun ta sani a ka se a ka kurunbokariyɔrɔ bɛnnen na. Wa ni, san saba, san naani walima duuru taama kɔfɛ, a bɛ ka gɛrɛ so ni fɛn lankolon dɔ bɛ a kɔnɔ—a fɔ, hali fiyɛlilan lankolon dɔ—o tuma na, a ka masɔrɔ-kunkolow bɛ mara ni mɔgɔ ye fo ka se laban ma ani fo a ka sankolola-kurunw ka don kurunbonkarila kuluw cɛma, a tɛ jigiya dabila pewu ka balema kelen wɛrɛ minɛ.

Sisan, i n’a fɔ jago min ye ka jɔ mast-kunkolow la, kɔgɔjida la walima ji la, o ye jago ye min kɔrɔlen don kosɛbɛ, wa a ka di, an ka dɔ fara a kan yan. N b’a ta, ko mast-kunkolo jɔbaga fɔlɔw tun ye Misira kɔrɔw ye; sabu, n ka ɲininiw bɛɛ la, n ma foyi sɔrɔ ka kɔn u ɲɛ. Sabu hali n’u bɛnbaw, Babɛl jɔbagaw, siga t’a la, u ka sankanso fɛ, u tun b’a fɛ ka masti kunkolo kɔrɔtalenba lamɔ Azi jamana bɛɛ la, walima Afiriki fana na; o bɛɛ n'a ta (sani kamiyɔn laban bilala a la) i n'a fɔ u ka kabakurunba min bɛ se k'a fɔ ko a tɛmɛna pankurun fɛ, Ala ka diminya fiɲɛba kɔnɔ; o de kosɔn , an tɛ se ka o Babɛl jɔbagaw bila jɔyɔrɔ fɔlɔ la Misirakaw kan . Wa ko Misirakaw tun ye jamana ye min tun bɛ jɔ ni masti kunkolo ye, o ye sɔsɔli ye min sinsinnen bɛ dannayaba kan min tun bɛ fɛnkɔrɔw dɔnbagaw cɛma, ko piramidi fɔlɔw sigira sen kan sankololafɛnw dɔnni kama: miiriya min bɛ dɛmɛ a kelen na ni o sow fan naani bɛɛ jɔcogo danma ye min bɛ i n’a fɔ ɛrɛzɛnsun o cogo la, n’u senw kɔrɔtacogo jan kabakomaw ye, o sankololafɛnw dɔnbaga kɔrɔw tun delila ka wuli ka taa u kuncɛ la, ka dɔnkili da dolow kura kama; hali i n’a fɔ bi kurunba kɔlɔsilikɛlaw bɛ dɔnkili da ka ɲɛsin kurunba ma, walima balema min bɛ ye dɔrɔn. Saint Stylites kɔnɔ, Krecɛn ermit tɔgɔba kɔrɔlen, min ye kabakurun jɔlan kɔrɔtalen dɔ jɔ a ye kungo kɔnɔ, k’a ka ɲɛnamaya laban bɛɛ kɛ a kuncɛ la, k’a ka dumuni kɔrɔta ka bɔ duguma ni minɛn ye ale kɔnɔ an bɛ misali kabakoma dɔ sɔrɔ min ye siranbaliya ye min bɛ jɔ-masti-kunkolow kan; min tun man kan ka gɛn ka bɔ a yɔrɔ la ni sisi walima nɛnɛ, sanji, sanbɛlɛ walima nɛnɛ ye; nka a kɛlen ka fɛn bɛɛ ɲɛnabɔ ni jagɛlɛya ye fo ka se laban ma, a sara tiɲɛ yɛrɛ la a ka jɔyɔrɔ la. Bi standers-of-mast-heads la an bɛ ni seti ɲɛnama ye dɔrɔn; kabakurun ni nɛgɛ ani nɛgɛso mɔgɔw dɔrɔn; minnu, hali n’u bɛ se ka fiɲɛba dɔ kunbɛn kosɛbɛ, hali bi, u tɛ se hali bi fewu dɔnkilida jago la, n’u ye yecogo kabakoma o kabakoma sɔrɔ. Napoléon bɛ yen; min, Vendome kulu sanfɛ, jɔlen bɛ ni a bolow da ɲɔgɔn kan, a bɛ se mɛtɛrɛ kɛmɛ ni bi duuru ɲɔgɔn ma fiɲɛ na; janto, sisan, jɔn bɛ dekiw mara duguma; a kɛra Louis Philippe ye, Louis Blanc ye wo, walima Louis Sitanɛ ye wo. Waʃintɔnnba fana jɔlen bɛ sanfɛ a ka jirisunba belebeleba kan Baltimore, wa i n’a fɔ Hɛrɛki ka jɔlan dɔ, a ka jɔn bɛ hadamadenw ka bonya yɔrɔ taamasiyɛn, mɔgɔ sataw damadɔ bɛna tɛmɛ min kan. Amiral Nelson, ale fana, marifa-nɛgɛ capstan dɔ kan, a jɔlen bɛ a ka mast-kunkolo la Trafalgar Square la; ani tuma o tuma ni o Lɔnduru sisi ye a datugu kosɛbɛ, taamasiyɛn bɛ di hali bi ko cɛfarin dogolen dɔ bɛ yen; sabu sisi bɛ yɔrɔ min na, tasuma ka kan ka kɛ yen. Nka Waʃintɔnnba, walima Napoléon, walima Nelson, tɛna sanbɛlɛ kelen jaabi ka bɔ duguma, hali n’u y’a wele cogo o cogo, u ka laadilikanw y’a wele cogo o cogo, u bɛ u ɲɛ jɔ deki minnu hakili ɲagamina, u ka teriya kɛ cogo o cogo; o bɛɛ n’a ta, a bɛ se ka miiri, k’u hakili bɛ don siniɲɛsigi sisiba kɔnɔ, ka bɔgɔdaga minnu ɲɛfɔ ani fara minnu ka kan ka kɔlɔsi.

A bɛ se ka kɛ ko a tɛ se ka kɛ ka dugukolo kan masti kunkolo jɔlenw ni kɔgɔji ta fara ɲɔgɔn kan cogo si la; nka ko tiɲɛ na, o tɛ ten, o bɛ jira ka jɛya fɛn dɔ fɛ, Obed Macy, n’o ye Nantucket tarikusɛbɛnbaga kelenpe ye, o bɛ jalaki min na. Obed min ka kan, o b’a fɔ an ye, ko balema mɔni daminɛ waatiw la, sanni kurunw tun bɛ bila tuma bɛɛ tulon in nɔfɛ, o gun mɔgɔw tun bɛ spars sanfɛtaw jɔ kɔgɔjida la, kɔlɔsilikɛlaw tun bɛ wuli ka taa minnu na ni cleats nɛgɛlenw ye, fɛn min bɛ kɛ i n’a fɔ kɔnɔw bɛ taa sanfɛ kɔnɔsogonin dɔ kɔnɔ. A san damadɔw filɛ nin ye, o labɛn kelen in kɛra Nuwɛlika jamana ka Bay balemakɛlaw fɛ, olu minnu ye tulon in ɲɛfɔ tuma min na, u ye kunnafoni di kurun labɛnnenw ma minnu bɛ kɔgɔjida la. Nka o laadalakow kɛra fɛn kɔrɔ ye sisan; an bɛ wuli o kɔfɛ ka taa masti-kunkolo kelen bɛnnen na, o min ye balema-kurun ye kɔgɔji la. Masti-kunkolo saba bɛ mara ni mɔgɔ ye k’a ta tile bɔli la fo ka se tilebin ma; kɔgɔjilamɔgɔw b’u ka ɲɔgɔn falen-falen kɛ tuma bɛɛ (i n’a fɔ kurunbonkarila la), ka ɲɔgɔn lafiya sanga fila o sanga fila. Dugukolo minnu ka timi waati lafiyalen na a ka di kosɛbɛ mast-head; ayi, sugokɛla hakilijakabɔla fɛ, o ka di. O yɔrɔ la, i jɔlen bɛ, mɛtɛrɛ kɛmɛ bɛ kurunbaw sanfɛ minnu ma kuma, i bɛ sennateliya ka taa duguma, i n’a fɔ jirisunw ye jirisunba belebelebaw ye, k’a sɔrɔ i jukɔrɔ ani i senw cɛ, i n’a fɔ a bɛ fɔ cogo min na, i bɛ kɔgɔji kɔnɔ waraba belebelebaw jigin, hali i n’a fɔ kurunw tun bɛ taa fɔlɔ Kolose tɔgɔba in ka butikiw cɛ Rode kɔrɔ la. O yɔrɔ la, i bɛ jɔ, i tununnen don kɔgɔji ka ɲɔgɔndan danmadɔw la, foyi tɛ wuli ka wuli ni jikuruw tɛ. Kurun min tigɛra, o bɛ girin ni sɛgɛn ye; jagokɛlaw ka fiɲɛw bɛ fiyɛ minnu bɛ sunɔgɔ; fɛn bɛɛ bɛ i ɲɛnabɔ ka kɛ languor ye. A fanba la, nin balema-ɲini-ɲɛnamaya in na, ko kɛlen tɛ min ka bon kosɛbɛ, o bɛ i bila; aw tɛ kibaruya si mɛn; gafe si kalan; extras ni kabako maanaw ye commonplaces kan, olu t’i lafili abada ka don nisɔndiya kunntanw na; aw tɛ sokɔnɔ tɔɔrɔ si mɛn; banki filili sɛbɛnw; stockw binni; tɛ jɔrɔ abada ni miirili ye i bɛna min sɔrɔ sufɛdumuni na—k’a masɔrɔ i ka dumuni bɛɛ min bɛ kɛ san saba kɔnɔ ani ka tɛmɛ o kan, o bɛ mara ka ɲɛ bidonw kɔnɔ, wa i ka wari sarata tɛ Changé.

O saheliyanfan balemakɛlaw dɔ la kelen na, san saba walima san naani taama jan kɔnɔ, i n’a fɔ a bɛ kɛ cogo min na tuma caman na, i bɛ lɛrɛ suguya minnu kɛ masti kunkolo la, olu bɛɛ lajɛlen bɛna kɛ kalo dafalen damadɔ ye. Wa, a ka kan ka dusukasi kosɛbɛ k’a ye ko i bɛ i ka ɲɛnamaya dafalen waati bɛɛ yɔrɔba dɔ di yɔrɔ min na, o ka kan ka kɛ yɔrɔ ye min tɛ ni dusukasi ye kosɛbɛ, fɛn o fɛn bɛ gɛrɛ sigiyɔrɔ duman na, walima min bɛ se ka ladamu walasa ka dusukunnataw sigida lafiyalen dɔ bange, i n’a fɔ min ɲɛsinnen bɛ dilan ma, hamakɛ, suwotoro, kɔlɔsilikɛsu, minɛnba, pankurun, walima o yɔrɔ fitininw ni fɛn nɔgɔlen wɛrɛw ma fɛɛrɛ minnu na cɛw b’u yɛrɛ mabɔ waati dɔɔnin kɔnɔ. I bɛ deli ka sigi yɔrɔ min na kosɛbɛ o ye t’ gallant-mast kun ye, i bɛ jɔ yɔrɔ min na bere finman fila kan minnu bɛ ɲɔgɔn ta (a bɛ se ka kɛ ko balemakɛlaw ka danfara don) minnu bɛ wele ko t’ gallant cross-trees. Yan, kɔgɔji kɛrɛfɛ, a daminɛbaga bɛ a yɛrɛ lafiya i n’a fɔ a bɛ kɛ cogo min na ni a jɔlen bɛ misi biɲɛw kan. Walasa ka da a la, nɛnɛ waati la, i bɛ se k’i ka so ta ka taa san fɛ n’i ye, k’a kɛ watɛsi ye; nka n’an y’a fɔ ka ɲɛ, kɔlɔsilikɛlan min ka bon kosɛbɛ, o tɛ so ye ka tɛmɛ farikolo kan min ma don; sabu i n’a fɔ ni nɔrɔlen bɛ a ka sogo fanibugu kɔnɔ cogo min na, wa a tɛ se ka yaala-yaala a kɔnɔ hɔrɔnya la, wa a tɛ se ka bɔ a kɔnɔ yɛrɛ, k’a sɔrɔ a ma faratiba lase a ma ka halaki (i n’a fɔ taamakɛla nalonma min bɛ nɛnɛba Alpew tigɛ nɛnɛ waati) o la sa, watɛsi tɛ so ye kosɛbɛ, nka a ye anwɛlɛki dɔrɔn ye, walima fari wɛrɛ min bɛ i datugu. I tɛ se ka etajɛri walima fɛnmarayɔrɔ dɔ bila i fari la, wa i tɛ se ka i ka watɛsi-kɔlɔsilikɛlan kɛ fɛn nɔgɔman ye tugun.

Nin bɛɛ ko la, a bɛ mɔgɔ dusu kasi kosɛbɛ k’a ye ko saheliyanfan balema kurunba dɔ ka kurunba kunkolow tɛ ni o fanibugu fitininw walima o minɛn fitininw ye minnu bɛ se ka keleya, n’u bɛ wele ko crow’s-nest , minnu kɔnɔ, Gronland balemakɛla dɔ ka kɔlɔsilikɛyɔrɔw bɛ lakana ka bɔ kɔgɔji nɛnɛmaw ka waati juguw la.
