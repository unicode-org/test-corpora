"Utayoowi lino," wakaamba, kumwi kamweta-mweta alimwi, "Queequeg awa takonzyi kuchisa masusu aamutwe wako."

"Leka kumweta-mweta," ndakakoongolola, "mpawo nkamboonzi nchootana ndaambila kuti ooyo sikuuma nkoli wakali sikulya bantu?"

"Ndakali kuyeeya kuti ulizi;—sena teensi ndakakwaambila, wakali sikusambala mitwe mudolopo?—pele kojokela alimwi koona. Queequeg, langa aawa—webo wandi sabbee, Ime sabbee—yebo oyu mwaalumi wakakoona—yebo sabbee?"

"Nda sabbee chiindi chilamfu" - wakatongooka Queequeg, kumwi kafwusa mupaipi wakwe mpawo wakakkala abulo.

"Konjila," wakayungizya, kunditondezya a tomahawk yakwe, mpawo wakasowa zizwaato kulubazu lumwi. Wakachita eezi kutali buyo munzila yabulemu pesi munzila yaluzyalo. Ndakaima kandimulanga kwakaindi kaniini. Kuli ma tattoo aakwe oonse wakali muntu uusalala, uuboneka kabotu uulya bantu. Ino ninzi zyoonse eezyi nzyondakachita, ndakayeeya muli ndime—mwaalumi oyu muntu mbweenya mbuli ndime: ulijisi twaambo tunji twakundiyoowa, mbweenya mbuli mbwendeelede kumuyoowa. Kuli kabotu koona amuntu uulya bantu uutakolwi kwiinda Munakristo uukoledwe.

"Mukamwini ŋanda," ndakaamba, "komwaambila kuti abike tomahawk yakwe aawo, naa paipi, naa kufumbwa mbomwiita; komwaambile kuti aleke kufweba, mubufwiifwi, mpawo ndiyoojokela anguwe. Pesi tandiyandi kuba amwaalumi uufweba andime. Nchintu chengozi. Kunze lyaboobo, tandijisi inshuwarensi pe."

Eezi nizyakaambilwa kuli Queequeg, wakazumina mpawo, mpawo wakanditondezya abulemu kuti ndilale—wakavwunguluka kulubazu lomwe mbuli kuti—“Nsikwe kujata kuulu kwako.”

"Koona kabotu, mukamwini ŋanda," ndakaamba, "ulakonzya kweenda."

Ndakajokela, mpo kunyina nindakoona kabotu mubuumi bwangu.
