Maanzi aatuba aakali kuzyana aakapangwa akutandaanya lino akali kuyaa kulibonya, akaambo kakuvwula kwamudima wamakumbi aakali kuvwuuma atalaa lwizi. Majeti aamuwo tanaakachili kusangana, pesi akali kuseluka koonse kuya kululyo akulumwensyi; nswi mpati zyakali kulibonya kuti zyakali kwaandaanya zibeela zyazyo. Maato akali kweenzyegwa kapati; Starbuck kalikutandaanya nswi zyotatwe zyakali kuchijaana kuya kulubazu lwamuwo. Ibbaasi lyesu lyakalibikkidwe, mpawo, guwo lyakali kuyaambele, twakachijaana; bwato bwakali kweenda chabufwubafwuba mumaanzi, zyakuti zibelesyo zyakutanta zyakali kukonzya kubelesegwa chakufwambaana kuti zitagwisyigwi kuzwa kuzijazyo zyamulongo.

Muchiindi chifwiifwi twakali kuzuzaana mukati kachivwunisyo chilamfu chakkungu; taakwe bwato naa bwato bwakabonwa.

"Amuleke, nobaalumi," wakavwiyavwiya Starbuck, kakwiinkila kunembo kusule lyachipepa chakwe; "kuli chiindi chakujaya nswi kaitanasika guwo pati. Kuli maanzi aatuba alimwi!—afwaafwi a! Chilimo!"

Mpawo biyo, kulila kobile kwakatobelana kulubazu lomwe lwesu kwakatondeezya kuti mato aambi akafwambaana; pesi tiibakamvwigwa pe, aawo Starbuck wakati: "Nyamuka!" mpawo Queequeg, kajisi nkoli mujanza, wakanyampuka.

Nikuba kuti taakwe naba omwe wabasikutanta bwato wakali kulanga ntenda yabuumi alufu afwaafwi ambabo, pesi kabalangide busyu bwamuntu wakali kusule lyabwaato, bakalizi kuti chiindi chakali afwaafwi chakasika; bakamvwa, abalo, muzuzumo mupati mbuli wa nzovu zili makumi musanu zyakali kuzungaana muzibbeeke zyabo. Aawo bwato bwakali kuzungaana mukati kakunkungu, mayuwe akali kuzungaana akuvwuuma mbuli nzoka zyakanyemede.

"Ooyo ngomukowa wakwe. Awo , awo , komupa!" wakavwiyavwiya Starbuck.

Muzuzumo mufwaafwi wakazwa mubwaato; wakali butale bwa Queequeg. Mpawo zyoonse muchiindi chomwe kwakasika kusungilizya kutalibonyi kuzwa kusule, mpawo kuya kunembo bwato bwakali kulibonya mbuli kuti bwakali kuuma achilundu; bbaasi lyakawida ansi mpawo lyakapwasuka; busi bupya bwakazwa afwaafwi; chimwi chintu chakazungaana akuwida ansi mbuli muzuzumo wanyika munsi lyesu. Basikubeleka boonse bakafwide muuya mbuli mbobakali kusowelwa mumuwo uusalala pyu. Squall, whale, an harpoon zyoonse zyakaswaangana antoomwe; mpawo nswi mpati, yakalyigwa buyo abutale, yakafwutuka.

Nikuba kuti lyakazwide maanzi, bwato bwakali afwaafwi kuchisa pe. Kuzuzaana kuzyunguluka ncico twakabweza zibbeeke zyakali kutanta, akuziuma atalaa gunwale, twakajokela kumasena eesu. Ooko twakakkala kusikila kumagolelo eesu mulwizi, maanzi akali kuvwumbilila mbabo zyoonse amapulanga, aboobo kumeso eesu aakali kulanga ansi bwato bwakaimvwi bwakali kulibonya mbuli bwato bwamakorali bwakakomenena kulindiswe kuzwa munsi lyalwizi.

Guwo lyakayungizya kusikila kulila; mayuwe akauma zikwabilizyo zyabo antoomwe; guwo lyoonse lyakavwuuma, lyakaandaana, akuvwuuma mbuli mulilo uutuba atalaa nyika, oomo, katutanapya, motwakali kupya; uutafwi mumilomo eeyi yalufu! Twakalumbaizya mato aambi mubuyo; mbuli kuvwuuma kumalasha apona munsi aamulyango wamulilo uuyaka mulilo mbuli kuvwuuma mato aayo muguwo eelyo. Muchiindi eecho, bbivwe, bbivwe, ankungu, zyakakomena akusiyana amweenzule wamasiku; taakwe chitondezyo chabwato chakabonwa. Lwizi lwakali kuyaabutanta lwakalesya kusoleka koonse kugwisya bwato. Mabbiza taakwe naakali kubeleka pe mbuli ma propeller, lino akali kubeleka mulimo wakukwabilila buumi. Aboobo, kugonka nkomo yamachisi aatanjili maanzi, nikwakainda kwaalilwa kunji Starbuck wakabamba nzila yakuumpa lampi mumuni; mpawo wakachitandabika achisamu, wakachipa kuli Queequeg mbuli sikunyamuna chitondezyo cha bulangilizi oobu bwakamana. Ooko, mpawo, wakakkala, kajisi kendulo eeyo iitakwe maanu mumoyo wakubula nguzu ooko kwanguzu zyoonse. Ooko, mpawo, wakakkala, chitondezyo achitondezyo chamuntu uutakwe lusyomo, katakwe bulangilizi kajisi bulangilizi mukati kakutyompwa.

Katutontola, katutontola, katutontola, katutajisi bulangilizi bwabwaato naa bwato, twakasumpula meso eesu aawo mafwumofwumo naakasika. Sikungu wakacili kumwaika atalaa lwizi, mumuni uutakwe chintu wakali kupwaigwa munsi lyabwaato. Chakutayeeyelwa Queequeg wakatalika kunyampuka, wakabikka janza lyakwe kumatwi. Toonse twakamvwa kuvwuuma kusyoonto, mbuli ntambo amayadi aakasinkilidwe aguwo pati. Ijwi lyakali kuyaabuswena afwaafwi; kkumbi pati lyakaandaanizyigwa achiimo chipati, chitalibonyi pe. Katuyoowede, toonse twakazuzaana kuya mulwizi aawo bwato nibwakali kulibonya, bwakatusikila musinzo uutali kwiinda bulamfu bwawo.

Katutanta mumayuwe twakabona bwato bwakalekelezyegwa, mbuli kuti kwakaindi kaniini bwakazungaana akuvwunguluka munsi lyabwaato mbuli chipwa kunsi aameso; mpawo bwato bupati bwakavwunguluka atala lyawo, mpawo tiibwakabonwa limbi kusikila bwakasika kusule. Twakadubbwa alimwi, twakazundwa alwizi, mpawo twakanyangwa akusika kabotu mubwato. Kakutanasika guwo pati, mato aambi akazwa kunswi zyawo mpawo akapiluka kubwato muchiindi cheelede. Bwaato bwakatusiya, pesi bwakali kweenda, kuti ambweni bwakali kukonzya kutupa chitondezyo chakufwa kwesu,—nkomo yabwato naa nkoli yamulambi.





CHAANDANO 49. Inziba.

Kuli ziindi zimwi zigambya muchintu eechi chisanganizidwe nchotwaamba kuti buumi aawo muntu nabweza nyika yoonse mbuli museko mupati uubeleka, nikuba kuti busongo bwawo tabuzyi pe, mpawo uladooneka kuti museko ooyu tauli wamuntu pe pele ngwakwe mwini. Nokuba boobo, taakwe chintu chityompya, mpawo taakwe chintu chilibonya kuti chili kabotu nikuba kuti nkukazyanya. Wabikka ansi zintu zyoonse zyakachitika, lusyomo loonse, alusyomo, akukombelezya, zintu zyoonse ziyumu zilibonya azitalibonyi, kakutalangwi mbuli mbozili ziyumu; mbuli nziba iijisi nguzu zyakugaya zyakulya mboilya zibulo amabwe aamfwulumende. Alimwi kujatikizya buyumuyumu busyoonto akulibilika, bulangizi bwantenda yakufwambaana, ntenda yabuumi alimwi azizo; zyoonse eezyi, alufu lwini, zilibonya kuli nguwe mbuli zyakubeja, zyakuuma zibotu, akuumwa kwakusekelela kulubazu kwakapegwa asikuseka-seka uchembede uutabonwi alimwi uutakwe mulandu. Ooku kulimvwa kutali kabotu nkondaambila, kulaboola kumuntu muchiindi chamapenzi mapati; chakasika aakati kakuyandisya kwakwe, aboobo eecho chakali kulibonya kuli nguwe kuti nchintu chipati loko, lino chilibonya mbuli kuti nchintu chipati loko. Taakwe chintu chili mbuli ntenda zyakuzuba nswi mpati kuti zizyale busongo oobu butaangulukide alimwi buuba-uba; mpawo aawo lino ndakabona lweendo loonse lwa Pequod, antoomwe a White Whale mupati mbuli chintu chakwe.

"Queequeg," ndakaamba, aawo nibakandikwelela, mwaalumi wamamanino, kuya kuchibbeeke, mpawo ndakali kuzungaanya mujakete lyangu kuti ndizwe mumaanzi; "Queequeg, mweenzuma mubotu, sena zintu ziliboobu zilachitika chiindi chiingi?" Kakwiina kulimvwa kupati, nikuba kuti wakazwide mbuli ndime, wakandipa kumvwisisisya kuti zintu ziliboobu zilachitika chiindi chiingi.

"Mwami Stubb," ndakaamba, ndakacengulukila kuli yooyo weelede, walo, wakasamide mabbatani mujaketi lyakwe lyamafuta, lino wakali kufweba paipi lyakwe mumvwula; "Mwami Stubb, ndiyeya kuti ndakamvwa mwaamba kuti akati kabantu boonse mbomwakaswaangana aabo, mweenzuma mupati, Mwami Starbuck, nguuchenjela loko. Ndiyeeya kuti, kuti kweenda amutwe wamulwizi uuuluka abwaanda bwanu bubikkidwe muguwo lyamvwula nchintu chiinda kubota mbuli basikuvwuba nswi mpati?"

"Masimpe. Ndaseluka kuti ndijane nswi mpati kuzwa mubwaato bwakali kuvwuuma mumuwo uuzwa ku Cape Horn."

"Mwami Flask," ndakaamba, ndakacengulukila kuli King-Post muniini, wakaliimvwi afwaafwi; "nywebo mulijisi luzibo muzintu eezyi, mebo tandijisi luzibo. Sena inga mwandaambila kuti naa mulawu uutakonzyi kuchincha mumulimo ooyu wakuzuba nswi, Mwami Flask, kuti sikukwela bwato atyole musana wakwe akulikwelela musyule-kunembo mumilomo yalufu?"

"Sena tokonzyi kuchincha eecho chiche?" wakaamba Flask. "Iyi, oyo ngomulawu. Ndiyanda kubona basikweenzya bwato kabajokezya maanzi kusikila kubusyu bwa nswi mpati. Ha, ha! nswi mpati inga yabapa meso aameso, koyeeya!"

Aawa mpawo, kuzwa kuli bakamboni botatwe batakwe lusalululo, ndakajisi kaambo kaamba mulandu woonse. Twalanga, aboobo, kuti guwo pati akuvwuuma mumaanzi antoomwe akukkala mumaanzi, zyakali zintu zyakali kuchitika lyoonse mubuumi buliboobu; kuyeeya kuti muchiindi chiyumu loko chakuunka ku nswi mpati ndeelede kuleka buumi bwangu mumaanza aayo wakasololela bwato—kanjikanji mweenzuma ooyo achiindi eecho wakali muchiimo chakwe chakufwambaana chakuti ulakonzya kutyola bwato nkaambo kakwe mwini; katuyeeya kuti ntenda yakachitika kubwaato bwesu yakachitika akaambo kakuti Starbuck wakakwela nswi yakwe mpati loko, mpawo katuyeeya kuti Starbuck, nikubaboobo, wakali ampuwo akaambo kakubikkila maanu kapati mukuzuba nswi; nkaambo ndakali wabwaato bwa Starbuck butazyibidwe pe; mpawo kumamanino kuyeeya kuti ndakajatikizyidwe mukutandaanya dyabulosi, kujata nswi iitegwa White Whale: kubweza zintu zyoonse antoomwe, ndaamba kuti, ndakayeeya kuti inga ndaunka munsi akuchita lugwalo lwakuyanda kwangu. "Queequeg," ndakaamba, "koboola, uyooba loya wangu, sikuzuzikizya, asikupa."

Zilakonzya kubonekaanga nchintu chakankamanisya kuti akati kabantu boonse basikweenzya bwato beelede kuti kabachita zyintu nzyobayanda, pesi taakwe bantu munyika bayandisya kusekelela ooku. Eechi chakaba chiindi chane mubuumi bwangu bwakutanta bwato ndakali kuchita chintu chomwe. Nikwakamana kusekelela achiindi eechi, ndakalimvwa kuubauba; bbwe lyakagwisigwa amoyo wangu. Kunze lyaboobo, mazuba woonse ngondeelede kupona lino anooli mabotu mbuli mazuba Lazaro ngaakapona naakabusyigwa; mpindu yakuyungizya iisalala yamyezi myiingi naa mvwiki mbuli mbukonzya kuba. Ndakafwutuka ndemwini; lufu lwangu akuzikkwa kwangu zyakajalilidwe mumoyo wangu. Nda kalanga kuzyunguluka ndime cakukkazika moyo akukkutila, mbuli moza uukkede mukati kaanda yamukwasyi.

Lino mpawo, ndakayeeya, kakunyina kuzyiba kandivwungulula maanza aachibaki changu, aawa nkuya kutontola, kuyobololwa kwakusotokela kulufu akunyonyoona, mpawo dyabooli wakabweza musule.





CHAANDANO 50. Bwaato bwa Ahabu ababelesi. Fedallah.

"Ngwani wakali kukonzya kuyeeya oobo, Flask!" wakalila Stubb; "ikuti nindakajisi buyo kuulu komwe nowatakandijata mubwaato, kunze kwakuti ambweni wakulesya mpako yakujala amunwe wangu wachisamu. Oh! mwaalumi uchembede mubotu loko!"

"Nsiboni kuti nchintu cheenzu, mubwini, nkaambo kaako," wakaamba Flask. "Ikuti kuulu kwakwe nikwali kuzwa kuchiuno, lino, nicali chintu chiliimpene. Eezyo nizyakamulesya; pesi ulijisi bbwe lyomwe, mpawo chibeela chibotu chachimwi chisyaala, ulizi."

"Nsezi pe, mwaalumi wangu muniini; teensi ndakamubona kafwugama."

Akati kabantu basongo kujatikizya nswi mpati, kwakali kukazyania kuti, kakulangwa bupati bwabuumi bwakwe kukuzwidilila kwalweendo, nkuko kabotu kuti mweendelezi wabasikuzuba nswi mpati abikke buumi bwakwe muntenda muntenda zyakutandaanya. Aboobo basilumamba ba Tamerlane bakali kukazyania amisozi mumeso aabo, kuti naa buumi bwakwe oobo buyandisi bweelede kutolwa munkondo mpati.

Asi kuli Ahabu mubuzyo wakacinca. Twayeeya kuti amawulu obile muntu uli buyo mbuli muntu uunyampuka muchiindi choonse cha ntenda; katuyeeya kuti kuyandula nswi zya whale lyoonse kuli mumapenzi mapati alimwi aatalikomeneni; kuti chiindi choonse, masimpe, mpawo chilaa ntenda; muchiimo eechi nchintu chabusongo kuli umwi muntu uulema kuti anjile mubwaato bwa whale mukuvwima? Mbuli chintu chidumide, basimakwebo antoomwe ba Pequod beelede kuti bakali kuyeeya kuti peepe.

Ahabu wakalizyi kabotu kuti nikuba kuti beenzinyina bakuŋanda tiibakali kuyeeya kuti ulakonzya kunjila mubwaato muzintu zimwi zitakwe ntenda zyakutandaanya, nkaambo kakuti wakali afwaafwi abusena bwakuchitila zintu akupa malailile aakwe lwakwe mwini, pesi kuti Kapitani Ahabu abe abwaato bwakapegwa kuli nguwe mbuli mweendelezi wachiindi choonse mukuvwima—atala akuti Mupati wabasikalumamba Ahabu wakapegwa baalumi bali musanu, basikweenzya bwato mbubonya oobo, wakalizyi kabotu kuti kulisumpula kuliboobu tiikwakanjila mumizeezo yabasimakwebo ba Pequod. Aboobo tanaakalomba basikweenzya bwato kuzwa kuli mbabo, mpawo tanaakatondezya nzyaakali kuyanda pe. Nikuliboobo wakabweza ntaamu zyakwe mwini zyakujatikizya zyoonse eezyo. Kusikila Cabaco naakajana makani, basikweenzya bwato tiibakachili kuzibona pe, nikuba kuti bakasinizya kuti, nikwakainda kaindi kaniini kabazwa kuchiteshi, boonse bakamanizya mulimo wakubamba mato aa whale kuti abelesyegwe; aawo chiindi chilamfu musule lyazeezi Ahabu wakali kujanika kuti wakali kulijata mumulimo wakupanga mapini amaanza aakwe aabwaato bwakali kuyeeyelwa kuti bwakali bwato butakwe mulimo, mpawo wakali kugela tuniini twamapulanga, ooto ntambo niyakamana tulabikkwa atala aamulyango wabwaato: aawo zyoonse eezyi nozyakali kubonwa muli nguwe, ikapati wakali kuyandisya kapati kuvwumbilila munsi lyabwaato, mbuli kuti bwakali kuyanda kuti bukonzye kuliyumya kukusinikizyigwa kwa janza lyakwe lya nzovu; alimwi akuyoowa nkwaakatondezya mukubumba kabotu-kabotu bbodela lyachibelo, naakuti bbaaji litakwe maanu, mbuli mboliitwa chimwi chiindi, cibeela chilangene mumutwe wabwaato chakuyumya bbwe lyakufwusa naa kuyasa nswi mpati; aawo nikwakabonwa kuti wakali kwiima mubwaato oobo kakuli bbwe lyakwe lilikke kaliimvwi muchibaka chamukati-kati, mpawo achisamu chamubezi chakali kugonkwa asyoonto aawa akuchilulamika asyoonto aawa; zyoonse eezyi, ndaamba, zyakabusya luyandisisyo akuyandisya kuziba achiindi eecho. Asi bantu boonse bakali kuyeeya kuti kulibambila ooku kuli Ahabu kweelede kuba buyo kakuyanda kutandaanya Moby Dick; nkaambo wakaliyubununa kale makanze aakwe aakuvwima silunya ooyo uufwa lwakwe mwini. Pesi kuyeeya kuliboobu tiikwakali kubikkilizya akudooneka pe kuti kuli basikweenzya bwato kuti bakapegwa mulimo mubwaato oobo.

Lino, antoomwe azilengaano zili munsi, eezyo zigambya zyakasyeede zyakamana; nkaambo muli whale zigambyo zilafwambaana kumana. Kunze lyaboobo, lino achiindi zintu zitaambiki ziliboobu zyamasi aatazyibidwe zilazwa mumasena aatazyibidwe aanyika kuti zikonzye kuchita kuti bantu aaba bazangi bavwubwa; mpawo mato ngawo kanjikanji alabweza zilenge zitazyibidwe ziliboobu zijanika kazizungaana mulwizi atala amapulanga, zibeela zyabwaato, mabbiza, mato aama whale, mato, zintu zyaku Japani zyakazwa mujulu, azimwi zimbi; kuti Beelzebub lwakwe mwini ulakonzya kutanta kumbali akuseluka mukaanda kuti akabandike amweendelezi, mpawo tensi chakapa kuti kube kubotelwa kutakonzyi kuzundwa mubusena bwakumbele.

Pesi nikuba kuti zyoonse eezyi kazili, masimpe ngakuti nikuba kuti zilengaano zyakali munsi lyabo zyakafwambaana kujana busena bwazyo akati kababelesi, nikuba kuti zyakaliindene ambabo, pesi Fedallah wakasamide masusu wakazumanana kuba maseseke kusikila kumamanino. Nkwaakazwa munyika yabukkale buliboobu, kwiinda mukujatana kuli buti nkwaakafwambaana kulitondezya kuti wakaliswaangene abuvwubi bwa Ahabu; peepe, kusikila kubaa nsungwaazyo imwi iitakwe mpindu; Ijulu lilizyi, asi inga lyakali nguzu atala lyakwe; zyoonse eezyi taakwe wakazyi pe. Asi umwi takonzyi kuzumanana kuba amuuya wakutabikkila maanu aatala aFedallah. Wakali chilengwa mbuli bantu basumpukide, bantu bamumunzi bakkala mumasena aatontola mbobabona biyo muziloto zyabo, mpawo eezi tazilibonyi pe; pesi mbuli mbabo lino achiindi bazuzaana akati kabantu bakuEzhiya batacinci, ikapati nsumbu zyakujwe kujwe lyanyika—eeyo zisi zikwabilidwe, zitayeeyeki, zitacinci, eezyo noziba mazubaano zicibamba bube bwakumuuya bwamazyalani aanyika, eelyo kuyeeya kwamuntu wakusaanguna nokwakali kuzyibwa, bantu bakwe boonse batazyibidwe nkwaakazwa, bakali kulangana mbuli zilengaano zini zini, mpawo bakabuzya zuba amwezi kuti nkamboonzi nzyaakalengelwa akuti nkamboonzi; nikubaboobo, kweendelanya a Matalikilo, bangelo bakaswaangana abana basimbi babantu, madaimona abalo, bakayungizya ba Rabi batazyibidwe, bakali kulijata muzintu zyabuzubaa buzuba.





CHAANDANO 51. Muuya-Mulomo.

Mazuba, nsondo zyakainda, mpawo kakunyina kweenda kabotu, bwato bwa Pequod bwakainda asyoonto-asyoonto mumasena aane aakutanta bwato; eeyo iili kunkomwe ya Azores; kuzwa ku Cape de Verdes; aaPuleti (iiitwa oobo), kalikuzwa kumulomo wa Rio de la Plata; a Carrol Ground, busena butakwe bantu, bujisi maanzi, kumusanza kuzwa ku St. Helena.

Chakali aawo naakali kuseluka mumaanzi aaya aamamanino masiku amwi aatakwe mwezi, aawo mayuwe woonse naakali kuzungaana mbuli mabbuku aansiliva; mpawo, kwiinda mukutontola kwabo, bakachita chintu chakali kulibonya mbuli kuti nkuumuna, kutali kuba alikke pe; mubusiku butakwe muzuzumo ndeke yasiliva yakabonwa kule kunembo aamabbubbu aatuba aali kunkomwe. Kwakali kumunika amwezi, kwakali kulibonya mbuli kujulu; kwakali kulibonya kuti leza umwi uujisi masusu uumweka-mweka wakali kuzwa mulwizi. Fedallah wakasaanguna kupandulula ndeke eeyi. Mumasiku aaya aamumuni wamwezi, wakali cilengwa cakwe kutanta kumutwe wabwaato bupati, akwiima kalangilila anze, munzila iiluzi mbuli kuti kwakali masyikati. Alimwi, nikuba kuti matanga aabaswi bakali kubonwa masiku, taakwe naba omwe muvwubi mwaanda wakali kukonzya kubaseluka. Asi aawo, naakamana kubelesya yunifomu yakwe kwamasiku manji aatobelana kakunyina kwaamba jwi noliba lyomwe; aawo, musule lyakuumuna koonse ooku, jwi lyakwe litali lyaanyika lyakamvwika kalikwaambilizya ndeke eeyo iilaa mwezi, sikutanta bwato uli woonse wakanyampuka mbuli kuti kuli muuya uujisi mababa wakanjila mubwato, mpawo wakalumbaizya basikweenzya bwato. "Ooko nkwaafwusa!" Kuti mweembo walubeta nowakalizyigwa, nibatakakonzya kuzungaana kwiinda waawo; pesi teensi bakalimvwa kuyoowa pe; pesi nkubotelwa. Nkaambo nikuba kuti bwakali woola litazyibidwe pe, pesi kulila kwakali kugambya loko, mpawo kwakali kukkomanisya loko, zyakuti bantu boonse bakali mubwato bakali kuyanda kuseluka.

Kakweenda atala abwaato chakufwambaana, ntaamu zyakutanta, Ahabu wakalailila kuti maseya mapati abwami abikkwe, mpawo maseya woonse akatandabika. Mwaalumi mubotu mubwato weelede kubweza bweendelezi. Mpawo, aawo mutwe wabwato uli oonse wakajisi bantu, bwato bwakavwumbilidwe bwakavwunguluka kakutanaba guwo. Ikugambya, kuzungaana, kusumpula kwamuwo wa taffrail uuzuzya zibulo zyamaseila manji, kwakapa kuti bwato bwakali kuuluka, bwakali kuzungaana bumvwe mbuli muuya munsi lyamaulu; mpawo wakachijaana, mbuli kuti zintu zyobile zyakali kumulwana—chimwi chakali kuya kujulu, chimwi chakali kuya kujulu. Alimwi kuti nowakalanga busyu bwa Ahabu masiku aayo, nowakayeeya kuti muli nguwe awalo zintu zyobile ziindene zyakali kulwana. Nikuba kuti kuulu kwakwe komwe kupona kwakali kulila mujulu, kufumbwa kuzungaana kwa janza lyakwe lyakafwide kwakali kulimvwisya mbuli bbokesi lyamutunta. Kubuumi alufu oyu mwaalumi uchembede wakeenda. Asi nikuba kuti bwato bwakafwambaana, alimwi nikuba kuti kuzwa kumeso woonse, mbuli mivwi, kulanga kwakuyandisisya kwakazwa, pesi ndeke yasiliva tiiyakachili kubonwa masiku aayo. Sikweenzya bwato woonse wakakonka kuti wakabona chiindi chomwe, pesi kutali chiindi chabili.

Ooyu mulomo akati kamasiku wakali afwaafwi kukomena kuba chintu chalubwa, aawo, mazuba amwi musule, amubone! muchiindi chomwe chakuumuna, kwakaambilizyigwa alimwi: alimwi kwakapandululwa abantu boonse; pesi aawo naakapanga bwato kuti buzunde, alimwi bwakasweeka mbuli kuti teensi bwakasweeka pe. Mpawo chakatubelekela masiku amasiku, kusikila taakwe wakaswiilizya pesi wakagambwa. Munzila iitazyibidwe yakazundwa mumumuni wamwezi uusalala, naa mumuni wanyenyeezi, mbuli mbokukonzya kuba; kuzimaana alimwi kwabuzuba bomwe boonse, naa mazuba obile, naa otatwe; alimwi munzila imwi kakuli kulibonya kuti kuli koonse kwakali kuyaambele mumootokala wesu, ndeke eeyi yakali kulibonya kuti yakali kutukkomanisya lyoonse.

Nikuba alusyomo lwachiindi lwamusyobo wabo, mpawo kweendelana abukkale butalibonyi, mbuli mbukwakalibonya, oobo muzintu zyiingi bwakabikka Pequod, tiibakayanda bamwi basikweenzya bwato aabo bakakonkezya kuti kufumbwa chiindi akufwumbwa nkobakali kwaambwa; nikuba muchiindi chili kulaale, naakuti muchiindi chili kule loko, mulomo ooyo uutakonzyi kuswena wakasowelwa a nswi yomwe; a eyo nswi mpati, Moby Dick. Kwaachiindi, kwakaba, akuyoowa kulibonya ooku, mbuli kuti kwakali kutukombelezya kuti tuzumanane, kuchitila kuti chinyama eechi chituzunguluke, mpawo chitujaye kumamanino mulwizi luli kule loko.

Ooku kulibilika kwakaindi kaniini, kutamvwisisiki pesi kuyoosya, kwakajana nguzu zigambya kuzwa kukukkazika moyo kwachiindi, oomo, munsi lyabubotu bwawo boonse, bamwi bakali kuyeeya kuti kwakali chintu cha dyabulosi, mbuli mazuba amazuba ngotwakali kweenda, kwiinda mulwizi katukatazyide, katuli tolikke, kuti mulengalenga woonse, twakali kuyanda kupilusya mulandu wesu, chakali kulibonya kuti chakazwa mubuumi kunembo lyesu lyesu lili mbuli nkapu.

Pesi, kumamanino, nitwakajokela kujwe, guwo lya Cape lyakatalika kuvwuuma kutuzinguluka, mpawo twakanyampuka akuwa atala aalwizi lulamfu, lujisi manyongwe oolo; aawo Pequod wakajisi meno aanzovu naakakotamina kupya, mpawo wakauma mayuwe aamudima mukupenga kwakwe, kusikila, mbuli mvwula ya nsiliva, mafoomu akauluka atala abwaanda bwakwe; mpawo buumi boonse oobu bwakamana, asi bwakapa busena kuzintu zitali kabotu kwiinda chiindi.

Afwiifwi abuta bwesu, zintu zitazyibidwe mumaanzi zyakali kuzuza kuya akuya kunembo lyesu; aawo musule lyesu nozyakali kuuluka nziba zyamulwizi zitamvwisyi pe. Alimwi mafwumofwumo woonse, notwakali kukkala mumasena eesu, milongo yabayuni aaba yakali kubonwa; mpawo nikuba kuti twakali kulila, kwachiindi chilamfu twakali kukakatila ku hemp, mbuli kuti bakali kubona bwato bwesu mbuli bwato butakwe bantu; chintu chakabikkidwe kuti chinyonyoonwe, aboobo cheelede kuba busena bwakukkala bwabo beni batakwe maanda. Mpawo wakasumpulwa akuzungaana, wakazumanana kuzungaana lwizi lusiya, mbuli kuti mayuwe aakwe mapati akali manjezeezya; mpawo moyo mupati wanyika wakali mumapenzi akuusa akaambo kachibi akupenga kwachiindi chilamfu nkwaakazyala.

Cape of Good Hope, sena balamwiita? Muciindi caboobo, Cape Tormentoso, mbuli mboyakali kwiitwa kaindi; kwachiindi chilamfu katukkomaninwa akuumuna kwakaindide, twakalijana kuti twakasowelwa mulwizi oolu lwakali kupenzyegwa, ooko bantu bakali amulandu nkobakasanduka kuba nkuku eezyo answi eezyi, nkobakali kulibonya kuti bakapegwa mulandu wakudubbwa kukabe kutamani kakunyina busena bwakuyubila, nokuba kuzunda muya ooyo uusiya kakunyina busena bwakuyubila. Pesi kukkalikana, kutuba mbuli chivwulamabwe, alimwi kutachincha pe; kachili kusololela kasensa kakwe kamababa kujulu; kuchili kututamba kuzwa chiindi, ndeke ilikke chimwi chiindi ilapandululwa.

Muchiindi choonse eechi chamudima, Ahabu, nikuba kuti wakalikuyeeyela kuti wakali kweendelezya bbuwa lyakazwide maanzi alimwi lyakali muntenda, wakatondeezya kuti wakajisi bubi kapati; mpawo tanaakali kwaambuula kuli aabo mbakwetene limwi. Muchiindi chamvwula mbuli yeeyi, nikwakamana kuti zyoonse zili atala azyakujulu zyakwabililwa, taakwe chintu chikonzya kuchitwa pele kulindila buyo guwo pati. CHAANDANO 52. Inswi ya Albatros.

Kumusanza lwaajwe kuzwa ku Cape, kuzwa ku Crozetts, busena bubotu bwakutanta bwato bwa Right Whalemen, lwizi lwakali kulibonya kunembo, Goney (Albatross) muzina. Mbuli mbwaakali kuyaabuswena asyoonto-asyoonto, kuzwa abusena bwangu busumpukide kumutwe wabwaato, ndakabona kabotu chintu eecho chakali kugambya kuli tyro mulwizi lwakuzuba nswi—sikuzuba nswi mulwizi, alimwi katakwe aŋanda kwachiindi chilamfu.

Mbuli kuti mayuwe akalizwide, bwato oobu bwakasalala mbuli chifuwa cha walrus yakaangidwe. Kumbali lyakwe koonse, ooku kulibonya kwakali kutobelezegwa anzila zilamfu zya nsimbi zisalala pyu, mpawo zyoonse nzyaakali kubelesya zyakali mbuli mitabi yazisamu mipati yakavwumbidwe aamabwe aatontola. Maseila aakwe aansi ngaakabikkidwe luzutu. Chakali chintu chakankamanisya kubona ndevu zyakwe zilamfu kalanga anze kumitwe yotatwe eeyo. Bakali kulibonya kuti basamide zikumba zyabanyama, aboobo bakazapuka akusama zizwaato zyakapona kwamyaka iili afwaafwi yone yakutanta bwato. Kabaimvwi muzikobela zyabutale zyakaangidwe kuchikobela, bakali kuzungaana akuzungaana atala aalwizi lutaambiki; mpawo, aawo bwato nibwakaseluka asyoonto-asyoonto munsi lyesu, swebo baalumi bali musanu ababili mujulu twakaswena afwaafwi loko cakuti twakali kukonzya kuzuza kuzwa kumutwe wabwaato bumwi kuya kuli bumwi; pesi, aabo bazubi banswi bakali kulibonya mbuli kuti baliimpene, kabatulanga kabotu-kabotu nibakali kwiinda, tiibakaamba jwi noliba lyomwe kuli basikulangilila besu, aawo chivwulamabwe chakali kumvwika kuzwa munsi.

"Bwaato ahoy! Sena mwakabona kale nswi iitegwa White Whale?"

Asi aawo mweendelezi wabasikalumamba uutazyibidwe, wakasendekede atala abwaanda buteteete, naakali kuyanda kubikka mweembo wakwe kumulomo wakwe, wakawida mulwizi kuzwa mujanza lyakwe; mpawo guwo lino lyakatanta amain, wakasoleka kakunyina mpindu kuti alimvwe kakwiina. Aawo bwato bwakwe bwakali kuyungizya musinzo aakati. Nikuba kuti munzila zisiyene-siyene zyakuumwine basikweenzya bwato bwa Pequod bakali kutondezya mbobakali kubona chintu eechi chibi loko nibakaamba zina lya White Whale kubwaato bumbi, Ahabu wakaima kwakaindi kaniini; chakali kulibonya mbuli kuti naakaselusya bwato kuti atanta mweenzu, ikuti guwo lyakali kuyoosya nolyakakasya. Pesi kabelesya busena bwakwe bwamuwo, wakabweza mweembo wakwe alimwi, mpawo kazyi kuti bwato butazyibidwe bwakali bwaku Nantucketer mpawo bwakali kuya kunganda, wakapozomoka chakupozomoka—“Ahoy! Ooyu ngo Pequod, wakaangidwe nyika yoonse! Kobaambila kuti balembe magwalo oonse aakumbele kulwizi lwa Pacific alimwi aciindi cino, ikuti naa nsili aŋanda, ndibaambile kuti ndibaambile myaka yotatwe! ku --"

Aawo chiindi eecho zibeela zyobile zyakazabuka, mpawo, mpawo, kweendelana anzila zyabo, tubunga twanswi ziche zitakwe ntenda, eezyo kwamazuba amwi musule zyakali kuzuzaana kumbali lyesu, zyakazuza amababa aakali kulibonya mbuli kuti zyakali kuzungaana, mpawo zyakali kulibamba kunembo akusule amabambo aamweenzu. Nikuba kuti mulweendo lwakwe lwakuzumanana Ahabu weelede kuti wakabona chintu chili mbuli cheechi, pesi, kumuntu uuli woonse uuyandisya zintu zilikke, zintu ziniini zilijisi bupanduluzi.

"Kozuza kuzwa kulindime, tee kayi?" wakatongooka Ahabu, kalanga mumaanzi. Kwakali kulibonya kuti kwakali buyo mbichani mumajwi, asi jwi lyakatondezya kuusa kupati kwakubula lugwasyo kwiinda mwaalumi uchembede wakapenga mbwaakatondezya. Pesi wakacengulukila kuli sikweenzya bwato, ooyo wakali kujatilide bwato mumuwo kuti butazungaani, wakapozomoka mujwi lyakwe lyamulavu wakaindi,—“Nyamuna! Komulesya kuzyunguluka nyika yoonse!”

Zinguluka nyika yoonse! Kuli zinji mujwi eelyo zyakukulwaizya kulimvwa kulisumpula; pesi ino nkuli kweendeenda koonse ooko nkokusika? Kwiinda buyo muntenda zitabaliki kusikila mpotwakatalika, ooko nkotwakasiya musyule nkotwakalikwabilidwe, nkobakali kunembo lyesu chiindi choonse.

Kuti nyika eeyi niyakali nyika iitakwe mamanino, mpawo kwiinda mukutanta bwato kuya kujwe twakali kukonzya kusika kumisela mipya, akujana zintu zibotu alimwi zigambya kwiinda ma Cyclades naa Islands zya Mwami Solomoni, nkokuti kwakali kusyomezyegwa mulweendo oolu. Pesi mukuyandaula maseseke aayo ngotulota, naakuti mukutandaanya madaimona aayo, aachiindi chimwi, aazuza kunembo lyamyoyo yabantu boonse; nitutandaanya ziliboobu atalaa nyika eeyi iizungulukide, balatusololela munzila iitakwe cintu naa kuti akati-kati balatusiya katugambwa.
