Beenzinyina bakali kugwasya umwi amweenzinyina muchiindi chakulindila, mpawo nkaambo kakuti bakali kulibonya kuti mbabakali balikke beendelezya bwato; pele zimwi ziindi bakali kuzwa mukaanda kabajisi malailile aacitika cakutayeeyelwa alimwi aakusinikizya, cakuti nikwakainda zyoonse cakali antangalala kuti bakali kulailila kwiinda mubusena bwabo. Iyi, simalelo wabo mupati asikulela wakaliko, nikuba kuti kusikila sunu tanaakali kubonwa ameso aali woonse aatazumizyidwe kunjila mubusena bwakulyookezyela lino busetekene.

Chiindi choonse nindakali kutanta abwato kuzwa kumawoola aangu munsi, ndakali kulanga musule kuti ndibone naa kuli busyu bumbi bwakali kulibonya; nkaambo kutamvwana kwangu kutaanzi kujatikizya mweendelezi uutazyibidwe, lino kali mumbali aalwizi, kwakaba chintu chakunyonganya.
