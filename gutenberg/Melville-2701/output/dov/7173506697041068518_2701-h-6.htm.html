CHAANDANO 32. Cetology.

Twakabikkwa kale mubusicamba kuya ansi; pesi ino-ino tulasweeka mubusena bwayo butakwe lwizi, butakwe busena bwakukkala. Ere eezyo zyakachitika; kakutanaba chikumba cha Pequod chivwunguluka kumbali abwaato bwa leviathan; kumatalikilo nkubotu kubikkila maanu kukaambo kayandikana loko mukuteelela kulumba kwakuyubununwa kwaalubazu kwa leviathanic akutondezya kwamisyobo yoonse ooko kuyootobela.

Nchitondeezyo chimwi chakabambwa kabotu cha nswi mpati mumisyobo yakwe mipati, eeyo njondiyanda lino kubikka kunembo lyako. pesi teensi mulimo muuba pe. Kwaandaanya zintu zipa kuti kube manyongwe, kunyina chintu chiche aawa kwaambwa. Swiilila kuli eezyo beendelezi bainda kubota alimwi bapya nzyobakaamba.

"Kunyina mutabi wa lwiiyo lwa banyama uujatikizyidwe mbuli oyo uutegwa Cetology," mbwaamba Kapitana Scoresby, AD 1820.

"Teensi makanze aangu, kuti nindakali munguzu zyangu, kuti ndinjile mubuvwuntauzi bwanzila ncobeni yakwaandaanya ba cetacea mutubunga amumikwasyi. * * * Kunyongana kupati kuliko akati kabasikulemba makani aamunyama ooyu" (sperm whale), mbwaamba Dokotela Beale, AD 1839.

"Kutakonzya kuchita buvwuntauzi bwesu mumaanzi aatakonzyi kuzyibwa." "Chivwumbyo chitakonzyi kunjililwa chivumba luzibo lwesu lwa cetacea." "Muunda uuzwide mamvwa." "Zyoonse eezyi zitondezyo zitazulide pesi zilatupenzya swebo tobasyaazibwene."

Aboobo amwaambe zya nswi mpati, Cuvier mupati, a John Hunter, a Lesson, eezyo mimuni ya lwiiyo lwa banyama a mubili. Nikuliboobo, nikuba kuti luzibo lwini-lwini ndusyoonto, pesi mabbuku manji; mpawo asyoonto buyo, a cetology, naakuti sayensi ya nswi mpati. Mbantu biingi, baniini abapati, bachembaala abapya, babelesi banyika abasikutanta bwato, aabo bajisi bupati naa buniini, kulemba aatala a nswi mpati. Amulange bamwi:—Balembi baBbayibbele; Aristotle; Pliny; Aldrovandi; Sir Thomas Browne; Gesner; Ray; Linnæus; Rondeletius; Willoughby; Sokwetete; Artedi; Sibbald; Brisson; Marten; Lacépède; Bonneterre; Desmarest; Baron Cuvier; Frederick Cuvier; John Hunter; Owen; Scoresby; Beale; Bennett; J. Ross Browne; Mulembi wa Miriam Coffin; Olmstead; a Mufundisi T. Cheever. Asi kuti zyoonse eezyi nzibakalemba, zibeela zyaambwa atala aawa ziyootondezya.

Akati kamazina aali mumulongo ooyu wabasikulemba nswi mpati, balikke aabo bakatobela Owen mbabakabona nswi mpati; alimwi pesi umwi wabo wakali sikuuma nkoli uuchibwene loko. Ndaamba Kapitani Scoresby. Aatala amutwe wamakani wa Greenland naa right-whale, nguwe mweendelezi wiinda kubota. Asi Scoresby taakwe nchaakazyi mpawo taakwe nchaakaamba aatala a nswi mpati ya sperm whale, kweelanisigwa a nswi mpati ya Greenland whale njiitayandi kwaambwa pe. Alimwi aawa kuti kwaambwa, kuti nswi mpati ya Greenland njakubba chibbula chabwami bwalwizi. Tali mupati kwiinda nswi mpati pe. Pesi, akaambo kakuti nzyaakaamba zyakali kuyandika kapati, akubula luzibo lupati ooko, kusikila myaaka iili makumi musanu atubili yainda, kwakabikka nswi iitegwa sperm-whale, mpawo kutaziba ooku kusikila sunu kuchili kulela mumasena oonse pele buyo mumasena masyoonto aasayaansi amasena aakuyobweda nswi mpati; ooku kunyangwa kwakamana munzila zyoonse. Kulanga-langa majwi oonse aa leviathanic mubasikulemba zyakuzekaula bapati bamazuba aakainda, kuyoomukkomanisya kuti nswi mpati ya Greenland, kakwiina sikukazyanya, kuli mbabo yakali mwami walwizi. Pesi chiindi chakasika chakuti kube kwaambilizya kupya. Ooyu ngu Charing Cross; amvwe! bantu babotu boonse,—nswi mpati ya Greenland yagusyigwa,—nswi mpati ya sperm whale lino ilalela!

Kuli buyo mabbuku obile aali mubuumi aayo aachita mbuli kuti abikka nswi iipona kunembo lyako, mpawo aawo, muchiindi chilamfu loko azwidilila mukusola ooku. Mabbuku aaya nga Beale a Bennett; boonse bobile muchiindi chabo basilisi ku England South-Sea whale-ships, mpawo boonse bobile baalumi basyomeka. Makani aakusaanguna aajatikizya nswi iitegwa sperm whale kuti ajanwe mumavolumu aabo ngamasyoonto; pesi kusikila mpokugolela, nchintu chibotu loko, nikuba kuti chili buyo abupanduluzi bwasayaansi. Kusikila lino, nokubaboobo, nswi mpati, yasayaansi naa yakulemba, buumi tabuzulide pe mumabbuku aali woonse. Kwiinda nswi zyoonse zivwimwa, buumi bwakwe tabulembedwe pe.

Lino misyobo isiyene-siyene yaba whale iyandika nzila imwi iizyibidwe yakuziba, nikuba kuti nchintu chiubauba lino, kuzwa lino chiyoozuzyigwa mumbazu zyoonse ababelesi batobela. Mbuli mbokutakwe muntu uuli kabotu uukonzya kubweza kaambo aaka, lino ndapa nguzu zyangu zyakuchita zintu zitali kabotu. Tandisyomezyi chintu chizulide pe; nkaambo kufumbwa chintu chabuntu cheelede kuti chilizulide, nkaambo kaako cheelede kuti chibe alubizya. Tandikonzyi kulicengeezya kuti ndaamba bupanduluzi buniini bwamisyobo yiindene-indene, naa—mubusena obu—bupanduluzi buli boonse. Makanze aangu aawa ngakuti nditondezye buyo nzila yakubamba zyintu munzila iili kabotu. Ndime sikuyaka, kutali muyaki.

Pesi mulimo muyumu; taakwe sikusalaula magwalo muOfesi lyaPosita uunga weelene aawe. Kuyandaula ansi aalwizi kubatobela; kuba amaanza aako akati kantalisyo zitaambiki, mbabo, antoomwe amutwe wanyika; eechi nchintu chiyoosya. Ino ndime ni kuti ndikonzye kujata nsingo ya leviathan eeyi! Kusampaula kwa Jobu kulakonzya kundigambya. Hena (leviathan) ulakonzya kuchita chizuminano ayebo? Amubone bulangilizi bwakwe mbwabuyo! Pesi ndakazuza mumalayibbulali akutanta bwato mulwizi; Ndakali kuchita abaswi ba whale amaanza aaya aalibonya; Ndili mukusinizya; mpawo ndiyoosola. Kuli zimwi zintu zyeelede kuchitwa.

Chakusanguna: Ibukkale butazibinkene, butazungaani bwa sayensi eeyi ya Cetology buli mubusena bwini bwakasinizyigwa akasimpe, kakuti mumasena amwi kuchili kaambo kakatazya kumvwisisisya kuti naa nswi mpati ya whale ninswi. Mubbuku lyakwe lya System of Nature, AD 1776, Linnæus wakaambilizya kuti, "Ndilaandaanya nswi mpati kuzwa kunswi." Pesi mukuziba kwangu, ndilizi kuti kusikila mumunyaka wa 1850, nswi zya sharks a shad, alewives antoomwe a herring, kazikazyanya amulawu wa Linnæus, zyakachili kujanika kabaandaanya lwizi ndyoonya a Leviathan.

Twaambo Linnæus nkwaakali kuyanda kuti agwisye nswi mpati mumaanzi, wakaamba boobu: "Akaambo kamoyo wabo uupya, mapunga aabo, zikobela zyabo zizungaana, matwi aabo aajisi mpako, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem," alimwi kumamanino, "ex lege naturæ jure meritoque." Ndakapa zyoonse eezyi kuli beenzuma Simeon Macey a Charley Coffin, baku Nantucket, boonse bobile mbotwakali kubeleka limwi mulweendo lumwi, mpawo bakamvwana mumuzeezo wakuti twaambo twakaambwa twakali tuzulide pe. Charley wakapa muzeezo wakusampaula kuti bakali bantu batakwe maanu.

Chizibwe kuti, kuleka kukazyania koonse, ndabweza ntaamu yakaindi yakuti nswi nswi, mpawo ndaita Jona uusalala kuti andigwasye. Eechi chintu chipati chakamana, kaambo katobela nkakuti, ino nswi mpati njiiindene kuli nswi zimwi. Atala, Linnæus wakakupa zintu eezyo. Pesi mubufwiifwi, nzizyeezyi: mapunga abulowa bupya; mpawo, nswi zimbi zyoonse tazikwe mapunga alimwi zilaa bulowa butontola.

Chitobela: ino inga twapandulula biyeni nswi mpati, kwiinda muzintu zyakwe zyaanze zilibonya, kuchitila kuti tukonzye kumupa zina lyachiindi choonse chiza? Mubufwiifwi, aboobo, nswi mpati iitegwa whale nswi iizwa mumulomo uujisi mucila uulangene . Ooko nkomujisi. Nikuba kuti kwacheya, bupanduluzi oobo mbuzwa mukuzinzibala kuyeeya kwakuyungizya. I walrus ilaamba mbuli nswi mpati, asi walrus taili nswi pe, nkaambo ilapona mumaanzi. Pesi bbala lyamamanino lyabupanduluzi lilaanguzu, mbuli mboliswaangene alyakusaanguna. Aafwaafwi kufumbwa muntu weelede kuti wakabona kuti nswi zyoonse nzyobazyi bantu bakkala anyika tazijisi mucila uupapalete pe, pele ulijisi mucila uuimvwi, naa uutanta akuseluka. Mpawo, aakati kanswi zizwa mumulomo, mucila, nikuba kuti ulakonzya kuba achiimo chili mbuli cheecho, ulatola busena buliimvwi.

Kwiinda mubupanduluzi buli atala aawa bwakuti nswi iitegwa whale ninzi, tandigwisyi pe kuzwa kubunyina bwa leviathanic chilenge chili coonse chamulwizi chili mbuli nswi mpati aba Nantucketers bajisi luzibo lupati; nokuba, kulubazu lumwi, kuswaanganya answi zili zyoonse zyakali kubonwa kuti nzyeenzu.* Aboobo, nswi zyoonse ziniini, zizwa, alimwi zijisi mucila uulangene zyeelede kubikkilizyigwa mububambe oobu bwa Cetology. Lino, aboobo, kwaboola zibeela zipati zya whale yoonse.

*Ndilizi kuti kusikila sunu, nswi zyaambwa kuti Lamatins a Dugongs (Pig-fish a Sow-fish of the Coffins of Nantucket) zilabikkilizyigwa abasyaazibwene banji akati kanswi mpati. Asi mbuli nswi eezyi zili muzuzumo, zilasampaulwa, bunji bwaziindi zilayubila mumilomo yamilonga, mpawo zilalya bwizu buteteete, ikapati mbuli mbuzitazwi, ndilakaka kuti tazili nswi mpati; mpawo bakabapa mapasipoti aabo kuti bazwe muBwami bwaCetology.

Chakusanguna: Kweelana abupati bwawo ndakaandaanya nswi mpati muzibeela zyotatwe (zikonzya kwaandaanizyigwa muzibeela), mpawo eezyi ziyoozimvwisisisya zyoonse, ziniini azipati.

I. I FOLIO WHALE; II. OCTAVO WHALE; III. DUODECIMO WHALE.

Mbuli musyobo wa FOLIO ndipa Sperm Whale ; ya OCTAVO, Grampus ; ya DUODECIMO, Porpoise .

FOLIOS. Akati kazyeezyi aawa ndibikkilizya zibalo zitobela:—I. I Sperm Whale ; II. Nswi mpati yakululyo ; III. nswi iitegwa Fin-Back Whale ; IV. Inswi iitegwa Hump-backed Whale ; V. Nswi ya Razor Back ; VI. Inswi ya Sulfur Bottom Whale .

IBBUKU I. ( Folio ), CHAPTER I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Eeyi nswi, akati kaba England bakaindi iizyibidwe kuti nswi ya Trumpa, a Physeter whale, a Anvil Headed whale, nji Cachalot yamazubaano yaba Fulenci, a Pottsfich yaba German, a Macrocelus ya Long Words. Nguwe, kakunyina kudooneka, muntu mupati kwiinda boonse munyika; nswi iiyoosya kwiinda nswi zyoonse nzyotukonzya kuswaangana; chibotu kwiinda zyoonse; mpawo chamamanino, nchintu chiyandikana loko mumakwebo; walo kali chilengwa alikke oomo cintu eeco ciyandisi, spermaceti, mocijanwa. Zibeela zyakwe zyoonse, mumasena manji, ziyookomezyegwa. Ikapati azina lyakwe ndendeelede kuchita lino. Kuti twalanga-langa munzila yabusongo, nchintu chitali kabotu pe. Myaanda yaminyaka yainda, aawo nswi mpati ya Sperm whale niyatakazyibidwe pe, mpawo mafuta aakwe naakajanwa biyo kuzwa kunswi yakasweeka; mumazuba aayo spermaceti, mbuli mbukunga, wakali kuyeeyelwa kuti wakazwa kuchilengwa chili mbuli eecho chakazyibidwe muEngland kuti Greenland naakuti Right Whale. Wakali muzeezo alimwi, wakuti ooyu spermaceti nguwenya wakali kuseka kwa Greenland Whale ooko bbala lyakusaanguna lyabbala mbolyaamba. Muchiindi eecho, awalo, spermaceti yakalikucheya loko, tiiyakali kubelesegwa mbuli mumuni, pesi yakali kubelesegwa buyo mbuli mafwuta amusamu. Chakali buyo kujanwa kuzwa kuli basikuuzya misamu nkaambo mazubaano ulaula ounce ya rhubarb. Eelyo, mbuli mbondiyeeya, mukuya kwachiindi, bube bwini bwa spermaceti bwakazyibwa, zina lyawo lyakusaanguna lyakazumanana kuba abasambazi; kakunyina kudooneka kuti iyungizye mpindu yayo kwiinda mumuzeezo uukankamanisya wakubula kwayo. Mpawo zina eeli lyeelede kuti lyakapegwa ku nswi mpati oomo mokwakazwa ncobeni spermaceti.

IBBUKU I. ( Folio ), CHAANDANO II. ( Right Whale ).—Munzila imwi ooyu ngomunyama uulemekwa kwiinda ba leviathan, nkaambo ngonguwe wakasaanguna kuvwimwa amuntu. Zipa chintu chizyibidwe kuti whalebone naakuti baleen; amafuta aazyibidwe kapati kuti "mafuta aa whale," nchintu chitali kabotu mumakwebo. Akati kabasikuzuba nswi, wakapegwa mazina aaya woonse: Nswi mpati; nswi mpati ya Greenland; nswi iitegwa Black Whale; nswi mpati; Nswi Yamasimpe; Nswi mpati yakululyo. Kuli kutamvwisisika kupati aatala akuti musyobo ooyu wakabbapatizyigwa mubunji. Ino ninzi nswi mpati, eeyo njondibikkilizya mumusyobo wabili wa Folios yangu? Ngu Mysticetus Mupati wabasyaabusongo baku England; nswi mpati ya Greenland yaba English whalemen; Baleine Ordinaire yaba French whalemen; inswi ya Growlands yaba Sweden. Nswi mpati eeyo kwamyaanda yamyaka iitandila kuli yobile yainda yakali kuvwimwa aba Dutch aba England mulwizi lwa Arctic; ninswi mpati eeyo bazubi banswi baku Amelika njobakali kutobela kwachiindi chilamfu mulwizi lwa India, kuBrazil Banks, kunkomwe yaNor' West Coast, amumasena aambi munyika, aayo aakasalwa kuti ngamasena aakuzuba nswi.

Bamwi bachita mbuli kuti babona musiyano aakati ka Greenland whale yaba England a right whale yaba Amelika. Pesi balazuminana muzibeela zyabo zyoonse zipati; alimwi kunyina kaambo komwe kakatondezyegwa kakonzya kupa kuti kube kwaandaanya kupati. Nkwiinda mukwaandaanya kutamani kweendelanya akusiyana kutamani, ooko zibeela zimwi zya makani aazilengwa leza nkoziba zintu zikatazya kumvwisisisya. Inswi mpati iitegwa right whale iyoobandikwa kubusena bumbi kwachiindi chilamfu, kakulangwa kupandulula nswi mpati ya sperm whale.

IBBUKU I. ( Folio ), CHAANDANO III. ( Fin-Back ).—Munsi lyamutwe ooyu ndibona kuti kuli silunya ooyo, amazina aasiyene-siyene aa Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, a Long-John, wakabonwa afwaafwi mulwizi loonse alimwi kanjikanji ngu whale ooyo ndeke yakwe iili kule iipandululwa kapati abantu bazabuka lwizi lwa Atlantic, munzila zya New York. Mubulamfu mbwasika, amubbaleen yakwe, Fin-back ilikozyene a right whale, pesi ilijisi bupati busyoonto, alimwi ilijisi mubala uusalala pyu, ilaswena afwaafwi a olive. Milomo yakwe mipati itondezya mbuli ntambo, yakapangwa akuvwundauka, kuvwundauka kwamakumba mapati. Cibeela chakwe chipati chakwaandaanya, nkumba, ooko nkwaakajana zina lyakwe, kanjikanji nchintu chilibonya. Eeyi nsingo ilalampa mamita aatandila kuli otatwe naa one, ilakomena kuzwa kusule lyamusana, ili muchimo chakona, mpo ilijisi mutwe uubosya loko. Nikuba kuti kakutalangwi cibeela chimwi chachilengwa, eechi chipapa chili alikke, chimwi chiindi, chilabonwa kachizwa atala. Eelyo lwizi noluliimvwi nji, mpo kalujisi mayuwe aali mbuli bbola, mpawo eechi chintu chilimbuli gnomon chinyampuka mpawo chibikka mitunta atala aamaanzi, chilakonzya kuyeeyelwa kuti lwizi lwamaanzi lulizingulukide lulakozyanya a dial, abukkale bwalyo amizila yamawoola iijisi mayuwe aalembedwe aali njiyo. Aawo Ahaz-dial chimvwule chilajokela musule. I Fin-Back taili yabulongwe pe. Uboneka mbuli kuti ngusikusulaika nswi mpati, mbuli bamwi baalumi mbobasulaika bantu. Ulaa nsoni loko; lyoonse koli alikke; kutalangilwi kuya mujulu mumaanzi aali kulaale loko alimwi aajisi busi; ndeke yakwe iiluleme alimwi yomwe iisumpukide iitanta mbuli sumo ndamfu yabantu batakwe luyando atalaa nyika iitakwe maanzi; upedwe nguzu zigambya akufwambaana mukudubbwa, zyakuti ulakonzya kukazya zyoonse nzyobayanda bantu; ooyu leviathan ulibonya mbuli Kaini wakatandwa alimwi uutakonzyi kuzundwa wamukowa wakwe, kajisi citondezyo chakwe chiimo eecho kumusana wakwe. Kuzwa kukuba a baleen mumulomo wakwe, Fin-Back chimwi chiindi ulabikkilizyigwa antoomwe a whale yalulyo, aakati kamusyobo uutegwa Whalebone whales , nkokuti, whale zili a baleen. Akati kazeezi zya Whalebone whales, kuli misyobo myiingi, bunji bwazyo, nokuba boobo, tazizyibidwe pe. Inswi iijisi nsingo mpati answi iijisi milomo; nswi iijisi mutwe wa pike; nswi mpati; nswi zili munsi aamalomo answi zijisi mababa, ngamazina aabasikuzuba nswi aamisyobo misyoonto.

Mukujatikizya bbala eeli lyakuti "Whalebone whales," nchintu chipati kwaamba, kuti nikuba kuti zyina liliboobu lilakonzya kugwasya kwaamba misyobo imwi yaba whale, pesi nkubula mpindu kusola kwaandaanya Leviathan, kweendelana a baleen, naa hump, naa fin, naa teeth; nikuba kuti zibeela eezyo zilembedwe naakuti zibeela zilibonya kuti zili kabotu kuti zikonzye kupa ntalisyo yabweende bwa Cetology kwiinda zimwi zibeela zya mubili ziliimpene, eezyo nswi mpati, mumusyobo wakwe, njiitondezya. Ino mbuti mpawo? Ibbaleen, hump, back-fin, antoomwe ameno; eezyi nzintu zijisi bube bwazyo bumwaikide akati kamisyobo yoonse yaba whale, kakunyina kulanga bube bwazyo muzintu zimwi ziyandika kapati. Aboobo, nswi mpati ya sperm whale a humpbacked whale, umwi aumwi ulaa nswi; pesi ooko kukozyanya kwamana. Mpawo, eeyi nswi njiyonya iijisi nswi iitegwa humpbacked whale a Greenland whale, aimwi aimwi ilijisi baleen; pesi aawo alimwi kukozyanya kwamana. Alimwi mbubwenya buyo azibeela zimwi zyaambwa atala aawa. Mumisyobo isiyene-siyene yaba whale, balapanga miswaangano iitaluleme; naakuti, muchiimo chakuti umwi wabo waandaanizyigwa, kwaandaanya kutali kabotu; mbuli kukazya nzila zyoonse zyakabambilwa aawo. Aa bbwe eeli umwi awumwi wabasyaazibwene mumakani aazilenge zya whale wakaandaana.

Pesi chilakonzya kuyeeyelwa kuti, muzibeela zyamukati zya nswi mpati, mumubili wakwe—ooko, asyoonto, tulakonzya kujana nzila yeelede. Peepe; nchintu nzi, mbuli chitondeezyo, chiliwo mumubili wa Greenland whale uugambya kwiinda baleen yakwe? Pesi twabona kuti kwiinda mukubelesya baleen yakwe tazikonzeki pe kwaandaanya nswi mpati ya Greenland. Alimwi kuti waseluka mukati kama leviathans aasiyene, nkamboonzi ooko nkotakonzyi kujana kwaandaanya kwamakumi osanwe mbuli mbukonzya kujanika kuli sikubamba mbuli eezyo zyaanze zyaambwa kale. Ino ncinzi chisyaala? kunyina pe pele kujata nswi mpati, mubunji bwazyo boonse, mpawo uzisalazye munzila eeyo. Alimwi eeyi njenzila yamabbuku aawa yakazuminwa; mpawo njiyo ilikke iikonzya kuzwidilila, nkaambo njiyo ilikke ilakonzya kuchitwa. Kuzumanana.

IBBUKU I. ( Folio ) CHAANDANO IV. ( Hump Back ).—Eeyi nswi iitegwa whale kanjikanji ilabonwa kunkomwe yalwizi lwa Amelika nkoili kunyika. Wakali kujatwa chiindi chiingi ooko, mpawo wakakwelwa kuya kuchibbeeke. Ulijisi nkomo mpati mbuli sikusambala; naakuti ulakonzya kumwiita kuti Nzovu aCastle whale. Kufumbwa mbocibede, zina lyakwe lidumide talimupa kwaandaanya pe, nkaambo nswi iitegwa sperm whale ayalo ilijisi nsingo nokuba kuti niini. Mafuta aakwe taali aamuulo pe. Ulijisi baleen. Nguwe uuyandisya kusobana alimwi ulaa moyo muubauba kwiinda ba whale boonse, ulapanga mafwuta manji aa gay foam alimwi amaanzi aatuba kwiinda bamwi boonse.

IBBUKU I. ( Folio ), CHAANDANO V. ( Razor Back ).—Kujatikizya nswi eeyi ncisyoonto buyo cizyibidwe pele buyo zina lyakwe. Ndakamubona kulaale kuzwa ku Cape Horn. Mbuli muntu uuleka mulimu, ulatantamuka bavwimi abasyaabusongo. Nikuba kuti teensi muyoofu, tanaakatondezya chibeela chakwe pele buyo musana wakwe, ooyo uutanta mumulongo mulamfu uubosya. Amumuleke aunke. Ndilizyi mbichani aatala anguwe, mpawo taakwe umbi uuzyi pe.

IBBUKU I. ( Folio ), CHAANDANO VI. ( Sulphur Bottom ).—Umwi mwaalumi uuzwa kumulimu, wakajisi mwida lyasulfure, cakutadooneka wakajana kwiinda mukukwasula matayili aa Tartarian mulizimwi zyakusotokela kwakwe. Tabwene pe; kunyina nindakamubona kunze lyamulwizi lwakumusanza, mpawo lyoonse kali kulaale loko kuti ndikonzye kwiiya busyu bwakwe. Kunyina natandaanizyigwa pe; wakali kuchija akweenda ntambo. Ba Prodigies baambilwa aa nguwe. Kozwa, Sulfur Bottom! Tandikonzyi kwaamba chintu chili choonzyo kuli nduwe, naakuti Nantucketer uchembede takonzyi kwaamba.

OCTAVOES.*—Eezi zijata nswi mpati zili akati-akati, akati kazyeezyo ziliwo zilakonzya kubalwa:—I., the Grampus ; II., Inswi Isiya ; III., Narwhale ; IV., Sikuuma ; V., Mujayi .

*Nkamboonzi bbuku eeli lyama whale ndyotakali kwiitwa kuti Quarto nchintu chilisalede. Nkaambo, nikuba kuti nswi zyamumulongo ooyu, nikuba kuti nzisyoonto kwiinda zyamulongo wakusaanguna, nokuba boobo zilazumanana kuba mbuli mbabo muchifanikisyo, pesi bbuku lya Quarto lyabasikubamba mabbuku muchiimo chalyo chabupati talikwabili chiimo cha bbuku lya Folio, pesi bbuku lya Octavo lilazumanana.

IBBUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAANDANO I. ( Grampus ).—Nikuba kuti inswi eeyi, iizwa mujulu, naakuti kuyoya, yakapa kaambyo kubantu bakkala mumaanzi, ilizyibidwe loko kuti ikkala mumaanzi, pesi taibikkilizyidwe akati ka nswi mpati. Pesi kajisi zitondezyo zyoonse zipati zya leviathan, bunji bwabasyaabusongo bazilenge bakamuzyiba kuti ngumwi. Ulijisi bupati bwa octavo buli akati-akati, bwiindene kuzwa kumaulu aali kkumi amusanu kusikila kumakumi obile amusanu mubulamfu, alimwi ulaa bupati bweelene kuzinguluka chiwuno. Uladubbwa mumatanga; kunyina navwimwa chiindi choonse, nikuba kuti mafuta aakwe manji loko, mpawo mabotu loko mumuni. Kuli bamwi basikuzuba nswi nzila yakwe ilabonwa mbuli chitondezyo chakuya kunembo kwa nswi mpati ya sperm whale.

IBBUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAANDANO II. ( Nswi Zisiya ).—Ndapa mazina aabasikuzuba nswi bazyibidwe kapati kunswi zyoonse eezyi, nkaambo kanjikanji nzibotu kwiinda. Aawo zina lili lyoonse nolitamvwisyi pe, ndilaamba oobo, mpawo ndasala limbi. Ndilachita oobo lino, kuguma Inswi Isiya, eeyo yiitwa kuti, nkaambo kusiya ngomulawu akati kabaswi boonse. Aboobo, komwiita kuti Hyena Whale, kuti koyanda. Busungu bwakwe bulizyibidwe, mpawo kuzwa muchiimo chakuti milomo yakwe yamukati yakakotama kuya mujulu, ulaa kumweta-mweta kutamani kwa Mephistophelean kubusyu bwakwe. Eeyi nswi mpati ilaa mweelwe waakati-kati wamafiti aali kkumi amusanu atutatu naa kkumi atutatwe mubulamfu. Ulajanika mumasena woonse. Ulijisi nzila iilibedelede yakutondezya nsingo yakwe yamusyule iijisi nkomo mukudubbwa, eeyo ilibonya mbuli nsingo yabana Loma. Eelyo nobatabeleki kabotu, bavwimi ba sperm whale chimwi chiindi balajata Hyena whale, kuti bazumanane kujana mafuta aataduli aakubelesya mumaanda—mbuli bamwi basikulanganya maanda batakwe mali, kabatakwe mweenzinyina, alimwi balikke, baumpa mafuta aatali kabotu muciindi ca wax uununkilila. Nikuba kuti mafuta aabo masyoonto loko, amwi aya ma whale alakonzya kukupa malita aayinda ku makumi otatwe aamafuta.

IBBUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAANDANO III. ( Narwhale ), nkokuti, Nostril whale .—Chimwi chikozyano cha whale iitegwa whale, yakapegwa zina eeli ndiyeya kuti nkaambo kameja aakwe aakali kuyeeyelwa kuti akali ampemo mpati. Chilengwa eechi chili amaulu aali kkumi amusanu mubulamfu, mpawo meja aacho ali akati-akati amamita aali musanu, nikuba kuti amwi alainda aali kkumi, mane asika kumamita aali kkumi amusanu. Mukwaamba biyo, lwija oolu nduno lulamfwu luzutu, lukomena kuzwa kumulomo mumulongo uunyonyooka asyoonto kuzwa kulubazu. Pesi lilajanika buyo kulubazu lubi, oolo lujisi ntenda, kupa mukamwini chintu chili mbuli muntu uubelesya janza lyalumwensyi uutakwe maanu. Ino makanze nzi eechi mweembo wa nzovu naa sumo ndyoliingula, inga tiikwaba kuyuma kwaamba. Tachilibonyi kuti chibelesegwa mbuli bbwe lyanswi yapanga answi; nikuba kuti bamwi basikweenzya bwato bandibuzya kuti Narwhale ichibelesya mbuli chintu chakusanzya lwizi kuti chijane chakulya. Charley Coffin wakati yakali kubelesegwa mbuli chintu chakubboola aisi; nkaambo Narwhale, yakatanta kuya atalaa Lwizi lwa Polar, mpawo yakajana kuti yakavwumbidwe aisi, yakanyamuna lwija lwakwe mujulu, mpawo yakainda. Asi tokonzyi kutondezya kuti zyoonse zyobile eezyi zililuzi. Muzeezo wangu ngwakuti, nikuba kuti mweembo ooyu uuli kulubazu lomwe ulakonzya kubelesegwa a Narwhale—nikuba kuti inga waba buti—inga chamugwasya kuti abe afooloda yakubala mapepa. Inswi iitegwa Narwhale njondakamvwa yiitwa kuti Tusked whale, Horned whale, antoomwe a Unicorn whale. Nchobeni nchikozyano chibotu cha Unicornism chijanika mubwami boonse bwazilenge zipona. Kuzwa kuli balembi bamwi bakaindi ndakajana kuti mweembo ooyu wa sea unicorn mumazuba aakaindi wakali kubonwa kuti ngo musamu mupati wakulesya poizoni, aboobo, kulibambila kwakali kuleta muulo mupati. Alimwi lyakali kusanzyigwa kuba munyo uukonzya kuzungaana kubanakazi bafwide nsoni, mbweenya mbuli meja aanziba mwalumi mbwaapangwa kuti abe hartshorn. Kumatalikilo lyakali kubonwa kuti nchintu chakuyandisya kuziba. Lugwalo Lusiya lwandibuzya kuti Sir Martin Frobisher naakapiluka kuzwa mulweendo oolo, aawo Mwami Mukaintu Bess naakamuzungaanya janza lyakwe lyamabwe mayandisi kuzwa kuwindo lyaGreenwich Palace, aawo bwato bwakwe nibwakali kweenda munsi lya Thames; "Chiindi Sir Martin naakapiluka kuzwa mulweendo oolo," wakaamba Black Letter, "kafwugama wakapa kuli simalelo wakwe mweembo mulamfu mupati wa Narwhale, walo wakalengwa muŋanda yamwami ku Windsor kwachiindi chilamfu." Mulembi waku Ireland wakaamba kuti Earl waku Leicester, wakafwugama, wakapa kuli silutwe wakwe luja lumbi, lwakali lwamunyama wamunyika wamusyobo wa unicorn.

Inswi iitegwa Narwhale ilibonya mbuli ingwe, ilijisi mubala uutuba mbuli malili, iijisi mabala aazungulukide aasalala pyu. Mafuta aakwe alisumpukide loko, asalala alimwi mabotu; pesi nchintu chiche, mpawo tavwimi pe. Ujanika kapati mulwizi lwa circumpolar.

IBBUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER IV. ( Mujayi ).—Kujatikizya nswi eeyi mbichani zyizyibidwe kuliba Nantucketer, mpawo kunyina pe kulibaabo balyaamba kuti syaazibwene mumakani aazilengwa leza. Kuzwa kuli ceeco ncendakamubona kulaale, inga ndaamba kuti wakali mbuli bupati bwa grampus. Uli silunya loko—mbuli nswi ya Feegee. Chimwi chiindi ulajata nswi mpati ya Folio kumulomo, mpawo ulaimvwi aawo mbuli nswi, kusikila munyama mupati uyoofwa. Sikujaya kunyina navwimwa pe. teensi ndakamvwa kuti ulijisi mafuta aali buti. Kusiyana kulakonzya kutolwa kuzina lyakapegwa kuli nswi eeyi, akaambo kakutasalala kwayo. Nkaambo toonse tuli bajayi, anyika amulwizi; Bonapartes a Sharks bakabikkilizyigwa.

IBBUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHAPTER V. ( Thrasher ).—Ooyu mwaalumi uulemekwa ulidumide amuchila wakwe, ooyo ngwabelesya mbuli ferule mukuuma basinkondonyina. Wakatanta musana wa Folio whale, mpawo mbuli mbwaakadubbwa, wakachita nzila yakwe kwiinda mukumuuma; mbuli bamwi bamayi mbobapona munyika kwiinda munzila iikozyenye. Kuchili chintu chiche chizyibidwe aatala aSikuuma kwiinda Sikujaya. Boonse bobile mbazangi, nikuba mulwizi lutakwe milawu.

Mbombubo mbolimana bbuku lyabili. ( Octavo ), mpawo watalika bbuku lyatatu. ( Duodecimo ).

DUODECIMOES.—Eezi zibikkilizya nswi nsyoonto. I. I Huzza Porpoise. II. Inswi yaku Algerine. III. Ipopwe lyamulomo uutegwa Mealy.

Kuli baabo batakwe mweenya wakwiiya makani aaya, chilakonzya kubonekaanga nchintu chakankamanisya, kuti nswi zitaindi maulu one naa osanwe zyeelede kubikwa akati ka WHALES—ibbala, eelyo, munzila iidumide, lyoonse litondezya muzeezo wabupati. Pesi zilenge zibikkidwe atala aawa mbuli Duodecimoes nzintu zitakwe kampenda, kweendelanya abupanduluzi bwangu bwakuti whale ninzi— nkokuti nswi iizwa, iijisi mucila uulangene.

Ibbuku lyatatu. ( Duodecimo ), CHAANDANO 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Ooyu ngo porpoise uujanika koonse koonse munyika. Izina lyakazwa kulindime ndemwini; nkaambo kuli misyobo yiinda kuli yomwe yaba porpoises, mpawo chimwi chintu cheelede kuchitwa kuti baandaanizyigwe. Ndimwiita oobo, nkaambo lyoonse uladubbwa muzilundu zisesya, eezyo atalaa lwizi lupati zizumanana kulifwusa kujulu mbuli zikobela mukabunga ka Fourth-of-July. Kuboneka kwabo kanjikanji kulalumbaizigwa abasikutanta bwato. Kazwide myuuya mibotu, lyoonse balazwa mumayuwe aajisi guwo kuya kulubazu lwamuwo. Mbalombwana bapona lyoonse kaitanachitika guwo. Zibonwa kuti nzitondezyo zyacoolwe. Kuti yebo omwini ulakonzya kuliyumya ziindi zyotatwe zyakusekelela kubona nswi eezyi zijisi buumi, mpawo julu likugwasye; muuya wakusobana kwabunaleza tauli muli ndinywe. Huzza Porpoise uulya kabotu, uuneneede ulakonzya kukupa galoni lyomwe lyamafuta mabotu. Pesi maanzi mabotu alimwi mabotu aazwa kumilomo yakwe alayandika loko. Nchikulomba kuli basikupanga mabwe mayandisi abasikupanga mawoola. Basikutanta bwato balachibikka kumahoni aabo. Nyama ya porpoise njibotu kulya, ulizi. Ambweni kunyina nowakasika mumizeezo yako kuti nziba ilazwa. Masimpe, mulomo wakwe muniini loko zyakuti taukonzyi kubonwa pe. Pesi chiindi chiboola kuti wajana choolwe, komulangisisya; mpawo uyoobona nswi mpati ya Sperm whale lwakwe mwini kali muniini.

Ibbuku lyatatu. ( Duodecimo ), CHAANDANO II. ( Algerine Porpoise ).—Mubbi. Lunya loko. Ulajanika biyo, ndiyeya, muPacific. Mupati asyoonto kwiinda Huzza Porpoise, pesi ulikozyenye. Amumunyemye, mpawo ulafwundilila kuba nswi mpati. Ndakamufwubaazya ziindi zyiingi, pesi teensi ndakamubona kajatwa.

Ibbuku lyatatu. ( Duodecimo ), CHAANDANO III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Musyobo mupati wa Porpoise; alimwi ilajanika buyo muPacific, mbuli mbuzyibidwe. Izina lilikke lyachiNgisi, ndyaakali kwiitwa kusikila sunu, ndyabasikuzuba nswi—Right-Whale Porpoise, kuzwa mubukkale bwakuti ujanika kapati afwaafwi a Folio eeyo. Muchiimo, uliimpene a Huzza Porpoise, mbwaanga tali muzungulukide pe mpawo ulabotezya; masimpe, uli kabotu loko alimwi ulimbuli mwaalumi mubotu. Takwe mababa kumusana wakwe (bunji bwama porpoises alijisi), ulijisi mucila mubotu, ameso aaku India aajisi mubala wa hazel. Asi mulomo wakwe uujisi fulaulu ulanyonyoona zyoonse. Nikuba kuti musana wakwe woonse kusikila kumababa aakwe uli aamusamu uusiya mbichaani, pesi kuli munyinza, uulibonya mbuli chitondezyo chabwato, wiitwa kuti "chibuno chimweka," ooyo muzila ulamutondezya kuzwa kuchisiko kusikila kusule, amibala yobile yiindene, mubala muubauba atala amubala uutuba munsi. Ikutuba kuli chibeela chamutwe wakwe, amulomo wakwe woonse, eecho chimupa kuti aboneke mbuli kuti wakachija kuzwa kukuswaya chibbeeke chakulya. Chintu chibi loko! Mafuta aakwe alikozyene aa porpoise.

* * * * * *

Kunze lya DUODECIMO, eeyi nzila taizumanani pe, nkaambo Porpoise ngomuniini kwiinda nswi mpati. Atala, ulijisi ba Leviathan boonse bazyibidwe. Pesi kuli nkamu ya nswi zitazibinkene, zitija, zitakwe maanu, eezyo, mbuli muvwubi waku Amelika, ndizizyi kwiinda muzina, pesi kutali ndemwini. Ndiyoobabala kwiinda muzina lyabo lyakumbele; nkaambo ambweni mulongo uuliboobu ulakonzya kugwasya kuli basikuvwuntauzya bakumbele, aabo bakonzya kumanizya nzyondijisi aawa pesi ndakatalika. Kuti umwi akati kaziswi zitobela, uyoojatwa akubikkwa chitondezyo, mpawo ulakonzya kubikwa mubweende oobu, kweendelanya a Folio, Octavo, naa Duodecimo magnitude:—I-Bottle-Nose Whale; Nswi iitegwa Junk Whale; Nswi iitegwa Pudding-Headed Whale; nswi mpati ya Cape; Nswi Iisololela; nswi iitegwa Cannon Whale; Scragg Whale; nswi iitegwa Coppered Whale; Nswi mpati ya Nzovu; Iceberg Whale; nswi mpati ya Quog; nswi mpati; azimwi. Kuzwa kuli beendelezi baku Iceland, Dutch, aba England bakaindi, inga kwazubululwa milongo imwi ya nswi mpati zitazyibidwe, zilongezyedwe amazina aatali kabotu. Pesi ndazisiya mbuli kuti tazibeleki pe; alimwi takukonzyi kugwasya pe kudooneka kuti nzintu zili buyo, zizwide bu Leviathanism, pesi tazitondezyi chintu pe.

Kumamanino: Kwakaambwa kumatalikilo, kuti eeyi bweende tabukonzyi kuba aano, mpawo mpawo, kulondolwa. Tokonzyi pele kubona antangalala kuti ndakabamba jwi lyangu. Pesi lino ndasiya nzila yangu yakuzyalwa kaitanamanizyigwa, mbweenya mbuli Ŋanda mpati ya Cologne mboyakasyaala, a crane kaimvwi atala aa ngazi iitamanizyidwe. Nkaambo mayake masyoonto alakonzya kumanizyigwa abasikuyaka bakusaanguna; bapati, bachoonzyo, lyoonse balasiya bbwe lyachikozyano kulibazyalani. Leza undikwabilile kuti nditamanizyi chintu chilichose. Ibbuku lyoonse eeli ndibbuku lyakulemba buyo—peepe, pesi ndibbuku lyakulemba. Oh, chiindi, manguzu, mali, akukkazika moyo!
