MUTU 4. Chigaŵa cha Kukhozgera.

Nkhati nawuka mulenji wa zuŵa lakulondezgapo pafupifupi muhanya, nkhasanga kuti woko la Queequeg laniponyera mu nthowa yacitemwa comene ndiposo yakutemweka. Mukaŵa pafupi kughanaghana kuti nkhaŵa muwoli wake. Counterpane yikaŵa ya patchwork, yakuzura na vigaŵa vichokovichoko vya mitundu yakupambanapambana na makona ghatatu; ndipo woko lake ili likaŵa na tattoo yose na cithuzithuzi cambura kumara ca ku Crete, palije vigaŵa viŵiri ivyo vikaŵa vya mtundu umoza weneco—cifukwa cakuti nkhughanaghana kuti wakasunga woko lake pa nyanja kwambura nthowa mu zuŵa na mthunzi, mawoko gha malaya ghake ghakaŵa ghakuvundulika mwambura kukhazikika pa nyengo zakupambanapambana—ici cikuwoneka nga ni caru cose, quilt yakupangika na vingwe. Nadi, cigaŵa cinyake cikagona pa iyo nga umo woko likacitiranga apo nkhawukanga pakwamba, nkhamanyanga yayi kufuma pa quilt, ŵakasazgananga comene mitundu yawo pamoza; ndipo nkhamanya waka kuti Queequeg wakunikumbatira chifukwa cha kujipulika kuti nkhulemera na kuchichizgika.

Ivyo nkhapulikanga vikaŵa vyakuzizika. Lekani niyezge kuŵalongosolera. Apo nkhaŵa mwana, nkhukumbuka makora cinthu cinyake cakuyana waka ico cikanicitikira; kwali vikaŵa vyaunenesko panji maloto, nkhatondekanga kukhazikika mwakukwana. Nkhani yikaŵa iyi. Nkhaŵa kuti nkhudumura caper panji chinyake—nkhughanaghana kuti chikayezganga kukhwaŵa pachanya pa chimbuzi, nga umo nkhawonera kusefa pachoko waka mazuŵa ghachoko waka ghajumphapo; ndipo amama ŵane ŵaciŵiri awo, mu nthowa yinyake panji yinyake, nyengo zose ŵakanitimbaniranga, panji kunituma ku bedi kwambura cakurya ca mise, —amama ŵakanikoranga na malundi kufuma mu chimbuzi na kunipakira kuti nkhagone, nangauli likaŵa waka 2 koloko muhanya pa Juni 21, zuŵa litali comene mu cilimika mu hemisphere yithu. Nkhaŵa na wofi chomene. Kweni pakaŵavya wovwiri, ntheura nkhakwera pa makwelero nkhaluta ku chipinda chane chichoko icho chikaŵa pa chipinda chachitatu, nkhavula vyakuvwara pachokopachoko mwakuti nikome nyengo, ndipo na kupuma mwakuŵinya nkhanjira pakati pa malaya.

Nkhagona penepara mwakusuzgika maghanoghano na kupenda kuti maora ghakukwana 16 ghakwenera kujumphapo pambere nindaŵe na cigomezgo ca ciwuka. Maora 16 pa bedi! chichoko cha msana wane chikanipwetekanga kughanaghanapo. Ndipo likaŵa lakupepuka comene nalo; zuŵa likaŵala pa windo, na kulira kukuru kwa makochi mu misewu, na kulira kwa mazgu gha ŵasepuka ŵasepuka mu nyumba yose. Nkhajipulika uheni comene—paumaliro nkhawuka, nkhavwara, ndipo nkhakhira mwakuzika mu malundi ghane ghakuvwara masitokisi, nkhapenja amama ŵane ŵaciŵiri, ndipo mwamabuci nkhajiponya mu malundi ghawo, kuŵaŵeyelera nga ni lusungu lwapadera kuti ŵanipe slipper yiwemi cifukwa ca nkharo yane yiheni; chilichose nadi kweni kunisuska kuti nigonenge nyengo yitali chomene yambura kuzizipizga. Kweni iyo wakaŵa muwemi comene ndiposo wakupwelelera comene ŵamama ŵaŵiri wose, ndipo para nawelera nkhakhumbikwiranga kuluta ku cipinda cane. Kwa maora ghanandi nkhagona penepara kwambura maso, ndipo nkhajipulikanga kuti nili wakusuzgika comene kuluska umo nkhajipulikiranga kufuma nyengo iyo, nanga ni cifukwa ca masuzgo ghakuru agho ghakaŵapo pamanyuma pake. Paumaliro nkhwenera kuti nkhawa mu maloto ghakusuzga gha tulo; ndipo pachokopachoko nkhawuka kufumamo—hafu yake yikaŵa yakuzura na maloto—nkhajura maso ghane, ndipo chipinda icho chikaŵa kuti chindaŵe na dazi sono chikaŵa na mdima wakuwalo. Nyengo yeneyiyo nkhapulika kukhwaskika kukuchimbira mu furemu yane yose; palije ico cikawonekanga, ndipo palije ico cikapulikikwanga; kweni woko lakuzizwiska likawonekanga kuti laŵikika mu lane. Mkono wane ukapendera pa counterpane, ndipo chithunzithunzi chambura zina, chambura kughanaghanirika, chambura kuyowoya panji chithuzithuzi, icho woko likaŵa lake, chikawonekanga kuti chakhala pafupi pafupi na bedi lane. Pakuti ivyo vikawonekanga nga ni misinkhu yakuwunjika pa misinkhu, nkhagona penepara, wakuzizima na wofi wakofya comene, nkhaŵavya cikanga ca kukoka woko lane; kweni nyengo zose nkhughanaghana kuti usange ningacisunkhunya waka inchi yimoza, mzimu wakofya ungaphwanyika. Nkhamanyanga yayi umo chimanyisko ichi paumaliro chikachimbira kufuma kwa ine; kweni apo nkhawukanga mulenji, nkhakumbukanga vyose mwakukhwaskika, ndipo kwa mazuŵa na masabata na myezi pamanyuma pake nkhajitaya mu kuyezgayezga kwakutimbanizga kulongosora cisisi. Yayi, m’paka pa ora ili, kanandi nkhujipusika navyo.

Sono, fumiskanipo wofi wakofya, ndipo vyakupulika vyane pakupulika woko lakuziziswa mu lane vikaŵa vyakuyana waka, mu kuzizwiska kwawo, na ivyo nkhakumana navyo pakuwuka na kuwona woko la Queequeg la ŵamitundu likaponyeka kuzingilizga ine. Kweni paumaliro vyose ivyo vikacitika usiku uwo ukajumpha vikaweleraso, cimoza na cimoza, mu unenesko wakukhazikika, ndipo pamanyuma nkhagona waka wamoyo ku suzgo lakusekeska. Pakuti nangauli nkhayezga kusunkhunya woko lake—kujura chingwe chake cha mwenekaya—kweni, apo wakagona, wakanivumbatira chomene, nga ni para palije chinthu chinyake kweni nyifwa iyo yikwenera kutipatura ŵaŵiri. Sono nkhayezgayezga kumuwuska—“Queequeg!”—kweni zgoro lake likaŵa lakuti wakakhuŵara waka. Pamanyuma nkhagubuduka, singo lane likaŵa nga ni para lili mu khola la hachi; ndipo mwamabuchibuchi wakapulika kukhwaŵa pachoko waka. Pakuponya pambali counterpane, pakaŵa tomahawk uyo wakagona pafupi na savage, nga ni para ni mwana wa nkhope ya hatchet. Pickle wakutowa, nadi, nkhaghanaghananga; wakagona pano mu nyumba yachilendo muhanya wose, na munthu wakurya ŵanthu na tomahawk! “Fumukazi!—mu zina la uwemi, Fumukazi, wukani!” Paumaliro, cifukwa ca kukhweŵa comene, na kuyowoya mwakukwezga na kwambura kuleka pa kuleka kwenelera kwake kukumbatira mwanalume munyake mu mtundu wa nthengwa, nkhakwaniska kufumiska msinjiro; ndipo sonosono apa, wakawezgerapo woko lake, wakajisunkhunya thupi lose nga ni galu wa ku Newfoundland kufuma waka pa maji, ndipo wakakhala pa bedi, wakukhora nga ni ndodo ya pike, wakanilaŵiskanga, na kusisitira maso ghake nga kuti wakukumbuka yayi umo nkhaŵira apo, nangauli chinthu chinyake chikaniwona kuti nkhumanya. Pa nyengo iyi, nkhagona mwakufwasa na kumulaŵiska, sono nkhaŵavya kukayika kukuru, ndipo nkhaŵa wakujipeleka kuti niwone waka cilengiwa ico cikaŵa cakukhumba kumanya vinandi comene. Apo, paumaliro, maghanoghano ghake ghakawonekanga kuti ghapangika kukhwaska mikhaliro ya munyake uyo wakagona nayo pa bedi, ndipo wakazgoka, nga ni para wakagowokerana na unenesko; wakajumphilira kuwaro pasi, ndipo na vimanyikwiro vinyake na mazgu wakanipangiska kupulikiska kuti, usange vinganikondweska, wazamuvwara dankha ndipo pamanyuma wazamunileka kuti nivware pamanyuma, kusiya nyumba yose kwa ine. Ine nkhughanaghana kuti, Queequeg, mu mikhaliro, iyi ni nthowa yakutukuka comene; kweni, unenesko ngwakuti, ŵankhaza aŵa ŵali na mzimu wakubabikira wa vyakurya vyakunozga, yowoyani ivyo mukukhumba; nchakuzizika comene umo ŵakuŵira ŵalusungu comene. Nkhulumba chomene Queequeg, chifukwa wakanichitira vinthu mwaulemu chomene na kunighanaghanira, apo ine nkhaŵa na mulandu wa kujiyuyura chomene; kumulaŵiska kufuma pa bedi, na kulaŵiska vyose ivyo wakuchita mu chimbuzi; kwa nyengo iyo chikhumbo chane cha kumanya vinandi chikuŵa chiwemi pa kubereka kwane. Nangauli vikaŵa nthena, munthu nga ni Queequeg uyo mukumuwona yayi zuŵa lililose, iyo na nthowa zake vikaŵa vyakuzirwa chomene vyambura kumanyikwa.

Wakamba kuvwara pachanya mwa kuvwara chipewa chake cha beaver, chitali chomene, pafupi, ndipo pamanyuma—wakaŵa kuti wachali kwambura thalauza lake—wakapenja nsapato zake. Ivyo wakachitira pasi pa kuchanya, nkhumanya yayi, kweni ivyo wakachita pamanyuma vikaŵa vyakuti wajifyenye—mabutu mu woko, na kuvwara chipewa—pasi pa bedi; apo, kufuma ku mzimu wa nkhaza zakupambanapambana na kupwetekeka, nkhaghanaghana kuti wakagwiranga nchito mwamwamphu kujikwezga yekha; nangauli palije dango lakwenelera ilo nkhapulikapo, munthu waliyose wakukhumbikwa kuŵa pa yekha para wakuvwara nsapato zake. Kweni Queequeg, kasi mukuwona, wakaŵa cilengiwa ico cikaŵa mu nyengo ya kusintha—cibowo panji gulugufe yayi. Wakaŵa waka wakutukuka mwakukwana kuti walongore kuti wakaŵa wakujikuzga mu mikhaliro yakuzizika comene. Masambiro ghake ghakaŵa kuti ghandamalizgike. Wakaŵa wakusambira pa sukulu ya pulayimale. Usange wangaŵa kuti wakaŵa wakutukuka yayi, mphanyi wakajisuzga yayi na vyakuvwara vyakuvwara; kweni pa nyengo iyo, usange wakaŵa kuti wachali msinjiro yayi, mphanyi wakalotangapo yayi kuti wakanjira pasi pa bedi kuti wakavivware. Paumaliro, wakafumapo na chipewa chake chakuvunda chomene na kuphwanyika pasi pa maso ghake, ndipo wakamba kukhweŵa na kukhweŵa mu chipinda, nga ni para, chifukwa chakuti wakazgoŵera yayi kuvwara vingwe vyake vyakunyeŵa, vyakukhwinyata vya chikopa cha ng’ombe—panji vikapangika yayi kuti viŵe vyakukhumbikwa—m’malo mwake vikamukhwaska na kumusuzga pa nyengo yakwamba.

Pakuwona, sono, kuti pakaŵavya makateni gha pa windo, ndiposo kuti msewu ukaŵa wakupapatizga comene, nyumba iyo yikaŵa kuseri kwake yikalamulira kuwona makora mu cipinda, na kuwona comene-comene cithunzithunzi cambura kutowa ico Queequeg wakapanga, wakuzungulirazungulira na kanthu kacoko waka kweni cipewa cake na skapato zake; Nkhamuŵeyelera nga umo ningafiskira, kuti wacimbizge luŵiro cimbuzi cake pachoko waka, ndipo comenecomene kuti wanjire mu pantaloons yake mwaluŵiro. Wakapulikira, ndipo pamanyuma wakalutilira kugeza. Pa nyengo iyo mulenji Mkhristu waliyose wangageza nkhope yake; kweni Queequeg, mwakuzizwa, wakakhorwa na kukanizga kusamba kwake pa chifuŵa, mawoko, na mawoko. Pamanyuma wakavwara chingwe chake, ndipo wakatora sopo wakukhora uyo wakaŵa pa thebulu la pakati pa malo ghakugezerapo, wakawuŵika mu maji na kwamba kupanga thovu pa nkhope yake. Ine nkhawoneleranga kuti niwone uko wakasunga lezala lake, apo lo ndipo wonani, wakatora harpoon kufuma pa ngodya ya bedi, wakafumiska mkondo utali wa makuni, wakafumiska mutu, wakawutimba pachoko pa boot yake, ndipo wakakwera ku chigaŵa cha gilasi pa mtenje, wakamba kukhwaska mwa nkhongono, panji m’malo mwake harpoo yake. Ine nkhughanaghana kuti, Queequeg, ichi chikugwiriskira ncito viŵiya viwemi vya Rogers na kuwezgerapo. Pamanyuma pake nkhazizwa comene pa nchito iyi apo nkhamanya kuti mutu wa mkondo ukupangika na cisulo ciwemi wuli, ndiposo umo nyengo zose ŵakusungiramo mphonje zitali zakunyoloka zakuthwa comene.

Chimbuzi chake chose chikakwaniskika mwaluŵiro, ndipo wakafumamo mu chipinda mwakujikuzga, wakajizingilizga mu jekete lake likuru la nyamudolo, ndipo wakaseŵeranga mkondo wake nga ni ndodo ya marshal.





MUTU 5. Chakurya cha mulenji.

Ine nkhamulondezga mwaluŵiro, ndipo nkhakhira mu chipinda cha bar ndipo nkhamuchema mweneko wa nyumba uyo wakamwemwetera mwakukondweska chomene. Ine nkhatemwanga yayi kuŵa na uheni kwa iyo, nangauli wakaŵa kuti wakhala wakunicezga pa nkhani ya munyane wa pa bedi.

Ndipouli, kuseka kuwemi nchinthu ciwemi comene, ndipo m’malo mwake nchinthu ciwemi cakusoŵa comene; chomene ndicho chitima. Ntheura, usange munthu waliyose, mu munthu wake wakwenelera, wakukwaniska vinthu vyakuseŵera viwemi kwa waliyose, waleke kuŵa wakuwelera nyuma, kweni wazomerezge mwakukondwa kugwiliskira nchito na kugwiliskira nchito mu nthowa iyo. Ndipo munthu uyo ​​wali na cinthu cilicose cakusekeska comene pa iyo, manya kuti pali vinandi mwa munthu yura kuluska umo mukughanaghanirapo.

Chipinda cha bar sono chikazura na ŵalendo awo ŵakaŵa kuti ŵakhalako usiku uwo ukajumpha, ndipo awo nkhaŵa kuti nindaŵawone makora. Pafupifupi wose ŵakaŵa ŵanyama ŵa whale; ŵalongozgi ŵa ŵanyawo, ŵanyawo ŵachiŵiri, ŵanyawo ŵachitatu, ŵakalipentala ŵa pa nyanja, ŵakupanga vitsulo ŵa mu nyanja, ŵakupanga vitsulo ŵa mu nyanja, ŵakupanga vingwe vya mkondo, na ŵakusunga ngalaŵa; gulu la bulawuni na brawny, na ndevu za bosky; seti yambura kugega, ya shaggy, wose ŵakavwara majekete gha nyamudolo ghakuvwara magawuni gha mulenji.

Mungamanya makora kuti waliyose wakakhala nyengo yitali wuli pa mtunda. Saya la munyamata uyu ilo lili na umoyo uwemi lili nga ni pear ilo likuwotcheka na zuŵa, ndipo lingawoneka kuti likununkhira pafupifupi nga ni musky; wangaŵa kuti wakafika pa nyanja mazuŵa ghatatu yayi kufuma pa ulendo wake wa ku India. Munthu uyo ​​wali pafupi nayo wakuwoneka kuti wakupepuka mithunzi yichoko waka; mungati kukhwaska kwa khuni la satin kuli mwa iyo. Mu mtundu wa wachitatu wachali lingers tropic tawn, kweni pachoko wakutoŵa withal; mosakayikira wakhala masabata ghose ku mtunda. Kweni kasi ni njani uyo wangalongora saya nga ni Queequeg? iyo, yikaŵa na mitundu yakupambanapambana, yikawonekanga nga ni mtunda wa kumanjiliro gha dazi kwa Andes, kuti yiwoneske mu mzere umoza, nyengo zakupambana, malo na malo.

“Kurya, ho!” sono wakachemerezga mweneko wa nyumba, wakajura chipata, ndipo tikanjira kukarya chakurya cha mulenji.

Ŵakuti ŵanalume awo ŵawona caru, mwa ntheura ŵakuŵa ŵakufwatuka comene mu nkharo, ŵakujikora comene para ŵali na ŵanyawo. Kweni nyengo zose yayi: Ledyard, mulendo mulara wa ku New England, na Mungo Park, wa ku Scotland; pa ŵanalume wose, ŵakaŵa na chigomezgo chichoko chomene mu chipinda chakuchezgeramo. Kweni panji kujumpha waka mu Siberia mu sledge iyo yikakoleka na ŵagalu nga umo Ledyard wakacitira, panji kwenda mtunda utali payekha na nthumbo yambura kanthu, mu mtima wa ŵafipa ŵa ku Africa, uwo ukaŵa chiŵelengero cha ivyo Mungo wakacitanga—mtundu uwu wa ulendo, nkhuyowoya kuti, ungaŵa nthowa yiwemi comene yayi yakusangirapo polish a. Ndipouli, pa vigaŵa vinandi, vinthu vyanthena ivi vikwenera kuŵa kulikose.

Maghanoghano agha pano pera ghakupangika na mkhaliro uwo pamanyuma pakuti tose takhala pa thebulu, ndipo nkhanozgekeranga kupulika nkhani zinyake ziwemi zakukhwaskana na somba za whale; nkhazizwa comene kuti pafupifupi mwanalume waliyose wakakhala chete comene. Ndipo ndivyo pera yayi, kweni ŵakawonekanga kuti ŵachita soni. Enya, pano pakaŵa gulu la ŵagalu ŵa mu nyanja, ŵanandi ŵa iwo kwambura kucita soni nanga nkhucepa ŵakakwera pa somba zikuruzikuru za m’nyanja zikuruzikuru—ŵalendo wose kwa iwo—na kuŵalimbana nawo ŵakufwa kwambura kudodoliska; ndipouli, pano ŵakakhala pa thebulu la chakurya cha mulenji cha ŵanthu wose—wose ŵakachemeka mwakuyana waka, wose ŵakatemwanga vinthu vyakuyana waka—ŵakalaŵiskana mwakuzingilira nga ni mberere nga ni para ŵandafumepo pa chiŵaya chinyake cha mberere pakati pa Mapiri Ghakubiriŵira. Kuwona kwakukhumba kumanya vinandi; zimbalame za soni izi, ŵasilikari ŵa whalemen ŵa soni aŵa!

Kweni pa nkhani ya Queequeg—cifukwa wuli, Queequeg wakakhalapo pakati pawo—pa mutu wa thebulu, nayo, vikacitika mwamwaŵi; kuzizima nga ni aisi. Kuti niŵe wakusimikizga ningayowoya vinandi yayi pa nkhani ya kubaba kwake. Munthu uyo ​​wakamutemwanga comene wangayowoya yayi kuti wakiza na mkondo wake ku cakurya ca mulenji pamoza nayo, na kuwugwiliskira nchito kwenekura kwambura kucita mwambo; kufika pa thebulu na iyo, ku ngozi iyo yikaŵa pafupi ya mitu yinandi, na kukhwaska nyama za ng’ombe kwa iyo. Kweni ico cikacitika nadi mwakuzizima comene na iyo, ndipo waliyose wakumanya kuti mu kughanaghana kwa ŵanthu ŵanandi, kucita cinthu cilicose mwakuzizima nkhucicita mwakujiyuyura.

Tizamuyowoyapo yayi vya vinthu vyose vyapadera vya Queequeg pano; umo wakacimbiranga khofi na ma hot roll, ndipo wakaŵikanga mtima wake wose pa nyama za ng’ombe, izo zikaŵanga zambura kumanyikwa. Kukwana, kuti apo chakurya cha mulenji chikamara wakafumapo nga mbanyakhe wose mu chipinda cha ŵanthu wose, wakagoleska chitoliro chake cha tomahawk, ndipo wakakhalapo chete wakugaya na kukhweŵa na chipewa chake chambura kugaŵikana, apo nkhafumapo kuti nkhayendere.





MUTU 6. Msewu.

Usange pakwamba nkhazizwa kuwona munthu wakuzizika comene nga ni Queequeg wakuzungulirazungulira pakati pa ŵanthu ŵalusungu ŵa mu tawuni yakutukuka, kuzizwa uko kukamara mwaluŵiro apo nkhayenda mu misewu ya New Bedford kakwamba muhanya.

Mu misewu yikuruyikuru pafupi na doko, doko lililose likuru kanandi likupereka mwaŵi wakuwona vinthu vyambura kumanyikwa ivyo vikuwoneka kufuma ku vigaŵa vinyake. Nanga mu misewu ya Broadway na Chestnut, ŵakwendeska ngalaŵa ŵa ku Meditereniyani nyengo zinyake ŵakuŵatimbanizga ŵanakazi awo ŵali na wofi. Msewu wa Regent ukumanyikwa yayi ku ŵanthu ŵa ku Lascar na Malawi; ndipo ku Bombay, mu Apollo Green, ŵa Yankees ŵamoyo kanandi ŵakofya ŵantu ŵa ku malo agha. Kweni New Bedford yikuthereska msewu wose wa Maji na Wapping. Mu malo agha agho ghazunulika paumaliro mukuwona waka ŵakwendeska ngalaŵa; kweni ku New Bedford, ŵanthu ŵaunenesko awo ŵakurya ŵanthu ŵakuyimilira na kucezga pa makona gha misewu; savages mwakudunjika; ŵanandi wa iwo ŵachali kunyamura nyama yambura kutuŵa pa viwangwa vyawo. Chikupangiska kuti mulendo wakulaŵiska.

Kweni, padera pa ŵa Feegeean, ŵa Tongatobooarrs, ŵa Erromanggoans, ŵa Pannangians, na ŵa Brighggians, ndiposo, padera pa vinyama vyakuthengere vya ngalaŵa yakukolera somba izo zikaŵanga zambura kupulikikwa mu misewu, muzamuwona vinthu vinyake ivyo vikukondweska comene, nadi, vyakusekeska comene. Ku malo agha sabata yiliyose ŵakufika mu tawuni iyi ŵanalume ŵanandi ŵa ku Vermont na New Hampshire, wose ŵali na nyota ya kusanga phindu na kucindikika mu ulovi. Ŵanandi ŵakuŵa ŵawukirano, ŵa mafuremu ghakukhora; ŵanyawo awo ŵali kudumura nkhalango, ndipo sono ŵakukhumba kuponya nkhwantha na kupoka mkondo wa somba za m’maji. Ŵanandi mbakubiriŵira nga ni Mapiri Ghakubiriŵira uko ŵakafuma. Mu vinthu vinyake mungaŵaghanaghanira kuti ni vya maora ghacoko waka. Wonani apo! chap iyo yikuzungulira pakona.
