MOKUNA 32. Cetology.

Ua lele wiwo ʻole ʻia mākou ma ka hohonu; aka, e nalo koke ana kakou iloko o kona mau wahi awa ole. Mamua o ka hiki ana mai; ma mua o ka holo ʻana o ka hull weedy o ka Pequod i ka ʻaoʻao me nā ʻaoʻao barnacled o ka leviatana; i ka hoʻomaka ʻana, ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ka mālama ʻana i kahi mea i makemake nui ʻia i ka hoʻomaopopo piha ʻana i nā hōʻike leviathanic kūikawā a me nā ʻōlelo hoʻohālikelike o nā ʻano āpau e hiki mai ana.

He hōʻike hōʻikeʻike ʻia o ke koholā i loko o kāna ʻano ākea, aʻu e waiho ai i mua o ʻoukou. ʻAʻole naʻe he hana maʻalahi. ʻO ka hoʻokaʻawale ʻana o nā mea o ka haunaele, ʻaʻohe mea liʻiliʻi ma ʻaneʻi i kākau ʻia. E hoʻolohe i nā mea a nā mana maikaʻi a me nā mana hou i waiho ai.

"ʻAʻohe lālā o ka Zoology i komo nui e like me ka mea i kapa ʻia ʻo Cetology," wahi a Kāpena Scoresby, AD 1820.

"ʻAʻole koʻu manaʻo, inā i loko o koʻu mana, e komo i ka nīnau e pili ana i ke ʻano maoli o ka māhele ʻana i ka cetacea i nā hui a me nā ʻohana. * * * Aia ka huikau nui ma waena o nā mea kākau moʻolelo o kēia holoholona" (sperm whale), wahi a Surgeon Beale, AD 1839.

"ʻAʻole kūpono e hoʻomau i kā mākou noiʻi i nā wai hiki ʻole ke ʻike." "Ka pale hiki ʻole ke uhi i ko mākou ʻike no ka cetacea." “He mahinaai i paapu i ke kakalaioa.” "ʻO kēia mau hōʻailona piha ʻole akā e hoʻomāinoino iā mākou nā naturalists."

Pela e olelo ai no ke kohola, ka Cuvier nui, a me John Hunter, a me ka Lesson, kela mau kukui o ka zoology a me ke anatomy. Eia naʻe, ʻoiai he ʻuʻuku ka ʻike maoli, akā he nui nā puke; a pela no ma kekahi kekee liilii, me ka cetology, a me ka epekema o na kohola. He nui na kanaka, liilii a nui, kahiko a me na mea hou, na aina aina a me na luina, ua kakau nui lakou i ke kohola. Holo i kekahi mau mea:—Ka poe kakau Baibala; Aristotle; Pliny; Aldrovandi; Sir Thomas Browne; Gesner; Ray; Linnæus; Rondeletius; Willoughby; ʻōmaʻomaʻo; Artedi; Sibbald; Brisson; Marten; Lacépède; Bonneterre; Desmarest; Baron Cuvier; Frederick Cuvier; John Hunter; Owen; Scoresby; Beale; Bennett; J. Ross Browne; ka mea kākau o Miriam Coffin; Olmstead; a me Rev. T. Cheever. Akā i ke kumu hoʻohālikelike hope loa i kākau ai kēia mau mea āpau, e hōʻike ʻia nā unuhi i ʻōlelo ʻia ma luna.

ʻO nā inoa ma kēia papa inoa o nā mea kākau koholā, ʻo ka poʻe ma hope o Owen wale nō i ʻike i nā koholā ola; a ʻo kekahi o lākou, he kanaka harpoone ʻoihana maoli a he kanaka koholā. ʻO kaʻu ʻōlelo ʻo Kāpena Scoresby. Ma ke kumuhana ʻokoʻa o ka Greenland a i ʻole koholā ʻākau, ʻo ia ka mana maikaʻi loa e kū nei. Akā, ʻaʻole ʻike ʻo Scoresby a ʻaʻole ʻōlelo iki no ke kohola sperm nui, ke hoʻohālikelike ʻia me kahi kohola Greenland ʻaʻole kūpono e haʻi ʻia. A eia ka mea i olelo ia, o ke kohola Greenland he mea lawe pio ia ma ka nohoalii o na kai. ʻAʻole ʻo ia ka mea nui o nā koholā. Akā naʻe, ma muli o ka lōʻihi o kāna mau koi, a me ka naʻaupō nui, a hiki i nā makahiki he kanahiku i hala aku nei, ua hoʻolilo i ka sperm whale maikaʻi ʻole a ʻike ʻole ʻia paha, a ʻo ia ka naʻaupō a hiki i kēia lā ke noho aliʻi nei i nā mea a pau a me nā wahi ʻepekema liʻiliʻi a me nā awa kohola; ua pau loa keia lawe pio ana. ʻO ka ʻike ʻana i ka ʻaneʻane o nā leviathanic allusions i loko o nā haku mele nui o nā lā i hala, e hōʻoluʻolu iā ʻoe ʻo ke kohola Greenland, ʻaʻohe mea hoʻokūkū hoʻokahi, iā lākou ke mōʻī o ke kai. Akā, ua hiki mai ka manawa no kahi hoʻolaha hou. ʻO Charing Cross kēia; e lohe oukou! na kanaka maikai a pau,—ua hoopauia ke kohola Greenland,—ke alii nui nei ka sperm whale!

Aia wale nō ʻelua mau puke i loko o ka mea i hoʻohālike i ka waiho ʻana i ke kohola sperm ola i mua o ʻoe, a ma ka manawa like, ma ka pae mamao loa e kūleʻa i ka hoʻāʻo. Na Beale lāua ʻo Bennett kēlā mau puke; i ko lakou manawa, he mau kauka lapaau i na moku okohola Enelani Hema, a me na kanaka pololei a hilinai. ʻO ka mea mua e pili ana i ka sperm whale i loaʻa i ko lākou mau puke he liʻiliʻi; akā, i ka wā e hele ai, ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ka maikaʻi, ʻoiai ka hapa nui o ka wehewehe ʻepekema. I kēia manawa naʻe, ʻaʻole i piha ka sperm whale, ʻepekema a poetic paha i nā palapala. Ma mua o nā koholā ʻē aʻe a pau, he ola kākau ʻole kona.

I kēia manawa, pono i nā ʻano koholā like ʻole kekahi ʻano hoʻokaʻawale ākea kaulana, inā he kikoʻī maʻalahi wale nō no kēia manawa, a ma kēia hope aku e hoʻopiha ʻia i kāna mau keʻena āpau e nā limahana ma hope. No ka mea, ʻaʻohe kanaka ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi e lawe i kēia mea ma ka lima, ke hāʻawi nei au i kaʻu mau hana ʻilihune. ʻAʻole au e hoʻohiki piha; no ka mea, ʻo nā mea a ke kanaka i manaʻo ʻia ua piha, ʻo ia ke kumu hewa ʻole. ʻAʻole wau e hoʻohālike i kahi wehewehe anatomical minute o nā ʻano ʻano like ʻole, a i ʻole - ma kēia wahi ma ka liʻiliʻi loa - i ka nui o nā wehewehe. ʻO kaʻu mea i ʻaneʻi ʻo ka hoʻolālā wale ʻana i ke kiʻi o ka systematization o ka cetology. ʻO wau ka mea kākau, ʻaʻole ka mea kūkulu hale.

Akā, he hana koʻikoʻi ia; ʻaʻohe mea hoʻoponopono leka maʻamau ma ka Hale Leta e like me ia. E hahau i lalo i lalo o ke kai ma hope o lākou; ka lima i waena o nā kumu hiki ʻole ke haʻi ʻia, nā iwi ʻaoʻao, a me ke pelvis loa o ka honua; he mea weliweli keia. He aha kaʻu e kākau ai e hoʻopaʻa i ka ihu o kēia leviatana! ʻO nā ʻōlelo hoʻomāinoino ʻino ma Ioba paha iaʻu. E hana anei ia (ka leviatana) i berita me oe? Aia hoʻi, ua makehewa ka manaʻolana iā ia! Akā, ua ʻau au ma nā hale waihona puke a ua holo au ma nā moana; Ua pili au i nā koholā me kēia mau lima ʻike ʻia; Ke hooikaika nei au; a e hoao au. Aia kekahi mau hoʻoholo mua.

ʻO ka mea mua: ʻO ke kūlana paʻa ʻole o kēia ʻepekema Cetology aia i loko o ka vestibule i hōʻike ʻia e ka ʻoiaʻiʻo, aia ma kekahi mau wahi ke waiho mau nei ia i kahi moot point inā he iʻa ke koholā. I loko o kāna System of Nature, AD 1776, ua ʻōlelo ʻo Linnæus, "Ke hoʻokaʻawale nei au i nā koholā mai ka iʻa." Akā, ma koʻu ʻike ponoʻī, ʻike wau a hiki i ka makahiki 1850, ua ʻike mau ʻia nā manō a me nā shad, nā alewives a me nā herring, e kūʻē i ke kauoha a Linnæus, e hoʻokaʻawale ana i ka waiwai o nā kai like me ka Leviatan.

ʻO ke kumu i makemake ai ʻo Linnæus i kipaku aku ai i nā koholā mai ka wai, ua ʻōlelo ʻo ia penei: “Ma muli o ko lākou puʻuwai puʻuwai pumehana, ko lākou māmā, ko lākou mau lihilihi hiki ke hoʻoneʻe ʻia, ko lākou mau pepeiao puka, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem,” a ʻo ka hope, “ex lege naturæ jure meritoque.” Ua waiho aku au i keia mau mea a pau i ko'u mau hoaloha Simeon Macey a me Charley Coffin, o Nantucket, o a'u mau hoa hele ma kekahi huakai, a ua lokahi laua me ka manao aole i lawa na kumu i hoikeia mai. Ua ʻōlelo haʻahaʻa ʻo Charley he humbug lākou.

E ʻike ʻia, me ka haʻalele ʻana i nā hoʻopaʻapaʻa a pau, lawe wau i ka ʻāina kahiko maikaʻi ʻo ke koholā he iʻa, a ke kāhea aku nei au iā Iona hemolele e kākoʻo mai iaʻu. Hoʻoholo ʻia kēia mea nui, ʻo ka mea hou aʻe, he aha ka ʻokoʻa o ke koholā mai nā iʻa ʻē aʻe. Ma luna, ua hāʻawi ʻo Linnæus iā ʻoe i kēlā mau mea. Akā ma ka pōkole, ʻo ia kēia: nā māmā a me ke koko mahana; akā, ʻo nā iʻa ʻē aʻe a pau, ʻaʻole maʻi a me ke koko anu.

A laila: pehea lā mākou e wehewehe ai i ke koholā, ma kona ʻano ʻike i waho, i ʻike maopopo ʻia e hōʻailona iā ia no ka wā e hiki mai ana? No ka pōkole, ʻo ke koholā, he iʻa ʻōpuʻu me ka huelo pae . Aia iā ʻoe. Eia naʻe i ʻaelike ʻia, ʻo ia wehewehe ka hopena o ka noʻonoʻo ʻana. Ua like loa ka walrus me he koholā, akā, ʻaʻole ia he iʻa, no ka mea, he amphibious ia. Akā ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ka huaʻōlelo hope o ka wehewehe, e like me ka mea mua. Aneane paha ua ʻike ka poʻe ʻaʻole he palahalaha nā iʻa a pau i kamaʻāina i ka poʻe kamaʻāina, akā he huelo kū pololei, a i ʻole i luna a lalo. ʻOiai, ma waena o ka iʻa ʻana i ka iʻa, ʻo ka huelo, ʻoiai ua like ke ʻano o kona ʻano, he kūlana paʻa mau.

Ma ka wehewehe ʻana i luna no ke ʻano o ke koholā, ʻaʻole au e hoʻokaʻawale i nā mea kai i ʻike ʻia i kēia manawa me ke koholā e nā Nantucketers ʻike maikaʻi loa; ʻaʻole hoʻi e hoʻopili me ia i kekahi iʻa i manaʻo ʻia he malihini. No laila, pono e hoʻokomo ʻia nā iʻa liʻiliʻi a pau, puʻu, a me nā iʻa huelo i kēia papa honua o Cetology. ʻĀnō, e hele mai nā māhele nui o ka pūʻali koholā holoʻokoʻa.

BUKE I. ( Folio ), MOKUNA I. ( Sperm Whale ).—O keia kohola, iwaena o ka poe Pelekane kahiko i ike maopopo ole ia o ke kohola Trumpa, a me ke kohola Physeter, a me ka Anvil Headed whale, oia ka Cachalot o na Farani i keia manawa, a me ka Pottsfich o na Geremania, a me ka Macrocephalus o na huaolelo loihi. ʻO ia, me ke kanalua, ʻo ia ka mea nui loa o ka honua; ka mea weliweli loa o na kohola a pau e halawai ai; ka mea hanohano loa ma ke ano; a ʻo ka hope loa, ʻo ia ka mea waiwai loa ma ke kālepa; ʻo ia wale nō ka mea i loaʻa mai kēlā mea waiwai, ʻo spermaceti. E hoʻonui ʻia kāna mau mea ʻokoʻa a pau, ma nā wahi ʻē aʻe. ʻO ka mea nui me kona inoa kaʻu e hana ai i kēia manawa. Ma ka noonoo ana, he mea lapuwale. I kekahi mau keneturia i hala aku nei, i ka wa i aneane maopopo ole ai ka Sperm whale i kona ano ponoi ponoi, a i ka wa i loaa wale mai ai kana aila mai ka i'a i paeia; I ia mau lā, ua manaʻo ʻia ʻo spermaceti ka mea i loaʻa mai kahi mea i like me ka mea i ʻike ʻia ma ʻEnelani i ia manawa ʻo ka Greenland a i ʻole ʻO ke kohola ʻākau. ʻO ia hoʻi ka manaʻo, ʻo kēia spermaceti hoʻi ʻo ia ka hoʻohenehene wikiwiki o ka Whale ʻo Greenland i hōʻike maoli ʻia e ka syllable mua o ka huaʻōlelo. I kēlā mau manawa hoʻi, ua ʻuʻuku loa ka spermaceti, ʻaʻole i hoʻohana ʻia no ka māmā, akā i mea ʻaila a me ka lāʻau lapaʻau. Loaʻa wale ia mai ka poʻe kūʻai lāʻau lapaʻau ʻoiai ʻoe e kūʻai nei i hoʻokahi auneke rhubarb. I koʻu manaʻo, i ka wā i ʻike ʻia ai ke ʻano maoli o ka spermaceti, ua paʻa mau kona inoa kumu e nā mea kūʻai aku; ʻaʻohe kanalua e hoʻonui i kona waiwai ma o ka manaʻo ʻano ʻē loa o kona hemahema. A no laila ua hiki mai ka inoa i hāʻawi ʻia i ke kohola i loaʻa maoli ai kēia spermaceti.

BUKE I. ( Folio ), MOKUNA II. ( Right Whale ).—Ma kekahi mea, o keia ka mea hanohano o na leviatana, oia ka mea i hahai mau ia e ke kanaka. Hāʻawi ia i ka ʻatikala i ʻike nui ʻia ʻo ka whalebone a i ʻole baleen; a ʻo ka ʻaila i ʻike nui ʻia ʻo "ʻaila koholā," he ʻatikala haʻahaʻa ma ke kālepa. Mawaena o ka poe lawaia, ua kapaia oia e keia mau inoa malalo iho nei: O ke kohola; ke kohola Greenland; ke kohola ʻEleʻele; ka Whale Nui; ka Whale Oiaio; ke kohola akau. Nui ka pohihihi e pili ana i ke ʻano o ia ʻano i bapetizo ʻia me ka lehulehu. He aha ke kohola, aʻu i hoʻokomo ai i loko o ka lua o kaʻu mau Folios? ʻO ia ka Mysticetus Nui o ka poʻe Hawaiʻi maoli; ke koholā ʻo Greenland o nā koholā Pelekane; ka Baleine Ordinaire o na kanaka kohola Farani; ka Growlands Walfish o na Suedena. ʻO ia ka koholā i ʻimi ʻia e ka Holani a me ka ʻEnelani ma ke kai ʻAtika no nā kenekulia ʻelua i hala aku nei; ʻO ia ka koholā a ka poʻe lawaiʻa ʻAmelika i hahai lōʻihi ai ma ka moana ʻĪnia, ma nā Banks Brazil, ma ke kahakai ʻo Nor' West Coast, a me nā wahi like ʻole o ka honua, i koho ʻia e lākou ʻo Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Manaʻo kekahi e ʻike i ka ʻokoʻa ma waena o ke koholā ʻo Greenland o ka ʻEnelani a me ke kohola ʻākau o nā ʻAmelika. Akā, ʻae pololei lākou i ko lākou mau hiʻohiʻona nui; ʻaʻole hoʻi i hōʻike ʻia kahi ʻoiaʻiʻo hoʻokahi e hoʻokumu ai i kahi ʻokoʻa koʻikoʻi. Ma muli o nā ʻāpana pau ʻole i hoʻokumu ʻia ma luna o nā ʻokoʻa like ʻole, e lilo ai kekahi mau keʻena o ka mōʻaukala kūlohelohe i mea paʻakikī loa. E mālama ʻia ka koholā ʻākau ma kahi ʻē aʻe ma kahi lōʻihi, e pili ana i ka wehewehe ʻana i ke kohola sperm.

BUKE I. ( Folio ), MOKUNA III. ( Fin-Back ).—Ma lalo o kēia poʻo ke manaʻo nei au he monster nona nā inoa like ʻole ʻo Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, a me Long-John, ua ʻike ʻia ma nā kai a pau a ʻo ia ka kohola nona ka mokulele mamao i hōʻike pinepine ʻia e nā poʻe e hele ana ma ka ʻaoʻao o ka ʻAkelanika, ma New York packet tracks. I ka lōʻihi i loaʻa iā ia, a i loko o kāna baleen, ua like ka Fin-back me ke kohola ʻākau, akā ʻoi aku ka liʻiliʻi o kona apo, a me ka waihoʻoluʻu māmā, kokoke i ka ʻoliva. ʻO kona mau lehelehe nui e hōʻike mai i kahi ʻano like me ke kaula, i hana ʻia e nā ʻāpana wiliwili o nā wrinkles nui. ʻO kāna hiʻohiʻona nui, ʻo ka ʻili, kahi i loaʻa ai kona inoa, he mea ʻike pinepine ʻia. ʻEkolu a ʻehā paha kapuaʻi ka lōʻihi o kēia peʻa, e ulu kū pololei ana mai ka ʻaoʻao hope o ke kua, he ʻano kihi, a ʻoi aku ka ʻoi loa. ʻOiai ʻaʻole ʻike ʻia kahi ʻāpana ʻē aʻe o ka mea i ʻike ʻia, ʻike ʻia kēia ʻili kaʻawale i kekahi manawa, e ʻike ʻia ana mai ka ʻili. I ka malie malie ana o ke kai, a hoailona iki ia me na ohe poepoe, a ku mai keia hope me he gnomon la a hoolele i ka malu maluna o ka ili nuku, ua like paha ke ano o ka poai wai e hoopuni ana iaia me ka dial, me kona kaila a me na laina o ka hora aleale i kahakahaia maluna. Ma kēlā ʻAhaza-dial e hoʻi pinepine ke aka. ʻAʻole pili ka Fin-Back. Me he mea lā he inaina koholā ʻo ia, ʻoiai he poʻe inaina kanaka kekahi. Hilahila loa; hele mehameha; ka piʻi ʻole ʻana i luna i ka ʻili i ka wai mamao loa a me nā wai ʻino loa; ʻO kāna mokulele kiʻekiʻe pololei a hoʻokahi e piʻi aʻe me he ihe misanthropic lōʻihi ma luna o kahi pāpū maloʻo; i hāʻawi ʻia me ka mana kupaianaha a me ka wikiwiki i ka ʻauʻau ʻana, e pale aku i ka hahai ʻana mai ke kanaka mai; ʻO kēia leviatana me he mea lā ʻo Kaina i hoʻokuke ʻia a lanakila ʻole ʻia o kona lāhui, e kau ana i kāna hōʻailona ma kona kua. Mai ka loaʻa ʻana o ka baleen i loko o kona waha, ua hoʻokomo ʻia ka Fin-Back i kekahi manawa me ke kohola ʻākau, ma waena o nā ʻano manaʻo i kapa ʻia ʻo Whalebone whale , ʻo ia hoʻi, nā koholā me ka baleen. ʻO kēia mau koholā i kapa ʻia ʻo Whalebone, me he mea lā he nui nā ʻano like ʻole, ʻaʻole naʻe i ʻike ʻia ka hapa nui. Nā koholā ihu ākea a me nā koholā nuku; nā koholā poʻo pike; nā kohola hui; ʻO nā koholā ʻauwae lalo a me nā kohola rostrate, ʻo ia nā inoa o nā lawaiʻa no kekahi mau ʻano.

Ma ka pili ana i keia huaolelo o "Whalebone whale," he mea nui e hai aku, aka nae ua kupono ia inoa no ka hoomaopopo ana i kekahi ano kohola, aka, he mea makehewa ka hoao ana e wehewehe pono i ka Leviatana, i hookumuia maluna o kona baleen, a hump, a fin, a me na niho; ʻoiai ʻo kēlā mau ʻāpana a i ʻole nā ​​hiʻohiʻona i kaha ʻia, ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ka hoʻololi ʻana i ke kumu no ka ʻōnaehana maʻamau o Cetology ma mua o nā ʻano ʻokoʻa kino ʻē aʻe, a ke koholā, ma kāna ʻano, e hōʻike nei. Pehea la? ʻO ka baleen, hump, back-fin, a me nā niho; ʻO kēia nā mea i hoʻopuehu wale ʻia ma waena o nā ʻano koholā a pau, me ka nānā ʻole i ke ʻano o ko lākou ʻano ma nā mea ʻē aʻe. No laila, ʻo ka sperm whale a me ka humpbacked whale, he hump ko kēlā me kēia; aka, ua pau ka hoohalike ana. A laila, ʻo kēia koholā humpbacked a me ke kohola Greenland, he baleen kēlā me kēia; aka, ua pau hou ka hoohalike ana. A ua like ia me nā ʻāpana ʻē aʻe i ʻōlelo ʻia ma luna. Ma nā ʻano koholā like ʻole, hana lākou i nā hui like ʻole; a i ʻole, inā kaʻawale kekahi o lākou, kahi kaʻawale ʻole; i mea e kue loa ai i na hana ma'amau a pau i hanaia ma ia kumu. Ma luna o kēia pōhaku ua moku kēlā me kēia o nā koholā-naturalists.

Akā, hiki paha ke manaʻo ʻia, ma nā ʻāpana o loko o ke koholā, i kona anatomia-ma laila, ma ka liʻiliʻi loa, hiki iā mākou ke paʻi i ka hoʻokaʻawale kūpono. ʻAʻole; He aha ka mea i ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ka anatomy o ke kohola Greenland ma mua o kāna baleen? Akā naʻe, ua ʻike mākou ma kāna baleen ʻaʻole hiki ke hoʻokaʻawale pololei i ke kohola Greenland. A inā ʻoe e iho i loko o ka ʻōpū o nā leviatana like ʻole, no ke aha ʻoe e ʻike ʻole ai i kahi hapa kanalima i loaʻa i ka systematizer e like me nā mea waho i helu mua ʻia. He aha ke koe? ʻAʻohe mea ʻē aʻe akā ʻo ka hopu ʻana i nā koholā ma ke kino, i ko lākou leo ​​manuahi holoʻokoʻa, a hoʻokaʻawale wiwo ʻole iā lākou ma ia ʻano. A eia ka Bibliographical system i aponoia; a ʻo ia wale nō ka mea hiki ke holomua, no ka mea, ʻo ia wale nō ka mea hiki ke hana. E hoʻomau.

BUKE I. ( Folio ), MOKUNA V. ( Razor Back ).—No keia kohola i ike iki ia kona inoa.
