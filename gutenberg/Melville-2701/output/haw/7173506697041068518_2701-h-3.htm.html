I koʻu hoʻi ʻana i ka Spouter-Inn mai ka Chapel, loaʻa iaʻu ʻo Queequeg ma laila wale nō; ua haʻalele ʻo ia i ka hale pule ma mua o ka hoʻomaikaʻi ʻana i kekahi manawa. Akā, i kēia manawa ua hoʻopau ʻia ʻo ia, kau ʻo ia i ke kiʻi; a me ka hikiwawe loa, hele aku la i ka papaaina, lalau aku la i kekahi buke nui malaila, a kau iho la maluna o kona uha, hoomaka iho la e helu i na aoao me ka manao mau; i kēlā me kēia ʻaoʻao kanalima - e like me kaʻu i manaʻo ai - kū i kahi manawa, e nānā hakahaka a puni iā ​​​​ia, a hāʻawi i ka ʻōlelo ʻana i kahi puʻupuʻu gurgling lōʻihi o ke kahaha. A laila e hoʻomaka hou ʻo ia i ke kanalima e hiki mai ana; Me he mea lā e hoʻomaka ana ma ka helu ʻekahi i kēlā me kēia manawa, me he mea lā ʻaʻole hiki iā ia ke helu ma mua o ke kanalima, a ma muli wale nō o ka nui o nā kanalima i ʻike pū ʻia, ua hoʻohauʻoli ʻia kona kahaha i ka lehulehu o nā ʻaoʻao.

Me ka hoihoi nui aʻu i noho e nānā iā ia. ʻOiai ʻo Savage ʻo ia, a hūnā ʻia ʻo ia e pili ana i ka maka - ma ka liʻiliʻi loa i koʻu ʻono ʻana - aia naʻe kona helehelena i kahi mea ʻaʻole i ʻae ʻia. ʻAʻole hiki iā ʻoe ke hūnā i ka ʻuhane. Ma o kāna mau peʻa ʻole ʻole, manaʻo wau ua ʻike au i nā meheu o ka puʻuwai ʻoiaʻiʻo; a i loko o kona mau maka nui a hohonu, ʻeleʻele ahi a wiwo ʻole, me he mea lā he mau hōʻailona o ka ʻuhane e ʻaʻa i hoʻokahi tausani daimonio. A ma waho o kēia mau mea a pau, aia kekahi manaʻo kiʻekiʻe e pili ana i ka Pagana, ʻaʻole hiki i kona kolohe ke hōʻeha loa. Ua like ʻo ia me ke kanaka ʻaʻole i ʻai ʻia a ʻaʻole loa i ʻaiʻē. Ina paha ua kahiia kona poo, ua hukiia kona lae me ka oluolu a oi aku ka oi aku o ka laula mamua o ka mea e ae, aole au e hooikaika e hooholo; akā naʻe, ʻo kona poʻo he phrenologically maikaʻi loa. He mea hoʻohenehene paha ia, akā, ua hoʻomanaʻo mai iaʻu i ke poʻo o General Washington, e like me ka mea iʻikeʻia ma nā puʻupuʻu kaulana ona. Loaʻa i ka piʻi hoʻihoʻi lōʻihi like ʻole mai luna aʻe o nā lae, a ʻo ia hoʻi ke ʻano o ka hoʻopololei ʻana, e like me ʻelua mau puʻupuʻu lōʻihi i ulu lāʻau mānoanoa ma luna. ʻO Queequeg ʻo George Washington i hoʻomohala ʻia.

Oiai au e nana pono aku ana iaia, me ka hoohalike ana me ka nana ana i ka ino mai ka pahu, aole loa oia i malama mai i ko'u alo, aole no hoi i hoopilikia ia ia iho me ka nana aku; aka, ua paa loa i ka helu ana i na aoao o ka buke kupanaha. Ma ka noonoo ana i ko maua moe pu ana i ka po i hala, a me ka noonoo pu ana i ka lima aloha a'u i ike ai i hooleiia mai maluna o'u i kona ala ana i ke kakahiaka, ua manao au i keia malama ole o kona ano e. Akā, he mea ʻē nā mea ʻino; i kekahi manawa ʻaʻole maopopo ʻoe pehea e lawe ai iā lākou. I ka wā mua, ua nui lākou; ʻO kā lākou hōʻiliʻili ʻoluʻolu o ka maʻalahi e like me ke akamai Socratic. Ua ʻike nō hoʻi au ʻaʻole ʻo Queequeg i hui pū me nā luina ʻē aʻe o ka hale hoʻokipa. ʻAʻole ʻo ia i holomua; ʻaʻohe makemake e hoʻonui i ka pōʻai o kāna mau hoa. ʻO kēia mau mea a pau i hahau mai iaʻu me he mea lā; akā naʻe, ma ka noʻonoʻo ʻana, aia kahi mea i ʻaneʻane hanohano i loko. Eia kekahi kanaka ma kahi o iwakālua tausani mile mai kona home aku, ma ke ala o Cape Horn, ʻo ia hoʻi-ʻo ia wale nō ke ala e hiki ai iā ia i laila-i hoʻolei ʻia i waena o nā poʻe i ʻike ʻole ʻia iā ia me he mea lā aia ʻo ia ma ka honua ʻo Jupiter; a me he mea la ua oluolu loa ia; mālama i ka maluhia loa; ʻoluʻolu me kona hoa ponoʻī; like mau me ia iho. ʻOiaʻiʻo, he pā kēia o ke akeakamai maikaʻi; ʻoiai ʻaʻole kanalua ʻaʻole ʻo ia i lohe i kekahi mea e like me ia. Aka, malia paha, he poe akeakamai oiaio, aole pono kakou ka poe make e ike i ka noho ana a me ka hooikaika. I koʻu lohe ʻana ua hāʻawi ʻo ia a i ʻole kēlā kanaka iā ia iho no ke akeakamai, manaʻo wau, e like me ka luahine dyspeptic, ua "haʻi ʻo ia i kāna digester."

I koʻu noho ʻana i laila i loko o kēlā lumi mehameha; o ke ahi e aa haahaa ana, ma ia kulana akahai, a mahope iho o kona ikaika mua i pumehana ai i ka lewa, alaila, e alohilohi wale ana ke nana aku; ʻO nā malu ahiahi a me nā phantoms e hōʻuluʻulu ana a puni nā pahu, a nānā mai iā mākou me ka haʻahaʻa, kaʻawale; ʻo ka ʻino e ulu nei me ka ʻole o ka pehu nui; Ua hoʻomaka wau e noʻonoʻo i nā manaʻo ʻē. Ua ʻike au i ka heheʻe ʻana i loko oʻu. ʻAʻole i huli hou koʻu puʻuwai maʻa a me koʻu lima huhū i ke ao ʻīlio hae. Ua kūʻai kēia mea ʻoluʻolu ʻoluʻolu iā ia. Ma laila ʻo ia i noho ai, ʻo kona manaʻo ʻole e ʻōlelo i ke ʻano i hūnā ʻole ʻia ai ka hoʻokamani makaʻala a me ka hoʻopunipuni ʻino. He hihiu ia; he mea makaikai loa e ike; aka, ua hoomaka au e manao ia'u iho i ka huki pohihihi ia ia. A ʻo kēlā mau mea e hoʻohuli ai i ka hapa nui o nā poʻe ʻē aʻe, ʻo ia nā mea hoʻokalakupua i huki mai iaʻu. E ho'āʻo wau i kahi hoaaloha pagan, manaʻo wau, no ka mea, ua hōʻike ʻia ka lokomaikaʻi Kalikiano akā he haʻahaʻa. Ua huki au i koʻu noho kokoke iā ia, a hana i kekahi mau hōʻailona aloha a me nā hōʻailona, ​​e hana ana i kaʻu mea maikaʻi loa e kamaʻilio pū me ia i kēia manawa. I kinohi ua ike iki oia i keia mau holomua; aka, i keia manawa, i ko'u olelo ana i kana mau hookipa o ka po hope, ua ninau mai oia ia'u ina paha e moe hou ana maua. Ua ʻōlelo au iā ia ʻae; kahi aʻu i manaʻo ai ua ʻoluʻolu ʻo ia, a mahalo iki paha.

A laila, huli pū mākou i ka puke, a hoʻoikaika au e wehewehe iā ia i ke kumu o ke paʻi ʻana, a me ke ʻano o nā kiʻi liʻiliʻi i loko. Pela au i hana koke ai i kona hoihoi; a mai laila mai ua hele aku makou i ka jabbering e pili ana i na mea like ole o waho e ike ia ma keia kulanakauhale kaulana. ʻAʻole i liʻuliʻu ua noi au i kahi uwahi kanaka; a, i ka hana ana i kana eke a me ka tomahawk, ua haawi malie mai oia ia'u i ka puff. A laila, noho mākou e pānaʻi ana i kāna paipu ahiu, a mālama mau i ka hele ʻana ma waena o mākou.

Ina paha e huna mai ana kekahi hau o ka manao ole ia'u iloko o ka umauma o ka Pegana, keia uwahi oluolu a me ka uwahi i loaa ia makou, ua hoopau koke iho la ia, a haalele iho la ia makou. Me he mea lā ua lawe mai ʻo ia iaʻu ma ke ʻano maʻamau a me ke kauoha ʻole ʻia e like me aʻu iā ia; a i ka pau ana o ko maua uwahi, hoopili mai la ia i kona lae i kuu lae, apo mai la ia'u ma ka puhaka, a olelo mai la ua mare kaua; ʻo ia hoʻi, ma ka ʻōlelo a kona ʻāina, he mau hoa aloha mākou; E make ʻoliʻoli ʻo ia noʻu, inā pono. I loko o kahi kama'āina, ua ʻike ʻia kēia lapalapa o ka hoaloha i ka wā mua loa, kahi mea e hilinaʻi ʻole ʻia; akā, i loko o kēia moʻokalaleo maʻalahi, ʻaʻole pili kēlā mau lula kahiko.

Ma hope o ka ʻaina ahiahi, a me kekahi kamaʻilio kamaʻilio a me ka uahi, hele pū mākou i ko mākou lumi. Ua hāʻawi mai ʻo ia iaʻu i makana no kona poʻo i ʻala ʻia; Wehe aku la i kana eke paka nui, hahau iho la malalo o ka baka, a unuhi mai la i kanakolu dala kala; a laila hohola ma luna o ka papaʻaina, a puunaue me ka mīkini i ʻelua mau ʻāpana like, hoʻokuʻu i kekahi o lākou iaʻu, a ʻōlelo mai ʻo wau ia. E hoʻopiʻi ana au; aka, ua hoomalielie mai oia ia'u ma ka ninini ana ia mau mea iloko o na eke o ko'u mau lole. Ua hoʻokuʻu wau iā lākou e noho. A laila hele ʻo ia i kāna pule ahiahi, wehe i kāna kiʻi, a wehe i ka papa ahi pepa. Ma kekahi mau hōʻailona a me nā hōʻailona, ​​manaʻo wau me he mea lā ua hopohopo ʻo ia iaʻu e hui pū me ia; aka, me ka ike pono i ka mea e hiki mai ana, noonoo iho la au ina paha e kono mai oia ia'u, e ae paha au.

He Kristiano maikai au; hānau ʻia a hānai ʻia ma ka poli o ka Halepule Presbyterian infallible. Pehea la wau e hui ai me keia hoomanakii ahiu i ka hoomana ana i kona apana laau? A heaha ka makemake o ke Akua?—e hana aku i ko'u hoa kanaka i ka mea a'u i makemake ai e hana mai ko'u hoa ia'u— oia ka makemake o ke Akua. ʻĀnō, ʻo Queequeg koʻu hoa kāne. A he aha kaʻu e makemake ai e hana mai kēia Queequeg iaʻu? No ke aha, e hui pū me aʻu i kaʻu ʻano hoʻomana Presbyterian kūikawā. No laila, pono wau e hui pū me ia ma kāna; no laila, pono wau e hoʻomana kiʻi. No laila, hoʻā wau i nā koʻi; kōkua i ke kākoʻo i ke kiʻi hewa ʻole; hāʻawi aku iā ia i ka biskuti kuni me Queequeg; aloha ʻia i mua ona i ʻelua a ʻekolu paha; honi i kona ihu; a pau ia, wehe mākou i ke kapa a moe, me ka maluhia me ko mākou lunamanaʻo a me ko ke ao holoʻokoʻa. ʻAʻole naʻe mākou i moe me ka ʻole o ke kamaʻilio liʻiliʻi.

Pehea, aole au i ike; akā, ʻaʻohe wahi e like me kahi moena no ka hōʻike huna ʻana ma waena o nā hoaaloha. Wahi a ke kāne a me ka wahine, ma laila e wehe ai ka ʻuhane o ko lāua ʻuhane i kekahi i kekahi; a ʻo kekahi mau kāne kahiko e hoʻopunipuni a kamaʻilio i nā wā kahiko a kokoke i ke kakahiaka. No laila, i loko o ka mahina meli o ko kāua puʻuwai, e waiho ana ʻo I a me Queequeg—he kāne ʻoluʻolu a aloha.





MOKUNA 11. Ka lole moe.

Ua moe makou pela ma kahi moe, e kamailio ana a e hiamoe ana i na manawa pokole, a o Queequeg i keia manawa a ia manawa, hoolei aloha mai la i kona mau wawae pe'a kalakala maluna o ko'u mau wawae, a huki hope; no laila, ʻoluʻolu loa mākou a me ka manuahi a me ka maʻalahi; a i ka hope, ma muli o ko mākou huikau ʻana, ua haʻalele loa ka ʻūlū liʻiliʻi i koe i loko o mākou, a manaʻo mākou e ala hou aʻe, ʻoiai ke ala aʻe o ke ao.

Ae, ua makaala loa makou; no ka mea, ua hoʻomaka ko mākou noho ʻana i ka luhi, a liʻiliʻi iki mākou e noho ana i luna; ua pili pono na lole a puni makou, e hilinai ana i ka papa poo me na kuli eha i pili, a e kulou ana ko makou mau ihu, me he mea la he mau ipu mehana ko makou mau kuli. Ua ʻoluʻolu a ʻoluʻolu hoʻi mākou, ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi ma muli o ke anuanu ma waho o nā puka; mailoko mai o na lole moe, me ke ahi ole o ka rumi. ʻOi aku kaʻu i ʻōlelo ai, no ka mea, no ka ʻoliʻoli ʻana i ka mehana o ke kino, he mea anuanu kekahi hapa liʻiliʻi o ʻoukou, no ka mea, ʻaʻohe mea maikaʻi o kēia ao ʻaʻole like me ke ʻano like ʻole. ʻAʻohe mea i loko ona iho. Inā ʻoe e ʻoluʻolu iā ʻoe iho, a ua lōʻihi ka manawa, ʻaʻole hiki ke ʻōlelo ʻia he ʻoluʻolu hou. Akā inā, e like me Queequeg a me aʻu i loko o kahi moena, e ʻoluʻolu iki ka piko o kou ihu a i ʻole ka lei aliʻi o kou poʻo, no ke aha lā, ʻoiaʻiʻo, i ka ʻike nui e ʻoluʻolu loa ʻoe a me ka mahana ʻole. No kēia kumu, ʻaʻole pono e hoʻolako ʻia kahi keʻena moe me ke ahi, ʻo ia kekahi o nā mea ʻino ʻole o ka poʻe waiwai. No ka mea, ʻo ke kiʻekiʻe o kēia ʻano ʻono, ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe ma waena o ʻoe a me kou ʻoluʻolu a me ke anuanu o ka ea waho. Ma laila ʻoe e moe ai e like me ke ahi wela hoʻokahi i loko o ka puʻuwai o kahi aniani arctic.

Ua noho iho makou ma keia kulou ana no kekahi manawa, manao iho la au e hookaakaa ko'u mau maka; no ka mea, ma ke ao a me ka po, a me ka hiamoe a me ke ala ʻana, he ala koʻu e hoʻopaʻa mau ai i koʻu mau maka, i ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ka noʻonoʻo ʻana i ka ʻoluʻolu o ka moe. No ka mea, ʻaʻole hiki i ke kanaka ke manaʻo i kona ʻano ponoʻī ke ʻole kona mau maka i pani ʻia; Me he mea lā ʻo ka pōʻeleʻele ke kumu kūpono o ko mākou mau ʻano, ʻoiai ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ka mālamalama i ko mākou ʻāpana pālolo. I ka wehe ʻana i koʻu mau maka, a puka i waho o koʻu ʻoluʻolu a i hana ʻia i loko o ka pōʻeleʻele o waho o ka hola ʻumikūmālua ʻole i ka pō, ua loaʻa iaʻu kahi hoʻomāinoino ʻole. Aole no hoi au i kue iki aku i ka olelo a Queequeg, he mea pono paha ke hahau i ke kukui, oiai ua makaala loa makou; a ma waho aʻe o kona manaʻo i ka makemake nui e loaʻa kekahi mau puff mālie mai kāna Tomahawk. E ʻōlelo ʻia, ʻoiai ua loaʻa iaʻu ka huhū nui i kāna puhi ʻana ma kahi moe i ka pō ma mua, akā e ʻike i ka ulu ʻana o kā mākou mau manaʻo koʻikoʻi i ka wā e hiki mai ai ke aloha e kulou iā lākou. I kēia manawa, ʻaʻohe oʻu makemake i ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi ma mua o ka puhi ʻana iā Queequeg ma oʻu nei, a ma kahi moe, no ka mea, ua piha ʻo ia i ka hauʻoli o ka hale i kēlā manawa. ʻAʻole au i manaʻo nui loa i ke kulekele ʻinikua a ke konohiki. Ua ola wale au i ka ʻoluʻolu huna o ka hoʻokaʻawale ʻana i kahi paipu a me kahi pākeʻe me kahi hoaaloha maoli. Me ka huki ʻana o ko mākou mau ʻaʻahu ʻulaʻula ma ko mākou mau poʻohiwi, ua hala mākou i ka Tomahawk mai kekahi a i kekahi, a hiki i ka wā i ulu mālie ai ma luna o mākou kahi mea hōʻike ʻulaʻula e kau ana i ka uahi, i hoʻomālamalama ʻia e ka lapalapa o ke kukui hou.

Ina paha na keia mea hoao owiliwili i olokaa aku i ka mea ino i na wahi mamao loa, aole au i ike, aka, ua olelo oia i keia manawa no kona moku aina; a no ko'u makemake e lohe i kana moolelo, noi aku au ia ia e hele e hai aku. Ua hoʻokō ʻo ia me ka hauʻoli. ʻOiai ʻaʻole au i hoʻomaopopo iki i kāna mau ʻōlelo i kēlā manawa, akā naʻe, ʻo nā hōʻike ʻana ma hope mai, i ka wā i kamaʻāina ai au i kāna ʻōlelo haʻihaʻi, ua hiki iaʻu ke hōʻike i ka moʻolelo holoʻokoʻa e like me ka mea e hōʻoiaʻiʻo ai ma ka ʻiʻo aʻu e hāʻawi nei.





MOKUNA 12. Pilikino.

He kamaaina ʻo Queequeg no Rokovoko, he mokupuni mamao loa i ke Komohana a me ka Hema. ʻAʻole ia i lalo ma kekahi palapala ʻāina; ʻaʻole loa nā wahi ʻoiaʻiʻo.

I ka wā e holo ai ka ʻāhiu hou i kona mau ulu lāʻau ma ka ʻūhā mauʻu, a ukali ʻia e nā kao nahu, me he mea lā he lāʻau ʻōmaʻomaʻo; ia manawa, i loko o ko Queequeg uhane hoʻomake nui e ʻike i kahi mea ʻoi aʻe o ka Christendom ma mua o kahi mea koho kohola a ʻelua paha. He Alii Kiekie kona makuakane, he Alii; he Kahuna Nui kona makuakane; a ma ka ʻaoʻao makuahine, ua kaena ʻo ia i nā ʻanakē he mau wahine a nā koa lanakila ʻole. He koko maikai loa iloko o kona mau aa—mea alii; ʻoiai me ke kaumaha, ke hopohopo nei au, ma muli o ka makemake ʻai kanaka āna i hānai ai i kona wā ʻōpiopio.

Ua kipa aku kekahi moku ʻo Sag Harbor i ke kai kūʻono o kona makuakāne, a ua ʻimi ʻo Queequeg i kahi alahele i nā ʻāina Kalikiano. Akā, ʻo ka moku, me ka piha piha o nā luina, ua hōʻole ʻo ia i kona lole; ʻaʻole hiki i ka mōʻī a pau ka mana o kona makua kāne ke lanakila. E haʻi aku kekahi mau wights pāʻani iā ​​ʻoe he pono lākou e kanu i ka mauʻu ma laila, ʻaʻole ulu maoli lākou; lawe mai lakou i na puakala o Canada; pono lākou e hoʻouna aku ma waho o ke kai no ka hoʻopaʻa ʻana i kahi leak i loko o kahi pahu aila; ua lawe ʻia nā ʻāpana lāʻau ma Nantucket e like me nā ʻāpana o ke keʻa maoli ma Roma; e kanu ka poe malaila i na kupapau imua o ko lakou mau hale, i mea e komo ai malalo o ka malu i ke kau wela; ʻO ka lau o ka mauʻu e hana i kahi oasis, ʻekolu mau lau i ka hele ʻana i ka lā he pā kula; ʻaʻahu lākou i nā kāmaʻa one wikiwiki, e like me nā kāmaʻa hau Laplander; ua paʻa loa, ua kāʻei ʻia, ua hoʻopaʻa ʻia, ua hoʻopuni ʻia, a ua lilo i mokupuni ʻē aʻe e ka moana, i hiki ai i ko lākou mau noho a me nā papaʻaina ke ʻike ʻia nā ʻili liʻiliʻi e pili ana, e like me ke kua o nā honu kai. Akā hōʻike wale kēia mau extravaganzas ʻaʻole ʻo Nantucket ʻo Illinois.

E nānā i kēia manawa i ka moʻolelo kuʻuna kupanaha o ka noho ʻana o kēia mokupuni e ka poʻe ʻulaʻula. Pela ka moolelo. I ka wa kahiko, lele iho la kekahi aeto ma ke kapakai o Nu Enelani, a lawe aku la i kahi keiki Inikini ma kona mau huelo. Me ka leo kanikau o na makua i ike aku ai i ka laua keiki i laweia mai ka nana aku i ka wai akea. Ua hoʻoholo lākou e hahai ma ke ala like. I ka holo ʻana ma ko lākou mau waʻa, ma hope o kahi ala pōʻino, ʻike lākou i ka mokupuni, a ma laila lākou i loaʻa ai kahi pahu niho ʻelepani kaʻawale, ʻo ka iwi ʻilihune ʻilihune o Inia.

No laila, he mea kupanaha ka lawe ʻana o kēia poʻe Nantucketers, i hānau ʻia ma kahakai, i ke kai i mea e ola ai! Ua hopu mua lākou i nā pāpaʻi a me nā ʻuo i ke one; ʻoi aʻe ka wiwo ʻole, hele lākou me nā ʻupena no ka ʻupena; ʻoi aku ka ʻike, hoʻokuke lākou i nā waʻa a hopu i ka cod; a i ka hopena, hoʻolana i nā moku nui ma ke kai, mākaʻikaʻi i kēia honua wai; e hoʻokomo i ke kāʻei hoʻomaha a puni ia; e nana aku ana i ke kowa o Behring; a i nā kau a pau a me nā moana a pau i hōʻike aku i ke kaua mau loa me ka ʻoi loa o nā mea ola i ola i ke kaiakahinalii; ʻoi aku ka weliweli a me ka mauna loa! ʻO kēlā Himmalehan, ka moana paʻakai Mastodon, i ʻaʻahu ʻia me ka mana o ka mana ʻike ʻole, ʻoi aku ka weliweli o kāna mau makaʻu ma mua o kāna mau hoʻouka kaua wiwo ʻole a me ka ʻino!

A pela i holo ai keia poe Nantucketers olohelohe, keia poe hermits kai, e puka ana mai ko lakou puu anana iloko o ke kai, a lanakila i ka honua wai e like me na Alekanedero he nui loa; e hoʻokaʻawale ana i waena o lākou ka moana ʻAkelanika, Pākīpika, a me India, e like me nā mana pōā ʻekolu i hana ai iā Polani. E hookui aku o Amerika ia Mekiko me Texas, a e hoahu ia Cuba maluna o Kanada; na Enelani e hoopumehana i ko Inia a pau, a e kau aku i ko lakou hae wela mai ka la aku; ʻO ka ʻelua hapakolu o kēia honua honua ʻo ka Nantucketer. No ka mea, nona ke kai; nona ia, e like me na Emepera nona na aupuni; he kuleana ko na luina e ae. ʻO nā moku kalepa he mau alahaka hoʻonui; nā mea kaua akā nā pāpū lana; ʻO ka poʻe pōā a me ka poʻe pilikino, ʻoiai e hahai ana i ke kai ma ke ʻano he poʻe alanui, ʻaihue wale lākou i nā moku ʻē aʻe, nā ʻāpana ʻē aʻe o ka ʻāina e like me lākou, me ka ʻimi ʻole e ʻimi i ko lākou ola mai ka hohonu hohonu. ʻO ka Nantucketer, ʻo ia wale nō ka noho a me ka haunaele ma ke kai; ʻO ia wale nō ma ka ʻōlelo Baibala, iho i laila ma nā moku; e oopalau ana ma ke ano o kana mahiko ponoi. Aia kona home; aia no kana oihana, ka mea a Noa i hoopau ole ai, oiai nae ua hoopiha ia i na miliona a pau ma Kina. Noho ʻo ia ma ke kai, e like me nā moa pāpū ma ke kula; peʻe ʻo ia i waena o nā nalu, piʻi ʻo ia iā lākou e like me ka piʻi ʻana o ka poʻe hahai holoholona chamois i nā Alps. No na makahiki, aole oia i ike i ka aina; no laila, i kona hiki ʻana aku i laila, ua ʻala ʻo ia me he honua ʻē aʻe, ʻoi aku ke ʻano ʻē aʻe ma mua o ka mahina i ke kanaka Honua. Me ka wili ʻāina ʻole, i ka napoʻo ʻana o ka lā, pelu aʻela i kona mau ʻēheu a hoʻoluliluli ʻia e hiamoe ma waena o nā ʻale; no laila, i ka pō, ʻo ka Nantucketer, ma waho o ka ʻāina, e hoʻomoe i kona mau peʻa, a hoʻomoe iā ia i kona wahi hoʻomaha, ʻoiai ma lalo o kona uluna e holo wikiwiki ana nā pūʻulu walrus a me nā kohola.





MOKUNA 15. Kopa.

I ke ahiahi, ua hiki mai ka Moss liʻiliʻi e heleuma, a hele māua ʻo Queequeg i uka; no laila ʻaʻole hiki iā mākou ke hele i kahi ʻoihana i kēlā lā, ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe he ʻaina ahiahi a me kahi moe. Na ke konohiki o ka Spouter-Inn i hoike mai ia makou i kona hoahanau o Hosea Hussey o ka Try Pots, ana i olelo ai he mea nona ka hale hokele maikai loa ma Nantucket a pau, a eia kekahi, ua hooiaio mai oia ia makou, ua kaulana o Cousin Hosea, e like me kana i kapa ai iaia, no kona mau moa. I ka pōkole, ua ʻōlelo maopopo ʻo ia ʻaʻole hiki iā mākou ke hana maikaʻi ma mua o ka hoʻāʻo ʻana i ka pot-luck ma ka Try Pots. Akā, ʻo nā kuhikuhi āna i hāʻawi mai ai iā mākou e pili ana i ka mālama ʻana i kahi hale waihona melemele ma ka lima hōkū a hiki i ka wehe ʻana i ka hale pule keʻokeʻo i ka papa larboard, a laila mālama mākou i kēlā ma ka lima larboard a hiki i kahi kihi ʻekolu i ka starboard, a pau kēlā, a laila nīnau i ke kanaka mua a mākou i ʻike ai i kahi o ia wahi: ʻo kēia mau ʻaoʻao kekee o kona wahi i hoʻohoka nui loa iā mākou i ka mua, ʻo ia hoʻi, ma ka ʻaoʻao mua o ka hale melemele. o ka haʻalele ʻana—pono e waiho ʻia ma ka lima larboard, ʻoiai ua maopopo iaʻu ʻo Peter Coffin i ʻōlelo ʻia aia ma ka starboard. Eia naʻe, ma muli o ka hahau iki ʻana i ka pōʻeleʻele, a i kēia manawa ke kīkēkē ʻana i kahi kamaʻāina maluhia e nīnau i ke ala, ua hiki mākou i kahi mea i kuhi hewa ʻole.

ʻElua mau ipu lāʻau nui i pena ʻia i ka ʻeleʻele, a i hoʻopaʻa ʻia e nā pepeiao o nā hoki, ua lele ʻia mai nā kumu lāʻau keʻa o kahi kia luna kahiko, i kanu ʻia ma ke alo o kahi puka kahiko. Ua ʻoki ʻia nā pepeiaohao o nā lāʻau keʻa ma kēlā ʻaoʻao, no laila ʻaʻole i like iki kēia kia luna kahiko me ke oʻa. Malia paha ua maʻalahi wau i kēlā mau manaʻo i kēlā manawa, akā ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke pale aku i ka nānā ʻana i kēia ʻolo me ka manaʻo kānalua. He ʻano ʻokiʻoki ma koʻu ʻāʻī i koʻu nānā ʻana aku i nā kiwi ʻelua i koe; ʻAe, ʻelua o lākou, hoʻokahi no Queequeg, a hoʻokahi noʻu. He mea ino, manao au. He pahu kupapau ko'u kahu hale i ka pae ana ma ko'u awa kohola mua; nā pōhaku kupapaʻu e nānā mai ana iaʻu i loko o ka hale pule o ka poʻe koholā; a eia kahi oʻa! a me nā ipu ʻeleʻele kupaianaha pū kekahi! ʻO kēia mau mea hoʻolei hope loa e pili ana iā Tophet?

Ua kāhea ʻia au mai kēia mau noʻonoʻo ʻana e ka ʻike ʻana aku i kahi wahine pehu me ka lauoho melemele a me ka ʻaʻahu melemele, e kū ana ma ka lanai o ka hale hoʻokipa, ma lalo o kahi kukui ʻulaʻula ʻulaʻula e koli ana i laila, me he mea lā he maka ʻeha, a e lawe pū ana me kahi kāne i loko o ka pālule huluhulu poni.

“E hele pū me ʻolua,” wahi āna i ke kāne, “a i ʻole, e wili ana au iā ʻoe!”

"E hele mai ʻoe, e Queequeg," wahi aʻu, "ʻAe, aia ʻo Mrs. Hussey."

A pela iho la; Mai ka home mai o Mr. I ko makou hoike ana mai i ko makou makemake i ka aina ahiahi a me kahi moe, ua hoopanee hou aku la o Mrs. Hussey ia makou i keia manawa, a hookomo mai la ia makou iloko o kekahi keena liilii, a hoonoho iho la ia makou ma ka papaaina i hoholaia me na koena o ka ai ana, huli mai la ia makou a olelo mai la—“Clam or Cod?”

“He aha kēlā e pili ana iā Cods, e ka wahine?” wahi a'u, me ke akahai nui.

“Clam or Cod?” wahi āna.

"He paʻi no ka ʻaina ahiahi? he pāpaʻu anu; ʻo ia anei kāu e ʻōlelo nei e Mrs. Hussey?" wahi a wau, "akā, he ʻahaʻaina anu a ʻuʻuku kēlā i ka wā hoʻoilo, ʻaʻole anei, e Mrs. Hussey?"

Aka, no ka wikiwiki loa e hoomau aku i ka hahau ana i ke kanaka me ka Pole poni, e kali ana ma ke komo ana, a me he mea la aole i lohe wale i ka huaolelo "clam," holo wikiwiki aku la o Mrs. Hussey i ka puka hamama e hiki aku ai i ka lumi kuke, a nalo aku la.

"Queequeg," wahi aʻu, "ke manaʻo nei ʻoe e hiki iā kāua ke hana i ka ʻaina ahiahi na kāua ma ka pā hoʻokahi?"

Eia nō naʻe, ʻo kahi mahu ʻono mahana mai ka lumi kuke i hōʻole i ka manaʻo hauʻoli i mua o mākou. Akā, i ka wā i komo mai ai kēlā ʻāpala puhi, wehewehe ʻia ka mea pohihihi. ʻŌ, nā hoa aloha! e hoolohe mai ia'u. Ua hana ʻia ia me nā pāpaʻu liʻiliʻi liʻiliʻi, ʻaʻole i ʻoi aku ka nui ma mua o nā hua hazel, i hui pū ʻia me ka biscuit moku i kuʻi ʻia, a me ka puaʻa paʻakai i ʻoki ʻia i loko o nā ʻāpana liʻiliʻi; ʻO ka mea a pau i hoʻonui ʻia me ka pata, a hoʻomoʻi nui ʻia me ka pepa a me ka paʻakai. Ua oi loa ko makou makemake i ka huakai hau, a o ka ike ana aku o Queequeg i kana mea ai lawaia punahele imua ona, a me ka maikai loa o ka puna, ua hoouna aku makou ia mea me ka huakai nui: i ka wa e hilinai ana i hope a noonoo ia'u i ka olelo hoolaha a Mrs. I koʻu hele ʻana i ka puka kīhini, hoʻopuka wau i ka huaʻōlelo "cod" me ka manaʻo nui, a hoʻi hou i koʻu noho. Iloko o na manawa pokole, puka hou mai la ka mahu ono, aka, he ano okoa no nae, a i ka manawa maikai, ua waihoia mai la ke koko cod-cowder maikai imua o makou.

Hoʻomaka hou mākou i ka ʻoihana; a oiai e oni ana i ko makou puna i ke pola, manao iho la au ia'u iho, ke manao nei au i keia manawa he hopena keia ma ke poo? He aha kēlā ʻōlelo paʻakikī e pili ana i ka poʻe poʻo chowder? "Akā, e nānā ʻoe, e Queequeg, ʻaʻole anei ia he tuna ola i loko o kāu kīʻaha? ʻAuhea kāu pū?"

ʻO nā ipu iʻa ʻoi loa o nā wahi iʻa a pau, ʻo ia ka ipu hoʻāʻo i kūpono i kona inoa; no ka mea, he mau paila paila no na ipuhao. ʻO ka ʻuala no ka ʻaina kakahiaka, a me ka ʻuala no ka ʻaina ahiahi, a me ka ʻōpala no ka ʻaina ahiahi, a hiki i kou hoʻomaka ʻana e ʻimi i nā iwi iʻa e hele mai ana ma kou ʻaʻahu. ʻO ka wahi ma mua o ka hale i paʻi ʻia me ka ʻili-ʻili. Ua ʻaʻahu ʻo Mrs. Hussey i ka lei i hoʻomaʻemaʻe ʻia o ka ʻiʻo codfish; a ua hoʻopaʻa ʻia ʻo Hosea Hussey i kāna mau puke moʻokāki me ka ʻili manō kahiko. He ʻono ʻono nō hoʻi i ka waiū, ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke noʻonoʻo iki, a hiki i kekahi kakahiaka e hele wāwae ana ma kahakai i waena o kekahi mau waʻa lawaiʻa, ʻike akula au i ka bipi ʻōniʻoniʻo a Hosea e ʻai ana i nā koena iʻa, a e hele ana ma ke one me kēlā me kēia wāwae me ke poʻo ʻokiʻoki o ka cod, ke ʻike aku nei au he pahee loa.

I ka pau ana o ka ahaaina, ua loaa ia makou ke kukui, a me na kuhikuhi mai ia Mrs. Hussey no ke ala kokoke loa e moe ai; aka, i ko Queequeg kokoke ana e hele mamua o'u iluna o ke alapii, lalau mai la ka lede i kona lima, a koi aku la i kana harpoon; ʻAʻole ʻo ia i ʻae i ka harpoon ma kona keʻena. "Kainō?" wahi a'u; "Ke moe nei kēlā me kēia kanaka koholā ʻoiaʻiʻo me kāna harpoon—akā, no ke aha?" "No ka mea he mea weliweli," wahi āna. "Mai ka wa i hele mai ai o Stiggs opio mai kela hana poino ona, i kona hele ana i na makahiki eha a me ka hapa, me na barela ekolu wale no o ka hale , ua ikeia ua make ma ko'u papahele mua, me kona harpoon ma kona aoao; mai ia manawa mai, aole au e ae aku i ka poe e lawe i na uwe weliweli ma ko lakou mau rumi i ka po. a hiki i kakahiaka, ʻo ka pāpaʻi a i ʻole ka ʻāpōpō no ka ʻaina kakahiaka, e nā kāne?

"ʻelua," wahi aʻu; “A e ʻai kākou i ʻelua mau mea puhi ʻia ma ke ʻano like ʻole.”





MOKUNA 16. Ka Moku.

Ma kahi moe mākou i hoʻolālā ai i kā mākou mau hoʻolālā no ka lā ʻapōpō. Aka, i ko'u pihoihoi a me ko'u manao nui, ua haawi mai o Queequeg ia'u i keia manawa e hoomaopopo, ua imi ikaika oia ia Yojo—ka inoa o kona wahi akua uuku eleele—a ua hai aku o Yojo ia ia elua a ekolu manawa, a koi ikaika mai i kela a me keia, ma kahi o ko maua hele pu ana iwaena o na moku okohola ma ke awa, a ma ka ahaolelo e koho ai i ko kakou hana; ma kahi o keia, wahi a'u, ua kauoha ikaika mai o Yojo e hoomaha loa ia'u ke koho ana o ka moku, oiai ua manao o Yojo e hoalohaloha ia makou; a, i mea e hana ai pela, ua kau mua aku au maluna o kekahi moku, ina e waiho ia'u iho, owau, o Isemaela, e malama pono ole ia, no ko ke ao nei a pau me he mea la ua puka wale ia; a ma ia moku e moku koke au ia'u iho, no keia manawa me ka manao ole ia Queequeg.

Ua poina iaʻu e haʻi aku, ma nā mea he nui, ua hilinaʻi nui ʻo Queequeg i ka maikaʻi o ka hoʻoholo ʻana a Yojo a me ka wānana kupanaha o nā mea; a ua malama ia Yojo me ka manao nui, ma ke ano he ano akua maikai loa, a ua lawa paha kona manao ma ke ano holookoa, aka, ma na mea a pau, aole i holopono kana mau manao lokomaikai.

ʻĀnō, ʻo kēia hoʻolālā a Queequeg, a i ʻole ʻo Yojo, e pili ana i ke koho ʻana o kā mākou hana; ʻAʻole loa au i makemake i kēlā papahana. ʻAʻole wau i hilinaʻi iki i ka naʻauao o Queequeg e kuhikuhi i ka moku okohola i kūpono loa e halihali iā mākou a me ko mākou waiwai me ka palekana. Aka, no ka mea, aole i hoopuka aku ka'u mau hoole a pau maluna o Queequeg, ua koiia au e ae aku; a no laila ua mākaukau e hoʻomaka i kēia ʻoihana me ka ikaika a me ka ikaika, e hoʻoponopono koke i kēlā hana liʻiliʻi. I ke kakahiaka nui aʻe, e haʻalele ana iā Queequeg me Yojo i loko o ko mākou lumi moe liʻiliʻi - no ka mea, me he mea lā he ʻano Lent a Ramadan paha, a i ʻole lā hoʻokē ʻai, hoʻohaʻahaʻa, a pule me Queequeg lāua ʻo Yojo ia lā; ʻAʻole hiki iaʻu ke ʻike, no ka mea, ʻoiai ua hoʻopili wau iaʻu iho i ia mau manawa, ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke haku i kāna mau liturgies a me XXXIX Articles - haʻalele ʻo Queequeg, a laila, hoʻokē ʻai ma kāna paipu tomahawk, a ʻo Yojo e hoʻomehana iā ia iho i kāna ahi ʻālana o nā shavings, ua haʻalele wau i waena o ka moku. Ma hope o ka holo lōʻihi ʻana a me ka nui o nā nīnau nīnau, ua ʻike au aia ʻekolu mau moku i hala no nā makahiki ʻekolu mau huakaʻi—The Devil-dam, the Tit-bit, and the Pequod. Devil-Dam , aole au i ike i ke kumu o; ʻIke ʻia ʻo Tit-bit ; ʻO Pequod , e hoʻomanaʻo paha ʻoe, ʻo ia ka inoa o kahi ʻohana kaulana o Massachusetts Indians; i kēia manawa e like me nā Media kahiko. Ua nana aku au a nana e pili ana i ka Diabolo-dam; mai iā ia, lele akula i ka Tit-bit; a i ka hope loa, e kau ana ma luna o ka Pequod, nana a puni iaia no kekahi manawa, a hooholo iho la o keia ka moku ponoi no makou.

Ua ʻike paha ʻoe i nā hana ʻano ʻē aʻe i kou lā, no ka mea, ʻaʻohe mea aʻu i ʻike; junks Iapana mauna; ʻO nā galliot pahu pata, a me ka mea ʻole; akā, e lawe i kaʻu ʻōlelo no ia mea, ʻaʻole ʻoe i ʻike i kahi hana kahiko loa e like me kēia Pequod kahiko loa. He moku ia no ke kula kahiko, he uuku no; me kahi hiʻohiʻona wāwae wāwae kahiko e pili ana iā ia. ʻO ka lōʻihi o ka wā a me ka ʻino i ka ʻino a me ka mālie o nā moana ʻehā a pau, ua ʻeleʻele ka ʻili o kona hull kahiko e like me ko Farani grenadier, i hakakā like ma ʻAigupita a me Siberia.
