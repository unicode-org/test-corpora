MOKUNA 135. Ka Hahai.—La La Ekolu.

ʻO ke kakahiaka o ke kolu o ka lā i puka mai ai ka nani a me ka hou, a ua hoʻomaha hou ke kanaka pō mehameha ma ke poʻo-mua-mua e ka lehulehu o nā mākaʻikaʻi o ke ao, ka poʻe e kikoo ana i kēlā me kēia kia a kokoke i kēlā me kēia spar.

"Ua ʻike ʻoe iā ia?" kahea aku la o Ahaba; aka, aole i ikeia ke kohola.

"Ma kona ala ala ʻole, ʻoiai; akā, e hahai i kēlā ala, ʻo ia wale nō. Helm ma laila; kupaa, i kou hele ana, a e hele ana. He lā nani hou! ina he ao hou ia, a i hanaia i hale kau wela no na anela, a i keia kakahiaka ka mua o kona kiola ana ia lakou, aole hiki i ka la maikai ke puka mai ma ia ao. Eia ka meaʻai no ka noʻonoʻo, loaʻa iā ʻAhaba ka manawa e noʻonoʻo ai; aka, aole manao o Ahaba; manaʻo wale ʻo ia, manaʻo, manaʻo; ua lawa ia no ke kanaka make! e noonoo i ka audacity. ʻO ke Akua wale nō ka pono a me ka pono. ʻO ka noʻonoʻo ʻana, a he pono paha, he ʻoluʻolu a me ka mālie; a ke kuʻi nei ko mākou naʻau ʻilihune, a ʻoi aku ka nui o ko mākou lolo ʻilihune no ia mea. Akā naʻe, ua manaʻo wau i kekahi manawa ua mālie loa koʻu lolo—ʻoluʻolu hauʻoli, pohā kēia iwi poʻo kahiko, me he aniani lā i lilo nā mea i loko i hau, a haʻalulu. A ke ulu nei kēia lauoho i kēia manawa; e ulu ana kēia manawa, a pono e hoʻohua ka wela; akā ʻaʻole, ua like ia me kēlā ʻano mauʻu maʻamau e ulu ana ma nā wahi a pau, ma waena o nā māwae honua o ka hau Greenland a i ʻole ka pele Vesuvius. Nani ka makani hihiu; Ua hahau mai lakou ia'u, e like me ka haehae ana o na pe'a nahaehae i ka moku i hooleleia a lakou e pipili nei. He makani ino ka mea i kanalua ole e pa mai ana ma na ala o ka halepaahao a me na keena, a me na keena o na halemai, a hookui ia lakou i ka ea, a i keia manawa ke pa mai nei me ka hala ole e like me ka huluhulu. Ma waho!—ua haumia. Inā ʻo wau ka makani, ʻaʻole au e puhi hou aku ma luna o kēia ao ʻino a pōʻino. Kolo aku au ma kahi o ke ana, a slink ma laila. Eia naʻe, he mea hanohano a me ke koa, ʻo ka makani! Na wai i lanakila? I kēlā me kēia hakakā, loaʻa iā ia ka hahau hope loa. Holo tilting at ia, a holo oe ma ia. Ha! he makani maka'u e hahau ana i na kanaka olohelohe, aole nae e ku i ka hahau hookahi. ʻO ʻAhaba hoʻi he mea ʻoi aku ka wiwo ʻole—he mea hanohano ma mua o kēlā . Ina paha he kino ka makani; aka, o na mea a pau i hoonaukiuki loa i ke kanaka make, he kino ole keia mau mea a pau, aka, he kino ole wale no ma ke ano he mau mea, aole ma ke ano he mea hana. Aia kahi ʻokoʻa kūikawā, kahi maalea loa, ʻē, kahi ʻokoʻa ʻino loa! Eia naʻe, ke haʻi hou aku nei au, a hoʻohiki i kēia manawa, aia kekahi mea nani a lokomaikaʻi i ka makani. ʻO kēia mau makani kālepa pumehana, ma ka liʻiliʻi loa, i ka lani akaka e pā pololei ai, me ka ikaika a kūpaʻa, me ka mālie ikaika; aole e huli ae mai ko lakou kaha aku, aka nae, e huli ae na au o ke kai a pa, a me na Mississippi ikaika loa o ka aina, e awiwi a me ka maopopo ole i kahi e hele ai. A e na Pole mau loa! ʻO kēia mau kālepa hoʻokahi i puhi pololei i koʻu moku maikaʻi ma luna; ʻO kēia mau Kalepa, a i ʻole kekahi mea like me lākou-he mea hiki ʻole ke hoʻololi, a piha hoʻi e like me ka ikaika, e puhi i koʻu ʻuhane keele! I ia mea! Aia ma laila! He aha kāu e ʻike nei?”

"ʻAʻohe mea, e ka haku."

“ʻAʻohe mea! a awakea ma ka lima! E noi ana ka lua! E ʻike i ka lā! ʻAe, ʻae, pono nō. Ua hoʻonui au iā ia. Pehea, i hoʻomaka ai? ʻAe, ke alualu nei ʻo ia iaʻu i kēia manawa; ʻaʻole wau, ʻo ia —ʻino; Ua ʻike paha wau. Puʻupuʻu! na laina—na harpoons ana e huki ana. ʻAe, ʻae, ua holo wau iā ia i ka pō nei. E pili ana i! pili! E iho mai ʻoukou a pau, akā, ʻo ka nānā maʻamau! E ke kanaka i nā pale pale!"

E like me kāna i hana ai, aia ka makani ma ka hapahā o ka Pequod, no laila ke kuhikuhi ʻia nei i ka ʻaoʻao ʻaoʻao, ua holo ikaika ka moku i hoʻopaʻa ʻia ma luna o ka makani ʻoiai ʻo ia e hoʻihoʻi hou i ka ʻaila i kona wake keʻokeʻo ponoʻī.

"E hoʻokele ana ʻo ia i ka makani no ka ʻāʻī hāmama," i ʻōhumu ai ʻo Starbuck iā ia iho, ʻoiai ʻo ia e wili ana i ka pale lima hou ma luna o ke kaʻaahi. "E malama mai ke Akua ia kaua, aka, ua pulu ko'u mau iwi iloko o'u, a mai loko mai i pulu ko'u io. Ke kānalua nei au i koʻu hoʻolohe ʻole i koʻu Akua ma ka hoʻolohe ʻana iā ia!

"E kū mai e hoʻoluliluli iaʻu!" kahea aku o Ahaba, e hele ana i ka hinai hepena. "Pono kaua e hui koke me ia."

"Ae, ae, e ka haku," a hooko koke aku la o Starbuck i ka olelo a Ahaba, a lele hou aku la o Ahaba iluna.

Ua hala ka hora holookoa; paʻi gula i nā makahiki. Ua lōʻihi ka hanu o ka manawa me ka hopohopo nui. Aka, i ka hope loa, ma kahi o ekolu mau kiko mai ke kakaka o ka makani, ua hai hou aku o Ahaba i ka uwa, a ia wa koke no, mai na poo kia ekolu i pii ae ai na leo uwao ekolu me he mea la ua leo mai na elelo ahi.

“Ma ka lae a ka lae, halawai au me oe, i keia kolu o ka manawa, e Moby Dick! Ma luna o ka ʻauamo i laila!—ʻoi aku ka ikaika; hoʻopili iā ia i loko o ka maka makani. Ua mamao loa ʻo ia e haʻahaʻa, e Mr. Starbuck. Haʻalulu nā peʻa! Kū ma luna o kēlā alakaʻi me ka top-maul! ʻano; holo wikiwiki ʻo ia, a pono wau i lalo. Aka, e ae mai oe ia'u i kekahi nana poepoe maikai loa maluna o ke kai; aia ka manawa no ia mea. He ʻike kahiko, ʻelemakule, a ʻōpiopio naʻe; ʻAe, ʻaʻole hoʻi i hoʻololi i kahi wink mai koʻu ʻike mua ʻana iā ia, he keikikāne, mai nā puʻu one o Nantucket! ʻO ia!—ʻo ia!—ʻo Noa me aʻu. Aia kahi ʻauʻau palupalu i ka leeward. ʻO nā leewardings nani! Pono lākou e alakaʻi i kahi - i kahi mea ʻē aʻe ma mua o ka ʻāina maʻamau, ʻoi aku ka nui o ka pālima ma mua o nā pālima. Leeward! hele ke kohola keʻokeʻo ma ia ala; e nana i ka makani, alaila; ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi inā ʻoi aku ka ʻawaʻawa. Akā aloha, aloha e, poʻo mast kahiko! He aha kēia?— ʻōmaʻomaʻo? ʻAe, liʻiliʻi liʻiliʻi i loko o kēia mau māwae. ʻAʻohe wahi ʻōmaʻomaʻo ma ke poʻo o ʻAhaba! Aia ka ʻokoʻa ma waena o ka ʻelemakule o ke kanaka a me ka mea. Akā, ʻae, ʻelemakule mast, ʻelemakule pū kāua; kani i loko o ko mākou hull, ʻaʻole anei mākou e kuʻu moku? ʻAe, emi ka wāwae, ʻo ia wale nō. Ma ka lani, ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o kēia lāʻau make i koʻu ʻiʻo ola i nā ʻano āpau. ʻAʻole hiki iaʻu ke hoʻohālikelike me ia; a ua ʻike au i kekahi mau moku i hana ʻia me nā lāʻau make i ʻoi aku ka lōʻihi ma mua o ke ola o nā kānaka i hana ʻia me nā mea koʻikoʻi o nā makua kāne koʻikoʻi. He aha kāna i ʻōlelo ai? e hele mau no ia mamua o'u, e kuu pailota; a e ike hou ia? Akā ma hea? E loaʻa iaʻu nā maka i lalo o ke kai, me ka manaʻo e iho wau i kēlā mau alapiʻi pau ʻole? a i ka po a pau, ua holo au mai ona aku la, ma na wahi a pau ana i poho ai. ʻAe, ʻae, e like me nā mea hou aʻe āu i haʻi aku ai i ka ʻoiaʻiʻo weliweli e pili ana iā ʻoe iho, e Parsee; akā, e ʻAhaba, ma laila i hāʻule ai kou pana. Aloha ʻoe, mast-head—e mālama pono i ke koholā, i koʻu wā e hele aku ai. E kamaʻilio ana kāua i ka lā ʻapōpō, ʻaʻole, i kēia pō, ke moe ke kohola keʻokeʻo i laila, nakinaki ʻia e ke poʻo a me ka huelo.

Hāʻawi ʻo ia i ka ʻōlelo; a e nānā mau ana iā ia, ua hoʻohaʻahaʻa ʻia i lalo i ka ea uliuli i ka papa.

I ka wa pono, ua hoohaahaa ia na waapa; aka, i kona ku ana ma ka hope o kona lihilihi, lele wale aku la o Ahaba ma ka lae o ka iho ana, lulu aku la ia i ka hoa,—ka mea nana i paa kekahi o na kaula ma ka papa—a kauoha aku ia ia e hoomaha.

“Starbuck!”

“E haku?”

"No ke kolu o ka manawa e hoʻomaka ai ka moku o koʻu ʻuhane ma kēia huakaʻi, Starbuck."

"ʻAe, e ka haku, makemake ʻoe pēlā."

"E holo ana kekahi mau moku mai ko lakou mau awa mai, a ma ia hope aku ua nalowale, Starbuck!"

"ʻOiaʻiʻo, e ka haku: ʻoiaʻiʻo kaumaha loa."

“E make ana kekahi mau kanaka i ke au ana; kekahi ma ka wai haʻahaʻa; ʻO kekahi i ka piha o ka waikahe;—a ke manaʻo nei au me he puʻu lā, ʻo ia hoʻi, ʻo Starbuck. Ua elemakule au;—e lulu lima me au, e ke kanaka."

Halawai ko laua mau lima; ua paʻa ko lākou mau maka; Ua waimaka ʻo Starbuck i ke kāpili.

“E kuu kapena, e kuu kapena!—puuwai hanohano—mai hele—mai hele—e nana, he kanaka koa e uwe ana; Nani ka ehaeha o ka hoohuli ana ia manawa!”

"E iho i lalo!" - i hoʻōho ai ʻo ʻAhaba, me ka kiola ʻana i ka lima o ka hoa mai ona aku. “E kū ma ka poʻe holokai!”

I ka manawa koke e huki ana ka waapa a kokoke i ka hope.

“ʻO nā manō! nā manō!” kahea mai la kekahi leo mai ka puka aniani haahaa ma laila; “E kuʻu haku, e hoʻi mai!”

Aole nae i lohe o Ahaba; no ka mea, ua hookiekieia kona leo ia manawa; a lele ka waapa.

He oiaio nae ka leo i olelo mai ai; No ka mea, ʻaʻole ʻo ia i hoʻokuke ʻia mai ka moku aku, i ka wā i ʻō mai ai ka nui o nā manō, me he mea lā mai ka wai ʻeleʻele o lalo o ka hull, e ʻōʻewaʻewa ana i nā ʻili o nā hoe, i kēlā me kēia manawa a lākou e luu ai i ka wai; a pela pu me ka waapa me ko lakou nahu. He mea kaa ole ia i na waapa kohola ma ia mau kai ehuehue ana; ʻO nā manō i kekahi manawa e hahai ana iā lākou e like me ke ʻano o ka lele ʻana o nā manu ma luna o nā hae o nā regimen hele ma ka hikina. Akā, ʻo kēia ka manō mua i ʻike ʻia e ka Pequod mai ka wā i wehewehe mua ʻia ai ka Whale Keʻokeʻo; a ina paha o ko Ahaba poe holookoa he mau tiger-yemele mau kamaaina, a nolaila, ua oi aku ko lakou io i ka manao o na mano—he mea i ike pono ia lakou i kekahi manawa,—eia nae, me he mea la ua hahai lakou i kela waapa me ka ole. hoʻomāinoino i nā mea ʻē aʻe.

“Naʻau o ke kila kila!” i ʻōhumu ai ʻo Starbuck e nānā ana ma ka ʻaoʻao, a e hahai ana me kona mau maka i ka waʻa e emi ana—“e hiki anei iā ʻoe ke kani wiwo ʻole i kēlā ʻike?—e hoʻohaʻahaʻa ana i kou kiʻi ma waena o nā manō hae, a hahai ʻia e lākou, me ka hāmama o ka waha i ka hahai ʻana; a ʻo ke kolu kēia o ka lā koʻikoʻi?—No ka mea, i ka wā e kahe like ai nā lā ʻekolu i ka ʻimi ikaika hoʻokahi; e ʻike pono ʻo ka mua ke kakahiaka, ʻo ka lua ke awakea, a ʻo ke kolu ke ahiahi a me ka hope o ia mea, ʻo ia ka hopena. ʻŌ! kuu Akua! He aha kēia e pana mai ana iaʻu, a waiho iaʻu i ka mālie make loa, akā naʻe e kali nei, - paʻa i ka piko o ka haʻalulu! ʻO nā mea e hiki mai ana e ʻauʻau i mua oʻu, e like me nā outline a me nā iwi iwi ʻole; ua powehiwehi ka hala a pau. Mary, kaikamahine! e mae ana oe me na nani haikea mahope o'u; keiki kāne! Me he mea lā ke ʻike aku nei au akā ua uliuli kou mau maka. ʻO nā pilikia ʻē aʻe o ke ola me he mea lā e maʻemaʻe; aka, ua uhi mai na ao ma waena—E hiki mai ana ka hopena o ka'u huakai? Ua maule ko'u mau wawae; e like me kona wāwae i ka lā a pau. Manaʻo i kou puʻuwai, - ke kuʻi nei? E hoʻāla iā ʻoe iho, e Starbuck!—ʻoki ʻoe—e neʻe, neʻe! e olelo leo nui!—Mast-head there! Ke ike nei anei oukou i ka lima o ka'u keiki maluna o ka puu?—He pupule;—iluna ilaila!—e nana pono i na waapa:—e hoailona pono i ke kohola!—Ho! hou!—e kipaku aku i kela hawk! ike! kuʻi ʻo ia—haehae ʻo ia i ka vane”—e kuhikuhi ana i ka hae ʻulaʻula e lele ana ma ke kalaka nui—“Ha! lele aku la ia!—Auhea ka elemakule i keia wa? ʻIke ʻoe i kēlā ʻike, e ʻAhaba!

ʻAʻole i mamao loa nā waʻa, aia ma ka hōʻailona mai nā poʻo kia—he lima kiko i lalo, ua ʻike ʻo ʻAhaba ua kani ke koholā; aka, i kona manao e hookokoke aku iaia i ke ala hou ana mai, paa iki aku la oia i kona ala mai ka moku aku; ʻo ka poʻe ʻauwaʻa e hoʻohauʻoli ʻia e mālama ana i ka hāmau loa, ʻoiai ke kuʻi ʻana o nā nalu poʻo a hāmama i ke kakaka.

“E kalaiwa, e kalaiwa i ko oukou mau kui, e na nalu! e kipaku aku ia lakou i ko lakou mau poo! ke hahau nei oukou i kekahi mea me ka uhi ole; ʻaʻole naʻu ka pahu kupapaʻu a me ke kaʻa kupapaʻu:—a ʻo ka hemp wale nō ke pepehi iaʻu! Ha! ha!”

Ia manawa, pehu malie mai la na wai a puni lakou ma na poai akea; a laila, hu'i koke a'ela, me he mea lā e he'e ana ma ka 'ao'ao mai kahi pu'u hau i lalo, e pi'i koke ana i luna. Ua lohe ʻia kekahi leo halulu haʻahaʻa; he hum malalo; alaila paa iho la na mea a pau i ko lakou hanu; me he mea la me na kaula hope, a me na harpoon, a me na ihe, he ano nui i pana ma ka loa, aka, obliquely mai ke kai. Ua uhi ʻia i loko o kahi pale ʻeleʻele ʻeleʻele o ka noe, ua lele ʻo ia no kekahi manawa i ke ea anuenue; a hāʻule hou i loko o ka hohonu. He kanakolu kapuaʻi ka piʻi ʻana i luna, ʻānai aʻela ka wai me he mau pūnāwai lā, a laila, hāʻule ihola i loko o ka ʻauʻau ʻauʻau, a waiho ʻia ka ʻili kaʻapuni e like me ka waiu hou a puni ke kumu maʻa o ke koholā.

“E hāʻawi ala!” kahea aku la o Ahaba i ka poe hoe, holo aku la na waapa i ke kaua; aka, me ka huhu i na hao hou o nehinei i poino iloko ona, me he mea la ua paa pu o Moby Dick e na anela a pau i haule mai ka lani mai. ʻO nā ʻanuʻu ākea o nā ʻāʻī i hoʻopaʻa ʻia ma luna o kona lae keʻokeʻo ākea, ma lalo o ka ʻili alohilohi, ua ʻike ʻia i hui pū ʻia; i kona poo, hele mai la ia e wili ana i kona huelo iwaena o na waapa; a huikala hou ia lakou; e kahe ana i na hao a me na ihe mai na waapa o na hoa elua, a e hahau ana ma kekahi aoao o ka piko o ko laua mau kakaka, aka, e haalele ana ia Ahaba me ka ili ole.

Oiai o Daggoo a me Queequeg e hooki ana i na papa i paa; a i ka au ana mai o ke kohola mai o lakou aku, huli ae la ia, a hoike mai i kekahi aoao holookoa i kona pana hou ana ia lakou; ia manawa, piʻi aʻela ka uē wikiwiki. Kaʻi ʻia a puni i ke kua o ka iʻa; i hoʻopaʻa ʻia i loko o nā ʻeke a me nā huli, i ka pō i hala iho nei, ua hoʻokuʻu ʻia ke koholā i ka neʻe ʻana o nā laina e hoʻopuni ana iā ia, ua ʻike ʻia ke kino haehae hapa o ka Parsee; ʻo kona ʻaʻahu sable i weluwelu i nā ʻāpana; Ua huli ae kona mau maka hulahula ia Ahaba kahiko.

Ua hāʻule ka hare mai kona lima aku.

“Hoʻopunipuni, hoʻopunipuni!”—e huki ana me ka hanu lōʻihi—“ʻAe, Parsee! Ke ike hou aku nei au ia oe.—Ae, hele oe mamua; a ʻo kēia, ʻo kēia ka kaʻa kupapaʻu āu i ʻōlelo ai. Akā, ke paʻa nei au iā ʻoe i ka palapala hope loa o kāu ʻōlelo. Aia ma hea ka lua kaʻa kaʻa? E nā hoa, i ka moku! makehewa kela mau waapa i keia manawa; e hooponopono ia mau mea ina hiki ia oukou i ka manawa, a e hoi mai io'u nei; ina aole, ua lawa o Ahaba i ka make—Iho, e na kanaka! ʻO ka mea mua aʻu e hāʻawi nei e lele mai kēia waʻa aʻu e kū nei, ʻo ia mea aʻu e haʻi nei. Aole oukou he mau kanaka e, aka, o ko'u mau lima a me ko'u mau wawae; a e hoolohe mai ia'u.—Auhea ke kohola? ua iho hou i lalo?"

Aka, nana aku la ia a kokoke i ka waapa; no ka mea, me he mea la e manao ana e pakele me ke kino kupapau ana i hapai ai, a me he mea la he kahua wale no o ka halawai hope loa o ka huakai lele, e au hou ana o Moby Dick i mua; a ua aneane e hala aku i ka moku, - e holo ana ma ka aoao kue iaia a hiki i keia manawa, oiai nae i keia manawa ua paa kona alahele. Me he mea lā ʻo ia e ʻauʻau ana me ka wikiwiki loa, a i kēia manawa wale nō ka manaʻo e alualu i kona ala pololei i ke kai.

“Auwe! ʻO ʻAhaba," i hoʻōho ai ʻo Starbuck, "ʻaʻole i lohi, ʻoiai i kēia manawa, ke kolu o ka lā, e hoʻōki. E nana! ʻAʻole ʻimi ʻo Moby Dick iā ʻoe. ʻO ʻoe nō ka mea i ʻimi hehena iā ia!”
