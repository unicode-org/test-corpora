A e kukulu mua ia'u, he umikumamalua mau kookoo no kona kumu, alaila makani, a wili, a hahau i keia mau umikumamalua e like me na wili a me na kaula kaulahao. E wikiwiki au e puhi i ke ahi."

I ka hanaia ana o na kookoo he umikumamalua, hoao pakahi aku la o Ahaba ia lakou, me kona lima ponoi, a puni i ka pahu hao loihi a kaumaha. “He hemahema!” e hoole ana i ka hope. "E hana hou i kēlā, e Perth."

I keia hana ana, e hoomaka ana o Perth e wili i ka umikumamalua i hookahi, paa iho la o Ahaba i kona lima, a olelo aku la e wili iho oia i kona hao ponoi. E like me ia, me ka hahau mau ana, ua hamau iho la oia i ka anvil, haawi mai la o Perth ia ia i na kookoo olinolino, kekahi ma hope o kekahi, a o ka laau paa ikaika e puhi ana i kona lapalapa pololei, hele malie aku la ka Parsee, a kulou iho la maluna o kona poo i ke ahi, me he mea la e olelo ana i kekahi poino a i kekahi pomaikai ma ka hana. Aka, i ka nana ana aku o Ahaba iluna, hele aku la ia.

“No ke aha ia hui o lucifers e alo nei ma laila?” wahi a Stubb me ka nana ana mai ka wanana mai. "Ua honi kēlā Parsee i ke ahi me he fusee la; a honi ʻo ia iā ia iho, e like me ka pauda pu wela."

I ka hopena, ua loaʻa i ka shank, i loko o ka lāʻau piha hoʻokahi, kona wela hope; a oiai o Perth, e hoonawaliwali ai, ua hookomo iho la oia ia mea a pau e hoohuoi ana iloko o ka pahu wai e kokoke mai ana, lele ae la ka mahu enaena ana ma ka helehelena o Ahaba.

"Makemake anei ʻoe iaʻu, e Perth?" wiliwili no kekahi manawa me ka ʻeha; "A laila, ua hana wale au i kaʻu hao hao?"

"E pule aku oe i ke Akua, aole pela, aka, ke makau nei au i kekahi mea, e Kapena Ahaba. Aole anei keia no ke kohola keokeo keia?"

"No ka mea keokeo! Aka, i keia manawa no ka laau, nau no e hana ia mau mea, e ke kanaka. Eia ka'u pahi-o ke kila maikai, eia, a e hookala i na laau e like me ka nila o ke Kai hau."

No kekahi manawa, nānā ka ʻeleʻele kahiko i nā pahi ʻumiʻumi me he mea lā ʻaʻole ʻo ia e hoʻohana.

"E lawe oe ia lakou, e ke kanaka, aole o'u hemahema ia lakou; no ka mea, aole au e kahi ana, aole e ai, aole hoi e pule a hiki i keia manawa—e hana."

Ua hana ʻia ʻo ia ma ke ʻano pua, a hoʻopili ʻia e Perth i ke kumu, ua kuhikuhi koke ke kila i ka hope o ka hao; a i ka wā e hoʻomaka ai ka ʻeleʻele e hāʻawi i ka ʻauʻau i ko lākou wela hope, ma mua o ka hoʻoluliluli ʻana iā lākou, kāhea akula ʻo ia iā ʻAhaba e hoʻokomo i ka pahu wai a kokoke.

"Aole, aole—aohe wai no ia mea; makemake au i ka huhu make maoli. Auwe, aia! Tasetego, Queequeg, Daggoo! He aha ka oukou e olelo mai ai, e ka poe pegana! E haawi mai anei oukou ia'u i ke koko e like me ka uhi ana o keia laau?" e paʻa ana i luna. Pane mai kekahi puʻupuʻu pōʻeleʻele, ʻAe. ʻEkolu mau pū i hana ʻia i loko o ka ʻiʻo peʻe, a ua hoʻohaʻahaʻa ʻia nā lāʻau o ka Whale Keʻokeʻo.

"ʻAʻole i bapetizo ʻia i ka inoa patris, i koho ʻia i ka diaboli nomine!" Ua uwē ʻo ʻAhaba me ka ʻakaʻaka, ʻoiai ʻo ka hao ʻino i ʻai i ke koko bapetizo.

ʻĀnō, ʻo ka hōʻuluʻulu ʻana i nā ʻauamo ʻokoʻa mai lalo, a koho i kekahi o ka hickory, me ka waiho ʻana o ka ʻili, hoʻokomo ʻo ʻAhaba i ka welau i ke kumu hao. A laila wehe ʻia kahi ʻōwili huki huki hou, a lawe ʻia kekahi mau anana i ke aniani makani, a hoʻomāliʻi loa ʻia. E kaomi ana i kona wawae maluna ona, a kani ke kaula e like me ke kaula lira, a kulou me ka manao nui maluna ona, me ka ike ole i na mea e paa ana, i ae la o Ahaba, "Maikai, ano no ka hopu ana."

I kekahi wēlau, ʻaʻole nakinaki ʻia ke kaula, a ʻo nā kaula i hohola ʻia a pau i wili ʻia a ulana ʻia a puni ke kumu o ka harpoon; a laila, hoʻokuke ikaika ʻia ka ʻauamo i ke kumu; mai ka ʻaoʻao haʻahaʻa ua ʻimi ʻia ke kaula i ka hapalua o ke ala ma ka lōʻihi o ka lāʻau, a paʻa paʻa ʻia pēlā, me nā hui ʻana o ka māhoe. ʻO kēia hana, ka pou, ka hao, a me ke kaula—e like me nā ʻEkolu Kaulana—ua noho ʻole ia e hoʻokaʻawale ʻia, a ʻo ʻAhaba naʻau i hele me ka mea kaua; ʻo ke kani ʻana o kona wāwae ʻelepani, a me ke kani ʻana o ka pou hickory, e kani pū ana lāua ma kēlā me kēia papa. Akā, ma mua o kona komo ʻana i loko o kona keʻena, ua lohe ʻia ka leo ʻoi loa o ke kaumaha. ʻAe, Pip! kou ʻakaʻaka ʻino, kou maka palaualelo ʻole; ʻAʻole i hui pū ʻia kāu mau mama ʻē a pau me ka pōʻino ʻeleʻele o ka moku melancholy, a hoʻomāʻewaʻewa iā ia!
