Aole pela ka luina e nana ana i na nanaina o ke kai Antartika; I kekahi manawa, ma muli o ka mana o ka hau a me ka ea, e haʻalulu ana ʻo ia me ka hapa o ka moku, ma kahi o nā anuenue e ʻōlelo ana i ka manaʻolana a me ka hōʻoluʻolu i kona pōʻino, ke nānā aku nei ʻo ia i kahi pā hale pule palena ʻole e ʻakaʻaka ana iā ia me kona mau kia hau wīwī a me nā keʻa maʻalahi.

Akā, ke ʻōlelo nei ʻoe, ke manaʻo nei ʻoe ʻo ka mokuna keʻokeʻo keʻokeʻo e pili ana i ka keʻokeʻo, he hae keʻokeʻo ia i kau ʻia mai kahi ʻuhane ʻino; ke hāʻawi aku nei ʻoe i kahi hypo, ʻo Isemaʻela.

E hai mai oe ia'u, no ke aha la keia keiki hoki ikaika, i puaa ma ke awawa maluhia o Veremona, mamao loa mai na holoholona hihiu a pau—no ke aha la i ka la o ka la, ina oe e lulu i ka aahu bufalo hou mahope ona, i hiki ole ai iaia ke ike aku, aka, honi wale i kona kino holoholona hihiu—no ke aha la oia e hoomaka ai, e honi, a me ka poha o na maka o ka lepo? ʻAʻohe mea i hoʻomanaʻo ʻia i loko ona i nā ʻō ʻana o nā mea hihiu ma kona home ʻōmaʻomaʻo akau, i hiki ʻole ai i ka muskiness ʻano ʻē āna e honi ai ke hoʻomanaʻo iā ia i kekahi mea e pili ana i ka ʻike o nā pōʻino mua; No ka mea, heaha ka mea i ike i keia keiki hoki o Enelani hou, o na bisona eleele o Oregona mamao?

ʻAʻole: akā ma ʻaneʻi ke ʻike nei ʻoe me ka leo leo ʻole, ke ʻano o ka ʻike o ka daimonio ma ka honua. ʻOiai he mau kaukani mile mai Oregona mai, i kona honi ʻana i kēlā musk ʻinoʻino, ʻo nā pua bisona ʻāhaehae a ʻeha e like me ke keiki ʻāhiu mehameha o nā pā kula, i kēia manawa a lākou e hehi ai i ka lepo.

No laila, ka ʻolokaʻa ʻana o ke kai waiu; ʻo ka ʻūhū ʻino o nā hau hau o nā mauna; ka neoneo neoneo ana o na hau hau o na papu; ʻO kēia mau mea a pau iā ʻIsemaʻela, ua like ia me ka lulu ʻana o kēlā ʻaʻahu bufalo i ke keiki hoki makaʻu!

ʻAʻole naʻe i ʻike i kahi e waiho ai nā mea inoa ʻole i hōʻike ʻia e ka hōʻailona pohihihi; eia nae me a'u, e like me ke keiki hoki, ma kahi e pono ai e noho ia mau mea. ʻOiai ma nā ʻano he nui o kēia ao ʻike ʻia i hana ʻia i ke aloha, ua hoʻokumu ʻia nā pōʻai ʻike ʻole i ka makaʻu.

ʻAʻole naʻe mākou i hoʻoholo i ke ʻano o kēia keʻokeʻo, a aʻo i ke kumu e hoʻopiʻi ai me ia mana i ka ʻuhane; a ʻoi aku ka ʻē aʻe a me nā mea kupanaha loa—no ke aha, e like me kā mākou i ʻike ai, ʻo ia ka hōʻailona ʻoi loa o nā mea ʻuhane, ʻaʻole, ʻo ka pale ponoʻī o ke akua Karistiano; aka, e like me ia, ka mea hooikaika ikaika i na mea weliweli loa i na kanaka.

ʻO ia anei ma kona palena ʻole e hoʻopololei mai ana i nā ʻeleʻele puʻuwai ʻole a me ka nui o ke ao holoʻokoʻa, a pēlā e hou ai iā mākou ma hope me ka manaʻo o ka luku ʻana, ke ʻike aku i ka hohonu keʻokeʻo o ke ala waiu? A i ʻole, e like me ke ʻano o ka keʻokeʻo, ʻaʻole ia he kala e like me ka ʻike ʻole ʻia o ke kala; a ma ka manawa hoʻokahi ka kekona o nā kala āpau; No ia mau kumu anei ka leo ʻole, piha i ka manaʻo, i loko o kahi ākea o ka hau—he kala ʻole a me ka ʻole o ka manaʻo ʻole a mākou e emi ai? A i ka wā e noʻonoʻo ai mākou i kēlā manaʻo ʻē aʻe o ka poʻe akeakamai maoli, ʻo nā waihoʻoluʻu ʻē aʻe a pau o ka honua-ʻo kēlā me kēia nani nani a nani paha nā ʻano nani o ka lani a me ka lāʻau; he oiaio, a me na velveti nani o na lelele, a me na papalina pepeke o na kaikamahine opiopio; ʻO kēia mau mea a pau he mau hoʻopunipuni maʻalea wale nō, ʻaʻole i loaʻa i loko o nā mea waiwai, akā i waiho wale ʻia ma waho; i pena ai na Nature a pau e like me ka wahine hookamakama, aole i uhiia kona mau mea hoohihia i ka hale o loko wale no; a ke hele hou aku kakou, a noonoo iho i na mea a pau, o na tulip a me na rose, me kona ano hakahaka—e noonoo ana i keia mau mea a pau, aia ke ao loloa imua o kakou he lepera; a e like me ka poe huakai manao ma Lapania, ka poe hoole aole e komo i na makaaniani kala a me na makaaniani kala ma ko lakou mau maka, pela ka mea hoomaloka poino e nana makapo mai ana i ka uhi keokeo nui e uhi ana i na mea a pau a puni ia. A o keia mau mea a pau, o ke kohola Albino ka hoailona. Ke haohao nei oukou i ka hahai ahi?
