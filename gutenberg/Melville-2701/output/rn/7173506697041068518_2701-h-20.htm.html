UMUGAMBI WA 101. Igikapu.

Imbere y’uko ubwato bw’Abongereza buzimangana, bube bushizwe hano, bwavuye i Londres, kandi bwiswe izina ry’umuhisi Samuel Enderby, umucuruzi w’ico gisagara, uwa mbere w’inzu izwi cane y’uburovyi bw’amabaleine yitwa Enderby & Sons; inzu iyo mu ciyumviro c’umukene wanje w’umunyabalene, itaza kure cane y’inzu z’ubwami zihurikiye hamwe z’Abatudor n’Ababourbon, mu bijanye n’ugushiramwo akamaro nyakuri k’amateka. Igihe kingana iki, imbere y’umwaka w’Umukama wacu 1775, iyo nzu nini y’uburovyi bw’amafi yari imaze, inyandiko zanje nyinshi z’amafi ntizisobanura neza; ariko muri uwo mwaka (1775) ryarashizeho ubwato bwa mbere bw’Abongereza bwahora buhiga iyo baleine yitwa Sperm Whale; naho nyene mu myaka mikeyi imbere y’aho (kuva mu 1726) amasandugu yacu y’intwari n’ama Maceys yo muri Nantucket no mu murima w’imizabibu yari yarakurikiye iyo Leviyatani mu mato maninimanini, ariko mu kiyaga ca Atalantika co mu Buraruko no mu Bumanuko gusa: atari ahandi hantu. Bibe vyanditswe neza aha, ko Abanyantuckets ari bo ba mbere mu bantu gutera inkoni n’icuma giteye imbere igikoko kinini citwa Baleine Sperm; kandi ko mu kiringo c’igice c’ikinjana ari bo bonyene bo kw’isi yose bamutera inkota gutyo.

Mu 1778, ubwato bwiza cane bwitwa Amelia, bwarateguwe kugira ngo bukore iyo ntumbero, kandi bwari burongowe n’ubwato bw’inkomezi bwitwa Enderbys, bwarazungurutse Cape Horn n’ubushizi bw’amanga, kandi ni bwo bwa mbere mu mahanga bwose bwamanuye ubwato bw’ubwoko bwose bw’ibikoko vyitwa baleine mu kiyaga kinini co mu Bumanuko. Urugendo rwari urw’ubuhinga kandi rw’amahirwe; kandi asubira aho yari ahagaze afise igipfukisho ciwe cuzuye intanga z’agaciro, akarorero ka Amelia ntikatevye gukurikirwa n’ayandi mato, ay’Abongereza n’ay’Abanyamerika, gutyo rero ahantu hanini cane h’Ikiyaga ca Pasifike h’i Sperm Whale haciye hafungurwa. Ariko rero, iyo nzu itarushe ntiyabumbwa n’ico gikorwa ciza, yongeye kwivyura umutima: Samweli n’Abahungu biwe bose—ni bangahe, nyina wabo ni we wenyene abizi—kandi bariko barayicungera, kandi igice kimwe, nibwira ko, kubera bo, Leta y’Ubwongereza yavyuriwe umutima wo kohereza ubwato bw’intambara bwitwa Rattler mu rugendo rwo kuroba baleine rwo mu Bumanuko rwitwa disco. Irongowe n’umukuru w’ingabo zo mu mazi, iyo Rattler yaragize urugendo rw’agahomerabunwa, kandi irakora igikorwa kinaka; ingene bitaboneka. Ariko ivyo si vyo vyose. Mu 1819, iyo nzu nyene yarashizeho ubwato bwabo bwite bwo kuvumbura baleine, kugira ngo baje mu rugendo rwo kurobanura amazi yo mu mazi ya kure y’Ubuyapani. Ubwo bwato—bwitwa neza “Sirene”—bwakoze urugendo rwiza cane rwo kugerageza; kandi ni ko ikibanza kinini c’Abayapani co gukoreramwo baleine camenyekanye muri rusangi. Ubwato bwa Syren muri urwo rugendo rurangwa n’icubahiro bwari burongowe n’umukuru w’ubwato Coffin, yari umunya Nantucket.

Icubahiro cose rero kibe ku ba Enderbies, inzu yabo, nibwira ko iriho gushika n’uyu musi; naho ata gukeka Samweli w’intango ategerezwa kuba yaranyegeje kera cane umugozi wiwe aja ku kiyaga kinini co mu Bumanuko c’iyindi si.

Ubwato bwiswe izina ryiwe bwari bukwiye guterwa iteka, kuko bwari ubwato bwihuta cane kandi bwari ubwato bwiza cane mu nzira zose. Kuko, nk’ikintu rusangi, ubwato bw’ubudandaji bw’Abongereza buraca abakozi babwo; ariko si ko biri ku bijanye n’umunyabalene w’Umwongereza. UMUGAMBI WA 102. Umunyagihugu mu ntambara z’Abanyarwanda.

Gushika ubu, mu kuvuga mu buryo adondora Inyamaswa yitwa Baleine Sperme, nagumye canecane ku bitangaza vy’uruhande rwayo rw’inyuma; canke bitandukanye kandi mu buryo burambuye ku bintu bimwebimwe bikeyi vy’imibumbe y’imbere. Ariko kugira ngo nshobore kumutahura neza kandi mu buryo bushitse, birabereye ko ubu ndamufungura n’ubundi, no gufungura amasonga y’uruzitiro rwiwe, no gufungura imigozi yiwe, no kurekura insenga n’amaso y’ingingo z’amagufa yiwe y’imbere, nkamushira imbere yawe mu gihe ciwe c’iherezo; ni ukuvuga mu gipfunsi ciwe kitagira aho kigarukira.

Ariko none Ishimayeli? Ni gute wewe, wewe uri umusozi gusa mu vyo kuroba, wigira nk’uwuzi ikintu na kimwe ku bijanye n’ibihimba vyo munsi y’ubutaka vy’ico gikoko? Mbega Stubb w’umunyabwenge, yari ku capstan yawe, yaratanga inyigisho ku bijanye n’imiterere y’ibikoko vyitwa Cetacea; kandi bifashijwe n’umuyaga, ushire hejuru urubavu rw’akarorero kugira ngo rwerekanwe? Isobanure wewe nyene Ishimayeli. Woba ushobora gutera balene yuzuye ku gisenge cawe kugira ngo uyisuzume, nk’uko umutetsi ateka ingurube yokeje? Nta gukeka ko ataco. Wabaye icabona nyakuri gushika ubu, Ishimayeli; ariko witwararike ingene ufata agateka ka Yona wenyene; agateka ko gutanga insiguro ku bipande vy’amabuye n’ivy’amabuye y’agaciro; ibiti vy’amabuye, ibiti vy’imirongo, ibitandara n’ibiti vyo munsi, bikaba ari vyo bigize uruzitiro rwa leviyatani; kandi nk’ibibindi vy’amavuta, ivyumba vy’amata, amavuta n’amaforomaje biri mu mara yiwe.

Ndatura, ko kuva kuri Yona, abafi ba baleine bakeyi ari bo binjiye kure cane munsi y’urukoba rw’ico gikoko gikuze; naho ari ukwo, narahezagiwe n’akaryo ko kumucapura mu buryo butoya. Mu bwato nari ndimwo, akagazi gatoyi k’inyamaswa yitwa Sperm Whale karigeze guterurwa ku mubiri ku gisenge c’ubwato kugira ngo kakoreshe igipfukisho canke umufuko wako, kugira ngo kakoreshe ibipfukisho vy’amahembe y’amacumu, no kugira ngo kakoreshe imitwe y’amacumu. Wibaza ko naretse ako karyo kakagenda, ntakoresheje inkota yanje y’ubwato n’icumu ry’ubwato, nkamena ikidodo nkasoma vyose biri muri ako gahungu gatoyi?

Kandi ku bijanye n’ubumenyi mfise nyabwo bw’amagufa y’ico gikoko citwa leviyatani mu gukura kwaco gukomeye kandi gukomeye, kubera ubwo bumenyi budasanzwe ndabufitiye umwenda w’umugenzi wanje w’umwami yapfuye yitwa Tranquo, umwami wa Tranque, umwe mu ba Arsacides. Kubera ko nari i Tranque, ubu hakaba haciye imyaka, igihe nari mfatanye n’ubwato bw’ubudandaji Dey bwa Algiers, naratumiwe kumarana igice c’imisi mikuru y’Abanyarwanda n’umukama wa Tranque, mu nzu yiwe y’imisukoni yari yararuhutse i Pupella; ikiyaya co ku nkengera y’ikiyaga kitari kure cane y’ico abasare bacu bita Bamboo-Town, umurwa mukuru wiwe.

Mu zindi kamere nziza nyinshi, umugenzi wanje w’umwami Tranquo, kubera ko yari afise ingabire y’urukundo rwinshi ku bintu vyose vy’ubunyamaswa, yari yarakoranije i Pupella ibintu vyose bidasanzwe abafise ubuhinga kuruta abandi bo mu bantu biwe boshobora guhingura; ahanini ibiti bibajwe vy’ibikoresho bitangaje, ibikoko bicuzwe, amacumu ashizwemwo ibintu, amasozi azimvye, ubwato bumota neza; kandi ivyo vyose vyagabanwa mu bitangaza vyose vy’akameremere, imiraba y’ibitangaza, y’ugutanga ikori yari yatereye ku nkengera ziwe.

Umukuru muri izo za nyuma yari baleine nini cane yitwa Sperm Whale, iyo nyuma y’igihuhusi kirekire kidasanzwe, yari yasanzwe yapfuye kandi ipfuye, umutwe wayo uhagaze ku giti c’ikawa, amababa yaco ameze nk’ay’amababa, afise amabara ameze nk’ay’indege yiwe y’ibara ry’agahama. Igihe uwo mubiri munini wari warakuweko ibipfukisho vyawo vy’imbere, amagufa na yo agacika inkungugu yumutse ku zuba, ni ho uwo muvyimba wajanwa witonze uduga mu mwonga wa Pupella, aho ubu urusengero runini rw’imisukoni y’abami rwari ruwukingiye.

Imbavu zamanitswe n’ibikombe; ama vertebræ yari abajwe ibitabu vy’ivya kera vy’i Arsacide, mu nyuguti zidasanzwe z’ikigiriki; mu gihanga, abaherezi baraguma bafise urubeya rw’akamoto rutazimye, ku buryo uwo mutwe w’amayobera wongera gutanga umupfunda wawo w’umwotsi; mu gihe, rwamanitswe ku ishami, urwo rwasaya rwo hasi ruteye ubwoba rwari runyiganyiga hejuru y’abasenga bose, nk’inkota yamanitswe ku mushatsi yateye ubwoba cane Damocles.

Uburyo bwihuta Kapiteni Ahabu yari yaravuye mu bwato bwa Samuel Enderby bwo mu gisagara ca Londres, ntibwari butagira uwubucungera n’ubukazi bukeyi ku muntu wiwe bwite.
