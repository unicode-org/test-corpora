Imvo Linnæus yari gushingira intahe ko yarirukanye izo balene mu mazi, avuga ati: “Kubera umutima wazo ushushe w’amaso abiri, amahaha yazo, ibipfunsi vyazo vyiyunguruza, amatwi yazo afise imyobo, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem,” n’ubwa nyuma, “ex merito lege naturæ jure.” Ivyo vyose narabishikirije abagenzi banje Simeon Macey na Charley Coffin, bo muri Nantucket, bompi bari kumwe nanje mu rugendo runaka, maze bahuriza ku ciyumviro c’uko imvo zavuzwe zidahagije na gato. Charley yaravuze mu buryo buteye isoni ko ari humbug.

Bimenyekane ko, n’uguheba impaka zose, mfata imvo nziza ya kera y’uko iyo balene ari ifi, nkahamagara Yona mweranda ngo anshigikire. Ico kintu c’ishimikiro caratunganijwe, ingingo ikurikira ni, ni mu buryo ubuhe bwo mu mutima iyo baleine itandukaniyeko n’izindi fi. Hejuru, Linnæus ni we yaguhaye ivyo bintu. Ariko muri make, ni ibi: amahaha n’amaraso asusurutse; ariko, izindi fi zose zitagira amahaha kandi zifise amaraso akanye.

Mu kuvuga rero mugufi, balene ni ifi ivuga amajambo ifise umurizo uringaniye . Aho ni ho umufise. Uko iyo nsobanuro yoba igabanutse gute, ni ingaruka y’uguzirikana kwagutse. Charley Coffin yavuze ko yakoreshwa mu gutobora urubura; kuko iyo Narwhale, iyo iduze ikaja hejuru y’ikiyaga ca Polar, ikasanga gipfutse urubura, ica itera ihembe ryayo hejuru, maze igaca imenagura. Ariko rero, ntushobora kwemeza ko ivyo bibiri vyose wiyumvira ari ukuri. Iciyumviro canje bwite ni uko, naho iryo hembe ry’uruhande rumwe ryoshobora gukoreshwa vy’ukuri n’Ikinyamaswa c’Ikinyamaswa c’Ikinyamasyo—naho ivyo vyoba ari kwo kwose—nta gukeka ko vyoba vyiza cane kuri we kugira ngo agire dosiye yo gusoma udutabu. Ico gikoko citwa Narwhale numvise citwa baleine y’amahembe, baleine y’amahembe n’ico gikoko citwa baleine unicorn. Nta gukeka ko ari akarorero gateye umunezero k’Ubuzima Bumwe (Unicornisme) buboneka hafi mu bwami bwose bw’ibinyabuzima. Nakuye mu banditsi bamwebamwe ba kera bari mu bigo vy’idini, nararonse yuko iryo hembe nyene ry’ikiyaga-unicorn mu misi ya kera ryabonwa ko ari umuti munini wo kurwanya ubumara, kandi nk’uko vyari biri, kuritegura vyazana ibiciro vyinshi cane. Yari kandi ihindurwa umunyu ushobora guhinduka umunyu w’abagore bariko barazimira, nk’uko nyene amahembe y’imbwebwe y’ingabo ahindurwa amahembe y’inzovu. Mu ntango ubwavyo vyari bifatwa nk’ikintu co kumenya cane. Black Letter imbwira ko Sir Martin Frobisher igihe yariko aragaruka avuye muri urwo rugendo, igihe Umwamikazi Bess yamuzungurutsa ukuboko kwiwe kw’amabuye y’agaciro ari mw’idirisha ry’Ingoro ya Greenwich, igihe ubwato bwiwe bw’ubushizi bw’amanga bwariko buramanuka mu ruzi Thames; Black Letter avuga ati: “Igihe Sir Martin yava muri urwo rugendo, yapfukamye, yashikirije Nyakubahwa wiwe ihembe rirerire ry’igikoko citwa Narwhale, iryo rikaba ryamaze igihe kirekire rimanitswe mu ngoro y’i Windsor.” Umwanditsi umwe w’Umunyairlande avuga yuko Earl wa Leicester, apfukamye, na we nyene yashikirije umukuru wiwe irindi hembe, ryerekeye igikoko co ku butaka c’akameremere k’inzoka imwe.

Ico gikoko citwa Narwhale gifise ishusho nziza cane, kimeze nk’ingwe, kikaba gifise ibara ry’ubutare ryera nk’amata, gifise amabara y’umwirabura azunguruka kandi azunguruka. Amavuta yiwe araruta cane, aratomoye kandi meza; ariko hariho bike, kandi ntabwo ahigwa cane. Akenshi aboneka mu biyaga vy’i circumpolaire.

IGITABO II. ( Okatavo ), IGICE CA IV. ( Killer ).—Ku bijanye n’iyi balene, nta kintu kinini kizwi neza na neza ku muntu wo muri Nantucket, kandi nta co kizi na gato ku muntu yiyita umuhinga mu vy’ibidukikije. Nkurikije ivyo namubonye ndi kure, novuga ko yari nk’ubunini bw’igiti citwa grampus. Ni umunyamaswa cane—ubwoko bw’ifi yitwa Feegee. Rimwe na rimwe afata izo baleine nini za Folio ku munwa, akazimanika ng’aho nk’inzoka, gushika iyo nyamaswa y’inkomezi ihagaritse umutima gushika ipfuye. Umwicanyi nta na rimwe ahigwa. Sinigeze numva amavuta afise ameze gute. Izina ry’iyi baleine ryoshobora gufatwa nk’iridasanzwe, kubera ukuntu ridasobanutse neza. Kuko twese turi abicanyi, ku butaka no ku kiyaga; Bonaparte n’Ibikoko vy’Imisozi birimwo.

IGITABO II. ( Octavo ), IGICE CA V. ( Thrasher ).—Uwo mugabo azwi cane kubera umurizo wiwe, uwo akoresha nk’igiti co gukubita abansi biwe. Aca yurira umugongo w’ico gikoko citwa baleine Folio, kandi uko yoga, akora inzira yiwe mu kuyikubita; nk’uko abarimu bamwebamwe bafatanya mw’isi biciye ku nzira isa n’iyo. Naho ari ukwo, hariho bike cane bizwi ku vyerekeye Thrasher kuruta ku vyerekeye Umwicanyi. Bompi ni abanyamabanga, mbere no mu biyaga bitagira amategeko.

Uko ni ko IGITABO ca II gihera. ( Octavo ), kandi itangura IGITABO ca III. ( Icumi na kabiri ).

DUODECIMOS.—Muri ivyo harimwo n’ibikoko bitobito vyitwa baleine. I. Inzoka y’i Huzza. II. Igikoko c’i Alijeriya. III. Igikoko citwa Porpoise c’akanwa k’ifu.

Ku bantu bataronse amahirwe yihariye yo kwiga iyo nkuru, birashoboka ko bisa n’ibitangaje, ko amafi adasanzwe arenga amafuti ane canke atanu akwiye gushirwa mu mabarabara y’amafi—iryo jambo, mu buryo buzwi cane, ryama ritanga iciyumviro c’ubunini. Ariko ibiremwa vyashizwe haruguru nk’ibiremwa vyitwa Duodecimoes ni baleine ata makosa, nk’uko nsobanura ico balene ari co— ni ukuvuga ifi ivuga, ifise umurizo uringaniye.

IGITABO CA III. ( Duodecimo ), IGICE CA 1. ( Inzoka y’i Huzza ).—Iyo ni yo nkoko isanzwe iboneka hafi kw’isi yose. Izina ni iry’ivyo jewe ubwanje natanze; kuko hari ubwoko burenze bumwe bw’ibisiga, kandi hari ico bitegerezwa gukorwa kugira ngo bitandukanye. Ndamwita gutyo, kuko yama yoga mu binogo vy’amazi biteye umunezero, ivyo ku kiyaga kinini biguma vyijugunya mw’ijuru nk’inkofero mu bantu benshi bo ku musi wa kane Nyakanga. Mu bisanzwe, ukuntu zisa birashimagizwa n’umunezero n’umusare. Yuzuye impwemu nziza, ata guhinduka iva mu mipfunda y’umuyaga ikaja mu muyaga. Ni bo bahungu bama baba imbere y’umuyaga. Bifatwa nk’ikimenyetso c’amahirwe. Nimba wewe ubwawe ushobora kwihanganira umunezero utatu wo kubona izo fi zifise ubuzima, rero ijuru rigufashe; impwemu y’ugukina n’Imana ntabwo iri muri mwebwe. Igikoko citwa Huzza Porpoise kigaburiwe neza kandi gikomeye kizoguha igaloni imwe nziza y’amavuta meza. Ariko amazi meza kandi ateye igomwe akurwa mu rwasaya rwiwe arafise agaciro kanini cane. Ni ikintu gikenewe mu bacuzi b’amabuye y’agaciro n’abahinguzi b’amasaha. Abasare barayishira ku nkweto zabo. Inyama z’ingurube ni nziza kurya, urazi. Bishobora kuba utigeze wiyumvira ko inzoka y’inzoka ivuga. Nkako, umunwa wiwe ni muto cane ku buryo udashobora gutahurwa vyoroshe cane. Ariko igihe kizoza uzoba ufise amahirwe, umurabe; kandi uzoheza ubone baleine nini yitwa Sperm ubwayo mu buryo butoyi.

IGITABO CA III. ( Cumi na kabiri ), IGICE CA II. ( Inzoka y’i Algérie ).—Umusuma w’amato. Inyamaswa cane. Nibwira ko aboneka mu kiyaga ca Pasifike gusa. Ni munini cane kuruta Huzza Porpoise, ariko vyinshi mu vyo umukuru umwe. Mumushavuze, azokwifatanya n’igikoko citwa shark. Naramumanuye kenshi, ariko sinari bwigere mbona afashwe.

IGITABO CA III. ( Icumi na kabiri ), IGICE CA III. ( Inzoka y’akanwa k’ifu ).—Ubwoko bunini kuruta ubundi bwose bw’Inyamaswa; kandi iboneka mu kiyaga ca Pasifike gusa, nk’uko bizwi. Izina ryonyene ry’icongereza, gushika ubu yari afise, ni iry’abarovyi—Right-Whale Porpoise, bivuye ku kuntu ahanini aboneka mu micungararo y’ico Folio. Mu buryo bw’imiterere, itandukanye n’iyitwa Huzza Porpoise ku rugero runaka, kuko idafise umubiri munini cane kandi ugira umunezero mwinshi; nkako, ni umuntu afise umubiri mwiza cane kandi umeze nk’uw’umugwaneza. Nta mababa afise ku mugongo (ibindi bikoko vyinshi bifise), afise umurizo mwiza, n’amaso y’Abahindi afise ibara ry’umuhondo. Ariko akanwa kiwe k’ifu karona vyose. Naho umugongo wiwe wose gushika ku mababa yiwe yo mu ruhande ari uw’igiti citwa sable gikomeye, yamara hari umurongo w’urubibi, utandukanye nk’ikimenyetso kiri mu gipfukisho c’ubwato, witwa “igisusu gikayangana,” uwo murongo uramuca ku nzira kuva ku nzira y’ubwato gushika ku nzira y’inyuma, ukaba ufise amabara abiri atandukanye, hejuru ari ay’umwirabura, munsi ari ay’umweru. Umweru ugizwe n’igice c’umutwe wiwe, n’akanwa kiwe kose, ivyo bikaba bituma asa n’uwuherutse guhunga urugendo rw’ububisha rwo gusura umufuko w’ibifungurwa. Umuce w’ububisha kandi w’ifu! Amavuta yiwe asa cane n’ay’inyamaswa yitwa porpoise isanzwe.

Inyuma ya DUODECIM, iyo nzira ntigenda, kuko Porpoise ari yo ntoyi kuruta izindi baleine. Hejuru, ufise Lewiyatani zose zihambaye. Ariko hariho umugwi w’ibikoko bitamenyekana, bihunga, bifise igice c’ibinyoma, ivyo, nk’umunyamerika w’umunyamerika, ndabizi kubera izina ryavyo, ariko si jewe ubwanje. Nzoziharura nkurikije amazina yabo y’imbere y’ingoro; kuko bishoboka ko urutonde nk’urwo rushobora kuba rw’agaciro ku bazokora itohoza, bashobora kurangiza ivyo mfise hano ariko ntanguye. Nimba hariho muri izo balene zikurikira, izofatwa hanyuma igashirwako ikimenyetso, rero arashobora kwinjizwa muri iyo Sisitemu bitagoranye, hakurikijwe ubunini bwiwe bwa Folio, Octavo canke Duodecimo:—I balene y’Izuru ry’Icupa; igikoko citwa Baleine Junk; igikoko c’umutwe w’i Pudding; igikoko citwa baleine ca Cape; iyo Baleine Irongoye; igikoko citwa Baleine Cannon; igikoko citwa baleine citwa Scragg; iyo Baleine y’Umuringa; igikoko citwa Baleine Inzovu; iyo Balene y’Ibarafu; igikoko citwa Baleine citwa Quog; iyo Balene y’Ubururu; etc. Bivuye ku bategetsi bo muri Islande, mu Buholandi no mu Bwongereza bwa kera, hoshobora gusubirwamwo izindi ntonde z’ibikoko bita baleine bitamenyekana, vyahezagiwe n’ubwoko bwose bw’amazina atari meza. Ariko ndazikuraho nk’aho zitagikoreshwa rwose; kandi nta co bimaze gukeka ko ari amajwi gusa, yuzuye ubuleviyatani, ariko ata co bisobanura.

Ubwa nyuma: Vyavuzwe mu ntango, ko uru rutonde rutazoba hano, kandi rimwe, rutunganye. Ntimushobora kutabona neza ko nashitse kw’ijambo ryanje. Ariko ubu ndasiga Sisitemu yanje y’ivy’ubuhinga bw’amabuye y’agaciro ihagaze gutyo itarangijwe, nk’uko nyene Katedrale nini ya Kolonye yasigaye, iyo crane ikaba ikiri hejuru y’uwo munara utaraheza. Kuko inyubakwa ntoyi zishobora kurangira n’abahinga mu vy’ubwubatsi bazo ba mbere; abakuru, aba nyakuri, bahora basiga ibuye ry’agaciro ku ruvyaro. Imana irinde ko ntazokwigera nheza ikintu na kimwe. Iki gitabu cose ni igitabu gusa—oya, ahubwo ni igitabu c’igitabu. Ewe, Igihe, Inkomezi, Amahera, n’Ukwihangana!
