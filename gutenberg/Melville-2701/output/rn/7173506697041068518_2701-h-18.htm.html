UMUGAMBI WA 89. Ifi yihuta n’ifi yihuta.

Ivyo bivugwa ku waif na waif-poles mu kigabane ca nyuma ariko kimwe, birakeneye inkuru y’amategeko n’ingingo ngenderwako vy’uburovyi bw’amabaleine, ivyo waif bishobora gufatwa nk’ikimenyetso gihambaye n’ikimenyetso cavyo.

Kenshi na kenshi iyo ubwato bwinshi buriko buragendagenda bufatanye, urufi rushobora gukubitwa n’ubwato bumwe, hanyuma rukahunga, amaherezo rugapfa rugafatwa n’ubwato bumwe; kandi aha harimwo mu buryo butaziguye ibintu vyinshi bitobito bishobora gushika, vyose bikaba bifise uruhara muri ico kintu kimwe gihambaye. Nk’akarorero,—inyuma y’ugukurikirana no gufata baleine irushe kandi iteye akaga, umurambo woshobora gusohoka mu bwato kubera igihuhusi gikomeye; kandi igenda igenda kure cane ija mu ruhande rw’umuyaga, igasubirwamwo n’umurovyi wa kabiri w’amabaleine, uwo na we akayikurura mu buryo butuje, ata co akora, ata ngorane y’ubuzima canke umurongo. Gutyo, amatati atera ubwoba cane kandi y’ubukazi kenshi yovyuka hagati y’abarovyi, iyo hataba itegeko ryanditse canke ritanditswe, ry’isi yose, ritagiramwo impaka, rikoreshwa mu manza zose.

Kumbure itegeko ryonyene ry’uguroba baleine ryemerewe n’itegeko, ryari iry’Ubuholandi. Yategetswe na Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika mu mwaka w’1695 inyuma ya Kristu. II. Ifi y’urukundo ni umukino ubereye ku muntu wese ashobora kuyifata vuba.

Na yo nyene ifi iranyaruka mu buryo bw’ubuhinga iyo ifise waif, canke ikindi kimenyetso cose cemewe c’uko ifise; igihe cose uwuriko arayirekura yerekanye neza ubushobozi bwabo igihe cose bwo kuyijana iruhande, be n’umugambi wabo wo kubigira.

UMUGAMBI 90. Imitwe canke Imira.

“Umwana w’umuhungu arahagije, umugabo afise ubuzima bwiwe, n’umugabo wiwe.” Brakitoni, l. 3, nk. 3.

Ikilatini kivuye mu bitabu vy’Amategeko y’Ubwongereza, kikaba gifatwa hamwe n’ivyo bivugwamwo, bisobanura, ko mu bikoko vyose vy’amazi bifatwa n’umuntu uwo ari we wese ku nkengera y’ico gihugu, Umwami, nk’Umunyabubasha w’Ikigobe, ategerezwa kuba afise umutwe, Umwamikazi na we agashikirizwa umurizo mu buryo bwubaha. Ugucapura, mu balene, bisa cane n’ugucapura igice c’ipome; nta n’igisigaye kiri hagati. None nk’uko iryo tegeko, mu buryo buhinduwe, riguma rikora gushika n’uyu musi mu Bwongereza; kandi nk’uko itanga mu buryo butandukanye ikintu kidasanzwe gikora ku mutima itegeko rusangi ry’Ifi Yihuta n’Iyibohoka, aha iravugwa mu kigabane gitandukanye, ku ngingo ngenderwako nyene y’ikinyabupfura ituma amagariyamoshi y’Abongereza aba ku giciro c’imodoka itandukanye, igenewe canecane uburaro bw’abami. Mu kibanza ca mbere, mu gutanga ikimenyamenya c’uko iryo tegeko twavuze haruguru rigikora, ndabandanya ndabashira imbere ikintu cabaye mu myaka ibiri iheze.

Bisa n’uko abasare bamwebamwe b’inzirabugunge bo muri Dover, canke Sandwich, canke umwe mu bivuko vya Cinque, bamaze gukurikirana cane, bashoboye kwica no gutera ku nkengera y’ikiyaga balene nziza cane bari baradondoye mu ntango kure y’inkombe. Ubu Ivyambu vya Cinque biri munsi y’ububasha bw’ubwoko bumwe bw’umupolisi canke umupolisi w’umunyabubasha, yitwa Lord Warden. Kubera ko iyo nshingano iva ku ngoma, nizera ko amahera yose y’umwami ashika ku turere tw’Ikivuko ca Cinque aba ari ay’uwo. Abanditsi bamwebamwe ico kibanza citwa sinecure. Ariko si ko biri. Kuko Umukama Warden akora cane rimwe na rimwe mu gukora ama perquisites yiwe; ivyo bikaba ari ivyiwe canecane kubera iyo fobbing nyene yavyo.

None igihe abo bakene b’abasare baturiwe n’izuba, ata n’ibirenge bafise, kandi bafise amapantalo yabo apfutse hejuru ku maguru yabo y’ibara ry’agahama, bari bararushe bakwega ifi yabo y’ibinure hejuru kandi yumutse, barisezeranira amapawundi 150 meza y’amavuta n’igufa vy’agaciro; kandi mu vyiyumviro banywa icayi kidasanzwe n’abagore babo, n’inzoga nziza n’abagenzi babo, ku nkomezi z’imigabane yabo; hejuru y’intambwe umugabo yize cane kandi w’umukirisu cane kandi w’umunyabuntu, afise kopi y’igitabu Blackstone munsi y’ukuboko kwiwe; maze akayishira ku mutwe w’ico gikoko, akavuga ati—“Mukureho amaboko, iyi fi, ba shebuja, ni Ifi yihuta, ndayifata nk’iy’Umukama Umucungezi.” Kuri ivyo, abakene b’abasare mu gutinya kwabo kw’icubahiro—ni co gituma vy’ukuri ari Abongereza—batazi ico bavuga, baca bagwa mu gukaraba imitwe yabo n’inkomezi hirya no hino; mu gihe nyene ariko araba n’agahinda kuva kuri iyo balene gushika kuri uwo munyamahanga. Ariko ivyo ntivyashoboye gutorera umuti ico kibazo, canke ngo biyorohereze umutima ukomeye w’uwo mugabo w’umunyabwenge akoresheje kopi ya Blackstone.
