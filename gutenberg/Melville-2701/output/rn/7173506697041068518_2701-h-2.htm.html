UMUGAMBI WA 4. Igipande c’Ibarabara.

Bukeye mu gitondo nkavyuka nk’umutaga, nasanze ukuboko kwa Queequeg kuntereye mu buryo buranga urukundo n’igishika kuruta ubundi bwose. Wari hafi kwiyumvira ko nari umugore wiwe. Ico gipfukisho cari gikozwe mu bipande, cuzuye utugingo ngengabuzima dutoduto tw’amabara atandukanye n’inkomoko zitatu; kandi uku kuboko kwiwe kwari kwanditsweko igishushanyo c’i Kirete kitagira iherezo, ata bice bibiri vyaco vyari vy’igitutu kimwe nyaco—nibaza ko yagumye afise ukuboko kwiwe mu kiyaga ataco akora mu zuba no mu gitutu, amaboko y’ishati yiwe yari azingiye mu bihe bitandukanye—uku kuboko kwiwe kwose kwari nk’uko, igitambaro co gupfuka. Nkako, igice kimwe cari kiryamyeko nk’uko ukuboko kwari kwari igihe navyuka ubwa mbere, ntashobora kukimenya kubera igitambaro co gupfuka, vyari bivanze cane amabara yavyo; kandi ni ukubera gusa ukuntu numva uburemere n’umukazo nari nzi ko Queequeg ariko arampobera.

Ivyo nagira vyari bitangaje. Reka ngerageze kubisigura. Igihe nari umwana, ndibuka neza ikintu kimwe kimeze nk’ico canshikiye; yaba ari ukuri canke indoto, sinari bwigere nshobora guhagarara neza. Ivyo vyari bimeze gutya. Nari ndiko ndakata igiti c’umukaraba canke ikindi—niyumvira ko cariko kigerageza gukwegakwega mu mugende w’umwotsi, nk’uko nari nabonye umusuku mutoyi akora imisi mikeyi imbere y’aho; na mawe wanje w’umuvyeyi, mu buryo bumwe canke ubundi, yama ankubita, canke akanrungika mu gitanda ata mfungurwa z’umugoroba, — mawe yarankuye mu maguru ankuye mu mugende w’umwotsi, aca anpakira kugira ngo ndyame, naho nyene hari isaha zibiri gusa z’ijoro ryo ku wa 21 Ruheshi, umusi muremure kuruta iyindi yose mu mwaka mu gice c’isi cacu. Numvise biteye ubwoba cane. Ariko nta co vyari bimaze, nca ndaduga ku ngazi nja mu cumba canje gitoyi co mw’igorofa ya gatatu, ndayambura buhoro buhoro uko bishoboka kwose kugira ngo nice umwanya, maze n’uguhema gusharira nca nshika hagati y’ibitambaro.

Nararyamye ng’aho n’umubabaro mwinshi ndiko ndaharura yuko amasaha cumi n’atandatu yose ategerezwa guhera imbere y’uko nizigira izuka. Amasaha cumi n’atandatu mu gitanda! n’agatoyi k’umugongo wanje karababaza iyo ndavyiyumvira. Kandi na vyo nyene vyari bimeze nk’umuco; izuba ryari rikayangana mw’idirisha, n’ugutera ijwi ry’amakoshi mu mabarabara, n’ijwi ry’amajwi y’abasambanyi mu nzu yose. Narumvise ndiko ndarushiriza kuba nabi—amaherezo narahagurutse, ndambara, nca manuka buhoro buhoro mu birenge vyanje vy’amastoke, ndondera mawe wanje w’umuvyeyi, maze nca nsubira kwijugunya ku birenge vyiwe, ndamwinginga nk’ubuntu bwihariye ngo ampe igitsindo ciza co kunyerera kubera inyifato yanje mbi; ikintu cose vy’ukuri kiretse kuncira urubanza rwo kuryama n’igihe kirekire nk’ico kidashobora kwihanganirwa. Ariko ni we yari mwiza kandi afise ijwi ryo mu mutima ryiza kuruta abandi bose mu bavyeyi b’abavyeyi, maze ngasubirayo nari ntegerezwa kuja mu cumba canje. Naramaze amasaha menshi ndyamye ng’aho ntavyuka, numva nabi cane kuruta uko nari narigeze kwumva kuva ico gihe, mbere n’amagorwa akomeye cane yakurikiye. Amaherezo ntegerezwa kuba naraguye mu ndoto mbi y’agahinda y’ugusinzira; kandi buhoro buhoro nvyuka muri ryo—igice kimwe c’indoto—narafungura amaso, maze icumba cari caramuritswe n’izuba mbere ubu cari kizingiye mu mwijima wo hanze. Ubwo nyene numva igihuhusi kiriko kiragenda mu gice canje cose; nta co cari co kubona, kandi nta co cari co kwumvikana; ariko ukuboko kudasanzwe kwasa n’ugushizwe mu kwanje. Ukuboko kwanje kwamanitse hejuru y’igitanda, kandi igishushanyo canke ikinyoma kitagira izina, kitashobora kwiyumvirwa, kicereje, ico kiganza cari kirimwo, cari kimeze nk’ikicaye hafi y’igitanda canje. Kuko ivyo vyasa n’imyaka vyirundanijwe ku myaka, nari ndyamye ng’aho, nkonjeshejwe n’ubwoba buteye ubwoba kuruta ubundi bwose, ntatinya gukurura ukuboko kwanje; yamara n’igihe cose niyumvira ko iyo nshobora gusa kuyivyura inch imwe gusa, iyo nkuru iteye ubwoba yoba yacitse. Sinari nzi ingene uwo mutima amaherezo wannyereye; ariko nvyuka mu gitondo, vyose ndavyibuka n’uguhinda umushyitsi, maze imisi n’amayinga n’amezi inyuma y’aho narazimiye mu kugerageza gutera urujijo gusigura iyo nkuru y’akabanga. Oya, gushika n’iri saha nyene, kenshi ndavyibazako.

None, kuraho ubwoba buteye ubwoba, kandi inyiyumvo zanje zo kwumva ukuboko kudasanzwe kuri muri jewe vyari bisa cane, mu buryo budasanzwe, n’ivyo nashikiwe igihe navyuka nkabona ukuboko kwa Queequeg kw’abapagani kuntereye. Ariko amaherezo ibintu vyose vyabaye mw’ijoro ryaheze vyarasubiyemwo, kimwe kimwe, mu vy’ukuri bihoraho, maze nca ndaryama gusa ndi muzima ku ngorane zitwenza. Kuko naho nagerageza kumunyiganyiga ukuboko—gufungura igipfukisho c’umukwe wiwe—ariko, nk’uko yari asinziriye, yaraguma anhobera cane, nk’aho ata kindi codutandukanya atari urupfu. Ubu naragerageje kumuvyura—“Queequeg!”—ariko inyishu yiwe yonyene yari ugusonza. Naciye nca nzunguruka, izosi ryanje ryumva nk’aho riri mu nkweto y’ifarashi; kandi bukwi na bukwi yumva n’agakoko gatoyi. Bajugunye ku ruhande igipande c’imbere, aho hari iyo tomahawk isinziriye iruhande y’uwo munyamaswa, nk’aho yoba ari uruyoya rufise mu maso h’inkota. Igikoko ciza cane, vy’ukuri, ni ko niyumvira; abed hano mu nzu y’abandi ku musi mukuru, n’umuntu arya abantu n’inyamaswa yitwa tomahawk! “Umwamikazi!—mw’izina ry’ineza, Umwamikazi, vyuka!” Amaherezo, kubera ugutera akagere kwinshi, n’ugusemerera n’ijwi rirenga kandi ridahera ku bijanye n’ukuntu bidakwiye guhobera umugabo mugenziwe muri ubwo buryo bw’ubugeni, narashoboye gukuraho ugutontomera; kandi ubu nyene, aca asubiza inyuma ukuboko kwiwe, yizunguruka hose nk’imbwa yo muri Terre-Neuve ivuye mu mazi, aca yicara mu gitanda, akomeye nk’inkoni y’inzoka, arandaba, kandi asiga amaso yiwe nk’aho ataribuka rwose ingene naje kuba ng’aho, naho nyene hari ikintu giteye ubwoba co kumubona. Hagati aho, nari ndyamye n’amaso n’agacerere, nta nkeka zikomeye nari mfise ubu, kandi nari nshaka kwihweza mu buryo bukeyi ikiremwa gifise amatsiko menshi cane. Igihe, amaherezo, ubwenge bwiwe bwasa n’ubushizweho kugira ngo bukore ku nyifato y’uwo bari kumwe mu gitanda, maze akagira nk’aho asubirana n’ico kintu; yasimbutse hasi, maze biciye ku bimenyetso bimwebimwe n’amajwi bimwebimwe yarampaye gutahura ko, iyo bimpa umunezero, yobanza kwambara hanyuma akansiga nkambara inyuma, nkasiga inzu yose kuri jewe. Niyumvira jewe, Queequeg, mu gihe ibintu vyifashe gutyo, iyi ni inzira y’ubuhinga cane; ariko, ukuri ni uko abo banyamaswa bafise ubushobozi bwo gukunda ivyiza, vuga ivyo ushaka; biratangaje ukuntu mu vy’ukuri bafise indero. Ivyo ndabishimira cane cane Queequeg, kuko yanfashe neza cane kandi akanyitaho, mu gihe jewe nari mfise icaha c’ubujuju bwinshi; kumuraba ari ku gitanda, no kuraba inyigisho ziwe zose zo mu bwiherero; kubera igihe ugushaka kwanje kugenda kurarushiriza kuronka ubworozi bwanje. Naho biri ukwo, umuntu nka Queequeg ntumubona buri musi, we n’inzira ziwe bari bafise agaciro kadasanzwe ku bijanye n’ivyo.

Yatanguye kwambara hejuru mu kwambara inkofero yiwe y’ibara ry’agahama, iyo yari ndende cane, iruhande, hanyuma—akiriko arakuramwo amapantalo yiwe—arahiga inkweto ziwe. Ico munsi y’ijuru yabikoreye, sinshobora kumenya, ariko igikorwa ciwe cakurikiyeho ni ukwimenagura—inkweto mu minwe, n’inkofero—munsi y’igitanda; igihe, bivuye ku guhema n’uguhagarika umutima bitandukanye, naciye mbona ko yariko arakora cane ariko aritera; naho ata tegeko ry’ubutungane nigeze numva, umuntu uwo ari we wese asabwa kuba ari wenyene igihe yambara inkweto ziwe. Ariko Queequeg, woba ubona, yari ikiremwa kiri mu gihe c’uguhinduka—ntiyari igikoko canke ikinyugunyugu. Yari gusa umuntu ateye imbere bihagije kugira ngo yerekane ubunyamahanga bwiwe mu buryo butangaje kuruta ubundi bwose. Ivyigwa vyiwe ntivyari bitaraheza. Yari umunyeshure w’isumbuye. Iyo ataba ari umuntu ateye imbere gatoyi, birashoboka cane ko atazoba yibabaza n’ibirato na gato; ariko rero, iyo ataba akiri umunyamaswa, ntiyari kwigera arota kwinjira munsi y’igitanda kugira ngo abishireko. Amaherezo, yarasohotse afise inkofero yiwe ipfutse cane kandi imenetse ku maso yiwe, aca atangura gusemerera no gucumbagira mu cumba, nk’aho atamenyereye cane inkweto, inkweto ziwe zitose kandi zipfutse z’urukoba rw’inka—kumbure na zo nyene zitari zakozwe ku buryo butegekanijwe—ahubwo zamupfutse zimubabaza cane ku ncuro ya mbere.

Kubera ko ata mabarabara yari ku idirisha, kandi ko ibarabara ryari rito cane, inzu yari iteganye na yo yari itegeka ko umuntu abona neza mu cumba, kandi akarushiriza kwihweza igishushanyo c’agaterasoni Queequeg yakoze, agendagenda ata kindi kintu na kimwe atari inkofero yiwe n’inkweto ziwe; Naramwinginga uko nshoboye kwose, ngo yihutishe umwobo wiwe gatoyi, cane cane ngo yinjire mu mpuzu ziwe z’imbere vuba. Yaravyemeye, hanyuma aca abandanya kwiyuhagira. Ico gihe mu gitondo umukristo uwo ari we wese yari gukaraba mu maso; ariko Queequeg, ku buryo natangajwe n’uko yabumbwa no gukaraba gusa mu gikiriza, mu maboko no mu biganza. Yaciye yambara ikoti yiwe, aca afata igipande c’isabune ikomeye cari ku meza yo hagati y’aho bogera, aragibika mu mazi aca atangura gutera ifu mu maso hiwe. Nariko ndarabira aho yabitse inzobe yiwe, maze mbona, afata inkota mu mfuruka y’igitanda, akuraho igiti kirekire, akuraho umutwe, awutera gatoyi ku nkweto yiwe, maze atera intambwe aja ku gice c’indorerwamo kiri ku ruhome, atangura gukaraba n’inkomezi, canke ahubwo inkota yiwe. Niyumvira jewe, Queequeg, ivyo ni ugukoresha ibikoresho vyiza kuruta ibindi vyose vya Rogers n’uguhora. Inyuma y’aho, narabajije ukuntu ivyo bikorwa bigenda bigabanuka igihe namenya ingene umutwe w’inkota ukozwe mu cuma ciza, be n’ingene imirongo miremire igororotse yama igumye ityaye cane.

Ibindi vy’umwobo wiwe ntivyatevye gushika, aca asohoka mu cumba n’ubwibone, azingiriye mu mpuzu yiwe nini y’inkende y’umudereva, kandi akoresha inkoni yiwe nk’inkoni y’umukuru w’ingabo.





UMUGAMBI WA 5. Ivyo kurya vyo mu gitondo.

Nanje ndabikurikira ningoga, maze manuka mu cumba c’ibarabara, nca nsemerera cane nyen’inzu yariko aratwenga. Nta bubi nari mfise kuri we, naho nyene yariko aravugana nanje ata gatoyi ku bijanye n’uwo twari dusangiye igitanda.

Ariko rero, ugutwenga neza ni ikintu ciza gikomeye cane, kandi ahubwo ni ikintu ciza gike cane; uko niko ikigongwe kirushiriza. Rero, nimba umuntu umwe, mu muntu wiwe bwite, ashobora gutanga ibintu vy’agatwengo keza ku muntu uwo ari we wese, ntasubire inyuma, ahubwo yemere n’umunezero gukoresha no gukoreshwa muri ubwo buryo. Kandi uwo mugabo afise ikintu cose kimutwenga cane, menya neza ko muri uwo mugabo harimwo vyinshi kuruta ivyo kumbure wiyumvira.

Icumba c’ibarabara cari cuzuye aba boarders bariko baragwa mw’ijoro ry’imbere y’aho, kandi ntari narigeze ndababona neza. Hafi bose bari abanyamaswa b’amabalene; abakuru b’abagenzi, n’abagenzi ba kabiri, n’abagenzi ba gatatu, n’ababizi bo mu kiyaga, n’abacuzi b’ivyuma bo mu kiyaga, n’abacuzi bo mu kiyaga, n’abacuzi b’inkoni n’abacungera ubwato; umugwi w’abantu b’inzobe kandi b’inzobe, bafise ubwana bw’inzobe; n’impuzu zitagira umusatsi, zifise ubwoya, bose bambaye amakoti y’inkende y’impuzu zo mu gitondo.

Woshobora kumenya neza igihe umwe wese yari amaze ku nkombe. Itama ry’uwo musore rifise amagara meza rimeze nk’ipera ry’izuba ry’ibara, kandi ryosa n’irinuka hafi nk’iry’umukara; ntashobora kuba amaze imisi itatu avuye mu rugendo rwiwe rwo mu Buhindi. Uwo mugabo ari iruhande yiwe asa n’uwufise ibitutu bikeyi vy’umuco; wovuga ngo ugukora ku giti c’isatini kuri muri we. Mu rangi y’umuntu wa gatatu haracariho ibara ry’agahama ry’ibara ry’agahama, ariko ryari ryera gatoyi; nta gukeka ko amaze amayinga yose ku nkombe. Ariko none ni nde yoshobora kwerekana itama nka Queequeg? iyo, ikaba yari ifise amabara atandukanye, yasa n’aho ari umusozi wo mu burengero bw’imisozi ya Andes, kugira ngo yerekane mu buryo bumwe, imihindagurikire y’ikirere itandukanye, akarere ku karere.

“Grub, ho!” ubu rero nyen’inzu araboroga, afungura urugi, maze twinjira tuja gufungura mu gitondo.

Bavuga ko abantu babonye isi, gutyo bacika abaruhutse cane mu migenzo, bakikunda cane iyo bari kumwe n’abandi. Ariko rero, si ko vyama bigenda: Ledyard, umugenzi akomeye w’Ubwongereza bushasha, na Mungo Park, uwo muri Écosse; mu bantu bose, ni bo bari bafise icizigiro gitoyi kuruta ibindi vyose mu cumba co gusangiriramwo. Ariko kumbure kujabuka Siberiya gusa mu nzira ikwegwa n’imbwa nk’uko Ledyard yabigenjeje, canke gukora urugendo rurerure rw’umuntu ari wenyene afise inda y’ubusa, mu mutima w’abazungu wo muri Afirika, ivyo bikaba vyari umugwi w’ibikorwa vy’abakene Mungo—ubwo bwoko bw’urugendo, ndavuga, bushobora kuba atari bwo buryo bwiza cane bwo gushika ku ntumbero yo mu kibano. Naho ari ukwo, ahanini, ivyo bintu nk’ivyo ni ivyo kuronka aho hose.

Ivyo vyiyumviro aha nyene biterwa n’ivyo twamaze kwicara ku meza twese, kandi nariko nditegurira kwumva inkuru nziza zimwe zimwe zijanye n’uguroba baleine; ntangazwa n’uko hafi umugabo wese yagumye acereje cane. Kandi si ivyo gusa, ahubwo basa n’abamaramaye. Egome, ng’aho hariho umugwi w’imbwa zo mu kiyaga, benshi muri bo ata n’agahinda gatoyi bari baje mu bikoko bininibinini vyitwa baleines mu kiyaga kinini—bakaba bari abanyamahanga kuri bo—maze bakabirwanya bapfuye ata n’umwe abipfutse; kandi naho ari ukwo, ng’aho bari bicaye ku meza y’ifunguro rya mu gitondo ry’abantu bose—bose bari bafise ihamagarwa rimwe, bose bari bafise ivyo bakunda—baraba hirya no hino nk’intama nk’aho batigeze bava mu maso y’urugo rw’intama rumwe ruri hagati y’Imisozi y’Ivyatsi Bitotahaye. Igishushanyo c’ugushaka kumenya; abo badubu b’isoni, abo ba baleine b’intambara b’isoni!

Ariko ku bijanye na Queequeg—kubera iki, Queequeg yari yicaye ng’aho hagati yabo—ku mutwe w’imeza, na we nyene, vyabaye ku mpanuka; nk’uko bimeze nk’urubura. Kugira ngo nshobore kumenya neza ko ntashobora kuvuga vyinshi ku bijanye no kuvyara. Uwo yamushima cane ntiyari gushobora gushingira intahe n’umutima ukunze ko yazanye inkota yiwe mu gitondo bari kumwe na we, maze akayikoresha ng’aho ata n’umugigwa; gushika hejuru y’imeza na ryo, ku kaga kari hafi k’imitwe myinshi, no gufata inyama z’inka zimugana. Ariko ivyo nta gukeka ko yabikoze mu buryo buteye igomwe cane, kandi umuntu wese arazi ko mu vyo abantu benshi bavyiyumvira, gukora ikintu cose mu buryo buteye igomwe ari ukugikora mu buryo buteye igomwe.

Ntituzovuga ivy’ubudasa bwose bwa Queequeg hano; ingene yaririnze ikawa n’ibifungurwa bishushe, maze agashira umutima wiwe wose ku nyama z’inka, zikorwa gake. Hagije, ko igihe icayi co mu gitondo cari caraheze, yaciye asubira inyuma nk’abandi yinjira mu cumba ca bose, acana umuriro w’umuyoboro wiwe wa tomahawk, aca yicaye aho ariko arafungura kandi anywa itabi yambaye inkofero yiwe idashobora gutandukana, igihe nasohoka n’amaguru kugira ngo ngende.





UMUGAMBI WA 6. Ibarabara.

Iyo ntangara mu ntango mbona umuntu w’agatangaza nka Queequeg agendagenda mu bantu b’indero bo mu gisagara giteye imbere, ukwo gutangara ntikwatevye guhera igihe nafata urugendo rwanje rwa mbere rwo ku murango mu mabarabara y’i New Bedford.

Mu mabarabara maninimanini ari hafi y’ibibanza vy’indege, ikivuko cose gikomeye co mu kiyaga kizokunda gutanga uburyo bwo kuraba ibintu biteye ubwoba cane biva mu bice vy’amahanga. Mbere no mu mabarabara ya Broadway n’aya Chestnut, abagendera mu mazi bo mu kiyaga Mediterane rimwe na rimwe bazotera ubwoba abo bagore bateye ubwoba. Ibarabara rya Regent ntirimenyekanye ku ba Lascars n’aba Malawi; kandi i Bombay, mu gisagara ca Apollo Green, aba Yankees babaho kenshi barateye ubwoba abavukiye ng’aho. Ariko New Bedford iratsinda amabarabara yose y’amazi n’i Wapping. Muri ivyo bibanza vya nyuma vyavuzwe ubona abasare gusa; ariko i New Bedford, abantu nyabo barya abantu barahagarara bariko barayaga ku mfuruka z’ibarabara; abanyamaswa ataco baciyeko; benshi muri bo baracariko baratwara amagufa yabo inyama zitari nziza. Bituma uwundi muntu atazi abona.

Ariko, uretse Abafeegee, Abatongatobooar, Abaerromanggo, Abapannangi, n’Ababrighggian, kandi, uretse ibikoko vy’ibikoko vy’ubwato bwo kuroba baleine vyari bigendagenda mu mabarabara ataco bimaze, uzobona n’ibindi bintu biteye umunezero kuruta, ata gukeka ko biteye umunezero kuruta. Aho ni ho buri ndwi hashika muri iki gisagara abagabo benshi cane b’ivyatsi bitotahaye bo muri Vermont n’abo muri New Hampshire, bose banyotewe inyungu n’ubuninahazwa mu bijanye n’uburovyi. Akenshi ni abato, b’imibiri ikomeye; bagenzi bacu batemye amashamba, ubu bakaba barondera gutera isōko no gufata icumu ry’ibaleine. Benshi ni ivyatsi bitotahaye nk’Imisozi y’Ivyatsi bitotahaye aho bavuye. Mu bintu bimwe bimwe wovyiyumvira ariko amasaha makeyi. Raba aho! uwo chap ariko arazunguruka imfuruka. Yambara inkofero y’inzoka n’ikoti ry’umurizo w’inzoka, akenyeye umukanda w’umusare n’icumu ry’umugozi. Aha haje uwundi afise sou’-wester n’impuzu y’ibombe.

Nta dandy wo mu gisagara azogereranywa n’uwo mu gihugu—ndashaka kuvuga dandy w’umunyaruyeri—umuntu, mu misi y’imbwa, azokeba amahegitare yiwe abiri akoresheje amaganzu y’urukoba rw’inzovu kubera ubwoba bwo gutera ibiganza vyiwe. None iyo umunyagihugu dandy nk’uyu abifashe mu mutwe kugira ngo agire izina ryiza, maze akifatanya n’uburovyi bukomeye bw’amafi, ukwiye kubona ibintu bitwenza akora iyo ashitse ku cambu. Mu kuvuga ivy’impuzu yiwe yo mu kiyaga, ategeka ko bamutera inkengeri ku makoti yiwe; imigozi ku mpuzu ziwe z’impuzu. Ah, umukene w’Imbuto y’Ivyatsi! mbega ukuntu iyo migozi izoturika bibabaje mu gihuhusi ca mbere kiboroga, iyo uzoba uriko uragenda, imigozi, amabuto, na vyose, bimanuka mu muhogo w’igihuhusi.

Ariko ntimwiyumvire ko ico gisagara kizwi cane gifise gusa abatera amabuye y’agaciro, abarya abantu n’ibikoko vyo mu mazi co kwereka abashitsi baco. Nta na kimwe. Naho ari ukwo, New Bedford ni ahantu h’agatangaza. Iyo tutaba twebwe aba baleine, kumbure uwo musi uno musi wari kuba uri mu bihe vy’uguboroga nk’inkengera y’ikiyaga ca Labrador. Nk’uko biri, ibice vy’igihugu ciwe c’inyuma birahagije kugira ngo umuntu atere ubwoba, bisa n’amagufa cane. Igisagara ubwaco kumbure ni co kibanza gikundwa kuruta ibindi vyose co kubamwo, muri Nouvelle-Angleterre yose. Ni igihugu c’amavuta, ni ukuri bihagije: ariko si nka Kanani; igihugu, na co nyene, c’ibigori n’umuvinyu. Ibarabara ntiryiruka n’amata; canke mu gihe c’impeshi ntibazitera amagi mashasha. Yamara, naho biri ukwo, nta hantu na hamwe muri Amerika yose uzosanga amazu ameze nk’ay’abapatiri; amaparike n’amatongo vyiza cane, kuruta i New Bedford. None bavuye hehe? ni gute vyatewe kuri iyi scoria ya kera y’igihugu?

Genda urabe ivyuma vy’ikigereranyo vy’inkoni zikikuje iyo nzu ndende, ikibazo cawe kizokwishurwa.
