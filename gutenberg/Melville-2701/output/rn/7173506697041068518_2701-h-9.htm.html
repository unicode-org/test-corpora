Naho, Ahabu yari yariwe n’umuriro ushushe w’umugambi wiwe, mu vyiyumviro vyiwe vyose no mu bikorwa vyiwe vyose yari afise iciyumviro co gufata Moby Dick mu buryo bw’iherezo; naho yasa n’uwuteguye gutanga inyungu zose z’ubuzima ku bw’ico cipfuzo kimwe; naho ari ukwo, bishobora kuba ari uko yari afise kamere n’akamenyero kamaze igihe kirekire cane, ku buryo atashobora guheba rwose igikorwa co gukurikirana urugendo.
