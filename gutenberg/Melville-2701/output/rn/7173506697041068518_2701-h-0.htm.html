Igitabo c’ubuhinga bwa none c’umugambi wa Gutenberg; Canke, Ikiyaga

Ico gitabu c’ubuhinga bwa none ni ic’umuntu wese aho ari ho hose muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika no mu bindi bice vyinshi vyo kw’isi ataco bimaze kandi hafi ata n’imwe ibujijwe. Ushobora kuyikopa, ukayitanga canke ugasubira kuyikoresha hakurikijwe amabwirizwa y'Uruhusha rw'Umugambi wa Gutenberg ruri muri iki gitabu canke kuri Internet kuri www.gutenberg.org . Nimba utari muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika, uzobwirizwa kugenzura amategeko y’igihugu urimwo imbere y’uko ukoresha iki gitabu c’ubuhinga bwa none.

IVYO BIKURAMWO. (Itangwa n’Umucungerezi w’Ibitabo).

Bizoboneka ko uwo mucukuzi w’umunyaruyeri n’inzoka y’umukene w’umudayimoni w’i Sub-Sub asa n’uwaciye mu ma Vatikani maremare no mu mabarabara y’isi, atoragura amajambo yose yerekeye baleine yoshobora gusanga mu gitabu ico ari co cose, ceranda canke gihumanye. Ntimukwiye rero, mu bihe vyose n’imiburiburi, gufata amajambo y’inyamaswa yitwa higgledy-piggledy whale, naho yoba ari ay’ukuri gute, muri ivyo bice, ngo ni cetologie y’ubutumwa bwiza nyakuri. Kure y’ivyo. Nk’uko bikora ku banditsi ba kera muri rusangi, be n’abanyamivugo bariko baraboneka ngaha, ivyo bice birafise agaciro canke biraryoshe gusa, kuko bituma umuntu abona nk’inyoni ivyo vyavuzwe, vyiyumviriwe, vyiyumviriwe, kandi biririmbwa ku bijanye na Leviyatani mu buryo buteye isoni, n’amahanga menshi n’ibihe vyacu, harimwo n’ivyacu.

Nuko rero genda neza, shetani w’umukene w’umu Sub-Sub, ndi umusobanuzi wiwe. Uri uwo mu muryango utagira icizigiro, w’umukara ata muvinyu w’iyi si uzokwigera ushusha; kandi kuri bo mbere na Pale Sherry yoba ari umunyabubasha cane; ariko uwo umuntu rimwe na rimwe akunda kwicarana na we, kandi akumva ari umukene-wa shetani, na we nyene; kandi ukure mu gukundana n’amarira; kandi ubabwire ataco ushizeko, amaso yuzuye n’ibirahuri ata co bimaze, kandi mu mubabaro udashimishije rwose—Mubihebe, Sub-Subs! Kuko uko muzorushiriza kwihatira guhimbara isi, ni ko muzorushiriza gushimira ibihe vyose! Iyo nshobora gukuraho Hampton Court n’i Tuileries ku bwanyu! Ariko mumire amarira yanyu, muduge hejuru ku nkingi y’ubwami n’imitima yanyu; kuko abagenzi bawe baciye imbere bariko barakuraho amajuru y’amagorofa indwi, bagahindura impunzi za Gaburiyeli, Mikaheli na Rafayeli bamaze igihe kirekire bakunda cane, ngo barwanye ukuza kwawe. Aha mukubita ariko imitima isaduka hamwe—aho, muzotera ibirahuri bitasaduka!
