BOQONNAA 55. Kan Suuraalee Binessaa Qurxummii Qurxummii.

I shall ere long paint to you as well as one can without canvas, waan akka bifa dhugaa qurxummii galaanaa akka inni dhugumatti ija nama qurxummii galaanaatti mul'atu yeroo qaama ofii isaa isa guutuu keessatti qurxummiin qurxummii galaanaa cinatti moored ta'e akka achitti haqaan tarkaanfachuun ni danda’ama. Kanaaf, kanaan dura suuraawwan yaadame hawwataa ta’an kanneen hanga har’aattillee amantii nama lafaa ofitti amanamummaadhaan morman sana beeksisuun gatii qabaachuu danda’a. Dhimma kana irratti addunyaa kana sirreessuu yeroon isaa amma, suuraawwan qurxummii akkasii hunduu dogoggora ta’uu isaanii mirkaneessuudhaan.

Maddi jalqabaa gowwoomsaa fakkii sana hundumaa fakkiiwwan Hindoo, Gibxii fi Giriikii durii keessaa kan argamu ta’uu danda’a. Bara kalaqaa garuu naamusa hin qabne sana irraa kaasee yeroo paanaalii marbilii mana qulqullummaa, bakka siidaa fi gaachana, meedaaliyaa, xoofoo fi saantima irratti, doolfiin madaallii hidhannoo sansalataa akka kan Salaadiin, fi mataa kophee akka fakkaatuun harkifama ture Qulqulluu Giyoorgis; ergasii kaasee fakkiiwwan qurxummii galaanaa beekamoo taʼan irra caalaan isaanii qofa utuu hin taʼin, dhiheessi saayinsii hedduu isa ilaalchisee wanti hayyama gosa wal fakkaatutu moʼeera.

Amma, haala hundumaan, suuraan hanga ammaatti jiru inni durii ta'ee fi kan qurxummii galaanaa ta'uu isaa kan himatu, holqa beekamaa Elephanta, Hindii keessatti argama. Braahmiin akka jedhanitti, fakkiiwwan dhuma hin qabne jechuun ni danda’ama kan paagodaa hin yaadatamne sanaa keessatti, daldalli fi hordoffiin hundi, hojiin namaa yaadamuu danda’u hundi, isaan keessaa tokkollee qabatamaan uumamuu isaa dura waggoota dursanii fakkeeffamanii turan. Kanaaf, gosa tokkoon ogummaan keenya inni kabajamaan qurxummii galaanaa adamsuu achitti golboo taʼee mulʼachuu akka qabu nama hin ajaaʼibu. Qurxummiin Hindoo jedhamu kan eerame, kutaa dallaa adda ta'e keessatti kan mul'atu yoo ta'u, foon uffachuu Viishnuu bifa leviathan, kan barame Matse Avatar jedhamuun beekamu agarsiisa. Garuu siidaan kun walakkaa namaa fi walakkaa qurxummii galaanaa ta'us, akka funyaan isa boodaa qofa kennuudhaaf, ammas kutaan xiqqaan isaa sun hundi dogoggora. Inni caalaatti funyaan anaconda kan qaxxaamuraa ta'e fakkaata, harka bal'aa 'whale' dhugaa majestic flukes caalaa.

Garuu gara Galleries durii dhaqi, amma immoo suuraa fakkii kaasu Kiristaana guddaa qurxummii kanaa ilaali; sababiin isaas inni Hindoo lola dura ture caalaa hin milkaa’u. Innis suuraa Guido kan Perseus Andromeda bineensa galaanaa ykn whale irraa baraarudha. Guidoon moodeela uumama ajaa'ibaa akkasii eessaa argate? Akkasumas Hogarth, “Perseus Descending” mataa isaa keessatti mul’ata wal fakkaatu dibuu isaatiin, xiqqoo fooyya’aa hin agarsiisu. Qaamni guddaan bineensicha Hogarthian sanaa bishaan inchii tokko baayʼee hin harkisu. Dugda isaa irratti howdah gosa tokkoo kan qabu yoo ta’u, afaan isaa kan distended tusked kan billows itti gulufu, Karra Gantootaa Teems irraa bishaaniin gara Tower geessuuf fudhatamuu danda’a. Achiis, qurxummiin Prodromus kan durii Iskootish Sibbald, fi qurxummiin Yoonaas, akkuma maxxansa Macaafa Qulqulluu durii fi ciccitaa priimeroota durii keessatti mul’atu jiru. Isaan kana maal jedhama? Waa’ee qurxummiin kitaaba hidhu akka citaa wayinii istookii ankuraa gadi bu’aa jirutti marfamee jiru—akka dugdaa fi fuula mata duree kitaabota hedduu durii fi haaraa irratti chaappaa fi warqeedhaan marfame—sun uumama baay’ee fakkii gaarii qabu garuu qulqulluu ta’ee fi sheekkoo ta’eedha, kan fakkeeffame, fakkiiwwan walfakkaatan vaazii durii irratti argaman irraan fudhadha. Akka addunyaatti doolfiin jedhamee kan waamamu taʼus, kanas taʼe sana qurxummii kitaaba hidhu kana yaaliin qurxummii galaanaa (whale) jedheen waama; sababni isaas yeroo meeshaan kun jalqaba dhiyaate akkasitti yaadamee waan tureef. Maxxansiisaa durii Xaaliyaanii tokkoon bakka tokkotti naannoo jaarraa 15ffaatti, yeroo Haaromsa Barnootaa; akkasumas bara sanatti, fi yeroo wal bira qabamee yoo ilaalamu yeroo boodaatti illee, doolfiinoonni gosa Leviyaatan akka taʼan namoota baayʼeedhaan yaadamaa turan.

Vignettes fi faaya biroo kitaabota durii tokko tokko keessatti yeroo tokko tokko tuqaalee baay'ee hawwataa ta'an waliin wal argitu, bakka gosti spouts, jets d'eau, burqaawwan ho'aa fi qorra, Saratoga fi Baden-Baden, kan isaa irraa bubbling ol dhufan sammuu hin dadhabne. Fuula mata duree maxxansa jalqabaa “Guddina Barnootaa” irratti qurxummii galaanaa (whales) hawwii guddaa qabu tokko tokko ni argattu.

Garuu yaaliiwwan ogummaa hin qabne kana hunda dhiisnee, suuraawwan leviathan of sober, scientific delineations, warra beekaniin jedhaman sana haa ilaallu. Kuusaa imala Hariis durii keessatti gabateewwan qurxummii galaanaa tokko tokko kitaaba imala biyya Nezerlaand, Dh.K.D. Gabateewwan sana keessaa tokko irratti qurxummiin galaanaa akkuma raaftii mukaa gurguddaa odolawwan bubbee gidduutti ciisanii, risaawwan adiin dugda isaanii lubbuu qabu irra fiigan bakka buʼaniiru. Gabatee kan biraa keessatti, dogongorri ajaaʼibsiisaa kun, qurxummii galaanaa sana bakka buʼuudhaan, flukes perpendicular flukes taʼaniin kan hojjetame dha.

Sana booda ammas, quarto ajaa’ibaa tokkotu jira, Kaapteen Kolnet tokkoon, Kaapteen Poostaa humna galaanaa Ingilizii keessatti barreesse, mata duree “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, for the purpose of extending the Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Kitaaba kana keessatti, “Fakkii qurxummii galaanaa Physeter ykn Spermaceti, isa qarqara galaanaa Meksikootti, Hagayya, 1793tti ajjeefamee fi deekii irratti ol kaafame irraa safartuudhaan kan kaafame” jedhamee yaadamu tokkotu jira. Kaapteeniin sun suuraa dhugaa kana faayidaa loltoota galaanaa isaatiif akka kaafamu hin shakku. Waa’ee isaa waan tokko qofa kaasuuf, ija akka madaallii isa waliin jiruutti, qurxummii isparmii guutummaatti guddate irratti dibame, ija qurxummii sanaa foddaa xiyyaa gara meetira shan dheeratu akka ta’u godha jedheen haa dubbadha. Ah, yaa ajajaa goota koo, maaliif Yoonaas ija sana keessaa ilaaluu nuuf hin kennine!

Akkasumas, qindeessitoonni Seenaa Uumamaa baay’ee qalbiidhaan guutaman faayidaa dargaggootaa fi gara laafessa ta’aniif, dogoggora fokkisaa wal fakkaatu irraa bilisa miti. Hojii beekamaa sana “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature” jedhu ilaalaa. Maxxansa gabaabfame Landan bara 1807 baʼe keessatti, gabateewwan “whale” jedhamanii fi “narwhale” jedhaman jiru. Ani nama bareedina hin qabne fakkaachuu hin barbaadu, garuu qurxummiin galaanaa ijaan hin mulʼanne kun baayʼee facaasaa qaamni isaa irraa cite fakkaata; akkasumas, waa’ee narwhale, ilaalchi tokko tokko nama ajaa’ibsiisuuf gahaadha, jaarraa kudha sagalffaa kana keessatti hippogriff akkasii ummata sammuu qabu kamiyyuu ijoollee ijoollee mana barumsaa kamiyyuu irratti dhugaa ta’ee palmed ta’uu danda’a.

Achiis, ammas, bara 1825tti, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, ogeessa uumamaa guddaa, kitaaba saayinsii sirna qurxummii galaanaa maxxansee, fakkiiwwan gosa adda addaa Leviathan hedduun keessatti argamu. Kun hundi sirrii miti qofa osoo hin taane, suuraan qurxummii Mysticetus ykn Greenland (jechuunis, Right whale), Scoresby illee, namni yeroo dheeraaf muuxannoo gosa sana tuquu qabu, uumamaan gita isaa akka hin qabne labsa.

Garuu daldala dogoggora kana hundaaf cap-sheaf kaa'uun saayinsii Frederick Cuvier, obboleessa Baron beekamaa sanaaf qofa ture. Bara 1836tti kitaaba Natural History of Whales jedhu kan maxxansee yoo ta'u, suuraa kana keessatti suuraa Sperm Whale jedhee waamu kenneera. Suuraa sana Nantucketer kamiif iyyuu agarsiisuu kee dura, Nantucket irraa duubatti deebi'uu gabaabduu kee qopheessuun kee gaarii ture. Jecha tokkoon yoo ilaalle, Ispeerm Whale kan Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale osoo hin taane, squash dha. Dhugaadha, faayidaa imala qurxummii galaanaa adamsuu argatee hin beeku (namoonni akkasii yeroo muraasa qofa qabaatu), garuu suuraa sana eessaa akka argate eenyutu beekuu dandaʼa? Tarii akka nama saayinsii isa duraa damee walfakkaatuun, Desmarest, ulfa isa dhugaa keessaa tokko argateetti argate ta’a; jechuunis fakkii Chaayinaa irraa. Akkasumas Chaayinoonni sun qalama sanaan gurbaa lubbuu qaban akkamii akka ta'an, kooppiiwwanii fi saawusaroonni quuqama qaban hedduun nutti himu.

Waa'ee qurxummii warra mallattoo dibu daandii irratti suuqii daldaltoota zayitaa irratti fannifamee mul'atu yoo ilaalle maaltu isaan jedhama? Isaanis akka waliigalaatti Riichaard 3ffaadha. qurxummii galaanaa, humps dromedary kan qabu, fi baay’ee gara jabeessa; ciree sailor tarts sadii ykn afur irratti nyaachuu, jechuunis bidiruuwwan qurxummii galaanaa kan namoota galaanaatiin guutaman: qaamni isaanii galaana dhiigaa fi halluu diimaa keessatti kan dhangala’u.

Garuu dogoggorri dachaa kunniin qurxummii galaanaa fakkeessuun hundumaafuu akkas baay'ee nama hin ajaa'ibu. Ilaalcha keessa galchuu! Fakkiiwwan saayinsii irra caalaan isaanii qurxummii qabamanii turan irraa kan fudhatamanidha; kunis akkuma fakkiin doonii caccabde, dugda isaa cabee, bineensa kabajamaa sana of tuulummaa qaama isaa isa hin ciccitamne hundumaa keessatti sirritti bakka bu’u sirrii dha. Elemoowwan dheerina isaanii guutuuf dhaabbatanis, Lewwiyaatan lubbuun jiru suuraa isaatiif hanga ammaatti haqaan of yaa'ee hin beeku. Whale lubbuun jiru, ulfinaa fi hiika isaa guutuudhaan, galaana irratti bishaan tilmaamamuu hin dandeenye keessatti qofa kan mul’atudha; akkasumas bishaan keessaa harki guddaan isaa akka doonii sarara waraanaa furguggifameetti ija namaa jalaa ba’a; elementii sana keessaas namni du’aa qaamaan qilleensa keessa isa ol kaasuun, akka dhiita’uu fi dambalii isaa isa jabaa hundumaa eeguuf, bara baraan waan hin danda’amnedha. Akkasumas, garaagarummaa kontooraa dargaggeessa qurxummii xuuxuu fi Leviyaataan Pilaatooniyaa guutummaatti guddate gidduu jiru baay’ee tilmaamamuu danda’u dubbachuu dhiisii; taʼus, dargaggoota qurxummii galaanaa xuuxan kanneen gara kutaa doonii tokkootti ol kaafaman sana keessaa tokko illee, yeroo sanatti boca isaa isa adda taʼe, kan akka eel fakkaatu, kan limbered, garaa garaa waan taʼeef, ibsi isaa inni sirrii taʼe seexanni ofii isaatii qabachuu hin dandeenye.

Garuu fakkeessuun ni danda'ama, lafee qullaa qurxummii galaanaa hidhamee jiru irraa, akeekkachiisni sirrii bifa isaa isa dhugaa tuquun argamuu danda'a. Gonkumaa miti. Sababni isaas, waa’ee Leewiyaatan kanaa wantoota caalaatti nama hawwatan keessaa tokko, lafeen isaa boca waliigalaa isaa ilaalchisee yaada baay’ee xiqqaa kennuu isaati. Lafeen Jeremy Bentham, kan mana kitaabaa raawwattoota isaa keessaa tokkoo keessatti kandelabraaf fannifame, yaada burly-browed utilitarian old gentleman sirritti kan dabarsu ta’us, amala dhuunfaa Jeremy adda duree biroo hundumaa wajjin; taʼus lafeewwan leeviyaataan kamiyyuu irraa wanti gosa kanaa hubachuun hin dandaʼamu ture. Dhugaa dubbachuuf, akkuma Hunter guddichi jedhu, lafeen qurxummii galaanaa qofti bineensa guutummaatti invastimantii fi padded wajjin walitti dhufeenya wal fakkaatu qaba, akkuma ilbiisonni chrysalis isa akkasitti geengoo ta’ee marsee jiruu wajjin qabu. Adda ta’uun kun mataa keessatti haala nama dinqisiisuun kan mul’atu yoo ta’u, kutaa kitaaba kanaa tokko tokko keessatti akka tasaa kan agarsiifamu ta’a. Akkasumas baay’ee nama ajaa’ibuun fincaan cinaa keessatti kan mul’atu yoo ta’u, lafeen isaa lafee harka namaa sirriitti jechuun ni danda’ama, qubbee harkaa qofa hir’isuun. Finni kun quba lafee idilee afur qaba, quba agarsiisaa, gidduu, ring fi quba xiqqaa. Garuu kun hundinuu akkuma quba namaa haguuggii namtolchee keessatti haguuggii foon isaanii keessatti dhaabbataadhaan kan buufaman. Gaaf tokko qoosaa kan taʼe Stubb, “Of eeggannoo malee qurxummii galaanaa yeroo tokko tokko nu tajaajiluu dandaʼus, dhuguma mittens malee nu qaba jedhamee gonkumaa hin dandaʼamu” jedheera.

Sababoota kana hundumaaf, egaa, karaa kamiinuu yoo ilaaltan, Lewiyaataniin guddaan uumama tokko addunyaa kanarra jiru isa hanga isa dhumaatti osoo hin dibamin hafuu qabu ta’uu isaa xumuruu qabdu. Dhugaadha, suuraan tokko mallattoo sana isa kaan caalaa baayʼee dhihoo taʼee rukutuu dandaʼa, garuu tokkollee sadarkaa baayʼee guddaa taʼeen sirriitti rukutuu hin dandaʼu. Kanaafuu, karaan lafa irraa qurxummiin galaanaa sun dhuguma maal akka fakkaatu sirriitti baruuf gargaaru hin jiru. Akkasumas haala tokkichi yaada obsa danda'u illee kontooraa isaa jiraataa irraa argachuu dandeessu, ofii keetii whaling deemuun; garuu akkas gochuudhaan bara baraan iddoo ibiddaa taʼuu fi isa bira liqimfamuuf balaa xinnoo miti. Kanaaf, fedhii beekuuf qabdu keessatti Leviyaatan kana tuquu kee keessatti garmalee fastidious ta'uu dhiisuun kee gaarii natti fakkaata.





BOQONNAA 56. Suuraalee Dogoggora Xiqqoo Qaban Kan Qurxummii Qurxummii, fi Suuraalee Dhugaa Mul’ata Qurxummii Qurxummii.

Suuraalee hamaa qurxummii galaanaa wajjin walqabatee, ani asitti seenaa isaan amma iyyuu hamaa ta’an kanneen kitaabota tokko tokko, durii fi ammayyaa, keessumaa Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, fi kkf Garuu ani dhimma sana bira darba.

Yaadannoowwan Ispeerm Wheel guddaa kanaa maxxanfaman afur qofan beeka; Kan Kolnet, kan Haggiins, kan Firdeeriik Kuuviyer fi kan Biil. Boqonnaa darbe keessatti Colnett fi Cuvier eerameera. Kan Huggins kan isaanii caalaa baayyee gaarii dha; garuu, carraa guddaadhaan, kan Beale isa hunda caaludha. Fakkiiwwan Beale waa'ee qurxummii galaanaa kanaa hundi gaarii dha, fakkii giddu galeessaa suuraa qurxummii galaanaa sadii ilaalcha adda addaa qaban irraa kan hafe, boqonnaa isaa isa lammaffaa kan xumure. Fuuldurri isaa, bidiruuwwan Sperm Whales haleelan, shakkii siiviilii dhiirota paarlaamaa tokko tokkoo kakaasuuf kan shallagame ta’us, bu’aa waliigalaa isaatiin haala dinqisiisaa ta’een sirrii fi jireenya kan fakkaatudha. Fakkiiwwan Sperm Whale J. Ross Browne keessatti mul’atan tokko tokko kontooraa isaaniitiin baay’ee sirrii dha; garuu haala gaddisiisaa ta’een bocamaniiru. Sun garuu badii isaa miti.

Of the Right Whale, fakkiiwwan outline gaariin Scoresby keessatti argamu; garuu yaada barbaadamu dabarsuudhaaf madaallii garmalee xiqqaa ta’een kan kaafamaniidha. Suuraa tokko qofa kan mul’ata qurxummii galaanaa adamsuu qaba, kunis hanqina gaddisiisaa dha, sababiin isaas suuraa akkasii qofaan, yeroo tasumaa akka gaariitti hojjetame, waan akka yaada dhugaa qurxummii lubbuu qabuu akka adamsitoonni isaa lubbuun jiran arganitti argachuu dandeessa.

Garuu, walumaa galatti hundumaaf yoo fudhatame, fagootti kan gaarii ta’e, bal’inaan tokko tokkoon sirrii ta’uu baatus, dhiheessiin qurxummii galaanaa fi mul’ata qurxummii galaanaa bakka kamittiyyuu argamuu danda’u, bocawwan gurguddoo Faransaayii lama, akka gaariitti kan raawwataman, fi fakkiiwwan Garnery tokkoon kan fudhatamanidha. Isaanis wal duraa duubaan haleellaa Ispeermii fi Qurxummii Mirgaa irratti raawwatamu bakka bu’u. Boca jalqabaa keessatti Sperm Whale kabajamaan tokko humna guutuudhaan kan agarsiifame yoo ta’u, gadi fageenya galaanaa irraa bidiruu jalatti reefuu ka’ee, qilleensa irratti ol ka’ee dugda isaa irratti caccabaa suukaneessaa pilaankii iddoo ibiddaan hojjetame sanaa baatee jira. Fuulli bidiruu gartokkoon kan hin cabne yoo ta’u, lafee dugdaa bineensichaa irratti madaaluudhaan qofa kan harkifame dha; akkasumas, yeroo tokko kan hin shallagamne sanaaf, nama doonii oofu tokko, walakkaan isaa dhangala’aa boba’aa aarii guddaa qabuun kan haguugamee fi akka waan cirracha irraa dhufeetti utaaluu jiru argita. Gochi waan hundumaa ajaa'iba gaarii fi dhugaa dha. Sararri-tuubii walakkaa duwwaa ta’e galaana adii ta’e irratti yaa’a; utubaawwan mukaa harpoons dhangala’an obliquely keessa bob; mataan garee bishaan daakuu ibsa sodaa faallaa taʼeen waaʼee qurxummii galaanaa sanaa facaʼanii jiru; fageenya obomboleettii gurraacha keessatti dooniin sun bakka sanatti gadi bu’aa osoo jirtuu. Bal’ina anatoomii qurxummii galaanaa kanaa irratti badiin guddaan argamu danda’a, garuu sun haa darbu; jireenya kootiif, isa gaarii akkasii kaasuun waanan hin dandeenyeef.

Boca lammaffaa keessatti, bidiruun kun gocha cinaacha barnacled kan Right Whale guddaa fiigu, kan akka dhagaa-slide mossy tokko tokkoo galaana keessatti rolls, kan barnacled flank cinatti kaasuun keessa jirti. Jeetiin isaa diriiree, guutuu fi akka sooftii gurraacha; akkasitti, aarri simintoo keessatti baay’ate irraa, garaacha guddaa armaan gadii keessatti irbaata jajjaboo bilcheessuun jiraachuu qaba jettee yaadda. Simbirroonni galaanaa, qamadii xixiqqoo, qurxummii qolaafi karameellaa galaanaa fi maccaroni kan biroo, kan Right Whale yeroo tokko tokko dugda isaa isa dhukkuba kana qabu irratti baattutti qama'aa jiru. Yeroo kana hundumaas leeviyaataniin funyaan furdaa qabu sun gadi fageenya keessa fiigaa jira, daakuun adii jeequmsaa toonii hedduu of duuba dhiisee, bidiruun xiqqoo sun akka doonii danfaa galaanaatti dhihoo taʼee fi dhiitaʼa sana keessatti akka raafamu godha. Kanaaf, fuulduraan hundi jeequmsa cimaa dha; garuu duubaan, faallaa aartii dinqisiisaa ta’een, sadarkaan geejjibaa galaana tasgabbaa’ee, doonii humna hin qabne kan istaarjii hin qabne kan gadi bu’ee fi tuuta qurxummii galaanaa du’ee hin sochoone, masaraa mo’amee, alaabaa qabachuu dadhabbiidhaan qurxummii galaanaa irraa fannifamee jira. utubaa boolla bishaan itti naqanii (spout-hole) isaa keessa galfame.

Garnery the painter eenyu akka ta'e, ykn ture, hin beeku. Garuu jireenyi koo isaaf yookaan qabatamaan dhimma isaa kan beeku ture, yookaan immoo haala ajaa'ibaatiin nama qurxummii galaanaa muuxannoo qabu tokko tokkoon barsiisa ture. Faranjoonni gocha dibudhaaf ijoollee dhiiraati. Deemi fakkiiwwan Awurooppaa hunda ilaali, galma jeequmsa jireenyaa fi hafuura baafannaa akkasii kanfaasi irratti eessatti argatta, akka galma injifannoo Versailles sana keessatti; bakka namni ilaalu karaa isaa, pell-mell, lola gurguddaa Faransaay walitti aanan keessaa lolu; bakka billaan hundinuu ibsaa Ifa Kaabaa fakkaatu, mootonniifi Emperors hidhatee walduraa duubaan akka himannaa seentaroota gonfoo godhataniitti fiigan? Guutummaatti galma sana keessatti bakka argachuuf kan hin malle miti, kunniin lola galaanaa Garnery.

Gahumsi uumamaa Faransaayiin fakkii wantootaa qabachuuf qaban fakkiiwwanii fi boca akkamii akka qaban mul’ata qurxummii galaanaa isaanii irratti adda ta’ee kan mul’atu fakkaata. Thus glistening for a moment, as slowly it subsided, and sank. Then once more arose, and silently gleamed. It seemed not a whale; and yet is this Moby Dick? thought Daggoo. Again the phantom went down, but on re-appearing once more, with a stiletto-like cry that startled every man from his nod, the negro yelled out—“There! there again! there she breaches! right ahead! The White Whale, the White Whale!”

Upon this, the seamen rushed to the yard-arms, as in swarming-time the bees rush to the boughs. Bare-headed in the sultry sun, Ahab stood on the bowsprit, and with one hand pushed far behind in readiness to wave his orders to the helmsman, cast his eager glance in the direction indicated aloft by the outstretched motionless arm of Daggoo.

Whether the flitting attendance of the one still and solitary jet had gradually worked upon Ahab, so that he was now prepared to connect the ideas of mildness and repose with the first sight of the particular whale he pursued; however this was, or whether his eagerness betrayed him; whichever way it might have been, no sooner did he distinctly perceive the white mass, than with a quick intensity he instantly gave orders for lowering.

The four boats were soon on the water; Ahab's in advance, and all swiftly pulling towards their prey. Soon it went down, and while, with oars suspended, we were awaiting its reappearance, lo! in the same spot where it sank, once more it slowly rose. Almost forgetting for the moment all thoughts of Moby Dick, we now gazed at the most wondrous phenomenon which the secret seas have hitherto revealed to mankind. A vast pulpy mass, furlongs in length and breadth, of a glancing cream-colour, lay floating on the water, innumerable long arms radiating from its centre, and curling and twisting like a nest of anacondas, as if blindly to clutch at any hapless object within reach. No perceptible face or front did it have; no conceivable token of either sensation or instinct; but undulated there on the billows, an unearthly, formless, chance-like apparition of life.

As with a low sucking sound it slowly disappeared again, Starbuck still gazing at the agitated waters where it had sunk, with a wild voice exclaimed—“Almost rather had I seen Moby Dick and fought him, than to have seen thee, thou white ghost!”

“What was it, Sir?” said Flask.

“The great live squid, which, they say, few whale-ships ever beheld, and returned to their ports to tell of it.”

But Ahab said nothing; turning his boat, he sailed back to the vessel; the rest as silently following.

Whatever superstitions the sperm whalemen in general have connected with the sight of this object, certain it is, that a glimpse of it being so very unusual, that circumstance has gone far to invest it with portentousness. So rarely is it beheld, that though one and all of them declare it to be the largest animated thing in the ocean, yet very few of them have any but the most vague ideas concerning its true nature and form; notwithstanding, they believe it to furnish to the sperm whale his only food. For though other species of whales find their food above water, and may be seen by man in the act of feeding, the spermaceti whale obtains his whole food in unknown zones below the surface; and only by inference is it that any one can tell of what, precisely, that food consists. At times, when closely pursued, he will disgorge what are supposed to be the detached arms of the squid; some of them thus exhibited exceeding twenty and thirty feet in length. They fancy that the monster to which these arms belonged ordinarily clings by them to the bed of the ocean; and that the sperm whale, unlike other species, is supplied with teeth in order to attack and tear it.

There seems some ground to imagine that the great Kraken of Bishop Pontoppodan may ultimately resolve itself into Squid. The manner in which the Bishop describes it, as alternately rising and sinking, with some other particulars he narrates, in all this the two correspond. But much abatement is necessary with respect to the incredible bulk he assigns it.

By some naturalists who have vaguely heard rumors of the mysterious creature, here spoken of, it is included among the class of cuttle-fish, to which, indeed, in certain external respects it would seem to belong, but only as the Anak of the tribe.





CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
