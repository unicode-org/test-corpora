55 JAQTHAPI.Ballenanakan Monstruosas Jallunakapata.

Nayax ere largo pintaña jumataki ukhamarak maynix jan lonampi, kunatix chiqpach forma de la ballena ukhama chiqpachan uñsti ballena jaqin nayraparu kunapachatix jupan pachpa absoluto cuerpopanx ballenax amarrado lado ballena-barco ukhamat jupax chiqaparuw ukanx kayump takt’asispa. Ukhamasti, nayrax uka yatiñ munir imaginario jamuqanak uñt’ayañax wali askïspawa, uka jamuqanakax jichhürunakkamas jan axsarasaw uraqini jaqin iyawsäwiparux ch’amanchaski. Aka tuqit akapachar chiqapar uñjañax pachaw purini, ballena ukan ukham jamuqanak uñacht’ayasa.

Inas taqe uka dibujonakan kʼarinak lurañan nayrïr uñstatapajj hinduismo, egipcio ukat griegonakan nayra esculturanakapan jikjjatasispa. Uka inventario ukampis jan escrupulos uka pachanakatpachaw templonakan mármol paneles ukanakan, estatuanakan pedestales ukanakan ukat escudonakan, medallón, copa ukat qullqinakan delfín ukarux Saladín ukar uñtasit cadena armadura balanzanakampi dibujat uñjasiwayi, ukat casco p’iqir uñtasita San Jorge markanwa; uka pachatpachaw mä pachpa licenciax utjawayxi, janiw ballena ukan jilpach uñt’at jamuqanak uñacht’ayañakikiti, jan ukasti walja cientificonakan jupat uñacht’ayatanakapansa.

Jichhax taqi kunat sipansa, juk’amp nayra jichhakamax utjki uka jamuqax kunjamakitix ballena ukan uñt’atawa, ukax Elephanta ukan uñt’at caverna-pagoda ukan jikxatasi, India markan. Brahmanes jupanakax sapxiwa, uka nayra pagoda ukan niya jan tukuskir esculturanakanx taqi oficios ukat arknaqawinaka, jaqin sapa avocacionapax amuyt’atäki ukax nayratpach uñacht’ayatänwa, janïr maynis chiqpachan uñstkipana. Ukhamajj janiw muspharkañäkiti, mä jukʼa tiemponjja, ballenanak katoqañ jachʼa profesionasajj ukan chʼamaktʼayatäñapänwa. Ballena hindú ukax uñt’atawa, pirqax mä sapa departamento ukanw uñsti, Vishnu ukan jaqir tukutapat uñacht’ayata, leviatán ukham uñt’ata, yatxatatanakax Matse Avatar ukham uñt’atawa. Ukampis aka esculturax chikat jaqi ukat chikat ballena ukhamäkchisa, ukhamat qhipa jaqin chuymap churañatakiki, ukampis uka jiskʼa tʼaqapax taqpach jan walipuniwa. Ukax mä anaconda sat animalan chʼullqhi chuymapar uñtasitawa, chiqpach ballenan jachʼa chʼakhanakapan jachʼa palmeranakapat sipansa.

Ukampis nayra Galerías ukar sarapxam, ukat jichhax mä jach’a cristiano pintoran aka challwat jamuqap uñakipt’apxam; kunatix jupax janiw juk’amp sum atipt’kiti antediluviano hindoo ukanakat sipansa. Guido chachan jamuqapawa, Perseo chachan Andrómeda sat chachar lamar qotankir monstruo jan ukajj ballena sat animalat qhespiyatapa. ¿Kawkitsa Guidox ukham jan uñt’at jaqin modelop apsuwaypacha? Janirakiw Hogarth chachajj pachpa “Perseus Descending” sat libropan pachpa escena pintasajj mä jukʼa jukʼamp sum uñachtʼaykiti. Uka Hogarthian monstruon jachʼa corpulenciapajj uma patjjan ondulado ukhamawa, jukʼakiw mä pulgada uma apsu. Ukax qhipäxanx mä kasta howdah ukaniwa, ukat jach’a lakapax ch’uxña ch’akhanakaniwa, ukaruw olas ukanakax liwxatapxi, ukax Támesis jawir uma tuqi Torre ukar mantañ Traidores Punkutakiw apasispa. Ukatxa, nayra Sibbald escocés ukankir ballenas Prodromus ukanakaw utjaraki, ukat Jonah sat ballenas ukanakaw utjaraki, kunjamtï nayra Biblianakan imprimitanakapan ukat nayra imprimadoranakan khuchhuratanakapan uñachtʼayaski ukhama. ¿Kunsa ukanak sasispa? Libronak encuadernadoran ballenapajj mä uva chʼaphir uñtataw mä ancla saraqir estocapar muyuntki uka toqetjja, —kunjamtï nayra ukat machaq walja libronakan qhepäjjanakapansa ukat pʼeqeñchäwinakansa sellat ukat qorimpi uchat ukhama— ukajj wali suma uñnaqtʼanwa, ukampis qʼoma fabuloso ukhamawa, imitata, nayax amuyta, antiguo jarrón ukan uñtasit figuranakapat apst’ata. Taqi chiqans delfín sasin sutichatäkchisa, ukhampachasa, aka libronak encuadernadoran challwaparux ballena ukar yantʼañaw sasaw sista; kunattix uka aparatox nayrïr kuti uñstkäna ukhaxa, ukham amtatarakïnwa. Ukax mä nayra italiano yatiyiriw uñt’ayäna, kawkhantix niya 15n pacha maranakanx uñt’ayasïna, Yatiqañ Revival ukan pachana; ukat uka tiemponjja, ukat mä jukʼa tiempokamas delfín sat animalanakajj Leviatán sat animalan mä kasta animaläkaspas ukhamwa jaqenakajj amuyapjjäna.

Yaqhip nayra libronakan viñetas ukat yaqha k’achacht’äwinakanx awisax ballena ukarux wali yatxatañ munir llamkt’awinakampiw jikisipxäta, kawkhantix taqi kasta esputos, jets d’eau, fuentes calientes ukat thaya, Saratoga ukat Baden-Baden, ukanakax jupan burbujas ukanakaw jutapxi jan qarjata p’iqi. “Avance del aprendizaje” sat libron nayrïr edicionapan pʼeqeñchäwipanjja, yaqhep yatiñ munir ballenanakaruw jikjjatäta.

Ukampis taqi uka jan profesionaljam yantʼanak jaytasaxa, uka leviatán sat fotonak uñakiptʼañäni, uka fotonakax wali amuytʼasir, cientificonakan uñachtʼäwinakapawa sasaw sapxi, ukax yatipki ukanakan luratawa. Nayra Harris chachan viajes ukanakat apthapitapanx mä qawqha placas de ballenas ukanakaw utji, ukax holandés libro de viajes ukan apsutawa, 1671 maranwa, ukax akham sutinchatawa “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson of Friesland, master”. Uka placanakat maynïrinjja, ballenanakajj jachʼa balsa tronconakar uñtasitaw hielo islanak taypin ikiskir uñachtʼayasi, ukat janqʼo osonakaw jakaskir qhepäjjapat tʼijtʼapjje. Yaqha placanxa, uka muspharkañ pantjasiwix ballena ukar uñtasit perpendicular flukes ukamp luratawa.

Ukat mayampxa, mä jach’a quarto ukaw utjaraki, mä Capitán Colnett, inglés marina ukan Capitán de Postes ukan qillqata, ukax akham sutinchatawa “A Viaje round Cape Horn into the South Seas, for the purpose of extending the Spermaceti Whale Fisheries”. Aka libronjja, mä bosquejow uñsti, ukajj “Mä ballena Physeter jan ukajj Spermaceti sat animalan fotopawa, ukajj México markan qota lakan jiwayat jaqetwa, agosto, 1793 maran jiwayat uñjasïna, ukat cubiertaruw liwjjatasïna” sasaw sapjje. Capitanajj marineronakapan askipatakiw uka cheqpach foto apsuñapatak yanaptʼäna, janiw pächaskti. Uka toqet mä jukʼa arsuñatakejja, mä nayranïkaspas ukham sañ munta, uka nayrajj mä ballena espermatozoide sat animalar phoqantat uñjasispa ukhajja, uka ballenan nayrapajj mä arco ventanaruw tukuyaspa, ukajj niya phisqa metronak largoruw tukuyaspa. ¡Ay, jan axsart’ir capitan, ¿kunatsa jan Jonasarux uka nayrat uñch’ukiñ churapxista!

Janirakiw Historia Natural sat libron jukʼamp concienciaparjam apthapitanakajj wayn tawaqonakan ukat llampʼu chuyman jaqenakan askipatakjamäkiti, janiw pachpa jan wali pantjasitanakatsa qhespiyatäpkiti. Uka wali uñtʼat qellqat “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature” uñxattʼañäni. Londres markan 1807 maran jiskʼa edición ukanjja, mä “ballena” ukat “narwhale” satäkis uka placanakaw utji. Janiw jan suma uñnaqtʼanïñ munkti, ukampis aka jan wali ballenajj mä amputat cerda ukar uñtasitawa; ukat, narwhale tuqitxa, mä juk’a uñakipañax muspharkañawa, aka tunka llätunk patak maranakanx ukham hipogrifox chiqpachapuniw palmed for any intelligent public of school boys.

Ukatxa, mayampi, 1825 maranxa, Bernard Germain, conde de Lacépède, jach’a naturalista, mä libro cientifico sistematizado de ballenas uñt’ayäna, ukanxa walja fotonakawa utji kunaymana kasta Leviatán ukanakata. Taqi ukanakax janiw jan walïkiti, jan ukasti Mysticetus jan ukax Groenlandia ballena (mä arunxa, Derecha ballena) ukan jamuqapawa, Scoresby, uka kasta uywar llamkt’añ tuqit nayratpach yatxatat jaqis ukhamarakiw uñt’ayi, janiw naturaleza ukan uñtasitapax utjkiti.

Ukampis taqi uka pantjasir negocionakar gorra-sheaf ukar uchañax Frederick Cuvier cientificotakikïnwa, jupax wali uñt’at Barón jilapan jilapawa. 1836 maranjja, Historia Natural de los Ballenas sat qellqat apsüna, ukanjja, Ballena Esperma sat animalan mä dibujopaw kuntï siskän uk uñachtʼayäna. Janïr uka jamuq kawkir Nantucketer ukar uñacht’ayañkamax, Nantucket markat sumario retiro ukar wakicht’añax wali askiwa. Mä arunxa, Frederick Cuvier jupan Ballena Esperma ukax janiw Ballena Esperma ukhamäkiti, jan ukasti mä calabaza ukhamawa. Chiqansa, janipuniw ballenanak katur sarañax askinak jikxatkänti (ukham jaqinakax jukʼakiw jikxatapxi), ukampis kawkitsa uka foto apsuwaypacha, ¿khitis uk yatpacha? Inas jupax uka pachpa yatxatäwin cientificonakan nayrïr Desmarest sat chiqpach abortopat maynïr katuqkchïn ukhama; mä arunxa, chino dibujot apstʼatawa. Ukat kuna kasta jakkir waynanakas lapisampi uka chinonakax, walja queer copas ukat platillos ukanakaw yatiyapxistu.

Letrero pintirinakan ballenanakapatjja, aceite aljirinakan tiendanakapan warktʼataw callenakan uñjasi, ¿kunsa jupanakat sasini? Jupanakax jilpachx Richard III satawa. ballenas, dromedarias humps ukanakampi, ukat wali sallqa; kimsa jan ukax pusi tarta marinero ukan desayunaña, mä arunxa, barcos ballenas ukanakax marineros ukanakamp phuqhantatänwa: deformidades ukanakax wila qutanakan liwxatasi ukat ch’uxña pintura.

Ukampis uka ballena uñachtʼayañan walja pantjasitanakajj janiw sinti muspharkañäkiti, taqe kunat sipansa. Uñjaña! Jilapart cientificonakan dibujonakapax ch’amakt’at challwanakat apsutawa; ukat ukanakxa niya ukhamarakiwa, kunjamtï mä tʼunjatäki uka barcon dibujopax chiqapa, qhipäxa pʼakjata, ukax pachpa jachʼa uywarux chiqaparuw uñachtʼayaspa, cascopampi ukat espaldanakapamp jan chʼiyjat jachʼa jachʼa tukutapampi. Elefantenakajj taqpach chʼamapatak saytʼapkchejja, jakaskir Leviatán chachajj janipuniw retratopatak cheqaparjam jalnaqkiti. Jakir ballenax, taqpach jach’a jach’a tukutapata ukhamarak wali wakiskirïtapata, qutan jan amuyt’kay uma taypin uñjañakiw; ukat uma taypinjja, jachʼa partepajj janiw uñjatäkiti, mä barcor uñtataw nuwasiñ barcojj lanzatäki ukhama; ukat uka elementotjja, wiñayatakiw jan jiwir jaqejj janchit thayar liwjjatkaspati, ukhamat taqe chʼamani chʼakhanakapasa, ondulacionanakapasa jan chhaqhañapataki. Ukat, janiw mä ballena chuchuñamp mä Leviatán platoniompi phuqtʼat jilir contorno mayjtʼäwipat parlañax wakiskiti; ukampis mä barcon cubiertapar liwxatatäki uka ballena chuchuñat maynïrix ukhamarakiw ukapachax jan uñtʼat, anguila ukham, limbered, kunayman uñnaqtʼanïtapa, ukat chiqpach uñnaqapax janiw saxra pachpa katjañjamäkänti.

Ukampis inas amuyaschispa, uka ballena ukan qʼala jan isin esqueletopatjja, cheqpach uñnaqap llamktʼasajj cheqa yatichäwinakaw apsusispa. Janiw kunatsa. Kunattix aka Leviatán tuqit jukʼamp muspharkañawa, esqueletopax taqpach uñnaqapat jukʼak amuytʼayi. Jeremy Bentham chachan esqueletopax mä ejecutoran bibliotecapan candelabrotak warkt’atäkchisa, chiqaparuw mä utilitario nayra señor chachan amuyuparux puriyi, Jeremy chachan taqi yaqha nayrïr uñacht’äwinakapampi; ukampis janiw kuna leviatán articulado chʼakhanakapatsa ukham uñtʼatäkänti. Chiqansa, kunjamtï jachʼa Hunter sat jaqix siskixa, ballena sat animalan esqueletopakiw taqpach qullqichatäki ukat acolchado animalampixa, kunjamtï uka laqʼux crisalis sat animalampix wali muyuntki ukhama. Uka peculiaridad ukax p’iqinx wali muspharkañ uñacht’ayatawa, kunjamatix aka libron yaqhip chiqanakanx ina ch’usat uñacht’ayasini. Ukhamaraki wali muspharkañawa lado aleta ukanxa uñacht’ayatarakiwa, ch’akhanakapaxa niya chiqapawa jaqina ampara ch’akhanakaparu jaysaña, ukatxa ampar luk’ana ch’akhanakakiwa apsutaraki. Aka aletax pusi regular ch’akha-dedoniwa, índice, chika, anillo ukat jisk’a dedo. Ukampis taqe ukanakajj wiñayatakiw aycha llawuntatapan qheparapjje, kunjamtï jaqen amparanakapajj mä artificial isimp isthaptʼatäki ukhama. Mä urux larusisaw Stubb sat chachax akham säna: “Ukampis jan amuytʼasisaw ballenax awisax luqtaspa, janipuniw chiqpachapunix jan guantesni apnaqapxitu sañjamakiti” sasa.

Ukhamasti, taqi ukanak uñjasaxa, kunayman tuqitwa uñakiptʼasma, jachʼa Leviatán ukax akapachan mä sapa luratapaw qhipakama jan pintat qhiparañapa sasaw amuyañama. Chiqansa, mä retratox yaqha retratot sipansa jukʼamp jakʼaruw uka markar chʼalltʼaspa, ukampis janiw maynis uka retratox wali sum chiqapar chʼalltʼkaspati. Ukhamasti, ballenax kunjamäpachas uk sum yatxatañatakix janiw aka Uraqin utjkaspati. Ukat ukakipkarakiw modalidad ukanx mä tolerable amuyt’awis contorno vivo ukat apsuñjamawa, ukax juma pachpaw mä ballena ukar sarañama; ukampis ukham lurasax wiñayatak estufampi ukat jupamp chhaqhayasiñatakix janiw mä jiskʼa jan waltʼäwis utjkiti. Ukhamasti, nayatakix wali askiwa aka Leviatán llamkt’añ munañamatakix jan sinti ch’amanchasiñamataki.





56 JAQTHAPI.Ballenas ukanakan jan sinti pantjasir jamuqanak uñacht’ayatapata, ukat Ballenas ukan chiqpach jamuqanak uñacht’ayatapata.

Ballenanakan monstruoso jamuqanakamp chikt’ataxa, nayax wali yant’ataw akanx uka juk’amp monstruoso sarnaqäwinakar mantañataki, ukax yaqhip pankanakan jikxatasi, nayra pachanakan ukhamarak jichha pachanakan, juk’ampirus Plinio, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, etc. Ukampis nayax uka tuqitx pasawaytwa.

Jachʼa Ballena Esperma sat animalan pusi bosquejonak apsutäki ukanak yatta; Colnett, Huggins, Frederick Cuvier ukat Beale jupanakan luratanakapa. Nayra jaljanxa Colnett ukat Cuvier sat jaqinakat parltʼasiwayi. Huggins chachan utapax jupanakat sipanx juk’amp askiwa; ukampis, jach’a jan walt’awinakampixa, Beale-n utapax wali askiwa. Taqi Beale chachan aka ballena ukan dibujonakapax wali askiwa, jan ukax chika taypinkir chimpux kimsa ballenas ukanakan jamuqapawa kunayman amuyuni, payïr jaljaparux tukuyi. Frontispiciopax, Ballenas Esperma ukar atacañ barconaka, yaqhip salón chachanakan escepticismo civil ukar ch’amanchañatakix jakthapitakïkchisa, wali muspharkañ chiqawa ukat jakäwiparjamaw efecto general ukanx utji. J. Ross Browne ukan yaqhip Ballena Esperma ukan dibujonakapax contorno ukanx wali chiqapawa; ukampis jan wali grabadonakäpxiwa. Ukampis ukajj janiw jupan juchapäkiti.

Ballena derecha ukanx wali suma contorno uñacht’äwinakax Scoresby ukan uñacht’ayatawa; ukampis wali jiskʼa escalaruw dibujatäpjje, ukhamatwa mä munat amuyunakajj uñachtʼayasi. Jupax mä jamuqakiw ballenas ukanakar katuntañ escenanakan utji, ukat akax mä llaki jan walt’awiwa, kunatix ukham jamuqanak tuqikiw, kunapachatix taqpach suma luratäki ukhax kunayman chiqpach amuyt’awinak jakañ ballena ukar uñtasit apsuñjama, kunjamatix jakkir uywa katurinakapax uñjapki ukhama.

Ukampis, taqpachan apsutaxa, juk’amp suma, yaqhip detalles ukanx janiw juk’amp chiqapäkiti, ballenas ukat ballenas ukanakar katuntañ escenanak uñacht’ayañax kawkhans jikxatasi, pä jach’a grabados francés ukanakawa, wali suma luratawa, ukat mä Garnery ukan pinturanakapat apsutawa. Sapa maynix Espermatozoide ukat Ballena Derecha ukanakar ataques uñacht’ayapxi. Nayrïr grabado ukanx mä noble Ballena Esperma ukaw taqpach jach’a ch’amampi uñacht’ayata, lamar quta manqhat bote manqhanx jichhakiw sartasi, ukat estufa tablanakan wali muspharkañ ch’usa ch’usapxa qhipäxaruw thaya taypin apt’ata. Boten proapax mä juk’a jan p’akjatawa, ukat monstruon columna vertebral ukar equilibrar ukhamakiw dibujata; ukat uka proa ukan sayt’atäsaxa, uka mä sapa jan jakt’kay pachan qhant’atapata, mä remo jaqiruw uñjta, chikat ballenan colerat t’amt’ir chhullunkhayapampi ch’uqt’ata, ukat mä ch’allat jaltkaspas ukhama. Taqi kunan lurawipax muspharkañ aski ukat chiqawa. Chika ch’usat linea-tub ukax janq’u qutanw jalnaqt’i; uka wartat arpón ukanakan lawat lurat postenakapajj oblicuamente ukan bob; nadadoranakan pʼeqenakapajj ajjsarañ uñachtʼayasaw ballena toqet chʼeqtʼatäpjje; ukat ch’iyar thaya jayanx barcox uka chiqar sarawayxäna. Inas uka ballenan anatómica tuqit yatxatatanakapanx jachʼa pantjasiw jikxataschispa, ukampis ukax pasañapawa; kunattix nayan jakäwijatakix janiw ukham suma dibujañjamäkänti.

Payïr grabado ukanx barcox mä jach’a t’ijtir Ballena Derecha ukan barnacled flanco ukamp chikaw dibujaski, ukax ch’iyar ch’uxña quqanak qutanx mä qawqha musgo qala-deslizamiento ukham Patagonia acantilados ukanakat liwxati. Jet-anakapax sayt’atawa, phuqt’atawa, ukat ch’iyara hollín ukham uñt’atawa; ukhamat chimenea ukan juk’amp chhullunkhayatapatxa, alaya jach’a ch’akhanakan jan axsart’as manq’a phayt’añax utjañapawa sasaw amuyasma. Lamar qotankir jamachʼinakajj jiskʼa cangrejonaka, concha challwanaka ukat yaqha lamar qotankir miskʼinakaru ukat macarrones ukanakaruw chʼalljjtayasipjje, ukanakjja Ballena Derecha sat animalajj awisajj usuntat qhepäjjaparuw aptʼasi. Ukat taqi uka pachanx thiya laphini leviatán ukax manqha taypin t’ijt’aski, tonelada ch’axwañ janq’u cuajas ukanakaw qhipäxan jaytawayi, ukat jisk’a botex ch’uxña ch’akhanakan ch’allxtayi, kunjamatix mä barcox lamar qutankir barcon pala ruedanakap jak’an katjatäki ukhama. Ukhamatwa, nayrïr chiqanx taqpach jach’a ch’axwawinakaw utji; ukampis qhipäxanxa, admirable contraste artístico ukanxa, mä qutan vidriot lurat nivelapaw samaratäxi, jan chʼamani barcon jan almidonat velanakapax liwxatasinkiwa, ukat mä jiwat ballena ukan inerte masapawa, mä fortaleza conquistada, ukat katuntañ banderapax qʼañu warktʼatawa ballena- poste ukax esputo-p’iyaparuw uchatäna.

Khitis Garnery pintorax, jan ukax khitis utjäna, janiw yatkti. Ukampis nayan jakäwijatakix jupax niyaw yatichäwip sum yatïna, jan ukax muspharkañ yatichataw yaqhip yatxatat ballenanak apnaqirinakan yatichata. Francés jaqinakax acción pintañatakix waynanakawa. Europa markan taqi pintatanakap uñch’ukis sarañamawa, ukat kawkhans mä galería de vida ukat samañ ch’axwañ lona ukan jikxatasma, kunjamatix Versalles markan uka triunfal sala ukanx ukhama; kawkhantix uñjirix thakipan ch’axwaski, pell-mell, Francia markan jach’a ch’axwawinakan jan jaytjasisa; kawkhantix sapa espadax Luz del Norte ukan qhant’kaspas ukhama, ukat maynit maynikam armat reyinakas Emperadores ukanakas t’ijt’apxi, mä carga de centauro coronados ukar uñtasita? Janiw taqpach jan uka galería ukan mä chiqar puriñax wakiskiti, aka quta ch’axwañ-piezas de Garnery.

Francés jaqinakan natural aptitude ukax yänakan pintoresco ukar katuntañatakix peculiar uñacht’ayataw uñjasi, kuna pinturanakas ukat grabados ukanakas ballenas ukanakar katuntañ escenanakapat utji. As with a low sucking sound it slowly disappeared again, Starbuck still gazing at the agitated waters where it had sunk, with a wild voice exclaimed—“Almost rather had I seen Moby Dick and fought him, than to have seen thee, thou white ghost!”

“What was it, Sir?” said Flask.

“The great live squid, which, they say, few whale-ships ever beheld, and returned to their ports to tell of it.”

But Ahab said nothing; turning his boat, he sailed back to the vessel; the rest as silently following.

Whatever superstitions the sperm whalemen in general have connected with the sight of this object, certain it is, that a glimpse of it being so very unusual, that circumstance has gone far to invest it with portentousness. So rarely is it beheld, that though one and all of them declare it to be the largest animated thing in the ocean, yet very few of them have any but the most vague ideas concerning its true nature and form; notwithstanding, they believe it to furnish to the sperm whale his only food. For though other species of whales find their food above water, and may be seen by man in the act of feeding, the spermaceti whale obtains his whole food in unknown zones below the surface; and only by inference is it that any one can tell of what, precisely, that food consists. At times, when closely pursued, he will disgorge what are supposed to be the detached arms of the squid; some of them thus exhibited exceeding twenty and thirty feet in length. They fancy that the monster to which these arms belonged ordinarily clings by them to the bed of the ocean; and that the sperm whale, unlike other species, is supplied with teeth in order to attack and tear it.

There seems some ground to imagine that the great Kraken of Bishop Pontoppodan may ultimately resolve itself into Squid. The manner in which the Bishop describes it, as alternately rising and sinking, with some other particulars he narrates, in all this the two correspond. But much abatement is necessary with respect to the incredible bulk he assigns it.

By some naturalists who have vaguely heard rumors of the mysterious creature, here spoken of, it is included among the class of cuttle-fish, to which, indeed, in certain external respects it would seem to belong, but only as the Anak of the tribe.





CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
