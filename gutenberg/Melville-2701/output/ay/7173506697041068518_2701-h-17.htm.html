JANK’A 86. Uka Chuyma.

Yaqha poetanakax antilope llamp’u nayrapan jach’añchawinakapwa, ukat janipuniw saraqantir jamach’in munat p’iqip jach’añchapxi; menos celestial, nayax mä chuyma jach’a phunchhawi.

Jach’a jach’a Ballena Esperma ukan chuymapax tronco ukan qalltañapatakiw jakthapita, kawkhantix niya jaqir uñtasit muyuñar puriñkama, ukax pata pata sapakiw utji, phisqa tunk pies cuadrados ukch’a uraqiniwa. Saphipan compacto muyu cuerpopax pä jach’a, ch’ullqi, ch’uqi palmeras jan ukax flukes ukar jilxati, juk’at juk’at mä pulgadat juk’ampi thiyaruw jalxati. entrepierna jan ukax junción ukanx uka flukes ukanakax mä juk’a ch’allt’asipxi, ukatx lado tuqirux ch’iwinakjamaw maynit maynikam jithiqtapxi, ukat uka taypinx mä jach’a ch’usawjax jaytawayi. Janiw kuna jakañansa suma uñnaqtʼan chimpunakapax jukʼamp sum qhanañchatäkiti, uka flukenakan fronteranakapat sipansa. Phuqat jilir ballena ukan juk’amp jach’aptañapatakix, chuymapax pä tunk metrot jilaw juk’amp jach’aptani.

Taqi uka miembrox mä denso cama de terrea de tenos soldados ukar uñtasitawa; ukampis ukar khuchhuqañamawa, ukat kimsa mayj mayj estratos ukanakaw uñstayi:—altu, chika, ukat aynacha. Uka fibras ukanakaxa pata ukhamaraki aynacha capas ukanakana, jaya ukhamaraki horizontal ukanakawa; chika taypinkirinakaxa, wali jisk’a, ukatxa anqäxa capas ukanaka taypina cruzada t’ijtiri. Aka kimsa tuqit lurat lurawixa, kunjamatixa kunaymana yänakaxa, chuyma ch’ama churaraki. Nayra romano pirqanakat yatiqiritakixa, chika capa ukaxa mä curiosa paralelo uñacht’ayani uka jisk’a curso de azulejos sapa kuti alternancia uka qalampi uka muspharkaña reliquias antiguo ukanakana, ukatxa jani pächasisaw wal yanapt’i albañilería ukan jach’a ch’amaparu.

Ukampis uka jach’a ch’ama local ukax tendinos cola ukanx janis askïkaspa ukhama, leviatán ukan taqpach jach’apax mä urdimbre ukat telar de fibras musculares ukat filamentos ukanakampiw tejidos, ukax lomo ukanakat mayni ladopat pasasinx flukes ukar t’ijt’i, jan amuyt’asisaw jupanakamp chikt’atäpxañapawa, ukat jilpachax ch’amaparuw yanapt’apxi; ukhamatwa chuymapanx taqpach ballena ukan jan tupuñ ch’amapax mä chiqar tantachatäkaspas ukhama. ¿Aniquilación ukax materia ukar purispa, akax kuna lurañas utjäna.

Janirakiw ukax —muspharkañ ch’amapax, janipuniw kuynt’awinakapan gracioso flexión ukarux jan ch’amanchañ munkiti; kawkhantix infantilidad de facilidad ukax mä Titanismo de poder tuqiw ondulata. Jan ukasti, uka kuyntʼanakajj ukatwa jukʼamp ajjsarkañ suma uñnaqtʼanïpjje. Chiqpach chʼamax janipuniw suma uñnaqtʼanïñsa ni mayachtʼasiñsa jan waltʼaykiti, ukampis walja kutiw chʼamañchtʼi; ukat taqi kunatï wali suma uñnaqtʼan utjki ukanakanxa, chʼamax magiampiw wal yanaptʼi. Hércules sat chʼiyar chʼiyar chʼaphin mármol sat qalat taqpach pʼakjaskir tendones aptʼasiñamawa, ukat encantopax janiw utjxaspänti. Kunjamtï Diosar yupaychir Eckerman chachajj Goethe chachan qʼala janchipat linot lurat sábana apsuskäna ukhajja, uka jaqen jachʼa pechopampiw chʼamaktʼäna, uka pechojj romanonakan triunfal arcopar uñtasitänwa. Kunawsatix Angelox Dios Awkirus jaqir uñtasit pintki ukhax kuna ch’amanïñas utji uk chimpuntañamawa. Ukat kunatix Yuqan divin munasiñapat uñacht’ayapxchi, llamp’u, k’uchi, hermafroditico italiano uñacht’äwinaka, ukanx amuyunakapax juk’amp sumaw uñacht’ayasi; uka jamuqanakxa, taqi ch’amanïpkchisa, janiw kuna ch’amanïñas uñacht’aykiti, jan ukasti, jan wali, warmin uñacht’äwipakiw jaysaña ukat aguantañapa, ukax taqi amparanakan iyawsatawa, yatichäwinakapan peculiar virtudes prácticas ukanakaruw uñstayi.

Ukhamaw uka órgano ukan sutil elasticidadapaxa, deporte tuqin apnaqatächisa, jan ukax taqi chuymampi, jan ukax colerasiñansa, kuna amuyunïkchisa, flexionanakapax sapa kutiw sinti graciampi chimpuntata. Ukanjja, janiw khiti hadan amparapajj ukat sipansa jukʼampïkaspati.

Phisqa jach’a movimientos ukax peculiar ukhamawa. Nayraqatxa, kunapachatix aleta ukham apnaqatäki ukhax progresión ukatakix; Payïri, kunapachatï nuwasïwin maza ukham apnaqatäki ukhaxa; Kimsïri, q’umachañ tuqinxa; Pusïri, lobtailing ukanxa; Phisqhïri, pico flukes ukanakanwa.

Nayrïri: Leviatán sat chʼojjñajj kawkhantï jikjjataski uka cheqan horizontalätap laykojja, lamar qotan utjki uka yaqha animalanakan chuymapat sipansa mayjawa. Janipuniw liwxataskiti. Jaqinsa jan ukax challwansa, chhuxriñchjasiñax jiskʼachatäñ uñachtʼayi. Ballenatakejja, chuymapakiw uka chʼukuñatak yanaptʼi. Rollo-wise coiled forwards under the body, ukatsti jank’aki sprung backwards, ukaxa uka singular darting, salto movimiento uka monstruo ukarux churaraki kunapachatix colerata nadaski ukhaxa. Lado aletanakapax uka chiqar irpañatakikiw yanaptʼi.

Payïri: Mä juk’a askipuniwa, mä ballena espermatozoide ukax yaqha ballena espermatozoide ukampiw p’iqipampi, chhuxriñchjatampiki nuwasi, ukhamakipans, jaqimp ch’axwañanx jilpachax ukat jisk’achasaw chuymap apnaqi. Mä barcor chʼalltʼasajja, jankʼakiw uka barcot jan waltʼäwinakap apaqtʼi, ukat uka chʼajjwañajj qhepar kuttʼañampikiw chʼalljjtayasi. Jan jarkʼat thayampi luratächi ukhajja, jukʼampejj markapar saraqani ukhajja, uka accidentejj jan jarkʼataw uñjasispa. Jaqen costillanakapas ni barcopas janiw aguantañjamäkiti. Juman qhispiyasiñamax ukat jaltxañakiw utji; ukampis uñisir uma taypit ladopar jutaspa ukhajja, mä jukʼajj ballena sat barcon kʼachachatapata, ukat materialanakan elasticidadapatwa, mä costilla chʼiyjata jan ukajj mä tabla chʼiyjata jan ukajj pä tabla chʼiyjata, ladopan mä kasta puntada, ukham lurañaw jila partejja ukax juk’amp jach’a resultadowa. Uka umamp chhaqhat lado ch’axwawinakax challwa katur utanakanx walja kutiw katuqasi, ukatwa wawanakan anatt’awipakïkaspas ukham uñjapxi. Maynix mä vestido apsu, ukat pʼiyax saytʼatawa.

Kimsïri: Janiw uñacht’ayañjamäkiti, ukampis nayatakix amuyasi, ballena ukanx llamkt’añ amuyux chuymapanw tantachasi; kunattix uka tuqitxa mä suma manqʼaw utji, uka manqʼax qarwa troncopan suma manqʼapampiw kikipaki. Uka suma manq’ax jilpachax q’umachañ lurawimpiw uñacht’ayasi, kunawsatix soltera llamp’u chuymanïñanx ballenax mä juk’a llamp’u llamp’u chuymampiw jach’a ch’akhanakap quta patat mä chiqat yaqha chiqar sarxati; ukat jupax mä marinero ukan ñik’utapjam amuyaschi ukhaxa, ay uka marinero, ñik’utanaka ukat taqi kuna. ¡Uka nayrïr llamktʼañanjja, ¡kunjam munasiñas utji! Aka chuymax kuna ch’amas prehensile ukhamäspa ukhax jank’akiw Darmonodes ukan elefante ukar amuyt’añaja, jupax panqara-markaruw walpun saräna, ukat jisk’a aruntt’awinakampiw nosegays ukanakax doncelas ukanakar uñacht’ayäna, ukatx zonas ukanakap acariciara. Mä kutit sipans jukʼamp cuentanakatjja, ballenajj janiw chuymapan uka prehensile virtud ukanïkiti, ukajj wali llakkañawa; kunattix yaqha elefantetwa istʼta, uka elefantex nuwasïwin usuchjatäsax troncoparuw muyuntäna ukat dardo apsuwayxäna.

Pusïri: Sapa quta taypin jan amuytʼasisaw ballena ukar lunthatasinxa, jachʼa jachʼa tukuñ jachʼa jachʼa tukutapat jan quntʼataw jikxatasma, ukat pusiniru uñtasitaw lamar qutan nina naktayañjam anattʼasi. Ukampis wali chʼamanïtap anattʼäwipan uñjta. Chuymapan ancho palmeranakapax wali jach’a thayaruw coqueteapxi; ukatsti uka patat chʼalltʼasajja, qʼejjo qʼejjo chʼalljjtayajj walja kilometronakwa istʼasi. Niya ukhamarakiw mä jach’a pistola jaquntatäkaspas ukham amuyasma; ukat mayni puntapan espiral sat cheqan vapor sat kʼachat kʼachat coronat uñjasma ukhajja, ukajj llamktʼañ pʼiyanakat chhullunkhapunïkaspas ukhamwa amuyasma.

Phisqhïri: Kunjamtï leviatán sat animalan ordinario postura flotante ukanjja, flukes ukanakajj espaldapan nivelapat wali aynacharuw ikipjje, ukatjja, uraq manqhanjja janiw uñjañjamäkiti; ukampis kunapachatï manqha manqhar jaquntañampïski ukhaxa, taqpach flukes ukanakapax kimsa tunk kayuni janchipampiw thayarux saytʼatäxi, ukhamatwa mä jukʼa chʼallxtasipxi, ukhamatwa alayaru jan uñjkaya chʼallxtapxi. Jan ukasti sublime breach —yaqha chiqanw qhanañchasispa— aka pico de la flukes de ballenas ukax inas taqi animados naturaleza ukan uñjañjam jach’a uñjäwïchispa. Jan manqhankir manqhanakatxa, jachʼa chuymapax espasmódicamente jachʼa alaxpachar apsuskaspas ukhamawa. Ukhamajj samkanakanjja, jachʼa Supayaruw Infiernon Báltico sat nina nakhantat tʼaqhesiyat jachʼa uñnaqap apsuskir uñjta. Ukampis ukham escenanakar uñchʼukiñanjja, kuna chuymanïtasa uka toqetwa parli; Dantean utjchi ukhaxa, saxranakaw jumanakar puripxätam; Isaiasan qillqatapanxa, arcángelanakax ukham lurapxäna. Mä kutix inti jalsu tuqin alaxpachampi qutampi carmesí uñtat inti jalsu pachan barcojan mástil p’iqipan sayt’atäkasinx inti jalsu tuqinx mä jach’a tama ballenas ukanakaruw uñjta, taqiniw inti tuqir sarasipkäna, ukat mä juk’a pachax concierto ukanw ch’allxtapxäna, pico flukes ukanakampi. Kunjamtï uka tiempon amuykitäna ukhama, diosanakar yupaychañ ukham jachʼa uñachtʼäwix janipuniw uñjaskänti, Persia markansa, nina yupaychirinakan utapansa. Kunjamtï Tolomeo Filópater chachajj africano elefantet siskänjja, nayajj uka ballenat qhanañchtʼarakta, jupajj taqe jaqenakat sipansa jukʼamp Diosar yupaychiriwa sasaw sista. Kunattix Juba reyirjamaxa, nayra pachan pallapallanakan elefantenakapax wali ch’ullqhi amukiw tronconakap jach’ar aptasin alwax jach’añchapxirïna.

Aka jaljanxa, ballena ukat elefante ukanakamp chikachasiñax accidental ukhamawa, kunatix yaqhip aspectos de la chupa de la mano y el tronco del mayni, ukax janiw uka pä contrario órganos ukanakarux mä igualdad ukar uñstayañ munañapäkiti, juk’ampirus kuna luratanakarusa jupanakaxa sapa mayniru mantapxi. Kunjamtï jukʼamp chʼamani elefantejj Leviatán chachan mä terrier ukhamakïkijja, ukhamarakiw Leviatán chachan chuymapar uñtasit troncopajj lirio sat chʼojjña chʼaphikïkaspas ukhama. Elefante troncopat jukʼamp jan wali chʼakhanakajj mä ventiladoran anattʼasir grifopjamaw jawqʼjäna, ukajj ballena espermática sat jachʼa jan waltʼäwinakan jan jaktʼkay tʼunjatätapa ukat tʼunjatätapamp chikachasiñawa, ukat walja kutiw mayat maynirojj taqpach botenak taqpach remonakapampi ukat tripulacionanakapamp jaqontapjje thaya, kunjamtï mä indio malabaristax pelotanakap jaquntaski ukhama.*

*Taqi comparación en manera de gran granel general de la ballena y el elefante ukhamäkchisa, kunatix uka particular ukanx elefantex ballena ukarux niya pachpa respetompiw sayt’i, kunjamtix mä allqamarix elefante ukar lurki ukhama; ukhampachasa, janiw yaqhip puntos de similitud curiosa ukanakax munaskiti; ukanak taypinxa, chhuxriñchjasiñaw utjaraki. Uka qarwax walja kutiw troncopan uma jan ukax laqʼa apthapi, ukat jachʼar aptasax mä jawiraruw jaquntañapa, ukax wali uñtʼatawa.

Uka ch’aman chuymap juk’amp amuyt’asax juk’ampiw llakista, jan arsuñjamätax layku. Awisax uka taypinx gestos ukanakaw utji, ukax jaqin amparapamp sum k’achacht’askchisa, jan qhanañchañjamaw qhiparaski. Mä jach’a tama taypinxa, wali muspharkañawa, awisax uka gestos místicos ukanakax utji, ukatwa uywa katurinakar ist’ta, jupanakax Free-Mason ukan chimpunakapar uñtasita ukat chimpunakapar uñtasitaw sasaw sapxi; ukax ballena, chiqpachansa, uka lurawinakampiw amuyt’asis akapachamp aruskipt’awayi. Janirakiw ballenan yaqha movimientonakap taqpach cuerpopan munañas utjkiti, muspharkañanakamp phuqtʼata, ukat jukʼamp yatxattʼat nuwasir jaqirux janiw cuenta churkaspati. Dissectar jupar kunjamsa, ukhamax, nayax ukampis ñik’ut manqhar sarawayta; Janiw jupar uñtʼkti, janirakiw uñtʼkäti. Ukampis nayatï aka ballenan chuymapsa jan yatkstjja, ¿kunjamarak pʼeqep amuyaskästi? jukʼampisa, ¿kunjamsa ajanupar amuytʼasma, kunapachatï ajanupax jan utjki ukhaxa? Jumaw qhipa chiqanakax uñjäta, chuymax, jupax siskaspas ukhamawa, ukampis ajanujax janiw uñjatäkaniti. Ukampis janiw qhepäjjat qhepäjjat yatjjatkti; ukat kuntï ajanupat munki uk uñachtʼayarakta, wasitatwa sista janiw ajanunïkiti.





87 JAQTHAPI.Jach’a Armada.

Malaca markan jachʼa ukat jiskʼa penínsulapajja, Birmá markan territorionakapat inti jalsu toqeruw puri, ukat Asia toqenkir markanakat sipansa jukʼamp sur toqenkir cheqawa. Uka península uksat mä línea continua ukanx Sumatra, Java, Bally ukat Timor jach’a islanakax jilxattawayiwa; ukax walja yaqhanakampi chikaw mä jach’a topo jan ukax muralla ukham uñt’ayasi, ukax Asia markamp Australia markamp jayaruw mayachthapi, ukatx jaya jan p’akjat océano Índico uksaruw oriental archipiélagos ukanakat jaljaraki. Aka murallax walja sally-portes ukanakamp ch’uqt’atawa, barconakatakis ballenanakatakis jan ch’amäñapataki; uka taypinjja, Sunda ukat Malaca sat estrechonakaw qhan uñjasi. Sunda estrecho uksanx jilpachax inti jalant tuqit China markar sarir barconakax China qutanakaruw mistu.

Sunda markan uka jisk’a estrechonakax Sumatra markaruw Java markat jaljapxi; ukat uka jachʼa islanakan murallapan chika thakin saytʼatäskäna, uka chʼojjña chʼojjña qollumpi chʼamañchtʼata, uka qota lamar qotankirinakajj Java Pʼeqe sasin uñtʼatäpjjewa; janiw mä juk’a chika taypinkir punkux mä jach’a pirqamp muyuntat imperio ukar jist’arañamp kikipäkiti: ukat jan tukuskir qamirïtap amuyt’asax especias, ukat sedas, ukat joyas, ukat quri, ukat marfil, ukampiw uka oriental qutan waranq islanakax qamiriptayatäpxi, ukhamaw amuyasi mä jach’a wakichawi naturaleza tuqita, uka tesoros ukaxa, uraqi luratapampixa, mä juk’a uñacht’ayañawa, jani ukaxa jani askikiti, taqi kuna katxaruña occidental pachata jark’aqatawa. Estrecho de Sunda quta thiyanakanx janiw uka jach’a fortalezanakax utjkiti, ukanakax Mediterráneo, Báltico ukat Propontis ukanakar mantañ punkunakaruw uñjapxi. Daneses ukanakat sipansa, uka orientales ukanakax janiw thaya nayraqatan jan tukuskir procesión de barcos ukanakat jisk’achat top-sails ukanakar obsequioso homenaje ukar mayipkiti, ukax patak patak maranakaw pasawayxi, arumanakas urus Sumatra ukat Java islas ukanakanx pasawayiwa, carganakampi inti jalsu tuqinkir juk’amp jila qullqin carganaka. Ukampis ukham ceremonial jan khitin jarkʼatäpkchisa, janiw jukʼamp chʼamani impuesto churañ munapkiti.

Tiempo de pensamiento de los proas piratas de malayos, escondidos entre las calas y islas bajas sombreadas de Sumatra, ukax estrecho uksan sarnaqir barconakaruw jaltxapxi, lanza puntapanx impuesto ukx wali ch’amampiw mayipxi. Europa markankir cruceronakan amparapamp walja kuti wila wartäwinak katuqapkchïnsa, uka corsarios ukanakan jan axsartʼirïtapax qhipa maranakanx mä jukʼa chhaqtayatawa; ukampis jichhürunakansa, awisax inglés ukat americano barconakat istʼtanwa, uka barconakarux uka uma taypinxa, jan arrepentisisaw mantapxi ukat lunthatapxi.

Mä suma, suma thayampixa, Pequod ukax jichhax uka estrechonakaruw jak’achasxäna; Acab chachajj jupanak taypit Javan qotar pasañ amtäna, ukat uka cheqatjja, alay toqer sarasaw Ballena Esperma sat animalajj akawjan ukat ukjan sarañapatak uñtʼatäki uka uma patjjarojj Islas Filipinas sat cheqanakar qotar qʼomachasïna, ukat Japón markan jaya qota lakapsa jikjjatasïna, tiempopajj uka jach’a ballena katuñ pachax ukan. Uka tuqinakatxa, Pequod sat muytaskir barcox niya taqpach uraqpachan uñtʼat Ballenas Esperma sat barconakan cruceronakaparuw qʼumachañapäna, janïr Pacífico uksan Línea sat chiqar saraqañkama; kawkhantix Acab chachax taqi chiqans arknaqañapatak jan ch’amanchatäkchïnxa, Moby Dick ukar nuwasiñ churañapatakix wali ch’amampiw jakt’äna, uka qutanx jupax juk’amp uñt’atawa; ukat kuna pachatix jupax juk’amp amuyt’asisaw uka pacharux uñch’ukiski sasin amuyasispa.

Ukampis ¿kunjamatsa jichhajj? aka zonado thaqhañanxa, ¿Ahab reyejj janiw kuna oraqsa llamktʼkiti? ¿tripulacionapax thaya umapxiti? Chiqpachansa, umatakiw saytʼani. Janiwa. Walja pachaw, jichhax circo-t’ijtir intix nina anillu manqhan t’ijt’awayxi, ukatx janiw kuna manq’añas munaskiti jan ukasti kunatix jupa pachpan utjki ukawa. Ukhamajj Acab. Ukxa, ukhamaraki, ballena katurinxa. Yaqha casconakax yaqha markankir yänakampi apkatat uñjasi, anqäx markan muellenakar apañataki; uraqpachan sarnaqir ballena barcox janiw kuna cargas apt’kiti jan ukasti jupa pachpa ukat tripulacionanakapa, armanakapa ukat munañanakap apt’ata. Jupajj walja qotan utjki ukanak botellaruw uchasi. Jupax utilidad ukanakamp balast’atawa; janiw taqpachax jan apnaqañjam khuchhi-plomo ukat perrera ukanakampikiti. Jupax walja maranakaw umax apt’asi. Qhana nayra prime Nantucket uma; ukax kimsa maraw uma taypin sarnaqäna, Nantucketer, Pacífico uksanx janïr salmuera líquido umañ munapxi, ukampis qharüruw barril ukan balsa ukar sarapxäna, peruano jan ukax indio jawiranakat. Ukatwa, yaqha barconakax Nueva York markat China markar sarapxaspäna, ukat wasitat kutt’anipxäna, walja puertonakar llamkt’asa, ballena-barcox, taqi uka pachanx inas jan mä grano uraqi uñjkchïnti; tripulacionapax janiw khitirus uñjapkänti, jan ukasti jupanakjam uma taypinkir qutankir jaqinakaruw uñjapxäna. Ukhamat jumanakax yaqha uma jalsu jutatap yatiyapxäta; jupanakax akham sasakiw jaysapxirïna: “¡Walikiwa, waynanaka, akax arcakiwa!”

Jichhax kunjamtï walja Ballenas Esperma ukanakax Java markan inti jalant tuqinkir quta thiyan katuntatäxäna, ukax Estrecho de Sunda jak’ankiwa; chiqpachansa, kunjamatix jilpach uraqix, muyu, challwa katur jaqinakax wali suma chiqaw crucero ukar sarañatakix uñt’atäna; ukatwa, Pequod ukax Java Head ukar juk’amp juk’amp jikxatäna, uñch’ukirinakax walja kutiw jach’añchapxäna, ukat wali jach’a jan ikiñapatakiw iwxt’apxäna. Ukampis uka oraqen chʼojjña palmera qalanakajj mä qhawqha tiempot estribor proa patjjan uñstkchïnjja, ukat kusisit nayranakampi fresco canelajj thayaruw chʼalljjtayasïna, ukampis janiw mä jet uñachtʼayaskänti. Niya taqi amuyunaka jaytasaxa, kuna anatt’awimpis aka chiqan jaltxañaxa, barcox niyaw estrecho ukar mantañampïxäna, ukatx costumbre ukarjam kusisiñ arnaqawix alaxpachat ist’asïna, ukat ere long mä espectáculo de singular magnificencia ukaw aruntt’apxitu.

Ukampis akanx akham sañjamawa, kunatix jan qarjataw lurawimp kunatix qhipa maranakanx pusi qutanak taypin thaqhapxi, Ballenas Esperma ukanakax niya sapa kutiw jisk’a empresas destacadas ukan sarnaqañat sipansa, kunjamatix nayrir pachanakanx ukhama, jichhax sapa kutiw in jachʼa tama tamanakaw utjäna, awisajj walja jaqenakaruw tantachtʼasipjjäna, ukat niyas jupanakat walja markanakajj maynit maynikam yanaptʼasiñatakisa ukat jarkʼaqasiñatakis liga ukat pacto solemne juramento lurapkaspa ukhamäspawa. “Look-e here,” said Queequeg, pointing down.

As when the stricken whale, that from the tub has reeled out hundreds of fathoms of rope; as, after deep sounding, he floats up again, and shows the slackened curling line buoyantly rising and spiralling towards the air; so now, Starbuck saw long coils of the umbilical cord of Madame Leviathan, by which the young cub seemed still tethered to its dam. Not seldom in the rapid vicissitudes of the chase, this natural line, with the maternal end loose, becomes entangled with the hempen one, so that the cub is thereby trapped. Some of the subtlest secrets of the seas seemed divulged to us in this enchanted pond. We saw young Leviathan amours in the deep.*

*The sperm whale, as with all other species of the Leviathan, but unlike most other fish, breeds indifferently at all seasons; after a gestation which may probably be set down at nine months, producing but one at a time; though in some few known instances giving birth to an Esau and Jacob:—a contingency provided for in suckling by two teats, curiously situated, one on each side of the anus; but the breasts themselves extend upwards from that. When by chance these precious parts in a nursing whale are cut by the hunter's lance, the mother's pouring milk and blood rivallingly discolour the sea for rods. The milk is very sweet and rich; it has been tasted by man; it might do well with strawberries. When overflowing with mutual esteem, the whales salute more hominum .

And thus, though surrounded by circle upon circle of consternations and affrights, did these inscrutable creatures at the centre freely and fearlessly indulge in all peaceful concernments; yea, serenely revelled in dalliance and delight. But even so, amid the tornadoed Atlantic of my being, do I myself still for ever centrally disport in mute calm; and while ponderous planets of unwaning woe revolve round me, deep down and deep inland there I still bathe me in eternal mildness of joy.

Meanwhile, as we thus lay entranced, the occasional sudden frantic spectacles in the distance evinced the activity of the other boats, still engaged in drugging the whales on the frontier of the host; or possibly carrying on the war within the first circle, where abundance of room and some convenient retreats were afforded them. But the sight of the enraged drugged whales now and then blindly darting to and fro across the circles, was nothing to what at last met our eyes. It is sometimes the custom when fast to a whale more than commonly powerful and alert, to seek to hamstring him, as it were, by sundering or maiming his gigantic tail-tendon. It is done by darting a short-handled cutting-spade, to which is attached a rope for hauling it back again. A whale wounded (as we afterwards learned) in this part, but not effectually, as it seemed, had broken away from the boat, carrying along with him half of the harpoon line; and in the extraordinary agony of the wound, he was now dashing among the revolving circles like the lone mounted desperado Arnold, at the battle of Saratoga, carrying dismay wherever he went.

But agonizing as was the wound of this whale, and an appalling spectacle enough, any way; yet the peculiar horror with which he seemed to inspire the rest of the herd, was owing to a cause which at first the intervening distance obscured from us. But at length we perceived that by one of the unimaginable accidents of the fishery, this whale had become entangled in the harpoon-line that he towed; he had also run away with the cutting-spade in him; and while the free end of the rope attached to that weapon, had permanently caught in the coils of the harpoon-line round his tail, the cutting-spade itself had worked loose from his flesh. So that tormented to madness, he was now churning through the water, violently flailing with his flexible tail, and tossing the keen spade about him, wounding and murdering his own comrades.

This terrific object seemed to recall the whole herd from their stationary fright. First, the whales forming the margin of our lake began to crowd a little, and tumble against each other, as if lifted by half spent billows from afar; then the lake itself began faintly to heave and swell; the submarine bridal-chambers and nurseries vanished; in more and more contracting orbits the whales in the more central circles began to swim in thickening clusters. Yes, the long calm was departing. A low advancing hum was soon heard; and then like to the tumultuous masses of block-ice when the great river Hudson breaks up in Spring, the entire host of whales came tumbling upon their inner centre, as if to pile themselves up in one common mountain. Instantly Starbuck and Queequeg changed places; Starbuck taking the stern.

“Oars! Oars!” he intensely whispered, seizing the helm—“gripe your oars, and clutch your souls, now! My God, men, stand by! Shove him off, you Queequeg—the whale there!—prick him!—hit him! Stand up—stand up, and stay so! Spring, men—pull, men; never mind their backs—scrape them!—scrape away!”

The boat was now all but jammed between two vast black bulks, leaving a narrow Dardanelles between their long lengths. But by desperate endeavor we at last shot into a temporary opening; then giving way rapidly, and at the same time earnestly watching for another outlet. After many similar hair-breadth escapes, we at last swiftly glided into what had just been one of the outer circles, but now crossed by random whales, all violently making for one centre. This lucky salvation was cheaply purchased by the loss of Queequeg's hat, who, while standing in the bows to prick the fugitive whales, had his hat taken clean from his head by the air-eddy made by the sudden tossing of a pair of broad flukes close by.

Riotous and disordered as the universal commotion now was, it soon resolved itself into what seemed a systematic movement; for having clumped together at last in one dense body, they then renewed their onward flight with augmented fleetness. Further pursuit was useless; but the boats still lingered in their wake to pick up what drugged whales might be dropped astern, and likewise to secure one which Flask had killed and waifed. The waif is a pennoned pole, two or three of which are carried by every boat; and which, when additional game is at hand, are inserted upright into the floating body of a dead whale, both to mark its place on the sea, and also as token of prior possession, should the boats of any other ship draw near.

The result of this lowering was somewhat illustrative of that sagacious saying in the Fishery,—the more whales the less fish. Of all the drugged whales only one was captured. The rest contrived to escape for the time, but only to be taken, as will hereafter be seen, by some other craft than the Pequod.





CHAPTER 88. Schools and Schoolmasters.

The previous chapter gave account of an immense body or herd of Sperm Whales, and there was also then given the probable cause inducing those vast aggregations.

Now, though such great bodies are at times encountered, yet, as must have been seen, even at the present day, small detached bands are occasionally observed, embracing from twenty to fifty individuals each. Such bands are known as schools. They generally are of two sorts; those composed almost entirely of females, and those mustering none but young vigorous males, or bulls, as they are familiarly designated.

In cavalier attendance upon the school of females, you invariably see a male of full grown magnitude, but not old; who, upon any alarm, evinces his gallantry by falling in the rear and covering the flight of his ladies. In truth, this gentleman is a luxurious Ottoman, swimming about over the watery world, surroundingly accompanied by all the solaces and endearments of the harem. The contrast between this Ottoman and his concubines is striking; because, while he is always of the largest leviathanic proportions, the ladies, even at full growth, are not more than one-third of the bulk of an average-sized male. They are comparatively delicate, indeed; I dare say, not to exceed half a dozen yards round the waist. Nevertheless, it cannot be denied, that upon the whole they are hereditarily entitled to en bon point .

It is very curious to watch this harem and its lord in their indolent ramblings. Like fashionables, they are for ever on the move in leisurely search of variety. You meet them on the Line in time for the full flower of the Equatorial feeding season, having just returned, perhaps, from spending the summer in the Northern seas, and so cheating summer of all unpleasant weariness and warmth. By the time they have lounged up and down the promenade of the Equator awhile, they start for the Oriental waters in anticipation of the cool season there, and so evade the other excessive temperature of the year.

When serenely advancing on one of these journeys, if any strange suspicious sights are seen, my lord whale keeps a wary eye on his interesting family. Should any unwarrantably pert young Leviathan coming that way, presume to draw confidentially close to one of the ladies, with what prodigious fury the Bashaw assails him, and chases him away! High times, indeed, if unprincipled young rakes like him are to be permitted to invade the sanctity of domestic bliss; though do what the Bashaw will, he cannot keep the most notorious Lothario out of his bed; for, alas! all fish bed in common. As ashore, the ladies often cause the most terrible duels among their rival admirers; just so with the whales, who sometimes come to deadly battle, and all for love. They fence with their long lower jaws, sometimes locking them together, and so striving for the supremacy like elks that warringly interweave their antlers. Not a few are captured having the deep scars of these encounters,—furrowed heads, broken teeth, scolloped fins; and in some instances, wrenched and dislocated mouths.

But supposing the invader of domestic bliss to betake himself away at the first rush of the harem's lord, then is it very diverting to watch that lord. Gently he insinuates his vast bulk among them again and revels there awhile, still in tantalizing vicinity to young Lothario, like pious Solomon devoutly worshipping among his thousand concubines. Granting other whales to be in sight, the fishermen will seldom give chase to one of these Grand Turks; for these Grand Turks are too lavish of their strength, and hence their unctuousness is small. As for the sons and the daughters they beget, why, those sons and daughters must take care of themselves; at least, with only the maternal help. For like certain other omnivorous roving lovers that might be named, my Lord Whale has no taste for the nursery, however much for the bower; and so, being a great traveller, he leaves his anonymous babies all over the world; every baby an exotic. In good time, nevertheless, as the ardour of youth declines; as years and dumps increase; as reflection lends her solemn pauses; in short, as a general lassitude overtakes the sated Turk; then a love of ease and virtue supplants the love for maidens; our Ottoman enters upon the impotent, repentant, admonitory stage of life, forswears, disbands the harem, and grown to an exemplary, sulky old soul, goes about all alone among the meridians and parallels saying his prayers, and warning each young Leviathan from his amorous errors.

Now, as the harem of whales is called by the fishermen a school, so is the lord and master of that school technically known as the schoolmaster. It is therefore not in strict character, however admirably satirical, that after going to school himself, he should then go abroad inculcating not what he learned there, but the folly of it. His title, schoolmaster, would very naturally seem derived from the name bestowed upon the harem itself, but some have surmised that the man who first thus entitled this sort of Ottoman whale, must have read the memoirs of Vidocq, and informed himself what sort of a country-schoolmaster that famous Frenchman was in his younger days, and what was the nature of those occult lessons he inculcated into some of his pupils.

The same secludedness and isolation to which the schoolmaster whale betakes himself in his advancing years, is true of all aged Sperm Whales. Almost universally, a lone whale—as a solitary Leviathan is called—proves an ancient one. Like venerable moss-bearded Daniel Boone, he will have no one near him but Nature herself; and her he takes to wife in the wilderness of waters, and the best of wives she is, though she keeps so many moody secrets.

The schools composing none but young and vigorous males, previously mentioned, offer a strong contrast to the harem schools. For while those female whales are characteristically timid, the young males, or forty-barrel-bulls, as they call them, are by far the most pugnacious of all Leviathans, and proverbially the most dangerous to encounter; excepting those wondrous grey-headed, grizzled whales, sometimes met, and these will fight you like grim fiends exasperated by a penal gout.

The Forty-barrel-bull schools are larger than the harem schools. Like a mob of young collegians, they are full of fight, fun, and wickedness, tumbling round the world at such a reckless, rollicking rate, that no prudent underwriter would insure them any more than he would a riotous lad at Yale or Harvard. They soon relinquish this turbulence though, and when about three-fourths grown, break up, and separately go about in quest of settlements, that is, harems.

Another point of difference between the male and female schools is still more characteristic of the sexes. Say you strike a Forty-barrel-bull—poor devil! all his comrades quit him. But strike a member of the harem school, and her companions swim around her with every token of concern, sometimes lingering so near her and so long, as themselves to fall a prey.
