Akax, jichhax pä jach’a ballenas ukanakaw p’iqinakap mayacht’asis uchasipxi; jupanakamp chikt'atäñäni, jiwasanaksa mayachasiñäni.

Folio leviatán sat jachʼa ordenanakatjja, Ballena Esperma ukat Ballena Derecha ukanakaw jukʼamp amuyasi. Jupanakakiw jaqejj sapa kuti katuntapjje. Kunjamatix jupanakan anqäx mayjt’awipax jilpachax p’iqinakapan uñjasi; ukat sapa p’iqinchirjamax aka pachax Pequod ladopat warkt’atawa; ukat kunjamtï maynit maynikam jan khitin jarkʼat sarksnajja, cubierta taypin kayuk sarañakiw wakisispa:—kawkhansa, nayajj yatiñ muntwa, aka cheqat sipansa, cetología práctica toqet yatjjatañatakejj jukʼamp suma chʼam jikjjatasma?

Nayraqatxa, uka pʼiqinakax kunjamsa mayj mayjäpxi uk uñjasax wal muspharta. Panpachaniw taqi conciencianx jach’a jach’a tukupxi; ukampis mä juk’a simetría matemática ukaw Ballena Esperma ukanx utji ukax Ballena Derecha ukanx llakisiñaw jan utjkiti. Ballena Esperma p’iqipanx juk’amp ch’amaw utji. Kunjamtï uñjktaxa, jan munkasaw jupat sipansa jukʼamp jachʼa kankañama, taqi chiqan jachʼañchatäñamataki. Jichha pachanx ukhamarakiw uka jach’añchäwix p’iqipan piminta ukat salt color ukamp jach’anchayata, ukax jilïr marani ukat jach’a experiencia uñacht’ayi. Mä juk’a arumpixa, jupax kuntix challwa katurinakax técnicamente “gris p’iqin ballena” sapki ukawa.

Jichhajj kunas uka pʼeqenakan jukʼamp jan kikpäki uk uñjañäni, mä arunjja, nayra ukat jiñchu satäkis uka pä órganonakajj wali wakiskiriwa. Pʼeqe ladopan jaya qhepäjjat, ukat alayaru, kawkïr ballenanakan ñikʼutapan ángulo jakʼanjja, mä jiskʼa thaqtʼäta ukhajja, qhepatjja jan pestañani nayra uñjäta, ukajj mä asno qallun nayrapäkaspas ukhamwa amuyasma; ukhamax taqi kunat sipansa p’iqin jach’a jach’a tukutaparuw uñt’ayasi.

Jichhax uka peculiar lado uñtawi ballena nayranakapatxa, qhanaw amuyasi, janipuniw mä yäx uñjkaspati, ukax chiqpachan nayraqatapankiwa, janiw juk’ampikiti, kunjamtï mä chiqpach qhipäxar uñjki ukhama. Mä arunjja, ballenan nayranakapajj kawkhantï jikjjataski ukajj mä jaqen jiñchupamp sasiwa; ukat inas juma pachpax kunjams jumamp sarnaqaspa uk amuytʼchisma, ¿jiñchunakamat yänak lado tuqit uñakiptʼirïtati. Chiqa lado uñkatasiñat sipansa, niya kimsa tunka gradonak uñjañakiw nayraqat kamachisma sasaw amuyasma; ukat niya kimsa tunka juk’ampi qhipäxanxa. Wali qhuru uñisirix chiqak jumar jak’achasispa, jach’a urux puñal jach’ar apt’ata, janiw uñjkasmati, qhipäxat lunthataskätam ukhama. Mä arunxa, pä qhipäxanïsmawa, sañjamawa; ukampis, pachpa pachanx, pä frente (frontes laterales) ukhamarakiwa: kunatix ¿kunas jaqin nayraqatapar tukuyi —chiqpachansa, ¿kunas nayranakapat sipansa?

Ukatjja, jichhajj amuytʼkta uka jila parte yaqha animalanakanjja, nayranakajj jan amuyañjamaw yapuchatäpjje, ukhamat uñjañ chʼamap mayachthapiñataki, ukhamat mä dibujo uñstayañataki, janiw pä dibujo pʼeqeru uñstayañatakikiti; ballena nayranakapajj mayj mayja uñstatapa, kunjamtï walja pies cúbicos pʼeqempi jaljatäki ukhama, uka pʼeqejj jupanak taypinjja mä jachʼa qollur uñtataw saytʼasi, ukajj pä qotaruw vallenakan jaljaski; ukax chiqans sapa independiente órgano ukan impresionanakap taqpach jaljañapawa. Ukhamasti, ballenax aka ladopanx mä yaqha uñacht’äwiw uñjañapa, ukat uka ladopanx yaqha yaqha uñacht’äwiw uñjasiñapa; ukat taqi ukanakax jupatakix wali chʼamaka chʼamakakïñapawa, janirakiw kunas utjañapäkiti. Jaqix mä caja de centinela ukan akapach uñch’ukispa sañjamawa, uka cajax ventanapatakix pä fajas mayachatawa. Ukampis ballenampixa, uka pä fajax sapa mayniw uchasi, ukat pä mayj mayj ventananakaw utji, ukampis llakisiñaw uñjañax jan waltʼayi. Aka peculiaridad de la ballena nayranakapaxa, challwa katurinakanxa sapa kutiwa amuyt’añasa; ukat yaqhip qhipa uñacht’äwinakanx ullart’irix amtañapawa.

Mä muspharkañ ukat juk’amp muspharkañ jiskt’awix aka uñjañ tuqitx Leviatán ukar llamkt’kaspas ukhamaw qalltaspa. Ukampis mä pʼeqeñchäwimpiw kusisiñaja. Ukhamajj mä jaqen nayranakapajj qhanan jistʼaratäki ukhajja, uñjañajj janiw munkiti; mä arunxa, ukapachax janiw kuna yänakatï nayraqatapan utjki ukanak mecánicamente uñjañ yanaptʼkaspati. Ukhamäkchisa, khitin experienciapajj yatichaniwa, mä kutikiw jan yaqhachasis kunanaksa apthapispa, ukampis janiw jupatakejj wali amuyumpi, ukat taqe chuymampi, kuna pä yänaksa mä kuti uñakiptʼañjamäkiti, mä jukʼa jachʼasa jan ukajj jiskʼaksa— ukat pachpa pachan pachapa; janipuniw lado lado ikisipki ukat llamktʼasipkchiti. Ukampis jichhax uka pä yänak jaljañataki jutapxäta, ukat sapa maynir mä jach’a ch’amaka muyump muyuntapxäta ukhaxa; ukatsti, jupanakat maynir uñjañatakejja, amuyumarjam uñjañatakejja, maynijj jichha tiempon utjki uka concienciamat qʼal jaqsutäniwa. Ukhamasti, ¿kunjamakis ballena sat animalampixa? Chiqansa, panpacha nayranakapaxa, jupanakpachanxa, mä pachanwa lurañapa; ukampis ¿pʼiqipax jaqit sipansa jukʼamp jachʼa, mayachtʼata ukat jan amuyañjamati, ukat mä pachpa tiempon pä mayj mayj suytʼäwinak sum uñakiptʼaspa, maynix mä ladopankiwa, maynïristi mayj mayjäki? Jupatï atipchi ukhajja, ¿jupanjja, mä muspharkañäpachati, mä jaqejj Euclides chachan pä mayj mayj jan waltʼäwinakan uñachtʼäwinakap mä pachpa tiempon pasaspa ukhama. Janirakiw, wali yatxatatäsa, janiw kuna incongruencias uka uñtasïwinx utjkiti.

Inas mä ina ch’usat capricho ukhamächispa, ukampis nayatakix nayratpachaw amuyasi, yaqhip ballenas ukanakax kimsa jan ukax pusi botes ukanakamp muyuntat uñjasipki ukhax extraordinario vacilaciones de movimiento uñacht’ayapxi; timididad ukat responsabilidad queer frights, ukhama común ukham ballenas ukanakataki; Nayax amuyta, taqi ukax indirectamente ukax volición ukan jan yanapt’ir perplejidad ukat juti, ukanx jupanakan dividido ukat diametralmente contrario poderes de visión ukanakax jupanakarux uñt’ayañapawa.

Ukampis ballenan jinchupajj nayrapjamaw yatiñ munir phoqantat uñjasi. Jumatï jupanakan jañchipat jan uñtʼatästa ukhajja, inas walja horanak uka pä pʼeqenak patjjar thaqsta, ukat janipuniw uka órgano jikjjatkasmati. Jinchuxa janiwa anqäxa laphinakapaxa kunasa utjkiti; ukat pachpa pʼiyarux janiw mä pluma uchañjamakiti, ukhamax muspharkañ minutokiwa. Nayra qhipäxan mä juk’a qurpachatawa. Jinchunakapar respetañ tuqitxa, uka wakiskir mayjtʼäwix ballena espermatozoide ukat derecha ukanakan uñjatäñapawa. Nayrïr jinchupax anqäx jist’aratawa, qhipïri jinchupax taqpach ukat mä kipkakiw mä membrana ukamp ch’uqt’ata, ukhamat anqäxat jan amuyañjama.

¿Janit yatiñ munki, ballena ukham jachʼa jaqejj akapach jiskʼa nayrampi uñjañapa, ukat liebret sipansa jukʼamp jiskʼa jinchut qʼejjo qʼejjo istʼañapa? Ukampis nayranakapajj Herschel chachan jachʼa telescopion lentepar uñtasit jachʼäspa ukhajja; ukat jinchunakapax catedralanakan pʼiyanakapjam jachʼänwa; ¿ukax jukʼamp uñjañapataki, jan ukax jukʼamp istʼañapatakicha? Janiwa.—Ukhamaxa, ¿kunatsa amuyunakam ‘jachʼaptayañataki’ chʼamachasta? Subtilizar ukax mä juk’a pachanakanwa.

Jichhax kuna palancas ukat motores de vapor ukanak amparan utjkistu ukanakampi, ballena espermatozoide p’iqip patxar cant, ukhamat manqhat patat ikiñapataki; ukatsti, mä escalerampiw qullu patat makhatasinxa, lakat mä jukʼa uñchʼukiñama; ukat janitix jichhax janchix ukat q’al jaljtatäkaspa ukhax mä linternampiw purakapan jach’a Kentucky Mammoth Cave ukar saraqasna. Ukampis aka kisut akan katxaruñäni, ukat kawkhans jikxatastan uk uñakiptʼañäni. ¡Chiqpachansa wali suma ukat qʼuma uñnaqtʼan lakapa! pampat techokama, forronakampi, jan ukax jukʼamp askixa, mä lliphipikir janqʼu membranampiw papelampi qillqtʼata, ukax novia sat satinanakar uñtataw lliphipi.

Ukampis jichhax mistum, ukat aka portentoso inferior jawq’a uñakipt’am, ukax mä jach’a tabaco caja ukan jach’a jisk’a tapapar uñtasitawa, bisagrax mä puntapankiwa, mä lado lanti. Ukax p’iqit apsuñapataki, ukat filas de kisunakap uñacht’ayasax, mä muspharkañ portcullis ukhamaw uñjasi; ukat ukham, ¡ay! ukax waljaniruw challwa katur jan wali wight uñacht’ayi, juparuw uka ch’aphinakax ch’akkatat ch’amampiw jaquqani. Ukampis juk’amp axsarañawa uñjañaxa, kunapachatix qutan mä juk’a aynacharu, mä qawqha sulky ballena uñjta, ukan warkt’ata, muspharkañ jawq’apampi, mä tunka phisqhan metros largo, chiqak alayaru warkt’ata chiqa ángulo cuerpopampi, taqi ukanakataki akapachanx mä barcon jib-boom ukham uñt’atawa. Aka ballenax janiw jiwatäkiti; jupax aynacht’atakiwa; kunayman tuqitwa, inas; hipocondría ukaxa; ukat wali quntʼatäskiwa, ukhamatwa chhuxriñchjasiñan bisagranakapax samarawayxi, ukat uka jan wali jan waltʼäwinwa ukan jaytawayi, taqi tribupatakix mä jiskʼachawiwa, jupanakax janiw pächasiñasäkiti, juparux llawintat jawqʼachasipxañapawa.

Jilapachax aka jisk’a jawq’a —mä artista practicatax jasakiw jan bisagrar apsutäki— marfil kisunak apsuñatakix cubiertaruw jan walt’ayasi ukat liwxataraki, ukat uka ch’akha janq’u ballena ch’akhax mä suministro churaraki, ukampiw challwa katurinakax taqi kasta yatxatañ yänak lurapxi, ukatsti cañas, paraguas-stocks ukat amparanakas riding-whips ukanakaw utjaraki.

Mä jachʼa, qarjata elevadorampiw jawqʼajj barcor ayxatatäjje, mä anclakaspas ukhama; ukat kunapachatï wakiskir pachax purinxi ukhaxa —yaqha irnaqäwit mä qawqha urunak qhipatxa— Queequeg, Daggoo ukat Tashtego jupanakax taqiniw wali yatxattʼat dentistanakäpxatapatxa, kisunak dibujañatak wakichtʼasipxi. Mä jach’a cutting-spade ukampixa, Queequeg jupax encías ukanakaruw lanza; ukatx jawq’jax anillo pernonakaruw ch’uqt’ata, ukatx mä aparejox alaxpachat rigged ukhamawa, uka kisunak apsusipxi, kunjamatix Michigan wakax nayra encinas tocóns sallqa quqa uraqinakat apsusipxi ukhama. Jilapachax taqpachax pusi tunk payan kisunakaw utji; nayra ballenanakanxa, wali ch’ama tukuta, ukampisa janiwa ismuñakiti; janirakiw jiwasan moda artificial ukarjam phuqhantatäkiti. Uka qhipatxa, jawq’ax losas ukar tukuyatawa, ukat utanaka lurañatakix vigas ukham pilat apthapitawa.





75 JAQTHAPI.Ch’iqa ballena p’iqi —Contrasta uñakipaña.

Cubierta chiqancht’asax jichhax Ballena Derecha p’iqip mä suma jaya uñakipt’añäni.

Kunjamatix forma general ukanx noble Ballena Esperma p’iqipax mä romano ch’axwañ carro ukar uñtasitäspawa (juk’ampirus nayraqatapanxa, kawkhantix wali jach’a muyuki); ukhamax mä jach’a uñtawinx Ballena Derecha ukan p’iqipax mä jach’a galiote kayu zapato ukar uñtasitaw mä juk’a jan suma uñt’ata. Pä patak maranak nayrajja, holandés markankir mä nayra viajerojja, zapato lurañ yatirin qhep qhepa uñnaqapampiw uñtasïna. Ukat aka pachpa qhipa jan ukax zapato ukanx, uka chuyman warmix guardería cuento ukanx, chhullunkhayat uywanakampi, wali sumaw qurpachasispa, jupax taqpach wawanakapampi.

Ukampis aka jach’a p’iqir jak’achasisax kunayman aspectos ukanakaw asumiñ qalltawayi, jumanakan amuyunakamarjama. Jumatix qullu patat sayt’asis uka pä F ukham uñtat boquillas uñch’ukisma ukhax taqpach p’iqip mä jach’a bajo-viol ukar apasma, ukat uka espirales, aperturas ukanakax tablero de sonido ukankiwa. Ukatxa, mayamp, nayram uñch’ukisma aka muspharkañ, cresta, peine ukham incrustación misa patat —aka ch’uxña, barnacled yä, ukax Groenlandia markankirinakax “corona” sapxiwa, ukat Sur uksankir challwa katurinakax “capó” sapxiwa ukat Ballena Derecha ukax; ukat ukakipkarakiw nayranakam uñchʼukisma, ukat pʼiqipxa mä jachʼa encina sat quqan troncoparuw apxasma, uka quqan chhuxriñchjatapax mä jamachʼin qullupawa. Kunjamakitix, kunapachatix uka jakkir cangrejos ukanakax akan aka capó ukan jikxatasipki uk uñch’ukipxäta ukhax niyas ukham amuyt’awix jumaruw purini; jan ukax chiqpachansa, fantasía ukax “corona” uka técnico término ukamp askichatäkchixa, ukax ukaruw churataraki; ukhamatwa kunjamsa aka chʼamani monstruojj cheqapuni lamar qotan diadamampi uchatäski uk amuytʼañ wal munäta, jupajj chʼojjña coronapampiw jupatak ukham muspharkañ uchasiwayi. Ukampis aka ballenax reyïchi ukhaxa, mä diadema k’achacht’añatakix wali sulky uñnaqt’an jaqiwa. ¡Uka warktʼat alaya laphip uñtam! ¡kuna jach’a sulk ukat pout ukax utji! mä sulk ukat pout, carpintero tupuparjama, niya pä tunk metro largo ukat phisqa metro manqha; mä sulk ukat pout ukax niya 500 litros aceite ukat juk’ampinakaw utji.

Mä jach’a llakiw, jichhax aka jan wali ballenax liebre-lipped ukhamäñapawa. Uka fisurajj niya mä kayu ukchʼawa. Inas uka taykax mä wakiskir pachanx Peru markan costa uksanx barcot saraskchïna, ukatx uraq khathatinakax quta thiyaruw jist’arawayi. Aka laka patxaru, kunjamatixa mä umbral lliphipiski ukhama, jichhaxa lakaru liwxattanxa. Nayan arunakajarjamax Mackinaw ukankäyätwa, akax mä wigwam indio ukan manqhapjam apsuñajawa. ¡Suma Tatitu! ¿aka thakix Jonasax sarawayki? Uka uta patax niya tunka payan metronakaw alturani, ukat mä suma ch’ullqhi ángulo ukar t’ijti, ukanx mä regular ridge-pole ukax utjkaspas ukhama; ukampirus uka costilla, arco, p’iqini ladonakax uka muspharkay, chikat sayt’at, escimetar ukham listones de ballena ch’akhanak uñacht’ayapxistu, sañäni kimsa patak mä lado, ukax p’iqin patat jan ukax corona ch’akhapat dependeriwa, uka persianas venecianas ukanakaw lurasi yaqha chiqanakanx mä juk’a arsutawa. Uka ch’akhanakan thiyanakapax ñik’utan fibras ukanakampiw muyuntata, uka tuqiw Ballena Derecha ukax umax ch’allt’i, ukat uka ch’akhanakanx jisk’a challwanakaruw katxaru, laka jist’arat ukhax manq’añ pachanx brit qutanak taypinw saraski. Chʼakhanakan chika persiananakapanjja, kunjamtï ordenaparjam saytʼatäki ukhama, yaqhep muspharkañ marcanaka, kurvanaka, chʼusawjanaka ukat chhullunkhanakaw utji, ukanwa yaqhep ballenanak katoqerinakajj uka animalan marap jaktʼapjje, mä encina sat animalan maraparjamaw muyu anillonakapampi jaktʼapjje. Uka criterion chiqapätapax janiw uñacht’ayañjamäkiti, ukampirus sabor de probabilidad analógica ukampiw utji. Kunjamäskpasa, uka toqet jay sapjjañäni ukhajja, nayrïr uñtañat sipansa jukʼamp jachʼa ballenaruw churañasa, ukajj walikïkaspas ukhamawa.

Nayra tiemponjja, uka juykhunakat jukʼamp yatiñ munir amuyunakajj utjpachänwa. Purchas markankir mä viajerojja, ballenan laka manqhankir muspharkañ “barba” sasaw säna;* maynïristi, “khuchhinakan cerdanakapa” sasaw säna; Hackluyt markankir kimsïr jilïr señorax aka suma arunak apnaqi: “Ala pata ch’uqipan sapa ladopanx niya pä patak phisqa tunka aletanakaw jilaski , ukax lakapan sapa ladopanx lakap patxaruw arco uchasi”.

Ukampis aka particular tuqinxa, demandax nayratpachaw juk’amp jisk’achasiwayi. Ana reina tiemponwa uka chʼakhajj jachʼañchatäjjäna, uka tiemponjja, farthingale sat manqʼajj taqe modapunïnwa. Ukat kunjamtï uka nayra damas ukanakax wali kusisitaw sarnaqapxirïna, ballena sat animalan lakapansa, kunjamtï jumax siskta ukhama; ukhamakipans, mä ducha ukanx, ukham jan amuyt’asis, jichhürunakanx pachpa jawq’a manqhan t’ijtxtan jark’aqasiñataki; paraguas ukax pachpa ch’akha patxar ch’allt’at carpa ukhamawa.

Ukampis jichhax mä juk’a pachatakix juykhunakatsa, ñik’utanakatsa taqi kuns armasim, ukat Ballena Derecha ukan lakapan sayt’asisaw wasitat muyuntat uñakipt’añama. Taqi uka ch’akhanakax wali metódicamente jayarst’at uñjasax, ¿janit jach’a Haarlem órgano manqhankasma, ukat waranq tubonakapar uñch’ukiskasmas ukham amuyasma? Ere this, jumanakax qhan uñjapxpachäta kunatix nayax qalltawaykta ukax chiqaw sañ muni —Ballena Esperma ukat Ballena Derecha ukax niya taqpach mayj mayj p’iqinipxiwa. Ukhamarusa, mä juk’a arumpixa: Ballena Derecha ukanxa janiwa jach’a phuju espermatozoides utjkiti; janiw marfil kisunakax utjkänti; janiw jach’a, jisk’a jawq’an mandibula jisk’a, Ballena Esperma ukar uñtasita. Janirakiw Ballena Esperma ukanx uka ch’akha juykhunakax utjkiti; janiw jach’a jisk’a lakax utjkiti; ukat juk’akiw mä laxrax utji. Maysatxa, Ballena Derecha ukax pä anqäx esputo-holes ukaniwa, Ballena Esperma ukax mäkiw utji.

Qhipa uñakipt’apxam, jichhax, aka jach’añchat p’iqinak encapuchata, kunapachatix wali mayacht’asis ikipki ukhaxa; kunattix maynix niyaw qutar chhaqtxani, jan qillqatäki; maynix janiw sinti jaya pachanak arktañapakiti.

¿Ukan utjki uka Ballena Esperma ukan uñacht’äwip katjasmati? Uka pachparakiw jupax jiwatayna, nayraqatapan mä qawqha jach’a arrugas ukanakakiw jichhax chhaqtawaykaspas ukhama. Yes, it was a running delivery, so it was.

Now, had Tashtego perished in that head, it had been a very precious perishing; smothered in the very whitest and daintiest of fragrant spermaceti; coffined, hearsed, and tombed in the secret inner chamber and sanctum sanctorum of the whale. Only one sweeter end can readily be recalled—the delicious death of an Ohio honey-hunter, who seeking honey in the crotch of a hollow tree, found such exceeding store of it, that leaning too far over, it sucked him in, so that he died embalmed. How many, think ye, have likewise fallen into Plato's honey head, and sweetly perished there?





CHAPTER 79. The Prairie.

To scan the lines of his face, or feel the bumps on the head of this Leviathan; this is a thing which no Physiognomist or Phrenologist has as yet undertaken. Such an enterprise would seem almost as hopeful as for Lavater to have scrutinized the wrinkles on the Rock of Gibraltar, or for Gall to have mounted a ladder and manipulated the Dome of the Pantheon. Still, in that famous work of his, Lavater not only treats of the various faces of men, but also attentively studies the faces of horses, birds, serpents, and fish; and dwells in detail upon the modifications of expression discernible therein. Nor have Gall and his disciple Spurzheim failed to throw out some hints touching the phrenological characteristics of other beings than man. Therefore, though I am but ill qualified for a pioneer, in the application of these two semi-sciences to the whale, I will do my endeavor. I try all things; I achieve what I can.

Physiognomically regarded, the Sperm Whale is an anomalous creature. He has no proper nose. And since the nose is the central and most conspicuous of the features; and since it perhaps most modifies and finally controls their combined expression; hence it would seem that its entire absence, as an external appendage, must very largely affect the countenance of the whale. For as in landscape gardening, a spire, cupola, monument, or tower of some sort, is deemed almost indispensable to the completion of the scene; so no face can be physiognomically in keeping without the elevated open-work belfry of the nose. Dash the nose from Phidias's marble Jove, and what a sorry remainder! Nevertheless, Leviathan is of so mighty a magnitude, all his proportions are so stately, that the same deficiency which in the sculptured Jove were hideous, in him is no blemish at all. Nay, it is an added grandeur. A nose to the whale would have been impertinent. As on your physiognomical voyage you sail round his vast head in your jolly-boat, your noble conceptions of him are never insulted by the reflection that he has a nose to be pulled. A pestilent conceit, which so often will insist upon obtruding even when beholding the mightiest royal beadle on his throne.

In some particulars, perhaps the most imposing physiognomical view to be had of the Sperm Whale, is that of the full front of his head. This aspect is sublime.

In thought, a fine human brow is like the East when troubled with the morning. In the repose of the pasture, the curled brow of the bull has a touch of the grand in it. Pushing heavy cannon up mountain defiles, the elephant's brow is majestic. Human or animal, the mystical brow is as that great golden seal affixed by the German emperors to their decrees. It signifies—“God: done this day by my hand.” But in most creatures, nay in man himself, very often the brow is but a mere strip of alpine land lying along the snow line. Few are the foreheads which like Shakespeare's or Melancthon's rise so high, and descend so low, that the eyes themselves seem clear, eternal, tideless mountain lakes; and all above them in the forehead's wrinkles, you seem to track the antlered thoughts descending there to drink, as the Highland hunters track the snow prints of the deer. But in the great Sperm Whale, this high and mighty god-like dignity inherent in the brow is so immensely amplified, that gazing on it, in that full front view, you feel the Deity and the dread powers more forcibly than in beholding any other object in living nature. For you see no one point precisely; not one distinct feature is revealed; no nose, eyes, ears, or mouth; no face; he has none, proper; nothing but that one broad firmament of a forehead, pleated with riddles; dumbly lowering with the doom of boats, and ships, and men. Nor, in profile, does this wondrous brow diminish; though that way viewed its grandeur does not domineer upon you so. In profile, you plainly perceive that horizontal, semi-crescentic depression in the forehead's middle, which, in man, is Lavater's mark of genius.

But how? Genius in the Sperm Whale? Has the Sperm Whale ever written a book, spoken a speech? No, his great genius is declared in his doing nothing particular to prove it. It is moreover declared in his pyramidical silence. And this reminds me that had the great Sperm Whale been known to the young Orient World, he would have been deified by their child-magian thoughts. They deified the crocodile of the Nile, because the crocodile is tongueless; and the Sperm Whale has no tongue, or at least it is so exceedingly small, as to be incapable of protrusion. If hereafter any highly cultured, poetical nation shall lure back to their birth-right, the merry May-day gods of old; and livingly enthrone them again in the now egotistical sky; in the now unhaunted hill; then be sure, exalted to Jove's high seat, the great Sperm Whale shall lord it.

Champollion deciphered the wrinkled granite hieroglyphics. But there is no Champollion to decipher the Egypt of every man's and every being's face. Physiognomy, like every other human science, is but a passing fable. If then, Sir William Jones, who read in thirty languages, could not read the simplest peasant's face in its profounder and more subtle meanings, how may unlettered Ishmael hope to read the awful Chaldee of the Sperm Whale's brow? I but put that brow before you. Read it if you can.





CHAPTER 80. The Nut.

If the Sperm Whale be physiognomically a Sphinx, to the phrenologist his brain seems that geometrical circle which it is impossible to square.

In the full-grown creature the skull will measure at least twenty feet in length. Unhinge the lower jaw, and the side view of this skull is as the side of a moderately inclined plane resting throughout on a level base. But in life—as we have elsewhere seen—this inclined plane is angularly filled up, and almost squared by the enormous superincumbent mass of the junk and sperm. At the high end the skull forms a crater to bed that part of the mass; while under the long floor of this crater—in another cavity seldom exceeding ten inches in length and as many in depth—reposes the mere handful of this monster's brain. The brain is at least twenty feet from his apparent forehead in life; it is hidden away behind its vast outworks, like the innermost citadel within the amplified fortifications of Quebec. So like a choice casket is it secreted in him, that I have known some whalemen who peremptorily deny that the Sperm Whale has any other brain than that palpable semblance of one formed by the cubic-yards of his sperm magazine. Lying in strange folds, courses, and convolutions, to their apprehensions, it seems more in keeping with the idea of his general might to regard that mystic part of him as the seat of his intelligence.

It is plain, then, that phrenologically the head of this Leviathan, in the creature's living intact state, is an entire delusion. As for his true brain, you can then see no indications of it, nor feel any. The whale, like all things that are mighty, wears a false brow to the common world.

If you unload his skull of its spermy heaps and then take a rear view of its rear end, which is the high end, you will be struck by its resemblance to the human skull, beheld in the same situation, and from the same point of view. Indeed, place this reversed skull (scaled down to the human magnitude) among a plate of men's skulls, and you would involuntarily confound it with them; and remarking the depressions on one part of its summit, in phrenological phrase you would say—This man had no self-esteem, and no veneration. And by those negations, considered along with the affirmative fact of his prodigious bulk and power, you can best form to yourself the truest, though not the most exhilarating conception of what the most exalted potency is.

But if from the comparative dimensions of the whale's proper brain, you deem it incapable of being adequately charted, then I have another idea for you. If you attentively regard almost any quadruped's spine, you will be struck with the resemblance of its vertebræ to a strung necklace of dwarfed skulls, all bearing rudimental resemblance to the skull proper. It is a German conceit, that the vertebræ are absolutely undeveloped skulls. But the curious external resemblance, I take it the Germans were not the first men to perceive. A foreign friend once pointed it out to me, in the skeleton of a foe he had slain, and with the vertebræ of which he was inlaying, in a sort of basso-relievo, the beaked prow of his canoe. Now, I consider that the phrenologists have omitted an important thing in not pushing their investigations from the cerebellum through the spinal canal. For I believe that much of a man's character will be found betokened in his backbone. I would rather feel your spine than your skull, whoever you are. A thin joist of a spine never yet upheld a full and noble soul. I rejoice in my spine, as in the firm audacious staff of that flag which I fling half out to the world.

Apply this spinal branch of phrenology to the Sperm Whale. His cranial cavity is continuous with the first neck-vertebra; and in that vertebra the bottom of the spinal canal will measure ten inches across, being eight in height, and of a triangular figure with the base downwards. As it passes through the remaining vertebræ the canal tapers in size, but for a considerable distance remains of large capacity. Now, of course, this canal is filled with much the same strangely fibrous substance—the spinal cord—as the brain; and directly communicates with the brain. And what is still more, for many feet after emerging from the brain's cavity, the spinal cord remains of an undecreasing girth, almost equal to that of the brain. Under all these circumstances, would it be unreasonable to survey and map out the whale's spine phrenologically? For, viewed in this light, the wonderful comparative smallness of his brain proper is more than compensated by the wonderful comparative magnitude of his spinal cord.

But leaving this hint to operate as it may with the phrenologists, I would merely assume the spinal theory for a moment, in reference to the Sperm Whale's hump. This august hump, if I mistake not, rises over one of the larger vertebræ, and is, therefore, in some sort, the outer convex mould of it. From its relative situation then, I should call this high hump the organ of firmness or indomitableness in the Sperm Whale. And that the great monster is indomitable, you will yet have reason to know.
