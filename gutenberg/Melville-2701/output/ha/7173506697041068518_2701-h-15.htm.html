BABI NA 80. The Nut.

Idan maniyyi Whale ya zama physiognomically Sphinx, ga phrenologist kwakwalwarsa alama da geometrical da'irar wanda ba shi yiwuwa a square.

A cikin cikakkiyar halitta kwanyar zai auna akalla ƙafa ashirin a tsayi. Cire muƙamuƙi na ƙasa, kuma ra'ayin gefen wannan kwanyar yana matsayin gefen jirgin sama mai matsakaicin karkata wanda ke hutawa a ko'ina a kan matakin tushe. Amma a rayuwa—kamar yadda muka gani a wasu wurare—wannan jirgin sama mai karkata ya cika sama da kusurwa, kuma yana kusan murabba'i da babban ɗimbin tarkace da maniyyi. A babban ƙarshen kwanyar ta samar da wani rami zuwa gado wanda ɓangaren taro; yayin da yake ƙarƙashin dogon bene na wannan rami-a cikin wani rami ba kasafai ba ya wuce inci goma tsawonsa da yawa a zurfi-yana dawo da dan kadan na wannan dodo. Kwakwalwa tana da akalla taku ashirin daga gabban goshinsa a rayuwa; yana ɓoye a bayan faffadan ayyukansa, kamar babban katanga na ciki a cikin ingantattun katangar Quebec. Don haka kamar akwatin akwatin zaɓaɓɓen an ɓoye a cikinsa, da na san wasu masu kifin kifin da suke ƙaryata cewa Maniyyi Whale yana da wata ƙwaƙwalwa fiye da irin wannan siffa ta nau'in yadudduka na mujallar maniyyinsa. Kwance a cikin bakuwar folds, darussa, da juzu'i, ga fargabarsu, ya fi dacewa da ra'ayin ƙarfinsa na gaba ɗaya don ɗaukar wannan ɓangaren sufanci a matsayin wurin basirarsa.

A bayyane yake cewa shugaban wannan Leviathan, a cikin yanayin rayuwar halitta gabaɗayan ruɗi ne. Amma game da kwakwalwarsa na gaskiya, ba za ka iya ganin alamunta ba, kuma ba za ka ji komai ba. Whale, kamar duk abubuwan da suke da ƙarfi, suna sanye da ɓarnar ƙarya ga duniyar gama gari.

Idan ka sauke kwanyarsa na tulin maniyyinsa, sannan ka kalli karshensa na baya, wanda shi ne babban karshensa, za a same ka da kamanninsa da kwanyar mutum, ana ganinsa a cikin yanayi guda, kuma ta fuska daya. Lalle ne, ka sanya wannan kwanyar da aka juyar da ita a cikin farantin ƙoƙon kan mutane, kuma kana rikita shi da su ba da gangan ba. da kuma yin la'akari da baƙin ciki a wani ɓangare na taron kolin, a cikin phrenological jimla za ku ce-Wannan mutumin ba shi da girman kai, kuma ba shi da daraja. Kuma ta waɗancan shawarwarin, waɗanda aka yi la’akari da su tare da tabbataccen gaskiyar girman girmansa da ikonsa, zaku iya samar da kanku mafi kyawun gaskiya, kodayake ba ra'ayi mai ban sha'awa ba game da abin da mafi girman iko yake.

Amma idan daga ma'auni na daidaitattun kwakwalwar whale, kuna ganin ba za a iya tsara shi ba, to ina da wani ra'ayi a gare ku. Idan ka lura da kusan kowane kashin baya mai rubu'i, za'a buga maka da kamannin kashin bayansa zuwa wani abin wuya na dwarfd kwanyar, duk yana da kamanceceniya da kwanyar daidai. Yana da girman kai na Jamus, cewa vertebræs ba su da cikakken skulls. Amma kamanni na waje mai ban sha'awa, na ɗauka cewa Jamusawa ba su ne farkon mazan da suka gane ba. Wani abokin na waje ya taba nuna min shi, a cikin kwarangwal din abokin gaba da ya kashe, da kashin bayansa da ya ke sakawa, a cikin wani irin basso-relievo, bakar kwale-kwalen nasa. Yanzu, na yi la'akari da cewa phrenologists sun bar wani muhimmin abu a cikin rashin tura binciken su daga cerebellum ta hanyar kashin baya. Domin na yi imani da cewa mafi yawan halayen mutum za a same shi a cikin kashin bayansa. Na gwammace in ji kashin bayanku fiye da kwanyar ku, ko wanene kai. Wani siririn dunƙule na kashin baya bai taɓa ɗaukar cikakken rai mai daraja ba tukuna. Ina murna da kashin bayana, kamar a cikin ƙwaƙƙwaran ma'aikatan wannan tuta wanda na jera rabin zuwa duniya.

Aiwatar da wannan reshe na kashin baya na phrenology zuwa maniyyi Whale. Ƙwajin cranial ɗinsa yana ci gaba tare da wuyansa na farko-vertebra; kuma a cikin wannan kashin baya kasan canal na kashin baya zai auna inci goma a fadin, yana da tsayi takwas, kuma mai siffar triangular tare da tushe zuwa ƙasa. Yayin da yake wucewa ta cikin ragowar ɓangarorin magudanar ruwa mai girma, amma ga nisa mai nisa ya rage girman iko. Yanzu, ba shakka, wannan magudanar ruwa yana cike da wani abu mai ban mamaki na fibrous-kashin baya-kamar kwakwalwa; kuma kai tsaye yana sadarwa da kwakwalwa. Kuma abin da ya fi haka, ga ƙafafu da yawa bayan fitowar su daga kogon kwakwalwa, kashin baya ya kasance na guntun da ba ya raguwa, kusan daidai da na kwakwalwa. A cikin duk waɗannan yanayi, zai zama rashin hankali don bincika da kuma tsara taswirar kashin baya na whale ta hanyar phrenologically? Domin, idan aka duba ta cikin wannan haske, ƙaramin kwatancen kwakwalwarsa mai ban sha'awa ya fi ramawa da girma mai ban mamaki na kashin bayansa.

Amma barin wannan alamar don yin aiki kamar yadda zai yiwu tare da masu ilimin phrenologists, zan ɗauki ka'idar kashin baya na ɗan lokaci, dangane da hump na maniyyi Whale. Wannan watan Agusta hump, idan na kuskure ba, yakan kan daya daga cikin ya fi girma vertebræ, kuma shi ne, sabili da haka, a wasu irin, m convex mold da shi. Daga halin da ake ciki to, ya kamata in kira wannan babban hump sashin ƙarfi ko rashin ƙarfi a cikin maniyyi Whale. Kuma cewa babban dodo ba shi da iyaka, har yanzu za ku sami dalilin sani.
