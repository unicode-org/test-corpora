BABI NA 55. Na Manyan Hotunan Whales.

Zan yi muku dogon fenti kamar yadda mutum zai iya ba tare da zane ba, wani abu mai kama da ainihin nau'in whale kamar yadda ya bayyana a idon mashin whale yayin da yake cikin cikakkiyar jikinsa kifin yana murɗa tare da jirgin ruwa don haka. Ana iya taka shi da kyau a wurin. Yana iya zama da amfani a baya, don tallata wa annan hotunan nasa masu ban sha'awa waɗanda har zuwa yau suna ƙalubalantar bangaskiyar mai gidan. Lokaci ya yi da za a daidaita duniya a cikin wannan al'amari, ta hanyar tabbatar da irin waɗannan hotuna na whale duk kuskure.

Wataƙila za a sami tushen asalin waɗannan ruɗaɗɗen hoto a cikin tsoffin sassaka na Hindoo, Masarawa, da na Girka. Tun daga waɗancan lokuta masu ƙirƙira amma waɗanda ba su da kyau lokacin da a kan ƙofofin marmara na haikalin, ginshiƙan mutum-mutumi, da garkuwoyi, lambobin yabo, kofuna, da tsabar kudi, an zana dabbar dolphin cikin ma'auni na sarƙoƙi kamar na Saladin, da hular kwalkwali kamar. St. George's; tun daga wannan lokacin akwai wani nau'in lasisi iri ɗaya ya rinjayi, ba kawai a cikin shahararrun hotuna na whale ba, amma a yawancin gabatarwar kimiyya game da shi.

Yanzu, bisa ga dukkan alamu, hoton da ya fi dadewa da ake cewa shi ne na whale, ana samunsa a cikin sanannen kogon-pagoda na Elephanta, a Indiya. Brahmins suna kula da cewa a cikin sassaƙaƙƙun sassaƙaƙe na waccan Pagoda na da dadewa, duk sana'o'i da abubuwan nema, duk wani yunƙurin da mutum zai yi, an riga an kwatanta shi da shekaru kafin ɗayansu ya kasance. Don haka, ba mamaki, da a ce a wani irin sana'ar mu mai daraja ta kifin kifin da yakamata ta kasance a cikin inuwa. Hindoo whale da ake magana a kai, yana faruwa a wani sashe daban na bangon, yana nuna jikin Vishnu a cikin sigar leviathan, wanda aka sani da Matse Avatar. Amma ko da yake wannan sassaƙaƙƙen rabin mutum ne da rabin whale, don kawai don ba da wutsiya na ƙarshen, duk da haka ƙananan ɓangarensa duk kuskure ne. Yayi kama da wutsiyar anaconda, fiye da faffadan tafin hannun manyan ƙwanƙolin kifin kifi na gaskiya.

Amma ku je tsohon Hotuna, ku duba yanzu ku kalli hoton wannan kifin mai girma mai zane; domin bai yi nasara ba fiye da Hindoo antiluvian. Hoton Guido ne na Perseus yana ceto Andromeda daga dodo ko kifi. A ina Guido ya sami samfurin irin wannan baƙon halitta? Haka kuma Hogarth, a cikin zana irin wannan yanayin a cikin nasa "Perseus Descending," ya fitar da mafi kyau. Babban dodo na wannan dodo na Hogarthian yana buɗewa a saman ƙasa, da kyar ya zana inci ɗaya na ruwa. Tana da wata irin howdah a bayanta, kuma bakinta da ba a kwance ba wanda buguwa ke birgima a ciki, ana iya kai shi zuwa Ƙofar Traitors da ke bi ta Thames ta ruwa zuwa Hasumiyar. Sannan, akwai kifin kifin Prodromus na tsohon Scotch Sibbald, da Yunana ta whale, kamar yadda aka kwatanta a cikin kwafin tsoffin Littafi Mai-Tsarki da yanke tsofaffin filaye. Me za a ce game da waɗannan? Amma ga kifin kifin littafi mai ɗaure mai jujjuyawa kamar itacen inabi zagaye da ginshiƙin anka mai saukowa-kamar yadda aka yi tambari da gilded a bayan baya da shafuffuka na littattafai da yawa na tsoho da sababbi—wato wata halitta ce mai kyan gani amma zalla ta ban mamaki. kwaikwayi, na dauka, daga irin su Figures on tsoho vases. Ko da yake a duk duniya ana kiransa dabbar dolphin, duk da haka na kira wannan kifin-binder's kifin ƙoƙari na kifin kifi; saboda an yi niyya ne lokacin da aka fara ƙaddamar da na'urar. Wani tsohon ɗan ƙasar Italiya ne ya gabatar da shi a wani wuri kusan ƙarni na 15, lokacin Farfaɗowar Koyo; kuma a wancan zamani, har ma zuwa wani lokaci na kwatankwacin lokaci, dabbar dolphins an fi saninta su zama jinsin Leviathan.

A cikin vignettes da sauran kayan ado na wasu litattafai na daɗaɗɗen za ku iya saduwa da su a wasu lokuta tare da sha'awar sha'awa a cikin whale, inda kowane nau'i na spouts, jets d'eau, ruwan zafi da sanyi, Saratoga da Baden-Baden, suna fitowa daga nasa. kwakwalwar da ba ta gajiyawa. A cikin taken-shafi na ainihin bugu na "ci gaban koyo" za ku sami wasu kifin kifi masu ban sha'awa.

Amma barin duk waɗannan yunƙurin da ba na fasaha ba, bari mu kalli waɗannan hotuna na leviathan da ke cewa suna da hankali, ƙayyadaddun kimiyya, ta waɗanda suka sani. A cikin tsohon tarin tafiye-tafiye na Harris akwai wasu faranti na whale da aka samo daga littafin tafiye-tafiye na Dutch, AD 1671, mai taken "Tafiya zuwa Spitzbergen a cikin jirgin ruwa Jonas a cikin Whale, Peter Peterson na Friesland, maigida." A cikin ɗaya daga cikin waɗannan faranti, whales, kamar manyan raƙuman katako, ana wakilta suna kwance a tsakanin tsibiran kankara, tare da farar beyar da ke gudana akan bayan rayuwarsu. A cikin wani farantin karfe, an yi babban kuskuren da ke wakiltar kifin kifi tare da fulawa a kai tsaye.

Sa'an nan kuma, akwai ƙaƙƙarfan kwata-kwata, wanda wani Kyaftin Colnett, wani Kaftin na Post Captain a cikin sojojin ruwa na Ingilishi ya rubuta, mai take "A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, don manufar tsawaita kamun kifi na Spermaceti Whale." A cikin wannan littafin akwai jita-jita da ke cewa ya zama "Hoton Physeter ko Spermaceti Whale, wanda aka zana da sikeli daga wanda aka kashe a bakin tekun Mexico, Agusta, 1793, kuma aka ɗaga sama a kan bene." Ina shakkar ba kyaftin din ya dauki wannan hoton na gaskiya ba don amfanin sojojin ruwa. In ambata amma abu ɗaya game da shi, bari in ce yana da idon da aka shafa, bisa ga ma'aunin da ke tare da shi, zuwa ga cikakken kifin kifin da ya girma, zai sa idon wannan kifin ya zama tagar baka mai tsawon ƙafa biyar. Kai, babban kyaftin, me ya sa ba ka ba mu Yunusa yana kallon wannan ido ba!

Haka nan kuma ba a haɗa mafi ƙwazo na Tarihin Halitta don amfanin matasa da masu taushi ba, waɗanda ba su da irin wannan mugunyar kuskure. Dubi wannan sanannen aikin "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." A cikin bugu na London na 1807 da aka taƙaita, akwai faranti na zargin "whale" da "narwhale." Ba na fatan ganin kamar mara kyau, amma wannan kifin kifi mara kyan gani yayi kama da shuka da aka yanke; kuma, game da narwhale, hangen nesa daya isa ya ba mutum mamaki, cewa a cikin wannan karni na sha tara irin wannan hippogriff na iya zama dabino da gaske akan duk wani hazikin jama'a na 'yan makaranta.

Sa'an nan kuma, a cikin 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, babban masanin halitta, ya buga littafin kimiya na kifaye, wanda a cikinsa akwai hotuna da dama na nau'in Leviathan. Duk waɗannan ba daidai ba ne kawai, amma hoton Mysticetus ko Greenland Whale (wato, Whale Dama), har ma Scoresby, wani mutum mai dadewa da ya taɓa wannan nau'in, ya bayyana cewa ba shi da takwarorinsa a cikin yanayi.

Amma sanya hular-sheaf ga duk wannan kasuwancin da ba a sani ba an keɓe shi don masanin kimiyya Frederick Cuvier, ɗan'uwan sanannen Baron. A shekara ta 1836, ya buga wani Tarihin Halitta na Whales, inda ya ba da abin da ya kira hoton maniyyi Whale. Kafin nuna wannan hoton ga kowane Nantucketer, kuna da mafi kyawun tanadi don taƙaitawar ku daga Nantucket. A cikin kalma, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale ba maniyyi Whale ba ne, amma squash. Tabbas, bai taɓa samun fa'idar tafiya ta whaling ba (irin waɗannan mutane ba safai ba ne), amma daga ina ya samo wannan hoton, wa zai iya faɗa? Watakila ya samu ne a matsayin magabacinsa na kimiyya a wannan fanni, Desmarest, ya samu daya daga cikin ingantattun zubar da ciki; wato daga zanen kasar Sin. Kuma wane irin samari ne masu fensir na Sinawa, kofuna masu yawa da miya suna sanar da mu.

Dangane da kifin kifin da aka gani a tituna suna rataye a kan shagunan dilolin mai, me za a ce a kansu? Yawanci sune Richard III. Whales, tare da ƙwanƙwasa ƙwanƙwasa, kuma masu banƙyama; suna yin karin kumallo a kan jirgin ruwa uku ko huɗu, wato kwale-kwalen ruwa cike da ma'aikatan ruwa: nakasarsu tana yawo cikin tekun jini da shuɗi.

Amma waɗannan kura-kurai da yawa a cikin kwatanta kifin ba abin mamaki ba ne sosai. Yi la'akari! Yawancin zane-zane na kimiyya an ɗauko su ne daga kifin da aka makale; kuma waɗannan sun kusan daidai kamar zanen jirgin ruwa da aka karye, tare da karyewar baya, daidai yake wakiltar dabbar dabbar da kanta a cikin duk girman girman da ba a taɓa gani ba. Ko da yake giwaye sun tsaya tsayin daka, Leviathan mai rai bai taɓa shawagi ba tukuna don hotonsa. Whale mai rai, a cikin cikakken girmansa da muhimmancinsa, kawai ana ganinsa a cikin teku a cikin ruwan da ba a iya ganewa; Kuma mafi yawansa ba a gani, kamar jirgin da aka harba. Kuma daga cikin wannan abu abu ne da ba zai taɓa yiwuwa mutum mai-mutuwa ya ɗaga shi cikin iska a jiki ba, domin ya kiyaye dukan kumbura da ɓarnansa. Kuma, ba don yin magana game da bambancin da ake tsammani na kwane-kwane ba tsakanin matashin tsotsa kifin kifi da cikakken Lewithan Platonian; duk da haka, ko da a cikin ɗaya daga cikin waɗancan kifin kifi masu shan nono da aka ɗaga a kan jirgin ruwa, irin wannan shi ne baƙon, mai kama da ƙwai, mai raɗaɗi, bambancin siffarsa, wanda ainihin yanayinsa shaidan ya kasa kamawa.

Amma ana iya hasashe, cewa daga tsiraicin kwarangwal na whale, ana iya samun ingantattun alamu da ke taɓa sifarsa ta gaskiya. Ba komai. Domin yana ɗaya daga cikin abubuwan ban sha'awa game da wannan Leviathan, cewa kwarangwal ɗinsa yana ba da ra'ayi kaɗan game da siffarsa gaba ɗaya. Ko da yake kwarangwal na Jeremy Bentham, wanda ke rataye don candelabra a cikin ɗakin karatu na ɗaya daga cikin masu zartar da shi, daidai yana ba da ra'ayin tsohon mai amfani da burly-browed, tare da sauran manyan halayen Jeremy; Duk da haka ba za a iya gane wani abu irin wannan daga kowane kasusuwan lefitan ba. A gaskiya ma, kamar yadda babban mafarauci ya ce, kwarangwal na whale yana da alaƙa iri ɗaya da cikakkiyar jari da dabba kamar yadda kwarin ke yi wa chrysalis wanda ya lulluɓe shi sosai. Wannan keɓantaccen abu yana bayyana a cikin kai, kamar yadda a wasu ɓangaren wannan littafin za a nuna kwatsam. Hakanan ana nuna shi da ban sha'awa a cikin gefen fin, wanda kusan ƙasusuwansu suna amsa ƙasusuwan hannun ɗan adam, ban da babban yatsan yatsa kawai. Wannan fin yana da yatsun kashi huɗu na yau da kullun, fihirisa, tsakiya, zobe, da ɗan yatsa. Amma duk waɗannan suna dawwama a cikin mayafinsu na nama, kamar yatsun ɗan adam a cikin suturar wucin gadi. "Duk da haka cikin rashin hankali whale na iya yi mana hidima a wasu lokuta," in ji Stubb mai ban dariya wata rana, "ba za a taɓa cewa da gaske ya ɗauke mu ba tare da mittens ba."

Domin duk waɗannan dalilai, to, kowace hanya za ku iya duba ta, dole ne ku kammala cewa babban Leviathan shine halitta ɗaya a cikin duniya wanda dole ne ya kasance ba tare da fenti ba har zuwa ƙarshe. Gaskiya ne, hoto ɗaya na iya buga alamar kusa da wani, amma babu wanda zai iya buga shi da kowane madaidaicin matsayi. Don haka babu wata hanyar duniya ta gano ainihin yadda kifin kifi ya yi kama. Kuma kawai yanayin a cikin abin da za ka iya samun ko da wani jurewa ra'ayin ya rai kwane-kwane, shi ne ta hanyar zuwa wani whaling kanka; amma ta yin haka, ba ƙaramin haɗarin zama murhu na har abada ba kuma ya nutse da shi. Don haka, ga alama a gare ni da bai kamata ku kasance da himma wajen sha'awar wannan Lewitan ba.





BABI NA 56. Na Hotunan Ƙananan Kuskure na Whales, da Hotunan Gaskiya na Whaling Scenes.

A hade tare da hotuna masu ban mamaki na whales, an jarabce ni a nan don in shigar da waɗancan labarun da suka fi girma a cikin wasu littattafai, na da da na zamani, musamman a Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, da dai sauransu Amma na wuce wannan al'amari.

Na san kawai hudu da aka buga shaci na babban maniyyi Whale; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, da na Beale's. A cikin babin da ya gabata an kira Colnett da Cuvier. Huggins's ya fi nasu kyau sosai; amma, ta babban rashin daidaito, Beale's shine mafi kyau. Duk zane-zane na Beale na wannan whale suna da kyau, ban da matsakaicin adadi a cikin hoton whales uku a cikin halaye daban-daban, yana ɗaukar babi na biyu. Gaban sa na gaba, kwale-kwalen da ke kai hari kan maniyyi Whales, ko da yake ba shakka an ƙididdige shi don tada shakkun jama'a na wasu mazajen falo, abin sha'awa daidai ne kuma mai kama da rayuwa a cikin tasirinsa gaba ɗaya. Wasu daga cikin zane-zanen maniyyi Whale a cikin J. Ross Browne suna da kyau daidai a kwane-kwane; amma sun yi tir da an rubuta su. Wannan ba laifinsa bane.

Na Dama Whale, mafi kyawun zane-zane suna cikin Scoresby; amma an zana su akan ma'auni kaɗan don nuna kyawawa. Yana da hoto ɗaya kawai na wuraren whaling, kuma wannan rashi ne na baƙin ciki, saboda ta irin waɗannan hotuna ne kawai, idan an yi kyau sosai, zaku iya samun wani abu kamar ra'ayi na gaskiya na kifin mai rai kamar yadda mafarautansa suka gani.

Amma, wanda aka ɗauka gaba ɗaya, mafi kyawun mafi kyau, kodayake a cikin wasu cikakkun bayanai ba mafi daidai ba, gabatarwar whales da wuraren whaling da za a same su a ko'ina, manyan zane-zanen Faransa ne guda biyu, waɗanda aka kashe da kyau, kuma an ɗauke su daga zane-zane ta Garnery ɗaya. Bi da bi, suna wakiltar hare-hare akan maniyyi da dama Whale. A cikin zanen farko na wani maniyyi mai daraja Whale an kwatanta shi da cikakken girman ƙarfi, ya taso a ƙarƙashin jirgin daga ɓangarorin teku, kuma yana ɗauke da iska a bayansa babban tarkace na katako. Ƙwararren jirgin ruwan ba ya karye, kuma an zana shi kawai yana daidaita kan kashin bayan dodo; Kuma a tsaye a cikin wannan firgici, don wannan walƙiya guda ɗaya da ba za a iya kwatantawa ba, sai ka ga wani ma'aikacin jirgin ruwa, rabinsa ya lulluɓe da tafasasshen kifin kifi mai zafi, yana tsalle, kamar daga wani tudu. Ayyukan duka abu ne mai ban mamaki mai kyau da gaskiya. Kwancen layin da aka kwashe rabin-rabi yana yawo a kan tekun da aka yi fari; sandunan katako na harpoons da aka zubar ba su da kyau a cikinsa; shugabannin ma'aikatan wasan iyo suna warwatse game da whale a cikin sabanin maganganun tsoro; yayin da a cikin baƙar guguwar tazara jirgin yana ɗora kan wurin. Za a iya samun kuskure mai tsanani tare da cikakkun bayanai na jikin kifin kifi, amma bari wannan ya wuce; tun da, ga rayuwata, ba zan iya zana mai kyau haka ba.

A cikin zane-zane na biyu, jirgin yana cikin aikin zane tare da gaɓar gefen wani babban Whale mai gudu mai gudana, wanda ke jujjuya yawancin ciyayi a cikin teku kamar wani dutsen dutsen dutsen Patagonian. Jirginsa a tsaye, cike, kuma baƙar fata kamar zomo; ta yadda daga yawan hayaki a cikin bututun hayaki, za ku yi tunanin dole ne a yi jifa-jifa na dafa abinci a cikin babban hanji a ƙasa. Tsuntsaye na teku suna kallon ƙananan kaguwa, kifi-kifi, da sauran alewa na teku da macaroni, waɗanda Dama Whale a wasu lokuta yakan ɗauka a bayansa. Kuma duk lokacin da Leviathan mai kauri yana ta cikin zurfin zurfin, yana barin tarin farar ƙudan zuma a farkensa, kuma ya sa ɗan ƙaramin kwale-kwalen ya girgiza cikin kumbura kamar ƙwanƙolin da aka kama kusa da takalmi na tudun teku. Don haka, gaba-gaba duk tashin hankali ne; amma a baya, cikin ban sha'awa na zane-zane, matakin teku mai gilashin ya nutsu, da faɗuwar jiragen ruwa marasa ƙarfi na jirgin da ba shi da ƙarfi, da kuma yawan mataccen kifin kifi, wani sansanin soja da aka ci nasara, tare da tutar kamawa a rataye a rataye a kan whale- sanda aka saka shi a cikin raminsa.

Wanene Garnery mai zane, ko ya kasance, ban sani ba. Amma rayuwata don haka ko dai ya kasance yana tattaunawa da batunsa, ko kuma da wani gogaggen ƙwararrun ƙwararru. Faransawa sune samari don aikin zanen. Ku je ku kalli dukkan zane-zane na Turai, kuma a ina za ku sami irin wannan hoton na rayuwa da hayaniya a kan zane, kamar yadda yake a wannan zauren nasara a Versailles; inda mai kallo ya yi yaƙi da hanyarsa, pell-mell, ta manyan yaƙe-yaƙe na Faransa a jere; Inda kowane takobi ya zama kamar walƙiya na Hasken Arewa, kuma sarakuna masu ɗauke da makamai da sarakunan da suka biyo baya suna tafe, kamar cajin kambi na centaurs? Ba gaba ɗaya rashin cancantar wuri a cikin wannan hoton ba, waɗannan rukunin yaƙin teku ne na Garnery.

Halayen dabi'ar Faransawa don kama kyawawan abubuwa da alama an tabbatar da su musamman a cikin irin zane-zane da zane-zanen da suke da shi na wuraren wasan kifaye. Ba tare da kashi ɗaya cikin goma na ƙwarewar Ingila a harkar kamun kifi ba, kuma ba kashi na dubun na Amurkawa ba, duk da haka, sun ba wa al'ummomin biyu abubuwan da aka gama kawai za su iya isar da ainihin ruhun farautar kifin. Ga mafi yawancin, ƴan wasan kifin kifi na Ingilishi da na Amurka suna ganin sun gamsu gaba ɗaya tare da gabatar da ƙayyadaddun abubuwa, kamar fakitin bayanan kifin; wanda, gwargwadon yanayin tasirin tasirin, yana daidai da zana bayanan martaba na dala. Ko da Scoresby, sanannen dama mai suna Whaleman, bayan ya ba mu tsayin tsayin kifin Greenland, da ƙanƙara uku ko huɗu na narwhales da porpoises, suna bi da mu ga jerin zane-zane na gargajiya na ƙugiya na jirgin ruwa, yankan wukake, da grapnels; kuma tare da ƙaramin ƙwazo na Leuwenhoeck ya mika wuya ga duban duniya mai girgiza kamanni casa'in da shida na lu'ulu'u na dusar ƙanƙara na Arctic. Ina nufin babu rashin mutunci ga kyakkyawan ɗan tafiya (Ina girmama shi don tsohon soja), amma a cikin mahimmancin al'amari, hakika kulawa ne da ba a samo wa kowane crystal rantsuwar rantsuwa da aka ɗauka a gaban Kotun Greenland Justice of the Peace.

Baya ga waɗancan zane-zane masu kyau daga Garnery, akwai wasu zane-zanen Faransa guda biyu waɗanda suka cancanci lura, ta wani wanda ya rubuta kansa “H. Durand." Ɗaya daga cikinsu, ko da yake bai dace da manufarmu ta yanzu ba, duk da haka ya cancanci ambaton wasu asusun. Yana da shiru a tsakar rana a tsakanin tsibiran na Pacific; wani maharbi ɗan Faransa ya kafa, a cikin teku, cikin nutsuwa, da kasala yana ɗaukar ruwa a cikin jirgin; sakkun tafiyar jirgin, da dogayen ganyen dabino a bayansa, duk suna faduwa tare cikin iska maras iska. Tasirin yana da kyau sosai, idan aka yi la'akari da shi game da gabatar da masunta masu ƙarfi a ƙarƙashin ɗaya daga cikin ƴan abubuwan da suka shafi natsuwa na gabas. Sauran zane-zanen wani al'amari ne na daban: jirgin ya yi shawagi zuwa kan buɗaɗɗen teku, da kuma a cikin zuciyar rayuwar Leviathan, tare da Whale Dama tare; Jirgin (a cikin aikin yanke-ciki) yana shawagi zuwa ga dodo kamar yana tafiya; kuma wani kwale-kwale, da sauri ya tashi daga wannan wurin da ake gudanar da ayyukan, yana shirin korar kifin kifi daga nesa. Harpoons da mashinan sun yi kwance don amfani; mahaya guda uku suna kafa mashigar cikin raminsa; yayin da daga kwatsam na birgima na teku, ƙaramin aikin ya tsaya rabin-tsaye daga cikin ruwa, kamar doki mai girma. Thus glistening for a moment, as slowly it subsided, and sank. Then once more arose, and silently gleamed. It seemed not a whale; and yet is this Moby Dick? thought Daggoo. Again the phantom went down, but on re-appearing once more, with a stiletto-like cry that startled every man from his nod, the negro yelled out—“There! there again! there she breaches! right ahead! The White Whale, the White Whale!”

Upon this, the seamen rushed to the yard-arms, as in swarming-time the bees rush to the boughs. Bare-headed in the sultry sun, Ahab stood on the bowsprit, and with one hand pushed far behind in readiness to wave his orders to the helmsman, cast his eager glance in the direction indicated aloft by the outstretched motionless arm of Daggoo.

Whether the flitting attendance of the one still and solitary jet had gradually worked upon Ahab, so that he was now prepared to connect the ideas of mildness and repose with the first sight of the particular whale he pursued; however this was, or whether his eagerness betrayed him; whichever way it might have been, no sooner did he distinctly perceive the white mass, than with a quick intensity he instantly gave orders for lowering.

The four boats were soon on the water; Ahab's in advance, and all swiftly pulling towards their prey. Soon it went down, and while, with oars suspended, we were awaiting its reappearance, lo! in the same spot where it sank, once more it slowly rose. Almost forgetting for the moment all thoughts of Moby Dick, we now gazed at the most wondrous phenomenon which the secret seas have hitherto revealed to mankind. A vast pulpy mass, furlongs in length and breadth, of a glancing cream-colour, lay floating on the water, innumerable long arms radiating from its centre, and curling and twisting like a nest of anacondas, as if blindly to clutch at any hapless object within reach. No perceptible face or front did it have; no conceivable token of either sensation or instinct; but undulated there on the billows, an unearthly, formless, chance-like apparition of life.

As with a low sucking sound it slowly disappeared again, Starbuck still gazing at the agitated waters where it had sunk, with a wild voice exclaimed—“Almost rather had I seen Moby Dick and fought him, than to have seen thee, thou white ghost!”

“What was it, Sir?” said Flask.

“The great live squid, which, they say, few whale-ships ever beheld, and returned to their ports to tell of it.”

But Ahab said nothing; turning his boat, he sailed back to the vessel; the rest as silently following.

Whatever superstitions the sperm whalemen in general have connected with the sight of this object, certain it is, that a glimpse of it being so very unusual, that circumstance has gone far to invest it with portentousness. So rarely is it beheld, that though one and all of them declare it to be the largest animated thing in the ocean, yet very few of them have any but the most vague ideas concerning its true nature and form; notwithstanding, they believe it to furnish to the sperm whale his only food. For though other species of whales find their food above water, and may be seen by man in the act of feeding, the spermaceti whale obtains his whole food in unknown zones below the surface; and only by inference is it that any one can tell of what, precisely, that food consists. At times, when closely pursued, he will disgorge what are supposed to be the detached arms of the squid; some of them thus exhibited exceeding twenty and thirty feet in length. They fancy that the monster to which these arms belonged ordinarily clings by them to the bed of the ocean; and that the sperm whale, unlike other species, is supplied with teeth in order to attack and tear it.

There seems some ground to imagine that the great Kraken of Bishop Pontoppodan may ultimately resolve itself into Squid. The manner in which the Bishop describes it, as alternately rising and sinking, with some other particulars he narrates, in all this the two correspond. But much abatement is necessary with respect to the incredible bulk he assigns it.

By some naturalists who have vaguely heard rumors of the mysterious creature, here spoken of, it is included among the class of cuttle-fish, to which, indeed, in certain external respects it would seem to belong, but only as the Anak of the tribe.





CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
