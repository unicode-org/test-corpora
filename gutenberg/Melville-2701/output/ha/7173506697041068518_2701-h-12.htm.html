BABI NA 55. Na Manyan Hotunan Whales.

Zan ba ku dogon fenti kamar yadda mutum zai iya ba tare da zane ba, wani abu mai kama da ainihin nau'in whale kamar yadda ya bayyana a idon mashin whale yayin da a cikin cikakkiyar jikinsa aka yi kifin kifi tare da jirgin ruwa don a iya taka shi daidai. Yana iya zama da amfani a baya, don tallata wa annan hotunan nasa masu ban sha'awa waɗanda har zuwa yau suna ƙalubalantar bangaskiyar mai gidan. Lokaci ya yi da za a daidaita duniya a cikin wannan al'amari, ta hanyar tabbatar da irin waɗannan hotuna na whale duk kuskure.

Wataƙila za a sami tushen asalin waɗannan ruɗaɗɗen hoto a cikin tsoffin sassaka na Hindoo, Masarawa, da na Girka. Domin tun daga waɗanda ƙirƙira amma unscrupulous sau a lokacin da a kan marmara panellings na temples, da pedestals na mutummutumai, kuma a kan garkuwoyi, medallions, kofuna, da tsabar kudi, dolphin da aka zana a cikin ma'auni na sarkar-sulke irin na Saladin, da hular kai kamar St. George; tun daga wannan lokacin akwai wani nau'in lasisi iri ɗaya ya rinjayi, ba kawai a cikin shahararrun hotuna na whale ba, amma a yawancin gabatarwar kimiyya game da shi.

Yanzu, bisa ga dukkan alamu, hoton da ya fi dadewa da ake cewa shi ne na whale, ana samunsa a cikin sanannen kogon-pagoda na Elephanta, a Indiya. Brahmins suna kula da cewa a cikin sassaƙaƙƙun sassaƙaƙe na waccan Pagoda na da dadewa, duk sana'o'i da abubuwan nema, duk wani yunƙurin da mutum zai yi, an riga an kwatanta shi da shekaru kafin ɗayansu ya kasance. Don haka, ba mamaki, da a ce a wani irin sana'ar mu mai daraja ta kifin kifin da yakamata ta kasance a cikin inuwa. Hindoo whale da ake magana a kai, yana faruwa a wani sashe daban na bangon, yana nuna halittar Vishnu a cikin sigar leviathan, wanda aka sani da Matse Avatar. Amma ko da yake wannan sassaƙaƙƙen rabin mutum ne da rabin whale, don kawai don ba da wutsiya na ƙarshen, duk da haka ƙananan ɓangarensa duk kuskure ne. Yayi kama da wutsiyar anaconda, fiye da faffadan tafin hannun manyan ƙwanƙolin kifin kifi na gaskiya.

Amma ku je tsohon Hotuna, ku duba yanzu ku kalli hoton wannan kifin mai girma mai zane; domin bai yi nasara ba fiye da Hindoo antiluvian. Hoton Guido ne na Perseus yana ceto Andromeda daga dodo ko kifi. A ina Guido ya sami samfurin irin wannan baƙon halitta? Haka kuma Hogarth, a cikin zana irin wannan yanayin a cikin nasa "Perseus Descending," ya fitar da mafi kyau. Babban dodo na wannan dodo na Hogarthian yana buɗewa a saman ƙasa, da kyar ya zana inci ɗaya na ruwa. Tana da wata irin howdah a bayanta, kuma bakinta da ba a kwance ba wanda buguwa ke birgima a ciki, ana iya kai shi zuwa Ƙofar Traitors da ke bi ta Thames ta ruwa zuwa Hasumiyar. Sannan, akwai kifin kifin Prodromus na tsohon Scotch Sibbald, da Yunana ta whale, kamar yadda aka kwatanta a cikin kwafin tsoffin Littafi Mai-Tsarki da yanke tsofaffin filaye. Me za a ce game da waɗannan? Amma ga littafin-binder's whale winding kamar kurangar itacen inabi zagaye hannun jari na gangarawa anka-kamar yadda hatimi da gilded a kan bayayyakinsu da lakabin-shafukan littattafai da yawa duka na da da sababbi-wanda ke da kyau sosai amma zalla fabulous halitta, kwaikwayi, Na dauka, daga irin Figures a kan tsoho vases. Ko da yake a duk duniya ana kiransa dabbar dolphin, duk da haka na kira wannan kifin-binder's kifin ƙoƙari na kifin kifi; saboda an yi niyya ne lokacin da aka fara ƙaddamar da na'urar. Wani tsohon ɗan ƙasar Italiya ne ya gabatar da shi a wani wuri kusan ƙarni na 15, lokacin Farfaɗowar Koyo; kuma a wancan zamani, har ma zuwa wani lokaci na kwatankwacin lokaci, dabbar dolphins an fi saninta su zama jinsin Leviathan.

A cikin zane-zane da sauran kayan ado na wasu litattafai na daɗaɗɗen za ku iya saduwa da su a wasu lokuta masu ban sha'awa a cikin whale, inda kowane nau'i na spouts, jets d'eau, ruwan zafi da sanyi, Saratoga da Baden-Baden, suna fitowa daga kwakwalwar da ba a gama ba. A cikin taken-shafi na ainihin bugu na "ci gaban koyo" za ku sami wasu kifin kifi masu ban sha'awa.

Amma barin duk waɗannan yunƙurin da ba na fasaha ba, bari mu kalli waɗannan hotuna na leviathan da ke cewa suna da hankali, ƙayyadaddun kimiyya, ta waɗanda suka sani. A cikin tsohon tarin tafiye-tafiye na Harris akwai wasu faranti na whale da aka samo daga littafin tafiye-tafiye na Dutch, AD 1671, mai taken "Tafiya zuwa Spitzbergen a cikin jirgin ruwa Jonas a cikin Whale, Peter Peterson na Friesland, maigida." A cikin ɗaya daga cikin waɗannan faranti, whales, kamar manyan raƙuman katako, ana wakilta suna kwance a tsakanin tsibiran kankara, tare da farar beyar da ke gudana akan bayan rayuwarsu. A cikin wani farantin karfe, an yi babban kuskuren da ke wakiltar kifin kifi tare da fulawa a kai tsaye.

Sa'an nan kuma, akwai ƙaƙƙarfan kwata-kwata, wanda wani Kyaftin Colnett, wani Kaftin na Post Captain a cikin sojojin ruwa na Ingilishi ya rubuta, mai take "A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, don manufar tsawaita kamun kifi na Spermaceti Whale." A cikin wannan littafin akwai jita-jita da ke cewa ya zama "Hoton Physeter ko Spermaceti Whale, wanda aka zana da sikeli daga wanda aka kashe a bakin tekun Mexico, Agusta, 1793, kuma aka ɗaga sama a kan bene." Ina shakkar ba kyaftin din ya dauki wannan hoton na gaskiya ba don amfanin sojojin ruwa. In ambata amma abu ɗaya game da shi, bari in ce yana da idon da aka shafa, bisa ga ma'aunin da ke tare da shi, ga cikakken kifin kifin da ya girma, zai sa idon wannan kifin ya zama taga baka mai tsawon ƙafa biyar. Kai, babban kyaftin, me ya sa ba ka ba mu Yunusa yana kallon wannan ido ba!

Haka nan kuma ba a haɗa mafi ƙwazo na Tarihin Halitta don amfanin matasa da masu taushi ba, waɗanda ba su da irin wannan mugunyar kuskure. Dubi wannan sanannen aikin "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." A cikin bugu na London na 1807 da aka taƙaita, akwai faranti na zargin "whale" da "narwhale." Ba na fatan ganin kamar mara kyau, amma wannan kifin kifi mara kyan gani yayi kama da shuka da aka yanke; kuma, game da narwhale, hangen nesa daya isa ya ba mutum mamaki, cewa a cikin wannan karni na sha tara irin wannan hippogriff na iya zama dabino da gaske ga duk wani hazikin jama'a na 'yan makaranta.

Sa'an nan kuma, a cikin 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, babban masanin halitta, ya buga littafin kimiya na kifaye, wanda a cikinsa akwai hotuna da dama na nau'in Leviathan. Duk waɗannan ba daidai ba ne kawai, amma hoton Mysticetus ko Greenland Whale (wato, Whale Dama), har ma Scoresby, wani mutum mai dadewa da ya taɓa wannan nau'in, ya bayyana cewa ba shi da takwarorinsa a cikin yanayi.

Amma sanya hular-sheaf ga duk wannan kasuwancin da ba ta dace ba an keɓe shi don masanin kimiyya Frederick Cuvier, ɗan'uwan sanannen Baron. A shekara ta 1836, ya buga wani Tarihin Halitta na Whales, inda ya ba da abin da ya kira hoton maniyyi Whale. Kafin nuna wannan hoton ga kowane Nantucketer, kuna da mafi kyawun tanadi don taƙaitawar ku daga Nantucket. A cikin kalma, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale ba maniyyi Whale ba ne, amma squash. Tabbas, bai taɓa samun fa'idar tafiya ta whaling ba (irin waɗannan mutane ba safai ba ne), amma daga ina ya samo wannan hoton, wa zai iya faɗa? Watakila ya samu ne a matsayin magabacinsa na kimiyya a wannan fanni, Desmarest, ya samu daya daga cikin ingantattun zubar da ciki; wato daga zanen kasar Sin. Kuma wane irin samari ne masu fensir na Sinawa, kofuna masu yawa da miya suna sanar da mu.

Dangane da kifin masu zanen alamar da aka gani a tituna suna rataye a kan shagunan dillalan mai, me za a ce a kansu? Yawanci sune Richard III. Whales, tare da ƙwanƙwasa ƙwanƙwasa, kuma masu banƙyama; suna yin karin kumallo a kan jirgin ruwa uku ko huɗu, wato kwale-kwalen ruwa cike da ma'aikatan ruwa: nakasarsu tana yawo cikin tekun jini da shuɗi.

Amma waɗannan kura-kurai da yawa a cikin kwatanta kifin ba abin mamaki ba ne sosai. Yi la'akari! Yawancin zane-zane na kimiyya an ɗauko su ne daga kifin da aka makale; kuma waɗannan sun kusan daidai kamar zanen jirgin ruwa da aka karye, tare da karyewar baya, daidai yake wakiltar dabbar dabbar da kanta a cikin duk girman girman da ba a taɓa gani ba. Ko da yake giwaye sun tsaya tsayin daka, Lewiathan mai rai bai taɓa yin shawagi ba tukuna don hotonsa. Whale mai rai, a cikin cikakken girmansa da muhimmancinsa, kawai ana ganinsa a cikin teku a cikin ruwan da ba a iya ganewa; Kuma mafi yawansa ba a gani, kamar jirgin da aka harba. Kuma daga cikin wannan abu abu ne da ba zai taɓa yiwuwa mutum mai-mutuwa ya ɗaga shi cikin iska a jiki ba, domin ya kiyaye dukan kumbura da ɓarnansa. Kuma, ba don yin magana game da bambancin da ake tsammani na kwane-kwane ba tsakanin matashin tsotsa kifin kifi da cikakken Lewithan Platonian; duk da haka, ko da a cikin ɗaya daga cikin waɗancan kifayen masu shan nono da aka ɗaga a kan jirgin ruwa, irin wannan baƙon abu ne, mai kama da ƙwai, masu rarrafe, sifarsa dabam-dabam, wanda ainihin yanayinsa shaidan ya kasa kamawa.

Amma ana iya hasashe, cewa daga tsiraicin kwarangwal na whale, ana iya samun ingantattun alamu da ke taɓa sifarsa ta gaskiya. Ba komai. Domin yana ɗaya daga cikin abubuwan ban sha'awa game da wannan Leviathan, cewa kwarangwal ɗinsa yana ba da ra'ayi kaɗan game da siffarsa gaba ɗaya. Ko da yake kwarangwal na Jeremy Bentham, wanda ke rataye don candelabra a cikin ɗakin karatu na ɗaya daga cikin masu zartar da shi, daidai yana ba da ra'ayin tsohon mai amfani da burly-browed, tare da sauran manyan halayen Jeremy; Duk da haka ba za a iya gane wani abu irin wannan daga kowane kasusuwan lefitan ba. A gaskiya ma, kamar yadda babban mafarauci ya ce, kwarangwal na whale yana da alaƙa iri ɗaya da cikakkiyar jari da dabba kamar yadda kwarin ke yi wa chrysalis wanda ya lulluɓe shi sosai. Wannan keɓantaccen abu yana bayyana a cikin kai, kamar yadda a wasu ɓangaren wannan littafin za a nuna kwatsam. Hakanan ana nuna shi da ban sha'awa a cikin gefen fin, wanda kusan ƙasusuwansu suna amsa ƙasusuwan hannun ɗan adam, ban da babban yatsan yatsa kawai. Wannan fin yana da yatsun kashi huɗu na yau da kullun, fihirisa, tsakiya, zobe, da ɗan yatsa. Amma duk waɗannan suna dawwama a cikin mayafinsu na nama, kamar yatsun ɗan adam a cikin suturar wucin gadi. "Duk da haka cikin rashin hankali whale na iya yi mana hidima a wasu lokuta," in ji Stubb mai ban dariya wata rana, "ba za a taɓa cewa da gaske ya ɗauke mu ba tare da mittens ba."

Domin duk waɗannan dalilai, to, kowace hanya za ku iya duba ta, dole ne ku kammala cewa babban Leviathan shine halitta ɗaya a cikin duniya wanda dole ne ya kasance ba tare da fenti ba har zuwa ƙarshe. Gaskiya ne, hoto ɗaya na iya buga alamar kusa da wani, amma babu wanda zai iya buga shi da kowane madaidaicin matsayi. Don haka babu wata hanyar duniya ta gano ainihin yadda kifin kifi ya yi kama. Kuma kawai yanayin a cikin abin da za ka iya samun ko da wani jurewa ra'ayin ya rai kwane-kwane, shi ne ta hanyar zuwa wani whaling kanka; amma ta yin haka, ba ƙaramin haɗarin zama murhu na har abada ba kuma ya nutse da shi. Don haka, ga alama a gare ni da bai kamata ku kasance da himma wajen sha'awar wannan Lewitan ba.





BABI NA 56. Na Hotunan Ƙananan Kuskure na Whales, da Hotunan Gaskiya na Whaling Scenes.

A hade tare da mugayen hotuna na whales, an jarabce ni sosai a nan in shigar da waɗancan labarun da suka fi girma a cikin wasu littattafai, na da da na zamani, musamman a Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, da sauransu. Amma na wuce wannan al'amari.

Na san kawai hudu da aka buga shaci na babban maniyyi Whale; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, da na Beale's. A cikin babin da ya gabata an kira Colnett da Cuvier. Huggins's ya fi nasu kyau sosai; amma, ta babban rashin daidaito, Beale's shine mafi kyau. Duk zane-zane na Beale na wannan whale suna da kyau, ban da matsakaicin adadi a cikin hoton whales uku a cikin halaye daban-daban, yana ɗaukar babi na biyu. Gaban sa na gaba, kwale-kwalen da ke kai hari kan maniyyi Whales, ko da yake ba shakka an ƙididdige shi don tada shakkun jama'a na wasu mazajen falo, abin sha'awa daidai ne kuma mai kama da rayuwa a cikin tasirinsa gaba ɗaya. Wasu daga cikin zane-zanen maniyyi Whale a cikin J. Ross Browne suna da kyau daidai a kwane-kwane; amma sun yi tir da an rubuta su. Wannan ba laifinsa bane.

Na Dama Whale, mafi kyawun zane-zane suna cikin Scoresby; amma an zana su akan ma'auni kaɗan don nuna kyawawa. Yana da hoto ɗaya kawai na wuraren whaling, kuma wannan rashi ne na baƙin ciki, saboda ta irin waɗannan hotuna ne kawai, idan an yi kyau sosai, zaku iya samun wani abu kamar ra'ayi na gaskiya na kifin mai rai kamar yadda mafarautansa suka gani.

Amma, wanda aka ɗauka gaba ɗaya, mafi kyawun mafi kyau, kodayake a cikin wasu cikakkun bayanai ba mafi daidai ba, gabatarwar whales da wuraren whaling da za a same su a ko'ina, manyan zane-zanen Faransa ne guda biyu, waɗanda aka kashe da kyau, kuma an ɗauke su daga zane-zane ta Garnery ɗaya. Bi da bi, suna wakiltar hare-hare akan maniyyi da dama Whale. A cikin zanen farko na wani maniyyi mai daraja Whale an kwatanta shi da cikakken girman ƙarfi, ya taso a ƙarƙashin jirgin daga ɓangarorin teku, kuma yana ɗauke da iska a bayansa babban tarkace na katako. Ƙwararren jirgin ruwan ba ya karye, kuma an zana shi kawai yana daidaita kan kashin bayan dodo; Kuma a tsaye a cikin wannan firgici, don wannan walƙiya guda ɗaya da ba za a iya kwatantawa ba, sai ka ga wani ma'aikacin jirgin ruwa, rabinsa ya lulluɓe da tafasasshen kifin kifi mai zafi, yana tsalle, kamar daga wani tudu. Ayyukan duka abu ne mai ban mamaki mai kyau da gaskiya. Kwancen layin da aka kwashe rabin-rabi yana yawo a kan tekun da aka yi fari; sandunan katako na harpoons da aka zubar ba su da kyau a cikinsa; shugabannin ma'aikatan wasan iyo suna warwatse game da whale a cikin sabanin maganganun tsoro; yayin da a cikin baƙar guguwar tazara jirgin yana ɗora kan wurin. Za a iya samun kuskure mai tsanani tare da cikakkun bayanai na jikin kifin kifi, amma bari wannan ya wuce; tun da, ga rayuwata, ba zan iya zana mai kyau haka ba.

A cikin zane-zane na biyu, jirgin yana cikin aikin zane tare da gaɓar gefen wani babban Whale mai gudu mai gudana, wanda ke jujjuya yawancin ciyayi a cikin teku kamar wani dutsen dutsen dutsen Patagonian. Jirginsa a tsaye, cike, kuma baƙar fata kamar zomo; ta yadda daga yawan hayaki a cikin bututun hayaki, za ku yi tunanin dole ne a yi jifa-jifa na dafa abinci a cikin babban hanji a ƙasa. Tsuntsaye na teku suna kallon ƙananan kaguwa, kifi-kifi, da sauran alewa na teku da macaroni, waɗanda Dama Whale a wasu lokuta yakan ɗauka a bayansa. Kuma duk lokacin da leviathan mai kauri yana ta ruga cikin zurfin, yana barin tarin farar ciyayi masu tashin hankali a farkensa, kuma ya sa ɗan ƙaramin kwale-kwalen ya girgiza cikin kumbura kamar ƙwanƙolin da aka kama kusa da takalmi na tudun teku. Don haka, gaba-gaba duk tashin hankali ne; amma a baya, cikin ban sha'awa na zane-zane, matakin teku mai gilashin ya nutsu, faɗuwar jiragen ruwa marasa ƙarfi na jirgin ruwa mara ƙarfi, da kuma yawan mataccen kifin kifi, sansanin yaƙi da aka ci, tare da tutar kama da kasala a rataye daga sandar whale da aka saka a cikin raminsa.

Wanene Garnery mai zane, ko ya kasance, ban sani ba. Amma rayuwata don haka ko dai ya kasance yana tattaunawa da batunsa, ko kuma da wani gogaggen ƙwararrun ƙwararru. Faransawa sune samari don aikin zanen. Ku je ku kalli dukkan zane-zane na Turai, kuma a ina za ku sami irin wannan hoton na rayuwa da hayaniya a kan zane, kamar yadda yake a wannan zauren nasara a Versailles; inda mai kallo ya yi yaƙi da hanyarsa, pell-mell, ta manyan yaƙe-yaƙe na Faransa a jere; Inda kowane takobi ya zama kamar walƙiya na Hasken Arewa, kuma sarakuna masu ɗauke da makamai da sarakunan da suka biyo baya suna tafe, kamar cajin kambi na centaurs? Ba gaba ɗaya rashin cancantar wuri a cikin wannan hoton ba, waɗannan rukunin yaƙin teku ne na Garnery.

Halayen dabi'ar Faransawa don kama kyawawan abubuwa da alama an tabbatar da su musamman a cikin irin zane-zane da zane-zanen da suke da shi na wuraren wasan kifaye. Ba tare da kashi ɗaya cikin goma na ƙwarewar Ingila a harkar kamun kifi ba, kuma ba kashi na dubun na Amurkawa ba, duk da haka, sun ba wa al'ummomin biyu abubuwan da aka gama kawai za su iya isar da ainihin ruhun farautar kifin. Ga mafi yawancin, ƴan wasan kifin kifi na Ingilishi da na Amurka suna ganin sun gamsu gaba ɗaya tare da gabatar da ƙayyadaddun abubuwa, kamar fakitin bayanan kifin; wanda, gwargwadon yanayin tasirin tasirin, yana daidai da zana bayanan martaba na dala. Ko da Scoresby, sanannen dama mai suna Whaleman, bayan ya ba mu tsayin tsayin kifin Greenland, da ƙanƙara uku ko huɗu na narwhales da porpoises, suna bi da mu ga jerin zane-zane na gargajiya na ƙugiya na jirgin ruwa, yankan wukake, da grapnels; kuma tare da ƙaramin ƙwazo na Leuwenhoeck ya mika wuya ga duban duniya mai girgiza kamanni casa'in da shida na lu'ulu'u na dusar ƙanƙara na Arctic. Ina nufin babu rashin mutunci ga kyakkyawan ɗan tafiya (Ina girmama shi don tsohon soja), amma a cikin mahimmancin al'amari, hakika kulawa ne da ba a samo wa kowane crystal rantsuwar rantsuwa da aka ɗauka a gaban Kotun Greenland Justice of the Peace.

Ban da waɗannan zane-zane masu kyau na Garnery, akwai wasu sassa na Faransanci guda biyu da suka cancanci a lura da su, ta wani wanda ya rubuta kansa "H. Durand." Ɗaya daga cikinsu, ko da yake bai dace da manufarmu ta yanzu ba, duk da haka ya cancanci ambaton wasu asusun. Yana da shiru a tsakar rana a tsakanin tsibiran na Pacific; wani maharbi ɗan Faransa ya kafa, a cikin teku, cikin nutsuwa, da kasala yana ɗaukar ruwa a cikin jirgin; sakkun tafiyar jirgin, da dogayen ganyen dabino a bayansa, duk suna faduwa tare cikin iska maras iska. Tasirin yana da kyau sosai, idan aka yi la'akari da shi game da gabatar da masunta masu ƙarfi a ƙarƙashin ɗaya daga cikin ƴan abubuwan da suka shafi natsuwa na gabas. Sauran zane-zanen wani al'amari ne na daban: jirgin ya yi shawagi zuwa kan buɗaɗɗen teku, da kuma a cikin zuciyar rayuwar Leviathan, tare da Whale Dama tare; Jirgin (a cikin aikin yanke-ciki) yana shawagi zuwa ga dodo kamar yana tafiya; kuma wani kwale-kwale, da sauri ya tashi daga wannan wurin da ake gudanar da ayyukan, yana shirin korar kifin kifi daga nesa. Harpoons da mashinan sun yi kwance don amfani; mahaya guda uku suna kafa mashigar cikin raminsa; yayin da daga kwatsam na birgima na teku, ƙaramin aikin ya tsaya rabin-tsaye daga cikin ruwa, kamar doki mai girma. Zuwa gefen kwale-kwalen an juye shi a cikin baho, ba kamar bututun tsutsa ba, amma don samar da zagaye guda ɗaya, nau'in cuku mai nau'in "sheaves," ko yadudduka na karkatar da hankali, ba tare da wani rami ba amma "zuciya," ko bututu mai tsayi da aka kafa a gefen cuku. Kamar yadda mafi ƙarancin tangle ko kink a cikin nadawa zai, yayin da yake gudu, ya cire hannun wani, ƙafar, ko duka jikinsa, ana amfani da iyakar yin taka tsantsan wajen ajiye layin a cikin baho. Wasu masu harpooneers za su cinye kusan safiya guda ɗaya a cikin wannan kasuwancin, suna ɗaukar layin sama sama da sama sannan su juye shi zuwa ƙasa ta hanyar wani shinge zuwa baho, ta yadda a cikin aikin nadawa don yantar da shi daga duk wani lamuni da murɗawa.

A cikin jiragen ruwa na Ingilishi ana amfani da baho biyu maimakon daya; layi daya da ake ci gaba da nadi a cikin bututun biyu. Akwai fa'ida a cikin wannan; saboda wadannan tagwayen banun da suke kanana suna shiga cikin jirgin cikin hanzari, kuma ba sa takura shi sosai; yayin da, baho na Amurka, mai kusan ƙafa uku a diamita kuma yana da zurfin madaidaicin, yana yin babban kaya ga sana'ar da katakon katakon kauri ɗaya ne kawai; Domin kasan jirgin ruwa na whale yana kama da ƙanƙara mai mahimmanci, wanda zai ɗauki nauyin rarraba mai yawa, amma ba mai yawa ba. Lokacin da murfin zanen fentin ya tafa akan layin layi na Amurka, jirgin yana kama da yana ja da wani babban kek ɗin bikin aure don gabatar wa whale.

Dukkanin ƙarshen layin suna fallasa; Ƙarshen ƙarshen ƙarewa a cikin madaidaicin ido ko madauki yana fitowa daga ƙasa zuwa gefen bahon, kuma yana rataye a gefensa gaba ɗaya ya rabu da komai. Wannan tsari na ƙananan ƙarshen ya zama dole akan asusun biyu. Na farko: Domin saukaka lika masa wani karin layin daga jirgin ruwan da ke makwabtaka da shi, idan har kifin da ya buge ya yi sauti mai zurfi har ya yi barazanar kashe duk layin da aka makala a cikin kwalekwalen. A cikin waɗannan lokuttan, ba shakka, kifi kifi yana jujjuya shi kamar mug na ale, kamar dai, daga wannan jirgin zuwa wancan; ko da yake jirgin na farko ko da yaushe yana shawagi a hannu don taimaka wa tawagarsa. Na biyu: Wannan tsari ba makawa ne don kare lafiyar jama'a; domin kuwa iyakar iyakar layin ta kowace hanya ce ta makale da jirgin, kuma su whale sai su gudu layin zuwa karshen kusan a cikin minti daya, shan taba kamar yadda ya saba yi, ba zai tsaya nan ba, domin jirgin da aka halaka zai yi kuskure ya ja shi a bayansa zuwa zurfin teku; kuma in haka ne babu mai kukan gari da zai sake samunta.

Kafin saukar da jirgin ruwan don korar, sai a ɗauko ƙarshen layin daga cikin baho, a zagaya ta can, a sake ɗauka gaba dayan tsayin jirgin, a kan rataye a kan maƙarƙashiya ko maƙarƙashiyar maƙarƙashiyar kowane mutum, ta yadda za ta yi karo da wuyansa a cikin tuƙi; da kuma wucewa tsakanin mazaje, yayin da suke zaune a kishiyar gunwales, zuwa ga ƙwanƙolin gubar ko ramuka a cikin matsananciyar nuna alama na jirgin, inda fil ko skewer mai girman girman ƙugiya na kowa, ya hana shi fita. Daga cikin kujerun da aka rataye a cikin wani ɗan biki a kan bakuna, sa'an nan kuma a sake wucewa cikin jirgin; da wasu fathom goma ko ashirin (wanda ake kira akwatin-layin) ana naɗe shi a kan akwatin da ke cikin bakuna, ya ci gaba da tafiya har zuwa gunwale kadan kadan, sannan a makala shi da gajeriyar warp — igiyar nan da nan aka haɗa ta da garaya; amma kafin wannan haɗin kai, gajeriyar warp tana tafiya ta asirai iri-iri mai ban gajiyar daki-daki.

Don haka layin whale ya naɗe dukkan jirgin a cikin sarƙaƙƙiya masu sarƙaƙƙiya, yana jujjuya shi yana murɗa shi ta kusan kowace hanya. Duk ma'aikatan jirgin suna da hannu cikin mugunyar ta'addanci; ta yadda a idon maigidan, suna ganin kamar ƴan wasan ƴan wasan Indiya ne, waɗanda macizai mafi muni suna fentin gaɓoɓinsu a wasa. Kuma ba zai iya kowane ɗan mace mai mutuwa, a karon farko, ya zaunar da kansa a cikin waɗancan intricacies na hempen, kuma yayin da yake matsar da iyakarsa, ya yi tunanin cewa a duk lokacin da ba a sani ba za a iya bugun garaya, kuma duk waɗannan mugayen contortions ana saka su cikin wasa kamar walƙiya mai zobe; Ba zai iya zama haka ba sai da wani firgita wanda ke sa kwarin ƙasusuwansa su yi rawar jiki a cikinsa kamar jelly mai girgiza. Duk da haka al'ada-bakon abu! abin da ba zai iya cim ma al'ada ba?— Gayer sallies, ƙarin jin daɗi, mafi kyawun barkwanci, da ɗimbin ɗimbin jama'a, ba ku taɓa jin kan mahogany ɗinku ba, fiye da yadda za ku ji kan farar cedar mai rabin inci na jirgin ruwa, lokacin da aka rataye shi a cikin kusoshi na rataye; kuma, kamar su shida burgers na Calais a gaban Sarki Edward, maza shidan da suka hada da ma'aikatan jirgin sun ja cikin muƙamuƙi na mutuwa, tare da tsayawa a kowane wuyansa, kamar yadda za ka iya ce.

Wataƙila ɗan tunani kaɗan ne zai ba ka damar yin lissafin waɗancan bala’o’in kifin da aka yi ta maimaita su—waɗansu kaɗan daga cikinsu an ba da labarinsu—na mutumin nan ko kuma mutumin da aka fitar da shi daga cikin jirgin ta hanyar layi, kuma suka yi hasara. Domin, lokacin da layin ke fitowa, don zama sannan a cikin jirgin ruwa, yana kama da zama a tsakiyar murhun injin tururi a cikin cikakken wasa, lokacin da kowane katako mai tashi, da ramuka, da dabaran, ke kiwo ku. Ya fi muni; domin ba za ku iya zama ba motsi a cikin zuciyar waɗannan haɗari, saboda jirgin yana girgiza kamar shimfiɗar jariri, kuma an kafa ku ta hanya ɗaya da ɗaya, ba tare da faɗakarwa ba; kuma kawai ta hanyar wani yunƙuri na daidaita kai da son kai da aiki a lokaci ɗaya, za ku iya kuɓuta daga zama Mazeppa, ku gudu da inda rana mai gani da kansa ba zai taɓa soki ku ba.

Bugu da ƙari: kamar yadda zurfin kwanciyar hankali wanda a fili kawai ke gaba da annabcin guguwa, watakila ya fi muni fiye da guguwar kanta; domin, lalle ne, natsuwar ba ta kasance ba fãce nade da ambulaf na guguwa; kuma ya ƙunshi shi a cikin kanta, kamar yadda bindigar da ba ta da lahani tana riƙe da foda mai kisa, da ball, da fashewa; don haka kyakkyawan hutun da aka yi a layin, yayin da yake yin shiru game da ma'aikatan jirgin kafin a kawo su cikin ainihin wasan - wannan wani abu ne da ke dauke da ta'addanci na gaskiya fiye da kowane bangare na wannan al'amari mai hatsari. Amma me yasa aka kara cewa? Duk maza suna zaune a lullube a cikin layin whale. Dukansu an haife su da ƙwanƙwasa a wuyansu; amma sai lokacin da aka kama shi cikin sauri, juyowar mutuwa kwatsam, ƴan adam za su fahimci hadurran rayuwa da ke cikin shiru, da hankali. Kuma idan kai masanin falsafa ne, ko da yake kana zaune a cikin kwale-kwalen kifi, ba za ka ji tsoro a zuciyarka ba, fiye da cewa ka zauna a gaban wutar maraice ɗinka tare da karta, ba garaya ba, a gefenka.
