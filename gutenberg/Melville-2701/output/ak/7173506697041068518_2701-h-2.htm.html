Makenkan hann a ɛyɛ kusuu a ebia ɛbɛyɛ me dea asuade a Yona kyerɛkyerɛ nnebɔneyɛfo nyinaa, na ɛno nti mo, na meda so ara dɔɔso ma me, efisɛ meyɛ ɔdebɔneyɛfo kɛse sen mo tie, bere a mo mu bi kenkan me saa asuade foforo a ɛyɛ hu sen saa a Yona kyerɛkyerɛ me , sɛ Onyankopɔn teasefo no wimhyɛnkafo Sɛnea sɛ́ ɔyɛ wimhyɛnkafo-odiyifo a wɔasra no, anaa ɔkasafo a ɔka nokware nneɛma, na Awurade frɛɛ no ​​sɛ ɔmmɔ saa nokware ahorow a wɔmpɛ no wɔ Niniwe bɔne bi aso mu, Yona, a ne ho dwiriw no wɔ ɔtan a ɛsɛ sɛ ɔsɔre no ho no, oguan fii n’asɛmpatrɛw adwuma no ho sɛ obeguan n’asɛyɛde ne ne Nyankopɔn denam hyɛn a ɔfaa so wɔ Yopa no so wɔkyekyeree ne ti, na amanehunu wiase a nsu wom no nyinaa bɔɔ ne ho ban —‘afiri hell yafunu mu’ —bere a ɔkraman no sii fam wɔ po no nnompe a ɛsen biara so no, saa bere no mpo, Onyankopɔn tee odiyifo a wɔakata ne ho, a ɔrenu ne ho bere a ɔteɛɛm no na ɛpo no tuntum no, ɔkraman no baa owia a ɛyɛ hyew na ɛyɛ dɛ no so, ne mframa ne asase mu anigyede nyinaa na ‘ɔfee Yona wɔ asase kesee no so;’ bere a Awurade asɛm no bae ne mprenu so na Yona, a wɔabɔ no akuturuku na wɔaboro no—n’aso, te sɛ ɛpo mu akuturuku abien, a ɔda so ara nwiinwii wɔ po no ho—Na dɛn ne saa, mfɛfo hyɛn mufo?

"Eyi, po so hyɛn mufo, eyi ne saa asuade foforo no, na nnue ma saa Onyankopɔn teasefo no wimhyɛnkafo a obu no animtiaa no. Due ma nea wiase yi twetwe no fi Asɛmpa asɛyɛde mu! Due, nea ɔhwehwɛ sɛ obehwie ngo agu nsu so bere a Onyankopɔn abɔ no ahum! Due, nea ɔhwehwɛ sɛ ɔsɔ ani sen sɛ ɔbɛma ne ho adwiriw no! Due, nea ne din pa dɔɔso ma no sen papa! Due nea, wiase yi mu no, ɔmfa animguase nni dwuma no, mmue, nea ɔnyɛ ɔnokwafo, ɛwom sɛ na atoro yɛ nkwagye de!

Ɔhwee ase na ɔhwee ne ho fii ne ho kakra; afei ɔmaa n’ani so bio kyerɛɛ wɔn, daa anigye a emu dɔ adi wɔ n’ani so, bere a ɔde ɔsoro anigye teɛɛm sɛ, —“Nanso o!, po so hyɛn mufo!, amanehunu biara benkum fam nsa so no, anigye a ɛyɛ nokware wɔ hɔ, na sɛ saa anigye no atifi korɔn, sen sɛnea amanehunu no ase no mu dɔ. So lɔre kɛse no korɔn sen kelson no sua? Anigye yɛ ma no—akyirikyiri, akyirikyiri, wɔ hɔ; akyirikyiri, ne komam anigye —a ɔsɔre tia asase yi anyame ahantan ne commodores, bere nyinaa Anigye yɛ ma nea n’abasa a ɛyɛ den da so ara gyina n’ase no, Anigye yɛ ma no, a ɔmma nokware no mu nkyem anan mu biako, na okum, hyew, na ɔsɛe bɔne nyinaa wɔ Mmarahyɛ Bagua no mufo ne Atemmufo ntade ase, —anigye a ɛkorɔn sen biara no yɛ ma no, a onnye mmara anaa owura biara ntom, na mmom ɔyɛ ɔman ho dɔfo ma ɔsoro Anigye yɛ ma no, a basabasayɛfo kuw a wɔyɛ dede no po mu asorɔkye nyinaa ntumi nwosow no mfi saa Mmere no mu Keel a ɛyɛ nokware yi mu de no to fam, betumi de ne home a etwa to aka sɛ—O Agya!—wɔde Wo poma na ehu me titiriw—owu anaa minwu, ɛha na mabɔ mmɔden sɛ mɛyɛ Wo dea, sen sɛ mɛyɛ wiase yi dea, anaa m’ankasa de, nanso eyi nyɛ hwee: Migyaw daa nkwa ma Wo;

Wanka bio, na mmom ɔde nkakrankakra wosow nhyira, de ne nsa kataa n’anim, na saa na ɔkɔɔ so kotow, kosii sɛ nkurɔfo no nyinaa kɔe, na wogyaw no nkutoo wɔ beae hɔ.
