TI 68. Ntamadan no.

Mede m’adwene asi saa asɛm a ɛnyɛ nea ɛhaw adwene no so, a ɛyɛ akraman were. Manya akyinnyegye wɔ ho ne whalefo a wɔn ho akokwaw a wɔte nsu so, ne abɔde ho animdefo a wɔasua ade wɔ mpoano. Mfitiase adwene no da so ara nsakrae; nanso ɛyɛ adwene bi kɛkɛ.

Asɛmmisa no ne sɛ, dɛn na ɛhe na whale no were wɔ? Wunim nea ne blubber yɛ dedaw. Saa blubber no yɛ biribi a ɛte sɛ nantwinam a ɛyɛ den na ɛbɛn, nanso ɛyɛ den, ɛyɛ den na ɛyɛ mmerɛw, na ne kɛse fi nsateakwaa awotwe anaa du kosi dumien ne dunum.

Afei, ɛmfa ho sɛnea mfiase no ɛbɛyɛ te sɛ nea ɛyɛ nkwaseasɛm sɛ wɔbɛka abɔde biara were ho asɛm sɛ ɛyɛ nea ɛkɔ so pɛpɛɛpɛ na ɛyɛ den saa no, nanso nokwarem no eyinom nyɛ akyinnyegye biara a wɔde tia nsusuwii a ɛte saa; efisɛ wuntumi mma ade foforo biara a ɛyɛ den a ɛkata so mfi whale no nipadua mu so gye saa blubber koro no ara; na aboa biara akyi fã a ɛkata so sen biara, sɛ ɛyɛ den yiye a, dɛn na ɛno betumi ayɛ gye honam ani? Nokwarem no, wubetumi de wo nsa apete ade a ɛyɛ tratraa a enni ano na ɛyɛ mmerɛw, a ɛte sɛ isinglass asinasin a ɛyɛ tratraa sen biara no kakra, nea ɛkame ayɛ sɛ ɛyɛ mmerɛw na ɛyɛ mmerɛw te sɛ satin nkutoo; kyerɛ sɛ, ansa na wɔayow, bere a ɛnyɛ sɛ ɛtwetwe na ɛyɛ den nko, na mmom ɛbɛyɛ den na ɛyɛ mmerɛw mmom no. Mewɔ nneɛma a ayow a ɛte saa pii, na mede yɛ agyiraehyɛde wɔ me whale-nhoma ahorow mu. Ɛyɛ nea ɛda adi pefee, sɛnea madi kan aka no; na esiane sɛ wɔde ato kratafa a wɔatintim no so nti, ɛtɔ mmere bi a m’ani agye sɛ mewɔ adwene sɛ ɛde nkɛntɛnso a ɛma ɛyɛ kɛse bae no so. Sɛnea ɛte biara no, ɛyɛ anigye sɛ wobɛkenkan whale ho asɛm denam wɔn ankasa ahwehwɛ so, sɛnea ebia wobɛka no. Nanso nea mereka kar wɔ ha ne eyi. Saa ade koro no ara a ɛyɛ tratraa a enni ano a ɛyɛ isinglass, a migye tom sɛ ɛde akraman nipadua nyinaa hyɛ mu no, sɛ yɛbɛka a, ɛnyɛ nea ɛsɛ sɛ wobu no kɛse sɛ abɔde no were, te sɛ honam ani were; efisɛ na ɛyɛ fɛwdi ara kwa sɛ yɛbɛka, sɛ whale kɛse no were a ɛfata no yɛ tratraa na ɛyɛ mmerɛw sen abofra a wɔawo no foforo honam ani. Nanso eyinom biara nni hɔ bio.

Sɛ yɛfa no sɛ blubber no yɛ whale no were; afei, bere a saa were yi, te sɛ nea ɛte wɔ Sperm Whale kɛse paa fam no, bɛma ngo nsuhina ɔha dodow no ara aba no; na, sɛ wosusuw sɛ, wɔ dodow mu, anaasɛ sɛ yɛbɛka no yiye a, emu duru mu no, saa ngo no, wɔ ne tebea a wɔada no adi mu no, yɛ nkyem anan mu abiɛsa pɛ, na ɛnyɛ atade no mu nneɛma nyinaa; enti wobetumi anya adwene bi wɔ sɛnea saa ade a nkwa wom no kɛse te ho, a ne ntini ara kwa no fã bi ma wonya nsu ɔtare te sɛ ɛno. Sɛ wobu nsuhina du ma tɔn biako a, wowɔ tɔn du ma akraman no were mu nneɛma nkyem anan mu abiɛsa pɛ.

Ɛkame ayɛ sɛ bere nyinaa wɔatwa ne nyinaa wɔ ɔkwan a ɛyɛ mmerɛw so na wɔasan atwa ho ahyia a agyiraehyɛde tẽẽ a enni ano a wɔahyehyɛ no den, biribi te sɛ nea ɛwɔ Italiafo nkyerɛwde a eye sen biara mu no. Nanso ɛte sɛ nea wɔankyerɛw saa agyiraehyɛde ahorow yi wɔ isinglass ade a yɛaka ho asɛm wɔ atifi hɔ no so, na mmom ɛte sɛ nea wɔfa mu hu, te sɛ nea wɔakyerɛw wɔ nipadua no ankasa so. Saa ara nso na ɛnyɛ eyi nyinaa. Wɔ nsɛm bi mu no, wɔ aniwa a ɛyɛ ntɛm na ɛhwɛ ade no fam no, saa agyiraehyɛde ahorow a ɛyɛ nsensanee no, te sɛ nea ɛwɔ nkyerɛwee ankasa mu no, nanso ɛma wotumi yɛ mfonini afoforo koraa. Efisɛ wɔakyekyere whale no wɔ ne blubber mu ampa te sɛ nea wɔde kuntu anaa counterpane ankasa ahyɛ mu; anaasɛ, nea eye sen saa no, Indiafo poncho bi hwirew ne ti so, na ɔde ne ho twaa ne ho hyiae.
