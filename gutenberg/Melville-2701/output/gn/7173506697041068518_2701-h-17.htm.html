MOAKÃHA 86. Pe Kuña.

Ambue ñe’ẽpapára ombotyryry umi ñemomorã antilope resa suave rehegua, ha guyra araka’eve ndoguejýiva pluma porãite; menos celestial, che afesteha peteĩ cola.

Ojekalkula pe Ballena Esperma jyva tuichavéva oñepyrũ haĝua upe punto tronco-pe oñembo’yhápe peteĩ kuimba’e rembe’ýpe rupi, ha’e ocompone ijyvy yvate gotyo añoite, peteĩ área por lo menos cincuenta pie cuadrado. Pe hapo rete redonda compacto oñembotuichave mokõi palma térã fluke ipukúva, ijyvytu ha ijyvyku'ívape, mbeguekatúpe oñemongu'e mombyry mbovyve peteĩ pulgada ijyvatekue peve. Ndaipóri mba’eve oikovévape umi línea iporãva ojedefini porãvéva umi frontera media luna ko’ã casualidad-peguágui. Oñembotuichavevévo pe ballena okakuaapaitévape, pe cola ohasáta tuichaiterei veinte pie ipukukue.

Pe miembro entero ha’ete peteĩ lecho densa de terreno de sódicos soldados; ha katu eikytĩ ipype, ha rejuhúta mbohapy estrato distinto omoheñóiha:—yvate, mbyte ha yvy gotyo. Umi fibra oíva capa yvate ha yvy gotyo, ipuku ha horizontal; umi oĩva mbytépegua, mbykymi, ha ohóva kurusu gotyo umi capa okápegua apytépe. Ko estructura triuna, opa mba’éicha, ome’ẽ pu’aka pe cola-pe. Pe estudiante-pe ĝuarã umi muro romano tuja rehegua, pe capa mbytegua ome’ẽta peteĩ paralelo curioso pe curso fino de azulejos akóinte oñealternava’ekue pe ita ndive umi reliquia hechapyrãva antigüedad-pe, ha katuete oipytyvõva’ekue tuichaiterei pe albañilería mbarete tuichaitereívape.

Ha katu ha’ete ku ko tuicha poder local oĩva pe cola tendinosa-pe ndaha’éiva suficiente, pe gran parte entero pe leviatán-gui oñembojehe’a peteĩ urdimbre ha tena de fibras ha filamentos musculares reheve, ohasáva mokõive lado umi lomo-pe ha oguejy umi casualidad-pe, insensiblemente oñembojehe’a hendivekuéra, ha tuicha oipytyvõ ipu’akakuérape; upéicha pe cola-pe pe fuerza inmedida confluente pe ballena entera rehegua ha’ete oñeconcentráva peteĩ punto peve. Ikatu piko oiko aniquilación materia-pe, kóva ha’eva’ekue pe mba’e ojejapo haĝua.

Ni kóva —imbarete hechapyrãva, mba’eveichavérõ oreko tendencia oinvalida haĝua pe flexión graciosa umi movimiento orekóvare; oimehápe infantilidad de facilidad ondula peteî Titanismo de poder rupive. Upéva rangue, umi movimiento oguenohẽ chugui iporãha ñanemondýivéva. Pe mbarete añetegua araka’eve nombyai pe iporãva ni pe armonía, ha katu py’ỹi ome’ẽ; ha opa mba’e iporãitereívape, mbarete oguereko heta mba’e ojoajúva pe mágia ndive. Peipe’a umi tendón oñeñapytĩva ha’etévaicha oparupiete ojepe’áva pe mármol oĩvagui pe Hércules tallado-pe, ha pe encanto opa va’erãmo’ã. Eckerman devoto ohupívo pe sábana de lino Goethe retekue desnudo-gui, oñembopy’aju pe kuimba’e pecho masivo rehe, ha’etévaicha peteĩ arco triunfal romano. Angelo opinta jave Ñandejára Túva jepe yvypóraicha, emarka mba’e robustezpa oĩ upépe. Ha oimeraẽ mba’e ikatúva ohechauka hikuái pe mborayhu divino oĩva Ta’ýrape, umi ta’anga italiano suave, rizado, hermafródico, oñemboguapy porãvévape iñe’ẽ; ko’ã ta’anga, ndoguerekóiramo jepe opa iñakãhatã, ndoinsinuái mba’eve mba’eveichagua poder, ha katu pe mera negativo, femenino sumisión ha aguante rehegua, opa pópe oñeconcedéva, omoheñóiva umi peculiar virtud práctica iñembo’ekuéra rehegua.

Péicha ha’e pe sutil elasticidad pe órgano atratáva rehegua, taha’e ojeporúva deporte-pe, térã en serio, térã pochýpe, taha’e ha’éva pe estado de ánimo oĩva, umi iflexión oñemarka invariablemente grásia tuichaitereíva reheve. Upépe ni peteĩ hada po ndaikatúi ohasa chugui.

Cinco movimiento tuicháva ha’e peculiar chupe ĝuarã. Primero, ojeporúramo aleta ramo progresión-rã; Mokõiha, ojepuru jave maza ramo ñorairõme; Mbohapyha, barrido-pe; Irundyha, lobtailing-pe; Quinto, umi casualidad pico-pe.

Primero: Oĩgui horizontal iposición-pe, Leviatán cola oactua iñambuéva opa ambue criatura mar-pegua cola-gui. Araka’eve ndojepy’apýi. Kuimba’épe térã pirakuérape, pe jere ha’e peteĩ señal de inferioridad. Pe ballena-pe g̃uarã, ikuã haʼe pe mbaʼe añoite opropulsa hag̃ua. Rollo-pe oñembojere tenonde gotyo hete guýpe, ha upéi pya’e ojere tapykue gotyo, ha’e kóva ome’ẽva upe movimiento singular darte, salto pe monstruo-pe onata jave pochýpe. Ialeta lateral oservínte ojedirigi haĝua.

Mokõiha: Iñimportantemi, peteĩ ballena espermatozoide oñorairõramo jepe ambue ballena espermatozoide ndive iñakã ha ijyva reheve añoite, upéicharõ jepe, umi conflicto orekóvape yvypóra ndive, ha’e oipuru principalmente ha despreciomente ikuã. Oinupãvo peteĩ várko, pyaʼe ojekurva mombyry chugui umi ifluke, ha pe golpe ojejapo pe retroceso rupive añoite. Ojejapóramo pe aire ndojejokóivape, koʼýte oguejy ramo imarka peve, upéicharõ pe golpe haʼe peteĩ mbaʼe ndaikatúiva ojejoko. Ndaipóri yvypóra costilla ni barco ikatúva oaguanta. Pende salvación añoite oĩ pejehekýivo chugui; ha katu oúramo lado gotyo pe y opuesto rupive, upéicharõ en parte pe flotabilidad ligero orekóvare pe barco de ballenas, ha pe elasticidad orekóva umi material, peteĩ costilla ojejapíva térã peteĩ tablón discontinuo térã mokõi, peteĩ especie de puntada lado-pe, ha’e generalmente pe resultado ivaivéva. Ko'ã golpe lateral sumergido ojehupyty jepi pesca-pe, ha upévare oñeconta mitãnguéra juego añónte. Oĩ oipeʼáva peteĩ vestido, ha ojejoko pe yvykua.

Mbohapyha: Ndaikatúi ahechauka, ha katu ha’ete chéve, pe ballena-pe pe sentido de tacto oñeconcentra pe cola-pe; pórke ko mbaʼépe oĩ peteĩ delicadeza ipype oñembojojáva pe elefante tronco delicioso rehe añoite. Ko delicadeza ojehechauka principalmente pe acción de barrido-pe, pe suave soltera-pe pe ballena peteĩ cierto mbegue suave reheve omomýivo umi inmenso casualidad peteĩ lado guive ambuépe pe mar superficie ári; ha oñandu ramo peteĩ marinero iñakãrague añónte, ay upe marinero, iñakãrague ha opa mba’e. ¡Mba’eichaitépa oĩ ternura upe toque preliminar-pe! Oguerekóramo ko cola mba’eveichagua poder de prehensile, pya’e apensa va’erã che elefante Darmonodes-re ohóva jepi yvoty-mercado-pe, ha baja saludo reheve opresenta nosegays umi doncella-pe, ha upéi acaricia zona-kuéra. Hetave cuenta peteĩgui, peteĩ ñembyasy ha’e pe ballena ndorekóiha ko virtud prehensile ikuãme; ahendúgui ambue elefante rehegua, ojeherida jave pe ñorairõme, ojere ijyva jerére ha oguenohẽ pe dardo.

Irundyha: Omonda reikuaa’ỹre pe ballena ári pe seguridad oñeimo’ãvape umi mar ha’eño mbytépe, rejuhúta chupe ndojedoblái pe tuicha corpulencia idignidad-gui, ha gato-icha, oñembosarái pe océano-pe ha’eramoguáicha peteĩ chimenea. Ha katu upéicharõ jepe rehecha ipu’aka iñoha’ãngápe. Umi ikuã po puku ojecoquetea yvate yvate gotyo; upéi oinupãvo pe superficie, pe conmoción arasunu ipu kilómetro pukukue. Haimete peimo’ãta ojedeskarga hague peteĩ arma tuicháva; ha rehechakuaáramo pe corona ligero de vapor oúva pe espiral-gui ambue extremo-pe, repensáta upéva ha’eha pe tatatĩ oúva pe touch-hole-gui.

Quinto: Pe postura flotante ordinaria leviatán-peguáicha umi casualidad oñeno considerablemente ijyva nivel guýpe, upéi oĩ completamente fuera de vista pe superficie guýpe; ha katu oñembohypýi potaitévo pypukúpe, umi ifluke entero orekóva por lo menos treinta pie hetegui oñembotyryry yvate gotyo, ha upéicha opyta oryrýi sapy’ami, oguejy peve odispara ojehecha’ỹre. Excepto pe sublime incumplimiento —ambue hendápe oñemombe’uva’erã —ko pico umi ballena casualidad-pe oiméne ha’e pe vista tuichavéva ojehecháva opa naturaleza animada-pe. Umi profundidad ipypukúvagui pe cola gigantesco ha’ete espasmódicamente oipe’áva pe yvága ijyvatevévape. Upéicharõ, che képe, ahechápa Satanás majestuoso ombohasaha ijyva colosal ojetorturava’ekue pe tatatĩ Báltico del Infierno-gui. Ha katu remañávo ko’ãichagua escena-re, opa mba’e ha’e opa mba’épe mba’e estado de ánimo-pepa reime; oiméramo pe Danteo-pe, umi aña oúta pene akãme; oiméramo Isaías mba’évape, umi arcángel. Añembo’ývo che barco mástil akãme peteĩ kuarahy resê aja ocarmesíva yvága ha mar, peteĩ jey ahecha peteĩ rebaño tuicháva ballenas este gotyo, opavave oho kuarahy gotyo, ha sapy’ami oryrýi concierto-pe umi casualidad pico reheve. Ha’eteháicha chéve upe jave, araka’eve ndojehechái peichagua gran encarnación de adoración umi tupãme, jepe Persia-pe, umi tata oadoráva róga. Tolomeo Filópatro otestifika haguéicha pe elefante africano rehe, upéi che atestifika pe ballena rehe, ha apronunsia chupe ha’eha pe devotovéva opa tekove apytépe. Pórke heʼiháicha rréi Juba, umi elefánte militár yma guare omombaʼeguasu jepi pyhareve ha ijyva ojehupi pe kirirĩ pypukuvévape.

Pe comparación casualidad ko capítulope, ballena ha elefante apytépe, oñeñe ẽvo algunos aspectos peteĩva cola ha ambuéva tronco rehegua, ndoguerekóiva’erã tendencia omoĩvo umi mokõi órgano opuesto peteĩ joja ári, ha sa’ive pe umi criatura ha’ekuéra oikehápe respectivamente. Pórke pe elefánte ipoderosovéva haʼe peteĩ terrier Leviatánpe g̃uarãnte, upéicha avei, oñembojojáramo Leviatán jyváre, ijyva haʼete peteĩ lirio raʼỹinte. MOAKÃHA 87. Pe Armada Guasu.

Pe península ipukúva ha ijyvyku'íva Malaca, ojepysóva sureste gotyo umi território Birmah-gui, ojapo pe punto sur gotyovéva opaite Asia-gui. Peteĩ línea continuo-pe upe península guive ojepyso umi isla puku Sumatra, Java, Bally ha Timor; ha'éva, heta ambue ndive, ojapo peteĩ topo tuicháva, térã muralla, ipukukue ombojoajúva Asia Australia ndive, ha omboja'óva océano Índico ipukúva noñembyai'ỹva umi archipiélago oriental-gui. Ko muralla ojekutu heta puerto sally-pe ikatu haguãicha umi barco ha ballena-kuérape; ojehecharamo umíva apytépe oĩ umi estrecho Sunda ha Malaca. Sunda estrecho rupive, tenonderãite, umi mba'yru ohóva China gotyo kuarahyreike guive, osẽ China yguasukuérape.

Umi estrecho estrecho Sunda-pegua omboja’o Sumatra-pe Java-gui; ha oñembo’y mbytépe upe isla muralla tuichaitévape, oñemopyendáva upe promontorio verde audaz, ojekuaáva marinero-kuérape Java Head ramo; ha’ekuéra ndaha’éi michĩmi okorrespondéva pe okẽ central ojeipe’áva algún imperio tuicha murállado-pe: ha ñakonsiderávo pe riqueza inexhaustiva especia, ha seda, ha joya, ha óro, ha marfil, oñeenriquecehápe umi mil isla upe mar oriental-pegua, ha’ete peteĩ disposición significativa naturaleza rehegua, ko’ãichagua tesoro, pe yvy formación rupive voi, por lo menos ogueraha va’erãha pe apariencia, jepémo ineficaz, oñeñangarekoha mundo occidental opa mba’e ojagarrávagui. Umi estrecho de Sunda rembe'y ndojeguerekói umi fuerte dominante oñangarekóva umi ojeikeha Mediterráneo, Báltico ha Propontis-pe. Ndojoguái umi danés-pe, ko'ã oriental ndojeruréi obsequio homenaje umi vela superior omboguejýva procesión opa'ÿva umi barco yvytu mboyve, ohasáva siglo ohasáva, pyhare ha ára, ohasáva isla Sumatra ha Java mbytépe, cargado umi carga hepyetéva kuarahyresẽ gotyo. Péro haʼekuéra orrenunsiaramo jepe lívremente peteĩ ceremonial péichagua, ni mbaʼevéicharõ ndorrenunsia hikuái pe reclamo orekóva hikuái peteĩ tributo sólidovévare.

Tiempo osẽva iñakãgui umi proas pirata malayo-kuéra, oñemo’ãva umi cala ha islote sombreado bajo Sumatra-pegua apytépe, osẽ umi barco ohóva estrecho rupi, ojerurévo mbarete porã tributo ilánsa punta-pe. Jepe umi castigo huguy pochýva jey jey ohupytýva hikuái umi crucero europeo pópe, ko’ã corsario audacia ko’ã ára pahápe ojereprimi’imi; jepémo upéicha, ko’áĝa peve, sapy’ánte ñahendu umi barco inglés ha americano rehegua, umi y-pe, ojejupi ha ojesakea oñembyasy’ỹre.

Yvytu porã ha ipyahúva reheve, pe Pequod ko’áĝa oñemoaguĩ ko’ã estrecho-gui; Acab ohasávo hesekuéra mar Javan-pe, ha upégui, ohóvo yvate gotyo, y ojekuaáva ári oho jepi ápe ha pépe pe Ballena Esperma, oityvyro y rembe’ýpe Islas Filipinas-pe, ha ohupyty Japón rembe’y mombyry, itiempoitépe pe tuicha temporada de caza de ballena upépe. Ko’ã mba’e rupive, pe Pequod ojeréva oitypáta haimete opa umi ojekuaáva Ballena Esperma crucero mundo-pe, oguejy mboyve Línea Pacífico-pe; upépe Acab, jepe opa hendápe oñembotapykue ipersecución-pe, oipapa mbarete ome’ẽtaha ñorairõ Moby Dick-pe, pe mar ojekuaavévape oho jepi; ha peteĩ época-pe ikatuhápe razonablemente oje’e hese opersegiha hese.

Péro mbaʼéichapa koʼág̃a? ko búsqueda zonada-pe, ¿opokópa Acab mba’eveichagua yvy rehe? hoy'úpa aire itripulación? Katuete, opytáta y rehehápe. Nahániri. Are guivéma, ko’áğa, pe kuarahy circo-pegua oñani iring tatatĩ ryepýpe, ha noikotevẽi sustento ha katu pe oĩva ijehe. Upéicha Acab. Emarka avei kóva, pe ballena-pe. Ambue casco-kuéra ojekarga jave oguejy mba'e extraterrestre, oñembohasa haguã muelle extranjero-pe; pe barco ballena-pegua ojeréva mundo-pe ndoguerahái carga ha katu ha’e ha tripulación, hi’arma ha oipotáva. Haʼe oreko peteĩ lago entero mbaʼe oĩva embotellado pe amplio sostenimiento orekóvape. Ha’e ojebalasta umi servicio público reheve; ndaha’éi en conjunto ryguasu-plomo ha perrero ndojeporúiva reheve. Haʼe ogueraha ipype y heta áñorema. Hesakã tuja prime Nantucket y; péva, mbohapy arýma oveve jave, Nantucketer, Pacífico-pe, oipotave hoy'u líquido salmuera mboyve, pero kuehe osê balsa-pe barril-pe, umi arroyo peruano térã indio-gui. Upégui ha’e, ikatu ramo jepe ambue barco oho China-pe Nueva York-gui, ha ou jey, opokóvo decena puerto-pe, pe barco-ballena, opa upe intervalo-pe, ikatu ndohechái ni peteĩ grano yvy rehegua; itripulación ndohechái haguére kuimba’épe ha katu umi marinero otyryrýva ha’ekuéraicha. Upéicharõ peẽ pegueraha chupekuéra marandu ouha ambue dilúvio; ombohovái mante hikuái —“Bueno, mitãkuimba’ekuéra, péina ápe pe árka!”

Ko’áğa, ojejapyhy haguéicha heta Ballena Esperma Java rembe’y kuarahyreike gotyo, hi’aguĩva estrecho de Sunda ypýpe; añetehápe, la mayoría pe yvy, rotonda, ojehechakuaaháicha generalmente umi pescador-kuéra ha’eha peteĩ tenda iporãitereíva ojejapo haĝua crucero; upévare, Pequod ohupytyvévo ohóvo Java Head-pe, ojeguerohory jey jey umi ojesarekóva, ha oñemoñe’ẽ chupekuéra oke’ỹre. Ha katu jepe umi acantilado palmido verde pe yvy rehegua pya’e ojere pe proa estribor-pe, ha umi nariz vy’a reheve pe canela pyahu oñembojehe’a pe aire-pe, jepeve ni peteĩ jet noñemombe’úi. Haimete orrenunsia opa pensamiento ho’a haĝua oimeraẽ juego ko’ápe, pe barco hi’aguĩma oike pe estrecho-pe, oñehendu jave pe jepokuaa sapukái vy’apavẽ yvate guive, ha ere long peteĩ espectáculo de magnificencia singular oresaluda.

Ha katu ko’ápe toñepremisa, pe actividad ikane’õ’ỹva rupi ko’ã ára pahápe ojecaza chupekuéra irundyve océano ári, umi Ballenas Esperma, haimete invariablemente oveve rangue empresa michĩvape, yma guaréicha, ko’áĝa oñembyaty jepi en umi rebaño tuicháva, sapy’ánte o’abrazáva hetaiterei multitud, haimete ha’etéta hetaiterei tetã ijapytepekuéra ohurava’ekue liga ha konvénio solemne oipytyvõ ha oñeñangareko haĝua ojupe. Ko agregación de la Ballena Esperma-pe ko’ãichagua caravana tuichaitereívape, ikatu oñeimputa pe circunstancia umi crucero iporãvévape jepe, ikatuha ko’áğa sapy’ánte peveve semana ha mese aja oñondive, peñemomaitei’ỹre ni peteĩ esputo; ha upéi oñemomaitei sapy’a umi mba’e sapy’ánte ha’etéva miles ha miles.

Ipypuku mokõive arco-pe, mombyry dos térã tres millas rupi, ha oformáva peteĩ semicírculo tuicháva, o’abrazáva peteĩ mitad pe horizonte nivelado-gui, peteĩ cadena continua de jets de ballenas oñembosarái ha omimbi pe aire asajepyte-pe. And thus, though surrounded by circle upon circle of consternations and affrights, did these inscrutable creatures at the centre freely and fearlessly indulge in all peaceful concernments; yea, serenely revelled in dalliance and delight. But even so, amid the tornadoed Atlantic of my being, do I myself still for ever centrally disport in mute calm; and while ponderous planets of unwaning woe revolve round me, deep down and deep inland there I still bathe me in eternal mildness of joy.

Meanwhile, as we thus lay entranced, the occasional sudden frantic spectacles in the distance evinced the activity of the other boats, still engaged in drugging the whales on the frontier of the host; or possibly carrying on the war within the first circle, where abundance of room and some convenient retreats were afforded them. But the sight of the enraged drugged whales now and then blindly darting to and fro across the circles, was nothing to what at last met our eyes. It is sometimes the custom when fast to a whale more than commonly powerful and alert, to seek to hamstring him, as it were, by sundering or maiming his gigantic tail-tendon. It is done by darting a short-handled cutting-spade, to which is attached a rope for hauling it back again. A whale wounded (as we afterwards learned) in this part, but not effectually, as it seemed, had broken away from the boat, carrying along with him half of the harpoon line; and in the extraordinary agony of the wound, he was now dashing among the revolving circles like the lone mounted desperado Arnold, at the battle of Saratoga, carrying dismay wherever he went.

But agonizing as was the wound of this whale, and an appalling spectacle enough, any way; yet the peculiar horror with which he seemed to inspire the rest of the herd, was owing to a cause which at first the intervening distance obscured from us. But at length we perceived that by one of the unimaginable accidents of the fishery, this whale had become entangled in the harpoon-line that he towed; he had also run away with the cutting-spade in him; and while the free end of the rope attached to that weapon, had permanently caught in the coils of the harpoon-line round his tail, the cutting-spade itself had worked loose from his flesh. So that tormented to madness, he was now churning through the water, violently flailing with his flexible tail, and tossing the keen spade about him, wounding and murdering his own comrades.

This terrific object seemed to recall the whole herd from their stationary fright. First, the whales forming the margin of our lake began to crowd a little, and tumble against each other, as if lifted by half spent billows from afar; then the lake itself began faintly to heave and swell; the submarine bridal-chambers and nurseries vanished; in more and more contracting orbits the whales in the more central circles began to swim in thickening clusters. Yes, the long calm was departing. A low advancing hum was soon heard; and then like to the tumultuous masses of block-ice when the great river Hudson breaks up in Spring, the entire host of whales came tumbling upon their inner centre, as if to pile themselves up in one common mountain. Instantly Starbuck and Queequeg changed places; Starbuck taking the stern.

“Oars! Oars!” he intensely whispered, seizing the helm—“gripe your oars, and clutch your souls, now! My God, men, stand by! Shove him off, you Queequeg—the whale there!—prick him!—hit him! Stand up—stand up, and stay so! Spring, men—pull, men; never mind their backs—scrape them!—scrape away!”

The boat was now all but jammed between two vast black bulks, leaving a narrow Dardanelles between their long lengths. But by desperate endeavor we at last shot into a temporary opening; then giving way rapidly, and at the same time earnestly watching for another outlet. After many similar hair-breadth escapes, we at last swiftly glided into what had just been one of the outer circles, but now crossed by random whales, all violently making for one centre. This lucky salvation was cheaply purchased by the loss of Queequeg's hat, who, while standing in the bows to prick the fugitive whales, had his hat taken clean from his head by the air-eddy made by the sudden tossing of a pair of broad flukes close by.

Riotous and disordered as the universal commotion now was, it soon resolved itself into what seemed a systematic movement; for having clumped together at last in one dense body, they then renewed their onward flight with augmented fleetness. Further pursuit was useless; but the boats still lingered in their wake to pick up what drugged whales might be dropped astern, and likewise to secure one which Flask had killed and waifed. The waif is a pennoned pole, two or three of which are carried by every boat; and which, when additional game is at hand, are inserted upright into the floating body of a dead whale, both to mark its place on the sea, and also as token of prior possession, should the boats of any other ship draw near.

The result of this lowering was somewhat illustrative of that sagacious saying in the Fishery,—the more whales the less fish. Of all the drugged whales only one was captured. The rest contrived to escape for the time, but only to be taken, as will hereafter be seen, by some other craft than the Pequod.





CHAPTER 88. Schools and Schoolmasters.

The previous chapter gave account of an immense body or herd of Sperm Whales, and there was also then given the probable cause inducing those vast aggregations.

Now, though such great bodies are at times encountered, yet, as must have been seen, even at the present day, small detached bands are occasionally observed, embracing from twenty to fifty individuals each. Such bands are known as schools. They generally are of two sorts; those composed almost entirely of females, and those mustering none but young vigorous males, or bulls, as they are familiarly designated.

In cavalier attendance upon the school of females, you invariably see a male of full grown magnitude, but not old; who, upon any alarm, evinces his gallantry by falling in the rear and covering the flight of his ladies. In truth, this gentleman is a luxurious Ottoman, swimming about over the watery world, surroundingly accompanied by all the solaces and endearments of the harem. The contrast between this Ottoman and his concubines is striking; because, while he is always of the largest leviathanic proportions, the ladies, even at full growth, are not more than one-third of the bulk of an average-sized male. They are comparatively delicate, indeed; I dare say, not to exceed half a dozen yards round the waist. Nevertheless, it cannot be denied, that upon the whole they are hereditarily entitled to en bon point .

It is very curious to watch this harem and its lord in their indolent ramblings. Like fashionables, they are for ever on the move in leisurely search of variety. You meet them on the Line in time for the full flower of the Equatorial feeding season, having just returned, perhaps, from spending the summer in the Northern seas, and so cheating summer of all unpleasant weariness and warmth. By the time they have lounged up and down the promenade of the Equator awhile, they start for the Oriental waters in anticipation of the cool season there, and so evade the other excessive temperature of the year.

When serenely advancing on one of these journeys, if any strange suspicious sights are seen, my lord whale keeps a wary eye on his interesting family. Should any unwarrantably pert young Leviathan coming that way, presume to draw confidentially close to one of the ladies, with what prodigious fury the Bashaw assails him, and chases him away! High times, indeed, if unprincipled young rakes like him are to be permitted to invade the sanctity of domestic bliss; though do what the Bashaw will, he cannot keep the most notorious Lothario out of his bed; for, alas! all fish bed in common. As ashore, the ladies often cause the most terrible duels among their rival admirers; just so with the whales, who sometimes come to deadly battle, and all for love. They fence with their long lower jaws, sometimes locking them together, and so striving for the supremacy like elks that warringly interweave their antlers. Not a few are captured having the deep scars of these encounters,—furrowed heads, broken teeth, scolloped fins; and in some instances, wrenched and dislocated mouths.

But supposing the invader of domestic bliss to betake himself away at the first rush of the harem's lord, then is it very diverting to watch that lord. Gently he insinuates his vast bulk among them again and revels there awhile, still in tantalizing vicinity to young Lothario, like pious Solomon devoutly worshipping among his thousand concubines. Granting other whales to be in sight, the fishermen will seldom give chase to one of these Grand Turks; for these Grand Turks are too lavish of their strength, and hence their unctuousness is small. As for the sons and the daughters they beget, why, those sons and daughters must take care of themselves; at least, with only the maternal help. For like certain other omnivorous roving lovers that might be named, my Lord Whale has no taste for the nursery, however much for the bower; and so, being a great traveller, he leaves his anonymous babies all over the world; every baby an exotic. In good time, nevertheless, as the ardour of youth declines; as years and dumps increase; as reflection lends her solemn pauses; in short, as a general lassitude overtakes the sated Turk; then a love of ease and virtue supplants the love for maidens; our Ottoman enters upon the impotent, repentant, admonitory stage of life, forswears, disbands the harem, and grown to an exemplary, sulky old soul, goes about all alone among the meridians and parallels saying his prayers, and warning each young Leviathan from his amorous errors.

Now, as the harem of whales is called by the fishermen a school, so is the lord and master of that school technically known as the schoolmaster. It is therefore not in strict character, however admirably satirical, that after going to school himself, he should then go abroad inculcating not what he learned there, but the folly of it. His title, schoolmaster, would very naturally seem derived from the name bestowed upon the harem itself, but some have surmised that the man who first thus entitled this sort of Ottoman whale, must have read the memoirs of Vidocq, and informed himself what sort of a country-schoolmaster that famous Frenchman was in his younger days, and what was the nature of those occult lessons he inculcated into some of his pupils.

The same secludedness and isolation to which the schoolmaster whale betakes himself in his advancing years, is true of all aged Sperm Whales. Almost universally, a lone whale—as a solitary Leviathan is called—proves an ancient one. Like venerable moss-bearded Daniel Boone, he will have no one near him but Nature herself; and her he takes to wife in the wilderness of waters, and the best of wives she is, though she keeps so many moody secrets.

The schools composing none but young and vigorous males, previously mentioned, offer a strong contrast to the harem schools. For while those female whales are characteristically timid, the young males, or forty-barrel-bulls, as they call them, are by far the most pugnacious of all Leviathans, and proverbially the most dangerous to encounter; excepting those wondrous grey-headed, grizzled whales, sometimes met, and these will fight you like grim fiends exasperated by a penal gout.

The Forty-barrel-bull schools are larger than the harem schools. Like a mob of young collegians, they are full of fight, fun, and wickedness, tumbling round the world at such a reckless, rollicking rate, that no prudent underwriter would insure them any more than he would a riotous lad at Yale or Harvard. They soon relinquish this turbulence though, and when about three-fourths grown, break up, and separately go about in quest of settlements, that is, harems.

Another point of difference between the male and female schools is still more characteristic of the sexes. Say you strike a Forty-barrel-bull—poor devil! all his comrades quit him. But strike a member of the harem school, and her companions swim around her with every token of concern, sometimes lingering so near her and so long, as themselves to fall a prey.
