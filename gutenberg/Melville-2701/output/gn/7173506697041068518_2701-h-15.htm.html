MOAKÃHA 74. Pe Ballena Esperma akã—Ojehecha ojoavyva.

Ko’ápe, ko’áĝa, oĩ mokõi ballena tuicháva, omoĩva iñakã oñondive; jajoaju hendivekuéra, ha ñamoĩ oñondive ñane mba’éva.

Pe gran orden de leviatán folio-gui, pe Ballena Esperma ha pe Ballena Derecha ha’e mombyry guive umi ojehechakuaavéva. Haʼekuéra añoite umi ballena ojagarráva jepi yvypóra. Pe Nantucketer-pe, opresenta hikuái umi mokõi extremo opa umi variedad ojekuaáva ballena rehegua. Pe diferencia externa oîva ijapytépekuéra ojehechaháicha principalmente iñakãme; ha peteĩ teĩ akãicha ko momento oñemoĩva Pequod ykére; ha ikatuháicha jaha sãsõme peteĩgui ambuépe, japytávo añónte pe cubierta ári:—moõpa, aikuaase, pehupytýta peteĩ oportunidad iporãvéva pe’estudia haĝua cetología práctica ko’águi?

Primero, peẽ peñembotavy pe contraste general oĩva ko’ã akã apytépe. Mokõivéva ha’e suficientemente masivo opa conciencia-pe; ha katu oĩ peteĩ cierto simetría matemática umi Ballena Esperma-pe ofaltáva ñembyasýpe umi Ballena Derecha-pe. Oĩve carácter pe Ballena Esperma akãme. Pehecha aja, peme’ẽ involuntariamente pe inmensa superioridad chupe, en punto de dignidad omyenyhẽva. Ko instancia presente-pe, avei, ko dignidad ojehupi pimiento ha juky color iñakãme cumbre-pe, ome'ëva señal de edad avanzada ha experiencia tuicha. En pocas palabras, ha’e pe técnicamente ohenóiva umi pescador “ballena iñakã gris”.

Koʼág̃a jahechakuaa mbaʼépa saʼive ojoavy koʼã akãme, haʼéva umi mokõi órgano iñimportantevéva, pe tesa ha pe apysa. Mombyry tapykue gotyo iñakã ykére, ha oguejy, peteĩva umi ballena jyva ángulo ypýpe, rehekáramo estrechamente, ipahápe rehecháta peteĩ tesa ndorekóiva pestaña, reimo’ãtava ha’eha peteĩ potro ra’y resa; upévare opa proporción-gui osẽ pe akã magnitud-pe.

Ko’áğa, ko peculiar posición lateral-gui pe ballena resa, ojekuaa porã araka’eve ndaikatuiha ohecha peteĩ mba’e oĩva exactamente tenonde gotyo, ndaha’éi hetave ikatúvagui peteĩ exactamente popa-pe. Peteĩ ñe’ẽme, pe ballena resa ñemohenda okorresponde peteĩ kuimba’e apysápe; ha ikatu peñeimahina, pendejupe ĝuarã, mba’éichapa ohóta penendive, pe’encuesta piko lado gotyo umi mba’e pende apysa rupive. Pejuhúta ikatuha pemanda treinta grado rupinte de visión pe línea lateral recta de visión mboyve; ha treinta rupive hapykuéri. Ne enemigo ipy’aroryvéva oguata ramo derecho nde gotyo, puñalada ojeipysóva ára pukukue, ndaikatumo’ãi rehecha chupe, ha’e omonda ramo nde ári hapykuéguio. Peteĩ ñe’ẽme, reguerekóta mokõi jyva, ja’eporãsérõ; ha katu, al mismo tiempo, avei, mokõi frente (frontes laterales): mba’épa pe ojapóva peteĩ kuimba’e renondépe —mba’épa, añetehápe, hesa’ỹi?

Hi’arive, la mayoría ambue mymbakuérape ko’áĝa ikatúva apensa, umi tesa oñeñotỹeterei ojehechakuaa’ỹre ombojehe’a haĝua ipu’aka ohecha haĝua, ikatu haĝuáicha ojapo peteĩ ta’anga ha ndaha’éi mokõi apytu’ũme; pe posición peculiar ballena resa rehegua, ojedividi efectivamente ha’eháicha heta pie cúbico iñakã sólido reheve, oñemopu’ãva ijapytepekuéra peteĩ montaña tuichávaicha omboja’óva mokõi lago valle-pe; péva, katuete, oseparava’erã enteramente umi impresión ome’ẽva cada órgano independiente. Upévare pe ballena ohechava’erã peteĩ ta’anga iñambuéva ko lado-pe, ha ambue ta’anga iñambuéva upe lado-pe; opa mba’e oĩva ijapytépe katu ha’eva’erã pytũmby pypuku ha mba’eve’ỹ chupe ĝuarã. Ikatu jaʼe yvypórape omañaha ko múndore peteĩ caja de centinela guive orekóva mokõi faja oñembojoajúva iventanarã. Ha katu pe ballena ndive, ko’ã mokõi faja oñemoinge por separado, ojapo mokõi ventána distinta, ha katu ñembyasýpe ombyai pe jehecha. Ko peculiaridad ballena resa rehegua ha’e peteĩ mba’e akóinte ojeguerekova’erã en cuenta pesca-pe; ha imandu’a haguã moñe’ẽhárape algunas escenas oúvape upe rire.

Ikatu oñepyrũ peteĩ porandu curioso ha desconcertantevéva ko materia visual rehegua opokoháicha pe Leviatán rehe. Péro oiméne aime contento peteĩ pista reheve. Peteĩ kuimba’e resa ojepe’a aja pe tesape’ápe, pe acto ohecha haĝua ha’e involuntario; he ise, ndaikatuiha upéi oipytyvõ ohechávo mecánicamente oimeraẽ mba e oĩva henondépe. Upéicharõ jepe, oimeraẽva experiencia ombo’éta chupe, ikatúramo jepe ogueraha peteĩ barrido indiscriminado umi mba’ére peteĩ jesarekópe, ndaikatumo’ãiha chupe, atención ha completamente, ohesa’ỹijo oimeraẽ mokõi mba’e —taha’e ha’éva tuicha térã michĩva —peteĩme ha peteĩchagua instante tiempo rehegua; araka’eve noimportái oñeno ramo ojoykére ha opoko ojuehe. Ha katu ko’áĝa peju ramo peipe’a haĝua ko’ã mokõi mba’e, ha pembojere peteĩ teĩme peteĩ círculo pytũmby pypuku reheve; upéi, rehecha haĝua peteĩva, péicha ogueru haĝua ne apytu’ũ ogueraha haĝua hese, ambue oñemboykétapaite nde conciencia contemporánea-gui. ¿Mbaʼéichapa upéicharõ pe ballena? Añete, mokõive hesa, ijehegui, simultáneamente oactua va’erã; ha katu, ¿iñapytu’ũ piko tuichaiterei oñemomba’eve, oñembojoaju ha sutil yvypóragui, ikatu hağuáicha al mismo momento de tiempo ohesa’ỹijo atentivamente mokõi perspectiva distinta, peteĩva peteĩ lado-pe ha ambue dirección exactamente opuesto-pe? Ikatúramo, upéicharõ, ha’épa peteĩ mba’e hechapyrãva ipype, ha’ete ku peteĩ kuimba’e ikatúva ohasa simultáneamente umi demostración mokõi problema distinto rehegua Euclides-pe. Ni, oñeinvestigáva estrictamente, ndaipóri incongruencia ko comparación-pe.

Ikatu ha’e peteĩ capricho ocioso añónte, ha katu ymaite guive ha’ete chéve, umi extraordinario vacilación movimiento rehegua ohechaukáva algunas ballenas ojejopy jave mbohapy térã irundy barco; pe timididad ha responsabilidad umi kyhyje queer rehe, ojehecharamóva ko’ãichagua ballena-pe; Che apensa opa ko’ã mba’e osẽha indirectamente pe perplexidad indefenso volición-gui, ipype umi ipoder de visión dividido ha diametralmente opuesto oinvolucrava’erã chupekuéra.

Péro pe ballena apysa henyhẽ oikuaasetereígui pe tesaicha. Oiméramo nde peteĩ extraño enteroite pe raza orekóvape hikuái, ikatu rejagarra koʼã mokõi akã ári heta óra, ha arakaʼeve nderejuhúi upe órgano. Pe apysa ndorekói hogue okáguio mba’eveichavérõ; ha pe yvykua ryepýpe voi apenas ikatu remoinge peteĩ pluma, upévare maravillosamente minuto ha’e. Oñemohenda michĩmi tesa rapykuéri. Ojehechávo ijapysakuéra, ko diferencia iñimportánteva ojehechakuaa vaʼerã pe ballena espermatozoide ha pe derecha apytépe. Peteĩha apysa oguerekóramo jepe peteĩ apertura okáguio, pe mokõiha apysa ojejaho’ipaite ha peteĩchaite peteĩ membrana reheve, upéicha rupi okáguio ojehechakuaa’ỹeterei.

¿Ndaha’éi piko curioso, peteĩ tekove tuichaitereíva ballena-icha ohechava’erãha ko mundo peteĩ tesa michĩetereíva rupive, ha ohendu pe arasunu peteĩ apysa michĩvéva peteĩ liebre-gui? Ha katu hesa tuicha ramo Herschel telescopio guasu lente-icha; ha ijapysa tuicha umi catedral pórtico-icha; ¿ojapótapa upéva ohechave hag̃ua, térã ohendu porãvétapa? Mbaʼeveichavérõ.—Mbaʼére piko upéicharõ reñehaʼã “rembotuichave” ne akã? Subtilizar upéva.

Jajapo ko’áğa oimeraẽ palanca ha motor de vapor jaguerekóva ñande pópe, cant pe ballena espermatozoide akã ári, ikatu haguã oñeno iguy guive yvate gotyo; upéi, ojupívo peteĩ escalera rupive pe cumbre peve, ejapo peteĩ jesareko juru guýpe; ha ndaha’éirire pe tete ko’áĝa ojeipe’apaite chugui, peteĩ linterna reheve ikatu jaguejy pe tuicha Kentucky Mamut Cueva ipy’ápe. Ha katu jajejagarra ko’ápe ko diente rehe, ha ñamaña ñande jerére moõpa ñaime. ¡Ajépa añetehápe iporã ha ipotĩva! piso guive techo peve, oñembojegua térã ja’e porãsérõ ojehai peteĩ membrana morotĩ omimbipáva reheve, omimbipáva satén nupcial-icha.

Ha katu pesẽ ko’áğa, ha pemaña ko portentoso mandibula inferior-re, ha’etévaicha pe tapa puku estrecha peteĩ inmensa caja de tabaco-pe, pe bisagra peteĩ extremo-pe, peteĩ lado rangue. Reipe’áramo, ikatu hağuáicha regueraha iñakã ári, ha rehechauka umi hatĩ fila, ha’ete peteĩ portcullis iporãitereíva; ha péicha, ¡ay! ohechauka heta tapichápe peteĩ wight mboriahu pesca-pe, hi’ári ho’a ko’ã espiga fuerza de impaling reheve. Ha katu mombyryve ivaivéva jahecha, ñañemo’ã jave pe mar-pe, pehecha algún ballena sulky, oveve upépe oñesuspendéva, ijyva prodigioso reheve, ipukúva quince pie rupi, oñemoĩva derecho oguejy ángulo recto-pe hete ndive, opa umi mundo peteĩ barco jib-boom-icha. Ko ballena nomanói; ha’e oñembopy’arory mante; fuera de clase, ikatu; hipocondría rehegua; ha ipy’aite guive, umi bisagra ijyva rehegua oñembopiro’y, ohejávo chupe upépe upeichagua situación vaietépe, peteĩ ñe’ẽ vai opa itribu-pe ĝuarã, ha’ekuéra, katuete, oimpretava’erã hese umi mandibula de bloqueo.

Heta vése ko mandibula inferior —ojedesconta fácilmente peteĩ artista opracticáva rupive— ojedesengancha ha ojeipyso cubierta-pe ojepe’a haĝua umi diente de marfil, ha ome’ẽ haĝua peteĩ suministro upe hueso de ballena morotĩ hatãvagui umi pescador omodeláva opáichagua artículo curioso, umíva apytépe umi bastón, paraguas-stock ha umi asa umi látigo de montar-pe g̃uarã.

Peteĩ elevador ipukúva ha ikane’õva reheve ojegueraha pe mandibula a bordo, ha’erõguáicha peteĩ ancla; ha og̃uahẽ vove pe tiémpo hekopete —unos días rire pe ótro tembiapo —Queequeg, Daggoo ha Tashtego, haʼégui enterovéva dentista ikatupyrýva, oñemoĩ odibuja hag̃ua hatĩ. Peteĩ pala de corte aguda reheve, Queequeg olanza umi encía; upéi ojejokua pe mandibula umi anillo-pe, ha peteĩ aparejo oñembojehe’ávo yvate guive, oguenohẽ hikuái ko’ã hatĩ, umi vaka Michigan-gua oguenohẽháicha umi tocón roble tuja umi yvy ka’aguy ka’aguygui. Oĩ generalmente cuarenta y dos hatĩ en total; umi ballena tujápe, heta ojedesgasta, ha katu noñembyaíriva; ni noñemyenyhẽiva ñande moda artificial rire. Upe rire ojeserr pe mandibula losa-pe, ha oñembyaty mombyry viga-icha oñemopu’ã haguã óga.





MOAKÃHA 75. Pe Ballena Derecha Akã—Ojehecha Ojoavyva.

Jahasávo pe cubierta, ko’áğa jajesareko puku porã pe Ballena Derecha akãre.

Oĩháicha en forma general pe noble Ballena Esperma akã ikatu oñembojoja peteĩ kárro de guerra romano rehe (especialmente henondépe, upépe tuichaiterei ojerredondea); upévare, peteĩ jehecha amplio-pe, pe Ballena Derecha akã ogueraha peteĩ ojoguáva bastante inelegante peteĩ sapatu gigantesco dedo galiote-pe. Ojapo doscientos áño peteĩ viajero holandés tuja ombojoja hague iforma peteĩ sapatu apoha ipahaitévare. Ha ko ipahaitépe térã sapatu-pe voi, upe kuñakarai tuja pe cuento de guardería-pegua, pe cría oñembotyryrýva ndive, ikatu opyta porãiterei, ha’e ha opa iñemoñare.

Ha katu reñemo’aguĩvo ko akã guasúre oñepyrũ oasumi diferente aspecto, según nde punto de vista. Reñembo’ýramo ijyva ári ha remañáramo ko’ã mokõi boquilla en forma de F rehe, regueraha va’erãmo’ã iñakã tuichakue peteĩ bajo-viol tuichaitereívape ĝuarã, ha ko’ã espiral, umi apertura oĩva itablero de sonido-pe. Upéi, jey, pemoĩramo pende resa ko incrustación extraña, crestada, peine-icha pe masa ári —ko mba’e hovy ha barnacled, umi groenlandés ohenóiva “corona”, ha umi pescador Sur-gua “capó” de pe Ballena Derecha rehegua; remañávo nde resa kóvare añoite, regueraha va’erãmo’ã iñakã peteĩ roble tuichaitereíva tronco-pe ĝuarã, peteĩ guyra ryguasu ijyva ári. Taha’e ha’éva, pehechávo umi cangrejo oikovéva oñemoĩva ko’ápe ko capó ári, haimete katuete oúta peẽme peichagua idea; ndaha’éiramo, añetehápe, nde fantasía ojefijáva pe término técnico “corona” oñeme’ẽva avei hese; upéicha jave tuicha peñeinteresáta pepensa haĝua mba’éichapa ko monstruo ipu’akapáva añetehápe ha’e peteĩ rréi del mar diademado, ikorona verde oñembojoajúva chupe ĝuarã ko manera hechapyrãvape. Ha katu ko ballena ha’éramo peteĩ rréi, ha’e peteĩ tapicha ojehecharamóva ipy’ahatãitereíva ombojegua haĝua peteĩ diadema. ¡Emañamína upe labio inferior colgante rehe! mba’eichaitépa tuicha sulk ha pout oĩ upépe! peteĩ sulk ha pout, carpintero medida rupive, ipukúva veinte metro rupi ha ipypuku cinco pie; peteĩ sulk ha pout ome’ẽtava ndéve 500 galones rupi aséite ha hetave.

Tuicha ñembyasy, ko’ágã, ko ballena desafortunada ojeguerekova’erãha liebre-pe. Pe fisura oguereko peteĩ pie rupi ipukukue. Oiméne pe sy peteĩ intervalo importante aja oveve hína kuri pe costa peruana-pe, upérõ umi yvyryrýi ojapo pe playa ojeabri. Ko juru ári, peteĩ umbral resbaladizo áriguáicha, ko’áğa jajedesliza juru ryepýpe. Che ñe’ẽ ári aime kuri Mackinaw-pe, arahava’erã kóva ha’eha peteĩ wigwam indio ryepy. ¡Ñandejára porã! ¿kóva piko pe tape Jonás oho hague? Pe techo ijyvate doce pie rupi, ha oho peteĩ ángulo haimbe porãvape, ha’ete ku oĩva upépe peteĩ poste de cresta regular; ko’ã lado costilla, arco, iñakãrague, opresenta aja ñandéve umi maravilloso, medio vertical, en forma de cimetar listón de hueso de ballena, ja’e trescientos peteĩ lado-pe, odependéva pe parte superior iñakã térã hueso corona-gui, omoheñóiva umi persiana veneciana ha’éva ha ambue hendápe oñeñe’ẽkuri cursoriamente. Ko’ã kangue rembe’y ojere umi fibra iñakãrague reheve, upéva rupive pe Ballena Derecha o’encola pe y, ha umi intricacidad-pe omantene umi pira michĩva, juru abierta jave ohasa umi mar brit-pe okaru jave. Umi persiana central hueso rehegua, oñembo’yháicha iórden natural-pe, oĩ ciertas marcas curiosas, curvas, huecos ha crestas, upépe oĩ ballena-kuéra okalkuláva pe criatura edad, peteĩ roble edad-icha umi anillo circular rupive. Jepémo pe certeza ko criterio rehegua mombyry oĩ demostrable-gui, jepeve oreko pe sabor probabilidad analógica rehegua. Taha’e ha’éva, ñañeme’ẽramo chupe, ñame’ẽva’erã peteĩ edad tuichavéva mombyry pe Ballena Derecha-pe, primera vista-pe ha’etétagui razonable.

Ymave, ha’ete ku ipu’akava’ekue umi fantasía curiosovéva oñe’ẽva ko’ã persiana rehe. Peteĩ viajero Purchas-pegua ohenói chupekuéra umi “bargo” hechapyrãva oĩva pe ballena juru ryepýpe;* ambue katu, “cerdas cerdas”; peteĩ tercer karai tuja Hackluyt-pegua oipuru ko lenguaje elegante: “Oĩ doscientos cincuenta aleta rupi okakuaáva káda lado ichop yvateguápe , oarco iñe’ẽ ári káda lado ijuru gotyo”.

*Kóva ñanemomandu'a Ballena Derecha añetehápe orekoha peteî especie de bigote, térã ja'e porãsérõ peteî bigote, oîva mbovymi iñakãrague morotî isarambipáva yvate gotyo pe extremo exterior mandibula inferior-pe. Sapy’ánte ko’ã mecho ome’ẽ peteĩ expresión bastante brigandia hova iñambuéva solemne-pe.

Opavave oikuaaháicha, ko’ã voi “cerdas cerdas”, “aletas”, “bargos”, “persianas” térã oimeraẽ mba’e peipotáva, ome’ẽ umi kuñakaraípe ibusks ha ambue contrivans de refuerzo. Pero ko particular-pe, demanda ymaite guive oguejy ohóvo. Reina Ana tiémpope ha’e pe kangue oĩkuri igloria-pe, pe farthingale ha’e upérõ opa moda. Ha umi dama yma guare omýiháicha vy’ápe, jepe pe ballena jyva ári, ikatuháicha peje; jepe upéicha, peteĩ ducha-pe, peichagua pensamiento’ỹre, ko’áĝa javeve peteĩchagua mandibula guýpe ñañangareko haĝua; pe paraguas ha’éva peteĩ tenda ojepysóva peteĩ kangue ári.

Ha katu ko’áğa ehesarái opa mba’e persiana ha bigote-gui sapy’ami, ha, reñembo’ývo pe Ballena Derecha jurúpe, emaña pyahu nde jerére. Pehechávo opa ko’ã colonnada de hueso ojedistancia metódicamente, ¿ndapepensái piko peimeha pe órgano Haarlem tuichaitéva ryepýpe, ha pemañávo mil tubo orekóvare? Peteĩ alfombra pe órgano-pe ĝuarã jaguereko peteĩ alfombra Turquía ipiro’yvévagui —pe lengua, ojepegáva, ja’eporãsérõ, juru piso-pe. Ikyraiterei ha ipiro’y, ha apto ojedesgarra haĝua ojeipysóvo cubierta-pe. Ko lengua particular ko’áğa ñane renondépe; peteĩ jesareko ohasávape ha’eva’erã ha’eha peteĩ seis barril; upéva heʼise omeʼẽtaha ndéve upe aséite rupi.

Ere kóva, oiméne pehecha porã pe añetegua pe añepyrũva’ekue —pe Ballena Esperma ha pe Ballena Derecha orekoha haimete iñakã iñambuéva. Ñamohu’ã haĝua, upéicharõ: pe Ballena Derecha-pe ndaipóri ykua tuicháva espermatozoide rehegua; ndaipóri mba’eveichavérõ hatĩ marfil-guigua; ndaha'éi mandibula ipukúva ha esbelto peteĩ mandibula inferior rehegua, Ballena Esperma-icha. Ni pe Ballena Esperma-pe ndaipóri umi persiana de hueso; ndaipóri juru inferior tuichaitereíva; ha sa’i mba’eve peteĩ ñe’ẽ rehegua. Jey, Ballena Derecha oguereko mokõi boquilla externa, Ballena Esperma peteĩnte.

Emaña nde ipahaite, ko’áğa, ko’ã venerable akã encapuchado rehe, oñeno aja gueteri hikuái oñondive; peteĩva pya’e ohypýitagui, ojehai’ỹre, yguasúpe; ambue katu ndaha’emo’ãi ipukuetereíva osegui haĝua.

¿Ikatu piko rejagarra pe expresión orekóva pe Ballena Esperma oĩva upépe? Ha’e pe omano va’ekue hendive, oĩnte umi arruga ipukuvéva isyváre ha’ete ko’áĝa oñembyaíva. Che apensa pe frente amplia henyhẽtaha peteĩ placidez ojoguáva pradera-pe, heñóiva peteĩ indiferencia especulativa-gui ñemano rehe. Péro emarka pe ótro akã expresión. Ehecha upe labio inferior asombroso, ojepresionáva accidentalmente pe vaso lado rehe, ikatu hağuáicha oñañuã mbarete pe mandibula. ¿Ha’ete piko ko akã pukukue oñe’ẽva peteĩ resolución práctica tuichaitereívare ombohovái haĝua ñemano? Ko Ballena Derecha che araha ha’e hague peteĩ estoico; pe Ballena Esperma, peteĩ platonio, ikatúva ogueraha Spinoza-pe umi áño paha.





MOAKÃHA 76. Pe Battering-Ram rehegua.

Ere pehejávo, por la nonce, pe Ballena Esperma akã, aipota peẽ, peteĩ fisiólogo sensato ramo, simplemente —particularmente pecomenta hi’aspecto delantero, opa ikoleksión compactada-pe. Aipota peinvestiga ko’áğa pe única vista reheve peforma haĝua pendejehe algún estimación no exagerado ha inteligente oimeraẽva potencia de carnero de golpe ikatúva oñemboguapy upépe. Ko’ápe oĩ peteĩ punto vital; peẽ pemohendava’erãgui satisfactoriamente ko asunto pendejehe, térã tapiaite pepyta peteĩ infiel ramo peteĩ umi acontecimiento oporomondýivéva rehe, ha katu ndaha’éi umi sa’ive añetegua, ikatu oimehápe ojejuhúva opa tembiasakue ojeregistrávape.

Midwifery should be taught in the same course with fencing and boxing, riding and rowing.

I know that this queer adventure of the Gay-Header's will be sure to seem incredible to some landsmen, though they themselves may have either seen or heard of some one's falling into a cistern ashore; an accident which not seldom happens, and with much less reason too than the Indian's, considering the exceeding slipperiness of the curb of the Sperm Whale's well.

But, peradventure, it may be sagaciously urged, how is this? We thought the tissued, infiltrated head of the Sperm Whale, was the lightest and most corky part about him; and yet thou makest it sink in an element of a far greater specific gravity than itself. We have thee there. Not at all, but I have ye; for at the time poor Tash fell in, the case had been nearly emptied of its lighter contents, leaving little but the dense tendinous wall of the well—a double welded, hammered substance, as I have before said, much heavier than the sea water, and a lump of which sinks in it like lead almost. But the tendency to rapid sinking in this substance was in the present instance materially counteracted by the other parts of the head remaining undetached from it, so that it sank very slowly and deliberately indeed, affording Queequeg a fair chance for performing his agile obstetrics on the run, as you may say. Yes, it was a running delivery, so it was.

Now, had Tashtego perished in that head, it had been a very precious perishing; smothered in the very whitest and daintiest of fragrant spermaceti; coffined, hearsed, and tombed in the secret inner chamber and sanctum sanctorum of the whale. Only one sweeter end can readily be recalled—the delicious death of an Ohio honey-hunter, who seeking honey in the crotch of a hollow tree, found such exceeding store of it, that leaning too far over, it sucked him in, so that he died embalmed. How many, think ye, have likewise fallen into Plato's honey head, and sweetly perished there?





CHAPTER 79. The Prairie.

To scan the lines of his face, or feel the bumps on the head of this Leviathan; this is a thing which no Physiognomist or Phrenologist has as yet undertaken. Such an enterprise would seem almost as hopeful as for Lavater to have scrutinized the wrinkles on the Rock of Gibraltar, or for Gall to have mounted a ladder and manipulated the Dome of the Pantheon. Still, in that famous work of his, Lavater not only treats of the various faces of men, but also attentively studies the faces of horses, birds, serpents, and fish; and dwells in detail upon the modifications of expression discernible therein. Nor have Gall and his disciple Spurzheim failed to throw out some hints touching the phrenological characteristics of other beings than man. Therefore, though I am but ill qualified for a pioneer, in the application of these two semi-sciences to the whale, I will do my endeavor. I try all things; I achieve what I can.

Physiognomically regarded, the Sperm Whale is an anomalous creature. He has no proper nose. And since the nose is the central and most conspicuous of the features; and since it perhaps most modifies and finally controls their combined expression; hence it would seem that its entire absence, as an external appendage, must very largely affect the countenance of the whale. For as in landscape gardening, a spire, cupola, monument, or tower of some sort, is deemed almost indispensable to the completion of the scene; so no face can be physiognomically in keeping without the elevated open-work belfry of the nose. Dash the nose from Phidias's marble Jove, and what a sorry remainder! Nevertheless, Leviathan is of so mighty a magnitude, all his proportions are so stately, that the same deficiency which in the sculptured Jove were hideous, in him is no blemish at all. Nay, it is an added grandeur. A nose to the whale would have been impertinent. As on your physiognomical voyage you sail round his vast head in your jolly-boat, your noble conceptions of him are never insulted by the reflection that he has a nose to be pulled. A pestilent conceit, which so often will insist upon obtruding even when beholding the mightiest royal beadle on his throne.

In some particulars, perhaps the most imposing physiognomical view to be had of the Sperm Whale, is that of the full front of his head. This aspect is sublime.

In thought, a fine human brow is like the East when troubled with the morning. In the repose of the pasture, the curled brow of the bull has a touch of the grand in it. Pushing heavy cannon up mountain defiles, the elephant's brow is majestic. Human or animal, the mystical brow is as that great golden seal affixed by the German emperors to their decrees. It signifies—“God: done this day by my hand.” But in most creatures, nay in man himself, very often the brow is but a mere strip of alpine land lying along the snow line. Few are the foreheads which like Shakespeare's or Melancthon's rise so high, and descend so low, that the eyes themselves seem clear, eternal, tideless mountain lakes; and all above them in the forehead's wrinkles, you seem to track the antlered thoughts descending there to drink, as the Highland hunters track the snow prints of the deer. But in the great Sperm Whale, this high and mighty god-like dignity inherent in the brow is so immensely amplified, that gazing on it, in that full front view, you feel the Deity and the dread powers more forcibly than in beholding any other object in living nature. For you see no one point precisely; not one distinct feature is revealed; no nose, eyes, ears, or mouth; no face; he has none, proper; nothing but that one broad firmament of a forehead, pleated with riddles; dumbly lowering with the doom of boats, and ships, and men. Nor, in profile, does this wondrous brow diminish; though that way viewed its grandeur does not domineer upon you so. In profile, you plainly perceive that horizontal, semi-crescentic depression in the forehead's middle, which, in man, is Lavater's mark of genius.

But how? Genius in the Sperm Whale? Has the Sperm Whale ever written a book, spoken a speech? No, his great genius is declared in his doing nothing particular to prove it. It is moreover declared in his pyramidical silence. And this reminds me that had the great Sperm Whale been known to the young Orient World, he would have been deified by their child-magian thoughts. They deified the crocodile of the Nile, because the crocodile is tongueless; and the Sperm Whale has no tongue, or at least it is so exceedingly small, as to be incapable of protrusion. If hereafter any highly cultured, poetical nation shall lure back to their birth-right, the merry May-day gods of old; and livingly enthrone them again in the now egotistical sky; in the now unhaunted hill; then be sure, exalted to Jove's high seat, the great Sperm Whale shall lord it.

Champollion deciphered the wrinkled granite hieroglyphics. But there is no Champollion to decipher the Egypt of every man's and every being's face. Physiognomy, like every other human science, is but a passing fable. If then, Sir William Jones, who read in thirty languages, could not read the simplest peasant's face in its profounder and more subtle meanings, how may unlettered Ishmael hope to read the awful Chaldee of the Sperm Whale's brow? I but put that brow before you. Read it if you can.





CHAPTER 80. The Nut.

If the Sperm Whale be physiognomically a Sphinx, to the phrenologist his brain seems that geometrical circle which it is impossible to square.

In the full-grown creature the skull will measure at least twenty feet in length. Unhinge the lower jaw, and the side view of this skull is as the side of a moderately inclined plane resting throughout on a level base. But in life—as we have elsewhere seen—this inclined plane is angularly filled up, and almost squared by the enormous superincumbent mass of the junk and sperm. At the high end the skull forms a crater to bed that part of the mass; while under the long floor of this crater—in another cavity seldom exceeding ten inches in length and as many in depth—reposes the mere handful of this monster's brain. The brain is at least twenty feet from his apparent forehead in life; it is hidden away behind its vast outworks, like the innermost citadel within the amplified fortifications of Quebec. So like a choice casket is it secreted in him, that I have known some whalemen who peremptorily deny that the Sperm Whale has any other brain than that palpable semblance of one formed by the cubic-yards of his sperm magazine. Lying in strange folds, courses, and convolutions, to their apprehensions, it seems more in keeping with the idea of his general might to regard that mystic part of him as the seat of his intelligence.

It is plain, then, that phrenologically the head of this Leviathan, in the creature's living intact state, is an entire delusion. As for his true brain, you can then see no indications of it, nor feel any. The whale, like all things that are mighty, wears a false brow to the common world.

If you unload his skull of its spermy heaps and then take a rear view of its rear end, which is the high end, you will be struck by its resemblance to the human skull, beheld in the same situation, and from the same point of view. Indeed, place this reversed skull (scaled down to the human magnitude) among a plate of men's skulls, and you would involuntarily confound it with them; and remarking the depressions on one part of its summit, in phrenological phrase you would say—This man had no self-esteem, and no veneration. And by those negations, considered along with the affirmative fact of his prodigious bulk and power, you can best form to yourself the truest, though not the most exhilarating conception of what the most exalted potency is.

But if from the comparative dimensions of the whale's proper brain, you deem it incapable of being adequately charted, then I have another idea for you. If you attentively regard almost any quadruped's spine, you will be struck with the resemblance of its vertebræ to a strung necklace of dwarfed skulls, all bearing rudimental resemblance to the skull proper. It is a German conceit, that the vertebræ are absolutely undeveloped skulls. But the curious external resemblance, I take it the Germans were not the first men to perceive. A foreign friend once pointed it out to me, in the skeleton of a foe he had slain, and with the vertebræ of which he was inlaying, in a sort of basso-relievo, the beaked prow of his canoe. Now, I consider that the phrenologists have omitted an important thing in not pushing their investigations from the cerebellum through the spinal canal. For I believe that much of a man's character will be found betokened in his backbone. I would rather feel your spine than your skull, whoever you are. A thin joist of a spine never yet upheld a full and noble soul. I rejoice in my spine, as in the firm audacious staff of that flag which I fling half out to the world.

Apply this spinal branch of phrenology to the Sperm Whale. His cranial cavity is continuous with the first neck-vertebra; and in that vertebra the bottom of the spinal canal will measure ten inches across, being eight in height, and of a triangular figure with the base downwards. As it passes through the remaining vertebræ the canal tapers in size, but for a considerable distance remains of large capacity. Now, of course, this canal is filled with much the same strangely fibrous substance—the spinal cord—as the brain; and directly communicates with the brain. And what is still more, for many feet after emerging from the brain's cavity, the spinal cord remains of an undecreasing girth, almost equal to that of the brain. Under all these circumstances, would it be unreasonable to survey and map out the whale's spine phrenologically? For, viewed in this light, the wonderful comparative smallness of his brain proper is more than compensated by the wonderful comparative magnitude of his spinal cord.

But leaving this hint to operate as it may with the phrenologists, I would merely assume the spinal theory for a moment, in reference to the Sperm Whale's hump. This august hump, if I mistake not, rises over one of the larger vertebræ, and is, therefore, in some sort, the outer convex mould of it. From its relative situation then, I should call this high hump the organ of firmness or indomitableness in the Sperm Whale. And that the great monster is indomitable, you will yet have reason to know.
