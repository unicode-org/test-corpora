ISAHLUKO 94. Ukucudisa isandla.

Loo mnenga we-Stubb's, uthengwa kakhulu, waziswa ngokufanelekileyo kwicala le-Pequod, apho yonke loo misebenzi yokusika kunye neyokuxhoma echazwe ngaphambili, yayihlala idlula, ukuya kuthi ga kwi-baling ye-Heidelburgh Tun, okanye i-Case.

Ngelixa abanye babezixakekise ngalo msebenzi wamva, abanye babeqeshwe ekurhuqeni iibhafu ezinkulu, ngokukhawuleza nje ukuba zizaliswe ngamadlozi; kwaye xa ixesha elifanelekileyo lafika, le sperm efanayo yaphathwa ngononophelo ere ukuya itrayi-imisebenzi, apho anon.

Yayipholile yaza yakhazimla ukusa kumlinganiselo othile, kangangokuba xa, nabanye abaliqela, ndahlala phantsi phambi kwebhafu enkulu kaConstantine yayo, ndayifumana ifakwe ngendlela engaqhelekanga ibe ngamaqhuma, apha naphaya iqengqeleka kwindawo engamanzi. Yayilishishini lethu ukukhama la maqhuma abuyele kulwelo. Umsebenzi omnandi kunye nongathandekiyo! Ayimangalisi ke into yokuba kumaxesha amandulo esi sidoda sasiyinto ethandwayo yokuthambisa. Oku kucace ngakumbi! isimuncumuncu esinje! isithambisi esinjalo! isinyibilikisi esimnandi kangaka! Emva kokuba ndifake izandla kwimizuzu nje embalwa, iminwe yam yaba ngathi yi-eels, yaza yaqalisa ukuba yinyoka nokujikeleza.

Njengoko ndandihleli apho ndikhululekile, ndinqumle imilenze kumgangatho womgangatho; emva kokusebenza okukrakra kwi-windlass; phantsi kwesibhakabhaka esizolileyo esiluhlaza; inqanawa iphantsi kweseyile engasebenziyo, kwaye ihamba izolile ngolo hlobo; njengoko ndandihlamba izandla zam phakathi kwezo glowubhu zithambileyo, ezithambileyo zezicubu ezingena ngaphakathi, ezilukiweyo malunga neyure; njengoko batyebileyo eminweni yam, bachitha konke ubutyebi babo, njengeediliya ezivuthiweyo iwayini yabo; njengoko ndalifunxayo elo vumba lingangcoliswanga,—ngokoqobo nangenyaniso, njengevumba leentyatyambo zasentwasahlobo; Ndiyanixelela ke, Oko bendihleli emathafeni amnyama; Ndalibala konke ngesifungo sethu esoyikekayo; kuloo madlozi angenakuchazwa, ndazihlamba izandla zam nentliziyo yam ngayo; Ndiphantse ndaqalisa ukuncoma inkolelo endala yeParacelsan yokuba amadlozi ayinto enqabileyo ekuthomalaliseni ubushushu bomsindo; Ngoxa ndandihlamba kuloo bhafu, ndandiziva ndikhululekile ngokobuthixo kuyo yonke inkcaso, okanye ukuthuka, okanye ulunya, naluphi na uhlobo.

Cwitsa! khama! khama! yonke intsasa; Ndawakhama loo madlozi ndade ndaphantse ndanyibilika kuwo; Ndalicudisa loo dlozi de kwabakho impambano engaqhelekanga phezu kwam; ndaye ndazibona ndingena ndingaqondanga ndicinezele izandla zabasebenzi endisebenza nabo kuyo, ndiphazama izandla zabo kwiiglowubhu ezithambileyo. Akwaba bendinokuqhubeka ndikhama loo sperm ngonaphakade! Okwangoku, ekubeni ngamava amaninzi exesha elide, aphindaphindiweyo, ndiye ndabona ukuba kuzo zonke iimeko umntu kufuneka ekugqibeleni athobe, okanye ubuncinane atshintshe, ingcamango yakhe yokonwaba okufikelelekayo; ungayibeki naphi na ngobukrelekrele okanye kwifashoni; kodwa kwinkosikazi, intliziyo, ibhedi, itafile, isali, indawo yomlilo, ilizwe; ngoku sele ndiyibonile yonke le nto, ndikulungele ukucudisa ityala ngonaphakade. Kwiingcinga zemibono yasebusuku, ndabona uthotho lwezithunywa zezulu eparadesi, ngasinye sifake izandla kwingqayi yespermaceti.

Ngoku, ngelixa sixoxa ngembewu yesidoda, kufanele ukuba kuthethwe ngezinye izinto ezifana nayo, kwishishini lokulungisa umnenga wamadlozi ukuze usebenze.

Kuqala kufika ihashe elimhlophe, elibizwa njalo, elifunyanwa kwindawo ecolekileyo yentlanzi, kwanakwiindawo ezingqindilili zeentlanzi zayo. Iqinile kunye nemisipha ejiyileyo-i-wad yezihlunu-kodwa isenayo ioli. Emva kokuba liqhawulwe kumnenga, ihashe elimhlophe liqala lisikwe libe zii-oblongs eziphathekayo ngaphambi kokuba liye kumnambisi. Zijongeka ngathi ziibhloko ze-Berkshire Marble.

I-Plum-pudding ligama elibekwe kwiindawo ezithile eziqhekekileyo zenyama yomnenga, apha naphaya zibambelela kwingubo ye-blubber, kwaye zihlala zithatha inxaxheba kwiqondo elithile ekungabini kwayo. Yeyona nto ihlaziyayo, ikholisayo, nentle ukubonwa. Njengoko igama layo lingeniswa kumazwe angaphandle, iyeyetyebe ngokugqithisileyo, i-tint enamabala, inomhlaba othe chatha wekhephu kunye negolide, onamachokoza obona bubomvurha nobumfusa. Ziiplums zeerubhi, kwimifanekiso yecitron. Nangona kukho isizathu, kunzima ukuzigcina ungatyi. Ndiyavuma, ukuba kanye bendibile emva kwe foromast ukuzama. Yangcamla into ethile njengoko bendifanele ukuba ndikhawule i-cutlet yasebukhosini ukusuka ethangeni likaLouis le Gros inokuba incasa, ndicinga ukuba ubulewe ngosuku lokuqala emva kwexesha lenyamakazi, kunye nelo xesha lenyama yenyama yelo xesha kunye nediliya entle ngokungaqhelekanga yezidiliya zeChampagne.

Kukho enye into, kunye nenye kakhulu, ejikayo ngexesha leli shishini, kodwa endivakalelwa kukuba iyadida kakhulu ukuyichaza. Ibizwa ngokuba yi-slobgollion; i-appellation original kunye ne-whalemen, kwaye kunjalo uhlobo lwento. Yinto erhabaxa ngendlela engaqondakaliyo, enentambo, edla ngokufumaneka kwimibhobho yesidoda, emva kokucudisa ixesha elide, kunye nokuqhawuka okulandelayo. Ndiyibambe njengeembumba ezinqabileyo ezimangalisayo, eziqhekekileyo zetyala, zidibanisa.

I-Gurry, ebizwa njalo, ligama ngokufanelekileyo le-right whalemen, kodwa ngamanye amaxesha lisetyenziswa ngabalobi be-sperm. Ichaza into emnyama, eyoyikisayo ekhutshiwe ngasemva kweGreenland okanye i-right whale, kwaye uninzi lwayo lugqume imiphefumlo yaloo miphefumlo iphantsi ezingela iLeviyatan enesidima.

AbaNippers. Ngokucacileyo eli gama alikho ngokwemveli kwisigama somnenga. Kodwa njengoko isetyenziswa yiminenga, iba njalo. I-whaleman's nipper sisiqwenga esifutshane esiqinileyo sezinto ezithambileyo ezisikwe kwindawo egobileyo yomsila weLeviyatan: inomlinganiselo we-intshi yobungqimba, kwaye kwezinye, ilingana nenxalenye yentsimbi yegaba. I-Edgewise ihamba ecaleni kwedekhi enamafutha, isebenza njenge-squilgee yesikhumba; nangezinyeliso ezingenagama, ezinjengomlingo, zirhwebesha nazo zonke izinto ezingcolileyo.

Kodwa ukufunda konke malunga nale miba yokubuyela umva, eyona ndlela ingcono yakho kukwehla kwangoko kwigumbi lamayeza, uthethe ixesha elide namabanjwa alo. Le ndawo ibikhe yakhankanywa ngaphambili njengendawo yokwamkela iziqwenga zengubo, xa zihlutywa kwaye ziphakanyiswa kumnenga. Xa ixesha elifanelekileyo lifika lokusika imixholo yayo, le ndlu yindawo yokoyikisa zonke iityros, ngakumbi ebusuku. Kwelinye icala, elikhanyiswe sisibane esiluthuntu, kukho indawo eshiywe icace kubasebenzi. Ngokuqhelekileyo bahamba ngababini,-i-pike-and-gaffman kunye ne-spade-man. I-whaling-pike iyafana nesixhobo sokukhwela se-frigate segama elifanayo. I-gaff yinto efana ne-boat-hook. Nge-gaff yakhe, i-gaffman ixhoma kwiphepha lelabhula, kwaye izama ukuyibamba ukuba ingatyibiliki, njengoko inqanawa ihamba kwaye ijikeleza. Ngeli xesha, umhlakulo ume kwiphepha ngokwalo, linqumle ngokuthe ngqo kwiziqwenga zehashe eziphathwayo. Lomhlakulo ubukhali njengoko usenziwa bubusi; Iinyawo zomhlakulo aziyimpahla; into ame phezu kwayo maxa wambi iya kutyibilika ngokungenakuthintelwa imke kuye, njengesileyi. Ukuba uthe wanqumla enye yeenzwane zakhe, okanye omnye wabancedi bakhe, ngaba uya kumangaliswa kakhulu? Iinzwane zinqabile phakathi kwamadoda angamagqala kwigumbi lokuhlambela.





ISAHLUKO 95. I-Cassock.

Ngaba unyathele kwi-Pequod kwindawo ethile yale post-morteizing of the whale; kwaye ukuba ubuqhubele phambili kufutshane ne-windlass, ndiqinisekile ukuba ungekhe wajonga ngaphandle kokufuna kuncinci ukwazi into engaqhelekanga, engaqondakaliyo, ngewuyibone apho, ilele ecaleni kobude kwi-lee scuppers. Hayi iqula elimangalisayo kwintloko enkulu yomnenga; hayi ubugorha bomhlathi wakhe ongezantsi ongathintelekiyo; hayi ummangaliso womsila wakhe olinganayo; Akukho nanye kwezi enokukothusa, njengesiqingatha sokujonga le cone ingaphendulekiyo,-inde kune-Kentuckian inde, ikufutshane neenyawo ububanzi kwisiseko, kwaye i-jet-black njengoYojo, i-ebony idol yaseQueequeg. Isithixo okunene sona; okanye, kunoko, kumaxesha amandulo, ukufana kwawo kwakunjalo. Umfanekiso oqingqiweyo onjengalowo wafunyanwa kwigcuma likakumkanikazi waseMahaka kwelakwaYuda; nangenxa yokunqula oko, uKumkani uAsa, unyana wakhe, wamguzulayo, wasitshabalalisa isithixo, wasitshisa njengesikizi emlanjaneni oyiKedron, njengoko kucacisiweyo ngobumnyama kwisahluko se-15 seNcwadi yokuQala yooKumkani.

Khangela umatiloshe, obizwa ngokuba ngumcebisi, ngoku ufikayo, encediswa ngamahlakani amabini, eyixhasa kakhulu igrandissimus, njengoko oomatiloshe beyibiza njalo, yaye ngamagxa agobileyo, egxadazela egxadazela nayo ngokungathi ungumkhuseli othwele iqabane elifileyo eliphuma entsimini. Eyongeza kumgangatho weqonga, ngoku uqhubela phambili ngokusilinda ukususa ipeliti yakhe emnyama, njengomzingeli wase-Afrika i-peliti ye-boa. Oku kwenziwa ujika i-pelt ngaphakathi ngaphandle, njengomlenze we-pantaloon; inika ukolula okulungileyo, ukuze phantse ukuphinda kabini ububanzi bayo; kwaye ekugqibeleni iyayixhoma, isasazeke kakuhle, kwintambo, ukuze yome. Ngaphambi kwexesha elide, yoyiswa; xa esusa iimitha ezintathu zayo, ukuya kwicala elitsolo, aze ke asike izikroba ezibini zemingxuma yeengalo kwelinye icala, uzityibiliza ngokwakhe kuyo ngomzimba. Umcebisi ngoku umi phambi kokuba utyale imali kwiicanonicals ezipheleleyo zobizo lwakhe. Isikhumbuzo sawo wonke umyalelo wakhe, olu tyalo-mali lulodwa luya kumkhusela ngokufanelekileyo, ngelixa eqeshwe kwimisebenzi ekhethekileyo yeofisi yakhe.

Loo ofisi iqulathe ukugrumba amaqhekeza eengqayi zamahashe eembiza; utyando olwenziwa kwihashe leplanga elinomdla, elityalwe ngakwicala lokhuselo, kunye nebhafu evulekileyo phantsi kwayo, apho amaqhekeza agayiweyo awela khona, ngokukhawuleza njengamaphepha avela kwidesika ye-raptor's orator. Wambathiswe ngesidima esimnyama; ukuhlala kwipulpiti ebonakalayo; ukuzimisela kumagqabi eBhayibhile; onjani yena umgqatswa wobubhishophu omkhulu, enjani yona inkwenkwe kaPopu eyayingulo mcebisi!*

ISAHLUKO 96. I-Try-Works.

Ngaphandle kwezikhephe zakhe eziphakanyisiweyo, umnenga waseMelika wahlulwe ngaphandle ngemisebenzi yakhe yokuzama. Ubonisa ukudida okumangalisayo kweyona masonry eyomeleleyo edityaniswe ne-oki kunye ne-hemp ekwenzeni inqanawa egqityiweyo. Kuba ngathi kuthuthwa izitena ezisuka ebaleni zisiwe kumaplanga akhe.

I-try-works ityalwe phakathi kwe-foremast kunye ne-mainmast, eyona ndawo inamagumbi omgangatho. Amaplanga angaphantsi omelele ngendlela ekhethekileyo, afakelwe ubunzima bobunzima bezitena nodaka oluphantse luqina, olumalunga neemitha eziziimitha ezisibhozo ubude nezikweritha ezintlanu ubude. Isiseko asingeni kumgangatho womgangatho, kodwa isiseko siqiniselwe ngokuqinileyo kumphezulu ngamadolo amakhulu entsimbi ayiqinisa macala onke, aze ayijije ukuya kumaplanga. Emacaleni ifakwe ngamaplanga, kwaye phezulu igutyungelwe ngokupheleleyo yi-hatchway enkulu, ethambekileyo, edibeneyo. Ukususa le hatch siveza iimbiza ezinkulu zetry-try, ezimbini ngenani, kunye nomthamo wemiphanda emininzi. Xa zingasetyenziswa, zigcinwa zicoceke ngendlela emangalisayo. Ngamanye amaxesha zikhazimliswa ngelitye lesepha kunye nesanti, de zibengezele ngaphakathi okwezitya zesilivere. Ebudeni bemilindo yasebusuku abanye oomatiloshe bakudala bakhasa baya kukhasa kubo baze bazipholele apho ukuze balale. Ngoxa beqeshwa ekuzikhazimliseni—indoda enye embizeni nganye, ecaleni—unxibelelwano oluninzi oluyimfihlo luqhutywa, kwimilebe yentsimbi. Ikwayindawo yokucamngca okunzulu kwimathematika. Kwakusesandleni sasekhohlo sokuzama imbiza ye-Pequod, ngelitye lesepha elindijikelezileyo ngenkuthalo, apho ndaqala ndachukunyiswa ngokungangqalanga yinyaniso ephawulekayo, yokuba kwijometri yonke imizimba ehamba nge-cycloid, ilitye lam lesepha ngokomzekelo, liya kuhla lisuka kuyo nayiphi na indawo kanye ngexesha elinye.

Ukususa ibhodi yomlilo phambi kwe-try-works, i-masonry engenanto yelo cala ibonakaliswe, ingene ngemilomo emibini yentsimbi yeziko, ngokuthe ngqo phantsi kweembiza. Le milomo ifakelwe iingcango ezinzima zentsimbi. Ukushisa okukhulu komlilo kuthintelwe ukunxibelelana ngokwawo kumgangatho, ngokusebenzisa i-reservoir enzulu ephuma phantsi kwendawo yonke evaliweyo yemisebenzi. Ngetonela efakwe ngasemva, eli dama ligcinwa lizaliswe ngamanzi ngokukhawuleza njengoko liphuma lingumphunga. Azikho iitshimini zangaphandle; zivula ngqo ukusuka eludongeni olungasemva. Kwaye apha masibuyele umva okomzuzwana.

Kwakumalunga nentsimbi yethoba ebusuku apho iitry-works zePequod zaqala ukuqaliswa kolu hambo lwangoku. YayiyekaStubb ukongamela ishishini.

Ulungile apho? Mqhawule, ngoko, kwaye umqalise. Pheka, tshisa imisebenzi. Le yayiyinto elula, kuba umchweli wayewafaka amacheba akhe ezikweni kulo lonke elo paseji. Apha makuthiwa kuhambo lokuloba iminenga umlilo wokuqala kwi-try-works kufuneka utyiswe okwexeshana ngamaplanga. Emva koko akukho nkuni isetyenzisiweyo, ngaphandle nje kwendlela yokucima ngokukhawuleza kwi-fuel staple. Ngamafuphi nje, emva kokuvavanywa, ibhulabhu ekrwada, eshwabeneyo, ngoku ebizwa ngokuba ngama-scraps okanye iifritters, isenezinto zayo ezininzi ezingathandekiyo. Ezi fritters zondla amadangatye. Njengomfeli-nkolo otshisayo, okanye umntu ozidlayo, xa sele etshisiwe, umnenga ukhupha amafutha kunye nomzimba wakhe. Akwaba ebedla owakhe umsi! kuba umsi wakhe uyoyikeka ukuphefumla, kwaye uwusezele kufuneka, kwaye kungekuphela nje, kodwa kufuneka uphile kuwo ixesha. Inevumba elingachazekiyo, lasendle, leHindoo malunga nayo, elifana nokuba lihlala kwindawo yeepayiri zokungcwaba. Kunuka iphiko lasekhohlo lemini yomgwebo; yingxabano yesihogo.

Ezinzulwini zobusuku umsebenzi wawusebenza ngokupheleleyo. Sasiphumile kwisidumbu; yayenziwe iseyile; umoya wawuhlaziya; ulwandle lwasendle lwalufutha. Kodwa obo bumnyama bakhothwa ngamadangatye avuthayo, athi ngamaxesha athile aphume kwimibhobho yesooty, kwaye akhanyisela zonke iintambo eziphakamileyo kwintambo yomlilo, njengomlilo odumileyo wamaGrike. Inqanawa evuthayo yaqhubela phambili, ngokungathi ithunywe ngokungenalusini ukwenza into ethile yokuziphindezela. Ke i-pitch kunye ne-sulphur ethwele i-brigs ye-Hydriote enesibindi, e-Canaris, ephuma kumazibuko azo ezinzulwini zobusuku, enamaphepha amadangatye eeseyile, awela phezu kweefrigate zaseTurkey, kwaye zazisonga zivutha.

I-hatch, isuswe phezulu kwimisebenzi, ngoku yanikezela iziko elibanzi phambi kwabo. Oku kwakumi iimilo zeTartarean zeeharpooneers zabahedeni, ezisoloko ziziinqanawa zenqanawa yomnenga. Ngezibonda ezikhulu ezinde babegalela intaphane yamafutha amdaka kwiimbiza ezitshisayo, okanye bavuse imililo engaphantsi, de loo madangatye aphumela ngaphandle, egobile, ephuma ngeengcango ukuze ababambe ngeenyawo. Umsi waqengqeleka waba ziimfumba ezimdaka. Kwinqanaba ngalinye lenqanawe kwakukho ioli ebilayo, eyayibonakala ifuna ukutsibela ebusweni babo. Ngokuchasene nomlomo wemisebenzi, kwicala elingaphezulu leenkuni ezibanzi zokhuni, kwakukho i-windlass. Oku kwasebenza kwisofa yolwandle. Apha iwotshi yayilele, xa ingasetyenziswanga ngenye indlela, ijonge kubushushu obubomvu bomlilo, de amehlo abo aziva egqabhukile entloko. Iimpawu zabo ezibhityileyo, ngoku zonke zizele ngumsi kunye nokubila, iindevu zabo eziphothiweyo, kunye nokuqaqamba okungafaniyo kwamazinyo abo, zonke ezi zatyhilwa ngokumangalisayo kwimikrolo engaqondakaliyo yemisebenzi. Njengoko babebalisa omnye komnye ngeziganeko zabo ezingengcwele, amabali abo oyikekayo abaliswa ngamazwi ovuyo; njengokuba ukuhleka kwabo kwaphumela entweni phezulu, njengamalangatye eziko; ukuya ngapha nangapha, phambi kwabo, iiharpooneers zidlala ngeefolokhwe neediphu zazo ezinkulu; njengokuba umoya ubhudla ubhudla, nolwandle lwaxhuma, yaza inqanawa yancwina yaza yantywila, ukanti ngokuzinzileyo wasidubula isihogo saso esibomvu ngakumbi nangakumbi ebumnyameni bolwandle nasebusuku, saza ngokugculela saligqogqa ithambo elimhlophe emlonyeni walo, saza sayitshicela ngokukhohlakeleyo macala onke; emva koko iPequod engxamile, ethwele iingxwabangxwaba, kwaye ithwele umlilo, kwaye itshisa isidumbu, kwaye ingena kobo bumnyama bobumnyama, yayibonakala ifana nomphefumlo womphathi wakhe we-monomaniac.

Kwabonakala kunjalo kum, njengoko ndandimi kumkhondo wakhe, kwaye kangangeeyure ezinde ndithe cwaka ndakhokela indlela yale nqanawa yomlilo elwandle. Ndisongelwe, ngelo xesha, ebumnyameni ngokwam, kodwa ngokukhona ndibona ubomvu, impambano, ububi babanye. Umbono oqhubekayo wemilo engapheliyo phambi kwam, ibeka isiqingatha somsi kunye nesiqingatha somlilo, ezi ekugqibeleni zazala imibono emphefumlweni wam, ngokukhawuleza nje ukuba ndiqalise ukunikezela kobo buthongo obungenakuphenduleka obunokuthi bufike phezu kwam ezinzulwini zobusuku.

Kodwa ngobo busuku, ngokukodwa, into engaqhelekanga (kwaye ukususela oko ingenakuchazwa) yenzeka kum. Ukuqala kobuthongo obufutshane, ndandisoyika ukuba kukho into engalunganga. Ithafa lomhlathi landibetha icala lam, elayama ngalo; ezindlebeni zam kwakukho isandi esiphantsi seeseyile, esaqala ukushukuma emoyeni; Ndacinga ukuba amehlo am avulekile; Ndandisele ndiphaphile ngokubeka iminwe yam kwiziciko kwaye ngomatshini ndiyolula iqelelene. Kodwa, phezu kwako nje konke oku, andizange ndikwazi ukubona khampasi phambi kwam ukuba ndiqhube; nangona kwakubonakala ngathi ngumzuzu okoko bendibukele ikhadi, ngesibane esiqinileyo esikhanyisayo. Ngephanyazo wayemi ngasemva kwesikhephe, yaye amadoda aseManilla ayegxalathelana ukuya emaphinini awo. Ngelilize uMphathi wamaNgesi wamncoma. Ngokubuyela enqanaweni yasemzini, kwaye ubuso bubekwe njengelitye leqhwitha kobakhe, uAhabhi wema nkqo kude ecaleni kwePequod.
