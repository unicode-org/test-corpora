ISAHLUKO SAMA-55. YeMifanekiso Emanyumnyezi Yeminenga.

Ndiya kupeyinta ixesha elide kuwe kunye njengoko umntu unako ngaphandle kweseyile, into efana neyona ndlela yokwenene yomnenga njengoko ebonakala ngokwenene kwiliso le-whaleman xa emzimbeni wakhe opheleleyo umnenga uxhonywe ecaleni kwenqanawa ye-whale ukuze anganyathelwa ngokufanelekileyo apho. Kusenokuba luncedo, ke ngoko, ngaphambili ukubhengeza loo mizobo inomdla yokucinga ngaye ethi kude kube namhlanje icel’ umngeni ukholo lomnini-mhlaba. Lixesha lokulungisa ihlabathi kulo mbandela, ngokubonisa ukuba imifanekiso elolo hlobo yomnenga iphosakele.

Kusenokwenzeka ukuba oyena mthombo uphambili wayo yonke loo mifanekiso iqingqiweyo iya kufunyanwa phakathi kweyona mifanekiso midala yamaHindoo, amaYiputa, namaGrike. Ukususela ngaloo maxesha okuvelisa kodwa angenangqiqo xa kwiipaneli zeemabhile zeetempile, iziseko zemifanekiso eqingqiweyo, kunye namakhaka, iimedallions, iikomityi kunye neengqekembe zemali, ihlengethwa lazotywa kwizikali ze-chain-armor njengeSaladin, kunye nentloko enesigcina-ntloko njenge George's; ukusukela ngoko kukho into efana nelayisenisi ekhoyo, kungekuphela nje kwimifanekiso emininzi edumileyo yomnenga, kodwa kwiinkcazo ezininzi zenzululwazi ngaye.

Ngoku, ngawo onke amathuba, owona mfanekiso wakudala ukhoyo ekucingelwa ukuba ngowomnenga, ufumaneka kwicavern-pagoda yodumo yase-Elephanta, eIndiya. AmaBrahmin akholelwa kwelokuba kwimifanekiso eqingqiweyo ephantse ingapheli yaloo pagoda yamandulo, lonke urhwebo nezinto ezenziwayo, yonke into enokucingeleka yomntu, yayiyiminyaka efanekiselwa kwangaphambili ngaphambi kokuba kubekho nayiphi na kuzo. Akumangalisi ke ngoko, ukuba ngandlel’ ithile umsebenzi wethu obekekileyo wokuloba iminenga bekufanele ukuba ukho apho. Umnenga weHindoo ekubhekiselwa kuwo, uvela kwisebe elahlukileyo eludongeni, ubonisa ukuzalwa kukaVishnu ngendlela yeleviathan, eyaziwa ngokuba yiMatse Avatar. Kodwa nangona lo mzobo sisiqingatha somntu kunye nesiqingatha somnenga, ukuze nje unike umsila wokugqibela, kodwa loo nxalenye encinci yakhe ayilunganga. Ikhangeleka ngakumbi njengomsila ogobhozayo wenhlwathi, kuneentende zezandla ezibanzi zomnenga wokwenene.

Kodwa yiya kwiiGalari zakudala, ukhangele ngoku kumfanekiso womzobi omkhulu ongumKristu wale ntlanzi; kuba akaphumeleli ngcono kuneHindoo yamandulo. Ngumfanekiso kaGuido kaPerseus ehlangula uAndromeda kwisilwanyana saselwandle okanye umnenga. Wawufumana phi uGuido umzekelo wesidalwa esingaqhelekanga ngolo hlobo? Kananjalo noHogarth, ekupeyinteni umfanekiso ofanayo kweyakhe “iPerseus Eyehlayo,” akayenzi ibengcono into enye. Ukonakala okukhulu kwesi silo saseHogarthian sithe nca phezu komhlaba, kunqabile ukuzoba i-intshi enye yamanzi. Inohlobo oluthile lwe-howdah emqolo, kwaye umlomo wayo othe tye othe tyaba ekuqengqeleka kuwo amaza, unokusiwa kwiSango labangcatshi eliphuma kumlambo iThames ngamanzi ukuya kwiNqaba. Emva koko, kukho iminenga ebizwa ngokuba yiProdromus ye<em>Scotch Sibbald endala, kunye ne<em>whale kaYona, njengoko kuboniswe kwimibhalo yeeBhayibhile ezindala kunye nokusikwa kwemibhalo emidala. Kuya kuthiwani na ngezi? Ngokuphathelele umnenga we-book-binder's whale ojikajikayo ngathi sisikhondo sediliya esijikeleza isitokhwe se-ankile ehlayo-njengoko igximfizwe kwaye ihonjiswe ngasemva kunye namaphepha ezihloko zeencwadi ezininzi ezindala nezintsha-eso sisidalwa esihle kakhulu kodwa esihle kakhulu, ilinganisiwe, ndiyithatha, kumanani afanayo kwiivasi zakudala. Nangona kwihlabathi jikelele ihlengesi, nangona kunjalo ndiyibiza le ntlanzi ye-book-binder's ilinge lomnenga; kuba yayicetyiwe xa isixhobo saziswa okokuqala. Yaziswa ngumvakalisi omdala waseTaliyane ndaweni ithile malunga nenkulungwane ye-15, ngexesha lokuVuselelwa kokuFunda; kwaye ngaloo mihla, kwanakwixesha lasemva kwexesha elithelekisekayo, amahlengesi ayedume ukuba luhlobo lweLeviyatan.

Kwiivignettes kunye nezinye izinto ezihonjisiweyo zezinye iincwadi zakudala ngamanye amaxesha uya kudibana nokuchukumisa okunomdla kakhulu kwi-whale, apho zonke iintlobo ze-spouts, ijets d'eau, imithombo eshushu kunye nokubanda, uSaratoga kunye neBaden-Baden, beza bephuma ingqondo engaphelelwanga. Kwiphepha lesihloko lohlelo lokuqala "Ukuqhubela phambili kokuFunda" uya kufumana iminenga enomdla.

Kodwa siyeka zonke ezi nzame zingekho ngcathu, makhe sijonge loo mifanekiso yeleviyatan ecinga ukuba inengqondo, inkcazo yenzululwazi, ngabo baziyo. Kwingqokelela yohambo lukaHarris endala kukho iipleyiti zeminenga ethatyathwe kwincwadi yesiDatshi yohambo, AD 1671, enomxholo othi “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson waseFriesland, master.” Kwelinye lala macwecwe iminenga, njengezihlenga ezinkulu zezigodo, imelwe ilele phakathi kweziqithi zomkhenkce, zineebhere ezimhlophe ezibaleka phezu komqolo ophilayo. Kwenye ipleyiti, impazamo enkulu yenziwe ngokumela umnenga oneempukane ezincanyana.

Kwakhona, kukho iquarto enomtsalane, ebhalwe ngomnye uMphathi uColnett, iPost Captain kumkhosi wasemanzini wamaNgesi, enomxholo othi “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, ngenjongo yokwandisa iSpermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Kule ncwadi kukho ulwandlalo olubizwa ngokuba "nguMfanekiso wePhyseter okanye i-Spermaceti whale, etsalwa ngokwemilinganiselo ukusuka kulowo wabulawa kunxweme lwaseMexico, ngo-Agasti, ngo-1793, kwaye wanyuselwa kumgangatho." Andithandabuzi ukuba ukhapteni uwuthathele lo mfanekiso ulungileyo ukuze kuxhamle oomatiloshe bakhe. Ukukhankanya nje into enye ngayo, mandithi ineliso elalisebenza, ngokwesikali esihamba nalo, kumnenga okhule ngokupheleleyo, elinokwenza iliso lalo mnenga libe yifestile yesaphetha emalunga neemitha ezintlanu ubude. Hayi, umthetheli wam oligorha, kwakutheni ukuze ungasiniki uYona ejonge ngelaa liso?

Kananjalo ayilulo olona qulunqo lwesazela lweMbali yeNdalo ukuze kuxhamle abancinci nabathambileyo, bakhululekile kubugwenxa bempazamo efanayo. Jonga laa ncwadi idumileyo ethi “Indalo Ephilayo Yomkhandi Wegolide.” Kuhlelo olufutshane lwaseLondon lowe-1807, kukho iipleyiti zomnenga ekuthiwa “umnenga” kunye “nomnenga.” Andinqweneli ukubonakala ndingenambeko, kodwa lo mnenga ungabukekiyo ufana nemazi yehagu enqunyuliweyo; yaye, ngokubhekisele kumnenga, ukukroba nje okukodwa kuyo kwanele ukukhwankqisa umntu, ukuba kule nkulungwane yeshumi elinesithoba loo mvubu inokuba nesundu ngokokwenene kulo naluphi na uluntu olukrelekrele lwamakhwenkwe esikolo.

Emva koko, kwakhona, ngo-1825, uBernard Germain, u-Count de Lacépède, isazi sendalo esikhulu, wapapasha incwadi yesayensi ye-whale, apho kukho imifanekiso emininzi yeentlobo ezahlukeneyo zeLeviyatan. Zonke ezi azichanekanga nje kuphela, kodwa umfanekiso Mysticetus okanye Greenland whale (oko kukuthi, umnenga Right), nkqu Scoresby, indoda ende amava ngokuchukumisa ukuba iintlobo, uvakalisa ukuba iqabane layo kwindalo.

Kodwa ukubekwa kwe-cap-sheaf kulo lonke eli shishini eliphazamisayo laligcinelwe inzululwazi uFrederick Cuvier, umzalwana kuBaron owaziwayo. Ngomnyaka we-1836, wapapasha iMbali yeNdalo yeMinenga, apho anika into ayibiza ngokuba ngumfanekiso weSperm Whale. Phambi kokuba ubonise lo mfanekiso kuyo nayiphi na i-Nantucketer, ububonelele ngcono ngesishwankathelo sakho sokurhoxa eNantucket. Ngelizwi nje, iSperm Whale kaFrederick Cuvier ayingoMnenga weSperm, kodwa sisikwashi. Kakade ke, akazange afumane ngenelo yohambo lokuloba iminenga (abantu abanjalo abafane babe nayo), kodwa ukuba wawuvela phi loo mfanekiso, ngubani onokuxela? Mhlawumbi wayifumana njengomanduleli wakhe kwezenzululwazi kwindawo enye, uDesmarest, wafumana enye yezisu zakhe eziyinyani; oko kukuthi, ukusuka kumzobo waseTshayina. Kwaye ngabaphi abafana abadlamkileyo abanepensile la maTshayina, iikomityi ezininzi ezityebileyo neesosi ziyasazisa.

Ngokuphathelele iminenga yabazobi bemiqondiso ebonwa ezitratweni ijinga phezu kweevenkile zabathengisi beoli, kuya kutshiwo ntoni ngayo? Ngokuqhelekileyo nguRichard III. iminenga, ene-dromedary humps, kwaye iyingozi kakhulu; Isidlo sakusasa kwiitiloshe ezintathu okanye ezine, iinqanawa zomnenga ezizele ngoomatiloshe: iziphene zabo zindanda kulwandle lwegazi kunye nepeyinti ezuba.

Kodwa ezi mpazamo zininzi ekufanekiseni umnenga azimangalisi kangako emva koko. Khawuqwalasele! Uninzi lwemizobo yezenzululwazi ithathwe kwiintlanzi ezixineneyo; yaye ezi zichane kanye njengomzobo wenqanawa eyaphukileyo, enomqolo owaphukileyo, unokusimela ngokufanelekileyo isilwanyana esibekekileyo ngokwaso kulo lonke ibhongo laso elingeyonxalenye yenyama yenqanawa. Nangona iindlovu zimile ubude bazo obupheleleyo, iLeviyatan ephilayo ayizange ikwazi ukudada ngokufanelekileyo kumfanekiso wayo. Umnenga ophilayo, ngobungangamsha bakhe obupheleleyo nokubaluleka kwawo, ubonakala kuphela elwandle kumanzi angenakuqondwa; kwaye inkitha yakhe ayibonakali, njengenqanawa eya kuluhlu lwedabi; kwaye ngaphandle kweso siqalelo yinto engenakwenzeka ngonaphakade ukuba umntu ofayo amphakamisele emoyeni emoyeni, ukuze alondoloze konke ukudumba kwakhe okunamandla kunye nokuphazamiseka. Kwaye, singasathethi ke ngomahluko oqikelelwayo wecontour phakathi komnenga omncinci owanyayo kunye neLeviyatan kaPlato ekhule ngokupheleleyo; ukanti, nakwimeko yomnye waloo minenga incinci ifunxayo iphakanyiselwe kumgangatho wenqanawa, unjalo ke umilo wakhe ongaqhelekanga, onjengeeel, onamaqunube, awahlukeneyo, kangangokuba intetho yakhe echanekileyo umtyholi ngokwakhe akanakuyibamba.

Kodwa kusenokuba yinto enqwenelekayo, ukuba kumathambo ahamba ze womnenga ovalelweyo, amacebiso achanekileyo anokuthathwa achukumisa ubume bakhe bokwenyani. Hayi akunjalo. Kuba yenye yezinto ezinomdla ngakumbi ngale Leviyatan, ukuba i-skeleton yakhe inika umbono omncinci wemilo yakhe jikelele. Nangona i-skeleton kaJeremy Bentham, ejinga kwi-candelabra kwithala leencwadi lomnye wababulali bakhe, idlulisela ngokuchanekileyo ingcamango yendoda endala ye-burly-browed utilitarian, nazo zonke ezinye iimpawu eziphambili zobuntu bukaJeremy; ukanti akukho nto ilolu hlobo inokuchazwa kuwo nawaphi na amathambo eleviyatan. Ngapha koko, njengoko uMzingeli omkhulu esitsho, amathambo omnenga anobudlelwane obufanayo nesilwanyana esityalwe ngokupheleleyo kunye nesinamaqhuqhuva njengoko isinambuzane sisenza kwi-chrysalis esiyigubungele ngokusangqa. Oku kungaqhelekanga kubonakaliswe ngokumangalisayo entlokweni, njengoko kwenye inxalenye yale ncwadi iya kuboniswa ngengozi. Ikwaboniswa ngendlela emangalisayo kwifin yecala, amathambo aphantse aphendule ngokuthe ngqo kumathambo esandla somntu, thabatha ubhontsi kuphela. Eli phiko lineminwe emine eqhelekileyo yamathambo, isalathisi, umbindi, umsesane, kunye nomnwe omncinci. Kodwa zonke ezi zihleli ngokusisigxina kwisigqubuthelo sazo senyama, njengeminwe yomntu kwisigqubuthelo sokwenziwa. “Noko ke ngokungakhathali maxa wambi umnenga usenokusikhonza,” watsho njalo uStubb owayeqhula ngenye imini, “akunakuze kuthiwe uyasiphatha ngaphandle kweemittens.”

Ngenxa yazo zonke ezi zizathu, ke, nangayiphi na indlela onokuyijonga ngayo, kufuneka ugqibe kwelokuba iLeviyatan enkulu seso sidalwa esinye ehlabathini esimele sihlale singapeyintwanga de kuse ekugqibeleni. Liyinyaniso elokuba, umfanekiso omnye unokufikela uphawu kufutshane kunomnye, kodwa akukho namnye unokuwubetha ngalo naluphi na uhlobo oluchanekileyo. Ngoko akukho ndlela yasemhlabeni yokufumanisa ukuba ngokwenene unjani umnenga. Kwaye ekuphela kwendlela onokufumana ngayo ingcamango enyamezelekayo yecontour yakhe ephilayo, kukuhamba ngeminenga ngokwakho; kodwa ngokwenza njalo, awukho mngciphekweni omncinci wokuba nesitovu sanaphakade kwaye utyiswe nguye. Ke ngoko, kubonakala kum ukuba ubungaphambuki kakhulu ekufuneni kwakho leleviyatan.





ISAHLUKO SAMA-56. NgeMifanekiso eNgenempazamo yeMinenga, kunye neMifanekiso eyiNyaniso yeZiqephu zoMnenga.

Ngokudityaniswa nemifanekiso eyoyikekayo yeminenga, ndilingeka kakhulu apha ukuba ndingene kula mabali awoyikekayo awo afumaneka kwiincwadi ezithile, zamandulo kunye nezanamhlanje, ngakumbi kwiPliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, etc. Kodwa ndiyawudlula lo mbandela.

Ndiyazi ngamagqabantshintshi amane kuphela eSperm Whale enkulu; UColnett, ekaHuggins, ekaFrederick Cuvier, kunye noBeale. Kwisahluko esidlulileyo uColnett noCuvier kubhekiselwe kubo. I-Huggins's ibhetele kakhulu kuneyabo; kodwa, ngeengxaki ezinkulu, iBeale yeyona ilungileyo. Yonke imizobo yeBeale yalo mnenga ilungile, ngaphandle komzobo ophakathi kumfanekiso wemikhomo emithathu kwiimo zengqondo ezahlukeneyo, ibamba isahluko sakhe sesibini. Umphambili wakhe, izikhephe ezihlasela iSperm Whales, nangona ngokungathandabuzekiyo zibalwa ukuvuselela ukuthandabuza kwamadoda athile ahlala kwigumbi lokuhlala, ichanekile ngokuncomekayo kwaye ifana nobomi kwisiphumo sayo ngokubanzi. Eminye imizobo yeSperm Whale eJ. Ross Browne ichanekile kakuhle kwicontour; zikrolwe ububi. Asilotyala lakhe nangona.

Kwi-Right Whale, eyona mifanekiso ibalaseleyo yolwandlalo iku-Scoresby; kodwa zitsalwa kwisikali esincinane kakhulu ukuze zidlulise ingcamango enqwenelekayo. Unomfanekiso omnye wemiboniso yeminenga, kwaye oku kukunqongophala okulusizi, kuba kungenxa yemifanekiso enjalo kuphela, xa konke kwenziwe kakuhle, unokufumana nantoni na efana nombono oyinyaniso womnenga ophilayo njengoko ubonwa ngabazingeli bakhe abaphilayo.

Kodwa, kuthathwe kubo bonke, okona kulungileyo, nangona kwezinye iinkcukacha kungezona ezona zichanekileyo, imiboniso yeminenga kunye nemiboniso yeminenga inokufumaneka naphi na, yimikrolo emibini emikhulu yesiFrentshi, eyenziwe kakuhle, kwaye ithathwe kwimizobo yeGarnery enye. Ngokulandelelana, bamele ukuhlaselwa kweSperm kunye neRight Whale. Kumkrolo wokuqala iSperm Whale ebekekileyo iboniswa ngobungangamsha obupheleleyo bamandla, ivele yaphakama nje ngaphantsi kwesikhephe ukusuka kumbindi wolwandle, kwaye ithwele phezulu emoyeni emqolo ukuqhekeka okumanyumnyezi kwamaplanga eziko. Umphambili wephenyane awophukanga ngokuyinxenye, kwaye uzotywa nje ulungelelaniswe phezu komqolo werhamncwa; Kwaye ndimi kuloo prow, kuba enye i-fack yexesha elingasasebenziyo, ubona i-oarsman, isiqingatha esifakwe yi-Spoing ebilisiweyo se-Wight Spote, kunye nesenzo sokutsiba, ngokungathi sivela kwi-precidice. Isenzo sento yonke silungile ngokumangalisayo kwaye siyinyani. Ibhafu yomgca enesiqingatha engenanto idada kulwandle olumhlophe; Izibonda zokhuni zeeheshhons eziphalaziweyo ziyibopha kuyo; iintloko zabasebenzi abaqubhayo zithe saa malunga nomnenga ngeendlela ezahlukeneyo zokoyika; ngelixa kumgama omnyama osisaqhwithi inqanawa ijonge ezantsi kuloo ndawo. Impazamo enkulu inokufumaneka ngeenkcukacha ze-anatomical yale whale, kodwa idlulile; ekubeni, ngenxa yobomi bam, Andikwazanga ukuzoba entle kangaka.

Kumzobo wesibini, isikhephe sikwisenzo sokuzoba ecaleni kwecala elibiyelweyo loMnenga omkhulu obalekayo, oqengqa ukhula lwakhe olumnyama elwandle ngathi yi-mossy rock-slide evela kumawa ePatagonian. Iindiza zayo zithe tye, zizele, zimnyama njengothuthu; ukuze kuphuphume umsi onjalo kwitshimini, ungacinga ukuba kufuneka kuphekwe isidlo sangokuhlwa ngenkalipho kwizibilini ezinkulu ezingezantsi. Iintaka zaselwandle zigqogqa oononkala abancinane, iintlanzi zoonokrwece, nezinye iilekese zaselwandle neemaccaroni, ezithi maxa wambi zithwalwe nguMnenga oyiRight kumqolo wakhe ophilayo. Kwaye ngalo lonke elo xesha ileviyatan enemilebe engqindilili igxalathelana enzonzobileni, ishiya iitoni zamagaqa amhlophe axokozelayo ekuvukeni kwayo, kwaye ibangela isikhephe esincinci ukuba sishukume kwimibhobho efana nesikhephe esibanjiswe kufutshane namavili omkhombe wolwandle. Ngaloo ndlela, umphambili yingxokozelo; kodwa ngasemva, ngokwahlukileyo kobugcisa obuncomekayo, inqanaba leglasi lolwandle lizolile, iiseyile ezijingayo ezingenastatshi zenqanawa engenamandla, kunye nobunzima obungangeni bomnenga ofileyo, inqaba eyoyisiweyo, kunye neflegi yokubamba ixhonywe ngobuvila kwi-whale- Ipali ifakwe kumngxuma wayo.

Ngubani uGarnery umzobi, okanye wayengubani, andazi. Kodwa ubomi bam ngenxa yabo wayekwazi ukuthetha ngesifundo sakhe, okanye ngenye indlela ngokumangalisayo wayefundiswe ngumnenga onamava. AmaFrentshi ngabantwana bokwenza umsebenzi wokupeyinta. Hamba uye kujonga kuyo yonke imizobo yaseYurophu, kwaye uya kuyifumana phi igalari yokuphila kunye nesiphithiphithi sokuphefumla kwiseyile, njengakuloo holo yoloyiso eVersailles; apho obukeleyo esilwa ngendlela yakhe, upell-mell, ngamadabi amakhulu alandelelanayo eFransi; apho lonke ikrele libonakala lidanyaza kwiZibane zaseMntla, kwaye ookumkani abaxhobileyo abalandelelanayo kunye nama-Emperors atyhutyha, njengentlawulo yeecentaurs ezithweswe isithsaba? Ayifanelanga kwaphela indawo kule galari, zezi zixhobo zaselwandle zaseGarnery.

Ubuchule bendalo bamaFrentshi bokubamba umzobo wezinto bubonakala bubonakaliswa ngokukodwa kwimizobo nemizobo abanayo kwimiboniso yabo yokuloba. Ngaphandle kwesinye eshumini samava eNgilani kushishino lokuloba, kwaye ingeyiyo inxalenye yewaka yabemi baseMelika, nangona kunjalo baye banika ezi zizwe zombini imizobo ekuphela kwayo egqityiweyo kwaphela ekwaziyo ukuhambisa umoya wokwenene wokuzingela iminenga. Ubukhulu becala, abadwebi beminenga bamaNgesi nabaseMelika babonakala baneliseke ngokupheleleyo ngokubonisa ulwandlalo lwezinto, ezifana neprofayili engenamntu yomnenga; leyo, ngokumalunga nokubukeka kwesiphumo, imalunga nokuzoba iprofayile yephiramidi. Nkqu Scoresby, ngokusesikweni eyaziwayo Umnenga Right, emva kokuba esinike ubude obuqinileyo ngokupheleleyo umnenga Greenland, kunye ezintathu okanye ezine miniatures eethe-ethe of narwhale kunye porpoises, usiphatha kuthotho imikrolo classical amagwegwe iphenyane, iimela zokunqunqa, kunye grapnels; kunye nenkuthalo encinci ye-Leuwenhoeck inikezela ekuhlolweni kwehlabathi elingcangcazelayo amashumi alithoba anesithandathu afane ne-fac-similes yeekristale zekhephu ze-Arctic ezandisiweyo. Andithethi ukudelela umkhenkethi ogqwesileyo (ndiyamnika imbeko njengegqala), kodwa kumcimbi obaluleke kakhulu ngokuqinisekileyo yayikukubeka iliso kungakhange kufumaneke i-afidavithi efungelweyo ethathwe phambi koBulungisa boXolo baseGreenland.

Ukongezelela kuloo mikrolo mihle evela eGarnery, kukho eminye imikrolo emibini yesiFrentshi ekufanele iphawulwe, ngumntu othi “H. Upon this, the seamen rushed to the yard-arms, as in swarming-time the bees rush to the boughs. A vast pulpy mass, furlongs in length and breadth, of a glancing cream-colour, lay floating on the water, innumerable long arms radiating from its centre, and curling and twisting like a nest of anacondas, as if blindly to clutch at any hapless object within reach. “The great live squid, which, they say, few whale-ships ever beheld, and returned to their ports to tell of it.”

But Ahab said nothing; turning his boat, he sailed back to the vessel; the rest as silently following.

Whatever superstitions the sperm whalemen in general have connected with the sight of this object, certain it is, that a glimpse of it being so very unusual, that circumstance has gone far to invest it with portentousness. So rarely is it beheld, that though one and all of them declare it to be the largest animated thing in the ocean, yet very few of them have any but the most vague ideas concerning its true nature and form; notwithstanding, they believe it to furnish to the sperm whale his only food. For though other species of whales find their food above water, and may be seen by man in the act of feeding, the spermaceti whale obtains his whole food in unknown zones below the surface; and only by inference is it that any one can tell of what, precisely, that food consists. At times, when closely pursued, he will disgorge what are supposed to be the detached arms of the squid; some of them thus exhibited exceeding twenty and thirty feet in length. They fancy that the monster to which these arms belonged ordinarily clings by them to the bed of the ocean; and that the sperm whale, unlike other species, is supplied with teeth in order to attack and tear it.

There seems some ground to imagine that the great Kraken of Bishop Pontoppodan may ultimately resolve itself into Squid. The manner in which the Bishop describes it, as alternately rising and sinking, with some other particulars he narrates, in all this the two correspond. But much abatement is necessary with respect to the incredible bulk he assigns it.

By some naturalists who have vaguely heard rumors of the mysterious creature, here spoken of, it is included among the class of cuttle-fish, to which, indeed, in certain external respects it would seem to belong, but only as the Anak of the tribe.





CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
