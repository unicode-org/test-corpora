ISAHLUKO 53. I-Gam.

Esona sizathu sicacileyo sokuba uAhabhi angangeni enqanaweni yokuloba iminenga esithe sayithetha yile: Umoya nolwandle lwazisa isaqhwithi. Kodwa nangona oku bekungenjalo, ebengayi kuthi emva kwayo yonke loo nto, mhlawumbi, akhwele kuye-egweba ngokuziphatha kwakhe okwalandelayo ngezihlandlo ezifanayo-ukuba bekunjalo, ngenkqubo yokudumisa, wafumana impendulo engalunganga kumbuzo awawubekayo. Kuba, njengoko kwavelayo ekugqibeleni, akazange akhathale ukunxulumana, nokuba yimizuzu emihlanu, kunye naye nawuphi na ukapteni wasemzini, ngaphandle kokuba wayenokunikela ngolunye ulwazi awayelufuna ngokunzulu. Kodwa konke oku kunokuhlala kuqikelelwa ngokungafanelekanga, ibingeyonto ithethwa apha ngokusetyenziswa okungaqhelekanga kwemikhumbi yokuloba iminenga xa idibana kulwandle lwasemzini, ngakumbi kumhlaba oqhelekileyo wokuhamba ngenqanawa.

Ukuba abantu ababini abangabaziyo bawela iPine Barrens kwiNew York State, okanye iSalisbury Plain eNgilane; ukuba ukudibana ngokungaqhelekanga kumhlaba ongento yanto, aba babini, ubomi babo bonke, abanakukuphepha kakuhle ukubulisa; kunye nokuma umzuzwana ukusasaza iindaba; kwaye, mhlawumbi, ehleli phantsi ixeshana kunye nokuphumla kwikonsathi: ke, kangakanani ngokwendalo ukuba phezu iillimitable Pine Barrens kunye Salisbury Amathafa olwandle, iinqanawa ezimbini whaling descrying enye kwenye ekupheleni komhlaba-off lone Fanning's Island, okanye kude Mills kaKumkani; kangakanani ngokwendalo, ndithi, ukuba phantsi kweemeko ezinjalo ezi nqanawa kufuneka zingatshintshi nje kuphela izichotho, kodwa zifike kuqhagamshelwano olusondeleyo, olunobuhlobo kunye nobudlelwane. Kwaye ngokukodwa oku kuya kubonakala kuyinyaniso, kwimiba yeenqanawa ezikwichweba elinye, ezithe abathetheli bazo, namagosa, namadoda aliqela abasazana ngokwawo; kwaye ngenxa yoko, ube nazo zonke iintlobo zezinto zasekhaya ezithandekayo zokuthetha ngazo.

Kuba inqanawa ende engekhoyo, umda wangaphandle, mhlawumbi, unoonobumba ebhodini; nangayiphi na indlela, uya kuqiniseka ukuba uya kumvumela ukuba abe namaphepha omhla emva konyaka okanye emibini emva kwelokugqibela kwiifayile zakhe ezimfiliba nezigutywe ngobhontsi. Kwaye ngembuyekezo yaloo mbeko, inqanawa eya ngaphandle yayiza kufumana ulwazi lwamva nje lokuloba iminenga kwindawo ekusenokwenzeka ukuba iya kuyo, eyona nto ibaluleke kakhulu kuyo. Kwaye ngokweqondo, konke oku kuya kuba yinyaniso malunga nemikhumbi yokuloba iminenga enqumla indlela enye kwenye kumhlaba wokukhenketha ngokwawo, nangona zide ngokulinganayo zingekho ekhaya. Kuba omnye wabo usenokuba ufumene ukudluliselwa kweencwadi ukusuka kwesinye isithathu, kwaye ngoku inqanawa ekude kakhulu; kwaye ezinye zezo leta zisenokuba zezabantu benqanawa adibana nabo ngoku. Ngaphandle koko, babetshintshiselana ngeendaba zokuloba iminenga, baze bancokole kamnandi. Kuba babengayi kuhlangana kuphela nazo zonke iimfesane zoomatiloshe, kodwa kwangokunjalo nangazo zonke iingqondo ezingaqhelekanga ezivela kusukelo olufanayo kunye nokuswela kunye neengozi ezikwabelwana ngazo.

Kananjalo umahluko welizwe awunakwenza nawuphi na umahluko obaluleke kakhulu; oko kukuthi, ukuba nje omabini amaqela ethetha ulwimi olunye, njengoko kunjalo naseMelika kunye nesiNgesi. Nangona, ngokuqinisekileyo, ukusuka kwinani elincinci labalobi bamaNgesi, iintlanganiso ezinjalo azisoloko zisenzeka, kwaye xa zisenzeka kukho kakhulu ukuba kubekho uhlobo lweentloni phakathi kwazo; kuba iNgesi yakho igcinwe, kwaye uYankee wakho, akayifuni loo nto nakubani na ngaphandle kwakhe. Ngaphandle koko, abalobi beminenga besiNgesi ngamanye amaxesha bachaphazela uhlobo oluthile lokuphakama kwe-metropolitan ngaphezu kwe-whalers yaseMelika; malunga ne-Nantucketer ende, ebhityileyo, kunye ne-nondescript provincialisms, njengohlobo lwabalimi baselwandle. Kodwa apho obu bukhulu kwi-whalemen yesiNgesi bubandakanya ngokwenene, kuya kuba nzima ukuthetha, ukubona ukuba i-Yankees ngosuku olunye, ngokudibeneyo, ibulala iminenga ngaphezu kwayo yonke iNgesi, ngokudibeneyo, kwiminyaka elishumi. Ngelixa, ezinye iinqanawa zabarhwebi eziwela ukuvuka omnye komnye kumbindi we-Atlantiki, ziya kudlula ngokufuthi ngaphandle kwegama elinye lokuqaphela, zisikwa ngokulinganayo kulwandle oluphezulu, njengebrace yedandi eBroadway; kwaye ngalo lonke ixesha sizixakekisa, mhlawumbi, ekugxekeni okokugqibela komnye nomnye. Ngokuphathelele amadoda eMfazwe, xa efumana ithuba lokudibana elwandle, aqala adlule kwimitya yokugoba yobudenge kunye ne-scrapings, i-ducking enjalo ye-signs, kangangokuba akubonakali kukho ukuthanda okuhle kunye nothando lobuzalwana malunga nalo. Ngokuphathelele amaPirates, xa efumana ithuba lokuwela amathambo anqamlezileyo omnye komnye, isichotho sokuqala sithi—“Zingaphi iinkakhayi?”—indlela efanayo naleyo abawa ngayo abalo balobi—“Mingaphi imiphanda?” Kwaye loo mbuzo wakha waphendulwa, abaphangi bayahlukana kwangoko, kuba bangama-infernal kumacala omabini, kwaye abathandi ukubona izinto ezininzi ezimbi zomnye nomnye.

Kutheni le nto bonke oomatiloshe, kwanabo bonke oomatiloshe kunye namadoda e-Man-of-War, kunye nabaqhubi beenqanawa ze-Slave-ship, bayayixabisa imvakalelo elolo hlobo yendelelo ngakwi-Whale-ships; lo ngumbuzo kunganzima ukuwuphendula. Kuba, kwimeko yabaphangi, ndithi, ndingathanda ukwazi ukuba ngaba lo msebenzi wabo unalo naluphi na uzuko olukhethekileyo malunga nawo. Ngamanye amaxesha iphelela ekuphakameni okungaqhelekanga, ngokwenene; kodwa kuphela emthini. Kwaye ngaphandle koko, xa umntu ephakanyiswe ngaloo ndlela ingaqhelekanga, akanaso isiseko esifanelekileyo sokuphakama kwakhe okuphezulu. Ngenxa yoko, ndiphetha, ukuba ngokuziqhayisa ngokuphakanyiswa ngaphezu kwe-whaleman, kuloo ntetho i-pirate ayinaso isiseko esiqinileyo sokuma.
