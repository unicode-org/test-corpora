KAPITULO 89. Napartak-Ikan ken Nalukay-Ikan.

Ti pannakatukoy ti waif ken waif-pole iti maudi a kapitulo ngem maysa, kasapulanna ti sumagmamano a salaysay maipapan kadagiti linteg ken pagannurotan ti panagkalap iti balyena, a ti waif ket mabalin a maibilang a ti engrande a simbolo ken badge.

Masansan a mapasamak a no sumagmamano a barko ti agdaldaliasat a kadua, mabalin a madungpar ti maysa a barko ti maysa a balyena, kalpasanna makalibas, ket kamaudiananna mapapatay ken matiliw ti sabali a barko; ket ditoy ket saan a direkta a buklen ti adu a babassit a pasamak, nga amin ket makipaset iti daytoy maysa nga engrande a tampok. Kas pagarigan,—kalpasan ti nabannog ken napeggad a panangkamat ken panangtiliw iti balyena, mabalin a maluk-atan ti bangkay manipud iti barko gapu iti naranggas a bagyo; ket agtaytayab iti adayo nga agturong iti leeward, alaen manen ti maikadua a mangngalap iti balyena, a, iti kalmado, nasayaat ti panangguyodna iti dayta iti abayna, nga awan ti peggad ti biag wenno linia. Gapuna masansan a tumaud dagiti makariribuk ken naranggas unay a panagsusupiat iti nagbaetan dagiti mangngalap, no awan ti sumagmamano a naisurat wenno di naisurat, sapasap, di masupiat a linteg a mayaplikar iti amin a kaso.

Nalabit ti kakaisuna a pormal a kodigo ti panagkalap iti balyena nga autorisado babaen ti lehislatibo a linteg, ket ti Holland. Daytoy ket inbilin babaen dagiti Estado-Heneral idi AD 1695. Ngem nupay awan ti sabali a nasion a naaddaan iti ania man a naisurat a linteg ti panagkalap ti balyena, kaskasdi dagiti mangngalap nga Amerikano ket nagbalinda a bukodda a lehislador ken abogado iti daytoy a banag. Nangipaayda iti sistema a para iti ababa a komprehensibo ket lab-awanna dagiti Pandect ni Justinian ken dagiti By-laws ti Chinese Society for the Suppression of Meddling with other People’s Business. Wen; dagitoy a linteg mabalin a naikitikit iti farthing ni Reyna Anne, wenno iti barb ti arpoon, ken maikawes iti tengnged, kasta unay ti kabassitda.

I. Ti Fast-Fish ket kukua ti party fast to it.

II. Ti Loose-Fish ket fair game para iti siasinoman a kabiitan a makatiliw iti dayta.

Ngem ti mangay-ayam iti kinaranggas iti daytoy nasigo a kodigo ket ti mapadayawan a kinaababa dayta, a kasapulan ti dakkel a tomo dagiti komentaryo tapno mailawlawag dayta.

Umuna: Ania ti Fast-Fish? Sibibiag man wenno natay ti ikan ket teknikal a napartak, no maikonektar dayta iti okupado a barko wenno bangka, babaen ti aniaman a medium a pulos a makontrol ti agluglugan wenno agluglugan,—maysa a palo, maysa a gaud, siam a pulgada a kable, alambre ti telegrapo, wenno maysa a tali ti uleg, agpapada amin dayta. Kasta met ti ikan ket teknikal a napartak no addaan iti waif, wenno aniaman a sabali pay a mabigbigbig a simbolo ti panagtagikua; no la ket ta ti partido a mangiwaksi iti dayta nalawag nga paneknekanna ti abilidadda iti aniaman nga oras a mangikuyog iti dayta, kasta met ti panggepda nga aramiden dayta.

Dagitoy ket sientipiko a komentaryo; ngem dagiti komentaryo dagiti mismo a balyena buklen no dadduma dagiti natangken a sasao ken narigrigat a panagtuktok —ti Coke-upon-Littleton ti dakulap. Pudno, kadagiti ad-adda a nalinteg ken nadayaw a balyena kanayon a maaramid dagiti allowance para kadagiti karkarna a kaso, a sadiay maysa a nakabutbuteng a moral a kinaawan hustisia ti maysa a partido a mangibaga nga addaan iti balyena a dati a kinamat wenno pinatay ti sabali a partido. Ngem saan a pulos a kasta ti kinaeskrupulo ti dadduma.

Agarup limapulo a tawenen ti napalabas adda maysa a makausioso a kaso ti whale-trover a naidarum idiay Inglatera, a sadiay dagiti nangidarum inlanad dayta kalpasan ti nainget a panangkamatda iti balyena kadagiti baybay ti Amianan; ket idi pudno a nagballigida (dagiti nangidarum) a nang-harpoon kadagiti ikan; kamaudiananna, babaen ti peggad ti biagda, obligadoda a mangbaybay-a saan laeng a dagiti liniada, no di ket ti bangkada a mismo. Kamaudiananna dagiti naidarum (dagiti tripulante ti sabali a barko) ket nakapanunotda iti balyena, kinabilda, pinapatayda, inagawda, ken kamaudiananna inyawatda dayta iti sanguanan dagiti mismo a mata dagiti nangidarum. Ket idi nasuppiat dagita a naidarum, insina ti kapitanda dagiti ramayna kadagiti ngipen dagiti nangidarum, ket impasiguradona kadakuada a babaen ti doxology iti aramid nga inaramidna, pagtalinaedenna itan ti linia, arpoon, ken bangkada, a nagtalinaed a naikapet iti balyena iti tiempo ti pannakasamsam. Gapuna dagiti nangidarum indarumda itan para iti pannakaisubli ti pateg ti balyena, linia, harpoon, ken bangkada.

Ni Mr. Erskine ti abogado dagiti naidarum; Ni Lord Ellenborough ti hues. Iti panaglabas ti depensa, intuloy ti napalangguad nga Erskine nga inladawan ti takderna, babaen ti panangtukoyna iti nabiit pay a krimen. con. kaso, a ti maysa a gentleman, kalpasan ti awan mamaayna a panangpadasna a mangtengngel iti kinaranggas ni baketna, kamaudiananna binaybay-anna isuna kadagiti baybay ti biag; ngem iti panaglabas dagiti tawen, gapu iti panagbabawina iti dayta nga addang, nangirugi iti tignay tapno makasubli iti panangtagikua kenkuana. Adda ni Erskine iti sabali a bangir; ket kalpasanna sinuportaranna dayta babaen ti panangibagana, a nupay ti gentleman ket orihinal a nang-harpoon iti señora, ken naminsan nga agayuno, ken gapu laeng iti dakkel a stress ti agtinnag a kinaranggasna, kamaudiananna binaybay-anna; kaskasdi a binaybay-anna isuna inaramidna, isu a nagbalin a nalukay nga ikan; ket ngarud idi a ti simmaruno a gentleman ti nang-harpoon manen kenkuana, nagbalin idin ti señora a sanikua dayta simmaruno a gentleman, agraman ti aniaman nga arpoon a mabalin a nasarakan a nakapetpet kenkuana.

Ita iti agdama a kaso inkontra ni Erskine a dagiti pagarigan ti balyena ken ti babai ket agsinnumbangir a mangiladawan iti tunggal maysa.

Dagitoy a panagpakaasi, ken dagiti kontra-pakaasi, gapu ta siuumiso a nangngegan, ti de adal unay nga ukom kadagiti naikeddeng a termino inkeddengna, a kayatna a sawen,—A no maipapan iti bangka, intedna dayta kadagiti nangidarum, gapu ta binaybay-anda laeng dayta tapno maisalakan ti biagda; ngem no maipapan iti mapagsusupiatan a balyena, harpoon, ken linia, kukuada dagiti naidarum; ti balyena, gapu ta maysa dayta a Loose-Fish idi tiempo ti maudi a pannakatiliwna; ken dagiti arpoon ken linia gapu ta idi nag-off ti ikan kadagita, nakagun-od dayta (ti ikan) iti sanikua kadagita nga artikulo; ket gapuna ti asinoman a kalpasanna nangala kadagiti ikan ket addaan kalintegan kadagita. Ita dagiti naidarum kalpasanna innalada dagiti ikan; ergo, kukuada dagiti nadakamat nga artikulo.

Ti gagangay a tao a mangmatmatmat iti daytoy a desision ti de adal unay nga Ukom, mabalin a bumusor iti dayta. Ngem naarado agingga iti kangrunaan a bato ti banag, dagiti dua a naindaklan a prinsipio a naikeddeng kadagiti singin a linteg ti panagkalap iti balyena a dati a naadaw, ken inyaplikar ken inlawlawag ni Lord Ellenborough iti nadakamat a kaso; dagitoy dua a linteg a mangsagid iti Fast-Fish ken Loose-Fish, kunak, no panunoten, masarakan dagiti pundamental ti amin a natauan a jurisprudence; ta iti laksid ti narikut a tracery ti eskulturana, ti Templo ti Linteg, kas iti Templo dagiti Filisteo, addaan laeng iti dua a props a pagtakderan.

Saan kadi a pagsasao iti ngiwat ti tunggal maysa, Ti panagtagikua ket kagudua ti linteg: kayatna a sawen, uray kasano ti pannakatagikua ti banag? Ngem masansan a ti panagtagikua ti pakabuklan ti linteg. Ania dagiti sinews ken kararua dagiti Russian serfs ken Republican slaves no di ti Fast-Fish, a ti panagtagikua ket isu ti intero a linteg? Ania iti rapacious landlord ti maudi a mite ti balo ngem maysa a Fast-Fish? Ania ti adda sadiay di naduktalan a mansion ti marmol ti kontrabida nga addaan iti door-plate para iti waif; ania dayta no di ti Fast-Fish? Ania ti makadadael a diskuento a magun-od ni Mardokeo, ti broker, manipud iti nakurapay a Woebegone, ti nabangkarote, iti pautang tapno saan a mabisin ti pamilia ni Woebegone; ania dayta makadadael a diskuento no di ti Fast-Fish? Ania ti kita ti Arsobispo ti Savesoul a £100,000 a naala manipud iti bassit a tinapay ken keso ti ginasut a ribu a naburak ti likudanna a trabahador (sigurado amin iti langit nga awan ti aniaman a tulong ni Savesoul) ania dayta globular a £100,000 no di ket maysa a Fast-Fish? Ania dagiti natawid nga ili ken purok ti Duke of Dunder no di ti Fast-Fish? Ania iti dayta mapagduaduaan a harpooneer, ni John Bull, ti nakurapay nga Ireland, ngem maysa a Fast-Fish? Ania ngay iti dayta apostoliko a lansa, ni Kabsat Jonathan, ti Texas ngem maysa a Fast-Fish? Ket maipanggep kadagitoy amin, saan kadi a ti Panagtagikua ti intero a linteg?

Ngem no ti doktrina ti Fast-Fish ket medio sapasap a mayaplikar, ti agkakabagian a doktrina ti Loose-Fish ket ad-adda pay a nasaknap a kasta. Mayaplikar dayta iti internasional ken sapasap.

Ania ti America idi 1492 no di ti maysa a Loose-Fish, a sadiay ni Columbus ket nangdungpar iti pagalagadan ti Espania babaen ti panangiwaksina iti dayta para iti naarian nga apo ken apona? Ania idi ti Poland ken ni Czar? Ania a Grecia iti Turko? Ania ti India nga agturong iti Inglatera? Anianto kamaudiananna ti Mexico iti Estados Unidos? Amin a Nalukay-Ikan.

Ania dagiti Karbengan ti Tao ken dagiti Wayawaya ti Lubong no di ti Loose-Fish? Ania amin ti panunot ken opinion dagiti lallaki no di ni Loose-Fish? Ania ti prinsipio ti narelihiosuan a pammati kadakuada no di ti Loose-Fish? Ania kadagiti agpampammarang nga agipuslit a berbalista ti pampanunot dagiti agpampanunot ngem ni Loose-Fish? Ania ti dakkel a globo a mismo no di ti Loose-Fish? Ket aniaka, agbasbasa, no di ket maysa a Loose-Fish ken maysa a Fast-Fish, met?





KAPITULO 90. Ulo wenno Ipus.

“Umdasen ti balena vero, si rex habeat caput, et regina caudam.” Bracton, nga l. 3, c. 3. 3.

Ti Latin manipud kadagiti libro ti Linteg ti Inglatera, a naala agraman ti konteksto, kaipapananna, a kadagiti amin a balyena a natiliw ti asinoman iti kosta dayta a daga, ti Ari, kas Honorary Grand Harpooneer, masapul nga addaan iti ulo, ken ti Reyna siraraem a naiparang a kadua ti ipus. Maysa a pannakabingbingay a, iti balyena, ket umasping unay iti pananggudua iti mansanas; awan ti nagbaetan a nabati. Ita kas daytoy a linteg, iti sidong ti nabaliwan a porma, ket agingga ita ket maipakat idiay Inglatera; ket bayat nga itukonna iti nadumaduma a banag ti karkarna nga anomalia a mangsagid iti sapasap a linteg ti Napartak ken Nalukay-Ikan, natratar ditoy iti naisina a kapitulo, iti isu met laeng a nadayaw a prinsipio a mangtignay kadagiti riles ti Inglatera nga adda iti gastos ti naisina a kotse , a naisangsangayan a naireserba para iti pagdagusan dagiti naarian. Iti umuna a lugar, kas mausisa a pammaneknek iti kinapudno a ti nadakamat a linteg ket maipakat pay laeng, ituloyko nga idatag iti sanguananyo ti maysa a kasasaad a napasamak iti uneg ti napalabas a dua a tawen.

Kasla dadduma a napudno a marinero ti Dover, wenno Sandwich, wenno dadduma kadagiti Cinque Ports, ket kalpasan ti nainget a panangkamatda nagballigi a nangpapatay ken nangikabil iti aplaya iti napino a balyena nga orihinal a dineskribirda iti adayo manipud iti takdang. Ita dagiti Cinque Ports ket paset wenno uray kasano ket adda iti sidong ti hurisdiksion ti maysa a kita ti polis wenno beadle, a maawagan iti Lord Warden. Ti panangtengngel iti opisina a direkta manipud iti korona, patiek, amin dagiti naarian nga emoluments a pasamak kadagiti teritorio ti Cinque Port agbalin babaen ti assignment a kukuana. Babaen ti dadduma a mannurat daytoy nga opisina ket maawagan iti sinecure. Ngem saan a kasta. Gapu ta okupado nga agtartrabaho ti Lord Warden no dadduma a mang-fobbing kadagiti perquisites-na; nga isuda ti kangrunaan a kukuana gapu iti dayta met laeng a fobbing kadakuada.

Ita idi dagitoy a napanglaw a marinero a napuoran iti init, a di nakasapatos, ken addaan kadagiti pantalon a nalukneng iti nangato kadagiti eely a sakada, ket nabannogda a nangguyod kadagiti nalukmeg nga ikanda iti nangato ken namaga, nga inkarida iti bagbagida ti nasayaat a £150 manipud iti napateg a lana ken tulang; ket iti pantasia a mangsipsiput iti manmano a tsa a kaduada dagiti assawada, ken nasayaat nga ale a kaduada dagiti kroniada, iti pigsa ti bukodda a bingay; up steps a de adal unay ken kaaduan a Kristiano ken naparabur a gentleman, nga addaan iti kopia ti Blackstone iti sirok ti takiagna; ket iparabawna dayta iti ulo ti balyena, kunana—“Ikkaten dagiti ima! daytoy nga ikan, appoko, ket Fast-Fish. Sakupek a kas kukua ti Lord Warden.” Iti daytoy dagiti napanglaw a marinero iti nadayaw a pannakakigtotda —pudno unay nga Ingles —a dida ammo no ania ti ibagada, matnagda a sipipinget a mangkugtar kadagiti uloda iti intero a aglawlaw; bayat a siladingit a nagsiddaaw manipud iti balyena nga agturong iti ganggannaet. Ngem dayta ti saan a pulos a nangtarimaan iti banag, wenno pulos a nangpalukneng iti natangken a puso ti de adal a gentleman babaen ti kopia ti Blackstone. Iti maysa a sao, natiliw ken nailako ti balyena, ket ti Grace-na a Duke ti Wellington ti nakaawat iti kuarta. Gapu ta impagarupna a no matmatan iti sumagmamano a partikular a lawag, ti kaso mabalin a babaen ti lamolamo a posibilidad iti sumagmamano a bassit a tukad ket maibilang, iti sidong dagiti kasasaad, a medio narigat, ti maysa a napudno a klero ti ili siraraem a nangiturong iti maysa a nota iti Grace-na, nga agpakpakaasi kenkuana nga alaenna ti kaso dagidiay daksanggasat a marinero iti naan-anay a konsiderasion. Iti dayta insungbat ni Apok a Duke iti substansia (agpada a naipablaak dagiti surat) nga inaramidnan dayta, ket naawatnan ti kuarta, ket obligado iti nadayaw a gentleman no para iti masakbayan isu (ti reverend gentleman) ket agkedked a makibiang kadagiti surat ti dadduma a tattao negosio. Daytoy kadi ti militante pay laeng a lakay, a nakatakder kadagiti suli ti tallo a pagarian, iti amin nga ima a mangpilpilit kadagiti limos dagiti agpalpalama?

Masapul a kasapulantayo ti agsaludsod kalpasanna no ania a prinsipio ti orihinal a napuonan ti Soberano iti dayta a kalintegan. Nailanaden ti linteg a mismo. Ngem ited kadatayo ni Plowdon ti rason dayta. Ngem apay koma a ti Ari ti addaan iti ulo, ken ti Reyna ti ipus? Maysa a rason iti dayta, dakayo nga abogado!

Ngem daytoy met laeng a tulang ket awan iti ipus; adda dayta iti ulo, a nakalkaldaang a biddut para iti nasirib nga abogado a kas ken ni Prynne. Ngem sirena kadi ti Reyna, a maiparang iti ipus? Mabalin nga aglemlemmeng ditoy ti alegorikal a kaipapanan.

Adda dua a naarian nga ikan iti kasta nga estilo dagiti Ingles a mannurat iti linteg —ti balyena ken ti sturgeon; agpada a naarian a sanikua iti sidong ti sumagmamano a limitasion, ken nominalmente a mangsuplay iti maikasangapulo a sanga ti gagangay a kita ti korona. Diak ammo nga adda sabali nga autor a nangipasimudaag iti dayta a banag; ngem babaen ti panangipapan kasla kaniak masapul a mabingay ti sturgeon iti isu met laeng a pamay-an a kas iti balyena, nga umawat ti Ari iti napuskol unay ken nababaneg nga ulo a karkarna iti dayta nga ikan, a, no simboliko a maibilang, mabalin a posible a nakakatkatawa a naibatay iti sumagmamano a maipagarup a kinamanagayat. Ket iti kasta kasla adda rason iti amin a banag, uray iti linteg.

“Awan mamaayna ti panangrake para ken Ambergriese iti paunch daytoy a Leviathan, di maibturan a fetor a mangilibak a saan a panagsaludsod.” Sir T. Browne, ken ni V. E

Maysa wenno dua a lawas kalpasan a naisalaysay ti maudi nga eksena ti panagkalap iti balyena, ket idi in-inut nga aglayagkami iti rabaw ti makaturog, naalingasaw, tengnga ti aldaw a baybay, a dagiti adu nga agong iti kubierta ti Pequod ket napaneknekan nga ad-adda a nasiput a makadiskobre ngem dagiti tallo a paris a mata iti ngato. Naangot iti baybay ti karkarna ken saan unay a makaay-ayo nga angot.

“Adda ipustaak itan,” kinuna ni Stubb, “nga iti sadinoman ditoy ket adda sumagmamano kadagidiay nadroga a balyena a kinititikmi idi napan nga aldaw. Impagarupko nga ag-keel up-da di agbayag.”

Iti agdama, nasaksakbay a nagdisso dagiti alingasaw; ket adda sadiay iti adayo ti maysa a barko, a dagiti nalukneng a layag ti mangipasimudaag a masapul nga adda iti sibay ti sumagmamano a kita ti balyena. Bayat nga umas-asidegkami, impakita ti ganggannaet dagiti kolor a Pranses manipud iti pantokna; ket babaen ti agtaytayab nga ulep dagiti buitre a tumatayab iti baybay a nagsirkulo, ken agtaytayab, ken agtaytayab iti aglawlawna, nalawag a ti balyena iti sibayna ket masapul nga awagan dagiti mangngalap a naburak a balyena, kayatna a sawen, maysa a balyena a natay a di nariribuk iti baybay , ket ngarud naglugan ti di maitutop a bangkay. Mabalin a maipagarup a naimbag, anian a di nasam-it nga angot ti masapul nga iruar ti kasta a masa; nakarkaro ngem iti siudad ti Asiria iti saplit, idinto ta saan a makabael dagiti sibibiag a mangitabon kadagiti pimmusay. Pudno a di maibturan unay dayta nga ibilang ti dadduma, nga awan ti kinaagum a makaallukoy kadakuada nga ag-moor iti sibay dayta. Kaskasdi adda kadi dagidiay mangaramidto pay laeng iti dayta; iti laksid ti kinapudno a ti lana a nagun-od manipud kadagita a suheto ket nababbaba unay ti kalidadna, ken saan a kas iti kinatao ti attar-of-rose.

Idi umas-asideg pay laeng gapu iti agleppas nga angin, nakitami nga adda maikadua a balyena ti Pranses iti sibayna; ket kasla ad-adda pay a nosegay daytoy maikadua a balyena ngem iti umuna. Kinapudnona, nagbalin a maysa dayta kadagidiay problemado a balyena a kasla agmaga ken matay nga addaan iti maysa a kita ti nakaskasdaaw a dyspepsia, wenno di pannakarunaw ti taraon; a mangbaybay-a kadagiti saanen a maus-usar a bagida a dandani interamente a nabangkarote iti aniaman a kas iti lana. Nupay kasta, iti umiso a lugar makitatayto nga awan ti makaammo a mangngalap a pulos a mangitag-ay iti agongna iti kasta a balyena a kas iti daytoy, nupay kasano ti panangliklikna kadagiti napabettak a balyena iti pangkaaduan.

Naasideg itan ti Pequod iti ganggannaet, isu nga inkarina ni Stubb a nabigbigna ti agputputed a palad-a-postena a naisinggalut kadagiti linia a naisinggalut iti ipus ti maysa kadagitoy a balyena.

I should like to conclude the chapter with the above appeal, but cannot, owing to my anxiety to repel a charge often made against whalemen, and which, in the estimation of some already biased minds, might be considered as indirectly substantiated by what has been said of the Frenchman's two whales. Elsewhere in this volume the slanderous aspersion has been disproved, that the vocation of whaling is throughout a slatternly, untidy business. But there is another thing to rebut. They hint that all whales always smell bad. Now how did this odious stigma originate?

I opine, that it is plainly traceable to the first arrival of the Greenland whaling ships in London, more than two centuries ago. Because those whalemen did not then, and do not now, try out their oil at sea as the Southern ships have always done; but cutting up the fresh blubber in small bits, thrust it through the bung holes of large casks, and carry it home in that manner; the shortness of the season in those Icy Seas, and the sudden and violent storms to which they are exposed, forbidding any other course. The consequence is, that upon breaking into the hold, and unloading one of these whale cemeteries, in the Greenland dock, a savor is given forth somewhat similar to that arising from excavating an old city grave-yard, for the foundations of a Lying-in Hospital.

I partly surmise also, that this wicked charge against whalers may be likewise imputed to the existence on the coast of Greenland, in former times, of a Dutch village called Schmerenburgh or Smeerenberg, which latter name is the one used by the learned Fogo Von Slack, in his great work on Smells, a text-book on that subject. As its name imports (smeer, fat; berg, to put up), this village was founded in order to afford a place for the blubber of the Dutch whale fleet to be tried out, without being taken home to Holland for that purpose. It was a collection of furnaces, fat-kettles, and oil sheds; and when the works were in full operation certainly gave forth no very pleasant savor. But all this is quite different with a South Sea Sperm Whaler; which in a voyage of four years perhaps, after completely filling her hold with oil, does not, perhaps, consume fifty days in the business of boiling out; and in the state that it is casked, the oil is nearly scentless. The truth is, that living or dead, if but decently treated, whales as a species are by no means creatures of ill odor; nor can whalemen be recognised, as the people of the middle ages affected to detect a Jew in the company, by the nose. Nor indeed can the whale possibly be otherwise than fragrant, when, as a general thing, he enjoys such high health; taking abundance of exercise; always out of doors; though, it is true, seldom in the open air. I say, that the motion of a Sperm Whale's flukes above water dispenses a perfume, as when a musk-scented lady rustles her dress in a warm parlor. What then shall I liken the Sperm Whale to for fragrance, considering his magnitude? Must it not be to that famous elephant, with jewelled tusks, and redolent with myrrh, which was led out of an Indian town to do honor to Alexander the Great?





CHAPTER 93. The Castaway.

It was but some few days after encountering the Frenchman, that a most significant event befell the most insignificant of the Pequod's crew; an event most lamentable; and which ended in providing the sometimes madly merry and predestinated craft with a living and ever accompanying prophecy of whatever shattered sequel might prove her own.

Now, in the whale ship, it is not every one that goes in the boats. Some few hands are reserved called ship-keepers, whose province it is to work the vessel while the boats are pursuing the whale. As a general thing, these ship-keepers are as hardy fellows as the men comprising the boats' crews. But if there happen to be an unduly slender, clumsy, or timorous wight in the ship, that wight is certain to be made a ship-keeper. It was so in the Pequod with the little negro Pippin by nick-name, Pip by abbreviation. Poor Pip! ye have heard of him before; ye must remember his tambourine on that dramatic midnight, so gloomy-jolly.

In outer aspect, Pip and Dough-Boy made a match, like a black pony and a white one, of equal developments, though of dissimilar colour, driven in one eccentric span. But while hapless Dough-Boy was by nature dull and torpid in his intellects, Pip, though over tender-hearted, was at bottom very bright, with that pleasant, genial, jolly brightness peculiar to his tribe; a tribe, which ever enjoy all holidays and festivities with finer, freer relish than any other race. For blacks, the year's calendar should show naught but three hundred and sixty-five Fourth of Julys and New Year's Days. Nor smile so, while I write that this little black was brilliant, for even blackness has its brilliancy; behold yon lustrous ebony, panelled in king's cabinets. But Pip loved life, and all life's peaceable securities; so that the panic-striking business in which he had somehow unaccountably become entrapped, had most sadly blurred his brightness; though, as ere long will be seen, what was thus temporarily subdued in him, in the end was destined to be luridly illumined by strange wild fires, that fictitiously showed him off to ten times the natural lustre with which in his native Tolland County in Connecticut, he had once enlivened many a fiddler's frolic on the green; and at melodious even-tide, with his gay ha-ha! had turned the round horizon into one star-belled tambourine. So, though in the clear air of day, suspended against a blue-veined neck, the pure-watered diamond drop will healthful glow; yet, when the cunning jeweller would show you the diamond in its most impressive lustre, he lays it against a gloomy ground, and then lights it up, not by the sun, but by some unnatural gases. Then come out those fiery effulgences, infernally superb; then the evil-blazing diamond, once the divinest symbol of the crystal skies, looks like some crown-jewel stolen from the King of Hell. But let us to the story.

It came to pass, that in the ambergris affair Stubb's after-oarsman chanced so to sprain his hand, as for a time to become quite maimed; and, temporarily, Pip was put into his place.

The first time Stubb lowered with him, Pip evinced much nervousness; but happily, for that time, escaped close contact with the whale; and therefore came off not altogether discreditably; though Stubb observing him, took care, afterwards, to exhort him to cherish his courageousness to the utmost, for he might often find it needful.

Now upon the second lowering, the boat paddled upon the whale; and as the fish received the darted iron, it gave its customary rap, which happened, in this instance, to be right under poor Pip's seat. The involuntary consternation of the moment caused him to leap, paddle in hand, out of the boat; and in such a way, that part of the slack whale line coming against his chest, he breasted it overboard with him, so as to become entangled in it, when at last plumping into the water. That instant the stricken whale started on a fierce run, the line swiftly straightened; and presto! poor Pip came all foaming up to the chocks of the boat, remorselessly dragged there by the line, which had taken several turns around his chest and neck.

Tashtego stood in the bows. He was full of the fire of the hunt. He hated Pip for a poltroon. Snatching the boat-knife from its sheath, he suspended its sharp edge over the line, and turning towards Stubb, exclaimed interrogatively, “Cut?” Meantime Pip's blue, choked face plainly looked, Do, for God's sake! All passed in a flash. In less than half a minute, this entire thing happened.

“Damn him, cut!” roared Stubb; and so the whale was lost and Pip was saved.

So soon as he recovered himself, the poor little negro was assailed by yells and execrations from the crew. Tranquilly permitting these irregular cursings to evaporate, Stubb then in a plain, business-like, but still half humorous manner, cursed Pip officially; and that done, unofficially gave him much wholesome advice. The substance was, Never jump from a boat, Pip, except—but all the rest was indefinite, as the soundest advice ever is. Now, in general, Stick to the boat , is your true motto in whaling; but cases will sometimes happen when Leap from the boat , is still better. Moreover, as if perceiving at last that if he should give undiluted conscientious advice to Pip, he would be leaving him too wide a margin to jump in for the future; Stubb suddenly dropped all advice, and concluded with a peremptory command, “Stick to the boat, Pip, or by the Lord, I won't pick you up if you jump; mind that. We can't afford to lose whales by the likes of you; a whale would sell for thirty times what you would, Pip, in Alabama. Bear that in mind, and don't jump any more.” Hereby perhaps Stubb indirectly hinted, that though man loved his fellow, yet man is a money-making animal, which propensity too often interferes with his benevolence.

But we are all in the hands of the Gods; and Pip jumped again. It was under very similar circumstances to the first performance; but this time he did not breast out the line; and hence, when the whale started to run, Pip was left behind on the sea, like a hurried traveller's trunk. Alas! Stubb was but too true to his word. It was a beautiful, bounteous, blue day; the spangled sea calm and cool, and flatly stretching away, all round, to the horizon, like gold-beater's skin hammered out to the extremest. Bobbing up and down in that sea, Pip's ebon head showed like a head of cloves. No boat-knife was lifted when he fell so rapidly astern. Stubb's inexorable back was turned upon him; and the whale was winged. In three minutes, a whole mile of shoreless ocean was between Pip and Stubb. Out from the centre of the sea, poor Pip turned his crisp, curling, black head to the sun, another lonely castaway, though the loftiest and the brightest.

Now, in calm weather, to swim in the open ocean is as easy to the practised swimmer as to ride in a spring-carriage ashore. But the awful lonesomeness is intolerable. The intense concentration of self in the middle of such a heartless immensity, my God! who can tell it? Mark, how when sailors in a dead calm bathe in the open sea—mark how closely they hug their ship and only coast along her sides.

But had Stubb really abandoned the poor little negro to his fate? No; he did not mean to, at least. Because there were two boats in his wake, and he supposed, no doubt, that they would of course come up to Pip very quickly, and pick him up; though, indeed, such considerations towards oarsmen jeopardized through their own timidity, is not always manifested by the hunters in all similar instances; and such instances not unfrequently occur; almost invariably in the fishery, a coward, so called, is marked with the same ruthless detestation peculiar to military navies and armies.

But it so happened, that those boats, without seeing Pip, suddenly spying whales close to them on one side, turned, and gave chase; and Stubb's boat was now so far away, and he and all his crew so intent upon his fish, that Pip's ringed horizon began to expand around him miserably. By the merest chance the ship itself at last rescued him; but from that hour the little negro went about the deck an idiot; such, at least, they said he was. The sea had jeeringly kept his finite body up, but drowned the infinite of his soul. Not drowned entirely, though. Rather carried down alive to wondrous depths, where strange shapes of the unwarped primal world glided to and fro before his passive eyes; and the miser-merman, Wisdom, revealed his hoarded heaps; and among the joyous, heartless, ever-juvenile eternities, Pip saw the multitudinous, God-omnipresent, coral insects, that out of the firmament of waters heaved the colossal orbs. He saw God's foot upon the treadle of the loom, and spoke it; and therefore his shipmates called him mad. So man's insanity is heaven's sense; and wandering from all mortal reason, man comes at last to that celestial thought, which, to reason, is absurd and frantic; and weal or woe, feels then uncompromised, indifferent as his God.

For the rest, blame not Stubb too hardly. The thing is common in that fishery; and in the sequel of the narrative, it will then be seen what like abandonment befell myself.
