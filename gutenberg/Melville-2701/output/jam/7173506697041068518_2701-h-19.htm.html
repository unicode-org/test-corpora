CHAPTER 94. A Squeeze a di Hand.

Dat deh whale a Stubb's, so dearly purchase, did duly bring to di Pequod's side, weh all dem cutting an hoisting operations weh did detailed before, did regularly gone through, even to di baling a di Heidelburgh Tun, or Case.

While some did a occupied wid dis latter duty, others did a employ fi drag weh di larger tubs, so soon as dem did full wid di sperm; an wen di proper time arrive, dis same sperm did carefully manipulate ere going to di try-works, of which anon.

It did cool an crystallize to such a degree, dat wen, wid several others, mi did a sidong before a big Constantine's bath a it, mi find it strangely concreted inna lumps, yah an deh a roll bout inna di liquid part. A did wi business fi squeeze dem lumps back inna fluid. A sweet an unctuous duty! No wanda seh inna old times dis sperm was such a favorite cosmetic. Such a clearer! such a sweetener! such a softener! such a delicious molifier! Afta mi have mi han dem inna it fi only a few minutes, mi finga dem did feel like eels, an staat, as it were, fi serpentine an spiralise.

As mi did a sidong deh at mi ease, cross-legged pan di deck; afta di bitter exertion at di windlass; anda a blue tranquil sky; di ship anda indolent sail, an gliding so serenely along; as mi bath mi han dem mongst dem soft, gentle globules a infiltrated tissues, woven almost widin di hour; as dem richly break to mi fingers, an discharge all dem opulence, like fully ripe grapes dem wine; as mi snuff up dat deh uncontaminated aroma,—literally an truly, like di smell a spring violets; Mi a tel yuh, seh fi di time mi did a live inna a musky meadow; Mi forget all bout wi horrible oath; inna dat inexpressible sperm, mi wash mi han dem an mi heart a it; Mi almost staat fi credit di old Paracelsan superstition seh sperm is a rare virtue inna allaying di heat a anger; while mi did a bath inna dat deh bath, mi did feel divinely free fram all ill-will, or petulance, or malice, a any sort whatsoever.

Kweez! kweez! kweez! all di morning long; Mi squeeze dat deh sperm til mi self almost melt inna it; Mi squeeze dat deh sperm til a strange sort a insanity come ova mi; an mi find miself unwittingly squeeze mi co-laborers's hands inna it, mistaking dem hands fi di gentle globules. Such an abunding, affectionate, friendly, loving feeling did dis avocation beget; dat at last mi did a continually squeeze dem hands, an look up inna dem eyes sentimentally; as much as fi seh,—Oh! mi dear fellow beings, why we shuda longer cherish any social acerbities, or know di slightest ill-humor or envy! Cum; mek wi squeeze han dem all round; nay, mek wi all squeeze wiself inna each ada; mek wi squeeze wiself universally inna di very milk an sperm a kindness.

Wuda dat mi coulda keep squeeze dat deh sperm fi eva! Fi now, since by many prolonged, repeated experiences, mi have perceived dat in all cases man haffi eventually lower, or at least shift, him conceit of attainable felicity; no put it noweh inna di intellect or di fancy; but inna di wife, di heart, di bed, di table, di saddle, di fireside, di country; now dat mi don perceive all a dis, mi ready fi squeeze case eternally. In thoughts a di visions a di night, mi si long rows a angels inna paradise, each wid dem han dem inna a jar a spermaceti.

Now, while discoursing of sperm, it behooves fi speak bout other tings akin to it, inna di business a preparing di sperm whale fi di try-works.

Fos come white-horse, so called, weh dem get fram di tapering part a di fish, an also fram di thicker portions a him flukes. It tough wid congealed tendons—a wad a muscle—but still contain some oil. Afta dem tek weh di whale, dem fos cut di white-horse inna portable oblongs ere go to di mincer. Dem look much like blocks a Berkshire marble.

Plum-pudding a di term weh dem use pan certain fragmentary parts a di whale's flesh, yah an deh adhering to di blanket a blubber, an often participating to a considerable degree inna its unctuousness. A one most refreshing, convivial, beautiful object fi behold. A one ineffably oozy, stringy affair, most frequently found inna di tubs a sperm, afta a prolonged squeezing, an subsequent decanting. Nippers. Strictly dis word a nuh indigenous to di whale's vocabulary. But as applied by whalemen, it become so. A whaleman's nipper a one shaat firm strip a tendinous stuff weh dem cut fram di tapering part a Leviathan's tail: it average an inch inna thickness, an fi di rest, a bout di size a di iron part a one hoe. But fi laan all bout dem recondite matters yah, yuh bes way is at once fi descend inna di blubber-room, an have a long talk wid di inmates dem. CHAPTER 96. Di Try-Works.

Besides har hoisted boats, an American whaler is outwardly distinguished by har try-works. Shi present di curious anomaly a di most solid masonry joining wid oak an hemp inna constituting di completed ship. A like fram di open field a brick-kiln did a transport to har planks.

Di try-works dem plant between di foremast an mainmast, di most roomy part a di deck. Di timbers dem unda it have a peculiar strength, fit fi sustain di weight a one almost solid mass a brick an mortar, some ten feet by eight square, an five in height. Di foundation no penetrate di deck, but di masonry dem firmly secure to di surface by ponderous knees a iron bracing it pan all sides, an screw it dung to di timbers. Pan di flanks it case wid wood, an at top completely cover by a large, sloping, battened hatchway. Wen wi tek weh dis hatch, wi expose di great try-pots, two inna numba, an each a di several barrels' capacity. Wen dem no inna use, dem keep dem remarkably clean. Somtaim dem polish dem wid soapstone an sand, til dem shine widin like silver punch-bowls. Durin di night-watches some cynical old sailors ago crawl inna dem an coil demself weh deh fi a nap. While employed in polishing dem—one man inna each pot, side by side—many confidential communications are carryed on, ova di iron lips. A one place fi profound mathematical meditation tu. A did inna di left han try-pot a di Pequod, wid di soapstone diligently circling roun mi, dat mi did fos indirectly struck by di remarkable fact, dat inna geometry all bodies gliding along di cycloid, mi soapstone fi example, ago descend fram any point inna precisely di same time.

Wen yuh tek weh di fire-board fram di front a di try-works, di bare masonry a dat deh side get expose, penetrated by di two iron mout a di furnaces, directly unda di pots. Dem mout deh fit wid heavy doors a iron. Di intense heat a di fire is prevent fram communicate itself to di deck, by means a a shallow reservoir extending unda di entire inclosed surface a di works. By a tunnel inserted at di rear, dis reservoir is keep replenished wid water as fast as it evaporate. No external chimneys nuh deh; dem open direct fram di rear wall. An yahso mek wi go back fi a moment.

A did bout nine o'clock at night dat di Pequod's try-works dem did fos staat pan dis present voyage. It belong to Stubb fi oversee di business.

“All ready deh? Off hatch, den, an staat har. Yuh cook, fire di works.” Dis was an easy ting, cause di carpenter did a thrust him shavings inna di furnace throughout di passage. Yahso be it seh dat inna a whaling voyage di first fire inna di try-works haffi be feed fi a time wid wood. Afta dat no wood no use, except as a means fi quick ignition to di staple fuel. In a word, afta dem try it out, di crisp, shriveled blubber, weh dem call scraps or fritters now, still contain considerable a it unctuous properties. Dem fritters deh feed di flames. Like a plethoric burning martyr, or a self-consuming misanthrope, once ignited, di whale supply him own fuel an burn by him own body. Wuda dat him did a consume him own smoke! fi him smoke horrible fi inhale, an inhale it yuh haffi, an not only dat, but yuh haffi live inna it fi di time. It have a unspeakable, wild, Hindoo odor bout it, such as may lurk inna di vicinity a funereal pyres. It smell like di left wing a di day a judgment; it a one argument fi di pit.

By midnight di works dem did inna full operation. Wi did clear fram di carcase; sail did mek; di wind did a freshene; di wild ocean darkness did intense. But dat deh darkness did lick up by di fierce flames, weh at intervals fork out fram di sooty flues, an illuminate every lofty rope inna di rigging, as wid di famed Greek fire. Di burning ship drive on, as if remorselessly commissioned to some vengeful deed. So di pitch an sulphur-freighted brigs a di bold Hydriote, Canaris, weh did a come fram dem midnight harbors, wid broad sheets a flame fi sails, bore dung pan di Turkish frigates, an fold dem inna conflagrations.

Di hatch, weh dem tek weh fram di top a di works, now afford a wide hearth inna front a dem. Standing pan dis was di Tartarean shapes a di pagan harpooneers, always di whale-ship's stokers. Wid huge pronged poles dem pitch hissing masses a blubber inna di scalding pots, or stir up di fires dem unda, til di snaky flames dem dart, curling, outa di doors fi catch dem by di feet. Di smoke roll weh inna sullen heaps. Tu every pitch a di ship deh did have a pitch a di boiling oil, weh did look like all eagerness fi leap inna dem face. Opposite di mout a di works, pan di further side a di wide wooden hearth, was di windlass. Dis did serve fi a sea-sofa. Yahso di watch did a lounge, wen dem neva did a work, a look inna di red heat a di fire, til dem eyes did a feel scorched inna dem head. Dem tawny features, now all begrimed wid smoke an sweat, dem matted beards, an di contrasting barbaric brilliancy a dem teet, all dem did strangely reveal inna di capricious emblazonings a di works. As dem did a narrate to each other dem unholy adventures, dem tales a terror did a tell dem inna words a mirth; as dem uncivilized laughter fork upwards outa dem, like di flames fram di furnace; as to an fro, inna dem front, di harpooneers wildly gesticulated wid dem huge pronged forks an dippers; as di wind did a howle on, an di sea did a leap, an di ship did a groan an dive, an yet steadfastly shot har red hell further an further inna di blackness a di sea an di night, an scornfully champ di white bone inna har mout, an viciously spat roun har pan all sides; den di rushing Pequod, freighted wid savages, an laden wid fire, an burning a corpse, an plunging inna dat deh blackness a darkness, seemed di material counterpart a har monomaniac commander's soul.

So it did feel to mi, as mi did a stan up a har helm, an fi long hours silently guide di way a dis fire-ship pan di sea. Wrap, fi dat deh interval, inna darkness miself, mi but di beta si di redness, di madness, di ghastliness a ada piipl. Di continual sight a di fiend shapes before mi, capering half inna smoke an half inna fire, dem at last begat kinred visions inna mi soul, so soon as mi staat fi yield to dat deh unaccountable drowsiness weh eva woulda come ova mi at a midnight helm.

But dat deh night, in particular, a strange (an ever since inexplicable) ting happen to mi. Startin fram a brief standing sleep, mi did horribly conscious a someting fatally wrong. Di jaw-bone tiller did a hit mi side, weh did a lean against it; inna mi ears was di low hum a sails, jus staatin fi shake inna di wind; Mi did tink seh mi eyes dem did open; Mi did half conscious a put mi finga dem to di lids an mechanically stretch dem still further apart. But, spite a all dis, mi couldn't see no compass before mi fi steer by; though it did look like a minute since mi did a watch di card, by di steady binnacle lamp weh did a illuminate it. Notn neva did a look before mi but a jet gloom, now an den mek ghastly by flashes a redness. Uppermost was di impression, dat whatever swift, rushing ting mi did stan pan neva so much bound to any haven ahead as rushing fram all havens astern. A stark, bewildered feeling, as a death, come ova mi. Convulsively mi han dem grab di tiller, but wid di crazy conceit seh di tiller did, somehow, inna some enchanted way, inverted. Mi Gad! wa a gwaan wid mi? mi did tink. Lo! inna mi brief sleep mi did turn miself bout, an did a front di ship's stern, wid mi back to har prow an di compass. Inna wan instant mi face back, jus in time fi prevent di vessel fram fly up inna di wind, an very probably capsizing har. How glad an how grateful di relief fram dis unnatural hallucination a di night, an di fatal contingency a being bringed by di lee!

No look too long inna di face a di fire, O man! Neva dream wid yuh han pan di helm! No ton yuh back to di compass; accept di first hint a di hitching tiller; no believe di artificial fire, wen it redness mek all tings look ghastly. To-morrow, inna di natural sun, di skai ago be bright; dem who did a glare like devils inna di forking flames, di morn ago show inna far other, at least gentler, relief; di glorious, golden, glad sun, di only true lamp—all others but liars!

Nevertheless di sun hide not Virginia's Dismal Swamp, nor Rome's accursed Campagna, nor wide Sahara, nor all di millions a miles a desert an a griefs unda di moon. Di sun nuh hide di ocean, weh a di dark side a dis earth, an weh a two thirds a dis earth. So, so, dat deh mortal man weh have more a joy dan sorrow inna him, dat deh mortal man cyaan be true—not true, or undeveloped. Wid books di same ting. Di truest a all man was di Man of Sorrows, an di truest a all books a Solomon's, an Ecclesiastes a di fine hammered steel a woe. “All a vanity.” ALL. Dis wilful worl neva get hold a unchristian Solomon's wisdom yet. But him who dodge hospitals an jails, an walk fast crossing graveyards, an woulda radda talk bout operas dan hell; call Cowper, Young, Pascal, Rousseau, poor devils all a sick man; an throughout a care-free lifetime swears by Rabelais as passing wise, an therefore jolly;—not dat man is fitted fi sit dung pan tomb-stones, an break di green damp mold wid unfathomably wondrous Solomon.

But even Solomon, him seh, “di man weh wander outa di way a understanding shall remain” ( ie , even while living) “inna di congregation a di dead.” No gi yuhself up to fire, so it mait inverte yuh, dead yuh; as fi di time it did mi. Deh have a wisdom weh woe; but deh deh a woe weh madness. An deh have a Catskill eagle inna some souls weh can alike dive dung inna di blackest gorges, an soar out a dem again an become invisible inna di sunny spaces. An even if him fi eva fly inna di gorge, dat deh gorge deh inna di mountains; so dat even inna him lowest swoop di mountain eagle still higher dan di ada birds pan di plain, even though dem soar.

CHAPTER 98. Stowing Down an Clearing Up.

Already it been related how di great leviathan is afar off described fram di mast-head; how dem chase him ova di watery moors, an dem slaughter him inna di valleys a di deep; how dem den tow him alongside an cut him head; an how (pan di principle weh did entitled di headsman a old to di garments in which di beheaded dem did kill) him great padded surtout become di property a him executioner; how, inna due time, him get condem to di pots, an, like Shadrach, Meshach, an Abednego, him spermaceti, oil, an bone pass unscathed thru di fire;—but now it remain fi conclude di last chapter a dis part a di description by rehearsing—singing, if mi may—di romantic proceding a striking off him casks inna di oil inna di hold, weh once again leviathan return to him native profundities, sliding along beath di surface as before; but, alas! neva more fi rise an blow.

While still warm, di oil, like hot punch, is received inna di six-barrel casks; an while, perhaps, di ship a pitch an roll dis way an dat inna di midnight sea, di enormous casks dem a slewed round an head ova, end fi end, an sometimes perilously scoot across di slippery deck, like so many land slides, til at last man-handled an stay inna dem course; an all round di hoops, rap, rap, go as many hammers as can play upon dem, fi now, ex officio , every sailor is a cooper.

At length, wen di laas pint casked, an all cool, den di great hatchways dem unsealed, di bowels a di ship get throw open, an dung go di casks to dem final rest inna di sea. Dis done, di hatches dem get replace, an hermetically close, like a closet walled up.

Inna di sperm fishery, dis a perhaps one a di most remarkable incidents inna all di business a whaling. One day di planks dem stream wid freshets a blood an oil; pan di sacred quarter-deck enormous masses a di whale's head dem profanely piled; great rusty casks lie bout, as inna a brewery yard; di smoke fram di try-works dem besoot all di bulwarks; di mariners dem go bout suffused wid unctuousness; di entire ship look great leviathan himself; while pan all hands di din a deafening.

But a day or two afta, yuh look roun yuh, an prick yuh ears inna dis self-same ship; an if it neva fi di tell-tale boats an try-works, yuh woulda all but swear yuh trod some silent merchant vessel, wid a most scrupulously neat commander. Di unmanufactured sperm oil possess a singularly cleansing virtue. Dis a di reason why di decks dem neva look so white as jus afta weh dem call an affair of oil. Besides, fram di ashes a di burned scraps a di whale, a potent lye is readily mek; an whenever any adhesiveness fram di back a di whale remain clinging to di side, dat deh lye quickly exterminate it. Hans go diligently along di bulwarks, an wid buckets a water an rags restore dem to dem full tidiness. Di soot brush fram di lower rigging. All di numerous implements dem weh dem did a use dem likewise faithfully cleanse an put weh. Di great hatch is scrub an put pan di try-works, completely hiding di pots; every cask deh out a sight; all tackles dem coil inna unseen nooks; an wen by di combined an simultaneous industry a almost di entire ship's company, di whole a dis conscientious duty is at last conclude, den di crew demself proceed to dem own ablutions; shift demself fram top to toe; an finally issue to di immaculate deck, fresh an all aglow, as bridegrooms new-leaped fram out di daintiest Holland.

Now, wid elated step, dem pace di planks inna twos an threes, an humorously discourse a parlors, sofas, carpets, an fine cambrics; propose fi mat di deck; tink bout having hanging to di top; object not to tek tea by moonlight pan di piazza a di forecastle. Fi hint to such musked mariners of oil, an bone, an blubber, dem did likkle short a audacity. Dem nuh know di ting yuh distantly allude to. Away, an bring wi napkins!

But mark: aloft deh, at di three mast heads, stan three man intent pan spy out more whales, weh, if dem kech dem, infallibly will again soil di ole oaken furniture, an drop at least one likkle grease-spot somweh. Yea; an plenty a di time, wen, afta di severest uninterrupted labours, weh no know no night; continuing straight through fi ninety-six hours; wen fram di boat, weh dem swel op dem wrist wid all day a rowing pan di Line,—dem only step to di deck fi carry vast chains, an heave di heavy windlass, an cut an slash, yea, an inna dem very sweatings fi be smoke an burn anew by di combined fires a di equatorial sun an try-works; wen, pan di heel a all dis, dem don finally bestirred demself fi cleanse di ship, an mek a spotless dairy room a it; plenty a di time di poor fellows, jus button di neck a dem clean frocks, get startled by di cry a “There she blows!” an weh dem fly fi fight anodda whale, an go chruu di whole weary ting agen. Awo! mi fren dem, but dis a man-killing! Yet dis a life. For hardly have we mortals by long toilings extracted from dis world's vast bulk its small but valuable sperm; an den, wid weary patience, clean wiself fram it defilements, an laan fi live yah inna clean tabernacles a di soul; hardly dis is done, wen— Deh she blow! —di ghost get spouted up, an weh we sail fi fight some ada worl, an go chuu young life's old routine again.

Awo! di metempsychosis! Awo! Pythagoras, dat inna bright Greece, two thousand years ago, did die, so good, so wise, so mild; Mi sail wid yuh along di Peruvian coast last voyage—an, foolish as mi is, teach yuh, a green simple bwoy, how fi splice a rope!





CHAPTER 99. Di Doubloon.

Ere now it been related how Ahab was wont to pace him quarter-deck, tek regular turns at either limit, di binnacle an mainmast; but inna di multiplicity a ada tings weh need narration it neva add how dat sometimes inna dem walks yah, wen most plunged inna him mood, him was wont fi pause in turn at each spot, an stan deh strangely eyeing di particular object before him. Now dis doubloon was of purest, virgin gold, rake somewhere outa di heart a gorgeous hills, from, east an west, ova golden sands, di head-waters a many a Pactolus flows. Somtaim dem did a talk it ova inna di weary watch by night, a wanda whose it was fi be at last, an weda him woulda eva live fi spen it.

“Ship, ahoy! Yuh si di White Whale?”

Inna di excitement a di moment, Ahab did forget seh since di loss a him leg him neva once step pan board a no vessel at sea but him own, an den it was always by an ingenious an very handy mechanical contrivance peculiar to di Pequod, an a ting not fi be rigged an ship in any other vessel at a warning moment's.
