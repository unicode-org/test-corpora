Yuh belong to dat deh hopeless, sallow tribe weh no wine a dis ya worl naa go eva warm; an fi who even Pale Sherry woulda be too rosy-strong; but wid whom one sometimes love fi sit, an feel poor-devilish, too; an grow convivial upon tears; an seh to dem bluntly, wid full eyes an empty glasses, an inna not altogether unpleasant sadness—Give it up, Sub-Subs!
