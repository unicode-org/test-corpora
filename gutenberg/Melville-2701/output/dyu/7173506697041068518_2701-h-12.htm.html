ƝƐFƆLI 55. Balɛnw ka jaaw koo la.

N’ bena ere jan pentiri kɛ i ye i n’a fɔ mɔgɔ be se ka min kɛ ni fani tɛ, fɛɛn dɔ i n’a fɔ balɛn cogoya yɛrɛ yɛrɛ i n’a fɔ a be cogo min na balɛntigi ɲɛɛ na k’a sɔrɔ a yɛrɛ farikolo la, balɛn be nɔrɔnin lo balɛn kurun gɛrɛfɛ walisa a ka se ka tagama yen. O kama, a be se ka kɛ ko nafa b’a la ka kɔn k’a ka jaaw lakali minw be mɔgɔ lasun k’a miiri ani hali bi, u be jamanaden ka limaniya sɔsɔ ni jigiya ye. Wagati sera an ka duniɲa latilen o koo la, an kɛtɔ k’a yira ko o jaa ɲɔgɔnw bɛɛ tɛ tiɲɛn ye.

N’a sɔrɔ o jaaw ka lafilikow bɛɛ bɔyɔrɔ fɔlɔ bena sɔrɔ Ɛnduw, Ezipitikaw ani Grɛkiw ka lɛsɛli kɔrɔmanw cɛma. Kabi o wagatiw la, mɔgɔw tun be fɛɛnw dilan nka u tun tɛ u yɛrɛ yira, tuma min na Alabatosow ka marburuw kan, jaaw ka boonw lɔyɔrɔw kan, ani nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw, medaliw, tasaw ani warigwɛw kan, u tun be delfin ja ni nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw ye i n’a fɔ Saladin ta, ani nɛgɛkunbɛnnan kungolo i n’a fɔ Saint George ta; kabini o wagati, o ɲɔgɔnna layisɛni dɔ lo be yen, a ma kɛ balɛn jaaw fanba dɔrɔn na minw ka di mɔgɔw ye, nka lɔnnikow caaman na minw y’a yira a koo la.

Sisan, a ka c’a la, jaa kɔrɔman min be yen hali bi ani a b’a fɔra ko a ye balɛni ta ye, o be sɔrɔ kuluwo tɔgɔba dɔ kɔnɔ min be weele ko Elephanta, Ɛndi jamana na. Brahminw b’a fɔ ko o pagoda kɔrɔman ka lɛsɛlifɛnw minw be se ka ban, jagokow ni baaraw bɛɛ, adamadenw ka baaraw bɛɛ, u tun be kɛra saan caaman kɔnɔ sanni u dɔ ka na. O la, a ma bari an na k’a ye ko cogo dɔ la, an ka baara ɲuman min ye balɛnw minɛ ye, o tun ka ɲi ka kɛ yen ka sumaya. Hindoo baleine min kofɔra, o be sɔrɔ kogo yɔrɔ dɔ la, min be Vishnu ka farikolo cogoya yira i n’a fɔ leviatan, min be weele ko Matse Avatar. Nka hali ni nin lɛsɛli ye mɔgɔ tilancɛ ye, baleni tilancɛ ye, walisa ka laban ka kukala dɔrɔn di, o bɛɛ n’a ta, o yɔrɔ fitinin min b’a la, o bɛɛ tɛ tiɲɛn ye. A be komi anakonda kukala min be dɔgɔyara, ka tɛmɛ baleine yɛrɛ yɛrɛ ka bolokunnandenw kan.

Nka aw ye taga Galeri kɔrɔw ra, ka kerecɛnba dɔ ka jɛgɛ nin ja filɛ sisan; sabu a ma ɲɛtaga sɔrɔ ka tɛmɛ Ɛnduw kan minw tun be yen ka kɔn sanjiba ɲɛ. O ye Guido ka jaa ye min b’a yira ko Perseus be Andromeda kisira baji kɔnɔfɛn wala baleine bolo. Guido ye o danfɛn kabakoman ɲɔgɔn ka misaliya sɔrɔ min? Hogarth fana, k’o yɔrɔ kelen lo yira a yɛrɛ ka “Perseus Descending” kɔnɔ, a t’a yira ko yɔrɔ kelen ka fisa n’o ye. O Hogarthi ka kongofɛnba nin ka farikolo belebeleba be yɛlɛmayɛlɛma dugukolo sanfɛ, a be se ka jii santimɛtiri kelen bɔ. Howdah sugu dɔ b’a kɔfɛ, ani a daa yɛlɛnin min kɔnɔ, jikuruw be yɛlɛma yɛlɛma, o be se ka ta ka taga Janfakɛlaw ka Da la min be bɔ Tamisi baji fɛ jii fɛ ka don Sankaso kɔnɔ. O kɔ, Prodromus balɛnw be yen minw be sɔrɔ Ekɔsi Sibbald kɔrɔ la, ani Yunusu ka balɛnw, i n’a fɔ u yiranin be cogo min na Bibulu kɔrɔw sɛbɛninw na ani sɛbɛnin kɔrɔw tigɛninw na. Mun le bɛna fɔ olugu ta ko ra sa? Gafew siribaga ka balɛn min be yɛlɛmayɛlɛma i n’a fɔ ɛrɛzɛnsun ka lɔgɔ min be jigira, o b’a lamini—i n’a fɔ a be sɛbɛri kɛ cogo min na ani a be sanulaman kɛ gafe kɔrɔw ni gafe kura caaman kɔfɛlaw n’u ɲɛɛw kan—o ye danfɛn cɛɲumanba ye nka min be mɔgɔ kabakoya, n’ y’a ladegi, n’ b’a miiri, ka bɔ jaa ɲɔgɔnw na. Hali ni diɲɛ bɛɛ b’a weele ko delfin, o bɛɛ n’a ta, n’ b’o gafew siribaga ka jɛgɛ weele ko baleine laɲini; sabu a tun laɲinina o lo la tuma min na o minan bɔra fɔlɔ la. Itali weleweledala kɔrɔ dɔ lo y’a daminɛ yɔrɔ dɔ la saan kɛmɛkulu 15nan ɲɔgɔn na, kalan lakununi wagati la; ani o wagati la, ani hali fɔɔ ka taga se wagati dɔ ma, mɔgɔw tun b’a miiri ko delfinw ye Leviatan sugu dɔ ye.

Gafe kɔrɔ dɔw ka videwow n’u ka masirifɛn wɛrɛw kɔnɔ, tuma dɔw la, i bena ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ ni mɔgɔ dɔw ye minw be maga a la kosɔbɛ, yɔrɔ min na ji bɔyɔrɔ sifa bɛɛ, jets d’eau, jii kalamanw ani nɛnɛ, Saratoga ani Baden-Baden, be bɔ a kunsɛmɛ na min ma sɛgɛ. “Advancement de l’apprentissage” gafe fɔlɔ ɲɛɛ fɔlɔ kan, i bena balɛn dɔw sɔrɔ minw b’a fɛ ka koow lɔn.

Nka n’an y’o laɲiniw bɛɛ dabila minw tɛ baarakɛlaw ka baara ye, an k’o leviyatan jaa nunu filɛ minw b’a fɔra ko u ye lɔnnikow lo yira, minw b’o lɔn. Harris ka voyaziw sɛbɛnin kɔrɔ kɔnɔ, balɛnw ka pilaki dɔw be yen minw bɔra Ɔsitarali ka voyaziw gafe dɔ kɔnɔ, saan 1671 KRISTA TILE kɔ, u tɔgɔ ko “Balɛnw minɛ ka taga Spitzbergen kurun kɔnɔ min tɔgɔ ko Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson min be bɔ Frisilandi, ale kuntigi.” O pilakiw dɔ la, baleniw lo yirala i n’a fɔ yirikurubaw, u lanin be gɛrɛsi gunw cɛma, ani urusi gwɛmanw be bolila u kɔkolow kan minw be niin na. Pɛti wɛrɛ kan, filiba min kɛra, o ye ka baleine yira ni nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw ye minw be ɲɔgɔn kunbɛn.

O kɔ, quarto dɔ be yen min be mɔgɔ kabakoya tugun, kapitɛn Colnett dɔ lo y’o sɛbɛ, min tun ye pɔsi kapitɛn ye Angletɛri ka baji kan, a tɔgɔ ko “Tagama min be kɛ ka Kapi Horn lamini ka taga worodugu fan fɛ bajiw la, walisa ka Spermaceti baleine jɛgɛminɛyɔrɔ bonya.” Gafe nin kɔnɔ, labɛn dɔ be yen min b’a fɔ ko a ye “Balɛn Physeter wala Spermaceti jaa ye, min bɔra dɔ la min fagara Mɛkisiki baji daa la saan 1793, ɔkutɔburukalo la ani u y’a lawuli kurun sanfɛ.” N’ siga t’a la ko kurun kapitɛni y’a to u y’o jaa tiɲɛnnin ta a ka kurun kɔnɔ baarakɛlaw nafa kama. N’ b’a fɛ ka koo kelen dɔrɔn fɔ a koo la, a to n k’a fɔ ko ɲɛɛ dɔ b’a la min be nɔrɔ, ka kɛɲɛ ni sɛgɛsɛgɛrikɛlan ye min be n’a ye, n’a be don jɛgɛ dafanin dɔ la, o tun bena o jɛgɛ ɲɛɛ kɛ i n’a fɔ bow-window min janya be se mɛtɛrɛ duuru ɲɔgɔn ma. E, ne ka sɔrɔdasi kuntigi jagwɛlɛn, mun na i ma Yunusu di an ma, min bɛ filɛra o ɲa ra?

Ani fana, Tariku minw be lajɛn ni dusukunnakow ye ka kanbelew ni sunguruw nafa, olu tɛ fili jugu kelen ye. O gafe min ka di mɔgɔw ye kosɔbɛ, o filɛ: “Nature animée de l’orsmith.” Saan 1807 ka Lɔnduru ka gafe surun dɔ kɔnɔ, u y’a fɔ ko “balɛni” dɔ ani “narwhale” dɔ ka sɛbɛw be yen. Ne t’a fɛ ka kɛ i n’a fɔ mɔgɔ min ma cɛɲumanya, nka nin balɛni cɛjugu bɔnin be lɛɛ tigɛnin ma kosɔbɛ; ani, narwhale ta fan fɛ, n’i y’a filɛ dɔɔni dɔrɔn, o be se ka mɔgɔ kabakoya, ko nin saan kɛmɛkulu tan ni kɔnɔntɔnnan na, o ɲɔgɔnna hippogriff tun be se ka jati sɔbɛ ye lakɔliden hakilitigiw bɛɛ kan.

O kɔ, saan 1825 la tugun, Bernard Germain, min tun ye kɔnti de Lacépède ye ani min tun ye danfɛnw lɔnbagaba ye, ale ye lɔnnikow gafe dɔ bɔ min be balɛnw koo fɔ cogo labɛnnin na, ani Leviatan sifa caaman jaa caaman be sɔrɔ o kɔnɔ. O bɛɛ tɛ tiɲɛn ye dɔrɔn, nka Mysticetus walima Greenland baleine (o kɔrɔ, Right baleine) jaa, hali Scoresby, min ye mɔgɔ ye min be koo caaman lɔn kabi wagatijan i n’a fɔ a be maga o sugu la, ale b’a fɔ ko a ɲɔgɔn tɛ yen danfɛnw na.

Nka, ka fanikolon bila o baara juguw bɛɛ la, o tun be kɛra lɔnnikɛla Frederick Cuvier dɔrɔn lo fɛ, min ye Baron tɔgɔtigi balimacɛ ye. Saan 1836, a ye gafe dɔ bɔ min tɔgɔ ko Naturel Histoire des baleines ani a be min weele ko baleine sperme jaa, a y’o di o kɔnɔ. Sanni i k’o jaa yira Nantucket duguden si la, a ka fisa i k’i ka wagati surun dɔ labɛn ka bɔ Nantucket. Kumaden kelen na, Frederick Cuvier ka sɛrɛkili tɛ sɛrɛkili ye, nka sɛrɛkili lo. Tiɲɛn lo, a ma deli ka nafa sɔrɔ balɛnw minɛ na (o cɛɛw man teli ka nafa sɔrɔ), nka a y’o jaa bɔ min na, jɔn lo be se k’o fɔ? N’a sɔrɔ a y’o sɔrɔ i n’a fɔ a ka lɔnnikɛla min tun be yen ka kɔn a ɲɛ o baara kelen na, Desmarest, y’a ka kɔnɔtiɲɛ lakika dɔ sɔrɔ; o kɔrɔ, a bɔra Sinuwaw ka jaa dɔ la. Ani, o Sinuwaw ye cɛden ɲɛnaman sifa jumanw ye minw be ni kiriyɔn ye, tasa ni tasa caaman b’an lalɔnniya.

Tagamasiɲɛw dilanbagaw ka balɛn minw be ye siraw kan, n’u be nɔrɔnin be tulufeerelaw ka feerekɛyɔrɔw sanfɛ, mun lo bena fɔ u koo la? U ka ca a la, u ye Risi 3nan lo ye. balɛniw, minw be ni dromedary gɛrɛnw ye, ani minw ka jugu kosɔbɛ; ka sɔgɔma dumuni kɛ kurunbolilaw ka tarte saba wala naani kan, o kɔrɔ, balɛni kurunw minw fanin be kurunbolilaw la: u ka fiɲɛw be yɛlɛmayɛlɛma joli ni pɛntiri bulaman bajiw kɔnɔ.

Nka o fili caaman minw kɛra baleni jaa yiracogo la, u tɛ mɔgɔ kabakoya kosɔbɛ. Bonya! Lɔnniya jaaw fanba bɔra jɛgɛ minw sirinin be jii la; ani nunu be bɛn i n’a fɔ kurun tiɲɛnin jaa, min kɔkolo karinin lo, o tun bena bɛgɛn ɲuman yɛrɛ yira ka ɲɛ a ka kurun n’a ka nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw bɛɛ la minw ma tiɲɛ. Hali ni samaw lɔra u ka janya bɛɛ la, Leviatan ɲɛnama ma deli k’a yɛrɛ yɛlɛma ka ɲɛ a ka jaa kama. Balɛn ɲanaman, a ka masaya n’a ka kɔrɔ bɛɛ la, a be ye baji dɔrɔn kɔnɔ jii kɔnɔ min tɛ se ka faamu; ani a fanba min be jigi, a tɛ ye, i n’a fɔ kurun min be kɛlɛ la; ani ka bɔ o fɛɛn na, o ye fɛɛn ye min tɛ se ka kɛ abada adamaden satɔ fɛ k’a farikolo kɔrɔta fiɲɛ fɛ, walisa k’a ka funu barikamanw n’a ka yɛlɛmaniw bɛɛ mara. Ani, an kana kuma danfara min be baleine denmisɛnnin ni Platon ka Leviatan dafanin cɛ, o min be se ka jati kosɔbɛ; nka, hali n’o balɛn denmisɛnw dɔ la kelen be yɛlɛla kurun sanfɛ, o tuma na, a cogoya be yɛlɛmayɛlɛma, a be komi jɛgɛ, a be yɛlɛmayɛlɛma, fɔɔ a ka ɲɛda tigitigi jinɛ yɛrɛ tun tɛ se k’o minɛ.

Nka a be se ka kɛ ko mɔgɔw be se ka hakilina tigitigi dɔw sɔrɔ ka bɔ baleine min tun be jii la, o kolo lankolon na min be kuma a cogoya yɛrɛ yɛrɛ koo la. Fiyewu. Sabu o ye Leviyatan nin ka koow la min be mɔgɔ kabakoya kosɔbɛ, o ye ko a kolow tɛ mɔgɔ hakili jigi kosɔbɛ a cogoya koo la. Hali ni Jeremy Bentham ka kolokolo, min sirinin be kandelaburu kama a fagabagaw dɔ ka biblotɛki kɔnɔ, o be cɛkɔrɔba dɔ ka miiriya yira ka ɲɛ min ɲɛda tun ka bon ani a tun be ni Jeremy ka jogo tɔɔw bɛɛ ye; o bɛɛ n’a ta, o ɲɔgɔnna foyi tun tɛ se ka bɔ leviyatan kolow si la minw tun be ɲɔgɔn minɛna. Tiɲɛn na, i ko Donsoba y’a fɔ cogo min na, balɛni kolo dɔrɔn be jɛnɲɔgɔnya kelen lo kɛ ni bɛgɛn ye min be ni fani ye ani a be ni fani ye i n’a fɔ fɛnɲɛnama be jɛnɲɔgɔnya min kɛ ni kirisalisi ye min b’a lamini cogo koorinin na. O koo kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnin be ye ka gwɛ kuun na, i n’a fɔ a bena yira cogo min na gafe nin yɔrɔ dɔw la cogoya dɔ la. A be yira fana ni ɲiningali ye kosɔbɛ kɛrɛfɛla nɛgɛkunbɛnnan na, a kolow be se ka kɛ i n’a fɔ adamaden bolo kolow, n’an be bolokunnaden dɔrɔn lo bɔ a la. O nɛgɛkunbɛnnan be ni bolokunnaden koloman naani ye minw be to ka kɛ: bolodennin, cɛmancɛ, bolodennin ani bolodennin fitinin. Nka o fɛɛnw bɛɛ be to u ka sogo datuguyɔrɔ la kudayi i n’a fɔ adamaden bolokunnadenw be to fani dilanin dɔ kɔnɔ cogo min na. Loon dɔ la, Stubb min tun be yɛlɛmisɛn kɛ, ale y’a fɔ ko: “Hali ni balɛn be baara kɛ an ye tuma dɔw la ni hakiliɲagami ye cogo o cogo, an tɛ se k’a fɔ abada tiɲɛn na ko a be an minɛ ni bololafɛnw tɛ.”

O la, o kuunw bɛɛ kosɔn, n’i y’a filɛ cogo o cogo, i ka ɲi k’a latigɛ ko Leviatanba ye o danfɛn kelen ye diɲɛ kɔnɔ min ka kan ka to a ma ɲɛgɛn fɔɔ ka taga se a laban ma. Tiɲɛn lo, jaa dɔ be se ka gɛrɛ tagamasiɲɛ la ka tɛmɛ dɔ wɛrɛ kan, nka u si tɛ se k’a digi ni tigitigi ye. O kama, dugukolo kan fɛɛrɛ si tɛ yen min b’a to an b’a lɔn tigitigi balɛni bɔnin be cogo min na tigitigi. Ani cogo kelen min na i be se ka hali miiriya dɔ sɔrɔ a ka ɲɛnamaya cogoya koo la, o ye ka taga balɛni minɛ i yɛrɛ ma; nka n’i y’o kɛ, faratiba b’i la ko a bena i faga fɔɔ abada ani k’i jigi. O ra, a bɛ ne fɛ, a ka fisa i kana i yɛrɛ kɔrɔbɔ kojugu i ka ɲininiw na, ka maga nin Leviatan ma.





ƝƐFƆLI 56. Balɛnw jaaw minw tɛ fili kosɔbɛ, ani balɛnw minɛcogo jaaw lakika.

Ka ɲɛsin balɛnw ka jaaw ma minw be mɔgɔ kabakoya, n’ b’a fɛ kosɔbɛ yan ka don u ka maana jugumanw na minw be sɔrɔ gafe dɔw kɔnɔ, kɔrɔmanw ni bi taw, sanko Plini, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, n’a ɲɔgɔnnaw. Nka n’ be tɛmɛ o koo kan.

N’ be sɛrɛkiliba naani dɔrɔn lo lɔn minw gansira; Kɔlnɛti, Huginsi, Frɛdɛriki Kuviyɛri ani Bili ka mɔgɔw lo y’o kɛ. Sapitiri tɛmɛnin kɔnɔ, an kumana Colnett ni Cuvier koo la. Huggins ta ka fisa n’u ta ye kosɔbɛ; nka, a ka c’a la, Beale ta le ka fisa n’a bɛɛ ye. Beale ye balɛn nin jaa minw bɛɛ dilan, u bɛɛ ka ɲi, fɔɔ cɛmancɛ jaa min be balɛn saba jaa kan minw be ni miiriya sifa caaman ye, o min b’a ka sapitiri filanan dafa. A ka ɲɛfɛla sɛbɛnin, kurunw be kɛlɛ kɛra ni balɛniw ye, hali ni siga t’a la, u y’a jati ko a bena mɔgɔ dɔw ka sigasigaliw lawuli, o ye tiɲɛn ye ani a be komi ɲɛnamaya lo a ka nɔɔw bɛɛ la. J. Ross Browne ka Sperm Whale jaa dɔw be bɛn kosɔbɛ u ka lamini na; nka o sɛbɛra cogo jugu ra. Nka, o tɛ ale ka jurumu ye.

Balɛn ɲuman koo la, jaa minw ka ɲi ka tɛmɛ tɔɔw bɛɛ kan, olu be sɔrɔ Scoresby kɔnɔ; nka u sɛbɛra ni fɛɛn fitininw ye minw tɛ se ka mɔgɔ hakili jigi. Balɛniw minɛcogo jaa kelen dɔrɔn lo b’a bolo, ani o ye dɛsɛ ye min be mɔgɔ dusukasi, sabu o jaaw dɔrɔn lo fɛ, n’u kɛra ka ɲɛ, i be se ka fɛɛn o fɛɛn sɔrɔ i n’a fɔ miiriya lakika min be sɔrɔ balɛni ɲɛnama koo la i n’a fɔ a ka donso ɲɛnamaw y’a ye cogo min na.

Nka, n’an y’u bɛɛ lajɛ, min ka fisa ni tɔɔw bɛɛ ye, hali n’a dɔw tɛ tiɲɛn ye, balɛnw ni balɛnw minɛcogo yiracogo minw be sɔrɔ yɔrɔ bɛɛ, olu ye Faransi ka sɛbɛri belebele fila ye, minw dilanna ka ɲɛ ani u bɔra Garnery dɔ ka jaaw la. U kelen kelen bɛɛ be binkanniw lo yira minw be kɛra Sperm ani Right Whale kan. O sɛbɛri fɔlɔ la, baleine ɲuman dɔ yirala a ka fanga bɛɛ la, a wulila ka bɔ kurun jukɔrɔ dɔrɔn ka bɔ baji kɔnɔ, ani a be fiɲɛ fɛ sanfɛ a kɔfɛ, a be tasuma yirikuruw tiɲɛninw ta. Kurun ɲɛfɛla fanba ma kari, ani a be sama dɔrɔn ka balansi kɛ kongofɛnba kɔkolo kan; ani n’i lɔnin be o ɲɛfɛla la, o wagati kelen kɔnɔ min tɛ se ka jati, i be kurunbolila dɔ ye, min tilancɛ datugunin lo ni balɛni fununin fununin ye, ani a be panpanna i n’a fɔ a be bɔra kongokolon dɔ kɔnɔ. O koo bɛɛ kɛcogo ka ɲi ani tiɲɛn lo cogo kabakoman na. Lini-tub min lakolon lo tilancɛ, o be yɛlɛ baji gwɛnin kan; yiribolo minw bɔnna, o bɛ yɛlɛma yɛlɛma a kɔnɔ; jikuruw kungolo be jɛnsɛnna balɛn gɛrɛfɛ ni siranya kumaw ye minw tɛ kelen ye; k’a sɔrɔ sanfiɲɛba yɔrɔjan na, kurun be jigira ka taga yɔrɔ min na. Jalakiba tun be se ka sɔrɔ o balɛn farikolo cogoya la, nka a to o ka tɛmɛ; sabu, ne ta ɲanamaya bɛɛ ra, ne tun tɛ se ka fɛn ɲuman ɲɔgɔn ja.

Loon filanan na, baleine ɲuman hakɛ dɔ yera, minw, u latanganin lo ka bɔ baleine Sperm ka kɛlɛ ma i n’a fɔ Pequod, n’u dakunw dayɛlɛnin ye, u tun be jigi dɔɔni dɔɔni ka tɛmɛ brit kɔnɔ, min, nɔrɔnin be o Venise fiyentɔ kabakoman ka faniw la u daa la, tun be o cogo la waterpeca min tun be dan na.

I n’a fɔ sɔgɔma yiritigɛlaw, minw be ɲɔgɔn gɛrɛfɛ dɔɔni dɔɔni ani u be u ka sɔgɔlanw lataga ɲɛfɛ binjugu janw kɔnɔ minw be kongokolon kɔnɔ; hali o fɛnɲɛnamanw tun bɛ jigi, ka mankan kabakoman dɔ bɔ, min bɛ i n’a fɔ bin, min bɛ tigɛ; ani u be yɔrɔ bulamanw to u kɔ minw tɛ se ka ban baji nɛrɛmuguman kan.*

*O baji yɔrɔ min be weele ko “Brezil Banks” o tɔgɔ tɛ i n’a fɔ Terre-Neuve baji daa, sabu yɔrɔ minw ka dun kɔsɔbɛ ani mankanw be yen, nka o yɔrɔ kabakoman min be komi kongokolon, o lo y’a to brit la titude is continually floated of the Wha, o be yɛlɛma yɔrɔ minw na.

Nka u tun be mankan min bɔra tuma min na u tun be briti tilan, o dɔrɔn lo tun be mɔgɔ hakili jigi tigɛlanw na. N’an y’u ye ka bɔ kurunw kuun na, sanko n’u lɔra ka lɔ ani u lɔnin be wagati dɔɔni kɔnɔ, u ka fanikolon belebelebaw tun be komi farakurunw minw tɛ niin na ka tɛmɛ fɛɛn tɔɔw bɛɛ kan. Ani i n’a fɔ Ɛndi jamana na, donsoya jamanabaw la, lonan min be yɔrɔjan, ale bena tɛmɛ tuma dɔw la kongokolonw ka samaw lanin be k’a sɔrɔ a t’a lɔn k’olu lo, k’u jati dugukolo sanfɛyɔrɔw ye minw gwansan lo ani u be fiɲɛ; hali o cogo kelen na, tuma caaman na, ale min be baji kɔnɔ leviatan sifa nin yera a siɲɛ fɔlɔ la. Ani, hali n’u y’a lɔn laban na, u ka bonyaba b’a to a ka gwɛlɛ kosɔbɛ ka la a la ko o fɛn camanba minw be falen kojugu, u be se ka kɛ u yɛrɛ ka fɛnw ye, u fan bɛɛ la, ni ɲɛnamaya sifa kelen ye min be sɔrɔ wulu wala soo kɔnɔ.

Tiɲɛn na, koo wɛrɛw la, i be se ka baji daa la danfɛn si jati ni dusukunnakow ye i n’a fɔ i be baji daa la danfɛn minw jati. ƝƐFƆLI 60. Liɲi.

Ka ɲɛsin balɛnw minɛko ma min bena ɲɛfɔ yanni dɔɔni, ani fana walisa ka o ɲɔgɔnna koow bɛɛ faamu ka ɲɛ yɔrɔ wɛrɛw la, n’ ka ɲi ka kuma yan balɛnw ka yɔrɔ kabakomanw koo la, minw be mɔgɔ lafili tuma dɔw la.

Fɔlɔ la, juru min tun be kɛ jɛgɛminɛ na, o tun ye ɲɔ ɲuman ye, a tun be funu dɔɔni ni goudron ye, a tun tɛ nɔgɔ n’a ye i n’a fɔ juru gansanw; sabu k’a sɔrɔ goudron, i n’a fɔ a be kɛ cogo min na tuma bɛɛ, o b’a to ɲɔ be nɔgɔya juru dilanbaga fɛ, ani fana a be juru yɛrɛ nɔgɔya kurunbolila fɛ ka baara kɛ n’a ye kurun kɔnɔ; o bɛɛ n’a ta, o hakɛ gansan tɛna baleni juru gɛlɛya kojugu dɔrɔn, a ka kan ka yɛlɛma cogo min na; nka i ko baji kan baarakɛlaw fanba y’a daminɛ k’a lɔn cogo min na, gudɔrɔn tɛ dɔ fara juru ka mɛɛn wala a ka fanga kan cogo si la, hali n’a be se k’a to a be nɔgɔya ani a be mɛnɛmɛnɛ cogo o cogo.

Arpooneer dɔw bena sɔgɔma kuru bɛɛ kɛ o baara la, u be juru ta ka taga sanfɛ ani u b’a yɛlɛma duguma ka tɛmɛ bolokuru dɔ fɛ ka taga jikuru fan fɛ, i n’a fɔ u b’u yɛrɛ yɛlɛma cogo min na walisa k’a bɔ nɔgɔw ni nɔgɔw bɛɛ la.

O koo nunu na, tiɲɛn na, baleine be yɛlɛma i n’a fɔ ale tasa, i n’a fɔ a be bɔ kurun dɔ kɔnɔ ka taga dɔ wɛrɛ la; hali ni kurun fɔlɔ be to ka yɛlɛ a gɛrɛfɛ tuma bɛɛ walisa k’a muso dɛmɛ. A filanan: O labɛn ka ɲi ka kɛ bɛɛ ka lafiya kama; sabu ni juru dugumayɔrɔ tun sirinin be kurun na cogo o cogo la, ani ni balɛn tun be juru boli ka bɔ kurun kɔnɔ fɔɔ ka taga se a laban ma miniti kelen kɔnɔ, sigarɛti min b’a la i n’a fɔ a b’a kɛ cogo min na tuma dɔw la, a tun tɛna lɔ yen, bari kurun min tun bena halaki, o tun bena sama ka jigi a kɔ ka taga baji kɔnɔ, fili tɛ min na; ani o cogo la, duguden si tun tɛna a sɔrɔ tugun.

Sanni u ka kurun jigi ka taga mɔgɔw kɔ, u be juru sanfɛla ta kɔfɛ ka bɔ jikuruw kɔnɔ, ani ka tɛmɛ yirikuru kunkolo lamini yen, u be taga n’a ye tugun kurun janya bɛɛ la, ka lafiɲɛ kuruwa kan cɛɛ kelen kelen bɛɛ ka kurun nɛgɛ wala bolo kan, fɔɔ a be boli ka nɔrɔ a bolo la kurun boli la; ani fana ka tɛmɛ cɛɛw cɛma, k’u to yɛlɛmayɛlɛma ka sigi marifaw gɛrɛfɛ, ka taga se nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw wala nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw ma minw be kurun ɲɛfɛla la, yɔrɔ min na yiri nɛgɛ wala nɛgɛkuru dɔ be yen min bonya be se nɛgɛkunbɛnnan kelen ma, o b’a bali ka bɔ. Ka bɔ sɔgɔlanw na, a be siri fani fitinin dɔ la kurun sanfɛ, o kɔ, a be tɛmɛ kurun kɔnɔ tugun; ani fatɔ tan walima mugan ɲɔgɔn (min be weele ko box-line) n’a be nɔrɔnin be kɛsu kan kalaw kɔnɔ, a be taga ɲɛfɛ ka taga gunwale la min bele be kɔfɛ dɔɔni, ani o kɔ, a be siri short-warp la—juru min be nɔrɔ yɔrɔnin kelen ni harpoon ye; nka sanni o jɛnɲɔgɔnya ka kɛ, o yɔrɔ surun be tɛmɛ gundo sifa caaman fɛ minw be mɔgɔ sɛgɛ kojugu ka se ka u ɲɛfɔ ka gwɛ.

O cogo la, baleine juru be kurun kuru bɛɛ nɔrɔ a ka nɛgɛ gɛlɛnw na, k’a lamini ani k’a yɛlɛma yɔrɔ bɛɛ la. Kurunbolilaw bɛɛ b’u sen don a ka yɛlɛmani faratininw na; o cogo la, dugutigi maloyatɔ ɲɛɛ na, u be komi Ɛndi jamana ka jɛgɛminɛlaw, ani saa minw ka jugu ka tɛmɛ tɔɔw bɛɛ kan, olu b’u farikolo yɔrɔw nɔrɔ ɲɔgɔn na ni farikoloɲanagwɛ ye. Muso satɔ dencɛ si fana tɛ se ka sigi o fɛnɲɛnamafagalanw cɛma, a siɲɛ fɔlɔ la, ani k’a to a b’a seko bɛɛ kɛra kurun boli la, k’a miiri ko wagati o wagati la, nɛgɛkunbɛnnan be se ka ci, ani o yɛlɛmani juguw bɛɛ be se ka kɛ i n’a fɔ sanpɛrɛnw; a tɛ se ka kɛ ten ni yɛrɛyɛrɛ tɛ min b’a to a kolow kɔnɔ nɛgɛ yɛrɛ yɛrɛ be yɛrɛyɛrɛ a kɔnɔ i n’a fɔ jeli min be yɛrɛyɛrɛ. O bɛɛ n’a ta, delinanko lo—ko kabakoman lo! delinanko tɛ se ka min kɛ?—Gayer sallies, nisɔndiya caman, tulon ɲumanw, ani repartees minw be kɛnɛya, i ma deli k’u mɛn i ka mahogany yiriw kan, i bena min mɛn baleine kurun ka sɛdiriyiri gwɛman kan min janya ye santimɛtiri tilancɛ ye, n’u be sirila o cogo la mɔgɔw ka nɛgɛw la; ani, i n’a fɔ Calais duguden wɔɔrɔ minw tun be masacɛ Edward ɲɛfɛ, cɛɛ wɔɔrɔ minw tun be kurun kɔnɔ, olu be sama ka don saya dakunw na, n’u be nɛgɛkunbɛnnan dɔ siri u kan kelen kelen bɛɛ la, i n’a fɔ i be se k’a fɔ cogo min na.

N’a sɔrɔ miiriya dɔɔni dɔrɔn lo bena a to i be se k’o kasara minw kɛra siɲɛ caaman balɛnw minɛ na, u dɔw lakali—u dɔw be fɔra cogo nɔgɔman na—o cɛɛ wala o cɛɛ bɔra kurun kɔnɔ juru fɛ, ani u tununa. Sabu, ni lɔgɔ be bolila ka bɔ, ka sigi kurun kɔnɔ o tuma na, o be komi i siginin be nɛgɛso dɔ ka pɛrɛnkanw cɛma min be baara kɛra, tuma min na lɔgɔw bɛɛ, ani lɔgɔw, ani lɔgɔw bɛɛ b’i ladon. A ka jugu ka tɛmɛ; sabu i tɛ se ka sigi yɔrɔnin kelen o faratiw kɔnɔ, sabu kurun bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛra i n’a fɔ nɛgɛso, ani i bɛ yɛlɛma yɔrɔ kelen na, k’a sɔrɔ i ma lasɔmi hali dɔɔni; ani yɛrɛ-labɛnni dɔ dɔrɔn lo fɛ ani i ka sago ni kɛwale ɲɔgɔn fɛ, i be se ka bɔ Mazeppa kɛli la, ka boli ni yɔrɔ min na, tile min be fɛɛn bɛɛ ye, ale yɛrɛ tun tɛ se k’i sɔgɔ abada.

O la fana: i n’a fɔ sumaninba min be sanfiɲɛ ɲɛ ani a be kiraya kɛ, n’a sɔrɔ o ka jugu ka tɛmɛ sanfiɲɛ yɛrɛ kan; sabu, tiɲɛ na, sumanin ye sanfiɲɛ ka fɛn n’a ka anvelɔpi dɔrɔn lo ye; ani a b’a kɔnɔ a yɛrɛ kɔnɔ, i n’a fɔ marifa min be komi a ma foyi kɛ, o be mugu min be mɔgɔ faga, ani bɔlɔ, ani pɛrɛnkan minɛ cogo min na; o la, nɛgɛjuru ka lafiɲɛ ɲuman, i n’a fɔ a be to ka kurunbolilaw lajɛ nɔgɔman na sanni a ka don tulon yɛrɛ yɛrɛ la—o ye koo ye min be siranya lakika lase mɔgɔw ma ka tɛmɛ o koo faratinin faan tɔɔw bɛɛ kan. Nka mun na an ka ɲi ka koo caaman kɛ? Cɛɛw bɛɛ be balo balɛnw ka faniw kɔnɔ. O bɛɛ bɛ wolo ni nɛgɛkunbɛnnanw ye o kan na; nka ni mɔgɔ satɔw minɛna saya ka yɛlɛmani telimanw na, minw be kɛ yɔrɔnin kelen, o lo b’a to u b’a faamu ko farati minw be yen tuma bɛɛ, u be yen tuma bɛɛ. Ani, n’i ye hakilijagabɔbaga ye, hali n’i siginin be balɛni kurun kɔnɔ, i dusukun tɛna siranya dɔɔni sɔrɔ ka tɛmɛ n’i siginin be i ka wulafɛ tasuma ɲɛfɛ ni pɔkɛri ye, n’i tɛ ni sɔgɔlan ye, i gɛrɛfɛ.
