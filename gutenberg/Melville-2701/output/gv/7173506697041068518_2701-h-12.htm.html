CAPPYL 60. Yn Line.

As ymmyd jeh’n scene whaling ta ry-hoi ry-hoi, as myrgeddin son tushtey smoo jeh ooilley ny sceneyn cosoylagh ayns ardjyn elley ta’n aght shoh er ny choyrt, ta mee ry-hoi ry-labhairt jeh’n whale-line magic, ry-hoi ry-hoi.

Yn line v'er ny h-ymmyrkey ec y toshiaght 'sy iasgagh v'eh jeh'n hemp share, beggan vapored lesh tar, cha nee impregnated lesh, myr v'eh 'sy cordaghyn ordinary; son tra ta tar, myr t'eh ry-chlashtyn, jannoo y hemp ny s'pliable da'n cord-maker, as myrgeddin jannoo y cord hene ny s'cosoylagh da'n marrey son ymmyd coontit 'sy lhong; agh, cha nee ny-lomarcan dy row y mhean ordinary ry-stiff y line-balley son y coiling close rish y row eh dy ve er ny choyrt fo; agh myr ta'n chooid smoo jeh ny marreyderyn toshiaght dy gliooney, cha nel tar ayns y chooid smoo cur rish ymmyd ny neart y cord, choud as t'eh cur compactness as gloss da.

Ayns ny bleeaney s'jerree ta'n cord Manilla ayns y iasgagh Americk er chur stiagh y hemp chamoo dy chooilley red myr studeyrys son lineenyn-balley; son, agh cha nee cho durable as hemp, t'eh ny s'creeney, as fadane ny s'boggey as ny s'elastic; as verym rish (son ta æsthetics ayns ooilley ny reddyn), dy vel eh mooar ny s’goan as ny s’goanney da’n baatey, na hemp. Ta Hemp ny ghooinney dorragh, dooghyssagh, sorch dy Indian; agh ta Manilla myr Circassian glen-oor dy row.

Ta'n line-balley agh daa-treeoo jeh'n inch dy three-feed. Ayns y chiad cheayrt, cha jean oo smooinaghtyn er cho stereotypagh as t’eh dy jarroo. Trooid y ghraih, bee e three-feed as feed sniaghtey croghey er gagh un three-feed as feed punt; son dy vod y cord ooilley strenght y ghoaill er-gerrey da three tonnyn. Ayns y fad, ta'n sperm-line coontit myr red erbee erskyn daa cheud fathom. Ta’n stern jeh’n baatey er ny choyrt stiagh ayns y tub, cha nee myr pipe-worm jeh’n still, agh dy yannoo un mass cronk, ayns cummey caas, dy “sheaves” dy ghraih, ny slaytyn spiralizations concentric, fegooish hollow erbee agh y “cree,” jeh’n tub cheese vertical. As t'eh y s'leaie tangle ny kink 'sy choiling, tra t'eh ry-reih, dy ghoaill arm, cosh, ny corp ooilley jeh dooinney erbee, t'eh y s'leaie 'sy stowing 'sy line 'sy tub. Ta roinney harpooneers gobbraghey cha row agh y moghrey immeeaght ayns y ghleashtan shoh, goaill y line erskyn-skyn as eisht reeve eh sheese trooid block gys y tub, myr shen ayns y ghraih coiling dy scarrey eh veih ooilley ny s'cosoylagh as ny s'cosoylagh.

Ayns ny baateyn Saggyrt t'ad uss daa tub y cleayney ayns ynnyd un tub; yn un line dy ve coiled dy kinjagh ayns ny daa tub. Ta cuid jeh’n vondeish ayn; son dy vel ny twin-tubs shoh cho beg as t'ad geddyn stiagh ny smoo 'sy lhong, as cha strain ad eh choud; agh, ta'n tub American, beggan as three trooid y diameter as dy doigh proportionagh, jannoo freight ry-hoi ry-hoi son ard-chreeagh nagh vel e plankyn agh un lesh-inch er y three-feed; son ta bun y baatey-balley myr gleashtan criticagh, as nee eh ymmyd y yannoo jeh weight sgaoilt, agh cha nee s'mooar jeh weight concentrated. As tra t'eh clashtyn y cover canvas peint er y line-tub American, ta'n baatey myr dy beagh eh ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi.

Ta'n daa çheu jeh'n line ry-akin; y çheu-mooie ta crommey ayns eye-splice ny loop ta cheet seose veih'n bodjal noi y çheu-mooie jeh'n tub, as croghey er e h-eddin dy-chooilley-heu dy chooilley nhee. Ta'n rang-oardagh shoh jeh'n cheu-sthie ymmyrchagh er daa choontey. Yn chiad: Er-yn-oyr dy row eh er ny h-earroo dy row line ymmyrchagh er ny choyrt rish veih baate ta ry-cheilley, my shegin da'n baljyn stroit soilshaghey cho dowin as dy row eh er ny threatey dy chur stiagh y line ooilley v'er ny ghleiy rish y harpoon ec y toshiaght. Ayns ny h-earrooyn shoh, ta'n baljyn ry-hoi ry-shiaght myr mug ale, myr dy beagh eh, veih'n un baate gys y jeh elley; agh ta'n chied baate dy bragh ry-hoi ry-hoi dy chur cooney da'n consort echey. Yn daa elley: Ta'n rang-oardagh shoh ry-hoi ry-hoi son sheean coontit; son my v’eh y lhong s’illey ayns aght erbee ceaut rish y lhong, as my v’eh y baljyn ec y traa shen dy reayll y lhong magh gys y lhong chamoo ayns un minute smooinaghtagh myr t’eh jannoo ry-cheilley, cha row eh staydys shen, son v’eh y lhong dooghyssagh er ny shleaystey sheese er e skyn ayns doghan y marrey; as ayns y chooish shen cha row town-crier erbee er gheddyn ee reesht.

Sushtal y lhong y lhoobey son y chaslys, t'eh er ny ghoaill er-gerrey da'n tub, as tra t'eh er n'gholl mygeayrt y loggerhead shen, t'eh er ny choyrt reesht er-ash ooilley ny lhongyn, as t'eh er ny hroggal crosswise er loom ny handle y lhong jeh gagh dooinney, dy vod eh joggal noi e laue ayns row; as myrgeddin goll er-gerrey eddyr ny fir, tra t'ad nyn soie ry-cheilley ec ny gunwales fastee, gys ny chocks ny grooves lead ayns y prow ard-pointeil jeh'n baate, raad ta pin ny skewer cowrey myr quill coontit, cur stiagh eh dy shliaghtey magh. Veih ny chocklyn t'eh croghey ayns festoon beg erskyn ny boggey, as t'eh eisht er ny choyrt stiagh 'sy lhong reesht; as tra ta mysh deig ny feed fathom (enmyssit box-line) er ny choyrt stiagh er y box ayns ny boggey, t'eh goll er e raad gys y gunwale foast beggan ny s'yiarn, as t'eh eisht ceaut rish y short-warp—y cord ta ceaut dy kiart rish y harpoon; agh roish y cheshaght shen, ta'n short-warp goll trooid mystifications ry-tedious dy ghra.

As myr shen ta'n line-balley foltaghey y baatey ooilley ayns e coillyn complicated, t'eh shleaystey as shleaystey mygeayrt echey ayns cha row ymmyd erbee jeh. Ta ooilley ny rowlaghyn ry-cheilley rish e chorreeaghyn peril; son dy vel ad myr juggleryn Indian, as ny naghtyn smoo marvaanagh festooning sportively nyn limmey. As cha vod mac ben marvaanagh erbee, son y chied cheayrt, soie eh hene ayns mean ny h-intricacies hempen shen, as tra t’eh streeu e hrooid er y rowl, smooinaghtyn er dy vod y harpoon ve dart ayns traa erbee nagh vel fys echey, as dy vod ooilley ny contortions s’odjey shoh ve currit ayns cloie myr laghyn ry-cheilley; cha vod eh ve myr shen gyn shliaghtey ta jannoo y mharroo hene ayns e chree dy shliaghtey ayn myr jelly shliaghtey. Agh habit—red s’diuney! cre nagh vod y ghlare y yannoo?—Sallies gayer, s’mish, jokes ny s’mish, as repartees ny s’brishey, cha chlashtyn oo rieau er dty mahogany, na chlashtyn oo er cedar geal lesh-inch y lhong-balley, tra t’eh er ny chroshaghey myr shen ayns nooseyn croghey; as, myr ny shey burgheyryn Calais roish y ree Edward, ta ny shey fir ta jannoo seose y chrew tarroogh stiagh ayns glennyn y baase, lesh halter mygeayrt gagh mullagh, myr oddys oo gra.

Ta’n smooinaght er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen ymmyrchagh son y scansh t’er-dy-hoshiaght—cuid jeh’n red shen t’er ny chronicle casually—dy row’n dooinney shoh ny’n dooinney shen er nyn goyrt magh ass y lhong liorish y line, as er nyn goyrt. Son, tra ta’n line dartaghey magh, dy ve ny hoie er-y-fa shen ayns y lhong, t’eh myr dy ve ny hoie ayns mean y whizzing manifold jeh’n steam-engine ayns laghyn y ghraih, tra ta dagh beam fliugh, as shaft, as wheel, dy ghra rhyt. T’eh ny s’mish; son cha vod oo soie dy moal ayns cree ny perilaghyn shoh, son ta’n baatey ry-chroo myr craid, as t’ou er ny hroggal er y raad as y raad elley, fegooish y s’mish y raad; as agh liorish red erbee ta ry-hoiaghey as ry-hoiaghey jeh volition as obbraghey, oddys oo geddyn magh dy ve jeant Mazeppa jeh, as reih ersooyl lesh raad nagh row y ghrian ooilley-fakin hene ry-hoiaghey oo.
