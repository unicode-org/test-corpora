"Gruaie!" dooyrt mee, "goaill rish shen e veeal? veeal feer vieoil eisht; agh cre'n aght ta'n annym echey, t'eh ymmyrkey eh hene; gow sheese, Queequeg, t'ou trome, t'eh glenney veeal y vooinjer vooar. Gow sheese, Queequeg! Cur-my-ner, t'eh shleaystey oo ersooyl dy hrooid. Ta mee smooinaghtyn nagh jean eh dty hrooid."

Va Queequeg er n’eiyrt eh hene gys right er-gerrey da ben y sleeper, as light eh e pipe tomahawk. Hoie mee ec ny cassyn. Keep shin y pipe dy gholl er-gerrey da’n sleeper, veih’n derrey yeh gys y jeh elley. As ec y traa cheddin, tra v’eh er cheau y cheshaght ayns e aght brisht, hug Queequeg dou tushtey dy row, ayns e thalloo, er-yn-oyr nagh row settees as sofas dy chooilley ghraih, y ree, ny chieftyn, as y phobble mooar ayns y chooid smoo, ayns y custom dy ghleiy cuid jeh’n ordreyn s’illey son ottomanyn; as dy hroggal thie dy s'cosoylagh ayns y chooish shen, cha row agh ymmyd jeh ocht ny jeih sleih s'leah, as dy hroggal ad mygeayrt ayns ny pier as ny alcoveyn. As s’cosoylagh, v’eh feer glen ayns excursion; mooar ny share na ny cadreyn gard shen ta ry-chasley gys stickyn-coshee; ec y traa shen, v'un chief goaill e obbree, as v'eh er ny ghra dy row eh er n'yannoo settee jeh hene fo craid spreadjagh, mraane ayns ard-valley marshy tammylt.

As eh dy ghra ny reddyn shoh, gagh traa ghow Queequeg y tomahawk veih mee, v'eh fliaghey y hatchet-side echey er ben y sleeper.

“Cre’n-fa shen, Queequeg?”

"Perry aashagh, marree-e; oh! aashagh!"

Va eshyn er-y-fa shen gobbraghey lesh reddyn fieau mychione e tomahawk-pipe, va, myr v’eh, er n’yannoo brainnaghey e noidyn as sooiltaghey e annym, tra v’ad er nyn skeaylley directly rish y rigger coodaghey. Yn vapoor creeney t’er n’yannoo y towse contractit dy kiaralagh, t’eh toshiaght dy ghra er. Va’n annym echey lesh sorch dy mraane; as eisht v’eh myr v’eh trome ayns y sniaghtey; as ren eh revolt harrish un vee ny daa vee; as hie eh seose as sniee eh e sooillyn.

“Hollo!” t’eh er n’anmey er-jerrey, “quoi shiu smooinee?”

“Gheiney shippit,” dreggyr mee, “cre’n traa t’ee shooyl?”

"Aye, aye, ta shiu goll ayn, nagh vel? T'ee shooyl y laa shoh. Threeg y Captan er-board aash."

“Cre’n Captan?—Ahab?”

“Quoi agh eshyn dy jarroo?”

Va mee er-chea dy ghoaill rish reddyn elley mychione Ahab, tra chleaysh shin noise er deck.

"Holloa! Astir Starbuck," dooyrt yn riggeyder. "T'eh ny chief mate beo, shen; dooinney mie, as pious; agh ooilley beo nish, shegin dou cheet er." As myr shen dooyrt eh, hie eh er deck, as haink shin er.

Va'n ghrian soilshey er-dy-henney. Gow yn skimmee er-board ayns daa as three; as ren ny riggeryn ad hene y ghleiy; v’ad ny mates glen; as va ymmodee jeh'n phobble 'sy chladagh busy tastey dy chur reddyn 'sy jerrey er-board. As ec y traa cheddin va Captan Ahab er-mayrn ayns e chaban.
