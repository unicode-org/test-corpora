As myr shen, va boggey as trimshey, dooyrt as aggle, myr v’ad er nyn skeaylley gys dust erbee, as v’ad er nyn powder, son y traa, ayns y mortar clampt jeh’n annym iarain jeh Ahab. Myr machineyn, v'ad glenney mygeayrt y deck, dy bragh coontit dy row sooillyn despot y seihll orroo.

Agh row oo scaanaghey eh dy doaltattym ayns e ooryn secretagh; tra nagh row eh smooinaghtyn nagh row sight agh un sight er; as eisht v’ou er vakin dy row sooillyn Ahab myr shen s’cosoylagh rish sooillyn y chrew, as s’cosoylagh rish sooillyn y Parsee inscrutable; ny ayns aght erbee, er-y-fa shen, ayns aght erbee fieau, ry-hoi ry-hoi. Ta’n aght shoh dy ghra, glidey, t’er n’investeil y Fedallah thin nish; s’cosoylagh dy row shliaghtey dy bragh er n’yannoo eh; dy row ny deiney jeeaghyn er dy doaltattym; lesh leshtal, myr v’eh er ny hoilshaghey, row eh dy jarroo substance marvaanagh, ny s’cosoylagh, s’cosoylagh dy row eh s’treishteil er y deck liorish corp dooinney erbee nagh row er ny hoiggal. As va’n skeeal shen dagh ooilley traa ry-hoi. Son cha nee 'sy noid, eddyragh, v'eh er ny hoiggal dy row Fedallah er n'gholl sheese, ny dy row eh er n'gholl sheese. Va’n stayd echey son ooryn: agh cha row eh rieau er ny hoiaghey ny er ny hroggal; e sooillyn wan agh ard-vooaralagh dooyrt eh soilleir—Cha nel shinyn daa watchman rieau ry-hoi.

As chamoo, ec traa erbee, ayns oie ny laa, cha row ny marreeyn nish stealt er y deck, mannagh row Ahab roish; ny seasagh ayns e thooill-pivot, ny packaghey dy kiart ny plankyn eddyr daa limmey nagh vel ry-chlashtyn,—y prìomh-mast as y mizen; ny s'cosoylagh honnick ad eh seose ayns y chaban-scuttle,—e chosh beo er-gerrey er y deck, myr dy beagh eh steep; slouch e hat trome er e sooillyn; son choud as v’eh ny s’moal, choud as v’eh er ny chur rish ny laa as ny oieyn, nagh row eh er shleaystey ayns e hammock; agh foast follit fo’n hat slouching shen, cha row ad ry-hoi ry-hoi dy ghra dy row, son ooilley ny reddyn shoh, e sooillyn ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi; ny my v’eh foast scanney ad dy glen; cha nel eh red erbee, agh v’eh sheese myr shen ayns y scuttle son oor immeeaght er y streng, as y ghrian-oie nagh row er ny h-earroo, v’eh er ny chruinnaghey ayns sniaghteyn sniaghtey er y choat as y hat shen v’er ny sniaghtey lesh cloie. Yn cleaysh v'er ny ghleiy 'sy noid, v'eh er n'eiyrt rish y ghrian 'sy laa er-giyn; as myr shen, laa er y laa, as oie er oie; cha jagh eh ny smoo fo ny plankyn; cre-erbee v’eh er ghoaill jeh’n chaban y red shen hug eh er.

Va’n ’eayst echey ayns yr un aer foshlit; shen-y-fa, e daa veeal ny lomarcan,—brastyl as dinner: supper cha row eh rieau er n’yannoo; as cha row eh er n’yannoo e ghraih; t’er n’gholl seose ayns dorraghys ooilley gnarled, myr freaystyn craid s’odjey s’odjey, ta foast glenney s’odjey er bun glen, agh t’ad er nyn stroie ayns y verdure ushtey. Agh geddyn dy row e vioys ooilley er-dy-henney er ny yannoo un watch er deck; as geddyn mystick y Parsee dy row eh fegooish ymmyd jeh; agh cha row ny daa ghooinney shoh er-y-fa shen er-y-fa dy row ad er loayrt—un ghooinney da’n jeh elley—myr shen, ec y traa t’er n’gholl shaghey, row red erbee nagh row ry-hoi ry-hoi er n’yannoo eh ry-hoi. C'raad as dy row spell potent myr shen ry-akin dy row eh ry-cheilley rish ny daa ghooinney; follaghtagh, as da'n skimmee s'aggleagh, v'ad myr pole-like er-chea. Mannagh row ad er y laa chaslys dy loayrt un fockle; rish y noid, v'ad ny-neesht, choud's v'eh ry-akin rish y s'beggey jeh'n ghrioghey. Ayns traa, son ooryn ny s’hickyr, fegooish un sniaghtey, sheese v’ad fadagh er-chea ayns soilshey ny reddyn; Ahab ayns e scuitch, y Parsee liorish y phreeu-mast; agh t’ad foast sheeyney er y derrey yeh; myr dy beagh eh ayns y Parsee er vaik Ahab e skeeal er-dy-hoshiaght, ayns Ahab y Parsee er vakin e ghraih er-dy-hoshiaght.

As foast, ayns aght erbee, ren Ahab — ayns e ghraih hene, myr v’eh er ny hoilshaghey dagh laa, dagh oor, as dagh traa, dy row eh er ny hoilshaghey ayns comandagh da e oikaghyn, — va Ahab myr ard-reilltagh neu-chrauee; yn Parsee agh e slane. Agh reesht va'n daa elley myr v'ad er nyn ghleiy, as tyrant nagh row er ny hoiggal dy row ad er nyn ghleiy; y sfeer lean siding y rib solid. Son bee'n Parsee shoh cre'n aght v'eh, ooilley rib as keel v'eh solid Ahab.

Ayns y chiad glimmering sloo jeh’n laa, v’eh cliaghtey e ghoo iarain veih’n chione,—“Man the mast-heads!”—as trooid y laa, derrey lurg y ghrian-faase as lurg y ghrian-chree, v’eh cliaghtey yn un ghoo gagh oor, tra v’eh er n’eiyrt clowan y stiureyder,—“What d’ye sharp?—sharp!”!

Agh tra v’eh er n’gholl shaghey trimmid ny ceithir laa, erreish da Rachel v’er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh cloan; as cha row spout erbee er ny akin foast; va'n seihll monomaniac myr dy row eh dy bragh creeney er fidelity e chrew; er-y-fa shen, jeh cha row ooilley agh ny harpooneeryn Pagan; v'eh myr dy row eh geddyn aggle, eddyragh, nagh beagh Stubb as Flask er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh'n sight v'eh er n'yannoo. Agh my v’ad ny ammyssyn shoh echey dy firrinagh, s’cosoylagh eh dy row eh er n’yannoo ad y ghra lesh goan, choud as v’eh ry-akin dy row e obbraghyn myr dy row ad ry-hoi.

“Vees mee my-hene er n’yannoo y chied sight jeh’n baljyn,”—as eshy. "Aye! Shegin da Ahab y doubloon y ghoaill!" as lesh e laueyn hene ren eh sniaghtey bowlineyn basket; as cur laue erskyn, lesh un block sheavey, dy ghleiy rish ben y phreeu-mast, ghow eh daa cheu jeh'n cord reeoil sheese; as hug eh un jeh er e basket, ren eh pin y yannoo aarloo son y cheu elley, dy vod eh eh y ghleashaghey ec y raad. As tra v’eh er n’yannoo shoh, lesh y chorneil shen foast ayns e laue as seose kiongoyrt rish y pin, honnick eh mygeayrt e skimmee, s’goanney jeh’n derrey yeh gys y jeh elley; as hug eh e sight stiagh er Daggoo, Queequeg, Tashtego; agh s'cosoylagh rish Fedallah; as eisht s’cosoylagh dy row e sheean s’creeney er y chief mate, dooyrt eh,—“Gow y cord, sir—ta mee cur eh ayns dty laueyn, Starbuck.” As ren eh e phersoon y rheynn ayns y basket, hug eh y goo daue dy hroggal eh gys e perch, as Starbuck y fer hug y cordyn er-gerrey; as ny lurg shen sheese eh kiongoyrt rish. As myr shen, lesh un laue cliaghtey mygeayrt y mast reeoil, ren Ahab jeeaghyn er-chea er y marrey son mylaghyn as mylaghyn, — roish, er-gerrey, er y taobh shoh, as shen, — ayns y chiark leah s’leshtal v’er ny choyrt gys ard-chree cho mooar.

As tra t'eh obbraghey lesh e laueyn ayns ard-valley erbee cha row eh er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen ayns y rigging, nagh row cochiangle erbee erbee, t'eh er ny hroggal seose gys y spot shen, as t'eh er ny choadey shen liorish y cord; fo ny staydaghyn shoh, t'eh er ny choyrt dagh ooilley traa ayns stiurey strimmey da dooinney erbee ta'n watch s'special echey. Son ayns y stayd shoh dy row rigging ry-reih, nagh vod ny caghlaaghyn diuree oc erskyn-towse ve dagh ooilley traa ry-akin dy bragh liorish ny ta ry-akin jeu er y deck; as tra ta ny h-eddinyn jeh ny cordaghyn shoh er nyn skeaylley sheese gagh beggan minutyn veih ny fasteningyn, cha bee eh agh marroo najooragh, my vees, fegooish ymmyd jeh gard-varroo dy kinjagh, y marshal hoistaghey liorish red erbee dy neu-choardail jeh'n skimmee er ny skeaylley er-gerrey as tuittym ooilley swooping gys y marrey. As cha row obbraghyn Ahab ayns y chooish shoh neu-ghlare; y red s’diuney jeu v’eh myr v’eh, dy row Starbuck, chamoo yn un dooinney v’er n’yannoo rish red erbee ’sy stayd sloo dy row eh er n’yannoo rish breearrey — un jeusyn myrgeddin, v’eh er n’yannoo rish y ghraih echey er y look-out v’eh er n’yannoo rish red erbee; dy choyrt e vioys ooilley ayns laueyn dooinney myr shen nagh row eh er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen.

Agh, y chied cheayrt va Ahab ny hoie erskyn; ere v'eh er ve ayn deig minutyn; un jeh ny h-eaystyn-marrey savage red-billed shen ta myr shen fliaghey dy row ad ry-cheilley ry-cheilley rish ny mast-heads manned jeh whalemen ayns ny latitudeyn shoh; t’un jeh ny eaystyn shoh er jeet wheeyl as screeagh mygeayrt e chree ayns maze dy circlingyn shleih nagh row ry-akin. As daag eh mil feed dy row dy kiart seose ayns an aer; as spiral eh sheese, as hie eh eddyn reesht mygeayrt e chree.

Agh lesh e sheean stiagh er y skyn-smooinaght s’diuney as s’diuney, cha row Ahab myr nagh row eh markee yn eayst-feeyney shoh; as chamoo, dy firrinagh, veagh dooinney erbee elley er markee eh mooar, er-yn-oyr nagh row eh ny s’cosoylagh; agh nish va’n sooill s’leaie er-y-fa shen er-y-fa dy row eh er vakin sorch dy ghraih s’creeney ayns chamoo dy chooilley sight.

“Dty hat, dty hat, sir!” ’s t’eh gra dy tappee y marrey Sicilian, v’er ny choyrt gys y mizen-mast-head, as v’eh sheese right er y çheu jeh Ahab, agh v’eh ny sloo na’n rang echey, as lesh gleashtan dooghyssagh jeh’n aer v’er ny rheynn.
