CAPÍTULO 86. Chupa.

Wak harawiqkunapas antilopepa llampu ñawinpa alabaynintam, mana haykapipas uraykamuq pisqupa kuyayllapaq plumachankunatapas warkurqaku; pisi hanaq pachamanta, huk chupata raymichani.

Aswan hatun sayayniyuq Ballena Esperma chupanta yupaspa chay troncopa puntanpi qallarinanpaq maypichus yaqa runapa muyuriqninman pisiyarun, chaymi hawallanpi sapallan, pichqa chunka metro cuadrado nisqallapas. Saphinpa compacto muyu kurkunqa iskay ancho, takyasqa, llañu palmerakunamanmi mastarikun otaq flukes nisqaman, pisi pisimantan kuyurispa mana huk pulgada rakhumanchu. Crotch icha junction nisqapiqa kay flukes nisqakunaqa pisillatam tupanakunku, chaymantataq huk ladoman raprakuna hina huknin hukninmanta kutirinku, chaymi chawpinpi hatun ch'usaq kaqta saqinku. Manan mayqen kawsaqpipas sumaq kaypa chirunkunaqa aswan sumaq sut’inchasqachu, kay suertekunaq killa k’uchunpi hinaqa. Hunt’asqa wiñasqa ballenapiqa aswan mast’arikuyninpiqa, chupanqa iskay chunka metromanta aswan hatunmi kanqa.

Llapan miembroqa huk denso webda camamanmi rikchakun, sinusos soldados nisqakunamanta; ichaqa chayman kuchuspa, tarinki kinsa sapaq estratokuna chayta ruwaq:—hanaq, chawpi, uray. Hawa, uray qatakunapi kaq q’aytukunaqa, suni, llañulla; chawpi kaqmanta kaq, ancha pisilla, hawa qatakuna chawpipi chakasqa puriq. Kay kimsayuq ruwayqa, imapas hina, chupamanmi atiyta qun. Ñawpa Roma pirqakunamanta yachakuqpaqqa, chawpi capaqa huk curioso paralelotam qunqa chay llañu kursu azulejokunawan sapa kuti alternancia rumiwan chay admirakuypaq reliquiakunapi antiguomanta, hinaspa mana iskayrayaspa anchata yanapan albañileríapa hatun kallpanpi.

Ichaqa kay hatun local atiy tendino chupapi manapas aypanmanchu hina, llapan leviatánpa hatun rakinmi awasqa kachkan huk urdimbrewan hinaspa telaswan aycha fibraswan filamentoswan, chaykunam iskaynin lawmanta lomokunata pasaspan hinaspa flukes nisqaman uraykamuspan, mana musyaylla paykunawan chaqrukuy, hinaspa hatunpi yanapay atiyninkupi; chaymi chupanpiqa llapan ballenapa mana tupuy atiq kallpan confluenteqa huk puntokama huñusqa hina. ¿Aniquilación pasanmanchu materiaman, kaymi karqan chayta ruwanapaq.

Nitaqmi kaypas —admirakuypaq kallpan, ni imapipas kuyuyninpa gracioso flexionninta mana puriyta munanchu; maypichus infantilidad de facilidad nisqa ondulakun huk Titanismo de poder nisqawan. Aswanpas chay kuyuykunaqa chaymantan aswan manchay sumaq kayninta horqonku. Chiqap kallpaqa manam haykapipas sumaq kayta nitaq hukllanakuytapas waqllichinchu, ichaqa sapa kutim quwanchik; hinaspa tukuy ima imposingly sumaq kaqkunapipas, kallpaqa anchatam tupan magiawan. Llapasqa Hérculespi mármolmanta tukuyninpi p’akikuq hina watasqa tendoneskunata qechuy, chaymi encantonqa chinkapunman. Dios sonqo Eckerman Goetheq q’ala ayanmanta lino p’achata hoqarishaqtinmi, chay runaq hatun sinqanwan llallichisqa kasharqan, chaymi rikch’akurqan Roma runakunaq atipasqanku arco hina. Angelo Dios Yayatapas runaman rikchakuqta pintaptinqa, ima kallpayuq kay kasqanmanta marcay. Hinallataq imatapas Churipi hanaq pacha munakuymanta rikuchinkuman chayqa, llamp’u, k’uyusqa, hermafroditico italiano siq’ikuna, chaypin yuyaynin aswan allinta hunt’asqa karqan; kay siq’ikunaqa, ancha pisi kallpayoqña kanku chaypas, manan ima atiytapas insinuanchu, aswanpas mana allin, warmi hinallan, kasukuq kaymanta, aguantaymantawan, chaykunan llapa makinpi concedikun, chaymi yachachikuyninkunaq peculiar virtudes prácticas nisqakunata ruwan.

Chayna sutil elasticidadninmi tratasqay órgano, chaymi pukllaypi, utaq sinchita, utaq piñakuypipas, ima sunqu kaptinpas, flexionninkunaqa mana tikrakuspalla ancha graciawan marcasqa. Chaypiqa manan mayqen hadaq makinpas chayta atipanmanchu.

Pichqa hatun kuyuykunam chaypaqqa sapaq. Ñawpaqtaqa, ñawpaqman purinapaq aleta hina llamk’achisqa kaptin; Iskay kaq, maqanakuypi maza hina utilizasqa kaqtin; Kimsa kaq, barreypi; Tawa kaq, lobtailing nisqapi; Pichqa kaq, pico flukes nisqapi.

Ñawpaq kaq: Maypi kasqanpi horizontal kasqanraykum Leviatánpa chupanqa huknirayta ruwan llapa lamar qochapi kawsaq animalkunapa chupanmantaqa. Manam haykapipas kuyurikunchu. Runapipas challwapipas, kuyuriyqa pisipaq qhawarisqa kayninmi. Ballenapaqqa chupanllam purichin. Scroll-wise coiled forwards under the body, chaymanta usqhaylla qhipaman saltasqa, kaymi chay singular darting, salta movimientota qun chay monstruoman phiñasqa nadachkaspa. Lado aletankunaqa diriginanpaqllan servin.

Iskay kaq: Aslla significativo, huk ballena espermatozoide huk ballena espermatozoidewan umanwan, quijadawanlla maqanakuptinpas, chaywanpas, runawan ch’aqwayninkunapi, payqa principalmente hinaspa despreciowanmi chupanta servichikun. Boteta maqaspaqa usqhayllan chaymanta kurkuykun, chay maqayqa qhepaman kutiyllanmi. Sichus mana hark’asqa wayrapi ruwasqa kanman, astawanqa marcanman uraykamun chayqa, chaymantaqa mana hark’ay atinallan chay accidente cerebrovascular. Manan runaq costillanpas nitaq barcopas aguantayta atinchu. Qespikuyniykiqa chaymanta ayqekuyllapin kashan; ichaqa sichus huk ladoman hamun contrario ununinta chayqa, huk chikanpiqa ballena-boteq llamp’u flotabilidadninraykun, hinallataq materialninkunaq elasticidadninraykun, huk costilla raqrasqa otaq huk tabla discontinua otaq iskay, huk clase puntada ladopi, generalmente aswan sinchi ruwaymi. Kay yakupi chinkasqa lado maqaykunataqa challwakuypiqa sapa kutim chaskinku, chaymi warmakunapa pukllasqan hina qawasqa kanku. Wakinqa huk p’achatan horqokun, chay t’oqontaq sayapun.

Kimsa kaq: Manam qawachiyta atiymanchu, ichaqa ñuqaman rikchakun, ballenapiqa llachpay yuyayqa chupapim huñusqa kachkan; imaraykuchus kaypiqa kanmi huk sumaq mikhuy, chaytaqa elefantepa tronconpa sumaq kayninwanmi kaqlla. Kay sumaq mikhuyqa aswantaqa rikukun barreq ruwaypi, maypachachus doncella llamp’u kayninpi ballena huk llamp’u pisi pisimanta hatun flukesninta huk ladomanta huk ladoman lamar qocha patapi kuyuchin; hinaspataq sichus huk marineropa chukchallanta sientekun chayqa, ay chay marineromanta, barbakuna hinaspa llapan. ¡Ima llamp’u sunqu kaymi chay ñawpaq llamiypi! Kay chupa ima hark’ay atiyniyoq kaspaqa, chay ratopin yuyaykunay karqan Darmonodespa elefantenta, chaymi t’ika qhatuman anchata riq, pisi napaykuykunawantaq nosegaykunata doncellakunaman presentaq, chaymantataq zonninkuta acariciaq. Aswan achka willakuykunapiqa, llakikuypaqmi ballenaqa mana chupanpi kay prehensile virtudniyuq kasqan; imaraykuchus huk elefantemantaraqmi uyarirqani, maqanakuypi k’irisqa kaspataq tronconta k’uyukuspa fledota horqoq.

Tawa kaq: Sapallan lamar-qochakunaq chawpinpi mana reparakuspalla ballenata suwakuspanmi, tarinki mana k’umuykachisqata hatun cuerponmanta, misicha hinataqmi lamar-qochapi nina rawray hina pukllan. Ichaqa chaywanpas rikunkin atiyninta pukllayninpi. Chupanpa ancho palmakunan alto wayraman coqueteasqa kashan; chaymantataq hawata maqaspa, chay t’uqyay hina t’uqyayqa kilómetros nisqakama uyarikun. Yaqapaschá piensawaq hatun pistola kacharisqa kasqanmanta; hinaspa huknin puntanpi espiralmanta vaporpa llampu coronanta qawaspaykiqa, piensawaqmi chay llachpanapaq uchkumanta qusni kasqanmanta.

Pichqa kaq: Imaynan leviatanpa ordinario flotante posturanpi hina chay flukes nisqakunaqa wasanpa nivelninmanta ancha uraypim tarikunku, chaymantaqa hawanpiqa manañam qawakunchu; ichaqa ukhuman chinkaykunanpaqña kashaqtinmi, kinsa chunka metrollapas cuerponwan llapa flukesninkuna wayraman sayarispa wikch’usqa kanku, chhaynapin huk ratulla kuyurispa qhepanku, urayman mana rikuy atina t’oqyanankama. Excepto sublime breach —huk cheqaspi willana— kay pico ballenapa flukes ichapas aswan hatun rikuy rikusqa llapa kawsaq naturalezapi. Mana ukhu ukhumanta hatun chupaqa aswan alto hanaq pachata espasmódicamente qichuq hinam. Chaymi musquykunapi, rikurqanichu hatun Satanasta ñak’arichisqa hatun uñanta Infierno Báltico nina rawraymanta horqoshaqta. Ichaqa chayna escenakunata qawaspaqa, tukuy imapas ima sunquyuq kasqaykipim; Danteanpi kaqtinqa, supaykunan hamusunkichis; Isaiaspa ruwasqanpi chayqa, arcángelkuna. Hanaq pachata lamar-qochatapas pukayasqa inti lloqsimushaqtinmi barcoypa mástil umanpi sayaspa, huk kutipin rikurqani inti lloqsimuy ladopi hatun t’aqa ballenakunata, llapanku intiman risqankuta, huk ratullataq huk ratulla kuyurirqanku pico flukekunawan. Chay tiempopi rikusqayman hinaqa, manan hayk’aqpas rikukurqanchu dioskunata yupaychay chhayna hatun ruwayqa, Persia nacionpipas, chaypin nina yupaychaqkunaq wasin. Tolomeo Filópater Africamanta elefantemanta willakusqanman hina, chaymantam ballenamanta willarqani, llapa kawsaqkunamanta aswan yupaychaq kasqanmanta. Imaraykuchus rey Juba nisqanman hinaqa, ñawpaq tiempopi soldado elefantekunaqa sapa kutillanmi tutamantan alabaqku tronconkuta hoqarispa aswan ukhu ch’inlla.

Kay capitulopi casualidad tupachiyqa, ballenawan elefantewan, maykamachus wakin aspectokuna hukninpa chupanmanta hukninpa tronconmanta rimaspaqa, manam tendenciayuqchu kanan chay iskay contrario órganokunata huk igualdadpi churananpaq, aswanraqmi chay unanchakuna mayqinkunamanmi sapakama pertenecen. Imaynan aswan atiyniyoq elefanteqa Leviatanpaq terrierlla, chay hinallataqmi Leviatanpa chupanwan tupachisqaqa, tronconpas lirioq k’aspillan. Elefantepa tronconmanta aswan manchakuypaq maqayqa karqan ventiladorpa pukllaq grifon hinan, chaytaqa tupachirqan ballena espermatozoideq llasa q’epikunaq mana tupuyniyoq ñut’usqanwan, urmasqanwan ima, chaymanmi sapa kutilla hukmanta hukmanta llapa botekunata llapa remonkuwan tripulantekunawan wikch’uykurqanku wayra, anchata imaynan huk indio malabarista pelotankunata wikch’uykushan hinata.*

*Aunque tukuy comparación de manera general granel ballenawan elefantewanqa mana yuyayniyoq hinaña, chay particularpiqa elefanteqa yaqa kaqllatan sayashan ballenawan imaynan huk allqo elefantewan ruwan hinata; chaywanpas, manan kanchu wakin puntokuna curioso rikch’akuy munasqa; chaykuna ukhupin kashan esputo. Allin reqsisqan kashan elefanteqa askha kutipin tronconpi unuta otaq ñut’u allpata horqonqa, chaymantataq hoqarispa mayuman phawaykachanqa.

Kay atiyniyoq chupata astawan qhawarispaymi astawan llakikuni mana chayta rimayta atisqaymanta. Wakin kutipiqa kanmi makiwan ruwaykuna, chaykunaqa runaq makinta allintaña sumaqyachinman chaypas, mana sut’inchanapaq hinan qhepan. Hatun t’aqapin, ancha admirakuypaq, wakin kutipiqa, kay místico gestokuna, chaymi uyarirqani cazadores nisqakunata, paykunan declararqanku Free-Mason nisqa señalkunaman, símbolokunaman rikch’akuqta; chay ballena, cheqaqtapuni, kay métodokunawan yuyaywan kay pachawan rimasqanmanta. Nitaqmi kanchu ballenapa huk kuyuyninkunata munasqan cuerpon generalpi, mana reqsisqa kaywan huntasqa, aswan experienciayoq maqaqninman mana cuenta qokuq. Diseccionay payta imayna atiyman, hinaqa, ichaqa rini qara ukhuman; Manam paytaqa riqsinichu, manataqmi haykapipas riqsisaqchu. Ichaqa, kay ballenapa chupantapas mana yachanichu chayqa, ¿imaynataq umanta entiendeni? aswan askharaq, ¿imaynatataq uyanta entienden, uya mana kashaqtin? Qanmi rikunki wasanpi kaq parteykunata, chupayta, nispas nin, ichaqa manan uyayqa rikukunqachu. Ichaqa manan llapantachu reparayman qhepa partenkunata; hinaspa insinua ima munasqanmanta uyanta, yapamanta nini mana uyayuq kasqanmanta.





CAPÍTULO 87. Hatun Armada nisqa.

Malaca suyupa suni, k'iti yaqa wat'an, Birmah suyumanta uralan inti lluqsimuy ladupi mast'arisqa, lliw Awya Yalapi aswan uralanpi kaq. Chay yaqa wat’amanta huk chirullapin mast’arikun Sumatra, Java, Bally, Timor nisqa hatun islakuna; chaytaq achka hukkunawan kuska hatun topo icha muralla nisqatam ruran, suni kayninpi Awya Yalata Awstralyawan tinkuchispa, unay mana p'akisqa indihina mama quchatataq rakhu k'aspiyuq oriental wat'akunamanta rakispa. Kay murallaqa achka sally-puertokunawanmi t’uqyachisqa kachkan, buquekunapa, ballenakunapa allin kananpaq; chaykuna ukhupin rikukun Sunda, Malaca estrechokuna. Sunda estrecho nisqapiqa, aswantaqa, inti chinkaykuy larumanta Chinaman riq buquekunam, China mama quchakunaman lluqsimun.

Sundapa chay k'iti estrechos nisqakunaqa Sumatra llaqtatam Java llaqtamanta rakin; hinaspa chay hatun islakunaq murallanpi chawpi ñanpi sayashaspa, chay mana manchakuq verde promontoriowan kallpachasqa, lamar qochapi puriqkunaq Java Head sutiwan reqsisqan; manan pisillatapas tupankuchu chawpi punkuwan kicharikusqanwan, mayqen hatun perqayoq imperiomanpas: hinallataq qhawarispaqa mana tukukuq qhapaq kay especiakuna, hinallataq sedakuna, hinallataq joyakuna, hinallataq qori, hinallataq marfil, chaykunawan qhapaqyachisqa kanku chay oriental lamar qochaq waranqa islakuna, rikch’akun naturalezapa hatun kamachikuynin, chayna qhapaq kaykunaqa, allpapa kikin formacionninwan, aswanpas apakunanmi rikchakuyta, mayna mana allinña kaptinpas, tukuy ima hapiq occidental pachamanta waqaychasqa kanankupaq. Estrecho de Sunda nisqapa quchapatankunaqa manam chay kamachikuq fortalezakunawanqa suministrasqachu, chaykunam Mediterráneo, Báltico, Propontis nisqaman yaykuykunata waqaychanku. Kay oriental runakunaqa manan Danes runakuna hinachu mañakunku, wayraq ñawpaqenpi barcokunaq mana tukukuq procesionninmanta uraykachisqa pata velakunaq obsequioso yupaychayta, chaykunaqa pachak watakunañan pasarqanku, tutapipas p’unchaypipas Sumatra, Java islakunaq chawpinta, cargakunawan apasqa inti lluqsimuy ladupi aswan chaninniyuq cargakuna. Ichaqa kay hina ceremonialta libreta saqespankupas, manan hayk’aqpas saqenkuchu aswan allin takyasqa tributota qonankupaq nisqankuta.

Tiempo manaña yuyayniyoqchu malayo runakunaq pirata proas nisqakunan, Sumatra llaqtaq pisi llanthunpi calakuna, islakuna ukhupi pakasqa, estrecho nisqapi puriq barcokunamanmi phawaykacharqanku, lanzankupa puntanpi tributota sinchita mañakuspanku. Europamanta crucerokunaq makinpi sapa kutilla yawarchasqa muchuchisqankuwanña chaypas, kay corsarios nisqakunaq mana manchakuq kayninqa kay qhepa watakunapin huk chhikanta hark’asqa; chaywanpas kunan p’unchaykunapipas wakin kutipin uyarinchis inglés, americano barcokunamanta, chaykunatan chay unukunapi mana pesapakuspa seqarqanku, suwarqanku ima.

Allin, musuq wayrawan, Pequodqa kunanqa kay estrechokunaman asuykuchkasqa; Ahabqa chaykunata pasaspa Javan lamar qochaman, chaymantataq wichay ladoman purispa, Ballena Esperma nisqa kaypi chaypi sapa kutilla risqan unu patapi, Filipinas islakunaq qocha pataman barrespa, Japón nacionpa karu lamar-qochantapas ganaspa, tiempollanpi chay chaypi hatun ballena hapiy pacha. Kaykunawanqa, muyuriq Pequodqa yaqa llapan riqsisqa Ballena Esperma nisqa purina pampakunatam barrenman, manaraq Pacífico nisqapi Línea nisqaman uraykamuchkaspa; maypichus Acab, tukuy hinantinpi qatipayninpi mana allinchu karqan chaypas, allinta yuparqan Moby Dickman maqanakuyta qonanpaq, aswan reqsisqa lamar qochapi; hinaspapas huk tiempopi, maypachachus aswan razonablemente piensankuman chayta qatiykachashasqanmanta.

Ichaqa, ¿imaynataq kunanqa? kay zonasqa maskhaypi, ¿Ahabqa mana hallp’ata llaminchu? ¿tripulantenkuna wayratachu upyanku? Cheqaqtapunin unu maskhaq sayanqa. Mana. Unayñan, kunan, circo phawaq intiqa nina anillo ukhupi phawarishan, manataqmi necesitanchu sustentota aswanpas imachus pay kikinpi kashan chayta. Chaymi Acabpas. Kaytapas, ballena hapiqpi marcay. Wak cascokunatataq hawa llaqtayuq kaqkunata urayman carganku, hawa llaqtamanta muellekunaman apanankupaq; kay pachapi puriq ballena barcoqa manan ima cargatapas apanchu, aswanpas pay kikinta, tripulantekunatawan, armankutapas, ima munasqankutapas. Payqa askha hap’isqanpin huk qocha hunt’a kaqninkunata botellapi churan. Payqa utilidadkunawanmi lastrechasqa kachkan; mana llapanpichu mana llamk’anapaq kuchi-plomowan, perrerawan ima. Payqa watakuna unuta paypi apan. Ch’uya mawk’a prime Nantucket yaku; chaytaq, kimsa wata yakupi kachkaptin, Nantucketer, Pacífico nisqapi, aswan allinta upyayta munan manaraq chay salmuera fluido kachkaptin, ichaqa qayna punchaw balsawan lluqsirqa barrilkunapi, Perumanta utaq indio mayukunamanta. Chayraykun, huk barcokuna Nueva Yorkmanta Chinaman rirqankuman, hinaspa kutimunkuman karqan, chunka puertokunata llamispa, chay ballena-buqueqa, tukuy chay chawpipi, manapaschá huk grano allpatapas rikurqanchu; tripulantenqa manan pitapas rikurqankuchu, aswanpas paykuna hina unupi puriq lamar qochapi puriqkunatan. Chaymi paykunaman aparqanki huk unu para hamusqanmanta willakuyta; nispalla kutichiqku: “¡Allinmi, waynakuna, kaypi arcaqa!”, nispa.

Kunanqa, imaynan askha Ballenas Esperma nisqakuna hap’isqa karqanku Java llaqtaq inti haykuy ladonpi, Sunda estrechoq qayllanpi; chiqapmi, imaynam yaqa llapan allpa, muyuriq, challwaqkunapa riqsisqan karqa, crucero nisqapi purinapaq allin sitio kasqanmanta; chayrayku, Pequod aswan aswanta Java Uma patapi ganasqanman hina, qhawaqkunata sapa kuti yupaycharqanku, hinaspataq yuyaycharqanku anchata rikch’asqalla kanankupaq. Ichaqa chay hallp’aq verde palmera qaqakuna pisi tiempollamanta estribor proa patapi rikhurimurqan, kusisqa sinqa t’oqokunawanña chay mosoq canela wayrapi ch’aqchusqa karqan chaypas, manan huk jettapas willakurqanchu. Yaqa llapan yuyaykunata saqespa kaypi ima pukllaywanpas urmaykuyta, barcoqa yaqañan estrechoman haykurqan, chaypin costumbreman hina kusikuy waqay uyarikurqan hanaqmanta, manaraq unaychu huk espectáculo de singular magnificencia saludawarqanku.

Ichaqa kaypi premisasqa kachun, chay mana sayk’usqa ruwaywan, chaywanmi kay qhepa watakunapi tawantin mamaqochakunapi cazasqa karqanku, chaymi Ballenas Esperma nisqakuna, yaqa mana cambiaspa huch’uy t’aqasqa empresakunapi purinankumantaqa, ñawpaq tiempokunapi hina, kunanqa sapa kutilla tupanku in hatun t’aqa t’aqakunata, mayninpiqa askha runakunata hap’ispa, yaqapaschá askha nacionkunapas jurarqanku liga solemneta, rimanakuytapas yanapanakunankupaq, amachanakunankupaqpas. Kay Ballena Esperma nisqapa chayna hatun caravanakunaman huñukuyninmanqa, imputakunmanmi kay circunstancia, aswan allin crucero pampakunapipas, kunanqa wakin kutipiqa semanakuna killakuna kuska velawaqchik, mana huk esputollapas chaskisqa kaspa; chaymantataq qonqayllamanta napaykusqa kanki, mayninpiqa waranqa waranqaman rikch’akuq.

Iskaynin arcokunapi ancho, yaqa iskay kimsa legua karullapi, hinaspa hatun kuska muyuta ruwaspa, huk kuskan horizonte nivelta abrazaspa, huk cadena ballena-jet nisqakuna sapa kutilla wichayman pukllachkasqa hinaspa chawpi punchaw wayrapi chispichkasqa. Paña Ballenapa chiqan perpendicular gemelo-jetninkuna hinachu, chaykunaqa, hawanpi rakikuspa, iskay k'allmapi urmaykun, saucepa raqrasqa urmaykuq k'allmankuna hina, Ballena Esperma nisqap sapallan ñawpaqman k'umuykuq boquillanmi yuraq rakhu k'uyusqa sach'ata rikuchin phuyu, sapa kuti wicharispa, wayra-paraman urmaykuspa.

Hinaptinqa, Pequodpa cubiertanmanta qawasqa, lamar quchapa hatun urqunpi wicharichkaptin, kay anfitrión vaporpa qaqakuna, sapakama wayraman k’uyusqa, hinaspa qawasqa huk chaqrusqa wayra pachata azul neblina, qawachirqa waranqa kusisqa chimeneakuna hina wakin denso metrópoli, willakun huk bálsamo otoño paqarinmanta, wakin caballo sillakuqpa alturapi.

Huknin kay huch’uy wawachakunamanta, wakin queer tokenkunamanta yaqa huk p’unchayniyuq hina, yaqa chunka tawayuq metro sayayniyuq, yaqa soqta metro muyuriq. Payqa huk chikan frisky karqan; ichaqa kunankamapas cuerponqa pisillaraqmi rikch’akurqan chaywanpas chay phiñachiq posiciónmanta allinyapurqan, chayllaraq materna retículo nisqapi hap’isqanmanta; where, tail to head, and all ready for the final spring, the unborn whale lies bent like a Tartar's bow. The delicate side-fins, and the palms of his flukes, still freshly retained the plaited crumpled appearance of a baby's ears newly arrived from foreign parts.

“Line! line!” cried Queequeg, looking over the gunwale; “him fast! him fast!—Who line him! Who struck?—Two whale; one big, one little!”

“What ails ye, man?” cried Starbuck.

“Look-e here,” said Queequeg, pointing down.

As when the stricken whale, that from the tub has reeled out hundreds of fathoms of rope; as, after deep sounding, he floats up again, and shows the slackened curling line buoyantly rising and spiralling towards the air; so now, Starbuck saw long coils of the umbilical cord of Madame Leviathan, by which the young cub seemed still tethered to its dam. Not seldom in the rapid vicissitudes of the chase, this natural line, with the maternal end loose, becomes entangled with the hempen one, so that the cub is thereby trapped. Some of the subtlest secrets of the seas seemed divulged to us in this enchanted pond. We saw young Leviathan amours in the deep.*

*The sperm whale, as with all other species of the Leviathan, but unlike most other fish, breeds indifferently at all seasons; after a gestation which may probably be set down at nine months, producing but one at a time; though in some few known instances giving birth to an Esau and Jacob:—a contingency provided for in suckling by two teats, curiously situated, one on each side of the anus; but the breasts themselves extend upwards from that. When by chance these precious parts in a nursing whale are cut by the hunter's lance, the mother's pouring milk and blood rivallingly discolour the sea for rods. The milk is very sweet and rich; it has been tasted by man; it might do well with strawberries. When overflowing with mutual esteem, the whales salute more hominum .

And thus, though surrounded by circle upon circle of consternations and affrights, did these inscrutable creatures at the centre freely and fearlessly indulge in all peaceful concernments; yea, serenely revelled in dalliance and delight. But even so, amid the tornadoed Atlantic of my being, do I myself still for ever centrally disport in mute calm; and while ponderous planets of unwaning woe revolve round me, deep down and deep inland there I still bathe me in eternal mildness of joy.

Meanwhile, as we thus lay entranced, the occasional sudden frantic spectacles in the distance evinced the activity of the other boats, still engaged in drugging the whales on the frontier of the host; or possibly carrying on the war within the first circle, where abundance of room and some convenient retreats were afforded them. But the sight of the enraged drugged whales now and then blindly darting to and fro across the circles, was nothing to what at last met our eyes. It is sometimes the custom when fast to a whale more than commonly powerful and alert, to seek to hamstring him, as it were, by sundering or maiming his gigantic tail-tendon. It is done by darting a short-handled cutting-spade, to which is attached a rope for hauling it back again. A whale wounded (as we afterwards learned) in this part, but not effectually, as it seemed, had broken away from the boat, carrying along with him half of the harpoon line; and in the extraordinary agony of the wound, he was now dashing among the revolving circles like the lone mounted desperado Arnold, at the battle of Saratoga, carrying dismay wherever he went.

But agonizing as was the wound of this whale, and an appalling spectacle enough, any way; yet the peculiar horror with which he seemed to inspire the rest of the herd, was owing to a cause which at first the intervening distance obscured from us. But at length we perceived that by one of the unimaginable accidents of the fishery, this whale had become entangled in the harpoon-line that he towed; he had also run away with the cutting-spade in him; and while the free end of the rope attached to that weapon, had permanently caught in the coils of the harpoon-line round his tail, the cutting-spade itself had worked loose from his flesh. So that tormented to madness, he was now churning through the water, violently flailing with his flexible tail, and tossing the keen spade about him, wounding and murdering his own comrades.

This terrific object seemed to recall the whole herd from their stationary fright. First, the whales forming the margin of our lake began to crowd a little, and tumble against each other, as if lifted by half spent billows from afar; then the lake itself began faintly to heave and swell; the submarine bridal-chambers and nurseries vanished; in more and more contracting orbits the whales in the more central circles began to swim in thickening clusters. Yes, the long calm was departing. A low advancing hum was soon heard; and then like to the tumultuous masses of block-ice when the great river Hudson breaks up in Spring, the entire host of whales came tumbling upon their inner centre, as if to pile themselves up in one common mountain. Instantly Starbuck and Queequeg changed places; Starbuck taking the stern.

“Oars! Oars!” he intensely whispered, seizing the helm—“gripe your oars, and clutch your souls, now! My God, men, stand by! Shove him off, you Queequeg—the whale there!—prick him!—hit him! Stand up—stand up, and stay so! Spring, men—pull, men; never mind their backs—scrape them!—scrape away!”

The boat was now all but jammed between two vast black bulks, leaving a narrow Dardanelles between their long lengths. But by desperate endeavor we at last shot into a temporary opening; then giving way rapidly, and at the same time earnestly watching for another outlet. After many similar hair-breadth escapes, we at last swiftly glided into what had just been one of the outer circles, but now crossed by random whales, all violently making for one centre. This lucky salvation was cheaply purchased by the loss of Queequeg's hat, who, while standing in the bows to prick the fugitive whales, had his hat taken clean from his head by the air-eddy made by the sudden tossing of a pair of broad flukes close by.

Riotous and disordered as the universal commotion now was, it soon resolved itself into what seemed a systematic movement; for having clumped together at last in one dense body, they then renewed their onward flight with augmented fleetness. Further pursuit was useless; but the boats still lingered in their wake to pick up what drugged whales might be dropped astern, and likewise to secure one which Flask had killed and waifed. The waif is a pennoned pole, two or three of which are carried by every boat; and which, when additional game is at hand, are inserted upright into the floating body of a dead whale, both to mark its place on the sea, and also as token of prior possession, should the boats of any other ship draw near.

The result of this lowering was somewhat illustrative of that sagacious saying in the Fishery,—the more whales the less fish. Of all the drugged whales only one was captured. The rest contrived to escape for the time, but only to be taken, as will hereafter be seen, by some other craft than the Pequod.





CHAPTER 88. Schools and Schoolmasters.

The previous chapter gave account of an immense body or herd of Sperm Whales, and there was also then given the probable cause inducing those vast aggregations.

Now, though such great bodies are at times encountered, yet, as must have been seen, even at the present day, small detached bands are occasionally observed, embracing from twenty to fifty individuals each. Such bands are known as schools. They generally are of two sorts; those composed almost entirely of females, and those mustering none but young vigorous males, or bulls, as they are familiarly designated.

In cavalier attendance upon the school of females, you invariably see a male of full grown magnitude, but not old; who, upon any alarm, evinces his gallantry by falling in the rear and covering the flight of his ladies. In truth, this gentleman is a luxurious Ottoman, swimming about over the watery world, surroundingly accompanied by all the solaces and endearments of the harem. The contrast between this Ottoman and his concubines is striking; because, while he is always of the largest leviathanic proportions, the ladies, even at full growth, are not more than one-third of the bulk of an average-sized male. They are comparatively delicate, indeed; I dare say, not to exceed half a dozen yards round the waist. Nevertheless, it cannot be denied, that upon the whole they are hereditarily entitled to en bon point .

It is very curious to watch this harem and its lord in their indolent ramblings. Like fashionables, they are for ever on the move in leisurely search of variety. You meet them on the Line in time for the full flower of the Equatorial feeding season, having just returned, perhaps, from spending the summer in the Northern seas, and so cheating summer of all unpleasant weariness and warmth. By the time they have lounged up and down the promenade of the Equator awhile, they start for the Oriental waters in anticipation of the cool season there, and so evade the other excessive temperature of the year.

When serenely advancing on one of these journeys, if any strange suspicious sights are seen, my lord whale keeps a wary eye on his interesting family. Should any unwarrantably pert young Leviathan coming that way, presume to draw confidentially close to one of the ladies, with what prodigious fury the Bashaw assails him, and chases him away! High times, indeed, if unprincipled young rakes like him are to be permitted to invade the sanctity of domestic bliss; though do what the Bashaw will, he cannot keep the most notorious Lothario out of his bed; for, alas! all fish bed in common. As ashore, the ladies often cause the most terrible duels among their rival admirers; just so with the whales, who sometimes come to deadly battle, and all for love. They fence with their long lower jaws, sometimes locking them together, and so striving for the supremacy like elks that warringly interweave their antlers. Not a few are captured having the deep scars of these encounters,—furrowed heads, broken teeth, scolloped fins; and in some instances, wrenched and dislocated mouths.

But supposing the invader of domestic bliss to betake himself away at the first rush of the harem's lord, then is it very diverting to watch that lord. Gently he insinuates his vast bulk among them again and revels there awhile, still in tantalizing vicinity to young Lothario, like pious Solomon devoutly worshipping among his thousand concubines. Granting other whales to be in sight, the fishermen will seldom give chase to one of these Grand Turks; for these Grand Turks are too lavish of their strength, and hence their unctuousness is small. As for the sons and the daughters they beget, why, those sons and daughters must take care of themselves; at least, with only the maternal help. For like certain other omnivorous roving lovers that might be named, my Lord Whale has no taste for the nursery, however much for the bower; and so, being a great traveller, he leaves his anonymous babies all over the world; every baby an exotic. In good time, nevertheless, as the ardour of youth declines; as years and dumps increase; as reflection lends her solemn pauses; in short, as a general lassitude overtakes the sated Turk; then a love of ease and virtue supplants the love for maidens; our Ottoman enters upon the impotent, repentant, admonitory stage of life, forswears, disbands the harem, and grown to an exemplary, sulky old soul, goes about all alone among the meridians and parallels saying his prayers, and warning each young Leviathan from his amorous errors.

Now, as the harem of whales is called by the fishermen a school, so is the lord and master of that school technically known as the schoolmaster. It is therefore not in strict character, however admirably satirical, that after going to school himself, he should then go abroad inculcating not what he learned there, but the folly of it. His title, schoolmaster, would very naturally seem derived from the name bestowed upon the harem itself, but some have surmised that the man who first thus entitled this sort of Ottoman whale, must have read the memoirs of Vidocq, and informed himself what sort of a country-schoolmaster that famous Frenchman was in his younger days, and what was the nature of those occult lessons he inculcated into some of his pupils.

The same secludedness and isolation to which the schoolmaster whale betakes himself in his advancing years, is true of all aged Sperm Whales. Almost universally, a lone whale—as a solitary Leviathan is called—proves an ancient one. Like venerable moss-bearded Daniel Boone, he will have no one near him but Nature herself; and her he takes to wife in the wilderness of waters, and the best of wives she is, though she keeps so many moody secrets.

The schools composing none but young and vigorous males, previously mentioned, offer a strong contrast to the harem schools. For while those female whales are characteristically timid, the young males, or forty-barrel-bulls, as they call them, are by far the most pugnacious of all Leviathans, and proverbially the most dangerous to encounter; excepting those wondrous grey-headed, grizzled whales, sometimes met, and these will fight you like grim fiends exasperated by a penal gout.

The Forty-barrel-bull schools are larger than the harem schools. Like a mob of young collegians, they are full of fight, fun, and wickedness, tumbling round the world at such a reckless, rollicking rate, that no prudent underwriter would insure them any more than he would a riotous lad at Yale or Harvard. They soon relinquish this turbulence though, and when about three-fourths grown, break up, and separately go about in quest of settlements, that is, harems.

Another point of difference between the male and female schools is still more characteristic of the sexes. Say you strike a Forty-barrel-bull—poor devil! all his comrades quit him. But strike a member of the harem school, and her companions swim around her with every token of concern, sometimes lingering so near her and so long, as themselves to fall a prey.
