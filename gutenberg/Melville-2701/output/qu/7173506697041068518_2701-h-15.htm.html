CAPÍTULO 74. Ballena Espermatográfica nisqapa uman —Contrastada nisqa qawariy.

Kaypi, kunan, iskay hatun ballenakuna, umankuta churaspa; paykunaman hukllawakusun, hinaspa kikinchikta huñusun.

Folio leviatán nisqakunaq hatun ordenninmantaqa, Ballena Esperma, Ballena Derecha nisqakunan aswan reparanapaq hina kanku. Paykunallam runapa sapa kuti hapisqan ballenakuna. Nantucketerpaqqa, llapan riqsisqa variedades de ballenakunapa iskay extremosnintam qawachinku. Imaynan paykunapura hawamanta t’aqanakuyqa aswantaqa umankupi rikukun; sapankankuq uman hinataq kay ratoqa Pequodpa ladonmanta warkusqa kashan; hinaspapas imaynan hukmanta hukninman mana hark’asqalla risunman hinata, cubierta chimpaspalla:—maypitaq, yachayta munani, aswan allin chansata tarinki cetología práctica nisqamanta yachanaykipaq kaymanta?

Ñawpaqtaqa, kay umakunapura general contraste nisqawanmi admirakunki. Iskayninkupas hatunmi kanku llapa concienciapi; ichaqa kanmi huk cierta simetría matemática Ballena Esperma nisqapi, chaytan Paña Ballena nisqapi llakikuypaq faltan. Ballena Esperma nisqapa umanpiqa aswanmi caracter kachkan. Qhawasqaykiman hinan mana munaspalla paymanta hatun aswan allin kayta qonki, tukuy hinantinpi dignidadpi. Kunan instanciapipas, kay dignidadqa aswan hatunmi umachanpa pimintawan kachiwan colorninwan puntapi, chaymi qun señal machu edadmanta hinaspa hatun experienciamanta. Pisi rimayllapi, paymi challwaqkuna técnicamente sutichasqanku “gris umayuq ballena”.

Kunanqa reparasun kay umakunapi imakuna aswan pisi mana rikch’akuq kaqta, chaymi iskay aswan importante órganokuna, ñawiwan rinriwan. Uma ladonpi karu qhepaman, uraymantaq, mayqen ballenaq quijadanpa ángulo qayllapi, sichus k’itita maskhanki chayqa, qhepamanmi mana chukchayoq ñawita rikunki, chaytaqa huk uña asno ñawi hinatan qhawariwaq; chaymi tukuy imamantapas umaq hatun kayninman.

Kunanqa, kay peculiar lado posición ballena ñawikunaq, sut’inmi mana hayk’aqpas rikunmanchu huk objeto, chaymi exactamente ñawpaqpi kashan, manan aswantachu huk exactamente popapi. Huk rimayllapim, ballenapa ñawinpa maypi kasqanqa runapa rinrinpa kasqanwanmi tupan; hinaspapas yuyaykunkimanmi, qan kikiykipaq, imaynatas qanwan pasananta, ladomanchu ninriykiwan imakunatapas t’aqwirqanki. Tariwaqmi yaqa kimsa chunka grados rikuyllata kamachiyta atisqaykita, chiqan lado qhawaymanta ñawpaqta; hinaspa yaqa kimsa chunka masnin qipanpi. Sichus aswan k’arak awqayki derechota purisunkiman, ancho p’unchaypi puñal hoqarispa chayqa, manan rikuwaqchu, aswanpas qhepamanta suwasunkiman hina. Huk rimayllapi, iskay wasanniyuq kankiman, nisunman; ichaqa, kaq pachallapi, iskay ñawpaqkunapas (lado frentekuna): imaraykutaq runaq ñawpaqninta ruwan —imataq, cheqaqtapuni, aswanpas ñawinta?

Chaymantapas, kunan yuyaykusqay yaqa llapan huk uywakunapipas, ñawikunaqa mana reparay atina hinan tarpusqa kashan rikuy atiyninta chaqrunanpaq, chhaynapin huk siq’ita ruwakun, manan iskaychu umaman; ballena ñawinkunaq sapaq posiciónnin, allinta t’aqasqa imayna kasqanman hina askha metro cúbico sinchi umayoq, chaymi paykunaq chawpinpi hatun urqu hina sayan iskay qochakunata wayq’okunapi t’aqaq; kayqa, chiqamanta, tukuyninpi t’aqanan tiyan chay impresionkunata mayqinkunatachus sapa independiente órgano qun. Chaymi ballenaqa kay ladonpi huk sapaq siq’ita rikunan, chay ladonpitaq huk sapaq siq’ita; tukuy chay chawpintaqmi ichaqa ukhu tutayaq, mana imapas paypaqqa kanan. Runaqa, yaqapaschá nisunman, ventananta iskay watasqa sach’akunayoq centinela cajamanta kay pachata qhawan. Ichaqa ballenawanqa kay iskay sach’akunatan sapaq-sapaq churanku, chaymi iskay sapaq ventanata ruwan, ichaqa llakikuypaqmi mana allintachu qhawariyta. Kay ballena ñawipa peculiaridadninqa challwakuypiqa sapa kutim yuyaypi hapina; hinaspapas wakin qatiqnin escenakunapi ñawinchaqpa yuyarinanpaq.

Huk curioso hinaspa aswan musphachiq tapukuy qallarikunman kay rikuy materiamanta Leviatán llamiy hina. Ichaqa huk pistawanmi contentakusaq. Runaq ñawin k’anchaypi kichasqa kashaqtinqa, rikuy ruwayqa mana munasqallanmi; chaymi, chaymantaqa manan yanapayta atinchu mecánicamente ima imakunachus ñawpaqenpi kashaqta rikuyta. Chaywanpas, piqpa experiencianpas yachachinqa, huk qhawariyllapi mana sapaqchakuspa imakunatapas barreyta atinman chaypas, manan atikunmanchu, allinta qhawarispa, hunt’asqata, mayqen iskay kaqkunatapas t’aqwiyta —mayña hatunña otaq may huch’uyña— hukllapi hinaspa kikin ratu pacha; mana imapas qonqanachu lado lado siriykuspanku llaminakuspankuqa. Ichaqa sichus kunan hamunkichis kay iskay imaymanakunata t’aqanaykichispaq, hinaspataq sapankankuta huk ukhu tutayaq muyuwan muyurichinkichis chayqa; chaymantataq, hukninta rikunaykipaq, yuyayniykita chaypi hap’inanpaq, hukninqa kunan pacha concienciaykimanta llapanpi qarqosqa kanqa. Chhaynaqa, ¿imaynataq ballenawan? Chiqapmi, iskaynin ñawinpas, kikinpiqa, huk kutillapim ruwanan; ichaqa, ¿umanmantaqa aswan hatunchu, hukllawakuq, mana reparayllachu umachan, chaymi huk ratollapi allinta qhawarinman iskay sapaq suyakuykunata, hukninmi huknin ladonpi, huknintaq huk ladomanpuni? Atiptinqa, ¿chayna admirakuypaqchu paypi kachkan, huk runapas Euclidepi iskay sapaq sasachakuykunapa qawachisqankunata huk kutillapi pasayta atichkanmanpas hina. Nitaqmi, allinta investigasqa, mana ima incongruenciapas kay tupanachiypiqa kanchu.

Ichapas mana imapaq valeq capricholla kanman, ichaqa ñuqapaqqa sapa kutim rikchakuwarqa, wakin ballenakunapa kimsa tawa botekunawan muyuykuptinku qawachisqanku kuyuypa extraordinario vacilacionninkuna; chay manchachikuy hinaspa responsabilidad queer manchakuykunaman, chaymi común chayna ballenakunapaq; Yuyaykuni tukuy kaykunaqa indirectamenten lloqsimushan volicionpa mana ima ruway atiq perplejidadninmanta, chaypin paykunaq t’aqasqa, diametralmente contrario rikuy atiyninkuqa paykunata involucrananku.

Ichaqa ballenapa ninrinmi ñawi hina hunt’a yachayta munashan. Sichus paykunaq irqinkumanta mana reqsisqa kanki chayqa, askha horakunan kay iskay uma patapi cazawaq, manataqmi hayk’aqpas chay órgano nisqataqa tarinkichu. Ninriqa manam hawa raphiyuqchu ima; hinaspa kikin t’oqonmanpas yaqapaschá plumata churayta atiwaq, chaymi admirakuypaq minutolla. Ñawipa qipanpi aslla samasqa kachkan. Ninrinkumanta respetowan rimaspaqa, kay importante diferenciatan qhawarina ballena espermatozoidewan pañawan. Ñawpaq kaqpa rinrin hawa kicharisqa kaptinpas, qhipa kaqpa rinrinqa llapanmi hinaspa kaqllata huk membranawan qatasqa kachkan, chaymi hawanmantaqa mana allintachu musyakun.

¿Manachu curioso kanman, ballena hina hatun kawsaq kay pachata ancha huch’uy ñawiwan rikuspa, liebremanta aswan huch’uy rinriwantaq t’oqyayta uyarinan? Ichaqa sichus ñawinkuna Herschelpa hatun telescopionpa lentesnin hina ancho kanman chayqa; rinrinkunapas hatun wasikunaq p’ortankuna hina hatunmi karqan; ¿chaywanchu aswan unayta rikunman, icha aswan filochu uyarinman? Manapuni....Chaynaqa, ¿imanasqataq yuyaynikita “hatunyachinaykipaq” kallpanchakunki? Subtilizarlo.

Kunanqa ima palancakunawanpas, vapor-motorkunawanpas makinchikpi kaptin, ballena espermatozoidepa umanpa hawanpi mana atispa, uraymanta wichayman puñunanpaq; chaymantataq, huk escalerawan wicharispa, puntaman, simi urayman huk qhawayta ruway; manataqchus kunan cuerpoqa chaymanta llapanta t’aqasqa kashan chayqa, huk linternawanmi wiksanpa hatun Kentucky Mamut machayman uraykusunman. Ichaqa kay kirumanta kaypi hap’ipakusun, hinaspa muyuriqninchista qhawarisun maypi kasqanchista. ¡Mayna sumaq hinaspa chuya rikchayniyuq simi! pampamanta techokama, qatasqa, utaq aswan allinta nisunman, llimp’iyuq yuraq membranawan papelchasqa, novia satinkuna hina llimp’iyuq.

Ichaqa kunan lloqsiy, hinaspa qhawariy kay portentoso uray quijadata, chaymi rikch’akun hatun tabaco cajaq suni k’iti tapanman, bisagran huk puntanpi, huk ladonmantaqa. Sichus chayta t’oqyachinki, uma pataman chayanaykipaq, hinaspataq kirun filakunata sut’inchanki chayqa, manchay portcullismanmi rikch’akun; hinaspa chayna, ¡ay! askha runakunamanmi rikuchin challwakuypi wakcha wight, paymanmi kay espigakuna urmaykun k’aspi k’aspiwan. Ichaqa aswan manchakuypaqmi qhaway, lamar qochapi allinta entiendespaqa, rikunki wakin sulky ballenata, chaypi warkusqata wayt’ashaqta, admirakuypaq quijadanwan, yaqa chunka phisqayoq metro sayayniyoq, derecho urayman warkusqa cuerponwan derecho ángulokunapi, llapanpaq pachaqa barcoq jib-boom nisqa hina. Kay ballenaqa manam wañusqachu; payqa hukmanyasqallañam kachkan; mana clasemanta, ichapas; hipocondria nisqa; hinaspa ancha tiyasqa, chaymi quijadanpa bisagrankuna samaykusqa, chaypi saqispa chay mana allin sasachakuypi, llapa ayllunpaq k’amiy, paykunaqa, mana iskayrayaspa, llachpay quijadakunata k’aminanku.

Yaqa llapanpim kay uray quijada —huk practicasqa artista facil-llata mana bisagrayuq kasqanrayku— desenganchakun hinaspa cubiertapi hoqarikun marfil kirukunata hurqunankupaq, hinaspa chay sinchi yuraq ballena tullumanta suministrota qunankupaq, chaywanmi challwaqkuna tukuy rikchaq curioso artículokunata ruwanku, chaymantapas kaspikuna, paraguas-stocks, hinaspa sillakuq-azotekuna hapinakuna.

Unay sayk’usqa elevadorwanmi quijadataqa barcoman aysanku, ancla hina; hinaspapas allin tiempo chayamuqtin —huk llank’aymanta yaqa pisi p’unchaykuna qhepaman— Queequeg, Daggoo, Tashtego ima, llapanku allin kiru hampiq kasqankurayku, kirukunata siq’inankupaq churasqa kanku. Queequegqa sinchi kuchuq palawanmi encías nisqakunata lanzakun; chaymantataq quijadataqa anillo pernokunaman watanku, huk aparejotaq hanaqmanta rigged kashaqtin, kay kirukunata aysanku, imaynan Michigan torokuna sallqa sach’a hallp’akunamanta mawk’a encina sach’akunaq tocónkunata aysanku hinata. Llapanpiqa tawa chunka iskayniyuq kirukunam kan; mawk’a ballenakunapiqa, ancha ususqa, ichaqa mana ismusqa; nitaqmi artificial modanchisman hinachu hunt’asqa. Chay qhepamanmi quijadataqa sierranku losas nisqaman, hinaspan wasikuna ruwanapaq vigas hina huñunku.





75 CAPÍTULO.Paña Ballena Uma —Contrastado Rikuy.

Cubierta chimpaspa, kunan allinta unayta qhawarisun Paña Ballenapa umanta.

Imaynan forma generalpi hina noble Ballena Esperma nisqa umanqa tupachisqa kanman huk Roma guerra-carretawan (aswantaqa ñawpaqninpi, maypichus ancha ancho muyusqa); chaymi, hatun qhawariypiqa, Paña Ballenaq umanqa aswan mana sumaq rikch’akuyninta apamun hatun galiote chakiyoq zapatoman. Iskay pachak wata ñawpaqtaqa huk machu holandés viajero runan rikch’ayninta tupachirqan zapato ruwaqpa qhepa kaqninwan. Hinaspa kay kikin qhipa utaq zapatopi, chay machu warmi wawa wasimanta, chay llalliq uñakunawan, ancha allinta samaykunman, paypas llapan mirayninkunawan.

Ichaqa kay hatun umaman asuykusqaykiman hinan hukniray aspectokunata hap’iyta qallarikun, qhawarisqaykiman hina. Sichus puntanpi sayaspa kay iskay F nisqaman rikch’akuq boquillakunata qhawarinki chayqa, llapan umatan hap’iwaq hatun bajo-viol nisqapaq, hinallataq kay espiralkunata, aperturakunata tabla de sonido nisqapi. Chaymantataq, yapamanta, sichus ñawiykita qhawarinki kay mana reqsisqa, crestayoq, peine hina incrustacionta masa patapi —kay verde, barnacled nisqa imapas, chaytan Groenlandia runakunaqa “corona” nispa sutichanku, Sur challwaqkunataq “capó” nispa Paña Ballena nisqa; kayllapi ñawiykita churaspaqa, ima hatun encina sach’aq tronconman umanta apawaq, wasanpi pisqupa quchanwan. Imaynaña kaqtinpas, chay kawsaq cangrejokunata kaypi kay capópi llanthukuqta qhawaspaqa, yaqapaschá chay hina yuyayqa yuyayniykiman hamunqa; mana chayqa, cheqaqtapuni, fantasiaykiqa “corona” nisqa término técnico nisqawanpas allichasqa karqan, chaymanpas qosqa; chay hina kaqtinqa anchatan interesakunki yuyaykuyta imaynatas kay atiyniyoq monstruo cheqaqtapuni lamar-qochapi diademayoq rey, paypa verde coronanmi kay admirakuypaq hina paypaq huñusqa. Ichaqa kay ballena rey kanman chayqa, ancha sulky rikch’ayniyoq runan huk diadema graciananpaq. ¡Qhawariy chay warkusqa uray simita! ¡ima hatun sulk y poutmi chaypi kachkan! huk sulk y pout, carpintero tupusqanman hina, yaqa iskay chunka metro sayayniyoq, phisqa metro ukhu; huk sulk y pout nisqa, chaymi yaqa 500 litro aceiteta aswan askhatawan qosunki.

Hatun llakikuy, kunan, kay desgraciado ballena liebre simiyuq kanan. Chay raqraqa yaqa huk chaki chimpapim. Yaqapaschá chay mamaqa huk importante intervalopi Peru nacionpa costanta barcopi risharqan, chaypin terremotokuna lamar qocha patapi kicharikurqan. Kay simi patapi, llimp’i umbral patapi hina, kunanqa simiman llimp’iykunchik. Rimasqayman hinam Mackinawpi karqani, kaytam hapinay karqa huk indio wigwampa ukunpi kananpaq. ¡Allin Señor! ¿kaychu Jonaspa risqan ñan? Techoqa yaqa chunka iskayniyuq metro sayayniyuqmi, hinaspapas sumaq filoyuq ángulo nisqamanmi kallpan, chaypi sapa kuti qaqa-postepas kanman hina; kay costillayuq, arcoyuq, chukchayuq ladokunañataqmi, chay admirakuypaq, kuskan sayaq, cimetar hina listonkuna ballena tulluwan qawachiwanchik, huk ladomanta kimsa pachak niy, chaykunam umapa hawanmanta utaq corona tullumanta hapipakuspa, chay veneciano persianakunata ruwanku, chaykunam huklawkunapipas kursuta rimasqaku. Kay tullukunapa kantunkunaqa chukchayuq q’aytukunawanmi qatasqa kachkan, chaynintam Paña Ballena yakuta chulluchin, chaypa sasachakuyninkunapim uchuy challwakunata waqaychan, simi kichasqa kaptinqa mikuy pachapi brit nisqa quchakunatam purin. Tullupa chawpi persianasninpiqa, orden naturalninpi sayasqanman hinam, kan wakin curioso marcakuna, kurkukuna, uchkukuna hinaspa qaqakuna, chaywanmi wakin ballena hapiqkuna chay kawsaqpa edadninta yupanku, encina sachapa edadninta hina muyu anillonkunawan. Kay criteriopa seguridadninqa karun mana qawachinapaq kaptinpas, chaywanpas sabor de probabilidad analógica nisqayuqmi. Imaynaña kaqtinpas, sichus chayman entregasun chayqa, Derecha Ballenamanmi aswan hatun edadta qonanchis, ñawpaq qhawariypi allinpaq qhawarisqamantaqa.

Ñawpa tiempopiqa yaqapaschá chay ñawsakunamanta aswan yachay munay yuyaykuykuna kamachikurqan. Purchas llaqtapi huk viajaqmi chaykunatan sutichan ballenaq simin ukhupi admirakuypaq “barba” nispa;* huknintaq “khuchikunaq chukchan” nispa; Hackluyt llaqtapi kinsa kaq machu wiraqochapas kay sumaq simitan utilizan: “Hanaq kuchusqanpa sapa ladonpin yaqa iskay pachak phisqa chunka aletakuna wiñan , chay aletakunan qallunpa hawanpi arco hina sapa siminpa ladonpi.”

*Kayqa yuyarichiwanchikmi Paña Ballena chiqaptapuni huk rikchaq chukchayuq kasqanmanta, utaq aswan allinta nisunman bigoteyuq kasqanmanta, chaymi ura quijadapa hawan puntanpi iskay kimsa chiqisqa yuraq chukchayuq. Wakin kutiqa kay mechos nisqakunaqa aswan brigante hina expresionta qun huk hina solemne uyanman.

Sapa runaq yachasqan hina, kay kikin “khuchikunaq cerdasnin”, “aletakuna”, “barbakuna”, “persianakuna”, otaq ima munasqaykipas, señoraskunamanmi qonku buskninkuta huk k’ulluyachiq aparatokunatapas. Ichaqa kay particularpiqa, unayñan demanda pisiyashan. Reina Anapa tiemponpin chay tulluqa k’ancharisharqan, chaypachaqa farthingale nisqa llapan moda karqan. Hinaspa imaynan chay ñawpaq damekuna kusisqa purirqanku, ballenaq quijadakunapiña, nisqaykiman hina; chaywanpas, duchapi, chay hina mana yuyayniyuq kaywan, kunan pachaqa, kikin kichkipa uranpim pawanchik amachanapaq; paraguasqa kikin tullu patapi mast’arisqa karpa kasqan.

Ichaqa kunanqa huk ratulla qunqay tukuy ñawsakunamanta, chukchakunamanta, hinaspa, Paña Ballenapa siminpi sayaspa, musuqmanta muyuriqniykita qaway. Tukuy kay tullu columnakunata chayna metódicamente karunchakuchkaqta qawaspaykiqa, ¿manachu piensawaq hatun Haarlem órganopa ukunpi kasqaykita, hinaspa waranqa pipas qawapayasqaykita? Órganoman alfombrapaqqa aswan llamp’u Turquía nisqamanta alfombratan kapuwanchis —qallu, chaymi simi pampamanpas pegasqa kashan, nisunman. Ancha wira hinaspa llampu, hinaspapas cubiertapi hoqarispaqa llik’inanpaqmi. Kay particular simi kunan ñawpaqenchispi; pasaq qhawariyllapin nisaq soqta barrilniyoq kasqanmanta; chaymi yaqa chayna aceiteta qusunki.

Ere kayta, sut’itachá rikurqanki qallariyniypa cheqaq kayninta —Ballena Espermawan Paña Ballenawan yaqa llapanpi hukniray umayoq kasqankuta. Hinaptinqa, resumenpi: Ballena Paña nisqapiqa manam hatun pukyupa espermatozoides kanchu; manataqmi ima marfil kirupas kanchu; mana suni, llañu mandibula uray quijadayuq, Ballena Esperma hina. Nitaqmi Ballena Esperma nisqapipas kanchu chay tullu ñawsakunamantapas; mana hatun uray simi; hinaspapas pisillatan imapas huk qallumanta rimaq. Yapamanta, Paña Ballena iskay hawa esputo-t'uquyuq, Ballena Esperma huklla.

Qhipa kaqniykita qhaway, kunan, kay yupaychasqa capuchayuq umakunata, kuskallaraq puñuchkaptinku; imaraykuchus hukninmi pisi tiempollamanta chinkapunqa, mana qelqasqa, lamar-qochapi; hukninñataqmi mana ancha unaychu qatipakunqa.

¿Hap’iwaqchu chaypi Ballena Esperma nisqapa expresionninta? Chaywantaqmi wañusqanpas, mat’inpi wakin aswan hatun arrugakunalla kunanqa chinkasqa hina. Yuyaykuni ancho cejanqa hunt’a kananpaq pradera hina placidezwan, wañuymanta especulativa indiferenciamanta nacesqa kananpaq. Ichaqa huknin umalliqpa uyanta marcay. Chay admirakuypaq uray simita qhaway, mana yuyaypichu vasoq ladonman ñit’isqa, chhaynapi quijadata allinta abrazananpaq. ¿Manachu kay enteron umaqa rimachkanman hina wañuywan tupaypi hatun ruwaypaq resolucionmanta? Kay Paña Ballenataqa estoico kasqanmantam hapini; chay Ballena Esperma, huk Platón, payqa Spinozata hap’ikunman karqan qhepa watakunapi.





CAPÍTULO 76. Chay Battering-Ram nisqa.

Ere saqispa, mana kutillapaq, Ballena Espermapa uman, ñuqa munani qam, huk yuyaysapa fisiólogo hina, simplemente —particularmente remar su aspecto delantero, tukuy compactado huñusqa kayninpi. Munaymanmi kunan investiganaykita, sapallayki qhawariywan, qan kikiykipaq wakin mana exagerado, yuyaysapa estimación ima battering-ram kallpa chaypi lodged kanman chayta formanaykipaq. Kaypin huk importante punto kashan; imaraykuchus kay asuntotaqa qan kikiykiwanmi allinta allichanayki, otaq wiñaypaq mana iñiq hina qhepakunayki, huknin aswan manchakuypaq, ichaqa manan pisi cheqaq sucesokunamanta, ichapas maypipas tarikunman llapa qelqasqa historiapi.

Qawasqaykiman hinaqa, Ballena Esperma nisqapa ordinario nadasqanpiqa umanpa ñawpaqninmi yaqa llapanpi sayaq planota yakuman qawachin; qawanki chay ñawpaqpa uraynin anchata qipaman siqasqanmanta, chaynapi aswanta huk retirota qunanpaq chay suni zócalopaq, chaymi chaskikun chay auge hina uray quijadata; reparankin simiqa llapan uma uranpi kashasqanmanta, yaqa kaqllata, cheqaqtapuni, kikiykiq simiykipas llapanpi chukchaykiq uranpi kashanman hina. Astawanpis qhawanki chay ballena mana hawa simiyuq kasqanmanta; hinaspapas ima siminpas —espuma t’oqon— umanpa hawanpi kasqanmanta; reparankin ñawinpas rinrinpas umanpa kinrayninkunapi kashasqanmanta, ñawpaqninmanta yaqa kinsa kaq parten llapan largonmanta. Chayrayku, kunanqa repararqankichá Ballena Esperma nisqa umanpa ñawpaqnin wañusqa ñawsa perqa kashasqanmanta, mana huk órganollapas nitaq ima clase llamp’u reqsisqapas. Midwifery should be taught in the same course with fencing and boxing, riding and rowing.

I know that this queer adventure of the Gay-Header's will be sure to seem incredible to some landsmen, though they themselves may have either seen or heard of some one's falling into a cistern ashore; an accident which not seldom happens, and with much less reason too than the Indian's, considering the exceeding slipperiness of the curb of the Sperm Whale's well.

But, peradventure, it may be sagaciously urged, how is this? We thought the tissued, infiltrated head of the Sperm Whale, was the lightest and most corky part about him; and yet thou makest it sink in an element of a far greater specific gravity than itself. We have thee there. Not at all, but I have ye; for at the time poor Tash fell in, the case had been nearly emptied of its lighter contents, leaving little but the dense tendinous wall of the well—a double welded, hammered substance, as I have before said, much heavier than the sea water, and a lump of which sinks in it like lead almost. But the tendency to rapid sinking in this substance was in the present instance materially counteracted by the other parts of the head remaining undetached from it, so that it sank very slowly and deliberately indeed, affording Queequeg a fair chance for performing his agile obstetrics on the run, as you may say. Yes, it was a running delivery, so it was.

Now, had Tashtego perished in that head, it had been a very precious perishing; smothered in the very whitest and daintiest of fragrant spermaceti; coffined, hearsed, and tombed in the secret inner chamber and sanctum sanctorum of the whale. Only one sweeter end can readily be recalled—the delicious death of an Ohio honey-hunter, who seeking honey in the crotch of a hollow tree, found such exceeding store of it, that leaning too far over, it sucked him in, so that he died embalmed. How many, think ye, have likewise fallen into Plato's honey head, and sweetly perished there?





CHAPTER 79. The Prairie.

To scan the lines of his face, or feel the bumps on the head of this Leviathan; this is a thing which no Physiognomist or Phrenologist has as yet undertaken. Such an enterprise would seem almost as hopeful as for Lavater to have scrutinized the wrinkles on the Rock of Gibraltar, or for Gall to have mounted a ladder and manipulated the Dome of the Pantheon. Still, in that famous work of his, Lavater not only treats of the various faces of men, but also attentively studies the faces of horses, birds, serpents, and fish; and dwells in detail upon the modifications of expression discernible therein. Nor have Gall and his disciple Spurzheim failed to throw out some hints touching the phrenological characteristics of other beings than man. Therefore, though I am but ill qualified for a pioneer, in the application of these two semi-sciences to the whale, I will do my endeavor. I try all things; I achieve what I can.

Physiognomically regarded, the Sperm Whale is an anomalous creature. He has no proper nose. And since the nose is the central and most conspicuous of the features; and since it perhaps most modifies and finally controls their combined expression; hence it would seem that its entire absence, as an external appendage, must very largely affect the countenance of the whale. For as in landscape gardening, a spire, cupola, monument, or tower of some sort, is deemed almost indispensable to the completion of the scene; so no face can be physiognomically in keeping without the elevated open-work belfry of the nose. Dash the nose from Phidias's marble Jove, and what a sorry remainder! Nevertheless, Leviathan is of so mighty a magnitude, all his proportions are so stately, that the same deficiency which in the sculptured Jove were hideous, in him is no blemish at all. Nay, it is an added grandeur. A nose to the whale would have been impertinent. As on your physiognomical voyage you sail round his vast head in your jolly-boat, your noble conceptions of him are never insulted by the reflection that he has a nose to be pulled. A pestilent conceit, which so often will insist upon obtruding even when beholding the mightiest royal beadle on his throne.

In some particulars, perhaps the most imposing physiognomical view to be had of the Sperm Whale, is that of the full front of his head. This aspect is sublime.

In thought, a fine human brow is like the East when troubled with the morning. In the repose of the pasture, the curled brow of the bull has a touch of the grand in it. Pushing heavy cannon up mountain defiles, the elephant's brow is majestic. Human or animal, the mystical brow is as that great golden seal affixed by the German emperors to their decrees. It signifies—“God: done this day by my hand.” But in most creatures, nay in man himself, very often the brow is but a mere strip of alpine land lying along the snow line. Few are the foreheads which like Shakespeare's or Melancthon's rise so high, and descend so low, that the eyes themselves seem clear, eternal, tideless mountain lakes; and all above them in the forehead's wrinkles, you seem to track the antlered thoughts descending there to drink, as the Highland hunters track the snow prints of the deer. But in the great Sperm Whale, this high and mighty god-like dignity inherent in the brow is so immensely amplified, that gazing on it, in that full front view, you feel the Deity and the dread powers more forcibly than in beholding any other object in living nature. For you see no one point precisely; not one distinct feature is revealed; no nose, eyes, ears, or mouth; no face; he has none, proper; nothing but that one broad firmament of a forehead, pleated with riddles; dumbly lowering with the doom of boats, and ships, and men. Nor, in profile, does this wondrous brow diminish; though that way viewed its grandeur does not domineer upon you so. In profile, you plainly perceive that horizontal, semi-crescentic depression in the forehead's middle, which, in man, is Lavater's mark of genius.

But how? Genius in the Sperm Whale? Has the Sperm Whale ever written a book, spoken a speech? No, his great genius is declared in his doing nothing particular to prove it. It is moreover declared in his pyramidical silence. And this reminds me that had the great Sperm Whale been known to the young Orient World, he would have been deified by their child-magian thoughts. They deified the crocodile of the Nile, because the crocodile is tongueless; and the Sperm Whale has no tongue, or at least it is so exceedingly small, as to be incapable of protrusion. If hereafter any highly cultured, poetical nation shall lure back to their birth-right, the merry May-day gods of old; and livingly enthrone them again in the now egotistical sky; in the now unhaunted hill; then be sure, exalted to Jove's high seat, the great Sperm Whale shall lord it.

Champollion deciphered the wrinkled granite hieroglyphics. But there is no Champollion to decipher the Egypt of every man's and every being's face. Physiognomy, like every other human science, is but a passing fable. If then, Sir William Jones, who read in thirty languages, could not read the simplest peasant's face in its profounder and more subtle meanings, how may unlettered Ishmael hope to read the awful Chaldee of the Sperm Whale's brow? I but put that brow before you. Read it if you can.





CHAPTER 80. The Nut.

If the Sperm Whale be physiognomically a Sphinx, to the phrenologist his brain seems that geometrical circle which it is impossible to square.

In the full-grown creature the skull will measure at least twenty feet in length. Unhinge the lower jaw, and the side view of this skull is as the side of a moderately inclined plane resting throughout on a level base. But in life—as we have elsewhere seen—this inclined plane is angularly filled up, and almost squared by the enormous superincumbent mass of the junk and sperm. At the high end the skull forms a crater to bed that part of the mass; while under the long floor of this crater—in another cavity seldom exceeding ten inches in length and as many in depth—reposes the mere handful of this monster's brain. The brain is at least twenty feet from his apparent forehead in life; it is hidden away behind its vast outworks, like the innermost citadel within the amplified fortifications of Quebec. So like a choice casket is it secreted in him, that I have known some whalemen who peremptorily deny that the Sperm Whale has any other brain than that palpable semblance of one formed by the cubic-yards of his sperm magazine. Lying in strange folds, courses, and convolutions, to their apprehensions, it seems more in keeping with the idea of his general might to regard that mystic part of him as the seat of his intelligence.

It is plain, then, that phrenologically the head of this Leviathan, in the creature's living intact state, is an entire delusion. As for his true brain, you can then see no indications of it, nor feel any. The whale, like all things that are mighty, wears a false brow to the common world.

If you unload his skull of its spermy heaps and then take a rear view of its rear end, which is the high end, you will be struck by its resemblance to the human skull, beheld in the same situation, and from the same point of view. Indeed, place this reversed skull (scaled down to the human magnitude) among a plate of men's skulls, and you would involuntarily confound it with them; and remarking the depressions on one part of its summit, in phrenological phrase you would say—This man had no self-esteem, and no veneration. And by those negations, considered along with the affirmative fact of his prodigious bulk and power, you can best form to yourself the truest, though not the most exhilarating conception of what the most exalted potency is.

But if from the comparative dimensions of the whale's proper brain, you deem it incapable of being adequately charted, then I have another idea for you. If you attentively regard almost any quadruped's spine, you will be struck with the resemblance of its vertebræ to a strung necklace of dwarfed skulls, all bearing rudimental resemblance to the skull proper. It is a German conceit, that the vertebræ are absolutely undeveloped skulls. But the curious external resemblance, I take it the Germans were not the first men to perceive. A foreign friend once pointed it out to me, in the skeleton of a foe he had slain, and with the vertebræ of which he was inlaying, in a sort of basso-relievo, the beaked prow of his canoe. Now, I consider that the phrenologists have omitted an important thing in not pushing their investigations from the cerebellum through the spinal canal. For I believe that much of a man's character will be found betokened in his backbone. I would rather feel your spine than your skull, whoever you are. A thin joist of a spine never yet upheld a full and noble soul. I rejoice in my spine, as in the firm audacious staff of that flag which I fling half out to the world.

Apply this spinal branch of phrenology to the Sperm Whale. His cranial cavity is continuous with the first neck-vertebra; and in that vertebra the bottom of the spinal canal will measure ten inches across, being eight in height, and of a triangular figure with the base downwards. As it passes through the remaining vertebræ the canal tapers in size, but for a considerable distance remains of large capacity. Now, of course, this canal is filled with much the same strangely fibrous substance—the spinal cord—as the brain; and directly communicates with the brain. And what is still more, for many feet after emerging from the brain's cavity, the spinal cord remains of an undecreasing girth, almost equal to that of the brain. Under all these circumstances, would it be unreasonable to survey and map out the whale's spine phrenologically? For, viewed in this light, the wonderful comparative smallness of his brain proper is more than compensated by the wonderful comparative magnitude of his spinal cord.

But leaving this hint to operate as it may with the phrenologists, I would merely assume the spinal theory for a moment, in reference to the Sperm Whale's hump. This august hump, if I mistake not, rises over one of the larger vertebræ, and is, therefore, in some sort, the outer convex mould of it. From its relative situation then, I should call this high hump the organ of firmness or indomitableness in the Sperm Whale. And that the great monster is indomitable, you will yet have reason to know.
