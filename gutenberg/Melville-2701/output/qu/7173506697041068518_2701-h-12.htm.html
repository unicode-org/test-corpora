CAPÍTULO 55. Ballenakunapa monstruoso siq’inkunamanta.

I shall ere long paint to you as well as one can without canvas, imapas ballenapa chiqap rikchaynin hina, chiqaptapuni ballenayuqpa ñawinman rikurisqanman hina, kikin absoluto cuerponpi ballena ballena-barcopa waqtanpi amarrasqa kaptin, chaynapi chaypiqa chanintan saruyta atikunman. Chayraykun valenman ñawpaqta chay curioso imaginario retratokunaman willakuy, chaykunan kunankamapas mana manchakuspa allpayoq runaq iñiyninta iskayrayachin. Tiempoñan kay asuntopi kay pachata allichananchispaq, pruebaspa chay hina ballenamanta dibujokuna llapan pantasqa kasqanmanta.

Ichapas chay llapa siq’ipi pantaykunapa punta kaq paqariyninqa tarikunqa aswan ñawpaq hindu, Egipto, Griego esculturakuna ukhupi. Imaraykuchus chay inventivo ichaqa mana escrupuloso pachakunamantapacha, templokunaq mármol panelesninpi, estatuakunaq pedestalninpi, escudokunapi, medallónkunapi, copakunapi, qolqekunapipas, delfín nisqataqa Saladino hina cadena armaduraq balanzakunapin dibujaqku, hinallataq cascoyoq uma hina San Jorge llaqtapi; chaymantapachan chay hina licenciayoq imapas kamachikurqan, manan yaqa llapan ballenamanta aswan reqsisqa dibujokunallapichu, aswanpas askha cientificokunaq presentacionninkunapin.

Kunanqa, tukuy imamanta, aswan ñawpaq kunankama kaq retrato imayna kaptinpas ballenapa kasqanmanta, tarikunmi Elephanta sutiyuq riqsisqa machay-pagoda nisqapi, India suyupi. Brahmankunaqa ninku, chay mana yuyarinapaq pagodapi yaqa mana tukukuq esculturakunapiqa, llapa oficiokuna, qatipasqankupas, runapa sapa yuyaymanay avocacionninpas, manaraq mayqinpas chiqaptapuni rikurimuchkaptin, watakuna ñawpaqmantaraq rikchachisqa kasqanmanta. Chhaynaqa manan admirakunapaqchu, ima clasepipas ballenakuna hap’iq noble profesionninchis chaypi llanthunpi kanan karqan. Chay hindu ballena nisqamanta rimasqa, pirqap sapaq departamentonpim rikurin, Vishnu runayachisqa kayninta qawachispan leviatán nisqa rikchaypi, yachaywan riqsisqa Matse Avatar nisqawan. Ichaqa kay escultura kuskan runa, kuskan ballenaña, chaymi qhepa kaqpa chupanllata qonanpaq, chaywanpas chay huch’uy t’aqanqa llapanmi pantasqa. Aswanmi rikchakun anacondapa llañu chupanman, chiqap ballenapa hatun flukesninpa ancho palmerankunamantaqa.

Ichaqa ñawpa Galerías nisqaman riy, hinaspa kunan qhaway huk hatun cristiano pintorpa kay challwamanta retratonta; imaraykuchus payqa manan aswan allintachu atipan antediluviano hindookunamantaqa. Chayqa Guidopa siq'inmi, Perseo Andrómeda llaqtata lamar quchapi monstruomanta utaq ballenamanta qispichisqanmanta. ¿Maymantataq Guidoqa chayna mana riqsisqa kawsaqpa modelonta hurqurqa? Nitaqmi Hogarthpas, kikinpa “Perseus Descending” nisqapi kaq escenata pintaspa, huk chikantapas aswan allintachu ruwan. Chay Hogarthian monstruoq hatun cuerponmi hawanpi ondulakun, yaqapaschá huk centímetro unuta aysan. Wasanpiqa huk rikchaq howdah nisqayuqmi, chaymantapas mastarisqa chukchayuq siminmi, chaymanmi olakuna kuyurichkan, chaytaqa Támesis mayumanta yakuwan Torreman riq Traidorkunapa Punkupaqmi apakunman. Chaymanta, kanmi ñawpaq Scotch Sibbaldpa Prodromus ballenas, hinallataq Jonahpa ballenas, ñawpaq Bibliakunaq impresionninkunapi, ñawpaq imprimadores nisqakunaq kuchusqakunapipas rikuchisqa hina. ¿Ima nisunmantaq kaykunamanta? Librokuna encuadernaqpa ballenan uvas sach’a hina uraykamuq anclaq stockninta muyuriqmanta rimaspaqa —askha librokunaq qhepanpi, mosoq librokunaq titulonpipas sellasqa qoriwan llusisqa hina— chayqa ancha sumaqmi ichaqa ch’uya fabulosa kawsaqmi, imitasqa, chaytam hapini, ñawpaq florerokunapi kaq rikchaq siqikunamanta. Tukuy pachantinpi delfín nispa sutichasqaña chaypas, chaywanpas kay librokuna encuadernaq challwatan sutichani ballenata hap’iyta munaspa; imaraykuchus chay aparatota ñawpaq kutita riqsichiptinku chayna munasqa karqa. Chaytaqa riqsichirqa huk ñawpaq Italiamanta qillqaqmi maypipas yaqa 15 siglopi, Yachay Kawsarichiy pachapi; chay p’unchaykunapipas, aswan qhepa tiempokamapas, delfín nisqakunaqa Leviatán nisqa animalpa rikch’ayninmi kananku karqan.

Wakin ñawpaq librokunaq vinyetesninpi huk adornokunapipas wakin kutipin tupanki ancha curioso llamiykunawan ballenapi, chaypin tukuy imaymana esputokuna, jets d’eau, rupha yaku pukyukuna hinallataq chiri, Saratoga hinallataq Baden-Baden, hamun burbujaspa paypamanta mana sayk’usqa uma. “Avance del aprendizaje” nisqapa original edicionninpa titulonpim tarinki wakin curioso ballenakunata.

Ichaqa tukuy kay mana profesional hina ruwaykunata saqespa, qhawarisun chay leviatánpa dibujonkunata, chaykunatan nirqanku sober, cientifico delineacionkuna, yachaqkunaq ruwasqan. Ñawpa Harrispa huñusqan viajekunapiqa kanmi wakin placakuna ballenakuna hurqusqa huk holandés libromanta viajekunamanta, 1671 watapi, sutichasqa “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson of Friesland, master”. Chay placakunamanta hukninpim ballenakuna, hatun balsa troncokuna hina, hielo islakunapa chawpinpi sirisqa kachkaqta qawachikun, kawsaq wasanpa hawanpi yuraq osokuna kallpachkanku. Huk placapiqa, chay prodigioso blunder nisqa ruwakun ballenata representaspa perpendicular flukes nisqawan.

Chaymanta yapamanta, kanmi huk hatun quarto, huk Capitán Colnettpa qillqasqan, Inglaterra marinapi Capitán de Poste, sutichasqa “A Viaje round Cape Horn into the South Seas, for the proposition of extending the Spermaceti Whale Fisheries”. Kay libropin huk bosquejo kashan, “Phiseter otaq Spermaceti ballenaq dibujon, México nacionpa lamar-qochanpi wañuchisqa kasqanmanta, agosto killapi, 1793 watapi, cubiertapi hoqarisqamanta” escalawan horqosqa. Iskayrayani mana capitan kay cheqaq fotota horqochisqanmanta, marinerokunaq allinninpaq. Hukllata chaymanta rimanaypaq, nisaq huk ñawiyuq kasqanmanta, chaymi, chaywan kuska kaq escalaman hina, hunt’asqa wiñasqa ballena espermatozoideman churasqa, chay ballenapa ñawinta huk arco-ventanaman tukuchinman yaqa pichqa metro sayayniyuq. ¡Ay, kallpasapa capitanniy, ¿imaraykutaq mana Jonasta chay ñawimanta qhawarispa qowarqankuchu!

Nitaqmi Historia Natural nisqamanta aswan concienciawan huñusqakunapas wayna-sipaskunapaq, llamp’u sonqokunapaqpas, mana kaqlla millay pantaykunamantachu. Qhawariy chay ancha reqsisqa qelqa “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature”. 1807 watapi Londres llaqtapi pisichasqa edición nisqapin kashan huk “ballena” nisqa placakuna, huk “narwhale” nisqa ima. Manam munanichu mana sumaq rikchayniyuq kayta, ichaqa kay mana allin qawaq ballenaqa rikchakunmi kuchusqa cerdaman; hinaspapas, narballenamanta rimaspaqa, huk qhawariyllaraqmi admirachinapaq, kay chunka isqonniyoq siglopiqa chhayna hipógrifoqa ima yachaysapa llaqtayoq escuelapi wawakunapipas cheqaqpaqmi palmasqa kanman.

Chaymantataq, yapamanta, 1825 watapi, Bernard Germain, conde de Lacépède, hatun naturalista, huk cientifico sistemaizado ballena librota horqomurqan, chaypin askha dibujokuna kashan Leviatán nisqapi imaymana especiekunamanta. Tukuy kaykunaqa manan pantasqallachu, aswanpas Mysticetus otaq Groenlandia ballenaq siq’inmi (chayqa niyta munan, Paña ballena), Scoresbypas, unayña experienciayoq runa chay especie llamiymanta, willakun mana naturalezapi kaqninta kasqanmanta.

Ichaqa tukuy kay pantasqa negocioman gorra-sheaf churayqa cientifico Frederick Cuvierpaqmi reservasqa karqan, payqa reqsisqa Barónpa wawqenmi. 1836 watapin Historia Natural de Ballenas nisqa qelqata horqomurqan, chaypin willan Ballena Esperma nisqa siq’ita. Manaraq chay siq’ita mayqin Nantucketermanpas rikuchichkaspa, aswan allin kanman Nantucketmanta retiro resumenniykipaq quy. Huk rimayllapi, Frederick Cuvierpa Ballena Esperma nisqanqa manam Ballena Espermachu, aswanqa squashmi. Arí, manan hayk’aqpas ballenakunata hap’ispaqa allinchu karqan (chay hina runakunaqa pisillatan chaskirqanku), ichaqa maymantan chay dibujota horqomurqan, ¿pitaq yachanman? Ichapas chayta chaskirqa imaynam chay kikin llamkaypi cientifico ñawpaqnin Desmarest huknin chiqap abortusninta chaskirqa; chaymi, chino siq’imanta. Hinaspa ima clase kawsaq waynakuna lapiswan chay chinokuna kanku, askha queer vasokuna platillokuna ima willawanchis.

Aceite vendeqkunaq tiendakunaq hawanpi warkusqa callekunapi rikusqa letrero pintorkunaq ballenankunamanta rimaspaqa, ¿ima nisunmantaq paykunamanta? Paykunaqa generalmente Richard III. ballenas, dromedario humps nisqayuq, hinaspa ancha sallqa; kimsa tawa tarta marinero nisqapi desayunaspa, chaymi ballena botekuna hunt’a marinerokunawan: deformidadninkuqa yawarwan azul pinturawan lamar qochakunapin kuyurishan.

Ichaqa kay imaymana pantaykuna ballenata siq’iypi manan ancha admirakuypaqchu tukuy imamanta. Umanchay! Yaqa llapan cientificokunapa dibujonkunaqa chay wichqasqa challwakunamantam hurqusqa kachkan; kaykunaqa yaqapaschá imaynan huk thuñisqa barcoq dibujon, wasan p’akisqa, allintan representanman kikin noble animalta llapa mana p’akisqa orgulloso cascomanta, esparankunamantawan. Elefantekunaña sayarqanku hunt’a sayayninkupaq chaypas, kawsaq Leviatanqa manan hayk’aqpas allintachu unupi phawaykurqan retrato ruwasqanmanta. Kawsaq ballenaqa, hunt’asqa hatun kayninpi, ancha chaniyoq kayninpipas, mana entiendey atina unukunapillan lamar qochapi rikukunan; unupitaq hatun parten mana rikuy atina, huk lanzasqa linea de pelea barco hina; chay elementomantataqmi wiñaypaq mana atikuq imapas wañuq runaq cuerponpi wayraman hoqarinanpaq, llapa kallpasapa punkiyninkunata, olacionninkunatapas waqaychananpaq. Hinaspa, mana rimanapaq, huk wayna chupakuq ballenawan huk hunt’asqa wiñasqa platonio Leviatánwan contorno diferenciamanta ancha suyasqamanta; chaywanpas, huknin chay chupa ballenakuna barcoq cubiertanman hoqarisqa kaqtinpas, chhaynapin chaymantaqa mana reqsisqa, anguila hina, llañullaña, imaymana rikch’ayniyoq, chaymi mana kikin supaypas hap’iyta atirqanchu cheqaq expresionninta.

Ichaqa yuyaykusunmanmi, chay q’ala esqueletomanta, chay q’ala esqueletomanta, cheqaq rikch’ayninta llamispa cheqaq p’anqakuna horqokunman. Manam, manapunim. Imaraykuchus kay Leviatanmanta aswan curioso kaqkunamanta hukninmi, esqueletonqa pisillatan yuyayta qon general formanmanta. Jeremy Benthampa esqueleton, huknin ejecutorninpa bibliotecanpi candelabrapaq warkusqa kaptinpas, allintam willakun huk utilitario machu wiraquchapa yuyayninta, Jeremypa llapa huk umalliq personal kayninkunawan; chaywanpas manan ima leviatanpa articulasqa tullunkunamantapas kay hinaqa inferiraqchu. Chiqaptaqa, hatun Cazadorpa nisqan hina, ballenapa esqueletonllaqa, chaynallatam tupan huntasqa qullqi churasqa hinaspa acolchado uywawan, imaynam kurupas crisalis nisqawanpas, chaymi anchata muyuchin. Kay peculiaridadqa umapiqa admirakuypaqmi rikukun, imaynan kay libroq wakin partenpipas qonqayllamanta rikuchikunqa hinata. Hinallataqmi ancha curiosamente rikuchikun lado aleta nisqapi, tullunkunaqa yaqa exactamente kutichin runaq makinpa tullunkunaman, menos hatun dedolla. Kay aletaqa tawa sapa kuti tullu-dedoyuqmi, índice, chawpi, anillo, uchuy dedo ima. Ichaqa llapan kaykunaqa wiñaypaqmi aycha hina qatakusqankupi samaykusqa kachkan, imaynam runapa dedonkuna artificial qataypi hina. Huk p’unchay asichikuq Stubb nirqan: “Mayninpiqa mana yuyayniyoq hinaña ballenaqa serviwasunman chaypas, manan hayk’aqpas cheqaqtapuniqa nisunmanchu mana guanteyoqta manejawananchispaq”, nispa.

Tukuy kay razonkunarayku, chhaynaqa, imayna qhawarispapas, necesitankin tukunayki hatun Leviatanqa kay pachapi kaq huklla kawsaqmi, chaymi qhepakama mana pintasqa qhepakunan. Cheqaqtapunin huk retratoqa huk retratomantaqa aswan qayllallapin chay marcata tupanman, ichaqa manan mayqenpas ancha allintachu tupachinman. Chaymi kay pachapiqa manan kanchu imaynapunichus chay ballena imayna kasqanmanta allinta yachanapaq. Hinaspa chay sapallan modo maypichus atiwaq derivayta huk tolerable yuyaytapas paypa kawsaq contornonmanta, chaymi rispa huk ballena hap’iq qan kikiyki; ichaqa chayta ruwaspaqa manan pisillachu peligropi tarikunki wiñaypaq estufa hina paywan chinkaykuspa. Chayrayku, ñuqaman rikch’awan aswan allin kanman mana ancha fastidioso kay curiosidadniykipi kay Leviatán llamiy.





CAPÍTULO 56. Ballenakunaq aswan pisi pantasqa siq’ikunamanta, hinallataq Ballena hap’iq escenakunamanta cheqaq siq’ikunamanta.

Ballenakunapa monstruoso dibujonkunawan tupachispaymi kaypi sinchita tentasqa kani chay aswan monstruosoraq willakuykunaman haykunaypaq, chaykunaqa tarikunmi wakin librokunapi, ñawpaqpipas kunan tiempopipas, astawanqa Plinio, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, etc. Ichaqa chay asuntota pasani.

Tawa qillqasqa bosquejokunallatam yachani hatun Ballena Esperma nisqamanta; Colnettpa, Hugginspa, Frederick Cuvierpa, Bealepapas. Ñawpaq capitulopiqa Colnettmanta Cuviermantawanmi rimasqaku. Hugginspa kaqninqa paykunamantaqa aswan allinmi; ichaqa, hatun probabilidadkunawan, Bealepa aswan allinmi. Kay ballenamanta Bealepa llapa siq’isqankunaqa allinmi, manataqmi kimsa ballenakunapa siq’inpi chawpi kaq siqichu imaymana actitudniyuq, iskay kaq capitulonta tapan. Paypa ñawpaq qillqasqanqa, Ballenas Esperma nisqakunata atacaq botekuna, ichaqa mana iskayrayaspa wakin salón qarikunapa iskepticismo civil nisqa kusichinanpaq yupasqa kaptinpas, admirakuypaqmi allin hinaspa kawsayman rikchakuq efecto generalninpi. J. Ross Browne nisqapi wakin Ballena Esperma nisqa siq’ikunaqa allin chiqanmi contorno nisqapi; ichaqa llakikuypaqmi grabasqa kashanku. Ichaqa manan chaychu paypa huchan.

Paña Ballenamantaqa, aswan allin contorno siq'ikunaqa Scoresby nisqapim kachkan; ichaqa ancha huch’uy escalapi siq’isqa kanku, mana munasqa yuyayta willanapaq. Payqa huklla siq’iyuqmi ballena hapiq escenakunamanta, kayqa llakikuypaq pisiyaymi, imaraykuchus chayna siq’ikunawanllam, allin ruwasqa kaptin, kawsaq ballenamanta chiqap yuyay hina imapas hurquyta atinki kawsaq cazaqninkunapa qawasqanman hina.

Ichaqa, llapanpi hapisqa, karumanta aswan sumaq, wakin detallekunapi mana aswan allin kaptinpas, ballenakunapa hinaspa ballena hapiy escenakuna maypipas tarinapaq presentacionkuna, iskay hatun francés grabadokuna, allin ruwasqa, hinaspa huk Garnerypa pinturankunamanta hurqusqa. Sapakamaqa, Espermatozoideman, Ballena Derechaman ima ataquekunatam representanku. Ñawpaq kaq grabadopin huk noble Ballena Esperma nisqa hatun kallpawan rikuchikun, chayraqmi boteq uranpi hatarirqan lamar-qochaq ukhunmanta, hinaspan alto wayrapi wasanpi apaspa, estufamanta tablakunaq manchay thuñikuyninta. Lanchapa puntanqa huk chikanmi mana pakisqa, hinaspapas chay monstruoq wasanpi equilibrakuspalla siq’isqa; hinaspa chay proapi sayaspa, chay huklla mana yupay atina pachaq llimp’iyninrayku, rikunki huk remo runata, kuskanninta ballenaq phiñasqa timpuq chhullunwan qatasqa, hinallataq saltashaqta, huk qaqamanta hina. Tukuy imapa ruwayninqa admirakuypaq allinmi, chiqaptaqmi. Kuskan ch’usaq chiru-tina yuraqyachisqa mama quchapi phawaykachan; chaypi hich’asqa arpónkunaq k’aspimanta k’aspikunan k’aspi hina kuyurikun; nadadorkunaq umankunan ballenamanta ch’eqesqa kashanku, huknirayta manchakuymanta rimaspa; yana wayra-para karupiñataqmi buqueqa chay sitioman uraykamuchkan. Kay ballenapa anatómica nisqa detallesninpiqa hatun pantaymi tarikunman, ichaqa chayta pasachun; imaraykuchus, kawsayniypaq, mana chhayna allinta siq’iyta atirqanichu.

Iskay kaq grabadopiqa, boteqa hatun kallpaq Paña Ballenapa barnacled flanconpa ladonpi siq’iypim kachkan, chaymi yana qurayuq hatunninta lamar quchapi rollochan wakin musgoyuq qaqa-deslizamiento hina Patagonia qaqakunamanta. Jetninkunaqa sayaq, hunt’a, hollín hina yana; chaymi chimeneapi q’osñi askha kasqanmanta, yuyaykunkiman uraypi hatun wiksakunapi mana manchakuq cena wayk’usqa kananta. Lamar qochapi pisqukunaqa huch’uy cangrejokunata, concha challwakunata, huk lamar qochapi misk’ikunata, macarrones nisqakunatapas llamiykushanku, chaykunatan mayninpiqa Paña Ballena nisqa onqoyniyoq wasanpi apakun. Hinaspa tukuy chay rakhu rakhu simiyoq leviatán ukhunta phawaylla phawaykachashan, chaymi tonelada ch’aqway yuraq cuajata qhepanpi saqeshan, hinaspan chay huch’uy botetaqa chay punkikunapi kuyurichin, huk barco hina, lamar qochapi vapor barcoq palanpa qayllanpi hap’isqa. Chay hinaqa, ñawpaqpiqa llapanmi phiñasqa ch’aqway; ichaqa qhepanpi, admirable contraste artístico nisqapi, huk lamar-qochaq vidrio hina nivelnin thasnusqa, mana atiyniyoq barcoq mana almidonniyoq velakuna urmaykusqan, hinallataq wañusqa ballenaq inerte masan, huk atipasqa fortaleza, hap’iy banderan qhella warkusqa ballenamanta- poste churasqa espuma-t’oqonman.

Pichus Garnery pintor kasqanmanta, otaq karqan, manan yachanichu. Ichaqa kawsayniy chayrayku payqa yaqapaschá yacharqan temanman, otaq admirakuypaq tutor karqan wakin experienciayoq ballenakuna hap’iqpa. Francés runakunaqa acción pintanankupaq waynakuna kanku. Rispa qhaway Europaq llapa pinturankunata, maypin tarinki chay hina galería kawsaq samay ch’aqwayta lonapi, Versalles llaqtapi chay triunfa salapi hina; maypichus qhawaqqa ñanninta maqanakun, pell-mell, Francia suyupa qatiqnin hatun maqanakuyninkunapi; maypichus sapa espadaqa Norte de Luz nisqakunaq llimp’inman rikch’akun, chaymantataq qatiqnin armasqa reykuna, Emperadorkunapas phawaylla pasanku, coronasqa centaurokunaq carga hina? Mana llapanpichu mana chay galeríapi huk sitiopaq hinachu, kaymi lamar qochapi maqanakuy-piezakuna Garnerypa.

Francés runakunaq natural aptitudninqa imakunaq sumaq kayninta hap’inankupaqqa, hukniraytan rikukun ima pinturakuna, grabadokuna ima ballenakuna hap’isqanku escenakunamanta. Inglaterrapa challwakuypi mana chunka kaq partenpas, manataqmi waranqa kaq partenpas norteamericanokunaq experiencianwanchu, chaywanpas iskaynin nacionkunamanmi qorqanku sapallan tukusqa bocetokunata, llapanpi, ballenakuna hap’iypa cheqaq espiritunta willayta atiq. Yaqa llapanpiqa, inglés, norteamericano ballena dibujaqkunaqa, llapanpi contento hinam kanku imakunapa contorno mecánico nisqa qawachisqankuwan, ahinataq ballenapa perfil vacante nisqa; chaytaq, pintoresco de efecto nisqamanta rimaspaqa, yaqapaschá pirámide nisqapa perfilninta bosquejar. Scoresbypas, chanin reqsisqa Paña ballena runapas, Groenlandia ballenamanta huk k’ullu hunt’a largonta qowaspanchis, hinallataq kinsa tawa delicado miniaturakunata narballenakunamanta, marisopakunamantawan, tratawanchis huk serie clásico grabadokunawan, bote ganchokunamanta, cuchillokuna kuchunapaq, grapnelkunamantawan; hinaspapas huk Leuwenhoeckpa microscópica diligencianwanmi isqun chunka suqtayuq fac-similes nisqa hatunyachisqa Ártico rit’i cristalkunapa qawariyninman churakun. Niyta munani manan pisichaytachu chay allin viajeroman (paytan honrani veterano kasqanrayku), ichaqa ancha importante asuntopiqa cheqaqtapunin huk qhawariy karqan mana sapa cristalpaq huk declaración jurada juradata procurasqay, Groenlandiamanta Juez de Pazpa ñawpaqenpi apasqa.

Garnerymanta chay sumaq grabacionkunamanta aswantaqa, kanmi iskay grabacionkunapas francés simipi reparanapaq hina, mayqenpas pay kikinmi suscribikun “H. Durand sutiyuq runaqa”, nispa. Hukninmi, manaña kunan imapaqchus ruwasqanchisman hinachu tupan chaypas, huk willakuykunapipas rimananchismi. Chayqa Pacífico islakuna ukhupi ch’in chawpi p’unchaymi; huk francés ballena hap’iq anclasqa, qocha patapi, thakpi, qellalla unuta barcoman apaspa; barcoq suchuchisqa velakuna, qhepapitaq palmerakunaq suni raphinkuna, iskayninku mana wayrayoq wayrapi kuska urmaykusqa. Thus glistening for a moment, as slowly it subsided, and sank. Then once more arose, and silently gleamed. It seemed not a whale; and yet is this Moby Dick? thought Daggoo. Again the phantom went down, but on re-appearing once more, with a stiletto-like cry that startled every man from his nod, the negro yelled out—“There! there again! there she breaches! right ahead! The White Whale, the White Whale!”

Upon this, the seamen rushed to the yard-arms, as in swarming-time the bees rush to the boughs. Bare-headed in the sultry sun, Ahab stood on the bowsprit, and with one hand pushed far behind in readiness to wave his orders to the helmsman, cast his eager glance in the direction indicated aloft by the outstretched motionless arm of Daggoo.

Whether the flitting attendance of the one still and solitary jet had gradually worked upon Ahab, so that he was now prepared to connect the ideas of mildness and repose with the first sight of the particular whale he pursued; however this was, or whether his eagerness betrayed him; whichever way it might have been, no sooner did he distinctly perceive the white mass, than with a quick intensity he instantly gave orders for lowering.

The four boats were soon on the water; Ahab's in advance, and all swiftly pulling towards their prey. Soon it went down, and while, with oars suspended, we were awaiting its reappearance, lo! in the same spot where it sank, once more it slowly rose. Almost forgetting for the moment all thoughts of Moby Dick, we now gazed at the most wondrous phenomenon which the secret seas have hitherto revealed to mankind. A vast pulpy mass, furlongs in length and breadth, of a glancing cream-colour, lay floating on the water, innumerable long arms radiating from its centre, and curling and twisting like a nest of anacondas, as if blindly to clutch at any hapless object within reach. No perceptible face or front did it have; no conceivable token of either sensation or instinct; but undulated there on the billows, an unearthly, formless, chance-like apparition of life.

As with a low sucking sound it slowly disappeared again, Starbuck still gazing at the agitated waters where it had sunk, with a wild voice exclaimed—“Almost rather had I seen Moby Dick and fought him, than to have seen thee, thou white ghost!”

“What was it, Sir?” said Flask.

“The great live squid, which, they say, few whale-ships ever beheld, and returned to their ports to tell of it.”

But Ahab said nothing; turning his boat, he sailed back to the vessel; the rest as silently following.

Whatever superstitions the sperm whalemen in general have connected with the sight of this object, certain it is, that a glimpse of it being so very unusual, that circumstance has gone far to invest it with portentousness. So rarely is it beheld, that though one and all of them declare it to be the largest animated thing in the ocean, yet very few of them have any but the most vague ideas concerning its true nature and form; notwithstanding, they believe it to furnish to the sperm whale his only food. For though other species of whales find their food above water, and may be seen by man in the act of feeding, the spermaceti whale obtains his whole food in unknown zones below the surface; and only by inference is it that any one can tell of what, precisely, that food consists. At times, when closely pursued, he will disgorge what are supposed to be the detached arms of the squid; some of them thus exhibited exceeding twenty and thirty feet in length. They fancy that the monster to which these arms belonged ordinarily clings by them to the bed of the ocean; and that the sperm whale, unlike other species, is supplied with teeth in order to attack and tear it.

There seems some ground to imagine that the great Kraken of Bishop Pontoppodan may ultimately resolve itself into Squid. The manner in which the Bishop describes it, as alternately rising and sinking, with some other particulars he narrates, in all this the two correspond. But much abatement is necessary with respect to the incredible bulk he assigns it.

By some naturalists who have vaguely heard rumors of the mysterious creature, here spoken of, it is included among the class of cuttle-fish, to which, indeed, in certain external respects it would seem to belong, but only as the Anak of the tribe.





CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
