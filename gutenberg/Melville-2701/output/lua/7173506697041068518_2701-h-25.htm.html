Ne mpindieu Stubb mupele udi uya kulala pa matela adi mapite bungi bu meme mua kuikala muwule tente ne nkunyi; Kuseka, wewe baleine udi useka! Ahab! Buanyi meme, lekela Stubb afue mu bibombi biende!—ma cherries!

"Ma cerise? Ndi anu musue bua tuetu kuikala muaba udiwu akola. Oh, Stubb, ndi mutekemene ne: mamuanyi mupele uvua muangate tshitupa tshianyi tshia makuta kumpala kua bualu ebu; bikalabi kabiyi nanku, tumuma tukese tua mukuba nebalue kudiye mpindieu, bualu luendu ndujika."

Ku mutu kua mazuwa, pabuipi ne bena mazuwa bonso bavua mpindieu kabayi benza mudimu; marto, bitupa bia mabaya, miele ya mvita, ne bingoma bia mvita, bivuabu balame mu bianza biabu, anu muvuabu banyeme ku midimu yabu mishilashilangane; mêsu abu onso a tshijila avua atangila baleine, uvua ku luseke ne ku luseke wenda unyunguluka mu mushindu wa dikema mutu wende uvuaye mudianjile kulongolola, wakatuma tshitupa tshinene tshia butoke bua tshitupa tshia tshijengu kumpala kuende pavuaye wenda lubilu. Difutu, disombuela dia lukasa, malu mabi a tshiendelele bivua mu tshimuenekelu tshiende tshijima, ne nansha muvua muntu mua kuenza malu onso, tshishimikidi tshitoke tshikole tshia ku mpala wende tshiakakuma tshilamba tshia ku dia balume tshia buatu, too ne pavua bantu ne mabaya bateketa. Bamue bakakuluka panshi panshi. Anu bu mashinyi manene avua mapatuke, mitu ya bena tshingoma ivua muulu ivua itenkakana pa nshingu yabu ivua bu ya ngombe. Mu tshina, bakumvua mayi apueka, bu mudi misulu ya mikuna ipueka mu tshina tshia mâyi.

"Mazuwa! Dikalu dia lufu!—ditempu dibidi dia lufu!" wakadila Ahaba mu buatu; "mutshi wandi udi mua kuikala anu wa bena Amerike!"

Mu dibuela muinshi mua buatu buvua buenda bupueka, baleine wakanyema wenda unyunguluka pa tshibandilu tshiende; kadi wakakudimuka muinshi mua mayi, wakatuta kabidi lukasa pa mutu pa mâyi, kule ne muele mukuabu, kadi mutantshi wa metre makese ne buatu bua Ahaba, muaba uvuaye mulale mupuwe bua tshikondo kampanda.

"Ndi nkudimuna mubidi wanyi ku dîba. Ntshinyi, Tashtego! Lekela mumvue marto webe. Oh, nuenu bimanu bianyi bisatu bidi kabiyi bimane kudifila; wewe tshilamba tshidi katshiyi tshipanduluke; ne tshilamba tshia nzambi tshidi tshikengesha; wewe tshibandilu tshikole, ne tshifulu tshia lutambishi, ne tshifulu tshia nsongo,—tshiamu tshia butumbi tshia lufu, nunku ndi ne bua kujimina tshiyi tshiyi ne bua kunshipa! lutambishi lua kapitene wa mazuwa adi manyanguke bikole? Oh, lufu lua nkayanyi pa muoyo wa nkayanyi! baleine ;

Tshingoma tshivua tshituta; baleine mushipa wakaya kumpala; ne lubilu lua kapia, mulongo uvua wenda upitshila mu milonda;—wakapita bibi. Ahaba wakainama bua kuwulengeja; wakatshilengeja; kadi dienda dia muulu diakamukuata ku nshingu, ne kayi ne dîyi bu muvua bantu ba mu Turquie bavua bapuwe bashipa muntu uvuabu bashipe, bakamupatula mu buatu, kumpala kua bena mudimu bamanye ne: uvua muye. Mu tshitupa tshîpi tshiakalonda, tshintu tshikole tshia ku mêsu tshivua ku ndekelu kua tshingoma tshiakapatuka mu tshina tshia mâyi tshivua katshiyi ne tshintu, tshiakakulula muendeshi wa buatu panshi, ne tshiakakuma mbuu, kujiminaye mu buondoke buawu.

Bua tshitupa tshîpi, bena mudimu ba mu buatu bavua bafue bavua bimane; pashishe wakakudimuka. "Mazuwa? Nzambi munene, mazuwa adi penyi?" Mu katupa kîpi emu, ku diambuluisha dia bintu bivua kabiyi bimueneka bimpe ne bivua bikemesha, bakamona tshishima tshiende tshivua tshienda tshijimina, bu mudi mu Fata Morgana wa luya; anu mitshi ya ku mutu idi kayiyi mu mayi; nansha muvuabu bashindame kudi dinanga, anyi lulamatu, anyi difutu, ku miaba yabu mitumbuke kumpala, ba-harpoon bampangano batshivua anu balame tshimuenekelu tshiabu tshia dibuela mu mbuu. Ne mpindieu, bijengu bivua bikuata buatu nkayabu, ne bena mudimu babu bonso, ne tshiamu tshionso tshivua tshienda pa mâyi, ne mutshi wa tshingoma tshionso, ne tshivua tshinyunguluka, tshivua ne muoyo ne tshivua katshiyi ne muoyo, tshionso tshinyunguluke ne tshinyunguluke mu tshipepele tshimue, tshivua tshimbusha tshitupa tshikese tshia Pequod tshivua katshiyi tshimueneka to.

Ino bu mudi bipupu bia ndekelu bibuelakana pa mutu wa muena Inde uvua mubuele mu mâyi ku tshibandilu tshinene, bishiya santimetre mikese ya dibendele divua dimane kumueneka, pamue ne ntanta mile ya dibendele, ivua mienda mipueka, ne malu avua menzeke mu mushindu wa dikema, pa mutu pa mavuala avua abutula bintu avua pabuipi ne kulenga—tshiamu tshikunze atshi, tshianza tshikunze ne tshianza tshikunze mu tshitupa tshîpi; mubandishibue kunyima mu lupepele, mu tshienzedi tshia kukuma dibendele lukasa ne lukasa ku spar udi wenda ukepela. Nyunyi wa sky-hawk uvua mulonde mashinyi manene avua afumina ku nzubu wende wa ku tshilelelu munkatshi mua mitoto, ulenga dibendele, ne wela Tashtego mpata kuntu aku; nyunyi eu uvua mpindieu mupete dipuapua diende dinene divua dienda dibuuka pankatshi pa marto ne nkunyi; ne musangu umue umvua disanka dia muulu, nyama wa luonji uvua muinshi mua mâyi, mu lufu luende, wakalama marto wende mu mashika apu; ne nunku nyunyi wa mu diulu, ne mbila ya banjelu banene, ne mukana wende wa bukalenge mubandishibue mulu, ne tshimuenekelu tshiende tshijima tshia bupika tshibuikila mu dibendele dia Ahaba, wakapueka ne buatu buende, buvua, bu Satana, kabuyi mua kuina mu inferno too ne pavuaye mukoke tshitupa tshia muoyo tshia mu diulu pamue nende, ne mudibuikile tshifulu tshiende.

Mpindieu nyunyi mikese ivua ipueka iela mbila pa mutu pa tshibandabanda tshivua tshienda tshikola; tshipepele tshitoka tshidi tshituta ku nseke yende mitumbuke; pashishe bionso biakakuluka, ne tshilamba tshinene tshia mbuu tshiakatungunuka anu muvuatshi tshienda tshinyunguluka kukadi bidimu binunu bitanu.





Ndekelu wa ndekelu

"NE NDI MUPANDUKE NYENYI BUA KUKUAMBILA" Yobo.

Drama mmujika. Bua tshinyi muntu udi ulua kumpala?—Bualu umue wakapanduka ku dibutuka dia mazuwa.

Bivua bienzeka ku mpukapuka, kunyima kua dijimina dia Parsee, meme ke uvua Fates muteke bua kuangata muaba wa muena mvita wa Ahaba, pavua muena mvita au muangate mudimu uvua kauyi ne muntu; umue umue au, uvua, pavua bantu basatu aba bapatuke mu buatu buvua bunyunguluka dituku dia ndekelu, uvua mupueka ku nyima. Nunku, mutangile ku muelelu kua tshikena kumona tshivua tshilonda, ne mu tshimuenekelu tshijima, pavua tshitupa tshia dipuekesha dia mazuwa avua majimine tshifika kundi, mvua dîba adi, kadi bitekete bitekete, mukokibue kudi tshipepele tshivua tshijika. Pamvua mufikamu, uvua mupuekele mu tshina tshia mâyi tshia creme. Tshinyunguluke ne tshijengu, pashishe, ne tshienda tshinyunguluka tshitangile ku tshipepele tshifiike tshidi tshifuanangane ne buto ku tshijengu atshi tshidi tshienda tshinyunguluka bitekete bitekete, bu Ixion mukuabu umvua muenze bua kunyunguluka. Too ne, pa kupeta muaba wa mushinga mukole au, tshipepele tshifiike tshidi tshiya mulu tshipatuka; ne mpindieu, mupikudibue ku diambuluisha dia tshina tshiawu tshia budimu, ne, bua bukole buende bua bungi, udi ubanda ne bukole bua bungi, tshibombi tshia muoyo tshia mu sanduku tshiakapatuka mu bule bua mbuu, tshiakuluka, ne tshiakalenga ku luseke luanyi. Meme mukolesha kudi sanduku au, munkatshi mua dituku dijima ne butuku bujima, ngakaenda pa mâyi manene avua matekete ne a madilu. Ba requin badi kabayi benza bibi, bavua bapita anu bu bavua ne bikunyibua ku mishiku yabu; nyunyi wa mbuu wa tshisuku uvua wenda ne mishiku mibuikila. Dituku dibidi, buatu buakasemena pabuipi, pabuipi, ne buakangata ndekelu wa bionso. Rachel uvua wenda mu njila wa budimu, uvua mu dikeba diende dia bana bende bavua bajimine, mupeta anu muana mukuabu wa nshiya.
