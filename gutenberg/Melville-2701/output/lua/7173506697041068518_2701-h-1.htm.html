Paudi ubuela mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi wa Spouter-Inn, udi udimona mu tshibuelelu tshinene, tshipuekele, tshidi ne bimanu bia kale, bivuluija tshimue tshia ku bimanu bia mazuwa a kale adibu bashipe. Kadi kuvuaku mushindu kampanda wa bunene budi kabuyi ne ndekelu, budi kabuyi bufika ku tshitupa, budi kabuyi mua kuela meji bua bualu ebu buvua bukuenzeje bua kubumona, too ne paudi muditshipe wewe nkayebe bua kumanya tshidi tshimfuanyi tshilenga atshi tshiumvuija. Misangu yonso lungenyi luimpe, kadi, diakabi, lua dishima luvua mua kukubueja.—Mbuu Mufiike mu tshipepele tshikole tshia munkatshi mua butuku.—Mmvita ya pa buayi ya bintu binayi bia kumpala.—Mmuaba wa tshisuku uvua mubutuke.—Mmuaba wa mu muvu wa mashika wa mu Hyperbore.—Mditapuluka dia musulu wa neje wa Time . Ino ku mfulo, bintu byonso ebi byafumikile ku kintu kimo kikatampe kyadi munkatshi mwa kifwatulo. Bionso ebi biakamueneka, ne bionso bikuabu bivua bimueneka patoke. Fuanyikijabi mpindieu, udi mua kunkuata pa dîba dia midi—mmunyi mundi mua kumanya ne: uvua ufumina mu tshina tshibi kayi?

"Muena nzubu! Ndi mushintulule lungenyi luanyi bua muena tshingoma au.—Tshiena mua kulala nende to. Nsa kuteta tshibasa apa."

"Anu bu muudi musue; mfuila koku luse tshiena mua kunulekela tshilamba tshia ku mesa bua matela, ne bidi anu bu tshilamba tshikole apa"—diumvua dia tusoso ne tusoso. "Kadi bindila kakese, Skrimshander; Ndi ne ndeke wa muena mabaya mu bar—indila, ndi ngamba, ne nenkuvuije bimpe." Pavuaye wamba nunku, wakasumba ndeke; ne ne tshilamba tshienda tshia kale tshia soie uvua ubanga kuela lupuishi pa tshibasa, ne bukole buonso bua kuendakana ku bulalu buanyi, eku wenda useka bu nyama wa ntambue. Bipese bivua bibuuka ku dia balume ne ku dia bakaji; too ne ku ndekelu tshiamu tshia ndeke tshiakalua kututa ku tshingoma tshidi katshiyi mua kubutuka. Muena nzubu uvua pabuipi ne kutshibula tshianza, ne ngakamuambila bua diakalenga dia diulu bua kulekela—bulalu buvua butekete bikumbane bua kunkumbanyina, ne tshivua mumanye muvua malu onso a pa buloba mua kuenza eider mupuekele ku mutshi wa pine to. Nunku wakasangisha bipese bia nsuki ne muoyo mujima, ne kubiela mu tshikuku tshinene tshivua munkatshi mua nzubu, wakaya ku midimu yende, ne kunshiya mu tshitanda tshia kulonga tshikunze.

Mpindieu ngakangata tshipiminu tshia tshibasa, meme kumona ne: tshivua tshitupa tshipi tshia dikasa dimue; kadi bidi mua kulongolola ne nkuasa. Ino yadi mikatampe, kadi tshibasa tshikuabu mu kazubu tshivua bule bua santimetre inayi kupita tshivuabu balongolole—nanku kakuvua mushindu wa kubasuika to. Pashishe ngakateka tshibasa tshia kumpala mu bule pa muaba umuepele uvua mutoke ku tshimanu, meme kushiya tshitupa tshîpi pankatshi, bua mukolo wanyi kusombamu. Kadi mutantshi mukese ngakamona ne: kuvua lupepele lua mashika luvua lulua pa mutu panyi lufumina muinshi mua dididishi, ne plan eu kavua mua kuenza nansha kakese, nangananga bualu luya lukuabu lua ku tshiibi tshivua tshinyanguke luakapetangana ne luvua lufuma ku dididishi, ne lupepele luonso luvua luenda pamue mu tshitupa tshîpi tshia lukasa pabuipi ne muaba umvua ngela meji bua kulala butuku abu.

Diabolo udi mua kuangata muena tshingoma au, meme kuela meji, kadi imana, tshivuaku mua kuiba tshiibi tshiende munda, ne kutuluka mu bulalu buende, bua kubenga kujuka ku dikokola dia tshikisu tshikole anyi? Kabivua bimueneka bu lungenyi lubi to; kadi kunyima kua meji abidi ngakabenga. Bualu nnganyi uvua mua kuamba anu tshinyi mu dinda diakalonda, diakamue pangakapatuka mu nzubu, muena tshingoma uvua mua kuikala muimane ku tshibuelelu, mudiakaje bua kunkuma panshi!

Nansha nanku, pamvua mutangile kabidi, ne mumone ne: kakuvua mushindu wa kupitshisha butuku bua dikenga to, anu mu bulalu bua muntu mukuabu, ngakatuadija kuela meji ne: kunyima kua bionso mvua mua kuikala ne lungenyi lua kansungansunga ludi kaluyi luakanyine kudi muena mvita eu uvua kayi mumanyike. Ndi ngela meji ne, nenindile tshitupa tshîpi; udi ne tshia kuikala ubuela mu katupa kîpi emu. Nenmutangile bimpa dîba adi, ne pamuapa tudi mua kulua balunda bimpe ba kulala nende kunyima kua bionso—kakuena mushindu wa kuamba to.

Kadi nansha muvua bena nzubu bakuabu batungunuka ne kubuela umue, babidi, ne basatu, ne baya kulala, nansha nanku kakuvua tshimanyinu tshia muena tshingoma tshianyi to.

"Muena nzubu!" Ngakamba ne, "udi muntu wa mushindu kayi—utu anu wenza mêba a bungi nunku anyi?" Bivua bikole pa dîba dia dikumi ne dibidi.

Muena nzubu wakaseka kabidi ne tuseku tuende tua tshianana, ne uvua umueneka bu uvua museke bikole bua bualu kampanda buvua kabuyi bumvua. "Tòo," yeye kuandamuna, "pa tshibidilu utu ulua kumpala kua dîba—airley uya ku bulalu ne airley bua kujuka—eyowa, yeye ke nyunyi udi ukuata nsese. Kadi butuku ebu mmupatuke bua kusumba bintu, udi mumone, ne tshiena mmona tshidi tshimupangisha bua kufika panyima pa dîba nunku to, anu padiye kayi mua kusumbisha mutu wende."

"Kakuena mua kusumbisha mutu wende anyi?—Mmuyuki kayi wa tshinyangu uudi ungambila eu?" kubuela mu tshiji tshikole. "Udi wenza bu udi wamba, muena nzubu, ne muena tshingoma eu udi bushuwa wenza mudimu mu Disambombo edi dia dibenesha, anyi mu Dia lumingu mu dinda, mu diendakana ne mutu wende mu tshimenga etshi anyi?"

"Ke tshidibi menemene," ke muakamba muena nzubu, "ne ngakamuambila ne kena mua kutshisumbisha apa to, tshisalu tshidi ne bintu bia bungi."

"Ne tshinyi?" ngakela mbila.

“Ne mitu bulelela, kakuena mitu ya bungi pa buloba anyi?”

"Ndi nkuambila tshidibi, muena nzubu," ngakamba ne bupole, "mbimpe uleke kunyungulula tshilamba atshi—tshiena muena tshisuku to."

"Pamuapa ki mmuomu to," udi upatula mutshi ne utapa tshintu tshia kuela natshi menu, "kadi ndi ngela meji ne: neujikije tshiji pikala muena tshingoma au mumvue wewe ukupenda mutu wende."

"Nendimutshibule," ngakamba, mpindieu mubuela mu tshisumi kabidi bua bualu ebu budi kabuyi mua kuandamuna bua muena nzubu.

"Tshidi tshinyanguke," udi wamba.

"Mvua mutshibuke," ngakamba—" mutshibuka , udi musue kuamba anyi?"

"Sartain, ne ke bualu kayi kena mua kumupana, ndi ngela meji."

"Muena nzubu," ngakamba, meme kuya kudiye mu mashika bu mudi mukuna wa Hecla mu tshipepele tshikole tshia neje—"muena nzubu, lekela kuela mbila. Meme ne wewe tudi ne bua kumvuangana, ne bualu ebu kabidi kakuyi lutatu. Ndi mulue kumbelu kuebe ne ndi musue bulalu; udi ungambila ne: udi mua kumpesha anu tshitupa tshia bulalu; ne tshitupa tshikuabu ntshia muena tshingoma kampanda. Ne bua muntu eu, tshiena muanji kumona tshingoma etshi to, tshiena muanji kumona tshingoma to. mu kungambila miyuki ya dikema ne ya tshiji idi itamba kunsaka bua kuikala ne lungenyi lubi kudi muntu uudi mulongolole bua mulunda wanyi wa bulalu—mushindu kampanda wa malanda, muena nzubu, udi wa pa muoyo ne wa tshisokomenu mu mushindu wa pa mutu ndi nkulomba mpindieu bua kuakula ne kungambila ne tshidi muena mvita eu, ne ndi ne bua kuikala ne kanemu konso. yeye. Ne mu muaba wa kumpala, udi mua kuikala muimpe bua kubenga kuamba muyuki wa disumbisha dia mutu wende, udi bu bulelela ndi ngangata bu tshijadiki tshimpe tshia se: muena tshingoma eu mmutshimbakane bikole, ne tshiena mumanye mua kulala ne mutshimbakane to;

"Tshimanu," ke muakamba muena nzubu, wela mupuya mule, "eu i sarmon mule mule bua muntu udi utapa kakese misangu ne misangu. Kadi wikale mupepele, wikale mupepele, muena tshingoma eu undi nkuambila udi ufuma ku mbuu wa ku Sud, muaba uvuaye musumbe 'mitu ya bungi ya mu Nouvelle-Zélande milengeja (udi mumanye,'), kadi yeye mumane kusumba bionso umue, ne udiye ukeba kusumba butuku ebu, bualu bua dia lumingu dia makelela, ne kabiena mua kuikala bimpe bua kuikala usumbisha mitu ya bantu mu misesu padi bantu baya mu ekeleziya to. Uvua musue, mu dia lumingu dishala, kadi ngakamukandika anu pavuaye upatuka ku tshiibi ne mitu inayi misuika ku tshingoma, bua lupepele luonso bu lupepele.”

Muyuki eu wakajikija bualu busokoka buvua kabuyi mua kuandamuna, ne wakaleja ne: muena nzubu kavua ne lungenyi lua kunshima to—kadi musangu umue au, ntshinyi tshimvua mua kuela meji bua muena tshingoma uvua mushale pambelu pa butuku bua Disambombo too ne mu Nsabatu wa tshijila, wenza mudimu wa didia bantu bu mudi disumbisha dia mitu ya batendeledi ba mpingu bafue?

"Ikala wimanyina pa bualu ebu, muena nzubu, muena tshingoma au mmuntu wa njiwu."

"Udi ufuta pa tshibidilu," ke diandamuna. "Kadi luaku, dîba didi dienda dikola, mbimpe wikale wenza malu a mpukapuka—mbulalu buimpe; meme ne Sal tuvua balale mu bulalu abu butuku butuvua balale. Kudi muaba wa bungi wa bantu babidi mua kuendakana mu bulalu abu; mbulalu bunene bua bukole buonso. Bua tshinyi, kumpala kua tuetu kubulekela, Sal uvua ne tshibidilu tshia kuteka Sam ne John muana wetu mukese. kulota ne kutambakana butuku bumue, ne mu mushindu kampanda, Sam wakakuluka panshi, ne wakalua pabuipi ne kutshibula diboko diende Arter, Sal wakamba ne: kabivua mua kukumbana Luayi apa, nennupeshe tshimuenekelu mu tshitupa tshîpi to;” ne wamba nanku, wakatemesha muendu ne kuwukuata kundi, udifila bua kundombola njila. Kadi ngakashala tshiyi ne dipangadika; Pavuaye utangila dîba mu ditumba, wakamba ne: “Ndi ngela meji ne: ndi dia lumingu—kuena mua kumona muena tshingoma au butuku ebu to; mmulue bua kuimana muaba kampanda—luayi; luayi ; kanuena mua kulua anyi?”

Ngakela meji bua bualu ebu tshitupa tshîpi, ne pashishe tuetu kubanda pa tshibandilu, ne bakangela mu kazubu kakese, mashika bu tshisuku, ne muikale ne bintu bia bungi, bushuwa, ne bulalu bua dikema, bunene bua bushuwa bua muena mvita yonso inayi kulala pamue.

"Kuntu aku," ke muakamba muena nzubu, uteka muendu pa tshibombi tshia kale tshia mu mbuu tshivua tshienza mudimu wa tshitekelu tshia mâyi ne mesa a munkatshi; "Kuntu aku, ikala mupole mpindieu, ne butuku buimpe kunudi." Ngakakudimuka bua kutangila bulalu, kadi uvua mujimine.

Meme kupingaja tshilamba tshia ku mesa, meme kuinama pa bulalu. Nansha mudibi kabiyi bia mushinga mukole, biakatantamena dikonkonona bimpe menemene. Pashishe ngakatangila mu nzubu mujima; ne pa kumbusha bulalu ne mesa a munkatshi, kakuvua mua kumona bintu bikuabu bia muaba au to, anu tshitekelu tshia bintu tshia tshianana, bimanu binayi, ne tshitekelu tshia kapia tshienza ne mabeji tshidi tshileja muntu udi ukuma baleine. Ku bintu bivua kabiyi biakane bia mu nzubu, kuvua tshitanda tshikanga, ne tshiela panshi mu ditumba dimue; kabidi tshibombi tshinene tshia muendeshi wa mazuwa, tshivua ne tshibuta tshia muena mvita, kakuyi mpata pa muaba wa tshibombi tshia pa buloba. Bia muomumue, kuvua tshitupa tshia mifuba ya mishipa ya pa mutu pa tshitekelu tshia kapia, ne tshingoma tshile tshiimane ku mutu kua bulalu.

Kadi ntshinyi tshidi pa tshiadi etshi? Ngakatshiangata, meme kutshikuata pabuipi ne butoke, ne kutshiumvua, ne kutshinunkila, ne meme kuteta mishindu yonso bua kufika ku ndekelu muimpe pa bidi bitangila bualu ebu. Ndi mua kutshifuanyikija ne tshintu tshikuabu to, anu tshilamba tshinene tshia ku tshiibi, tshilengeja ku mpenga ne tumanyinu tukese tudi tuenza mutoyi bu mudi miosa ya ngulube milengeja idi minyunguluke tshilamba tshia mu Inde. Kuvua tshina anyi tshina munkatshi mua tshilamba etshi, anu muudi mua kumona mu bilamba bia ku Amerike wa ku Sud. Kadi bidi mua kuenzeka ne: muena tshingoma yonso udi kayi ne maluvu udi mua kubuela mu tshitanda tshia ku tshiibi, ne kuendakana mu misesu ya mu tshimenga tshionso tshia bena Kristo mu tshimuenekelu tshia mushindu eu anyi? Ngakatshivuala, bua kutshiteta, ne tshiakantonda bu tshibombi, tshikale ne nsuki mikole ne tshikole, ne ngakela meji ne: tshivua ne mâyi makese, bienze anu bu ne: muena tshingoma etshi uvua mutshivuale mu dituku dia mvula. Ngakabanda munda muawu too ne ku tshitupa tshia dikalashi tshilamakaja ku tshimanu, ne tshivua muanji kumona bualu bua nunku mu nsombelu wanyi to. Ngakadipatula mu bualu ebu ne lukasa luonso, ke meme kudipesha tshinyongopelu mu nshingu.

Ngakasomba ku luseke lua bulalu, meme kutuadija kuela meji bua muena tshingoma eu uvua usumbisha mutu, ne tshilamba tshiende tshia ku tshiibi. Panyima pa meme mumane kuela meji ndambu ku luseke lua bulalu, ngakajuka ne meme kumbusha tshilamba tshianyi tshia nkima, ne pashishe meme kuimana munkatshi mua nzubu ngelangana meji. Pashishe ngakambula kazaku kanyi, ne meme kuela meji kakese mu maboko a kazaku kanyi. Ino pa kubanga kwivwanija mashika a bungi mpindieu, tshiyi muvuale tshitupa, ne mvuluka tshivua muena nzubu muambe bua muena tshingoma kavua mua kulua kumbelu butuku abu to, bualu buvua bumane kupita, tshivua kabidi muenze bualu nansha bumue to, kadi ngakapatuka mu bilamba bianyi bia pantalone ne mu bisabata bianyi, ne pashishe meme kujima butoke, meme kuditua mu bulalu, ne kudiela kalumbandi kudi diulu dia dilama.

Nansha ni bulalu abu buvua buwule tente ne mabele anyi bintu bivua binyanguke, kakuena mushindu wa kuamba to, kadi mvua ntantamena bikole, ne tshivua mua kulala mutantshi mule to. Ndekelu wa bionso ngakapueka mu tulu tukese, ne mvua pabuipi ne kuenza luendu luimpe mutangile ku buloba bua Nod, pangakumvua dikasa dikole mu tshibuelelu, ne meme kumona butoke buenda bubuela mu nzubu kumbukila muinshi mua tshiibi.

Mukalenge unsungile, ndi ngela meji, udi ne tshia kuikala muena tshingoma, muena mutu wa inferno. Kadi mvua mulale anu mupuwe, ne mvua mupangadije bua kubenga kuamba dîyi nansha dimue too ne pavuabu mua kungambila. Mukuate muendu ku tshianza tshimue, ne mutu wa muomumue wa mu Nouvelle-Zélande ku tshikuabu, muenyi wakabuela mu nzubu, ne kayi mutangile ku bulalu, wakateka muendu wende kule nanyi panshi mu ditumba dimue, ne pashishe kutuadijaye kuenza mudimu kule ne mionji mikanga ya tshibombi tshinene tshimvua muakule kumpala ne: tshivua mu nzubu. Mvua ne dijinga dikole dia kumona mpala wende, kadi wakawulama mutantshi mule pavuaye wenza mudimu wa diunzulula mukana mua tshibombi. Bualu ebu buakakumbaja, nansha nanku, wakakudimuka—pavua, diulu dimpe! bualu bua dikema! Mpala wa nunku! Tshivua tshia dikala difiike, dia violet, dia jaune, apa ne apu tshilamakaja ne bipese binene bivua bimueneka bu bifiike. Eyowa, anu mumvua ngela meji, udi muena bulalu mubi; uvua mu mvita, uvua mutshibuke bibi menemene, ne monayi, anu kudi munganga wa dipanda. Kadi mu tshikondo atshi wakakudimuna mpala wende mutangile ku butoke, meme kumona patoke ne: kabivua mua kuikala bipese bia tshilamba nansha tshimue, bipese abi bifiike bivua pa matama ende. Bavua mabala a mushindu kampanda anyi kansanga. Ku ntuadijilu tshivua mumanye tshia kuenza ne bualu ebu to; kadi mutantshi mukese lungenyi lua bulelela luakalua mu lungenyi luanyi. Ngakavuluka muyuki wa muntu mutoke—muena baleine pende—uvua, mukuluke munkatshi mua bantu bavua badia bantu, uvuabu bafunde tshimanyinu kudibu. Ngakafika ku diamba ne: muena tshingoma eu, mu ngendu yende ya kule, uvua ne bua kuikala mupete bualu bua muomumue. Ne ntshinyi, meme kuela meji, kunyima kua bionso! Bidi anu pambelu biende; muntu udi mua kuikala muena bululame mu mishindu yonso ya dikoba. Kadi pashishe, tshitudi mua kuenza ne dikoba diende didi kadiyi dia pa buloba, tshitupa atshi, ndi musue kuamba ne: tshidi tshilala tshinyunguluke, ne tshidi katshiyi tshienza mudimu bimpe ne tshitupa tshia tshimfuanyi. Bua kuamba bulelela, bidi mua kuikala anu tshilamba tshimpe tshia dilengeja dia mu matunga a mashika; kadi tshitu muanji kumvua mudi dîba dikole divuija muntu mutoke mubidi wa jaune wa violet to. Kadi, tshivua muanji kuya mu Mbuu wa ku Sud to; ne pamuapa dîba divuaku divua dienza malu a pa buawu aa pa dikoba. Mpindieu, pavua ngenyi yonso eyi ipita munda muanyi bu mupenyi, muena tshingoma eu kavua mummone nansha kakese to. Ino, kunyima kua lutatu lua kunzulula tshibombi tshienda, wakabanga kutshibula, ne diakamue wakapatula mushindu kampanda wa tomahawk, ne tshibombi tshia dikoba dia mbua tshivua ne nsuki. Wakateka bintu ebi pa tshibuta tshia kale tshivua munkatshi mua nzubu, pashishe wakangata mutu wa Nouvelle-Zélande—tshintu tshibi menemene—ne kutshibueja mu tshibombi. Yeye mpindieu wakambusha tshifulu tshienda—tshifulu tshipiatshipia tshia beaver—pangakafika pabuipi ne kuimba ne dikema dipiadipia. Kakuvua nsuki ku mutu kuende to—kakuyi nansha umue wa kuakula—kakuvua tshintu tshikuabu to, anu ka-tshitupa kakese ka ku mutu kavua kalengeja pa mpala wende. Mutu wandi mutoke wa violet uvua mpindieu utangila buloba bujima bu tshifu tshikole. Bu muenyi kayi muimane pankatshi panyi ne tshiibi, mvua mua kuikala mupatuke lukasa kupita mumvua muanji kupatuka mu tshiibi tshia dilolo.

Nansha muvuabi nanku, mvua ngela meji ne: mvua mua kupatuka ku dididishi, kadi bivua ku tshisasa tshibidi tshia panyima. Nki muena buôwa to, kadi tshindi mua kuenza bua muena tshinyangu eu udi unyanga mutu tshiakapita lungenyi luanyi. Dipanga kumanya didi difumina ku buôwa, ne bu mundi tshiyi ne buôwa ne mubungame bua muenyi, ndi ngamba ne: mvua mmutshina bikole anu bu ne: uvua diabolo nkayende uvua mubuele mu nzubu wanyi butuku. Mu bulelela, mvua mumutshina bikole, ke bualu kayi tshivua ne mushindu wa kuakula nende, ne kulomba diandamuna dimpe pa bidi bitangila tshivua tshimueneka tshiyi mua kumvuika munda muende to.

Mu tshikondo atshi, wakatungunuka ne mudimu wa divula bilamba, ne ndekelu wa bionso wakaleja tshiadi tshiende ne maboko ende. Bu mundi ne muoyo, bitupa biende ebi bivua bibuikila bivua bienza ne bipese bia muomumue ne bia mpala wende; nyima wandi, kabidi, uvua pa mutu pa miaba mifiike ya muomumue; uvua umueneka bu uvua mu Mvita ya Bidimu Makumi Asatu, ne uvua anu munyeme ne tshilamba tshia plaster tshivua tshilamakaja. Nansha nanku, maulu ende avua ne tshimanyinu, bienze anu bu ne: tshisumbu tshia tululu tua mubidi wa vert tshifiike tshivua tshienda tshibanda pa mitshi ya mitshi ya nkunde. Mpindieu bivua bimueneka patoke ne: uvua ne bua kuikala nyama wa luonji mubi anyi mukuabu uvuabu batume mu mazuwa a baleine mu Mbuu wa ku Sud, ne wakafika mu ditunga edi dia bena Kristo. Ngakateketa pamvua ngela meji bua bualu ebu. Musumbishi wa mitu pende—pamuapa mitu ya bana babu. Udi mua kuangata dijinga dianyi—diulu! Tangila tomahawk au!

Kadi kakuvua dîba dia kuteketa to, bualu mpindieu muena tshinyangu au wakenza bualu buvua bunkoke ntema yanyi bikole, ne bunjadikila ne: uvua ne bua kuikala bushuwa muena bisamba bia bende. Kuyaye ku grego wende mule, anyi wrapall, anyi dreadnaught, uvuaye mulengeje kumpala pa nkuasa, wakakeba mu mifuku, ne ndekelu wa bionso kupatulaye tshimfuanyi tshikese tshilema tshia dikema tshivua ne tshilamba tshia nsongo ku mukolo, ne tshivua menemene ne dikala dia muana wa mu Congo wa matuku asatu. Pamvua mvuluka mutu uvuabu balengeje, ku ntuadijilu mvua ngela meji ne: tshilamba etshi tshifiike tshivua muana mulelela uvuabu balame mu mushindu kampanda wa muomumue. Kadi bu mumvua mumone ne: kavua mutekete nansha kakese, ne uvua ukenkesha bimpe bu mutshi wa ébène mulengeja, ngakamona ne: kavua ne bua kuikala tshintu tshikuabu to, anu lupingu lua mutshi, luvua bushuwa lumueneka. Bua mpindieu muena tshinyangu udi uya ku tshikuku tshia kapia tshidi katshiyi ne tshintu, ne umbusha tshilamba tshia kapia tshidibu bafunde ne mabeji, uteka tshimfuanyi etshi tshikese tshidi ne mukolo, bu tshintu tshia tenpin, pankatshi pa biamu bia kapia. Bimanu bia tshiibi tshia kapia ne mabue onso avua munda avua ne lupuishi lua bungi, ke bualu kayi mvua ngela meji ne: muaba wa kapia eu uvua muenze tshitendelelu tshikese anyi nzubu wa tshitendelelu muimpe menemene bua lupingu luende lua mu Congo.

Mpindieu ngakatangila tshimfuanyi tshisokoka tshitupa, ndiumvua kadi tshiyi ne disulakana mu tshikondo atshi—bua kumona tshivua tshilonda. Tshia kumpala udi wangata bipese bia miosa bia bungi mu tshibombi tshienda tshia grego, ne ubiteka bimpe kumpala kua lupingu; pashishe wakateka tshitupa tshia biscuit pa mutu ne kuelaye lubimbi lua muendu, wakatemesha bitupa abi bua kulua kapia ka mulambu. Mu tshikondo etshi, kunyima kua dikuata dia lukasa mu kapia, ne dipatula dia lukasa dia minu yende (muvuaye umueneka bu uvua uyiosha bibi), ndekelu wa bionso wakafika ku dipatula biscuit; pashishe yeye kupuekesha kapia ne butu kakese, wakafila ne kanemu kudi muana mutoke mukese. Kadi diabolo mukese kavua umueneka musue biakudia bia mushindu eu to; kavua unyunguluka mishiku yende nansha kakese. Bionso ebi bia dikema bivua bienda pamue ne mutoyi wa mukana wa muena nzambi, uvua umueneka bu uvua usambila mu musambu anyi wimba misambu ya bampangano anyi mikuabu, mu tshikondo atshi mpala wende uvua wenda unyunguluka mu mushindu uvua kauyi wa pa tshibidilu to. Ndekelu wa bionso pakajimaye kapia, wakambula lupingu luonso kayi ne tshilele, ne wakaluela kabidi mu tshibombi tshiende tshia grego ne budimu buonso bu muvuaye muena manaya uvua wela nkutshi mufue mu tshibombi.

Bionso ebi bia dikema biakavudija kanyinganyinga kanyi, ne pamvua mumumone mpindieu uleja bimanyinu bikole bia dijikija dia midimu yende ya bungenda, ne ubuela mu bulalu nanyi, ngakela meji ne: tshikondo tshivua tshikumbane, mpindieu anyi nansha kakese, kumpala kua butoke kujima, bua kutshimuna tshishima tshimvua mubuikila mutantshi mule.

Kadi tshikondo tshimvua mupitshishe mu diela meji a tshimvua mua kuamba, tshivua tshia lufu. Wakambula tomahawk wende ku mesa, wakakonkonona mutu wende bua tshitupa tshîpi, ne pashishe wakamukuata ku butoke, ne mukana muende ku tshianza, wakapatula matutu manene a muishi wa makanya. Mu tshikondo tshiakalonda, butoke buakajima, ne muena tshisuku eu uvua udia bantu, tomahawk pankatshi pa meno ende, wakabuela mu bulalu nanyi. Ngakimba, tshivua mua kudikanda mpindieu to; ne kuela mbila ya tshimpitshimpi ya dikema wakatuadija kumvuila.

Meme kuamba bualu kampanda, tshiyi mumanye tshinyi, meme kumuela kule ne tshimanu, ne pashishe kumulomba, nansha yeye muikale nganyi anyi tshinyi, bua kuikalaye talalaa, ne meme kujuka ne kutemesha kabidi muendu. Kadi mandamuna ende a mu mishiku akansankisha diakamue bualu kavua mumvue bimpe diumvuija dianyi to.

"Nnganyi udi ukushima?"—wakamba ndekelu wa bionso—"kuena wakula, dam-me, ndi nkushipa." Ne kuamba nunku, tomahawk mutoke wakatuadija kunyunguluka mu mîdima.

"Muena nzubu, bua bualu bua Nzambi, Peter Coffin!" Ngakela mbila. "Muena nzubu! Tangila! Sanduku! Banjelu! Nsungile!"

"Akula-e! ngambila-ee nganyi-ee, anyi dam-me, ndi mushipe-e!" wakadila kabidi muntu uvua udia bantu, eku dikola diende dia tomahawk ditangalaja butu bua makanya a kapia pabuipi nanyi too ne pangakela meji ne: tshilamba tshianyi tshia bilamba tshivua mua kutema kapia. Kadi tuasakidila diulu, dîba adi muena nzubu wakabuela mu nzubu ne butoke mu tshianza, ne meme kutuluka pa bulalu meme kuya lubilu kudiye.

"Kutshinyi mpindieu," udi wamba, useka kabidi, "Queequeg apa kena mua kukuenzela nansha nsuki ya ku mutu bibi to."

Ngakela mbila ne: “Lekela kuseka, ne bua tshinyi kuvua mungambile ne: muena mvita au uvua mudia bantu?”

"Mvua ngela meji ne: nuvua bamanye;—tshivuaku mukuambile ne: uvua mupanyishi wa mitu mu tshimenga anyi?—kadi pingana kabidi ne nuye kulala. Queequeg, tangila apa—udi unsamba, ndi ngela—wewe muntu eu udi mukulale—wewe sabbee?"

"Meme nsambila bia bungi"—ke muvua Queequeg muele mbila, wela mupuya wende ne musombe pa bulalu.

"Udi mua kubuela," wakasakidila, unleja ne tomahawk wende, ne wela bilamba ku luseke lumue. Uvua bushuwa muenze bualu ebu ki nganu mu mushindu wa tshididi to, kadi mu mushindu wa bulenga ne wa luse. Ngakaimana mutangile tshitupa tshîpi. Bua bionso bivuaye muenze, uvua muntu mukezuke, muimpe kumona uvua udia bantu. Ntshinyi tshionso tshindi ngenza, ngakela meji munda muanyi—muntu eu udi muntu anu bu meme: udi ne bualu bua bungi bua kuntshina, anu bu mundi meme ne bua kumutshina. Mbimpe kulala ne muntu udi udia bantu udi kayi ne maluvu pamutu pa kulala ne muena Kristo mukuatshike maluvu.

"Muena nzubu," ngakamuambila, "muambila bua asokoke tomahawk wende, anyi pipe, anyi mushindu kayi wonso uudi umubikila; umuambile bua alekele kunua makanya, mu tshikoso, ne nendue nende. Kadi tshiena musue kuikala ne muntu udi unua makanya mu bulalu nanyi to. Bidi ne njiwu. Bualu bukuabu, tshiena ne tshishimbi to."

Pavuabu bambile Queequeg bualu ebu, wakatumikila diakamue, ne kungambilaye kabidi ne bupole buonso bua meme kulala—kudikunguluka ku luseke lumue bua kuamba ne: “Tshiakulenga dikasa diebe nansha dimue to.”

"Butuku buimpe, muena nzubu," meme kuamba, "udi mua kuya."

Ngakapingana, ne tshitu muanji kulala bimpe mu nsombelu wanyi to.
