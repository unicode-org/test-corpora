Ne tshiji tshikole, uvua mpindieu wenza mudimu ne sanduku wende wa lufu bu sanduku wa mbuu; ne wela mu tshibombi tshienda tshia bilamba, kubiteka mu bulongame. Mêba a bungi a dikisha avuaye upitshisha, mu disonga tshibuikidiji ne mishindu yonso ya bimfuanyi ne bimfuanyi bia dikema; ne bivua bimueneka ne: uvua udienzeja, mu mushindu wende wa tshikisu, bua kukopa bitupa bia tshimfuanyi tshinyanguke tshivua pa mubidi wende. Ne tshitampi etshi tshivua mudimu wa muprofete ne mumonyi wa malu uvua mufue wa mu tshidila tshiende, uvua, ku diambuluisha dia bimanyinu bia hieroglyphe abi, mufunde pa mubidi wende lungenyi lujima lua diulu ne buloba, ne mukanda wa malu masokoka uvua wakula bua dimanya dia kupeta bulelela; nunku Queequeg mu bumuntu buende uvua tshishima tshia kujingulula; mudimu wa dikema mu mukanda umue; kadi uvua nansha yeye nkayende kayi mua kubala malu ende masokoka, nansha muvua mutshima wende wa muoyo ututa; ne malu masokoka aa avua ne bua kujika ku ndekelu ne tshikandakanda tshia muoyo tshivuabu baafunde, ne nunku kaayi mua kujinguludibua too ne ku ndekelu. Ne lungenyi elu luvua ne bua kuikala luvua lufikishe Ahaba ku diela mbila yende ya tshikisu, pavuaye dimue dituku mu dinda ulekela kutangila Queequeg mupele—“Oh, dikokangana dia diabolo dia nzambi!”





SHAPITA 111. Mbuu wa Pasifike.

Patuvua tuenda tuendakana ku bidiila bia Bashee tuakafika ndekelu wa bionso ku Mbuu munene wa ku Sud; Bu kabiyi bua malu makuabu, mvua mua kuikala muele munanga wanyi wa Pacifique muoyo ne dianyisha dia bungi, bualu mpindieu disengelela dikole dia bunsonga buanyi diakaandamuna; mbuu wa ditalala uvua unyunguluka mutangile ku est kumbukila kuanyi mutantshi wa kilometre tshinunu ya bule.

Kudi, muntu kena mumanye bualu busokoka budi butangila mbuu eu, udi ne dikankamika dia bupole didi dimueneka bu diakula bua anyima kampanda musokome muinshi mua mbuu eu; bu mudi mavuala a mu mianu a buloba bua Efeso pa mutu pa Muambi wa lumu luimpe St. Yone uvuabu bajiike. Ne bidi bikumbane, bua se: pa mutu pa madimi a mu mbuu aa, pa madimi a mâyi manene ne pa madimi a ba-Potters a mu matunga onso anayi, mavuala adi ne bua kubanda ne kupueka, ne kupueka ne kupueka kayi ujika; bualu apa, miliyo ya bindidimbi ne bindidimbi bisambakaja, bilota bia mu mayi, somnambulisme, reveries; bionso bitudi tubikila ne mioyo ne anyima, bidi bilota, bilota, anu; badi batuta bu bantu badi balala tulu mu malalu abu; mavuala adi enda anyanguka kadi menza nunku kudi dipanga diawu dia dikisha.

Ku muena Maji yonso udi wela meji, Pacifique eu udi ne ditalala, udiye mumone musangu umue, udi ne bua kuikala anu mbuu wa dianyisha diende. Udi unyunguluka mu mayi a munkatshi mua buloba, mbuu wa Inde ne Atlantique bidi anu maboko ende. Mavuala a muomumue adi asukula bipese bia mu bimenga bipiabipia bia mu Californie, kadi malaba bivua bikunyibue kudi tshisamba tshia bantu tshia matuku mashale aa, ne asukula bilamba bivua binyanguke kadi bitshidi anu bilenga bia matunga a mu Asie, a kale kupita Abalahama; pabi bionso bidi pankatshi pa njila ya mabele ya bidiila bia mabue a koray, ne bidiila bia muinshi mua buloba bidi kabiyi ne ndekelu, bidi kabiyi bimanyike, ne Japon idi kayiyi mua kubuelamu. Nunku bualu ebu budi kabuyi bumanyike, bua mu Pasifike budi buenza buloba bujima; udi wenza miaba yonso ya ku muelelu wa mâyi umue; udi umueneka bu mutshima wa buloba udi unyunguluka. Mubandishibue kudi bipupu bia kashidi abi, udi ne tshia kuikala ne nzambi wa dishima, uinama mutu webe kudi Pan.

Ino meji makese a Pan akalenga lungenyi lua Ahaba, bu muvuaye muimane bu lupingu lua tshiamu pa muaba wende wa pa tshibidilu ku luseke lua tshiamu tshia mizen, ne diulu dimue uvua ufuisha musk wa sukadi wa mu bidiila bia Bashee kayi wela meji (mu mitshi yende mimpe ne: bananga ba bupole badi ne bua kuikala benda), ne ne lukuabu lukuabu uvua mupuya mupuya muimpe wa mukele wa mbuu mupiamupia; mbuu au mudi Baleine Mutoke udibu bakine ne bua kuikala nansha dîba adi wenda uowa mâyi. Pavuabu bamutume mu bule pa mayi aa pabuipi ne a ndekelu, ne wenda unyunguluka batangile ku tshipalu tshia mazuwa tshia bena Japon, tshipatshila tshia muntu mukulumpe etshi tshiakakola. Mishiku yende mikole yakapetangana bu mishiku ya tshinyangu; Delta wa mishipa ya mpala wende uvua muvula bu misulu miule tente; mu tulu tuende, mbila yende yakapita mu tshibumba tshia buatu ne: “Nuikale ne bukole buonso! Baleine Mutoke udi upatula mashi makole!”





SHAPITA 112. Muena tshiamu tshia mitaku.

Ku diambuluisha dia tshikondo tshia mashika tshia mu muvu wa été tshivua mpindieu tshikokesha mu latitude eyi, ne bua kudilongolola bua midimu ya pa buayi ivua ne bua kuenzeka mu katupa kîpi emu, Perth, muenji wa tshiamu tshia mitaku mukulakaje uvua ne tshiji ne uvua ne mputa, kavua mumbushe kabidi tshiamu tshiende tshia tshiamu tshia mitaku mu tshitudilu to, yeye mumane kujikija mudimu wende wa dikuatshisha bua dikasa dia Ahab, kadi uvua anu mutshilame ne lukasa udi muela ku tshingoma kudi tshiamu tshia kumpala; mpindieu badi bababikila misangu yonso kudi bamfumu, ne ba-harpooner, ne ba-bows bua kubenzela mudimu kampanda mukese; kushintulula, anyi kulongolola, anyi kulongolola bia mvita biabu bishilangane ne bintu bia mu buatu. Misangu ya bungi uvua munyunguluka kudi tshisumbu tshia bantu bavua ne dijinga dikole, bonso bindile bua kubambuluisha; mukuate bingoma bia buatu, mitu ya nyunyi, bingoma, ne mikete, ne utangila ne mukawu tshionso tshivuaye wenza, pavuaye wenza mudimu mukole. Nansha nanku, wa mununu eu uvua marto wa lutulu uvuabu bakuate kudi diboko dia lutulu. Kakuena dikokangana nansha dimue, kakuena dipanga lutulu, kakuena tshiji tshiakafuma kudiye to. Mupuwe, bitekete bitekete, ne mu budimu; wakainama kabidi pa mutu pa mukolo wende uvua mutshibuke, wakadienzeja bikole, bienze anu bu ne: mudimu mukole uvua muoyo nkayawu, ne dikuma dia marto wende dikole divua dikuma dikole dia mutshima wende. Ke muvuabi.—Bivua bibi menemene!

Dienda dia pa buadi dia mununu eu, dienda dia patupu kadi dia kanyinganyinga mu dienda diende, divua dijule dijinga dia kumanya dia malu a bena mazuwa ku ntuadijilu kua luendu. Ne ku dilomba dia nkonko yabu ivua mitungunuka, ndekelu wa bionso wakalekela; ne nunku biakalua ne: muntu yonso wakamanya muyuki wa bundu wa dikenga diende.

Kunyima, ne ki nkubi to, mu muvu wa mashika, mu njila uvua wenda pankatshi pa bimenga bibidi bia mu mpata, muena tshiamu tshia mitaku wakamvua bupote bua lufu buenda bumukuata, ne wakakeba tshisokomenu mu tshikumbi tshivua tshinyanguke ne tshinyanguke. Tshilumbu tshivua, dijimija dia mishiku ya makasa onso abidi. Mu dibuluibua edi, tshitupa ku tshitupa, ndekelu wa bionso biakapatuka bienzedi binayi bia disanka, ne tshienzedi tshitanu tshile, ne katshiyi tshinyanguke tshia kanyinganyinga ka drama wa muoyo wende.

Uvua muntu mukulakaje, uvua, ne bidimu bitue ku makumi asambombo, mupetangane ne tshintu atshi mu malu a kanyinganyinga adibu babikila ne: dibutuka. Uvua muena mudimu wa bianza wa lumu lua bungi, ne uvua ne bia bungi bia kuenza; uvua ne nzubu ne budimi; wakakidila mukaji wa nsonga, udi bu muana wa bakaji, wa dinanga, ne bana basatu ba disanka, ba mubidi mutoke; Dia lumingu dionso uvua uya mu ekeleziya uvua umueneka muimpe, mukuna mu tshisuku. Ino butuku bumo, muinshi mua mîdima, ne musokome kabidi mu mushindu wa budimu, muivi uvua mutekete mu mikolo wakabuela mu nzubu muende wa disanka, ne kubanyenga bintu bionso. Ne bualu bua dikema budi ne: muenji wa tshiamu tshia mitaku nkayende wakabueja muivi eu mu mutshima wa dîku diende mu dipanga dimanya. Uvua muena tshinyangu wa mulondo! Pa diunzulula dia tshibuikidiji tshia lufu, tshipupu tshiakapatuka, ne tshiakanyanguka nzubu wende. Mpindieu, bua malu a budimu, a meji a bungi ne a mpetu, nzubu wa mudimu wa tshiamu tshia mitaku uvua mu nzubu wende wa muinshi mua buloba, kadi ne tshibuelelu tshikuabu; nunku misangu yonso mukaji wa nsongakaji ne wa dinanga uvua ne makanda a mubidi uvua uteleja kayi ne buôwa, kadi ne disanka dia bungi, ku dikubakuba dikole dia marto wa bayende mukulakaje uvua ne maboko a nsonga; uvua mutoyi wende, mupuekeshibue pa kupitshila mu bimanu ne panshi, uvua mulue kudiye, ki mmubi to, mu nzubu wende wa bana; ne nunku, bua kukolesha musambu wa Labor wa tshiamu tshia mitaku, bana ba muenji wa tshiamu tshia mitaku bavua banyunguluka bua kulala tulu.

Oh, diakabi pa diakabi! Oh, Lufu, bua tshinyi kuena mua kuikala misangu mikuabu pa dîba? Bu wewe muangate muena tshiamu tshia mitaku eu kudi wewe nkayebe kumpala kua dibutuka diende dijima kulua kudiye, dîba adi mukaji mukamba nsongakaji uvua mua kuikala ne kanyinganyinga kakole, ne bana bende ba nshiya bavua mua kuikala tatu wa kanemu wa bushuwa, wa lumu wa kulota mu bidimu biabu bia panyima; ne buonso buabu badi ne dimanya dia kushipa bantu. Ino Lufu luakakosa muanetu wa balume mukuabu wa bulenga, uvua mudimu wende wa ku dituku ne ku dituku uvua anu ne majitu a dîku dikuabu, ne wakashiya muntu mununu mubi menemene muimane, too ne pavua dibola dibi dia muoyo mua kumuvuija bipepele bua kumunowa.

Bua tshinyi kuamba bionso? Bikuma bia marto wa muinshi mua buloba dituku dionso bivua bienda bivula pankatshi pa; ne tshipupu tshionso dituku dionso tshivua tshienda tshikepela kupita tshia ndekelu; mukaji uvua musombe mupuwe ku dididishi, ne mêsu kaayi ne binsonji, utangila mpala ya bana bende bavua badila; tshiombelu tshiakakuluka; tshiamu tshia mitaku tshivua tshiuwule tente ne mabue; nzubu uvua mupane; mamu wakaditua panshi mu bisonsa bile bia mu lupangu lua ekeleziya; bana bende bakamulonda misangu ibidi kuntu aku; ne muntu mukulakaje uvua kayi ne nzubu, kayi ne dîku uvua wenda unyunguluka mu tshilamba tshia crape; dikenga diende dionso kadiyi ne kanemu; mutu wandi mutoke udi upepeja nsuki mikunzubile!

Lufu ludi lumueneka bu tshintu tshimuepele tshidi tshilonda tshia mudimu bu eu; kadi lufu i anu dibuela mu tshitupa tshia tshidi katshiyi tshiteta tshia dikema; i anu dianyisha dia kumpala dia malu adi mua kuenzeka mu tshitupa tshinene tshia kule, tshia muitu, tshia mâyi, tshia tshianana; nunku, ku mêsu a bantu ba mushindu eu badi ne dijinga dia lufu, badi batshidi bashiye munda muabu amue malu adi kaayi makane bua kudishipa, mbuu udi ne bintu bionso ne udi wangata bintu bionso udi utangalaja mu mushindu udi ukoka tshibandabanda tshiende tshijima tshia malu adi kaayi mua kuela meji, a buôwa, ne a dikema, a nsombelu mupiamupia; ne mu mitshima ya Pasifike idi kayiyi ne ndekelu, bana ba mu mâyi tshinunu badi babimbila—“Luayi apa, ne mitshima mitshioke; monayi muoyo mukuabu udi kayi ne tshibawu tshia lufu lua pankatshi; monayi malu a kukema adi kaayi a ku tshilelelu, kuyi ufua bua bualu buawu. Luayi apa! udijike mu nsombelu udi, kudi wewe mpindieu udi mukine ne mukine, udi ne dianyisha dia bungi kupita lufu! dibue diebe dia lukita, kabidi, munda mua lubanza lua ekeleziya, ne ulue apa, too ne patudi tukusela!”

Kuteleja mêyi aa, ku Est ne ku Ouest, ku dipatuka dia dîba dia kumpala, ne ku dikuluka dia eve, anyima wa muena tshiamu tshia mitaku wakandamuna ne, Eyowa, ndi mulue! Nunku Perth wakaya kuloba baleine.





SHAPITA 113. Tshiamu tshia nzembu.

Ne ndevu mikole, ne tshilamba tshia dikoba dia requin, pabuipi ne munkatshi mua dituku, Perth uvua muimane pankatshi pa tshiamu tshienda tshia mitaku ne tshingoma, uvua muteka pa mutshi wa tshiamu tshia mitaku, ne tshianza tshimue tshikuate mutu wa nkutshi mu makala, ne tshikuabu ku mapapa a tshiamu tshiende tshia mitaku, pavua Kapitene Ahab mulue, muambule tshibombi tshiende tshikese tshia dikoba dia tshiamu tshia mitaku mu tshianza tshiende. Patshivuaye mutantshi mukese ne nzubu wa tshiamu tshia mitaku, Ahaba uvua ne tshiji wakaimana; too ne ku ndekelu, Perth, mupatule tshiamu tshienda mu kapia, kutuadijaye kutshikuma pa tshiamu tshia mitaku—tshintu tshikunze tshivua tshituma kapia mu bipepele bikole, bimue bia kudibi bivua bienda pabuipi ne Ahaba.

"Aa i nkutshi ya mamuebe Carey, Perth? idi misangu yonso ipueka mu tshiadi tshiebe; nyunyi ya tshimanyinu tshimpe, kabidi, kadi ki mbua bonso to;—tangila apa, idi yosha; kadi wewe—udi musombele munkatshi muayi kuyi ne kapia."

"Bualu ndi muosha mubidi wonso, Kapitene Ahaba," Perth wakandamuna, wikisha tshitupa tshîpi pa marto wende; “Kuena mua kuosha mputa bipepele to.”

"Eyowa, eyowa; kakuyi kabidi. Dîyi diebe ditekete didi diumvuika ditalala, dia kanyinganyinga kakole kundi. Mu Mparadizu nansha umue, tshiena ne lutulu lua makenga onso adi mu bakuabu adi kaayi mutshimbakane. Udi ne bua kulua mutshimbakane, muenji wa tshiamu tshia mitaku; wamba ne, bua tshinyi kuena mutshimbakane? Mmunyi muudi mua kutantamena kuyi mutshimbakane, udi ne tshinunu tshia mu diulu? kuena mua kuikala mutshimbakane anyi?—Uvua wenza tshinyi kuntu aku?”

"Tudi tuela mutu wa nkutshi wa kale, mfumu; uvua ne milonda ne milonda munda muawu."

"Ne kuenaku mua kuenza bua bionso bikale bimpe kabidi, muena tshiamu tshia mitaku, kunyima kua mudimu mukole bu muvuabi anyi?"

"Ndi ngela meji nanku, mfumu."

“Ne ndi ngela meji ne: kuena mua kulengeja milonda yonso ne milonda yonso to; nansha mudi tshiamu tshikole, muenji wa tshiamu tshia mitaku?”

"Eyowa, mfumu, ndi ngela meji ne ndi mua kuenza nanku; milonda yonso ne milonda anu umue."

"Tangilayi apa," ke muvua Ahaba wela lubila, wenda uya kumpala ne tshisumi, ne muimanyine ne bianza bionso bibidi pa makaya a Perth; "Tangila apa— apa —udi mua kulengeja tshilamba tshia mushindu eu, muena tshiamu tshia mitaku," wela tshianza tshimue pa mpala wende udi ne mifuba; "Wewe kuyi mua kuenza nanku, muenji wa tshiamu tshia mitaku, ndi ne disanka dia bungi bua meme kuteka mutu wanyi pa tshingoma tshiebe, ne kumvua marto webe mule menemene pankatshi pa mêsu anyi. Diandamuna! Kuenaku mua kulengeja tshilamba etshi anyi?"

"Oh! ke yeye, mfumu! Tshivua muambe ne: tshivua ne milonda yonso ne milonda to, anu umue anyi?"

"Eyowa, muenji wa tshiamu tshia mitaku, udi umue; eyowa, muntu, udi kayi mua kulengejibua; bualu nansha muudi mua kumumona anu mu mubidi wanyi, mmuenze mudimu too ne mu mufuba wa mutu wanyi— adi anu milonda! Kadi, kule ne dinaya dia bana; kakuena kabidi bipese ne bipese lelu. Tangila apa!" udi ulenga tshibombi tshia tshiseba, bienze anu bu ne: tshivua tshiuwule tente ne makuta a or. "Meme panyi ndi musue kuenza tshingoma tshia tshingoma; tshidi tshikokedi tshinunu tshia bademon katshiyi mua kutapulula, Perth; tshintu tshidi mua kulamakaja mu baleine bu mufuba wende. Kudi bintu," wela tshibombi pa tshingoma. "Tangila, muenji wa tshiamu tshia mitaku, ebi ke bipese bia misonso bia tshiamu tshia mitaku bia tubalu tua lubilu."

"Bintu bia ku makasa a kabalu, mfumu? Bua tshinyi, Kapitene Ahaba, udi ne bintu bitambe buimpe ne bia mutu mukole bitudi tuetu bena tshiamu tshia mitaku tuenza."

"Ndi mumanye, muntu mukulakaje; bisonsa ebi nebikuate pamue bu dilamakaja dia mifuba ya bashipianganyi. Lua lukasa, unfumbile tshingoma. Ungenzele kumpala mionji dikumi ne ibidi bua dikoba diatshi; pashishe unyungulule, ne unyungulule, ne unyungulule ne marto pamue bu mionji ne mionji ya tshiamu tshia kukoka natshi. Lua lukasa! Nentute kapia."

Ndekelu wa bionso pavuabu benze mionji dikumi ne ibidi, Ahaba wakayiteta, umue ku umue, pa kuyinyungulula, ne tshianza tshiende nkayende, ku tshingoma tshile tshia tshiamu tshikole. "Tshilema!" kubenga tshia ndekelu. "Wenza kabidi bualu ebu, Perth."

Panyima pa bualu ebu, Perth uvua pabuipi ne kutuadija kusuika bintu dikumi ne bibidi mu tshintu tshimue, pavua Ahaba mulekele tshianza tshiende, ne muambe ne: uvua ne bua kusuika tshiamu tshiende nkayende. Pavuaye, ne bikono bia pa tshibidilu, bivua bipuyakana, uvua ukuma marto pa tshingoma, Perth umupesha mionji ivua ikenka, umue kunyima kua mukuabu, ne tshiamu tshia mitaku tshikole tshivua tshituta lubimbi lukole lululame, muena Parsee wakapita mupuwe, ne kuinamaye mutu wende mutangile ku kapia, uvua umueneka bu uvua ulomba mulawu kampanda anyi dibenesha kampanda pa kapia. Kadi, pavua Ahaba utangila mulu, wakasesuka.

"Bua tshinyi tshisumbu tshia lucifer tshidi tshinyema kuntu aku?" Stubb unena, utangila ku tshiibi tshia kumpala. “Muena Parsee au udi ununka kapia bu kapia, ne yeye nkayende udi ununka bu tshilongo tshia lupuishi lua muskete.”

Ndekelu wa bionso, mutshi wa tshikokedi, mu mutshi umue mujima, wakapeta kapia kawu ka ndekelu; ne pavua Perth, bua kutshitekesha, muele bionso mu tshibombi tshia mâyi tshivua pabuipi apu, luya lua kapia luakabanda mu mpala wa Ahaba uvua muinama.

"Udiku mua kungambila anyi, Perth?" kuela mbila bua tshitupa tshîpi ne kanyinganyinga; "Ndi anu mufuka tshiamu tshianyi tshia tshimanyishilu anyi?"

"Sambila Nzambi, ki mmuomu to; kadi ndi ne buôwa bua bualu kampanda, Kapitene Ahaba. Tshingoma etshi ki ntshia Baleine Mutoke anyi?"

"Bua nyunyi mutoke! Kadi mpindieu bua mionji; udi ne bua kuyienza wewe nkayebe, muntu. Tangila mishiku yanyi—ya tshiamu tshitambe buimpe; apa, ne wenze mionji mikole bu mvula wa mabue a mashika."

Bua tshitupa tshîpi, muena tshiamu tshia mitaku mukulakaje wakatangila biamu bia kukosa nabi mishiku bienze anu bu uvua musue kubenga kubienza nabi mudimu.

“Bangate, muntu, tshiena nabu dijinga to, bualu mpindieu tshiena nkosa mishiku, tshiena nsambila too ne ku mudimu!”

Ndekelu wa bionso muenza bu muele wa mvita, ne muela kudi Perth ku tshiadi, tshiamu tshia mitaku tshiakafika ku ndekelu kua tshiamu; ne pavua muenji wa tshiamu tshia mitaku ukeba kufila kapia ka ndekelu kudi mionji, kumpala kua kuyilengeja, wakadila kudi Ahaba bua kuteka tshibombi tshia mâyi pabuipi.

"Tòo, tòo—kakuyi mayi bua bualu abu; ndi musue bua tshiji tshia lufu tshilelela. Ahoy, kuntu aku! Tashtego, Queequeg, Daggoo! Nudi nuamba tshinyi, bampangano! Nudi mua kumpesha mashi bungi budi mua kubuikila tshitupa etshi anyi?" kutshikuata muulu. Tshisumbu tshia bantu bafiike bakandamuna ne, Eyowa. Bavua benze bipendu bisatu mu mubidi wa bampangano, ne pashishe bavua balengeja mishiku ya baleine mutoke.

"Ego non baptizo te in nomine patris, sed in nomine diaboli!" wakadila bikole Ahaba, bu muvua tshiamu tshibi tshiosha mashi a batismo.

Mpindieu, yeye muangate mitshi ya pa mutu idi kuinshi, ne musungula umue wa mutshi wa hickory, ne dikoba ditshidi anu dimuteka, Ahaba wakateka ndekelu ku tshitekelu tshia tshiamu. Tshibombi tshipiatshipia tshia tshiamu tshia kukoka natshi tshiakabuluibua, ne ndambu wa tshiamu atshi tshiakabuela mu tshiamu tshia mashinyi, ne kutshiolola too ne ku dikokangana dikole. Wakafina dikasa diende patshi, too ne pavua tshingoma tshiela mbila bu tshiombelu tshia lunzenze, pashishe kuinamaye ne disanka dionso, ne kumonaye ne: kakuvua tshintu tshivua tshikuate, Ahaba wakamba ne: “Mbimpe!

Ku muelelu umua tshingoma katshivua tshisuika, ne mionji ivua mitangalake ivua yonso miluka ne miluka ku tshitekelu tshia tshingoma; mutshi uvua pashishe mubandishibue bikole mu tshina; ku ndekelu kua kuinshi, tshingoma tshivua tshilonda tshitupa tshia bule bua mutshi, ne tshilamakaja bikole nunku, ne mionji ya nshinga. Kunyima kua bualu ebu, mutshi, tshiamu, ne tshingoma—bu mudi malu asatu—biakashala kabiyi mua kutapuluka, ne Ahaba wakaya ne tshingoma; mutoyi wa dikasa diende dia meno a ntambue, ne mutoyi wa mutshi wa hickory, bionso bibidi bivua bienza mutoyi pa dibaya dionso. Ino kumeso kwa yeye kubuela mu kazubu kende, mutoyi mupepele, kauyi wa ku tshilelelu, wa tshitupa tshia tuseku, kadi mutambe bubi uvua mumvuika. Oh, Pip! tuseku tuebe tua kanyinganyinga, dîsu diebe dia tshianana kadi kadiyi dikisha; mummeries yebe yonso ya dikema kayiyi misambakane kakuyi diumvuija ne dikenga difiike dia mazuwa a kanyinganyinga, ne wakaseka!
