SHAPITA 74. Mutu wa baleine wa mabele—Mushindu mushilangane.

Mona, mpindieu, kudi baleine minene ibidi, miteka mitu yayi pamue; tubasangishe, ne tusangishe bietu pamue.

Mu tshisumbu tshinene tshia folio leviathans, baleine wa sperme ne baleine wa ku dia balume ke badi batamba kumanyika. Bobu ke baleine nkayabu badi bantu balonda pa tshibidilu. Ku bena Nantucketer, badi baleja mishindu ibidi ya mishindu yonso ya baleine. Bu mudi dishilangana dia pambelu pankatshi pabu dimueneka nangananga mu mitu yabu; ne bu mutu wa muntu ne muntu udi tshikondo etshi tshilenga ku luseke lua Pequod; ne bu mutudi mua kuya mu budishikaminyi kumbukila ku umue kuya ku mukuabu, anu pa kusabuka tshibandilu:—ndi musue kumanya penyi, udi mua kupeta mushindu muimpe wa kulonga cetology wa mudimu kupita apa?

Kumpala, udi ukema bua dishilangana didi pankatshi pa mitu eyi. Bionso bibidi bidi bikumbane mu kondo ka muoyo konso; kadi kudi diumvuangana kampanda dia makumi mu baleine wa sperm udi baleine wa ku dia balume kayi nawu. Kudi ngikadilu ya bungi mu mutu wa baleine wa mabele. Paudi umona bualu ebu, udi ufila bumfumu bua bungi kudiye ku budisuile, mu mushindu wa kanemu ka bungi. Mu tshikondo etshi, kabidi, bunême ebu budi buvula kudi dikala dia pepper ne dia mukele dia mutu wende ku mutu kua tshibandabanda, tshifila tshimanyinu tshia bukulakaje ne dimanya dia bungi. Mu tshikoso, yeye ke tshidi balobi babikila ne: "baleine wa mutu mutoke."

Tumonayi mpindieu tshidi katshiyi tshifuanangane mu mitu eyi, mbuena kuamba ne: bitupa bibidi bia mushinga mukole, dîsu ne ditshi. Kunyima ku luseke lua mutu, ne panshi, pabuipi ne tshitupa tshia mpala wa baleine, wewe mukebe bimpe, ndekelu wa bionso neumone dîsu didi kadiyi ne mishiku, diudi mua kuela meji ne: ndisu dia muana wa mpunda; nunku udi pa mutu pa bunene bua mutu.

Mpindieu, ku muaba wa pa buende wa mêsu a baleine, bidi bimueneka patoke ne: kena mua kumona tshintu tshidi kumpala menemene to, anu mudiye mua kumona tshidi panyima menemene. Mu kuamba kuimpe, muaba udi mêsu a baleine udi upetangana ne udi matshi a muntu; ne udi mua kuela meji, buebe wewe nkayebe, mushindu kayi uvuabi mua kukuenzekela, wewe muele bintu ku luseke ku matshi ebe. Udi mua kumona ne udi mua kukokesha anu bipimu makumi asatu bia tshimuenenu kumpala kua mulongo mululame wa tshimuenenu; ne bakuabu batue ku makumi asatu panyima pende. Bu muena lukuna webe mutambe bukole wenda ulua buludiludi kuudi, ne tshingoma tshibandisha mu munya, kuena mua kumumona to, anu bu yeye muikale ukuiba kunyima. Mu kuamba kuimpe, udi mua kuikala ne mikolo ibidi, mu mushindu kampanda; kadi, musangu umue, kabidi, mpala ibidi (mpala ya ku luseke): bualu ntshinyi tshidi tshienza mpala wa muntu—tshinyi, bulelela, pa kumbusha mêsu ende?

Kabidi, padi mu nyama mikuabu ya bungi indi mua kuela meji mpindieu, mêsu adi mateka mu mushindu udi kauyi umueneka bua kusangisha bukole buawu bua kumona, bua kupatula tshimfuanyi tshimue kadi ki ntshibidi kudi buongo to; muaba wa pa buende wa mêsu a baleine, matapulula bimpe menemene bu mudiwu ku metre a bungi a mutu mukole, udi mubandile pankatshi pawu bu mukuna munene udi utapulula majiba abidi mu mikuna; bualu ebu, bulelela, budi ne tshia kutapulula bimpe menemene ngenyi idi tshitupa tshionso tshia pa nkayatshi tshifila. Nunku, baleine udi ne tshia kumona tshimfuanyi tshimue tshisunguluke ku luseke elu, ne tshimfuanyi tshikuabu tshisunguluke ku luseke alu; eku bionso bidi pankatshi bidi ne tshia kuikala mîdima mikole ne tshianana kudiye. Muntu udi mua kuamba ne: udi utangila pa buloba ku kazubu ka balami kadi ne mikaba ibidi misangisha bua dididishi diende. Ino na baleine, bipese bibidi ebi bidi bibueja pa nkayabi, bienza madidishi abidi mashilangane, kadi bia dibungama bidi binyanga tshimuenekelu. Bualu bua pa buabu bua mêsu a baleine budi ne tshia kuvuluka misangu yonso mu mudimu wa diloba; ne bua kuvuluka kudi mubadi mu bimue bintu bidi bilonda.

Lukonku lua dikema ne lua dikema ludi mua kutuadija pa bidi bitangila bualu ebu budi butangila Leviatana. Kadi ndi ne tshia kusanka ne lungenyi. Bu mudi mêsu a muntu mabulule mu butoke, tshienzedi tshia kumona tshidi katshiyi tshia budisuile; mmumue ne: kena mua kuambuluisha bua kumona bintu bionso bidi kumpala kuende to. Nansha nanku, malu adi muntu yonso mupete neamulongeshe ne: nansha mudiye mua kumona malu mu mushindu udi kauyi ne kansungansunga musangu umue, kabiena mua kuenzeka bua yeye, ne ntema yonso, ne mu mushindu mujima, kukonkonona bintu bibidi nansha biobi bikale binene anyi bikese mu tshikondo tshimue; kabiena ne bualu nansha bobu balale ku luseke ne ku luseke ne balengangana. Ino shi udi mufika ku kutapulula bintu bibidi ebi, ne kunyunguluka tshionso ne tshijengu tshia mîdima mikole; pashishe, bua kumona umue wa kudiyi, mu mushindu udi mua kufikisha lungenyi luebe ku ditshitaba, mukuabu neapatuke bikole mu lungenyi luebe lua matuku etu aa. Nunku, bidi bishi bua baleine? Bushuwa, mêsu ende onso abidi, munda muawu nkayawu, adi ne bua kuenza mudimu musangu umue; kadi buongo buende budi ne malu a bungi, adi asangisha, ne adi kaayi amueneka patoke kupita a muntu, bua se: udi mua kukonkonona ne ntema yonso malu abidi mashilangane, umue ku luseke luende, ne mukuabu ku luseke lukuabu menemene anyi? Shi udi mua kuenza nanku, bidi anu bu bualu bua dikema munda muende, bienze anu bu ne: muntu udi mua kupitshila musangu umue mu bilejilu bia ntatu ibidi mishilangane mu Euclid. Nansha, mu dikonkonona dikole, kakuena bualu nansha bumue budi kabuyi bupetangana mu difuanyikija edi to.

Bidi mua kuikala anu bualu bua patupu, kadi bivua anu bimueneka kundi ne: dishintuluka dia dikema dia dienda didi dileja kudi bamue baleine padibu babakuata kudi mazuwa asatu anyi anayi; buôwa ne dibanza dia kuenza buôwa bua bantu badi kabayi baselangane, bidi bitamba kuenzeka kudi baleine ya nunku; Ndi ngela meji ne: bionso ebi bidi bifumina ku dipanga dia diambuluisha dia disua, mudi bukole buabu bua dimona budi butapuluke ne budi bushilangane bikole budi ne bua kubabueja.

Kadi ditshi dia baleine didi diuwule tente ne dijinga dia kumanya malu anu bu dîsu. Shi udi muenyi wa tshisamba tshiabu, udi mua kukeba mitu ibidi eyi munkatshi mua mêba a bungi, ne kuyi muanji kupeta tshitupa atshi to. Ditshi kadiena ne dibeji dia pambelu nansha dikese; ne mu tshina nkayatshi kuena mua kubueja tshiamu tshia nzembu to, nunku ntshikese tshia dikema. Udi musombele kakese kunyima kua dîsu. Pa bidi bitangila matshi abu, dishilangana edi dia mushinga didi ne tshia kumueneka pankatshi pa baleine wa sperme ne wa ku dia balume. Nansha mudi ditshi dia kumpala dikale ne tshibuelelu tshia pambelu, dia ndekelu didi dibuikila bimpe menemene ne tshilamba, nunku kadiena mua kumueneka pambelu to.

Kabienaku bikemesha bua se: tshintu tshinene bu mudi baleine tshidi mua kumona buloba ku dîsu dikese nunku, ne kumvua makubakuba ku ditshi didi dikese kupita dia ngulube anyi? Ino shi meso ende mialabale bu tshiamu tshia kutangila natshi bintu tshia Herschel; ne matshi ende avua manene bu mudi bimanu bia nzubu minene ya nzambi; bidi mua kumuvuija kayi umona anyi umvua bimpe anyi? Nansha kakese.—Bua tshinyi udi udienzeja bua ‘kudiundisha’ lungenyi luebe? Utshifunde.

Tuenzayi mpindieu ne biamu bionso bia mudimu ne biamu bia luya bitudi nabi ku bianza, pa mutu pa baleine wa sperme, bua ikale ulala kuinshi too ne kuulu; pashishe, paudi ubanda ku tshibandilu too ne ku mutu, ela mêsu panshi ku mishiku; ne bu mubidi kauyi mpindieu mutapuluke bimpe menemene nende, ne muendu tuvua mua kupueka mu tshina tshinene tshia Kentucky Mammoth tshia mu difu diende. Kadi tulamayi apa ku dînu edi, ne tutangile muaba utudi. Mmukana mulenga mulelela ne mukezuke! kumbukila panshi too ne ku plafond, mulongolola, anyi mua kuamba ne: mufunda ne tshilamba tshitoke tshidi tshikenka, tshikenkesha bu satin ya dibaka.

Kadi patuka mpindieu, ne tangila mpala wa kuinshi eu udi umueneka bu tshibuikidiji tshile tshikese tshia tshibombi tshinene tshia mvinyo, ne tshibuikidiji ku luseke lumue, pamutu pa ku luseke lumue. Wewe mumubandishe, bua kumuteka pa mutu, ne kumuleja milongo ya meno ende, udi umueneka bu portcullis wa dikema; ne nunku, diakabi! bidi bileja kudi ba bungi bu muntu mupele mu mudimu wa diloba, udi misongo eyi ikulukila ne bukole bua dikuma. Ino i biyampe bininge mwanda wa kumona, patudi mu mbuu, udi umona mushipa wa baleine udi ne tshiji, wenda unyunguluka, ne mpala wende munene, bule bua metre matue ku dikumi ne atanu, mulengeja panshi mu njila muakane ne mubidi wende, bua buloba bujima bu tshiona tshia mazuwa. Baleine eu ki mmufue to; udi anu mubungame; pambelu pa mishindu, pamuapa; hypochondriac; ne uvua mulale panshi bikole, bienza bua ne: bipese bia ku mpala wende bivua bipuekeshibue, bimushiye muaba au mu nsombelu mubi au, tshipendu kudi tshisamba tshiende tshijima, tshidi ne bua kuikala, kakuyi mpata, kumuela mpala wa lock-jaws.

Misangu ya bungi, mpala wa kuinshi eu—udibu mua kupatula bipepele kudi muenji wa mudimu wa bianza—utu upatuka ne ubandishibua pa mazuwa bua kupatula meno a meno a meno, ne kufila tshintu tshia mufuba wa baleine mutoke mukole udi balobi benza nawu bintu bia mishindu yonso bia dikema, bu mudi: mitshi, milondo, ne mishiku ya ku bianza.

Ne tshiamu tshile, tshikole tshia kubandisha natshi, mpala udi ukoka pa buatu, bienze anu bu ne: udi nanga; ne padi tshikondo tshikumbane tshikumbana—matuku makese kunyima kua mudimu mukuabu—Queequeg, Daggoo, ne Tashtego, bu mudibu bonso baminganga ba menu bapiluke, badi badilongolole bua kukosa meno. Ne tshingoma tshikole, Queequeg udi utapa mishiku; pashishe mpala udi muela panshi ku bipese bia nshinga, ne tshintu tshia kuenza natshi mudimu tshidi tshipatuka muulu, badi bakoka meno aa, bu mudi ngombe ya mu Michigan ikoka mitshi ya kale ya mitshi ya chêne mu maloba a mitshi ya muitu. Pa tshibidilu mudi meno makumi anayi ne abidi mu kabujima; mu baleine ya kale, minyanguke bikole, kadi kayiyi mibole; anyi kuwuja bilondeshile tshilele tshietu tshia kuenza. Mushiku udi pashishe mukosolola mu mabaya, ne mubuejakaja bu mabaya a kuibaka nawu nzubu.





SHAPITA 75. Mutu wa baleine wa ku dia balume—Mushindu mushilangane.

Patudi tusabuka tshibandilu, mpindieu tuangate tshimuenekelu tshimpe ku mutu wa baleine wa ku dia balume.

Mu mushindu wa pa tshibidilu, mutu wa Sperm Whale udi mua kufuanyikijibua ne ditempu dia mvita dia bena Lomo (nangananga kumpala, muaba udiwu mubumbakane bikole); nunku, mu tshimuenekelu tshinene, mutu wa baleine wa ku dia balume udi ne tshimuenekelu tshibi tshia dikasa dinene dia galliot-toed. Kukadi bidimu nkama ibidi, muendeshi wa mazuwa mukulakaje wa mu Pays-Bas wakafuanyikija tshimuenekelu tshiawu ne tshia ndekelu tshia muenji wa bisabata. Ne mu tshitupa etshi tshia ndekelu, mamu mukulakaje wa mu tshilongelu tshia bana, ne bana babu, uvua mua kuikala musombe bimpe, yeye ne bana bende bonso.

Kadi paudi wenda usemena pabuipi ne mutu munene eu udi utuadija kuangata malu mashilangane, bilondeshile mmuenenu webe. Wewe muimane pa mutu patshi ne mutangile bipese bibidi ebi bia mu tshimfuanyi tshia F, udi mua kuangata mutu mujima bua tshiombelu tshinene tshia bass-viol, ne spiracles eyi, bipese bia mu tshisanji tshiatshi tshia dîyi. Pashishe, kabidi, wewe muteke dîsu diebe pa tshintu etshi tshia dikema, tshidi ne nsongo, tshidi tshifuanangane ne tshipese tshidi pa mutu pa tshisumbu etshi—tshintu etshi tshia matamba, tshidi ne barnacles, tshidi bena Greenland babikila ne: “tshifulu tshia butumbi,” ne balobi ba ku Sud ne: “tshifulu” tshia baleine wa ku dia balume; wewe mutangile anu pa bualu ebu, udi mua kuangata mutu bua mutshi wa mutshi munene wa chêne, udi ne tshikuku tshia nyunyi mu tshiadi tshiende. Nansha biobikala mushindu kayi, paudi utangila bikunyibua bidi ne muoyo bidi bisomba apa pa tshilamba etshi, lungenyi lua nunku nelukufikile; pa kumbusha, bushuwa, dijinga diebe dijadikibua kudi muaku wa malu a tekiniki “tshifulu tshia butumbi” tshidibu bapeshe kabidi; mu bualu ebu neuditatshishe bikole bua kuela meji mudi tshintu tshinene etshi tshikale bushuwa mukalenge wa mbuu uvua ne tshifulu tshia bukalenge, uvuabu bateke tshifulu tshiende tshia butumbi mu mushindu wa dikema eu. Ino shi baleine ewu i mukalenge, udi muntu udi umueneka mubi bua kuikala ne tshifulu tshia butumbi. Tangila mushiku wa kuinshi udi mulenga! mmunyi mudi tshiji tshikole ne tshiji tshikole! tshitupa tshia mpala ne tshiji, bilondeshile tshipiminu tshia muena mabaya, bule bua metre makumi abidi ne bunene bua metre atanu; tshiji ne tshiji tshidi mua kukupetesha 500 gallons ya mafuta ne kupita apu.

Luse lunene, mpindieu, bua se: baleine eu udi kayi ne diakalenga udi ne bua kuikala ne mishiku ya mbuji. Ditapuluka didi pabuipi ne dikasa dimue mu butshiama. Pamuapa mamu mu tshikondo kampanda tshia mushinga uvua wenda mu buatu ku muelelu wa mâyi wa mu Pérou, pavua bikumina bifikishe ku muelelu wa mâyi. Pa mutu pa mushiku eu, bu pa mutu pa tshibuelelu tshidi tshisesuka, mpindieu tudi tubuela mukana. Ku dîyi dianyi, meme mu Mackinaw, mvua ne bua kuangata bualu ebu bu munda mua nzubu wa tshilala benyi wa bena Inde. Mukelenge muimpe! eu ke njila uvua Yona muye anyi? Tshisasa tshidi ne bule bua metre dikumi ne abidi, ne tshidi tshienda mu nseke mikole, bienze anu bu ne: kudi mutshi wa pa tshibidilu udiku; eku nseke eyi idi ne mifuba, milengeja, idi ne boya, idi ituleja ne bipese bia dikema, tshitupa tshimane, bia mu tshimfuanyi tshia tshiamu tshia baleine, tuambe ne: nkama isatu ku luseke, bidi bilondeshila ku tshitupa tshia kuulu tshia mutu anyi tshia mufuba wa tshifulu tshia butumbi, bidi bienza bilamba bia mu Venise bidibu batele miaba mikuabu. Ku mpenga kua mifuba eyi kudi mionji idi ne boya, idi baleine wa ku dia balume upita nayi mâyi, ne mu mishindu eyi udi ulama mushipa mukese, padiye mubulule mukana udi upitshila mu mbuu ya brit mu tshikondo tshia kudia. Mu bipese bia munkatshi bia mifuba, bu mudibi bimane mu bulongame buabi, mudi bimanyinu kampanda bia dikema, bipese, bipese, ne bipese, mudi bamue bena baleine babala bidimu bia tshifukibua etshi, bu mudi bidimu bia mutshi wa chêne ku diambuluisha dia bipese biende bia tshijengu. Nansha mudi bujadiki bua tshipiminu etshi kabuyi mua kuleja, kadi budi ne mupuya wa probabilité analogique. Nansha biobikala mushindu kayi, tuetu bitabe, tudi ne bua kupesha Baleine wa ku dia balume bidimu bia bungi menemene kupita mudibi mua kumueneka biakane ku mêsu.

Mu bikondo bia kale, bidi bimueneka ne: kuvua lungenyi lua dikema lua malu avua atangila bilamba ebi. Muena ngendu mukuabu wa mu Purchas udi ubikila ne: “mishiku” ya dikema idi munda mua mukana wa baleine;* mukuabu ne: “mishiku ya ngulube”; muntu mukulumpe muisatu wa mu Hackluyt udi wenza mudimu ne muakulu muimpe udi ulonda eu: "Kudi mapapa matue ku nkama ibidi ne makumi atanu adi akola ku luseke luonso lua tshiadi tshiende tshia kuulu, adi alenga ludimi luende ku luseke luonso lua mukana muende."

*Etshi tshidi tshituvuluija ne: Baleine wa ku dia balume udi bushuwa ne mushindu kampanda wa mpala, anyi mpala wa mpala, udi ne nsuki mikese mitoke mitangalake ku tshitupa tshia kuulu tshia ku ndekelu kua mpala wa kuinshi. Imue misangu, bipese ebi bidi bifila tshimuenekelu tshia bupote ku mpala wende udi ne kanemu.

Anu mudi muntu yonso mumanye, “miosa ya ngulube,” “mapapa,” “mishiku,” “mishiku,” anyi tshionso tshiudi musue, idi ifila kudi bakaji bilamba biabu ne bintu bikuabu bia kukolesha nabi mubidi. Kadi mu bualu ebu, dijinga didi dienda dikepela katshia ku kale. Mu tshikondo tshia mukalenge mukaji Anne ke muvua mufuba muikale mu butumbi buende, ne farthingale uvua tshikondo atshi tshintu tshionso tshia mushinga mukole. Ne pavua bakaji ba kale abu bendakana ne disanka, nansha mu mishiku ya baleine, bu muudi mua kuamba; nansha nanku, mu tshina tshia mâyi, ne dipanga dia meji dia muomumue, lelu tudiku tuenda muinshi mua mishiku ya muomumue bua kudikuba; tshilongo tshidi ntenta mutangalake pa mufuba umue.

Ino mpindieu, upua muoyo bionso bidi bitangila bilamba ne nsuki bua tshitupa tshîpi, ne, muimane mukana mua Baleine wa ku dia balume, tangila tshiakabidi. Paudi umona mifuba yonso eyi milongolola bimpe, kuenaku wela meji ne: udi munda mua tshiamu tshinene tshia Haarlem, ne utangila milonda yatshi tshinunu anyi? Bua tshilamba tshia ku tshianza tshia mubidi tudi ne tshilamba tshia Turquie tshitambe butekete—ludimi, ludibu balamakaja, bu mudibi, panshi pa mukana. Udi ne mafuta a bungi ne mutekete, ne udi mua kutshibula mu bitupa bitupa padibu bamubandisha pa mazuwa. Ludimi elu lua pa bualu mpindieu kumpala kuetu; mu dimona dia patupu ndi mua kuamba ne: uvua wa milondo isambombo; mmumue ne: neakupeteshe bungi bua mafuta abu.

Kumpala kua bualu ebu, udi ne bua kuikala mumone bulelela bua tshimvua mutuadije natshi—ne: baleine wa mabele ne baleine wa ku dia balume badi ne mitu mishilangane bikole. Bua kuamba mu tshikoso, nunku: mu baleine wa ku dia balume kamuena tshina tshinene tshia mabele to; kakuyi meno a meno a meno nansha makese; ki mbua bule, butekete bua tshiadi tshia kuinshi, bu tshia Sperm Whale to. Kabidi mu Sperm Whale kamuena tshimue tshia ku bintu ebi bia mufuba to; kakuyi mushiku munene wa kuinshi; ne kintu nansha kimo kya ludimi. Musangu eu kabidi, Baleine wa ku dia balume udi ne mabulu abidi a pambelu, Baleine wa Mabele udi anu ne umue.

Tangila tshia ndekelu, mpindieu, pa mitu eyi milengeja ne tshilamba, padiyi milale pamue; bualu umue neakuluke mu katupa kîpi emu, kayi mufunda, mu mbuu; mukuabu kakuikala mutantshi mule mu dilonda to.

Udiku mua kumona tshimuenekelu tshia Sperm Whale kuntu aku anyi? I bia momumua bivuaye mufue nabi, anu ndambu wa milonda mile ya ku mpala idi imueneka mpindieu mimane kujika. Ndi ngela meji ne: mpala wende mualabale uvua muule tente ne ditalala dia buena dia muitu, diledibua ku dipanga dia lungenyi lua lufu. Kadi funda tshimuenekelu tshia mutu mukuabu. Tangila mushiku wa kuinshi wa dikema, mufina ku mpukapuka ku luseke lua tshintu, bua kukuata tshianza bikole. Mutu eu wonso kawena umueneka bu udi wakula bua dipangadika dinene dia kuenza mudimu dia kutantamena lufu anyi? Baleine wa ku dia balume eu ndi ngangata bu uvua muena Stoïcien; baleine wa mabele, muena Platon, uvua mua kuikala muangate Spinoza mu bidimu biende bia ndekelu.





SHAPITA 76. Mukoko wa mvita.

Kumpala kua kulekela, bua tshitupa tshîpi, mutu wa Sperm Whale, ndi musue bua wewe, bu mumanyi wa malu a mubidi wa meji, anu—nangananga umone tshitupa tshiende tshia kumpala, mu disangisha diatshi dionso dikole. Ndi musue bua wewe kulonga bualu ebu mpindieu ne lungenyi lua kudienzela bualu kampanda budi kabuyi bupite bungi, bua meji bua bukole kayi buonso budi mua kuikala muaba au.
