SHAPITA 32. Cetologie.

Tudi bamane kutuadija ne dikima dionso pa mutu pa mâyi manene; kadi mu katupa kîpi emu netujimine mu bunene buende budi kabuyi ne muelelu, budi kabuyi ne tshitudilu. Kumpala kua bualu ebu buenzeke; kumpala kua tshilamba tshia Pequod tshidi ne bisonsa tshienda tshibuela ku luseke ne ku luseke ne tshilamba tshia barnacled tshia leviathan; ku mbangilu bidi anu bimpa bua kutabalela bualu budi pabuipi ne kuikala ne mushinga bua kumvua bimpe ne dianyisha dia dibuluibua dia pa buadi dia leviathanic ne bilejilu bia mishindu yonso bidi ne bua kulonda.

I tshilejilu tshilongolola tshia baleine mu mishindu yende minene, tshindi musue mpindieu kunuteka kumpala kuenu. Kadi ki mmudimu mupepele to. Ditapulula dia bintu bidi bienza tshimvundu, ki ntshintu tshikuabu tshidi tshileja apa to. Teleja tshidi bamfumu batambe buimpe ne bapiabapia bateke.

Kapitene Scoresby, AD 1820 udi wamba ne: “Kakuena tshitupa tshia malu a nyama tshidi ne mudimu wa bungi bu mudi tshidibu babikila ne: Cetology to.”

"Ki mbualu buanyi to, bu biobi bikale mu bukokeshi buanyi, bua kubuela mu dikonkonona dia mushindu mulelela wa kutapulula cetacea mu bisumbu ne mu mêku. * * * Dikokangana dikole didiku munkatshi mua bafundi ba malu a kale a nyama eu" (baleine wa mabele), ke mudi munganga Beale, AD 1839 wamba.

"Dibenga kukumbana bua kulonda makebulula etu mu mayi adi kaayi mua kumvuika." "Tshilamba tshidi katshiyi mua kubuela tshidi tshibuikila dimanya dietu dia cetacea." “Budimi buule tente ne mêba.” "Bimanyinu bionso ebi kabiena bikumbane to, kadi bidi bitukengesha tuetu bena malu a bufuki."

Nunku tuakula bua baleine, Cuvier munene, ne John Hunter, ne Lesson, butoke bua malu a nyama ne a mubidi. Nansha mudi dimanya dilelela dikale dikese, kadi mikanda idi ya bungi; ne nunku mu tshitupa tshikese, ne cetology, anyi malu a sianse a baleine. Bantu ba bungi, bakese ne banene, bakulumpe ne bapiabapia, bena pa buloba ne bena mazuwa, badi ne bungi anyi bukese, bafunde bua baleine. Tangila amue:—Bafundi ba Bible; Aristote; Pliny; Aldrovandi; Sir Thomas Browne; Gesner; Ray; Linnæus; Rondeletius; Willoughby; Vert; Artedi; Sibbald; Brisson; Marten; Lacépède; Bonneterre; Desmarest; Baron Cuvier; Frederick Cuvier; John Hunter; Owen; Scoresby; Beale; Bennett; J. Ross Browne; Mufundi wa Miriam Coffin; Olmstead; ne Rev. T. Cheever. Ino ku kipatshila ka ndekelu ka bionso ebi bafunde, bitupa bidi kulu eku nebileje.

Mu mêna adi mu mulongo wa bafundi ba baleine, anu aba badi balonda Owen ke bavua bamone baleine badi ne muoyo; ne kadi umue wa kudibu uvua muena mudimu wa tshingoma ne wa baleine. Ndi musue kuamba Kapitene Scoresby. Pa tshilumbu tshikuabu tshia Greenland anyi baleine wa ku dia balume, yeye ke muntu mutambe buimpe udiku. Kadi Scoresby kavua mumanye bualu nansha bumue ne kavua wamba bualu nansha bumue bua baleine munene wa mabele to, patudi tumufuanyikija ne baleine wa mu Greenland uvua pabuipi ne kuikala kayi ne mushinga wa kutela. Ne apa tudi mua kuamba ne: baleine wa mu Greenland udi muena tshinyangu pa nkuasa wa bukalenge wa mbuu. Ki mmunene mutambe baleine to. Ino, pa mwanda wa bukomo bwa bininge bya bine bya bine, ne pa mwanda wa busendwe bukatampe bwadi, too ne ku myaka makumi muanda mutekete kumpala, bufikile baleine wa sperm-whale wa dikema nansha ukavua kayi mumanyike nansha kakese, ne dipanga dia lungenyi edi too ne lelu ditshidi anu dikokesha mu miaba yonso ya malu a sianse ne mu bipalu bia baleine; dikuata dia bintu edi ndikumbane mu mishindu yonso. Paudi utangila malu onso adi atangila leviathani mu bafundi banene ba tusala ba matuku a kale, neakujadikile ne: baleine wa mu Groenland, kayi ne muena lukuna nansha umue, uvua kudibu mukalenge wa mbuu. Kadi tshikondo tshiakafika ndekelu wa bionso bua dimanyisha dipiadipia. Etshi i Charing Cross; telejayi! bantu bonso biyampe,—baleine wa Greenland mmumbushibue ku nkuasa,—baleine munene wa sperme udi mpindieu ukokesha!

Kudi anu mikanda ibidi idi yenza bu idi iteka baleine wa sperme udi ne muoyo kumpala kuebe, ne musangu umue, mu tshitupa tshia kule menemene idi ikumbaja diteta edi. Mikanda ayi i ya Beale ne ya Bennett; bonso babidi mu tshikondo tshiabu baminganga ba mu mazuwa a baleine a mu Mbuu wa ku Sud wa mu Angleterre, ne bonso babidi bantu ba bulelela ne ba kueyemena. Tshintu tshia ntuadijilu tshidi tshilenga baleine wa sperme bua kupeta mu volime yabu tshidi anu tshikese; kadi bilondeshile mudibi bienda, bidi bia mushinga mukole, nansha mudibi bitamba kuikala anu mu diumvuija dia malu a sianse. Nansha nanku, baleine wa sperme, wa malu a sianse anyi wa tusala, kena ne muoyo mujima mu mikanda nansha mikese to. Ku mutu kua baleine mikuabu yonso idibu baloba, nsombelu wende udi kayi mufunda.

Mpindieu mishindu mishilashilangane ya baleine idi dijinga ne mushindu kampanda wa ditapulula dia bantu bonso, nansha biobi bikale anu dilongolola dipepele bua mpindieu, ne pashishe nebiuwujibue mu bitupa biende bionso kudi bena mudimu badi balonda. Bu mudiku kakuyi muntu mutambe buimpe udi mua kuya kumpala bua kuangata bualu ebu mu tshianza, ndi nnupesha mudimu wanyi mubi. Tshiena mulaye tshintu tshikumbane to; bualu bua tshintu tshionso tshia muntu tshidi ne tshia kuikala tshikumbane, tshidi ne tshia kuikala ne bilema bua bualu abu. Tshiena mua kuenza bu ne: ndi ne diumvuija dikese dia mubidi wa nyama mishilashilangane, anyi—muaba eu nansha—ku diumvuija dia bungi dia mishindu yonso. Tshipatshila tshianyi apa ntshia kuleja tshilejelu tshia dilongolola dia cetology. Ndi muenji wa nzubu, ki mmuibaki to.

Kadi mmudimu mukole; kakuena muntu udi ulongolola mikanda ya patupu mu Biro bia Poste udi mufuanangane nende to. Bua kubunda muinshi mua mbuu panyima pabu; kuikala ne bianza bia muntu munkatshi mua bishimikidi bidi kabiyi mua kuakula, mifuba, ne tshifu tshia pa buloba; bualu ebu budi buenza buôwa. Ndi tshinyi tshindi ne bua kuenza bua kukuata muulu wa leviathan eu! Bipendu bibi bia mu Yobo bidi mua kuntonda bikole. Yeye (leviatana) neenze nebe tshipungidi anyi? Tangila, ditekemena diandi ndia patupu! Kadi ndi mupitshile mu nzubu ya mikanda ne mupitshile mu mbuu; Ndi ne tshia kuenza ne baleine idi ne bianza ebi bidi bimueneka; Ndi ne muoyo mujima; ne ndi nteta. Kudi amue malu a kumpala a kujikija.

Tshia kumpala: Nsombelu udi kayi mujadika, udi kayi mujalame wa malu a sianse wa Cetology udi mu tshibuelelu tshidi tshijadikibua kudi bualu budi ne: mu imue miaba bidi anu bualu budi kabuyi bujadika bikala baleine muikale mushipa. Mu mukanda wende wa System of Nature, AD 1776, Linnæus udi wamba ne, "Ndi ntapulula baleine ne mishipa." Ino ku bwino bwami, ndi mumanye amba kufika mu mwaka wa 1850, sharks ne shad, alewives ne herring, bilondeshela mukenji wa patoke wa Linnæus, batshivua babanyangana bumpianyi bwa mbuu ya muomumue ne Leviathan.

Bualu buvua Linnæus mua kuipata bua kuipata baleine mu mayi, udi wamba nunku: "Bua muoyo wabu wa bilocular wa kapia, mifuba yabu, bipese biabu bia mêsu bidi biendakana, matshi abu adi kaayi ne tshintu, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem," ne ndekelu wa bionso, "ex lege naturæ jure meritoque." Ngakafila bionso ebi kudi balunda banyi Simeon Macey ne Charley Coffin, ba mu Nantucket, bonso babidi bavua balunda banyi mu luendu kampanda, ne bakadisangisha mu lungenyi lua se: malu avuabu baleje kaavua makumbane to. Charley wakaleja mu mushindu mubi ne: bavua bantu ba tshianana.

Tumanye ne, kulekela bilumbu bionso, ndi ngangata lungenyi lua kale lua se: baleine mmushipa, ne ndi mbila Yona wa tshijila bua kungambuluisha. Bualu bua nshindamenu ebu budi bujika, bualu budi bulonda budi ne: mu mushindu kayi udi baleine mushilangane ne mishipa mikuabu. Pa mutu, Linnæus mmukupeshe bintu abi. Kadi mu tshikoso, bidi ebi: mifuba ne mashi a luya; kadi, mishipa mikuabu yonso kayena ne mifuba ne mashi mashika.

Tshidi tshilonda: mmunyi mutudi mua kumvuija baleine, ku bintu biende bia pambelu bidi bimueneka patoke, bua kumubikila patoke bua tshikondo tshionso tshidi tshilua? Bua kuamba mu tshikoso, nunku, baleine mmushipa udi utua mishiku ne mukila mutangile kuulu . Kuoku aku udi nende. Nansha mudibi bikese, diumvuija edi didi difumina ku dielangana meji dia bungi. Mushipa wa walrus udi utua mishiku anu bu baleine, kadi walrus ki mmushipa to, bualu udi usomba pa mâyi. Kadi muaku wa ndekelu wa diumvuija udi anu ne bukole bua bungi, bu mudiwu musangisha ne wa kumpala. Muntu yonso udi ne bua kuikala mumone ne: mishipa yonso idi bantu ba pa buloba bamanye kayena ne mukila mupanda to, kadi udi ne mukila muimane anyi mubandile ne panshi. Kadi, munkatshi mua mishipa idi ipatuka, mukila, nansha mudiwu mua kuikala ne tshimuenekelu tshia muomumue, utu misangu yonso wangata muaba wa kuulu.

Bilondeshile diumvuija didi kulu edi dia tshidi baleine, tshiena mua kumbusha mu tshisumbu tshia bana betu ba leviathanic tshifukibua nansha tshimue tshia mu mâyi tshidibu bamanyishe too ne mpindieu ne baleine kudi bena Nantucket badi bamanye malu bimpe to; anyi, ku luseke lukuabu, kulamakaja nende mushipa nansha umue uvuabu bangata bu wa bende to.* Nunku, mishipa yonso mikese, idi ne mikila mishindame idi ne bua kuikala mu plan wa Cetology eu. Mpindieu, nunku, kudi bitupa binene bia tshisumbu tshijima tshia baleine.

*Ndi mumanye ne too ne lelu, mishipa idibu babikila ne: Lamatins ne Dugongs (Pig-fish ne Sow-fish of the Coffins of Nantucket) idi mibueja kudi bamanyi ba bungi ba malu a bufuki munkatshi mua baleine. Ino bu mudi mishipa ya ngulube eyi mikale ne mutoyi, idi kayiyi ne mushinga, idi misangu ya bungi misokome mu mishiku ya misulu, ne idia bisonsa biume, ne nangananga bu mudiyi kayiyi ipatuka, ndi nbenga bumanyishi buayi bu baleine; ne babaleje mikanda yabu ya ditunga bua kumbuka mu Bukalenge bua Cetology.

Tshia kumpala: Bilondeshile bunene, ndi ntapulula baleine mu mikanda isatu minene (idi mua kutapuluka mu NSHAPITA), ne aba nebayimvuije yonso, mikese ne minene.

I. THE FOLIO WHALE; II. baleine wa OCTAVO; III. DUODECIMO WHALE.

Bu mushindu wa FOLIO ndi nfila Sperm Whale ; wa OCTAVO, Grampus ; wa DUODECIMO, Porpoise .

FOLIOS. Munkatshi mua bintu ebi ndi ngela nshapita idi ilonda eyi:—I. Baleine wa mabele ; II. baleine wa ku dia balume ; III. baleine wa Fin-Back ; IV. baleine wa mukolo wa nsongo ; V. Baleine wa Razor Back ; VI. baleine wa muinshi mua sufre .

MUKANDA I. ( Folio ), SHAPITRE I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Baleine eu, munkatshi mua bena Angleterre ba kale uvuabu babikila ne: Trumpa whale, ne Physeter whale, ne Anvil Headed whale, udi mpindieu Cachalot wa bena France, ne Pottsfich wa bena Allemagne, ne Macrocelus wa Long Words. Kakuyi mpata, yeye ke muntu mutambe bunene udi musombele pa buloba bujima; baleine mutambe bubi kupita yonso ya kusambakena nayi; tshitamba bunene mu tshimuenekelu; ne tshia ndekelu, tshidi ne mushinga wa bungi mu malu a bungenda; yeye muikale tshifukibua tshimuepele tshidibu bapeta tshintu tshia mushinga mukole atshi, spermaceti. Bintu biende bionso bia pa buabi nebivudijibue, mu miaba mikuabu ya bungi. Ndi ne tshia kuenza mpindieu ne dina diende. Pa kutangila malu a ntendelelu, mbualu bua bupote. Kukadi nkama ya bidimu, pavua baleine wa Sperm kayi mumanyike bimpe mu bumuntu buende, ne pavua mafuta ende afumina anu ku mpukapuka kudi mushipa uvua mubuele mu tshina; mu mafuku a spermaceti, bidi mua kumueneka bu, bantu ba bungi bavua bela meji ne: uvua mufumine ku tshifukibua tshivua tshifuanangane ne tshivuabu babikila mu Angleterre ne: Greenland anyi baleine wa ku dia balume. Lungenyi luvua kabidi ne: spermaceti umue umue eu uvua tshisekedi tshia baleine wa mu Greenland tshidi tshikuma tshia kumpala tshia muaku etshi tshileja. Mu bikondo abi, kabidi, spermaceti uvua mukese menemene, kayivua wenza mudimu bua butoke to, kadi anu bu mafuta ne buanga. Bivua anu bua kubipeta kudi bafidi ba manga bualu lelu udi usumba onze wa rhubarb. Pavua, bu mundi ngela meji, mu kupita kua matuku, bumuntu bulelela bua spermaceti bumanyike, dîna diende dia ntuadijilu divua anu dilama kudi basumbishi; kakuyi mpata bua kubandisha mushinga watshi ku diambuluisha dia lungenyi lua dikema lua dipanga diawu. Ne nunku dina edi didi ne tshia kuikala ndekelu wa bionso difidibue kudi baleine uvuabu bafumine menemene kudi spermaceti eu.

MUKANDA I. ( Folio ), SHAPITRE II. ( Right Whale ).—Mu mushindu kampanda, eu ke nyama wa leviathan udi ne kanemu ka bungi, bualu udi muntu wa kumpala uvuabu balonda pa tshibidilu kudi bantu. Bidi bipatula tshintu tshidibu babikila ne: whalebone anyi baleen; ne mafuta adibu babikila nangananga ne: “mafuta a baleine,” tshintu tshia mushinga mukesa mu malu a bungenda. Munkatshi mua balobi, badi bamubikila kakuyi kansungansunga ne mêna onso adi alonda aa: Baleine; baleine wa Groenland; baleine mufike; baleine munene; baleine mulelela; Baleine wa ku dia balume. Kudi malu a bungi adi kaayi malelela pa bidi bitangila bumuntu bua mishindu ya nyama idibu batambule nunku. Leine udi tshinyi, undi mubueja mu mushindu muibidi wa Folios wanyi? I Mysticetus Munene wa bamanyi ba malu a bufuki ba mu Angleterre; baleine wa mu Groenland wa bena Angleterre; Baleine Ordinaire wa baleine ba mu France; Growlands Walfish wa bena Suède. I baleine udi mu bidimu bipite pa nkama ibidi mipita kudi bena Pays-Bas ne bena Angleterre mu mbuu ya Arctique; i baleine udi balobi ba mu Amerike balonda mu mbuu wa Inde, ku Brésil Banks, ku Nor' West Coast, ne mu bitupa bikuabu bia pa buloba, bidibu babikila ne: Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Bamue badi benza bu badi bamona dishilangana pankatshi pa baleine wa mu Greenland wa bena Angleterre ne baleine wa ku dia balume wa bena Amerike. Kadi badi bumvuangana menemene mu malu abu onso manene; nansha kakuena bualu nansha bumue budibu baleje budi mua kujadika dishilangana dinene to. Ku diambuluisha dia matapuluka adi kaayi ne ndekelu adi mamanyine pa dishilangana didi kadiyi dijika, ke mudi bimue bitupa bia malu a kale a bufuki bilua bikole menemene. Baleine wa ku dia balume nebakuate muaba mukuabu mu bule kampanda, bilondeshile diumvuija dia baleine wa mabele.

MUKANDA I. ( Folio ), SHAPITRE III. ( Fin-Back ).—Muinshi mua mutu eu ndi ngela meji ne: tshintu tshinene tshidi, ku mêna mashilangane a Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, ne Long-John, tshidi tshimueneka pabuipi ne mu mbuu yonso ne tshidi pa tshibidilu baleine udibu batamba kuamba kudi bantu badi basabuka Mbuu wa Atlantique, mu njila ya mu New York. Mu bule budiye upeta, ne mu baleen wende, Fin-back udi ufuanangana ne baleine wa ku dia balume, kadi udi ne tshitupa tshikese tshia portly, ne udi ne dikala dimpe, udi usemena pabuipi ne olive. Mishiku yende minene idi ileja tshimuenekelu tshia nkamba, mienza kudi milonda minene idi mibumbakane. Tshintu tshiende tshinene tshidi tshimusunguluja, dikasa, didiye mupete dina diende, ditu misangu ya bungi tshintu tshidi tshimueneka patoke. Dikasa edi didi ne bule bua metre matue ku asatu anyi anayi, dikola diakane kumbukila ku tshitupa tshia panyima tshia nyima, dia mu tshimfuanyi tshia nseke, ne didi ne ndekelu wa nsongo mukola. Nansha mudi tshitupa tshikuabu tshikese tshia tshifukibua katshiyi tshimueneka, dikasa edi didi pa nkayadi, imue misangu, nedimueneke patoke dipatuka pa mutu pa buloba. Padi mbuu muikale mupole, ne muikale ne mavuala makese, ne dikasa edi didi bu dia gnomon dijuka ne diela mundidimbi pa mutu pa buloba budi bunyanguke, tudi mua kuela meji ne: tshijengu tshia mâyi tshidi tshitshinyunguluke tshidi tshifuanangane ne tshiamu tshia nzembu, ne mushindu watshi ne milongo ya mêba idi ne mavuala mifundapu. Pa tshiamu tshia Ahaza atshi mundidimbi utu misangu ya bungi upingana panyima. Fin-Back ki mmuena tshisumbu to. Udi umueneka bu muena lukuna wa baleine, anu mudi bamue bantu bakine bantu. Muntu wa bundu; misangu yonso uya nkayebe; kubanda pa mutu pa mâyi mu tshimpitshimpi mu mayi a kule ne a tshinyangu; jet wende mululame ne umuepele mubandile udi ubanda bu difuma dinene dia tshinyangu pa tshibandabanda tshidi katshiyi ne bintu; udi ne tshipedi tshia bukole bua dikema ne lubilu lua kuowa mâyi, bua kukandamena malu onso adi muntu ukeba; leviathan eu udi umueneka bu Kayina wa mu tshisa tshiende uvuabu bipate ne kayi mua kutshimuna, muikale ne tshimanyinu tshia mushindu au pa mukolo wende. Ku dikala ne baleine mukana muende, Fin-Back utu imue misangu mubuela mu baleine wa ku dia balume, munkatshi mua mishindu ya baleine idibu babikila ne: Whalebone whales , mmumue ne: baleine idi ne baleine. Mu bintu ebi bidibu babikila ne: baleine ya baleine, bidi bimueneka ne: kudi mishindu ya bungi, nansha nanku, ya bungi ya kudiyi ki mmimanyike bimpe to. Baleine ya mishiku mialabale ne baleine ya mishiku; baleine ya mutu wa brochet; baleine misangisha; baleine idi muinshi mua mishiku ne baleine idi ne mishiku, ke mêna a balobi bua mishindu mikese.

Mu diumvuangana ne dibikila edi dia “baleine ya mifuba ya baleine,” bidi ne mushinga wa bungi bua kuamba ne: nansha mudi dibikila dia nunku mua kuikala dimpe bua kufila bilejilu bia mishindu kampanda ya baleine, kadi mbibi bua kuteta bua kulongolola Leviathan mu mushindu mutoke, bilondeshile baleen wende, anyi nsongo, anyi fin, anyi teeth; nansha mudibi bimueneka patoke ne: bitupa abi anyi ngikadilu idi mimanyike bimpe idi milongolola bimpe bua kufila nshindamenu wa ndongoluelu wa pa tshibidilu wa Cetology kupita dishilangana dionso dia mubidi didi ditapuluke, didi baleine, mu mishindu yende, uleja. Mmunyi pashishe? Baleen, hump, back-fin, ne menu; bintu ebi bidi ne ngikadilu yabi idi mitangalake mu mishindu yonso ya baleine, kakuyi kutangila tshidi mua kuikala mushindu udiyi mienza mu malu makuabu ne a mushinga mukole. Nunku, baleine wa mabele ne baleine wa mu tshiadi, yonso udi ne tshifulu; kadi kuntu aku difuanangana didi dijika. Pashishe, baleine umue umue eu ne baleine wa Groenland, yonso wa kudibu udi ne baleine; kadi kuntu aku kabidi difuanangana didi dijika. Ne bidi anu bia muomumue ne bitupa bikuabu bitudi batele kulu eku. Mu mishindu mishilashilangane ya baleine, idi yenza bisumbu bidi kabiyi biakane; anyi, mu tshilumbu tshia umue wa kudibu mutapuluke, ditapuluka dia nunku didi kadiyi dia pa tshibidilu; bu bua kubenga tshishiki mishindu yonso ya dienza dia malu mienza pa tshishimikidi tshia nunku. Pa dibue edi muntu yonso wa ku bamanyi ba malu a bufuki ba baleine mmutapuluke.

Kadi bidi mua kuikala biumvuika ne, mu bitupa bia munda bia baleine, mu mubidi wende—kuoku aku, nansha kakese, netufike ku dipeta mushindu muimpe wa kuenza mudimu. Tòo; tshilejilu, ntshinyi tshidi mu mubidi wa baleine wa mu Greenland tshidi tshikemesha kupita baleine wende? Kadi tudi bamone ne: ku diambuluisha dia baleine wende kabiena mua kuenzeka bua kuteka baleine wa mu Groenland mu tshisumbu to. Ne shi udi upuekela mu mala a leviathans mishilashilangane, bua tshinyi kuena mua kupeta dishilangana tshitupa tshia makumi atanu tshidi tshikumbane kudi muena bulongolodi bu mudi adi pambelu adibu bamane kutela. Ntshinyi tshidi tshishala pashishe? kakuena tshikuabu to, anu kukuata baleine ku mubidi, mu bungi buabu buonso bua budikadidi, ne kubasungula ne dikima mushindu au. Ne etshi ke ndongoluelu wa mikanda udibu bangate apa; ne i yeye nkayende udi mua kukumbaja, bualu yeye nkayende ke udi mua kuenzeka. Bua kutungunuka.

MUKANDA I. ( Folio ) SHAPITRE IV. ( Hump Back ).—Baleine eu utu umueneka misangu mivule ku muelelu wa mâyi wa ku Nord kua Amerike. Bavua bamukuate misangu ya bungi kuntu aku, ne bamukoka mu tshitudilu tshia mazuwa. Udi ne tshibombi tshinene pa mutu pende bu mupanyi; anyi udi mua kumubikila ne: Nzolo ne baleine wa Nzubu wa Bukalenge. Nansha biobikala mushindu kayi, dina dimanyike kadiena dimusunguluja to, bualu baleine udi kabidi ne tshiadi nansha muditshi tshikese. Mafuta ende kaena ne mushinga wa bungi to. Udi ne baleen. Udi mutambe buimpe ne udi ne muoyo mupepele kupita baleine yonso, udi wenza bintu bia bungi bia gay foam ne mâyi matoke kupita bakuabu bonso.

MUKANDA I. ( Folio ), SHAPITRE V. ( Razor Back ).—Bua baleine eu katuena bamanye malu a bungi to, anu dîna diende. Ndi mumumone kule ne Cape Horn. Bu mudiye ulekela mudimu, udi unyema bapote ne bena meji. Nansha mudiye kayi muena buôwa, ki mmuanji kuleja tshitupa nansha tshimue tshia mubidi wende to, anu mukolo wende udi ubanda mu tshibandabanda tshile tshikole. Muleke aye. Ndi mumanye malu makese pa bidi bitangila yeye, nansha muntu mukuabu kena mumanye to.

MUKANDA I. ( Folio ), SHAPITRE VI. ( Sulphur Bottom ).—Muntu mukuabu uvua uya ku pansio, uvua ne difu dia sufre, kakuyi mpata uvua mupete mudimu wa dikosa dia mabue a mu Tartare mu amue a ku mavuala ende a muinshi mua buloba. Katu utamba kumueneka to; nansha kakese tshitu muanji kumumona to, anu mu mbuu ya ku Sud, ne pashishe misangu yonso ku bule bua bungi bua kulonga mpala wende. Kabena bamulonda nansha kakese; uvua mua kunyema ne mionji ya mulongo. Badi bakula bua bualu buende. Adieu, Sulfur Bottom! Tshiena mua kuamba bualu bukuabu budi bulelela buebe wewe, nansha muena Nantucketer mukulumpe kena mua kuamba to.

MUKANDA II. ( Octavo ), SHAPITRE I. ( Grampus ).—Nansha mudi mushipa eu, udi ne mupuya wende wa dîyi dikole, anyi udi upuyakana, mupeshe bantu ba pa buloba lusumuinu, mmumanyike bimpe bu muena mu mâyi manene, kadi ki mmumanyike bikole bu muena mu mâyi manene to. Kadi bu mudiye ne ngikadilu yonso minene ya leviathan, bamanyi ba bungi ba malu a bufuki mbamumanye bu umue wa kudiyi. Udi ne bunene bua octavo bua munkatshi, budi bushilangane kubangila ku metre dikumi ne atanu too ne ku makumi abidi ne atanu mu bule, ne udi ne bunene budi bupetangana mu tshimono. Kudi bamue balobi badi bamona mushindu wende wa kuenza mudimu bu tshijadiki tshia dienda dia baleine munene wa sperme.

MUKANDA II. ( Octavo ), SHAPITRE II. ( Black Fish ).—Ndi mfila mêna a balobi ba mishipa yonso eyi, bualu pa tshibidilu ke idi mitambe buimpe. Padi dina kampanda kadiyi diumvuika bimpe anyi kadiyi dileja, nenkuamba nanku, ne nenkuleje dikuabu. Ndi ngenza nanku mpindieu, nlenga Mushipa Mufiike, udibu babikila ne:, bualu bufiike budi tshilele munkatshi mua baleine pabuipi ne yonso. Nunku, umubikile ne: Baleine wa Hyena, wewe musue. Bukole buende bua kudia budi bumanyike bimpe, ne bua mudi mishiku yende ya munda mitangile kuulu, udi ne tshimuenekelu tshia kashidi tshia Mephistophelean ku mpala kuende. Mushipa eu udi ne bule bua metre dikumi ne asambombo anyi dikumi ne muanda mukulu. Udi pabuipi ne mu latitude yonso. Udi ne mushindu wa pa buende wa kuleja dikasa diende dia mu nyima padiye wela mâyi, didi dimueneka bu diulu dia bena Lomo. Padibu kabayi benza mudimu bimpe, balobi ba baleine ba sperme batu imue misangu bakuata baleine wa Hyena, bua kulama mafuta a mushinga mukese bua kuenza nawu mudimu kumbelu—bu mudi bamue balami ba nzubu badi kabayi ne makuta a bungi, padibu kabayi ne bantu, ne badi nkayabu, bosha mafuta adi kaayi mimpe pamutu pa wax udi ne mupuya muimpe. Nansha mudi mafuta abu matekete, amue a ku baleine aa neakupeshe litre makumi asatu ya mafuta.

MUKANDA II. ( Octavo ), SHAPITRE III. ( Narwhale ), mmumue ne: baleine wa mu mishiku .—Tshilejilu tshikuabu tshia baleine udibu babikila mu mushindu wa dikema, udibu babikila nunku ndi ngela meji ne: bualu lusengu luende lua pa bualu luvuabu banji kuela meji ne: uvua ne mushiku wa nsongo. Tshifukibua etshi tshidi ne bule bua metre dikumi ne asambombo, eku lusengu luende lufika ku metre atanu, nansha mudi amue mapite pa dikumi, ne adi mene afika ku metre dikumi ne atanu. Mu kuamba kuimpe, lusengu elu ludi anu dinu dile, didi dipatuka ku tshiadi mu mulongo udi mupuekele kakese ku tshidi tshitangile ku luseke. Kadi udi anu ku luseke lubi, ludi ne buenzeji bubi, lupesha muenalu tshintu tshidi tshifuanangane ne tshimuenekelu tshia muntu wa tshianza tshia bakaji udi kayi ne lungenyi. Bidi mua kuikala bikole bua kuamba tshipatshila kayi tshilelela tshia lusengu lua meno a ntambue anyi muele wa mvita eu. Kawena umueneka bu udi wenza mudimu bu difuma dia mushipa wa mvita ne wa mushipa wa nsongo to; nansha mudi bamue bendeshi ba mazuwa bangambila ne: Narwhale utu wenza nende mudimu bua kukudimuna muinshi mua mbuu bua kupeta biakudia. Charley Coffin unena amba badi benza nabi mudimu bua tshiamu tshia ice-piercer; bualu bua Narwhale, udi ubanda pa mutu pa Mbuu wa Polar, ne umupeta muwule tente ne mabue a mashika, udi ubandisha lusengu luende, ne nunku udi utshibula. Kadi kuena mua kujadika ne: nansha umue wa ku malu adibu bela meji aa mmulelela to. Lungenyi luanyi nkayanyi ndua se: nansha mudi lusengu elu lua luseke lumue mua kuikala luenza mudimu kudi Narwhale—nansha biobi mua kuikala mushindu kayi—nebikale bimpe menemene kudiye bua kuikalaye ne tshibuta tshia mikanda ya kubala. Narwhale undi mumvue bamubikila ne: baleine wa meno, baleine wa nsengu, ne baleine wa Unicorn. Udi bushuwa tshilejilu tshia dikema tshia Unicornism tshidi tshipetangana pabuipi ne bukalenge buonso bua bintu bidi ne muoyo. Kudi bamue bafundi ba kale ba mu nzubu wa tshitendelelu ndi mupeta ne: lusengu lua nyunyi wa mu mbuu elu luvua mu matuku a kale luangatshibua bu buanga bunene bua kuluisha buanga, ne ke bualu kayi, dilongolola dialu divua difila mushinga mukole. Bavua kabidi bawuteka bua kuenza mukele udi unyanguka bua bakaji badi bafua, anu mudibu benza nsengu ya nyama wa ntambue mulume bua kulua hartshorn. Ku ntuadijilu uvua muangatshibue bu tshintu tshia dijinga dia kumanya. Mukanda mufike udi ungambila ne: Sir Martin Frobisher pavuaye mupingane ku luendu alu, pavua mukalenge mukaji Bess mumuele tshianza tshiende tshia mabue a mushinga mukole ku dididishi dia nzubu wa bukalenga wa Greenwich, pavua mazuwa ende a dikima enda apueka mu musulu wa Thames; "Pavua Sir Martin mupingane ku luendu alu," ke mudi Black Letter wamba, "pa binu biende, wakapesha mukalenge wende lusengu lunene lua Narwhale, luvua lulengeja mu nzubu wa bukalenge wa Windsor mutantshi mule." Mufundi mukuabu wa mu Irlande udi wamba ne: Earl wa Leicester, uvua muinamine panshi, wakapesha kabidi mukalenge wende lusengu lukuabu, lua nyama wa pa buloba wa bumuntu bua unicorn.

Narwhale udi ne tshimuenekelu tshilenga, tshidi tshifuanangane ne ntambue, udi ne dikala dia panshi ditoke bu mabele, muikale ne mabala mafike a tshijengu ne a bule. Mafuta ende mmapite a bungi, matoke ne mimpe; kadi kudi bikese, ne kabatu batamba kumulonda to. Udi utamba kuikala mu mbuu ya tshijengu.

MUKANDA II. ( Octavo ), SHAPITRE IV. ( Killer ).—Mu Nantucketer ki mmumanye malu makese pa bidi bitangila baleine eu to, kadi mumanyi wa malu a bufuki kena mumanye bualu nansha bumue to. Bilondeshile tshindi mumumone ku bule, ndi mua kuamba ne: uvua bunene bua grampus. Udi muena tshikisu tshikole—mushindu wa mushipa wa Feegee. Imue misangu utu ukuata baleine minene ya Folio ku mishiku, ne umulenga kuntu aku bu tshisuku, too ne padi nyama wa bukole eu utshioka too ne ku lufu. Mushipianganyi kena ulonda to. Tshitu muanji kumvua mafuta a mushindu kayi adiye nawu to. Dîna didibu bapeshe baleine eu didi mua kuikala dishilangane, bua mudiye kayi umvuika bimpe to. Bualu tuetu bonso tudi bashipianganyi, pa buloba ne pa mbuu; Bonapartes ne Sharks badimu.

MUKANDA II. ( Octavo ), SHAPITRE V. ( Thrasher ).—Muntu eu mmumanyike bua mukila wende, udiye wenza nende mudimu bu tshiamu tshia mvita bua kukuma baluishi bende. Udi ubanda pa mukolo wa baleine wa Folio, ne padiye wela mâyi, udi wenza mudimu wende pa kumukuma; bu mudi bamue balongeshi ba tulasa bapetangana pa buloba ku diambuluisha dia mushindu wa muomumue. Nansha nanku, malu makese ke adibu bamanye bua Thrasher kupita bua Killer. Bonso babidi badi bashipi ba mikenji, nansha mu mbuu idi kayiyi ne mikenji.

Ke mudi MUKANDA II ujika. ( Octavo ), ne udi utuadija MUKANDA III. ( Duodecimo ).

DUODECIMOES.—Mu bintu ebi mudi baleine mikese. I. Mushipa wa Huzza. II. Mushipa wa Algérine. III. Nyunyi wa mukana wa bukula.

Kudi aba badi kabayi banji kulonga bualu ebu, bidi mua kumueneka bu bualu bua dikema, bua se: mishipa idi kayiyi mipite bule bua metre anayi anyi atanu idi ne bua kuikala misangisha munkatshi mua BALENE—muaku udi, mu ngumvuilu wa bantu ba bungi, misangu yonso uleja lungenyi lua bunene. Kadi bifukibua bidibu batele kulu eku bu Duodecimoes bidi baleine idi kayiyi ne bilema, bilondeshile diumvuija dianyi dia tshidi baleine— mmumue ne: mushipa udi utua mishiku, ne mukila mutangile kuulu.

MUKANDA III. ( Duodecimo ), SHAPITRE 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Eu ke mushipa wa porpoise udi pa buloba bujima. Dîna edi ndifumine ku meme nkayanyi; bualu kudi mishindu mipite pa umue ya mbua, ne bidi bikengela kuenza bualu kampanda bua kubasunguluja. Ndi mmubikila nunku, bualu utu anu wela mu mayi adi asekesha, adi pa mbuu mualabale adi atungunuka ne kudiela mu diulu bu bifulu mu tshisumbu tshia bantu tshia mu dia matuku anayi a ngondo wa muanda mutekete. Tshimuenekelu tshiabu tshitamba kuikala tshianyishibue ne disanka kudi muena mazuwa. Buwule tente ne nyuma mimpe, batu anu bafuma ku mavuala a lupepele bua kuya ku lupepele. Badi bana ba balume badi misangu yonso ne muoyo kumpala kua lupepele. Badi babamona bu tshimanyishilu tshia diakalenga. Shi wewe nkayebe udi mua kutantamena masanka asatu paudi umona mishipa eyi, dîba adi diulu dikuambuluishe; nyuma wa manaya a buena Nzambi kawena munda muenu. Mushipa wa Huzza Porpoise udi mudia bimpa, munene neakupeshe litre umue muimpe wa mafuta mimpe. Kadi mâyi mimpe ne matekete adibu bumbushe mu mishiku yende adi ne mushinga wa bungi menemene. Bidi mu dilomba kudi benji ba mabwe a mushinga mukole ne benji ba nsa. Bena mazuwa batu bawuteka ku maboko abu. Munyinyi wa mbuji udi muimpe bua kudia, udi mumanye. Kabiena mua kuikala bikufikile mu lungenyi ne: nyama wa porpoise udi utua mishiku to. Bushuwa, mushiku wende mmukese menemene udi kauyi umueneka bipepele to. Kadi musangu udi ulonda paudi upeta mushindu, umutangile; ne pashishe neumone baleine munene wa Sperm nkayende mu tshimfuanyi tshikese.

MUKANDA III. ( Duodecimo ), SHAPITRE II. ( Algérine Porpoise ).—Muena tshinyangu. Tshibi be. Ndi ngela meji ne: udi anu mu Mbuu wa Pasifike. Udi munene ndambu kupita Huzza Porpoise, kadi udi anu bu wa muomumue. Mufuishe tshiji, ne yeye neakuluke ku requin. Ndi mumupuekeshe misangu ya bungi, kadi tshitu muanji kumumona mukuatshibue to.

MUKANDA III. ( Duodecimo ), SHAPITRE III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Mushindu munene wa Porpoise; ne udi anu mu Mbuu wa Pasifike, bilondeshile mudibu bamanye. Dîna dimuepele dia mu Anglais, didibu bamubikila too ne mpindieu, ndia balobi—Right-Whale Porpoise, bualu udi nangananga pabuipi ne Folio au. Mu tshimuenekelu, udi mushilangane mu mushindu kampanda ne Huzza Porpoise, bualu udi ne tshitupa tshikese tshia tshijengu ne tshia disanka; bulelela, udi ne tshimuenekelu tshimpe ne tshia muntu muimpe. Kena ne mapuapua ku mukolo to (ba porpoises bakuabu ba bungi badi nawu), udi ne mukila muimpe, ne mêsu a bena Inde adi ne mubidi wa hazel. Kadi mukana muende wa bukula udi unyanga bionso. Nansha mudi mukolo wende mujima too ne ku mapapa ende a ku luseke muikale wa mutshi wa sable mule, kadi mulongo wa mikalu, udi mushilangane ne tshimanyinu tshidi mu tshilamba tshia mazuwa, tshidibu babikila ne: “tshilamba tshitoke,” mulongo au udi umukosolola kumbukila ku mutshi too ne ku nyima, ne mekala abidi mashilangane, mufike kuulu ne mutoke kuinshi. Tshitoka tshidi ne tshitupa tshia mutu wende, ne mukana muende mujima, tshidi tshimuvuija bu uvua munyeme ku dikumbula dia tshinyangu dia mu tshibombi tshia biakudia. Bualu bubi menemene ne bua bukula! Mafuta ende adi anu bu a porpoise wa pa tshibidilu.

* * * * * *

Pa kumbusha DUODECIMO, bulongolodi ebu kabuena buenda to, bualu Porpoise ke baleine mutambe bukese. Pa mutu, udi ne Leviathans yonso ya lumu. Ino kudi tshisumbu tshia baleine badi kabayi bajadike, badi banyema, badi kabayi bamanye malu a bungi, badi, bu muena Amerika, ndi mumanye ku lumu, kadi ki mbua meme nkayanyi to. Nembabale bilondeshile mêna abu a tshibumba tshia kumpala; bualu pamuapa liste wa nunku udi mua kuikala ne mushinga kudi bakebuludi ba matuku atshilualua, badi mua kujikija tshindi natshi apa kadi tshindi mutuadije. Shi umo wa ku baleine udi ulonda, udi mua kukuata ne kuela tshimanyinu, dîba adi udi mua kubueja bipepele mu bulongolodi ebu, bilondeshile bunene buende bua Folio, Octavo, anyi Duodecimo:—Baleine wa Mushiku wa Mulondo; baleine wa Junk; baleine wa mutu wa pudding; baleine wa ku Cap; baleine wa kumpala; baleine wa Cannon; baleine wa Scragg; baleine wa mukuba; Baleine wa Nzolo; Iceberg Whale; baleine wa Quog; baleine wa bleu; ne bikuabu. Kudi bamfumu ba mu Islande, ba mu Pays-Bas ne ba mu Angleterre ba kale, badi mua kufila liste mikuabu ya baleine idi kayiyi mijadika, mibeneshibue ne mêna a mishindu yonso adi kaayi makane. Kadi ndi mbilekela bu bintu bia kale; ne kabiena mua kuambuluisha bua kuela meji ne: mbintu bia patupu, biuwule tente ne malu a Leviathanisme, kadi kabiyi biumvuija bualu nansha bumue.

Ndekelu wa bionso: Bavua bambe ku ntuadijilu, ne: bulongolodi ebu kabuvua mua kuikalaku to, ne diakamue, kubupuangane. Kuena mua kubenga kumona patoke ne: ndi mulame dîyi dianyi. Kadi mpindieu ndi nshiya bulongolodi buanyi bua cetologique buimane nunku kabuyi bujikije, anu muvua Nzubu munene wa nzambi wa Cologne mushale, ne tshiamu tshia nzembu tshitshidi tshiimane pa mutu pa tshibumba tshidi katshiyi tshijika. Bualu nzubu mikese idi mua kujika kudi bafundi babu ba kumpala; banene, balelela, batu bashiya dibue dia nshindamenu kudi ndelanganyi. Nzambi unlame bua kujikija bualu nansha bumue. Mukanda ewu mujima udi anu tshilejelu—to, kadi tshilejelu tshia tshilejelu. Oh, Dîba, Bukole, Makuta, ne Lutulu!
