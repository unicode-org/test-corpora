SHAPITUA 63. Tshitupa tshia mubidi.

Mu mutshi, matamba adi akola; munda muabu, matamba. Nunku, mu biena-bualu bidi bifila bipeta, vula nshapita.

Tshitupa tshia mubidi tshidibu batele mu dibeji dia kumpala tshidi tshikumbanyine bua kutshitela nkayatshi. Nunku tshingoma tshidi pabuipi ne muntu udi utshiela, udi utshiangata bipepele anu bu mudi muena mudimu wa mu mpata wela bingoma biende ku tshimanu. I divula dia mpunga misangu ibidi. Kadi misangu ya bungi bitu bienzeka ne: bua dinyema dia lukasa, dia tshikisu, dia dikankamika dia baleine padiye upeta tshiamu tshia kumpala, bidi bilua bikole bua muena tshingoma, nansha yeye muikale bu mupenyi mu ngendu yende, kuela tshiamu tshibidi munda muende. Nansha nanku, bu mudi tshiamu tshibidi tshikale tshilamakaja ne muonji, ne muonji wenda, ke bualu kayi tshingoma atshi tshidi ne bua kuikala, mu mishindu yonso, tshipatuka mu buatu, mu mushindu kampanda ne muaba kampanda; bikalabi kabiyi nanku, njiwu mikole menemene idi mua kukuata bianza bionso. Kuela mu mayi, bidi nanku mu nsombelu ya nunku; bipese bia pa mutu bia mulongo wa kazubu (bitela mu nshapita udi mupita) bidi bienza bua bualu ebu, misangu ya bungi, buenzeke bimpe. SHAPITA 65. Baleine bu dilongo.

Muntu udi ufua udi ne tshia kudia tshifukibua tshidi tshidisha muendu wende, ne, bu mudi Stubb, kumudia ku butoke buende nkayende, bu muudi mua kuamba; bualu ebu budi bumueneka bu bualu bua pa buabu budi muntu ne bua kubuela kakese mu malu a kale ne mu lungenyi lua bualu ebu.

Bidi bifunda, ne kukadi bidimu nkama isatu, ludimi lua baleine wa ku dia balume luvua luangatshibue bu tshiakudia tshinene mu France, ne luvua ne mushinga mukole kuntu aku. Kabidi, mu tshikondo tshia Henri VIII, muteki kampanda wa mu nzubu wa mukalenge wakapeta difutu dimpe bua muvuaye mupatule tshintu tshimpe tshia kudia ne nyama ya porpoises mioshe, idi, udi uvuluka, mushindu wa baleine. Porpoises, bushuwa, too ne lelu badi babamona bu badia bimpe. Munyinyi udi muenza bu mipinganyi ya bunene bua mipinganyi ya billiard, ne padiyi milengejibue bimpe ne milengejibue bimpe idi mua kuikala mipinganyi ya turtle-balls anyi ya nyama wa ngombe. Ba-monk ba kale ba mu Dunfermline bavua babanange bikole. Bavua ne dipa dinene dia porpoise dia kudi mukalenge.

Bualu bulelela budi ne: munkatshi mua balobi bende, baleine uvua mua kuikala muangatshibue bu tshiakudia tshia mushinga mukole, bu yeye kayiku; kadi paudi ulua kusomba kumpala kua tshiakudia tshia nyama tshia bule bua metre lukama, bidi bikumbusha dijinga dia kudia. Anu bantu badi kabayi ne kansungansunga bu mudi Stubb ke badi badia baleine miteka; kadi bena Esquimaux kabena ne tshikisu tshia bungi to. Tuetu bonso tudi bamanye mushindu udibu basombela pa baleine, ne tudi ne mafuta a kale a kale adi kaayi atamba kumueneka. Zogranda, umue wa ku baminganga babu bamanyike bikole, udi ufila lungenyi lua se: bana batekete badi ne mafuta a bungi, bualu adi ne mâyi a bungi ne adi akolesha mubidi. Ne bualu ebu budi bunvuluija ne: bamue bena Angleterre, bavua kale bashiye mu Greenland ku mpukapuka kudi buatu bua diloba dia baleine—ne: bantu aba bavua bushuwa basombele ngondo ya bungi pa bitupa bia baleine bivua ne nkunde bivuabu bashiye ku muelelu wa mâyi panyima pa bamane kuteta mafuta a baleine. Munkatshi mua bena baleine ba mu Pays-Bas batu babikila bintu ebi ne: “fritters”; tshidi, bushuwa, tshifuanangane bikole, tshikala tshikunze ne tshitoke, ne tshinunkila tshintu kampanda bu mudi mampa anyi oly-cooks a bakaji ba kale ba mu Amsterdam, padibi bipiabipia. Badi ne tshimuenekelu tshia kudia tshia nunku muenyi udi udibenga bikole kena mua kulama bianza biende to.

Kadi tshidi kabidi tshipepeja mushinga wa baleine bu tshiakudia tshimpe, mbubanji buende bua bungi. Yeye i ngombe munene wa difutu dia mbuu, udi ne mafuta a bungi bua kuikala muimpe. Tangila tshitupa tshienda, tshidi mua kuikala tshimpe tshia kudia anu bu tshia nyama wa mbuji (tshidibu bangata bu tshiakudia tshidi katshiyi tshitamba kumueneka), bu katshiyi tshibumba tshia mafuta tshikole nunku. Kadi spermaceti nkayende, mmunyi mudiye mutoke ne mutoke; bu mudi munyinyi mutoke wa tshimuma tshia nkunde mu ngondo muisatu wa dikola diawu, kadi muikale ne bintu bia bungi bia kupingana pa muaba wa manteka. Nansha nanku, bena baleine ba bungi batu ne mushindu wa kuwubueja mu tshintu tshikuabu, ne pashishe kuwudia. Mu tshikondo tshile tshia kuteta butuku, mbualu bua pa tshibidilu bua bena mazuwa kuela bishikudi biabu bia mu mazuwa mu bibombi binene bia mafuta ne kubilekela bikuate muaba au bua tshitupa tshîpi. Ndi muenze nunku biakudia bia dilolo bimpe bia bungi.

Mu tshilumbu tshia baleine mukese, buongo budi buangatshibua bu tshiakudia tshimpe. Tshibombi tshia tshifu tshidi tshikosolola ne tshingoma, ne bitupa bibidi binene, bitoke bidi bipatuka (bifuanangane menemene ne pudding ibidi minene), pashishe badi babisambakaja ne bukula, ne babiteka mu tshintu tshimpe tshitambe, tshidi ne dipembu difuanangane ne mutu wa ngombe, tshidi tshiakudia tshimpe munkatshi mua bamue ba-epicure; ne muntu yonso mmumanye ne: bamue bana ba ngombe munkatshi mua ba-epicure, padibu badia misangu yonso buongo bua bana ba ngombe, badi bapeta buongo bukese buabu nkayabu, bua kumona mua kusunguluja mutu wa ngombe ku mitu yabu; tshidi, bushuwa, tshilomba kansungansunga ka pa tshibidilu. Ne ke bualu kayi muana wa ngombe udi ne mutu wa muana wa ngombe udi umueneka bu wa meji kumpala kuende, udi mu mushindu kampanda tshimue tshia ku bintu bia dibungama biudi mua kumona. Mutu udi umutangila ne tshikisu, ne “Et tu Brute!” tshiambilu.

Kabiena, pamuapa, anu bualu baleine udi ne mafuta a bungi to, ke bualu kayi bena pa buloba badi bamueneka bu badi bamona didia diende ne lukuna; tshidi tshimueneka tshifumina, mu mushindu kampanda, ku dikonkonona didibu batele kumpala eku: mbuena kuamba ne : muntu udi ne bua kudia tshintu tshipiatshipia tshia mu mbuu tshidibu bashipe, ne kudia kabidi ku butoke buatshi nkayatshi. Kadi kakuyi mpata, muntu wa kumpala uvua mushipe ngombe uvua muangatshibue bu mushipianganyi; pamuapa bavua bamukuate ku mutshi; ne bu yeye mutekibue ku diteta diende kudi ngombe, uvua bushuwa mua kuikala mutekibue; ne uvua bushuwa mukumbanyine bikala mushipianganyi yonso wenza nanku. Ndaku mu tshisalu tshia nyama mu Disambombo butuku ne umone bisumbu bia nyama idi ne makasa abidi idi ne muoyo itangila milongo mile ya nyama idi ne makasa anayi mifue. Tshimuenekelu etshi katshienaku tshipatula dinu mu tshiadi tshia muntu udi udia bantu anyi? Bantu badi badia bantu anyi? nganyi udi kayi mudia bantu? Ndi nnuambila ne: nebikale bipepele bua Fejee uvua mushipe misionere mutekete mu nzubu wende wa muinshi mua buloba bua nzala ivua yenda ilua; Nebikale bipepele bua Fejee udi ne budimu, ndi ngamba, mu dituku dia dilumbuluisha, kupita wewe, muena tshididi mupiluke ne mutoke, udi ukuma mbuji panshi ne udia ne mifuba yabu mikole mu paté-de-foie-gras webe.

Kadi Stubb, udi udia baleine ku butoke buende nkayende, ki mmuomu anyi? ne bualu ebu budi buvuija tupendu ku bubi, ki mmuomu anyi? Tangila tshianza tshiebe tshia muele, kuntu aku, muena mudimu wanyi wa tshididi udi udia nyama wa ngombe muosha au, tshianza atshi ntshienza ne tshinyi?—tshinyi pa kumbusha mifuba ya muanabu ne ngombe uudi udia? Ne udi utapa meno ebe ne tshinyi, kunyima kua wewe mumane kudia mbuji wa mafuta au? Ne dikasa dia nyunyi wa muomumue. Ne mufundi wa Société pour la Supression de la Cruelté a Ganders uvua mufunde mikanda yende ne tshikandakanda kayi? Anu mu ngondo umue anyi ibidi mimane kupita ke muvua tshisumbu atshi tshiangate dipangadika dia kubenga kuanyisha tshintu tshikuabu to, anu bipese bia tshiamu.





SHAPITUA 66. Dishipangana dia Requin.

Padibu mu mudimu wa diloba wa ku Sud, baleine wa Sperm mukuate, kunyima kua mudimu mule ne mukole, balua nende butuku, ki mbilele bua kutuadija diakamue mudimu wa kumukosa to. Bualu mudimu au mmudimu mukole menemene; ki mmujikije lukasa to; ne bidi bikengela bianza bionso bua kubienza. Nunku, mudimu udibu batamba kuenza udi wa kuangata mazuwa onso; lash the helm a'lee; ne pashishe kutuma muntu yonso udi kuinshi ku tshitanda tshiende too ne mu munya, ne dipangadika dia se: too ne dîba adi, nebashale balami ba mazuwa; mbuena kuamba ne, babidi ne babidi mu diba dimua, mulume ne mukaji, bena mudimu mu tshijengu badi ne tshia kubanda pa tshibandilu bua kumona ni bionso bidi bienda bimpa.

Kadi imue misangu, nangananga pa Mulongo wa mu Mbuu wa Pasifike, plan eu kawena mua kuandamuna nansha kakese; bualu bua bisumbu bia bungi bia requin bidi bibuelakana pabuipi ne tshitalu tshilamakaja, bua se: yeye mushale nanku munkatshi mua mêba asambombo, tuambe ne: mu tshitupa kampanda, tshitupa tshikese tshia mufuba tshivua mua kumueneka mu dinda. Mu bitupa bikuabu bia bungi bia mbuu, nansha nanku, mudi mishipa eyi kayiyi mivule, didia diabu dia dikema didi mua kukepeshibua imue misangu bikole, padibu babanyunguluja ne bukole ne miele ya baleine mikole, nansha mudibi nanku, udi, imue misangu, umueneka anu bu udi ubasaka bua kuenza mudimu mukole. Kadi kabivua nanku mu tshilumbu tshia mpindieu ne requin ya Pequod to; nansha, bua kuikala mu bulelela, muntu yonso udi kayi muibidilangane ne bintu bia nunku, bua kuikala mutangile ku luseke luende butuku abu, uvua mua kuikala wela meji ne: mbuu mujima uvua tshijengu tshimue tshia fromaje, ne ba-requin abu bavua tuishi tua munda muawu.

Nansha nanku, pavua Stubb muteke tshitekelu tshia mazuwa panyima pa didia diende dia dilolo dimane kujika; ne pavua, bilondeshile bualu ebu, Queequeg ne muena mazuwa wa mu forecastle balue pa mazuwa, kakuvua disanka dikese diakafukibua munkatshi mua ba-requin to; bualu bua kuela diakamue bipese bia kukosa nabi bintu ku luseke, ne kupuekesha miendu isatu, bua yoyi kuela butoke bule pa mbuu mutoke, bena matu babidi aba, batuta bingoma biabu bile bia kuloba nabi baleine, batungunuka ne kushipa ba-requin kabayi balekela, * pa kukuma tshiamu tshia mitaku munda menemene mua mitu yabu, tshimueneka anu tshitupa tshia mitu yabu tshia mushinga mukole. Kadi mu tshimvundu tshia baluishi babu bavua basambakane ne bavua baluangana, bena mvita kabavua misangu yonso mua kukuma tshipatshila tshiabu to; ne bualu ebu buakafila dibuluibua dipiadipia dia tshikisu tshia dikema tshia muluishi. Bavua batuta ne tshikisu, ki nganu ku dikosa dia mifuba ya muntu ne muntu to, kadi bu mikete mikole, mikoseka, ne misuma yabu; too ne pavua bitupa bia munda abi bimueneka bu bivua bimina misangu ne misangu kudi mukana umue, bua kuikala bipatuka mu mushindu mukuabu kudi mputa uvua mupatuke. Kabidi kabivua bionso to. Kabivua bimpa bua kubuela mu bitalu ne nyuma ya bifukibua ebi to. Mushindu kampanda wa bukole bua pa tshibidilu anyi wa Pantheisme uvua umueneka bu uvua musokome mu bitupa biabu ne mu mifuba yabu, panyima pa tshivuabu mua kubikila ne: muoyo wa muntu pa nkayende tshimane kujika. Mushipa ne mubandisha pa mazuwa bua dikoba diende, umue wa ku ba-requin aba uvua pabuipi ne kuangata tshianza tshia Queequeg mupele, pavuaye uteta bua kukanga tshibuikidiji tshifue tshia mpala wende wa lufu.

*Tshiamu tshia kuloba natshi baleine tshidibu benza natshi mudimu bua kukosa tshidi tshienza ne tshiamu tshia mitaku tshimpe menemene; udi utangila bunene bua tshianza tshia muntu tshitangalake; ne mu tshimuenekelu tshijima, tshidi tshipetangana ne tshiamu tshia mudimu tshia mu budimi tshidibu batshipeshe dîna; anu nseke yende ke idi mibalakane bimpe, ne ndekelu wende wa kuulu udi mukese bikole kupita wa kuinshi. Tshia mvita etshi tshidi misangu yonso tshilama bimpe menemene; ne padiye wenza mudimu udi ulengejibua imue misangu, anu bu tshiamu tshia kukosa natshi mishiku. Mu tshitekelu tshiawu, mutshi mukole, wa bule bua metre makumi abidi to ne makumi asatu, udi mubueja bua kukuata.

"Queequeg kena uditatshisha bua tshinyi nzambi uvua mumuvuije requin," ke muakamba muena tshisuku, wenda ubandisha tshianza tshiende mulu ne panshi ne kanyinganyinga; "Nzambi wa Fejee anyi nzambi wa Nantucket; kadi nzambi uvua mufuke requin udi ne bua kuikala umue Ingin."
