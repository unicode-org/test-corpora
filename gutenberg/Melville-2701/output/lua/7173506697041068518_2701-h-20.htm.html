Ku tshitupa tshiabu tshia muele wa mvita mu tshifu, bakuidi bakamona meme ngangata bule bua tshiadi tshia ndekelu, "Mmunyi mpindieu!" bakela mbila; "Udi mua kupima nzambi wetu eu, udi wetu." "Eyowa, bakuidi—bimpe, nudi mua kumuenza too ne dîba kayi?" Kadi pashishe, dikokangana dikole diakajuka munkatshi muabu, pa bidi bitangila maulu ne santimetre; bakapandangana ne mitshi yabu ya tshipiminu—tshitupa tshinene tshiakakuma—ne meme kuangata diakalenga adi, ngakajikija ne lukasa luonso bipiminu bianyi.

Bipimu ebi ndi mpindieu musue kunuteka kumpala kuenu. Kadi tshia kumpala, bikale bifunda, ne, mu bualu ebu, tshiena ne budikadidi bua kuamba tshipiminu nansha tshimue tshindi musue to. Bualu kudi bamfumu ba mifuba baudi mua kutangila, bua kuteta bujalame buanyi. Badi bangambila ne: kudi nzubu wa tshilaminu tshia bintu bia kale wa Leviathanic, mu Hull, mu Angleterre, umue wa ku miaba idibu baloba baleine mu ditunga adi, mudibu ne bimfuanyi bilenga bia baleine ya fin-backs ne baleine mikuabu. Bia muomumue, ndi mumvue ne: mu nzubu wa tshilaminu tshia bintu bia kale wa mu Manchester, mu New Hampshire, badi ne tshidi bamfumu ba nzubu babikila ne: “tshilejilu tshimuepele tshimpe tshia baleine wa mu musulu wa Greenland anyi wa mu musulu wa mu États-Unis.” Kabidi, muaba kampanda mu Yorkshire, mu Angleterre, Burton Constable ku dina, Sir Clifford Constable kampanda udi ne mufuba wa Sperm Whale, kadi wa bunene bua pa tshibidilu, kayi ne bunene bua mulunda wanyi Mukalenge Tranquo to.

Mu bilumbu bionso bibidi, baleine mishikile idi mifuba ibidi eyi mikale, ivua milomba kudi bamfumu babu bua malu a muomumue. Mukalenge Tranquo wangata wende bualu uvua musue; ne Sir Clifford, bualu uvua mfumu wa bamfumu ba mu bitupa abi. Baleine wa Sir Clifford mmuyukidibua mu tshitupa tshionso; nunku, bu tshibutshilu tshinene tshia bintu, udi mua kumukangula ne kumukanga, mu miaba yende yonso ya mifuba—kutangalaja mifuba yende bu tshipepele tshinene—ne kumunyunguluka dituku dijima pa tshiadi tshiende tshia kuinshi. Badi ne tshia kuteka makunji ku bimue bia ku biibi biende bia mateyi ne ku bikunyibua; ne muendeshi wa makasa nealeje benyi ba matuku atshilualua ne tshisumbu tshia nsapi ku luseke luende. Sir Clifford udi wela meji a kulomba makuta abidi bua kutangila tshibumba tshia mukolo tshidi mu mukolo; threepence bua kumvua tshiona mu tshina tshia buongo buende; ne sixpence bua tshimuenekelu tshia patupu tshia ku mpala wende.

Bunene bua mifuba bundi ne bua kutungunuka ne kuteka panshi budi bufunda muaku ku muaku ku diboko dianyi dia balume, muaba umvua muenze tatuaje; anu bu mu ngendu yanyi ya tshisuku tshikondo atshi, kakuvua mushindu mukuabu muimpe wa kulama bungi bua mushinga nunku to. Kadi bu mumvua muwule tente ne muaba, ne mvua musue bua bitupa bikuabu bia mubidi wanyi bishale anu dibeji dia tshianana bua tshikuma tshimvua mfunda tshikondo atshi—nansha, bitupa kayi bivua kabiyi bifunda bivua mua kushala—tshivua muditatshishe ne santimetre mishilangane to; nansha, bulelela, inches kayena ne tshia kubuela mu tshipiminu tshimpe tshia baleine to.





SHAPITA 103. Dipima dia mufuba wa baleine.

Mu muaba wa kumpala, ndi musue kunuleja meyi kampanda, patoke, adi atangila tshitupa tshinene tshia muoyo tshia leviathan eu, udi mufuba wende utudi ne bua kuleja mu tshikoso. Mêyi a nunku adi mua kuikala ne mushinga muaba eu.

Bilondeshile dibala dimpe dimvua muenze, ne ndi mushindamene pa dibala dia Kapitene Scoresby, dia tone makumi asambombo bua baleine munene wa mu Greenland wa bule bua metre makumi asambombo; bilondeshile dibala dianyi dimpe, ndi ngamba ne: baleine wa Sperm wa bunene bua bungi, pankatshi pa metre makumi asambombo ne atanu ne makumi asambombo mu bule, ne tshintu tshidi muinshi mua metre makumi anayi mu tshijengu tshiende tshijima, baleine wa nunku neikale ne bujitu bua tone makumi asambombo; nunku, pa kubala bantu dikumi ne basatu ku tone, uvua mua kupita bungi bua bantu bonso ba mu musoko mujima wa bantu tshinunu ne lukama.

Kuenaku wela meji ne: buongo, bu mudi ngombe mikanga, budi ne bua kutekibua kudi leviathan eu, bua kumuenza bua ikale mu lungenyi lua muntu yonso wa pa buloba anyi?

Bu mundi mumane kukuleja mu mishindu mishilashilangane tshifu tshiende, tshina tshia mukana, tshiadi, meno, mukila, mpala, mapapa, ne bitupa bikuabu bia bungi, mpindieu nenkuleje anu tshidi tshitamba kusankisha mu tshitupa tshinene tshia mifuba yende idi kayiyi mikanga. Kadi bu mudi tshifuba tshinene tshikuata tshitupa tshinene tshia bunene buonso bua tshifuba; bu mudibi tshitupa tshikole menemene; ne bu mudiku kakuyi bualu budibu ne bua kupingaja pa bidi bitangila bualu ebu mu nshapita eu, kuena ne bua kupanga kubumbula mu lungenyi luebe, anyi muinshi mua diboko diebe, patudi tuenda tuenda, tshianana kuakupeta lungenyi lujima lua bulongolodi buonso butudi pabuipi ne kumona to.

Mu bule, mufuba wa Sperm Whale ku Tranque uvua ne bule bua metre makumi asambombo ne abidi; nunku padiye mumane kuikala ne muoyo mujima ne mualabale mu muoyo, uvua ne bua kuikala ne bule bua metre makumi asambombo; bualu mu baleine, mufuba udi ujimija bule bua tshitupa tshimue tshia bitanu patudi tumufuanyikija ne mubidi udi ne muoyo. Mu maboko makumi asambombo ne abidi aa, tshifu tshienda ne mpala wende bivua ne maboko makumi abidi, bishiya maboko matue ku makumi atanu a mufuba wa mukolo. Ku mufuba wa mukolo eu, bua tshintu tshidi katshiyi tshifika ku tshia bisatu tshia bule buawu, kuvua tshibombi tshinene tshia mifuba tshivua tshibuikila tshitupa tshiende tshia muoyo.

Buanyi meme, tshifukilu etshi tshinene tshia mifuba ya meno a ntambue, ne mukolo mule udi kauyi mulengejibue, mutangile kule natshi mu mulongo mululame, katshivua tshifuanangane ne tshilamba tshia buatu bunene bupiabupia buvuabu bateka pa mitshi, pavuabu babueje anu mifuba yende mitue ku makumi abidi ya butaka, ne tshilamba tshia ku makasa tshivua, bua tshikondo kampanda, mutshi mule, kadi mukosolola.

Mifuba ivua dikumi ku luseke lumue. Tshia kumpala, tshia kutuadija ku nshingu, tshivua ne bule bua metre matue ku asambombo; tshibidi, tshisatu, ne tshinayi bivua bilondangane bipite bule, too ne paudi ufika ku tshibungubungu tshia tshitanu, anyi tshimue tshia ku mifuba ya pankatshi, tshivua ne bule bua metre muanda mukulu ne ndambu wa santimetre. Kumbukila ku tshitupa atshi, mifuba ivua mishale yakakepela, too ne ku tshia dikumi ne tshia ndekelu tshivua anu ne bule bua metre atanu ne ndambu wa santimetre. Mu bunene buonso, bionso bivua ne dipetangana dimpe ne bule buabi. Mifuba ya pankatshi ivua mikole menemene. Mu imue Arsacides badi benza nayi mudimu bu mitshi idibu mua kuenza nayi bimanu bia njila ya makasa pa misulu mikese.

Patudi tuela meji ku mifuba eyi, tshivua mua kubenga kukema kabidi ne bualu ebu, budibu bapingaje mu mukanda eu mu mushindu mushilangane, bua se: mufuba wa baleine ki mmufuanyikiji wa tshimuenekelu tshiende to. Tshitupa tshinene tshia mifuba ya Tranque, tshimue tshia munkatshi, tshivua tshiangata tshitupa tshia mushipa tshidi, mu muoyo, tshinene mu buondoke. Mpindieu, bunene bua mubidi wa baleine eu budi ne bua kuikala bu metre dikumi ne asambombo; kadi, tshiadi tshidi tshipetangana natshi tshivua ne bunene bua metre muanda mukulu. Nunku mufuba eu uvua anu uleja tshitupa tshia lungenyi lulelela lua bunene bua muoyo bua tshitupa atshi. Kabidi, mu mushindu kampanda, muaba umvua mpindieu mmona anu mukolo wa munda mutupu, bionso bivua kumpala bibuikila ne bintu bia bungi bia mubidi, mifuba, mashi ne mala. Nansha nanku, bua bipese bia bungi, mvua mumone anu bitupa bikese bia mubidi bivua kabiyi bilongolola; ne pa muaba wa bintu bia bujitu ne bia butumbi, kadi kabiyi ne mifuba, tshintu tshia tshianana!

Nunku, ngakela meji ne: mbualu bua tshianana ne bupote bua muntu udi kayi muanji kuenza ngendu udi ne buôwa bua kuteta bua kumvua bimpe baleine wa dikema eu, pa kutangila anu mufuba wende mufue mutekete, muolola mu tshisuku etshi tshia ditalala. Tòo. Anu mu mutshima wa njiwu ya lukasa; anu padiye munda mua tshikondo tshia tshiji tshienda; anu pa mbuu munene udi kayi ne mikalu, ke mudibu mua kupeta baleine udi ne muoyo mujima.

Kadi mukolo. Bua bualu ebu, mushindu mutambe buimpe utudi mua kuela meji udi, ne tshiamu tshia nzembu, bua kuela mifuba yende muulu. Kakuyi mudimu wa lukasa. Kadi mpindieu mbimane kujika, bidi bimueneka anu bu dikunji dia Pompey.

Kudi mifuba makumi anayi ne mishilangane mu kabujima, idi mu mufuba kayiyi mikanga pamue. Bidi bitamba kulala bu mabue manene a nsongo pa tshibumba tshia Gothic, bienza njila mikole ya mabue makole. Tshinene, tshia munkatshi, tshidi ne bule bua metre makese kupita maboko asatu, ne mu butshiama bupite pa inayi. Tshikese menemene, mudi mutshi wa mukolo wenda uya too ne ku mukila, tshidi anu ne butshiama bua santimetre ibidi, ne tshidi tshimueneka bu mupimbu wa billard mutoke. Bakangambila ne: kutshivua anu mikese, kadi ivua mijimine kudi tuishi tukese tudi tudia bantu, bana ba nsaserdose, bavua babibe bua kunaya nabi mabue a marbre. Nunku tudi tumona mudi mutshi wa tshikokedi nansha wa bintu binene bidi ne muoyo unyanguka ndekelu wa bionso mu manaya a bana.





SHAPITA 104. Baleine wa kale.

Ku bunene buende bunene, baleine udi ufila tshiena bualu tshimpe tshia kudiundisha, kukolesha, ne kualabaja. Udi mua kuenza nanku, kuena mua kumuenzeja to. Ku diambuluisha dia makokeshi mimpe, udi ne tshia kuenzela anu malu mu mukanda wa bukalenge. Bua kubenga kuamba kabidi bule buende kumbukila ku spiracle too ne ku mukila, ne yards idiye upima pabuipi ne tshimono; Ela meji anu bua dishintuluka dinene dia mifuba yende, mudiyi milala munda muende bu nkamba minene ne hawsers mibuikila kule mu tshibandilu tshia muinshi mua buloba tshia mulongo wa mazuwa a mvita.

Bu mundi mudisuike bua kuenza mudimu ne Leviatana eu, bidi bikengela meme kuitaba bua meme kuikala mumanye malu onso mu mudimu eu; kayi ulengulula tuishi tukese tua mashi ende, ne kumunyungulula too ne ku ndekelu kua mala ende. Bu muvuaye mumane kumumvuija mu malu ende a bungi a muaba uvuaye musombele ne a mubidi wende, mpindieu bidi bishala bua kumubandisha mu mushindu wa malu a kale, a bintu bia kale ne a kumpala kua mvula wa kabutu. Padibu bakula bua tshifukibua tshikuabu tshionso pa kumbusha Leviyatana—ku nsese anyi ku nsese—miaku ya nunku idi mua kuikala miangatshibue bu idi kayiyi ne bualu. Kadi padi Leviatana muikale mukanda, tshilumbu tshidi tshishintuluka. Ndi mupangile bua kufika ku tshitudilu etshi muinshi mua miaku ya bujitu ya mu dictionnaire. Ne apa tudi tuamba ne: dîba dionso divuabi bikumbane bua kuebeja muntu kampanda mu tshikondo tshia mikanda eyi, mvua anu ngenza mudimu ne mukanda munene wa Johnson uvua musumbibue bua bualu abu; bualu bua mufundi wa miaku au uvua ne bujitu bua pa buabu buakamuambuluisha bua kulongolola miaku bua kuenza nayi mudimu kudi mufundi wa baleine bu meme.

Misangu ya bungi muntu utu umvua bafundi badi bajuka ne bavula ne tshiena bualu tshiabu, nansha muditshi mua kumueneka bu tshia patupu. Mmunyi, nunku, ne meme, difunda dia Leviatana eu? Mu tshiyi mumanye, chirographie wanyi udi wenda uvula too ne ku maleta manene. Mpesha tshilamba tshia nyunyi wa condor! Mpesha tshina tshia Vesuvius bua tshitekelu tshia encre! Balunda, kuatayi maboko anyi! Bualu mu tshienzedi tshia patupu tshia kufunda ngenyi yanyi ya Leviathan eu, badi bantshiokesha, ne bantekesha mu mikolo ne dimanya diabu dia malu a bungi dia dikomba, bienze anu bu ne: badi babueja tshisumbu tshijima tshia malu a sianse, ne bipungu bionso bia baleine, ne bantu, ne mastodons, bia kale, bia mpindieu, ne bidi bilua, ne bintu bionso bidi bienda bishintuluka bia bukalenge pa buloba bujima, ne pa buloba bujima, ki mbua tshibuashibuashi to pa kumbusha misoko yende. Ke bualu kayi, ne udi utumbisha, ke buimpe bua tshiena bualu tshinene ne tshia budikadidi! Tudi tuvula too ne ku tshitupa tshiende tshinene. Bua kupatula mukanda wa bukole, udi ne tshia kusungula tshiena bualu tshia bukole. Kakuena mukanda munene ne wa kashidi udi mua kufunda pa tshisuku to, nansha mudiku bantu ba bungi badi batete.

Kumpala kua meme kubuela mu tshiena bualu tshia Fossil Whales, ndi nfila bumanyishi buanyi bu mumanyi wa malu a buloba, pa kuamba ne: mu tshikondo tshianyi tshia malu mashilangane mvua muena mabue, ne kabidi muena mudimu munene wa dimbula dia milonda, misulu ne mishimi, nzubu ya mvinyo, nzubu ya muinshi mua buloba, ne bisuku bia mishindu yonso. Bia muomumue, mu mushindu wa kumpala, ndi musue kuvuluija mubadi, ne: nansha mu tshitupa tshia kumpala tshia buloba mudibu bapete bifukibua bia nyama idi mpindieu pabuipi ne kujimina; bintu bia kale bivuabu bapete mu tshidibu babikila ne: tertiaire formations bidi bimueneka bu bidi bipetangana, anyi mu mushindu kayi wonso bipetangana, pankatshi pa bifukibua bidi kabiyi bia kale, ne aba badibu bamba ne: ndelanganyi yabu ya kule ivua mibuele mu Mushete; Baleine yonso ya Fossile idibu bapete too ne mpindieu idi ya mu tshikondo tshia Tertiaire, tshidi tshia ndekelu tshidi kumpala kua dienza dia bintu bia pa mutu. Ne nansha mudiku nansha umue wa kudibu kayi wandamuna bimpe ku mishindu yonso ya nyama idi mimanyike ya tshikondo etshi, idi anu mifuanangane nayi mu malu onso, bua kubingisha diangatshibua diabu bu bintu bia kale bia mu Cetacean.

Bifukibua bia baleine ya kumpala kua Adamite, bitupa bia mifuba yabu ne mifuba yabu, mu bidimu makumi asatu bishale ebi, mu bikondo bishilangane, bivua bipetekibua kuinshi kua mikuna ya Alpes, mu Lombardie, mu France, mu Angleterre, mu Écosse, ne mu matunga a Louisiane, Mississippi ne Alabama. Munkatshi mua bintu bidi bishala bia dikema mudi tshitupa tshia tshifu, tshivuabu bumbushe mu tshidimu tshia 1779 mu Rue Dauphine mu Paris, njila muipi uvua unzuluka pabuipi ne nzubu wa bukalenge wa Tuileries; ne mifuba ivuabu bumbushe pavuabu bumbula mazuwa manene a mu Anvers, mu tshikondo tshia Napoléon. Cuvier unena amba bitupa ebi byadi bya mishindu kampanda ya Leviathanic idi kayiyi mimanyike nansha kakese.

Ino kintu kikatampe bininge kupita bintu byonso bya mu Cetacean i mufuba mukatampe wa kintu kikatampe kyadi kijimine, kyapeta mu mwaka wa 1842, mu budimi bwa Judge Creagh, mu Alabama. Bapika bavua ne buôwa bavua pabuipi apu bakangata bu mufuba wa umue wa ku banjelu bavua bakuluke. Baminganga ba mu Alabama bakamba ne: uvua nyama munene wa tshisuku, ne bakamupesha dîna dia Basilosaurus. Ino imue mifuba ya tshilejelu tshiayi pavuabu baya nayi dia muamua dia mbuu kudi Owen, mumanyi wa malu a mubidi wa mu Angleterre, biakamueneka ne: tshintu etshi tshivuabu bamba ne: tshivua baleine, nansha muvuatshi tshifue. Tshilejilu tshinene tshia bualu ebu, tshidibu bapingaja misangu ne misangu mu mukanda eu, tshia se: mufuba wa baleine udi ufila anu tshijadiki tshikese tshia mushindu udi mubidi wende mujima. Nunku Owen wakapingaja dîna dia tshintu tshibi tshia Zeuglodon; ne mu mukanda wende uvuabu babale kumpala kua London Geological Society, wakamba ne: uvua umue wa ku bifukibua bia dikema bivua dishintuluka dia buloba dijimije.

Pandi muimane munkatshi mua mifuba ya bukole ya Leviathan eyi, mitu, menu, mishiku, mifuba, ne mifuba, bionso ebi bidi bifuanangane ne mishindu ya nyama ya mu mbuu idiku; kadi musangu umua udi ne malanda a muomumue ne Leviathans antichronical mubutula, bakulumpe babu badi kabayi mua kubala; Ndi, kudi mvula wa bungi, mupingajibua ku tshikondo tshia dikema atshi, kumpala kua dîba nkayadi mua kuamba ne: ndituadije; bualu tshikondo tshiakatuadija ne muntu. Apa, tshimvundu tshia Saturne tshidi tshinyunguluka, ne ndi mpeta tshimuenenu tshitekete, tshidi tshinyunguluka mu bikondo bia kashidi bia Polar; pavua bimanu bia mabue a mashika bikuate bikole pa tshidi mpindieu Tropique; ne mu bule buonso bua kilometre 25 000 ya tshijengu tshia buloba ebu, kakuvua bunene bua tshianza tshimue tshia buloba buvua mua kuikala bumueneka to. Pashishe buloba bujima buvua bua baleine; ne, mukalenge wa bufuki, wakashiya njila wende mu milongo ya mpindieu ya Andes ne Himmalehs. Nnganyi udi mua kuleja ndelanganyi bu mudi Leviyatane? Tshingoma tshia Ahaba tshivua tshiele mashi a kale kupita a Palô panshi. Metusela udi umueneka bu muana wa mu kalasa. Ndi ntangila bua kuela Shem tshianza. Ndi ne buôwa bua bungi bua dikalaku edi dia antemosaïque, dia tshianana dia buôwa budi kabuyi mua kuakula bua baleine, budi, bu mudibi kumpala kua bikondo bionso, budi ne bua kuikalaku kunyima kua bikondo bionso bia bantu bimane kujika.

Kadi ki nganu Leviathan eu ke uvua mushiye bimanyinu biende bia kumpala kua adamite mu mabeji a bufuki, ne mu mabue a calcaire ne a marle uvua mushiye tshimfuanyi tshiende tshia kale to; kadi pa mabue a mu Ejipitu, adi kale adi amueneka bu adi aleja ne: adi ne ngikadilu wa mabue a kale, tudi tupeta tshimfuanyi tshia dikasa diende didi kadiyi mua kuela mpata. Mu nzubu kampanda wa ntempelo munene wa Denderah, bidimu bitue ku makumi atanu bishale ebi, bakapeta pa tshibandilu tshia mabue a granite tshimfuanyi tshisonga ne tshipenta, tshiuwule tente ne centaurs, griffins ne dolphins, bifuanangane ne bimfuanyi bia dikema bidi pa buloba bua mu diulu bua matuku etu aa. Pavuaye wenda unyunguluka munkatshi muabu, Leviyatana mukulakaje uvua wela mâyi bu wa kale; uvuaku wenda uya mu tshibuashibuashi atshi, bidimu nkama ya bungi kumpala kua Solomo kulelabu.

Kabidi, katuena ne bua kulekela tshijadiki tshikuabu tshia dikema tshia kale tshia baleine, mu nsombelu wende wa panyima pa mvula wa kabutu, bu mudibi bileja kudi muena kanemu John Leo, ngenda-mushinga wa kale wa mu Barbary.

“Kakuyi mutantshi mule ne Mbuu, badi ne ntempelo, mitshi yende ne mitshi yende mienza ne mifuba ya baleine, bualu bua baleine ya bunene bua dikema itu misangu ya bungi ifua ku muelelu au. Bantu ba patupu badi bela meji ne: ku diambuluisha dia bukole busokoka budi Nzambi mupeshe ntempelo, baleine nansha umue kena mua kupitshilamu kayi mufue diakamue to. ku luseke luonso lua Nzubu wa Nzambi, kudi mabue adi atuta kilometre ibidi mu Mbuu, ne atapa Baleine padiwu afika pa mutu pawu. Badi balama Mufuba wa Baleine wa bule bua dikema bua Tshishima, udi mulale pa buloba ne tshitupa tshiende tshibumbakane tshidi kuulu menemene, wenza tshibumba, mutu watshi kawena mua kufika kudi Baleine to. (udi wamba ne: John Leo) badi bamba ne: uvua mulale muaba au bidimu lukama kumpala kua meme kumona bualu ebu.

Mu ntempelo wa baleine wa mu Afrike eu ndi nkushiya, mubadi, ne wewe muikale muena Nantucketer, ne muena baleine, neutendelelemu mu musokoko.





SHAPITA 105. Bunene bua baleine budiku bukepela anyi?—Nebujimine anyi?

Nunku, bu mudi Leviatana eu ulua kutudi ufumina ku mutu kua mayi a kashidi, bidi mua kukonkibua bimpe, bikalaye, mu tshikondo tshile tshia bipungu biende, ki mmunyanguke ku tshitupa tshia ntuadijilu tshia bankambua bende to.
