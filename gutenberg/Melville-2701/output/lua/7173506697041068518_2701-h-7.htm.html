Kabidi dibue dia tshiamu tshia mitaku divua bena mpungi bambula mu mifuku yabu, bua mikete yabu ne bia mvita bikuabu; ne mabue a tshiamu, pa bidia bia dilolo, bavua bakolesha miele yabu mu mushindu wa kudileja; mutoyi wa tshiji au kauvua mua kusankisha Dough-Boy mupele to. Mmunyi muvuaye mua kupua muoyo ne: mu matuku ende a mu tshidila, Queequeg, bu umue, uvua ne bua kuikala muenze amue malu a dishipangana, a dipanga dia lungenyi. Aa! Dough-Boy! mudimu mukola wa muena mudimu mutoke udi windila bantu badi badia bantu. Kena ne bua kuambula tshilamba tshia kujingila ku diboko, kadi mukaba. Mu tshikondo tshimpe, nansha nanku, bua disanka diende dinene, basalayi basatu ba mu mbuu wa mukele bavua ne bua kujuka ne kuya; ku matshi ende adi kaayi malelela, adi aleja mianu, mifuba yabu yonso ya mvita idi ilenga munda muabu ku tshitupa tshionso, bu bingoma bia bena Maure mu bibombi.

Kadi, nansha muvua bena tshinyangu aba badia mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi, ne bavua basombelamu; nansha nanku, bu muvuabu kabayi basombe mu bibidilu biabu, kabavua batamba kuikalamu to, anu mu bikondo bia kudia, ne kumpala kua dîba dia kulala, pavuabu bapitamu bua kuya mu nzubu yabu ya pa buayi.

Mu bualu ebu, Ahaba kavua umueneka bu muntu mukuabu kudi bamfumu ba bungi ba baleine ba mu Amerike to, bavua, bu tshisumbu, basue kuela meji ne: bilondeshile bukenji buabu, nzubu wa mazuwa uvua wabu; ne se, anu ku bulenga nkayabu ke kudi muntu mukuabu yonso, dîba kayi dionso, muanyishibue kuntu aku. Nunku, mu bulelela, balunda ne bena tshingoma ba Pequod badi mua kuamba bimpe ne: bavua basombele pambelu pa nzubu pamutu pa kuikalamu. Bualu pavuabu babuelamu, bivua anu bu tshiibi tshia mu njila tshidi tshibuela mu nzubu; kukudimuka munda bua tshitupa tshîpi, anu bua kukudimuka pambelu; ne, bu tshintu tshia kashidi, tshisombela mu lupepele. Kabidi kabavua bajimije bia bungi to; mu kazubu kakuvua bulunda to; mu nsombelu, Ahaba kavua mua kufika to. Nansha muvuabu bamubueje mu dibala dia bantu dia Bukua-Buena-Kristo, uvua anu muenyi kudibu. Uvua musombele pa buloba, bu muvua wa ndekelu wa Grisly Bears musombele mu Missouri. Ne pavua tshikondo tshia printemps ne été tshimane, Logan wa muitu au, mudijike mu tshina tshia mutshi, uvua musombele mu muvu wa mashika kuntu aku, unua maboko ende nkayende; nunku, mu bukulakaje buende bua dibungama, anyima wa Ahaba, mubuikila mu tshina tshia mubidi wende, uvua udia ku makasa a mîdima yende!





SHAPITUA 35. Mutu wa tshibumba.

Mu tshikondo tshia luya luimpe, mu tshikondo tshia dishintuluka diakane ne bena mazuwa bakuabu, mutu wanyi wa kumpala wa mazuwa wakalua kunyunguluka.

Mu baleine ba bungi ba mu Amerike, mitu ya mazuwa idi ne bantu pabuipi ne musangu umue ne padi mazuwa afuma ku tshitudilu tshiawu; nansha mudiye mua kuikala ne kilometre binunu dikumi ne bitanu, ne kupita apu, bua kuendela kumpala kua kufikaye muaba wende muimpe wa kuendela. Ne shi, kunyima kua luendu lua bidimu bisatu, binayi, anyi bitanu udi usemena pabuipi ne nzubu ne tshintu tshionso tshia tshianana munda muende—tuambe ne: tshibombi tshia tshianana nansha—pashishe, mitu yende ya mazuwa idi milama kudi bantu too ne ku ndekelu; ne too ne padi mitshi yende ya skysail ibuela munkatshi mua bimanu bia tshitudilu tshia mazuwa, udi ulekela tshishiki ditekemena dia kukuata baleine mukuabu.

Mpindieu, bu mudi mudimu wa kuimana kua mitu ya mazuwa, ku muelelu anyi pa mâyi, muikale wa kale menemene ne udi usankisha, tuelayi mu mushindu kampanda malu apa. Ndi ngela meji ne: bantu ba kumpala bavua bimane mitu ya mazuwa bavua bena Ejipitu ba kale; bualu, mu makebulula anyi onso, tshiena mpeta nansha umue kumpala kuawu to. Bualu nansha mudi bankambua babu, bibaki ba Babele, kabayi mpata, ku diambuluisha dia tshibumba tshiabu, bavua ne lungenyi lua kuibaka tshibumba tshitambe bule mu Asie mujima, anyi mu Afrike; kadi (kumpala kua diteka dia mashinyi a ndekelu) bu mudibu mua kuamba ne: tshibumba tshinene tshia dibue tshiabu tshivua tshipita ku tshibumba, mu tshipepele tshikole tshia tshiji tshia Nzambi; nunku, katuena mua kupesha bibaki ba Babele aba muaba wa kumpala kupita bena Ejipitu to. Ne se bena Ejipitu badi tshisamba tshia bantu badi bimana ku mitu ya mazuwa, mbualu budi bushindamene pa ditabuja dia bena malu a kale, ne: bimanu bia kumpala bivua bienza bua malu a mitoto: lungenyi ludi lujadikibua kudi dienza dia tshibandilu tshia pa buadi tshia nseke yonso inayi ya nzubu ayi; mu mushindu eu, ne dibandisha dia mikolo yabu mile, bena malu a mitoto ba kale abu bavua ne tshibidilu tshia kubanda ku mutu kua mitoto, ne kuimba mitoto mipiamipia; nansha bu mudi bintu bia kutangila nabi mazuwa a matuku etu aa bimba bua tshilamba tshia kuendesha natshi mazuwa, anyi baleine udi anu umueneka. Mu Saint Stylites, muena kilisto muende lumu wa kale, uvua mumuibakile dikunji dia mabue mu tshipela ne mupitshishe tshitupa tshijima tshia ndekelu tshia muoyo wende pa mutu pa dikunji adi, wela biakudia biende panshi ne tshintu tshia kuenza natshi mudimu; munda muende tudi ne tshilejilu tshia dikema tshia muena mudimu udi kayi ne buôwa; uvua kayi ne bua kuipata muaba wende kudi lupuishi anyi mashika, mvula, mabue a mvula anyi mvula wa mabue; kadi ne dikima dionso wakatantamena malu onso too ne ku ndekelu, wakafua ku mudimu wende. Mu mitu ya mitshi ya matuku etu aa tudi anu ne tshisumbu tshidi katshiyi ne muoyo; bantu ba patupu ba mabue, tshiamu tshifike, ne tshiamu tshia mitaku; badi, nansha mudibu mua kutantamena tshipepele tshikole, batshidi anu kabayi bamanye mudimu wa kuimba misambu padibu bapeta tshintu kampanda tshia dikema. Kudi Napoléon; udi, pa mutu pa mulongo wa Vendome, muimane ne maboko makuate, bule bua metre lukama ne makumi atanu mu lupepele; kayi ne budimu, mpindieu, udi ukokesha mazuwa adi kuinshi eku; nansha Louis Philippe, Louis Blanc, nansha Louis Diabolo. Washington munene, pende, mmuimane muulu pa tshibandilu tshiende tshinene mu Baltimore, ne anu bu umue wa ku makunji a Hercules, tshibumba tshiende tshidi tshileja tshitupa tshia bunene bua muntu tshidi bantu bakese mua kupita. Admiral Nelson, pende, pa tshibandilu tshia tshiamu tshia bingoma, muimane mutu wende wa tshibumba mu Trafalgar Square; ne dîba dionso didibu basokome kudi muishi wa mu Londres, tshimanyishilu tshidi anu tshifila tshia se: muena mvita musokome udiku; bualu muaba udi muishi, udi ne bua kuikala kapia. Kadi nansha Washington munene, nansha Napoleon, nansha Nelson, kabena mua kuandamuna ku mvula wa mvula nansha umue udi ufuma kuinshi, nansha mudibu bababikile ne tshikisu bua kudia bulunda ne mibelu yabu ne mazuwa adibu batangila; nansha mudibi mua kuikala biumvuika, ne: nyuma yabu idi ibuela mu lupuishi lukole lua matuku atshilualua, ne idi ileja bibandabanda kayi ne mabue kayi adibu ne bua kuepuka.

Bidi mua kumueneka kabiyi biakane bua kusangisha mu mushindu kayi wonso biamu bia ku mutu kua mazuwa bia pa buloba ne bia mu mbuu; kadi mu bulelela kabiena nanku to, bidi bileja patoke kudi bualu budi Obed Macy, mufundi wa malu a kale umuepele wa mu Nantucket, udi ne tshia kuandamuna. Obed udi mukumbane udi utuambila ne, mu bikondo bia kumpala bia mudimu wa diloba dia baleine, kumpala kua mazuwa kutuadijabu kulonda nyama eyi, bantu ba mu tshidila atshi bavua bibaka bimanu bibandile ku muelelu wa mbuu, muvua bantu bavua babandila ku diambuluisha dia biamu bia misonso, tshintu tshivua tshienze bu nyunyi ivua ibanda mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi. Bidimu bikese bishale ebi, plan umue umue eu uvua muangatshibue kudi bena baleine ba mu Nouvelle-Zélande, bavua, pavuabu bapenda manaya aa, bamanyishe mazuwa avua pabuipi ne muelelu wa mâyi. Kadi tshilele etshi ntshilue mpindieu tshia kale; tuetu kupingana ku mutu umuepele muimpe wa mazuwa, wa mazuwa a baleine mu mbuu. Mitu isatu ya mazuwa idi milama kudi bantu kubangila ku dipatuka dia dîba too ne ku dibuela dia dîba; bena mazuwa benza mudimu wabu pa tshibidilu (bu mudi ku mutu kua mazuwa), ne bambuluishangana kunyima kua mêba abidi. Mu tshikondo tshia ditalala tshia mu matunga a mashika bidi bisankisha bikole mutu wa tshiamu tshia mazuwa; tòo, kudi muntu udi wela meji ne tshilota bidi bisankisha. Kuoku aku, udi muimane, metre lukama pa mutu pa mazuwa adi kaayi ne dîyi, wenda wenda mu mayi manene, bienze anu bu ne: mabaya adi mitshi minene, eku muinshi muebe ne pankatshi pa maulu ebe, bu mudibi, mudi nyama minene ya mu mbuu iowa mâyi, anu bu muvua mazuwa avua enda kumpala pankatshi pa bisabata bia Colossus uvua mumanyike bikole mu Rhodes wa kale. Kuoku aku udi muimane, mujimine mu mulongolongo wa mbuu udi kauyi ne ndekelu, kuyi ne tshintu tshidi tshinyunguluka anu mavuala a mâyi. Mazuwa avua manyanguke adi enda anyanguka; lupepele lua ngendu lua tulu ludi lupuekesha; bionso bidi bikufikisha ku dipanga dia lungenyi. Mu tshitupa tshinene, mu nsombelu wa diloba dia baleine wa mu matunga a mashika, dipanga dia malu dia dikema didi dikubueja; kuena umvua lumu nansha lumue to; kubala bikandakanda to; bintu bia pa mutu bidi ne miyuki ya dikema ya malu a pa tshibidilu kabiena mua kukushima mu malu adi kaayi ne mushinga to; kuena umvua bualu bua makenga a mu nzubu to; bintu bia mushinga mukole; dikuluka dia bintu; Kabena ne lutatu lua kuela meji a tshiudi mua kudia mu dilolo to—bualu biakudia biebe bionso bia bidimu bisatu ne kupita apu mbilama bimpe mu bibombi, ne mukanda webe wa mashinyi kawena mua kushintuluka to.

Mu umue wa ku bena baleine ba ku Sud, mu luendu lule lua bidimu bisatu anyi binayi, anu mudibi bienzeka misangu ya bungi, bungi bua mêba mashilangane audi upitshisha ku mutu kua mazuwa budi mua kufika ku ngondo ya bungi mijima. Ne bidi bia kanyinganyinga bua se: muaba uudi ufila tshitupa tshinene tshia tshikondo tshijima tshia nsombelu webe wa ku tshilelelu, udi ne bua kuikala kayi ne tshintu nansha tshimue tshidi tshisemena pabuipi ne muaba muimpe wa kusombela, anyi mulongolola bua kulela muaba muimpe wa kudiumvua, bu mudi udi utangila bulalu, tshitanda, ditempu dia bafue, kazubu ka balami, tshibandilu tshia mashinyi, tshintu tshionso tshikese anyi tshikuabu tshionso mishindu idi bantu balume baditapulula bua tshitupa tshîpi. Muaba uudi utamba kusomba udi ku mutu kua t' gallant-mast, muaba uudi muimane pa mitshi ibidi mitekete idi mifuanangane (idi pabuipi ne ya baleine) idibu babikila ne: t' gallant cross-trees. Apa, mutangile kudi mbuu, mubangi udi udiumvua bimpe anu bu mudiye mua kuikala muimane pa nsengu ya ngombe. Bua kuamba bulelela, mu tshikondo tshia mashika udi mua kuambula nzubu webe muulu, mu tshimfuanyi tshia tshilamba tshia kutangila natshi; kadi mu kuamba kuimpe, tshilamba tshia mudimu tshitambe bunene katshiena anu bu nzubu to, anu mubidi udi kauyi muvuale; bualu bu mudi anyima mulamakaja munda mua ntenta wende wa mubidi, ne kena mua kuendakana munda muawu, nansha kupatukamu, kayi ne njiwu minene ya kufua (bu muenyi udi kayi mumanye malu udi usabuka mikuna ya Alpes idi ne neje mu muvu wa mashika); nunku tshilamba tshia kumona natshi dîba ki ntshia nzubu to, kadi ntshiamu tshia patupu, anyi dikoba dikuabu didi dikubuikila. Kuena mua kuteka tshitekelu anyi tshibutshilu mu mubidi webe to, ne kuena mua kuenza kabidi tshibuta tshimpe tshia tshilamba tshiebe tshia kumona natshi dîba to.

Pa bidi bitangila bionso ebi, bidi bikengela kunyingalala bua se: mitu ya mazuwa a baleine ya ku Sud kayena ne ntenta mikese anyi nzubu ya tshilala benyi idibu babikila ne: nzubu ya nyunyi ya nkutshi , idibu balama miaba ya kutangila baleine ya mu Groenland ku mvula mibi ya mbuu idi ne mashika. Mu muyuki wa kapia wa Kapitene Sleet, udi ne tshiena bualu tshia ne: “Luendu lua munkatshi mua mabue a mashika, mu dikeba dia baleine wa mu Greenland, ne mu mushindu kampanda bua kupeta kabidi maloba a mu Islande avua majimine a mu Greenland wa kale;” mu mukanda ewu muimpe, bintu bionso bia ku mitu ya mazuwa bidi ne muyuki wa dikema wa tshikuku tshia nkutshi tshia Glacier tshivuabu bapatule matuku mashale aa, tshivua dîna dia mazuwa mimpe a Kapitene Sleet. Wakawubikila ne : tshikuku tshia Sleet , bua kuditumbisha; yeye muikale muenji wa bintu bia kumpala ne muena bukenji, ne udi kayi ne malu a dishima a tshianana, ne udi wamba ne: tuetu babikila bana betu ku mêna etu (tuetu batatu tudi bafuki ba bintu ne bena bukenji), ke mutudi kabidi ne bua kubikila bintu bikuabu bionso bitudi mua kulela ku mêna etu. Mu tshimuenekelu, tshikuku tshia nkutshi tshia Sleet tshidi tshifuanangane ne tshibandilu tshinene anyi pipe; udi munzulula kuulu, nansha nanku, muaba udiye ne tshilamba tshia ku luseke tshidi mua kuendakana bua kuikala mutangile ku mutu webe mu tshipepele tshikole. Bu muudi muimane ku mutu kua tshibumba, udi ubandamu kupitshila ku tshibuelelu tshikese tshidi kuinshi. Ku luseke lua panyima, anyi ku luseke lua kunyima kua mazuwa, kudi nkuasa muimpe, ne tshibuta tshia muinshi muatshi tshia kulama mitoyi, bilamba bia kulala nabi ne bilamba. Kumpala kudi tshitekelu tshia tshiseba, muaba uudi mua kulama mpungi webe wa kuakula, tshiamu tshia nzembu, teleskope ne bintu bikuabu bia mu mayi. Pavua Kapitene Sleet muine muimane mutu wende mu tshikuku tshiende etshi, udi utuambila ne: uvua anu ne tshingoma (tshivua kabidi tshiteka mu tshitekelu), pamue ne tshibombi tshia lupuishi ne tshingoma, bua tshipatshila tshia kupatula narwhales, anyi unicorns ya mu mbuu idi itambakana mu mâyi au; bualu kuena mua kubakuma bimpe ku tshibandilu tshia mazuwa bua ditantamana dia mâyi to, kadi kubatuta panshi mbualu budi bushilangane bikole. Mpindieu, bivua bimueneka patoke ne: bivua mudimu wa dinanga kudi Kapitene Sleet bua kumvuija, anu mudiye wenza, malu makese onso a mu tshikuku tshiende tshia nkutshi; kadi nansha mudiye uvudija bia bungi bia kudibi, ne nansha mudiye utupesha muyuki wa malu a sianse wa mateta ende mu tshisuku etshi, ne busole bukese buvuaye mulame muaba au bua kujikija bilema bidi bifumina ku tshidibu babikila ne: “dikokangana dia muaba” dia biamu bionso bia binnacle; tshilema tshidi mua kuikala tshifumina ku dikala dia tshiamu tshia mitaku tshidi mu mabaya a mazuwa, ne mu tshilumbu tshia Glacier, pamuapa, bualu kuvua bena mudimu ba bungi ba tshiamu tshia mitaku bavua banyanguke munkatshi mua bena mudimu bende; Ndi ngamba ne, nansha mudi Kapitene muikale ne budimu ne mumanyi wa malu a sianse apa, nansha mudiye mulonge malu onso a “binacle deviations,” “azimuth compass observations,” ne “approximate errors,” mmumanye bimpe, Kapitene Sleet, ne: kavua muditue bikole mu dielangana meji dia bukole dia magnétique to, kadi kavua mua kukokibua misangu mikuabu kudi bintu bikese bivua biuja bimpe to musokome ku luseke lumue lua tshikuku tshiende tshia nkutshi, muaba uvuaye mua kufika bipepele ku tshianza tshiende. Nansha, mu tshibungi, ndi nsanka bikole ne mene munange Kapitene wa dikima, wa bululame ne mulonge; kadi ndi ngangata bibi bua muvuaye mua kulengulula tshibombi atshi, mumone muvuatshi mua kuikala mulunda wa lulamatu ne musambakaji, pavuaye ne minu milengeja ne mutu mubuikila uvua ulonga makumi muulu mu tshikumbi tshia nyunyi au mutantshi wa miaba isatu anyi inayi ya ku mutshi.

Ino bituikala balobi ba baleine ba ku Sud katuyi basombele bimpe muulu bu muvua Kapitene Sleet ne bena Greenland bende; kadi dipanga edi didi dilengejibua bikole kudi ditalala dishilangane dia mbuu idi ikoka bantu itudi tuetu balobi ba ku Sud tutamba kuenda. Tshia kumpala, mvua ntamba kuikisha pa tshiamu tshia mashinyi, nkisha pa mutu bua kuyukila ne Queequeg, anyi muntu mukuabu yonso uvua kayi ku mudimu umvua mua kusanganamu; pashishe kubanda mutantshi mukese, ne kuela dikasa dia budinangi pa mutu pa lupangu lua mazuwa a kuulu, kuangata tshimuenenu tshia kumpala tshia madimi a mâyi, ne nunku ndekelu wa bionso meme kuya muaba umvua nya.

Lekela ngenza tshifu tshikezuke tshia bualu ebu apa, ne ngitaba ne muoyo mujima ne: mvua mulame kadi luse. Ne lutatu lua tshibuashibuashi ludi luenda lunyunguluka munda muanyi, mmunyi mundi mua—kushala nkayanyi nkayanyi ku bule bua nunku budi bujula meji—mmunyi mundi mua kuikala anu ne bujitu buanyi bua kutumikila mikenji ya mazuwa onso a baleine, “Ikala ne dîsu diebe dia tshikondo tshia mvula, ne wimbe dîba dionso.”

Ne lekelayi nnubela mu muaba ewu, nuenu bamfumu ba mazuwa ba mu Nantucket! Ikala mudimuke bua kubueja mu mudimu webe wa diloba dia nsongalume yonso udi ne mpala mutekete ne dîsu didi kadiyi ne tshintu; udi mufila ku dielangana meji didi kadiyi diakane; ne udi udifila bua kutuma ne Phædon pamutu pa Bowditch mu mutu wende. Ndi ngamba ne: nudimukile muntu wa nunku; badi ne tshia kumona baleine yebe kumpala kua kuyishipa; ne nsongalume wa mêsu mapuekele eu muena Platon neakukokeshe misangu dikumi pa buloba bujima, ne kakuakuvuija nansha kakese tshintu tshimue tshia mabele tshipite bubanji to. Kabidi, malu aa kaena ne mushinga to. Bua matuku etu aa, mudimu wa diloba dia baleine udi ufila muaba wa tshisokomenu kudi bansongalume ba bungi badi ne dinanga, badi ne kanyinganyinga ne badi kabayi ne lungenyi, badi ne tshiji bua malu adi enzeka pa buloba, ne badi bakeba lungenyi luabu mu goudron ne mu mafuta. Childe Harold utu misangu ya bungi usomba pa mutu pa mazuwa a baleine adi kaayi ne diakalengele, ne mu mêyi a tshikisu udi utua mishiku:—

"Tungunuka, wewe mbuu wa bule mutoke ne wa bule, tungunuke!
Balobi ba mafuta binunu dikumi badi bakukuata tshianana.”

Misangu ya bungi bamfumu ba mazuwa a mushindu eu batu bangata bansonga bena meji badi kabayi ne meji abu bua kuenza mudimu, babapenda ne: kabatu bumvua “dijinga” dikumbane dia luendu to; tshitupa tshimue tshidi tshileja ne: mbajimine bikole ku majinga abu onso a kanemu, bu mudi mu mioyo yabu ya mu musokoko kabayi basue kumona baleine pamutu pa kumona mushindu mukuabu. Kadi bionso bia tshianana; bansonga bena Platon aba badi ne lungenyi lua se: tshimuenenu tshiabu ki ntshipuangane to; badi ne lungenyi luipi; nunku, ntshinyi tshidi ne mushinga bua kukolesha mutshima wa kumona? Badi bashiye makalashi abu a opera kumbelu.
