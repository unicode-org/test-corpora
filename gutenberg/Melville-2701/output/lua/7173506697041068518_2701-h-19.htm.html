Bimpe; yeye mumane kuela mbila, katuakamumona kabidi mutantshi mule to; mu bulelela, anu mumvua muambe kumpala, tshivua mumanye tshikondo atshi tshivua baleine uvua mungambuluishe bua kuenza bualu ebu to, too ne panyima pa matuku, patuakapingana ku Mulongo, tuakumvua bakula bua Moby Dick—mudi bamue bamubikila—ne mvua mumanye ne: uvua nanku.”

"Kuvua musabuke kabidi tshibilu tshienda anyi?"

"Misangu ibidi."

"Kadi kuena mua kulama anyi?"

"Tshivua musue kuteta: tshitupa tshimue tshia mubidi katshienaku tshikumbane anyi? Ndi mua kuenza tshinyi tshiyi ne diboko dikuabu edi? Ne ndi ngela meji ne: Moby Dick katu usuma bikole padiye umina to."

"Eyowa, nunku," Bunger wakakosa, "mupesha diboko diebe dia balume bua kupeta tshintu tshiakane. Nudi bamanye anyi, banene"—muinama ne kanemu kakole ne mu mushindu wa makumi kudi Kapitene yonso mu mulongolongo—"Nudi bamanye, banene, ne: bitupa bia mubidi bia baleine mbienza mu mushindu udi kauyi umvuika kudi Divine Providence, ne kabiena mua kuenzeka bua yeye kudia nansha muntu nansha umue Diboko? Ne yeye pende mmumanye. Nunku tshiudi wangata bu tshinyangu tshia Baleine Mutoke ntshilema tshiende tshia tshianana. Bualu yeye kena musue kumina tshitupa tshimue tshia mubidi to; ne muoyo mujima, ne wakashalaku munkatshi mua ngondo dikumi ne ibidi anyi kupita apu; pangakamupesha tshintu tshia kuondopa natshi mubidi, ne yeye kutshibandisha mu bitupa bikese, udi umona. dijika diakane kudi mukuabu, bua tshinyi mu nsombelu eu diboko didi diebe;

"Tòo, tuasakidila, Bunger," ke muakamba Kapitene wa bena Angleterre, "udi muakidibue ku diboko didiye nadi, bualu tshiena mua kudikanda to, ne tshivua mumumanye tshikondo atshi to; kadi ki mmukuabu to. Kakuena kabidi baleine mitoke bua meme to; mvua mupuekele bua bualu buende musangu umue, ne bualu ebu mbunsankishe. Nekuikale butumbi bunene bua kumushipa, ndi mumanye ne: kudi tshinyangu tshinene, kadi tshidi ne mushinga wa bungi munda muende; ye, udi muimpe kulekela; kuena wela meji nanku, Kapitene?”—utangila dikasa dia meno a ntambue.

"Udi. Ino udi anu ukeba, bua bionso ebi. Ntshinyi tshidi tshimpe menemene, tshintu tshidi ne mulawu katshiena misangu yonso tshidi tshikoka bantu to. Yeye udi tshintu tshidi tshikoka bantu! Mmutantshi kayi uudi mumumone bua musangu wa ndekelu? Udi uya mu njila kayi?"

"Benesha anyima wanyi, ne mulawu wa bademon babi," ke muakamba Bunger, wenda unyunguluka Ahaba, ne bu mbwa, wenda unyunguluka mu mushindu wa dikema; "mashi a muntu ewu—lua ne tshipiminu tshia luya!—adi pa tshipiminu tshia luya!—dikuma diende didi dienza bua mabaya aa atute!—mfumu!"—wakangata muele wa mvita mu tshibombi tshiende, ne usemena pabuipi ne diboko dia Ahaba.

"Avast!" ke muvua Ahaba muele mbila, umukuma ku bimanu bia tshibumba—“Muntu wa buatu, udi uya ku luseke kayi?”

"Nzambi muimpe!" wakadila Kapitene wa bena Angleterre, uvuabu bele lukonko elu. "Ntshinyi tshidi tshienzeke? Uvua uya ku est, ndi ngela meji.— Kapitene wenu mmutshimbakane anyi?" Fedallah udi wela mbila.

Ino Fedallah, wela munu pa mishiku yende, wakapita pa bimanu bua kuangata tshiamu tshia kulombola natshi buatu, ne Ahaba, wela tshiamu tshia kukosa natshi buatu kudiye, wakambila bendeshi ba buatu bua kuimana pabuipi bua kupueka.

Mu tshitupa tshîpi uvua muimane ku nyima kua buatu, ne bantu ba mu Manilla bavua benda banyema buatu. Kapitene wa bena Angleterre wakamuela kalumbandi tshianana. Mupingane ku buatu bua benyi, ne mpala muteka bu dibue dia mabue kudi wende, Ahaba wakimana mululame too ne ku luseke lua Pequod.
