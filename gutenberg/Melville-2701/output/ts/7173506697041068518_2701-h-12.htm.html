NDZIMA YA 55. Ya Swifaniso Swa Xiharhi Swa Ti-Whale.

I shall ere long paint to you as well as one can without canvas, nchumu wo fana na xivumbeko xa ntiyiso xa whale tani hi leswi entiyisweni yi vonakaka eka tihlo ra whaleman loko eka miri wa yena lowu heleleke whale yi bohiwile etlhelo ka whale-ship leswaku a nga kandziyeriwa hi ku lulama kwalaho. Hikwalaho, swi nga ha va swi fanerile, khale ku navetisa eka swifaniso sweswo swa miehleketo leswi lavaka ku tiva swa yena leswi hambi ku ri ku fikela namuntlha swi tlhontlhaka ripfumelo ra n’wini wa misava hi ku tiyiseka. I nkarhi wo lulamisa misava emhakeni leyi, hi ku kombisa swifaniso swo tano swa whale hinkwaswo swi hoxile.

Swi nga ha endleka leswaku xihlovo xo sungula xa ku kanganyisa koloko hinkwako ka swifaniso xi ta kumeka exikarhi ka swifaniso swa khale swinene swa Mahindu, Vaegipta ni Magriki. Ku sukela eminkarhini yoleyo ya vutumbuluxi kambe leyi nga riki na vuxiyaxiya loko yi ri eka swiphepherhele swa marble swa titempele, swiseketelo swa swifaniso, ni le ka switlhangu, timendhe, swikotela ni mali ya nsimbi, dolphin a yi kokiwa hi swikalo swa swisirhelelo swa tinketana swo fana ni swa Saladin, ni nhloko leyi fanaka ni leyi nga ni xihuku George’s ya St. ku sukela kwalaho ku ve ni nchumu wa muxaka lowu fanaka wa layisense lowu hluleke, ku nga ri eka swifaniso swo tala leswi dumeke swa whale ntsena, kambe ni le ka swikombiso swo tala swa sayense swa yena.

Sweswi, hi ku hambana hinkwako, xifaniso xa khale swinene lexi nga kona hambi swi ri tano lexi tivulaka xa whale, xi kumeka eka cavern-pagoda leyi dumeke ya Elephanta, eIndiya. Vabrahmin va vula leswaku eka swifaniso leswi vatliweke leswi lavaka ku va leswi nga heriki swa pagoda yoleyo leyi nga tsundzukiki, mintirho hinkwayo ni ku hlongorisa, ntirho wun’wana ni wun’wana lowu nga ehleketiwaka wa munhu, a swi fanekiseriwe ka ha ri emahlweni malembe yo tala ku nga si va ni xin’we xa swona hakunene. Kutani, a swi hlamarisi leswi hi muxaka wo karhi ntirho wa hina lowu tlakukeke wa ku hlota ti- whale a wu fanele wu ri kona wu funengetiwe hi ndzhuti. Hindo whale leyi ku vulavuriwaka ha yona, yi humelela eka ndzawulo leyi hambaneke ya rirhangu, leyi kombisaka ku hundzuka ka Vishnu hi xivumbeko xa leviathan, leyi tiviwaka hi ku dyondza tanihi Matse Avatar. Kambe hambi leswi xifaniso lexi xi nga hafu ya munhu na hafu ya whale, leswaku xi ta nyika ntsena ncila wa loyi wo hetelela, hambiswiritano xiphemu xexo lexitsongo xa yena hinkwaxo xi hoxile. Yi languteka ngopfu kufana na ncila lowu rhetaka wa anaconda, kutlula mavoko yo anama ya ti flukes letikulu ta whale ya ntiyiso.

Kambe yana eka ti Galleries ta khale, naswona languta sweswi eka xifaniso xa muvalangi lonkulu wa Mukriste xa nhlampfi leyi; hikuva a nga humeleli ku antswa ku tlula Hindoo ya le mahlweni ka ndhambi. I xifaniso xa Guido xa Perseus a ri karhi a ponisa Andromeda eka xiharhi xa le lwandle kumbe whale. Xana Guido u wu kume kwihi xikombiso xa xivumbiwa lexi nga tolovelekangiki xo fana ni xexo? Naswona Hogarth, hi ku penda xivono lexi fanaka eka “Perseus Descending” ya yena n’wini, a nga endli ku antswa ka xin’we. Vukulu lebyikulu bya xivandzana xexo xa Hogarthian byi rhendzeleka ehenhla, byi koka mati hi ku nonoka intshi yin’we. Yina muxaka wa howdah enhlaneni wa yona, naswona nomu wa yona lowu pfulekeke wa tinsiva lowu swidzedze swi rhendzelekaka eka wona, wunga tekiwa kuya eka Gede ya Vaxengi leyi sukaka e Thames hi mati kuya eka Xihondzo. Kutani, ku ni ti- whale ta Prodromus ta khale ta Scotch Sibbald, ni ti- whale ta Jonah, hilaha swi kombisiweke hakona eka swifaniso leswi kandziyisiweke swa Tibibele ta khale ni ku tsemiwa ka ti- primer ta khale. Xana ku ta vuriwa yini hi lava? Loko ku ri whale ya muhlanganisi wa tibuku leyi rhendzelekaka ku fana ni xirhabyani xa vhinya yi rhendzela xitoko xa anchor leyi xikaka—tanihi leyi stemiwe ni ku pendiwa hi nsuku endzhaku ni le ka matluka ya nhloko-mhaka ya tibuku to tala ta khale ni letintshwa—xolexo i xivumbiwa lexi nga ni swifaniso leswinene ngopfu kambe lexi nga riki xa xiviri, . yi tekeleriwile, ndzi yi teka, eka swifaniso leswi fanaka eka swibya swa khale. Hambileswi emisaveni hinkwayo yi vuriwaka dolphin, hambi swi ri tano ndzi vitana nhlampfi leyi ya muhlanganisi wa tibuku ku ringeta ku hlasela whale; hikuva a yi kunguhatiwe tano loko xitirhisiwa lexi xi sungula ku tirhisiwa. Yi sunguriwe hi mukandziyisi wa khale wa Mutaliyana kun’wana kwalomu ka lembe-xidzana ra vu-15, hi nkarhi wa ku Pfuxetiwa ka Dyondzo; naswona emasikwini wolawo, hambi ku ri ku ya fika enkarhini lowu hundzeke loko wu pimanisiwa, hi vanhu vo tala a ku tekiwa leswaku tidolfini i muxaka wa Leviathan.

Eka ti vignettes na swin’wana swo sasekisa swa tibuku tin’wana ta khale minkarhi yin’wana uta hlangana na ku khumba loku lavaka ku tiva swinene eka whale, laha tinxaka hinkwato ta ti spouts, jets d’eau, swihlovo swo hisa na xirhami, Saratoga na Baden-Baden, swi taka swi bubbling up kusuka eka swa yena byongo lebyi nga karhaliki. Eka tluka ra nhloko-mhaka ya nkandziyiso wo sungula wa “Nhluvukiso wa Dyondzo” u ta kuma tinhlampfi tin’wana leti vuriwaka ti- whale leti lavaka ku tiva.

Kambe hi tshika matshalatshala lawa hinkwawo lama nga riki ya xiphurofexinali, a hi languteni swifaniso sweswo swa leviathan leswi tivulaka swifaniso leswi nga ni vuxiyaxiya, swa sayense, hi lava va swi tivaka. Eka nhlengeleto wa maendzo ya Harris wa khale kuna tipuleti tin’wana ta ti whale leti humesiweke eka buku ya maendzo ya Madachi, AD 1671, leyi nga na nhlokomhaka leyi nge “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson of Friesland, master.” Eka yin’wana ya tipuleti teto ti- whale, ku fana ni swikepe leswikulu swa timhandzi, ti yimeriwe ti etlele exikarhi ka swihlala swa ayisi, ti ri ni tibere to basa leti tsutsumaka ehenhla ka milenge ya tona leyi hanyaka. Eka pulati yin’wana, xihoxo lexi hlamarisaka xi endliwe hi ku yimela whale hi swiphepherhele leswi yimisiweke.

Kutani nakambe, ku ni quarto leyikulu, leyi tsariweke hi Captain Colnett un’wana, Post Captain eka vuthu ra le matini ra Manghezi, leri nga ni nhloko-mhaka leyi nge “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, hi xikongomelo xo andlala Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Ebukwini leyi ku ni xivumbeko lexi tivulaka “Xifaniso Xa Physeter kumbe Spermaceti whale, lexi tekiweke hi xikalo eka lexi dlayiweke eribuweni ra Mexico, hi August, 1793, ivi xi tlakusiwa ehenhla ka xitepisi.” A ndzi kanakana leswaku mukaputeni u endle leswaku xifaniso lexi xa ntiyiso xi tekiwe leswaku ku vuyeriwa masocha yakwe ya le matini. Ku boxa nchumu wun’we ntsena hi yona, ndzi pfumeleleni ndzi vula leswaku yi na tihlo leri tirhisiwaka, hi ku ya hi xikalo lexi fambisanaka na xona, eka sperm whale leyi kuleke hi ku helela, swi nga endla leswaku tihlo ra whale yoleyo ri va bow-window kwalomu ka ntlhanu wa timitara hi ku leha. Oh, ndhuna ya mina ya xivindzi, ha yini u nga hi nyikanga Yonasi a languta ehandle ka tihlo rero!

Naswona tibuku leti hlengeletiweke leti nga ni ripfalo swinene ta Matimu ya Ntumbuluko leswaku ti vuyerisa vantshwa ni lava nga ni vuxiyaxiya, a ti ntshunxekanga eka ku nyenyetsa loku fanaka ka swihoxo. Languta buku yoleyo leyi dumeke leyi nge “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature.” Eka nkandziyiso lowu pfuxetiweke wa le London wa 1807, ku ni tipuleti ta “whale” leyi ku vuriwaka leswaku i “whale” ni “narwhale.” A ndzi swi lavi ku vonaka ndzi nga ri wa xiyimo xa le henhla, kambe xiharhi lexi xa whale lexi nga langutekiki xi fana swinene ni nguluve leyi tsemiweke; naswona, loko ku ri narwhale, ku yi languta kan’we swi ringanerile ku hlamarisa munhu, leswaku eka lembe xidzana leri ra vukhume-nhungu hippogriff yo tano yi nga palmed for genuine eka vaaki vahi na vahi vo tlhariha va vafana va xikolo.

Kutani, nakambe, hi 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, mutivi lonkulu wa ntumbuluko, u humese buku ya sayense leyi hlelekeke ya ti whale, laha ku nga ni swifaniso swo hlayanyana swa tinxaka to hambana-hambana ta Leviathan. Hinkwaswo leswi a swi hoxanga ntsena, kambe xifaniso xa Mysticetus kumbe Greenland whale (hileswaku, Right whale), hambi ku ri Scoresby, wanuna la nga ni ntokoto wo leha malunghana ni ku khumba muxaka wolowo, u tivisa leswaku a nga na muxaka wa yona hi ntumbuluko.

Kambe ku vekiwa ka cap-sheaf eka bindzu leri hinkwaro leri hoxeke a ku vekeriwe mutivi wa sayense Frederick Cuvier, makwavo wa Baron la dumeke. Hi 1836, u kandziyise buku leyi nge Natural History of Whales, leyi eka yona a nyikeke leswi a swi vitanaka xifaniso xa Sperm Whale. Loko unga se komba xifaniso xexo eka Nantucketer yin’wana na yin’wana, swinga antswa u nyika nkarhi wa wena wo wisa wo koma kusuka e Nantucket. Hi rito rin’we, Sperm Whale ya Frederick Cuvier a hi Sperm Whale, kambe i xikwavava. I ntiyiso leswaku a nga si tshama a vuyeriwa hi riendzo ro hlota tinhlampfi ta tinhlampfi (vavanuna vo tano a hi kanyingi va nga vuyeriwa), kambe xana xifaniso xexo u xi kume kwihi, i mani la nga swi tivaka? Kumbexana u yi kumile tanihi leswi loyi a n’wi rhangeleke hi sayense eka ntirho lowu fanaka, Desmarest, a kumeke yin’wana ya ku susa khwiri ka yena ka ntiyiso; hi leswaku, ku suka eka xifaniso xa Xichayina. Naswona i muxaka muni wa majaha lama hanyaka lama nga ni pensele Machayina wolawo, swikotela swo tala swa queer ni swikhwama swi hi tivisa.

Loko ku ri ti whale ta vapendi va swikombiso leti voniwaka eswitarateni ti lenga-lenga ehenhla ka mavhengele ya vaxavisi va oyili, ku ta vuriwa yini hi tona? Hi ntolovelo i Richard wa Vunharhu. ti whale, leti nga ni ti- dromedary humps, naswona ti ni tihanyi swinene; ku dya swakudya swa nimixo hi ti- tart tinharhu kumbe mune ta vatluti, ku nga swikepe swa whale leswi teleke hi vatluti: ku onhaka ka swona ku rhurhumela emalwandle ya ngati ni pende ya wasi.

Kambe swihoxo leswi swo hambana-hambana eku kombiseni ka whale a swi hlamarisi ngopfu phela. Tekela enhlokweni! Vunyingi bya swifaniso swa sayense swi tekiwe eka tinhlampfi leti pfaleriweke; naswona leswi swi lulamile ku fana ni xifaniso xa xikepe lexi tshovekeke, lexi nga ni nkolo lowu tshovekeke, xi nga yimela kahle xiharhi lexi xiximekaka hi xoxe eka ku tikukumuxa ka xona hinkwako loku nga tshovekiki ka xirhendzevutana ni swiphepherhele. Hambileswi tindlopfu ti yimeleke ku leha ka tona loku heleleke, Levhiyathani la hanyaka a nga si tshama a tiphaphamala hi ndlela leyi faneleke hikwalaho ka xifaniso xa yena. Whale leyi hanyaka, hi vukulu bya yona lebyi heleleke ni nkoka wa yona, yi fanele yi voniwa ntsena elwandle ematini lama nga twisisekiki; naswona loko a papamala xiphemu lexikulu xa yena a xi nga voniwi, ku fana ni xikepe lexi rhumeriweke lexi nga ni layini ya nyimpi; naswona eka xiaki xexo i nchumu lowu nga kotekiki hilaha ku nga heriki leswaku munhu la faka a n’wi tlakusela emoyeni hi miri, leswaku a hlayisa ku pfimba ka yena hinkwako lokukulu ni magandlati. Naswona, hi nga ha vulavuli hi ku hambana loku ehleketiwaka swinene ka contour exikarhi ka whale leyintsongo leyi mamaka na Leviathan ya Platonia leyi kuleke hi ku helela; kambe, hambi ku ri emhakeni ya yin’wana ya ti- whale toleto letitsongo leti an’waka leti tlakusiweke ehenhla ka xikepe, hi nkarhi wolowo xivumbeko xa yena lexi nga tolovelekangiki, lexi fanaka ni ximanga, lexi nga ni swirhumbana, lexi cinca-cincaka, lerova xivumbeko xa yena lexi kongomeke diyavulosi hi yexe a nga swi kotanga ku xi khoma.

Kambe swi nga ha ehleketiwa, leswaku eka marhambu lama nga ambalangiki ya whale leyi pfaleriweke, switsundzuxo leswi kongomeke swi nga ha kumiwa leswi khumbaka xivumbeko xa xona xa xiviri. A swi tano. Hikuva i xin’wana xa swilo leswi hlamarisaka ngopfu hi Levhiyathani loyi, leswaku marhambu ya yena ma nyika mianakanyo yitsongo swinene ya xivumbeko xa yena hi ku angarhela. Hambi leswi marhambu ya Jeremy Bentham, lawa ya lengalengaka candelabra elayiburari ya un’wana wa vaendli va yena, hi ndlela leyinene ya hundzisela mianakanyo ya burly-browed utilitarian old gentleman, na swihlawulekisi swin’wana hinkwaswo swa Jeremy leswi rhangelaka swa munhu hi xiyexe; kambe a ku na nchumu wa muxaka lowu lowu a wu ta ringanyetiwa eka marhambu ya leviathan yihi na yihi lama hlanganisiweke. Entiyisweni, hilaha Hunter lonkulu a vulaka hakona, marhambu ntsena ya whale ma ni vuxaka lebyi fanaka ni xiharhi lexi vekiweke hi ku helela ni lexi nga ni swiphepherhele tanihi leswi xitsotswana xi xi endlaka eka chrysalis leyi yi funengetaka hi ndlela leyi rhendzelekaka. Ku hlawuleka loku ku vonaka hi ndlela leyi hlamarisaka enhlokweni, tanihi leswi eka xiphemu xin’wana xa buku leyi ku nga ta kombisiwa hi xihoxo. Nakambe yi kombisiwa hi ndlela yo hlamarisa swinene eka xiphemu xa le tlhelo, marhambu ya yona kwalomu ka ku hlamula kahle eka marhambu ya voko ra munhu, ku susiwa xikunwana ntsena. Fin leyi yina mune wa tintiho ta marhambu leti tolovelekeke, index, middle, ring na little finger. Kambe leswi hinkwaswo swi tshama hilaha ku nga heriki eka xifunengeto xa swona xa nyama, tanihi tintiho ta munhu eka xifunengeto xo endliwa. Siku rin’wana Stubb la hlekisaka u te: “Hambi swi ri tano hi ku pfumala vuxiyaxiya nkarhi wun’wana whale yi nga hi tirhela, a swi nge pfuki swi vuriwile leswaku yi hi khoma handle ka swihuku.”

Hikwalaho, hikwalaho ka swivangelo leswi hinkwaswo, hi ndlela yihi na yihi leyi u nga yi langutaka ha yona, u fanele u lava ku gimeta hi leswaku Levhiyathani lonkulu i xivumbiwa xexo xin’we emisaveni lexi faneleke ku tshama xi nga pendiwanga ku fikela emakumu. I ntiyiso leswaku xifaniso xin’wana xi nga ha ba mfungho ekusuhi swinene ku tlula xin’wana, kambe a ku na lexi nga xi ba hi mpimo wihi na wihi lowu xiyekaka swinene wa ku kongoma. Kutani a ku na ndlela ya laha misaveni yo kuma hi ku kongoma leswaku whale yi languteka njhani hakunene. Naswona ndlela yin’we ntsena leyi u nga kumaka hambi ku ri mianakanyo leyi tiyiseleriwaka ya contour ya yena leyi hanyaka, i ku ya eku hloteni ka whale hi wexe; kambe hi ku endla tano, a wu ngheni ekhombyeni leritsongo ro va xitofu ni ku nwela hi yena hilaha ku nga heriki. Hikwalaho, swi vonaka eka mina leswaku a swi antswa u nga vi na vuxiyaxiya ngopfu eka ku lava ka wena ku tiva ku khumba Levhiyathani leyi.





NDZIMA YA 56. Ya Swifaniso Leswi Nga Riki Na Xihoxo Xa Ti-Whale, Ni Swifaniso Swa Ntiyiso Swa Swifaniso Swa Ku Dlaya Ti-Whale.

Hi ku fambisana na swifaniso swo chavisa swa ti whale, ndzi ringeka swinene laha ku nghena eka switori sweswo swa ha ri swa xihangu swinene swa tona leswi kumekaka eka tibuku to karhi, ta khale na ta manguva lawa, ngopfu-ngopfu eka Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, . etc. Kambe ndzi hundza mhaka yoleyo.

Ndzi tiva ntsena mune wa swivumbeko leswi kandziyisiweke swa Sperm Whale leyikulu; Ya Colnett, ya Huggins, ya Frederick Cuvier na ya Beale. Eka ndzima leyi hundzeke ku vulavuriwa hi Colnett na Cuvier. Ya Huggins yi antswa swinene ku tlula ya vona; kambe, hi ku hambana lokukulu, ya Beale hi yona leyinene ngopfu. Swifaniso hinkwaswo swa Beale swa whale leyi i swinene, handle ka xifaniso xa le xikarhi eka xifaniso xa whale tinharhu eka mavonelo yo hambana hambana, xi hlanganisa ndzima ya yena ya vumbirhi. Xifaniso xa yena xa le mahlweni, swikepe leswi hlaselaka Ti- Sperm Whale, hambi leswi handle ko kanakana swi hlayiweke ku pfuxa ku kanakana ka vaaki ka vavanuna van’wana va le parlor, xi lulamile hi ndlela leyi hlamarisaka naswona xi fana ni vutomi hi vuyelo bya xona lebyi tolovelekeke. Swin’wana swa swifaniso swa Sperm Whale eka J. Ross Browne swi lulamile swinene hi contour; kambe ti vatliwe hi ndlela yo vava. Sweswo a hi xihoxo xa yena hambiswiritano.

Eka Right Whale, swifaniso leswinene ngopfu swa outline swi le Scoresby; kambe ti dirowiwa hi xikalo lexitsongo swinene leswaku ti hundzisa langutelo leri navetisiwaka. U na xifaniso xin’we ntsena xa swivono swa ku hlota ti whale, naswona leswi i ku pfumaleka loku khomisaka gome, hikuva hi swifaniso swo tano ntsena, loko swi endliwe kahle, laha u nga kumaka xin’wana na xin’wana xo fana na mianakanyo ya ntiyiso ya whale leyi hanyaka tanihilaha swi voniwaka hakona hi vahloti va yona lava hanyaka.

Kambe, loko swi tekiwa hinkwaswo hi ku angarhela, ku fikela sweswi leswinene ngopfu, hambi leswi eka vuxokoxoko byin’wana swi nga riki ntiyiso swinene, swikombiso swa ti whale ni swifaniso swa ku hlota ti whale leswi nga kumekaka kun’wana ni kun’wana, i swifaniso swimbirhi leswikulu swa le Furwa leswi vatliweke, leswi endliweke kahle, naswona swi tekiwe eka swifaniso leswi vatliweke hi Garnery un’we. Hi ku landzelelana, ti yimela minhlaselo ya Sperm na Right Whale. Eka xifaniso xosungula Sperm Whale ya xiyimo xale henhla yi kombisiwile hi vukulukumba lebyi heleleke bya matimba, yi tlakukile ntsena ehansi ka xikepe kusuka eka vuenti bya lwandle, naswona yi rhwale ehenhla emoyeni ehenhla ka nhloko ya yona ku tshoveka loku chavisaka ka mapulanga lawa ya stoven. Xirhendzevutana xa xikepe lexi a xi tshovekanga hi xiphemu, naswona xi kokiwa ntsena xi ringanisela ehenhla ka nkolo wa xiharhi xa nhova; naswona u yime eka xirhendzevutani xexo, eka ku vonakala koloko kun’we loku nga hlayekiki ka nkarhi, u vona mufambisi wa xikepe, hafu ya yena a funengetiwile hi xipfalo xo vila lexi hlundzukeke xa whale, naswona a ri karhi a tlula-tlula, onge hiloko a huma exihlobyeni. Xiendlo xa mhaka hinkwayo i xinene hi ndlela yo hlamarisa naswona i ntiyiso. Line-tub leyi nga riki na nchumu hafu yi papamala elwandle leri basa; tinsika ta mapulanga ta ti- harpoon leti halatiweke ti rhendzeleka hi ku olova eka yona; tinhloko ta ntlawa lowu hlambelaka ti hangalakile hi whale hi swikombiso leswi hambaneke swa ku chava; kasi eka mpfhuka wa xidzedze xa ntima xikepe xi ri karhi xi rhelela ehansi ehenhla ka ndhawu ya mhangu. Xihoxo lexikulu xi nga ha kumiwa eka vuxokoxoko bya anatomical ya whale leyi, kambe sweswo a swi hundze; tanihi leswi, hikwalaho ka vutomi bya mina, a ndzi nga ta swi kota ku dirowa leyinene swonghasi.

Eka xifaniso xa vumbirhi lexi vatliweke, xikepe lexi xiri eka xiendlo xo dirowa etlhelo ka barnacled flank ya Right Whale leyikulu leyi tsutsumaka, leyi rhendzelekaka xiphemu xa yona xa ntima xa nhova elwandle kufana na mossy rock-slide yin’wana kusuka eka maboboma ya Patagonia. Tijeti ta yena ti yimile, ti tele naswona ti ri ta ntima ku fana ni nkuma; leswaku ku suka eka musi lowu a wu tele ngopfu exichimini, u nga ehleketa leswaku ku fanele ku ri ni swakudya swa nimadyambu swa xivindzi leswi swekaka emarhumbyini lamakulu lama nga laha hansi. Tinyenyana ta le lwandle ti ri karhi ti rhurhumela ti- crab letitsongo, ti- shell-fish ni swin’wana swo sweka swa le lwandle ni maccaroni, leswi minkarhi yin’wana Right Whale yi swi rhwalelaka enhlaneni wa yona lowu nga ni ntungu. Naswona nkarhi wolowo hinkwawo leviathan leyi nga ni milomu yo tiya yi tsutsumela exikarhi ka mati, yi siya tithani ta ti- curd to basa leti nga ni mpfilumpfilu endzhaku ka yona, naswona yi endla leswaku xikepe lexitsongo xi tsekatseka eka swirhumbana ku fana ni xikepe lexi khomiweke ekusuhi ni mavhilwa ya xikepe xa le lwandle. Xisweswo, ndhawu ya le mahlweni hinkwayo i mpfilumpfilu lowukulu; kambe endzhaku, hi ku hambana loku hlamarisaka ka vutshila, ku ni mpimo wa nghilazi wa lwandle leri rhuleke, maseyila lama rhetaka lama nga riki na starch ya xikepe lexi nga riki na matimba, ni nhlayo leyi nga tirhiki ya whale leyi feke, khokholo leri hluriweke, leri nga ni mujeko wa ku khomiwa lowu lengalengaka hi ku loloha eka whale- pole leyi nghenisiweke eka spout-hole ya yena.

I mani Garnery muvalangi, kumbe a ri mani, a ndzi swi tivi. NDZIMA YA 57. Ya Ti- Whale leti nga eka Pende; eka Meno; eka Wood; eka Xiphepherhele-Nsimbi; eRibyeni; eTintshaveni; eka Tinyeleti.

Eka Tower-hill, loko u ri karhi u rhelela etibuweni ta le London, u nga ha va u vone mukomberi la lamaleke (kumbe kedger , hilaha vatluti va vulaka hakona) a khome bodo leyi pendiweke emahlweni ka yena, leyi yimelaka xivono lexi khomisaka gome lexi a lahlekeriweke hi nenge eka xona. Ku ni ti- whale tinharhu ni swikepe swinharhu; naswona xin’wana xa swikepe (leswi ku ehleketiwaka leswaku xi ni nenge lowu kayivelaka hi vutshembeki bya xona hinkwabyo byo sungula) xi ri karhi xi pfotlosiwa hi tinhlaya ta whale leyi nga emahlweni swinene. Nkarhi wun’wana na wun’wana malembe lawa ya khume, va ndzi byela, xana wanuna yoloye u tlakusile xifaniso xexo, naswona u kombisile xikundzu xexo eka misava leyi nga tshembiki. Kambe nkarhi wa ku lulamisiwa ka yena sweswi wu fikile. Ti whale ta yena tinharhu i ti whale letinene ku fana na leti tshameke ti kandziyisiwa eka Wapping, hi ndlela yihi na yihi; naswona xikundzu xa xona i xikundzu lexi nga kanakanisiki ku fana ni xin’wana ni xin’wana lexi u nga ta xi kuma etindhawini leti nga riki na nchumu ta le vupela-dyambu. Kambe, hambi leswi hi laha ku nga heriki a khandziyiweke ehenhla ka xikundzu xexo, a nga si tshama a endla nkulumo ya xikundzu leyi mufambisi wa whale wa xisiwana a yi vulaka; kambe, hi mahlo lama nga ni gome, u yime hi ku vilela a anakanyisisa hi ku tsemiwa ka yena n’wini.

Eka Pacific hinkwayo, naswona nakambe e Nantucket, nale New Bedford, na Sag Harbor, uta hlangana na swifaniso leswi hanyaka swa ti whale na whale-scenes, leswi vatliweke hi vaphasi va tihlampfi hi voxe eka Sperm Whale-teeth, kumbe mabazi ya vavasati lawa ya endliweke kusuka e Right Whale-bone, na swin’wana swo fana na swihloko swa skrimshander, tani hi leswi vahlayisi va whale va swi vitanaka switirhisiwa swo tala leswintsongo swa vutlhari leswi va swi vatlaka hi vutshila eka swilo leswi nga riki na nchumu, eka tiawara ta vona ta ku wisa elwandle. Tin’wana ta tona ti ni mabokisi lamatsongo ya switirhisiwa leswi langutekaka swi ri swa meno, leswi endleriweke bindzu ra skrimshandering hi ndlela yo hlawuleka. Kambe, hi ku angarhela, va tirha hi matimba hi mabanga ya vona ya jack-knives ntsena; naswona, hi xitirho xexo lexi lavaka ku va ni matimba hinkwawo xa mutluti, va ta ku hundzula xin’wana ni xin’wana lexi u xi tsakelaka, hi ndlela ya ku navela ka mutluti.

Ku va evuhlongeni nkarhi wo leha eka Vujagana ni nhluvuko handle ko kanakana swi vuyisela munhu eka xiyimo xexo lexi Xikwembu xi n’wi vekeke eka xona, i.e. leswi vuriwaka vuharhi. Muhloti wa wena wa ntiyiso wa ti whale i munhu wa tihanyi ku fana ni Muiroquois. Mina hi ndzexe ndzi xiharhi xa tihanyi, a ndzi na vutshembeki handle ka Hosi ya Vadyi; naswona a lunghekele nkarhi wihi na wihi ku n’wi xandzukela.

Sweswi, xin’wana xa swihlawulekisi swo hlawuleka swa xiharhi xa tihanyi eka tiawara ta xona ta le kaya, i ku lehisa mbilu ka yena loku hlamarisaka ka tiindasitiri. Xipano xa nyimpi xa khale xa le Hawaii kumbe xikepe xa fumu, hi ku tala ka xona loku heleleke ni ku andlala ka xona ka ku vatla, i xidlodlo lexikulu xa ku tiyisela ka vanhu ku fana ni xihlamusela-marito xa Xilatini. Hikuva, hi ntsena xiphepherhele xa lwandle lexi tshovekeke kumbe meno ya shaka, ku rharhangana koloko ka singita ka nete ya mapulanga ku fikeleriwile; naswona yi durhe malembe yo tiya ya ku tirhisiwa loku tiyeke.

Ku fana ni swiharhi swa le Hawaii, swi tano ni hi sailor-savage yo basa. Hi ku lehisa mbilu loku fanaka loku hlamarisaka, na meno ya shaka yin’we, ya banga ra yena rin’we ra xisiwana ra jack-knife, u ta ku vatla xiphemu xa xifaniso xa marhambu, ku nga ri ku fana na ku fana na mutirhi, kambe ku fana na ku pakiwile ekusuhi eka maziness ya xona ya dizayini, ku fana na Xigriki savage, xitlhangu xa Achilles; naswona yi tele hi moya wa vuharhi ni ku ringanyeta, tanihi swifaniso leswi kandziyisiweke swa xiharhi xexo xo saseka xa khale xa Mudachi, Albert Durer.

Ti- whale ta mapulanga, kumbe ti- whale leti tsemiweke hi xivumbeko xa le henhla eka swiphepherhele leswitsongo swa munyama swa tihunyi ta nyimpi leti tlakukeke ta Lwandle ra Dzonga, hakanyingi ti hlangana na tona eka swivono swa vaphasi va ti- whale va le Amerika. Swin’wana swa swona swi endliwa hi ku pakanisa lokukulu.

Eka tindlu tin’wana ta khale ta le makaya leti fuleriweke hi ti- gable u ta vona ti- whale ta koporo leti hayekiweke hi ncila leswaku ti ta gongondza enyangweni lowu nga etlhelo ka patu. Loko mufambisi wa swilo a khomiwe hi vurhongo, whale leyi nga ni nhloko ya anvil yi nga va leyinene ngopfu. Kambe ti whale leti to gongondza a hi kanyingi ti hlamarisaka tanihi switlhokovetselo leswi tshembekaka. Eka swihondzo swa tikereke tin’wana ta khale u ta vona ti- sheet-iron whale leti vekiweke kwalaho leswaku ti ta tirhisiwa ti- weather-cocks; kambe ti tlakukile swinene, naswona handle ka sweswo ti lebuliwile swinene hi “ Hands off! ” a wu nge swi kambisisi hi vukheta lerova u endla xiboho xa ku faneleka ka swona.

Kutani, nakambe, ematikweni ya tintshava laha mufambi a tshamelaka ku bohiwa hi tindhawu leti tlakukeke ta mintlango ya mintlango; laha na laha kusuka eka mavonelo yokarhi ya nkateko uta khoma swivono leswi hundzaka swa ti profile ta ti whale leti hlamuseriweke etlhelo ka swirhendzevutani leswingana magandlati. Kambe u fanele u va mutivi wa ti- whale loyi a nga ni vukheta, leswaku u vona swivono leswi; naswona ku nga ri sweswo ntsena, kambe loko u lava ku tlhelela eka xivono xo tano nakambe, u fanele ku tiyiseka no teka latitude na longitude leyi hlanganisaka hi ku kongoma ya xiyimo xa wena xo sungula, else so chance-like are such observations of the hills, that your xiyimo lexi kongomeke, xa khale a xi ta lava ku tshuburiwa nakambe hi matimba; ku fana na Swihlala swa Soloma, leswi swa ha tshamaka swi nga tiviwi, hambi leswi khale a Mendanna wa xiyimo xa le henhla a swi kandziyeke naswona Figuera wa khale a swi tsale hi matimu.

Naswona loko u tlakusiwa hi ku andza hi nhloko-mhaka ya wena, a wu nge tsandzeki ku lavisisa tinhlampfi letikulu ta ti- whale etilweni leri nga ni tinyeleti, ni swikepe leswi ti hlongorisaka; tanihi loko nkarhi wo leha ma tele hi mianakanyo ya nyimpi matiko ya le Vuxeni ma voneke mavuthu lama pfaleriweke enyimpini exikarhi ka mapapa. Xisweswo eN’walungwini ndzi hlongorisile Leviathan ndzi rhendzeleka ni ku rhendzeleka na Pole hi ku rhendzeleka ka tinhla to vangama leti sunguleke ku n’wi hlamusela eka mina. Naswona ehansi ka xibakabaka xo vangama xa Antarctic ndzi khandziyile xikepe xa Argo-Navis, naswona ndzi joyine ku hlongorisa ku lwisana na Cetus leyi nga na tinyeleti ekule swinene ku tlula xiphemu lexikulu xa Hydrus na Flying Fish.

Hi ti anchors ta frigate ta ti bridle-bitts ta mina na ti fasces ta harpoons for spurs, xana ndzi nga khandziya whale yoleyo kutani ndzi tlula empfhukeni wa le henhla swinene, ku vona loko matilo ya ntsheketo na matende ya wona hinkwawo yo hlaya ya etlele hakunene ya dzime mixaxa ehandle ka ku vona ka mina loku faka!

Hakunene, eka tindlela tin’wana, a wu nge swi koti nikatsongo ku languta swivumbiwa swihi na swihi swa le ndzeni ka mati hi mintlhaveko leyi fanaka ni leyi u yi langutaka ni ya le ribuweni. Shark leyi rhukaniweke ntsena hi ndlela yihi na yihi leyi tolovelekeke ku nga vuriwa leswaku yi ni xifaniso lexi ringanisiwaka na yona.

Kambe hambiswiritano, eka vanhu va misava hi ku angarhela, vaaki va ndzhavuko va malwandle va tshame va langutiwa hi mintlhaveko leyi nga hlamuselekiki leyi nga riki ya ntshamisano ni leyi hlongolaka; hambileswi hi tivaka lwandle tanihi terra incognita leyi nga heriki, lerova Columbus u fambe hi xikepe ehenhla ka misava leyi nga hlayekiki leyi nga tiviwiki leswaku a ya kuma ya yena yin’we ya le henhla ya le vupela-dyambu; hambiswiritano, hi ku hambana lokukulu, makhombo lama chavisaka swinene eka hinkwawo lama faka ma humelele hi ndlela leyi nga tsundzukiki ni hi ku pfumala xihlawuhlawu makume ni madzana ya magidi ya lava va yeke ehenhla ka mati; hambileswi kambe ku anakanyisisa ka xinkarhana ku ta dyondzisa, leswaku hambi swi ri tano n’wana wa wanuna a nga tibuma hi sayense ni vutshila bya yena, naswona hambi swi ri tano, eka vumundzuku lebyi tsakisaka, leswaku sayense ni vutshila swi nga ha engeteleka; kambe hilaha ku nga heriki, ku ya fika eku pandzeni ka khombo, lwandle ri ta n’wi rhuketela ni ku n’wi dlaya, ni ku pfotlosa xikepe xa le henhla xa xiyimo xa le henhla, xo tiya swinene lexi a nga xi endlaka; hambi swi ri tano, hi ku phindha-phindha loku hambetaka ka miehleketo leyi hi yoxe, munhu u lahlekeriwe hi ku twisisa koloko ka ku chavisa loku heleleke ka lwandle leri hi ntumbuluko ri nga ra rona.

Xikepe xo sungula lexi hi xi hlayeke, a xi papamala elwandle, lexi hi ku rihisela ka Maputukezi xi hluleke misava hinkwayo handle ko siya ngopfu hambi ku ri noni. Lwandle ni tiko swi hambana kwihi, leswaku singita eka un'wana a hi singita eka un'wana? Kambe a hi lwandle ntsena nala wo tano eka munhu loyi a nga muendzi eka rona, kambe ri tlhela ri va xikwembu eka vana va rona; ku bihe ngopfu ku tlula masocha ya le Peresiya lama dlayeke vaendzi va yena n’wini; ku nga ri ku ponisa swivumbiwa leswi hi swoxe swi swi tswaleke. Ku hava tintswalo, ku hava matimba kambe ya wona ya yi lawula. Hi ku hefemula ni ku rhurhumela ku fana ni hanci ya nyimpi leyi pengaka leyi lahlekeriweke hi mukhandziyi wa yona, lwandle leri nga riki na vutshila ri funengeta misava hinkwayo.

Anakanya hi ku tumbela ka lwandle; ndlela leyi swivumbiwa swa yona leswi chavisaka swinene swi rhelelaka ha yona ehansi ka mati, leswi nga vonakiki hi xiphemu lexikulu, naswona hi vukanganyisi swi tumbele ehansi ka mihlovo yo saseka swinene ya azure. Nakambe anakanya hi ku vangama ni ku saseka ka diyavulosi ka tinxaka ta yona to tala leti nga tisoliki, tanihi xivumbeko xo saseka lexi khavisiweke xa tinxaka to tala ta tishaka. Anakanya, nakambe, ku dya vanhu ka misava hinkwayo ka lwandle; hinkwavo lava swivumbiwa swa vona swi dlayanaka, swi ya emahlweni ni nyimpi leyi nga heriki ku sukela loko misava yi sungula.

Xiya hinkwaswo leswi; kutani u hundzukela eka misava leyi ya rihlaza, yo olova, ni leyi yingisaka swinene; va anakanye hi vumbirhi bya vona, lwandle ni tiko; naswona xana a wu kumi xifaniso lexi nga tolovelekangiki ni nchumu wo karhi eka wena n’wini? Hikuva tanihi leswi lwandle leri ro chavisa ri rhendzeleke tiko leri nga ni rihlaza, hi ndlela yoleyo emoyeni wa munhu ku ni Tahiti yin’we ya le xihlaleni, leyi teleke hi ku rhula ni ntsako, kambe yi rhendzeriwe hi swilo hinkwaswo leswi chavisaka swa vutomi lebyi tiviwaka hafu. Xikwembu xi ku hlayise! U nga susumeti ekule exihlaleni xexo, a wu nge pfuki u vuyile!





NDZIMA YA 59. Squid.

Hi ku nonoka yi ri karhi yi famba-famba emakhwatini ya brit, Pequod ya ha khome endleleni ya yona yi kongoma en’walungu-vuxa yi kongoma exihlaleni xa Java; moya wo olova lowu a wu susumeta xirhendzevutana xa yena, lerova eka ku rhula loku rhendzeleke swigolonyana swa yena swinharhu swo leha leswi rhetaka swi tshukatshuka hi ku olova eka moya wolowo wo olova, tanihi mavoko manharhu yo olova erivaleni. Naswona hambi swi ri tano, hi nkarhi wo anama nivusiku bya silivhere, xihaha-mpfhuka lexi nga xoxe, lexi kokaka rinoko a xi ta voniwa.

Kambe mixo wun’wana wa wasi lowu nga erivaleni, loko ku miyela loku lavaka ku va loku nga riki ka ntumbuluko ku hangalaka ehenhla ka lwandle, hambi swi ri tano ku nga langutiwi hi ku rhula kwihi na kwihi loku yimisiweke; loko xisirhelelo xo leha xa dyambu lexi hatimaka ematini xi vonaka xi ri rintiho ra nsuku leri andlariweke ehenhla ka wona, ri lerisa xihundla xo karhi; loko magandlati lama rhetaka ma hlevetela swin’we loko ma ri karhi ma tsutsuma hi ku olova; eka ku miyela loku lokukulu ka xirhendzevutana lexi vonakaka xipoko xo hlamarisa xi voniwe hi Daggoo kusuka eka main-mast-head.

Ekule, ntshungu lowukulu wo basa wu tlakukile hi ku loloha, naswona wu tlakuka wu ya ehenhla na le henhla, naswona wu tihambanisa na muhlovo wa azure, eku heteleleni wu hatima emahlweni ka xirhendzevutani xa hina ku fana na xirhendzevutani xa gamboko, lexintshwa lexi rhetaka ku suka eswitsungeni. Thus glistening for a moment, as slowly it subsided, and sank. Then once more arose, and silently gleamed. It seemed not a whale; and yet is this Moby Dick? thought Daggoo. Again the phantom went down, but on re-appearing once more, with a stiletto-like cry that startled every man from his nod, the negro yelled out—“There! there again! there she breaches! right ahead! The White Whale, the White Whale!”

Upon this, the seamen rushed to the yard-arms, as in swarming-time the bees rush to the boughs. Bare-headed in the sultry sun, Ahab stood on the bowsprit, and with one hand pushed far behind in readiness to wave his orders to the helmsman, cast his eager glance in the direction indicated aloft by the outstretched motionless arm of Daggoo.

Whether the flitting attendance of the one still and solitary jet had gradually worked upon Ahab, so that he was now prepared to connect the ideas of mildness and repose with the first sight of the particular whale he pursued; however this was, or whether his eagerness betrayed him; whichever way it might have been, no sooner did he distinctly perceive the white mass, than with a quick intensity he instantly gave orders for lowering.

The four boats were soon on the water; Ahab's in advance, and all swiftly pulling towards their prey. Soon it went down, and while, with oars suspended, we were awaiting its reappearance, lo! in the same spot where it sank, once more it slowly rose. Almost forgetting for the moment all thoughts of Moby Dick, we now gazed at the most wondrous phenomenon which the secret seas have hitherto revealed to mankind. A vast pulpy mass, furlongs in length and breadth, of a glancing cream-colour, lay floating on the water, innumerable long arms radiating from its centre, and curling and twisting like a nest of anacondas, as if blindly to clutch at any hapless object within reach. No perceptible face or front did it have; no conceivable token of either sensation or instinct; but undulated there on the billows, an unearthly, formless, chance-like apparition of life.

As with a low sucking sound it slowly disappeared again, Starbuck still gazing at the agitated waters where it had sunk, with a wild voice exclaimed—“Almost rather had I seen Moby Dick and fought him, than to have seen thee, thou white ghost!”

“What was it, Sir?” said Flask.

“The great live squid, which, they say, few whale-ships ever beheld, and returned to their ports to tell of it.”

But Ahab said nothing; turning his boat, he sailed back to the vessel; the rest as silently following.

Whatever superstitions the sperm whalemen in general have connected with the sight of this object, certain it is, that a glimpse of it being so very unusual, that circumstance has gone far to invest it with portentousness. So rarely is it beheld, that though one and all of them declare it to be the largest animated thing in the ocean, yet very few of them have any but the most vague ideas concerning its true nature and form; notwithstanding, they believe it to furnish to the sperm whale his only food. For though other species of whales find their food above water, and may be seen by man in the act of feeding, the spermaceti whale obtains his whole food in unknown zones below the surface; and only by inference is it that any one can tell of what, precisely, that food consists. At times, when closely pursued, he will disgorge what are supposed to be the detached arms of the squid; some of them thus exhibited exceeding twenty and thirty feet in length. They fancy that the monster to which these arms belonged ordinarily clings by them to the bed of the ocean; and that the sperm whale, unlike other species, is supplied with teeth in order to attack and tear it.

There seems some ground to imagine that the great Kraken of Bishop Pontoppodan may ultimately resolve itself into Squid. The manner in which the Bishop describes it, as alternately rising and sinking, with some other particulars he narrates, in all this the two correspond. But much abatement is necessary with respect to the incredible bulk he assigns it.

By some naturalists who have vaguely heard rumors of the mysterious creature, here spoken of, it is included among the class of cuttle-fish, to which, indeed, in certain external respects it would seem to belong, but only as the Anak of the tribe.





CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
