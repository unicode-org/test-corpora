Chikonzero chakaita kuti Ahabhu arege kukwira muchikepe chatakataura chaiva ichi: mhepo negungwa zvakaunza dutu. Asi kunyangwe dai zvisina kudaro, angadai asina kuzomukwira, pamwe, achitonga nemaitiro ake akazotevera panguva dzakafanana - dai zvaive zvakadaro, nenzira yekurumbidza, akawana mhinduro yakaipa kumubvunzo waakabvunza. Kana vatorwa vaviri vachiyambuka Pine Barrens muNew York State, kana iyo yakaenzana dongo yeSalisbury Plain muEngland; kana vachingosangana mumasango asina kunaka akadaro, vaviri ava, kweupenyu hwavo hwose, havangagoni kudzivisa kukwazisana; ndokumira kwechinguvana kuti vapane nhau; uye, zvichida, kugara pasi kwekanguva uye kuzorora mukoniti: zvino, zvikuru sei zvomuzvarirwo kuti pazvisingaverengeki Pine Barrens uye Salisbury Mapani egungwa, maviri whaling midziyo descrying mumwe pamigumo yenyika-kubva chete Fanning's Island, kana kure kure King's Mills; zvikuru sei zvomuzvarirwo, ndinotaura, kuti mumamiriro ezvinhu akadaro zvikepe izvi hazvifanire kungochinjana chimvuramabwe, asi kuuya kuzosangana zvichiri pedyo, zvine hushamwari uye kushamwaridzana. Kune chikepe chakareba chisipo, muganhu wekunze, zvichida, une tsamba pabhodhi; chero zvazvingava, achava nechokwadi chekumubvumira kuva nemamwe mapepa ezuva rimwe chete gore kana maviri gare gare pane rekupedzisira pamafaira ake asina kujeka uye akapfekwa nezvigunwe. Uye pamwero, zvese izvi zvichave zvechokwadi maererano nengarava dze whaling dzinoyambuka imwe neimwe paivhu remafambiro pachayo, kunyangwe zvazvo dzakareba zvakaenzana dzisipo pamba. Nokuti mumwe wavo angave akagamuchira kuendeswa kwetsamba kubva kune mumwe wechitatu, uye zvino kure kure chikepe; uye mamwe emavara iwayo anogona kunge ari evanhu vengarava yaanosangana naye zvino.
