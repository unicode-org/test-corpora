CHITSAUKO 86. Muswe.

Vamwe vadetembi vakarumbidza ziso rakapfava remhembwe, neminhenga yakanaka yeshiri isingambopuki; shoma yekudenga, ndinopemberera muswe.

Kuverenga muswe mukuru weSperm Whale kuti utange panzvimbo iyoyo yehunde painotsvedza kusvika pabhanhire remurume, unosanganisira pamusoro wayo wepamusoro wega, nzvimbo inosvika makumi mashanu masikweya mafiti. Muviri wemidzi yaro inokura kuita mapanza maviri akafara, akasimba, akati sandara kana kuti mafizu, zvishoma nezvishoma zvichienda kune isingasviki inchi muukobvu. Pa crotch kana junction, idzi flukes dzinopindirana zvishoma, zvino mativi dzinodzokera kubva kune mumwe semapapiro, zvichisiya nzvimbo yakakura pakati. Muzvinhu zvipenyu hapana mitsara yerunako inotsanangurwa zvakanyanyisa kupfuura mumiganhu yecrescent yeiyi fluke. Pakukura kwayo kukuru muwhale yakazara, muswe unodarika mafiti makumi maviri kureba.

Nhengo yacho yese inoita kunge mubhedha wakakora wetsinga dzakasungwa; asi cheka mariri, uye unoona kuti mitsara mitatu yakasiyana inoiumba:—yepamusoro, yepakati, nechepazasi. Idzo shinda dziri kumusoro nezasi, dzakareba uye dzakachinjika; zviya zvepakati, zvipfupi kwazvo, uye zvichimhanya zvakachinjika pakati pezvikamu zvekunze. Ichi chimiro chevatatu, sechimwe chinhu chipi nechipi, chinopa simba kumuswe. Kumudzidzi wemadziro ekare echiRoma, musara wepakati unopa kuenderana kunoenderana nekosi yakatetepa yematiles inogara ichichinjana nedombo mune izvo zvinoshamisa zvisaririra zvekare, uye izvo pasina mubvunzo zvinopa zvakanyanya kusimba guru remasonry.

Asi sokunge kuti simba guru renzvimbo iyi mumuswe une tendinous rakanga risingakwani, kuwanda kwerevhiatani yose kunorukwa pamusoro neshinda uye shinda yemhasuru fibers uye filaments, iyo inopfuudza kune rumwe rutivi chiuno uye ichimhanya ichidzika ichidzika mumakumbo, zvisinganzwisisike kusanganiswa nawo, uye zvakanyanya kubatsira kusimba ravo; zvekuti mumuswe simba rakabatana risingayereki rewhale yese rinoita kunge rakanangana kusvika padanho. Kuparadza kunogona kuitika kune chinhu, ichi ndicho chaiva chinhu chekuita.

Uye izvi hazviiti—simba rayo rinoshamisa, zvachose rinokombamira kuremadza kutenderedza kunofadza kwekufamba kwayo; uko hucheche hwekureruka hunowedzera kuburikidza neTitanism yesimba. Mukupesana, zvimiro izvozvo zvinowana runako rwazvo runotyisa zvikuru kubva mazviri. Simba chairo harimbokanganisi runako kana kuwirirana, asi rinowanzoripa; uye muzvinhu zvose zvinoyevedza, simba rine zvakawanda zvokuita nemashiripiti. Bvisa matinji akasungwa ayo ese anoita seanoputika kubva kumarble muHercules yakavezwa, uye kunaka kwayo kwaizoenda. Sezvo Eckerman akazvipira achisimudza jira rerineni kubva pachitunha chisina kusimira chaGoethe, akakurirwa nechipfuva chihombe chemurume, chaiita sechitsiga chekukunda kweRoma. Kana Angelo akapenda kunyangwe Mwari Baba ari muchimiro chemunhu, tarisa kuti kusimba kuripo kwakadii. Uye chipi kana chipi chavanogona kuzivisa nezvorudo rwoumwari muMwanakomana, mifananidzo yakapfava, yakamonereka, ine hermaphroditical Italian, umo mufungo wake wakaiswa zvinobudirira zvikurusa; mapikicha aya, ane hurombo sehwune hukasha hwese, haatauri chero simba ripi zvaro, asi kungoipa, kwechikadzi kwekuzviisa pasi uye kutsungirira, izvo zvinobvumwa nemaoko ese, zvinoumba hunoshamisa hunoshanda kunaka kwedzidziso dzake.

Ikoko ndiko kunyoroka kwenhengo yandinobata nayo, kuti ingave inoitwa mumutambo, kana muchokwadi, kana mukutsamwa, chero chimiro chayo, mapeji ayo anogara achiratidzwa nenyasha dzinopfuura. Imomo hakuna ruoko rwemunyambiri runogona kupfuura narwo.

Mafambiro mashanu makuru akasiyana nazvo. Kutanga, kana ichishandiswa sechiperedzo chekufambira mberi; Chechipiri, painoshandiswa semase muhondo; Chechitatu, mukutsvaira; Yechina, mukuvhima; Chechishanu, mukukwira kukuru.

Kutanga: Zvawakachinjika panzvimbo yawo, muswe weRevhiatani unoshanda nenzira yakasiyana nemiswe yezvimwe zvisikwa zvose zvomugungwa. Haambozununguki. Mumunhu kana hove, kugwinha-gwinha chiratidzo chekuderera. Kune whale, muswe wayo ndiyo yega nzira yekumhanyisa. Mupumburu-ungwaru wakasungirirwa kumberi pasi pemuviri, uye wobva wasimuka nekumashure, ndizvo zvinopa kuti museve umwechete, kusvetuka kuenda kune chikara kana ichituhwina nehasha. Maside-fins ake anoshanda chete kutungamirira.

Chechipiri: Kunokosha zvishoma, kuti nepo imwe sperm whale ichingorwisana neimwe sperm whale nomusoro wayo neshaya, zvisinei, mukunetsana kwayo nomunhu, iye zvikurukuru uye nenzira yokuzvidza anoshandisa muswe wake. Mukurova chikepe, anokurumidza kutenderedza matsimba ake kubva pachiri, uye kurohwa kunongoitwa nekudzoreredza. Kana ikagadzirwa mumhepo isina kuvharwa, kunyanya kana ichidzika kune chiratidzo chayo, sitiroko inongove isingadzivisiki. Hapana mbabvu dzemunhu kana igwa rinogona kumira nazvo. Ruponeso rwako rwega rwuri mukunzvenga; asi kana ikauya nedivi nemumvura inopikisa, zvimwe nekuda kwekupenya kwechikepe chewhale, uye kunyungudika kwemidziyo yayo, mbabvu yakatsemuka kana puranga rakapwanyika kana maviri, rudzi rwekusona padivi, kazhinji ndiyo yakanyanya mhedzisiro. Uku kurova kweparutivi kwakanyudzwa mumvura kunowanzogamuchirwa muhove, zvokuti kunorangarirwa kuva kutamba kwomwana. Mumwe anobvisa dombo, gomba romiswa.

Chechitatu: Handigoni kuzviratidzira, asi zvinoratidzika kwandiri, kuti muwhale pfungwa yekubata inonangidzirwa mumuswe; nokuti panyaya iyi mune chinonaka chirimo chinongoenzana nekunaka kwehunde yenzou. Kunaka uku kunonyanya kuratidzwa mukutsvaira, apo mukupfava kwehumhandara whale ine kumwe kunonokera kwakapfava ichifambisa mhepo dzayo huru kubva kune rumwe rutivi kuenda kune rumwe pamusoro pegungwa; uye kana achinzwa ndebvu dzemufambisi wechikepe, ane nhamo mufambisi wechikepe uyo, ndebvu nezvose. Unyoro hwakadini huri mukubata ikoko kwokutanga! Dai muswe uyu uine simba risingaite, ndaifanira kubva ndatofunga nezve nzou yeDarmonodes yaiwanzogara pamusika wemaruva, uye nekukwazisa kwakaderera kwakaunza mhino kuvasikana, vobva vapuruzira nzvimbo dzavo. Pamusoro peakaunti dzakawanda kupfuura imwe, tsitsi ndedzekuti whale haina hunhu hwepamberi mumuswe wayo; nokuti ndakanzwa nezveimwe nzou, kuti payakakuvadzwa mukurwa, yakakombamira pahunde ndokuvhomora museve.

Chechina: Kubira asingazivi pamusoro pehove mukati mekuchengetedzwa kwekufungidzira kwepakati pemakungwa ari oga, unomuwana asina kunyungudika kubva muhukuru hukuru hwechiremera chake, uye semwana wekatsi, anotamba pamusoro pegungwa sokunge kuti inzvimbo yemoto. Asi zvakadaro unoona simba rake mukutamba kwake. Machanza akafara emuswe wayo akatambanudzirwa mudenga; ipapo kurova pamusoro, iko kutinhira kunonzwika kwemamaira. Ungafunga kuti pfuti huru yabudiswa; uye kana ukacherechedza chiedza chemhute kubva kune spiracle kune imwe nzvimbo yake, iwe unofunga kuti iyo ndiyo iutsi kubva paburi rekubata.

Chechishanu: Sezvakangoita kuyangarara kwerevhiatani matsimba anorara zvakanyanya pazasi pehupamhi hwemusana wake, anobva asisaonekwe zvachose pasi pepamusoro; asi kana ave kuda kunyura mumvura yakadzika, matsimba ake ose nemamita angangoita makumi matatu emuviri wake anokandwa mudenga mudenga, uye ogara achibvunda kwekanguva, kusvika apfura pasi asingaonekwe. Kunze kwekutyoka kukuru—kumwewo kunofanira kurondedzerwa—uku kukwira kwemafungu ewhale pamwe ndicho chiono chakanakisisa chingaonekwa muzvisikwa zvose zvipenyu. Kubva muhudzamu husina magumo muswe muhombe unoratidzika kunge uri kubvuta nepamusoro pedenga. Saka muzviroto, ndakaona satani mukuru achiburitsa kachira kake kekurwadza kubva mumurazvo weBaltic weGehena. Asi mukutarisa zviitiko zvakadaro, zvese zviri muchimiro chauri; kana muDhani, madhimoni achaitika kwamuri; kana mune iyo yaIsaya, ngirozi huru. Ndakamira pamusoro-soro wechikepe changu mukati mekubuda kwezuva kwakatsvuka denga negungwa, ndakamboona boka guru remawhale kumabvazuva, ose akananga kuzuva, uye kwechinguvana achibvunda mukonzati nemafungu akanyanya. Sezvazvairatidzika kwandiri panguva iyoyo, mufananidzo wakaisvonaka wakadaro wokunamatwa kwavamwari hauna kumboonekwa, kunyange muPersia, musha wavanamati vemoto. Sokupupurira kwakaita Ptolemy Philopater nezvenzou yomuAfrica, ndakabva ndapupurira whale, ndichimuti munhu anotya Mwari kupfuura vanhu vose. Nekuti sekutaura kwaMambo Juba, nzou dzechiuto dzekare dzaiwanzorumbidza mangwanani nehunde yadzo yakasimudzwa mukunyarara kwakanyanya.

Mukana wekuenzanisa muchitsauko chino, pakati pewhale nenzou, kusvika pakuita zvimwe zvinhu zvemuswe weimwe uye hunde yeimwe, hazvifanirwe kuisa iyo miviri miviri yakapesana pane yakaenzana, ndoda zvisikwa zvavanenge vari. Nokuti sezvo nzou ine simba kwazvo inongova chiseko cheRevhiatani, saizvozvowo kana ichienzaniswa nemuswe weRevhiatani, hunde yayo inongova dzinde rehapa. Kurova kwaityisa zvikuru kwakabva pahunde yenzou kwaiva kuridza fani kwefani, kana zvichienzaniswa nekupwanya uye kubondera kusingayereki kwehosha dzesperm whale, iyo muzviitiko zvakadzokororwa inoita kuti imwe pashure peimwe ikandira magwa ose ane zvikwasvi zvose nevashandi vawo mudenga, kungofanana nomurwi wokuIndia anokandira mabhora ake.

*Kunyange zvazvo kuenzanisa kwose munzira yokuwanda kukuru pakati pewhale nenzou kusingafadzi, sezvinongoitawo nzou inomira nenzira yakafanana kune whale iyo imbwa inoita kunzou; zvisinei, hapasi kuda mamwe mapoinzi ekuda kuziva fananidzo; pakati pazvo pane mupopo. Zvinonyatsozivikanwa kuti nzou inowanzochera mvura kana huruva muhunde yayo, yozoisimudza, yoikanda murukova.

Pandinonyanya kufunga nezvemuswe une simba uyu, ndipo pandinonyanya kushungurudzira kusakwanisa kwangu kuutaura. Pane dzimwe nguva kune majeisheni mairi, ayo, kunyangwe angada zvakanaka ruoko rwemunhu, anoramba asingatsananguriki zvachose. Muboka rakakura, zvinoshamisa, apo neapo, aya maitiro asinganzwisisike, zvekuti ndakanzwa vavhimi vakazvitaura zvakafanana nezviratidzo nezviratidzo zveFree-Mason; kuti whale, zvechokwadi, nenzira idzi dzakangwara dzakakurukura nenyika. Uye hapana kuda kumwe kufamba kwewhale mumuviri wake wese, azere nekukatyamadza, uye asingazvidavirire kune wake ane ruzivo rwakanyanya. Mumuzunungurirei, zvino ini ndingapinda munyama; ini handivazivi, uye handingatongovazivi. Asi kana ndisingazivi kunyange muswe wehove iyi, musoro wayo unonzwisisa sei? Ndoda kunzwisisa chiso chake sei, zvaasina chiso? Uchaona musana wangu, muswe wangu, anoita seanotaura, asi chiso changu hachionekwi. Asi handigoni kududzira musana wake; uye ndiratidze zvaachada pamusoro pechiso chake, ndinotizve haana chiso.





CHITSAUKO 87. The Grand Armada.

Iyo yakareba uye yakamanikana peninsula yeMalacca, inotambanukira kumaodzanyemba-kumabvazuva kubva munzvimbo dzeBirmah, inoumba nzvimbo yekumaodzanyemba kweAsia yese. Mumutsara unopfuurira unobva mupeninsula iyoyo unotambanudza zvitsuwa zvirefu zveSumatra, Java, Bally, uye Timor; iyo, nemamwe akawanda, inoumba mhasuru hombe, kana kuti mhanda, inobatanidza Asia neAustralia kureba, uye inokamura gungwa reIndia refu risina kutsemuka kubva muzvitsuwa zvakakora zvekumabvazuva. Iyi rampart inoboorwa nemasally-ports akati wandei kuitira kurerukira ngarava nemawhale; anoonekwa pakati pawo madhirigi eSunda neMalacca. Nekuyerera kweSunda, kunyanya, ngarava dzinoenda kuChina kubva kumadokero, dzinobuda mumakungwa eChina.

Idzo nzira dzakatetepa dzeSunda dzinopatsanura Sumatra kubva kuJava; uye ndimire pakati nepakati pemurwi mukuru wezvitsuwa, wakatsigirirwa neuyo denga rakasvibira, rinozivikanwa nevafambisi vezvikepe seJava Head; hazvifananidzi zvishoma nesuwo repakati rinopinda mune humwe hukuru hwakakomberedzwa humambo: uye tichifunga nezve hupfumi husingaperi hwezvinonhuwira, nesirika, nematombo, negoridhe, nenyanga dzenzou, izvo zvinopfumiswa nezvitsuwa zviuru zvegungwa rekumabvazuva, zvinoratidzika sechipo chakakosha chezvakasikwa, kuti hupfumi hwakadaro, nekuumbwa kweiyo nyika, kunofanira kuchengetedza chitarisiko, zvisinei. nyika yekumadokero inobata zvese. Mhenderekedzo dzeStraits of Sunda hadzina kupihwa neiyo nhare dzine simba dzinorinda mikova yeMediterranean, Baltic, uye Propontis. Kusafanana navaDanes, ava vokuMabvazuva havadi kuti ruremekedzo rwakashata rwemaseiri akadzikiswa kubva mudungwe risingaperi rengarava pamberi pemhepo, idzo kwemazana amakore apfuura, usiku namasikati, dzaipfuura pakati pezvitsuwa zveSumatra neJava, dzakatakurwa nemitoro inodhura zvikuru yokumabvazuva. Asi kunyange zvazvo vachisiya vakasununguka mhemberero yakadai, havamborambi kutaura kwavo kune mutero wakasimba.

Nguva yekusarangarira piratical proas dzeMalay, dzakavanda pakati pezvitsuwa zvine mumvuri uye zvitsuwa zveSumatra, vakakwira pamusoro pezvikepe zviri kufamba nemumakwara, vachida mutero zvine utsinye papfumo remapfumo avo. Kunyange zvazvo nokurangwa kunoteura ropa kunoramba kuchiitwa kwavakaitwa nevafambisi vengarava vokuEurope, ushingi hwemakorosari aya hwakambodzvinyirirwa; asi, kunyangwe nanhasi uno, tinombonzwa nezve ngarava dzeChirungu neAmerica, idzo, mumvura idzodzo, dzakakwidzwa nekupambwa nekusazvidemba.

Nemhepo yakanaka, yakachena, Pequod yakanga yava kuswedera pedyo nezvinetso izvi; Ahabhi achivavarira kupfuura nepakati pavo achipinda mugungwa reJavani, uye kubva ipapo, achienda kuchamhembe, pamusoro pemvura inozivikanwa kuva inogarwa pano neapo neSperm Whale, kutsvaira mumhenderekedzo yeChitsuwa chePhilippine, uye kuwana mhenderekedzo yegungwa iri kure yeJapan, munguva yemwaka mukuru wehove imomo. Nenzira idzi, Pequod inotenderera yaizotsvaira dzinenge dzese dzinozivikanwa Sperm Whale cruising nzvimbo dzepasirese, isati yadzika paMutsetse muPacific; uko Ahabhi, kunyange kumwe kwose kwose akakanganisa mukutsvaka kwake, akaverenga zvakasimba kuti aizorwa naMoby Dick, mugungwa aizivikanwa zvikuru kugara; uye panguva yaanogona kufungidzirwa kuti ari kunetsa.

Asi sei zvino? mukutsvaga kwenzvimbo iyi, Ahabi haabati nyika here? vashandi vake vanonwa mhepo here? Zvirokwazvo, achamira achitsvaka mvura. Kwete. Kwenguva refu, zvino, zuva rinomhanya sekisikisi rakamhanya mukati memhete yake yemoto, uye haridi chekudya kunze kwezviri maari. Saka Ahabhu. Maka izvi, zvakare, mune whaler. Nepo mamwe mabhuru akaremerwa nezvinhu zvekunze, kuti zviendeswe kune dzimwe nyika; ngarava yewhale-nyika inodzungaira haitakuri zvinhu asi iye pachake nevashandi, zvombo zvavo nezvavanoda. Ane zviri mudhamu rose zvakaiswa mumabhodhoro muhomwe yake yakakwana. Ane ballasted nezvinhu zvinobatsira; kwete pamwe chete nenguruve-lead uye kentledge isingashandisi. Anotakura makore mazhinji maari. Bvisa mvura yekare prime Nantucket; iyo, kana makore matatu achiyangarara, iyo Nantucketer, muPacific, inofarira kunwa pamberi pemvura ine brackish, asi nezuro yakasimudzwa mumakasi, kubva kuPeruvia kana Indian nzizi. Saka zviri, kuti, nepo dzimwe ngarava dzingave dzakaenda kuChina kubva kuNew York, uye kudzoka zvakare, dzichibata pazvikepe zvezvikepe, iyo whale-ngarava, munguva yose iyoyo, inogona kunge isina kuona tsanga imwe yevhu; vashandi vake vakanga vasina kuona murume kunze kwevafambisi vezvikepe vaiyangarara sezvavo. Saka wakavasvitsa shoko rokuti imwe mafashamo auya; vaizopindura bedzi kuti—“Eya, vakomana, heyi areka!”

Zvino, sezvo maSperm Whale mazhinji akanga atorwa kubva kumhenderekedzo yokumadokero yeJava, munharaunda iri pedyo yeStraits of Sunda; zvirokwazvo, sezvo nzvimbo zhinji, yakatenderedzwa, yaiwanzozivikanwa nevabati vehove senzvimbo yakanaka kwazvo yekufamba nechikepe; saka, sezvo Pequod yakawedzera kuwedzera pamusoro peJava Head, kutarisa-outs kwaiwanzorumbidzwa, uye yakarayirwa kuti irambe yakamuka. Asi kunyange zvazvo mawere akasvibira epanyika akakurumidza kuoneka pauta hwenyeredzi, uye nemhino dzinofadza sinamoni itsva yakafutirwa mumhepo, zvakadaro hapana kana jeti imwe yakashorwa. Vachidokuramba pfungwa yose yokupinda mumhuka, ngarava yakanga yotopinda munzira, apo mheremhere yetsika yakanzwika mudenga, uye takabva takwazisa runako rwakareba.

Asi pano ngazviitwe, kuti nekuda kwechiitiko chisina kuneta chavakavhimwa nacho pamusoro pemakungwa ese mana, maSperm Whales, panzvimbo yekuti anogara achifamba mumakambani madiki akavharirwa, semunguva dzakapfuura, zvino anowanzo kusangana nemapoka makuru, dzimwe nguva achimbundira chaunga chikuru, zvekuti zvingaite sekunge nyika mbiri nedziviriro dzakabatana. Kune uku kuunganidzwa kweSperm Whale mumakaravhani akakura kudaro, anogona kuverengerwa mamiriro ekuti kunyangwe munzvimbo dzakanakisa dzekufamba, iwe zvino dzimwe nguva unogona kufamba nechikepe kwemavhiki nemwedzi pamwe chete, pasina kukwaziswa neimwe spout; uye wozokwaziswa kamwe kamwe neicho dzimwe nguva chinoratidzika kuva zviuru nezviuru.

Yakapamhama pauta hwese, pachinhambwe chemamaira maviri kana matatu, uye ichigadzira semicircle huru, ichimbundikira hafu yenhanho yehusiku, cheni inoenderera yewhale-jets yaive ichitamba uye ichipenya mumhepo yemasikati. Kusiyana neakatwasuka perpendicular twin-jets eRight Whale, ayo, akakamura kumusoro, anowira pamusoro mumatavi maviri, sematavi akatsemuka ewillow, iyo imwe chete inotsvedza inoyerera yeSperm Whale inopa gwenzi gobvu rakamonereka remhute chena, ichiramba ichikwira uye ichidonha ichienda kure.

Kuonekwa kubva padhiza rePequod, zvino, paaikwira pamusoro pechikomo chegungwa, iyi mhute yemhute, yega yega ichikwidibira mudenga, uye ichiona nemhepo yakasanganiswa yehute hutema, yairatidza sechiuru chinofara cheimwe metropolis yakaomesesa, inotsanangurwa nezvemutyairi webhiza anotonhorera mangwanani, nepamusoro-soro.

Sezvo mauto anofamba achiswedera kune tsvina isina ushamwari mumakomo, anokurumidzisa kufamba kwawo, chido chose chokuisa nzira iyoyo ine ngozi kumashure kwawo, uyezve kuwedzera mukuchengeteka kunoenzaniswa pabani; kunyange zvakadaro boka guru remhungu zvino rakaita serikumhanyira mberi nomumakwava; zvishoma nezvishoma vachibata mapapiro e semicircle yavo, uye kushambira pamusoro, mune imwe yakasimba, asi ichiri crescentic centre.

Kuwanda kwese kwaifamba Pequod yakatsikirira shure kwavo; varwi vakabata zvombo zvavo, uye vachipururudza nepamusoro pemisoro yezvikepe zvavo zvakamiswa. Kudai mhepo yakaramba chete, panikiro duku vakanga vadai, vakatandanisa neaya maStraits of Sunda, boka guru raizongopinda mumakungwa okuMabvazuva kuti rione kutorwa kwevashoma vechiverengero chavo. Uye ndiani aigona kuziva kana, mukaravhani iyoyo yakaungana, Moby Dick pachake angave asiri kushambira kwenguva pfupi, senzou chena inonamatwa iri mumudungwe wokugadzwa pachigaro wevaSiamese! Naizvozvo, takanga tichifamba nechikepe, tichidzingirira ngwena idzi pamberi pedu; apo, kamwe kamwe, inzwi raTashtego rakanzwika, richinangidzira ngwariro zvikuru kune chimwe chinhu mukumuka kwedu.

Zvichienderana nekamwe kacho mukombi yedu, takaona imwe kumashure kwedu.
