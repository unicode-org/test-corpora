Pano, zvino, pane hove huru mbiri, dzakaisa misoro yadzo pamwe chete; ngatizvibatanidze, tigoisa zvedu pamwechete.

Pakurongeka kukuru kwefolio leviathans, Sperm Whale neRight Whale ndizvo zvinonyanya kukosha. Ndiwo ega hove dzinogarovhimwa nevanhu. KuNantucketer, vanopa iwo maviri akanyanyisa emarudzi ese anozivikanwa ewhale. Sezvo musiyano wekunze pakati pavo uchinyanya kucherechedzwa mumisoro yavo; uye semusoro weumwe neumwe inguva ino yakarembera kubva kudivi rePequod; uye sezvatinogona kubva takasununguka kubva kune imwe tichienda kune imwe, nekungotsika mhiri dhizaini: - kupi, pandinofanira kuziva, iwe uchawana mukana uri nani wekudzidza inoshanda cetology kupfuura pano?

Chekutanga, unoshamiswa nekusiyana kwakawanda pakati pemisoro iyi. Ose ari maviri akakura zvakakwana muhana dzose; asi pane imwe symmetry yemasvomhu muSperm Whale iyo iyo Right Whale inoshaya zvinosiririsa. Kune humwe hunhu mumusoro weSperm Whale. Sezvaunozviona, unopa hukuru hukuru kwaari nokusazvidira, muchimiro chechiremera chakazara. Muchiitiko chazvino, zvakare, chiremerera ichi chinokwidziridzwa nepepi uye ruvara rwemunyu wemusoro wake pamusangano, zvichipa chiratidzo chezera uye ruzivo rwakakura. Muchidimbu, ndiye anonzi nevaredzi “grey-headed whale.”

Iye zvino ngaticherechedzei zvisinganyanyi kusiyana pamisoro iyi—zvinoti, nhengo mbiri dzinonyanya kukosha, ziso nenzeve. Kure kumashure padivi pemusoro, uye pasi pasi, pedyo nekona yerushaya rwewhale, kana ukatsvaga neburi retsono, pakupedzisira unozoona ziso risina mhezi, raungada kuti rive ziso remwana wembongoro; naizvozvo kubva pachiyero chiduku kusvika paukuru hwomusoro.

Zvino, kubva padivi penzvimbo iyi yemaziso ewhale, zviri pachena kuti haigoni kuona chinhu chiri mberi chaiko, sezvaanogona kuona kuzasi chaiko. Mushoko rimwe, nzvimbo yemaziso ewhale inofanana neyenzeve dzomunhu; uye unogona kuzvifungira, kuti zvichakufambira sei kwauri, waiongorora zvinhu nemunzeve dzako. Waizoona kuti waikwanisa kuraira mamwe madhigirii makumi matatu ekuona pamberi pemutsara wakatwasuka wekuona; uye vamwe vanenge makumi matatu shure kwayo. Kana muvengi wako ane hasha achifamba akananga kwauri, munondo wakasimudzwa masikati machena, haungamuoni, kungofanana kana akakuba pamusoro pako kubva kumashure. Mushoko, unenge uine misana miviri, sekutaura; asi, panguva imwe cheteyo, maivawo nemativi maviri: nokuti chii chinogadzira mberi kwemunhu, asi maziso ake?

Uyezve, nepo mune dzimwe mhuka dzakawanda dzandinogona kufunga nezvadzo zvino, maziso akadyarwa zvisingaoneki kuti asanganise simba rawo rokuona, kuti abudise mufananidzo mumwe chete kwete miviri kuuropi; nzvimbo yechienzi yemaziso ewhale, akakamurwa zvinobudirira sezvaari nemakubhiki mafiti akawanda emusoro wakasimba, uyo unopfumbira pakati pawo segomo guru rinoparadzanisa madziva maviri mumipata; izvi, zvamazvirokwazvo, zvinofanirwa kupatsanura zvizere zvinoonekwa zvinopihwa nenhengo yega yega yakazvimirira. Iyo whale, naizvozvo, inofanira kuona mufananidzo wakajeka kune rumwe rutivi, uye mumwe mufananidzo wakasiyana kune iyo; nepo zvose zviri pakati zvinofanira kuva rima guru uye pasina chinhu kwaari. Munhu anogona kunge achinzi anotarisa pasirese kubva mubhokisi revarindi rine masashi maviri akabatanidzwa pahwindo rake. Asi ne whale, mabhanhire maviri aya akaiswa zvakasiyana, achiita mahwindo maviri akasiyana, asi zvinosuruvarisa kukanganisa maonero. Ichi chimiro chemaziso ewhale chinhu chinofanira kugara chichiyeukwa mupfungwa mukubata hove; uye kurangarirwa nemuverengi mune zvimwe zviitiko zvinotevera.

Mubvunzo unokatyamadza uye unokatyamadza ungatangwa pamusoro pechinhu ichi chinooneka sekubata Revhiatani. Asi ndinofanira kugutsikana nezano. Chero bedzi maziso omunhu akasvinura muchiedza, chiito chokuona hachidikanirwi; ndiko kuti, haakwanisi ipapo kubatsira kuona chero zvinhu zviri pamberi pake. Kunyanguvezvo, chinoitika chomunhu upi noupi chichamudzidzisa, kuti kunyange zvazvo achigona kutsvaira asingasaruri zvinhu nokungotarira kamwe, hakubviri zvikuru kuti iye, nokungwarira, uye zvakakwana, aongorore zvinhu zviviri zvipi nezvipi—zvisinei zvikuru kana kuti zviduku sei—pachimwe chete. nenguva imwe cheteyo yenguva; hazvinetse kana vakarara nedivi vakabatana. Asi kana mauya kuzoparadzanisa zvinhu zviviri izvi, uye mokomberedza chimwe nechimwe nedenderedzwa rerima guru; zvino, kuitira kuti uone imwe yadzo, nenzira yekuunza pfungwa dzako kuti dzizviise pairi, imwe yacho inozobviswa zvachose kubva mukuziva kwako kwazvino. Zvino sei ne whale? Chokwadi, maziso ake ose ari maviri mavari, anofanira kuita zvinhu panguva imwe chete; asi uropi hwake hunonyatsonzwisisa here, hunobatanidza, uye husina mano kupfuura hwomunhu, zvokuti panguva imwe cheteyo anogona kunyatsoongorora kariro mbiri dzakasiyana, imwe kune rimwe divi rake, uye imwe iri kune rimwe divi chairo? Kana achikwanisa, saka chinhu chinoshamisa maari, sekunge murume aikwanisa panguva imwe chete kuratidzwa kwezvinetso zviviri zvakasiyana muEuclid. Uyewo, kunyatsoongororwa, hapana kusawirirana mukuenzanisa uku.

Kungangove kungova kupenga, asi zvakagara zvichiratidzika kwandiri, kuti kusagadzikana kunoshamisa kwekufamba kunoratidzwa nemamwe mawhale kana akakomberedzwa nezvikepe zvitatu kana zvina; kutya uye mutoro wekutyisa zvinotyisa, zvakajairika kune akadaro whale; Ini ndinofunga kuti zvese izvi zvisina kunanga zvinobva mukukanganisika kusingabatsiri kwekuzvidira, umo masimba avo akapatsanurwa uye akapesana nepakati ekuona anofanira kuvasanganisa.

Asi nzeve yewhale yakazara kuda kuziva seziso. Kana iwe uri mutorwa zvachose kurudzi rwavo, unogona kuvhima pamusoro pemisoro miviri iyi kwemaawa, uye usamboona iyo nhengo. Nzeve haina shizha rekunze chero; uye mugomba pacharo haugone kupinza quill, saka zvinoshamisa miniti ndiyo. Yakaiswa zvishoma kuseri kweziso. Nekuremekedza nzeve dzavo, musiyano unokosha uyu unofanirwa kuonekwa pakati pesperm whale uye yakarurama. Nepo nzeve yepakutanga iine kuvhura kwekunze, iyo yekupedzisira yakazara uye yakaenzana yakavharwa ne membrane, kuitira kuti isanyatso kuoneka kubva kunze.

Hazvisi kuda kuziva here, kuti chisikwa chakakura kudai sewhale chinofanira kuona nyika neziso diki, uye kunzwa kutinhira nenzeve idiki pane yetsuro? Asi kana maziso ake aive akafara selenzi yeteresikopu huru yeHerschel; nzeve dzake dzakadzivira semaberere edzimba dzokutonga; Izvozvo zvingamuita kuti aonezve kana kupinza pakunzwa here? Kwete.—Saka nei uchiedza ku“kudza” pfungwa dzako? Subtilize it.

Ngatiregei zvino nezvipimo nezvipi-injini zvatinazvo pedyo, tisingakwanise pamusoro pemusoro wesperm whale, kuti irare pasi kumusoro; zvino, uchikwira nemanera kuenda kunhongonya, tarisa pasi muromo; uye dai pasina kuti mutumbi zvino waparadzaniswa zvachose nawo, nerambi taigona kudzika muKentucky Mammoth Cave guru redumbu rake. Asi ngatibatirei pano nezino iri, titarise kwatiri. Muromo wakanaka chaizvo uye wakachena! kubva pauriri kusvika kusirin'i, ine mitsetse, kana kuti tingatoti yakanamirwa nepepa jena rinopenya, rinopenya sema satin emwenga.

Asi buda zvino, uye utarise shaya iri yezasi inoshamisa, inoita sevhavha refu nhete yebhokisi refodya hombe, rine hinji kumucheto, panzvimbo yedivi rimwe. Kana iwe ukaiputira kumusoro, kuitira kuti uiise pamusoro, uye ibudise mitsara yemazino ayo, inoita seyakaipa portcullis; uye zvakadaro, maiwe! zvinoratidza kuvazhinji varombo mukubata hove, pavari pavari mapikisi aya anowira nesimba rokuroverera. Asi chinotyisa zvikuru kuona, kana mafatomu ari pasi mugungwa, unoona whale yakanyungudika, ichiyangarara ipapo yakarembera, ine rushaya rwayo runoyevedza, rwakareba mafiti gumi nemashanu, yakarembera yakananga kumakona ekurudyi nemuviri wake, nyika kunge jib-boom yechikepe. Hove iyi haina kufa; anongoora moyo; kunze kwemhando, pamwe; hypochondriac; uye kunyungudika, zvokuti mahinji eshaya dzake asununguka, zvichimusiya arimo murudzi rwakaipa kudaro rwemamiriro ezvinhu, ruzvidzo kurudzi rwake rwose, uyo anofanira, pasina panikiro, kuroverera shaya dzokukiya paari.

Muzviitiko zvizhinji iyi shaya yepasi—ichisununguka nyore nyore nenyanzvi yokudhirowa—inobviswa uye inosimudzwa pamusoro pedenga nechinangwa chokubvisa mazino enyanga dzenzou, uye kupa mugove we<em>whalebone jena rakaomarara iro vabati vehove vanogadzira naro marudzi ose ezvinhu zvinofadza, zvinosanganisira nzimbe, amburera-zvitokisi, uye mibato yekutasva-zvikwapuro.

Nekurebesa, kuneta, rushaya runokweverwa pabhodhi, sechinhu chinomisa; uye apo nguva yakafanira inosvika—mamwe mazuva mashomanene pashure perimwe basa—Queequeg, Daggoo, naTashtego, vose vari vanachiremba vemazino vane unyanzvi, vanogadzirira kukweva mazino. Nekunaka kwekucheka-spade, Queequeg inobaya matinji; zvino shaya inoroverwa pasi kusvika kumabhaudhi, uye simbi ichicherwa kubva kumusoro, vanokweva mazino aya, semombe dzeMichigan dzinokweva zvigutsa zveoki kubva musango. Kazhinji kune mazino makumi mana nemaviri pamwe chete; mumawhale ekare, akasakara zvakanyanya, asi asina kuora; kana kuzadzwa nefashoni yedu. Rushaya rwunozocherwa kuita zvidimbu, zvounganidzwa semajoini ekuvaka dzimba.





CHITSAUKO 75. Musoro Werudyi Whale—Maonero Akasiyana.

Tichiyambuka dhizaini, ngativei nekutarisa kwenguva refu kumusoro weRight Whale.

Sezvo muchimiro chakazara musoro weSperm Whale wakaisvonaka ungaenzaniswa nengoro yehondo yeRoma (zvikurukuru pamberi, apo yakatenderedzwa zvakafara); saka, pakuona kwakafara, musoro weRight Whale unobereka zvakada kufanana neshangu huru-yechigunwe. Makumi maviri emakore apfuura An Old Dutch Voyager akafananidza chimiro chayo kune iyo yekupedzisira yekupedzisira. Uye mune iyi yekupedzisira kana shangu, chembere iya yengano, ine vana vanofamba, inogona kugara zvakanaka, iye nevana vake vese.

Asi paunosvika padhuze nemusoro uyu mukuru unotanga kutora mativi akasiyana, zvichienderana nemaonero ako. Kana iwe ukamira pamusoro wayo uye ukatarisa aya maviri-akaita F-spoutholes, iwe unotora musoro wese kune yakakura bass-viol, uye aya maspiracles, maapertures mubhodhi rayo rekuridza. Zvadaro, zvakare, kana iwe ukagadzirisa ziso rako pane izvi zvinoshamisa, mucheche, kunge wakashata, ichi chakasvibirira, icho maGreenlandders, uye kuSouth Firthers "bonnet" ye Right Whale; uchitarisisa maziso ako pane izvi chete, waizotora musoro kuhunde yemumwe muouki muhombe, une dendere reshiri pachidyiro chawo. Chero zvazvingava, kana uchiona aya magaka mhenyu anorara pano pabhoneti iyi, pfungwa yakadai inenge ine chokwadi kuti ichaitika kwauri; kunze kwekunge, zvirokwazvo, fashoni yako yakagadziriswa nehunyanzvi izwi rekuti "korona" rakapihwawo pairi; muchiitiko icho iwe uchafarira zvikuru kufunga kuti ichi chikara chine simba chiri chaizvoizvo mambo wegungwa ane korona, ane korona yakasvibira yakaiswa pamwe chete nokuda kwake nenzira iyi inoshamisa. Asi kana hove iyi iri mambo, munhu ane hunyoro zvikuru kuti agadzirire korona. Tarisa muromo wezasi wakarembera! kwakakura sei sulk uye pout iripo! nechiyero chomuvezi, kureba kwacho kungasvika mamita makumi maviri pakureba, nokudzika mamita mashanu; sulk uye pout izvo zvinokupa iwe marita mazana mashanu emafuta uye anopfuura.

Tsitsi huru, ikozvino, kuti iyi whale inosuruvarisa inofanirwa kuve hare-lipped. Kutsemuka kunenge tsoka imwe kuyambuka. Zvichida amai panguva inokosha vaifamba nechikepe vachidzika nemhenderekedzo yePeruvia, apo kudengenyeka kwenyika kwakaita kuti mahombekombe egungwa apenye. Pamusoro pemuromo uyu, sepamusoro pechikumbaridzo chinotsvedza, isu zvino tinotsvedza mumuromo. Pamusoro peshoko rangu ndaiva kuMackinaw, ndinofanira kutora izvi kuva mukati meIndia wigwam. Makanaka Ishe! ndiyo nzira yakaenda Jona here? Denga racho rinosvika mamita gumi nemaviri pakukwirira, uye rinomhanya kukona kwakapinza, sekunge kune nguva dzose danda-danda ipapo; nepo mativi aya ane mbabvu, arched, ane mvere, anotipa neaya anoshamisa, hafu yakatwasuka, scimetar-shaped slats of whalebone, anoti mazana matatu parutivi, izvo zvinoenderana nepamusoro pemusoro kana korona pfupa, zvinogadzira iwo mapofu eVenetian ayo kune dzimwe nzvimbo dzakambotaurwa. Micheto yemapfupa aya ine shinda dzine mvere, kuburikidza neiyo Right Whale inonamira mvura, uye mune intricacies yaanochengeta hove diki, kana akashama muromo anopinda nemumakungwa ebrit munguva yekudyisa. Mapofu pakati pefupa, pavanomira mune yavo yekare, kune mamwe mamakisi ekuda kuziva, curves, mapopoma, nekwavangori veWhitle Kunyangwe chokwadi chechirevo ichi chiri kure nekusanotaridza, asi chine kunhuhwirira kweanoroji yemukana. Chero zvazvingava, kana tikazvibvumira, tinofanira kupa zera rakakura zvikuru kuRight Whale kupfuura pakuona kwekutanga kuchaita sekwakanaka.

Munguva dzakare, zvinoita sekunge zvakakunda zvishuwo zvekuda kuziva nezvemapofu aya. Mumwe mufambi muPurchas anodzidana kuti “ndebvu” dzinoshamisa dziri mukati memukanwa wewhale;* mumwewo, “mabristles enguruve”; mumwe murume wechitatu wekare muHackluyt anoshandisa mutauro unotevera wakanaka: “Kune zvimbi zvinenge mazana maviri nemakumi mashanu zvinomera kurutivi rumwe norumwe rwechibayo chake chepamusoro, chinovharira rurimi rwake kudivi rimwe nerimwe romuromo wake.”

*Izvi zvinotiyeuchidza kuti Right Whale zvechokwadi ine rudzi rwendebvu, kana kuti ndebvu, dzinosanganisira shoma shoma dzakapararira bvudzi chena pachikamu chepamusoro chekunze kweshaya yepasi. Dzimwe nguva idzi tufts dzinopa kutaridza kwehumbwende kune huso hwake hune hunyoro.

Sekuziva kwemunhu wese, aya ma“hogi,” “zvimbi,” “ndebvu,” “mapofu,” kana chipi zvacho chaunoda, anopa kumadzimai mabhasikoro avo nezvimwe zvinoomesa musoro. Asi mune izvi, zvinodiwa kare zvave kuderera. Yakanga iri munguva yaMambokadzi Anne apo bhonzo rakanga riri mukubwinya kwaro, iyo farthingale ndiyo iyo yose fashoni. Uye sezvo vakadzi ivavo vekare vaifamba-famba vachifara, kunyange mushaya dzewhale, sezvaungareva; Saizvozvovo, mukusasana, nokusafunga kwakafanana, isu nhasi tinobhururuka pasi peshaya imwe kuti tizvidzivirire; amburera iri tende rakawaridzwa pamusoro pebvupa rimwe.

Asi zvino kanganwa zvese nezvemapofu nendebvu kwechinguva, uye, wakamira mumuromo weRight Whale, tarisa zvakakupoteredza patsva. Kuona ese makoronganiso emapfupa akanyoroveswa zvakanyanya, hausati wanga uchifunga kuti uri mukati mehukuru hweHaarlement, uye uchitarisa mapepa ane chiuru? Kune kapeti kune nhengo tine rug yeTurkey yakapfava-rurimi, rwakanamira, sezviri, pasi pemuromo. Yakakora kwazvo uye ine nyoro, uye inokwanisa kubvarura kuita zvidimbu zvidimbu pakuisimudza panhovo. Rimwe rurimi rakati zvino pamberi pedu; nekutarisa zvishoma ndingati yaive nhanhatu-barreler; ndiko kuti, ichakupa huwandu hwemafuta iwayo.

Izvi zvisati zvaitika, unofanira kunge wakanyatsoona chokwadi chezvandakatanga nazvo-kuti Sperm Whale neRight Whale vane misoro yakasiyana zvachose. Kuti ubude kumusoro, ipapo: mune yakakodzera whale hapana tsime guru remasherm; hapana mazino enzou zvachose; kwete kwenguva refu, mutete mandible weshaya yezasi, seSperm Whale's. Kana mune sperm whale pane chero mapofu emapfupa; hapana muromo mukuru wezasi; uye kashoma nendimi. Zvekare, iyo yekurudyi whale ine maviri ekunze spout-maburi, iyo sperm whale chete.

Tarirai kokupedzisira, zvino, pamisoro inokudzwa ine vhudzi, vachavata pamwechete; nekuti munhu achakurumidza kunyura, asina kunyorwa, mugungwa; imwe yacho haizoreberi kutevera.

Unogona kubata kutaura kweSperm Whale ikoko? Ndizvo zvimwe chete zvaakafa nazvo, mamwe makwinya akareba pahuma anoita seapera. Ini ndinofunga huro yake yakafara kunge izere ne prairie-se placidity, yakazvarwa nekufungidzira kusava nehanya nerufu. Asi maka kutaura kwemumwe musoro. Ona iyo inoshamisa muromo wezasi, wakatsikirirwa netsaona kudivi rechikepe, kuitira kuti ugombundikira rushaya. Musoro wese uyu hauratidzike seunotaura nezvechisarudzo chikuru chinoshanda mukutarisana nerufu here? Iyi Right Whale ndinotora kunge yanga iri muStoic; iyo Sperm Whale, Platoian, angave akatora Spinoza mumakore ake ekupedzisira.





CHITSAUKO 76. The Battering-Ram.

Ere kusiya, nekuda kwekusarongeka, musoro weshemu whale, ndingadai ndakakuita iwe, seyechinyakare phepfenyuro, uchingotaura nezvake chimiro chayo, mune yese yekuunganidzwa. Ndinoda kuti iwe uiongorore iye zvino nemaonero ega ekuzvigadzirira imwe fungidziro isina kuwedzeredza, yehungwaru yechero simba rekurovera rinogona kuiswa ipapo. Iyi inhivi yakakosha; nokuti unofanira kugadzirisa nyaya iyi newe pachako zvinogutsa, kana kuti nekusingaperi unoramba uri musingatendi pane chimwe chezviitiko zvinotyisa zvikuru, asi kwete zviitiko zvechokwadi zvishoma, zvichida chero kupi zvingawanikwa munhoroondo yose yakanyorwa.

Iwe unoona kuti munzvimbo yakajairwa yekutuhwina yeSperm Whale, kumberi kwemusoro wayo kunopa inenge yakamira yakamira ndege kumvura; unoona kuti chikamu chezasi chepamberi icho chinotenuka zvakanyanya kumashure, kuitira kuti upe zvakawanda zvekudzokera kune socket refu inogamuchira boom-senge shaya yezasi; unocherekedza kuti muromo uri pasi pemusoro wose, zvikuru nenzira imwe cheteyo, zvamazvirokwazvo, sokunge kuti muromo wako umene uri pasi pechirebvu chako chose. Uyezve unocherechedza kuti whale haina mhino yekunze; uye kuti chero mhino yaanayo—buri romuromo wayo, riri pamusoro womusoro wayo; unoona kuti maziso ake nenzeve zviri kumativi omusoro wake, chinenge chikamu chimwe muzvitatu chourefu hwake hwose kubva mberi. Naizvozvo, iwe unofanirwa kunge waona kuti kumberi kwemusoro weSperm Whale imadziro akafa, bofu, pasina nhengo imwe chete kana mukurumbira wakapfava wechero rudzi. Uyezve, iwe zvino wava kufunga kuti chete munzvimbo yakanyanyisa, yakaderera, yakarerekera shure chikamu chepamberi pemusoro, pane kasara kadiki kebhonzo; uye kwete kusvika wasvika padhuze nemamita makumi maviri kubva pahuma ndipo paunosvika pakukura kwecranial. Zvekuti iyi yakakura yese isina bhonzo misa sewadhi imwe. Pakupedzisira, kunyanguvezvo, sezvazvichaziviswa munguva pfupi iri kutevera, zviri mariri zvimwe zvinoumba mafuta asina kusimba; zvakadaro, iwe zvino wave kuzodzidziswa nezve hunhu hwechinhu icho chinodyara zvisingagoneki zvese izvo zvinoonekwa senge effeminacy. Ndizvo chete nemusoro; asi nemusiyano uyu: pamusoro pemusoro hamvuropu iyi, nyangwe isina kukora kudaro, ndeyekuomarara kusina bhonzo, kusingaenzaniswi nemunhu chero upi zvake asina kuibata. CHAPTER 79. The Prairie.

To scan the lines of his face, or feel the bumps on the head of this Leviathan; this is a thing which no Physiognomist or Phrenologist has as yet undertaken. Such an enterprise would seem almost as hopeful as for Lavater to have scrutinized the wrinkles on the Rock of Gibraltar, or for Gall to have mounted a ladder and manipulated the Dome of the Pantheon. Still, in that famous work of his, Lavater not only treats of the various faces of men, but also attentively studies the faces of horses, birds, serpents, and fish; and dwells in detail upon the modifications of expression discernible therein. Nor have Gall and his disciple Spurzheim failed to throw out some hints touching the phrenological characteristics of other beings than man. Therefore, though I am but ill qualified for a pioneer, in the application of these two semi-sciences to the whale, I will do my endeavor. I try all things; I achieve what I can.

Physiognomically regarded, the Sperm Whale is an anomalous creature. He has no proper nose. And since the nose is the central and most conspicuous of the features; and since it perhaps most modifies and finally controls their combined expression; hence it would seem that its entire absence, as an external appendage, must very largely affect the countenance of the whale. For as in landscape gardening, a spire, cupola, monument, or tower of some sort, is deemed almost indispensable to the completion of the scene; so no face can be physiognomically in keeping without the elevated open-work belfry of the nose. Dash the nose from Phidias's marble Jove, and what a sorry remainder! Nevertheless, Leviathan is of so mighty a magnitude, all his proportions are so stately, that the same deficiency which in the sculptured Jove were hideous, in him is no blemish at all. Nay, it is an added grandeur. A nose to the whale would have been impertinent. As on your physiognomical voyage you sail round his vast head in your jolly-boat, your noble conceptions of him are never insulted by the reflection that he has a nose to be pulled. A pestilent conceit, which so often will insist upon obtruding even when beholding the mightiest royal beadle on his throne.

In some particulars, perhaps the most imposing physiognomical view to be had of the Sperm Whale, is that of the full front of his head. This aspect is sublime.

In thought, a fine human brow is like the East when troubled with the morning. In the repose of the pasture, the curled brow of the bull has a touch of the grand in it. Pushing heavy cannon up mountain defiles, the elephant's brow is majestic. Human or animal, the mystical brow is as that great golden seal affixed by the German emperors to their decrees. It signifies—“God: done this day by my hand.” But in most creatures, nay in man himself, very often the brow is but a mere strip of alpine land lying along the snow line. Few are the foreheads which like Shakespeare's or Melancthon's rise so high, and descend so low, that the eyes themselves seem clear, eternal, tideless mountain lakes; and all above them in the forehead's wrinkles, you seem to track the antlered thoughts descending there to drink, as the Highland hunters track the snow prints of the deer. But in the great Sperm Whale, this high and mighty god-like dignity inherent in the brow is so immensely amplified, that gazing on it, in that full front view, you feel the Deity and the dread powers more forcibly than in beholding any other object in living nature. For you see no one point precisely; not one distinct feature is revealed; no nose, eyes, ears, or mouth; no face; he has none, proper; nothing but that one broad firmament of a forehead, pleated with riddles; dumbly lowering with the doom of boats, and ships, and men. Nor, in profile, does this wondrous brow diminish; though that way viewed its grandeur does not domineer upon you so. In profile, you plainly perceive that horizontal, semi-crescentic depression in the forehead's middle, which, in man, is Lavater's mark of genius.

But how? Genius in the Sperm Whale? Has the Sperm Whale ever written a book, spoken a speech? No, his great genius is declared in his doing nothing particular to prove it. It is moreover declared in his pyramidical silence. And this reminds me that had the great Sperm Whale been known to the young Orient World, he would have been deified by their child-magian thoughts. They deified the crocodile of the Nile, because the crocodile is tongueless; and the Sperm Whale has no tongue, or at least it is so exceedingly small, as to be incapable of protrusion. If hereafter any highly cultured, poetical nation shall lure back to their birth-right, the merry May-day gods of old; and livingly enthrone them again in the now egotistical sky; in the now unhaunted hill; then be sure, exalted to Jove's high seat, the great Sperm Whale shall lord it.

Champollion deciphered the wrinkled granite hieroglyphics. But there is no Champollion to decipher the Egypt of every man's and every being's face. Physiognomy, like every other human science, is but a passing fable. If then, Sir William Jones, who read in thirty languages, could not read the simplest peasant's face in its profounder and more subtle meanings, how may unlettered Ishmael hope to read the awful Chaldee of the Sperm Whale's brow? CHAPTER 80. The Nut.

If the Sperm Whale be physiognomically a Sphinx, to the phrenologist his brain seems that geometrical circle which it is impossible to square.

In the full-grown creature the skull will measure at least twenty feet in length. Unhinge the lower jaw, and the side view of this skull is as the side of a moderately inclined plane resting throughout on a level base. But in life—as we have elsewhere seen—this inclined plane is angularly filled up, and almost squared by the enormous superincumbent mass of the junk and sperm. At the high end the skull forms a crater to bed that part of the mass; while under the long floor of this crater—in another cavity seldom exceeding ten inches in length and as many in depth—reposes the mere handful of this monster's brain. The brain is at least twenty feet from his apparent forehead in life; it is hidden away behind its vast outworks, like the innermost citadel within the amplified fortifications of Quebec. So like a choice casket is it secreted in him, that I have known some whalemen who peremptorily deny that the Sperm Whale has any other brain than that palpable semblance of one formed by the cubic-yards of his sperm magazine. Lying in strange folds, courses, and convolutions, to their apprehensions, it seems more in keeping with the idea of his general might to regard that mystic part of him as the seat of his intelligence.

It is plain, then, that phrenologically the head of this Leviathan, in the creature's living intact state, is an entire delusion. As for his true brain, you can then see no indications of it, nor feel any. The whale, like all things that are mighty, wears a false brow to the common world.

If you unload his skull of its spermy heaps and then take a rear view of its rear end, which is the high end, you will be struck by its resemblance to the human skull, beheld in the same situation, and from the same point of view. Indeed, place this reversed skull (scaled down to the human magnitude) among a plate of men's skulls, and you would involuntarily confound it with them; and remarking the depressions on one part of its summit, in phrenological phrase you would say—This man had no self-esteem, and no veneration. And by those negations, considered along with the affirmative fact of his prodigious bulk and power, you can best form to yourself the truest, though not the most exhilarating conception of what the most exalted potency is.

But if from the comparative dimensions of the whale's proper brain, you deem it incapable of being adequately charted, then I have another idea for you. If you attentively regard almost any quadruped's spine, you will be struck with the resemblance of its vertebræ to a strung necklace of dwarfed skulls, all bearing rudimental resemblance to the skull proper. It is a German conceit, that the vertebræ are absolutely undeveloped skulls. But the curious external resemblance, I take it the Germans were not the first men to perceive. A foreign friend once pointed it out to me, in the skeleton of a foe he had slain, and with the vertebræ of which he was inlaying, in a sort of basso-relievo, the beaked prow of his canoe. Now, I consider that the phrenologists have omitted an important thing in not pushing their investigations from the cerebellum through the spinal canal. For I believe that much of a man's character will be found betokened in his backbone. I would rather feel your spine than your skull, whoever you are. A thin joist of a spine never yet upheld a full and noble soul. I rejoice in my spine, as in the firm audacious staff of that flag which I fling half out to the world.

Apply this spinal branch of phrenology to the Sperm Whale. His cranial cavity is continuous with the first neck-vertebra; and in that vertebra the bottom of the spinal canal will measure ten inches across, being eight in height, and of a triangular figure with the base downwards. As it passes through the remaining vertebræ the canal tapers in size, but for a considerable distance remains of large capacity. Now, of course, this canal is filled with much the same strangely fibrous substance—the spinal cord—as the brain; and directly communicates with the brain. And what is still more, for many feet after emerging from the brain's cavity, the spinal cord remains of an undecreasing girth, almost equal to that of the brain. Under all these circumstances, would it be unreasonable to survey and map out the whale's spine phrenologically? For, viewed in this light, the wonderful comparative smallness of his brain proper is more than compensated by the wonderful comparative magnitude of his spinal cord.

But leaving this hint to operate as it may with the phrenologists, I would merely assume the spinal theory for a moment, in reference to the Sperm Whale's hump. This august hump, if I mistake not, rises over one of the larger vertebræ, and is, therefore, in some sort, the outer convex mould of it. From its relative situation then, I should call this high hump the organ of firmness or indomitableness in the Sperm Whale. And that the great monster is indomitable, you will yet have reason to know.
