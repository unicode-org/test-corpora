CHITSAUKO 101. The Decanter.

Chikepe cheChirungu chisati chapera, chingave chakagara pano, chaakabva kuLondon, uye akatumidzwa zita remushakabvu Samuel Enderby, mutengesi weguta iroro, yepakutanga yeimba yakakurumbira ye whaling yeEnderby & Sons; imba iyo mumaonero angu evarombo whaleman, inouya isiri kure kumashure kwedzimba dzehumambo dzakabatana dzeTudors neBourbons, padanho rekufarira chaiko kwenhoroondo. Inguva yakareba sei, pamberi pegore raShe wedu 1775, iyi imba huru yehove yakanga iripo, zvinyorwa zvangu zvakawanda zvehove hazvibudisi pachena; asi mugore iroro (1775) yakagadzirisa ngarava dzokutanga dzechiRungu dzaigara dzichivhima Sperm Whale; kunyangwe kwemamwe mamakisi emakore apfuura (kubvira 1726) edu akashinga Coffins uye Maceys eNantucket neMunda wemizambiringa aive mumakungwa makuru akateedzera iyo Revhiatani, asi muNorth neSouth Atlantic chete: kwete kumwe kunhu. Ngazvive zvakanyatsonyorwa pano, kuti veNantucketer vaive vekutanga pakati pevanhu kubatanidza nesimbi yakabudirira iyo huru Sperm Whale; uye kuti kwehafu yezana remakore ndivo chete vanhu vepasi rose vakamukurudzira.

Muna 1778, ngarava yakaisvonaka, Amelia, yakagadzirira nokuda kwedonzo chairoiro, uye pakupomerwa kwoga kweEnderbys ine simba, yakatenderedza Cape Horn noushingi, uye yakanga iri yokutanga pakati pamarudzi kudzikisa igwa rewhale rorudzi rwupi norwupi muSouth Sea huru. Rwendo rwacho rwakanga rwune hunyanzvi uye rombo rakanaka; uye achidzokera kunzvimbo yake yokugara akabata akabata urume hunokosha, muenzaniso waAmelia wakakurumidza kuteverwa nedzimwe ngarava, dzechiNgezi neAmerica, uye nokudaro nzvimbo huru dzeSperm Whale dzePacific dzakakandwa. Asi isingagutsikane nechinhu ichi chakanaka, imba isinganeti yakazvisimbisa zvakare: Samuel neVanakomana vake vese - vangani, amai vavo vanongoziva - uye pasi pekuda kwavo, uye pamwe, ndinofunga, nemubhadharo wavo, hurumende yeBritain yakanyengerwa kutumira sloop-ye-hondo Rattler parwendo rwewhaling rwekuwana muSouth Sea. Achirairwa naPost-Kaputeni wemugungwa, Rattler akaita rwendo rune ruzha pamusoro payo, uye akaita rimwe basa; yakawanda sei isingaonekwe. Asi izvi hazvisi zvose. Muna 1819, imba imwechete yakagadzira ngarava yewhale yekuwana yavo, kuti vaende kunoravira kumvura dziri kure dzeJapan. Ngarava iyoyo — inodaidzwa kuti “Siren”—yakaita rwendo rwakanaka rwokuedza; uye kwakanga kuri nokudaro apo nzvimbo huru yeJapan Whaling Ground yakatanga kuzivikanwa navose. Syren murwendo urwu rwakakurumbira yakarairwa naKaputeni Coffin, muNantucketer.

Rukudzo rwese kune veEnderbies, saka, vane imba, ndinofunga, iripo kusvika nhasi; kunyange zvazvo pasina panikiro Samueri wapakuvamba anofanira kuva kare kare akatsveta tambo yake nokuda kweGungwa guru reMaodzanyemba reimwe nyika.

Ngarava yakatumidzwa zita rake yakanga yakakodzera kukudzwa, kuva mufambisi wezvikepe anokurumidza uye mhizha inokudzwa munzira dzose. Ndakamukwira kamwe chete pakati pehusiku kumwe kumhenderekedzo yePatagonian, uye ndikanwa zvakanaka pafiripi. Rakanga riri gamu rakanaka ratakanga tinaro, uye ose akanga ari mabhosvo—mweya wose waiva mungarava. Hupenyu hupfupi kwavari, uye kufa kunofadza. Uye gamu rakanaka randakanga ndave naro—kare, nguva refu kwazvo mushure mokunge Ahabhu abata mapuranga ake nechitsitsinho chake chenyanga dzenzou—zvinondifunga nezvemutsa, wakasimba, wemuSaxon wechikepe; uye dai mumwe wangu akandikanganwa, uye dhiyabhorosi andirangarire, kana ndikazotadza kuzviona. Flip? Ndati tave nepi? Hongu, uye takaipindurudza nechiyero chemarita gumi paawa; uye pakauya mhungu (nokuti yabva ipapo nePatagonia), uye maoko ose—vaenzi navose—vakashevedzwa kumateya epamusoro emakungwa, takanga tichiremerwa zvikuru zvokuti taifanira kusveerera mumwe nomumwe mudenga mumiseve yomuuta; uye nokusaziva takaisa mipendero yebhachi dzedu mumaseiri, zvokuti takarembera ipapo, takarembera zvakasimba mumhepo inovhuvhuta, muenzaniso unonyevera kutara dzose dzakadhakwa. Zvisinei, matanda emvura haana kupfuura; uye nechinguva chidiki takatsikitsira pasi, takadzikama, zvekuti taifanira kupfuudza pafiripi zvakare, kunyangwe yakapfapfaidza yemunyu yakapfachukira ichidzika neiyo forecastle scuttle, yakanyanyisa kunyungudutswa ndokuinyudza kuravira kwangu.

Mhou yacho yakanga yakanaka—yakaoma, asi ine muviri mairi. Vakati imombe yemombe; vamwe, kuti yakanga iri nyama yemombe; asi handizivi, chokwadi, kuti zvakaitika sei. Vaiva nemarara zvakare; diki, asi rakakura, symmetrically globular, uye isinga peri dumplings. Ndakafunga kuti unodzinzwa, wodziumburudza mukati mako dzamedzwa. Kana iwe ukanyungudika kuresa kumberi, waisa panjodzi yekukanda kwavo kubva mauri semabhiridhi-mabhora. Chingwa—asi izvozvo hazvina kubatsira; kunze kwaizvozvo, yakanga iri anti-scorbutic; muchidimbu, chingwa ndicho chaingove nerwendo rutsva rwavaiva narwo. Asi nhare yacho yakanga isina kunyanya kujeka, uye zvaive nyore kutsika mukona ine rima kana waidya. Asi zvose mune zvose, kumutora kubva kurori kuenda kune imwe, tichifunga nezvehukuru hwemabhoilers emubiki, kusanganisira mabhailer ake ematehwe mhenyu; mberi neshure, ndinoti, Samuel Enderby chaive ngarava yaifara; zvekufamba zvakanaka uye zvakawanda; flip yakanaka uye yakasimba; crack vamwe vese, uye capital from boot heels kusvika heti-bhendi.

Asi sei zvakaitika, munofunga imi, kuti Samuel Enderby, nevamwe vanobata whale vechiRungu vandinoziva—kwete vose—vakanga vari ngarava dzine mukurumbira, dzine mutsa; vaipfuudza nyama yemombe, nechingwa, negaba, nejee; uye havana kuzokurumidza kuneta nekudya, nekunwa, nekuseka? Ndichakuudza. Kufara kukuru kweaya mawhale echiRungu inyaya yenzvero dzezvakaitika kare. Kana kuti handina kumbosiya tsvakiridzo yewhale yenhoroondo, kana yaita sezvinodiwa.

MaRungu akatangirwa mukubata hove whale nevaHollanders, Zealanders, uye Danes; kwavakabva kwaari mazwi mazhinji achiripo mukubata hove; uye zvimwezve, mafashoni avo akakora, achibata zvakawanda zvekudya nekunwa. Nokuti, sechinhu chakajairika, ngarava yeChirungu yevatengesi inodzinga vashandi vayo; asi hazvina kudaro murwi wechirungu. Saka, muChirungu, chinhu ichi che whaling yakanaka cheer hachisi chakajairwa uye chakasikwa, asi chinongoitika uye chakanyanya; uye, naizvozvo, inofanira kuva nemamwe mabviro anokosha, ayo ataurwa pano, uye acharamba achijekeswa.

Panguva yekutsvagisa kwangu munhoroondo dzeRevhiatani, ndakasangana nevhoriyamu yekare yechiDutch, iyo, nekunhuhwirira kwaro, ndaiziva kuti inofanira kunge iri pamusoro pemawhalers. Musoro waiti, "Dan Coopman," nokudaro ndakagumisa kuti iyi inofanira kuva ndangariro dzinokosha dzemumwe mushandi weAmsterdam muhove, sezvo ngarava yewhale yega yega inofanirwa kutakura cooper yayo. Ini ndakasimbiswa mune iyi pfungwa nekuona kuti kwaive kugadzirwa kweimwe "Fitz Swackhammer." Asi shamwari yangu Dr. Snodhead, murume akadzidza zvikuru, purofesa weLow Dutch neHigh German mukoreji yeSanta Claus neSt. Pott’s, kwandakagamuchidza basa rokushandura, ndichimupa bhokisi ramakenduru embeu nokuda kwechinetso chake—uyu Dr. Snodhead mumwe cheteyo, naizvozvo nokukurumidza sezvaakasora bhuku racho, akandivimbisa kuti “Dan Coopman” akanga asingarevi “The Merchant” asi “Dan Coopman.” Muchidimbu, bhuku iri rekare uye rakadzidza reLow Dutch rakabata nezvekutengeserana kweHolland; uye, pakati pezvimwe zvidzidzo, ine nhoroondo inonakidza yekubata hove kwayo whale. Uye muchitsauko chino maive, ane musoro unoti, “Smeer,” kana kuti “Fat,” ndipo pandakawana runyoro rwakadzama rwezvipfeko zvema lard uye cellars ye180 sail yeDutch whalemen; kubva pamazita api, sekududzirwa kwaDr. Snodhead, ndinonyora zvinotevera:

400,000 lbs. yemombe. 60,000 lbs. Friesland nyama yenguruve. 150,000 lbs. yestock fish. 550,000 lbs. zvebhisikiti. 72,000 lbs. yechingwa nyoro. 2,800 mafikins ebhota. 20,000 lbs. Texel uye Leyden chizi. 144,000 lbs. cheese (zvichida chinyorwa chakaderera). 550 ankers yeGeneva. 10,800 madhiramu edoro.

Matafura akawanda enhamba akaoma mukuverenga; hazvina kudaro muchiitiko chazvino, zvisinei, apo muverengi anozadzwa nemapombi akazara, mbiya, quarts, uye gill yejini yakanaka uye kufara kwakanaka.

Panguva iyoyo, ndakapedza mazuva matatu ndichinyatsogaya doro rose iri, nyama yemombe, nechingwa, apo pfungwa dzakadzama dzakawanda dzakangotaurwa kwandiri, dzichikwanisa kushandisa dzidziso dzaPlato; uyezve, ndakagadzira mamwe matafura angu, ndichibata huwandu hungangove hwehove dzestock-hove, nezvimwewo, dzinodyiwa nevese Low Dutch harpooneer mune iya yekare Greenland neSpitzbergen whale fishery. Mukutanga, huwandu hwebhotela, uye Texel neLeyden cheese inopedzwa, inoita seinoshamisa. Ndinozviverengera, kunyanguvezvo, kuhunhu hwavo husina kunaka, huchiri kuitwa husina kunaka nehunhu hwechiito chavo, uye kunyanya nekutsvaga kwavo mutambo muMagungwa anotonhora ePolar, kumahombekombe chaiko kwenyika iyoyo yeEsquimaux uko vagari vemo vanovimbisana mabumpers emafuta echitima.

Huwandu hwedoro, zvakare, hwakakura kwazvo, 10,800 madhiramu. Zvino, sezvo idzo hove dzepolar dzaingogona kutongerwa muzhizha pfupi remamiriro ekunze iwayo, zvekuti kufamba kwese kweimwe yeava maDutch whalemen, kusanganisira rwendo rupfupi kuenda nekubva kugungwa reSpitzbergen, hakuna kupfuudza mwedzi mitatu, toti, uye kuverenga varume makumi matatu kune imwe neimwe yezvikepe zvavo zve180, isu tine 5 seamen yese muDutch; nokudaro, ndinoti, tine madhiramu maviri chaiwo edoro pamunhu, kwemuripo wevhiki gumi nembiri, kunze kwechikamu chake chakanaka cheiyo 550 ankers of gin. Zvino, kunyangwe aya magini nedoro, akavhiringika sekunge munhu aafungira kuti akange ari, vaive varume vakakodzera kusimuka mumusoro wechikepe, uye kutora chinangwa chakanaka pakubhururuka kwewhale; izvi zvingaita sezvisingabvirike. Kunyange zvakadaro vakavavavarira, vakavarovawo. Asi uku kwaive kure kuMaodzanyemba, ngazvirangarirwe, uko doro rinowirirana nebumbiro remutemo; paEquator, kuhove dzedu dzekumaodzanyemba, doro rinokwanisa kuita kuti mhungu arare pamusoro wemasti uye ane doro muchikepe chake; uye kurasikirwa kunorwadza kunogona kuitika kuNantucket neNew Bedford.

Asi kwete; zvakakwana zvakataurwa kuratidza kuti varwi vekare veDutch whalers vemazana maviri kana matatu emakore apfuura vaiva zviropa zvakakwirira; uye kuti vabati vehove dzechiRungu havana kuregeredza muenzaniso wakaisvonaka kudaro. Nokuti, vanoti, kana uchifamba nechikepe chisina chinhu, kana iwe usingakwanise kuwana chinhu chiri nani kubva munyika, wana chisvusvuro chakanaka kubva mairi, zvishoma. Uye izvi zvinobvisa decanter.





CHITSAUKO 102. Bower in the Arsacides.

Kusvika ikozvino, mukutsanangura zvinotsanangurwa zveSperm Whale, ndakanyanya kugara pane zvinoshamisa zvekunze kwake; kana zvakasiyana uye zvakadzama pane zvimwe zvishoma zvemukati zvimiro zvemukati. Asi pakumunzwisisa kukuru uye kwakadzama, zvinofanirwa kuti ndimusunungure zvakare, uye ndisunungure nhungo dzepaipi yake, ndichisunungura machira ake, nekusunungura zvikorekedzo uye maziso emajoini emapfupa ake emukati-kati, ndimuise pamberi penyu mukupedzisira kwake; ndiko kuti, muskeleton yake isina magumo.

Asi sei zvino, Ishmaeri? Ko, iwe, mufambisi wezvikwasvi zvake muhove, unonyepedzera sei kuziva pamusoro penzvimbo dziri pasi pegungwa rewhale? Ko erudite Stubb, yakakwira pamusoro pecapstan yako, yakapa hurukuro pamusoro pechimiro cheCetacea; uye nerubatsiro rwemhepo, ungasimudza mbabvu yemuenzaniso kuti uratidze? Zvitsanangure, Ishmaeri. Unogona here kumhara whale yakura padhishi yako kuti iongororwe, sezvinoita mubiki anodya nguruve yakagochwa? Kwete. Iwe Ishimaeri, uri chapupu chechokwadi kusvikira zvino; asi chenjerai kuti matora sei ropafadzo yaJona moga; ropafadzo yekukurukura pamusoro pemajoists nematanda; mapango, nembariro, nezvitsigiro, nembariro, nembambo, nembambo yerevhiatani; uye zvakaita sezvisviniro, nedzimba dzomukaka, nezvizoro zvenyama, nezvinotapira muura hwake.

Ndinoreurura, kuti kubvira pana Jona, vanhu vashomanene ve<em>whale vakapinda kure zvikuru pasi peganda rehove huru; zvisinei, ndakakomborerwa nemukana wokumutsemura muchidimbu. Muchikepe chandaive, mwana mudiki Sperm Whale akambosimudzwa nemuviri kumusoro kuti atore poke kana bhegi rake, kugadzira masheti ematanda ehaponi, uye nemisoro yemapfumo. Unofunga kuti ndinorega mukana iwoyo uchienda, ndisina kushandisa bhoti-hatchet uye jack-banga, uye kutyora chisimbiso uye kuverenga zvese zviri mukati meiyo mwana mudiki?

Uye kana iri ruzivo rwangu chairwo rwemapfupa erevhiatani mukukura kwavo kukuru, kukura kwakazara, nekuda kweruzivo rwusingawanzo ini ndine chikwereti kune shamwari yangu yekupedzisira yehumambo Tranquo, mambo weTranque, mumwe weArsacides. Nekuda kwekunge ndiri kuTranque, makore apfuura, pandakabatanidzwa nechikepe chekutengesa cheDey yeAlgiers, ndakakokwa kuti ndipedze chikamu chezororo reArsacidean naishe weTranque, paimba yake yemichindwe paPupella; kudivi regungwa kusiri kure zvakanyanya kubva kune yaidanwa nevafambisi vedu Bamboo-Town, dzimbahwe rake.

Pakati pehumwe hunhu huzhinji hwakanaka, shamwari yangu yehumambo Tranquo, aive nechipo cherudo rwakazvipira kune zvese zvehutsinye vertu, akange aunganisa pamwe muPupella chero zvipi zvisingawanzo zvinhu izvo vanhu vake vakangwara vaigona kugadzira; zvikurukuru matanda akavezwa ezvigadzirwa zvinoshamisa, mateko akachekwa, mapfumo akaiswa, zvikwashi zvinodhura, magwa anonhuhwirira; uye izvi zvose zvikagoverwa pakati pezvishamiso zvipi nezvipi zvechisikigo, zvaiiswa pamahombekombe egungwa nemafungu ezvipo.

Mukuru pakati peaya ekupedzisira aive hombe Sperm Whale, iyo, mushure mekuputika kukuru kwenguva refu, yakange yawanikwa yakafa uye yakasungwa, musoro wayo wakatarisana nemuti wekoko-nzungu, uyo wakaita semapfunde, akanyungudutswa aiita sejeti rake rakasvibira. Apo mutumbi muhombe wakazobvisirwa mativi akadzika-dzika, uye mapfupa ooma guruva muzuva, ipapo marangwanda acho akatakurwa nokungwarira kukwidza nePupella glen, uko kwaichengeterwa temberi huru yemichindwe yamambo.

Mbabvu dzakanga dzakaturikwa nemikombe; vertebræ yakavezwa neArsacidean annals, mune hieroglyphics yechienzi; mudehenya, vaprista vakachengeta murazvo unonhuhwirira usingadzimwi, zvokuti musoro unoshamisa wakabudisazve mhute yawo; nepo, yakaturikwa kubva padavi, inotyisa yepasi rushaya yakazununguka pamusoro pevose vakazvipira, sebakatwa rakarembera bvudzi iro rakavhundutsa Damocles.

Zvaishamisa kuona. Huni dzaive dzakasvibira kunge mosses dzeIcy Glen; miti yakamira yakakwirira uye ichizvikudza, ichinzwa muto wayo mupenyu; ivhu raiva pasi rinobishaira rakanga rakaita somucheka womuruki, une kapeti yakaisvonaka pamusoro payo, ine matavi omuzambiringa akachinjika akachinjika neshinda, uye maruva mapenyu ari mufananidzo. Miti yose namatavi ayo ose akaremerwa; namakwenzi ose, namafemo, nouswa; mhepo inotakura mashoko; zvose izvi zvakanga zvisingaregi. Kuburikidza nekumisikidzwa kwemashizha, zuva guru rakaita sechifambiso chinobhururuka chichiruka basa risina kuneta. O, muruki akabatikana! muruki asingaoneki!—imbomira!—izwi rimwe!—rinoyerera jira racho kupi? iimba yamambo ipi? neiko kubata uku kusina magumo? Taura, iwe muruki; dzora ruoko rwako, asi shoko rimwe chete kwauri. Kwete—chirukwa chinobhururuka—mifananidzo inoyangarara ichibva pachirukwa; iyo freshet-rushing carpet zvachose inotsvedza. Muruki, ndiye anoruka; uye nokuruka uku wakaita matsi, kuti anzwe inzwi rinofa; uye nokuhon'era ikoko, isuwo, avo vanotarisa pachirukwa tinonzwa matsi; uye chete patichapukunyuka ndipo patinonzwa manzwi ane chiuru anotaura nemairi. Nokuti kunyange zvakadaro zviri mumafekitari zvinhu zvose. Mazwi anotaurwa asinganzwike pakati pezvirukwa zvinobhururuka; iwo mazwi mamwechete anonzwika zvakajeka pasina madziro, achiputika kubva kumakesi akavhurwa. Nekudaro kwaonekwa nhubu. Ah, munhu anofa! zvino chenjerai; nokuti nokudaro, muruzha rwose urwu rwemushonga mukuru wenyika, kufunga kwako kwakadzikama kunogona kunzwikwa kure.

Zvino, pakati pemiti yakasvibirira, isingazorori yomuti iwoyo weArsacidean, marangwanda makuru, machena, anonamatwa akanga akarembera—chirume chihombe! Asi, shinda neshinda zvakasvibirira zvaigara zvakarukwa zvaipindirana uye zvichirira zvakamupoteredza, simbe yacho yaiita semuruki ane hunyanzvi; iye pachake akarukwa nemizambiringa; mwedzi woga woga tichifunga kuti yakasvibira, yakasvibira verdure; asi iye skeleton. Hupenyu hwakapetwa Rufu; Rufu trellised Hupenyu; mwari akashata akave neHupenyu hwehudiki, uye akamuberekera mbiri yakamonereka.

Zvino, apo nehumambo Tranquo ndakashanyira iyi whale inoshamisa, ndikaona dehenya iri atari, uye utsi hwekugadzira huchikwira kubva paive nejeti chaiyo yakabuda, ndakashamisika kuti mambo aifanira kuona chapel sechinhu chevertu. Akabva aseka. Asi chakanyanya kundishamisa kuti vapristi vaifanira kupika kuti jeti yake inopfungaira yaiva yechokwadi. Kuenda nekudzoka ndakafamba ndisati skeleton iyi - yakakweshera mizambiringa padivi - yakatsemura mbabvu - uye nebhora reArsacidean twine, yakadzungaira, yakanyura pakati pemakona ayo akawanda anomonereka, ane mimvuri uye arbor. Asi munguva pfupi tambo yangu yakanga yapera; ndichiitevera kumashure, ndakabva ndabuda nepaburi randakapinda. Handina kuona chinhu chipenyu mukati; pakanga pasina asi mapfupa chete.

Ndichindicheka chitanda chekuyeresa chegirini, ndakanyurazve mukati meskeleton. Kubva pakatsemurwa museve mudehenya, vaprista vakandiona ndichisimudza mbabvu yokupedzisira, “Ko zvino!” vakadanidzira; "Mungapima here uyu mwari wedu! Ndezvedu." "Hongu, vaprista, saka, muchamuita kusvikira rinhiko?" Asi ipapo kukakavadzana kukuru kwakamuka pakati pavo, tsoka nemasendimita; vakatsemurana zvikwambo zvavo netsvimbo yeruvanze — dehenya guru rakaita maungira — ndokutora mukana iwoyo wemhanza, ndakakurumidza kupedzisa zviyedzo zvangu.

Zviyero izvi ndave kufunga kuisa pamberi penyu. Asi chekutanga, ngazvirekodzwe, kuti, mune iyi nyaya, handina kusununguka kutaura chero kuyerwa kwandinoda. Nekuti kune skeleton zviremera zvaunogona kutaura nezvazvo, kuyedza chokwadi changu. Kune Leviathanic Museum, vanondiudza kudaro, muHull, England, chimwe chezviteshi zvewhaling zvenyika iyoyo, uko vane mimwe mienzaniso yakaisvonaka yemafin-back nemamwe mawhale. Saizvozvowo, ndakanzwa kuti mumiziyamu yeManchester, muNew Hampshire, vane inonzi nevaridzi “muenzaniso wakakwana weGreenland kana kuti River Whale muUnited States.” Uyezve, pane imwe nzvimbo muYorkshire, England, Burton Constable nezita, mumwe Sir Clifford Constable ane skeleton yeSperm Whale, asi ane ukuru huri pakati nepakati, asingamborevi ukuru hwakazara hweshamwari yangu King Tranquo.

Muzviitiko zvese izvi, mawhale akasungwa ayo maskeleton maviri aya aive, akatanga kunzi nevaridzi vawo pazvikonzero zvakafanana. Mambo Tranquo achitora yake nekuti aiida; naChangamire Clifford, nekuti vaive ishe wenzvimbo idzi. Nepo, mumazuva evavhimi vekare veCanada neIndia nevateyi vekuMadokero, apo kure kumadokero (mukunyura kwezuva richiri kubuda) kwaiva renje nemhandara, nhamba imwe cheteyo yavarume vemoccasined, kwenhamba imwe cheteyo yemwedzi, vakatasva bhiza panzvimbo pokufamba mungarava, vangadai vakauraya kwete makumi mana, asi zviuru makumi mana kana kupfuura; idi rokuti, kana kuri madikanwa, raigona kutaurwa nenhamba.

Kana, kana zvichionekwa sezvakanaka, hazviratidzi here kupokana kwekuda kutsakatika zvishoma nezvishoma kweSperm Whale, semuenzaniso, kuti mumakore ekare (chikamu chekupedzisira chezana ramakore rapfuura, taura) aya maRevhiatani, ari mumapodhi madiki, aisangana kakawanda kupfuura parizvino, uye, semugumisiro, nzendo dzacho hadzina kurebeswa, uye zvakare dzaibhadhara zvakanyanya. Nemhaka yokuti, sezvave zvichicherekedzwa kune dzimwe nzvimbo, ma<em>whale iwayo, achipesvedzerwa nemimwe mirangariro kuchengeteko, zvino anoshambira makungwa ari mumapoka makuru emakaravhani, zvokuti pamwero mukuru akapararira ari oga, majoko, uye mapodhi, uye zvikoro zvamamwe mazuva zvino zvaunganidzwa kuva mauto makuru asi akaparadzana zvikuru, asingawanzoitiki. Ndizvo chete. Uye kunyengera kwakaenzana kunoratidzika kuva kuzvitutumadza, kuti nemhaka yokuti anonzi whale-bone whales haachanyanyi zvikonzero zvakawanda mumakore ekare akazara nawo, nokudaro rudzi irworwowo rwuri kuderera. Nokuti vari kungodzingwa chete kubva kumusoro kuenda kuCape; uye kana imwe mhenderekedzo yegungwa isingachagadziriswi nejeti dzavo, saka, iva nechokwadi, imwe uye iri kure tambo yakave ichangobva kushamiswa nechitarisiko chisina kujairika.

Uyezve: maererano neRevhiatani idzi dzataurwa pakupedzisira, dzine nhare mbiri dzakasimba, iyo, mumikana yose yevanhu, icharamba isingapindiki. Uye sepakuvhozhokerwa kwemipata yavo, maSwiss ane chando akadzokera kumakomo awo; saka, achivhimwa kubva mumasavanna uye maglades epakati pemakungwa, whale-bone whales inogona pakupedzisira kuenda kuPolar citadels dzawo, uye kunyura pasi pemagirazi ekupedzisira zvipingamupinyi uye madziro ikoko, kuuya pakati peminda yechando uye floes; uye mudenderedzwa rinokwezva raZvita asingaperi, kumbira kushora kune zvese zvinotsvakwa nemunhu.

Asi sezvo pamwe makumi mashanu emawhale-bone whales aya akasungirirwa kune imwe cachalot, vamwe vazivi veiyo forecastle vakagumisa kuti kuparara kwakanaka uku kwakatoderedza zvakanyanya mauto avo. Asi kunyange zvazvo kwenguva yakati yapfuura chiverengero chakati cheaya ma<em>whale, asingasviki 13 000, akaurawa gore negore panor’ west coast navaAmerica bedzi; asi pane pfungwa dzinopa kunyange mamiriro ezvinhu aya asina basa senharo inopikisa panyaya iyi.

Zvazvakasikwa sezvazvingava zvisingadaviriki pamusoro pokuwanda kwezvisikwa zvakakura zvikuru zvepasi, asi tichatii kuna Harto, wezvenhau weGoa, paanotiudza kuti pakuvhima kumwe chete Mambo weSiam akatora nzou 4 000; kuti munzvimbo idzodzo nzou dzakawanda semapoka emombe munzvimbo dzinopisa. Uye zvinoratidzika pasina chikonzero chekusava nechokwadi kuti kana nzou idzi, idzo dzave dzichivhimwa kwezviuru zvemakore, naSemiramis, naPorus, naHannibal, uye nemadzimambo ose anotevedzana eMabvazuva—kana vachiri kurarama imomo munhamba huru, zvikuru sei whale huru ingakunda kuvhima kwose, sezvo ine mafuro okupararira mukati, iyo yakakura seNew America yose, Europe neAfrica yose, America neHolland, Holland neAfrica. Zvitsuwa zvegungwa zvakabatanidzwa.

Uyezve: isu tinofanirwa kufunga, kuti kubva mukufungidzirwa kwehupenyu hurefu hwemawhale, avo vanogona kunge vachisvika zera rezana ramakore uye kupfuura, saka panguva ipi neipi yenguva, akati wandei zvizvarwa zvevakuru zvakasiyana zvinofanirwa kunge zviripo. Uye kuti chii ichocho, tinogona nokukurumidza kuwana imwe pfungwa, kupfurikidza nokufungidzira makuva ose amakuva, makuva, uye makamuri emhuri echisiko achipa miviri mipenyu yavarume vose, vakadzi, uye vana vakanga vari vapenyu makore makumi manomwe namashanu apfuura; uye kuwedzera uyu mugamuchiri asingaverengeki kuhuwandu hwevanhu huripo pasi rose.

Nokudaro, nokuda kwezvinhu zvose izvi, tinoona kuti whale haifi mumarudzi ayo, zvisinei kuti inoparara sei muunhu hwake. CHITSAUKO 106. Gumbo raAhabhi.

Nzira yekukurumidza iyo Mutungamiriri Ahabhu akanga asiya nayo Samuel Enderby yeLondon, akanga asina kuchengetwa nechisimba chiduku kumunhu wake pachake. Ainge avheneka nesimba padanda rechikepe chake zvekuti gumbo rake renyanga dzenzou rakarohwa nehana. Uye mushure mekunge awana dhizaini yake, uye pivot-gomba rake ipapo, akatenderedza mavhiri ane simba achitenderedza nekuchimbidza kuraira kumudhiraivha (chaive, senguva dzose, chimwe chinhu pamusoro pekusafambisa kwake zvisingaite); zvino, nyanga dzenzou dzanga dzatozungunuswa dzakagamuchira kumwe kumonyoroka uye chipanera, zvokuti kunyange yakaramba ichiri yose, uye netarisiro yose ine ruchiva, asi Ahabhi haana kuiona yakavimbika zvachose.

Uye, zvamazvirokwazvo, zvakaratidzika kuva chinhu chiduku nokuda kwechishamiso, kuti nokuda kwokusangwarira kwake kwose, kwoupengo, pane dzimwe nguva Ahabhi ainyatsoteerera mamiriro epfupa rakafa iro raakanga akamira pariri. Nokuti yakanga isati yava nguva refu zvikuru Pequod asati afamba nechikepe kubva kuNantucket, kuti akanga awanikwa humwe usiku akarara pasi, uye asinganzwisisi; nevamwe vasingazivikanwi, uye vanoita seasingatsananguriki, akakuvara asingafungidziriki, nhengo yake yenyanga dzenzou yakange yakasimudzwa zvine simba, zvokuti yakarohwa nenjodzi, uye vose vakabaya chidya chake; uyewo pakanga pasina kuomerwa kukuru kuti ronda racho rairwadza rakapora zvachose.

Uyewo, panguva iyeyo, kwakanga kusina kukundikana kupinda mundangariro yake yokuva mumwe, kuti nhamo yose yokutambura ikoko kwazvino kwakanga kuri bedzi nhau yakananga yenhamo yekare; uye iyewo zviripachena akaita seanoona, kuti sezvo chikara chine muchetura chemumatope chichipfuudzira rudzi rwacho nenzira isingadzivisiki semuimbi anotapira kwazvo wemusango; saka, zvakaenzana nekufara kwese, zviitiko zvese zvinosiririsa zvinoita zvinozvara zvavanoda. Zvirokwazvo, Ahabhi akafungawo zvimwe chetezvo; sezvo zvese madzitateguru nedzinza raKusuwa zvichienda mberi kupfuura madzitateguru nedzinza raMufaro. Nokuti, tisingarevi izvi: kuti ifungidziro inobva kune dzimwe dzidziso dzechokwadi, kuti nepo zvimwe zvinofadza zvomuzvarirwo pano hazvizovi nevana vanoberekerwa kune imwe nyika, asi, pane kudaro, zvichateverwa nokusafara-kusava nevana kwokuora mwoyo kwose kwehero; nepo, mamwe matambudziko erufu ane mhosva acharamba achizviberekera chizvarwa chekusingaperi chenhamo kunze kweguva; kwete zvachose kuratidza izvi, pachiri kuratidzika kusaenzana mukuongorora kwakadzama kwechinhu. Nokuti, akafunga Ahabhu, kunyange vakanaka zvikuru vepanyika vakambova nekamwe kahudiki kasina chinhu kakavanda mavari, asi, pasi, matambudziko ese emwoyo, kukosha kusinganzwisisike, uye, mune vamwe vanhu, hukuru hwengirozi huru; saka ivo vanoshingairira kutsvaga-kunze kusatenda kubviswa kuri pachena. Kutevera madzinza ematambudziko makuru aya anofa, kunotitakura pakupedzisira pakati pevamwari vasina mavambo; kuitira kuti, pamberi pemazuva ose anofara, anoita uswa, uye makandira akapfava, mwedzi yekukohwa inotenderera, tinofanira kubvuma kune izvi: kuti vamwari pachavo havazofari nekusingaperi. Chiratidzo chisingagoneki, chinosuwisa chekuzvarwa pahuma pemunhu, chinongova chidhindo chekusuwa mune vanosaina.

Nekusaziva pano chakavanzika chakaburitswa, icho pamwe chingave zvakanyanya, nenzira yakatarwa, yakamboburitswa. Nezvimwe zvakawanda zvemashoko pamusoro paAhabhi, nguva dzose yakanga yakaramba yakavanzika kune vamwe, nei zvakadaro, kuti kwenguva yakati, zvose zvisati zvaitika uye shure kwekufamba kwechikepe chePequod, akanga azvivanza pachake neakadaro Grand-Lama-akafanana; uye, kwenguva imwe chete iyoyo, akatsvaka utiziro mbeveve, sokunge, pakati pedare remabhura revakafa. Mutungamiri Peleg akakuvara chikonzero chechinhu ichi chakaonekwa chisina kukwana; kunyangwe, chokwadi, sekubata kwakadzika kwese kwaAhabhu, chizaruro chega chega chakatora rima guru pane chiedza chinotsanangura. Asi, pakupedzisira, zvose zvakabuda; nyaya imwe chete iyi yakaita. Njodzi inotyisa iyoyo yakanga iri pasi pekusagadzikana kwake kwechinguvana. Uye kwete izvi chete, asi kune uyo anogara achibvumirana, achidonhedza denderedzwa kumahombekombe, uyo, nokuda kwechikonzero chipi nechipi, akawana ropafadzo yenzira shoma yakarambidzwa kwaari; kune vaya vanotya vakaurayiwa nezvakataurwa pamusoro apa—zvakasara, sezvazvakaita, zvisingatarisirwi naAhabhi—zvakazvipa zvinhu zvinotyisa, zvisingabviswi zvachose munyika yemidzimu neyokuungudza. Naizvozvo, kubudikidza nokumushingairira kwavo, vose vakanga varangana, kusvikira mavari, kuti vavhare ruzivo rwechinhu ichi kubva kune vamwe; uye saka zvaive, kuti hakuna kutozosvikira nguva yakati rebei yapera, zvakazoitika pamadhishi ePequod.

Asi ita zvose izvi sezvazvingava; regai zvisingaoneki, sinodhi isinganzwisisiki mumhepo, kana kuti machinda anotsiva uye vatsivi vemoto, vanofanira kuita kana kuti kwete naAhabhi wapasi, asi, muiyi nhau yazvino yegumbo rake, iye akatora miitiro inoshanda iri pachena;—iye akadana muvezi.

Uye apo mushandi uyu akazviratidza pamberi pake, akamuudza pasina kunonoka kutanga kugadzira gumbo idzva, uye akaraira varoora vake kuti vamuone achipihwa matanda ese uye majoists eshaya-yenyanga dzenzou (Sperm Whale) izvo zvakange zvaunganidzwa parwendo kusvika zvino, kuitira kuti kunyatsosarudzwa kwezvinhu zvakaomarara, zvakajeka-zvakacheneswa zvichengetedzwe. Izvi zvaitwa, muvezi akagamuchira murayiro wokuti gumbo ripere usiku ihwohwo; uye kugovera zvese zvinoiisa pairi, pasina kune zvine chekuita neasingavimbike ari kushandiswa. Uyezve, foreji yengarava yakarairwa kuti ikwidzwe kubva mukusashanda kwayo kwechinguvana munhare; uye, kuti nyaya yacho ikurumidze, mupfuri wesimbi akarayirwa kuti aende pakarepo kugadzirwa chero chipi zvacho chesimbi chingadiwa.
