CHITSAUKO 55. Yemifananidzo Inotyisa yeWhales.

Ini ndichagadzira pende refu kwauri pamwe chete nemunhu anogona pasina canvas, chimwe chinhu chakaita sechimiro chechokwadi chewhale sezvaanonyatso kuoneka kune ziso rewhaleman kana ari mumuviri wake wakakwana whale inosungirirwa padivi peiyo whale-ngarava kuitira kuti agone kutsika ipapo. Zvingave zvakakosha kuti, saka, kare kuzivisa kune iyo yekufungidzira mifananidzo yake iyo kunyange kusvika nhasi inopikisa nechivimbo kutenda kwemuridzi wenyika. Yasvika nguva yekugadzirisa nyika munyaya iyi, nekuratidza mifananidzo yakadai yewhale kuti haina kururama.

Kungava kuti manyuko makuru ezviyero zvose izvozvo zvemifananidzo achawanwa pakati pezvivezwa zvekaresa zvechiHindoo, zveEgipita, uye zvechiGiriki. Kubva panguva idzodzo dzekutanga asi dzisina kutendeseka apo pamatombo ematombo etemberi, zvigaro zvezvidhori, uye panhovo, medallions, makapu nemari, dolphin yakakweverwa muzvikero zvecheni-zvombo zvakafanana neSaladin, uye musoro wakavharwa seSt. George; kubva ipapo pane chimwe chinhu cherudzi rwakafanana rwerezinesi chakakunda, kwete chete mumifananidzo yakakurumbira yewhale, asi mune dzakawanda zvesainzi mharidzo dzake.

Iye zvino, nezvinonetsa, mufananidzo wekare zvikuru uripo chero uchitaurwa kuti ndewewhale, unowanikwa mucavern-pagoda yakakurumbira yeElephanta, kuIndia. VaBrahmin vanoomerera pakuti muzvivezwa zvinenge zvisingaperi zvepagoda iyoyo yekare, kutengeserana kwose nokutsvakisisa, kurangarira kwose kusingafungidziriki kwomunhu, kwakanga kuri mazera akafanofananidzirwa asati atombovapo. Hazvishamisi zvino, kuti mune imwe nzira basa redu rakanaka rekubata whaling raifanira kunge rakavharwa. Iyo Hindoo whale inotaurwa nezvayo, inoitika mune rimwe dhipatimendi remadziro, rinoratidzira kuumbwa kweVishnu muchimiro cheLeviathan, inozivikanwa seMatse Avatar. Asi kunyangwe chivezwa ichi chiri hafu yemunhu nehafu whale, saka kungopa muswe wekupedzisira, asi chikamu chidiki ichocho chakashata. Inotaridzika semuswe weanaconda, kupfuura zvanza zvakafara zvewhale dzechokwadi dzinotyisa.

Asi enda kumaGaleri akare, uye tarisa zvino pamufananidzo wemuKristu mukuru wependi wehove iyi; nekuti haabudiriri kupfuura Hindoo yepamberi pemvura. Uyu ndiwo mufananidzo waGuido waPerseus achinunura Andromeda kubva kugungwa-monster kana whale. Guido akawanepi muenzaniso wechisikwa chinoshamisa chakadaro? Kana Hogarth, mukudhirowa chiitiko chimwe chete mu "Perseus Achiburuka," yake pachake haagadzirise kamwechete nani. Hushasha hukuru hweiyo Hogarthian monster inonyungudika pamusoro, isingakwanisi kudhonza inchi imwe yemvura. Iine rudzi rwakati rwehove kumusana wayo, uye muromo wayo wakanyunguduka une muromo une mafungu, unogona kuendeswa kuGedhi revapanduki rinobva kuThames nemvura kupinda muShongwe. Bva, kune maProdromus whales ekare anonzi Scotch Sibbald, uye whale yaJona, sezvinoratidzirwa mumatsinhiro amaBhaibheri ekare uye kucheka kwezvinyoro zvekare. Ko ivava vachati kudiniko? Kana iri whale yebhuku-binder inomonereka sedzinde remuzambiringa rinotenderedza matanda echitsigiso chinodzika - sekudhindwa nekunamirwa kumusana uye mapeji ezvinyorwa zvemabhuku mazhinji ekare neatsva - icho chisikwa chinoyevedza asi chakanyatsonaka, chakateedzerwa, ndinochitora, kubva kune akafanana manhamba ari pamavhasi ekare. Kunyange zvazvo pasi rose richinzi dolphin, zvakadaro ndinodaidza hove iyi yebook-binder kuedza kubata whale; nekuti yaive yakarongedzerwa pakatanga kuiswa mudziyo. Yakatangwa nomuparidzi wekare weItaly pane imwe nzvimbo munenge muzana ramakore rechi 15, mukati meKumutsidzira Kudzidza; uye mumazuva iwayo, uye kunyange kusvika kunguva yakanonoka, ma dolphin aizivikanwa zvikuru kuva rudzi rweRevhiatani.

Mune mavignettes uye kumwe kushongedza kwemamwe mabhuku ekare iwe dzimwe nguva uchasangana neakanyanya kubata kubata whale, uko marudzi ese emhepo, jets d'eau, zvitubu zvinopisa uye kutonhora, Saratoga naBaden-Baden, vanouya vachiputika kubva muuropi hwake husina kupera. Mupeji yemusoro weiyo yekutanga edition ye "Kufambira mberi Kwekudzidza" iwe unowana mamwe mawhale anoda kuziva.

Asi tichisiya zvese izvi zvisiri zvehunyanzvi kuedza, ngatitarise pamifananidzo yerevhiatani ichiita seyakapusa, delineations yesainzi, nevanoziva. Muunganidzwa wenzendo dzaHarris wekare mune mamwe mahwendefa emawhale akatorwa mubhuku rechiDutch renzendo, AD 1671, rine musoro unoti “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen muchikepe Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson weFriesland, tenzi.” Mune rimwe remahwendefa iwayo ma<em>whale, semapuranga makuru ematanda, anomiririrwa akarara pakati pezvitsuwa zveaizi, nemapere machena achimhanya pamusoro pemisana yawo mipenyu. Mune imwe ndiro, iyo yakashata blunder inogadzirwa yekumiririra whale ine perpendicular flukes.

Zvakare, kune quarto inokatyamadza, yakanyorwa nomumwe Captain Colnett, Mutungamiriri wePost muuto rengarava reChirungu, rine musoro unoti “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, nedonzo rokutambanudzira Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Mubhuku rino mune rondedzero inodaidzira kuve "Mufananidzo wePhyseter kana Spermaceti whale, yakatorwa nechiyero kubva kune yakaurayiwa pamhenderekedzo yeMexico, Nyamavhuvhu, 1793, uye yakasimudzwa padhishi." Ini handipokane kuti kaputeni uyu akatorwa pikicha iyi kuitira kubatsira vemauto ake. Kungotaura chinhu chimwe chete pamusoro pazvo, regai nditi ine ziso rakashandiswa, maererano nechikero chiripo, kune yakakura yakazara sperm whale, yaizoita ziso rewhale iro hwindo remamita anenge mashanu kureba. Ah, mutungamiri wangu ane simba, sei usina kutipa Jona achitarisa neziso iro!

Uyewo hakusi kuumbwa kwehana kwakanyanyisa kweNatural History kuitira kubatsira vadiki uye vanyoro, kusununguka kubva muhuipi hwakafanana hwekukanganisa. Tarisa bhuku rakakurumbira riya rinonzi “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature.” Muchinyorwa chakapfupikiswa cheLondon cha1807, mune mahwendefa e“whale” anonzi “whale” uye “narwhale.” Handidi kuita sendisina kunaka, asi whale iyi isingataridzike inotaridzika kunge nguruve yakagurwa; uye, kana iri mbira, kungoitarisa kamwe chete pairi zvakakwana kushamisa munhu, kuti muzana rino remakore rechigumi nemapfumbamwe mvuu yakadaro inogona kugadzirwa nemichindwe nokuda kwechokwadi paruzhinji rwevakomana vechikoro vakangwara.

Zvino, zvakare, muna 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, nyanzvi yezvezvisikwa, akabudisa bhuku resainzi rakarongwa newhale, umo mune mifananidzo yakawanda yemarudzi akasiyana eRevhiatani. Zvose izvi hazvina kururama chete, asi mufananidzo weMysticetus kana Greenland whale (ndiko kuti, Right whale), kunyange Scoresby, murume ane ruzivo kwenguva refu sekubata mhuka iyo, anozivisa kuti haana mumwe wake mune zvakasikwa.

Asi kuiswa kwekepisi-chisote kune zvese izvi bhizinesi rekukanganisa kwakachengeterwa sainzi Frederick Cuvier, mukoma waBaron anozivikanwa. Muna 1836, akabudisa Natural History of Whales, umo anopa chaanoti mufananidzo weSperm Whale. Usati waratidza mufananidzo iwoyo kune chero Nantucketer, iwe wakanyatso kupa yako pfupiso yekudzoka kubva kuNantucket. Mune izwi, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale haisi Sperm Whale, asi squash. Chokwadika, haana kumbova nebetsero yerwendo rwokubata whale (varume vakadaro havawanzowana), asi kwaakabva mufananidzo iwoyo, ndiani angaziva? Zvichida akazviwana semutangiri wake wezvesainzi munharaunda imwe cheteyo, Desmarest, akawana imwe yekubvisa kwake pamuviri kwechokwadi; ndiko kuti, kubva pamufananidzo weChinese. Uye rudzii rwevakomana vanofara vane penzura avo maChina, makapu akawanda emushonga nemasosa anotizivisa.

Kana ari ma<em>whale evanopenda zviratidzo anoonekwa mumigwagwa akarembera pamusoro pezvitoro zvevatengesi vemafuta, zvichanzii nezvawo? Vanowanzova Richard III. mawhale, ane dromedary humps, uye anotyisa kwazvo; kudya kwemangwanani pamatart matatu kana mana evafambisi vezvikepe, ndiko kuti zvikepe zvewhale zvizere nevafambisi vezvikepe: kuremara kwavo kunoyerera mumakungwa eropa nependi yebhuruu.

Asi izvi zvikanganiso zvakawanda mukuratidza whale hazvishamisi zvachose. Funga! Mazhinji emifananidzo yesainzi yakatorwa kubva kuhove dzakasungwa; uye iwaya anenge akarurama sokudhirowa kwengarava yaparara, ine musana wakatyoka, ingamirira nenzira yakarurama mhuka inokudzwa imene mukudada kwayo kwose kusingaputsikidzwi kweganda namapfumo. Kunyangwe nzou dzakamira nehurefu hwadzo hwakazara, Revhiatani mhenyu haisati yamboyangarara pamufananidzo wayo. Whale mhenyu, muhukuru hwayo hwakazara uye kukosha, inongoonekwa chete mugungwa mumvura isinganzwisisike; uye boka guru riri kutatarika harichaoneki, sengarava inoenda kuhondo; uye kubva muchinhu ichocho chinhu chisingabviri nekusingaperi kuti munhu anofa amusimudze nemuviri mudenga, kuitira kuchengetedza kuzvimba kwake kukuru uye kusateerera. Uye, tisingatauri nezve musiyano mukuru unofungidzirwa we contour pakati pe whale yechidiki inoyamwa neLeviathan yaPlatoian Leviathan; asi, kunyangwe kana iri imwe yevadiki vanoyamwa whales vakasimudzwa padhishi rechikepe, akadaro panguva iyoyo anoshamisa, akafanana eel, ane makumbo, akasiyana-siyana maumbirwo ake, zvekuti kutaura kwake chaiko dhiyabhorosi pachake aisagona kubata.

Asi zvinogona kufungidzirwa, kuti kubva muskeleton yakashama yewhale yakasungwa, mazano akarurama anogona kutorwa achibata chimiro chake chechokwadi. Kana bodo. Nekuti ndechimwe chezvinhu zvinonyanya kuda kuziva nezve Revhiatani iyi, kuti skeleton yake inopa pfungwa shoma yechimiro chake. Kunyangwe skeleton yaJeremy Bentham, iyo inorembera candelabra muraibhurari yemumwe wevaurayi vake, inonyatso buritsa pfungwa yehure-browed utilitarian jenduru, ane ese aJeremy anotungamira maitiro; asi hapana cherudzi urwu chaigona kutorwa kubva kune chero mapfupa erevhiatani akataurwa. Muchokwadi, sekutaura kunoita Muvhimi mukuru, marangwanda ewhale ane hukama hwakafanana nemhuka yakadyarwa uye yakafukidzwa sezvinoitwa netumbuyu kune chrysalis inoitenderedza. Hunhu uhu hunoratidzwa zvinoshamisa mumusoro, sezvo mune chimwe chikamu chebhuku rino zvichange zvichiratidzwa. Iyo zvakare inoratidzwa zvine mutsindo mudivi fin, mapfupa ayo anenge achipindura mapfupa eruoko rwemunhu, kubvisa chigunwe chete. Iyi fin ine zvigunwe zvina zvenguva dzose pfupa, index, yepakati, mhete, uye kagunwe. Asi ose aya anogara zvechigarire muchifukidziro chazvo chenyama, seminwe yomunhu muchifukidziro chokugadzira. “Zvisinei nokusangwarira whale padzimwe nguva ingatibatira,” akadaro Stubb anosetsa rimwe zuva, “haambogoni kunzi zvirokwazvo anotibata pasina mittens.”

Nokuda kweizvi zvikonzero zvose, ipapoka, nzira ipi neipi yaungazvitarira nayo, unofanira kugumisa kuti Revhiatani huru ndicho chisikwa chimwe chete chiri munyika chinofanira kuramba chisina kupendwa kusvikira pakupedzisira. Chokwadi, mufananidzo mumwe unogona kusvika pachiratidzo chiri pedyo zvikuru kupfuura mumwe, asi hapana anogona kuurova nemwero wakakura kwazvo weururami. Saka hapana nzira yepanyika yekuziva kuti whale yakaita sei chaizvo. Uye iyo chete modhi iyo iwe yaunogona kuwana kunyange pfungwa inoshivirira yehupenyu hwake contour, ndeye kuenda whaling iwe pachako; asi nekuita izvi, unomhanya mungozi isiri diki yekuve chitofu chekusingaperi nekunyudzwa naye. Naizvozvo, ndinoona sokuti waisanyanya kunyanyokurumidza mukuda kwako kubata Revhiatani iyi.





CHITSAUKO 56. Pamusoro peMifananidzo Isina Mhosva yeMakudo, uye Mifananidzo Yechokwadi yeWhaling Scenes.

Mukubatana nemifananidzo inotyisa yemawhale, ndinoyedzwa zvakanyanya pano kuti ndipinde pane idzo nyaya dzinotyisa dzadzo dzinowanikwa mune mamwe mabhuku, ekare uye emazuva ano, kunyanya muPliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, nezvimwewo. Asi ndinopfuudza nyaya iyoyo.

Ndinoziva nezve mana chete akabudiswa maratidziro eSperm Whale huru; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, uye Beale's. Muchitsauko chakapfuura Colnett naCuvier vakarehwa. Huggins's iri nani kupfuura yavo; asi, nekusawirirana kukuru, Beale's ndiyo yakanyanya kunaka. Mifananidzo yose yeBeale yewhale iyi yakanaka, kunze kwechimiro chepakati mumufananidzo wehutatu hutatu mumafungiro akasiyana-siyana, kuvhara chitsauko chake chechipiri. Nzvimbo yake yepamberi, zvikepe zvinorwisa Sperm Whales, kunyangwe pasina mubvunzo zvakaverengerwa kufadza kusahadzika kwevagari vevamwe varume vepamusha, kwakaringana uye kwakafanana nehupenyu mukuita kwayo. Mamwe eSperm Whale mifananidzo muJ. Ross Browne yakanaka chaizvo mucontour; asi akanyorwa zvinosuruvarisa. Iyo haisi mhosva yake zvakadaro.

YeRight Whale, iyo yakanakisa rondedzero mifananidzo iri muScoresby; asi anokweverwa pachikero chiduku zvikuru kuti abudise murangariro unodikanwa. Ane mufananidzo mumwe chete wezviratidziro zvewhaling, uye uku kushomeka kunosuruvarisa, nekuti zviri nemifananidzo yakadaro chete, kana waita zvakanaka, iwe unogona kuwana chero chinhu senge pfungwa yechokwadi yewhale mhenyu sekuonekwa nevavhimi vake vapenyu.

Asi, zvakatorwa kune vese mune zvese, zvakanyanya kunaka, kunyangwe mune mamwe mashoko asiri iwo akanyanya kunaka, kuratidzwa kwemawhale uye whaling zviratidziro zvingawanikwa chero panowanikwa, maviri makuru echiFrench akavezwa, akanyatso itwa, uye akatorwa kubva mumifananidzo neGarnery imwe. Zvichienderana, vanomiririra kurwiswa kweSperm uye Right Whale. Mukunyora kwekutanga, Sperm Whale inoratidzwa muhukuru hwakazara hwesimba, ichangobva kusimuka pasi pechikepe kubva pakadzika kwegungwa, uye ichitakura mudenga mumhepo kumusana kwake kuputsika kunotyisa kwemapuranga akapiswa. Kumberi kwechikepe hakuna kumbotyoka zvishoma, uye kunokweva kwakangomira pamuzongoza wechikara; uye wakamira nechekumberi ikoko, kwenguva imwe chete iyoyo isingaenzaniswi, unoona mukwasvi, hafu akafukidzwa nemhepo inovira inopisa yewhale, uye ari kuita kusvetuka, sokunge ari kubva mumawere. Chiito chechinhu chose chakanaka uye chechokwadi zvinoshamisa. Iyo hafu-isina tambo-tabhu inoyangarara pamusoro pegungwa rakachena; matanda emapuranga akadururwa hapoons obliquely bob mairi; misoro yevashandi vekushambira yakapararira pamusoro pewhale mumashoko akasiyana ekutya; apo chikepe chinenge chiri kudzika panzvimbo ine dutu. Mhosva yakakomba inogona kuwanikwa neruzivo rweanatomical yewhale iyi, asi ngazvipfuure; sezvo, kwehupenyu hwangu, ndakanga ndisingakwanisi kudhirowa yakanaka kudaro.

Muchinyorwa chechipiri, chikepe chiri muchiitiko chekudhirowa padivi pemupendero webarnacled yehombe yeRight Whale, iyo inokungurutsa husora hwayo nhema mugungwa seimwe mossy rock-slide kubva kumawere ePatagonian. Masvikiro ake akati twi, azere, uye akasviba sedota; kuitira kuti kubva pakuwanda kweutsi muchimuni, ungafunga kuti panofanira kunge kune ushingi hwekubikira chirairo muura hukuru huri pasi apa. Shiri dzemugungwa dziri kudongorera makakanje madiki, hove dzemagoko, uye mamwe masiwiti emugungwa nemakaroni, ayo anombotakurwa neRight Whale pamusana wake une nhenda. Uye nguva yese iyi revhiatani ine miromo mikobvu iri kumhanyidzana nepakati pemvura yakadzika, ichisiya matani emutsinga machena mumutsara wayo, uye ichikonzera chikepe chidiki kuzununguka mumapundu sechikepe chakabatwa pedyo nemavhiri echitsiko chegungwa. Nokudaro, kumberi kune mheremhere yose; asi kumashure, mukupesana kunoyemurika kwehunyanzvi, kune mwero wegirazi wegungwa wakagadzikana, maseiri akanyura asina kuomeswa echikepe chisina simba, uye inert mass yewhale yakafa, nhare yakakundwa, ine mureza wekubatwa neusimbe wakarembera kubva padanda rewhale rakapinzwa mugomba rake.

Garnery mupendi ndiani, kana aive, handizive. Asi hupenyu hwangu nekuda kwahwo aingove achitaura nezvechidzidzo chake, kana zvimwe akadzidziswa zvinoshamisa nemumwe ane ruzivo rwewhaleman. MaFrench ndivo vakomana vekuita kupenda. Enda undotarisa mifananidzo yese yeEurope, uye uchawanepi shongwe yakadai yekugara nekufema pacanvas, semuhoro yekukunda kuVersailles; apo muoni anorwa nzira yake, pell-mell, kuburikidza nehondo huru dzinotevedzana dzeFrance; uko munondo wese unoratidzika kunge kupenya kweMaodzanyemba Mwenje, uye madzimambo ane zvombo anotevedzana nemaEmperors vanodhuma, semhosva yemacentaurs ane korona? Kwete zvachose asina kukodzera nzvimbo mune iyo gallery, aya egungwa ehondo-zvidimbu zveGarnery.

Hunhu hwechisikigo hwevaFrench hwekutora kunaka kwezvinhu hunoratidzika kunge hwakanyatso ratidzwa mune izvo mifananidzo uye mifananidzo yavanayo yezviratidziro zvavo zvekubata whaling. Pasina chikamu chimwe chete chegumi cheruzivo rweEngland mukubata hove, uye kwete chikamu chechiuru cheavo veAmerica, zvakadaro vakapa nyika mbiri nemifananidzo yakapedzwa chete inokwanisa kuendesa mweya chaiwo wekuvhima whale. Kazhinji, maChirungu neAmerica whale draughtsmen vanoita sevanogutsikana zvachose nekuratidza maitiro ekugadzirisa zvinhu, zvakadai semufananidzo usina munhu we whale; izvo, maererano nekunaka kwemaitiro, zvakangofanana nekudhirowa chimiro chepiramidhi. Kunyange Scoresby, uyo ane mukurumbira wakarurama Right whaleman, mushure mekunge atipa hurefu hwakaoma hwakazara hweGreenland whale, uye zvitatu kana zvina zvakapfava zviduku zvenarwhale uye porpoises, inobata isu kune nhevedzano yezvinyorwa zvechinyakare zvezvikouro zvezvikepe, mapanga ekucheka, uye grapnels; uye nekuchenjerera kusingaonekwi kweLeuwenhoeck inobvuma kuongororwa kwenyika inodedera makumi mapfumbamwe nenhanhatu emhando dzemakristaro echando eArctic akakurisa. Ndinoreva kusazvidza kune mufambi akanaka kwazvo (Ndinomukudza semurwi), asi mune yakakosha nyaya yaive tarisiro kuti ndisatore kristaro yega yega afidaviti yakatorwa pamberi peGreenland Justice yeRugare.

Mukuwedzera kune izvozvo zvakavezwa zvakaisvonaka zvinobva kuGarnery, kune zvimwe zvakanyorwa zviviri zvechiFrench zvakafanira kucherekedzwa, nomumwe anozvinyoresa amene “H. Durand.” Imwe yacho, kunyange isina kunyatsoenderana nechinangwa chedu chazvino, inofanirwa kutaurwa pane dzimwe nhoroondo. Inzvimbo yemasikati yakanyarara pakati pezvitsuwa zvePacific; murwi wechiFrench whaler akadzika, kumahombekombe, akadzikama, uye neusimbe achitora mvura muchikepe; maseiri akasunungurwa echikepe, uye mashizha marefu emichindwe kumashure, ose ari maviri akarembera pamwe chete mumhepo isingafefeki. Mhedzisiro yacho yakanaka kwazvo, kana ikatariswa maererano nekupa kwayo vabati vehove vakasimba pasi pechimwe chezvikamu zvavo zvishoma zvekuzorora kwekumabvazuva. Chimwe chinyorwa chakasiyana chaizvo: chikepe chinosimuka pagungwa, uye mumoyo chaimo wehupenyu hweRevhiatani, neRight Whale parutivi; mudziyo (muchiitiko chekucheka-mukati) fambisa kune monster sekunge kune quay; uye igwa, richisunda nokuchimbidza kubva panzvimbo iyi yebasa, rava kuda kudzingirira mawhale ari kure. Mapfumo nemapfumo zvakatambanudzwa kuti zvishandiswe; vakwasvi vatatu vachangogadzika danda mugomba rayo; nepo kubva mumhuru yakangoerekana yegungwa, chikepe chidiki chinomira pakati-kati chakamira kunze kwemvura, sebhiza rinosimudza. kubva muchikepe, utsi hwemarwadzo ehova iri kuvira soutsi hunokwira pamusoro pemusha wevasori; uye kumhepo, gore dema, rinokwira nesimba rokupopota nemvura, rinoratidzika kuva rinokurumidzisa basa ravafambisi vezvikepe vari kufara.

Nechekumashure kwechikepe inomoneredzwa nemhepo mubhabhu, kwete sepombi yehonye yechiri, asi kuti igadzire "zvisote" zvakamisikidzwa zvakamisikidzwa zvakaita sechizi, kana zvidimbu zve concentric spiralizations, pasina buri asi "moyo," kana miniti yakatwasuka chubhu inoumbwa paaxis yechizi. Sezvinoita kakupengereka kana kunyunguduka pakukochekera kwaizoita, mukubuda, nokusatadza kubvisa ruoko rwemumwe munhu, gumbo, kana muviri wese, kungwarira kwakanyanya kunoshandiswa mukumisikidza tambo mubhabhu yayo. Mamwe maharpooneers anopedza angangoita mangwanani ese mubhizinesi iri, achitakura mutsetse wakakwira mudenga uye vozoudzoreredza pasi nemubhuroko rakananga kutabhu, kuitira kuti iite zvekukochekera kuti isunungure kubva kune zvese zvinobvira kuunyana nekumonyoroka.

Muzvikepe zvechiRungu matubhu maviri anoshandiswa pachinzvimbo cherimwe; mutsetse mumwechete uchiramba uchikochekerwa mumabhabhu ese ari maviri. Pane chimwe chinhu chakanaka pane izvi; nokuti aya mapatya ari maduku zvikuru anokwana zviri nyore kupinda muchikepe, uye haazvinetse zvikuru; nepo, tabhu yekuAmerica, ingangoita mafiti matatu pakufara uye yakadzama zvakaenzanirana, inogadzira zvinhu zvakati wandei zvechikepe chine mapuranga anongoita hafu yeinji mukukora; nokuti pasi pechikepe chewhale chakafanana nechando chakakosha, icho chichatakura huremu hwakagoverwa, asi husina kunyanya kukoshesa. Kana chifukidziro checanvas chakapendwa chichiomberwa pabhavhu rekuAmerica, chikepe chinoratidzika kunge chiri kusimuka nekeke remuchato rinoshamisa kuti ripe kune whale.

Migumo miviri yemutsara inoratidzirwa; iyo yepasi yekupedzisira inogumira muziso-splice kana chiuno chinokwira kubva pasi chakatarisana nedivi rebhabhu, uye chakarembera pamusoro pemupendero waro wakasununguka zvachose kubva kune zvese. Uku kurongeka kwekupedzisira kwepasi kunodiwa pamaakaundi maviri. Chekutanga: Kuitira kufambisa kusungirirwa kwairi kweimwe mutsara kubva muchikepe chakavakidzana, kana whale yarohwa ikarira yakadzika zvekutyisidzira kutakura mutsara wese wainge wakasungirirwa paharpoon. Muzviitiko izvi, whale zvechokwadi inoshandurwa semug yeale, sokunge zvakadaro, kubva kune imwe igwa kuenda kune imwe; kunyange igwa rekutanga rinogara richitenderera riri pedyo kuti ribatsire shamwari yacho. Chechipiri: Urongwa uhwu hunokosha kuitira kuchengetedzwa kwakafanana; nokuti kana magumo ezasi emutsara nenzira ipi zvayo akabatanidzwa nechikepe, uye iyo whale ipapo kumhanya mutsara kunze kusvika kumagumo anenge muminiti imwe chete, yekusvuta sezvaanoita dzimwe nguva, aisazomira ipapo, nokuti chikepe chekuparadzwa chaizokweverwa pasi shure kwake kusvika pakadzika kwegungwa; uye kana zvakadaro hapana mucheri weguta aizomuwana zvakare.

Asati adzikisa igwa kuti adzingirwe, mugumo wepamusoro wetambo unotorwa nechekumashure kubva mubhabhu, uye uchipfuura uchitenderedza musoro werogi ipapo, unotakurirwazve mberi kureba kwechikepe chose, uchitsamira zvakachinjika pachirukiso kana mubato wechikwasvi chomunhu ari wose, zvokuti chinomhanya chichirwisana nechanza chake mukukwasva; uye vachipfuurawo pakati pevarume, sezvavanogara pakatarisana nepfuti, kuenda kune inotungamira chocks kana grooves mune yakanyanyisa kunongedza kumberi kwechikepe, apo pini yemapuranga kana skewer saizi yequill yakajairika, inodzivirira kuti isatsvedza kunze. Kubva pazvimedu zvinorembera mune zvishoma festoon pamusoro peuta, uye zvino inopfuudzwa mukati mechikepe zvakare; uye mamwe mafatomi gumi kana makumi maviri (anonzi mutsara webhokisi) ari kumonererwa pamusoro pebhokisi riri muuta, inopfuurira nzira yayo ichienda kupfuti ichiri mberi zvishomanana, uye ipapo inonamirwa kupfuti-tambo—tambo inobatanidzwa pakarepo nembira; asi yapfuura kune icho chinongedzo, iyo pfupi-warp inoenda kuburikidza nezvakawanda zvisinganzwisisike zvinonetesa kutsanangura.

Saka whale-line inopeta chikepe chese mumakoiri ayo akaomarara, ichimonyoroka uye ichimonyorora ichitenderedza munenge mativi ese. Vafambisi vose vanokwasva vanobatanidzwa mukukoromoka kwaro kune ngozi; zvokuti kune ziso rinotya romuridzi wenyika, vanoita sevatambi vokuIndia, vane nyoka dzinouraya zvikuru dzinonakisa makumbo avo nokutamba. Kana kuti hapana mwanakomana wemukadzi anofa, kekutanga, angazvigadzika pakati pezviya zvinooma zvehempen, uye achikwesha hukuru hwake pakukwasva, achimufunga kuti panguva ipi neipi isingazivikanwe hapuni inogona kurohwa, uye nyonganiso dzese idzi dzinotyisa dzinoitwa semheni dzine mhete; haakwanisi kudzvinyirirwa saizvozvo pasina kudedera kunoita kuti mwongo uri mumapfupa ake uzununguke maari sezvinoita jelly riri kuzununguswa. Asi tsika-chinhu chinoshamisa! chii chisingagone kuita tsika?—Gayer sallies, mufaro wakawanda, majee ari nani, uye mareparte akajeka, iwe hauna kumbonzwa pamusoro pemahogany yako, kupfuura iwe uchanzwa pamusoro pehafu-inch yemusidhari muchena wewhale-boat, apo saizvozvo wakarembera mumakumbo e hangman; uye, semavharo matanhatu eCalais pamberi paMambo Edward, varume vatanhatu vanoumba vashandi vanokwevera mushaya dzerufu, nesimbi yakatenderedza muhuro wese, sezvaungataura.

Zvichida kufunga kuduku zvikuru zvino kuchakugonesa kuzvidavirira nokuda kwengwavaira dziya dzinodzokororwa dzinodzokororwa—dzimwe shomanene dzadzo dzinongoitika—dzouyu murume kana kuti murume uya achibudiswa muigwa nomutsetse, ndokurasika. Nokuti, kana tambo yave kubuda, kuti ugare ipapo muigwa, zvakafanana nokugara pakati pekutinhira kuzhinji kweinjini inopfungaira mukutamba kwakazara, apo danda rose rinobhururuka, nomugove, nevhiri, zvinenge zvichikufudza. Zvakaipa; nokuti haugoni kugara usina kupfakanyika mumwoyo wenjodzi idzi, nokuti igwa riri kuzununguka sekamba, uye unokandwa uku nokuno, pasina kana yambiro; uye chete nekumwe kuzvigadzirisa kunakidzwa uye panguva imwe chete yekuzvidira uye kuita, unogona kupukunyuka kuitwa Mazepa, uye kutiza uko zuva rinoona rose pacharo haringambokuboora kunze.

Zvekare: sezvo kudzikama kukuru uko kunongo tungamira uye kuporofita nezvedutu, pamwe kunotyisa kupfuura dutu chairo; nekuti, zvirokwazvo, kudzikama chinongova chokuputira nemvuropu yedutu; uye iri mairi pachayo, sezvo pfuti inoita seisingakuvadzi inobata upfu hunouraya, nebhora, uye kuputika; saka kuzorora kwakanaka kwetambo, sezvainorova nyoka chinyararire pamusoro pevakwasvi isati yaunzwa mumutambo chaiwo-ichi chinhu chinotakura kutyisa kwechokwadi kupfuura chero chinhu chipi nechipi chechiitiko chine ngozi ichi. Asi nei kutaura zvakawanda? Varume vese vanogara vakafukidzwa newhale-mitsetse. Vose vanozvarwa vaine zvikwapuro pamitsipa yavo; asi ndipo pazvinongobatwa mukukurumidza, kushanduka kwekamwe kamwe kwerufu, apo vanhu vanofa vanoona njodzi dzakanyarara, dzakajeka, dzinogara dziripo dzehupenyu. Uye kana iwe uri muzivi, kunyangwe wakagara muchikepe chewhale, haungambonzwa kana kutyisa zvakanyanya, kupfuura kuti ugare pamberi pemoto wako wemanheru uine poker, uye kwete harpoon, padivi pako.
