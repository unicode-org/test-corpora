FOLIOS. Pakati peizvi ini pano ndinosanganisira zvitsauko zvinotevera:—I. The Sperm Whale ; II. The Right Whale ; III. iyo Fin-Back Whale ; IV. Hump-backed Whale ; V. Razor Back Whale ; VI. Sulfur Bottom Whale .

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO I. ( Sperm Whale ).— Iyi whale, pakati pechiNgezi chekare chisingazivikanwi seTrumpa whale, uye Physeter whale, uye Anvil Headed whale, ndiyo Cachalot yazvino yeFrench, uye Pottsfich yeGermany, uye Macrocephalus of the Long Words. Iye, pasina mubvunzo, ndiye mugari mukurusa wepasi; anotyisa kupfuura mamwe mawhale ose kusangana nawo; yakanakisa pakuonekwa; uye chekupedzisira, chakanyanya kukosha mukutengeserana; iye ari iye chete chisikwa icho chinhu chinokosha, spermaceti, chinowanikwa. Zvese zvaari kuda, mune dzimwe nzvimbo dzakawanda, zvichawedzerwa pazviri. Iri kunyanya nezita rake randinofanira kuita. Philologically inofungidzirwa, hazvina musoro. Mazana amakore akapfuura, apo Sperm whale yakanga isingazikanwi chose chose muunhu hwayo chaihwo chaihwo, uye apo oiri yake yakangowanwa netsaona kubva kuhove dzakasungwa; mumazuva iwayo spermaceti, zvingaita sokuti, yaifarirwa zvikuru kuti yakabva muchisikwa chakafanana neiya yaizivikanwa panguva iyoyo muEngland seGreenland kana kuti Right Whale. Yakanga iri pfungwa zvakare, kuti iyi spermaceti imwechete yaive iyo inokurumidza kuseka yeGreenland Whale iyo syllable yekutanga yezwi inodudza. Munguva idzodzo, zvakare, spermaceti yainyanya kushomeka, isingashandiswe sechiedza, asi sechizoro uye medicament. Yaingove kuwanikwa kubva kune vemishonga sezvo iwe mazuvano uchitenga imwe rhubarb. Apo, sezvandinofunga, nekufamba kwenguva, chimiro chechokwadi che spermaceti chakazozivikanwa, zita rayo rekutanga rakanga richiri kuchengetwa nevatengesi; pasina panikiro kuwedzera ukoshi hwayo nepfungwa inokosha zvinoshamisa yokushomeka kwayo. Uye saka zita racho rinofanirwa kunge rasvika pakuiswa pane whale iyo iyi spermaceti yakatorwa chaizvo.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO II. ( Right Whale ).—Mune rumwe rutivi iyi ndiyo inoremekedzwa zvikurusa yerevhiatani, ichiva iyo inotanga kuvhimwa nguva dzose nomunhu. Inopa chinyorwa chinowanzozivikanwa se whalebone kana baleen; uye oiri yacho inonyanya kuzivikanwa se“mafuta ewhale,” chinhu chisingakoshi mukutengeserana. Pakati pevabati vehove, iye anosarudzwa zvisina kutsarukana nemazita ose anotevera: The Whale; Greenland Whale; Black Whale; Great Whale; Whale Yechokwadi; Right Whale. Pane kusanyatsozivikanwa kwerudzi urwu rwakabhabhatidzwa kakawanda. Zvino chii chinonzi whale, chandinosanganisira mumhando yechipiri yeFolios yangu? Ndiyo Great Mysticetus yeChirungu naturalists; Greenland Whale yeChirungu whalemen; Baleine Ordinaire yeFrench whalemen; iyo Growlands Walfish yeSweden. Ndiyo whale iyo kweanopfuura mazana maviri emakore apfuura yakavhimwa nemaDutch neChirungu mumakungwa eArctic; iwhale iyo vabati vehove vekuAmerica vagara vachitevera mugungwa reIndia, paBrazil Banks, paNor' West Coast, uye nedzimwe nzvimbo dzakasiyana-siyana dzepasi, dzakasarudzwa navo Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Vamwe vanonyepedzera kuona musiyano pakati peGreenland whale yeChirungu neright whale yeAmerica. Asi vanonyatsobvumirana muzvinhu zvavo zvose zvikuru; uye hapasati pave nechimwe chokwadi chakatemerwa chekuisa musiyano mukuru. Kuburikidza nezvikamu zvisingaperi zvakavakirwa pamisiyano isingaperi, kuti mamwe madhipatimendi enhoroondo yechisikigo anove akaoma kunzwisisa. The right whale ichange ichibatwa kumwe kureba, maererano nekujekesa sperm whale.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO III. ( Fin-Back ).—Pasi pomusoro uyu ndinorangarira chikara icho, namazita akasiana-siana aFin-Back, Tall-Spout, uye Long-John, chave chichionekwa munenge mugungwa riri rose uye chinowanzova whale ine jeti iri kure rinowanzorondedzerwa zvikuru nevafambi vanoyambuka Atlantic, muNew York packet-tracks. Muhurefu hwaanowana, uye mubaleen yake, iyo Fin-back inofanana neiyo whale yakarurama, asi ndeye girth shoma, uye ruvara rwakareruka, ichiswedera kumuorivhi. Miromo yake mikuru inopa tambo-yakafanana netambo, inoumbwa neyakavhengana, inotsvedza mapete emakwinya makuru. Chimiro chake chikuru chinosiyanisa, chipedzi, chaanotora zita rake, chinowanzova chinhu chinooneka. Ichi chipedzi chakareba mafiti matatu kana mana, chichikura chakamira kubva nechekuseri kweshure, chechimiro cheangona, uye chine magumo akapinza. Kunyange kana chisiri chimwe chikamu chidiki chechisikwa chinoonekwa, iyi mhedzi iri yoga, dzimwe nguva, inozoonekwa ichibuda kubva pamusoro. Kana gungwa rakadzikama zvine mwero, uye rakatarwa zvishoma nedenderedzwa, uye ichi chipedzi-chakaita semagnomon chinosimuka uye chichiisa mimvuri pamusoro penzvimbo yakaunyana, zvinogona kunge zvichifungidzirwa kuti denderedzwa remvura rakaritenderedza rakaita sedhiri, ine chimiro uye wavy awa-mitsara yakatemerwa pairi. Padhiari iroro raAhazi mumvuri unowanzodzokera shure. Iyo Fin-Back haina kushamwaridzana. Anoita seanovenga whale, sevamwe varume vanovenga vanhu. Kunyara kwazvo; uchigara uri woga; kusimuka zvisingatarisirwi kumusoro mumvura dziri kure uye dzakasvava; ndege yake yakatwasuka uye imwe chete yakakwirira ichikwira sepfumo refu risinganzwisisike riri pamupata usina miti; vane chipo chesimba rinoshamisa uye nekumhanya mukushambira, sekuzvidza zvese zvinotsvakwa nemunhu; revhiatani uyu anoita seakadzingwa uye asingakundwi Kaini werudzi rwake, aine chiratidzo chechimiro ichocho pamusana wake. Kubva pakuva nebhareen mumuromo make, iyo Fin-Back dzimwe nguva inosanganisirwa neiyo whale yakarurama, pakati pemhando yetioretic inonzi Whalebone whales , kureva, mawhale ane baleen. Pakati peaya anonzi Whalebone whales, panogona kunge paine marudzi akati wandei, mazhinji ayo, zvisinei, anozivikanwa zvishoma. Nyeredzi dzakafara nehove dzine muromo; pike-headed whales; mabhurukwa whales; whales dziri pasi peshaya uye hove dzakaonda, ndiwo mazita evaredzi vemarudzi mashoma.

Pamusoro pechikumbiro ichi che "Whalebone whales," zvakakosha zvikuru kutaura, kuti zvisinei kuti mazita akadaro angave ari nyore mukufambisa zvinyorwa kune rumwe rudzi rwemawhale, asi hazvina maturo kuedza kujekesa kwakajeka kweRevhiatani, yakavakirwa pane imwe baleen yake, kana hump, kana fin, kana mazino; zvisinei kuti izvo zvakaiswa zvikamu kana zvimiro zviri pachena zvinoratidzika zviri nani zvakagadziridzwa kuti zvikwanise kuwana hwaro hwenguva dzose sisitimu yeCetology pane chero imwe yakasarudzika misiyano yemuviri, iyo whale, mumhando dzayo, inopa. Saka sei? The baleen, hump, back-fin, uye mazino; izvi zvinhu izvo zvakasarudzika zvakaparadzirwa zvisina tsarukano pakati pemhando dzese dzemawhale, pasina kutarisisa kuti chingave chimiro chechimiro chavo mune zvimwe uye zvakanyanya kukosha. Nokudaro, sperm whale uye humpbacked whale, imwe neimwe ine hump; asi ipapo mufananidzo unogumira. Bva, iyi whale imwe cheteyo ine humpbacked whale neGreenland whale, imwe neimwe yeidzi ine baleen; asi ipapo mufananidzo unoguma. Uye zvakangofanana nezvimwe zvikamu zvataurwa pamusoro apa. Mumarudzi akasiyana-siyana emawhale, anoumba misanganiswa yakadaro isina kujairika; kana, muchiitiko chemumwe wavo akavharirwa, kusarudzika kwakadai; sekushora zvachose nzira dzese dzakaumbwa pahwaro hwakadaro. Padombo iri mumwe nemumwe we whale-naturalists akatsemuka.

Asi zvinogona kufungidzirwa kuti, muzvikamu zvemukati zvewhale, mune yake anatomy-imomo, zvirinani, isu tichakwanisa kurova iyo chaiyo classification. Kwete; Chii, semuenzaniso, chiri muGreenland whale's anatomy inonyanya kushamisa kupfuura baleen yake? Asi isu taona kuti ne baleen yake hazvibviri kurongedza nenzira kwayo iyo Greenland whale. Uye kana iwe ukadzika mumatumbo emhando dzakasiyana dzerevhiatani, nei ikoko usingazowani misiyano yechikamu chemakumi mashanu sechinowanikwa kune systematizer seizvo zvekunze zvatonyorwa. Zvino chii chinosara? hapana chimwe kunze kwekubata mawhale nemuviri, muhuwandu hwawo hwakasununguka, uye nekuaronga neushingi nenzira iyoyo. Uye iyi ndiyo Bibliographical system yakagamuchirwa pano; uye ndiyo chete inogona kubudirira, nekuti ndiyo chete inogoneka. Kuenderera mberi.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ) CHITSAUKO IV. ( Hump Back ).— whale iyi inowanzoonekwa pamhenderekedzo yegungwa yokuchamhembe kweAmerica. Anogara achitorwa ikoko, uye akakweverwa kuchiteshi chezvikepe. Ane paki huru paari somutengesi; kana kuti ungamuti Nzou neCastle whale. Chero zvazvingava, zita raanozivikanwa naro harimusiyanise zvakakwana, sezvo sperm whale inewo hump kunyangwe iri diki. Mafuta ake haana kukosha zvakanyanya. Ane baleen. Iye ndiye anonyanya kutamba uye ane moyo wakareruka pane ese mawhale, achigadzira yakawanda gay furo nemvura chena kazhinji kupfuura chero ipi zvayo.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO V. ( Razor Back ).— Pairi whale zvishoma zvinozivikanwa kunze kwezita rake. Ndamuona ari chinhambwe kubva kuCape Horn. Nechimiro chokurega basa, anonzvenga vavhimi nevazivi. Kunyange zvazvo akanga asiri mbwende, haasati amboratidza chimwe chikamu chake kunze kwemusana wake, uyo unokwira mumupata wakareba wakapinza. Ngaaende. Ndinoziva zvishoma nezvake, uye hapana mumwe munhu anoziva.

BHUKU I. ( Folio ), CHITSAUKO VI. ( Sulfur Bottom ).—Mumwe murume akarega basa, ane dumbu resarufa, pasina mubvunzo akawana nokukwenya achitevedza mataira eTartarian mune mamwe emitambo yake yakadzama yokunyura. Haawanzoonekwa; handina kumbobvira ndamuona kunze kwekure kure kumakungwa ekumaodzanyemba, uye nguva dzose ari kure zvakanyanya kuti ndidzidze chiso chake. Haambodzingirwi; aitiza netambo. Prodigies inoudzwa nezvake. Adieu, Sulfur Pazasi! Ini handikwanise kutaura chimwe chinhu chiri chokwadi kwauri, kana wekare Nantucketer.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO I. ( Grampus ).—Kunyange zvazvo hove iyi, iyo ine kufema kukuru, kana kuti kutoti kuvhuvhuta, yakagovera chirevo kuvanhu venyika, inosanozivikanwa kwazvo kuti inogara pakadzika, bva haisati yazivikanwa zvikuru pakati pe<em>whale. Asi iine mativi ose akaisvonaka akasiyana erevhiatani, nyanzvi zhinji dzezvakasikwa dzakamuziva nokuda kwechimwe chete. Ane mwero octavo saizi, anosiyana kubva gumi nemashanu kusvika makumi maviri neshanu mafiti pakureba, uye anoenderana zviyero zvakatenderedza chiuno. Anoshambira ari mapoka; haagari achivhimwa, kunyange zvazvo mafuta ake akawanda muhuwandu, uye akanakira chiedza. Nevamwe vabati vehove maitiro ake anoonekwa sechiratidzo chekufambira mberi kweiyo great sperm whale.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO II. ( Black Fish ).—Ndinopa mazita evaredzi vakakurumbira vehove dzose idzi, nokuti kazhinji ndidzo dzakanakisisa. Kana chero zita risinganzwisisike kana risingatsananguriki, ndichataura kudaro, ndopa rimwe zano. Ini ndinoita izvozvi, ndichibata Hove nhema, dzinonzi, nekuti kusviba ndiyo mutemo pakati peanenge ese mawhale. Saka, mudaidza kuti Hyena Whale, kana uchida. Kushama kwake kunonyatsozivikanwa, uye kubva mumamiriro ezvinhu emukati makona emiromo yake akakomberedzwa kumusoro, anotakura kusingaperi Mephistophelean grin pachiso chake. Whale iyi inosvika mafiti gumi nematanhatu kana gumi nemasere pakureba. Anowanikwa munzvimbo dzinenge dzese. Ane nzira yakasarudzika yekuratidza mhedzi yake yakakochekera mukutuhwina, inoita senge mhuno yeRoma. Kana vasingashandiswi zvine pundutso, vavhimi vesperm whale dzimwe nguva vanobata Hyena whale, kuti varambe vachiwana mafuta akachipa ebasa remumba — sevamwe vachengeti vemba vasina hanya, pasina kambani, uye vari voga, vanopisa tallow isina kunaka pachinzvimbo chewakisi inonhuwa. Kunyange zvazvo blubber yavo yakaonda zvikuru, mamwe emawhale aya achakupa mari inopfuura makumi matatu emarita emafuta.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO III. ( Narwhale ), ndiko kuti, Nostril whale .—Mumwe muenzaniso wewhale inodaidzwa zvinoshamisa, inodanwa saizvozvi ndinofunga kuti kubva munyanga yake yakasiyana-siyana yaifungidzirwa pakutanga zvisizvo nemhino yakamira pamusoro. Chisikwa ichi chakareba mafiti gumi nematanhatu pakureba, ukuwo nyanga yacho inosvika mafiti mashanu, asi mamwe achidarika gumi, uye achitosvika gumi neshanu. Kutaura chokwadi, nyanga iyi ingori nyanga yakarebeswa, inokura kubva mushaya mumutsara yakaora mwoyo zvishoma kubva kune yakachinjika. Asi inongowanikwa padivi rakashata, iyo ine mhedzisiro yakaipa, ichipa muridzi wayo chimwe chinhu chakafanana nechimiro chemurume akapusa-kuruboshwe. Ndechipi chinangwa chaicho nyanga yenzou kana pfumo rinopindura, zvingava zvakaoma kutaura. Hazviratidzike sezvinoshandiswa sebadza rehove yemunondo nebhill-fish; kunyangwe vamwe vafambisi vezvikepe vachindiudza kuti Narwhale inoishandisa sereki mukupidigura pasi pegungwa kuti vawane chikafu. Charley Coffin akati raishandiswa pakubaya aizi; nokuti Narwhale, ichikwira pamusoro peGungwa Dzvuku, uye ikariwana yakafukidzwa nechando, inosimudza nyanga yayo, uye naizvozvo ikabvarura. Asi haugone kuratidza chimwe chezvirevo izvi kuti ndezvechokwadi. Maonero angu ndeekuti, zvisinei kuti nyanga ine divi rimwe chete iyi inogona kunyatso shandiswa neNarwhale-zvisinei zvingadaro-zvingave zvakamuitira zvakamunakira folda mukuverenga mapepa. Iyo Narwhale yandakanzwa ichinzi Tusked whale, Horned whale, uye Unicorn whale. Iye zvirokwazvo muenzaniso wekuda kuziva weUnicornism inowanikwa munenge humambo hwese hwehupenyu hwehupenyu. Kubva kune vamwe vanyori vekare ndakaunganidza kuti iyi nyanga yegungwa-unicorn yaive mumazuva ekare yaionekwa semushonga mukuru wechepfu, uye nekudaro, kugadzirira kwayo kwakaunza mitengo yakakurisa. Yakanga yakanyungudutswa kuita munyu unonyunguduka wevakadzi vari kufenda, nenzira imwechete iyo nyanga dzemhembwe dzinogadzirwa kuita hartshorn. Pakutanga iyo pachayo yakaverengerwa chinhu chekuda kuziva kukuru. Black Letter inondiudza kuti Sir Martin Frobisher pakudzoka kwavo kubva kurwendo irworwo, Mambokadzi Bess paakaninira neushingi ruoko rwake rwaiva nematombo anokosha kwaari kubva pafafitera reGreenwich Palace, sezvo ngarava yake yakashinga yaifamba ichidzika nerwizi Thames; "Apo Sir Martin akadzoka kubva kurwendo irworwo," anodaro Black Letter, "akagwadama mabvi akamupa nyanga refu yeNarwhale, iyo kwenguva yakareba mushure mekurembera munhare yeWindsor." Mumwe munyori weIrish anopokana kuti Earl weLeicester, akapfugama namabvi, akapawo kuhumambo hwake imwe nyanga, ine chekuita nechikara chepasi chechimiro cheunicorn.

Iyo Narwhale ine chimiro chakanakisa, sembada, iine ruvara rwevhu-chena mukaka, ine mavara akatenderera uye akatenderera edema. Mafuta ake akakwirira zvikuru, akajeka uye akanaka; asi ishoma, uye haawanzovhimwa. Anonyanya kuwanikwa mumakungwa e circumpolar.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO IV. ( Muurayi ).—Paiyi whale zvishoma zvinonyatsozivikanwa kuNantucketer, uye hapana zvachose kune anozviti muongorori wezvisikwa. Zvandakamuona ari nechekure, ndingati aitova nehukuru hwema grampus. Ane utsinye zvikuru—rudzi rwehove inonzi Feegee. Dzimwe nguva anotora hove huru dzeFolio nemuromo, orembera ipapo sechikweva, kusvikira hanzi yanetseka kusvika yafa. Mhondi haivhimwe. Handina kumbonzwa kuti ane mafuta akaita sei. Kunze kunogona kuendeswa kune zita rakapihwa kune whale iyi, nekuda kwekusajeka kwayo. Nekuti tose tiri vaurayi panyika napagungwa; Bonapartes uye Sharks inosanganisirwa.

BHUKU II. ( Octavo ), CHITSAUKO V. ( Thrasher ).— Uyu munyoro ane mukurumbira nokuda kwomuswe wake, waanoshandisa nokuda kworuzhowa mukurova vavengi vake. Iye akati kurei kupfuura iyo Huzza Porpoise, asi yakawanda yakafanana general make. Mudenha, uye achakoira sehove. Ndakamuderedza kakawanda, asi handisati ndambomuona akabatwa.

BHUKU III. ( Duodecimo ), CHITSAUKO III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).— Rudzi rukurusa rwePorpoise; uye inongowanikwa muPacific, kusvika pakuzivikanwa kwayo. Zita chete rechirungu, raakadomwa naro kusvika zvino, ndere vabati vehove-Right-Whale Porpoise, kubva mumamiriro ekuti anowanikwa zvakanyanya pedyo neFolio iyoyo. Muchimiro, anosiyana mune imwe nhanho kubva kuHuzza Porpoise, ari wemudiki rotund uye jolly girth; zvirokwazvo, ane chimiro chakatsvinda uye chakafanana nemunhu. Haana zvimbi kumusana kwake (dzimwe mhuka dzakawanda), ane muswe wakanaka, uye maziso eIndia anonzwira tsitsi eruvara rwehazel. Asi muromo wake une hupfu unoparadza zvose. Kunyangwe musana wake wese kudzika kusvika kune zvimbi zvekurutivi rwakadzika, asi mutsara wemuganhu, wakasiyana sechiratidzo muchikepe chechikepe, chinonzi "chiuno chakajeka," mutsara iwoyo unomufambisa kubva padzinde kuenda shure, nemavara maviri akaparadzana, dema kumusoro uye chena pasi. Muchena unosanganisira chikamu chemusoro wake, uye muromo wake wese, izvo zvinoita kuti ataridzike sekunge achangobva kupukunyuka kushanya kwakashata kuenda kubhegi rekudya. Chimiro chakashata uye cheupfu! Mafuta ake akafanana neayo enguruvewo zvayo.

Kupfuura iyo DUODECIMO, iyi sisitimu haienderere mberi, sezvo iyo Porpoise iri idiki pane mawhale. Pamusoro, une ese maRevhiatani anokosha. Asi kune rabble yeasina chokwadi, anotiza, hafu-yakanakisa whales, iyo, seAmerican whaleman, ndinoziva nemukurumbira, asi kwete pachangu. Ndichavaverenga mazita avo enhare; nokuti zvichida runyoro rwakadaro runogona kuva rwakakosha kune vaongorori venguva yemberi, vanogona kupedza zvandinazvo pano asi vatanga. Kana imwe yeanotevera whale, ikazobatwa nekumakwa, ipapo inogona kuve nyore kuiswa muSystem ino, maererano nehukuru hwake hweFolio, Octavo, kana Duodecimo:—The Bottle-Nose Whale; the Junk Whale; the Pudding-Headed Whale; Cape Whale; the Leading Whale; Cannon Whale; Scragg Whale; iyo Coppered Whale; the Elephant Whale; the Iceberg Whale; Quog Whale; Blue Whale; zvichingodaro. Kubva muzviremera zveIcelandic, Dutch, uye chiNgezi chekare, pangava norumwe rudungwe rwema<em>whale asina chokwadi, akakomborerwa nemarudzi ose emazita eucouth. Asi ndinoasiya zvachose; uye haakwanise kubatsira kuvafungira nekuda kwemanzwi chete, azere neRevhiathaniism, asi zvisingarevi chinhu.

Pakupedzisira: Zvakataurwa pakutanga, kuti gadziriro ino yaisazova pano, uye pakarepo, inokwaniswa. Iwe haugoni kuona kuti ndakachengeta shoko rangu. Asi ini zvino ndinosiya cetological System yangu yakamira yakadaro isina kupera, kunyangwe iyo huru Cathedral yeCologne yakasiiwa, crane ichiri yakamira pamusoro peshongwe isina kupera. Nokuti madiki erections anogona kupedzwa nevagadziri vavo vekutanga; vakuru, vechokwadi, vanombosiya copestone kune vazukuru. Mwari vandichengete ndisapedzisire chero chinhu.
