SEHLUKO 55. Setitfombe Letimbi Temikhoma.

Ngitawupenda sikhatsi lesidze kuwe njengoba umuntfu angakhona ngaphandle kwe canvas, intfo lefana nesimo seliciniso semkhoma njengoba empeleni abonakala emehlweni emkhoma uma emtimbeni wakhe lophelele lomkhoma uboshelwe eceleni kwemkhumbi wemkhoma kute akhone kunyatselwa kahle lapho. Kungaba kuhle, ngako-ke, ngaphambilini kukhangisa kulabo titfombe leticabangako letifuna kwati ngaye letiphikisa kukholwa kwemuntfu waseveni ngekwetsembeka kuze kube ngulamuhla. Sesikhatsi sekulungisa umhlaba kuloludzaba, ngekufakazela titfombe letinjalo temkhoma tonkhe atilungile.

Kungenteka kutsi umtfombo loyinhloko wato tonkhe leto tintfo letikhohlisako letinetitfombe utawutfolakala emkhatsini wetintfo letindzala kakhulu letibatiwe temaHindu, emaGibhithe kanye nemaGriki. Kusukela ngaleto tikhatsi tekusungulwa kepha letingakacoceki lapho khona kumaphaneli emabhali emathempelini, etinyaweni tetitfombe, kanye nasetihlangwini, emamedali, tinkomishi, kanye netinhlavu temali, le dolphin beyidvwetjwa ngetikali te chain-armor njenga Saladin, kanye nenhloko lenesigcoko njenga St. George; kusukela ngaleso sikhatsi kunentfo lefanako yelayisensi leyabusa, hhayi kuphela etitfombeni letithandwa kakhulu temkhoma, kepha etinkhulumeni letinyenti tesayensi ngaye.

Manje, ngatotonkhe tintfo, lomfanekiso wasendvulo kakhulu lokhona nomakunjalo lotsi nguwemkhoma, utfolakala e cavern-pagoda ledvumile yase Elephanta, eNdiya. Ema Brahmins agcina kutsi kuletintfo letibatiwe leticishe tingapheli taleyo pagoda lengakhunjulwa, yonkhe imisebenti kanye nekuphishekela, yonkhe imisebenti lecabangakalako yemuntfu, bekucatshangelwa iminyaka ngaphambi kwekutsi noma nguyiphi yato ibe khona. Akumangalisi-ke, kutsi ngandlela tsite umsebenti wetfu lohloniphekile wekubamba imikhoma bekufanele ube khona lapho utfuntiwe khona. Lomkhoma we Hindoo lokukhulunywa ngawo, wenteka etikolweni lelehlukile lelubondza, lokhombisa kutsatfwa kwemtimba kwa Vishnu ngendlela ye leviathan, lefundvwako leyatiwa ngekutsi yi Matse Avatar. Kepha noma ngabe lesifanekiso sihhafu semuntfu nehhafu yemkhoma, ngako-ke kuphela kuniketa umsila walokugcina, nomakunjalo leso sigaba lesincane sakhe konkhe akukalungi. Ibukeka njengemsila lonciphako we anaconda, kunetintende letibanti tema flukes lamakhulu emkhoma sibili.

Kepha hamba uye eMagalari lamadzala, ubuke nyalo umfanekiso wemdwebi lomkhulu wemKhristu walenhlanti; ngobe akaphumeleli ncono kunemaHindu langaphambi kwazamcolo. Kusitfombe saGuido saPerseus akhulula Andromeda kulesilwane lesikhulu saselwandle noma umkhoma. Guido wayitfolaphi imodeli yesidalwa lesimangalisako njengaleso? Futsi Hogarth, ekudwebeni lendzawo lefanako ku “Perseus Descending” yakhe, akakwenti lokunye lokumhlophe lokuncono. Le corpulence lenkhulu yaleso silwane lesikhulu sase Hogarthian iyagucuka etulu, ayidvwebi intshi yinye yemanti. Ineluhlobo lwe howdah ngemuva kwayo, kanye nemlomo wayo lovulekile lonetinyombo lapho khona ema billows agijima khona, kungenteka kutsi kutsatfwe kuya eSangweni le Traitors’ leliholela kusuka eThames ngemanti kuya eTower. Bese, kunemikhoma ye Prodromus ye Scotch Sibbald lendzala, kanye nemkhoma wa Jonah, njengoba kukhonjisiwe ekushicilelweni kwemaBhayibheli lamadzala kanye nekusikwa kwema primer lamadzala. Kutawutsiwani ngaletintfo? Njengoba umkhoma we-book-binder ujikajika njengesicu sevini lesijikeleta sitoko sesikhonkwane lesehlako—njengobe sigcizelelwa futsi sigolide ngemuva nasemakhasini esihloko etincwadzi letinyenti letindzala naletinsha—loko kusidalwa lesihle kakhulu kepha lesiyinsumansumane, lesilingiswako, ngiyakutsatsa, kusuka kuletibalo letifanako te-vatique an. Noma nje ibitwa ngekutsi yi dolphin emhlabeni wonkhe, nomakunjalo ngibita le mfishi yekubopha tincwadzi ngekutsi ngumtamo wemkhoma; ngoba bekuhloswe kanjalo ngesikhatsi le-device icala kwetfulwa. Kwetfulwa ngumshicileli lomdzala waseNtaliyane ndzawonye cishe ngelikhulu lesi-15, ngesikhatsi seMvuselelo Yekufundza; futsi ngalawo malanga, futsi ngisho nasesikhatsini lesifanako, ema dolphin bekatsandvwa kutsi aluhlobo lwe Leviathan.

Ema vignettes kanye naletinye tintfo tekuhlotjiswa kwaletinye tincwadzi tasendvulo ngalesinye sikhatsi utohlangana nekutsintsa lokufuna kwati kakhulu kulomkhoma, lapho khona tonkhe tinhlobo tetimpompo, ema jets d'eau, titfombo letishisako kanye nekubandza, iSaratoga ne Baden-Baden, tifika tibhubhudza kusuka engcondvweni yakhe lengakhatsali. Ekhasini lesihloko le-edishini yekucala ye- “Intfutfuko Yekufundza” utawutfola imikhoma letsite lefuna kwati.

Kepha siyekele yonkhe lemizamo lengasiyo yebungcweti, asibuke leto titfombe te-leviathan letitsi tiyintfo lecaphelile, lekhombisa ngekwesayensi, ngulabo labatiko. Ekuhlanganisweni kwa Harris lokudzala kwekuhamba ngemikhumbi kunemapuleti lamanye emikhoma lekhishwe encwadzini yema Dutch yekuhamba ngemikhumbi, AD 1671, lenesihloko lesitsi “Luhambo Lwekugibela Imikhoma loluya eSpitzbergen emkhunjini Jonas eMkhomeni, Peter Peterson wase Friesland, inkhosi.” Kulelinye lalawo mapuleti imikhoma, njengema rafts lamakhulu etigodvo, imelelwa ilele emkhatsini we ice-isles, nemabhele lamhlophe agijima ngemuva kwawo laphilako. Kulelinye ipuleti, le blunder lemangalisako yentiwa ngekumelela umkhoma ngema flukes lamile.

Bese futsi, kune quarto lemangalisako, leyabhalwa ngulomunye Kaputeni Colnett, Kaputeni wePosi emphini yasemantini yemaNgisi, lenesihloko lesitsi “Luhambo lolujikeleta iCape Horn kuya eLwandle LwaseNingizimu, ngenhloso yekwandzisa iSpermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Kulencwadzi kuneluhlaka lolutsi “Sitfombe Semkhoma we- Physeter nobe we- Spermaceti, lotsatfwe ngesikali kulowo lowabulawa elugwini lwaseMexico, nga-August, 1793, futsi waphakanyiswa emkhunjini.” Angingabati kutsi kapteni watsatsa lesitfombe lesicinile kute kuzuze emasotja akhe aselwandle. Kusho kepha intfo yinye ngayo, ake ngitsi ineliso lelisebenta, ngekuya kwesikali lesihambisanako, kumkhoma wesilisa lokhule ngalokugcwele, bekungenta liso lalowo mkhoma libe lifasitelo le-bow lelingaba ngemamitha lasihlanu ngebudze. Ah, kapteni wami lonesibindzi, kungani ungasiniki Jona abuka ngalelo liso!

Futsi akusito tihlanganiso letinembeza kakhulu teMlandvo Wemvelo kute kuzuze labasha nalabamnene, tikhululekile ebubini lobufanako beliphutsa. Buka lowo msebenti lodvumile lotsi “Imvelo Lephilako YaGoldsmith.” Kuloluhlelo lolufinyetiwe lwaseLondon lwanga-1807, kunemapuleti latsiwa “umkhoma” kanye “ne- narwhale.” Angifisi kubonakala ngingakahle, kepha lomkhoma longabukeki kahle ubukeka kakhulu njengengulube lejutjiwe; futsi, mayelana ne narwhale, kuyibona kancane kwanele kumangalisa umuntfu, kutsi kulelikhulu lemashumi lasiphohlongo le hippogriff lenjalo ingatsatfwa ngesandla njengentfo sibili kunobe ngumuphi ummango lohlakaniphile webafana besikolo.

Kwabese kutsi, futsi, nga 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, sati lesikhulu semvelo, washicilela incwadzi yesayensi lehlelekile yemkhoma, lapho khona kunetitfombe letinyenti tetinhlobo letehlukene te Leviathan. Konkhe loku akusiko nje lokungakalungi, kepha sitfombe se Mysticetus noma iGreenland whale (lokusho kutsi, iRight whale), ngisho na Scoresby, indvodza lenesipiliyoni lesidze njengekutsintsa lolohlobo, umemetela kutsi ayinalo umlingani wayo emvelweni.

Kepha kubekwa kwe cap-sheaf kuyo yonkhe lebhizinisi lephutsako bekubekelwe Frederick Cuvier wetesayensi, umnakabo Baron lodvumile. Nga 1836, washicilela Umlandvo Wemvelo Wemikhoma, lapho aniketa khona loko lakubita ngekutsi sitfombe se Sperm Whale. Ngaphambi kwekukhombisa leso sitfombe kunobe nguyiphi iNantucketer, bekuncono kutsi unikete sifinyeto sakho sekubuyela emuva kusuka eNantucket. Ngeligama linye, Umkhoma Wesidvodza waFrederick Cuvier akusiyo iMkhoma Yesidvodza, kepha sikwashi. Kuliciniso kutsi akazange abe nenzuzo yeluhambo lwekugibela imikhoma (emadvodza lanjalo akavami kuba nawo), kodvwa wasitsatsaphi leso sitfombe, ngubani longatjela? Mhlawumbe wayitfola njengemlandzeli wakhe wetesayensi emkhakheni munye, Desmarest, watfola lenye yekukhipha sisu kwakhe lokuliciniso; lokusho kutsi, kusuka emdvwebeni waseShayina. Futsi nguluphi luhlobe lwebafana labaphilako labanepeniseli labo baseShayina, tinkomishi letinyenti te-queer kanye netitja tiyatisa.

Mayelana nemikhoma yebapendi betimphawu lebonwa emigwacweni lelenga etitolo tebatsengisi be oyili, kutawutsiwani ngayo? Ngalokuvamile nguRichard III. imikhoma, lenema humps e dromedary, futsi lehlasela kakhulu; kudla kwasekuseni kuma-tart lamatsatfu nobe lamane ematilosi, lokusho tikebhe temikhoma letigcwele bantfu labahamba ngemikhumbi: kukhubateka kwabo kubhubha etilwandle letinengati kanye nepende leluhlata sasibhakabhaka.

Kepha lamaphutsa lamanyenti ekuveteni umkhoma akumangalisi kakhulu phela. Cabanga! Liningi lemidvwebo yesayensi itsatfwe kuletimfishi letibhajwe; futsi leti ticishe tibe nguletilungile njengemdvwebo wemkhumbi lowephukile, lonemuva lowephukile, bekungamelela kahle lesilwane lesihloniphekile sona ngekwaso kuko konkhe kutigcabha kwaso lokungakapheli kwe hull kanye ne spars. Noma tindlovu time ngebudze bato lobugcwele, Leviathan lophilako akakaze antanta kahle ngesitfombe sakhe. Lomkhoma lophilako, ngebukhosi bakhe lobugcwele nekubaluleka kwawo, ubonakala kuphela elwandle emantini langacondzakali; futsi kuntanta incenye lenkhulu yakhe ayibonakali, njengemkhumbi wemphi losunguliwe; futsi kuleso sici kuyintfo lengeke yenteke phakadze kutsi umuntfu lofako amphakamisele emoyeni ngekwemtimba, kute alondvolote konkhe kuvuvuka kwakhe lokunemandla kanye nekugucuka kwakhe. Futsi, hhayi kutsi ngikhulume ngemehluko locatjangwa kakhulu we contour emkhatsini wemkhoma lomncane lomunyisako kanye ne Leviathan yase Platonian lekhule ngalokugcwele; nomakunjalo, ngisho nasendzabeni yalenye yaleyo mikhoma lencane lemunyisako lephakanyiselwe emkhunjini, leyo-ke indlela yakhe lemangalisako, lefana ne-eel, le-limbered, leyehlukahlukene, kutsi inkhulumo yakhe lecondzile sathane yena matfupha akazange akhone kuyibamba.

Kepha kungenteka kutsi kucatshangelwe, kutsi kusuka ematsambo langcunu emkhoma lobhajwe, tintfo letinembile tingatfolakala letitsintsa simo sakhe seliciniso. Nakancane. Ngobe kungulenye yetintfo letimangalisako ngale Leviathan, kutsi umgogodla wakhe uniketa umcondvo lomncane kakhulu ngesimo sakhe jikelele. Noma nje umgogodla waJeremy Bentham, lolengako ku candelabra emtapweni wemabhuku walomunye webabulali bakhe, udlulisela kahle umcondvo wemnumzane lomdzala we-utilitarian lone-burly-browed, nato tonkhe letinye timphawu taJeremy letiholako; nobe kunjalo kute intfo yaloluhlobo lebeyingaphetfwa kunobe ngumaphi ematsambo lahlanganisiwe e-leviathan. Empeleni, njengoba umtingeli lomkhulu atsi, umgogodla nje wemkhoma utfwala buhlobo lobufanako nesilwane lesitjalwe ngalokuphelele futsi lesinemaphepha njengoba lesinambuzane senta ku chrysalis leyimbonya ngendlela leyindingilizi. Loku lokumangalisako kubonakala ngendlela lemangalisako enhloko, njengobe kulenye incenye yalencadzi kutawukhonjiswa ngendlela lemangalisako. Iphindze ikhonjiswe ngekulangatelelela kakhulu ku-fin leseceleni, ematsambo ayo acishe aphendvule ngco ematsambo esandla semuntfu, asuse sitfupha kuphela. Le fin ineminwe lemine levamile yematsambo, inkomba, lesemkhatsini, indandatho, kanye nemunwe lomncane. Kepha konkhe loku kuhlala unomphela esimbonyweni sayo lesinenyama, njengeminwe yemuntfu esimbonyweni lesentiwe. Stubb lohlekisanako watsi ngalelinye lilanga: “Noma ngabe ngaletinye tikhatsi lomkhoma ungasikhonta ngekunganaki kangakanani, angeke kushiwo mbamba kutsi uyasiphatsa ngaphandle kwema- mittens.”

Ngato tonkhe letizatfu, ke, noma ngabe nguyiphi indlela longayibuka ngayo, kumele udzingeke uphetse ngekutsi iLeviathan lenkhulu nguleso sidalwa sinye emhlabeni lekumele sihlale singakapendwa kuze kube sekugcineni. Kuliciniso kutsi lesinye sitfombe singase sishaye leliphawu edvute kakhulu kunalesinye, kodvwa kute lesingasishaya ngalokucondzile. Ngako kute indlela yasemhlabeni yekutfola kahle kutsi lomkhoma ubukeka njani mbamba. Futsi indlela kuphela lapho ungatfola khona ngisho umcondvo lobeketelelekako we contour yakhe lephilako, ngekuya whaling wena; kodvwa ngekwenta njalo, awusiyo ingoti lencane yekuba sitofu saphakadze futsi ucwiliswe nguye. Ngako-ke, kubonakala kimi kutsi bekuncono ungasheshi kakhulu ekufuneni kwakho kwati kutsintsa leLeviathan.





SEHLUKO 56. Setitfombe Letingasiwo Emaphutsa Emikhoma, Netitfombe Teliciniso Tetindzawo Tekubulala Imikhoma.

Ngekuphatselene netitfombe letimbi kakhulu temikhoma, ngilingeka kakhulu lapha kutsi ngingene kuleto tindzaba letimbi kakhulu ngato letitawutfolakala etincwadzini letitsite, kokubili tasendvulo natalamuhla, ikakhulukati ku Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, njll Kepha ngidlula leyondzaba.

Ngiyati ngetinhlaka letine kuphela letishicilelwe te-Sperm Whale lenkhulu; Colnett, Huggins, Frederick Cuvier kanye naBeale. Esahlukweni lesengcile kukhulunywe ngabo Colnett naCuvier. Huggins's ncono kakhulu kunabo; kepha, ngekutsi kube nematfuba lamakhulu, iBeale nguyona lencono kakhulu. Yonkhe imidvwebo yaBeale yalomkhoma mihle, ngaphandle kwesibalo lesisemkhatsini esitfombeni semikhoma lemitsatfu ngesimo sengcondvo lesehlukahlukene, levala sehluko sakhe sesibili. I-frontispiece yakhe, tikebhe letihlasela i-Sperm Whales, noma kungabateki kutsi tibalwe kujabulisa kungabateka kwebantfu kwalamanye emadvodza e-parlor, kulungile ngendlela lenconywako futsi kufana nekuphila ngemphumela wayo jikelele. Leminye imidvwebo ye Sperm Whale ku J. Ross Browne ilungile kahle ngekucondzisa; kodvwa abhalwe ngendlela lebuhlungu. Nobe kunjalo, loko akusilo liphutsa lakhe.

Kule Whale Lefanele, titfombe letinhle kakhulu letiluhlaka tise Scoresby; kodvwa tidvwetjwe ngesikali lesincane kakhulu kute tidlulisele umbono lofiselekako. Unesitfombe sinye kuphela setindzawo tekubulala imikhoma, futsi loku kushoda lokudzabukisako, ngoba ngetitfombe letinjalo kuphela, uma nhlobo kwentiwe kahle, kutsi ungatfola noma yini lefana nemcondvo weliciniso wemkhoma lophilako njengoba ubonakala batingeli bakhe labaphilako.

Kepha, kutsatfwa ngako konkhe, ngekukhashane lokuhle kakhulu, noma ngabe kuleminye imininingwane hhayi lelungile kakhulu, kwetfulwa kwemikhoma kanye netindzawo tekubulala imikhoma kutsi titfolakale noma ngukuphi, kukhona lokubhalwe phansi lokubili lokukhulu kwema French, lokwentiwe kahle, futsi lokutsatfwe emidwebeni ngu Garnery munye. Ngekulandzelana, bamele kuhlaselwa kwe Sperm kanye ne Right Whale. Kulokubhalwe kwekucala iSperm Whale lehloniphekile ikhonjiswa ngebukhosi lobugcwele bemandla, ivele ivuke ngaphansi kwesikebhe kusuka ekujuleni kwelwandle, futsi ithwele etulu emoyeni ngemuva kwayo imfucumfucu leyesabekako yemapulangwe laphekwe esitofini. I-prow yalesikebhe ayiphukile kancane, futsi idvonswa nje ilinganisela emgogodleni walesilwane lesikhulu; futsi umile kuleyo prow, ngaleso sikhatsi sinye lesingabalwa, ubona umgwedli, hhafu ambonywe ngumlomo lobilako lovutsiwe wemkhoma, futsi asesenzweni sekugcuma, kube shengatsi uvela emhosheni. Sento sayo yonkhe intfo sihle ngendlela lemangalisako futsi siliciniso. Le line-tub lengakagcwali ngesigamu intanta elwandle lolumhlophe; tigodvo tetigodvo tema-harpoon lacitsekile tiboba ngendlela lecondzile kuwo; tinhloko telicembu lekubhukusha tisakateke ngalomkhoma ngetindlela letihlukile tekuveta kwesaba; ngesikhatsi ebangeni lelimnyama lelinesiphepho umkhumbi uthwala phansi kulendzawo. Liphutsa lelikhulu lingatfolakala ngemininingwane ye-anatomical yalo mkhoma, kepha akuvumele loko kudlule; njengobe, ngekuphila kwami, bengingakhoni kudvweba lenye lenhle kangaka.

Kulokubhalwe kwesibili, lesikebhe sisemsebentini wekudvweba eceleni kwe barnacled flank ye Right Whale lenkhulu legijimako, legicita lukhula lwayo lolumnyama elwandle njengalelinye lidvwala le mossy-slide kusuka emaweni ase Patagonia. Emajethi akhe amile, agcwele, futsi amnyama njengemlotsa; ngako-ke kusuka ekugcwaleni kwentfutfu eshimela, ungacabanga kutsi kufanele kube nesidlo sakusihlwa lesinesibindzi lesiphekwa emathunjini lamakhulu langephansi. Tinkhukhu taselwandle tikhotsa tinkala letincane, i-shell-fish, kanye nalamanye emaswidi elwandle kanye ne-maccaroni, le Right Whale ngalesinye sikhatsi iphatsa ngemuva kwayo lokunesifo. Futsi sonkhe lesikhatsi le leviathan lenetindzebe letindze igijima ekujuleni, ishiya emathani ema curds lamhlophe lanesiphitsiphitsi ekuvukeni kwayo, futsi yenta kutsi lesikebhe lesincane sinyakate etindzaweni letivuvukile njengesikebhe lesibanjwe edvute nemasondvo ekugwedla emkhunjini wekuhamba ngemphunga elwandle. Ngaleyo ndlela, lokusembili konkhe kusiphitsiphitsi lesivutsako; kepha ngemuva, ngekungafani lokubabatekako kwebuciko, lizinga lelingilazi lelwandle loluthulisiwe, imikhumbi lelahlako lengakafakwa sitashi yemkhumbi longenamandla, kanye nebuningi lobungasebenti bemkhoma lofile, inqaba lencotjiwe, nemjeka wekubanjwa lolenga ngebuvila ku whale-pole lefakwe emgodzini wakhe we spout.

Ngubani Garnery umdwebi, noma bekanguye, angati. Kepha imphilo yami ngayo bekakhuluma ngesihloko sakhe, noma-ke bekafundziswa ngendlela lemangalisako ngulomunye umkhoma lonelwati. Likhono lemvelo lema French lekutsatsa buhle betintfo kubonakala ngatsi lifakazelwa ngendlela lemangalisako kutsi ngumiphi imidvwebo kanye nekubaza labanako kwetindzawo tabo tekubulala imikhoma. Ngako kuyavela kutsi kubonakale, kutsi noma munye nabo bonkhe bamemetela kutsi yintfo lenkhulu lephilako elwandle, kepha bambalwa kakhulu kubo labanemibono kepha lengakacaci kakhulu mayelana nesimo sayo seliciniso kanye nesimo sayo; nomakunjalo, bakholelwa kutsi kuniketa umkhoma wesilisa kudla kwakhe kuphela. Ngoba noma letinye tinhlobo temikhoma titfola kudla kwato ngetulu kwemanti, futsi tingabonwa ngumuntfu ngesento sekudla, le spermaceti whale itfola kudla kwayo konkhe etindzaweni letingatiwa ngaphansi kwemhlabatsi; futsi kuphela ngekuphetsa kutsi noma ngubani angasho kutsi yini, ngco, kutsi kudla kuhlanganisa. Ngalesinye sikhatsi, uma alandzelwa edvute, utawukhipha loko lokufanele kube yimikhono leyehlukene ye squid; labanye babo ngaleyo ndlela bakhombisa ngetulu kwemamitha langemashumi lamabili nemashumi lamatsatfu ngebudze. Bacabanga kutsi lesilwane lesikhulu lebesikhona kuletingalo ngalokuvamile sinamatsela ngabo embhedzeni welwandle; futsi nekutsi lomkhoma wesilisa, ngekungafani naletinye tinhlobo, uniketwa ematinyo kute uhlasele futsi uwudzabule.

Kubonakala ngatsi kunesizatfu sekucabanga kutsi iKraken lenkhulu ya Bishop Pontoppodan ingagcina itisombulule ibe yi Squid. Indlela uMbhishobhi lachaza ngayo, njengekushintjashintja kuvuka nekucwila, naletinye tintfo latilandzisako, kuko konkhe loku lokubili kuyahambisana. Kepha abatement kakhulu kuyadingeka mayelana bulk Amazing uyabela yona.

Ngalabanye bososayensi bemvelo labave ngalokungakacaci emahemuhemu alesilwane lesiyimfihlakalo, lapha kukhulunywa ngaso, sifakwe emkhatsini wesigaba se cuttle-fish, lapho, ngempela, ngetindlela letitsite tangephandle bekungabonakala ngatsi kungekwakhe, kepha njenge Anak yesive kuphela.





SEHLUKO 60. Umgca.

Ngekubhekisa endzaweni yekubulawa kwemikhoma letawuchazwa masinyane, kanye nekuvisisa kancono tonkhe tindzawo letifanako kuletinye tindzawo letetfulwa, nginalo lapha kukhuluma ngemlingo, ngalesinye sikhatsi lowesabekako we whale-line.

Lomugca lowasetjentiswa ekucaleni ekudobeni bewuyi hemp lencono kakhulu, lehwamuke kancane ngetitini, ingagcwaliswa ngayo, njengasendzabeni yetintsambo letivamile; ngoba ngesikhatsi liti, njengoba lisetjentiswa ngalokujwayelekile, lenta le hemp ibe nemandla kakhulu kumenzi wentsambo, futsi iphindze yente lentsambo yona ngekwayo ibe lula kakhulu kumkhumbi kwentela kusetjentiswa kwemkhumbi lovamile; nomakunjalo, hhayi kuphela kutsi linani lelijwayelekile belingacinisa kakhulu umgca wemkhoma wekugoca lokusedvute lapho kumele kubekwe khona; kepha njengoba bantfu labaningi labahamba ngetilwandle bacala kufundza, litiyela ngalokujwayelekile alikwengeti nganoma nguyiphi indlela ekuciniseni kwentsambo noma emandla, noma ngabe ingayinika kangakanani kuhlanganiswa kanye nekucwebetela.

Eminyakeni leyengcile lentsambo ye Manilla seyibe sekudobeni kwase Melika cishe yatsatsa sikhundla se hemp njengentfo ye whale-lines; ngoba, noma ingacini njenge hemp, icinile, futsi ithambile kakhulu futsi i-elastic; futsi ngitawufaka (njengoba kune æsthetics kutotonkhe tintfo), muhle kakhulu futsi uba sesikebheni, kune hemp. iHemp ngumuntfu lomnyama, lomnyama, luhlobo lwemaNdiya; kepha Manilla ufana neCircassia lenetinwele letigolide kutsi uyibone.

Lomgca wemkhoma uyincenye lemibili kuphela kuletintsatfu te-intshi ngebukhulu. Nawuyibona kwekucala, angeke ucabange kutsi icinile kangaka njengobe injalo mbamba. Ngekulinga imicu yayo yinye nemashumi lasihlanu ngamunye utawumisa sisindvo sema pounds lalikhulu nemashumi lamabili; ngako-ke yonkhe lentambo itawutfwala umfutfo locishe ulingana nemathani lamatsatfu. Ngebudze, le sperm whale-line levamile ilinganisa intfo lengetulu kwema fathoms langemakhulu lamabili. Ngasemuva kwesikebhe kugocotwa ngendlela lejikeletako ebhafini, hhayi njenge worm-pipe ye still nomakunjalo, kepha kwentela kwakha sicumbi sinye lesiyindingilizi, lesimise njengashizi se “ticumbi” letiminyene, noma tincenye tekujikeleta letisemkhatsini, ngaphandle kwemgodzi kepha “inhlitiyo,” ye cheese lencane lemile le tube. Njengoba kuhlangana lokuncane noma kugoca ku coiling kungaba, ngekugijima kuphume, ngekungaphutsi kutsatse umkhono wemuntfu, umlente, noma umtimba wonkhe, kucaphela lokukhulu kusetjentiswa ekubekeni umugca ebhafini layo. Labanye ba harpooneers batawudla cishe ekuseni lonkhe kulebhizinisi, batfwale umugca etulu etulu bese bayawukhipha phansi ngekusebentisa ibhulokhi babheke ebhafini, ngako njengesento sekugoca kwentela kuwukhulula kutotonkhe tintfo letingaba khona tekushwaphana kanye nekujika.

Etikebheni temaNgisi kusetjentiswa emabhabhu lamabili esikhundleni salelinye; umugca lofanako uchubeka ugocotwa kuwo omabili emabhabhu. Kukhona inzuzo letsite kuloku; ngoba lama-twin-tubs abe mancane kakhulu angena kalula esikebheni, futsi akasicindzeteli kakhulu; lapho khona, le tub yase Melika, lecishe ibe ngemamitha lamatsatfu ngebubanti kanye nekujula lokulinganako, yenta umtfwalo lomkhulu kakhulu we craft lenemapulangwe ayo kepha linye hhafu we intshi ngebukhulu; ngoba phansi kwe whale-boat kufana ne ice lebucayi, letawutfwala sisindvo lesikhulu lesisakateke, kepha hhayi kakhulu lesigcizelele. Uma lesembonyo se canvas lesipendiwe sishaywa tandla kule line-tub yase Melika, lesikebhe sibukeka shengatsi siyadvonsa ngekhekhe lelikhulu lemshado lelimangalisako lekwetfula imikhoma.

Totimbili letiphetfo temugca tiyavetwa; umkhawulo longephansi lophelela nge eye-splice noma loop lekhuphuka kusuka phansi ibhekene neluhlangotsi lwe tub, bese ilenga ngetulu kwemphetfo wayo ihlukaniswe ngalokuphelele kuko konkhe. Leli lungiselelo lekuphela lokuphansi liyadzingeka kuma-akhawunti lamabili. Kwekucala: Kute kube lula kubopha kuwo umugca lowengetiwe lovela esikebheni lesingumakhelwane, uma kwenteka lomkhoma loshaywe kufanele uvakale kujule kakhulu kangangekutsi usongele kutsatsa wonkhe umugca lobewunamatsele ekucaleni kule harpoon. Kuletikhatsi, lomkhoma vele uyashintjwa njenge mug we ale, njengobe bekungatsiwa, kusuka kulesinye sikebhe kuya kulesinye; noma sikebhe sekucala sihlale sizulazula esandleni kute sisite umfati waso. Kwesibili: Lelilungiselelo liyadzingeka kakhulu kute kuphephe bantfu bonkhe; ngoba kube umkhawulo longephansi welayini nganoma nguyiphi indlela bewunamatsele esikebheni, futsi kube lomkhoma ngaleso sikhatsi bewutawugijima lomugca uphume kuze kube sekugcineni cishe ngemzuzu munye, lobhemako njengoba enta ngalesinye sikhatsi, bekangame lapho, ngoba lesikebhe lesilahliwe besitawudvonswa phansi ngemuva kwakhe ngekungaphutsi siye ekujuleni kwelwandle; futsi kuleso simo kute ummemeti welidolobha lobekangaphindze amtfole.

Ngaphambi kwehlisa sikebhe kwentela kujaha, umkhawulo longetulu welayini utsatfwa ngemuva kusuka ebhafini, bese udlula ujikeleta inhloko yetigodvo lapho, uphindze utsatfwe phambili bonkhe budze besikebhe, uphumule ngekuphambana etulu kwe loom noma sibambo se oar yawo wonkhe umuntfu, kwentela kutsi igijime sibhekane nesihlakala sakhe ekugwedleni; futsi futsi kudlula emkhatsini wemadvodza, njengoba ashintjashintja ahlala kuma gunwales laphambene, kuya kuma chocks laholako noma ema grooves ku prow lecijile kakhulu yesikebhe, lapho khona iphini yetigodvo noma skewer lelinganako ne quill levamile, ivimbela kutsi ingaphumi. Kusuka kuma chocks ilenga nge festoon lencane ngetulu kweminsalo, bese idluliswa ngekhatsi kwesikebhe futsi; futsi ema fathoms langaba lishumi noma lamashumi lamabili (lebitwa ngekutsi yi box-line) igocotwa ngetulu kwelibhokisi kuma bows, iyachubeka nendlela yayo kuya e gunwale solo kancane ngemuva, bese inamatsele ku short-warp—lentsambo lehlanganiswa ngekushesha ne harpoon; kepha ngaphambi kwaloko kuxhumana, le-short-warp idlula ngetimfihlakalo letehlukahlukene letidziniwe kakhulu kutsi tingachazwa ngemininingwane.

Ngaleyo ndlela lomgca wemkhoma ugoca sonkhe lesikebhe ngemakhoyili aso layinkimbinkimbi, ujike futsi ujikelete cishe kuto tonkhe tindlela. Bonkhe labagwedlako bayabandzakanyeka ekugucukeni kwayo lokuyingoti; ngako-ke emehlweni lanemahloni emuntfu wasendzaweni, babonakala njengema-juggler aseNdiya, netinyoka letibulalako kakhulu letihlobisa tincenye tabo ngendlela yekudlala. Futsi kute indvodzana yemfati lofako, kwekucala ngca, ingahlala emkhatsini waleto tintfo letiyinkimbinkimbi te hempen, futsi ngesikhatsi acindzetela ngako konkhe lokusemandleni akhe ekugwedleni, amcabangele kutsi nganoma ngusiphi sikhatsi lesingatiwa le harpoon ingashaywa, futsi konkhe lokugucuka lokusabisako kubekwe emdlalweni njengembane lenendandatho; angeke abe sesimeni lesinjalo ngaphandle kwekuvevetela lokwenta wona kanye umgogodla losematsambo akhe uvevetele kuye njengejeli lenyakatiswako. Nobe kunjalo umkhuba—intfo lemangalisako! yini lengeke ifezwe ngumkhuba?—Gayer sallies, kujabula lokunyenti, emahlaya lancono, kanye ne repartees lekhanyako, awuzange sewukuve ngetulu kwe mahogany yakho, kunaloko lotawukuva ngetulu kwemsedari lomhlophe lohhafu we-intshi wesikebhe semikhoma, uma ulengiswa kanjalo etintsambo tebantfu labalengisako; futsi, njengema burghers lasitfupha ase Calais ngaphambi kweNkhosi Edward, lamadvodza lasitfupha lakha licembu advonsa emihlatsini yekufa, nge halter lejikeleta yonkhe intsamo, njengoba ungasho.

Mhlawumbe umcabango lomncane kakhulu nyalo utakusita kutsi ulandzise ngaleto tinhlekelele letiphindzaphindvwako tekubulawa kwemikhoma—letinye letimbalwa tato tibhalwe phansi ngekunganaki—lendvodza nobe leyondvodza lekhishwa esikebheni ngelayini, futsi yalahleka. Ngobe, uma umugca ugijima uphuma, kuhlala ke esikebheni, kufana nekuhlala emkhatsini wekuvunguta lokunyenti kwenjini-yemfutfo ngekudlala ngalokugcwele, lapho yonkhe imisebe lendizako, neshaft, nelisondvo, kukudla. Kubi kakhulu; ngobe angeke uhlale unganyakati enhlitiyweni yaletingoti, ngobe sikebhe siyanyakata njengembhedze, futsi ubekwe ngakulolunye luhlangotsi, ngaphandle kwesecwayiso lesincane; futsi kuphela ngekutilungisa lokutsite kwekuntanta kanye nekutsandza kanye nesento, ungaphunyuka kwentiwa Mazeppa we, bese ubaleka nalapho khona lilanga lelibona konkhe lona ngekwalo lingaze likubhobote.

Kuphindze futsi: njengoba kuthula lokujulile lokubonakala kuphela kucala futsi kuphrofetha siphepho, mhlawumbe kwesabeka kakhulu kunesiphepho sona; ngobe, mbamba, kuthula kumane nje kugoca nemvelopho yesiphepho; futsi icuketse yona, njengoba lesibhamu lesibonakala singenabungoti siphetse imphuphu lebulalako, nelibhola, kanye nekuqhunyiswa; ngako-ke kuphumula lokunemusa kwelayini, njengoba kuthule kukhuluma ngebagwedli ngaphambi kwekutsi kuletfwe emdlalweni mbamba—le yintfo lethwele kwesaba kweliciniso lokunyenti kunanoma ngusiphi lesinye sici salendzaba leyingoti. Kodvwa kungani usho lokunyenti? Onkhe emadvodza ahlala ahlanganiswe ngemigca yemikhoma. Bonkhe batalwa banema- halter etintsanyeni tabo; kodvwa kulapho kuphela lapho babanjwe ekujika lokusheshako, lokungakalindzeleki kwekufa, lapho bantfu labafako babona khona bungoti bekuphila lobuthulile, lobungabonakali, lobuhlala bukhona. Futsi nangabe usifilosofi, noma uhleti esikebheni semikhoma, angeke enhlitiyweni utive wesaba kakhulu, kunaloko uma uhleti embikwemlilo wakho wakusihlwa une-poker, hhayi i-harpoon, eceleni kwakho.
