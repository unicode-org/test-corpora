SEHLUKO 32. I-Cetology.

Kwekucala: Simo lesingaciniseki, lesingahlaliseki salesayensi ye Cetology sise vestibule lefakazelwa liciniso, kutsi kuletinye tindzawo kusahlala kuliphuzu lelingaphikiswa kutsi umkhoma uyinhlanti yini. Encwadzini yakhe letsi System of Nature, AD 1776, Linnæus umemetela kutsi, “Ngaloku ngihlukanisa imikhoma netimfishi.” Kepha ngekwati kwami, ngiyati kutsi phansi ngemnyaka wa 1850, boshaka kanye ne shad, alewives kanye ne herring, ngekumelene nemyalo wa Linnæus locacile, bekusolo kutfolakala kwehlukanisa kuba netilwandle letifanako ne Leviathan.

Tizatfu Linnæus labengasho kutsi bekacoshe ngato lemikhoma emantini, usho loku lokulandzelako: “Ngenca yenhlitiyo yayo lefutfumele lenemehlo lamabili, emaphaphu ayo, emajwabu ayo lahambako, tindlebe tawo letinembobo, i- penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem,” futsi ekugcineni, “ex merito lege naturé jure.” Ngakwetfula konkhe loku kubangani bami Simeon Macey na Charley Coffin, base Nantucket, bobabili bebahamba nami eluhambeni lolutsite, futsi bahlangana ngembono wekutsi tizatfu letibekiwe betingakeneli ngalokuphelele. Charley waveta ngendlela lehlambalatako kutsi bebayi-humbug.

Kwatiwe kutsi, ngiyekela yonkhe imphikiswano, ngitsatsa sizatfu lesihle sasendvulo sekutsi lomkhoma uyimfishi, futsi ngibita Jona longcwele kutsi angisekele. Lentfo lesisekelo yahlala, liphuzu lelilandzelako kutsi, nguyiphi inhlonipho lengekhatsi umkhoma wehlukile kuletinye timfishi. Ngetulu, Linnæus ukunikete leto tintfo. Kepha kafishane, nguleti: emaphaphu nengati lefutfumele; kantsi, tonkhe letinye timfishi atinamaphaphu futsi tinengati lebandzako.

Lokulandzelako: sitawuchaza njani lomkhoma, ngetintfo takhe letisobala tangephandle, ngako-ke ngendlela lebonakalako kutsi simbhale ngaso sonkhe sikhatsi lesitako? Ngako-ke, nangabe sifishane, umkhoma yinhlanti lekhipha umlomo lenemsila lovundlile . Lapho unaye. Noma ngabe inkontileka kangakanani, leyo ncazelo ngumphumela wekuzindla lokwengetiwe. I-walrus ikhipha umlomo njengemkhoma, kepha i-walrus akusiyo imfishi, ngoba ihlala emantini. Kepha ligama lekugcina lenchazelo lisacinile kakhulu, njengobe lihambisana nekucala. Cishe noma ngubani kumele kutsi wabona kutsi tonkhe timfishi letijwayelekile kubantfu bemhlaba atinawo umsila losicaba, kepha umsila lomile, noma lokhuphuka naphansi. Noma, emkhatsini wetinhlanti letiphumako umsila, noma ungaba ngendlela lefanako, ungagucuki utsatsa indzawo levundlile.

Ngencazelo lengenhla yekutsi umkhoma uyini, angikhiphi neze ebuzalwaneni be leviathanic noma ngusiphi silwane saselwandle kuze kube ngunyalo lesikhonjwe nemkhoma ngebantfu base Nantucketers labanelwati loluhle kakhulu; noma, ngakulolunye luhlangotsi, uhlanganise nayo noma nguyiphi imfishi lebeyitsatfwa njengebantfu bekuhamba kuze kube ngunyalo.* Ngako-ke, tonkhe timfishi letincane, letikhipha umlomo, naletinemsila lovundlile kumele tifakwe kule ground-plan ye Cetology. Nyalo-ke, kufika kwehlukana lokukhulu kwawo wonkhe umkhoma.

*Ngiyati kutsi kuze kube ngunyalo, letimfishi letinesitayela se Lamatins kanye ne Dugongs (Ingulube-imfishi kanye ne Sow-fish yemabhokisi e Nantucket) tifakwa tati temvelo letiningi emkhatsini wemikhoma. Kepha njengoba letinhlanti-tengulube tiyintfo lenemsindvo, leyedzelelekako, kakhulu tibhaca emilonyeni yemifula, futsi tidla hay lemanti, futsi ikakhulu njengoba tingakhiphi umlomo, ngiyakuphika bufakazi bato njengemikhoma; futsi babanikete emapasipoti abo kutsi bayekele uMbuso weCetology.

Kwekucala: Ngekuya kwebukhulu ngihlukanisa imikhoma ibe TINCWADZI letintsatfu letiyinhloko (letihlukaniswa tibe TENHLUKO), futsi leti titawuticondza tonkhe, letincane naletinkhulu.

EMAFOLIYO. Emkhatsini waletintfo I lapha ngifaka letehluko letilandzelako:—I. Umkhoma Wesidvodza ; II. i- Whale Lesekudla ; III. i- Whale Lene- Fin ; IV. i- Whale lenemhlane we Hump ; V. Umkhoma Lonemuva Lonesikele ; VI. umkhoma Lophansi Wesulphur .

INCWADZI I. ( Folio ), SEHLUKO I. ( Umkhoma Wesilisa ).—Lomkhoma, emkhatsini wemaNgisi asendvulo lowatiwa ngalokungakacaci ngekutsi ngumkhoma we-Trumpa, kanye nemkhoma we-Physeter, kanye nemkhoma we-Anvil Headed, nguye lokhona nyalo weCachalot wemaFulentshi, kanye ne-Pottsfich yema-Macroce emaJalimane, kanye ne-Pottsfich yema-Macroce emaJalimane. Yena, ngaphandle kwekungabata, nguye lohlala emhlabeni wonkhe; leyesabisa kakhulu kuyo yonkhe imikhoma kutsi uhlangabetane nayo; lephakeme kakhulu ngesici; futsi kwekugcina, ngekude kakhulu lokubaluleke kakhulu kutekutsengiselana; yena kuba nguye kuphela sidalwa lapho leyo ntfo lebalulekile, i-spermaceti, itfolakala khona. Tonkhe tintfo takhe letingakavami, kuletinye tindzawo letinyenti, titawukhuliswa. Kuyinto ikakhulukati ngegama lakhe kutsi manje kufanele ngikwente. Uma kucatshangelwa ngekwelulwimi, akunangcondvo. Emakhulwini eminyaka latsite leyengcile, ngesikhatsi umkhoma we Sperm bewucishe ungatiwa ngalokuphelele ngebuntfu bakhe lobufanele, futsi ngesikhatsi oyili wakhe atfolakala ngengoti kuphela kulemfishi lebhajwe; ngalawo malanga i-spermaceti, bekungabonakala shengatsi bekufanele kutsi isuselwa esidalweni lesifanana naleso lebesatiwa ngaleso sikhatsi eNgilandi ngekutsi yi-Greenland nobe i-Right Whale. Kwaba ngumcondvo futsi, kutsi le spermaceti lefanako bekunguleyo ndlela yekuhlekisa lesheshisako ye Greenland Whale leyo syllable yekucala yeligama leveta ngalokucondzile. Ngaleto tikhatsi, futsi, ispermaceti beyishoda kakhulu, ingasetjentiswa kukhanya, kepha njengemafutsa kanye nemitsi kuphela. Bekufanele kutsi kutfolakale kuphela kubatsengisi bemitsi njengobe kulamalanga utsenga i-ounce ye rhubarb. Ngesikhatsi, njengoba ngicabanga, ngekuhamba kwesikhatsi, simo seliciniso se spermaceti satiwa, ligama layo lekucala lalisagcinwa batsengisi; akungabateki kutsi kutfutfukise linani layo ngemcondvo lobaluleke ngendlela lemangalisako wekushoda kwayo. Futsi ngako leligama kumele kutsi ekugcineni lifike laniketwa lomkhoma lapho khona le spermaceti yasuselwa khona mbamba.

INCWADZI I. ( Liphepha ), SEHLUKO II. ( Right Whale ).—Ngalenye indlela lena nguyona lehlonishwa kakhulu kulama leviathan, ngoba nguyona yekucala letingelwa njalo ngumuntfu. Ikhicita lesihloko lesatiwa kakhulu ngekutsi yi whalebone noma baleen; kanye ne oyili leyatiwa kakhulu ngekutsi “emafutsa emikhoma,” lokuyintfo lephansi kutekutsengiselana. Emkhatsini webadobi, ubekwa ngekungakhetsi ngato tonkhe leticu letilandzelako: Umkhoma; umkhoma waseGreenland; umkhoma Lomnyama; umkhoma Lomkhulu; umkhoma Weliciniso; umkhoma Lofanele. Kukhona sivumelwano sekungacaci mayelana nebunjalo betinhlobo ngaleyondlela multitudinously kubhajatiswa. Yini-ke umkhoma, lengiwufaka eluhlobo lwesibili lwemaFolios ami? Ngu Mysticetus Lomkhulu wetifundziswa temvelo temaNgisi; iGreenland Whale yebantfu labadlala imikhoma baseNgilandi; iBaleine Ordinaire yebantfu baseFrance labadla imikhoma; i-Walfish lekhulako yaseSweden. Ngumkhoma losekupheleni kweminyaka lengetulu kwemakhulu lamabili leyengcile bewutingelwa ngema Dutch nemaNgisi etilwandle te Arctic; ngumkhoma labadobi base Melika labawulandzela sikhatsi lesidze elwandle lwase India, eBrazil Banks, eNor’ West Coast, kanye naletinye tindzawo letehlukene temhlaba, letibekwe ngibo kutsi tibe yi Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Labanye benta shengatsi babona umehluko emkhatsini wemkhoma waseGreenland wemaNgisi kanye nemkhoma wangasekudla wemaMelika. Kepha bavumelana ngalokucondzile kuto tonkhe timphawu tabo letinkhulu; futsi akukaze kwetfulwe liciniso linye lelicinile lelingasekelwa kulo umehluko lomkhulu. Kungekuhlukaniswa lokuncane lokungapheli lokusekelwe emehlukweni langapheli kakhulu, kutsi lamanye ematiko emlandvo wemvelo aba yinkimbinkimbi lecoshako. Lomkhoma lofanele utawuphatfwa kulenye indzawo ngesikhatsi lesitsite, ngekubhekisa ekucaciseni umkhoma wesilisa.

INCWADZI I. ( Liphepha ), SEHLUKO III. ( Fin-Back ).—Ngaphansi kwalenhloko ngicabanga kutsi silwane lesikhulu leso, ngemagama lehlukahlukene latsi Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, na Long-John, sibonakele cishe kuto tonkhe tilwandle futsi kuvame kuba ngumkhoma lonendiza lekhashane levame kuchazwa bagibeli labawela i-Atlantic, eNew York-packet-tracks. Ngebudze labutfolako, kanye nase baleen yakhe, iFin-back ifana ne whale lesekudla, kepha ine girth lencane le portly, kanye nembala lokhanyako, usondzela ku olive. Tindzebe takhe letinkhulu tetfula sici lesifana nekhebula, lesakhiwa ngekugoca lokuhlangene, lokugocekile kwemibimbi lemikhulu. Sici sakhe lesikhulu lesimehlukanisako, i- fin, lapho atsatsa khona ligama lakhe, kuvame kuba yintfo lebonakalako. Le fin ingaba ngemamitha lamatsatfu noma lamane ngebudze, ikhula ngco kusuka endzaweni lesemuva ngemuva, ngesimo se angular, futsi inesiphelo lesikhaliphile kakhulu lesicijile. Noma kungabonakali lenye incenye lencane yalesilwane, le fin leyehlukile, ngalesinye sikhatsi, itawubonakala iphuma ngalokucacile kusuka etulu. Uma lwandle luthulile ngesilinganiso, futsi luphawulwe kancane ngemagagasi layindingilizi, futsi le fin lefana ne gnomon isukuma futsi iphosa titfunti endzaweni leshwabene, kungenteka kutsi kucatjangwe kutsi lomjikeleto wemanti lolutungeletile ngandlela tsite ufana ne dial, ngesitayela sayo kanye ne wavy on hour-lines. Kulelo Ahazi-dial umtfunti uvame kubuyela emuva. Le Fin-Back ayisiyo indzawo yekuhlangana. Ubonakala atondza imikhoma, njengobe lamanye emadvodza atondza bantfu. Unemahloni kakhulu; njalo ahamba yedvwa; kukhuphukela etulu ngekungalindzeleki emantini lakhashane kakhulu futsi ladzabukisako; jet yakhe lecondzile naleyodwa lephakeme lekhuphuka njengemkhonto lomudze we-misanthropic etulu ethafeni lelingakakhiciti; unesiphiwo semandla lamahle kakhulu kanye nekushesha ekubhukudeni, kangangekutsi angamelana nako konkhe kulandzelwa lokukhona nyalo lokuvela kumuntfu; le leviathan ibonakala njengaKhayini locoshiwe futsi longancotjwa wesive sakhe, aphetse luphawu lwakhe leso sitayela ngemuva kwakhe. Kusuka ekubeni ne baleen emlonyeni wakhe, iFin-Back ngalesinye sikhatsi ifakwa nemkhoma wangasekudla, emkhatsini wetinhlobo tetifundvo letibitwa ngekutsi yi Whalebone whales , lokusho kutsi, imikhoma lene baleen. Kuletintfo letibitwa ngekutsi yi Whalebone whales, bekungabonakala ngatsi kunetinhlobo letiningi, linyenti lato, nomakunjalo, atiwa kakhulu. Imikhoma lenemphumulo lebanti kanye nemikhoma lenemilomo; imikhoma lenetinhloko letifana ne-pike; imikhoma lehlanganisiwe; imikhoma lengaphansi kwemahlathi kanye nemikhoma legucukile, ngemagama ebadobi etinhlobo letimbalwa.

Mayelana nalesibito se “Whalebone whales,” kubaluleke kakhulu kusho, kutsi nomakunjalo lendlela yekubeka emagama ingahle ibe lula ekwenteni lula kukhuluma ngeluhlobo lolutsite lwemikhoma, nomakunjalo kulite kwetama kuhlukanisa ngalokucacile iLeviathan, lesekelwe noma ku baleen yakhe, noma ku hump, noma ku fin; nomakunjalo kutsi leto tincenye noma timphawu letiphawulwe kakhulu kubonakala ngatsi tilungiswe kancono kutsi tikhone kukhokha sisekelo seluhlelo lolujwayelekile lwe Cetology kunanoma ngukuphi lokunye kwehlukana kwemtimba lokuhlukile, lokutsi umkhoma, ngetinhlobo takhe, wetfula. Kanjani-ke? Le baleen, i-hump, i-fin yangemuva, kanye nematinyo; leti tintfo letinetintfo tato letingakavami letisakateke ngekungakhetsi emkhatsini watotonkhe tinhlobo temikhoma, ngaphandle kwekucabanga kutsi kungaba yini bunjalo besakhiwo sato kuletinye tintfo letibaluleke kakhulu. Ngaleyo ndlela, umkhoma wesilisa kanye nemkhoma lone-humpback, ngamunye une-hump; kodvwa lapho kufana kuyaphela. Bese, lomkhoma lofanako lonemgogodla kanye nemkhoma wase Greenland, ngamunye walaba une baleen; kodvwa lapho futsi kufana kuyaphela. Futsi kuyafana nje naletinye tincenye letishiwo ngenhla. Etinhlotjeni letehlukene temikhoma, takha inhlanganisela lenjalo lengakavami; noma, esimeni sanoma ngumuphi wabo lohlukanisiwe, kuhlukaniswa lokunjalo lokungakavami; njengekutsi ngalokuphelele kuphikisa yonkhe indlela jikelele leyakhiwe ngesisekelo lesinjalo. Kuleli dwala wonkhe umuntfu we-whale-naturalists uhlukene.

Kepha kungenteka kutsi kucatjangwe kutsi, etincenyeni tangekhatsi temkhoma, emtimbeni wakhe—lapho, lokungenani, sitawukhona kushaya kuhlukaniswa lokufanele. Cha; sibonelo, nguyiphi intfo lekhona emtimbeni wemkhoma waseGreenland lemangalisako kune- baleen yakhe? Nomakunjalo sibonile kutsi nge baleen yakhe akunakwenteka kahle kuhlukanisa umkhoma wase Greenland. Futsi uma wehlela emathunjini ema leviathans lehlukahlukene, kungani lapho ungatfoli umehluko incenye yemashumi lasihlanu njengoba itfolakala kumhleli njengalabo bangaphandle labavele babalwe. Yini-ke lesele? lutfo ngaphandle kwekubamba imikhoma ngemtimba, kuyo yonkhe ivolumu yayo lekhululekile, bese uyihlukanisa ngesibindzi ngaleyondlela. Futsi lena yindlela ye-Bibliographical lapha lemukelwe; futsi ngiyo kuphela lengaphumelela, ngobe ngiyo kuphela lesebentako. Kute sichubeke.

INCWADZI II. ( Octavo ), SEHLUKO I. ( Grampus ).—Nanobe lemfishi, lephefumula kwayo lokukhulu, nobe kunaloko kuphefumula kwayo, inikete bantfu labahlala kulelive saga, yatiwa kakhulu njengemuntfu lohlala endzaweni lejulile, kodvwa ayifakwa emkhatsini wemkhoma. Kepha ngekuba nato tonkhe timphawu letinkhulu letihlukile te- leviathan, tati letinyenti temvelo timbonile njengalomunye. Ungu octavo lolinganiselwe, uhluka kusuka kumamitha lalishumi nesihlanu kuya kumashumi lamabili nesihlanu ngebudze, futsi unebukhulu lobuhambisanako lobujikeleta lukhalo. Ubhukusha ngemihlambi; akaze atingelwe njalo, noma oyili wakhe makhulu ngesilinganiso, futsi muhle kakhulu ekukhanyeni. Ngalabanye badobi indlela yakhe itsatfwa njengekukhombisa kuhamba embili kwemkhoma lomkhulu wesilisa.

INCWADZI II. ( I-Octavo ), SEHLUKO II. ( Black Fish ).—Nginika emagama ebadobi labadumile ato tonkhe letimfishi, ngobe ngalokuvamile ngito letihamba embili. Lapho kwenteka khona kutsi noma nguliphi ligama lingacaci kahle noma lingaveti, ngitawusho njalo, bese ngiphakamisa lelinye. Ngikwenta njalo nyalo, ngitsintsa iNhlanti Lemnyama, lebitwa ngekutsi, ngoba bumnyama ngumtsetfo cishe emkhatsini wayo yonkhe imikhoma. Ngako-ke, umbite ngekutsi nguMkhoma weMvu, uma ungatsandza. Kudla kwakhe kwatiwa kahle, futsi kusuka esimeni sekutsi ema-angle langekhatsi etindzebe takhe agobekile etulu, utfwala kumomotseka lokuphakadze kweMephistophelean ebusweni bakhe. Lomkhoma ulinganiselwa kumamitha lalishumi nesitfupha noma lalishumi nesiphohlongo ngebudze. Utfolakala cishe kuwo onkhe ema-latitude. Unendlela lemangalisako yekukhombisa i-dorsal hooked fin yakhe ekubhukudeni, lebukeka njengemphumulo yemaRoma. Uma bangasebenti ngendlela lezuzisa kakhulu, batingeli be sperm whale ngalesinye sikhatsi babamba iHyena whale, kwentela kugcina oyili loshibhile wemsebenti wasekhaya-njengoba labanye labagcina tindlu labacongako, ngekungabikhona kwenkampani, futsi bodvwa, bashisa emafutsa langanambitseki esikhundleni se wax lenukako. Noma iblubber yabo incane kakhulu, leminye yalemikhoma itakuniketa ngetulu kwema gallon lamashumi lamatsatfu emafutsa.

INCWADZI II. ( I-Octavo ), SEHLUKO III. ( Narwhale ), lokusho kutsi, Nostril whale .—Lesinye sibonelo semkhoma lobitwa ngekulangatelela kwati, ngicabanga kutsi wabitwa kanjalo ngekutsi luphondo lwakhe lolungakavami ekucaleni beluphutsa ngekutsi unemphumulo lenesicoco. Lesilwane sinemamitha langaba lishumi nesitfupha ngebudze, kantsi luphondo lwaso lulinganiselwa kumamitha lasihlanu, noma letinye tidlula emamitha lalishumi, futsi tize tifike kumamitha lalishumi nesihlanu. Kukhuluma ngalokucinile, loluphondo luyintfo lendze, lekhula iphume emhlatsini emgceni locindzetelekile kancane kusuka kulokuvundlile. Kepha itfolakala kuphela eceleni kwe sinister, lokunemphumela lomubi, kuniketa umnikati wayo intfo lefanako nesici sendvodza lesesandleni sesincele le-clumsy. Nguyiphi injongo lecondzile leluphondvo lwetindlovu nobe umkhonto lowuphendvulako, kungaba matima kukusho. Akubonakali kutsi isetjentiswa njengelibala lemfishi-yenkemba kanye nemfishi-ye-bill; noma labanye bantfu labahamba ngemikhumbi bangitjela kutsi iNarwhale iyisebentisa njenge rake ekuguculeni phansi kwelwandle kute itfole kudla. Charley Coffin watsi beyisetjentiselwa umshini wekubhobota ice; ngoba iNarwhale, ikhuphukela etulu eLwandle Lwase Polar, futsi itfole kutsi igcwele ice, iphusha luphondo lwayo etulu, futsi ngaleyondlela iyaphuka. Kodvwa angeke ukwati kufakazela kutsi lokubili lokucabangako kulungile. Umbono wami kutsi, noma ngabe loluphondvo loluseceleni lunye lungasetjentiswa mbamba yi Narwhale—noma ngabe loko kungaba njalo—kungaba lula kakhulu kuye ngalokucinisekile ngefolda ekufundzeni emapheshana. I-Narwhale lengiyivile ibitwa ngekutsi ngumkhoma lonetimphondvo, umkhoma lonetimphondvo, kanye nemkhoma we-Unicorn. Nakanjani usibonelo se-Unicornism letfolakala cishe kuwo wonkhe umbuso wemvelo lophilako. Kusukela kulabanye babhali labadzala laba cloistered ngibutsile kutsi loluphondo lolufanako lwe sea-unicorn emalangeni asendvulo belutsatfwa njengemutsi lomkhulu wekulwa nebuthi, futsi njengaloko, kulungiselela kwayo kwaletsa emanani lamakhulu kakhulu. Kwaphindze kwahlungwa kwaba luswayi lolugucugucukako lwebafati labaqulekako, ngendlela lefanako nekutsi timphondvo tetinyamatane letindvuna takhiwe tibe yi hartshorn. Ekucaleni kwaba ngokwayo kulandziswa intfo yekulangatelela lokukhulu. Incwadzi Lemnyama ingitjela kutsi Sir Martin Frobisher ngekubuya kwakhe kuloluhambo, ngesikhatsi Indlovukati Bess yenta ngesibindzi kuveta sandla sakhe lesinemagugu kuye kusuka efasitelweni le Greenwich Palace, njengoba umkhumbi wakhe lonesibindzi bewuhamba wehlela eThames; “Ngesikhatsi Sir Martin abuya kuloluhambo,” kusho Black Letter, “aguce ngemadvolo wetfula kumphatsi wakhe luphondo loludze lolumangalisako lwe Narwhale, lolwalenga sikhatsi lesidze ngemuva kwaloko lwalenga encabeni yase Windsor.” Umbhali wase Irish avers kutsi i Earl yase Leicester, ngekugoba ngemadvolo, nayo yetfula kulolunye luphondvo lwakhe, loluphatselene nesilwane sasemhlabatsini se unicorn.

Le Narwhale inekubukeka lokuhle kakhulu, lokufana nengwe, kuba nembala wemhlabatsi lomhlophe njengelubisi, lenemabala layindingilizi nalayindingilizi lamnyama. Emafutsa akhe aphakeme kakhulu, acacile futsi acocekile; kepha kuncane kwayo, futsi akakavami kutingelwa. Utfolakala kakhulu etilwandle letiseceleni kwe circumpolar.

INCWADZI II. ( I-Octavo ), SEHLUKO IV. ( Killer ).—Ngalomkhoma kuncane lokwatiwa kahle ngumuntfu waseNantucketer, futsi kute nhlobo kuloyo lotsi ungumfundzisi wemvelo. Kuloko lengikubonile ngaye ngikhashane, kufanele ngitsi bekacishe abe bukhulu be-grampus. Uyi-savage kakhulu-luhlobo lwemfishi ye-Feegee. Ngalesinye sikhatsi utsatsa imikhoma lemikhulu ye Folio ngemlomo, bese ulenga lapho njenge leech, kuze kube ngulapho lesilwane lesinemandla sikhatsateka size sife. Umbulali akaze atingelwe. Angikaze ngive kutsi unawo oyili lonjani. Kungatsatfwa lokuhlukile kuleligama leliniketwe lomkhoma, ngesizatfu sekungacaci kwawo. Ngobe sonkhe sibabulali, emhlabatsini naselwandle; Bonapartes kanye naBoshaka kufakwe.

INCWADZI II. ( Octavo ), SEHLUKO V. ( Thrasher ).—Lomnumzane udvume ngemsila wakhe, lawusebentisa njenge- ferule ekushayeni titsa takhe. Ugibela ngemuva kwe Folio whale, futsi njengoba abhukusha, usebenta umzila wakhe ngekumshaya; njengobe labanye bafundzisi besikolo bavumelana emhlabeni ngenchubo lefanako. Kusekhona lokuncane lokwatiwako ngeThrasher kuneMbulali. Bobabili baphula umtsetfo, ngisho nasetilwandle letite umtsetfo.

DUODECIMOS.—Leti tifaka ekhatsi imikhoma lemincane. I. Ingulube yase Huzza. II. Ingulube yase-Algeria. III. Le Porpoise lenemlomo lofana ne Mealy.

Kulabo labangakatfoli litfuba lelikhetsekile lekufundza lendzaba, kungenteka kubonakale kuyintfo lemangalisako, kutsi timfishi letingakavami kwedlula emamitha lamane noma lasihlanu kumele tihlanganiswe emkhatsini we WHALES—ligama, le, ngemcondvo lovamile, lihlala lidlulisela umcondvo webukhulu. Kepha letidalwa letibekwe phansi ngenhla njenge Duodecimoes tiyimikhoma lengaphutsi, ngemigomo yenchazelo yami yekutsi yini umkhoma— lokusho imfishi lephumako, lenemsila lovundlile.

INCWADZI III. ( Duodecimo ), SEHLUKO 1. ( Ingulube yaseHuzza ).—Lena yingulube levamile letfolakala cishe emhlabeni wonkhe. Leligama lingukuniketwa kwami; ngoba kunetinhlobo letingetulu kwayinye tema porpoises, futsi kumele kwentiwe lokutsite kute tihlukaniswe. Ngimbita kanjalo, ngoba uhlale abhukusha ema-shoals lahlekisanako, laselwandle lolubanti ahlala atijikijela ezulwini njengetigcoko esicukwini se-Fourth-of-July. Kubukeka kwawo kuvame kunconywa ngenjabulo ngumshayeli wemkhumbi. Tigcwele imimoya lemihle, tivela ngekungagucuki kusuka emagagasi lanemoya kuya ngasemoyeni. Ngubo labafana labahlala baphila embikwemoya. Tibalwa njengesibonakaliso senhlanhla. Uma wena ungamelana nekujabula lokutsatfu ngekubona letimfishi letiphilako, khona-ke lizulu likusite; umoya wekudlala lohlonipha Nkulunkulu awukho kini. iHuzza Porpoise leyondliwe kahle, lekhuluphele itakuniketa ligaloni linye lelihle lemafutsa lamahle. Kodvwa loludzaka loluncane nalolubucayi lolukhishwa emihlatsini yakhe luligugu kakhulu. Kuyacelwa emkhatsini webatsengisi bemagugu kanye nebakhi bemawashi. Bashayeli bemikhumbi bayifaka ema- hones abo. Inyama ye Porpoise idliwa kahle, uyati. Kungenteka kutsi akukaze kufike engcondvweni yakho kutsi i- porpoise iyaphuma. Ngempela, umlomo wakhe mncane kakhulu kangangekutsi awubonakali kalula kakhulu. Kodvwa ngesikhatsi lesitako nawutfola litfuba, mbukele; futsi utawubese ubona umkhoma lomkhulu we-Sperm yena ngekwakhe ngendlela lencane.

INCWADZI III. ( Duodecimo ), SEHLUKO II. ( Ingulube yase-Algeria ).—Umphangi. I-savage kakhulu. Utfolakala kuphela, ngicabanga kutsi, ePacific. Ukhulu ngandlela tsite kune Huzza Porpoise, kepha lokunyenti lokufanako jikelele kwenta. Mtfukutselise, futsi utawubopha kushaka. Ngimhlisele tikhatsi letinyenti, kodvwa angikaze ngimbone atfunjwa.

INCWADZI III. ( Duodecimo ), SEHLUKO III. ( I-Porpoise lenemlomo lonemlomo ).—Luhlobo lolukhulu kakhulu lwe-Porpoise; futsi itfolakala kuphela ePacific, ngendlela leyatiwa ngayo. Ligama lesiNgisi kuphela, labekwe ngalo kuze kube ngunyalo, ngulelo lebadobi—Right-Whale Porpoise, kusuka esimeni sekutsi utfolakala kakhulu edvute naleyo Folio. Ngesimo, wehluka ngendlela letsite ku Huzza Porpoise, ngekuba ne girth lencane leyindingilizi futsi lejabulisako; ngempela, unesibalo lesicocekile futsi lesifana nemnumzane. Akanawo emaphiko ngemuva kwakhe (lamanye ema porpoises lamaningi anawo), unemsila lotsandzekako, kanye nemehlo aseNdiya lanemizwa ye hazel hue. Kepha umlomo wakhe lonefulawa umosha konkhe. Noma wonkhe umhlane wakhe phansi kuya etimphikweni takhe letiseceleni ungumgca we sable lojulile, kepha umugca wemncele, lohlukile njengeluphawu lolusemkhunjini, lobitwa ngekutsi “lukhalo lolukhanyako,” lowo mugca umdvonsa kusuka esicumbini kuya emuva, ngemibala lemibili lehlukene, lemnyama ngetulu kanye nalomhlophe ngephansi. Lokumhlophe kufaka incenye yenhloko yakhe, kanye nemlomo wakhe wonkhe, lokumenta abukeke shengatsi usandza kubaleka ekuvakashelweni lokubi esikhwameni sekudla. Sici lesibi kakhulu futsi lesine-mealy! Emafutsa akhe afana kakhulu nalawo e-porpoise levamile.
