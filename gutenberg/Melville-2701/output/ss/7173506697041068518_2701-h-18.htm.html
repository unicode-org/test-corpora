SEHLUKO 89. Inhlanti-Lesheshako kanye neNhlanti-Lekhululekile.

Kukhulunywa nge waif kanye ne waif-poles esehlukweni sekugcina kepha sinye, kudzinga indzaba letsite yemitsetfo kanye nemitsetfo yekudoba imikhoma, lapho khona le waif ingatsatfwa njengeluphawu lolukhulu kanye nebheji.

Kuvame kwenteka kutsi uma imikhumbi leminyenti ihamba ngenkampani, umkhoma ungashaywa ngumkhumbi munye, bese uyabaleka, bese ekugcineni uyabulawa futsi utfunjwa ngulomunye umkhumbi; futsi lapha kuhlanganiswe ngalokungacondzi tintfo letinyenti letincane letingakahleleki, tonkhe tihlanganyela kulesici sinye lesikhulu. Sibonelo, —emuva kwekujaha nekubamba umkhoma lokudziniwe futsi lokuyingoti, lomtimba ungakhululeka emkhunjini ngenca yesiphepho lesinemandla; futsi ikhukhuleka khashane iye emoyeni, itsatfwe kabusha ngumkhoma wesibili, lo, ngekuthula, ngekucophelela ayidonsa eceleni, ngaphandle kwengoti yekuphila noma umugca. Ngaleyondlela timphikiswano leticasulako kakhulu naletinebudlova betivame kuvela emkhatsini webadobi, kube bekute umtsetfo lobhalwe phansi noma longabhalwa, wemhlabawonkhe, longaphikiswako losebenta kuwo onkhe emacala.
