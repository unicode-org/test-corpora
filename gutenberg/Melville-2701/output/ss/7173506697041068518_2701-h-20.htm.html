Futsi ngive, ngeligunya le whalemen, kutsi iSperm Whales itfunjwe cishe ngemamitha lalikhulu ngebudze ngesikhatsi sekubanjwa.

Kepha kungabi njalo, kutsi ngesikhatsi imikhoma yelihora lanyalo iyinchubekelembili ngebukhulu kunaleto tato tonkhe tikhatsi temhlaba letidlule; kungenteka yini kutsi kusukela ngesikhatsi sa-Adamu sebawohlokile?

Nakanjani, kufanele siphetse kanjalo, nangabe sitawubonga ema-akhawunti emadvodza lafana naPliny, kanye netifundziswa temvelo tasendvulo jikelele. Ngobe Pliny usitjela ngemikhoma lebeyimukela emahekitha etintfo letinyenti letiphilako, kanye ne Aldrovandus yaletinye lebetilinganisa emamitha langemakhulu lasiphohlongo ngebudze—Tindlela Tetintsambo Nemihubhe Yemikhoma yase Thames! Futsi ngisho nasemalangeni abo Banks na Solander, tati temvelo ta Cooke, sitfola lilunga lase Denmark le Academy of Sciences libeka phansi imikhoma letsite yase Iceland (reydan-siskur, noma Tisu Letishwabene) emayadi lalikhulu nemashumi lamabili; lokusho kutsi, emamitha langemakhulu lamatsatfu nemashumi lasitfupha. Futsi Lacépède, umfundzi wemvelo wase France, emlandvweni wakhe lobanti wemikhoma, ekucaleni kakhulu kwemsebenti wakhe (likhasi 3), ubeka phansi iRight Whale kumamitha lalikhulu, emakhulu lamatsatfu nemashumi lamabili nesiphohlongo. Futsi lomsebenti washicilelwa sekwephutile nga-AD 1825.

Kodvwa ngabe kukhona yini umkhoma lotawukholwa letindzaba? Cha, umkhoma walamuhla mkhulu njengabokhokho bakhe ngesikhatsi saPliny. Futsi uma ngabe ngiya lapho Pliny akhona khona, mine, umkhoma (ngetulu kwakhe), ngitawenta sibindzi sekumtjela kanjalo. Ngoba angikhoni kuvisisa kutsi kunjani, kutsi ngesikhatsi ema mummies aseGibhithe langcwatjwa tinkhulungwane teminyaka ngaphambi ngisho na Pliny atalwe, akalinganisi kakhulu emabhokisini abo njenge Kentuckian yesimanje emasokisini akhe; futsi ngesikhatsi tinkhomo naletinye tilwane letibatiwe etibhebhedzeni letindzala kakhulu taseGibhithe naseNineve, ngekulinganisa lokuhlobene lapho tidvwetjwe khona, njengoba nje kufakazela ngalokucacile kutsi tinkhomo letizaliwe letisetulu, letidliwa esitebeleni, letinemklomelo taseSmithfield, atilingani nje kuphela, kepha tidlula kakhulu ngebukhulu tinkhomo letikhuluphele taFaro;' ebusweni bako konkhe loku, angeke ngivume kutsi kuto tonkhe tilwane umkhoma kuphela bekufanele kutsi wehle.

Kodvwa kusekhona lolunye luphenyo lolusele; munye lovame kuphazanyiswa bantfu baseNantucket labakhululekile kakhulu. Noma ngabe ngenca yekubuka lokucishe kwati konkhe etinhloko tema mast temikhumbi-yemikhoma, nyalo ingena ngisho nase Behring’s straits, futsi ingena ema drawers lakhashane kakhulu layimfihlo kanye nema lockers emhlaba; futsi tinhlavu letiyinkhulungwane kanye nemikhonto yagijima kuwo onkhe emagceke elivekati; liphuzu leliphikisanako kutsi, ngabe Leviathan angabeketelela sikhatsi lesidze kujaha lokubanti kangaka, kanye nekubhujiswa lokungenakutisola kangaka; noma ngabe akukafaneli ekugcineni abhujiswe emantini, bese umkhoma wekugcina, njengemuntfu wekugcina, ubhema liphayiphi lakhe lekugcina, bese yena matfupha uyahwamuka ngekuphefumula kwekugcina.

Kucatsanisa imihlambi yemikhoma lenemagobolondo nemihlambi lenemagobolondo yetinyathi, le, hhayi eminyakeni lengemashumi lamane leyengcile, yasakateka ngemashumi etinkhulungwane emadlelweni ase Illinois nase Missouri, futsi yanyakatisa ema-manes abo ensimbi futsi yakhukhumala ngemashiya abo lahlanganiswe nekudvuma etindzaweni te-capitals lenebantfu labanyenti no river-i-polite intshi; ngekucatsanisa lokunjalo imphikiswano lengaphikiswa ingabonakala iniketwe, kukhombisa kutsi lomkhoma lotingelwako nyalo angeke ubaleke kubhubha ngekushesha.

Kodvwa kufanele niyibuke ngato tonkhe tindlela lendzaba. Noma nje sikhatsi lesifishane kangaka lesengcile—hhayi sikhatsi lesihle sekuphila—kubalwa kwetinyathi eIllinois kwedlula kubalwa kwemadvodza nyalo aseLondon, futsi noma nje lamuhla kute luphondo noma luselo lwato lolusele kuso sonkhe leso sigodzi; futsi noma imbangela yalokubhujiswa lokumangalisako bekungumkhonto wemuntfu; nobe kunjalo, simo lesehluke kakhulu sekutingela imikhoma sivimbela ngalokuphelele kuphela kweLeviathan lokungenadvumo kangaka. Emadvodza langemashumi lamane emkhunjini munye atingela iSperm Whales tinyanga letingemashumi lamane nesiphohlongo acabanga kutsi ente kahle kakhulu, futsi abonga Nkulunkulu, uma ekugcineni atfwala ekhaya emafutsa etimfishi letimashumi lamane. Ngesikhatsi, emalangeni ebantfu labadzala base Canada nase India labatingelako kanye nebacuphi base Nshonalanga, lapho khona inshonalanga lekhashane (lapho lilanga layo lelishonako lisaphuma khona) bekulihlane kanye nentfombi, linani lelifanako lemadvodza la moccasined, linani lelifanako letinyanga, agibele ehhashi esikhundleni sekuhamba ngemikhumbi kanye ne buffalo for more, bekutawuba; liciniso lekutsi, nangabe kunesidzingo, lingashiwo ngetibalo.

Noma, kucatjangwa kutsi kulungile, kubonakala ngatsi kukhona impikiswano levumelana nekuphela kancane kancane kwe Sperm Whale, sibonelo, kutsi eminyakeni leyengcile (incenye yekugcina yekhulu leminyaka leyengcile, sitsi) lama Leviathans, ngema pods lamancane, ahlangana kakhulu kunanyalo, futsi, ngenca yaloko, le voyages kanye ne voyagetive bekungaphindze kube sikhatsi lesidze kakhulu. Ngoba, njengoba sekuphawulwe kuletinye tindzawo, leyo mikhoma, lephendvulwa nguleminye imibono yekuphepha, nyalo ibhukusha etilwandle ngemakharavani lamakhulu, kangangekutsi ngesilinganiso lesikhulu ema solitaries lasakateke, emajoko, kanye nema pods, kanye netikolo talamanye emalanga nyalo sekuhlanganiswa kube ngema armi lamakhulu kepha lahlukaniswe kakhulu, langakavami. Nguloko kuphela. Futsi ngendlela lefanako fallacious kubonakala conceit, kutsi ngoba lokubitwa nge whale-bone whales akusahlaseli tindzawo letiningi eminyakeni leyengcile legcwele ngayo, kungako leyo nhlobo nayo iyancipha. Ngobe bacoshwa kuphela kusuka e-promontory kuya e-cape; futsi uma ngabe lolunye lugwini lungasaphiliswa ngema jets abo, ke, ciniseka, lolunye luhlangotsi lolukhashane lusandza kwetfuswa kakhulu ngumbukiso longatayeleki.

Ngetulu kwaloko: mayelana nalama Leviathans lashiwo ekugcineni, anetinqaba letimbili leticinile, leti, kuwo onkhe ematfuba ebantfu, titawuhlala phakadze tingavinjelwa. Futsi njengasekuhlaselweni kwetigodzi tabo, emaSwitzerland lanesitfwatfwa ahlehlele etintsabeni tawo; ngako, kutingelwa kusuka kuma savannas kanye nema glades etilwandle letisemkhatsini, le whale-bone whales ingagcina iphendvukele kuma citadels abo ePolar, bese icwila ngaphansi kwetihibe tekugcina letingilazi kanye netindvonga lapho, ikhuphuke emkhatsini wemasimu lane icy kanye ne floes; futsi emjikelezweni lotsandzekako waDecember lophakadze, bid defiance kukho konkhe kulandzelela lokuvela kumuntfu.

Kepha njengoba mhlawumbe emashumi lasihlanu alemikhoma lenematsambo emikhoma ibhajwe nge harpoon kwentela i cachalot yinye, labanye bososayensi be forecastle baphetse ngekutsi loku kucekela phansi lokuhle sekuvele kunciphise kakhulu emabutfo abo. Kepha noma sikhatsi lesitsite sendlulile linani lalemikhoma, hhayi ngaphansi kwa 13,000, beyibulawa njalo ngemnyaka elugwini lwasenyakatfo' yasenshonalanga ngemaMelika kuphela; noma kunjalo kunetintfo leticatshangelwako letenta ngisho nalesi simo sibe nendzaba lencane noma lengabi khona njengemphikiswano lephikisako kuloludzaba.

Kuyimvelo njengoba kuyimvelo kutsi kube nekungakholwa ngandlela tsite mayelana nebuningi betilwane letikhulu kakhulu temhlaba, nomakunjalo sitawutsini ku Harto, umlandzi wase Goa, uma asitjela kutsi ngalesinye sikhatsi kutingela iNkhosi yase Siam yatsatsa tindlovu letingu 4,000; kutsi kuleto tigodzi tindlovu tiningi njengemihlambi yetinkhomo etindzaweni letinesimo selitulu lesifutfumele. Futsi kubonakala kute sizatfu sekungabata kutsi nangabe letindlovu, lesetitingelwa iminyaka letinkhulungwane, nguSemiramis, nguPorus, nguHannibal, kanye nawo onkhe emakhosi lalandzelanako aseMphumalanga—nangabe asaphila lapho ngebunyenti, kakhulu kungaba ngumkhoma lomkhulu ngetulu kwako konkhe kutingela, njengobe anemadlelo lamabili njenge lar e-Asia. Emave aseMelika, eYurophu ne-Afrika, eNew Holland, kanye nato tonkhe Tichingi taselwandle letihlanganisiwe.

Ngetulu kwaloko: kumele sicabangele, kutsi kusuka ekuphileni lokudze lokukhulu lokucatshangwako kwemikhoma, kungenteka kutsi ifinyelele eminyakeni lelikhulu kanye nangetulu, ngako-ke nganoma ngusiphi sikhatsi sinye, titukulwane letiningi letihlukile tebantfu labadzala kumele tibe tesikhatsi lesiphila kuso. Futsi kutsi yini leyo, singasheshe sitfole umbono lotsite, ngekucabanga onkhe emathuna, emathuna, kanye netindzawo tekugcina temndeni tekudala letiniketa imitimba lephilako yawo onkhe emadvodza, bafati, nebantfwana lebebaphila eminyakeni lengemashumi lasikhombisa nesihlanu leyengcile; kanye nekwengeta lomngenisi longenakubalwa esibalweni sebantfu lesikhona emhlabeni wonkhe.

SEHLUKO 106. Umlente wa-Ahabi.

Indlela lesheshako lapho khona Captain Ahab abeshiye khona Samuel Enderby wase London, bekungakagadzeki ngebudlova lobuncane kumuntfu wakhe. Abekhanyise ngemandla lamakhulu ngesikhatsi sikebhe sakhe siphazamiseka kangangekutsi umlente wakhe wetindlovu bewutfole kwetfuka lokuhhafu. Futsi ngesikhatsi ngemuva kwekutfola umkhumbi wakhe, kanye nemgodzi wakhe we-pivot lapho, wajikeleta ngamandla kakhulu ngemyalo lophutfumako kumcondzisi (kwaba, njengasekucaleni, intfo mayelana nekungacondzi kwakhe ngekungagucugucuki ngalokwenele); ke, le ivory lebeyivele inyakatiswa yatfola twist kanye ne wrench lengetiwe, kangangekutsi noma beyisolo iphelele, futsi kukho konkhe kubonakala inenkhanuko, nomakunjalo Ahabi akazange ayibone ithembekile ngalokuphelele.

Futsi, mbamba, bekubonakala kuyintfo lencane yekumangalisa, kutsi ngako konkhe kunganaki kwakhe lokugcwele, lokuhlanyako, Ahabi ngaletinye tikhatsi bekanakisisa simo salelitsambo lelifile lebekame kulo. Ngobe bekungakapheli sikhatsi lesidze kakhulu ngaphambi kwekutsi iPequod ihambe ngemkhumbi isuka eNantucket, kutsi atfolakele ngalelinye lilanga ebusuku alele alele phansi, futsi angaboni; ngulokunye lokungatiwa, futsi lokubonakala kungachazeki, kulimala lokungacabangeki, sicu sakhe setinyosi besisuswe ngebudlova, kangangekutsi besishaywe ngekuhlakanipha kwesigodvo, futsi konkhe kepha kwabhobota umgogodla wakhe; futsi bekungasiko ngaphandle kwebumatima lobukhulu kutsi lesilonda lebesibuhlungu selapheke ngalokuphelele.

Noma, ngaleso sikhatsi, bekungehlulekile kungena engcondvweni yakhe ye monomaniac, kutsi konkhe kuhlupheka kwaloko kuhlupheka kwakhona ngaleso sikhatsi bekumane nje kuyindaba lecondzile yemaye langaphambilini; futsi naye bekabonakala abona ngalokucacile, kutsi njengoba silwane lesihucukako lesinebuthi kakhulu se marsh sichubeka neluhlobo lwaso ngendlela lengagwemeki njenge songster lemnandzi kakhulu ye grove; ngako, ngekulingana nayo yonkhe felicity, tonkhe tigameko letibuhlungu tenta ngekwemvelo titale letifana nato. Yebo, ngetulu kwekulingana, kwacabanga Ahabi; njengobe kokubili bukhokho nesitukulwane seLusizi kuhamba ngetulu kwesitukulwane nesitukulwane seNjabulo. Ngoba, hhayi kukhombisa loku: kutsi kuyintfo lephethwe kusuka etimfundzisweni letitsite te-canonic, kutsi ngesikhatsi letinye tintfo letijabulisako temvelo lapha tingabi nebantfwana labatalelwe ngulelinye live, kepha, ngekuphikisana, titawulandzelwa injabulo-kungabi nebantfwana kwako konkhe kuphelelwa litsemba kwesihogo; lapho khona, letinye tinhlupheko letifako letinelicala tisatawutalela tona inzalo lechubeka phakadze yelusizi ngale kwelithuna; hhayi nhlobo kukhombisa loku, kusekhona kungalingani ekuhlaziyeni lokujulile kwentfo. Ngobe, kwacabanga Ahabi, ngesikhatsi ngisho netinjabulo letiphakeme kakhulu tasemhlabeni tike taba nebuncane lobutsite lobungasho lutfo lobucashe kuto, kepha, phansi, tonkhe tinhlupheko tenhlitiyo, kubaluleka lokuyimfihlakalo, futsi, kulamanye emadvodza, bukhulu betingilosi letikhulu; ngako-ke benta tracings yabo ngekutimisela-out hhayi belie kudonswa lokusobala. Kulandzela titukulwane taletinhlupheko letisetulu letifako, kusitfwala ekugcineni emkhatsini we primogenitures lengenamtfombo yabonkulunkulu; ngako-ke, ebusweni bawo onkhe emalanga lajabulisako, lawenta hay, kanye nekushaya emacimba lathambile, tinyanga-tekuvuna letiyindingilizi, kumele sidzingeke kutsi sinikele kuloku: kutsi bonkulunkulu bona ngekwabo abajabuli phakadze. Luphawu lwekutalwa lolungacimi, loludzabukisako esiphongweni semuntfu, luyisitembu selusizi kulabasayinile.

Ngekungati lapha imfihlo seyivetwe, mhlawumbe kungenteka kutsi kufanele kakhulu, ngendlela lebekiwe, beyivetwe ngaphambilini. Ngeminye imininingwane leminyenti lephatselene na Ahabi, njalo bekuhlala kuyimfihlakalo kulabanye, kungani bekunjalo, kutsi sikhatsi lesitsite, kokubili ngaphambi nangemuva kwekuhamba kwe Pequod, bekatifihlile ngekukhetseka lokufana ne Grand-Lama; futsi, ngaleso sikhatsi sinye, wafuna siphephelo sekungakhulumi, kube shengatsi, emkhatsini we-marble senate yalabafile. Sizatfu saKaputeni Peleg lesilimele salentfo sabonakala singakafaneli; noma kunjalo, mbamba, njengobe kutsintsa yonkhe incenye lejulile ya-Ahabi, sonkhe sembulo besitsatsa bumnyama lobubalulekile kunekukhanya lokuchazako. Kepha, ekugcineni, konkhe kwaphuma; lendzaba yinye yenta, lokungenani. Leyo ngoti lembi kakhulu beyisephansi kwekuhlala kwakhe yedwa kwesikhashana. Futsi hhayi loku kuphela, kepha kulowo mbutsano lohlale ukhona, lowela elugwini, lobeka, nganoma ngusiphi sizatfu, abe nelilungelo lekusondzela kuye lokungakavinjelwa kakhulu; kulowo mbutsano lonemahloni lokulimala lokushiwo ngenhla—kwasala, njengobe kwenta, kungalandziswa ngu-Ahabi—kwatifaka ngekwesaba, hhayi ngalokuphelele eveni lemimoya nekukhala. Ngako-ke, ngenshisekelo yabo ngaye, bonkhe bebente licebo, ngendlela lebeyikhona ngayo, kutsi bafihle lwati lwalentfo kulabanye; futsi kungako kwaba njalo, kutsi hhayi kuze kube sikhatsi lesidze sesiphelile, kwavela emadekhi ePequod.

Kodvwa konkhe loku kube njalo; ake sinodi lengabonakali, lengacaci kahle emoyeni, noma tindvuna letiphindiselako kanye nemandla emlilo, kufanele kutsi asebente noma cha na-Ahabi wasemhlabeni, noma kunjalo, kulendzaba lekhona nyalo yemlente wakhe, watsatsa tindlela letisebentako leticacile;—wabita umbati.

Futsi ngesikhatsi loyo msebenti avela embikwakhe, wamyala ngaphandle kwekulibala kutsi acale kwenta umlente lomusha, futsi wacondzisa laba labashade nabo kutsi bambone aniketwa tonkhe tikhonkwane kanye netikhonkwane te jaw-ivory (Sperm Whale) lebetibutfwe kuze kube ngunyalo kuloluhambo, kwentela kutsi kukhetsa ngekucophelela kwe stouteine, cleared segra kungaba yintfo lecacile. Loku kwentiwa, umbati watfola imiyalo yekutsi umlente uphele ngalobo busuku; kanye nekuniketa tonkhe tintfo letifanele kuyo, ngaphandle kwaleto letiphatselene naleyo lengatsembeki lesetjentiswako. Ngetulu kwaloko, indzawo yekukhicita umkhumbi yayalwa kutsi iphakanyiswe ngaphandle kwekungasebenti kwayo kwesikhashana kulendzawo yekubamba; futsi, kwentela kusheshisa lendzaba, umkhandzi wayalwa kutsi achubeke ngaso leso sikhatsi ekubumbeni noma ngutiphi tintfo tensimbi letingadzingeka.
