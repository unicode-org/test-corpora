SEHLUKO 28. Ahabi.

Kwaphela emalanga lambalwa ngemuva kwekusuka eNantucket, kute intfo lengetulu kwema hatches leyabonakala nga Captain Ahab. Laba labashadile bebahlala bakhululekana ngesikhatsi sekulindza, futsi nganoma yini lebeyingabonakala lephikisanako, bekubonakala shengatsi ngibo kuphela labahola lomkhumbi; kuphela ngalesinye sikhatsi bakhipha kusuka endlini ngemiyalo lengakalindzeleki futsi lecindzetelako, kutsi ngemuva kwako konkhe bekucaca kutsi kepha bayala vicariously.
