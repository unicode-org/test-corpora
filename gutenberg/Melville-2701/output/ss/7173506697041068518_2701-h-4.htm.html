Ngenca yekutsi ngashaywa ngulombuto locacile, ngaphendvula ngatsi, “Yebo, bengicabanga kutsi ngibone emadvodza lamane nobe lasihlanu, kodvwa bekufiphele kakhulu kutsi ngingaciniseka.”

Ekugcineni, nasigibela iPequod, satfola konkhe kuthule kakhulu, kute umphefumulo lonyakatako. Umnyango wendlu bewuvaliwe ngekhatsi; ema hatches abevuliwe onkhe, futsi agcwele emakhoyili e rigging. Kuya phambili forecastle, satfola slide scuttle kuvulekile. Ngekubona kukhanya, sehla, satfola kuphela i-rigger lendzala lapho, igocotwe nge-pea-jacket ledzabukile. Wajikwa ngebudze bonkhe etifubeni letimbili, buso bakhe bubheke phansi futsi avalwe ngetingalo takhe letigocekile. Buthongo lobujulile kakhulu bebulala phezu kwakhe.

“Labo bantfu labahamba ngemikhumbi lesibabonile, Queequeg, kungenteka kutsi bayaphi?” kusho mine ngibuka loyo lolele ngekungabata. Kepha bekubonakala shengatsi, ngesikhatsi ase wharf, iQueequeg beyingakakunaki nhlobo loko lebengikhuluma ngako nyalo; ngako-ke bengitawucabanga kutsi ngikhohlisiwe ngekwemehlo kuleyondzaba, kube bekungasiyo umbuto wa-Elija longachazeki ngalenye indlela. Kodvwa ngayishaya phansi lentfo; futsi aphindze amaka lolele, ngekuhlekisa akhombisa Queequeg kutsi mhlawumbe bekuncono sihlale phansi nesidumbu; amtjele kutsi atisungule ngendlela lefanele. Wabeka sandla sakhe ngemuva kwaloyo lolele, kwaba shengatsi uva kutsi sitsambile ngalokwenele yini; futsi-ke, ngaphandle kwekutsi ngikhulume kakhulu, wahlala phansi lapho athule.

Queequeg watisusa waya ngale nje kwenhloko yemuntfu lolele, futsi wakhanyisa liphayiphi lakhe le tomahawk. Ngahlala etinyaweni. Besigcina leliphayiphi lidlula ngetulu kwemlala, kusuka kulomunye kuya kulomunye. Ngaleso sikhatsi, ngesikhatsi ngimbuta ngendlela yakhe lephukile, Queequeg wanginika kutsi ngivisise kutsi, eveni lakhe, ngenca yekungabikhona kwema settees kanye nema sofa atotonkhe tinhlobo, inkhosi, tindvuna, kanye nebantfu labakhulu jikelele, bebasemkhubeni wekukhuluphalisa lamanye ema oda laphansi ema ottomans; futsi kuniketa indlu ngendlela lentofontofo ngaleyo ndlela, bekufanele uthenge kuphela bantfu labavilaphako labasiphohlongo noma labalishumi, bese ubabeka nxazonke ema piers nasema alcoves. Ngaphandle kwaloko, bekulula kakhulu eluhambeni; ncono kakhulu kunaleto tihlalo-tengadze letiguculwa tibe tindvuku tekuhamba; ngalesinye sikhatsi, indvuna ibita inceku yayo, futsi ifisa kutsi yente umhlalo wakhe ngaphansi kwesihlahla lesisakateke, mhlawumbe endzaweni letsite lemanti lenemaxhaphozi.

Ngesikhatsi alandzisa letintfo, njalo uma Queequeg atfola le tomahawk kimi, bekachuma i-hatchet-side yayo ngetulu kwenhloko yemuntfu lolele.

Waphefumula ngendlela letsite yekufiphala; kwabese kubonakala kukhatsateka emphumulweni; bese iyajikeleta kanye nobe kabili; wabese uyahlala phansi ahlikihla emehlo.
