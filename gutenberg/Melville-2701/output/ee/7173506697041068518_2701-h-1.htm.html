TA 1. Nusiwo le ŋgɔ yim.

Yɔm be Ismael. Ƒe aɖewoe nye esi va yi—ɣeyiɣi didi si tututu metsɔ ɖeke le eme o—esi ga sue aɖe alo naneke menɔ asinye le nye gakotoku me o, eye naneke tɔxɛ aɖeke menɔ asinye si ado dzidzɔ nam le ƒuta o ta la, mesusu be maɖo tɔdziʋu anɔ abe vie ene akpɔ xexeame ƒe akpa si tsi le. Enye mɔ aɖe si le asinye si dzi mato aku ʋu le aɖuɖɔtoea ŋu eye mawɔ ɖoɖo ɖe ʋu ƒe sisi ŋu. Ɣesiaɣi si mekpɔ be nye dzi le nu ƒom le nu me ŋu; ɣesiaɣi si wònye November si me tsi dzana eye tsi dzana le nye luʋɔ me; ɣesiaɣi si mekpɔ be metɔ vie le aɖakadzraɖoƒewo ŋkume le nye lɔlɔ̃nu me, eye metsɔ kunuwɔwɔ ɖesiaɖe si medo goe ƒe megbe va; eye vevietɔ ɣesiaɣi si nye hypos ɖu dzinye alea gbegbe, be ebia agbenyuinɔnɔ ƒe gɔmeɖose sesẽ aɖe be wòaxe mɔ nam be nyemaɖoe koŋ aɖo afɔ ablɔdzi o, eye matsɔ mɔnu aƒo amewo ƒe kposɔwo ɖa o—ekema, mebunɛ be ɣeyiɣia de be maɖo ƒua dzi kaba ne mate ŋui. Esiae nye nye nusi woatsɔ aɖɔli tu kple bɔl. Cato tsɔ xexemenunya ƒe dzidzedzekpɔkpɔ ƒu gbe ɖe eƒe yi dzi; Metsɔa kpoo yia melia gbɔ. Naneke mewɔ nuku le esia me o. Ne wonyae ko la, ŋutsu siwo katã kloe le woƒe ɖaseɖigbalẽ me, ɣeyiɣi aɖe alo bubu, dea asixɔxɔ seselelãme ɖeka ma ke kloe ɖe atsiaƒu ŋu kplim.

Fifia miaƒe ƒukpodzidu si nye Manhattoes, si melidzeƒewo ƒo xlãe abe India-ƒukpowo ene kple koral-towo le—asitsatsa ƒo xlãe kple eƒe ƒutsotsoewo. Le ɖusime kple miame la, ablɔwo kplɔa wò yia tsi gbɔ. Eƒe dua ƒe titina si gbɔ emee nye batri, afisi ƒutsotsoewo klɔa lãkle bubume ma le, eye yaƒoƒo siwo gaƒoƒo ʋɛ aɖewo do ŋgɔ la megakpɔa anyigba o la nana wòfana le. Kpɔ ameha siwo le tsi kpɔm le afima ɖa.

Miƒo xlã du si me Sabat ŋdɔ si me drɔ̃e le. Yi tso Corlears Hook yi Coenties Slip, eye tso afima, to Whitehall, yi dziehe gome. Nukae nèkpɔ?—Woɖo ame kuku akpe geɖe siwo le tsitre ɖe atsiaƒu me drɔ̃ewo me abe dzɔla siwo meƒoa nu o ene. Ame aɖewo nɔ te ɖe tsikpeawo ŋu; ame aɖewo bɔbɔ nɔ tɔdziʋudzeƒewo ƒe tawo dzi; ame aɖewo le ŋku lém ɖe meli siwo tso China ƒe mɔ̃ sesẽwo dzi; ɖewo le kɔkɔƒe le rigging la me, abe ɖe wole agbagba dzem be yewoakpɔ atsiaƒu ƒe dzedzeme si nyo wu ene. Gake amesiawo katã nye anyigbadzitɔwo; ƒe kwasiɖanuwuwu siwo woɣla ɖe lath kple plaster me—wobla wo ɖe kplɔ̃wo ŋu, woƒo wo ɖe zikpuiwo ŋu, wobla wo ɖe kplɔ̃wo ŋu. Ekema aleke esia le? Ðe agble damawo nu yia? Nukae wowɔna le afisia?

Gake kpɔ ɖa! afisiae ameha geɖe wu va, siwo le zɔzɔm tẽe yina tsia gbɔ, eye edze abe wobla wo be yewoaƒu tsi ene. Si womekpᴐ kpᴐ o! Naneke madze wo ŋu o negbe anyigba la ƒe seɖoƒe si gbɔ eme wu; tsaɖiɖi le nudzraɖoƒe siwo le afima ƒe ya si vɔvɔli le te masɔ gbɔ o. Ao, ele be woate ɖe tsia ŋu alesi woate ŋui evɔ womage ɖe eme o. Eye afimae wotsi tre ɖo—kilometa geɖe tso wo gbɔ—leaguƒewo. Inlanders katã, wotsoa mɔwo kple mɔdodowo, ablɔwo kple mɔdodowo dzi—dziehe, ɣedzeƒe, dzigbe, kple ɣetoɖoƒe. Ke hã le afisia la, wo katã wowɔ ɖeka. Gblɔe nam be ɖe magnet ƒe nɔnɔme nyui si le meli mawo katã ƒe kɔmpasi ƒe aŋewo ŋu hea wo yia afima?

Zi ɖeka ake. Gblɔ be yele dukɔa me; le ta kɔkɔ aɖewo dzi. Mito mɔ ɖesiaɖe kloe si nèdi, eye ewo va ɖo ɖeka la, etsɔa wò yia anyime le bali aɖe me, eye wògblẽa wò ɖe afima le ta aɖe to le tɔsisia me. Akunyawɔwɔ le eme. Na ame siwo ƒe susu mebu o wu la nanyrɔ ɖe eƒe drɔ̃e goglotɔwo kekeake me—tsi tre ɖe ŋutsu ma ŋu ɖe eƒe afɔwo dzi, tsɔ eƒe afɔwo nayi, eye wòakplɔ wò ayi tsi gbɔ vodadamanɔmee, ne tsi le nuto ma katã me. Ne tsikɔ ​​wu wò le Amerika-dzogbe gã la dzi gbeɖeka la, ke te dodokpɔ sia kpɔ, ne edzɔ be wotsɔ nukpɔsusuŋutinunyafialagã aɖe na wò mɔzɔha la. Ẽ, abe alesi amesiame nyae ene la, ŋugbledede kple tsi nye srɔ̃ɖeɖe tegbee.

Gake nutala aɖee nye esi. Edi be yeata lɔlɔ̃ ƒe nɔnɔme si me drɔ̃e le wu, vɔvɔlitɔ kekeake, tomefafa wu, si doa dzidzɔ na ame wu le Saco balia katã me la wò. Nukae nye nu vevitɔ si wòzãna? Eƒe atiwo le tsitre ɖe afima, eye wo dometɔ ɖesiaɖe ƒe ke me do le, abe ɖe saɖagaxɔmenɔla kple atitsoga le eme ene; eye afi sia mlɔ eƒe gbedadaƒo, eye afi ma eƒe nyiwo mlɔna; eye dzudzɔ aɖe si le alɔ̃ dɔm la tso aƒe sue si le afima la dzi. Ya ƒona yia avetsu didiwo me goglo, eye wòɖoa to siwo le wo nɔewo dzi siwo wotsɔ woƒe blɔ si le togbɛa to la me. Gake togbɔ be nɔnɔmetata la mlɔ anyi alea, eye togbɔ be pine-ti sia ʋuʋua eƒe hũɖeɖewo abe aŋgba siwo le alẽkplɔla sia ƒe ta dzi ene hã la, wo katã nye dzodzro, negbe ɖe alẽkplɔla la ƒe ŋku nɔ akunyawɔwɔ ƒe tɔsisi si le eŋgɔ la ŋu ko hafi. Yi ɖasrã Prairies kpɔ le June me, esime nèƒua tsi kilometa gbogbo aɖewo le Tiger-lilies dome vaseɖe klo dzi—nukae adzewɔwɔ ɖeka dim?—Tsi—tsi ɖeka pɛ hã mele afima o! Ðe Niagara nye ke ƒe ŋkumeʋuʋu ko la, ɖe nàzɔ mɔ wò kilometa akpe ɖeka aɖakpɔea? Nukatae Tennessee hakpanyaŋlɔla dahe la, esi wòxɔ klosalo asi eve zi ɖeka la, ɖoe be yeaƒle awudziwui aɖe nɛ, si wòhiã nublanuitɔe, alo atsɔ eƒe ga ade afɔtoƒe yi Rockaway Beach? Nukatae ŋutsuvi ɖesiaɖe kloe si le lãmesẽ me sesẽ si si luʋɔ sesẽ si le lãmesẽ me le, le ɣeyiɣi aɖe me alo bubu me la nye tagbɔsesẽtɔ be wòayi ƒua dzi? Nukatae le wò mɔzɔzɔ gbãtɔ abe mɔzɔla ene me la, wò ŋutɔ nèse ʋuʋudedi ɣaɣla sia tɔgbe, esi wogblɔ na wò zi gbãtɔ be wò kple wò melia megale anyigba dzi fifia o? Nu ka tae Persiatɔ xoxoawo bu atsiaƒua nu kɔkɔe? Nukatae Helatɔwo tsɔ mawu tɔxɛ aɖe nɛ, eye wònye Jove ŋutɔ nɔviŋutsu? Kakaɖedzitɔe la, esiawo katã menye nusi ŋu gɔmesese aɖeke mele o. Eye gɔmesese si le Narcissus ƒe ŋutinya ma ŋu gade to ɖe edzi, amesi esi mete ŋu se legba si le funyafunya wɔm, si fa miamiamia si wòkpɔ le vudoa me la gɔme o ta la, enyrɔ ɖe eme eye wònyrɔe. Gake nɔnɔmetata ma ke, mía ŋutɔwo míekpɔna le tɔsisiwo kple atsiaƒuwo katã me. Enye agbe ƒe gbɔgbɔ si gɔme womate ŋu ase o la ƒe nɔnɔmetata; eye esiae nye nu vevitɔ le wo katã me.

Fifia, ne megblɔ be ezu numame nam be mayi ƒua dzi ɣesiaɣi si medze ŋkuwo ƒe ʋuʋu gɔme, eye medze nye lãkusiwo ƒe ŋudzedzekpɔkpɔ gɔme akpa la, nyemedi be mana woaƒo nya ta be meyina ƒua dzi gbeɖe abe mɔzɔla ene o. Elabena hafi nàyi abe mɔzɔla ene la, ele be gakotoku nanɔ asiwò, eye gakotoku nye avɔ ko negbe ɖe nane le eme hafi. Gakpe ɖe eŋu la, mɔzɔlawo dzea ƒudzidɔ—wova nɔa dzre wɔm—womedɔa alɔ̃ zã bliboa o—womekpɔa dzidzɔ boo o, abe nusi bɔ ene;—ao, nyemeyina abe mɔzɔla ene gbeɖe o; eye togbɔ be dze aɖee menye hã la, nyemeyia ƒua dzi abe Commodore, alo Captain, alo Cook ene gbeɖe o. Meɖea asi le ɖoƒe mawo ƒe ŋutikɔkɔe kple vovototodedeameme ŋu na amesiwo lɔ̃a wo. Le nye gome la, meƒoa ŋu ɖe dɔ sesẽ siwo ŋu bubu le, dodokpɔwo, kple xaxa ƒomevi ɖesiaɖe si ŋu bubu le la katã ŋu. Enye nusi mate ŋu awɔ be malé be na ɖokuinye, evɔ nyemelé be na meliwo, barques, brigs, schooners, kple nusiwo menye nenema o. Eye le yiyi abe nuɖala ene gome la,—togbɔ be meʋu eme be ŋutikɔkɔe geɖe le ema me hã la, nuɖala nye amegã ƒomevi aɖe si le meli me—ke hã, le mɔ aɖe nu la, nyemesusu xevi siwo wotɔ dzoe kpɔ o;—togbɔ be woɖae zi ɖeka, wotsɔ bɔta ƒo wo nunyatɔe, eye wotsɔ dze kple atadi de wo me le ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃ me hã la, ame aɖeke meli si aƒo nu bubutɔe wu, ne míagblɔe bubutɔe o, tso xevi si woɖa ŋu o xevi wu nye ŋutɔ. Egiptetɔ xoxoawo ƒe trɔ̃subɔsubɔ ƒe lɔlɔ̃ ɖe ibis si wotɔ dzoe kple tɔsisi me sɔ si wotɔ dzoe dzi mee nèkpɔa nuwɔwɔ mawo ƒe ame kukuwo le woƒe abolomeƒe gã siwo nye piramidwo me.

Ao, ne meyi ƒua dzi la, meyina abe ƒudzidela bɔbɔe ene, do ŋgɔ na tɔdziʋua ƒe ʋuƒoa tututu, metsɔa tsi ƒua du yia ŋgɔgbexɔa me, yia dzi le afima va ɖoa fia ƒe tɔdziʋua ƒe ta gbɔ. Nyateƒee, woɖea gbe nam boŋ tso ɖewo ŋu, eye wonana meti kpo tso spar yi spar, abe ʋetsuvi si le May ƒe gbedadaƒo ene. Eye le gɔmedzedzea me la, nu sia ƒomevi mevivina o ale gbegbe. Ewɔa dɔ ɖe ame ƒe bubu ƒe seselelãme dzi, vevietɔ ne ƒome xoxo aɖe si woɖo anyi le anyigbaa dzi, si nye Van Rensselaers, alo Randolphs, alo Hardicanutes, mee nètso. Eye wu wo katã la, ne do ŋgɔ teti na wò asi tsɔtsɔ de aŋɔka me la, ke èɖu edzi abe kɔƒenutome sukutatɔ ene, si na ŋutsuvi kɔkɔtɔwo tsi tre ɖe vɔvɔ̃ na wò. Meka ɖe edzi na wò be tɔtrɔa nye esi me dzo le, tso sukutatɔ dzi va zu ƒudzidela, eye ebia Seneca kple Stoatɔwo ƒe ʋeʋẽ sesẽ aɖe si ana nàte ŋu ako alɔgbɔnu ahado dzi. Gake esia gɔ̃ hã nu yina le ɣeyiɣi aɖe megbe.

Ke ne ƒudzisrafomegã aɖe ƒe akpa xoxo aɖewo ɖe gbe nam be matsɔ ʋuʋudedi aɖe atutu ʋua ƒe akpatawo ya ɖe? Nukae bubumademade ame ŋu ma nye, ne wodae, mebe, le Nubabla Yeyea ƒe nudanu me? Èsusu be mawudɔla gã Gabriel bua naneke le ŋunye wu, elabena meɖoa to hunk xoxo mawo enumake kple bubutɔe le nɔnɔme ma koŋ mea? Amekae menye kluvi o? Gblɔ nya ma nam. Enyo, ekema aleke kee ƒudzisrafomegã xoxoawo ate ŋu aɖe gbe nam be magblɔe o—aleke kee woɖaƒom kple atam o, edzɔa dzi nam be menya be enyo; be wosubɔa ame bubu ɖesiaɖe le mɔ ɖeka alo bubu nu le mɔ ma ke nu—le ŋutilã alo nu ŋutɔŋutɔ ƒe nukpɔsusu nu, si nye; eye aleae wotsɔa xexeame katã ƒe ƒoƒoa ƒoa xlãe, eye ele be asiwo katã natutu wo nɔewo ƒe abɔta, eye woƒe dzi nadze eme.

Le afisia hã la, meyina ƒua dzi ɣesiaɣi abe ƒudzidela ene, elabena woɖoe be yewoaxe fe nam ɖe nye kuxia ta, evɔ womexea gaku ɖeka pɛ hã na mɔzɔlawo si ŋu mese kpɔ o. Kura o, ele be mɔzɔlawo ŋutɔ naxe fe. Eye vovototo katã le xexeame le fexexe kple fexexe dome. Ðewohĩ fexexe ƒe nuwɔnae nye nusi me dziɖeleameƒo le wu si atikutsetsebɔdzifila eveawo he vɛ na mí. Gake esi woxe fe na ,—nukae asɔ kplii? Dugã me dɔwɔna si ame tsɔ xɔa ga la wɔ nuku ŋutɔŋutɔ, ne míebu eŋu be míexɔe se vevie ale gbegbe be gae nye anyigbadzidɔlélewo katã ƒe ke, eye be le mɔ aɖeke nu la, ame si si ga le mate ŋu age ɖe dziƒo o. Ah! aleke gbegbe míetsɔa mía ɖokui dea tsɔtsrɔ̃ me kple dzidzɔ enye si!

Mlɔeba la, meyina ƒua dzi ɣesiaɣi abe ƒudzidela ene, le kamedede nyui kple ya dzadzɛ si le ŋgɔgbexɔa ƒe akpata me ta. Elabena abe alesi wòle le xexe sia me ene la, ta ƒe ya bɔ sã wu ya si tso megbe (si fia be ne mèda Pythagoras ƒe nyagbɔgblɔ dzi gbeɖe o), eyata le akpa gãtɔ me la, Commodore si le ʋua ƒe akpa enelia la xɔa eƒe yame ƒe nɔnɔme le mɔ evelia nu tso ƒudzidela siwo le ŋgɔgbekpa dzi gbɔ. Esusu be yegbɔnɛ gbã; gake menye nenemae o. Mɔ ma ke nue ame ɖekawo kplɔa woƒe ŋgɔnɔlawo le nu bubu geɖe me, le ɣeyiɣi ma ke me la, kplɔlawo mesusunɛ boo o. Gake esia tae wònye be esi mese atsiaƒua ƒe ʋeʋẽ enuenu abe asitsala ƒudzidela ene vɔ la, ele be matsɔe ade ta me azɔ be mayi tɔmelãwo léle ƒe mɔzɔzɔ dzi; esiae nye Fates ƒe kpovitɔ makpɔmakpɔ, amesi léa ŋku ɖe ŋunye ɣesiaɣi, eye wònaa avuwo nɔa ŋunye le adzame, eye wòkpɔa ŋusẽ ɖe dzinye le mɔ aɖe si ŋu womate ŋu abu akɔnta le o nu—ate ŋu aɖo eŋu nyuie wu ame bubu ɖesiaɖe. Eye ɖikeke mele eme o be nye tɔmelãwo léle ƒe mɔzɔzɔ sia yiyi, nye Providence ƒe ɖoɖo gã si wowɔ ɣeyiɣi didi aɖee nye sia la ƒe akpa aɖe. Eva abe dometsotso kpui aɖe ƒomevi kple ame ɖeka ko le fefe siwo keke ta wu dome ene. Mebui be sea ƒe akpa sia anya ƒu du nane si le abe esia ene:

“ United States ƒe Dukplɔla ƒe Tiatia Gã si Woʋli Ho. “WHALING VOYAGE BY ONE ISHMAEL. “ƲUƲUƲU LE AFFGHANISTAN.”

Togbɔ be nyemate ŋu agblɔ nusita tututu wònye be fefewɔƒedzikpɔla mawo, siwo nye Fates, tsɔm ɖo anyi ɖe tɔmelãwo léle ƒe mɔzɔzɔ ƒe akpa gbegblẽ sia ta, esime woɖo ame bubuwo ɖe akpa wɔnukuwo dzi le nublanuinya gãwo me, kple akpa kpui siwo le bɔbɔe le nukokoedonamenya siwo me nunya le me, kple akpa siwo doa dzidzɔ na ame le fefenyawo me o—togbɔ be nyemate ŋu agblɔ nusita tututue nye esia o hã; ke hã, fifia si meɖo ŋku nɔnɔmeawo katã dzi la, mesusu be mate ŋu akpɔ tsidzɔƒewo kple susu siwo wotsɔ ayedzedze nam le atsyɔ̃ɖoɖo vovovowo me, ʋãm be madze akpa si mewɔ la wɔwɔ gɔme, hekpe ɖe alesi wòƒoe ɖe nunye be masusu be enye tiatia si do tso nye ŋutɔ nye tiatiawɔblɔɖe si me ameŋkumemakpɔmakpɔ le kple ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃ si me ameŋkumemakpɔmakpɔ le gbɔ la me vie.

Susu siawo dometɔ vevitɔe nye tɔmelã gã la ŋutɔ ƒe susu si ƒo ɖesiaɖe ta. Lã wɔadã aɖe si nye dzesi eye wònye nya ɣaɣla alea nyɔ nye didi be manya nu geɖe. Emegbe atsiaƒu gbemelãwo kple didiƒe ʋĩ siwo me wòmli eƒe ƒukpoa ƒe akpa gãtɔ le; afɔku siwo womate ŋu atsɔ ana o, siwo ŋkɔ mele o, siwo le tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale me; esiawo, kple Patagoniatɔwo ƒe nukpɔkpɔ kple gbeɖiɖi akpe ɖeka ƒe nukunu siwo katã va la kpe ɖe ŋunye meʋuʋu ɖe nye didi ŋu. Ðewohĩ le ŋutsu bubuwo gome la, nu mawo manye ameʋãŋusẽ o; ke nye ya la, wotsɔa ʋuʋudedi mavɔ le fu ɖem nam le nu siwo le didiƒe ʋĩ ta. Melɔ̃a tɔdziʋukuku le atsiaƒu siwo ŋu wode se ɖo dzi, eye meɖina ɖe ƒuta siwo me wowɔa nu manyatalenuwo le. Ne nyemeŋe aɖaba ƒu nusi nyo dzi o la, mekpɔa nu dziŋɔ aɖe dzea sii kaba, eye mate ŋu anɔ hadome kplii kokoko—ɖe woaɖe mɔ nam—elabena enyo ŋutɔ be manɔ xɔlɔ̃wɔwɔ me kple teƒe si ame dzena le ƒe gamenɔlawo katã.

Eyata le nusiawo ta la, woxɔ tɔmelãwo léle ƒe mɔzɔzɔa nyuie; nukunuwɔƒea ƒe tsiɖɔɖɔ ƒe agbo gãwo ʋu, eye le dada gbegblẽ siwo ʋãm yi nye tameɖoɖo gbɔ me la, ame eve kple eve nɔ afima nɔ tsia dzi ge ɖe nye luʋɔ ememetɔ me, tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale ƒe mɔzɔzɔ siwo nuwuwu meli na o, eye le wo dometɔ akpa gãtɔ dome la, gbɔgbɔ vɔ̃ gã ɖeka si do awu ʋlaya, abe sno togbɛ si le yame ene.





TA 2. Kapeti-Akplo.

Metsɔ awudziwui ɖeka alo eve de nye modzakaɖekotoku xoxoa me, tsɔe de nye alɔnu te, eye medze mɔ yi Cape Horn kple Pacific. Esi medzo le du nyui si nye Manhatto xoxoa me la, meva ɖo New Bedford wòsɔ. December ƒe Memleɖa zã aɖee. Dzi ɖe le ƒonye ŋutɔ esi mese be agbalẽvi sue si wotsɔ yi Nantucket la ɖo tɔdziʋu xoxo, eye be mɔ aɖeke si dzi mato aɖo teƒe ma manɔ anyi o, vaseɖe Memleɖa si kplɔe ɖo dzi.

Esi sɔhɛ akpa gãtɔ siwo di be yewoaxɔ tɔmelã léle ƒe vevesesewo kple tohehewo la tɔ ɖe New Bedford sia ke, tso afima be yewoadze yewoƒe mɔzɔzɔa gɔme la, ate ŋu agblɔe hã be nye, le ɖeka gome la, nyemenya kura be mawɔe nenema o. Elabena nye susue nye be maɖo tɔdziʋu le mɔ bubu aɖeke me wu Nantucket-ʋu, elabena nane nyui aɖe si me zitɔtɔ le nɔ nusianu si do ƒome kple ƒukpo xoxo xɔŋkɔ ma ŋu, si do dzidzɔ nam nukutɔe. Gakpe ɖe eŋu la, togbɔ be New Bedford va le tɔmelãwo léle ƒe asitsatsa xɔm vivivi, eye togbɔ be le nya sia me la, Nantucket tsitsi dahe la le megbe nɛ ŋutɔ fifia hã la, ke hã Nantucket ye nye eƒe gbãtɔ gãtɔ—Carthage sia ƒe Taya;—teƒe si Amerika tɔmelã kuku gbãtɔ tsi tre ɖo. Afikae aboriginetɔ mawo siwo nye Red-Men, ƒu du do go le tɔdziʋuwo me gbã be yewoati Leviathan yome negbe Nantucket ko? Eye afikae, negbe Nantucket hã, si sloop sue gbãtɔ ma si me afɔku le, si ƒe akpa aɖe tsɔ kpe gbadzɛ siwo wotsɔ tso duta vɛ—aleae ŋutinyaa gblɔe—tsɔ ƒu gbe ɖe tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale la ŋu, be yeake ɖe ɣeyiɣi si gogo ale gbegbe be woate ŋu atsɔ harpoon si tso bowsprit la ade afɔku me la adze sii?

Fifia esi zã ɖeka, ŋkeke ɖeka, kple zã bubu si kplɔm ɖo le New Bedford, hafi mate ŋu aɖo tɔdziʋu ayi melidzeƒe si woɖo tae la, eva zu nusi ŋu metsi dzi ɖo le afisi wòle be maɖu nu adɔ alɔ̃ le le ɣeyiɣi sia me. Enye zã si ƒe dzedzeme ɖikeke ŋutɔ, ao, zã doviviti kple nublanuinya ŋutɔ, si fa miamiamia eye dzidzɔ aɖeke menɔ eŋu o. Nyemenya ame aɖeke le teƒea o. Metsɔ dzimaɖitsitsi ƒe grapnels ƒo nye kotoku, eye klosalo ʋee aɖewo koe metsɔ vɛ,—Eyata, afisiafi si nàyi, Ismael, megblɔ na ɖokuinye, esi metsi tre ɖe mɔdodo dziŋɔ aɖe titina tsɔ nye akplo ɖe abɔta, eye metsɔ viviti si le dziehe gome sɔ kple viviti si le dzigbe gome—afisiafi si le wò nunya nu la, àte ŋu aƒo nya ta be yeaɖi ɖe eme zã la, nye Ismael lɔlɔ̃tɔ, kpɔ egbɔ be yebia ga home si woaxe, eye mègagblɔe tẽ akpa o.

Metsɔ afɔɖeɖe siwo le tsitre zɔ mɔwo dzi, eye meto dzesi si nye “The Crossed Harpoons” ŋu—gake edze abe exɔ asi akpa eye wònye dzidzɔkpɔkpɔ ene le afima. Yi ŋgɔe la, keklẽ sesẽwo do tso “Sword-Fish Inn” ƒe fesre dzĩ siwo le keklẽm la me, ale gbegbe be edze abe ɖe wòfa sno kple tsikpe siwo yɔ fũu tso aƒea ŋgɔ ene, elabena le teƒe bubu ɖesiaɖe la, vuvɔ si xɔ dzo la mlɔ afɔtoƒe sesẽ aɖe si wotsɔ aŋɔ wɔe si ƒe titrime nye sentimeta ewo,—ɖeɖi te eŋu boŋ, esime meƒo nye afɔ ɖe flinty projections ŋu, elabena tso subɔsubɔdɔ sesẽ, si me vevesese aɖeke mele o me la nye afɔkpawo ƒe afɔkpodziwo nɔ nɔnɔme wɔnublanui aɖe me. Exɔ asi akpa eye wònye dzidzɔ, megasusui ake, eye metɔ vie be makpɔ keklẽ gbadzaa si le ablɔa dzi, eye mase ŋkumeɖobolo siwo le ɖiɖim le eme ƒe gbeɖiɖiwo. Gake yi edzi, Ismael, megblɔe mlɔeba; mèsee oa? midzo le ʋɔtrua ŋgɔ; wò afɔkpa siwo wotsɔ lãfu wɔe la le mɔa tɔm. Eyata meyi edzi. Fifia le dzɔdzɔme nu la, mezɔ mɔ siwo kplɔm yi tsi gbɔ la dzi, elabena ɖikeke mele eme o be amedzrodzeƒe siwo ƒe asi bɔbɔ wu, ne menye esiwo me dzidzɔ le wu o la, nɔ afima.

Ablɔ siwo dzi nu te ɖe ame dzi alea! xɔtunu yibɔwo, ke menye aƒewo o, le axa eveawo dzi, eye akaɖi le afisia kple afima, abe bosomikaɖi si le tsatsam le yɔdo me ene. Le zã ƒe gaƒoƒo sia, si nye kwasiɖaa ƒe ŋkeke mamlɛtɔ dzi la, dua ƒe akpa enelia ma zu ame aɖeke kura o. Gake fifia la, meva ɖo akaɖi aɖe si me dzudzɔ le dodom tso xɔ aɖe si le bɔbɔe eye wòkeke, si ƒe ʋɔtru le ʋuʋu ɖi si kpena la gbɔ. Eƒe dzedzeme ŋumaɖɔɖotɔe nɔ, abe ɖe woɖoe na dukɔa ƒe zazã ene; eyata, esi mege ɖe eme la, nu gbãtɔ si mewɔe nye be mekli aŋɔɖaka aɖe le akpata me. Ha! mesusu be, ha, esi nu suesuesue siwo le dzodzom la ƒo nye nu kloe la, ɖe afi siawo tso du ma si wogbã, si nye Gomora, mea? Gake “Akpa Siwo Wotso Atitsoga,” kple “Yi-Tlã?”—ekema ele be esia nanye “Mɔ̃” ƒe dzesi. Gake mefɔ ɖokuinye eye mese gbe sesẽ aɖe le ememe, metu yi ŋgɔ heʋu ʋɔtru evelia si le eme.

Edze abe Ameyibɔwo ƒe Sewɔtakpekpe gã si bɔbɔ nɔ Tophet ene. Ameyibɔ mo alafa ɖeka trɔ le woƒe fli me be yewoakpɔ ŋkume; eye le go kemɛ dzi la, Ameyibɔ aɖe si nye Ameyibɔ si le tsɔtsrɔ̃ ƒe Mawudɔla aɖe nɔ agbalẽ aɖe ƒom le nuƒolanɔƒe aɖe. Ameyibɔwo ƒe sɔlemexɔe wònye; eye mawunyagblɔla la ƒe mawunyakpukpui la ku ɖe viviti ƒe viviti, kple avifafa kple avifafa kple aɖututu le afima ŋu. Ha, Ismael, meƒo nu le ɣlidodo me, hegbugbɔ yi megbe, Modzaka wɔnublanui le dzesi si nye ‘Mɔ̃a!’ la nu.

Esi menɔ yiyim la, meva ɖo kekeli aɖe si me mekɔ tututu o si medidi tso melidzeƒeawo gbɔ o gbɔ mlɔeba, eye mese ame aɖe si tsi dzodzodzoe ƒe ɣlidodo le yame; eye esi wòfɔ mo dzi la, ekpɔ dzesi aɖe si le ʋuʋum le ʋɔtrua tame si dzi wota nu ɣi aɖe ɖo, si tsi tre ɖi na aŋɔ si le dzɔdzɔe kɔkɔ aɖe si me kuɖiɖi le, eye nya siawo le ete—“The Spouter Inn:—Peter Coffin.”

Aɖaka?—Spouter?—Mesusu be enye nusi vɔ̃ɖi boŋ le kadodo ma koŋ me. Gake wogblɔ be enye ŋkɔ si bɔ le Nantucket, eye mesusu be Petro sia si le afisia nye ʋuʋula tso afima. Esi kekelia dze abe ɖe wòdo viviti ale gbegbe ene, eye teƒea, le ɣeyiɣi ma me, dze abe ɖe ɖoɖoezizi le eme ene, eye atixɔ sue si gbã la ŋutɔ dze abe ɖe woate ŋu atsɔe tso nuto aɖe si wotɔ dzoe ƒe glikpowo me va afisia ene, eye esi dzesi si le ʋuʋum la ƒe ɖiɖi ƒomevi aɖe si me ahedada le la nɔ eŋu la, mesusu be afisia tututue nye teƒe si woate ŋu akpɔ dzeƒe siwo ƒe asi bɔbɔ, kple pea kɔfi nyuitɔ kekeake.

Enye teƒe si mewɔ nuku o—xɔ xoxo aɖe si ƒe nuwuwu nye agbo, le kpɔɖeŋunyagbɔgblɔ nu la, eƒe akpa ɖeka ƒo ƒu, eye wòbɔbɔ nɔ anyi nublanuitɔe. Etsi tre ɖe dzogoe ɖaɖɛ aɖe si me do viviti dzi, afisi ya sesẽ ma si woyɔna be Euroclydon la nɔ ɣli dom vɔ̃ɖi wu tsã le Paulo dahe si wòtsɔ ƒu gbe la ŋu. Ke hã, Euroclydon nye zephyr vivi sesẽ aɖe na amesiame si le xɔa me, eye eƒe afɔwo le dzodoƒea nɔa toast kpoo hena aba. Agbalẽŋlɔla tsitsi aɖe—amesi ƒe agbalẽwo ƒe kɔpi ɖeka kolia si le asinye la, gblɔ be: “Le ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃ le ya sesẽ ma si woyɔna be Euroclydon ŋu me la, ewɔa vovototo wɔnuku aɖe, ne èkpɔe tso ahuhɔ̃e fesre si me vuvɔ le gota katã le, alo ne èlé ŋku ɖe eŋu tso fesre ma si me ka mele o, afisi vuvɔ le le axa eveawo, eye si me yame ƒe Ku koe nye la me glazier.” Nyateƒee, mesusui, esi kpukpui sia va susu me nam—ŋɔŋlɔdzesi yibɔ xoxo, èbu tame nyuie. Ẽ, ŋku siawo nye fesrewo, eye nye ŋutilã siae nye aƒea. Nublanuinya kae nye si wònye be wometɔ te chinks kple crannies o ke hã, eye wotsɔ lint vi aɖe ƒu gbe ɖe afisia kple afima. Gake etsi megbe akpa be woawɔ ŋgɔyiyi aɖeke fifia. Xexeame katã wu enu; copestone la le edzi, eye wotsɔ tasiaɖam tsɔ chips la dzoe ƒe miliɔn ɖeka enye sia. Lazaro dahe si le afima, si le eƒe aɖuwo ƒom ɖe mɔa ƒe kpe si wotsɔ ɖo eƒe akɔtaɖonu ŋu la ŋu, eye wònɔ eƒe ʋuʋudediwo ʋuʋum kple eƒe ʋuʋudedi la, ate ŋu atsɔ avɔ abla to eveawo, eye wòatsɔ bli-ku aɖe ade nu me, eye ke hã ema maxe mɔ na Euroclydon si le ahom sesẽ la nu o. Euroclydon ƒe ŋkɔ! Dives tsitsi aɖe gblɔ, le eƒe seda ƒe agbalẽ xatsaxatsa dzĩ me—(ekpɔ esi biã wu le ema megbe) pooh, pooh! Zã nyui ka gbegbee nye si vuvɔ nɔ wɔwɔm; alesi Orion klẽnae; dziehe ƒe akaɖi kawo gbegbee nye si! Mina woaƒo nu tso woƒe ɣedzeƒetɔwo ƒe dzomeŋɔli ƒe yame ƒe nɔnɔme siwo nye nudzraɖoƒe siwo nɔa anyi ɖaa ŋu; na mɔnukpɔkpɔm be matsɔ nye ŋutɔ nye dzoka xɔxɔwo awɔ nye ŋutɔ nye dzomeŋɔli.

Gake nukae Lazaro susu? Ðe wòate ŋu ado dzo na eƒe asi blɔawo to wo léle ɖe dziehe ƒe akaɖi gãwo gbɔa? Ðe Lazaro madi be yeanɔ Sumatra wu afisia oa? Ðe madi be wòatsɔe mlɔ anyigba ƒe didime nu le equator ƒe fli dzi oa; ẽ, mi mawuwo! ɖiɖi yi do si me dzo le la ŋutɔ me, be yeatsɔ axe mɔ na vuvɔ sia?

Fifia, be Lazaro natsi afima le mɔdodo ƒe kpe si le Dives ƒe ʋɔtru ŋgɔ nu la, esia wɔ nuku wu be woatsɔ tsikpe gã aɖe atɔ ɖe Molucca-toawo dometɔ ɖeka ŋu. Ke hã Dives ŋutɔ hã nɔa agbe abe Czar ene le tsikpexɔ si wotsɔ hũɖeɖe siwo xɔ dzo wɔ me, eye esi wònye ɖokuidziɖuɖu habɔbɔ ƒe zimenɔla ta la, tsyɔ̃eviwo ƒe aɖatsi fafɛwo koe wònona.

Gake blubbering sia maganɔ anyi fifia o, míele a-whale lãm, eye ema sɔ gbɔ ŋutɔ. Mina míaɖe tsikpe siwo le míaƒe afɔ siwo xɔ dzo la dzi, eye míakpɔ teƒe ka ƒomevie “Spouter” sia ate ŋu anye.





TA 3. Spouter-Inn ƒe dɔwɔƒea.

Esi nège ɖe Spouter-Inn ma si ƒe nuwuwu nye gable la, èkpɔ ɖokuiwò le mɔ si keke, si bɔbɔ, si me ʋuʋudedi le si me wainscot xoxowo le, si ɖo ŋku aɖaŋudɔ xoxo aɖewo siwo wobu fɔe ƒe mɔ̃ sesẽwo dzi na ame ɖeka. Amitata gã aɖe ŋutɔ si wotsɔ dzudzɔ ƒoe nyuie, eye wògblẽ le mɔ ɖesiaɖe nu ale gbegbe be le akaɖi siwo mesɔ o siwo dzi nèto kpɔe me la, to nusɔsrɔ̃ veviedodotɔe kple sasrãkpɔ ɖe edzi ɖe ɖoɖo nu, kple aƒelikawo biabia nyuie me koe nàte ŋu ase eƒe taɖodzinu gɔme le mɔ sia mɔ nu. Vɔvɔli kple vɔvɔli gbogbo aɖewo siwo ŋu womebu akɔnta le o ale gbegbe be le gɔmedzedzea me la, èsusu kloe be ɖekakpui nutala aɖe si di vevie be yeaxɔ ŋkɔ, le New England ƒe hagwo ƒe ɣeyiɣia me, dze agbagba be yeaɖe zitɔtɔ ƒe nɔnɔmetata si wotsɔ afakaka wɔe. Gake to ŋugbledede geɖe kple veviedodo me, kple ŋugbledede enuenu zi geɖe, kple vevietɔ to fesre suea tsɔtsɔ ƒu gbe ɖe ʋɔtrua ƒe megbe gome la, èva ƒo nya ta mlɔeba be susu ma tɔgbe, aleke kee wòɖanye gbemelã o, ɖewohĩ masɔ kura o.

Gake nusi tɔtɔ wò wu eye wòtɔtɔ wò wue nye nane si le ʋuʋum le nɔnɔmetata la titina le fli blɔ etɔ̃ siwo me mekɔ o, siwo le tsiatrenu siwo le tsia dzi le amɔwɔʋãnu si ŋkɔ mele o me la ƒe ƒuƒoƒo didi aɖe si me mekɔ o, si me kɔ, si nye dzesi, si le yibɔ me. Nɔnɔmetata si me tsikpe le, si me tsi le, si me ʋuʋudedi le vavã, si sɔ gbɔ be wòana ŋutsu si tsi dzodzodzoe la ƒe susu nadzo. Ke hã ɖe kɔkɔƒe aɖe si seɖoƒe meli na o, si gbɔ wòtso afã kple afã, si womate ŋu asusu o nɔ eŋu si na wò tsikpe ɖe eŋu nyuie, vaseɖe esime nèka atam kple ɖokuiwò le lɔlɔ̃nu faa be yeanya nusi nutata wɔnuku ma fia. Ever and anon a bright, but, alas, deceptive idea would dart you through.—Enye Ƒuyibɔa le zãtitina ƒe ahomya me.—Enye nu vevi eneawo ƒe aʋawɔwɔ si mele dzɔdzɔme nu o.—Enye dzoxɔxɔ si wogbã.—Enye Hyperborean dzomeŋɔli ƒe nukpɔkpɔ.—Enye Ɣeyiɣi ƒe tɔsisi si tsikpe bla la ƒe gbagbã. Gake mlɔeba la, nususukpɔ siawo katã ɖe asi le nane ɖeka ma si nye dzesi si le nɔnɔmetata la titina la ŋu. Ema va nya zi ɖeka, eye susɔeawo katã dze ƒã. Gake dzudzɔ; ɖe meɖi tɔmelã gã aɖe vie oa? leviatan gã la ŋutɔ gɔ̃ hã?

Le nyateƒe me la, nutala la ƒe aɖaŋuwɔwɔ dze abe alea ene: nye ŋutɔ nye nufiafia mamlɛtɔ, si ƒe akpa aɖe nɔ te ɖe ame tsitsi geɖe siwo meɖo dze kplii tso nya sia ŋu ƒe nukpɔsusu siwo woƒo ƒu dzi. Nɔnɔmetata la tsi tre ɖi na Cape-Horner aɖe le ahom sesẽ aɖe me; meli si ƒe gɔmeɖoanyi afã le ʋuʋum le afima eye eƒe ʋuƒo etɔ̃ siwo wogbã ɖeɖeko dzena; eye tɔmelã aɖe si do dziku, si ɖoe be yeadzo le aɖaŋunu la dzi, le nuwɔna gã aɖe si nye be yeaklã ye ɖokui ɖe ati etɔ̃awo ƒe ta dzi.

Wotsɔ atikplɔ kple akplɔ dziŋɔwo ƒe hatsotso aɖe si le abe trɔ̃subɔlawo tɔ ene la ƒo xlã mɔ sia ƒe gli si tsi tre ɖe eŋu la katã. Wotsɔ aɖu siwo le keklẽm siwo ɖi nyitsu ƒe aŋetuwo ɖo wo dometɔ aɖewo ŋu wòbiã; bubuwo hã nye esiwo wotsɔ amegbetɔ ƒe taɖa ƒe ƒuƒoƒo ƒo ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu; eye ɖeka nye esi ƒe nɔnɔme le abe hɛ ene, eye eƒe asiléƒe gã aɖe le ʋuʋum godoo abe akpa si lãtsomɔ̃ si ƒe alɔ didi wɔ le gbe yeye si wotso me ene. Èʋuʋu esi nènɔ ŋku lém ɖe nu ŋu, eye nènɔ ɖokuiwò biam be ameɖula kple gbemelã vɔ̃ɖi kae ate ŋu atsɔ dɔwɔnu dziŋɔ sia si agbã nu dziŋɔ sia ayi kuŋeŋe me hã. Wotsaka kple esiawo nye tɔmelãwo léle ƒe akplɔ xoxo siwo xɔ dzo kple akplɔ siwo katã gbã eye woƒe nɔnɔme gblẽ. Wo dometɔ aɖewo nye aʋawɔnu siwo ŋu ŋutinya le. Nathan Swain tsɔ akplɔ sia si didi tsã, si ƒe abɔta le abe gbemelã ene fifia, wu tɔmelã wuiatɔ̃ le ɣedzeƒe kple ɣeɖoto dome ƒe blaatɔ̃ enye sia. Eye wotsɔ harpoon ma—si le abe korkscrew ene fifia—ƒu gbe ɖe Javan-ƒuwo me, eye ƒumelã aɖe si dzo kplii, si wowu le ƒe geɖe megbe le Cape of Blanco. Ga gbãtɔa ge ɖe asikea gbɔ, eye abe aŋe si meɖea dzi ɖi o si nɔa ŋutsu ƒe ŋutilã me ene la, ezɔ afɔ blaene bliboe, eye mlɔeba la, wokpɔe wòge ɖe akɔta la me.

Ne ètso mɔ sia si me do viviti le, eye nèyi edzi to yon mɔ si ƒe aɖakawo le bɔbɔe dzi—si wotso nusi anya nye titina dzodoƒe gã aɖe si me dzodoƒewo le ƒo xlãe le blema—la, ègena ɖe dutoƒoxɔa me. Teƒe si do viviti wue nye esia, eye atikpo siwo bɔbɔ ɖe anyi le etame, eye ʋuƒo xoxo siwo le fli me le ete ale gbegbe be àsusui kloe be yeatu afɔkpo aɖaŋudɔ xoxo aɖewo ƒe yameʋukuƒe, vevietɔ le zã si me wole ɣli dom alea, esime aɖakaʋu xoxo sia si wotu ɖe dzogoe dzi la ʋuʋu dzikutɔe alea gbegbe. Kplɔ̃ didi aɖe si le bɔbɔe, si le abe agbalẽdzraɖoƒe ene, si dzi wotsɔ ahuhɔ̃e siwo me gbagbã le, si me nu siwo mebɔ o siwo me ke le siwo woƒo ƒu tso xexeame ƒe dzogoe gbadza sia si le didiƒe wu la yɔ fũ la nɔ tsitre ɖe akpa ɖeka. Do aɖe si dze abe ɖe wòdo viviti ene—si nye ahadzraƒea—si nye agbagbadzedze amemabumabutɔe be yeadze ɖusime tɔmelã aɖe ƒe ta ŋu la le dodom tso xɔa ƒe dzogoe si didi wu dzi. Esi menɔ nane ƒom le ɣlidodo me la, nyemenya nusi o, meʋuʋu le egbɔ le gli ŋu, eye emegbe meƒo afu nɛ, amesiame alo nusianu si wòate ŋu anye, be wòazi ɖoɖoe, eye mana mafɔ agado akaɖia ake. Gake eƒe dɔgbo ƒe ŋuɖoɖowo na nye dzi dze eme zi ɖeka be gake mese nye gɔmesese gɔme nyuie o.

“Who-e debel you?”—egblɔ mlɔeba be—“you no speak-e, dam-me, I kill-e.” Eye alea gbɔgblɔ tomahawk si me akaɖi le la te tsitsi ɖe ŋunye le viviti me.

“Aƒetɔ, le Mawu ta, Peter Coffin!” medo ɣli be: “Aƒetɔ! Kpɔ ɖa! Aɖaka! Mawudɔlawo! ɖem!”

“Ƒo nu-e! gblɔ-ee nam amesi-ee be, alo dam-me, mewu-e!” ameɖula la gaƒo nu kple ɣli ake, esime eƒe tomahawk ƒe dzidzedze dziŋɔwo kaka atama ƒe aŋɔ dzodzoeawo ɖe ŋunye vaseɖe esime mesusu be nye aklala atɔ dzo. Gake akpe na dziƒo, le ɣeyiɣi ma me la, aƒetɔa va xɔa me kekeli ɖe asi, eye esi meti kpo tso aba dzi la, meƒu du va egbɔ.

Eko alɔgbɔnu ake gblɔ be: “Mègavɔ̃ fifia o, Queequeg si le afisia magblẽ nu le wò taɖa ɖeka pɛ hã ŋu o.”

Medo ɣli be: “Dzudzɔ wò alɔgbɔnukoko, eye nukata mègblɔ nam be dzomavɔ me akplɔla ma nye ameɖula o?”

“Mesusu be yenyae;—megblɔ na wò be, enye peddlin’ ta le dua me oa?—gake trɔ flukes ake eye nàdɔ alɔ̃. Queequeg, kpɔ afisia ɖa—wò sabbee nye, nye sabbee—wò ŋutsu sia mlɔ wò—wò sabbee?”

“Me sabbee geɖe”—Quequeg ƒo nu kple ɣlidodo, henɔ eƒe pɔmpia ƒom henɔ anyi ɖe aba dzi.

Egblɔ kpee be: “Ège ɖe eme,” eye wòtsɔ eƒe tomahawk la wɔ dzesi nam, eye wòtsɔ awuawo ƒu gbe ɖe axa ɖeka. Menye dukɔmeviwo ƒe mɔnu koe wòwɔ esia ŋutɔŋutɔ o, ke boŋ le dɔmenyonyo kple dɔmenyonunana ƒe mɔnu ŋutɔŋutɔ nu. Metsi tre nɔ ekpɔm ɣeyiɣi kpui aɖe. Le eƒe nutatawo katã ta la, le bliboa me la, enye ameɖula dzadzɛ, si ƒe dzedzeme dze tugbe. Nukae nye zitɔtɔ siawo katã si menɔ wɔwɔm, mesusui le ɖokuinye me—ŋutsua nye amegbetɔ abe nye ke ene: susu geɖe le esi si ta wòavɔ̃m, abe alesi wòle be mavɔ̃e ene. Enyo wu be nàmlɔ ame ɖula si ƒe susu le nu ŋu wu Kristotɔ si mu aha.

Megblɔ be: “Aƒetɔ, gblɔ nɛ be wòaɣla eƒe tomahawk, alo pɔmpi, alo ŋkɔ ɖesiaɖe si nèyɔ la ɖe afima; gblɔ nɛ be wòadzudzɔ atamanono, kpuie ko, eye matrɔ ɖe eŋu kplii. Gake nyemedi be ŋutsu aɖe nano atama le aba dzi kplim o. Afɔku le eme. Gakpe ɖe eŋu la, nugblẽfexeɖoɖo mele ŋunye o.”

Esi wogblɔ nya sia na Queequeg la, ewɔ ɖe edzi enumake, eye wògawɔ dzesi nam bubutɔe be mamlɔ aba dzi—eʋuʋu ɖe axa ɖeka ale gbegbe be wògblɔ be—“Nyemaka asi mia ƒe afɔ ɖeka ŋu o.”

Megblɔ be: “Zã na wò, aƒetɔ, àte ŋu ayi.”

Metrɔ ɖe eme, eye nyemedɔ alɔ̃ nyuie wu le nye agbe me kpɔ o.
