Elabena nu si wòɖu la meɖe eƒe dɔwuame dzi kpɔtɔ boo o, ke boŋ ena wònɔ eme makumaku. Flask susui be ŋutifafa kple dzidzeme dzo le nye dɔgbo me tegbee. Asrafomegãe menye; gake, aleke gbegbe medi be maɖe nyilã xoxo vi aɖe le ŋgɔgbexɔa me, abe alesi mewɔna tsã esime menɔ tɔdziʋua ŋgɔ ene. Dodoɖeŋgɔ ƒe kutsetsewo li fifia; ŋutikɔkɔe ƒe toflokonyenye li: agbe ƒe tagbɔsesẽ hã li! Gakpe ɖe eŋu la, nenye be Pequod-ʋua me ƒudzidela dzro aɖe si do dziku ɖe Flask ŋu le Flask ƒe dziɖuɖumegã ƒe ɖoƒe me la, nusi wòle be ƒudzidela ma nawɔ, be wòakpɔ hlɔ̃biabia geɖe, enye be wòadzo le megbe le fiẽnuɖuɣi, eye wòakpɔ Flask to ʋua ƒe dziŋgɔli ƒe akaɖi me, anɔ anyi bometsitsi kple movidzɔdzɔ le Ahab dziŋɔ la ŋkume.

Fifia, Axab kple eƒe kpeɖeŋutɔ etɔ̃awo ɖo nusi woate ŋu ayɔ be kplɔ̃ gbãtɔ le Pequod ƒe xɔdɔme me. Esi woƒe dzodzo, si yi edzi le ɖoɖo si wogbugbɔ trɔ me vaseɖe woƒe vava megbe la, woɖea avɔ si wotsɔ avɔ wɔe la ɖa, alo ne míagblɔe tututu la, aƒedzikpɔla si ƒe amadede nye aŋutiɖiɖi la gbugbɔe ɖo ɖe ɖoɖo aɖe si wowɔ kabakaba me. Eye emegbe woyɔ harpooneer etɔ̃awo va kplɔ̃ɖoƒea, woawoe nye eƒe domenyinu susɔeawo. Wowɔ ɣeyiɣi kpui aɖe ƒe subɔviwo ƒe xɔ ƒomevi aɖe tso xɔ kɔkɔ si me ŋusẽ le la me.

Nusi to vovo kura na mɔxenu si dzi womate ŋu alɔ̃ ɖo kura o kple dziɖuɖumegã ƒe kplɔ̃a dzi ɖuɖu makpɔmakpɔ si ŋkɔ mele o la, enye mɔɖegbalẽ kple bɔbɔe si metsɔ ɖeke le eme o bliboa, si nye hati ame tsɛ mawo siwo nye harpooneers ƒe demokrasi si me dziɖeleameƒo kloe nɔ. Togbɔ be edze abe woƒe aƒetɔwo, srɔ̃tɔwo, vɔ̃na na woawo ŋutɔ ƒe aɖuwo ƒe hinge ƒe ɖiɖi ene hã la, harpooneers ɖua woƒe nuɖuɖu kple vivisese ale gbegbe be nyatakaka aɖe nɔ anyi tso eŋu. Woɖu nu abe aƒetɔwo ene; wotsɔa nu ʋeʋĩwo yɔa woƒe ƒodowo me abe Indiatɔwo ƒe meliwo ene ŋkeke bliboa katã tsɔa nu ʋeʋĩwo yɔa wo me. Queequeg kple Tashtego ƒe nuɖuɖudzroame si nye dzesi ale gbegbe, be be woatsɔ aɖo teƒe ƒuƒlu siwo nuɖuɖu si woɖu va yi na la, zi geɖe la, Dough-Boy si ƒe amadede nye aŋutiɖiɖi la dina be yeahe dze-gbeɖuɖɔ gã aɖe vɛ, si dze abe ɖe woɖe kpe tso nyitsu sesẽa me ene. Eye ne metsɔ dzo ɖe eŋu o, ne meyi kple hop-skip-and-jump si me aɖaŋu le o la, ekema mɔnu aɖe si me bubu mele o nɔ Tashtego si si dzi wòato ana wòaƒu du to fɔkpa ƒoƒo ɖe eƒe megbe me, le harpoon nu. Eye ɣeaɖeɣi la, Daggoo, si nukokoedonamenya kpata aɖe lé la, kpe ɖe Dough-Boy ƒe ŋkuɖodzinyawo ŋu to exɔ le eɖokui si le ŋutilã me, eye wòtsɔ eƒe ta ƒu gbe ɖe atikutsetse gã aɖe si le ƒuƒlu me, esime Tashtego, si lé hɛ ɖe asi la, dze gotagome la ɖoɖo gɔme do ŋgɔ be yeaɖe ta nɛ. Le dzɔdzɔme nu la, enye ame sue aɖe si tsia dzodzodzoe ŋutɔ, si ʋuʋuna, aƒedzikpɔla sia si ƒe mo le abe abolo ene; abolomela aɖe si do kpo nu kple kɔdzi dɔnɔdzikpɔla aɖe ƒe dzidzimeviwo. Eye nukae nye ameyibɔ dziŋɔ Axab ƒe nukpɔkpɔ si nɔa tsitrenu, kple gbemelã etɔ̃ siawo ƒe sasrãkpɔ siwo me zitɔtɔ le ɣeaɖewoɣi la, Dough-Boy ƒe agbe bliboa nye nuyi ʋuʋu ɖeka si yia edzi ɖaa. Zi geɖe la, ne ekpɔ be wotsɔ nusiwo katã wobia tso saŋkuƒolawo si la, esina le woƒe asi me yia eƒe nuɖaƒe sue si te ɖe eŋu la me, eye wòtsɔa vɔvɔ̃ nɔa ŋku lém ɖe wo ŋu to eƒe ʋɔtru ƒe ʋɔtrutiwo me, vaseɖe esime nusianu wu enu.

Enye nukpɔkpɔ be woakpɔ Queequeg wòanɔ anyi ɖe Tashtego ŋu, anɔ tsitre tsim ɖe eƒe aɖu siwo wotsɔ lãgbalẽ wɔe ŋu na Indiatɔwo tɔ: le atitsoga gome le wo gome la, Daggoo bɔbɔ nɔ anyigba, elabena zikpui aɖe ahe eƒe ta si me amekukuʋu le la ava ʋu siwo bɔbɔ ɖe anyi la gbɔ hafi; le eƒe abɔ alo afɔ gãwo ƒe ʋuʋu ɖesiaɖe me, si wɔnɛ be ʋua ƒe akpa si le bɔbɔe la ʋuʋuna, abe ne Afrika-sɔ yi meli me mɔzɔla ene. Gake le esiawo katã ta la, ameyibɔ gã la melɔ̃a nuɖuɖu o nukutɔe, ne míagblɔe la, evivina o. Edze abe manya wɔ kura be to nu suesuesue mawo siwo ne wotsɔe sɔ kple wo nɔewo me la, ate ŋu alé ŋusẽ si kaka ɖe ame si keke, si nye baronial, eye wòde ŋgɔ alea me la me ɖe asi o ene. Gake ɖikeke mele eme o be, gbemelã bubume sia ɖua nu sesĩe eye wònoa ya ƒe akpa si sɔ gbɔ la vevie; eye to eƒe ŋɔti siwo keke si wotsɔ ƒo xexewo ƒe agbe deŋgɔ la me. Menye to nyilã alo abolo dzi o, wotsɔa ame dzɔtsuwo wɔa alo wotsɔa nunyiame naa woe. Gake Queequeg, eƒe nuyi ƒe ƒoƒo si kuna, si nye ŋutasẽnuwɔwɔ le nuɖuɖu me—gbeɖiɖi nyɔŋu aɖe si sɔ gbɔ—ale gbegbe be Dough-Boy si nɔ ʋuʋum la nɔ ŋku lém ɖe eŋu kloe be yeakpɔe ɖa be aɖu ƒe dzesi aɖewo le ye ŋutɔ yeƒe alɔ siwo le ƒuƒlu la me hã. Eye ne ese Tashtego nɔ ha dzim nɛ be wòaɖe eɖokui afia, be woaɖe eƒe ƒuwo la, aƒedzikpɔla si ƒe susu le bɔbɔe la gbãa nugoe siwo nɔ eŋu le nuɖaƒea, to eƒe lãmetutudɔ si wòxɔ kpata la me. Eye kpe gbadzɛ si harpooneers tsɔna ɖe woƒe kotokuwo me, hena woƒe akplɔwo kple aʋawɔnu bubuwo hã metsɔe o; eye kpe gbadzɛ siwo wotsɔ ƒoa hɛwoe, le fiẽnuɖuɣi, tsɔ ƒoa woƒe hɛwo le fefewɔwɔ me; gbeɖiɖi ma si nye grating melɔ̃na kura be yeana Dough-Boy dahe la ƒe dzi nadze eme o. Aleke wòaŋlɔ be be le yeƒe Ƒukpoa dzi ŋkekeawo me la, Queequeg, le ɖeka gome la, anya ɖi fɔ godoo le amewuwu, xɔlɔ̃wɔwɔ ƒe aɖaŋumadzemadze aɖewo ta. Nyateƒee! Dough-Ŋutsuvi! hard fares yevu nuɖukplɔ̃dzikpɔla si lala ameɖulawo. Menye aŋekotokue wòle be wòatsɔ ɖe eƒe alɔnu o, ke boŋ akɔtaɖonu. Gake le ɣeyiɣi nyui aɖe megbe la, edzɔa dzi nɛ ŋutɔ be dze-ƒudziʋawɔla etɔ̃awo atso adzo; na eƒe to siwo mexɔ se o, siwo ƒoa nu tso gliwo ŋu, woƒe aʋawɔƒuwo katã nɔa ɖiɖim le wo me le afɔɖeɖe ɖesiaɖe me, abe Moortɔwo ƒe scimetar siwo le lãgbalẽgolowo me ene.

Gake, togbɔ be amedzro siawo ɖua nu le xɔa me, eye le ŋkɔ me la, wonɔa afima hã; ke hã, esi wònye be womenɔa anyi kpoo le woƒe numamewo me o ta la, ƒã hafi wonɔa eme negbe le nuɖuɣiwo, kple do ŋgɔ teti na alɔ̃dɔdɔ, ne woto eme yi woawo ŋutɔ ƒe nɔƒe tɔxɛwo.

Le nya ɖeka sia me la, edze abe Axab meto vovo na Amerikatɔ tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale ƒe amegã akpa gãtɔ o ene, amesiwo, abe ƒuƒoƒo ene la, wodina boŋ ɖe nukpɔsusu si nye be le gomenɔamesiwo nu la, melia ƒe akpata nye yewo tɔ boŋ; eye be ameŋububu ɖeɖeko mee woɖe mɔ na ame bubu ɖesiaɖe, ɣesiaɣi, le afima. Ale be le nyateƒe me la, woate ŋu agblɔ be Pequod-ʋua ƒe srɔ̃tɔwo kple haƒolawo nɔ agbe le ʋua godo wu be wonɔ eme wòsɔ wu. Elabena esi woge ɖe eme la, enye nane abe alesi ablɔdzi ʋɔtru gena ɖe aƒe me ene; tɔtrɔ yi eme hena ɣeyiɣi kpui aɖe, gake woatrɔe ado le esi kplɔe ɖo me; eye abe nusi nɔa anyi ɖaa ene la, enɔa yame. Eye womebu nu geɖe hã le esia me o; le ʋua me la, zɔhɛ aɖeke menɔ anyi o; le hadome la, womate ŋu aɖo Axab gbɔ o. Togbɔ be ŋkɔe wotsɔe de Kristodukɔa ƒe amexexlẽ me hã la, egakpɔtɔ nye amedzro na Kristodukɔa. Enɔ xexeame, abe alesi Grisly Bears mamlɛtɔ nɔ Missouri si va nɔ anyi ene. Eye abe esime Dzomeŋɔli kple Dzomeŋɔli dzo ene la, Logan gbemelã ma si tso ave me, si ɖi eɖokui ɖe ati aɖe ƒe do me, nɔ agbe le dzomeŋɔli le afima, nɔ eya ŋutɔ ƒe afɔwo nom; eyata le eƒe tsitsi vɔ̃ɖi, si le ɣli dom me la, Axab ƒe luʋɔ, si wotu ɖe eƒe ŋutilã ƒe agado si me do le la me, le afima nɔ eƒe viviti ƒe afɔkpodzi ƒoɖiwo ɖum!





TA 35. Mast-Ta la.

Yame ƒe nɔnɔme si vivina wu mee, le tɔtrɔ si sɔ kple ƒudzidela bubuawo me la, nye tɔdziʋu ƒe ta gbãtɔ va ƒo xlãe.

Le Amerika tɔmelã siwo tsɔa tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whalemen akpa gãtɔ gome la, amewo nɔa tɔdziʋua ƒe ta siwo le tɔdziʋua me la me le ɣeyiɣi ɖeka kloe me esime tɔdziʋua dzona le eƒe melidzeƒea; togbɔ be ate ŋu anye kilometa akpe wuiatɔ̃, kple esi wu nenema, be wòaɖo tɔdziʋu hafi aɖo eƒe tɔdziʋudzeƒe si sɔ hã. Eye ne, le ƒe etɔ̃, ene, alo atɔ̃ ƒe mɔzɔzɔ megbe la, ele aƒe gogom kple nusianu si le ƒuƒlu le eme—míagblɔ be, nugoe ƒuƒlu gɔ̃ hã—la, ekema, wotsɔa amewo ɖoa eƒe mast-tawo ŋu vaseɖe mamlɛtɔ dzi; eye menye vaseɖe esime eƒe yamenutomeyimɔ̃wo naɖo melidzeƒea ƒe togbɛwo dome o la, meɖea asi le mɔkpɔkpɔ si nye be yeagalé tɔmelã ɖeka bubu ŋu kura o.

Fifia, esi wònye be mast-ta siwo le tsitrenu ƒe asitsatsa, le ƒuta alo le tsia dzi, nye blematɔ ŋutɔ eye wòdoa dzidzɔ na ame ta la, mina míakekee ɖe enu le afisia vaseɖe afi aɖe. Mexɔe se, be ame gbãtɔ siwo tsi tre ɖe mast-ta ŋue nye Egiptetɔ xoxoawo; elabena, le nye numekukuwo katã me la, nyemekpɔ ɖeke do ŋgɔ na wo o. Elabena togbɔ be ɖikeke mele eme o be wo tɔgbuiwo, Babel-tulawo, ɖoe be yewoato yewoƒe xɔ kɔkɔa dzi aɖo ta si kɔkɔ wu le Asia katã, alo Afrika hã; ke hã (hafi wotsɔ agbatsɔʋu mamlɛtɔ da ɖe edzi) abe alesi woate ŋu agblɔ be woƒe kpe gã ma si wotsɔ kpe xɔasiwo wɔe la yi ɖato ʋuƒoa gbɔ ene, le Mawu ƒe dziku ƒe ahom dziŋɔ la me; eyata míate ŋu atsɔ Babel-tula siawo aɖo nɔƒe gbãtɔ wu Egiptetɔwo o. Eye be Egiptetɔwo nye dukɔ si me tsitrenu siwo ƒe ta le abe atrakpui ene, nye nya si wotu ɖe dzixɔse si bɔ ɖe blematomenukulawo dome dzi, be ɣletiviŋutinunya ƒe taɖodzinuwo tae woɖo piramid gbãtɔwo ɖo: nufiafia si dzi xɔ mawo ƒe akpa eneawo katã ƒe wɔwɔme tɔxɛ si le abe atrakpui ene ɖo kpee ɖekaɖeka; si me ɣletiviŋutinunyala xoxo mawo toa woƒe afɔwo ƒe kɔkɔƒe didi wɔnukuwo dzi, eye wotoa dzi yia ta la dzi, eye wodzia ha na ɣletivi yeyewo; abe alesi egbegbe meli ƒe ŋkuléleɖenuŋulawo le ha dzim na ƒutsotsoe, alo tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale si le dzedzem ko ene gɔ̃ hã. Le Saint Stylites, Kristotɔ saɖagaxɔmenɔla xɔŋkɔ si nɔ anyi le blema, amesi tu kpe sɔti kɔkɔ aɖe nɛ le gbedadaƒo eye wòzã eƒe agbe ƒe akpa mamlɛa katã ɖe eƒe ta dzi, tsɔa lãnu kɔa eƒe nuɖuɖu tso anyigba; le eya me la, ame aɖe si tsi tre ɖe mast-ta dzi vɔvɔ̃manɔmee ƒe kpɔɖeŋu ɖedzesi aɖe le mía si; ame si mele be afu alo vuvɔ, tsidzadza, tsikpe, alo tsikpe nanya le eƒe nɔƒe o; gake esi wòdze ŋgɔ nusianu dzinɔameƒotɔe vaseɖe mamlɛtɔ dzi la, eku ŋutɔŋutɔ le eƒe ɖoƒe. Le egbegbe standers-of-mast-heads gome la, ƒuƒoƒo si me agbe mele o koe le mía si; kpe, gayibɔ, kple akɔbli ŋutsu dzrowo; amesiwo, togbɔ be woate ŋu adze ŋgɔ ahom sesẽ aɖe nyuie hã la, wogabi ɖe hadzidzi si nye haƒoƒo ƒe asitsatsa me kura ne woke ɖe nukpɔkpɔ wɔnuku aɖeke ŋu o. Napoleon hã li; amesi, le Vendome ƒe sɔti la tame, tsi tre kple abɔwo ƒo ƒu, anɔ yame abe afɔ alafa ɖeka blaatɔ̃ ene; ŋumaɖɔɖotɔe, fifia, amesi ɖua dzi le ʋua ƒe akpata siwo le ete la dzi; eɖanye Louis Philippe, Louis Blanc, alo Louis Abosam o. Washington Gãtɔ hã le tsitre ɖe eƒe sɔti gã kɔkɔ si le Baltimore la dzi, eye abe Hercules ƒe sɔtiwo dometɔ ɖeka ene la, eƒe sɔti la de dzesi amegbetɔ ƒe gãnyenye ƒe teƒe ma si ame ʋɛ aɖewo siwo kuna ayi ŋgɔe. Admiral Nelson, hã, le tu-ga ƒe capstan dzi, le tsitre ɖe eƒe mast-ta dzi le Trafalgar Square; eye ɣesiaɣi si London ƒe dzudzɔ ma do viviti wu la, wogaɖea dzesi kokoko be kalẽtɔ ɣaɣla aɖe le afima; elabena afisi dzudzɔ le la, ele be dzo nanɔ. Gake Washington gã la, alo Napoleon, alo Nelson, maɖo tsikpe ɖeka pɛ hã ŋu tso ete o, aleke kee woƒe aɖaŋuɖoɖowo yɔe aɖaʋatɔe be yewoadze xɔlɔ̃ ʋuɖoƒe siwo ƒe susu ɖa le wo ŋu siwo dzi wole ŋku lém ɖo o; ke hã woate ŋu asusui be woƒe gbɔgbɔwo gena ɖe etsɔme ƒe ahom sesẽa me, eye woƒoa nu tso ƒutsotsoe siwo wòle be woaƒo asa na kple kpe siwo ŋu wòle be woaƒo asa na ŋu.

Ðewohĩ adze abe ɖe mesɔ be woatsɔ anyigba dzi tɔ siwo ƒe ta le abe ƒuta ene akpe ɖe atsiaƒu dzi tɔ ŋu le go aɖeke me o; gake be le nyateƒe me la, mele nenema o la dze ƒã le nusi ŋu Obed Macy, si nye Nantucket ƒe ŋutinyaŋlɔla ɖeka kolia, bu akɔnta le la me. Obed si dze la gblɔ na mí, be le tɔmelã siwo woyɔna be whale lãɖeɖe ƒe ɣeyiɣi gbãtɔwo me la, hafi wodzea meliwo edziedzi tsɔ tia fefea yome la, ƒukpo ma dzi tɔwo tua spar kɔkɔwo ɖe atsiaƒua to, afisi ŋkulélawo toa cleats siwo wotsɔ ati ƒoe dzi lia yia, nane si xeviwo yia dziƒoxɔ dzi le kokloxɔ me. Ƒe ʋɛ aɖewoe nye esi va yi la, New Zealand ƒe Bay tɔmelã siwo tsɔa tɔmelãwo tsɔa lãwo ƒua gbe la da asi ɖe ɖoɖo sia ke dzi, eye esi woɖɔ fefea la, wona nyanya tɔdziʋu siwo me ame le klalo siwo te ɖe ƒuta ŋu. Gake kɔnu sia megali fifia o; emegbe míetrɔ ɖe mast-ta ɖeka si sɔ ŋu, si nye tɔmelã si woyɔna be whale-meli si le ƒua dzi tɔ. Wona ame nɔa mast-ta etɔ̃awo ŋu tso ɣedzeƒe vaseɖe ɣeɖoto; ƒudzidelawo nɔa woƒe tɔtrɔ edziedzi (abe alesi wole ʋua ƒe ʋua ƒe ʋukuku me ene), eye woɖea gbɔdzɔe na wo nɔewo gaƒoƒo eve ɖesiaɖe. Le teƒe xɔdzowo ƒe yame ƒe fafa me la, mast-ta vivina ŋutɔ; ao, na ŋutsu si dea ŋugble le drɔ̃e me la, edoa dzidzɔ na ame. Afimae nètsi tre ɖo, afɔ alafa ɖeka le xɔtaxɔ siwo me ɖoɖoezizi le la tame, hele afɔ ɖem le gogloƒea abe ɖe ʋuƒo gãwo nye atikpo gãwo ene, esime le wò te kple wò afɔwo dome, le kpɔɖeŋunyagbɔgblɔ nu la, ƒua me lã wɔadã gãtɔwo kekeake le tsi ƒum, abe alesi meliwo zɔa mɔ tsã le Kolose xɔŋkɔa ƒe afɔkpawo dome le Rhodes xoxoa gɔ̃ hã ene. Afimae nètsi tre ɖo, èbu le atsiaƒu ƒe tsiatretsitsi si seɖoƒe meli na o me, eye naneke meʋuʋu o negbe ƒutsotsoeawo ko.
