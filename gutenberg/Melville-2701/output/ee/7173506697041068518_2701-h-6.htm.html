AGBALẼ I. ( Folio ), TA I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Whale sia, le blema Eŋlisiawo dome si woyɔna le mɔ si me mekɔ o nu be Trumpa whale, kple Physeter whale, kple Anvil Headed whale, nye Franseawo ƒe Cachalot fifia, kple Germanytɔwo ƒe Pottsfich, kple Macrocephalus of the Long Words. Ðikekemanɔmee la, eyae nye xexeamenɔla gãtɔ kekeake; tɔmelã si dzi ŋɔ wu le tɔmelã siwo katã wodo goe dome; esi lolo wu le eƒe akpa dzi; eye mlɔeba la, enye esi xɔ asi wu le asitsatsa me sãsãsã; esi wònye nuwɔwɔ ɖeka kolia si me wokpɔa nu xɔasi ma, si nye spermaceti, tsoe ta. Woakeke eƒe etɔxɛnyenyewo katã ɖe enu, le teƒe bubu geɖe. Eƒe ŋkɔ koŋue wòle be mawɔ fifia. Ne míebu eŋu le gbeŋutinunya me la, susu mele eme o. Ƒe alafa aɖewoe nye esi va yi, esime womenya Sperm whale la kura kloe le eya ŋutɔ ƒe amenyenye si sɔ me o, eye esime wokpɔ eƒe ami le vo me tso tɔmelã siwo tsi ƒu me la me; le ŋkeke mawo me la, adze abe ame geɖe susui be wokpɔa spermaceti tso nuwɔwɔ aɖe si sɔ kple esi woyɔna ɣemaɣi le England be Greenland alo Right Whale me ene. Enye susua hã, be spermaceti sia ke nye Greenland Whale ƒe nukokoedoname ma si doa ŋusẽ kabakaba si nya la ƒe gbeɖiɖi gbãtɔ ɖe fia ŋutɔŋutɔ. Le ɣeyiɣi mawo hã me la, spermaceti mebɔ kura o, womezãnɛ na kekeli o, ke boŋ abe ami kple atike ene ko. Ðeko wòle be woaxɔe tso atikedzralawo gbɔ esi nèƒlea rhubarb ounce ɖeka egbea. Esi, abe alesi mesusui ene la, le ɣeyiɣi aɖe megbe la, wova nya spermaceti ƒe nɔnɔme ŋutɔŋutɔ la, eƒe ŋkɔ gbãtɔ gakpɔtɔ nɔ edzralawo si; ɖikeke mele eme o be be wòana asixɔxɔ si le eŋu nadzi ɖe edzi to nukpɔsusu aɖe si ɖe dzesi ŋutɔ le eƒe ʋɛnyenye ŋu alea gbegbe dzi. Huzza Porpoise si woɖu nyuie eye wòlolo la ana ami nyui galɔn nyui ɖeka nasu asiwò. Gake tsi nyui si le tsɛ si woɖena tso eƒe aɖuwo me la xɔ asi ŋutɔ. Atsyɔ̃ɖonuwɔlawo kple gaƒoɖokuiwɔlawo biae. Ƒudzidelawo tsɔe da ɖe woƒe hones dzi. Porpoise lã nye nuɖuɖu nyui, ènya. Ðewohĩ meva susu me na wò kpɔ be akpɔkplɔ aɖe nado go o. Nyateƒee, eƒe nugbɔ le sue ale gbegbe be womete ŋu dea dzesii bɔbɔe tututu o. Gake ɣebubuɣi si mɔnukpɔkpɔ asu asiwò la, lé ŋku ɖe eŋu; eye emegbe àkpɔ Sperm whale gã la ŋutɔ le nɔnɔmetata sue aɖe me.

AGBALẼ III. ( Duodecimo ), TA II. ( Algerine Porpoise ).—Adzodala aɖe. Gbedadaƒo ŋutɔ. Mesusu be Pasifik-ƒua dzi koe wokpɔnɛ le. Elolo vie wu Huzza Porpoise, gake eƒe akpa gãtɔ nye esi wowɔna le xexeame katã. Do dziku nɛ, eye wòabla ɖe ƒumelã gã aɖe ŋu. Meɖiɖi ɖe anyi nɛ zi geɖe, gake nyemekpɔe wolée haɖe o.

AGBALẼ III. ( Duodecimo ), TA III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Amegãxi ƒomevi gãtɔ kekeake; eye Pasifik-ƒua dzi koe wokpɔnɛ le, le alesi wonyae nu. Eŋlisigbe me ŋkɔ ɖeka kolia si wotsɔ yɔe vaseɖe fifiae nye tɔƒodelawo tɔ—Right-Whale Porpoise, le nɔnɔme si me wokpɔnɛ koŋ le Folio ma ƒe nuto me ta. Le eƒe nɔnɔme gome la, eto vovo tso Huzza Porpoise gbɔ le mɔ aɖe nu, elabena eƒe lolome medea goglo o eye wòdoa dzidzɔ na ame; nyateƒee, eƒe nɔnɔme le dzadzɛ ŋutɔ eye wòle abe aƒetɔ ene. Asike aɖeke mele eƒe akɔta o (wole porpoise bubu akpa gãtɔ si), asike lɔlɔ̃tɔe le esi, eye Indiatɔwo ƒe ŋku siwo me seselelãme le siwo ƒe amadede nye hazel le esi. Gake eƒe nu si me lãgbalẽ le la gblẽa nusianu. Togbɔ be eƒe akɔta bliboa vaseɖe eƒe axadzi ƒe asikewo nye sable goglo aɖe, ke hã liƒo aɖe, si to vovo abe dzesi si le meli ƒe ʋuƒo me, si woyɔna be “akɔta si le keklẽm” ene hã la, fli ma ƒo xlãe tso ke vaseɖe eƒe megbe, eye amadede vovovo eve le eŋu, yibɔ le etame eye wòle ɣie le ete. Yevu la nye eƒe ta ƒe akpa aɖe, kple eƒe nu bliboa, si na wòdze abe ɖe wòsi le nuvlowɔwɔ ƒe sasrãkpɔ aɖe si yi nuɖuɖukotoku aɖe me teti koe nye ema ene. Akpa si me nu gbegblẽ le wu eye wònye nuɖuɖu si me nunyiame le wu! Eƒe ami sɔ kple tɔmelã si woyɔna be porpoise si bɔ la ŋutɔ.

Le DUODECIMO godo la, ɖoɖo sia meyia edzi o, elabena Porpoise nye tɔmelã suetɔ kekeake le tɔmelãwo dome. Le etame la, Leviatan siwo katã ɖe dzesi la le asiwò. Gake tɔmelã siwo ŋu kakaɖedzi mele o, siwo sina, siwo ƒe nya wogblɔna afã kple afã la ƒe ha aɖe li, siwo, abe Amerikatɔ tɔmelãkula ene la, menya le ŋkɔ nyui si le wo si ta, gake menye nye ŋutɔe o. Maxlẽ wo to woƒe ŋkɔ siwo wozãna le ŋgɔgbexɔ me dzi; elabena ɖewohĩ xexlẽdzesi ma tɔgbe ate ŋu anye nusi ŋu asixɔxɔ le na numekula siwo ava va, amesiwo ate ŋu awu nusi le asinye le afisia gake medze egɔme la nu. Ne woalé tɔmelã siwo gbɔna dometɔ aɖe, le esia megbe ahade dzesii la, ekema woate ŋu atsɔe ade Nuɖoanyi sia me bɔbɔe, le eƒe Folio, Octavo, alo Duodecimo ƒe lolome nu:—The Bottle-Nose Whale; Junk Whale si woyɔna be Junk Whale la; Whale si ƒe ta le abe Pudding ene; Cape Whale si woyɔna be Cape Whale ƒe lã; Whale si Le Kplɔla; Cannon Whale si woyɔna be Cannon Whale ƒe lã; Scragg Whale si woyɔna be Whale si woyɔna be Whale; Akɔbli si woyɔna be Coppered Whale; Elephant Whale si woyɔna be Elephant Whale ƒe lã; si nye Iceberg Whale si woyɔna be Whale; Quog Whale si woyɔna be Whale; Blue Whale (ƒumelã si woyɔna be Blue Whale); etc. Woate ŋu ayɔ tɔmelã siwo ŋu kakaɖedzi mele o ƒe ŋkɔ bubuwo tso Icelandtɔwo, Hollandtɔwo, kple Eŋlisitɔwo ƒe dziɖuɖumegã xoxowo gbɔ, eye woyra wo kple ŋkɔ madzeto ƒomevi ɖesiaɖe. Gake meɖea wo ɖa be womegahiã kura o; eye mate ŋu awɔ naneke kura be woake ɖi wo le gbeɖiɖi dzrowo ta, siwo yɔ fũ kple Leviatan-subɔsubɔ, gake womefia naneke o.

Mlɔeba: Wogblɔ le gɔmedzedzea me, be nuɖoanyi sia manɔ afisia o, eye zi ɖeka, wòade blibo o. Màte ŋu akpɔe kɔte be mewɔ nye nya dzi o. Gake fifia megblẽ nye lãmenugbagbeviŋutinunya ƒe Ðoɖoa ɖe tsitrenu alea womewu enu o, abe alesi wogblẽ Cologne Sɔlemexɔ gã la ɖi ene gɔ̃ hã, eye crane la gakpɔtɔ le tsitre ɖe xɔ kɔkɔ si womewu enu haɖe o la tame. Elabena woate ŋu awu xɔtuɖaŋu suewo nu to woƒe xɔtuɖaŋunyala gbãtɔwo dzi; gãwo, vavãwo, gblẽa copestone la ɖi na dzidzimeviwo ɣesiaɣi. Mawu xe mɔ nam be nyemawu naneke nu gbeɖe o.
