BUNG 62. The Dart.

Bung hnuhnung bera thil thleng chungchanga thumal.

Ngawpui hman dan danglam zel ang chuan, whale-boat chu lawng aṭangin a nawr chhuak a, headsman emaw whale-killer emaw chu temporary steersman atan a hmang a, harpooneer emaw whale-fastener emaw chuan oar hmasa ber, harpooneer-oar tia hriat chu a hrual a ni.
