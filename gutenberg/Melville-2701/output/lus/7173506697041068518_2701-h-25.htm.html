BUNG 135. The Chase.—Ni thumna.

Ni thumna zing chu a fair leh thar a, vawi khat chu fore-mast-head-a zan lama mi pakhat chauh awm chu ni êng thlirtu mipui tam tak, mast tin leh spar zawng zawng deuhthaw-a dot-tute chuan an ti hahdam leh ta a ni.

“D’ye hmu em?” Ahaba chuan a ti a; mahse, whale chu hmuh tur a la awm lo.

“A tihsual theih lohnaah erawh chuan; mahse, chu harh chu zawm la, chu chu a ni mai. Chutah chuan helm rawh; nghet takin, i kal ang zelin, i kal tawh ang. Ni duhawm tak a va ni leh ta! khawvel siam thar, vantirhkohte tan thlasik in atan siam ni ta se, vawiin zing khan an tan hawn hmasak ber ni ta se, chu khawvel chungah chuan ni mawi zawk a lo thleng thei lo vang. Hetah hian ngaihtuahna chaw a awm a, Ahaba ngaihtuah hun a nei a; mahse, Ahaba chuan a ngaihtuah ngai lo; a hriat, a hriat, a hriat chauh a ni; chu chu mihring thi thei tan chuan a tingling tawk! ngaihtuah tur chuan audacity a ni. Chu dikna leh chanvo chu Pathian chauhvin a nei a ni. Ngaihtuahna hi, a nih loh leh a ni tur a ni, coolness leh calmness; tin, kan thinlung rethei tak takte chu a na a, chutiang tur chuan kan thluak rethei tak takte chu a na lutuk bawk. Tin, chuti chung pawh chuan, ka thluak chu a reh hle niin ka ngai fo thin—frozen calm, he luruh hlui hi a chhe nasa hle a, a chhunga thil awmte chu ice-ah a chantir glass ang maiin, a khur ta a ni. Tin, chuti chung pawh chuan he sam hi tunah chuan a lo thang lian zel a; he hun hi a lo thang lian a, lumna chuan a hring chhuak ngei tur a ni; mahse, ni lo, chutiang chi common grass ang chi chu khawi hmunah pawh, Greenland ice leia clefts inkar emaw, Vesuvius lava-ah emaw a lo thang lian ang. Ramsa thlipuiin a nuai dan; an whip it about me, chu chu split sails shreds te chuan an vawn tlat tossed ship chu an rawn beng a. Thli tenawm tak chu rinhlelh rual lohvin he thil hi jail kawngkhar leh cell, leh damdawi in ward hrang hrang kal tlangin a lo tleh tawh a, boruak a luh tir tawh a, tunah chuan hetah hian vun vun ang maia sual nei lovin a lo thawk ta a ni. Out upon it!—a bawlhhlawh tawh. Thli chu kei ni ila, chutiang khawvel sual, hrehawm tak chu ka thawk tawh lo ang. Khawi emaw laiah chuan lungpui pakhatah ka tlan phei ang a, chutah chuan ka slink ang. Tin, chuti chung pawh chuan, 'tis thil ropui leh huaisen tak, thli! tuin nge hneh tawh? Inhnek apiangah vuakna hnuhnung ber leh na ber a nei thin. Run tilting at it, nangmah erawh chu tlan pel rawh. Ha! thli hlauhawm tak, mipa saruak tak takte rawn nuai, mahse vawi khat mah vuak pawh dawng thei lo tur. Ahaba pawh hi thil huaisen zawk a ni— chu aia thil ropui zawk . Would now the thli mahse taksa nei; mahse, mihring thi thei tithinurtu leh tithinur bertu zawng zawngte chu, heng zawng zawng hi taksa nei lo an ni a, mahse thil anga taksa nei lo chauh an ni a, agent angin an nei lo. A danglam bik ber, a finthlak ber, aw, a malicious ber! Tin, chuti chung pawh chuan, ka sawi nawn leh a, tunah chuan ka tiam a ni, thlipuiah hian thil ropui leh khawngaihna zawng zawng a awm tih hi. Heng Trade Winds lum tak takte hi, a tlem berah chuan, van thianghlim takah chuan a rawn thawk nghal zung zung a, chak leh nghet tak, chak taka ngilnei takin; leh an chhinchhiahna atang chuan veer lo, mahse tuifinriat baser currents te chu an inher leh tack thei a, leia Mississippies chak ber berte chu an tlan chak hle a, an tlan kual vel a, a tawpah chuan khawiah nge an kal dawn tih an hre chiang lo. Tin, chatuan Poles te pawhin! heng Trades ngei hian ka lawng tha tak chu direct takin a thlawh tir a; heng Trades te, emaw, chutiang chi emaw—thil danglam thei lo tak, leh chak ang maia khat, ka keled soul chu a rawn thlawh tir ta! To it! Aloft ah chuan! Eng nge d’ye hmuh?”

“Engmah lo, ka pu.”

"Engmah! leh chawhnu lam a hnai tawh bawk! Doubloon chu a kal a-begging! Ni chu hmu rawh! Aye, aye, chutiang chu a ni ngei ang. Ka oversail tawh a ni. Engtin nge, bul tanna i neih? Aye, tunah chuan min rawn um ta a; kei ni lovin, ani —chu chu a tha lo; Kei pawhin ka hre ve mai thei. Mawl! line-te—a towing harpoon-te chu. Aye, aye, nizan khan ka tlan pel tawh. Lam! lam! Lo chhuk rawh u, nangni zawng zawng, mahse regular look outs! Man the braces chu!”

Steering a tih ang khan thli chu Pequod-a quarter-ah a awm deuh tawh a, chuvang chuan tunah chuan a hnunglam hawiin, braced lawng chuan thlipui chungah nasa takin a kal a, amah ngeiin a white wake-ah cream chu a rechur leh ta a ni.

“Thli lakah chuan tunah chuan open jaw atan a steer a ni,” Starbuck chuan a rilruin a ti mur mur a, main-brace thar chhuah chu rel chungah a coil a. “Pathianin min vawng reng a, mahse ka ruhte chu ka chhungah a hnim tawh a, chhungril atang chuan ka tisa chu a hnim tawh bawk. Ka Pathian thu ka awih lohzia hi ka ringhlel sual a ni!”

“Min hrual turin ding rawh!” Ahaba chuan hempen bawm lam pan chuan a rawn ti a. “Kan hmu thuai tur a ni.”

“Aye, aye, sir,” tiin Starbuck chuan Ahaba thupek chu a ti nghal a, vawi khat chu Ahab chu a sang chho leh ta a.

Tunah chuan darkar khat pum a liam ta a; kum tam tak chhung chu rangkachak hneh tawh. Hun ngei pawh chuan tunah chuan thawk rei tak tak chu keen suspense nen a chelh ta a ni. Mahse a tawpah chuan weather bow atanga point thum vel a hlaah chuan Ahaba chuan spout chu a sawifiah leh a, chutah chuan mast-head pathum atang chuan mei lei chuan a aw chhuah ang maiin au thawm pathum a rawn sang chho ta a.

“Hmai atanga hmai thleng ka hmu che, tun vawi thumna atan, Moby Dick! Chutah chuan deck-ah chuan!—brace sharper up; thli mitmengah chuan a rawn luah khat ta a. A la hla lutuk a, a tlahniam thei lo, Mr. Starbuck. Sail te chu a nghing! Chu helmsman chungah chuan top-maul hmangin ding rawh! Chuvangin, chutiang chuan; a zin chak a, ka chhuk a ngai a ni. Mahse, hetah hian tuipuiah hian round look tha tak pakhat ka nei leh ang; chutiang tur chuan hun a awm. Hmuh hlui, hlui tak, mahse engtin emaw tak chuan a naupang em em a; aye, leh ka hmuh hmasak ber atang khan mitmeng pawh ka thlak lo, tlangval, Nantucket-a sand-hills atanga lo kal! Chutiang bawk chu!—chutiang bawk!—Nova tan pawh keimah ang bawkin. Leeward lam pan chuan soft shower a awm. Chutiang leewardings duhawm tak chu! Khawi emaw laiah an hruai tur a ni—ram pangngai ni lo, kutphah aiin palmy zawkah. Leeward a ni! white whale chu chutiang lam chuan a kal a; thli lam hawi la, chuti a nih chuan; a tha zawk chu a bitterer quarter. Mahse, good bye, good bye, mast-head hlui! Hei hi eng nge ni?—green? aye, heng warped crack-ah hian mosses te tak te te. Chutiang green weather stains chu Ahaba lu-ah a awm lo! Tunah chuan mihring tar leh matter te hi a danglamna a awm ta. Mahse aye, old mast, kan pahnih hian kan tar dun a; kan hulls-ah erawh chuan kan ri lo em ni, ka lawng? Aye, minus a ke, chu chu a ni mai. Vanah chuan he thing thi hian kawng tinrengah ka tisa nung aia tha zawk a nei a ni. Ka tehkhin thei lo; tin, thing thi aṭanga siam lawng ṭhenkhat chu pa pawimawh ber berte thil pawimawh ber aṭanga siam mihringte nunna aiin an dam rei tih ka hria bawk. Eng nge chu chu a sawi? ka hmaah a la kal tur a ni, ka pilot; tin, hmuh leh tur a la ni lo em ni? Mahse khawiah nge? Chu stairs tawp nei lo chu ka chhuk ang tih ngaihtuah chuan tuifinriat hnuaiah mit ka nei ang em? tin, zankhuain a hnen atangin lawngin ka kal a, a pilna hmun apiangah. Aye, aye, mi tam zawk ang bawkin nangmah leh nangmah i khawih chungchangah thudik rapthlak tak i sawi a, Aw Parsee; nimahsela, Ahaba, chutah chuan i silai chu a tlai lo. Good-bye, mast-head—whale chu uluk takin en reng ang che, ka awm loh chhung hian. Naktuk, ni lo, zanin, chutah chuan white whale chu a lu leh a hmui hmanga khin a mut hunah chuan kan inbia ang.”

Thu a pe a; tin, a chhehvel chu a la en reng a, boruak dum hring hring hring hring hring karah chuan deck-ah chuan a hniam chho ta zel a.

A hun takah lawngte chu an chhuk thla ta a; mahse, a shallop hnung lama ding lai chuan Ahaba chu a chhukna hmunah chuan a zuang thla mai a, a kawppui hnenah chuan a kut a phar a,—chu chuan deck-a tackle-rope pakhat chu a keng a—a chawlhsan ta a ni.

“Starbuck!”

"Ka pu?"

“A vawi thumna atan ka thlarau lawng chu he zinkawngah hian a intan ta a, Starbuck.”

“Aye, ka pu, chutiang chuan i nei ang.”

“Lawng thenkhat chu an lawng chawlhna atang hian an kal a, chumi hnuah chuan an bo fo thin, Starbuck!”

“Thudik, ka pu: thudik lungchhiatthlak ber.”

“Mipa thenkhat chu ebb tide-ah an thi a; ṭhenkhat chu tui hniam takah; ṭhenkhat chu tuilianin a khat laiah;—chuan tunah chuan billow ang maiin ka inhria a, chu chu crested comb pakhat chauh a ni a, Starbuck. Ka tar tawh a;—kut min vuan ang che, mipa.”

An kut chu a inhmachhawn a; an mit chu a nghet tlat a; Starbuck chuan glue chu a tikehsawm a.

“Aw, ka captain, ka captain!—thinlung ropui tak—kal suh—kal suh!—en teh, mi huaisen tap thin a ni; chuti a nih chuan thlemna hrehawmna chu a va nasa tak êm!”

“Hun thla rawh!”—Ahaba chuan a kawppui kut chu a hnen aṭangin a hlip a. “Crew bulah ding rawh!”

Thla khat chhungin lawng chu a hnunglam hnuaiah chuan a rawn tlan hnai ta hle mai.

“Shark te chu! shark-te chu!” chuta cabin-window hniam tak atang chuan aw a rawn chhuak a; “Aw pu, ka pu, lo haw leh rawh!”

Mahse, Ahaba chuan engmah a hre lo; chutih lai chuan a aw ngei chu a sang hle si a; tichuan lawng chu a zuang thla ta a.

Mahse, aw chuan thudik a sawi a; lawng aṭanga a nawr chhuah pawh a la harsa hle a, chutih lai chuan, lawng hnuai tui thim aṭanga lo chhuak ang maia lang shark tam tak chuan tuiah an inhnim pil apiangin, duhthusam takin lawng khalhte zungbun chu an rawn tikehsawm a tin, chutiang chuan lawng chu an seh nen chuan an zui ve bawk. Chu tuipui lian tak takah chuan whale-boat-te chungah chuan thil thleng mak tak a ni lo; shark-te chuan a châng chuan khawchhak lama marching regiment-te banner chunga vulture-te an thlawh ang bawkin an zui ve niin a lang. Mahse hengte hi White Whale an sawi hmasak ber atanga Pequod-a hmuh hmasak ber shark an ni a; tin, Ahaba lawnga thawkte chu chutiang sakeibaknei dum barbarian zawng zawng an ni em tih te, chuvangin an sa chu shark-te hriatnaah a musky zawk em ni—chu chu a châng chuan anmahni nghawngtu nia hriat lar thil a ni—chutiang pawh ni se, chu lawng pakhat chu tel lovin an zui niin a lang midangte chu pawngsual (molesting) a ni.

“Thlarau siam chhuah thinlung!” Starbuck-a chuan a sir lam hawi chungin a ti mur mur a, a mitmengin lawng hnungtawlh mek chu a zui a—“chu thil hmuh chu huaisen takin i la ri thei em?—i keel chu shark râpthlâk tak takte zîngah a hniam a, anmahni chu a zui a, a zui tûr chuan hmui hawng chungin tin, hei hi ni thumna pawimawh tak a ni em?—Ni thum chu ûmna nasa tak pakhata a luang khawm lai avângin; a hmasa ber chu zing a ni a, a pahnihna chu chawhnu a ni a, a pathumna chu tlai leh chu thil tawp a ni tih chiang rawh—chu tawp chu eng pawh ni se. Awh! ka Pathian! eng nge hei hi ka chhungah a kap a, chutiang taka thihna thlen thei khawpa thlamuang, mahse beisei nei,—fixed at the top of a shudder! Nakin lawka thil lo thleng tur chu ka hmaah a inbual a, outline leh skeleton hrui hring ang maiin; hun kal tawh zawng zawng chu engtin emaw takin a lo thim vek tawh. Mary, nula! ka hnungah ropuina hring dup takah i bo ta a; mipa naupang! Ka hmu ang maiin i mit chu mak tak maiin a rawn sen chhuak ta. Nunna buaina mak ber berte chu a chiang hle niin a lang; mahse, chhum chuan a inkar a rawn nuai a—Ka zinkawng tawp chu a lo thleng dawn em ni? Ka ke chu a chak lo hlein ka hria; nilengin kea kal tawh a ta ang maiin. I thinlung chu hre rawh,—a la vuak reng em? Nangmah leh nangmah inhnukdawk rawh, Starbuck!—stave it off—move, move! thu sawi rawh!—Mast-head there! Tlânga ka fapa kut chu i hmu em?—I rilru a buai em em a;—chutah chuan chung lamah chuan!—lawngte chu i mit fim ber ang che:—whale chu chhinchhiah ṭha rawh!—Ho! again!—chu hawk chu khalh chhuak rawh! hmu! a peck—vane chu a tikehsawm”—main-truck-a flag sen thlawk chu a kawhhmuh a—“Ha! a thlawk bo ta a ni!—Tunah chuan khawiah nge putar chu a awm? chu thil hmuh chu i hmu em, aw Ahaba!— khur, khur rawh!”

Lawngte chu an kal hla vak lo va, chutih lai chuan mast-heads atanga signal—a hnuai lam hawia kut zungtang, Ahaba chuan whale chu a ri tih a hria a; mahse, a thawh leh hunah chuan a bula awm a tum avangin, lawng a\angin a sir lam deuhin a kal ta a; becharmed crew chuan ngawi renga thuk ber chu an vawng reng a, lu-a beat waves chuan a khingpui bow chu a hammer leh hammer a.

“Drive la, i nail-ah khalh rawh, oh ye waves! an lu tawp thlengin a hruai lut ta! nangni erawh chuan khuhna tel lovin thil pakhat in vuak thin; tin, bawm leh ruang phur motor engmah ka ta a ni thei lo:—chuan hemp chauhvin min that thei bawk! Ha! ha!”

Vawikhatmah an chhehvela tui chu zawi zawiin a lo hring chhuak ta a; tichuan, rang takin a inher a, tui hnuaia vur berg aṭanga sir lam hawia tlan chhuak ang maiin, rang takin lei chung lamah a chho ta a ni. Rum ri hniam tak a ri a; lei hnuaia hum a ni; tichuan, an vai chuan an thaw chu an vawng tlat a; as bedraggled with trailing ropes, and harpoons, and lances, a form lian tak chu a sei zawngin, mahse tuifinriat atang chuan oblique takin kah a ni. Mist veil te tak te, tla thlain a khuh a, ruah sur boruakah chuan rei vak lo a inkhuar a; tin, chutah chuan tui thuk takah chuan a tla leh ta a ni. Feet sawmthum zeta sangah a inhnim pil a, tui chu tuikhuah tam tak ang maiin rei lo te chhung chu a eng a, chutah chuan a chhe vek a, flakes shower-ah chuan a chhe vek a, a circling surface chu whale marble trunk vel chu milk thar ang maia creamed-in a awm ta a ni.

“Hmun pe rawh!” Ahaba chuan lawng khalhtute hnenah chuan a au va, lawngte chu beih tumin hma an sawn ta a; mahse, nizan lama thir thar a chhunga a lo hring chhuakte chuan a ti â a, Moby Dick chu van atanga tla thla vantirhkoh zawng zawngte chuan an rawn luah khat vek ang maiin a lang. A hmai dum zau tak, a vun langsar tak hnuaia weld tendon tier zau tak takte chu a inzawm khawm ang maiin a lang a; lu nghatin lawngte zingah chuan a hmui chu a rawn herh chhuak a; vawi khat chu a flail leh ta a; an kawppui pahnih lawng aṭanga thir leh fei chu an paih chhuak a, an thal chunglam sir khatah an tlan lut a, mahse Ahaba ta chu hliam nei lo tluk zetin a awm ta a ni.

Daggoo leh Queequeg-a te chuan plank strained te chu an titawp lai chuan; tin, an hnen atanga whale rawn inher chhuak, a hawi let a, anmahni a kah leh lai chuan a sir lam pakhat pum pui a lantir lai chuan; chutih lai tak chuan au thawm a ri nghal vat a. Nga hnunglam thlengin lashed round leh round; pinioned in the turns upon turns in which, during the past night, the whale had reeled the involutions of the lines around him, Parsee taksa chanve hrual chu hmuh tur a awm a a sable puan chu a chhe vek tawh a; a mitmeng zau tak chu tar Ahaba lam chu a hawi ta vek a.

Harpoon chu a kut atang chuan a tla thla ta a.

“Bum, bum!”—thâwk chhuak rei tak a la a—“Aye, Parsee! Ka hmu leh che.—Aye, i hmaah i kal a; tin, hei hi, chuti a nih chuan ruang lawng i tiam kha a ni,” a ti a. Nimahsela i thu lehkha hnuhnung ber thlengin ka chelh tlat che a ni. Khawiah nge ruang lawng pahnihna chu? Away, mates, lawng lam pan rawh! chu lawngte chu tunah chuan a tangkai tawh lo; a hun takah in theih chuan siam that ula, ka hnenah kir leh rawh u; a nih loh chuan Ahaba chu thihpuiin a tawk a ni—Down, man! thil hmasa ber chu mahse he lawng ka dinna atang hian zuang thla turin a rawn offer a, chu thil chu ka harpoon a ni. Mi dang in ni lo va, ka kut leh ka ke in ni zawk; tichuan, ka thu awih rawh.—Khawiah nge whale chu a awm? a chhuk leh ta?”

Mahse lawng chu a hnaih lutuk a; a phur ruang nen tlanchhuah tura inbeng ang maiin, a inhmuhna hnuhnung ber hmun bik chu a leeward zinkawng stage khat chauh ni tawh ang maiin, tunah chuan Moby Dick chu nghet takin hmalam a pan leh ta a tin, lawng chu a paltlang tawh mai a,—tunah chuan a hmalam panna chu tihtawp a ni tawh nachungin, tun thleng hian a lam hawiin a kal tawh a ni. A chakna sang ber nen a inbual ang maiin a lang a, tunah chuan tuipuiah chuan a kawng dik tak zawh mai mai a tum ta a ni.

"Awh! Ahab,” Starbuck chuan a ti a, “tunah pawh hian, ni thumnaah pawh hian, bânsan a tlai lo. Hmu! Moby Dick hian a zawng lo che. Nang, nang, â takin amah chu i ni!”

Thlipui tleh nasa takah chuan a kal a, lawng pakhat chauh chu rang takin leeward-ah a hruai a, lawng leh canvas pahnih hmangin. Tin, a tawpah chuan Ahaba chu lawng bulah a tlan phei a, rel chunga a innghah lai chuan Starbuck-a hmel chu chiang taka hriat theih turin a hnaih em em bawk a, lawng chu hawi kual turin a fak a, rang lutuk lovin, hun remchang takah a zui ta a ni. Chunglam hawiin Tashtego, Queequeg leh Daggoo te chu duhthawh takin mast-head pathum chungah chuan an chuang a; chutih lai chuan lawng khalhtute chu staved lawng pahnih, a sir lama chawi chhuah chauh tawhah chuan an inher a, siamthat hna thawkin an buai hle bawk. A hnungzuiin, port-holes kal tlangin, a tlan chak lai chuan Stubb leh Flask-a te pawh thlawk chhuakin a hmu bawk a, deck-ah chuan thir leh lance thar bundle zingah an buai hle a ni. Heng zawng zawng a hmuh ang hian; lawng chhe tawha hammer ri a hriat angin; far other hammers chuan a thinlungah nail a rawn luh tir ang maiin a lang. Mahse, a rally ta a ni. Tin, tunah chuan main-mast-head atanga vane emaw flag emaw chu a bo tawh tih chhinchhiahin, chu perch hmu thar Tashtego chu flag dang, leh hammer leh nail zawng turin chhuk leh turin a au a, chutiang chuan mast-ah chuan nail rawh .

Ni thum chhung tlan chakna avanga a fagged emaw, a phur hamper knotted-a a inbualna dodalna te pawhin; emaw, a chhunga bumna leh sualna thup eng emaw a awm em tih emaw: eng pawh ni se, White Whale kawng chu tunah chuan a reh tan ta a, a lan dan chuan, vawi khat tal a hnaih lehna lawng atang chuan mahse, whale bul ṭan hnuhnung ber chu a hma ang khan a rei lo tak zet a ni. Tin, Ahaba chu tuipui chungah a tlan kual lai chuan khawngaihna nei lo shark-te chuan an zui ve bawk a; tin, chutiang chuan lungngai takin lawngah chuan a innghat a; tin, chutiang chuan plying oars-te chu a seh chhunzawm zel a, chutiang chuan a zungte chu a hrual a, a hrual a, tuifinriatah chuan hrual te te a hnutchhiah a, dip apiangah te pawh.

“Anmahni chu ngaithla suh! chu ha chu mahse i oars-ah rowlock thar pe rawh. Pull on rawh! 'tis the better rest, tui rah chhuah aiin shark-a hmui a ni.”

“Mahse, ka seh apiangin, ka pu, a zungpui te tak te chu a te deuh deuh thin!”

“An daih rei tawk ang! pull on!—Mahse, tuin nge hril thei ang”—a ti muangchang a—“hêng shark-te hian whale-te chaw ei tûrin an inbual nge, Ahaba-i chungah nge?—Mahse, hrual rawh!
