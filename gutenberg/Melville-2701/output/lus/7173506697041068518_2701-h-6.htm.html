Mahse a chunga Duodecimoes tia tarlan thilsiamte hi dik lo thei lo whale an ni a, whale chu eng nge a nih tih ka sawifiahna thumal hmang chuan— ie a spouting fish, with a horizontal tail.

LEHKHABU III. ( Duodecimo ), BUNG 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Hei hi khawvel hmun hrang hranga hmuh theih porpoise tlanglawn tak a ni. Hming hi keimah ngeiin ka pêk a ni a; porpoise chi khat aia tam an awm si a, anmahni thliar hrang turin thil tih a ngai a ni. Chutiang chuan ka ko a, a chhan chu tuifinriat zau takah chuan Fourth-of-July mipui zingah kawr ang maia vanah an inhnim pil reng thin, hilarious shoals-ah a inbual fo thin a ni. An hmel lan dan hi lawnga chuangte chuan lawm takin an fak tlangpui. Thlarau ṭha tak takin an khat a, thlipui tleh aṭanga thli tleh lam panin an lo kal ṭhin. Thli hmaa awm reng thin tlangvalte an ni. Anni hi vanneihna chhinchhiahna anga ngaih an ni. Nangmah ngei pawhin heng sangha nung tak takte hi enin hlimna vawi thum i tuar thei a nih chuan, van chuan min pui rawh u; Pathian ngaihsakna rilru chu nangmahniah a awm lo. Huzza Porpoise chaw tha tak, thau tak chuan oil tha gallon tha tak a pe ang che. Mahse a hmui atanga tui tha tak leh nalh tak tak lakchhuah chu a hlu hle. Jewelers leh darthlalang siamtute zingah dilna a ni. Sailor-te chuan an hones-ah an dah a. Porpoise sa hi ei tha tak a ni tih i hria em? Porpoise a chhuak tih hi i rilruah a lo lang ngai lo mai thei. Dik tak chuan a spout chu a te lutuk a, hriat awlsam vak a ni lo. Mahse, a dawt leha chance i neih hunah chuan amah chu en reng ang che; tin, chutah chuan Sperm whale ropui tak ngei pawh chu miniature-in i hmu ang.

LEHKHABU III. ( Duodecimo ), BUNG II-NA. ( Algerine Porpoise ).—Tui rûk chhuaktu. Savage tak a ni. Pacific-ah chauh hmuh tur a awm niin ka hria. Huzza Porpoise aiin a lian deuh a, mahse general make inang deuh deuh a ni. Amah chu tithinur la, shark-ah a buckle ang. Vawi tam tak a tan ka lower tawh a, mahse man a nih ka la hmu ngai lo.

LEHKHABU III. ( Duodecimo ), BUNG III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Porpoise chi lian ber; leh Pacific-ah chauh hmuh tur a awm a, a hriat dan chuan. English hming pakhat chauh, tun thlenga a hming vuah chu sangha mantute hming a ni—Right-Whale Porpoise, chu Folio bulah chuan a awm ber tih dinhmun a\angin. A pianziaah chuan Huzza Porpoise nen hian eng emaw chen a danglam a, a rotund lo zawk leh jolly girth a ni a; dik tak chuan, a hmel chu a felfai hle a, mi fel tak ang mai a ni. A hnungzang ah hian fins a nei lo (porpoise dang tam zawk chuan an nei), a hmui duhawm tak a nei a, sentimental Indian eyes of a hazel hue a nei bawk. Mahse a mealy-mouth chuan a tichhe vek. A hnungzang zawng zawng chu a sir lam fin thlengin sable thuk tak ni mah se, ramri line, lawng chhunga chhinchhiahna ang maia danglam, “bright waist” tia koh, chu line chuan a zung atanga a hnunglam thlengin a hrual a, a chungah chuan rawng hrang hrang pahnih a awm a, a chungah chuan dum leh a hnuaiah chuan a var a ni. White hian a lu thenkhat a huam a, a hmui pum pui pawh a huam a, chu chuan meal-bag-a felonious visit atanga tlanchhuak chauh ang maiin a lantir a ni. A most mean leh mealy aspect a ni! A hriak hi porpoise pangngai nen a inang hle.

DUODECIMO piah lamah chuan he system hi a kal zel lo a, Porpoise hi whale zingah chuan a te ber a nih avangin. A chungah hian Leviathans of note zawng zawng i nei vek a. Mahse, rinhlelhna nei lo, tlanchhia, half-fabulous whale rabble an awm a, chu chu American whaleman ka nih angin hmingthannain ka hre a, mahse mimal takin ka hre lo. An fore-castle appellation hmangin ka chhiar chhuak ang; chutiang list chu nakin lawka chhuitute tan chuan hlu tak a ni thei a, hetah hian ka neih tawh, mahse ka tan tawh chu an ti zo mai thei a ni. A hnuaia whale zinga pakhat emaw, a hnua man leh chhinchhiahna siam a nih chuan, a Folio, Octavo, emaw Duodecimo magnitude angin he System-ah hian awlsam takin a tel thei ang:—The Bottle-Nose Whale Junk Whale tih chu a ni a; Pudding-Headed Whale tih chu a ni a; Cape Whale te chu an awm a; Leading Whale tih chu a ni a; Cannon Whale tih chu a ni a; Scragg Whale tih chu a ni a; Coppered Whale tih chu a ni a; Elephant Whale tih chu a ni a; Iceberg Whale tih chu a ni a; Quog Whale tih chu a ni a; Blue Whale tih chu a ni a; etc. Icelandic, Dutch leh English thuneitute a\angin, whale chiang lo list dang, hming mawi lo chi hrang hranga malsawmna pawh a awm thei. Mahse, hmanlai tawh lo vek angin ka paih chhuak a; tin, Leviathanism-a khat, mahse engmah tilang lo ri mai mai avânga rinhlelh chu a ti thei lo.

A tawp berah chuan: A tir lamah khan sawi a ni a, he system hi hetah hian a awm dawn lo va, vawi khatah, a famkim dawn lo. Ka thu ka zawm tih chiang takin i hmu thei lo. Mahse tunah chuan ka cetological System chu chutianga tihfel lohvin ka dah ta a, Cologne Cathedral ropui tak chu a awm tawh ang bawkin, crane chu tower zawh loh chung lamah a la ding reng a ni. Erection tenau te chu an architect hmasa berte’n an zo thei a; grand te, dik tak te chuan copestone chu thlahtute hnenah an hnutchhiah fo thin. Pathianin engtikah mah ka tihfel loh nan min veng rawh. He lehkhabu pumpui hi draught mai a ni—ni lo, draught draft mai a ni. Aw, Hun, Chakna, Sum leh Dawhtheihna!
