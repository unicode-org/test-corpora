BUNG 101. Decanter-a chu.

English lawng chu hmuh theih loha a bo hma chuan, hetah hian dah pawh ni se, London atanga lo piang a ni a, chu khawpuia sumdawng, Enderby & Sons-a whaling house hmingthang tak bulpui ber Samuel Enderby-a hming chawia sak a ni a in pakhat chu ka whaleman rethei tak ngaihdanah chuan Tudor leh Bourbon-ho lal in inzawmkhawm aiin a hnungtawlh hla vak lo a, in point of real historical interest. Kan Lalpa kum 1775 hma khan eng chen nge he whale man in ropui tak hi a awm tawh tih ka fish-document tam tak hian a tichiang lo; mahse chu kum (1775) chuan Sperm Whale man fo thin English lawng hmasa berte chu a fit out ta a; mahse, a hma kum sawmhnih engemaw zat (kum 1726 a\ang khan) kan Coffins leh Maceys huaisen tak takte chuan Nantucket leh Vineyard-a awmte chuan lawng lian tak takah chu Leviathan chu an lo zui tawh a, mahse North leh South Atlantic-ah chauh an zui a: hmun dangah ni lovin. Hetah hian chiang takin ziak ni se, Nantucketer-ho hi mihring zingah chuan civilized steel hmanga Sperm Whale ropui tak harpoon hmasa ber an ni tih; tin, kum zabi chanve chhung zet chu khawvel pum huapa hetiang taka harpoon-tu mite chauh an nih thu pawh a sawi bawk.

Kum 1778-ah chuan lawng ṭha tak, Amelia chu chutiang thil tum tak atân chuan a insiam a, Enderby-ho chak tak takte chauhin a enkawl a, huaisen takin Cape Horn chu a rawn kual a, hnamte zînga South Sea ropui takah chuan eng chi pawh whale-boat a tihhniam hmasa ber a ni. Khualzinna chu thiam tak leh vanneihna a ni a; tin, a chenna hmuna sperm hlu tak tak nen a vawn lai chu a kir leh a, Amelia-i entawn tur chu rei lo teah lawng dang, English leh American-te pawhin an zui ve ta a, chutiang chuan Pacific tuipui kama Sperm Whale hmun zau tak chu an paih chhuak ta a ni. Mahse, he thil \ha tihnaah hian lungawi lovin, in chau lo tak chuan a inhnukdawk leh ta a: Samuela leh a Fapa zawng zawngte—engzat nge an nih, an nu chauhin a hriat—chuan an enkawlna hnuaiah, a then chu, ka ngaihdan chuan, an sum senso hmangin, British sorkar chu sloop-of-war Rattler chu South Sea-a whaling-a thil hmuhchhuahna zinnaah tirh turin a fuih ta a ni. Naval Post-Captain kaihhruaina hnuaiah Rattler lawng chuan rattling travel a nei a, service engemaw zat a thawk bawk a; engzat nge a lang lo. Mahse hei hi a ni lo. Kum 1819 khan chu in vek chuan anmahni ngeia discovery whale lawng an siam a, Japan tuipui hla tak takah tasting cruise-a kal turin. Chu lawng—“Syren” tia koh ṭha tak—chuan experimental cruise ropui tak a siam a; tin, chutiang chuan Japanese Whaling Ground ropui tak chu mipui hriat hlawh hmasa ber a ni ta a ni. He lawng hmingthang takah hian Syren lawng hi Captain Coffin, Nantucketer pakhatin a kaihruai a.

Chuvangin, Enderbies-ho chawimawina zawng zawng, an in chu tun thleng hian a awm tawh niin ka hria; mahse, rinhlelh rual lohvin Samuela hmasa ber chuan khawvêl dang South Sea ropui tak atân a cable chu a slip tawh ngei ang.

A hming chawia lawng hming vuah chu chawimawi tlak a ni a, lawng khalh chak tak leh kawng tinrenga kutchhuak ropui tak a ni. Patagonia tuipui kam hmun khatah zanlai pelh hnuah vawi khat chu ka chuang a, forecastle-ah chuan flip down tha tak ka in bawk. Kan neih gam tha tak a ni a, an vai hian trump an ni vek a—chuta chuang thlarau zawng zawng. An tan dam rei lo, leh thihna hlim tak. Tin, chu gam tha tak ka neih chu—Ahaba upain a sakeibaknei kekawrte hmanga a thingphel a khawih hnu rei tak, rei tak hnuah—chu lawnga Saxon-ho mikhualna ropui tak, nghet tak, Saxon-ho mikhualna chu min ngaihtuah tir a tin, ka parson chuan min theihnghilh sela, Diabola chuan min hre reng ang che, ka hmuh loh chuan. Vai? Flip kan nei tih ka sawi em? Ni e, darkar khata gallon sawm velin kan flip bawk a; tin, thlipui a lo thlen chuan (for it’s squally off there by Patagonia), kut zawng zawng—tlawhtu leh mi zawng zawng—reef topsail-a koh an nih chuan, kan top-heavy em em a, bowline-ah kan inswing a ngai ta a tin, hriat lohvin kan jacket-te kawrfual chu lawng lianah chuan kan thun lut a, chuvang chuan chutah chuan kan innghat a, thlipui au thawm karah chuan kan reefed chak hle a, zu rui tar zawng zawngte tana vaukhânna entawn tur a ni. Mahse, mast-te chu a kal lutuk lo va; tin, rei lo teah kan tlan thla a, kan fimkhur em em a, flip chu kan paltlang leh a ngai ta a, forecastle scuttle-a savage salt spray puak chhuak chuan, rather too much diluted and pickled it to my taste.

Bawngsa chu a tha hle—a khauh hle a, mahse taksa a awm thung. Bull-beef a ni an ti a; mi dangte chuan, dromedary bawnghnute a nih thu; mahse, chu chu engtin nge a nih tih erawh ka hre lo, chiang takin. Dumplings an nei bawk a; dumplings te tak te, mahse a lian tham, symmetrically globular, leh tihchhiat theih loh dumplings. I hre thei ang tih ka ngaihtuah a, an ei hnuah i chhungah i roll kual thei bawk. Hmalam pan lutuka i kun chuan billiard-ball ang maiin an pitch out che chu i risk a ni. Chhang chu—mahse chu chu a pui thei lo; chu bâkah, anti-scorbutic a ni bawk; a tawi zawngin, chhang chhungah chuan an fare thar awmchhun a awm a. Mahse forecastle chu a eng vak lo va, i ei chuan kil thim takah chuan ke pen a awlsam hle. Mahse a vaiin, truck atanga helm dangah hruaiin, cook-a boiler-te dimension ngaihtuah chungin, a mah ngeiin live parchment boiler-te pawh a tel a; hma leh hnung lamah, ka ti a, Samuel Enderby chu lawng hlim tak a ni a; fare tha leh tam tak; fine flip leh chak tak; crack fellows zawng zawng, leh boot heels atanga hat-band thlengin capital.

Mahse, engvangin nge, in ngaihtuah teh, Samuel Enderby-a te, leh English whaller dang ka hriat ṭhenkhat—a zawng zawng ni lo mah se—chutiang lawng hmingthang, mikhual lawm thei tak an nih; chu chuan bawngsa, leh chhang, leh can, leh fiamthu chu a vel a; tin, ei leh in leh nuih te kha an chau thuai lo em ni? Ka hrilh ang che. Hêng English whaller-te hlimna ṭha tak hi history zir chianna atân thil pawimawh tak a ni. Chutiang bawkin, historical whale research pawh ka hmang lo, a tul nia a lan lai pawhin.

English-ho hma hian whale man kawngah Hollander, Zealander leh Dane-ho an awm a; chuta ṭang chuan sangha man lama la awm thumal tam tak an la chhuak a; tin, chu aia nasa zawk chu, an fakna hlui thau tak takte, ei leh in tur tam tak khawih te hi a ni. A chhan chu, thil tlangpui angin, English sumdawng lawng chuan a thawktute chu a scrimp a; mahse, English whaler-te erawh chu chutiang chu a ni lo. Chuvangin, English-ah chuan he whaling good cheer tih hi thil pangngai leh thilsiam a ni lo va, incidental leh particular a ni zawk a; tin, chuvangin, bul tanna bik engemaw zat a nei tur a ni a, chu chu hetah hian tarlan a ni a, chu chu chiang zawkin kan la chiang zawk ang.

Leviathanic history-a ka zirchiannaah khan Dutch volume hlui pakhat ka hmu a, chu chu, a rim hnim hring tak avang chuan, whaler-te chungchang a ni ngei ang tih ka hria. A thupui chu, “Dan Coopman” a ni a, chuvangin hei hi Amsterdam cooper thenkhatte hriatrengna hlu tak, sangha mantute hriatrengna hlu tak a ni ngei ang, whale lawng tin hian an cooper an phur vek tur a ni tih ka thutlukna siam ta a ni. He ngaihdan hi “Fitz Swackhammer” pakhat siam a nih thu ka hmuh hian min tichak lehzual a ni. Mahse ka thianpa Dr. Snodhead, mi thiam tak, Santa Claus leh St. Pott’s college-a Low Dutch leh High German professor, lehlin hna chu ka hlan a, a harsatna atan sperm candle box khat ka pe a—he Dr. Snodhead ngei hian lehkhabu a en meuh chuan “Dan Coopman” tih hian “The Cooper” tihna a ni lo tih min tiam a, “The Thilzuartu." A tawi zawngin, he Low Dutch lehkhabu hlui leh zir chhuah tak hian Holland sumdawnna chungchang a sawi a; tin, thupui dangte zîngah chuan a whale man chungchâng chanchin ngaihnawm tak tak a keng tel bawk. Tin. chu list atang chuan Dr. Snodhead-a lehlin angin a hnuaia mi hi ka transcribe a ni:

Chutih lai chuan, ni thum chhung chu heng beer, bawnghnute, leh chhangphut zawng zawngte hi zir chiang taka ei chhuah nan ka hmang a, chutih lai chuan ngaihtuahna thuk tak tak tam tak ka ngaihtuah a, chu chu thil danglam bik leh Plato-a hman theih a ni a tin, chu bâkah, chu hmân lai Greenland leh Spitzbergen whale man chhuahna hmuna Low Dutch harpooneer zawng zawngin an ei ṭhin stock-fish, etc. zat awm thei chu ka khawih a, chu bâkah, keimah ngeiin supplementary table ka siam bawk. A hmasa berah chuan butter tam zia, leh Texel leh Leyden cheese ei zat hi mak tak a ni. Mahse, an pianphungah chuan an rilru natna (naturally unctuous natures) a ni tih ka puh a, an kohna nihna avang leh a bik takin chu Polar Tuipui lum takah chuan an game an zawm avangin, chu Esquimaux ram tuipui kamah, convivial native-te’n rel oil bumper-a an intiamna hmunah chuan.

Beer pawh a tam hle a, barrel 10,800 a ni. Tunah chuan, chu polar fisheries te chu chu climate thlasik rei lo te chhung chauha thubuai siam theih a nih avangin, heng Dutch whalemen zinga pakhat cruise zawng zawng, Spitzbergen tuipui atanga lo haw leh hawna hun rei lo te pawh tiamin, thla thum aia rei lo, sawi ta ila, an lawng 180 chuang zinga pakhatah mi 30 chhiar chuan, a vaiin Low Dutch seamen 5,400 kan nei a;.. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. .. chuvangin, ka ti a, mi pakhat tan beer barrel hnih chiah kan nei a, kar sawm leh pahnih allowance atan, chu gin ankers 550 atanga a zat dik tak chu telh loh chuan. Tunah chuan, heng gin leh beer harpooneer-te hi, mi pakhatin a ngaihtuah ang maia fuddled tak takte hi lawng lu chunga ding tur leh flying whale-te tum tha tak nei tur mi dik tak an ni em? hei hi eng emaw chen chu thil theih loh angin a lang ang. Mahse, an tum ve tho a, anmahni pawh an kap ve bawk. Mahse hei hi North hla tak a ni a, hriatreng ni se, beer hi constitution nen a inmil hle a; Equator-ah chuan, kan khawthlang lam sangha mannaah chuan, beer chuan harpooneer chu mast-head-ah muthilh tir leh a lawnga boozy-a siam turin a tling ang; leh Nantucket leh New Bedford-te tan hlohna nasa tak a thleng thei bawk.

Mahse, a awm tawh lo; kum zabi hnih emaw, kum zabi thum emaw kalta a Dutch whaller hluite chu liver sang tak an ni tih tilang thei khawpa sawi a ni tawh a; tin, English whaller-te chuan hetiang entawn tur tha tak hi an ngaihthah lo tih pawh a sawi bawk. A chhan chu, an ti a, lawng khar chhunga i zin hian khawvel a\ang hian thil tha zawk i hmu thei lo a nih chuan, a tlem berah chuan zanriah tha tak i ei chhuak ang. Tin, hei hian decanter chu a ti hring vek bawk.





BUNG 102. Arsacides-a Bower pakhat.

Tun thleng hian Sperm Whale chungchang sawifiahna hmanga ka enkawl danah hian a pawn lam thil mak tak takte ka sawi ber a; emaw, a hranpaa leh chipchiar takin interior structural feature tlemte thenkhatah emaw a ni thei bawk. Mahse. chu chu a ruhro (unconditional skeleton)-ah tihna a ni.

Mahse, engtin nge tunah, Ismaela? Engtin nge, nang, sangha man lama lawng khalhtu mai mai hian, whale-te lei hnuaia thil awmte chungchâng engmah hre lo anga inlan? I capstan-a chuang, erudite Stubb-a chuan Cetacea taksa peng hrang hrang chungchang zirtirna a pe em; tin, windlass puihnain, exhibition atan specimen rib pakhat chu han chawi sang em? Ismaela, inhrilhfiah rawh. I deck-ah whale puitling tak chu enfiah turin i thlawh tir thei ang em, chaw siamtuin vawk roast a dish angin? Ni lo tak zet a ni. Tun thleng hian thuhretu dik tak i ni tawh a, Ismaela; mahse, Jona chauh chanvo i hman dan chu fimkhur rawh; joist leh beam-a thusawi theihna chanvo; rafters, ridge-pole, sleepers, leh under-pinnings te hian leviathan frame-work a siam a; leh a rilru chhunga tallow-vat, dairy-room, buttery leh cheeseries te ang bawk.

Jona a\ang khan whale puitling vun hnuaiah hian whaleman tlemte chauh an lut tawh tih ka inpuang a; chuti chung pawh chuan, miniature-a dissect theihna hun remchang ka dawng a ni. Ka awmna lawng pakhatah chuan vawi khat chu a poke emaw bag emaw atan te, harpoon barb atan te, lance lu atan te, sheath siam nan te, Sperm Whale naupang te tak te chu taksain deck-ah an chawi sang a. Chu hun remchang chu ka thlahthlam ta niin i ngai em, ka lawng-hatchet leh jack-knife hmang lovin, seal chu ka tichhia a, chu naupang no chhunga thil awm zawng zawng chu chhiar lovin?

Tin. Tranque-a ka awm avangin, kum tam tak kal ta khan, Algiers-a sumdawnna lawng Dey-a ka inzawm lai khan, Arsacidean holidays thenkhat chu Tranque lal nen, Pupella-a a palm villa pension tawhah hmang turin min sawm a kan lawng khalhtute’n a khawpui Bamboo-Town an tih aṭanga hla vak lo tuipui kama luipui pakhat.

Mize ṭha dang tam tak zîngah chuan ka lal ṭhian Tranquo-a chu barbaric vertu thil zawng zawnga hmangaihna rilru pu tak pêk a nih avângin, a mite zînga mi fing zâwkte thil hmuh chhuah theih tûr thil tlemte chu Pupella-ah a rawn hruai khâwm a a bik takin hmanraw mak tak tak thing phun, chiselled shell, inlaid spears, paddle man to tak tak, aromatic canoes; tin, chûng zawng zawng chu thilsiam thilmak eng pawh, thilmak phur, chhiah pe thei thlipuiin a tuipui kamah a rawn thlak a, chu chu an sem darh vek a ni.

Chung hnuhnung zawk zinga langsar ber chu Sperm Whale ropui tak a ni a, chu chu, a danglam bik taka thlipui na tak hnuah, thi leh tangkhang anga hmuh a ni a, a lu chu cocoa-nut thing chungah a nghat a, a hmul ang maia, tufted droopings chu a verdant jet ang mai a ni. A tawpah chuan taksa zau tak chu a fathom-dep enfoldings te chu an hlihsak a, ruhte chu ni chhuahnaah leivut a lo vawt ta a, chutah chuan ruhro chu uluk takin Pupella glen-ah chuan an phur chho a, chutah chuan tunah chuan lordly palms temple ropui takin a khuh ta a ni.

Rib-ah chuan trophy an tar a; vertebræ-te chu Arsacidean annals-a phun a ni a, hieroglyphics mak tak tak hmanga ziah a ni a; luruh chhungah chuan puithiamte chuan meialh rimtui tak, tihmit loh chu an vawng reng a, chutiang chuan thuruk lu chuan a vapory spout chu a rawn thawn leh a; chutih lai chuan, thinghnah pakhata innghatin, a hnuai lam hmui mak tak chu devotee zawng zawng chungah chuan a rawn nghing a, Damocles-a ti hlauhawm em emtu sam hrual khandaih ang maiin.

Thil mak tak mai a ni. Thing chu Icy Glen-a mosses ang maiin a green a; thingte chu sang tak leh chapo takin an ding a, an tui nung chu an hre ta a; a hnuaia lei taima tak chu puan puan sin ang mai a ni a, a chungah chuan carpet mawi tak a awm a, chu chu leia grep hrui hringte chuan warp leh woof a siam a, pangpar nungte chuan lem a siam bawk. Thingkung zawng zawng, a hnah phur zawng zawng nen; thinghnah te, fern te, thlai chi hrang hrang te; thuchah kengtu boruak; heng zawng zawng hi chawlhsan lovin an thawk chhuak vek a ni. A hnah lacings hmang chuan ni ropui tak chu thlawk chhuak shuttle ang maiin, chau lo verdure chu a hrual a. Aw, puan siamtu buai tak! hmuh loh puan siamtu!—pause!—thumal pakhat!—khawiah nge puan chu a luang? eng palace nge a deck theih ang? engvangin nge heng thawhrimna tawp lo zawng zawng hi? Thu sawi rawh, puan siamtu!—i kut chu awm rawh!—mahse, thu pakhat chauh sawi rawh! Ni lo—shuttle chu a thlawk a—a lemte chu loom hma lam aṭangin a lo thlawk chhuak a; freshet-rushing carpet chu chatuan atan a tlanbo ta a ni. Puan siamtu pathian, a puan a; chu puan siamna chuan a beng a tichhe a, thi thei aw engmah a hre lo; tin, chu hum hum avang chuan keini pawh, loom entu te pawh kan beng a tichhe ta a; tin, chuta kan tlanchhuah hunah chauh chuan a hmanga thusawi aw sang tam takte chu kan hre ang. Chuti chung pawh chuan material factory zawng zawngah pawh a awm vek si a. Thlawhna spindle zinga hriat theih loh thu sawite; chu thute tho chu bang tel lo pawhin chiang takin a ri a, casement hawn a\angin a puak chhuak a ni. Chutiang chuan villainies an hmuchhuak ta a ni. Ah, thi thei tak! tichuan, fimkhur rawh; chutiang chuan khawvel ropui tak lo thleng tur ri zawng zawngah hian i ngaihtuahna fiah lo ber berte chu hla tak atangin hriat theih a ni si a.

Tunah chuan, chu Arsacidean thing hring, nunna hahdam thei lo loom karah chuan, ruhro lian tak, var, biak thin chu a lo muhil ta a—midler lian tak mai! Mahse, a ever-woven verdant warp leh woof chu a inhmeh tawn a, a hum lai chuan, idler chak tak chu puan siamtu fing tak ang maiin a lang a; amah ngei pawh grep hrui nen a inthuam vek a; thla tin greener, fresher verdure anga ngaihin; mahse amah ngei chu ruhro a ni. Nunna folded Thihna; Thihna trellised Nunna; pathian lungchhiatthlak tak chuan tleirawl Nunna nen a nupui a, lu hrual ropuina a hring a.

Tunah chuan lal Tranquo nen he whale mak tak hi ka tlawh a, a luruh chu maicham, leh jet tak tak chhuahna hmun atanga meikhu siam chhuah chu ka hmuh chuan lal chuan chapel chu thil (object of vertu) anga a ngaih tur chu mak ka ti hle a ni. A nui suk a. Mahse, chu aia nasa zawk chu puithiamte chuan a smoky jet chu a tak tak a ni tih an tiam tur a ni. He ruhro hmaah hian ka kal kual a—grep hnahte chu ka brush a—a ruhte chu a tikehsawm a—Arsacidean twine ball nen chuan, a colonnade leh arbour tam tak, hrual, thlamuang tak tak karah chuan ka vak kual a, rei tak ka vak kual a. Mahse rei lo teah ka line chu a chhuak ta a; tin, chu chu ka zui leh a, ka luhna kawngkhar atang chuan ka chhuak ta a. A chhungah hian thil nung reng reng ka hmu lo; ruh tih loh chu engmah a awm lo.

Green measuring-rod min cut a, vawi khat chu ruhro chhungah chuan ka dive leh ta a. Luruh chhunga an thal hrual atang chuan puithiamte chuan ka ruh hnuhnung ber san zawng ka lak lai chu an hmu a, “Engtin nge tunah chuan!” an au chhuak a; “He kan pathian hi i teh darh em!Chu chu kan tan a ni.” “A ni, puithiamte u—a tha e, eng chen nge in siam ang, chuti a nih chuan?” Nimahsela, hetih lai hian an zîngah inelna nasa tak a lo chhuak a, ke leh inches chungchângah; an yard-stick hmangin an sconces an tikehsawm a—luruh ropui tak chu a ri chhuak ta a—chu vanneihna hun remchang chu hmang tangkaiin ka admeasurements chu rang takin ka titawp ta a ni.

Tunah hian heng admeasurements te hi in hmaah dah ka rawt a ni. Mahse, a hmasa berin, record ni rawh se, he thu-ah hian ka duh ang tehfung (fancied measurement) engmah sawi turin ka zalen lo. A chhan chu skeleton authority i refer theih a awm a, ka dikna test nan. Chu rama whale man theihna hmun pakhat, England ram Hull-ah chuan Leviathanic Museum a awm tih min hrilh a, chutah chuan fin-back leh whale dang specimen tha tak tak an nei a ni. Chutiang bawkin, New Hampshire-a Manchester museum-ah pawh, a neitute’n “United States-a Greenland emaw River Whale emaw specimen ṭha famkim awmchhun” an tih chu an nei tih ka hre tawh bawk. Chu bâkah, Yorkshire, England-a hmun pakhatah chuan, a hming Burton Constable-ah chuan Sir Clifford Constable pakhat chuan Sperm Whale ruhro chu a nei a, mahse a lian tawk lo va, ka thianpa King Tranquo-a ruhro ang kha a puitling lo tak zet a ni.

Heng pahnihah hian heng ruhro pahnih awmna, stranded whale te hi a tir chuan an neitute chuan chhan inang chiah chiah hmangin an claim a ni. King Tranquo chuan a duh avangin a ta chu a man a; leh Sir Clifford-a te, chu hmun hrang hranga seignories-te lal a nih avangin. Sir Clifford-a whale hi a pumpuiah articulated a ni tawh a; chutiang chuan, chest of drawer lian tak ang maiin, i hawng thei a, i khar thei a, a ruh hring zawng zawngah—a ruhte chu fan lian tak ang maiin i theh darh thei a—a hmui hnuai lam chungah nilengin i inher thei bawk. A trap-door leh shutter thenkhatah chuan lock dah tur a ni a; tin, footman pakhat chuan a bulah chabi bunch khat kengin round future visitors a entir ang.
