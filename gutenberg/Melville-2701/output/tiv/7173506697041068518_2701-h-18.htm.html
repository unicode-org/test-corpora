I ôr kwagh u waif man waif-poles ken ityough ki masetyô kpa ki môm tseegh, nahan gba u a ôr kwagh u atindi man akaawan a i we sha kwagh u ishu i i yer ér whale la, a alaghga a nenge ér waif la ka ikyav i vesen man baaji u i yô.

Ashighe kpishi ka a er nahan yô, shighe u utso kpishi ve lu zenden imôngo yô, alaghga tso môm una gbidye zegeikpelaior igen, maa ia yevese, ken masejime yô, tso ugen una wua un, una kôr un; man heen ne i nger akaa a kiriki kpishi a a er sha ivande la ker jighilii, a cii nga a zua a kwagh u vesen môm ne yô. Ikyav i tesen yô,—ka vea zenda ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la vea kôr i sha gbenda u vôron man u vihin tsung yô, alaghga iyol i ikyôhô la ia due ken tso la kera sha ci u ahumbe a taver kpishi; man u yevese yemen ica u zan hen kpezegemnger la, or u kôron ishu u i yer ér whale la nana hide nana kôr un, nana kôr un sha mlu u kundu kundu, nana cir un sha kpe, a kwagh u vihin uma shin ikyav shio. Nahan ashighe kpishi ayôôso a hemban vihin shi a vihin tsung la aa due hen atô u mbakôronishu mbara, aluer tindi ugen u i nger shin i nger ga, u a lu sha tar cii, u i lu a anyiman sha mi ga, u a lu sha akaa cii la ngu ga yô.

Alaghga tsô, tindi u yange i wa sha kwagh u kôron ishu i i yer ér whale la, lu tindi u tar u Holland tseegh. Yange i wa tindi ne sha ikyev i States-General ken inyom i 1695. Kpa er ikyurior igen i lu a vande lun a tindi u i nger sha kwagh u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la ga nahan kpa, mbakôron ishu mba ken tar u Amerika yange ve lu mbawan tindi man mbahemenev vev mba ôron ijir sha kwagh ne. Ve na mzough u a lu a inja kpishi, u a hembe mzough u Justinian u i yer ér Pandects la man atindi a kiriki a Mzough u Mbahemenev mba Shin Itine u tar u China u yangen ior u nyôron iyol ken akaa a ior mbagenev la. Aan; alaghga a nger atindi ne sha inyaregh ki Torkwase Anne, shin sha ikyondo i i yer ér harpoon la, a cir sha ityou, nahan a lu cuku.

I. A Fast-Fish ka a lu ken pati u a lu a mi la.

II. Loose-Fish ka kwagh u vough u hanma or u nana kôr un fese cii nana er yô.

Kpa kwagh u a ne ve tindi u sha kwaghfan ne a lu vihin yô, ka mlu u kpuaa u a doo u nengen a mi la, u a ne ve i gbe u a nger akaaôron kpishi sha mi ve a pase un yô.

Hiihii: Ka nyi i lu Fast-Fish? Iyôngo i lu uma shin i kpe yô, ka i gba yevese fele fele, ka ia zua a tso shin tso u ior ve lu ker yô, sha hanma gbenda u or u nan lu shin tso la shin mba ve lu shin tso la vea kôrcio u eren tom a mi cii yô,—ka ikyagh ki eren tom a mi, shin ikyagh ki eren tom a mi, shin ikyagh ki eren tom a mi ki lun a iniembe i inch anigheni, shin waya u telegraf, shin ikyagh ki eren tom a mi yô, akaa ne cii ka kwagh môm. Kape ishu kpa ka i lu a ikyav i waif, shin ikyav igen i i fe ér ngi a kwagh je la; aluer mba ve lu eren kwagh ne la tese wang ér vea fatyo u tôôn kwagh ne sha hanma shighe cii yô, kua awashima ve u eren nahan la kpaa.

Akaa ne ka akaaôron a sainsi; kpa mbamlumun mba mbatomov mba ken ikyôhô i whale la iyol ve ve ôron la ashighe agen ka ve lu a asember a taver man ikyav mbi hemban taver—ka Coke-upon-Littleton u i yer ér fist la je la. Sha mimi yô, hen atô u mba ve hembe lun perapera shi ve lu a icivir yô, hanma shighe yô, ka i na ve inyaregh sha akaa a a lu kposo yô, ape a lu kwagh u vihin tsung sha kwagh u aeren a wang aluer orgen kaa ér nan ngu a ikyôhô i orgen vande zendan shin wuan nan yô. Kpa mbagenev yô, mba a mnenge u vough nahan ga.

Anyom nga kar er akunduatar nahan yô, ijir igen i i yer ér whale-trover la yange i ôr ken tar u Ingila, nahan mba ve yer ijir la kaa ér yange ve zenda zegemnger u i yer ér whale la kpoghuloo ken zegemnger u ken Imbusutarimese la; man shighe u mimi je ve (mba ve yer ijir la) ve hemba u kôron ishu la sha harpoon la; ken masejime yô, sha u veren uma ve sha zongo yô, gba u vea undu ikyav vev tseegh ga, kpa vea undu tso ve iyol na kpaa. Ken masejime yô, mba i yer ve ijir la (mbatomov mba shin tso ugen) va a ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la, ve gbidye i, ve wua i, ve kôr i, ve mase ngohol i sha ishigh ki mba ve yer ijir la. Shighe u i ôr mba ve yer ve ijir la kwagh yô, orvesen ve u tso la cir ave sha mba ve lu ôron ijir la, shi na ve jighjigh ér sha u tesen ér un er kwaghbo yô, una lu a ikyav vev mbi utyaav man ikyav mbi utyaav mbi ve lu a mi la man tso ve, u yange lu her sha zegeikpelaior la shighe u i kôr un la her. Hegen mba i yer ve ijir la yange ve tôô ishu la ken masejime; ergo, ngeren mba i ter sha heen ne lu ve.

Or u ken igbar u nan nenge a kwagh u Orjir u a fe kwagh kpishi ne a tsough u eren ne yô, alaghga nana venda kwagh ne. Kpa aluer i ôr kwagh u akaawan a vesen ahar a i we ken atindi a twin whaling a se vande teren la, shi Lord Ellenborough yange er tom a mi shi pase kwagh u a ken ijir i i ter sha heen ne yô; atindi ahar a a bende a Fast-Fish man Loose-Fish ne, m kaa mer, aluer i hen sha mi yô, a zua a akaa a vesen a a gbe a zough sha kwagh u tindi u uumace cii yô; gadia shin er i lu a ikyav mbi eren tom a mi mbi taver ishe nahan kpa, Tempel u Tindi la ngu a akaa ahar tseegh a tilen sha mi, er Tempel u Mbafiliti nahan.

“Ikyav mbi tomov mbi a kuma, tor ngu a uma, man tor ngu a uma.” Brakton, l. 3, c. 3.

IKYAV I TESE 91. Pequod Zua a Rose-Bud.

“Yange lu gbilinkwagh u keren Ambergriese ken iyav mbu Leviathan ne, fetor u a lu a tahav mbu wan ishima ga, u a venda u pinen kwagh ga la.” Orvesen T. Browne, ken tar u VE

Ka kasua môm shin uhar cii ve i hide i ôr kwagh u ikyav i masetyô i i yer ér whale la ye, shi shighe u se lu yemen sha zegemnger u a lu a mnyam ma cir, u a lu a ahumbe ker, u a lu hen atô u iyange la, ikyav mbi tswamev kpishi mbi mbi lu sha kpekpe u tso u Pequod la tese ér mbi hemba lun tsevaa sha kwagh u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la, a ashe atar a a lu sha la. Stubb kaa ér: “Me wa kwagh hegen mer, hen ijiir igen heen yô, ka ikyôhô i i yer ér whales, i se cir i sha iyange igen la, i lu her, m hen mer ica a gba ga tsô ia cir.”

Hegen ne, mngerem ma ma lu ken hemen la ma cir ken ijime; man hen ijiir la ica je, tso ugen lu, u ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i cir sha mi la tese ér gba u ma inja i zege inyam i i yer ér whale la ia lu hen kpe. Er se lu yevese ikyua yô, orvannya la tese se ikyav mbi ken zwa French sha ityough nagh; man sha ikyônough ki torough ki inyamtoho i i yer ér vulture sea-fowl la, ki ki lu zenden cir, shi ki lu yevese, shi ki lu yevese ki kase un la yô, yange i nenge wang ér gba u zegeikpelaior i i lu hen kpe na la ia lu kwagh u mbakôron ishu ve yer ér blasted whale la, inja na yô, ka zegeikpelaior i i kpe sha zegemnger i i vihi un iyol ga yô, nahan i cir ikyom i or u nan kom ga yô. Alaghga a hen ér, ka nyi i lu ihyuma i vihin tsung i i gbe u imba ikyav ne ia due a mi la; hemba vihin a gar u Ashiria u a lu ken ican la, shighe u mba ve lu uma la ve lu a tahav mbu iin or u nan kpe la ga yô. Sha mimi yô, mbagenev nenge ér kwagh ne ka u wan ishima a mi ga, nahan ma isharen i yaven a or môm kpa ia mgbegha ve u eren kwagh a mi ga. Kpa mbagenev mba vea er kwagh ne her yô mba; shin er mkurem ma i zough a mi hen ambaakaa la ka ma ma lu sha inja ga kpishi nahan kpaa, shi ma lu er ka attar-of-rose nahan kpaa.

Er se lu van ikyua ikyua a ahumbe a a lu been la yô, se nenge or u ken tar u France la ngu a zegeikpelaior i sha uhar hen kpe; man zegeikpelaior i sha uhar ne yange i lu inja er ka ikyôhô i i yer ér nosegay la je kpaa.
