Ahaba kende angahar sha mi, zan zan ikyav la hingir u ungwan er ka ikyagh ki kuhwan ityogholough nahan, maa a gure sha mi sha gbashima, a nenge ér kwagh môm ngu u una cir ga yô, a kaa ér, “Doo, man hegen yô, ka u kôron ikyav.”

Sha ityough kigen yô, i cir ikyagh ki tswamegh kira ga, shi i cir ikyav mbi i samber a mi kposo kposo la cii, i cir mbi sha ityough ki ikyagh ki tswamegh ki i yer ér harpoon la; maa i kende kon la sha kpoghuloo ken soket la; hii hen kpe u shin ityô la je, i cir ikyav la sha avegher ahar sha ityough ki kon la, i kange i sha mi kpoghuloo, i wa ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér twine la ker. Kwagh ne er yô, kon man iyôgh man ikyondo—er Mbaahendanev mba Utar nahan—ve lu her, ve pav ker ga, nahan Ahaba gba yevese yemen a ikyagh ki shojaa la sha ishimavihin; ikyenge i angahar a na a i er sha iyôngo la, man ikyenge i kon u hickory, ve cii ve lu ungwan sha hanma kon cii. Kpa cii man a nyôr ken iyou na yô, i ungwa imo i wanger, i i lu i sha marami ga, i i lu eren kwagh sha inja ga, kpa i hembe vihin cii yô. Kpa, Pip! m-ban u ishimavihin wou, ishigh yough ki ki lu gbilin kpa ki lu a mkom ga la; mummeries ou mba kpilighyol cii yange ve zua sha kwagh u vihin u vihin u tso u lun a mciem iyol la sha inja ga, shi ve laha un kpaa!
