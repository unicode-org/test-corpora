Gadia hegen yô, or u vihin tu la za sha ijiir i usu u yeghen gbilin la, maa a dugh ikyagh ki usu ki i nger sha takerada la kera, a ver eev mbu kiriki mbu mbu lu a ikyav mbi eren tom a mi la, er ka tenpin nahan, hen atô u ikyav mbi usu mbi i yer ér andirons la. Ikyav mbi usu u latriki man briki mba ve lu ker la cii lu a mngerem kpishi, nahan m hen mer ijiir i usu ne er ijiir i civirigh shin iyou i adua i kiriki i i kom sha eev nav mbu ken tar u Congo la yô.

Hegen yô, m kende ashe am sha ityough ki eev mbu mbu lu ken myer la kpoghuloo, m lu zungwen kpa m lu zungwen hen shighe ne—sha u me nenge kwagh u una dondo yô. Hiihii yô, a dugh ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kuman er kwa har nahan ken akpa na a grego la, a ver mbi sha ishigh ki eev mbura tsembelee; maa a ver biskit u tso kpuaa sha mi, a cir usu u u lu ken imenger la, a cir ikyav mbi i gber mbi la hingir usu u nan nagh. Hegen ne, er a cir ikyav mbi tswamev mbi ken usu la fese fese kpishi, shi a dugh ave na kera fese fese her yô (sha kwagh ne yô, lu inja er ngu vihin mbi kpishi nahan), ken masejime yô, a kôrcio u dughun biskit la kera; maa a cir usu man mngerem mara kpuaa, a na wan u kiriki u lun a ikyav mbi utyaav la kwagh sha icivir. Kpa lu inja er diabolo u cuku la soo imba mlu u mngerem ma doon ga la ga nahan; mayange je a cir zwa na ga. Akaa a kpilighyol ne cii yange a lu a ityogholough mbi doon tsung mbi or u nan civir Aôndo la nan lu ungwan yô, lu inja er nan ngu eren msen ken icam i wan shin nan ngu wan icam i mbaaôndo mbaaiev shin icam igen nahan, shighe u i lu eren msen la, ishigh nagh ki lu eren msen sha gbenda u hemban lun sha inja ga yô. Ken masejime yô, a tim eev mbura sha apera, a hide a wa ken akpa na a grego la sha mkighir ga, inja er ka or u eren anumbe u nan lu wan ikyôhô i i kpe la ken akpa nahan.

Akaa a kpilighyol ne cii seer mlu wam u ken ishima la, man er m nenge un hegen lu tesen akav a taver a been tom na u kasua la, shi lu yevese yaven a mo imôngo yô, m hen mer shighe kuma, hegen shin mayange ga, cii man iwanger la i bee ye, u me vihi mlu u m lu ker ica i gbe la.

Kpa shighe u m lu henen sha kwagh u me ôr la, lu shighe u wuan or kpishi. A tôô tomahawk na u a lu sha tebul la, a nenge ityough ki ki la sha anshighe kpuaa, maa a kôr ki sha iwanger, a cir zwa na sha ikyav, a cir zege abeen a mngerem ma taav. Hen shighe u dondon la, iwanger la bee, nahan or u yan ior u ken toho ne, tomahawk u lu hen atô u ashe a na la, gba yevese nyôron sha gambe vea mo. M wa icam, m kera fatyo u eren kwagh ga hegen; man u nan zwa u kpilighyol la a hii u ungwan mo.

M lu ôron kwagh sha zwa u hôngorough, m fa kwagh u a lu ga yô, m gba yevese m gba yevese m gba sha kpekpe, maa m ôr un kwagh, aluer ka an nan lu or shin ka nyi je kpa, a lu viing, nahan m mough m hide m ta imenger la. Kpa mbamlumun nav mba sha zwa la na yô, m zua a mkom fese je ér yange kav inja i kwaghôron wam la ga.

“Ka an-e debel u?”—a mase kaan ér—“u ngu ôron kwagh ga, dam-me, m wua-e.” Man m ôr nahan yô, tomahawk u i wanger la hii u eren tom sha a mo ken ime.

“Or u nan lu a ya, sha ci u Aôndo, Peter Coffin!” I kaa a mo ér: “Or u nan lu a ya! Nenge! Akwati! Mbatyomov mba shaav! yimam!”

“Ôr-e! ôrom or u-ee u lu, shin dam-mo, m wua-e!” or u yan ior la shi ôr kwagh sha mciem, er mzehemen na u cieriyol u tomahawk la lu samber a mngerem ma taav ma tseen la sha a mo zan zan m hen mer ikyondo yam i linen la ia gba usu yô. Kpa sugh sha, hen shighe la or u nan lu a iyou la va nyôr ken iyou la a iwanger ken uwe, nahan m gba duen sha gambe m gba yevese van hen a na.

A hide a ôr kwagh a mo sha mciem ér: “De cian hegen ga, Queequeg heen una vihi ice you i môm ga.”

M kaa a na sha imo i taver mer: “De u eren kwagh sha mciem, man er nan u kaa a mo wer or u nan lu a ikyagh ki tswamegh ki i yer ér infernal harpooneer la ka or u yan ior ga?”

“M hen mer ne fa kwagh ne;—m kaa a we mer, yange lu or u teen akaa ken gar ga he?—kpa hide gema flukes za yav. Queequeg, nenge heen—u sabbee mo, mo sabbee—we or ne yav we—u sabbee?”

“Me sabbee kpishi”—Queequeg ôr kwagh sha mciem, lu ôôn pipa na shi tema sha gambe.

A seer ér: “You gettee in,” maa a er mo ikyav sha tomahawk na, maa a kende akondo la sha vegher ugen. Sha mimi yô, yange er kwagh ne sha gbenda u gomoti tseegh ga, kpa lu sha gbenda u eren kwagh sha mhôôn man u eren iyua sha mimi. M tile m kenger un shighe kar kpuaa. Sha akaa a yange nger sha iyol na cii yô, lu or u yan ior u wang shi lun a inja kpaa. Ka nyi i lu mzeyol u m lu eren ne cii, m hen ken ishima yam mer—or la ka orumace vough er mo nahan: ngu a ityôkyaa i cian mo vough er mo kpa i gbe u me cian un nahan. Hemba doon u yaven vea or u yan ior u nan lu a mciem iyol ga la, a Orkristu u nan me msôrom yô.

"Or u nan lu a ya la," m kaa a na mer, "kaa a na wer a ver tomahawk na her, shin paipi, shin nyityô kwagh u u yer cii; kaa a na wer a de u man taav, sha mkur u kwaghôron yô, nahan me za nyôr vea na. Kpa m soo mer orgen nana lu man taav sha gambe vea mo ga. Ka kwagh u vihin kpishi. Heela tseegh ga, m ngu a insurance ga."

Er i ôr Queequeg kwagh ne yô, a er kwagh sha mi fese, shi a hide a er mo ikyav sha icivir ér m za yav—a cir ishigh sha vegher ugen er ka u kaan ér—“Me bende ne angahar môm ga.”

M kaa a na mer: “M sugh we, or u nan lu a iyou la, alaghga u za.”

M gema m nyôr ker, nahan mayange m yav tsembelee ken uma wam ga.
