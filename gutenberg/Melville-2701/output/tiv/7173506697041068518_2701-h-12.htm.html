IKYAV I TESE 55. Kwagh u Ufoto mba Ikyôhô i I yer ér Whales la.

M ngu ôron ne kwagh u a lu er ka mlu u mimi u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la nahan, er ka i lu sha ishigh ki or u kôron ikyôhô la, shighe u i cir ikyôhô la hen kpe tso u i yer ér whale la sha er or a kôr nan sha mi vough vough yô. Nahan yô, alaghga a doo u se vande yôôn kwagh u ufoto mba ior ve nengen a mi mba ve soo u fan kwagh u a lu zan hemen la, mba ve lu hendan a jighjigh u nan u or u ken tar la zan zan nyian je kpaa la. Ka shighe u a sôr tar sha kwagh ne je ne, sha u tesen ér imba ufoto mba ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la cii mba shami ga.

Alaghga tsô, kwagh u hiihii u a ne ve ior ve lu a mbamhen mba kpeegh mba sha ufoto mbara cii yô, a lu ken akaa a ior mba ken tar u Hindu man Mbaigipiti man Mbagrika ve er sha ave a tse la. Gadia hii sha ashighe a ior lu eren akaa a he kpa ve lu a mimi ga la, shighe u i lu eren ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér marble la sha utempel, man sha akôv a ieev, man sha ikyav mbi utyaav man sha medallion man sha ikyav mbi utyaav man sha ikyav mbi inyar, i lu kôron dolphin sha ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér chain-armor la er mbi Saladin nahan, shi i lu a ityough ki i cir sha ityou er ki St. George nahan la; hii shighe la je kwagh u a lu imba ian i eren kwagh ne la hemba lun ken ufoto mba ior kpishi ve nengen a mi sha kwagh u zege inyam i i yer ér whale la tseegh ga, kpa ka ken akaaôron a sainsi kpishi a ior ve tesen sha kwagh na la kpaa.

Hegen yô, sha hanma gbenda cii yô, foto u a hembe lun u tse u a lu her, u i kaa ér ka u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la, ka i zua a mi ken iyou i i yer ér cavern-pagoda, i i yer ér Elephanta, ken tar u India la. Mba Brahmin kaa ér ken akaa a i er sha ave a i yer ér pagoda la, a a lu a mkur ga la, yange i tsengaôron akaa a kasua man akaa a ior ve eren cii, man hanma kwagh u or nana hen ér nana er cii, ica i gba cii ve mô ve môm ken akaa ne mase van kpôô kpôô ye. Nahan ka kwagh u kpilighyol ga er sha gbenda ugen yô, tom wase u icivirigh u kôron ishu i i yer ér whale la yange ma u lu her sha mlu u ken myer yô. Iyôngo i i yer ér Hindoo whale i i er kwagh na la, ngi hen vegher ugen u kpekpe la, i tese mlu u Vishnu u yange hingir or u ken iyol i or la, u i yer ér Matse Avatar la. Kpa er ikyav ne i lu vegher u or man vegher u inyam i i yer ér whale la nahan kpa, sha u nan ikyav i ikyav i masetyô la tseegh nahan kpa, vegher u kiriki u un la cii ngu shami ga. I hemba lun er ka ityough ki anaconda ki ki lu a mshi ga la nahan, a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér true whale’s majestic flukes la.

Kpa za hen Galleries mba tsev, za nenge hegen foto u zege Orkristu ugen u a gber foto u ishu ne yô; gadia a hemba eren kwagh a Mbahindu mba sha ayange a Mngerem ma Deemee la ga. Ka foto u Guido a tese er Perseus lu yiman Andromeda sha ikyev i zege inyam i i yer ér whale la. Ka hana Guido yange zua a ikyav i kwagh u kpilighyol u i gbe un laa? Shi Hogarth kpa yange nger kwagh u yange er ken takerada na u i yer ér “Perseus Descending” la, nahan a hemba fan kwagh u a lu ken takerada na la ga. Zege ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér Hogarthian monster la mbi eren tom sha ityough ki mnger, mbi kera kôr mngerem kuma inch môm tseegh ga. I lu a inja i howdah sha ityou, man zwa u i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi mbi lu yeghen ker la, alaghga a tôô ér ka Hunda u Mbaahendanev u u dugh hen Ifi u Thames sha mnger u nyôron ken Iyou i Taven Sha la. Shi, ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér Prodromus whales mbi i yer ér Scotch Sibbald la kpa mbi, man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér Jonah’s whale la, er i tese mbi ken ityakerada i i gber ken Ubibilo mba tsev la, kua akaa a i gber sha ikyav mbi tswamev mbi tsev la nahan. Ka nyi se ôr sha kwagh u akaa nee? Kpa kwagh u takerada u i yer ér whale la, u u lu eren tom er ka kon u wain nahan, u u lu cir ikyav i anchor u u lu senen la yô—er i nger u sha ityou man sha upeeji mba ityakerada kpishi i tse man i he cii nahan—kwagh la ka kwagh u doon tsung kpa ka kwagh u kpilighyol tseegh, u i dondo un yô, m nenge mer, ka kwagh u i yer ér vatique figures la. Shin er ior cii ve yer ishu i i yer ér dolphin la nahan kpa, m yila ishu i or u karen ityakerada ne mer ka iniôngon i nôngon ér i zua a ishu i i yer ér whale la; gadia yange i wa ishima ér i er nahan shighe u i hii u eren tom a ikyagh kin la. Or u gberen ityakerada ugen ken tar u Italia u tse yange hii u duen a mi hen ijiir igen ken derianyom u sha 15 la, shighe u i lu Mnder u Henen Kwagh la; man sha ayange la, man zan zan shighe ugen la je kpa, ior kpishi hen ér inyam i i yer ér dolphin la ka inyam i i yer ér Leviathan la.

Ken ufoto mba i er sha mi la man akaa agen a i er sha u sôron ityakerada i tsuaa la ashighe agen u zua a akaa a doon tsung a a bende a zege inyam i i yer ér whale la, ape hanma inja i mngerem ma duen, man jets d’eau, man mngerem ma tseen man mngerem ma doon tsung, Saratoga man Baden-Baden, ka ve due ken ityou na i i lu a vôr ga la. Ken peeji u hiihii u takerada u “Advancement of Learning” la u zua a ikyav mbi tswamev mbigenev mbi mbi soo u fan kwagh yô.

Kpa se de u eren akaa a ior ve fe u eren ga ne cii yô, de se nenge nen ufoto mba leviathan mba ve senge ér ka mbafankwagh mba ve lu a kwaghfan u sangen yeke, mba ve fe kwagh la ve er la. Ken takerada u Harris u tse u yange nger kwagh u zende u sha mnger la, i nger akaa agen a i nger sha kwagh u zende u sha mnger u ken tar u Netherlands, u i nger ken inyom i 1671 la, i yilan ér “Zende u Zende u sha mnger u zan hen gar u Spitzbergen ken tso u i yer ér Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson u ken tar u Friesland, u lu orvesen u tso la.” Ken môm ken ikyav mbi tswamev mbin yô, i tese ikyav i ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whales la, mbi mbi lu er ka zege ikyav mbi tswamev nahan, mbi mbi lu hen atô u ice-isles, mbi i yer ér white bears la mbi lu yevese sha ityough mbi mbi lu uma la. Ken ikyav igen yô, i er kwagh u kpilighyol u i yer ér prodigious blunder la sha u tilen sha ityough ki zegeikpelaior i i yer ér whale la, i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi mbi lu sha ityough ki zegemnger la.

Shi, takerada ugen u i yer ér quarto la kpa ngu, u orgen u i yilan un ér Colnett, u lu Orvesen u Mbahemenev mba Shin Itine ken shoja i tar u Ingila yô, yange nger yô, itinekwagh ér “Zende u zende u zenden hen kpezegemnger u Cape Horn u nyôron ken Zegemnger u ken Imbusutarimese la, sha u seer mzehemen u ijiir i kôron ishu i i yer ér Spermaceti Whale la.” Ken takerada ne yô, i nger kwagh u i nger ér ka “Foto u inyon i i yer ér Physeter shin Spermaceti whale, i i tôô sha ikyav i i wua hen kpezegemnger u tar u Mexico, ken uwer u Agusutu, inyom i 1793 la, i tôô i sha ityough ki tso yô.” M ngu a akperan mer yange orvesen u tso la yange tôô foto u mimi ne sha ci u mtsera u mbatomov nav mba shin tso la ga. U teren kwagh môm tseegh sha kwagh na yô, de m kaa mer, ngu a ishigh ki ki lu a ishigh ki ki lu sha ikyav i tesen ér kwagh ngu zan hemen la, ki ki lu sha zege inyam i i yer ér sperm whale i i vese vough yô, ki a er ishigh ki zege inyam la ki hingir windoo u bow-window u a lu er angahar ataan nahan yô. Ah, orvesen wam u taver iyol, er nan u ne se Yona u nengen sha ishigh kira ga?

Shi ityakerada i Natural History i i hembe kohol sha imoshima sha ci u mtsera u agumaior man mbayev mba kiriki la kpa ngi a kwaghbo u vihin tsung u i er la ga. Nenge ase takerada u ior kpishi ve fe u la, u i yer ér “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature” la. Ken takerada u i gber ken London ken inyom i 1807, u i nger u kpuaa la, i nger akaa a i kaa ér ka “whale” man “narwhale” ker. M soo mer m lu er m ngu a inja ga nahan ga, kpa ikyôhô i i yer ér whale i i nengen a mi ga ne ngi er ka ikyôhô i i gber ikyôhô nahan; man sha kwagh u narwhale yô, aluer or nenge a mi kpuaa tseegh yô, kwagh la una kpiligh nan iyol, sha ci u ken derianyom u sha pue kar anigheni ne, imba hippogriff ne ia lu kwagh u mimi sha hanma nongo u mbayevmakeranta mba lun a kwaghfan cii.

Tsô, shi ken inyom i 1825 la, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, or u fan kwagh u akaa a i gbe a la kpishi, gber takerada ugen u u er kwagh u ikyav mbi tomov mbi i yer ér whale la, u i nger ufoto mba ikyav mbi tomov mbi i yer ér Leviathan la ker kpishi yô. Akaa ne cii ka a shami ga tseegh ga, kpa foto u Mysticetus shin Greenland whale (inja na yô, ka Right whale), je yô, Scoresby, or u nan fe kwagh u benden a inja inyam shon ica i gbe la kpa, pase ér nan ngu a kwagh u nan lu a mi ken mlu u akaa a i gbe a la ga.

Kpa u veren ikyav i cap-sheaf la sha kwagh u kpenga u eren sha inja ga ne cii yô, lu sha ci u or u timen sha akaa a sainsi, Frederick Cuvier, anmgbian u Baron u i fa un kpishi la tseegh. Ken inyom i 1836 la, a gber takerada ugen u i yer ér Natural History of Whales la, a na kwagh u a yer ér foto u ikyôhô i i yer ér Sperm Whale la ker. Cii man u tese hanma or u ken gar u Nantucket cii foto la yô, yange ma u er kwagh u una wase u u hiden ken tar u Nantucket yô. Sha mkaanem mom yô, ikyagh ki tswamegh ki Frederick Cuvier yange nger la ka ikyagh ki tswamegh ga, kpa ka ikyagh ki tswamegh. Sha mimi yô, mayange zua a mtsera u zende u kôron ishu i i yer ér whale la ga (mba ior mban ka ve zua a mi kpishi ga), kpa ka han yange zua a foto shon, ka an nana fa? Alaghga yange zua a mi er or u yange nan lu hemen un ken kwaghfan u sangen yeke, u i yilan un ér Desmarest la kpa yange zua a môm ken akaa a mimi a yange er sha u dughun iyav la nahan; inja na yô, ka kwagh u i gber ken zwa China yô. Shi ka nyi inja i mbayevnomso mba lun a uma mba ve lu a pensel mba ken tar u China mbara ve lu yô, kape ikyav mbi eren kwagh a mi mbi i yer ér queer cups and saucers la mbi pase se je la.

Kpa kwagh u ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i nengen a mi sha ugodobi mbi mbi lu sha ajiir a teen mkurem la yô, ka nyi a ôr sha mbi? Ior kpishi lu Richard u sha Utar la. mbaatsator mba i yer ér whales la, mba lun a ikyav mbi dromedary, shi ve lu a ikyav mbi utyaav kpishi; u yan kwaghyan u aikighe sha tart u mbatsoov utar shin unyiin, inja na yô, ka utso mba i yer ér whaleboats mba ve iv a mbatsoov yô: mbamzeyol vev mba eren tom ken zegemnger u awambe man penti u bluu.

Kpa mbamshi mba ve lu kposo kposo mba i er sha u tesen ikyav i zege inyam i i yer ér whale la ka kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi ga. Wa iko! I tôô ufoto mba sainsi kpishi ken ishu i i cir ken mnger la; man akaa ne nga vough vough er foto u tso u a vihi, u a lu a ityough ki ki vihi la nahan, una tile sha ityough ki inyam i civirigh la iyol i i vough ken iyol i i i lu a ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér hull man spars la cii. Shin er mbaatsator ve tile sha ityough ki mlu ve u vough nahan kpa, Leviathan u a lu uma la ngu a vande eren iyol na sha inja sha inja sha ci u foto na ga. Iyôngo i i yer ér whale i i lu uma la, ken icivir na man inja na cii yô, ka i nenge a mi ken zegemnger ken mngerem ma ior ve fe u ga yô tseegh; man sha mnger yô, vegher na u vesen la kera ngu ken igbar ga, er tso u i cir sha mnger nahan; man ken kwagh u a lu ken mlu la yô, ka kwagh u or u nan kpen nana kera kôr un sha iyol i nan sha ahumbe ga gbem sha won yô, sha er nana kura ikyav mbi nan mbi ageegh mbi nan lu a mi la cii yô. Shi, u ôron kwagh u mkposo u i hen ér ngu hen atô u wan u inyam i i yer ér sucking whale la man inyam i i yer ér Platonian Leviathan, i i vese vough la ga; nahan kpa, sha kwagh u môm ken mbayev mba ikyôhô i i yer ér sucking whales la je kpa, i kende un sha ityough ki tsoo yô, ka imba mlu na u ken won, u a lu er ka eel nahan, u a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev, u a lu kposo kposo la je la, nahan mlu na u vough la diabolo iyol na kpa una kôr un ga.

Kpa alaghga a hen ér, alaghga a zua a akaa a vough a a bende a mlu na u mimi la sha ikyav i ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale la. Venda. Gadia ka môm ken akaa a a hembe lun kwagh u doon u fan sha kwagh u Leviathan ne yô, ka ikyav mbi tswamev mbi nan mhen sha kwagh u mlu na jimin cii kpuaa tseegh. Shin er ikyav i Jeremy Bentham, i i cir sha ci u candelabra ken iyou i koson ityakerada i orgen ken mba ve wua un la, i tese mhen u orbeenyol u lun a mshi iyol, u lun a mshi iyol, u lun a aeren a Jeremy agen a vesen cii nahan kpa; nahan kpa, kwagh môm u a lu inja ne yô, lu u a fa kwagh u a lu ken ikyav mbi leviathan mbi mbi lu a ikyav mbi tswamev la ga. Sha mimi yô, er zege Orkôronishu la a kaa nahan, ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale la tseegh kpa mbi a mzough vea inyam i i we ikyav mbi tswamev mbi iv shi mbi lu a ikyav mbi tswamev la vough er inyam i i yer ér insect la kpa i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér chrysalis mbi mbi cir un iyol la nahan. I tese kwagh u a lu kposo ne ken ityou sha gbenda u kpilighyol, er a tese ken vegher ugen u takerada ne sha apera nahan. Shi i tese un sha gbenda u kpilighyol kpishi ken ikyav mbi ken kpe mbi i yer ér side fin la, ikyav mbi mbi lu er ka ikyav mbi uwegh ku orumace nahan, i dugh ikyav mbi vesen tseegh kera yô. Fin ne ngu a ave anyiin a a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev hanma shighe yô, ka ikyav mbi tesen man mbi ken atô man mbi ring man mbi kiriki je la. Kpa akaa ne cii ka a lu ken ikyondo i i cir iyol la gbem sha won, er ave a orumace a lu ken ikyondo i i er sha ave la nahan. Iyange igen yô, Stubb u a eren kwagh sha inja ga la kaa ér: “Shin er ashighe agen ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la ia er se tom sha lan nahan kpa, mayange je a kera kaa ér una kôr se a ikyav mbi tswamev shio ga.”

Nahan sha atôakyaa ne cii yô, hanma gbenda u u nenge a mi cii, gba u u kure ikyaa wer zege Leviatan ka kwagh u i gbe un môm ken tar u i gbe u una lu her a mshi ga zan zan mkur la. Ka mimi, alaghga foto môm una kôr ikyav la ikyua ikyua a ugen la ica je, kpa mô ve môm kpa nana kôr un vough vough ga. Nahan gbenda u shin tar u fan er ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la i lu jighilii yô ngu ga. Shi gbenda môm u ú zua a mhen u vough je kpaa sha kwagh u mlu na u uma la yô, ka u zan u za kôron ishu i i yer ér whale la iyol you; kpa aluer u er nahan yô, u lu ken kwaghbo u kiriki u una va hingir stove shi una nyôr shin mnger gbem sha won ga yô. Nahan yô, ka inja er yange ma u lu a mciem iyol kpishi sha kwagh u Leviatan ne ga nahan.





IKYAV I TESE 56. Sha kwagh u ufoto mba i yer ér Whale mba ve lu a mbamshi ga la, man ufoto mba mimi mba i tese sha kwagh u ikyôhô i i yer ér Whale la.

Sha kwagh u ufoto mba vihin tsung mba ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la yô, m ngu a ishimaverenkeghen kpishi heen mer me nyôr sha akaa a vihin tsung a i er sha kwagh ve la, a i gbe u a zua a mi ken ityakerada igen, i tsuaa man i ainge cii, hemban je yô, ken ityakerada i Pliny man Purchas man Hackluyt man Harris man Cuvier man mbagenev la. Kpa m kar kwagh la kera.

Ka ityakerada inyiin i i gber sha kwagh u zege inyam i i yer ér Sperm Whale la tseegh m fe ye; Mbayevkasev mba ken tar u Colnett man Huggins man Frederick Cuvier man Beale. Ken ityough ki ki kar la, i ôr kwagh u Colnett man Cuvier. Kwagh u Huggins la hemba u ve la ica je; kpa, sha mlu u vesen yô, u Beale la hemba doon cii. Ikyav mbi Beale a gber sha kwagh u ikyôhô ne cii doo, saa di or u ken atô u foto u ikyôhô itar i i lu a aeren kposo kposo la tseegh, u a kure ityough nagh ki sha uhar la tseegh. Kwaghngeren na u sha ishi, u a lu utso mba ve lu tan num sha Sperm Whales la, shin er a shi nan kpa i hen ér una na ior mbagenev mba ve lu ken iyou i tsan la vea lu a akperan sha kwagh u ior mba ken tar la nahan kpa, ka kwagh u vough shi a lu er ka uma nahan sha mlu u a lu ker la cii. Ikyav mbigenev mbi i yer ér Sperm Whale mbi i gber ken takerada u J. Ross Browne la mbi vough vough; kpa i nger ve sha gbenda u vihin tsung. Kpa ka ibo na ga.

Sha kwagh u Right Whale yô, ufoto mba ve hembe doon cii mba ken Scoresby; kpa i nger ve sha ikyav i kiriki kpishi i ia tese kwagh u i soo yô. Ngu a foto môm tseegh u tesen akaa a ior ve eren sha u kôron ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la, man kwagh ne ka kwagh u vihin kpishi, sha ci u ka sha imba foto ne tseegh, shighe u i er un tsembelee yô, u zua a kwagh u a lu er ka mhen u mimi u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la nahan, er mbakôronishu nav mba ve lu uma la ve nenge a mi nahan.

Kpa aluer i tôô akaa ne cii yô, kwagh u hemban doon cii, shin er sha akaa agen a lu vough ga nahan kpaa, ka akaa a vesen ahar a i gber ken tar u France, a i gber sha inja yô, shi i tôô a ken ufoto mba Garnery ugen a gber yô. Ve tile sha ityough ki ityav mbi i yer ér Sperm man Right Whale la. Ken ikyav i hiihii la i tese zege inyam i i yer ér Sperm Whale la ken agee a na cii, i sember moughon shin inya shin inya hen kpezegemnger, shi i lu tôôn ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i er sha ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér stoven planks la sha ahumbe sha ityou. Ityough ki tso la ki vihi avegher ahar ga, shi i cir ki sha ityough ki zege inyam la tseegh; man u tile sha ityough ki tso la, sha ci u shighe môm u i fe u ôron un ga la, u nenge a or u nahan tso, u nan lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale la, nan cir vegher ugen, shi nan lu eren kwagh u nan lu yevese, inja er nan due hen kpezegemnger nahan. Ieren i kwagh la cii ka i dedoo man i mimi sha gbenda u kpilighyol. Line-tub u i cir un avegher ahar la ngu zenden sha zegemnger u a lu pupuu la; ikyon i i cir sha ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i cir la mbi cir sha mi; ityough ki mbatomov mba shin mnger la ki samber sha zegemnger la sha u tesen mciem kposo kposo; shighe u ken mlu u ahumbe a taver kpishi la, tso la lu yevese sha ijiir la. Alaghga a zua a ibo i vesen sha kwagh u iyol i inyam i i yer ér whale ne, kpa de kwagh la a kar; gadia, sha uma wam cii, m kera fatyo u kôron kwagh u dedoo nahan ga.

Ken ikyav i sha uhar la, tso la ngu eren tom u kôron ikyav hen kpe u zege inyon i i yer ér Right Whale, i i lu yevese la, i i lu eren ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér black weedy la ken zegemnger er ka iwen igen i i lu a mngerem kpishi, i i dugh sha iwo i Patagonia la nahan. Jet na mba tile sha, ve iv, shi ve lu er ka mngerem nahan; nahan sha ci u usu u yeghen ken ikyônough ki torough kira kpishi yô, u hen wer gba u a ya kwaghyan u aikie u taver ishima u i lu yan ken ikyav mbi vesen mbi shin inya la. Iyôngo i shin zegemnger ngi yan ikyav mbi kiriki mbi i yer ér crabs la, man ishu i i yer ér shell-fish la, kua ikyav mbigenev mbi i yer ér candies man maccaroni, mbi ashighe agen Right Whale ka a tôô sha ityough nagh ki ki lu a angev mbura la. Man hen shighe ne cii, leviathan u a lu a zwa u taver la ngu yevese nyôron ken mnger, ngu undun ikyav mbi tswamev mbi pupuu mbi lun a ayôôso kpishi ken ijime na, shi ngu nan tso u cuku la a lu tenger ken mnger er ka tso u i kôr un ikyua a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi tso u eren tom sha mnger nahan. Nahan yô, kwagh u a lu ken hemen la cii ka ayôôso a a lu zan hemen la; kpa ken ijime, sha gbenda u kpilighyol yô, ka mlu u zegemnger u i cir un iyol la, man ikyav mbi tso u a lu a tahav ga la mbi mbi lu a mshi ga la, man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér dead whale la, mbi mbi lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i hemba mbi ityav yô, mbi i we tuta u kôron un la sha ikyônough ki torough ki i yer ér whale-pole la, mbi i we ken ihyungwa na i eren kwagh a mi la.

Ka an nan lu Garnery or u eren tom u pendan akaa la, shin nan lu la, m fa ga. Kpa uma wam sha ci u yange lu or u fan kwagh u yange lu henen la tsembelee, shin or u fan kwagh u ishu i i yer ér whale la tese un kwagh sha gbenda u kpilighyol. Mbafaransa ka ve lu mbayev mba eren tom u pendan akaa. IKYAV I 57. Kwagh u Whale ken Penti; ken zwa; ken Wood; ken Sheet-Iron; ken Iwen; ken Uwo; ken Stars.

Sha uwo u Tower-hill, er u lu yemen shin kpezegemnger u London la, alaghga u nenge a or u zamber kwagh u nan lu a angev mbu kpen iyol (shin kedger , er mbatsoov ve kaa nahan) nan kôr ikyondo i i cir sha mi sha ishigh nagh yô, i tile sha ityough ki kwagh u vihin u yange er shighe u nan kpe angahar la. Kpa, shin er i lu sha ityough ki ikyônough kira gbem sha won nahan kpa, mayange je or u nan lu icankwase u i yer ér whaleman la nan ôr kwagh u ikyônough ki torough ga; kpa, a tile sha ashe a na sha ishimavihin, lu henen sha kwagh u i gber un iyol la.

Ken tar u Pacific cii, kua ken gar u Nantucket man New Bedford man Sag Harbor kpaa, u nenge a ufoto mba ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la, mba mbakôron ishu la iyol ve ve gber sha akôv a ikyôhô i i yer ér Sperm Whale la, shin ikyav mbi i yer ér ladies’ busks mbi i er mbi sha ikyav mbi i yer ér Right Whale la, kua akaa agen kpishi. akaa a injaa a ka ve er sha kwaghfan sha akaa a a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev la, ken ahwa a ve lu memen shin zegemnger la. U lun ken kpan ica i gbe ken Mbakristu Mbaaiev man mzehemen la ka a hide a or ken mlu u Aôndo yange ver nan ker la, inja na yô, ka kwagh u i yer ér savagery la. Mo iyol yam m ngu or u vihin tu, m ngu a jighjigh u nan ga, saa di hen Tor u Mbayan Ior; shi kegh iyol u hendan a na hanma shighe cii.

Ikyagh ki tswamegh ki ken tar u Hawaii ki tsuaa ki i yer ér war-club shin spear-paddle la, ki ki lu a akaa kpishi shi ki lu a akaa a eren tom a mi tsembelee la, ka kwagh u vesen u tesen ishimawan i uumache er takerada u ôron kwagh u zwa Latin nahan. Gadia, ka sha ikyav i zegemnger i i vihi kpuaa tseegh shin ikyav i shark tseegh, i zua a kwagh u kpilighyol u i er sha kon u i yer ér net-net la ye; man i vihi anyom kpishi u eren tom a mi kpoghuloo.

Sha ishimawan i kpilighyol shon i môm, man sha ityough ki shark ki môm tseegh, ki jack-knife na môm u a lu icankwase la, una cir we ikyav i ikyav mbi tswamev, mbi mbi lu er ka ortom nahan ga, kpa mbi i cir mbi kôôsôô ken mlu u mbi lu a mi la, er ikyav mbi tswamev mbi ken tar u Grika, mbi i yer ér Achilles la nahan; man iv a jijingi u hôngorough man mhen u nan ior mhen, er ikyav mbi or u ken tar u Netherlands u tse u injaa ugen, u i yilan un ér Albert Durer la nahan.

Ior ka ve nenge a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér wooden whales, shin ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i gber mbi sha ikyav mbi kiriki mbi lun a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér noble South Sea war-wood la, ken akaa a mbakôron ikyav mbi tswamev mba ken tar u Amerika ve tsengan veren ér vea er la. Mbagenev ken a ve ka i er ve vough vough kpishi.

Hen uya mbagenev mba tsev mba ken agar mba i cir ve sha kpekpe u iyou la yô, u nenge a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér brass whales la, mbi i cir mbi sha ityou sha ci u mba ve gbidyen hunda u hen kpegbenda la yô. Kpa gba u ú lu or u fan kwagh u ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér whale la vighe vighe, ve ú nenge a akaa ne ye; man ka nahan tseegh ga, kpa aluer u soo u hiden nengen a imba ijiir la yô, gba u u lu a vangertiôr shi u tôô latitude man longitude mba ve zough vough vough hen ijiir you i hiihii i tilen her la, gayô akaa a u nenge a mi sha iwo la ka a lu kwagh u shami ga, nahan ijiir you i yange u tile her vough vough la ia gba u a hide a zua a mi sha tom kpoghuloo; er Ityoughkitaregh ki Soloma nahan, ki ki lu her zan zan nyian la, shin er ngise Mendanna u lu a ikyav mbi tswamev kpishi la yange a henda sha mi shi Figuera u beenyol la kpa a nger kwagh u mi nahan kpaa.

Shi shin inya hen kpezegemnger u Antarctica u a lu wanger wanger la m nyôr tso u i yer ér Argo-Navis la, m nyôr nongo u zendan Cetus u a lu a asan la ica je, u a lu ica a ijiir i i yer ér Hydrus man Flying Fish la.

*Ikyav i zegemnger i ior mba kôron ishu i i yer ér whale la ve fe i ér “Brazil Banks” la ngi a iti ne er Banks of Newfoundland nahan ga, sha ci u i lu a ajiir a lun a mngerem ker kpishi ga, shi i lu a ityogholough mbi ungwan kpaa, kpa ka sha ci u mluashe u kpilighyol u a lu er ka toho nahan, u a dugh sha ci u zege mngerem ma brit la ma ma lu sha mnger u Wha la.

Aluer u nenge a mi sha ityouv mbi mast-heads la yô, hemban je yô, shighe u mbi mem shi mbi lu her shighe gôgônan yô, ikyav mbi vesen mbi mbi lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi lun a uma ga la mbi hemba lun er ka iwen i i lu a uma ga la nahan, hemba hanma kwagh cii. Shi er ken ityar i vesen i ken tar u India i i lu keren inyam nahan, ashighe agen orvannya u nan lu ica la nana kar sha ajiir a a lu sha ityoughkitaregh la, nana fa ér ka nahan ga, nana tôô ér ka ajiir a a lu a kwagh u eren ga, a a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev yô; nahan kpa, ashighe kpishi, ka a lu un, u a nenge a inja i leviathan u shin zegemnger ne sha kwa u hiihii la. Shi shighe u i mase kaven ve ken masejime je kpa, mzehemen ve u vesen la ka a na yô, i taver kpishi u nan jighjigh ér alaghga ambaakaa a vesen a a vese kpishi ne aa fatyô u lun kwagh u i gbe un ken iyol, sha avegher a na cii, a uma u a lu ken aniwa shin nyinya la.

Sha kpôô yô, sha avegher agen yô, u kera nenge ma kwagh u i gbe un ken mnger la sha mlu u u nengen a mba ve lu hen kpezegemnger la ga. Gadia shin er mbafankwagh mbagenev mba sha ayange a tsuaa la ve kaa ér akaauma a a lu shin tar cii nga inja i môm ken zegemnger nahan kpa; man shin er i lu u a nenge kwagh la sha gbenda u vesen nahan kpa, alaghga kwagh ne una lu nahan vough; kpa u va sha kwagh u specialties yô, ka han, u tesen ikyav yô, zegemnger a ne ishu i i lu a mlu u mlumun sha erdoo u ikyôhô la? Ka shark u i laha un la tseegh a fatyo u kaan ér ngu a ikyav i tesen ér un ngu kwagh môm a na ye.

Kpa nahan kpa, hen ior mba ken tar la jimin cii yô, i vande nengen ior mba ve lu marnya u tar u zegemnger la ér mba a mlu u ken ishima u a lu kwagh u ior ga shi ve lu eren kwagh a ve sha inja ga yô; shin er se fe ser zegemnger ka terra incognita u a lu gbem sha won nahan kpaa, nahan Columbus yange zende sha ityar i i fe i ga kpishi la sha u za zuan a tar na u ken imbusutarimese u u lu sha ityoughkitaregh la; nahan kpa, sha gbenda u kpilighyol yô, akaa a vihin a hemban vihin cii ken akaa a vihin a a er or cii la, a er ior udubu pue man udubu uderi imôngo mba ve ze sha mnger la sha gbenda u a kera umbur ga shi a lu a inja ga yô; shin er aluer se hen sha mi kpuaa tseegh kpa, se hen ser, er wanye u kiriki nana gba ahon sha kwaghfan u sangen yeke man kwaghfan u nan nahan kpaa, man er nan lu kpishi, ken hemen u doon tsung la, kwaghfan u sangen yeke man kwaghfan la una seer nahan kpaa; nahan kpa gbem sha won, zan zan mkur u mtim la, zegemnger una tuhwa un shi una wua un, shi una vihi tso u hemban lun a icivir man u taver iyol u una er la; nahan kpa, sha u hiden ôron akaa ne hanma shighe yô, or kera ngu a mnenge u cieriyol u zegemnger u yange lu u na sha hiihii la ga.

Tso u hiihii u se ôr kwagh na la, yange lu sha zegemnger, u sha mkighir u Mbaportugal la yange cir tar cii a undu kwagh u kwasecôghol shio yô. Zegemnger shon môm ngu eren tom hegen; zegemnger shon môm yange vihi utso mba ve vihi ken inyom i i kar la. Een, ne ior mba bumen, mngerem ma deemee ma Noa ma bee ga; uhar ken atô u utar mba tar u vough u u lu her la.

Ka nyi i lu mkposo u zegemnger man tar, u uivande mba ve lu sha môm la ve lu uivande mba ve lu sha ugen la ga? Mciem ma cieryol ma ma lu sha tar ga la yange ma lu sha Mbaheberu, zum u shin angahar a Koraki man mba ve lu a na la, tar u uma bugh, u mena ve gbem sha won la; nahan kpa iyange i ainge ne ngi a mi ga, kpa ka sha gbenda shon môm vough zegemnger u uma la ka a mena utso man mbatomov mba shin tso la ye.

Kpa ka zegemnger tseegh a lu orihyom u or u nan lu orvannya hen a na la ga, kpa ka orihyom u ônov nav kpaa; hemba vihin a or u Mbapershia u yange wua mbavanya nav la; de kera wa akaauma a iyol i a mar la ikyo ga. Er ikyôhô i i yer ér savage tigress i i lu yevese ken toho la i lu cir ônov mba i yô, kape zegemnger kpa ka a gbidye ikyav mbi tswamev mbi hemban lun a tahav je kpa sha awen, a undu mbi her vegher ugen a ikyav mbi utso mbi mbi pav ker la je la. Mhôônom ma zungwen ma lu ga, tahav mbu mbu lu hemen un la kpa mbu lu ga. Zegemnger u a lu a orvesen ga ne ngu ôôn iyol shi ngu ôôn iyol er nyinya i utya i i lu a kwaghfan ga, i or u nan lu sha mi la nan kpe yô, i cir tar cii.

Nenge ase er zegemnger a lu ken myer yô; er akaauma a a hembe cier iyol la a lu yevese shin mnger, a lu ken igbar kpishi ga, shi a lu yeren sha atseregh shin inya sha ikyav mbi doon tsung mbi i yer ér azure la yô. Shi nenge ase mlu u diabolo man mdoo u akuraior a ker kpishi a a hembe lun a mhôônom ma zungwen ga la, er mlu u injaa u i er sha inja i anzaakaa kpishi a i yer ér shark la nahan. Shi hen ase sha kwagh u ior mba ve ye ior sha tar cii, mba ve lu shin zegemnger la; mba akaa a ve gbe la cii ve lu yan ayol a ve, ve lu nôngon ityav mbi tsôron hii shighe u tar hii la je.

Nenge ase akaa ne cii; man gema hen tar u u lu a ikyayue, u u lu kundu kundu shi u hembe lun a inja ne; nenge nen sha akaa ne cii, zegemnger man tar; man u zua a kwagh u kpilighyol u a zough sha kwagh ugen ken iyol you ga he? Gadia er zegemnger u cieriyol ne a kase tar u u lu a ikyayue la nahan, kape ken uma u or kpa i lu a tar u Tahiti môm u u lu hen kpezegemnger, u u iv a bem man iember, kpa u lu a akaa a cieriyol a uma u i fe un vegher môm la cii je la. Aôndo a kura u! De gbe duen hen kpezegemnger la kera ga, mayange u kera hide ga!

IKYAV I TESE 60. Kwagh u Line.

Sha kwagh u mlu u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la, u se lu ôron kwagh na kpuaa ne, kua sha u se hemba kaven akaa a a lu kwagh môm a mi a i tese sha ajiir agen la cii yô, m ngu ôron kwagh u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale-line i i eren ahir, i ashighe agen i lu kwagh u vihin tsung la heen.

Ikyav i yange i hii eren tom a mi u kôron ishu la lu i hemp u hemban doon cii, i wa tar sha mi kpuaa, i wa un ker ga, er ka i er sha ikyav mbi tswamev nahan; gadia er tar, er ka i er tom a mi hanma shighe nahan kpa, ka a na hemp la a hemba lun a inja hen or u nan gber ikyav la, shi a na ikyav la iyol i i hemba lun or u nahan tso u eren tom a mi hanma shighe yô; nahan kpa, ka iyenge i ior mba ve lu a mi la tseegh ia na ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale-line la mbi taver kpishi sha ci u ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i gbe u a cir mbi la ga; kpa er mbatsoov kpishi ve hii u henen nahan, tar jimin cii ka a seer mlu u ikyagh ki tswamegh shin tahav ga, shin er ki lu a mshi ga shi ki lu a mshi ga nahan kpaa.

Ken anyom a karen ne, ikyagh ki kôron ishu ki i yer ér Manilla la hemba eren tom a hemp sha u eren ikyav mbi i yer ér whale-lines la cii; gadia, shin er i lu a mshi er hemp nahan ga nahan kpa, i hemba taver, shi i hembe lun a mshi shi i lu a mshi ga; man me seer mer (er æsthetics a lu ken akaa cii nahan), hemba doon shi hemba lun vough a tso, a hemp. Hemp ka or u dusky, u lun a mshi ga, u nan lu inja er ka or India nahan; kpa Manilla yô, ngu er ka or u ken tar u Circassia u lun a ice i zenaria nahan.

Iyôngo i i yer ér whale-line la ngi a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi uhar ken atô u atar tseegh. Aluer u nenge a mi sha hiihii la yô, u hen wer u taver er u lu la ga. Sha u karen yô, ikyav mbi tswamev mbi môm man akunduatargber mbi hanma mbi yô, mbi a kôr ikyav mbi tswamev mbi lun a ikyav mbi yuhwan deri môm man ikyundu; nahan ikyagh kira cii kia tôô ikyav mbi yuhwan mbi kuman er tôn utar nahan. Ikyav i sperm whale-line i ior kpishi ve fe i la ngi a mzehemen u hemban er ukilomita deri uhar nahan. I cir un ken ijime i tso la ken mnger, lu er ka ikyagh ki esen iyou ki i yer ér worm-pipe of a still though la nahan ga, kpa ka sha er una hingir kwagh u lun er ka cheese nahan, u a lu a “sheaves” mba ve lu a ikyav mbi tswamev kpishi yô, shin ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér concentric spiralizations la, mbi lun a ikyav mbi tswamev ga, kpa mbi lu “heart” u cheese u a til sha ityough ki tube la. Er kwagh u a lu a mshi shin a lu a mshi ga ken ikyav la una, una yevese una due yô, una tôô uwegh ku orgen shin angahar a nan shin iyol i nan cii kera nahan, ka i er tom a kwagh u hemban cii sha u wan ikyav la ken ikyondo i i cir la. Mbatomov mbagenev mba ve eren tom ne yô, vea vihi shighe ve kuma er pepe môm nahan, vea tôô ikyav la vea za ver sha kwavaôndo, maa vea cir ikyav la vea yem a mi shin inya, vea yem a mi hen ijiir i i yer ér tub la, sha er vea cir ikyav la sha er ia kera lu a ikyav mbi tswamev man mbi mbi lu a mshi ga yô.

Ken utso mba ken tar u Ingila yô, ka i er tom a ikyav mbi tswamev mbihar sha ityough ki ikyav mbi tswamev mbi môm; line môm la ka i lu eren tom a mi gbem sha won ken ubar mba uhar mbara cii. Kwagh u injaa ugen ngu ken kwagh ne; sha ci u ikyav mbi tswamev mbi uhar mbin ka mbi kiriki kpishi mbi hemba nyôron ken tso la fese, shi mbi kera cir tso la kpishi ga; kpa, ikyagh ki esen iyou ki ken tar u Amerika, ki ki lu er angahar atar nahan la, ki ki lu a ikyav mbi eren tom a mi vough vough la yô, ka ki er tom a ikyav mbi yuhwan kpishi sha ci u tso u ikyav mbi u lu a mi la mbi lu di inch môm man tiôn tseegh yô; gadia ityough ki tso u i yer ér whale-boat la ki lu er ka ice i vesen nahan, ia tôô ikyav mbi yuhwan kpishi mbi i samber a mi yô, kpa ia tôô ikyav mbi yuhwan kpishi ga. Shighe u i cir ikyav i canvas i i cir sha mi la sha line-tub u ken tar u Amerika la yô, tso la a lu inja er ngu duen a zege cake u ivesegh u kpilighyol u una na mbaatsator mba i yer ér whales la nahan.

I ver avegher a ikyav la cii ken igbar; ityough ki shin ityô ki ki kure sha eye-splice shin loop ki ki dugh shin inya ki ki lu sha kpe u tub la, ki ki lu cir sha ityough ki ki ki lu paleghaa a hanma kwagh cii la. Mzough u mkur u shin inya ne ka kwagh u hange hange sha akaa ahar. Ken akaa ne cii, ka i gema ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la er ka ikyagh ki man msôrom nahan, inja na yô, i due a mi hen tso ugen i yem a mi hen ugen la; shin er tso u hiihii la ka a lu ikyua a na hanma shighe sha u wasen kwase na nahan kpaa.
