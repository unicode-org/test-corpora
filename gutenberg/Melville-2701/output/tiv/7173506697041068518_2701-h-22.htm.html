Ken tso u i yer ér forecastle la, mbatsoov mbara yange ve cir akaa a ve lu a mi la sha mimi, ve iv a mi; i seer sha kwagh ne sha gbenda u doon tsung ér, or u nan lu yan kwagh la yange nan gbidye ityough sha ikyagh ki esen kwagh ki ki hembe lun a tahav cii la, maa nan iv a mi; ér or u nengen sha iyou la yange cir ikyagh ki kôfi ki ki lu her la, maa iv ki; ér mbatomov mba eren ikyav mbi utyaav mbara yange ve cir ityough sha akôv a ikyav mbi utyaav vev, ve iv a mi; ér mimi je hanma kwagh cii iv a mshiam ma nomsoor, saa di akpa a pantaloons a orvesen u tso la tseegh, man mba yange ver ér una wa ave ker la, sha u eren shiada sha iyol na ér un zua a mkom cii.

Sha ityough ki tso u i yer ér quarter-deck la, mbatomov mba gomoti man mba ve lu eren tom a harpoone la lu eren iniumbe vea mbayevkasev mba lun a ikyav mbi olev mba ve yevese vea ve ken Icile i Polynesia la; shighe u i lu eren tom a mi ken tso u i er un sha inja, u i kange un sha ityough ki tso u vesen la, ior mba lun a ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér whale ivory mbi mbi lu wanger wanger la, lu hemen iniumbe i i yer ér hilarious jig la. Shighe ne, mbagenev mba ve lu ken kômpeni u tso la lu eren ayôôso sha kwagh u sôron awen a i yer ér try-works la, ape i dugh zege ikyav mbi tswamev mbin la kera yô. U hen wer ve lu tuur Bastille u i laha un la, imba ihyer i vihin tsung i ve lu ôron la, er i lu haan briki man mngerem ma ma lu a inja ga hegen la shin zegemnger nahan.

“Va nyôr tso, va nyôr tso!” orvesen u shoja i Bachelor u lun nomsoor la kaa nahan, a kende gilasi man ikyagh ki man kwagh sha ahumbe.

"U ngu a iember kpishi. Za hemen a tso. U vihi ior mbagenev kpa?"

"Ka u ôron kwagh ga—mba ve lu hen icile la uhar, ka nahan tseegh;—kpa va nen shin tso, mbabeenyol, va nen. Ica a gba ga tsô me dugh ikyav mbi tswamev mbin sha ishigh yough. Va nen, ne (merry's the play); tso u iv a ior man u hiden ken ya."

Nahan za sha igbenda you, mo kpa me za wam. Za hemen her! Ver tso cii, shi kura un sha ahumbe!"

IKYAV I TESE 117. Mzough u Mbahemenev mba Shin Itine.

Ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la inyiin i yange i wua hen tugh mbura la kpe kpe kpe kpe; môm, ica a ahumbe; môm, u a lu ica ga yô, ngu zan hen ahumbe; môm ken hemen; môm ken ijime. IKYAV I TESE 118. Kwagh u Kwatar.

Shighe u Line la mgbôghom; man hanma iyange yô, Ahaba yange una due ken iyou na i tsan la una kenger sha yô, or u hemen tso u nan lu tsevaa la nan kôr ikyav mbi nan sha inja, nahan mbatsoov mba ve lu a gbaishima la ve yevese ve za hen ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i cir mbi la fese, ve tile her ashe a ve cii a ver sha ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i cir mbi sha kon la; yange lu a ishimawan sha kwagh u tindi u i kaa ér a tese ityough ki tso la sha gbenda u i yer ér equator la ga. Shighe kar yô, tindi la va. Yange lu ican kpishi sha iyange i atetan; man Ahaba tema sha ityough ki tso na u a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev kpishi la, lu nôngon u nengen sha iyange ayange ayange sha er una fa latitude na yô.

Hegen ne, ken zegemnger u Japan la, ayange a ken shighe u uraahumbe la ka a lu er ka mngerem ma doon tsung nahan. Iyange i ken tar u Japan i i lu wanger wanger, i i lu tan iwanger ga la, ngi inja er ka kwagh u a lu yeghen sha zegemnger u a lu er ka gilasi nahan, u i fe un ga la. Sha ngu inja er ka lac; abeen nga ga; horizon la ngu eren tom; man mlu u ken igbar u iwanger i i lu a mkur ga ne ngu er iengem i ikyonough ki torough ki Aôndo ki i fetyô u wan ishima a mi ga la nahan.
