IKYAV I 94. Ityough ki sha ave.

Yange i va a ishu i i yer ér whale i Stubb la, i yange i yam i sha inyaregh kpishi la, i va a mi hen kpe u Pequod la sha inja, ape i eren ityom i sôron man u tôôn i sha, i yange i vande ôron kwagh u i vighe vighe la cii, hanma shighe yô, zan zan i za ar sha u eren baling u i yer ér Heidelburgh Tun, shin Case la je kpaa.

Shighe u mbagenev lu eren tom u masetyô ne la, mbagenev lu eren tom u tôôn ikyav mbi vesen mbi i yer ér tubs la yemen a mi, nahan fese je mbi iv a ikyav mbi nomsoor; man shighe u vough la una va yô, i er tom a ikyav mbi tswamev mbin môm tsembelee cii ve i mase zan hen ijiir i karen ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér try-works la ye, mbi i yer ér anon la.

Yange cir shi hingir krista je yô, shighe u m tema vea mbagenev kpishi sha ishigh ki zege mngerem ma Constantine ma ôôn iyol ker la, m nenge a mi sha gbenda u kpilighyol, i hingir ikyav mbi tswamev, mbi lu eren msen heen man heen ken vegher u mngerem la. Yange lu tom wase u sôron ikyav mbi tswamev mbin u hiden mbi hingir mngerem. Ka tom u doon tsung man u lun a mshi ga! Ka nahan ve sha ayange a tsuaa la, ior kpishi soo kwagh u sôron iyol ne ye. Imba kwagh ne hemba wanger! imba kwagh u doon tsung ne! imba kwagh ne! imba kwagh u doon tsung ne! Yange m wa ave am ker miniti kpuaa tseegh yô, ave am hingir er ka ikyôhô nahan, maa a hii u eren serpentine shi eren spiralise.

Er m tema her kundu kundu, m cir angahar am sha kpekpe u tso la; zum u i er tom kpoghuloo sha u eren windlass la kera yô; sha ikyev i sha kwavaôndo u lun bem bem; tso la lu sha ikyev i indolent sail, shi lu yevese sha mkor nahan; er m lu ôôn ave am hen atô u ikyav mbi tswamev mbi lun a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi mbi lu a mshi ga, mbi i cir mbi ken atô u ihwa môm la; er yange ve vihi akaa kpishi sha ave am nahan, shi ve haa akaa a ve lu a mi la cii kera, er wain ve u a lu a ikyon i gerep i i vese tsembelee la nahan; er m lu ôôn mkurem ma ma lu a ihyôghonough ga la yô,—sha kpôô kpôô man sha mimi yô, lu er mkurem ma ikyon i i yer ér spring violets la nahan; M ngu pasen ne mer, sha shighe la m lu uma er ka ken toho u musky nahan; M hungur kwagh u iceghzwa yase i vihin tsung la cii; ken mngerem ma nomsoor ma ma lu a mshi ga la, m ôô ave am man ishima yam sha mi; Yange m hii u nan jighjigh u aiegh u tse u i yer ér Paracelsan la icivir ér awambe a nomsoor nga a inja kpishi sha u been a ishimavihin; shighe u m lu ôôn iyol ken mnger la, m nenge mer m kera ngu a isharen i bo, shin ishimavihin, shin ipila i eren ga, sha hanma inja cii.

Kam! kam! kam! pepe la cii; M cir mngerem ma nomsoor mara zan zan mo iyol yam kpa m nôngo u nyôron ker; M cir ikyav mbi tswamev mbin zan zan mlu u mciem iyol u kpilighyol va sha a mo; man m nenge mer m ngu eren ave a mba m eren tom a ve imôngo la ken mi sha apera, m lu henen mer ave a ve ka globules mba lun a mshi ga la. Imba mlu u mlu u ken ishima u a ngee kpishi, u dooshima, u lun ijende, u dooshima ne yange mar kwagh u avocation ne ye; ér ken masejime yô, m lu eren ve ave hanma shighe, shi m lu kenger ve sha ashe sha ishimavihin; er u kaa wer,—Oh! mbadoon mo ishima, er nan ve se kera lu a iwuese sha hanma kwagh u ior ve eren ken nongo u ior cii ga, shin se kera fa kwagh u vihin shin iwuhe kpuaa tseegh ga? Va; se cir nen ave sha ajiir cii; ei, se cii se cir ayol a ase ken ayol a ase; se cir nen ayol a ase sha tar cii ken mlu u mlu u dedoo man mlu u mlu u dedoo la.

Yange ma m za hemen u eren tom a mshiam mara gbem sha won yô! Hegen yô, er sha akaa kpishi a a er mo ica i gbe, a m hide m nenge a mi la yô, m kav mer sha akaa cii yô, gba u or nana va mase hiden ijime, shin nana gema mnenge u nan lu a mi ér nana zua a msaanyol la; u veren un hen ijiir igen ken kwaghfan shin ken mlu u ken ishima la ga; kpa ken kwase yô, ishima, gambe, tebul, ikyônough ki torough, ijiir i usu, tar; hegen u m kav akaa ne cii ne, m wa iyol u eren kwagh sha mi gbem sha won. Er m lu henen sha mbampase mba sha mnenge mba m nenge a mi tugh la yô, m nenge a mbatyomov mba shaav mba ve lu sha ityough ki paradiso la, hanmô ve lu a uwegh ku nan ken iyongo i spermaceti.

Hegen ne, er se lu ôron kwagh u mzeram ma nomsoor yô, doo u se ôr akaa agen a a lu kwagh môm a mi yô, ken tom u wan agoyol sha mzeram ma nomsoor sha ci u ityom i karen iyol la.

Hiihii yô, ka white-horse, u i yer ér nahan la, ka i zua a mi sha vegher u ishu u a lu a mshi ga la, shi i zua a mi sha avegher a a lu a mshi ga la kpaa. Ka i taver a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi mbi lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér congealed tendons la—ka ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér wad of muscle la—kpa mbi lu a mkurem her. Ka a pav nyinya i i yer ér white-horse la kera yô, i hii u eren ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér oblongs la cii ve i mase zan a mi hen ijiir i i eren ikyav mbi tswamev la ye. Ve lu er ka ikyav mbi i yer ér Berkshire la nahan.

Plum-pudding ka ishember i ka i yila avegher agen a inyam i i yer ér whale la, a a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér blubber la heen man heen, shi ashighe kpishi a lu a inja kpishi ken mlu u inyam i i yer ér whale la. Ka kwagh u a hembe nan or mmem, u nan lu a inja shi a doo u nengen a mi yô. Er iti na i tese nahan, i lu a ikyav mbi doon tsung, mbi lun a ikyav mbi doon tsung, mbi lun a ikyav mbi doon tsung mbi lun a ikyav mbi doon tsung mbi i yer ér crimson man purple la. Ka plum u rubi, u i tese ufoto mba citron la. Er u lu a ityôkyaa nahan kpa, ka i taver we u yangen iyol you u yan kwagh ne. M pase, mer shighe ugen la m ii ken ijime i foremast la sha u me kar un yô. I lu kwagh u doon tsung er yange ma m hen mer alaghga kwaghyan u tor u i dugh ken ikyer i Louis le Gros la yange una lu nahan, m hen mer yange i wua un sha iyange i hiihii i shighe u iyôngo i i yer ér venison la been kera yô, man shighe u iyôngo la lu shighe u i lu eren wain u injaa ugen ken sule u wain u ken tar u Champagne la.

Kwagh ugen kpa ngu, man u a lu kwagh môm tseegh yô, u a dugh ken mlu u kasua ne yô, kpa m nenge mer ka kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi u pasen un. I yila un ér slobgolion; iti i hiihii i i yer mba ve lu a ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér whalemen la, man kape mlu u kwagh u i yer ér substance la kpa a lu je la. Ka kwagh u a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér inefably oozy, stringy affair la, u ka i hemba zuan a mi ken ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér sperm la, shighe u i cir mbi ica i gbe, maa i cir mbi kera yô. M nenge mer ka ikyav mbi doon tsung mbi mbi lu a mshi ga, mbi mbi lu ken ikyav la, mbi mbi lu kohol imôngo la.

Gurry, u i yer ér Gurry la ka ishember i i doo u mbakôron ishu mba i yer ér right whale la vea ôron yô, kpa ashighe agen ka mbakôron ishu mba i yer ér sperm la ve eren tom a mi sha apera ye. Ishember ne ngi ôron kwagh u kwagh u a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér glutinous la, u i cir sha ityough ki zegemnger u i yer ér Greenland shin right whale la, man akaa ne kpishi ka a cir ikyav mbi uumace mba ve lu sha ian i vesen ga, mba ve lu keren Leviathan u a lu a icivir ga la.

Mbanipi. Strictly ishember ne ka ishember i ken zwa u whale la ga. Kpa er mbakôronishu mba i yer ér whalemen la ve eren tom a mi nahan, kwagh ne hingir nahan. Ikyagh ki eren tom a mi ki i yer ér whaleman’s nipper la ka ikyagh ki ki lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kpuaa, ki i gber ki sha vegher u ityough ki Leviathan ki ki lu a mshi ga la: ki lun a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi inch môm, man ki ki shi her la yô, ki lu er ka ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér hoe la nahan. Edgewise yange mough sha ityough ki tso u a lu a mkurem la, nahan a er tom er ka squilgee u i er sha ikyondo nahan; man sha u eren akaa a doon tsung a a lu a iti ga, er ka ahir nahan yô, ka a urugh akaa a hôngorough cii a va a mi imôngo.

Kpa u fan akaa a a lu ken myer ne cii yô, gbenda wou u hemban doon cii yô, ka u senen ken iyou i blubber-room la fese, nahan u lam a mbakwarev mba ken purusu la ica i gba. I vande ôron kwagh u ijiir ne ér ka ijiir i i we ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér blanket-pieces la ker, shighe u i dugh mbi kera shi i kende mbi sha zegemnger u i yer ér whale la yô. Shighe u vough u sôron akaa a a lu ker la una va yô, iyou ne ka i lu ijiir i ior mba ken tar u Amerika cii ve cian yô, hemban je yô tugh. Sha vegher ugen la, i wanger sha lantern u a lu a iwanger ga yô, i ver ijiir igen sha ci u mbatomov mbara. Ka ve hemba zan uhar uhar,—or u eren pike man gaff man or u eren spade. Ikyekye i i yer ér whaling-pike la ngi kwagh môm a ikyagh ki tswamegh ki i yer ér frigate la. Gaff la ka kwagh u a lu er ka ikyagh ki tswamegh nahan. Or u eren tom a ikyagh ki tswamegh la ka nan kôr ikyagh ki tswamegh ki i yer ér blubber la sha ikyagh ki tswamegh kigen, nan nôngo ér ki de cir ga, er tso la a lu yevese shi a lu yevese yemen yô. Shighe ne, or u eren tom a spade la tile sha ikyondo la iyol i i, lu eren tom a mi sha inja vough vough, hingir ikyav mbi anyinya mbi i yer ér portable horse-pieces la. Spade ne ngu a ikyav er hone una er nahan; angahar a or u eren tom a spade la nga a akôv ga; kwagh u a til sha mi la una gba yevese un ica ashighe agen, una kera lu a tahav mbu yangen un ga, er ka ikyagh ki tswamegh nahan. Aluer a cir angahar a na môm, shin a or u wasen un tom la yô, a kpiligh we iyol kpishi kpa? Ior mba ve lu mbatomov mba blubber room la mba a angahar kpishi ga.





IKYAV I 95. Kwagh u Kaso.

Aluer yange u nyôr tso u i yer ér Pequod la sha shighe ugen u i lu eren tom u wuan inyam i i yer ér whale la yô; man yange wea zende u za hemen ikyua a ikyônough ki torough kira yô, m na jighjigh mer ma u nenge a kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi, u a lu kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi yô, u ma u nenge a mi her, u a lu cir sha ityough ki ikyônough ki torough ki i yer ér lee scuppers la. Ka ityungu i kpilighyol i i lu ken zege ityough ki zege inyam i i yer ér whale la ga; ka kwagh u kpilighyol u a lu ken zwa na u shin inya u u lu a ikyav ga la ga; ka ivande i ityough nagh ki ki lu vough la ga; kwagh môm ken akaa ne una kpiligh we iyol ga, er vegher u ikyondo i i yer ér cone i i fe kwagh u i ga la nahan,—i hemba or u ken tar u Kentucky gôgônan, i lu er angahar ahar nahan hen ityough nagh, shi i lu jet-black er Yojo, eev mbu ebony mbu ken gar u Queequeg la nahan. Man mimi je, ka eev; shin, sha ayange a tsuaa la, mlu u ú la lu. Imba eev mbu i zua a mi ken ikyon i ken myer i Torkwase Maaka ken Yudia la; man sha ci u mcivir u Tor Asa, wan na, yange dugh un sha, vihi eev mbura, nande mbu hingir kwagh u hôngorough hen kpezegemnger u Kederon, er i nger ken ityough ki sha 15 ki Takerada u Utor u Hiihii la ken ime nahan.

Nenge ase or u nahan tso u i yer ér mincer la, u a lu van hegen ne, shi mba ve lu a na imôngo uhar mba wasen un yô, ngu suen tso u i yer ér grandissimus la kpoghuloo, er mbatsoov ve yer un nahan, shi a cir ave, a gba yevese yemen a mi inja er ka or u nan lu a grenadier u nan lu tôôn ijende i nan i i kpe ken toho la nahan. A kende a mi sha ityough ki tso u i yer ér forecastle la, nahan hegen a za hemen u dughun ikyav mbi mbi lu a ikyav mbi tswamev la kera, er or u keren inyam ken Afrika ka nan dugh ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér boa la nahan. Kwagh ne ka a er yô, a gema ikyondo la ken won, er ka angahar a pantaloon nahan; ka a na un mzehemen u dedoo, nahan a nôngo u seer mzehemen na kwa har; man ken masejime yô, a penda un, a samber a mi tsembelee, ken ikyagh ki esen kwagh la, sha er una hôr yô. Ere long, i tôô un inya; zum u a dugh angahar atar a a lu er angahar atar nahan la kera, a yem sha ityough ki ki lu a ikyav la, maa a cir ajiir ahar a ior vea cir ave sha mi sha ityough kigen la yô, a cir iyol na sha iyol na ken mi. Mincer la tile hegen sha ishigh ki u invest ken canonicals mba i yila un la cii. Er i lu u una umbur tindi na cii nahan kpa, inyaregh kin tseegh kia kura un sha inja, shighe u a lu eren ityom i vesen i ken afishi na la.

Ofishi la ka a lu u sôron ikyav mbi anyinyav mbi i yer ér blubber la sha u eren ikyav mbi eren ikyav mbi eren; ka i er tom ne sha nyinya i i er sha kon, i i lôô i sha ityough ki kpekpe u iyou la, shi i lu a ikyagh ki esen iyou ki ki lu a ikyav mbi eren tom ker la shin inya, ki ikyav mbi i gber mbi la mbi gba shin mi, fese er ikyav mbi i gber sha tebul u or u nan lu a kwaghfan kpishi la nahan. I cir ikyav mbi tswamev mbi dedoo; u teman sha ikyônough ki torough ki ior ve nengen a mi yô; awashima u sha kwagh u Bibilo; ka or u nan soo u lun bisôp u tamen je ne, ka wanye u lun Pope je ne!*

*Bibilo ngi ôron kwagh! Bibilo ngi ôron kwagh! Ka ikyenge i i gem ga i i dugh hen mba ve vese ayol a ve la zan hen or u nan lu eren mincer la je ne. I wa un kwagh ér a wa ikyo, shi a cir tom na sha avegher a kiriki er una fetyô la cii, sha ci u aluer un er nahan yô, tom u sôron mkurem la ka u za hemen fese, shi iyenge i mkurem la kpa i seer kpishi, shi alaghga u seer mlu u mkurem mara kpaa.





IKYAV I 96. Tom u Try-Works.

Dugh utso nav mba i kende sha la kera yô, or u ken tar u Amerika u nan lu a ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér whale la kpa nan ngu kposo sha kwagh u nan eren sha u nôngon kwagh la. A tese kwagh u kpilighyol u a er yô, ka iwen i hemban taver cii i i kohol a kon u oak man hemp la, i hingir tso u i maa un been la. Ka inja er i tôô ikyagh ki esen kwagh a mi ki i yer ér briki la i yem a mi hen akôv a na nahan.

I ver ikyav mbi eren tom a mi mbi i yer ér try-works la hen atô u foremast man mainmast, ka vegher u a hembe lun a ian i eren tom a mi je la. Ikya i i lu shin inya la ngi a tahav mbu kpilighyol, i sôr i sha er ia kôr cio u wan ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i er sha briki man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi lun er ka ikyav mbi tswamev nahan, mbi lun er angahar pue nahan, shi mbi lu angahar ataankaranyiin yô. Ityough ki iyou la ki nyôr ken kpekpe u iyou la ga, kpa i kange iwen la sha ityough ki iyou la sha akôv a iyôgh a lun a ikyav mbi yuhwan, a i cir sha mi sha avegher a na cii, shi i cir ki sha ikyon la. Sha avegher a iyou la yô, i cir i sha kon, shi sha ityough ki iyou la cii i cir zege hunda u u lu a kpekpe, u i cir sha kpekpe u iyou la yô. Aluer se dugh ikyav mbi tswamev mbin kera yô, se tese ikyav mbi tswamev mbi vesen mbi i yer ér try-pots la, mbi mbi lu uhar la, man hanma mbi yô, mbi lu a ikyav mbi tswamev kpishi. Shighe u i lu eren tom a mi ga yô, ka i kura ve sha gbenda u kpilighyol. Ashighe agen ka i cir ve sha iwen i soap man mngerem, zan zan ve kar wanger ken atô er ka ikyav mbi punch mbi azurfa nahan. Shighe u i lu kuran tugh la, mbatomov mba shin tso mbabeenyol mbagenev mba lun a inja ga vea nyôr ker vea cir ayol a ve her vea za yav. Shighe u i lu eren tom u sôron ve la—or môm ken hanma ikyagh, ve lu vegher ugen a ugen la—ka i za hemen u lamen a ior kpishi ken myer, sha zwa u iyôgh. Shi ka ijiir i henen sha kwagh u namba vighe vighe kpaa. Ka ken try-pot u sha uwegh ku yanegh u Pequod la, u iwen i soap la i lu zenden cir mo sha gbashima la, yange m hii u nengen a kwagh u kpilighyol ne ye, ér ken kwagh u geometry yô, akaa a a lu zenden sha cycloid la cii, inja na yô, iwen yam i soapstone la, aa mough sha hanma ijiir cii sha shighe môm vough.

Aluer i dugh ikyagh ki usugh ki ki lu sha ishigh ki ajiir a karen kwagh la kera yô, i nenge a iwen i i lu gbilin sha vegher shon la, i nyôr ker sha zwa u iyôgh uhar u ikyagh ki usu la, ki ki lu shin inya hen atô u ikyav mbi usu la jighilii. I wa hunda u iyôgh u taver kpishi sha zwa ne. I yange usu u yeghen kpishi la u nyôron hen kpemnger, sha u eren tom a ikyagh ki koson mngerem ki ki lu shin inya hen ijiir i i cir tom la cii yô. Sha ikyev i ikyagh ki i we ken ijime yô, mngerem ma ma lu ken ikyagh kin la ma hide ma iv fese er ma lu yeghen nahan. Ior mba ken won mba a ikyav mbi utyaav ga; ve bugh jighilii sha kpekpe u ken jime la. Man heen yô, de se hide se nenge sha mi kpuaa.

Ka er ahwa ataankaranyiin tugh nahan i hii u eren tom u karen tso u i yer ér Pequod la sha zende u i lu eren hegen ne ye. Yange lu u Stubb una nenge sha kwagh u kasua la ye.

"U wa iyol cii her? Dugh ikyônough ki torough, nahan, hii u yan kwagh. U sôr kwaghyan, u cir tom la." Kwagh ne lu kwagh u ican ga, sha ci u or u gban ikyon la yange lu eren tom a ikyav nav mbi eren tom a mi la ken atse a usu la sha gbenda la cii. Ka heen je i kaa ér ken zende u kôron ishu i i yer ér whale la, gba u a na usu u hiihii u u lu ken try-works la kwaghyan sha anshighe kpuaa. Shighe ka una karen yô, i kera er tom a kon ga, saa di sha u tan usu fese sha mkurem ma vesen. Sha mkaanem mom yô, ka a kar ikyagh ki esen iyou ki i yer ér scraps shin fritters la kera yô, ki lu a akaa a doon tsung a ki lu ker la her. Fritters mban ka ve na usu u yeghen la kwaghyan. Er or u nan kpe sha ci u jighjigh u nan u i yer ér plethoric burning martyr la nahan, shin or u nan lu a isharen i vihin iyol i nan tseegh, ka nana cir usu yô, ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la ka i na un mkurem shi iyol i nan kpa i cir usu. Yange ma a vihi mngerem ma nan iyol i nan! gadia mngerem nam ma vihin tsung u ôôn, man gba u ú ôôn ma, man ka nahan tseegh ga, kpa gba u ú lu ker sha ci u shighe la. I lu a ihyuma i ior mba ken toho, i ior mba ken tar u Hindu ve fe u ôron ga yô, er alaghga ia lu hen ijiir i i iin ior la nahan. I ungwa er ka ikyônough ki torough ki iyange i ijirôron la nahan; ka kwagh u a lu anyiman sha mi yô.

Shighe u iyange i lu nôngon u been yô, ityom la hii u eren tom vough vough. Se lu wang sha ikyom la; yange i er sail; ahumbe lu tan sha mi; ime i zegemnger u ken toho la yange i lu kpishi. Kpa ime la yange i cir sha usu u yeghen, u yange u due ken mngerem ma doon tsung la ashighe ashighe, u wanger hanma ikyagh ki esen iyou ki ki lu sha ityough ki ikyagh ki eren kwagh a mi la cii, er usu u i fe u ken tar u Grika la nahan. Shighe u tso u lu yeghen la za hemen, inja er i na un tom ér a er kwagh u vihin un iyol a ishimavihin shio nahan. Nahan utso mba ve lu a ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér Hydriote, Canaris, mbi mbi lu a ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér pitch man sulphur la, mbi mbi lu duen hen ajiir a ve a hen kpezegemnger la, mbi lun a ikyav mbi usu mbi vesen mbi i eren tom a mi u eren ikyav mbi utyaav la, mbi gba yevese sha utso mba Turkey mbara, mbi cir mbi sha usu u yeghen.

I dugh ikyônough ki torough ki ki lu sha ityough ki ityom la kera, nahan hegen i na ve ikyagh ki usugh ki vesen sha ishigh vev. I tile sha kwagh ne sha ikyav mbi Tartarean mbi mbaaôndo mbaaiev mba ve lu mbautyaav mba i yer ér harpooneers la, mba ve lu mbautyaav mba ken tso u i yer ér whale la hanma shighe yô. Ve tôô ikyav mbi vesen mbi lun a ave la ve haa ikyav mbi doon tsung mbi i yer ér blubber la ken ikyav mbi mbi lu yeghen la, shin ve cir usu u u lu shin inya la, zan zan usu u yeghen u u lu er iyongo nahan la u gba yevese, u cir, u due sha ihinda la sha u kôron ve sha angahar. Mngerem mara ma cir sha ajiir kposo kposo. Hanma kpekpe u tso la cii, kpekpe u mkurem ma ma lu yeghen la lu her, ma lu inja er ka isharen i vesen cii i nyôron sha ishigh vev nahan.
