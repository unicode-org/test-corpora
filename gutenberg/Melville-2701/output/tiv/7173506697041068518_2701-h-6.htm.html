IKYAV I 32. Kwagh u mlu u inyamtoho.

I vande yemen a vese sha ishimataver sha mnger; kpa ica a gba ga tsô se tsume ken zegemnger u a lu a kpezegemnger ga, u a lu a kpemnger ga la. Ere kwagh la va er; ere tso u Pequod u a lu a ikyav mbi utyaav la una cir ikyav mbi barnacles mbi leviathan la; sha mhii u kwaghôron la yô, doo u se ver ishima sha kwagh u a lu hange hange u se kav kwagh u mbampase mba ken takerada u Leviathan mba ve hembe lun kposo la man akaa a i er sha mi la cii, a aa dondo la.

Ka kwagh u i tese sha inja sha inja sha kwagh u inyam i i yer ér whale la ken tsombor na u vesen la, u me soo u veren sha ishigh yough hegen yô. Kpa ka tom u ican ga. I pav akaa a a lu ken mzough u chaos la ker, kwagh u a lu kpuaa ga yô, i nger heen. Kegh ato a kwagh u mbahemenev mba hemban doon cii man mba hev ve ver la.

Captain Scoresby, ken inyom i 1820 la kaa ér: “Ma vegher u kwaghfan u sangen yeke ugen ngu u a we kwagh ker kpishi er u i yer ér Cetology la nahan ga.”

Ortwer ugen u i yer iti na ér Surgeon Beale, ken inyom i 1839 la kaa ér: “Ka awashima wam ga, aluer ka sha tahav av yô, me nyôr ken kwaghtôvon u i lu timen sha mi sha kwagh u gbenda u mimi u paven inyam i i yer ér cetacea la ker avegher ahar man icombor.

“U lun a tahav mbu zan hemen a kwaghtôvon wase ken mngerem ma ma lu a mshi ga la ga.” “Ikyondo i i cir mfe wase u sha kwagh u inyam i i yer ér cetacea la, i ior vea kera nyôr ker ga yô.” “Isule i i lu a asaa ker yô.” “Akaa a tesen a a lu vough ga ne cii kpa nga eren tom u tôvon se mba se fe kwagh u akaa a i gbe a la ican.”

Nahan ôr kwagh u zege inyam i i yer ér whale la, man zege Cuvier la, man John Hunter, man Lesson, mba ve lu iwanger i kwaghfan u sangen yeke man mlu u iyol i or la. Nahan cii kpa, shin er mfe u mimi a lu kpuaa nahan kpa, ityakerada ngi kpishi; man kape sha anshighe kpuaa kpa i lu je la, sha kwagh u cetology, shin kwaghfan u sangen yeke u i yer ér whales la. Ior kpishi, mba kiriki man mba vesen, mba tsev man mba hev, mbatomov mba shin tar man mba shin zegemnger, mba ve lu a kwagh u vesen shin kpuaa, u i nger kwagh u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la yô. Run sha akaa kpuaa:—Mba ve nger Bibilo la; Aristote; Pliny; Aldrovandi; Orgen u i yilan un ér Thomas yô; Gesner; Ray; Line; Rondeleti; Willoughby; Kweegh dindee; Artedi; Sibaldi; Brisson; Marten; Lasepedi; Boneter; Desmarest; Orvesen ugen; Orgen u i yilan un ér Frederick Cuvier yô; Yohane Or u eren tom; Owen; Scoresbi; Beale; Benneti; J. Ross u ken tar ugen; or u yange nger takerada u Miriam Coffin la; Olm; man Orhemen T. Cheever. Kpa ka nyi i lu awashima u masetyô u akaa ne cii a nger la, akaa a i ter sha heen ne aa tese.

Ken ati a i nger sha kwagh u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale ne cii, ka mba ve dondo Owen la tseegh yange ve nenge a ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la ye; man kpa môm ken a ve lu or u fan tom u kôron ikyav mbi tswamev man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale la kpôô kpôô. M ngu ôron kwagh u Kapiteni Scoresby. Sha kwagh u tar u Greenland shin right-whale u u lu kposo la yô, ka un a lu or u hemban lun a tahav cii ye. Kpa Scoresby yange fa kwagh môm ga shi ôr kwagh môm sha kwagh u zege inyam i i yer ér sperm whale la kpaa ga, aluer i tôô i kar sha inyam i i yer ér Greenland whale la yô, i kera kuma u a ter i ga. Man heen yô, i kaa ér, inyon i i yer ér Greenland whale la ka or u ngohol tahav sha ikyonough ki torough ki zegemnger. Ka un a hembe lun a ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér whale la je kpaa ga. Kpa, sha ci u kwagh u yange ôr la yange lu kwagh u hiihii ica i gba, man m-ban u fan kwagh u yange er zan zan anyom akunduatargber ken ijime la, yange na ior ve fa kwagh u sperm-whale u yange lu kwagh u kpilighyol hen shighe la, man m-ban u fan kwagh zan zan nyian kpa ngu hemen ken ajiir a sainsi a ior ve zenden her la cii, saa di ajiir kpuaa tseegh; m-ban u tar ne yange kure sha hanma gbenda cii. Aluer u ôr kwagh u ior mba ve nger ityogholough mba vesen mba sha ayange a tsuaa la, u a lu inja er ka kwagh u leviathan la cii nahan yô, u zua a mkom wer, zegeikpelaior i ken tar u Greenland, i i lu a or môm u hendan kwagh a na ga la, yange i lu tor u zegemnger hen a ve. Kpa shighe kuma u a yôô kwagh u he ken masejime. Kwagh ne ka Charing Cross; ungwa nen! ior mba dedoo cii,—i dugh zege inyam i i yer ér Greenland la sha,—zege inyam i i yer ér sperm whale la ngi hemen hegen!

Ka ityakerada ihar tseegh i lu ken mlu u i er inja er ngi veren ikyav i uma i i yer ér sperm whale la sha ishigh yough ye, man hen shighe shon môm, i hemba eren kwagh u i nôngo la tsembelee yô. Ityakerada la ka i Beale man i Bennett; ve cii sha shighe ve la, ve lu mba sôron utso mba i yer ér whale-ships mba ken tar u Ingila mba i yer ér South-Sea la, shi ve lu ior mba eren kwagh vough vough shi i na ve jighjigh kpaa. Kwagh u hiihii u a bende a sperm whale u a zua a mi ken ityakerada ve la ka kwagh u kiriki; kpa er i lu nahan yô, ka i doo kpishi, shin er i hembe lun kwagh u sainsi tseegh nahan kpaa. Kpa zan zan nyian yô, kwagh u sperm whale, u a lu kwagh u sainsi shin kwagh u ityogholough la, ngu uma vough vough ken ma takerada ga. Hemba ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la cii, uma na ka u i nger kwagh sha mi ga yô.

Hegen yô, gba u a ver ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale la sha gbenda ugen u ior kpishi ve fe mbi yô, aluer ka ikyav i tesen kwagh u a lu zan hemen hegen la tseegh yô, ken hemen yô, mbatomov mba vea dondo la vea iv mbi ken afishi a mbi cii. Er or u hemban doon cii nan lu zan hemen u ngohol kwagh ne sha ikyev ga yô, m ngu nan ne iniôngon yam i injaa ga ne. M er ityendezwa a kwagh u vough ga; gadia hanma kwagh u orumace u i nenge ér ngu vough cii, gba u una lu a ibo sha ityôkyaa shon i môm. M ngu eren inja er m ngu pasen kwagh u mlu u iyol i annyamev kposo kposo la sha miniti môm nahan ga, shin—sha ijiir ne yô—m ngu pasen kwagh u hanma kwagh u i pase cii. Kwagh u m lu ôron heen yô, ka u eren kwagh u i yer ér systematization of cetology la tseegh. Ka mo m lu or u maan kwagh ye, ka mo m lu or u maan kwagh ga.

Kpa ka tom u taver kpishi; or u eren tom u paven uwashika ken Post-Office môm kpa nan ngu kwagh môm a na ga. U eren tom a mi shin mnger u zegemnger sha u dondon ve ijime; u lun a ave hen atô u akaa a i gbe u a ôr a ga la, man ikyav mbi tswamev man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi tar ne; kwagh ne ka kwagh u cieryol. Ka nyi m lu ve me er kwagh u me kôr ityough ki leviathan ne? Alaghga akaa a vihin a i lu ôron Yobu la aa cier mo iyol kpishi. Un (leviathan) una ya ikyur a we kpa? Nenge ishimaverenkeghen i sha a na la ka gbilinkwagh! Kpa m zende ken ajiir a koson ityakerada shi m zende ken zegemnger kpaa; M vande eren kwagh a mbaatsator mba i yer ér whales la sha ave a i nengen a mi ne; M ngu a gbashima; man me nôngo. Akaa agen nga a i gbe u se er ve se kure yô.

Hiihii: Mlu u a lu a vangertiôr ga, u a lu a mzough ga u kwaghfan u sangen yeke u i yer ér Cetology ne la ngu ken vestibule u i tese ikyav sha mi la, ér sha ajiir agen yô, kwagh ngu her u ior ve lu a fa aluer ka ishu i i yer ér whale la ga yô. Linnæus pase ken takerada na u i yer ér System of Nature, ken inyom i 1776 la ér, “M pav ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whales la sha ishu ne.” Kpa sha mfe wam yô, m fa je mer zan zan inyom i 1850 la, i zua a ishu i i yer ér shark la man shad, man alewives man herring, i i lu hendan a tindi u Linnæus yange wa wang la, i lu paven akaa a a lu hen zegemnger môm la vea Leviathan her.

Ityôkyaa i Linnæus yange una hen ér yange una zenda ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la ken mnger kera yô, a kaa ér: “Sha ci u ishima ve i i lu a ashe ahar, i i lu a ahumbe ker la, man mshiam ma ve lu a mi la, man ato a ve a a lu a ikyav ker la, man penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem la,” man ken masejime yô, “ex merito lege naturæ jure.” M na akaa ne cii hen azende am Simeon Macey man Charley Coffin, mba ken gar u Nantucket, mba ve lu mbatomov av mba ken zende ugen la, nahan ve cii ve lumun ér atôakyaa a i ter la kuma ga cii. Charley yange ôr kwagh sha inja ga ér ve mba humbug.

I fa er, m de hanma anyiman cii yô, m tôô ityôkyaa i dedoo i tse i i kaa ér zegeikpelaior la ka ishu la, m yila icighan Yona mer a suem. Kwagh u vesen ne kure, kwagh u a dondo yô, ka sha nyi gbenda u ken atô u ishu i i yer ér whale la i lu kposo a ishu igen? Sha heen yô, Linnæus na u akaa shon. Kpa sha mkur u kwaghôron yô, ka akaa ne: mshiam man awambe a doon tsung; kpa ishu igen cii yô, ngi a mshiam ga shi ngi a awambe a doon ga.

Kwagh u dondon yô: se pase kwagh u zege inyam i i yer ér whale la nena, sha akaa a ken won a a lu ken igbar la, sha er se fa kwagh u a lu ken igbar sha u veren un iti sha ci u shighe u a lu van la cii? Nahan yô, aluer se ôr kwagh kpuaa yô, ishu i i yer ér whale la ka ishu i i eren zwa, i lu a ityough ki ki lu gbilin yô . Ka hen ijiir la u lu a na ye. Nahan cii kpa, mpase shon ka kwagh u a dugh ken m-er u gbidyen kwar sha akaa kpishi la. Walrus ka a ôr kwagh er whale nahan, kpa walrus ka ishu ga, sha ci u ka a lu shin mnger. Kpa ishember i masetyô i mpase u kwaghôron la hemba lun a inja her, er i zough a i hiihii la nahan. Alaghga hanma or nana nenge ér ishu i ior mba ken tar ugen ve fe i la cii ngi a ikyav mbi tswamev ga, kpa ngi a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi tilen sha, shin mbi mbi lu sha man shin la. Kpa hen atô u ishu i i yer ér spouting fish la yô, ikyav mbi tswamev mbin mbi lu kwagh môm nahan kpa, mbi lu a mshi ga.

Sha mpase u i pase kwagh u a lu ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la sha heen ne yô, mayange je m dugh ma kwagh u shin zegemnger u ior mba ken gar u Nantucket mba ve fe kwagh tsembelee la ve fe ér ka ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la ken mzough u anmgbianev mba leviathanic la kera ga; shi sha vegher ugen di yô, u zua a ishu i i nengen ér ka i ken tar ugen zan zan nyian la ga.* Nahan yô, gba u a wa ishu i kiriki i i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev shi i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi eren tom sha mi la cii ken mzough u i yer ér ground-plan of Cetology ne. Nahan hegen yô, mzough u vesen u ior mba ve lu hen ijiir i i yer ér whale la cii va.

( Sperm Whale ).—Iyôngo ne, hen atô u Mbangerenkwagh mba tsuaa mba i yilan ve sha inja ga ér iyôngo i i yer ér Trumpa la, man iyôngo i i yer ér Physeter la, man iyôngo i i yer ér Anvil Headed la, ka iyôngo i i yer ér Cachalot i ior i France ve yer hegen ne, man iyôngo i i yer ér Pottsfich i ior i Macroce i i yer ér Longphalus la, man iyôngo i i yer ér Pottsfich la. Anyom uderi imôngo ken ijime, shighe u i lu nôngon u fan kwagh u ishu i i yer ér Sperm whale la sha mlu na u vough la ga, shi shighe u i zua a mkurem na sha ishu i i cir ken mnger la tseegh la; sha ayange la, a lu inja er ior kpishi hen ér spermaceti due ken inyam i i lu kwagh môm a i i yilan ken tar u Ingila hen shighe la ér Greenland shin Right Whale la nahan. Shi lu mhen u ior lu a mi ér, spermaceti ne môm ka kwagh u doon tsung u i yer ér Greenland Whale, u ishember i hiihii i ishember ne i tese la. Shi sha ashighe la kpa, spermaceti lu kpuaa kpishi, i lu eren tom a mi sha u tan iwanger ga, kpa lu di u eren mkurem man twer tseegh. Yange lu u a zua a mi hen mbateen mcimbim ma bom tseegh er ayange ne u yamen ounce u rhubarb nahan. Shighe u, er m hen nahan, shighe kar yô, i fa mlu u mimi u spermaceti la, mbateen un lu a iti na i hiihii la her; akperan nga ga, sha u seer mluainja u i sha mhen u a lu a inja kpishi sha kwagh u mlu u i ban la. Man ka nahan ve, alaghga tsô ken masejime yô, i va hingir u yilan zegeikpelaior i i yer ér whale la, i yange i dugh spermaceti ne ker jighilii ye.

( Right Whale ).—Sha gbenda ugen yô, ka ikyôhô ne i hembe nan ior icivir ken ikyôhô i i yer ér leviathan la ye, sha ci u ka ikyôhô i ior ve hii u keren hanma shighe yô. TAKERADA I. ( Folio ), IKYAV I TESE I TER. ( Fin-Back ).—Sha ityough kin yô, m ôr kwagh u inyamkyume igen i i yer i sha ati kposo kposo ér Fin-Back man Tall-Spout man Long-John yô, i nenge a mi ken hanma zegemnger cii, shi ka i lu zegemnger u ior mba ve zenden sha zegemnger u Atlantic ve hembe ôron kwagh u jet na u icaa la, ken ikyav mbi eren tom a mi ken gar u New York la. Sha mze u a zough a mi la, man ken baleen na yô, Fin-back la ngu er right whale nahan, kpa ngu a ikyav mbi eren tom ga, shi ngu a ikyav mbi doon tsung, mbi mbi lu ikyua ikyua a ikyav mbi olev yô. Ikyev na i vesen la ngi a mlu u lun er ka ikyav nahan, i i er sha ikyav mbi vesen mbi mbi lu a ikyav mbi eren tom imôngo, mbi mbi lu a ikyav mbi eren tom la. Kwagh u vesen u a tese un kposo yô, ka ikyôhô i i yer ér fin, i i dugh iti na sha mi la, ashighe kpishi ka i lu kwagh u ior ve nengen a mi yô. Ikyôhô ne ngi a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kuman er angahar atar shin anyiin nahan, mbi vesen sha ityough ki ikyav mbi tswamev mbi ken ijime mbi ken ijime, mbi lu er ka angôghime nahan, shi mbi lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi lun a ikyav mbi tswamev kpishi. Aluer vegher ugen u inyam ne ngu kpuaa tseegh kpa, ashighe agen a nenge a ikyav i i lu paleghaa ne, i due sha ityough ki inyam la wang. Shighe u zegemnger a lu ving kpuaa, shi a lu a ikyav i mngerem ma lun er ka gnomon nahan ne, man ikyav i i lu er ka gnomon nahan ne i tile sha, i lu tan mngerem sha ityough ki zegemnger ki ki lu a ikyav sha mi la yô, alaghga a hen ér ikyav i mngerem i i kase un la ngi er ka dial nahan, sha gbenda u i eren kwagh a mi la, shi i lu a ikyav i ahwa i i yer ér wavy hour-lines la. Sha shighe u Ahashi-dial la yô, ashighe kpishi mule la ka u hide ijime. Fin-Back ka kwagh u mzough ga. Ka inja er ka or u kôron ishu i i yer ér whale la ihyom nahan, er nomso mbagenev kpa ve ker ior ihyom nahan. Kunya kôron kpishi; hanma shighe yô, m zaan tswen tswen; u mough sha mnger u a lu ica shi a lu a mngerem kpishi la sha ivande; jet na u taven sha u a lu jighilii shi a lu môm tseegh la, lu moughon sha er ka sanker u taver u misanthropic nahan sha ityough ki ikyônough ki torough ki ki lu a ikyon ga yô; i na un iyua i tahav mbu kpilighyol man mzehemen u eren anumbe a ôôn iyol, u una hendan a hanma kwagh u or nan lu keren hegen cii yô; leviathan ne ngu inja er ka Kain u ken tsombor na u i zenda un kera shi i hemba un ga la nahan, ngu a ikyav na i eren kwagh sha mi la sha ityou. Hii shighe u Fin-Back lu a baleen ken zwa na la je, ashighe agen ka i wa un vea ikyôhô i i yer ér right whale la, ken ikyav mbi i yer ér Whalebone whales , inja na yô, ka ikyôhô i i yer ér baleen la. Ken inyam i i yer ér Whalebone whale ne cii, a lu inja er ngi a anza kposo kposo nahan, kpa ior kpishi fa i ga. Iyôngo i i yer ér whale la ngi a ityough ki gbar gbar; mbaatsator mba lun a ityough ki pike; mbaatsator mba i yer ér whale la; ishu i i yer ér under-jawed whale la man ishu i i yer ér rostrated whale la, ka ati a mbakôron ishu ve yer anzaakaa kpuaa je la.

Sha kwagh u iti i i yer ér “Whalebone whales” ne yô, ka hange hange u ôron ér, shin er imba iti ne ia wase u ôron kwagh u inja igen i i yer ér whales la nahan kpa, ka gbilinkwagh u nôngon ér i pav Leviathan ker wang, sha mlu u i yer ér baleen, shin teeth, shin hump, shin fin na la; shin er avegher shin akaa a i ver ikyav sha mi la a lu wang je ér i hemba sôron a sha u nan ityôkyaa i eren kwagh u i yer ér Cetology la hanma shighe cii, a hanma kwagh ugen u a tese mkposo u iyol i or cii, u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la, ken anza a na, a tese la nahan kpaa. Nahan a er nena? Iyôngo i baleen man hump man back-fin man atôakaa; akaa ne ka akaa a akaa a a lu kposo kposo la a samber sha ajiir kposo kposo hen atô u hanma inja inyam i i yer ér whale la cii, a u nengen sha kwagh u alaghga una lu mlu u akaa ne ken akaa agen a a hembe lun hange hange la shio. Nahan yô, ishu i i yer ér sperm whale la man ishu i i yer ér humpbacked la cii, hanma ve cii nan ngu a ikyav; kpa hen ijiir la yô, mlu u kwagh môm la bee. Tsô, ikyôhô i i yer ér humpbacked whale ne man ikyôhô i i yer ér Greenland la cii, hanma ikyôhô ne cii ngi a baleen; kpa hen ijiir la kpa mlu u kwagh môm la bee. Shi kape avegher agen a se ter sha heen ne kpa a lu je la. Ken anzaakaa kposo kposo a inyam i i yer ér whale la, ka i er imba mzough u a lu vough ga la; shin, aluer mô ve nan ngu paleghaa yô, imba mlu u lun paleghaa a ior sha inja ga la; er ka u vendan hanma gbenda u i yer ér general methodization u i er sha imba ityôkyaa ne cii nahan. Sha iwen ne yô hanma or u nan fe kwagh u ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér whale la nan pav ker.

Kpa alaghga a hen ér, ken avegher a ken atô a inyam i i yer ér whale la, ken mlu u iyol na—hen ijiir la, a lu nahan kpa, se zua a ian i eren kwagh u vough. Ei; u tesen ikyav yô, ka nyi kwagh i lu ken iyol i inyam i i yer ér whale i ken tar u Greenland la i hembe kpilighyol a inyam i i yer ér baleen la? Kpa se nenge ser sha baleen na yô, a lu ican u paven zegeikpelaior i i yer ér Greenland whale la sha inja. Shi aluer u sen ken atô u ikyôhô i leviathan kposo kposo la yô, er nan ve hen ijiir la u zua a mbamgem mba ve lu vegher u sha utaankarunyiin la er mba ve lu ken won mba i vande ôron ve la nahan ga? Nahan ka nyi i shi her? kwagh môm ga saa di u kôron ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whales la sha iyol, ken mlu mbi mbi lu a mi la cii, shi veren mbi sha ishimataver sha gbenda la. Man ka gbenda u i yer ér Bibliographical u i lumun a mi heen ne je ne; man ka un tseegh una lu a tahav mbu eren kwagh ye, gadia ka un tseegh a lu kwagh u eren ye. U zan hemen.

Iyôngo ne ngi a mzehemen u kuman er angahar pue kar ataratar nahan, kpa ikyônough ki torough ki ki lu a mi la ki kuma angahar ataan, shin er kigen ki hembe pue nahan kpaa, shi ki kuma angahar pue kar ataan je kpaa. Aluer se ôr kwagh sha mimi yô, ikyônough kin ka di ikyônough ki torough ki ki lu a mshi ga, ki ki vese ken zwa la sha gbenda u ki lu a mshi kpuaa sha ityough ki ki lu sha mi la. Kpa ka sha vegher u vihin tu la tseegh i zua a mi ye, u a lu a inja i bo yô, nahan a na or u nan lu a mi la kwagh u a zough sha mlu u or u nan lu a uwegh ku yanegh u nan lu a kwaghfan ga yô. Ka awashima u nyi jighilii ikyônough ki torough shin ikyagh ki tswamegh kin ki ne mlumun sha mi la, a taver u fan. Ka inja er ka i er tom a mi er ka ikyagh ki ishu i i yer ér sword-fish man bill-fish la nahan ga; shin er mbatsoov mbagenev ve kaa a mo ér Narwhale ka a er tom a mi sha u geman ityough ki zegemnger sha u keren kwaghyan nahan kpaa. Charley Coffin kaa ér yange i er tom a mi u eren kwagh u eren ice; gadia Narwhale yange una mough sha ityough ki Zegemnger u Polar la, una nenge ér i cir un sha ice yô, a kende ikyônough nagh sha, nahan a gba duen ker. Kpa u ngu a tese wer kwagh môm ken akaa a u hen ne cii ka u mimi ga. Mhen wam iyol yam yô, ka er, aluer mimi je Narwhale ngu eren tom a ikyônough ki torough ki ki lu vegher môm ne nahan kpaa—shin aluer kwagh la una lu nan nan kpa—sha mimi yô, a doo un kpishi sha ci u folder u ôron ityakerada i kiriki. Iyôngo i i yer ér Narwhale la, m ungwa i yila i ér Tusked whale man Horned whale man Unicorn. Sha mimi yô, ka ikyav i tesen ér kwagh u i yer ér Unicornism la ngu a inja kpishi, u i zough a mi ken hanma tartor u akaa a lun uma cii yô. M kohol hen mbafantakerada mba tsev mbagenev mba ve lu ken mzough u mbahemenev mba aduaav la mer, sha ayange a tsuaa la, i nengen ér ikyônough ki torough ki ikyôhô i i yer ér sea-unicorn ne ka ki vesen ki yangen angev mbu ken iyol, nahan sha nahan yô, u wan agoyol sha mi la yange ki va a inyaregh kpishi. Shi yange i sôr un hingir mngerem ma ma lu a mshi ga sha ci u kasev mba ve lu kpen iyol la, vough er ka i er akor a inyam i i yer ér male deer la hingir hartshorn nahan. Sha hiihii la, i nenge ér ka kwagh u ior kpishi ve soo u fan yô. Black Letter kaa a mo ér, shighe u Sir Martin Frobisher hide ken zende la, shighe u Torkwase Bess yange kende uwegh nagh ku ku lu a akaa a injaa sha mi la sha windou u iyou i tor i Greenwich la sha ishimataver, er tso na u lun a ishimataver la lu yemen sha mnger u Thames la; Black Letter kaa ér: “Shighe u Sir Martin hide ken zende la yô, a gure inya, a na un ityough ki Narwhale ki ki lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi doon tsung, mbi yange mbi lu ken iyou i tor i ken gar u Windsor la, shighe kar kpishi yô.” Orngeren takerada ugen ken tar u Ireland kaa ér, Earl u Leicester, u yange gure inya la kpa yange na un ityough kigen, ki ki lu inyam i i yer ér unicorn la.

Narwhale ngu a ikyav mbi doon tsung, mbi lun er ka ikyôhô nahan, mbi lun a ikyav mbi doon tsung mbi lun er ka mngerem nahan, mbi lun a ikyav mbi doon tsung mbi lun a ikyav mbi doon tsung. Mkurem na hemba doon cii, ma wanger shi ma doo kpaa; kpa kwagh ngu kpuaa, shi ka i ker un kpishi ga. I hemba zuan a na ken zegemnger u circumpolar la.

TAKERADA U sha II. ( Oktavo ), Ityough ki sha IV. ( Killer ).—Ior mba ken gar u Nantucket fa kwagh u inyam i i yer ér whale ne jighilii ga, shi or u nan senge ér nan ngu or u timen sha akaa a i gbe a la kpa nan fa kwagh môm ga. Sha kwagh u m nenge a na ken ica yô, doo u me kaa mer yange lu er ka grampus nahan. Ka a vihi iyol kpishi—ka inja ishu i i yer ér Feegee la. Ashighe agen ka a kôr zege inyam i i yer ér Folio la sha zwa, a cir her er ka ikyôhô nahan, zan zan inyam i taver iyol la i hingir u lun a ishimanyian zan zan i kar kpen. Mayange i ker Orwuanor la ga. Mayange m ungwa kwagh u mkurem ma a lu a mi la ga. Alaghga a tôô iti i i yer ikyôhô i i yer ér whale ne la i lu kposo a iti ne, sha ci u i fa i tsembelee ga. Gadia se cii se mba mbawuan ior, sha tar man sha zegemnger kpaa; Bonaparte man Sharks kpa lu ker.

TAKERADA U sha II. ( Octavo ), IKYAV I V. ( Thrasher ).—Or ne fa un sha ityough nagh, ki ka a er tom a mi u eren ferule sha u gbidyen mbaihyomov nav yô. A nyôr sha ityough ki tso u i yer ér Folio whale la, nahan er a lu ôôn mngerem la, a er tom a gbenda na sha u gbidyen un; er mbatesen mbagenev mba ken makeranta ka ve zua ken tar sha imba gbenda ne nahan. Shi kwagh u Thrasher la kpa i fa kwagh u Killer ga. Ve cii ka mbaaferev, ken zegemnger u a lu a tindi ga la je kpaa.

Ka nahan ve TAKERADA u sha II la a kure ye. ( Octavo ), shi hii takerada u sha utar la. ( Mbauhar ).

DUODECIMOS.—Akaa ne wa ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kiriki kpa ker. I. Iyôngo i Huzza. II. Iyôngo i ken tar u Algeria. III. Iyôngo i i yer ér Porpoise la.

Hen mba ve lu a zua a ian i henen kwagh ne sha inja ga yô, alaghga a lu ve er ka kwagh u kpilighyol nahan, er i lu u a kohol ishu i i hembe angahar anyiin shin ataan ga la hen atô u WHALES—ka ishember i, sha mnenge u ior kpishi ve fe yô, hanma shighe yô, i tese mhen u vesen yô. Kpa akaauma a i ver sha heen ér Duodecimoes la ka ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la, sha gbenda u m pase kwagh u i yer ér whale la— inja na yô, ka ishu i i eren zwa, i lu a ityough ki ki lu gbilin yô.

TAKERADA U sha III. ( Duodecimo ), IKYAV I TESE 1. ( Ikyôhô i Huzza ).—Ka ikyôhô i ior kpishi ve fe i sha tar cii je ne. Iti la ka kwagh u mo iyol yam m ne yô; gadia anzaakaa a porpoise nga hemba môm, nahan gba u a er kwagh sha u paven a. M yila un nahan, sha ci u hanma shighe yô, ka a ôô iyol ken uifi mba doon tsung, mba ve lu sha zegemnger u vesen la ve lu yevese sha er ka ikyav mbi tswamev mbi ken ikpelaior i sha iyange i Julai 4 la nahan. Or u nahan tso la ka nan wuese mluashe ve la sha iember kpishi. Ve iv a ujijingi mba dedoo, nahan hanma shighe yô, ka ve due hen ahumbe a a lu yeghen la ve yem hen kpe ahumbe. Ka mbayevnomso mba ve lu uma sha ishigh ki ahumbe hanma shighe la. I nenge ér ka ikyav i tesen ér kwagh ngu zan hemen. Aluer we iyol you kpa u ngu a tahav mbu wan ishima a iemberyolough itar sha u nengen a ishu i lun uma ne yô, sha a wase u; jijingi u eren anumbe a Aôndo ngu ken a ven ga. Huzza Porpoise u i ne un kwaghyan tsembelee shi a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev yô, una na u mkurem ma dedoo galonu môm. Kpa mngerem ma dedoo ma ma lu a inja kpishi ma i dugh ken zwa na la ma a inja kpishi. Ior mba eren akaa a injaa man mba eren agogo ka ve sôn un. Mbatsoov yange ve wa un sha hones vev. Inyam i porpoise ka i doo u yan, u fa. Alaghga mayange je u hen wer porpoise ka a ôr kwagh ga. Sha kpôô yô, zwa na ngu cuku je yô, ka i nenge a mi fese ga. Kpa shighe ugen u u zough a ian yô, nenge a na; man u mase nengen a zege inyam i i yer ér Sperm whale la iyol na ken ikyav i kiriki.

TAKERADA U sha III. ( Duodecimo ), IKYAV I SHA II. ( Iyôngo i ken tar u Algeria ).—Iyôngo i i yer ér Porpoise la. Savage kpishi. M hen mer ka ken zegemnger u Pacific tseegh i zua a na ye. A hemba Huzza Porpoise la sha gbenda ugen, kpa akaa kpishi a i er sha kwagh u shoja i môm la. Vihi un ishima, nahan una gba eren kwagh a shark. M cir sha ci na acin imôngo, kpa mayange m nenge a na i kôr un ga.

TAKERADA U sha III. ( Duodecimo ), Ityough ki sha utar. ( Iyôngo i i yer ér Porpoise la ).—Iyôngo i i hembe lun a zwa u vesen yô; man ka ken zegemnger u Pacific tseegh i zua a mi ye, er i fe un nahan. Iti i ken zwa Buter i môm tseegh, i i yer un zan zan nyian yô, ka iti i mbakôron ishu la—Right-Whale Porpoise, sha mlu u i hembe zuan a na hen haregh u Folio la yô. Sha mlu u iyol na yô, a kaha a Huzza Porpoise sha ma gbenda, sha ci u ngu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi lun a mshi ga shi lun a mshi ga; sha mimi yô, ngu a iyol i dedoo shi i lu er ka or u lun a ieren i dedoo nahan. Ngu a ikyav mbi tswamev sha ityou ga (iyôngo i i yer ér porpoises la kpishi ngi a ikyav mbi tswamev), shi ngu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi doon tsung, shi ashe a ior i India a a lu a mlu u hazel la. Kpa zwa na u mealy la vihi akaa cii. Shin er ijime na cii zan zan ar sha akôv a na a ken kpe la i lu i sable u a lu ken atô u tso nahan kpa, ikyav igen ngi i i yer ér boundary line, i i lu kposo er ikyav i i ver ken ikyônough ki tsoo nahan, i i yer ér “bright waist” la yô, ikyav la cir un hii sha ityough ki tso zan zan ar sha ityough ki tso, i lu a ikyav mbi lun kposo kposo mbihar, mbi lun nyian sha man mbi pupuu shin inya. Ikyav i pupuu la ngi a vegher u ityough nagh, man zwa na cii, nahan kwagh ne na yô, a lu inja er a sember yevese sha kwagh u iferkwagh i i ze hen ijiir i teen kwaghyan nahan. A hemba lun a inja shi mealy! Mkurem nam mara ma zua sha ma iyôngo i i yer ér porpoise la kpishi.

Kpa ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér whales mbi mbi lu a vangertiôr ga, mbi yevese, mbi mbi lu a mshi ga la mbi, er m lu or u kôron ikyav mbi utyaav mbi ken tar u Amerika yô, m fa mbi sha iti, kpa ka mo iyol yam ga. Me nger ve sha ati a ve a i yer ér fore-castle la; gadia alaghga imba takerada ne ua lu mbatôvonkwaghfan mba ken hemen mba vea kure kwagh u m lu a mi heen kpa m hii la a inja yô. Aluer môm ken mbaatsator mba ve dondo heen ne, i kôr un shi i wa un ikyav ken hemen yô, a gba u a nyôr a na ken Mzough ne fese je, sha mzehemen na u Folio, Octavo, shin Duodecimo la:—Inyam i i yer ér Bottle-Nose Whale la; u i yer ér Junk Whale la; ikyôhô i i yer ér Whale la; ikyôhô i i yer ér Cape Whale la; ka Whale u Hemen la; ikyôhô i i yer ér Cannon la; ikyôhô i i yer ér Scragg la; ikyôhô i i yer ér Copper la; ikyôhô i i yer ér Elefant la; ikyôhô i i yer ér Iceberg la; ikyôhô i i yer ér Quog la; iyôngo i i yer ér Blue Whale la; etc. Hii hen mbahemenev mba ken tar u Iceland man Dutch man tar u Ingila mba tsuaa mbara yô, alaghga a ôr akaa agen a i nger sha kwagh u ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér whales mbi i fe mbi ga yô, mbi i ver mbi doo doo sha hanma inja iti i i lu sha inja ga cii yô. Kpa m de ve kera er ve kera lu a inja ga cii nahan; man una wase u u henen ér ka ityogholough mbi ungwan tseegh, mbi mbi iv a kwaghaôndo u Leviathan, kpa mbi tesen kwagh môm ga yô.

Ken masejime yô: Yange i ôr sha mhii u kwaghôron ne ér, mlu ne una lu heen ga, shi una lu vough kwa môm tseegh. U nenge wang wer m kura kwaghôron wam. Kpa hegen yô, m de Mzough wam u Cetological System la tile nahan m bee ga, vough er yange i de zege Iyou i Mkohol i Cologne la nahan, man crane la tile sha ityough ki iyou i taven sha i i bee ga la her.
