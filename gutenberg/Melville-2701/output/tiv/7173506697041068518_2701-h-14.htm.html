Orgen ken mbautyaav mba ve lu zan mkohol la ka nan za hemen hegen a ikyagh ki tswamegh ki ki lu a ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér boarding-sword la, nahan er a lu nengen a ian na yô, a cir ikyagh ki tswamegh ki ki lu shin inya ki ikyagh ki tswamegh ki ki lu tenger la sha kwaghfan. IKYAV I 68. Kwagh u Blanket.

M ver ishima sha itinekwagh i i lu a vihi ishima ga la kpuaa ga, ka ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale la je la. M ngu a anyiman sha kwagh ne vea ior mba fan kwagh u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la mba ve lu shin mnger la, kua mbafankwagh mba ve hen kwagh u akaa a i gbe a la hen kpezegemnger. Mhen wam u hiihii la gema ga; kpa ka mhen tseegh.

Mpin u a lu yô, ka nyi man ka han i lu ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale laa? U fa kwagh u a lu blubber na yô. Blubber la ka kwagh u a lu er ka inyam i i lu a mshi ga, i i lu ikyua ikyua la nahan, kpa i hembe taver, i hembe lun a mshi shi i lu a mshi ga, shi i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kuman er inch anigheni shin pue zan zan pue kar uhar man pue kar utaan nahan.

Hegen yô, shin er sha hiihii la a lu inja er ka kwagh u gbilinigh u ôron kwagh u hanma kwagh u i gbe un cii ér ngu a mlu u vough man u lun a mshi ga nahan kpa, sha mimi yô, akaa ne ka akaa a hendan a imba mhen la ga; gadia u ngu a fatyo u kenden a ma kwagh ugen u a cir iyol i inyam i i yer ér whale la sha ga, saa di blubber shon môm tseegh; man ikyav i ken won i i cir hanma inyam cii, aluer i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev yô, ka nyi ia lu ikyav mbi tswamev tseegh? Sha mimi yô, alaghga u cir kwagh u a lu a mshi ga, u a lu wanger wanger la sha uwegh ou, u a lu er ka ikyav mbi isinglass mbi mbi hembe lun a mshi ga la nahan, kpa ka kwagh u a lu a mshi ga shi a lu a mshi ga er satin nahan tseegh; inja na yô, cii man i mase ôôn un yô, shighe u a cir shi a cir tseegh ga, kpa a hingir u taver shi a lu a mshi ga yô. M ngu a ambaakaa a i yer ér dried bits la kpishi, a m eren tom a mi u eren ikyav ken ityakerada yam i i yer ér whale-books la yô. Ka kwagh u a lu ken igbar, er m vande ôron nahan; man er i lu veren un sha peeji u i gber la yô, ashighe agen m ember iyol yam sha u henen mer yange lu a tahav mbu vesen. A lu nyi je ia er kpa, ka i doo u ôron kwagh u ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la sha ikyav mbi ve nengen a mi la, er alaghga u kaa nahan. Kpa kwagh u m lu eren heen yô, ka kwagh ne. Kwagh u a lu er ka isinglass nahan, u a lu a ikighir ga, u m lumun mer, a we iyol i inyam i i yer ér whale la cii ker la, ka kwagh u i gbe u a nenge un ér ka ikyav mbi inyam la ga, ka ikyav mbi i gbe mbi la, se kaa ser ka ikyav mbi i gbe mbi la ga; gadia yange lu kwagh u doon ga u kaan ér, ikyav mbi tswamev mbi vesen mbi i yer ér tremend whale la mbi hemba lun a ikyav mbi tswamev shi mbi hemba lun a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi wan u i sember maren un la. Kpa kwagh ne kera ngu ga.

U tôôn wer blubber la ka ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale la; nahan, shighe u ikyav mbi tswamev mbin, er mbi i yer ér Sperm Whale mbi vesen kpishi nahan, mbi a na ikyav mbi tswamev mbi vesen mbi kuman barel deri môm la; man, zum u i nenge ér, sha iyenge i mkurem mara, shin sha mlu u ma i tese la yô, ka avegher atar ken atô u anyiin tseegh, ka kwagh u a lu ken ikyondo la cii ga; nahan alaghga a lu a mhen ugen sha kwagh u mze u kwagh u a lu uma la, u vegher u a lu di ikyav mbi tswamev tseegh kpa ka a due a mngerem ma lun er ka zegemnger nahan la. Aluer u ôr barel pue sha ton môm yô, u lu a ton pue sha ci u ikyav mbi tomov mbi i yer ér whale la mbi kuman er vegher utar nahan tseegh.

Ken uma yô, mlu u i nengen a mi u i yer ér Sperm Whale la ka kwagh u kiriki ken akaa a kpilighyol kpishi a a tese la ga. Ka i cir sha mi cii sha gbenda u obliquely shi i hide i cir sha mi sha ikyav i jighilii i i fe iyenge i i ga yô, i lu kwagh môm a i i nger sha ikyav i Italia i i hembe doon cii la. Kpa ka inja er i nger akav ne sha kwagh u i yer ér isinglass u se ter sha heen ne ga nahan, kpa ka inja er i nenge a mi sha mi nahan, inja er i nger a sha iyol i or la iyol i nan nahan. Shi ka kwagh ne tseegh ga. Ashighe agen yô, sha ishigh ki or u nengen fese, u nan nengen kwagh fese yô, akav a a lu sha mi la, er ken kwagh u i gber sha mi kpôô kpôô nahan, kpa a na ian i eren akaa agen kpishi. Akaa ne ka akaa a i yer ér hieroglyphic la; inja na yô, aluer u yila akaa a myer a i nger sha kpekpe u pyramid la wer hieroglyphics yô, ka ishember i i doo u a yar tom a mi ken mzough u i lu ôron hegen ne je la. Er m umbur akaa a i nger sha ikyav i i yer ér hieroglyphic la sha kpezegemnger u i yer ér Sperm Whale la yô, kwagh ne kpiligh mo iyol kpishi, m nenge a ikyav i tesen ikyav i ior mba ken tar u India mba tsuaa mbara, i i nger sha ikyav i i yer ér hieroglyphic palisades, i i lu hen kpezegemnger u Upper Mississippi la. Er iwen i myer la nahan, zegeikpelaior i i yer ér mystic-marked whale la kpa ngi her, or a kera fa u ôron kwagh u i ga. Kwagh u i er sha kwagh u awen a ken tar u India ne umbur mo kwagh ugen kpaa. Dugh akaa agen a ken won u Sperm Whale la cii kera yô, ka a tese ikyav i ken ijime la kpishi ga, hemban je yô, ikyav na i ken ijime la, i i vihi ken vegher u vesen u mluashe u i lu a mi hanma shighe la, sha ci u ikyav mbi doon tsung mbi i nger sha mi la, mbi mbi lu sha inja ga, mbi i er sha inja ga la. M kaa mer, awen a ken tar u New England a a lu hen kpezegemnger la, a Agassiz a hen ér nga a ikyav i m-er u eren kwagh sha tswam a icebergs mba vesen mba ve lu sha mnger la—m kaa mer, gba u awen shon aa zua sha kwagh u i yer ér Sperm Whale la kpuaa ga. Shi ka inja er mo yô, alaghga tsô ka sha ci u ihyom i kôron ikyôhô igen i i yer ér whale la i eren imba ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale la ye; gadia m hemba nengen a ve ken ibua i vesen i i lu a mshi ga la.

Kwaghôron môm shin uhar mbagenev sha kwagh u ikyav mbi tswamev shin ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér whale la. I vande ôron ér, ka i dugh un sha avegher a doon tsung, i yilan ér blanket-pieces. Er ikyav mbi shin zegemnger kpishi nahan, ikyav ne kpa ka i saan iyol kpishi shi i lu a inja kpaa. Gadia mimi je, i cir ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la ken blubber na er ka ikyondo i cir shin ikyondo i cir sha mi kpôô kpôô nahan; shin, kwagh u hemban doon cii yô, poncho u tar u India yange cir sha ityou na, shi cir ityough nagh. Ka sha ci u mlu u iyol na i i lu a mshi ga ne ve, ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la i lu a tahav mbu lun ken mkpeyol sha hanma shighe cii, man sha hanma zegemnger cii, man sha hanma shighe cii, man sha hanma shighe cii ye. Ka nyi ia er zegemnger u ken Imbusutarimese u a lu tenger shi a lu a ice la, aluer i na un ikyav mbi tswamev mbi doon tsung mbi i yer ér surtout la ga? Sha mimi yô, i zua a ishu igen i i lu a mngerem kpishi ken mngerem ma Hyperborean la; kpa akaa ne yô, i nenge a mi, ka ishu you i i lu a awambe a ndôhôr, i lu a mshiam ga, i iyav mbu i lu er ka friiji nahan la; akaauma a a lu ôôn iyol shin inya hen kpe u iceberg, er or u zenden sha shighe u uraahumbe ka nan ôôn iyol sha ishigh ki usu u hen iyou i mbavannya nahan; kpa, er or nahan, ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la ngi a mshiam man awambe a tseen. I cir awambe a na, nahan a kpe. Ka kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi hen shighe la—saa di shighe u i pase un kwagh kera yô—er zege inyamkyume ne, i mlu u iyol i or a lu un kwagh u hange hange er ka kwagh u hange hange hen or nahan la; ka kwagh u doon kpishi er yange i zua a na hen ya, i mir un sha zwa sha ci u uma na ken mngerem ma Arctic mara yô! hen ape mbatsoov ka vea gba shin zegemnger yô, ashighe agen ka i zua a ve, iwer ngi kar imôngo yô, ve lu cir sha ityough ki ishule i ice, er ka i zua a ikyôhô i i cir sha amber nahan. Kpa kwagh u a hembe kpilighyol yô, ka u fan, er i tese sha akaa a i kar sha mi la nahan, ér awambe a inyam i i yer ér Polar whale la ka a hemba doon a a inyam i i yer ér Borneo negro la shighe u uraahumbe.

Ka inja er, heen ne se nenge a mlu u injaa u a lu a tahav mbu or môm môm nan lu a mi ga la, man mlu u injaa u a lu u kpekpe u a lu a ikyav mbi tswamev la, man mlu u injaa u a lu ken atô u iyou la. Oh, or! admire shi dondo ikyav i whale la! We kpa, lu a mngerem ken atô u ice. We kpa, u ngu ken tar ne a u lun ken tar ne shio kpa? Ka i lu inja er ka equator nahan; m lu a awambe a ou hen Pole. Kpa nenge er i lu kwagh u ican ga u tesen akaa a injaa ne shi a lu kwagh u lun a ishimaverenkeghen ga sha wono! Sha kwagh u erections yô, ka kpuaa tseegh i lu a dome er St. Peter nahan ye! akaauma a i gbe a la, a lu kpuaa er ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la nahan!





IKYAV I TESE 69. M-ii u or.

“Kôr nen ikyav mbi tswamev, de nen ikyom la i yem ken ijime!”

Mba tackles mba vesen mbara er tom ve hegen. Iyol i pupuu i i gber ityough ki ikyôhô i i yer ér whale la ngi tan iwanger er ka uwar u i er sha awen a marble nahan; shin er i gema mlu u i nahan kpa, i nenge ér kwagh môm vihi un kpishi ga. Shi ka mkurtar je ne ga. Er i vihi iyol la nahan kpa, jijingi u or u nan er kwaghbo yô, nan war, nan ngu zenden sha iyol i nan sha u nan nan mciem. Ior mbagenev mba ve lu a mciem iyol shin tso u keren akaa u a lu eren tom sha inja ga la nenge a mi ica, shighe u ica i lu cir inyon i i lu yevese la, nahan kpa, i lu tesen ikyav i pupuu i i lu yevese sha iyange la her, man mngerem ma pupuu ma ma lu yeghen sha mi la; fese je i ver ikyom i whale la i i vihi iyol ga la, i i lu a ave a tenger la shin inya— ikyônough ki torough man awen man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi hen ijiir ne: wa ikyo! Kwagh u ortodoksi ngu!

Nahan, er ken uma na yô, alaghga iyol i zege inyam i i yer ér whale la yange i lu mbaihyomov nav kwagh u cier iyol sha mimi nahan kpa, ken ku na yô, jijingi na hingir kwagh u cier tar ugen iyol u lun a tahav ga.
