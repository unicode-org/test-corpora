MUTU 86. Mchira.

Alakatuli ena aimba matamando a diso lofewa la agwape, ndi nthenga zokongola za mbalame zomwe sizitsika; zochepa zakumwamba, ndimakondwerera mchira.

Kuwerengera mchira waukulu kwambiri wa Sperm Whale kuti uyambike pa thunthu pomwe umakhala pafupi ndi chiuno cha munthu, umakhala pamtunda wake wokha, malo osachepera masikweya mita makumi asanu. Mizu yake yozungulira yozungulira imakula kukhala zikhatho ziwiri zolimba, zolimba, zafulati kapena zopindika, pang'onopang'ono kutsika mpaka kuchepera inchi imodzi. Palibe chamoyo chomwe mizere ya kukongola imatanthauzidwa mopambanitsa kuposa m'malire a kachidutswa kameneka. Pakukula kwake kwakukulu mu whale wamkulu wathunthu, mchirawo udzapitirira mamita makumi awiri m'mimba mwake.

Chiwalo chonsecho chimawoneka ngati bedi lowundana la minyewa yolumikizidwa; koma dulani, ndipo mupeza kuti pali mitundu itatu yosiyana siyana:—chapamwamba, chapakati, ndi chapansi. Ulusi womwe uli pamwamba ndi pansi, ndi wautali komanso wopingasa; zapakati, zazifupi kwambiri, ndipo zimayenda mopingasa pakati pa zigawo zakunja. Kapangidwe kautatu kameneka, mofanana ndi china chilichonse, chimapereka mphamvu kumchira. Kwa wophunzira wa makoma akale achiroma, wosanjikiza wapakati adzapereka chidwi chofananira ndi njira yopyapyala ya matailosi omwe nthawi zonse amasinthana ndi mwala muzotsalira zodabwitsa zamakedzana, zomwe mosakayikira zimathandizira kwambiri ku mphamvu yayikulu ya zomangamanga.

Koma ngati kuti mphamvu yaikulu ya m’deralo mumchira wovutayo siinali yokwanira, unyinji wonse wa leviathan umalukidwa ndi ulusi wopingasa ndi ulusi wa minofu ndi ulusi, umene umadutsa mbali zonse za m’chiuno ndi kuthamangira m’mitsempha, mopanda nzeru kusakanikirana nawo, ndipo makamaka zimathandizira ku mphamvu zawo; kotero kuti mumchira mphamvu yolumikizana yopanda malire ya nangumi yonse imawoneka yokhazikika kwambiri. Chiwonongeko chikadakhala chofunikira, ichi chinali chinthu choti tichite.

Ngakhalenso izi—mphamvu zake zodabwitsa, nkomwe zimalepheretsa kupendekera kwake kokongola; kumene ukhanda womasuka undulates kudzera Titanism mphamvu. M'malo mwake, mayendedwe amenewo amapeza kukongola kwawo kowopsa kwambiri. Mphamvu zenizeni sizimasokoneza kukongola kapena mgwirizano, koma nthawi zambiri zimazipereka; ndipo m'chilichonse chokongola kwambiri, mphamvu zimakhala zogwirizana ndi matsenga. Chotsani minyewa yomangika yomwe ponseponse ikuwoneka ikuphulika kuchokera ku marble mu Hercules wosemedwa, ndipo chithumwa chake chikanatha. Pamene Eckerman wodzipereka anakweza nsalu yansaluyo kuchokera pa mtembo wamaliseche wa Goethe, iye anadzazidwa ndi chifuwa chachikulu cha mwamunayo, chomwe chinkawoneka ngati chigonjetso chachipambano cha Aroma. Pamene Angelo apenta ngakhale Mulungu Atate mu mawonekedwe aumunthu, zindikirani kulimba komwe kulipo. Ndipo zirizonse zimene zingavumbule za chikondi chaumulungu mwa Mwana, zithunzi zofewa, zopindika, zachitaliyana zachikale, m’mene lingaliro lake laikidwamo bwino lomwe; Zithunzi izi, zaumphawi monga momwe zilili zolimba mtima, sizikuwonetsa mphamvu iliyonse, koma zachikazi, zachikazi zakugonjera ndi kupirira, zomwe zimavomerezedwa m'manja onse, zimapanga ukoma wodabwitsa wa ziphunzitso zake.

Umu ndimo kuthanuka kobisika kwa chiwalo chomwe ndimachichitira, kuti kaya ndikuchita masewera, kapena moona mtima, kapena mwaukali, kaya ndi momwe mungakhalire, kupindika kwake kumakhala kodziwika ndi chisomo chopambana. M'menemo palibe mkono wanthambi ungapyole izo.

Zoyenda zisanu zazikulu ndizachilendo kwa izo. Choyamba, pamene amagwiritsidwa ntchito ngati chipsepse chakupita patsogolo; Chachiwiri, akagwiritsidwa ntchito ngati nkhonya pankhondo; Chachitatu, mu kusesa; Chachinayi, pakulota; Chachisanu, m'maseŵera othamanga kwambiri.

Chachitatu: Sindingathe kusonyeza, koma zikuwoneka kwa ine, kuti mu chinsomba lingaliro la kukhudza ndilokhazikika mumchira; pakuti pamenepa muli chakudya chokoma chongofanana ndi kukongola kwa chitamba cha njovu. Kukoma kumeneku kumaonekera makamaka m’ntchito ya kusesa, pamene m’moyo wachikazi wofatsa namgumi mofatsa kwinakwake amasuntha mafunde ake aakulu uku ndi uku pamwamba pa nyanja; ndipo ngati akumva koma ndevu za amalinyero, tsoka kwa woyendetsa panyanja uyo, ndevu ndi zonse. N'chikondi chotani nanga chimene chilipo m'mawu oyamba amenewo! Ndikadakhala kuti mchirawu unali wamphamvu, ndiyenera kundiganizira nthawi yomweyo za njovu ya Darmonodes yomwe imakonda kugulitsa maluwa, ndipo moni wochepa umapereka ma mphuno kwa atsikana, kenako ndikusisita madera awo. Pa nkhani zambiri kuposa imodzi, chisoni n'chakuti namgumi alibe ukoma prehensile mchira wake; Pakuti ndamvanso za njovu ina, imene inavulazidwa pankhondoyo, inkapindikira mozungulira chitamba chake ndi kutulutsa muvi.

Chachinayi: Kuba mosadziŵika pa namgumi m’chisungiko chongopeka chapakati pa nyanja zapayekha, mumampeza wosapendekeka kuchoka pagulu lalikulu laulemu wake, ndipo ngati mphaka, akuseŵera panyanjapo ngati kuti ndi malo otenthetserako moto. Koma mukuwonabe mphamvu yake mumasewera ake. Zikhato zazikulu za mchira wake zimatambasulidwa mmwamba; ndiye kugunda pamwamba, phokoso la bingu limamveka kwa mailosi. Mutha kuganiza kuti mfuti yayikulu idatulutsidwa; ndipo ngati muwona nkhata yowala ya nthunzi kuchokera ku spiracle kumalekezero ake ena, mungaganize kuti ndi utsi wochokera pabowo logwira.

Chachisanu: Monga momwe zimayandama za leviathan, ma flukes amagona kwambiri pansi pa msinkhu wa msana wake, ndiye kuti sakuwonekeratu pansi; koma akatsala pang'ono kugwera mu kuya, mafunde ake onse ndi mapazi osachepera makumi atatu a thupi lake amagwedezeka mumlengalenga, ndipo amakhala akunjenjemera kwa kamphindi, mpaka kutsika pansi kuti asawoneke. Kupatulapo kusweka kwakukulu—pamalo enanso kumene tinganene—chinsomba chimenechi cha zinsomba za nangumi mwina ndicho chochititsa chidwi kwambiri chooneka m’chilengedwe chonse. Kuchokera kuzinthu zopanda malire, mchira wawukulu umawoneka ngati ukukwatula kumwamba kwambiri. Chifukwa chake mmaloto, ndawonapo satana wamkulu akutulutsa chikhadabo chake chozunzika kuchokera kumoto wa Baltic wa Gahena. Koma poyang'ana zochitika zotere, zimadalira momwe mulili; ngati m’Danti, ziwanda zidzakufikirani; ngati mwa Yesaya, angelo akulu. Nditaimirira pamwamba pa mlongoti wa ngalawa yanga pamene dzuŵa likutuluka thambo ndi nyanja yofiira, ndinaona gulu lalikulu la anamgumi kum’maŵa, zonse zikupita kudzuwa, ndipo kwa kamphindi zikunjenjemera pamodzi ndi nsonga zazikuluzikulu. Monga momwe ndinadzionera panthaŵiyo, kupembedzedwa kwakukulu koteroko kwa milungu sikunaoneke konse, ngakhale ku Perisiya, nyumba ya olambira moto. Monga momwe Ptolemy Philopater anachitira umboni za njovu ya mu Afirika, ine ndiye ndinachitira umboni za namgumiyo, ndimati iye anali wodzipereka kwambiri pa zamoyo zonse. Kuyerekeza mwamwayi m'mutu uno, pakati pa namgumi ndi njovu, malingana ndi mbali zina za mchira wa imodzi ndi thunthu la inayo, siziyenera kuyika ziwalo ziwirizo pamlingo wofanana, mochulukira zolengedwa zomwe iwo ali.
