MUTU 101. Chophimba.

Sitima yapamadzi ya Chingerezi isanazimiririke, zikhale pansi pano, kuti adachokera ku London, ndipo adatchedwa malemu Samuel Enderby, wamalonda wa mzinda umenewo, choyambirira cha nyumba yodziwika bwino ya whaling ya Enderby & Sons; nyumba yomwe mwa lingaliro langa la whaleman wosauka, imabwera pafupi ndi nyumba zachifumu zogwirizana za Tudors ndi Bourbons, ponena za chidwi chenicheni cha mbiri yakale. Kwautali wotani, chisanafike chaka cha Ambuye wathu 1775, nyumba yosokera yamchere imeneyi inalipo, zolembedwa zanga zambiri za nsomba sizimamveketsa bwino; koma m’chaka chimenecho (1775) chinalinganiza zombo zoyambirira za Chingelezi zimene nthaŵi zonse zimasaka Nangumi wa Umuna; ngakhale kwa zaka zingapo zapitazo (kuyambira 1726) a Coffins athu olimba mtima ndi Maceys a Nantucket ndi Munda Wamphesa anali ndi zombo zazikulu zothamangitsa Leviathan, koma Kumpoto ndi Kumwera kwa Atlantic: osati kwina kulikonse. Zikhale zolembedwa momveka bwino apa, kuti a Nantucketers anali oyamba pakati pa anthu kulumikiza ndi chitsulo chotukuka kwambiri Whale Whale; ndi kuti kwa theka la zana anali anthu okhawo padziko lonse lapansi omwe adamuzunza.

Mu 1778, sitima yabwino kwambiri, Amelia, inakonzekera cholinga chenichenicho, ndipo pa mlandu wokhawo wa Enderbys amphamvu, molimba mtima anazungulira Cape Horn, ndipo inali yoyamba pakati pa mayiko kutsitsa bwato la namgumi lamtundu uliwonse mu Nyanja Yaikulu ya South. Ulendowu unali waluso komanso wamwayi; ndipo pobwerera kumalo ake ogonerako ndi kugwira kwake kodzala ndi umuna wamtengo wapatali, chitsanzo cha Amelia posapita nthaŵi chinatsatiridwa ndi zombo zina, Chingelezi ndi Chiamerika, ndipo chotero mabwalo aakulu a Sperm Whale a Pacific anatsegulidwa. Koma osakhutitsidwa ndi ntchito yabwinoyi, nyumba yosatopayo idakhazikikanso: Samuel ndi Ana ake onse - angati, amayi awo amangodziwa - komanso mothandizidwa ndi iwo, ndipo mwina, ndikuganiza, molipira, boma la Britain lidakopeka kuti atumize Rattler paulendo wamansomba wotulukira ku South Sea. Polamulidwa ndi Post-Captain wapamadzi, Rattler anayenda ulendo wokhotakhota, ndipo anachita ntchito ina; sizikuwoneka bwanji. Koma izi siziri zonse. Mu 1819, nyumba yomweyo inapanga sitima yapamadzi yotchedwa discovery whale yawoyawo, kuti ipite paulendo wokoma kumadzi akutali a Japan. Sitimayo, yotchedwa "Syren" - idayenda bwino kwambiri poyesera; ndipo zinali choncho pamene malo aakulu a Whaling a ku Japan anayamba kudziwika mofala. The Syren mu ulendo wotchuka umenewu analamulidwa ndi Captain Coffin, Nantucketer.

Ulemu wonse kwa a Enderbies, chifukwa chake, omwe nyumba yawo, ndikuganiza, ilipo mpaka lero; ngakhale kuti mosakayikira Samueli woyambirirayo ayenera kuti kalekalelo anazembetsa chingwe chake ku Nyanja Yakummwera yaikulu ya dziko lina.

Sitimayo yotchedwa pambuyo pake inali yoyenerera ulemu, pokhala wothamanga kwambiri komanso luso lapamwamba kwambiri. Ndinamukwera kamodzi pakati pausiku kwinakwake kugombe la Patagonia, ndikumwa madzi abwino m'malo owonetsera. Anali masewera abwino omwe tinali nawo, ndipo onse anali malipenga - aliyense m'bwalomo. Moyo waufupi kwa iwo, ndi imfa yosangalatsa. Ndipo masewera abwino aja amene ine ndinali nawo—nthawi yaitali, motalika kwambiri Ahabu wokalamba atagwira matabwa ake ndi chidendene cha minyanga—izo zimandikumbukira ine za kuchereza kolemekezeka, kolimba, kwa Saxon kwa ngalawayo; ndipo mulole mwana wangayo andiiwale ine, ndipo mdierekezi andikumbukire ine, ngati ine nditasiya konse kupenya kwa izo. Kutembenuza? Kodi ndati tinali ndi zopindika? Inde, ndipo tidachitembenuza pamlingo wa magaloni khumi pa ola; ndipo pamene chiphokosocho chinabwera (pakuti chinachoka kumeneko pafupi ndi Patagonia), ndipo manja onse—alendo ndi onse—anaitanidwa ku nsonga za pamwamba pa matanthwe, tinali olemera kwambiri kotero kuti tinkakwera m’mwamba wina ndi mnzake m’mwamba; ndipo mosadziwa tinaveka masiketi a jekete zathu m’matanga, kotero kuti tinapachikidwa pamenepo, tikumangirira mwamphamvu mumphepo yamkuntho, chitsanzo chochenjeza kwa phula loledzera. Komabe, milongotiyo sinapitirire; ndipo pang'ono ndi pang'ono tidatsika, osaganiza bwino, kotero kuti tidayenera kudutsanso, ngakhale utsi wonyezimira wamchere udasefukira pansi pa scuttle, m'malo mwake adasungunuka ndikuzifutsa zomwe ndimakonda.

Ng'ombeyo inali yabwino-yolimba, koma yokhala ndi thupi. Iwo anati iyo inali ng'ombe-ng'ombe; ena, kuti anali dromedary ng'ombe; koma sindikudziwa, motsimikiza, momwe zidakhalira. Iwo anali nawonso zinyenyeswazi; tinthu tating'ono, koma tokulirapo, tofanana ndi globular, ndi dumplings zosawonongeka. Ndinkalakalaka kuti mungazimvere, ndikuzigudubuza mwa inu zitamezedwa. Ngati mutawerama patsogolo kwambiri, mumayika pachiwopsezo kuti akutulutseni ngati mipira ya mabiliyoni. Mkate—koma zimenezo sizikanatheka; pambali pake, chinali chotsutsa-scorbutic; Mwachidule, mkatewo unali ndi ndalama yokhayo yomwe anali nayo. Koma malo achitetezowo sanali opepuka kwambiri, ndipo zinali zosavuta kuponda pakona yamdima mutadya. Koma zonse, kumutenga kuchokera ku galimoto kupita ku woyendetsa, poganizira za kukula kwa ma boilers a wophika, kuphatikizapo zikopa zake zamoyo; kutsogolo ndi kumbuyo, ndikunena, Samuel Enderby inali sitima yapamadzi; zabwino ndi zoipa; chopindika chabwino ndi cholimba; crack anzake onse, ndi likulu kuchokera nsapato zidendene kuti chipewa-band.

Koma nchifukwa ninji, mukuganiza kuti, Samuel Enderby, ndi amphaka ena Achingelezi omwe ndimawadziwa—osati onse—anali zombo zotchuka, zochereza; amene ankadutsa mozungulira ng'ombe, ndi mkate, ndi chitini, ndi nthabwala; ndipo sanatope posachedwa kudya, ndi kumwa, ndi kuseka? Ndikuuzani. Chisangalalo chochuluka cha nsomba za ku England zimenezi ndi nkhani yofufuza mbiri yakale. Komanso sindinasiye kufufuza za mbiri yakale ya whale, pamene zikuwoneka kuti ndizofunikira.

Angerezi anali patsogolo pa kusodza anamgumi ndi Hollanders, Zealanders, ndi Danes; amene adachokera kwa mawu ambiri omwe adakalipobe pa usodzi; ndipo chowonjezerapo, zizolowezi zawo zakale zonenepa, zokhuza kudya ndi zakumwa zambiri. Pakuti, mwachizolowezi, sitima yapamadzi ya ku England imasokoneza antchito ake; koma sizinali choncho ndi whale wa Chingerezi. Choncho, mu Chingerezi, chinthu ichi cha whaling chimwemwe sichachilendo komanso chachibadwa, koma mwangozi komanso makamaka; ndipo, chifukwa chake, ziyenera kukhala ndi chiyambi chapadera, chomwe chasonyezedwa apa, ndipo chidzafotokozedwabe.

Pakufufuza kwanga mu mbiri ya Leviathan, ndidakumana ndi voliyumu yakale yachi Dutch, yomwe, chifukwa cha fungo lake lonunkhira bwino, ndidadziwa kuti iyenera kukhala yokhudzana ndi nsomba zam'madzi. Mutuwu unali wakuti, "Dan Coopman," motero ndinaganiza kuti ichi chiyenera kukhala chikumbutso chamtengo wapatali cha munthu wina wa ku Amsterdam wogwira ntchito pa usodzi, popeza sitima yamtundu uliwonse iyenera kunyamula cooper yake. Ndinalimbikitsidwa ndi lingaliro ili powona kuti inali kupanga "Fitz Swackhammer" imodzi. Koma bwenzi langa Dr. Snodhead, munthu wophunzira kwambiri, profesa wa Low Dutch ndi High German pakoleji ya Santa Claus ndi St. Pott’s, kwa amene ndinam’patsa ntchito yomasulira, kum’patsa bokosi la makandulo a umuna kaamba ka vuto lake—Dr. Snodhead yemweyu, atangoona bukhulo, ananditsimikizira kuti “Dan Coopman” sanatanthauze “The Merchant” koma “Dan Coopman.” Mwachidule, buku lakale ndi lophunzira la Low Dutch lomwe limafotokoza za malonda aku Holland; ndipo, mwa nkhani zina, munali nkhani yosangalatsa kwambiri ya usodzi wake wa anamgumi. Ndipo m'mutu uno munali, mutu wakuti, "Smeer," kapena "Fat," kuti ndinapeza mndandanda wautali watsatanetsatane wa zovala zosungiramo zosungiramo katundu ndi zosungiramo za 180 za Dutch whalemen; kuchokera pamndandanda womwe, monga momwe anamasuliridwa ndi Dr. Snodhead, ndikulemba zotsatirazi:

400,000 lbs. wa ng'ombe. 60,000 lbs. Friesland nkhumba. 150,000 lbs. nsomba zamasamba. 550,000 lbs. za biscuit. 72,000 lbs. wa mkate wofewa. Mafuta 2,800 a mafuta. 20,000 lbs. Texel & Leyden tchizi. 144,000 lbs. tchizi (mwinamwake nkhani yotsika). 550 ankers aku Geneva. 10,800 migolo ya mowa.

Matebulo ambiri owerengera amakhala owuma powerenga; sizili choncho m’nkhani ino, komabe, pamene woŵerenga amasefukira ndi mapaipi athunthu, migolo, quarts, ndi gills za jini yabwino ndi chisangalalo chabwino.

Panthawiyo, ndinathera masiku atatu ndikugayitsa mowa moŵa, nyama ya ng'ombe, ndi mkate wonsewo kwa masiku atatu, pamene malingaliro ozama anaperekedwa kwa ine, okhoza kugwiritsira ntchito mopitirira muyeso ndi Plato; ndipo, kuonjezera apo, ndinapanga ma tebulo enaake, okhudza kuchuluka kwa nsomba za nsomba, ndi zina zotero, zodyedwa ndi msodzi aliyense wa Low Dutch muusodzi wamakedzana wa Greenland ndi Spitzbergen. Poyambirira, kuchuluka kwa batala, ndi Texel ndi Leyden tchizi amadya, zikuwoneka zodabwitsa. Ndikunena, komabe, ku chikhalidwe chawo chonyansa, kuchitiridwa nkhanza kwambiri ndi ntchito yawo, makamaka pochita masewera awo m'nyanja zozizira za Polar, m'mphepete mwa dziko la Esquimaux kumene anthu omwe ali ndi moyo amalonjezana wina ndi mzake mu mafuta a sitima.

Mowa nawonso ndi waukulu kwambiri, migolo 10,800. Tsopano, monga momwe nsomba za polar zija zikanatha kuimbidwa mlandu m'chilimwe chachifupi cha nyengoyo, kotero kuti ulendo wonse wa mmodzi wa nsombazi za Dutch, kuphatikizapo ulendo waufupi wopita ndi kuchokera ku nyanja ya Spitzbergen, sunapitirire miyezi itatu, kunena kuti, ndi kuwerengera amuna 30 ku gulu lililonse la zombo 180, tili ndi 5,400 mu Dutch; chifukwa chake, ndikunena, tili ndi migolo iwiri ya mowa pa munthu aliyense, kwa malipiro a masabata khumi ndi awiri, kupatula gawo lake labwino la ma ankers 550 a gin. Tsopano, kaya ma gin ndi mowa wa harpooneers, ogwedezeka monga momwe wina angaganizire kuti anali, anali amuna oyenerera oimirira pamutu wa ngalawa, ndi kukhala ndi cholinga chabwino pa zinsomba zowuluka; izi zingawoneke ngati zosatheka. Koma iwo anawalondolera iwo, nawamenya iwonso. Koma uku kunali kutali kwambiri Kumpoto, zikumbukiridwe, kumene mowa umagwirizana bwino ndi malamulo; pa Equator, m'mwezi wathu wakumwera, moŵa ukhoza kupangitsa ng'ombeyo kugona pamutu wa mast ndi mowa m'ngalawa yake; ndipo kutayika komvetsa chisoni kungathe kuchitika ku Nantucket ndi New Bedford.

Koma osatinso; zokwanira zanenedwa kusonyeza kuti Dutch whalers akale a zaka mazana awiri kapena atatu zapitazo anali ziŵindi zazikulu; ndi kuti owomba namgumi achingelezi sananyalanyaze chitsanzo chabwino kwambiri. Pakuti, amati, poyenda m'chombo chopanda kanthu, ngati simungapeze chilichonse chabwinoko kuchokera kudziko lapansi, tengani chakudya chamadzulo chabwino, osachepera. Ndipo izi zimachotsa decanter.





MUTU 102. Woponya Mphepete mwa Nkhondo.

Mpaka pano, pofotokoza mofotokozera za Umuna Whale, ine makamaka ndakhala pa zodabwitsa za mawonekedwe ake akunja; kapena payekhapayekha komanso mwatsatanetsatane pazinthu zina zingapo zamkati. Koma pakumvetsetsa kwakukulu ndi kozama za iye, ndikuyenera tsopano kumumasula iye mopitirirabe, ndi kumasula nsonga za payipi yake, kumasula nsonga zake, ndikumasula mbedza ndi maso a zimfundo za mafupa ake amkati, ndikumuika patsogolo panu mu ultimatum yake; ndiko kunena kuti, mu mafupa ake opanda malire.

Koma bwanji tsopano, Ismayeli? Nanga bwanji, iwe, wopalasa wongopalasa msodzi, ukudziyesa ngati sudziwa kanthu za madera a pansi pa nyanja ya namgumi? Kodi erudite Stubb, atakwera pamwamba pa capstan wanu, adapereka maphunziro a anatomy a Cetacea; ndipo mothandizidwa ndi mphepo, mutenge nthiti yachitsanzo kuti muwonetsere? Dzifotokozere wekha, Ishmaeli. Kodi mungakwere nangumi wachikulire kuti ayesedwe, monga momwe wophika amawotcha nkhumba yowotcha? Ayi ndithu. Iwe ndiwe mboni yowona, Ismayeli mpaka pano; koma samalani kuti mulanda ukulu wa Yona yekha; mwayi wokambilana pa ma joists ndi matabwa; ndi denga, ndi zipinjiri, ndi zogona, ndi zansi, ndi zomangira za mdulidwe wa Leviatani; ndi kufanana ndi nkhokwe, zipinda zamkaka, zophika mafuta, ndi tchizi m'matumbo ake.

Ndikuvomereza, kuti kuyambira Yona, ansomba ochepa adalowa pansi pa khungu la namgumi wamkulu; komabe, ndadalitsidwa ndi mwayi womusweka pang'ono. M'sitima yomwe ndinali nayo, kamwana kakang'ono ka Sperm Whale kamodzi kamene kanakwezedwa ndi thupi kumtunda kuti apange poko kapena thumba lake, kuti apange masiketi a nthiti za makoko, ndi mitu ya mikondo. Mukuganiza kuti ndalola mwayi umenewo kupita, osagwiritsa ntchito chipewa changa cha bwato ndi mpeni, ndikuswa chisindikizo ndikuwerenga zonse za mwana wamng'onoyo?

Ndipo ponena za chidziwitso changa chenicheni cha mafupa a leviathan mu kukula kwawo kwakukulu, kwakukulu, chifukwa cha chidziwitso chosowacho ndili ndi ngongole kwa bwenzi langa lachifumu Tranquo, mfumu ya Tranque, mmodzi wa Arsacides. Chifukwa chokhala ku Tranque, zaka zapitazo, pamene ndinalumikizidwa ku sitima yamalonda ya Dey ya Algiers, ndinaitanidwa kuti ndikakhale ndi nthawi ya tchuthi cha Arsacidean ndi mbuye wa Tranque, ku nyumba yake ya kanjedza yopuma pantchito ku Pupella; Mphepete mwa nyanja yotalikirana kwambiri ndi imene amalinyero athu ankatcha Bamboo-Town, likulu lake.

Pakati pa mikhalidwe ina yambiri yabwino, bwenzi langa lachifumu Tranquo, pokhala ndi mphatso ya chikondi chodzipereka pa nkhani zonse za vertu zankhanza, anasonkhanitsa pamodzi mu Pupella zinthu zilizonse zosoŵa zimene anthu ake akanatulukira mwanzeru; makamaka mitengo yosema ya zipangizo zodabwitsa, zigoba zonyezimira, mikondo, zopalasa zamtengo wapatali, mabwato onunkhira; ndipo zonsezo zinagawika mwa zozizwa za cilengedwe, ndi mafunde a msonkho anaponya m'mbali mwace.

Mkulu pakati pa omalizirawa anali Nangumi wamkulu wa Umuna, yemwe, pambuyo pa chiwopsezo chambiri chowopsa, adapezeka atafa ndipo ali wosokonekera, mutu wake uli pamtengo wa koko, womwe mapiko ake okhala ngati nthenga, zopindika zidawoneka ngati ndege yake yobiriwira. Thupi lalikululo litatha kuvula zopindika mozama kwambiri, ndipo mafupawo atauma padzuwa, mafupawo ananyamulidwa mosamala kupita ku Pupella glen, komwe kunali kachisi wamkulu wa kanjedza.

Nthitizo zinapachikidwa ndi zikho; fupa la msanawo linajambulidwa ndi zolemba za Arsacidean, m’zolembedwa zachilendo; m'chigaza, ansembe ankasunga lawi lonunkhira losazimitsidwa, kotero kuti mutu wachinsinsi unatulutsanso mpweya wake; pamene, ataimitsidwa panthambi, nsagwada zapansi zowopsya zinkagwedezeka pa onse odzipereka, monga lupanga lopachikidwa tsitsi lomwe lidachititsa mantha kwambiri Damocles.

Zinali zochititsa chidwi kwambiri. Mtengowo unali wobiriwira ngati mosses wa Icy Glen; mitengo inaima pamwamba ndi yodzikuza, ikumva kuyamwa kwawo kwamoyo; Pansi pake nthaka yogwira ntchitoyo inali ngati nsalu yoluka nsalu, yokhala ndi kapeti yokongola kwambiri, imene nthambi za mpesawo zinkapanga ulusi wopota ndi ulusi, ndipo maluwa amoyo ankapanga zithunzizo. Mitengo yonse, ndi nthambi zake zonse zolemedwa; zitsamba zonse, ndi ferns, ndi udzu; mpweya wofalitsa uthenga; zonsezi zinali zogwira ntchito mosalekeza. Kupyolera mu kutsetsereka kwa masamba, dzuwa lalikulu linkawoneka ngati shuttle yowuluka yoluka verdure wosatopa. O, woluka wotanganidwa! Woluka nsalu wosaoneka!—Imani kaye!—mawu amodzi! nyumba yachifumu iti? chifukwa chiyani ntchito zosatha zonsezi? Lankhula iwe mmisiri, gwira dzanja lako, koma mawu amodzi ndi iwe! Ayi, ntchentche ziuluka—zithunzi ziyandama kuchokera pansalu; kapeti wothamanga wa freshet nthawi zonse amachoka. Mulungu woluka, iye amaluka; ndi kuluka kumeneko agontha, kuti asamve mau a munthu; ndi kung’ung’udza kumeneko, ifenso, amene tiyang’ana pa nsalu yoluka, timagontha; ndipo pokha pamene ife tidzauthawa ife tidzamva mawu chikwi amene amalankhula kupyolera mwa icho. Pakuti ngakhale zili choncho m'mafakitale onse zinthu. Mawu olankhulidwa omwe samveka pakati pa zopota zowuluka; mawu omwewo amamveka momveka bwino popanda makoma, kuphulika kuchokera ku mazenera otsegulidwa. Potero apezeka oyipa. Ah, munthu! pamenepo, samalani; pakuti kotero, mu kulira konseku kwa dziko lalikulu, maganizo anu ochenjera angamveke patali.

Tsopano, pakati pa matabwa obiriŵira, opanda mpumulo a mtengo wa Arsacidean umenewo, chigoba chachikulu, choyera, cholambiridwa chinali kulira—chizilombo chachikulu! Komabe, pamene ulusi wobiriŵira wolukidwa nthaŵi zonse unkasanganikirana ndi kumvekera momuzungulira, walesi wamphamvuyo anawoneka ngati mmisiri waluso; iye yekha analukidwa ndi mpesa; mwezi uliwonse kuganiza zobiriwira, zatsopano; koma iye mwini mafupa. Moyo wopindidwa Imfa; Imfa trellised Moyo; mulungu woyipa adakhala ndi Moyo wachinyamata, ndipo adamuberekera ulemerero wamutu wopindika.

Tsopano, pamene ndi Tranquo yachifumu ndinayendera chinsomba chodabwitsa ichi, ndipo ndinawona chigaza cha guwa la nsembe, ndi utsi wochita kupanga ukukwera kuchokera pamene ndege yeniyeni inatulutsa, ndinadabwa kuti mfumu iyenera kuona tchalitchi ngati chinthu cha vertu. Iye anaseka. Koma ndinadabwa kwambiri kuti ansembe ayenera kulumbira kuti jeti yake yofuka inali yowona. Ndidayenda uku ndi uku ndisanagonjetse chigoba ichi - chinaphwanya mipesa pambali - idathyola nthiti - komanso ndi mpira wa twine wa Arsacidean, woyendayenda, wodumphira pakati pa makhonde ake ambiri okhotakhota, okhala ndi mithunzi ndi mabwalo. Koma posakhalitsa mzere wanga unatha; ndikuyitsatira mmbuyo, ndidatulukira pachitseko chomwe ndidalowa. Sindinaona zamoyo mkati; panalibe koma mafupa basi.

Pondidula ndodo yobiriwira, ndinadumphiranso m'mafupa. Ansembewo atang’ambika m’chigaza chamutu, anandiona ndikukwera pamwamba pa nthiti yomaliza kuti, “Bwanji tsopano!” iwo anafuula; “Kodi muyesa mulungu wathu uyu! "Inde, ansembe - chabwino, mudzamupanga mpaka liti?" Koma pamenepo panabuka mkangano woopsa pakati pawo, wa mapazi ndi mainchesi; Anathyolana ma sconces wina ndi mzake ndi ndodo zawo pabwalo - chigaza chachikulu chinamvekera - ndipo nditapeza mwayi umenewo, ndinamaliza kuyesa kwanga mwamsanga.

Zoyezetsa izi tsopano ndikufuna kuziyika pamaso panu. Koma choyamba, zilembedwe, kuti, pankhani iyi, sindine womasuka kunena muyeso wamtundu uliwonse womwe ndikufuna. Chifukwa pali maulamuliro a mafupa omwe mungatchule, kuyesa kulondola kwanga. Kuli Nyumba yosungiramo zinthu zakale yotchedwa Leviathan Museum, akundiuza ine, ku Hull, England, limodzi la madoko ochitira anamgumi a m’dzikolo, kumene ali ndi zitsanzo zabwino za anamgumi ndi anamgumi ena. Mofananamo, ndamva kuti m’nyumba yosungiramo zinthu zakale ya Manchester, ku New Hampshire, ali ndi chimene eni ake amachitcha “chitsanzo chokhacho changwiro cha Greenland kapena River Whale ku United States.” Komanso, pamalo ena ku Yorkshire, England, dzina lake Burton Constable, Sir Clifford Constable wina ali ndi mafupa a Nangumi wotchedwa Sperm Whale, koma aakulu pang’ono, osafanana ndi a mnzanga a King Tranquo.

M'zochitika zonsezi, anamgumi omwe adasokonekera omwe mafupa awiriwa anali ake, adatengedwa ndi eni ake pazifukwa zofanana. Mfumu Tranquo kulanda ake chifukwa ankafuna; ndi Sir Clifford, chifukwa anali mbuye wa ziwembu za zigawozo. Nangumi wa Sir Clifford wakhala akufotokozedwa paliponse; kotero kuti, monga bokosi lalikulu la zotengera, muthe kumutsegula ndi kutseka, m’mabowo ake onse a mafupa—kutambasula nthiti zake ngati chopikusira chachikulu—ndi kugwedezeka tsiku lonse pansagwada yake yakumunsi. MUTU 103. Kuyeza Mafupa a Nangumi.

Poyamba, ndikufuna ndikukhazikitseni mawu omveka bwino, okhudza zamoyo zambiri za Leviathan uyu, yemwe tikuwonetsa mwachidule mafupa ake. Mawu oterowo angakhale othandiza pano.

Kotero kuti nthiti iyi inangopereka theka la lingaliro lenileni la ukulu wamoyo wa gawolo. Koma msana. Chifukwa chake, njira yabwino kwambiri yomwe tingaganizire ndiyo, ndi crane, kuunjikira mafupa ake m'mwamba. Palibe bizinesi yothamanga. Koma tsopano zatha, zikuwoneka ngati Mzati wa Pompey.

MUTU 104. Nangumi Wakufa.

Chifukwa cha kuchuluka kwake kwamphamvu, namgumiyo amapereka mutu wabwino kwambiri woti akulitse, kukulitsa, komanso kukulitsa. Kodi inu, inu simukanakhoza kumukakamiza iye. Ndi ufulu wabwino ayenera kuthandizidwa kokha m'mabuku achifumu. Osawerengeranso makonde ake ozungulira, kuyambira kozungulira mpaka kumchira; tangoganizani za kusinthika kwakukulu kwa matumbo ake, momwe amagona mwa iye ngati zingwe zazikulu ndi ma hawsers atakulungidwa mumtunda wa orlop wapansi pa sitima yankhondo.

Popeza ndakhala ndikugwira ntchito ya Leviathan iyi, ndiyenera kuvomereza kuti ndine wotopa kwambiri pantchitoyi; osayang'anitsitsa tizilombo tating'onoting'ono ta magazi ake, ndikumuzungulira mpaka kumatumbo ake. Popeza tamufotokozera kale m'malo ambiri omwe amakhalapo komanso mawonekedwe ake, tsopano zikuyenera kumukulitsa mumalingaliro akale, zakale, komanso zakale. Akagwiritsidwa ntchito kwa cholengedwa china chilichonse kupatulapo Leviathan—ku nyerere kapena utitiri—mawu oterowo angaonedwe kuti ndi aulemu mopanda chifukwa. Koma pamene Leviathan ndiye lemba, mlanduwo umasinthidwa. Ndikungofuna kuzandima ndi mawu olemera kwambiri a mtanthauzira mawu. Ndipo izi zinenedwe, kuti nthawi iliyonse pamene kwakhala koyenera kufunsira wina m'kati mwa zolembedwa izi, nthawi zonse ndagwiritsa ntchito buku lalikulu la quarto la Johnson, logulidwa mwachindunji kaamba ka cholinga chimenecho; chifukwa chochulukira chosadziwika bwino cha wolemba dikishonale uja chinamuyenereza kulemba dikishonale yoti igwiritsidwe ntchito ndi wolemba chinsomba ngati ine.

Nthawi zambiri munthu amamva za olemba omwe amadzuka ndikukulitsa nkhani yawo, ngakhale ingawoneke ngati wamba. Nanga bwanji, ndi ine, kulemba za Leviathan uyu? Mosazindikira chirography yanga imakula kukhala zilembo zazikulu. Ndipatseni cholembera cha condor! Ndipatseni chigwa cha Vesuvius choyikapo inki! Anzanga, gwirani manja anga! Chifukwa pongolemba malingaliro anga a Leviathan uyu, amanditopetsa, ndikundipangitsa kukomoka ndi kusesa kwawo, ngati kuti ndikuphatikiza gulu lonse la sayansi, mibadwo yonse ya anamgumi, anthu, ndi mastodon, akale, apano, ndi omwe akubwera, ndi zozungulira zonse zapadziko lonse lapansi, osati padziko lonse lapansi. Chotere, komanso chokulitsa, ndi ukoma wa mutu waukulu ndi womasuka! Timakulitsa kuchuluka kwake. Kuti mupange buku lamphamvu, muyenera kusankha mutu wamphamvu. Palibe mawu akulu ndi okhalitsa omwe angalembedwe pa utitiri, ngakhale pali ambiri omwe adayesapo.

Nditalowa m’nkhani ya Zinsomba za Zakale, ndikupereka ziyeneretso zanga monga katswiri wa sayansi ya nthaka, ponena kuti m’nthaŵi zanga zosiyanasiyana ndakhala womanga miyala, ndiponso wokumba kwambiri maenje, ngalande ndi zitsime, mosungiramo vinyo, zipinda zosungiramo zinthu zakale, ndi zitsime zamitundumitundu. Momwemonso, mwa njira yoyambira, ndikufuna kukumbutsa owerenga kuti, ngakhale kuti m'madera oyambirira a geological strata pali zotsalira za zinyama zomwe zatsala pang'ono kutha; zotsalira zotsatiridwa pambuyo pake zopezedwa mu zimene zimatchedwa mapangidwe Apamwamba zimawoneka ngati zolumikizana, kapena pamlingo uliwonse wolumikizidwa, pakati pa zolengedwa zotsutsana ndi nthawi, ndi zija zomwe mibadwo yakutali imanenedwa kuti idalowa m'Likasa; Mingumi yonse ya Zakale zokwiriridwa pansi zakale zomwe zapezedwa mpaka pano zili m’nyengo Yapamwamba, imene ili yomalizira kupangidwa kwachiphamaso. Ndipo ngakhale palibe mmodzi wa iwo amene amayankha ndendende za mtundu uliwonse wodziwika wa nthawi ino, iwo akadali ofanana mokwanira ndi iwo mu mbali zonse, kulungamitsa kukhala kwawo ngati Cetacean fossils.

Zakale zosweka za anamgumi a pre-adamite, zidutswa za mafupa ndi mafupa awo, zapezeka m'munsi mwa Alps, Lombardy, France, England, Scotland, ndi Louisiana, Mississippi, ndi Alabama. Pakati pa chidwi kwambiri za mabwinja oterowo ndi mbali ya chigaza, chimene mu chaka 1779 disinterred mu Rue Dauphine mu Paris, lalifupi msewu kutsegula pafupifupi mwachindunji pa nyumba ya Tuileries; ndipo mafupa analekanitsidwa pofukula madoko aakulu a ku Antwerp, m’nthawi ya Napoliyoni. Koma chodabwitsa kwambiri mwa zotsalira zonse za Cetacean chinali chigoba chachikulu cha chilombo chomwe chinatha, chomwe chinapezeka mchaka cha 1842, pamunda wa Judge Creagh, ku Alabama. Koma zitsanzo zina za mafupa ake akuwoloka nyanja kwa Owen, English Anatomist, zinapezeka kuti chokwawa chonenedwacho chinali chinsomba, ngakhale kuti chinali chamoyo chomwe chinachoka. Chitsanzo chodziwika bwino cha mfundoyi, yobwerezedwa mobwerezabwereza m'bukuli, kuti mafupa a namgumi amangopereka chidziwitso chochepa chabe cha mawonekedwe a thupi lake lomwe analipeza mokwanira. Ndiye dziko lonse linali la chinsomba; ndipo, mfumu ya chilengedwe, iye anasiya kudzuka kwake motsatira mizere yamakono ya Andes ndi Himmalehs. Ndani angasonyeze mbadwa ngati Leviathan? Nkhuku wa Ahabu anakhetsa magazi akale kuposa a Farao. Metusela akuoneka ngati mwana wasukulu. Ndikuyang'ana uku ndikugwirana chanza ndi Shemu. MUTU 105. Kodi Kukula kwa Nangumi Kuchepa?—Kodi Adzawonongeka?

Chotero, pamene Leviathan ameneyu akubwera mosefukira pa ife kuchokera kumutu wa madzi a Muyaya, tingafunsidwe moyenerera, ngati, m’kupita kwa nthaŵi yaitali m’mibadwo yake, sanafooke kuchokera ku unyinji woyambirira wa mabwana ake.

Koma tikafufuza tikupeza, kuti si anamgumi amasiku ano okha amene ali apamwamba kuposa awo amene zotsalira za mafupa awo akupezeka mu Tertiary system (kuphatikiza nyengo yosiyana ya chilengedwe munthu asanakhalepo), koma mwa anamgumi opezeka m’dongosolo Lapamwamba limenelo, amene ali m’mipangidwe yake yomalizirayo amaposa kukula kwake kwa mipangidwe yake yoyambirira.

Pa zinsomba zonse za pre-adamite zomwe zidafukulidwa, zazikulu kwambiri ndi Alabama zomwe zatchulidwa m'mutu womaliza, ndipo zinali zosakwana mamita makumi asanu ndi awiri m'litali mwa chigobacho. Pomwe, tawona kale, kuti tepi-muyeso umapereka mapazi makumi asanu ndi awiri mphambu ziwiri kwa mafupa a chinsomba chachikulu chamakono. Ndipo ndamva, paulamuliro wa whalemen, kuti ma Sperm Whales adagwidwa pafupi ndi mamita zana pa nthawi yogwidwa.

Koma sikungakhale, kuti pamene anamgumi a nthawi ino ali patsogolo mu ukulu pa aja a nyengo zonse zam'mbuyo za geological; Kodi sikutheka, kuti kuyambira nthawi ya Adamu iwo akhala ofooka?

Ndithudi, tiyenera kunena choncho, ngati tikufuna kuyamikira nkhani za njonda monga Pliny, ndi akatswiri akale a zachilengedwe nthaŵi zambiri. Kwa Pliny amatiuza za anamgumi amene anali ndi maekala a moyo wochuluka, ndi Aldrovandus a ena amene anatalika mamita mazana asanu ndi atatu muutali—Rope Walks and Thames Tunnels of Whales! Ndipo ngakhale mu masiku a Banks ndi Solander, akatswiri a zachilengedwe a Cooke, ife timapeza membala wa Chidanishi wa Academy of Sciences atakhala pansi Nyama zina zaku Iceland (reydan-siskur, kapena Matumbo Okwinya) pa mayadi zana limodzi ndi makumi awiri; ndiye mapazi mazana atatu ndi makumi asanu ndi limodzi. Ndipo Lacépède, katswiri wa zachilengedwe wa ku France, m’mbiri yake yowonjezereka ya anamgumi, koyambirira kwenikweni kwa ntchito yake (tsamba 3), amaika Chinsomba Choyenera pa mamita zana limodzi, mapazi mazana atatu ndi makumi awiri kudza asanu ndi atatu. Ndipo ntchitoyi idasindikizidwa mochedwa kwambiri mu AD 1825.

Koma kodi msodzi aliyense adzakhulupirira nkhani zimenezi? Ayi. Nangumi wamakono ndi wamkulu mofanana ndi makolo ake a m’nthaŵi ya Pliny. Ndipo ngati ine ndipita kumene Pliny ali, ine, msodzi (kuposa iye anali), ndidzapanga molimba mtima kumuuza iye chomwecho. Chifukwa sindingathe kumvetsa momwe zimakhalira, kuti pamene amayi a ku Aigupto omwe anaikidwa m'manda zaka zikwi zambiri asanabadwe ngakhale Pliny, samayesa kwambiri m'mabokosi awo monga Kentuckian wamakono mu masokosi ake; ndipo pamene ng’ombe ndi nyama zina zinasema pa magome akale kwambiri a Aigupto ndi Nineve, ndi milingo yachibale imene amakokedwamo, monga momwe zimatsimikizira momveka bwino kuti ng’ombe zonenepa kwambiri, zodyetsedwa m’khola, zamtengo wapatali za Smithfield, osati zofanana zokha, koma zimaposa patali mu ukulu wa ng’ombe zonenepa za Farao; poyang'anizana ndi zonsezi, sindidzavomereza kuti pa zinyama zonse, namgumi yekha ndiye ayenera kufota.

Koma funso lina lidakalipo; omwe nthawi zambiri amakhumudwitsidwa ndi ma Nantucketers omwe amayambiranso. MUTU 106 Mwendo wa Ahabu.

Njira yofulumira yomwe Kaputeni Ahabu adasiyira Samuel Enderby waku London, sizinali zosasamalidwa ndi ziwawa zazing'ono kwa iyemwini. Anayatsa ndi mphamvu pa bwato lake lomwe linabowoka moti mwendo wake wa mnyanga wa njovu unagwedezeka kwambiri. Ndipo atatha kupeza sitima yake, ndi bowo lake la pivot kumeneko, adayendetsa mozungulira mozungulira ndikulamula woyendetsa (zinali, monga kale, chinachake chokhudza kusawongolera kwake mokwanira); ndiye, minyanga ya njovu yogwedezeka kale inalandira chopindika ndi chopindika chowonjezera, kotero kuti ngakhale idakhalabe yathunthu, ndi maonekedwe onse okhumbira, komabe Ahabu sanauone wodalirika kotheratu.

Ndipo, ndithudi, chinali chinthu chaching’ono chodabwitsa, kuti chifukwa cha kusasamala kwake konsekonse, kochita misala, nthaŵi zina Ahabu anasamala mosamalitsa za mkhalidwe wa fupa lakufalo limene mbali ina anaimapo. Pakuti sipanatenge nthawi yaitali kuti Pequod ayambe kuyenda kuchokera ku Nantucket, kuti adapezeka usiku wina atagona pansi, ndipo alibe nzeru; ndi wina wosadziwika, ndi wowoneka ngati wosamvetsetseka, wovulala wosayerekezeka, chiwalo chake cha minyanga ya njovu chinali chitasunthidwa mwamphamvu, kotero kuti chinakanthidwa mwanzeru, ndipo onse adangopyoza ntchafu zake; komanso sizinali zovuta kwambiri kuti chilondacho chinachiritsika.

Ndiponso, panthaŵiyo, sikunalephereke kuloŵa m’maganizo ake a munthu mmodzi, kuti kusautsika konse kwa kuzunzika kumene kunalipo panthaŵiyo kunali kokha nkhani yachindunji ya tsoka lakale; ndipo iyenso mowonekera ankawoneka kuti akuwona, kuti monga chokwawa chakupha kwambiri cha m’dambo chimapitirizabe mtundu wake mosakayika monga woyimba nyimbo wokoma kwambiri wa m’nkhalango; kotero, mofanana ndi chisangalalo chilichonse, zochitika zonse zomvetsa chisoni mwachibadwa zimabala zomwezo. Inde, koposa mofanana, anaganiza Ahabu; popeza makolo ndi mbadwa za Chisoni zimapita patsogolo kuposa makolo ndi mbadwa za Joy. Pakuti, osati kuloza za izi: kuti ndi lingaliro lochokera ku ziphunzitso zina zovomerezeka, kuti pamene zosangalatsa zina zachibadwa pano sizidzakhala ndi ana obadwa kwa iwo ku dziko lina, koma, mosiyana, zidzatsatiridwa ndi kusasangalala-kupanda ana kwa kuthedwa nzeru konse kwa gehena; pamene, ena ochimwa ochimwa adzadzibweretsera iwo okha mbadwa zopitirira patsogolo kwamuyaya za chisoni kuseri kwa manda; osanenanso za izi, zikuwonekabe kuti pali kusalingana pakuwunika mozama kwa chinthucho. Pakuti, anaganiza Ahabu, pamene ngakhale zokondweretsa zapamwamba zapadziko lapansi zinayamba zakhalapo ndi zonyozeka zosazindikirika zomwe zimabisala mwa iwo, koma, pansi, zowawa zamtima zonse, tanthauzo lachinsinsi, ndipo, mwa anthu ena, ukulu wa angelo wamkulu; momwemonso Kufufuza kwawo mwachangu-kusakhulupirira kuchotsera koonekeratu. Kutsata mibado ya mikwingwirima yachivundi iyi, imatitengera ife pakati pa zoyamba zopanda maziko za milungu; kotero kuti, pamaso pa onse okondwa, dzuwa kupanga udzu, ndi zinganga zofewa, yozungulira yokolola-miyezi, ife tiyenera kugonjera kwa ichi: kuti milungu iyo si kukondwera kosatha. Chizindikiro chosatheka, chomvetsa chisoni cha kubadwa pamphumi pa munthu, ndi chizindikiro cha chisoni mwa osayina.

Mosazindikira apa chinsinsi chawululidwa, chomwe mwina chikhoza bwino, mwa njira yokhazikitsidwa, chawululidwa kale. Ndi zina zambiri zokhudza Ahabu, nthawi zonse zinali chinsinsi kwa ena, chifukwa chake zinali, kuti kwa nthawi inayake, asanayambe kapena atatha ulendo wa Pequod, adabisala yekha ndi Grand-Lama-monga yekha; ndipo, kwa nthawi imodzi imeneyo, anafuna pothaŵira wopanda chonena, titero kunena kwake, pakati pa bwalo lamilandu la akufa. Chifukwa chopwetekedwa cha Captain Peleg cha chinthu ichi chinawoneka chosakwanira; ngakhale, ndithudi, kukhudza mbali yonse yakuya ya Ahabu, bvumbulutso lirilonse lidatengapo mdima waukulu kuposa kuwala kofotokozera. Koma, pamapeto pake, zonse zinatuluka; chinthu chimodzi ichi chinachita, osachepera. Tsoka loopsya limenelo linali m'munsi mwa kusakhazikika kwake kwakanthawi. Ndipo osati izi zokha, komanso kwa wobwereketsa nthawi zonse, akugwetsa bwalo kumtunda, yemwe, pazifukwa zilizonse, anali ndi mwayi wocheperako woletsedwa kwa iye; Pamaso pa anthu amanthawo amene anaphedwa amene tawatchula pamwambapa—otsala, monga momwe zinakhalira, osadziŵika kuti Ahabu anali ndani—anachita zoopsa kwambiri, osati kungochoka m’dziko la mizimu ndi maliro. Kotero kuti, kupyolera mu changu chawo pa iye, iwo onse adapangana chiwembu, monga momwe iwo adagona, kuti asokoneze chidziwitso cha chinthu ichi kuchokera kwa ena; ndipo chifukwa chake zidachitika, mpaka pakapita nthawi yayitali, zidachitika pamapiri a Pequod.

Koma zikhale zonsezi momwe zingakhalire; lolani sinodi yosaoneka, yosaoneka bwino m’mlengalenga, kapena akalonga obwezera ndi amphamvu amoto, achite kapena ayi ndi Ahabu wapadziko lapansi, komabe, m’nkhani yamasiku ano ya mwendo wake, iye anatenga kachitidwe kogwirika koonekeratu;—anatcha mmisiri wa matabwa.

Ndipo pamene wogwira ntchitoyo adawonekera pamaso pake, adamuuza mosazengereza kuti ayambe kupanga mwendo watsopano, ndipo adauza abwenziwo kuti amuwone ali ndi zida zonse za nsagwada za njovu (Sperm Whale) zomwe zidasonkhanitsidwa paulendowu, kuti asankhe mosamala zinthu zolimba kwambiri, zowoneka bwino kwambiri zisungidwe. Izi zitachitika, mmisiri wa matabwa analamulidwa kuti mwendowo umalizidwe usiku womwewo; ndi kupereka zoikidwiratu zake zonse, osadalira zokhuza wosakhulupirikayo. Kuwonjezera apo, chiboliboli cha ngalawacho chinalamulidwa kuti chichotsedwe kwa kanthawi kochepa m'malo osungiramo; ndipo kuti nkhaniyo ifulumire, wosula zitsulo analamulidwa kuti apite nthawi yomweyo kupanga zitsulo zilizonse zomwe zingafune.
