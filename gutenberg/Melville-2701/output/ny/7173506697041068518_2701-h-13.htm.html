MUTU 62. The Dart.

Mawu okhudza zomwe zidachitika m'mutu wapitawu.

Malinga ndi kagwiritsidwe ntchito kosasinthika ka nsombazi, bwato la namgumi limakankhira kuchoka m'sitimayo, ndi mtsogoleri kapena whale-wakupha ngati woyendetsa kwakanthawi, ndipo harpooneer kapena whale-fastener amakoka nkhafi yakutsogolo, yomwe imadziwika kuti harpooneer-oar. Tsopano pamafunika mkono wamphamvu, wamanjenje kuti ukanthe chitsulo choyamba mu nsomba; chifukwa nthawi zambiri, chomwe chimatchedwa dart lalitali, chida cholemera chimayenera kuponyedwa pamtunda wa mapazi makumi awiri kapena makumi atatu. Koma ngakhale kuthamangitsa kwanthawi yayitali komanso kutopa, wopalasayo akuyembekezeka kukoka nkhafi yake mpaka kumapeto; ndithudi, akuyembekezeredwa kupereka chitsanzo cha zochita zamphamvu zoposa zaumunthu kwa ena onse, osati kokha mwa kupalasa kodabwitsa, koma mwa kufuula kobwerezabwereza ndi mopanda mantha; ndi kulira mofuula pamwamba pa kampasi, pamene minyewa yonse yalemedwa, ndipo theka linayamba, palibe amene akudziwa koma amene adayesa. Choyamba, sindingathe kulira ndi mtima wonse ndikugwira ntchito mosasamala nthawi imodzi. Mumkhalidwe woterewu, wogwedera, ndiyeno, nsana wake uli pansomba, zonse nthaŵi yomweyo wotopayo wotopayo akumva mfuu yosangalatsa—“Nyamuka, um’patse iye!” Tsopano akuyenera kugwetsa ntchentche yake, kutembenukira chapakati pa njira yake, kunyamula kalulu wake pamphambano, ndipo ndi mphamvu zochepa zomwe zatsala, amalemba nkhani kuti amuponyere mu namgumi. Nzosadabwitsa, kutenga gulu lonse la whalemen mu thupi, kuti mwa makumi asanu mwayi wabwino kwa mivi, si asanu ndi opambana; n’zosadabwitsa kuti anthu ambiri opanda ngozi amatembereredwa ndi kunyozedwa; nzosadabwitsa kuti ena a iwo anaphulitsadi mitsempha yawo ya mwazi m’ngalawamo; n'zosadabwitsa kuti ena umuna whalemen kulibe zaka zinayi ndi migolo inayi; n’zosadabwitsa kuti kwa eni zombo zambiri, kuwomba anamgumi kumangotaya nkhawa; chifukwa ndi harpooneer amene amapanga ulendo, ndipo ngati mutulutsa mpweya m'thupi mwake mungayembekezere bwanji kuupeza kumeneko pamene ambiri akufunidwa!

Apanso, ngati muviyo wapambana, ndiye pa nthawi yachiwiri yovuta, ndiko kuti, pamene chinsomba chikuyamba kuthamanga, woyendetsa sitimayo ndi harpooneer nayenso amayamba kuthamanga kutsogolo ndi kumbuyo, kungozi yomwe ili pafupi ndi iwo eni ndi wina aliyense. Kenako amasintha malo; ndipo kapitawo, kapitawo wamkulu wa bwato laling'ono, aima moyenerera mu mauta a ngalawa.

Tsopano, ine sindisamala yemwe akutsutsa izo, koma zonsezi ndi zopusa ndi zosafunikira. Mtsogoleriyo akhalebe mu mauta kuyambira pachiyambi mpaka kumapeto; Ayenera kudumpha chingwe ndi mkondo, osapalasa chilichonse chomwe angayembekezere, kupatula ngati zinthu zikuwonekera kwa msodzi aliyense. Ndikudziwa kuti izi nthawi zina zingaphatikizepo kuchepa pang'ono kwa liwiro pakuthamangitsa; koma chidziŵitso chanthaŵi yaitali cha anthu osodza anamgumi osiyanasiyana amitundu yoposa umodzi chanditsimikizira kuti m’kulephera kochuluka muusodzi, sikunakhaleko liŵiro la namgumi monga mmene tafotokozera kale kutopa kwa msodzi komwe kwawapangitsa.

MUTU 63. Nkhonya.

Kuchokera mu thunthu, nthambi zimamera; kuchokera mwa iwo, nthambi. Choncho, m'maphunziro opindulitsa, onjezerani mitu.

Nkhota yomwe yatchulidwa patsamba lapitalo iyenera kutchulidwa paokha. Ndi ndodo yachilendo ya mawonekedwe, ena mapazi awiri m'litali, amene perpendicular anaikapo mu starboard gunwale pafupi uta, ndi cholinga cha kupereka mpumulo kwa matabwa malekezero a harpoon, amene ena maliseche, minga mapeto ntchito motsetsereka kuchokera kutsogolo. Potero chidacho chimayandikira kwa wochiwomberayo, amene amachilanda msanga pamalo ake monga mmene munthu wa m’nkhalango akukankhira mfuti yake kukhoma. Ndi mwambo kukhala ndi ma harpoons awiri reposing mu crotch, motero amatchedwa chitsulo choyamba ndi chachiwiri.

Koma nsonga ziwiri izi, iliyonse ndi chingwe chake, zonse zimagwirizana ndi chingwe; chinthu chiri ichi: kuwaponyera iwo onse awiri, ngati nkotheka, wina nthawi yomweyo m'chinsomba chomwecho; kotero kuti ngati wina akakoka, wina akakoka, wina akagwirebe. Ndi kuchulukitsa mwayi. Koma nthawi zambiri zimachitika kuti chifukwa cha kuthamanga kwadzidzidzi, kwachiwawa, kugwedezeka kwa namgumi atalandira chitsulo choyamba, zimakhala zosatheka kwa harpooneer, ngakhale mayendedwe ake ngati mphezi, kuponya chitsulo chachiwiri mwa iye. Komabe, monga chitsulo chachiwiri chikugwirizana kale ndi mzerewo, ndipo mzerewo ukuyenda, choncho chida chimenecho chiyenera, pazochitika zonse, kuyembekezera kuponyedwa kunja kwa ngalawa, mwanjira ina ndi kwinakwake; apo ayi chiwopsezo choopsa kwambiri chikakhudza manja onse. Kugwera m'madzi, molingana ndi izi; zitsulo zotsalira za mzere wa bokosi (zotchulidwa m'mutu wapitawu) zomwe zimapangitsa kuti izi, nthawi zambiri, zikhale zotheka mwanzeru. Koma sikuti nthawi zonse mchitidwe wovutawu umakhala wosasamalidwa ndi anthu omvetsa chisoni kwambiri komanso opha anthu.

Kuonjezera apo: muyenera kudziwa kuti chitsulo chachiwiri chikaponyedwa m'nyanja, kuyambira pamenepo chimakhala cholendewera, choopsa kwambiri, chokhota m'ngalawa ndi namgumi, chimangirira mizere, kapena kudula, ndi kumveka modabwitsa kumbali zonse. Komanso, kawirikawiri, n'zotheka kuchitetezanso mpaka chinsomba chikagwidwa bwino ndi mtembo.

Talingalirani, tsopano, mmene ziyenera kukhalira m’mabwato anai onse akuloŵerera limodzi lamphamvu modabwitsa, lokangalika, ndi namgumi wodziŵa; chifukwa cha mikhalidwe iyi mwa iye, komanso chifukwa cha ngozi zikwizikwi za bizinesi yolimba mtima yotere, zitsulo zisanu ndi zitatu kapena khumi zotayirira za sekondi zingakhale zikulendewera panthawi imodzi. Pakuti, ndithudi, bwato lirilonse limaperekedwa ndi ma harpoons angapo kuti apirire pamzere ngati loyambalo litadutsa mopanda mphamvu popanda kuchira. Zonse izi zafotokozedwa mokhulupirika pano, chifukwa sizingalephere kufotokoza ndime zingapo zofunika kwambiri, ngakhale zovuta kwambiri, muzithunzi zomwe zidzapentidwe.

Nangumi wa Stubb anali ataphedwa patali ndi ngalawayo. Kunali bata; kotero, kupanga tandem wa mabwato atatu, tinayamba ntchito pang'onopang'ono kukokera chikho ku Pequod. Ndipo tsopano, pamene ife amuna khumi ndi asanu ndi atatu okhala ndi manja athu makumi atatu ndi asanu ndi limodzi, ndi zala zala zazikulu zana ndi makumi asanu ndi atatu, tinkagwira ntchito mwapang'onopang'ono ola ndi ola pa mtembo wosauka, waulesi m'nyanja; ndipo chinkawoneka chosasunthika konse, kupatula pazigawo zazitali; Umboni wabwino udaperekedwa pakuchulukira kwa unyinji womwe tidasuntha. Pakuti, pa ngalande yaikulu ya Hang-Ho, kapena chirichonse chimene iwo amachitcha icho, ku China, antchito anayi kapena asanu panjira yapansi adzakoka zinyalala zonyamula katundu zambiri pamlingo wa mailosi pa ola; koma mwano waukuluwu tidaukoka wopeka kwambiri, ngati wolemedwa ndi mtovu wa nkhumba wochuluka.
