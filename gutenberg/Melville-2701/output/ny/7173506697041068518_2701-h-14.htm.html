MUTU 72. Nyani-Chingwe.

M’bizinesi yosokonekera yodula ndi kusamalira namgumi, pali zambiri zothamangira chammbuyo ndi kutsogolo pakati pa ogwira ntchito. Tsopano manja akufunika pano, ndiyenonso manja amafunidwa kumeneko.
