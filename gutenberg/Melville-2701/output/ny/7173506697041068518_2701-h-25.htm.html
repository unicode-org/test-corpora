Inu; kuchokera mu mtima wa gehena ndibaya pa inu; chifukwa cha udani ndikulavulira mpweya wanga womaliza pa inu dziwe lonse lakufa, ndipo popeza palibe chomwe chingakhale changa, ndilole ndikuthamangitseni, ngakhale nditamangidwa kwa inu, perekani !

Nyaluyo anathamanga; chinsomba chokanthidwacho chinawulukira kutsogolo; ndi liwiro loyatsira chingwecho chinadutsa m'mphako; Ahabu anawerama kuti achotsepo; adachichotsa; koma kutembenuka kowuluka kunamugwira m'khosi, ndipo mopanda mawu pomwe osalankhula aku Turkey adamuwombera, adamuwombera m'ngalawamo, asanadziwe kuti wapita. Nthawi yomweyo, nsonga yolemera ya diso yomwe inali kumapeto kwa chingwecho inatuluka mumphika wopanda kanthu, ndikugwetsa wopalasa, ndikumenya nyanjayo, ndikuzimiririka mkati mwake.

Posakhalitsa iwo anadutsa mdima wandiweyani, asing'anga ododometsa adamuwona mbali yake ikuzimiririka, monga mu mpweya wa Fata Morgana; mizati yapamwamba yokha kuchokera m'madzi; Pamene kuli kwakuti oimiridwa ndi kutengeka maganizo, kapena kukhulupirika, kapena choikidwiratu, kumalo awo omwe kale anali okwezeka, oimba nyimbo zachikunjawo anapitirizabe kuyang’ana panyanjapo. Epilogue

“NDIPO INE NDINAPULUMUKA NDEKHA KUTI NDIKUWUZE” Yobu.

Sewero latha. Nangano n’chifukwa chiyani munthu akupita patsogolo apa?—Chifukwa chakuti wina anapulumuka ngoziyo.

Zinangochitika mwamwayi kuti Parsee atasowa, ineyo ndi amene Mathero adamuika kuti alowe m'malo mwa woponya uta wa Ahabu, pamene woponya miviyo adatenga malo opanda munthu; yemweyo, pamene tsiku lomaliza amuna atatuwo anaponyedwa kuchokera m’ngalawa yogwedezeka, anaponyedwa chakumbuyo. Chifukwa chake, ndikuyandama m'mphepete mwa zochitika zomwe zidachitika, ndipo ndikuwona bwino, pamene kuyenderera kwa theka la ngalawa yomira kunandifikira, ndinali pamenepo, koma pang'onopang'ono, kukokeredwa ku vortex yotseka. Nditafika, inali itatsikira padziwe lokoma. Kuzungulira ndi kuzungulira, ndiye, ndikuyenda nthawi zonse kupita ku batani lokhala ngati batani lakuda pamtunda wa bwalo loyenda pang'onopang'ono, ngati Ixion ina yomwe ndidazungulira. Mpaka, ndikupeza malo ofunikirawo, kuwira kwakuda kopita mmwamba kunaphulika; ndipo tsopano, atamasulidwa chifukwa cha kasupe wake wochenjera, ndipo, chifukwa cha kugwedezeka kwake kwakukulu, kukwera ndi mphamvu yaikulu, bokosi la moyo-buoy linawombera utali kuchokera ku nyanja, linagwa, ndipo linayandama pambali panga. Polimbikitsidwa ndi bokosi lamaliro limenelo, pafupifupi usana ndi usiku wonse, ndinayandama pa chingwe chachikulu chofewa chonga nyimbo ya maliro. Nsomba zosavulaza, zinkadutsa ngati kuti zili ndi zokowera pakamwa; amphawi ankhalwe ankayenda ndi milomo yotchinga. Pa tsiku lachiwiri, ngalawa inayandikira, inayandikira, ndipo inandinyamula. Anali Rakele wachinyengo, yemwe pofufuzanso ana ake omwe adasowa, adangopeza mwana wina wamasiye.
