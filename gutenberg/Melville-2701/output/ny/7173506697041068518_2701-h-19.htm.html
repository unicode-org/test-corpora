MUTU 96. Mayesero-ntchito.

Kupatula mabwato ake okwera, wowombera whale waku America amadziwika ndi ntchito zake zoyesa. Amapereka chidwi chodabwitsa cha zomangamanga zolimba kwambiri zomwe zimalumikizana ndi oak ndi hemp popanga chombo chomalizidwa. Zili ngati kuti akuuzira njerwa kuchokera kutchire kupita ku matabwa ake.

Zoyeserera zimabzalidwa pakati pa foromast ndi mainmast, gawo lalikulu kwambiri la sitimayo. Mitengo yomwe ili pansi pake ndi yolimba kwambiri, yokwanira kuti isamalemedwe ndi njerwa ndi dothi lolemera pafupifupi mamita 10 m'litali mwake ndi masikweya asanu, ndi utali wake asanu. Mazikowo salowa m’sitimayo, koma zomangira zake zimakhazikika pamwamba pake ndi mawondo olimba achitsulo omangirira mbali zonse, ndikuzigwetsera mpaka kumitengo. M'mphepete mwake amakutidwa ndi matabwa, ndipo pamwamba pake amakutidwa ndi kanjira kakang'ono, kotsetsereka, komenyedwa. Kuchotsa hatch iyi tikuwonetsa miphika yayikulu yoyesera, iwiri mu chiwerengero, ndi migolo ingapo ingapo. Akasagwiritsidwa ntchito, amasungidwa aukhondo kwambiri. Nthawi zina amapukutidwa ndi sopo ndi mchenga, mpaka amawala mkati ngati mbale zasiliva. Pa nthawi ya ulonda wausiku amalinyero akale onyoza amakwawira mmenemo ndi kudzizungulira kuti agone. Pamene kuli kwakuti amawapukuta—mwamuna mmodzi mumphika uliwonse, mbali ndi mbali—zachinsinsi zambiri zimachitidwa, pamilomo yachitsulo. Ndi malo omwenso amasinkhasinkha mozama masamu. Munali kudzanja lamanzere mphika woyesera wa Pequod, ndi mwala wa sopo ukundizungulira mwachangu, pomwe ndidakhudzidwa koyamba ndi mfundo yodabwitsa, kuti mu geometry matupi onse akuyenda motsatira cycloid, mwala wanga wa sopo mwachitsanzo, udzatsika kuchokera pamalo aliwonse ndendende nthawi yomweyo.

Kuchotsa bolodi lamoto kutsogolo kwa ntchito zoyesera, zomangira zopanda kanthu za mbali imeneyo zimawonekera, zolowetsedwa ndi milomo iwiri yachitsulo ya ng'anjo, mwachindunji pansi pa miphika. Pakamwa pawo pali zitseko zolemera zachitsulo. Kutentha kwakukulu kwa moto kumalepheretsedwa kudziyankhulana ndi sitimayo, pogwiritsa ntchito madzi osaya omwe amatuluka pansi pa malo onse otsekedwa a ntchito. Ndi ngalande yomwe imalowetsedwa kumbuyo, mosungiramo madzi amasungidwanso ndi madzi mwachangu momwe amachitira nthunzi. Palibe ma chimney akunja; amatsegula mwachindunji kuchokera ku khoma lakumbuyo. Ndipo apa tiyeni tibwerere mmbuyo kwa kamphindi.
