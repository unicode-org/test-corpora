MUTU 53. The Gam.

Chifukwa choonekeratu chimene Ahabu sanakwerere m'ngalawayo tinanena chinali ichi: mphepo ndi nyanja zinachititsa namondwe. Koma ngakhale zikanakhala kuti sizinali choncho, iye sakanati amukwere, mwinanso, kuweruza ndi khalidwe lake lotsatira pazochitika zofanana - ngati zikanakhala kuti, mwa kuyamikira, adapeza yankho lolakwika ku funso lomwe anafunsa. Pakuti, monga momwe zinakhalira, sanasamale kuti asamayanjane, ngakhale kwa mphindi zisanu, ndi kaputeni aliyense wachilendo, kupatula ngati akanatha kupereka zina mwazomwe adazifuna. Koma zonsezi zikhoza kukhala zosayerekezeka, sizinali zina zomwe zimanenedwa pano za kagwiritsidwe ntchito kachilendo ka zombo zopha anangumi zikakumana wina ndi mnzake kunyanja zakunja, makamaka paulendo wamba wapamadzi.

Ngati alendo awiri akuwoloka Pine Barrens ku New York State, kapena Salisbury Plain yomwe ilinso bwinja ku England; Ngati akumana mwachisawawa M'chipululu chotere, awiriwa, kwa moyo wawo wonse, Sangapewe kulonjerana; ndi kuyima kwa kamphindi kusinthana nkhani; ndipo, mwinamwake, atakhala pansi kwa kanthawi ndi kupumula mu konsati: ndiye, ndimotani mwachibadwa kuti pa illimitable Pine Barrens ndi Salisbury Plains m'nyanja, ziwiya ziwiri whaling descrying wina ndi mzake pa malekezero a dziko lapansi-kuchoka yekha Fanning's Island, kapena kutali King's Mills; mochuluka bwanji mwachilengedwe, ndikunena, kuti pansi pazifukwa zotere zombozi zisamangosintha matalala, koma zimabwera moyandikana kwambiri, mwaubwenzi komanso momasuka. Ndipo makamaka ichi chikawoneka kukhala chowona, pa nkhani ya zombo za pa doko limodzi, ndi akapitawo, akapitawo, ndi si owerengeka a amuna adziwika kwa wina ndi mzake; chifukwa chake, khalani ndi mitundu yonse ya zinthu zapakhomo zomwe mungakambirane.

Kwa ngalawa yomwe ilibe nthawi yayitali, malire akunja, mwinamwake, ali ndi makalata omwe ali nawo; mulimonse mmene zingakhalire, iye adzaonetsetsa kuti amulola kukhala ndi mapepala a deti patatha chaka chimodzi kapena ziwiri kusiyana ndi lomalizira pa mafaelo ake osaoneka bwino komanso ovala chala chachikulu. Ndipo chifukwa cha ulemu umenewo, ngalawa yopita kunja ikanalandira nzeru zaposachedwa kwambiri zakusokera kuchokera kumalo osokera kumene iye angapiteko, chinthu chofunika kwambiri kwa iye. Ndipo pamlingo wake, zonsezi zidzakhala zoona ponena za zombo zopha anangumi zowolokerana njanji pabwalo lokha, ngakhale kuti sizikhala kwawo kwautali. Pakuti mmodzi wa iwo analandira kusamutsa makalata kuchokera ena atatu, ndipo tsopano kutali kutali chotengera; ndipo ena mwa makalatawo angakhale a anthu a m’sitima imene akukumana nayo tsopano. Kupatula apo, amasinthanitsa nkhani za whale, ndikucheza bwino. Pakuti sikuti amangokumana ndi chifundo chonse cha amalinyero, komanso momwemonso ndi zidziwitso zonse zachilendo zochokera ku ntchito imodzi ndi kugawana zosowa ndi zoopsa.

Komanso kusiyana kwa dziko sikungapangitse kusiyana kulikonse; ndiye kuti, bola ngati onse awiri amalankhula chinenero chimodzi, monga momwe zimakhalira ndi Amereka ndi Chingerezi. Ngakhale, zowonadi, kuchokera kwa owerengeka ochepa achingerezi, misonkhano yotereyi sichitika kawirikawiri, ndipo zikachitika pali bwino kwambiri kuti pakhale mtundu wamanyazi pakati pawo; chifukwa Mngelezi wanu ndi wosungidwa, ndipo Yankee wanu, sakonda chinthu choterocho mwa wina aliyense koma iyemwini. Kupatula apo, ma whalers achingerezi nthawi zina amakhudza mtundu wina wapamwamba kwambiri kuposa waku America; ponena za Nantucketer wautali, wowonda, ndi zikhulupiriro zake zopanda pake, ngati wamba wapanyanja. Koma pamene kupambana uku mu English whalemen kumapangadi, zingakhale zovuta kunena, powona kuti Yankees mu tsiku limodzi, pamodzi, amapha anamgumi ambiri kuposa onse a Chingerezi, pamodzi, m'zaka khumi. Koma ichi ndi cholakwika chaching'ono chosavulaza mu English whale-hunters, chomwe Nantucketer sichimatengera kwambiri; mwina, chifukwa amadziwa kuti ali ndi zofooka zochepa.

Choncho, tikuona kuti pa zombo zonse zimene zimayenda paokha panyanja pawokha, nsombazi zili ndi zifukwa zambiri zokhalira ochezeka—ndipo zili choncho. Pamene, zombo zina zamalonda zimawoloka wina ndi mzake mkatikati mwa Atlantic, nthawi zambiri zimadutsa popanda mawu amodzi odziwika, kudulana wina ndi mzake pamtunda wa nyanja, ngati chingwe cha dandies ku Broadway; ndipo nthawi zonse kudzipereka, mwina, kutsutsa komaliza pa zomangira za wina ndi mzake. Ponena za Amuna Ankhondo, akapeza mwayi wokumana panyanja, amayamba kudutsa mikwingwirima yopusa komanso zosemphana, kunyamula zikwangwani, kotero kuti sizikuwoneka kuti pali chikomerero chochokera pansi pamtima komanso chikondi chaubale pa izo nkomwe. Zokhudza kukumana kwa Sitima za Akapolo, chifukwa chake, ali mwachangu kwambiri, amathawa wina ndi mnzake mwachangu momwe angathere. Ndipo ponena za a Pirates, akapeza mwayi wowoloka mafupa opingasana, matalala oyamba amakhala akuti, “Zigaza zingati?”—mofanana ndi mmene amavumbi amatchulira—“Ndi migolo ingati?” Ndipo funsoli litayankhidwa, achifwamba nthawi yomweyo amapatukana, chifukwa ndi anthu oyipa mbali zonse, ndipo sakonda kuwona mochulukira za zoyipa za anzawo.

Koma yang’anani pa wowopa Mulungu, woona mtima, wosaoneka bwino, wochereza alendo, wochezeka, womasuka ndi wosavuta! Kodi namgumiyo amachita chiyani akakumana ndi whale wina panyengo yabwino? Ali ndi " Gam ," chinthu chosadziwika bwino kwa zombo zina zonse zomwe sizinamvepo za dzina; ndipo ngati mwamwayi amva za izo, amangosekerera, ndi kubwereza zinthu zamasewera za "spouters" ndi "blubber-boilers," ndi mawu ofuula okongola. N'chifukwa chiyani onse Oyenda panyanja, komanso amuna onse a Pirates ndi Man-of-War, ndi oyendetsa sitima ya Akapolo, amayamikira kunyozedwa koteroko kwa zombo za Whale; ili ndi funso lomwe lingakhale lovuta kuliyankha. Chifukwa, ponena za achifwamba, titi, ine ndikanafuna kudziwa ngati ntchito yawo imeneyo ili ndi ulemerero wachilendo pa izo. Nthawi zina zimathera mu kukwera kwachilendo, ndithudi; koma pamtengo wokhawokha. Komanso, pamene munthu akwezedwa m’njira yodabwitsayo, alibe maziko oyenerera a utali wake. Chifukwa chake, ndikumaliza, kuti podzitamandira kuti adakwezedwa pamwamba pa namgumi, m'mawu ake kuti wachifwamba alibe maziko olimba oti aimepo.

Koma Gam ndi chiyani? Mutha kuwononga chala chanu cholozera m'mwamba ndikutsika m'mawu otanthauzira mawu, osapeza mawuwo. Dr. Johnson sanafikire konse ku chidziwitso chimenecho; Chombo cha Nowa Webster sichichigwira. Komabe, liwu lofotokozera lomweli tsopano kwa zaka zambiri lakhala likugwiritsidwa ntchito mosalekeza pakati pa ma Yankee obadwa enieni okwana zikwi khumi ndi zisanu. Zachidziwikire, zimafunikira tanthauzo, ndipo ziyenera kuphatikizidwa mu Lexicon. Ndi lingaliro limenelo, ndiloleni ndifotokoze mophunzira.

( Monga yalongosoledwa ku Golden Inn. )
