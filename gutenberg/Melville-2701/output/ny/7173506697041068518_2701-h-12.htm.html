MUTU 55. Za Zithunzi Zowopsya Za Zinsomba.

Ndidzakupatsirani utoto wautali komanso momwe mungapangire popanda chinsalu, chofanana ndi mawonekedwe enieni a chinsomba momwe amawonekera m'diso la whaleman pomwe ali m'thupi lake lathunthu chinsombacho chimakhazikika pambali pa ngalawa ya whale kuti. iye akhoza kupondapo ndithu. Zingakhale zopindulitsa, chifukwa chake, m'mbuyomu kulengeza kwa zithunzi zongopeka za iye zomwe ngakhale mpaka lero molimba mtima zimatsutsa chikhulupiriro cha mwini malo. Yakwana nthawi yokonza dziko pankhaniyi, mwa kutsimikizira zithunzi zotere za nangumi kuti zonse ndi zolakwika.

N’kutheka kuti magwero aakulu a zithunzithunzi zonsezo adzapezeka pakati pa ziboliboli zakale kwambiri za Ahindoo, Aigupto, ndi Agiriki. Kuyambira nthawi zomwe zidapangidwa koma zosalongosoka, pomwe pamiyala ya akachisi a miyala ya marble, mizati ya ziboliboli, ndi zishango, ma medallions, makapu, ndi ndalama, dolphin adakokedwa mumiyeso ya zida zankhondo ngati za Saladin, ndi mutu wokhala ndi chisoti ngati cha Saladin. St. George's; kuyambira pamenepo pali china chamtundu womwewo wa chiphaso chomwe chidakula, osati m'zithunzi zodziwika bwino za namgumi, komanso m'mawonedwe ambiri asayansi a iye.

Tsopano, mosakayikira, chithunzi chakale kwambiri chomwe chimadziwika kuti ndi namgumi, chikupezeka ku cavern-pagoda yotchuka ya Elephanta, ku India. A Brahmins amanena kuti m’zosema pafupifupi zosatha za pagoda wakale uja, malonda onse ndi zofunafuna, chilichonse chimene munthu angachiganizire, chinali mibadwo yofaniziridwa kale iriyonse ya izo isanakhaleko. Ndiye nzosadabwitsa kuti mwa mtundu wina ntchito yathu yolemekezeka yoweta anamgumi inayenera kukhala itaphimbidwa. Mbalame yotchedwa Hindoo whale imapezeka mu dipatimenti ina ya khoma, kusonyeza kubadwa kwa Vishnu mu mawonekedwe a leviathan, omwe amadziwika kuti Matse Avatar. Koma ngakhale chosema ichi ndi theka munthu ndi theka chinsomba, kotero kuti kungopereka mchira wa yotsirizira, komabe kachigawo kakang'ono ka iye zonse ndi zolakwika. Imafanana kwambiri ndi mchira wa anaconda, kusiyana ndi zikhatho zazikulu za nangumi woona.

Koma pitani ku Magalasi akale, ndipo yang'anani tsopano pa chithunzi cha mmisiri wamkulu wa Chikhristu cha nsomba iyi; chifukwa sachita bwino kuposa Hindoo ya chigumula. Ndi chithunzi cha Guido cha Perseus akupulumutsa Andromeda ku chilombo cha m'nyanja kapena chinsomba. Guido anachitenga kuti chitsanzo cha cholengedwa chodabwitsa ngati chimenecho? Ndiponso Hogarth, pojambula chithunzi chofananacho mu “Perseus Descending” yakeyake, sapanga kumveka bwino kumodzi. Kuchuluka kwamphamvu kwa chilombo cha Hogarthian kumadzitukumula pamwamba, sikumakoka inchi imodzi yamadzi. Kumsana kwake kuli ndi ng'anjo yochititsa kaso, ndipo kukamwa kwake kopindika kumene mafunde akugudubuzika, akhoza kupita ku Chipata cha Oukira chochokera ku mtsinje wa Thames pamadzi kupita ku Nsanja. Ndiyeno, pali anamgumi a Prodromus of the Old Scotch Sibbald, ndi whale wa Yona, monga momwe amasonyezedwera m’zosindikizira za Mabaibulo akale ndi mabala a zoyamba zakale. Adzanene chiyani za izi? Ponena za chinsomba chomangirira mabuku chomazungulira ngati phesi la mpesa kuzungulira tsinde la nangula wotsikirapo—monga chodindidwa ndi chokongoletsedwa pamsana ndi masamba amutu a mabuku ambiri akale ndi atsopano—chimenecho ndi chokongola kwambiri koma chochititsa chidwi kwambiri, kutsanzira, ndikutengera, kuchokera ku ziwerengero zofananira pamiphika yamakedzana. Ngakhale kuti padziko lonse lapansi pali dolphin, komabe ndimatcha nsomba ya binder's iyi kuyesa nangumi; chifukwa chidali cholinga pomwe chipangizocho chidayambitsidwa koyamba. Inayambitsidwa ndi wofalitsa wakale wa ku Italy kwinakwake pafupi ndi zaka za zana la 15, pa Chitsitsimutso cha Kuphunzira; ndipo m’masiku amenewo, ndipo ngakhale mpaka kunthaŵi yakumapeto, ma dolphin anali ofala kuyenera kukhala mtundu wa Leviathan.

Mu vignettes ndi zokometsera zina za mabuku ena akale nthawi zina mumakumana ndi chidwi chokhudza namgumi, kumene mitundu yonse ya spouts, jets d'eau, akasupe otentha ndi ozizira, Saratoga ndi Baden-Baden, amabwera akuphulika ubongo wosatopa. Patsamba lamutu la buku loyambirira la “Kupita patsogolo kwa Maphunziro” mudzapeza anamgumi ena ochita chidwi.

Koma kusiya kuyesayesa konse kopanda ukatswiri kumeneku, tiyeni tiyang'ane pa zithunzi za Leviathan zomwe zimanenedwa kukhala zolongosoka, zofotokozera zasayansi, ndi iwo omwe akudziwa. M’maulendo apanyanja a Harris wakale muli mbale zina za anamgumi zotengedwa m’buku lachidatchi la maulendo, AD 1671, lamutu wakuti “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson wa Friesland, master.” Mu imodzi mwa mbale zimenezo anamgumi, mofanana ndi mitengo ikuluikulu ya mitengo, akuimiridwa ali m’zisumbu za ayezi, ndi zimbalangondo zoyera zikuthamanga pamisana yawo yamoyo. Mu mbale ina, kulakwitsa kwakukulu kumapangidwa ndikuyimira chinsomba chokhala ndi perpendicular flukes.

Ndiponso, pali quarto yochititsa chidwi, yolembedwa ndi Captain Colnett, Post Captain m’gulu lankhondo la pamadzi lachingelezi, lamutu wakuti “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, kaamba ka chifuno chofutukula Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” M'bukuli muli autilaini yoti ndi "Chithunzi cha Physeter kapena Spermaceti whale, chojambulidwa ndi sikelo kuchokera kwa munthu yemwe adaphedwa pagombe la Mexico, Ogasiti, 1793, ndikukwezedwa pamtunda." Sindikukayika kuti woyendetsa ndegeyo adajambula chithunzi chowona ichi kuti apindule ndi asitikali ake. Kuti nditchule chinthu chimodzi chokha, ndiloleni ndinene kuti ili ndi diso lomwe, malinga ndi sikelo yomwe ili m'munsiyi, limapanga diso la namgumiyo kukhala windo la uta wotalika mamita asanu. Ah, kapitao wanga wolimba mtima, bwanji sunatipatse Yona akuyang'ana kunja kwa diso lake!

Ngakhalenso zophatikiza mosamala kwambiri za Natural History kuti zithandizire achichepere ndi achifundo, zopanda kulakwitsa komweko. Yang'anani buku lodziwika bwino la "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." M’kope lachidule la London la 1807, muli mbale za “nangumi” amene amati ndi “nangumi” ndi “narwhale.” Sindikufuna kuoneka wopusa, koma chinsomba chosawoneka bwinochi chimawoneka ngati nkhumba yodulidwa; ndipo, ponena za narwhale, kungoyang’ana pang’ono pa izo nkokwanira kudabwitsa munthu, kuti m’zaka za zana lakhumi ndi chisanu ndi chinayi mvuu woteroyo angakhoze kuchitidwa pamanja kaamba ka gulu lirilonse laluntha la anyamata asukulu.

Kenaka, kachiwiri, mu 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, katswiri wamkulu wa zachilengedwe, adafalitsa bukhu la sayansi la whale, momwe muli zithunzi zingapo za mitundu yosiyanasiyana ya Leviathan. Zonsezi sizolakwika, koma chithunzi cha Mysticetus kapena Greenland whale (ndiko kuti, Whale Whale), ngakhale Scoresby, munthu wodziwa zambiri pokhudza zamoyo zomwezo, amalengeza kuti alibe mnzake m'chilengedwe.

Koma kuyika kapu-mtolo ku bizinesi yonse yosokonezayi kunasungidwa kwa sayansi Frederick Cuvier, m'bale wa Baron wotchuka. Mu 1836, adafalitsa Natural History of Whales , momwe amapereka zomwe amachitcha chithunzi cha Umuna Whale. Musanawonetse chithunzichi kwa Nantucketer aliyense, mudapereka bwino zachidule chanu chochokera ku Nantucket. Mwachidule, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale si Umuna Whale, koma sikwashi. Zoonadi, iye sanakhalepo ndi phindu la ulendo wa namgumi (amuna oterowo sakhala nawo kawirikawiri), koma pamene anachitengera chithunzicho, ndani angadziwe? Mwinamwake iye anazipeza izo monga wotsogolera wake wasayansi mu gawo lomwelo, Desmarest, anatenga imodzi ya kuchotsa mimba kwake koona; ndiko kuti, kuchokera ku chojambula cha ku China. Ndipo ma Chinese amenewo ndi anyamata okondwa otani omwe ali ndi pensulo, makapu ang'onoang'ono ndi mbale zambiri zimatiuza.

Ponena za anamgumi a ojambula zikwangwani owonedwa m’makwalala akulendewera m’mashopu a ogulitsa mafuta, tidzanene chiyani ponena za iwo? Nthawi zambiri amakhala Richard III. nsomba zam'madzi, zokhala ndi nyundo za dromedary, ndi zonyansa kwambiri; kudya chakudya cham'mawa pa ma tarts atatu kapena anayi amalinyero, ndiwo mabwato a anamgumi odzaza ndi amalinyero: kupunduka kwawo kumayandama m'nyanja yamagazi ndi utoto wabuluu.

Koma zophophonya zochulukirachulukirazi posonyeza namgumi si zodabwitsa kwenikweni. Talingalirani! Zambiri mwazojambula zasayansi zatengedwa kuchokera ku nsomba zomangika; ndipo izi ziri zolondola monga momwe kujambula kwa chombo chosweka, chothyoka msana, kungaimire molondola nyama yaulemuyo mu kunyada kwake kopanda kuphwanyidwa kwa ziboliboli. Ngakhale njovu zimayimira utali wawo wonse, Leviathan wamoyo sanadziyandamebe bwino chifukwa cha chithunzi chake. Nangumi wamoyo, mu ukulu wake wonse ndi kufunikira kwake, amangowoneka panyanja m'madzi osawerengeka; ndipo unyinji wake woyandama suoneka, ngati ngalawa yankhondo yonyamuka; ndipo kuchokera mu chinthu chimenecho ndi chinthu chosatheka kwamuyaya kwa munthu kuti amunyamule ndi thupi lake mlengalenga, kuti asunge zotupa zake zonse zamphamvu ndi zopinga. Ndipo, osanenapo za kusiyana koyerekezeka kwa mizere pakati pa namgumi wachichepere woyamwa ndi Leviathan wa Platoni; komabe, ngakhale m’nkhani ya mmodzi wa anangumi achichepere akuyamwa atakwezedwa ku sitima ya sitimayo, woteroyo ali wodabwitsa, wonga nsonga, wamiyendo, wosiyana siyana, kotero kuti mawu ake enieni sakanatha kuwagwira.

Koma zikhoza kuganiziridwa, kuti kuchokera ku mafupa amaliseche a whale wosokonekera, malingaliro olondola angatengedwe okhudza mawonekedwe ake enieni. Ayi konse. Chifukwa ndi chimodzi mwazinthu zochititsa chidwi kwambiri za Leviathan uyu, kuti mafupa ake amapereka lingaliro lochepa kwambiri la mawonekedwe ake. Ngakhale mafupa a Jeremy Bentham, omwe amapachikidwa pa candelabra mu laibulale ya m'modzi wa omwe adamupha, amapereka molondola lingaliro la njonda yachikulire yowoneka bwino, yokhala ndi mikhalidwe ina yonse ya Jeremy; Komabe palibe chilichonse chamtundu uwu chomwe chikanakhoza kudziwika kuchokera ku mafupa omveka a leviatani. M'malo mwake, monga momwe Hunter wamkulu amanenera, chigoba chokha cha namgumi chimakhala ndi ubale womwewo ndi nyama yomwe ili ndi mphamvu zambiri komanso yokhazikika monga momwe tizilombo timachitira ndi chrysalis zomwe zimamuzungulira mozungulira. Kusiyanitsa kumeneku kukuwonekera mochititsa chidwi m'mutu, monga momwe gawo lina la bukhuli lidzasonyezedwera mwangozi. Imawonetsedwanso modabwitsa kwambiri pamapiko am'mbali, mafupa omwe pafupifupi ndendende amayankha mafupa a dzanja la munthu, kupatula chala chachikulu. Chipsepsechi chimakhala ndi zala zinayi zokhazikika za mafupa, cholozera, chapakati, mphete, ndi chala chaching'ono. Koma zonsezi zimabindikiritsidwa m’chophimba chawo cha mnofu, monga zala za munthu m’chophimba chochita kupanga. “Komabe mosasamala kanthu za namgumi nthaŵi zina angatitumikire,” anatero Stubb wanthabwala tsiku lina, “sanganenedwe kuti amatigwira popanda nsabwe.”

Pazifukwa zonsezi, ndiye, mwanjira iriyonse yomwe mungawonere, muyenera kuganiza kuti Leviathan wamkulu ndi cholengedwa chimodzi padziko lapansi chomwe chiyenera kukhala chosapenta mpaka kumapeto. Zowona, chithunzi chimodzi chikhoza kufika pachimake pafupi kwambiri kuposa china, koma palibe chomwe chingachimveke molondola kwambiri. Chotero palibe njira yapadziko lapansi yodziŵira ndendende mmene namgumi amawonekera. Ndipo njira yokhayo yomwe mungapezere ngakhale lingaliro lolekerera la mdulidwe wake wamoyo, ndi kupita kukawedza wekha; koma potero, mumakhala pachiwopsezo chachikulu chokhala ndi chitofu chamuyaya ndikumizidwa ndi iye. Chifukwa chake, zikuwoneka kwa ine kuti simunachite changu kwambiri pakufuna kwanu kukhudza Leviathan uyu.





MUTU 56. Pa Zithunzi Zochepa Zolakwika za Zinsomba, ndi Zithunzi Zowona za Zithunzi za Whaling.

Mogwirizana ndi zithunzi zoopsa kwambiri za anamgumi, ndikuyesedwa kwambiri pano kuti ndilowe mu nkhani zowopsa kwambiri za izo zomwe zimapezeka m'mabuku ena, akale ndi amakono, makamaka Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, etc. Koma ndimadutsa nkhaniyo.

Ndikudziwa zolemba zinayi zokha zosindikizidwa za Nangumi wamkulu wa Umuna; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, ndi Beale's. M'mutu wapitawu Colnett ndi Cuvier adatchulidwa. Huggins ndi wabwino kwambiri kuposa wawo; koma, mwazovuta kwambiri, Beale ndi yabwino kwambiri. Zojambula zonse za Beale za chinsomba ichi ndi zabwino, kupatulapo chithunzi chapakati pa chithunzi cha zinsomba zitatu mumaganizo osiyanasiyana, ndikujambula mutu wake wachiwiri. Malo ake akutsogolo, mabwato akuukira Sperm Whales, ngakhale mosakayika amawerengera kuti asangalatse kukayikira kwa amuna ena a m'nyumba, ndi zolondola modabwitsa komanso ngati moyo wake wonse. Zina mwa zojambula za Sperm Whale mu J. Ross Browne ndizolondola kwambiri mu contour; koma Alembedwa moipa. Ilo siliri vuto lake.

Za Nangumi Wabwino, zithunzi za autilaini zabwino kwambiri zili mu Scoresby; koma amakokedwa pamlingo waung’ono kwambiri kuti apereke lingaliro lofunika. Iye ali ndi chithunzi chimodzi chokha cha zochitika za whale, ndipo ichi ndi kusowa komvetsa chisoni, chifukwa ndi zithunzi zoterozo zokha, pamene mwachita bwino, kuti mutha kupeza chirichonse chonga lingaliro loona la chinsomba chamoyo monga momwe alenje ake amoyo amawonera.

Koma, zotengedwa kwa onse muzonse, zabwino kwambiri, ngakhale kuti mwatsatanetsatane sizinali zolondola kwambiri, ziwonetsero za anamgumi ndi ziwonetsero za namgumi zomwe zingapezeke kulikonse, ndizozokota ziwiri zazikulu za Chifalansa, zojambulidwa bwino, zotengedwa kuchokera ku zojambula za Garnery imodzi. Motsatira, amaimira kuukira kwa Umuna ndi Right Whale. Pazojambula koyamba, chinsomba chodziwika bwino cha Sperm Whale chikuwonetsedwa mu ukulu wa mphamvu zonse, chongonyamuka pansi pa ngalawayo kuchokera mkatikati mwa nyanja, ndikunyamula mlengalenga pamsana pake kuwonongeka kowopsa kwa matabwa. Kutsogolo kwa ngalawayo ndi yosasweka pang'ono, ndipo amakokedwa ndi msana wa chilombocho; ndi kuyimirira kutsogolo kumeneko, chifukwa cha kung'anima kumodzi kosawerengeka kwa nthawi imeneyo, mukuwona munthu wopalasa, ataphimbidwa ndi nsonga yotentha ya chinsombacho, ndipo akudumphadumpha, ngati kuti akuchokera pamtunda. Zochita za chinthu chonsecho ndizabwino modabwitsa komanso zowona. Bafa la mzere wopanda kanthu limayandama panyanja yoyera; mizati ya matabwa a nsonga zotayikira imagwera m'menemo; mitu ya osambira inabalalika pa namgumi mosiyanasiyana mochititsa mantha; pamene patali namondwe wakuda chombocho chikutsika powonekera. Kulakwitsa kwakukulu kungapezeke ndi tsatanetsatane wa anatomical wa nsomba iyi, koma izi zichitike; popeza, kwa moyo wanga, sindingathe kujambula yabwino kwambiri.

Pazozokota zachiwiri, bwatoli likujambula m'mbali mwa nthiti yayikulu ya Right Whale, yomwe imagudubuza udzu wake wakuda m'nyanja ngati mwala wa mossy kuchokera kumapiri a Patagonian. Majeti ake ndi oongoka, odzaza, ndi akuda ngati mwaye; kotero kuti chifukwa cha kuchuluka kwa utsi mu chimney, mungaganize kuti payenera kukhala kuphika mgonero wolimba mtima m'matumbo akulu pansipa. Mbalame zam'nyanja zikuyang'ana nkhanu, nsomba zam'madzi, ndi masiwiti ena am'nyanja ndi makaroni, zomwe Nangumi Wam'madzi nthawi zina amanyamula pamsana wake wankhanza. Ndipo nthawi yonseyi, leviathan wamilomo yokhuthala akuthamanga mozama, akusiya matani amtundu woyera wonyezimira, ndikupangitsa bwato laling'ono kuti ligwedezeke m'mabwalo ngati skiff wogwidwa pafupi ndi mawilo opalasa a sitima yapanyanja. Motero, kutsogolo kuli chipwirikiti chonse; koma kumbuyo, mosiyana kwambiri ndi luso laluso, pali mulingo wagalasi wa nyanja yokhazikika, matanga osasunthika osasunthika a sitima yapamadzi yopanda mphamvu, komanso kuchuluka kwa chinsomba chakufa, linga logonjetsedwa, ndi mbendera yogwidwa mwaulesi ikulendewera ku whale- mtengo wolowetsedwa mu dzenje lake.

Kodi Ganery wojambulayo ndi ndani, kapena anali, sindikudziwa. Koma moyo wanga chifukwa cha izi mwina anali wodziwa bwino za phunziro lake, kapena wophunzitsidwa modabwitsa ndi whaleman wina wodziwa zambiri. A French ndi anyamata ochita kujambula. Pitani mukayang'ane zojambula zonse za ku Ulaya, ndipo mudzapeza kuti malo owonetserako zamoyo ndi kupuma pansalu, monga muholo yopambana ija ku Versailles; kumene wopenya akumenyana njira yake, pell-mell, kupyolera mu nkhondo zazikulu zotsatizana za France; kumene lupanga lililonse limawoneka ngati kuwala kwa Kuwala kwa Kumpoto, ndipo mafumu otsatizana okhala ndi zida ndi mafumu akudutsa, ngati chiwongolero cha centaurs ovekedwa korona? Osati osayenera konse kukhala ndi malo mu nyumbayi, ndi zigawo zankhondo zapanyanja za Garnery.

Kuthekera kwachilengedwe kwa Afalansa kutengera kukongola kwa zinthu kukuwoneka kuti kumawonekera mwapadera ndi zojambula ndi zojambula zomwe ali nazo pazithunzi zawo zakusokonekera. Pokhala opanda gawo limodzi mwa magawo khumi la zochitika za ku England pa usodzi, ndipo osati gawo limodzi mwa magawo 1,000 a Amereka, iwo aperekabe maiko onse aŵiri zithunzi zomalizidwa zotha kusonyeza mzimu weniweni wa kusaka anamgumi. Kwa mbali zambiri, English ndi American whale Draughters akuwoneka kuti ali okhutira kwathunthu ndi kupereka ndondomeko ya makina a zinthu, monga mbiri yopanda kanthu ya whale; zomwe, potengera kukongola kwake, zili ngati kujambula mbiri ya piramidi. Ngakhale Scoresby, munthu wodziwika bwino wa Right whaleman, atatipatsa utali wouma wa nangumi wa Greenland, ndi tinthu tating'ono ting'onoting'ono titatu kapena tinayi ta narwhales ndi porpoises, amatichitira ku mndandanda wa zolemba zakale za mbedza za ngalawa, mipeni yodula, ndi ma grapnel; ndi khama laling'ono la Leuwenhoeck amagonjera kuyendera dziko lonjenjemera makumi asanu ndi anayi mphambu asanu ndi limodzi azithunzithunzi zazikuluzikulu za chipale chofewa cha Arctic. Sindikutanthauza kunyozetsa woyenda bwino kwambiri (Ndimamulemekeza chifukwa cha msilikali wakale), koma pa nkhani yofunika kwambiri kunali kuyang'anira kuti tisatengere kristalo aliyense lumbiro lolumbirira loperekedwa pamaso pa Greenland Justice of the Peace.

Kuonjezela pa zozokota zabwinozo za ku Garnery, pali zozokota zina ziŵiri za Chifalansa zoyenera kuzindikila, zolembedwa ndi wina amene amadzilembetsa kuti “H. Durand.” Chimodzi mwa izo, ngakhale kuti sichinagwirizane ndendende ndi cholinga chathu chamakono, komabe chiyenera kutchulidwa m'nkhani zina. Ndi malo abata masana pakati pa zisumbu za Pacific; whale wa ku France anazika, kumtunda, modekha, ndi kutengera madzi m'bwalo mwaulesi; matanga omasuka a m’ngalawamo, ndi masamba aatali a kanjedza m’mbuyo, onse akutsamira pamodzi mumpweya wopanda mphepo. Zotsatira zake ndi zabwino kwambiri, zikaganiziridwa mokhudzana ndi kupereka kwa asodzi olimba pansi pa chimodzi mwazochepa zawo zakupuma zakum'mawa. Chojambula china ndi chosiyana kwambiri: chombocho chimayenda panyanja, ndipo mkati mwa moyo wa Leviatanic, ndi Whale Whale pambali; chombo (podula-mu) yendani ku chilombocho ngati kuti chapita ku quay; ndipo bwato, likukankha mwachangu kuchoka pa chochitika ichi, lili pafupi kuthamangitsa anamgumi patali. Mikondo ndi mikondo yagona kuti igwiritsidwe ntchito; opalasa atatu akungoika mlongoti m’dzenje lake; Pamene chombo chaching'onocho chinagwedezeka mwadzidzidzi, chinaima chilili m'madzi, ngati kavalo woweta. CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
