MUTU 55. Za Zithunzi Zowopsa za Zinsomba.

Ndidzakupatsirani utoto wautali komanso momwe munthu angathere popanda chinsalu, chinachake chonga mawonekedwe enieni a whaleman pamene akuwonekera pa diso la whaleman pamene mumtheradi wa thupi lake nsombayi imayikidwa pambali pa sitima ya whale kuti athe kupondapo. Zingakhale zopindulitsa, chifukwa chake, m'mbuyomu kulengeza kwa zithunzi zongopeka za iye zomwe ngakhale mpaka lero molimba mtima zimatsutsa chikhulupiriro cha mwini malo. Yakwana nthawi yokonza dziko pankhaniyi, mwa kutsimikizira zithunzi zotere za nangumi kuti zonse ndi zolakwika.

N’kutheka kuti magwero aakulu a zithunzithunzi zonsezo adzapezeka pakati pa ziboliboli zakale kwambiri za Ahindoo, Aigupto, ndi Agiriki. Chiyambireni nthawi zoyambilira koma zosalongosoka pamene pamiyala ya akachisi a nsangalabwi, mizati ya ziboliboli, ndi pa zishango, ma medallions, makapu, ndi ndalama zachitsulo, dolphin anakokedwa m’mamba a zida za unyolo monga za Saladin, ndi mutu wa zisoti monga St. George’s; kuyambira pamenepo pali china chamtundu womwewo wa chiphaso chomwe chidakula, osati m'zithunzi zodziwika bwino za namgumi, komanso m'mawonedwe ambiri asayansi a iye.

Tsopano, mosakayikira, chithunzi chakale kwambiri chomwe chimadziwika kuti ndi namgumi, chikupezeka ku cavern-pagoda yotchuka ya Elephanta, ku India. A Brahmins amanena kuti m’zosema pafupifupi zosatha za pagoda wakale uja, malonda onse ndi zofunafuna, chilichonse chimene munthu angachiganizire, chinali mibadwo yofaniziridwa kale iriyonse ya izo isanakhaleko. Ndiye nzosadabwitsa kuti mwa mtundu wina ntchito yathu yolemekezeka yoweta anamgumi inayenera kukhala itaphimbidwa. Mbalame yotchedwa Hindoo whale imapezeka mu dipatimenti ina ya khoma, kusonyeza kubadwa kwa Vishnu mu mawonekedwe a leviathan, omwe amadziwika kuti Matse Avatar. Koma ngakhale chosema ichi ndi theka munthu ndi theka chinsomba, kotero kuti kungopereka mchira wa yotsirizira, komabe kachigawo kakang'ono ka iye zonse ndi zolakwika. Imafanana kwambiri ndi mchira wa anaconda, kusiyana ndi zikhatho zazikulu za nangumi woona.

Koma pitani ku Magalasi akale, ndipo yang'anani tsopano pa chithunzi cha mmisiri wamkulu wa Chikhristu cha nsomba iyi; chifukwa sachita bwino kuposa Hindoo ya chigumula. Ndi chithunzi cha Guido cha Perseus akupulumutsa Andromeda ku chilombo cha m'nyanja kapena chinsomba. Guido anachitenga kuti chitsanzo cha cholengedwa chodabwitsa ngati chimenecho? Ndiponso Hogarth, pojambula chithunzi chofananacho mu “Perseus Descending” yakeyake, sapanga kumveka bwino kumodzi. Kuchuluka kwamphamvu kwa chilombo cha Hogarthian kumadzitukumula pamwamba, sikumakoka inchi imodzi yamadzi. Kumsana kwake kuli ndi ng'anjo yochititsa kaso, ndipo kukamwa kwake kopindika kumene mafunde akugudubuzika, akhoza kupita ku Chipata cha Oukira chochokera ku mtsinje wa Thames pamadzi kupita ku Nsanja. Ndiyeno, pali anamgumi a Prodromus of the Old Scotch Sibbald, ndi whale wa Yona, monga momwe amasonyezedwera m’zosindikizira za Mabaibulo akale ndi mabala a zoyamba zakale. Adzanene chiyani za izi? Ponena za chinsomba chomangirira mabuku chomwe chimapindika ngati phesi la mpesa kuzungulira tsinde la nangula wotsikirapo—monga chodindidwa ndi chokongoletsedwa pamsana ndi masamba amutu a mabuku ambiri akale ndi atsopano—chimenecho ndi chokongola kwambiri koma chokongola kwambiri, chotsanzira, ndikuchitengera, kuchokera ku ziwerengero zonga za miphika yakale. Ngakhale kuti padziko lonse lapansi pali dolphin, komabe ndimatcha nsomba ya binder's iyi kuyesa nangumi; chifukwa chidali cholinga pomwe chipangizocho chidayambitsidwa koyamba. Inayambitsidwa ndi wofalitsa wakale wa ku Italy kwinakwake pafupi ndi zaka za zana la 15, pa Chitsitsimutso cha Kuphunzira; ndipo m’masiku amenewo, ndipo ngakhale mpaka kunthaŵi yakumapeto, ma dolphin anali ofala kuyenera kukhala mtundu wa Leviathan.

Mu vignettes ndi zokometsera zina za mabuku ena akale nthawi zina mumakumana ndi chidwi chokhudza chinsomba, kumene mitundu yonse ya spouts, jets d'eau, akasupe otentha ndi ozizira, Saratoga ndi Baden-Baden, amabwera akuphulika kuchokera ku ubongo wake wosatopa. Patsamba lamutu la buku loyambirira la “Kupita patsogolo kwa Maphunziro” mudzapeza anamgumi ena ochita chidwi.

Koma kusiya kuyesayesa konse kopanda ukatswiri kumeneku, tiyeni tiyang'ane pa zithunzi za Leviathan zomwe zimanenedwa kukhala zolongosoka, zofotokozera zasayansi, ndi iwo omwe akudziwa. M’maulendo apanyanja a Harris wakale muli mbale zina za anamgumi zotengedwa m’buku lachidatchi la maulendo, AD 1671, lamutu wakuti “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson wa Friesland, master.” Mu imodzi mwa mbale zimenezo anamgumi, mofanana ndi mitengo ikuluikulu ya mitengo, akuimiridwa ali m’zisumbu za ayezi, ndi zimbalangondo zoyera zikuthamanga pamisana yawo yamoyo. Mu mbale ina, kulakwitsa kwakukulu kumapangidwa ndikuyimira chinsomba chokhala ndi perpendicular flukes.

Ndiponso, pali quarto yochititsa chidwi, yolembedwa ndi Captain Colnett, Post Captain m’gulu lankhondo la pamadzi lachingelezi, lamutu wakuti “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, kaamba ka chifuno chofutukula Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” M'bukuli muli autilaini yoti ndi "Chithunzi cha Physeter kapena Spermaceti whale, chojambulidwa pamlingo wa munthu yemwe adaphedwa pagombe la Mexico, Ogasiti, 1793, ndikukwezedwa pamtunda." Sindikukayika kuti woyendetsa ndegeyo adajambula chithunzi chowona ichi kuti apindule ndi asitikali ake. Kuti nditchule chinthu chimodzi chokha, ndiloleni ndinene kuti ili ndi diso lomwe, malinga ndi sikelo yomwe ili m'munsiyi, limapanga diso la namgumiyo kukhala mawindo a uta wotalika mamita asanu. Ah, kapitao wanga wolimba mtima, bwanji sunatipatse Yona akuyang'ana kunja kwa diso lake!

Ngakhalenso zophatikiza mosamala kwambiri za Natural History kuti zithandizire achichepere ndi achifundo, zopanda kulakwitsa komweko. Yang'anani buku lodziwika bwino la "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." M’kope lachidule la London la 1807, muli mbale za “nangumi” amene amati ndi “nangumi” ndi “narwhale.” Sindikufuna kuoneka wopusa, koma chinsomba chosawoneka bwinochi chimawoneka ngati nkhumba yodulidwa; ndipo, ponena za narwhale, kungoyang’ana pang’ono pa izo nkokwanira kudabwitsa munthu, kuti m’zaka za zana lakhumi ndi chisanu ndi chinayi mvuu woteroyo angakhoze kuchitidwa pamanja kaamba ka gulu lirilonse laluntha la anyamata asukulu.

Kenaka, kachiwiri, mu 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, katswiri wamkulu wa zachilengedwe, adafalitsa bukhu la sayansi la whale, momwe muli zithunzi zingapo za mitundu yosiyanasiyana ya Leviathan. Zonsezi sizolakwika, koma chithunzi cha Mysticetus kapena Greenland whale (ndiko kuti, Whale Whale), ngakhale Scoresby, munthu wodziwa zambiri pokhudza zamoyo zomwezo, akulengeza kuti alibe mnzake m'chilengedwe.

Koma kuyika kapu-mtolo ku bizinesi yonse yosokonezayi kunasungidwa kwa sayansi Frederick Cuvier, m'bale wa Baron wotchuka. Mu 1836, adafalitsa Natural History of Whales , momwe amapereka zomwe amachitcha chithunzi cha Umuna Whale. Musanawonetse chithunzichi kwa Nantucketer aliyense, mudapereka bwino zachidule chanu chochokera ku Nantucket. Mwachidule, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale si Umuna Whale, koma sikwashi. Zoonadi, iye sanakhalepo ndi phindu la ulendo wa namgumi (amuna oterowo sakhala nawo kawirikawiri), koma pamene anachitengera chithunzicho, ndani angadziwe? Mwinamwake iye anazipeza izo monga wotsogolera wake wasayansi mu gawo lomwelo, Desmarest, anatenga imodzi ya kuchotsa mimba kwake koona; ndiko kuti, kuchokera ku chojambula cha ku China. Ndipo ma Chinese amenewo ndi anyamata okondwa otani omwe ali ndi pensulo, makapu ang'onoang'ono ndi mbale zambiri zimatiuza.

Ponena za anamgumi a ojambula zikwangwani owonedwa m’makwalala akulendewera m’mashopu a ogulitsa mafuta, tidzanene chiyani ponena za iwo? Nthawi zambiri amakhala Richard III. nsomba zam'madzi, zokhala ndi nyundo za dromedary, ndi zonyansa kwambiri; kudya chakudya cham'mawa pa ma tarts atatu kapena anayi amalinyero, ndiwo mabwato a anamgumi odzaza ndi amalinyero: zopunduka zawo zikuyandama m'nyanja zamagazi ndi utoto wabuluu.

Koma zophophonya zochulukirachulukirazi posonyeza namgumi si zodabwitsa kwenikweni. Talingalirani! Zambiri mwazojambula zasayansi zatengedwa kuchokera ku nsomba zomangika; ndipo izi ziri zolondola monga momwe kujambula kwa chombo chosweka, chothyoka msana, kungaimire molondola nyama yaulemuyo mu kunyada kwake kopanda kuphwanyidwa kwa ziboliboli. Ngakhale njovu zimayimira utali wawo wonse, Leviathan wamoyo sanadziyandamebe bwino chifukwa cha chithunzi chake. Nangumi wamoyo, mu ukulu wake wonse ndi kufunikira kwake, amangowoneka panyanja m'madzi osawerengeka; ndipo unyinji wake woyandama suoneka, ngati ngalawa yankhondo yonyamuka; ndipo kuchokera mu chinthu chimenecho ndi chinthu chosatheka kwamuyaya kwa munthu kuti amunyamule ndi thupi lake mlengalenga, kuti asunge zotupa zake zonse zamphamvu ndi zopinga. Ndipo, osanenapo za kusiyana koyerekezeka kwa mizere pakati pa namgumi wachichepere woyamwa ndi Leviathan wa Platoni; komabe, ngakhale m’nkhani ya mmodzi wa anangumi achichepere akuyamwa atakwezedwa ku sitima ya sitimayo, woteroyo ali wodabwitsa, wonga nsonga, wamiyendo, wosiyana siyana, kotero kuti mawu ake enieni sakanatha kuwagwira.

Koma zikhoza kuganiziridwa, kuti kuchokera ku mafupa amaliseche a whale wosokonekera, malingaliro olondola angatengedwe okhudza mawonekedwe ake enieni. Ayi konse. Chifukwa ndi chimodzi mwazinthu zochititsa chidwi kwambiri za Leviathan uyu, kuti mafupa ake amapereka lingaliro lochepa kwambiri la mawonekedwe ake. Ngakhale mafupa a Jeremy Bentham, omwe amapachikidwa pa candelabra mu laibulale ya m'modzi wa omwe adamupha, amapereka molondola lingaliro la njonda yachikulire yowoneka bwino, yokhala ndi mikhalidwe ina yonse ya Jeremy; Komabe palibe chilichonse chamtundu uwu chomwe chikanakhoza kudziwika kuchokera ku mafupa omveka a leviatani. M'malo mwake, monga momwe Hunter wamkulu amanenera, chigoba chokha cha namgumi chimakhala ndi ubale womwewo ndi nyama yomwe ili ndi mphamvu zambiri komanso yokhazikika monga momwe tizilombo timachitira ndi chrysalis zomwe zimamuzungulira mozungulira. Kusiyanitsa kumeneku kukuwonekera mochititsa chidwi m'mutu, monga momwe gawo lina la bukhuli lidzasonyezedwera mwangozi. Imawonetsedwanso modabwitsa kwambiri pamapiko am'mbali, mafupa omwe pafupifupi ndendende amayankha mafupa a dzanja la munthu, kupatula chala chachikulu. Chipsepsechi chimakhala ndi zala zinayi zokhazikika za mafupa, cholozera, chapakati, mphete, ndi chala chaching'ono. Koma zonsezi zimabindikiritsidwa m’chophimba chawo cha mnofu, monga zala za munthu m’chophimba chochita kupanga. “Komabe mosasamala kanthu za namgumi nthaŵi zina angatitumikire,” anatero Stubb wanthabwala tsiku lina, “sanganenedwe kuti amatigwira popanda nsabwe.”

Pazifukwa zonsezi, ndiye, mwanjira iriyonse yomwe mungawonere, muyenera kuganiza kuti Leviathan wamkulu ndi cholengedwa chimodzi padziko lapansi chomwe chiyenera kukhala chosapenta mpaka kumapeto. Zowona, chithunzi chimodzi chikhoza kufika pachimake pafupi kwambiri kuposa china, koma palibe chomwe chingachimveke molondola kwambiri. Chotero palibe njira yapadziko lapansi yodziŵira ndendende mmene namgumi amawonekera. Ndipo njira yokhayo yomwe mungapezere ngakhale lingaliro lolekerera la mdulidwe wake wamoyo, ndi kupita kukawedza wekha; koma potero, mumakhala pachiwopsezo chachikulu chokhala ndi chitofu chamuyaya ndikumizidwa ndi iye. Chifukwa chake, zikuwoneka kwa ine kuti simunachite changu kwambiri pakufuna kwanu kukhudza Leviathan uyu.





MUTU 56. Pa Zithunzi Zochepa Zolakwika za Zinsomba, ndi Zithunzi Zowona za Zithunzi za Whaling.

Pogwirizana ndi zithunzi zowopsya za anamgumi, ndikuyesedwa kwambiri pano kuti ndilowe pa nkhani zowopsya kwambiri za izo zomwe zimapezeka m'mabuku ena, akale ndi amakono, makamaka ku Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, etc. Koma ndimadutsa nkhaniyi.

Ndikudziwa zolemba zinayi zokha zosindikizidwa za Nangumi wamkulu wa Umuna; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, ndi Beale's. M'mutu wapitawu Colnett ndi Cuvier adatchulidwa. Huggins ndi wabwino kwambiri kuposa wawo; koma, mwazovuta kwambiri, Beale ndi yabwino kwambiri. Zojambula zonse za Beale za chinsomba ichi ndi zabwino, kupatulapo chithunzi chapakati pa chithunzi cha zinsomba zitatu mumaganizo osiyanasiyana, ndikujambula mutu wake wachiwiri. Malo ake akutsogolo, mabwato akuukira Sperm Whales, ngakhale mosakayika amawerengera kuti asangalatse kukayikira kwa amuna ena a m'nyumba, ndi zolondola modabwitsa komanso ngati moyo wake wonse. Zina mwa zojambula za Sperm Whale mu J. Ross Browne ndizolondola kwambiri mu contour; koma Alembedwa moipa. Ilo siliri vuto lake.

Za Nangumi Wabwino, zithunzi za autilaini zabwino kwambiri zili mu Scoresby; koma amakokedwa pamlingo waung’ono kwambiri kuti apereke lingaliro lofunika. Iye ali ndi chithunzi chimodzi chokha cha zochitika za whale, ndipo ichi ndi kusowa komvetsa chisoni, chifukwa ndi zithunzi zoterozo zokha, pamene mwachita bwino, kuti mutha kupeza chirichonse chonga lingaliro loona la chinsomba chamoyo monga momwe alenje ake amoyo amawonera.

Koma, zotengedwa kwa onse muzonse, zabwino kwambiri, ngakhale kuti mwatsatanetsatane sizinali zolondola kwambiri, ziwonetsero za anamgumi ndi ziwonetsero za namgumi zomwe zingapezeke kulikonse, ndizozokota ziwiri zazikulu za Chifalansa, zojambulidwa bwino, zotengedwa kuchokera ku zojambula za Garnery imodzi. Motsatira, amaimira kuukira kwa Umuna ndi Right Whale. Pazojambula koyamba, chinsomba cholemekezeka cha Sperm Whale chikuwonetsedwa mu ukulu wa mphamvu zonse, chongokwera pansi pa ngalawayo kuchokera mkatikati mwa nyanja, ndikunyamula mlengalenga pamsana pake kuwonongeka kowopsa kwa matabwa. Kutsogolo kwa ngalawayo ndi yosasweka pang'ono, ndipo amakokedwa ndi msana wa chilombocho; ndi kuyimirira kutsogolo kumeneko, chifukwa cha kung'anima kumodzi kosawerengeka kwa nthawi imeneyo, mukuwona munthu wopalasa, ataphimbidwa ndi nsonga yotentha ya chinsombacho, ndipo akudumphadumpha, ngati kuti akuchokera pamtunda. Zochita za chinthu chonsecho ndizabwino modabwitsa komanso zowona. Bafa la mzere wopanda kanthu limayandama panyanja yoyera; mizati ya matabwa a nsonga zotayikira imagwera m'menemo; mitu ya osambira inabalalika pafupi ndi namgumi m’mawu osiyana a mantha; pamene patali namondwe wakuda chombocho chikutsika powonekera. Kulakwitsa kwakukulu kungapezeke ndi tsatanetsatane wa anatomical wa nsomba iyi, koma izi zichitike; popeza, kwa moyo wanga, sindingathe kujambula yabwino kwambiri.

Pazozokota zachiwiri, bwatoli likujambula m'mbali mwa nthiti yayikulu ya Right Whale, yomwe imagudubuza udzu wake wakuda m'nyanja ngati mwala wa mossy kuchokera kumapiri a Patagonian. Majeti ake ndi oongoka, odzaza, ndi akuda ngati mwaye; kotero kuti chifukwa cha kuchuluka kwa utsi mu chimney, mungaganize kuti payenera kukhala kuphika mgonero wolimba mtima m'matumbo akulu pansipa. Mbalame zam'nyanja zikuyang'ana nkhanu, nsomba zam'madzi, ndi masiwiti ena am'nyanja ndi makaroni, zomwe Nangumi Wam'madzi nthawi zina amanyamula pamsana wake wankhanza. Ndipo nthawi yonseyi, leviathan wamilomo yokhuthala akuthamanga mozama, akusiya matani amtundu woyera wonyezimira, ndikupangitsa bwato laling'ono kuti ligwedezeke m'mabwalo ngati skiff wogwidwa pafupi ndi mawilo opalasa a sitima yapanyanja. Motero, kutsogolo kuli chipwirikiti chonse; koma kumbuyo, mosiyana ndi luso lodabwitsa, ndi galasi la nyanja yokhazikika, matanga osasunthika osasunthika a ngalawa yopanda mphamvu, ndi chinsomba chakufa, linga logonjetsedwa, ndi mbendera yogwidwa mwaulesi ikulendewera pamtengo wa chinsomba wolowetsedwa mu dzenje lake.

Kodi Ganery wojambulayo ndi ndani, kapena anali, sindikudziwa. Koma moyo wanga chifukwa cha izi mwina anali wodziwa bwino za phunziro lake, kapena wophunzitsidwa modabwitsa ndi whaleman wina wodziwa zambiri. A French ndi anyamata ochita kujambula. Pitani mukayang'ane zojambula zonse za ku Ulaya, ndipo mudzapeza kuti malo owonetserako zamoyo ndi kupuma pansalu, monga muholo yopambana ija ku Versailles; kumene wopenya akumenyana njira yake, pell-mell, kupyolera mu nkhondo zazikulu zotsatizana za France; kumene lupanga lililonse limawoneka ngati kuwala kwa Kuwala kwa Kumpoto, ndipo mafumu otsatizana okhala ndi zida ndi mafumu akudutsa, ngati chiwongolero cha centaurs ovekedwa korona? Osati osayenera konse kukhala ndi malo mu nyumbayi, ndi zigawo zankhondo zapanyanja za Garnery.

Kuthekera kwachilengedwe kwa Afalansa kutengera kukongola kwa zinthu kukuwoneka kuti kumawonekera mwapadera ndi zojambula ndi zojambula zomwe ali nazo pazithunzi zawo zakusokonekera. Pokhala opanda gawo limodzi mwa magawo khumi la zochitika za ku England pa usodzi, ndipo osati gawo limodzi mwa magawo 1,000 a Amereka, iwo aperekabe maiko onse aŵiri zithunzi zomalizidwa zotha kusonyeza mzimu weniweni wa kusaka anamgumi. Kwa mbali zambiri, English ndi American whale Draughters akuwoneka kuti ali okhutira kwathunthu ndi kupereka ndondomeko ya makina a zinthu, monga mbiri yopanda kanthu ya whale; zomwe, potengera kukongola kwake, zili ngati kujambula mbiri ya piramidi. Ngakhale Scoresby, munthu wodziwika bwino wa Right whaleman, atatipatsa utali wouma wa nangumi wa Greenland, ndi tinthu tating'onoting'ono titatu kapena tinayi ta narwhales ndi porpoises, amatichitira ku mndandanda wa zolemba zakale za mbedza za ngalawa, mipeni yodula, ndi ma grapnel; ndi khama laling'ono la Leuwenhoeck amagonjera kuyendera dziko lonjenjemera makumi asanu ndi anayi mphambu asanu ndi limodzi azithunzithunzi zazikuluzikulu za chipale chofewa cha Arctic. Sindikutanthauza kunyozetsa woyenda bwino kwambiri (Ndimamulemekeza chifukwa cha msilikali wakale), koma pa nkhani yofunika kwambiri kunali kuyang'anira kuti tisatengere kristalo aliyense lumbiro lolumbirira loperekedwa pamaso pa Greenland Justice of the Peace.

Kuwonjezera pa zozokotedwa zabwino za ku Garnery zimenezo, palinso zozokotedwa ziŵiri za Chifalansa zoyenera kuzindikiridwa, zolembedwa ndi munthu wina amene amalembetsa kuti “H. Durand.” Chimodzi mwa izo, ngakhale kuti sichinagwirizane ndendende ndi cholinga chathu chamakono, komabe chiyenera kutchulidwa m'nkhani zina. Ndi malo abata masana pakati pa zisumbu za Pacific; whale wa ku France anazika, kumtunda, modekha, ndi kutengera madzi m'bwalo mwaulesi; matanga omasuka a m’ngalawamo, ndi masamba aatali a kanjedza m’mbuyo, onse akutsamira pamodzi mumpweya wopanda mphepo. Zotsatira zake ndi zabwino kwambiri, zikaganiziridwa mokhudzana ndi kupereka kwa asodzi olimba pansi pa chimodzi mwazochepa zawo zakupuma zakum'mawa. Chojambula china ndi chosiyana kwambiri: chombocho chimayenda panyanja, ndipo mkati mwa moyo wa Leviatanic, ndi Whale Whale pambali; chombo (podula-mu) yendani ku chilombocho ngati kuti chapita ku quay; ndipo bwato, likukankha mwachangu kuchoka pa chochitika ichi, lili pafupi kuthamangitsa anamgumi patali. Mikondo ndi mikondo yagona kuti igwiritsidwe ntchito; opalasa atatu akungoika mlongoti m’dzenje lake; Pamene chombo chaching'onocho chinagwedezeka mwadzidzidzi, chinaima chilili m'madzi, ngati kavalo woweta. Kuchokera m’chombomo, utsi wa mazunzo a chinsomba chowira, ukwera ngati utsi pa mudzi wa akapolo; ndipo polowera mphepo, mtambo wakuda, womwe umatuluka molimba mtima chifukwa cha zipolowe ndi mvula, ukuwoneka kuti ukufulumizitsa ntchito ya amalinyero okondwa.

Zaka zaposachedwapa chingwe cha Manilla chakhala mu nsomba za ku America pafupifupi choposa hemp ngati chinthu chopangira mizere ya whale; pakuti, ngakhale sichiri cholimba ngati hemp, ndi champhamvu, komanso chofewa kwambiri komanso chotanuka; ndipo ine ndiwonjezera (popeza pali æsthetics mu zinthu zonse), ndi wokongola kwambiri ndi kukhala kwa ngalawa, kuposa hemp. Hemp ndi mdima, munthu wakuda, mtundu wa Mmwenye; koma Manilla ali ngati Circassian watsitsi lagolide kuti awone.

Mzere wa whale ndi magawo awiri mwa atatu okha a inchi mu makulidwe. Poyamba, simungaganize kuti ndi zamphamvu monga momwe zilili. Mwa kuyesa ulusi wake umodzi ndi makumi asanu aliyense adzaimitsa kulemera kwa mapaundi zana ndi makumi awiri; kotero kuti chingwe chonsecho chidzanyamula kupsyinjika pafupifupi kofanana ndi matani atatu. M'litali mwake, mzere wa sperm whale-line amayesa chinthu choposa mamita mazana awiri. Kumbuyo kwa ngalawayo imakulungidwa mozungulira mumphika, osati ngati chitoliro cha nyongolotsi, koma kuti ipange "mitolo" yozungulira yozungulira, yozungulira ngati tchizi, kapena zigawo za concentric spiralizations, popanda dzenje lililonse koma "mtima," kapena chubu choyimirira pang'onopang'ono chopangidwa mozungulira tchizi. Monga momwe kupiringa pang'ono kapena kugwedezeka kungapangitse, potha, kuchotsa mkono, mwendo, kapena thupi lonse la munthu wina, kusamala kwambiri kumagwiritsidwa ntchito poyimitsa chingwe mu chubu chake. Ena ma harpooneers amadya pafupifupi m'mawa wathunthu mubizinesi iyi, kunyamula mzerewo m'mwamba kenako ndikuwukweza pansi ndikudutsa mumdadada wopita ku chubu, kuti azitha kuzungulira kuti amasule makwinya ndi zopindika.

M'mabwato a Chingerezi amagwiritsidwa ntchito miphika iwiri m'malo mwa imodzi; mzere womwewo ukukulungidwa mosalekeza m'machubu onse awiri. Pali ubwino wina mwa izi; chifukwa mapasa awa pokhala ang'onoang'ono amalolera mosavuta m'ngalawamo, ndipo samachipanikiza kwambiri; pamene chubu ya ku America, yomwe ili m'mimba mwake ndi mamita atatu, imapanga katundu wochuluka kwambiri wa sitima yomwe matabwa ake ndi theka la inchi basi; pakuti pansi pa bwato la chinsombacho ndi ngati ayezi wovuta kwambiri, womwe udzakhala wolemera kwambiri, koma osati wochuluka kwambiri. Pamene chivundikiro cha chinsalu chopentidwa chikuwomberedwa pa tubu ya ku America, bwato limawoneka ngati likunyamuka ndi keke yaukwati yochititsa chidwi kuti iperekedwe kwa anamgumi.

Mapeto onse a mzerewo akuwonekera; mapeto apansi amathera mu kaphatikizidwe ka diso kapena kuzungulira kochokera pansi kuseri kwa chubu, ndikulendewera m'mphepete mwake popanda chilichonse. Kukonzekera kumeneku kwa mapeto apansi ndi kofunikira pa nkhani ziwiri. Choyamba: Pofuna kulumikiza mzere wowonjezera kuchokera ku boti loyandikana nalo, ngati namgumi wogwidwayo amveka mozama kwambiri mpaka kuopseza kuchotsa mzere wonse womwe unamangidwa pa harpoon. Muzochitika izi, chinsombacho chimasunthidwa ngati kapu ya ale, titero, kuchokera ku bwato lina kupita ku lina; ngakhale bwato loyamba limayandama nthawi zonse kuti lithandizire mnzake. Chachiwiri: Dongosololi ndilofunika kwambiri chifukwa cha chitetezo; pakuti anali m'munsi mapeto a mzere mwa njira iliyonse Ufumuyo ngalawa, ndipo anali chinsomba ndiye kuthamanga mzere mpaka mapeto pafupifupi mu miniti imodzi, kusuta monga nthawi zina, iye sakanatha kuima pamenepo, pakuti bwato lowonongedwa likanakhala mosalephera kukokera pansi pambuyo pake mu kuya kwa nyanja; ndipo zikatero palibe wolira m’tauni amene adzam’pezanso.

Asanatsitse bwato kuti athamangitse, mapeto apamwamba a mzerewo amatengedwa kumbuyo kwa chubu, ndikudutsa pamtunda wa loggerhead kumeneko, amapititsidwanso patsogolo utali wonse wa ngalawayo, akupumula modutsa pansalu kapena chogwirira cha nkhafi ya munthu aliyense, kotero kuti imathamanga motsutsana ndi dzanja lake popalasa; komanso podutsa pakati pa amunawo, pamene akukhala mosinthana ndi zida zotsutsana nazo, kupita kumalo otsogola kapena ma grooves kutsogolo kwenikweni kwa ngalawa, pomwe pini yamatabwa kapena skewer kukula kwa quill wamba, imalepheretsa kuti isatuluke. Kuchokera pazitsulo zimapachikidwa pang'onopang'ono pamwamba pa mauta, ndipo kenako amadutsa mkati mwa ngalawa kachiwiri; ndi mamita khumi kapena makumi awiri (otchedwa bokosi) akukulungidwa pabokosi la mautawo, amapitiriza njira yake yopita ku gunwale akadali m’tsogolo pang’ono, ndiyeno amangiriridwa ku chingwe chachifupi—chingwe chimene nthawi yomweyo chimalumikizidwa ndi kalulu; koma m'mbuyomu kulumikizako, njira yofupikitsa imadutsa zobisika zambiri zotopetsa mwatsatanetsatane.

Motero chingwe cha namgumi chimapinda bwato lonselo m’njira zake zovuta kumvetsa, kulipotokola ndi kulizungulira pafupifupi mbali iliyonse. Opalasa onse akukhudzidwa ndi mikwingwirima yake yoopsa; kotero kuti kwa diso lamantha la mwini dzikolo, amaoneka ngati majuga aku India, okhala ndi njoka zakupha kwambiri zotambasula manja awo mwamaseŵera. Kapena palibe mwana wamwamuna wa chivundi, kwa nthawi yoyamba, adzikhala yekha pakati pa zovuta za hempen, ndipo pamene akuyesetsa kwambiri pa opalasa, amamuganizira kuti nthawi ina iliyonse yosadziwika buluyo akhoza kugwedezeka, ndipo mikangano yowopsya yonseyi idzaseweredwa ngati mphezi zamphezi; Iye sangakhoze kukhala motere popanda kunjenjemera kumene kumapangitsa mafuta m'mafupa ake kunjenjemera mwa iye ngati mafuta ogwedezeka. Komabe chizolowezi-chinthu chachilendo! Kodi chizolowezi sichingakwaniritse chiyani?—Osewera a Gayer, chisangalalo chochuluka, nthabwala zabwino kwambiri, ndi ma reparte owoneka bwino, simunamvepo pa mahogany anu, kuposa momwe mungamve pamwamba pa mkungudza woyera wa theka la inchi wa bwato la whale, mutapachikidwa motere muzitsulo za hangman; ndipo, monga zigawenga zisanu ndi chimodzi za Calais pamaso pa King Edward, amuna asanu ndi mmodzi omwe amapanga gululo amakokera m'nsagwada za imfa, ndi chotchinga pakhosi lililonse, monga munganene.

Mwinamwake kulingalira pang’ono kwambiri tsopano kudzakuthandizani kuŵerengera za masoka a anangumi obwerezedwabwerezedwa—ena oŵerengeka chabe amene angolembedwa mwachisawawa—a munthu ameneyu kapena munthu uja akutulutsidwa m’ngalawa ndi mzere, ndi kutayika. Pakuti, pamene mzere ukudumpha, kukhala pansi ndiye mu ngalawa, kuli ngati kukhala pakati pa mamvekedwe ochuluka a injini ya nthunzi mukusewera kwathunthu, pamene mtengo uliwonse wowuluka, ndi tsinde, ndi gudumu, zikukudyani inu. Ndizoipa; pakuti simungakhoze kukhala wosasunthika mu mtima wa zowopsa izi, chifukwa ngalawa ikugwedezeka ngati chokumbatira, ndipo inu mukukankhidwira kwina ndi kwina, popanda chenjezo ndi pang'ono; ndipo kokha mwa kukhazikika kwina kodzilungamitsa ndi munthawi yomweyo kuvomera ndi kuchitapo kanthu, mutha kuthawa kupangidwa Mazeppa, ndikuthawa komwe dzuwa lowona zonse silingakubooleni.

Apanso: monga bata lalikulu lomwe mwachiwonekere limatsogola ndikunenera za namondwe, mwina ndi lowopsa kuposa mkuntho womwewo; pakuti bata ndi chofunda nacho; ndipo liri nalo mwa ilo lokha, monga ngati mfuti yooneka ngati yopanda vuto ikugwira ufa wakupha, ndi mpira, ndi kuphulika; kotero kupuma mokoma kwa mzerewo, monga momwe amachitira mwakachetechete opalasa asanalowe m'masewero enieni - ichi ndi chinthu chomwe chimawopsa kwambiri kuposa mbali ina iliyonse ya ngoziyi. Koma bwanji kunena zambiri? Anthu onse amakhala atazunguliridwa ndi mizere ya anamgumi. Onse amabadwa ndi zingwe pakhosi; koma kokha pamene agwidwa m’kutembenuka kofulumira, kwadzidzidzi kwa imfa, pamene anthu amazindikira mangozi achete, obisika, opezeka nthaŵi zonse a moyo. Ndipo ngati iwe uli wanthanthi, ngakhale utakhala mu bwato la chinsomba, iwe sukanati mu mtima umvere mantha ngakhale pang'ono, kuposa kukhala pansi pamoto wako wamadzulo ndi poker, ndipo osati kapu, pambali pako.
