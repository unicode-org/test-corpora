MUTU 74. Mutu wa Nangumi wa Umuna—Maonedwe Osiyana.

Pano, tsopano, pali anamgumi awiri aakulu, atagona mitu yawo palimodzi; tiyeni tilumikizane nawo, ndipo tigone pamodzi zathu.

Mwa dongosolo lalikulu la leviathans, Sperm Whale ndi Right Whale ndizodziwika kwambiri. Ndiwo anamgumi okha amene amasakidwa ndi anthu. Kwa Nantucketer, amapereka mitundu iwiri yamitundu yonse yodziwika ya namgumi. Monga kusiyana kwakunja pakati pawo kumawonekera makamaka m'mitu yawo; ndipo monga mutu wa aliyense ndi mphindi iyi ikulendewera ku mbali ya Pequod; ndipo momwe tingayendere momasuka kuchokera ku chimodzi kupita ku chimzake, pongodutsa pamtunda: -komwe, ndikufuna kudziwa, mudzapeza mwayi wabwinoko wophunzirira zasayansi yothandiza kuposa pano?

Poyamba, mumachita chidwi ndi kusiyana kwakukulu pakati pa mitu iyi. Onse ndi aakulu mokwanira mu chikumbumtima chonse; koma pali masamu masamu symmetry mu Nangumi Umuna zimene Nangumi Chomvetsa chisoni akusowa. Pali makhalidwe ambiri pamutu wa Sperm Whale. Mukachiwona, mwadala mumapereka ukulu wokulirapo kwa iye, m'malo mwa ulemu wofalikira. Munthawi yapano, ulemuwu ukukulitsidwa ndi tsabola ndi mtundu wamchere wa mutu wake pamsonkhano, kupereka chizindikiro cha ukalamba ndi chidziwitso chachikulu. Mwachidule, iye ndi amene asodziwo mwaukadaulo amawatcha kuti “grey-headed whale.”

Tiyeni tsopano tione chimene chili chosiyana kwambiri m’mitu imeneyi, ndicho ziwalo ziŵiri zofunika kwambiri, diso ndi khutu. Kumbuyo kutali kumbali ya mutu, ndi pansi, pafupi ndi ngodya ya nsagwada za whale, ngati mutafufuza mozama, pamapeto pake mudzawona diso lopanda khungu, lomwe mungakonde kukhala diso la mwana wa bulu; momwemo ndi kukula kwa mutu.

Tsopano, kuchokera pa malo odabwitsa awa a mmbali mwa maso a chinsomba, ziri zoonekeratu kuti iye sangakhoze konse kuwona chinthu chimene chiri ndendende kutsogolo, monganso momwe iye sangakhozere kumodzi ndendende kumtunda. M’mawu amodzi, malo amene maso a namgumi amafanana ndi makutu a munthu; ndipo mungadziganizire nokha, momwe zingakuyendereni, mutafufuza zinthu m'makutu mwanu. Mudzapeza kuti mungathe kulamulira madigiri makumi atatu a masomphenya patsogolo pa mzere wowongoka wa mawonekedwe; ndi enanso pafupi makumi atatu kumbuyo kwake. Ngati mdani wanu waukali akuyenda molunjika kwa inu, ndi lupanga lonyamulidwa usana, simukamuona, monga ngati akuba kumbuyo kwanu. M’mawu amodzi, mukanakhala ndi misana iwiri, titero kunena kwake; koma kutsogolo kwa munthu nchiyani, koma maso ake?

Komanso, pamene kuli kwakuti m’zinyama zina zambiri zimene ndimatha kuzilingalira tsopano, maso ali obzalidwa mopanda kuoneka bwino kuti agwirizanitse mphamvu zawo zopenya, kotero kuti apange chithunzi chimodzi osati ziŵiri ku ubongo; malo achilendo a maso a namgumi, motheka ogawanika monga momwe aliri ndi mapazi ambiri a cubic a mutu wolimba, womwe umatalika pakati pawo ngati phiri lalikulu lolekanitsa nyanja ziwiri m'zigwa; Izi, ndithudi, ziyenera kulekanitsa kwathunthu malingaliro omwe chiwalo chilichonse chodziyimira pawokha chimapereka. Nangumiyo, chotero, ayenera kuwona chithunzi chimodzi chosiyana mbali iyi, ndi china chosiyana mbali iyo; pamene zonse zapakati ziyenera kukhala mdima wandiweyani ndipo palibe kanthu kwa iye. Munthu anganenedwe kuti amayang'ana padziko lapansi ali m'bokosi lokhala ndi zomangira ziwiri zolumikizira zenera lake. Koma ndi chinsomba, ma sashes awiriwa amalowetsedwa padera, kupanga mawindo awiri osiyana, koma zomvetsa chisoni zimasokoneza maonekedwe. Izi zachilendo za maso a namgumi ndi chinthu choyenera kukumbukira nthawi zonse mu usodzi; ndi kukumbukiridwa ndi wowerenga muzithunzi zina zotsatila.

Funso lochititsa chidwi komanso lododometsa lingayambitsidwe pa nkhani yowoneka ngati yokhudza Leviathan. Koma ndiyenera kukhutira ndi lingaliro. Malingana ngati maso a munthu ali otseguka powala, mchitidwe wopenya ndi wodzifunira; ndiye kuti, sangadzithandizire kuwona zinthu zilizonse zomwe zili patsogolo pake. Komabe, chokumana nacho cha munthu aliyense chidzamphunzitsa, kuti ngakhale kuti angakhoze kusesa mopanda tsankho pakuyang’ana kumodzi, nkosathekadi kwa iye, mosamalitsa, ndi kotheratu, kupenda zinthu ziŵiri zirizonse—kaya zikhale zazikulu kapena zazing’ono bwanji—panthaŵi imodzi; osadandaula ngati agona mbali imodzi ndikugwirana wina ndi mzake. Koma ngati mwabwera kudzalekanitsa zinthu ziwirizi, ndi kuzizinga chilichonse ndi mdima wandiweyani; ndiye, kuti muwone mmodzi wa iwo, m'njira yotere kuti abweretse malingaliro anu pa izo, winayo adzachotsedwa kwathunthu ku chidziwitso chanu chamasiku ano. Nanga bwanji chinsombachi? Zoonadi, maso ake onse aŵiri, mwa iwo okha, ayenera kuchita zinthu nthawi imodzi; koma kodi ubongo wake ndi wokwanira, wophatikizana, ndi wochenjera kwambiri kuposa wa munthu, kotero kuti panthaŵi imodzimodziyo angathe kupenda mosamalitsa ziyembekezo ziŵiri zosiyana, imodzi kumbali imodzi ya iye, ndi ina kumbali yosiyana ndendende? Ngati angathe, ndiye kuti ndi chinthu chodabwitsa mwa iye, ngati kuti munthu adatha nthawi imodzi kudutsa ziwonetsero za mavuto awiri osiyana mu Euclid. Komanso, kufufuzidwa mosamalitsa, palibe kusagwirizana kulikonse mu kufananitsa uku.

Zitha kukhala zongoganiza chabe, koma zakhala zikuwoneka kwa ine nthawi zonse, kuti kusayenda bwino komwe kumawonetsedwa ndi anamgumi ena akakumana ndi mabwato atatu kapena anayi; manyazi ndi udindo wa queer mantha, zofala kwa anamgumi otero; Ndikuganiza kuti zonsezi zimachokera ku kusokonezeka kopanda chithandizo, komwe mphamvu zawo zogawanika komanso zosiyana kwambiri za masomphenya ziyenera kuwakhudza.

Koma khutu la chinsombacho ndi lodzaza ndi chidwi ngati diso. Ngati ndinu mlendo ku mtundu wawo, mutha kusaka mitu iwiriyi kwa maola ambiri, osapeza chiwalocho. Khutu lilibe tsamba lakunja chirichonse; ndipo mu dzenje lomwe simungalowetse cholembera, mphindi yodabwitsa kwambiri. Imakhala pang'ono kuseri kwa diso. Ponena za makutu awo, kusiyana kwakukulu kumeneku kuyenera kuwonedwa pakati pa sperm whale ndi kumanja. Ngakhale khutu lakale liri ndi kutseguka kwakunja, lakumapetolo limakutidwa ndi nembanemba, kotero kuti lisawonekere kuchokera kunja.

Kodi sizodabwitsa, kuti munthu wamkulu kwambiri monga namgumi ayenera kuwona dziko kudzera m'diso laling'ono, ndikumva bingu kudzera m'khutu lomwe ndi laling'ono kuposa la kalulu? Koma ngati maso ake anali otakasuka ngati disolo la telesikopu yaikulu ya Herschel; ndi makutu ake ngati makhonde a akachisi; kodi icho chingamupangitse iye kuwonanso motalikirapo, kapena kutchera makutu? Ayi.—N'chifukwa chiyani mumayesetsa 'kukulitsa' maganizo anu? Subtilize izo.

Tiyeni tsopano ndi zitsulo zilizonse ndi injini za nthunzi zomwe tili nazo pafupi, sitingathe kuziyika pamwamba pa mutu wa sperm whale, kuti ugone pansi; ndiye, kukwera ndi makwerero ku nsonga, ndi peep pansi pakamwa; ndipo pakapanda kuti thupilo tsopano lalekanitsidwa kwathunthu kwa ilo, ndi nyali tikanatsikira mu Phanga lalikulu la Kentucky Mammoth la mimba yake. Koma tiyeni tigwire pano ndi dzino ili, ndi kuyang'ana za ife pamene ife tiri. Ndi pakamwa kokongola ndi koyera bwanji! kuchokera pansi mpaka padenga, zokhala ndi mizere, kapena zokhala ndi nembanemba yonyezimira yonyezimira, yonyezimira ngati ma satin a mkwatibwi.

Koma tuluka tsopano, ndipo yang'anani nsagwada zapansi zochititsa chidwizi, zomwe zimawoneka ngati chivindikiro chachitali chopapatiza cha bokosi la fodya wofowokera, chokhala ndi hinje kumapeto kumodzi, m'malo mwa mbali imodzi. Ngati mutaying'amba, kuti mutenge pamwamba, ndikuwululira mizere yake ya mano, zikuwoneka ngati portcullis yowopsya; ndi zotero, kalanga! zikutsimikizira kwa ambiri osauka Wight mu usodzi, amene spikes awa amagwera ndi mphamvu yokhomerera. Koma choyipa kwambiri ndicho kuona, pamene mapiko ake afika pansi pa nyanja, inu mukuwona namngumi wina wonyezimira, akuyandama pamenepo itaimitsidwa, ndi nsagwada yake yodabwitsa, ena utali wa mapazi khumi ndi asanu, ikulendewera pansi pa ngodya za kumanja ndi thupi lake, ku dziko lonse lapansi ngati jib-boom ya chombo. Nangumi ameneyu sanafe; Iye wataya mtima; zachilendo, mwina; hypochondriac; ndipo nsagwada zake zakhala zomasuka, n’kumusiya m’mavuto amtundu umenewu, chitonzo kwa fuko lake lonse, amene mosakayikira ayenera kumutsekera m’kamwa.

Nthaŵi zambiri nsagwada ya m’munsi imeneyi—yosagwedezeka mosavuta ndi wojambula—imachotsedwa ndi kukwezedwa pamwamba pa sitimayo n’cholinga chochotsa mano a minyanga ya njovu, ndi kupereka fupa la nangumi lolimba limene asodzi amapangira zinthu zamitundumitundu, kuphatikizapo ndodo, maambulera, ndi zogwirira zikwapu.

Ndi kukweza kwautali, kotopa nsagwada zimakokedwa m'bwato, ngati kuti ndi nangula; ndipo ikadzafika nthaŵi yake—masiku angapo pambuyo pa ntchito ina—Queequeg, Daggoo, ndi Tashtego, pokhala madokotala odziŵa bwino mano, ayamba kuzula mano. Queequeg imawombera m'kamwa ndi khama; ndiye nsagwada zimakhomeredwa pansi mpaka ku zingwe, ndipo chomenyeracho chikugwedezeka kuchokera mmwamba, iwo amakoka mano awa, pamene ng'ombe za ku Michigan zimakoka zitsa za thundu zakale kuchokera ku nkhalango zakuthengo. Pamakhala mano makumi anayi ndi awiri onse; mu anamgumi akale, otopa kwambiri, koma osavunda; kapena kudzazidwa ndi mafashoni athu ochita kupanga. Pambuyo pake, nsagwada zimadulidwa muzitsulo, ndikuwunjika ngati zomangira nyumba.





MUTU 75. Mutu wa Nangumi Woyenera—Maonedwe Osiyana.

Kuwoloka sitimayo, tiyeni tsopano tione bwinobwino mutu wa Nangumi Wabwino.

Monga mwa kawonekedwe kaŵirikaŵiri mutu wolemekezeka wa Nangumi wa Umuna ungayerekezedwe ndi gareta lankhondo Lachiroma (makamaka kutsogolo, kumene kuli kozungulira mokulirapo); kotero, pakuwona kwakukulu, mutu wa Whale Woyenera umafanana kwambiri ndi nsapato yaikulu ya galliot-toed. Zaka 200 zapitazo woyenda panyanja wachidatchi wina anayerekezera kaonekedwe kake ndi kamene kamapanga nsapato komaliza. Ndipo mu nsapato yomweyi, mayi wachikulire wa ku nazale, ndi ana ochuluka, akhoza kukhala momasuka, iye ndi mbadwa zake zonse.

Koma pamene mukuyandikira mutu waukuluwu umayamba kuganiza mosiyanasiyana, malinga ndi malingaliro anu. Mukayimirira pamutu pake ndikuyang'ana ma spouthole awiri ooneka ngati F, mutha kutenga mutu wonse kukhala woyimba woyimba wokulirapo, ndi ma spiracles awa, zolowera mu bolodi lake loyimbira. Ndiyeno, kachiŵirinso, ngati muyang’ana diso lanu pa kuloŵerera kodabwitsa kumeneku, kokhala ngati chisa, konga chipeso pamwamba pa chisa—chinthu chobiriŵira, chodetsedwa chimenechi, chimene anthu a ku Greenland amachitcha “korona,” ndipo asodzi a Kummwera “fupa” la Nangumi Woyenera; Mukayang’anitsitsa zimenezi, mumatha kutenga mutuwo n’kupita ku thunthu la mtengo wina waukulu kwambiri, womwe uli ndi chisa cha mbalame. Mulimonse momwe zingakhalire, mukamawonera nkhanu zamoyo zomwe zimakhazikika pano pa boneti iyi, lingaliro lotereli lidzakuchitikirani; pokhapokha ngati, ndithudi, kukongola kwanu kwakhazikitsidwa ndi mawu aukadaulo oti “korona” nawonso apatsidwa; pamenepa mudzakhala ndi chidwi chachikulu m’kulingalira mmene chilombo champhamvu chimenechi chiridi mfumu yapanyanja yachifumu yachifumu, imene korona wake wobiriwira waikidwa pamodzi kaamba ka iye m’njira yodabwitsa imeneyi. Koma ngati chinsomba ichi chikhala mfumu, iye ndi munthu wowoneka wokwiya kwambiri kuti akomere korona. Yang'anani mlomo wakumunsi ukulendewera! pali kukhumudwa kwakukulu ndi kukhumudwa komwe kulipo! sulk ndi pout, ndi muyeso wa kalipentala, pafupifupi mapazi makumi awiri utali wake ndi mapazi asanu kuya; sulk ndi pout zomwe zingakupatseni magaloni 500 amafuta ndi zina zambiri.

Chisoni chachikulu, tsopano, kuti chinsomba chomvetsa chisonichi chiyenera kukhala ndi milomo ya hare. Kupasukako kuli pafupi phazi kudutsa. Mwinamwake amayiwo panthawi yofunika kwambiri anali kuyenda panyanja pamphepete mwa nyanja ya Peru, pamene zivomezi zinachititsa kuti gombeli lisungunuke. Pa mlomo uwu, monga poterera poterera, tsopano tikulowa mkamwa. Pa mawu anga ndinali ku Mackinaw, ndiyenera kutenga izi kukhala mkati mwa Indian wigwam. Ambuye wabwino! Kodi iyi ndi njira imene Yona anapita? Denga liri pafupi mamita khumi ndi awiri m'litali, ndipo limathamangira ku ngodya yokongola kwambiri, ngati kuti pali mzati wokhazikika pamenepo; pamene nthiti izi, arched, mbali zaubweya, kutipatsa ife ndi zodabwitsa, theka ofukula, scimetar woboola pakati slats a whalebone, kunena mazana atatu mbali, amene malinga ndi kumtunda kwa mutu kapena korona fupa, kupanga izo akhungu Venetian amene kwina zatchulidwa mochedwa. Mphepete mwa mafupawa amakhala ndi ulusi waubweya, womwe Whale Woyenera amathira madzi, ndipo m'njira zake zovuta amasunga nsomba zing'onozing'ono, akatsegula pakamwa amadutsa m'nyanja ya brit panthawi yodyera. Pakatikati pa mafupa a khungu, pamene amaima m'dongosolo lawo lachilengedwe, pali zizindikiro zina zochititsa chidwi, zokhotakhota, mabowo, ndi zitunda, zomwe zinanso zimawerengera zaka za cholengedwacho, monga zaka za thundu ndi mphete zake zozungulira. Ngakhale kutsimikizika kwa mulingo uwu sikungawonekere, komabe ili ndi fungo la kuthekera kofananira. Mulimonse mmene zingakhalire, ngati tilolera, tiyenera kupereka zaka zokulirapo kwambiri kwa Nangumi Woyenera kuposa mmene tingaonere poyamba.

M'nthawi zakale, zikuwoneka kuti zidakhalapo zokonda chidwi kwambiri zokhudzana ndi akhungu awa. Woyenda panyanja wina ku Purchas amawatcha “ndevu” zodabwitsa mkati mwa kamwa la namgumi; njonda yachitatu yachikulire ku Hackluyt imagwiritsa ntchito chinenero chokongola chotsatirachi: “Pali zipsepse pafupifupi mazana aŵiri ndi makumi asanu zomwe zimamera mbali iriyonse ya chiphuphu chake chapamwamba, chomwe chimapindirira lilime lake mbali iriyonse ya kamwa yake.”

*Izi zikutikumbutsa kuti Nangumi Woyenera ali ndi ndevu za mtundu wina, kapena kuti masharubu, opangidwa ndi tsitsi loyera lomwazika pang’ono kumtunda kwa nsagwada yakunja. Nthawi zina zibolibolizi zimasonyeza nkhope yake yaulemu.

Monga aliyense akudziwa, “zipsepse za nkhumba,” “zipsepse,” “ndevu,” “akhungu,” kapena chirichonse chimene mungafune, zimapatsa amayi mabasi awo ndi zinthu zina zowumitsa. Koma makamaka izi, zofuna zakhala zikuchepa. Inali mu nthawi ya Mfumukazi Anne kuti fupa linali mu ulemerero wake, farthingale ndiye mafashoni onse. Ndipo pamene adamu akale aja ankayenda mosangalala, ngakhale m’nsagwada za chinsomba, monga munganene; momwemonso, m’mvumbi, ndi kupanda kulingalira, kodi masiku ano tiwulukira pansi pa nsagwada zomwezo kudzitetezera; ambulera pokhala chihema choyalidwa pa fupa lomwelo.

Koma tsopano iwalani zonse za khungu ndi ndevu kwa kamphindi, ndipo, mutayima mkamwa mwa Nsomba Yabwino, yang'anani mozungulira inu mwatsopano. Kuwona zipilala zonsezi za mafupa zikuzungulira mozungulira, kodi simungaganize kuti muli mkati mwa chiwalo chachikulu cha Haarlem, ndikuyang'ana mapaipi ake zikwizikwi? Kwa kapeti ku chiwalo tili ndi chiguduli chofewa kwambiri cha Turkey-lilime, lomwe limamatiridwa, titero, mpaka pansi pakamwa. Ndiwonenepa kwambiri ndipo ndi wofewa, ndipo ndi wokwanira kukhadzula poukweza pamwamba pake. Lilime limeneli tsopano pamaso pathu; pakungoyang'ana pang'ono ndiyenera kunena kuti inali migolo isanu ndi umodzi; ndiko kuti, adzakupatsani inu pafupifupi kuchuluka kwa mafuta.

Izi zisanachitike, muyenera kuti mwawona zowona za zomwe ndidayamba nazo - kuti Sperm Whale ndi Right Whale zili ndi mitu yosiyana kotheratu. Pomaliza, ndiye: mu Right Whale's mulibe chitsime chachikulu cha umuna; opanda mano a njovu nkomwe; sipatali, wowonda mandible wa nsagwada m'munsi, monga Umuna Whale's. Ngakhale mu Nangumi wa Umuna mulibe khungu lililonse la mafupa; palibe mlomo waukulu wapansi; ndipo ngakhale lilime lililonse. Apanso, Whale Yabwino ili ndi mabowo awiri akunja, Sperm Whale imodzi yokha.

Yang'anani kotsiriza, tsopano, pa mitu iyi yolemekezeka ya zisoti, pamene iwo akagona pamodzi; pakuti posachedwapa munthu adzamira, wosalembedwa, m’nyanja; winayo sadzakhala motalika kwambiri kutsatira.

Kodi mungagwire mawu a Sperm Whale's pamenepo? Ndizofanana ndi zomwe adamwalira nazo, makwinya ena ataliatali pamphumi akuwoneka tsopano akutha. Ndikuganiza kuti mphuno yake yotakata imakhala yodzaza ndi kukhumudwa ngati prairie, wobadwa ndi mphwayi wongoganizira za imfa. Koma chongani mawu a mutu winawo. Onani mlomo wodabwitsa wapansi, woponderezedwa mwangozi kumbali ya chombocho, kuti mugwire mwamphamvu nsagwada. Kodi mutu wonsewu sukuwoneka kuti ukunena za chigamulo chothandiza poyang'anizana ndi imfa? Nangumi Wolondola uyu ndimamutenga kuti anali Mstoiki; Sperm Whale, wa Platonian, yemwe mwina adatenga Spinoza m'zaka zake zomaliza.





MUTU 76. Kumenyetsa Nkhosa.

Nditasiya, kwanthawi yayitali, mutu wa Sperm Whale, ndikufuna inu, ngati katswiri wazamakhalidwe, mophweka - makamaka munene zakutsogolo kwake, pakusonkhanitsidwa kwake konse. Ndikufuna kuti mufufuze tsopano ndi lingaliro lokhalo lodzipangira nokha kuyerekeza kosakokomeza, kwanzeru kwa mphamvu iliyonse yomenyanitsa yomwe ingakhale pamenepo. Nayi mfundo yofunika kwambiri; pakuti muyenera kuthetsa nkhani imeneyi mokhutiritsa ndi inu nokha, kapena kukhalabe wosakhulupirira nthawi zonse pa chimodzi mwa zochitika zowopsya kwambiri, koma osati zochitika zenizeni zochepa, mwinamwake paliponse zomwe zimapezeka m'mbiri yonse yolembedwa.

Mukuwona kuti pamalo osambira wamba a Sperm Whale, kutsogolo kwa mutu wake kumapereka ndege yowongoka kwathunthu kumadzi; mukuwona kuti gawo lakumunsi lakutsogololo limatsetsereka kwambiri kumbuyo, kuti lipatsenso malo othawirako ku socket yayitali yomwe imalandira nsagwada yapansi ngati boom; mukuwona kuti pakamwa pali pansi pamutu kwathunthu, mofananamo, ngati kuti pakamwa panu muli pansi pa chibwano chanu. Komanso mukuona kuti chinsomba chilibe mphuno yakunja; ndi kuti mphuno yake ili pamutu pake; mukuwona kuti maso ndi makutu ake ali m’mbali mwa mutu wake, pafupifupi gawo limodzi mwa magawo atatu a utali wake wonse kuchokera kutsogolo. Chifukwa chake, muyenera tsopano kuti mwazindikira kuti kutsogolo kwa mutu wa Nangumi wa Umuna ndi khoma lakufa, lakhungu, lopanda chiwalo chimodzi kapena kutchuka kwamtundu uliwonse. Kuonjezera apo, tsopano muyenera kulingalira kuti pokhapo mbali yowonjezereka, yapansi, yobwerera kumbuyo ya kutsogolo kwa mutu, pali kachigawo kakang'ono ka fupa; ndipo mpaka mutayandikira mapazi makumi awiri kuchokera pachipumi ndikufika pakukula kwathunthu. Kotero kuti unyinji wonsewu wopanda mafupa uli ngati wadi imodzi. Koma potsirizira pake, monga momwe zidzasonyezedwe posachedwapa, m'kati mwake muli mafuta osalimba kwambiri; komabe, tsopano muyenera kudziwitsidwa za chikhalidwe cha zinthu zomwe zimapatsa mphamvu zonse zowoneka bwino.
