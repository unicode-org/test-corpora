MUTU 74. Mutu wa Nangumi wa Umuna—Maonedwe Osiyana.

Pano, tsopano, pali anamgumi awiri aakulu, atagona mitu yawo palimodzi; tiyeni tilumikizane nawo, ndipo tigone pamodzi zathu.

Mwa dongosolo lalikulu la leviathans, Sperm Whale ndi Right Whale ndizodziwika kwambiri. Ndiwo anamgumi okha amene amasakidwa ndi anthu. Kwa Nantucketer, amapereka mitundu iwiri yamitundu yonse yodziwika ya namgumi. Monga kusiyana kwakunja pakati pawo kumawonekera makamaka m'mitu yawo; ndipo monga mutu wa aliyense ndi mphindi iyi ikulendewera ku mbali ya Pequod; ndipo momwe tingayendere momasuka kuchokera ku chimodzi kupita ku chimzake, pongodutsa pamtunda: -komwe, ndikufuna kudziwa, mudzapeza mwayi wabwinoko wophunzirira zasayansi yothandiza kuposa pano?

Poyamba, mumachita chidwi ndi kusiyana kwakukulu pakati pa mitu iyi. Onse ndi aakulu mokwanira mu chikumbumtima chonse; koma pali masamu masamu symmetry mu Nangumi Umuna zimene Nangumi Chomvetsa chisoni akusowa. Pali makhalidwe ambiri pamutu wa Sperm Whale. Mukachiwona, mwadala mumapereka ukulu wokulirapo kwa iye, m'malo mwa ulemu wofalikira. Munthawi yapano, ulemuwu ukukulitsidwa ndi tsabola ndi mtundu wamchere wa mutu wake pamsonkhano, kupereka chizindikiro cha ukalamba ndi chidziwitso chachikulu. Mwachidule, iye ndi amene asodziwo mwaukadaulo amawatcha kuti “grey-headed whale.”

Tiyeni tsopano tione chimene chili chosiyana kwambiri m’mitu imeneyi, ndicho ziwalo ziŵiri zofunika kwambiri, diso ndi khutu. Kumbuyo kutali kumbali ya mutu, ndi pansi, pafupi ndi ngodya ya nsagwada za whale, ngati mutafufuza mozama, pamapeto pake mudzawona diso lopanda khungu, lomwe mungakonde kukhala diso la mwana wa bulu; momwemo ndi kukula kwa mutu.

Tsopano, kuchokera pa malo odabwitsa awa a mmbali mwa maso a chinsomba, ziri zoonekeratu kuti iye sangakhoze konse kuwona chinthu chimene chiri ndendende kutsogolo, monganso momwe iye sangakhozere kumodzi ndendende kumtunda. M’mawu amodzi, malo amene maso a namgumi amafanana ndi makutu a munthu; ndipo mungadziganizire nokha, momwe zingakuyendereni, mutafufuza zinthu m'makutu mwanu. Mudzapeza kuti mungathe kulamulira madigiri makumi atatu a masomphenya patsogolo pa mzere wowongoka wa mawonekedwe; ndi enanso pafupi makumi atatu kumbuyo kwake. Ngati mdani wanu waukali akuyenda molunjika kwa inu, ndi lupanga lonyamulidwa usana, simukamuona, monga ngati akuba kumbuyo kwanu. M’mawu amodzi, mukanakhala ndi misana iwiri, titero kunena kwake; koma kutsogolo kwa munthu nchiyani, koma maso ake?

Ndiponso, pamene kuli kwakuti m’zinyama zina zambiri zimene ndimatha kuzilingalira tsopano, maso ali obzalidwa mopanda kuoneka bwino kuti agwirizanitse mphamvu zawo zopenya, kotero kuti apange chithunzi chimodzi osati ziŵiri ku ubongo; malo achilendo a maso a namgumi, motheka ogawanika monga momwe aliri ndi mapazi ambiri a cubic a mutu wolimba, womwe umatalika pakati pawo ngati phiri lalikulu lolekanitsa nyanja ziwiri m'zigwa; Izi, ndithudi, ziyenera kulekanitsa kwathunthu malingaliro omwe chiwalo chilichonse chodziyimira pawokha chimapereka. Nangumiyo, chotero, ayenera kuwona chithunzi chimodzi chosiyana mbali iyi, ndi china chosiyana mbali iyo; pamene zonse zapakati ziyenera kukhala mdima wandiweyani ndipo palibe kanthu kwa iye. Munthu anganenedwe kuti amayang'ana padziko lapansi ali m'bokosi lokhala ndi zomangira ziwiri zolumikizira zenera lake. Koma ndi chinsomba, ma sashes awiriwa amalowetsedwa padera, kupanga mawindo awiri osiyana, koma zomvetsa chisoni zimasokoneza maonekedwe. Izi zachilendo za maso a namgumi ndi chinthu choyenera kukumbukira nthawi zonse mu usodzi; ndi kukumbukiridwa ndi wowerenga muzithunzi zina zotsatila.

Funso lochititsa chidwi komanso lododometsa lingayambitsidwe pa nkhani yowoneka ngati yokhudza Leviathan. Koma ndiyenera kukhutira ndi lingaliro. Malingana ngati maso a munthu ali otseguka powala, mchitidwe wopenya ndi wodzifunira; ndiye kuti, sangadzithandizire kuwona zinthu zilizonse zomwe zili patsogolo pake. Komabe, chokumana nacho cha munthu aliyense chidzamphunzitsa, kuti ngakhale kuti angakhoze kusesa mopanda tsankho pakuyang’ana kamodzi, nkosatheka kwa iye, mosamalitsa, ndi kotheratu, kupenda zinthu ziŵiri zirizonse—kaya zikhale zazikulu kapena zazing’ono bwanji—pa chimodzi. ndi nthawi yomweyo ya nthawi; osadandaula ngati agona mbali imodzi ndikugwirana wina ndi mzake. Koma ngati mwabwera kudzalekanitsa zinthu ziwirizi, ndi kuzizinga chilichonse ndi mdima wandiweyani; ndiye, kuti muwone mmodzi wa iwo, m'njira yotere kuti abweretse malingaliro anu pa izo, winayo adzachotsedwa kwathunthu ku chidziwitso chanu chamasiku ano. Nanga bwanji chinsombachi? Zoonadi, maso ake onse aŵiri, mwa iwo okha, ayenera kuchita zinthu nthawi imodzi; koma kodi ubongo wake ndi wokwanira, wophatikizana, ndi wochenjera kwambiri kuposa wa munthu, kotero kuti panthaŵi imodzimodziyo angathe kupenda mosamalitsa ziyembekezo ziŵiri zosiyana, imodzi kumbali imodzi ya iye, ndi ina kumbali yosiyana ndendende? Ngati angathe, ndiye kuti ndi chinthu chodabwitsa mwa iye, ngati kuti munthu adatha nthawi imodzi kudutsa ziwonetsero za mavuto awiri osiyana mu Euclid. Komanso, kufufuzidwa mosamalitsa, palibe kusagwirizana kulikonse mu kufananitsa uku.

Zitha kukhala zongoganiza chabe, koma zakhala zikuwoneka kwa ine nthawi zonse, kuti kusayenda bwino komwe kumawonetsedwa ndi anamgumi ena akakumana ndi mabwato atatu kapena anayi; manyazi ndi udindo wa queer mantha, zofala kwa anamgumi otero; Ndikuganiza kuti zonsezi zimachokera ku kusokonezeka kopanda chithandizo, komwe mphamvu zawo zogawanika komanso zosiyana kwambiri za masomphenya ziyenera kuwakhudza.

Koma khutu la chinsombacho ndi lodzaza ndi chidwi ngati diso. Ngati ndinu mlendo ku mtundu wawo, mutha kusaka mitu iwiriyi kwa maola ambiri, osapeza chiwalocho. Khutu lilibe tsamba lakunja chirichonse; ndipo mu dzenje lomwe simungalowetse cholembera, mphindi yodabwitsa kwambiri. Imakhala pang'ono kuseri kwa diso. Ponena za makutu awo, kusiyana kwakukulu kumeneku kuyenera kuwonedwa pakati pa sperm whale ndi kumanja. Ngakhale khutu lakale liri ndi kutseguka kwakunja, lakumapetolo limakutidwa ndi nembanemba, kotero kuti lisawonekere kuchokera kunja.

Kodi sizodabwitsa, kuti munthu wamkulu kwambiri monga namgumi ayenera kuwona dziko kudzera m'diso laling'ono, ndikumva bingu kudzera m'khutu lomwe ndi laling'ono kuposa la kalulu? Koma ngati maso ake anali otakasuka ngati disolo la telesikopu yaikulu ya Herschel; ndi makutu ake ngati makhonde a akachisi; kodi icho chingamupangitse iye kuwonanso motalikirapo, kapena kutchera makutu? Ayi.—N'chifukwa chiyani mumayesetsa 'kukulitsa' maganizo anu? Subtilize izo.

Tiyeni tsopano ndi zitsulo zilizonse ndi injini za nthunzi zomwe tili nazo, sitingathe kuziyika pamwamba pa mutu wa sperm whale, kuti ugone pansi; ndiye, kukwera ndi makwerero ku nsonga, ndi peep pansi pakamwa; ndipo pakapanda kuti thupilo tsopano lalekanitsidwa kwathunthu kwa ilo, ndi nyali tikanatsikira mu Phanga lalikulu la Kentucky Mammoth la mimba yake. Koma tiyeni tigwire pano ndi dzino ili, ndi kuyang'ana za ife pamene ife tiri. Ndi pakamwa kokongola ndi koyera bwanji! kuchokera pansi mpaka padenga, zokhala ndi mizere, kapena zokhala ndi nembanemba yonyezimira yonyezimira, yonyezimira ngati ma satin a mkwatibwi.

Koma tuluka tsopano, ndipo yang'anani nsagwada zapansi zochititsa chidwizi, zomwe zimawoneka ngati chivindikiro chachitali chopapatiza cha bokosi la fodya wofowokera, chokhala ndi hinje kumapeto kumodzi, m'malo mwa mbali imodzi. Ngati mutaying'amba, kuti mutenge pamwamba, ndikuwululira mizere yake ya mano, zikuwoneka ngati portcullis yowopsya; ndi zotero, kalanga! zikutsimikizira kwa ambiri osauka Wight mu usodzi, amene spikes awa amagwera ndi mphamvu yokhomerera. Koma choyipa kwambiri ndicho kuona, pamene mapiko ake afika pansi pa nyanja, iwe uwona namngumi wina wonyezimira, akuyandama pamenepo ataimitsidwa, ndi nsagwada yake yodabwitsa, yotalika mapazi khumi ndi asanu, ikulendewera pansi pa ngodya za kumanja ndi thupi lake. dziko ngati jib-boom chombo. Nangumi ameneyu sanafe; Iye wataya mtima; zachilendo, mwina; hypochondriac; ndipo nsagwada zake zamasuka kwambiri, n'kumusiya ali m'mavuto amtundu woterewo, chitonzo kwa fuko lake lonse, lomwe mosakayikira liyenera kumutsekera nsagwada.

Nthaŵi zambiri nsagwada ya m’munsi imeneyi—yomasuliridwa mosavuta ndi wojambula wozoloŵereka—imatayidwa ndi kunyamulidwa pa sitimayo n’cholinga chochotsa mano a mnyanga wa njovu, ndi kupereka fupa la namgumi loyera lolimba limene asodzi amapangira nalo mitundu yonse ya zinthu zosangalatsa. kuphatikiza ndodo, maambulera-sitoko, ndi zogwirira kwa zikwapu.

Ndi kukweza kwautali, kotopa nsagwada zimakokedwa m'bwato, ngati kuti ndi nangula; ndipo ikadzafika nthaŵi yake—masiku angapo pambuyo pa ntchito ina—Queequeg, Daggoo, ndi Tashtego, pokhala madokotala odziŵa bwino mano, ayamba kuzula mano. Queequeg imawombera m'kamwa ndi khama; ndiye nsagwada zimakhomeredwa pansi mpaka ku zingwe, ndipo chomenyeracho chikugwedezeka kuchokera mmwamba, iwo amakoka mano awa, pamene ng'ombe za ku Michigan zimakoka zitsa za thundu zakale kuchokera ku nkhalango zakuthengo. Pamakhala mano makumi anayi ndi awiri onse; mu anamgumi akale, otopa kwambiri, koma osavunda; kapena kudzazidwa ndi mafashoni athu ochita kupanga. Pambuyo pake, nsagwada zimadulidwa muzitsulo, ndikuwunjika ngati zomangira nyumba.





MUTU 75. Mutu wa Nangumi Woyenera—Maonedwe Osiyana.

Powoloka sitimayo, tiyeni tsopano tione bwinobwino mutu wa Nangumi Wabwino.

Monga mwa kawonekedwe kaŵirikaŵiri mutu wolemekezeka wa Nangumi wa Umuna ungayerekezedwe ndi gareta lankhondo Lachiroma (makamaka kutsogolo, kumene kuli kozungulira mokulirapo); kotero, pakuwona kwakukulu, mutu wa Whale Woyenera umafanana kwambiri ndi nsapato yaikulu ya galliot-toed. Zaka 200 zapitazo woyenda panyanja wachidatchi wina anayerekezera kaonekedwe kake ndi kamene kamapanga nsapato komaliza. Ndipo mu nsapato yomweyi, mayi wachikulire wa ku nazale, ndi ana ochuluka, akhoza kukhala momasuka, iye ndi mbadwa zake zonse.

Koma pamene mukuyandikira mutu waukuluwu umayamba kuganiza mosiyanasiyana, malinga ndi malingaliro anu. Mukayimirira pamutu pake ndikuyang'ana ma spouthole awiri ooneka ngati F, mutha kutenga mutu wonse kukhala woyimba woyimba wokulirapo, ndi ma spiracles awa, zolowera mu bolodi lake loyimbira. Ndiyeno, kachiŵirinso, ngati muyang’ana diso lanu pa chisa chodabwitsa ichi, chokwiririka, chonga chisa pamwamba pa unyinji—chinthu chobiriŵira ichi, chodetsedwa, chimene anthu a ku Greenland amachitcha “korona,” ndipo asodzi a Kummwera “fupa” la Chinsomba Choyenera; Mukayang’anitsitsa zimenezi, mumatha kutenga mutuwo n’kupita ku thunthu la mtengo wina waukulu kwambiri, womwe uli ndi chisa cha mbalame. Mulimonse momwe zingakhalire, mukamawonera nkhanu zamoyo zomwe zimakhazikika pano pa boneti iyi, lingaliro lotereli lidzakuchitikirani; pokhapokha ngati, ndithudi, kukongola kwanu kwakhazikitsidwa ndi mawu aukadaulo oti “korona” nawonso apatsidwa; pamenepa mudzakhala ndi chidwi chachikulu m’kulingalira mmene chilombo champhamvu chimenechi chiridi mfumu yapanyanja yachifumu yachifumu, imene korona wake wobiriwira waikidwa pamodzi kaamba ka iye m’njira yodabwitsa imeneyi. Koma ngati chinsomba ichi chikhala mfumu, iye ndi munthu wowoneka wokwiya kwambiri kuti akomere korona. Yang'anani mlomo wakumunsi ukulendewera! pali kukhumudwa kwakukulu ndi kukhumudwa komwe kulipo! sulk ndi pout, ndi muyeso wa kalipentala, pafupifupi mapazi makumi awiri utali wake ndi mapazi asanu kuya; sulk ndi pout zomwe zingakupatseni magaloni 500 amafuta ndi zina zambiri.

Chisoni chachikulu, tsopano, kuti chinsomba chomvetsa chisonichi chiyenera kukhala ndi milomo ya hare. Kupasukako kuli pafupi phazi kudutsa. Mwinamwake amayiwo panthawi yofunika kwambiri anali kuyenda panyanja pamphepete mwa nyanja ya Peru, pamene zivomezi zinachititsa kuti gombeli lisungunuke. Pa mlomo uwu, monga poterera poterera, tsopano tikulowa mkamwa. Pa mawu anga ndinali ku Mackinaw, ndiyenera kutenga izi kukhala mkati mwa Indian wigwam. Ambuye wabwino! Kodi iyi ndi njira imene Yona anapita? Denga liri pafupi mamita khumi ndi awiri m'litali, ndipo limathamangira ku ngodya yokongola kwambiri, ngati kuti pali mzati wokhazikika pamenepo; pomwe mbali zokhala ndi nthiti, zopindika, zaubweya, zimatipatsa ma slats odabwitsa, ofukula, owoneka ngati scimetar a whalebone, amati mazana atatu kumbali, zomwe kutengera kumtunda kwa mutu kapena fupa la korona, zimapanga akhungu a Venetian omwe. zina zatchulidwa mwachidwi. Mphepete mwa mafupawa amakhala ndi ulusi waubweya, womwe Whale Woyenera amathira madzi, ndipo m'njira zake zovuta amasunga nsomba zing'onozing'ono, akatsegula pakamwa amadutsa m'nyanja ya brit panthawi yodyera. Pakatikati pa mafupa a khungu, pamene amaima m'dongosolo lawo lachilengedwe, pali zizindikiro zina zochititsa chidwi, zokhotakhota, mabowo, ndi zitunda, zomwe zinanso zimawerengera zaka za cholengedwacho, monga zaka za thundu ndi mphete zake zozungulira. Ngakhale kutsimikizika kwa mulingo uwu sikungawonekere, komabe ili ndi fungo la kuthekera kofananira. Mulimonse mmene zingakhalire, ngati tilolera, tiyenera kupereka zaka zokulirapo kwambiri kwa Nangumi Woyenera kuposa mmene tingaonere poyamba.

M'nthawi zakale, zikuwoneka kuti zidakhalapo zokonda chidwi kwambiri zokhudzana ndi akhungu awa. Woyenda panyanja wina ku Purchas amawatcha “ndevu” zodabwitsa mkati mwa kamwa la namgumi; njonda yachitatu yachikulire ku Hackluyt imagwiritsa ntchito chinenero chokongola chotsatirachi: “Pali zipsepse pafupifupi mazana aŵiri ndi makumi asanu zomwe zimamera mbali iriyonse ya chiphuphu chake chapamwamba, chomwe chimapindirira lilime lake mbali iriyonse ya kamwa yake.”

*Izi zikutikumbutsa kuti Nangumi Woyenera ali ndi ndevu za mtundu wina, kapena kuti masharubu, opangidwa ndi tsitsi loyera lomwazika pang’ono kumtunda kwa nsagwada yakunja. Nthawi zina zibolibolizi zimasonyeza nkhope yake yaulemu.

Monga aliyense akudziwa, “zipsepse za nkhumba,” “zipsepse,” “ndevu,” “akhungu,” kapena chirichonse chimene mungafune, zimapatsa amayi mabasi awo ndi zinthu zina zowumitsa. Koma tsopano iwalani zonse za khungu ndi ndevu kwa kamphindi, ndipo, mutayima mkamwa mwa Nsomba Yabwino, yang'anani mozungulira inu mwatsopano. Kwa kapeti ku chiwalo tili ndi chiguduli chofewa kwambiri cha Turkey-lilime, lomwe limamatiridwa, titero, mpaka pansi pakamwa. Izi zisanachitike, muyenera kuti mwawona zowona za zomwe ndidayamba nazo - kuti Sperm Whale ndi Right Whale zili ndi mitu yosiyana kotheratu. Ngakhale mu Nangumi wa Umuna mulibe khungu lililonse la mafupa; palibe mlomo waukulu wapansi; ndipo ngakhale lilime lililonse. Apanso, Whale Yabwino ili ndi mabowo awiri akunja, Sperm Whale imodzi yokha.

Yang'anani kotsiriza, tsopano, pa mitu iyi yolemekezeka ya zisoti, pamene iwo akagona pamodzi; pakuti posachedwapa munthu adzamira, wosalembedwa, m’nyanja; winayo sadzakhala motalika kwambiri kutsatira.

Kodi mungagwire mawu a Sperm Whale's pamenepo? Ndizofanana ndi zomwe adamwalira nazo, makwinya ena ataliatali pamphumi akuwoneka tsopano akutha. Ndikuganiza kuti mphuno yake yotakata imakhala yodzaza ndi kukhumudwa ngati prairie, wobadwa ndi mphwayi wongoganizira za imfa. Koma chongani mawu a mutu winawo. MUTU 76. Kumenyetsa Nkhosa.

Nditasiya, kwanthawi yayitali, mutu wa Sperm Whale, ndikufuna inu, ngati katswiri wazamakhalidwe, mophweka - makamaka munene zakutsogolo kwake, pakusonkhanitsidwa kwake konse. Ndikufuna kuti mufufuze tsopano ndi lingaliro lokhalo lodzipangira nokha kuyerekezera kosakokomeza, kwanzeru kwa mphamvu iliyonse yomenyanitsa yomwe ingakhale pamenepo. Nayi mfundo yofunika kwambiri; pakuti muyenera kuthetsa nkhani imeneyi mokhutiritsa ndi inu nokha, kapena kukhalabe wosakhulupirira nthawi zonse pa chimodzi mwa zochitika zowopsya kwambiri, koma osati zochitika zenizeni zochepa, mwinamwake paliponse zomwe zimapezeka m'mbiri yonse yolembedwa.

Mukuwona kuti pamalo osambira wamba a Sperm Whale, kutsogolo kwa mutu wake kumapereka ndege yowongoka kwathunthu kumadzi; mukuwona kuti gawo lakumunsi lakutsogololo likutsetsereka kumbuyo kwambiri, kuti liperekenso malo ochulukirapo a socket yayitali yomwe imalandira nsagwada yapansi ngati boom; mukuwona kuti pakamwa pali pansi pamutu kwathunthu, mofananamo, ngati kuti pakamwa panu muli pansi pa chibwano chanu. Komanso mukuona kuti chinsomba chilibe mphuno yakunja; ndi kuti mphuno yake ili pamutu pake; mukuwona kuti maso ndi makutu ake ali m’mbali mwa mutu wake, pafupifupi gawo limodzi mwa magawo atatu a utali wake wonse kuchokera kutsogolo. Chifukwa chake, muyenera tsopano kuti mwazindikira kuti kutsogolo kwa mutu wa Nangumi wa Umuna ndi khoma lakufa, lakhungu, lopanda chiwalo chimodzi kapena kutchuka kwamtundu uliwonse. Kuonjezera apo, tsopano muyenera kulingalira kuti pokhapo mbali yowonjezereka, yapansi, yobwerera kumbuyo ya kutsogolo kwa mutu, pali kachigawo kakang'ono ka fupa; ndipo mpaka mutayandikira mapazi makumi awiri kuchokera pachipumi ndikufika pakukula kwathunthu. Kotero kuti unyinji wonsewu wopanda mafupa uli ngati wadi imodzi. Koma potsirizira pake, monga momwe zidzasonyezedwe posachedwapa, m'kati mwake muli mafuta osalimba kwambiri; komabe, tsopano muyenera kudziwitsidwa za chikhalidwe cha zinthu zomwe zimapatsa mphamvu zonse zowoneka bwino. CHAPTER 79. The Prairie.

To scan the lines of his face, or feel the bumps on the head of this Leviathan; this is a thing which no Physiognomist or Phrenologist has as yet undertaken. Such an enterprise would seem almost as hopeful as for Lavater to have scrutinized the wrinkles on the Rock of Gibraltar, or for Gall to have mounted a ladder and manipulated the Dome of the Pantheon. Still, in that famous work of his, Lavater not only treats of the various faces of men, but also attentively studies the faces of horses, birds, serpents, and fish; and dwells in detail upon the modifications of expression discernible therein. Nor have Gall and his disciple Spurzheim failed to throw out some hints touching the phrenological characteristics of other beings than man. Therefore, though I am but ill qualified for a pioneer, in the application of these two semi-sciences to the whale, I will do my endeavor. I try all things; I achieve what I can.

Physiognomically regarded, the Sperm Whale is an anomalous creature. He has no proper nose. And since the nose is the central and most conspicuous of the features; and since it perhaps most modifies and finally controls their combined expression; hence it would seem that its entire absence, as an external appendage, must very largely affect the countenance of the whale. For as in landscape gardening, a spire, cupola, monument, or tower of some sort, is deemed almost indispensable to the completion of the scene; so no face can be physiognomically in keeping without the elevated open-work belfry of the nose. Dash the nose from Phidias's marble Jove, and what a sorry remainder! Nevertheless, Leviathan is of so mighty a magnitude, all his proportions are so stately, that the same deficiency which in the sculptured Jove were hideous, in him is no blemish at all. Nay, it is an added grandeur. A nose to the whale would have been impertinent. As on your physiognomical voyage you sail round his vast head in your jolly-boat, your noble conceptions of him are never insulted by the reflection that he has a nose to be pulled. A pestilent conceit, which so often will insist upon obtruding even when beholding the mightiest royal beadle on his throne.

In some particulars, perhaps the most imposing physiognomical view to be had of the Sperm Whale, is that of the full front of his head. This aspect is sublime.

In thought, a fine human brow is like the East when troubled with the morning. In the repose of the pasture, the curled brow of the bull has a touch of the grand in it. Pushing heavy cannon up mountain defiles, the elephant's brow is majestic. Human or animal, the mystical brow is as that great golden seal affixed by the German emperors to their decrees. It signifies—“God: done this day by my hand.” But in most creatures, nay in man himself, very often the brow is but a mere strip of alpine land lying along the snow line. Few are the foreheads which like Shakespeare's or Melancthon's rise so high, and descend so low, that the eyes themselves seem clear, eternal, tideless mountain lakes; and all above them in the forehead's wrinkles, you seem to track the antlered thoughts descending there to drink, as the Highland hunters track the snow prints of the deer. But in the great Sperm Whale, this high and mighty god-like dignity inherent in the brow is so immensely amplified, that gazing on it, in that full front view, you feel the Deity and the dread powers more forcibly than in beholding any other object in living nature. For you see no one point precisely; not one distinct feature is revealed; no nose, eyes, ears, or mouth; no face; he has none, proper; nothing but that one broad firmament of a forehead, pleated with riddles; dumbly lowering with the doom of boats, and ships, and men. Nor, in profile, does this wondrous brow diminish; though that way viewed its grandeur does not domineer upon you so. In profile, you plainly perceive that horizontal, semi-crescentic depression in the forehead's middle, which, in man, is Lavater's mark of genius.

But how? Genius in the Sperm Whale? Has the Sperm Whale ever written a book, spoken a speech? No, his great genius is declared in his doing nothing particular to prove it. It is moreover declared in his pyramidical silence. And this reminds me that had the great Sperm Whale been known to the young Orient World, he would have been deified by their child-magian thoughts. They deified the crocodile of the Nile, because the crocodile is tongueless; and the Sperm Whale has no tongue, or at least it is so exceedingly small, as to be incapable of protrusion. If hereafter any highly cultured, poetical nation shall lure back to their birth-right, the merry May-day gods of old; and livingly enthrone them again in the now egotistical sky; in the now unhaunted hill; then be sure, exalted to Jove's high seat, the great Sperm Whale shall lord it.

Champollion deciphered the wrinkled granite hieroglyphics. But there is no Champollion to decipher the Egypt of every man's and every being's face. Physiognomy, like every other human science, is but a passing fable. If then, Sir William Jones, who read in thirty languages, could not read the simplest peasant's face in its profounder and more subtle meanings, how may unlettered Ishmael hope to read the awful Chaldee of the Sperm Whale's brow? I but put that brow before you. Read it if you can.

So like a choice casket is it secreted in him, that I have known some whalemen who peremptorily deny that the Sperm Whale has any other brain than that palpable semblance of one formed by the cubic-yards of his sperm magazine. Lying in strange folds, courses, and convolutions, to their apprehensions, it seems more in keeping with the idea of his general might to regard that mystic part of him as the seat of his intelligence.

It is plain, then, that phrenologically the head of this Leviathan, in the creature's living intact state, is an entire delusion. As for his true brain, you can then see no indications of it, nor feel any. The whale, like all things that are mighty, wears a false brow to the common world.

If you unload his skull of its spermy heaps and then take a rear view of its rear end, which is the high end, you will be struck by its resemblance to the human skull, beheld in the same situation, and from the same point of view. Indeed, place this reversed skull (scaled down to the human magnitude) among a plate of men's skulls, and you would involuntarily confound it with them; and remarking the depressions on one part of its summit, in phrenological phrase you would say—This man had no self-esteem, and no veneration. And by those negations, considered along with the affirmative fact of his prodigious bulk and power, you can best form to yourself the truest, though not the most exhilarating conception of what the most exalted potency is.

But if from the comparative dimensions of the whale's proper brain, you deem it incapable of being adequately charted, then I have another idea for you. If you attentively regard almost any quadruped's spine, you will be struck with the resemblance of its vertebræ to a strung necklace of dwarfed skulls, all bearing rudimental resemblance to the skull proper. It is a German conceit, that the vertebræ are absolutely undeveloped skulls. But the curious external resemblance, I take it the Germans were not the first men to perceive. A foreign friend once pointed it out to me, in the skeleton of a foe he had slain, and with the vertebræ of which he was inlaying, in a sort of basso-relievo, the beaked prow of his canoe. Now, I consider that the phrenologists have omitted an important thing in not pushing their investigations from the cerebellum through the spinal canal. For I believe that much of a man's character will be found betokened in his backbone. I would rather feel your spine than your skull, whoever you are. A thin joist of a spine never yet upheld a full and noble soul. I rejoice in my spine, as in the firm audacious staff of that flag which I fling half out to the world.

Apply this spinal branch of phrenology to the Sperm Whale. His cranial cavity is continuous with the first neck-vertebra; and in that vertebra the bottom of the spinal canal will measure ten inches across, being eight in height, and of a triangular figure with the base downwards. As it passes through the remaining vertebræ the canal tapers in size, but for a considerable distance remains of large capacity. Now, of course, this canal is filled with much the same strangely fibrous substance—the spinal cord—as the brain; and directly communicates with the brain. And what is still more, for many feet after emerging from the brain's cavity, the spinal cord remains of an undecreasing girth, almost equal to that of the brain. Under all these circumstances, would it be unreasonable to survey and map out the whale's spine phrenologically? For, viewed in this light, the wonderful comparative smallness of his brain proper is more than compensated by the wonderful comparative magnitude of his spinal cord.

But leaving this hint to operate as it may with the phrenologists, I would merely assume the spinal theory for a moment, in reference to the Sperm Whale's hump. This august hump, if I mistake not, rises over one of the larger vertebræ, and is, therefore, in some sort, the outer convex mould of it. From its relative situation then, I should call this high hump the organ of firmness or indomitableness in the Sperm Whale. And that the great monster is indomitable, you will yet have reason to know.
