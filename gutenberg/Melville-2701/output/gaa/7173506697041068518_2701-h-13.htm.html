YITSƆƆ 61. Stubb Gbe Ŋshɔŋloo Ko.

Kɛji akɛ kɛha Starbuck lɛ, Squid lɛ kpojiemɔ lɛ ji okadi nɔ ko lɛ, kɛha Queequeg lɛ eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto kwraa.

Kooloi awuiyelɔ lɛ kɛɛ, ni ekɛ e-harpoon lɛ miitswa elɛlɛ ni ewó nɔ lɛ hiɛ lɛ akɛ: “Kɛ́ ona lɛ ‘quid’ lɛ, no lɛ obaana lɛ oya akɛ ‘parm whale’.

Enɔ jetsɛremɔ lɛ, eyɛ dioo waa ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔ waa, ni akɛni nɔ ko krɛdɛɛ ko bɛ ni amɛkɛbaatsu nii hewɔ lɛ, Pequod lɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ nyɛɛɛ amɛte shi amɛwo wɔɔ ni ŋshɔ ni bɛ nɔ ko tamɔ nɛkɛ haa amɛwɔɔ lɛ. Ejaakɛ India Ŋshɔ lɛ fã nɛɛ ni wɔtsɔɔ mli yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ jeee nɔ ni ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nilelɔi tsɛɔ lɛ akɛ shikpɔŋ ni wala yɔɔ nɔ; ni ji akɛ, ehaa anaa porpoises, dolphins, flying-fish, kɛ kooloi krokomɛi ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni hiɔ nu ni mli wa waa lɛ amli lɛ fioo ko pɛ, fe nɔ ni yɔɔ Rio de la Plata, loo ŋshɔnaa shikpɔŋ ni yɔɔ Peru lɛ naa lɛ.

Eshɛ minɔ akɛ madamɔ foremast-yitso lɛ naa; ni mikɛ mitsitsii fĩ maŋtsɛ atadei ni egbɔjɔ lɛ ahe lɛ, mikɛ mihe fɔ̃ɔ nɔ ko ni tamɔ kɔɔyɔɔ ni akɛ ŋmiŋmi fee lɛ mli kɛyaa kɛbaa. Yiŋkpɛɛ ko bɛ ni baanyɛ adamɔ naa; yɛ nakai lamɔ mli henumɔ ni mihe ejɔ mi lɛ mli lɛ, naagbee lɛ misusuma je migbɔmɔtso lɛ mli; eyɛ mli akɛ migbɔmɔtso lɛ tee nɔ ekpokpo lolo tamɔ bɔ ni pendulum baafee lɛ moŋ, shi be kakadaŋŋ sɛɛ beni ajie hewalɛ ni tsirɛ lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ.

Beni hiɛkpamɔ kwraa ba minɔ lɛ, no mli lɛ miyɔse akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ wulu lɛ kɛ mizzen-mast-head lɛ nɔ lɛ ehiɛ edɔ momo. No hewɔ lɛ, naagbee lɛ wɔyi etɛ lɛ fɛɛ wɔfo kɛjɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli ni wala bɛ mli, ni yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli ŋshɔ lɛ fɛɛ ni wɔfeɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni ewɔ lɛ tsɔɔ shishi kɛjɛɔ shishi. Ŋshɔkei lɛ hu tsɔɔ amɛyitsei ni bɛ hewalɛ lɛ anɔ; ni yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli ŋwɛi ŋta ni mli kwɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, bokagbɛ tsɔɔ anaigbɛ, ni hulu lɛ yeɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ nɔ.

Trukaa lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ŋmeii miitswa yɛ mihiŋmɛii ni miŋa lɛ ashishi; tamɔ nibii fɔji miniji mɔmɔ atadei lɛ amli; heyeli ko ni anaaa, ni mli hi lɛ bu mihe; kɛ shoofeemɔ ko miku misɛɛ kɛba wala mli ekoŋŋ. Ni naa! ni bɛŋkɛ wɔ ŋshɔkei lɛ ashishi, ni kɛ wɔ teŋ jɛkɛmɔ shɛɛɛ ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai ejwɛ lɛ, Sperm Whale wulu ko kã shi ni ekɛ ehe miibɔle nu lɛ mli tamɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ ko ni efo lɛ, esɛɛ ni mli kwɔ ni kpɛɔ, ni ji Etiopia su, ni miikpɛ yɛ hulu la lɛ mli tamɔ ashwishwɛ. Shi beni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ miiho yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli, ni ekɛ e-vapory jet lɛ miiho yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli be fɛɛ be lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ tamɔ portly burgher ni miishɛre epaipi ni ekɛtsu nii yɛ shwane ni mli edɔ lɛ mli lɛ. Shi nakai paipii lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ohiafo lɛ, ji onaagbee nɔ. Taakɛ eji akɛ ŋwɛibɔfo ko tso lɛ etswa lɛ lɛ, lɛlɛ ni ewɔ lɛ kɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ni ewɔ yɛ mli lɛ fɛɛ je shishi amɛte shi shikome; ni gbeei ni fa fe nyɔŋmai abɔ ni jɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fãi fɛɛ amli, kɛ gbeei etɛ ni jɛ ŋwɛi lɛ yɛ be kome mli lɛ, bolɔ bɔ ni amɛfɔɔ gbee lɛ, beni loo wulu lɛ shwieɔ nu ni mli tse lɛ mli fiofio kɛyaa kɔɔyɔɔ mli daa lɛ.

"Jiemɔ lɛlɛi lɛ amli! Luff!" Ahab bolɔ. Ni akɛni ebo lɛ diɛŋtsɛ efamɔ toi hewɔ lɛ, ekɛ lɛlɛ kudɔmɔ tsɔne lɛ shwie shi dani lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ nyɛ emɔ lɛlɛ kudɔmɔ tsɔne lɛ mli.

Ekolɛ bɔ ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi lɛ bolɔɔ trukaa lɛ ha ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he fee lɛ gbeyei; ni dani lɛlɛi lɛ baa shi, ni ekɛ anunyam miitsɔ ehe lɛ, efo kɛtee kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ naa, shi kɛ toiŋjɔlɛ ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ, ni ekɛ ŋshɔkei fioo komɛi pɛ fee beni efoɔ lɛ, ni beni esusu akɛ ekolɛ ebaafee gbeyei lolo lɛ, Ahab wo famɔ akɛ akakɛ lɛlɛ ni akɛwoɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ atsu nii, ni esaaa akɛ mɔ ko mɔ ko wieɔ ja yɛ ŋmɔlɔ mli. No hewɔ lɛ, beni wɔtara lɛlɛi lɛ a-gunwales lɛ anɔ tamɔ Ontario Indiabii lɛ, wɔfo oya shi wɔfee dioo; toiŋjɔlɛ ni akpɛlɛɛɛ lɛlɛ ni bɛ gbee ni ato lɛ nɔ. Amrɔ nɛɛ, beni wɔmiiho yɛ nɛkɛ gbɛ nɔ kɛmiitiu wɔ lɛ, kooloo wulu lɛ tsɔ etsitsi nɔ efo kɛtee kɔɔyɔɔ mli ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai ejwɛ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ efo mli ni enaaa lɛ tamɔ ŋwɛitsu ko ni emi.

“Flukii yaa!” ji bolɔmɔ lɛ, adafitswaa ko ni nyiɛ sɛɛ amrɔ nɔŋŋ ni Stubb jie e-match lɛ kpo ni ekɛ epaipi lɛ shã la, ejaakɛ amrɔ nɛɛ aha lɛ hejɔɔmɔ. Beni egbɛɛmɔ lɛ mli be muu lɛ fɛɛ ho lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ te shi ekoŋŋ, ni akɛni amrɔ nɛɛ etsɔ tawashɛrelɔ lɛ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ, ni ebɛŋkɛ lɛ kwraa fe ekrokomɛi lɛ ateŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ hewɔ lɛ, Stubb bu woo ni akɛbaamɔ lɛ lɛ he akɔntaa. Amrɔ nɛɛ, eka shi faŋŋ akɛ, yɛ be kakadaŋŋ mli lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ele mɛi ni nyiɛɔ esɛɛ lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, hiɛdɔɔ he dioofeemɔ fɛɛ he ehiaaa dɔŋŋ. Akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔnei shwie shi, ni ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔnei ni akɛwoɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ ba shwɛmɔ mli waa. Ni beni Stubb miitswa epaipi lɛ lolo lɛ, ekɛ miishɛɛ wo egbɛfalɔi lɛ ahiɛ koni amɛbatutua lɛ.

Hɛɛ, tsakemɔ kpele ko eba loo lɛ nɔ. Eyɛ wala mli fɛɛ kɛyashi oshara ni eyɔɔ lɛ mli, ni eeya “eyitso”; nakai fã ni jeɔ kpo kɛjɛɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni ekɛfee lɛ mli lɛ.*

*Abaana yɛ he kroko nɔ ni ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ mlɛo waa ni yɔɔ sperm whale lɛ yitso wulu lɛ mli fɛɛ lɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ etamɔ nɔ ni eji nɔ ni da fe fɛɛ moŋ, shi eji nɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli fe fɛɛ yɛ ehe. Bɔni afee ni ekɛ mlɛoyeli awo nɔ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli, ni efeɔ nakai be fɛɛ be kɛ́ eeya oya fe fɛɛ. Kɛfata he lɛ, nakai ji bɔ ni eyitso hiɛ fã ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ mlilɛɛmɔ ji, ni nakai ji bɔ ni nu ni afo mli ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ fã lɛ mli lɛ ji, ni kɛtsɔ eyitso ni ewoɔ nɔ yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛɛ akɛ etsake ehe kɛjɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni ekotoɔ shi ni etsɔ New York lɛlɛ kudɔmɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ mli.

Stubb bolɔ, ni ekɛ lasu lɛ shwieɔ lasu lɛ nɔ beni ewieɔ lɛ akɛ: “Nyɛjea shishi, mihii! “Jee lɛ shishi, bianɛ; ha amɛ strok kakadaŋŋ ni mli wa lɛ, Tashtego. Je lɛ shishi, Tash, mibi nuu—je lɛ shishi, fɛɛ; shi ha ohe ajɔ bo, ha ohe ajɔ—kukumba ji wiemɔ lɛ—mlɛo, mlɛo—jeɔ lɛ shishi pɛ tamɔ gbele ni yɔɔ gbeyei kɛ daimonioi ni ŋmɔɔ, ni otee gbohii ni afu amɛ lɛ ashi, ni amɛte shi amɛwo gbohii ni afu amɛ lɛ fɛɛ—amɛ gbohii lɛ fɛɛ. e!"

"Woo-hoo! Wa-hee!" Gay-Header lɛ bolɔ yɛ hetoo mli, ni ewo tawuu-gbee momo ko nɔ kɛtee ŋwɛi; be mli ni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ fɛɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ ni mli ewo wu lɛ mli lɛ jɛɛɛ etsui mli efoɔ kɛyaa hiɛ kɛ tsɔne kome ni fe fɛɛ ni Indianyo ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ lɛ kɛha lɛ lɛ.

Shi mɛi krokomɛi hu ha ebolɔmɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ hetoo tamɔ bɔ ni ebolɔɔ waa lɛ nɔŋŋ. "Kee-hee! Kee-hee!" Daggoo bolɔ, ni ekɛ ehe miiwo hiɛ kɛ sɛɛ yɛ esɛi lɛ nɔ, tamɔ jata ni nyiɛɔ yɛ etsu lɛ mli.

"K-la! Kɔɔ-loo!" Queequeg bolɔ, tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ enaabu miitswa Grenadier tsina loo ni eyi enaa lɛ nɔ. Ni nakai ji bɔ ni kɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔnei kɛ bolɔmɔi lɛ shwieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli. Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, Stubb ni hiɛ egbɛhe yɛ tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ, woɔ ehii lɛ hewalɛ lolo koni amɛje shishi, ni be fɛɛ be lɛ, ekɛ lasu ni jɛɔ enaa lɛ shwieɔ shi. Tamɔ mɛi ni enijiaŋ eje wui lɛ, amɛgbala ni amɛtsi amɛhe, kɛyashi anu gbee ni akɛhereɔ mɔ hiɛmɛɛ lɛ—“Te shi, Tashtego!—ha lɛ!” Akɛ harpoon lɛ shwie shi. “Stern fɛɛ!” Mɛi ni fãa lɛlɛ lɛ fiɔ nu sɛɛ; nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ lɛ nɔ ko mli edɔ ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ tswa amɛnine fɛɛ nine nɔ. No ji majik line lɛ. Be fioo dani no baashɛ lɛ, Stubb kɛ oyaiyeli emɔ sɛɛkuu krokomɛi enyɔ ni ekɛbɔle tso lɛ yitso lɛ he, ni yɛ bɔ ni ebɔleɔ ehe oya lɛ hewɔ lɛ, hempen bluu lasu ko je kpo agbɛnɛ ni ekɛ lasu ni jɛɔ epaipi lɛ mli lɛ futu. Beni tsɔne lɛ hoɔ kɛbɔleɔ tso lɛ yitso lɛ he lɛ; nakai nɔŋŋ hu, dani ebaashɛ nakai he lɛ, etsɔ Stubb niji enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ mli ni etsɔ mli ni etsɔ ŋmɔtɔ mli, ni nine-atadei, loo ŋmɔtɔ ni akɛfee ŋmɔtɔ ni akɛwoɔ bei komɛi amli yɛ nɛkɛ bei nɛɛ amli lɛ mli ŋmɔtɔi lɛ jɛ mli yɛ oshara mli. Etamɔ nɔ ni ohiɛ henyɛlɔ ko klante ni hiɛ ŋwɛi enyɔ ni naa wa lɛ mli, ni nakai henyɛlɔ lɛ miibɔ mɔdɛŋ be fɛɛ be koni ejie kɛjɛ odɛŋ.

"Fɔmɔ line lɛ! fɔmɔ line lɛ!" Stubb bolɔ etsɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔne kudɔlɔ lɛ (lɛ eta ŋshɔ lɛ naa) ni ejie etsitsi lɛ, ni ekɛ ŋshɔ mli nu shwie mli.* Atsake ŋshɔ lɛ mli babaoo, bɔni afee ni ŋshɔ lɛ bɔi egbɛhe lɛ mlihimɔ. Agbɛnɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fo kɛtsɔ nu ni mli edɔ lɛ mli tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni etsitsii fɛɛ yɔɔ mli. Stubb kɛ Tashtego tsake amɛhe yɛ biɛ—stem kɛha stern—jarayeli ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ diɛŋtsɛ yɛ nakai basabasafeemɔ ni hosoɔ lɛ mli.

*Kɛha bɔ ni nifeemɔ nɛɛ he hiaa ha lɛ fã ko lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛɛ yɛ biɛ, akɛ, yɛ Dutch wuoyeli tsutsu lɛ mli lɛ, akɛ mop tsuɔ nii ni akɛ nu shwieɔ ŋshɔ ni hoɔ lɛ nɔ; yɛ lɛlɛi krokomɛi babaoo amli lɛ, atoɔ tso piggin, loo bailer he gbɛjianɔ ahaa nakai yiŋtoo lɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, otsitsi lɛ ji nɔ ni hi fe fɛɛ.

Kɛjɛ tsɔne ni hosoɔ ni tsɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ ŋwɛigbɛ fã lɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ nɔ, kɛjɛ bɔ ni amrɔ nɛɛ eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ fe saŋku tsɔne lɛ nɔ lɛ, kulɛ obaasusu akɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yɛ ŋmeii enyɔ—ekome gbalaa nu lɛ mli, ekroko lɛ gbalaa kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli—beni lɛlɛ lɛ tsɔɔ nibii enyɔ ni teɔ shi woɔ amɛhe lɛ amli shikome lɛ. Cascade ko ni yaa nɔ ashwɛɔ yɛ gãimlibii lɛ ahe; ŋshɔkei ni shwieɔ shi ni ekpaaa yɛ ehiɛ; ni, kɛji akɛ ajie ehe fioo kɛjɛ emli, shi jeee kɛtsɔ nine bibioo ko nɔ po lɛ, lɛlɛ ni hosoɔ ni egbɛɔ eshwaa lɛ shwieɔ e-gunwale ni shwieɔ shi lɛ nɔ kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ mli. Nakai ji bɔ ni amɛfee oya; nuu fɛɛ nuu kɛ hewalɛ kɛ titri ni kɛ ehe kpɛtɛɔ esɛi lɛ he, koni akashwie lɛ kɛya ŋmɔtɔ lɛ mli; kɛ Tashtego su kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ tsɔne kudɔmɔ tsɔne lɛ he lɛ ni ekotoɔ aaafee shii enyɔ, bɔni afee ni ekɛ e-center of gravity lɛ aba shi. Etamɔ nɔ ni Atlantik kɛ Pasifik Ŋshɔi lɛ fɛɛ ho beni amɛtuɔ tu yɛ amɛ gbɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, kɛyashi naagbee lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ha efomɔ lɛ ba shi fioo.

“Gbalamɔ mli—gbalamɔ mli!” Stubb bolɔ etsɔɔ gãimlibi lɛ! ni, ni amɛkwɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he kɛkpe lɛ, niji fɛɛ bɔi lɛlɛ lɛ gbalamɔ kɛmiiya eŋɔɔ, yɛ be mli ni loloolo lɛ, agbalaa lɛlɛ lɛ kɛyaa lɛ. Etsɛɛɛ ni Stubb tsɔ esɛɛ kɛtee esɛɛ, ni ekɛ enaneshi shiŋŋ wo tsɔne ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ mli, ni ekɛ gãimlibii tswa gãimlibii kɛtee gãimlibii amli kɛbote loo ni miiho lɛ mli; yɛ famɔ wiemɔ lɛ naa lɛ, lɛlɛ lɛ tsakeɔ ehe kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ gbɛ lɛ nɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ eyaa ŋwɛi kɛyaa ŋshɔŋloo kroko.

Agbɛnɛ ŋshɔ tsuru lɛ ho kɛjɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he fɛɛ he tamɔ faai ni hoɔ kɛjɛɔ gɔŋ ko nɔ. Egbɔmɔtso ni apila lɛ lɛ shwieee shi yɛ nu ni mli edɔ mli, shi moŋ yɛ lá mli, ni shwieɔ shi ni eshwieɔ shi kɛyaa furlongs yɛ amɛsɛɛ yɛ amɛsɛɛ. Hulu ni shwieɔ shi ni miitswa yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli nubu tsuru nɛɛ nɔ lɛ, tsu ekpɛtɛmɔ lɛ ku esɛɛ kɛtee hiɛ fɛɛ hiɛ nɔ, bɔni afee ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛkpɛ amɛhe tamɔ hii tsuji. Ni yɛ be nɛɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, lasu yɛŋ ni jɛɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ŋshɔŋ lɛ mli lɛ tswaa yɛ piŋmɔ mli, ni lasu ni naa wa jɛɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ yitso ni miishɛɛ yɔɔ ehe lɛ daaŋ; taakɛ eji yɛ dart fɛɛ dart mli, ni ekɛ e-lance ni ebɔle ehe lɛ miigba mli (kɛtsɔ kpãa ni akɛkpɛtɛ he lɛ nɔ lɛ), Stubb jajeɔ lɛ shii abɔ, kɛtsɔ oyaiyeli fioo komɛi ni ekɛtswaa gunwale lɛ nɔ, kɛkɛ ni ekɛyaa ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli shii abɔ.

“Gbalamɔ kɛya ŋwɛi—gbalamɔ kɛya ŋwɛi!” agbɛnɛ ebolɔ etsɛ gãimlibii lɛ, beni ŋshɔŋloo ni miiba shi lɛ jɔɔ ehe yɛ emlifu lɛ mli lɛ. “Gbalamɔ kɛya ŋwɛi!—bɛŋkɛ!” ni lɛlɛ lɛ nyiɛ loo lɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa. Beni Stubb shɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa shɔŋŋ lɛ, ekɛ e-lance kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ lɛ shwie loo lɛ mli fiofio, ni ekɛto jɛmɛ, ni ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ miitswa ni ekɛmiitswa, tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ miitao ni enu shika tsuru watsi ko ni ekolɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ emi lɛ sɛɛ, ni esheɔ gbeyei akɛ ebaakumɔ dani enyɛ ejie lɛ. Shi nakai shika tsuru watsi ni etaoɔ lɛ ji loo lɛ mli wala ni yɔɔ mli fe fɛɛ lɛ. Ni agbɛnɛ atswa lɛ; ejaakɛ, kɛjɛ e-trance lɛ mli kɛtee nakai nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ awie he ni atsɛɔ lɛ “flurry” lɛ mli lɛ, kooloo lɛ tsɔ ela lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɔ, ni ekɛ ŋmɔtɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔ mli, ni mli efu, ni mli edɔ ha ehe, bɔni afee ni lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ oshara mli lɛ, ni shwieɔ shi amrɔ nɔŋŋ kɛyaa kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ, ná shwilafoi babaoo koni ekɛ shwilafoi abɔ mɔdɛŋ ni ekɛje nakai hulutsoo be lɛ mli la lɛ mli.

Ni agbɛnɛ ni egbɔjɔ yɛ e-flurry lɛ mli lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ shwie shi shikome ekoŋŋ kɛba mɛi ahiɛ; ni shwieɔ shi kɛjɛɔ he kome kɛyaa he kroko; spasmodically dilating ni ekɛ e-spout-hole lɛ shwieɔ shi, kɛ mumɔi ni mli wa, ni egbɛɔ eshwaa, ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli. Yɛ naagbee lɛ, lá tsuru ni ebɔle ehe lɛ hoɔ yɛ sɛɛ mli, tamɔ nɔ ni eji wain tsuru mli ŋmɔtɔi ni tsuɔ tamɔ ŋmɔtɔ lɛ, ni etswa kɛtee kɔɔyɔɔ ni woɔ mɔ he gbeyei lɛ mli; ni egbee shi ekoŋŋ, ni ejo foi ni ekɛ etsitsii ni tsiii ehe lɛ miiho kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ mli. No mli lɛ, etsui etswa!

Daggoo kɛɛ akɛ: “Egbo, Owula Stubb.”

“Hɛɛ; paipii enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ shwie lasu!” ni beni ejie lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enɔ lɛ kɛjɛ enaabu mli lɛ, Stubb shɛrɛ sũ ni egbo lɛ eshwã nu lɛ nɔ; ni, be fioo ko lɛ, edamɔ shi ni esusu gbonyo wulu ni efee lɛ he.





YITSƆƆ 62. Dart lɛ.

Wiemɔ ko ni kɔɔ sane ko ni ba yɛ naagbee yitso lɛ mli lɛ he.

Taakɛ bɔ ni akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛ nitsumɔ lɛ tsuɔ nii ni tsakeee lɛ tsɔɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛ lɛ tsiɔ ehe kɛjɛɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni eyitso loo ŋshɔŋlooi-gbelɔ lɛ ji be kukuoo mli kudɔlɔ, ni harpooneer loo whale-fastener lɛ gbalaa hiɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ, nɔ ni ale lɛ akɛ harpooneer-oar lɛ. Amrɔ nɛɛ ehe miihia ni ekɛ nine ni mli wa ni he yɔɔ gbeyei atswa klɛŋklɛŋ dade lɛ awo loo lɛ mli; ejaakɛ bei pii lɛ, yɛ nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ dart kakadaŋŋ lɛ mli lɛ, esa akɛ ashɛrɛ dɛŋdade ni tsii lɛ ashwie shi kɛyashɛ he ko ni kɛ jɛmɛ jɛkɛmɔ ji ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai enyɔ loo nyɔŋmai etɛ. Shi bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni sɛɛtiumɔ lɛ heɔ be babaoo ni ehaa mɔ he jɔ̃ɔ ehe lɛ, akpaa gbɛ akɛ harpooneer lɛ baagbala e-oar lɛ yɛ nakai beaŋ kɛyashɛ naagbee; lɛɛlɛŋ, akpaa gbɛ akɛ ebaafee nitsumɔ ni fe adesa nɔ lɛ he nɔkwɛmɔnɔ eha mɛi krokomɛi lɛ, jeee kɛtsɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔne ni akɛfoɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ pɛ nɔ, shi moŋ kɛtsɔ gbee ni wa ni ekɛ ekãa tswaa shii abɔ lɛ nɔ; kɛ nɔ ni eji akɛ mɔ ko baaya nɔ abolɔ yɛ ekɔmpas lɛ yiteŋ, yɛ be mli ni fãji krokomɛi lɛ fɛɛ egbɔjɔ ni fã ko eje shishi—nɔ ni ji nakai lɛ, mɔ ko mɔ ko leee ja mɛi ni eka lɛ amɛkwɛ. Nɔ kome ji akɛ, minyɛɛɛ majɛ mitsui mli mabolɔ waa ni matsu nii yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ yɛ be kome mli. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ nɛkɛ shihilɛ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli, ni ekɛ esɛɛ tsɔɔ loo lɛ mli lɛ, shikome pɛ lɛ, harpoon tswalɔ ni etɔ lɛ lɛ nuɔ bɔlɔmɔ ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ lɛ—“Te shi, ni okɛha lɛ!” Agbɛnɛ esa akɛ ekɛ e-oar lɛ afɔ shi ni ekɛfi shi, ni etsɔ ehe yɛ e-teŋgbɛ gbɛ lɛ fã, ni emɔ e-harpoon lɛ kɛjɛ etsitsi nɔ, ni ekɛ hewalɛ fioo ni eshwɛ lɛ aka akɛ ekɛbaawo ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ. Ebɛ naakpɛɛ akɛ, kɛ akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi akuu muu lɛ fɛɛ wo gbɔmɔtso kome mli lɛ, akɛ yɛ hegbɛi nyɔŋmai enumɔ ni sa kɛha dart lɛ mli lɛ, jeee enumɔ yeɔ omanye; ebɛ naakpɛɛ akɛ alomɔ harpooneers babaoo ni bɛ miishɛɛ lɛ ni amɛhe ejɔ amɛ yɛ shwilafo mli; ebɛ naakpɛɛ akɛ amɛteŋ mɛi komɛi tswa amɛlá fãji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli; ebɛ naakpɛɛ akɛ sperm whalebii komɛi bɛ jɛmɛ afii ejwɛ kɛ tsɛnsii ejwɛ; ebɛ naakpɛɛ akɛ yɛ lɛlɛtsɛmɛi babaoo agbɛfaŋ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ ji nɔ ko ni sɛɛ efo kɛkɛ; ejaakɛ harpooneer lɛ ji mɔ ni fãa gbɛ lɛ, ni kɛji ojie mumɔ kɛjɛ egbɔmɔtso lɛ mli lɛ te ooofee tɛŋŋ okpa gbɛ akɛ obaana lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ be mli ni mɛi babaoo taoɔ lɛ!

Shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, kɛji akɛ dart lɛ ye omanye lɛ, no lɛ yɛ be ni he hiaa waa ni ji enyɔ lɛ mli, ni ji, beni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ jeɔ foijee shishi lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ kɛ harpooneer lɛ hu jeɔ shishi amɛjoɔ foi kɛyaa hiɛ kɛ sɛɛ, kɛyaa oshara ni baa amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe kɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ nɔ lɛ nɔ. No mli lɛ amɛtsakeɔ amɛhe; ni lɛlɛ lɛ nɔkwɛlɔ lɛ, ni ji lɛlɛ bibioo lɛ nɔkwɛlɔ lɛ, heɔ egbɛhe ni sa yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, misusuuu mɔ ni hiɛɔ nɔ ni teɔ shi woɔ enɛ lɛ mli lɛ he, shi enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ ji kwashiai asane kɛ nɔ ni he ehiaaa. Esa akɛ yitso lɛ ahi ŋwɛibɔfoi lɛ amli kɛjɛ klɛŋklɛŋ kɛyashi naagbee; esa akɛ etswa ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ fɛɛ, ni ekɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ akafo nɔ ko ni esa akɛ akpa gbɛ kɛjɛ edɛŋ, ja yɛ shihilɛi ni yɔɔ faŋŋ kɛha wuolɛɛlɔ fɛɛ wuolɛɛlɔ. Mile akɛ bei komɛi lɛ, enɛ baaha oyaiyeli ni baa shi fioo yɛ sɛɛtiumɔ mli lɛ aba; shi niiashikpamɔ ni mikɛkpe be kakadaŋŋ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi srɔtoi ni jɛ maji ni fe ekome mli lɛ amli lɛ eha miná nɔmimaa akɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhe lɛ mli shigbeemɔi babaoo amli lɛ, jeee ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ oyaiyeli lɛ ji nɔ ni kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ahe nitsumɔ ni atsɔɔ mli kɛtsɔ hiɛ lɛ ji nɔ ni kɛ amɛba lɛ.

Bɔni afee ni anyɛ atsu nii jogbaŋŋ fe fɛɛ yɛ dart lɛ mli lɛ, esa akɛ je nɛɛ mli harpooneers lɛ aje amɛnaji anɔ kɛjɛ nitsumɔ ni amɛfeee lɛ mli, shi jeee kɛjɛ nitsumɔ ni amɛtsuɔ lɛ mli.





YITSƆƆ 63. Krɔt lɛ.

Niji lɛ jɛɔ tso lɛ shishi; kɛjɛ amɛmli lɛ, tsei tsei lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ saji otii ni woɔ yibii amli lɛ, ha yitsei lɛ aya hiɛ.

Krotch ni awie he yɛ baafa ko ni eho lɛ sa akɛ atsĩ tã yɛ heyeli mli. Eji tso ko ni afolɔ ni yɔɔ srɔto, ni elɛɛmɔ aaafee ninetalɔi enyɔ, ni akɛwoɔ ŋwɛigbɛ tu lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni ja nɔ ni bɛŋkɛ ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ lɛ mli, kɛ yiŋtoo akɛ ekɛ hejɔɔmɔ aha harpoon lɛ naagbee ni akɛ tso fee lɛ, ni enaagbee kroko ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ lɛ jeɔ kpo kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, tawuu nii lɛ shɛɔ mɔ ni shwieɔ lɛ lɛ dɛŋ amrɔ nɔŋŋ, ni mɔ lɛ hu shwieɔ lɛ oya kɛjɛɔ he ni eyɔɔ lɛ tamɔ bɔ ni sɛɛtsulɔ ko shwieɔ etu lɛ kɛjɛɔ gbogbo lɛ he lɛ. Eji kusum nifeemɔ akɛ aŋmɛɔ dade enyɔ ni akɛwoɔ mɔ mli lɛ he, ni atsɛɔ amɛ dade klɛŋklɛŋ kɛ dade ni ji enyɔ lɛ.

Shi nɛkɛ ŋtayaai enyɔ nɛɛ, ni eko fɛɛ eko kɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ekpãa lɛ, fɛɛ kɛ kpãa lɛ kpãa gbee; nɔ ni yɔɔ mli ji enɛ: ni akɛ amɛyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ, kɛji ebaahi lɛ, atswa amɛ eko sɛɛ amrɔ nɔŋŋ awo ŋshɔŋloo kome mli; koni kɛji akɛ, yɛ drag ni baa lɛ mli lɛ, eko ajie lɛ, ekroko lɛ anyɛ ahiɛ mli lolo. Eji hegbɛi ni yɔɔ lɛ mlijaa enyɔ. Shi bei pii lɛ ebaa lɛ akɛ, yɛ bɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ joɔ foi amrɔ nɔŋŋ, ni ekɛ yiwalɛ tsuɔ nii, ni ehaa mɔ he jɔ̃ɔ ehe beni enine shɛɔ klɛŋklɛŋ dade lɛ nɔ lɛ hewɔ lɛ, ebafeɔ nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsu he nii aha harpooneer lɛ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni efeɔ tamɔ sarawagbamɔ yɛ egbɛfaa mli lɛ, ni ekɛ dade ni ji enyɔ lɛ awo emli. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, akɛni dade ni ji enyɔ lɛ kɛ tsɔne lɛ ebɔ momo, ni tsɔne lɛ miiho hewɔ lɛ, esa akɛ, yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, ashɛrɛ nakai tawuu nii lɛ kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli kɛ hiɛnɔkamɔ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ kɛ he ko; kɛ jeee nakai lɛ, oshara ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ lɛ baakɔ niji fɛɛ he. Tumbled kɛtee nu lɛ mli, lɛ yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ yɛ shihilɛi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli; spare coils of box line (ni atsĩ tã yɛ yitso ko ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ mli) ni haa nɛkɛ nifeemɔ nɛɛ, yɛ bei pii amli lɛ, anyɛɔ atsuɔ he nii yɛ hiɛshikamɔ mli. Shi jeee be fɛɛ be nɛkɛ nifeemɔ ni he hiaa waa nɛɛ ji nɔ ni mɔ ko mɔ ko kwɛɛɛ nɔ ni mɛi ni yeɔ awerɛho fe fɛɛ ni gbeɔ mɛi fe fɛɛ lɛ baa.

Agbɛnɛ hu: esa akɛ ole akɛ kɛ́ ashɛrɛ dade ni ji enyɔ lɛ ashwie ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, kɛjɛ nakai beaŋ kɛyaa lɛ ebafeɔ gbeyeishemɔ ko ni shwieɔ shi, ni ehiɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ shwieɔ lɛlɛ lɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ fɛɛ he, ni ekɛ ŋmeii lɛ ebɔleɔ amɛhe, loo efoɔ amɛ, ni ehaa mɔ nuɔ he waa yɛ he fɛɛ he. Ni asaŋ, yɛ gbɛ ni da nɔ lɛ, anyɛŋ atswa lɛ shi ekoŋŋ kɛyashi be mli ni abaamɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ jogbaŋŋ ni efee gbonyo.

Agbɛnɛ susumɔ bɔ ni esa akɛ efee yɛ lɛlɛi ejwɛ ni fɛɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋloo kome ni he wa, ni tsuɔ nii waa, ni le nii jogbaŋŋ lɛ miiwu lɛ he okwɛ; beni yɛ sui nɛɛ ni yɔɔ emli lɛ ahewɔ, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu osharai akpei abɔ ni kɛ amɛhe kpãa gbee ni jɛɔ nitsumɔ ni yɔɔ ekãa ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli baa lɛ hewɔ lɛ, dade ni ji sɛkɛndii ekpaa loo nyɔŋma ni etse ehe lɛ baanyɛ abɔle ehe yɛ be kome mli. Ejaakɛ, yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi babaoo haa lɛlɛ fɛɛ lɛlɛ koni ekɛkoto ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ kɛji akɛ atswa klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ lɛ ni etsuuu nii jogbaŋŋ ni ekɛ ehe wooo mli. Akɛ anɔkwayeli gbaa nɛkɛ saji nɛɛ fɛɛ he sane yɛ biɛ, ejaakɛ amɛbaafee nɔ ni tsɔɔ kukuji babaoo ni he hiaa fe fɛɛ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni amɛhe yɔɔ mlɛo ha lɛ mli, yɛ nibii ni baaba yɛ sɛɛ mli ni abaatsɔɔ mli lɛ amli.





YITSƆƆ 64. Stubb Gbɛkɛ Niyenii lɛ.

Agbe Stubb ŋshɔŋloo lɛ yɛ he ko ni kɛ lɛlɛ lɛ teŋ jɛkɛmɔ fioo. Eji he ko ni yɔɔ kpoo; no hewɔ lɛ, beni wɔfee lɛlɛi etɛ ni feɔ ekome lɛ, wɔje nitsumɔ ni yaa nɔ blɛoo ni ji trofi lɛ ni agbalaa kɛyaa Pequod lɛ shishi. Ni agbɛnɛ, beni wɔ hii nyɔŋma kɛ kpaanyɔ ni hiɛ wɔ niji nyɔŋmai etɛ kɛ enumɔ, kɛ wɔ tsitsii kɛ wɔ niji oha kɛ nyɔŋmai kpaanyɔ lɛ, wɔtsuɔ nii fiofio ŋmɛlɛtswaa kɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa yɛ nakai gbonyo ni bɛ hewalɛ, ni egbɔjɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ nɔ lɛ; ni etamɔ nɔ ni etsiii ehe kwraa, ja yɛ be kakadaŋŋ mli; atsɔ enɛ nɔ akɛ odaseyeli kpakpa ha yɛ bɔ ni mɛi babaoo ni wɔfã kɛtee lɛ fa babaoo ha lɛ he. Ejaakɛ, yɛ Hang-Ho faa wulu lɛ nɔ, loo nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni amɛtsɛɔ lɛ, yɛ China lɛ, nitsulɔi ejwɛ loo enumɔ ni yɔɔ nane-gbɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ baajie jwɛi ni akɛwoɔ jatsu mli ni fa babaoo yɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa kome mli kilomita kome; shi nɛkɛ argosy kpeteŋkpele nɛɛ ni wɔgbala lɛ mli wa waa, tamɔ nɔ ni akɛ tsina-lead ewo mli babaoo.

Duŋ ba; shi kanei etɛ ni yɔɔ Pequod tsɔne wulu lɛ mli lɛ kudɔ wɔ gbɛ lɛ fioo; kɛyashi be mli ni wɔbɛŋkɛ lɛ wɔna Ahab ni ekɛ kanei krokomɛi babaoo lɛ ateŋ ekome miishwie gbogboi lɛ anɔ. Beni ekwɛɔ ŋshɔŋloo ni miihoso lɛ be fioo lɛ, ekɛ famɔi ni afɔɔ feemɔ koni akɛkwɛ nɔ jogbaŋŋ kɛha gbɛkɛ lɛ ha, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekɛ ekane lɛ ha ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔ ko, etee ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli, ni ebaaa hiɛ dɔŋŋ kɛyashi leebi.

Eyɛ mli akɛ, yɛ ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ sɛɛtiumɔ nɔkwɛmɔ mli lɛ, Kapten Ahab ejie esubaŋ nitsumɔ lɛ kpo, koni etsɛ lɛ nakai moŋ; shi amrɔ nɛɛ ni adebɔɔ lɛ egbo lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni tsui ni nyɔɔ mɔ mli ni bɛ faŋŋ, loo tsuishitoo ni bɛ, loo hiɛnɔkamɔ ni bɛ faŋŋ miitsu nii yɛ emli; tamɔ nɔ ni nakai gbonyo lɛ ni ena lɛ kaiɔ lɛ akɛ esa akɛ agbe Moby Dick lolo; ni eyɛ mli akɛ akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi krokomɛi akpe ba elɛlɛ lɛ mli moŋ, shi enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ haŋ nɔ ko kwraa aha enibii wuji ni ji monomaniac lɛ aya hiɛ. Etsɛɛɛ tsɔ kulɛ obaasusu kɛjɛ gbee ni yɔɔ Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ mli, akɛ niji fɛɛ miisaa amɛhe koni amɛshɛrɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli ŋshɔ lɛ mli; ejaakɛ agbala ŋwɛi ŋtayaai ni tsii kɛmiitsɔ lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ, ni ajieɔ kɛ gbee ni tswaa kɛmiijɛ lɛlɛdaamɔhei lɛ amli. Shi kɛtsɔ nakai tsɔnei ni tswaa lɛ nɔ lɛ, esa akɛ atswa gbonyo wulu lɛ diɛŋtsɛ, shi jeee lɛlɛ lɛ. Amrɔ nɛɛ, akɛ eyitso fĩ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ, ni akɛ etsitsi fĩ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ, ni amrɔ nɛɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kã shi ni etsitsi diŋ lɛ bɛŋkɛ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ, ni anaa kɛtsɔɔ gbɛkɛ duŋ lɛ mli, ni eha ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ahe nibii ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi lɛ mli ewo duŋ, ni amɛyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ—lɛlɛ lɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, tamɔ nɔ ni akɛ amɛ ebɔ he tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi wuji, ni ŋshɔŋlooi wuji krokomɛi lɛ ehi shi.

*Ekolɛ nɔ bibioo ko hu baanyɛ afee nɔ ni kɔɔ he yɛ biɛ. Nɔ ni mli wa fe fɛɛ ni anyɛɔ akɛ he fɔ̃ɔ nɔ ni lɛlɛ lɛ yɔɔ yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔ kɛji akɛ ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he ji ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ loo ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ; ni akɛni kɛjɛ bɔ ni egbɔjɔ ha lɛ mli lɛ, nakai fã lɛ tsii fe ekrokomɛi fɛɛ (bɛja sɛɛ-fãi lɛ), bɔ ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ shi yɛ gbele mli po lɛ, haa efoɔ shi yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ shishi; bɔni afee ni okɛ onine akashɛ he kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, koni okɛ ŋwɛi ŋta lɛ abɔle he. Shi akɛ ŋaa yeɔ naagba nɛɛ nɔ kunim: asaa tsɔne bibioo ko ni mli wa ni akɛ tso ni akɛfoɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ woɔ esɛɛgbɛ, ni akɛ tsɔne ko ni tsiimɔ yɔɔ teŋgbɛ, yɛ be mli ni akɛ naagbee kroko lɛ fĩɔ lɛlɛ lɛ he. YITSƆƆ 65. Whale lɛ akɛ Niyenii.

Esa akɛ nakai gbɔmɔ ni gboɔ lɛ aye adebɔɔ ni haa ekane lɛ niyenii lɛ, ni, tamɔ Stubb lɛ, eye lɛ kɛtsɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ekane lɛ nɔ, taakɛ obaanyɛ okɛɛ lɛ; etamɔ nɔ ni enɛ ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ waa akɛ esa akɛ mɔ ko aya ehe yinɔsane kɛ jeŋ nilee mli fioo.

Eyɛ wolo nɔ akɛ, afii ohai etɛ ni eho nɛ lɛ, abuɔ Right Whale lilɛi akɛ eji niyenii ni ŋɔɔ waa yɛ France, ni abiɔ jara babaoo yɛ jɛmɛ. Agbɛnɛ hu, akɛ yɛ Henry VIII beaŋ lɛ, maŋtsɛwe lɛ nihoolɔ ko ná nyɔmɔwoo fɛfɛo ko yɛ bɔ ni ekɛ ŋmɔtɔ ko ni sa yijiemɔ fee ni akɛ porpoises ni ashã, ni, okaiɔ lɛ, eji ŋshɔŋlooi srɔtoi ko lɛ fee lɛ hewɔ. Lɛlɛŋ, abuɔ ŋshɔŋlooi akɛ niyenii kpakpa kɛbashi ŋmɛnɛ. Akɛ loo lɛ feɔ bɔɔlu ni dalɛ tamɔ billiard bɔɔlu, ni kɛji akɛ awo mli tsofa jogbaŋŋ ni awo mli tsofa-kɛ-ŋma lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛfee turtle-ball loo veal bɔɔlu. Dunfermline osɔfoi onukpai lɛ sumɔɔ amɛ waa. Amɛyɛ porpoise shika babaoo kɛjɛ maŋtsɛtsɛ lɛ dɛŋ.

Anɔkwale lɛ ji akɛ, kɛ hoo lɛ, yɛ egbɔbilɔi lɛ ateŋ lɛ, abaabu ŋshɔŋloo lɛ akɛ niyenii ni hi jogbaŋŋ, kɛji akɛ ebɛ babaoo nakai; shi kɛ́ obatara loo-pie ko ni kɛlɛ miihe ashɛ ninetalɔi oha lɛ hiɛ lɛ, ejieɔ niyenii he akɔnɔ kɛjɛɔ ohe. Hii ni bɛ jwɛŋmɔ ni ejaaa fe fɛɛ tamɔ Stubb pɛ, ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ lɛ yeɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni ahoo lɛ eko; shi Esquimauxbii lɛ jeee mɛi ni feɔ nibii oya nakai. Wɔ fɛɛ wɔle bɔ ni amɛhiɔ shi amɛhaa yɛ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji anɔ, ni amɛyɛ oketeke mli mu momo ni anáaa waaa. Zogranda, ni ji amɛ datrɛfoi ni ehe gbɛi fe fɛɛ lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome lɛ woɔ blubber ni ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ mli lɛ he ŋaa kɛhaa gbekɛbii bibii, akɛ eji nɔ ni mli edɔ waa ni ehaa mɔ hewalɛ. Ni enɛ kaiɔ mi akɛ Ŋleshibii komɛi, ni be saŋŋ ni eho lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛlɛ ko shi amɛ yɛ Greenland yɛ oshara mli lɛ—akɛ nɛkɛ hii nɛɛ hi shi diɛŋtsɛ nyɔji babaoo yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni etsɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni ashi amɛ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa beni amɛka blubber lɛ amɛkwɛ sɛɛ lɛ nɔ. Yɛ Dutch ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ lɛ, atsɛɔ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi nɛɛ akɛ “fritters”; ni, lɛɛlɛŋ, amɛtamɔ amɛhe waa, akɛ amɛhe yɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni amɛhe yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni amɛhe yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ tamɔ Amsterdam shia ŋamɛi onukpai a-dough-nuts loo oly-cooks, kɛji akɛ amɛfee ehee. Amɛyɛ hiɛ ni ayeɔ nii aahu akɛ gbɔ ni kpooɔ ehe fe fɛɛ lɛ nyɛŋ ajie eniji kɛjɛ he.

Shi nɔ ni haa ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he baa shi fe tsutsu lɛ akɛ niyenii ni yɔɔ jeŋba kpakpa, ji enii babaoo ni eyɔɔ fe nine lɛ. Ni no ji nɔ ni ha tsinanuu bibioo ko ni yɔɔ tsinabi yitso ni tamɔ mɔ ni yɔɔ nilee yɛ ehiɛ lɛ, ji nibii ni yɔɔ awerɛho fe fɛɛ ni obaanyɛ ona lɛ ateŋ ekome yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ. Amɛtswa yɛ yiwalɛ gbɛ nɔ, jeee yɛ amɛhe fãji ni ajie lɛ pɛ mli, shi moŋ tamɔ gãimlibii ni nyɛɔ efoɔ, ni amɛkotoɔ amɛhe, ni amɛtswaa amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛmli; kɛyashi etamɔ nɔ ni nakai mligbɛ nibii lɛ mimɔ shii abɔ kɛtsɔ daaŋ kome lɛ nɔŋŋ nɔ, ni pilamɔ ni egbɛ eshwa lɛ jie amɛ kpo yɛ gbɛ ni teɔ shi woɔ enɛ nɔ. Ni jeee enɛ pɛ hu. Ebɛ shweshweeshwe akɛ aaakɛ ohe awo bɔɔ nii nɛɛ agboi kɛ amɛmumɔi amli. Etamɔ nɔ ni generic loo Pantheistic hewalɛ ko ni yɔɔ amɛfãji kɛ amɛwui amli diɛŋtsɛ, beni nɔ ni abaanyɛ atsɛ akɛ aŋkroaŋkroi ashihilɛ lɛ eshi lɛ sɛɛ. Akɛni agbe lɛ ni awo lɛ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ yɛ ehewolo hewɔ lɛ, eshwɛ fioo ni nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi nɛɛ ateŋ mɔ kome baajie Queequeg ohiafo lɛ nine, beni ebɔ mɔdɛŋ ni eŋa etsitsi ni gbeɔ mɔ lɛ naa lɛ.

*Akɛ dade ni hi fe fɛɛ fee whaling-spade ni akɛfoɔ nii lɛ; ji bɔ ni gbɔmɔ nine ni egbɛ eshwa lɛ dalɛ ji lɛ he; ni yɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ lɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ abɔɔ mli nitsumɔ ni akɛ gbɛi wo mli lɛ kpãa gbee; esɛɛgbɛ pɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni eŋwɛi naagbee lɛ mli etse kwraa fe shishigbɛ nɔ lɛ. Akɛ tawuu nii nɛɛ hiɔ shi jogbaŋŋ be fɛɛ be bɔ ni aaanyɛ; ni kɛ́ akɛtsu nii lɛ, ashɛreɔ lɛ yɛ bei komɛi amli, tamɔ tsɔne ni akɛfoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ. Akɛ tso ni wa, ni kɛlɛ ji ninetalɔi nyɔŋmai enyɔ kɛyashi nyɔŋmai etɛ woɔ eshishigbɛ koni akɛhiɛ mli.

“Queequeg susuuu nɔ nyɔŋmɔ ni ha efee shark lɛ he,” kooloi fɔji lɛ kɛɛ, ni ekɛ piŋmɔ wo enine nɔ kɛtee ŋwɛi kɛba shi; “gbalashihilɛ Fejee nyɔŋmɔ loo Nantucket nyɔŋmɔ; shi esa akɛ nyɔŋmɔ ni fee shark lɛ afee dam Ingin kome.”
