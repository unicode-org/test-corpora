YITSƆƆ 4. Nɔ ni Akɛwoɔ Mli lɛ.

Beni mite shi enɔ jetsɛremɔ aaafee hulutsoo be lɛ, mina akɛ Queequeg nine eshɛ minɔ yɛ gbɛ ni suɔmɔ kɛ suɔmɔ yɔɔ mli fe fɛɛ nɔ. Shwɛ fioo ni obaasusu akɛ miji eŋa. Akɛ tsɔne ni akɛkpɛtɛɔ he lɛ fee, ni eyi kɛ skwɛji bibii ni yɔɔ srɔtoi ni yɔɔ srɔtoi kɛ triangles; ni nɛkɛ enine nɛɛ he fɛɛ ji Cretan labyrinth ni ji mfoniri ko ni bɛ naagbee, ni efãi enyɔ ko bɛ ni yɔɔ su kome pɛpɛɛpɛ—misusuɔ akɛ akɛni ekɛ enine hi ŋshɔ hiɛ yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ yɛ hulu kɛ susuma mli lɛ hewɔ lɛ, eshiɛti lɛ niji lɛ ebɔle ehe yɛ bei srɔtoi amli—nɛkɛ enine lɛ nɔŋŋ ji nɔ ni sa akɛ, enine lɛ nɔŋŋ. quilt ni akɛkpɛtɛɔ he. Lɛlɛŋ, fã ko ni mikɛkã nɔ tamɔ bɔ ni nine lɛ fee beni mite shi klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ, ewaaa akɛ manyɛ makɛɛ kɛjɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ mli, amɛfutu amɛhe sui lɛ ekome; ni bɔ ni minuɔ he akɛ mihe wa kɛ nɔnyɛɛ pɛ ji nɔ ni ha mina akɛ Queequeg miifu mi.

Mihenumɔi lɛ yɛ naakpɛɛ. Ha mabɔ mɔdɛŋ ni matsɔɔ amɛ mli. Beni gbekɛ ji mi lɛ, mikaiɔ shihilɛ ko ni tamɔ nakai fioo ni ba minɔ lɛ jogbaŋŋ; kɛji eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ loo lamɔ ko lɛ, minyɛɛɛ mafee nɔ ko kwraa kɔkɔɔkɔ. Shihilɛ lɛ ji enɛ. No mli lɛ, mifolɔ kapɛ loo ekrokomɛi komɛi—misusuɔ akɛ eebɔ mɔdɛŋ ni efo lasu tsu lɛ mli, taakɛ mina tsɔne bibioo ko ni shwieɔ lasu tsu lɛ mli lɛ fee gbii fioo komɛi ni eho lɛ; kɛ mitsɛkwɛ̃ ni, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ loo gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ, etswaa mi be fɛɛ be, loo etsuɔ ​​mi kɛyaa wɔɔhe ni miyeee gbɛkɛ niyenii lɛ, —mimami gbala mi kɛjɛ lasu tsu lɛ mli ni ekɛ mi tee wɔɔhe, eyɛ mli akɛ no mli lɛ eji June 21 lɛ shwane ŋmɛlɛtswai enyɔ pɛ, ni ji gbi ni kɛlɛ fe fɛɛ yɛ afi lɛ mli yɛ wɔ hemisphere lɛ mli lɛ. Minu he akɛ mihe fee mi gbeyei. Shi yelikɛbuamɔ ko bɛ kɛha lɛ, no hewɔ lɛ mikwɔ atswele lɛ nɔ kɛtee mitsu bibioo ni yɔɔ tsũ lɛ yiteŋ ni ji etɛ lɛ mli lɛ mli, ni mijie mihe atadei lɛ blɛoo bɔ ni manyɛ koni magbe be, ni mikɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni mli wa mibote tsɔji lɛ ateŋ.

Mika jɛmɛ kɛ awerɛho ni mibu akɔntaa akɛ esa akɛ ŋmɛlɛtswai muu nyɔŋma kɛ ekpaa ho dani manyɛ mahiɛ ahi gbohiiashitee nɔ. ŋmɛlɛtswai nyɔŋma kɛ ekpaa yɛ saatso nɔ! misɛɛ bibioo lɛ yeɔ mi awui kɛ misusu he. Ni eyɛ mlɛo waa hu; hulu ni tsoɔ yɛ samflɛ mli lɛ, kɛ tsɔnei ni akɛtswaa tsɔnei wuji ni gbɛɔ waa yɛ gbɛjegbɛi lɛ anɔ lɛ, kɛ hii kɛ hii ni kɛ amɛhe feɔ yakayaka nii lɛ agbɛ́ii ni gbɛɔ waa yɛ shia lɛ mli fɛɛ. Minu he akɛ mihe efee mi shoo fe tsutsu lɛ—naagbee lɛ mite shi, miwo atade, ni mikɛ minaji ni awo mi stɔki lɛ shwie shi blɛoo, mitao mitsɛkwɛ̃ lɛ, ni trukaa lɛ mikɛ mihe shwie enaji ahe, ni mikpa lɛ fai akɛ eji duromɔ pɔtɛɛ ko ni eha mi slipper kpakpa yɛ mijeŋba gbonyo lɛ hewɔ; nɔ ko nɔ ko diɛŋtsɛ shi moŋ ebuɔ mi fɔ akɛ makã shi be kakadaŋŋ ni anyɛɛɛ aŋmɛ he gbɛ tamɔ nɛkɛ. Shi lɛ ji nyɛmɛi ni hi fe fɛɛ ni kwɛɔ amɛnɔ jogbaŋŋ fe fɛɛ, ni kɛ́ miku misɛɛ lɛ, esa akɛ maya mitsu lɛ mli. Mikɛ ŋmɛlɛtswai babaoo ka jɛmɛ ni mihiɛ kã shi, ni minuɔ he akɛ mihe ejɔ mi waa fe bɔ ni minu he kɛjɛ nakai beaŋ kɛbaa nɛɛ, kɛjɛ amanehului wuji ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ po amli. Naagbee lɛ, ekolɛ mibote lamɔ ko ni haoɔ mɔ ni ji wɔɔhe mli; ni beni mite shi fiofio kɛjɛ mli—ni lamɔi amli fã ko eyi mli obɔ lɛ—migbele mihiŋmɛii, ni agbɛnɛ kponɔgbɛ duŋ ehà tsu ni hulu lɛ tsoɔ yɛ mli dani eba lɛ mli. Amrɔ nɔŋŋ kɛkɛ ni minu akɛ shoofeemɔ ko miiho kɛmiitsɔ mifrɛm lɛ fɛɛ mli; anaaa nɔ ko nɔ ko, ni anuuu nɔ ko nɔ ko; shi etamɔ nɔ ni akɛ nine ko ni fe adebɔɔ nɔ lɛ wo minɔ lɛ mli. Mi nine lɛ fĩ ŋwɛi ŋta lɛ nɔ, ni etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ni bɛ gbɛi, ni anyɛɛɛ asusu he, ni yɔɔ dioo loo ŋmiŋmi, ni nine lɛ fata he lɛ, eta mi saatso lɛ masɛi kpaakpa. Ejaakɛ nɔ ni tamɔ afii ni ato afii babaoo lɛ, mika jɛmɛ, ni gbeyeishemɔi ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ lɛ efee fɛ̃i, ni mibɛ ekãa ni mikɛgbala minine; shi kɛlɛ misusuɔ be fɛɛ be akɛ kɛji manyɛ mafolɔ mli inchi kome pɛ lɛ, shɛɛ sane ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ baafite. Mileee bɔ ni fee ni nɛkɛ jwɛŋmɔ nɛɛ shwie mihe yɛ naagbee; shi beni mite shi leebi lɛ, mikɛ hekpokpomɔ kai fɛɛ, ni gbii kɛ otsii kɛ nyɔji babaoo yɛ no sɛɛ lɛ, mifite mihe yɛ mɔdɛŋ ni mibɔɔ koni matsɔɔ teemɔŋ sane lɛ mli ni haa mɔ tsui fãa lɛ mli. Dabi, kɛyashi ŋmɛlɛtswaa nɛɛ nɔŋŋ mli lɛ, bei pii lɛ mikɛ mihe woɔ mli.

Agbɛnɛ, jiemɔ gbeyeishemɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ, ni mihenumɔi ni minuɔ he akɛ minuɔ nine ni fe adebɔɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ minɔ lɛ mli lɛ tamɔ nɔ ni mina beni mite shi ni mina Queequeg wɔŋjalɔi anine ni ashɛrɛ ashwie mihe lɛ nɔŋŋ, yɛ amɛ naakpɛɛfeemɔ mli. Shi yɛ be ni sa mli lɛ, nibii fɛɛ ni tee nɔ yɛ gbɛkɛ ni eho lɛ mli lɛ ba ekoŋŋ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ nɔ, komekomei, yɛ shihilɛ ni yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ mli, ni no sɛɛ lɛ, mika shi ni mihiɛ kã shi kɛha shihilɛ ni mli wa ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ lɛ pɛ. Ejaakɛ eyɛ mli akɛ mibɔ mɔdɛŋ ni matsi enine lɛ—mijie eyemforowu lɛ ŋmɛlɛ lɛ—shi kɛlɛ, beni ewɔ lɛ, efu mi waa lolo, tamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ni baagbala wɔteŋ fe gbele. Agbɛnɛ mibɔ mɔdɛŋ ni mate lɛ shi—“Queequeg!”—shi hetoo pɛ ni eha ji ŋmɔlɔ. Kɛkɛ ni mifo mihe, ni mikuɛ fee tamɔ nɔ ni eyɛ okpɔŋɔ-kɔla mli; ni trukaa lɛ enu ŋmɔlɔ fioo ko he. Beni ashɛrɛɔ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ ashwieɔ shi lɛ, tomahawk lɛ kã shi ni ewɔ yɛ kooloo lɛ masɛi, tamɔ nɔ ni eji abifao ko ni hiɛ tsɔne ni akɛtswaa tsɔne. Pickle fɛfɛo ko, anɔkwale, misusu he; ŋmɛ biɛ yɛ shĩa ko ni aleee mli yɛ gbi lɛ mli, kɛ gbɔmɔyelɔ kɛ tomahawk! “Kweek!—yɛ ekpakpafeemɔ gbɛi mli lɛ, Kweek, te shi!” Yɛ naagbee lɛ, kɛtsɔ ŋmɔlɔ babaoo, kɛ wiemɔi ni naa wa ni ekpaaa ni kɔɔ bɔ ni esaaa akɛ ekɛ enaanyo nuu ko feɔ ekome yɛ nakai gbalashihilɛ gbɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, miye omanye yɛ ŋmɔlɔ ko ni mijie lɛ mli; ni amrɔ nɛɛ, ekɛ enine ku esɛɛ, ekpokpo ehe fɛɛ tamɔ Newfoundland gbee ni jɛ nu lɛ mli pɛ, ni ete shi yɛ saatso lɛ nɔ, ni ehiɛ wa tamɔ pike-tso, ni ekwɛ mi, ni ekɛ ehiŋmɛii miikpa mihe tamɔ nɔ ni ekaiii bɔ ni fee ni miba jɛmɛ lɛ kwraa, eyɛ mli akɛ nɔ ko ni bɛ faŋŋ ni tsɔɔ akɛ mile lɛ miikwɛ mi moŋ. Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, mika shi dioo ni mikwɛɔ lɛ, ni mibɛ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ ko ni hiɛdɔɔ yɔɔ mli bianɛ, ni mikɛ mihe fɔ̃ɔ nɔ akɛ makwɛ adebɔɔ ko ni miitao ele nii waa lɛ mli fioo. Beni, yɛ naagbee lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ejwɛŋmɔ ekpɛ eyiŋ akɛ ebaata enaanyo ni ewɔ shi lɛ subaŋ he, ni ebatsɔ, taakɛ atsɔɔ mli lɛ, ekɛ anɔkwale lɛ kpã gbee lɛ; efo kɛje kpo yɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ, ni kɛtsɔ okadii kɛ gbɛɛmɔi komɛi anɔ lɛ, eha minu shishi akɛ, kɛji esa mi lɛ, ebaasaa mihe klɛŋklɛŋ ni no sɛɛ lɛ ebaashi mi koni masaa mihe yɛ no sɛɛ, ni eshi shia muu lɛ fɛɛ eha mi diɛŋtsɛ mihe. Misusuɔ akɛ, mi, Queequeg, yɛ shihilɛi lɛ amli lɛ, enɛ ji shishitoo mla ko ni yɔɔ jeŋba kpakpa waa; shi, anɔkwale lɛ ji akɛ, nɛkɛ kooloi awuiyelɔi nɛɛ yɛ fɔmɔ mli henumɔ ko ni haa amɛnuɔ nii ahe jogbaŋŋ, kɛɛmɔ nɔ ni osumɔɔ; eji naakpɛɛ bɔ ni amɛji bulɛ titri lɛ. Mikɛ yijiemɔ pɔtɛɛ nɛɛ haa Queequeg, ejaakɛ ekɛ mi ye yɛ bulɛ kɛ susumɔ babaoo mli, yɛ be mli ni miye fɔ yɛ bulɛ ni bɛ mli babaoo he; ni ekwɛɔ lɛ kɛjɛɔ saatso lɛ nɔ, ni ekwɛɔ e-toilet nifeemɔi lɛ fɛɛ; kɛha be mli ni mishwelɛ ni miyɔɔ akɛ male nɔ ni yaa nɔ lɛ miiya hiɛ yɛ mifɔmɔ mli lɛ. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, nuu ko tamɔ Queequeg ni onaaa lɛ daa gbi lɛ, lɛ kɛ egbɛi lɛ sa jogbaŋŋ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ srɔto nɔ yɛ he.

Ebɔi atadewoo yɛ ŋwɛi kɛtsɔ e-beaver tsitsi, ni kɛlɛ waa lɛ ni ewo lɛ nɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ—ni etrɔsa lɛ fataaa he lolo lɛ—ekɛ e-boots lɛ shwie shi. Nɔ ni efee yɛ ŋwɛi shishi lɛ, minyɛɛɛ makɛɛ, shi nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ ni efee ji ni ekɛ ehe atswa—ekɛ atadei ni yɔɔ enine mli, ni ewo tsitsi—yɛ saatso lɛ shishi; beni, kɛjɛ yiwalɛ ŋmɔlɔi kɛ ŋmɔlɔi srɔtoi amli lɛ, misusu akɛ eetsu nii waa ni ekɛ ehe miiwo tsɔne mli; eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ mla ko ni kɔɔ jalɛsaneyeli he ni minu he pɛŋ lɛ naa lɛ, abiɔ ni nuu ko afee mɔ ko ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ beni ewoɔ e-boots lɛ. Shi Queequeg, ani ona, eji adebɔɔ ko ni yɔɔ tsakemɔ mli—jeee ŋmei loo ŋmei. Ehe esa bɔ ni sa pɛ ni ekɛjie ehe kpo yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ fe fɛɛ nɔ. No mli lɛ egbeko enikasemɔ lɛ naa lolo. No mli lɛ, eji mɔ ko ni eyako skul. Eji ejeee mɔ ko ni yɔɔ jeŋba fioo kulɛ, ekolɛ ekɛ bɔɔtui haoŋ ehe kwraa; shi no sɛɛ lɛ, eji ejeee kooloo ni yɔɔ gbeyei lolo kulɛ, elaŋ lamɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ akɛ ebaabote saatso lɛ shishi ni ekɛ amɛ awo nɔ. Yɛ naagbee lɛ, eje kpo kɛ etsitsi ni efo waa ni efolɔ ehiŋmɛii anɔ, ni ebɔi ŋmɔlɔ ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ shwie shi yɛ tsu lɛ mli, tamɔ nɔ ni, akɛni ekɛ etsitsii ahewolo ni mli ewo fɔ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ etsɔko ehe tsɔ hewɔ lɛ—ekolɛ afeee lɛ yɛ gbɛ ni sa nɔ—shi moŋ etswa lɛ ni epila lɛ yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ ŋmɔlɔ mli.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, beni ena akɛ muji ko bɛ samflɛ lɛ he, ni gbɛjegbɛ lɛ mli etse waa lɛ, shĩa ni kɛ lɛ kpeɔ lɛ haa anyɛɔ akwɛɔ tsu lɛ mli jogbaŋŋ, ni ekwɛɔ mfoniri ni esaaa ni Queequeg fee lɛ babaoo, ni ekɛ nɔ ko fioo pɛ shwieɔ shi fe etsitsi kɛ etsitsii; Mikpa lɛ fai bɔ ni manyɛ, koni ekɛ e-toilet lɛ aya oya fioo, ni titri lɛ ni ebote e-pantaloons lɛ amli oya bɔ ni eeenyɛ. Ekɛ ehe wo mli, ni no sɛɛ lɛ etee nɔ efɔ ehe. Nakai be lɛ mli leebi lɛ kulɛ Kristofonyo fɛɛ Kristofonyo baafɔ ehiɛ; shi Queequeg, ni fee mi naakpɛɛ ji akɛ, ekɛ ehe wo e-ablution lɛ ni ekɛbaatsu nii yɛ etsitsi, eniji, kɛ eniji amli pɛ lɛ mli. Kɛkɛ ni ewo e-waistcoat lɛ, ni eŋɔ samla ni wa ko ni yɔɔ wash-stand lɛ teŋgbɛ okpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekɛwo nu mli ni ebɔi ehiɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ. Mikwɛɔ koni mana he ni ekɛ e-razor lɛ to, ni lo kɛ behold, ejie harpoon lɛ kɛjɛ saatso lɛ koŋ nɔ, ejie tso kakadaŋŋ lɛ, ejie eyitso lɛ, ekɛwo e-boot lɛ nɔ fioo, ni enyiɛ kɛtee ashwishwɛ bibioo ni ekɛkpeɔ gbogbo lɛ he lɛ nɔ, ebɔi harpoo ni mli wa, loo e-cheek lɛ tswaa. Misusuɔ akɛ, Queequeg, enɛ ji Rogers tsɔne ni hi fe fɛɛ ni akɛtsuɔ nii kɛ hejoomɔ. No sɛɛ lɛ, misusu bɔ ni nɛkɛ nitsumɔ nɛɛ he ehiaaa tsɔ lɛ he beni mibale dade kpakpa ni akɛfeɔ harpoon yitso lɛ, kɛ bɔ ni akwɛɔ ŋwɛi ŋta kakadaŋŋ ni ja lɛ nɔ jogbaŋŋ fe nine be fɛɛ be lɛ.

Etsɛɛɛ ni enine shɛ e-toilet lɛ fã ni eshwɛ lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ henɔwomɔ je kpo kɛjɛ tsu lɛ mli, ni ekɛ e-pilot monkey jakɛt wulu lɛ ha ehe, ni ekɛ e-harpoon lɛ miitswa shwɛmɔ tamɔ marshal tso.





YITSƆƆ 5. Gbijurɔyeli niyenii.

Mikɛ amɛ fee oya, ni beni miba shi kɛbote dãatɔɔhe lɛ, mikɛ shikpɔŋtsɛ lɛ ni miiŋmɔ lɛ wie jogbaŋŋ. Misusuuu jwɛŋmɔ fɔŋ ko he kɛha lɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ ekɛ mi ebɔ ŋwɛi ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ fioo ko yɛ mihefatalɔ ni mikɛwɔɔ lɛ he sane lɛ mli moŋ.

Shi kɛlɛ, ŋmɔlɔ kpakpa ji nɔ kpakpa ni yɔɔ hewalɛ waa, ni moŋ eji nɔ kpakpa ni anaaa tsɔ; babaoo ni mɔbɔnalɛ ji lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, kɛji gbɔmɔ kome, yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ egbɔmɔtso mli lɛ, ha nibii kɛha shwɛmɔ kpakpa eha mɔ ko lɛ, ekafee mɔ ni sɛɛ efo, shi moŋ ekɛ miishɛɛ aŋmɛ gbɛ ni efite ni afite lɛ yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ. Ni nuu ni yɔɔ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ babaoo yɛ ehe lɛ, kwɛmɔ akɛ nɔ ko yɛ nakai nuu lɛ mli fe bɔ ni ekolɛ osusuɔ.

Agbɛnɛ, dãatɔɔhe lɛ eyi kɛ mɛi ni yɔɔ shĩa lɛ mli ni eba jɛmɛ gbɛkɛ ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ, ni mikwɛko amɛ jogbaŋŋ lolo lɛ. Nɔ ni miihe ashɛ amɛ fɛɛ amɛji ŋshɔŋlooi; hefatalɔi onukpai, kɛ hefatalɔi ni ji enyɔ, kɛ hefatalɔi ni ji etɛ, kɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ kapintɛfoi, kɛ ŋshɔ mli dadefeelɔi, kɛ ŋshɔ mli dadefeelɔi, kɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋtayaai, kɛ lɛlɛ nɔkwɛlɔi; kuu ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni amɛhiɛ tsitsii ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ; tsɔne ko ni afolɔko, ni eyɛ tsitsii, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛwo ŋmeii jakɛtii kɛha leebi atadei.

Obaanyɛ ona be abɔ ni amɛteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ehi ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ jogbaŋŋ. Obalanyo nɛɛ tsitsi ni yɔɔ hewalɛ lɛ tamɔ pear ni hulu etswa lɛ yɛ su mli, ni ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni ehiɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni miihe ashɛ musky; enyɛŋ eba shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ gbii etɛ kɛjɛ e-India gbɛfaa lɛ mli. Nakai nuu ni bɛŋkɛ lɛ lɛ hiɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ fioo; obaanyɛ okɛɛ akɛ satin tso ko ni tamɔ nɔ ko yɛ emli. Yɛ mɔ ni ji etɛ lɛ hewolo nɔ lɛ, tropic tawn ko yɛ lolo, shi eyɛ ŋmɔlɔ fioo; ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ ehi ŋshɔ lɛ naa otsii muu fɛɛ. Shi namɔ baanyɛ ajie ŋmɔlɔ tamɔ Queequeg kpo? ni, akɛ ŋmɔlɔi srɔtoi efo mli lɛ, etamɔ Andes anaigbɛ ŋshɔ lɛ, koni ejie ehe kpo yɛ gbɛjianɔtoo kome mli, kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔi ni yɔɔ srɔtoi, kpokpaa fɛɛ kpokpaa.

“Grub, ho!” agbɛnɛ shikpɔŋtsɛ lɛ bolɔ, ni ekɛ tsɔne gbele shinaa ko, ni wɔbote mli koni wɔyaye leebi niyenii.

Amɛkɛɛ akɛ hii ni ena je lɛ, no hewɔ lɛ amɛfeɔ mɛi ni he jɔ kwraa yɛ jeŋba mli, ni amɛyeɔ amɛhe nɔ kwraa yɛ kuu mli. Shi jeee be fɛɛ be: Ledyard, New England gbɛfalɔ kpeteŋkpele lɛ, kɛ Mungo Park, Scotlandnyo lɛ; yɛ gbɔmɛi fɛɛ ateŋ lɛ, amɛyɛ nɔmimaa fioo fe fɛɛ yɛ shĩa lɛ mli. Shi ekolɛ Siberia fomɔ kɛkɛ yɛ tsɔne ni gbeei gbalaa lɛ mli tamɔ bɔ ni Ledyard fee lɛ, loo nyiɛmɔ kakadaŋŋ ni mɔ kome pɛ nyiɛɔ yɛ musu ni nɔ ko bɛ mli, yɛ Afrika tsui ni ji mɛi diji lɛ amli, ni ji Mungo ohiafoi anifeemɔi lɛ fɛɛ ni abua naa lɛ—gbɛfaa nɛɛ, mikɛɛ, ekolɛ jeee gbɛ ni hi fe fɛɛ ni atsɔɔ nɔ anáa shihilɛ mli nibii ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli. Shi kɛlɛ, yɛ bei babaoo mli lɛ, esa akɛ ana nakai nɔ lɛ yɛ he fɛɛ he.

Nɛkɛ susumɔi nɛɛ ni yɔɔ biɛ pɛ lɛ jɛ shihilɛ ni ba akɛ beni wɔ fɛɛ wɔtara okpɔlɔ lɛ sɛɛ, ni miisaa mihe koni manu saji kpakpai komɛi ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ he lɛ; nɔ ni fee mi naakpɛɛ ji akɛ, nɔ ni miihe ashɛ nuu fɛɛ nuu fee dioo waa. Ni jeee no pɛ, shi moŋ amɛfee tamɔ mɛi ni ehiɛ egbo. Hɛɛ, ŋshɔ mli gbeei akuu ko yɛ biɛ, ni amɛteŋ mɛi babaoo kɛ hiɛgbele fioo ko pɛ etara ŋshɔŋ loofɔji wuji amli yɛ ŋshɔi wuji lɛ anɔ—gbɔi kwraa kɛha amɛ—ni amɛwu amɛshi amɛ ni amɛgboi ni amɛkwɛɛɛ amɛhiɛ; ni kɛlɛ, amɛtara gbɔmɛi ateŋ leebi niyenii okpɔlɔ ko he yɛ biɛ—amɛ fɛɛ amɛtsɛɔ amɛhe, amɛ fɛɛ amɛsumɔɔ wekumɛi—ni amɛmiikwɛ amɛhe kɛ hiɛgbele tamɔ nɔ ni amɛjeko tooi akuu ko ni yɔɔ Green Mountains lɛ ateŋ lɛ mli pɛŋ. Nɔ ko ni haa mɔ taoɔ ele nɔ ko; nɛkɛ bears ni hiɛ gboɔ nɛɛ, nɛkɛ whalemen ni ji tawulɔi ni sheɔ gbeyei nɛɛ!

Shi yɛ Queequeg gbɛfaŋ lɛ—mɛni hewɔ, Queequeg ta jɛmɛ yɛ amɛteŋ—yɛ okpɔlɔ lɛ yitso lɛ hu, eba trukaa; ni mli ejɔ tamɔ ais. Kɛha nɔmimaa lɛ minyɛɛɛ makɛɛ babaoo yɛ efɔmɔ he. Kulɛ mɔ ni nyaa ehe fe fɛɛ lɛ nyɛŋ ajɛ mlihilɛ mli abu ehe fɔ akɛ ekɛ e-harpoon lɛ baye leebi niyenii yɛ jɛmɛ, ni ekɛtsu nii yɛ jɛmɛ ni ekɛ nifeemɔ ko tsuuu nii; ni ekɛshɛɔ okpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ, kɛyaa yitsei babaoo ahe oshara ni miiba lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ tsina loo lɛ miimɔ eŋɔɔ. Shi eji anɔkwale akɛ lɛ ji mɔ ni fee nakai yɛ mlihilɛ mli waa, ni mɔ fɛɛ mɔ le akɛ yɛ mɛi babaoo asusumɔ naa lɛ, kɛ́ mɔ ko fee nɔ fɛɛ nɔ yɛ mlihilɛ mli lɛ, no lɛ efee lɛ yɛ mlihilɛ mli.

Wɔwieŋ Queequeg nibii ni yɔɔ srɔto lɛ fɛɛ ahe yɛ biɛ; bɔ ni etsi ehe kɛjɛ kɔfe kɛ ŋmɔtɔ ni mli edɔ lɛ he, ni ekɛ ejwɛŋmɔ ni mli ejaaa lɛ tsu tsina loo ni afee lɛ tsɔ lɛ he nii. Eshɛɔ he, akɛ beni leebi niyenii lɛ ba naagbee lɛ, ejie ehe tamɔ mɛi krokomɛi lɛ kɛtee maŋ tsu lɛ mli, ni etswa e-tomahawk-paipi lɛ, ni eta jɛmɛ dioo ni ekɛ etsitsi ni mli egbalaaa lɛ miiye nii ni ekɛmiishɛre tawa, beni mije kpo kɛtee nyiɛmɔ.





YITSƆƆ 6. Gbɛjegbɛ lɛ nɔ.

Kɛji akɛ shishijee lɛ efee mi naakpɛɛ akɛ mina aŋkro ko ni yɔɔ srɔto tamɔ Queequeg ni miibɔle maŋ ko ni yɔɔ jeŋba kpakpa lɛ mli bii ni yɔɔ bulɛ lɛ ateŋ kulɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni nakai naakpɛɛ lɛ shi beni mikɛ mihe wo New Bedford gbɛjegbɛi lɛ anɔ klɛŋklɛŋ kwraa yɛ hulutsoo be mli lɛ.

Yɛ gbɛjegbɛi wuji ni bɛŋkɛ lɛlɛdaamɔhei lɛ anɔ lɛ, ŋshɔkpɔi wuji fɛɛ ŋshɔkpɔi wuji baaha bei pii lɛ amɛkwɛ nibii ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni jɛ maŋsɛɛ hei lɛ. Yɛ Broadway kɛ Chestnut gbɛjegbɛi lɛ anɔ po lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ Mediteranea ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ baatsi yei ni sheɔ gbeyei lɛ anaa. Jeee akɛ Lascarbii kɛ Malaybii leee Regent Gbɛjegbɛ lɛ; ni yɛ Bombay, yɛ Apollo Green lɛ, Yankees ni hiɔ shi lɛ ewo maŋbii lɛ ahe gbeyei shii abɔ. Shi New Bedford ye Nu Gbɛjegbɛ kɛ Wapping fɛɛ nɔ kunim. Yɛ nɛkɛ naagbee hei ni atsĩ tã nɛɛ amli lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi pɛ onaa; shi yɛ New Bedford lɛ, gbɔmɔyelɔi diɛŋtsɛ damɔ shi ni amɛmiigba sane yɛ gbɛjegbɛi lɛ ashishi; savages tɛ̃ɛ; ni amɛteŋ mɛi babaoo hiɛ loo ni he tseee lolo yɛ amɛwui lɛ anɔ. Ehaa gbɔ ko kwɛɔ lɛ hiɛ.

Shi, kɛfata Feegeeanbii, Tongatobooarrs, Erromanggoanbii, Pannangianbii, kɛ Brighggianbii lɛ ahe lɛ, kɛfata ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛgbeɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni tsɔɔ gbɛjegbɛi lɛ anɔ lɛ ahe lɛ, obaana nibii krokomɛi ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lolo, ni ji nɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli fe tsutsu lɛ. Vermontbii kɛ New Hampshirebii hii babaoo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji amli lɛ baa maŋ nɛɛ mli daa otsi, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛmiiye sɛɛnamɔ kɛ anunyam yɛ wuoyeli mli. Amɛteŋ mɛi babaoo ji oblahii kɛ oblayei, ni amɛhe wa; nanemɛi ni efo kooi, ni agbɛnɛ amɛmiitao ni amɛshɛrɛ ŋmei lɛ amɛshwie shi ni amɛŋɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ŋmei lɛ. Mɛi babaoo yɛ ŋmɔji tamɔ Green Mountains ni amɛjɛ mli lɛ. Yɛ nibii komɛi amli lɛ obaasusu amɛhe shi amɛye ŋmɛlɛtswai fioo komɛi. Kwɛmɔ jɛmɛ! nakai chap ni miitsi ehe kɛmiibɔle koŋ lɛ he lɛ. Ewoɔ beaver tsitsi kɛ atade ni tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo, ni ekɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ-bɛlt kɛ ŋshɔŋloo-ŋmei fĩ ehe. Naa mɔ kroko ni hiɛ sou’-wester kɛ bombazine atade.

Dandy ni afɔ lɛ yɛ maŋ lɛ mli lɛ eko bɛ ni kɛ maŋ nɔ ni afɔ lɛ lɛ baato he—mitsɔɔ bumpkin dandy ni yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ—naanyo ko ni, yɛ gbee-gbii amli lɛ, ebaafolɔ e-acre enyɔ lɛ yɛ tsina hewolo glɔvs mli yɛ gbeyeishemɔ akɛ ebaatsɔ eniji ahe ŋmɔlɔ hewɔ. Amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛ́ maŋ dandy ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kɛwo eyitsoŋ koni ená gbɛi ni yɔɔ srɔto, ni ekɛ ehe wo ŋshɔŋlooi-wuoyeli wulu lɛ mli lɛ, esa akɛ ona nibii ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ ni efeɔ kɛ eshɛ ŋshɔnaa lɛjiadaamɔhe lɛ. Beni ewieɔ e-ŋshɔ-atade lɛ he lɛ, efãa ni akɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa-buttons awo e-waistcoats lɛ nɔ; tsɔnei ni akɛwoɔ e-kanvas troza lɛ he. Ah, Hay-Seed ohiafo! bɔ ni nakai tsɔji lɛ baatswa waa yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ ahum ni tswaa lɛ mli, beni ashwieɔ bo, tsɔji, bɔtin, kɛ fɛɛ, kɛyaa ahum lɛ tsitsi nɔ lɛ.

Shi kaasusu akɛ nɛkɛ maŋ ni ehe gbɛi waa nɛɛ yɛ harpooneers, gbɔmɔyelɔi, kɛ bumpkins pɛ ni ekɛtsɔɔ esaralɔi lɛ. Jee nakai kraa. New Bedford ji he ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lolo. Eji jeee wɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ kulɛ, ekolɛ nakai shikpɔŋ bibioo lɛ baafee nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ŋmɛnɛ tamɔ Labrador ŋshɔnaa lɛ. Taakɛ eji lɛ, esɛɛ maŋ lɛ fãi komɛi fa bɔ ni sa ni baaha mɔ ko afee gbeyei, amɛtamɔ nɔ ni amɛji wui waa. Ekolɛ maŋ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ji he ni asumɔɔ waa akɛ ahi fe fɛɛ, yɛ New England fɛɛ. Eji shikpɔŋ ni mu yɔɔ nɔ, anɔkwale: shi jeee tamɔ Kanaan; shikpɔŋ, hu, ni ŋmãa kɛ wain yɔɔ nɔ. Gbɛjegbɛi lɛ nɔ kɛ mliki hoɔɔ; ni asaŋ yɛ fɛ̃i be mli lɛ, amɛkɛ ŋmeii heei shwieɔ amɛhe. Shi, yɛ enɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, he ko he ko bɛ Amerika fɛɛ ni obaana shĩai ni tamɔ maŋtsɛmɛi ashiai fe nɛkɛ; shwɛmɔhei kɛ abɔɔi ni yɔɔ fɛo fe New Bedford. Nɛgbɛ amɛjɛ? te adu maŋ ko ni be ko ni eho lɛ eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ nɛɛ nɔ tɛŋŋ?

Yaa ni oyakwɛ dade okadi ŋtayaai ni bɔle maŋtsɛwe ni nɔ kwɔ ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ he lɛ, ni abaaha osanebimɔ lɛ hetoo. Hɛɛ; nɛkɛ tsũi ni yɔɔ ekãa nɛɛ kɛ fɔfɔii abɔɔi nɛɛ fɛɛ jɛ Atlantik, Pasifik, kɛ India ŋshɔi lɛ amli. Ekome kɛ fɛɛ, akɛ harpoon wo amɛmli ni agbala amɛ kɛjɛ ŋshɔ lɛ shishi kɛba biɛ. Ani Herr Alexander baanyɛ afee nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ?

Amɛkɛɛ akɛ yɛ New Bedford lɛ, tsɛmɛi kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi haa amɛbiyei lɛ akɛ amɛbaafee shika, ni amɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi fioo komɛi ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi fioo komɛi jaa amɛtsɛkwɛ̃mɛi yei lɛ. Esa akɛ oya New Bedford koni oyakwɛ yookpeemɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ; ejaakɛ amɛkɛɛ amɛyɛ mu ni akɛtoɔ shia fɛɛ shia, ni daa gbɛkɛ lɛ amɛkɛ hiɛdɔɔ shãa amɛhe lɛ yɛ spermaceti kanei amli.

Yɛ hulutsoo be mli lɛ, maŋ lɛ ŋɔɔ waa akɛ oookwɛ; ni maple tsei kpakpai eyi mli obɔ—gbɛjegbɛi kakadaji ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji kɛ shika tsuru. Ni yɛ August mli, yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli shɔŋŋ lɛ, okpɔŋɔ-chestnut tsei fɛfɛji ni fa babaoo, ni tamɔ kane lɛ, kɛ amɛ fɔfɔi ni abua naa lɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ lɛ haa mɔ ni hoɔ lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, ŋaa ji hewalɛ fɛɛ; ni yɛ New Bedford kpokpai babaoo amli lɛ ekɛ fɔfɔii ni kpɛɔ waa ebɔle jwɛi tɛi ni bɛ yibii ni ashɛrɛ ashwie shi yɛ adebɔɔ naagbee gbi lɛ nɔ lɛ anɔ.

Ni New Bedford yei lɛ, amɛfɔ̃ɔ tamɔ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ roses tsuji. Shi roses lɛ fɔɔ yɛ hulutsoo be mli pɛ; yɛ be mli ni amɛtsitsii anɔ karnation fɛfɛo lɛ hiɔ shi daa tamɔ hulu la ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ni ji kpawo lɛ mli lɛ. He kroko ni kɛ amɛ fɔfɔi lɛ yeɔ egbɔ lɛ, nyɛnyɛŋ, ja Salem, he ni amɛkɛɛ mi akɛ gbekɛbii yei bibii lɛ muɔ musk ni tamɔ nɛkɛ, amɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ni amɛsumɔɔ amɛ waa lɛ nuɔ amɛhe kilomitai babaoo kɛjɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa, tamɔ nɔ ni amɛmiibɛŋkɛ Moluccas ni ŋmei yɔɔ mli lɛ he yɛ Puritanic ŋãi lɛ najiaŋ.





YITSƆƆ 7. Sɔlemɔtsu lɛ.

Yɛ nɛkɛ New Bedford nɛɛ nɔŋŋ mli lɛ, Whaleman Sɔlemɔtsu ko damɔ jɛmɛ, ni mɛi fioo ko pɛ ji wuolɛɛmɛi ni yɔɔ tsui ni nyɔɔ mɔ mli, ni etsɛɛɛ ni amɛmiiya India Ŋshɔ lɛ loo Pasifik, ni nyɛɛɛ ayasara jɛmɛ Hɔgbaa lɛ. Miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ mifeee nakai.

Beni miku misɛɛ kɛjɛ mi klɛŋklɛŋ leebi nyiɛmɔ lɛ mli lɛ, mikɛ mihe wo nitsumɔ krɛdɛɛ nɛɛ mli ekoŋŋ. Ŋwɛi lɛ etsake kɛjɛ fɛ̃i ni mli tse, ni hulu lɛ tsoɔ waa lɛ nɔ, kɛtee hulutsoo kɛ afua ni tswaa tsɔne mli. Mikɛ mi-jakɛti ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ bearskin lɛ ha mihe, ni mikɛ ahum ni mli wa lɛ wu. Beni miboteɔ mli lɛ, mina lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi asafo bibioo ko ni egbɛ eshwã, kɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi aŋamɛi kɛ okulafoi. Diŋŋfeemɔ ko ni bɛ faŋŋ ye nɔ, ni bei komɛi lɛ ahum lɛ bolɔmɔi pɛ fiteɔ. Etamɔ nɔ ni jálɔ fɛɛ jálɔ ni efee dioo lɛ yiŋtoo ko hewɔ eta shi yɛ ekroko lɛ he, tamɔ nɔ ni awerɛhoyeli ni efee dioo lɛ eko fɛɛ eko ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ mli. No mli lɛ, osɔfo lɛ ebako lolo; ni jɛmɛ ji he ni hii kɛ yei ŋshɔkpɔi ni efee dioo nɛɛ tara shi shiŋŋ ni amɛmiikwɛ tɛi ni jara wa waa, ni husui diji yɔɔ nɔ, ni akɛwo gbogbo lɛ he yɛ wiemɔ kpoku lɛ afãi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ lɛ. Amɛteŋ mɛi etɛ jo foi tamɔ nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ nɛɛ, shi mifeee tamɔ mɔ ko ni miitsɛ wiemɔi asɛɛ:—

HE KRƆŊŊŊŊ AKƐHAA ROBERT LONG, WILLIS ELLERY, NATHAN COLEMAN, WALTER CANNY, SETH MACY, KƐ SAMUEL GLEIG AKƐBAA, Amɛfee lɛlɛi lɛ amli nitsulɔi lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome LƐLƐ ELIZA Ni ŋshɔŋloo ko ni ji Whale lɛ gbala amɛ kɛjɛ hiŋmɛi nɔ yɛ December mli lɛ , , 1839. Beni mijieɔ hulutsoo be ni yɔɔ mitsitsi kɛ mijakɛti ni akɛ ais ewo mli lɛ mli lɛ, mita shinaa lɛ masɛi, ni beni mitsɔ mihe kɛtee afã lɛ, minaa kpɛ mihe akɛ mina Queequeg ni bɛŋkɛ mi. Akɛni bɔ ni shihilɛ lɛ mli wa ha lɛ ná enɔ hewalɛ hewɔ lɛ, naakpɛɛ hiŋmɛi ni tsɔɔ akɛ esumɔɔ ni ele nɔ ni yaa nɔ lɛ yɛ ehiɛ. Nɛkɛ kooloo fɔŋ nɛɛ pɛ ji mɔ ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ ni etamɔ nɔ ni yɔse mibotemɔ; ejaakɛ lɛ pɛ ji mɔ ni nyɛɛɛ akane nii, ni no hewɔ lɛ, ekaneee nakai niŋmaai ni mli ejɔ ni yɔɔ gbogbo lɛ he lɛ. Kɛji ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi ni amɛgbɛi je kpo yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ ateŋ mɔ ko yɛ asafo lɛ mli agbɛnɛ lɛ, mileee; shi osharai ni aŋmalako amɛhe saji awoko mli yɛ ŋshɔŋ wuoyeli lɛ mli lɛ fa babaoo, ni eka shi faŋŋ akɛ yei babaoo ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ wo hiɛ kɛji jeee awerɛhoyeli ko ni bɛ naagbee lɛ he tsɔnei lɛ, ni minuɔ he akɛ abua mɛi ni yɔɔ mihiɛ lɛ anaa yɛ biɛ, ni yɛ amɛtsuii ni tsaaa lɛ amli lɛ, nakai tɛi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ ni amɛnaa lɛ haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli.

Ohh! nyɛ ni afu nyɛgbohii yɛ jwɛi ŋmɔji ashishi lɛ; ni damɔ fɔfɔii ateŋ lɛ baanyɛ akɛɛ—biɛ, misuɔlɔ lɛ kã biɛ ; nyɛleee amaŋfɔfeemɔ ni baa yɛ mɛi ni tamɔ enɛɛmɛi amli lɛ. Mɛɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ nakai tɛi diji ni yɔɔ husu diji ni haaa sũ ko nɔ lɛ amli nɛkɛ! Mɛɛ nijiaŋwujee ni yɔɔ nakai niŋmaai ni nyɛɛɛ atsake lɛ mli nɛkɛ! Egbee shi ni egbee shi kɛjɛ ehe be fioo; kɛkɛ ni ewo ehiɛ nɔ ekwɛ amɛ ekoŋŋ, ni ejie miishɛɛ ni mli kwɔ kpo yɛ ehiŋmɛii amli, beni ekɛ ŋwɛi miishɛɛ bolɔ akɛ,—“Shi oh! lɛlɛ mli nanemɛi! yɛ kelboard nine ni yɔɔ haomɔ fɛɛ haomɔ mli lɛ, miishɛɛ ko ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ yɛ; ni nakai miishɛɛ lɛ yiteŋ nɔ kwɔ, fe haomɔ lɛ shishi lɛ mli kwɔ. shɔŋŋ, shɔŋŋ kɛyaa ŋwɛi, kɛ mligbɛ miishɛɛ—ni teɔ shi ewoɔ shikpɔŋ nɛɛ nɔ nyɔŋmɔi ni yɔɔ henɔwomɔ lɛ kɛ maŋtsɛmɛi lɛ, be fɛɛ be lɛ, edamɔ shi shiŋŋ kɛha mɔ ni eniji ni he wa lɛ fiɔ esɛɛ lolo lɛ, beni shishigbɛ shishiumɔ je nɛɛ lɛlɛ lɛ etee shi yɛ eshishi lɛ Miishɛɛ ji lɛ, mɔ ni kɛ esha ko haaa lɛ, ni egbeɔ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ. yɛ Senators kɛ Kojolɔi atadei lɛ ashishi.Miishɛɛ, —miishɛɛ ni fe fɛɛ ji lɛ, mɔ ni ekpɛlɛɛɛ mla loo nuŋtsɔ ko nɔ, shi moŋ eji maŋhedɔɔ kɛha ŋwɛi Miishɛɛ ji lɛ, mɔ ni ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ basabasafeelɔi akuu lɛ mli lɛ nyɛŋ akpokpo kɔkɔɔkɔ kɛjɛ esuɔmɔnaa nii ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ nɛɛ mli koni ekɛ lɛ awo shi, baanyɛ ekɛ enaagbee mumɔ akɛɛ—O Tsɛ!—ni mile titri kɛtsɔ Otso lɛ nɔ—mɔ ni gboɔ loo mɔ ni gbooo, biɛ migbo.

Ewieee nɔ ko dɔŋŋ, shi moŋ ekɛ jɔɔmɔ ko shwie shi fiofio, ni ekɛ eniji ha ehiɛ nɔ, ni nakai ehi shi ni ekoto shi, kɛyashi gbɔmɛi lɛ fɛɛ shi, ni lɛ pɛ eshwɛ yɛ he ni eyɔɔ lɛ.
