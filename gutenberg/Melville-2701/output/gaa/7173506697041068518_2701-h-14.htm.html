YITSƆƆ 67. Kpoomɔ.

Hɔgbaa gbɛkɛ ji no, ni Hejɔɔmɔ Gbi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ nyiɛ sɛɛ! Hejɔɔmɔ Gbi lɛ mligbalamɔ he tsɔɔlɔi ni ji ex officio lɛ fɛɛ ji ŋshɔŋlooi. Atsake Pequod ni akɛ shwuɔ wu fee lɛ afee nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ; lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ fɛɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ji loogbelɔ. Kulɛ obaasusu akɛ wɔkɛ tsinai tsuji akpei nyɔŋma miihã ŋshɔ mli nyɔŋmɔi lɛ.

Yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, tsɔnei wuji ni akɛfoɔ nii lɛ, kɛ nibii krokomɛi ni tsii ni yɔɔ blɔkii akuu ni akɛ eŋɔli fee, ni gbɔmɔ kome pɛ nyɛŋ awó nɔ lɛ—atswa wain yibii akuu wulu nɛɛ kɛtee ŋwɛigbɛ ni akɛwo shishigbɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yitso lɛ nɔ, ni ji he ni wa fe fɛɛ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ. No sɛɛ lɛ, akɛ kpãa ni tamɔ hawser ni tsɔɔ nɛkɛ nibii ni mli wa nɛɛ amli lɛ naagbee lɛ yaa windlass lɛ nɔ, ni akɛ tackles lɛ shishigbɛ blɔki wulu lɛ shwieɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔ; akɛ blubber ŋshɔŋloo wulu ni tsiimɔ aaafee pound oha lɛ fĩ nɛkɛ blɔki nɛɛ he. Ni agbɛnɛ ni amɛfo amɛhe yɛ fãi srɔtoi anɔ yɛ sɛɛgbɛ lɛ, Starbuck kɛ Stubb, ni ji hefatalɔi lɛ, ni amɛkɛ amɛ ŋtayaai kakadaji lɛ ewo amɛhe lɛ, bɔi bu ko ni akɛwoɔ gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli koni akɛ ŋtayaa lɛ awo mli yɛ sɛɛgbɛ ŋtayaai enyɔ lɛ ateŋ nɔ ni bɛŋkɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ nɔ pɛ. Kɛ afee enɛ lɛ, afoɔ line ko ni mli kwɔ ni ji fã ko ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ he kɛbɔleɔ bu lɛ he, ni akɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ woɔ mli, ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi lɛ akuu titri lɛ tswaa lalai ni yɔɔ gbeyei, ni amrɔ nɛɛ amɛjeɔ shishi amɛhoɔ yɛ asafo babaoo kome mli yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa. Beni amrɔ nɔŋŋ lɛ, lɛlɛ muu lɛ fɛɛ shwieɔ ehe kɛyaa esɛɛ; bolt fɛɛ bolt ni yɔɔ emli lɛ jeɔ shishi tamɔ shĩa momo ko mli ŋmeii ayitsei yɛ fɛ̃i be mli; ekpokpoɔ, ekpokpoɔ, ni ekɛ e-mast-heads ni eshe gbeyei lɛ tsɔɔ ŋwɛi. Ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔ babaoo, yɛ be mli ni kɔɔyɔɔŋ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni shwieɔ shi ni yeɔ ebuaa kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ amli lɛ haa hetoo; kɛyashi naagbee lɛ, anuɔ gbee ko ni yɔɔ oya ni haa mɔ naa kpɛɔ ehe; kɛ ŋmɔlɔ kpele lɛ, lɛlɛ lɛ shwieɔ shi kɛyaa ŋwɛi kɛ sɛɛ kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli, ni kunimyeli tsɔne lɛ teɔ shi kɛyaa hiŋmɛi nɔ ni egbalaa klɛŋklɛŋ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ naagbee ni ji fã ni ajie lɛ kpo lɛ sɛɛ. Amrɔ nɛɛ, taakɛ blubber lɛ haa ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he pɛpɛɛpɛ tamɔ bɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hewolo lɛ feɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he lɛ, nakai nɔŋŋ ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli pɛpɛɛpɛ tamɔ bɔ ni bei komɛi lɛ ajieɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛtsɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ni akɛwoɔ ŋshɔŋ lɛ nɔ lɛ. Ejaakɛ tsɔne ni kɔɔyɔɔŋ tsɔne lɛ hiɛɔ mli be fɛɛ be lɛ haa ŋshɔŋloo lɛ shwieɔ shi kɛyaa kɛbaa yɛ nu lɛ mli, ni be mli ni blubber ni yɔɔ tsɔne kome mli lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ gbɛ kome nɔ yɛ tsɔne ni atsɛɔ lɛ “sɛkɛf” lɛ nɔ lɛ, yɛ be kome mli lɛ, Starbuck kɛ Stubb, ni ji hefatalɔi lɛ atsɔnei lɛ shwieɔ shi; ni bɔ ni ajieɔ lɛ oya yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ lɛ, ni lɛɛlɛŋ hu, kɛtsɔ nakai nifeemɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ nɔ lɛ, be fɛɛ be lɛ, awoɔ lɛ nɔ kɛyaa ŋwɛi kɛyaa ŋwɛi aahu kɛyashiɔ eŋwɛi naagbee lɛ yeɔ ŋwɛi titri lɛ; no sɛɛ lɛ hii ni yɔɔ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ kpaɔ amɛhe shwiemɔ, ni be kukuoo ko loo enyɔ ko lɛ, lá ni hoɔ waa lɛ shwieɔ shi kɛyaa kɛbaa tamɔ nɔ ni ajie kɛjɛ ŋwɛi, ni esa akɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ akwɛ ehe jogbaŋŋ koni etsi ehe kɛjɛ he kɛji efo, kɛjee nakai lɛ, ebaanyɛ ekɛ etoi awo adeka mli ni ekɛ eyitso ashwie ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ.

Agbɛnɛ, harpooneers ni baa kpee lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome kɛ tawuu nii kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ boarding-sword lɛ yaa hiɛ, ni beni ekwɛɔ hegbɛ ni eyɔɔ lɛ, ekɛ ŋaa shwieɔ bu ko ni da waa yɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni miihoso lɛ shishigbɛ fã lɛ mli. No sɛɛ lɛ, akɛ tsɔne wulu ni ji enyɔ ni tsakeɔ ehe lɛ naagbee lɛ woɔ bu nɛɛ mli bɔni afee ni ehiɛ blubber lɛ nɔ, bɔni afee ni asaa he aha nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, nɛkɛ klantetsɛ ni he esa nɛɛ, ni bɔɔ niji fɛɛ kɔkɔ koni amɛtsi amɛhe lɛ, feɔ jeŋ nilee mli ŋmɔlɔ shikome ekoŋŋ yɛ mass lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ fãi fioo komɛi ni yɔɔ sɛɛgbɛ, ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ, ni shwieɔ shi lɛ, fãa lɛ mli enyɔ kwraa; bɔni afee ni yɛ be mli ni shishigbɛ fã kuku lɛ yaa oya lolo lɛ, ŋwɛi fã kakadaŋŋ ni atsɛɔ lɛ blanket-piece lɛ shwieɔ shi faŋŋ, ni fɛɛ efee klalo kɛha shibaa. Agbɛnɛ, mɛi ni yaa hiɛ lɛ jeɔ amɛ lala lɛ shishi ekoŋŋ, ni yɛ be mli ni tsɔne kome lɛ miifo ni ekɛ tsɔne ni ji enyɔ lɛ miiho kɛmiijɛ ŋshɔŋ loo lɛ mli lɛ, ekroko lɛ shwieɔ shi fiofio, ni klɛŋklɛŋ tsɔne lɛ yaa shi kɛtsɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa gbɛjegbɛ wulu ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ lɛ nɔ, kɛyaa shĩa ko ni bɛ tsɔne mli ni atsɛɔ lɛ blubber-room lɛ mli. Niji srɔtoi ni yɔɔ oya lɛ yaa nɔ amɛfoɔ blanket kakadaŋŋ lɛ mli tamɔ nɔ ni eji onufui ni akɛ tsɔne ebɔ he ni hiɛ kã babaoo. Ni nakai ji bɔ ni nitsumɔ lɛ yaa nɔ; tackles enyɔ lɛ ni hoɔ kɛ shi yɛ be kome mli; whale kɛ windlass fɛɛ miihoso, heavers lɛ miilá, blubber-room gentlemen lɛ miihoso, hefatalɔi lɛ miihoso amɛhe, lɛlɛ lɛ miihoso, ni niji fɛɛ miikã kitã yɛ bei komɛi amli, kɛtsɔ gbɛ ni atsɔɔ nɔ ajieɔ béi ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ shi lɛ nɔ.





YITSƆƆ 68. Blanket lɛ.

Mikɛ mijwɛŋmɔ ema nakai saneyitso ni bɛ mlifu, ni ji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hewolo lɛ nɔ fioo. Mikɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ niiashikpamɔ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ ebɔ he ŋwanejeei, kɛ adebɔɔ he nilelɔi ni ekase nii ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ. Mishishijee susumɔ lɛ tsakeee; shi eji susumɔ ko kɛkɛ.

Sanebimɔ lɛ ji, mɛni ji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hewolo lɛ, ni nɛgbɛ yɔɔ? O le nɔ́ nɛ e blubber ɔ ji momo. Nakai blubber lɛ ji nɔ ko ni tamɔ tsina loo ni mli wa, ni bɛŋkɛ kpaakpa, shi eyɛ mlɛo, eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni elɛɛmɔ jeɔ shishi kɛjɛɔ inchi kpaanyɔ loo nyɔŋma kɛyashiɔ inchi nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ kɛ nyɔŋma kɛ enumɔ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni shishijee lɛ ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni eji kwashiai asane akɛ aaawie adebɔɔ fɛɛ adebɔɔ hewolo he akɛ eji nakai nɔ ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ kɛ nɔ ni mli wa lɛ, shi yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, enɛɛmɛi jeee ŋwanejeei ni teɔ shi ewoɔ susumɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ; ejaakɛ onyɛŋ owó ŋshɔŋloo kroko ni mli eyi obɔ kɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ nɔ fe nakai ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔŋŋ; kɛ kooloo fɛɛ kooloo kponɔgbɛ fã ni haa ehe lɛ, kɛji akɛ eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ bɔ ni sa lɛ, mɛni ji nɔ ni baanyɛ afee ja hewolo lɛ? Eji anɔkwale akɛ, obaanyɛ okɛ onine ajie nɔ ko ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni bɛ naagbee ni mli tse, ni tamɔ isinglass ni mli tse fe fɛɛ lɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, kɛjɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ gbonyo ni efiteee lɛ mli, shi eshwɛ fioo ni ebaafee nɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni eyɛ blɛoo tamɔ satin; ni ji, dani ebaagbi, beni jeee akɛ egbɔjɔɔ ni egbɔjɔɔ kɛkɛ, shi moŋ ebafeɔ nɔ ni wa ni egbɔjɔɔ moŋ. Miyɛ ŋmeii ni egbi tamɔ nɛkɛ babaoo, ni mikɛtsuɔ nii kɛkadiɔ mi-whale-woji lɛ. Eyɛ faŋŋ, taakɛ mikɛɛ tsutsu lɛ; ni akɛni akɛwo baafa ni akala lɛ nɔ hewɔ lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ mikɛ mihe ewo mli akɛ eyɛ hewalɛ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli. Bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, eji nɔ ni ŋɔɔ akɛ oookane ŋshɔŋlooi ahe saji kɛtsɔ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhiŋmɛii anɔ, taakɛ ekolɛ obaakɛɛ lɛ. Shi nɔ ni mikɛ tsɔne miikudɔ yɛ biɛ lɛ ji enɛ. Nakai nɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni bɛ naagbee, ni ji isinglass, ni, mikpɛlɛɔ nɔ akɛ, ekɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ gbɔmɔtso muu lɛ fɛɛ woɔ mli lɛ, jeee nɔ ni esa akɛ abu lɛ akɛ adebɔɔ lɛ hewolo, tamɔ hewolo lɛ hewolo, kɛ aawie he lɛ; ejaakɛ eji ŋmɔlɔ kɛkɛ akɛ aaakɛɛ, akɛ ŋshɔŋloo kpeteŋkpele lɛ hewolo ni sa lɛ mli tse ni eyɛ mlɛo fe gbekɛ ni afɔ lɛ ehee lɛ hewolo. Shi jeee enɛ dɔŋŋ.

Kɛ wɔsusu akɛ blubber lɛ ji ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hewolo; no sɛɛ lɛ, beni nɛkɛ hewolo nɛɛ, tamɔ yɛ Sperm Whale ni da waa lɛ gbɛfaŋ lɛ, baaha mu ni fa fe tsɛnsii oha lɛ aba; ni, kɛ́ asusu akɛ, yɛ yibɔ naa, loo yɛ etsitsinɔnii mli lɛ, nakai mu lɛ, yɛ bɔ ni ejie lɛ kpo lɛ mli lɛ, eji mlijaa ejwɛ mli etɛ pɛ, ni jeee atade lɛ mli nibii fɛɛ; no hewɔ lɛ abaanyɛ aná susumɔ ko yɛ bɔ ni nakai nibii ni wala yɔɔ amɛmli lɛ fa ha lɛ he, ni efã ko kɛkɛ haa nu ŋshɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ baa. Kɛ́ okɛ barɛl nyɔŋma to tɔn kome he lɛ, oyɛ tɔn nyɔŋma kɛha ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hewolo lɛ mli nibii lɛ mlijaa etɛ pɛ ni tsiimɔ yɔɔ mli lɛ.

Yɛ shihilɛ mli lɛ, Sperm Whale lɛ hiɛ ni anaa lɛ jeee nɔ ni baa shi fe fɛɛ yɛ naakpɛɛ nii babaoo ni ekɛhaa lɛ ateŋ. Nɔ ni miihe ashɛ be fɛɛ be lɛ, afo mli fɛɛ ni afo mli ekoŋŋ kɛ okadii ni ja ni anyɛɛɛ akane ni ato he gbɛjianɔ jogbaŋŋ, nɔ ko tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ Italia line ni hi fe fɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ. Shi etamɔ nɔ ni aŋmalaaa okadii nɛɛ awoɔ isinglass nibii ni atsĩ tã yɛ yiteŋgbɛ lɛ anɔ, shi moŋ etamɔ nɔ ni anaa kɛtsɔɔ nɔ, tamɔ nɔ ni aŋmala amɛ awo gbɔmɔtso lɛ diɛŋtsɛ nɔ. Ni jeee enɛ pɛ hu. Yɛ shihilɛi komɛi amli lɛ, kɛha hiŋmɛi ni kwɛɔ nii oya lɛ, nakai okadii ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ, tamɔ yɛ niŋmaa diɛŋtsɛ mli lɛ, shi amɛhaa shikpɔŋ kɛha okadii krokomɛi ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ. Enɛɛmɛi ji hieroglyphic; ni ji akɛ, kɛji otsɛ nakai teemɔŋ cyphers ni yɔɔ piramid gbogboi lɛ anɔ lɛ akɛ hieroglyphics lɛ, belɛ no ji wiemɔ ni sa ni akɛbaatsu nii yɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ tsakpaa lɛ mli. Kɛtsɔ hieroglyphic ni yɔɔ Sperm Whale kome nɔ lɛ he kaimɔ ni mihiɛɔ mli lɛ nɔ lɛ, minaa kpɛ mihe waa kɛ plɛte ko ni damɔ shi kɛha Indiabii niŋmaa okadii momo ni akpɛ́ yɛ hieroglyphic ni ale waa ni yɔɔ Mississippi Ŋwɛi ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ nɔ. Taakɛ nakai teemɔŋ tɛi lɛ ji lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ni akadi teemɔŋ lɛ hu kã he eji nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ shishi. Nɛkɛ wiemɔ ni awie yɛ India tɛi lɛ ahe nɛɛ kaiɔ mi nɔ kroko hu. Kɛfata nibii krokomɛi fɛɛ ni Sperm Whale lɛ kponɔgbɛ jieɔ lɛ kpo lɛ he lɛ, efɔɔɔ esɛɛ kpojiemɔ, ni titri lɛ esɛɛgbɛ, ni ajie yɛ daa linear hiɛ su lɛ fã ko mli, yɛ ŋmɔlɔi babaoo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ hewɔ, fɛɛ ni bɛ gbɛjianɔtoo mli, nɔ ni baa trukaa. Esa akɛ makɛɛ akɛ nakai New England tɛi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, ni Agassiz susuɔ akɛ amɛhiɛ yiwalɛ ni akɛ ais gɔji wuji ni hoɔ lɛ kpeɔ lɛ he okadii lɛ—esa akɛ makɛɛ, akɛ esaaa akɛ nakai tɛi lɛ tamɔ Sperm Whale lɛ fioo yɛ enɛ pɔtɛɛ mli. Etamɔ nɔ ni misusuɔ hu akɛ ekolɛ ŋmeii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli lɛ jɛɔ henyɛlɔi ni kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi krokomɛi yeɔ lɛ mli; ejaakɛ mikadi amɛ fe fɛɛ yɛ tsinahii wuji ni eda ni ji tsinahii srɔtoi lɛ amli.

Wiemɔ kome loo enyɔ krokomɛi ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hewolo loo etsitsi he sane nɛɛ he. Akɛɛ momo, akɛ ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ edɛŋ yɛ fãi kakadaji amli, ni atsɛɔ lɛ blanket-pieces. Taakɛ ŋshɔ mli wiemɔi babaoo ji lɛ, enɛ hu yɛ miishɛɛ waa ni ehe hiaa waa. Ejaakɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ebɔle e-blubber lɛ he diɛŋtsɛ tamɔ nɔ ni akɛwoɔ blanket loo counterpane diɛŋtsɛ mli; aloo, nɔ ni hi fe fɛɛ lɛ, India poncho ko shwie eyitso nɔ, ni efo enaagbee lɛ he. Nɛkɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ egbɔmɔtso lɛ mli ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli nɛɛ hewɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nyɛɔ ehiɔ shi jogbaŋŋ yɛ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔi fɛɛ amli, yɛ ŋshɔi, bei, kɛ ŋshɔkei fɛɛ amli lɛ. Mɛni baaba Greenland ŋshɔŋloo ko nɔ, kɛ wɔkɛɛ lɛ, yɛ nakai ŋshɔi ni hosoɔ ni ais yɔɔ mli ni yɔɔ Kooyigbɛ lɛ amli, kɛji akɛ akɛ e-surtout ni yɔɔ fɛo lɛ haaa lɛ? Eji anɔkwale akɛ, anaa loo krokomɛi ni he yɔɔ oya fe nine yɛ nakai Hyperborean nui lɛ amli; shi enɛɛmɛi, akadi, ji oloo ni lá mli ejɔ, ni bɛ fɛ̃i, ni emli diɛŋtsɛ ji friji; bɔɔ nii, ni shãa amɛhe la yɛ ais gɔŋ ko shishi, taakɛ gbɛfalɔ ko ni yɔɔ fɛ̃i be mli lɛ baafee la yɛ gbɔiatoohe la hiɛ lɛ; yɛ be mli ni, tamɔ gbɔmɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ yɛ fɛ̃i kɛ lá ni mli edɔ. Ha ela lɛ afee fɛ̃i, ni egbo. Kwɛ bɔ ni eji naakpɛɛ ha nakai beaŋ—ja shishitsɔɔmɔ sɛɛ—akɛ nɛkɛ kooloo wulu nɛɛ, ni gbɔmɔtsoŋ dɔlɛ he hiaa lɛ tamɔ bɔ ni ehe hiaa gbɔmɔ lɛ; kwɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ naakpɛɛ ha akɛ abaana lɛ yɛ shia, ni ekɛ ehe awo nakai Arctic nui lɛ amli kɛyashi enaabu mli kɛha wala! he ni, kɛ́ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi gbee shi yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ anaa amɛ, yɛ nyɔji komɛi asɛɛ, ni amɛfee ais ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ kɛtee ais ŋmɔji amli, tamɔ bɔ ni anaa ŋmei ni akɛ amber ekpɛtɛ he lɛ. Shi nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ fe fɛɛ ji ni ale, taakɛ shikpamɔi etsɔɔ mli lɛ, akɛ Polar whale lá mli dɔɔ fe Borneo negro lá yɛ hulutsoo be mli.

Etamɔ nɔ ni misusuɔ akɛ, wɔnaa su kpakpa ni anáaa ni ji aŋkroaŋkro hewalɛ ni mli wa, kɛ su kpakpa ni anáaa ni ji gbogboi ni mli tse, kɛ su ni anáaa ni ji mligbɛ ŋwɛi ŋta lɛ yɛ biɛ. Oh, nuu! kwɛ ni ofee ohe nɔkwɛmɔnɔ yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ sɛɛ! Ani bo hu, okɛ ohe awo mli yɛ ais teŋ. Ani bo hu, ohi shi yɛ je nɛɛ mli ni obɛ mli. Feemɔ kpoo yɛ equator lɛ nɔ; ha ola mli nu lɛ ahi Pole lɛ nɔ. Tamɔ St. Peter dome wulu lɛ, kɛ tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo wulu lɛ, hiɛ mli, O gbɔmɔ! yɛ be fɛɛ mli lɛ, bo diɛŋtsɛ ohe dɔlɛ.

Shi kwɛ bɔ ni eji nɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni hiɛnɔkamɔ ko bɛ he akɛ aaatsɔɔ nibii kpakpai nɛɛ! Yɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai amli lɛ, kwɛ bɔ ni mɛi fioo ko pɛ yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ tamɔ St. Peter nɔ lɛ! bɔɔ nii, kwɛ bɔ ni amɛhe faaa tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ!





YITSƆƆ 69. Fuumɔ.

“Gbalamɔ kɔsɔŋkɔsɔi lɛ, nyɛhaa gbonyo lɛ aya sɛɛgbɛ!”

Amrɔ nɛɛ tackles wuji lɛ etsu amɛnitsumɔ. Ŋshɔloo ni afo eyitso lɛ gbɔmɔtso yɛŋ ni ajie lɛ kpo lɛ kpɛɔ tamɔ marble gbonyobu; eyɛ mli akɛ etsake yɛ ehiɛ su mli moŋ, shi elaajeee nɔ ko nɔ ko babaoo yɛ gbɛ ni anyɛɔ ayɔseɔ nɔ. Eji nɔ ko wulu lolo. Fiofio lɛ, efoɔ kɛyaa shɔŋŋ, nu ni ebɔle ehe lɛ mligbala ni ŋshɔŋlooi ni eshɛɛɛ amɛhe lɛ efolɔ amɛshwie shi, ni kɔɔyɔɔ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi lɛ mli ewo wu kɛ kooloi ni bolɔɔ waa, ni amɛnaabu tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi babaoo ni woɔ mɔ he guɔ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ amli lɛ. Fantom agbo yɛŋ ni bɛ yitso lɛ hoɔ kɛyaa shɔŋŋ kɛjɛɔ lɛlɛ lɛ he, ni tso fɛɛ tso ni efoɔ lɛ, nɔ ni tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi akutsei ni ji sqɛɛ kɛ kooloi akutsei ni ji kubik lɛ, haa gbɔmɔgbee gbee lɛ yaa hiɛ. ŋmɛlɛtswai babaoo kɛ ŋmɛlɛtswai babaoo kɛjɛɔ lɛlɛ ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaadamɔ shi lɛ mli lɛ, anaa nakai nɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ. Yɛ ŋwɛi ni atatu bɛ mli ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ shishi, yɛ ŋshɔ ni ŋɔɔ lɛ hiɛ ni yɔɔ fɛo lɛ nɔ, ni miishɛɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ tswaa lɛ, nakai gbele babaoo lɛ hoɔ kɛyaa kɛbaa, kɛyashiɔ be mli ni elaajeɔ yɛ nibii ni bɛ naagbee lɛ amli.

Ayɛ gbonyo bu ko ni yɔɔ awerɛho fe fɛɛ ni yeɔ mɔ he fɛo fe fɛɛ! Ŋshɔ mli ŋmeii lɛ fɛɛ yɛ Nyɔŋmɔjamɔ mli awerɛhoyeli mli, kɔɔyɔŋ mli ŋmeii lɛ fɛɛ yɛ be mli ni amɛwoɔ atadei diji loo ŋmeii. Yɛ shihilɛ mli lɛ, kulɛ amɛteŋ mɛi fioo ko pɛ baaye abua ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, I ween, kɛji ekolɛ ehe bahia lɛ; shi yɛ egbonyo bu lɛ mli okpɔlɔŋmɛɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, amɛfeɔ amɛhe fɛo fe fɛɛ. Oo, shikpɔŋ nɔ vultureism ni yɔɔ gbeyei! ni jeee ŋshɔŋloo ni he wa fe fɛɛ lɛ eye ehe kɛjɛ mli.

Ni jeee naagbee lɛ hu nɛ. Bɔ ni gbɔmɔtso lɛ he tseee lɛ, mumɔ ko ni yeɔ he nyɔmɔ lɛ hiɔ shi ni efoɔ ehe koni ekɛwo ehe gbeyei. Tawuulɔ ko ni sheɔ gbeyei loo tsɔne ni akɛtaoɔ nibii amli ni tsɔɔ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ kwɛɔ kɛjɛɔ shɔŋŋ, beni he ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ haa okpoi ni shwieɔ shi lɛ feɔ duŋ lɛ, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, etsɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi yɛŋ ni hoɔ yɛ hulu lɛ mli lɛ lolo, kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi yɛŋ ni shwieɔ shi kɛyaa ŋwɛi kɛteɔ shi ewoɔ lɛ; amrɔ nɔŋŋ lɛ, akɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ gbonyo ni yeee lɛ awui, ni eniji ni hosoɔ lɛ ewo tso lɛ mli— ŋshɔkei, tɛi, kɛ ŋshɔkei ni fiteɔ nii yɛ biɛ: kwɛmɔ ohe jogbaŋŋ! Ni ekolɛ afii babaoo yɛ no sɛɛ lɛ, lɛlɛi tsiɔ amɛhe kɛjɛɔ he lɛ; ni amɛtuɔ amɛhe tamɔ bɔ ni tooi ni bɛ jwɛŋmɔ lɛ tuɔ amɛhe kɛjɛɔ he ko ni nɔ ko bɛ mli lɛ, ejaakɛ shishijee lɛ amɛhiɛnyiɛlɔ lɛ tuɔ yɛ jɛmɛ beni ahiɛ tso ko mli lɛ. O mla ni kɔɔ nɔkwɛmɔnii ahe lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ; jɛmɛ ji he ni otsuɔ kusumii ahe nii yɛ; sane ni kɔɔ ohemɔkɛyelii momoi ni eshɛko shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ lɛ ahe, ni amrɔ nɛɛ efoŋ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli po lɛ he lɛ yɛ! Ɔrtodɔksi yɛ!

No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ be mli ni yɛ shihilɛ mli lɛ, ekolɛ ŋshɔŋloo wulu lɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ ji gbeyeishemɔ diɛŋtsɛ kɛha ehenyɛlɔi lɛ, yɛ egbele mli lɛ, esusuma lɛ bafeɔ gbeyeishemɔ ni bɛ hewalɛ kɛha jeŋ ko.

Ani oheɔ mumɔi oyeɔ, minaanyo? Mumɔi krokomɛi yɛ fe Cock-Lane nɔ lɛ, kɛ hii ni mli kwɔ kwraa fe Datrɛfonyo Johnson ni heɔ amɛ amɛyeɔ.





YITSƆƆ 70. Sfinx lɛ.

Kulɛ esaaa akɛ ajieɔ kɛjɛɔ mli akɛ dani abaajie leviathan lɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ kwraa lɛ, no mli lɛ afo eyitso. Amrɔ nɛɛ, Sperm Whale lɛ yitso ni afo lɛ ji jeŋ nilee mli gbɔmɔtso lɛ he nitsumɔ ko, ni whale opireshɛnfeelɔi ni yɔɔ niiashikpamɔ lɛ kɛ amɛhe fɔ̃ɔ nɔ waa: ni jeee akɛ yiŋtoo ko bɛ he.

Susumɔ he akɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ bɛ nɔ ko ni abaanyɛ atsɛ lɛ tsitsi yɛ gbɛ ni ja nɔ; nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ enɛ lɛ, he ni etamɔ nɔ ni eyitso kɛ egbɔmɔtso lɛ feɔ ekome lɛ, jɛmɛ, yɛ nakai he lɛ nɔŋŋ, ji he ni egbɔmɔtso lɛ fã ni mli wa fe fɛɛ lɛ yɔɔ. Kaimɔ hu akɛ, esa akɛ datrɛfonyo ni feɔ mɔ opireshɛn lɛ afee opireshɛn kɛjɛ ŋwɛi, ni aaafee ninetalɔi kpaanyɔ loo nyɔŋma abote ekɛ ehe awo ekɛ e-sane lɛ teŋ, ni eshwɛ fioo ni nakai sane lɛ baatsɔ ŋshɔ ko ni ehiɛ etsake, ni ebɔle ehe, ni bei pii lɛ efeɔ basabasa ni etswaa lɛ mli. Ha ehi ojwɛŋmɔ mli hu akɛ yɛ shihilɛi ni esaaa nɛɛ amli lɛ, esa akɛ efo naji babaoo yɛ heloo lɛ mli; ni yɛ nakai shikpɔŋ shishi gbɛ nɔ lɛ, ni ekɛ ehiɛ kome po akakwɛ ŋmei ni shwieɔ shi be fɛɛ be ni afee nakai lɛ mli lɛ, esa akɛ ekɛ ŋaa atsu nii ni ekɛtsi ehe kɛjɛ fãi fɛɛ ni bɛŋkɛ, ni agu lɛ ahe, ni ejara sɛɛgbɛ lɛ mli pɛpɛɛpɛ yɛ he ko ni he hiaa waa waa kɛtsɔ ekɛwomɔ tsitsi lɛ mli lɛ nɔ. Belɛ, ani Stubb henɔwomɔ akɛ ebi minitii nyɔŋma pɛ ni ekɛfo sperm whale ko yitso lɛ haaa ona naakpɛɛ?

Kɛji akɛ afo lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ, ashwieɔ yitso lɛ kɛyaa sɛɛgbɛ ni akɛ kpãa fĩɔ jɛmɛ kɛyashiɔ be mli ni ajieɔ gbɔmɔtso lɛ. Kɛ afee nakai lɛ, kɛji akɛ eji ŋshɔŋloo bibioo ko nɔ lɛ, awoɔ lɛ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ koni ajɛ hiɛ ashɛrɛ ashwie. Shi, kɛ leviathan ni eda kwraa lɛ, enɛ nyɛŋ aba mli; ejaakɛ sperm whale lɛ yitso lɛ fuaa nɔ ni miihe ashɛ egbɔmɔtso muu lɛ fɛɛ mlijaa etɛ mli ekome, ni koni akɛ jatsu ni tamɔ nɛkɛ awo mli kwraa, kɛtsɔ whaletsɛ ko nitsumɔi wuji ni ekɛbaatsu nii lɛ po nɔ lɛ, enɛ ji yakayaka nii tamɔ bɔ ni abɔɔ mɔdɛŋ akɛ akɛ Dutch ŋmɔ ko asusu jwetrii ahe sɛkɛl nɔ lɛ.

Beni afo Pequod ŋshɔŋloo lɛ yitso ni ajie egbɔmɔtso lɛ, awo eyitso lɛ nɔ kɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ—aaafee ŋshɔ lɛ fã kɛjɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli, bɔni afee ni eshishijee nibii lɛ anyɛ atswa lɛ yɛ gbɛ ni da nɔ lolo. Ni jɛmɛ kɛ lɛlɛ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ enɔ waa lɛ, yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ shishigbɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ ayitso ni gbalaa kɛbaa shi lɛ hewɔ, kɛ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ nakai fã lɛ fɛɛ nine ni jeɔ kpo tamɔ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkei lɛ anɔ lɛ; jɛmɛ lɛ, nakai yitso ni lá hoɔ lɛ fĩ Pequod lɛ tsitsi nɔ tamɔ Holoferne wulu lɛ yitso ni jɛ Yudit bɛlɛ mli lɛ.

Beni agbe naagbee nitsumɔ nɛɛ naa lɛ, no mli lɛ shwane shɛ, ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi lɛ tee shishi kɛtee amɛ gbɛkɛ niyenii lɛ shishi. Diŋŋfeemɔ ye ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ tsutsu basabasafeemɔ shi amrɔ nɛɛ efee hamahama lɛ nɔ. Akɔɔble ni mli wa ni yɔɔ dioo, tamɔ jeŋ muu fɛɛ lotus ni ji ŋmɔŋ lɛ, miitsɔɔ ebaa ni bɛ gbee ni asusuuu he lɛ mli babaoo yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ.

Be kuku ko ho, ni Ahab pɛ jɛ etsu lɛ mli kɛtee nɛkɛ dede ni bɛ nɛɛ mli. Beni etsɔ ehe fioo yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, emɛ ni ekwɛ ŋshɔ lɛ sɛɛ, kɛkɛ ni ebote ŋshɔ lɛ mli fiofio ni eŋɔ Stubb ŋshɔ lɛ mli ŋshɔ lɛ sɛɛ—ni eshwɛ yɛ jɛmɛ lolo yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ yitso ni afo lɛ sɛɛ—ni etswa lɛ kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ shishigbɛ fã lɛ mli, ni ekɛ enine kroko lɛ wo ŋshɔ lɛ shishi, ni ekɛ enine kroko lɛ fɔ shi. ni akɛ hiɛdɔɔ kwɛ yitso nɛɛ nɔ.

Eji yitso diŋŋ ni awo nɔ tsɔi; ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ jɛmɛ yɛ dioofeemɔ ni naa wa waa lɛ mli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni Sphynx nɔ yɛ ŋa lɛ nɔ. “Wiemɔ, bo yitso wulu ni abuɔ lɛ,” Ahab wie kɛ ŋmɔlɔ, “ni, eyɛ mli akɛ obɛ tsitsi, shi eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ yɛ biɛ kɛ biɛ; wiemɔ, yitso ni he wa, ni okɛɛ wɔ teemɔŋ nɔ ni yɔɔ omli. Nuŋtsɔmɛi fɛɛ ateŋ lɛ, bo ji mɔ ni bote mli fe fɛɛ. Nakai yitso lɛ bɛ nɔ, je lɛŋ shishitoo mlai lɛ sɛɛ, he ni nyɛmɛi ni bɛ wɔɔ lɛ kɛ amɛwala baaha koni amɛkɛ amɛ awo shi lɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ni egbe lɛ lɛ tee nɔ ehi shi lolo ni nɔ ko nɔ ko efeee lɛ—yɛ be mli ni sarawagbamɔi ni yaa oya lɛ hosoɔ lɛlɛ ni bɛŋkɛ lɛ ni kulɛ ebaawo wu ni ja ni shweɔ nii waa lɛ, O yitso, ona nɔ ni fa bɔ ni sa ni baaha shibɔlemɔ ŋulamii lɛ amli agbala mli ni efee nɔ ko ni bɛ hemɔkɛyeli mli!

“Lɛlɛ ho!” kunimyeli gbee ko bolɔ kɛjɛ main-mast-yitso lɛ mli.

“Aye? Ojogbaŋŋ, agbɛnɛ, no ji miishɛɛ,” Ahab bolɔ, ni ete shi trukaa, yɛ be mli ni atatui muu fɛɛ ni tamɔ ŋwɛi ŋmɔlɔ shwieɔ ehe kɛjɛɔ ehiɛ he lɛ. “Ekolɛ eshwɛ fioo ni nakai bolɔmɔ ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni yɔɔ nɛkɛ toiŋjɔlɛ ni gbeɔ mɔ nɛɛ nɔ lɛ baatsake gbɔmɔ ni hi fe lɛ.—Nɛgbɛ shɔŋŋ?”

“Points etɛ yɛ starboard bow lɛ nɔ, onukpa, ni ekɛ ekɔɔyɔɔ lɛ baa shi kɛbaa wɔŋɔɔ!

"Ehi jogbaŋŋ, gbɔmɔ. Ani agbɛnɛ St. Paul baatsɔ nakai gbɛ lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ ekɔɔyɔɔ lɛ aba mihe ni mibɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli! O Adebɔɔ, kɛ O gbɔmɔ susuma! bɔ ni okɛ nɔkwɛmɔnii ni tsa nɔ lɛ fe wiemɔi fɛɛ! jeee akɛ atom bibioo fe fɛɛ lɛ tsirɛɔ ehe loo ehiɔ shi yɛ nibii anɔ, shi moŋ eyɛ eshishinumɔ ni ji enyɔ lɛ yɛ ejwɛŋmɔ mli."





YITSƆƆ 71. Yerobeam sane lɛ.

Nine kɛ nine, lɛlɛ kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ tswa; shi kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ ba oya fe lɛlɛ lɛ, ni etsɛɛɛ ni Pequod lɛ bɔi hosomɔ.

Be fioo sɛɛ lɛ, kɛtsɔ glase lɛ nɔ lɛ, gbɔ lɛ lɛlɛi kɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi ni mɛi yɔɔ mli lɛ tsɔɔ akɛ eji ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛ. Shi akɛni ekɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ teŋ jɛkɛ waa, ni etswa tu kɛho, ni etamɔ nɔ ni etsɔɔ gbɛ kɛyaa shikpɔŋ kroko nɔ hewɔ lɛ, Pequod lɛ nyɛɛɛ ahiɛ ahi ehe nɔ akɛ ebaashɛ ehe. No hewɔ lɛ, ato okadi lɛ koni ana hetoo ni abaaha.

Akɛɛ yɛ biɛ akɛ, tamɔ asraafoi a-ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi lɛ, American Whale Fleet lɛlɛi lɛ eko fɛɛ eko yɛ teemɔŋ okadi; nibii fɛɛ ni tsɔɔ akɛ abua naa yɛ wolo ko mli ni lɛlɛi lɛ eko fɛɛ eko agbɛi fata he lɛ, ahaa lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ fɛɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ anɔkwɛlɔi lɛ nyɛɔ amɛyɔseɔ amɛhe yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ, yɛ he ko ni kɛ amɛhe jɛkɛ waa po, ni amɛbɛ he ko bibioo po.

Naagbee lɛ, gbɔ lɛ ha Pequod okadi lɛ hetoo kɛtsɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enɔ ni ekɛto lɛ nɔ; ni tsɔɔ akɛ lɛlɛ lɛ ji Yerobeam ni jɛ Nantucket lɛ. Beni ekɛ e-yards lɛ shwieɔ shi lɛ, efoɔ shi, ekɛ abeam shwieɔ Pequod lɛ shishi, ni ekɛ lɛlɛ ko ba shi; etsɛɛɛ ni ebɛŋkɛ; shi, beni Starbuck famɔ ji nɔ ni akɛmiitswa sɛɛ-atswere lɛ koni akɛha lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni ebasara lɛ lɛ, gbɔ ni awieɔ ehe lɛ tsɔ enine nɔ kɛjɛ elɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ kɛtsɔɔ akɛ nakai nifeemɔ lɛ he ehiaaa kwraa. Ebafee akɛ Yeroboam lɛlɛ lɛ yɛ hela ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni Mayhew, ni ji e-kapita lɛ miishe gbeyei akɛ ebaatswa Pequod lɛlɛ kuu lɛ hela lɛ. Ejaakɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ kɛ lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ tee nɔ amɛhi shi ni amɛhe tseee, ni eyɛ mli akɛ elɛlɛ lɛ ji fã ni akɛ tu etswa, ni ŋshɔ kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni fiteee miihoso ni eho yɛ amɛteŋ moŋ; shi kɛlɛ, akɛni ekɛ henilee kpɛtɛɔ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ he ni akɛtoɔ he ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ he hewɔ lɛ, ekpoo waa akɛ ekɛ Pequodbii lɛ baabɔ tɛ̃ɛ.

Shi enɛ etsĩii sanegbaai fɛɛ naa kwraa. Beni Yerobeam lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛɔ gbɛ ni ji mitai fioo komɛi ni kã ekɛ lɛlɛ lɛ teŋ lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ etsɔnei ni ekɛtsuɔ nii yɛ bei komɛi amli lɛ fee nɔ ko koni ekɛ Pequod lɛ afee ekome, beni etsɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli waa lɛ (ejaakɛ yɛ nɛkɛ be nɛɛ mli lɛ, etswaa ehee waa), ni ekɛ e-topsail titri lɛ efo sɛɛ; shi, lɛɛlɛŋ, bei komɛi lɛ, kɛtsɔ ŋshɔkei agbo ko ni miihoso trukaa lɛ nɔ lɛ, ebaatsi lɛlɛ lɛ kɛya hiɛ gbɛ ko nɔ; shi etsɛŋ ni abaatsɔ ŋaa gbɛ nɔ akɛba egbɛhe ni sa lɛ nɔ ekoŋŋ. Yɛ enɛ, kɛ gbɛtsii nii krokomɛi ni tamɔ nakai lɛ shishi amrɔ nɛɛ kɛ agbɛnɛ lɛ, sanegbaa ko tee nɔ yɛ kui enyɔ lɛ ateŋ; shi yɛ be kɛ bei amli lɛ jeee ni gbɛtsii nii kroko ni yɔɔ srɔto kwraa bɛ mli.

Ni gbála ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔne yɛ Yerobeam lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ, ji nuu ko ni hiɛ su kome pɛ, yɛ nakai koo mli ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ shihilɛ mli po he ni aŋkroaŋkroi ni ale amɛ waa lɛ feɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ lɛ. Eji nuu bibioo, kuku, oblanyo fioo, ni tsɔji egbɛ eshwa ehiɛ fɛɛ, ni ewo yitsɔi ŋmɔŋ ni fa babaoo. Atade kakadaŋŋ ko ni yɔɔ sɛkɛti kakadaŋŋ ni akɛ cabalistic fee ni walnut ŋmɔlɔ ni efite lɛ ha ehe; ni akɛ niji ni tsɔɔ nɔ lɛ ebɔle eniji ahe lɛ. Delirium ni mli kwɔ, ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ, ni mli wa waa yɛ ehiŋmɛii amli.

Yɛ basabasafeemɔ nitsumɔ ni ji ŋshɔŋloo ko ni afoɔ mli kɛ ekwɛmɔ lɛ mli lɛ, foidamɔ babaoo yaa sɛɛ kɛ hiɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ ateŋ. Nakai nɔŋŋ eji yɛ mɔ ni bɔɔ mɔdɛŋ ni etsɔɔ shihilɛ lɛ mli lɛ hu he. Agbɛnɛ esa akɛ wɔku wɔsɛɛ wɔkwɛ wɔgbɛ lɛ nɔ fioo. Atsĩ tã akɛ, kɛ́ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ sɛɛ shwie shikpɔŋ klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ, akɛ blubber-hook lɛ woɔ shishijee bu ni ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ a-spade lɛ fo yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ mli. Shi te fee tɛŋŋ ni nɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni tsii tamɔ nakai ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔŋŋ lɛ nyɛ ebote nakai bu lɛ mli? Minaanyo pɔtɛɛ Queequeg ji mɔ ni kɛwo jɛmɛ, ni eji enitsumɔ, akɛ harpooneer, akɛ ebaa shi yɛ kooloo lɛ sɛɛ kɛha yiŋtoo krɛdɛɛ ni awieɔ he lɛ. Shi yɛ shihilɛi babaoo amli lɛ, shihilɛi biɔ ni harpooneer lɛ ahi ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔ kɛyashi abaagbe flensing loo stripping nitsumɔ lɛ fɛɛ naa. Kɛji akɛ ana lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kã shi yɛ nu shishi, ja fãi ni atsuɔ he nii amrɔ nɔŋŋ lɛ pɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ jɛmɛ, aaafee ninetalɔi nyɔŋma yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ shishi lɛ, harpooneer ohiafo lɛ shwieɔ shi, fã yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ nɔ, ni fã hu yɛ nu lɛ mli, beni ŋshɔŋlooi wuji lɛ shwieɔ amɛhe tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛnyiɛɔ shi yɛ eshishi lɛ. Yɛ be ni awieɔ he lɛ mli lɛ, Queequeg jie ehe kpo yɛ Highland atade lɛ mli—shɛɛti kɛ sɔks—ni kɛ hoo lɛ, yɛ mihiŋmɛii amli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni eyɛ sɛɛnamɔ ko ni afɔɔɔɔ namɔ; ni mɔ ko mɔ ko bɛ ni yɔɔ hegbɛ ni hi fe lɛ ni ekwɛ lɛ, taakɛ abaana amrɔ nɛɛ.

Akɛni miji kooloo lɛ gãimlibii, ni ji mɔ ni gbalaa gãimlibii lɛ yɛ elɛlɛ lɛ mli (ni ji enyɔ lɛ kɛjɛ hiɛ) hewɔ lɛ, eji mi miishɛɛ nitsumɔ akɛ makwɛ enɔ yɛ be mli ni mikɛ nakai gãimlibii ni wa lɛ shwieɔ ŋshɔŋloo ni egbo lɛ sɛɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ. Ona Italiabii ni kɛ amɛhe woɔ lalai amli lɛ ni hiɛ kpã kakadaŋŋ ko ni akɛjoɔ-ape ko. Nakai pɛpɛɛpɛ, kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ ŋshɔkpɔ ni nɔ kwɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, mimɔ Queequeg yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli jɛmɛ, kɛtsɔ nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ yɛ ŋaa gbɛ nɔ akɛ ŋshɔŋloo-kpãa, ni akɛkpɛtɛɔ kanvas ni mli wa ni akɛfiɔ etsitsi he lɛ nɔ.

Eji jarayeli nitsumɔ ko ni yɔɔ oshara waa kɛha wɔyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ. Ejaakɛ, dani wɔbaatsa nɔ lɛ, esa akɛ akɛɛ akɛ ŋmei-kpãa lɛ he yɛ oya yɛ naagbeehei enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ; oya kɛya Queequeg kanvas bɛlt ni mli kwɔ lɛ nɔ, kɛ oya kɛya mihewolo nɔ ni mli tse lɛ nɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, kɛji ehi loo ehiii lɛ, wɔyi enyɔ lɛ, kɛha be lɛ, wɔbote gbalashihilɛ mli; ni kɛji akɛ Queequeg ohiafo lɛ gbee shi koni ete shi dɔŋŋ lɛ, no lɛ nitsumɔ kɛ woo fɛɛ biɔ, akɛ yɛ kpãa lɛ ni eeefo lɛ najiaŋ lɛ, esa akɛ egbala mi kɛba shi yɛ ehiɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, Siamese ligature ko ni kɛlɛ lɛ ha wɔfee ekome. Queequeg ji mi diɛŋtsɛ minyɛmi nuu ni ji tsɛkwɛ̃ ni nyɛŋ agbala mli; ni asaŋ minyɛɛɛ majie nyɔmɔwoo ni yɔɔ oshara ni hempen bond lɛ kɛba lɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ.

Misusu mishihilɛ lɛ he yɛ nakai beaŋ waa ni mikɛ hiɛdɔɔ kwɛɔ egbɛfaa lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni minaa faŋŋ akɛ amrɔ nɛɛ akɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ mihe ebɔ nitsumɔhe ko ni mɛi enyɔ yɔɔ mli lɛ he; akɛ miheyeli ni miyɔɔ akɛ mihala nɔ ni misumɔɔ lɛ ená pilamɔ ni gbeɔ mɔ; ni akɛ mɔ kroko tɔmɔ loo shihilɛ gbonyo baanyɛ aha mi ni efeko efɔŋ lɛ awo oshara kɛ gbele ni esaaa mli. No hewɔ lɛ, mina akɛ maŋtsɛyeli ko ni yɔɔ Providence lɛ ji biɛ; ejaakɛ ejalɛsaneyeli ni yeɔ egbɔ lɛ nyɛŋ afee jalɛsane ni ayeee ni naa wa tamɔ nɛkɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ. Ni kɛlɛ, beni misusuɔ he lolo lɛ—be mli ni mikɛ mihe woɔ mli bianɛ kɛ agbɛnɛ kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛ lɛlɛ lɛ teŋ, ni baawo ehe gbeyei akɛ ebaatswa lɛ lɛ—beni misusuɔ he lolo lɛ, mikɛɛ, mina akɛ shihilɛ ni miyɔɔ mli nɛɛ ji shihilɛ pɔtɛɛ ni gbɔmɔ fɛɛ gbɔmɔ ni gboɔ ni muɔ lɛ yɔɔ mli; pɛ, yɛ bei babaoo mli lɛ, lɛ, yɛ gbɛ kome loo gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ, eyɛ nɛkɛ Siamese tsakpaa nɛɛ kɛ gbɔmɛi krokomɛi ni gboiɔ babaoo. Kɛji obanki lɛ fite lɛ, okɛ snap; kɛji akɛ otsofafeelɔ lɛ tɔ̃ ni ekɛ poison maje bo yɛ otsofai lɛ amli lɛ, ogboɔ. Eji anɔkwale akɛ, ekolɛ obaakɛɛ akɛ, kɛtsɔ hiɛdɔɔ ni fe nine nɔ lɛ, ekolɛ obaajo enɛɛmɛi kɛ shihilɛ mli hegbɛi gbohii krokomɛi babaoo lɛ anaa foi. Shi okɛ Queequeg ŋmei-kpãa lɛ atsu nii jogbaŋŋ tamɔ bɔ ni mafee lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ, ekɛwoɔ mli bɔ ni sa, ni mibɛŋkɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ shwiemɔ waa. Ni mihiɛ kpaaa nɔ hu akɛ, mifee nɔ ni mafee lɛ, miyɛ naagbee kome pɛ nɔkwɛmɔ.*

*Anaa ŋshɔŋloo-kpãa lɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi fɛɛ amli; shi Pequod lɛ mli pɛ ji he ni akɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛ mɔ ni hiɛ lɛ lɛ fĩɔ amɛhe pɛŋ. Nɛkɛ hiɛyaa ni ba shishijee nitsumɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni nuu ko ni baa shi fe Stubb ba, bɔni afee ni ekɛ nɔmimaa ni mli wa fe fɛɛ aha anɔkwayeli kɛ hiɛdɔɔ ni ehiɛɔ e-monkey-rope holder lɛ.

Mikɛ mihe ewo mli akɛ bei pii lɛ, mafo Queequeg ohiafo lɛ kɛjɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛ lɛlɛ lɛ teŋ—he ni ebaagbee shi yɛ yɛ bei komɛi amli lɛ, kɛjɛ amɛyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ ni amɛshwieɔ shi ni amɛshwieɔ shi ni amɛkpaaa lɛ mli. Shi jeee enɛ pɛ ji jamming he oshara ni ekɛkpe lɛ. Akɛni gbɔmɔgbee ni agbe amɛ gbɛkɛ lɛ haaa ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ahe afee shoo hewɔ lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ lá ni tsɔ hiɛ efo mli ni je shishi ejɛ gbonyo lɛ mli ho lɛ miigba ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ahe waa ni amɛmiifee amɛ ehee waa—bɔɔ nii ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ bɔle ehe tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi atsu mli lɛ.

Ni nakai ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ pɛpɛɛpɛ ji Queequeg; ni bei pii lɛ ekɛ enaji ni shwieɔ shi lɛ tsiɔ amɛ kɛyaa he kroko lɛ. Nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ kwraa ji akɛ jeee akɛ koolooi tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo ni egbo lɛ gbalaa lɛ, ni kɛ jeee nakai lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo ni yeɔ loo srɔtoi lɛ efɔɔɔ gbɔmɔ he.

Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, abaanyɛ ahe aye jogbaŋŋ akɛ, akɛni amɛyɛ nine ni shwieɔ mɔ tamɔ nɛkɛ yɛ pai lɛ mli hewɔ lɛ, abuɔ lɛ akɛ shi nilee yɛ mli akɛ aaakwɛ amɛ jogbaŋŋ. Yɛ enɛ naa lɛ, kɛfata ŋmei kpãa lɛ he, ni mikɛtswaa ohiafo lɛ kɛjɛɔ he ko ni bɛŋkɛ tsɔ kɛyaa nɔ ko ni tamɔ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa lɛ tsitsi nɔ lɛ—aha lɛ hebuu kroko lolo. Akɛni Tashtego kɛ Daggoo fĩ amɛhe yɛ fãi lɛ eko nɔ hewɔ lɛ, amɛyaa nɔ amɛshwieɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni ji whale-spades lɛ anɔ yɛ eyitso nɔ, ni amɛkɛgbeɔ ŋshɔŋlooi babaoo bɔ ni amɛbaanyɛ amɛshɛ he. Amɛ gbɛjianɔtoo nɛɛ, kɛ aawie he lɛ, eji nɔ ni bɛ he miishɛɛ kwraa kɛ ejurɔfeemɔ kɛha amɛ. Amɛtsɔɔ Queequeg miishɛɛ ni hi fe fɛɛ, mikpɛlɛɔ nɔ; shi yɛ amɛ ekãa ni amɛyɔɔ oya koni amɛkɛ lɛ afee naanyo lɛ mli, kɛjɛ shihilɛ ni bei komɛi lɛ lɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ fɛɛ fã kɛtee nu ni lá eyi mli obɔ lɛ mli lɛ mli lɛ, nakai amɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni bɛ hiɛshikamɔ lɛ baabɛŋkɛ ni amɛfo naji fe ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ. Shi Queequeg ohiafo lɛ, misusuɔ akɛ, ekɛ nakai dade ŋtayaa kpeteŋkpele lɛ miigba ehe ni ekɛmiifo jɛmɛ—misusuɔ akɛ Queequeg ohiafo lɛ sɔle kɛha e-Yojo lɛ pɛ, ni ekɛ ewala wo enyɔŋmɔi lɛ adɛŋ.

Ojogbaŋŋ, ojogbaŋŋ, minaanyo ni misumɔɔ waa kɛ minyɛmi nuu ni ji tsɛkwɛ̃ lɛ, misusu he, beni mikɛ mihe woɔ mli ni no sɛɛ lɛ mikɛ kpãa lɛ shwieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli ŋshɔkei lɛ fɛɛ anɔ lɛ—mɛɛ he hia, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ? Ani jeee bo ji wɔteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ kɛ wɔ fɛɛ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔ je nɛɛ mli lɛ he mfoniri ni jara wa? Nakai ŋshɔ ni gbee bɛ mli ni okɛ ohe woɔ mli lɛ, ji Wala; nakai ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ, ohenyɛlɔi; nakai spadei lɛ, onanemɛi; kɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ sharks kɛ spades teŋ lɛ oyɔɔ yɛ awerɛho pickle kɛ oshara mli, gbekɛ nuu ohiafo.

Shi ekãa! miishɛɛ kpakpa yɛ ni ato aha bo, Queequeg. Amrɔ nɛɛ, taakɛ eji yɛ naabu bluu kɛ hiŋmɛii ni lá etsɔ mli lɛ, naagbee lɛ, kooloo ni etɔ lɛ lɛ kwɔ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai lɛ anɔ ni edamɔ shi ni ekɛ nu miiho ni ekpokpo yɛ sɛɛgbɛ ni ejɛɛɛ etsui mli; shĩa nɔkwɛlɔ lɛ yaa hiɛ, ni ekɛ mlihilɛ, miishɛjemɔ hiŋmɛii kwɛɔ lɛ—mɛɛ? Cognac ni mli edɔ ko? Daabi! nine lɛ, nyɛ nyɔŋmɔi! ekɛ ŋmei ni mli edɔ kɛ nu kɔɔpoo ko haa lɛ!

Kɛkɛ ni edamɔ shi tamɔ nɔ ni eheee eyeee be fioo, ni enyiɛ blɛoo kɛtee shĩa nɔkwɛlɔ lɛ ŋɔɔ ni enaa kpɛ ehe lɛ blɛoo ni ekɛɛ lɛ akɛ, “Ginger? ginger? ni ani obaaná ekpakpafeemɔ ni okɛbaakɛɛ mi, Mr. Dough-Boy, nɛgbɛ ji ginger su kpakpa lɛ?
