YITSƆƆ 22. Blonya ni ŋɔɔ.

Yɛ naagbee lɛ, beni shɛɔ shwane mli lɛ, beni ajie lɛlɛ lɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ naagbee lɛ, kɛ be mli ni ajie Pequod lɛ kɛjɛ lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ sɛɛ, kɛ be mli ni Charity ni susuɔ nii ahe be fɛɛ be lɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛ ko mli eba shi, kɛ enaagbee nikeenii—gbɛkɛ atade kɛha Stubb, hefatalɔ ni ji enyɔ lɛ, nɛkɛ etsɛkwɛ̃ nɛɛ—sparter lɛ fɛɛ kɛha Biblia lɛ, Kapten, Peleg kɛ Bildad je kpo kɛjɛ tsɔne lɛ mli, ni etsɔ ehe ekwɛ tsɔne kudɔlɔ nukpa lɛ, ni Peleg kɛɛ:

"Amrɔ nɛɛ, Mr. Starbuck, ani oyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ja? Kapten Ahab efee klalo fɛɛ—ekɛ lɛ wie kɛkɛ—nɔ ko bɛ ni abaanyɛ ana kɛjɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa dɔŋŋ, eh? Ojogbaŋŋ, tsɛmɔ niji fɛɛ, no lɛ. Bua amɛ naa yɛ biɛ—tswaa amɛ!"

Bildad kɛɛ akɛ: “Wiemɔi ni yɔɔ muji he ehiaaa, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni oyaiyeli lɛ ji ha, Peleg, shi okɛ ohe aya, naanyo Starbuck, ni ofee wɔfamɔ lɛ.”

Kwɛ agbɛnɛ! Biɛ yɛ he ni amɛjeɔ gbɛfaa lɛ shishi yɛ lɛ, Kapten Peleg kɛ Kapten Bildad miiya kɛ nine ni nɔ kwɔ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fã lɛ nɔ, tamɔ nɔ ni amɛbaafee ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ni fata amɛhe yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu kɛya hei fɛɛ ni amɛbaaje kpo yɛ lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ. Ni yɛ Kapten Ahab gbɛfaŋ lɛ, anako ehe okadi ko lolo; pɛ, amɛkɛɛ eyɛ tsɔne lɛ mli. Shi no sɛɛ lɛ, susumɔ ni yɔɔ mli ji akɛ, ehe ehiaaa kwraa yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ ni abaaha eshɛ shi lɛ mli, ni ekudɔ lɛ jogbaŋŋ kɛya ŋshɔ lɛ naa. Lɛlɛŋ, akɛni jeee nakai ji enitsumɔ ni sa kwraa hewɔ lɛ, shi moŋ kɔɔyɔŋ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ nɔ; ni akɛni ehe ejɔɔɔ lɛ kwraa lolo hewɔ lɛ—nakai amɛkɛɛ lɛ—no hewɔ lɛ, Kapten Ahab tee nɔ ehi shishi. Ni enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ tamɔ nɔ ni ji adebɔɔ naa nɔ bɔ ni sa; titri lɛ taakɛ yɛ jarayeli nitsumɔ lɛ mli lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi babaoo ejieee amɛhe kpo yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ be babaoo beni amɛwo ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ sɛɛ, shi moŋ amɛhiɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli okpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ, ni amɛkɛ amɛ ŋshɔnaa nanemɛi lɛ feɔ sɛɛsaramɔ he miishɛɛ, dani amɛkɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ shiɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli kɛya naanɔ.

Shi hegbɛ babaoo bɛ ni akɛaasusu sane lɛ he, ejaakɛ amrɔ nɛɛ Kapten Peleg fɛɛ yɛ wala mli. Etamɔ nɔ ni lɛ ji mɔ ni feɔ wiemɔi kɛ famɔi babaoo, shi jeee Bildad.

Ebolɔ akɛ: “Nyɛshi biɛ, nyɛ oshijafoi abii,” beni ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ ŋmɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ. “Owula Starbuck, kudɔ amɛ kɛya sɛɛ.”

“Nyɛtswaa buu lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ!”—ji famɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ. Taakɛ mitsɔɔ mli tsutsu lɛ, atswaaa nɛkɛ whalebone marquee nɛɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ ja lɛlɛdaamɔhe; ni yɛ Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ, afii nyɔŋmai etɛ sɔɔ lɛ, ale jogbaŋŋ akɛ famɔ ni akɛha akɛ atswa buu lɛ ji nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ ni akɛwoɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ sɛɛ.

“Gbɔmɔ ni ji kapstan lɛ! La kɛ nugbɔshiimɔ!—tumɔ!”—ji famɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ, ni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ jo foi kɛtee nineshi tsɔnei lɛ anɔ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ yɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ mli lɛ, he ni tsɔne kudɔlɔ lɛ fɔɔ nitsumɔ ji lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ fã lɛ. Ni Bildad, ni kɛ Peleg, ni ale lɛ, kɛfata esɔɔlɔi krokomɛi lɛ ahe lɛ, eji lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ni yɔɔ hegbɛ ni amɛyɔɔ lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome—asusuɔ akɛ efee ehe lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ bɔni afee ni ekɛ Nantucket lɛlɛ kudɔmɔ nyɔmɔwoo lɛ aha lɛlɛi fɛɛ ni esusuɔ he lɛ, ejaakɛ ekwɛko lɛlɛ kroko nɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ, gãimlibi kɛha ŋshɔkei ni miiba lɛ, ni yɛ be kɛ bei amli lɛ amɛlaa nɔ ni tamɔ lala lalai ni yɔɔ awerɛho, koni amɛkɛwo niji ni kɔɔ kɔɔyɔɔŋ tsɔne kudɔlɔ lɛ he lɛ miishɛɛ, ni ekɛ tsuiŋ suɔmɔ kpakpa tswa lala ko ni kɔɔ gbekɛbii yei ni yɔɔ Booble Alley lɛ ahe lɛ. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, jeee gbii etɛ ni eho lɛ, Bildad ekɛɛ amɛ akɛ aŋmɛŋ lalai ni yɔɔ muji ko gbɛ yɛ Pequod lɛlɛ lɛ mli, titri lɛ yɛ shwiili ni baa shi lɛ mli; ni Charity, ni ji enyɛmi yoo lɛ kɛ Watts wolo bibioo ko ni ahala lɛ ewo ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi lɛ ateŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ.

Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, beni Kapten Peleg kwɛɔ lɛlɛ lɛ fã kroko lɛ nɔ lɛ, efo ni eka kitã yɛ sɛɛgbɛ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ nɔ. Shwɛ fioo ni misusu akɛ ebaaŋɔ lɛlɛ lɛ awo nu mli dani abaanyɛ ajie ŋshɔ lɛ naa; mikɛ mihe wooo mi-nineshi tsɔne lɛ nɔ, ni mikɛɛ Queequeg akɛ efee nakai nɔŋŋ, ni misusu osharai ni wɔyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ wɔjo foi kɛba lɛ he, yɛ gbɛfaa lɛ shishijee mli kɛ abonsa ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kɛha kɔɔyɔŋ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ. Shi kɛlɛ, mikɛ susumɔ ni ji akɛ abaanyɛ ana yiwalaheremɔ ko yɛ Bildad ni ji Nyɔŋmɔ gbeyeishelɔ lɛ mli lɛ miishɛje mimii, yɛ e-lay ni ji ohai kpawo kɛ nyɔŋmai kpawo kɛ kpawo lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ; beni minu trukaa akɛ tsɔne ko ni mli wa miitswa misɛɛ, ni mitsɔ mihe lɛ, mihe fee mi gbeyei yɛ Kapten Peleg kpojiemɔ lɛ he yɛ nifeemɔ ni ji enane ni ejie kɛjɛ he ni miyɔɔ lɛ mli amrɔ nɔŋŋ lɛ hewɔ. No ji mi klɛŋklɛŋ kikɛ.

“Ani nakai ji bɔ ni amɛwoɔ amɛhe nɔ yɛ jarayeli sɔɔmɔ lɛ mli?” ebolɔ waa. "Fɛ̃i, bo too-yitso; fɛ̃i, ni ogbala osɛɛ! Mɛni hewɔ nyɛfɔmɔ, mikɛɛ, nyɛ fɛɛ—fɛ̃i! Quohog! fɛ̃i, bo chap ni yɔɔ tsitsii tsuji; fɛ̃i yɛ jɛmɛ, Scotch-cap; fɛ̃i, bo pantalo eŋɔli. Fɛ̃i, mikɛɛ, nyɛ fɛɛ, ni nyɛjie nyɛhiŋmɛii kpo!" Ni beni ekɛɔ nakai lɛ, enyiɛ kɔɔyɔɔŋ tsɔne lɛ he, ni ekɛ enane tsuɔ nii yɛ biɛ kɛ biɛ yɛ heyeli mli waa, yɛ be mli ni Bildad ni bɛ haomɔ lɛ yaa nɔ ekɛ elala lɛ nyiɛɔ hiɛ. Misusuɔ akɛ, ekolɛ Kapten Peleg miinu nɔ ko ŋmɛnɛ.

Naagbee lɛ, ŋshɔkei lɛ te shi, ato ŋshɔkei lɛ ahe gbɛjianɔ, ni wɔfã gbɛ. Blonya kuku ni fɛ̃i yɔɔ mli ji no; ni beni kooyigbɛ gbi kuku lɛ kɛ ehe woɔ gbɛkɛ mli lɛ, wɔna wɔhe akɛ wɔmiihe ashɛ ŋshɔ ni mli edɔ waa lɛ nɔ, ni e-ais ni shwieɔ shi lɛ ha wɔfee ais, tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ tawuu nii ni akpɛ́ mli lɛ mli. Nyanyɔŋ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ kakadaji ni yɔɔ gbogboi lɛ anɔ lɛ kpɛɔ yɛ nyɔŋtsere la lɛ mli; ni tamɔ shwuɔ agbo ko ŋmeii yɛŋ ni akɛ shwuɔ wu fee lɛ, ŋmeii wuji ni ebɔle amɛhe lɛ damɔ ŋmeii lɛ anɔ.

Lank Bildad, akɛ kɔɔyɔŋ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ, nyiɛ klɛŋklɛŋ bulɔi lɛ ahiɛ, ni be fɛɛ be, beni lɛlɛ momo lɛ boteɔ ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji amli lɛ amli shɔŋŋ, ni ekɛ fɛ̃i ni hosoɔ lɛ tsuɔ ehe fɛɛ, ni kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ tswaa, ni kpãa lɛ tswaa, anuɔ egbɛi ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ lɛ,—

      “Ŋmɔji ni ŋɔɔ ni yɔɔ nu afua ni mli efu lɛ sɛɛ, .
Damɔ shi ni owo atadei ni yɔɔ wala mli.
No hewɔ lɛ, kɛha Yudafoi lɛ, Kanaan momo lɛ damɔ shi,
Be mli ni Jordan shwieɔ ehe yɛ teŋ lɛ.”

Nakai wiemɔi ni ŋɔɔ lɛ etswako mi ŋɔɔ fe nakai beaŋ pɛŋ. Amɛyi obɔ kɛ hiɛnɔkamɔ kɛ yibiiwoo. Yɛ fɛ̃i be mli gbɛkɛ ni mli ejɔ waa yɛ Atlantik ŋshɔ ni mli ewo hoo lɛ mli nɛɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, yɛ minaji ni efɔ lɛ kɛ minaji ni efɔ lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, no mli lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni misusuɔ akɛ, he ko ni ŋɔɔ babaoo yɛ lolo; kɛ ŋmɔji kɛ ŋmɔji ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji amli lɛ ji fɛ̃i be kɛya naanɔ, ni jwɛi ni fɛ̃i be lɛ shwieɔ shi, ni mɔ ko mɔ ko etaaa nɔ, ni egbooo lɛ, hiɔ shi yɛ hulutsoo be lɛ teŋgbɛ.

Naagbee lɛ wɔnine shɛ hegbɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ nɔ, ni ehe ehiaaa kɔɔyɔŋ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi enyɔ lɛ dɔŋŋ. Lɛlɛ ni akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ ni tsii ni fata wɔhe lɛ bɔi sɛɛkuu kɛmiiya wɔmasɛi.

Eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni jeee nɔ ni esaaa mɔ he, bɔ ni ena Peleg kɛ Bildad nɔ hewalɛ yɛ nɛkɛ be nɛɛ mli, titri lɛ Kapten Bildad nɔ hewalɛ eha lɛ. Ejaakɛ hetsɛ̃ ni ashiɔ, lolo; ehiɛ edɔ waa akɛ eeeshi, kɛha kpakpa, lɛlɛ ko ni miiya gbɛfaa kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ oshara waa mli—kɛho Capes enyɔ ni ahum yɔɔ mli lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ; lɛlɛ ko ni akɛ shika akpei abɔ ni ekɛtsu nii waa lɛ wo mli; lɛlɛ ko, ni lɛlɛ mli hefatalɔ onukpa ko fãa gbɛ akɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ; nuu ko ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaagbɔ tamɔ lɛ, ni shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, ebɔi tsitsi ni bɛ mɔbɔnalɛ lɛ he gbeyeishemɔi lɛ fɛɛ kpemɔ; esumɔɔɔ ni ekɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ ni eyi obɔ kɛ miishɛɛ fɛɛ lɛ baakpa shɛɛ, —Bildad onukpa ohiafo lɛ hi shi be kakadaŋŋ; ekɛ haomɔ nyiɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ; ejo foi kɛba shi kɛtee tsɔne lɛ mli koni eyawie sɛɛsaramɔ wiemɔ kroko yɛ jɛmɛ; eba ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ ekoŋŋ, ni ekwɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ; ekwɛ nui ni mli kwɔ ni bɛ naagbee lɛ anɔ, ni Bokagbɛ Shikpɔŋ wuji ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni anaaa lɛ pɛ ji nɔ ni tsiɔ amɛnaa; ekwɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ; kwɛ ŋwɛi; kwɛ ninejurɔgbɛ kɛ abɛkugbɛ; ekwɛ he fɛɛ he ni ekwɛɛɛ he ko he ko; ni naagbee lɛ, ekɛ tsɔne ko miibɔle kpãa he yɛ e-pin lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ mɔ Peleg ni he wa lɛ nine mli, ni ewo kane ko nɔ, ni edamɔ shi be fioo ni ekwɛ ehiɛ kɛ ekaa, tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛɛ akɛ, “Shi kɛlɛ, naanyo Peleg, manyɛ madamɔ shi; hɛɛ, manyɛ.”

Yɛ Peleg diɛŋtsɛ gbɛfaŋ lɛ, ebu lɛ tamɔ jeŋ nilelɔ fe tsutsu lɛ; shi yɛ ejeŋ nilee lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, yaafo nu ko miiho yɛ ehiŋmɛii amli, beni kane lɛ bɛŋkɛ tsɔ lɛ. Ni lɛ hu, ejooo foi fioo kɛjɛɛɛ tsɔne lɛ mli kɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ—amrɔ nɛɛ wiemɔ ko yɛ shishi, ni agbɛnɛ wiemɔ ko kɛ Starbuck, ni ji hefatalɔ nukpa lɛ.

Shi, naagbee lɛ, etsɔ ehe ekwɛ enaanyo lɛ, kɛ naagbee hiŋmɛi ni ebɔle ehe lɛ,—“Kapten Bildad—ba, lɛlɛ mlinyo momo, esa akɛ wɔya. Ku sɛɛ kɛya jɛmɛ! Lɛlɛ ahoy! Damɔ shi ni obɛŋkɛ, bianɛ! Kwɛmɔ ohe jogbaŋŋ, kwɛmɔ ohe jogbaŋŋ!—ba, Bildad, yeckbu, gbekɛ nuu kɛya Starck—kɛɛmɔ o naagbee. Stubb—shweremɔ aha nyɛ, Mr. Flask—shweremɔ kɛ shweremɔ kpakpa aha nyɛ fɛɛ—ni gbi nɛɛ afii etɛ mafee gbɛkɛ niyenii ni mli edɔ ni mishɛreɔ tawa maha nyɛ yɛ Nantucket momo lɛ kɛ he kroko!”

“Nyɔŋmɔ ajɔɔ nyɛ, ni eha nyɛ ahi E-buu krɔŋkrɔŋ lɛ mli, hii,” Bildad onukpa lɛ wie huhuuhu, ni eshwɛ fioo ni ekɛ ehe kpaaa gbee. “Miyɛ hiɛnɔkamɔ akɛ nyɛbaaná kɔɔyɔŋ kpakpa bianɛ, koni etsɛŋ ni Kapten Ahab aya nyɛteŋ—hulu ni ŋɔɔ pɛ ji nɔ ni he hiaa lɛ, ni nyɛbaaná amɛ babaoo yɛ hulutsoo be mli gbɛfaa ni nyɛyaa lɛ mli. Nyɛkwɛa nyɛhe nɔ jogbaŋŋ yɛ gbɔbimɔ mli, nyɛ hefatalɔi. Nyɛkatswaa lɛlɛi lɛ ahe tsɔne ni he ehiaaa, nyɛ harpooneers lɛ. Kaaha ohiɛ miikpa osɔlemɔi lɛ anɔ, hu. Stubb; eji nɔ ko ni hoɔ fioo, misusu he.Kɛ́ nyɛta he yɛ ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ lɛ, nyɛkwɛa nyɛhe nɔ jogbaŋŋ yɛ ajwamaŋbɔɔ he.

“Ba, ba, Kapten Bildad, kpa ŋmɔlɔ, —yaa!” ni ekɛ nakai wie lɛ, Peleg kɛ lɛ tee ŋshɔ lɛ naa oya, ni amɛyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ amɛbote lɛlɛ lɛ mli.

Lɛlɛ kɛ lɛlɛ fã amɛhe; gbɛkɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli ejɔ ni mli edɔ lɛ tswa yɛ teŋ; ŋshɔŋloo ko ni miibolɔ lɛ fo kɛho eyiteŋ; ŋshɔkei enyɔ lɛ shwie shi waa; wɔkɛ miishɛɛ ni mli wa ha shii etɛ, ni wɔkɛ shwilafoi bote Atlantik ŋshɔ ni yɔɔ ekomekomei lɛ mli tamɔ shwilafoi.





YITSƆƆ 23. Lee Ŋshɔ lɛ naa.

Yitsei komɛi ni eho lɛ, awie Bulkington kome he, ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔ ko ni kɛlɛ, ni eba shikpɔŋ hee nɔ, ni akɛkpe yɛ New Bedford yɛ gbɔiatoohe lɛ.

Beni yɛ nakai fɛ̃i be mli gbɛkɛ ni hosoɔ lɛ mli lɛ, Pequod lɛ kɛ ekpɔlɔi ni yeɔ he nyɔmɔ lɛ shwie ŋshɔkei ni mli ejɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ amli lɛ, namɔ esa akɛ mana ni damɔ etsɔne lɛ hiɛ ja Bulkington! Mikɛ musuŋtsɔlɛ gbeyeishemɔ kɛ gbeyeishemɔ kwɛ nuu lɛ, ni yɛ fɛ̃i be lɛ teŋgbɛ lɛ, ejɛ afii ejwɛ gbɛfaa ni yɔɔ oshara mli lɛ eba shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ pɛŋ, ni ebaanyɛ ekɛ ehe awo mli ekoŋŋ ni ekɛ ehe wooo mli kɛha ahum be kroko. Etamɔ nɔ ni shikpɔŋ lɛ miitso kɛyashi enaji ahe. Naakpɛɛ nibii fe fɛɛ ji nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsĩ tã; kaimɔi ni mli kwɔ lɛ haaa epitaphs ko aba; yitso ni kɛlɛ ji inchi enumɔ nɛɛ ji Bulkington gbonyobu ni tɛi bɛ mli lɛ. Ha makɛɛ pɛ akɛ, ehi eha lɛ tamɔ lɛlɛ ni ahum etswa lɛ, ni fãa gbɛ kɛyaa shikpɔŋ ni kɔɔyɔɔ tsɔɔ nɔ lɛ nɔ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ awerɛho nɔ lɛ. Lɛjiadaamɔhe lɛ baa fain aha yelikɛbuamɔ; lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ ji mɔbɔnalɛ; yɛ lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ ji shweshweeshwe shihilɛ, miishɛjemɔ, lasu tɛ, gbɛkɛ niyenii, tsɔji ni mli edɔ, nanemɛi, nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni ji mlihilɛ kɛha wɔ gbohiiashitee lɛ. Shi yɛ nakai ahum lɛ mli lɛ, lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ, shikpɔŋ lɛ ji nakai lɛlɛ lɛ he oshara fe fɛɛ; esa akɛ efã gbɔfeemɔ fɛɛ; shikpɔŋ kome pɛ ni etaa he, eyɛ mli akɛ eyeɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa moŋ, shi ebaaha ekpokpo kɛtsɔ mli fɛɛ. Ekɛ ehewalɛ fɛɛ buaa lɛlɛ fɛɛ naa kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa; yɛ nakai feemɔ mli lɛ, ekɛ ‘kɔɔyɔɔ ni fain baatswa lɛ kɛya shia lɛ diɛŋtsɛ wuɔ; etaoɔ ŋshɔ ni atswa lɛ shikpɔŋ ni bɛ nɔ lɛ fɛɛ ekoŋŋ; kɛha abobaahe ni kɛ oyaiyeli miiya oshara mli lɛ hewɔ; enaanyo kome pɛ ni ji ehenyɛlɔ ni mli wa fe fɛɛ lɛ!

Ani nyɛle agbɛnɛ, Bulkington? Etamɔ nɔ ni nyɛnaa nakai anɔkwale ni gboɔ ni anyɛɛɛ akpɛlɛ nɔ lɛ he mfoniri fioo komɛi; akɛ susumɔi ni mli kwɔ, ni hiɛdɔɔ yɔɔ mli fɛɛ ji mɔdɛŋ ni susuma lɛ bɔɔ kɛ ekãa koni ehiɛ e-ŋshɔ lɛ heyeli ni yɔɔ faŋŋ lɛ mli kɛkɛ; yɛ be mli ni ŋwɛi kɛ shikpɔŋ kɔɔyɔɔi ni naa wa fe fɛɛ lɛ feɔ ekome koni amɛshɛrɛ lɛ amɛshwie ŋshɔkpɔ ni shishiuɔ mɔ, ni ji nyɔŋyeli nɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ?

Shi taakɛ shikpɔŋ ni bɛ nɔ pɛ ji he ni anɔkwale ni nɔ kwɔ fe fɛɛ, ni bɛ ŋshɔnaa, ni bɛ naagbee tamɔ Nyɔŋmɔ yɔɔ lɛ—nakai nɔŋŋ hu, ehi akɛ oookpata yɛ nakai naagbee ni bɛ naagbee ni bolɔɔ lɛ mli, fe ni abaashwie ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ yɛ anunyam ni bɛ mli, kɛji akɛ no ji shweshweeshwe shihilɛ po! Kɛha ŋmei-tamɔ, no lɛ, oh! mɔ ni baasumɔ ni ekɛ ehe awo shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ! Gbeyei ni yɔɔ gbeyei! ani nɛkɛ piŋmɔ nɛɛ fɛɛ ji yakayaka nɔ ko? Okɛ ohe awo mli, owo otsui mli, O Bulkington! Tsɔ bo shi kɛ mlifu, demi-nyɔŋmɔ! Kɛjɛ o-ŋshɔ-fitemɔ lɛ mli nu lɛ mli—kɛtee ŋwɛi tɛ̃ɛ, efoɔ o-apotheosis lɛ!





YITSƆƆ 24. Mɔ ni Kwɛɔ Nɔ.

Akɛni mi kɛ Queequeg ebɔi ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ nitsumɔ nɛɛ jogbaŋŋ amrɔ nɛɛ hewɔ lɛ; ni akɛni nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔ nɛɛ ebatsɔ nɔ ni abuɔ yɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ bii ateŋ akɛ eji nitsumɔ ko ni jeee lalafoo wiemɔi ni bɛ gbɛi kpakpa moŋ lɛ; no hewɔ lɛ, miyɛ haomɔ fɛɛ akɛ mahã nyɛ, nyɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ bii lɛ, ana jalɛsane ni ayeee wɔ ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbeelɔi lɛ.

Yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, abaanyɛ abu akɛ ehe ehiaaa ni ama anɔkwale lɛ nɔ mi, akɛ yɛ gbɔmɛi babaoo ateŋ lɛ, abuuu ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ nitsumɔ lɛ he akɔntaa yɛ nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ heyeli nitsumɔi lɛ he. Kɛji akɛ akɛ gbɔ ko ba maŋtiase wulu mli akutso srɔtoi komɛi amli lɛ, ebaaha susumɔ ni mɛi fɛɛ yɔɔ yɛ ehilɛ-kɛhamɔ he lɛ aya hiɛ fioo pɛ, kɛji akɛ akɛ lɛ ha nitsumɔhe lɛ akɛ harpooneer, kɛ wɔkɛɛ lɛ; ni kɛji akɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafoi lɛ akasemɔ mli lɛ, ekɛ shishijee niŋmaa okadii ni ji SWF (Sperm Whale Fishery) lɛ fata esaramɔ kaadi lɛ he lɛ, abaabu gbɛ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ akɛ eji nɔ ko ni tsɔɔ akɛ eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ ni ji ŋmɔlɔ.

Ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ yiŋtoo kome ni he hiaa waa ni haa je lɛ kpooɔ wɔ ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ woo lɛ ji enɛ: amɛsusuɔ akɛ, kɛ hoo lɛ, wɔ nitsumɔ lɛ tamɔ loogbee nitsumɔ ko; ni akɛ, kɛ́ wɔkɛ wɔhe wo mli kɛ ekãa lɛ, mujiwoo srɔtoi fɛɛ ebɔle wɔhe. Butchers wɔji, eji anɔkwale. Shi loogbelɔi, hu, kɛ loogbelɔi ni yɔɔ baaji ni lá hoɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ fɛɛ ji Tawuu Nɔkwɛlɔi ni je lɛ sumɔɔ ni ewo amɛhiɛ nyam be fɛɛ be. Ni yɛ sane ni kɔɔ he ni akɛɛ wɔ nitsumɔ lɛ he tseee lɛ he lɛ, etsɛŋ ni abaaje nyɛ shishi yɛ anɔkwalei komɛi ni aleee kwraa kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ amli, ni kɛ hoo lɛ, ebaaha sperm whale-lɛlɛ lɛ atsɔ kunimyeli mli kɛfata shikpɔŋ ni yɔɔ fɛo nɛɛ nɔ nibii ni he tse fe fɛɛ lɛ ahe. Shi kɛji akɛ aŋmɛ gbɛ ni fɔbuu ni awieɔ he lɛ po afee anɔkwale; mɛɛ ŋshɔŋlooi a-lɛlɛ ni shwieɔ shi ni yɔɔ basabasa lɛ ji nɔ ni akɛtoɔ nakai tawuu hei ni asraafoi babaoo kuɔ amɛsɛɛ amɛbaa koni amɛbanuɔ nu yɛ yei fɛɛ ahiɛsɔɔ mli lɛ ahe gbohii ni awieee he lɛ he? Ni kɛji akɛ oshara he susumɔ lɛ haa asraafonyo nitsumɔ lɛ he henɔwomɔ ni mɛi babaoo yɔɔ lɛ yaa hiɛ babaoo lɛ; ha makɛ nɔmimaa aha nyɛ akɛ asraafoi ni ewuu ta lɛ ateŋ mɛi babaoo ni kɛ amɛhe ewo mli kɛtee batiri ko he lɛ, baaku amɛsɛɛ oya kɛji sperm whale lɛ tsitsi wulu lɛ je kpo, ni ebaaha kɔɔyɔɔ ni yɔɔ eyitso nɔ lɛ atsɔ ŋshɔŋlooi. Ejaakɛ mɛni ji gbɔmɔ gbeyeishemɔi ni anuɔ shishi lɛ kɛ akɛto Nyɔŋmɔ gbeyeishemɔi kɛ naakpɛɛ nii ni kɛ amɛhe kpaa gbee lɛ ahe!

Shi, eyɛ mli akɛ je lɛ kwɛɔ wɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbeelɔi lɛ moŋ, shi ekɛ woo ni mli kwɔ fe fɛɛ haa wɔ yɛ gbɛ ni wɔleee nɔ; hɛɛ, jamɔ ni fa babaoo! ejaakɛ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ tɛi, kanei, kɛ kanei ni shãa yɛ shikpɔŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ he lɛ fɛɛ shãa, tamɔ tsutsu lɛ, shãa shĩai babaoo, kɛhaa wɔ anunyam!

Shi kwɛmɔ sane nɛɛ yɛ kanei krokomɛi amli; nyɛsusua lɛ yɛ nsɛnii srɔtoi fɛɛ amli; kwɛmɔ nɔ ni wɔ whalemen lɛ ji, kɛ nɔ ni wɔji.

Mɛni hewɔ Dutchbii ni yɔɔ De Witt beaŋ lɛ yɛ amɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛi lɛ amli onukpai lɛ? Mɛni hewɔ Louis XVI lɛ. ni yɔɔ France lɛ, kɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eshika, ekɛ lɛlɛi ni akɛyaa ŋshɔŋ loofɔji ni jɛ Dunkirk lɛ saa, ni ekɛ bulɛ fɔ wekui nyɔŋmai abɔ loo enyɔ ni jɛ wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔ ŋshɔkpɔ ni ji Nantucket lɛ nɔ lɛ nine kɛba nakai maŋ lɛ mli? Mɛni hewɔ Britain wo e-whalemen lɛ nyɔmɔ ni fe £1,000,000 yɛ afii 1750 kɛ 1788 teŋ lɛ? Ni naagbee lɛ, te efeɔ tɛŋŋ ni wɔ Amerika ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ayi fa fe ŋshɔŋlooi krokomɛi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ je lɛŋ lɛ amrɔ nɛɛ; ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi ni fa fe ohai kpawo; hii akpei nyɔŋma kɛ kpaanyɔ yɔɔ mli; daa afi lɛ, eyeɔ dɔlai 4,000,000; lɛlɛi lɛ jara ji, yɛ be mli ni amɛfãa gbɛ lɛ, dɔlai 20,000,000! ni daa afi lɛ, wɔkɛ ŋmaa ni akpa jogbaŋŋ ni ji dɔlai 7,000,000 baa wɔ ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ. Te efeɔ tɛŋŋ ni enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ baa, kɛji akɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ hewalɛ bɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ mli?

Shi jeee fã ji enɛ; kwɛmɔ ekoŋŋ.

Mikɛ mihe fɔ̃ɔ nɔ akɛ, jeŋ nilelɔ ni yɔɔ jeŋ fɛɛ lɛ nyɛŋ, yɛ eshihilɛ mli lɛ, atsɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ hewalɛ kome pɛ, ni yɛ afii nyɔŋmai ekpaa ni eho lɛ mli lɛ etsu nii jogbaŋŋ yɛ jeŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ nɔ, kɛ akɛto he kome, fe ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ nitsumɔ ni nɔ kwɔ ni yɔɔ hewalɛ waa lɛ. Yɛ gbɛ kome nɔ kɛ gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ, efɔ nibii ni sa kadimɔ waa yɛ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe, ni amɛhe hiaa waa yɛ amɛsɛɛkuu mli, ni abaanyɛ abu ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ akɛ nakai Mizraim nyɛ lɛ, ni lɛ diɛŋtsɛ efɔ bii ni hiɛ musu kɛjɛ efɔfɔ mli lɛ. Ebaafee nitsumɔ ni hiɛnɔkamɔ bɛ mli, ni bɛ naagbee akɛ aaabu nibii nɛɛ fɛɛ anaa. Ha nine kome pɛ afee nɔ ni fa. Afii babaoo ni eho nɛ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛ lɛ ji gbɛgbalɔ ni tsɔɔ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ hei ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ ni aleee amɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ amli. Ekwɛ ŋshɔi kɛ ŋshɔkpɔi akui ni bɛ wolo ko nɔ, he ni Cook loo Vancouver ko efã gbɛ pɛŋ. Kɛji akɛ Amerika kɛ Europa tawuulɔi taraa ŋshɔ hiɛ yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli amrɔ nɛɛ yɛ ŋshɔkpɔi ni be ko ni eho lɛ amɛji ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ anɔ lɛ, nyɛhaa amɛtswaa la ni akɛjieɔ ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ nyam kɛ anunyam, ni shishijee lɛ etsɔɔ amɛ gbɛ, ni etsɔɔ shishi klɛŋklɛŋ yɛ amɛ kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ lɛ. Amɛbaanyɛ amɛye gbijurɔ tamɔ bɔ ni amɛbaafee lɛ, mɛi ni he wa yɛ Shikpamɔ Gbɛfaa lɛ mli, o-Cooks lɛ, o-Krusensterns lɛ; shi mikɛɔ akɛ Kaptenbii babaoo ni aleee amɛ gbɛi lɛ efã gbɛ kɛjɛ Nantucket, ni amɛdara tamɔ nɛkɛ, ni amɛdara fe nyɛ Cook kɛ nyɛ Krusenstern lɛ. Ejaakɛ yɛ amɛ nine folo ni bɛ yelikɛbuamɔ mli lɛ, amɛ, yɛ wɔŋjalɔi a-nui ni ŋshɔŋlooi yɔɔ mli lɛ amli, kɛ ŋshɔkpɔi ni aŋmalako amɛhe saji, ni ji ŋshɔkpɔi ni akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ebɔ he lɛ anɔ lɛ, kɛ naakpɛɛ nii kɛ gbeyeishemɔi ni Cook kɛ e-ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafoi kɛ e-musket lɛ fɛɛ jɛɛɛ amɛsuɔmɔ mli amɛfeee lɛ wu. Nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni afee ni eshwere waa yɛ South Sea Voyages momo lɛ mli lɛ, nakai nibii lɛ ji wɔ Nantucketbii ni he wa lɛ ashihilɛ be mli nibii ni afɔɔ feemɔ kɛkɛ. Bei pii lɛ, nibii ni Vancouver jɔɔ yitsei etɛ nɔ ahaa lɛ, abuɔ hii nɛɛ akɛ esaaa akɛ akɛ amɛ woɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli wolo ni aŋmalaa nɔ nii lɛ mli. Ah, je lɛ! Oo, je lɛ!

Kɛyashi be mli ni ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhe lɛ bɔle Cape Horn he lɛ, jarayeli ko bɛ shi moŋ maŋkwramɔŋ nɔyeli, bɔlɛnamɔ ko bɛ shi moŋ maŋkwramɔŋ nɔyeli, ni tee nɔ yɛ Europa kɛ Spain kpokpai ni yɔɔ Pasifik ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ ateŋ. Whaleman lɛ ji mɔ klɛŋklɛŋ ni tsɔ Spain maŋtsɛyeli lɛ awuŋyeli mla lɛ mli, ni eta nakai maji ni yɔɔ shishi lɛ ahe; ni, kɛji akɛ gbɛ ŋmɛ gbɛ lɛ, abaanyɛ atsɔɔ bɔ ni kɛjɛ nakai ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ adɛŋ yɛ naagbee lɛ, Peru, Chili, kɛ Bolivia ye amɛhe kɛjɛ Spain Blema lɛ kɔŋtso lɛ mli, kɛ naanɔ demokrase ni ato shishi yɛ nakai hei lɛ amli lɛ mli faŋŋ.

Nakai Amerika kpeteŋkpele ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ŋta lɛ fa kroko lɛ, Australia lɛ ji nɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ha jeŋ ni mli ewo la lɛ. Beni Dutchnyo ko na lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ kwraa yɛ tɔmɔ mli lɛ, lɛlɛi krokomɛi fɛɛ tsi amɛhe kɛjɛ nakai ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ ahe be kakadaŋŋ akɛ amɛji ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa; shi ŋshɔŋloo lɛlɛ lɛ yashɛ jɛmɛ. Whale-lɛlɛ lɛ ji nakai maŋ ni yɔɔ hewalɛ amrɔ nɛɛ nyɛ diɛŋtsɛ. Agbɛnɛ hu, yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ Australiabii ashihilɛhe lɛ shishijee mli lɛ, ahere mɛi ni fã kɛba jɛmɛ lɛ ayiwala kɛjɛ hɔmɔ mli shii abɔ kɛtsɔ ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ lɛ biskit ni mli hi ni ekɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ shwie amɛnui lɛ amli yɛ shweremɔ mli lɛ nɔ. Ŋshɔkpɔi ni yɔɔ Polynesia fɛɛ ni akaneee lɛ jajeɔ anɔkwale kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ, ni amɛwoɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ahiɛ nyam yɛ jarayeli gbɛfaŋ, ni no gbele gbɛ kɛha maŋsɛɛ sanekpakpa shiɛlɔ lɛ kɛ jarayelɔ lɛ, ni yɛ bei babaoo mli lɛ, ekɛ blema maŋsɛɛ sanekpakpa shiɛlɔi lɛ tee amɛ klɛŋklɛŋ hei ni amɛyaa lɛ. Kɛji akɛ nakai shikpɔŋ ni yɔɔ ŋmeii enyɔ, Japan lɛ baafee gbɔfeemɔ he gbi ko lɛ, no lɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ pɛ ji mɔ ni abaajie yijiemɔ lɛ kpo; ejaakɛ eshɛ shinaa lɛ naa momo.

Shi kɛji akɛ, yɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ hiɛ lɛ, ojaje lolo akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ bɛ æsthetically noble associations ni kɛ lɛ yɔɔ tsakpaa lɛ, belɛ ani mifee klalo akɛ mikɛ bo baakpokpo ŋshɔŋlooi nyɔŋmai enumɔ yɛ jɛmɛ, ni mikɛ dadefai ni mli eja lɛ ajie bo kɛjɛ okpɔŋɔ nɔ be fɛɛ be.

Obaakɛɛ akɛ, ŋshɔŋloo lɛ bɛ woloŋmalɔ ko ni ehe gbɛi, ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ hu bɛ yinɔsaneŋmalɔ ko ni ehe gbɛi.

Ani ŋshɔŋloo lɛ jeee woloŋmalɔ ni ehe gbɛi, ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ jeee yinɔsane ŋmalɔ ni ehe gbɛi? Namɔ ŋma klɛŋklɛŋ sane ni kɔɔ wɔ Leviatan he lɛ? Namɔ ja Hiob hewalɛ! Ni namɔ ŋma klɛŋklɛŋ sane ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa he lɛ? Namɔ, shi jeee maŋtsɛ bi ni baa shi fe Alfred Kpeteŋkpele lɛ, mɔ ni kɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ emaŋtsɛyeli niŋmaatso lɛ jie wiemɔi lɛ kɛjɛ Other, Norway ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbeetsɛ ni yɔɔ nakai beaŋ lɛ ŋɔɔ lɛ! Ni namɔ tswa wɔ eulogy ni kpɛɔ lɛ yɛ Parliament lɛ? Namɔ, ja Edmund Burke!

Eji anɔkwale akɛ, shi no sɛɛ lɛ, whalemen lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ji daimonioi ohiafoi; amɛbɛ lá kpakpa yɛ amɛfãji amli.

Ani lá kpakpa bɛ amɛ lá fãji amli? Amɛyɛ nɔ ko ni hi fe maŋtsɛ lá yɛ jɛmɛ. Benjamin Franklin nana ji Mary Morrel; no sɛɛ lɛ, kɛtsɔ gbalashihilɛ nɔ lɛ, Mary Folger, ni ji Nantucketbii ni hi shi tsutsu lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome, kɛ Folgers kɛ harpooneerbii akuu kakadaŋŋ atsɛkwɛ̃—ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛji kith kɛ wekumɛi kɛha Benjamin ni hiɛ gbɛi kpakpa lɛ—ŋmɛnɛ gbi nɛɛ amɛmiitswa dade ni yɔɔ ŋmeii lɛ kɛmiijɛ je lɛŋ he kome kɛmiiya he kroko lɛ.

Ehi ekoŋŋ; shi no sɛɛ lɛ amɛ fɛɛ amɛjajeɔ akɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, abuuu ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ.

Whaling jeee bulɛ? Whaling ji maŋtsɛyeli! Yɛ Ŋleshi mla momo lɛ naa lɛ, atswaa ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he adafi akɛ eji “maŋtsɛ loo.” *

Oh, no ji gbɛi pɛ! Whale lɛ diɛŋtsɛ etsɔɔɔ gbɛ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ pɛŋ.

Ani ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ejeee kpo yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ? Yɛ kunimyelii wuji ni akɛha Roma asraafonyo nukpa ko beni eboteɔ je lɛŋ maŋtiase lɛ mli lɛ ateŋ ekome mli lɛ, ŋshɔŋloo wui, ni akɛba kɛjɛ Siria ŋshɔnaa lɛ ji nɔ ni anaa fe fɛɛ yɛ gbɛfaa ni akɛ saŋkui tswaa lɛ mli.*

*Kwɛmɔ yitsei ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ amli kɛha nɔ kroko ni kɔɔ yitso nɛɛ he.

Ha lɛ, akɛni otsɛɔ tã lɛ hewɔ; shi, kɛɛmɔ nɔ ni osumɔɔ, bulɛ diɛŋtsɛ ko bɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ mli.

Ani bulɛ ko bɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ mli? Bulɛ ni yɔɔ wɔ tsɛmɔ ni ji ŋwɛi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ he lɛ yeɔ he odase. Cetus ji ŋulamii akuu ko ni yɔɔ Anaigbɛ! Dabi dɔŋŋ! Tsɔ otsitsi lɛ shi yɛ Czar lɛ hiɛ, ni ojie kɛya Queequeg! Dabi dɔŋŋ! Mile nuu ko ni, yɛ ewala beaŋ lɛ, eŋɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ohai etɛ kɛ nyɔŋmai enumɔ. Mibuɔ nakai nuu lɛ akɛ eji mɔ ni yɔɔ nyam fe nakai blema asraafonyo nukpa kpeteŋkpele ni kɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ nɔ akɛ eŋɔ maji babaoo ni atswa gbogbo awo he lɛ.

Ni, yɛ migbɛfaŋ lɛ, kɛji akɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, nɔ ko ni he hiaa waa yɛ mimli ni anako lolo; kɛji akɛ esa akɛ maná gbɛi diɛŋtsɛ ko yɛ nakai jeŋ bibioo shi nɔ ni yɔɔ dioo ni ekolɛ mikɛ mihe wooo mli yɛ gbɛ ni jwɛŋmɔ bɛ mli nɔ lɛ mli; kɛji akɛ yɛ enɛ sɛɛ lɛ, mafee nɔ ko ni, kɛ asusu he fɛɛ lɛ, kulɛ gbɔmɔ ko baanyɛ afee moŋ fe ni eeeshi ni efeee; kɛji akɛ, yɛ migbele mli lɛ, mɛi ni gbe mi lɛ, loo bɔ ni sa jogbaŋŋ lɛ, mɛi ni hiɛ mi nyɔmɔ lɛ na MSS ko ni jara wa. yɛ mi okpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ, no lɛ biɛ lɛ mikɛ woo kɛ anunyam fɛɛ haa ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ; ejaakɛ ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ ji mi Yale Kɔleji kɛ mi Harvard.





YITSƆƆ 25. Nɔ ni aŋma yɛ sɛɛ mli.

Yɛ bulɛ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ he lɛ najiaŋ lɛ, mikɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko baatsɔɔ hiɛ ja anɔkwalei ni yɔɔ odaseyeli. Shi beni ekɛ e-anɔkwalei lɛ etsu nii sɛɛ lɛ, ani mɔ ko ni fĩɔ esɛɛ ni esa akɛ efo susumɔ ko ni jeee nɔ ni jwɛŋmɔ bɛ mli, ni baanyɛ atsɔɔ nɔ ni ekɛbaa lɛ mli jogbaŋŋ lɛ naa kwraa—ni ji mɔ ko ni fĩɔ esɛɛ lɛ, ani esaaa akɛ ashwaa lɛ?

Ale jogbaŋŋ akɛ yɛ maŋtsɛmɛi kɛ maŋtsɛmɛi yei, ni ji ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ maŋtsɛmɛi lɛ po anɔ lɛ, atsɔɔ gbɛ ko ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni akɛwoɔ amɛmli kɛha amɛnitsumɔi lɛ amli. Maŋ ŋmei ŋtayaa ko yɛ, ni atsɛɔ lɛ nakai, ni ekolɛ maŋ castor hu yɛ. Bɔ ni amɛkɛ ŋmei lɛ tsuɔ nii amɛhaa, pɔtɛɛ—namɔ le? Shi kɛlɛ, miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ awoɔ maŋtsɛ yitso he mu yɛ hiɛdɔɔ mli yɛ be mli ni awoɔ lɛ maŋtsɛ lɛ, tamɔ salad yitso po. Shi kɛlɛ, ani ebaanyɛ efee akɛ amɛfɔ lɛ mu kɛ jwɛŋmɔ akɛ ebaaha emli nibii aya nɔ jogbaŋŋ, taakɛ amɛfɔɔ tsɔnei ahe mu lɛ? Abaanyɛ asusu nibii babaoo he yɛ biɛ, yɛ bulɛ ni he hiaa ni yɔɔ maŋtsɛyeli gbɛjianɔtoo nɛɛ he lɛ he, ejaakɛ yɛ shihilɛ mli lɛ, wɔbuɔ mɔ ko ni fɔɔ eyitsɔi mu, ni enuɔ nakai fɔmɔ lɛ he lɛ akɛ eji mɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei kɛ bulɛ. Yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, nuu ni eda ni kɛ yitsɔi-fɔ tsuɔ nii, ja yɛ tsofafeemɔ gbɛ nɔ lɛ, ekolɛ nakai nuu lɛ ená quoggy spot yɛ emli yɛ he ko. Taakɛ mla ji lɛ, enyɛŋ eshɛ babaoo yɛ egbɛfaŋnɔ fɛɛ mli.

Shi nɔ pɛ ni esa akɛ asusu he yɛ biɛ ji enɛ—mɛɛ mu akɛtsuɔ nii yɛ maŋtsɛyelii ahe nitsumɔ mli? Eji anɔkwale akɛ enyɛŋ efee oliv fɔ, loo macassar fɔ, loo castor fɔ, loo bear fɔ, loo oketeke fɔ, loo cod-liver fɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, mɛni ji nɔ ni ekolɛ ebaanyɛ efee, shi jeee fɔmɔ nii amli fɔ yɛ eshihilɛ ni afeee lɛ mli, ni awooo mli muji, ni ŋɔɔ fe fɔ fɛɛ fɔ lɛ?

Nyɛsusua enɛ he, nyɛ Britaniabii anɔkwafoi! wɔ whalemen lɛ kɛ maŋtsɛmɛi kɛ maŋtsɛmɛi yei lɛ haa maŋtsɛyeli nibii!





YITSƆƆ 26. Knights kɛ Squires.

Pequod lɛ hefatalɔ titri ji Starbuck, ni ji Nantucket maŋnyo, ni eji Quaker yɛ weku mli. Eji nuu kakadaŋŋ ni hiɛdɔɔ yɔɔ mli, ni eyɛ mli akɛ afɔ lɛ yɛ ŋshɔnaa ni ais yɔɔ nɔ moŋ, shi etamɔ nɔ ni enyɛ eŋmɛ etsui shi jogbaŋŋ yɛ latitude ni mli edɔ lɛ mli, ni eheloo lɛ mli wa tamɔ biskit ni ashã shii enyɔ. Kɛ́ akɛ lɛ tee Indies lɛ, ela ni hiɛ kã lɛ fiteŋ tamɔ ale ni akɛwoɔ tɔ mli lɛ. Ekolɛ afɔ lɛ yɛ hɔmɔ kɛ hɔmɔ be ko mli, loo yɛ nakai hɔmɔ gbii ni emaŋ lɛ ehe gbɛi yɛ he lɛ ateŋ ekome nɔ. Latsaa bei aaafee nyɔŋmai etɛ pɛ ni ena; nakai hulutsoo bei lɛ eha egbɔmɔtsoŋ nibii ni he ehiaaa lɛ fɛɛ egbi. Shi enɛ, ni ji egbɔjɔmɔ lɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, etamɔɔɔ nɔ ni eji haomɔi kɛ haomɔi ni efiteɔ lɛ he okadi, fe bɔ ni etamɔ nɔ ni etsɔɔ gbɔmɔtsoŋ hela ko. Nuu lɛ mlifu kɛkɛ ji no. Ejeee mɔ ko ni hiɛ bɛ ehe nɔ kwraa; nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ nakai kwraa. Ehewolo ni he tse ni mli wa lɛ ji nɔ ni sa jogbaŋŋ; ni akɛwo mli jogbaŋŋ, ni akɛ mligbɛ hewalɛnamɔ kɛ hewalɛ wo mli, tamɔ Mizraimnyo ko ni atswa lɛ shi ekoŋŋ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni Starbuck nɛɛ esaa ehe koni eŋmɛ etsui shi kɛyashi afii babaoo ni baaba lɛ, ni eŋmɛ etsui shi be fɛɛ be, tamɔ amrɔ nɛɛ; ejaakɛ eji Polar snoo loo hulu ni tsoɔ waa, tamɔ patent chronometer lɛ, esa akɛ emli hewalɛ lɛ atsu nii jogbaŋŋ yɛ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔi fɛɛ amli. Kɛ́ okwɛ ehiŋmɛii amli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ona nakai osharai akpei abɔ ni ekɛkpe yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli yɛ shihilɛ mli lɛ ahe mfonirii ni kã shi lolo lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ. Nuu ko ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ, ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ, ni eshihilɛ be babaoo ji nifeemɔ he mfoniri ni tsɔɔ nɔ ko, shi jeee gbeei ni yɔɔ mlɛo. Shi, yɛ ehiɛdɔɔ kɛ hewalɛ ni eyɔɔ lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, sui komɛi yɛ emli ni bei komɛi lɛ enaa mɔ nɔ hewalɛ, ni yɛ shihilɛi komɛi amli lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaashɛ ekrokomɛi lɛ fɛɛ ahe. No hewɔ lɛ, akɛni eji mɔ ni henilee bɛ mli kɛha ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔ ko, ni ekɛ adebɔɔ mli bulɛ ni mli kwɔ toɔ etsui shi hewɔ lɛ, eshihilɛ mli nu mli shihilɛ ni yɔɔ ŋa nɔ lɛ tsirɛ lɛ waa kɛtee apasa hemɔkɛyeli mli; shi moŋ kɛyaa nakai apasa hemɔkɛyeli ni tamɔ nɛkɛ nɔ, ni yɛ gbɛjianɔtoi komɛi amli lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ejɛɔ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, kɛjɛɔ nilee mli moŋ fe nilee ni bɛ mli. Kponɔgbɛ okadii kɛ mligbɛ henumɔi ji enɔ. Ni kɛji akɛ bei komɛi lɛ, nɛkɛ nibii nɛɛ haa esusuma dade ni akɛwo mli lɛ shwieɔ shi lɛ, no lɛ, ekaimɔi ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni kɔɔ eŋa kɛ ebi lɛ he lɛ haa eshwieɔ shi babaoo kɛjɛɔ ebɔɔ su lɛ mli, ni egbeleɔ ehe gbɛ lolo kɛhaa nakai hewalɛi ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ ni, yɛ hii anɔkwafoi komɛi amli lɛ, amɛtsiɔ amɛnaa babaoo lɛ, shihilɛi ni yɔɔ oshara ni baa wuoyeli lɛ mli lɛ. Starbuck kɛɛ akɛ: “Mibɛ gbɔmɔ ko yɛ milɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni sheee ŋshɔŋloo ko gbeyei.” Etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ enɛ miitsɔɔ, jeee akɛ ekãa ni anyɛɔ akɛ he fɔ̃ɔ nɔ ni he yɔɔ sɛɛnamɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ ji nɔ ni jɛɔ oshara ni akɛkpeɔ lɛ he akɔntaabuu ni ja mli baa lɛ pɛ, shi moŋ akɛ gbɔmɔ ni sheee gbeyei kwraa lɛ ji nanemɛi ni yɔɔ oshara kwraa fe gbeyeishelɔ.

Stubb, ni ji hefatalɔ ni ji enyɔ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Aye, aye, Starbuck, jɛmɛ, ji gbɔmɔ ni kwɛɔ ehe nɔ jogbaŋŋ tamɔ bɔ ni obaana yɛ he fɛɛ he yɛ nɛkɛ wuoyelihe nɛɛ.” Shi wɔbaana nɔ ni nakai wiemɔ ni ji “kwɛ ohe nɔ jogbaŋŋ” lɛ tsɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ kɛ nuu ko tamɔ Stubb, loo nɔ ni miihe ashɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbeelɔ kroko fɛɛ kɛtsu nii lɛ be kakadaŋŋ.

Starbuck jeee krɔs hefatalɔ yɛ osharai asɛɛ; yɛ emli lɛ, ekãa jeee henumɔ ko; shi moŋ eji nɔ ko ni he yɔɔ sɛɛnamɔ kɛkɛ kɛha lɛ, ni yɔɔ edɛŋ be fɛɛ be yɛ bei fɛɛ ni he hiaa ni haa mɔ gboɔ lɛ amli. Kɛfata he lɛ, esusu akɛ, ekolɛ, yɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔ nɛɛ mli lɛ, ekãa ji atadei wuji ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ ateŋ ekome, tamɔ etsina loo kɛ eblodo, ni esaaa akɛ afiteɔ yɛ kwashiai agbɛ nɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, ebɛ suɔmɔ ko akɛ ebaaba shi kɛtao ŋshɔŋ loofɔji beni hulu lɛ enyɔ shi lɛ sɛɛ; asaŋ akɛni etee nɔ ekɛ loo ko ni tee nɔ fe nine ni ekɛ lɛ wu lɛ hewɔ. Ejaakɛ, Starbuck susu akɛ, miyɛ biɛ yɛ ŋshɔ ni he hiaa waa nɛɛ mli koni magbe ŋshɔŋlooi kɛha mishihilɛ, ni jeee koni amɛgbe mi kɛha amɛshihilɛ; ni akɛ agbe hii ohai abɔ nakai lɛ Starbuck le jogbaŋŋ. Mɛɛ hiɛkpatamɔ ji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ etsɛ nɔ? Nɛgbɛ, yɛ ŋshɔ ni mli kwɔ ni bɛ shishi lɛ mli lɛ, ebaana enyɛmi lɛ niji ni etse lɛ yɛ?

Kɛ kaimɔi ni tamɔ enɛɛmɛi yɛ emli, ni, kɛfata he lɛ, akɛha apasa hemɔkɛyeli ko, taakɛ awie lɛ; Ekãa ni Starbuck nɛɛ ni baanyɛ, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, aya hiɛ lolo lɛ yɔɔ lɛ, esa akɛ efee nɔ ni fe nine diɛŋtsɛ. Shi jeee yɛ jwɛŋmɔŋ su mli ji nɔ ni ha gbɔmɔ ko to gbɛjianɔ nakai, ni ekɛ niiashikpamɔi kɛ kaimɔi ni yɔɔ gbeyei tamɔ nɔ ni eyɔɔ lɛ tsu nii; jeee adebɔɔ mli ji akɛ nɛkɛ nibii nɛɛ baafee nɔ ni bɛ sɛɛnamɔ yɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ emli lɛ ni, yɛ shihilɛi ni sa mli lɛ, ebaaje kpo kɛjɛ etsuŋwoo mli, ni eshã ekãa fɛɛ lɛ mli. Ni bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ ekãa ha lɛ, eji nakai ekãa lɛ titri, ni anaa yɛ hii komɛi ni yɔɔ ekãa lɛ amli, ni, yɛ be mli ni ehiɔ shi shiŋŋ yɛ ŋshɔi, loo kɔɔyɔɔ, loo ŋshɔŋlooi, loo je lɛŋ gbeyeishemɔi ni bɛ jwɛŋmɔ lɛ eko mli lɛ, shi kɛlɛ enyɛŋ edamɔ nakai gbeyeishemɔi ni fe fɛɛ lɛ anaa, ejaakɛ mumɔŋ gbeyeishemɔi babaoo ni jɛɔ mihe lɛ, gbɔmɔ ni mli efu ni ehe wa.

Shi kɛji akɛ sanegbaa ni baa lɛ baajie lɛ kpo yɛ shihilɛ fɛɛ shihilɛ mli, Starbuck ohiafo lɛ hewalɛ ni eba shi kwraa lɛ, ekolɛ ewaaa akɛ maná tsui ni mikɛŋma; ejaakɛ eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ awerɛho fe fɛɛ, dabi, nɔ ni haa mɔ naa kpɛɔ ehe, ni aaajie ekãa ni gbee shi yɛ susuma lɛ mli lɛ kpo. Ekolɛ ebaafee tamɔ mɛi ni yɔɔ nyaŋemɔ akɛ nitsumɔhei ni kɛ amɛhe feɔ ekome kɛ maji; shishiulɔi, kwashiai, kɛ gbɔmɔgbelɔi baanyɛ ahi jɛmɛ; hii baanyɛ ahiɛ afee shoo ni amɛhe efee shoo; shi gbɔmɔ, yɛ susumɔ kpakpa mli lɛ, eji mɔ ko ni nɔ kwɔ waa ni ekpɛɔ waa, eji adebɔɔ ko ni da ni kpɛɔ waa, ni esa akɛ enanemɛi lɛ fɛɛ ajo foi kɛyashɛrɛ amɛ atadei ni jara wa fe fɛɛ lɛ ashwie. Nakai nuufeemɔ ni he tseee ni wɔnuɔ he yɛ wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔmli, kɛyashi wɔmli shɔŋŋ, ni ehiɔ shi eyɛ mli akɛ etamɔ nɔ ni kponɔgbɛ su lɛ fɛɛ etee; lá hoɔ kɛ piŋmɔ ni mli wa fe fɛɛ yɛ nuu ko ni ekãa efite lɛ nifeemɔ ni awooo he atade lɛ mli. Ni Nyɔŋmɔjamɔ diɛŋtsɛ hu nyɛŋ afee, yɛ hiɛgbele ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ, ni ekɛtsi ehe guɔ ni ekɛshiɔ ŋulamii ni ŋmɛɔ gbɛ lɛ naa kwraa. Shi nɛkɛ bulɛ ni nɔ kwɔ ni mikɛtsuɔ nii nɛɛ, jeee maŋtsɛmɛi kɛ atadei abulɛ, shi moŋ nakai bulɛ babaoo ni bɛ atadei ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ. Obaana lɛ ni ekpɛɔ yɛ nine ni hiɛɔ ŋmei loo ni kudɔɔ ŋmei lɛ mli; nakai demokrase bulɛ ni, yɛ niji fɛɛ nɔ lɛ, ekpɛɔ ni naagbee bɛ he kɛjɛɔ Nyɔŋmɔ ŋɔɔ lɛ; Lɛ diɛŋtsɛ! Nyɔŋmɔ kpeteŋkpele lɛ diɛŋtsɛ! Demokrasi fɛɛ teŋgbɛ kɛ esɛɛgbɛ! Ehe ni eyɔɔ he fɛɛ he, wɔ ŋwɛi ekomefeemɔ!

No hewɔ lɛ, kɛji akɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfalɔi ni bɛ sɛɛnamɔ fe fɛɛ, kɛ atuatselɔi kɛ mɛi ni eshi ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ, mikɛ sui ni nɔ kwɔlɔ baaha amɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ amɛji duŋ moŋ; tsɔ duromɔi ni yɔɔ awerɛho nɔ obɔle amɛhe; kɛji mɔ ni yeɔ awerɛho fe fɛɛ po, ekolɛ mɔ ni baa ehe shi fe fɛɛ, yɛ amɛ fɛɛ ateŋ lɛ, baawó ehe nɔ yɛ bei komɛi amli kɛya gɔji ni nɔ kwɔlɔ lɛ anɔ; kɛji akɛ mikɛ ethereal kane ko ta nakai nitsulɔ lɛ nine he; kɛji mikɛ ŋwɛibɔfo ko shwie ehulu ni enyɔ shi ni ekɛ oshara baa lɛ nɔ; no sɛɛ lɛ, te shi owo mɛi ni gboiɔ lɛ fɛɛ ni wieɔ ashiɔ lɛ, jiemɔ mi kpo yɛ mli, bo Ekomefeemɔ Mumɔ jalɛ, ni egbɛ adesa maŋtsɛ atade kome eshwa mihenɔi lɛ fɛɛ nɔ lɛ! Tsɔɔmɔ mi yɛ mli, bo demokrase Nyɔŋmɔ kpeteŋkpele! ni ekpooo mɔ ni abu lɛ fɔ ni ji swart lɛ, Bunyan, ni ji lalafoo wiemɔi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ; Bo ni okɛ shika tsuru ni hi fe fɛɛ baai ni akɛ hama etswa shii enyɔ wo atade lɛ, Cervantes onukpa lɛ nine ni egbɔjɔ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ; Bo ni ojie Andrew Jackson kɛjɛ tɛi bibii lɛ amli lɛ; ni shwie lɛ ewo tawuu okpɔŋɔ nɔ lɛ; ni tswa lɛ ŋwɛi ŋwɛi fe maŋtsɛ sɛi lɛ! Bo ni, yɛ Ohewalɛ, shikpɔŋ nɔ nyiɛmɔi fɛɛ amli lɛ, ogbeɔ Ohefatalɔi ni ohala fe fɛɛ lɛ kɛjɛɔ maŋtsɛmɛi akuu lɛ mli be fɛɛ be lɛ; jiemɔ mi kpo yɛ mli, Nyɔŋmɔ!





YITSƆƆ 27. Knights kɛ Squires.

Stubb ji hefatalɔ ni ji enyɔ. Eji Cape Cod maŋnyo; ni no hewɔ lɛ, taakɛ bɔ ni akɛtsuɔ nii yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ tsɔɔ lɛ, atsɛɔ lɛ Cape-Cod-nuu. Miishɛɛ-yaa-shweremɔ; jeee craven loo ekãalɔ; osharai ni akɛtsuɔ nii beni amɛbaa kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni bɛ he miishɛɛ lɛ; ni beni ekɛ ehe woɔ haomɔ ni bɛŋkɛ fe fɛɛ ni ji sɛɛtiumɔ lɛ mli lɛ, etsuɔ ​​nii waa, efeɔ kpoo ni ebua ehe naa akɛ gbɛfalɔ ni kɛ ehe woɔ nitsumɔ mli kɛha afi lɛ. Eyɛ miishɛɛ, eyɛ mlɛo, ni ekwɛɛɛ ehe nɔ jogbaŋŋ, ni ekwɛ e-whale-lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ tamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ni gbeɔ mɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ ji gbɛkɛ niyenii kɛkɛ, ni e-lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ fɛɛ fɔɔ gbɔi nine. Esusu bɔ ni ato lɛlɛ lɛ fã ni eyɔɔ lɛ he gbɛjianɔ jogbaŋŋ lɛ he titri, taakɛ tsɔne kudɔlɔ onukpa ko susuɔ bɔ ni e-adeka lɛ mli ŋɔɔ lɛ he lɛ. Beni ebɛŋkɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, yɛ tawuu lɛ mli gbele mli lɛ, ekɛ e-lance ni mɔbɔnalɛ bɛ mli lɛ tsu nii yɛ mlijɔlɛ mli ni ekɛ enine tsuuu nii, tamɔ mɔ ko ni tswaa ŋmɛlɛ ni ekɛ e-hama lɛ tsuɔ nii lɛ. Shi eji lɛlɛ lɛ mli onukpai etɛ ni ji hefatalɔi lɛ afã titri, ni abu he akɔntaa yɛ hewalɛ mli fe fɛɛ koni ejie nɛkɛ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔi ni bɛ su nɛɛ, ni ekɛ hekɛnɔfɔɔ kɛ miishɛɛ aba yɛ gbɛfaa lɛ mli nifeemɔ fɛɛ nifeemɔ mli. Anyɛɛɛ ana ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafoi kɛ hii etɛ ni hi fe amɛ, ni ekolɛ amɛteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ yɛ egbɛ nɔ oya, ni amɛteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ji Amerikanyo; Nantucketnyo ko, Wein ŋmɔ mli nitsulɔ ko, Capenyo ko. Amrɔ nɛɛ, akɛni Blonya ji beni lɛlɛ lɛ tu kɛjɛ e-lɛjiadaamɔhe lɛ, kɛha he ko ni wɔyɔɔ Polar kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ ni naa wa waa, eyɛ mli akɛ be fɛɛ be lɛ wɔjoɔ foi kɛjɛɔ jɛmɛ kɛyaa anaigbɛ moŋ; kɛ latitude degree kɛ miniti fɛɛ miniti ni wɔfã gbɛ lɛ nɔ, ni fiofio lɛ wɔshiɔ nakai fɛ̃i be ni mɔbɔnalɛ bɛ mli lɛ, kɛ ekɔɔyɔɔ ni anyɛɛɛ aŋmɛ gbɛ lɛ fɛɛ yɛ wɔsɛɛ. Eji nakai leebi ni baa shi fioo, shi eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lolo lɛ ateŋ ekome, beni kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ kpakpa miiho kɛmiitsɔ nu lɛ mli kɛ oyaiyeli ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli kɛ awerɛhoyeli, ni beni mitara lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ yɛ shwane ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ tsɛmɔ naa lɛ, amrɔ nɔŋŋ ni mijo foi kɛtee mi mi. Nɔ ni yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ fe gbeyeishemɔ; Kapten Ahab damɔ e-lɛlɛ lɛ fã ni ji ejwɛ lɛ nɔ.

Etamɔ nɔ ni gbɔmɔtsoŋ hela ni afɔɔ namɔ he okadi ko bɛ ehe, loo akɛ ehiɛ ejɔ ehe kɛjɛ eko mli. Etamɔ mɔ ko ni afo lɛ kɛjɛ tso lɛ he, beni la lɛ efite nibii fɛɛ ni efite ni efiteee amɛ, loo ejiee nɔ ko bibioo ko kɛjɛ amɛhewalɛ ni egbɔ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ mli lɛ. Ehe ni nɔ kwɔ ni mli kwɔ lɛ fɛɛ tamɔ nɔ ni akɛ akɔɔble ni wa fee, ni afee lɛ yɛ tsɔne ko ni nyɛɛɛ atsake mli, tamɔ Perseus ni Cellini fee lɛ. Beni okɛ tsɔne lɛ shwieɔ shi kɛjɛɔ eyitsɔi ŋmɔji lɛ ateŋ, ni eyaa nɔ kɛyaa ehiɛ kɛ etsitsi ni edɔ waa lɛ fã kome pɛpɛɛpɛ, kɛyashi elaaje yɛ etadei lɛ amli lɛ, ona okadi bibioo ko ni tamɔ tso, ni eyɛ futafuta waa. Etamɔ nakai ŋmei ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ ni bei komɛi lɛ afee yɛ tso wulu ko shishigbɛ ni ja ni nɔ kwɔ lɛ mli lɛ, beni ŋwɛi sarawagbamɔ lɛ shwieɔ shi kɛyaa shi lɛ, ni ekɛ tso nine kome po efolɔɔɔ mli, ni efoɔ tso lɛ yibii lɛ kɛjɛɔ ŋwɛi kɛyaa shishi, dani ejoɔ foi kɛyaa shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ, ni ehaa tso lɛ feɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ shi eyɛ wala mli lolo. Kɛji akɛ nakai okadi lɛ kɛ lɛ fɔ lɛ, loo eji ŋmei ni pilamɔ ko ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ eshi lɛ jio lɛ, mɔ ko mɔ ko nyɛŋ akɛɛ nɔmimaa. Kɛtsɔ kpɛlɛmɔ ko ni yɔɔ dioo nɔ lɛ, yɛ gbɛfaa lɛ mli fɛɛ lɛ, awieee he fioo loo awieee he kwraa, titri lɛ hefatalɔi lɛ. Shi be ko lɛ, Tashtego onukpa, ni ji Gay-Head Indianyo onukpa ko ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ ateŋ lɛ, kɛ apasa hemɔkɛyeli ma nɔ mi akɛ, jeee kɛyashi eye afii nyɔŋmai ejwɛ ni Ahab batsɔ nakai gbɛi lɛ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ eba enɔ, jeee yɛ gbele mlifu ko mli, shi moŋ yɛ shishijee béi ko ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ mli. Shi, nɛkɛ ŋa nɔ tsɔɔmɔ nɛɛ tamɔ nɔ ni ehiii kwraa, kɛtsɔ nɔ ni Manxnyo ko ni hiɛ tsuru ni ji gbonyobu mli nuu onukpa ko tsɔɔ mli lɛ nɔ, ni, akɛni ekɛ lɛlɛ eshiko Nantucket pɛŋ hewɔ lɛ, ekɛ ehiŋmɛii efɔko ŋa nɔ Ahab nɔ pɛŋ lɛ nɔ. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ kusumii momo lɛ, hemɔkɛyelii ni akaiɔ lɛ, ha Manxman onukpa nɛɛ kɛ sɛɛyoomɔ hewalɛi ni fe adebɔɔ nɔ lɛ wo mli yɛ gbɛ ni mɛi babaoo sumɔɔ nɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko ni ji blɔfonyo ko kɛ hiɛdɔɔ teee shi ewo lɛ beni ekɛɛ akɛ kɛji akɛ abaawo Kapten Ahab shi yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli gbi ko lɛ—ni ekolɛ ebaaba mli kwraa lɛ, no hewɔ lɛ ewie huhuuhu—no lɛ, mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ni baatsu nakai naagbee nitsumɔ lɛ eha gbohii lɛ, baaná fɔmɔ he okadi yɛ ehe kɛjɛ etsitsi nɔ kɛyashi enaneshi.

Ahab su ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ fɛɛ ná minɔ hewalɛ waa, kɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ mli lɛ, ni yɛ klɛŋklɛŋ bei fioo lɛ amli lɛ, miyɔseee akɛ nɛkɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni yeɔ mɔ nɔ nɛɛ fioo ko pɛ jɛɛɛ naji yɛŋ ni edamɔ nɔ lɛ fã ko hewɔ. No mli lɛ eba mijwɛŋmɔ mli akɛ, akɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni ji sperm whale lɛ tsitsi ni akpɛ́ lɛ fee nɛkɛ naji ni akɛ shwuɔ wu fee nɛɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ. Be ko lɛ, Indianyo onukpa ni ji Gay-Head lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Hɛɛ, enijiaŋjewui kɛjɛ Japan; “shi tamɔ lɛlɛ ni efite lɛ, ekɛ lɛlɛ kroko maje ni ebaaa shia kɛha lɛ.

Bɔ ni ehiɛ shi yɛ gbɛ kome nɔ lɛ ha minaa kpɛ mihe waa. Yɛ Pequod ŋshɔ lɛ fã fɛɛ fã, ni ebɛŋkɛ mizzen shrouds lɛ jogbaŋŋ lɛ, auger bu ko yɛ, ni efo aaafee inchi fã loo nɔ ni fe nakai, kɛbote tso lɛ mli. Enaji ni ji wu lɛ ma shi shiŋŋ yɛ nakai bu lɛ mli; nine kome ni awo nɔ, ni akɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ mɔ mli lɛ hiɛɔ; Kapten Ahab damɔ shi shiŋŋ, ni ekwɛ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ ni shwieɔ shi be fɛɛ be lɛ sɛɛ tɛ̃ɛ. Hewalɛ ni mli wa fe fɛɛ ni bɛ naagbee, yiŋkpɛɛ, yiŋkpɛɛ ni anyɛɛɛ ajie lɛ kpo yɛ, yɛ nakai hiŋmɛigbelemɔ lɛ hetuu-kɛhamɔ ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ ni gbeyeishemɔ bɛ mli, ni yaa hiɛ lɛ mli. Ewieee wiemɔ kome folo po; ni esɔɔlɔi lɛ hu ekɛɛɛɛ lɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko; eyɛ mli akɛ kɛtsɔ amɛ nifeemɔi kɛ wiemɔi bibii fɛɛ nɔ lɛ, amɛjie bɔ ni amɛyɔɔ nuŋtsɔ hiŋmɛi ni haoɔ lɛ shishi lɛ kpo faŋŋ, kɛ jeee piŋmɔ. Ni jeee no pɛ, shi moŋ Ahab ni mli efu lɛ damɔ amɛhiɛ kɛ sɛŋmɔtso yɛ ehiɛ; yɛ maŋtsɛyeli bulɛ ni yeɔ nɔ ni bɛ gbɛi lɛ fɛɛ mli ni ji haomɔ kpeteŋkpele ko.

Etsɛɛɛ tsɔ, kɛjɛ eklɛŋklɛŋ saramɔ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli lɛ, ejie ehe kɛtee etsu bibioo lɛ mli. Shi yɛ nakai leebi lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ naa lɛ daa gbi; loo edamɔ e-pivot-hole lɛ mli, loo eta shwuɔ wu sɛi ko ni eyɔɔ lɛ nɔ; loo enyiɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ waa. Beni ŋwɛi lɛ mli ewo duŋ lɛ; lɛɛlɛŋ, ebɔi mlihilɛ fioo shweremɔ, ebatsɔ mɔ ko ni kɛ ehe wooo shihilɛ mli lolo; tamɔ nɔ ni, beni lɛlɛ lɛ fã gbɛ kɛjɛ shia lɛ, nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ni no ha ekɛ ehe ebɔ ehe nakai beaŋ tamɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli fɛ̃i be mli ŋmɔlɔ ni egbo lɛ. Ni, be fioo ko lɛ, eba mli akɛ, eshwɛ fioo ni ebaahi kɔɔyɔɔ mli daa; shi, kɛyashi amrɔ nɛɛ, yɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni ewie, loo ni efee yɛ gbɛ ni anyɛɔ ayɔseɔ nɔ lɛ sɛɛ, yɛ naagbee hulutsoo be lɛ nɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ehe ehiaaa yɛ jɛmɛ tamɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kroko. Shi Pequod lɛ miifee gbɛfaa pɛ bianɛ; jeee ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfaa daa; nɔ ni miihe ashɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe saamɔi fɛɛ ni he hiaa ni akwɛ nɔ lɛ hefatalɔi lɛ he esa jogbaŋŋ, no hewɔ lɛ nɔ ko fioo pɛ loo nɔ ko bɛ, kɛjɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ emli, ni ekɛbaatsu nii loo ekɛwo Ahab miishɛɛ, bianɛ; ni no hewɔ lɛ amɛshwieɔ amɛhe, yɛ nakai be kome lɛ mli lɛ, atatui ni shwereɔ amɛhe lɛ toɔ ehiɛ he, taakɛ atatui fɛɛ halaa gɔji ni nɔ kwɔlɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ koni amɛto amɛhe amɛwo nɔ lɛ.

Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, etsɛɛɛ tsɔ, ni etamɔ nɔ ni gbijurɔyeli kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ ni ŋɔɔ ni wɔbashɛ mli lɛ mli la ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ miitsi lɛ fiofio kɛjɛ esusumɔi amli. Ejaakɛ, tamɔ be mli ni gbekɛbii yei ni hiɛ tsitsii tsuru, ni joɔ, April kɛ May, fãa gbɛ kɛyaa shia kɛyaa fɛ̃i be mli kooi ni mɛi bɛ mli lɛ amli lɛ; kɛ hoo lɛ, tso momo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ po baatsu ŋmeii fioo komɛi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, koni ekɛhere gbɔi ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ tamɔ nɛkɛ atuu; no hewɔ lɛ Ahab fee, yɛ naagbee lɛ, hetoo fioo yɛ nakai gbekɛ yoo kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ shwɛmɔ nibii ni gbalaa mɔ lɛ ahe. Nɔ ni fe shikome jie hiŋmɛi nɔ fɔfɔi ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ kpo, ni yɛ gbɔmɔ kroko mli lɛ, etsɛŋ ni ebaaje kpo yɛ ŋmɔlɔ mli.
