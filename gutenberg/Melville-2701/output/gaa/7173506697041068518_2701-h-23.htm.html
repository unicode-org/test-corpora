"Ha, ha, mi lɛlɛ! Ekolɛ abaaŋɔ bo bianɛ akɛ hulu lɛ ŋshɔ shwiili lɛ. Ho, ho! Nyɛ jeŋmaji fɛɛ ni yɔɔ mihiɛ lɛ, mikɛ hulu lɛ baa nyɛŋɔɔ! Mikɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ anɔ ŋshɔkei lɛ anɔ; hallo! Tandem, mikɛ ŋshɔ lɛ kudɔɔ!"

“Hɛɛ; shi ebako minɔ dã, onukpa,” hefatalɔ ni hiɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ kɛɛ kɛ mlifu.

Esa akɛ akɛɛ yɛ biɛ akɛ osharai ni tamɔ nɛkɛ eba lɛlɛi ni yɔɔ ahum ni naa wa mli lɛ anɔ yɛ shihilɛi ni fa fe ekome mli. Shi yɛ shihilɛi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, tsɔne lɛ diɛŋtsɛ, enaaa shishijee su kpakpa ni efite loo elaaje yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ lɛ ekoŋŋ kɔkɔɔkɔ; ni kɛji akɛ binnacle kɔmpas lɛ ná he sɛɛ lɛ, nakai shihilɛ lɛ nɔŋŋ baa mɛi krokomɛi fɛɛ ni baanyɛ ahi lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ anɔ; po ji nɔ ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ ni akɛwo kelson lɛ mli.

Beni nuumo lɛ jɛ etsui mli edamɔ binnacle lɛ hiɛ, ni ekwɛ kɔmpas ni atsɔɔ mli lɛ, ni ekɛ enine ni ekpã mli lɛ mli ŋmɔlɔ lɛ, agbɛnɛ eŋɔ hulu lɛ he pɛpɛɛpɛ, ni etsui enyɔ emli akɛ ŋmeii lɛ etsɔ amɛhe pɛpɛɛpɛ, ni ebolɔ efã akɛ atsake lɛlɛ lɛ gbɛ lɛ yɛ gbɛ ni sa nɔ. Yaadi lɛ mli wa; ni shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, Pequod lɛ kɛ ekpɔlɔi ni esheee gbeyei lɛ shwie kɔɔyɔɔ ni teɔ shi woɔ lɛ lɛ mli, ejaakɛ mɔ ni asusuɔ akɛ ehi jogbaŋŋ lɛ ebɔi lɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ pɛ.

Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni ji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eteemɔŋ susumɔi lɛ, Starbuck ewieee nɔ ko, shi moŋ ekɛ famɔi fɛɛ ni he hiaa lɛ ha dioo; yɛ be mli ni Stubb kɛ Flask—ni yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni yɛ gbɛ bibioo ko nɔ lɛ, amɛmiigba ehenumɔi ahe sane lɛ—lɛ hu kpɛlɛ nɔ ni amɛwieee huhuuhu. Yɛ hii lɛ agbɛfaŋ lɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ amɛteŋ mɛi komɛi kɛ amɛhe baawo gbee mli moŋ, shi gbeyeishemɔ ni amɛsheɔ Ahab lɛ fa fe gbeyeishemɔ ni amɛsheɔ Fate lɛ. Shi taakɛ eji tsutsu lɛ, eshwɛ fioo ni wɔŋjalɔi ni tswaa harpoo lɛ tee nɔ amɛfeee nɔ ko kwraa; aloo kɛji akɛ ehiɛ fee amɛ yaa lɛ, no lɛ eji kɛtsɔ magnetism ko ni jɛ Ahab tsui ni tsakeee lɛ mli lɛ pɛ nɔ etswa amɛtsui ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ lɛ mli.

Kɛha be ko lɛ, nuumo lɛ nyiɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ yɛ jwɛŋmɔi ni mli wa mli. Shi beni ekɛ e-ivory heel lɛ shwie shi lɛ, ena akɔɔble sight-tubes ni ekpɔtɔ ni yɔɔ quadrant ni eyɔɔ gbi ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ mli lɛ ni efo kɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ.

"Bo ohiafo, ŋwɛi-kwɛlɔ ni yɔɔ henɔwomɔ kɛ hulu lɛ kudɔlɔ! nyɛ mifite bo, ni ŋmɛnɛ lɛ, kulɛ kɔmpas lɛ baafite mi. No hewɔ lɛ, nakai. Shi Ahab ji nuŋtsɔ yɛ jatsu tɛ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ nɔ lolo. Mr. Starbuck—lanse ni bɛ tso; top-maul, kɛ sa lɛ mli bibioo fe fɛɛ!"

Ekolɛ, nɔ ni fata kanyamɔ ​​ni tsɔɔ nɔ ni eefee amrɔ nɛɛ he ji yiŋtoi komɛi ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ, ni ekolɛ oti ni ma ehiɛ ji ni ekɛ ehesaamɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ atsu nii ni ekɛha egbɛfalɔi lɛ amumɔi aya hiɛ ekoŋŋ, yɛ sane ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ tamɔ kɔmpas ni atsake lɛ nɔ lɛ mli. Kɛfata he lɛ, nuumo lɛ le jogbaŋŋ akɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ eji nɔ ni anyɛɔ atsuɔ he nii yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo nɔ moŋ, shi jeee nɔ ko ni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ni heɔ apasa hemɔkɛyeli amɛyeɔ lɛ baatsɔ hiɛ, ni amɛhe hosoɔ kɛ okadii gbohii komɛi bɛ mli.

Ekɛɛ, ni etsɔ ehe ekwɛ lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ fiofio, beni hefatalɔ lɛ kɛ nibii ni ebi lɛ lɛ haa lɛ lɛ, “mihii, nugbɔshiimɔi lɛ tsɔ Ahab ŋmeii momo lɛ, shi Ahab baanyɛ ekɛ dade bibioo nɛɛ afee lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eko, ni baatsɔɔ anɔkwale tamɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ.”

Lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ tsake amɛhiɛgbele ni tsɔɔ nyɔŋ naakpɛɛ nii, taakɛ awie enɛ lɛ; ni amɛkɛ hiŋmɛii ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ miimɛ majik fɛɛ majik ni baanyɛ aba yɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ. Shi Starbuck kwɛ he kroko.

Kɛkɛ ni, beni ekɛ maul lɛ etswa dade tso nɛɛ ŋwɛi naagbee lɛ shii abɔ sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ ŋmei ni efo lɛ naagbee lɛ fɔ̃ɔ enɔ, ni ekɛ hama ni mli wa fioo tswaa nakai, shii abɔ, ni hefatalɔ lɛ hiɛ tso lɛ lolo tamɔ tsutsu lɛ. Shishijee lɛ, dade lɛ tee nɔ ebɔle ehe, ni ekpokpo ni ekpokpo yɛ enaagbee fɛɛ naagbee; shi naagbee lɛ, ehi shi yɛ egbɛhe lɛ, beni Ahab, ni kɛ ejwɛŋmɔ fɛɛ ekwɛ nɔ ni baaba lɛ, ku esɛɛ kɛjɛ ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ enine ni ekpã mli lɛ tsɔɔ jɛmɛ, ni ebolɔ akɛ, —“Nyɛkwɛa, nyɛ diɛŋtsɛ nyɛhe, kɛji Ahab jeee jatsu tɛ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi lɛ nuŋtsɔ!

YITSƆƆ 125. Log kɛ Line.

Yɛ be mli ni amrɔ nɛɛ Pequod ni ato he gbɛjianɔ lɛ ehi nu lɛ nɔ be kakadaŋŋ yɛ gbɛfaa nɛɛ mli lɛ, akɛ tso lɛ kɛ tsɔne lɛ tsuuu nii tsɔ. Akɛni jarayelɔi komɛi, kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi babaoo, titri lɛ kɛ́ amɛmiiya ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ, kɛ amɛhe fɔ̃ɔ gbɛi krokomɛi ni atsɔɔ nɔ aleɔ he ni lɛlɛ lɛ yɔɔ lɛ anɔ kɛ hekɛnɔfɔɔ hewɔ lɛ, amɛkuɔ amɛhiɛ amɛshwieɔ tso lɛ nɔ kwraa; shi yɛ nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ, ni bei pii lɛ, yɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ ha lɛ hewɔ fe nɔ fɛɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ gbɛ ni lɛlɛ lɛ kudɔɔ lɛ fɔ̃ɔ kusum naa slate lɛ nɔ daa, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu bɔ ni asusuɔ akɛ eyaa hiɛ ehaa yɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa fɛɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa mli lɛ. Nakai ji bɔ ni eba Pequod lɛ he. Tso ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli kɛ tso ni yɔɔ koŋ ni akɛkpɛtɛ he lɛ fĩ shi, be kakadaŋŋ ni mɔ ko mɔ ko etako he, yɛ sɛɛ gbogboi lɛ ashishigbɛ pɛ. Nugbɔ kɛ nu ni shwieɔ shi lɛ eha edɔ; hulu kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ etsɔ mli; nibii fɛɛ ni yɔɔ mli lɛ ebɔ he ni amɛfite nɔ ko ni fĩ shi ni efeee nɔ ko lɛ. Shi beni ekwɛɛɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ nɔ lɛ, esusumɔi mɔ Ahab, beni ebalɛ akɛ ekwɛ reel lɛ nɔ, ŋmɛlɛtswai babaoo sɛɛ beni magnet nifeemɔ lɛ ba lɛ, ni ekai bɔ ni e-quadrant lɛ bɛ dɔŋŋ lɛ, ni ekai ekita ni ekɛ mlifu wo yɛ tso ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ kɛ line lɛ he lɛ. Lɛlɛ lɛ miiho kɛmiiya ŋshɔ lɛ mli; astern lɛ, ŋshɔkei lɛ shwie shi yɛ basabasafeemɔ mli.

"Yaa hiɛ, jɛmɛ! Wo tso lɛ nɔ!"

Ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi enyɔ ba. Tahitinyo ni hiɛ shika tsuru kɛ Manxman ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ. “Nyɛŋɔ reel lɛ, nyɛteŋ mɔ kome, mawo nɔ.”

Amɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛgbɛ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛgbɛ, he ni lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ, kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ hewalɛ ni tsɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ, miihe agbɛnɛ eshwɛ fioo ni ebaatsɔ ŋshɔ ni mli edɔ ni hoɔ oya yɛ esɛɛgbɛ lɛ mli lɛ.

Manxman lɛ ŋɔ tsɔne lɛ, ni ehiɛ ŋwɛi shɔɔŋ, kɛtsɔ tsɔne lɛ nine ni jeɔ kpo lɛ nɔ, ni tsɔne lɛ tsɔne lɛ bɔleɔ he lɛ, ni edamɔ shi ni tso ni yɔɔ koŋ lɛ fĩ shi kɛtee shishi, kɛyashi Ahab ba eŋɔɔ.

"Onukpa, miheee miyeee; nɛkɛ line nɛɛ tamɔ nɔ ni etee shɔŋŋ, dɔlɛ kakadaŋŋ kɛ fɛ̃i efite lɛ."

"'Twill hold, old gentleman. Dɔlɛ kakadaŋŋ kɛ fɛ̃i, ani amɛfite bo? Etamɔ nɔ ni ohiɛ. Aloo, anɔkwale ekolɛ, wala hiɛ bo; jeee bo."

"Mihiɛ spool lɛ mli, onukpa. Shi taakɛ mi kapten lɛ kɛɛ lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ. Kɛ mi yitsɔi ŋmɔŋ nɛɛ 's sɛɛnamɔ bɛ he akɛ mikɛ mihe aaaje ŋwane, 'titri lɛ kɛ onukpa ko, ni jajeŋ kɔkɔɔkɔ."

"Mɛni ji no? Amrɔ nɛɛ tsɔɔlɔ ko ni yɔɔ Queen Nature tɛi ni akɛfee lɛ kɔleji lɛ; shi misusuɔ akɛ ebaa ehe shi tsɔ. Nɛgbɛ afɔ bo yɛ?"

“Yɛ Man Ŋshɔkpɔ bibioo ni tɛi yɔɔ nɔ lɛ nɔ, onukpa.”

"Ehi waa! Otswa je lɛ kɛtsɔ enɛ nɔ."

"Yɛ Man Ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, hɛɛ? Ojogbaŋŋ, gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ, ehi. Nuu ko ni jɛ Gbɔmɔ mli nɛ; nuu ko ni afɔ lɛ yɛ Gbɔmɔ ni be ko ni eho lɛ eye ehe nɔ, ni amrɔ nɛɛ gbɔmɔ bɛ mli; ni aŋɔ lɛ awo mli—kɛtsɔ mɛni nɔ? Kɛ tsɔne lɛ nɔ! Gbogbo ni egbo lɛ tswaa yitsei ni biɔ saji lɛ fɛɛ naagbee. No hewɔ lɛ!

Ni agbɛnɛ ni yɛ be kɛ he ni sa mli, yɛ shishijee ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfaa ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ nɛkɛ sɛɛ lɛ, Ahab, —ni ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nui krokomɛi fɛɛ efo mli lɛ —etamɔ nɔ ni etiu ehenyɛlɔ lɛ kɛtee ŋshɔkpɔ ko nɔ, koni egbe lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ shweshweeshwe fe tsutsu lɛ; agbɛnɛ, akɛ ena ehe akɛ ewa yɛ latitude kɛ longitude lɛ diɛŋtsɛ he ni akɛ epilamɔ ni yeɔ mɔ awui lɛ wo ehe lɛ; amrɔ nɛɛ ni awie lɛlɛ ko ni yɛ gbi ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ nɔŋŋ mli lɛ ekɛ Moby Dick kpe diɛŋtsɛ lɛ;—ni agbɛnɛ ni ekɛ lɛlɛi srɔtoi kpeei ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ fɛɛ kpɛlɛ nɔ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ srɔto nɔ koni etsɔɔ daimonioi ahesusumɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo yɛŋ lɛ kɛgbalaa egbɔbilɔi lɛ, kɛji efee esha loo efee esha eshi lɛ; agbɛnɛ eji akɛ nɔ ko yɛ nuumo lɛ hiŋmɛii amli, ni ewaaa akɛ susumai ni bɛ hewalɛ lɛ baakwɛ. Taakɛ polar ŋulami ni sɛɛ efooo, ni tsɔɔ wala be fɛɛ mli, arctic, nyɔji enumɔ gbɛkɛ lɛ mli lɛ, ehiɛɔ ehiŋmɛii ni tsɔɔ mli, ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ, ni yɔɔ teŋgbɛ lɛ mli lɛ; no hewɔ lɛ Ahab yiŋtoo lɛ kpɛɔ shiŋŋ agbɛnɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi ni yɔɔ shoo lɛ ni amɛfeɔ shwane teŋ be fɛɛ be lɛ nɔ. Eye amɛnɔ aahu akɛ amɛ bodings, yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔi, yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔi, gbeyeishemɔi fɛɛ, ji fain ni akɛtee amɛsusumai ashishi, ni amɛkwɛɛɛ akpɔlɔ loo baa kome po.

Yɛ nɛkɛ be ni tsɔɔ nɔ ni baaba lɛ mli hu lɛ, shwɛmɔi fɛɛ, ni akɛ nyɛɛ loo adebɔɔ naa lɛ, laaje. Stubb bɔɔɔ mɔdɛŋ ni ewo ŋmɔlɔ nɔ dɔŋŋ; Starbuck bɔɔ mɔdɛŋ dɔŋŋ ni ekwɛ eko mli. Nakai nɔŋŋ, miishɛɛ kɛ awerɛho, hiɛnɔkamɔ kɛ gbeyeishemɔ, tamɔ nɔ ni atsɔ sũ ni hi fe fɛɛ nɔ, ni etsɔ powda, yɛ be nɛɛ mli lɛ, yɛ Ahab dade susuma lɛ sũ ni akɛwo mli lɛ mli. Tamɔ tsɔnei lɛ, amɛtsiɔ amɛhe kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ dioo, ni amɛleɔ be fɛɛ be akɛ nuumo ni yeɔ nɔ lɛ hiŋmɛi miikwɛ amɛ.

Shi ani okwɛ lɛ jogbaŋŋ yɛ eteemɔŋ teemɔŋ ŋmɛlɛtswai ni fe fɛɛ lɛ amli; beni esusu akɛ hiŋmɛi ko bɛ shi moŋ eko pɛ yɔɔ enɔ lɛ; no lɛ obaana akɛ, taakɛ Ahab hiŋmɛii haa lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ ahiŋmɛii feɔ gbeyei waa lɛ po lɛ, Parsee ni anyɛɛɛ anu shishi lɛ hiŋmɛii lɛ hu haa ehiŋmɛii feɔ lɛ gbeyei; aloo yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, kɛ hoo lɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɔ lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ ena enɔ hewalɛ. Nɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni akɛfata he, ni ji naakpɛɛ nii ni shwieɔ shi lɛ bɔi Fedallah ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ mlibotemɔ bianɛ; ŋmɔlɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni ekpaaa lɛ hoso lɛ; akɛ hii lɛ kwɛ lɛ kɛ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ; fã ko ni bɛ faŋŋ, taakɛ etamɔ nɔ ni eji lɛ, kɛji eji nɔ ko ni gboɔ diɛŋtsɛ, loo eji ŋmɔlɔ ni hosoɔ ni gbɔmɔ ko ni anaaa lɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ shwie ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ. Ni nakai hulutsoo lɛ shwieɔ jɛmɛ be fɛɛ be. Ejaakɛ jeee gbɛkɛ po, ji be ni ale Fedallah akɛ ewɔɔ, loo eyaa shishi pɛŋ. Ebaadamɔ shi shiŋŋ ŋmɛlɛtswai babaoo: shi etaaa shi loo ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔɔɔ shi kɔkɔɔkɔ; ehiŋmɛii ni etse shi eyɛ naakpɛɛ lɛ kɛɛ faŋŋ akɛ—Wɔ bulɔi enyɔ lɛ wɔjɔɔɔ wɔhe kɔkɔɔkɔ.

Ni asaŋ, be fɛɛ be, gbɛkɛ loo shwane mli lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ nyɛŋ ashɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ agbɛnɛ, ja Ahab yɛ amɛhiɛ; loo edamɔ e-pivot-hole lɛ mli, loo enyiɛ tsei lɛ anɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ yɛ husui enyɔ ni sɛɛ efooo lɛ ateŋ, —main-mast lɛ kɛ mizen lɛ; aloo amɛna lɛ ni edamɔ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ mli, —enane ni hiɛ ka lɛ tee hiɛ yɛ tsɔne lɛ yiteŋ, tamɔ nɔ ni eefo; etsitsi lɛ shwie ehiŋmɛii anɔ waa; no hewɔ lɛ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni edamɔ shi ni etsiii ehe lɛ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni gbii kɛ gbɛkɛ fata he lɛ, ekɛ ehe efɔɔɔ e-hamɔ lɛ mli; shi kɛlɛ, yɛ nakai tsitsi ni shwieɔ shi lɛ shishi lɛ, amɛnyɛŋ amɛle kɔkɔɔkɔ ni tɔmɔ bɛ he kɛji, yɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, ehiŋmɛii efo diɛŋtsɛ yɛ bei komɛi amli; aloo kɛji ekɛ ejwɛŋmɔ miikwɛ amɛ lolo; ekɔɔɔ he eko, eyɛ mli akɛ edamɔ nakai yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli ŋmɛlɛtswaa muu fɛɛ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni gbɛkɛ mli fɛ̃i ni abuuu lɛ toi lɛ ebua ehe naa yɛ ŋmɔtɔ mli yɛ nakai atade kɛ tsitsi ni akɛ tɛ fee lɛ nɔ moŋ. Atadei ni gbɛkɛ lɛ efɔ lɛ, enɔ jetsɛremɔ hulu la lɛ gĩ yɛ enɔ; ni nakai eji, gbi kɛ gbi, kɛ gbɛkɛ kɛ gbɛkɛ; eyaaa tsei lɛ ashishi dɔŋŋ; nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni etaoɔ kɛjɛ tsɔne bibioo lɛ mli nakai nɔ lɛ etsu koni aya.

Eye nii yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ lɛ nɔŋŋ mli; ni ji, eniyenii enyɔ pɛ, —leebi niyenii kɛ gbɛkɛ niyenii: gbɛkɛ niyenii ni etaaa he kɔkɔɔkɔ; ni ekpaaa etsitsi hu; ni kwɛ̃ yɛ duŋ mli lɛ fɛɛ efee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, tamɔ tsei shishifãi ni ajie kɛjɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ ni atswa, ni kwɛ̃ɔ lolo yɛ shishigbɛ ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko lɛ nɔ, eyɛ mli akɛ ekpãtã yɛ ŋwɛi tsei lɛ amli moŋ. Shi eyɛ mli akɛ eshihilɛ fɛɛ ebatsɔ watsi kome yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ agbɛnɛ moŋ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ Parsee teemɔŋ watsi lɛ bɛ hejɔɔmɔ tamɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enɔ moŋ; shi kɛlɛ etamɔ nɔ ni nɛkɛ mɛi enyɔ nɛɛ ewieee kɔkɔɔkɔ—nuu kome kɛ mɔ kroko lɛ—ja yɛ be kakadaŋŋ mli lɛ sane ko ni hoɔ ni he ehiaaa lɛ ha ehe bahia. Eyɛ mli akɛ etamɔ nɔ ni shɛɛ sane ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni yɔɔ hewalɛ waa lɛ kɛ amɛyi enyɔ lɛ baafata he yɛ teemɔŋ moŋ; yɛ faŋŋ mli, ni kɛha lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi ni he efee gbeyei lɛ, amɛtamɔ tsei ni mli egbala. Kɛji akɛ gbi lɛ mli lɛ, amɛwie wiemɔ kome pɛ; gbɛkɛ mli lɛ, hii mumui ji amɛyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ, yɛ wiemɔi ni akɛtsakeɔ amɛhe fioo po lɛ he lɛ. Bei komɛi lɛ, ŋmɛlɛtswai babaoo fe fɛɛ, ni nyɔŋmɔŋtɛi kome po shwieee shi lɛ, amɛdamɔ shi shɔŋŋ yɛ ŋulamii lɛ amli; Ahab yɛ e-scuttle lɛ mli, Parsee lɛ yɛ lɛlɛ wulu lɛ he; shi amɛmiikwɛ amɛhe lolo; tamɔ nɔ ni yɛ Parsee lɛ mli lɛ Ahab na ehiɛ ni ekɛshwie shi lɛ, yɛ Ahab ni ji Parsee lɛ mli lɛ ena enibii ni eshi lɛ.

Ni kɛlɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, Ahab—yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe, taakɛ ejieɔ lɛ kpo daa gbi, ŋmɛlɛtswaa fɛɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa, kɛ be fɛɛ be, yɛ famɔ mli etsɔɔ mɛi ni yɔɔ eshishi lɛ,—Ahab tamɔ nuŋtsɔ ni yɔɔ eheyeli; Parsee lɛ shi enyɔŋ lɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni amɛyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ amɛfee ekome, ni yiwalɔ ko ni anaaa lɛ miikudɔ amɛ; shade ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni fiɔ tsitsi ni wa lɛ he lɛ. Ejaakɛ nɛkɛ Parsee nɛɛ ji nɔ fɛɛ nɔ, tsitsi kɛ tsitsi fɛɛ ji Ahab ni wa.

Yɛ leebi maŋkpa klɛŋklɛŋ la ni kpɛɔ fioo fe fɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ, anuɔ edade gbee kɛjɛɔ esɛɛ,—“Man the mast-heads!”—ni yɛ gbi muu lɛ fɛɛ mli, kɛyashi hulu ni enyɔ shi kɛ shwane sɛɛ lɛ, anuɔ gbee kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ daa ŋmɛlɛtswaa, yɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ tswamɔ mli lɛ—“What d’ye sharp?—sharp!”!

Shi beni gbii etɛ loo ejwɛ ho, beni ekɛ Rahel ni miitao gbekɛbii lɛ kpe sɛɛ lɛ; ni anako nubu ko lolo; etamɔ nɔ ni nuumo ni yɔɔ monomaniac lɛ kɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔɔ e-nitsulɔi lɛ anɔkwayeli nɔ; kɛ hoo lɛ, yɛ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ amɛ fɛɛ mli ja Wɔŋjalɔi ni tswaa harpoon lɛ pɛ; etamɔ nɔ ni eyiŋ fee lɛ kɔshikɔshi, po akɛ ekolɛ Stubb kɛ Flask jɛɛɛ amɛsuɔmɔ mli amɛku amɛhiɛ amɛshwie nɔ ni etaoɔ lɛ nɔ. Shi kɛji akɛ nɛkɛ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔi nɛɛ ji lɛ nɔ diɛŋtsɛ lɛ, ekɛ nilee tsi ehe kɛjɛ wiemɔi ni ekɛjieɔ lɛ kpo lɛ he, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni enifeemɔi lɛ tsɔɔ amɛ.

Ekɛɛ akɛ: “Mi diɛŋtsɛ mana ŋshɔŋloo lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ kwraa.” “Hɛɛ, esa akɛ Ahab aná tsɔne ni ji enyɔ lɛ!” ni ekɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eniji fee ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛwoɔ tsɛnsi mli lɛ ashĩa; ni ekɛ enine tsu kɛtee ŋwɛi, kɛ sũ kome ni akɛkpɛtɛɔ he, koni ekɛfi tsɔne lɛ yitso wulu lɛ he lɛ, enine shɛ kpãa ni akɛwo shi kɛyaa shishi lɛ naagbeehei enyɔ lɛ nɔ; ni ekɛ eko fĩɔ etsɛnsi lɛ he lɛ saa ŋmei kɛha naagbee kroko lɛ, bɔni afee ni ekɛfi oketeke lɛ he. Beni efee enɛ, ni nakai naagbee lɛ kã enine mli lolo ni edamɔ pin lɛ masɛi lɛ, ekwɛ egbɛfalɔi lɛ ahe kɛkpe, ni efolɔ kɛjɛ eko nɔ kɛtee ekroko nɔ; ni ekɛ ehiŋmɛii ŋmɛ Daggoo, Queequeg, Tashtego be kakadaŋŋ; shi etsi ehe kɛjɛ Fedallah he; ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekɛ ehiŋmɛi ni ma shi shiŋŋ ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ hefatalɔ nukpa lɛ nɔ lɛ kɛɛ,—“Ŋɔɔ kpãa lɛ, onukpa—mikɛwo onine mli, Starbuck.” Kɛkɛ ni eto egbɔmɔtso lɛ he gbɛjianɔ yɛ tsɛnsi lɛ mli, ni ekɛ wiemɔ ha amɛ koni amɛwo lɛ nɔ kɛya esɛi lɛ nɔ, ni Starbuck ji mɔ ni fi kpãa lɛ shi yɛ naagbee; ni no sɛɛ lɛ edamɔ emasɛi. Ni nakai, ni ekɛ enine kome ebɔle maŋtsɛ lɛ ŋshɔ lɛ he lɛ, Ahab kwɛ ŋshɔ lɛ sɛɛ kilomitai babaoo, —hiɛ, sɛɛgbɛ, nɛkɛ fã nɛɛ, kɛ jɛmɛ, —yɛ kooyigbɛ ni mli kwɔ ni alɛɛ mli ni afã yɛ he ko ni nɔ kwɔ waa lɛ mli.

Kɛji akɛ ekɛ eniji miitsu nii yɛ he ko ni nɔ kwɔ ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaatsɔ he ko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni ekolɛ enyɛŋ ekɛ enaji ama shi lɛ, awoɔ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ kɛyaa nakai he lɛ, ni akɛ kpãa lɛ yeɔ ebuaa lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ; yɛ shihilɛi nɛɛ amli lɛ, akɛ enaagbee ni akɛfiɔ lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ lɛ haa nuu kome ni yɔɔ enɔkwɛmɔ krɛdɛɛ lɛ be fɛɛ be. Ejaakɛ yɛ ŋa ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni tsɔnei ni akɛfoɔ foi lɛ yɔɔ mli, ni anyɛɛɛ ana amɛwekukpaai srɔtoi ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi lɛ be fɛɛ be ni tɔmɔ bɛ he kɛtsɔ nɔ ni anaa yɛ amɛhe yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ lɛ nɔ; ni kɛ́ ashɛ nɛkɛ kpãai nɛɛ ashishifãi lɛ ashwie shi yɛ minitii fioo fɛɛ minitii fioo mli kɛjɛ nɔ ni akɛfiɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ebaafee adebɔɔ mli gbele kɛkɛ, kɛji akɛ, kɛji akɛ bulɔ ko ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ bɛ lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni awo lɛ lɛ yɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ ateŋ mɛi ni bɛ hiɛdɔɔ ko mli lɛ, atsɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli ni egbee shi kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ mli. No hewɔ lɛ bɔ ni Ahab tsu nii yɛ sane nɛɛ mli lɛ jeee nɔ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto; etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ pɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ yɛ amɛhe ji, akɛ Starbuck, ni miihe ashɛ gbɔmɔ kome pɛ ni ekɛ ehe ewo mli pɛŋ ni ete shi ewo lɛ kɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni bɛŋkɛɔ yiŋkpɛɛ he yɛ gbɛ bibioo ko nɔ lɛ—mɛi lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome hu, ni etamɔ nɔ ni eyiŋ efee lɛ anɔkwayeli yɛ kwɛmɔ nɔ lɛ he yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ;—eyɛ naakpɛɛ, akɛ nuu nɛɛ diɛŋtsɛ ji e-watchman ni esa akɛ ehala eha lɛ; ni ekɛ ewala fɛɛ baaha mɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni kɛ jeee nakai lɛ, eheee eyeee lɛ dɛŋ kɛjɛ eheyeli mli.

Agbɛnɛ, klɛŋklɛŋ be ni Ahab ta ŋwɛi; dani eshɛ jɛmɛ minitii nyɔŋma; nakai ŋshɔ mli loofɔji ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni bei pii lɛ amɛfoɔ ni amɛbɛŋkɛɔ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji ni yɔɔ latitude nɛɛ amli lɛ ayitsei ni mɛi yɔɔ mli lɛ ahe lɛ ateŋ ekome; nɛkɛ okpoi nɛɛ ateŋ ekome ba ni ekɛ shwiili miibɔle eyitso he yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ oya ni anyɛɛɛ akwɛ nɔ lɛ mli. Kɛkɛ ni efo kɛtee kɔɔyɔɔ mli tɛ̃ɛ ninetalɔi akpe; no sɛɛ lɛ, ebɔle ehe kɛtee shi, ni ebɔle eyitso he ekoŋŋ.

Shi akɛni Ahab kwɛɔ ŋwɛi ni mli ewo duŋ ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ hewɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ekadiii koo mli loofɔlɔ nɛɛ; ni asaŋ, lɛɛlɛŋ, kulɛ mɔ kroko hu ekadiŋ lɛ babaoo, ejaakɛ ejeee shihilɛ ko ni baa trukaa; amrɔ nɛɛ pɛ etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ni miihe ashɛ hiŋmɛi ni boɔ sane toi fioo fe fɛɛ lɛ naa shishinumɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋaa yɛ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni enaa lɛ mli.

“Otsitsi, otsitsi, onukpa!” trukaa lɛ, Sicily ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ, ni akɛ lɛ ema mizen-mast-yitso lɛ nɔ lɛ bolɔ, ni edamɔ Ahab sɛɛ tɛ̃ɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ eyɛ shishi fioo fe egbɛhe lɛ, ni kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli kwɔ ko gbala amɛteŋ.

Shi sable fɛ̃i lɛ yɛ nuumo lɛ hiŋmɛii ahiɛ momo; ŋwɛi ŋta kakadaŋŋ ni akɛwo eyitso he lɛ: kɛ bolɔmɔ, ŋmei diŋ lɛ kɛ ejweremɔ nii lɛ jo foi kɛtee.

Okropɔŋ ko fo kɛbɔle Tarquin yitso he shii etɛ, ni ejie etsitsi nɔ tsɔne lɛ koni ekɛto najiaŋ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ Tanaquil, ni ji eŋa lɛ jaje akɛ Tarquin baafee Roma maŋtsɛ. Shi kɛtsɔ tsitsi lɛ tsakemɔ nɔ pɛ ji nɔ ni ha abu nakai okadi lɛ akɛ ehi. Akɛ Ahab tsitsi lɛ ehãaa lɛ dɔŋŋ; koo mli ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛ lɛ tee nɔ ekɛtee; etsɔ hiɛ shɔŋŋ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ: ni naagbee lɛ elaaje; yɛ be mli ni kɛjɛ he ni nakai laajemɔ lɛ elaaje lɛ, ana minitii fioo ko ni ji ŋmɔtɔ diŋŋ ko, ni miijɛ nakai ŋwɛi ŋsɔɔdo wulu lɛ mli egbee shi kɛmiiya ŋshɔ lɛ mli.
