YITSƆƆ 114. Shika tsuru lɛ.

Beni Pequod lɛ boteɔ Japan ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ he lɛ mli babaoo lɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni lɛlɛ lɛ fɛɛ bɔi ŋshɔ hiɛ wuoyeli nitsumɔ lɛ. Bei pii lɛ, yɛ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ ni mli jɔ, ni ŋɔɔ mli lɛ, ŋmɛlɛtswai nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ, nyɔŋma kɛ enumɔ, nyɔŋma kɛ kpaanyɔ, kɛ nyɔŋmai enyɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, amɛkɛ amɛhe woɔ lɛlɛi lɛ amli, ni amɛgbalaa, loo amɛfãa gbɛ, loo amɛnyiɛɔ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji lɛ asɛɛ, loo minitii nyɔŋmai ekpaa loo nyɔŋmai kpawo ni amɛmiimɛ amɛ shitee-kɛ-woo lɛ mli yɛ toiŋjɔlɛ mli; eyɛ mli akɛ amɛye omanye fioo pɛ yɛ amɛpiŋmɔi lɛ ahewɔ.

Yɛ bei ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli, yɛ hulu ni edɔ lɛ shishi; shwieɔ nu lɛ nɔ gbi muu lɛ fɛɛ yɛ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni shwieɔ shi blɛoo lɛ anɔ; eta elɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni eyɛ mlɛo tamɔ birch lɛlɛ; ni amɛkɛ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ blɛoo lɛ diɛŋtsɛ futuɔ amɛhe yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ nɔ, ni tamɔ shĩa-tɛ ŋmɔtɔi amɛtswaa gunwale lɛ; enɛɛmɛi ji lamɔ mli dioofeemɔ bei, beni mɔ ko na ŋshɔ lɛ hewolo lɛ he fɛo ni yɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ kɛ ehe kpɛmɔ lɛ, ehiɛ kpaa jata tsui ni shwieɔ eshishi lɛ nɔ; ni ejɛɛɛ etsui mli ekaiŋ, akɛ nɛkɛ velvet tsitsi nɛɛ shi ekɛ ŋmei ni shwaaa ehe lɛ teeɔ.

Bei nɛɛ ji bei, ni yɛ e-whale-lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ, rover lɛ nuɔ he blɛoo akɛ eji gbekɛ, hekɛnɔfɔɔ, shikpɔŋ nɔ henumɔ ko kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ he; akɛ ebuɔ lɛ akɛ shikpɔŋ ni fɔfɔi babaoo yɔɔ nɔ; ni lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni jieɔ e-masts lɛ ayiteŋ pɛ kpo lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni eebɔ mɔdɛŋ kɛmiiya hiɛ, jeee kɛtsɔ ŋshɔkei ni nɔ kwɔlɔ ni shwieɔ shi lɛ anɔ, shi moŋ kɛtsɔ jwɛi kakadaŋŋ agbo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔ ko ni shwieɔ shi lɛ nɔ: tamɔ be mli ni anaigbɛ mɛi ni fã kɛba jɛmɛ lɛ okpɔŋɔi lɛ jieɔ amɛtoi ni amɛma shi lɛ pɛ kpo, yɛ be mli ni amɛgbɔmɔtsei ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ lɛ tsɔɔ naakpɛɛ ŋmɔji lɛ amli babaoo lɛ.

Obalayoo fro ni ajie lɛ be kakadaŋŋ lɛ; gɔŋji lɛ ahe bluu ni mli jɔ lɛ; taakɛ enɛɛmɛi anɔ lɛ, jɛmɛ juɔ dioofeemɔ, gbee ni tswaa lɛ; eshwɛ fioo ni obaakã kitã akɛ gbekɛbii ni shwɛmɔ etɔ amɛ lɛ wɔɔ yɛ nɛkɛ hei ni amɛkomekomei yɔɔ nɛɛ, yɛ May-be ko ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ mli, beni ajieɔ koo lɛ mli fɔfɔi lɛ. Ni enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ kɛ osusuma ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ fe fɛɛ lɛ futuɔ; koni anɔkwale kɛ fancy, fã-fã kpee lɛ, abote mli, ni efee nɔ kome ni bɛ naagbee.

Ni asaŋ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni nibii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ, kɛ hoo lɛ, amɛnáaa Ahab nɔ hewalɛ yɛ be kukuoo mli. Shi kɛji akɛ etamɔ nɔ ni nɛkɛ teemɔŋ shika tsuru saji nɛɛ gbele lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eteemɔŋ shika tsuru jwetrii lɛ yɛ emli lɛ, no lɛ emumɔ ni ekɛbaa amɛnɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni fiteɔ mɔ kɛkɛ.

Oh, jwɛi ŋmɔji! oh, be fɛɛ be vernal shikpɔji ni bɛ naagbee yɛ susuma lɛ mli; yɛ nyɛ mli, —eyɛ mli akɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ wala lɛ mli hɔmɔ ni egbo lɛ eha egbi be kakadaŋŋ moŋ, —yɛ nyɛmli lɛ, gbɔmɛi baanyɛ atsɔ amɛhe lolo, tamɔ okpɔŋɔi bibii ni yɔɔ leebi klover hee mli; ni yɛ be kukuoo mli fioo komɛi amli lɛ, amɛnu wala ni gbooo lɛ mli ŋmɔtɔ ni mli ejɔ lɛ he yɛ amɛnɔ. Ani Nyɔŋmɔ baaha nɛkɛ jɔɔmɔi ni yɔɔ kpoo nɛɛ ahi shi daa. Shi wala mli kpãai ni efutu, ni efutu lɛ, tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli kɛ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ tsɔ mli: hei ni yɔɔ kpoo ni ahum foɔ mli, ahum kɛha kpoofeemɔ fɛɛ kpoofeemɔ. Nɔyaa ko bɛ ni yaa nɔ daa ni sɛɛ efooo yɛ shihilɛ nɛɛ mli; wɔyaaa hiɛ kɛtsɔɔ ŋwɛi gbɛhei ni ato he gbɛjianɔ lɛ anɔ, ni yɛ naagbee nɔ lɛ wɔkpaa:—kɛtsɔ gbekɛbiiashi shɛɛ sane ni bɛ jwɛŋmɔ nɔ, gbekɛbiiashi hemɔkɛyeli ni asusuuu he, oblahiiaŋ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ (ni ji gbele ni afɔɔ namɔ), no sɛɛ lɛ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ, no sɛɛ lɛ hemɔkɛyeli ni bɛ, hejɔɔmɔ yɛ naagbee yɛ nuufeemɔ hejɔɔmɔ ni ji If lɛ mli. Shi kɛ wɔtsɔ mli pɛ lɛ, wɔnyiɛɔ kpokpaa lɛ nɔ ekoŋŋ; ni amɛji abifabii, gbekɛbii hii, kɛ hii, kɛ Ifs kɛya naanɔ. Nɛgbɛ ji naagbee ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ lɛjiadaamɔhe lɛ, ni wɔjieee ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ dɔŋŋ? Mɛɛ rapt ether tsɔɔ je lɛ mli, ni mɔ ni etɔ lɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ nijiaŋ ejeee wui kɔkɔɔkɔ? Nɛgbɛ ji he ni gbekɛ ni ana lɛ tsɛ yɔɔ? Wɔsusumai tamɔ nakai gbekɛbii ni bɛ fɔlɔi ni amɛnyɛmɛi ni eboteee gbalashihilɛ mli lɛ gboi yɛ amɛfɔmɔ mli lɛ: wɔtsɛyeli he teemɔŋ sane lɛ yɛ amɛgbonyobu lɛ mli, ni esa akɛ wɔya jɛmɛ koni wɔkase.

Ni nakai gbi lɛ nɔŋŋ lɛ hu, beni ekwɛɔ shikpɔŋ shɔŋŋ kɛjɛɔ elɛlɛ lɛ masɛi kɛyaa nakai shika tsuru ŋshɔ lɛ nɔŋŋ mli lɛ, Starbuck kɛ heshibaa wie huhuuhu akɛ:—

“Suɔmɔ ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ shishi, taakɛ suɔlɔ ena yɛ eyemforo oblayoo lɛ hiŋmɛi mli pɛŋ lɛ!—Kaakɛɛ mi o-shark ni yɔɔ ŋmeii, kɛ gbɔmɔyeli gbɛi ni okɛyaa mɛi ahiɛ lɛ ahe sane. Ha hemɔkɛyeli ajie anɔkwale;

Ni Stubb, ni tamɔ loo, ni ehiɛ ŋmeii ni kpɛɔ lɛ, tu kɛtee ŋwɛi yɛ nakai shika tsuru kane lɛ nɔŋŋ mli:—

“Miji Stubb, ni Stubb yɛ eyinɔsane; shi yɛ biɛ lɛ Stubb kãa kitã akɛ eyɛ miishɛɛ be fɛɛ be!”





YITSƆƆ 115. Pequod lɛ Kɛ Owu lɛ Kpee.

Ni nɔ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli ji nibii ni anaa kɛ gbɛɛmɔi ni baa shi yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ hiɛ, aaafee otsii fioo komɛi asɛɛ beni akɛ Ahab tsɔne lɛ tswa lɛ.

Nantucket lɛlɛ ko, ni ji Bachelor lɛ ji lɛ, ni ekɛ ehe wo enaagbee mu tsɛnsi lɛ mli pɛŋ, ni ekɛ bolt etswa e-hatches ni miitswa lɛ; ni agbɛnɛ, yɛ miishɛɛ gbijurɔyeli atadei amli lɛ, ekɛ miishɛɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ eji yakayaka nii-anunyam yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, ni enyiɛ lɛlɛi ni mli egbala babaoo ni yɔɔ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ lɛ ateŋ, dani etsɔɔ ehiɛgbɛ kɛyaa shia.

Hii etɛ ni yɔɔ e-mast-yitso lɛ wo tsɔji kakadaji ni akɛ tsɔne tsuru ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ fee lɛ yɛ amɛ tsitsii lɛ ahe; kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ ko fĩ shi, kɛjɛ shishi kɛba shi; ni ana naagbee ŋshɔŋloo ni amɛgbe lɛ shishigbɛ tsitsi kakadaŋŋ ni akɛkpɛtɛ bowsprit lɛ he lɛ. Okadii, aflaŋaai, kɛ jaki ni yɔɔ sui srɔtoi fɛɛ miiho kɛmiijɛ etsɔne lɛ mli, yɛ he fɛɛ he. Akɛ sperm barrels enyɔ ewo e-basket tops etɛ lɛ eko fɛɛ eko mli yɛ afã; yɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ, yɛ e-top-mast cross-trees lɛ amli lɛ, ona nu ni jara wa nɛɛ nɔŋŋ mli fãi bibii ni fãa; ni akɛ akɔɔble kane ko fĩ etsɔne wulu lɛ he.

Taakɛ ale yɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ, Bachelor lɛ kɛ omanyeyeli ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ fe fɛɛ ekpe; nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ fe fɛɛ ji akɛ, beni lɛlɛi krokomɛi babaoo nyiɛɔ yɛ ŋshɔi nɛɛ nɔŋŋ amli lɛ, amɛtee nyɔji muu fɛɛ ni amɛnako loo kome po. Jeee akɛ akɛ tsina loo kɛ blodo tsɛnsii eha koni akɛha gbɛ aha nuu fɔmɔ nii ni jara wa kwraa lɛ kɛkɛ, shi moŋ akɛ tsɛnsii krokomɛi ni fata he lɛ etsake, kɛjɛ lɛlɛi ni ekɛkpe lɛ amli; ni akɛ enɛɛmɛi to lɛlɛ lɛ he, kɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ kɛ asraafoi akuu lɛ mli. Atswa tsɔne lɛ mli okpɔlɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ po ni efee tso ni akɛshãa la; ni tsɔne lɛ mli muji lɛ ye niyenii kɛjɛ mu-butt ko yitso ni mli kwɔ lɛ nɔ, ni akɛshwie shikpɔŋ koni akɛfee nɔ ko ni yɔɔ teŋgbɛ. Yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ efolɔ amɛtsitsii lɛ amli ni amɛwo amɛmli diɛŋtsɛ, ni amɛyi mli obɔ; akɛ ŋmɔlɔ fata he, akɛ niyelɔ lɛ etswa eyitso yɛ e-boiler ni da fe fɛɛ lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛwo mli; akɛ shĩa nɔkwɛlɔ lɛ efo e-coffee-pot ni eshwɛ lɛ mli ni ewo mli; akɛ harpooneers lɛ efo amɛ dadei lɛ ashishigbɛi lɛ ayitsei ni amɛyi mli obɔ; akɛ lɛɛlɛŋ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ eyi kɛ fɔmɔ nii, ja ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ pantaloons kotokui lɛ, kɛ nɔ ni ekɛto koni ekɛ eniji awo mli lɛ, yɛ ehe-kɛ-afɔleshaa odaseyeli ni tsɔɔ bɔ ni etsui nyɔɔ emli fɛɛ lɛ mli.

Beni nɛkɛ miishɛɛ lɛlɛ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli nɛɛ shwieɔ Pequod ni mli efu lɛ nɔ lɛ, barbarian gbɛɛmɔ ni ji tsɔnei wuji ni akɛtswaa tsɔne lɛ jɛ etsɔne lɛ mli; ni beni ebɛŋkɛɔ lolo lɛ, ana ehii akuu ko ni damɔ e-try-pots wuji lɛ ahe, ni loo diŋ lɛ poke loo musu hewolo ni tamɔ wolokpo lɛ ehà nɔ lɛ, ni ehaa gbee ni naa wa waa baa yɛ lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ aniji ni amɛmɔmɔ lɛ fɛɛ ni amɛtswaa lɛ mli. Yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fã ni ji enyɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, hefatalɔi lɛ kɛ harpooneers lɛ kɛ gbekɛbii yei ni hiɛ oliv su ni kɛ amɛ jo foi kɛjɛ Polynesia Ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ lɛ miijo; beni akɛ amɛ fĩ lɛlɛ ko ni akɛwula lɛ mli, ni akɛwo ŋwɛi shiŋŋ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ kɛ lɛlɛ wulu lɛ teŋ lɛ, Long Island negrobii etɛ, ni hiɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe fiddle-bows ni kpɛɔ lɛ miikwɛ jig ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ nɔ. Yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ, mɛi krokomɛi ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ miifee basabasa yɛ tɛi ni akɛkaa nibii akwɛɔ lɛ ahe nitsumɔ mli, he ni ajie tsɛnsii wuji lɛ kɛjɛ mli lɛ. Kulɛ eshwɛ fioo ni obaasusu akɛ amɛmiigbala Bastille ni alomɔ lɛ, gbee ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni amɛwo lɛ, beni ashɛrɛɔ brikii kɛ sũ ni he ehiaaa amrɔ nɛɛ ashwieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ.

Nuŋtsɔ kɛ nuŋtsɔ yɛ shihilɛ nɛɛ fɛɛ nɔ lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ damɔ shi shiŋŋ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ ŋwɛitsu ni nɔ kwɔ lɛ nɔ, bɔni afee ni miishɛɛ drama lɛ fɛɛ eyi ehiɛ obɔ, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ kɛkɛ kɛha lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eŋkroaŋkroi agbɛjianɔtoo.

Ni Ahab, lɛ hu edamɔ e-quarter deck lɛ nɔ, ni eyɛ tsitsii ni eyɛ ediŋŋ, ni ehiɛ edɔ waa; ni beni lɛlɛi enyɔ lɛ foɔ amɛhe ŋmɛlɛtswai lɛ amli lɛ—kome ji miishɛɛ fɛɛ ni kɔɔ nibii ahe lɛ ho, ekroko lɛ ji nibii ni baaba lɛ ahe tsɔɔmɔi fɛɛ—amɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi enyɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ fee amɛhe mfoniri yɛ srɔtofeemɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni yɔɔ shihilɛ lɛ mli lɛ fɛɛ he.

“Ba lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ba lɛlɛ lɛ mli!” Bachelor ni ji hii kɛ hii ni kɛ amɛhe feɔ yakayaka nii lɛ anɔkwɛlɔ lɛ bolɔ, ni ewo glase kɛ tɔ ko nɔ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli.

“Ani ona Whale Eyɛŋ lɛ?” Ahab kɛ ŋmɔlɔ ha hetoo.

Mɔ kroko lɛ kɛ miishɛɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Daabi, minu ehe kɛkɛ, shi nyɛkahea lɛ nyɛyea kwraa.” “Ba lɛlɛ lɛ mli!”

"Oyɛ miishɛɛ waa. Yaa nɔ. Ani hii komɛi elaaje?"

"Eshɛɛɛ ni awieɔ he—ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ bii enyɔ, no pɛ;—shi nyɛbaa lɛlɛ lɛ mli, tsui onukpa, nyɛbaa. Etsɛŋ ni majie nakai diŋ lɛ kɛjɛ nyɛhiɛ. Nyɛbaa, nyɛbaa (shwɛmɔ lɛ miishɛɛ); lɛlɛ ni eyi obɔ kɛ shia-miiya."

“Kwɛ bɔ ni kwashiai le nii jogbaŋŋ ha!” Ahab wie ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ; no sɛɛ lɛ, okɛ gbee ni wa kɛɛ akɛ, "Oji lɛlɛ ni eyi obɔ ni ooya shia, no hewɔ lɛ, tsɛmɔ mi lɛlɛ ni nɔ ko bɛ mli, ni miiya kponɔgbɛ. No hewɔ lɛ yaa ogbɛi anɔ, ni mi hu mafee minɔ̃. Yaa hiɛ yɛ jɛmɛ! Okɛ lɛlɛ fɛɛ aya, ni okɛ lɛ aya kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ he!"

Ni nakai ji bɔ ni eji, beni lɛlɛ kome lɛ kɛ miishɛɛ yaa kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ hiɛ lɛ, ekroko lɛ kɛ yitsoŋwalɛ wuɔ eshiɔ lɛ; ni nakai ji bɔ ni tsɛnsii enyɔ lɛ mli gbala; Pequod lɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ kɛ hiŋmɛii ni mli wa, ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ miikwɛ Bachelor ni miiku esɛɛ lɛ; shi Bachelor hii lɛ booo amɛhiŋmɛii lɛ atoi kɔkɔɔkɔ yɛ gbijurɔyeli ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni amɛyɔɔ mli lɛ hewɔ. Ni beni Ahab, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ taffrail lɛ nɔ, ni ekwɛɔ lɛlɛ ni yaa shia lɛ, ejie ŋã bibioo ko kɛjɛ ekotoku lɛ mli, ni no sɛɛ lɛ, ekwɛ ŋã tsinsii lɛ mli kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli kɛtee tsinsii lɛ mli, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ fickete kroko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ miiba, ni kɛ Nantuual miibɔ naanyo.





YITSƆƆ 116. Ŋshɔloo ni Gboɔ lɛ.

Jeee akɛ efɔɔɔ kaa yɛ shihilɛ nɛɛ mli, beni, yɛ ninejurɔgbɛ lɛ, shweremɔ nibii ni esumɔɔ waa lɛ fãa gbɛ kɛbɛŋkɛɔ wɔ lɛ, wɔ, eyɛ mli akɛ wɔ fɛɛ wɔfoɔ wɔhiɛ moŋ, shi wɔmɔɔ kɔɔyɔɔ ni tswaa oya lɛ eko, ni wɔkɛ miishɛɛ nuɔ he akɛ wɔbaagi mli lɛlɛi lɛ eyi obɔ. Nakai eji yɛ Pequod lɛ he. Ejaakɛ enɔ jetsɛremɔ lɛ, beni ekɛ Bachelor ni ji nuu kɛ nuu loo yoo kɛ yoo ni feɔ yakayaka nii lɛ kpe sɛɛ lɛ, ana ŋshɔŋlooi ni agbe ejwɛ; ni amɛteŋ ekome ji nɔ ni Ahab ŋma.

No mli lɛ, shwane mli eshɛ shɔŋŋ; ni beni agbe ta ni tsuɔ tamɔ ŋmɔtɔ lɛ mli akpɔlɔi lɛ fɛɛ naa lɛ: ni amɛmiiho yɛ ŋshɔ kɛ ŋwɛi ni hulu enyɔ shi fɛfɛo lɛ mli lɛ, hulu kɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ fɛɛ gboi dioo; no sɛɛ lɛ, ŋmɔlɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ, orisons ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni ebɔle ehe yɛ nakai kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli tse lɛ mli, ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni kɛjɛ Manilla ŋshɔkpɔi lɛ anɔ jɔɔi ni mli kwɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔji amli lɛ ahe shɔŋŋ lɛ, Spain shikpɔŋ nɔ kɔɔyɔɔ, ni etsɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ lɛ, kɛ nɛkɛ vesperm, freight nɛɛ etee ŋshɔ hiɛ.

Akɛni etsui enyɔ emli ekoŋŋ, shi etsui enyɔ emli kɛtee duŋ ni mli kwɔ mli pɛ hewɔ lɛ, Ahab ni eshi ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli lɛ ta shi ni ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ miikwɛ enaagbee sɛɛkuu kɛjɛ lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ bianɛ lɛ mli. Ejaakɛ nakai naakpɛɛ nii ni anaa yɛ sperm whales fɛɛ ni gboiɔ lɛ amli—yitso ni tsɔɔ hulu lɛ mli, ni egboɔ nakai lɛ—nakai naakpɛɛ nii ni anaa yɛ gbɛkɛ ni yɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, ejie naakpɛɛ nii ko ni aleee dã kpo etsɔɔ Ahab.

“Etsɔ ehe ni etsɔ ehe kɛtee jɛmɛ, —kwɛ bɔ ni efeɔ blɛoo, shi bɔ ni etswaa shi shiŋŋ, ehiɛ ni ekɛwoɔ bulɛ kɛ tsɛmɔ, kɛ enaagbee gbele nifeemɔi. Lɛ hu ejáa la; anɔkwafo fe fɛɛ, ni mli kwɔ, hulu lɛ nyɔŋ ni ji baronial vassal!—Oh akɛ nɛkɛ hiŋmɛii ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ fe fɛɛ nɛɛ baakwɛ nɛkɛ too-favook nɛɛ; hum fɛɛ ni ji adesa weal loo hao; yɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ faŋŋ fe fɛɛ ni tsɔɔɔ hiɛaŋkwɛmɔ lɛ mli lɛ; ebɔle gbonyo lɛ he, ni ekɛ eyitso tsɔɔ gbɛ kroko nɔ.

“Oh, bo Hindoo adebɔɔ mli fã ni yɔɔ duŋ, ni okɛ wui ni egboi ema omaŋtsɛ sɛi ni yɔɔ srɔto yɛ he ko yɛ ŋshɔi ni shwieee shi nɛɛ ateŋ; oheee yeee, bo maŋnyɛ, ni okɛ mi wieɔ anɔkwale tsɔ yɛ hulutsoo be ni gbeɔ mɛi babaoo lɛ mli yɛ Norhal ni egbo lɛ sɛɛ. etsɔ eyitso ni miigbo lɛ nɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ebɔle ehe ekoŋŋ, ni ekɛ nikasemɔ ko haaa mi.

“Oo, hewalɛ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ni akɛwo mli ni akɛwo mli! Oh, ŋwɛi ŋta ni nɔ kwɔ!—nakai mɔ lɛ bɔɔ mɔdɛŋ, mɔ nɛɛ tswaa fɛɛ efolo! Efolo, oh ŋshɔŋloo, ani otaoɔ kpatamɔi kɛ hulu ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ, ni tsɛɔ mi wala pɛ, shi ekɛ tɛ ko haaa mi, shi ehaaaa mi ekoŋŋ, kɛji akɛ, okɛ lɛ haaa bo ekoŋŋ. hemɔkɛyeli ni mli ewo duŋ fe tsutsu lɛ, nibii ni akɛfutuɔ nii ni anyɛɛɛ atsɛ amɛ gbɛi lɛ fɛɛ miiho yɛ mishishi yɛ biɛ, ni be ko ni eho lɛ, nibii ni yɔɔ wala mli lɛ amumɔi tsirɛɔ mi, shi nu amrɔ nɛɛ;

“No hewɔ lɛ nyɔŋmɔŋtɛi, nyɔŋmɔŋtɛi kɛya naanɔ, bo ŋshɔ, ni ŋa mli loofɔji pɛ naa hejɔɔmɔ yɛ enaanɔ shwiemɔi amli: Shikpɔŋ nɔ afɔ lɛ, shi ŋshɔ lɛ haa nu nu, eyɛ mli akɛ gɔŋ kɛ jɔɔ lɛ fɔ mi moŋ, shi nyɛ ŋshɔkei lɛ ji minyɛmimɛi ni kwɛɔ mi!”





YITSƆƆ 117. Whale Buu lɛ.

Ŋshɔŋlooi ejwɛ ni agbe amɛ nakai gbɛkɛ lɛ egboi kwraa; ekome, ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ kɛyaa kɔɔyɔɔ he; eko, ni kɛ ehe jɛkɛɛɛ tsɔ, kɛyaa kɔɔyɔɔ mli; mɔ kome hiɛ; eko yɛ sɛɛgbɛ. Akɛ naagbee etɛ nɛɛ ba masɛi dani gbɛkɛ shɛ; shi anyɛɛɛ ashɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ he lɛ he kɛyashi leebi; ni lɛlɛ ni gbe lɛ lɛ kã emasɛi gbɛkɛ muu lɛ fɛɛ; ni nakai lɛlɛ lɛ ji Ahab nɔ̃.

Akɛ waif-pole lɛ shwieɔ ŋshɔŋloo ni egbo lɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli tɛ̃ɛ; ni kane ni fĩɔ eyiteŋ lɛ, kɛ la ni tsoɔ ni haoɔ mɔ lɛ shwieɔ sɛɛ diŋ ni kpɛɔ lɛ nɔ, ni eyaa shɔŋŋ yɛ ŋshɔkei ni hoɔ yɛ gbɛkɛ teŋgbɛ lɛ anɔ, ni shwieɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ fã ni mli kwɔ lɛ blɛoo, tamɔ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ blɛoo ni hoɔ yɛ ŋshɔnaa lɛ nɔ.

Etamɔ nɔ ni Ahab kɛ elɛlɛ lɛ mli bii lɛ fɛɛ ewɔ shi Parseebii lɛ; ni amɛkoto ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni amɛtara shi ni amɛmiikwɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ, ni shwɛɔ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ amɛbɔleɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he lɛ, ni amɛkɛ amɛtsitsii tswaa tsei tsei ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ. Gbee ko tamɔ ŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ asphaltites ni ji Gomora mumɔi ni akɛkeee amɛ lɛ anɔ lɛ, jo foi kɛhoso kɛtsɔ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli.

Kɛjɛ ewɔ mli lɛ, Ahab, hiɛ kɛ hiɛ, na Parseenyo lɛ; ni gbɛkɛ mli duŋ lɛ ebɔle amɛhe lɛ tamɔ nɔ ni amɛji naagbee gbɔmɛi yɛ jeŋ ni nu afua eyi mli obɔ lɛ mli. Ekɛɛ akɛ: “Mila lɛ ekoŋŋ.”

“Yɛ gbonyo tsɔnei lɛ ahe? Ani mikɛɛko, onukpa, akɛ gbonyo tsɔne loo gbonyo adeka nyɛŋ afee onɔ̃?”

“Ni namɛi anuɔ ni amɛgboiɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ?”

“Shi mikɛɛ, nuumo, akɛ dani obaagbo yɛ gbɛfaa nɛɛ mli lɛ, esa akɛ ona gbonyo tsɔnei enyɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ diɛŋtsɛ; klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ lɛ jeee gbɔmɔ niji ni gboiɔ fee; ni esa akɛ akwɛ naagbee nɔ lɛ tso ni anaa lɛ yɛ Amerika.”

"Aye, aye! nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni, Parsee:—gbonyo tsɔne kɛ etsitsii ni hoɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ kɛ ŋshɔkei kɛha mɛi ni hiɛɔ gbonyo lɛ. Ha! Etsɛɛɛ ni wɔnaŋ nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ."

“Ohe oye loo ohemɔ oyeee, onyɛŋ ogbo kɛyashi abaana, onukpa.”

“Ni mɛni ji nakai wiemɔ ni kɔɔ ohe lɛ he?”

“Eyɛ mli akɛ ebaashɛ naagbee lɛ he moŋ, shi maya otsɔne kudɔlɔ lɛ hiɛ lolo.”

"Ni beni otsɔ hiɛ nakai—kɛji nakai ba lɛ—belɛ dani manyɛ manyiɛ osɛɛ lɛ, esa akɛ ojie ohe kpo otsɔɔ mi lolo, ni okɛ mi akudɔ lɛ lolo?—Ani jeee nakai? Ojogbaŋŋ, no lɛ, ani mihe nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni nyɛkɛɔ lɛ miye, oh mi kɔɔyɔŋ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ! Miyɛ shiwooi enyɔ yɛ biɛ akɛ magbe Moby Dick lolo ni maye wala mli."

“Ŋɔɔ shiwoo kroko, nuumo,” Parsee lɛ kɛɛ, beni ehiŋmɛii kpɛɔ tamɔ la-ŋmei yɛ duŋ lɛ mli lɛ—“Hemp pɛ baanyɛ agbe bo.”

“Tso lɛ, nyɛtsɔɔ.—No hewɔ lɛ, migbooo, yɛ shikpɔŋ kɛ ŋshɔ nɔ,” Ahab bolɔ, kɛ ŋmɔlɔ ni akɛyeɔ ehe fɛo;—“Migbooo yɛ shikpɔŋ kɛ ŋshɔ nɔ!”

Mɛi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ fee dioo ekoŋŋ, tamɔ gbɔmɔ kome. Leebi hulutsoo be lɛ ba, ni lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi ni ewɔ lɛ te shi kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ shishi, ni dani shwane baashɛ lɛ, akɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni egbo lɛ ba lɛlɛ lɛ mli.





YITSƆƆ 118. Kwadrant lɛ.

Be ni akɛbaatsu Line lɛ he nii lɛ bɛŋkɛ; ni daa gbi kɛ́ Ahab, ni jɛ etsu lɛ mli eba lɛ, ekɛ ehiŋmɛii shwie ŋwɛi lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni hiɛ kã shi lɛ kɛ e-spoke lɛ tsuɔ nii kɛjieɔ ehe kpo, ni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ lɛ joɔ foi oya kɛyaa tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ he, ni amɛdamɔ jɛmɛ ni amɛhiŋmɛii fɛɛ kwɛɔ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ nɔ; ebɛ tsuishitoo kɛha famɔ ni akɛha lɛ akɛ etsɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ kɛya equator lɛ he. Yɛ be kpakpa mli lɛ, famɔ lɛ ba. Ewa yɛ shwane mli; ni Ahab ni eta elɛlɛ ni nɔ kwɔ lɛ hiɛ lɛ miisumɔ ni ekɛ hulu lɛ kwɛmɔ ni ekɛtsuɔ nii daa gbi lɛ atsu nii koni ekɛna he ni eyɔɔ lɛ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, yɛ nakai Japan ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ, gbii ni yɔɔ hulutsoo be mli lɛ tamɔ la ni tsoɔ waa. Etamɔ nɔ ni nakai Japan hulu ni mli tse ni ŋmɛɛɛ lɛ ji nɔ ni ŋshɔ ni tamɔ glase ni tsoɔ ni anyɛɛɛ asusu he lɛ kwɛɔ nɔ. Ŋwɛi lɛ tamɔ nɔ ni akɛ lac; atatui bɛ; ŋwɛi ŋwɛi lɛ hoɔ; ni nɛkɛ la ni kpɛɔ ni anyɛɛɛ ajie lɛ kpo nɛɛ tamɔ Nyɔŋmɔ maŋtsɛ sɛi lɛ nyamɔ ​​ni anyɛɛɛ aŋmɛ gbɛ lɛ. Ojogbaŋŋ, akɛ glasei ni yɔɔ sui sɔrɔtoi wo Ahab ŋwɛitsu lɛ mli, ni abaanyɛ atsɔ nɔ ana nakai hulu la lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, beni ekɛ ehe ni eta shi lɛ shwieɔ shi kɛyaa lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni ekɛ edɛŋdade ni tamɔ ŋulamiiaŋkwɛlɔ lɛ fɔ̃ɔ ehiŋmɛi nɔ lɛ, ehi nakai shihilɛ lɛ mli be fioo komɛi koni emɔ be pɔtɛɛ ni esa akɛ hulu lɛ aná e-meridian pɔtɛɛ lɛ. Yɛ nakai beaŋ beni ejwɛŋmɔ fɛɛ kã nɔ lɛ, Parseenyo lɛ ekoto eshishi yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ shishi, ni ekɛ ehiɛ efɔ ŋwɛi tamɔ Ahab nɔ lɛ, ni ekɛ lɛ miikwɛ hulu kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ; ehiŋmɛii lɛ ashishifãi pɛ ji nɔ ni ha amɛ ŋmeii lɛ fã, ni ehiɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ eba shikpɔŋ nɔ henumɔ ni bɛ mli lɛ shishi. Yɛ naagbee lɛ, ajie nɔ ni ataoɔ lɛ; ni etsɛɛɛ ni Ahab kɛ e-pensil lɛ fɔ enaji ni akɛ shwuɔ wu fee lɛ nɔ, ni ebu bɔ ni esa akɛ e-latitude lɛ afee yɛ nakai be lɛ mli pɛpɛɛpɛ lɛ he akɔntaa. Kɛkɛ ni beni ebote be kukuoo ko mli lɛ, ekwɛ hulu lɛ nɔ ekoŋŋ ni ewie huhuuhu etsɔɔ ehe akɛ: “Bo ŋshɔ hiɛ okadi! bo kɔɔyɔŋ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ni nɔ kwɔ ni he wa! okɛɛɔ mi he ni miyɔɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ —shi ani obaanyɛ okɛ nɔ ko bibioo ko atsɔɔ he ni mahi ? Aloo obaanyɛ okɛɛ he ni nɔ kroko ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ nɛɛ yɔɔ? esa akɛ ekwɛ lɛ.Mihiŋmɛii nɛɛ kwɛɔ hiŋmɛi ni miikwɛ lɛ amrɔ nɛɛ po lɛ mli pɛpɛɛpɛ, kɛ hiŋmɛi ni miikwɛ nibii ni yɔɔ he ni aleee lɛ, bo hulu lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ lɛ mli!”

Kɛkɛ ni ekwɛ e-quadrant lɛ, ni ekɛ e-cabalistical convances babaoo lɛ miitsu nii, ekome sɛɛ, ni esusu he ekoŋŋ, ni ewie huhuuhu akɛ: “Kwashiai ashwɛmɔ! gbekɛbii ashwɛmɔ nibii ni Admirals, kɛ Commodores, kɛ Captains ni woɔ amɛhe nɔ lɛ feɔ; je lɛ kɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ onɔ, yɛ oŋaawoo kɛ hewalɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ nɔ; shi mɛni ji nɔ ni poor baanyɛ afee, shi nɔ fɛɛ nɔ sɛɛ, shi mɔbɔnalɛ tsɔɔ, shi nɔ fɛɛ nɔ sɛɛ lɛ, mɔbɔnalɛ baanyɛ afee; bo diɛŋtsɛ oyɛ shibɔlemɔ ŋulami ni mli kwɔ nɛɛ nɔ, ni nine ni hiɛ bo lɛ: daabi! Onyɛɛɛ okɛɛ he ni nu ŋmɔtɔ kome loo ŋã kome pɛ baahi wɔ shwane; shihilɛ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ shi eshãa lɛ, taakɛ nɛkɛ hiŋmɛii momo nɛɛ eshã lɛ po kɛ o-la lɛ, O hulu! Gbɔmɔ hiŋmɛii ni kwɛɔ shikpɔŋ nɛɛ nɔ lɛ eshɛɛɛ eyitso nɔ, tamɔ nɔ ni Nyɔŋmɔ tsɔɔ akɛ ekwɛ e-quarant lɛ nɔ, Loomɔ!” ni ekɛmiiya lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ lɛ, "mikɛ bo baakudɔ mishikpɔŋ nɔ gbɛ lɛ dɔŋŋ; lɛlɛ lɛ kɔmpas ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi, kɛ gbonyo he akɔntaabuu ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi, kɛtsɔ tso kɛ kpã nɔ; enɛɛmɛi baatsɔɔ mi gbɛ, ni amɛbaatsɔɔ mi he ni miyɔɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ. gbala mli ni ekpãtã ohiɛ!”

Beni nuumo ni mli efu waa lɛ wieɔ nakai ni ekɛ enaji ni hiɛ kã kɛ gbohii lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ lɛ, kunimyeli ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ ni tamɔ nɔ ni aha Ahab, kɛ hiɛnɔkamɔ ni gbeɔ mɔ ni tamɔ nɔ ni aha lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe—enɛɛmɛi ho Parsee ni ewieee ni etsiii ehe lɛ hiɛ nɔ. Mɔ ko mɔ ko enaaa lɛ ni ete shi ni efo kɛtee; yɛ be mli ni amɛ tatsɛ lɛ fã lɛ he fee amɛ gbeyei lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi lɛ bua amɛhe naa yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ, kɛyashi Ahab, ni kɛ haomɔ miinyiɛ ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ lɛ, bolɔ akɛ—“Kɛya ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ hiɛ!

Yɛ be kukuoo mli lɛ, yadii lɛ tsɔ amɛhe; ni beni lɛlɛ lɛ fã fã tsɔɔ enajiaŋ lɛ, etsɔnei etɛ ni yɔɔ fɛo ni eta shi shiŋŋ ni ema shi shiŋŋ yɛ etsitsi kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ ŋmeii lɛ anɔ lɛ tamɔ nɔ ni Horatii etɛ lɛ miiho yɛ okpɔŋɔ kome ni fa bɔ ni sa nɔ.

Beni Starbuck damɔ knight-yitsei lɛ ateŋ lɛ, ekwɛɔ Pequod lɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ basabasa lɛ, kɛ Ahab hu, beni eyaa ni ekɛ ehe shwieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ.

“Mita la ni tsoɔ waa lɛ hiɛ ni mikwɛ bɔ ni etsoɔ, ni eyi obɔ kɛ ewala ni tsoɔ waa lɛ; ni mina akɛ egbɔjɔ naagbee, shi, shi, kɛbatsɔ sũ ni mli ewo muji fe fɛɛ. Ŋshɔi anɔ onukpa! yɛ owala ni tamɔ la nɛɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, mɛni baashwɛ yɛ sɛɛ mli akɛ sũ bibioo ko pɛ!”

Stubb bolɔ akɛ: “Hɛɛ, shi ŋshɔ mli sũ—kaimɔ nakai, Mr. Starbuck—ŋshɔ mli sũ, jeee osũ ni okɛtsuɔ nii daa lɛ. Ojogbaŋŋ, ojogbaŋŋ; minu Ahab ni miiwie ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ akɛ, ‘Biɛ mɔ ko kɛ kaadii nɛɛ woɔ miniji momo nɛɛ amli; Ni fɔ mi, Ahab, shi ofeɔ onii yɛ gbɛ ni ja nɔ, ni ohiɔ shi yɛ shwɛmɔ lɛ mli, ni ogboɔ yɛ mli!”





YITSƆƆ 119. Kanei lɛ.

Kɔɔyɔɔ ni mli edɔ fe fɛɛ shi ekwɛɔ ŋmeii ni yitsoŋ wa fe fɛɛ lɛ anɔ: Bengal jata lɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ tsei ni akɛ tsofa-kɛ-ŋma ewo mli ni ŋmɔji ni bɛ naagbee lɛ mli. Ŋwɛi ni kpɛɔ fe fɛɛ shi ŋwɛi ŋmɔlɔ ni gbeɔ mɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ yɔɔ mli: Cuba fɛfɛo lɛ le ahum ni tswaaa kooyigbɛ shikpɔji ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ. Nakai eji hu akɛ yɛ Japan ŋshɔi ni kpɛɔ nɛɛ amli lɛ, ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ kɛ ahum ni naa wa fe fɛɛ, ni ji Ahum ni naa wa lɛ kpeɔ. Bei komɛi lɛ, ebaatswa kɛjɛ nakai ŋwɛi ni atatu bɛ mli lɛ mli, tamɔ okpɛlɛmii ni tswaa yɛ maŋ ko ni egbɔjɔ ni ewɔ lɛ nɔ.
