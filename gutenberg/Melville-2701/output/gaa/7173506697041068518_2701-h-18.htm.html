YITSƆƆ 89. Loo-Oya kɛ Loo-Loo.

Nɔ ni awie yɛ waif kɛ waif-poles he yɛ naagbee yitso lɛ mli shi ekome pɛ, biɔ ni awie mlai kɛ gbɛjianɔtoi ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhe lɛ he, ni abaanyɛ abu waif lɛ akɛ eji okadi wulu kɛ baaji.

Bei pii lɛ, ebaa lɛ akɛ, kɛ́ lɛlɛi babaoo miifã gbɛ yɛ kuu mli lɛ, lɛlɛ kome baanyɛ atswa ŋshɔŋloo ko, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ejo foi, ni yɛ naagbee lɛ, lɛlɛ kroko baagbe lɛ ni emɔ lɛ; ni nibii bibii babaoo ni baa trukaa lɛ fata he yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛyeɔ nɛkɛ su wulu kome nɛɛ mli gbɛfaŋnɔ. Akɛ nɔkwɛmɔ nɔ lɛ, —yɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni egbɔjɔ ni eyɛ oshara sɛɛtiumɔ kɛ emɔmɔ sɛɛ lɛ, gbɔmɔtso lɛ baanyɛ ajie ehe kɛjɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli yɛ ahum ni naa wa ko hewɔ; ni eefo kɛtee shɔŋŋ kɛmiiya kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ naa lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛlɔ ni ji enyɔ lɛ baamɔ lɛ ekoŋŋ, ni yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ kpoo nɔ, ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ gbalaa lɛ kɛyaa emasɛi, ni wala loo ŋshɔŋlooi ahe oshara ko bɛ he. No hewɔ lɛ bei pii lɛ, béi ni mli wa fe fɛɛ ni yiwalɛ yɔɔ mli lɛ baaje wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ ateŋ, kɛji akɛ mla ko ni aŋma loo aŋmako, ni kɔɔ jeŋ muu fɛɛ he, ni ŋwanejee ko bɛ he ni kɔɔ saji fɛɛ ahe lɛ bɛ.

Ekolɛ mla pɛ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji ahe ni mla wo hegbɛ lɛ ji Holland nɔ lɛ. States-General lɛ ji mɔ ni wo mla yɛ AD 1695. Shi eyɛ mli akɛ maŋ kroko bɛ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe mla ni aŋma pɛŋ moŋ, shi kɛlɛ Amerika wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ ji amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ mlafeelɔi kɛ mlalelɔi yɛ sane nɛɛ mli. Klɛŋklɛŋ: Mɛni ji Fast-Fish? Loo ni hiɛ kã loo egbo lɛ yaa oya yɛ ŋaa gbɛ nɔ, kɛji akɛ ekɛ lɛlɛ loo lɛlɛ ko ni mɔ ko yɔɔ mli lɛ tsa, kɛtsɔ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ nɔ ni mɔ ni yɔɔ mli lɛ loo mɛi ni yɔɔ mli lɛ baanyɛ akudɔ, — ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔne, ŋshɔ hiɛ tsɔne, kpãa ni kɛlɛ ji inchi nɛɛhu, tɛligraf waya, loo ŋshɔŋlooi ahe tsɔne ko lɛ, fɛɛ ji nɔ kome. Nakai nɔŋŋ loo hu yaa oya yɛ ŋaa gbɛ nɔ kɛ́ ehiɛ waif, loo okadi kroko ni ayɔseɔ akɛ eyɛ nɔ ko; kɛji akɛ kuu ni jieɔ lɛ kpo lɛ tsɔɔ faŋŋ akɛ amɛbaanyɛ amɛkɛ lɛ aya be fɛɛ be, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu amɛyiŋtoo akɛ amɛbaafee nakai.

Enɛɛmɛi ji jeŋ nilee mli saji amlitsɔɔmɔi; shi bei komɛi lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ashishitsɔɔmɔi lɛ ji wiemɔi ni wa kɛ gbee ni wa fe tsutsu lɛ—Coke-upon-Littleton ni ji fist lɛ. Eji anɔkwale akɛ, yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsulɔi ni ja ni yɔɔ bulɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ ateŋ lɛ, ahaa shika be fɛɛ be kɛha saji krɛdɛɛi, he ni ebaafee jeŋba mli jalɛsane ni ayeee ni yɔɔ gbeyei waa akɛ kuu kome baakɛɛ akɛ eyɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni kuu kroko tiu lɛ loo egbe lɛ tsutsu lɛ. Shi mɛi krokomɛi hu bɛ hiɛdɔɔ tamɔ nɛkɛ kwraa.

Aaafee afii nyɔŋmai enumɔ ni eho nɛ lɛ, sane ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni kɔɔ whale-trover he lɛ tee kojomɔhe yɛ England, ni mɛi ni kɛ sane lɛ ba lɛ tsɔɔ akɛ beni amɛtiu whale ko waa yɛ Kooyigbɛ ŋshɔi lɛ amli lɛ; ni beni amɛ (mɛi ni kɛ sane lɛ ba lɛ) eye omanye diɛŋtsɛ yɛ loo lɛ ni akɛ harpoon tswa lɛ mli lɛ; naagbee lɛ, kɛtsɔ amɛwala ni akɛwoɔ oshara mli lɛ nɔ lɛ, ehe bahia ni amɛshi jeee amɛlɛlɛi lɛ pɛ, shi moŋ amɛlɛlɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ. Yɛ naagbee lɛ, mɛi ni awieɔ ashiɔ amɛ lɛ (lɛlɛ kroko mli nitsulɔi lɛ) kɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ba, amɛtswa lɛ, amɛgbe lɛ, amɛmɔ lɛ, ni naagbee lɛ amɛŋɔ lɛ yɛ mɛi ni kɛ sane lɛ ba lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ahiɛ. Ni beni ajie nakai mɛi ni awieɔ ashiɔ amɛ lɛ ahiɛ lɛ, amɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ tswa eniji yɛ mɛi ni kɛ sane lɛ ba lɛ anaji amli, ni ema nɔ mi eha amɛ akɛ kɛtsɔ doxology nɔ yɛ nifeemɔ ni efee lɛ he lɛ, agbɛnɛ ebaahiɛ amɛ line, harpoons, kɛ lɛlɛ lɛ mli, ni ehi shi yɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ he yɛ be mli ni aŋɔ amɛ lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, mɛi ni kɛ sane lɛ ba lɛ tee kojomɔ he agbɛnɛ koni aku sɛɛ aha amɛ ŋshɔŋlooi, ŋshɔŋlooi, ŋshɔŋlooi, kɛ lɛlɛ lɛ jara.

Owula Erskine ji ŋaawolɔ kɛha mɛi ni awieɔ ashiɔ amɛ lɛ; Nuŋtsɔ Ellenborough ji kojolɔ lɛ. Yɛ yijiemɔ lɛ mli lɛ, Erskine ni yɔɔ ŋaa lɛ tee nɔ efee eshidaamɔ he mfoniri, kɛtsɔ awuiyeli ko ni tee nɔ yɛ nyɛsɛɛ nɛɛ he ni ewie lɛ nɔ. con. sane ko, ni nuu ko ni ji nuu kpakpa, ni ebɔ mɔdɛŋ ni eye eŋa lɛ yiwalɛ nifeemɔi lɛ anɔ yakatswaa sɛɛ lɛ, naagbee lɛ eshi lɛ yɛ wala ŋshɔi lɛ anɔ; shi yɛ afii ni eho lɛ amli, beni etsake etsui yɛ nakai gbɛ lɛ he lɛ, eto nifeemɔ ko shishi koni enine ashɛ enɔ ekoŋŋ. Erskine ji mɔ ni yɔɔ afã kroko lɛ; ni no sɛɛ lɛ efi sɛɛ kɛtsɔ wiemɔ ni ekɛɛ lɛ nɔ akɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ shishijee lɛ nuu lɛ tswa yoo lɛ tsɔne, ni ekɛ lɛ ehi shi be ko, ni yɛ efɔŋfeemɔ ni miiho lɛ he haomɔ kpele lɛ pɛ hewɔ lɛ, shi naagbee lɛ eshi lɛ; shi kɛlɛ eshi lɛ, ni no hewɔ lɛ ebatsɔ loo ni bɛ mlɛo; ni no hewɔ lɛ beni nuu ko ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ tswa lɛ harpoon ekoŋŋ lɛ, no sɛɛ lɛ yoo lɛ batsɔ nakai nuu ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ nibii, kɛ harpoon fɛɛ harpoon ni ekolɛ abaana ni ekɛkpɛtɛ ehe lɛ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ yɛ sane ni yɔɔ biɛ lɛ mli lɛ, Erskine wie akɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ kɛ yoo lɛ he nɔkwɛmɔnii lɛ ji nɔkwɛmɔnii ni tsɔɔ amɛhe.

Beni abo nɛkɛ kpatamɔi nɛɛ, kɛ kpatamɔi ni akɛteɔ shi awoɔ lɛ atoi jogbaŋŋ lɛ, kojolɔ ni le nii waa lɛ kpɛ eyiŋ yɛ gbɛ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ nɔ, ni ji, —Akɛ yɛ lɛlɛ lɛ he lɛ, ekɛha mɛi ni kɛ sane lɛ ba lɛ, ejaakɛ amɛshi kɛkɛ koni amɛhere amɛwala; shi akɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi, harpoons, kɛ line ni awieɔ he lɛ he lɛ, amɛji mɛi ni awieɔ ashiɔ amɛ lɛ anɔ̃; ŋshɔŋloo lɛ, ejaakɛ eji Loose-Fish yɛ be mli ni amɔ lɛ naagbee lɛ; kɛ harpoons kɛ line ejaakɛ beni loo lɛ kɛ amɛ fã gbɛ lɛ, lɛ (loo lɛ) ná nɔ ko yɛ nakai nibii lɛ amli; ni no hewɔ lɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ni sɛɛ mli lɛ eŋɔ loo lɛ yɛ hegbɛ akɛ eŋɔɔ amɛ. Agbɛnɛ, mɛi ni awieɔ ashiɔ amɛ lɛ ŋɔ loo lɛ yɛ no sɛɛ; ergo, saji ni atsĩ tã kɛtsɔ hiɛ lɛ ji amɛ nɔ.

Gbɔmɔ folo ko ni miikwɛ Kojolɔ ni le nii waa lɛ yiŋkpɛɛ nɛɛ, ekolɛ ebaate shi ewo lɛ. Shi kɛ ashɛ shishitoo mlai wuji enyɔ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ yɛ twin whaling mlai ni atsĩ tã kɛtsɔ hiɛ lɛ amli lɛ he lɛ, ni Lord Ellenborough kɛtsu nii ni etsɔɔ mli yɛ sane ni atsĩ tã yɛ yiteŋgbɛ lɛ mli lɛ; nɛkɛ mlai enyɔ nɛɛ ni kɔɔ Fast-Fish kɛ Loose-Fish he lɛ, mikɛɛ, kɛji akɛ asusu he lɛ, abaana akɛ amɛji adesai amlai ahe nilee fɛɛ shishitoo mlai; ejaakɛ yɛ bɔ ni akpɛ́ lɛ mli nibii ni yɔɔ hwanyaŋŋ lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, Mla Sɔlemɔtsu lɛ, tamɔ Filistibii Asɔlemɔtsu lɛ, yɛ nibii enyɔ pɛ ni ekɛbaadamɔ nɔ.

Mɛi komɛi buɔ lɛ akɛ nɔ ko ni anyɛɛɛ akpɛlɛ nɔ diɛŋtsɛ, ni akɔnɔ ko bɛ ni baanyɛ atsɔse amɛ koni amɛkã emasɛi. Shi kɛlɛ, mɛi komɛi yɛ ni baafee nakai lolo; yɛ anɔkwale ni eji akɛ mu ni anáa kɛjɛɔ mɛi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ mli lɛ ji nɔ ni baa shi kwraa lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ, ni jeee attar-of-rose su yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ.

Beni wɔbɛŋkɛ lolo kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni miiho lɛ, wɔna akɛ Frenchnyo lɛ yɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni ji enyɔ yɛ emasɛi; ni nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo ni ji enyɔ nɛɛ tamɔ nɔ ni eji nosegay po fe klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, ehiɛ etoiŋjɔlɛ yɛ nakai yitso lɛ nɔ, shi kɛ jeee nakai lɛ, ekɛ lɛ wieɔ faŋŋ ni ekɛ lɛ wieɔ teemɔŋ saji ahe, bɔni afee ni amɛyi enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ amɛfee gbɛjianɔtoo fioo ko oya koni amɛkɛbɔle Kapten lɛ he ni amɛkɛye ehe fɛo, ni esusuuu he kwraa akɛ ekɛ ehe efɔɔɔ amɛ anɔkwayeli lɛ nɔ. Enɛ nɔ lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ je shishi, ni ekɛ miishɛɛ miisumɔ ni ele babaoo.

"Mɛni hewɔ, akɛni ekɛ ehe woɔ mli mlɛo hewɔ lɛ, kɛɛmɔ lɛ akɛ amrɔ nɛɛ mikwɛ lɛ jogbaŋŋ, miyɛ nɔmimaa kwraa akɛ esaaa akɛ ekwɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi-lɛlɛ nɔ fe St. Jago ŋshɔŋloo ko. Yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, kɛɛmɔ lɛ kɛjɛ midɛŋ akɛ eji ŋshɔŋloo."

“Ewo shi ni ejaje, Monsieur, akɛ ŋshɔŋloo kroko lɛ, ni egbi lɛ, gbeɔ mɔ kwraa fe nɔ ni atswa lɛ; yɛ naagbee lɛ, Monsieur, ekanyaa wɔ, taakɛ wɔbuɔ wɔwala akɛ nɔ ko ni jara wa lɛ, koni wɔtsi wɔhe kɛjɛ loo nɛɛ he.”

Amrɔ nɔŋŋ ni lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ jo foi kɛtee hiɛ, ni ekɛ gbee ni wa fã etsulɔi lɛ akɛ amɛkpa tsɔnei ni akɛfoɔ ŋshɔŋ lɛ awomɔ, ni amrɔ nɔŋŋ lɛ amɛjie kpãai kɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ni tsiɔ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji lɛ anaa lɛ.

“Mɛni agbɛnɛ?” Guernsey-nuu lɛ kɛɛ, beni Kapten lɛ ku esɛɛ eba amɛŋɔɔ lɛ.

“Mɛni hewɔ, ha makwɛ; hɛɛ, obaanyɛ okɛɛ lɛ bianɛ akɛ—nakai—yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, okɛɛ lɛ akɛ mikɛ mihe ewo mli, ni (kɛ ajie lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe) ekolɛ mɔ kroko.”

“Ekɛɛ, Monsieur, akɛ eyɛ miishɛɛ waa akɛ ekɛ sɔɔmɔ ko etsu nii eha wɔ.”

Beni ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ nu enɛ lɛ, ewo shi akɛ amɛji mɛi ni hiɛ shidaa lɛ (ni tsɔɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ kɛ ehefatalɔ) ni egbe naa kɛtsɔ nine ni efɔ̃ Stubb kɛba shi kɛtee etsu lɛ mli koni ebanu Bordeaux tsofa lɛ tu kome lɛ nɔ.

Wiemɔ shishitsɔɔlɔ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Esumɔɔ ni okɛ wain glase kome aya.”

"Miida lɛ shi kɛjɛ mitsui mli; shi kɛɛmɔ lɛ akɛ ekɛ nuu ni mikɛ lɛ ebɔ lɛ baanu dãa lɛ teɔ shi ewoɔ mishishitoo mlai. Yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, kɛɛmɔ lɛ akɛ esa akɛ maya."

“Ekɛɛ, Monsieur, akɛ eshishitoo mlai lɛ ekpɛlɛŋ nɔ akɛ enuɔ dãa; shi akɛ kɛji Monsieur miisumɔ ni ehi shi gbi kroko ni enu dãa lɛ, no lɛ ehi jogbaŋŋ akɛ Monsieur baashɛ lɛlɛi ejwɛ lɛ fɛɛ shi, ni egbala lɛlɛ lɛ kɛjɛ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji nɛɛ ahe, ejaakɛ eyɛ dioo waa ni amɛshwieŋ.”

No hewɔ lɛ, beni Frenchnyo lɛ lɛlɛi lɛ miigba lɛlɛ lɛ gbɛ kome nɔ lɛ, Stubb jɛ mlihilɛ mli ekɛ e-whale lɛ tee gbɛ kroko nɔ, ni ekɛ ehe wo mli ni ekɛ tsɔne ni akɛgbalaa lɛlɛ kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ fe fɛɛ lɛ shwie shi.

Amrɔ nɛɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ko tswa; Stubb fee ehe tamɔ mɔ ko ni shwieɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ; beni ewoɔ elɛlɛi lɛ anɔ lɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni Frenchnyo lɛ ha esɛɛ jɛkɛmɔ tee hiɛ, yɛ be mli ni Pequod lɛ shwieɔ ekɛ Stubb ŋshɔŋloo lɛ teŋ lɛ. No sɛɛ lɛ, Stubb gbala ehe oya kɛtee gbɔmɔtso ni hoɔ lɛ he, ni ejie Pequod lɛ yi koni ekɛ eyiŋtoi lɛ aha ale, ni ebɔi eshishiumɔ ni ejaaa lɛ yibii lɛ amrɔ nɔŋŋ. Beni emɔ elɛlɛ-spade ni yɔɔ oya lɛ mli lɛ, ebɔi gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli taomɔ, yɛ sɛɛ ŋshɔ lɛ sɛɛ fioo. Kulɛ eshwɛ fioo ni obaasusu akɛ eefo tsũ shishi ŋshɔ lɛ mli jɛmɛ; ni beni yɛ naagbee lɛ e-spade lɛ tswa tsitsii ni egbɔjɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ Roma tile momoi kɛ sũ mli nibii ni afu yɛ Ŋleshi sũ ni mli eyi obɔ kɛ sũ mli lɛ miiho. Mɛi ni tsuɔ nii yɛ elɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ fɛɛ miishɛ amɛhe waa, ni amɛkɛ miishɛɛ miiye amɛbua amɛ onukpa lɛ, ni amɛmiifee mɛi ni haoɔ tamɔ mɛi ni taoɔ shika tsuru lɛ.

Stubb bɔi nijiaŋwujee, titri lɛ beni nosegay ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ yaa hiɛ lɛ, beni trukaa lɛ, tsofa-kɛ-ŋma ni mli edɔ fioo ko jɛ hela nɛɛ mli, ni hoɔ kɛtsɔɔ tsofa-kɛ-ŋma gbonyo lɛ mli ni ekɛ ehe wooo mli, taakɛ faa kome baaho kɛbote mli ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekɛ be fɛɛ be, kɛ ekroko baatsɔ mli lɛ.

YITSƆƆ 92. Amber.

Amrɔ nɛɛ ambergris nɛɛ ji nɔ ko ni haa mɔ taoɔ ele nɔ ni yaa nɔ waa, ni ehe hiaa waa akɛ jarayeli nibii, ni yɛ afi 1791 lɛ, akwɛ Kapten Coffin ko ni afɔ lɛ yɛ Nantucket lɛ mli yɛ Ŋleshi maŋtsɛwe lɛ dãatɔɔhe lɛ yɛ nakai sane lɛ he. Ejaakɛ yɛ nakai beaŋ, kɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, kɛyashi gbi ko ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ waa lɛ, he pɔtɛɛ ni ambergris jɛ lɛ tee nɔ efee naagba kɛha nikaselɔi, tamɔ amber diɛŋtsɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ wiemɔ ambergris lɛ ji French wiemɔ ni akɛfee gray amber moŋ, shi kɛlɛ nibii enyɔ lɛ yɛ srɔtoi kwraa. Ejaakɛ amber, eyɛ mli akɛ bei komɛi lɛ anaa yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa moŋ, shi atsaa hu yɛ shikpɔji komɛi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli shɔŋŋ lɛ anɔ, yɛ be mli ni anaaa ambergris kɔkɔɔkɔ ja ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ pɛ. Kɛfata he lɛ, amber ji nɔ ko ni wa, ni mli tse, ni shwieɔ shi, ni ŋmɔlɔ bɛ mli, ni akɛtsuɔ nii kɛfeɔ naabu-kɛ-paipii, kɛfeɔ ŋmeii kɛ ŋmɔlɔ; shi ambergris lɛ yɛ bɔdɔɔ, eyɛ wax, ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔ waa ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔ, ni akɛtsuɔ nii babaoo yɛ tsofa-kɛ-ŋma mli, yɛ pastiles, kanei ni jara wa, yitsɔi-powda, kɛ pomatum mli. Turkeybii lɛ kɛtsuɔ nii yɛ niyenii homɔ mli, ni amɛkɛyaa Mecca hu, kɛha yiŋtoo kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ ni akɛ tsofa-kɛ-ŋma yaa St. Peter yɛ Roma lɛ. Wein jarayelɔi komɛi kɛ ŋmaa fioo komɛi woɔ claret mli, koni amɛkɛwo mli ŋɔɔ.

Belɛ, namɔ baasusu akɛ esa akɛ yei kɛ hii kpakpai ni tamɔ nɛkɛ lɛ kɛ tsofa ko ni anaa yɛ ŋshɔŋloo ko ni hela mɔ lɛ musu ni bɛ nyam lɛ mli lɛ aye amɛhe! Shi kɛlɛ, nakai eji. Mɛi komɛi susuɔ akɛ ambergris ji nɔ ni kɛ dyspepsia ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mli lɛ baa, ni mɛi krokomɛi hu susuɔ akɛ eji nɔ ni jɛɔ mli baa. Ewa akɛ aaatsɔɔ bɔ ni aaafee atsa dyspepsia ni tamɔ nɛkɛ, ja kɛtsɔ Brandreth tsofai ni akɛwoɔ lɛlɛ mli ni eyi obɔ lɛ etɛ loo ejwɛ nɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ejoɔ foi kɛjɛɔ awuiyeli gbɛ nɔ, taakɛ nitsulɔi feɔ yɛ tɛi ni amɛtswaa lɛ mli lɛ nɔ.

Mihiɛ ekpa nɔ akɛ makɛɛ akɛ ana yɛ ambergris nɛɛ mli, plɛtei komɛi ni wa, ni yɔɔ kokroo, ni yɔɔ wui, ni shishijee lɛ Stubb susu akɛ ekolɛ eji ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔi atrɔsai akutsei; shi sɛɛ mli lɛ ebafee akɛ amɛjeee nɔ ko nɔ ko fe ŋshɔŋloo wui bibii ni akɛ tsofa wo mli yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ lɛ amli fãi.

Amrɔ nɛɛ ni esa akɛ ana nɛkɛ ambergris ni ŋɔɔ fe fɛɛ nɛɛ fitemɔ yɛ nɛkɛ fitemɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ tsui mli lɛ; ani enɛ jeee nɔ ko? Susumɔ nakai wiemɔ ni Paulo Krɔŋkrɔŋ lɛ wie yɛ Korintobii lɛ amli lɛ he, ni kɔɔ fitemɔ kɛ fitemɔ ni bɛ lɛ he; bɔ ni adu wɔ yɛ hiɛgbele mli, shi atee wɔ shi yɛ anunyam mli. Ni nakai nɔŋŋ kaimɔ Paracelsus wiemɔ ni kɔɔ nɔ ni feɔ musk ni hi fe fɛɛ lɛ he lɛ. Agbɛnɛ hu, kaahã ohiɛ kpa anɔkwale ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni ji akɛ yɛ nibii fɛɛ ni ŋɔɔ bɛ mli lɛ ateŋ lɛ, Cologne-nu, yɛ eshishijee nifeemɔi amli lɛ ji nɔ ni ehiii fe fɛɛ lɛ nɔ hu.

Miisumɔ ni mikɛ ŋwɛi shɛɛ sane lɛ agbe yitso lɛ naa, shi minyɛɛɛ, yɛ mihaomɔ ni miyɔɔ akɛ mafo fɔbuu ni afɔɔ kɛwoɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛ mli lɛ hewɔ, ni yɛ jwɛŋmɔi komɛi ni tsɔɔ mɛi ahe jwɛŋmɔ ni ejaaa momo lɛ asusumɔ naa lɛ, abaanyɛ abu lɛ akɛ nɔ ni awie yɛ Frenchnyo lɛ ŋshɔŋlooi enyɔ lɛ ahe lɛ maa nɔ mi yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ. Yɛ wolo nɛɛ mli hei krokomɛi lɛ, akpoo wiemɔ fɔŋ ni awieɔ ashiɔ lɛ, akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɔ nitsumɔ lɛ ji nitsumɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni bɛ gbɛjianɔtoo mli fɛɛ. Shi nɔ kroko hu yɛ ni esa akɛ akpoo. Amɛtsɔɔ akɛ ŋshɔŋ loofɔji fɛɛ shãa be fɛɛ be. Agbɛnɛ te fee tɛŋŋ ni nɛkɛ heguɔgbee ni yɔɔ gbeyei nɛɛ je shishi?

Misusuɔ akɛ, abaanyɛ ana lɛ faŋŋ kɛjɛ Greenland ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhei lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ ba London, nɔ ni fe afii ohai enyɔ ni eho nɛ. Ejaakɛ nakai ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ aka amɛ mu lɛ amɛkwɛɛɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ yɛ nakai beaŋ, ni amɛkaaa amrɔ nɛɛ amɛkwɛɛɛ tamɔ bɔ ni Anaigbɛ lɛlɛi lɛ feɔ be fɛɛ be lɛ; shi ni ooofolɔ blubber hee lɛ mli bibii bibii, ni okɛwoɔ tsɛnsii wuji amli bung bu lɛ mli, ni okɛyaa shia yɛ nakai gbɛ nɔ; be kuku ni yɔɔ nakai Icy Ŋshɔi lɛ amli lɛ, kɛ ahum ni baa trukaa ni mli wa ni amɛkpeɔ lɛ, ni guɔ gbɛ kroko ni amɛtsɔɔ nɔ amɛyaa lɛ. Nɔ ni jɛɔ mli baa ji akɛ, kɛ́ akumɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli, ni ajie nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi gbohiiashiteehei nɛɛ eko, yɛ Greenland lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ, anáa ŋmɔlɔ ko ni tamɔ nɔ ni jɛɔ maŋ momo ko gbonyobu ko ni akumɔ mli baa lɛ mli, kɛha Lying-in Hospital shishitoo lɛ.

Misusuɔ hu akɛ, nɛkɛ fɔyeli fɔŋ ni akɛteɔ shi awoɔ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛmɛi nɛɛ hu baanyɛ ajɛ Dutch akrowa ko ni atsɛɔ lɛ Schmerenburgh loo Smeerenberg ni yɔɔ Greenland ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ mli, yɛ tsutsu bei amli, ni naagbee gbɛi lɛ ji nɔ ni nikaselɔ Fogo Von Slack kɛtsu nii yɛ ewolo bibioo ko ni kɔɔ Smells he lɛ mli. Taakɛ egbɛi lɛ tsɔɔ lɛ (smeer, fat; berg, to put up), ato akrowa nɛɛ shishi bɔni afee ni aná he ko ni akɛ blubber ni yɔɔ Dutch whale lɛlɛ lɛ mli lɛ aka, ni akɛtee shia kɛya Holland kɛha nakai yiŋtoo lɛ. Eji flɔnɔɔi, fɔ-kettles, kɛ mu-kɛtlɛi ni abua naa; ni beni nitsumɔi lɛ jeɔ shishi lɛ, ejieee ŋmɔlɔ ko ni ŋɔɔ waa kpo. No hewɔ lɛ gbɔmɔ shwilafofeemɔ ji ŋwɛi jwɛŋmɔ; ni beni efãa kɛjɛɔ jwɛŋmɔ fɛɛ ni gboɔ lɛ mli lɛ, naagbee lɛ ebaa nakai ŋwɛi susumɔ lɛ he, ni kɛ asusu he lɛ, eji nɔ ni shishinumɔ bɛ he ni ehaa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli; kɛ ninamɔ loo haomɔ, nuɔ he nakai beaŋ akɛ ekɛ ehe wooo mli, ni esusuuu he akɛ e-Nyɔŋmɔ.

Kɛha mɛi krokomɛi lɛ, kaashwa Stubb tsɔ. Nɔ ni afɔɔ namɔ yɛ nakai wuoyeli lɛ mli; ni yɛ sanegbaa ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ, no sɛɛ lɛ abaana nɔ ni tamɔ shikpamɔ ni ba mi diɛŋtsɛ minɔ.
