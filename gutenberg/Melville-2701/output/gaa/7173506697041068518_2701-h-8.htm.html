No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ saji ni awieɔ kɛ okadii ni kɔɔ ehe lɛ hewɔ lɛ, wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ ateŋ mɛi fioo ko pɛ kaiɔ, yɛ Moby Dick he lɛ, shishijee gbii ni akɛ Sperm Whale wuolɛɔ lɛ tsuɔ nii yɛ mli lɛ, beni bei pii lɛ ewaa akɛ aaaha Right whalemen ni kɛ amɛhe ewo mli be kakadaŋŋ lɛ abɔi ta hee ni yɔɔ ekãa nɛɛ he osharai lɛ; hii ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni teɔ shi woɔ akɛ eyɛ mli akɛ abaanyɛ atiu leviathan krokomɛi kɛ hiɛnɔkamɔ moŋ, shi kɛlɛ, ni amɛtiu ni amɛtsɔɔ kpojiemɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ tamɔ Sperm Whale lɛ jeee gbɔmɔ ni gboɔ lɛ nɔ. No akɛ, kɛji akɛ aka lɛ akwɛ lɛ, ebaafee nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ ajo naa foi akɛ abaagbala mli kɛya naanɔ oya mli. Yɛ yitso nɛɛ nɔ lɛ, woji komɛi ni sa kadimɔ waa yɛ ni abaanyɛ akwɛ mli.

Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, mɛi komɛi yɛ, ni yɛ nibii nɛɛ ahiɛ po lɛ, amɛfee klalo akɛ amɛbaatiu Moby Dick; kɛ mɛi babaoo ni fa fe nakai lolo ni, yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ kɛkɛ ni amɛnu ehe yɛ he ko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ ni bɛ faŋŋ, ni amanehulu ko he saji pɔtɛɛ komɛi bɛ mli, ni apasa hemɔkɛyeli fataaa he lɛ, amɛhe wa bɔ ni sa ni amɛjoŋ ta lɛ naa foi kɛji akɛ aha amɛ.

Ŋaawoi ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni awie he lɛ ateŋ ekome, akɛ naagbee lɛ eba akɛ akɛ White Whale lɛ yɔɔ tsakpaa yɛ mɛi ni sumɔɔ apasa hemɔkɛyeli lɛ ajwɛŋmɔ mli lɛ ji henɔwomɔ ni jeee shikpɔŋ nɔ nɔ akɛ Moby Dick yɛ he fɛɛ he; akɛ ekɛ lɛ ekpe diɛŋtsɛ yɛ latitudei ni teɔ shi woɔ amɛhe lɛ amli yɛ be kome mli.

Ni asaŋ, bɔ ni esa akɛ jwɛŋmɔi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ afee mɛi ni heɔ amɛyeɔ lɛ, nɛkɛ henɔwomɔ nɛɛ jeee nɔ ko kwraa ni apasa hemɔkɛyeli ni baanyɛ aba lɛ he nɔ ko ni yɔɔ mlɛo bɛ mli. Ejaakɛ ajieee ŋshɔi ni hoɔ lɛ ahe teemɔŋ saji lɛ akpo pɛŋ, kɛha niiamlitaolɔi ni he esa fe fɛɛ lɛ po; no hewɔ lɛ gbɛi ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ ni Sperm Whale lɛ tsɔɔ nɔ beni eyɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ shishi lɛ kã he eji, yɛ fa ko mli lɛ, nɔ ni mɛi ni nyiɛɔ esɛɛ lɛ nyɛŋ abu he akɔntaa; ni yɛ be kɛ bei amli lɛ, amɛjie susumɔi ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli fe fɛɛ ni teɔ shi woɔ amɛhe lɛ kpo yɛ amɛhe, titri lɛ yɛ teemɔŋ gbɛi ni, beni etswa gbee ni mli kwɔ waa sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ oyaiyeli kpele ni tamɔ nɛkɛ yaa hei ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ lɛ ahe.

Eji nɔ ko ni Amerika kɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛlɛi fɛɛ le jogbaŋŋ, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu nɔ ko ni Scoresby kɛwo wolo ni yɔɔ hegbɛ nɔ afii komɛi ni eho nɛ, akɛ amɔmɔ ŋshɔŋlooi komɛi yɛ Pasifik kooyigbɛ shɔŋŋ, ni ana ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛtswaa ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛtswaa Greenland ŋshɔi lɛ amli lɛ yɛ amɛgbɔmɔtsei lɛ amli. Ni esaaa akɛ awieɔ he hu akɛ, yɛ shihilɛi nɛɛ ekomɛi amli lɛ, atswa adafi akɛ be ni kã tutuamɔi enyɔ lɛ ateŋ lɛ nyɛŋ afee nɔ ni fe gbii babaoo kwraa. No hewɔ lɛ, kɛtsɔ susumɔ nɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nilelɔi komɛi ehe amɛye akɛ, Nor’ West Passage, ni ji naagba kɛha gbɔmɔ be kakadaŋŋ lɛ, jeee naagba kɛha ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ kɔkɔɔkɔ. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ biɛ, yɛ gbɔmɛi ni hiɛ ka lɛ anifeemɔ diɛŋtsɛ ni yɔɔ wala mli lɛ mli lɛ, naakpɛɛ nii ni awie he yɛ blema bei amli yɛ Strello gɔŋ ni yɔɔ Portugal shikpɔŋ lɛ mli lɛ he (ni akɛɛ ŋshɔ ko ni bɛŋkɛ eyiteŋ lɛ yɛ ni lɛlɛi ni efite lɛ hoɔ kɛyaa ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ lɛ); kɛ nakai sane ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ fe no lolo ni kɔɔ Arethusa nu hiɛ ni bɛŋkɛ Syracuse lɛ he (ni aheɔ ayeɔ akɛ enui lɛ jɛ Shikpɔŋ Krɔŋkrɔŋ lɛ nɔ kɛtsɔ shikpɔŋ shishi gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ); nɛkɛ saji ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɛɛ kɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ ni kɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe lɛ yeɔ egbɔ kwraa.

No hewɔ lɛ, anyɛɔ mɔ nɔ koni ekɛ naakpɛɛ nibii tamɔ enɛɛmɛi ale jogbaŋŋ; ni ele akɛ yɛ tutuamɔi ni tee nɔ shii abɔ ni ekɛ ekãa fee lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, White Whale lɛ ejo foi ni ehiɛ kã; enyɛŋ efee wɔ naakpɛɛ waa akɛ esa akɛ ŋshɔŋlooi komɛi aya hiɛ lolo yɛ amɛ apasa hemɔkɛyelii lɛ amli; ni ejajeɔ akɛ Moby Dick lɛ bɛ he fɛɛ he kɛkɛ, shi moŋ egbooo (ejaakɛ gbele ni bɛ lɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ he fɛɛ he yɛ be mli kɛkɛ); akɛ eyɛ mli akɛ abaadu akpɔlɔi tsei yɛ esɛɛ moŋ, shi ebaatsɔ nu lɛ mli lolo ni nɔ ko nɔ ko efeee lɛ; aloo kɛji akɛ lɛɛlɛŋ lɛ, abaaha ekɛ lá ni mli eyi obɔ lɛ afɔ shi gbi ko lɛ, nɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni enaa lɛ baafee lakamɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei kɛkɛ; ejaakɛ ekoŋŋ yɛ ŋshɔkei ni lá bɛ mli ni kɛ jɛmɛ jɛkɛmɔ ji kilomitai ohai abɔ lɛ amli lɛ, abaana e-jet ni he tseee lɛ shikome ekoŋŋ.

Shi kɛji akɛ ajie nɛkɛ susumɔi ni fe adebɔɔ nɔ nɛɛ po lɛ, nɔ ni fa bɔ ni sa yɛ shikpɔŋ nɔ nifeemɔ kɛ subaŋ ni anyɛɛɛ he ŋwane aje lɛ mli ni baanyɛ atswa susumɔ lɛ kɛ hewalɛ ni anyɛɛɛ afɔ lɛ. Ejaakɛ, jeee egbɔmɔtso ni da ni bɛ tɛ̃ɛ lɛ ji nɔ ni haa esoroɔ lɛ kwraa yɛ sperm whales krokomɛi ahe, shi moŋ, taakɛ ashɛrɛ ashwie shi yɛ hei krokomɛi lɛ—hiɛ ni yɔɔ srɔto ni tamɔ snoo yɛŋ ni ebɔle ehe, kɛ tsitsi ni nɔ kwɔ ni tamɔ pyramid yɛŋ. Enɛɛmɛi ji ehe nibii ni jeɔ kpo faŋŋ; okadii ni ekɛjie mɔ ni eji lɛ kpo yɛ ŋshɔi ni bɛ husu, ni aleee lɛ po amli, yɛ he ko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ, etsɔɔ mɛi ni le lɛ lɛ.

Egbɔmɔtso lɛ fã ni eshwɛ lɛ mli eyi kɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni yɛ naagbee lɛ, enine eshɛ egbɛi ni yɔɔ srɔto ni ji White Whale lɛ nɔ; gbɛi ko, lɛɛlɛŋ, ni abuɔ lɛ ja diɛŋtsɛ yɛ efã ni mli ka shi faŋŋ lɛ hewɔ, kɛ́ ana lɛ ni efoɔ yɛ shwane mli kɛtsɔɔ ŋshɔ bluu ni mli edɔ mli, ni eshiɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni tamɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni tamɔ ŋmɔtɔ, ni fɛɛ eyi kɛ shika tsuru ni kpɛɔ.

Ni jeee egbɔmɔtso ni dalɛ bɛ mli, loo ehiɛ su ni sa kadimɔ waa, loo eshishigbɛ tsitsi ni efite lɛ ji nɔ ni ha ŋshɔŋloo lɛ wo adebɔɔ mli gbeyeishemɔ babaoo mli, tamɔ nakai nɔkwɛmɔnɔ, nilee ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni, taakɛ amaniɛbɔi pɔtɛɛ komɛi tsɔɔ lɛ, ejie lɛ kpo shii abɔ yɛ etutuamɔi lɛ amli lɛ. Nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, eshishiumɔ ni ekɛku esɛɛ lɛ ha ehiɛ fee lɛ yaa fe ekolɛ nɔ kroko. Ejaakɛ, beni efoɔ nu yɛ mɛi ni nyiɛɔ esɛɛ ni amɛmiishɛ amɛhe lɛ ahiɛ, kɛ gbeyeishemɔ he okadi fɛɛ okadi ni tsɔɔ akɛ eyɛ gbeyeishemɔ lɛ, ale lɛ shii abɔ akɛ etsɔɔ ehe trukaa, ni, beni ekɛ amɛ shwieɔ shi lɛ, ekɛ amɛlɛlɛi lɛ tswaa amɛhe ni amɛfiteɔ, loo ekɛ gbeyeishemɔ kuɔ amɛsɛɛ kɛyaa amɛlɛlɛ lɛ mli.

No mli lɛ, mɛi ni gboi babaoo eba esɛɛtiumɔ lɛ shishi momo. Shi eyɛ mli akɛ osharai ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni amɛfeee nɔ ko kwraa yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, jeee nɔ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto kwraa yɛ wuoyeli mli; shi, yɛ bei babaoo mli lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni White Whale lɛ hɛl susumɔ ni kɔɔ yiwalɛ he lɛ ji nɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ, akɛ abuuu gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli fãi loo gbele fɛɛ gbele ni ekɛbaa lɛ kwraa akɛ mɔ ko ni bɛ nilee ji mɔ ni kɛba.

No hewɔ lɛ, kojomɔ, bɔ ni mlifu ni mli edɔ, ni gbalaa mɔ jwɛŋmɔ lɛ tsirɛɔ egbɔbilɔi ni he ejɔ amɛ waa lɛ ajwɛŋmɔi, beni amɛyɔɔ lɛlɛi ni atswa amɛ lɛ ateŋ, kɛ nanemɛi ni ekpɔtɔ lɛ aniji ni miigbee shi lɛ ateŋ lɛ, amɛfo kɛjɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ mlifu ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ mli kɛtee hulutsoo be mli, loo ex, ayemforo.

White Whale lɛ fo yɛ ehiɛ akɛ nakai nifeemɔi fɔji fɛɛ ni hii komɛi ni mli kwɔ lɛ nuɔ he akɛ amɛmiiye nii yɛ mli lɛ agbɔmɔtso ni ji monomaniac lɛ, kɛyashi amɛshiɔ amɛ ni amɛhiɔ shi kɛ tsui fã kɛ fɛ̃i fã. Nakai efɔŋfeemɔ ni anyɛɛɛ ana ni yɔɔ kɛjɛ shishijee lɛ; ni ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ Kristofoi po kɛ je lɛ fã ko woɔ enɔyeli mli lɛ; ni blema Ofibii ni yɔɔ bokagbɛ lɛ buɔ lɛ waa yɛ amɛ amaga abonsam lɛ mli lɛ;—Ahab egbeee shi ni ejáaa lɛ tamɔ amɛ; shi beni ekɛ esusumɔ lɛ tee ŋshɔŋloo yɛŋ ni asumɔɔɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekɛ ehe wo mli, ni egbɔjɔ ehe fɛɛ, ni ekɛte shi ewo lɛ. Ebɛ faŋŋ akɛ nɛkɛ monomania ni yɔɔ emli nɛɛ tee hiɛ amrɔ nɔŋŋ yɛ be pɔtɛɛ ni egbɔmɔtso lɛ mli fãi lɛ fã lɛ mli. Kɛkɛ ni, yɛ gbee ni ekɛtswaa kooloo lɛ he, ni ehiɛ kakla lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ ehe eha trukaa, ni mli wa, ni ji gbɔmɔtsoŋ henyɛlɔi; ni beni enine shɛ strok ni gbala lɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekolɛ enu gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli fãji ni yeɔ mɔ awui lɛ pɛ he, shi enuuu nɔ ko fe nakai. Shi, beni nɛkɛ gbeekpamɔ nɛɛ nyɛ enɔ koni etsɔ ehe kɛtee shia, kɛ nyɔji babaoo ni ji gbii kɛ otsii babaoo lɛ, Ahab kɛ haomɔ lɛ kã shi yɛ ŋwɛi ŋta kome mli, ni amɛbɔle nakai Patagonia Ŋshɔkpɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho ni miihoso lɛ he yɛ fɛ̃i be lɛ teŋgbɛ; no sɛɛ lɛ, egbɔmɔtso ni etse kɛ esusuma ni etse lɛ lá ho kɛbote amɛhe mli; ni no hewɔ lɛ ekɛ ehe woɔ mli lɛ, ha ehiɛ fee lɛ yaa. Akɛ nakai beaŋ pɛ, yɛ shia gbɛfaa lɛ mli, yɛ kpee lɛ sɛɛ, ni naagbee monomania lɛ mɔ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni eji nɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ kɛjɛ anɔkwale ni eji akɛ, yɛ bei komɛi amli yɛ gbɛfaa lɛ mli lɛ, eji mɔ ko ni egbɔjɔ waa; ni, eyɛ mli akɛ enane ko bɛ ehe moŋ, shi hewalɛ ni he hiaa waa tamɔ nɛkɛ kã e-Mizraim tsitsi lɛ mli lolo, ni nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, e-delirium lɛ ha ehiɛ wa waa, ni no ha ehefatalɔi lɛ nyɛ amɛnɔ koni amɛwo lɛ oya, yɛ jɛmɛ po, beni enyiɛ lɛlɛ mli, ni ekɛ ehe miiwo e-hammock lɛ mli lɛ. Yɛ strait-jacket mli lɛ, ekɛ ehe shwie ahum ni naa wa lɛ mli. Ni, beni ejoɔ foi kɛyaa latitude ni yɔɔ piŋmɔ fe tsutsu lɛ mli lɛ, lɛlɛ lɛ, ni ekɛ stun’sails ni mli jɔ lɛ egbɛ eshwa lɛ, tsɔ tropics ni yɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ lɛ anɔ, ni, yɛ bɔ ni ejieɔ lɛ kpo ehaa lɛ fɛɛ naa lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni nuumo lɛ delirium lɛ eshi esɛɛ kɛ Cape Horn swells lɛ, ni ejɛ e-den ni mli ewo duŋ lɛ mli kɛtee b air lɛ mli; nakai beaŋ po, beni ewo nakai hiɛ ni ma shi shiŋŋ lɛ, ebua ehe naa, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ha lɛ, ni ekɛ efamɔi ni yɔɔ kpoo lɛ ha shikome ekoŋŋ; ni ehefatalɔi lɛ da Nyɔŋmɔ shi akɛ shwilafofeemɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ etee agbɛnɛ; nakai beaŋ po lɛ, Ahab, yɛ ehe ni ekɛtee lɛ mli lɛ, tee nɔ ekɛ ehe wo mli. Bei pii lɛ, adesa shwilafofeemɔ ji ŋaa kɛ feline nɔ ko. Kɛ́ osusu akɛ ejo foi lɛ, ekolɛ etsake shi etsɔ su ko ni yɔɔ mlɛo lolo. Ahab shwilafofeemɔ ni yeɔ emuu lɛ sɛɛ efooo, shi moŋ eba shi waa; tamɔ Hudson ni sɛɛ efooo lɛ, beni nakai Northman ni nɔ kwɔ lɛ hoɔ yɛ gbɛ bibioo nɔ, shi etsɔɔɔ Highland gɔŋ lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni anyɛɛɛ anu shishi lɛ nɔ lɛ. Shi, taakɛ eji yɛ e-monomania ni hoɔ oya lɛ mli lɛ, Ahab shwilafofeemɔ ni mli kwɔ lɛ mli nɔ ko bibioo po eshwɛko; no hewɔ lɛ yɛ nakai shwilafofeemɔ ni mli kwɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ebɔɔ su mli nilee kpeteŋkpele lɛ mli nɔ kome po efiteee. Nakai dani ehiɛ kã lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ ebatsɔ dɛŋdade ni hiɛ kã lɛ. Kɛji akɛ nɛkɛ mlifu trope nɛɛ baanyɛ adamɔ shi lɛ, e-lunacy krɛdɛɛ lɛ tswa ejwɛŋmɔ fɛɛ, ni ewo, ni etsɔ e-cannon ni ekɛ ehe ewo mli lɛ fɛɛ nɔ ewo lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-mad mark lɛ nɔ; no hewɔ lɛ, yɛ nɔ najiaŋ ni Ahab, kɛyashɛ nakai naagbee kome lɛ he lɛ, eyɛ hewalɛ ni fa fe tsutsu ko ni ekɛ jwɛŋmɔŋ hewalɛ tsu nii yɛ nɔ kome ni jwɛŋmɔ yɔɔ mli lɛ nɔ shii akpe agbɛnɛ.

Enɛ fa babaoo; shi kɛlɛ, Ahab fã ni da, ni yɔɔ duŋ, ni mli kwɔ lɛ bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko. Shi yakayaka nii akɛ aaaha nibii ni mli kwɔ lɛ ale, ni anɔkwale fɛɛ mli kwɔ. Ni oootsɔ shɔŋŋ kɛjɛ nɛkɛ Hotel de Cluny ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi ŋta ni wɔdamɔ biɛ nɛɛ tsui lɛ mli diɛŋtsɛ—bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ wulu ni eyɛ naakpɛɛ ha lɛ, agbɛnɛ nyɛkpaa;—ni nyɛyaa, nyɛ susumai ni nɔ kwɔlɔ, ni yɔɔ awerɛho lɛ, kɛya nakai Roma asai wuji ni ji Thermes lɛ anɔ; he ni gbɔmɔ ŋwɛi shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ tsũi ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ ashishi shɔŋŋ lɛ, eshishijee ni ji agbojee lɛ, egbɔmɔtso ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ fɛɛ ta shi yɛ tsitsiiaŋ; blema nɔ ko ni afu yɛ blema nibii ashishi, ni akɛma maŋtsɛ sɛi nɔ yɛ gbɔmɔtsei anɔ! No hewɔ lɛ, kɛ maŋtsɛ sɛi ni ekumɔ lɛ, nyɔŋmɔi wuji lɛ yeɔ nakai maŋtsɛ ni aŋɔ lɛ nom lɛ he fɛo; no hewɔ lɛ, tamɔ Caryatid lɛ, helatsɛ lɛ ta shi, ni ekɛ afii ni eho lɛ amli entablatures ni ato lɛ fĩɔ ehiɛ ni edɔ lɛ nɔ. Nyɛshɛjea kɔɔyɔɔ yɛ jɛmɛ, nyɛ susumai ni woɔ amɛhe nɔ, ni yeɔ awerɛho lɛ! sanebimɔ nakai maŋtsɛ ni woɔ ehe nɔ, ni yeɔ awerɛho lɛ he! Weku ko ni tamɔ nakai! aye, efɔ nyɛ, nyɛ maŋtsɛmɛi oblahii kɛ oblayei ni ajie amɛ kɛjɛ nom mli lɛ; ni otsɛ ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ pɛ ji he ni Maŋ-teemɔŋ sane momo lɛ baajɛ.

Agbɛnɛ, yɛ etsui mli lɛ, Ahab ná enɛ he susumɔ fioo, ni ji: migbɛi fɛɛ yɛ jwɛŋmɔ mli, miyiŋtoo kɛ nɔ ni mikɛtsuɔ nii lɛ ji shwilafo. Shi kɛlɛ, ebɛ hewalɛ ni ekɛgbeɔ mɔ, loo etsakeɔ, loo etsiɔ ehe kɛjɛɔ anɔkwale lɛ he; lɛ hu ele akɛ ekɛ adesai feɔ ehe mfoniri be kakadaŋŋ; yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, efee lolo. Shi nakai nɔ ni ekɛfee ehe tamɔ mɔ ko lɛ damɔ bɔ ni enuɔ nii ahe ehaa lɛ pɛ nɔ, shi jeee nɔ ni esuɔmɔnaa nii ji nɔ ni tsɔɔ. Shi kɛlɛ, eye omanye jogbaŋŋ yɛ nakai sɛɛkuu lɛ mli, aahu akɛ beni ekɛ naji ni tamɔ shwuɔ wu shɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, Nantucketnyo ko bɛ ni susuuu ehe akɛ eji awerɛhoyeli ni jɛ adebɔɔ naa, ni no kɛha oyaiyeli lɛ, kɛ piŋmɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni ba enɔ lɛ.

Amaniɛbɔɔ ni kɔɔ bɔ ni egbɔjɔ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ni anyɛɛɛ akɛɛ nɔ ko lɛ he lɛ hu ji nɔ ni mɛi babaoo kɛɔ akɛ eji nɔ ko ni tamɔ nakai nɔŋŋ. Ni nakai nɔŋŋ hu, jwɛŋmɔŋ haomɔi fɛɛ ni fata he ni be fɛɛ be yɛ sɛɛ mli, kɛyashi gbi ni akɛ Pequod lɛ fãa gbɛ yɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ gbɛfaa lɛ mli lɛ, tara ehiɛ nɔ ni esusu he. Ni ebɛ faŋŋ hu akɛ, yɛ hekɛnɔfɔɔ ni bɛ akɛ ehe esa kɛha ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa kroko, yɛ okadii ni yɔɔ duŋ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ hewɔ lɛ, nakai ŋshɔkpɔ ni yɔɔ nilee lɛ nɔ bii ni buɔ akɔntaa lɛ sumɔɔ ni amɛhiɛ henɔwomɔ lɛ mli, akɛ yɛ nakai yiŋtoi lɛ ahewɔ lɛ, ehi jogbaŋŋ fe fɛɛ ni ekɛ ehe ewo mli, kɛha nitsumɔ ko ni eyi obɔ kɛ mlifu kɛ kooloi awuiyeli. Akɛ ŋmɔ mli ni ashã yɛ kponɔgbɛ, kɛ susumɔ ko ni anyɛɛɛ atsa lɛ he ŋmeii ni yɔɔ mli, ni sɛɛ efooo lɛ; mɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ, kɛji akɛ abaanyɛ ana lɛ, ebaafee tamɔ mɔ diɛŋtsɛ ni baatswa edade lɛ ni ewo e-akpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ eshi kooloi ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ lɛ. Aloo, kɛji akɛ yɛ yiŋtoo ko hewɔ lɛ asusu akɛ ebɛ nyɛmɔ yɛ gbɔmɔtsoŋ kɛha nakai feemɔ lɛ, shi kɛlɛ ebaafee tamɔ mɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ lɛ he esa waa ni ekɛ miishɛɛ baaha mɛi ni yɔɔ eshishi lɛ ayi ni amɛbolɔ amɛha tutuamɔ lɛ. Shi bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni enɛ fɛɛ ji lɛ, eji nɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ akɛ, kɛ emlifu ni bɛ naagbee lɛ he teemɔŋ sane lɛ ni ekɛwo emli lɛ, Ahab tsɔ hiɛ efã gbɛ kɛtee ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ gbɛfaa lɛ nɔ kɛ nɔ kome pɛ ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli ni ji White Whale lɛ gbee. Eji enanemɛi tsutsu lɛ ateŋ mɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ fã ko la nɔ ni yɔɔ emli yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ kulɛ, kwɛ bɔ ni etsɛŋ ni amɛsusumai ni he efee amɛ yaa ni amɛja lɛ baafo lɛlɛ lɛ mli kɛjɛ nuu ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni ji daimonio lɛ dɛŋ! Amɛkɛ amɛhe ewo ŋshɔ hiɛ gbɛfaa ni sɛɛnamɔ yɔɔ he lɛ mli, ni sɛɛnamɔ ni abaaná kɛjɛ shikatoohe lɛ mli lɛ ji dɔlai. Ekɛ eyiŋtoo ema nɔ akɛ ebaawo he nyɔmɔ ni yɔɔ ekãa, ni anyɛɛɛ ajie lɛ kpo, ni fe adebɔɔ nɔ.

No hewɔ lɛ, nɛkɛ nuumo ni hiɛ yitso ŋmɔŋ, ni sheee Nyɔŋmɔ gbeyei nɛɛ ji he ni ekɛ loomɔi miitiu Hiob ŋshɔŋloo ko yɛ je lɛŋ he fɛɛ he, ni enyiɛɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋshɔŋlooi akuu ko hiɛ, ni amɛ hu amɛteŋ mɛi titri ji mongrel renegades, kɛ mɛi ni ashwie amɛ, kɛ gbɔmɔyelɔi—ni egbɔjɔ yɛ jeŋba gbɛfaŋ hu, kɛtsɔ nyɛmɔ ni bɛ ni ji Startueright or mere unaidemick, . miishɛɛ ni anyɛɛɛ atswa ni ji henumɔ ni bɛ kɛ hiɛdɔɔ ni bɛ Stubb mli, kɛ mɛi ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko ni egbɛ eshwa Flask mli lɛ. Etamɔ nɔ ni mɛi ni tamɔ nɛkɛ, ni ato amɛhe gbɛjianɔ jogbaŋŋ lɛ, ji mɛi ni hɛl gbele ko hala amɛ krɛdɛɛ ni amɛwo amɛmli koni amɛye amɛbua lɛ yɛ e-monomaniac hejoomɔ lɛ mli. Kwɛ bɔ ni amɛha nuumo lɛ mlifu lɛ hetoo babaoo nɛkɛ—kɛtsɔ majik fɔŋ ni amɛsusumai yɔɔ nɔ lɛ nɔ, ni bei komɛi lɛ etamɔ nɔ ni ehenyɛlɔi lɛ miihe afee amɛ nɔ; White Whale lɛ amɛhenyɛlɔ ni anyɛɛɛ aŋmɛ etsui shi yɛ he lɛ tamɔ lɛ hu; bɔ ni enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ ba mli—nɔ ni White Whale lɛ ji kɛha amɛ, loo bɔ ni amɛ shishinumɔi ni bɛ jwɛŋmɔ lɛ, agbɛnɛ hu, yɛ gbɛ ko ni mli ewo duŋ, ni amɛsusuuu he lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekolɛ ebaafee tamɔ wala ŋshɔi lɛ amli daimonio kpeteŋkpele ni shwieɔ shi lɛ, —enɛmɛi fɛɛ ni abaatsɔɔ mli lɛ, ebaafee ŋshɔ mli gbee ni mli kwɔ fe bɔ ni Ishmael baanyɛ aya. Shikpɔŋ shishi shika tsuru ŋmɔ ni tsuɔ nii yɛ wɔ fɛɛ wɔmli lɛ, te aaafee tɛŋŋ ni mɔ ko aaale he ni ekɛ e-shaft lɛ yaa kɛtsɔ e-pick lɛ gbɛɛmɔ ni tsakeɔ be fɛɛ be, ni ehaa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ nɔ? Namɔ nuuu nine ni anyɛɛɛ akpee naa lɛ ni gbalaa lɛ he? Mɛɛ lɛlɛ bibioo ko ni mɛi nyɔŋmai kpawo kɛ ejwɛ tsiɔ lɛ baanyɛ adamɔ shi shiŋŋ? Nɔ kome ji akɛ, mikɛ mihe ha be kɛ he ni miyɔɔ lɛ shimɔ; shi yɛ be mli ni mɛi fɛɛ miiho oya koni amɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo lɛ akpee lɛ, amɛnaaa nɔ ko nɔ ko yɛ nakai kooloo lɛ mli ja hela ni gbeɔ mɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ.





YITSƆƆ 42. Ŋshɔ lɛ He Eyɛŋ.

Nɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo yɛŋ lɛ ji kɛha Ahab lɛ, atsɔɔ mli; nɔ ni, yɛ bei komɛi amli lɛ, eji kɛha mi lɛ, awieee he lolo.

Kɛ ajie nakai susumɔi ni yɔɔ faŋŋ fe tsutsu lɛ ni kɔɔ Moby Dick he, ni nyɛŋ atswa gbɔmɔ ko susuma shi yɛ bei komɛi amli lɛ, susumɔ kroko hu yɛ, loo moŋ, gbeyeishemɔ ni bɛ faŋŋ, ni bɛ gbɛi ni kɔɔ ehe, ni bei komɛi lɛ kɛtsɔ ehewalɛ nɔ lɛ eyeɔ mɛi krokomɛi fɛɛ anɔ kunim kwraa; ni kɛlɛ eji teemɔŋ sane ni eshwɛ fioo ni anyɛɛɛ awie he, ni eshwɛ fioo ni minijiaŋ baaje wui akɛ mikɛbaawo gbɛ ni shishinumɔ yɔɔ mli nɔ. Bɔ ni ŋshɔŋloo lɛ yɛŋ lɛ ji nɔ ni ha mihe fee mi yaa fe nɔ fɛɛ nɔ. Shi te mafee tɛŋŋ mahiɛ ahi mihe nɔ akɛ matsɔɔ mihe mli yɛ biɛ; ni kɛlɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko ni bɛ faŋŋ, ni baa trukaa nɔ lɛ, matsɔɔ mihe mli akɛ esa akɛ mafee, kɛjee nakai lɛ yitsei nɛɛ fɛɛ baanyɛ afee nɔ ko nɔ ko.

Eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ adebɔɔ mli nibii babaoo amli lɛ, eyɛŋ haa fɛo yaa hiɛ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ fɛo nɔ moŋ, tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ su kpakpa krɛdɛɛ ko ni ji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enɔ haa, tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ marble, japonica, kɛ ojarawatɛi amli lɛ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ maji srɔtoi ekpɛlɛ maŋtsɛyeli nɔyeli ko ni nɔ kwɔ yɛ nɛkɛ su nɛɛ mli lɛ nɔ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ moŋ; Pegu maŋtsɛmɛi wuji ni ji yiwalɔi, ni ji maŋtsɛmɛi wuji lɛ po kɛ gbɛi ni ji “Shɔji Eyɛŋ lɛ Nuŋtsɔ” lɛ toɔ amɛ nɔyeli he wiemɔi krokomɛi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ nyam lɛ ahe; kɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ Siam maŋtsɛmɛi ni jieɔ snoo-yɛŋ ni yɔɔ naji ejwɛ lɛ nɔŋŋ kpo yɛ maŋtsɛyeli aflaŋaa lɛ mli; kɛ Hanover aflaŋaa ni hiɛ snoo-yɛŋ tsɔne ko ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ he mfoniri kome lɛ; kɛ Austria Maŋtsɛyeli wulu lɛ, Cæsarian, ni ji gboshiniyelɔ ni yeɔ Roma nɔ lɛ, ni eyɛ maŋtsɛyeli su lɛ nɔŋŋ kɛha maŋtsɛyeli su lɛ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ nɛkɛ hiɛnyiɛmɔ ni yɔɔ mli nɛɛ kɔɔ adesai aweku lɛ diɛŋtsɛ he moŋ, shi ehaa blɔfonyo lɛ yeɔ akutso fɛɛ akutso ni yɔɔ duŋ lɛ nɔ jogbaŋŋ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ, kɛfata enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ he lɛ, afee eyɛŋ po akɛ nɔ ni tsɔɔ miishɛɛ, ejaakɛ yɛ Romabii lɛ ateŋ lɛ, tɛ yɛŋ kadiɔ miishɛɛ gbi; ni eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ gbohiiashitee he musuŋtsɔlɛi kɛ okadifeemɔi krokomɛi amli lɛ, afee nɛkɛ su nɛɛ nɔŋŋ nibii babaoo ni taa mɔ tsuiŋ, ni nɔ kwɔ lɛ ahe okadi—ayemforofoi ni bɛ efɔŋ, afii ni amɛye lɛ mlihilɛ; eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ Amerika Hii Tsuru lɛ ateŋ lɛ, wampum bɛlɛ yɛŋ lɛ ni akɛhaa lɛ ji woo shiwoo ni mli kwɔ fe fɛɛ; eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔi babaoo amli lɛ, eyɛŋ feɔ Jalɛsaneyeli lɛ anunyam yɛ Kojolɔ lɛ ermine lɛ mli lɛ he mfoniri, ni eyeɔ ebuaa maŋtsɛmɛi kɛ maŋnyɛi ni okpɔŋɔi ni tamɔ mliki lɛ gbalaa amɛ lɛ daa gbi shihilɛ; eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ jamɔi ni yɔɔ nyam fe fɛɛ lɛ amli teemɔŋ saji ni nɔ kwɔlɔ lɛ amli po lɛ, afee lɛ ŋwɛi muji kɛ hewalɛ he okadi moŋ; kɛtsɔ Persia la jalɔi lɛ anɔ lɛ, la ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ krɔŋkrɔŋ fe fɛɛ yɛ afɔleshaa latɛ lɛ nɔ; ni yɛ Hela adesãi amli lɛ, afee Jove Kpeteŋkpele lɛ diɛŋtsɛ gbɔmɔtso yɛ tsina ko ni tamɔ snoo mli; ni eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ Iroquoisbii ni yɔɔ nyam lɛ agbɛfaŋ lɛ, fɛ̃i be teŋgbɛ afɔleshaa ni ji White Dog krɔŋkrɔŋ lɛ ji amɛ Nyɔŋmɔjamɔ he gbijurɔyeli lɛ mli gbijurɔyeli krɔŋkrɔŋ fe fɛɛ, ni nakai adebɔɔ ni he bɛ muji, anɔkwafo lɛ ji maŋsɛɛ sanekpakpa shiɛlɔ ni he tse fe fɛɛ ni amɛbaanyɛ amɛtsu kɛya Mumɔ Kpeteŋkpele lɛ ŋɔɔ kɛ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ anɔkwayeli he sane ni afeɔ daa afi lɛ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ Latin wiemɔ ni tsɔɔ yɛŋ lɛ mli tɛ̃ɛ moŋ, shi Kristofoi osɔfoi fɛɛ ná amɛ atade krɔŋkrɔŋ lɛ fã kome, ni ji alb loo tunic, ni awoɔ yɛ cassock lɛ shishi lɛ gbɛi; ni eyɛ mli akɛ eji Roma hemɔkɛyeli lɛ mli fɛoyeli krɔŋkrɔŋi lɛ ateŋ moŋ, shi akɛ yɛŋ tsuɔ nii titri yɛ wɔ-Nuŋtsɔ lɛ Piŋmɔ lɛ gbijurɔyeli mli; Nɛkɛ su ni anyɛɛɛ ana nɛɛ ji nɔ ni haa susumɔ ni kɔɔ blɔfofeemɔ he lɛ, kɛji akɛ ajie kɛjɛ mlihilɛ mlihilɛ babaoo mli, ni akɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ emli fata he lɛ, haa nakai gbeyeishemɔ lɛ yaa hiɛ kɛyashɛɔ husui ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ lɛ ahe. Ye bear yɛŋ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔkei lɛ anɔ lɛ he odase, kɛ shark yɛŋ ni yɔɔ hulutsoo shikpɔji lɛ anɔ lɛ; mɛni ji amɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni haa amɛfeɔ gbeyeishemɔi ni fe fɛɛ ni amɛji lɛ? Nakai futafuta ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ ji nɔ ni haa mlijɔlɛ ni yɔɔ nyaŋemɔ tamɔ nɛkɛ, ni yɔɔ nyaŋemɔ po fe gbeyeishemɔ, kɛhaa amɛfã lɛ mli ŋmɔlɔ ni bɛ wiemɔ lɛ. Bɔni afee ni jata ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ e-heraldic atade lɛ mli lɛ akafee ekãa tamɔ bear loo shark ni hiɛ atade yɛŋ lɛ.*

*Yɛ Polar bear lɛ he lɛ, ekolɛ mɔ ni baasumɔ ni eya sane nɛɛ mli jogbaŋŋ lɛ baawo lɛ hewalɛ, akɛ jeee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, ni abuɔ lɛ srɔto lɛ ji nɔ ni haa nakai kooloo fɔŋ lɛ he gbeyeishemɔ ni anyɛɛɛ akpɛlɛ nɔ lɛ yaa hiɛ; ejaakɛ, kɛ apɛi mli lɛ, nakai hiɛgbele ni yaa hiɛ lɛ, abaanyɛ akɛɛ, teɔ shi kɛjɛɔ shihilɛ lɛ mli pɛ, akɛ adebɔɔ lɛ yiwalɛ nifeemɔ ni sɔ̃ kãaa nɔ lɛ damɔ ŋwɛi efɔŋfeemɔ kɛ suɔmɔ hetsɔi lɛ mli; ni no hewɔ lɛ, kɛtsɔ henumɔi enyɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni teɔ shi woɔ amɛhe ni ekɛbaa wɔjwɛŋmɔ mli lɛ nɔ lɛ, Polar bear lɛ woɔ wɔ gbeyei kɛ srɔtofeemɔ ni jeee adebɔɔ nɔ nɛkɛ. Shi kɛji akɛ asusu akɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ ji anɔkwale po lɛ; shi kɛlɛ, eji jeee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ kulɛ, obɛ nakai gbeyeishemɔ ni mli wa lɛ.

Yɛ shark yɛŋ lɛ gbɛfaŋ lɛ, hejɔɔmɔ ni yɔɔ nakai adebɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ, kɛ́ ana lɛ yɛ esusumɔi amli lɛ, ekɛ su kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ ni yɔɔ Polar quadruped lɛ mli lɛ kpãa gbee yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɔ. Nɛkɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ srɔto nɛɛ ji nɔ ni Frenchbii lɛ naa waa fe fɛɛ yɛ gbɛi ni amɛkɛwoɔ nakai loo lɛ mli lɛ mli. Romabii a-misa ni akɛhaa gbohii lɛ jeɔ shishi kɛ “Requiem eternam” (naanɔ hejɔɔmɔ), ni Requiem ni tsɔɔ misa lɛ diɛŋtsɛ gbɛi, kɛ gbonyo lalai krokomɛi fɛɛ lɛ jɛɔ. Amrɔ nɛɛ, yɛ gbele ni yɔɔ dioo ni yɔɔ ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ mli lɛ mli, kɛ esubaŋi ni gbeɔ mɔ fioo lɛ he wiemɔ mli lɛ, Frenchbii lɛ tsɛɔ lɛ Requin .

Susumɔ albatross lɛ he okwɛ, nɛgbɛ nakai mumɔŋ naakpɛɛ kɛ gbeyeishemɔ ni mli tseee atatui lɛ, ni nakai ŋmiŋmi yɛŋ lɛ fãa gbɛ yɛ susumɔi fɛɛ amli lɛ jɛɔ? Jeee Coleridge ji mɔ klɛŋklɛŋ ni shɛrɛ nakai shɛɛ sane lɛ; shi Nyɔŋmɔ jweremɔ nii kpeteŋkpele ni bɛ ŋmɔlɔ, Adebɔɔ * lɛ.

*Mikaiɔ klɛŋklɛŋ albatross ni mina pɛŋ lɛ. No mli lɛ, eji ahum ni tswaa be kakadaŋŋ, yɛ nu ni mli wa ni yɔɔ Antarctica ŋshɔi lɛ anɔ lɛ mli. Mijɛ mi shwane ŋmɛlɛtswaa ni yɔɔ shishi lɛ mli kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ ni atatu eyi nɔ obɔ lɛ nɔ; ni jɛmɛ, ni mikɛ mihe wo ŋwɛitsu wuji lɛ anɔ lɛ, mina maŋtsɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ he, ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ, Roma ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni nɔ kwɔ. Yɛ be kɛ bei amli lɛ, ekɛ ebɔfo nukpa lɛ fɛ̃i wuji lɛ shwieɔ shi, tamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ ehe miifu adeka krɔŋkrɔŋ ko mli. Naakpɛɛ gbeei ni tswaa kɛ gbeei ni tswaa lɛ hoso lɛ. Eyɛ mli akɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ pilaaa lɛ moŋ, shi ebolɔ, tamɔ maŋtsɛ ko susuma ni yɔɔ haomɔ ni fe adebɔɔ mli lɛ mli. Kɛtsɔ ehiŋmɛii ni anyɛɛɛ ajie lɛ kpo, ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ anɔ lɛ, misusu akɛ mikwɛɔ teemɔŋ saji ni mɔ Nyɔŋmɔ he lɛ amli. Tamɔ Abraham yɛ ŋwɛibɔfoi lɛ ahiɛ lɛ, mikoto mihe; nɔ ni yɔɔ futafuta lɛ yɛ futafuta waa, etsitsii lɛ mli lɛɛ waa, ni yɛ nakai nui ni ajie amɛ kɛya naanɔ lɛ amli lɛ, mibɛ kusumii kɛ maji ahe kaimɔi ni yɔɔ awerɛho ni fiteɔ lɛ. Mikwɛ nakai ŋmeii ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ be kakadaŋŋ. Minyɛɛɛ makɛɛ, manyɛ matsɔɔ kɛkɛ, nibii ni tsɔ mihe yɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ. Shi naagbee lɛ, mite shi; ni etsɔ ehe, ni ebi lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko akɛ mɛɛ okpo ji enɛ. A goney, eha hetoo. Etee! enuko nakai gbɛi lɛ dã; ani abaanyɛ asusu he akɛ nɛkɛ anunyam nɔ nɛɛ ji nɔ ni gbɔmɛi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ leee kwraa! daabi da! Shi be ko sɛɛ lɛ, mikase akɛ goney ji ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi komɛi agbɛi ni akɛtsɛɔ albatross. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, Coleridge ŋa nɔ Rhyme lɛ nyɛŋ afee nɔ ko kɛ nakai teemɔŋ susumɔi ni ji mi nɔ lɛ, beni mina nakai okpo lɛ yɛ wɔ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ. Ejaakɛ nakai beaŋ lɛ mi hu mikaneko Rhyme lɛ, ni mileee hu akɛ okpo lɛ ji albatross. Shi, yɛ enɛ wiemɔ mli lɛ, mikɛ lala lɛ kɛ lalafolɔ lɛ sɛɛnamɔ ni nɔ kwɔ lɛ shãa fioo yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ.

No hewɔ lɛ, mikɛɔ akɛ, yɛ okpo lɛ gbɔmɔtso ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ, shɛɛ sane lɛ he teemɔŋ sane lɛ yɔɔ titri; anɔkwale ko ni jeɔ kpo faŋŋ yɛ enɛ mli ji akɛ, kɛtsɔ wiemɔi ni akɛtsuɔ nii pɛpɛɛpɛ nɔ lɛ, okpoi ni atsɛɔ amɛ gray albatrosses lɛ yɛ; ni mina enɛɛmɛi shii abɔ, shi minaaa amɛ henumɔi tamɔ beni mina Antarctica loofɔji lɛ pɛŋ.

Shi te fee tɛŋŋ ni amɔmɔ teemɔŋ nii lɛ? Kaawie yɛ gbee bibioo mli, ni makɛɛ bo; kɛ ŋmei kɛ ŋmei ni lakaa mɔ, beni okpo lɛ shwieɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ. Naagbee lɛ, Kapten lɛ fee lɛ postman; ni akɛ niŋmaa okadii ni akɛ kooloo hewolo fee lɛ fĩɔ etsitsi he, ni akɛ lɛlɛ lɛ be kɛ he ni eyɔɔ lɛ woɔ mli; ni no sɛɛ lɛ, eha ejo foi. Shi miyiŋ efeee mi kɔshikɔshi akɛ, ajie nakai hewolo tally, ni ato he gbɛjianɔ aha gbɔmɔ lɛ yɛ Ŋwɛi, beni okpo yɛŋ lɛ fo koni ekɛ ehe awo kerubim ni shwieɔ fɛ̃i, ni tsɛɔ, ni amɛjáa lɛ mli lɛ mli lɛ!

Nɔ ni ehe gbɛi fe fɛɛ yɛ wɔ Anaigbɛ yinɔsane woji kɛ Indiabii akusum nifeemɔi amli ji Okpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ ni yɔɔ Prairies lɛ nɔ lɛ; charger fɛfɛo ko ni tamɔ mliki yɛŋ, ni hiŋmɛii wuji, eyitso bibii, ni eyɛ bluff-chest, ni eyɛ maŋtsɛmɛi akpe bulɛ yɛ etsɔne ni nɔ kwɔ ni feɔ mɔ he fɛo lɛ mli. Lɛ ji Xerxes ni ahala lɛ yɛ koo mli okpɔŋɔi akui babaoo amli, ni yɛ nakai gbii lɛ amli lɛ, Rocky Mountains kɛ Alleghanies pɛ ebɔle amɛ kooloi alɛɛhei lɛ ahe gbogbo. Yɛ amɛyitso ni tsoɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, ekɛ lɛ tee anaigbɛ tamɔ nakai ŋulami ni ahala ni daa gbɛkɛ lɛ enyiɛɔ la asraafoi lɛ anɔ lɛ. Etsitsi ni kpɛɔ, ni ji ŋulami ni yɔɔ etsitsi mli ni ebɔle ehe lɛ, ha ená tsũi ni kpɛɔ fe shika tsuru kɛ jwiɛtɛi ni akɛtswaa mɛi lɛ baanyɛ aha lɛ. Nakai anaigbɛ je ni egbeee shi lɛ mli maŋtsɛyeli kɛ bɔfo onukpa fe fɛɛ kpojiemɔ, ni yɛ tsutsu ŋshɔŋlooi kɛ gbɔbilɔi lɛ ahiŋmɛii amli lɛ, ekɛ nakai shishijee bei lɛ amli anunyam lɛ tee shi ekoŋŋ beni Adam nyiɛ kɛ nyam tamɔ nyɔŋmɔ, ni ehiɛ edɔ ni esheee gbeyei tamɔ nɛkɛ okpɔŋɔ ni he wa nɛɛ. Kɛji akɛ enyiɛ eyelikɛbualɔi kɛ asraafoi akuu lɛ mli onukpai ni yɔɔ kuu ni anyɛɛɛ akane ni hoɔ yɛ shikpɔŋ kplanaa lɛ nɔ ni bɛ naagbee lɛ ateŋ, tamɔ Ohio ko; aloo kɛji akɛ ekɛ eshishitoo mlai ni ebɔle ehe lɛ miikwɛ he fɛɛ he yɛ ŋwɛi lɛ, Okpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ kɛ oyaiyeli kwɛɔ amɛmli kɛ etsitsii ni mli edɔ lɛ ni miifee tɔrɔmɔɔ kɛtsɔ e-milkiness ni mli ejɔ lɛ nɔ; yɛ gbɛ fɛɛ gbɛ ni etsɔɔ nɔ ejieɔ ehe kpo lɛ mli lɛ, be fɛɛ be lɛ, Indiabii ni yɔɔ ekãa fe fɛɛ lɛ ji mɔ ni bulɛ kɛ gbeyeishemɔ ni hosoɔ lɛ haa lɛ. Ni anyɛɛɛ abi he sane hu kɛjɛ nɔ ni damɔ okpɔŋɔ ni nɔ kwɔ nɛɛ he adesãi amli lɛ nɔ, akɛ emumɔŋ eyɛŋfeemɔ ji nɔ titri ni wo lɛ nyɔŋmɔyeli he atade nakai; ni akɛ nɛkɛ nyɔŋmɔyeli nɛɛ yɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ mli ni, eyɛ mli akɛ efãa jamɔ moŋ, shi yɛ nakai beaŋ nɔŋŋ lɛ, ekɛ gbeyeishemɔ ko ni bɛ gbɛi baa.

Shi bei krokomɛi yɛ ni nɛkɛ ŋmɔlɔ nɛɛ laajeɔ nakai nibii kɛ anunyam ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni kɛwoɔ White Steed kɛ Albatross lɛ mli lɛ fɛɛ.

Mɛni ji nɔ ni yɔɔ Albino mli lɛ nuu lɛ shwieɔ mɔ hiŋmɛi yɛ gbɛ krɛdɛɛ ko nɔ ni bei pii lɛ ehaa mɔ he jɔ̃ɔ ehe, tamɔ bɔ ni bei komɛi lɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ewekumɛi kɛ ewekumɛi nyɛɔ lɛ! Nakai futafuta lɛ ji nɔ ni kɛ ehe woɔ mli, nɔ ko ni gbɛi ni ehiɛ lɛ jieɔ lɛ kpo. Afee Albino lɛ jogbaŋŋ tamɔ hii krokomɛi pɛpɛɛpɛ—ebɛ gbɔlɛ ko kwraa—ni kɛlɛ nɛkɛ eyɛŋ ni egbɛ eshwa he fɛɛ he nɛɛ fã kɛkɛ nɛɛ haa efeɔ mɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe musuŋtsɔmɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei fe fɛɛ lɛ. Mɛni hewɔ esa akɛ enɛ aba mli nakai lɛ?

Ni asaŋ, yɛ gbɛi krokomɛi anɔ lɛ, Adebɔɔ yɛ egbɛjianɔtoi ni anyɛɛɛ ana shi jeee egbɛjianɔtoi ni ehiii kwraa lɛ amli lɛ, nyɛɛɛ ekɛ gbeyeishemɔ su ni fe fɛɛ nɛɛ awo ehewalɛi lɛ ateŋ. Kɛjɛ bɔ ni snoo yɔɔ mli lɛ nɔ lɛ, atsɛ Ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ Anaigbɛ lɛ amli mumɔ ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi lɛ akɛ White Squall. Ni asaŋ, yɛ yinɔsane mli saji komɛi amli lɛ, adesa efɔŋfeemɔ he ŋaa lɛ ejieee yelikɛbuamɔ ni he wa waa kɛjɛ mli. Kwɛ bɔ ni nakai kuku lɛ he hewalɛ ni yɔɔ Froissart lɛ mli lɛ yaa hiɛ waa ehaa, beni, ni amɛkɛ amɛkuu lɛ he okadi ni snoo yɔɔ mli lɛ wo amɛhe lɛ, White Hoods ni yɔɔ Ghent ni enijiaŋ eje wui lɛ gbe amɛ bailiff lɛ yɛ jarayelihe lɛ!

Ni asaŋ, yɛ nibii komɛi amli lɛ, niiashikpamɔ ni afɔɔ namɔ, ni ji gboshinii ni adesai fɛɛ yɔɔ lɛ yeee bɔ ni nɛkɛ su nɛɛ fe adebɔɔ nɔ lɛ he odase. Ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ, su kome ni anaa yɛ gbohii lɛ ahe ni haa mɔ ni kwɛɔ lɛ lɛ he feɔ lɛ gbeyei fe fɛɛ lɛ ji marble pallor ni hiɔ shi yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ; tamɔ nɔ ni anɔkwale mli lɛ nakai ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ tamɔ gbeyeishemɔ he baaji yɛ je kroko lɛ mli lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ, tamɔ gbele gbeyeishemɔ ni yɔɔ biɛ lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ. Ni kɛjɛ nakai gbohii lɛ ahe ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ mli lɛ, wɔfaa atade ni wɔkɛwoɔ amɛhe lɛ mli su ni jieɔ mɔ he kpo lɛ. Ni yɛ wɔ apasa hemɔkɛyelii lɛ amli po lɛ, wɔshɛɛɛ snoo atade lɛ nɔŋŋ wɔshwieɔ wɔ ŋwɛibɔfoi lɛ ahe; mumɔi fɛɛ ni teɔ shi yɛ afua ni tamɔ mliki mli lɛ—Hɛɛ, yɛ be mli ni nɛkɛ gbeyeishemɔi nɛɛ yeɔ wɔ lɛ, nyɛhaa wɔkɛfataa he akɛ, gbeyeishemɔi amaŋtsɛ lɛ po, kɛ́ sanekpakpa jajelɔ lɛ ji mɔ ni feɔ lɛ gbɔmɔ lɛ, etaa ekpɔŋɔ ni eyɛ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ nɔ.

No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ esusumɔi krokomɛi amli lɛ, ni ekɛ eyɛŋfeemɔ feɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni da loo duromɔ ni esumɔɔ lɛ he mfoniri lɛ, gbɔmɔ ko nyɛŋ aje he ŋwane akɛ yɛ eshishinumɔ ni mli kwɔ fe fɛɛ ni asusuɔ he lɛ mli lɛ, etsɛɔ susuma lɛ kpojiemɔ krɛdɛɛ ko.

Shi eyɛ mli akɛ abaato nɛkɛ sane nɛɛ he gbɛjianɔ ni shitee-kɛ-woo bɛ mli moŋ, shi te aaafee tɛŋŋ ni gbɔmɔ ni gboɔ lɛ abu he akɔntaa? Kɛ́ wɔpɛi mli lɛ, ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni enyɛŋ eba mli. No hewɔ lɛ, ani wɔbaanyɛ kɛtsɔ nakai nɔkwɛmɔnii lɛ ekomɛi ni wɔtsɛɔ ni nɛkɛ nɔ ni ji futafuta nɛɛ—eyɛ mli akɛ ajie kɛjɛ tsakpaai fɛɛ ni yɔɔ tɛ̃ɛ ni asusu he akɛ ekɛ nɔ ko ni yɔɔ gbeyei baaha lɛ lɛ mli kwraa loo efã ko mli moŋ, shi kɛlɛ, ana akɛ ekɛ nakai abɛi lɛ nɔŋŋ miitsu nii yɛ wɔ nɔ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni atsake lɛ aha lɛ; hewɔ ni wɔtaoɔ?
