YITSƆƆ 33. Speksnyder lɛ.

Yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi akuu lɛ mli asraafoi lɛ ahe lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni enɛ ji he ko ni hi jogbaŋŋ ni abaanyɛ ato shia mli nɔ ko fioo yɛ lɛlɛ mli, ni jɛ asraafoi akuu ni ji harpooneer lɛ ahilɛ mli, kuu ko ni aleee yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kroko mli fe ŋshɔŋlooi akuu lɛ.

Hiamɔ kpele ni akɛhaa harpooneer nitsumɔ lɛ he odaseyeli ji anɔkwale ni eji, akɛ shishijee lɛ yɛ Dutch Fishery momo lɛ mli, afii ohai enyɔ kɛ nɔ ni fe nakai ni eho nɛ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi lɛlɛ he famɔ lɛ bɛ mɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ amrɔ nɛɛ akɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ dɛŋ kwraa, shi moŋ aja mli yɛ lɛ kɛ Specks asraafonyo nukpa ko ni atsɛɔ lɛ Specks lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ teŋ. Yɛ wiemɔ nɛɛ shishi diɛŋtsɛ lɛ, Fat-Cutter; shi kɛlɛ, bɔ ni akɛtsuɔ nii ahaa lɛ ha ekɛ Chief Harpooneer lɛ yeɔ egbɔ yɛ be ni sa mli. Yɛ nakai gbii lɛ amli lɛ, lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ hegbɛ ji lɛlɛ kudɔmɔ kɛ lɛlɛ lɛ nɔkwɛmɔ fɛɛ; yɛ be mli ni Specksnyder loo Chief Harpooneer lɛ yeɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔhe lɛ kɛ nibii fɛɛ ni kɔɔ he lɛ anɔ lɛ. Yɛ British Greenland Fishery lɛ mli, yɛ gbɛi ni efite ni ji Specksioneer lɛ shishi lɛ, ahiɛ Dutch maŋ onukpa onukpa nɛɛ mli lolo, shi etsutsu bulɛ lɛ efo mli kuku yɛ awerɛho gbɛ nɔ. Amrɔ nɛɛ, eyɛ gbɛhe ni ji Harpooneer onukpa kɛkɛ; ni akɛni nakai eji hewɔ lɛ, eji ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafonyo nukpa lɛ shishibii ni baa shi fe fɛɛ lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome pɛ. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, taakɛ harpooneers lɛ ajeŋba kpakpa nɔ lɛ, ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbɛfaa mli omanyeyeli damɔ babaoo nɔ lɛ, ni akɛni yɛ Amerika Fishery lɛ mli lɛ, jeee lɛlɛ mli onukpa ko ni he hiaa waa pɛ ji lɛ hewɔ lɛ, shi moŋ yɛ shihilɛi komɛi amli (gbɛkɛ bulɔi yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe nitsumɔ he ko) lɛlɛ lɛ mli ŋshɔŋlooi ahe famɔ hu ji lɛ; no hewɔ lɛ maŋkwramɔŋ shishitoo mla wulu ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ biɔ, akɛ ehi shi yɛ gbɛi mli kɛjɛ hii ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ hiɛ lɛ ahe, ni ajie lɛ kpo yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ akɛ amɛ nitsumɔ mli onukpa; eyɛ mli akɛ be fɛɛ be lɛ, amɛbuɔ lɛ akɛ amɛ kɛ amɛhe yeɔ egbɔ yɛ shihilɛ mli moŋ.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, srɔtofeemɔ kpele ni yɔɔ asraafonyo nukpa kɛ gbɔmɔ teŋ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ ji enɛ—klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ lɛ hiɔ shi yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ sɛɛ, naagbee nɔ lɛ yɛ hiɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi kɛ jarayelɔi fɛɛ amli lɛ, hefatalɔi lɛ yɛ amɛshihilɛhei yɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ ŋɔɔ; ni nakai nɔŋŋ eji yɛ Amerika ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhei lɛ ateŋ mɛi babaoo amli lɛ, harpooneers lɛ hiɔ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ fã lɛ mli. No ji akɛ, amɛyeɔ amɛ niyenii yɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ tsɔne lɛ mli, ni amɛwɔɔ he ko ni amɛkɛ lɛ wieɔ tɛ̃ɛ.

Eyɛ mli akɛ be kakadaŋŋ ni akɛyaa Southern whaling gbɛfaa mli lɛ (ni ji gbɛfaai fɛɛ ni gbɔmɔ efee pɛŋ lɛ ateŋ nɔ ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ fe fɛɛ) moŋ, shi osharai ni yɔɔ mli lɛ, kɛ miishɛɛ ni yɔɔ kuu ko mli lɛ, ni amɛ fɛɛ, nɔ ni nɔ kwɔ loo nɔ ni baa shi lɛ, damɔ amɛ sɛɛnamɔi lɛ anɔ, jeee nyɔmɔwoo ni ato he gbɛjianɔ nɔ, shi moŋ amɛ shweremɔ ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛyɔɔ kɛ hiɛdɔɔ ni amɛyɔɔ lɛ nɔ; eyɛ mli akɛ nɛkɛ nibii nɛɛ fɛɛ feɔ yɛ shihilɛi komɛi amli ni amɛfɔɔ tsɔsemɔ ni mli wa fioo fe jarayelɔi fɛɛ moŋ; shi kɛlɛ, ekɔɔɔ he eko bɔ ni nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋlooi nɛɛ baanyɛ afee tamɔ Mesopotamia weku momo ko, yɛ blema bei komɛi amli lɛ, amɛhi shi yɛ he kome; yɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ hewɔ lɛ, kɛ hoo lɛ, kponɔgbɛ nibii ni yɔɔ be mli lɛ, efɔɔɔ heloonaa nibii ni haa mɔ he jɔɔ lɛ, ni yɛ be ko mli lɛ, ajieee kɛjɛɛɛ mli. Lɛlɛŋ, Nantucket lɛlɛi ni obaana lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ lɛ miitsɔɔ e-quarter-deck lɛ mli kɛ miishɛɛ ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni nɔ ko bɛ ni asraafoi ŋshɔ hiɛ asraafoi akuu ko bɛ mli lɛ ji nɔ ni fa; dabi, ni ekɛ bulɛ ni miihe ashɛ kponɔgbɛ babaoo miihe tamɔ nɔ ni ewo maŋtsɛ atade ni ji purple lɛ, shi jeee atade ni akɛwoɔ kɔɔyɔŋ lɛlɛ mli ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ.

Ni eyɛ mli akɛ yɛ gbɔmɛi fɛɛ ateŋ lɛ, Pequod lɛ nɔkwɛlɔ lɛ ji mɔ ni kɛ nakai susumɔ ni mli ekɔɔɔ he eko lɛ ha fe fɛɛ moŋ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ woo pɛ ni ekɛha pɛŋ moŋ, shi eji toiboo ni yɔɔ faŋŋ, ni baa amrɔ nɔŋŋ; eyɛ mli akɛ ebiii mɔ ko mɔ ko ni ejie tokotai lɛ kɛjɛ enaji ahe dani ekɛ enaji afɔ ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ moŋ; ni eyɛ mli akɛ bei komɛi yɛ ni, yɛ shihilɛi pɔtɛɛi ni kɔɔ nibii ni baaba yɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ ahe lɛ hewɔ lɛ, ewie amɛ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ srɔto nɔ, kɛji eji heshibaa loo in terrorem , loo gbɛ kroko nɔ; shi kɛlɛ, jeee akɛ Kapten Ahab po leee bɔ ni ŋshɔ lɛ yɔɔ ha kɛ bɔ ni akɛtsuɔ nii ahaa lɛ he nɔ ko kwraa.

Ni ekolɛ, ebaafee nɔ ni anyɛɛɛ ana yɛ naagbee, akɛ yɛ nakai henɔi kɛ nitsumɔi lɛ asɛɛ, taakɛ atsɔɔ mli lɛ, bei komɛi lɛ ekɛ ehe woɔ atade mli; yɛ gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ, amɛkɛtsuɔ nii kɛhaa nibii krokomɛi ni kɔɔ teemɔŋ nibii ahe fe bɔ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ yɛ gbɛ ni ja nɔ akɛ amɛbaatsu he nii lɛ. Nakai sultanism pɔtɛɛ ko ni yɔɔ ejwɛŋmɔ mli, ni kulɛ ejieee ehe kpo yɛ gbɛ kpakpa nɔ lɛ tee nɔ ehi shi; kɛtsɔ nakai gbɛi lɛ anɔ lɛ, nakai sultanism lɛ nɔŋŋ batsɔ gbɔmɔtso yɛ nɔyeli ni anyɛɛɛ akpee naa lɛ mli. Ejaakɛ gbɔmɔ nilee mli nɔyeli lɛ ji nɔ ni esumɔɔ lɛ, enyɛŋ esusu nɔyeli ni anyɛɔ atsuɔ he nii, ni yɔɔ lɛ he kɔkɔɔkɔ, kɛji akɛ yelikɛbuamɔ ko jɛɛɛ kponɔgbɛ ŋaa kɛ shishitoo mlai komɛi amli, be fɛɛ be, yɛ amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛhe, nɔ ni fe loo nɔ ni bɛ sɛɛnamɔ kɛ shishitoo nɔ. Enɛ ji nɔ ni tsiɔ Nyɔŋmɔ anɔkwa maŋtsɛmɛi ni yɔɔ Maŋtsɛyeli lɛ mli lɛ anaa kɛjɛɔ je lɛŋ ŋmɔlɔi lɛ ahe kɛyaa naanɔ; ni eshiɔ woo ni nɔ kwɔ fe fɛɛ ni kɔɔyɔɔ nɛɛ baanyɛ aha, eha nakai hii ni ehe gbɛi babaoo kɛtsɔ amɛshishijee ni bɛ naagbee kɛha Ŋwɛi Inert lɛ nine ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ ni ahala lɛ nɔ, fe amɛ nɔyeli ni ŋwanejee ko bɛ he yɛ mɛi babaoo ni egboi lɛ agbɛhe lɛ nɔ. Sui kpakpai wuji ni tamɔ nɛkɛ shwereɔ yɛ nibii bibii nɛɛ amli beni maŋkwramɔŋ apasa hemɔkɛyelii ni fe nine kɛ amɛhe woɔ mli lɛ, ni yɛ maŋtsɛmɛi ashihilɛi komɛi amli lɛ, amɛkɛ hewalɛ eha kwashiai ashishi po. Shi beni, taakɛ eji yɛ Nicholas the Czar lɛ gbɛfaŋ lɛ, shikpɔŋ nɔ maŋtsɛyeli lɛ akekere ni akɛ dade fee lɛ bɔleɔ maŋtsɛ jwɛŋmɔ ko he lɛ; no sɛɛ lɛ, plebeian tooi akui lɛ shwieɔ amɛhe shi dani amɛfeɔ maŋ lɛ teŋgbɛ kpeteŋkpele lɛ. Ni nakai nɔŋŋ hu, drama ŋmalɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho ni baajie gbele ni anyɛɛɛ aye nɔ kunim yɛ egbɛjianɔtoo kɛ egbɛjianɔtoo ni yaa hiɛ tɛ̃ɛ lɛ mli lɛ hiɛ kpaaa nɔ ko nɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ, ni he hiaa waa yɛ enitsumɔ mli, tamɔ nɔ ni awieɔ he bianɛ lɛ.

Shi Ahab, mi Kapten lɛ, nyiɛɔ mihiɛ lolo yɛ e-Nantucket ŋmɔlɔ kɛ ŋmɔlɔ fɛɛ mli; ni yɛ nɛkɛ sane ni kɔɔ Maŋtsɛmɛi kɛ Maŋtsɛmɛi ahe nɛɛ mli lɛ, esaaa akɛ mikɛteeɔ akɛ mikɛ ohiafo ko ni ji ŋshɔŋlooi ahe gbee tamɔ lɛ pɛ yɔɔ; ni, no hewɔ lɛ, akpooɔ mi kponɔgbɛ anunyam tsɔnei kɛ tsũi fɛɛ. Oo, Ahab! nɔ ni baafee nɔ ni da yɛ omli lɛ, esa akɛ ajie kɛjɛ ŋwɛi, ni ajie lɛ awo ŋshɔ lɛ mli, ni ajie lɛ kpo yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ni bɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ mli!





YITSƆƆ 34. Kabin-Okpɔlɔ lɛ.

Eshɛ shwane; ni Dough-Boy, ni ji shĩa nɔkwɛlɔ lɛ, ni ekɛ ehiɛ ni tamɔ blodoo ni mli tse lɛ shwieɔ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ mli lɛ, etswaa gbɛkɛ niyenii he adafi etsɔɔ enuŋtsɔ kɛ enuŋtsɔ lɛ; ni, eta lee quarter-boat lɛ mli lɛ, ekwɛ hulu lɛ mli pɛŋ; ni amrɔ nɛɛ eebu latitude lɛ he akɔntaa yɛ tɛtrɛɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni tamɔ medallion, ni ato aha nakai daa gbi yiŋtoo lɛ yɛ enane ni akɛ shwuɔ wu fee lɛ ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ. Kɛjɛ bɔ ni ekɛ ejwɛŋmɔ emaaa sane lɛ nɔ kwraa lɛ nɔ lɛ, obaasusu akɛ Ahab ni mli efu lɛ enuko ehe ni ekɛbaa shi lɛ he. Shi amrɔ nɛɛ, ni emɔ mizen atadei lɛ amli lɛ, efo ehe kɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ yiteŋ, ni ekɛ gbee ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni bɛ miishɛɛ kɛɛ, “Gbi gbɛkɛ niyenii, Mr. Starbuck,” elaaje kɛtee lɛlɛ lɛ mli.

Beni e-sultan lɛ najiaŋdamɔlɔi lɛ anaagbee gbee lɛ egbo, ni Starbuck, klɛŋklɛŋ Emir lɛ yɛ yiŋtoo fɛɛ yiŋtoo ni ekɛaasusu akɛ eta shi lɛ, no lɛ Starbuck teɔ shi kɛjɛɔ eshihilɛ ni yɔɔ dioo lɛ mli, ni etsɔɔ tsei lɛ anɔ fioo, ni, beni ekwɛ gbonyobu lɛ mli jogbaŋŋ sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛɛ, kɛ miishɛɛ ko ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli, Mr. de, “Dinner. Emir ni ji enyɔ lɛ kwɛɔ tsɔne lɛ he be fioo, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ekpokpoɔ tsɔne titri lɛ fioo, koni ena kɛji ebaahi jogbaŋŋ kɛ nakai kpã ni he hiaa lɛ, lɛ hu ewoɔ jatsu momo lɛ, ni ekɛ oyaiyeli ni ji “Gbi gbɛkɛ niyenii, Mr. Flask,” nyiɛɔ mɛi ni tsɔ ehiɛ lɛ asɛɛ.

Shi etamɔ nɔ ni Emir ni ji etɛ lɛ, ni amrɔ nɛɛ enaa ehe pɛ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ lɛ fã lɛ nɔ lɛ, enuɔ he akɛ ehe ejɔ lɛ kɛjɛ shwelɛ ko ni tsiɔ mɔ naa lɛ mli; ejaakɛ, beni ekɛ hiŋmɛii ni le nii srɔtoi fɛɛ shwieɔ shi yɛ hei srɔtoi fɛɛ, ni ekɛ etsitsii shwieɔ ekotokui lɛ anɔ lɛ, etswaa gbee ni naa wa shi ebɛ gbee ko ni tamɔ gbee ni yɔɔ Grand Turk lɛ yitso nɔ tɔ̃ɔ; ni no sɛɛ lɛ, kɛtsɔ ŋaa ni yɔɔ ŋaa nɔ lɛ, ekɛ etsitsi lɛ shwieɔ mizentop lɛ nɔ kɛha shwɛmɔhe ko, eyaa shi ni ekɛ ehe shwieɔ shi kɛyashɛɔ he ko ni kɛ hoo lɛ, ehiɔ shi ni anaa kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ sɛɛkuu krokomɛi fɛɛ kuɔ sɛɛ, kɛtsɔ sɛɛgbɛ ni ekɛ lala baa lɛ nɔ. Shi dani eboteɔ tsɔne lɛ shinaa ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ lɛ mli lɛ, emɛɔ, ni ekɛ hiɛ hee ko kwraa tsuɔ nii, ni, no sɛɛ lɛ, Flask bibioo ni yɔɔ eheyeli, ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ lɛ boteɔ Maŋtsɛ Ahab hiɛ, yɛ Abjectus, loo Nyɔŋ lɛ su mli.

Jeee nɔ ni baa shi fe fɛɛ yɛ naakpɛɛ nibii ni ŋshɔ hiɛ nibii ni akɛtsuɔ nii ni akɛfee nibii ni mli wa lɛ kɛbaa lɛ ateŋ, akɛ, yɛ be mli ni asraafoi onukpai komɛi yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, kɛji amɛmli efu amɛ lɛ, amɛkɛ ekãa kɛ shitee-kɛ-woo ni fa bɔ ni sa baaha amɛ tatsɛ lɛ; shi kɛlɛ, nyɔŋma kɛ ekome, ha nakai asraafoi lɛ diɛŋtsɛ aya amɛ kusum gbɛkɛ niyenii lɛ yɛ nakai asraafonyo nukpa lɛ tsu lɛ nɔŋŋ mli yɛ be ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ mli, ni amɛjie amɛhe kpo amrɔ nɔŋŋ, ni jeee akɛ amɛjie heshibaa kɛ heshibaa kɔɔyɔɔ kpo amɛtsɔɔ lɛ, beni eta okpɔlɔ lɛ yitso lɛ; enɛ ji naakpɛɛ nii, bei komɛi lɛ ehaa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli fe fɛɛ. Mɛni hewɔ srɔtofeemɔ nɛɛ? Naagba ko? Ekolɛ jeee nakai. Koni eji Belshazar, Babilon Maŋtsɛ lɛ; ni kɛji akɛ eji Belshazar, jeee yɛ henɔwomɔ mli shi moŋ yɛ bulɛ mli lɛ, no lɛ, ekolɛ eji nɔ ko ni tsɔɔ akɛ eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi. Shi mɔ ni yɛ maŋtsɛyeli kɛ nilee mumɔ ni ja mli lɛ kwɛɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-teemɔŋ gbɛkɛ niyeli okpɔlɔ ni gbɔi ni afɔ̃ amɛ nine lɛ yeɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ, nakai gbɔmɔ lɛ hewalɛ kɛ nɔyeli ni anyɛɛɛ akaŋ aŋkroaŋkroi ahewalɛ ni eyɔɔ kɛha nakai be lɛ; maŋtsɛyeli ni nakai nuu lɛ yɔɔ lɛ fe Belshazar nɔ lɛ, ejaakɛ jeee Belshazar ji mɔ ni da fe fɛɛ. Mɔ ni eye enanemɛi niyenii shikome pɛ lɛ, eka nɔ ni Cæsar ji lɛ ekwɛ. Eji shihilɛ mli maŋtsɛyeli he abɛi ni mɔ ko mɔ ko nyɛŋ adamɔ shi. Amrɔ nɛɛ, kɛji akɛ okɛ lɛlɛ kudɔlɔ ko nɔyeli ni yɔɔ maŋ lɛ mli lɛ fata susumɔ nɛɛ he lɛ, no lɛ, kɛtsɔ susumɔ nɔ lɛ, obaana nɔ ni kɛ nakai ŋshɔ mli wala ni yɔɔ srɔto ni atsĩ tã kɛkɛ nɛɛ ba lɛ.

Ahab nyiɛ okpɔlɔ ni akɛ shwuɔ wu ewula lɛ nɔ tamɔ ŋshɔ mli jata ko ni wieɔ ni ehiɛ tsitsi ni yɔɔ ŋshɔnaa koral yɛŋ lɛ nɔ, ni ebii ni sumɔɔ tawuu shi amɛbuɔ mɔ lolo lɛ ebɔle ehe. Yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ egbɛ ni sa mli lɛ, asraafonyo nukpa fɛɛ asraafonyo nukpa mɛɔ koni asɔmɔ lɛ. Amɛji gbekɛbii bibii yɛ Ahab hiɛ; ni kɛlɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni shihilɛ mli henɔwomɔ bibioo ko kwraa bɛ Ahab mli. Kɛ jwɛŋmɔ kome lɛ, amɛhiŋmɛii ni yɔɔ yiŋtoo lɛ fɛɛ kwɛ nuumo lɛ ŋmei lɛ nɔ, beni ekpɛɔ niyenii titri ni yɔɔ ehiɛ lɛ. Misusuuu akɛ kɛha je lɛ kulɛ amɛbaafite nakai be lɛ kɛ nɔ ko bibioo ko pɛ ni amɛkwɛɔ, yɛ saneyitso ko ni kɛ ehe wooo maŋ saji amli tamɔ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ po nɔ. Daabi! Ni beni Ahab shɛ e-knife kɛ fork, ni tsina loo fã lɛ efo mli lɛ he lɛ, ekɛ enine tsɔɔ Starbuck tsɛnsi lɛ nɔ kɛbaa eŋɔɔ lɛ, hefatalɔ lɛ nine shɛ eloo lɛ nɔ tamɔ nɔ ni enine shɛɔ ejurɔfeemɔ nɔ; ni ofo lɛ blɛoo; ni ebɔi fioo kɛji, ekolɛ, ŋmei lɛ tswa plɛte lɛ; ni ekɛyeɔ nii ni efeee gbee; ni emi lɛ, jeee akɛ hiɛdɔɔ bɛ mli. Ejaakɛ, tamɔ Maŋtsɛyeli lɛ nɔyeli okpɔlɔŋmɛɛ ni tee nɔ yɛ Frankfort, he ni German Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛ Maŋtsɛyeli lɛ Shiɛlɔi kpawo lɛ yeɔ niyenii yɛ mli waa lɛ, nakai nɔŋŋ nɛkɛ shĩa mli niyenii nɛɛ ji niyenii ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, ni ayeɔ yɛ dioofeemɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei mli; ni kɛlɛ yɛ okpɔlɔ nɔ lɛ, Ahab onukpa lɛ guuu sanegbaa; lɛ diɛŋtsɛ pɛ ji mumu. Mɛɛ hejɔɔmɔ eji nɛkɛ akɛ aaatsi Stubb naa, beni ŋmei ko fee ŋmei trukaa yɛ ŋmei ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ lɛ mli lɛ. YITSƆƆ 35. Mast-Yitso lɛ.

Yɛ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ ni ŋɔɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ mli ji be ni yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi krokomɛi lɛ atsakemɔ ni sa mli lɛ, mi klɛŋklɛŋ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ yitso lɛ babɔle ehe.

Yɛ Amerika ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ mɛi babaoo amli lɛ, akwɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ayitsei lɛ anɔ yɛ nɔ ni miihe ashɛ be kome mli kɛ lɛlɛ lɛ shĩɔ e-lɛjiadaamɔhe lɛ; eyɛ mli akɛ ekolɛ eyɛ kilomitai akpei nyɔŋma kɛ enumɔ, kɛ nɔ ni fe nakai, ni ekɛbaafã gbɛ dani eshɛ he ni sa ni ekɛfãa gbɛ lɛ. Ni kɛji akɛ, yɛ afii etɛ, ejwɛ, loo enumɔ gbɛfaa sɛɛ lɛ, eebɛŋkɛ shia kɛ nɔ ko ni bɛ nɔ ko yɛ emli—kɛ wɔkɛɛ lɛ, tsinsii ni nɔ bɛ po—no lɛ, akwɛɔ e-mast-heads lɛ anɔ ni mɛi yɔɔ mli kɛyashiɔ naagbee; ni jeee kɛyashi be mli ni e-skysail-poles lɛ baatsɔ lɛlɛdaamɔhe lɛ ŋwɛitsu lɛ teŋ lɛ, no mli lɛ eshiɔ hiɛnɔkamɔ akɛ ebaamɔ ŋshɔŋloo kome kroko lɛ kwraa.

Amrɔ nɛɛ, akɛni ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ yitsei ni damɔ shi, yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa loo yɛ nu lɛ hiɛ lɛ he nitsumɔ lɛ ji blema nɔ ko ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ waa hewɔ lɛ, nyɛhaa wɔtsɔɔ mli yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ yɛ biɛ. Misusuɔ akɛ, mɛi ni damɔ mast-yitsei lɛ anɔ mra fe fɛɛ lɛ ji blema Mizraimbii lɛ; ejaakɛ, yɛ minibii amlitaomɔi lɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, minaaa eko kwraa dani amɛba. Ejaakɛ eyɛ mli akɛ ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ esa akɛ amɛ blematsɛmɛi, ni ji Babel tsumalɔi lɛ, kɛtsɔ amɛ ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ lɛ, amɛto amɛyiŋ akɛ amɛbaawo ŋwɛitsu yitso ni nɔ kwɔ fe fɛɛ yɛ Asia fɛɛ, loo Afrika hu nɔ moŋ; shi kɛlɛ (dani akɛ naagbee lɔle lɛ wo mli) taakɛ abaanyɛ akɛɛ nakai amɛ tɛ tsɔne wulu lɛ tsɔ tsɔne lɛ he, yɛ Nyɔŋmɔ mlifu ahum ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ mli lɛ; no hewɔ lɛ, wɔnyɛŋ wɔha nɛkɛ Babel tsutswalɔi nɛɛ aye hiɛ fe Mizraimbii lɛ. Ni akɛ Mizraimbii lɛ ji maŋ ni damɔ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai anɔ lɛ, ji wiemɔ ko ni damɔ hemɔkɛyeli ni yɔɔ shitsalɔi ateŋ lɛ nɔ, akɛ ato klɛŋklɛŋ piramid lɛ shishi kɛha ŋulamiiaŋkwɛmɔ yiŋtoi: susumɔ ko ni nakai tsũi lɛ afãi ejwɛ lɛ fɛɛ ni tamɔ ŋtayaai ni yɔɔ srɔto lɛ fiɔ sɛɛ; ni no hewɔ lɛ, kɛ amɛnaji ni amɛwoɔ nɔ kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ, nakai ŋulamiiaŋkwɛlɔi momo lɛ fɔɔ ŋwɛi ŋwɛi ŋwɛi, ni amɛláa kɛhaa ŋulamii heei; po taakɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ lɛlɛ mli kwɛmɔi lɛ laa lala kɛhaa ŋshɔŋloo ko, loo ŋshɔŋloo ko ni hiɛ kɛkɛ ni anaa lɛ. Yɛ Saint Stylites, Kristofonyo ni ehe gbɛi waa yɛ blema beaŋ, ni ma tɛ akulashiŋ ko ni nɔ kwɔ eha lɛ yɛ ŋa lɛ nɔ ni ekɛ eshihilɛ naagbee fã muu lɛ fɛɛ hi eyiteŋgbɛ, ni ekɛ tsɔne ko ni akɛwoɔ niyenii mli lɛ woɔ eniyenii nɔ kɛjɛɔ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ lɛ; yɛ emli lɛ wɔyɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ ni sa kadimɔ waa ni kɔɔ mɔ ko ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ ni bɛ gbeyeishemɔ he; ni esaaa akɛ afua loo fɛ̃i, nugbɔ, nyɔŋmɔŋtɛi, loo hulutsoo be shwieee lɛ kɛjɛɔ eshihilɛhe lɛ; shi ekɛ ekãa kpe nɔ fɛɛ nɔ kɛyashi naagbee lɛ, ni egbo diɛŋtsɛ yɛ egbɛhe lɛ. Yɛ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ standers-of-mast-heads lɛ ateŋ lɛ, wɔyɛ kuu ko ni wala bɛ mli pɛ; tɛi, dade, kɛ akɔɔble hii kɛkɛ; ni, eyɛ mli akɛ amɛbaanyɛ amɛkpe ahum ni naa wa ko naa jogbaŋŋ moŋ, shi amɛhe esaaa kwraa lolo yɛ lalatswaa nitsumɔ lɛ mli kɛji amɛna nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni amɛnaa. Napoleon yɛ; ni, yɛ Vendome ŋwɛitsu lɛ yiteŋ lɛ, edamɔ shi ni ekɛ eniji efolɔ ehe, aaafee ninetalɔi oha kɛ nyɔŋmai enumɔ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli; mɛi ni bɛ hiɛdɔɔ, amrɔ nɛɛ, mɔ ni yeɔ ŋshɔkei ni yɔɔ shishi lɛ anɔ; kɛji Louis Philippe, Louis Blanc, loo Louis Abonsam jio. Washington Kpeteŋkpele lɛ hu damɔ ŋwɛi shɔɔŋ yɛ e-mast wulu ni kwɔ waa lɛ nɔ yɛ Baltimore, ni tamɔ Hercules akulashiŋi lɛ ateŋ ekome lɛ, e-column lɛ kadiɔ nakai adesa agbojee he ni gbɔmɛi fioo ko pɛ ni gboiɔ lɛ baatsɔ hiɛ. Admiral Nelson, lɛ hu, yɛ tu-dade tsɔne ko nɔ lɛ, damɔ e-mast yitso lɛ nɔ yɛ Trafalgar Square; ni be fɛɛ be ni nakai London lasu lɛ haa efeɔ duŋ fe fɛɛ lɛ, ahaa okadi lolo akɛ henyɛlɔ ko ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ yɛ jɛmɛ; ejaakɛ he ni lasu yɔɔ lɛ, esa akɛ la ahi jɛmɛ. Shi Washington kpeteŋkpele lɛ, loo Napoleon, loo Nelson lɛ haŋ nyɔŋmɔŋtɛi kome po hetoo kɛjɛ shishigbɛ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni amɛ ŋaawoi lɛ kɛ shwilafoi tsɛɔ amɛ koni amɛkɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ ŋtayaai ni amɛkwɛɔ nɔ lɛ afee naanyo; shi kɛlɛ abaanyɛ asusu akɛ, amɛmumɔi lɛ boteɔ wɔsɛɛ be mli afua ni mli ewo wu lɛ mli, ni amɛbuɔ nɔ ni ji ŋshɔkei kɛ nɔ ni ji tɛi ni esa akɛ ajie kɛjɛ mli lɛ he guɔ.

Ekolɛ ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni esaaa akɛ akɛ shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ mɛi ni damɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ yitsei lɛ kɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ mɛi lɛ baafata he yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ; shi akɛ yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ jeee nakai eji lɛ, nɔ ko ni Obed Macy, ni ji Nantucket yinɔsaneŋmalɔ kome pɛ lɛ buɔ he akɔntaa lɛ yeɔ he odase faŋŋ. Obed ni sa lɛ kɛɛ wɔ akɛ, yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛhe lɛ shishijee bei lɛ amli, dani abaaje lɛlɛi amli daa koni amɛyatiu shwɛmɔ lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, nakai ŋshɔkpɔ lɛ nɔ bii lɛ ma ŋshɔŋlooi ni nɔ kwɔlɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni mɛi ni kwɛɔ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ anɔ lɛ tsɔɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni akɛ dade ekpɛtɛ amɛhe lɛ nɔ amɛkwɔɔ kɛyaa jɛmɛ, nɔ ko ni tamɔ ŋshɔŋlooi ni yɔɔ kooloi amli lɛ. Afii fioo komɛi ni eho nɛ lɛ, nɛkɛ gbɛjianɔtoo nɛɛ nɔŋŋ ji nɔ ni Bay whalemen ni yɔɔ New Zealand lɛ kpɛlɛ nɔ, ni beni amɛbu shwɛmɔ lɛ he guɔ lɛ, amɛha lɛlɛi ni yɔɔ klalo ni bɛŋkɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ le. Shi kusum nɛɛ ebafee nɔ ni be eho agbɛnɛ; no sɛɛ lɛ wɔtsɔ wɔhe kɛya ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ yitso kome ni sa lɛ nɔ, ni ji ŋshɔŋ loofɔji lɛlɛ ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ. Akɛ gbɔmɛi toɔ mast-yitsei etɛ lɛ ahe kɛjɛɔ hulushitee mli kɛyashiɔ hulushinyɔɔ mli; ŋshɔ hiɛ nitsulɔi lɛ ni amɛtsakeɔ amɛhe daa (tamɔ bɔ ni amɛfeɔ yɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kudɔmɔ he lɛ), ni amɛhaa amɛhe jɔɔ amɛ daa ŋmɛlɛtswai enyɔ. Yɛ hulutsoo shikpɔji lɛ anɔ kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔ ni yɔɔ toiŋjɔlɛ lɛ mli lɛ, mast-head lɛ ŋɔɔ waa diɛŋtsɛ; daabi, kɛha gbɔmɔ ni jwɛŋɔ nii anɔ yɛ lamɔ mli lɛ, eji nɔ ni ŋɔɔ. Jɛmɛ ji he ni odamɔ, ni ji ninetalɔi oha kɛjɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛi ni yɔɔ dioo lɛ anɔ, ni onyiɛɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli, tamɔ nɔ ni ŋshɔ lɛ mli ŋshɔkei lɛ ji ŋshɔkei wuji, yɛ be mli ni ŋshɔ lɛ mli kooloi wuji ni fe fɛɛ lɛ miiho yɛ oshishi kɛ onaji ateŋ, taakɛ be ko ni eho lɛ lɛlɛi nyiɛɔ yɛ Kolos ni ehe gbɛi waa lɛ atadei ateŋ yɛ Rodo blema lɛ po. Jɛmɛ ji he ni odamɔ, ni olaaje yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli nibii ni bɛ naagbee lɛ amli, ni nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ ni shwieɔ shi fe ŋshɔkei lɛ. Lɛlɛ ni etsɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ shwieɔ shi ni ebɛ hewalɛ; jarayeli kɔɔyɔɔ ni haa mɔ wɔɔ lɛ tswaa; nɔ fɛɛ nɔ tsuɔ bo he nii kɛyaa languor mli. Yɛ bei babaoo mli lɛ, yɛ nɛkɛ tropic whaling shihilɛ nɛɛ mli lɛ, nɔ ko ni nɔ ko bɛ mli ni nɔ kwɔ waa kɛ bo woɔ mli; onuuu adafitswaa ko; kaneee woji tɛtrɛbii; nibii krokomɛi ni fata he ni yɔɔ hei ni afɔɔ feemɔ lɛ ahe saji ni haa mɔ naa kpɛɔ ehe lɛ lakaaa bo kɛyaaa miishɛɛ ni he ehiaaa mli kɔkɔɔkɔ; onuuu shia piŋmɔ ko he; shika helɛtemɔ nibii ni efite; shika ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ gbee shi; nɔ ni obaaye gbɛkɛ niyenii lɛ he susumɔ gbaaa amɛnaa kɔkɔɔkɔ—ejaakɛ niyenii fɛɛ ni oye afii etɛ kɛyaa lɛ, akɛtoɔ tsɛnsii amli jogbaŋŋ, ni otsɔne he nyɔmɔwoo lɛ tsakeee.

Yɛ nakai anaigbɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome mli lɛ, yɛ afii etɛ loo ejwɛ gbɛfaa kakadaŋŋ mli, taakɛ bei pii lɛ ebaa lɛ, ŋmɛlɛtswai srɔtoi ni oyeɔ yɛ ŋshɔŋlooi lɛ ayitso lɛ fɛɛ baafee nyɔji muu babaoo. Ni eji nɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho waa akɛ he ni okɛ oshihilɛ be lɛ fɛɛ fã ko haa lɛ, esa akɛ efee nɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho waa akɛ nɔ ko bɛ ni bɛŋkɛɔ shihilɛhe ni hi jogbaŋŋ, loo atsake koni ekɛ henumɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ aba, tamɔ nɔ ni kɔɔ saatso, tsɔne bibioo ko he, tsɔne bibioo ko ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli, loo tsɔne kroko ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ he. kɛ snug contrivances ni hii tsiɔ amɛhe kɛjɛɔ mɛi ahe yɛ be kukuoo mli. He ni ofɔɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛ ji t’ gallant-mast lɛ yitso, he ni odamɔɔ tsei bibii enyɔ ni kɛ amɛhe kpãa gbee (ni miihe ashɛ ŋshɔŋlooi ahe) ni atsɛɔ amɛ t’ gallant cross-trees lɛ anɔ lɛ. Biɛ, ni ŋshɔ lɛ shwieɔ lɛ lɛ, mɔ ni jeɔ shishi lɛ nuɔ he akɛ eyɛ hejɔɔmɔ tamɔ bɔ ni ebaanu he akɛ edamɔ tsina akoloŋtoi anɔ lɛ. Kɛha nɔmimaa lɛ, yɛ fɛ̃i be mli lɛ obaanyɛ okɛ oshia lɛ aya ŋwɛi, yɛ watsi-atade he mfoniri mli; shi kɛ aawie he jogbaŋŋ lɛ, watsi atade ni mli wa fe fɛɛ lɛ jeee shia fe gbɔmɔtso ni awooo lɛ atade lɛ; ejaakɛ taakɛ susuma lɛ ekpɛtɛ eheloo buu lɛ mli, ni enyɛɛɛ efã gbɛ yɛ mli, ni enyɛŋ eshi mli po, ni ekɛ ehe wooo oshara kpele mli akɛ ebaafite (tamɔ gbɛfalɔ ko ni leee nɔ ko ni foɔ Alps gɔji ni snoo yɔɔ mli lɛ yɛ fɛ̃i be mli lɛ); no hewɔ lɛ watsi-kotu jeee shia babaoo tamɔ bɔ ni eji wolokpo ko kɛkɛ, loo hewolo kroko ni fata he ni ha ohe lɛ. Onyɛŋ okɛ shɛlɛf loo adeka awo ogbɔmɔtso lɛ mli, ni onyɛŋ okɛ owatsi-atade lɛ afee tsɔne ni hi jogbaŋŋ dɔŋŋ.

Yɛ enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ he lɛ, eji nɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho waa akɛ, abɛ nakai buui bibii loo wiemɔ kpokui ni haa mɔ he jɔ̃ɔ ehe lɛ, ni atsɛɔ amɛ crow’s-nests , ni abuɔ Greenland ŋshɔŋloo ko hiɛkwɛmɔhei lɛ ahe kɛjɛɔ ŋshɔi ni edɔ lɛ amli kɔɔyɔŋ tsakemɔi ni ehiii lɛ ahe lɛ. Yɛ Kapten Sleet la he sanegbaa lɛ mli, ni yitso ji “Gbɛfaa yɛ Ais Gɔji lɛ ateŋ, yɛ Greenland Whale lɛ taomɔ mli, kɛ gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ, koni ana Icelandbii Akutsei ni Elaaje ni yɔɔ Greenland Blema lɛ ekoŋŋ;” yɛ wolo ni sa yijiemɔ nɛɛ mli lɛ, akɛ shihilɛ mli amaniɛbɔɔ ko ni yɔɔ fɛo ni kɔɔ nakai beaŋ ni afee nyɛsɛɛ nɛɛ Glacier ŋshɔŋlooi akuu ni ji ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ kpakpa lɛ gbɛi lɛ he lɛ eha mɛi ni damɔ ŋshɔ hiɛ lɛlɛ yitsei anɔ lɛ fɛɛ. Etsɛ lɛ Sleet ŋmei ŋtayaa , koni ekɛwo lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe hiɛ nyam; lɛ ji mɔ ni fee nibii nɛɛ kɛ mɔ ni yɔɔ hegbɛ lɛ, ni eye ehe kɛjɛ amale nibii ni ŋɔɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ fɛɛ he, ni ehiɛ mli akɛ kɛji wɔtsɛ wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔbii lɛ yɛ wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔgbɛi amli (wɔ tsɛmɛi ji mɛi ni fee nibii nɛɛ shishijee kɛ mɛi ni yɔɔ hegbɛ lɛ), no lɛ nakai nɔŋŋ esa akɛ wɔwo wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔhe gbɛi kɛha nibii krokomɛi ni wɔbaanyɛ wɔfɔ lɛ. Yɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ ha lɛ mli lɛ, Sleet ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ji nɔ ko tamɔ tierce loo paipii agbo ko; shi kɛlɛ, egbele yɛ ŋwɛi, he ni akɛ sɛɛ-kɛ-sɛɛ ni ajieɔ lɛ ewo mli koni ekɛ oyitso he kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ akashɛ he yɛ ahum ni naa wa mli lɛ. Akɛni okɛ ohe fɔ̃ɔ ŋshɔ lɛ yiteŋ hewɔ lɛ, okwɔɔ kɛtsɔɔ tsɔne bibioo ko ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ lɛ nɔ kɛboteɔ mli. Yɛ sɛɛgbɛ, loo sɛɛgbɛ ni bɛŋkɛ lɛlɛ lɛ sɛɛ lɛ, ayɛ sɛi ko ni yɔɔ fɛo, ni tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ mli lɛ yɔɔ shishi ni akɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai, miishɛjemɔ atadei, kɛ atadei woɔ mli. Yɛ hiɛ lɛ, tsɔne ko ni akɛ kooloo hewolo fee, ni obaanyɛ okɛ owiemɔ tɛtɛrɛmantɛrɛ, paipi, tɛleskopu, kɛ ŋshɔ hiɛ nibii krokomɛi awo mli. Beni Kapten Sleet diɛŋtsɛ damɔ e-mast-head yɛ e-crow’s-nest nɛɛ mli lɛ, ekɛɛ wɔ akɛ be fɛɛ be lɛ, ehiɛ tu (ni akɛwo tsɔne lɛ mli hu), kɛ powder flask kɛ tu, kɛ yiŋtoo akɛ ebaajie narwhales ni egbɛ eshwa, loo vagters sea unicorns ni yeɔ nakai wa lɛ; ejaakɛ onyɛɛɛ otswa amɛ tu kɛjɛ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ yɛ nu lɛ shitee-kɛ-woo lɛ hewɔ, shi ni oootswa amɛ tu lɛ ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto kwraa. Amrɔ nɛɛ, eji suɔmɔ nitsumɔ ko ni yɔɔ faŋŋ kɛha Kapten Sleet akɛ ebaatsɔɔ, taakɛ efeɔ lɛ, nibii bibii ni yɔɔ fitsofitso ni yɔɔ e-crow’s-nest lɛ mli lɛ fɛɛ mli; shi eyɛ mli akɛ etsɔɔ enɛɛmɛi ateŋ babaoo mli babaoo nakai, ni eyɛ mli akɛ ekɛ jeŋ nilee mli amaniɛbɔɔ ko ni kɔɔ ekasemɔi ni efee yɛ nɛkɛ ŋshɔŋloo nɛɛ mli lɛ he lɛ haa wɔ moŋ, shi ekɛ kɔmpas bibioo ko to jɛmɛ kɛ yiŋtoo akɛ ebaatsi tɔmɔi ni jɛɔ nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ “he ni akɛgbalaa nii” ni binnacle magnets fɛɛ yɔɔ lɛ mli baa lɛ naa; tɔmɔ ko ni abaanyɛ akɛto dade ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ lɛ tsei lɛ amli lɛ he ni ebɔle ehe lɛ he, ni yɛ Glacier lɛ gbɛfaŋ lɛ, ekolɛ, dadefeelɔi ni efite babaoo ni yɔɔ lɛlɛ mli nitsulɔi lɛ ateŋ lɛ; Mikɛɔ akɛ, eyɛ mli akɛ Kapten lɛ ji mɔ ko ni le nii waa ni etsɔɔ jeŋ nilee yɛ biɛ moŋ, shi kɛlɛ, yɛ “binnacle deviations” ni ekase lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ, “azimuth compass observations,” kɛ “tɔmɔi ni miihe ashɛ” lɛ, ele jogbaŋŋ, Kapten Sleet, akɛ ekɛ ehe wooo nakai magnetic littled meditations ni mli kwɔ lɛ amli tsɔ, ni ekɛ ehe wooo mli . case-bottle, ni akɛwo e-crow shĩa lɛ fã kome mli fɛfɛo, ni enine baanyɛ ashɛ he oya. Eyɛ mli akɛ, kɛ asusu fɛɛ he lɛ, mináa Kapten ni yɔɔ ekãa, anɔkwafo, kɛ nikaselɔ lɛ he miishɛɛ waa ni misumɔɔ lɛ po moŋ; shi kɛlɛ, mibuɔ lɛ akɛ eji nɔ ni ehiii kwraa akɛ ebaaku ehiɛ eshwie nakai saji-tsɛnsi lɛ nɔ kwraa, ni ena bɔ ni esa akɛ efee naanyo anɔkwafo kɛ miishɛjelɔ, yɛ be mli ni ekɛ eniji ni akɛ tsitsii ebɔ he kɛ eyitso ni akɛ tsitsii ebɔ he lɛ miikase akɔntaabuu yɛ ŋwɛi jɛmɛ yɛ nakai okpo lɛ shĩa lɛ mli yɛ tso lɛ he ŋwɛi ŋsɔɔdoi etɛ loo ejwɛ mli lɛ.

Shi kɛji akɛ wɔ Anaigbɛ ŋshɔŋlooi alɛɛlɔi lɛ bɛ ŋwɛi shĩai ni yɔɔ ŋwɛi shɔɔŋ tamɔ bɔ ni Kapten Sleet kɛ e-Greenlandbii lɛ yɔɔ lɛ; shi kɛlɛ nakai sɛɛnamɔ ni bɛ lɛ ji nɔ ni nakai ŋshɔi ni lakaa mɔ ni wɔ Anaigbɛ wuolɛɛmɛi lɛ wɔfɔɔ mli babaoo lɛ amli toiŋjɔlɛ ni yɔɔ srɔto kwraa lɛ teɔ shi ewoɔ lɛ waa. Nɔ kome ji akɛ, mikɛ mihe fɔ̃ɔ tsɔne lɛ nɔ blɛoo, ni mikɛ mihe jɔɔ mihe yɛ ŋwɛi koni mikɛ Queequeg, loo mɔ kroko ni bɛ nitsumɔ mli ni manyɛ mana yɛ jɛmɛ lɛ agba sane; no sɛɛ lɛ, mikwɔɔ gbɛ fioo kɛyaa hiɛ, ni mikɛ nane ni bɛ hewalɛ ko shwieɔ ŋwɛi-lɛlɛ lɛ ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ, ni mikwɛɔ kooloi alɛɛhei ni nu yɔɔ mli lɛ dani mikwɛɔ, ni no hewɔ lɛ naagbee lɛ mikwɔ kɛtee he ni miyaa lɛ naagbee lɛ.

Ha mafee lɛ tsitsi ni he tse yɛ biɛ, ni makpɛlɛ nɔ yɛ anɔkwale mli akɛ mikwɛ shi edɔ mi. Akɛni jeŋ muu lɛ fɛɛ he naagba lɛ miibɔle mihe hewɔ lɛ, te mafee tɛŋŋ—ni ashi mi kwraa aha mi diɛŋtsɛ mihe yɛ he ko ni nɔ kwɔ tamɔ nɛkɛ ni haa mɔ susuɔ nii ahe lɛ—te mafee tɛŋŋ matsu misɔ̃i ni ji whale-lɛlɛi fɛɛ ni damɔ shi shiŋŋ lɛ ahe nii lɛ ahe nii yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo nɔ, “Ha ohiɛ ahi ohe nɔ, ni ola lala be fɛɛ be.”
