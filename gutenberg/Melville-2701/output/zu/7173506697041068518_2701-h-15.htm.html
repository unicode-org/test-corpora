ISAHLUKO 74. Ikhanda Lomkhomo Wembewu—Umbono Ohlukene.

Nansi, manje, imikhomo emibili emikhulu, ibeka amakhanda ayo ndawonye; Ake sihlanganyele nabo, futhi sihlale sibethu.

Ohlelweni oluhle lwe-folio leviathan, i-Sperm Whale kanye ne-Right Whale yizona eziphawuleka kakhulu. Iwukuphela kwemikhomo ezingelwa umuntu njalo. Ku-nantuckter, baletha izisekelo ezimbili zazo zonke izinhlobo ezaziwayo ze-whale. Njengoba umehluko wangaphandle phakathi kwawo ubonakalayo ikakhulukazi emakhanda abo; Futhi njengekhanda lalesi sikhashana silenga ohlangothini lukaPequod; futhi njengoba singase sisuke komunye siye komunye ngokukhululeka, ngokumane siwele idekhi:—lapho, ngithanda ukwazi, ingabe uzothola ithuba elingcono lokufunda i-cetology esebenzayo kunalapha?

Endaweni yokuqala, ushaywe umehluko omkhulu phakathi kwalezi zihloko. Zombili zinkulu ngokwanele kuwo wonke unembeza; kodwa kukhona ukulinganisa okuthile kwezibalo ku-Sperm Whale lapho i-Right Whale ishoda ngokudabukisayo. Kunomlingiswa owengeziwe ekhanda le-sperm whale. Njengoba uyibona, unikezela ngokuzenzakalelayo ukuphakama okukhulu kakhulu kuye, ngokwephuzu lokugcwala kwesithunzi. Esimeni samanje, futhi, lesi sithunzi siphakanyiswa umbala wepelepele nosawoti wekhanda lakhe engqungqutheleni, okunikeza uphawu lokuguga kanye nolwazi olukhulu. Kafushane, uyilokho abadobi abakubiza ngobuchwepheshe ngokuthi “umkhomo onekhanda elimpunga.”

Manje ake siphawule ukuthi yini engafani nhlobo kula makhanda—okungukuthi, izitho ezimbili ezibaluleke kakhulu, iso nendlebe. Ingemuva elikude eceleni kwekhanda, futhi liphansi phansi, eduze ne-angle yomhlathi womkhomo, uma uphenya kancane, ekugcineni uzobona iso elingenasici, obungafisa ukuba iso lethole lethole; ngakho kuya ngesilinganiso kuye ngobukhulu bekhanda.

Manje, kulendawo eyinqaba eseceleni yamehlo omkhomo, kusobala ukuthi ayikwazi ukubona into engaphambili ncamashi, njengoba nje ingakwazi ukubona impumalanga ncamashi. Ngamafuphi, ukuma kwamehlo omkhomo kufanelana nendawo yezindlebe zomuntu; Futhi ungahle uthande, ngoba wena, ukuthi kuzokuhamba kanjani kuwe, ingabe ukufunwa eceleni kwezinto ezindlebeni zakho. Uzothola ukuthi ungayala kuphela amadigri angamashumi amathathu okubona ngaphambi komugqa oqondile oseceleni wokubona; futhi cishe amashumi amathathu ngaphezulu ngemuva kwayo. Uma isitha sakho esibuhlungu besihamba siqonde kuwe, siphakamise inkemba emini, ubungeke ukwazi ukusibona, njengoba nje besikweba ngemuva. Ngamafuphi nje, ungaba nemihlane emibili, ngomqondo ongokomfanekiso; kodwa, ngesikhathi esifanayo, futhi, izinhlangothi ezimbili (izinhlangothi ezisemaceleni): ngoba yini eyenza ingaphambili lomuntu - yini ngempela, ngaphandle kwamehlo akhe?

Ngaphezu kwalokho, nakuba kwezinye izilwane eziningi engingazicabanga manje, amehlo atshalwe ngendlela engaqondakali ukuze ahlanganise amandla azo okubona, ukuze enze isithombe esisodwa hhayi ezimbili ebuchosheni; indawo engavamile yamehlo omkhomo, ahlukaniswe ngokuphumelelayo njengoba engamamitha ama-cubic amaningi ekhanda eliqinile, eliphakeme phakathi kwawo njengentaba enkulu ehlukanisa amachibi amabili ezigodini; Lokhu, yiqiniso, kufanele ukwehlukanise ngokuphelele okuvelayo okukuphambana nalo ngakunye kwesitho. Ngakho-ke, umkhomo-ke, kufanele ubone isithombe esisodwa esihlukile ngalapha, nesinye isithombe esihlukile ngalapha; kuyilapho konke okuphakathi kufanele kube ubumnyama obukhulu futhi kube yize kuye. Eqinisweni umuntu, empeleni, kuthiwa abheka umhlaba ebhokisini lomlingwana elinama-sashes amabili ajoyine ngewindi lakhe. Kodwa nge-whale, lawa ma-sashes amabili afakwe ngokuhlukana, okwenza amafasitela amabili ahlukene, kodwa ngokudabukisayo akhinyabeze ukubuka. Lokhu okungavamile kwamehlo omkhomo kuyinto okufanele ihlale ikhunjulwa endabeni yokudoba; futhi ukhunjulwe ngumfundi kwezinye izigcawu ezalandela.

Kungase kuqalwe umbuzo onelukuluku nodidayo mayelana nale ndaba ebonwayo njengokuthinta uLeviyathani. Kodwa kufanele ngenelise ukusikisela. Uma nje amehlo omuntu esavuleke ekukhanyeni, isenzo sokubona asisona sokuzithandela; okungukuthi, akakwazi ukusiza ngokubona noma yiziphi izinto eziphambi kwakhe. Nokho, noma imuphi okuhlangenwe nakho komuntu kuyomfundisa, ukuthi nakuba ekwazi ukushanela izinto ngokungakhethi kanye nje, akunakwenzeka ngaye, ngokucophelela, futhi ngokuphelele, ukuba ahlole noma yiziphi izinto ezimbili—kungakhathaliseki ukuthi zinkulu noma zincane kangakanani—ngabanye. kanye nesikhathi esifanayo; kungabi ndaba zalutho uma zilala zihlangene futhi zithintana. Kepha uma manje usuza ukuhlukanisa lezi zinto ezimbili, uzungeze ngamunye ngombuthano wobumnyama obukhulu; khona-ke, ukuze ubone enye yazo, ngendlela yokuthi ulethe ingqondo yakho kukho, enye izokhishwa ngokuphelele ekuqapheleni kwakho kwamanje. Unjani-ke ngomkhomo? Yiqiniso, womabili amehlo akhe, kuwo ngokwawo, kumelwe enze kanyekanye; kodwa ingabe ingqondo yakhe ibanzi, ihlanganisa, futhi icashe kakhulu kunomuntu, kangangokuthi angahlola ngokucophelela amathemba amabili ahlukene, elinye libe ngakolunye uhlangothi lwakhe, kanti elinye libheke kolunye uhlangothi? Uma ekwazi, ingabe kuyinto emangalisayo kuye, sengathi umuntu ukwazi kanyekanye ukudlula imibukiso yezinkinga ezimbili ezihlukene e-Euclid. Futhi, uma kuphenywa ngokuqinile, akukho ukungahambisani nalokhu kuqhathaniswa.

Kungase kube nje ukufisa okungenzi lutho, kodwa kuye kwahlala kubonakala kimi, ukuthi ukuguquguquka okungavamile kokunyakaza okuboniswa eminye imikhomo lapho ivinjezelwa izikebhe ezintathu noma ezine; uvalo kanye nesibopho sokwethuka, okuvame kakhulu emikhomo enjalo; Ngicabanga ukuthi konke lokhu ngokungaqondile kuphuma ekudidekeni okungenakuzisiza kwentando yeningi, lapho amandla abo okubona ahlukene futhi ahluke kakhulu kufanele ababandakanye.

Kodwa indlebe yomkhomo igcwele ilukuluku njengeso. Uma ungumuntu ongaziwa ngokuphelele emncintiswaneni wabo, ungase uzingele phezu kwalawa makhanda amabili amahora, futhi ungalokothi uthole leso sitho. Indlebe ayinalo iqabunga elingaphandle; futhi embobeni ngokwayo awukwazi ukufaka i-quill, ngakho-ke umzuzu omangalisayo. Ifakwe ngemuva kancane kweso. Ngokuphathelene nezindlebe zabo, lo mehluko obalulekile kufanele ubonwe phakathi kwe-sperm whale kanye kwesokudla. Ngenkathi indlebe yangaphambili inembobo yangaphandle, leyo yokugcina imbozwe ngokuphelele futhi ngokulinganayo phezu kolwelwesi, ukuze ingabonakali kahle ngaphandle.

Akuyona yini ilukuluku, ukuthi isidalwa esikhulu kangaka njengomkhomo kufanele sibone umhlaba ngeso elincane kangaka, futhi sizwe ukuduma kwendlebe ngendlebe encane kunogwaja? Kodwa uma amehlo akhe ayebanzi njengelensi yesibonakude esikhulu sikaHerschel; nezindlebe zakhe zivulekile njengemipheme yezindlu eziphakeme; lokho kungamenza angabe esakwazi ukubona, noma ukuzwa okubukhali? Lutho neze.—Kungani-ke uzama “ukukhulisa” ingqondo yakho? Yibeke ngaphansi.

Manje masibe nanoma yiziphi izihibe nezinjini zesitimu esinazo, singakwazi ukungena phezu kwekhanda lomkhomo wesidoda, ukuze ulale phansi phezulu; khona-ke, ukhuphuka ngeladi ukuya esiqongweni, ube nokulunguza emlonyeni; futhi ukube bekungengenxa yokuthi umzimba manje usuhlukene ngokuphelele nawo, ngesibani besingase sehlele eMgedeni omkhulu waseKentucky Mammoth wesisu sakhe. Kodwa ake sibambelele lapha ngalelizinyo, futhi sibheke ngathi lapho sikhona. Yeka umlomo omuhle ngempela nobukeka umsulwa! ukusuka phansi kuye kusilingi, kufakwe ulayini, noma kunalokho kufakwe iphepha ngolwelwesi olumhlophe olubenyezelayo, olucwebezelayo njengama-satin omshado.

Kodwa phuma manje, futhi ubuke lo mhlathi omangalisayo ongezansi, obonakala njengesivalo eside esiwumngcingo sebhokisi elikhulu lesinemfu, elinehinji ekugcineni, esikhundleni sohlangothi olulodwa. Uma uyihlwitha, ukuze uyithole phezulu, futhi uveze imigqa yayo yamazinyo, ibonakala iyi-portcullis esabekayo; futhi, maye! kufakazela kwabaningi abampofu emkhakheni wezokudoba, okuwela phezu kwabo la ma-spikes ngamandla okubethelwa. Kodwa okubi kakhulu nakakhulu ukubona, lapho kufinyela olwandle, ubona umkhomo omanzi, untanta lapho ulenga, nomhlathi wawo omangalisayo, ubude obungamafidi ayishumi nanhlanu, ulenga phansi ngqo ema-engeli angakwesokudla nomzimba wakhe, umhlaba njenge-jib-boom yomkhumbi. Lomkhomo awufile; udangele kuphela; ngaphandle kwezinhlobo, mhlawumbe; i-hypochondriac; futhi uphansi phezulu, kangangokuthi izintiko zemihlathi yakhe ziye zaxega, zamshiya lapho ekulolo hlobo losizi olubi, isihlamba kuso sonke isizwe sakubo, okufanele, ngokungangabazeki, simbethe imihlathi.

Ezimweni eziningi lo mhlathi ongezansi—owokuxetshulwa kalula yingcweti esetshenziswayo—uyakhishwa futhi uphakanyiswe emphemeni ngenjongo yokukhipha amazinyo endlovu, futhi unikeze ingxenye yalelothambo lomkhomo elimhlophe abadobi abaklama ngalo zonke izinhlobo zezinto ezithakazelisayo. okuhlanganisa izinduku, izambulela-stocks, futhi izibambo ukuze ogibele-iziswebhu.

Ngokuphakamisa okude, okukhathele umhlathi uhudulwa ebhodini, njengokungathi ihange; futhi lapho kufika isikhathi esifanele—ezinsukwini ezimbalwa ngemva komunye umsebenzi—uQueequeg, uDaggoo, noTashtego, njengoba bonke bengodokotela bamazinyo abanekhono, baqala ukudonsa amazinyo. Nge-spade ebukhali, i-Queequeg ihlaba izinsini; Lapho-ke umhlathi ugcotshwe phansi engqondweni, futhi ukubhekana nokuthengiswa okuvela ebukwayo, badonsela lawa amazinyo, njengoba ama-michigan izinkabi ahudulwa ama-oki amadala emazweni okhuni asendle. Ngokuvamile kunamazinyo angamashumi amane nambili esewonke; emikhomo emidala, eguge kakhulu, kodwa engaboli; futhi agcwaliswe ngemfashini yethu yokwenziwa. Umhlathi emva kwalokho usahwa ube ngama-slabs, bese unqwabelaniswa njengezinsimbi zokwakha izindlu.





ISAHLUKO 75. Ikhanda Lomkhomo Wakwesokudla—Umbono Ohlukene.

Seqa umkhumbi, manje ake sibheke kahle ikhanda le-Right Whale.

Njengokwakheka okujwayelekile ikhanda le-Sperm Whale elihloniphekile lingase lifaniswe nenqola yempi yamaRoma (ikakhulukazi ngaphambili, lapho iyindilinga ebanzi kakhulu); ngakho-ke, ekubukeni okubanzi, ikhanda le-Right Whale liphethe ukufana okungafani nesicathulo esikhulu se-galliot-toed. Eminyakeni engamakhulu amabili edlule umkhumbi waseDutch omdala wafanisa ukuma kwawo nokokugcina komthungi wezicathulo. Futhi kulesi sicathulo esifanayo sokugcina, leso salukazi sasenkulisa, esinezingane eziswebezelayo, singahlala ngokunethezeka, sona nayo yonke inzalo yaso.

Kodwa njengoba usondela kuleli khanda elikhulu iqala ukuthatha izici ezihlukene, ngokombono wakho. Uma umi esiqongweni sayo bese ubheka lezi zimpophoma ezimbili ezimise okuka-F, ungathatha ikhanda lonke libe i-bass-viol enkulu, kanye nalezi zimbobo, izimbobo ebhodini layo lokukhala. Khona-ke, futhi, uma ugxilisa iso lakho phezu kwalokhu kugqunyelelwa okungavamile, okuklanyiwe, okunjengekhekheba phezulu kwenqwaba—le nto eluhlaza, i-barnacle, abantu baseGreenland abayibiza ngokuthi “umqhele,” nabadobi baseNingizimu ngokuthi “ibhonethi” yayo. i-Right Whale; ugxilisa amehlo akho kulokhu kuphela, wawuthatha ikhanda ulibhekise esiqwini se-oki esikhulukazi, esinesidleke senyoni endaweni yaso. Kunoma ikuphi, uma ubuka lezo izinkalankala ezihlala lapha kule bhonethi, umqondo onjalo cishe uzofika kuwe; ngaphandle uma, ngempela, ukuthanda kwakho kumiswe yigama lobuchwepheshe elithi “umqhele” nalo elinikezwe wona; lapho uyoba nesithakazelo esikhulu ekucabangeni ukuthi lesi silo esinamandla empeleni siyinkosi yolwandle enomqhele, umqhele wayo oluhlaza ubekwe ndawonye ngalendlela emangalisayo. Kodwa uma lo mkhomo uyinkosi, ungumuntu obukeka enokuthukuthela ukuze ahlobise umqhele. Bheka lolodebe lwangezansi olulengayo! yeka i-sulk ne-pout enkulu lapho! i-sulk ne-pout, ngesilinganiso sombazi, cishe amafidi angamashumi amabili ubude namamitha amahlanu ukushona; i-sulk ne-pout ezokunikeza amalitha angama-500 kawoyela nangaphezulu.

Kuyadabukisa kakhulu, manje ukuthi lo mkhomo oneshwa kufanele ube ne-hare-lipped. Umfantu ucishe ube yimitha ukuwela. Mhlawumbe umama ngesikhathi esibalulekile wayehamba ngomkhumbi ehla ogwini lwasePeru, lapho ukuzamazama komhlaba kubangela ukuba ulwandle lugcwale amanzi. Phezu kwaloludebe, njengaphezu kombundu oshelelayo, manje sesishelela emlonyeni. Ngezwi lami ngangiseMackinaw, kufanele ngithathe lokhu kube ingaphakathi le-wigwam yaseNdiya. Kuhle Nkosi! yiyona ndlela uJona ahamba ngayo na? Uphahla lucishe lube ngamafidi ayishumi nambili ukuphakama, futhi lugijima luye endaweni ecijile kahle, njengokungathi kukhona isigxobo esivamile lapho; kuyilapho lezi zinhlangothi ezinezimbambo, ezinama-arched, ezinoboya, zisethula ngalawo ma-slats amangalisayo, amile kancane, amise okwe-scimetar e-whalebone, athi amakhulu amathathu ohlangothini, okuncike engxenyeni engenhla yekhanda noma ithambo lomqhele, akha lezo zimpumputhe zaseVenice kwezinye izindawo kuye kwashiwo ngokungananazi. Emaphethelweni ala mathambo anemicu enoboya, iRight Whale ehluza ngayo amanzi, futhi ebunzimeni bayo igcina inhlanzi encane, lapho ivule umlomo idlula olwandle lwe-brit ngesikhathi sokudla. Ezingxenyeni ezimaphakathi zethambo, njengoba emi ngokulandelana kwazo okungokwemvelo, kunezimpawu ezithile ezimangalisayo, amajika, izimbobo, namagquma, lapho amadoda athile emikhomo ebala ubudala besidalwa, njengobudala be-oki ngamasongo aso ayindilinga. Nakuba isiqiniseko salesi sici sisekude nokukhonjiswa, nokho sinephunga elimnandi lamathuba okufanisa. Kunoma ikuphi, uma sivumela kukho, kufanele sinikeze iminyaka engaphezulu kakhulu ku-Right Whale kunalokho okuyobonakala kunengqondo ekuqaleni.

Ezikhathini zasendulo, kubonakala sengathi kuye kwanqoba izifiso ezinelukuluku mayelana nalezi zimpumputhe. Omunye umgibeli ePurchas uwabiza ngokuthi “amadevu” amangalisayo angaphakathi emlonyeni womkhomo;* omunye, “amaklebe ezingulube”; ikhehla lesithathu eHackluyt lisebenzisa ulimi olumnandi olulandelayo: “Kunezimpiko ezingaba amakhulu amabili namashumi amahlanu ezimila ohlangothini ngalunye lwenqunu yalo engaphezulu, ezigobe phezu kolimi lwayo ohlangothini ngalunye lomlomo walo.”

* Lokhu kusikhumbuza ukuthi umkhomo ofanele uluhlobo lwe-whisker, noma kunalokho amadevu, aqukethe izinwele ezimbalwa ezihlanzisiwe engxenyeni engenhla yomhlathi ongaphansi womhlathi ophansi. Ngezinye izikhathi lawa ma-tuft aveza ukubukeka kwakhe okunesizotha.

Njengoba wonke umuntu azi, zona lezi “zingulube zezingulube,” “izimpiko,” “izindevu,” “izimpumputhe,” nanoma yini oyithandayo, zinikeza abesifazane amabhasi abo nezinye izinto eziqinisayo. Kodwa kulokhu, isidingo sekuyisikhathi eside sincipha. Kwakungesikhathi seNdlovukazi u-Anne lapho ithambo lase likhazimula khona, i-farthingale yiyona yonke imfashini. Futhi njengoba lawo ma-dame asendulo ayehambahamba kancane, nakuba emihlathini yomkhomo, njengoba ungase usho; noma kunjalo, eshaweni, ngokungacabangeli okufanayo, namuhla sindiza ngaphansi kwemihlathi efanayo ukuze sivikeleke; isambulela siyitende elendlalwe phezu kwethambo elifanayo.

Kodwa manje khohlwa konke mayelana nezimpumputhe nentshebe isikhashana, futhi, ukuma emlonyeni we-Right Whale, qala kabusha eduze kwakho. Uma ubona zonke lezi zinsika zamathambo zihamba ngendlela ehlelekile, ubungeke yini ucabange ukuthi ubungaphakathi kwesitho esikhulu se-Haarlem, futhi ugqolozele amapayipi ayo ayinkulungwane? Ngokhaphethi esithweni sinombhede weTurkey ethambile kakhulu-ulimi, olunamathele, njengokungathi, phansi emlonyeni. Ikhuluphele kakhulu futhi ithambile, futhi ikwazi ukuklebhula izicucu ngokuyiphakamisa emphemeni. Lolu limi oluphambi kwethu manje; ngokubuka nje ngingasho ukuthi kwakuyimiphongolo eyisithupha; okungukuthi, izokunikeza cishe lelo nani lamafutha.

Ngaphambi kwalokhu, kufanele ngabe walibona ngokucacile iqiniso lalokho engaqala ngakho—ukuthi iSperm Whale kanye ne-Right Whale zinamakhanda ahluke ngokuphelele. Ukuhlanganisa-ke: ku-Right Whale's awukho umthombo omkhulu wesidoda; awekho nhlobo amazinyo endlovu; Akunakude, kuncibilikeke okuphelele komhlathi ophansi, njenge-sperm whale's. Futhi ku-Sperm Whale azikho zalezo zimpumputhe zethambo; akukho udebe olukhulu olungezansi; futhi kayilutho ngolimi. Futhi, i-Right Whale inezimbobo ezimbili ezingaphandle, i-Sperm Whale eyodwa kuphela.

Bheka okokugcina, manje, kulawa makhanda ahloniphekile anezigqoko, ngenkathi elele ndawonye; ngoba umuntu uzocwila masinyane, engabhalwanga, olwandle; Okunye ngeke kube isikhathi eside ngokulandela.

Ungakwazi yini ukubamba inkulumo ye-Sperm Whale lapho? Kuyafana nokufa nayo, eminye imibimbi emide esebunzini ibonakala manje isiphelile. Ngicabanga ukuthi ibunzi lakhe elibanzi ligcwele i-placidity efana ne-prairie, ezalwa ngokunganaki okucatshangelwayo mayelana nokufa. Kodwa maka isisho selinye ikhanda. Bheka lolo debe olumangalisayo olungezansi, olucindezelwe ngephutha ohlangothini lomkhumbi, ukuze ubambe umhlathi ngokuqinile. Ingabe lonke leli khanda alibonakali likhuluma ngesinqumo esikhulu esisebenzayo sokubhekana nokufa? Lo Mkhoma Olungile ngiwuthatha ngokuthi wawungumStoyiku; i-Sperm Whale, i-Platonia, okungenzeka ukuthi wathatha i-Spinoza eminyakeni yakhe yokugcina.





ISAHLUKO 76. Inqama Eshayayo.

Ngaphambi kokuyeka, okokuqala, ikhanda leSperm Whale, ngingathanda ukuthi, njengesazi sokusebenza komzimba esinengqondo, umane-uphawule isici saso sangaphambili, kukho konke ukuqoqwa kwawo okuhlangene. Ngingathanda ukuphenya manje ngokubona nje kokubheka ngokwakho kokunye okungekho emthethweni, ukulinganisa okukhaliphile kwanoma imaphi amandla okushaya ahlasela lapho. Nali iphuzu elibalulekile; ngoba kufanele uxazulule ngokugculisayo lolu daba nawe, noma uhlale ungongakholwayo ngesinye sezehlakalo ezimbi kakhulu, kodwa hhayi ezeqiniso elincane, mhlawumbe noma yikuphi okungatholakala kuwo wonke umlando oqoshiwe.

Uyabona ukuthi endaweni evamile yokubhukuda ye-Sperm Whale, ingaphambili lekhanda layo lethula indiza ecishe iqonde ngokuphelele emanzini; uyabona ukuthi ingxenye engezansi yaleyo ngaphambili yehlela kakhulu emuva, ukuze inikeze indawo eningi yokuhlehla yesokhethi ende ethola umhlathi ongezansi onjenge-boom; Uyabona ukuthi umlomo ungaphansi kwekhanda, kakhulu ngendlela efanayo, impela, sengathi umlomo wakho ungaphansi kwe-chin yakho. CHAPTER 79. The Prairie.

To scan the lines of his face, or feel the bumps on the head of this Leviathan; this is a thing which no Physiognomist or Phrenologist has as yet undertaken. Such an enterprise would seem almost as hopeful as for Lavater to have scrutinized the wrinkles on the Rock of Gibraltar, or for Gall to have mounted a ladder and manipulated the Dome of the Pantheon. Still, in that famous work of his, Lavater not only treats of the various faces of men, but also attentively studies the faces of horses, birds, serpents, and fish; and dwells in detail upon the modifications of expression discernible therein. Nor have Gall and his disciple Spurzheim failed to throw out some hints touching the phrenological characteristics of other beings than man. Therefore, though I am but ill qualified for a pioneer, in the application of these two semi-sciences to the whale, I will do my endeavor. I try all things; I achieve what I can.

Physiognomically regarded, the Sperm Whale is an anomalous creature. He has no proper nose. And since the nose is the central and most conspicuous of the features; and since it perhaps most modifies and finally controls their combined expression; hence it would seem that its entire absence, as an external appendage, must very largely affect the countenance of the whale. For as in landscape gardening, a spire, cupola, monument, or tower of some sort, is deemed almost indispensable to the completion of the scene; so no face can be physiognomically in keeping without the elevated open-work belfry of the nose. Dash the nose from Phidias's marble Jove, and what a sorry remainder! Nevertheless, Leviathan is of so mighty a magnitude, all his proportions are so stately, that the same deficiency which in the sculptured Jove were hideous, in him is no blemish at all. Nay, it is an added grandeur. A nose to the whale would have been impertinent. As on your physiognomical voyage you sail round his vast head in your jolly-boat, your noble conceptions of him are never insulted by the reflection that he has a nose to be pulled. A pestilent conceit, which so often will insist upon obtruding even when beholding the mightiest royal beadle on his throne.

In some particulars, perhaps the most imposing physiognomical view to be had of the Sperm Whale, is that of the full front of his head. This aspect is sublime.

In thought, a fine human brow is like the East when troubled with the morning. In the repose of the pasture, the curled brow of the bull has a touch of the grand in it. Pushing heavy cannon up mountain defiles, the elephant's brow is majestic. Human or animal, the mystical brow is as that great golden seal affixed by the German emperors to their decrees. It signifies—“God: done this day by my hand.” But in most creatures, nay in man himself, very often the brow is but a mere strip of alpine land lying along the snow line. Few are the foreheads which like Shakespeare's or Melancthon's rise so high, and descend so low, that the eyes themselves seem clear, eternal, tideless mountain lakes; and all above them in the forehead's wrinkles, you seem to track the antlered thoughts descending there to drink, as the Highland hunters track the snow prints of the deer. But in the great Sperm Whale, this high and mighty god-like dignity inherent in the brow is so immensely amplified, that gazing on it, in that full front view, you feel the Deity and the dread powers more forcibly than in beholding any other object in living nature. For you see no one point precisely; not one distinct feature is revealed; no nose, eyes, ears, or mouth; no face; he has none, proper; nothing but that one broad firmament of a forehead, pleated with riddles; dumbly lowering with the doom of boats, and ships, and men. Nor, in profile, does this wondrous brow diminish; though that way viewed its grandeur does not domineer upon you so. In profile, you plainly perceive that horizontal, semi-crescentic depression in the forehead's middle, which, in man, is Lavater's mark of genius.

But how? Genius in the Sperm Whale? Has the Sperm Whale ever written a book, spoken a speech? No, his great genius is declared in his doing nothing particular to prove it. It is moreover declared in his pyramidical silence. And this reminds me that had the great Sperm Whale been known to the young Orient World, he would have been deified by their child-magian thoughts. They deified the crocodile of the Nile, because the crocodile is tongueless; and the Sperm Whale has no tongue, or at least it is so exceedingly small, as to be incapable of protrusion. If hereafter any highly cultured, poetical nation shall lure back to their birth-right, the merry May-day gods of old; and livingly enthrone them again in the now egotistical sky; in the now unhaunted hill; then be sure, exalted to Jove's high seat, the great Sperm Whale shall lord it.

Champollion deciphered the wrinkled granite hieroglyphics. But there is no Champollion to decipher the Egypt of every man's and every being's face. Physiognomy, like every other human science, is but a passing fable. If then, Sir William Jones, who read in thirty languages, could not read the simplest peasant's face in its profounder and more subtle meanings, how may unlettered Ishmael hope to read the awful Chaldee of the Sperm Whale's brow? I but put that brow before you. Read it if you can.





CHAPTER 80. The Nut.

If the Sperm Whale be physiognomically a Sphinx, to the phrenologist his brain seems that geometrical circle which it is impossible to square.

In the full-grown creature the skull will measure at least twenty feet in length. Unhinge the lower jaw, and the side view of this skull is as the side of a moderately inclined plane resting throughout on a level base. But in life—as we have elsewhere seen—this inclined plane is angularly filled up, and almost squared by the enormous superincumbent mass of the junk and sperm. At the high end the skull forms a crater to bed that part of the mass; while under the long floor of this crater—in another cavity seldom exceeding ten inches in length and as many in depth—reposes the mere handful of this monster's brain. The brain is at least twenty feet from his apparent forehead in life; it is hidden away behind its vast outworks, like the innermost citadel within the amplified fortifications of Quebec. So like a choice casket is it secreted in him, that I have known some whalemen who peremptorily deny that the Sperm Whale has any other brain than that palpable semblance of one formed by the cubic-yards of his sperm magazine. Lying in strange folds, courses, and convolutions, to their apprehensions, it seems more in keeping with the idea of his general might to regard that mystic part of him as the seat of his intelligence.

It is plain, then, that phrenologically the head of this Leviathan, in the creature's living intact state, is an entire delusion. As for his true brain, you can then see no indications of it, nor feel any. The whale, like all things that are mighty, wears a false brow to the common world.

If you unload his skull of its spermy heaps and then take a rear view of its rear end, which is the high end, you will be struck by its resemblance to the human skull, beheld in the same situation, and from the same point of view. Indeed, place this reversed skull (scaled down to the human magnitude) among a plate of men's skulls, and you would involuntarily confound it with them; and remarking the depressions on one part of its summit, in phrenological phrase you would say—This man had no self-esteem, and no veneration. And by those negations, considered along with the affirmative fact of his prodigious bulk and power, you can best form to yourself the truest, though not the most exhilarating conception of what the most exalted potency is.

But if from the comparative dimensions of the whale's proper brain, you deem it incapable of being adequately charted, then I have another idea for you. If you attentively regard almost any quadruped's spine, you will be struck with the resemblance of its vertebræ to a strung necklace of dwarfed skulls, all bearing rudimental resemblance to the skull proper. It is a German conceit, that the vertebræ are absolutely undeveloped skulls. But the curious external resemblance, I take it the Germans were not the first men to perceive. A foreign friend once pointed it out to me, in the skeleton of a foe he had slain, and with the vertebræ of which he was inlaying, in a sort of basso-relievo, the beaked prow of his canoe. Now, I consider that the phrenologists have omitted an important thing in not pushing their investigations from the cerebellum through the spinal canal. For I believe that much of a man's character will be found betokened in his backbone. I would rather feel your spine than your skull, whoever you are. A thin joist of a spine never yet upheld a full and noble soul. I rejoice in my spine, as in the firm audacious staff of that flag which I fling half out to the world.

Apply this spinal branch of phrenology to the Sperm Whale. His cranial cavity is continuous with the first neck-vertebra; and in that vertebra the bottom of the spinal canal will measure ten inches across, being eight in height, and of a triangular figure with the base downwards. As it passes through the remaining vertebræ the canal tapers in size, but for a considerable distance remains of large capacity. Now, of course, this canal is filled with much the same strangely fibrous substance—the spinal cord—as the brain; and directly communicates with the brain. And what is still more, for many feet after emerging from the brain's cavity, the spinal cord remains of an undecreasing girth, almost equal to that of the brain. Under all these circumstances, would it be unreasonable to survey and map out the whale's spine phrenologically? For, viewed in this light, the wonderful comparative smallness of his brain proper is more than compensated by the wonderful comparative magnitude of his spinal cord.

But leaving this hint to operate as it may with the phrenologists, I would merely assume the spinal theory for a moment, in reference to the Sperm Whale's hump. This august hump, if I mistake not, rises over one of the larger vertebræ, and is, therefore, in some sort, the outer convex mould of it. From its relative situation then, I should call this high hump the organ of firmness or indomitableness in the Sperm Whale. And that the great monster is indomitable, you will yet have reason to know.
