“Gracious! Queequeg, ungahlali lapho,” kusho mina.

“Oh! isihlalo se-perry dood,” kusho u-Queequeg, “indlela yami yasezweni; ngeke ngilimaze ubuso bakhe.”

“Ubuso!” ngathi, "ngibize lobo buso bakhe? ubuso obuhle kakhulu ke; kodwa ukuthi uphefumula kanzima kangakanani, uyaziqinisa; yehla, Queequeg, uyasindwa, ugaya ubuso bompofu. Hluka, Queequeg! Bheka, uzokucisha ngokushesha. Ngiyamangala ukuthi akavuki."

U-Queequeg wazisusa waya ngale kwekhanda lomuntu olalayo, walayitha ipayipi lakhe le-tomahawk. Ngahlala ezinyaweni. Saligcina ipayipi lidlula phezu komuntu olalayo, lisuka kwelinye liya kwelinye. Phakathi naleso sikhathi, lapho ngimbuza ngendlela yakhe ephukile, u-Queequeg wanginika ukuqonda ukuthi, ezweni lakubo, ngenxa yokungabikho kwama-settees nosofa bazo zonke izinhlobo, inkosi, izinduna, nabantu abakhulu ngokuvamile, babenomkhuba wokukhuluphalisa ezinye zemithetho ephansi ye-ottoman; futhi ukuze unikeze indlu ngokunethezeka ngaleyo ndlela, kwakufanele uthenge kuphela abantu abayisishiyagalombili noma abayishumi abavilaphayo, futhi ubabeke nxazonke ezinsikeni nasezindlini. Ngaphandle kwalokho, kwakulula kakhulu ohambweni; kangcono kakhulu kunalezo zihlalo zasengadini eziguqulwa zibe izinti zokuhamba; ngesinye isikhathi, induna yabiza isikhonzi sayo, yayicela ukuba izenzele indawo yokuhlala ngaphansi kwesihlahla esivundile, mhlawumbe endaweni ethile ewugwadule emanzi.

Ngenkathi elandisa lezi zinto, ngaso sonke isikhathi lapho u-Queequeg ethola i-tomahawk kimi, wayeqhakaza uhlangothi lwayo lwe-hatchet phezu kwekhanda lomuntu olele.

“Kwenzelweni lokho, Queequeg?”

“Perry easy, kill-e; oh! perry easy!”

Wayeqhubeka nokukhumbula okuthile okusendle mayelana nepayipi lakhe le-tomahawk, okwakubonakala sengathi kuzo ezimbili lisebenzise ingqondo yezitha zakhe futhi laduduza umphefumulo wakhe, lapho sikhangwa ngokuqondile i-rigger elele. Umhwamuko onamandla manje owawugcwalisa ngokuphelele imbobo eboshiwe, waqala ukuvela kuye. Waphefumula ngendlela engezwakali; kwase kubonakala ukhathazekile ekhaleni; bese ijikeleza kanye noma kabili; wabe esehlala ngezinqa wacikica amehlo.

“Hhawu!” waphefumula ekugcineni, "obani ababhemayo?"

“Amadoda athunyelwe ngomkhumbi,” ngaphendula mina, “uhamba nini ngomkhumbi?”

"Yebo, yebo, uzongena kuye, ube wena? Uhamba ngomkhumbi namuhla. UKapteni ugibele izolo ebusuku."

“Yini Kapteni?—Ahabi?

"Ubani ngaphandle kwakhe?"

Bengizombuza eminye imibuzo eyengeziwe mayelana no-Ahabi, ngenkathi sizwa umsindo edekhini.

“Holloa! “Ungumngane omkhulu ophilayo, lowo; indoda elungile, futhi eqotho; kodwa bonke abaphilayo manje, kufanele ngiphendukele kuye.” Esho kanjalo wangena emphemeni, salandela.

Lase liphumile ilanga. Ngokushesha izisebenzi zangena esikebheni ngababili nantathu; ama-riggers azenzela wona; abangane bomshado babematasatasa; futhi abantu abambalwa basogwini babematasa beletha izinto ezihlukahlukene zokugcina emkhunjini. Ngaleso sikhathi uKaputeni u-Ahabi wahlala engabonakali ngaphakathi kwendlu yakhe.
