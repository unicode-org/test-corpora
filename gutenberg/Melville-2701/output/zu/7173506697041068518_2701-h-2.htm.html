ISAHLUKO 4. I-Counterpane.

Lapho ngivuka ekuseni ngakusasa emini, ngathola ingalo kaQueequeg iphonseke phezu kwami ​​ngendlela enothando nenothando kakhulu. Ucishe wacabanga ukuthi ngiwunkosikazi wakhe. I-counterpane yayiyi-patchwork, igcwele izikwele ezincane ezinemibala ehlukene kanye nonxantathu; futhi lengalo yomdwebo wayo yonke indawo nge-labyrinth yaseKrethe engapheli yomfanekiso, zingekho izingxenye ezimbili zazo ezazinomthunzi owodwa oqondile-ngenxa yokuthi ngicabanga ngokugcina ingalo yakhe olwandle ngokungafanele elangeni nasethunzini, imikhono yehembe lakhe igoqwe ngokungavamile ngezikhathi ezihlukahlukene-lengalo yakhe efanayo, ngithi, ibheka umshuqulo womhlaba ofanayo. Ngempela, ingxenye ilele phezu kwayo njengoba kwenza ingalo lapho ngiqala ukuphaphama, ngangingakwazi ukukuhlukanisa ngendwangu, ngakho bahlanganisa imibala yabo ndawonye; futhi kwaba kuphela umuzwa wesisindo kanye nengcindezi ukuthi ngingasho ukuthi Queequeg wangigona.

Imizwa yami yayixakile. Ake ngizame ukuwachaza. Lapho ngiseyingane, ngisikhumbula kahle isimo esicishe sifane esangehlela; kungakhathaliseki ukuthi kwakungokoqobo noma iphupho, angizange ngikwazi ngokuphelele ukuzinza. Isimo kwaba yilesi. Kade ngisika enye i-caper noma enye—ngicabanga ukuthi ibizama ukukhasa phezulu kushimula, njengoba ngangibone ukushanela okuncane ezinsukwini ezimbalwa ezedlule; kanye nomama wami ongamzali, ngandlela thize noma okunye, wayehlale engishaya, noma engilalisa ngingadlile—umama wangidonsa ngemilenze ephuma kushimula wangifaka embhedeni, nakuba kwakuyihora lesibili kuphela ntambama mhla zingama-21 kuNhlangulana, usuku olude kunawo wonke onyakeni endaweni yethu yenkabazwe. Ngazizwa nginokwesaba. Kodwa kwakungekho usizo kukho, ngakho ngaya phezulu ngezitebhisi ngaya ekamelweni lami elincane esitezi sesithathu, ngakhumula kancane ngangokunokwenzeka ukuze ngibulale isikhathi, futhi ngiphefumula okubuhlungu ngangena phakathi kwamashidi.

Ngalala lapho ngibala ngokudabukisayo ukuthi kufanele kudlule amahora ayishumi nesithupha ngaphambi kokuba ngibe nethemba lovuko. Amahora ayishumi nesithupha embhedeni! iqolo lami libuhlungu ngicabanga. Futhi kwakukhanya kakhulu futhi; ilanga libalele efasiteleni, nokugedla okukhulu kwamakhoshi emigwaqweni, nomsindo wamazwi ezitabane endlini yonke. Ngazizwa ngiba nzima nakakhulu—ekugcineni ngasukuma, ngagqoka, futhi ngehla kancane ezinyaweni zami ezisibeke, ngafuna usingamama wami, futhi kungazelelwe ngaziphonsa ezinyaweni zakhe, ngimncenga njengomusa othile ukuba anginikeze ukushelela okuhle ngenxa yokungaziphathi kahle kwami; noma yini ngempela ngaphandle kokungilahla ukuthi ngilale isikhathi eside esingabekezeleleki. Kodwa wayengcono kakhulu futhi enonembeza kunabo bonke omama bokutholwa, futhi kwadingeka ngibuyele ekamelweni lami. Kwaphela amahora ambalwa ngilele lapho, ngiphapheme, ngizizwa ngibi kakhulu kunangaphambili, ngisho nakumashwa amakhulu alandelayo. Ekugcineni kufanele ngabe ngiwele ephusheni elibi elibi lokuqunjelwa; futhi ngiphaphama kulo kancane kancane—ngingene shí emaphusheni—ngavula amehlo ami, futhi igumbi langaphambili elikhanyiswe yilanga lase ligubuzelwe ubumnyama obungaphandle. Ngokushesha ngezwa ukwethuka kugijima kulo lonke uhlaka lwami; kwakungabonwa lutho, futhi kwakungazwakali lutho; kodwa isandla esingaphezu kwemvelo sabonakala sibekwe kwesami. Ingalo yami ilenga phezu kwe-counterpane, futhi ifomu elingenagama, elingacabangeki, elithule noma i-phantom, isandla esasiphethe, yayibonakala ihlezi eduze nombhede wami. Ngoba lokho okwakubonakala sengathi izikhathi zinqwabelana ezikhathini zasendulo, ngangilele lapho, ngigodole ngokwesaba okukhulu kakhulu, ngingesabi ukudonsa isandla sami; kodwa ngihlale ngicabanga ukuthi uma ngingayinyakazisa iyintshi eyodwa, isipelingi esibi siyophuka. Ngangingazi ukuthi ekugcineni lokhu ukwazi kwangibalekela kanjani; kodwa ngivuka ekuseni, ngakhumbula konke ngokuthuthumela, futhi izinsuku namasonto nezinyanga kamuva ngalahlekelwa emizamweni edidayo yokuchaza imfihlakalo. Cha, kuze kube yileli hora, ngivame ukuzixaka ngakho.

Manje, susa ukwesaba okukhulu, futhi imizwa yami lapho ngizwa isandla esingaphezu kwemvelo kimi yayifana kakhulu, ngokuxakayo, naleyo engaba nakho lapho ngivuka futhi ngibona ingalo yobuqaba kaQueequeg iphonswa nxazonke zami. Kodwa ngokuhamba kwesikhathi zonke izehlakalo zobusuku obudlule zaphindaphindeka ngokudabukisayo, ngayinye ngayinye, eqinisweni elingaguquki, ngase ngilala ngiphila kule nkinga ehlekisayo. Ngoba noma ngazama ukunyakazisa ingalo yakhe—ngivule ibhande lomyeni wakhe—nokho, elele kanjalo, waqhubeka wanga wangiqinisa, kwangathi akulutho kodwa ukufa kufanele kusehlukanise sobabili. Manje ngalwela ukumvusa—“Queequeg!”—kodwa okuwukuphela kwempendulo yakhe kwaba ukuhona. Ngibe sengiginqika, intamo yami izwa sengathi yisekholoni lehhashi; futhi kungazelelwe wezwa ukuklwebheka kancane. Iphonsa eceleni i-counterpane, nanso i-tomahawk ilele eceleni kwalesi sigebengu, njengokungathi ingane enobuso be-hatchet. I-pickle enhle, ngempela, ngacabanga; lala lapha endlini engaziwa emini kabha, kunomuntu odla inyama kanye nethongo! "Queequeg!—egameni lobuhle, Queequeg, vuka! Ekugcineni, ngokushibilika okukhulu, nokuchayeka okukhulu nokungapheli ngokungafaneleki kokugona kwakhe owesilisa akanye naye ngaleyo ndlela yokushada, ngiphumelele ukukhipha ukukhononda; futhi okwamanje, wahlehlisa ingalo yakhe, wazithintitha umzimba wonke njengenja yaseNewfoundland ephuma emanzini, wahlala embhedeni, eqinile njengesikhonkwane, engibuka, futhi ecikica amehlo akhe njengokungathi akakhumbuli ngokuphelele ukuthi ngaba kanjani lapho, nakuba ukwazi okufiphele kokwazi okuthile ngami kwabonakala kuphuma kancane kancane kuye. Ngaleso sikhathi, ngilele ngithule ngimbhekile, ngingenakho ukungabaza okukhulu manje, futhi ngizimisele ngokubuka isidalwa esinelukuluku lokufuna ukwazi. Lapho, ekugcineni, ingqondo yakhe yabonakala ifinyeleleke ekuthinteni isimilo somngane wakhe wasembhedeni, futhi waba, njengokungathi, wabuyelana neqiniso; wagxumela phansi, futhi ngezimpawu nemisindo ethile kwangenza ngiqonde ukuthi, uma kungijabulisa, wayezogqoka kuqala bese engishiya ngigqoke ngemva kwalokho, ngishiye ngingedwa. Ngicabanga ukuthi mina, Queequeg, ngaphansi kwezimo, lokhu kuwumbono ophucuzeke kakhulu; kodwa, iqiniso liwukuthi, lezi zixhwanguxhwangu zinomuzwa ongokwemvelo wobubiliboco, shoni lokho enikufunayo; iyamangalisa indlela abahlonipheke ngayo. Ngikhokha lokhu kuncoma okukhethekile kuQueequeg, ngoba wangiphatha ngokuziphatha okuhle nokucabangela, kuyilapho nginecala lokudelela okukhulu; emgqolozele esembhedeni, ebuka konke ukunyakaza kwakhe endlini yangasese; okwesikhathi ilukuluku lami liba ngcono ekuzaleni kwami. Noma kunjalo, indoda efana ne-Queequeg ongayiboni nsuku zonke, yona nezindlela zayo zazifaneleka ngendlela engavamile.

Waqala ukugqoka phezulu ngokufaka isigqoko sakhe se-beaver, eside kakhulu, eduze, wabe—esakhipha ibhulukwe lakhe—wazingela amabhuzu akhe. Ukuthi wakwenzelani ngaphansi kwamazulu, angikwazi ukukusho, kodwa umnyakazo wakhe olandelayo kwaba ukuzichoboza—amabhuzu esandleni, nesigqoko—ngaphansi kombhede; lapho, kusukela ekuhefuzeleni okunobudlova okuhlukahlukene kanye nokudonsa kanzima, ngaphetha ngokuthi wayezikhandla esebenza ngokuzikhandla; nakuba kungekho mthetho wokufaneleka engake ngawuzwa, noma iyiphi indoda kudingeka ibe ngasese lapho igqoka amabhuzu ayo. Kodwa u-Queequeg, uyabona, wayeyisidalwa esisesigabeni soshintsho—kungesona isibungu noma uvemvane. Wayesephucuzeke ngokwanele ukuthi abukise ubukhazikhazi bakhe ngendlela exakile. Imfundo yakhe yayingakaqedi. Wayengumfundi we-undergraduate. Ukube akazange abe nezinga elincane eliphucuzekile, cishe wayengeke azihluphe ngamabhuzu nhlobo; kodwa-ke, ukube ubengakabi yisikhohlakali, ubengeke aphuphe ukungena ngaphansi kombhede ukuze azibeke. Ekugcineni, wavela nesigqoko sakhe sibosheke kakhulu futhi sigaywe emehlweni akhe, waqala ukugedla nokuxhuga egumbini, njengokungathi, wayengawajwayele kangako amabhuzu, amabhuzu akhe amabili aswakeme, ashwabene—okungenzeka ukuthi ayenga-odanga—wake wammpintsha futhi wamhlukumeza ekuseni ephuma emakhazeni ashubisa umnkantsha.

Ngokubona, manje, ukuthi amakhethini efasiteleni ayengekho, nokuthi umgwaqo wawumngcingo kakhulu, indlu emelene nayo yayala ukuba kubonakale indawo ethafeni ekamelweni, futhi ibheka ngokwengeziwe umfanekiso ongcolile owenziwa uQueequeg, enyakaza ngokunye ngaphandle kwesigqoko namabhuzu akhe; Ngamncenga ngakho konke engangingakwenza, ukuthi asheshise indlu yakhe yangasese kancane, futhi ikakhulukazi ukuthi angene kuma-pantaloon akhe ngokushesha ngangokunokwenzeka. Wavuma, wabe eseyageza. Ngaleso sikhathi ekuseni noma yimuphi umKristu wayeyogeza ubuso bakhe; kodwa uQueequeg, ngokumangala kwami, wazanelisa ngokukhawulela ukugeza kwakhe esifubeni, ezingalweni, nezandla. Wabe esegqoka ijazi lakhe, futhi wathatha ucezu lwensipho eqinile phezu kwetafula eliphakathi nendawo yokuwasha, walicwilisa emanzini wayeseqala ukucwebisa ubuso bakhe. Ngangibhekile ukuze ngibone lapho egcine khona insingo yakhe, bheka futhi, bheka, ethatha ihabhu ekhoneni lombhede, akhiphe isitokwe eside sokhuni, akhumule ikhanda, alivuthulule kancane ebhuthini yakhe, futhi anyukele esibukweni esisodongeni, aqale ukunwaya ngamandla, noma kunalokho ahlanganise izihlathi zakhe. Ngicabanga ukuthi mina, Queequeg, lokhu kusebenzisa i-cutlery ehamba phambili kaRogers ngokuphindiselela. Ngemva kwalokho ngazibuza kancane kulokhu kuhlinzwa lapho ngithola ukwazi ukuthi ikhanda le-harpoon lenziwe yiphi insimbi enhle, nokuthi imiphetho emide eqondile ibukhali kangakanani njalo.

Yonke enye indlu yangasese yatholwa ngokushesha, futhi ngokuziqhenya waphuma ekamelweni, ezisonge ngejakhethi yakhe enkulu yezinkawu, futhi eshaya ihabhu lakhe njengenduku ye-marshal.





ISAHLUKO 5. Ukudla kwasekuseni.

Ngalandela ngokushesha, futhi ngehlela egumbini lokuphuzela ngahlangana nomnikazi wendawo owayesinekile. Angizange ngibe nenzondo kuye, nakuba wayelokhu engishaya ngezulu ngendaba yomngane wami wombhede.

Nokho, ukuhleka okuhle kuyinto enhle kakhulu, futhi kunalokho kuyindlala kakhulu into enhle; kulapho isihawu. Ngakho-ke, uma umuntu enza inhlekisa ngomuntu ngokuqondene naye siqu, makangabuyeli emuva, kodwa makajabulele ukusebenzisa futhi asetshenziswe ngaleyo ndlela. Futhi indoda enanoma yini ehlekisayo ngaye, qiniseka ukuthi kuningi kuleyondoda kunalokho okungenzeka ukucabange.

I-bar-room manje yayigcwele abagibeli ababefika ngobusuku obudlule, futhi engangingakababoni kahle. Cishe bonke babengabadobi bemikhomo; abalingane, nabalingane besibili, nabalingane besithathu, nababazi basolwandle, nabasebenza olwandle, nabakhandi bensimbi, nabakhandi bemikhumbi, nabagcini bemikhumbi; inkampani ensundu nensundu, enentshebe ye-bosky; isethi engagundiwe, e-shaggy, bonke begqoke amajakhethi ezinkawu ama-morning gown.

Ubungasho ngokucacile ukuthi umuntu ngamunye ubenesikhathi esingakanani ogwini. Isihlathi esiphile kahle salo mfana sinjengepheya elitoswe yilanga ngombala, futhi libonakala linuka cishe njenge-musky; akanakuba nezinsuku ezintathu efikile ohambweni lwakhe lwaseNdiya. Leyo ndoda eseduze kwayo ibukeka ilula kancane; ungase uthi ukuthintwa kwezinkuni ze-satin kuye. Esikhumbeni sesithathu kusekhona i-tawn yasezindaweni ezishisayo, kodwa ifakwe i-bleach kancane; ngokungangabazeki uhlale amasonto wonke ogwini. Kodwa ubani ongabonisa isihlathi esifana ne-Queequeg? okwakuthi, ivinjelwe ngama-tinti ahlukahlukene, kwakubonakala kufana nomthambeka osentshonalanga we-Andes, ukuze ubonakale endaweni eyodwa, izimo zezulu ezingafani, indawo ngayinye.

“Hhawu, hawu!” wakhala manje umnikazi wendawo, evula umnyango, sangena sayodla isidlo sasekuseni.

Bathi abantu abawubonile umhlaba, ngaleyo ndlela bakhululeke ngempela, bazidle ngokwabo. Nokho, hhayi ngaso sonke isikhathi: uLedyard, umhambi omkhulu waseNew England, noMungo Park, owaseScotch; Kubo bonke abantu, babenesiqiniseko esincane kakhulu endlini. Kodwa mhlawumbe ukuwela nje iSiberia ngesihlibhi esidonswa izinja njengoba kwenza uLedyard, noma ukuthatha uhambo olude lwedwa nesisu esingenalutho, enhliziyweni ye-Afrika yama-negro, okwakuyisamba semidlalo engemihle kaMungo—lolu hlobo lohambo, ngithi, kungase kungabi indlela engcono kakhulu yokuthola ipholishi ephezulu yezenhlalo. Noma kunjalo, ingxenye enkulu, into enjalo kufanele ibe noma kuphi.

Lokhu kuzindla lapha nje kubangelwa yisimo sokuthi ngemva kokuba sonke sihlezi etafuleni, futhi ngangilungiselela ukuzwa izindaba ezimnandi mayelana nomkhomo; okwangimangaza kakhulu, cishe wonke amadoda agcina ukuthula okukhulu. Futhi hhayi lokho kuphela, kodwa babebukeka benamahloni. Yebo, nanti iqoqo lezinja zasolwandle, eziningi zazo ngaphandle kokuhlambalaza nakancane zazigibele imikhomo emikhulu olwandle—abantu ababengabazi nhlobo—futhi bayinqoba ifile ngaphandle kokucwayiza; futhi nokho, lapha bahlezi etafuleni lokudlelana lasekuseni—konke kuwubizo olufanayo, konke ukunambitheka kwezihlobo—bebukana ngokwezimvu njengokungathi babengakaze babonakale esibayeni sezimvu phakathi kweziNtaba Eziluhlaza. Umbono onelukuluku; lamabhele amahloni, lamadoda amaqhawe anamandla!

Kodwa u-Queequeg-kungani, u-Queequeg wayehlezi lapho phakathi kwabo-enhloko yetafula, futhi, kwenzeka ngenhlanhla; kupholile njengeqhwa. Ukuqinisekisa ukuthi angikwazi ukusho okuningi ngokuzalanisa kwakhe. Umhlonishwa wakhe omkhulu wayengeke athethelele ngokunethezeka kwakhe ukuletha ihabhu lakhe ekudleni kwasekuseni kanye naye, nokulisebenzisa lapho ngaphandle komkhosi; efinyelela phezu kwetafula ngayo, ebungozini obuseduze bamakhanda amaningi, futhi ebamba ama-beefsteaks ngakuye. Kodwa lokho wakwenza ngokupholile impela, futhi wonke umuntu uyazi ukuthi ngokulinganisa kwabantu abaningi, ukwenza noma yini ngokupholile kuwukukwenza ngobumnene.

Ngeke sikhulume ngazo zonke izici ze-Queequeg lapha; indlela abalekela ngayo ikhofi nama- hot rolls, futhi wasebenzisa ukunaka kwakhe okuphelele kuma-beefsteaks, ayenziwa ngokungajwayelekile. Kwanele, kwathi lapho ukudla kwasekuseni sekuphelile wahoxa njengabanye wangena egumbini lomphakathi, wakhanyisa ipayipi lakhe le-tomahawk, futhi wayehlezi lapho ethule egaya futhi ebhema efake isigqoko sakhe esingahlukaniseki, lapho ngiphuma ngihamba ngezinyawo.

Kodwa lapho uJona ekhipha isikhwama sakhe esikhwameni sakhe, izinsolo ezinobuhlakani namanje ahlukumeza ukaputeni. Ukhala yonke imali yohlamvu lwemali ukuthola umgunyathi. Hhayi ukujikisa, nganoma iyiphi indlela, uyashintsha; UJona ubeke phansi 'Khombisa ikamelo lami,' kusho uJona manje, 'Ngikhathele ukuhamba.' 'Ubukeka unje,' kusho uKapteni, 'nali ikamelo lakho.' UJona uyangena, futhi uzokhiya umnyango, kodwa ingidi ayinawo ukhiye lapho, uKapteni uhleka yedwa, futhi ukhuluma okuthile mayelana nezicabha zeziboshwa ezingavunyelwe ukuba zikhiyelwe ngaphakathi, uJona uziphonse egumbini lakhe lokulala, futhi uthola u-Jonah uhlezi eduze kwendlu yakhe. Khona-ke, kulowo mgodi oboshiwe, wacwila, futhi, ngaphansi komzila wamanzi womkhumbi, uJona uzwa isimemezelo salelohora lobunzima, lapho umkhomo uzombambela emathunjini akhe amancane kakhulu.

“Sibhace eksisini yaso eceleni, isibani esinyakazayo sinyakaza kancane ekamelweni likaJona; futhi umkhumbi, ubheke esikhumulweni esinesisindo samabhele okugcina owamukelwe, isibani, amalangabi nakho konke, nakuba kuhamba kancane, usalokhu ubophekile unomphela ngokubhekisela ekamelweni; nakuba, eqinisweni, wenza amazinga aqondile phakathi kwawo ngokungeyona iphutha. Isibani se-alamu futhi simesabise uJona; njengoba amehlo akhe ahlukunyeziwe ezungeza indawo, futhi lo mbaleki ophumelelayo akatholi isiphephelo sokubheka kwakhe okungaphumuli. uyabubula, 'uqonde phezulu, ngakho uyasha;

“Njengomuntu othi ngemva kobusuku bokudakwa alale embhedeni wakhe, esanyakaza, kodwa unembeza usamshaya, njengokuphonseka kwehhashi lomjaho laseRoma kodwa washaya kakhulu insimbi yakhe kuye; njengalowo osekulolo sizi oludabukisayo esaphenduka futhi aphenduke osizini olukhulu, ethandazela uNkulunkulu ekubhujisweni kwakhe kuze kube sekufeni kwakhe; ezwa, ubuthongo obujulile bumntshontsha, njengoba phezu komuntu owopha aze afe, ngoba unembeza uyisilonda, futhi akukho lutho olumcindezelayo, ngakho, ngemva kokubambana kanzima egunjini lakhe, usizi lukaJona olunzima lumdonsela phansi elele.

Manje sesifikile isikhathi samagagasi; umkhumbi uyazilahla izintambo zawo; usuka esikhumulweni esiwugwadule umkhumbi onganqanyuliwe waseTharishishi, ukhathazile, ushelela olwandle. Lowo mkhumbi, bangane bami, wawungowokuqala kubashushumbisi abalotshiwe, umthengisi kwakunguJona. Kodwa manje lapho umkhumbi ubiza zonke izandla ukuba zimnciphise; lapho amabhokisi, amabhala, nezimbiza zishaya phezulu, futhi amadoda ekhala, futhi wonke amapulangwe aduma ngezinyawo phezu kwekhanda likaJona; uyalalela ukugijima komkhomo onamandla, okuthi ngisho namanje ngomlomo ovulekile udabula ulwandle emva kwakhe, uJona wehlele emaceleni omkhumbi-indawo yokulala ekhunjini njengoba ngiwuthathile, futhi wayelele ubuthongo obukhulu, futhi ukhala endlebeni yakhe efile! Ethuswa yileso sikhalo esibuhlungu, uJona uyadiyazela ema ngezinyawo, akhubeke aze afike esitezini, abambe indwangu, abuke olwandle. iminza isantanta futhi, njengoba inyanga emhlophe ikhombisa ubuso bayo obuthukile emazingeni aphakeme kumnyama bhuqe, uJona uyamangala ebona umcibisholo obheke phezulu ubheke phezulu, kodwa ngokushesha uphinde ushaye phansi ubheke ekujuleni okuhlushwayo.

“Ukwesabeka phezu kwezinto ezesabisayo kugijima kumemeza emphefumulweni wakhe. Kuzo zonke izimo zakhe zengqondo ezintengayo, umbaleki kaNkulunkulu manje useyaziwa ngokusobala kakhulu. Amatilosi ammaka; aya ngokuya ekhula ukusola kwawo ngaye, futhi ekugcineni, ukuze avivinye iqiniso ngokugcwele, ngokudlulisela yonke indaba eZulwini eliphezulu, bawela ekwenzeni inkatho, ukuze babone ukuthi kwakuyisizathu sikabani lesi siphepho uJona; ngokuthukuthela bayamxosha ngemibuzo yabo, 'Uvelaphi wena ezweni lakini?

Manje bheka, uJona wathatha ihange, waphonswa elwandle; khona lapho ukuzola okunamafutha kwantanta ngasempumalanga, futhi ulwandle luthule, njengoba uJona edonsa isiphepho kanye naye, eshiya amanzi abushelelezi ngemuva. Kuwo wonke amazinyo akhe ophondo lwendlovu, etilongweni lakhe, uJona wakhuleka eNkosini esesiswini senhlanzi, kodwa bheka umkhuleko wakhe, futhi ufunde isifundo esinzima, uJona akakhali futhi ecela ukukhululwa okuqondile. Ithempeli lakhe elingcwele futhi lapha, bazalwane, ukuphenduka kweqiniso nokwethembeka; njengoJona.”

Esakhuluma la mazwi, ukuhhewula kwesivunguvungu esiklabalasayo ngaphandle kwaso kwabonakala kunezela amandla amasha kumshumayeli, okwathi lapho echaza isivunguvungu solwandle sikaJona, wabonakala epheshulwa yisiphepho ngokwakhe. Isifuba sakhe esijulile sasigubha kungathi sivuvukele; izingalo zakhe ezijikijelwayo zazibonakala ziyizimpi ezisebenzayo; kanye nokuduma kwezulu okwakusuka ebunzini bakhe obunsundu, nokukhanya kugxuma esweni lakhe, kwenza zonke izilaleli zakhe ezilula zibheke kuye ngokwesaba okusheshayo okwakuyinqaba kubo.

Kwase kufika ukuthula ekubukeni kwakhe, njengoba ephinda ephenya amaqabunga eNcwadi ngokuthula; futhi, ekugcineni, emi enganyakazi, amehlo evaliwe, okwamanje, kwakubonakala ekhuluma noNkulunkulu kanye naye uqobo.

“Bahambeli bomkhumbi, uNkulunkulu ubeke isandla esisodwa phezu kwenu, izandla zakhe zombili zicindezela phezu kwami. Nginifundile ngalokho ukukhanya okulufifi okungaba kimi isifundo uJona asifundisa zonke izoni; ngakho-ke kini, futhi kakhulu kimi, ngokuba mina ngiyisoni esikhulu kunani. Manje-ke ngingajabula kangakanani ukwehla kule nhloko yensika, ngihlale ezingwegweni, kuyilapho omunye elalela lapho nilalela futhi nilalele lapho nihlezi .
