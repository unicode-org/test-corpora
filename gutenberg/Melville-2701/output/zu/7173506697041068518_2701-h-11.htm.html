ISAHLUKO 53. I-Gam.

Isizathu esizwakalayo sokuthi kungani u-Ahabi engagibelanga umkhombi esasishilo yilesi: umoya nolwandle kwavusa iziphepho. Kodwa noma ngabe bekungenjalo, ubengeke phela, mhlawumbe, amgibele—ehlulela ngokuziphatha kwakhe okwalandela ngezikhathi ezifanayo—uma bekunjalo, ngenqubo yokuhalalisela, wathola impendulo engeyinhle embuzweni awubuzayo. Ngoba, njengoba ekugcineni kwavela, wayenendaba nokungahlali naye, ngisho imizuzu emihlanu, nanoma yimuphi ukaputeni ongaziwa, ngaphandle kokuthi ayenganikela ngolunye lwalolo lwazi ayelufuna ngokujulile. Kodwa konke lokhu kungase kuhlale kulinganiselwe ngokwanele, kwakungeyona into eshiwo lapha mayelana nokusetshenziswa okungavamile kwemikhumbi yomkhomo lapho ihlangana olwandle lwangaphandle, futhi ikakhulukazi endaweni evamile yokudoba.

Uma abantu ababili abangabazi bewela i-Pine Barrens e-New York State, noma i-Salisbury Plain engenalutho ngokulinganayo e-England; uma behlangana ngokunganaki emathangeni angenabungane, laba bobabili, impilo yabo yonke, abakwazi ukugwema ukubingelelana; nokuma isikhashana ukuxoxa izindaba; futhi, mhlawumbe, ehlezi phansi isikhashana futhi ephumula ekhonsathini: ke, kangakanani okungokwemvelo ukuthi phezu iillimitable Pine Barrens futhi Salisbury Plains olwandle, izitsha ezimbili whaling descrying nomunye emikhawulweni yomhlaba-off lone Fanning's Island, noma kude Mills King; kungakanani okungokwemvelo, ngithi, ukuthi ngaphansi kwezimo ezinjalo le mikhumbi akufanele nje ishintshane ngezichotho, kodwa ifike ekuxhumaneni okuseduze, okunobungane nokunobungane. Futhi ikakhulukazi lokhu kubonakala kuyindaba eyiqiniso, endabeni yemikhumbi yechweba elilodwa, futhi izinduna zayo, izikhulu, kanye namadoda angeyingcosana eyaziwa ngomunye nomunye; futhi ngenxa yalokho, ube nazo zonke izinhlobo zezinto zasekhaya ezithandekayo ongakhuluma ngazo.

Ngomkhumbi omude ongekho, umngcele wangaphandle, mhlawumbe, unezinhlamvu ebhodini; noma yikuphi, uzoqiniseka ukuthi umvumela ukuba abe namaphepha athile osuku ngemva konyaka noma emibili kunelokugcina emafayeleni akhe afiphalisiwe nagqokwe nezithupha. Futhi ngenxa yalokho kuhlonishwa, umkhumbi obheke ngaphandle wawuzothola ubuhlakani bakamuva bokudoba imikhomo endaweni okungenzeka uya kuyo, into ebaluleke kakhulu kuye. Futhi ngokwezinga elithile, konke lokhu kuzoba yiqiniso mayelana nemikhumbi edoba imikhomo ewela umzila womunye endaweni yomkhumbi ngokwayo, nakuba ingekho isikhathi eside ngokulinganayo ekhaya. Ngoba omunye wabo kungenzeka wathola ukudluliswa kwezincwadi ezivela kwesithathu, futhi manje umkhumbi kude kude; futhi ezinye zalezo zincwadi kungenzeka zingezabantu bomkhumbi ahlangana nabo manje. Ngaphandle kwalokho, babeshintshana ngezindaba zomkhomo, futhi babe nengxoxo evumelanayo. Ngoba babengeke nje bahlangane nakho konke ukuzwelana kwamatilosi, kodwa ngokufanayo nakho konke okungavamile okuvela ekuphishekeleni okufanayo kanye nokuswela kanye nobungozi obabelana ngakho.

Futhi umehluko wezwe ngeke wenze noma yimuphi umehluko obaluleke kakhulu; okungukuthi, inqobo nje uma zombili izinhlangothi zikhuluma ulimi olulodwa, njengoba kwenzeka kwabaseMelika kanye nesiNgisi. Nakuba, ukuqiniseka, kusukela enanini elincane labadobi bemikhomo abangamaNgisi, imihlangano enjalo ayivamile ukwenzeka, futhi lapho yenzeka kusuke kufaneleke kakhulu ukuba kube khona uhlobo lwamahloni phakathi kwayo; ngoba iNgisi lakho ligodliwe, futhi uYankee wakho, akaluthandi lolu hlobo lwento kunoma ubani ngaphandle kwakhe. Ngaphandle kwalokho, abadobi bemikhomo abangamaNgisi ngezinye izikhathi bathinta uhlobo oluthile lokuphakama kwedolobha elikhulu kunabadobi baseMelika; mayelana ne-Nantucketer ende, ethambile, nezifundazwe zayo ezingasho lutho, njengohlobo oluthile lwabantu basolwandle. Kodwa lapho lokhu kuphakama kwama-whalemen angamaNgisi kuhlanganisa khona ngempela, kungaba nzima ukusho, njengoba amaYankees ngosuku olulodwa, ehlangene, abulala imikhomo eminingi kunawo wonke amaNgisi, ngokuhlanganyela, eminyakeni eyishumi. Kodwa lokhu kuyiphutha elincane elingenabungozi kubazingeli bemikhomo abangamaNgisi, iNantucketer engayifaki kakhulu enhliziyweni; mhlawumbe, ngoba uyazi ukuthi unamaphutha ambalwa ngokwakhe.

Ngakho-ke, siyabona ukuthi kuyo yonke imikhumbi ehamba ngokuhlukana olwandle, abadobi banezizathu eziningi zokuba nobudlelwane—futhi kunjalo. Nakuba, eminye imikhumbi yabahwebi enqamula ukuvuka komunye nomunye maphakathi ne-Atlantic, ngokuvamile izodlula ngaphandle kwegama elilodwa lokuqashelwa, ihlukanisana olwandle oluphakeme, njenge-brace of dandies e-Broadway; futhi ngaso sonke isikhathi ukuzitika, mhlawumbe, ekugxekeni kokugcina phezu kwesisekelo somunye nomunye. Ngokuqondene ne-Men-of-War, lapho ethola ithuba lokuhlangana olwandle, aqale adlule ochungechungeni lokukhothama nokuklwebheka, ukudada kwezimpawu, kangangokuthi akubonakali kukhona okuhle okuqotho nothando lobuzalwane ngakho nhlobo. Ngokuthinta umhlangano wemikhumbi yezigqila, ingani, ijahile ngendlela emangalisayo, ibalekelana ngokushesha ngangokunokwenzeka. Futhi ngokuqondene namaPirates, lapho ethola ithuba lokuwela amathambo awela omunye komunye, isichotho sokuqala sithi—“Zingaki izigebhezi zekhanda?”—indlela efanayo naleyo abadobi bemikhomo aba ngayo—“Mingaki imiphongolo?” Futhi lowo mbuzo wake waphendulwa, izigebengu zivele zihlukane phakathi, ngoba ziyizigebengu nhlangothi zombili, futhi azithandi ukubona izinto eziningi ezimbi zomunye nomunye.

Kodwa bheka umdibi womkhomo okhululekile futhi othembekile, ongabukeki, ongenisa izihambi, onobungane, okhululekile futhi olula! Wenzani umkhomo lapho ehlangana nomunye umkhomo kunoma yiluphi uhlobo lwesimo sezulu esikahle? Unegama elithi “ Gam ,” into engaziwa nhlobo kuyo yonke eminye imikhumbi kangangokuthi abakaze bezwe nangegama leli gama; futhi uma ngenhlanhla bengase bezwe ngakho, bavele bamoyizele, futhi baphinde izinto ezihlekisayo mayelana “nama-spouters” kanye “nama-blubber-boiler,” kanye nezibabazo ezinjengalezi. Kungani bonke omatilosi oMhwebi, kanye nawo wonke amadoda amaPirates kanye neMan-of-War, namatilosi emikhumbi yezigqila, bawazisa umuzwa onjalo wokudelela ngemikhumbi yemikhomo; lo ngumbuzo okungaba nzima ukuwuphendula. Ngoba, endabeni yezigebengu, ake ngithande ukwazi ukuthi lowo msebenzi wabo unalo yini inkazimulo eyinqaba ngawo. Kwesinye isikhathi igcina ngokuphakama okungajwayelekile, impela; kodwa kuphela esigodweni. Futhi ngaphandle kwalokho, lapho umuntu ephakanyiswe ngaleyo ndlela eyinqaba, akanaso isisekelo esifanele sokuphakama kwakhe okuphezulu. Ngakho-ke, ngiphetha ngokuthi ngokuziqhayisa ngokuphakama ngaphezu komkhomo, kulokho kugomela isigebengu asinaso isisekelo esiqinile sokuma kuso.

Kodwa yini iGam? Ungase uguge umunwe wakho wenkomba wehla wenyuka amakholomu ezichazamazwi, futhi ungalokothi uthole igama. UDkt. Johnson akazange afinyelele kulokho kufunda; Umkhumbi kaNoah Webster awuwubambi. Noma kunjalo, leli gama elifanayo elichazayo manje selineminyaka eminingi lisetshenziswa njalo phakathi kwamaYankee azalwa ngokweqiniso angaba yizinkulungwane eziyishumi nanhlanu. Impela, idinga incazelo, futhi kufanele ifakwe ku-Lexicon. Ngalowo mbono, ake ngiwuchaze ngokufunda.

I-GAM. NOUN— Umhlangano womphakathi wemikhumbi emibili ( noma ngaphezulu ) Yemikhomo, ngokuvamile endaweni yokuvakasha; lapho, ngemva kokunana isichotho, bavakashelana ngabasebenzi bezikebhe: izinduna ezimbili ezisele, isikhathi, emkhunjini owodwa, nezinduna ezimbili komunye.

Kukhona enye into encane mayelana neMidlalo Yamageyimu okungafanele ikhohlwe lapha. Zonke izingcweti zinemininingwane yazo emincane; kanjalo nemboni yokudoba imikhomo. Emkhunjini wasolwandle, wezimpi, noma wezigqila, lapho ukaputeni egwedla noma yikuphi esikebheni sakhe, uhlala ehlala emashidini angemuva esihlalweni esinethezekile, ngezinye izikhathi esithokomele lapho, futhi ngokuvamile uziqondisa ngetiyela elincane elihle elihlotshiswe ngezintambo nezitabane. Kodwa isikebhe somkhomo asinaso isihlalo esingemuva, asinaso usofa walolo hlobo, futhi asinalo nhlobo. Izikhathi eziphakeme ngempela, uma okaputeni abadoba imikhomo bebeshayelwa ngamasondo emanzini kuma-castor njengama-gouty old aldermen ezihlalweni zelungelo lobunikazi. Futhi mayelana ne-tiller, i-whale-boat ayivumi neze ukuthi kukhona ubuhle obunjalo; ngakho-ke njengasekudlaleni izisebenzi zesikebhe eziphelele kufanele ziwushiye umkhumbi, futhi ngenxa yalokho njengoba umqondisi wesikebhe noma i-harpooneer ingeyenani, lowo ongaphansi ungumphathi weso senzakalo, futhi ukaputeni, engenandawo yokuhlala, uyadonselwa ekuhambeleni kwakhe bonke bemi njengesihlahla sikaphayini. Futhi ngokuvamile uzoqaphela ukuthi ukuqaphela amehlo alo lonke izwe elibonakalayo ehlezi phezu kwakhe kusukela ezinhlangothini zemikhumbi emibili, lo kaputeni omile uyaphila ekubalulekeni kokugcina isithunzi sakhe ngokugcina imilenze yakhe. Futhi lena akuyona indaba elula kakhulu; ngoba ngemuva kukhona isigwedlo esikhulukazi esiphumayo esimshaya kancane kancane emhlane wakhe, isigwedlo sangemuva siphindaphinda ngokugaqa amadolo akhe ngaphambili. Ngakho-ke uboshwe ngokuphelele ngaphambili nangemuva, futhi angakwazi ukuzikhulisa emaceleni ngokuhlala phansi ngemilenze yakhe elulekile; kodwa ukugxusha kwesikebhe kungazelelwe, okunobudlova ngokuvamile kuyofika kude ukuze simwise, ngoba ubude besisekelo abuyinto engenabubanzi obulinganayo. Yenza nje i-engeli yokwendlaleka ngezinti ezimbili, futhi awukwazi ukuzimisa. Khona-ke, futhi, kwakungeke kwenzeke emehlweni obala emehlweni omhlaba, kwakungeke kwenzeke, ngisho, ukuthi lo kaputeni ohambahambayo abonakale ezimisa nokuncane ngokubamba noma yini ngezandla zakhe; ngempela, njengophawu lwakho konke, ukuzilaya kwakhe, ngokuvamile uphatha izandla zakhe emaphaketheni ebhulukwe lakhe; kodwa mhlawumbe njengoba ngokuvamile zinkulu kakhulu, izandla ezisindayo, uzithwala lapho ukuze athole i-ballast. Nokho kube nezimo, eziqinisekisiwe futhi, lapho ukaputeni aziwa khona ngesikhathi esibucayi noma ezimbili ezibucayi, ngokungazelele athi—ukubamba izinwele zomgwedli oseduze, futhi abambelele lapho njengokufa kabuhlungu.





ISAHLUKO 54. Indaba Yedolobha-Ho.

( Njengoba ilandiswa eGolden Inn. )

ICape of Good Hope, kanye nayo yonke indawo enamanzi ezungeze lapho, ifana namanye amagumbi amane omgwaqo omkhulu, lapho ohlangana khona nabahambi abaningi kunanoma iyiphi enye ingxenye.

Akuphelanga sikhathi esingakanani ngemva kokukhuluma i-Goney lapho kwahlangana khona nenye indoda yomkhomo eya ekhaya, iTown-Ho. Wayephethwe cishe ngokuphelele abasePolynesia. Emdlalweni omfushane owalandela usinikeze izindaba eziqinile zikaMoby Dick. Kwabanye isithakazelo esivamile kuMkhomo Omhlophe manje sase sidlondlobale ngenxa yesimo sendaba yaseTown-Ho, okwakubonakala kungaqondakali ukuthi ihilela umkhomo ukuvakasha okuthile okumangalisayo, okuhlanekezelwe kwenye yalezo ezibizwa ngokuthi izahlulelo zikaNkulunkulu okuthi ngezinye izikhathi kuthiwa zidlula amadoda athile. Lesi simo sakamuva, esihambisana naso ngokukhethekile, esakha lokho okungase kubizwe ngokuthi ingxenye eyimfihlo yenhlekelele ezolandiswa, asizange sifinyelele ezindlebeni zikaKaputeni u-Ahabi noma abangane bakhe bomshado. Ngoba leyo ngxenye eyimfihlo yendaba yayingaziwa ukapteni weTown-Ho ngokwakhe. Kwakuyimpahla yangasese yamatilosi amathathu amhlophe ahlangene alowo mkhumbi, omunye wabo, okubonakala sengathi, wayidlulisela kuTashtego ngemiyalo yamaRomish yokufihla, kodwa ngobusuku obulandelayo uTashtego wagabavula ebuthongweni bakhe, futhi wembula okuningi kwakho ngaleyo ndlela, kangangokuthi lapho evuswa wayengakwazi ukugodla okunye. Noma kunjalo, le nto yaba nethonya elinamandla kulaba basolwandle ePequod abafika olwazini olugcwele ngakho, futhi ngobumnene obuyinqaba, ukuyibiza kanjalo, ingabe babebuswa kulolu daba, kangangokuthi bagcina imfihlo phakathi kwabo ukuze ingalokothi ivele ishiye i-main-mast yePequod. Ngixhuma endaweni yayo efanele le ntambo emnyama nendaba njengoba ilandiswa esidlangalaleni emkhunjini, yonke le ndaba eyinqaba manje ngiyaqhubeka nokuyibeka erekhodini elihlala njalo.

*Ukukhala komkhomo wasendulo lapho kuqala ukubona umkhomo usuka esihlokweni sensika, esasetshenziswa ama-whalemen ekuzingeleni i-Gallipagos terrapin edumile.

Ngenxa yamahlaya ami, ngizosigcina isitayela engake ngayilandisa ngaso e-Lima, embuthanweni wabangane bami base-Spain, usuku olwandulela usuku olulodwa lukasanta, bebhema kuthayela owugqinsi we-Golden Inn. Kulabo bagibeli bamahhashi abahle, abakwaDon, uPedro noSebastian, babesondelene nami; futhi yingakho imibuzo ephakathi abayibeka ngezikhathi ezithile, futhi ephendulwa ngokufanelekile ngaleso sikhathi.

“Eminyakeni emibili ngaphambi kokuba ngiqale ngifunde izehlakalo engizonixoxela zona, madoda, iTown-Ho, iSperm Whaler yaseNantucket, yayihamba ngomkhumbi ePacific yakho lapha, kungakapheli izinsuku eziningi ibheke empumalanga isuka emaphethelweni aleGolden Inn enhle. Yayindawana thize enyakatho ye-Line. Ngolunye usuku ekuseni lapho ephatha amaphampu, wayenza ngaphezulu kwalokho ayekusebenzise nsuku zonke. I-sword-fish yayimgwazile, madoda. Kodwa ukaputeni, enesizathu esingavamile sokukholelwa ukuthi wayelindelwe inhlanhla kulezo zindawo, futhi ngenxa yalokho engafuni ukuziyeka, futhi ukuvuza kwakungabhekwa njengengozi nhlobo, nakuba, ngempela, abakwazanga ukuyithola ngemva kokusesha indawo ephansi njengoba kwakungenzeka esimweni sezulu esinzima, umkhumbi wawusebenza ngokukhululekile ngesikhathi sokuhamba kwawo; inhlanhla yafika; kwadlula izinsuku eziningi, futhi kwakungavuzanga nje kuphela, kodwa kwanda ngendlela enengqondo, kangangokuthi, ngokuthatha inhlabamkhosi ethile, ukaputeni, wenza konke okusemandleni akhe, wama ethekwini eliseduze phakathi kweziqhingi, ukuze akhiphe umkhumbi wakhe futhi ulungiswe.

“Nakuba kwakungencane indawo eyayiphambi kwakhe, nokho, uma ithuba elivamile lalivuma, wayengenalo nhlobo uvalo lokuthi umkhumbi wakhe uzosungula indlela, ngoba amaphampu akhe ayengcono kakhulu, futhi ekhululeka ngezikhathi ezithile kuwo, lawo madoda akhe aneminyaka eyisithupha namashumi amathathu ayekwazi ukuwugcina ukhululekile umkhumbi; ungakhathazeki uma ukuputshuka kwakungase kuphindwe kabili kuye. Impela wafika ephepheni lakhe ngaphandle kokushona okuncane, ukube bekungengenxa yempatho enesihluku ka-Radney, umlingani, uMvini, kanye nokuziphindiselela okudabukisayo kuka-Steelkilt, i-Lakeman kanye ne-desperado evela e-Buffalo.

“'Lakeman!—Nyathi! kusho uDon Sebastian, esukuma ecansini lakhe elishwibayo.

“Ogwini olusempumalanga yeChibi lethu i-Erie, uDon; kodwa—ngifisa umusa wakho—kungenzeka ukuthi, maduze nje nizokuzwa okwengeziwe ngakho konke lokho. Manje, madoda, omaseyili ayisikwele kanye nemikhumbi enezinsika ezintathu, ecishe ibe mikhulu futhi iqinile njenganoma yimuphi owake wasuka eCallao yakho yakudala waya eManilla ekude; le ndoda yeLake, ihlezi ezweni laseMelika Okuvelayo kwe-agrarian freebooting okudume ngokuxhunyaniswa nolwandle oluvulekile. Kodwa lo Nantucketer wayeyindoda enenhliziyo enhle; isithunzi sokuqashelwa komuntu okuyilungelo lesigqila elibi kunawo wonke, le-Steelkilt yayigcinwe isikhathi eside ingenabungozi futhi ithambile.

"Akuzange kube ngaphezu kosuku olulodwa noma ezimbili ekujuleni kakhulu ngemva kokukhomba indawo yakhe yokuphumula esiqhingini, lapho ukuvuza kwe-Town-Ho kubonakala futhi kwanda, kodwa kuphela ukuze kudinga ihora noma ngaphezulu kumaphampu nsuku zonke. Kumelwe wazi ukuthi olwandle oluzinzile noluphucuzekile njenge-Atlantic yethu, isibonelo, abanye okaputeni bacabanga kancane ngokumpompa ubusuku bonke, nakuba kufanele kwenzeke ukuthi iphoyisa liwele kulo; Ngokuphathelene nalokho, kungenzeka ukuthi yena kanye nozakwabo bangase baphinde bawukhumbule, ngenxa yezandla eziye zehla kancane zifike ezansi, noma olwandle oluzodwa noludlangile kude nawe ukuya ngasentshonalanga, madoda, akujwayelekile yini ukuthi imikhumbi ihlale iklabalasa ezibambolweni zayo ngobude obungafinyeleleka, noma ngabe ingakanani ukufinyelela kwayo; noma uma kukhona okunye ukuhlehla okunengqondo kuphela lapho umkhumbi ovuzayo ungaphandle kakhulu kwengxenye yalawo manzi, indawo ethile engenamhlaba ngempela, lapho ukapteni wakhe eqala khona ukuzizwa ekhathazekile.
