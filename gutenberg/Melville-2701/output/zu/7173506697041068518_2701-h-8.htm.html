Umuzwa ongalawuleki, ongaqondakali, wozwelo wawukimi; Ingxabano engapheli ka-Ahabi yabonakala ingeyami. Ngezindlebe ezinomhobholo ngafunda umlando waleso silo esingumbulali mina nabo bonke abanye esasifunge ngaso ubudlova nokuziphindiselela.

Esikhathini esithile esidlule, nakuba ngezikhathi ezithile kuphela, i-White Whale engaphelezelwa muntu, eyimfihlo yayilokhu ihlupha lezo zilwandle ezingaphucuzekile ezivame ukuvalelwa abadobi be-Sperm Whale. Kodwa akubona bonke ababebazi ngobukhona bakhe; abambalwa kuphela kubo, ngokuqhathanisa, ababembonile bazi; kuyilapho isibalo esasisenempi ngempela futhi ngamabomu sisesincane impela. Ngoba, ngenxa yenani elikhulu lama-whale-cruisers; indlela engahlelekile ababefafazwe ngayo kuwo wonke umzuliswano wamanzi, abaningi babo ngokuzumayo bephushela ukufuna kwabo ezindaweni ezizimele zodwa, kangangokuthi kwakuqabukela noma ungalokothi kwaphela izinyanga eziyishumi nambili noma ngaphezulu ekujuleni, ukuze bahlangane noseyili owodwa obika izindaba wanoma yiluphi uhlobo; ubude obudlulele bohambo ngalunye oluhlukene; ukungahambi kahle kwezikhathi zokuhamba ngomkhumbi usuka ekhaya; zonke lezi, nezinye izimo, eziqondile nezingaqondile, kudala zathiba ukusabalala kwemikhumbi yomhlaba wonke yokudoba imikhomo yezindaba ezikhethekile eziqondene nomuntu ngamunye eziphathelene no-Moby Dick. Kwakungeyona neze ukungabaza, ukuthi izitsha eziningana kubikwa ukuthi wahlangabezana, ngesikhathi esinjalo noma esinjalo, noma kuleyo noma enjalo meridian, i-Sperm Whale ubukhulu obungavamile nobubi, okuyinto umkhomo, ngemva kokwenza okubi kakhulu kubahlaseli bakhe, wababalekela ngokuphelele; kwezinye izingqondo kwakungekona ukucabangela okungalungile, ngithi, ukuthi umkhomo okukhulunywa ngawo kumelwe ukuba wawungeyena omunye ngaphandle kukaMoby Dick. Nokho kusukela kamuva nje ukudoba i-Sperm Whale kwakuphawulwe yizimo ezihlukahlukene futhi ezingavamile zobudlova obukhulu, ubuqili, nonya ku-monster ehlaselwa; ngakho-ke kwaba, ukuthi labo abathi ngengozi ngokungazi balwa noMoby Dick; abazingeli abanjalo, mhlawumbe, ngokwengxenye enkulu, babeneliswa ukusho ukwesabeka okungavamile akuzalela, ngomqondo ongokomfanekiso, ezingozini zokudoba i-Sperm Whale ngobubanzi, kunombangela ngamunye. Ngaleyo ndlela, ikakhulukazi, ukungqubuzana okuyinhlekelele phakathi kuka-Ahabi nomkhomo kuze kube manje bekusathandwa kakhulu.

Kepha labo abake bezwa ngoMkhoma Omhlophe ngenhlanhla bambona; ekuqaleni into ababenayo ngamunye wabo, cishe, njengoba ngesibindi nangokungesabi wehliselwa kuye, njenganoma yimuphi omunye umkhomo walolo hlobo. Kodwa ngokuhamba kwesikhathi, lezi zinhlekelele zavela kulokhu kuhlasela—okungagcini nje ngokuba nezihlakala namaqakala, ukuphuka kwemilenze, noma ukunqunywa izitho ezishwabadelayo—kodwa okwabulala kwaze kwaba sekugcineni; lezo zibuyekezo eziphindiwe eziyinhlekelele, zonke ziqongelela futhi zinqwabelanise ukwesabeka kwazo ku-Moby Dick; lezo zinto zase zihambe ibanga elide ukuze zinyakazise isibindi sabazingeli abaningi abanesibindi, okwagcina kufike kubo indaba yoMkhoma Omhlophe.

Futhi amahemuhemu angalawuleki azo zonke izinhlobo awazange ahluleke ukwenza ihaba, futhi asabisa nakakhulu imilando eyiqiniso yalezi zenzakalo ezibulalayo. Ngokuba akukhona nje kuphela ukuthi amahemuhemu amahle akhula ngokwemvelo emzimbeni wazo zonke izenzakalo ezimbi ezimangalisayo—njengoba isihlahla esishayiwe sizala isikhunta saso; kodwa, ekuphileni kwasolwandle, okungaphezu kakhulu kwalokho kwe-terra firma, amahemuhemu asendle ayanda, nomaphi lapho kukhona noma yiliphi iqiniso elanele ukuba babambelele kulo. Futhi njengoba ulwandle ludlula umhlaba kule ndaba, kanjalo ukudoba imikhomo kudlula zonke izinhlobo zezilwane zasolwandle, ngokumangalisayo nokwesaba amahemuhemu asakazwa lapho ngezinye izikhathi. Ngoba akukhona nje kuphela ukuthi ama-whalemen njengomzimba ongakhululekile kulokho kungazi nokukholelwa ezeni okuyifa lawo wonke amatilosi; kodwa kuwo wonke amatilosi, ngawo wonke amathuba yiwona ahlangana ngokuqondile nanoma yini emangalisayo ngokushaqisayo olwandle; ubuso nobuso ababoni nje kuphela izimangaliso zayo ezinkulu, kodwa, isandla emhlathini, balwa nabo. Wedwa emanzini akude kakhulu, kangangokuthi noma wawuhamba amamayela ayinkulungwane, wedlula ogwini oluyinkulungwane, wawungeke ufike kunoma yiliphi itshe elicwengekile leziko, noma kunoma iyiphi indawo yokungenisa izihambi ngaphansi kwaleyo ngxenye yelanga; emazingeni anjalo nama-longitude, ephishekela ubizo olunjalo njengoba enza, umkhomo ugoqwe amathonya wonke avame ukwenza ukuthanda kwakhe kukhule ngezizukulwane eziningi ezinamandla.

Ngakho-ke, akumangalisi ukuthi njalo ukuqoqa ivolumu ezindleleni zamanzi ezibanzi kakhulu, amahemuhemu aphihliwe e-White Whale ekugcineni ahlanganisa nawo wonke amasu abi, kanye nokusikisela kwenganekwane okuyingxenye kwama-ejensi angaphezu kwawemvelo, okwagcina kutshale uMoby Dick ngezinto ezesabekayo ezingabolekwa kunoma yini ebonakalayo. Kangangoba ezikhathini eziningi ukwethuka okunjalo ekugcineni wagadla, kangangokuthi abambalwa ngalawo mahemuhemu, okungenani, abezwa ngoMkhoma Omhlophe, abambalwa balabo bazingeli ababezimisele ukuhlangabezana nezingozi zomhlathi wakhe.

Kodwa kwakusekhona amanye amathonya awusizo nabaluleke ngokwengeziwe emsebenzini. Ngisho nanamuhla alukho udumo lokuqala lweSperm Whale, njengoba luhlukaniswa ngokwesabekayo kuzo zonke ezinye izinhlobo zeleviyathani, olwafa ezingqondweni zama-whalemen njengomzimba. Kukhona labo namuhla phakathi kwabo, okuthi nakuba behlakaniphe futhi benesibindi ngokwanele ekunikeleni impi eGreenland noma i-Right whale, mhlawumbe—kungaba ngenxa yokungabi nalwazi kochwepheshe, noma ukungakwazi, noma ukusaba, benqaba umncintiswano neSperm Whale; noma yikuphi, kunenqwaba yama-whalemen, ikakhulukazi phakathi kwalezo zizwe ezibamba imikhomo ezingahambanga ngaphansi kwefulegi laseMelika, ezingakaze zihlangane ne-Sperm Whale, kodwa olwazi lwaso kuphela lweleviathan lukhawulelwe esilo esingenanhlonipho esasiphishekelwa eNyakatho; behlezi phezu kwezichanyuselwa zabo, la madoda azolalela ngesithakazelo somlilo wengane kanye nokwesaba, endle, izinganekwane eziyinqaba ze-Southern whaling. Futhi ubukhulu bangaphambili be-Sperm Whale obukhulu abuqondwa ngokuzwakalayo, ukwedlula ukugibela lezo zinhlamvu ezimvimbayo.

Futhi njengokungathi iqiniso elivivinyiweyo lamandla akhe ezikhathini zangaphambili laliphonsa isithunzi salo phambi kwalo; sithola ezinye zezazi zemvelo zezincwadi—u-Olassen no-Povelson—bememezela ukuthi iSperm Whale akukona nje kuphela ukuthusa zonke ezinye izidalwa olwandle, kodwa futhi nokuba nonya ngendlela engakholakali njengokuhlala womele igazi lomuntu. Noma kuze kube sekwephuzile kakhulu njengesikaCuvier, ngabe lokhu noma okucishe kufane nokuvelayo kwaqedwa. Ngoba encwadini yakhe ethi Natural History, uBaron ngokwakhe uqinisekisa ukuthi lapho zibona iSperm Whale, zonke izinhlanzi (okuhlanganisa noshaka) “zihlaselwa izinto ezesabisa kakhulu,” futhi “ngokuvamile lapho zindiza zigijimela emadwaleni ngobudlova obungabulala ngokushesha.” Futhi noma kunjalo ulwazi olujwayelekile endaweni yokudoba lungachibiyela imibiko enjengale; nokho ekwesabeni kwabo okugcwele, ngisho nasentweni egcwele igazi kaPovelson, inkolelo eyinkolelo-ze kubo, kwezinye iziguquko zobizo lwabo, ivuselelwe ezingqondweni zabazingeli.

Ngakho-ke lokho kuthuthumela ngamahemuhemu nezimangaliso eziphathelene naye, abadobi abambalwa abakhumbulayo, ngokubhekisela ku-Moby Dick, ezinsukwini zangaphambili zokudoba i-Sperm Whale, lapho kwakunzima izikhathi eziningi ukuyenga ama-whalemen asebenza isikhathi eside ukuba angenele ezingozini zale mpi entsha futhi enesibindi; amadoda anjalo ephikisana ngokuthi nakuba amanye amaleviyathani angase alandelwe ngethemba, kodwa ukujaha nokukhomba ngomkhonto ebonakala sengathi iSperm Whale kwakungekona okomuntu ofayo. Ukuthi ukukuzama, nakanjani kuzodatshulwa ingunaphakade elisheshayo. Kuleli khanda, kukhona imibhalo ephawulekayo okungase kubhekwe kuyo.

Noma kunjalo, kwakukhona abanye, okwathi naphezu kwalezi zinto base bekulungele ukuxosha uMoby Dick; futhi isibalo esikhulu nakakhulu okwathi, safuna nje ukuzwa ngaye kude futhi ngokungacacile, ngaphandle kwemininingwane eqondile yanoma iyiphi inhlekelele ethile, futhi ngaphandle kokuhambisana nezinkolelo-ze, ayeseqina ngokwanele ukuba angabalekela impi uma enikezwa.

Okunye kokusikisela okusendle okukhulunywa ngakho, njengokufika ekugcineni ukuxhunyaniswa noMkhoma Omhlophe ezingqondweni zabantu abathambekele ezeni, kwakuwukuzidla okungenasisekelo kokuthi uMoby Dick wayekuyo yonke indawo; ukuthi wayehlangane nezindawo ezihlukene ngesikhathi esisodwa.

Futhi, kumelwe ukuba izingqondo ezinjalo zaziwubuwula, kwakungenakho konke lokhu kuzikhukhumeza ngaphandle kokubonisa kancane amathuba okukholelwa ezeni. Ngoba njengoba izimfihlo zemisinga olwandle zingakaze zidalulwe, ngisho nasekucwaningeni okuhlakaniphile kakhulu; ngakho-ke izindlela ezifihliwe zeSperm Whale lapho ngaphansi kobuso zihlala, ingxenye enkulu, zingaphenduleki kubasukeli bakhe; futhi ngezikhathi ezithile kuye kwavela ukuqagela okunelukuluku kakhulu nokuphikisanayo mayelana nazo, ikakhulukazi mayelana nezindlela ezingaqondakali lapho, ngemva kokuzwakala ngokujula okukhulu, ezihambisa ngokukhulu ukushesha okungaka ziye ezindaweni ezikude kakhulu.

Kuyinto eyaziwa kahle kuyo yomibili imikhumbi yama-whale yaseMelika neyamaNgisi, kanye nento eyabekwa emlandweni ogunyaziwe eminyakeni edlule ngabakwaScoresby, ukuthi eminye imikhomo ibanjwe kude le enyakatho yePacific, okutholwe kuyo izidumbu zama-harpoon azulazula olwandle lwaseGreenland. Futhi akufanele kuphikiswe, ukuthi kwezinye zalezi zimo kuye kwamenyezelwa ukuthi isikhawu sesikhathi phakathi kokuhlaselwa okubili besingeke seqe izinsuku eziningi kakhulu. Ngakho-ke, ngokulandela, kukholwe kwabanye abamhlophe, ukuthi 'yindawo yaseNtshonalanga, inkinga ende kumuntu, ayikaze ibe yinkinga kumkhomo. Ukuze lapha, ekuhlangenwe nakho kwangempela kokuphila kwamadoda aphilayo, izinto ezidabukisayo ezazihlobene ezikhathini zakudala zentaba iStrello emaphakathi nezwe ePortugal (eduze kwayo kwakuthiwe kunechibi lapho ukuphahlazeka kwemikhumbi kwakuntanta phezulu phezulu); kanye naleyo ndaba enhle kakhulu yomthombo we-Arethusa eduze kwaseSirakhuse (okukholelwa ukuthi amanzi awo aphuma eZweni Elingcwele ngendlela engaphansi komhlaba); lokhu kulandisa okumangalisayo kucishe kulingane ngokugcwele namaqiniso ama-whalemen.

Ukuphoqeleka ukuba ajwayelane, ngakho-ke, ngama-prodigies afana nalawa; futhi ngokwazi ukuthi ngemva kokuhlasela okuphindaphindiwe, ngokungesabi, uMkhomo Omhlophe wawubaleke uphila; akunakumangaza ukuthi abanye abadobi bemikhomo kufanele baqhubekele phambili ezinkolelweni zabo; ukumemezela u-Moby Dick hhayi nje kuphela ukuthi ukhona yonke indawo, kodwa ongafi (ngoba ukungafi kodwa ukuvela yonke indawo ngesikhathi); ukuze kuthi noma kutshalwe imikhonto emaceleni akhe, usabhukuda engalimele; noma uma ngempela engase enziwe aphalaze igazi eliwugqinsi, umbono onjalo kungaba inkohliso eyesabekayo; ngoba futhi emabhukwini angaguquliwe kusele amakhulu amaligi, indiza yakhe engangcolisiwe izophinde ibonakale.

Kodwa ngisho nokuphucwa lezi zibikezelo ezingaphezu kwemvelo, kwakukhona okwanele emfanekisweni wasemhlabeni kanye nohlamvu olungenakuphikiswa lwe-monster ukushaya umcabango ngamandla angajwayelekile. Ngoba, kwakungeyona inqwaba yakhe engavamile kangangokuthi yayimhlukanisa kakhulu kwamanye ama-sperm whales, kodwa, njengoba kwaxoshwa kwenye indawo-isiphongo esingavamile esimhlophe qhwa esishwabene, kanye ne-hump ephakeme, e-pyramidical white. Lezi kwakuyizici zakhe ezivelele; izimpawu lapho, ngisho nasolwandle olungenamkhawulo, olungaziwa, aveza ubuyena bakhe, ebangeni elide, kulabo ababemazi.

Umzimba wakhe wonke wawunemishwe, futhi unamachashazi, futhi ufakwe imabula ngombala ofanayo oguquliwe, kangangokuthi, ekugcineni, wayesezuze igama lakhe elihlukile loMkhoma Omhlophe; igama, ngempela, elilungisiswa ngokoqobo ngenxa yesici sakhe esicacile, lapho libonakala lintweza emini enkulu phakathi kolwandle oluluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, lishiya igwebu elinokhilimu, konke okugcwele ukucwebezela kwegolide.

Futhi kwakungebona ubukhulu bakhe obungajwayelekile, noma umbala wakhe omangalisayo, noma umhlathi wakhe ophansi okhubazekile, owafaka umkhomo kakhulu ngokwesaba kwemvelo, njengaleyo nzondo engenakuqhathaniswa, ehlakaniphile, ngokusho kwe-akhawunti ethile, ayelokhu eveza ngokuphindaphindiwe ekuhlaselweni kwakhe. Ngaphezu kwakho konke, ukuhlehla kwakhe okukhohlisayo kwamdumaza kakhulu kunanoma yini enye. Ngokuba, lapho ebhukuda phambi kwabalandeli bakhe abajabulayo, nazo zonke izimpawu ezisobala zokwethuka, wayaziwa izikhathi eziningana ukuthi uphenduka ngokuzumayo, futhi, ewela phezu kwabo, noma agxilise izikebhe zabo ezinsikeni, noma abaphindisele emuva ngokumangala emkhunjini wabo.

Izigameko eziningi zokushona zase zihambele ukujaha kwakhe. Kodwa nakuba izinhlekelele ezifanayo, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi zazilimele kangakanani ogwini, zazingeyona neze into engavamile endaweni yokudoba; nokho, ezikhathini eziningi, lokho kwakubonakala kuwukucatshangelwa kwangaphambili komkhomo Omhlophe wonya, ukuthi konke ukunqunywa noma ukufa akubangela, kwakungabhekwa ngokuphelele njengokubangelwa i-ejenti engahlakaniphile.

Khona-ke, iJaji, intukuthelo evuthayo, ephazamisekile izingqondo zabazingeli bakhe ababephelelwe yithemba kakhulu zashukunyiswa, lapho phakathi kwezikebhe ezihlafunwayo, nezitho ezicwilayo zamaqabane adwenguliwe, zibhukuda ziphuma ezintendeni ezimhlophe zolaka olwesabekayo lomkhomo ziye endaweni ezolile, ecasulayo njengokukhanya kwelanga, okumamatheka ekuzalweni.

Izikebhe zakhe ezintathu zamzungeza, nezigwedlo namadoda ezungeza emikhawulweni; omunye ukaputeni, ethatha ummese ogqagqene ngaphambili, waphonseka emkhomeni, njengomdlali wase-Arkansas elwa nesitha sakhe, efuna ngobumpumputhe ngensingo eyi-intshi ayisithupha ukuze afinyelele ukuphila okujulile komkhomo. Lowo kaputeni kwakungu-Ahabi. Kwathi, kungazelelwe eshanela umhlathi wakhe ongezansi omise okwesikela ngaphansi kwakhe, uMoby Dick wayesevune umlenze ka-Ahabi, njengomsiki wensini yotshani ensimini. Akekho umTurk oyisigqoko, akekho umuntu waseVenetian oqashiwe noma waseMalaysia, owayengamshaya ngonya olwengeziwe. Ngakho-ke, kwakunesizathu esincane sokungabaza ukuthi selokhu kwenzeka leso sigameko esicishe sambulala, u-Ahabi wayekujabulele ukuziphindiselela okweqile kumkhomo, kwaba nakakhulu ukuthi ngenxa yokugula kwakhe okuphithizelayo ekugcineni wakwazi ukuhlobana naye, hhayi nje kuphela zonke izinkinga zakhe zomzimba, kodwa konke ukucasuka kwakhe kwengqondo nokungokomoya. Umkhoma Omhlophe wabhukuda phambi kwakhe njengomzimba we-monomaniac wazo zonke lezo zinhlangano ezinonya amadoda athile ajulile azizwa edla kuzo, aze asale ephila nenhliziyo nengxenye yamaphaphu. Lobo bubi obungaphatheki obukhona kusukela ekuqaleni; ekubuseni kwawo ngisho namaKrestu anamuhla abeka ingxenye eyodwa kwezimbili zomhlaba; ama-Ofi asendulo asempumalanga ayehlonishwa ngesithombe sawo sikadeveli;—U-Ahabi akazange awe phansi futhi asikhonze njengabo; kodwa edlulisela umqondo wawo kumkhomo onyanyekayo, wazilimaza wonke, wacwiywa kuwo. Konke lokho kakhulu maddens futhi izinhlungu; konke okuvusa imisila yezinto; lonke iqiniso linobubi kulo; konke okuqhekeza imisipha namakhekhe ubuchopho; wonke amademoni acashile okuphila nokucabanga; bonke ububi, ku-Ahabi ohlanyayo, benziwe samuntu ngokusobala, futhi benziwa ukuthi bahlaseleke ku-Moby Dick. Wanqwabelanisa phezu kwesifumbu esimhlophe somkhomo isamba sayo yonke intukuthelo nenzondo ezwiwa yilo lonke uhlanga lwakhe kusukela ku-Adamu kwehle; bese, sengathi isifuba sakhe besiwudaka, waqhuma igobolondo lenhliziyo yakhe eshisayo phezu kwaso.

Akunakwenzeka ukuthi le monomania kuye yaphakama ngokushesha ngesikhathi esinembile sokucwiywa kwakhe komzimba. Khona-ke, lapho egijimela esilwaneni, ephethe ummese, wayesededele inzondo engazelelwe, evuthayo, nengokwenyama; futhi lapho ethola isifo sohlangothi esamdwengula, mhlawumbe wezwa inxeba elibuhlungu emzimbeni, kodwa akukho okunye. Nokho, lapho ngalokhu kungqubuzana kuphoqeleka ukuba ajike abheke ekhaya, futhi izinyanga ezinde zezinsuku namaviki, u-Ahabi nosizi kwakulele ndawonye ku-hammock eyodwa, kuzungeza phakathi nobusika leyo ndawo esabekayo, ehhewula iPatagonian Cape; kwase kuba, lapho umzimba wakhe odabukile kanye nomphefumulo wakhe ophukile wopha komunye nomunye; kanjalo egxambukela, kwamhlanyisa. Ukuthi kwakungaleso sikhathi kuphela, ohambweni olubuyela ekhaya, ngemva kokuhlangana, lapho i-monomania yokugcina yambamba khona, kubonakala kuqinisekile eqinisweni lokuthi, ngezikhathi ezithile phakathi nephaseji, wayeyihlanya elidla lubi; futhi, nakuba ayengenawo umlenze emlenzeni, nokho amandla abaluleke kangaka ayecashe esifubeni sakhe saseGibhithe, futhi ngaphezu kwalokho kwaqiniswa ukudangala kwakhe, kangangokuthi abangane bakhe bomshado baphoqeleka ukuba bambophe ngokushesha, nalapho, lapho ehamba ngomkhumbi, egijima ku-hammock yakhe. Egqoke ijakhethi eyi-strait-jacket, washwibeka enyakaza ohlanyayo. Futhi, lapho ugijimela ezindaweni ezihlupheka kakhulu, umkhumbi, onama-stun'seil athambile wasabalala, wantanta ngaphesheya kwezindawo ezishisayo ezipholile, futhi, kukho konke ukubonakala, i-delirium yendoda endala yabonakala ishiywe ngemuva kwayo ne-Cape Horn swells, futhi yaphuma emgodini wayo omnyama wangena ekukhanyeni okubusisiwe nomoya; nalapho, lapho ethwele leyo nkampani, waqoqa i-front, kodwa ephaphathekile, futhi wakhipha imiyalo yakhe ezolile futhi; Futhi abangane bakhe bebonge kwabonga uNkulunkulu ubuhlakani obunesifo basehambile; nalapho, u-Ahabi, ekusithekeni kwakhe, wazidla. Ubuhlanya bomuntu ngokuvamile buwubuqili futhi buyinto edla kakhulu. Uma ucabanga ukuthi yabaleka, kungenzeka kodwa yaguqulwa yaba ngenye indlela ecashile. Ukuhlanya okugcwele kuka-Ahabi akuzange kudambe, kodwa kwangena ngokujula; njengo-Hudson onganqamuki, lapho lowo mhlonishwa uNorthman egeleza kancane, kodwa ngokungaqondakali edabula umhosha wase-Highland. Kodwa, njengasemonomania yakhe egelezayo, akukho nelilodwa gadi lohlanya olubanzi luka-Ahabi elalishiywe ngemuva; ngakho kulobo buhlanya obubanzi, akukho nelilodwa gatshana lokuhlakanipha kwakhe okukhulu kwemvelo elalifile. Lokho ngaphambi kokuba i-ejenti ephilayo, manje isibe yithuluzi eliphilayo. Lokho umkhomo omhlophe owawuyikho ku-Ahabi, sekuphawuliwe; ukuthi wayeyini, ngezinye izikhathi kimi, namanje akukashiwo.

Ngaphandle kwalokho okucatshangelwayo okusobala kakhulu okuthinta u-Moby Dick, owayengakwazi kodwa ngezikhathi ezithile ukuvusa umphefumulo wanoma yimuphi umuntu i-alamu ethile, kwakunomunye umcabango, noma kunalokho okungacacile, okwesabisayo okungenagama ngaye, okuthi ngezinye izikhathi ngokuqina kwawo kunqobe ngokuphelele konke okunye; futhi nokho kwakungaqondakali futhi kwakusondele kakhulu ekungakhulumeki, kangangokuthi ngacishe ngaphelelwa yithemba lokuyibeka ngendlela eqondakalayo.
