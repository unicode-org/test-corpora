EISHOMO 74. Omutwe gw’enyanja ya Sperm Whale—Okureeba okurikutaana.

Hati, hanu hariho enyanja ibiri empango, zitaire emitwe hamwe; reka tubagaite hamwe, tuteere hamwe ebyaitu.

Omu nkora mpango y’ebinyonyi ebirikwetwa folio leviathans, ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Sperm Whale n’ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Right Whale nizo zirikukirayo kwetegyerezibwa. Nizo nshozi zonka ezirikuhiigwa abantu butoosha. Aha Munantucketer, nibabaha ebipimo bibiri ebirengire aha miringo yoona erikumanywa ey’enyanja. Nk’oku entaaniso y’aheeru eri ahagati yaabo erikureebeka munonga omu mitwe yaabo; kandi nk’omutwe gwa buri omwe akaire aka nikahanikwa aha rubaju rwa Pequod; kandi nk’oku turikubaasa kuruga ahari kimwe kuza aha kindi omu bwengye, turikugyendera aha rubaju rw’enyanja kwonka:—ni nkahi, nshemerire kumanya, ngu noija kutunga omugisha murungi gw’okwega cetology erikukora kukira aha?

Omu mwanya gw’okubanza, nookwatwaho entaaniso eri ahagati y’emitwe egi omu bwijwire. Byona ni bihango munonga omu muntu weena ow’omunda; kwonka hariho okushushana kw’okubara okumwe omu Sperm Whale oku Right Whale erikubura omu muringo gw’obusaasi. Hariho emitwarize mingi omu mutwe gwa Sperm Whale. Ku orikukireeba, nooba otarikwenda kumuha obukuru bwingi munonga, omu nshonga y’okumuha ekitiinisa ekirikujanjaara. Omu nshonga eriho hati, n’ekitiinisa eki nikitunguuka ahabw’erangi y’omutwe gwe ey’entangahuuzi n’omunyugunyu aha mutwe, ekirikuha akamanyiso k’emyaka y’obukuru n’okumanya kwingi. Omu bugufu, niwe abajubi omu by’obuhangwa barikweta “enyanja y’omutwe gw’enzirugavu.”

Hati ka tureebe ekitarikushushana munonga omu mitwe egi—nikwo, ebicweka bibiri ebikuru munonga, eriisho n’okutu. Enyima munonga aha rubaju rw’omutwe, kandi ahansi, haihi n’enkokora y’orubaju rw’enyanja yoona, waasherura omu muringo mukye, aha muheru noija kureeba eriisho eritaine nshonda, eri orikuteekateeka ngu n’eriisho ry’omwana gw’endogoya muto; n’ahabw’ekyo omu bipimo byona n’obuhango bw’omutwe.

Hati, kuruga omu mwanya ogu ogw’omutaano ogw’aha rubaju rw’amaisho g’enyanja, nikireebeka gye ngu tarikubaasa kureeba ekintu ekiri omu maisho kyonyini, ti kukira oku arikubaasa kureeba ekindi ekiri enyima kyonyini. Omu kigambo kimwe, omwanya gw’amaisho g’enyanja niguhikaana n’ogw’amatu g’omuntu; kandi noobaasa kuteekateeka, ahabwawe, oku kyakubaire nikikukwataho, waaba nooshwijuma ebintu aha rubaju kurabira omu matu gaawe. Okashanga ngu noobaasa kuragiira diguri nka makumi ashatu zonka z’okureeba omu maisho g’omurongooti gw’okureeba ogw’orubaju orugoroire; kandi ezindi nka makumi ashatu ziri enyima yaayo. Omuzigu waawe orikukirayo obushaarizi ku araabe yaabaire naagyendera akuhikire, aine empi eimukire omu ihangwe, okaba otarikubaasa kumureeba, nk’oku yaabaire naakwiba kuruga enyima. Omu kigambo kimwe, okaabaire oine emigongo ebiri, nk’oku turikubaasa kugira; kwonka, omu bwire nibwo bumwe, n’eby’omumaisho bibiri (eby’omumaisho by’aha rubaju): ahabw’okuba n’enki ekirikuhindura omu maisho g’omuntu—n’enki, buzima, kureka amaisho ge?

Ekindi, obwo omu nyamaishwa ezindi ezirikukira obwingi ezi ndikuteekateekaho hati, amaisho gateirwe omu muringo ogutarikwetegyerezibwa kubaasa kujwanzya amaani gaago g’okureeba, kugira ngu gareete ekishushani kimwe beitu bitari bibiri aha bwongo; omwanya gw’omutaano gw’amaisho g’enyanja, agabaganisiibwemu gye nk’oku gari ahabw’omutwe guhamire ogwa cubic feet nyingi, ogurikutunguuka ahagati yaago nk’orushozi ruhango orurikutaanisa enyanja ibiri omu bihanga; eki, buzima, kiine kutaanisa kimwe ebiteekateeko ebi buri kicweka ky‘omubiri ekiine obugabe kirikuha. N’ahabw’ekyo, ekinyonyi ekiine kureeba ekishushani kimwe eky’omutaano aha rubaju oru, n’ekindi eky’omutaano aha rubaju orwo; obwo byona ebiri ahagati biine kuba biri omwirima mwingi kandi obutagira kintu ahariwe. Omu mazima, omuntu naabaasa kugambwa ngu naareeba ensi ari omu kabokisi k’okurinda akarimu ebikwato bibiri ebikwataniise eby’okukoresa omu dirisa ye. Kwonka aha nkungu, ebikwato ebi bibiri nibiteebwamu bitarikushushana, bikora amadirisa abiri gatari gamwe na gamwe, kwonka eky’obusaasi nibiremesa okureeba. Obumanyiso obu obw’omutaano obw’amaisho g’enyanja n’ekintu eki oine kuteekateekaho obwire bwona omu by’obuvubi; kandi n’okwijukwa omushomi omu bimwe ebirikukurataho.

Ekibuuzo ky’okwenda kumanya kandi ekirikukirayo kushoberwa nikibaasa kutandika aha nshonga egi erikureebeka nk’erikukwata ahari Leviathan. Kwonka nyine kumarwa n’ekigambo. Amaisho g’omuntu kasita gakingire omu mushana, ekikorwa ky’okureeba nikiba kitari ky’okwekundira; nikwo kugira ngu, tarikubaasa kuhwera omu muringo gw’ebyoma kureeba ebintu byona ebiri omu maisho ge. Nangwa n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, eki omuntu weena arikurabamu nikiija kumwegyesa, ngu n’obu araabe naabaasa kutwara omu kusheenya ebintu ataine kushoroora omu kureeba kumwe, nikiba kitarikubaasika ahariwe, n’obwegyendesereza, kandi omu bwijwire, kushwijuma ebintu bibiri byona—n’obu biraabe biri bihango nari bikye—omu kaanya kamwe; never mind baaba nibagwejegyera aha rubaju kandi bakwatagana. Kwonka hati ku oraabe noija kutaanisa ebintu ebi bibiri, kandi buri kimwe okyehinguririize oruzingo rw’omwirima mwingi; obwo, kubaasa kureeba kimwe aharibyo, omu muringo gw’okureetera ebiteekateeko byawe kukiteekateekaho, ekindi nikiija kurugaho kimwe omu biteekateeko byawe eby’obunaku obu. Mbwenu shi, n’eky’enyanja kiri kita? N’amazima, amaisho ge gombi, omurigo, gaine kukora omurundi gumwe; kwonka shi obwongo bwe nibukira obw’omuntu, nibuteerana, kandi nibukora gye, ngu omu kaanya kamwe nibubaasa kushwijuma n’obwegyendesereza ebintu bibiri ebitarikushushana, kimwe aha rubaju rwe rumwe, kandi ekindi kiri aha rubaju orurikuhakanisa rwona? Ku araabe naabaasa, obwo n’ekintu ky’okutangaaza omuriwe, nk’oku omuntu yaabaire naabaasa omurundi gumwe kuraba omu kworeka ebizibu bibiri eby’omutaano omuri Euclid. Kandi, twacondooza gye, tihaine obutaikirizana omu kugyeragyeranisa oku.

Nikibaasa kuba kiri ekishuba ky’obusha, kwonka kikaba nikireebeka ahariinye obwire bwona, ngu okutengeeta kw’omutaano okw’okutambura okurikworekwa enyanja ezimwe ku zirikuteganisibwa amaato ashatu nari ana; obwoba n’obujunaanizibwa bw’okutiinatiinisa okutari kwa buriijo, okurikukirayo omu nshozi nk’ezo; Ninteekateeka ngu ebi byona nibiruga omu kushoberwa okutaine buhwezi okw’okwenda, omu maani gaabo ag’okureeba agabaganisiibwemu kandi agarikuhakanisa munonga gaine kubakwataho.

Kwonka okutu kw’enyanja y’enyanja kwijwire okwenda kumanya nk’eriisho. Waaba ori omugyenyi weena omu ruganda rwabo, noobaasa kuhiiga aha mitwe egi ebiri kumara eshaaha nyingi, kandi otakazoora ekicweka ekyo. Amatu tigaine ibabi ry’aheeru ryona; kandi omu kiina kyonyini torikubaasa kutaho ekipande ky’okuhandiikaho, n’ahabw’ekyo n’edakiika y’okutangaaza. Nikituura kakye enyima y’eriisho. Aha bikwatiraine n’amatu gaabo, entaaniso egi nkuru neebaasa kureebwa ahagati y’enyanja y’omukazi n’ey’aha rubaju rwa buryo. N’obu okutu kw’okubanza kuraabe kwine ekicweka ky’aheeru, okw’aha muheru nikukingwaho kimwe kandi omu muringo ogurikushushana n’ekipande, kugira ngu tikurikureebwa gye kuruga aheeru.

Tikirikutangaaza kumanya, ngu ekihangirwe kihango nk’enyanja y’enyanja nikibaasa kureeba ensi kurabira omu riisho rikye munonga, kandi kihurire enkuba kurabira omu kutu okukye okukira okw’enkongoro? Kwonka amaisho ge ku garaabe gaabaire gari mahango nk’ekishushani ky’ekipimo kihango kya Herschel; kandi amatu ge gakaba gari maingi nk’ebishengye bya katedrali; ekyo kikaba nikiija kumureetera kwongyera kureeba gye, nari kuhurira gye? Tikwo kiri na kakye.—Mbwenu shi ahabw’enki nooteeraho “kugaziha” ebiteekateeko byawe? Okihindure.

Hati reka n’ebikwato byona eby’okuteeraho n’eby’okukoresa omu muriro ebi twine omu ngaro, tubikore aha mutwe gw’enyanja y’omukazi, ngu egwejegyeire ahansi kuza ahaiguru; reero, orikutemba aha madaara kuhika aha mutwe, otungye okureeba ahansi omu kanwa; kandi kuri kitari ngu omubiri hati gutaanisiibweho kimwe, n’etabaaza tukaba nitubaasa kushuuma omu mpango mpango ya Kentucky Mammoth ey’omu nda ye. Kwonka reka tukwate aha ryino eri, kandi tureebe ahu turi. Buzima ka n’akanwa karungi kandi akarikureebeka nk’akashemeire! okuruga ahansi kuhika ahaiguru, erimu orunyiriri, nari shi empapura ezirimu ekipande ky’omweru ekirikumurinkana, ekirikumurinkana nk’ebijwaro by’abagore.

Kwonka rugayo hati, oreebe orubaju oru orurikumanyisa orubaju rwahansi, orurikushusha nk’ekifundikizo kiraingwa ekikye eky’ekibokisi ky’ebinyonyi ekihango munonga, ekiine ekikwato ky’okukinga aha muheru gumwe, omu mwanya gw’orubaju rumwe. Waagiteera ahaiguru, kugira ngu ogitungye aha mutwe, kandi okashohoza emirongooti y’ameino gaayo, neeshusha nk’enyamaishwa erikutiinisa; kandi nk'abo, kasha! nikihamya baingi ngu n’omuntu mubi omu by’obuvubi, ou ebihimba ebi birikugwaho n’amaani g’okuhanika. Kwonka ekirikutiinisa munonga n’okureeba, ku orikuza ahansi omu nyanja, nooreeba ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa sulky whale, kirikuzengyerera aho kihanikire, n’orubaju rwakyo oruhango, oburaingwa bwakyo nka fuuti ikumi n’etaano, kihanikire kigoroire ahansi omu nshonda ezihikire n’omubiri gwe, ahabw’ensi yoona nk’ekibunu ky’emeeri. Ekinyonyi eki tikifiire; aine omwoyo gwonka; kuruga omu miringo, obundi; okubura amaani; kandi akagwejegyera munonga, ngu ebihimba by’orubaju rwe bihuumwire, byamusiga aho omu mbeera egyo etarikushemeza, okujumwa aha ruganda rwe rwona, oruine, hatariho kubangaanisa, kumuteera enshaya z’okukinga.

Omu mbeera nyingi orubaju oru orw’ahansi—rurikuba rurikwanguha kushohozibwa omunyabwengye—nirurugaho kandi ruteebwa aha mutwe gw’enyanja n’ekigyendererwa ky’okuihaho ameino g’amahembe g’entare, n’okuha eby’okurya by’eigufa eryo erigumire erikwera eri abajubi barikukoresa omu kuhanga ebintu by’emiringo yona eby’okutangaaza, otwariiremu emiti y’okuteeraho, ebikwato by’okuteeraho n’ebikwato by’okuteeraho omu ngaro.

N’ekikwato kiraingwa kandi ekiruhire, orushaya nirukururwa aha bwato, nk’oku ruri enkongi; kandi obwire obuhikire ku burikuhika—ebiro bikye bwanyima y’omurimo ogundi—Queequeg, Daggoo, na Tashtego, ahabw’okugira ngu boona n’abashaho b’amaino abamanyiriire, nibatebeekanisa kuteera ameino. Queequeg aine ekikwato ky’okusharaho ekigumire, naateera ebihaha; obwo orushaya niruteerwa ahansi kuhika aha bikomo by’empeta, kandi ekikwato ky’okuteeraho kirikuteebwaho kuruga ahaiguru, nizikurura ameino aga, nk’oku ente za Michigan zirikukurura ebihimba by’emiti y’emizeituuni eya ira kuruga omu itaka ry’emiti y’omu ishwa. Okutwarira hamwe hariho ameino makumi ana na abiri goona; omu nshozi z’omu nyanja ezikuzire, ezijwaire munonga, kwonka zitavundiire; nari kwijura bwanyima y’omurembe gwaitu ogw’obuhangwa. Enshaya bwanyima neesharwa omu bipande, kandi erundwa hare nk’ebipande by’okwombekyeramu amaju.





EISHOMO RYA 75. Omutwe gw’Enyanja ya Buryo—Okureeba okurikutaana.

Okuraba aha rubaju rw’enyanja, hati ka tureebe gye omu bwire buraingwa aha mutwe gwa Right Whale.

Nk’oku kiri omu nkora yoona omutwe gw’enyanja ya Sperm Whale ey’ekitiinisa nigubaasa kugyeragyeranisibwa n’ekigaari ky’orutaro eky’Abarooma (na munonga omu maisho, ahu kirikwetoorora munonga); n’ahabw’ekyo, ku orikureeba omu bwijwire, omutwe gwa Right Whale nigushushana n’enkaito mpango erimu ebigyere bya galliot. Emyaka magana abiri enyima omuvugi w’enyanja omugurusi kuruga Nedaaki akagyeragyeranisa ekishushani kyayo n’eky’omukozi w’enkaito ekyahererukireyo. Kandi omu nshonga egi niyo emwe ey’aha muheru nari enkaito, omukaikuru ogwo ow’omu nfumu y’okureeberera abaana, n’abaana abarikuzaara, akaba naabaasa kuraara gye munonga, we n’oruzaaro rwe rwona.

Kwonka ku orikweyongyera haihi n’omutwe ogu omukuru nigutandika kutwara emiringo etari emwe n’emwe, kurugiirira aha miteekateekyere yaawe. Waaba oyemereire aha mutwe gwayo okareeba ebiina ebi bibiri ebiri omu muringo gwa F, noija kutwara omutwe gwona ahabwa bass-viol mpango munonga, kandi ebi spiracles, ebiina ebiri omu sounding-board yaayo. Reero, kandi, waata amaisho gaawe aha kintu eki eky’okutangaaza, ekiine ekibunu, ekirikushushana n’ekicupuri aha mutwe gw’ekibunu—ekintu eki eky’ekibira, ekiine ebibunu, eki Abanya-Greenland barikweta “omutwe,” kandi abajubi b’omu Mashuma “ekikopo” ky’Enyanja ya Right Whale; orikuta amaisho gaawe ahari eki kyonka, okaba otwara omutwe gw’omuti gw’omuti muhango munonga, ogurimu ekiina ky’enyonyi omu kifuba kyagwo. Omu muringo gwona, waareeba ebyo bikooko ebihuriire ebirikuteera aha aha boneti egi, ekiteekateeko nk’ekyo nikiija kuba kiri haihi kukuhikaho; kureka, buzima, okukunda kwawe kwateebwaho ekigambo ky’obuhangwa “engure” nakyo ekiheebwayo; omu mbeera egyo noija kufayo munonga kuteekateeka oku ekicuncu eki eky’amaani buzima kiri omugabe w’enyanja oine ekirunga, ou ekirunga kye kya kinyasi kimuteirweho omu muringo ogu ogw’okutangaaza. Kwonka ekinyonyi eki kyaba kiri omugabe, n’omuntu orikureebeka nk’omunyabwengye munonga kubaasa kushemeza ekirunga. Reeba ogwo munywa gwa hansi ogurikuhanikwa! ka hariho sulk na pout mpango! sulk na pout, omu kupima kw’omubeizi, oburaingwa bwa fuuti nka makumi abiri n’oburaingwa bwa fuuti itaano; sulk and pout erikuza kukuha galoni nka 500 z’amajuta n’ebindi.

Eky’obusaasi kihango, hati, ngu ekinyonyi eki eky’omugisha mubi kishemerire kuba kiine eminywa y’enkyenzi. Ekicweka eki nikiba kiri nk’ekigyere kimwe omu buraingwa. Obundi nyina omwana omu bwire bw’okuhuumuraho obukuru akaba naavuga emeeri aha rubaju rw’enyanja ya Peru, obu emisinde yaareetera enyanja kusheesheka. Aha mutwe gw’omunwa ogu, nk’aha mutwe gw’omuryango ogurikusheesheka, hati nitusheesheka omu kanwa. Aha kigambo kyangye nkaba ndi Mackinaw, nkaba nyine kutwara eki nk’omunda ya wigwam y’Abahinda. Mukama murungi! ogu nigwo muhanda ogu Yona yaagyendeiremu? Obusingye buri nk’oburaingwa bwa fuuti ikumi na ibiri, kandi niburuga omu rubaju orurungi munonga, nk’oku hariho omuti gw’omugongo ogwa buriijo aho; n’obu embaju ezi eziine embaju, eziine obugoye, ezirimu obwoya, nizituha ebyo bipande by’okutangaaza, ebiine ekicweka ky’ahaiguru, ebiine omubiri nk’ogwa scimetar eby’amagufa g’enyanja, tugire ngu magana ashatu aha rubaju rumwe, ebirikurugiirira aha kicweka kya ruguru ky’omutwe nari eigufa ry’omutwe, birikukora ebyo bifunjo bya Venetian ebyagambirweho ahandi omu muringo gw’okushoboorora. Empimbi z’amagufa aga zirimu ebihimba by’obwoya, ebi Right Whale erikurabiramu kusheenya amaizi, kandi omu nshonga zaayo ezirimu obuzibu bw’eby’enyanja ebikye, ku birikuba bikingire akanwa nibiraba omu nyanja za brit omu bwire bw’okuriisa. Omu bifunjo bya rwagati eby’amagufa, nk’oku byemereire omu ntebeekanisa yaabyo ey’obuhangwa, harimu obubonero obumwe oburikushemeza, ebifunjo, ebiina, n’ebifunjo, ahu abamwe abavugi b’enyanja barikubara emyaka y’ekihangirwe, nk’emyaka y’omuti gw’omukyere barikurabira omu mpeta zaagwo ezirikwetoorora. N’obu obuhame bw’omutindo ogu buraabe buri hare n’okworekwa, kwonka bwine obuhooho bw’okugyeragyeranisa. Omu muringo gwona, twaba nitukiikiriza, twine kuha Right Whale emyaka mingi munonga kukira oku orikureeba omu kubanza kirikureebeka nk’ekihikire.

Omu bwire bwa ira, nikireebeka ngu hakaba hariho ebiteekateeko by’okwenda kumanya ebirikukirayo ebirikukwata aha mpumi ezi. Omuvugi omwe omuri Purchas naazeeta “ebihimba” by’okutangaaza ebiri omunda y’akanwa k’enyanja;* ondiijo, “ebihimba by’empunu”; omugurusi wa kashatu omuri Hackluyt naakoresa orurimi oru orurikushemeza: “Hariho ebipapaijo nka magana abiri na makumi ataano ebirikukura ahari buri rubaju rw’ekicweka kye eky’ahaiguru , ebirikugurukaguruka aha rurimi rwe ahari buri rubaju rw’akanwa ke.”

*Eki nikitwijutsya ngu buzima Right Whale eine ekika ky'ebireju, nari shi ebireju, ebirimu obwoya bukye oburikwera obujanjaire aha kicweka kya ruguru eky'omuheru gw'aheeru gw'orubaju rwa hansi. Obumwe ebicupuri ebi nibireetaho ekishushani ky’obushuma aha maisho ge agaabaire gari ag’ekitiinisa.

Nk’oku buri omwe arikumanya, “ebihimba by’empunu” ebyo byonka, “ebipapaijo,” “ebihimba,” “ebishumuruzo,” nari eki orikwenda kyona, nibiha abakazi ebibunu byabo n’ebindi bikoreso by’okuhamya. Kwonka omu nshonga egi yonka, okwetenga kumazire obwire buraingwa nikukyendeera. Omu bwire bwa Queen Anne nibwo eigufa ryabaire riri omu kitiinisa kyaryo, farthingale ekaba eri omurembe gwona obwire obwo. Kandi nk’oku abo bakazi ba ira baabaire nibagyendera omu kushemererwa, n’obu baraabe bari omu nshaya z’enyanja, nk’oku orikubaasa kugira; n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, omu kunywa amaizi, n’obutateekateeka nk’obwo, ebiro ebi nituguruka ahansi y’ebishaya nibyo bimwe ahabw’okwerinda; omukyeeka kuba eihema erijanjaire aha igufa rimwe.

Kwonka hati yeebwa byona ebirikukwata aha mpumi n’ebireju kumara akaire kakye, kandi, oyemereire omu kanwa ka Right Whale, oreebe okwehinguririize busya. Okureeba enyomyo ezi zoona ez’amagufa zitebeekanisiibwe omu muringo gw’oburyo, torikuteekateeka ngu ori omunda y’ekicweka kihango kya Haarlem, kandi orikureeba aha mipiira yaakyo rukumi? Ahabwa kapeti aha kicweka ky’omubiri twine omukyeeka gw’enkoko erikukirayo kworoba—orurimi, orurikuhamibwa, nk’oku kirikugambwa, ahansi y’akanwa. Nikiba kiri ekishaju munonga kandi nikikora gye, kandi nikibaasa kusheenyagurika omu kukihanika aha nkungu. Orurimi oru rwonka hati ruri omu maisho gaitu; omu kureeba okurikuhinguraho nshemerire kugira ngu ekaba eri ey’ebikopo mukaaga; nikwo kugira ngu, nikiija kukuha amajuta nk’ago.

Ere eki, oine kuba waarebire gye amazima g’ebi natandikireho—ngu Sperm Whale na Right Whale ziine emitwe haihi etarikushushana. Nahabwekyo, omubugufu: omu Right Whale’s tihariho iziba rihango ry’amaizi g’ekihama; tihariho meno g’enjojo na kakye; tihariho rubaju ruraingwa kandi orukye orw’orubaju rw’ahansi, nk’orwa Sperm Whale. Kandi n’omu Sperm Whale tiharimu n’emwe ahari ezo mpumi z’amagufa; tihariho munwa muhango ogwahansi; kandi n’ekintu kyona eky’orurimi.
