"Eby'okuvuga! eby'okuvuga! Shuka ahansi omu buhango bwawe, iwe nyanja, ngu kitakakyerereirwe munonga, Ahabu ashereekye omurundi ogu ogw'aha muheru, ogw'aha muheru aha kamanyiso ke! Ninreeba: emeeri! emeeri! Mugyende, abashaija bangye! Timurikwija kujuna emeeri yangye?"

Kwonka abavugi b’obwato ku baabaire nibagyema obwato bwabo n’amaani kuraba omu nyanja ezirikuteera enyondo, obuta bw’embaaho ibiri obubaire butakateirwe enyanja z’enyanja bukaguruka, kandi omu kaanya kakye haihi, obwato obwabaire buremirwe kumara akaire kakye bukaba buri haihi kuhikaana n’ebicu; abavugi baayo abarikugyendera omu kicweka, barikusheesheka, barikuteeraho munonga kwemereza ekiina kandi bakasheenya amaizi agarikusheesheka.

Omu bwire obwo, ahabw’akaanya kakye ako akarikureeba, enyundo ya Tashtego ey’omutwe gw’omuti ekaguma ehanikire omu ngaro ze; kandi ebendera erikutukura, erikumuzingira kimwe kya kabiri nk’erimu ekijwaro ky’okujwara, reero yaayesheesheka egororokire kuruga ahariwe, nk’omutima gwe wenka ogurikuza omu maisho; obwo Starbuck na Stubb, beemereire aha bowsprit ahansi, bakareeba ekicuncu ekirikushuuma ahonaaho nk’oku we yaareebire.

"Ekinyonyi, ekinyonyi! Ahaiguru, aha mutwe! Oh, imwe mwena amaani g'omuyaga, hati munnywegyere haihi! Reka Starbuck atafa, ku araabe naafa aine, omu kuzirika kw'omukazi. Ahaiguru, ningira—imwe abashema, orushaya! orushaya, orushaya! Egi n'enkomerero y'okushaba kwangye kwona? Oh okushaba kwangye kwona? Ahabu, reeba, omuvugi w'emotoka y'omwika, omuvugi w'emotoka y'emigugu, naahinduka kutubugana, ou omurimo gwe gurikumugira ngu tarikubaasa kurugaho hati!

"Otayemerera aharubaju rwangye, kureka oyemerere ahansi yangye, weena ou ori hati orikwija kuhwera Stubb; ahabw'okuba Stubb, nawe, naakwata aha. Ninkushekyerera, iwe ekinyonyi ekirikushekyerera! Ahabu, off enkaito n’jaketi ahariyo;

"Cherries? Ninyenda ngu tube ahu zirikukura kwonka. Oh, Stubb, nyine amatsiko ngu maama omworo akashohoza part-pay yangye ere eki; kyaba kitari kityo, coppers nkye hati niziija kumuhikaho, ahabw'okuba orugyendo rwahwa."

Kuruga aha buta bw’emeeri, haihi abavugi b’emeeri boona hati bakaba bahanikwa bataine ki barikukora; enyundo, ebicweka by’embaaho, amacumu, n’eby’okuteera, ebyabaire bigumire omu ngaro zaabo omu muringo gw’ebyoma, nk’oku baabaire bahunga kuruga omu mirimo yaabo etari emwe n’emwe; amaisho gaabo goona agabaire gashemeize gakaba nigareeba ekinyonyi ekikuru, ekyabaire nikitengeeta omutwe gwakyo ogwabaire gutebeekanisiibwe omu muringo gw’okutangaaza kuruga aha rubaju rumwe kuza aha rundi, kyayohereza ekibunu kihango ky’ebicupuri by’ekicweka ky’enziga ebyabaire nibijanjaara munonga omu maisho ge obwo arikwiruka. Okuhoorera enzigu, okuhoorera enzigu okwa juba, obubi obutahwaho bikaba biri omu rubaju rwe rwona, kandi n’obu omuntu orikufa araabe yaabaire naabaasa kukora byona, omusingye gw’omweru ogugumire ogw’omu buso bwe gukaba guteera omutwe gw’emeeri aha rubaju rwa buryo, okuhitsya obu abantu n’embaaho byayehuzya. Abamwe bakagwa ahansi amaisho. Nk’emotoka ezaabaire ziihirweho, emitwe y’abavugi b’enkoni abaabaire bari ahaiguru ekaba neetengeeta aha nsingo zaabo eziri nk’ez’ente. “Emeeri! Emotoka y’okuziika!—emotoka y’okuziika eya kabiri!” Ahabu akagamba n’eiraka rihango ari omu bwato; “embaaho zaayo zikaba nizibaasa kuba ez’Abamerika bonka!”

Okwejumba ahansi y’emeeri eyabaire neeshutama, ekinyonyi eki kikaba nikiiruka nikitetema aha mutwe gwakyo; kwonka akahinduka ahansi y’amaizi, yaagaruka yaateera amasasi ahonaaho, hare n’obuta obundi, kwonka omu yaadi nkye kuruga aha bwato bwa Ahabu, ahu, kumara obwire, yaabaire agwejegyeire.

"Ninhindura omubiri gwangye kuruga aha izooba. Niki ho, Tashtego! reka mpurire enyundo yaawe. Oh! imwe emitwe yangye eshatu etaine rubaju; iwe ekibunu ekitaine rubaju; n'ekibunu ky'emeeri kyonka ekirikuteganisibwa ruhanga; iwe ekibumbe ky'emeeri ekigumire, n'omutwe gw'okwetunguura, n'omutwe gw'emeeri oguri nk'omuti gw'omuti, - n'okufa-ekitiinisa n'emeeri! fond pride of meanest shipwreck captains? Oh, okufa kw'omuntu wenka aha magara g'omuntu wenka! byona-okucwekyereza kwonka ekitarikusingura whale; kuhika aha muheru ninkuteera; whale! N’ahabw’ekyo , n’okureka eicumu!”

Eicumu rikaba riteerwa; ekinyonyi ekyateirwe kikaguruka kyaza omu maisho; n’omuvuyo gw’okuta omuriro orunyiriri rukaba rurikuraba omu bifunjo;—run foul. Ahabu yainama kugisheenya; akagisheenya; kwonka okuhinduka kw’enyonyi kukamukwata omu bitsya, kandi ataine iraka nk’oku abaturuki abatema barikuteera obuta omunyabuzaare waabo, akateerwa amasasi kuruga omu bwato, abavugi batakamanyire ngu agiire. Omu kaanya kakye akakuratsireho, ekikwato ky’okuteera amaisho ekiremeire ekyabaire kiri aha muheru gw’omugoye ogw’aha muheru kikaguruka kyaruga omu ntuumo eyabaire eri busha, kyateera omuvugi w’enyanja, kandi kyateera enyanja, kyabura omu buhango bwayo.

Okumara akaanya kakye, abavugi b’obwato obwabaire buri omu nshobi bakeemerera; reero yaahinduka. “Emeeri? Ruhanga omukuru, emeeri eri nkahe?” Tihakyahingwireho obwire buraingwa, kurabira omu bafumu abatarikureebwa gye kandi abarikuteganisa bakareeba ekishushani kye ekirikuhwaho aha rubaju, nk’omu Fata Morgana ey’omuyaga; emiti y’ahaiguru yonka ekaruga omu maizi; n’obu baraabe baabaire bahamire ahabw’okukunda munonga, nari obwesigwa, nari enkomerero, aha myanya yaabo eyabaire eri ahaiguru munonga, abapagani abavugi b’enkoni bakaba bakigumizamu nibareeba enyanja erikubira. Kandi hati, enzindo eziri ahagati zikakwata obwato bwonka, n’abavugi baarwo boona, na buri nshonda erikuzengyerera, na buri nkoni y’eicumu, kandi erikwetoorora, erimu amagara n’etaine magara, yoona eyehinguririize kandi eyehinguririize omu nkungu emwe, ekatwara akacweka kakye munonga aka Pequod kuruga omu maisho.

Kwonka nk’oku ebinyonyi ebirikuhererukayo byabaire nibyeshuka aha mutwe gw’Omuhindi ogwabaire gubiikire aha mutwe gw’enyanja, bikasigaho inci nkye z’ekibunu ekigumire kirikureebeka, hamwe n’ebishengye by’ebendera ebiraingwa ebirikusheesheka, ebyabaire nibigurukaguruka omu bucureezi, n’okuteerana kw’okushekyerera, aha mutwe gw’enyanja erikucwekyereza; enyundo ekaba neeguruka enyima eimutsirwe omu mwanya, omu kikorwa ky’okuteera ebendera omu bwangu kandi obwo omu bwangu aha rubaju orurikugwa. Ekicuncu ky’omu mwanya ekyabaire nikishekyerera kikaba kikuratiire emotoka mpango kuruga omu nju yaakyo ey’obuhangwa omu nyonyoozi, kirikuteera ebendera, kandi kirikuteganisa Tashtego okwo; ekinyonyi eki hati kikatunga omugisha gw’okutaho eipapa ryakyo rihango eririkugurukaguruka ahagati y’enyundo n’enku; kandi omu bwire nibwo bumwe arikuhurira okushemererwa okwo okw’omu mwanya, omunyamaishwa owaabaire azibiire ahansi, omu kuhuuta kwe okw’okufa, akaguma aine enyundo ye egumire aho; kandi nikwo ekinyonyi ky’omu iguru, n’okuyomba kwa baamaraika bakuru, n’omunwa gwakyo ogw’obugabe guteirwe ahaiguru, n’ekishushani kye kyona eky’obukwatwa kizingire omu bendera ya Ahabu, kyashuuma n’emeeri yaakyo, eyaabaire, nka Sitaane, etarikwija kugwa omu muriro ogutaraara okuhitsya obu yaaheza kukurura ekicweka ky’eiguru ekiine amagara hamwe nawe, kandi helme na helme.

Hati obunyonyi obukye bukaguruka nibuteera orwaari aha mutwe gw’enyanja eyaabaire neekyebera; omuyaga ogurikwera ogurimu obuzibu gukaba niguteera aha mbaju zaagwo eziri aha rubaju; obwo byona bikagwa, kandi ekikingirizo kihango ky’enyanja kyagumizamu nikizengyerera nk’oku kyabaire nikizengyerera emyaka enkumi itaano enyima.





Okuhendera

“KANDI NYOWE NKAHONOKA KUKUGAMBA” Yobu.

Omuzaano gwahwa. Mbwenu shi ahabw’enki hanu hariho omwe orikurugayo?— Ahabw’okuba omwe akahonokaho omu kucwekyerera kw’emeeri.

Kikabaho omu muringo gw’akabi, ngu bwanyima y’okubura kwa Parsee, nkaba ndi ogwo ou Fates yaateireho kutwara omwanya gw’omuteezi w’obuta wa Ahabu, obu omuteezi w’obuta ogwo yaatwara omwanya ogutarimu kintu kyona; niwe omwe, ou, aha izooba ry’aha muheru abashaija bashatu ku baasheeshekire kuruga omu bwato oburikutengeeta, akateerwa aha rubaju rw’enyima. Nahabwekyo, ndikuzengyerera aha rubaju rw’ekishushani ekyakuratsireho, kandi omu kureeba kwakyo kwona, obu okunuuna okwabaire kuhweireho ekicweka ky’emeeri eyabiire kwahikire ahariinye, nkaba obwo, kwonka mpora mpora, ninkururwa kuza omu nkungu y’okukinga. Ku naahikireyo, ekaba eherize kukyendeera ekahinduka ekiziba ky’ekirimu. Okwetoorora n’okwetoorora, obwo, kandi kuguma nikuteerana kuza aha kicupuri ekirikwiragura ekirikushushana n’eibaare aha rubaju rw’enziga egyo erikwetoorora mpora mpora, nk’oku Ixion endiijo ei naakozire erikwetoorora. Till, okutunga omwanya ogwo ogw’omugasho, ekibumba ekirikwiragura ekirikuza ahaiguru kyaturika; kandi hati, kicungwirwe ahabw’entuura yaakyo ey’obugobya, kandi, ahabw’okuguruka kwayo kwingi, erikwimuka n’amaani maingi, ekibunu ky’okucungura amagara eky’omu sanduuki kikateera oburaingwa kuruga omu nyanja, kyagwa ahaiguru, kandi kyaguruka aha rubaju rwangye. N’okushemezibwa ahabw’eshanduukye egyo, kumara haihi ekiro kimwe n’ekiro kimwe, nkaba nshutama aha maizi makuru agarikworoba kandi agari nk’aga dirge. Enyanja ezitarikuhutaaza, zikaba zigyendera aha rubaju nk’eziine enkufuro aha kanwa; enkongoro z’omu nyanja ez’omu ishwa zikaba zigyendera aha meeri ziine eminwa erimu ebijwaro. Aha kiro kya kabiri, emeeri ekahika haihi, yaayeyongyera kwirira haihi, kandi aha muheru yantwara. Rachel akaba ari omuvugi w’emotoka y’emigugu, ngu omu kugaruka kusherura abaana be abaabuzire, akashanga ondiijo mwana w’empumi wenka.
