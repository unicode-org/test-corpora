EISHOMO 81. Pequod Nabugana Omuhara Ow’omuhara.

Ekiro ekyatebeekanisiibwe kare kikahika, kandi nk’oku kihikire tukabugana emeeri Jungfrau, Derick De Deer, mukama wayo, kuruga Bremen.

Omu bwire bumwe abantu abakuru omu nsi yoona abaabaire nibahiiga enyanja, Abadaaki n’Abagirimaani hati bari omu bakye; kwonka aha n’aha omu myanya mihango munonga ey’oburaingwa n’oburaingwa, obumwe n’obumwe noobugana ebendera yaabo omu nyanja ya Pacific.

Ahabw’enshonga zitari zimwe na zimwe, aba Jungfrau bakaba nibareebeka nk’abaine ekihika ky’okumuha ekitiinisa. Obu yaabaire akiri hare n’obwato bwa Pequod, akeetoorora, kandi yaateera obwato, kapiteeni we akagyemeserezibwa kuza ahu turi, ataine kugumisiriza ayemereire omu buta omu mwanya gw’enyima y’obwato.

“Aine ki omu ngaro ze okwo?” Starbuck akarira, arikworeka ekintu ekikwatsirwe Omugirimaani arikukihuuta. “Tikirikubaasika!—omuriisa w’etabaaza!”

"Tikyo," Stubb akagira, "ngaaha, ngaaha, n'ekiyungu ky'omukaru, Mr. Starbuck; naija kutukorera omukaru gwaitu, ni Yarman; torikureeba ekyo kikopo kihango ky'etini aho aharubaju rwe?—ago n'amaizi ge garikwotsya. Oh! ari gye, ni Yarman."

"Gyenda naiwe," Flask akarira, "n'ekirikugaburira etabaaza n'ekikopo ky'amajuta. Amajuta gahwaho, kandi aizire-kushaba."

N’obu kiraabe nikireebeka nk’eky’okutangaaza emeeri y’amajuta kuba neeguza amajuta aha itaka ry’enyanja, kandi n’obu kiraabe nikibaasa kuhakanisa enfumu eya ira erikugamba aha kutwara amakara kuza Newcastle omu muringo oguhindukire, kwonka obumwe ekintu nk’ekyo nikibaho buzima; kandi omu nshonga eriho hati Captain Derick De Deer akakora hatariho kubangaanisa okuriisa etabaaza nk’oku Flask yaarangirire.

Ku yaabaire naaza aha bwato, Ahabu akamuteera ahonaaho, atafayo na kakye aha ki yaabaire aine omu ngaro; kwonka omu rurimi rwe orucwekire, Omugirimaani omu bwire bukye akooreka obutamanya bwe bwona ahari White Whale; ahonaaho akahindura ekiganiiro kye aha kikopo kye ky’okuriisa etabaaza n’amajuta, n’ebigambo ebimwe ebirikukwata aha kuhinduka kwe omu kishengye kye ky’okuraaramu nyekiro omu mwirima mwingi—eitondo rye ry’aha muheru ry’amajuta ga Bremen rikaba rihweireho, kandi hatariho n’ekyenyanja kyona ekirikuguruka ekyakwatsirwe kuha ekibaire kitarimu; okuhendera arikuha obuhabuzi ngu buzima emeeri ye ekaba eri eki omu by’obuvubi kirikwetwa omu by’obuhangwa ekishemeire (nikwo kugira ngu, ekitarimu kintu kyona), ekihikire gye eiziina rya Jungfrau nari Bikira Maria.

Ebyetengo bye byatunga, Derick yaarugaho; kwonka akaba atatungire orubaju rw’emeeri ye, obu enyanja z’omu nyanja zabaire niziimuka haihi omurundi gumwe kuruga aha mitwe y’emiti y’emeeri zombi; kandi Derick akaba aine ekihika ky’okubinga, n’ahabw’ekyo atarikuhuumura kuta ekikopo kye ky’amajuta n’ekikwato ky’okuriisa etabaaza aha bwato, yaayehinguririza obwato bwe kandi yaakuratira eby’okuriisa etabaaza bya leviathan.

Hati, omuzaano ku gwaherize kutunguuka gwahika aha rubaju rw’omuyaga, we n’amaato agandi ashatu ag’Abagirimaani agaabaire gamukuratiire juba, bakaba baine entandikwa mpango y’ebigyere bya Pequod. Hakaba hariho enyanja munaana, pod eri ahagati. Ahabw’okumanya akabi kaabo, boona bakaba nibaza omu rubaju n’obwangu bwingi barikugorora omu maisho g’omuyaga, barikusiiga aha rubaju rwabo nk’oku embaraasi nyingi eziri omu nkomo zirikusiiga. Bakasigaho ekizibu kihango kandi kihango, nk’oku barikuguma nibasheeshuura oruhu rwingi oruhango aha nyanja.

Oijwire omu kuhuumura oku okwa juba, kandi n’oburaingwa bwingi enyima, akaba naayoga ente mpango y’omugurusi erimu ebihimba, eyaabaire neeshusha nk’eine oburwaire bwa jaundice, nari oburwaire obundi ahabw’okutunguuka kwayo mpora mpora, hamwe n’ebizimba ebitari bya buriijo ebyabaire nibimukura. Okuba ekinyonyi eki eky’enyanja kikaba kiri eky’omu pod omu bwire, kikaba nikireebeka nk’ekirikubuuzabuuza; ahakuba ti mugyenzo ngu ebinyonyi nk’ebyo ebirikuheebwa ekitiinisa bibe eby’abantu na kakye. Nangwa n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, akaguma aha nshozi zaabo, n’obu buzima amaizi gaabo g’enyima garaabe gaabaire nigabaasa kumugarura enyima, ahabw’okuba eigufa ryeru nari ekizimba aha munwa gwe omuhango kikaba kiri ekizimba ekicwekire, nk’ekizimba ekirikubaho emigyera ebiri ey’obuzigu ku erikuteerana. Omucuucu gwe gukaba guri mugufu, nigukora mpora kandi nigukora munonga; okurugayo n’omuringo gw’okusheeshasheesha okurikunyiganyiga, kandi kwemara omu bicweka ebitaagukire, kukuratirwa okuyomba okutarikumanywa okw’ahansi y’itaka omuriwe, okwabaire nikureebeka nk’okuine okuruga aha rubaju rwe orundi oruziikirwe, ekirikureetera amaizi agaabaire gari enyima ye kusheesheka.

“N’oha oine ekigambo ky’okugaaniira?” Stubb akagira ati, "aine obusaasi omunda, nintiina. Mukama, teekateeka aha kugira ekicweka kya yiika y'okushaasha omunda! Emiyaga mibi ekwatsire Noiri y'omushema omuriwe, aboojo. Nigwo muyaga gw'okubanza ogubi ogu naabaire nimmanya ngu niguhunga kuruga omu burengyerwa-izooba; kwonka reeba, hakaba hariho ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa whale yaw kiine kuba kiri ekyawe enyima."

Nk’omuhindi orikuremeera munonga arikwekorera aha nyanja ya Hindostan aine omugugu gw’embaraasi ezitiinire, careens, buries, okuzingazinga, n’okuziika omu muhanda gwe; nikwo n’enyanja egi eya ira yaabaire neeimutsya ekicweka kyayo ekikuzire, kandi hati n’obunaku obu ekicweka kyayo kikahinduka aha nshonda z’embaju zaayo ezigumire, kikashohoza ekyareeteire okusisimuka kwe okw’obugobya omu kifunjo ekitari ky’obuhangwa eky’ekibunu kye eky’aha rubaju rwa buryo. Yaaba akafeerwa ekibunu ekyo omu rutaro, nari akazaarwa ataine, kikaba kigumire kukigamba.

“Rindaho kakye kwonka, chap mukuru, kandi ndyakuha omuhoro gw’omukono ogwo oguhutaire,” Flask omubi akarira, arikworeka omurongooti gw’enyanja ogwabaire guri haihi nawe.

“Omutima ngu tarikuteera n’ekyoma,” Starbuck akarira. “Muhe omuhanda, narishi Omugirimaani naija kumugira.”

Omu kigyendererwa kimwe amaato goona agateeraine agabaire nigahakanisa gakaba nigashobororwaho ekyenyanja eki kimwe, ahabw’okuba tiwe wenka owaabaire nakirayo obuhango, n’ahabw’ekyo akaba ari enyanja y’omuhendo munonga, kureka akaba ari haihi nabo, kandi enyanja ezindi zikaba nizigyenda n’obwangu bwingi munonga, ekindi, nk’oku zikaba ziri haihi kuhakanisa okukuratira ahabw’obwire obwo. Omu bwire obu ebigyere bya Pequod bikaba biteirwe amasasi n’amaato ashatu g’Abagirimaani agaahererukire kushuuma; kwonka kuruga omu ntandikwa mpango ei yaabaire aine, obwato bwa Derick bukaba bukyayebembera okubinga, n’obu buri kaanya kaabaire nikahika haihi n’abazigu be ab’aheeru y’ihanga. Ekintu kimwe eki baabaire nibatiina, n’okugira ngu ahabw’okugira ngu akaba yaaheza kuba ari haihi munonga n’akamanyiso ke, akaba naija kubaasa kuteera ekyoma kye batakamuhikireho kimwe bakamuhinguraho. Kandi Derick, akaba nareebeka nk’oine obwesigye bwingi ngu eki nikiija kubaho, kandi obumwe n’obumwe n’akamanyiso k’okushekyerera akaba azunguza ekikwato kye ky’okuriisa etabaaza aha bwato obundi.

“Embwa etaine mbabazi kandi etarikusiima!” Starbuck akarira; “nashekyerera kandi ampa obumanzi n’akabokisi k’obworo munonga aku naamuijwire ti dakiika itaano ezihweire!”—bwanyima omu kuhwehura kwe okwa ira okw’amaani—“Muhe omuhanda, embwa z’omukishaka!

“Nimbagambira eki kiri, abashaija”—Stubb akarira abavugi be—“nikihakanisa ediini yangye okugwa omu iraru; kwonka nkayenzire kurya Yarman ogwo omubi—Kukurura—timurikukora? Nimuza kureka ogwo mubi kubateera? Nimukunda brandy? Omutwe gw’empunu gwa brandy, mbwenu, ota, Ija omushaija omwe ow’okuba. omutsi gw'eshagama?N'oha ogwo obaire nateera enkongi aha nyanja—titurikuhunga inci n'emwe—tuhuumuriize.

"Oh! reeba ebicupuri ebi arikukora!" Flask akarira, arikuzina ahaiguru n’ahansi—“Ka n’ekibunu—Oh, kora ekirundo aha nyama y’ente—ekigwejegyeire nk’omuti! Oh! abashaija bangye, kora ekicupuri—ebikopo by’okuteera n’ebikopo by’okurya bya kyamushana, nimumanya, abashaija bangye—ebihimba ebikozirwe omu nkoko n’ebihimba by’omukishaka—oh, kora , kora , ekicupuri,—tihariho igana ry’okufeerwa! —reeba ngu Yarman—Oh, timurikwija kukurura ahabwa duff yaanyu, abashaija bangye—sog nk’egyo timurikukunda sperm nk’egyo?—banka yoona eya Bungyereza!—Oh, do , do , do !

Omu kaanya aka Derick akaba ari omu kikorwa ky’okuteera ekikopo kye ky’okuriisa etabaaza aha bwato oburikweyongyera, kandi n’ekikopo kye ky’amajuta; obundi n’emiteekateekyere ebiri ey’okugarura enyima omuhanda gw’abazigu be, kandi omu bwire nibwo bumwe akatunguura eby’entaasya bye ahabw’okugyemeserezibwa kw’akaanya kakye okw’okuteera enyima.

“Embwa y’omudaaki etaine mitwarize!” Stubb akarira. "Mukurure hati, abashaija, nk'enkumi ataano z'emigugu y'omurongooti gw'emeeri z'orutaro z'emizimu y'eishokye erikutukura. Nimugira ki, Tashtego; nimwe omushaija w'okushara omugongo gwawe omu bicweka makumi abiri n'ebibiri ahabw'ekitiinisa kya Gayhead omugurusi? Nimugira ki?"

“Ningira ngu, kwata nka god-dam,”—Omuhindi akarira.

Omu muringo gw’obushaarizi, kwonka omu muringo ogurikushushana ahabw’okushekyererwa kw’Omugirimaani, amaato ashatu ga Pequod hati gakatandika kugyendera haihi n’omwe; kandi, ahabw’okugira omutima nk’ogwo, akamwehinguririza kumara akaire kakye. Omu mitwarize egyo mirungi, etaine nshonga, ey’obumanzi ey’omukuru w’enyanja ku yaabaire ari haihi n’omuhiigo gwe, abashaija abo bashatu bakaba beemerera n’okwehimbisa, obumwe n’obumwe bakaba bashagika omuvugi orikukurataho n’okurira okurikushemeza ngu, “Aho niho arikusheesheka, hati!

Kwonka nikwo kucwamu entandikwa y’okubanza kuri Derick yaabaire aine, ngu n’obu baraabe baabaire baine obumanzi bwona, akaba naija kuhamya omusinguzi omu mpaka ezi, kuri hatariho okucwamu okuhikire okwamushuumireho omu nkoko eyakwatsire orubaju rw’omuvugi we w’ahagati y’emeeri. Ogu lubber omukyeenyo ku yaabaire naateeraho kwihaho eishokye rye erikwera, kandi obwo, ahabw’ekyo, obwato bwa Derick bwabaire buri haihi kuhinduka, kandi akaba naateera enkuba arikuteera abantu be omu kiniga kingi;—obwo bukaba buri obwire burungi ahari Starbuck, Stubb, na Flask. Omu kugamba n'eiraka rihango, bakatandika omumaisho g'okufa, kandi bateera oruhi aha rubaju rw'omugirimaani. An instant more, kandi amaato goona ana gakaba gari diagonically omu bwire bw’okuhuumura kw’enyanja y’enyanja, obwo arikuguruka kuruga aharigo, aha mbaju zombi, n’okuzimba kw’ebicupuri oku yaakozire.

Kikaba kiri ekintu ky’akabi munonga, eky’okusaasirwa munonga, kandi ekirikuteganisa. Ekinyonyi ekikuru hati kikaba nikiza omutwe aheeru, kandi kirikwohereza omucuucu gwakyo omu maisho ge omu nyonyi erikuguma neebonabonesibwa; obwo ekibunu kye kimwe eky’omworo kirikuteera orubaju rwe omu busaasi bw’okutiina. Hati aha mukono ogu, hati aha ogwo, akaba agurukaguruka omu kuguruka kwe okurikuteganisibwa, kandi nabwo ahari buri nshozi ei yaabaire ahenda, akaba agwejegyera omu nyanja, nari aha rubaju arikugurukaguruka arikuza omu iguru ekibunu kye kimwe ekyabaire nikiteera. N’ahabw’ekyo nkareeba ekinyonyi ekiine amapapa agashazirwe kirikukora enzindo ezicwekire ezitiinire omu mwanya, kirikuteeraho busha kuhunga enkongoro z’abanyabuzaare. Kwonka ekinyonyi kiine eiraka, kandi n’okurira okurikuteganisa nikiija kumanyisa okutiina kwakyo; kwonka okutiina kw’ekinyamaishwa eki kihango ekitarikugamba eky’omu nyanja, kukaba kubohirwe kandi kwamuteganisa; akaba ataine iraka, kureka okuhuuta okwo okurikunyiganyiga kurabira omu nshonda zoona ze, kandi eki kikaba nikireetera okureeba kwe kuba okw’obusaasi okutarikugambwa; obwo akiriho, omu buhango bwe oburikutangaaza, portcullis jaw, n’omukira gwe ogw’obushoboorozi bwona, hakaba hariho ekirikumara kutiinisa omushaija orikukirayo obuhango owaabaire aine embabazi nyingi.

Okureeba hati ngu kwonka obwire bukye munonga oburikukiraho bukaba nibwija kuha amaato ga Pequod oburungi, kandi omu mwanya gw’okuremwa omu muzaano gwe, Derick akacwamu kuta omu kabi eki ahariwe kiine kuba kyabaire nikireebeka nk’omusyo muraingwa munonga ogutari gwa buriijo, omugisha gw’aha muheru gutakatoroka ebiro byona.

Kwonka omuvugi we w’enkoni ku yaaherize kwemerera ahabw’okuteera, enkongoro zoona ishatu—Queequeg, Tashtego, Daggoo—omu buhangwa zikaguruka ziimuka, kandi zeemereire omu murongooti gw’orubaju, omu bwire nibwo bumwe zaayoreka amabaare gaazo; kandi bakaguruka aha mutwe gw’omuvugi w’emotoka y’omwika Omugirimaani, ebyoma byabo bishatu ebya Nantucket bikataaha omu nshozi y’enyanja. Emicu y’okuhumiza amaisho ey’ekicupuri n’omuriro ogurikwera! Amaato ashatu, omu kiniga ky’okubanza eky’okuhunga kw’omutwe gw’enyanja, gakateera ag’Omugirimaani aha rubaju n’amaani maingi, kuhitsya obu Derick n’omuvugi we w’enkoni owaabaire ashoberwa bombi baasheesheka, kandi baagyendera aha rubaju rw’ebinyonyi bishatu ebyabaire nibiguruka.

“Mutatiina, ebibokisi byangye by’amajuta,” Stubb akagamba n’eiraka rihango, arikubireebaho n’amaisho g’okuhinguraho obwo arikuteera amasasi; “muryatwarwa hati—kihikire—nkareeba enyanja ezimwe z’omu nyanja aha rubaju rw’enyima—embwa za St. Bernard, nimumanya—nizihuumuza abagyenyi abaine obusaasi. Hurrah! ogu nigwo muringo gw’okugyenderamu hati. Buri keel omushana gw’eizooba! Hurrah!—Hanu nituza nk’ebikopo bishatu by’amabaare a magard in me aha mukira! enjojo omu tilbury aha ruhanga—neereetera ebipimo by’enziga kuguruka, aboojo, ku orikumukwataho omu muringo ogwo, ku orikuteera orushozi Hurrah ogu nigwo muringo ogu omugyenzi we arikwehuriramu ku arikuza Davy Jones omu rush e! obutumwa obutahwaho!”

Kwonka okuhunga kw’ekicuncu kukaba kuri okw’obwire bukye. Okuha omuyaga gw’ahonaaho, akateera akaari. N’okwiruka okurikuteera, emirongooti eshatu ekaba eguruka eyehinguririize emitwe y’emiti n’amaani g’okugiteeramu emiina y’omunda munonga; obwo abateezi b’enkoni baabaire nibatiina munonga ngu okuhurira oku okwa juba kukaba nikwija kumaraho emirongooti juba, ngu barikukoresa amaani gaabo goona ag’obukugu, bakakwata emirundi mingi barikuteera etaabe n’omugoye gw’okukwatiraho; okuhitsya aha muheru—ahabw’okuremeera okuhikire kuruga omu nshonda z’amaato ezirimu emirongooti y’ekyoma, ahu emirunga eshatu yaabaire neeruga ekahika ahansi omu bururu—embundu z’obuta zikaba ziri haihi kuhikaana n’amaizi, obwo emirunga eshatu y’enyima erikwehindura ahaiguru omu mwanya. Kandi ekinyonyi ekikuru kikarekyera aho kuhurirwa, kumara obwire bakaguma omu mitwarize egyo, barikutiina kukoresa omurongooti ogundi, n’obu omwanya gwabaire gurimu okuteganisibwa kakye. Kwonka n’obu amaato garaabe gateirwe ahansi gakabura omu muringo ogu, kwonka n’oku “okukwatiraho,” nk’oku kurikwetwa; oku kukwatwaho amabaare g’omubiri gwe oguhuriire kuruga enyima; eki nikyo kirikukira kubonabonesa Leviyatani ngu juba aimukye kubugana eicumu ry’abazigu be eririkwotsya. Kwonka tutarikugamba aha kabi k’ekintu, n’okubuuzabuuza yaaba omuhanda ogu nigwo gurikukirayo oburungi obwire bwona; ahakuba nikiba kiri eky’obwengye kuteekateeka, ngu oku ekinyonyi ekiteirwe kirikuguma ahansi y’amaizi, nikwo kirikweyongyera kuruha. Ahabw’okuba, ahabw’okugira ngu aine obuhango bwingi munonga—omu nshozi y’ekinyonyi ekikuzire munonga ekintu ekiri ahansi ya square feet 2000—okugyemeserezibwa kw’amaizi ni kwingi munonga. Twena nitumanya oburemeezi bw’omu mwanya oburikutangaaza obu itwe ahabwaitu twemereire ahansi; nangwa n’aha, ahaiguru y’ensi, omu mwanya; n’ahabw’ekyo, omugugu gw’enyanja y’enyanja, ogurikwekorera aha mugongo gwayo enyomyo y’enyanja erikwingana fuuti magana abiri n’omuhango munonga! Haakiri kiine kwingana n’oburemeezi bw’empeho makumi ataano. Omuvugi omwe w’enyanja akateekateeka ngu neeremeera nk’emeeri makumi abiri z’omurongooti gw’orutaro, n’embundu zaazo zoona, n’amaduuka, n’abantu abari omu meeri.

Obu amaato ashatu gaabaire gagwejegyeire aho aha nyanja egyo erikwehindura mpora, garikureeba ahansi omu mwabazyo gwayo ogwa bururu ogutahwaho; kandi nk’oku hatariho kuborooga nari okurira kw’omuringo gwona, ngaaha, n’omuyaga nari ekicupuri ekyarugire omunda yaakyo; eki omunyaitaka yaabaire naija kuteekateeka, ngu ahansi y’okuhunama okwo kwona n’obusingye, ekicuncu ekikuru munonga eky’omu nyanja kikaba nikinyiganyiga kandi nikicumita omu busaasi! Tihariho inci munaana z’omugoye oguhikire ogwabaire nigureebeka aha buta. Nikireebeka nikihamibwa ngu ahabw’enyuguta ishatu nk’ezo ezikye munonga Leviyatani omukuru akahanikwa nk’oburemeezi bwingi aha shaaha y’ebiro munaana. Okubinga? kandi ahari ki? Kuhika aha bipande bishatu by’orubaaho. Eki nikyo kihangirwe eki kyagambirweho ira omu businguzi—“Noobaasa kwijuza oruhu rwe ebyoma by’amabaare? Nainga omutwe gwe amacumu g’eby’enyanja? Nikwe! ngu ebitarikuhikiirira bikuratire baanabi. Omu mushana ogwo ogw’omu mwabazyo ogwabaire nigushendashenda, ebicuucu ebi amaato ashatu gaabaire nigasindika ahansi y’enyanja, biine kuba byabaire biri biraingwa kandi bihango munonga kubaasa kukingira ekicweka ky’eihe rya Xerxes. N’oha orikubaasa kumanya oku ebishushani bihango nk’ebyo ebyabaire nibiguruka aha mutwe gwe byabaire nibitiinisa munonga!
