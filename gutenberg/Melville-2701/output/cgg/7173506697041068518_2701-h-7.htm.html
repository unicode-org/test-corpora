Ahabw’okuba obukuru bw’omuntu omu by’obwengye eki burikwenda, tiburikubaasa kuteekateeka aha bukuru oburikukorwa, oburiho aha bashaija abandi, hatariho buhwezi bw’omuringo gw’eby’obuhangwa eby’aheeru n’ebirikuhamya, buriijo, omuribyo, ebirikukiraho nari bikye ebitari bikuru kandi eby’ahansi. Eki nikyo, ekirikurinda ebiro byona abakuru ba Ruhanga ab’amazima ab’Obukama obutakwatwaho ensi; kandi asigaho ebitiinisa by’ahaiguru ebi empeho egi erikubaasa kuha, ahari abo bashaija abarikumanywa munonga kurabira omu bukye bwabo obutaine bugarukiro aha kutoorana omukono ogusherekirwe ogwa Divine Inert, okukira kurabira omu bukuru bwabo obutaine kubangaanisa aha rurengo orufiire orw’abantu baingi. Emitwarize mirungi nk’egyo neeyeshereka omu bintu ebi ebikye obu enyikiriza z’eby’obutegyeki ezirengyesereize zirikubitaho, ngu omu mbeera ezimwe z’obugabe nangwa n’omu bushema bw’obushema zihaire amaani. Kwonka ku, nk’oku kyabaire ahari Nicholas the Czar, engure y’obugabe bw’ensi erimu empeta erikwehinguririza obwongo bw’obugabe; obwo, amasyo g’abantu buntu nigafukama ahansi omu maisho g’okutaho obushoboorozi bw’amaani. Kandi, omuhandiiki w’emizaano y’obusaasi owaabaire naija kworeka obutabaasa kusingurwa kw’okufa omu kusheenya kwayo okuhikire n’okuhinduka okuhikire, tarikwija kwebwa ekigambo, ekikuru munonga omu by’obuhangwa bwe, nk’ekirikugambwaho hati.

Kwonka Ahabu, Kapiteeni wangye, naakigyendera omu maisho gangye omu kushoberwa kwe kwona okwa Nantucket n’okushekyerera; kandi omu kicweka eki ekirikukwata aha Bagabe n’Abagabe, tindikushereka ngu nyine eki ndikukora n’omuhiigi w’enyanja omugurusi omworo nk’ogwo wenka; kandi, n’ahabw’ekyo, emitego yoona ey’ekitiinisa ey’aheeru n’amaju nibinyimwa. Ai, Ahabu! ekiraabe kikuru omuriiwe, kiine kwetengyesa kusharaho kuruga omu iguru, kandi kikazibirwa omu maizi maingi, kandi kikareebeka omu mwanya ogutaine mubiri!

Omu bwire bw’obwire oburikukirayo kushemeza, nibwo omu kuhinduka okuhikire n’abavugi b’enyanja abandi omutwe gwangye gw’okubanza ogw’omuti gwaija gwayetoorora.
