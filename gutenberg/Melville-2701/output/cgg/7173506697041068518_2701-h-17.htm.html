EISHOMO RYA 86. Omukira.

Abahandiiki b’ebitebyo abandi bakahindura okuhimbisa kw’eriisho ry’enkongoro eririkworoba, n’amapapa g’ekinyonyi agarikushemeza agatarikugwa; less celestial, ninkuza omukira.

Okubara omukira gwa Sperm Whale ogurikukirayo obuhango ngu nigutandika aha nshonga egyo ey’omubiri ahu gurikukyendeera kuhika aha rubaju rw’omushaija, nigukora aha mutwe gwagwo ogw’ahaiguru gwonka, omwanya ogurikwingana square feet makumi ataano. Omubiri gw’omuzi gwayo ogurikwetoorora ogugumire nigutunguuka guhinduka ebihimba bibiri bihango, ebigumire, ebiine ebihimba nari ebihimba, mporampora nibisheesheka kuhika aha buhango obutarikuhika inci emwe. Aha rubaju rw’omubiri nari aha rubaju orurikuteerana, ebinyonyi ebi nibiteerana kakye, reero bigaruka enyima aha rubaju nk’amapapa, bikasigaho omwanya muhango ahagati. Tihariho kintu kyona ekiine amagara ahu emiringo y’oburungi erikushoboororwa gye kukira omu nshonda z’okwezi z’ebicupuri ebi. Omu kutunguuka kwayo okurikukirayo omu nshozi y’enyanja ehikire, omukira nigwija kurenga munonga fuuti makumi abiri omu buraingwa.

Ekicweka kyona nikireebeka nk’ekitanda ekirimu orukoba orurimu emitsi egumire; kwonka waagitemamu, nooshanga ngu emiringo eshatu etari emwe n’emwe neegikora:—ey’ahaiguru, eya rwagati, n’ey’ahansi. Ebicweka by’omubiri ebiri aha mutwe n’eby’ahansi, nibireingwa kandi nibishushana; ebyo eby’ahagati, bigufu munonga, kandi nibigyendera omu musharaba ahagati y’ebicweka by’aheeru. Omubumbi ogu ogw’obushatu, nk’ekintu kyona, niguha omukira amaani. Aha mwegi w’ebisiika bya Rooma ebya ira, orubaju rwa rwagati nirwija kuha ekishushani ky’okutangaaza ekirikushushana n’omuhanda gw’ebipande ebikye ebirikuguma nibihindukahinduka n’eibare omu bisigire ebyo eby’okutangaaza eby’eira, kandi hatariho kubangaanisa ebirikuhwera munonga omu maani maingi g’okuteera amabaare.

Kwonka nk’oku amaani aga maingi ag’omu kicweka omu mukira gw’ebihaha gatarikumara, ekicweka kihango kyona eky’enyamaishwa erikwetwa leviathan nikirukwaho n’ekirungo ky’ebihimba n’ebihimba, ebirikuraba aha rubaju rwona orw’ebibero kandi bikairukira ahansi omu bihimba, bikajwanzya nabyo omu muringo ogutarikwetegyerezibwa, kandi bikahwera munonga omu maani gaabyo; n’ahabw’ekyo omu mukira amaani agatarikupimwa agarikuteerana ag’enyanja yoona nigareebeka nk’agateeraine kuhika aha nshonga. Okucwekyerera kukaba nikubaasa kubaho aha nshonga, eki nikyo kyabaire kiri ekintu ky’okukikora.

Kandi eki—amaani gaayo ag’okutangaaza, na kakye tikirikukunda kuremesa okugurukaguruka kw’embabazi kw’okutambura kwayo; ahu obuto bw’okwanguha burikugyendera omu Titanism y’obushoboorozi. Okutaana n’ekyo, ebishushani ebyo nibitunga oburungi bwabyo oburikukirayo kutiinisa kuruga aharibyo. Amaani gaabuzima tigarikushiisha oburungi nari okukwatanisa, kureka nigakira kubuha; kandi omuri buri kimwe ekirungi munonga, amaani gaine bingi ebi garikukora n’obufumu. Ihaho emitsi ebohirwe erikureebeka hoona nk’erikuturika kuruga omu mabaare g’omuhendo omu Hercules ebazirwe, kandi okushemeza kwayo kukaba nikwija kuhwaho. Eckerman omunyadiini ku yaabaire naimutsya orubaaho rwa kitaani kuruga aha mubiri gwa Goethe ogwabaire guri busha, akakwatwaho ekifuba kihango ky’omushaija ogwo, ekyabaire nikireebeka nk’ekibunu ky’obusinguzi bw’Abarooma. Angelo ku arikusiiga nangwa na Ruhanga Isheboona omu muringo gw’omuntu, taho akamanyiso k’obuhame oburiho. Kandi kyona eki barikubaasa kushoboorora ahari rukundo ya Ruhanga eri omu Mwana, ebishushani by’Abaitali ebigumire, ebizingire, eby’abashaija n’abakazi, ebi ekiteekateeko kye kirikukirayo kusingura; ebishushani ebi, nk’oku biine obutagira obwongo bwona, tibirikumanyisa kintu kyona eky’amaani goona, kureka ekibi kyonka, eky’obukazi eky’okwehayo n’okugumisiriza, eki aha mikono yoona kirikwikirizibwa, nikikora emitwarize mirungi ey’omutaano ey’omugasho ey’enyegyesa ze.

Okuhinduka kw’ekicweka eki ndikujanjaba nikwo kuri, ngu yaaba nikikoresibwa omu mizaano, nari omu bwesigwa, nari omu kiniga, n’obu kiraabe kiri omu mbeera ki, okuguruka kwakyo nikumanyirwa aha mbabazi ezirengire. Omuri ekyo tihariho mukono gw’omufumu ogurikubaasa kukirenga.

Emivuyo etaano empango n’ey’omutaano ahariyo. Eky’okubanza, kyakoresibwa nk’ekibunu ky’okutunguuka; Ekyakabiri, ku kirikukoresibwa nk’omuhoro omu rutaro; Ekyakashatu, omu kusheenya; Ekyakana, omu kuteera akaari; Ekyakatano, omu kutunguuka kw’ebicupuri.

Eky’okubanza: Ahabw’okugira ngu omukira gwa Leviathan niguteerana omu mwanya gwagwo, nigukora omu muringo ogurikutaana n’emikira y’ebihangirwe ebindi byona eby’omu nyanja. Tikirikuhindukahinduka. Omu muntu nari omu kyanyanja, okugurukaguruka n’akamanyiso k’okugira ngu n’ow’ahansi. Aha kinyonyi ekikuru, omukira gwakyo nigwo gwonka ogurikugikoresa. Scroll-wise coiled forwards ahansi y’omubiri, reero bwanyima eguruka juba kugaruka enyima, nikyo ekirikuha ekyo kimwe ekirikuguruka, okuguruka ekicuncu kyaba nikiyoga n’ekiniga kingi. Ebipapaijo bye eby’aha rubaju nibihwera omu kuhabura kwonka.

Ekyakabiri: Nikimanyisa kakye, ngu n’obu enyanja emwe erikurwanisa endiijo n’omutwe n’orubaju rwonka, n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, omu ntaro zaayo n’omuntu, neekoresa omukira gwayo okukira munonga kandi omu muringo gw’okugaya. Omu kuteera obwato, ahonaaho ahindura ebicupuri bye kuruga aharibwo, kandi okuteera nikureetwa okugaruka enyima kwonka. Ku kiraabe kikozirwe omu mwanya ogutarimu kizibu, namunonga kyashuuma kuhika aha kamanyiso kaakyo, obwo okuteera nikuba kutarikubaasa kuzibirwa. Tihariho mbaju z’omuntu nari obwato ezirikubaasa kukyemera. Okujunwa kwawe kwonka kuri omu kukyetantara; kwonka ku kiraabe nikiija aha rubaju kurabira omu maizi agarikuhakanisa, obwo ekicweka ahabw’okuguruka kw’obwato bw’enyanja, n’okugumira kw’ebintu byabwo, orubaju orucwekire nari orubaaho oruriho obucweka bubiri nari bibiri, omuringo gw’okutunga omu rubaju, okutwarira hamwe nikyo kirikurugamu ekirikukirayo obubi. Ebikwato ebi eby’aha rubaju ebiri omu maizi nibikira kutunga omu by’obuvubi, n’ahabw’ekyo nibitwarwa nk’okuzaana kw’omwana kwonka. Omuntu aihaho ekijwaro ky’okujwara, ekiina kikyemerezibwa.

Ekyakashatu: Tinkubaasa kukyoreka, kwonka nikireebeka ahariinye, ngu omu nshozi y’enyanja obumanyiso bw’okukoraho nibuta omutima aha mukira; ahakuba omu nshonga egi harimu oburungi oburikwingana n’oburungi bw’omubiri gw’enjojo bwonka. Oburungi obu nibureebeka munonga omu kikorwa ky’okusheenya, obu omu bucureezi bw’obuhara ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa whale n’obucureezi obumwe oburikworoba kirikugyendesa ebicupuri byakyo ebihango kuruga aha rubaju rumwe kuza aha rundi aha mutwe gw’enyanja; kandi ku araabe naahurira kwonka ebireju by'omuvugi w'enyanja, akabi ahari ogwo muvugi w'enyanja, ebireju na byona. Ka hariho obucureezi omu kukwataho okwo okw’okubanza! Omukira ogu ku guraabe gwabaire gurimu amaani g’okukwaata, nkaba nyine kuteekateeka aha njojo ya Darmonodes eyaabaire neekira kuza omu katare k’ebimuri, kandi n’okuramutsya okukye ekaba neeha abahara ebijwaro by’okukwaata ebimuri, reero ekaba neekwata aha nshonda zaabo. Aha nshonga nyingi ezirikukira ahari emwe, eky’obusaasi n’okugira ngu ekinyonyi eki tikiine mutwarize ogu ogw’okubanza omu mukira gwakyo; ahakuba mpurire aha njojo endiijo, ngu ku yaabaire ahutaziibwe omu rutaro, ekaba eyehinguririza omubiri gwayo, ekaihaho omusyo.

Ekyakana: Okwiba atamanyire aha nyanja y’enyanja omu bukuumi oburikuteekateekwaho ahagati y’enyanja eziri zonka, noomushanga ataine nshonga ahabw’okugira ngu aine ekitiinisa kingi, kandi nk’akaana k’embwa, naazaana aha nyanja nk’oku eri omu muriro. Kwonka nabwo nooreeba amaani ge omu muzaano gwe. Ebiganja bye ebihango eby’omukira gwe nibiteera ahaiguru omu mwanya; reero kuteera aha mutwe, okuteera kw’enkuba nikuhurirwa kumara mairo nyingi. Noobaasa kuteekateeka ngu embundu mpango ekaba eshohoziibwe; kandi ku waakureeba ekirungo ky’omuriro ekikye kuruga omu spiracle aha rubaju rwe orundi, okateekateeka ngu ogwo n’omwika ogurikuruga omu touch-hole.

Ekyakatano: Nk’oku kiri omu nkora ya buriijo ey’okuzengyerera eya leviathan, ebicupuri nibigwejegyera munonga ahansi y’omutindo gw’omugongo gwe, obwo nibiba bitarikureebwa kimwe ahansi y’eitaka; kwonka ku arikuba ari haihi kuza omu maizi maingi, ebicupuri bye byona ebiine haakiri fuuti makumi ashatu z’omubiri gwe nibigurukaguruka bigumire omu mwanya, kandi biguma nibitengeeta akaanya kakye, kuhitsya obu birikuteera ahansi bitarikureebwa. Oyihireho okuhendagura okurikukirayo obuhango —ahandi ahu okurikubaasa kushoboororwa —okuhika aha mutwe oku kw’ebicupuri by’enyanja obundi n’ekintu kikuru munonga ekirikureebwa omu bihangirwe byona ebiine amagara. Omuri obuhango obutaine bugarukiro omukira muhango nigushusha nk’ogurikunyaga omu iguru eririkukirayo obuhango. Nahabwekyo omu birooto, nkareeba Sitaane ow’ekitiinisa arikuteera orukumu rwe oruhango orurikubonabonesibwa kuruga omu muriro gwa Baltic ogwa Gehena. Kwonka omu kureeba ebishushani nk’ebyo, byona omu byona n’omutima ogu orimu; ku oraabe ori omu Dantean, baadaimoni nibaija kukuhikaho; ku kiraabe kiri omu kya Isaaya, abakuru ba baamaraika. Nyemereire aha mutwe gw’emeeri yangye omu bwire bw’okuruga kw’eizooba okwabaire nikutukura eiguru n’enyanja, rimwe nkareeba eisyo rihango ry’enyanja omu burugwa-izooba, zoona niziza omu izooba, kandi kumara akaire kakye nizitengeeta omu kuteerana n’ebinyonyi ebiine enshonda z’ahaiguru. Nk’oku kyabaire nikireebeka ahariinye omu bwire obwo, okuramya baaruhanga omu muringo gw’amaani nk’ogwo gukaba gutakareebekaga, nangwa n’omuri Buperesi, ahu abaramya b’omuriro baabaire nibatuura. Nk’oku Ptolemy Philopater yaabaire naaha obujurizi aha njojo y’omu Afirika, nkagaruka naaha obujurizi aha nshozi y’enyanja, ndikugira ngu niyo erikukizayo okwehayo omu bihangirwe byona. Ahabw’okuba kurugiirira aha Mugabe Juba, enjojo z’amahe eza ira zikaba zikira kushemeza omu kasheeshe ziine ebihimba byazo ziteirwe ahaiguru omu kasirise akarikukirayo.

Okugyeragyeranisa kw’omugisha omu shuura egi, ahagati y’enyanja n’enjojo, nk’oku ebicweka ebimwe eby’omukira gw’ekimwe n’omubiri gw’ekindi biri, tikushemerire kukunda kuta ebicweka ebyo bibiri ebirikuhakanisa aha kwingana, n’okukiraho ebihangirwe ebi buri kimwe kirikurugamu. Ahabw’okuba nk’oku enjojo erikukirayo amaani eri nk’enjojo y’omukishaka ahari Leviyatani, nikwo n’okugigyeragyeranisa n’omukira gwa Leviyatani, omubiri gwe niguba guri nk’ekiti ky’ekimuri kyonka. Okuteera okurikukirayo kutiinisa kuruga omu mubiri gw’enjojo kukaba kuri nk’okuteera kw’omuyaga okurikuzaana, kugyeragyeranisa n’okuteera n’okuteera kw’enyanja y’omunyanja erikwetwa sperm whale, erikuguma neeteera amaato goona n’ebigyere byago byona n’abavugi baayo omu mwanya, juggler munonga nk’omupiira gwe ogw’omuhindi.

*N’obu okugyeragyeranisa kwona omu muringo gw’obuhango bw’abantu boona ahagati y’enyanja n’enjojo kuraabe kuri okw’obushema, ahabw’okuba omu nshonga egyo enjojo neeyemerera omu kitiinisa kimwe n’enyanja nk’oku embwa eri aha njojo; kwonka nabwo, tihariho okwetenga enshonga ezimwe ez’okushushana kw’okwenda kumanya; omu bintu ebi harimu n’ekibunu. Nikimanyirwa gye ngu enjojo neekira kunywa amaizi nari omucuucu omu nshaho yaayo, reero kugiimutsya, egisheeshe omu mugyera.

Oku ndikweyongyera kuteekateeka aha mukira ogu ogw’amaani, nikwo ndikweyongyera kwefuuza ahabw’okuremwa kugugamba. Obumwe harimu obubonero oburimu, obu, n’obu buraabe nibubaasa kushemeza gye omukono gw’omuntu, nibuguma butarikushoboororwa kimwe. Omu igana rihango, ebirikutangaaza munonga, obumwe n’obumwe, hariho obubonero obu obw’amazima, ngu mpurire abahiigi abarangirire ngu nibushushana n’obumanyiso n’obumanyiso bwa Free-Mason; ngu ekinyonyi ekikuru, buzima, omu miringo egi kikagaaniira n’ensi omu bwengye. Kandi tihariho n’okwenda ebindi bigyendererwa by’enyanja omu mubiri gwe gwona, ebiijwire obutamanya, kandi ebitarikugarukwamu aha muteezi we orikukirayo obumanyirizi. Mumushara oku ndikubaasa, obwo, nyowe kwonka ninza oruhu omunda; Tindikumumanya, kandi tindikwija kumumanya. Kwonka shi ku ndaabe ntarikumanya n'omukira gw'enyanja egi, nimbaasa kwetegyereza nta omutwe gwe? n'okukiraho, nitubaasa kwetegyereza tuta amaisho ge, obwo ataine maisho? Noija kureeba ebicweka byangye by’omugongo, omukira gwangye, nashusha ngu nagira ngu kwonka omu maisho gangye tihariho. Kwonka tindikubaasa kushoboorora gye ebicweka bye eby’enyima; kandi hint eki arikwenda aha maisho ge, ningaruka ningira ngu taine maisho.





EISHOMO 87. Ekicweka ky’amahe ekikuru.

Ekizinga kiraingwa kandi kikye ekya Malacca, ekirikuhika omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho bw’oburengyerwa-izooba kuruga omu bicweka bya Birmah, nikyo kirikukora ekicweka ekirikukirayo obukiizi-bwa-bumosho bwa Asia yoona. Omu murongooti ogutarikuhwaho kuruga aha kizinga ekyo, hariho ebizinga biraingwa ebya Sumatra, Java, Bally, na Timor; ebi, hamwe n’ebindi bingi, birikukora ekibunu kihango, nari orugo, orurikugaita Asia na Australia omu buraingwa, kandi rurikubaganisamu enyanja mpango eya Buhindi etarikuhendagurika kuruga aha bizinga by’omu burugwa-izooba ebiine amabaare maingi. Ekigo eki nikicumita emyanya mingi y’okushutamamu (sally-ports) ahabw’okuhwera emeeri n’enyanja; ebirikureebwa gye omuri ebyo n’enyanja ya Sunda na Malacca. Aha rubaju rw’enyanja ya Sunda, okukira munonga, emeeri ezirikuza China kuruga omu burengyerwa-izooba, niziruga omu nyanja za China.

Ezo nkungu ezikye eza Sunda nizibaganisamu Sumatra na Java; kandi eyemereire ahagati y’omuhanda omu rugo orwo oruhango orw’ebizinga, orurikuhamibwa oruhanga orwo oruhango orurikwiragura, orurikumanyirwa abavugi b’enyanja nka Java Head; tizirikuhikaana n’omuryango gwa rwagati ogurikwigura omu bugabe bumwe obuhango oburimu ebisiika: kandi waateekateeka aha bugabe obutarikuhwaho obw’ebirungo, n’obujwara, n’amabaare g’omuhendo, n’ezaabu, n’amahembe g’entare, ebi ebizinga rukumi eby’omu nyanja egyo ey’oburengyerwa-izooba birikugaigahazibwamu, nikireebeka ngu n’ekintu kikuru ekirikuheebwayo omu buhangwa, ngu ensi nk’egyo . kwemera okureebeka, n’obu kiraabe kitaine mugasho, eky’okurindwa ensi y’oburengyerwa-izooba erikukwata byona. Embaju z’enyanja ya Sunda tiziine bigo ebyo ebirikutegyeka ebirikurinda emiryango y’enyanja ya Mediterenia, Baltic, na Propontis. Obutashushana n’Abanya-Denmark, Abanya-Oburugwa-izooba aba tibarikushaba ekitiinisa ky’okutiinisa eky’okugaruza ahansi ebigyere by’ahaiguru kuruga omu ruguuto orutarikuhwaho orw’emeeri omu maisho g’omuyaga, ogwabaire niguhingura ahagati y’ebizinga bya Sumatra na Java kumara ebyasha bingi, nyekiro n’omu ihangwe, gurikutwara emigugu y’omuhendo munonga ey’omu burugwa-izooba. Kwonka n’obu baraabe nibarekura omukoro nk’ogu omu bwengye, tibarikwehakana na kakye eki barikugira ngu nibaija kutunga omushoro ogugumire munonga.

Time out of mind abashuma b’enyanja b’Abamalaya, abarikwehereera omu nkungu n’ebizinga bya Sumatra ebiine ebicuucu bikye, bakateera emeeri ezirikuraba omu nyanja, barikushaba munonga omushoro aha mutwe gw’amacumu gaabo. N’obu baraabe baagarukiremu kufubirwa n’eshagama omu mikono y’abavugi b’emotoka z’emigugu aba Buraaya, obumanzi bw’abavugi b’emotoka z’emigugu aba omu bwire obuhweire bukaba bukyeenirwe; kwonka, n’obunaku obu, obumwe n’obumwe nituhurira emeeri z’Abangyereza n’Abamerika, ezi, omu maizi ago, ziteirwe kandi zinyagwa bataine kwefuuza.

Ahabw’omuyaga murungi kandi ogurikwera, hati Pequod ekaba neehika haihi n’enyanja ezi; Ahabu aine ekigyendererwa ky’okurabamu akaza omu nyanja ya Java, kandi kurugayo, akagyendera omu nyanja y’omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho, aha maizi agamanyirwe ngu nigakira kuzamu aha n’aha n’enyanja ya Sperm Whale, akasheenya aha rubaju rw’enyanja ahabw’ebizinga bya Philippines, kandi akatunga orubaju rw’enyanja rwa Japan orwa hare, omu bwire bw’obwire bw’okuroba enyanja mpango okwo. Omu miringo egi, Pequod erikwetoorora ekaba neija kusheenya haihi emyanya yoona emanyirwe ey’okugyenderamu eya Sperm Whale omu nsi yoona, etakashuuma aha Line omu nyanja ya Pacific; aho Ahabu, n’obu ahandi hoona yaabaire aremirwe omu kumukuratira, akaba aine amatsiko g’okuha Moby Dick orutaro, omu nyanja ei yaabaire naakira kumanywa ngu naakira kuzamu; kandi omu bwire obu arikubaasa kuteekateekwaho kuba naagiteganisa.

Kwonka shi hati? omu kusherura oku okwa zoned, Ahabu tarikukwata aha itaka? abavugi be nibanywa empeho? Buzima, naija kwemerera kutunga amaizi. Ngaaha. Okumara obwire buraingwa, hati, eizooba eririkugyendera omu circus ryiruka omunda y’empeta ye ey’omuriro, kandi tiririkwetenga by’okurya kureka ebiri omunda ye. N’ahabw’ekyo Ahabu. Eki nakyo, n’omu nshonga y’okuteera enyanja. Obu ebindi bimotoka by’enyanja birikuteebwa ahansi n’ebintu by’abanyamahanga, kuza omu myanya y’okushutamamu ey’aheeru y’eihanga; emeeri y’enyanja erikuzengyerera omu nsi yoona terikutwara migugu yoona kureka we n’abavugi baayo, eby’okurwanisa byabo n’ebi barikwenda. Aine ebiri omu nyanja yoona ebiteirwe omu cupa omu kiina kye ekirikumara. Naaba aine ballast n’ebintu by’okukoresa; ti byona hamwe n’empunu-lead n’empunu ezitarikubaasa kukoresibwa. Nikyo kiri, ngu, n’obu emeeri ezindi ziraabe zaabaire niziza China kuruga New York, kandi zigarukireyo, zikwatsire aha myaaro mingi, emeeri y’enyanja, omu bwire obwo bwona, neebaasa kuba etareebire akajuma kamwe k’itaka; abavugi be ahabw’okutareeba muntu weena kureka abavugi b’enyanja abarikuzengyerera nk’abo. Nk’omunyaekitiinisa Daniel Boone oine ebireju by’omushenyi, tarikwija kugira muntu weena haihi nawe kureka Obuhangwa bwonka; kandi we naamutwara nk'omukazi omu ihamba ry'amaizi, kandi n'omukazi orikukirayo oburungi, n'obu araabe aine ebihama bingi munonga eby'omutima.

Amashomero agatarimu n’omwe kureka abashaija abato kandi ab’amaani, agagambirweho enyima, nigareetaho entaaniso y’amaani n’amashomero g’abakazi. Ahabw’okuba n’obu ezo nshozi enkazi ziraabe ziine emitwarize y’okutiina, enshaija ezito, nari ente z’ebikopo makumi ana, nk’oku zirikuzeeta, nizo zirikukirayo kurwana omu nshozi zoona eza Leviathans, kandi omu nfumu nizo zirikukirayo kuba ez’akabi kubugana; okwihaho ebyo by’okutangaaza ebiine emitwe erikutukura, ebiine emitwe erikutukura, obumwe ebirikubugana, kandi ebi nibiija kukurwanisa nk’ebinyonyi ebirikutiinisa ebiine ekiniga ahabw’okukwatwa endwara ya penal gout.

Amashomero g’ente z’ebidomora makumi ana nigakira amashomero g’abakazi. Nk’ekimbuuro ky’abato abari omu mashomero ga yunivasite, baijwire okurwana, okushemererwa, n’obubi, barikugwa omu nsi yoona aha rurengo orutarikufayo, orurikugurukaguruka, ngu tihaine omuhandiiki w’obwengye orikubaasa kubaha inshuwarensi nk’oku arikukora aha mutsigazi w’obutabanguko omuri Yale nari Harvard. Nibareka juba akavuyo aka n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, kandi ku barikukura nk’ebicweka bishatu ahari bina, nibataana, kandi baza omu myanya etari emwe n’emwe barikuronda emyanya y’okutuuramu, nikwo kugira ngu, amaju g’abakazi.

Enshonga endiijo erikutaana ahagati y‘amashomero g‘abashaija n‘abakazi n‘obunaku obu n‘eky‘okureeberaho ky‘obuhangwa bw‘abantu. Gamba ngu nooteera ente y’ebipimo Makumi Ana—sitaani omworo! bagyenzi be boona bakamureka. Kwonka teera omwe aha b’eishomero rya harem, kandi bagyenzi be bamwehinguririize n’akamanyiso koona ak’okufayo, obumwe baguma haihi munonga nawe kandi obwire buraingwa munonga, nk’oku bo bonka barikugwa omunyamishwa.
