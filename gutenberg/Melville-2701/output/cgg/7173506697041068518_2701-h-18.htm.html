EISHOMO 89. Eby’enyanja ebirikurahuka n’eby’enyanja ebitarikureebwa.

Okujuriza waif na waif-poles omu shuura erikuhererukayo kwonka emwe, nikyetengyesa okushoboorora okumwe aha biragiro n’ebiragiro by’okuroba eby’enyanja, ebi waif erikubaasa kuteekateekwaho nk’akamanyiso kakuru n’akabonero.

Nikikira kubaho ngu emeeri nyingi ku zirikugyendera omu nyanja ziri omu kigombe, ekinyonyi ekikuru nikibaasa kuteerwa emeeri emwe, reero kitorokye, bwanyima kiitwe kandi kikwatwe emeeri endiijo; kandi aha harimu omu muringo ogutarikuhikaana n’ebintu bingi ebikye ebirikubaho omu bwire bukye, byona nibikwatanisa aha kintu eki kimwe ekikuru. Nk’eky’okureeberaho,—bwanyima y’okubinga n’okukwata enyanja y’enyanja eruhire kandi ey’akabi, omubiri nigubaasa kuruga omu bwato ahabw’omuyaga gw’amaani; kandi erikugyendera hare kuza omu rubaju rw’omuyaga, egarukye ekwatwe omuvugi w’enyanja owa kabiri, orikugikurura omu muringo ogucureire kandi ogurikushemeza aha rubaju, hatariho kabi k’amagara nari omurunga. Nahabwekyo obutaikirizana oburikukirayo kuteganisa n’obushaarizi bukaba nibukira kubaho ahagati y’abajubi, kuri hatariho ebiragiro ebihandiikirwe nari ebitahandiikirwe, eby’ensi yoona, ebitarikuhakanisibwa ebirikukora aha nshonga zoona.

Obundi ebiragiro byonka ebyabaire biri omu butongore ebyabaire nibiheebwa orusa omu biragiro, bikaba biri ebya Holland. Kikaragirwa States-Generals omu mwaka gwa AD 1695. Kwonka n’obu haraabe hatariho ihanga erindi eryatungire ekiragiro ekihandiikirwe eky’okuroba enyanja, kwonka abajubi ba America babaire bari abategyeki baabo n’abaramuzi omu nshonga egi. Baheireyo enkora erikurenga aha biragiro bya Justinian ebya Pandects n’ebiragiro by’ekibiina ky’Abachaina eby’okuzibira okwejumba omu bizinesi z’abantu abandi. Yego; ebiragiro ebi bikaba nibibaasa kuhandiikwa aha nshaho y’omugabekazi Anne, nari aha rubaju rw’omuhoro, kandi bikajwarwa omu bitsya, n’ahabw’ekyo ni bikye.

I. Ekyanyanja kya Fast-Fish n’eky’ekibiina ekirikusiiba aharikyo.

II. Loose-Fish n’omuzaano gw’oburingaaniza aha muntu weena orikubaasa kukikwata juba.

Kwonka ekirikuzaana obubi n’ebiragiro ebi eby’obukugu n’obugufu bwabyo oburikushemeza, oburikwetenga ebiteiso bingi munonga kubaasa kubishoboorora.

Eky’okubanza: Ekyanyanja ekirikurahuka n’enki? Ekyanyanja kihuriire nari kifiire nikirahuka omu by’obuhangwa, kyaba kikwataniise n’emeeri nari obwato oburimu abantu, omu muringo gwona ogurikubaasa kutegyekwa orikugivuga nari abagirimu,—omuti, omugoye, akawaya ka inci mwenda, waaya ya telegirafu, nari orukoba rw’enkobe, byona nibishushana. Nikyo kimwe n’ekyanyanja nikirahuka omu by’obuhangwa kyaba kiine waif, nari akamanyiso koona akamanyirwe ak’okugira ngu kiine; kasita ekibiina ekirikukihaho kirikworeka gye obushoboorozi bwabo obwire bwona kukitwara aha rubaju, hamwe n‘ekigyendererwa kyabo eky‘okukikora.

Ebi n’ebigambo bya sayansi ebirikushoboorora; kwonka ebitebyo by’abavugi b’enyanja bonka obumwe nibiba birimu ebigambo bigumire n’okuteera okugumire munonga—Coke-upon-Littleton y’ekiganja. N’amazima, omu bavugi b’enyanja abahikire kandi ab’ekitiinisa, obutoosha nihaheebwayo ahabw’emisango y’omutaano, ahu kiraabe kiri obutari buringaaniza bw’emitwarize oburengire aha rubaju rumwe kugira ngu ruine enyanja y’enyanja eyabaire neebinga nari eitsirwe orubaju orundi enyima. Kwonka abandi tibarikwegyendesereza nk’okwo na kakye.

Emyaka nka makumi ataano enyima hakabaho omushango gw’okutangaaza ogwa whale-trover ogwabaire gujuriziibwe omuri Bungyereza, ahu abajulizi baashoboroire ngu bwanyima y’okubinga munonga enyanja y’omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho; kandi obu buzima bo (abajulizi) baabaire basingwire omu kuteera eby’enyanja; aha muheru, barikurabira omu kabi k’amagara gaabo, bakaba baine obujunaanizibwa bw’okureka ti nshonda zaabo zonka, kureka n’obwato bwabo bwonka. Aha muheru abajulizi (abavugi b’emeeri endiijo) bakaija n’enyanja y’enyanja, baagiteera, baagiita, baagikwata, kandi aha muheru baagitwara omu maisho g’abajulizi bonka. Kandi abo bajulizi ku baaherize kukyeenwa, kapiteeni waabo akateera enkumu ze omu meno g’abajulizi, kandi yaabahamiza ngu omu muringo gw’okuhakanisa ekikorwa eki yaakozire, hati naija kuguma aine omurongooti gwabo, enkoni, n’obwato, ebyabaire bigumire bikwatsire aha nshozi y’enyanja omu bwire bw’okukwatwa. Nahabwekyo abajulizi hati bakajurira ngu bagarurire omuhendo gw’enyanja yaabo ey’enyanja, omurunga, enkoni, n’obwato.

Mr. Erskine akaba ari omuhabuzi w’abavunanwa; Omukama Ellenborough niwe yaabaire ari omuramuzi. Erskine akaba ari aha rubaju orundi; kandi bwanyima akakishagika arikugira ngu, n’obu omushaija w’ekitiinisa araabe yaabaire abanza kuteera omukazi ogwo enkoni, kandi akagira ngu akasiiba omurundi gumwe, kandi ahabw’okushoberwa kwingi okw’obushaarizi bwe oburikuza omu maizi kwonka, aha muheru akamureka; kwonka akamureka, yaahinduka ekyenyanja ekirikusheesheka; kandi n’ahabw’ekyo omushaija owaakuratsireho ku yaagarukiremu kumuteera enkoni, omukazi ogwo akaba ahinduka eby’obugaiga by’omushaija ogwo owaakuratsireho, hamwe n’enkoni yoona eyaabaire neebaasa kushangwa emukwatsiremu.

Hati omu nshonga eriho hati Erskine akahakanisa ngu eby’okureeberaho by’enyanja n’omukazi bikaba nibishushana.

Eby’okweshengyereza ebi, n’eby’okweshengyereza eby’okugarukamu, byahurirwa gye, omuramuzi omwegyesiibwe munonga omu biragiro ebitebeekanisiibwe akacwamu, nikwo kugira ngu,—Ngu aha bwato, akabuha abajulizi, ahabw’okuba bakaba baburekire kwonka kujuna amagara gaabo; kwonka ngu aha bikwatiraine n’enyanja y’enyanja erikuhakanisibwa, ebikwato by’okuteera, n’orukoba, bikaba biri eby’abavunanwa; ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa whale, ahabw’okuba kikaba kiri Loose-Fish omu bwire bw’okukwatwa okwahamuheru; n’ebikwato by’okuteera n’orukoba ahabw’okuba eby’enyanja ku byabaire birugaho nabyo, (eby’enyanja) bikaba bitunga obugabe omu bintu ebyo; kandi n’ahabw’ekyo omuntu weena bwanyima y’okutwara eby’enyanja akaba aine obugabe aharibyo. Mbwenu abajulizi bwanyima bakatwara eby'enyanja; ergo, ebihandiiko ebyagambwaho bikaba biri ebyabo.

Omuntu wa buriijo arikureeba ensharamu egi ey’Omuramuzi omwegyesiibwe munonga, naabaasa kubaasa kugihakanisa. Kwonka okuhinga kuhika aha ibaare ry’okubanza ery’enshonga, emisingye ebiri mikuru eyateirweho omu biragiro by’okuteera enyanja z’amapapa ebyagambirweho enyima, kandi bikakoresibwa kandi byashoboororwa Lord Ellenborough omu nshonga egambirweho ahaiguru; ebiragiro ebi bibiri ebirikukwata aha Fast-Fish na Loose-Fish, ningira ngu, ku birikuteekateekwaho, nibiija kushangwa biri emisingi y’ebiragiro byona eby’abantu; ahakuba n’obu eraabe eine ebishushani ebigumire eby’okubumba, Hekalu y’Ebiragiro, nka Hekalu y’Abafilistia, eine ebikwato bibiri byonka eby’okwemereraho.

EISHOMO 90. Emitwe nari Emikira.

“Balena ekaba neemara, omukazi aine amagara gaabo, kandi omukazi we aine amagara marungi.” Omunyabwengye, l. 3, c. 3.

Orulatini kuruga omu bitabo by’ebiragiro bya Bungyereza, ebyatwairwe hamwe n’ebigambo ebiri omu nshonga, nikimanyisa, ngu aha nshozi zoona ezakwatsirwe omuntu weena aha rubaju rw’enyanja y’ensi egyo, Omugabe, nk’Omuteezi w’Enyonyi Ow’Ekitiinisa, aine kuba aine omutwe, kandi Omugabekazi aheebwa omukira omu kitiinisa. Okugabanisa oku, omu nshozi y’enyanja, kuri nk’okugabanisa kimwe kya kabiri ky’apuri; tihariho ekisigaireho eky’ahagati. Hati nk’oku ekiragiro eki, omu muringo oguhindwirwemu, kiri omu nkora n’okuhitsya hati omuri Bungyereza; kandi nk’oku kirikuha omu miringo etari emwe n’emwe obutashushana obutari bwa buriijo oburikukwata aha kiragiro ky’omutaano ekya Fast and Loose-Fish, aha nikigambwaho omu shuura endiijo, aha musingi nigwo gumwe ogw’obucureezi ogurikureetera egari y’omwika z’Orungyereza kuba aha nshonga y’emotoka y’omutaano, erikubiikwa omu muringo gw’omutaano ey’okushutamamu ab’obugabe. Omu mwanya gw’okubanza, omu buhame bw’okwenda kumanya ngu ekiragiro ekigambirweho aharuguru nikikyakora, ningumizamu ninkutaho embeera eyaabaireho omu myaka ebiri ehweire.

Nikireebeka ngu abavugi b’enyanja abamwe ab’amazima aba Dover, nari Sandwich, nari omwe aha myaaro ya Cinque, bwanyima y’okuhiiga munonga bakasingura omu kwita n’okuteera aha nyanja ekinyonyi kirungi eki baabaire baabanza kushoboorora hare n’enyanja. Hati emyanya ya Cinque erimu ekicweka nari omu muringo gumwe ahansi y’obushoboorozi bw’omuringo gw’omuporiisi nari beadle, orikwetwa Lord Warden. Okukwaata ofiisi kuruga aha ngoma, ninyesiga ngu emoluments zoona ez’obugabe ezirikubaho omu bicweka bya Cinque Port nizihinduka ahabw’okuheebwa omurimo gwe. Abahandiiki abamwe ofiisi egi neeyetwa sinecure. Kwonka tikwo kiri. Ahabw’okuba Lord Warden naakora munonga obumwe omu kufoba ebishare bye; ebiri ebye okukira munonga ahabw’okubifubira okwo kumwe.

Hati obu abavugi b’enyanja abooro abaabaire bayokize eizooba, bataine bigyere, kandi n’empare zaabo zizingire ahaiguru aha bigyere byabo ebiine ebishaju, bakaba bakuruire eby’enyanja byabo ebishaju ahaiguru kandi byomire, barikweraganisa £150 nungi kuruga omu mafuta n’eigufa eby’omuhendo; kandi omu kuteekateeka barikunywa chai etarikukira kureebwa n’abakazi baabo, n’okunywa amaarwa marungi n’abanywani baabo, aha maani g’emigabo yaabo; up steps omushaija omwegire munonga kandi orikukirayo kuba Omukristaayo kandi omunyambabazi, aine kopi ya Blackstone ahansi y’omukono gwe; kandi akagita aha mutwe gw'enyanja, agira ati—“Emikono!, bakama bangye, ekyenyanja eki n'ekyenyanja ky'omuvugi w'emotoka y'omwika. Ninkikwata nk'ekya Mukama Warden." Aha kintu eki abavugi b’enyanja abooro omu kushoberwa kwabo okw’ekitiinisa—n’ahabw’ekyo buzima Abajungu—batarikumanya eky’okugamba, bagwa omu kushara emitwe yaabo n’amaani okwehinguririza; omu bwire obwo arikureeba n’obusaasi kuruga aha nkungu kuza aha mugyenyi. Kwonka ekyo tikikashoboroire enshonga omu muringo gwona, nari kugorobya omutima ogugumire gw’omushaija omunyabwengye n’okukopa kwa Blackstone. Aha muheru omwe aharibo, bwanyima y’okumara obwire buraingwa arikushwijuma ebiteekateeko bye, akagira obumanzi bw’okugamba,

“Hakyiri, sebo, Mukama Warden n’oha?”

“Omugabe.”

“Kwonka omugabe akaba ataine kakwate n’okutwara ekyenyanja eki?”

“N’ekye.”

“Tubaire turi omu buremeezi bwingi, n’akabi, n’okushohoza sente ezimwe, kandi ebyo byona nibiija kugasira Duke; titurikutunga kintu kyona ahabw’obusaasi bwaitu kureka ebizimba byaitu?”

“N’ekye.”

“Mbwenu shi Duke n’omworo munonga kubaasa kugyemeserezibwa omu muringo ogu ogw’okuhwamu amaani gw’okutunga eby’okurya?”

“N’ekye.”

“Nkateekateeka kuhuumuza maama omugurusi owaabaire ari omu kitanda ahabw’ekicweka ky’omugabo gwangye gw’enyanja egi.”

“N’ekye.”

“Mbwenu shi Duke tarikwija kumarwa n’ekicweka kya kana nari ekicweka?”

“N’ekye.”

Omukigambo kimwe, ekinyonyi eki kikakwatwa kyaguzibwa, kandi Grace we Duke wa Wellington akatunga esente ezo. Okuteekateeka ngu ku orikureeba omu miringo emwe, omushango gukaba nigubaasa kuteekateekwaho, omu mbeera ezo, nk’ogugumire, omunyadiini omwesigwa ow’omu tauni akahandiikira Grace we akahandiiko omu kitiinisa, arikumushaba ngu atware enshonga y’abavugi b’enyanja abo abatari b’omugisha omu bwijwire. Eki Lord wangye Duke omu nshonga yaakigarukamu (amabaruha gombi gakashohozibwa) ngu akaba yaaheza kukora ekyo, kandi akatunga esente, kandi ngu akaba naija kugyemeserezibwa aha mushaija w’ekitiinisa ku araabe omu biro by’omumaisho we (omushaija w’ekitiinisa) yaayanga kwejumba omu nshonga z’abandi. Ogu n’omugurusi okiri omu ntaro, ayemereire aha nshonda z’obugabe bushatu, aha mikono yoona arikugyema abashabi kuhayo?

Nikiija kureebwa juba ngu omu nshonga egi obugabe oburikuteekateekwaho kuba bwa Duke aha nshozi y’enyanja bukaba buri obw’okuheebwayo kuruga ahari Mukama. Twine kwetenga kubuuza obwo aha musingye ki ogu Omutegyeki yaabandize kutaho obugabe obwo. Ebiragiro byonka bikaba byaheza kuteebwaho. Kwonka Plowdon naatuha enshonga ahabw’enki kiri kityo. Plowdon naagira ngu ekinyonyi eki kikwatsirwe omu muringo ogu n’eky’Omugabe n’Omugabekazi, “ahabw’okugira ngu nikikirayo oburungi.” Kandi abacondoozi abahikire eki kibaireho nk’enshonga egumire omu nshonga nk’ezo.

Kwonka shi ahabw’enki Omugabe aine kugira omutwe, kandi Omugabekazi aine omukira? Enshonga y’ekyo, imwe ba looya!

Omu kitabo kye ekirikukwata ahari “Queen-Gold,” nari Queen-pinmoney, omuhandiiki omwe omukuru owa King’s Bench, omwe William Prynne, naagamba atyo: “Omukira gwawe n’ogwa Omugabekazi, ngu akabokisi k’ebijwaro k’Omugabekazi katungye eigufa ry’enyanja.” Hati eki kikahandiikwa omu bwire obu eigufa ry’enyanja erikwiragura ery’enyanja ya Greenland nari Right whale ryabaire nirikoresibwa munonga omu bijwaro by’abakazi. Kwonka eigufa eryo ryonka tiriri omu mukira; kiri omu mutwe, eki n’enshobi y’obusaasi ahari looya w’obwengye nka Prynne. Kwonka shi Omugabekazi n’omukazi w’omu nyanja, okuheebwayo omukira? Amakuru g’enfumu nigabaasa kwehereera aha.

Hariho eby’enyanja bibiri eby’obugabe ebi abahandiiki b’ebiragiro b’Orungyereza baateireho omu muringo ogu—ekyenyanja ekirikwetwa whale na sturgeon; byombi eby’obugaiga by’obugabe ahansi y’obugarukiro obumwe, kandi omu iziina nibiha eitaagi rya ikumi ry’esente z’omugabe eza buriijo. Tindikumanya ngu hariho omuhandiiki ondiijo owaagambire aha nshonga egyo; kwonka ahabw’okuteekateeka, nikireebeka ahariinye ngu ekyenyanja kya sturgeon kiine kugabwa omu muringo nigwo gumwe n’ekyenyanja kya whale, Omugabe arikutunga omutwe ogurikweyongyera munonga kandi ogurikweshushaniriza nk’ogw’ekyenyanja ekyo, ogu, ku gurikuteekateekwaho omu muringo gw’akamanyiso, gurikubaasa kuba niguba guri ogw’okushemeza ogurikurugiirira aha kuteekateeka ngu hariho okushemererwa. Kandi n’ahabw’ekyo nikireebeka ngu hariho enshonga omu bintu byona, nangwa n’omu biragiro.





EISHOMO 91. Pequod Nabugana Rose-Bud.

“Busha kikaba kiri okuronda Ambergriese omu nda ya Leviathan egi, fetor etarikugumisiriza erikwehakana obutabuuza.” Omukuru T. Browne, VE

Hakaba hahingwireho wiiki emwe nari ibiri bwanyima y’ekishushani ky’okuroba enyanja ekyahererukireyo kugambwaho, kandi obu twabaire nituvuga mpora mpora aha nyanja erimu oturo, erimu omuyaga, ahagati y’eizooba, obwo enyindo nyingi eziri aha mutwe gw’enyanja ya Pequod zikahamya ngu n’abavumbuzi abarikwegyendesereza kukira amaisho ashatu agaabaire gari ahaiguru. Ekinuuko ky’omutaano kandi ekitarikushemeza munonga kikaba nikihurirwa omu nyanja.

Stubb akagira ati: “Ninyija kuteera akaruuru hati, ngu ahu hariho ebimwe aha binyonyi ebyo ebiine emibazi ebi twateireho eizooba erindi. Nkateekateeka ngu nibiija kuguruka omu bwire bukye.”

Obwahati, emicuucu omu maisho ekasheesheka aha rubaju; kandi aho hare hakaba hariho emeeri, erimu emipira yaayo ezingire erikworeka ngu hariho ekika ky’enyanja y’enyanja ekiine kuba kiri aha rubaju. Ku twabaire nitugyendera haihi, omugyenyi akooreka erangi z’Orufaransa kuruga aha mutwe gwe; kandi ahabw’ekicu ky’enyonyi z’omu nyanja ezirikwetwa vulture sea-fowl ekyabaire nikyetoorora, kandi kirikugurukaguruka, kandi kimwehinguririize, kikaba nikireebeka gye ngu ekinyonyi ekikuru ekyabaire kiri aha rubaju kiine kuba kiri eki abajubi barikweta ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa blasted whale, nikwo kugira ngu ekinyonyi ekifiire kitaine ki kirikuteganisibwa aha nyanja, kandi kikaguruka omutumbi ogutahikire.
