EISHOMO 1. Ebirikubaho.

Munyete Ishimaeli. Emyaka emwe enyima—n’obu ndaabe n’obwire ki buzima—n’okugira sente nkye nari ntaine sente omu nshaho yangye, kandi hatariho kintu kyona eky’omutaano ekyabaire kinshemeza aha rubaju rw’enyanja, nkateekateeka ngu ndyagyendera aha bwato nk’akakye n’okureeba ekicweka ky’ensi ekirimu amaizi. Ni omuringo ogu nyine ogw’okubinga omunda y’omubiri n’okutebeekanisa oku eshagama erikutambura. Buri murundi ogu ndikweshanga ngu ninkura n’obusaasi ahabw’akanwa; buri bwire obu kirikuba kiri Novemba y’obufuki, ey’enjura y’amaani omu mutima gwangye; buri kweshanga ntarikweyendeza n’okuhuumuraho omu maisho g’ebibiiko by’eshandukye, kandi n’okureetaho enyima ya buri kuziika oku ndikubugana; kandi na munonga buri hypos yangye erikuntunga omukono gw’ahaiguru, ngu nikyetengyesa omusingye gw’emitwarize gw’amaani kunzibira okutaaha omu muhanda omu buteeka, n’okuteera abantu enkofiira z’omu muringo gw’oburyo—bwanyima, ninteekateeka ngu n’obwire bw’okuhika omu nyanja ahonaaho nk’oku ndikubaasa. Eki nikyo kintu kyangye ekirikuhindura pistol n’omupiira. N’okutunguuka kw’obuhangwa Cato naayeteera aha rusyo rwe; N’obucureezi n’okutwara omu bwato. Tihariho ekirikutangaaza omuri eki. Ku baraabe baakimanyire, haihi abashaija boona omu diguri yaabo, obwire obumwe nari obundi, nibakunda munonga haihi n’enyehurira zimwe aha nyanja nanye.

Hati hariho orurembo rwawe orw’omu kizinga kya Manhattoes, orurikwehinguririza emyanya y’okushutamamu nk’ebizinga bya Buhindi ebirikwehinguririza ebibungo by’amaizi—eby’obushuubuzi nibyehinguririize n’omugyera gwe. Aha rubaju rwa buryo n’orwa bumosho, enguuto nizikutwara omu maizi. Omu rurembo rwayo orurikukirayo obuhango ni battery, ahu enkongoro egyo ey’ekitiinisa erikwogibwa ebicu, kandi ekafukiirizibwa omuyaga, ogwabaire gutarikureebwa eshaaha nkye enyima. Reeba abantu baingi abarikureeba amaizi okwo.

Yetoroore orurembo rw’ekirooto kya Sabato omu mwabazyo. Gyenda kuruga Corlears Hook oze Coenties Slip, kandi kurugayo, oze omu Whitehall, omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho. Nooreeba ki?—Bateirwe nk’abarinzi abatarikugamba okwehinguririza orurembo rwona, beemereire enkumi n’enkumi z’abantu abarikufa abateirweho omu biteekateeko by’enyanja. Abamwe beesigamiire aha bifunjo; abamwe bakaba bashutami aha mitwe y’enyanja; abamwe barikureeba ahaiguru y’ebigo by’emeeri kuruga China; abamwe bari ahaiguru munonga omu nshaho, nk’abarikuteeraho kutunga ekishushani kirungi munonga eky’okureeba omu nyanja. Kwonka aba boona n’abantu b’omu nsi; z’ebiro bya wiiki ziteirwe omu nshaho n’omu pulasita—zibohirwe aha bitanda, ziteirwe emisumaari aha ntebe, zikwatsirwe aha meeza. Mbwenu shi eki nikibaho kita? Emisiri ya kinyaatsi ehweireho? Nibakora ki aha?

Kwonka reeba! aha nihaija abantu baingi, barikugyendera omu maizi bagoroire, kandi barikureebeka nk’abarikuza kuza omu maizi. Ekitari kya buriijo! Tihariho ekirikubaasa kubamara kureka ekigarukwamu ky’ensi ekirikukirayo; okuzengyerera ahansi y’ekicuucu ky’amabiikiro g’okwo tikirikwija kumara. Ngaaha, ziine kuhika haihi n’amaizi nk’oku zirikubaasa zitarikugwamu Kandi niho zirikwemerera—mairo nyingi aharizo—ligi. Abanyabuzaare boona, nibaruga omu mihanda n’omu mihanda, omu nguuto n’omu mihanda—omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho, omu burugwa-izooba, omu bukiizi-bwa-buryo, n’omu burengyerwa-izooba. Kwonka hanu boona nibeegaita. Ngambira, shi oburungi bw’amaani g’empimbi za kampasi z’emeeri ezo zoona nibuzikurura kuzayo?

Ogundi murundi. Gamba ngu ori omu ihanga; omu itaka rimwe erihango ery’enyanja. Twara haihi omuhanda gwona ogu orikwenda, kandi ikumi ahari gumwe gukutwara ahansi omu kiina, gukurekye aho aha rubaju rw’ekidiba omu mugyera. Hariho oburogo omuri kyo. Reka omuntu orikukirayo obutagira biteekateeko agwe omu biteekateeko bye ebirikukirayo obuhango—yemerera ogwo mushaija aha bigyere bye, oteho ebigyere bye a-going, kandi naija kukutwara omu maizi ataine nshobi, amaizi gaaba gariho omu kicweka ekyo kyona. Waba oine eiriho omu ihamba rihango rya America, gyezaho okugyezesa oku, karavani yaawe yaaba neetunga purofeesa w’eby’obuhangwa. Eego, nk’oku buri omwe arikumanya, okuteekateeka n’amaizi nibikwatanisa ebiro byona.

Kwonka hanu hariho omunyabwengye. Naayenda kukusiiga ekishushani ky’omubonano ekirikukirayo obuhango, ekirikukirayo obucuucu, ekirikukirayo obucureezi, ekirikukirayo kushemeza omu kihanga kyona ekya Saco. Ni kintu ki ekikuru eki arikukoresa? Aho emiti ye eyemereire, buri emwe eine omuti ogurimu ekiina, nk’oku omunyabwengye n’omusharaba biri omunda; kandi hanu nihagwejegyera omusiri gwe, kandi aho niho ente ze zigwejegyeire; kandi ahaiguru kuruga omu kaju ako nihaza omwika gw'okugwejegyera. Omunda y’ebibira bya hare munonga omuyaga niguteera omu muringo gw’obushema, guhika aha nshozi ezirikweshushaniriza ezirikwoga omu rangi yaazo eya bururu ey’aha rubaju rw’ensozi. Kwonka n’obu ekishushani kyabaire kigwejegyeire kityo, kandi n’obu omuti ogu ogw’omupaini guraabe niguzunguza okuhuuta kwagwo nk’ebibabi aha mutwe gw’omuriisa ogu, kwonka byona bikaba biri ebya busha, okwihaho eriisho ry’omuriisa ryaba riteire aha mugyera gw’amahano ogwabaire guri omu maisho ge. Gyenda otaayaayire Prairies omu Kwamukaaga, obu orikugyendera omu mairo nyingi kuhika aha mavi omu bimuri bya Tiger-lilies—n’enki ekirikushemeza kimwe ekirikwenda?—Amaizi—tihariho n’eitonyezo ry’amaizi aho! Ku ogire ngu Niagara ekaba eri omushenyi gwonka, okaabaire nooza kugyendera mairo zaawe rukumi kugireeba? Ahabw’enki omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo omworo owa Tennessee, ku yaatungire esente ibiri z’efeeza ahonaaho, yaateekateeka yaaba naija kumugurira ekooti, ​​ei yaabaire naayetenga omu muringo gw’obusaasi, nari yaateeka esente ze omu rugendo rw’okugyendera aha bigyere kuza Rockaway Beach? Ahabwenki haihi buri mwojo oine amagara marungi kandi oine omutima gw’amagara marungi omuriwe, omu bwire bumwe nari obundi ari omushema kuza omu nyanja? Ahabw’enki aha rugyendo rwawe orw’okubanza nk’omuvugi, iwe wenka waahurira okutengeeta kw’amazima nk’okwo, obu waabanza kugambirwa ngu iwe n’emeeri yaawe hati timurikureebeka aha itaka? Ahabw’enki Abaperesi aba ira baabaire nibatwara enyanja nk’erikwera? Ahabwenki Abagriika bakagiha ruhanga ow’omutaano, kandi murumuna we wenka owa Jove? Buzima ebi byona tibiine makuru. Kandi n’okukiraho amakuru g’ekitebyo ekyo ekya Narcissus, owaabaire atarikubaasa kushoboorora ekishushani ekirikubonabonesa, eky’obucureezi eki yaareebire omu iziba, akakijubamu yaamira amaizi. Kwonka ekishushani ekyo nikyo kimwe, itwe ahabwaitu nitukireeba omu migyera yoona n’omu nyanja. Ni ekishushani ky’ekishushani ky’amagara ekitarikubaasa kukwatwa; kandi eki nikyo kishumuruzo kya byona.

Hati, ku ndikugira ngu nyine omuze gw’okuza omu nyanja buri kutandika kugira ekizibu ky’amaisho, kandi nkatandika kuba n’okumanya okurengire aha bihaha byangye, tindikumanyisa ngu n’okuteekateeka ngu nkaba ntakaza omu nyanja nk’omusaabazi. Ahabw'okuba okuza nk'omusaabazi oine kuba oine akashaho, kandi akashaho n'ekikuta kyonka okwihaho waaba oine eki oinemu. Oyihireho ekyo, abavugi nibarwara endwara z’enyanja—bakura barikutongana—tibarikugwejegyera ekiro—tibarikweshemeza munonga, nk’ekintu ky’omutaano;—ngaaha, tindikuzayo nk’omuvugi; kandi n’obu ndaabe ndi ekintu ky’omunyugunyu, tindikuza omu nyanja nka Commodore, nari Captain, nari Cook. Ekitiinisa n’entaaniso y’ebicweka nk’ebyo ninbireka abo abarikubikunda. Aha rubaju rwangye, ninyanga emirimo yoona ey’ekitiinisa ey’ekitiinisa, okugyezibwa n’okubonabona kw’omuringo gwona. Nikiba kiri kimwe nk’oku ndikubaasa kukora kwereeberera, ntarikureeberera emeeri, amaato g’omu nyanja, amaato g’omu nyanja, amaato g’omu nyanja n’ebindi. Kandi aha by’okuza nk’omuteeki,—n’obu ndaabe ninkiikiriza ngu hariho ekitiinisa kingi omu ekyo, omuteeki ari nk’omukuru w’amahe aha bwato—kwonka, omu muringo gumwe, tinkakundaga enkoko z’okwotsya;—n’obu ziraabe zikaba ziyokize, ziteirwemu amajuta omu muringo gw’obwengye, kandi ziteirwemu omunyugunyu omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa, tihaine ondiijo orikubaasa kugira ngu, enkoko eziyokize okukira oku ndikwenda. Nikuruga omu kuramya ebishushani kw’Abanyamisri aba ira aha mbaraasi y’omugyera eyokize n’embaraasi y’omugyera eyokize, ngu nooreeba ebishushani by’ebihangirwe ebyo omu nju zaabo empango z’okuteekamu emigaati nk’empimbi.

Ngaaha, ku ndikuza omu nyanja, ninza nk’omuvugi w’enyanja owanguhi, omu maisho g’omuti gwonka, nshutama ahansi omu kigo ky’okubanza, ahaiguru aho kuhika aha mutwe gw’omuti gw’obugabe. N’amazima, nibakunda kunragiira aha bimwe, kandi bankora okuguruka kuruga aha spar kuza aha spar, nk’enzigye omu kishaka kya Maaci. Kandi omu kubanza, ekintu ky’omuringo ogu tikirikushemeza ekirikumara. Nikikwata aha kuteekateeka kw’omuntu okw’ekitiinisa, namunonga waaba nooruga omu ka eya ira ehamire omu nsi, aba Van Rensselaers, nari aba Randolphs, nari aba Hardicanutes. Kandi okukira byona, ku oraabe otakatandikire kuta omukono gwawe omu n’ekiyungu ky’etara, obaire nookitegyeka nk’omushomesa w’eishomero ry’omu ihanga, orikureetera aboojo abaraingwa kwemerera barikukutiina. Okuhinduka n’okw’amaani, ninkuhamiza kimwe, kuruga aha mwegyesa w’eishomero kuza aha muvugi w’enyanja, kandi nikwetengyesa omubazi gw’amaani ogwa Seneca n’Abastoiki kukubaasisa kushekyerera n’okugugumisiriza. Kwonka n’eki nikihwaho omu bwire.

Kandi shi, ebicweka bimwe ebya ira eby’omuvugi w’enyanja byaragiira ngu ntunge ekyererezi n’okusheenya ahansi aha bishengye by’enyanja? Obutagira kitiinisa obwo nibuhika ki, bwapimirwa, ninmanyisa, omu minzaani y’endagaano ensya? Noogira ngu maraika omukuru Gaburieli nateekateeka ekintu kyona ekikye ahariinye, ahabw’okugira ngu ahonaaho kandi omu kitiinisa nyorobera ogwo mugurusi hunks omu mbeera egyo yonka? N'oha otari muhuuku? Ngambira ekyo. Mbwenu, mbwenu, n’obu abakuru b’enyanja abakuru baraabe bararagiira aha nshonga egi—n’obu baraabe nibabaasa kunteera ebikonde n’okunteera ebikonde, nyine okumarwa ahabw’okumanya ngu byona biri gye; ngu abandi boona n’omu muringo gumwe nari ogundi nibaheereza omu muringo nigwo gumwe munonga—yaaba omu kureeba kw’omubiri nari okw’omubiri, nikwo kugira ngu; kandi nikwo ekibunu ky’ensi yoona kihinguraho okwehinguririza, kandi emikono yoona eshemerire kusiiga ebibega bya buri omwe, kandi eshemererwe.

Kandi, butoosha ninza omu nyanja nk’omuvugi w’enyanja, ahabw’okuba nibakora enshonga y’okumpa sente ahabw’oburemeezi bwangye, obwo batarikushashura abavugi n’esente n’emwe ei naahuriire. Kureka, abavugi nibo baine kushashura. Kandi hariho entaaniso yoona omu nsi ahagati y’okushashura n’okushashurwa. Ekikorwa ky’okushashura obundi nikyo kintu ekirikukirayo obutashemererwa eki abashuma babiri ab’omu misiri y’ebijuma baatureeteire. Kwonka okushashurwa ,—niki ekiraagyeragyeranisibwe nakyo? Omurimo gw’omu rurembo ogu omuntu arikutungamu esente buzima n’ogw’okutangaaza, twateekateeka ngu nitwikiriza munonga ngu esente nizo zirikureeta endwara zoona ez’omu nsi, kandi ngu omu muringo gwona omuntu oine esente tarikubaasa kutaaha omu iguru. Ah! ka turikwehayo n’okushemererwa omu kucwekyerera!

Aha muheru, butoosha ninza omu nyanja nk’omuvugi w’enyanja, ahabw’okukora ebisasayizi birungi n’omuyaga ogushemeire oguri aha rubaju rw’omu maisho g’enyanja. Ahabw’okuba nk’oku kiri omu nsi egi, omuyaga gw’omutwe nigukira munonga omuyaga ogurikuruga omu rubaju rw’enyima (nikwo kugira ngu, waaba otakahendaga ekiragiro kya Pythagoras), n’ahabw’ekyo omu bicweka ebikuru Commodore aha quarter-deck naatunga empeho ye omu mukono gwa kabiri kuruga aha bavugi b’enyanja abari aha forecastle. Naateekateeka ngu naabanza kugihuuta; kwonka tikwo kiri. Omu muringo nigwo gumwe abantu boona nibeebembera abeebembezi baabo omu bintu ebindi bingi, omu bwire nibwo bumwe obu abeebembezi barikukiteekateekaho kakye. Kwonka ahabwenki kyabaireho ngu bwanyima y’okuguma ninnuuka enyanja nk’omuvugi w’enyanja omushuubuzi, hati nkaba nyine kugitwara omu mutwe gwangye kuza omu rugyendo rw’okuroba enyanja; ogu n’omuporiisi otarikureebwa owa Fates, orikunreeba obutoosha, kandi omu bwesherekye arikunteera embwa, kandi arikunhindura omu muringo gumwe ogutarikubaasika—naabaasa kugarukamu gye okukira ondiijo muntu weena. Kandi, hatariho kubangaanisa, okuza kwangye omu rugyendo oru orw’okuroba enyanja, kikaba kiri kimwe aha puroguraamu mpango ya Providence eyaabaire etebeekanisiibwe ira munonga. Kikaija nk’omuringo gw’okuteera akaari omu bugufu n’okweshongora wenka ahagati y’ebyeshongoro bingi. Ninkitwara ngu ekicweka eki eky'ebiragiro kiine kuba kyagyendeire aha kintu nk'eki:

“ Okuteera akaruuru k’amaani akarikuhakanisibwa aha ntebe y’obwa Purezidenti wa America. “ORUTARO RW’ESHAGAMA OMURI AFFGANISTAN.”

N’obu ndaabe ntarikubaasa kumanya ahabw’enki kyabaire kiri ekyo buzima ngu abo bakuru b’emizaano, aba Fates, bakanta ahansi ahabw’ekicweka eki eky’obushema eky’orugyendo rw’okuroba enyanja, obu abandi baabaire batebeekanisiibwe ahabw’ebicweka by’omutaano omu bihikiirizi by’ahaiguru, n’ebicweka bigufu kandi ebyanguhi omu mizaano y’okushemeza ey’ekitiinisa, n’ebicweka by’okushemererwa omu mizaano y’okushemeza—n’obu eki kiraabe ntarikubaasa kugamba ahabw’enki; kwonka, hati ku ndikwijuka embeera zoona, ninteekateeka ngu nimbaasa kureeba kakye omu nshonga n’ebigyendererwa ebyabaire nibimpa omu muringo gw’obugobya ahansi y’okwehindura, byandeetera kutandika kukora ekicweka eki naakozire, okwihaho okunshobera omu kuhabisa ngu kikaba kiri okutoorana okurikuruga omu kuteekateeka kwangye okutaine rubaju n’okucwamu kwangye.

Ekikuru omu bigyendererwa ebi kikaba kiri ekiteekateeko ky’amaani eky’enyanja mpango ahabwayo. Ekicuncu nk’ekyo eky’okutangaaza kandi eky’amazima kikandeetera kwenda kumanya byona. Reero enyanja z’omu ishwa n’eza hare ahu yaabaire azingira ekizinga kye ekihango; akabi akatarikuhikirizibwa, akataine iziina ak’enyanja; ebi, n’eby’okutangaaza byona ebyabaire biriho eby’okureeba n’okuhurira eby’omu Patagonia rukumi, bikanyamba kunhindura aha ki naabaire ninyenda. Aha bashaija abandi, obundi, ebintu nk’ebyo bikaba bitarikwija kuba eby’okuhiga; kwonka nyowe, ninbonabonesibwa ahabw'okusheeshasheesha okutahwaho ahabw'ebintu ebiri hare. Ninkunda kugyendera omu nyanja ezizibiirwe, n’okuhika aha nyanja z’obunyamahe. Obutafayo aha kirungi, ninrahuka kureeba ekirikutiinisa, kandi nkaba nimbaasa kuguma ninkwatanisa nakyo—bakaba nibaija kunikiriza—ahabw’okuba ni kirungi kuba omu nkoragana n’abasibe boona ab’omu mwanya ogu omuntu arikuraaramu.

N’ahabw’ekyo, ahabw’ebintu ebi, orugyendo rw’okuroba enyanja rukaba nirushemeza; emiryango mikuru y’omwegyemure ey’ensi y’eby’okutangaaza ekazunguruka, kandi omu biteekateeko by’omu ishwa ebyabaire binshutama aha kigyendererwa kyangye, bibiri na bibiri aho bikaza omu mutima gwangye ogw’omunda, emigyenzo etaine bugarukiro ey’enyanja, kandi, ahagati y’ebingi aharibyo byona, ekishushani kimwe kihango ekiine enkofiira, nk’orushozi rw’orubaare omu mwanya.





EISHOMO 2. Enshaho ya Kapeti.

Nkata esaati emwe nari ibiri omu nshaho yangye eya ira eya kapeti, naagiteeka ahansi y’omukono gwangye, reero naatandika kuza Cape Horn na Pacific. Nkaruga omu rurembo orurungi orwa Manhatto orwa ira, nkahika omuri New Bedford nk’oku kihikire. Ekiro kikaba kiri eky’Orwamukaaga omu Kwaikumi-n’abiri. Nkahwamu amaani munonga ku naamanyire ngu akapapura kakye akarikuza Nantucket kakaba kaherize kugyendera aha meeri, kandi ngu tihaine muringo gwona gw’okuhika omu mwanya ogwo ogwabaire nigwija kumpa, kuhitsya aha Rwokubanza orwakuratsireho.

Nk’oku abato abaingi abarikwenda obusaasi n’ebibonerezo by’okuteera enyanja barikwemerera aha New Bedford niyo emwe, kurugayo kutandika orugyendo rwabo, nikibaasa kuba nikikwatagana ngu nyowe, ahabw’omwe, nkaba ntaine kiteekateeko ky’okukora ekyo. Ahabw’okuba ebiteekateeko byangye bikateebwaho kugyendera omu meeri etariho endiijo kureka omu bwato bwa Nantucket, ahabw’okuba hakaba hariho ekintu kirungi, ekirimu oruyombo ahari buri kimwe ekiine akakwate n’ekizinga ekyo ekya ira ekimanyirwe munonga, ekyanshemeize omu muringo gw’okutangaaza. Oyihireho ekyo n’obu New Bedford omu bwire obuhweire ebaire neetwara mporampora bizinesi y’okuroba enyanja, kandi n’obu omu nshonga egi Nantucket omugurusi omworo hati ari enyima ye munonga, kwonka Nantucket ekaba eri ey’okubanza ye mpango—Tire ya Carthage egi;—omwanya ogu enyanja y’omu Amerika eyabandize kufa yaabaire ekwatsiremu. Ni nkahi ahu abo bavugi b’ebinyonyi abajungu, aba Red-Men, baabandize kuruga omu bwato kuza kubinga Leviathan kureka kuruga Nantucket? Kandi shi ni nkahi kureka kuruga Nantucket, nabwo, obwo bwato bukye obw’okubanza obw’okweshemeza bwateirweho, ekicweka kimwe kirimu amabaare g’omuhendo kuruga aheeru y’ihanga—nikwo ekigano kirikugyenda—kuteera enyanja z’ebinyonyi, kubaasa kuzoora obu zaabaire ziri haihi munonga kubaasa kuta omu kabi omuhoro kuruga aha bowsprit?

Hati ahabw’okugira ekiro, eizooba, n’ekiro ekindi ekirikukuratira omu maisho gangye omuri New Bedford, ntakatandikire kuza aha mwaaro ogu naabaire ninza kuza, kikaba enshonga y’okwerarikirira ahu naabaire nyine kurya n’okubyama omu bwire obwo. Kikaba kiri ekiro ekirikureebeka nk’ekirikubanganisa munonga, ngaaha, ekiro ky’omwirima munonga kandi eky’obusaasi, ekirimu obufuki oburikuruma kandi ekitaine kushemererwa. Nkaba ntarikumanya muntu weena omu mwanya ogwo. Eego, nikwo naabaire ninteekateeka, n’omuraaro orikutiinisa; abaire ari omu kurwana, akasharaho omu muringo gw'okutiina, kandi hanu ari, kuruga aha mubazi gw'okushemeza kwonka. Kwonka omu kaanya ako akatunga omugisha gw’okuhindura amaisho ge kureeba omushana, ngu nkareeba gye ngu tibarikubaasa kuba sticking-plasters na kakye, ebyo bipande ebirikwiragura aha matama ge. Zikaba ziri amabara g’omuringo gumwe nari ogundi. Omu kubanza nkaba ntarikumanya eki ndikukora ahari eki; kwonka omu bwire bukye, nkateekateeka aha mazima. Nkaijuka ekitebyo ky’omushaija omweru—omunyabwengye nawe—owaabaire agwire omu bantu abarikurya abantu, bakaba bamuteireho tattoo. Nkahendera ngu omuvugi ogu w’enkoni, omu rugendo rwe orwa hare, aine kuba yaatungire oburemeezi nk’obwo. Kandi shi n’enki, nkateekateeka, bwanyima y’ebyo byona! Ni aheeru ye yonka; omushaija naabaasa kuba omwesigwa omu ruhu rwona. Kwonka obwo, eki arikukora aha rangi ye etari y’ensi, ekicweka ekyo kyayo, ninmanyisa, kigwejegyeire kyehinguririize, kandi ekitaine rubaju rwona aha bikwatiraine n’okuteeraho ebishushani. Okuhamya, nikibaasa kuba kitaine kindi kureka ekoti nungi y’okujwara erangi y’omu kicweka ky’obutiti; kwonka tinkahuriraga eizooba eririkwotsya ririkuhindura omweru kuba omweru owa kyenju erikutukura. Kwonka, nkaba ntakazaga omu Nyanja z’Oburugwa-izooba; kandi obundi eizooba eryo rikareetaho ebintu ebi eby’omutaano aha ruhu. Hati, ebiteekateeko ebi byona ku byabaire nibinhinguraho nk’enkuba, omuteezi w’enkoni ogu tarandeetegyereza na kakye. Kwonka, bwanyima y’okugumirwa okukye kwigura enshaho ye, akatandika kugifukaana, kandi omu bwire obwo akaihayo ekika kya tomahawk, n’akashaho k’oruhu rw’enyanja akarimu obwoya. Ebi akabita aha kifuba ekya ira ahagati y’ekishengye, bwanyima yaatwara omutwe gwa New Zealand—ekintu ekirikutiinisa munonga—yaagunyiganyiga ahansi omu nsaho. Hati akaihaho enkofiira ye—enkofiira nsya y’ebinyonyi ebirikwetwa beaver—obu naahikire haihi kweshongora n’okutangaara okusya. Tihariho ishokye aha mutwe gwe—tihariho n’omwe orikubaasa kugambaho ​​haakiri—tihariho ekindi kureka akarungo kakye k’omutwe akaabaire kahindukire aha buso bwe. Omutwe gwe ogwabaire guri nk’ekiharaani hati gukaba nigusherura ensi yoona nk’ekihanga ekiine obukooko. Kuri noogira ngu omugyenyi ogwo tiwemereire ahagati yangye n’orwigi, nkaba ninza kururugaho ahonaaho okukira oku naabaire ninteeraho ekiihuro.

Nangwa n’obu kyabaire kiri, nkateekateeka ekintu ky’okusheesheka kuruga omu dirisa, kwonka kikaba kiri ekishengye kya kabiri enyima. Tindikugira obwoba, kwonka eki ndikukora aha mushema ogu orikuteera omutwe ogwa purpura kikahinguraho kimwe okwetegyereza kwangye. Obutamanya n’omuzaire w’okutiina, kandi ahabw’okugira ngu ntaine nshonga n’okushoberwa ahabw’omugyenyi, ninkihamya ngu hati nkaba nimutiina munonga nk’oku kyabaire kiri sitaani wenka owaabaire ahenda omu kishengye kyangye omu kiro. Nangwa, nkaba nimutiina munonga ngu nkaba ntari game enough omu bwire obwo bwonka kumugambira, n’okushaba eky’okugarukamu ekirikumara aha bikwatiraine n’ekyabaire nikireebeka nk’ekitarikushoboororwa omuriwe.

Omu bwire obwo, akagumizamu n’emirimo y’okujumbura, kandi aha muheru yaayoreka ekifuba kye n’emikono ye. Nk’oku ndikutuura, ebicweka bye ebi ebishwekirwe bikaba biine ebishushani by’ebikwato nk’eby’omu maisho ge; omugongo gwe nagwo gukaba guri aha bifunjo nibyo bimwe eby’omwirima; akaba nashusha nk’owaabaire ari omu Rutaro rw’Emyaka Makumi Ashatu, kandi akarugaho kwonka aine esaati y’okuhamira aha pulasita. Kandi ekindi, amaguru ge gonka gakaba gaine akamanyiso, nk’oku ekipapura ky’ebikyere ebirikwiragura byabaire nibihunga aha bihimba by’emiti y’emizeituuni emito. Hati kikaba nikireebeka gye ngu aine kuba ari omunyamaishwa orikuzira nari ondiijo owaabaire atwairwe aha bwato bw’enyanja omu nyanja z’omumashuma, kandi atyo akahika omu ihanga eri ery’Ekikristaayo. Nkatetema ku ndikukiteekateekaho. Omushuubuzi w’emitwe nawe—obundi emitwe ya barumuna be. Naabaasa kutwara ekihika kyangye—eiguru! reeba egyo nkongoro!

Kwonka hakaba hatariho obwire bw’okutetema, ahabw’okuba hati omunyamaishwa ogwo akaza omu kintu ekyanshemeize kimwe, kandi kyampamiza ngu buzima aine kuba ari omupagani. Okuza aha grego ye erikuremeera, nari wrapall, nari dreadnaught, ei yaabaire ahanikire aha ntebe enyima, akafukaana omu nsaho, kandi yaarugamu omu bwire buraingwa ekishushani kikye ekiine obuhuta ekirikwenda kumanya ekiine ekibunu aha mugongo, kandi erangi yonka ey’omwana w’omu Congo ow’ebiro bishatu. Okwijuka omutwe ogwabaire guteirweho amajuta, omu kubanza nkaba ndi haihi kuteekateeka ngu ekishushani eki ekirikwiragura n’omwana buzima owaabaire arinzirwe omu muringo gumwe ogurikushushana. Kwonka ku naareebire ngu kikaba kitari kikye na kakye, kandi ngu kikaba nikishemeza munonga nk’omuti gw’omuti gw’omusheenyi ogushemeziibwe, nkacwamu ngu tikiine kuba kiri ekishushani ky’embaaho, eki buzima kyabaire kiri. Ahabw’okuba hati omunyamaishwa naaza ahaiguru omu mwanya gw’omuriro ogutarimu kintu kyona, kandi arikwihaho orubaaho rw’omuriro orurimu empapura, ateekaho ekishushani eki ekikye ekiine omugongo gw’okuhungira, nk’ekikopo kya tenpin, ahagati y’ebihimba by’omuriro. Ebipande by’omukyeeka n’amatafaari goona agabaire gari omunda gakaba garimu omucuucu mwingi, n’ahabw’ekyo nkateekateeka ngu omwanya ogu gw’omuriro gukakora akacweka kakye akahikire munonga ak’okuramizamu ekishushani kye ekya Congo.

Hati nkaba nshutama amaisho gangye munonga aha kishushani ekyabaire kisherekirwe kimwe kya kabiri, ndikuhurira kwonka ntaine busingye omu bwire obwo—kureeba ekyabaire nikiza kukuratiraho. Okubanza aiha ebihimba nk’emirundi ebiri omu nsaho ye eya grego, abiteeka gye omu maisho g’ekishushani; reero akataho akacweka ka bisikwiti y’emeeri ahaiguru kandi akataho omuriro kuruga omu ntambi, yaayotsya ebishaju ebyo omu muriro gw’ekitambo. Obwahati, bwanyima y’okunyaga emirundi mingi omu muriro omu bwangu, n’okugaruka enyima omu bwangu bw’okugarurayo enkumu ze (ahu yaabaire nareebeka nk’orikuziyotsya kubi), aha muheru akasingura okushohoza bisikwiti; reero akafukaho omuriro n’evu kakye, yaabiha omujungu muto omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa. Kwonka aka sitaani akakye kakaba nikareebeka nk’akatarikukunda munonga ebishare by’omuringo gw’okwoma nk’ogwo; tarateireho kuhindura eminwa ye. Ebikorwa ebi byona eby’okutangaaza bikaba bihererukayo n’amaraka g’omu kanwa agatari ga buriijo kuruga aha muramya, owaabaire nareebeka nk’orikushaba omu kyeshongoro ky’okweshongora nari shi arikweshongora zaaburi y’ekipagani nari endiijo, omu bwire obwo amaisho ge gakaba nigagurukaguruka omu muringo ogutari gw’obuhangwa munonga. Aha muheru kuzima omuriro, akatwara ekishushani ekyo ahaiguru ataine mukoro munonga, kandi yaagaruka yaakiteeka omu nsaho ye eya grego ataine kufayo nk’oku ari omuzaani w’emizaano arikuteera enkoko y’enku efiire omu nsaho.

Ebikorwa ebi byona eby’okutangaaza bikayongyera aha kutashemererwa kwangye, kandi kumureeba hati arikworeka obumanyiso bw’amaani obw’okuhendera emirimo ye y’obushuubuzi, kandi arikuguruka omu kitanda naanye, nkateekateeka ngu obwire bukahika, hati nari obutabaho, omushana gutakazimire, kuhenda omubazi ogu naabaire nkomirwemu kumara obwire buraingwa.

Kwonka obwire obu naamazire ninteekateeka aha ky’okugamba, bukaba buri obw’okufa. Akaiha tomahawk ye aha meeza, yaakyebera omutwe gwayo kumara akaanya kakye, reero yaagikwata aha mushana, akanwa ke kari aha mukono, yaafuka ebicu bihango by’omwika gw’etaabe. Akaanya kakye akakuratsireho omushana gukazima, kandi omunyamaishwa ogu ow’omu ishwa, tomahawk ahagati y’ameino ge, akaguruka yaaza omu kitanda naanye. Nkeeshongora, nkaba ntarikubaasa kukyemera hati; kandi arikuha okugurukaguruka kw’ahonaaho okw’okutangaara akatandika kunhurira.

N’okushekyerera ekintu, ntarikumanya eki, nkamugurukira aha kisiika, reero naamuroga, weena nari eki yaakuba ari, ngu ahuname, kandi reka nyimukye n’okugaruka kumurikira etabaaza. Kwonka okugarukamu kwe okw’omu kanwa kukanshemeza ahonaaho ngu akaba atarikwetegyereza gye amakuru gangye.

“N’oha-e kukuteganisa?”—aha muheru akagira—“iwe torikugamba-e, dam-me, ninyita-e.” Kandi okugira atyo tomahawk erimu omushana ekatandika kutunguuka ahariinye omu mwirima.

“Mukama wa itaka, ahabwa Ruhanga, Peter Coffin!” nkagamba n’eiraka rihango I. “Mukama wa itaka! Reeba! Eshandukye! Bamaraika! munjune!”

"Gamba-e! gamba-ee n'oha-ee kuba, nari dam-me, nyita-e!" kandi omunya-nyama akagaruka yaagurukaguruka, obwo okutunguuka kwe okurikutiinisa okwa tomahawk kurikujanjaaza evu y’etaabe erikwotsya aha rubaju rwangye kuhitsya obu naateekateekire ngu ekijwaro kyangye kya kitaani nikiija kukwatwa omuriro. Kwonka eiguru rebare, omu kaanya ako mukama wa nju akaija omu kishengye omushana omu ngaro, kandi n’okuguruka aha kitanda nkairuka naamuhikaho.

Akagira ati: “Otatiina hati,” kandi yaagaruka yaamwenya, “Queequeg hanu tarikubaasa kukora kubi n’eishokye ry’omutwe gwawe.”

Nkagamba n’eiraka rihango nti: “Reka okusheka, kandi ahabw’enki otagambiire ngu ogwo muteezi w’enkoni y’omuriro ogutaraara akaba ari omunyabuzaare?”

“Nkaba ninteekateeka ngu nimukimanya;—tindabagambiire, akaba ari omushuubuzi w’emitwe okwetoorora orurembo?—kwonka mugarukye muhindure flukes muze kubyama. Queequeg, reeba aha—nonshaba, nyowe sabbee—iwe omushaija ogu noobyama—iwe sabbee?”

“Me sabbee bingi”—Queequeg akagamba n’okugurukaguruka, arikufukaana n’omupiira gwe kandi yaashutama omu kitanda.

Akongyeraho ati: “You gettee in,” arikunyoreka omu ngaro n’ekimotoka kye ekya tomahawk, kandi yaateera emyenda aha rubaju rumwe. Buzima eki akakikora omu muringo gw’obuntu gwonka kureka buzima omu muringo gw’embabazi n’ogw’okuhwera. Nkemerera ndikumureeba akaanya kakye. Ahabw’okutaho ebishushani bye byona akaba ari omunyamaishwa oshemeire kandi orikureebeka gye. Niki ekirikureetaho okuyomba oku kwona oku mbaire ninkora, nkateekateeka omu mutima gwangye—omushaija n’omuntu nk’oku ndi: aine enshonga nyingi z’okuntiina, nk’oku nyine kumutiina. Nikirungi kubyama n’omunyabuzaare orikurya abantu okukira omukristaayo otamiire.

"Mukama w'enju," nkagira, "mugambire ngu abike tomahawk ye aho, nainga payipu, nainga eki orikukyeta kyona; mugambire arekye kunywa etaabe, omubugufu, kandi ndaza kuhinduka nawe. Kwonka tindikukunda okugira omushaija orikunywa etaabe omukitanda naanye. Ni eky'akabi. Oyihireho ekyo, tindikugira inshuwarensi."

Eki ku kyagambirwe Queequeg, ahonaaho akakihikiiriza, kandi yaagaruka yanmanyisa omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa ngu nshutame omu kitanda—akazingazinga aha rubaju rumwe nk’okugira ngu—“tindikwija kukwata aha kuguru kwaimwe.”

“Oraare gye, mukama w’enju,” nkamugira, “orabaasa kuzayo.”

Nkahinduka, kandi tinkagwejegyeire gye nk’oku kiri omu magara gangye.
