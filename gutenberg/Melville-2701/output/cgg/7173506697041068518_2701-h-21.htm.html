"Ninkimanya, mugurusi; ebi bihimba nibiija kukwatira hamwe nka glue kuruga omu magufa g'abaitsi agashazirwe. Rahuka! oncumbire eicumu. Kandi obanze oncumbire, enkoni ikumi n'ebiri ahabw'omugoye gwayo; reero oteere omuyaga, kandi ohindure, kandi oteere enyundo ezi ikumi n'ebiri hamwe nk'ehuuzi n'enyuguta z'omurunga gw'okukurura."

Aha muheru emigoye ikumi n’ebiri ku yaaherize kukorwa, Ahabu akagigyezaho, emwe ahari emwe, arikugizingira, n’omukono gwe wenka, okwehinguririza omurunga gw’ekyoma muraingwa kandi oguremeire. “Ekizibu!” okwanga eky’aha muheru. “Okore ekyo kandi, Perth.”

Eki kyakorwa, Perth akaba ari haihi kutandika kushemeza ikumi na babiri kuba kimwe, obu Ahabu yaaguma omukono gwe, kandi yaagira ngu naija kushemeza ekyoma kye. Nk’oku, obwo, n’emikono erikuguma neehuuta, yaabaire naateera enyundo aha nshonda zoona, Perth arikumuhitsyaho emiti erikumurinkana, emwe bwanyima y’endiijo, kandi ekyoma ekigumire kirikuteera omuriro gwakyo ogugoroire munonga, Omuparsee akaba ahinguraho atarikugamba, kandi arikufukama aha mutwe gwe arikureeba omuriro, arikushusha nk’orikushaba omukyeeno gumwe nari emigisha emwe. Kwonka, Ahabu ku yaabaire naareeba ahaiguru, akashekyerera aha rubaju.

“Ekyo kibunu kya lucifers kirikwetantarayo ahabw’enki?” Stubb akagamba n’eiraka rihango, arikureeba ari aha rubaju rw’enyanja. “Ogwo Parsee naanuuka omuriro nk’omufuka; kandi naagunuuka we wenka, nk’ekiyungu ky’esaano y’omusyo erikwotsya.”

Aha muheru omugoye, omu nkoni emwe ehikire, gukatunga obutagatsi bwagwo obw’aha muheru; kandi ku Perth, okugikyendeeza, yaagiteeka yoona erikweshushaniriza omu nshaho y’amaizi eyabaire eri haihi, omuyaga ogurikwotsya gukateera aha maisho ga Ahabu agafukamire.

“Noija kunteera akamanyiso, Perth?” okunyiganyiga kumara akaanya kakye ahabw’obusaasi; “mbaire ninkora ekyoma kyangye ky’okutaho akamanyiso, mbwenu?”

"Shaba Ruhanga, kitari ekyo; kwonka nyine eki ndikutiina, Kapiteeni Ahabu. Egi nkoni ti y'enyanja erikwera?"

"Ahabw'omuzimu omweru! Kwonka hati ahabw'amabaare; oine kwekorera, mushaija. Hati hariho enkoni zangye—ezirikukirayo oburungi omu byoma; hanu, kandi okore amabaare gashasha nk'empeta z'omu nyanja y'orubaare."

Okumara akaanya kakye, omuheisi omugurusi akareeba enkoni nk’orikuza kufa atazikoresa.

“Bitware, mushaija, tindibyetaaga, ahabw’okuba hati tindikushara, tindikurya, nari kushaba okuhitsya—kureka hanu—kukora!”

Ekyoma ekyo kikakorwa aha muheru omu muringo gw’obusyo, kandi kikashemezibwa Perth aha rubaju rw’ekyoma, ekyoma ekyo kikarahuka kworeka omuheru gw’ekyoma; kandi omuheisi ku yaabaire ari haihi kuha ebibaare ebyo obutagatsi bwabyo eby’aha muheru, atakabiteire, akarira Ahabu ngu ate ekikopo ky’amaizi haihi.

"Ngaaha, ngaaha—tihariho maizi g'ekyo; ninyenda ag'ekiniga ky'okufa eky'amazima. Ahoy, aho! Tashtego, Queequeg, Daggoo! Nimugira ki, abapagani! Nimuza kumpa eshagama nyingi nk'erikubaasa kushweka orubaare oru?" okugikwata ahaiguru. Ekirundo ky’ebicupuri by’omwirima kikagarukamu kiti, Eego. Ebicweka bishatu bikakorwa omu nyama y’abapagani, reero amabaare ga White Whale gakakyeberwa.

“Ego otarikubatizibwa omu iziina rya patris, sed omu iziina rya diabolo!” Ahabu akaborooga omu muringo gw’okushekyerera, obwo ekyoma ky’akabi kirikurya eshagama y’okubatizibwa omu muringo gw’okwotsya.

Hati, arikuteerana emiti y’okusigazaho kuruga ahansi, kandi akatooranaho emwe ey’omuti gwa hickory, n’ekishaka ekikiriyo nikigiteekamu, Ahabu akateera omuheru aha nshonda y’ekyoma. Coil y'omugoye musya gw'okukurura ekarugaho yaazingurwa, kandi fathom ezimwe zaayo zitwarwa aha windlass, kandi zigororwa kuhika aha kuteganisibwa kwingi. Akaginyiganyiga ekigyere kye, okuhitsya obu omurunga gwahuriire nk’orukoba rw’endongo, reero yaagufukama n’ekihika, kandi akareeba ngu tihaine ahu gurikuteera, akagira ati, “Nikirungi, kandi hati ahabw’okukwaata.”

Aha rubaju rumwe omurunga gukaba gutarimu nshonda zoona, kandi oburungo obujanjaire obutaine rubaju bwona bukaba oburukire kandi burukwa okwehinguririza ekitebe ky’ekyoma; omuti ogwo gukaba gugyemeserezibwa munonga kuza ahaiguru omu nshonda; okuruga aha muheru gwa hansi omugoye gukaba gukuratirwa ahagati y’oburaingwa bw’omuti, kandi guhamirwe gye gutyo, n’okuteerana kw’emirunga. Eki kyakorwa, omuti, ekyoma, n’omugoye—nk’Ebintu Bishatu—bikaguma bitataanisiibwe, kandi Ahabu akagyendera omu mutima aine eky’okurwanisa; eiraka ry’okuguru kwe okw’amahembe g’entare, n’eiraka ry’omuti gw’omuti gwa hickory, byombi birikuteera nk’ebiina ahari buri mbaaho. Kwonka atakataahire omu kaju ke, hakahurirwa eiraka ry’omushana, eritari ry’obuhangwa, eririkushekyerera kimwe kya kabiri, kwonka eririkushaasha munonga.
