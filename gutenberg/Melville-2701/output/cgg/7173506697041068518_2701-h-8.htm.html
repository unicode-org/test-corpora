Eki ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa white whale kyabaire kiri ahari Ahabu, kigambirweho; eki, obumwe, yaabaire ari ahariinye, nk’oku kiri hati kitagambirwe.

Obweru bw’enyanja y’enyanja nibwo bwantiiniise okukira byona. Kwonka shi nimbaasa kugira nta amatsiko g’okweshoboorora aha; kandi nabwo, omu muringo gumwe ogutarikureebwa gye, ogutarikwetegyerezibwa, kweshoboorora nyine, kitari ekyo eshuura ezi zoona nizibaasa kuba zitaine mugasho.

Kandi ku turikuteekateeka aha nteekateeka egyo endiijo ey’abanyabwengye b’obuhangwa, ngu erangi ezindi zoona ez’omu nsi—buri kishushani ky’ekitiinisa nari eky’okukunda—erangi nungi z’eiguru n’ebibira ebirikurenga omu izooba; eego, n'ebijwaro by'ebinyonyi ebiine ezaabu, n'amatama g'ebinyonyi ag'abaishiki bato; ebi byona n’obugobya obutarikwetegyerezibwa, obutarikuzaarwa omu bintu buzima, kureka oburikuteebwaho kuruga aheeru yonka; n’ahabw’ekyo Obuhangwa bwona obuhinduziibwe ruhanga nibusiiga buzima nk’omukazi maraaya, ou okushemezibwa kwe kutarikushweka kintu kyona kureka enju ya charnel eri omunda; kandi ku turikugumizamu, kandi tukateekateeka ngu ekirungo ky’amazima ekirikurugamu buri kimwe aha rangi ze, omusingye muhango gw’omushana, ebiro byona niguguma guri ogw’omweru nari gutaine rangi omurigwo, kandi gwaba nigukora hatariho nkora aha kintu, gukaba nigwija kukwata aha bintu byona, nangwa n’ebimuri bya tulips n’ebimuri, n’erangi yaakyo ey’omutaano omu maisho g’ensi yoona egi a liper all us—ponde all us; kandi nk’abagyenyi abagyendereire omuri Lapland, abarikwanga kujwara ebirahuri by’erangi n’eby’erangi aha maisho gaabo, nikwo omukaafiiri w’obusaasi arikwereeba nk’omuhumire aha kijwaro ky’ekiijutsyo eky’omweru ekirikuzingira amatsiko goona agamwehinguririize. Kandi omu bintu ebi byona ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Albino whale nikyo kyabaire kiri akamanyiso. Mbwenu shi nimutangaara ahabw'okuhiiga kw'omuriro?
