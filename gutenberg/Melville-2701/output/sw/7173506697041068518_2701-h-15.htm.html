SURA YA 74. Kichwa cha Nyangumi wa Manii—Maoni Tofauti.

Hapa, sasa, kuna nyangumi wawili wakubwa, wakiweka vichwa vyao pamoja; tuungane nao, na tuweke chetu pamoja.

Kati ya aina kuu za leviathan za folio, Nyangumi wa Manii na Nyangumi wa Kulia ndio wanaovutia zaidi. Ni nyangumi pekee wanaowindwa mara kwa mara na mwanadamu. Kwa Nantucketer, wanawasilisha tofauti mbili za aina zote zinazojulikana za nyangumi. Kwa vile tofauti ya nje kati yao inaonekana hasa katika vichwa vyao; na kama mkuu wa kila ni wakati huu kunyongwa kutoka upande Pequod ya; na jinsi tunavyoweza kwenda kwa uhuru kutoka kwa moja hadi nyingine, kwa kuvuka tu sitaha: - wapi, ningependa kujua, je, utapata nafasi nzuri ya kusoma cetology ya vitendo kuliko hapa?

Katika nafasi ya kwanza, unapigwa na tofauti ya jumla kati ya vichwa hivi. Zote mbili ni kubwa vya kutosha katika dhamiri zote; lakini kuna ulinganifu fulani wa hisabati katika Nyangumi wa Manii ambao Nyangumi wa kulia anaukosa kwa masikitiko. Kuna tabia zaidi katika kichwa cha Nyangumi wa Manii. Unapoitazama, kwa hiari yako unajitolea ukuu mkuu kwake, katika hatua ya kueneza hadhi. Katika hali ya sasa, pia, heshima hii inaongezwa na rangi ya pilipili na chumvi ya kichwa chake kwenye kilele, na kutoa ishara ya uzee na uzoefu mkubwa. Kwa ufupi, yeye ndiye yule ambaye wavuvi wanamwita kitaalamu “nyangumi mwenye kichwa kijivu.”

Acheni sasa tuone kile ambacho hakifanani katika vichwa hivi—yaani, viungo viwili muhimu zaidi, jicho na sikio. Mbali nyuma upande wa kichwa, na chini chini, karibu na pembe ya taya aidha nyangumi, kama narrowly search, utakuwa mwisho kuona jicho lashless, ambayo ungependa dhana kuwa jicho la mwana punda; hivyo nje ya uwiano wote ni kwa ukubwa wa kichwa.

Sasa, kutokana na hali hii ya pekee ya kando ya macho ya nyangumi, ni wazi kwamba hawezi kamwe kuona kitu kilicho mbele kabisa, zaidi ya vile anavyoweza kuona astern hasa. Kwa neno, nafasi ya macho ya nyangumi inafanana na masikio ya mtu; na unaweza kujionea mwenyewe jinsi mambo yatakavyokuwa kwako, je, ulichunguza vitu kwa njia ya masikio yako. Ungepata kwamba ungeweza kuamuru tu digrii thelathini za maono kabla ya mstari wa moja kwa moja wa maono; na karibu thelathini zaidi nyuma yake. Ikiwa adui yako mkali angekuwa akienda moja kwa moja kwako, na panga likiwa limeinuliwa mchana, hungeweza kumwona, kama angekuwa anakuibia kwa nyuma. Kwa neno moja, ungekuwa na migongo miwili, kwa kusema; lakini, wakati huo huo, pia, pande mbili (mbele ya upande): kwa maana ni nini kinachofanya mbele ya mtu - ni nini, kwa kweli, lakini macho yake?

Zaidi ya hayo, ingawa katika wanyama wengine wengi ambao ninaweza kufikiria sasa, macho yamepandwa kwa njia isiyoonekana ili kuchanganya nguvu zao za kuona, ili kutoa picha moja na si mbili kwenye ubongo; nafasi ya pekee ya macho ya nyangumi, effectually kugawanywa kama wao ni kwa miguu ya ujazo wengi wa kichwa imara, ambayo minara kati yao kama mlima mkubwa kutenganisha maziwa mawili katika mabonde; hii, bila shaka, lazima itenganishe kabisa hisia ambazo kila kiungo huru hutoa. Nyangumi, kwa hiyo, lazima aone picha moja tofauti upande huu, na picha nyingine tofauti upande ule; wakati wote kati yao lazima giza zito na si kitu kwake. Mwanadamu, kwa kweli, anaweza kusemekana kutazama ulimwengu kutoka kwa sanduku la mlinzi na sashi mbili zilizounganishwa kwa dirisha lake. Lakini pamoja na nyangumi, sashes hizi mbili zimeingizwa tofauti, na kufanya madirisha mawili tofauti, lakini kwa huzuni huharibu mtazamo. Upekee huu wa macho ya nyangumi ni jambo la kukumbuka daima katika uvuvi; na kukumbukwa na msomaji katika baadhi ya matukio yanayofuata.

Swali la kudadisi na la kutatanisha zaidi linaweza kuanzishwa kuhusu jambo hili la kuona kama kugusa Leviathan. Lakini lazima niridhike na kidokezo. Ilimradi macho ya mtu yamefumbuka kwenye nuru, kitendo cha kuona ni cha kujitolea; yaani, hawezi basi kusaidia kuona vitu vyovyote vilivyo mbele yake. Hata hivyo, uzoefu wa mtu ye yote utamfundisha, kwamba ingawa anaweza kufagia mambo bila kubagua kwa mtazamo mmoja, haiwezekani kabisa kwake, kwa uangalifu, na kabisa, kuchunguza mambo yoyote mawili—hata yawe makubwa au madogo jinsi gani—kwa moja. na wakati huo huo wa wakati; usijali ikiwa watalala upande kwa upande na kugusana. Lakini ikiwa sasa unakuja kutenganisha vitu hivi viwili, na kuzunguka kila moja kwa duara la giza kuu; basi, ili kuona mmoja wao, kwa namna ambayo italeta mawazo yako juu yake, mwingine atatengwa kabisa na ufahamu wako wa kisasa. Ni jinsi gani, basi, na nyangumi? Kweli, macho yake yote mawili, yenyewe, lazima yatende wakati huo huo; lakini je, ubongo wake ni mpana zaidi, unaochanganya, na wa hila zaidi kuliko wa mwanadamu, hivi kwamba anaweza kuchunguza kwa makini matazamio mawili tofauti, moja upande mmoja wake, na mwingine upande tofauti kabisa? Ikiwa anaweza, basi ni jambo la kustaajabisha ndani yake, kana kwamba mtu aliweza kwa wakati mmoja kupitia maonyesho ya matatizo mawili tofauti katika Euclid. Wala, kuchunguzwa kikamilifu, hakuna ulinganifu wowote katika ulinganisho huu.

Inaweza kuwa lakini whim wavivu, lakini imekuwa daima walionekana kwangu, kwamba vacillations ajabu ya harakati visas na baadhi ya nyangumi wakati wanakabiliwa na boti tatu au nne; woga na dhima ya kutisha queer, hivyo kawaida kwa nyangumi vile; Nadhani haya yote kwa njia isiyo ya moja kwa moja yanatokana na mkanganyiko usio na msaada wa hiari, ambapo nguvu zao za maono zilizogawanyika na kinyume cha diametrically lazima ziwahusishe.

Lakini sikio la nyangumi limejaa udadisi kama jicho. Ikiwa wewe ni mgeni kabisa kwa mbio zao, unaweza kuwinda juu ya vichwa hivi viwili kwa masaa, na usigundue chombo hicho. Sikio halina jani la nje chochote; na ndani ya shimo yenyewe huwezi kuingiza quill, kwa hivyo ni dakika ya kushangaza. Imewekwa nyuma ya jicho kidogo. Kuhusiana na masikio yao, tofauti hii muhimu inapaswa kuzingatiwa kati ya nyangumi wa manii na kulia. Wakati sikio la kwanza lina ufunguzi wa nje, ule wa mwisho umefunikwa kabisa na sawasawa juu ya utando, ili usionekane kabisa kutoka nje.

Je, si ajabu kwamba kiumbe mkubwa sana kama nyangumi anapaswa kuona ulimwengu kupitia jicho dogo sana, na kusikia ngurumo kupitia sikio ambalo ni dogo kuliko la sungura? Lakini ikiwa macho yake yalikuwa mapana kama lenzi ya darubini kubwa ya Herschel; na masikio yake ni meusi kama kumbi za makanisa; hiyo ingemfanya awe na macho tena, au awe mkali wa kusikia? Sivyo kabisa.—Kwa nini basi unajaribu “kupanua” akili yako? Subtilize yake.

Hebu sasa na chochote levers na mvuke-injini tuna karibu, cant juu ya kichwa nyangumi manii, kwamba inaweza kulala chini juu; kisha, ukipanda kwa ngazi hadi kileleni, chungulia mdomoni; na kama si kwamba mwili sasa umetenganishwa kabisa nao, kwa taa tungeweza kushuka kwenye Pango kubwa la Kentucky Mammoth la tumbo lake. Lakini hebu tushikilie hapa kwa jino hili, na tuangalie kuhusu sisi tulipo. Ni kinywa kizuri sana na chenye sura safi kama nini! kutoka sakafu hadi dari, iliyopangwa, au tuseme iliyopakwa karatasi na utando mweupe unaometa, unaometa kama satin za arusi.

Lakini njoo nje sasa, na uangalie taya hii ya ajabu ya chini, ambayo inaonekana kama kifuniko kirefu chembamba cha sanduku kubwa la ugoro, na bawaba upande mmoja, badala ya upande mmoja. Ikiwa utaifuta, ili kuipata juu, na kufichua safu zake za meno, inaonekana kama portcullis kali; na vile, ole! inathibitisha kwa watu wengi maskini katika uvuvi, ambao spikes hizi huanguka kwa nguvu ya kupachika. Lakini jambo la kuogofya zaidi ni kuona, wakati wa kuzama baharini, unaona nyangumi fulani mwenye majimaji, akielea pale amesimamishwa, na taya yake ya ajabu, yenye urefu wa futi kumi na tano, akining'inia chini moja kwa moja kwenye pembe za kulia na mwili wake, kwa ulimwengu kama jib-boom ya meli. Nyangumi huyu hajafa; amekata tamaa tu; nje ya aina, labda; hypochondriamu; na hivyo supine, kwamba hinges ya taya yake na walishirikiana, na kumwacha huko katika kwamba ungainly aina ya hatma, aibu kwa kabila yake yote, ambao lazima, bila shaka, imprecate lock-taya juu yake.

Katika hali nyingi taya hii ya chini—ikiwa imefunguliwa kwa urahisi na msanii anayefanya mazoezi—hutolewa na kuinuliwa juu ya sitaha kwa madhumuni ya kung’oa meno ya tembo, na kutoa ugavi wa ule mfupa mgumu wa nyangumi ambao wavuvi hutengeneza kila aina ya vitu vya kuvutia. ikiwa ni pamoja na vijiti, mwavuli-hifadhi, na vipini kwa viboko vya kupanda.

Kwa pandisho refu, lililochoka, taya inaburutwa kwenye ubao, kana kwamba ni nanga; na wakati ufaao ufikapo—siku chache baada ya kazi nyingine—Queequeg, Daggoo, na Tashtego, wakiwa madaktari wa meno waliobobea, wanaanza kuchora meno. Kwa nia ya kukata-jembe, Queequeg lances ufizi; kisha taya ni lashed chini kwa ringbolts, na kukabiliana na kuwa wizi kutoka juu, wao Drag nje meno haya, kama ng'ombe Michigan Drag stumps ya mialoni ya zamani nje ya ardhi ya misitu ya mwitu. Taya hukatwa kwa msumeno baadaye, na kurundikwa kama viungio vya kujenga nyumba.





SURA YA 75. Kichwa cha Kulia cha Nyangumi—Mtazamo Unaotofautiana.

Kuvuka sitaha, hebu sasa tuangalie vizuri kwa muda mrefu kichwa cha Nyangumi wa Kulia.

Kama katika umbo la jumla kichwa cha nyangumi cha manii kinaweza kulinganishwa na gari la vita la Kirumi (haswa mbele, ambalo lina mviringo kwa upana); kwa hivyo, kwa mtazamo mpana, kichwa cha Nyangumi wa kulia kinafanana sana na kiatu kikubwa cha galliot-toed. Miaka mia mbili iliyopita msafiri mzee wa Uholanzi alifananisha umbo lake na la mwisho la fundi viatu. Na katika mwisho huo huo au kiatu, yule mwanamke mzee wa hadithi ya kitalu, pamoja na watoto wachanga, anaweza kukaa kwa raha, yeye na uzao wake wote.

Lakini unapokaribia kichwa hiki kikuu huanza kuchukua vipengele tofauti, kulingana na mtazamo wako. Ukisimama juu ya kilele chake na kutazama mikunjo hii miwili yenye umbo la F, ungechukua kichwa kizima kama viol kubwa ya besi, na miindo hii, vijitundu kwenye ubao wake wa kutoa sauti. Kisha, tena, ukikazia jicho lako juu ya mkunjo huu wa ajabu, uliochongwa, na kama sega juu ya misa hiyo—kitu hiki cha kijani kibichi, chenye mahaba, ambacho Wagreenland hukiita “taji,” na wavuvi wa Kusini “bonneti” ya Nyangumi wa kulia; ukikazia macho tu juu ya hili, ungechukua kichwa kwa shina la mwaloni mkubwa, na kiota cha ndege kwenye gongo lake. Kwa vyovyote vile, unapowatazama kaa hao wanaoishi hapa kwenye boneti hii, wazo kama hilo litakuwa karibu kukutokea; isipokuwa, kwa kweli, dhana yako imedhamiriwa na neno la kitaalamu "taji" ambalo pia limetolewa juu yake; katika hali ambayo utavutiwa sana na kufikiria jinsi mnyama huyu mkubwa ni mfalme wa baharini mwenye kilemba, ambaye taji yake ya kijani kibichi imewekwa pamoja kwa ajili yake kwa namna hii ya ajabu. Lakini kama nyangumi huyu atakuwa mfalme, yeye ni mtu mwenye sura mbaya sana ili kupamba taji. Angalia huo mdomo wa chini unaoning'inia! kuna uchungu mkubwa kama nini! sulk na pout, kwa kipimo cha seremala, kama urefu wa futi ishirini na kina cha futi tano; siki na pout ambayo itakuletea takriban galoni 500 za mafuta na zaidi.

Huruma kubwa, sasa, kwamba nyangumi hii bahati mbaya inapaswa kuwa hare-lipped. Mpasuko ni kama futi moja kuvuka. Pengine mama wakati wa muda muhimu alikuwa akisafiri kwa meli chini ya pwani ya Peru, wakati matetemeko ya ardhi yalisababisha ufuo wa baharini. Juu ya mdomo huu, kama juu ya kizingiti kinachoteleza, sasa tunateleza kwenye mdomo. Juu ya neno langu walikuwa katika Mackinaw, mimi lazima kuchukua hii kuwa ndani ya Wigwam Hindi. Bwana mwema! hii ndiyo njia aliyoiendea Yona? Paa hiyo ina urefu wa futi kumi na mbili, na inaenda kwa pembe kali, kana kwamba kuna nguzo ya kawaida hapo; wakati pande hizi zenye mbavu, zenye upinde, zenye manyoya, zinatuletea zile slats za ajabu, nusu wima, zenye umbo la scimetar za nyangumi, sema mia tatu kwa upande, ambayo kulingana na sehemu ya juu ya kichwa au mfupa wa taji, huunda vipofu hivyo vya Venetian ambavyo. zimetajwa mahali pengine kwa harakaharaka. Kingo za mifupa hii zimeunganishwa na nyuzi zenye nywele, ambazo Nyangumi wa Kulia huchuja maji, na ambaye kwa ugumu wake huhifadhi samaki wadogo, wakati mdomo wazi hupitia bahari ya brit katika wakati wa kulisha. Katika sehemu za kati za upofu wa mifupa, zinavyosimama katika mpangilio wao wa asili, kuna alama fulani za ajabu, mikunjo, mashimo, na matuta, ambapo baadhi ya nyangumi huhesabu umri wa kiumbe huyo, kama umri wa mwaloni kwa pete zake za mviringo. Ingawa uhakika wa kigezo hiki uko mbali na kudhihirishwa, bado una harufu ya uwezekano wa kilinganishi. Kwa vyovyote vile, tukikubali, ni lazima tupe umri mkubwa zaidi kwa Nyangumi wa Kulia kuliko itakavyoonekana kuwa sawa.

Hapo zamani za kale, inaonekana kumeshinda matamanio ya kuvutia zaidi kuhusu vipofu hivi. Msafiri mmoja katika Purchas anaziita “ndevu” za kustaajabisha zilizo ndani ya mdomo wa nyangumi;* mwingine, “nyumbu za nguruwe”; mzee wa tatu katika Hackluyt anatumia lugha ya kifahari ifuatayo: “Kuna mapezi mia mbili na hamsini hivi yanayokua kila upande wa kipande chake cha juu , ambayo yanapinda juu ya ulimi wake kila upande wa mdomo wake.”

Kabla ya hili, lazima uwe umeona wazi ukweli wa kile nilichoanza nacho - kwamba Nyangumi wa Manii na Nyangumi wa Kulia wana vichwa tofauti kabisa. Tazama mwisho wako, sasa, juu ya vichwa hivi vya heshima vilivyofunikwa, wakati bado wamelala pamoja; kwa maana hivi karibuni mtu atazama, bila kuandikwa, baharini; nyingine haitachukua muda mrefu sana kufuata.

Kabla ya kuacha, kwa nonce, kichwa Whale manii, ningependa wewe, kama fiziolojia busara, tu-hasa remark mbele yake kipengele, katika mkusanyiko wake wote Kuunganishwa. Unaona kwamba katika nafasi ya kawaida ya kuogelea ya Nyangumi wa Manii, sehemu ya mbele ya kichwa chake inatoa ndege karibu kabisa wima kwa maji; unaona kwamba sehemu ya chini ya sehemu hiyo ya mbele inatelemka kwa kiasi kikubwa kuelekea nyuma, ili kutoa nafasi zaidi kwa tundu refu ambalo hupokea taya ya chini kama boom; unaona kuwa mdomo uko chini ya kichwa kabisa, kwa njia ile ile, kwa kweli, kana kwamba mdomo wako ulikuwa chini ya kidevu chako. Aidha unaona kwamba nyangumi hana pua ya nje; na kwamba kile pua anacho—shimo lake—liko juu ya kichwa chake; unaona kwamba macho na masikio yake yapo kando ya kichwa chake, karibu theluthi moja ya urefu wake wote kutoka mbele. Kwa hivyo, lazima sasa umegundua kuwa sehemu ya mbele ya kichwa cha Nyangumi wa Manii ni ukuta uliokufa, usio na kipofu, usio na chombo kimoja au umaarufu wa zabuni wa aina yoyote. Zaidi ya hayo, sasa unapaswa kuzingatia kwamba tu katika sehemu iliyokithiri, ya chini, ya nyuma ya sehemu ya mbele ya kichwa, kuna sehemu ndogo ya mfupa; na sio mpaka ufikie futi ishirini kutoka kwenye paji la uso ndipo ufikie ukuaji kamili wa fuvu. Hatimaye, ingawa, kama itakavyofunuliwa hivi karibuni, yaliyomo ndani yake yanajumuisha mafuta dhaifu zaidi; bado, sasa utafahamishwa kuhusu asili ya dutu ambayo huwekeza nguvu zote hizo dhahiri. Katika sehemu fulani iliyopita nilikuelezea jinsi blubber inavyofunika mwili wa nyangumi, kama kaka hufunika chungwa. Vivyo hivyo kwa kichwa; lakini kwa tofauti hii: kuhusu kichwa bahasha hii, ingawa si nene sana, ni ya ukakamavu usio na mfupa, usioweza kukadiriwa na mwanaume yeyote ambaye hajaishughulikia. Chusa kali zaidi iliyochongoka, mkuki mkali zaidi unaotolewa na mkono wenye nguvu zaidi wa mwanadamu, hujirudia kwa nguvu kutoka humo. CHAPTER 79. The Prairie.

To scan the lines of his face, or feel the bumps on the head of this Leviathan; this is a thing which no Physiognomist or Phrenologist has as yet undertaken. Such an enterprise would seem almost as hopeful as for Lavater to have scrutinized the wrinkles on the Rock of Gibraltar, or for Gall to have mounted a ladder and manipulated the Dome of the Pantheon. Still, in that famous work of his, Lavater not only treats of the various faces of men, but also attentively studies the faces of horses, birds, serpents, and fish; and dwells in detail upon the modifications of expression discernible therein. Nor have Gall and his disciple Spurzheim failed to throw out some hints touching the phrenological characteristics of other beings than man. Therefore, though I am but ill qualified for a pioneer, in the application of these two semi-sciences to the whale, I will do my endeavor. I try all things; I achieve what I can.

Physiognomically regarded, the Sperm Whale is an anomalous creature. He has no proper nose. And since the nose is the central and most conspicuous of the features; and since it perhaps most modifies and finally controls their combined expression; hence it would seem that its entire absence, as an external appendage, must very largely affect the countenance of the whale. For as in landscape gardening, a spire, cupola, monument, or tower of some sort, is deemed almost indispensable to the completion of the scene; so no face can be physiognomically in keeping without the elevated open-work belfry of the nose. Dash the nose from Phidias's marble Jove, and what a sorry remainder! Nevertheless, Leviathan is of so mighty a magnitude, all his proportions are so stately, that the same deficiency which in the sculptured Jove were hideous, in him is no blemish at all. Nay, it is an added grandeur. A nose to the whale would have been impertinent. As on your physiognomical voyage you sail round his vast head in your jolly-boat, your noble conceptions of him are never insulted by the reflection that he has a nose to be pulled. A pestilent conceit, which so often will insist upon obtruding even when beholding the mightiest royal beadle on his throne.

In some particulars, perhaps the most imposing physiognomical view to be had of the Sperm Whale, is that of the full front of his head. This aspect is sublime.

In thought, a fine human brow is like the East when troubled with the morning. In the repose of the pasture, the curled brow of the bull has a touch of the grand in it. Pushing heavy cannon up mountain defiles, the elephant's brow is majestic. Human or animal, the mystical brow is as that great golden seal affixed by the German emperors to their decrees. It signifies—“God: done this day by my hand.” But in most creatures, nay in man himself, very often the brow is but a mere strip of alpine land lying along the snow line. Few are the foreheads which like Shakespeare's or Melancthon's rise so high, and descend so low, that the eyes themselves seem clear, eternal, tideless mountain lakes; and all above them in the forehead's wrinkles, you seem to track the antlered thoughts descending there to drink, as the Highland hunters track the snow prints of the deer. But in the great Sperm Whale, this high and mighty god-like dignity inherent in the brow is so immensely amplified, that gazing on it, in that full front view, you feel the Deity and the dread powers more forcibly than in beholding any other object in living nature. For you see no one point precisely; not one distinct feature is revealed; no nose, eyes, ears, or mouth; no face; he has none, proper; nothing but that one broad firmament of a forehead, pleated with riddles; dumbly lowering with the doom of boats, and ships, and men. Nor, in profile, does this wondrous brow diminish; though that way viewed its grandeur does not domineer upon you so. In profile, you plainly perceive that horizontal, semi-crescentic depression in the forehead's middle, which, in man, is Lavater's mark of genius.

But how? Genius in the Sperm Whale? Has the Sperm Whale ever written a book, spoken a speech? No, his great genius is declared in his doing nothing particular to prove it. It is moreover declared in his pyramidical silence. And this reminds me that had the great Sperm Whale been known to the young Orient World, he would have been deified by their child-magian thoughts. They deified the crocodile of the Nile, because the crocodile is tongueless; and the Sperm Whale has no tongue, or at least it is so exceedingly small, as to be incapable of protrusion. If hereafter any highly cultured, poetical nation shall lure back to their birth-right, the merry May-day gods of old; and livingly enthrone them again in the now egotistical sky; in the now unhaunted hill; then be sure, exalted to Jove's high seat, the great Sperm Whale shall lord it.

Champollion deciphered the wrinkled granite hieroglyphics. But there is no Champollion to decipher the Egypt of every man's and every being's face. Physiognomy, like every other human science, is but a passing fable. If then, Sir William Jones, who read in thirty languages, could not read the simplest peasant's face in its profounder and more subtle meanings, how may unlettered Ishmael hope to read the awful Chaldee of the Sperm Whale's brow? I but put that brow before you. Read it if you can.





CHAPTER 80. The Nut.

If the Sperm Whale be physiognomically a Sphinx, to the phrenologist his brain seems that geometrical circle which it is impossible to square.

In the full-grown creature the skull will measure at least twenty feet in length. Unhinge the lower jaw, and the side view of this skull is as the side of a moderately inclined plane resting throughout on a level base. But in life—as we have elsewhere seen—this inclined plane is angularly filled up, and almost squared by the enormous superincumbent mass of the junk and sperm. At the high end the skull forms a crater to bed that part of the mass; while under the long floor of this crater—in another cavity seldom exceeding ten inches in length and as many in depth—reposes the mere handful of this monster's brain. The brain is at least twenty feet from his apparent forehead in life; it is hidden away behind its vast outworks, like the innermost citadel within the amplified fortifications of Quebec. So like a choice casket is it secreted in him, that I have known some whalemen who peremptorily deny that the Sperm Whale has any other brain than that palpable semblance of one formed by the cubic-yards of his sperm magazine. Lying in strange folds, courses, and convolutions, to their apprehensions, it seems more in keeping with the idea of his general might to regard that mystic part of him as the seat of his intelligence.

It is plain, then, that phrenologically the head of this Leviathan, in the creature's living intact state, is an entire delusion. As for his true brain, you can then see no indications of it, nor feel any. The whale, like all things that are mighty, wears a false brow to the common world.

If you unload his skull of its spermy heaps and then take a rear view of its rear end, which is the high end, you will be struck by its resemblance to the human skull, beheld in the same situation, and from the same point of view. Indeed, place this reversed skull (scaled down to the human magnitude) among a plate of men's skulls, and you would involuntarily confound it with them; and remarking the depressions on one part of its summit, in phrenological phrase you would say—This man had no self-esteem, and no veneration. And by those negations, considered along with the affirmative fact of his prodigious bulk and power, you can best form to yourself the truest, though not the most exhilarating conception of what the most exalted potency is.

But if from the comparative dimensions of the whale's proper brain, you deem it incapable of being adequately charted, then I have another idea for you. If you attentively regard almost any quadruped's spine, you will be struck with the resemblance of its vertebræ to a strung necklace of dwarfed skulls, all bearing rudimental resemblance to the skull proper. It is a German conceit, that the vertebræ are absolutely undeveloped skulls. But the curious external resemblance, I take it the Germans were not the first men to perceive. A foreign friend once pointed it out to me, in the skeleton of a foe he had slain, and with the vertebræ of which he was inlaying, in a sort of basso-relievo, the beaked prow of his canoe. Now, I consider that the phrenologists have omitted an important thing in not pushing their investigations from the cerebellum through the spinal canal. For I believe that much of a man's character will be found betokened in his backbone. I would rather feel your spine than your skull, whoever you are. A thin joist of a spine never yet upheld a full and noble soul. I rejoice in my spine, as in the firm audacious staff of that flag which I fling half out to the world.

Apply this spinal branch of phrenology to the Sperm Whale. His cranial cavity is continuous with the first neck-vertebra; and in that vertebra the bottom of the spinal canal will measure ten inches across, being eight in height, and of a triangular figure with the base downwards. As it passes through the remaining vertebræ the canal tapers in size, but for a considerable distance remains of large capacity. Now, of course, this canal is filled with much the same strangely fibrous substance—the spinal cord—as the brain; and directly communicates with the brain. And what is still more, for many feet after emerging from the brain's cavity, the spinal cord remains of an undecreasing girth, almost equal to that of the brain. Under all these circumstances, would it be unreasonable to survey and map out the whale's spine phrenologically? For, viewed in this light, the wonderful comparative smallness of his brain proper is more than compensated by the wonderful comparative magnitude of his spinal cord.

But leaving this hint to operate as it may with the phrenologists, I would merely assume the spinal theory for a moment, in reference to the Sperm Whale's hump. This august hump, if I mistake not, rises over one of the larger vertebræ, and is, therefore, in some sort, the outer convex mould of it. From its relative situation then, I should call this high hump the organ of firmness or indomitableness in the Sperm Whale. And that the great monster is indomitable, you will yet have reason to know.
