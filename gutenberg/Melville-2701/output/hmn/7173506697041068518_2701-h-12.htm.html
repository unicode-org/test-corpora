QHOV 55. Ntawm Cov Duab Zoo Nkauj ntawm Whales.

Kuv yuav tau pleev xim ntev rau koj nrog rau ib qho tuaj yeem tsis muaj canvas, ib yam dab tsi zoo li daim ntawv tiag tiag ntawm whale raws li nws tshwm sim rau lub qhov muag ntawm tus whaleman thaum nyob rau hauv nws tus kheej lub cev tag nrho lub whale yog moored nrog whale-nkoj yog li ntawd nws tuaj yeem ua ncaj ncees rau ntawm qhov ntawd. Tej zaum nws yuav tsim nyog thaum, yog li ntawd, yav tas los tshaj tawm rau cov kev xav xav txog cov duab ntawm nws uas txawm mus txog rau niaj hnub no tau tawm tsam kev ntseeg ntawm cov neeg hauv av. Nws yog lub sijhawm los teeb tsa lub ntiaj teb txoj cai hauv qhov teeb meem no, los ntawm kev ua pov thawj xws li cov duab ntawm whale txhua yam tsis raug.

Tej zaum nws yuav yog qhov tseem ceeb ntawm tag nrho cov duab delusions yuav pom ntawm cov hindoo, Egyptian, thiab Grecian sculptures. Txij li thaum lub sij hawm inventive tab sis unscrupulous thaum nyob rau hauv lub marble panellings ntawm lub tuam tsev, lub pedestals ntawm cov pej thuam, thiab nyob rau hauv cov ntaub thaiv npog, medallions, khob, thiab npib, tus ntses taub ntswg ntev tau kos nyob rau hauv scale ntawm chain- armor zoo li Saladin's, thiab ib tug kaus mom hlau zoo li lub taub hau. St. George's; puas tau txij thaum ntawd los muaj ib yam dab tsi ntawm tib yam ntawm daim ntawv tso cai prevailed, tsis tsuas yog nyob rau hauv feem ntau nrov duab ntawm whale, tab sis nyob rau hauv ntau yam kev nthuav qhia ntawm nws.

Tam sim no, los ntawm txhua qhov tsis sib xws, cov duab qub qub tshaj plaws nyob rau hauv qhov tseeb tias yog whale's, yuav pom nyob rau hauv lub qhov tsua nto moo ntawm Elephanta, hauv Is Nrias teb. Cov Brahmins khaws cia tias nyob rau hauv cov duab puab yuav luag tsis muaj qhov kawg ntawm lub tuam tsev tsis nco qab, tag nrho cov kev lag luam thiab kev nrhiav, txhua qhov kev xav ntawm tus txiv neej, tau prefigured hnub nyoog ua ntej ib qho ntawm lawv tau los ua. Tsis muaj qhov xav tsis thoob, tias hauv qee yam peb txoj haujlwm zoo ntawm whaling yuav tsum tau muaj qhov ntxoov ntxoo tawm. Lub Hindoo whale raug xa mus rau, tshwm sim nyob rau hauv ib tug nyias muaj nyias ib chav tsev ntawm phab ntsa, depicting lub incarnation ntawm Vishnu nyob rau hauv daim ntawv ntawm leviathan, kawm paub hu ua Matse Avatar. Tab sis txawm hais tias cov duab puab no yog ib nrab txiv neej thiab ib nrab whale, yog li tsuas yog muab tus Tsov tus tw tom kawg, tab sis qhov me me ntawm nws yog txhua yam tsis raug. Nws zoo nkaus li zoo li tus Tsov tus tw ntawm ib qho anaconda, dua li cov xib teg dav ntawm qhov tseeb whale lub majestic flukes.

Tab sis mus rau lub qub Galleries, thiab tam sim no saib ib tug zoo kawg thiab Christian painting tus duab ntawm tus ntses no; vim nws ua tsis tau zoo dua li cov antediluvian Hindoo. Nws yog Guido daim duab ntawm Perseus cawm Andromeda los ntawm hiav txwv-dab los yog whale. Guido qhov twg tau tus qauv ntawm tus tsiaj txawv li ntawd? Tsis yog Hogarth, hauv kev pleev xim tib yam hauv nws tus kheej "Perseus Descending," ua kom zoo dua. Lub corpulence loj heev ntawm tus dab Hogarthian undulates rau saum npoo, tsis tshua kos ib nti dej. Nws muaj ib hom howdah ntawm nws nraub qaum, thiab nws lub qhov ncauj distended tusked nyob rau hauv uas lub billows dov, tej zaum yuav raug coj mus rau lub Traitors 'Gate uas coj los ntawm Thames los ntawm dej mus rau hauv lub Ntauwd. Tom qab ntawd, muaj Prodromus whales ntawm qub Scotch Sibbald, thiab Jonah's whale, raws li tau piav qhia nyob rau hauv cov ntawv luam ntawm cov ntawv qub qub thiab txiav cov qub qub. Yuav hais li cas txog cov no? Raws li rau phau ntawv-binder lub whale winding zoo li ib tug vine-stalk puag ncig lub Tshuag ntawm ib tug nqis los thauj tog rau nkoj-raws li stamped thiab gilded rau sab nraub qaum thiab cov nplooj ntawv npe ntawm ntau phau ntawv qub thiab tshiab-uas yog ib qho zoo nkauj heev tab sis zoo kawg nkaus tsiaj txhu, imitated, kuv coj nws, los ntawm zoo li cov duab ntawm antique vases. Txawm hais tias universally denominated ib tug ntses taub ntswg ntev, txawm li cas los xij, kuv tseem hu rau phau ntawv no-binder tus ntses ib sim ntawm whale; vim tias nws tau npaj siab heev thaum lub cuab yeej tau tshaj tawm thawj zaug. Nws tau qhia los ntawm ib tug qub Italian tshaj tawm qhov chaw nyob rau hauv lub xyoo pua 15th, thaum lub sij hawm Revival of Learning; thiab nyob rau hauv cov hnub ntawd, thiab txawm mus rau ib lub sij hawm piv rau lig, ntses taub ntswg ntev tau nrov yuav tsum yog ib hom ntawm Leviathan.

Nyob rau hauv vignettes thiab lwm yam embellishments ntawm ib co ancient phau ntawv koj yuav nyob rau lub sij hawm ntsib nrog xav paub heev kov ntawm whale, qhov twg txhua yam ntawm spouts, dav hlau d'eau, kub springs thiab txias, Saratoga thiab Baden-Baden, tuaj bubbling los ntawm nws. lub hlwb tsis tag. Nyob rau hauv lub npe-nplooj ntawv ntawm thawj tsab ntawm "Kev Kawm Ua Ntej" koj yuav pom qee qhov xav paub whales.

Tab sis txiav tawm tag nrho cov kev sim tsis ua haujlwm no, cia peb saib ntawm cov duab ntawm leviathan uas qhia txog kev ua siab ntev, kev paub txog kev tshawb fawb, los ntawm cov neeg paub. Nyob rau hauv cov qub Harris sau los ntawm voyages muaj ib co daim hlau ntawm whales rho tawm los ntawm Dutch phau ntawv ntawm voyages, AD 1671, muaj cai "A Whaling Voyage rau Spitzbergen nyob rau hauv lub nkoj Jonas nyob rau hauv lub Whale, Peter Peterson ntawm Friesland, tswv." Nyob rau hauv ib qho ntawm cov phiaj, cov whales, zoo li cov rafts loj ntawm cov cav, yog cov sawv cev ntawm cov dej khov-isles, nrog cov bears dawb khiav hla lawv lub nraub qaum. Nyob rau hauv lwm lub phaj, lub prodigious blunder yog ua los ntawm sawv cev rau whale nrog perpendicular flukes.

Tom qab ntawd ib zaug ntxiv, muaj ib qho quarto imposing, sau los ntawm ib tug Captain Colnett, Tus Thawj Tub Ceev Xwm hauv lub nkoj Askiv, hu ua "A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, for the purpose of expanding the Spermaceti Whale Fisheries." Nyob rau hauv phau ntawv no yog ib qho piv txwv qhia tias yog "Daim duab ntawm Physeter los yog Spermaceti whale, kos los ntawm nplai los ntawm ib tug tua ntawm ntug dej hiav txwv ntawm Mexico, Lub Yim Hli, 1793, thiab hoisted on deck." Kuv tsis ntseeg tsis yog tus thawj coj muaj daim duab veracious no coj los siv rau nws cov tub rog. Hais txog tab sis ib yam hais txog nws, cia kuv hais tias nws muaj qhov muag uas siv, raws li qhov ntsuas nrog, mus rau qhov loj hlob phev whale, yuav ua rau lub qhov muag ntawm tus whale ib hneev-qhov rai qee tsib taw ntev. Au, kuv tus thawj tub rog uas muaj hwjchim, ua cas nej tsis muab Yauna ntsia ntawm qhov muag ntawd rau peb!

Tsis yog cov feem ntau conscientious compilations ntawm Natural History rau cov txiaj ntsim ntawm cov tub ntxhais hluas thiab kev sib tw, dawb los ntawm tib heinousness ntawm yuam kev. Saib cov hauj lwm nrov "Goldsmith's Animated Nature." Nyob rau hauv tsab ntawv London tsab ntawm 1807, muaj cov phiajcim ntawm "whale" thiab "narwhale." Kuv tsis xav kom zoo li inelegant, tab sis qhov no unsightly whale zoo li ib tug amputated sow; thiab, raws li rau tus narwhale, ib ntsia ntawm nws yog txaus rau amaze ib tug, tias nyob rau hauv lub xyoo pua cuaj caug xyoo no xws li ib tug hippogriff yuav tsum tau palmed rau tiag tiag rau txhua tus neeg txawj ntse ntawm cov tub ntxhais kawm ntawv.

Tom qab ntawd, dua, nyob rau hauv 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, ib tug zoo naturalist, luam tawm ib tug scientific systemized whale phau ntawv, uas yog ob peb daim duab ntawm ntau hom ntawm Leviathan. Tag nrho cov no tsis yog tsis yog xwb, tab sis daim duab ntawm Mysticetus los yog Greenland whale (uas yog hais tias, Txoj Cai whale), txawm tias Scoresby, ib tug txiv neej ntev ntev li kov cov tsiaj, tshaj tawm tias tsis muaj nws tus yam ntxwv.

Tab sis qhov muab lub hau-sheaf rau tag nrho cov blundering lag luam no tau tshwj tseg rau scientific Frederick Cuvier, tus kwv tij rau lub npe nrov Baron. Xyoo 1836, nws tau luam tawm Phau Ntawv Keeb Kwm Ntawm Whales, uas nws muab qhov nws hu ua daim duab ntawm Sperm Whale. Ua ntej qhia cov duab ntawd rau ib qho Nantucketer, koj tau muab qhov zoo tshaj plaws rau koj cov ntsiab lus tawm ntawm Nantucket. Hauv ib lo lus, Frederick Cuvier's Sperm Whale tsis yog Sperm Whale, tab sis yog squash. Tau kawg, nws yeej tsis tau txais txiaj ntsig ntawm kev caij nkoj whaling (cov txiv neej zoo li no tsis tshua muaj), tab sis thaum twg nws tau txais daim duab ntawd, leej twg tuaj yeem qhia tau? Tej zaum nws tau txais nws raws li nws qhov kev tshawb fawb ua ntej hauv tib daim teb, Desmarest, tau txais ib qho ntawm nws qhov tseeb rho menyuam; uas yog, los ntawm Suav daim duab. Thiab dab tsi ntawm cov menyuam yaus nrog cov cwj mem uas Suav yog, ntau lub khob khob thiab saucers qhia peb.

Raws li cov kws kos duab kos npe 'whale pom nyob rau hauv txoj kev dai ntawm cov khw muag roj, yuav hais li cas rau lawv? Lawv feem ntau yog Richard III. whales, nrog dromedary humps, thiab savage heev; noj tshais ntawm peb lossis plaub tus neeg tsav nkoj tarts, uas yog whaleboats puv ntawm mariners: lawv deformities floundering nyob rau hauv seas ntawm cov ntshav thiab xiav xim.

Tab sis cov manifold yuam kev hauv depicting lub whale tsis yog li ntawd xav tsis thoob tom qab tag nrho. Xav txog! Feem ntau ntawm cov duab kos tau raug coj los ntawm cov ntses tso tseg; thiab cov no yog hais txog kom raug raws li ib tug kos duab ntawm lub nkoj wrecked, nrog tawg rov qab, yuav raug sawv cev rau cov noble tsiaj nws tus kheej nyob rau hauv tag nrho nws unashed kev khav theeb ntawm hull thiab spars. Txawm hais tias ntxhw tau sawv rau lawv qhov ntev, Leviathan uas muaj sia nyob tseem tsis tau floated nws tus kheej rau nws cov duab. Lub whale nyob, nyob rau hauv nws lub majesty thiab qhov tseem ceeb, tsuas yog pom nyob rau hauv hiav txwv nyob rau hauv unfathomable dej; thiab ntog rau ntawm nws qhov loj ntawm qhov tsis pom, zoo li lub nkoj uas tau pib ua rog; thiab tawm ntawm lub ntsiab lus ntawd nws yog ib yam uas nyob mus ib txhis tsis muaj peev xwm rau tib neeg hauv ntiaj teb los nqa nws lub cev mus rau saum huab cua, txhawm rau khaws tag nrho nws cov o muaj zog thiab undulations. Thiab, tsis txhob hais txog qhov sib txawv ntawm qhov contour ntawm ib tug hluas nqus whale thiab loj hlob Platonian Leviathan; Txawm li cas los xij, txawm hais tias ib tug ntawm cov tub ntxhais hluas nqus whales hoisted rau lub nkoj lub lawj, xws li yog lub outlandish, zoo li eel-zoo li, limbered, txawv ntawm nws, hais tias nws meej qhia dab ntxwg nyoog nws tus kheej yuav ntes tsis tau.

Tab sis nws yuav yog fancied, uas los ntawm lub cev liab qab ntawm lub whale stranded, cov lus qhia tseeb yuav muab tau los kov nws daim ntawv tseeb. Tsis yog kiag li. Rau nws yog ib qho xav paub ntxiv txog Leviathan no, tias nws lub cev pob txha muab lub tswv yim me me ntawm nws cov duab dav dav. Txawm hais tias Jeremy Bentham lub cev pob txha, uas dai rau candelabra hauv lub tsev qiv ntawv ntawm ib tus ntawm nws cov neeg tua neeg, raug coj los siv lub tswv yim ntawm tus txiv neej laus laus, nrog rau tag nrho Jeremy tus cwj pwm coj tus kheej; Txawm li cas los xij, tsis muaj ib yam dab tsi ntawm qhov no tuaj yeem raug xam los ntawm cov pob txha leviathan. Qhov tseeb, raws li tus neeg saib xyuas zoo hais tias, lub cev pob txha ntawm tus whale dais tib yam kev sib raug zoo rau cov tsiaj nyeg thiab padded zoo li cov kab ua rau cov chrysalis uas yog li ntawd roundingly hnab ntawv nws. Qhov peculiarity no yog strikingly evinced nyob rau hauv lub taub hau, raws li nyob rau hauv ib co ntawm cov phau ntawv no yuav raug xwm txheej qhia. Nws tseem yog heev curiously nthuav tawm nyob rau sab fin, cov pob txha uas yuav luag raws nraim teb rau cov pob txha ntawm tib neeg txhais tes, rho tawm tsuas yog tus ntiv tes xoo. Cov fin no muaj plaub tus ntiv tes tsis tu ncua, qhov ntsuas, nruab nrab, ntiv nplhaib, thiab ntiv tes me me. Tab sis tag nrho cov no yog nyob mus ib txhis nyob rau hauv lawv cov fleshy npog, raws li tib neeg cov ntiv tes nyob rau hauv ib tug dag cover. "Txawm li cas los xij, qee zaum tus whale tuaj yeem ua haujlwm rau peb," Stubb hais lus lom zem ib hnub, "nws tsis tuaj yeem hais tiag los tuav peb yam tsis muaj mittens."

Rau tag nrho cov laj thawj no, yog li ntawd, txhua txoj hauv kev uas koj tuaj yeem saib, koj yuav tsum tau txiav txim siab tias Leviathan zoo yog ib tus tsiaj nyob hauv lub ntiaj teb uas yuav tsum tsis txhob pleev xim rau qhov kawg. Muaj tseeb, ib qho duab kos tuaj yeem ntaus lub cim ze dua li lwm tus, tab sis tsis muaj leej twg tuaj yeem ntaus nws nrog ib qho kev txiav txim siab ntawm qhov tseeb. Yog li tsis muaj txoj hauv kev hauv ntiaj teb los tshawb pom qhov tseeb tias whale zoo li cas. Thiab tib hom uas koj tuaj yeem tau txais txawm tias lub tswv yim tolerable ntawm nws lub neej contour, yog los ntawm kev mus whaling koj tus kheej; tab sis los ntawm kev ua li ntawd, koj tsis muaj kev pheej hmoo me me ntawm qhov cub mus ib txhis thiab poob los ntawm nws. Yog li ntawd, nws zoo li kuv tias koj tau zoo tshaj tsis txhob nrawm dhau ntawm koj qhov kev xav paub kov tus Leviathan no.





QHOV 56. Ntawm Cov Duab Tsis Txaus Siab ntawm Whales, thiab cov duab tseeb ntawm Whaling Scenes.

Hauv kev sib txuas nrog cov duab zoo nkauj ntawm whales, Kuv tau raug ntxias ntawm no kom nkag mus rau cov dab neeg tseem ceeb ntawm lawv uas yuav tsum muaj nyob hauv qee phau ntawv, ob qho tib si thaum ub thiab niaj hnub, tshwj xeeb tshaj yog hauv Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, etc. Tab sis kuv dhau qhov teeb meem los ntawm.

Kuv paub tsuas yog plaub daim ntawv luam tawm ntawm tus phev Whale zoo; Colnett's, Huggins's, Frederick Cuvier's, thiab Beale's. Hauv tshooj dhau los Colnett thiab Cuvier tau raug xa mus. Huggins's yog qhov zoo dua li lawv li; tab sis, los ntawm qhov tsis txaus ntseeg, Beale's yog qhov zoo tshaj plaws. Tag nrho Beale cov duab kos ntawm lub whale no zoo, tshwj tsis yog cov duab nruab nrab hauv daim duab ntawm peb lub whales hauv ntau tus cwj pwm, capping nws tshooj thib ob. Nws qhov tseem ceeb, cov nkoj tawm tsam Sperm Whales, txawm hais tias tsis muaj qhov tsis ntseeg tau suav tias ua rau muaj kev ntxhov siab rau pej xeem tsis txaus ntseeg ntawm qee tus txiv neej parlor, yog qhov tseeb thiab zoo li lub neej zoo li nws cov txiaj ntsig. Qee qhov Sperm Whale kos duab hauv J. Ross Browne yog qhov tseeb zoo nkauj hauv contour; tab sis lawv yog wretchedly engraved. Qhov ntawd tsis yog nws qhov txhaum.

Ntawm Txoj Cai Whale, cov duab qhia zoo tshaj plaws yog nyob rau hauv Scoresby; tab sis lawv raug kos rau ntawm qhov me me dhau los qhia qhov kev xav zoo. Nws muaj tab sis ib daim duab ntawm whaling scenes, thiab qhov no yog ib tug tu siab tsis txaus, vim hais tias nws yog los ntawm xws li cov duab xwb, thaum tag nrho cov ua tau zoo, uas koj muaj peev xwm muab tau ib yam dab tsi zoo li lub tswv yim tseeb ntawm lub whale nyob raws li pom los ntawm nws cov neeg yos hav zoov.

Tab sis, coj mus rau tag nrho cov, nyob deb ntawm qhov zoo tshaj plaws, txawm hais tias nyob rau hauv qee cov ntsiab lus tsis yog qhov tseeb tshaj plaws, kev nthuav qhia ntawm whales thiab whaling scenes yuav tsum tau pom nyob qhov twg, yog ob tug loj Fabkis engravings, zoo tua, thiab coj los ntawm paintings los ntawm ib tug Garnery. Raws li txoj cai, lawv sawv cev rau kev tawm tsam ntawm Sperm thiab Right Whale. Nyob rau hauv thawj engraving ib tug Noble Sperm Whale yog depicted nyob rau hauv tag nrho majesty ntawm lub hwj chim, cia li sawv hauv qab lub nkoj los ntawm lub profundities ntawm dej hiav txwv, thiab bearing siab nyob rau hauv cov huab cua nyob rau hauv nws nraub qaum ris zoo kawg nkaus wreck ntawm lub stoven planks. Lub prow ntawm lub nkoj yog ib feem tsis tawg, thiab yog kos cia li ntsuas raws li dab tus txha nraub qaum; thiab sawv hauv qhov kev ua si ntawd, rau ib lub sijhawm uas tsis tuaj yeem suav tsis tau ntawm lub sijhawm, koj pom ib tug oarsman, ib nrab shrouded los ntawm cov tswm ciab npau npau ntawm lub whale, thiab nyob rau hauv kev ua ntawm leaping, zoo li yog los ntawm ib tug precipice. Qhov kev txiav txim ntawm tag nrho cov khoom yog amazingly zoo thiab muaj tseeb. Lub ib nrab-emptied kab-tub floats ntawm lub hiav txwv whitened; ntoo ncej ntawm cov spilled harpoons obliquely bob nyob rau hauv nws; lub taub hau ntawm cov neeg ua luam dej tau tawg tawm ntawm cov whale nyob rau hauv kev sib piv cov lus ntawm affright; thaum nyob rau hauv lub dub cua daj cua dub deb lub nkoj tab tom dais rau ntawm qhov chaw. Kev txhaum loj yuav raug pom nrog cov ntsiab lus anatomical ntawm no whale, tab sis cia li dhau mus; txij li thaum, rau lub neej ntawm kuv, kuv yuav tsis kos ib tug zoo li ntawd.

Nyob rau hauv lub thib ob engraving, lub nkoj yog nyob rau hauv txoj cai ntawm kos ib sab ntawm lub barnacled flank ntawm ib tug loj khiav Right Whale, uas yob nws cov dub weedy tej nyob rau hauv lub hiav txwv zoo li ib co mossy pob zeb-swb los ntawm Patagonian pob tsuas. Nws cov dav hlau yog erect, puv, thiab dub zoo li soot; yog li ntawd los ntawm ntau cov pa luam yeeb nyob rau hauv lub chimney, koj yuav xav tias yuav tsum muaj ib tug siab tawv noj hmo ua noj nyob rau hauv lub Great plob tsis so tswj. Hiav txwv fowls tab tom pecking ntawm me me crabs, plhaub-ntses, thiab lwm yam dej qab zib hiav txwv thiab macaroni, uas lub Right Whale qee zaum nqa nws cov kab mob rov qab. Thiab tag nrho thaum lub tuab-lipped leviathan yog rushing los ntawm qhov sib sib zog nqus, tawm hauv tons ntawm tumultuous dawb curds nyob rau hauv nws sawv, thiab ua rau lub me ntsis lub nkoj mus pob zeb nyob rau hauv lub swells zoo li ib tug skiff ntes tau nyob ze lub paddle-wheels ntawm dej hiav txwv steamer. Yog li ntawd, lub foreground yog tag nrho cov raging kev kub ntxhov; Tab sis tom qab, nyob rau hauv ib qho kev qhuas zoo sib xws, yog lub khob iav ntawm lub hiav txwv becalmed, lub drooping unstarched sails ntawm lub nkoj tsis muaj zog, thiab inert loj ntawm ib tug whale tuag, ib tug conquered fortress, nrog tus chij ntawm ntes tub nkeeg dai ntawm lub whale- ncej ntxig rau hauv nws lub qhov taub.

Leej twg Garnery tus neeg pleev kob yog, lossis yog, kuv tsis paub. Tab sis kuv lub neej rau nws nws yog ib qho kev sib tham nrog nws cov ntsiab lus, los yog lwm yam zoo kawg nkaus qhia los ntawm qee tus neeg paub txog whaleman. Cov Fabkis yog cov menyuam yaus rau kev ua yeeb yam. Mus thiab saib tag nrho cov duab ntawm cov teb chaws Europe, thiab qhov twg koj yuav pom xws li ib tug gallery ntawm kev nyob thiab ua tsis taus pa ntawm lub canvas, xws li nyob rau hauv lub triumphal Hall ntawm Versailles; qhov twg tus beholder fights nws txoj kev, pell-mell, los ntawm kev sib law liag loj sib ntaus sib tua ntawm Fabkis; Qhov twg txhua rab ntaj zoo li lub teeb ntawm Sab Qab Teb Teeb, thiab cov vaj ntxwv ua tub rog thiab Emperors sib tsoo los ntawm, zoo li tus nqi ntawm crowned centaurs? Tsis yog qhov tsis tsim nyog ntawm qhov chaw hauv qhov chaw ntawd, yog cov dej hiav txwv sib ntaus sib tua ntawm Garnery.

Lub ntuj aptitude ntawm Fabkis rau txeeb lub picturesqueness ntawm tej yam zoo li yuav peculiarly evinced nyob rau hauv dab tsi paintings thiab engravings lawv muaj ntawm lawv whaling scenes. QHOV 59. Squid.

Maj mam taug kev los ntawm cov tiaj nyom ntawm brit, Pequod tseem tuav nws txoj kev mus rau sab qaum teb sab hnub tuaj mus rau cov kob ntawm Java; huab cua maj mam impelling nws keel, yog li ntawd nyob rau hauv ib puag ncig serenity nws peb lub siab tapering masts me ntsis yoj mus rau languid breeze, li peb xib teg me me nyob rau hauv ib tug tiaj. Tab sis ib tug pob tshab xiav thaum sawv ntxov, thaum ib tug tseem yuav luag preternatural kis thoob plaws lub hiav txwv, txawm li cas los unattended nrog tej yam stagnant calm; Thaum lub hnub ci ntsa iab ntev ntev ntawm cov dej zoo li ib tus ntiv tes golden nteg hla lawv, enjoining ib co secrecy; thaum cov nthwv dej nplawm ntxhi ua ke thaum lawv maj mam khiav mus; Nyob rau hauv no muaj nuj nqis hush ntawm pom sphere ib tug coj txawv txawv specter tau pom los ntawm Daggoo los ntawm lub ntsiab-mast-lub taub hau.

Nyob rau hauv qhov kev ncua deb, ib tug loj dawb pawg tub nkeeg sawv, thiab nce siab dua thiab siab dua, thiab disentangling nws tus kheej los ntawm lub azure, thaum kawg gleamed ua ntej peb prow zoo li ib tug snow-slide, tshiab swb los ntawm lub toj. Thus glistening for a moment, as slowly it subsided, and sank. Then once more arose, and silently gleamed. It seemed not a whale; and yet is this Moby Dick? thought Daggoo. Again the phantom went down, but on re-appearing once more, with a stiletto-like cry that startled every man from his nod, the negro yelled out—“There! there again! there she breaches! right ahead! The White Whale, the White Whale!”

Upon this, the seamen rushed to the yard-arms, as in swarming-time the bees rush to the boughs. Bare-headed in the sultry sun, Ahab stood on the bowsprit, and with one hand pushed far behind in readiness to wave his orders to the helmsman, cast his eager glance in the direction indicated aloft by the outstretched motionless arm of Daggoo.

Whether the flitting attendance of the one still and solitary jet had gradually worked upon Ahab, so that he was now prepared to connect the ideas of mildness and repose with the first sight of the particular whale he pursued; however this was, or whether his eagerness betrayed him; whichever way it might have been, no sooner did he distinctly perceive the white mass, than with a quick intensity he instantly gave orders for lowering.

The four boats were soon on the water; Ahab's in advance, and all swiftly pulling towards their prey. Soon it went down, and while, with oars suspended, we were awaiting its reappearance, lo! in the same spot where it sank, once more it slowly rose. Almost forgetting for the moment all thoughts of Moby Dick, we now gazed at the most wondrous phenomenon which the secret seas have hitherto revealed to mankind. A vast pulpy mass, furlongs in length and breadth, of a glancing cream-colour, lay floating on the water, innumerable long arms radiating from its centre, and curling and twisting like a nest of anacondas, as if blindly to clutch at any hapless object within reach. No perceptible face or front did it have; no conceivable token of either sensation or instinct; but undulated there on the billows, an unearthly, formless, chance-like apparition of life.

As with a low sucking sound it slowly disappeared again, Starbuck still gazing at the agitated waters where it had sunk, with a wild voice exclaimed—“Almost rather had I seen Moby Dick and fought him, than to have seen thee, thou white ghost!”

“What was it, Sir?” said Flask.

“The great live squid, which, they say, few whale-ships ever beheld, and returned to their ports to tell of it.”

But Ahab said nothing; turning his boat, he sailed back to the vessel; the rest as silently following.

Whatever superstitions the sperm whalemen in general have connected with the sight of this object, certain it is, that a glimpse of it being so very unusual, that circumstance has gone far to invest it with portentousness. So rarely is it beheld, that though one and all of them declare it to be the largest animated thing in the ocean, yet very few of them have any but the most vague ideas concerning its true nature and form; notwithstanding, they believe it to furnish to the sperm whale his only food. For though other species of whales find their food above water, and may be seen by man in the act of feeding, the spermaceti whale obtains his whole food in unknown zones below the surface; and only by inference is it that any one can tell of what, precisely, that food consists. At times, when closely pursued, he will disgorge what are supposed to be the detached arms of the squid; some of them thus exhibited exceeding twenty and thirty feet in length. They fancy that the monster to which these arms belonged ordinarily clings by them to the bed of the ocean; and that the sperm whale, unlike other species, is supplied with teeth in order to attack and tear it.

There seems some ground to imagine that the great Kraken of Bishop Pontoppodan may ultimately resolve itself into Squid. The manner in which the Bishop describes it, as alternately rising and sinking, with some other particulars he narrates, in all this the two correspond. But much abatement is necessary with respect to the incredible bulk he assigns it.

By some naturalists who have vaguely heard rumors of the mysterious creature, here spoken of, it is included among the class of cuttle-fish, to which, indeed, in certain external respects it would seem to belong, but only as the Anak of the tribe.





CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
