VAHE 1. Ngaahi Loom.

Ui au ko ʻIsimeʻeli. ʻI ha ngaahi taʻu kuohilí —ʻo tatau ai pē pe ko e hā hono fuoloa ʻo e taimi totonú —ʻi he siʻisiʻi pe ʻikai haʻaku paʻanga ʻi heʻeku kato paʻangá, pea ʻikai ha meʻa makehe ke u saiʻia ai ʻi ʻuta, ne u fakakaukau te u kiʻi folau holo ʻo sio ki he konga vai ʻo e māmaní. Ko ha founga ia ʻoku ou maʻu ʻo hono fakaʻuli ʻo e spleen mo fakatonutonu ʻa e tafe ʻa e totó. Ko e taimi pē ʻoku ou ʻiloʻi ai ʻoku ou tupulaki ʻo fakaʻofaʻia ʻi he ngutú; ʻi ha taimi pē ʻoku ʻi ai ai ha Nōvema ʻuha mo ʻuha ʻi hoku laumālié; ʻi ha taimi pē ʻoku ou ʻiloʻi ai ʻoku ou kiʻi tuʻu taʻefakakaukauʻi ʻi muʻa ʻi he ngaahi fale tukuʻanga koloa ʻo e puha maté, pea ʻomi hake ʻa e tuʻa ʻo e meʻafakaʻeiki kotoa pē ʻoku ou fetaulaki mo iá; pea tautautefito ki ha taimi pē ʻoku maʻu ai ʻe heʻeku hypos ha mālohi ʻiate au, ʻo fie maʻu ai ha tefitoʻi moʻoni fakamōlale mālohi ke taʻofi au mei heʻeku laka ʻosi ʻuhingamālie ki he halá, mo tuki fakafounga ʻa e ngaahi hēkesi ʻo e kakaí —pea, ʻoku ou lau kuo taimi ke u aʻu ki tahi ʻi he vave tahá. Ko 'eku fetongi 'eni ki he me'afana mo e polo. ‘I ha lakalakaimonū fakafilōsofia ‘oku lī ai ‘e Cato ia ki he‘ene heletaá; ʻOku ou ʻalu fakalongolongo atu ki he vaká. ʻOku ʻikai ha meʻa ʻe fakaʻohovale ʻi he meʻá ni. Kapau naʻa nau ʻiloʻi pē ia, ʻoku meimei ko e kau tangata kotoa pē ʻi honau tuʻungá, ʻi ha taimi pe ʻe taha, ʻoku nau fakaʻamua ʻa e ngaahi ongo meimei tatau mo au ki he ʻoseni.

‘Oku ‘i ai he taimí ni homou kolo ‘i he motu ko e Manhattoes, ‘oku fusi takai ‘e he ngaahi taulangá ‘o hangē ko e ngaahi motu ‘Initiá ‘e he ngaahi ‘ā ‘o e ‘aá—‘oku ‘ātakai‘i ia ‘e he fefakatau‘akí ‘aki ‘ene ngaluopé. ‘I he to‘omata‘ú mo e to‘ohemá, ‘oku ‘ave koe ‘e he ngaahi halá ki he vaí. Ko hono loto kolo māʻolungá ko e pātí, ʻa ia ʻoku fufulu ai ʻe he ngaahi peaú ʻa e mole fakaʻeiʻeiki ko iá, pea fakamokomokoʻi ʻe he ngaahi matangí, ʻa ia ʻi ha ngaahi houa siʻi kimuʻá naʻe ʻikai ke sio ki ai ʻa e fonuá. Sioloto atu ki he fu‘u kakai tokolahi ‘o e kau sio vai ‘i aí.

Takai holo ʻi he kolo ʻo ha hoʻatā Sāpate fakaʻofoʻofa. Alu mei Koleasi Hook ki Koeniti Slip, pea mei ai, ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo Uiteholó, ʻo fakatokelau. Ko e hā ‘okú ke sio ki aí?—Kuo fokotu‘u ‘o hangē ha kau le‘o fakalongolongo ‘i he kotoa ‘o e koló, ‘oku tu‘u ai ‘a e kau tangata matelie ‘e lauiafe ‘oku nau fakapapau‘i ‘i he ngaahi fakakaukau ‘o e ‘oseni. Ko e ni‘ihi ‘oku nau falala ki he ngaahi spiles; ko e niʻihi ʻoku nau tangutu ʻi he ngaahi ʻuluʻi taulanga; ko e ni‘ihi ‘oku nau sio ki he ngaahi ‘ā ‘o e ngaahi vaka mei Siainá; ko e niʻihi ʻoku nau māʻolunga ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he rigging, ʻo hangē ʻoku nau feinga ke maʻu ha peep ʻoku kei lelei ange ʻi he tahí. Ka ko e kau tangata fonua kotoa eni; ‘o e ngaahi ‘aho ‘o e uiké na‘e faka‘ai‘ai ‘i he lath mo e palasitaá—na‘e ha‘i ki he ngaahi laupapa, fa‘o ki he ngaahi tangutu‘angá, pipiki ki he ngaahi tēpilé. Pea ʻoku fēfē leva ʻa e meʻá ni? Kuo mole atu ‘a e ngaahi ngoue lanu mata? Ko e hā ʻoku nau fai ʻi hení?

Kae vakai! ʻoku haʻu heni ha fuʻu kakai tokolahi ange, ʻoku nau luelue hangatonu ki he vaí, pea ʻoku ngali ʻoku nau haʻi ki ha luelue. Faikehe! ‘Oku ‘ikai ha me‘a te ne fakafiemālie‘i kinautolu ka ko e ngata‘anga taupotu taha ‘o e fonuá; he ʻikai feʻunga ʻa e ʻeveʻeva holo ʻi he malumalu ʻo e ngaahi fale tukuʻanga koloa ʻi aí. ‘Ikai Kuo pau ke nau ofi ki he vaí ‘i he lahi taha te nau ala lavá ‘o ‘ikai ke nau tō ki loto. Pea ‘oku nau tu‘u ai—‘i he ngaahi maile ‘o kinautolu—‘i he ngaahi liiki. Ko e kau nofo fonua kotoa, ‘oku nau ha‘u mei he ngaahi hala mo e ngaahi hala lalahi, ngaahi hala mo e ngaahi hala lalahi—‘i he tokelaú, hahaké, tongá, mo e hihifó. Ka ʻi heni ʻoku nau fakatahataha kotoa. Tala mai, ‘oku tohoaki‘i kinautolu ki ai ‘e he ‘ulungaanga lelei fakamākinito ‘o e ngaahi tuitui ‘o e ngaahi kāpasa ‘o e ngaahi vaka kotoa ko iá?

Toe tuʻo taha. Talamai 'oku ke 'i he fonua; ‘i he ngaahi fonua mā‘olunga ‘e ni‘ihi ‘o e ngaahi lepá. ʻAlu ʻi ha meimei hala pē ʻokú ke loto ki ai, pea hongofulu ki he taha ʻokú ne ʻave koe ki lalo ʻi ha teleʻa, pea tuku koe ʻi ai ʻi ha vaitupu ʻi he vaitafé. ʻOku ʻi ai ha meʻa fakaofo ʻi ai. Tuku ke tō ʻa e tangata ʻoku ʻikai ke ne ʻiloʻi ʻa e ʻatamaí ʻi heʻene ngaahi fakakaukau loloto tahá —tuʻu ʻa e tangata ko iá ʻi hono vaʻé, fokotuʻu hono vaʻé ke ʻalu, pea te ne tataki taʻehalaia koe ki he vaí, kapau ʻoku ʻi ai ha vai ʻi he vahefonua kotoa ko iá. Kapau te ke fieinua ʻi ha taimi ʻi he toafa lahi ʻo ʻAmeliká, ʻahiʻahiʻi ʻa e ʻahiʻahi ko ʻení, kapau ʻoku hoko ʻo ʻoatu hoʻo fonongá ha palōfesa fakametāfisika. ‘Io, hangē ko ia ‘oku ‘ilo‘i ‘e he tokotaha kotoa pē, ‘oku mali ‘a e fakalaulaulotó mo e vaí ‘o ta‘engata.

Ka ko ha ʻātisi ʻeni. 'Oku ne faka'amu ke valivali koe 'a e dreamiest, shadiest, fakalongolongo taha, faka'ofo'ofa taha 'a e konga 'o e fonua faka'ofa 'i he vanu kotoa 'o e Saco. Ko e hā ‘a e ‘elemēniti tefito ‘okú ne ngāue‘akí? ʻOku tuʻu ai ʻene ngaahi ʻakaú, ʻoku takitaha ʻi ai hono fuʻu ʻakau ʻoku ava, ʻo hangē ʻoku ʻi loto ha ʻēmití mo ha kalusefai; pea ʻoku mohe ʻi heni hono ngoueʻangá, pea ʻoku mohe ai ʻene fanga manú; pea 'alu hake mei he fale 'i 'olunga 'oku 'alu ha kohu mohe. ‘Oku ‘i he loloto ‘o e ngaahi vaotā mama‘ó ‘oku matangi ha founga valevale, ‘o a‘u atu ki he ngaahi ‘ulu‘akau ‘o e ngaahi mo‘unga ‘oku ‘olunga ‘oku kaukau ‘i honau lanu pulū ‘i he tafa‘aki mo‘ungá. Ka neongo ʻoku tokoto pehē ʻa e fakatātaá, pea neongo ʻoku lulululu hifo ʻa e ʻakau paini ko ʻení ʻo hangē ha ngaahi lau ʻi he ʻulu ʻo e tauhisipi ko ʻení, ka naʻe taʻeʻaonga kotoa pē, tukukehe kapau naʻe sio fakamamaʻu ʻa e mata ʻo e tauhisipí ki he vaitafe fakameaʻa ʻi muʻa aí. ‘Alu ‘o ‘a‘ahi ki he Ngaahi Tele‘á ‘i Sune, ‘i he taimi ‘okú ke luelue ai ‘i ha ngaahi maile ‘e laui hongofulu ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e ngaahi Tiger-lilies ‘o a‘u ki ho tuliki—ko e hā ‘a e me‘a ‘oku fiema‘u ‘e he me‘a faka‘ofo‘ofa ‘e tahá?—Vai—‘oku ‘ikai ha mata‘itofe vai ‘i ai! Kapau ko Niagara ko ha fo‘i ‘one‘one pē, te ke fononga ‘i ho‘o maile ‘e taha afe ke sio ki ai? Ko e hā naʻe fakakaukauʻi ai ʻe he tangata faʻu-tohi masiva ʻo Tenesií, ʻi heʻene maʻu fakafokifā ha foʻi siliva ʻe ua, pe te ne fakatau mai haʻane kote, ʻa ia naʻá ne fie maʻu fakaloloma, pe ʻinivesi ʻene paʻangá ʻi ha fononga lue lalo ki Rockaway Beach? Ko e ha 'oku meimei tamasi'i mo'ui lelei fefeka kotoa pe 'oku 'i ai ha laumalie mo'ui lelei fefeka 'iate ia, 'i ha taimi pe kehe ke vale ke 'alu ki tahi? Ko e hā ‘i ho‘o ‘uluaki folau ko ha pāsesé, na‘á ke ongo‘i ai tonu ha ngaueue fakamisiteli pehē, ‘i he ‘uluaki tala atu kiate koe mo ho vaká ‘oku ‘ikai ke ke toe sio ki he fonuá he taimí ni? Ko e hā na‘e tauhi ai ‘e he kau Pēsia motu‘á ‘a e tahí ke mā‘oni‘oní? Ko e ha na'e 'oange ai 'e he kau Kalisi ha 'otua mavahe, pea ko e tokoua 'o Jove? Ko e mo‘oni ‘oku ‘ikai ke ta‘e‘uhinga ‘a e me‘á ni kotoa. Pea ‘oku toe loloto ange ‘a e ‘uhinga ‘o e talanoa ko ia ‘o Nasisó, ‘a ia koe‘uhi na‘e ‘ikai ke ne lava ‘o ma‘u ‘a e ‘īmisi fakamamahi mo anga-malū na‘á ne sio ki ai ‘i he matavaitupú, na‘á ne tō ki ai ‘o melemo. Ka ko e ‘īmisi tatau pē, ‘oku tau sio tonu ki ai ‘i he ngaahi vaitafe mo e ngaahi ‘oseni kotoa pē. Ko e ‘īmisi ia ‘o e phantom ta‘ealapuke ‘o e mo‘uí; pea ko e kī ʻeni ki ai kotoa.

Ko ʻeni, ʻi heʻeku pehē ʻoku ou ʻi he tōʻonga ʻo e ʻalu ki tahí ʻi ha taimi pē ʻoku kamata ke u tupu ʻo ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi ʻa e matá, pea kamata ke u ʻover conscious ʻi hoku maʻamaʻá, ʻoku ʻikai ke u ʻuhinga ke ʻi ai haʻaku fakakaukau ʻoku ou ʻalu ki tahi ko ha pasese. He ke ke ‘alu ko ha pāsese kuo pau ke ‘i ai ha‘o kato pa‘anga, pea ko e kato pa‘angá ko ha tupenu pē tukukehe kapau ‘oku ‘i ai ha‘o me‘a ‘i ai. ʻIkai ngata aí, ʻoku puke ʻa e kau pāsesé ʻi he tahí —ʻoku nau tupu ʻo fakakikihi —ʻikai ke nau mohe ʻi ha ngaahi pō —ʻoku ʻikai ke nau fiefia lahi, ko ha meʻa fakalūkufua; —ʻikai, ʻoku ʻikai ke u teitei ʻalu ko ha pasese; pea neongo ko ha meʻa au ʻo e māsimá, ʻoku ʻikai ke u ʻalu ki tahi ko ha Komoto, pe ko ha Kapiteni, pe ko ha Kuki. ʻOku ou liʻaki ʻa e nāunau mo e makehe ʻo e ngaahi lakanga peheé kiate kinautolu ʻoku nau saiʻia aí. Ko au, ʻoku ou fakaliliʻa ki he ngaahi ngāue fakaʻapaʻapa fakaʻapaʻapa kotoa pē, ngaahi ʻahiʻahi, mo e ngaahi faingataʻa ʻo e faʻahinga kotoa pē. ʻOku lahi ʻaupito ia ʻi he meʻa te u lava ʻo fai ke tokangaʻi au, ʻo ʻikai ke u tokangaʻi ʻa e ngaahi vaka, ngaahi vakapuna, ngaahi vakapuna, ngaahi vakapuna, mo e ngaahi meʻa kehe. Pea ko e me'a ki he 'alu ko e kuki, —neongo 'oku ou fakahaa'i 'oku 'i ai ha langilangi lahi 'i he me'a ko ia, ko ha kuki ko ha fa'ahinga 'ofisa 'i he vaka-vaka-ka, 'i ha fa'ahinga founga, na'e 'ikai te u fancied broiling manupuna;—neongo 'i he taimi 'e taha broiled, fakakaukau fakapotopoto buttered, mo fakamaau respectatically salted mo peppered, 'oku 'ikai ha taha 'e 'ikai ke spefu, 'oku 'ikai toe 'i ai ha taha 'e 'ikai ke spefu, . moa tunu ʻi heʻeku loto ki aí. Ko e mei he dotings tauhi ‘aitoli ‘a e kau ‘Isipite motu‘a ‘i he ibis tunu mo e hoosi vaitafe tunu, ‘oku ke sio ai ki he ngaahi mummies ‘o e ngaahi me‘amo‘ui ko ia ‘i honau ngaahi fale ta‘o lalahi ‘a e ngaahi pilamití.

‘Ikai, ‘i he‘eku ‘alu ki tahí, ‘oku ou ‘alu ko ha tangata toutai faingofua, ‘i mu‘a tonu ‘i he poú, ‘o palamu hifo ki he forecastle, ‘i ‘olunga ‘i ai ki he ‘ulu-pou fakatu‘í. Mo'oni, 'oku nau sai'ia ange ke 'ota au fekau'aki mo ha ni'ihi, pea 'ai au ke u puna mei he spar ki he spar, 'o hange ha he'e 'i ha vao 'akau 'o Me. Pea ‘i he ‘uluaki taimí, ‘oku ta‘efakafiefia fe‘unga ‘a e fa‘ahinga me‘a ko ení. ʻOkú ne uesia ʻa e ongoʻi fakaʻapaʻapa ʻa ha taha, tautautefito kapau ʻokú ke haʻu mei ha fāmili motuʻa kuo fokotuʻu ʻi he fonuá, ko e fāmili Van Rensselaers, pe Randolphs, pe Hardicanutes. Pea ʻoku mahulu hake ʻi he meʻa kotoa pē, kapau naʻe kimuʻa pē ʻi hono ʻai ho nimá ki he ipu tā, kuó ke puleʻi ia ko ha puleako ʻi he fonuá, ʻo ʻai ai ke tuʻu ʻa e fānau tangata lōloa tahá ʻi he manavahē ʻiate koe. Ko e liliu ko ha liliu vēkeveke, ʻoku ou fakapapauʻi atu, mei ha puleako ki ha tangata toutai, pea ʻoku fie maʻu ha decoction mālohi ʻo Seneca mo e kau Sitoikí ke ke lava ʻo malimali mo kātakiʻi ia. Ka na‘a mo e me‘á ni ‘oku ‘osi ia ‘i he faai mai ‘a e taimí.

Fēfē ia, kapau ʻe fekauʻi au ʻe ha ngaahi hunks motuʻa ʻo ha kapiteni tahi ke u ʻomi ha tafiʻanga ʻo tafiʻi hifo ʻa e ngaahi funga vaká? Ko e hā ʻa e lahi ʻo e taʻefakaʻapaʻapa ko iá, ʻi hono fua, ʻoku ou ʻuhingá, ʻi he meʻafua ʻo e Fuakava Foʻoú? ʻOkú ke pehē ʻoku fakakaukau ʻa e ʻāngelo lahi ko Kepalelí ki ha meʻa ʻoku siʻisiʻi ange ʻiate au, koeʻuhí he ʻoku ou talangofua vave mo fakaʻapaʻapa ki he motuʻa hunks ko iá ʻi he meʻa pau ko iá? Ko hai ʻoku ʻikai ko ha pōpula? Tala mai ia kiate au. Sai, ko ia, neongo pe ko e hā hono fekauʻi au ʻe he kau kapiteni tahi motuʻá —neongo pe ko e hā haʻanau tukituki mo tukiʻi au, ʻoku ou maʻu ʻa e fiemālie ʻi heʻeku ʻiloʻi ʻoku sai pē ia; ko e tokotaha kehe kotoa pē ʻoku ʻi ha founga ʻe taha pe ʻoku ngāue ʻi ha founga meimei tatau —pe ʻi ha vakai fakatuʻasino pe fakametāfisika, ʻa ia; pea ko ia 'oku paasi takai 'a e thump 'o e 'univeesi, pea 'oku totonu ke fe'auhi 'a e nima kotoa pe 'i he ngaahi uma 'o e taha mo e taha, pea fiemalie.

Toe 'alu ma'u pe au ki tahi ko e folau, he 'oku nau fai ha poini ke totongi mai au ki he'eku faingata'a'ia, ka 'oku 'ikai ke nau teitei totongi ki he kau pasese ha seniti 'e taha 'oku ou fanongo ki ai. ‘I hono kehé, kuo pau ke totongi ‘e he kau pāsesé tonu. Pea 'oku 'i ai 'a e kehekehe kotoa pe 'i mamani 'i he vaha'a 'o e totongi mo e totongi. Ko e ngāue ‘o e totongí mahalo ko e me‘a ta‘efakafiemālie taha ia na‘e fakahoko mai ‘e he ongo kaiha‘a ngoue fua‘i‘akaú kiate kimauá. Ka ko e totongi ,—ko e hā ‘e fakahoa ki aí? Ko e ngāue fakakolo ‘oku ma‘u ai ‘e ha tangata ‘a e pa‘angá ‘oku fakaofo mo‘oni, ‘i he‘etau fakakaukau atu ki he‘etau tui fakamātoato ko e pa‘angá ‘a e aka ‘o e ngaahi mahaki fakamāmani kotoa pē, pea ‘oku ‘ikai ha ‘uhinga ‘e lava ke hū ai ha tangata pa‘anga ki hēvani. ʻĀ! he fiefia ē ʻoku tau tukuange kitautolu ki he malaʻiá!

Faka‘osí, ‘oku ou ‘alu ma‘u pē ki tahi ko ha tokotaha toutai, koe‘uhi ko e fakamālohisino lelei mo e ‘ea ma‘a ‘o e fungavaka ‘o e fore-castle. He hange ko ia 'i he mamani ko 'eni, 'Oku lahi ange 'a e ngaahi matangi 'ulu 'i he ngaahi matangi mei he astern ('a ia, kapau 'oku 'ikai te ke teitei maumau'i 'a e Pythagorean maxim), ko ia ki he konga lahi 'oku ma'u 'e he Commodore 'i he kuata-deck hono 'atakai 'i he nima hono ua mei he kau folau 'i he forecastle. ʻOkú ne fakakaukau ʻokú ne ʻuluaki mānavaʻi ia; ka ʻoku ʻikai pehē. ‘I he founga tatau pē ‘oku taki ai ‘e he kakai anga-mahení honau kau takí ‘i ha ngaahi me‘a lahi kehe, ‘i he taimi tatau ‘oku si‘i ‘a e mahalo‘i ‘e he kau takí. Ka ko e hā naʻe hoko ai ʻi he hili ʻeku toutou nanamuʻi ʻa e tahí ʻi heʻeku hoko ko ha tangata toutai fakatau koloá, ke u toʻo ia he taimí ni ki hoku ʻulú ke u ʻalu ʻi ha folau toutai tafola; ko e ʻōfisa polisi taʻehāmai ʻeni ʻo e Fates, ʻa ia ʻokú ne maʻu ʻa e siofi maʻu pē aú, pea ʻokú ne kulii fakafufū au, mo tākiekina au ʻi ha founga ʻoku ʻikai lava ke taliui —ʻokú ne lava ʻo tali lelei ange ʻi ha toe taha kehe. Pea, ʻoku ʻikai ha veiveiua, ko ʻeku ʻalu ʻi he folau toutai tafola ko ʻení, naʻe hoko ia ko ha konga ʻo e polokalama lahi ʻo Poloviteni ʻa ia naʻe faʻu ʻi ha taimi fuoloa kuohilí. Naʻe haʻu ia ko ha faʻahinga interlude nounou mo e solo ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ngaahi fakaʻaliʻali lahi ange. 'Oku ou to'o ia ko e konga ko 'eni 'o e lao kuo pau ke lele ha me'a pehe ni:

“ Fili Lahi Fakafe’auhi ki he Palesiteni ‘o e ‘Iunaiteti Siteiti. “FOLAU TOHI TALO ‘A E ‘ISIMĒLI ‘E TAHA. “TAU TOTO ‘I ‘AFAKANISITANI.”

Neongo ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo tala ʻa e ʻuhinga tonu naʻe tuku hifo ai au ʻe he kau pule siteisi ko iá, ʻa e Fates, ki he konga kovi ko ʻeni ʻo ha folau toutai tafola, ʻi he taimi naʻe fokotuʻu ai ʻa e niʻihi kehé ki he ngaahi konga fakaofo ʻi he ngaahi meʻa fakamamahi māʻolungá, mo e ngaahi konga nounou mo faingofua ʻi he ngaahi meʻa fakakata fakaʻeiʻeikí, mo e ngaahi konga fakafiefia ʻi he ngaahi meʻa fakakata fakaʻeiʻeikí —neongo ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo tala exact ʻeni; ka, ko ʻeni ʻoku ou manatuʻi ʻa e ngaahi tūkunga kotoa pē, ʻoku ou fakakaukau ʻoku ou lava ʻo sio ki ha kiʻi meʻa siʻisiʻi ki he ngaahi matavai mo e ngaahi taumuʻa ʻa ia ʻi hono ʻoatu fakapotopoto mai kiate au ʻi he ngaahi fakapulipuli kehekehe, naʻá ne fakaʻaiʻai au ke u fokotuʻu ke fakahoko ʻa e konga naʻá ku faí, tukukehe ʻene fakaʻaiʻai au ki he fakakaukau hala ko ha fili ia ʻoku tupu mei heʻeku jugwill freewill mo e discaline taʻefilifilimānako pē ʻaʻakú.

Ko e me‘a tefito ‘i he ngaahi taumu‘a ko ení ko e fakakaukau lōmekina ‘o e tafola lahí tonu. Naʻe langaʻi ʻe ha fuʻu manu fekai fakaʻofoʻofa mo fakamisiteli pehē ʻeku fie ʻiló kotoa. Pea ko e ngaahi tahi vao mo mama'o 'a ia na'a ne fakatakataka'i ai hono motu lahi; ko e ngaahi fakatu‘utāmaki ta‘ealafakahaofi, ta‘ehingoa ‘o e tafola; ko e ngaahi meʻá ni, mo e ngaahi meʻa fakaofo kotoa ʻoku ʻi ai ʻo e ngaahi sioʻata mo e ngaahi leʻo ʻe taha afe ʻo e kau Patakoniá, naʻa nau tokoni ke ueʻi au ki heʻeku fakaʻamú. ‘I he kau tangata kehé, mahalo pē, na‘e ‘ikai mei hoko ‘a e ngaahi me‘a peheé ko ha ngaahi faka‘ai‘ai; ka ko au, ‘oku fakamamahi‘i au ‘e ha ‘u‘ulu ta‘engata ki he ngaahi me‘a mama‘ó. 'Oku ou sai'ia ke folau 'i he ngaahi tahi tapu, pea tu'uta 'i he ngaahi matātahi barbarous. ʻI he ʻikai ke u tukunoaʻi ʻa e meʻa ʻoku leleí, ʻoku ou vave ke ʻiloʻi ha meʻa fakalilifu, pea ʻe kei lava pē ke u fakasōsiale mo ia —te nau fakaʻatā au —he ʻoku sai pē ke ʻi ha ngaahi tuʻunga fakakaumeʻa mo e kau pōpula kotoa ʻo e feituʻu ʻoku nofo ai ha taha.

Ko ia ai, koe‘uhi ko e ngaahi me‘á ni, na‘e talitali lelei ai ‘a e folau toutai tafola; naʻe taʻalo fakaava ʻa e ngaahi matapā tāfea lalahi ʻo e māmani fakaofó, pea ʻi he ngaahi fakakaukau vao naʻá ne fakaʻaiʻai au ki heʻeku taumuʻá, naʻe lele mai ha toko ua mo toko ua ʻi ai ki hoku laumālie ʻi lotó, ko ha ngaahi fononga taʻe ngata ʻa e tafola, pea, ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e tokolahi taha ʻo kinautolú kotoa, ko ha foʻi ʻata lahi ʻe taha ʻoku ʻi ai hono fulufuluʻi ʻulu, ʻo hangē ha moʻunga sinou ʻi he ʻeá.





VAHE 2. Koe Kapeti-Bag.

Naʻá ku fakahū ha kofukofu ʻe taha pe ua ki hoku kato kāpeti motuʻá, ʻo u fakahū ia ʻi lalo ʻi hoku nimá, peá u kamata atu ki Keipi Honi mo e Pasifikí. ʻI heʻeku mavahe mei he kolo lelei ʻo Manhatto motuʻá, ne u aʻu totonu ki Niu Petifootí. Ko ha pō Tokonaki ia ʻo Tīsema. Naʻá ku loto-mamahi lahi ʻi heʻeku ʻiloʻi kuo ʻosi folau ʻa e kiʻi pakete ki Nantucket, pea ʻoku ʻikai ha founga ke aʻu ai ki he feituʻu ko iá ʻe ʻoatu, kae ʻoua kuo ʻosi ʻa e Mōnite hono hokó.

Ko e tokolahi taha ʻo e kau kanititeiti kei talavou ki he ngaahi mamahi mo e tautea ʻo e toutai tafola ʻoku nau tuʻu ʻi he New Bedford tatau ko ʻení, mei ai ke kamata ʻenau folaú, ʻe lava ke fekauʻaki foki mo au, ko e taha, naʻe ʻikai haʻaku fakakaukau ke fai pehē. He naʻe fakapapauʻi hoku ʻatamaí ke u folau ʻo ʻikai ko ha vakapuna kehe ka ko ha vakapuna ʻo Nantucket, koeʻuhí he naʻe ʻi ai ha meʻa lelei mo longoaʻa fekauʻaki mo e meʻa kotoa pē naʻe fekauʻaki mo e motu motuʻa ʻiloa ko iá, ʻa ia naʻá ne fakafiefiaʻi fakaofo au. Makehe mei ai neongo kuo fakamuimuitaha ʻa e fakaʻauha ʻe Niu Petifoti ʻa e pisinisi ʻo e toutai tafola, pea neongo ʻi he meʻá ni ʻoku fuʻu ʻi mui ʻaupito ʻa e motuʻa masiva ko Nantucket ʻiate ia he taimí ni, ka ko Nantucket ʻa hono ʻuluaki ʻuluaki meʻakaí —ko e Taia ʻo e Carthage ko ʻení; —ko e feituʻu naʻe tō ai ʻa e ʻuluaki tafola ʻAmelika mate. Ko fē ha toe feituʻu kehe ka ko Nantucket naʻe ʻuluaki ʻalu atu ai ʻa e kau tangata tafola ʻāpilikani ko iá, ʻa e kau Red-Men, ʻi ha ngaahi vakapuna ke tuli ʻa e Livaiataní? Pea ko fē ka mei Nantucket foki, naʻe ʻoatu ai ʻa e ʻuluaki kiʻi vakapuna fakaʻofoʻofa ko iá, ʻoku uta ʻi ha konga ʻa e ngaahi makamaka ʻoku ʻomi mei muli —ʻoku pehē ʻa e talanoá —ke lī ki he fanga tafola, koeʻuhí ke ʻiloʻi ʻa e taimi naʻa nau ofi feʻunga ai ke fakatuʻutāmakiʻi ha foʻi ʻakau mei he bowsprit?

Ko ʻeni ʻi heʻeku maʻu ha pō, ʻaho, pea mo ha toe pō ʻe taha ʻoku muimui mai ʻi muʻa ʻiate au ʻi Niu Petifootí, kimuʻa peá u lava ʻo heka ki hoku taulanga ʻoku fakataumuʻá, naʻe hoko ia ko ha meʻa ke hohaʻa ki ai pe ko e fē te u kai mo mohe ai ʻi he lolotonga ko iá. Ko ha pō naʻe fuʻu veiveiua ʻaupito, ʻikai, ko ha pō fakapoʻuli mo fakaloloma ʻaupito, momoko ʻaupito mo taʻefiefia. Naʻe ʻikai ke u ʻilo ha taha ʻi he feituʻú. Naʻá ku fakaongoʻi hoku kató ʻi he ngaahi foʻi grapnels hohaʻá, pea naʻá ku ʻomi pē ha ngaahi konga siliva siʻisiʻi, —Ko ia, ko e fē pē ha feituʻu te ke ʻalu ki ai, ʻIsimeʻeli, ko ʻeku leá ia kiate au, ʻi heʻeku tuʻu ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo ha hala fakaloloma ʻo ʻai hoku kató, pea fakafehoanaki ʻa e fakapoʻulí ki he tokelaú mo e fakapoʻuli ki he cohere may you in you pō, ʻeku ʻofaʻanga ko ʻIsimeʻelí, fakapapauʻi ke ke ʻeke ki he totongí, pea ʻoua naʻá ke fuʻu fakatefito.

Naʻá ku luelue ʻi he ngaahi halá ʻi ha ngaahi sitepu tuʻu, pea fakalaka atu ʻi he fakaʻilonga ʻo e “The Crossed Harpoons” —ka naʻe fuʻu fakamole lahi mo fakafiefia ʻi ai. ‘Ikai ngata aí, mei he ngaahi matapā sio‘ata kulokula ngingila ‘o e “Sword-Fish Inn,” na‘e ha‘u ai ha ngaahi huelo vela lahi, ‘o hangē na‘á ne fakavaivai‘i ‘a e sinou mo e ‘aisi na‘e fakafonu mei mu‘a ‘i he falé, he ‘i he feitu‘u kehe kotoa pē na‘e tokoto ai ‘a e ‘uha momokó ‘o ‘inisi ‘e hongofulu hono matolú ‘i ha hala fefeka mo ‘asifaliti ‘o fakafepaki‘i au,,—‘i he taimi na‘á ku flick ai ‘a e . ngaahi fakafuofua, koeʻuhí he mei he ngāue fefeka mo taʻefakatomala naʻe ʻi ha tuʻunga fakaloloma ʻaupito ʻa e lalo ʻo hoku ngaahi sū. Fuʻu fakamole lahi mo fakafiefia, ko ʻeku toe fakakaukau ia, ʻo u kiʻi tuʻu ʻi ha momeniti ʻe taha ke siofi e ulo lahi ʻi he halá, mo fanongo ki he ngaahi leʻo ʻo e ngaahi sioʻata ʻoku ngingila ʻi lotó. Ka ke hoko atu, ʻIsimeʻeli, ko ʻeku lea fakamuimui ia; 'oku 'ikai te ke fanongo? mavahe mei muʻa ʻi he matapaá; ko ho'o ngaahi sū kuo fakapipiki 'oku ne ta'ofi 'a e hala. Ko ia naʻá ku ʻalu atu. Naʻá ku muimui he taimí ni ʻi heʻeku ʻinasí ʻi he ngaahi hala naʻá ne ʻave au ki he vaí, he ʻoku ʻikai ha veiveiua, naʻe ʻi ai ʻa e ngaahi fale talitali kakai maʻamaʻa tahá, kapau ʻoku ʻikai ko e ngaahi fale talitali kakai fakafiefia tahá.

Ko e ngaahi hala fakaloloma peheé! ko e ngaahi poloka ‘uli‘uli, ‘o ‘ikai ko ha ngaahi fale, ‘i he ongo nimá, pea ‘i heni mo hena ha fo‘i te‘elango, ‘o hangē ha te‘elango ‘oku nga‘unu holo ‘i ha fa‘itoka. ʻI he houa ko ʻeni ʻo e poʻulí, ʻo e ʻaho fakaʻosi ʻo e uiké, naʻe fakamoʻoniʻi ʻe he kuata ko ia ʻo e koló ʻoku ʻikai ha taha ʻi ai. Ka ʻi he taimi ko iá ne u aʻu atu ki ha maama kohu ʻoku ʻalu atu mei ha fale maʻulalo mo fālahi, ʻa ia naʻe tuʻu fakaafe ʻa e matapaá. Naʻe ʻi ai hono fōtunga taʻetokanga, ʻo hangē naʻe ʻuhinga ia ki he ngaahi ngāueʻaki ʻa e kakaí; ko ia, ʻi heʻeku hū atú, ko e ʻuluaki meʻa ne u faí ko ʻeku tūkia ʻi ha puha efuefu ʻi he ʻaá. Ha! naʻá ku fakakaukau, ha, ʻi he meimei fakaʻauha au ʻe he ngaahi momoʻi meʻa lele, ko e efu ko ʻení mei he kolo fakaʻauha ko iá, ʻa Komolá? Ka ko e “Ngaahi Ha‘aponi Kolosi,” mo e “Ika-Heletā?”—‘eni, kuo pau leva ke hoko ia ko e faka‘ilonga ‘o e “Tauhele.” Kae kehe, naʻá ku hiki hake au ʻo fanongo ki ha leʻo leʻolahi ʻi lotó, ne u tekeʻi atu ʻo fakaava ha matapā hono ua, ʻi loto.

Na'e hange ko e Fale Alea 'Uli'uli lahi 'oku tangutu 'i Tofeti. Naʻe tafoki takai ha fofonga ʻuliʻuli ʻe teau ʻi honau ngaahi laine ʻo sio fakamamaʻu; pea ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá, naʻe tā ʻe ha ʻĀngelo ʻuliʻuli ʻo e Fakaʻauhá ha tohi ʻi ha tuʻunga malangá. Ko ha siasi negro ia; pea ko e text 'a e tangata malanga na'e fekau'aki ia mo e 'uli 'o e po'uli, pea mo e tangi mo e tangi mo e 'u'ulu nifo 'i ai. Ha, ʻIsimeʻeli, ko ʻeku ngutungutu ia, ʻo u holomui, Fakafiefia fakaʻofa ʻi he fakaʻilonga ʻo e 'Ko e Tauhele!'

ʻI heʻeku hoko atu aí, naʻe faifai peá u aʻu ki ha faʻahinga maama fakapoʻuli ʻoku ʻikai fuʻu mamaʻo mei he ngaahi tauʻanga vaká, peá u fanongo ki ha ngungu fakaʻuli ʻi he ʻeá; pea ʻi heʻene sio haké, naʻá ku sio ki ha fakaʻilonga ʻoku taʻalo ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he matapaá ʻoku ʻi ai ha tā valivali hinehina, ʻokú ne fakafofongaʻi vaivai ha jet hangatonu māʻolunga ʻo e ʻuʻulu ʻo e kohu, pea mo e ngaahi lea ko ʻení ʻi lalo —“Ko e Spouter Inn:—Peter Coffin.”

Coffin?—Spouter?—ʻOku ʻikai ke fuʻu fakatuʻutāmaki ʻi he fehokotaki pau ko iá, ko ʻeku fakakaukau ia. Ka ko ha hingoa angamaheni ia ʻi Nantucket, ʻoku nau pehē, pea ʻoku ou mahalo ko e Pita ko ʻeni ʻi hení ko ha tokotaha hikifonua mei ai. ʻI he hā ngali fakapoʻuli ʻaupito ʻa e māmá, pea mo e feituʻú, ʻi he taimi ko iá, naʻe hā ngali fakalongolongo feʻunga, pea mo e kiʻi fale ʻakau ʻoku ʻosi ʻauʻauhá ʻo hangē naʻe ʻave ia ki heni mei he ngaahi toenga ʻo ha vahefonua vela, pea ʻi he fakaʻilonga taʻaloʻaló naʻe ʻi ai ha faʻahinga ngingila masiva ki ai, naʻá ku fakakaukau ko e feituʻu ʻeni ʻoku loffed be the pead, spot for.

Ko ha faʻahinga feituʻu fakaofo ia —ko ha fale motuʻa ʻoku ʻosi ʻa e ʻā, ʻo hangē ʻoku ʻosi ʻa e tafaʻaki ʻe tahá, pea ʻoku punou fakaloloma. Naʻe tuʻu ia ʻi ha tuliki fakapoʻuli māsila, ʻa ia naʻe tauhi ai ʻe he matangi afā ko ia ʻa ʻIulokilitoní ha tangi kovi ange ʻi ha toe taimi fekauʻaki mo e vakapuna masiva ko Paulá naʻe lī. Euroclydon, neongo ia, ko ha zephyr fakafiefia malohi ki ha taha pe 'i he ngaahi matapa, mo hono va'e 'i he hob toasting fakalongolongo ki he mohenga. “ʻI hono fakamāuʻi ʻo e matangi afā ko ia ʻoku ui ko ʻIulokilitoní,” ko e lau ia ʻa ha tokotaha tohi motuʻa —ʻa ia ko ʻene ngaahi ngāué ʻoku ou maʻu ʻa e tatau pē ʻe taha ʻoku kei ʻi aí —“ʻokú ne fai ha faikehekehe fakaofo, pe ʻokú ke sio ki tuʻa ki ai mei ha matapā sioʻata ʻa ia ʻoku ʻi tuʻa kotoa ai ʻa e ʻuha momokó, pe kapau ʻokú ke fakatokangaʻi ia ʻi he matapā sioʻata ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai ʻa e ʻuha momokó mei he, . ‘a ia ko e wight Mate ko e sio‘ata pē ia ‘e taha.” Moʻoni feʻunga, ko ʻeku fakakaukau ia, ʻi he haʻu ʻa e konga ko ʻení ki hoku ʻatamaí —mataʻitohi ʻuliʻuli motuʻa, ʻokú ke fakakaukau lelei. ‘Io, ko e ongo mata ko ‘eni ko e ngaahi matapa sio’ata, pea ko hoku sino ko ‘eni ko e fale. Ko e me'a faka'ofa na'e 'ikai ke nau ta'ofi hake 'a e chinks mo e crannies neongo, pea thrust 'i ha ki'i lint 'i heni mo hena. Ka kuo fu'u tuai ke fai ha fakalelei he taimi ni. Kuo ʻosi ʻa e ʻunivēsí; ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e maka kope, pea naʻe ʻave ʻa e ngaahi siisi ʻi he taʻu ʻe taha miliona kuohilí. Ko Lasalosi masiva ʻi aí, ʻokú ne ngingila hono nifó ʻi he maka ʻo e ʻaá ki hono ʻulumotuʻá, mo lulululu hono ngaahi foʻi nifó ʻaki ʻene tetetete, te ne lava ʻo fakaʻauʻauhifo ʻa e ongo telingá ʻaki ha ngaahi kongokonga, pea ʻai ha foʻi koane ki hono ngutú, pea neongo ia he ʻikai ke ne taʻofi ʻe he meʻa ko iá ʻa e ʻIulokilitoni ʻoku afā. ʻIulokalaitoni! ko e lau ia ʻa e motuʻa ko Dives, ʻi hono takai silika kulokulá—(naʻá ne maʻu ha taha kulokula ange ʻi he hili iá) pooh, pooh! Ko ha pō momoko lelei ē; ko e anga ‘o e ngingila ‘a ‘Olioni; ko e ngaahi maama fakatokelau! Tuku ke nau talanoa ki honau ngaahi ‘ea fakahahake ‘o e fa‘ahita‘u māfaná ‘o e ngaahi fale tauhi‘anga me‘akai ta‘engatá; foaki mai kiate au ʻa e monūʻia ke u ngaohi ʻeku māfana ʻaki ʻeku mahoaʻá.

Ka ko e hā ‘a e fakakaukau ‘a Lasalosí? ‘E lava ke ne fakamāfana‘i hono ongo nima lanu pulū ‘aki hono puke hake kinaua ki he ngaahi maama fakatokelau faka‘ofo‘ofá? ‘Ikai ‘e sai ange ‘a Lasalosi ke ‘i Sumatra ‘i heni? ‘Ikai ‘e mama‘o ange ke ne fakatokoto ia ‘o fakalōloa ‘i he laine ‘o e ‘ekuatoá; ʻio, ʻakimoutolu ʻae ngaahi ʻotua! ‘alu hifo ki he luo afi tonu, koe‘uhi ke ta‘ofi ‘a e ‘uha momoko ko ení?

Ko ʻeni, ke tokoto ʻa Lasalosi ʻo tō ʻi ai ʻi he maka ʻo e ʻaá ʻi muʻa he matapā ʻo e Dives, ʻoku fakaofo ange ʻeni ʻi hano tauʻanga ha ʻaisi ki he taha ʻo e ngaahi moʻunga Moluká. Ka ko Dives ia, ‘okú ne mo‘ui foki ‘o hangē ha Czar ‘i ha palasi ‘aisi ‘oku ngaohi ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi mapuhoi ‘aisi, pea ‘i he‘ene hoko ko ha palesiteni ‘o ha sōsaieti faka‘ehi‘ehi, ‘okú ne inu pē ‘a e lo‘imata momoko ‘o e fānau paeá.

Ka 'oku 'ikai toe 'i ai ha blubbering ko 'eni he taimi ni, 'oku tau 'alu a-whaling, pea 'oku lahi 'a e me'a ko ia 'oku kei hoko mai. Tau toʻo ʻa e ʻaisí mei hotau vaʻe kuo ʻuʻuú, pea vakai pe ko e hā nai ʻa e faʻahinga feituʻu ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e “Spouter” ko ʻení.





VAHE 3. Ko e Fale Tauhi-Spouter.

ʻI hoʻo hū atu ki he Spouter-Inn ko ia ʻoku ʻi ai hono ʻā ʻo e ʻaá, naʻá ke ʻiloʻi ai koe ʻi ha hūʻanga ʻoku fōlahi, maʻulalo, mo fakaʻauʻauhifo mo e ngaahi wainscots motuʻa, ʻo fakamanatu mai ai ha taha ʻo e ngaahi ʻā maluʻi ʻo ha ngaahi vakapuna motuʻa kuo fakahalaiaʻi. 'I he tafa'aki 'e taha na'e tautau ai ha oilpainting lahi 'aupito besmoked faka'aufuli, pea defaced 'a e founga kotoa pe, 'i he ngaahi crosslights ta'etatau 'a ia na'a ke vakai ki ai, na'e toki 'i he ako faivelenga mo ha hokohoko 'o e ngaahi 'a'ahi fakahokohoko ki ai, mo e faka'eke'eke fakalelei 'o e ngaahi kaunga'api, 'e lava ke ke a'u ki ha purpose 'o e i. Ko e faʻahinga fuʻu kakai taʻefakaʻatuʻi ʻo e ngaahi ʻata mo e ngaahi ʻata, ʻa ia ʻi he ʻuluaki taimí naʻá ke meimei fakakaukau ko ha kau ʻātisi kei talavou ʻoku nau fie maʻu lahi, ʻi he taimi ʻo e kau hags ʻo Niu ʻIngilaní, naʻa nau feinga ke fakaʻilongaʻi ʻa e moveuveu fakaʻofoʻofá. Ka ʻi he fakakaukau lahi mo fakamātoato, mo e toutou fakakaukauloto, pea tautautefito ki hono lī ʻo fakaava ʻa e kiʻi matapā sioʻatá ki mui ʻi he hūʻangá, ʻokú ke faifai pea aʻu ki he fakamulituku ko ha fakakaukau pehē, neongo ʻene vao, mahalo ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu taʻefakapotopoto.

Ka ko e meʻa naʻá ne fakatupu puputuʻu mo fakatupu puputuʻu lahi taha kiate koé ko ha fuʻu meʻa lōloa, limber, portentous, ʻuliʻuli ʻo ha meʻa ʻoku ʻalu holo ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e fakatātaá ʻi ha ngaahi laine lanu pulū, fakapoʻuli, mo fakahangatonu ʻe tolu ʻoku ʻalu holo ʻi ha ʻisite taʻehingoa. Ko ha boggy, soggy, squitchy 'ata mo'oni, fe'unga ke faka'uli ha tangata manavasi'i fakahoha'asi. Ka naʻe ʻi ai ha faʻahinga ʻo e maʻolunga taʻefakangatangata, vaeua-aʻusia, taʻe-faʻa-fakakaukauʻi fekauʻaki mo ia naʻá ne fairly froise koe ki ai, kae ʻoua kuó ke fai taʻefakakaukauʻi ha fuakava mo koe ke ke ʻiloʻi ʻa e ʻuhinga ʻo e tā valivali fakaofo ko iá. Ever and anon ha fakakaukau ngingila, ka, 'oiauē, kākā 'e dart koe 'o fou atu.—Ko e Tahi 'Uli'uli ia 'i ha matangi 'o e vaeua po'uli.—Ko e tau ta'efakanatula ia 'o e ngaahi 'elemeniti 'uluaki 'e fa.—Ko ha heath 'oku pahū.—Ko ha faka'ali'ali 'o e fa'ahita'u momoko 'o e Hyperborean.—Ko e ice-up 'a e vaitafe. Ka naʻe faifai pea tukulolo ʻa e ngaahi fakakaukau fakaʻofoʻofa kotoa ko ʻení ki he meʻa fakaʻilonga ʻe taha ko ia ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e fakatātaá. Naʻe ʻiloʻi ia ʻi ha taimi, pea naʻe mahino kotoa ʻa e toengá. Kae tuku; ‘oku ‘ikai ‘okú ne ma‘u ha ki‘i faitatau mo ha fu‘u ika lahi? na‘a mo e fu‘u levaiatani tonu?

Ko hono moʻoní, naʻe hangē ʻeni ʻa e tisaini ʻa e ʻātisí: ko ha teolia fakaʻosi ʻaʻaku, ʻo makatuʻunga ʻi ha konga ʻi he ngaahi fakakaukau fakatahataha ʻa e kakai taʻumotuʻa tokolahi ne u fetalanoaʻaki mo kinautolu ʻi he kaveingá. ʻOku fakafofongaʻi ʻe he fakatātaá ha Cape-Horner ʻi ha afā lahi; ko e vaka vaeua-fakavaʻe ʻoku weltering ʻi ai mo hono ngaahi pou ʻe tolu kuo veteki ʻoku ʻasi pē; pea ko ha tafola ‘ita, ‘okú ne fakataumu‘a ke puna ma‘a ‘i he vaká, ‘okú ne ‘i he ngāue lahi ‘aupito ‘o hono tutuki ia ‘i he ‘ulu-pou ‘e tolú.

Naʻe tautau kotoa ʻa e ʻā fehangahangai ʻo e hūʻanga ko ʻení ʻaki ha ngaahi foʻi ʻakau mo e ngaahi tao fakalilifu fakapangani. Ko e ni‘ihi na‘e seti matolu ‘aki ‘a e nifo ngingila ‘o hangē ha ngaahi hele ‘aivolí; ko e ni‘ihi na‘e fakapipiki ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi no‘o ‘o e fulufulu‘i tangatá; pea ko e taha na‘e fōtunga hangē ha heletaá, mo ha fu‘u nima lahi ‘oku tafi takai ‘o hangē ko e konga na‘e ngaohi ‘i he mohuku kuo toki tu‘usi ‘e ha me‘angāue tu‘usi nima lōloa. Naʻá ke tetetete ʻi hoʻo sio fakamamaʻú, peá ke fifili pe ko e hā ha meʻa kai tangata mo e manu fekai fakalilifu naʻe mei lava ʻo ʻalu ki ha ututaʻu mate ʻaki ha meʻangāue hacking mo fakalilifu pehē. Na‘e fefiofi mo e ngaahi me‘á ni ‘a e ngaahi tao tafola motu‘a ‘oku ‘uli‘uli mo e ngaahi fo‘i heleta ‘oku motumotu kotoa mo faka‘au‘auhifo. Ko e ni‘ihi ko e ngaahi me‘atau talanoa. ʻI he tao lōloa ko ʻeni ʻi ha taimi, ʻa ia kuo ʻosi fakaʻauʻauhifo he taimí ni, ʻi he taʻu ʻe nimangofulu kuohilí naʻe tāmateʻi ai ʻe Nētane Sueni ha tafola ʻe hongofulu mā nima ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e hopo ʻa e laʻaá mo e tō ʻa e laʻaá. Pea ko e foʻi ʻakau ko iá —ʻo hangē ha meʻa fakaʻauha ʻo e ʻakaú he taimí ni —naʻe lī ia ʻi he ngaahi tahi ʻo Siavaní, pea hola mo ia ʻe ha tafola, hili ha ngaahi taʻu mei ai naʻe tāmateʻi ia ʻi he matātahi ʻo e Keipi ʻo Palanikó. Naʻe hū mai ʻa e ʻuluaki ukameá ʻo ofi ki he hiku, pea, ʻo hangē ha tuitui taʻefiemālie ʻoku nofo ʻi he sino ʻo ha tangatá, naʻá ne fononga ʻi ha fute ʻe fāngofulu kakato, pea faifai pea ʻiloʻi ʻoku fakahū ʻi he ʻuʻuú.

ʻI hoʻo kolosi ʻi he hūʻanga fakapoʻuli ko ʻení, pea hoko atu ʻi he hala ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e ʻā maʻulaló —ʻo motuhi ʻa e meʻa ʻi he kuonga muʻá ʻoku pau ko ha fuʻu ʻaofi afi ʻi he lotolotongá ʻoku ʻi ai ha ngaahi afi takatakai kotoa —ʻokú ke hū atu ki he loki ʻo e kakaí. Ko ha feituʻu ʻoku kei fakapoʻuli ange ʻeni, ʻoku ʻi ai ha ngaahi ʻakau ponderous maʻulalo ʻi ʻolunga, mo ha ngaahi laupapa motuʻa ʻoku ngingila ʻi lalo, ʻa ia te ke meimei fakakaukau naʻá ke laka ʻi ha ngaahi cockpits ʻo ha ngaahi vaka motuʻa, tautautefito ki ha pō ʻoku ʻuʻulu pehē, ʻi he taimi naʻe lulululu fakaʻitaʻi ai ʻa e vaka motuʻa ko ʻeni ʻoku taula ʻi he tuliki. Naʻe tuʻu ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá ha tēpile lōloa, maʻulalo, hangē ha fata ʻoku ʻufiʻufi ʻaki ha ngaahi puha sioʻata kuo mafesifesi, ʻoku fonu ʻi ha ngaahi meʻa efuefu ʻoku ʻikai ke faʻa maʻu naʻe tānaki mei he ngaahi tuliki mamaʻo taha ʻo e māmani lahi ko ʻení. ‘Oku tu‘u mei he tuliki mama‘o ange ‘o e lokí ha ‘ana ‘oku hā fakapo‘uli—ko e paá—ko ha feinga ta‘efaka‘apa‘apa ki ha ‘ulu ‘o ha tafola to‘omata‘u. Pe ko e hā pē ha me‘a ‘e hoko, ‘oku tu‘u ai ‘a e fu‘u hui faka‘ā ‘o e kauvae ‘o e tafola, ‘o fu‘u fālahi, ‘e meimei faka‘uli nai ai ha kosi ‘i lalo ai. ʻOku ʻi loto ha ngaahi fata ʻoku ʻikai ke lelei, ʻoku fakahokohoko takai mo e ngaahi decanter motuʻa, ngaahi hina, ngaahi foʻi hina; pea ʻi he ngaahi nifo ko ia ʻo e fakaʻauha vave, ʻo hangē ko ha toe Siona fakamalaʻiaʻi (ko e hingoa moʻoni naʻa nau ui ia ʻaki iá), ʻoku femoʻuekina ai ha kiʻi motuʻa mae, ʻa ia, koeʻuhí ko ʻenau paʻangá, ʻokú ne fakatau atu ʻi he mamafa ki he kau folaú ʻa e ngaahi deliriums mo e mate.

‘Oku fakalielia ‘a e ngaahi ipu ‘okú ne lilingi ki ai ‘ene koná. Neongo 'a e ngaahi silini mo'oni 'o 'ikai-'i loto, 'a e villanous lanumata goggling sio'ata kākā tapered hifo ki ha lalo kākā. Ko e ngaahi meridian fakafehoanaki ‘oku peck ta‘efaka‘apa‘apa ki he sio‘atá, ‘oku nau ‘ātakai‘i ‘a e ngaahi ipu ‘o e ngaahi va‘e ko ení. Fakafonu ki he faka‘ilonga ko ení , pea ko ho‘o totongí ko ha sēniti pē; ki he meʻá ni ha sēniti lahi ange; pea pehē atu ki he ipu sio‘ata fonu—ko e me‘afua ‘o e Cape Horn, ‘a ia te ke lava ‘o ngutu hifo ‘i ha silingi.

ʻI heʻeku hū atu ki he feituʻú ne u maʻu ha kau toutai kei talavou tokolahi ʻoku nau fakatahataha ʻi ha tēpile, ʻo nau sivisiviʻi ʻi ha maama ʻoku ʻikai fuʻu lahi ʻa e ngaahi fakatātā ʻo e skrimshander . Naʻá ku kumi ki he ʻeiki ʻo e ʻapí, pea ʻi heʻeku talaange ʻoku ou fakaʻamu ke nofoʻi au mo ha lokí, naʻá ku maʻu ha tali ʻoku fonu hono falé —ʻo ʻikai ko ha mohenga ʻoku ʻikai nofoʻi. “Ka avast,” naʻá ne tānaki mai, ʻo ne tukituki ʻi hono laʻé, “ʻoku ʻikai haʻo fakafepaki ki hono vahevahe ʻo e palanikete ʻo e harpooneer, ʻikai ko ia? ʻOku ou fakakaukau ʻokú ke ʻalu ʻo a-whalin', ko ia ʻoku sai ange ke ke maheni mo e faʻahinga meʻa ko iá.”

Naʻá ku talaange ki ai ʻoku ʻikai ke u teitei saiʻia ke mohe toko ua ʻi ha mohenga; kapau te u fai pehē, ʻe makatuʻunga ia ʻi he tokotaha ʻoku ʻaʻana ʻa e ʻaá, pea kapau ʻoku ʻikai moʻoni haʻane (ʻeiki ʻapí) ha feituʻu kehe maʻaku, pea naʻe ʻikai ke fakafepakiʻi ʻaupito ʻa e ʻaá, ko e hā ʻoku ʻikai ke u ʻeveʻeva holo ai ʻi ha kolo kehe ʻi ha pō ʻoku fuʻu kona ʻaupito, te u kātakiʻi ʻa e vaeua ʻo ha tangata fakaʻapaʻapa.

“Naʻá ku fakakaukau pehē. Sai; tangutu hifo. Kai efiafi?—ʻokú ke fie maʻu kai efiafi? ʻE mateuteu hangatonu ʻa e kai efiafí.”

Naʻá ku tangutu hifo ʻi ha nofoʻanga ʻakau motuʻa, naʻe tongitongi kotoa ʻo hangē ha tangutuʻanga ʻi he Pātí. ʻI he ngataʻanga ʻe tahá naʻe kei teuteuʻi lahi ange ia ʻe ha tā ʻoku ruminating ʻaki ʻene hele siaké, ʻo ne punou hifo ʻo ngāue faivelenga ʻo mamaʻo ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo hono ongo vaʻé. Naʻá ne ʻahiʻahiʻi hono nimá ʻi ha vaka ʻi he lalo folau kakató, ka naʻe ʻikai ke ne fuʻu fakalakalaka lahi, ko ʻeku fakakaukaú ia.

Na‘e faifai pē ‘o ui mai ha toko fā pe toko nima nai ‘o kimautolu ki he‘emau kai ‘i ha loki ofi mai. Naʻe momoko ʻo hangē ko ʻAisilaní —ʻikai ʻaupito ha afi —naʻe pehē ʻe he tokotaha ʻoku ʻaʻana ʻa e ʻapí he ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo totongi ia. ʻIkai ha meʻa ka ko ha ongo teʻelango lolo fakaloloma, takitaha ʻi ha laʻipepa takai. Naʻá ma fain ke button hake homa ngaahi kote manukí, pea puke ki homa loungutú ha ngaahi ipu tī vela ʻaki homa ongo louhiʻi nima ʻoku vaeua ʻaisi. Ka ko e totongi ʻo e meʻalelé naʻe ʻi he faʻahinga mahuʻinga tahá —ʻo ʻikai ko e kakanoʻi manu mo e pateta pē, ka ko e dumplings; langi lelei! dumplings ki he kai efiafí! Ko ha talavou ʻe taha ʻi ha kote puha lanu mata, naʻá ne fakafeangai kiate ia ʻa e ngaahi dumplings ko ʻení ʻi ha founga fakalilifu ʻaupito.

“ʻEku tama,” ko e lea ia ʻa e ʻeiki ʻapí, “te ke maʻu ʻa e misi fakalilifu ki ha sartainty mate.”

“ʻEiki ʻapi,” ko ʻeku muhumuhu atu ia, “ʻikai ko e tangata ʻaiʻangakai ʻo e ʻaá ia?”

“ʻOiauē, ʻikai,” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻo hā ngali fakakata fakatēvolo, “ko e tangata tā-haʻá ko ha tangata lanu ʻuliʻuli. ʻOku ʻikai ke ne teitei kai ʻa e dumplings, ʻoku ʻikai —ʻoku ʻikai ke ne kai ha meʻa ka ko e steaks pē, pea ʻokú ne saiʻia ʻi heʻene tātātaha ke kai.”

“Ko e tēvolo ‘okú ne faí,” ko ‘eku leá ia. “Ko fē ‘a e tokotaha-ha‘apo ko iá? ‘Okú ne ‘i heni?”

“Te ne ʻi heni ʻi ha taimi fuoloa,” ko e talí ia.

Naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo mapukepuke ia, ka naʻe kamata ke u ongoʻi mahaloʻi ʻa e tangata ʻaiʻangakai ʻo e manú “ʻuliʻuli” ko ʻení. Ko e hā pē ha meʻa ʻe hoko, naʻá ku fakapapauʻi kapau ʻe ʻasi mai ʻoku totonu ke ma mohe fakataha, kuo pau ke ne vete hono valá ʻo mohe kimuʻa peá u toki fai iá.

ʻI he ʻosi ʻa e kai efiafí, naʻe foki ʻa e kautahá ki he loki paá, ʻi he ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi pe ko e hā ha toe meʻa te u fai kiate aú, ne u fakakaukau ke fakamoleki ʻa e toenga ʻo e efiafí ko ha tokotaha sio.

ʻI he taimi ní naʻe ongo mai ha longoaʻa fakamoveuveu ʻi tuʻa. ʻI heʻene kamata haké, naʻe tangi ʻa e ʻeiki ʻo e ʻapí, “Ko e kau ngāue ia ʻo e Grampus. Naʻá ku seed ia kuo lipooti ʻi he offing he pongipongí ni; ko ha folau taʻu ʻe tolu, mo ha vaka fonu. Hurrah, tamaiki tangata; ko ʻeni te tau maʻu ʻa e ongoongo fakamuimuitaha mei he Feegees."

Naʻe fanongoa ha luelue ʻo e ngaahi sū tahí ʻi he hūʻangá; naʻe fakaava ʻa e matapaá, pea hū mai ha seti vao ʻo e kau toutaí ʻo feʻunga. Naʻa nau ʻufiʻufi ʻaki honau ngaahi kote uati fulufuluá, pea ʻoku ʻufiʻufi honau ʻulú ʻaki ha ngaahi kofukofu fulufuluʻisipi, ʻoku nau ʻuʻulu kotoa mo fuopotopoto, pea fefeka honau ʻulú ʻi he ʻaisí, naʻa nau hangē ha puna ʻa e fanga pea mei Lapalatoá. Naʻá na toki tūʻuta mai mei hona vaká, pea ko e ʻuluaki fale ʻeni naʻá na hū ki aí. Ko ia ai, ‘oku ‘ikai ha ofo ‘i he‘enau fai ha ‘ā hangatonu ki he ngutu ‘o e tafola—‘a e paá—‘i he taimi na‘e vave ai ‘a e lilingi atu ‘e he ki‘i motu‘a ko Sonasi ngingilá, ‘a ia na‘á ne fakahoko ‘a e ngāué, ‘a e ngaahi brimmers takatakai kotoa. Naʻe lāunga ha taha ʻi ha momoko kovi ʻi hono ʻulú, ʻa ia naʻe fefiofi ai ʻe Siona ha faitoʻo hangē ha pitch ʻo e gin mo e molasses, ʻa ia naʻá ne fuakava ko ha faitoʻo fakatuʻi ki he momoko mo e catarrhs ​​kotoa pē, ʻo ʻikai ke ne tokanga ki he fuoloa ʻo ʻene tuʻú, pe pe ʻoku maʻu ia ʻi he matāfanga ʻo Labraceis, pe ʻi ha fonua ʻea.

Naʻe vave pē ʻa e ʻalu hake ʻa e kava mālohí ki honau ʻulú, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku faʻa hoko naʻa mo e ngaahi topers arrantest naʻe toki tūʻuta mei tahí, pea naʻa nau kamata ke capering fekauʻaki mo e obstreperously taha.

Kae kehe, naʻá ku fakatokangaʻi naʻe kiʻi mamaʻo ha taha ʻo kinautolu, pea neongo naʻe ngali fakaʻamu ke ʻoua naʻá ne maumauʻi ʻa e fiefia ʻa hono kaungā vaká ʻaki hono fofonga fakapotopotó tonu, ka ʻi hono fakakātoá naʻá ne fakaʻehiʻehi mei heʻene fai ha longoaʻa lahi ʻo hangē ko e toengá. Naʻe mahuʻingaʻia ʻiate au ʻa e tangatá ni ʻi he taimi pē ko iá; pea koeʻuhí naʻe tuʻutuʻuni ʻe he ngaahi ʻotua ʻo e tahí ke vave pē ʻene hoko ko hoku kaungā vaká (neongo ko ha kaungā mohe pē, ʻo fakatatau ki he fakamatala ko ʻení), te u feinga heni ke fai ha kiʻi fakamatala kiate ia. Naʻá ne tuʻu ʻo fute ʻe ono kakato hono māʻolungá, mo hono ongo uma fakaʻeiʻeikí, mo ha fatafata ʻo hangē ha puha ʻo e puhá. Kuo teʻeki ai ke u sio ki ha brawn pehē ʻi ha tangata. Naʻe lanu engeenga loloto hono fofongá mo vela, ʻo ne ʻai ke ngingila hono nifo hinehina ʻi he faikehekehé; lolotonga ia ʻoku ʻalu holo ʻi he ʻata loloto ʻo hono matá ha ngaahi manatu ʻe niʻihi ʻa ia naʻe ʻikai ke ngali ʻoange kiate ia ha fiefia lahi. Naʻe fakahā mai ʻe hono leʻó ʻi he taimi pē ko iá ko ha tokotaha Tonga ia, pea mei hono sino leleí, ne u fakakaukau kuo pau pē ko e taha ia ʻo e kau kaka moʻunga māʻolunga ko ia mei he ʻAlepeni ʻi Veisiniá. ʻI he ʻalu hake ʻa e fiefia ʻa hono ngaahi kaungāmeʻá ki hono māʻolungá, naʻe hū atu ʻa e tangatá ni ʻo ʻikai ke fakatokangaʻi ia, pea naʻe ʻikai ke u toe sio kiate ia kae ʻoua kuó ne hoko ko hoku kaungāmeʻa ʻi he tahí. Kae kehe, ʻi ha ngaahi miniti siʻi pē, naʻe misi ia ʻe hono kaungā vaká, pea ʻi heʻene hoko, ʻi ha ʻuhinga, ko ha fuʻu manakoa lahi ʻiate kinautolu, naʻa nau ʻohake ha tangi ʻo pehē “Bulkington! Bulkington! ʻoku ʻi fē ʻa Bulkington?” peá ne lele atu mei he falé ʻo tuli ia.

Ko e taimi hiva nai ia he taimí ni, pea ʻi he ngali meimei fakanatula ʻa e longoaʻa ʻa e lokí hili ʻa e ngaahi fakafiefia fakaʻofoʻofa ko ʻení, naʻá ku kamata ke fakamālōʻiaʻi au ʻi ha kiʻi palani naʻe haʻu kiate au kimuʻa pē ʻi he hūʻanga ʻo e kau toutaí.

ʻOku ʻikai ha tangata ʻe saiʻia ange ke mohe toko ua ʻi ha mohenga. Ko hono mo‘oní, te ke sai‘ia ange ke ‘oua ‘e mohe mo ho tokouá tonu. 'Oku 'ikai keu 'ilo pe 'oku fefe, ka 'oku manako 'a e kakai ke nau fakapulipuli 'i he taimi 'oku nau mohe ai. Pea ʻi heʻene hoko mai ki he mohe mo ha muli taʻeʻiloa, ʻi ha fale talitali kakai muli, ʻi ha kolo muli, pea ko e muli ko iá ko ha tokotaha tā ʻakau, pea ʻoku fakautuutu taʻefakangatangata leva hoʻo ngaahi fakafepakí. Naʻe ʻikai foki ke ʻi ai ha ʻuhinga fakamāmani ke u mohe ai ʻi heʻeku hoko ko ha tangata toutaí ʻo mohe toko ua ʻi ha mohenga, ʻo lahi ange ʻi ha toe taha kehe; he ʻoku ʻikai ke toe mohe toko ua ʻa e kau folaú ʻi ha mohenga ʻi tahi, ʻo hangē ko e mohe ʻa e kau Tuʻi taʻemali ʻi ʻuta. Ke fakapapau'i 'oku nau mohe fakataha kotoa 'i ha fale nofo'anga 'e taha, ka 'oku 'i ai ho'o hama, pea ke 'ufi'ufi 'aki ho'o palanikete, pea ke mohe 'i ho'o kili.

Ko e lahi ange ʻeku fakalaulauloto ki he tangata ʻakau ko ʻení, ko e lahi ange ia ʻeku fakaliliʻa ki he fakakaukau ke mohe mo iá. Na‘e totonu ke fakakaukau ko e hoko ko ha tokotaha tā-ha‘aponé, ko ‘ene līnení pe fulufulu‘isipí, ‘o hangē ko ia ‘e hokó, ‘e ‘ikai ko e me‘a ia ‘oku maau tahá, ‘o pau pē ‘oku ‘ikai ko ha taha ia ‘o e ngaahi me‘a lelei tahá. ʻEiki fakahaofi au, ʻoku ou fakakaukau, kuo pau ko e harpooneer ia, ko e ʻulu-peddler fakaheli. Ka naʻá ku tokoto haohaoa, peá u fakapapauʻi ke ʻoua naʻá ku lea ʻaki ha foʻi lea kae ʻoua kuo leaʻaki au. Naʻá ne puke ha maama ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá, pea mo e ʻulu Nuʻu Sila tatau ko iá ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá, naʻe hū atu ʻa e muli ko iá ki he lokí, pea ʻikai ke ne sio ki he mohengá, ʻo ne tuku ʻene teʻelango ʻi ha mamaʻo lelei meiate au ʻi he falikí ʻi he tuliki ʻe tahá, peá ne kamata leva ke ngāue mamaʻo ʻi he ngaahi maea kuo noʻo ʻo e kato lahi naʻá ku lea ʻaki kimuʻá ʻo pehē ʻoku ʻi he lokí. Naʻá ku vēkeveke kotoa ke sio ki hono fofongá, ka naʻá ne tauhi ia ke fakaʻehiʻehi mei ai ʻi ha taimi lolotonga ʻene ngāue ʻi hono vete ʻo e ngutu ʻo e kató. Kae kehe, na‘e fakahoko ‘a e me‘á ni, na‘á ne tafoki—‘i he taimi, langi lelei! ko ha meʻa fakaʻofoʻofa! Ko ha fofonga pehē! Naʻe lanu ʻuliʻuli, lanu vāleti, kulokula, ʻo pipiki ʻi heni mo hena ʻaki ha ngaahi tapafā lalahi ʻoku fōtunga ʻuliʻuli. ‘Io, ‘oku hangē pē ko ‘eku fakakaukaú, ko ha kaungā mohe fakalilifu ia; kuo ne ʻi ha fuhu, kuo motuhia fakalilifu, pea ko ʻeni ia, mei he toketā tafa pē. Ka ʻi he momeniti ko iá naʻá ne tuʻumālie ʻo tafoki hono fofongá ʻo fakahangatonu ki he māmá, ʻo u sio mahino ai ʻoku ʻikai ke nau lava ʻo hoko ko ha ngaahi palasitaa pipiki ʻi ha meʻa, ʻa e ngaahi tapafā ʻuliʻuli ko ia ʻi hono ongo kouʻahé. Ko e ngaahi ʻuli ia ʻo ha faʻahinga pe kehe. ʻI he kamataʻangá naʻe ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi e meʻa te u fai ki he meʻá ni; ka naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo haʻu kiate au ha fakakaukau ki he moʻoní. Naʻá ku manatuʻi ha talanoa ʻo ha tangata hinehina —ko ha tangata tafola foki —ʻa ia, ʻi heʻene tō ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e kau kai tangatá, naʻe tā ʻe kinautolu. Naʻá ku fakaʻosiʻaki ko e tangata ʻuhila ko ʻení, ʻi he lolotonga ʻo ʻene ngaahi folau mamaʻó, kuo pau pē naʻá ne fetaulaki mo ha meʻa fakaofo tatau. Pea ko e hā ia, ko ʻeku fakakaukau ia, hili iá! Ko hono tu'a pe; ‘e lava ke faitotonu ha tangata ‘i ha fa‘ahinga kili pē. Ka ʻi he hili iá, ko e hā ke fai ʻaki hono lanu taʻefakamāmaní, ko e konga ko iá, ʻoku ou ʻuhinga, ʻoku tokoto takai holo, pea tauʻatāina ʻaupito mei he ngaahi sikuea ʻo e tā-tataú. Ke fakapapau‘i, ‘oku ‘ikai nai ko ha me‘a kehe ia ka ko ha kote lelei ‘o e tanning fakatalopiki; ka naʻe ʻikai ke u teitei fanongo ki ha laʻā vela ʻokú ne fakaʻaiʻai ha tangata hinehina ke hoko ko ha tangata kulokula lanu vāleti. Kae kehe, na‘e te‘eki ai ke u ‘alu ki he Ngaahi Tahi Tongá; pea mahalo na‘e fakatupu ‘e he la‘ā ‘i aí ‘a e ngaahi ola makehe ko ení ki he kilí. Ko ʻeni, lolotonga ʻa e ʻalu atu ʻa e ngaahi fakakaukau kotoa ko ʻení ʻiate au ʻo hangē ha ʻuhilá, naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke fakatokangaʻi au ʻe he tangata ʻakau ko ʻení. Ka, hili haʻane faingataʻaʻia ʻi heʻene fakaava ʻene kató, naʻá ne kamata ke fumbling ʻi ai, pea ʻi he taimi ko iá naʻá ne toʻo mai ha faʻahinga tomahawk, mo ha kato paʻanga kiliʻi sila ʻoku ʻi ai e fulufuluʻi ʻulú. ʻI hono fokotuʻu ʻeni ʻi he puha motuʻa ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e lokí, naʻá ne toʻo leva ʻa e ʻulu Nuʻu Silá —ko ha meʻa fakalilifu feʻunga —pea ne fakapipiki hifo ia ki he kato. Naʻá ne toʻo he taimí ni hono hēkesí —ko ha hēkesi piva foʻou —ʻi heʻeku ofi atu ʻo hivaʻi atu mo ha ʻohovale foʻou. Naʻe ʻikai ha fulufuluʻi ʻulu ʻi hono ʻulú —ʻikai ha taha ke lea ʻaki ʻo ʻikai toe siʻi hifo —ʻikai ha meʻa ka ko ha kiʻi noʻo ʻulu naʻe mioʻi hake ʻi hono laʻé. Naʻe kumi he taimí ni hono ʻulu lanu vāleti fulufuluá ki he māmaní kotoa ʻo hangē ha ʻuluʻulu kuo ʻosi ʻuʻulu. Kapau naʻe ʻikai ke tuʻu ʻa e muli ʻi hoku vahaʻá mo e matapaá, naʻá ku mei vave ange ʻeku hū ki tuʻa mei ai ʻi ha toe taimi naʻá ku hū ai ki ha maʻu meʻatokoni efiafi.

Naʻa mo e meʻa ko iá, ne u fakakaukau ki ha meʻa ʻo e hū ki tuʻa mei he matapaá, ka ko e fungavaka ua ia ʻi muí. ʻOku ʻikai ko ha tokotaha ilifia au, ka ko e hā ke fai ʻaki ʻa e tangata fakaʻuli lanu vāleti ʻulu-peddling ko ʻení naʻe fakalaka fakaʻaufuli ʻi heʻeku mahinó. Ko e taʻeʻiló ʻa e mātuʻa ʻo e manavaheé, pea ʻi heʻeku ʻikai ke u fuʻu nonplussed mo puputuʻu ʻaupito fekauʻaki mo e muli ko iá, ʻoku ou fakahaaʻi naʻá ku manavasiʻi lahi he taimí ni kiate ia ʻo hangē ko e tēvoló tonu naʻá ne hū mai ki hoku lokí ʻi he poʻulí. Ko hono moʻoní, naʻá ku fuʻu manavasiʻi ʻaupito ʻiate ia ʻo ʻikai ke u vaʻinga feʻunga ʻi he taimi pē ko iá ke u lea kiate ia, mo fie maʻu ha tali fakafiemālie fekauʻaki mo e meʻa naʻe ngali taʻefakamaama ʻiate iá.

Lolotonga iá, naʻá ne hoko atu ʻa e pisinisi ʻo e vete valá, pea faifai peá ne fakahaaʻi hono fatafatá mo hono ongo nimá. ʻI heʻeku moʻuí, naʻe fakaʻilongaʻi ʻa e ngaahi konga ʻufiʻufi ko ʻeni ʻo ia ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi tapafā tatau mo hono fofongá; ko hono tuʻá foki, naʻe ʻi he kotoa ʻo e ngaahi sikuea fakapoʻuli tatau; naʻe hangē naʻá ne ʻi ha Tau ʻo e Taʻu ʻe Tolungofulú, pea naʻá ne toki hola mei ai mo ha kofukofu palasitaa pipiki. Ka na‘e toe lahi ange, na‘e faka‘ilonga‘i ‘a hono ongo va‘é tonu, ‘o hangē ‘oku lele hake ha konga ‘o e fanga poto lanu mata ‘uli‘ulí ‘i he ngaahi fu‘u ‘akau ‘o e fanga ki‘i paame kei talavoú. Naʻe ʻosi mahino ʻaupito he taimí ni kuo pau pē ko ha manu fekai fakalielia pe ko ha taha kehe naʻe fakafolau ʻi ha vakapuna ʻi ha tangata toutai tafola ʻi he Ngaahi Tahi Tongá, pea ko ia naʻá ne tūʻuta ai ʻi he fonua Kalisitiane ko ʻení. Naʻá ku tetetete ʻi heʻeku fakakaukau ki aí. Ko ha tokotaha fakatau ‘ulu foki—mahalo ko e ‘ulu ‘o hono fanga tokouá tonu. Mahalo te ne toʻo ha fakaʻofoʻofa ki heʻeku —hēvani! vakai ki he tomahawk ko ia!

Ka naʻe ʻikai ha taimi ke tetetete ai, he ko ʻeni naʻe ʻalu ʻa e manu fekaí ʻo fai ha meʻa naʻá ne fakamānakoʻi kakato ʻeku tokangá, pea fakalotoʻi au kuo pau pē ko ha hiteni moʻoni ia. ʻI heʻene ʻalu ki heʻene grego mamafá, pe wrapall, pe dreadnaught, ʻa ia naʻá ne tautau kimuʻa ʻi ha seá, naʻá ne fufulu ʻi he ngaahi kato, peá ne fakatupu ʻi ha taimi lōloa ha kiʻi ʻīmisi fakaʻauʻausino fie ʻilo mo ha foʻi ʻuhiki ʻi hono tuʻá, pea ʻoku lanu tonu pē ia ʻi ha pēpē Kongo ʻaho ʻe tolu. ‘I he‘eku manatu‘i ‘a e ‘ulu na‘e faka‘auhá, ‘i he ‘uluaki taimí na‘á ku meimei fakakaukau ko e manikin ‘uli‘uli ko ení ko ha pēpē mo‘oni ia na‘e tauhi ‘i ha founga meimei tatau. Ka ʻi heʻeku sio ʻoku ʻikai ʻaupito ke fuʻu limber, pea ʻoku ngingila lahi ʻo hangē ha ʻēponi kuo fakangingilá, ne u fakaʻosiʻaki ʻoku pau pē ʻoku ʻikai ko ha meʻa kehe ia ka ko ha ʻaitoli ʻakau, ʻa ia naʻe fakamoʻoniʻi moʻoni ia. He ko ʻeni ʻoku ʻalu hake ʻa e manu fekaí ki he feituʻu afi ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa ʻi aí, pea ʻi heʻene toʻo ʻa e laupapa afi kuo ʻosi fakapepá, ʻokú ne fokotuʻu ʻa e kiʻi ʻīmisi ko ʻeni ʻoku ʻi ai hono tuʻunga ʻo e hunch, ʻo hangē ha tenpin, ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ngaahi andirons. Naʻe fuʻu sooty ʻaupito ʻa e ngaahi ʻā ʻo e chimney mo e ngaahi piliki kotoa pē ʻi lotó, ko ia ne u fakakaukau ai naʻe ngaohi ʻe he afi-afi ko ʻení ha kiʻi faletapu pe falelotu feʻunga ʻaupito ki hono ʻaitoli Kongo.

Naʻá ku fakaʻauʻauhifo leva hoku matá ki he ʻīmisi naʻe fufū vaeuá, ʻo ongoʻi ka naʻe ʻikai ke u fiemālie ʻi he lolotonga ko iá —ke sio ki he meʻa ʻe hokó. ʻUluakí ʻokú ne toʻo mai ha ngaahi foʻi ʻakau ʻoku liunga ua nai mei hono kato grego, ʻo ne fokotuʻu fakalelei ia ʻi muʻa ʻi he ʻaitoli; peá ne hili leva ha kiʻi konga pisikete vaka ʻi ʻolunga pea ʻai ʻa e ulo mei he māmá, naʻá ne tutu ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻakaú ke hoko ko ha ulo feilaulau. ʻI he lolotongá ni, hili ha ngaahi puke fakavavevave lahi ki he afí, pea kei toʻo fakavavevave ange hono ongo louhiʻi nimá (ʻa ia naʻe hangē naʻá ne vela kovi kinautolú), naʻe faifai peá ne lavameʻa ʻi hono toʻo mai ʻa e pisiketí; peá ne kiʻi puhiʻi atu ʻa e velá mo e efuefuʻi ʻakaú, naʻá ne fai ha tuʻuaki fakaʻapaʻapa ʻo e meʻa ko iá ki he kiʻi negro. Ka naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke saiʻia ʻa e kiʻi tēvoló ʻi ha faʻahinga totongi mōmoa peheé; na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito te ne ue‘i hono loungutú. Naʻe ʻalu fakataha ʻa e ngaahi fakakata ngali kehe kotoa ko ʻení mo ha ngaahi longoaʻa guttural ʻoku kei ngali kehe ange mei he tokotaha lotu, ʻa ia naʻe hangē naʻá ne lotu ʻi ha hiva-hiva pe hivaʻi ha faʻahinga saame fakapangani pe ko ha meʻa kehe, lolotonga ia naʻe luelue holo hono fofongá ʻi ha founga taʻefakanatula tahá. ʻI heʻene faifai pea tāmateʻi ʻa e afí, naʻá ne toʻo hake ʻa e ʻaitolí ʻo fuʻu taʻefakaʻeiʻeiki, peá ne toe ʻai ia ʻi hono kato grego ʻo taʻetokanga ʻo hangē haʻane tangata sipoti ʻokú ne ʻai ha foʻi ʻakau mate.

Naʻe fakalahi ʻe he ngaahi meʻa fakaofo kotoa ko ʻení ʻeku taʻefiemālié, pea ʻi heʻeku sio kiate ia ʻokú ne fakahaaʻi he taimí ni ha ngaahi fakaʻilonga mālohi ʻo hono fakaʻosi ʻene ngaahi ngāue fakapisinisí, pea puna mo au ki hoku mohengá, ne u fakakaukau kuo taimi, he taimí ni pe ʻikai, kimuʻa pea toki tāmateʻi e māmá, ke u motuhi ʻa e sipele ʻa ia naʻá ku fuoloa ʻeku haʻi aí.

Ka ko e vahaʻataimi naʻá ku fakamoleki ʻi hono fakakaukauʻi ʻo e meʻa ke lea ʻakí, ko ha vahaʻataimi fakatupu mate ia. ʻI heʻene toʻo hake ʻene tomahawk mei he tēpilé, naʻá ne sivisiviʻi hono ʻulú ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou, peá ne toki puke ia ki he māmá, ʻo ʻi hono ngutú ʻi he meʻa puké, naʻá ne puhiʻi mai ha ngaahi ʻao lalahi ʻo e kohu tapaká. ʻI he momeniti hono hokó naʻe mate ʻa e māmá, pea naʻe puna mai ʻa e tangata kai tangata vao ko ʻení, ko e tomahawk ʻi hono vahaʻa ʻo hono nifó, ʻo mohe mo au. Naʻá ku hivaʻi leʻolahi, naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo tokoniʻi ia he taimí ni; pea ʻi heʻene ʻoatu ha ngungu fakafokifā ʻo e ofo naʻá ne kamata ke ongoʻi au.

ʻI heʻeku fakaʻiseʻisa ʻi ha meʻa, ʻo ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi pe ko e hā, ne u fakateka atu meiate ia ʻo fakafepakiʻi e ʻā, peá u toki fakaʻaiʻai ia, pe ko hai pe ko e hā pē ia, ke ne fakalongolongo, pea tuku ke u tuʻu hake ʻo toe tutu ʻa e māmá. Ka naʻe fakafiemālieʻi au ʻe heʻene ngaahi tali guttural ʻi he taimi pē ko iá ʻo ʻikai ke ne mahinoʻi lelei ʻeku ʻuhingá.

“Ko hai-e debel koe?”—na‘á ne faifai pē ‘o pehē—“‘oku ‘ikai te ke lea-e, dam-me, ‘oku ou tāmate‘i-e.” Pea ʻi heʻene lea peheé naʻe kamata ke tupulaki ʻa e tomahawk kuo fakamaama ʻo takatakai ʻiate au ʻi he poʻulí.

“ʻEiki kelekele, koeʻuhí ko e ʻOtuá, Pita Kofini!” ko e kaila ia ʻa I. “ʻEiki kelekelé! Leʻo! Puha mate! Kau ʻāngelo! fakahaofi au!”

“Lea-e! talamai-ee kiate au pe ko hai-ee ke, pe dam-me, te u tamateʻi-e!” naʻe toe ngungu ʻa e tangata kai tangatá, lolotonga ia ʻoku fakamovetevete ʻe heʻene ngaahi tupu fakalilifu ʻo e tomahawk ʻa e efuefu tapaka vela ʻo takatakai ʻiate au kae ʻoua kuó u fakakaukau ʻe vela ʻeku līnení. Kae fakafeta'i ki langi, 'i he momeniti ko ia na'e ha'u ai 'a e landlord ki he loki 'o maama 'i hono nima, pea puna mei he mohenga na'a ku lele hake kiate ia.

“ʻOua naʻá ke manavasiʻi he taimí ni,” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻo ne toe malimali, “ʻE ʻikai ke maumauʻi ʻe Queequeg ʻi hení ha fulufuluʻi ʻulu ʻo ho ʻulú.”

“Tuku hoʻo malimali,” ko ʻeku kailá ia, “pea ko e hā naʻe ʻikai ke ke talamai ai kiate au ko e tokotaha kai tangata ʻo e ʻaá ʻi heli ko iá?”

“Naʻá ku fakakaukau ʻoku mou ʻiloʻi ia;—ʻikai naʻá ku talaatu kiate kimoutolu, ko ha ʻulu peddlin ia ʻi he koló?—ka ke toe tafoki flukes pea ʻalu ʻo mohe. Queequeg, vakai ki heni—ʻokú ke sabbee au, ʻoku ou sabbee—ʻoku mohe ʻa e tangatá ni ʻiate koe—ʻokú ke sabbee?”

“Me sabbee lahi” —ko e ngungu ia ʻa Kuiki, ʻo ne puhiʻi ʻene paipá pea tangutu hake ʻi hono mohengá.

“You gettee in,” ko ʻene tānaki atu ia, ʻo ne fakaʻilonga mai kiate au ʻaki ʻene tomahawk, pea lī ʻa e kofú ki he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá. Naʻá ne fai moʻoni ʻeni ʻo ʻikai ngata pē ʻi ha founga sivile ka ʻi ha founga angaʻofa mo ʻofa moʻoni. Naʻá ku tuʻu ʻo sio kiate ia ʻi ha kiʻi taimi. Mo ʻene ngaahi tā kotoa pē naʻá ne ʻi he fakakātoa ko ha tokotaha kai tangata maʻa mo fakaʻofoʻofa. Ko e hā ʻa e fuʻu longoaʻa kotoa ko ʻeni kuó u faí, ko ʻeku fakakaukau ia kiate aú —ko e tangata ʻa e tangatá ʻo hangē pē ko aú: ʻoku ʻi ai ʻene ʻuhinga lahi ke manavahē ai kiate au, ʻo hangē pē ko ʻeku manavahē kiate iá. Sai ange ke mohe mo ha kai tangata konā ʻi ha Kalisitiane konā.

"ʻEiki ʻo e ʻapí," ko ʻeku leá ia, "talaange ke ne tuku ʻene tomahawk ʻi aí, pe paipa, pe ko ha meʻa pē ʻokú ke ui ki aí; talaange ke ne tuku ʻene ifi tapaká, ko hono fakanounoú, pea te u liliu mo ia. Ka ʻoku ʻikai ke u saiʻia ke ʻi ai ha tangata ʻe ifi ʻi hoku mohengá. ʻOku fakatuʻutāmaki. ʻIkai ngata aí, ʻoku ʻikai ke u maluʻi."

ʻI hono talaange ʻeni kia Kuikeí, naʻá ne talangofua leva, peá ne toe fakaʻilongaʻi anga fakaʻapaʻapa mai ke u heka ki hoku mohengá —ʻo ne fakatakataka ki he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo hangē pē ko ʻene pehē —“ʻE ʻikai te u ala ki ha vaʻe ʻo kimoutolu.”

Naʻá ku pehē ange, “Po lelei, ʻeiki ʻapi, ʻe lava ke ke ʻalu.”

Naʻá ku tafoki ki loto, pea ʻikai ke u toe mohe lelei ange ʻi heʻeku moʻuí.
