“Ko hai naʻá ne mei fakakaukau ki ai, Flask!” ko e kaila ia ʻa Situpi; “kapau naʻe ʻi ai pē hoku vaʻe ʻe taha he ʻikai te ke puke au ʻi ha vaka, tukukehe kapau mahalo ke taʻofi ʻa e plug-hole ʻaki hoku louhiʻi vaʻe ʻakaú. ʻOiauē! ko ha motuʻa fakaofo ia!”

‘I he lotolotonga ‘o e kakai poto ‘i he tafola kuo fa‘a fakakikihi‘i pe, ‘i he fakakaukau atu ki he mahu‘inga lahi taha ‘o ‘ene mo‘uí ki he lavame‘a ‘o e folaú, ‘oku totonu ki ha kapiteni toutai tafola ke ne fakatu‘utāmaki‘i ‘a e mo‘ui ko iá ‘i he ngaahi fakatu‘utāmaki mālohi ‘o e tulimanú. Ko ia na‘e fa‘a fakakikihi ‘a e kau sōtia ‘a Tamerlane ‘o lo‘imata‘ia, pe ‘oku totonu ke ‘ave ‘a ‘ene mo‘ui mahu‘inga ko ia ki he matolu taha ‘o e taú.

Ka ‘ia ‘Ēhapi na‘e ma‘u ‘e he fehu‘í ha tafa‘aki kuo fakalelei‘i. ‘I he fakakaukau atu ki he va‘e ua ‘a e tangatá ko ha me‘a faka‘au‘auhifo pē ia ‘i he taimi kotoa pē ‘o e fakatu‘utāmakí; ‘i he fakakaukau ko e tuli ki he fanga tafola ‘oku ‘i he malumalu ma‘u pē ‘o e ngaahi faingata‘a lahi mo makehe; ko e momeniti fakafoʻituitui kotoa pē, ko e moʻoni, ʻoku kau leva ai ha fakatuʻutāmaki; ‘i he ngaahi tu‘unga ko ení ‘oku fakapotopoto nai ki ha tangata pē ‘oku heke ke ne hū ki ha vaka-tafola ‘i he tulimanú? Ko e me'a fakalukufua, kuo pau ke fakakaukau mahino 'a e kau joint-owners 'o e Pequod 'oku 'ikai.

Naʻe ʻiloʻi lelei ʻe ʻĒhapi neongo ʻe siʻisiʻi ʻa e fakakaukau ʻa hono ngaahi kaungāmeʻa ʻi ʻapí ki heʻene hū ki ha vaka ʻi ha ngaahi vicissitudes fakafehoanaki ʻoku ʻikai fakatuʻutāmaki ʻo e tulimanú, koeʻuhí ko e ofi ki he feituʻu ʻoku fai ai ʻa e ngāué mo ʻoatu ʻene ngaahi tuʻutuʻuní ʻi heʻene fakafoʻituituí, ka ki he ʻEikitau ʻĒhapi ke maʻu ha vaka ʻoku vahevahe moʻoni kiate ia ʻa e Kapiteni ʻo e ʻulu kotoa pē— ke tokonaki mai mo ha kau tangata ʻe toko nima kehe, ʻo hangē ko e kau ngāue ʻo e vaka tatau ko iá, naʻá ne ʻiloʻi lelei ʻoku ʻikai ʻaupito ke hū ʻa e ngaahi fakakaukau nima-homo peheé ki he ʻulu ʻo e kau ʻea ʻo e Pequod. Ko ia naʻe ʻikai ke ne kole ha kau ngāue vaka meiate kinautolu, pe naʻe ʻikai ke ne fakaʻilongaʻi ʻi ha faʻahinga founga ʻene ngaahi holí ʻi he ʻulu ko iá. Ka neongo ia naʻá ne fai ha ngaahi meʻa fakatāutaha ʻaʻana ʻo uesia ʻa e meʻa kotoa ko iá. Kae ʻoua kuo pulusi ʻa e ʻilo ʻa Cabaco, naʻe siʻisiʻi ʻa e tomuʻa ʻiloʻi ia ʻe he kau folaú, neongo ke fakapapauʻi ʻa e taimi, hili ha kiʻi taimi ʻi tuʻa mei he taulangá, kuo fakaʻosi ʻe he nima kotoa pē ʻa e pisinisi angamaheni ʻo hono fakafeʻungaʻi ʻo e ngaahi vaka tafola ki he ngāué; 'i he taimi 'oku 'i ai ha taimi hili 'eni 'a 'Ehapi na'e he taimi ni pea toki ma'u bestirring ia 'i he me'a 'o e ngaohi 'o e thole-pins 'aki hono nima 'o'ona ki he me'a na'e fakakaukau ko e taha 'o e ngaahi vaka faka'aonga'i, pea na'a mo e solicitously motuhi 'a e fanga ki'i skewers 'akau, 'a ia 'i he taimi 'oku lele ai 'a e laine 'oku pinned 'i he funga 'o e groove 'i he kaufana na'e ko ia kotoa 'eni ha extra 'i he, kote ‘o e ‘ufi‘ufi ‘i he lalo vaká, ‘o hangē ‘oku ‘ai ai ia ke ne matu‘uaki lelei ange ‘a e tenge mata‘itofe ‘o hono va‘e ‘aivolí; pea mo e hohaʻa foki naʻá ne fakahaaʻi ʻi hono faʻu tonu ʻo e laupapa ʻo e ʻulú, pe ko e clumsy cleat, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku ui ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi, ko e konga fakahosite ʻi he kaufana ʻo e vaká ki hono fakamālohiʻi ʻo e tuí ʻi he lele pe hokaʻi ʻo e tafola; ʻi hono fakatokangaʻi ʻa e tuʻo lahi ʻene tuʻu hake ʻi he vaka ko iá ʻo fakapipiki hono tuliki tokotahá ʻi he foʻi ʻaofi ʻoku vaeua takai ʻi he cleat, pea ʻaki ʻa e kiʻi ʻuʻulu ʻo e tufunga ʻakaú ʻi heni pea kiʻi fakatonutonu ia ʻi ai; ko e ngaahi meʻá ni kotoa, ʻoku ou pehē, naʻá ne langaʻi hake ha mahuʻingaʻia mo ha fie ʻilo lahi ʻi he taimi ko iá. Ka naʻe meimei fakakaukau ʻa e tokotaha kotoa pē ko e tokanga teuteu makehe ko ʻeni ʻia ʻĒhapí kuo pau ke ʻi he fakakaukau pē ki he tuli fakaʻosi ʻo Moby Dick; he naʻá ne ʻosi fakahā ʻene fakakaukau ke tuli fakafoʻituitui ʻa e manu fekai fakamatelie ko iá. Ka he ʻikai lava ʻe ha taha ʻo poupouʻi ha ʻea taʻetokanga fekauʻaki mo Fedallah. Ko ha meʻamoʻui ia ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku sio pē ki ai ʻa e kakai sivilaise, fakaʻapi ʻi he feituʻu ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e ʻeá ʻi heʻenau misí, pea ko ia ka ʻoku fakapoʻuli; ka ko e ngaahi me‘a hangē ko ia ‘i he taimí ni pea ‘i he taimi ko iá ‘oku nau lele holo ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e ngaahi komiunitī ‘Ēsia ‘oku ‘ikai ke liliú, tautefito ki he ngaahi motu ‘o e Hahaké ki he hahake ‘o e konitinēnití—ko e ngaahi fonua ‘oku ‘ikai lava ke liliu, ‘a ia na‘a mo e ngaahi ‘aho fakaonopooni ko ení ‘oku nau kei tauhi ‘a e lahi taha ‘o e ghostly aboriginalness ‘o e māmaní mo e ngaahi to‘utangata ‘uluaki ‘o e dille, . ko e kakai kotoa pē ko hono hakó, ‘o ‘ikai te nau ‘ilo‘i ‘a e feitu‘u na‘á ne ha‘u mei aí, na‘a nau fesiofaki ‘o hangē ha ngaahi faka‘ānaua mo‘oní, pea nau ‘eke ki he la‘aá mo e māhiná pe ko e hā na‘e fakatupu ai kinauá pea ko e hā hono taumu‘á; neongo ia, fakatatau kia Sēnesi, na‘e feohi mo‘oni ‘a e kau ‘āngeló mo e ngaahi ‘ofefine ‘o e tangatá, ko e kau tēvoló foki, tānaki atu ki ai ‘a e kau Lāpai ta‘efakalao, ‘o nau faka‘aonga‘i ‘a e ngaahi ‘ofa fakamāmaní.





VAHE 51. Koe Laumalie-Spout.

Naʻe ʻosi atu ha ngaahi ʻaho, ngaahi uike, pea ʻi he folau faingofuá, naʻe tafiʻi māmālie atu ʻe he vaka ʻaivolí ʻa e Pequod ʻi ha ngaahi malaʻe folau vakapuna ʻe fā; ko ia ʻi he ʻOtu Motu ʻAsoá; ʻi he matātahi ʻo Keipi ti Vetesí; ʻi he Peletí (ʻoku ui pehē), ʻi heʻene ʻi he ngutu ʻo e Rio de la Plata; pea mo e Carrol Ground, ko ha feituʻu ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai ha siteiki, ʻoku ʻi ai ha vai, ʻi he fakatonga mei St.

Ko e lolotonga ʻeku lele ʻi he ngaahi vai fakamuimui ko ʻení ʻi ha pō nonga mo maama ʻo e māhiná ʻe taha, ʻa ia naʻe takai holo ai ʻa e ngaahi peaú kotoa ʻo hangē ha ngaahi takainga tohi silivá; pea, ʻi heʻenau ngaahi vela molumalu mo fakaʻauhá, naʻa nau ngaohi ʻa e meʻa naʻe ngali ko ha fakalongolongo siliva, ʻo ʻikai ko ha nofo tokotaha; ʻi ha pō fakalongolongo pehē naʻe sio ki ha vakapuna siliva ʻi muʻa mamaʻo ʻi he ngaahi pupula hinehina ʻi he kaufaná. Naʻe fakamaama ʻe he māhiná, naʻe hā fakasilesitialé; na‘e hā ngali ‘oku tu‘u hake ha ngaahi ‘otua fulufulua mo ngingila mei he tahí. Naʻe ʻuluaki fakamatalaʻi ʻe Fedallah ʻa e vakapuna ko ʻení. He ʻi he ngaahi pō māhina ko ʻení, ko ʻene tōʻongá ia ke heka ki he ʻulu ʻo e pou lahí, pea tuʻu ʻo sio ki tuʻa ʻi ai, ʻi he tonu tatau ʻo hangē pē naʻe ʻahó. ‘I he fakatonga-hahake mei he Keipí, ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘o e Crozetts mama‘ó, ko ha feitu‘u folau lelei ia ki he kau Right Whalemen, na‘e ‘asi mai ai ha folau ‘eve‘eva, ko e Goney (Albatross) ‘i hono hingoá. ʻI heʻene ofi māmālie mai, mei hoku tuʻunga māʻolunga ʻi he ʻuluʻi pou ʻi muʻá, ne u maʻu ha vakai lelei ki he sio ko ia naʻe fakaofo ʻaupito ki ha tyro ʻi he ngaahi toutai ʻoseni mamaʻó —ko ha toutai tafola ʻi tahi, pea fuoloa ʻene ʻikai ʻi ʻapí.

Neongo ‘i he lolotonga ‘o ‘ene ngaahi folau hokohoko kuo pau ke fa‘a fakatokanga‘i ki mu‘a ‘e ‘Ēhapi ha me‘a meimei tatau, ka, ki ha tangata pē ‘oku ‘ikai ke ‘i ai hano ‘uhinga, ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e ngaahi ‘uhinga faka‘ulia ‘a e ngaahi me‘a si‘isi‘i tahá.

“Mou luelue atu meiate au, ʻikai ko ia?” ko e lāunga ia ‘a ‘Ēhapí, ‘o ne sio fakamama‘u atu ki he vaí. Naʻe ngali siʻisiʻi pē ʻa e ngaahi leá, ka naʻe fakahaaʻi ʻe he leʻó ha loto-mamahi loloto taʻelavameʻa ʻi he meʻa naʻe fakamoʻoniʻi ʻe he motuʻa valevalé kimuʻa. Ka ʻi heʻene tafoki ki he tangata fakaʻuli vaká, ʻa ia naʻá ne pukepuke ʻa e vaká ʻi he matangí ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimí ni ke fakasiʻisiʻi ʻene laka ki muʻá, naʻá ne kaila ʻi hono leʻo laione motuʻá, —“ʻAlu hake ʻa e taumuʻá!

Takai holo ʻi he māmaní! ʻOku lahi ʻa e meʻa ʻi he leʻo ko iá ke ne ueʻi ha ngaahi ongo pōlepole; ka ʻoku ʻave ki fē ʻa e takai kotoa ko iá? ʻI he ngaahi fakatuʻutāmaki taʻefaʻalaua pē ʻo aʻu ki he tuʻunga tonu naʻa mau kamata mei aí, ʻa ia ko kinautolu naʻa mau tuku ʻi mui ʻoku malu, naʻa nau ʻi muʻa maʻu pē ʻiate kimautolu.

Kapau naʻe hoko ʻa e māmani ko ʻení ko ha teleʻa taʻe ngata, pea ʻi heʻemau folau fakahahaké te mau lava ʻo aʻu taʻengata ki ha ngaahi mamaʻo foʻou, pea ʻiloʻi ha ngaahi meʻa ʻoku melie mo ngali kehe ange ʻi ha toe ʻOtu Motu Saikalati pe ʻOtu Motu ʻo Tuʻi Solomoné, pea ʻoku ʻi ai leva ha ngaahi talaʻofa ʻi he folaú. Ka ʻi he tuli ki he ngaahi misiteli mamaʻo ko ia ʻoku tau misi ki aí, pe ʻi he tuli fakamamahiʻi ʻo e phantom tēvolo ko ia ʻoku, ʻi ha taimi pe ʻe taha, ʻoku luelue ʻi muʻa ʻi he loto ʻo e tangatá kotoa; lolotonga ʻenau tuli ʻa e faʻahinga peheé ʻi he kolope takai ko ʻení, ʻoku nau taki kitautolu ʻi ha ngaahi maze taʻeʻaonga pe ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e halá ʻo tuku kitautolu whelmed.
