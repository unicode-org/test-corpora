ʻI heʻeku ʻalú, ne u fonu ʻi he fakakaukau; ko e meʻa naʻe fakahā fakatuʻupakē mai kiate au ʻo kau kia ʻEikitau ʻĒhapí, naʻá ne fakafonu au ʻaki ha faʻahinga taʻemahino vao ʻo e mamahi fekauʻaki mo iá. Pea ʻi ha faʻahinga founga, ʻi he taimi ko iá, ne u ongoʻi ha kaungāongoʻi mo ha loto mamahi kiate ia, ka he ʻoku ʻikai ke u ʻilo pe ko e hā, tukukehe kapau ko e mole anga-fakamamahi ʻo hono vaʻé. Pea neongo ia naʻá ku ongoʻi foki ha manavahē kehe kiate ia; ka ko e faʻahinga manavahē ko iá, ʻa ia ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo fakamatalaʻi ʻaupito, naʻe ʻikai ko ha manavahē tonu ia; ʻOku ʻikai ke u ʻilo pe ko e hā ia. Ka naʻá ku ongoʻi ia; pea naʻe ʻikai ke ne fakalotosiʻi au kiate ia; neongo naʻá ku ongoʻi taʻekātaki ʻi he meʻa naʻe ngali misiteli ʻiate iá, ʻo fuʻu taʻehaohaoa ʻo hangē ko ia naʻá ku ʻiloʻi ʻi he taimi ko iá. Kae kehe, na‘e faifai pē ‘o ‘ave ‘eku ngaahi fakakaukaú ki ha ngaahi tafa‘aki kehe, ‘o ‘i he taimi fakapo‘uli lolotongá na‘e hē ai ‘a ‘Ēhapi ‘i hoku ‘atamaí.





VAHE 17. Koe Lamatani.

Koeʻuhí he naʻe pau ke hokohoko atu ʻa e Ramadan ʻa Queequeg, pe ʻAukai mo e Fakamāʻí, ʻi he ʻaho kakató, naʻe ʻikai ke u fili ke fakahohaʻasi ia kae ʻoua kuo ofi ki he poʻulí; he ʻoku ou fakaʻamua ʻa e fakaʻapaʻapa lahi taha ki he ngaahi fatongia fakalotu ʻo e tokotaha kotoa pē, ʻo ʻikai ke u tokanga ki heʻene fakakata, pea naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo maʻu ia ʻi hoku lotó ke fakasiʻisiʻi naʻa mo ha fakatahaʻanga ʻo e fanga ʻanité ʻoku nau lotu ki ha toad-stool; pe ko e ngaahi me‘amo‘ui kehe ko ia ‘i he ngaahi feitu‘u pau ‘o hotau māmaní, ‘a ia ‘i ha tu‘unga ‘o e footmanism ‘oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ke ‘i ai hano tatau ‘i he ngaahi palanite kehé, ‘oku nau punou hifo ‘i mu‘a ‘i he sino ‘o ha tokotaha ‘oku ‘a‘ana ‘a e kelekele kuo pekia koe‘uhi pē ko e ngaahi koloa ta‘efakangatangata ‘oku kei ma‘u mo totongi ‘i hono hingoá.

ʻOku ou pehē, ko kitautolu kau Kalisitiane Pelesipitiliane leleí ʻoku totonu ke tau ʻofa ʻi he ngaahi meʻá ni, pea ʻoua naʻa tau fakakaukauʻi ʻoku tau fuʻu māʻolunga ange ʻi he kakai matelie kehé, kau hiteni mo e ngaahi meʻa kehe, koeʻuhí ko ʻenau ngaahi fakakaukau fakavalevale vaeua ʻi he ngaahi kaveinga ko ʻení. Naʻe ʻi ai ʻa Queequeg, he taimí ni, ʻokú ne fakafiefiaʻi moʻoni ʻa e ngaahi fakakaukau fakavalevale taha fekauʻaki mo Yojo mo ʻene Ramadan;—ka ko e hā ʻa e meʻa ko iá? Naʻe fakakaukau ʻa Kuiki ʻokú ne ʻiloʻi ʻa e meʻa naʻá ne fekauʻaki mo iá, ʻoku ou mahalo; naʻe hangē naʻá ne fiemālié; pea tuku ke ne mālōlō ai. Ko ‘emau fakakikihi kotoa pē mo iá ‘e ‘ikai ‘aonga; tuku ke ne ʻi ai, ʻoku ou pehē: pea ʻaloʻofa mai ʻa e Langí kiate kitautolu kotoa pē —ʻa e kau Pelesipiteliane mo e kau Hiteni fakatouʻosi —he ʻoku tau mafesifesi fakalilifu kotoa pē ʻi ha faʻahinga founga fekauʻaki mo e ʻulú, pea ʻoku tau fie maʻu fakaloloma ke fakaleleiʻi.

ʻI he fakaofi ki he efiafí, ʻi heʻeku ongoʻi fakapapauʻi kuo pau ke ʻosi kotoa ʻene ngaahi fakaʻaliʻalí mo e ngaahi ouaú, ne u ʻalu hake ki hono lokí ʻo tukituki ʻi he matapaá; ka ʻoku ʻikai ha tali. Naʻá ku feinga ke fakaava ia, ka naʻe fakamaʻu ia ʻi loto. “Queequeg,” ko ʻeku lea māmālie ia ʻi he ava-kií: —fakalongolongo kotoa. “ʻOku ou pehē atu, Queequeg!ko e hā ʻoku ʻikai ke ke leá ai?Ko au ia﻿—ʻIsimeʻeli.” Ka naʻe kei nofo pē kotoa ʻo hangē ko ia kimuʻá. Naʻe kamata ke u tupu ʻo manavasiʻi. Naʻá ku fakaʻatā kiate ia ha taimi lahi pehē; Naʻá ku fakakaukau mahalo naʻá ne maʻu ha mahaki ʻapoplectic. Naʻá ku sio ʻi he ava-kií; ka ko e matapā ʻoku fakaava ki ha tuliki kehe ʻo e lokí, ko e ʻamanaki ʻo e ava kií ko ha ʻamanaki pikopiko mo fakalilifu pē. Naʻá ku lava pē ʻo sio ki ha konga ʻo e foot-board ʻo e mohengá mo ha laine ʻo e ʻā, ka naʻe ʻikai toe lahi ange. Naʻá ku ʻohovale ʻi heʻeku sio ki he mālōlō ʻi he ʻā ʻa e foʻi ʻakau ʻo e foʻi ʻakau ʻa Kuiki, ʻa ia naʻe toʻo meiate ia ʻe he fineʻeiki ʻo e ʻapí ʻi he efiafi kimuʻá, kimuʻa peá ma heka ki he lokí. ʻOku ngali kehe ia, ko ʻeku fakakaukau ia; ka ʻi ha faʻahinga tuʻunga pē, koeʻuhí ʻoku tuʻu ʻa e foʻi ʻaá ʻi ai, pea ʻoku ʻikai faʻa pe ʻikai ke ne ʻalu ki muli taʻekau ai ia, ko ia ai kuo pau ke ne ʻi loto heni, pea ʻoku ʻikai ha fehālaaki ʻe ala hoko.

“Kuikui!—Kuikui!”—‘oku nau nonga kotoa. Kuo pau pē naʻe ʻi ai ha meʻa naʻe hoko. ʻApoleksi! Naʻá ku feinga ke fakaava ʻa e matapaá; ka naʻá ne fakafepakiʻi fefeka. ʻI heʻeku lele hifo ʻi he sitepú, ne u fakahā vave ʻeku ngaahi mahaloʻí ki he fuofua tokotaha ne u fetaulaki mo iá —ko e fefine ngāue ʻi he lokí. “La! la!” naʻá ne kaila, “Naʻá ku fakakaukau ʻoku pau pē ʻoku ʻi ai ha meʻa ʻoku hoko. Naʻá ku ʻalu ʻo teuteuʻi ʻa e mohengá hili ʻa e maʻu meʻatokoni pongipongí, pea naʻe lokaʻi ʻa e matapaá; pea ʻikai ha kiʻi mouse ʻe ongo mai; pea kuo fuʻu fakalongolongo pē ia talu mei ai. Ka naʻá ku fakakaukau, mahalo, kuó mo fakatou ʻalu atu ʻo lokaʻi hoʻomo uta ki loto ke tauhi malu. La!— laMisseyr!' apoplexy!”—pea ʻi he ngaahi tangi ko ʻení, naʻá ne lele atu ki he loki kuí, ʻo u muimui atu.

Naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo hā mai ʻa Misa Husi, mo ha ipu musita ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá mo ha vineka-kulueti ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá, ʻi heʻene toki mavahe mei he ngāue ʻo e tokangaʻi ʻo e kau kasitoló, pea ʻokú ne valokiʻi ʻene kiʻi tamasiʻi ʻuliʻulí ʻi he lolotonga ko iá.

“Fale-ʻakau!” naʻá ku kaila, “ko e fē ʻa e hala ki aí? Lele koeʻuhí ko e ʻOtuá, pea ʻomi ha meʻa ke fakaava ʻaki ʻa e matapaá —ʻa e toki!—ʻa e toki! kuó ne maʻu ha stroke; fakafalala ki ai!”—pea ʻi heʻeku pehē naʻá ku toe lele taʻefakapotopoto hake ʻi he sitepú ʻo ʻikai haʻaku meʻa, ʻi he taimi naʻe fakafetongi ai ʻe Misa Hussey ʻa e vigarre andc enti-rune ʻa e vigar. ko hono fofongá.

“Ko e hā e meʻa ʻoku hoko kiate koé, talavou?”

“To‘o mai ‘a e toki! Koe‘uhi ko e ‘Otuá, lele atu ki he toketaá, ko ha taha, lolotonga ia ‘oku ou fakaava ia!”

“Sio ki heni,” ko e lea ia ‘a e fefine ‘oku ‘a‘ana ‘a e ‘apí, ‘o ne tuku hifo fakavavevave ‘a e vineka-kulutí, koe‘uhi ke tau‘atāina ‘a e nima ‘e tahá; “sio ki heni; ʻokú ke talanoa ki hono fakaava ha taha ʻo hoku ngaahi matapaá?”—pea ʻi heʻene peheé naʻá ne puke hoku nimá. “Ko e hā e meʻa ʻoku hoko kiate koé?Ko e hā e meʻa ʻoku hoko kiate koé, kaungā vaka?”

ʻI ha founga nonga, ka ʻi ha founga vave ʻi he lahi tahá, ne u ʻoange kiate ia ke mahino kiate ia ʻa e keisi kotoa. ʻI heʻene pakipaki taʻeʻiloʻi ʻa e vineka-kulutí ki he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo hono ihú, naʻá ne fakakaukauloto ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou; peá ne kalanga leva—“ʻIkai! Kuo teʻeki ai ke u sio ki ai talu ʻeku tuku ia ʻi aí.” ʻI heʻene lele atu ki ha kiʻi loki ʻi lalo he tuʻuʻanga ʻo e sitepú, naʻá ne sio atu ki loto, pea ʻi heʻene foki maí, ʻo ne talamai ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai ha foʻi ʻakau ʻa Kuiki. Naʻá ne tangi, “Kuó ne tāmateʻi ia.” “ʻOku fakaloloma ʻa e toe fai ʻe Stiggs —ʻoku ʻalu ha toe counterpane ʻe taha —ʻofa ʻa e ʻOtuá ki heʻene faʻē masivá! —ʻe hoko ia ko e maumau ʻo hoku falé. ʻOku ʻi ai ha tuofefine ʻo e tamasiʻi masivá? ʻOku ʻi fē ʻa e taʻahine ko iá? — ʻi ai, Peti, ʻalu kia Snarles ko e Valivalí pea talaange ke ne valivali au mo e — no heni a ʻi he lokí;”—ʻe lava pē ke tāmateʻi ʻa e ongo manupuná ʻi he taimi pē ʻe taha. Tāmateʻi? Ke ʻaloʻofa ʻa e ʻEikí ki hono laumālié! Ko e hā ʻa e longoaʻa ko ia ʻi aí? Ko koe, talavou, avast ʻi ai!”

Pea ʻi heʻene lele hake ʻo muimui ʻiate aú, naʻá ne maʻu au ʻi heʻeku toe feinga ke fakaava fakamālohi ʻa e matapaá.

"ʻOku ʻikai ke u fakaʻatā ia; he ʻikai ke u ʻai ke maumauʻi hoku feituʻú. ʻAlu ki he tangata ngaohi loká, ʻoku ʻi ai ha taha ʻoku maile nai ʻe taha mei heni. Ka ʻoku avast!" ʻo ne ʻai hono nimá ki hono kato tafaʻakí, “ko ha kī ʻeni ʻe feʻunga, ʻoku ou mahalo; tau sio.” Pea ʻi he meʻa ko iá, naʻá ne liliu ia ʻi he loká; ka, ʻoiauē! Na'e kei 'ikai ke to'o 'a e bolt fakalahi 'a Queequeg 'i loto.

“Have to burst it open,” ko ʻeku leá ia, pea naʻá ku kiʻi lele hifo ʻi he hūʻangá, ki ha kamataʻanga lelei, ʻi he taimi naʻe maʻu ai au ʻe he fineʻeiki ʻo e ʻapí, ʻo ne toe fakapapauʻi mai ʻoku ʻikai totonu ke u holoki hono ʻapí; ka naʻá ku haeʻi meiate ia, pea ʻi ha lele fakafokifā fakaesino naʻá ku lele fakafonu au ki he fakaʻilongá.

ʻI ha longoaʻa fakaofo naʻe lele atu ʻa e matapaá ʻo fakaava, pea ʻi he pā ʻa e foʻi tuʻuʻangá ʻi he ʻā, ʻo fekauʻi atu ʻa e palasitaá ki he silingí; pea ʻi ai, ʻa e ngaahi langi lelei! naʻe tangutu ai ʻa Queequeg, ʻo fakaʻau ke māfana mo tānaki pē ʻe ia ia; ʻi he lotolotonga tonu ʻo e lokí; ʻo ne fakaʻuli ʻi hono ngaahi hams, mo puke ʻa Yojo ʻi ʻolunga ʻi hono ʻulú. Naʻe ʻikai ke ne sio ki he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá pe ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá, ka naʻá ne tangutu ʻo hangē ha ʻīmisi tongitongi ʻoku siʻisiʻi ha fakaʻilonga ʻo e moʻui longomoʻuí.

“Kuiki,” ko ʻeku leá ia, ʻo ʻalu hake kiate ia, “Kuiki, ko e hā e meʻa ʻoku hoko kiate koé?”

“Naʻe ʻikai ke ne hoko ko ha sittin' ʻi he ʻaho kakató, ʻikai ko ia?” ko e lau ia ʻa e fineʻeiki ʻo e ʻapí.

Ka ko e meʻa pē naʻá ma lea ʻakí, naʻe ʻikai ha foʻi lea te ma lava ʻo toho meiate ia; Naʻá ku meimei ongoʻi ke u tekeʻi ia, koeʻuhí ke liliu hono tuʻungá, he naʻe meimei ʻikai lava ke kātakiʻi, naʻe ngali fuʻu fakamamahi mo taʻefakanatula hono fakangatangata; tautautefito, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku ngalingali kotoa pē naʻá ne tangutu pehē ʻo laka hake ʻi ha houa ʻe valu pe hongofulu, ʻo ʻalu foki ʻo ʻikai haʻane meʻakai angamahení.

Naʻá ku pehē ange, “Misa Husei, ʻokú ne moʻui ʻi he meʻa kotoa pē; ko ia tukuange kimaua, kapau te ke kātaki, pea te u tokangaʻi ʻe au ʻa e meʻa ngali kehe ko ʻení.”

ʻI heʻeku tāpuniʻi e matapaá ki he fineʻeiki ʻo e ʻapí, ne u feinga ke ikunaʻi ʻa Queequeg ke ne toʻo ha sea; kae taʻeʻaonga. Naʻá ne nofo ai; pea ko e meʻa pē naʻá ne lavá —ki heʻeku ngaahi ʻātisi fakaʻapaʻapa mo e fakaʻaiʻai kotoa pē —he ʻikai ke ne hiki ha foʻi ʻakau, pe lea ʻaki ha foʻi lea ʻe taha, pe naʻa mo e sio mai kiate au, pe fakatokangaʻi ʻeku ʻi aí ʻi ha kiʻi founga siʻisiʻi taha.

ʻOku ou fifili, ko ʻeku fakakaukau ia, pe ʻe lava ke hoko ʻeni ko ha konga ʻo ʻene Ramadan; 'oku nau 'aukai 'i he'enau ngaahi hams 'i he founga ko ia 'i hono motu tupu'anga. Kuo pau ke pehē; 'io, ko e konga ia 'o 'ene tui fakalotu, 'oku ou mahalo; sai, ko ia, tuku ke ne mālōlō; te ne tuʻu hake vave pe ʻamui ange, ʻoku ʻikai ha veiveiua. He'ikai lava ke tolonga ia 'o ta'engata, fakafeta'i ki he 'Otua, pea 'oku tu'o taha pe 'ene Ramadan 'i he ta'u; pea 'oku 'ikai keu tui 'oku fu'u punctually ia he taimi ko ia.

Naʻá ku ʻalu hifo ki he maʻu meʻatokoni efiafí. Hili ʻeku tangutu fuoloa ʻo fanongo ki he ngaahi talanoa lōloa ʻa e kau folau ʻe niʻihi naʻa nau toki haʻu mei ha folau plum-pudding, ʻo hangē ko ʻenau uí (ʻa ia, ko ha folau-tafola nounou ʻi ha vakapuna pe vakapuna, ʻoku fakangatangata pē ki he tokelau ʻo e lainé, ʻi he ʻŌseni ʻAtalanitiká pē); hili ʻeku fanongo ki he ngaahi palamu-pudingers ko ʻení ʻo aʻu ki he meimei taimi hongofulu mā tahá, ne u ʻalu hake ʻi he sitepú ke mohe, ʻo ongoʻi pau ʻaupito ʻi he taimi ko ʻení kuo pau pē kuo ʻomi ʻe Queequeg ʻene Ramadan ki ha fakangata. Ka ʻoku ʻikai; naʻá ne ʻi ai pē ʻi he feituʻu naʻá ku tuku ai iá; naʻe teʻeki ke ne ueʻi ha ʻinisi. Naʻe kamata ke u ʻita ʻiate ia; naʻe ngali fuʻu taʻeʻuhinga mo valevale ʻaupito ʻene tangutu ʻi ai ʻi he ʻaho kakato mo e vaeua ʻo e pō ʻi hono ngaahi ham ʻi ha loki momoko, ʻo ne puke ha konga ʻakau ʻi hono ʻulú.

“Koeʻuhí ko e langí, Kuiki, tuʻu hake ʻo lulululu koe; tuʻu hake ʻo kai efiafi. Te ke fiekaia; te ke tāmateʻi koe, Kuiki." Ka naʻe ʻikai ke ne tali mai ha foʻi lea.

Ko ia ai, ‘i he‘eku loto-mo‘ua kiate iá, na‘á ku fakapapau‘i ai ke u ‘alu ‘o mohe pea mohe; pea ʻoku ʻikai ha veiveiua, kimuʻa ʻi ha taimi lahi, te ne muimui mai ʻiate au. Ka kimuʻa peá u tafoki ki lotó, ne u toʻo hoku kote kiliʻi pea mamafá, ʻo lī ia kiate ia, he naʻe talaʻofa mai ko ha pō momoko ʻaupito; pea naʻe ʻikai haʻane meʻa ka ko hono kote takai angamahení pē. ʻI ha taimi, fai ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē te u faí, naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo hū ki he mohe vaivai tahá. Naʻá ku puhiʻi ʻa e teʻelango; pea ko e fakakaukau pē kia Kuiki —ʻoku ʻikai fute ʻe fā hono mamaʻó —ʻoku tangutu ʻi ai ʻi he tuʻunga taʻefiemālie ko iá, ʻo fuʻu tokotaha pē ʻi he momokó mo e fakapoʻulí; naʻe ʻai au ʻe he meʻá ni ke u fakaʻofa moʻoni. Fakakaukau ki ai; mohe ʻi he pō kakato ʻi he loki tatau mo ha pangani ʻā lahi ʻi hono ngaahi hams ʻi he Ramadan fakaloloma mo taʻefakaʻatuʻi ko ʻení!

Ka ʻi ha faʻahinga founga naʻá ku faifai pea tō, pea ʻikai ke u toe ʻilo ha meʻa ʻo aʻu ki he mafoa ʻa e ʻahó; ʻi heʻene sio atu ki he tafaʻaki mohengá, naʻe ʻi ai ʻa e tuʻu ʻa Kuiki, ʻo hangē kuo fakaʻauʻauhifo ia ki he falikí. Ka ʻi he hū pē ʻa e ʻuluaki sio ʻa e laʻaá ʻi he matapaá, naʻá ne ʻalu hake, mo e ngaahi hui fefeka mo ngingila, ka mo ha sio fiefia; naʻá ne luelue mai kiate au ʻi he feituʻu naʻá ku tokoto aí; naʻá ne toe lomiʻi hono laʻé ki hoku laʻé; pea ne pehē kuo ʻosi ʻene Ramadan.

Ko 'eni, hange ko ia na'a ku faka'ilonga'i atu kimu'a, 'oku 'ikai ha'aku fakafepaki ki he lotu 'a ha taha, pe ko e ha pe, 'o kapau 'oku 'ikai ke tamate'i pe fakaanga'i 'e he tokotaha ko ia ha taha kehe, he 'oku 'ikai ke tui foki ki ai 'a e tokotaha kehe ko ia. “Kuo mou fakafolau ʻiate ia?” naʻá ne toe pehē mai.

“ʻOku ou ʻuhinga ki he vaka Pequod, ʻoku ou mahalo,” ko ʻeku leá ia, ʻi heʻeku feinga ke maʻu ha kiʻi taimi lahi ange ki haʻaku sio taʻe-motuʻa kiate ia.

“ʻIo, ko e Pequod —ko e vaka ko ia ʻi aí,” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻo ne toho ki mui hono nimá kotoa, peá ne toki tekeʻi fakavavevave ia ʻo hangatonu meiate ia, ʻo lele fakafonu ʻa e bayonet tuʻu maʻu ʻo hono louhiʻi nima mataʻitofé ʻi he meʻá.

Na‘á ku pehē ange, “‘Io, kuo mau toki fakamo‘oni hingoa ‘i he ngaahi kupú.”

“Ko ha meʻa ʻi lalo fekauʻaki mo homou ngaahi laumālié?”

“Fekauʻaki mo e hā?”

“ʻOiauē, mahalo ʻoku teʻeki ke ke maʻu ha taha,” ko ʻene lea vave ia. “Neongo ia, ʻoku ou ʻiloʻi ha ngaahi chaps tokolahi ʻoku teʻeki ke nau maʻu ha taha, —monūʻia kiate kinautolu; pea ʻoku nau lelei ange kotoa ki ai. Ko ha laumālie ko ha faʻahinga ʻo e uili hono nimá ki ha saliote.”

“Ko e hā ʻokú ke jabbering fekauʻaki mo iá, kaungā vaka?” ko ʻeku leá ia.

“ Kae kehe, ‘okú ne ma‘u ‘a e me‘a fe‘unga ke ne fakakakato ‘a e ngaahi tōnounou kotoa pē ‘o e fa‘ahinga ko iá ‘i he ngaahi vahe kehé,” ko e lea fakafokifā ia ‘a e muli ko iá, ‘o ne fakamamafa‘i faka‘ohovale ‘a e fo‘i lea ko ia .

“Queequeg,” ko ʻeku leá ia, “tau ō; kuo movete ʻa e tangatá ni mei ha feituʻu; ʻokú ne talanoa ki ha meʻa mo ha taha ʻoku ʻikai ke tau ʻiloʻi.”

"Tuku!" ko e tangi ia ʻa e muli ko iá. “Naʻa mou pehē moʻoni —ʻoku teʻeki ke mou sio ʻi he Manamana Motuʻá, ʻikai ko ia?”

“Ko hai ʻa e Manamana Motuʻá?” ko ʻeku leá ia, ʻo u toe fakaʻofoʻofaʻia ʻi he fakamātoato vale ʻo ʻene foungá.

“Kapiteni ʻĒhapi.”

“Ko e hā! ko e kapiteni ʻo ʻemau vaká, ko e Pequod?”

“ʻIo, ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e niʻihi ʻo kimautolu kau toutai motuʻá, ʻokú ne ʻalu ʻi he hingoa ko iá. ʻOku teʻeki ke mou sio kiate ia, ʻikai ko ia?”

“ʻIkai, ʻoku teʻeki ke mau fai ia. ʻOku nau puke ʻoku nau pehē, ka ʻoku fakaʻau ke sai ange, pea ʻe toe sai pē ia ʻi ha taimi nounou.”

“Toe sai pē ʻo ʻikai fuoloa!” ko e kata ia ʻa e muli ko iá, ʻaki ha faʻahinga kata fakaanga fakaʻeiʻeiki. “Vakai, ʻo ka sai ʻa e ʻEikitau ko ʻĒhapí, ʻe toki sai ʻa hoku nima toʻohemá ni, ʻo ʻikai kimuʻa.”

“Ko e hā ʻokú ke ʻilo fekauʻaki mo iá?”

“Ko e hā naʻa nau talaatu kiate koe fekauʻaki mo iá? Lea ʻaki ia!”

“Naʻe ʻikai ke nau talanoa lahi ki ha meʻa fekauʻaki mo ia;ko ʻeku fanongo pē ko ha tokotaha tuli tafola lelei ia, pea ko ha kapiteni lelei ki heʻene kau ngāué.”

“Ko e moʻoni ia, ʻoku moʻoni ia—ʻio, ʻoku moʻoni feʻunga fakatouʻosi. Ka kuo pau ke ke puna ʻi he taimi ʻokú ne ʻoatu ai ha tuʻutuʻuní. Laka mo ngungu; ngungu mo ʻalu—ko e lea ia mo Kapiteni ʻĒhapí. Ka ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa fekauʻaki mo e meʻa ko ia naʻe hoko kiate ia ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo Keipi Honí, ʻi ha taimi fuoloa kuohilí, ʻi heʻene tokoto ʻo hangē ha mate ʻi he ʻaho ʻe tolu ʻo e nothing skrad; afore 'a e 'olita 'i Santa?—'Ikai ha me'a fekau'aki mo e kalasi siliva na'a ne 'anuhi ki ai? Pea 'ikai ha me'a fekau'aki mo 'ene mole hono va'e 'i he folau fakamuimuitaha. mahalo, kuo mou fanongo ʻoku talanoa ki he vaʻé, pea mo e founga naʻá ne mole ai iá, ʻoku ou loto-toʻa ke pehē. ʻOiauē, ʻoku ʻiloʻi ʻe he tokotaha kotoa pē ʻa e vaʻe ʻe tahá;

“Siʻoku kaungāmeʻa,” ko ʻeku leá ia, “ko e hā ʻoku fekauʻaki mo hoʻo ngaahi lea fakapikopiko kotoa ko ʻení, ʻoku ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi, pea ʻoku ʻikai ke u fuʻu tokanga ki ai; he ʻoku hangē kiate au ʻoku pau pē ʻoku kiʻi maumauʻi ho ʻulú. Ka ʻo kapau ʻokú ke lea ʻo kau kia Kapiteni ʻĒhapi, ʻo e vaka ko ia ʻi aí, ko e Pequod, pea tuku ke u talaatu kiate koe ʻa e less ʻo e meʻa kotoa pē, ʻoku ou ʻiloʻi.”

“ Ko e meʻa kotoa pē , eh—pau ʻokú ke fai ia?—kotoa?”

“ʻOku fuʻu pau.”

ʻI he tuhu hono louhiʻi nimá mo fakahangatonu hono matá ki he Pequod, naʻe tuʻu ʻa e muli hangē ha kolekolé ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti, ʻo hangē ʻokú ne ʻi ha fakakaukau faingataʻaʻiá; pea kamata ki'i, tafoki 'o pehe:—“Kuo mou fakafolau, 'oku mou? Hingoa hifo 'i he ngaahi pepa? Sai, sai, ko e me'a kuo fakamo'oni hingoa, kuo fakamo'oni hingoa; pea ko e me'a ke hoko, 'e hoko; pea hili ia, mahalo 'e 'ikai ke hoko ia, hili ia. Anyhow, 'oku fokotu'utu'u kotoa ia mo fokotu'utu'u 'a e ni'ihi kehe 'o hange ko ia kuo pau ke 'alu mo ia; ko ha kau tangata kehe, ʻofa ʻa e ʻOtuá kiate kimoutolu, kaungā vaka, pongipongi ʻoku tāpuakiʻi kimoutolu ʻe he ngaahi langi taʻefaʻalauá;

“Sio heni, kaungāmeʻa,” ko ʻeku leá ia, “kapau ʻoku ʻi ai haʻo meʻa mahuʻinga ke talamai kiate kimautolu, ʻalu atu mo ia; ka ʻo kapau ʻokú ke feinga pē ke fakaʻaiʻai kimautolu, ʻokú ke fehālaaki ʻi hoʻo vaʻingá; ko e meʻa pē ia ʻoku ou fie lea ʻakí.”

“Pea ʻoku leaʻaki lelei ʻaupito ia, pea ʻoku ou saiʻia ke fanongo ki ha chap ʻoku lea hake ʻi he founga ko iá; ko e tangata pē koe kiate ia —ʻa e ngaahi meʻa hangē ko kimoutolú. Pongipongi kiate kimoutolu, kaungā vaka, pongipongi! ʻOiauē! ʻi hoʻomou aʻu ki aí, talaange kiate kinautolu kuó u fakapapauʻi ke ʻoua naʻá ku ngaohi ha taha ʻo kinautolu.”

“ʻĀ, ʻe hoku kaungāmeʻa ʻofeina, he ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo kākaaʻi kimaua ʻi he founga ko iá﻿—he ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo kākaaʻi kimaua.Ko e meʻa faingofua taha ia ʻi he māmaní ki ha tangata ke ne fōtunga ʻo hangē ʻokú ne maʻu ha fuʻu fakapulipuli lahi ʻiate iá.”

“Pongipongi kiate kimoutolu, kaungā vaka, pongipongi.”

“Ko e pongipongí ia,” ko ʻeku leá ia. “Haʻu, Kuiki, tau mavahe mei he tangata vale ko ʻení. Kae tuʻu, talamai ho hingoá, ʻikai ko ia?”

“ʻIlaisiā.”

ʻIlaisiā! ko ʻeku fakakaukau ia, peá ma lue atu, ʻo ma fakatou fakamatala, ʻo fakatatau ki he founga ʻo e taha mo e taha, ʻa e tangata toutai motuʻa kofukofu ko ʻení; pea naʻá ne lototaha ʻoku ʻikai ko ha meʻa kehe ia ka ko ha humbug, ʻo feinga ke hoko ko ha bugbear. Ka naʻe teʻeki ke mau ʻalu mahalo ʻo laka hake ʻi ha ʻiate ʻe teau, ʻi heʻemau afe faingamālie ʻi ha tuliki, pea sio ki mui ʻi heʻeku fai iá, ko hai ʻoku totonu ke sio ki aí ka ko ʻIlaisiā pē ʻoku muimui mai ʻiate kimautolu, neongo ʻoku mamaʻo. ʻI ha faʻahinga founga, naʻe fuʻu taaʻi au ʻe heʻeku sio kiate iá, ʻo ʻikai ke u lea ʻaki ha meʻa kia Kuiki ʻo kau ki heʻene ʻi muí, ka naʻá ku fakalaka atu mo hoku kaungāmeʻá, ʻi heʻeku hohaʻa ke vakai pe ʻe afe ʻa e muli ʻi he tuliki tatau mo ia naʻá ma faí. Naʻá ne fai ia; pea naʻe hangē leva kiate au naʻá ne fakaʻuliʻi kimaua, ka ko e hā ha taumuʻa naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo fakakaukau ki ai mo e moʻui ʻaʻakú. Ko e tūkunga ko ʻení, fakataha mo ʻene faʻahinga lea ʻoku ʻikai mahino, vaeua fakaʻilonga, vaeua fakahā, mo ʻufiʻufi, naʻá ne fakatupu ʻiate au he taimí ni ʻa e faʻahinga fakaofo taʻemahino kotoa pē mo e ngaahi manavasiʻi vaeua, pea ʻoku fehokotaki kotoa ia mo e Pequod; mo e ʻEikitau ko ʻEhapi; pea ko e vaʻe naʻe mole meiate iá; pea ko e Keipi Honi 'oku fe'unga; mo e kalapasi siliva; pea mo e meʻa naʻe lea ʻaki ʻe he ʻEikitau ko Peleki ʻo kau kiate iá, ʻi heʻeku mavahe mei he vaká ʻi he ʻaho kimuʻá; pea mo e kikite ʻa e squaw Tistig; pea mo e folau naʻa mau haʻi ʻakimautolu ke folau; mo e ngaahi meʻa fakaʻata kehe ʻe teau.

Naʻá ku fakapapauʻi ke fakafiemālieʻi au pe ʻoku kulii moʻoni kimaua ʻe ʻIlaisiā kovi ko ʻení pe ʻikai, pea ʻi he taumuʻa ko iá naʻá ku kolosi ʻi he halá mo Kuiki, pea ʻi he tafaʻaki ko iá naʻá ku toe muimuiʻi ai ʻemau ngaahi sitepú. Ka naʻe fakalaka atu ʻa ʻIlaisiā, ʻo hangē naʻe ʻikai ke ne fakatokangaʻi kimautolú. Naʻe fakafiemālieʻi au ʻe he meʻá ni; pea toe tuʻo taha, pea fakaʻosí ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe ngali kiate aú, ne u puʻaki ia ʻi hoku lotó, ko ha humbug.





VAHE 20. Koe Astir kotoabe.

Naʻe ʻosi atu ha ʻaho ʻe taha pe ua, pea naʻe lahi ʻa e ʻekitivitī ʻi he vakapuna Pequod. Naʻe ʻikai ngata pē ʻi hono fakaleleiʻi ʻo e ngaahi folau motuʻá, ka naʻe haʻu ha ngaahi folau foʻou ki he vaká, mo e ngaahi poulu ʻo e kaniva, mo e ngaahi foʻi koila ʻo e ngaahi meʻangāué; ko hono fakanounoú, naʻe fakaʻilongaʻi ʻe he meʻa kotoa pē ʻoku fakavavevave ʻa e teuteu ʻa e vaká ke ʻosi. Naʻe ʻikai ke faʻa ʻalu pe ʻikai ʻaupito ke ʻalu ʻa Kapiteni Peleki ki ʻuta, ka naʻá ne tangutu ʻi hono wigwam ʻo tauhi ha sio māsila ki he ongo nimá: Naʻe fai ʻe Pilitati ʻa e fakatau mo e tokonaki kotoa pē ʻi he ngaahi falekoloá; pea ko e kau tangata naʻe ngāue ʻi he tauʻanga vaká mo e ngaahi vakapuná naʻa nau ngāue ʻo aʻu ki he taimi fuoloa hili ʻa e tō ʻa e poʻulí.

‘I he ‘aho hili hono fakamo‘oni hingoa ‘a Queequeg ki he ngaahi fakamatalá, na‘e ‘oatu ai ‘a e ongoongo ‘i he ngaahi fale talitali kakai kotoa pē na‘e tu‘u ai ‘a e kautaha ‘o e vaká, kuo pau ke ‘i vaka honau ngaahi fatafatá ki mu‘a ke po‘ulí, he na‘e ‘ikai ha tala ‘a e vave ‘e ala folau ai ‘a e vaká. Ko ia na‘á ku hifo mo Queequeg mei he‘ema ngaahi tauhele, ‘o fakapapau‘i, neongo ia, ke ma mohe ‘i ‘uta ‘o a‘u ki he faka‘osí. Ka 'oku hange 'oku nau 'oatu ma'u pe 'a e fakatokanga loloa 'aupito 'i he ngaahi keisi ko 'eni, pea na'e 'ikai ke folau 'a e vaka 'i ha ngaahi 'aho lahi. Ka ʻoku ʻikai ha ofo; na'e 'i ai 'a e me'a lelei ke fai, pea 'oku 'ikai ha tala 'o e lahi 'o e ngaahi me'a ke fakakaukau ki ai, kimu'a pea toki fakanaunau kakato 'a e Pequod.

‘Oku ‘ilo‘i ‘e he tokotaha kotoa pē ‘a e lahi fau ‘o e ngaahi me‘á—ngaahi mohenga, ngaahi pani sosi, ngaahi hele mo e ngaahi fo‘i sō, ngaahi fo‘i sō mo e ngaahi fo‘i sō, ngaahi napkin, ngaahi me‘a-nati, mo e ngaahi me‘a kehe, ‘oku fu‘u fiema‘u lahi ia ki he pisinisi tauhi ‘apí. ‘Oku pehē pē mo e toutai tafola, ‘a ia ‘oku fiema‘u ai ha tauhi fale ‘i he ta‘u ‘e tolu ‘i he ‘oseni lahí, ‘o mama‘o mei he kau fakatau koloa kotoa pē, kau fakatau totongi, kau toketā, kau ta‘o mā, mo e kau ngāue pangikē. Pea neongo ‘oku mo‘oni foki eni ki he ngaahi vaka fakatau koloá, ka ‘oku ‘ikai ‘i ha fa‘ahinga founga pē ‘o a‘u ki he tu‘unga tatau mo e kau toutai tafola. He tukukehe 'a e loloa lahi 'o e folau tafola, 'a e ngaahi fakamatala lahi peculiar ki he faka'ilo 'o e toutai, pea mo e ta'emalava ke fetongi kinautolu 'i he ngaahi taulanga mama'o 'oku fa'a toutou 'a'ahi ki ai, kuo pau ke manatu'i, 'a e ngaahi vaka kotoa pe, 'oku 'asi lahi taha 'a e ngaahi vaka tafola ki he ngaahi me'a 'o e estruction mo e uspecially 'a e fa'ahinga kotoa pe, . ko e lavame‘a ‘o e folaú ‘oku fakatu‘unga lahi taha ia. Ko ia ai, ko e ngaahi vaka spare, spar spar, mo e ngaahi laine spare mo e harpoons, pea spare 'a e me'a kotoa pe, meimei, ka ko ha Kapiteni spare mo e vaka tatau.

ʻI he vahaʻataimi ʻo ʻemau aʻu atu ki he ʻOtu Motú, naʻe meimei ʻosi ʻa e tauhiʻanga koloa mamafa taha ʻo e Pequod; ‘oku kau ai ‘ene kakano‘i pulu, mā, vai, lolo, mo e ngaahi fo‘i hoop mo e ngaahi va‘akau ukamea. Ka, hangē ko ia naʻe fakaʻilongaʻi kimuʻá, ʻi ha taimi naʻe hokohoko atu hono ʻomi mo ʻave ʻi he vaká ʻa e ngaahi meʻa kehekehe ʻoku faingataʻa mo e ngaahi ngataʻanga ʻo e ngaahi meʻá, ʻa e lalahí mo e iiki fakatouʻosi.

Ko e pule ʻiate kinautolu naʻa nau fai ʻa e ʻomi mo e ʻave ko ʻení ko e tuofefine ʻo Kapiteni Pilitati, ko ha fineʻeiki sino ʻo ha laumālie fakapapauʻi mo taʻe-vaivai, ka naʻe loto-lelei ʻaupito, ʻa ia naʻe ngali fakapapauʻi, kapau te ne lava ʻo tokoni ki ai, ʻoku ʻikai totonu ke maʻu ha meʻa ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai ʻi he Pequod, hili ʻene aʻu totonu ki tahí. ʻI ha taimi ʻe taha te ne haʻu ki vaka mo ha hina pickles ki he falekai ʻo e setuata; ko e taimi ʻe taha mo ha fuʻu ngaahi foʻi peni ki he tēpile ʻa e hoa tefitó, ʻa ia naʻá ne tauhi ai ʻene ʻakaú; ko e taimi hono tolu mo ha takai flannel ki he si'isi'i 'o e tu'a rheumatic 'o ha taha. Naʻe ʻikai ha fefine ʻe toe taau ange mo hono hingoá, ʻa ia ko Charity —ʻAuntie Charity, ʻo hangē ko hono ui ia ʻe he tokotaha kotoa pē. Pea hangē ha tuofefine ʻo e ʻofá naʻe femoʻuekina holo ʻa e ʻAuniti ʻofa ko ʻení ʻi heni mo hena, ʻo mateuteu ke tafoki hono nimá mo hono lotó ki ha meʻa pē ʻe talaʻofa ke ne ʻomi ha malu, fakafiemālie, mo ha fakafiemālie kiate kinautolu kotoa pē ʻi ha vaka naʻe hohaʻa ki ai hono tokoua ʻofeina ko Pilitatí, pea naʻá ne maʻu ai ha paʻanga ʻe ua pe ko ia.

Ka naʻe fakaʻohovale ʻa e sio ki he haʻu ʻa e Kueiki loto lelei ko ʻení ki he vaká, ʻo hangē ko ʻene fai ʻi he ʻaho fakaʻosí, mo ha ipu lolo lōloa ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá, pea mo ha tao toutai tafola ʻoku kei lōloa ange ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá. Pea na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito ke holomui ‘a Pilitati tonu pe ko Kapiteni Peleki. Ko e me‘a kia Pilitati, na‘á ne ‘ave holo mo ia ha lisi lōloa ‘o e ngaahi me‘a na‘e fiema‘ú, pea ‘i he a‘u fo‘ou kotoa pē, na‘e ‘alu hifo ‘ene faka‘ilonga ‘o fehangahangai mo e me‘a ko iá ‘i he pepá. ʻI he taimi ʻe niʻihi naʻe haʻu ʻa Peleki ʻo luelue mai mei hono ʻana ʻo e hui tafola, ʻo ne ngungu ki he kau tangata ʻi he ngaahi hūʻanga ʻo e vaká, ʻo ne ngungu hake ki he kau vakapuna ʻi he ʻulu ʻo e pou vaká, peá ne toki fakaʻosi ʻaki ʻene toe ngungu ki hono wigwam.

Lolotonga e ngaahi ʻaho ko ʻeni ʻo e teuteú, naʻá ku faʻa ʻaʻahi mo Kuiki ki he vaká, pea hangē ko e taimi lahi naʻá ku ʻeke ai fekauʻaki mo ʻEikitau ʻĒhapí, mo ʻene fēfē, pea mo e taimi ʻe haʻu ai ʻo heka ki hono vaká. Ki he ngaahi fehuʻi ko ʻení te nau tali, ʻokú ne fakaʻau ke sai ange, pea ʻoku ʻamanaki ke ne ʻi vaka ʻi he ʻaho kotoa pē; lolotonga iá, na‘e lava ke tokanga‘i ‘e he ongo ‘eikitaú, ‘a Peleki mo Pilitati, ‘a e me‘a kotoa pē na‘e fiema‘u ke fe‘ungamālie ai ‘a e vaká ki he folaú. Kapau naʻá ku faitotonu ʻaupito kiate au, naʻá ku mei sio mahino ʻaupito ʻi hoku lotó naʻá ku fai pē ʻa e vaeua ʻo e fakakaukau ʻi heʻeku tukupā ʻi he founga ko ʻení ki ha folau lōloa pehē, ʻo ʻikai ke u tuʻo taha ʻo sio ki he tangata ʻe hoko ko e pule fakatikitato ʻaupito ʻo e folau ko iá, ʻi he taimi pē naʻe folau atu ai ʻa e vaká ʻi he tahi ʻataʻataá. Ka ‘i he taimi ‘oku mahalo‘i ai ‘e ha tangata ha hala, ‘oku hoko ia ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi kapau kuó ne ‘osi kau ‘i he me‘á, ‘okú ne feinga ta‘e‘uhinga ke ‘ufi‘ufi ‘ene ngaahi mahalo‘í na‘a mo ia tonu. Pea ko e lahi ʻo e founga ko ʻení naʻe ʻiate au. Naʻe ʻikai ke u lea ʻaki ha meʻa, pea feinga ke ʻoua naʻá ku fakakaukau ki ha meʻa.

Naʻe faifai pea ʻoatu ʻe ʻi ai ha taimi ʻi he ʻaho hono hokó ʻe folau moʻoni ʻa e vaká. Ko ia ‘i he pongipongi hono hokó, na‘á ku kamata vave ‘aupito mo Queequeg.





VAHE 21. Ko e 'alu ki he vaka.

Naʻe meimei taimi onó, ka ko e mafoa ʻa e atá pē ʻa e kohu taʻehaohaoa hina, ʻi heʻemau ofi atu ki he uafú.

“ʻOku ʻi ai ha kau folau ʻe niʻihi ʻoku nau lele ʻi muʻa ʻi ai, kapau ʻoku ou sio tonu,” ko ʻeku leá ia kia Kuiki, “ʻoku ʻikai lava ke hoko ia ko ha ngaahi ʻata; ʻoku ou mahalo ʻoku ʻalu ia ʻi he hopo ʻa e laʻaá; haʻu!”

“ʻAvasiti!” ko e kaila ia ʻa ha leʻo, ʻa ia ko hono ʻeikí ʻi he taimi tatau naʻá ne ofi mai mei mui ʻiate kimaua, ʻo ne hilifaki ha nima ki homa ongo umá fakatouʻosi, peá ne toki fakaʻaiʻai ia ʻi homa vahaʻá, ʻo ne tuʻu ʻo kiʻi punou ki muʻa, ʻi he fakapoʻuli taʻepaú, ʻo sio fakaofo mai mei Kuikiki kiate au. Ko ʻIlaisiā ia.

“Ko e ʻalu ki vaka?”

“Tuku nima, te ke fai,” ko ʻeku leá ia.

“Lookee heni,” ko e lea ia ʻa Kuiki, ʻo ne lulululu, “ʻalu ʻo ʻalu!”

“ʻIkai ʻalu ki vaka, pea?”

“ʻIo, ʻoku mau pehē,” ko ʻeku leá ia, “ka ko e hā hoʻo meʻa ko iá? ʻOkú ke ʻiloʻi, Misa ʻIlaisiaa, ʻoku ou lau koe ʻokú ke kiʻi taʻefakaʻapaʻapa?”

“ʻIkai, ʻikai, ʻikai;naʻe ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻIlaisiaá, ʻo ne sio māmālie mo ofo meiate au kia Kuiki, ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi sio taʻefakaʻatuʻi tahá.

Naʻá ku pehē ange, “ʻIlaisiā, te ke fakamālohiʻi au mo hoku kaungāmeʻá ʻaki haʻo mavahe. ʻOkú ma ʻalu ki he ʻŌseni ʻInitiá mo e ʻŌseni Pasifikí, pea ʻoku mau saiʻia ange ke ʻoua naʻa puke kimaua.”

“ʻOku mou, ʻoku mou? ʻOku mou foki mai kimuʻa he maʻu meʻatokoni pongipongí?”

“Kuó ne mafesifesi, Kuiki,” ko ʻeku leá ia, “haʻu.”

“ʻOloa!” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻIlaisiā naʻe tuʻu maʻú, ʻo ne fakafeʻiloaki kimaua ʻi heʻema toʻo ha ngaahi laka siʻisiʻí.

“ʻOua naʻá ke tokanga kiate ia,” ko ʻeku leá ia, “Kuiki, haʻu.”

Ka naʻá ne toe kaihaʻasi mai kiate kimaua, pea fakafokifā pē ʻene paki hono nimá ʻi hoku umá, ʻo ne pehē mai —“Naʻá mo sio ki ha meʻa ʻoku hangē ha kau tangatá ʻoku ʻalu atu ki he vaka ko iá ʻi ha taimi kimuʻa?”

ʻI heʻeku ofo ʻi he fehuʻi mahino moʻoni ko ʻení, ne u tali ange, ʻo pehē, “ʻIo, naʻá ku fakakaukau naʻá ku sio moʻoni ki ha kau tangata ʻe toko fā pe toko nima, ka naʻe fuʻu fakapoʻuli ke fakapapauʻi.”

“ʻOku fuʻu fakapoʻuli, fuʻu fakapoʻuli,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻIlaisiaá. “Pongipongi kiate kimoutolu.”

Naʻá ma toe tukuange ia; ka naʻá ne toe muimui māmālie mai ʻiate kimaua; peá ne toe ala ki hoku umá, ʻo ne pehē mai, “Sio pe te ke lava ʻo maʻu kinautolu he taimí ni, ʻikai ko ia?

“Kumi ko hai?”

“Pongipongi kiate kimoutolu! pongipongi kiate kimoutolu!” naʻá ne toe kau mai, ʻo ne toe hiki atu. “ʻOiauē! Naʻá ku teu ke fakatokanga atu kiate kimoutolu ki ai —ka ʻoua naʻa tokanga, ʻoua naʻa tokanga —ʻoku taha kotoa pē, ʻi he fāmilí foki;—ʻuha momoko māsila ʻi he pongipongí ni, ʻikai ko ia? Māvae kiate kimoutolu. ʻE ʻikai ke mou toe sio vave ʻaupito, ʻoku ou mahalo; tukukehe kapau ʻoku ʻi he ʻao ʻo e Kau Fakamaau Lahí.” Pea ʻi he ngaahi lea mafesifesi ko ʻení naʻá ne faifai pea mavahe, ʻo ne tuku au, ʻi he momenití, ʻo ʻikai ha kiʻi ofo ʻi heʻene taʻefakaʻapaʻapa fakaʻauhá.

Fakaʻosí, ʻi heʻemau heka ki he vakapuna Pequod, ne mau ʻiloʻi ʻoku fakalongolongo loloto ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē, ʻo ʻikai ha laumālie ʻoku ngaʻunu. Naʻe lokaʻi ʻa e hūʻanga ʻo e kāpiní ʻi loto; naʻe ʻi ai kotoa ʻa e ngaahi matapaá, pea naʻe lumbered ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi coils ʻo e rigging. ʻI heʻemau ʻalu atu ki muʻa ki he forcastle, ne mau ʻilo ʻoku fakaava ʻa e ʻasi ʻo e scuttle. ʻI heʻemau sio ki ha maama, naʻá ma ʻalu hifo, ʻo ma ʻilo pē ha rigger motuʻa ʻi ai, kuo takai ʻi ha kofukofu pea kuo haehae. Naʻe lī ia ʻi hono lōloá kakato ʻi ha ongo fatafata, ʻo hanga hifo hono fofongá pea ʻoku ʻātakaiʻi ʻi hono ongo nima kuo fakapipikí. Naʻe mohe ʻiate ia ʻa e mohe loloto tahá.

“Ko e kau folau ko ia naʻa mau sio ki aí, Kuiki, naʻa nau mei ʻalu ki fē?” ko ʻeku leá ia, ʻo sio veiveiua ki he tokotaha mohé. Ka naʻe hangē, ʻi heʻene ʻi he uafú, naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke fakatokangaʻi ʻe Queequeg ʻa e meʻa naʻá ku lave ki ai he taimí ni; ko ia naʻá ku mei fakakaukau ai naʻá ku kākaaʻi fakaʻata au ʻi he meʻa ko iá, kapau naʻe ʻikai ko e fehuʻi ʻa ʻIlaisiā ʻoku ʻikai lava ke fakamatalaʻi ʻi ha founga kehe. Ka naʻá ku taaʻi ʻa e meʻá; pea toe fakaʻilongaʻi ʻa e tokotaha mohé, ʻo fakaʻilongaʻi fakakata kia Queequeg mahalo naʻe sai ange ke ma tangutu hake mo e sinó; ‘o tala ange ke ne fokotu‘u ia ‘o fakatatau ki ai. Naʻá ne hili hono nimá ki he tuʻa ʻo e tokotaha mohé, ʻo hangē ʻokú ne ongoʻi pe ʻoku molū feʻunga; pea hili iá, ʻo ʻikai toe ʻi ai haʻane lea, naʻá ne tangutu fakalongolongo hifo ʻi ai.

Naʻá ku pehē ange, “ʻAloʻofa!Kuikuiki, ʻoua naʻá ke tangutu ai.”

“ʻOiauē! sea perry dood,” ko e lea ia ʻa Queequeg, “ko hoku founga fonuá; ʻe ʻikai ke ne fakamamahiʻi hono fofongá.”

"Fesiofaki!" naʻá ku pehē ange, "ui ia ko hono fofongá? fofonga angaʻofa ʻaupito leva; ka ko e hā hono mālohi ʻo ʻene mānavá, ʻokú ne fakamamafaʻi ia; hifo, Kuiki, ʻokú ke mamafa, ʻokú ne vili ʻa e fofonga ʻo e masivá. Hifo, Kuiki! Vakai, te ne vave ʻene luelue atu. ʻOku ou fifili pe ʻoku ʻikai ke ne ʻā hake."

Naʻe toʻo ʻe Queequeg ia ki he tafaʻaki pē ʻe taha ʻo e ʻulu ʻo e tokotaha mohé, peá ne tutu ʻene paipa tomahawk. Naʻá ku tangutu ʻi he vaʻé. Naʻá ma tauhi ʻa e paipá ke fakalaka ʻi he mohé, mei he taha ki he taha. Lolotonga iá, ʻi hono fakafehuʻia ia ʻi heʻene founga motuʻá, naʻe ʻomi ʻe Queequeg ke mahino kiate au, ʻi hono fonuá, koeʻuhí ko e ʻikai ha ngaahi sete mo e ngaahi sofa ʻo e faʻahinga kotoa pē, naʻe ʻi he tōʻonga ʻa e tuʻí, houʻeikí, mo e kakai lalahí fakalūkufua, ke nau fakamomoa ha niʻihi ʻo e ngaahi tuʻunga māʻulalo angé maʻá e kau ʻotomani; pea ke fakanaunau fakafiemālie ha fale ‘i he tafa‘aki ko iá, na‘e pau pē ke ke fakatau ha kau tangata paie ‘e toko valu pe hongofulu, pea fakatoka takai kinautolu ‘i he ngaahi taulanga mo e ngaahi ‘ākoví. ‘Ikai ngata aí, na‘e mātu‘aki faingofua ‘i ha ‘a‘ahi; ‘oku lelei ange ‘aupito ia ‘i he ngaahi sea ngoue ko ia ‘oku lava ke liliu ko e ngaahi va‘akau luelue; ‘i ha taimi, na‘e ui ai ‘e ha ‘eiki ‘a ‘ene sevānití, ‘o ne faka‘amu ke ne ngaohi ha sea ‘o‘ona ‘i lalo ‘i ha fu‘u ‘akau ‘oku mafola, mahalo ‘i ha feitu‘u ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e ‘uha ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e ‘uha.

Lolotonga hono fakamatalaʻi ʻo e ngaahi meʻá ni, ʻi he taimi kotoa pē naʻe maʻu ai ʻe Queequeg ʻa e tomahawk meiate aú, naʻá ne fakatupulaki ʻa e hatchet-side ʻo ia ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he ʻulu ʻo e tokotaha mohé.

“Ko e hā ʻa e meʻa ko iá, Kuiki?”

“Peli faingofua, tamateʻi-e; ʻoiau! peli faingofua!”

Naʻá ne hoko atu ʻaki ha ngaahi manatu vao fekauʻaki mo ʻene tomahawk-pipe, ʻa ia, naʻe hangē, ʻi hono ongo fakaʻaongaʻí naʻá ne fakatou ʻutoʻi hono ngaahi filí mo fakanonga hono laumālié, ʻi he taimi naʻe tohoakiʻi fakahangatonu ai kimaua ki he rigger mohé. Ko e kohu mālohi ʻokú ne fakafonu kakato he taimí ni ʻa e ava kuo fakaʻauʻauhifó, naʻe kamata ke ne tala kiate ia. Naʻá ne mānava ʻi ha faʻahinga fakalongolongo; pea ngali hohaʻa ʻi he ihú; pea vilo holo leva tuʻo taha pe tuʻo ua; peá ne tangutu hake ʻo milimili hono matá.

“ʻOloa!” naʻá ne mānava fakaʻosi, “ko hai ʻa kimoutolu kau ifi tapaká?”

“Kau tangata vaka,” ko ‘eku talí ia, “ko e fē taimi ‘okú ne folau aí?”

“ʻIo, ʻio, ʻoku mou ʻalu ʻi ai, ʻikai? ʻOku folau he ʻaho ni. Naʻe heka mai ʻa e ʻEikivaká ʻaneafi.”

“Ko e Kapiteni fē?—ʻĒhapi?”

“Ko hai mo‘oni ka ko ia pē?”

Naʻá ku teu ke ʻeke ange ha ngaahi fehuʻi lahi ange fekauʻaki mo ʻĒhapi, ʻi heʻemau fanongo ki ha longoaʻa ʻi he funga vaká.

“ʻOloa! Ko e ʻasi ʻa Sitāpaká,” ko e lea ia ʻa e tokotaha fakaʻuli ʻo e meʻalelé. “Ko ha hoa pule moʻui ia, ʻa ia; tangata lelei, mo ha tokotaha lotu; ka ʻoku moʻui kotoa he taimí ni, kuo pau ke u tafoki ki ai.” Pea ʻi heʻene lea peheé naʻá ne ʻalu hake ki he funga vakapuná, pea mau muimui atu.

Naʻe mahino leva ʻa e hopo ʻa e laʻaá. Naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo heka mai ʻa e kau ngāué ʻo tautau toko ua mo toko tolu; naʻe fakaʻaiʻai ʻe he kau riggers kinautolu; naʻe feohi mālohi ʻa e ongo hoá; pea naʻe femoʻuekina ha niʻihi ʻo e kakai ʻo e matāfangá ʻi hono ʻomi ʻo e ngaahi meʻa fakaʻosi kehekehe ki he vaká. Lolotonga iá naʻe kei fakaʻilongaʻi taʻehāmai pē ʻa Kapiteni ʻĒhapi ʻi loto ʻi hono kāpiní.
