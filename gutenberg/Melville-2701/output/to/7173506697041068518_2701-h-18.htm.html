Ko ia, neongo ‘i he ‘ea ma‘a ‘o e ‘ahó, ‘oku tautau ‘i ha kia ‘oku ‘i ai hono ngaahi veini lanu pulū, ‘e ulo mo‘ui lelei ‘a e mata‘itofe taiamoni ‘oku vai ma‘á; ka, ‘i he taimi ‘e fakahaa‘i atu ai ‘e he tokotaha ngaohi siueli kākaá ‘a e taiamoní ‘i hono ngingila fakaofo tahá, ‘okú ne fakatoka ia ‘i ha kelekele fakapo‘uli, pea toki fakamaama ia, ‘o ‘ikai ‘aki ‘a e la‘aá, ka ‘aki ha ngaahi kasa ta‘efakanatula ‘e ni‘ihi. Pea ‘oku ‘asi mai leva ‘a e ngaahi ngingila vela ko iá, ‘oku faka‘ofo‘ofa fakaheli; pea ko e taiamoni vela koví, ‘a ia na‘e hoko ‘i ha taimi ko e faka‘ilonga faka‘otua taha ia ‘o e langi sio‘atá, ‘oku hangē ia ha ngaahi siueli kalauni na‘e kaiha‘asi mei he Tu‘i ‘o Helí. Ka tau ki he talanoa.

Naʻe hoko ʻo pehē, ʻi he meʻa ʻo e ambergris naʻe tuʻumālie ʻa e tangata folau hili ʻa Stubb ʻo ne maʻu hono nimá, ʻo hangē ko ha taimi ke ne fuʻu helaʻia ʻaupito; pea, fakataimi, naʻe tuku ʻa Pipi ki hono tuʻungá.

ʻI he fuofua taimi naʻe hifo ai ʻa Stubb mo iá, naʻe fakahaaʻi ʻe Pip ha manavasiʻi lahi; ka ko e me‘a fakafiefiá, ki he taimi ko iá, na‘e hao mei he fetu‘utaki vāofi mo e tafola; pea ko ia naʻe ʻikai ke ʻalu atu ʻo fakaʻauha fakaʻaufuli; neongo naʻe siofi ia ʻe Situpi, ka naʻá ne tokanga, ʻi he hili iá, ke ne enginakiʻi ia ke ne fakaʻamua ʻene loto-toʻá ʻo aʻu ki he ngataʻangá, he mahalo te ne faʻa ʻiloʻi ʻoku fie maʻu ia.

Ko ʻeni ʻi heʻene hifo hono uá, naʻe folau ʻa e vaká ʻi he tafola; pea ʻi hono maʻu ʻe he iká ʻa e ʻaione darted, naʻá ne ʻoange ʻene rap angamahení, ʻa ia naʻe hoko, ʻi he meʻa ko ʻení, ke ʻi lalo tonu ʻi he sea ʻo e masiva ko Pip. Naʻe fakatupu ʻe he hohaʻa taʻefakakaukauʻi ʻo e momeniti ko iá ke ne puna, ʻo folau ʻi hono nimá, ʻo ʻalu ki tuʻa mei he vaká; pea ʻi ha founga pehē, ko e konga ko ia ʻo e laine tafola lōloá ʻoku haʻu ki hono fatafatá, naʻá ne fatafata ia ʻi ʻolunga mo ia, koeʻuhí ke ne lavelavea ʻi ai, ʻi heʻene faifai pea tō ki he vaí. ʻI he momeniti ko iá naʻe kamata lele fefeka ai ʻa e tafola naʻe taaʻí, naʻe vave ʻa e fakahangatonu ʻa e lainé; mo e presto! naʻe haʻu ʻa Pipi masivá ʻo foam kotoa hake ki he ngaahi foʻi ʻakau ʻo e vaká, ʻo toho taʻefakatomala ia ki ai ʻe he laine, ʻa ia naʻá ne fai ha ngaahi tafoki ʻi hono fatafatá mo hono kiá.

Naʻe tuʻu ʻa Tasiteko ʻi he ngaahi kaufaná. Naʻá ne fonu ʻi he afi ʻo e tulimanú. Naʻá ne fehiʻa ʻia Pipi ʻi ha poltroon. ʻI heʻene toʻo ʻa e hele-vaka mei hono ʻaá, naʻá ne tautau hono tafaʻaki māsila ʻi he lainé, peá ne tafoki atu kia Situpi, ʻo ne kaila fakafehuʻi, “Motuhi?” Lolotonga iá naʻe sio mahino ʻa e fofonga lanu pulū mo lōmekina ʻo Pipí, Fai, koeʻuhí ko e ʻOtuá! Naʻe paasi kotoa ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou. ʻI ha siʻi hifo ʻi ha vaeua miniti, naʻe hoko ʻa e meʻa kotoa ko ʻení.

“Malaʻiaʻi ia, motuhi!” ko e ngungu ia ʻa Situpi; pea ko ia naʻe mole ai ʻa e tafola pea fakahaofi ʻa Pipi.

Ko ia ‘i he‘ene fakaakeake pē iá, na‘e ‘ohofi ‘a e ki‘i negro masivá ‘e he ngaahi kaila mo e ngaahi fakaanga mei he kau ngāué. ʻI heʻene fakaʻatā ʻi he nonga ʻa e ngaahi fakamalaʻia taʻefakalao ko ʻení ke ʻauha, naʻe fakamalaʻiaʻi fakaʻofisiale leva ʻe Stubb ʻi ha founga mahino, hangē ha pisinisí, ka ʻoku kei vaeua pē ʻa e fakakata ʻa Pip; pea ko e meʻa ko iá, naʻá ne ʻoange taʻefakaʻofisiale kiate ia ha faleʻi lelei lahi. Ko e meʻa naʻe ʻi aí, ʻOua naʻá ke teitei puna mei ha vaka, Pipi, tukukehe pē —ka ko e toenga kotoa pē naʻe taʻefakangatangata, ʻo hangē ko e faleʻi lelei taha kuo ʻosi ʻi aí. Ko ʻeni, ʻi he fakalūkufuá, Pipiki ki he vaká , ko hoʻo moto moʻoni ia ʻi he toutai tafola; ka 'e hoko 'a e ngaahi keisi 'i he taimi 'e ni'ihi 'i he taimi 'oku kei lelei ange ai ' a e Puna mei he vaka ,. ‘Ikai ngata aí, ‘o hangē ‘okú ne ‘ilo‘i faka‘osí kapau te ne ‘oange ha fale‘i fakakonisēnisi ta‘efakavaivai‘i kia Pip, te ne tuku ange kiate ia ha tafa‘aki ‘oku fu‘u lahi ke ne puna ki ai ki he kaha‘ú; Naʻe fakafokifā pē ʻa e tukuange ʻe Stubb ʻa e faleʻi kotoa pē, peá ne fakaʻosi ʻaki ha fekau fakamālohi, “Pipiki ki he vaká, Pip, pe ʻe he ʻEikí, he ʻikai te u toʻo hake koe kapau te ke puna; fakakaukau ki he meʻa ko iá. ʻOku ʻikai ke tau lava ʻo fakaʻatā ke mole ʻa e fanga tafola ʻe he kakai hangē ko koé; toe puna.” Mahalo naʻe fakaʻilongaʻi taʻefakahangatonu ʻe Stubb ʻi heni, neongo naʻe ʻofa ʻa e tangatá ʻi hono kaungāmeʻá, ka ko e tangatá ko ha monumanu kumi paʻanga, ʻa ia ʻoku fuʻu faʻa fakalaveaʻi ʻe he propensity ʻene angaʻofá.

Ka ‘oku tau ‘i he nima kotoa ‘o e ngaahi ‘Otua; pea toe puna ʻa Pipi. Naʻe ʻi ha ngaahi tūkunga meimei tatau ʻaupito ia mo e ʻuluaki fakaʻaliʻalí; ka ʻi he taimi ko ʻení naʻe ʻikai ke ne fatafata ʻa e lainé; pea ko ia ai, ‘i he kamata ke lele ‘a e tafola, na‘e tuku ‘a Pipi ‘i he tahí, ‘o hangē ha fu‘u ‘akau ‘o ha tokotaha fononga fakavavevavé. ‘Oiauē! Naʻe fuʻu moʻoni ʻa Stubb ki heʻene leá. Ko ha ʻaho fakaʻofoʻofa, lahi, mo lanu pulū ia; ko e tahi ngingilá ‘oku nonga mo momoko, pea ‘oku mafola faka‘aufuli, ‘o takai kotoa, ki he ‘ataá, ‘o hangē ha kili ‘o e tokotaha tā koulá ‘oku hāmala‘i ki tu‘a ki he tu‘unga mā‘olungá. ʻI heʻene bobbing hake mo hifo ʻi he tahi ko iá, naʻe hā mai ʻa e ʻulu ʻeponi ʻo Pipí ʻo hangē ha ʻulu ʻo e ʻakaú. Naʻe ʻikai ke hiki hake ha meʻafana vaka ʻi heʻene tō vave pehē ki he tafaʻaki ki muí. Naʻe tafoki ʻa e tuʻa taʻe-fakaʻatuʻi ʻo Stubb kiate ia; pea naʻe kapakau ʻa e tafola. ʻI ha miniti ʻe tolu, naʻe ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo Pipi mo Situpi ha maile kakato ʻo e ʻoseni taʻe ʻuta. ʻI tuʻa mei he lotolotonga ʻo e tahí, naʻe tafoki ʻe Pipi masivá hono ʻulu ngingila, ngingila, mo ʻuliʻulí ki he laʻaá, ko ha toe tangata lī tokotaha pē, neongo ʻoku māʻolunga taha mo ngingila tahá.

Ko e taimí ni, ‘i he ‘ea nonga, ke luelue ‘i he ‘oseni ‘ata‘ataá ‘oku faingofua pē ia ki he tokotaha luelue ‘oku ‘osi ako‘í ‘o hangē pē ko e heka ‘i ha saliote puna ‘i ‘uta. Ka ko e nofo tokotaha fakalilifú ʻoku ʻikai lava ke kātakiʻi. Ko e fakakaukau lahi ʻo e self ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo ha immensity taʻeʻofa pehē, ʻe hoku ʻOtua! ko hai ʻe lava ke tala ia? Maʻake, ʻa e founga ʻoku kaukau ai ʻa e kau folaú ʻi ha nonga mate ʻi he tahi ʻataʻataá —fakaʻilongaʻi ʻa e vāofi ʻo ʻenau ʻuma ki honau vaká pea ʻuta pē ʻi hono ongo tafaʻakí.

Ka naʻe liʻaki moʻoni ʻe Stubb ʻa e kiʻi negro masivá ki hono ikuʻangá? ʻIkai; naʻe ʻikai ke ne ʻuhinga ki ai, ʻi he siʻisiʻi tahá. He naʻe ʻi ai ha ongo vaka ʻi hono ʻaá, pea naʻá ne fakakaukau, ʻoku ʻikai ha veiveiua, ko e moʻoni te na haʻu vave ʻaupito kia Pip, ʻo ʻave ia; neongo, ko e mo‘oni, ko e ngaahi fakakaukau peheé ki he kau folau vakapuna ‘oku fakatu‘utāmaki‘i ‘i he‘enau mā pē ‘anautolú, ‘oku ‘ikai ke fakahaa‘i ma‘u pē ia ‘e he kau tulimanú ‘i he ngaahi tu‘unga meimei tatau kotoa pē; pea ʻoku ʻikai ke taʻefaʻa hoko ʻa e ngaahi meʻa peheé; meimei ‘i he taimi toutai, ‘oku faka‘ilonga‘i ai ha tokotaha manavahē, ‘oku ui pehē, ‘aki ‘a e fakalili‘a ta‘e‘ofa tatau ‘oku makehe ki he ngaahi tautahi fakakautaú mo e ngaahi kau taú.

Ka naʻe hoko ia, ʻo tafoki ʻa e ngaahi vaka ko iá, ʻo ʻikai ke nau sio kia Pip, ʻo nau sipai fakafokifā ʻa e fanga tafola ofi mai kiate kinautolu ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá, ʻo nau tuli; pea naʻe fuʻu mamaʻo ʻaupito ʻa e vaka ʻo Situpú he taimí ni, pea naʻá ne fuʻu fakakaukau mo ʻene kau ngāué kotoa ki heʻene iká, ʻo kamata ke fakalahi fakaʻofa ʻa e ʻataʻatā mama ʻo Pipí ʻo takatakai ʻiate ia. ‘I he tu‘unga fakatu‘upakē pē na‘e faifai pē ‘o fakahaofi ia ‘e he vaká tonu; ka mei he houa ko iá naʻe ʻalu holo ʻa e kiʻi negro ʻi he funga vaká ko ha vale; pehē, ʻi he siʻisiʻi tahá, naʻa nau pehē ko ia. Naʻe tauhi fakakata ʻe he tahí hono sino fakangatangatá, ka naʻá ne melemo ʻa e taʻefakangatangata ʻo hono laumālié. Kae kehe, ‘ikai melemo faka‘aufuli. Ka na‘e ‘ave mo‘ui hifo ia ki he ngaahi loloto fakaofo, ‘a ia na‘e lele holo ai ‘a e ngaahi fōtunga ngali kehe ‘o e māmani ‘uluaki ta‘efakapikopikó ‘i mu‘a ‘i hono ongo mata ta‘e‘aonga; pea naʻe fakahā ʻe he miser-merman, ko Potó, ʻene ngaahi fuʻu ʻakau kuo tānakí; pea ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi ʻitāniti fiefia, taʻeloto, mo kei talavou maʻu peé, naʻe sio ʻa Pipi ki he fanga kiʻi ʻinisēkite ʻātomi tokolahi, ʻoku ʻi he feituʻu kotoa pē ʻa e ʻOtuá, ʻa ia naʻa nau ʻohake mei he langi ʻo e ngaahi vaí ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻataʻatā lalahí. Naʻa ne mamata ki he vaʻe ʻoe ʻOtua ʻi he tuʻunga ʻoe tulanga, pea ne lea ʻaki ia; pea ko ia naʻe ui ai ia ʻe hono kaungā vaká ko e vale. Ko ia ko e vale ‘a e tangatá ko e ongo‘i ‘o e langí; pea ʻi heʻene hē mei he ʻuhinga fakamatelie kotoa pē, ʻoku faifai pea aʻu mai ʻa e tangatá ki he fakakaukau fakasilesitiale ko iá, ʻa ia, ki he fakakaukau, ʻoku fakavalevale mo fakatupu ʻohovale; pea koloa‘ia pe mala‘ia, ‘okú ne ongo‘i leva ‘oku ‘ikai ke ne fakangaloku, ‘o ‘ikai ke ne tokanga ‘o hangē ko hono ‘Otuá.

Mo e toenga, tukuaki'i 'oua 'e Stubb fu'u fefeka. Ko e meʻa ʻoku angamaheni ʻi he toutai ko iá; pea ʻi he hokohoko ʻo e fakamatalá, ʻe toki sio ki he meʻa naʻe hoko kiate au ʻo hangē ko e liʻakí.
