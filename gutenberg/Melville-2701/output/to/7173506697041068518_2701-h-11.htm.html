VAHE 53. Koe Kami.

Ko e ʻuhinga hā mai naʻe ʻikai ke heka ai ʻa ʻĒhapi ki he vaka tafola naʻá ma talanoa ki aí ko ʻení: naʻe fakaʻilongaʻi ʻe he matangí mo e tahí ha ngaahi afā. Ka neongo kapau naʻe ʻikai ke pehē ʻeni, mahalo naʻe ʻikai ke ne mei heka kiate ia —ʻo fakamāuʻi ʻaki ʻene tōʻonga kimui ange ʻi he ngaahi taimi tatau —kapau naʻe pehē, ʻi he founga ʻo e fakafetaʻí, naʻá ne maʻu ai ha tali kovi ki he fehuʻi naʻá ne ʻeké. He, hangē ko ia naʻe faifai pea hokó, naʻá ne tokanga ke ʻoua naʻá ne feohi, naʻa mo ha miniti ʻe nima, mo ha kapiteni muli, tukukehe kapau te ne lava ʻo tokoniʻi ha niʻihi ʻo e fakamatala ko ia naʻá ne fekumi fakamātoato ki aí. Ka 'e kei ta'efe'unga nai 'a e fakafuofua kotoa 'o e me'a ni, kapau na'e 'ikai ko ha me'a 'oku lea'aki heni 'o kau ki he ngaahi ngaue'aki makehe 'o e ngaahi vaka toutai tafola 'i he taimi 'oku nau fetaulaki ai 'i he ngaahi tahi muli, pea tautautefito ki ha feitu'u folau 'eve'eva angamaheni.

Kapau ko ha ongo muli ʻokú na kolosi ʻi he Pine Barrens ʻi he Siteiti Niu ʻIoké, pe ko e Teleʻa Salisbury ʻi ʻIngilani ʻoku maomaonganoa tatau; kapau ʻoku nau fetaulaki fakatuʻupakē ʻi ha ngaahi vaotā taʻefakaʻapaʻapa pehē, ʻe ʻikai lava ʻe he ongo meʻá ni, koeʻuhí ko ʻena moʻuí, ke fakaʻehiʻehi mei ha fefakafeʻiloakiʻaki; pea tuʻu ʻi ha kiʻi taimi ke fefakatauʻaki ʻa e ongoongó; pea, mahalo, ko e tangutu hifo ʻi ha kiʻi taimi ʻo mālōlō fakataha: pea, ko e meʻa fakanatula ange ia ʻi he ʻolunga ʻo e ngaahi ʻOtu Paini taʻefakangatangata mo e ʻOtu Moʻunga Salisbury ʻo e tahí, ʻoku fefakaangaʻaki ha ongo vaka toutai tafola ʻi he ngaahi ngataʻanga ʻo e māmaní —ʻi he ʻOtu Motu Fanning tokotaha pē, pe ko e ʻOtu Motu Tuʻi mamaʻó; ʻoku fakanatula ange ʻaupito, ʻoku ou pehē, ʻi he ngaahi tūkunga peheé ʻoku ʻikai ngata pē ʻi he fefakatauʻaki ʻa e ngaahi vaka ko ʻení ʻi he ʻuha maka, ka ʻoku nau toe fetuʻutaki vāofi ange, anga fakakaumeʻa mo fakafeohi ange. Pea tautautefito ki he ngali ko ha meʻa pau ʻeni, ʻi he ngaahi vaka ʻoku ʻi ha taulanga ʻe taha, pea ʻoku feʻiloaki fakatāutaha ʻenau kau kapitení, kau ʻōfisá, pea ʻikai ko ha tokosiʻi ʻo e kau tangatá; pea ko hono olá, ʻoku ʻi ai ha ngaahi meʻa fakaʻapi ʻofeina kehekehe ke talanoa ki ai.

Mo e vaka fuoloa 'ene 'ikai ke 'i ai, ko e outward-bounder, mahalo, 'oku 'i ai ha ngaahi tohi 'i vaka; ʻi ha faʻahinga tuʻunga pē, te ne fakapapauʻi ke tuku ke ne maʻu ha ngaahi pepa ʻo ha ʻaho ʻi ha taʻu ʻe taha pe ua kimui ange ʻi he fakamuimuitaha ʻi heʻene ngaahi faile ʻoku ʻikai ke hā mahino mo ʻosi ʻi he louhiʻi nimá. Pea ko e totongi ‘o e anga-faka‘apa‘apa ko iá, ‘e ma‘u ai ‘e he vaka ‘oku ‘alu ki tu‘á ‘a e ‘atamai fakamuimuitaha ‘o e toutai tafola mei he feitu‘u folau ‘eve‘eva ‘a ia ‘e fakataumu‘a nai ki aí, ko ha me‘a ‘oku mahu‘inga taha kiate ia. Pea ‘i he tu‘unga, ‘e mo‘oni kotoa ‘eni fekau‘aki mo e ngaahi vaka toutai tafola ‘oku nau fekolosi‘aki ‘i he hala ‘o e folau ‘eve‘evá tonu, neongo ‘oku nau fuoloa tatau pē ‘o ‘ikai ke nau ‘i ‘api. He mahalo naʻe maʻu ʻe ha taha ʻo kinautolu ha hiki tohi mei ha vahe tolu ʻe niʻihi, pea ko e vaka mamaʻo ʻaupito he taimí ni; pea ko e niʻihi ʻo e ngaahi tohi ko iá mahalo ʻoku maʻá e kakai ʻo e vaka ʻokú ne fetaulaki mo ia he taimí ni. ‘Ikai ngata aí, te na fefakatau‘aki ‘a e ongoongo ‘o e toutai tafola, pea fai ha talanoa fakafiefia. He ʻe ʻikai ngata pē ʻi heʻenau fetaulaki mo e ngaahi kaungāongoʻi kotoa ʻa e kau folaú, ka ʻe pehē pē mo e ngaahi fiefia makehe kotoa ʻoku tupu mei ha tulituli angamaheni mo e ngaahi faingataʻaʻia mo e ngaahi fakatuʻutāmaki ʻoku nau fevahevaheʻaki.

Pea ‘e ‘ikai foki ke fai ‘e he kehekehe ‘o e fonuá ha kehekehe mātu‘aki mahu‘inga; ko ia, 'o kapau 'oku lea 'a e ongo paati 'i he lea 'e taha, 'o hange ko e me'a 'oku hoko ki he kau 'Amelika mo e kau 'Ingilani. Neongo, ke fakapapau'i, mei he tokosi'i 'o e kau toutai tafola 'Ingilani, 'oku 'ikai ke fu'u fa'a hoko 'a e ngaahi fakataha pehe, pea 'i he taimi 'oku nau hoko ai 'oku fu'u apt ke 'i ai ha fa'ahinga maa 'i hona vaha'a; he 'oku 'ikai ke reserved ho'o tangata 'Ingilani, pea ko ho'o Yankee, 'oku 'ikai ke ne fancy 'a e fa'ahinga me'a ko ia 'i ha taha ka ko ia pe. ‘Ikai ngata aí, ‘oku uesia ‘e he kau toutai tafola ‘Ingilaní ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi ha fa‘ahinga mā‘olunga ‘i he kolomu‘á ‘i he kau toutai tafola ‘Ameliká; fekau'aki mo e Nantucketer loloa mo sino, mo 'ene ngaahi fakavahefonua 'oku 'ikai ke fakamatala'i, ko ha fa'ahinga 'o e tangata ngoue tahi. Ka ko e feituʻu ʻoku ʻi ai moʻoni ʻa e maʻolunga ko ʻeni ʻi he kau tafola ʻIngilaní, ʻe faingataʻa ke pehē, ʻi heʻeku sio ki he Yankees ʻi ha ʻaho ʻe taha, fakatahatahaʻi, ʻoku nau tāmateʻi ha fanga tafola lahi ange ʻi he kau ʻIngilani kotoa pē, fakatahatahaʻi, ʻi ha taʻu ʻe hongofulu. Ka ko e ki'i vaivai'anga ta'efakatupu maumau 'eni 'i he kau tuli tafola 'Ingilani, 'a ia 'oku 'ikai ke fu'u tokanga ki ai 'a e kau Nantucketer; mahalo pē, koeʻuhí he ʻokú ne ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻi ai ʻene ngaahi vaivaiʻanga siʻisiʻi ʻiate ia pē.

Ko ia, ‘oku tau sio ai ‘i he ngaahi vaka kotoa pē ‘oku nau folau kehekehe ‘i he tahí, ‘oku ma‘u ‘e he kau toutai tafola ‘a e ‘uhinga lahi taha ke nau feohi ai—pea ‘oku nau pehē. Ka, ko e ngaahi vaka fakatau ʻe niʻihi ʻoku nau fekolosiʻaki ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ʻAtalanitikí, ʻe faʻa ʻalu atu ʻo ʻikai ha foʻi lea ʻe taha ʻo e fakaʻilongaʻí, ʻo nau femotuʻaki ʻi he ngaahi tahi māʻolungá, ʻo hangē ha fuʻu ʻakau ʻo e kau dandies ʻi Broadway; pea ko e taimi kotoa pe indulging, mahalo, 'i he fakaanga finical 'i he rig 'o e taha mo e taha. Ko e meʻa ki he Kau Tau-Taú, ʻi he taimi ʻoku nau faingamālie ai ke fetaulaki ʻi tahí, ʻoku nau ʻuluaki fou ʻi ha faʻahinga punou fakavalevale mo e toʻotoʻo, ko ha ducking ʻo e ngaahi fuka, ʻoku ʻikai ke ngali lahi ha loto-lelei mo e ʻofa fakatokoua ʻi he loto-toʻomataʻú fekauʻaki mo ia ʻi ha meʻa pē. As touching Slave-ships fakataha, ko e ha, 'oku nau 'i ha fakavavevave fakaofo pehe, 'oku nau hola mei he taha mo e taha 'i he vave taha. Pea ko e kau Kaihaʻá, ʻi heʻenau faingamālie ke fekolosiʻaki ʻi he ngaahi hui kolosi ʻo e taha mo e taha, ko e ʻuluaki ʻuhá ko e —“Ko e ʻuluʻulu ʻe fiha?”—ko e founga tatau pē ʻoku ʻuha ai ʻa e kau kaihaʻá—“Ko e paʻale ʻe fiha?” Pea ko e fehu'i ko ia 'i he taimi 'oku tali ai, 'oku steer hangatonu 'a e kau kaiha'a, he ko e kau infernal villains kinautolu 'i he ongo tafa'aki, pea 'oku 'ikai ke nau sai'ia ke sio ki he overmuch 'o e ngaahi fakatata villanous 'o e taha mo e taha.

Kae vakai ki he tangata toutai tafola anga faka‘otua, faitotonu, ta‘efaka‘ali‘ali, talitali kakai, feohi, tau‘atāina-mo-faingofua! Ko e hā ‘oku fai ‘e he tangata toutai tafola ‘i he‘ene fetaulaki mo ha toutai tafola ‘e taha ‘i ha fa‘ahinga ‘ea faka‘apa‘apa pē? ‘Oku ‘i ai ‘ene “ Gam ,” ko ha me‘a ‘oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ‘ilo‘i ia ‘e he ngaahi vaka kehe kotoa pē ‘o ‘ikai te nau teitei fanongo ai ki he hingoá na‘a mo ia; pea kapau ʻoku nau fanongo fakatuʻupakē ki ai, ʻoku nau malimali pē ki ai, pea toutou fai ha ngaahi meʻa fakaʻofoʻofa fekauʻaki mo e “spouters” mo e “blubber-boilers,” mo e ngaahi meʻa hangē ko e ngaahi kaila fakaʻofoʻofá. Ko e ha 'oku faka'amua ai 'e he kau Merchant-seamen kotoa pe, pea pehe foki ki he kau Pirates kotoa pe mo e kau tangata 'o e Man-of-War, mo e kau folau vaka-Popula, 'a e ongo fakaanga pehe ki he ngaahi vaka-Tafola; ko e fehu'i 'eni 'e faingata'a ke tali. He, ʻi he meʻa ʻa e kau kaihaʻá, talamai, ʻoku totonu ke u fie ʻilo pe ʻoku ʻi ai ha nāunau makehe ʻo ʻenau ngāue ko iá fekauʻaki mo ia. ʻOku ngata ia ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻi ha māʻolunga taʻe angamaheni, moʻoni; ka ʻi he tautauʻanga pē. Pea ‘ikai ngata aí, ‘i he taimi ‘oku hiki hake ai ha tangata ‘i he founga kehekehe ko iá, ‘oku ‘ikai ha‘ane makatu‘unga totonu ki hono mā‘olunga angé. Ko ia ai, ‘oku ou faka‘osi‘aki, ‘i he‘ene pōlepole ‘i he‘ene hiki hake mā‘olunga ‘i ha tangata tafola, ‘i he fakapapau ko iá ‘oku ‘ikai ha makatu‘unga fefeka ‘a e kaiha‘á ke ne tu‘u ai.

Ka ko e hā ha Gam? Mahalo te ke vaivai ho louhiʻi nima fakaʻilonga ʻi he lele hake mo hifo ʻi he ngaahi kōlomu ʻo e ngaahi tikisinalé, pea ʻikai ke ke teitei maʻu ʻa e foʻi leá. Naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke aʻusia ʻe Toketā Sionisoni ʻa e ʻilo ko iá; ʻOku ʻikai ke pukepuke ia ʻe he ʻaʻake ʻo Noa Uepisitá. Ka neongo ia, ko e foʻi lea fakahaaʻi tatau ko ʻení kuo ngāueʻaki maʻu pē he taimí ni ʻi ha ngaahi taʻu lahi ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e kau Yankees fanauʻi moʻoni ʻe toko hongofulu mā nima afe nai. Ko e mo'oni, 'oku fiema'u ha faka'uhinga, pea 'oku totonu ke fakakau ia ki he Lexicon. ʻI he vakai ko iá, tuku ke u fakaʻuhingaʻi fakaako ia.

GAM. NOUN— Ko e fakataha fakasosiale 'a e ongo ( pe lahi ange ) ' o e Vaka Tafola, 'oku angamaheni 'aki 'i ha cruising-ground; ‘i he taimi, hili ‘enau fefakatau‘aki ‘a e ‘uhá, ‘oku nau fefakatau‘aki ‘a‘ahi ai ‘a e kau ngāue ‘o e ngaahi vaká: ko e ongo kapitení ‘oku kei toe, ‘i he taimi ko iá, ‘i he vaka ‘e tahá, pea ko e ongo hoa pule ‘i he vaka ‘e tahá.

'Oku 'iai moe ki'i me'a 'e taha fekau'aki mo e Gamming 'oku 'ikai totonu ke ngalo heni. Ko e ngaahi ngāue kotoa pē ‘oku ‘i ai ‘enau fanga ki‘i me‘a makehe ‘o e fakaikiikí; ‘oku pehē pē mo e toutai tafola. ʻI ha vaka kaihaʻa, tangata-tau, pe vaka pōpula, ʻi he taimi ʻoku folau ai ʻa e ʻeikitaú ʻi ha feituʻu pē ʻi heʻene vaká, ʻokú ne tangutu maʻu pē ʻi he ngaahi laʻipepa ʻi mui ʻi ha sea fakafiemālie, ʻoku ʻi ai ha taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻoku ʻi ai hano fakaʻofoʻofa ʻi ai, pea faʻa fakaʻuli ia ʻaki ha kiʻi meʻa fakaʻofoʻofa ʻo e milliner ʻoku teuteuʻi ʻaki ha ngaahi maea faka-gay mo e ngaahi lipine. Ka ko e vaka-tafola 'oku 'ikai ha'ane sea 'i mui, 'ikai ha sofa 'o e fa'ahinga ko ia pe, pea 'ikai ha tiller 'aupito. Taimi ma'olunga mo'oni, kapau na'e faka'uli 'a e kau kapiteni toutai tafola 'i he vai 'i he castors hange ko e gouty motu'a aldermen 'i he ngaahi sea pateni. Pea ko e me‘a ki ha tiller, ‘oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ke fakahaa‘i ‘e he vaka-tafola ha fa‘ahinga fakaefefine pehē; pea ko ia ai ʻo hangē ko e vaʻingá kuo pau ke mavahe ha kau ngāue vaka kakato mei he vaká, pea ko ia ai ʻi he ʻi he tokolahí ʻa e tokotaha fakaʻuli vaká pe fakaʻuli vaká, ko e tokotaha maʻulalo ko iá ʻa e tokotaha fakaʻuli vaká ʻi he taimi ko iá, pea ko e kapitení, ʻi he ʻikai haʻane feituʻu ke tangutu aí, ʻoku toho ia ki heʻene ʻaʻahí ʻoku nau tuʻu kotoa ʻo hangē ha fuʻu painé. Pea te ke fa'a fakatokanga'i 'i he'ene 'ilo'i 'a e mata 'o e mamani 'oku 'asi kotoa 'oku malolo kiate ia mei he ongo tafa'aki 'o e ongo vaka, 'oku mo'ui kotoa 'a e kapiteni tu'u ko 'eni ki he mahu'inga 'o hono poupou'i hono ngeia 'aki hono tauhi hono ongo va'e. Pea ‘oku ‘ikai foki ko ha me‘a faingofua ‘aupito eni; he ʻi hono tuʻá ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e fuʻu ʻauʻau fakaʻuli ʻoku ʻasi hake ʻokú ne taaʻi ia he taimí ni pea ʻi he kiʻi konga ʻo hono tuʻá, ʻoku reciprocating ʻa e ʻauʻau ʻi muí ʻaki ʻene rapping hono tuí ʻi muʻa. Ko ia ‘oku ne wedged kakato ‘i mu‘a mo mui, pea ‘oku lava pē ke ne fakalahi ia ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘aki ‘ene nofo hifo ‘i hono ongo va‘e kuo mafao atu; ka ko ha pā fakafokifā mo fakamālohi ʻo e vaká ʻe faʻa ʻalu mamaʻo ia ke fakahifo ia, koeʻuhí he ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa ʻa e lōloa ʻo e fakavaʻé ʻo ʻikai ha laulahi feʻunga. Fai pē ha tuliki mafola ʻo ha ongo pou, pea he ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo tuʻu hake kinaua. Pea, ko e toe, he ʻikai pē ke ne lava ʻi he sio mahino ʻa e mata ʻo e māmaní ʻoku fakaʻilongaʻí, he ʻikai pē ke ne lava, ʻoku ou pehē, ki he kapiteni ʻoku ʻalu holo ko ʻení ke sio ki ai ʻokú ne fakatuʻumaʻuʻi ia ʻi he kiʻi konga siʻisiʻi tahá ʻaki ʻene puke ha meʻa ʻaki hono ongo nimá; mo‘oni, ko e faka‘ilonga ‘o ‘ene pule‘i-kita fakakātoa mo faka‘au‘auhifó, ‘okú ne ‘ave fakalūkufua hono ongo nimá ‘i he ngaahi kato ‘o hono talausese; ka mahalo koe‘uhi ‘i he tu‘unga fakalūkufuá ‘oku fu‘u lahi mo mamafa ‘a e nimá, ‘okú ne ‘ave kinautolu ki ai ko ha me‘a faka‘ai‘ai. Ka neongo ia kuo hoko ha ngaahi meʻa, ʻoku fakamoʻoniʻi lelei foki, ʻa ia kuo ʻiloa ai ʻa e ʻeikitaú ʻi ha momeniti fakatuʻutāmaki taʻe angamaheni ʻe taha pe ua, ʻi ha lea fakafokifā ʻa e ʻuha —ke ne puke ʻa e fulufuluʻi ʻulu ʻo e tokotaha folau ofi tahá, pea puke maʻu ʻi ai ʻo hangē ha mate fakalilifú.





VAHE 54. Koe Talanoa oe Kolo-Ho.

( Hangē ko ia naʻe fakamatalaʻi ʻi he Fale Tauhiʻanga Koulá. )

Ko e Keipi ʻo e ʻAmanaki Leleí, mo e vahefonua vai kotoa ʻoku takatakai ʻi aí, ʻoku hangē ia ha ngaahi tuliki ʻe fā ʻoku fakatokangaʻi ʻe niʻihi ʻo ha hala lahi, ʻa ia ʻokú ke fetaulaki ai mo ha kau fononga tokolahi ange ʻi ha toe feituʻu kehe.

Na‘e ‘ikai fu‘u fuoloa mei he‘ene lea‘aki ‘a e lea faka-Goney na‘e fetaulaki ai mo ha tangata tafola ‘e taha ‘oku foki ki ‘api, ko e Town-Ho,*. Naʻe meimei fakaʻaongaʻi kotoa ia ʻe he kau Polinisiá. ʻI he gam nounou naʻe hokó naʻá ne ʻomi ha ongoongo mālohi ʻo e Moby Dick. Ki he niʻihi naʻe fakautuutu ʻaupito ʻa e mahuʻingaʻia fakalūkufua ʻi he Tafola Hinehina he taimí ni ʻe ha tūkunga ʻo e talanoa ʻa Town-Ho, ʻa ia naʻe ngali fakapoʻuli ʻoku kau ai mo e tafola ha ʻaʻahi fakaofo mo fakafetongi pau ʻo e taha ʻo e ngaahi fakamaau ʻoku ui ʻe he ʻOtuá ʻa ia ʻoku pehē ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻoku ne ʻohofi ha kau tangata ʻe niʻihi. Ko e tu‘unga fakamuimui ko ení, mo hono ngaahi ‘alu fakataha makehé, ‘a ia ‘okú ne fa‘u ‘a e me‘a ‘e ui nai ko e konga fakapulipuli ‘o e me‘a fakamamahi ‘oku teu ke fakamatalá, na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito ke a‘u ia ki he telinga ‘o e ‘Eikitau ko ‘Ēhapí pe ko hono ngaahi hoá. He ko e konga fakapulipuli ko ia ʻo e talanoá naʻe ʻikai ʻiloʻi ia ʻe he ʻeikivaka ʻo e Town-Ho tonu. Ko e koloa fakafoʻituitui ia ʻa ha kau toutai hinehina ʻe toko tolu ʻo e vaka ko iá, ʻa ia ko e taha ʻo kinautolú, ʻoku hangē, naʻá ne fakahoko ia kia Tasiteko ʻaki ha ngaahi fekau ʻa Loma ʻo e fakapulipulí, ka ʻi he pō hono hokó naʻe ʻalu holo ʻa Tasitego ʻi heʻene mohé, pea fakahaaʻi ʻa e lahi ʻo e meʻa ko iá ʻi he founga ko iá, ʻo ʻi he taimi naʻe fafangu ai iá naʻe ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo maʻu ʻa e mālōloó. “‘Ko ia tokoni mai kiate au Hēvani, pea ʻi hoku lāngilangí ko e talanoa kuó u talaatu kiate kimoutolu, houʻeiki tangata, ʻoku ʻi he kakanó mo hono ngaahi meʻa lalahí, ʻoku moʻoni. ʻOku ou ʻiloʻi ʻoku moʻoni ia; naʻe hoko ia ʻi he foʻi pulu ko ʻení; naʻá ku laka ʻi he vaká; naʻá ku ʻiloʻi ʻa e kau ngāué; kuó u sio mo talanoa mo Steelkilt talu mei he pekia ʻa Radney.’”
