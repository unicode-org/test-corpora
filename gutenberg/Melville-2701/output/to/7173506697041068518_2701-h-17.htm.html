Neongo ko e tokolahi taha ‘o e fanga tafola, ‘o hangē ko ia kuo ‘osi lea‘akí, na‘a nau ‘i he nga‘unu fakamālohi, ka ‘oku totonu ke fakatokanga‘i ‘i hono fakakātoá na‘e ‘ikai ke laka ki mu‘a pe holomui ‘a e tākangá, ka na‘a nau nofo fakatahataha pē ‘i ha feitu‘u pē ‘e taha. Hangē ko ia ‘oku anga-maheni‘aki ‘i he ngaahi tu‘unga ko iá, na‘e mavahe leva ‘a e ongo vaká, ‘o taki taha ngaohi ha tafola ‘e taha ‘i he tuliki ‘o e ‘aá. ʻI ha miniti ʻe tolu nai, naʻe lī ʻa e foʻi ʻakau ʻa Queequeg; naʻe lele fakaʻuli ʻa e ika naʻe taaʻí ʻi homa fofongá, peá ne toki hola mo kimaua ʻo hangē ha māmá, ʻo fakaʻuli hangatonu ki he loto ʻo e tākangá. Neongo ko ha nga‘unu pehē ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘o e tafola na‘e taa‘i ‘i he ngaahi tu‘unga peheé, ‘oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ko ha me‘a ta‘ehanotatau ia; pea ko e moʻoni ʻoku meimei ke lahi ange pe siʻisiʻi ange ʻa e ʻamanaki ki aí; ka ‘okú ne ‘omai ‘a e taha ‘o e ngaahi liliu fakatu‘utāmaki ange ‘o e toutaí. He ʻi hono toho koe ʻe he monster vave ʻo loloto ange mo loloto ange ki he shoal frantic, ʻokú ke bid adieu ke circumspect moʻui pea ʻoku ʻi ai pē ʻi ha throb delirious.

ʻI heʻene kui mo tuli, naʻe tō atu ʻa e tafola ki muʻá, ʻo hangē ʻi he mālohi ʻo e vave ke ne toʻo meiate ia ʻa e ʻuhikiʻi ukamea naʻe fakamaʻu kiate iá; ʻi heʻemau haeʻi pehē ha foʻi ava hinehina ʻi he tahí, ʻo fakamanamanaʻi ʻi he tafaʻaki kotoa pē ʻi heʻemau puná, ʻe he fanga kiʻi meʻamoʻui valevale ʻoku nau lele takai holo ʻiate kimautolú; naʻe hangē ʻemau vaka naʻe ʻohofi iá ha vaka ʻoku fakaʻauha ʻe he ngaahi motu ʻaisí ʻi ha afā, pea feinga ke fakaʻuli ʻi honau ngaahi halanga faingataʻá mo e ngaahi hala ʻoku ʻikai ke nau ʻiloʻi pe ko e fē ʻa e momeniti ʻe ala lokaʻi mo laiki ai iá.

Ka naʻe ʻikai ke kiʻi ilifia, naʻe fakahinohinoʻi fakatangata kimaua ʻe Queequeg; ko ʻeni ʻoku sheering off mei he monster ko ʻeni ʻo fakahangatonu ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo ʻemau halá ʻi muʻa; ko ʻeni ʻoku ne fakaʻau ke mamaʻo mei he meʻa ko iá, ʻa ia naʻe tautau ʻi ʻolunga ʻene ngaahi fuʻu ʻuhiki lalahí, lolotonga ia ʻi he taimi kotoa pē, naʻe tuʻu hake ʻa Starbuck ʻi he ngaahi kaufaná, ʻo ʻi hono nimá ʻa e tao, ʻo ne tuʻusi mei homau halá ha faʻahinga tafola pē te ne lava ʻo aʻu ki ai ʻaki ha ngaahi foʻi ngahau nounou, he naʻe ʻikai ha taimi ke ne ngaohi ai ha ngaahi foʻi tafola lōloa. Naʻe ʻikai foki ke fuʻu noaʻia ʻa e kau folaú, neongo naʻe ʻosi fakaʻauha fakaʻaufuli ʻa honau fatongia angamahení he taimí ni. Naʻa nau tefito ʻi he konga kaila ʻo e pisinisí. “Mavahe mei he halá, Komotā!” ko e kaila ia ʻa e taha, ki ha fuʻu dromedary lahi ʻa ia naʻe fakafokifā pē ʻene ʻalu hake fakasinó ki he fukahi vaí, pea ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou naʻá ne fakamanamanaʻi ke ne fakaʻauha kimautolu. “Fefeka hifo mo ho hiku, ʻi ai!” ko e tangi ia ʻa ha sekoni ki ha taha ʻe taha, ʻa ia, ʻi heʻene ofi ki heʻemau gunwale, naʻe hangē ʻokú ne fakamokomokoʻi fakanonga ia ʻaki hono tafaʻaki ʻoʻona ʻoku hangē ha faná.

Ko e ngaahi vaka tafola kotoa pē ‘oku nau ‘ave ‘a e ngaahi me‘a fakatupu fie‘ilo pau, ‘a ia na‘e ‘uluaki fa‘u ‘e he kau ‘Initia Nantucket, ‘a ia ‘oku ui ko e druggs. Ko e ongo tapafā ‘akau matolu ‘oku tatau hono lahí ‘oku fakapipiki fefeka fakataha ia, koe‘uhi ke na fekolosi‘aki ‘i he ngaahi tenga‘i ‘akau ‘i he ngaahi tuliki totonu; ‘oku fakapipiki leva ha laine ‘oku fu‘u lōloa ‘aupito ki he lotolotonga ‘o e poloka ko ení, pea ‘i hono takai‘i ‘a e ngata‘anga ‘e taha ‘o e lainé, ‘e lava ke fakama‘u ia ‘i ha ki‘i momeniti ki ha fo‘i ‘akau. Ko e tefito'i 'i he lotolotonga 'o e fanga tafola gallied 'oku faka'aonga'i 'a e drugg ko 'eni. He ko ia, ‘oku lahi ange ‘a e fanga tafola ‘oku nau ofi mai ‘iate koe ‘i he me‘a te ke lava ‘o tuli ‘i ha taimi ‘e taha. Ka ‘oku ‘ikai ke fetaulaki ‘i he ‘aho kotoa pē ‘a e fanga tafola sperm; ko ia, lolotonga ho‘o malavá, kuo pau ke ke tāmate‘i ‘a e me‘a kotoa pē te ke lavá. Pea kapau ‘oku ‘ikai te ke lava ‘o tāmate‘i kotoa kinautolu ‘i he taimi pē ‘e taha, kuo pau ke ke kapakau‘i kinautolu, koe‘uhi ke lava ‘o tāmate‘i kinautolu hili iá ‘i ho‘o taimi mālōloó. Ko ia ai, 'i he ngaahi taimi hange ko 'eni 'oku hoko mai 'a e drugg, ki he requisition. Naʻe fakanaunauʻi homau vaká ʻaki ha toko tolu ʻo kinautolu. Naʻe lavameʻa hono leleʻi ʻo e ʻuluaki mo e uá, pea naʻá ma sio ki he lele fakaofo ʻa e fanga tafola, ʻo haʻi ʻe he fuʻu fakafepaki lahi ʻo e tafaʻaki ʻo e towing drugg. Naʻa nau fītaʻa ʻo hangē ha kau faikoví ʻaki ʻa e seini mo e foʻi pulú. Ka ʻi hono lī ʻo e tolú, ʻi heʻene lī ki ʻolunga ʻa e poloka ʻakau kovi ʻo e vaká, naʻe maʻu ia ʻi lalo ʻi he taha ʻo e ngaahi sea ʻo e vaká, pea ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou pē naʻá ne haeʻi ia ʻo ʻave ia, ʻo ne tō hifo ʻa e tokotaha folau ʻeveʻevá ʻi he lalo vaká ʻi he tō hifo ʻa e seá mei hono lalo vaká. ʻI he ongo tafaʻakí fakatouʻosi naʻe hū mai ʻa e tahí ʻi he ngaahi laupapa laveá, ka naʻá ma fakafonu ha ongo tuʻasila mo ha ngaahi kofukofu ʻe ua pe tolu, pea ko ia naʻe taʻofi ai ʻa e ngaahi hūʻangá ki he taimí.

Naʻe meimei taʻemalava ke fanaʻi ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻakau faitoʻo konatapu ko ʻení, ʻikai ko ia ʻi heʻemau laka atu ki he tākangá, naʻe fakasiʻisiʻi lahi ʻa e hala ʻo ʻemau tafola; ʻikai ngata aí, ʻi heʻemau kei ʻalu atu pē mei he takatakai ʻo e moveuveú, naʻe hangē ʻoku holo hifo ʻa e ngaahi fakamoveuveu fakalilifú. Ko ia ʻi he taimi naʻe faifai pea toho mai ai ʻa e foʻi ʻakau luelue, pea puli atu ʻa e tafola toho ʻi he tafaʻakí; pea, ʻi he mālohi fakasiʻisiʻi ʻo ʻene mālohi ʻo e mavahé, naʻá ma luelue ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo ha ongo tafola ki he loto ʻo e ʻaá, ʻo hangē naʻá ma luelue atu mei ha ngaahi vaitafe moʻunga ʻe niʻihi ki ha anovai ʻo e teleʻá ʻoku nonga. Naʻe ongo heni ʻa e ngaahi matangi ʻi he ngaahi ʻotu moʻunga ʻoku ngungulu ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ngaahi tafola ʻi tuʻá, ka naʻe ʻikai ke ongoʻi. ‘I he ‘ata‘atā lotoloto ko ení na‘e fakahaa‘i ai ‘e he tahí ‘a e funga ngingila ko ia ‘oku hangē ha satiní, ‘a ia ‘oku ui ko ha sleek, ‘a ia na‘e fakatupu ‘e he ‘uha fakapulipuli ‘oku lī atu ‘e he tafola ‘i hono ngaahi ongo‘i fakalongolongo angé. ʻIo, naʻa mau ʻi he nonga fakaʻofoʻofa ko ia he taimí ni ʻa ia ʻoku nau pehē ʻoku toitoi ʻi he uho ʻo e longoaʻa kotoa pē. Pea kei ʻi he mamaʻo fakahohaʻá naʻá ma sio ki he ngaahi longoaʻa ʻo e ngaahi siakale fakatefito ʻi tuʻá, peá ma sio ki ha ngaahi foʻi tafola hokohoko, ʻo taki valu pe hongofulu, ʻoku nau ʻalu takai holo vave, ʻo hangē ha ngaahi foʻi hoosi kuo fakalahi ʻi ha mama; pea vāofi ʻaupito ʻa e uma ki he uma, ʻo faingofua pē nai ke ʻover-arch ʻe ha taha heka sākasi ʻi he vakapuna Titanic ʻa e kau ʻi lotomālié, pea pehē ʻenau ʻalu takai holo ʻi honau tuʻá. Koeʻuhí ko e lahi ʻo e fuʻu kakai ʻo e fanga tafola ʻoku nau mālōloó, ʻo nau ʻātakaiʻi vave ange ʻa e ʻasi ʻo e tākangá, naʻe ʻikai ha faingamālie ʻe lava ke mau hola ai ʻi he lolotongá ni. Kuo pau ke tau leʻo ki ha ava ʻi he ʻā moʻui naʻá ne ʻātakaiʻi kitautolú; ko e ʻā naʻá ne fakahū pē kimauá koeʻuhí ke tāpuniʻi kimaua. ‘I he‘emau nofo ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e lepá, na‘e ‘a‘ahi mai ai kimaua ‘i he taimi ki he taimi ‘e ha fanga ki‘i pulu mo e fanga ki‘i pulu faka‘auha; ko e kau fefine mo e fānau ʻo e kau talitali kuo fakaʻauha ko ʻení.

Ko ʻeni, ʻo kau ai ʻa e ngaahi vahaʻataimi lahi ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ngaahi siakale ʻi tuʻa ʻoku vilo holo, pea fakakau ai ʻa e ngaahi feituʻu ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ngaahi foʻi ʻakau kehekehe ʻi ha taha pē ʻo e ngaahi siakale ko iá, ko e feituʻu kotoa ʻi he taimi ko ʻení, ʻoku ʻuma ki ai ʻa e fuʻu kakaí kotoa, kuo pau ke ʻi ai ha maile tapafā ʻe ua pe tolu. ʻI ha faʻahinga tuʻunga pē —neongo ʻe kākaaʻi moʻoni ha ʻahiʻahi pehē ʻi ha taimi pehē —ʻe lava ke ʻiloʻi ha ngaahi foʻi ʻuʻulu mei homau vaka māʻulaló ʻa ia naʻe ngali vaʻinga hake ʻo meimei mei he tafaʻaki ʻo e ʻataá. ʻOku ou lave ki he tūkunga ko ʻení, koeʻuhí, ʻo hangē naʻe fakataumuʻa ke lokaʻi ʻa e fanga pulú mo e fanga pulú ʻi he lotoʻā ʻi loto taha ko ʻení; pea hangē naʻe taʻofi kinautolu ʻe he lahi ʻo e tākangá ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimí ni mei hono ʻiloʻi ʻa e tupuʻanga pau ʻo ʻene tuʻú; pe, mahalo, ko e fuʻu kei talavou, taʻe-fakapotopoto, pea ʻi he founga kotoa pē ʻoku taʻehalaia mo taʻe-aʻusia; ka neongo ia, mahalo naʻe pehē, ko e fanga kiʻi tafola iiki ange ko ʻení —ʻi he taimi ní mo e ʻaʻahi mai ki homau vaka kuo fakanonga mei he tafaʻaki ʻo e lepá —naʻa nau fakahaaʻi ha taʻemanavahē mo ha falala fakaofo, pe ko ha manavasiʻi kei fakaʻofoʻofa ʻa ia naʻe ʻikai ke lava ke ʻoua naʻa ofo ai. Hangē ha fanga kuli ʻi ʻapí naʻa nau haʻu ʻo ngungu takatakai ʻiate kimautolu, ʻo aʻu tonu ki homau ngaahi gunwales, ʻo ala kiate kinautolu; kae ʻoua kuo meimei hangē kuo fakafokifā pē hono fakaʻaongaʻi kinautolu ʻe ha sipele ʻe niʻihi. Naʻe tataʻo ʻe Kuiki hona laʻé; Naʻe toʻotoʻo ʻe Starbuck hona tuʻá ʻaki ʻene tao; ka naʻá ne manavasiʻi ki he ngaahi nunuʻá, he naʻe fakaʻehiʻehi e taimí mei hono darting ia.

Ka ʻi lalo mamaʻo ʻi he māmani fakaofo ko ʻeni ʻi he fukahi tahí, naʻe fetaulaki mai ha māmani ʻe taha mo kei kehe ange mo homa matá ʻi heʻema sio fakamamaʻu atu ki he tafaʻakí. He, ‘i hono tautau ‘i he ngaahi fale vai ko iá, na‘e ‘alu holo ai ‘a e ngaahi fōtunga ‘o e ngaahi fa‘ē tauhi ‘o e fanga tafola, pea mo kinautolu ‘i honau fu‘u ‘ātakaí na‘e hā ngali vave ‘enau hoko ko e ngaahi fa‘eé. Ko e vaitoó, hangē ko ia kuó u fakaʻilongaʻí, naʻe ʻi ha loloto lahi ʻo fuʻu ʻataʻatā; pea hangē ko e fanga ki‘i pēpē fakaetangatá lolotonga ‘enau huhu‘á te nau sio nonga mo tu‘u ma‘u mei he fatafatá, ‘o hangē ‘oku nau mo‘ui ‘aki ha mo‘ui kehekehe ‘e ua ‘i he taimi ko iá; pea lolotonga hoʻomou kei maʻu ʻa e meʻakai fakamatelié, ke mou kei kai fakalaumālie pē ʻi ha ngaahi manatu taʻefakamāmani;—naʻa mo e hangē naʻe sio hake ʻa e fanga kiʻi tafola ko ʻení kiate kimautolu, ka ʻoku ʻikai kiate kimautolu, ʻo hangē ko ha kiʻi konga siʻi pē ʻo e Gulfweed ʻi heʻenau sio toki fāʻeleʻí. ʻI heʻenau lele holo ʻi honau ongo tafaʻakí, naʻe hangē foki ʻoku sio fakalongolongo mai ʻa e ngaahi faʻeé kiate kimauá. Ko e taha ʻo e fanga kiʻi pēpē ko ʻení, ʻa ia mei he ngaahi fakaʻilonga fakaofo pau naʻe ngali ʻaho ʻe taha hono motuʻá, mahalo naʻe fute ʻe hongofulu mā fā nai hono lōloá, pea fute ʻe ono nai hono ʻātakaí. Naʻá ne kiʻi frisky; neongo naʻe kei ngali siʻisiʻi hono sinó ka naʻe toe fakaakeake mei he tuʻunga fakatupu ʻita ko ia naʻá ne nofoʻi kimuí ni mai ʻi he reticule fakafaʻeé; ‘a ia, ‘i he hiku ki he ‘ulu, pea mateuteu kotoa ki he fa‘ahita‘u failau faka‘osí, ‘oku tokoto punou ai ‘a e tafola te‘eki fanau‘í ‘o hangē ha kaufana ‘a e Tātaá. Ko e ngaahi side-fins maʻamaʻa, mo e ongo nima ʻo hono ngaahi flukes, naʻe kei kei maʻu pē ʻa e fōtunga plaited crumpled ʻo e telinga ʻo ha pēpē naʻe toki aʻu mai mei he ngaahi konga muli.

“Laine! laine!” ko e kaila ia ʻa Kuiki, ʻo ne sio atu ki ʻolunga ʻi he gunwale; “him fast! him fast!—Ko hai ‘oku ne laine‘i ia! Ko hai na‘á ne taa‘i?—Ko e tafola ‘e ua; ko e taha lahi, ko e taha si‘isi‘i!”

“Ko e hā hoʻo mahakí, tangata?” ko e kaila ia ʻa Sitāpakí.

“Sio-e heni,” ko e lea ia ʻa Kuiki, ʻo ne tuhu hifo.

Hangē ko e taimi naʻe taaʻi ai ʻa e tafola, ʻa ia mei he tēpilé kuo ne tafe mai ha ngaahi fute ʻe laungeau ʻo e maea; hange ko ia, hili 'ene le'o loloto, 'oku ne toe lele hake, pea fakahaa'i 'a e laine curling slackened buoyantly 'alu hake mo spiraling ki he 'ea; ko ia ko ʻeni, naʻe sio ʻa Starbuck ki ha ngaahi foʻi koini lōloa ʻo e afo ʻo e ʻuhiki ʻo Madame Leviathan, ʻa ia naʻe ngali kei haʻi ai ʻa e kiʻi pulú ki hono ʻaá. ‘Oku ‘ikai ke tātātaha ‘i he ngaahi liliu vave ‘o e tulimanú, ‘oku hoko ‘a e laine fakanatula ko ení, ‘a ia ‘oku vetevete ‘a e ngata‘anga fakafa‘eé, ‘o lavelavea mo e laine hempen, ‘o ma‘u ai ‘a e ki‘i pulú. Naʻe hangē naʻe fakahā mai kiate kimautolu ʻi he vaitupu fakaʻofoʻofa ko ʻení ha niʻihi ʻo e ngaahi fakapulipuli fakapulipuli taha ʻo e ngaahi tahí. Na‘á ma sio ki he fanga ki‘i ‘ofa‘anga Līvaiataní ‘i he lolotó.*

*Ko e sperm whale, hangē ko e ngaahi fa‘ahinga kehe kotoa pē ‘o e Levaiataní, ka ‘oku ‘ikai hangē ko e lahi taha ‘o e fanga ika kehé, ‘okú ne fanafanau ta‘etokanga ‘i he ngaahi fa‘ahita‘u kotoa pē; hili ha feitama ‘a ia ‘e ngalingali ‘e fokotu‘u hifo ia ‘i he māhina ‘e hiva, ‘o fakatupu ka ko e taha pē ‘i he taimi; neongo ‘i ha ngaahi tu‘unga si‘isi‘i ‘e ni‘ihi ‘oku ‘ilo‘i ‘okú ne fā‘ele‘i ha ‘Īsoa mo Sēkope:—ko ha me‘a fakatu‘upakē ‘oku tokonaki ‘i he huhu ‘e ha ongo fo‘i hu‘akau, ‘oku tu‘u faka‘ohovale, ko e taha ‘i he ongo tafa‘aki ‘o e ‘anusí; ka ko e ngaahi fatafata tonu ʻoku nau ʻalu hake mei ai. ‘I he taimi ‘oku motuhia tu‘uma‘u ai ‘a e ngaahi konga mahu‘inga ko eni ‘i ha tafola ‘oku fakahuhú ‘e he tao ‘a e tulimanú, ‘oku fakafe‘au‘auhi ai ‘e he hu‘akau mo e toto ‘oku lilingi ‘e he fa‘eé ‘a e lanu ‘o e tahí ki ha ngaahi va‘akau. Ko e huʻakaú ʻoku fuʻu melie mo koloaʻia; kuo ʻahiʻahiʻi ia ʻe he tangatá; mahalo ʻe lelei ia mo e sitolōpeli. ‘I he taimi ‘oku nau fonu ai ‘i he fefaka‘apa‘apa‘akí, ‘oku salute lahi ange ‘a e fanga tafola ki he hominum .

He lolotonga ‘oku ‘ulungaanga faka‘ulia ‘a e fanga tafola fefine ko iá, ko e fanga pulu tangata kei talavoú, pe ko e fanga pulu-pa‘ale ‘e fāngofulu, ‘o hangē ko ‘enau uí, ‘oku nau mama‘o ‘aupito ‘i he fanga Livaiatani kotoa pē, pea ‘i he lea fakatātaá ‘oku nau fakatu‘utāmaki taha ke fetaulaki mo kinautolu; tukukehe ʻa e fanga tafola ʻulu hina, grizzled fakaofo ko ia, ʻoku nau fetaulaki ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi, pea ʻe tauʻi koe ʻe he ngaahi meʻá ni ʻo hangē ha kau fiends fakalilifu ʻoku fakaʻitaʻi ʻe ha kauti tauteá.

Ko e ngaahi ako'anga pulu-pa'ale 'e Fangofulu 'oku lahi ange ia 'i he ngaahi ako'anga harem. Hangē ha fuʻu kakai ʻo e kau ako kolisi kei talavoú, ʻoku nau fonu ʻi he fuhu, fakafiefia, mo e angakovi, ʻo nau tō holo ʻi he māmaní ʻi ha tuʻunga taʻefakakaukauʻi mo fakaʻauʻauhifo, ʻo ʻikai ha taha maluʻi fakapotopoto te ne maluʻi kinautolu ʻo lahi ange ʻi heʻene maluʻi ha tamasiʻi moveuveu ʻi Yale pe Harvard. ‘Oku vave ‘enau tukuange ‘a e moveuveu ko ‘ení neongo ia, pea ‘i he taimi ‘oku tupu ai ‘a e vahe fā nai ‘e tolu, ‘oku nau movete, pea ‘alu kehekehe ‘o kumi ki he ngaahi nofo‘angá, ‘a ia, ko e ngaahi harems.

Ko e poini 'e taha 'o e faikehekehe 'i he vaha'a 'o e ako'anga tangata mo e fefine 'oku kei 'ulungaanga lahi ange 'a e ngaahi tangata mo e fefine. Talamai ʻokú ke taaʻi ha pulu paʻale ʻe Fāngofulu —tēvolo masiva! naʻe tukuange ia ʻe hono ngaahi kaungāmeʻá kotoa. Kae taaʻi ha mēmipa ʻo e akoʻanga harem, pea luelue takai holo hono ngaahi kaungāmeʻá ʻiate ia mo e fakaʻilonga kotoa pē ʻo e hohaʻá, ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻoku nau nofo ofi ʻaupito kiate ia mo fuoloa ʻaupito, ʻo hangē pē ko kinautolú ke nau tō ko ha manupuna.
