VAHE 135. Koe Tuli.—Aho Tolu.

Naʻe mafoa ʻa e pongipongi ʻo e ʻaho hono tolú ʻo lelei mo foʻou, pea naʻe toe fakanonga ʻa e tangata pō tokotaha ʻi he ʻuluʻi pou ʻi muʻá ʻe he fuʻu kakai tokolahi ʻo e kau sio ʻi he maama ʻo e ʻahó, ʻa ia naʻa nau toti ʻi he pou kotoa pē mo e meimei spar kotoa pē.

“ʻOkú ke sio kiate ia?” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻĒhapí; ka naʻe teʻeki ke ʻasi mai ʻa e tafola.

“ʻI heʻene ʻā taʻe-halaʻiá, neongo ia; ka ke muimui ʻi he ʻā ko iá, ko e meʻa pē ia. Helm ʻi ai; tuʻu maʻu, ʻi hoʻo ʻalú, pea kuo ʻosi ʻalu. Ko ha ʻaho fakaʻofoʻofa ē ʻoku toe! naʻe hoko ia ko ha māmani foʻou-ngaohi, pea naʻe ngaohi ko ha fale-faʻahitaʻu māfana ki he kau ʻāngeló, pea ʻi he pongipongí ni ko e ʻuluaki ʻo hono lī ʻo e mafoa ʻa e atá kiate kinautolu, naʻe ʻikai ke Ne lava ʻo maʻu ha ʻaho lelei ange kiate kinautolu. ‘Oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ke fakakaukau ‘a ‘Ēhapi; ‘okú ne ongo‘i pē, ‘okú ne ongo‘i ‘ a e tingling fe‘unga ki he tangata fakamatelié Ko e ‘Otuá pē ‘okú ne ma‘u ‘a e totonu mo e monū‘ia ko iá, pe ‘oku totonu ke hoko ia ko ha nonga mo ha nonga ‘o ‘etau taimi lahi pē. na'e nonga 'aupito 'a e 'uto—nonga 'aisi, 'oku mafesifesi 'a e 'ulu motu'a ko 'eni 'a ia 'oku liliu ai 'a e me'a 'i loto ki he 'aisi, pea tetetete ia. Pea 'oku kei tupulaki 'a e fulufulu'i 'ulu ko 'eni 'i he taimi ni 'oku tupulaki 'a e vela, ka 'oku 'ikai, 'oku hange ko ia 'a e mohuku angamaheni 'e tupu 'i ha feitu'u pe 'a e lavace ift . 'oku puhi'i ia 'e he ngaahi matangi vao; 'oku nau uipi ia 'o takai 'iate au 'i he ngaahi fo'i vaka 'oku haehae 'oku nau piki ki ai. 'Oku 'ikai te u toe puhi 'i ha mamani kovi pehe, pea slink 'i ai. ʻikai ke tuʻu ke maʻu ha tā ʻe taha.Naʻa mo ʻĒhapi ko ha meʻa fakaʻeiʻeiki ange ia ʻi he meʻa ko iá .Naʻe mei maʻu ʻe he matangí ha sino; pea fuakava ia he taimi ni, 'oku 'i ai ha me'a kotoa 'oku langilangi'ia mo kelesi 'i he matangi Ko e ngaahi Matangi Fefakatau'aki mafana ko 'eni, 'i he si'isi'i taha, 'oku 'i he langi mahino 'oku puhi hangatonu atu, 'i he malohi mo e tu'u ma'u, 'a e angavaivai malohi pea veer 'ikai mei honau faka'ilonga, neongo 'a e baser 'o e ngaahi 'au 'o e tahi might turn mo e tack, taʻepau ʻa e feituʻu ke ʻalu ki ai ʻi he fakaʻosí.Pea ʻe he ngaahi Fefakatauʻaki taʻengata ko ʻení ʻoku nau puhiʻi fakahangatonu ʻeku vaka leleí, pe ko ha meʻa ʻoku fuʻu taʻe-liliu ʻo hangē ko iá, ʻoku puhiʻi atu hoku laumālie ʻoku keiled ʻi aí!

“ʻIkai ha meʻa, tangataʻeiki.”

"ʻIkai ha meʻa! pea ʻoku ofi mai ʻa e hoʻataá! ʻOku ʻalu ʻa e doubloon ʻo kolekole! Sio ki he laʻaá! ʻIo, ʻio, kuo pau ke pehē. Kuó u ʻova ʻiate ia. ʻOku fēfē, maʻu ʻa e kamataʻangá? ʻIo, ʻokú ne tuli au he taimí ni; ʻikai ko au, ia —ʻoku kovi ia; mahalo naʻá ku ʻiloʻi ia, foki. Vale! ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e ngaahi laine —ʻa e ngaahi laʻā ʻoku lele ʻiate ia. Fekauʻaki mo e!

ʻI heʻene fakaʻuli ʻo hangē ko ʻene faí, naʻe kiʻi ʻi he kuata ʻo e Pequod ʻa e matangí, ko ia ʻi heʻene fakahangatonu he taimí ni ki he tafaʻaki fakafepakí, naʻe folau mālohi ʻa e vaka kuo fakamālohí ʻi he matangí ʻi heʻene toe fakafonu ʻa e kilimí ʻi hono ʻā hinehina pē ʻoʻoná.

“Fakafepakiʻi ʻa e matangí ʻokú ne fakaʻuli he taimí ni ki he kauʻā ʻoku ʻatā,” ko e lāunga ia ʻa Sitāpaki kiate iá, ʻi heʻene fakatakatakaʻi ʻa e meʻa fakaʻaiʻai tefito naʻe toho foʻoú ʻi he lēlué. “Tauhi kimaua ‘e he ‘Otua, ka ‘oku ‘osi ongo‘i ‘oku sūsū hoku ngaahi hui ‘i loto ‘iate au, pea ‘oku sūsū mei loto hoku kakanó. ‘Oku ou veiveiua hala ‘oku ou talangata‘a ki hoku ‘Otuá ‘i he‘eku talangofua kiate iá!”

“Tuʻu mai ke lulululu au ki ʻolunga!” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻĒhapí, ʻo ne ʻunu atu ki he kato hemení. “ʻOku totonu ke ta fetaulaki vave mo ia.”

“ʻIo, ʻio, tangataʻeiki,” pea naʻe fai leva ʻe Starbuck ʻa e fekau ʻa ʻĒhapí, pea naʻe toe taʻalo ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi ʻolunga.

Naʻe ʻosi atu ha houa kakato he taimí ni; koula-ta'i 'o 'alu ki he ngaahi kuonga. Naʻe puke ʻe he taimí tonu he taimí ni ha ngaahi mānava lōloa mo ha fakakaukau lahi. Ka naʻe faifai pea ʻi ha poini ʻe tolu nai mei he kaufana ʻo e ʻeá, naʻe toe fakamatalaʻi ʻe ʻĒhapi ʻa e foʻi ʻuʻuú, pea ʻi he taimi pē ko iá naʻe ʻalu hake ha kaila ʻe tolu mei he ʻuluʻi pou ʻe tolú ʻo hangē naʻe fakaleʻolahiʻi ia ʻe he ngaahi ʻelelo ʻo e afí.

“Laʻe ki he laʻe ʻoku ou fetaulaki mo koe, ko e taimi hono tolu ko ʻení, Moby Dick! ʻI he funga vaká ʻi aí!—fakaʻaiʻai māsila ange hake; fakaʻauha ia ki he mata ʻo e matangí. ʻOkú ne fuʻu mamaʻo ke tuku hifo ʻoku kei ʻi ai, Misa Sitāpaki. ʻOku lulululu ʻa e ngaahi folau! Tuʻu ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he tangata folau ko iá mo ha top-maul ʻe taha! Ko ia, kuo pau ke u fononga takai holo. 'i 'olunga heni 'i he tahi; 'oku 'i ai 'a e taimi ki he me'a ko ia. Ko ha motu'a, motu'a 'a e sio, pea 'oku 'ikai ke liliu ha kuikui talu mei he'eku fuofua sio ki ai, ko ha tamasi'i, mei he ngaahi mo'unga 'one'one 'o Nantucket Ko e me'a tatau!—ko e me'a tatau pe kia Noah 'a e left a! ‘i ha feitu‘u—ki ha me‘a kehe ‘i he fonua anga-mahení, ‘oku lahi ange ‘a e ngaahi me‘a ‘i he ngaahi ‘aá. Leeward! ʻoku ʻalu pehē ʻa e tafola hinehina; vakai ki he matangí; ko e lelei ange ia kapau ko e kuata konaá. Ka ko e bye lelei, bye lelei, motu'a mast-head! Ko e hā ʻeni? —lanumata? aye, fanga ki‘i mosses ‘i he ngaahi mafesifesi warped ko ení. ‘Oku ‘ikai ha ngaahi ‘uli ‘ea lanumata pehē ‘i he ‘ulu ‘o ‘Ēhapí! 'Oku 'i ai 'a e faikehekehe he taimi ni 'i he vaha'a 'o e motu'a 'o e tangata mo e me'a. mo'ui 'a e kau tangata na'e ngaohi 'aki 'a e ngaahi me'a mahu'inga taha 'o e ngaahi tamai mahu'inga na'a ne pehe 'oku totonu ke ne kei 'alu 'i mu'a 'iate au, 'Oku kei 'i ai pe 'a e mata 'i he lalo tahi, 'o pehe 'oku ou hifo 'a e ngaahi sitepu ta'e ngata ko ia ke ayek lahi ange? moʻoni ʻo hangē ko e lave kiate koé, ʻEhapi, ʻi ai naʻe tō nounou hoʻo meʻafaná, ʻulu-pou —tauhi ha mata lelei ki he tafola, ʻi he lolotonga ʻoku ou ʻalu aí Te ta talanoa ʻapongipongi, ʻikai, ʻi he pō ní, ʻi he taimi ʻe tokoto ai ʻa e ʻulu tafola hinehina ʻi aí, ʻo haʻi .”

Naʻá Ne ʻomi ʻa e folofolá; pea kei sio takai holo pē ʻiate ia, naʻe tuku hifo maʻu pē ia ʻi he ʻea lanu pulū cloven ki he funga vaká.

ʻI hono taimi totonu naʻe tuku hifo ʻa e ngaahi vaká; ka ʻi heʻene tuʻu ʻi he mui ʻo ʻene vakapuná, naʻe luelue pē ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi he feituʻu ʻo e hifoʻangá, naʻá ne lulululu ki he hoá, —ʻa ia naʻá ne puke ʻa e taha ʻo e ngaahi maea ʻo e vaká ʻi he funga vaká —peá ne fekauʻi ia ke ne kiʻi tuʻu.

“Sitāpaki!”

"Tangata'eiki?"

“Ko e tuʻo tolu ʻeni ʻa e kamata ʻa e vaka ʻo hoku laumālié ʻi he folau ko ʻení, ko e Starbuck.”

“ʻIo, tangataʻeiki, te ke maʻu ia ke pehē.”

“ʻOku folau ha ngaahi vaka ʻe niʻihi mei honau ngaahi taulangá, pea ʻi he hili iá ʻoku nau mole, Starbuck!”

“Moʻoni, tangataʻeiki: moʻoni fakaloloma tahá.”

“ʻOku mate ha kau tangata ʻe niʻihi ʻi he taimi ʻoku tō hifo ai ʻa e vaí; ha niʻihi ʻi he taimi ʻoku maʻulalo ai ʻa e vaí; ha niʻihi ʻi he fonu ʻa e tāfeá;—pea ʻoku ou ongoʻi he taimí ni ʻo hangē ha peau ʻoku ʻi ai kotoa ha selu ʻoku ʻi ai hono tumuʻaki ʻe taha, Starbuck. ʻOku ou motuʻa;—lulululu mo au, tangata.”

Naʻe fetaulaki hona ongo nimá; naʻe sio fakamamaʻu honau matá; ʻOku haeʻi ʻe he Starbuck ʻa e kuluú.

“ʻOiauē, ʻeku ʻeikivalé, ʻeku ʻeikivalé!—loto fakaʻeiʻeiki—ʻoua naʻá ke ʻalu—ʻoua naʻá ke ʻalu!—vakai, ko ha tangata loto-toʻa ia ʻoku tangi; hono lahi ʻo e mamahi ʻo e fakalotoʻí ʻi he taimi ko iá!”

“Hifo hifo!”—ko e kaila ia ‘a ‘Ēhapí, ‘o ne lī ‘a e nima ‘o e hoá meiate ia. “Tuʻu ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e kau ngāué!”

ʻI ha kiʻi taimi nounou pē naʻe toho takai holo ʻa e vaká ʻo ofi ʻi lalo ʻi he muiʻi vaká.

“Ko e fanga ʻangá! ʻa e fanga ʻangá!” ko e kaila ia ʻa ha leʻo mei he matapā-sioʻata māʻulalo ʻo e kāpiní ʻi aí; “ʻE ʻeiki, ko hoku ʻeiki, foki mai!”

Ka naʻe ʻikai fanongo ʻa ʻEhapi ki ha meʻa; he naʻe māʻolunga hono leʻo ʻoʻoná ʻi he taimi ko iá; pea puna atu ʻa e vaká.

Ka naʻe lea moʻoni ʻae leʻo; he naʻe teʻeki ke ne teke mei he vaká, ʻi he taimi naʻe tō ai ha fanga ʻanga lahi, ʻoku ngali ʻalu hake mei tuʻa ʻi he ngaahi vai fakapoʻuli ʻi lalo ʻi he ʻaá, ʻo nau pāʻusiʻi fakakovi ʻa e ngaahi mata ʻo e ngaahi foʻi vaká, ʻi he taimi kotoa pē naʻa nau hū ai ki he vaí; pea ʻi he founga ko ʻení naʻa nau ʻalu fakataha mo e vaká mo ʻenau ngaahi uʻú. Ko ha meʻa ia ʻoku ʻikai ko ha meʻa foʻou ʻoku hoko ki he ngaahi vaka-tafola ʻi he ngaahi tahi ʻoku ʻuʻulu ko iá; ‘oku hā ngali ‘oku muimui ‘a e fanga ‘angá ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi ‘iate kinautolu ‘i he founga faka‘ilonga tatau ‘oku puna holo ai ‘a e fanga vultures ‘i he ngaahi fuka ‘o e ngaahi lēsitimeni ‘oku nau laka ‘i he hahaké. Ka ko e ngaahi fuofua ʻangaʻanga ʻeni naʻe siofi ʻe he Pequod talu mei hono fuofua fakamatalaʻi ʻo e Tafola Hinehina; pea pe ko e kau ngāue ʻa ʻĒhapí ko e kau pāpelia kulokula taika kotoa pē kinautolu, pea ko ia ai naʻe musky ange honau kakanó ki he ngaahi ongo ʻo e fanga ʻangá —ko ha meʻa ʻoku ʻiloa ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻokú ne uesia kinautolu, —neongo pe ko e hā, naʻe hangē naʻa nau muimui ʻi he vaka ʻe taha ko iá ʻo ʻikai ke nau fakaʻauha ʻa e niʻihi kehé.

“Loto ʻo e ukamea ngaohi!” ko e lāunga ia ʻa Starbuck ʻo sio fakamamaʻu ki he tafaʻakí, pea muimui ʻaki hono matá ʻi he vaka ʻoku holomuí —“te ke kei lava ʻo ta loto-toʻa ki he sio ko iá?—ʻo tuku hifo hoʻo kiʻi ʻangaʻangá ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e fanga ʻangaʻangá, pea muimui atu ʻiate kinautolu, ʻo fakaava-ngutu ki he tulituli; pea ko e ʻaho ʻeni ʻe tolu ʻoku fakatuʻutāmaki ʻa e ʻaho ʻe tolu ʻoku hokohoko atu ʻi he tulimanú?—He ʻi he taimi ʻoku hokohoko atu ai ʻa e ʻaho ʻe tolu ʻi he tulimanú; ko e ua 'a e ho'ata, pea ko e tolu 'a e efiafi mo e ngata'anga 'o e me'a ko ia 'e lava ke hoko. ta'ahine 'oku ke 'osi 'i he ngaahi langilangi 'i hoku tu'a; Starbuck!—fakaʻilongaʻi ia — ngaʻunu, lea leʻolahi! — ʻOku mou sio ki he nima ʻo ʻeku tamasiʻí ʻi he moʻungá? lele ʻi he loli-lahí—“Ha! ʻokú ne lele hake mo ia!—ʻOku ʻi fē ʻa e motuʻá he taimí ni? ʻokú ke sio ki he sio ko iá, ʻe ʻĒhapi!—tetetete, tetetete!”

Naʻe teʻeki ke fuʻu mamaʻo ʻa e ngaahi vaká, ʻi ha fakaʻilonga mei he ngaahi ʻuluʻi pou ʻo e vaká —ko ha nima ʻoku tuhu hifo, naʻe ʻiloʻi ai ʻe ʻĒhapi kuo leʻo ʻa e tafola; ka ʻi heʻene fakakaukau ke ofi kiate ia ʻi he ʻalu hake hono hokó, naʻá ne pukepuke ʻene halá ʻo kiʻi tafaʻaki mei he vaká; ko e kau ngāue fakaʻofoʻofa ʻoku nau tauhi ʻa e fakalongolongo loloto tahá, ʻi he hāmala mo hāmala ʻa e ngaahi peau ʻoku tā ʻuluʻi ʻulú ki he kaufana fakafepakí.

“Tuli, tuli ʻi hoʻomou faʻó, ʻe ngaahi peau! ki honau ʻulu ngataʻangá! ʻoku mou taaʻi pē ha meʻa ʻoku ʻikai hano ʻufiʻufi; pea ʻoku ʻikai ha puha mate mo ha saliote mate ʻe lava ke ʻaʻaku:—pea ko e hemp pē ʻe lava ke ne tāmateʻi au! Ha! ha!"

Fakafokifā pē kuo puna māmālie ʻa e ngaahi vai takatakai ʻiate kinautolú ʻi ha ngaahi siakale fōlahi; pea vave leva hono lulululu, ‘o hangē ‘oku tō tafa‘aki mei ha fu‘u ‘aisi ‘oku ngoto, ‘o ‘alu hake vave ki ‘olunga. Naʻe ongo mai ha leʻo ngungu māʻulalo; ko ha humu ʻi lalofonua; pea naʻe toki taʻofi kotoa ʻenau mānavá; hange ko ia kuo tohoaki'i 'aki 'a e ngaahi maea muimui, mo e ngaahi heleta, mo e ngaahi tao, ko ha fu'u fotunga lahi 'oku fana'i fakaloloa, ka 'oku fakahangatonu mei he tahi. ʻI hono ʻufiʻufi ʻe ha veili manifinifi ʻo e kakapu ʻoku tō hifo, naʻá ne puna ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti ʻi he ʻea ʻuhaʻulá; pea toki tō ʻo tō ʻo foki ki he lolotó. ʻI hono holoki hake ʻi ha fute ʻe tolungofulu ki ʻolunga, naʻe ulo ʻa e ngaahi vaí ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou ʻo hangē ha ngaahi fuʻu matavai, pea ngoto motuʻa ʻi ha ʻuha ʻo e ngaahi foʻi ʻuʻulu, ʻo tuku ʻa e funga takai ʻoku kilimi ʻo hangē ha huʻakau foʻoú ʻo takatakai ʻi he fuʻu ʻakau māpele ʻo e tafola.

“Tukuange!” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻĒhapi ki he kau folau, pea naʻe lele atu ʻa e ngaahi vaká ki muʻa ke ʻohofi; ka ʻi heʻene vale ʻi he ngaahi ʻaione foʻou ʻaneafi naʻe ʻauha ʻiate iá, naʻe hangē naʻe maʻu fakataha ʻe Mopi Tiki ʻa e kau ʻāngelo kotoa pē naʻe tō mei he langí. Ko e ngaahi laʻi ʻakau lahi ʻo e ngaahi uoua kuo uelo ʻoku nau mafola ʻi hono laʻe hinehina fālahí, ʻi lalo ʻi he kili ʻataʻataá, naʻe hā ngali lalanga fakataha; ʻi heʻene ʻalu haké, naʻá ne haʻu ʻo fakafefekaʻi hono hiku ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi vaká; pea naʻá ne toe tāmateʻi kinaua; ‘o ne lilingi atu ‘a e ngaahi ‘aione mo e ngaahi tao mei he ongo vaka ‘o e ongo hoá, pea nau lele ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘e taha ‘o e konga ‘i ‘olunga ‘o ‘ena kaufaná, ka na‘e meimei ‘ikai ha ma‘u ‘a e kaufana ‘a ‘Ēhapí.

Lolotonga hono taʻofi ʻe Daggoo mo Queequeg ʻa e ngaahi laupapa kuo mafasiá; pea ʻi he luelue atu ʻa e tafola meiate kinautolú, naʻá ne tafoki, ʻo fakahaaʻi ha tafaʻaki kakato ʻe taha ʻi heʻene toe fana ʻiate kinautolú; ʻi he momeniti ko iá naʻe ʻalu hake ha tangi vave. Lashed takai mo takai ki he tu'a 'o e ika; pinioned 'i he ngaahi tafoki 'i he ngaahi tafoki 'a ia, lolotonga 'a e po kuo 'osi, na'e reeled 'e he tafola 'a e ngaahi involutions 'o e ngaahi laine 'oku takatakai 'iate ia, na'e sio ki he sino haea vaeua 'o e Parsee; ko hono kofu sable kuo mafesifesi; naʻe tafoki fonu hono mata ʻoku fakaʻauʻauhifó ki he motuʻa ko ʻĒhapí.

Naʻe tō hifo ʻa e foʻi ʻaá mei hono nimá.

“Vale, vale!”—ʻo ne toʻo ʻi ha mānava lōloa ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu lahi—“ʻIo, Pālesi! ʻOku ou toe sio kiate koe.—ʻIo, pea ʻoku ke ʻalu muʻomuʻa; pea ko ʻeni, ko ʻeni leva ʻa e meʻalele mate naʻá ke talaʻofá. Ka ʻoku ou pukepuke koe ki he mataʻitohi fakaʻosi ʻo hoʻo leá. ʻOku ʻi fē ʻa e vaka mate hono uá, ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻalele ko iá? fakaleleiʻi kinautolu kapau ʻoku mou lava ʻi hono taimí, pea foki mai kiate au ʻo kapau ʻoku ʻikai, ʻoku feʻunga ʻa ʻĒhapi ke mate —Hifo, ʻa e ʻuluaki meʻa ʻoku ou tuʻu ai, ʻoku ʻikai ko ha kau tangata kehe ʻa kimoutolu, ka ko ia ʻoku ʻalu hifo ʻa e tafola ko iá?

Ka naʻá ne sio ofi ʻaupito ki he vaká; he hangē naʻá ne punou ke hola mo e sino naʻá ne fuá, pea hangē ko e feituʻu pau ʻo e fetaulaki fakaʻosí ko ha siteisi pē ia ʻi heʻene folau fakaʻosí, naʻe toe luelue tuʻumaʻu ʻa Mopi Tiki he taimí ni ki muʻa; pea naʻá ne meimei fakalaka ʻi he vaká, —ʻa ia naʻe folau ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimí ni ʻi he tafaʻaki fehangahangai mo iá, neongo ʻi he taimi ní kuo taʻofi ʻene laka ki muʻá. Naʻe hangē naʻá ne luelue ʻaki ʻene vave tahá, pea ko ʻeni ʻokú ne fakataumuʻa pē ke tuli hono hala hangatonu pē ʻoʻoná ʻi he tahí.

"ʻOiauē! ʻĒhapi," ko e kaila ia ʻa Sitāpakí, "ʻoku ʻikai fuʻu tōmui, naʻa mo e taimí ni, ko e ʻaho hono tolú, ke tuku. Vakai! ʻOku ʻikai kumi koe ʻe Mopi Tiki. Ko koe, ʻa koe, ʻokú ke kumi valevale kiate iá!"

‘I he‘ene folau ki he matangi ‘oku ‘alu haké, na‘e vave hono faka‘ai‘ai ‘a e vaka tokotahá ki he tafa‘aki ‘o e matangí, ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi folau mo e kaniva fakatou‘osi. Pea faifai pea ʻi he taimi naʻe luelue ai ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e vaká, ʻo ofi ʻaupito ke fakafaikehekeheʻi ʻa e fofonga ʻo Sitāpakí ʻi heʻene punou ʻi he lēlué, naʻá ne fakafeʻiloaki ia ke ne fakatafoki ʻa e vaká, pea muimui ʻiate ia, ʻo ʻikai fuʻu vave, ʻi ha vahaʻataimi fakapotopoto. ʻI heʻene sio hake ki ʻolungá, naʻá ne sio kia Tashtego, Queequeg, mo Daggoo, ʻoku nau heka vēkeveke ki he ʻuluʻi pou ʻe tolú; lolotonga ia ʻoku lulululu ʻa e kau folau ʻeveʻevá ʻi he ongo vaka ʻoku ʻi ai ha ʻakau ʻa ia naʻe toki hiki hake ki he tafaʻakí, pea naʻa nau femoʻuekina ʻi he ngāue ʻi hono fakaleleiʻi kinauá. ʻI he hokohoko atu ʻa e taha, ʻi he ngaahi ava ʻo e taulangá, ʻi heʻene lele vave atú, naʻá ne toe maʻu foki ha ngaahi sioʻata lele ʻo Stubb mo Flask, ʻo na femoʻuekina ʻi he funga vaká ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi fuʻu ʻaione mo e ngaahi tao foʻoú. ʻI heʻene mamata ki he meʻa ni kotoa; ʻo hangē ko ʻene fanongo ki he ngaahi hāmala ʻi he ngaahi vaka kuo maumaú; naʻe hangē naʻe fakaʻuli ʻe ha ngaahi hāmala kehe mamaʻo ha faʻo ki hono lotó. Ka naʻá ne fakatahataha. Pea ko ʻeni ʻi heʻene fakaʻilongaʻi kuo ʻosi ʻa e vane pe fuká mei he ʻuluʻi-pou lahí, naʻá ne kaila kia Tasiteko, ʻa ia naʻá ne toki maʻu ʻa e tuʻunga ko iá, ke ne toe hifo ki ha fuka ʻe taha, mo ha hāmala mo ha ngaahi faʻo, pea ko ia naʻá ne faʻo ia ki he pou-poú.

Pe fagged 'e he tuli lele 'aho 'e tolu, mo e fakafepaki ki he'ene kaukau 'i he hamper knotted na'a ne fua; pe ko ha faʻahinga kākā mo ha angakovi fakapulipuli ʻiate ia: pe ko e fē naʻe moʻoní, naʻe kamata ke holo hifo ʻa e hala ʻo e Tafola Hinehina he taimí ni, ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe ngali, mei he vaka naʻe toe vave ʻene ofi mai kiate iá; neongo ko e moʻoni naʻe ʻikai ke fuʻu lōloa ʻa e kamata fakaʻosi ʻo e tafola ʻo hangē ko ia kimuʻá. Pea ʻi he kei lele pē ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi he ngaahi peaú naʻe ʻalu fakataha mo ia ʻa e fanga ʻangá taʻeʻofa; pea ko ia naʻe piki fakaʻaufuli ki he vaká; pea naʻe hokohoko atu ʻene uʻu ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻauʻau ʻoku lele aí, ʻo hoko ai ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻaá ʻo ngingila mo ngingila, pea tuku ha fanga kiʻi foʻi ʻakau iiki ʻi he tahí, ʻi he meimei luelue kotoa pē.

"ʻOua naʻá ke tokanga kiate kinautolu! ʻa e nifo ko iá ka ʻoange ha ngaahi foʻi ʻakau foʻou ki hoʻo ngaahi foʻi ʻaá. Toʻo atu! Ko e mālōlōʻanga lelei ange ia, ko e kauʻā ʻo e ʻangá ʻi he vai ʻoku tukulolo.”

“Ka ʻi he uʻu kotoa pē, tangataʻeiki, ʻoku tupulaki ʻo siʻisiʻi ange ʻa e ngaahi mataʻi ʻakau manifi!”

“Te nau tolonga feʻunga! toʻo atu!—Ka ko hai te ne lava ʻo tala” —naʻá ne lāunga —“pe ʻoku luelue ʻa e fanga ʻangá ni ke nau kai ʻi he tafola pe ʻia ʻĒhapi?—Kae toʻo atu! ʻIo, moʻui kotoa, he taimí ni—ʻoku tau ofi kiate ia. Ko e taumuʻá! toʻo ʻa e taumuʻá! tuku ke u tokoniʻi ia ʻe he boar ki he kei vaka.

ʻI he taimi naʻe faifai pea lī ai ʻa e vaká ki he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá, pea lele holo fakataha mo e tafaʻaki ʻo e Tafola Hinehina, naʻe ngali kehe ʻene ngalo ʻi heʻene laka ki muʻá —ʻo hangē ko ia ʻe fai ʻe he tafola ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi —pea naʻe ʻi loto totonu ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi he kohu kohu ʻo e moʻungá, ʻa ia, ʻi hono lī atu mei he fuʻu huck ʻo e tafola ko hono Mon curnoad ,; na‘e a‘u ‘o ne ofi pehē kiate ia; ʻi he taimi, mo e sino ʻoku fakaʻauʻauhifo ki mui, pea fakatouʻosi hono ongo nimá ʻi hono lōloá ʻo hiki māʻolunga ki he tuʻunga māʻolungá, naʻá ne lī ʻene ukamea fefeká, mo ʻene fakamalaʻia fefeka mamaʻó ki he tafola fehiʻanekinaʻí. ʻI he ngoto fakatouʻosi ʻa e ukameá mo e fakamalaʻiá ki he soté, ʻo hangē kuo mimisi ki ha momoá, naʻe luelue ʻa Mopi Tiki ʻi he tafaʻakí; spasmodically rolled hono nigh flank ki he kaufana, pea, 'o 'ikai staving ha ava 'i ai, ko ia fakafokifā canted 'a e vaka 'o 'olunga, 'a ia kapau na'e 'ikai ko e konga ma'olunga 'o e gunwale 'a ia na'a ne piki ki ai, na'e mei toe lī 'a 'Ehapi ki tahi. Hangē ko ia naʻe hokó, naʻe lī ki tuʻa ha toko tolu ʻo e kau folau ʻeveʻevá —ʻa ia naʻe ʻikai ke nau tomuʻa ʻiloʻi ʻa e taimi tonu ʻo e foʻi ngahaú, pea ko ia ai naʻe ʻikai ke nau mateuteu ki hono ngaahi olá —naʻe lī ʻeni ki tuʻa; ka naʻe tō pehē, ʻo, ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou pē naʻe toe puke ʻe ha toko ua ʻo kinaua ʻa e gunwale, pea naʻá na ʻalu hake ki hono tuʻunga ʻi ha peau selu, ʻo na toe lī fakasino kinaua ki loto; ko e tangata hono tolú ʻoku tō taʻelavameʻa ki he tafaʻaki ki muí, ka ʻoku kei ʻalu holo pē mo luelue.

Meimei ʻi he taimi tatau, ʻi ha loto mālohi ʻo e vave taʻefakaʻosi, ʻi he taimi pē ko iá, naʻe lele vave atu ʻa e Tafola Hinehina ʻi he tahi ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai ha ʻuha. Ka ʻi he kalanga ʻa ʻEhapi ki he tangata fakaʻuli ke fai ha ngaahi afe foʻou ʻaki ʻa e laine, pea puke ia ʻo pehē; pea ne fekau ki he kau ngaue ke nau tafoki ʻi honau ngaahi nofoʻanga, ʻo toho hake ʻa e vaka ki he fakaʻilonga; ko e momeniti pē naʻe ongoʻi ai ʻe he laine kākaá ʻa e mafasia mo e toho lōua ko iá, naʻá ne pā ʻi he ʻea ʻataʻataá!

“Ko e hā ‘oku motuhi ‘iate aú? Ko e ngaahi mafesifesi ‘o e ngaahi uoua ‘e ni‘ihi!—‘oku toe kakato ia; folau! folau! Pa mai kiate ia!”

‘I he‘ene fanongo ki he lele lahi ‘a e vaka ‘oku tō ‘i he tahí, na‘e vili takai holo ai ‘a e tafola ke fakahaa‘i hono la‘e ‘ata‘atā ‘i he matātahí; ka ‘i he ‘evalūsio ko iá, ‘o sio ki he ofi mai ‘a e ‘uli‘uli ‘o e vaká; ngali sio ki ai ʻa e tupuʻanga ʻo ʻene ngaahi fakatanga kotoa pē; fakakaukau ki ai —mahalo ko ha fili lahi ange mo fakaʻeiʻeiki ange; fakafokifā pē, na‘á ne tō hifo ki hono mu‘a ‘o e vaká ‘oku ‘alu atu, ‘o ne taa‘i hono ongo kavá ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e ngaahi ‘uha vela ‘o e fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i manú.

Naʻe tetetete ʻa ʻĒhapi; naʻe taaʻi ʻe hono nimá hono laʻé. “ʻOku ou kui: mafao atu ʻae ongo nima ʻi hoku ʻao, koeʻuhi ke u toe ala ʻi hoku hala: ʻikai ko e pō?”

“Ko e tafola! Ko e vaká!” ko e kaila ia ʻa e kau folau ʻeveʻevá.

“Ngaahi folau! folau! Hifo hifo ki ho loloto, ʻe tahi, koeʻuhí ke ʻoua naʻa fuʻu tōmui ʻo taʻengata, ʻe lava ʻe ʻĒhapi ʻo tō ʻi he taimi fakaʻosi ko ʻení, fakaʻosi ʻi hono fakaʻilongá! ʻOku ou sio: ko e vaká! ko e vaká! Lele atu, ʻeku kau tangatá! ʻE ʻikai te mou fakahaofi ʻeku vaká?”

Ka ʻi hono fakamālohiʻi fakamālohi ʻe he kau folau ʻeveʻevá honau vaká ʻi he ngaahi tahi ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻuhikiʻi ʻakaú, naʻe pa ʻa e ngaahi ngataʻanga kaufana ʻo e ongo laupapa naʻe ʻosi taaʻi ʻe he tafola kimuʻá, pea ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou meimei, naʻe meimei tatau ʻa e vaka naʻe faingataʻaʻia fakataimi mo e ngaahi peaú; ko ‘ene kau ngāue ‘oku nau vaeua‘i ‘a e vaí, ‘o nau puna, ‘o nau feinga mālohi ke ta‘ofi ‘a e ava ‘o e vai ‘oku lilingí.

Lolotonga iá, ki he momeniti sio ko ia ʻe taha, naʻe kei tautau pē ʻi hono nimá ʻa e hāmala ʻulu-pou ʻo Tasitekó; pea ko e fuka kulokulá, ʻo ne takaiʻi ia ʻo vaeua ʻo hangē ha peletí, pea tafe hangatonu mai ia meiate ia, ʻo hangē ko hono loto ʻoʻona ʻoku tafe ki muʻá; lolotonga ia ko Starbuck mo Stubb, ʻi heʻena tuʻu ʻi he bowsprit ʻi laló, naʻá na sio ki he fuʻu manu fekai ʻoku ʻalu hifo ʻi he taimi pē ko iá.

“Ko e tafola, ko e tafola! Hake 'a e taumu'a, 'i 'olunga 'a e taumu'a! 'Oiauē, 'a kimoutolu kotoa 'o e ngaahi malohi melie 'o e 'ea, ko 'eni 'uma mai kiate au ofi! 'Oua na'a mate 'a Starbuck, kapau 'e mate kuo pau ke ne, 'i ha fefine 'oku 'ikai ke ne toe lava. ʻEhapi, ko hoʻo ngāué.Tuʻumaʻu, ʻikai!

"'Oua 'e tu'u 'i hoku tafa'aki, ka ke tu'u 'i hoku lalo, pe ko hai koe 'e tokoni kia Stubb he taimi ni; he ko Stubb, foki, 'oku ne piki heni. 'Oku ou grin kiate koe, 'a e tafola malimali! Ko hai na'a ne tokoni'i 'a Stubb, pe tauhi 'a Stubb ke 'ā, ka ko e mata 'o Stubb tonu 'oku 'ikai ke kuikui? Pea ko 'eni 'oku 'alu 'a Stubb masiva ke mohe 'i ha fu'u bruff ia kotoa pe; grin kiate koe, 'a e tafola 'oku malimali! Kiate au, off sū mo e kote ki ai;

"Sieli? ʻOku ou fakaʻamu pē ke ma ʻi he feituʻu ʻoku nau tupu aí. ʻOiauē, Situpi, ʻoku ou ʻamanaki kuo toʻo ʻe heʻeku faʻē masivá ʻeku konga totongi kimuʻa ʻi he meʻá ni; kapau ʻoku ʻikai, ʻe siʻisiʻi pē ʻa e ngaahi kopa ʻe haʻu kiate ia he taimí ni, he kuo ʻosi ʻa e folaú."

Mei he ngaahi kaufana ‘o e vaká, na‘e meimei tautau kotoa ai ‘a e kau toutaí he taimí ni ‘o ‘ikai ke nau ngāue; ngaahi hāmala, ngaahi konga laupapa, ngaahi tao, mo e ngaahi heletā, na‘e puke fakamīsini ‘i honau nimá, ‘o hangē pē ko ‘enau lele vave mei he‘enau ngaahi ngāue kehekehé; ko honau mata fakaʻofoʻofa kotoa ʻoku fakataumuʻa ki he tafola, ʻa ia mei he tafaʻaki ki he tafaʻaki ʻoku ngalulululu ngali kehe hono ʻulu kuo ʻosi fakanofo, ʻo ne fekauʻi atu ha fuʻu ʻakau lahi ʻo e foam semicircular ʻoku mafola ʻi muʻa ʻiate ia ʻi heʻene lele vave atú. Naʻe ʻi hono tafaʻaki kotoa ʻa e tauteá, tautea vave, angakovi taʻengatá, pea neongo ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē naʻe lava ʻe he tangata fakamatelié ʻo faí, ka naʻe taaʻi ʻe he poupou hinehina fefeka ʻo hono laʻé ʻa e kaufana ʻo e vaka ʻi he tafaʻaki toʻomataʻú, kae ʻoua kuo luelue ʻa e tangatá mo e ʻakaú. Naʻe tō ʻa e niʻihi ʻo foʻi fofonga. Hangē ko e ngaahi loli kuo fakaʻauhá, naʻe lulululu ʻa e ʻulu ʻo e kau haʻapo ʻi ʻolungá ʻi honau kia hangē ha pulú. ‘I he fou ‘i he mavahé, na‘a nau fanongo ai ki he lilingi ‘a e vaí, ‘i he tafe hifo ‘a e ngaahi vaitafe ‘o e mo‘ungá ‘i ha vaitafe.

“Ko e vaká! Ko e saliote mate!—ko e saliote mate hono uá!” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻĒhapi mei he vaká; “ko hono ʻakaú ʻe lava pē ke ʻAmelika!”

‘I he‘ene luelue ‘i lalo ‘i he vaka nofo‘angá, na‘e lele tetetete ‘a e tafola ‘i hono ‘ulú; ka ʻi heʻene tafoki ʻi lalo vaí, ʻo ne toe fana fakavavevave ki ʻolunga, ʻo mamaʻo mei he kaufana ʻe tahá, ka ʻi ha ngaahi ʻiate siʻi pē mei he vaka ʻo ʻĒhapí, ʻa ia, ʻi ha taimi, naʻá ne tokoto fakalongolongo ai.

“ʻOku ou tafoki hoku sinó mei he laʻaá. Ko e hā ho, Tasitego! tuku ke u fanongo ki hoʻo hāmalá. ʻOiauē! ʻa kimoutolu ʻe tolu ʻeku ngaahi tumuʻaki ʻoku ʻikai ke tukulolo; ʻa koe ʻa e ʻulu ʻoku ʻikai ke mafesifesi; pea ko e vakapuna pē ʻoku fakatangaʻi ʻe he ʻotuá; ʻa e fungavaka fefeká, mo e taumuʻa hīkisiá, mo e muʻa ʻo e Pole-pointed,—ʻoku mate-langilangiʻi ʻa e vaká ʻiate au!, pea kuo pau ke u ʻalu atu mei he vaka ʻoku ʻikai ke mou ʻalu atu! fond pride of meanest shipwreck captains? 'Oiauē, ko 'eni 'oku ou ongo'i 'oku 'i ai hoku lahi taha 'i 'olunga 'i hoku ngaahi ngata'anga mama'o taha, lilingi 'a kimoutolu he taimi ni ki loto, kimoutolu 'a e ngaahi peau lotolahi 'o 'eku mo'ui 'e taha kotoa 'o e thou, pea! tafola faka'auha kotoa pe ka 'oku 'ikai ke ikuna'i au; ki he faka'osi 'oku ou fefa'uhi mo koe; tafola! Ko ia , ‘oku ou tukuange ‘a e taó!”

Naʻe fakaʻauha ʻa e haʻapuní; naʻe lele ki muʻa ʻa e tafola naʻe taaʻí; mo e vave faka'auha na'e lele 'a e laine 'i he ngaahi 'aofi;—lele foul. Na‘e punou ‘a ‘Ēhapi ke fakama‘a ia; naʻá ne fakamaʻa ia; ka naʻe maʻu ia ʻe he tafoki lele ʻi hono kiá, pea ʻi he leʻo taʻeʻaonga ʻi he kaufana ʻa e kau mute ʻo Take ʻi heʻenau vikitiá, naʻe fanaʻi ia mei he vaká, kimuʻa pea toki ʻilo ʻe he kau ngāué kuó ne ʻosi ʻalu. ʻI he momeniti hono hokó, naʻe lele atu ʻa e foʻi mata mamafa ʻi he ngataʻanga fakaʻosi ʻo e maeá mei he tāpuni ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa ʻi aí, ʻo ne tuki hifo ha tangata folau, pea ʻi heʻene taaʻi ʻa e tahí, ʻo puli atu ʻi hono lolotó.

ʻI ha kiʻi taimi nounou, naʻe tuʻu maʻu ʻa e kau ngāue ʻo e vaka naʻe ʻosi fakaʻauʻauhifó; pea tafoki leva. “Ko e vaka? ‘Otua lahi, ‘oku ‘i fe ‘a e vaka?” Naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo nau sio ʻi he ngaahi meʻangāue fakapoʻuli mo fakatupu puputuʻú ki hono phantom ʻoku ʻosi ʻi he tafaʻakí, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻi he Fata Morgana kasa; ko e ngaahi pou pē ‘i ‘olungá mei he vaí; lolotonga hono fakapapau‘i ‘e he ‘ofa ‘i he ‘ofá, pe faitōnungá, pe iku‘angá, ki honau ngaahi tu‘unga mā‘olunga ‘i ha taimi, na‘e kei tauhi pē ‘e he kau tautau fakapanganí ‘enau ngaahi sio‘ata ngoto ‘i he tahí. “PEA KO AU PE KUO HOLA TOKOTAHA KE TALA KIATE KOE” Siope.

Kuo 'osi 'a e talanoa. Ko e hā leva ‘oku laka atu ai ha taha heni?—Koe‘uhi na‘e hao mo‘oni ha taha mei he maumaú.

Naʻe hoko fakatuʻupakē pē, ʻi he hili ʻa e puli ʻa e Parsee, ko au ia naʻe fakanofo ʻe he Fates ke u fetongi ʻa e tangata fanafana ʻa ʻĒhapí, ʻi he taimi naʻe maʻu ai ʻe he tangata fanafana ko iá ʻa e lakanga ʻataʻatā; ko e tokotaha tatau pē, ʻa ia, ʻi he ʻaho fakaʻosí naʻe lī ai ʻa e kau tangata ʻe toko tolú mei tuʻa mei he vaka luelué, naʻe fakatō hifo ia ki mui. Ko ia, ʻi heʻeku ʻalu holo ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e meʻa naʻe hoko atu aí, pea ʻi heʻeku sio kakato ki aí, ʻi he aʻu mai kiate au ʻa e huhuʻa vaeua ʻo e vaka ngotó, naʻe tohoakiʻi au ʻi he taimi ko iá, ka naʻe māmālie pē, ki he vortex tāpuní. ʻI heʻeku aʻu ki aí, kuo holo hifo ia ʻo hoko ko ha vaitupu kilimi. Takai mo takai, pea, pea maʻu pē ʻo fakaʻau ke fakahangatonu ki he foʻi pulu ʻuliʻuli hangē ha pātení ʻi he ʻasi ʻo e siakale ʻoku uili māmālie ko iá, ʻo hangē ha toe Ixion naʻá ku fai ʻo vilo. Till, maʻu ʻa e senitā mahuʻinga ko iá, naʻe pa ʻa e pupula ʻuliʻuli ki ʻolungá; pea ko ʻeni, kuo fakatauʻatāinaʻi koeʻuhí ko ʻene matavai kākaá, pea, koeʻuhí ko ʻene puna lahi, ʻo ʻalu hake ʻi ha mālohi lahi, naʻe fanaʻi lōloa ʻa e puha fakahaofi moʻui ʻo e puha maté mei he tahí, ʻo tō hifo, ʻo puna ʻi hoku tafaʻakí. ʻI hono fakaʻaiʻai au ʻe he puha mate ko iá, ʻi ha meimei ʻaho mo e pō kakato ʻe taha, ne u lele ʻi ha vakapuna lahi molumalu mo hangē ha dirge. Ko e fanga ‘anga‘anga ta‘efakalaveá, na‘a nau lele holo ‘o hangē ‘oku ‘i ai ha ngaahi loka ‘i honau ngutú; na‘e folau ‘a e fanga manupuna tahi-tahí mo honau ngutu ‘oku ‘ufi‘ufi. ʻI he ʻaho hono uá, naʻe ofi mai ha folau, ʻo ofi ange, pea faifai pea ʻohake au. Ko e fefine folau vakapuna kākā ko Lesielí, ʻa ia ʻi heʻene toe fekumi ki heʻene fānau ne molé, naʻá ne maʻu pē ha toe tamasiʻi mate ʻe taha.
