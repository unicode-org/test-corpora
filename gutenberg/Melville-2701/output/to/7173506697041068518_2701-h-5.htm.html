VAHE 22. Kilisimasi fiefia.

Faifai pea, ʻi he ofi ki he hoʻataá, ʻi hono fakaʻatā fakaʻosi ʻo e kau ngāue ʻo e vaká, pea hili hono toho ʻo e Pequod mei he taulangá, pea hili ʻa e ʻalu hifo ʻa e Charity fakakaukau maʻu peé ʻi ha vaka tafola, mo ʻene meʻaʻofa fakaʻosí —ko ha pulou poʻuli maʻa Stubb, ko e hoa hono uá, ko hono tokoua-ʻi-he-fono ko ʻení —ko e Tohitapu kotoa ko e ua, Naʻe ʻalu atu ʻa e ongo ʻeikitaú, ko Peleki mo Pilitati, mei he kāpiní, pea ʻi heʻene tafoki ki he hoa pule, naʻe pehē ʻe Peleki:

“Ko ʻeni, Misa Sitāpaki, ʻokú ke fakapapauʻi ʻoku tonu ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē? Kuo mateuteu kotoa ʻa Kapiteni ʻĒhapi —naʻá ku lea pē kiate ia —ʻoku ʻikai ha toe meʻa ke maʻu mei ʻuta, ʻē? Sai, ui ʻa e nima kotoa pē, pea. Fakatahatahaʻi kinautolu ʻi mui ʻi heni —fakapaku kinautolu!"

“ʻOku ʻikai fie maʻu ha ngaahi lea fakalieliá, neongo pe ko e hā hono lahi ʻo e fakavavevavé, Peleki,” ko e lea ia ʻa Pilitatí, “ka ke ʻalu atu, kaungāmeʻa Sitāpaki, pea fai ʻemau fekaú.”

Fēfē he taimí ni! ʻI heni ʻi he taimi tonu ʻo e kamataʻanga ʻo e folaú, naʻe ʻalu atu ʻa Kapiteni Peleki mo Kapiteni Pilitati ʻaki ha nima māʻolunga ʻi he kuata-ʻaá, ʻo hangē pē ko ʻena hoko ko ha ongo ʻeikitau fakataha ʻi tahí, pea pehē ki he ngaahi hā kotoa pē ʻi he taulangá. Pea ko e meʻa ki he ʻEikitau ko ʻEhapi, naʻe teʻeki ke ʻasi ha fakaʻilonga ʻo ia; ko e meʻa pē, naʻa nau pehē ʻokú ne ʻi he kāpiní. Ka ʻi he taimi ko iá, ko e fakakaukaú, naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito fie maʻu ʻene ʻi aí ʻi hono ʻai ke mamafa ʻa e vaká, mo hono fakahinohinoʻi lelei ia ki tahi. Ko e moʻoni, ʻi he ʻikai ʻaupito ko ʻene meʻa totonu ia, ka ko e pailate; pea koeʻuhí naʻe teʻeki ke ne moʻui kakato —ko ia ʻenau leá —ko ia, naʻe nofo pē ʻa e ʻEikitau ko ʻĒhapí ʻi lalo. Pea naʻe ngali fakanatula feʻunga ʻeni kotoa; tautautefito ki he ngāue fakakomēsialé ʻoku ʻikai ʻaupito ke fakahaaʻi ʻe he kau kapiteni tokolahi ʻa kinautolu ʻi he funga vaká ʻi ha taimi lahi hili ʻenau hiki hake ʻa e taulá, ka ʻoku nau kei ʻi ʻolunga pē ʻi he tēpile ʻo e kāpiní, ʻo fai ha fakafiefia fakamāvae mo honau ngaahi kaungāmeʻa ʻi he matāfangá, kimuʻa pea nau toki mavahe mei he vaká ʻo taʻengata mo e pailaté.

Ka naʻe ʻikai ha faingamālie lahi ke fakakaukauʻi ʻa e meʻá ni, he naʻe moʻui kotoa ʻa Kapiteni Peleki he taimí ni. Naʻe hangē naʻá ne fai ʻa e konga lahi ʻo e talanoá mo e fekaú, kae ʻikai ko Pilitati.

“Mou ʻalu ʻi heni, ʻa kimoutolu ngaahi foha ʻo e kau taʻemali,” ko ʻene kalanga ia, ʻi he nofo ʻa e kau folaú ʻi he pou vaká. “Misa Sitāpaki, fakaʻuli kinautolu ki mui.”

“Taa‘i ‘a e tēnití ‘i aí!”—ko e fekau hono hokó ia. Hange ko ia ne u hint kimu'a, na'e 'ikai 'aupito ke fokotu'u 'a e whalebone marquee ko 'eni tukukehe 'i he taulanga; pea ʻi he vakapuna Pequod, ʻi ha taʻu ʻe tolungofulu, naʻe ʻiloa lelei ʻa e fekau ke taaʻi ʻa e tēnití ko e meʻa ia hono hoko ki hono hiki hake ʻo e taulá.

“Man the capstan! Toto mo e manamana!—puna!”—ko e fekau hono hokó ia, pea na‘e puna ‘a e kau ngāué ki he ngaahi fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i nimá.

Ko ʻeni ʻi he maʻu ʻi lalo ʻi he mamafá, ko e tuʻuʻanga ʻoku nofoʻi fakalūkufua ʻe he pailate ko e konga ki muʻa ʻo e vaká. Pea ko Pilitati heni, ʻa ia, mo Peleki, ke ʻiloʻi, ʻo tānaki atu ki heʻene kau ʻōfisa kehé, ko e taha ia ʻo e kau pailate laiseni ʻo e taulangá —ʻi hono mahaloʻi naʻá ne ʻai ia ke ne hoko ko ha pailate koeʻuhí ke fakahaofi ʻa e totongi pailate ʻo Nantucket ki he ngaahi vaka kotoa pē naʻá ne hohaʻa ki aí, he naʻe ʻikai ke ne teitei pailateʻi ha toe vakapuna kehe naʻe sio ki ai ʻa Pilitati punou ki he taula ʻoku fakaofi maí, pea ʻi ha ngaahi vahaʻataimi ʻo hivaʻi ʻa e meʻa naʻe ngali ko ha foʻi hiva fakaloloma ʻo e saame, ke fakafiefiaʻi ʻa e ongo nimá ʻi he windlass, ʻa ia naʻá ne ngungulu mai ha faʻahinga hiva fakatahataha fekauʻaki mo e fānau fefine ʻi he Booble Alley, ʻaki ha loto lelei loto-māfana. Ka neongo ia, naʻe ʻikai ʻaho ʻe tolu kimuʻá, naʻe talaange ʻe Pilitati kiate kinaua ʻe ʻikai fakaʻatā ha ngaahi hiva fakalieliá ʻi he vaka Pequod, tautautefito ki he maʻu ʻo e mamafá; pea naʻe ʻosi ʻai ʻe Kaleti, ko hono tuofefiné, ha kiʻi tatau fili ʻo Uati ʻi he tauʻanga ʻo e kau toutaí takitaha.

Lolotonga iá, ʻi heʻene tokangaʻi ʻa e konga ʻe taha ʻo e vaká, naʻe haeʻi mo fuakava ʻa ʻEikitau Peleki ki he tafaʻaki ki mui ʻi he founga fakailifia tahá. Naʻá ku meimei fakakaukau te ne fakangoto ʻa e vaká kimuʻa pea toki fokotuʻu hake ʻa e taulá; naʻá ku kiʻi tuʻu taʻefakakaukauʻi ʻi hoku foʻi ʻuhikiʻi nimá, peá u talaange kia Kuiki ke ne fai ʻa e meʻa tatau, ʻi heʻeku fakakaukau ki he ngaahi fakatuʻutāmaki naʻá ma fakatou lele aí, ʻi he kamata ʻo e folaú mo ha tēvolo pehē ki ha pailate. Kae kehe, naʻá ku fakafiemālieʻi au ʻaki ʻa e fakakaukau ʻe maʻu nai ha fakamoʻui ʻi he tangata anga fakaʻotuá ko Pilitati, neongo ʻene nofo ʻi hono fitungeau fitungofulu mā fitú; ʻi heʻeku ongoʻi ha tukituki māsila fakafokifā ʻi hoku tuʻá, peá u tafoki takai, naʻá ku ilifia ʻi he hā mai ʻa e ʻEikitau Pelekí ʻi heʻene toʻo hono vaʻé mei hoku ʻātakai ofí. Ko ʻeku fuofua ʻaka ia.

“Ko e founga ia ʻoku nau hiki hake ai ʻi he ngāue fakatauʻanga koloá?” naʻá ne ngungulu. “Puna, ʻa koe ʻulu-sipi; puna, pea motuhi ho hui tuʻá! Ko e hā ʻoku ʻikai ke mou puna ai, ʻoku ou pehē, ʻa kimoutolu kotoa —puna! Quohog! puna, ʻa koe chap mo e ngaahi nifo kulokula; puna ʻi ai, Scotch-cap; puna, koe talausese lanumata. Puna, ʻoku ou pehē, ʻa kimoutolu kotoa, pea puna homou matá ki tuʻa!" Pea ʻi heʻene lea peheé, naʻá ne ngaʻunu ʻi he hala ʻo e matangí, ʻi heni mo hena ʻo fakaʻaongaʻi tauʻatāina ʻaupito hono vaʻé, lolotonga ia ʻoku kei takimuʻa atu pē ʻa Pilitati taʻe-moveuveú mo ʻene hiva sāmé. Fakakaukau au, kuo pau pe na'e inu 'e Kapiteni Peleki ha me'a he 'aho ni.

Naʻe faifai pea ʻalu hake ʻa e taulá, kuo fokotuʻu ʻa e ngaahi folaú, pea mau lele atu. Ko ha Kilisimasi nounou mo momoko ia; pea ʻi he fakatahaʻi ʻe he ʻaho nounou ʻo e tokelaú ʻa e poʻulí, naʻá ma ʻiloʻi ʻoku mau meimei ʻataʻatā ʻi he ʻoseni momokó, ʻa ia ko hono ʻuʻulu ʻaisi naʻá ne ʻufiʻufi kimautolu ʻi he ʻaisí, ʻo hangē ko ha ngaahi teunga tau kuo fakangingilá. Naʻe ulo ʻa e ngaahi laine lōloa ʻo e nifo ʻi he ngaahi ʻā ʻi he maama ʻo e māhiná; pea hangē ko e ngaahi nifo ‘aivoli hinehina ‘o e fanga ‘elefānite lalahi ‘e ni‘ihi, na‘e fakafalala ‘a e ngaahi ‘aisi lalahi pikopiko mei he ngaahi kaufaná.

Naʻe taki ʻe Lank Bildad, ʻi heʻene hoko ko e pailate, ʻa e ʻuluaki leʻó, pea ʻi he taimi kotoa pē, ʻi he lue loloto ʻa e vaka motuʻá ki he ngaahi tahi lanu mata, pea ʻave ʻa e ʻuha momoko tetetete ʻiate ia kotoa, pea ʻuʻulu ʻa e ngaahi matangí, pea tatangi ʻa e afo, naʻe ongo mai ʻene ngaahi nota tuʻumaʻú,—

      “Ngaahi ngoue melie ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘o e tāfea ‘oku fula, .
Tuʻu ʻo teunga ʻi he lanu mata moʻuí.
Ko ia ki he kau Siú naʻe tuʻu ʻa Kēnani motuʻa, .
Lolotonga e takai holo ʻa Sioatani ʻi he vahaʻá.”

Naʻe teʻeki ai ke ongo melie ange ʻa e ngaahi lea melie ko iá kiate au ʻi he taimi ko iá. Naʻa nau fonu ʻi he ʻamanaki leleí mo e fuá. Neongo ʻa e pō momoko ko ʻeni ʻo e faʻahitaʻu momokó ʻi he ʻAtalanitiki longoaʻá, neongo ʻeku vaʻe sūú mo hoku kote sū angé, naʻe kei ʻi ai pē, naʻe ngali kiate au ʻi he taimi ko iá, ha ngaahi hūfangaʻanga fakafiefia lahi ʻi he falekoloá; pea ko e ngaahi vaotā mo e ngaahi vaotā ʻoku fuʻu taʻengata ʻaupito ʻa e faʻahitaʻu failaú, ʻo ʻikai ke ʻalu hake ʻa e mohuku ʻoku fanaʻi hake ʻe he matavai, ʻo ʻikai ke laiki, ʻoku ʻikai ke mae, ʻi he vaeuaʻanga ʻo e faʻahitaʻu māfaná.

Naʻe faifai peá ma maʻu ha ofing pehē, ʻo ʻikai ke toe fie maʻu ai ʻa e ongo pailaté. Naʻe kamata ke lele ʻi he tafaʻakí ʻa e vaka folau fefeka naʻe ʻalu fakataha mo kimauá.

Naʻe fakatupu fieʻilo pea ʻikai ke taʻefakafiefia, ʻa e founga naʻe uesia ai ʻa Peleki mo Pilitati ʻi he taimi ko ʻení, tautautefito kia ʻEikitau Pilitati. Mo fakalili'a ke mavahe, ka; fakaliliʻa ʻaupito ke mavahe, ki he leleí, mei ha vaka ʻoku haʻi ki ha folau lōloa mo fakatuʻutāmaki pehē —ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e ongo Keipi afā fakatouʻosi; ko ha vaka na‘e ‘inivesi ai ‘a e ngaahi pa‘anga ‘e lauiafe nai na‘á ne ma‘u faivelengá; ko ha vaka, naʻe folau ai ha kaungāvaka motuʻa ko e kapiteni; ko ha tangata meimei motuʻa tatau mo ia, ʻo toe kamata ke ne fetaulaki mo e ngaahi fakailifia kotoa ʻo e kauʻā taʻeʻofa; fakaliliʻa ke lea māvae ki ha meʻa ʻoku fuʻu fonu ʻi he founga kotoa pē ʻo e mahuʻingaʻia kotoa pē kiate iá, —naʻe fuoloa ʻa e nofo ʻa e motuʻa masiva ko Pilitati; naʻá ne luelue ʻi he funga vaká ʻaki ha ngaahi laka hohaʻa; naʻá ne lele hifo ki he lokí ke toe fai ha lea fakamāvae ʻi ai; naʻá ne toe haʻu ki he funga vakapuná, ʻo ne sio ki he matangi; na‘e sio atu ki he ngaahi vai ‘oku fōlahi mo ta‘e ngata, ‘a ia na‘e fakangatangata pē ia ‘e he ngaahi Konitinēniti Hahake mama‘o ‘oku ‘ikai ke sio ki aí; naʻe sio atu ki he fonuá; naʻe sio ki ʻolunga; sio toʻomataʻu mo toʻohema; sio ki he feituʻu kotoa pē pea ʻikai ha feituʻu; pea faifai pea, ʻi heʻene fakaʻauha fakamīsini ha maea ʻi hono pine, ʻo ne puke fakaʻauʻauhifo ʻa Peleki fefeka ʻi hono nimá, mo puke hake ha maama, ʻi ha kiʻi taimi naʻá ne tuʻu ʻo sio fakatoʻa ki hono fofongá, ʻo hangē pē ko ʻene pehē, “Ka neongo ia, kaungāmeʻa Peleki, te u lava ʻo tuʻu ki ai; ʻio, te u lava.”

Ko Peleki tonu, na‘á ne to‘o ia ‘o hangē ha filōsefa lahi ange; ka neongo ʻene filōsofia kotoa pē, naʻe ʻi ai ha loʻimata naʻe ngingila ʻi hono matá, ʻi he taimi naʻe fuʻu ofi mai ai ʻa e māmá. Pea ko ia foki, naʻe ʻikai ke ne kiʻi lele mei he kāpiní ki he funga vaká —ko ha foʻi lea ʻi lalo he taimí ni, pea ko ha foʻi lea he taimí ni mo Starbuck, ko e hoa tefitó.

Ka, faifai pea, naʻá ne tafoki ki hono kaungāmeʻá, mo ha faʻahinga sio fakaʻosi fekauʻaki mo ia,—“Kapiteni Pilitati—haʻu, kaungā vaka motuʻa, kuo pau ke ta ʻalu. Foki ki he lotoʻā lahi ʻi aí! Vaka ahoy! Tuʻu ʻo ofi mai ʻi he tafaʻakí, he taimí ni! Tokanga, tokanga!—haʻu, Pilitati, kia Luckbu—talaange hoʻo yest. Stubb —monūʻia kiate kimoutolu, Misa Flask —māvae mo e monūʻia kiate kimoutolu kotoa —pea ko e ʻaho ní ʻi ha taʻu ʻe tolu te u maʻu ai ha maʻu meʻatokoni efiafi vela ʻo ifi maʻamoutolu ʻi Nantucket motuʻa pea ʻalu atu!”

“Ke tāpuakiʻi kimoutolu ʻe he ʻOtuá, pea ke mou ʻi Heʻene tauhi māʻoniʻoní, ʻe kau tangata,” ko e lāunga ia ʻa e motuʻa ko Pilitati, ʻo meimei taʻefeʻunga. “ʻOku ou ʻamanaki te mou maʻu ha ʻea lelei he taimí ni, koeʻuhí ke vave ʻa e ngaʻunu ʻa Kapiteni ʻĒhapí ʻi homou lotolotongá —ko ha laʻā fakafiefia pē ʻokú ne fie maʻú, pea te mou maʻu lahi ʻi he folau fakatalopiki ʻoku mou ʻalú. 'Oua 'e ngalo ho'o ngaahi lotu, pe Mr. Starbuck, 'atamai 'oku 'ikai ke maumau'i 'e he cooper 'a e ngaahi fo'i 'akau 'oku toe 'i he loki lanumata 'o e 'aho 'o e tafola, kau tangata; Stubb;naʻe kiʻi leakage, naʻá ku fakakaukau.Kapau te mou ala ki he ngaahi motú, Misa Flask, tokanga ki he feʻauakí, māvae, ʻOua naʻá ke tauhi ʻa e siisi ko iá ʻo fuʻu lōloa ʻi lalo ʻi he ʻaá, Misa Starbuck ʻe maumauʻi ia kapau naʻe p—

“Haʻu, haʻu, ʻEikitau Pilitati, tuku hoʻo palaver, —ʻalu!” pea ʻi heʻene peheé, naʻe fakavavevaveʻi ia ʻe Peleki ʻi he tafaʻakí, pea naʻá na fakatou tō ki he vaká.

Naʻe mavahe ʻa e vaká mo e vaká; naʻe angi ʻa e havili momoko mo sūsū ʻo e poʻulí ʻi he vahaʻá; naʻe lele hake ha ʻuhikiʻi manupuna kaila ʻi ʻolunga; naʻe takai holo ʻa e ongo huló; naʻá ma fai ha ngaahi hiva fakafiefia loto mamafa ʻe tolu, pea tō fakakuihi ʻo hangē ha ikuʻangá ki he ʻAtalanitiki tokotaha pē.





VAHE 23. Ko e 'Otu Motu Lee.

ʻI ha ngaahi vahe ʻe niʻihi kimuʻa, naʻe lea ʻaki ha taha ʻo Bulkington, ko ha tangata toutai lōloa mo foʻou, naʻá ne fetaulaki ʻi Niu Petifooti ʻi he fale talitali kakaí.

ʻI he taimi ʻi he pō tetetete ko ia ʻo e faʻahitaʻu momokó, naʻe lī ai ʻe he Pequod ʻene ngaahi kaufana tauteá ki he ngaahi peau kovi momokó, ko hai ʻoku totonu ke u sio ki ai ʻoku tuʻu ʻi hono taumuʻá ka ko Bulkington pē! Naʻá ku sio ʻi he manavahē mo e manavasiʻi kaungāongoʻi ki he tangatá, ʻa ia ʻi he vaeuaʻanga ʻo e faʻahitaʻu momokó naʻá ne toki tūʻuta mai mei ha folau fakatuʻutāmaki ʻi ha taʻu ʻe fā, ʻe lava ke ne toe tekeʻi taʻefiemālie atu ki ha toe taimi ʻe taha ʻo e afā. Naʻe hangē naʻe vela ʻa e fonuá ki hono vaʻé. Ko e ngaahi meʻa fakaofo tahá ko e ngaahi meʻa ʻoku ʻikai lava ke lave ki aí; ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻomi ʻe he ngaahi manatu lolotó ha ngaahi ʻepitafi; ko e vahe ʻinisi ʻe ono ko ʻení ko e faʻitoka taʻemaka ʻo Bulkington. Tuku ke u pehē pē naʻe lelei pē ia kiate ia ʻo hangē ko e vaka naʻe lī ʻe he matangí, ʻa ia ʻoku lele fakaʻofa ʻi he fonua ʻoku ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e matangí. Ko e taulanga te ne fain 'oatu 'a e tokoni; ʻoku fakaʻofa ʻa e taulanga; ʻi he taulangá ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e malu, fakafiemālie, maka ʻo e ʻaá, maʻu meʻatokoni efiafi, ngaahi palanikete māfana, ngaahi kaungāmeʻa, ko e meʻa kotoa pē ʻoku angaʻofa ki hotau ngaahi moʻui fakamatelié. Ka ʻi he matangi ko iá, ko e taulangá, ʻa e fonuá, ko e fakatuʻutāmaki lahi taha ia ʻo e vaka ko iá; kuo pau ke ne lele ʻa e talitali kakai kotoa pē; ko ha paʻanga ʻe taha ʻo e kelekelé, neongo ʻokú ne kaikai pē ʻi he ʻotu moʻungá, te ne ʻai ia ke ne tetetete ʻo ʻosi mo ʻosi. ‘I hono mālohí kotoa ‘okú ne fakatahataha‘i ‘a e folau kotoa mei ‘uta; ‘i he‘ene fai iá, ‘okú ne tau‘i ai ‘a e ngaahi matangi tonu ‘e puhi‘i ia ‘e he fain ki ‘apí; ‘okú ne toe kumi ki he ngaahi fonua ta‘e‘aonga kotoa ‘o e tahi kuo taa‘í; koeʻuhi ko e hūfangaʻanga ʻoku lele fakatuʻutāmaki ki he fakatuʻutāmakí; ko hono kaume‘a pē ‘e tahá ko hono fili mamahi tahá!

Ko ʻene taʻeʻilo mo taʻeʻiloʻi ko ʻení naʻá ne ʻai ia ke ne kiʻi waggish ʻi he meʻa fekauʻaki mo e fanga tafola; naʻá ne muimui ʻi he fanga ika ko ʻení koeʻuhí ko e fakafiefia ʻo e meʻa ko iá; pea ko ha folau taʻu ʻe tolu ʻo takai ʻi Keipi Honi ko ha fakakata fakafiefia pē ia naʻe tolonga ʻi he taimi lōloa ko iá. Ko Tashtego ko Stubb ko e squire ia ʻo e hoa hono uá.

Ko e tolu ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e kau tā-haá ko Daggoo, ko ha fu‘u tangata negro-savage ‘uli‘uli hangē ha mahoa‘á, ‘oku ‘i ai hono laka hangē ha laioné—ko ha ‘Ahasivelo ke sio ki ai. Naʻe tautau ʻi hono telingá ha ongo foʻi ʻakau koula, ʻo fuʻu lahi ʻaupito ʻo ui kinaua ʻe he kau folaú ko e ngaahi foʻi mama, pea te nau talanoa ki hono fakamaʻu ʻo e ngaahi foʻi folau ʻi ʻolungá kiate kinaua. ʻI heʻene kei talavoú naʻe folau loto fiemālie ʻa Daggoo ʻi ha vaka tafola, ʻo tokoto ʻi ha matātahi tuēnoa ʻi hono matāfanga tupuʻangá. Pea ‘i he ‘ikai ‘aupito ke ne ‘i ha feitu‘u ‘i he māmaní ka ‘i ‘Afilika, Nantucket, mo e ngaahi taulanga fakapangani ‘oku toutou ‘a‘ahi lahi taha ki ai ‘a e kau toutai tafola; pea ʻi heʻene taki he taimí ni ʻi ha ngaahi taʻu lahi ʻa e moʻui loto-toʻa ʻo e toutaí ʻi he ngaahi vaka ʻo e kau ʻea ʻoku nau tokanga taʻe angamaheni ki he faʻahinga tangata ʻoku nau fakafolau atú; Naʻe kei maʻu ʻe Daggoo ʻene ngaahi ʻulungaanga lelei faka-barbary kotoa pē, pea tuʻu hake ʻo hangē ha silafá, ʻo ne ngaʻunu holo ʻi he ngaahi funga vaká ʻi he fakaʻofoʻofa kotoa ʻo e fute ʻe ono nima ʻi hono soté. Naʻe ʻi ai ha loto fakatōkilalo fakaesino ʻi he sio hake kiate iá; pea ko ha tangata hinehina naʻe tuʻu ʻi hono ʻaó naʻe hangē ha fuka hinehina naʻe haʻu ke kole fakanonga ʻo ha kolotau. ʻI heʻene fie ʻilo ke tala, ko e negro fakaʻemipolá ni, ko ʻAhasivelo Tako, ko e Squire ʻo e kiʻi Flask, ʻa ia naʻe hangē ha tangata siasi ʻi hono tafaʻakí. Ko e me'a ki he toenga 'o e kautaha 'a e Pequod, ke pehe, 'i he 'aho ni 'oku 'ikai ha taha 'i he toko ua 'o e kau tangata 'e toko lauiafe kimu'a 'i he pou 'oku ngaue 'i he toutai tafola 'Amelika, ko e kau 'Amelika 'oku fanau'i mai, neongo 'oku meimei ko e kau 'ofisa kotoa pe. ‘Oku tatau pe heni mo e toutai tafola ‘a ‘Amelika ‘o hange ko e kau tau ‘a ‘Amelika mo e kau tau tahi fakakautau mo fakatau, pea mo e ngaahi kau ‘enisinia na’e ngaue’aki ‘i hono langa ‘o e ngaahi Kanali mo e Ngaahi Hala Lelue ‘a ‘Amelika. Ko e meʻa tatau pē, ʻoku ou pehē, koeʻuhí he ʻi he ngaahi meʻa kotoa ko ʻení ʻoku tokonaki tauʻatāina ʻe he ʻAmelika tuʻufonuá ʻa e ʻutó, ko e toenga ʻo e māmaní ʻo hangē ko hono tokonaki nima-homo ʻa e ngaahi uouá. ‘Oku ‘ikai ko ha tokosi‘i ‘o e kau toutai tafola ko ení ‘oku nau kau ki he ‘Asoá, ‘a ia ‘oku fa‘a pa ki ai ‘a e kau toutai tafola Nantucket ‘oku nau ‘alu ki tu‘á ke fakalahi ‘enau kau ngāué mei he kau ngoue fefeka ‘o e ngaahi matātahi makamaka ko iá. ‘I he founga meimei tatau, ko e kau toutai tafola Greenland ‘oku nau folau atu mei Hull pe Lonitoni, ‘oku nau hū ki he ‘Otu Motu Setilani, ke ma‘u ‘a e fakakakato kakato ‘o ‘enau kau ngāué. ‘I he‘enau ‘alu atu ki ‘apí, ‘oku nau toe tuku hifo kinautolu ki ai. Ko e anga fēfē, ʻoku ʻikai ha tala, ka ʻoku hangē ʻoku ngaohi ʻe he kau ʻOtu Motú ʻa e kau tafola lelei tahá. Ko e meimei kau ʻOtu Motu kotoa pē kinautolu ʻi he Pequod, ko e kau ʻIsolato foki, ʻoku ou ui kinautolu ko e pehē, ʻo ʻikai ke u fakamoʻoniʻi ʻa e konitinēniti angamaheni ʻo e tangatá, ka ʻoku nofo ʻa e kau ʻIsolato takitaha ʻi ha konitinēniti mavahe ʻoʻona. Ka ko eni, ‘i he‘enau fakatahataha‘i ‘i he fo‘i ‘aofi ‘e taha, ko ha seti lahi ē ko e ngaahi ‘Isolato ko ení! Ko ha fakafofonga Anacharsis Clootz mei he ngaahi motu kotoa pe 'o e tahi, mo e ngaahi ngata'anga kotoa pe 'o mamani, 'o 'alu fakataha mo Motu'a 'Ehapi 'i he Pequod ke fokotu'u 'a e ngaahi tangi 'a mamani 'i he 'ao 'o e pa ko ia 'oku 'ikai fu'u tokolahi 'o kinautolu 'oku nau toe foki mai mei ai. Kiʻi Pipi ʻUliʻulí —naʻe ʻikai ke ne teitei fai ia —ʻoiauē, ʻikai! naʻá ne ʻalu muʻomuʻa. Tamasiʻi ʻAlapamā masiva! ʻI he ʻā ʻo e Pequod fakalilifú, te mou sio fuoloa kiate ia, ʻokú ne tā ʻene meʻalelé; prelusive 'o e taimi ta'engata, 'i he taimi na'e fekau'i atu ai, ki he kuata-deki lahi 'i 'olunga, na'e fekau'i ia ke ne taa'i 'i he kau 'angelo, pea taa'i 'ene timulini 'i he kololia; ui ko ha tokotaha manavahē heni, fakahikihikiʻi ko ha toʻa ʻi ai!





VAHE 28. Ko Ehapi.

ʻI ha ngaahi ʻaho lahi hili ʻene mavahe mei Nantucket, naʻe ʻikai ke sio ki ha meʻa ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he ngaahi ʻaá ʻo ʻEikitau ʻĒhapí. Na‘e fefakanonga‘aki ma‘u pē ‘a e ongo hoá ‘i he ngaahi le‘ó, pea ‘i ha me‘a pē na‘e lava ke sio ki ai ‘o kehe, na‘e hā ngali ko kinaua pē ‘a e ongo ‘eikitau ‘o e vaká; ko kinautolu pē naʻa nau tukuange mai he taimi ʻe niʻihi mei he kāpiní ʻaki ha ngaahi fekau fakafokifā mo fakamālohi, ʻa ia hili ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē naʻe mahino pē naʻa nau fekau fakafofonga pē. ‘Io, na‘e ‘i ai honau ‘eiki mā‘olunga tahá mo e pule fakatikitató, neongo na‘e ‘ikai ke sio ki ai ha mata ‘o a‘u mai ki he taimí ni ‘o ‘ikai faka‘atā ke ne hū atu ki he feitu‘u toputapu he taimí ni ‘o e fale nofo‘angá.

Ko e taimi kotoa pē naʻá ku ʻalu hake ai ki he funga vaká mei heʻeku ngaahi uati ʻi laló, naʻá ku sio fakamamaʻu leva ki mui ke fakaʻilongaʻi pe ʻoku ʻasi mai ha fofonga kehe; he ko ʻeku ʻuluaki hohaʻa taʻemahino ʻi heʻeku lave ki he ʻeikivaka taʻeʻiloá, ʻa ia ʻoku ʻi he feituʻu mavahe ʻo e tahí he taimí ni, naʻe meimei hoko ia ko ha fakahohaʻa. Naʻe fakalahi ngali kehe ʻeni ʻi ha ngaahi taimi ʻe he ngaahi taʻefeʻunga fakatēvolo ʻa ʻIlaisiā ʻoku fuopotopoto ʻoku toe hoko mai taʻefakaafeʻi mai kiate au, ʻaki ha ivi fakapulipuli naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo fakakaukau ki ai kimuʻa. Ka naʻe kovi ʻeku lava ʻo matuʻuaki kinautolú, ʻo hangē pē ko e ngaahi ʻulungaanga kehé naʻá ku meimei mateuteu ke malimali ʻi he ngaahi fakaʻofoʻofa fakaʻeiʻeiki ʻa e palōfita fakatuʻutāmaki ko ia ʻo e ngaahi taulangá. Ka ko e hā pē ha meʻa ʻo e manavasiʻi pe taʻefiemālie —ke ui ia ʻo pehē —ʻa ia naʻá ku ongoʻí, ka ʻi he taimi pē naʻá ku haʻu ai ke sio takai holo ʻiate au ʻi he vaká, naʻe hangē naʻe fakafepakiʻi ʻa e ngaahi fakamoʻoni kotoa pē ke fakaʻamua ʻa e ngaahi ongo peheé. He neongo ko e kau harpooneers, mo e sino lahi 'o e kau ngaue, ko ha seti 'oku lahi ange 'a e barbaric, hiteni, mo e motley 'i ha taha 'o e ngaahi kautaha vaka fakatau tame 'a ia na'e 'ai au 'e he'eku ngaahi a'usia kimu'a ke u maheni mo ia, ka na'a ku kei faka'uhinga'i 'eni —pea 'oku totonu ke faka'uhinga'i ia —ki he makehe fefeka 'o e wildvina 'a ia na'e 'i ai . li‘aki ‘o heka vakapuná. Ka naʻe tautautefito ki he tafaʻaki ʻo e kau ʻōfisa pule ʻe toko tolu ʻo e vaká, ʻa e ngaahi hoá, ʻa ia naʻe fakafuofuaʻi fakamālohi taha ke fakanonga ʻa e ngaahi veiveiua taʻelanu ko ʻení, pea fakatupu ʻa e falala mo e fiefia ʻi he ngaahi fakahā kotoa pē ʻo e folaú. Naʻe ʻikai lava ke maʻu vave ha kau ʻōfisa mo ha kau tangata tahi ʻe toko tolu lelei ange, ʻoku ngalingali ange, ʻoku nau takitaha ʻi hono founga kehekehé, pea ko kinautolu takitaha ko e kau ʻAmelika; ko ha tangata Nantucket, ko ha tangata Ngoue Vaine, ko ha tangata Keipi. Ko ʻeni, ko e Kilisimasi ia ʻi he taimi naʻe fana ai ʻa e vaká mei tuʻa ʻi hono taulangá, ki ha feituʻu naʻa mau maʻu ai ʻa e ʻea Polar ʻoku ʻuʻua, neongo ʻoku mau hola ʻi he taimi kotoa pē mei ai ki he fakatongá; pea ʻi he tikilī mo e miniti kotoa pē ʻo e latitūte naʻá ma folau aí, ʻo tuku māmālie atu ʻa e faʻahitaʻu momoko taʻeʻofa ko iá, mo hono ngaahi ʻea taʻe-kātakiʻi kotoa pē ʻi homau tuʻá. Ko e taha ia ʻo e ngaahi pongipongi siʻisiʻi ange ʻo e tukuhifo, ka ʻoku kei hina mo fakapoʻuli feʻunga ʻo e liliú, ʻi he taimi naʻe lele ai ʻa e vaká ʻi ha matangi totonu ʻi he vaí mo ha faʻahinga puna tautea mo e vave fakaloloma, ʻa ia ʻi heʻeku heka ki he funga vaká ʻi he ui ʻo e leʻo ʻo e forenoon shivers, ko ia ʻoku vave pē ʻi he taimi naʻá ku lele ai ʻi heʻeku . au. Naʻe mahulu hake ʻa e moʻoní ʻi he manavaheé; Naʻe tuʻu ʻa e ʻEikitau ko ʻĒhapí ʻi hono kuata-fungavaka.

Naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai ha fakaʻilonga ʻo ha mahaki fakaesino angamaheni fekauʻaki mo ia, pe ko haʻane moʻui mei ha taha. Naʻá ne hangē ha tangata kuo motuhia mei he siteikí, ʻi he taimi kuo ʻosi fakaʻauha lahi ai ʻe he afí ʻa e ngaahi kupu kotoa pē ʻo ʻikai ke ne keina kinautolu, pe toʻo ha konga ʻe taha mei honau mālohi motuʻa kuo fakamālohiʻí. Ko hono fōtunga māʻolunga mo fōlahi kotoa, naʻe hangē naʻe ngaohi ia ʻaki ʻa e palasa fefeká, pea naʻe fōtunga ʻi ha founga ʻoku ʻikai lava ke liliu, ʻo hangē ko e fōtunga ʻo Peseusi ʻa Selini. ʻI heʻene filo atu mei he lotolotonga ʻo hono louʻulu hinaá, pea hoko atu ʻo ʻalu hifo ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo hono fofonga mo hono kia kuo vela lanu engeengá, kae ʻoua kuo puli atu ʻi hono kofú, naʻá ke sio ki ha fakaʻilonga fuopotopoto hangē ha vaʻakaú, ʻoku hinehina ʻaupito. Naʻe hangē ia ko e tuitui fakahangatonu ko ia ʻoku fai ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻi he fuʻu ʻakau hangatonu mo māʻolunga ʻo ha fuʻu ʻakau lahi, ʻi he taimi ʻoku haeʻi hifo ai ʻe he ʻuhila ʻi ʻolungá, pea ʻo ʻikai ke ne haeʻi ha vaʻa ʻe taha, ʻokú ne kiliʻi mo ʻauʻau ʻa e kili ʻo e ʻakaú mei ʻolunga ki lalo, ʻo ʻikai ke ne lele atu ki he kelekelé, ʻo tuku ʻa e ʻakaú ʻo kei lanu mata ka ʻoku kei moʻui. Pe naʻe fanauʻi mai ʻa e fakaʻilonga ko iá mo ia, pe ko e maʻulalo ia naʻe tuku ʻe ha lavea fakaʻauha, naʻe ʻikai lava ʻe ha taha ʻo tala pau. ‘I ha ngaahi fakangofua fakalongolongo, ‘i he kotoa ‘o e folaú na‘e si‘isi‘i pe ‘ikai ha lave ki ai, tautefito ki he ongo hoá. Ka ʻi ha taimi ʻe taha naʻe fakapapauʻi ai ʻe he seniale ʻo Tashtego, ko ha motuʻa ʻInitia Gay-Head ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e kau folaú, ʻo pehē naʻe ʻikai ke hoko ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻo fakaʻilongaʻi ʻa e founga ko iá kae ʻoua kuó ne taʻu fāngofulu kakato, pea naʻe toki hoko mai ia kiate ia, ʻo ʻikai ʻi he ʻita ʻo ha fepaki fakamatelie, ka ʻi ha fepaki fakaʻelemēniti ʻi tahi. Ka, naʻe ngali fakaʻikaiʻi fakaʻuhinga ʻa e fakaʻilonga vao ko ʻení, ʻi he meʻa naʻe fakaʻaiʻai ʻe ha tangata Manxman hina, ko ha tangata motuʻa faʻitoka, ʻa ia, ʻi heʻene teʻeki ai ke ne folau atu mei Nantucket kimuʻa, naʻe teʻeki ai ke ne sio kimuʻa ki he ʻĒhapi vao ko ʻení. Ka neongo ia, ko e ngaahi tukufakaholo motu‘a ‘o e tahí, ko e ngaahi tui ta‘efa‘alaua, na‘a nau ‘inivesi manakoa ‘a e motu‘a Manxman ko ení ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi mālohi fakae‘ilo‘ilo pau ki mu‘a ‘i he fakanatulá. Ko ia naʻe ʻikai ha kauvaka hinehina ʻe fakafepakiʻi fakamātoato ia ʻi heʻene pehē kapau ʻe ʻi ai ha taimi ʻe fakatoka fakanonga ai ʻa Kapiteni ʻĒhapi —ʻa ia mahalo ʻe ʻikai ke meimei hoko, ko ia naʻá ne lāunga —pea, ko hai pē te ne fai ʻa e lakanga fakaʻosi ko ia maʻá e kau pekiá, te ne maʻu ha fakaʻilonga fāʻeleʻi ʻiate ia mei he kalauní ki he vaʻé.

Naʻe fuʻu mālohi ʻaupito ʻa e uesia au ʻe he tafaʻaki fakalilifu kotoa ʻo ʻĒhapí, pea mo e fakaʻilonga livid ʻa ia naʻá ne fakaʻilongaʻi iá, ʻo ʻikai ke u fuʻu fakatokangaʻi ʻi he ʻuluaki ngaahi momeniti siʻí ʻoku ʻikai ko ha kiʻi konga siʻi ʻo e fakalilifu fakamālohi ko ʻení naʻe tupu ia mei he vaʻe hinehina faka-barbaric ʻa ia naʻá ne tuʻu ai ʻi ha konga. Naʻe ʻosi haʻu kiate au kimuʻa ko e vaʻe ʻaivoli ko ʻení naʻe ʻi tahí naʻe faʻu ia mei he hui fakangingila ʻo e kauʻā ʻo e tafola sperm. “ʻIo, naʻe fakaʻauha ia mei Siapani,” ko e lau ia ʻa e motuʻa ʻInitia Gay-Head ʻi ha taimi ʻe taha; “ka ʻo hangē ko ʻene vaka kuo maumaú, naʻá ne fakafolau atu ha pou ʻe taha ʻo ʻikai ke ne foki mai ki ʻapi ki ai. ʻOkú ne maʻu ha foʻi ʻuʻulu ʻo kinautolu.”

Naʻá ku ofo ʻi he tuʻunga fakafoʻituitui naʻá ne tauhi maʻú. ʻI he tafaʻaki takitaha ʻo e fungavaka kuata ʻo e Pequod, pea ʻoku fuʻu ofi ki he ngaahi ʻufiʻufi mizzen, naʻe ʻi ai ha ava auger, ʻoku bored ʻo meimei ʻinisi ʻe taha vaeua pe lahi ange, ki he laupapa. Naʻe tuʻu maʻu hono vaʻe huí ʻi he luo ko iá; ko e nima ʻe taha ʻoku hiki hake, pea ʻoku puke ʻe ha ʻufiʻufi; Naʻe tuʻu hangatonu ʻa e ʻEikitau ko ʻĒhapí, ʻo sio hangatonu ki tuʻa ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e ʻuluʻi vaka ʻoku ʻalu hake maʻu peé. Naʻe ʻi ai ha taʻefakangatangata ʻo e mālohi fefeka tahá, ha loto-fiemālie fakapapauʻi, taʻe-tukulolo, ʻi he fakatapui tuʻu maʻu mo taʻemanavahē, ki muʻa ʻo e sio ko iá. Naʻe ʻikai ha lea ʻe taha naʻá ne lea ʻaki; pea naʻe ʻikai foki ke lea ʻaki ʻe heʻene kau ʻōfisá ha meʻa kiate ia; neongo ʻi heʻenau ngaahi fakaʻilonga mo e ngaahi fakahāhā miniti siʻisiʻi tahá kotoa, naʻa nau fakahaaʻi mahino ʻa e ʻiloʻi taʻefiemālie, kapau ʻoku ʻikai ke fakamamahi, ʻo e ʻi he lalo mata ʻeiki ʻoku faingataʻaʻiá. Pea ʻikai ko ia pē, ka naʻe tuʻu ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi honau ʻaó mo ha kalusefai ʻi hono fofongá; ‘i he kotoa ‘o e ngaahi lāngilangi fakatu‘i ta‘ehingoa ‘o e ngaahi mala‘ia lahi ‘e ni‘ihi.

ʻI ha taimi fuoloa, mei heʻene fuofua ʻaʻahi ʻi he ʻeá, naʻá ne mavahe ki hono fale nofoʻangá. Ka ʻi he hili ʻa e pongipongi ko iá, naʻá ne ʻasi ʻi he ʻaho kotoa pē ki he kau ngāué; pe tuʻu ʻi hono pivot-hole, pe tangutu ʻi ha sea ʻaivoli naʻá ne maʻu; pe mamafa 'a e lue 'i he deck. ʻI he siʻisiʻi ange ʻa e fakapoʻuli ʻo e langí; moʻoni, kamata ke tupulaki ha kiʻi genial, naʻá ne hoko ʻo kei siʻisiʻi ange mo siʻisiʻi ange ʻene recluse; ʻo hangē, ʻi he taimi naʻe folau ai ʻa e vaká mei ʻapí, naʻe ʻikai ha meʻa kehe ka ko e fakapoʻuli mate ʻo e tahí ʻi he faʻahitaʻu momokó naʻá ne tauhi ia ke ne nofo mavahe pehē ʻi he taimi ko iá. Pea, naʻe faifai pea hoko ʻo pehē, naʻá ne meimei ʻi he ʻeá maʻu pē; ka, ʻi heʻene kei ʻi aí, ki he meʻa kotoa pē naʻá ne lea ʻakí, pe naʻá ne fai ʻi he ʻiloʻiló, ʻi he fungavaka laʻā fakaʻosí, naʻe ngali ʻikai fie maʻu ia ʻi ai ʻo hangē ha pou ʻe tahá. Ka naʻe toki fai pē ʻe he Pequod ha paasi he taimí ni; ‘ikai ke folau vakapuna ma‘u pē; meimei ko e ngaahi teuteu toutai tafola kotoa pē ʻoku fie maʻu ke tokangaʻi naʻe taukei kakato ki ai ʻa e ngaahi hoá, ko ia naʻe siʻisiʻi pe ʻikai ha meʻa, meiate ia, ke fakangāueʻi pe fakafiefiaʻi ʻa ʻĒhapi, he taimí ni; pea ko ia naʻe tuli atu, ʻi he vahaʻataimi ʻe taha ko iá, naʻe fakaputu ʻa e ngaahi ʻao naʻe laʻi ʻi he laʻi ʻi hono laʻé, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku fili ʻe he ngaahi ʻao kotoa pē ʻa e ngaahi tumutumu māʻolunga tahá ke nau fakaputu ai kinautolú.

Ka neongo ia, naʻe ʻikai fuoloa, ko e fakalotoʻi māfana mo fakaʻauʻauhifo ʻo e ʻea fakafiefia mo ʻaho mālōlō ne mau aʻu ki aí, naʻe hangē naʻá ne fakamānakoʻi māmālie ia mei heʻene ongoʻí. He, hangē ko e taimi ʻoku fononga ai ʻa e fānau fefine kulokula mo hulohulá, ʻa ʻEpeleli mo Mē, ki ʻapi ki he vaotā momoko mo taʻeʻofa ki he tangatá; na‘a mo e ‘oke motu‘a ‘oku ‘ikai ha me‘a ‘i aí, kovi tahá, mo manamana lahi tahá ‘e ‘ikai si‘i hifo ‘i he‘ene fekau‘i atu ha ngaahi fu‘u ‘akau lanu mata si‘isi‘i, ke talitali ‘aki ‘a e kau ‘a‘ahi loto fiefia peheé; ko ia naʻe fai ʻe ʻĒhapi, ʻi he fakaʻosí, ha kiʻi tali ki he ngaahi fakatauele vaʻinga ʻo e ʻea fakataʻahine ko iá. Naʻe laka hake ʻi he tuʻo tahá ʻene ʻomi ʻa e matalaʻiʻakau vaivai ʻo ha sio, ʻa ia, ʻi ha toe tangata kehe, naʻe mei vave pē ʻene matalaʻiʻakau ʻi ha malimali.
