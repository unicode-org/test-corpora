VAHE 74. Ko e 'Ulu 'o e Sperm Whale—Ko e Vakai Fakafehoanaki.

Ko eni, ko eni, ko e ongo tafola lalahi, ʻoku na fakatoka fakataha hona ʻulu; tau kau fakataha mo kinautolu, ʻo fokotuʻu fakataha ʻetau meʻa ʻatautolu.

‘I he hokohoko lahi ‘o e fanga folio leviathan, ko e Sperm Whale mo e Right Whale ‘oku mama‘o ‘aupito ‘a e ongo me‘a ‘oku fakatokanga‘i lahi tahá. Ko e fanga tafola pē kinautolu ‘oku tuli ma‘u pē ‘e he tangatá. Ki he kau Nantucketer, ‘oku nau ‘omai ‘a e ongo tafa‘aki faka‘auha ‘o e ngaahi fa‘ahinga kotoa ‘o e tafola ‘oku ‘iloá. Hange ko e kehekehe 'i tu'a 'i hona vaha'a 'oku tefito 'i he observable 'i honau 'ulu; pea ko e 'ulu 'o e takitaha 'oku tautau 'a e momeniti ko 'eni mei he tafa'aki 'o e Pequod; pea ʻi heʻetau lava ʻo ʻalu tauʻatāina mei he taha ki he taha, ʻaki ʻetau laka pē ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e funga vaká:—ko fē, ʻoku totonu ke u fie ʻilo, te ke maʻu ai ha faingamālie lelei ange ke ako ʻa e setolosia ʻaonga ʻi heni?

‘I he ‘uluaki tu‘ungá, ‘okú ke ofo ‘i he kehekehe fakalūkufua ‘i he vaha‘a ‘o e ngaahi ‘ulu ko ení. ‘Oku fakatou massive fe‘unga ‘i he konisēnisi kotoa pē; ka 'oku 'i ai ha symmetry fakafika pau 'i he Sperm Whale 'a ia 'oku fakaloloma 'a e 'ikai ke ma'u 'e he Right Whale. 'Oku lahi ange 'a e 'ulungaanga 'i he 'ulu 'o e Sperm Whale. ‘I ho‘o sio ki aí, ‘okú ke tukuange ta‘efakakaukau‘i ‘a e mā‘olunga lahi kiate iá, ‘i he tu‘unga ‘o e lāngilangi ‘oku mafola. ‘I he tu‘unga lolotongá foki, ‘oku fakalahi ‘a e lāngilangi ko ení ‘e he lanu pepa mo e māsima ‘o hono ‘ulú ‘i he tumutumú, ‘o ‘omai ai ‘a e faka‘ilonga ‘o e ta‘u motu‘a mo e a‘usia lahi. Ko hono fakanounoú, ko ia ‘a e me‘a ‘oku ui fakatekinikale ‘e he kau toutaí ko ha “tafola ‘ulu hina.”

Tau fakatokanga‘i he taimí ni ‘a e me‘a ‘oku si‘i taha ‘ene faikehekehe ‘i he ongo ‘ulu ko ení—‘a ia, ko e ongo ‘ōkani mahu‘inga tahá, ‘a e matá mo e telingá. ‘I he mama‘o ki mui ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘o e ‘ulú, pea ‘i lalo ki lalo, ofi ki he tuliki ‘o e taha ‘o e ongo tafola ko iá, kapau te ke fekumi fakapuliki, te ke faifai pea sio ki ha mata ‘oku ‘ikai ha‘ane lash, ‘a ia te ke fakakaukau ko ha mata ‘o ha ki‘i fo‘i pulu; ko ia mei he fakafehoanaki kotoa pē ʻoku ʻi he lahi ʻo e ʻulú.

Ko ʻeni, mei he tuʻunga tafaʻaki makehe ko ʻeni ʻo e mata ʻo e tafola, ʻoku mahino ʻoku ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo sio ki ha meʻa ʻoku ʻi muʻa tonu, ʻo ʻikai toe lahi ange ʻi heʻene lava ʻo sio ki ha meʻa ʻi muʻa tonu. ‘I ha fo‘i lea pē, ko e tu‘unga ‘o e mata ‘o e tafola ‘oku fehoanaki ia mo e telinga ‘o ha tangata; pea te ke fakakaukau nai, maʻau, pe ʻe anga fēfē ʻene hoko kiate koé, naʻá ke saveaʻi tafaʻaki ʻa e ngaahi meʻá ʻo fakafou ʻi ho telingá. Te ke ʻiloʻi te ke lava pē ʻo fekauʻi ha ngaahi tikilī ʻe tolungofulu nai ʻo e sió kimuʻa ʻi he tafaʻaki-laine hangatonu ʻo e sió; pea ko e toko tolungofulu nai ʻi mui ai. Kapau naʻe lue hangatonu atu ho fili mamahi tahá kiate koe, mo ha heletā kuo hiki hake ʻi he ʻaho lahi, he ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo sio kiate ia, ʻo hangē pē ko ʻene kaihaʻasi koe mei muí. ‘I ha fo‘i lea pē, te ke ma‘u ha tu‘a ‘e ua, ‘i he‘etau leá; ka, ‘i he taimi tatau, foki, ko e ongo mu‘a (mu‘a tafa‘aki): he ko e hā ‘a e me‘a ‘okú ne ngaohi ‘a e mu‘a ‘o ha tangata—ko e hā, mo‘oni, ka ko hono ongo matá?

‘Ikai ngata aí, lolotonga ia ‘i he lahi taha ‘o e fanga monumanu kehe ‘oku ou lava ‘o fakakaukau ki ai he taimí ni, ‘oku fu‘u tō ‘a e matá ‘o ‘ikai lava ke fakatokanga‘i ke fefiofi honau mālohi fakaefakakaukau, koe‘uhi ke ne fakatupu ha ‘ata ‘e taha kae ‘ikai ko e ua ki he ‘utó; ko e tu‘unga makehe ‘o e mata ‘o e tafola, ‘oku vahevahe ola lelei ‘o hangē ko kinautolú ‘e he ngaahi fute kiupiki lahi ‘o e ‘ulu fefeká, ‘a ia ‘oku taulanga ‘i hona vaha‘á ‘o hangē ha mo‘unga lahi ‘okú ne fakamavahe‘i ha ongo vaitupu ‘i he ngaahi tele‘á; ko e mo‘oni, kuo pau ke fakamavahe‘i kakato ‘e he me‘á ni ‘a e ngaahi ongo ‘oku ‘omai ‘e he ‘ōkani tau‘atāina taki taha. Ko ia ai, kuo pau ke sio ‘a e tafola ki ha ‘ata makehe ‘e taha ‘i he tafa‘aki ko ení, pea mo ha ‘ata makehe ‘e taha ‘i he tafa‘aki ko iá; lolotonga ia ko e meʻa kotoa pē ʻi he vahaʻá kuo pau ke fakapoʻuli loloto mo taʻeʻaonga kiate ia. Ko hono mo‘oní, ‘e pehē nai ‘oku sio atu ‘a e tangatá ki he māmaní mei ha puha le‘o ‘oku ‘i ai ha ongo no‘o ‘oku fakataha‘i ki hono matapaá. Ka ‘i he tafola, ‘oku fakahū kehekehe ‘a e ongo fo‘i sū ko ení, ‘o ‘ai ai ha ongo matapā-sio‘ata kehekehe, ka ‘oku fakaloloma ‘ene fakavaivai‘i ‘a e sió. Ko e me‘a makehe ko eni ‘o e mata ‘o e tafola ko ha me‘a ia ke manatu‘i ma‘u pē ‘i he toutaí; pea ke manatu‘i ia ‘e he tokotaha laú ‘i ha ngaahi fakatātā ‘e ni‘ihi ki mui aí.

‘E kamata nai ha fehu‘i fie‘ilo mo fakatupu puputu‘u taha fekau‘aki mo e me‘a faka‘ata ko ení ‘o hangē ko e lave ki he Līvaiataní. Ka kuo pau ke u fiemālie pē ʻi ha fakaʻilonga. Ko ia ʻi he lolotonga ʻoku ʻā ʻa e mata ʻo ha tangata ʻi he māmá, ʻoku ʻikai loto-fiemālie ʻa e ngāue ʻo e sió; ‘a ia, ‘oku ‘ikai leva ke ne lava ‘o tokoni fakamīsini ke sio ki ha fa‘ahinga me‘a pē ‘oku ‘i mu‘a ‘iate ia. Ka neongo ia, ʻe akoʻi ia ʻe he meʻa ʻoku aʻusia ʻe ha taha pē, neongo ʻe lava ke ne toʻo ha tafi taʻefakafaikehekeheʻi ʻo e ngaahi meʻá ʻi ha sio pē ʻe taha, ʻoku ʻikai ʻaupito malava ke ne, tokanga, mo kakato, ke ne sivisiviʻi ha ongo meʻa —neongo pe ko e hā hono lahi pe siʻisiʻí —ʻi ha taimi pē ʻe taha; never mind kapau 'oku na tokoto tafa'aki 'o fe'ofa'aki. Ka ʻo kapau te ke haʻu he taimí ni ke fakamavaheʻi ʻa e ongo meʻá ni, pea ʻātakaiʻi takitaha ʻaki ha siakale ʻo e fakapoʻuli loloto; pea, koeʻuhí ke ke sio ki ha taha ʻo kinaua, ʻi ha founga ʻe ʻomi ai ho ʻatamaí ke ne fuesia ia, ʻe fakaʻauha fakaʻaufuli ʻa e taha mei hoʻo ʻiloʻi ʻo e kuonga ko iá. ‘Oku fēfē leva ‘a e tafola? Mooli, ko e tau mata tokoua, ia lautolu ni, kua lata ke gahua he magaaho taha; ka ‘oku fu‘u lahi ange ‘a hono ‘utó, fakataha‘i, mo fakapulipuli ange ‘i he ‘utó ‘i he ‘utó, ‘o ne lava ai ‘i he momeniti tatau ‘o sivisivi‘i tokanga ‘a e ongo ‘amanaki kehekehe ‘e ua, ko e taha ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘e taha ‘o ia, pea ko e taha ‘i ha tafa‘aki fehangahangai tofu pē? Kapau te ne lava, pea ko ha meʻa fakaofo ia ʻiate ia, ʻo hangē naʻe lava ʻe ha tangata ʻo fou ʻi he taimi tatau ʻi he ngaahi fakaʻaliʻali ʻo e ongo palopalema kehekehe ʻia ʻIukilití. Pea, ‘i hono fakatotolo‘i pau, ‘oku ‘ikai ha ta‘efe‘unga ‘i he fakahoa ko ení.

Mahalo ko ha fakakaukau taʻeʻaonga pē ia, ka kuo ngali maʻu pē kiate au, ko e ngaahi vaʻinga makehe ʻo e ngaʻunu ʻoku fakahaaʻi ʻe ha fanga tafola ʻe niʻihi ʻi he taimi ʻoku ʻohofi ai kinautolu ʻe ha vaka ʻe tolu pe fā; ko e mā mo e mo‘ua ki he ngaahi manavahē fakaofo, ‘a ia ‘oku fu‘u anga-maheni‘aki ki he fanga tafola peheé; ʻOku ou fakakaukau ʻoku tupu taʻefakahangatonu ʻeni kotoa mei he puputuʻu taʻe-malava ʻo e loto-fiemālié, ʻa ia kuo pau ke fakakau kinautolu ʻe honau ngaahi mālohi ʻo e vīsoné ʻoku mavahevahe mo fehangahangai fakaʻaufuli.

Ka ʻoku fonu ʻa e telinga ʻo e tafola ʻi he fie ʻilo ʻo hangē pē ko e matá. Kapau ko ha muli kakato koe ki honau matakalí, te ke tulimanu nai ‘i he ongo ‘ulu ko ení ‘i ha ngaahi houa lahi, pea ‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito te ke ‘ilo‘i ‘a e ‘ōkani ko iá. Ko e telinga 'oku 'ikai ha lau 'i tu'a 'i ha me'a pe; pea ki he luó tonu ʻoku ʻikai ke ke meimei lava ʻo fakahū ha peni, ko ia ʻoku fakaofo ʻene miniti. ‘Oku ki‘i nofo ia ‘i mui ‘i he matá. ‘I he fekau‘aki mo honau telingá, ko e faikehekehe mahu‘inga ko ení ‘oku totonu ke fakatokanga‘i ia ‘i he vaha‘a ‘o e tafola sperm mo e to‘omata‘ú. Lolotonga ‘oku ‘i ai ha ava ‘i tu‘a ‘i he telinga ‘o e telinga ki mu‘á, ko e telinga ‘o e telinga ki muí ‘oku ‘ufi‘ufi kakato mo tatau ia ‘aki ha me‘a ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e me‘a ‘oku ‘i ai, ‘o ‘ikai ‘aupito lava ke ‘ilo‘i ia mei tu‘a.

ʻIkai ʻoku fakatupu fifili, ke sio ha meʻamoʻui lahi ʻo hangē ko e tafola ki he māmaní ʻi ha mata siʻisiʻi ʻaupito, pea fanongo ki he mana ʻi ha telinga ʻoku siʻisiʻi ange ʻi ha telinga ʻo ha lēpisi? Ka ʻo kapau naʻe fōlahi hono matá ʻo hangē ko e matasioʻata ʻo e telesikopu lahi ʻa Herschel; pea ko hono telingá ‘oku lahi ‘o hangē ko e ngaahi holisi ‘o e ngaahi falelotu lalahí; ‘e ‘ai nai ‘e he me‘a ko iá ke ne toe sio fuoloa ange, pe māsila ange ‘ene fanongó? ‘Ikai ‘aupito.—Ko e hā leva ‘okú ke feinga ai ke “fakalahi” ho ‘atamaí? Fakapulipuliʻi ia.

Tau tau leva he taimi ni mo ha fa'ahinga leva mo e ngaahi misini kohu 'oku tau ma'u 'i hotau nima, cant 'i he 'ulu 'o e sperm whale, koe'uhi ke ne tokoto 'i lalo ki 'olunga; pea, ʻi heʻene ʻalu hake ʻi ha lueʻanga ki he tumutumú, fai ha sio hifo ki he ngutú; pea kapau naʻe ʻikai ke mavahe ʻaupito ʻa e sinó mei ai he taimí ni, ʻaki ha maama mahalo te tau hifo ki he ʻAna lahi ʻo Kentucky Mammoth ʻo hono keté. Ka tau puke heni ʻi he nifo ko ʻení, pea sio takai holo ʻiate kitautolu ʻa e feituʻu ʻoku tau ʻi aí. Ko ha ngutu faka‘ofo‘ofa mo‘oni mo hā ma‘a! mei he falikí ki he silingí, ‘oku laine‘i, pe ‘oku lelei ange ke pehē ‘oku pepa‘i ‘aki ha me‘a hinehina ngingila, ‘o ngingila ‘o hangē ha ngaahi satin mali.

Kae haʻu ki tuʻa he taimí ni, ʻo vakai ki he kauʻā ʻi lalo fakaʻilonga ko ʻení, ʻa ia ʻoku hangē ko e ʻufiʻufi fāsiʻi lōloa ʻo ha puha snuff lahi ʻaupito, ʻoku ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻe taha ʻa e hingoá, kae ʻikai ko e tafaʻaki ʻe taha. Kapau te ke pry ia hake, koe'uhi ke ma'u ia 'i 'olunga, pea fakahaa'i hono ngaahi laine nifo, 'oku hange ko ha portcullis fakalilifu; pea ko e faʻahinga pehē, ʻoiauē! ‘okú ne fakamo‘oni‘i ki he tokolahi ko ha wight masiva ‘i he toutaí, ‘a ia ‘oku tō ki ai ‘a e ngaahi fo‘i ‘akau ko ení ‘aki ‘a e mālohi fakatu‘utāmaki. Ka ʻoku fakalilifu ange ʻaupito ʻa e sio, ʻi he taimi ʻoku fathoms hifo ai ʻi he tahí, ʻokú ke sio ki ha fanga tafola sulky, ʻoku nau ʻalu holo ʻi ai ʻo tautau, mo hono kauvae fakaofo, ʻoku fute ʻe hongofulu mā nima nai hono lōloá, ʻoku tautau hangatonu hifo ʻi he ngaahi tuliki totonu mo hono sinó, ki he māmaní kotoa ʻo hangē ha jib-boom ʻo ha vaká. ‘Oku ‘ikai ke mate ‘a e tafola ko ení; ʻokú ne loto-mamahi pē; mei he faʻahinga, mahalo; ko e hipokonitia; pea ʻoku fuʻu supine, ʻo mālōlō ai ʻa e ngaahi hingoa ʻo hono kaá, ʻo ne tuku ia ʻi ai ʻi he faʻahinga faingataʻaʻia taʻefakaʻatuʻi ko iá, ko ha fakaanga ki hono matakali kotoa, ʻa ia kuo pau, ʻoku ʻikai ha veiveiua, ke nau imprecate lock-jaws kiate ia.

‘I he ngaahi tu‘unga lahi tahá ko e kauvae ‘i lalo ko ení—‘i he faingofua hono vete ‘e ha tokotaha ‘ātisi ‘oku ‘osi ngāue‘akí—‘oku vete ia pea hiki hake ‘i he funga vaká ‘i he taumu‘a ke to‘o ‘a e ngaahi nifo ‘aivolí, pea ‘omai ha tokonaki ‘o e hui tafola hinehina fefeka ko iá ‘a ia ‘oku fa‘u‘aki ‘e he kau toutaí ‘a e ngaahi fa‘ahinga me‘a fakatupu ‘ilo kotoa pē, kau ai ‘a e ngaahi tokotoko, ngaahi fakamalu mo e ngaahi me‘a fakamalu.

‘I ha me‘a hiki lōloa mo vaivai ‘oku toho ai ‘a e kaá ‘i he vaká, ‘o hangē ha taulá; pea ʻi he hoko mai ʻa e taimi totonú —ʻi ha ngaahi ʻaho siʻi hili ʻa e ngāue ʻe tahá —ʻoku fokotuʻutuʻu ʻa Queequeg, Daggoo, mo Tashtego, ʻi heʻenau hoko kotoa ko ha kau toketā nifo taukeí, ke nau tā nifo. ‘I ha fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i-motu‘a māsila, ‘oku lances ai ‘e Queequeg ‘a e ngaahi nifó; pea ‘oku toki lavea hifo ‘a e kaá ki he ngaahi ringbolts, pea ‘i hono faka‘ai‘ai ha me‘angāue mei ‘olunga, ‘oku nau toho mai ‘a e ngaahi nifo ko ení, ‘o hangē ko hono toho ‘e he fanga pulu Misikení ‘a e ngaahi fu‘u ‘akau ‘oke motu‘a mei he ngaahi fonua ‘akau vaó. ʻOku fakalūkufua ʻoku ʻi ai ha nifo ʻe fāngofulu mā ua fakakātoa; ‘i he fanga tafola motu‘á, kuo lahi ‘enau ‘au‘auhá, ka ‘oku ‘ikai ke ‘au‘auha; pe fakafonu ‘o fakatatau ki he‘etau founga ngaohí. Ko e kauvae 'oku hili ia 'oku 'osi 'a e ngaahi laupapa, pea 'oku fakaputu 'o 'ave 'o hange ko e ngaahi joists ki hono langa 'o e ngaahi fale.





VAHE 75. Ko e 'Ulu 'o e Tafola To'omata'u—Ko e Vakai Fakafehoanaki.

Ko e kolosi ʻi he funga vaká, tau sio lōloa lelei he taimí ni ki he ʻulu ʻo e Tafola Toʻomataʻú.

Hangē ko ia ‘i he fōtunga fakalūkufuá ‘e lava ke fakahoa ‘a e ‘ulu ‘o e Sperm Whale faka‘ei‘eikí ki ha saliote tau ‘a Loma (tautautefito ki mu‘a, ‘a ia ‘oku fu‘u takai lahi ai); ko ia, ‘i ha vakai fakalūkufua, ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e ‘ulu ‘o e Tafola To‘omata‘ú ‘oku ‘i ai ‘ene faitatau ‘oku ‘ikai fu‘u faka‘ofo‘ofa mo ha sū lahi ‘oku ‘i ai hono va‘e galliot. ‘I he ta‘u ‘e uangeau kuohilí na‘e fakahoa ai ‘e ha motu‘a folau Hōlani hono fōtungá ki he fōtunga faka‘osi ‘o ha tokotaha ngaohi sū. Pea ʻi he fakaʻosi pe sū tatau pē ko ʻení, ʻe lava ke nofo fiemālie ʻaupito ai ʻa e finemotuʻa ko ia ʻo e talanoa nesilií, mo e fanga kiʻi ʻuhikiʻi manupuna ʻoku nau ʻuʻulu, ʻa ia mo hono hakó kotoa.

Ka ʻi hoʻo ofi ange ki he ʻulu lahi ko ʻení ʻoku kamata ke ne maʻu ha ngaahi tafaʻaki kehekehe, ʻo fakatatau ki hoʻo fakakaukaú. Kapau te ke tu'u 'i hono tumutumu 'o sio ki he ongo ava ko 'eni 'oku fōtunga F, te ke to'o 'a e 'ulu kotoa ki ha fu'u bass-viol lahi, pea mo e ngaahi spiracles ko 'eni, 'a e ngaahi ava 'i hono sounding-board. Pea, kapau te ke toe fakahangataha ho matá ki he fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i manu ngali kehe, ‘oku ‘i ai hono tumu‘aki, ‘o hangē ha selu ko ‘ení ‘i he tumutumu ‘o e fu‘u ‘akaú—ko e me‘a lanu mata ko eni ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e ‘uhiki‘i manupuna, ‘a ia ‘oku ui ‘e he kau Greenland ko e “kalauni,” pea ko e kau toutai ‘o e Tongá ko e “bonnet” ‘o e Tafola To‘omata‘ú; ‘i ho‘o sio pē ki he me‘á ni, te ke to‘o ‘a e ‘ulú ki he fu‘u ‘akau ‘o ha fu‘u ‘oke lahi, ‘oku ‘i ai ha ‘uhiki‘i manupuna ‘i hono kongalotó. Ko e hā pē ha tu‘unga, ‘i ho‘o siofi ‘a e fanga ki‘i pā mo‘ui ko ia ‘oku nau ‘uhiki‘i heni ‘i he pūlou ko ení, ‘e meimei pau ke hoko mai kiate koe ha fakakaukau pehē; tukukehe, ko e mo‘oni, kuo fakapapau‘i ho‘o faka‘ofo‘ofá ‘e he fo‘i lea fakatekinikale ko e “kalauni” kuo foaki foki ki ai; ‘i he tu‘unga ko iá te ke mahu‘inga‘ia lahi ‘i he fakakaukau ki he founga ‘oku hoko mo‘oni ai ‘a e fu‘u manu fekai mālohi ko ení ko ha tu‘i ‘o e tahí ‘oku ‘i ai hono pale, ‘a ia kuo fakatahataha‘i hono kalauni lanu mata ma‘ana ‘i he founga fakaofo ko ení. Ka 'o kapau ko e tafola ko 'eni ko ha tu'i, ko ha tokotaha sio sulky 'aupito ia ke faka'ofo'ofa'ia 'i ha diadem. Sio ki he loungutu lalo tautau ko iá! ko e fu'u sulk mo e pout lahi 'oku 'i ai! ko e sulk mo e pout, ʻi he fua ʻo e tufunga, ko e fute ʻe uofulu nai hono lōloá pea fute ʻe nima hono lolotó; ko ha sulk mo e pout ‘a ia te ne ‘oatu kiate koe ‘a e kāloni lolo ‘e 500 nai mo e lahi ange.

Ko e faka'ofa lahi, he taimi ni, ke hare-lipped 'a e tafola faka'ofa ko 'eni. Ko e moveteveté ʻoku fute ʻe taha nai hono fālahí. Mahalo pē naʻe folau hifo ʻa e faʻeé lolotonga ha vahaʻataimi mahuʻinga ʻi he matāfanga ʻo Peluú, ʻi he taimi naʻe fakatupu ai ʻe he ngaahi mofuiké ke ngangau ʻa e matātahí. ‘I he ‘olunga ‘o e loungutu ko ení, ‘o hangē ko e ‘i ha hū‘anga hekeheke, ‘oku tau hekeheke ai he taimí ni ki he ngutú. ʻI heʻeku leá naʻá ku ʻi Mackinaw, ʻoku totonu ke u toʻo ʻeni ko e loto ʻo ha wigwam ʻInitia.
