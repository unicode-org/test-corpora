Naʻe sio fakamamaʻu atu ʻa Fedallah ʻi he mata taʻeʻohovale, ʻo ne kolosi hono ongo nimá; ka naʻe feʻauʻauhi ʻa e kau folau kulokula taiká ʻi he ʻulu ʻo e taha mo e taha ke maʻu ʻa e muiʻi vakapuna fakaʻosí.

Pea ko ʻeni, lolotonga ʻa e puna ʻa e ongo gunwales ʻelasitikí ki loto mo tuʻa, ʻi he dallied ʻa e tafola mo e vakapuna ʻoku malaʻiá ʻi he founga fakatēvolo ko ʻení; pea mei heʻene ngoto hono sinó ʻi lalo ʻi he vaká, naʻe ʻikai lava ke leleʻi ia mei he ngaahi kaufaná, he naʻe meimei ʻi loto ʻa e ngaahi kaufaná ʻiate ia, ʻo hangē haʻane pehē; pea lolotonga e tuʻu taʻefakakaukauʻi ʻa e ngaahi vaka kehé, ʻo hangē ko ia kimuʻa ʻi ha faingataʻaʻia vave ʻoku ʻikai lava ke matuʻuaki, pea ko ʻĒhapi monomaniac ko iá, ʻi heʻene ʻita ʻi he ʻātakai fakatupu ʻohovale ko ʻeni ʻo hono filí, ʻa ia naʻá ne fokotuʻu ia ke ne moʻui kotoa mo taʻelavameʻa ʻi he ngaahi nifo tonu naʻá ne fehiʻa ki aí; ‘i he‘ene hoha‘a ‘i he me‘á ni kotoa, na‘á ne puke ‘a e hui lōloá ‘aki hono ongo nima telefuá, peá ne feinga fakamālohi ke to‘o ia mei hono ngāngá. ʻI heʻene feinga taʻeʻaonga peheé he taimí ni, naʻe hē ʻa e kaá meiate ia; naʻe punou ʻa e ngaahi gunwales vaivaí ki loto, holo, mo pā, ʻi he fakatou luelue atu ʻa e ongo kaá, ʻo hangē ha fuʻu kosi lahi ʻaupitó, ʻo na uʻu fakaʻaufuli ʻa e vaká ʻo ua, pea naʻá na toe lokaʻi vave kinaua ʻi he tahí, ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ongo vakapuna ʻoku ʻalu holo. Pea ko ia, ko e ngaahi loto pehe, neongo 'oku fakanounou 'i he takitaha mamahi; ka neongo ia, kapau ʻe tuʻutuʻuni ia ʻe he ngaahi ʻotuá, ʻi honau taimi moʻuí ʻoku nau fakatahatahaʻi ha kuonga kakato ʻo e malaʻiá, ʻoku faʻu kakato ʻaki ha ngaahi mālohi fakavavevave; he naʻa mo honau ngaahi senitā taʻeʻaongá, ʻoku ʻi he ngaahi natula fakaʻeiʻeiki ko iá ʻa e ngaahi takatakai kotoa ʻo e ngaahi laumālie māʻulalo angé.

“Tuku ia ʻi hoku ʻaó;—ko ha kau tangata ʻoku mole?”

“Taha, ua, tolu, fā, nima;—naʻe nima ʻa e ngaahi folau, tangataʻeiki, pea ko e kau tangata ʻeni ʻe toko nima.”

“Ko e me‘a lelei ia.—Tokoni mai kiate au, tangata; ‘oku ou faka‘amu ke tu‘u. Ko ia, ko ia, ‘oku ou sio kiate ia! ‘i ai! ‘i ai! ‘oku ‘alu ki he matangi ‘oku kei ‘i aí; ko e hā ha fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i manu puna!—To‘o ‘a e nimá meiate au! ‘Oku toe lele hake ‘a e vai ta‘engatá ‘i he ngaahi hui ‘o ‘Ēhapí!

‘Oku fa‘a hoko ia ‘i he taimi ‘oku sitofu ai ha vaka, ‘oku tokoni ‘a hono kau ngāué, ‘i hono ‘ave kinautolu ‘e ha vaka ‘e tahá, ke ngāue‘i ‘a e vaka hono ua ko iá; pea ‘oku hoko atu ai ‘a e tulimanú ‘aki ‘a e me‘a ‘oku ui ko e ngaahi folau ‘oku ‘i ai hono ongo ‘otu. Ka naʻe ʻikai tatau ʻa e mālohi ʻo e vaká ʻi he tānaki atu ʻo e mālohi ʻo e tafola, he naʻe hangē naʻá ne ʻosi fakaʻauʻauhifo ʻene ʻuhikiʻi manupuna kotoa pē; luelue ʻi ha vave ʻa ia naʻe fakahaaʻi mahino, kapau ʻi he taimí ni, ʻi he ngaahi tūkunga ko ʻení, ʻe tekeʻi atu, ʻe fakamoʻoniʻi ʻe he tulimanú ha lōloa taʻefakangatangata, kapau ʻoku ʻikai ko ha ʻamanaki taʻeʻaonga; pea na‘e ‘ikai foki ke lava ‘e ha kau ngāue ‘o kātaki‘i ‘i ha vaha‘a taimi lōloa pehē, ha fakamālohi‘i ta‘etuku mo lahi pehē ‘i he fo‘i ‘aá; ko ha meʻa ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu kātakiʻi pē ʻi ha ngaahi vicissitude nounou ʻe taha. Dʻye see him?” pea kapau ko e tali, ʻIkai, tangataʻeiki! naʻá ne fekauʻi leva kinautolu ke nau hiki hake ia ki hono nofoʻangá. ‘I he founga ko ení na‘e hokohoko atu ai ‘a e ‘ahó; Ko ‘Ēhapi, ‘oku ‘i ‘olunga he taimí ni pea ‘oku ‘ikai ke ne ngaue; anon, ʻo ne luelue taʻefiemālie ʻi he ngaahi laupapa.

Naʻe sio ʻa Situpi ʻokú ne kiʻi tuʻu; pea mahalo ʻi heʻene fakataumuʻa, ʻo ʻikai taʻeʻaonga, neongo ia, ke fakahaaʻi ʻene mālohi taʻefakasiʻisiʻí tonu, pea ke ne tauhi ai ha tuʻunga toʻa ʻi he ʻatamai ʻo ʻene ʻEikí, naʻá ne laka atu, pea ʻi heʻene sio ki he vakapuna naʻe maumaú naʻá ne kaila —“Ko e ʻakau talatala naʻe fakafisi ʻe he ʻasí; naʻá ne tuʻusi lahi hono ngutú, tangataʻeiki; ha! ha!”

“ʻIo, tangataʻeiki,” ko e lea ia ʻa Sitāpaki ʻi heʻene fakaofi maí, “ko ha meʻa fakaʻeiʻeiki ia; ko ha fakaʻilonga, mo ha meʻa kovi.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo sio ki he foʻi ʻuʻuú he taimí ni, tangataʻeiki;—fuʻu fakapoʻuli” —ko e kaila ia ʻa ha leʻo mei he ʻeá.

“ʻOku fēfē ʻa e ʻuluʻi ʻi he taimi naʻe sio fakamuimuitaha aí?”

“Hangē ko ia kimuʻá, tangataʻeiki, —hangatonu ki he matangi.”

“Lelei! te ne fononga māmālie ange he taimí ni 'i he pō ko ʻení. Hifo ʻa e ngaahi folau fakatuʻí mo e ngaahi folau fakaʻofoʻofa ʻo e tumutumu, Misa Sitāpaki. VAHE 134. Ko e Tuli—Aho Ua.

‘I he mālōlō ‘ahó, na‘e fakafoki taimi totonu ai ‘a e ngaahi ‘ulu-pou ‘e tolú.

“ʻOkú ke sio kiate ia?” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻĒhapí hili ʻene tuku ha kiʻi feituʻu ke mafola ai ʻa e māmá.

“ʻOua naʻá ke sio ki ha meʻa, tangataʻeiki.”

“Tafoki hake ʻa e nima kotoa pē pea folau! ʻokú ne fononga vave ange ʻi he meʻa ne u fakakaukau ki aí;—ʻa e ngaahi folau ʻoku ʻi ʻolunga-toʻá!—ʻio, naʻe totonu ke tauhi kinautolu ʻiate ia ʻi he pō kakató. Ka ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa —ʻoku mālōlō pē ia ki he fakavavevavé.”
