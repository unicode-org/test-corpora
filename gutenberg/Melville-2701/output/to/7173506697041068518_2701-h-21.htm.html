VAHE 111. Koe Pasifiki.

ʻI heʻemau lele ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e ngaahi motu ʻo Bashee naʻa mau faifai pea ʻasi hake ʻi he Tahi Tonga lahí; kapau naʻe ʻikai ha ngaahi meʻa kehe, naʻá ku mei lava ʻo fakafeʻiloaki hoku ʻofaʻanga Pasifikí ʻaki ha fakamālō taʻefaʻalaua, he ko ʻeni kuo tali ʻa e kole lōloa ʻo ʻeku kei talavoú; naʻe tafe fakahahake ʻa e ʻoseni nonga ko iá meiate au ʻi ha liiki ʻe taha afe ʻo e lanu pulū.

ʻOku ʻi ai, ʻoku ʻikai ʻilo ʻe ha taha pe ko e hā ʻa e misiteli melie fekauʻaki mo e tahí ni, ʻa ia ʻoku hangē ʻoku lea ʻene ngaahi ueʻi fakalilifu māmālié ki ha laumālie fufuu ʻi lalo; hangē ko e ngaahi peau talatupuʻa ko ia ʻo e kelekele ʻo ʻEfesoó ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he ʻEvangeliō ko St. Pea ʻoku fetaulaki mo ia, ʻi he ʻolunga ʻo e ngaahi kaikaiʻanga tahi ko ʻení, ngaahi vaotā vai ʻoku takai holo mo e Ngaahi Ngoueʻanga ʻo e Ngaahi Tufunga ʻUmeá ʻo e ngaahi konitinēniti ʻe faá kotoa, ʻe ʻalu hake mo tō hifo ʻa e ngaahi peaú, pea tō mo tafe taʻetuku; he ‘i heni, ko e ngaahi ‘ata mo e ngaahi ‘ata fefiofi ‘e laui miliona, ngaahi misi melemo, ngaahi somnambulism, ngaahi fakakaukau fakakaukau; ko e meʻa kotoa pē ʻoku tau ui ko e moʻuí mo e laumālié, ʻoku loi ʻo misi, misi, kei tuʻu pē; ‘oku nau luelue ‘o hangē ha kau mohe ‘i honau ngaahi mohengá; ko e ngaahi peau ‘oku tafe ma‘u pē ka na‘e ‘ai ia ‘e he‘enau ta‘efiemālié.

Ki ha taha pē ‘o e kau Magian rover fakalaulaulotó, ko e Pasifiki nonga ko ení, ‘i he taimi pē ‘oku sio ki aí, kuo pau ke hoko ia ‘o ta‘engata ko e tahi ‘o ‘ene ohí. ‘Okú ne fakatakataka‘i ‘a e ngaahi vai lotoloto taha ‘o e māmaní, ko e ‘oseni ‘Initiá mo e ‘Atalanitiká ko hono ongo nimá pē. Ko e ngaahi peau tatau pē ʻoku nau fufulu ʻa e ngaahi molé ʻo e ngaahi kolo ʻo Kalefōnia naʻe langa foʻoú, ka naʻe tō ʻaneafi ʻe he matakali tangata fakamuimui tahá, pea fufulu ʻa e ngaahi siketi ʻo e ngaahi fonua ʻĒsiá kuo ʻosi ʻosi ka ʻoku kei fakaʻofoʻofa, ʻo motuʻa ange ʻia ʻĒpalahame; lolotonga ia kotoa ‘i he vaha‘a ‘o e ngaahi motu ‘o e ‘aá ‘oku ‘alu holo, mo e ngaahi ‘Otu Motu ‘oku ma‘ulalo, ta‘e ngata, ta‘e‘iloa, mo e ngaahi Siapani ‘oku ‘ikai lava ke hū ki ai. Ko ia ‘oku sone‘i ‘e he Pasifiki fakamisiteli mo faka‘otua ko ení ‘a e konga lahi ‘o e māmaní kotoa ‘o fekau‘aki mo ia; ‘okú ne ‘ai ‘a e ngaahi matātahi kotoa pē ke taha ki ai; ‘oku hā ngali ko e loto ‘o e māmaní ‘oku tā-tafe. Hiki hake 'e he ngaahi fula ta'engata ko ia, 'Oku fie ma'u ke ke ma'u 'a e 'otua fakatauele, punou ho 'ulu kia Pan.

Ka ko e ngaahi fakakaukau siʻisiʻi ʻo Pan naʻá ne ueʻi ʻa e ʻuto ʻo ʻĒhapí, ʻo hangē ko ʻene tuʻu ʻo hangē ha fakatātā ukameá ʻi hono feituʻu angamaheni ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e mizen rigging, ʻi he ihu ʻe tahá naʻá ne snuffed taʻefakakaukauʻi ʻa e musk suka mei he ngaahi motu ʻo Bashee (ʻa ia ʻi hono ngaahi vaotā melié kuo pau ke ʻeveʻeva ʻa e kau ʻofaʻanga anga-malū ʻo e sehalta foʻoú), pea mo e taha ʻoku ʻiloʻi ʻa e manava foʻou ʻo e; ko e tahi ko ia ‘a ia kuo pau ke luelue ai ‘a e Tafola Hinehina fehi‘anekina‘í na‘a mo e taimi ko iá. ʻI hono fakapuna lōloa ʻi he ngaahi vai meimei fakaʻosi ko ʻení, pea lele atu ki he malaʻe folau ʻeveʻeva ʻa e kau Siapaní, naʻe fakautuutu ange ʻa e taumuʻa ʻa e motuʻá. Naʻe fetaulaki hono loungutu fefeká ʻo hangē ko e loungutu ʻo ha vice; naʻe fula ʻa e Delta ʻo e ngaahi veini ʻo hono laʻé ʻo hangē ha ngaahi vaitafe ʻoku ʻufiʻufi; ʻi heʻene mohé tonu, naʻe lele atu ʻene kalanga taʻanga ʻi he ʻaofinima ʻo e vakapuná, “Stern all! ʻoku puna ʻe he Tafola Hinehina ʻa e toto matolu!”





VAHE 112. Ko e Tufunga Ukamea.

Availing ia 'o e 'ea angavaivai, fa'ahita'u mafana-mokomoko 'oku pule he taimi ni 'i he ngaahi latitudes ko 'eni, pea 'i he teuteu ki he ngaahi tulituli peculiarly 'oku vave ke 'amanaki ki ai, Perth, 'a e begrimed, blistered motu'a tufunga ukamea, na'e 'ikai ke ne to'o 'ene forge to'oto'o ki ai 'a e pukepuke 'i he'ene kei tokoni, hili 'a e reclusion'. deck, vave hono lashed ki he ngaahi ringbolts 'e he pou mu'a; ʻi he meimei taʻetuku hono ui he taimí ni ʻe he kau ʻuluʻi ʻaá, mo e kau tangata fana, mo e kau fanafana ke nau fai ha kiʻi ngāue maʻanautolu; liliu, pe fakalelei‘i, pe fakafotunga fo‘ou ‘enau ngaahi me‘atau kehekehe mo e ngaahi nāunau ‘o e vaká. ʻI he taimi lahi ʻe ʻātakaiʻi ia ʻe ha siakale vēkeveke, ʻo tatali kotoa ke tauhi kinautolu; ‘o ne puke ‘a e ngaahi fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i vaka, ngaahi ‘ulu-paike, ngaahi fo‘i ‘akau, mo e ngaahi tao, pea siofi loto-mamahi ‘ene nga‘unu sooty kotoa pē, ‘i he‘ene ngāue mālohí. Ka neongo ia, ko e meʻa ʻa e motuʻá ni ko ha hāmala kātaki ʻoku ngāueʻaki ʻe ha nima kātaki. Naʻe ʻikai ha lāunga, ʻikai ha taʻekātaki, ʻikai ha fakaʻiseʻisa naʻe haʻu meiate ia. Fakalongolongo, tuai, mo fakaʻeiʻeiki; ʻi heʻene punou hifo ʻo toe lahi ange ʻi hono tuʻa naʻe motuʻa tuʻuloá, naʻá ne ngāue mālohi ʻo ʻalu, ʻo hangē ko e ngāué ko e moʻuí tonu, pea ko e tā mamafa ʻo ʻene hāmalá ko e tā mamafa ia ʻo hono lotó. Pea na‘e pehē pē.—Ko e me‘a faka‘ofa taha!

Ko ha lue makehe ʻa e motuʻa ko ʻení, ko ha kiʻi ʻasi ka ʻoku fakamamahi ʻene luelue ʻi heʻene lué, naʻá ne fakatupu ʻi ha vahaʻataimi kimuʻa ʻo e folaú ʻa e fie ʻilo ʻa e kau toutaí. Pea ki he fakamālohi ʻo ʻenau ngaahi fehuʻi fakaʻauhá naʻá ne faifai pea tukulolo; pea naʻe hoko ia ʻo ʻilo ʻe he tokotaha kotoa pē he taimí ni ʻa e talanoa fakamā ʻo hono ikuʻanga fakaʻofaʻiá.

ʻI heʻene tōmui, pea ʻikai halaia, ʻi ha vaeua poʻuli ʻe taha ʻo e faʻahitaʻu momokó, ʻi he hala ʻoku lele ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo ha ongo kolo ʻi he fonuá, naʻe ongoʻi ʻe he tufunga ukameá ʻi ha vaeua fakavalevale ʻa e kaihaʻasi ʻa e ngingila fakatupu maté ʻiate iá, peá ne kumi hūfanga ki ha fale tauhiʻanga ʻoku fakaʻauʻauhifo mo ʻauha. Ko e issue, ko e mole 'a e ngaahi ngata'anga 'o e ongo va'e. Mei he fakahā ko ʻení, ʻi ha konga ki he konga, naʻe faifai pea ʻasi mai ʻa e ngaahi ngāue ʻe fā ʻo e fiefiá, pea mo e ʻekitivitī nima ʻe taha ʻoku lōloa, pea ʻoku teʻeki ke fakatuʻutāmakí ʻo e loto mamahi ʻo e talanoa ʻo ʻene moʻuí.

Ko ha motuʻa ia, ʻa ia, ʻi hono taʻu meimei onongofulú, naʻá ne toloi ʻene fetaulaki mo e meʻa ko ia ʻi he ngaahi fakatekinikale ʻo e loto mamahí ʻoku ui ko e maumaú. Naʻá ne hoko ko ha tokotaha tufunga ʻo e lelei taha ʻiloa, pea naʻe lahi ʻene meʻa ke faí; naʻá ne maʻu ha fale mo ha ngoue; naʻá ne ʻuma ki ha uaifi kei talavou, hangē ha ʻofefiné, mo ʻofa, mo ha fānau ʻe toko tolu ʻoku nau fiefia mo kulokula; ʻi he Sāpate kotoa pē naʻá ne ʻalu ki ha falelotu ʻoku fōtunga fiefia, naʻe tō ʻi ha vaotā. Ka ʻi ha pō ʻe taha, ʻi he ʻufiʻufi ʻe he fakapoʻulí, pea toe fakapulipuliʻi ange ʻi ha fakapulipuli kākā tahá, naʻe hū fakavavevave atu ha kaihaʻa loto-moʻua ki hono ʻapi fiefiá, ʻo ne kaihaʻasi kotoa meiate kinautolu ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē. Pea ʻoku toe fakapoʻuli ange ke tala, ko e tangata ukameá tonu naʻá ne fakahoko taʻeʻilo ʻa e kaihaʻa ko ʻení ki he loto ʻo hono fāmilí. Ko e Fakaʻaiʻai ʻo e Hiná ia! ʻI hono fakaava ʻo e foʻi ʻakau fakatupu mate ko iá, naʻe lele atu ʻa e tēvoló, ʻo ne ngingila hake hono ʻapí. Ko ʻeni, ʻi ha ngaahi ʻuhinga fakapotopoto, fakapotopoto taha, mo fakaʻekonōmika, naʻe ʻi he lalo fale ʻo hono nofoʻangá ʻa e falekoloa ʻo e tufunga ukameá, ka naʻe ʻi ai ha hūʻanga mavahe ki ai; ko ia naʻe fanongo maʻu pē ʻa e uaifi moʻui lelei kei talavou mo ʻofá ʻo ʻikai ha manavasiʻi taʻefiefia, ka ʻi he fiefia mālohi, ki he tatangi mālohi ʻo e hāmala ʻa hono husepāniti motuʻa kei talavou nima-homó; ʻa ia ko hono ongoʻí, ʻa ia naʻe fakalongolongo ʻi heʻene fou atu ʻi he ngaahi falikí mo e ngaahi ʻā, naʻe haʻu kiate ia, ʻo ʻikai taʻemelie, ʻi hono nesilií; pea ko ia, ke fakafefekaʻi ʻa e hiva fakaʻaiʻai ukamea ʻa Leipa, naʻe lulululu ʻa e fanga kiʻi pēpē ʻa e tufunga ukameá ke nau mohe.

ʻOiauē, ʻoiauē ʻi he ʻoiauē! ʻOiauē, Mate, ko e hā ʻoku ʻikai ke ke lava ai ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻo taimi totonú? Kapau naʻá ke ʻave ʻa e tangata ukamea motuʻa ko ʻení kiate koe kimuʻa pea toki hoko mai ʻene maumau kakató kiate iá, pea naʻe mei maʻu leva ʻe he uitou kei talavoú ha loto mamahi fakafiefia, pea ko ʻene fānau paeá ko ha tamai fakaʻapaʻapaʻia moʻoni, talatupuʻa ke nau misi ki ai ʻi honau ngaahi taʻu hili iá; pea ko kinautolu kotoa ko ha taukei fakapoongi tokanga. Ka naʻe toʻo hifo ʻe Mate ha tokoua lahi angamaʻa, ʻa ia naʻe tautau pē ʻi heʻene ngāue fakaʻaho ʻoku ifi ʻa e ngaahi fatongia ʻo ha fāmili kehe, pea tuku ʻa e motuʻa kovi ange ʻi he taʻeʻaongá ke tuʻu, kae ʻoua kuo ʻai ʻe he pala fakalielia ʻo e moʻuí ke faingofua ange hono utu.

Ko e hā ke tala fakakātoa aí? Naʻe tupulaki ʻa e ngaahi taa ʻo e hāmala ʻi lalofonuá ʻi he ʻaho kotoa pē ʻo lahi ange ʻi he vahaʻá; pea naʻe tupulaki ʻa e tā takitaha ʻi he ʻaho kotoa pē ʻo vaivai ange ʻi he tā fakamuimui tahá; naʻe tangutu ʻaisi ʻa e uaifí ʻi he matapaá, mo ha mata ʻoku ʻikai ke loʻimataʻia, ʻo sio ngingila ki he fofonga tangi ʻo ʻene fānaú; naʻe tō ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻuhilá; naʻe lōmekina ʻa e fale ngaohiʻanga meʻalelé ʻaki ʻa e efuefu; naʻe fakatau atu ʻa e falé; naʻe lue hifo ʻa e faʻeé ki he mohuku lōloa ʻo e lotoʻā ʻo e siasí; naʻe muimui tuʻo ua ʻene fānaú ʻiate ia ki ai; pea naʻe tetetete ʻa e motuʻa taʻeʻapí, taʻeʻiai ha fāmilí mei ha vagabond ʻi he crape; ko ʻene malaʻia kotoa pē ʻoku ʻikai fakaʻapaʻapaʻi; hono 'ulu hina ko ha manuki ki he flaxen curls!

‘Oku hā ngali ko e mate pē ‘a e hokohoko ‘oku fakamānako ki ha ngāue hangē ko ‘ení; ka ko e Mate ko e launching pe ia ki he vahefonua 'o e Untried ngali kehe; ko e ʻuluaki fakafeʻiloaki pē ia ki he ngaahi meʻa ʻe lava ke hoko ʻi he fuʻu Mamaʻo lahi faú, ʻa e Vaó, ʻa e Vaí, ʻa e Taʻe-ʻUtá; ko ia ai, ki he mata faka‘amu mate ‘o e kau tangata peheé, ‘a ia ‘oku nau kei tuku pē ‘iate kinautolu ha ngaahi faka‘ise‘isa ‘i loto ‘o fakafepaki‘i ‘a e fakapoongi-kitá, ‘oku mafola fakamānako atu ai ‘e he ‘oseni ‘oku tokoni kotoa mo tali kotoa ‘a ‘ene tele‘a kotoa ‘o e ngaahi me‘a fakalilifu ta‘ealafakakaukau‘i, ‘oku to‘o, mo e ngaahi me‘a fakaofo, mo‘ui fo‘ou; pea mei he loto ʻo e kau Pasifiki taʻefakangatangatá, ʻoku hiva ʻa e kau fefine ʻo e tahí kiate kinautolu —“Haʻu ki heni, loto-mafesifesi; ko ha moʻui ʻeni ʻe taha ʻoku ʻikai ha halaia ʻo e mate fakavahaʻapuleʻangá; ko e ngaahi meʻa fakaofo ʻeni ʻoku mahulu hake ʻi he fakanatulá, ʻo ʻikai mate koeʻuhí ko kinautolu. Haʻu ki heni! tanu koe ʻi ha moʻui ʻa ia, ki hoʻomou māmani he taimí ni ʻoku lahi ange ʻa e fakaliliʻá ʻi he ab, mate, ha’u ki heni, ‘o fokotu’u foki ho maka fakamanatu, ‘i loto ‘i he loto’ā ‘o e falelotu, pea ha’u ki heni, kae ‘oua kuo mau mali mo koe!”

ʻI he fanongo ki he ngaahi leʻo ko ʻení, ʻa e Hahaké mo e Hihifó, ʻi he hopo vave ʻa e laʻaá, pea ʻi he tō ʻa e efiafí, naʻe tali ʻe he laumālie ʻo e tufunga ukameá, ʻIo, ʻoku ou haʻu! Pea ko ia naʻe ʻalu ʻa Perth ʻo a-whale.





VAHE 113. Ko e Fale Ngaohi.

Mo e 'ulu'ulu matted, pea swathed 'i ha bristling 'a e kili 'o e 'anga, 'i he vaeua'aho nai, na'e tu'u 'a Perth 'i he vaha'a 'o 'ene ngaue'anga mo e anvil, 'o tuku 'a e kimui ni 'i ha fu'u 'akau ukamea, mo e nima 'e taha 'oku ne puke ha 'ulu-paiki 'i he malala, pea mo e nima 'e taha 'i he ma'ama'a 'o hono ngaue'anga, 'i he taimi na'e ha'u ai 'a e Kapiteni ko 'Ehapi ha ki'i nima 'oku lele mai. Lolotonga ‘ene kei ki‘i mama‘o mei he fale ngaohi‘anga ukameá, na‘e ki‘i tu‘u ‘a ‘Ēhapi loto-mamahi; kae ʻoua kuo faifai pea toʻo ʻe Peti ʻene ukameá mei he afí, ʻo kamata ke ne tā ia ʻi he ʻanili —ko e fuʻu kulokula ʻo ne fekauʻi atu ʻa e ngaahi ʻuhilá ʻi ha ngaahi lele matolu ʻoku lele holo, ʻa ia ko e niʻihi ʻo kinautolu naʻe lele ofi kia ʻĒhapi.

“Ko e fanga moa ʻeni ʻa hoʻo Faʻē Kelí, Peati? ʻoku nau lele maʻu pē ʻi hoʻo ʻaá; fanga manupuna ʻo e fakaʻilonga leleí, foki, ka ʻoku ʻikai ki he tokotaha kotoa pē;—vakai heni, ʻoku nau vela; ka ko koe—ʻokú ke nofo ʻi honau lotolotongá ʻo ʻikai ha vela.”

“Koeʻuhí he ʻoku ou vela kotoa, ʻEikitau ʻĒhapi,” ko e tali ia ʻa Petí, ʻo ne mālōlō ʻi ha kiʻi taimi ʻi heʻene hāmalá; “Kuó u ʻosi ʻi he velá: ʻoku ʻikai faingofua ke ke vela ha maʻuʻanga tokoni.”

"Sai, sai; 'ikai toe. 'Oku ongo fu'u nonga ho le'o shrunk, sanily woeful kiate au. 'I ha Palataisi 'oku 'ikai ha'aku, 'Oku ou ta'efa'a kataki ki he mamahi kotoa pe 'i he ni'ihi kehe 'oku 'ikai ke vale. 'Oku totonu ke ke vale, tufunga ukamea; talamai, ko e ha 'oku 'ikai ke ke vale ai? 'E lava fēfē ke ke kataki'i 'a e ma 'oku 'ikai ke ke vale? ʻoku ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo vale?—Ko e hā naʻá ke fai ʻi aí?”

“Ko hono uelo ha ʻulu paike motuʻa, tangataʻeiki; naʻe ʻi ai ha ngaahi tuitui mo ha ngaahi ava ʻi ai.”

“Pea te ke lava ʻo toe ngaohi ia ke ngingila kotoa, tufunga ukamea, hili hono fakaʻaongaʻi fefeka ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe ʻi aí?”

“ʻOku ou fakakaukau pehē, tangataʻeiki.”

“Pea ʻoku ou mahalo te ke lava ʻo fakaleleiʻi ha meimei tuitui mo e ngaahi foʻi ava; ʻoua naʻa ke tokanga ki he fefeka ʻo e ukameá, tufunga ukameá?”

“ʻIo, tangataʻeiki, ʻoku ou pehē te u lava; ʻa e ngaahi tuitui kotoa pē mo e ngaahi ava ka ko e taha pē.”

“Ko ia, sio ki heni,” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻĒhapí, ʻo ne ʻunu atu ʻi he loto-māfana, mo fakafalala ʻaki hono ongo nimá ʻi he uma ʻo Pētí; “sio ki heni — heni —te mou lava nai ʻo fakaleleiʻi ha tuitui hangē ko ʻení, tufunga ukamea,” ʻo ne tafiʻi hono nima ʻe tahá ʻi hono laʻe ʻoku ʻi ai hono ngaahi hui; “kapau te ke lava, tufunga ukamea, fiefia feʻunga te u hili hoku ʻulú ki hoʻo ʻaní, pea ongoʻi hoʻo hāmala mamafa tahá ʻi hoku vahaʻa ʻo e ongo matá. Tali! ʻE lava nai ke ke fakamolemoleʻi ʻa e tuitui ko ʻení?”

"ʻOiauē! ko e taha ia, tangataʻeiki! Naʻá ku pehē ʻoku ʻikai ko e ngaahi tuitui mo e ngaahi foʻi ava kotoa pē ka ko e taha pē?”

“ʻIo, tufunga ukamea, ko e taha ia; ʻio, tangata, ʻoku ʻikai ke fakamoʻui ia; he neongo ʻoku ke sio pē ki ai ʻi heni ʻi hoku kakanó, ka kuo ngāue hifo ia ki he hui ʻo hoku ʻuluʻi ʻulú—ko e ngaahi fuopotopoto kotoa pē ia ! Ka, ʻave ʻa e vaʻinga ʻa e fānaú; ʻikai toe ʻi ai ha ngaahi foʻi ʻakau mo ha ngaahi foʻi ʻakau ʻi he ʻaho ní. Sio ki heni!” ‘o ngingila ‘a e kato kili‘imanu, ‘o hangē ‘oku fonu ‘i he ngaahi koini koulá. "Ko au foki, ʻoku ou fie maʻu ha foʻi ʻakau ke ngaohi; ko ha taha ʻe ʻikai lava ʻe ha ʻioke ʻe taha afe ʻo e fanga tēvoló ʻo mavahevahe, Peati; ko ha meʻa ʻe pipiki ʻi ha tafola ʻo hangē ko hono hui-ʻuluʻi ʻulú tonu. ʻOku ʻi ai ʻa e meʻá,” ʻo ne lī ʻa e kato ʻi he ʻanvil. “Sio, tufunga ukamea, ko e ngaahi foʻi faʻo ʻeni kuo tānaki ʻo e ngaahi sū ukamea ʻo e fanga hoosi lová.”

“Ngaahi foʻi sū hoosi, tangataʻeiki? Ko e hā, ʻEikitau ʻĒhapi, ʻokú ke maʻu heni, ko ia, ʻa e meʻa lelei taha mo fefeka taha kuo mau ngāueʻi ʻe kimautolu kau tufunga ukameá.”

"ʻOku ou ʻiloʻi ia, motuʻa; ʻe uelo fakataha ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻakau ko ʻení ʻo hangē ha kuluu mei he ngaahi hui vaia ʻo e kau fakapoó. Vave! ngaohi mai ʻa e foʻi ʻakaú. Pea ʻuluaki ngaohi mai kiate au, ha ngaahi vaʻa ʻe hongofulu mā ua ki hono vaʻé; pea matangi, mo mioʻi, pea hamalaʻi fakataha ʻa e hongofulu mā ua ko ʻení ʻo hangē ko e ngaahi filo mo e ngaahi filo ʻo ha laine toho.”

ʻI he faifai pea ngaohi ʻa e ngaahi vaʻakau ʻe hongofulu mā uá, naʻe ʻahiʻahiʻi kinautolu ʻe ʻĒhapi, ʻo takitaha, ʻaki ʻene takaiʻi kinautolu, ʻaki hono nimá tonu, ʻo takai ʻi ha foʻi ʻakau ukamea lōloa mo mamafa. “Ko ha mele!” ‘o fakafisinga‘i ‘a e fakamuimui tahá. “Toe ngāueʻi ʻa e meʻa ko iá, Peati.”

ʻI hono fai ʻení, naʻe teu ke kamata ke uelo ʻe Peati ʻa e toko hongofulu mā uá ke nau hoko ko e taha, ʻi he taimi naʻe taʻofi ai ʻe ʻĒhapi hono nimá, ʻo ne pehē te ne uelo ʻene ukamea pē ʻaʻaná. Ko ia, mo e hems tu'uma'u, gasping, na'a ne hammered 'i he anvil, Perth paasi kiate ia 'a e ngaahi va'a ulo, 'o taha hili 'a e taha, pea mo e forge lomi'i fefeka 'o fana'i hake hono ulo hangatonu malohi, na'e paasi fakalongolongo 'a e Parsee, pea punou 'i hono 'ulu ki he afi, hange 'oku ne ui ha ngaahi fakamala'ia pe ha ngaahi bleils. Ka, ‘i he sio hake ‘a ‘Ēhapí, na‘á ne hē ki he tafa‘akí.

“Ko e hā ʻa e fuʻu lusifā ko ia ʻoku nau kalo holo ʻi aí?” ko e lāunga ia ʻa Situpi, ʻo ne sio atu mei he ʻaá. “Ko e Pālesi ko iá ‘oku namu afi ‘o hangē ha fuseé; pea ‘okú ne nanamu ki ai ‘iate ia pē, ‘o hangē ha pani efuefu ‘o ha musikete vela.”

Naʻe faifai pea maʻu ʻe he shank, ʻi ha vaʻa kakato ʻe taha, ʻa hono vela fakaʻosí; pea ʻi hono lī kotoa ia ʻe Peati, ke fakanongaʻi iá, ʻo ngingila ki he hina vai ofi maí, naʻe fana hake ʻa e kohu vela ki he fofonga punou ʻo ʻĒhapí.

“Te ke fakaʻilongaʻi au, Peati?” ʻo ngungu ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti ʻi he mamahí; “kuó u faʻu pē ʻeku ʻumea fakaʻilongaʻi pē ʻaʻakú, pea?”

“Lotu ki he ‘Otua, ‘oua ‘e pehē; ka ‘oku ou manavahē ki ha me‘a, ‘Eikitau ‘Ēhapi. ‘Ikai ko e fo‘i tau ko ení ki he Tafola Hinehina?”

“Mo e white fiend! Ka ko eni ki he ngaahi foʻi ʻakau; kuo pau ke ke ngaohi pē ʻe koe, tangata. Ko ʻeku ngaahi hele ʻeni —ko e lelei taha ʻo e ukamea; ʻi heni, pea ngaohi ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻakau ke māsila ʻo hangē ko e ʻuha tuitui ʻo e Tahi ʻAisi.”

ʻI ha kiʻi momeniti, naʻe siofi ʻe he motuʻa tufunga ukameá ʻa e ngaahi heletaá ʻo hangē ʻe ʻikai ke ne fakaʻaongaʻi kinautolú.

“ʻAve kinaua, tangata, ʻoku ʻikai ke u fie maʻu kinaua, he ʻoku ʻikai ke u tafi he taimí ni, kai, pe lotu kae ʻoua kuo —ka ʻi heni —ke ngāue!”

Naʻe fakaʻosi ʻaki hono faʻu ʻo hoko ko ha fōtunga ngahau, pea uelo ʻe Peati ki he ʻakaú, naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo tuhu ʻa e ukameá ki he ngataʻanga ʻo e ʻaioné; pea ʻi he teu ke ʻoange ʻe he tufunga ukameá ki he ngaahi ʻakaú honau vela fakaʻosí, kimuʻa peá ne fakamāfanaʻi kinautolú, naʻá ne tangi kia ʻĒhapi ke ne fokotuʻu ʻa e hina vaí ʻo ofi.

"ʻIkai, ʻikai —ʻikai ha vai ki he meʻa ko iá; ʻoku ou fie maʻu ia ʻo e ʻita mate moʻoní. ʻOhoi, ʻi ai! Tashtego, Queequeg, Daggoo! Ko e hā hoʻomou leá, kau hiteni! Te mou ʻomi kiate au ʻa e toto lahi ʻo hangē ko ia te ne ʻufiʻufi ʻa e foʻi ʻakau ko ʻení?" ʻo pukepuke ia ʻo māʻolunga. Naʻe tali mai ʻe ha fuʻu luelue fakapoʻuli, ʻIo. Naʻe fai ha ngaahi puncture ʻe tolu ʻi he kakano hiteni, pea naʻe toki fakaʻaiʻai leva ʻa e ngaahi foʻi nifo ʻo e Tafola Hinehina.

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u papitaiso ʻi he hingoa ʻo e ʻeikí, ka ʻi he hingoa ʻo e tēvoló!” ko e tangi faka‘ohovale ia ‘a ‘Ēhapí, ‘i hono keina vela ‘e he ‘aione kovi ‘a e toto ‘o e papitaisó.

Ko ʻeni, ʻi heʻene tānaki ʻa e ngaahi pou ʻoku toe ʻi laló, pea fili ha taha ʻo e ʻakau hikolí, ʻo kei ʻinivesi pē ʻe he kili ʻo e ʻakaú, naʻe fakafeʻungaʻi ʻe ʻĒhapi ʻa e ngataʻangá ki he sote ʻo e ukameá. Naʻe vete leva ha foʻi koini ʻo e laine toho foʻoú, pea ʻave ha ngaahi fathom ʻe niʻihi ʻo ia ki he windlass, pea fakaloloa atu ki ha fuʻu tenge lahi. ‘I he‘ene lomi‘i hono va‘é ki aí, kae ‘oua kuo ngingila ‘a e maeá ‘o hangē ha afo ha‘apé, peá ne punou vēkeveke leva ki ai, pea ‘ikai ke ne sio ki ha ngaahi ha‘i, na‘e kaila ‘a ‘Ēhapi, “Sai! pea ko eni ki he ngaahi puké.”

ʻI he ngataʻanga ʻe tahá naʻe ʻikai ke haʻi ʻa e maeá, pea ko e ngaahi filo mafola mavahevahé naʻe lalanga kotoa ia mo lalanga takai ʻi he sote ʻo e foʻi ʻaá; naʻe teke mālohi leva ʻa e pou ʻo ʻalu hake ki he soté; mei he ngataʻanga ʻi laló naʻe muimuiʻi ʻa e maeá ʻo vaeua ʻi he lōloa ʻo e pou, pea fakamaʻu fefeka ʻo pehē, ʻaki ha ngaahi mioʻi ʻo e afo. Ko e meʻa ko ʻení naʻe fai, pou, ukamea, mo e maea —hangē ko e Ngaahi Tuʻunga ʻe Tolú —naʻe kei ʻikai lava ke mavahevahe, pea naʻe ʻalu loto-mamahi ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻo ʻalu mo e meʻataú; ko e leʻo ʻo hono vaʻe ʻaivolí, mo e leʻo ʻo e pou hikolí, ʻo fakatou taʻanga ʻi he laupapa kotoa pē. Ka kimuʻa peá ne hū atu ki hono lokí, naʻe ongo mai ha leʻo maʻamaʻa, taʻefakanatula, vaeua fakakata, ka ʻoku fakaʻofa tahá. ʻOiauē, Pipi! hoʻo kata fakaʻofa, ko ho mata ʻoku ʻikai ke ngāue ka ʻoku ʻikai ke nonga; ko ho'o ngaahi mummeries ngali kehe kotoa pe 'oku 'ikai ta'e'uhinga na'e fefiofi mo e fakamamahi 'uli'uli 'o e vaka fakaloloma, pea manuki'i ia!
