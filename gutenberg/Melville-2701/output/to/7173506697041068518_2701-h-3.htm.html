VAHE 10. Ko e Kaume'a Bosom.

ʻI heʻeku foki ki he Spouter-Inn mei he Falelotú, ne u ʻilo ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa Queequeg ʻoku fuʻu tokotaha pē; ʻi heʻene mavahe mei he Falelotú kimuʻa ʻi he tāpuakí ʻi ha taimi. Naʻá ne tangutu ʻi ha funga tēpile ʻi muʻa he afí, ʻo ʻi hono vaʻé ʻi he ʻaiʻangakai ʻo e sitoú, pea ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá naʻá ne puke ofi ki hono fofongá ʻa ʻene kiʻi ʻaitoli negro ko iá; ‘o sio fakamama‘u ki hono fofongá, pea ‘i ha hele siaki ‘oku ne ngingila māmālie atu ‘i hono ihú, lolotonga iá ‘okú ne humu kiate ia pē ‘i he‘ene founga fakapanganí.

Ka ʻi heʻene fakalaveaʻi iá, naʻá ne fokotuʻu hake ʻa e ʻīmisí; pea ʻikai fuoloa, ʻi heʻene ʻalu ki he tēpilé, ʻo ne toʻo hake ha tohi lahi ʻi ai, pea ʻi hono tuku ia ʻi hono umá naʻá ne kamata lau ʻa e ngaahi peesí ʻi ha tuʻumaʻu fakakaukauʻi; ʻi he peesi hono nimangofulu kotoa pē —ʻo hangē ko ʻeku fakakaukau ki aí —ʻo tuʻu ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti, ʻo sio ʻataʻatā pē ʻi hono tafaʻakí, mo ʻoange ha lea ki ha ifi ʻo e ʻohovale ʻoku fuoloa hono toʻó. Te ne toe kamata leva ʻi he nimangofulu hono hokó; ʻo hangē ʻoku kamata ʻi he fika ʻuluakí ʻi he taimi takitaha, ʻo hangē ʻoku ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo lau ʻo laka hake ʻi he nimangofulú, pea naʻe toki maʻu fakataha ha tokolahi pehē ʻo e nimangofulú, naʻe fakatupu ʻene ofo ʻi he lahi ʻo e ngaahi peesí.

Naʻá ku tangutu ʻo siofi ia ʻi he mahuʻingaʻia lahi. Neongo naʻá ne anga-fakamamahi, pea naʻe maumauʻi fakalielia ʻene fofongá —ʻo ʻikai toe siʻi hifo ʻi heʻeku saiʻiá —naʻe kei ʻi ai pē ha meʻa ʻi hono fofongá ʻa ia naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke taʻefakafiemālie. He ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo fufū ʻa e laumālié. ʻI heʻene ngaahi tā-tatau taʻefakamāmaní kotoa, ne u fakakaukau ne u sio ki he ngaahi fakaʻilonga ʻo ha loto faitotonu faingofua; pea ʻi hono ongo mata lalahi mo lolotó, ʻuliʻuli vela mo loto-toʻá, naʻe ngali ʻi ai ha ngaahi fakaʻilonga ʻo ha laumālie ʻe loto-toʻa ki ha tēvolo ʻe taha afe. Pea tukukehe ʻa e ngaahi meʻá ni kotoa, naʻe ʻi ai ha faʻahinga ʻulungaanga māʻolunga fekauʻaki mo e Hitení, ʻa ia naʻa mo ʻene taʻefakaʻapaʻapá naʻe ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo fakaʻauha fakaʻaufuli. Naʻá ne hangē ha tangata naʻe teʻeki ai ke ne ngungu pea teʻeki ai ke ʻi ai haʻane moʻua. Pe naʻe pehē foki, ko hono tafi hono ʻulú, naʻe toho mai hono laʻé ʻi ha fakaʻofoʻofa tauʻatāina mo ngingila ange, pea hā ngali fakalahi ange ʻi he meʻa naʻe mei hokó, ʻe ʻikai te u loto-toʻa ke fakapapauʻi ʻeni; ka ko e pau ko hono 'ulu na'e phrenologically ko ha taha lelei 'aupito. Mahalo ʻe ngali fakakata, ka naʻá ne fakamanatu mai kiate au ʻa e ʻulu ʻo Seniale Uasingatoní, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku hā ʻi he ngaahi fakatātā manakoa ʻo ia. Naʻe ʻi ai ʻene slope retreating lōloa tatau ʻoku fakakalasi maʻu pē mei ʻolunga ʻi he ngaahi laʻé, ʻa ia naʻe fuʻu projecting tatau pē, ʻo hangē ha ongo promontories lōloa matolu ʻakau ʻi ʻolunga. Ko Queequeg ko Siaosi Uasingatoni naʻe fakatupulaki ʻi he kai tangatá.

Lolotonga ʻeku siakaleʻi ofi pehē iá, ʻo fakangalingali vaeua ʻi he lolotonga ko iá ʻoku ou sio ki tuʻa ki he afā mei he ʻaá, naʻe ʻikai ke ne teitei tokanga ki hoku ʻi aí, naʻe ʻikai ke ne teitei fakahohaʻasi ia ʻaki haʻane sio pē ʻe taha; ka naʻe hā ngali femoʻuekina kakato ʻi hono lau ʻo e ngaahi peesi ʻo e tohi fakaofó. ʻI heʻeku fakakaukau ki heʻemau mohe fakataha fakasōsiale ʻi he pō kimuʻá, pea tautautefito ki he nima ʻofa naʻá ku maʻu ʻoku lī mai kiate au ʻi heʻeku ʻā hake ʻi he pongipongí, ne u fakakaukau ʻoku fuʻu kehe ʻaupito ʻa e taʻetokanga ko ʻení. Ka ko e fanga manu fekaí ko e ngaahi meʻamoʻui ngali kehe; ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi ‘oku ‘ikai te ke ‘ilo‘i pau ‘a e founga ke to‘o ai kinautolú. ‘I he ‘uluaki taimí ‘oku nau ‘ova; ko ‘enau tānaki fakafo‘ituitui nonga ‘o e faingofuá ‘oku hā ngali ko ha poto faka-Sōkalate. Naʻá ku fakatokangaʻi foki naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke feohi ʻa Queequeg, pe ka naʻe siʻisiʻi ʻaupito, mo e kau toutai kehe ʻi he fale talitalí. Naʻe ʻikai ke ne fai ha fakalakalaka ʻi ha faʻahinga meʻa pē; na‘e hā ngali ‘ikai ha‘ane holi ke fakalahi ‘a e siakale ‘o hono ngaahi mahení. Naʻe taaʻi au ʻe he meʻá ni kotoa ko ha tokotaha mālohi; ka, ‘i he fakakaukau hono uá, na‘e ‘i ai ha me‘a na‘e meimei mā‘olunga ‘i ai. Naʻe ʻi ai ha tangata ʻeni ʻi ha maile ʻe uofulu afe nai mei ʻapi, ʻi he hala ʻo Keipi Honí, ʻa ia —ʻa ia ko e founga pē ia ʻe taha te ne lava ʻo aʻu ki aí —naʻe lī ia ki he lotolotonga ʻo e kakai ʻoku kehe kiate ia ʻo hangē pē ʻokú ne ʻi he palanite ko Siupitá; pea neongo ia naʻá ne ngali ʻi heʻene fiemālie kakató; tauhi ‘a e nonga lahi tahá; fiemālie pē ʻi hono feohi pē ʻaʻaná; ‘oku tatau ma‘u pē mo ia. Ko e mo‘oni ko ha ki‘i filōsofia lelei eni; neongo ʻoku ʻikai ha veiveiua naʻe teʻeki ai ke ne fanongo ʻoku ʻi ai ha meʻa pehē. Ka, mahalo, ke hoko ko ha kau filōsefa moʻoni, ʻoku ʻikai totonu ke tau ʻiloʻi ʻe kitautolu kakai matelié ʻa e moʻui peheé pe feinga pehē. Ko ia ‘i he‘eku fanongo pē ‘oku foaki ‘e ha tangata pehē pe pehē ia ‘a ia tonu ma‘á ha filōsefa, ‘oku ou faka‘osi‘aki, ‘o hangē ko e finemotu‘a dyspeptic, kuo pau pē na‘á ne “maumau‘i ‘ene digester.”

ʻI heʻeku tangutu ʻi ai ʻi he loki ko ia ʻoku tuēnoa he taimí ni; ko e afi ‘oku vela ma‘ulalo, ‘i he tu‘unga anga-malū ko iá ‘i he hili ‘ene ‘uluaki mālohí kuó ne fakamāfana‘i ‘a e ‘eá, ‘oku ulo pē leva ia ke sio ki ai; ʻoku fakatahataha mai ʻa e ngaahi ʻataʻatā ʻo e efiafí mo e ngaahi meʻa fakaʻofoʻofá ʻo takatakai ʻi he ngaahi ʻaá, ʻo sio fakamamaʻu mai kiate kimaua ko e ongo meʻa fakalongolongo mo nofo tokotaha; ko e matangi booming taʻe ʻi he ngaahi puna fakaʻeiʻeiki; Naʻe kamata ke u ongoʻi ha ngaahi ongo ngali kehe. Naʻá ku ongoʻi ha vaia ʻiate au. Naʻe ʻikai ke toe tafoki hoku loto moveteveté mo hoku nima valevalé ki he māmani ʻo e ulofí. Naʻe huhuʻi ia ʻe he manu fekai fakanonga ko ʻení. Naʻá ne tangutu ai, ʻo lea ʻaki ʻene taʻetokanga ʻaupito ha natula ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai ha ngaahi mālualoi fakasivilaise mo ha ngaahi kākā fakaʻulia. Naʻá ne vao; ko ha sioʻata ʻaupito ʻo e ngaahi sioʻata ke sio ki ai; ka neongo ia naʻe kamata ke u ongoʻi ʻoku tohoakiʻi fakamisiteli au kiate ia. Pea ko e ngaahi meʻa tatau pē ko ia naʻá ne mei tekeʻi ʻa e tokolahi taha ʻo e niʻihi kehé, ko e ngaahi mākiniti tonu ia naʻá ne tohoakiʻi au ʻi he founga ko iá. Te u ʻahiʻahiʻi ha kaungāmeʻa pangani, ko ʻeku fakakaukau ia, he kuo fakamoʻoniʻi ʻe he angaʻofa faka-Kalisitiané ka ko e anga fakaʻapaʻapa ʻoku ʻikai hano ʻuhinga. Naʻá ku toho hoku tangutuʻangá ʻo ofi kiate ia, peá u fai ha ngaahi fakaʻilonga mo ha ngaahi fakahinohino fakakaumeʻa, ʻo u fai hoku lelei tahá ke talanoa mo ia lolotonga iá. ‘I he kamatá na‘e si‘i ‘ene fakatokanga‘i ‘a e ngaahi fakalakalaka ko ení; ka ʻi he taimi ní, ʻi heʻeku lave ki heʻene talitali kakai ʻi he pō fakaʻosí, naʻá ne fakakaukau ke ʻeke mai pe te ma toe hoko ko ha ongo kaungā mohe. Naʻá ku talaange ʻio; whereat naʻá ku fakakaukau naʻá ne hā fiefia, mahalo naʻe kiʻi fakamālōʻiaʻi.

Naʻá ma fulihi fakataha leva ʻa e tohí, peá u feinga ke fakamatalaʻi ange kiate ia ʻa e taumuʻa ʻo hono pulusí, mo e ʻuhinga ʻo e ngaahi fakatātā siʻisiʻi naʻe ʻi aí. Ko ia na‘e vave ‘eku fakahoko ‘ene mahu‘inga‘iá; pea mei ai naʻa mau ʻalu ʻo jabbering ʻa e lelei taha te mau lavá fekauʻaki mo e ngaahi meʻa kehekehe ʻi tuʻa ke sio ki ai ʻi he kolo ʻiloa ko ʻení. Naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuó u fokotuʻu ha kohu fakasōsiale; pea, ʻi heʻene ʻomi ʻene kato mo e tomahawk, naʻá ne ʻomai fakalongolongo mai haʻaku foʻi pulu. Pea naʻá ma tangutu leva ʻo fefakatauʻaki ha ngaahi pupuha mei heʻene paipa vao ko iá, pea tauhi ia ke ne fakalaka maʻu pē ʻi homa vahaʻá.

Kapau naʻe kei ʻi ai ha ʻaisi ʻo e taʻetokanga kiate au ʻi he fatafata ʻo e Paganí, ko e kohu fakafiefia mo fakaʻofoʻofa ko ʻeni naʻá ma maʻú, naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuó ne fakamokomoko ia, pea tuku kimaua ko e ongo kaungāmeʻa. Naʻe hangē naʻá ne ʻave au ʻo fakanatula mo taʻefakakau ʻo hangē pē ko ʻeku ʻave iá; pea ʻi he ʻosi ʻema kohu, naʻá ne lomiʻi hono laʻé ki hoku laʻé, ʻo ne fusi au ʻi hoku kongalotó, peá ne pehē mai ʻo kamata mei heni kuó ma mali; ʻuhinga, ʻi he kupuʻi lea ʻa hono fonuá, ko e ongo kaungāmeʻa ʻo e fatafatá kimaua; te ne mate fiefia maʻaku, kapau ʻe fie maʻu. ‘I ha tangata fonua, na‘e mei ngali fu‘u vave ‘aupito ‘a e ulo fakafokifā ko eni ‘o e kaume‘á, ko ha me‘a ke fu‘u ta‘efalala ki ai; ka ʻi he savage faingofua ko ʻení he ʻikai ke ʻaonga ʻa e ngaahi tuʻutuʻuni motuʻa ko iá.

Hili ʻemau maʻu meʻatokoni efiafí, mo ha toe talanoa fakasōsiale mo ifi tapaka, naʻá ma ʻalu fakataha ki homa lokí. Naʻa ne ngaohi maʻaku ko e meʻaʻofa hono ʻulu kuo ʻosi fakaʻauha; naʻá ne toʻo mai ʻene fuʻu kato paʻanga tapaká, pea ʻi heʻene tataʻo ʻi lalo he tapaká, naʻá ne toʻo mai ha paʻanga siliva ʻe tolungofulu nai; peá ne folahi leva kinaua ʻi he funga tēpilé, pea vahevahe fakamīsini kinaua ki ha ongo konga tatau, ʻo ne tekeʻi mai ha taha ʻo kinaua kiate au, ʻo ne pehē ko ʻeku ia. Naʻá ku ʻalu ke fakaangaʻi; ka naʻá ne fakalongolongoʻi au ʻaki ʻene lilingi kinautolu ki he kato ʻo hoku talausese. Naʻá ku tuku ke nau nofo. Naʻá ne ʻalu leva ʻo fai ʻene ngaahi lotu efiafí, ʻo toʻo mai ʻene ʻaitoli, pea toʻo ʻa e laupapa afi pepá. ʻI ha ngaahi fakaʻilonga mo ha ngaahi fakaʻilonga pau, ne u fakakaukau naʻá ne ngali hohaʻa ke u kau fakataha mo ia; ka ʻi heʻeku ʻiloʻi lelei ʻa e meʻa ʻe hokó, ne u fakakaukauʻi ha kiʻi taimi pe, kapau te ne fakaafeʻi au, te u talangofua pe ʻikai.

Ko ha Kalisitiane lelei au; naʻe fāʻeleʻi mo fakatupu ʻi he fatafata ʻo e Siasi Pelesipitiliane taʻehalaia. ‘E anga-fēfē leva ha‘aku fā‘ūtaha mo e tokotaha tauhi ‘aitoli vao ko ‘ení ‘i he lotu ki he‘ene konga ‘akaú? Ka ko e hā ‘a e lotú? thought I. ʻOkú ke pehē he taimí ni, ʻIsimeʻeli, ʻe lava ke meheka nai ʻa e ʻOtua lahi ʻo e langí mo e māmaní —kau ai ʻa e kau hitení mo e kakai kotoa pē —ki ha konga ʻakau ʻuliʻuli taʻe mahuʻinga? Ta'emalava! Ka ko e hā ‘a e lotu?—ke fai ‘a e finangalo ‘o e ‘Otuá— ko e lotu ia. Pea ko e hā ʻa e finangalo ʻo e ʻOtuá?—ke fai ki hoku kaungā tangatá ʻa e meʻa te u loto ke fai mai ʻe hoku kaungā tangatá kiate aú— ko e finangalo ia ʻo e ʻOtuá. Ko ʻeni, ko Queequeg ʻa hoku kaungā tangatá. Pea ko e hā ʻoku ou fakaʻamu ke fai ʻe he Queequeg ko ʻení kiate aú? Ko e hā, fakatahataha mai mo au ʻi heʻeku founga lotu faka-Pelesipitiliane makehé. Ko hono olá, kuo pau leva ke u fakatahataha mo ia ʻi heʻene; ergo, kuo pau ke u liliu ʻo tauhi ʻaitoli. Ko ia naʻa ku tutu ʻae ngaahi ʻakau tafi; tokoni ke poupouʻi ʻa e kiʻi ʻaitoli taʻehalaia; naʻá ne ʻoange kiate ia ha pisikete vela mo e Queequeg; salamed ʻi hono ʻaó tuʻo ua pe tuʻo tolu; ʻuma ki hono ihú; pea ʻi he ʻosi iá, naʻá ma vete homa valá ʻo mohe, ʻi he melino mo homa konisēnisí tonu mo e māmaní kotoa. Ka naʻe ʻikai ke ma mohe taʻe ʻi ai ha kiʻi talanoa.

ʻOku ʻikai te u ʻilo pe ʻoku fēfē; ka ʻoku ʻikai ha feituʻu ʻe tatau mo ha mohenga ki he ngaahi fakahā fakapulipuli ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ngaahi kaungāmeʻá. ʻOku pehē ʻe he tangatá mo e uaifí, ʻoku fakaava ai ʻa e lalo ʻo hona laumālié kiate kinaua; pea 'oku fa'a loi mo talanoa 'a e ngaahi hoa motu'a 'e ni'ihi 'i he ngaahi taimi motu'a 'o a'u ki he ofi ki he pongipongi. Ko ia ai, ‘i he māhina mali ‘o homa lotó, na‘á ku tokoto ai mo Queequeg—ko ha ongo me‘a mali fakafiemālie mo ‘ofa.





VAHE 11. Ko e kofu po'uli.

Naʻá ma tokoto pehē ʻi hoku mohengá, ʻo talanoa mo mohe ʻi ha ngaahi vahaʻataimi nounou, pea naʻe lī ʻe Kuiki ʻi he taimi ko iá ʻi he ʻofa hono ongo vaʻe tā lanu engeengá ʻi hoku vaʻé, peá ne toki toho mai kinaua ki mui; ko ia naʻá ma feohi kakato mo tauʻatāina mo faingofua; ʻi he taimi, naʻe faifai pea, koeʻuhí ko ʻemau ngaahi fetalanoaʻakí, naʻe mavahe fakaʻaufuli ʻa e kiʻi nappishness naʻe kei ʻiate kimauá, pea naʻá ma ongoʻi ke ma toe tuʻu hake, neongo naʻe kei ʻi ai pē ʻa e mālōlō ʻaho ʻi he kahaʻú.

‘Io, na‘a mau hoko ‘o fu‘u ‘ā‘ā; ʻo kamata ke fakaʻau ke vaivai homau tuʻunga tokoto ʻi aí, pea naʻe faifai pea mau ʻiloʻi ʻoku mau tangutu hake; naʻe fakapipiki lelei ʻa e ngaahi kofú ʻo takatakai ʻiate kimaua, ʻo fakafalala ki he ʻulu-papa ʻo toho ofi mai homa ongo tuí ʻe faá, pea punou homa ongo ihú ʻi ʻolunga ai, ʻo hangē ko homa ongo tuí ko ha ngaahi pani fakamāfana. Naʻá ma ongoʻi lelei mo fakafiemālie ʻaupito, ʻo toe lahi ange ia he naʻe fuʻu momoko ʻaupito ʻi tuʻa; moʻoni mei he ngaahi kofu mohe foki, ʻi heʻene vakai naʻe ʻikai ha afi ʻi he lokí. Ko e lahi angé, ʻoku ou pehē, koeʻuhí he ke fiefia moʻoni ʻi he māfana ʻo e sinó, kuo pau ke momoko ha kiʻi konga ʻo koe, he ʻoku ʻikai ha ʻulungaanga ʻi he māmani ko ʻení ʻoku ʻikai ko e meʻa ia ʻoku ʻi aí ʻi he fakafehoanaki pē. ʻOku ʻikai ha meʻa ʻe ʻi ai ʻiate ia pē. Kapau te ke faka'ai'ai koe 'oku ke fiemalie kotoa, pea kuo fuoloa pehe, pea 'e 'ikai leva ke lava 'o pehe 'oku ke toe fiemalie. Ka ʻo kapau, hangē ko au mo Queequeg ʻi he mohengá, ʻoku kiʻi momoko ʻa e tumuʻaki ʻo ho ihú pe ko e kalauni ʻo ho ʻulú, ko e hā leva, moʻoni, ʻi he ʻatamai fakalūkufuá ʻokú ke ongoʻi fakafiefia taha mo māfana taʻealafakaʻikaiʻi aí. Ko e ‘uhinga eni ‘oku ‘ikai totonu ke ‘aupito fakanaunau‘i ai ha fale mohe ‘aki ha afi, ‘a ia ko e taha ia ‘o e ngaahi ta‘efiemālie faka‘ofo‘ofa ‘a e kau koloa‘iá. He ko e ma'olunga 'o e fa'ahinga deliciousness ko 'eni ko e 'ikai ha me'a ka ko e palanikete 'i ho vaha'a mo ho'o snugness mo e momoko 'o e 'ea 'i tu'a. Pea ‘i ai ‘okú ke tokoto ‘o hangē ko e ulo māfana ‘e taha ‘i he loto ‘o ha kilisitala ‘ātika.

Naʻá ma tangutu ʻi he founga punou ko ʻení ʻi ha taimi, ʻi he taimi pē ko iá ne u fakakaukau te u fakaava hoku matá; he ʻi he taimi ʻoku ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e ngaahi laʻipepá, ʻi he ʻahó pe ʻi he poʻulí, pea ʻoku ou mohe pe ʻā, ʻoku ʻi ai ʻeku founga ke tauhi maʻu pē hoku matá ke moʻui, koeʻuhí ke lahi ange ʻeku fakatefito ʻa e fiemālie ʻo e ʻi he mohengá. He ʻoku ʻikai ha tangata te ne lava ʻo ongoʻi totonu hono ʻulungāngá tonu tukukehe kapau ʻe moʻui hono matá; hangē ko e fakapoʻulí ʻa e ʻelemēniti totonu moʻoni ʻo ʻetau ngaahi ʻulungāngá, neongo ʻoku fakaʻofoʻofa ange ʻa e māmá ki hotau konga ʻumeá. ʻI heʻeku fakaava hoku matá ʻi he taimi ko iá, pea ʻalu atu mei hoku fakapoʻuli fakafiefia mo fakatupu pē ʻe au ki he fakapoʻuli ʻi tuʻa ʻoku fakamālohiʻi mo fefeka ʻo e taimi hongofulu mā ua ʻo e poʻulí ʻoku ʻikai ke fakamaamaá, ne u aʻusia ha fakaliliʻa taʻefakafiemālie. Naʻe ʻikai foki ke u teitei fakafepakiʻi ʻa e fakahinohino meia Queequeg mahalo ʻoku lelei taha ke taaʻi ha maama, ʻi heʻemau sio ʻoku mau fuʻu ʻā lahi; pea ʻikai ngata aí naʻá ne ongoʻi ha holi mālohi ke maʻu ha ngaahi puhi fakalongolongo siʻisiʻi mei heʻene Tomahawk. Ke pehē pē, neongo naʻá ku ongoʻi ha fakaliliʻa mālohi pehē ki heʻene ifi ʻi he mohengá ʻi he pō kimuʻá, ka ke vakai ki he tupulaki ʻa e ʻelasitiki ʻo ʻemau ngaahi fakakaukau fakaanga fefeká ʻi he taimi ʻoku haʻu ai ʻa e ʻofá ʻi ha taimi ʻe taha ke punouʻi kinautolú. He ko ʻeni naʻe ʻikai ha meʻa te u saiʻia ange ai ʻi heʻeku ifi ʻa Queequeg ʻi hoku tafaʻakí, naʻa mo hono mohengá, koeʻuhí he naʻe hangē naʻá ne fonu ʻi he fiefia fakaʻapi nonga pehē ʻi he taimi ko iá. Naʻe ʻikai ke u toe ongoʻi hohaʻa taʻetotonu ki he tuʻutuʻuni maluʻi ʻa e ʻeiki ʻapí. Naʻá ku moʻui pē ki he fiemālie fakapulipuli fakanounou ʻo e vahevahe ha paipa mo ha palanikete mo ha kaungāmeʻa moʻoní. ʻI hono toho homau ngaahi kote fulufuluá ʻi homa umá, naʻá ma fakalaka atu he taimí ni ʻi he Tomahawk mei he taha ki he taha, kae ʻoua kuo tupu māmālie hake ʻiate kimaua ha meʻa sivi kohu tautau lanu pulū, ʻo fakamaama ʻe he ulo ʻo e maama naʻe tutu foʻoú.

Pe naʻe fakatakatakaʻi ʻe he tokotaha sivi ʻoku ʻalu holo ko ʻení ʻa e manu fekaí ki ha ngaahi sioʻata mamaʻo mamaʻo, ʻoku ʻikai ke u ʻilo, ka naʻá ne lea he taimí ni ʻo kau ki hono motu tupuʻangá; pea ʻi heʻeku vēkeveke ke fanongo ki hono hisitōliá, ne u kole ange ke ne hoko atu ʻo talamai ia. Naʻá ne talangofua fiefia ki ai. Neongo ʻi he taimi ko iá naʻe ʻikai ke u mahinoʻi lelei ha niʻihi ʻo ʻene ngaahi leá, ka ko e ngaahi fakahā kimui aí, ʻi heʻeku maheni ange mo ʻene ngaahi kupuʻi lea motuʻá, ʻoku nau fakaʻatā au he taimí ni ke u ʻoatu ʻa e talanoá kotoa ʻo hangē ko ia ʻe lava ke fakamoʻoniʻi ʻi he foʻi ʻangaʻanga pē ʻoku ou ʻoatú.





VAHE 12. Ko e fakamatala mo'ui.

Ko Queequeg ko ha tangata‘ifonua ‘o Rokovoko, ko ha motu mama‘o ‘i he Hihifó mo e Tongá. ʻOku ʻikai ke ʻi lalo ia ʻi ha mape; ʻoku ʻikai ʻaupito ke pehē ʻa e ngaahi feituʻu moʻoní.

‘I he taimi ‘oku lele holo ai ha manu fekai fo‘ou ‘i hono ngaahi vaotā fakafonuá ‘i ha fo‘i mohuku, ‘o muimui atu ai ‘a e fanga kosi ‘oku nau ngungu, ‘o hangē ko ha fu‘u ‘akau lanu mata; na‘a mo e taimi ko iá, ‘i he laumālie loto-māfana ‘o Queequeg, na‘e toitoi ai ha holi mālohi ke sio ki ha me‘a lahi ange ‘i he Pule‘anga-Ha‘a-Kalisitiané ‘i ha fakatātā ‘o e toutai tafola pe ua. Ko ʻene tamaí ko ha ʻEiki Māʻolunga, ko ha Tuʻi; ko ʻene tangataʻeikí ko ha Taulaʻeiki Lahi; pea ʻi he tafaʻaki fakafaʻeé naʻá ne pōlepole ʻi he ngaahi fineʻeikí ʻa ia ko e ngaahi uaifi ʻo e kau tau taʻe-faʻa-ikunaʻí. Naʻe ʻi ai ha toto lelei ʻaupito ʻi hono ngaahi uoua —ko e ngaahi meʻa fakatuʻi; neongo naʻe fakaʻauha fakaloloma, ʻoku ou manavasiʻi, ʻe he hehema kai tangata naʻá ne fafangaʻi ʻi hono kei talavou taʻe akoʻí.

Naʻe ʻaʻahi ha vaka ʻo e Taulanga Sag ki he matātahi ʻo ʻene tamaí, pea naʻe kumi ʻe Queequeg ha hala ki he ngaahi fonua faka-Kalisitiané. Ka ko e vaká, ʻi heʻene maʻu ʻene kau toutai kakató, naʻá ne fakafisingaʻi hono kofú; pea naʻe ʻikai lava ke ikuna ʻa e tākiekina kotoa ʻa e Tuʻí ʻene tamaí. Ka naʻe fuakava ʻa Queequeg ʻi ha fuakava. Naʻá ne folau tokotaha pē ʻi heʻene vaká ki ha teleʻa mamaʻo, ʻa ia naʻá ne ʻiloʻi kuo pau ke fou atu ai ʻa e vaká ʻi heʻene mavahe mei he motú. ‘I he tafa‘aki ‘e tahá na‘e ‘i ai ha ‘ā maka; ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘e tahá ko ha ‘elelo mā‘ulalo ‘o e fonuá, ‘oku ‘ufi‘ufi ‘e he ngaahi vaotā ‘o e ‘akau ko e mangrove na‘e tupu hake ki tu‘a ki he vaí. ʻI heʻene fufū ʻene vaká, ʻa ia naʻe kei ʻalu holo, ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi vaotā ko ʻení, mo hono muʻá ʻo hanga ki tahí, naʻá ne tangutu hifo ʻi he muiʻi vaká, ʻo maʻulalo hono nimá ʻi he foʻi vaká; pea ʻi he ʻalu atu ʻa e vaká, naʻá ne lele atu ʻo hangē ha ulo ʻo e ʻuhilá; maʻu hono tafaʻakí; ʻi ha foʻi tā ki mui ʻe taha ʻo hono vaʻé naʻe fulihi ʻo ngoto ʻene vaká; kaka hake ʻi he ngaahi seini; pea lī ia ʻi he lōloa kakato ki he funga vaká, ʻo ne puke ha foʻi mama ʻi ai, peá ne fuakava ke ʻoua naʻá ne tukuange ia, neongo kuo haʻi ʻo kongokonga.

Naʻe taʻeʻaonga ʻa e fakamanamana ʻa e ʻeikitaú ke lī ia ki tahi; naʻá ne tautau ha meʻafana ʻi hono ongo louhiʻi nimá telefua; Ko Queequeg ko e foha ia ʻo ha Tuʻi, pea naʻe ʻikai ke luelue ʻa Queequeg. ‘I he‘ene lavea ‘i he‘ene ta‘emanavahē faka‘auhá, mo ‘ene holi fakamālohi ke ‘a‘ahi ki he Pule‘anga-Ha‘a-Kalisitiané, na‘e faifai pē ‘o faka‘atā ‘e he ‘eikitaú, peá ne tala ange kiate ia te ne ‘ai nai ia ke ne ‘i ‘api. Ka ko e talavou anga-fakamanu lelei ko ʻení —ko e Pilinisi tahi ko ʻeni ʻo Uēlesí, naʻe ʻikai ke ne teitei sio ki he fale ʻo e ʻEikivaká. Naʻa nau tuku hifo ia ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e kau folaú, ʻo nau ngaohi ia ko ha tangata tafola. Kae hangē ko e fiemālie pē ‘a Czar Peter ke ngāue mālohi ‘i he ngaahi taulanga vaka ‘o e ngaahi kolo mulí, na‘e ‘ikai ke faka‘ai‘ai ‘e Queequeg ha me‘a ‘oku hā ngali fakamā, kapau ‘e lava ke ne ma‘u fiefia ai ‘a e mālohi ke fakamaama‘i ‘a hono kakai ‘o e fonua ta‘eako‘í. He ʻi lalo —ko ia naʻá ne talamai kiate aú —naʻe fakaʻaiʻai ia ʻe ha holi loloto ke ako ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e kau Kalisitiané, ʻa e ngaahi ʻātisi ʻa ia ke ne ʻai hono kakaí ke nau kei fiefia ange ʻiate kinautolu; pea ʻoku lahi ange ʻi he meʻa ko iá, ʻoku kei lelei ange pē ʻiate kinautolu. Ka, ‘oiauē! na‘e vave ‘a e fakatuipau‘i ia ‘e he ngaahi tō‘onga ‘a e kau tangata tafola na‘a mo e kau Kalisitiané ‘e lava ke nau fakatou faka‘ofa mo angakovi; ʻo lahi ange taʻefakangatangata, ʻi he kau hiteni kotoa pē ʻo ʻene tamaí. Naʻe aʻu fakaʻosi ki he Taulanga Sag motuʻá; pea sio ki he meʻa naʻe fai ʻe he kau kauvaká ʻi aí; pea hoko atu leva ki Nantucket, pea sio ki he'enau fakamoleki honau totongi 'i he feitu'u ko ia foki, na'e tukuange ia 'e he masiva ko Queequeg koe'uhi ko e mole. Naʻá ne fakakaukau, ko ha māmani kovi ia ʻi he ngaahi meridian kotoa pē; Te u mate ko e hiteni.

Pea ko ia ko ha tokotaha tauhi ‘aitoli motu‘a ‘i hono lotó, na‘á ne kei nofo pē ‘i he lotolotonga ‘o e kau Kalisitiane ko ení, ‘o ne tui honau ngaahi kofú, pea feinga ke lea‘aki ‘enau ngaahi lea faka‘uliá. Ko ia ai 'a e ngaahi founga queer fekau'aki mo ia, neongo ko 'eni ha taimi mei 'api.

ʻI ha ngaahi fakahinohino, ne u ʻeke ange pe ʻoku ʻikai ke ne fokotuʻu ke foki, pea fai ha fakanofo tuʻi; koeʻuhí he te ne lau nai he taimí ni kuo mate ʻene tamaí pea ʻosi ʻalu, he naʻá ne fuʻu motuʻa mo vaivai ʻaupito ʻi he ngaahi fakamatala fakaʻosí. Naʻá ne tali ʻikai, ʻoku teʻeki; pea tānaki atu ko ia ko e lotu faka-Kalisitiane manavahē, pe ko e kau Kalisitiane, naʻá ne taʻefeʻunga ia ʻi heʻene ʻalu hake ki he taloni maʻa mo taʻeʻuli ʻo e kau Tuʻi hiteni ʻe toko tolungofulu ʻi muʻa ʻiate iá. Ka ʻe faifai pea, naʻá ne pehē, te ne foki mai, —ʻi heʻene ongoʻi pē kuó ne toe papitaiso. Kae kehe, ki he nonce, naʻá ne fokotuʻu ke folau holo, ʻo tō ʻene ʻotisi vaó ʻi he ʻoseni kotoa ʻe faá. Naʻa nau ngaohi ia ko ha meʻafana, pea ko e ukamea talatala ko iá naʻe fetongi ia ʻaki ha sepita he taimí ni.

Naʻá ku ʻeke ange pe ko e hā nai ʻene taumuʻa vave tahá, ʻo uesia ʻene ngaahi ngaʻunu ʻi he kahaʻú. Naʻá ne tali mai, ke toe ʻalu ki tahi, ʻi hono uiuiʻi motuʻá. ʻI he meʻá ni, ne u talaange ki ai ko e toutai tafola ko ʻeku tisaini pē ʻaʻaku, peá u fakahā ange ʻeku fakakaukau ke folau atu mei Nantucket, ko e taulanga ʻoku ʻamanaki lelei taha ia ki ha tangata toutai tafola fakaʻofoʻofa ke ne folau mei ai. Naʻá ne fakakaukau leva ke ʻalu mo au ki he motu ko iá, vaka ʻi he vaka tatau, heka ki he uati tatau, vaka tatau, mess tatau mo au, ʻi hono fakanounoú ke vahevahe ʻeku hap kotoa pē; mo hoku ongo nimá ʻi hono ongo nimá, ʻo hū loto-toʻa ki he Potluck ʻo e ongo māmaní fakatouʻosi. Ki he me‘á ni kotoa na‘á ku loto-fiefia ki ai; he tuku kehe ʻa e ʻofa naʻá ku ongoʻi he taimí ni kia Kuiki, ko ha tokotaha taukei ia ʻi he ʻaiʻangakai ʻo e manú, pea ʻi heʻene peheé, naʻe ʻikai lava ke taʻeʻaonga lahi ia ki ha taha, ʻa ia, hangē ko aú, naʻe ʻikai ke ne ʻiloʻi kakato ʻa e ngaahi misiteli ʻo e toutai tafola, neongo naʻá ne maheni lelei mo e tahí, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku ʻiloʻi ʻe he kau toutai fakatau koloá.

ʻI hono fakaʻosi ʻene talanoá ʻaki ʻene puhi fakaʻosi ʻo ʻene paipá ʻoku mate, naʻe ʻuma mai ʻa Kuiki kiate au, ʻo ne lomiʻi hono laʻé ki hoku laʻé, peá ne puhiʻi ʻa e māmá, naʻá ma feʻauʻauhi mei he taha mo e taha, ʻi he tafaʻaki ko ʻení mo e tafaʻaki ko iá, pea ʻikai fuoloa kuó ma mohe.





VAHE 13. Ko e uili.

ʻI he pongipongi hono hokó, Mōnité, hili ʻeku liʻaki ʻa e ʻulu kuo ʻosi fakaʻauhá ki ha tokotaha ʻuluʻulu, ki ha poloka, ne u fakaleleiʻi ʻeku totongi pē ʻaʻakú mo e kaungāmeʻá; kae kehe, ‘i hono ngāue‘aki ‘a e pa‘anga ‘a hoku kaungāme‘á. Naʻe ngali fakaofo ʻa e ngingila ʻa e ʻeiki ʻapi ʻoku malimali, pea pehē ki he kau nofo ʻapí, ʻi he feohi fakakaumeʻa fakafokifā naʻe tupu hake ʻi hoku vahaʻá mo Kuiki —tautautefito ki he ngaahi talanoa ʻa Pita Kofini ki he moa mo e pulu fekauʻaki mo iá naʻá ne fuʻu fakatupu manavasiʻi lahi kiate au kimuʻa fekauʻaki mo e tokotaha tonu ʻoku ou feohi mo ia he taimí ni.

Naʻá ma nō ha saliote uili, pea ʻi heʻemau heka ʻemau ngaahi meʻá, kau ai ʻeku kato kāpeti masiva pē ʻaʻakú, mo e tangai kaniva mo e hāmoka ʻa Kuikikí, ʻo mau ʻalu hifo ki he “Moss,” ko e kiʻi vakapuna pakete ʻo Nantucket naʻe tau ʻi he uafú. ʻI heʻemau ʻalú naʻe sio fakamamaʻu mai ʻa e kakaí; ‘ikai ‘i Queequeg ‘o fu‘u lahi —he na‘a nau angamaheni ‘aki ‘a e sio ki he kau kai tangata hangē ko iá ‘i honau ngaahi halá, —ka ‘i he‘enau sio kiate ia mo au ‘i ha ngaahi tu‘unga fakapulipuli pehē. Ka naʻe ʻikai ke ma tokanga kiate kinaua, ʻo ma ʻalu atu ʻo fakaʻuli ʻi he salioté ʻo taufetongi, pea tuʻu ʻa Kuiki ʻi he taimi ko iá ke fakatonutonu ʻa e ʻaiʻangakai ʻo ʻene ngaahi foʻi ʻakau ʻo e ʻaá. Naʻá ku ʻeke ange pe ko e hā ʻokú ne ʻave ai ha meʻa fakatupu faingataʻa pehē mo ia ki ʻuta, pea pe ʻoku ʻikai ke maʻu ʻe he ngaahi vaka toutai tafola kotoa pē ʻenau ngaahi foʻi ʻaá. Ki he meʻá ni, ʻi hono kakanó, naʻá ne tali mai, neongo naʻe moʻoni feʻunga ʻa e meʻa naʻá ku fakaʻilongaʻí, ka naʻá ne maʻu ha ʻofa makehe ki heʻene foʻi ʻakau pē ʻaʻaná, koeʻuhí he naʻe ʻo e meʻa fakapapauʻi, kuo ʻahiʻahiʻi lelei ʻi ha ngaahi tau fakamatelie lahi, pea ʻoku loloto ʻene feohi mo e loto ʻo e fanga tafola. Ko hono fakanounoú, hangē ko e kau utu-ta‘u mo e kau tu‘usi ‘akau tokolahi ‘i ‘uta, ‘a ia ‘oku nau ‘alu ki he ngaahi ngoue‘anga ‘o e kau ngoué ‘o nau ma‘u ‘enau ngaahi heletā pē ‘anautolu—neongo ‘oku ‘ikai ha founga fakapotopoto ke fakamo‘ua‘i kinautolu ke nau tokonaki kinautolu—na‘a mo ia, na‘e sai‘ia ange ‘a Queequeg, koe‘uhi ko ‘ene ngaahi ‘uhinga fakafo‘ituituí tonu, ‘i he‘ene fo‘i heletaá tonu.

ʻI heʻene hiki ʻa e salioté mei hoku nimá ki hono nimá, naʻá ne talamai ha talanoa malie fekauʻaki mo e fuofua saliote uili naʻá ne sio aí. Naʻe ʻi Taulanga Sag ia. ‘Oku hā ngali na‘e nō ange ‘e he kau ma‘u ‘o ‘ene vaká ha taha, ‘a ia ke ne ‘ave ai hono fatafata mamafá ki hono fale nofo‘angá. Ke ʻoua naʻa ngali taʻeʻilo ki he meʻá —neongo ʻi hono moʻoní naʻá ne pehē fakaʻaufuli, fekauʻaki mo e founga pau ke puleʻi ʻaki ʻa e salioté —ʻoku ʻai ʻe Kuiki hono fatafatá ki ai; lashes vave ia; pea toki uma ʻa e salioté ʻo laka hake ʻi he uafú. “Ko e hā,” ko ʻeku leá ia, “Queequeg, mahalo naʻá ke ʻiloʻi lelei ange ʻi he meʻa ko iá, ʻe fakakaukau ha taha. ʻIkai naʻe kata ʻa e kakaí?”

ʻI he meʻá ni, naʻá ne talamai ha talanoa ʻe taha. Ko e kakai ‘o hono motu ko Rokovoko, ‘oku hā ngali, ‘i he‘enau ngaahi kātoanga malí ‘oku nau fakahaa‘i ‘a e vai namu kakala ‘o e fanga ki‘i niú ki ha fu‘u fo‘i ‘akau ‘oku ‘uli‘uli ‘o hangē ha fo‘i ‘ai‘angakai ‘o e manú; pea ʻoku faʻu maʻu pē ʻe he punchbowl ko ʻení ʻa e teuteu lotoloto lahi ʻi he fala lalanga ʻoku fai ai ʻa e kātoangá. Ko ʻeni naʻe ʻi ai ha taimi naʻe aʻu ai ha vaka fakatau lahi pau ki Rokovoko, pea ko hono ʻeikitaú —mei he ngaahi fakamatala kotoa pē, ko ha tangataʻeiki ʻoku fuʻu fakaʻeiʻeiki ʻaupito, ʻo ʻikai toe siʻi hifo ʻi ha kapiteni tahi —naʻe fakaafeʻi ʻa e ʻeikitau ko ʻení ki he kātoanga mali ʻo e tuofefine ʻo Queequeg, ko ha pilinisesi kei talavou fakaʻofoʻofa naʻe toki hoko hono taʻú. Lelei; 'i he taimi na'e fakatahataha kotoa ai 'a e kau fakaafe mali ki he fale 'akau 'o e fefine mali, 'oku laka atu 'a e Kapiteni ko 'eni, pea 'i hono vahe'i 'a e lakanga fakalangilangi, na'a ne fokotu'u ia 'i he tafa'aki 'o e punchbowl, pea 'i he vaha'a 'o e Taula'eiki Lahi mo hono 'afio ko e Tu'i, ko e tamai 'a Queequeg. Ko hono leaʻaki ʻo e ʻaloʻofá, —he ʻoku maʻu ʻe he kakai ko iá ʻenau ʻaloʻofá ʻo hangē pē ko kitautolú —neongo naʻe talamai ʻe Queequeg kiate au ʻoku ʻikai hangē ko kimautolu, ʻa ia ʻi he ngaahi taimi peheé ʻoku mau sio hifo ki heʻemau ngaahi peletí, ka ko kinautolu, ʻi hono kehé, ʻi heʻenau kapeʻi ʻa e fanga pató, ʻoku nau sio hake ki he Tokotaha Foaki lahi ʻo e ngaahi kātoanga kotoa pē —Ko e ʻaloʻofá, ʻoku ou pehē, ʻoku pehē, ʻoku fakaava ʻe he Taulaʻeiki Lahi ʻo e imme imme the banland ʻa e; ‘a ia ko hono unu hono ongo louhi‘i nima kuo fakatapui mo fakatapuí ki he poulú ki mu‘a ke tafe holo ‘a e inu tāpuakí. 'I he'ene sio kiate ia 'oku fokotu'u ia 'i he tafa'aki 'o e Taula'eiki, pea fakatokanga'i 'a e ouau, pea fakakaukau kiate ia —ko e Kapiteni 'o ha vaka —'oku ne ma'u 'a e mu'omu'a mahino 'i ha Tu'i motu noa'ia, tautautefito ki he fale 'o e Tu'i tonu —'oku hoko atu 'a e Kapiteni 'i he momoko ke fufulu hono nima 'i he punchbowl finger supper;- for taking a huglass it; “Ko ʻeni,” ko e lea ia ʻa Queequeg, “ko e hā ʻokú ke tink he taimí ni?—ʻIkai naʻe kata homau kakaí?”

“Kapiteni Peleki,” ko e lea tuʻumaʻu ia ʻa Pilitati, “ʻoku ʻikai te u lava ʻo tala ʻe ho konisēnisí ʻa e ʻinisi ʻe hongofulu ʻo e vaí, pe ko e fute ʻe hongofulu, ka ʻi heʻeku kei hoko ko ha tangata taʻefakatomala, Kapiteni Peleki, ʻoku ou manavasiʻi lahi naʻa hoko ho konisēnisí ko ha taha pē ʻoku tafe; pea ʻe iku ʻo ʻiloʻi ʻe Peta ʻo fakangoto ʻa e pie.”

Ko Peleki, hili ʻene tukuange ʻene ʻitá ʻo hangē ko ia naʻá ne faí, naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai toe ʻi ai ha meʻa ʻiate ia, pea ko ia foki, naʻá ne tangutu hifo ʻo hangē ha lamí, neongo naʻá ne kiʻi ngungu ʻo hangē ʻokú ne kei fakahohaʻasi ʻi he manavasiʻí. “ʻOiauē!” naʻá ne ifi ʻi he fakaʻosí —“kuo ʻalu atu ʻa e matangí ki he matangi, ʻoku ou fakakaukau. Pilitati, naʻá ke faʻa poto ʻi hono fakamāsila ha tao, fakaleleiʻi ʻa e peni ko iá, ʻe ʻikai ko ia. ʻOku fie maʻu ʻe heʻeku hele-siaka ʻi heni ʻa e maka-momosi. Ko ia ia; fakamālō atu, Pilitati. Ko ʻeni leva, ko ʻeku talavou ʻi lalo, ko ʻIsimeʻeli, ʻoku pehē mai ʻe heʻeku talavoú, ko ʻIsimeʻeli? ‘alu ki heni, ‘Isime‘eli, he na‘e tokoto ‘a hono tolungeau.”

“Kapiteni Peleki,” ko ʻeku leá ia, “ʻoku ʻi ai haʻaku kaungāmeʻa ʻoku fie folau foki —te u ʻomi ia ki lalo ʻapongipongi?”

Naʻe pehē ʻe Peleki, “Ke fakapapauʻi.” “ʻOmi ia, pea te ta sio kiate ia.”

“Ko e hā e lay ʻokú ne fie maʻú?” ko e tangi ia ʻa Pilitati, ʻo ne sio hake mei he tohi naʻá ne toe tanu ai iá.

“ʻOiauē, ʻoua naʻá ke tokanga ki he meʻa ko iá, Pilitati,” ko e lea ia ʻa Peleki. “Kuó ne ʻosi tafolaʻi ia ha taha?” ʻo tafoki mai kiate au.

“Naʻá ne tāmateʻi ha fanga tafola lahi ange ʻi he meʻa te u lava ʻo laú, ʻEikitau Peleki.”

“Sai, ʻomi ia ʻi he taimi ko iá.”

Pea, hili ʻeku fakamoʻoni hingoa ʻi he ngaahi laʻipepá, naʻá ku ʻalu; ʻikai ha meʻa ʻe veiveiua ka ko ʻeku fai ha ngāue pongipongi lelei, pea ko e Pequod ko e vaka tatau pē ia naʻe tokonaki ʻe Yojo ke ʻave au mo Queequeg ʻo takai ʻi he Keipí.

Ka naʻe teʻeki ai ke u laka mamaʻo atu, ʻi heʻeku kamata ke fakakaukau ʻoku kei ʻikai pē ke u sio ki ai ʻa e ʻEikitau ʻa ia te u folau mo iá; neongo ia, ko e mo‘oni, ‘i he ngaahi tu‘unga lahi, ‘e fakanaunau‘i kakato ha vaka-tafola, pea tali kotoa ‘ene kau ngāué ‘i he vaká, ki mu‘a ke fakahaa‘i ia ‘e he ‘eikitaú ‘aki ‘ene a‘u mai ke to‘o ‘a e fekaú; he ko e taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻoku fuʻu lōloa ʻaupito ʻa e ngaahi folau ko ʻení, pea ʻoku fuʻu nounou ʻaupito ʻa e ngaahi vahaʻataimi ʻi ʻuta ʻi ʻapí, ʻo kapau ʻoku ʻi ai ha fāmili ʻo e ʻeikitaú, pe ko ha faʻahinga hohaʻa fakatupu ʻita ʻo e faʻahinga ko iá, ʻoku ʻikai ke ne fuʻu hohaʻa lahi ki heʻene vaká ʻi he taulangá, ka ʻokú ne tuku pē ia ki he kau ʻea ʻo e vaká kae ʻoua kuo mateuteu kotoa ki tahi. Kae kehe, ‘oku lelei ma‘u pē ke ke sio kiate ia ki mu‘a ke ke tukupā ta‘ealafakafoki koe ki hono nimá. ʻI heʻeku tafoki ki muí ne u ʻohofi ʻa ʻEikitau Peleki, ʻo ʻeke ange pe ʻe maʻu ʻi fē ʻa ʻEikitau ʻĒhapí.

“Pea ko e hā ʻokú ke fie maʻu meia ʻEikitau ʻĒhapí? ʻOku sai pē ia; ʻoku fakafolau koe.”

“ʻIo, ka ʻoku totonu ke u saiʻia ke sio kiate ia.”

“Ka ʻoku ʻikai ke u tui te ke lava ʻi he taimi ní. ʻOku ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi pau pe ko e hā ʻa e meʻa ʻoku hokó; ka ʻokú ne nofo ofi pē ʻi loto ʻi he falé; ko ha faʻahinga mahaki, pea neongo ia ʻoku ʻikai ke ne fōtunga pehē. Ko hono moʻoní, ʻoku ʻikai ke ne puke; ka ʻikai, ʻoku ʻikai foki ke ne moʻui lelei. Any don so supp' me, young will' koe.Ko e tangata queer ia, ko ia 'oku fakakaukau 'a e ni'ihi — ka ko ha taha lelei fe'unga, 'ikai ha manavasi'i. Kuo ʻi he ngaahi kolisí ʻa ʻĒhapi, pea pehē ki he ʻmoʻui ʻa e kau tangata kai; kuo ngāue‘aki ia ki he ngaahi me‘a fakaofo loloto ange ‘i he ngaahi peaú; na‘á ne fakalelei‘i ‘a hono ta‘ofi vela ‘i he ngaahi fili mālohi ange mo muli ‘i he fanga tafola. Ko ʻene taʻú! aye, 'a e fie ma'u mo e fakapapau'i 'a e me'a ko ia mei hotau motu kotoa pe! ʻOiauē! ʻoku ʻikai ko e Kapiteni Pilitatí; ʻikai, pea ʻoku ʻikai ko e ʻEikitau Peleki; ko ʻĒhapi , tamasiʻi; mo ʻEhapi ʻo e kuonga muʻá, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi, ko ha tuʻi kuo fakakalauni!”

“Pea ko ha taha fakalielia ‘aupito: ‘I he taimi na‘e tāmate‘i ai ‘a e tu‘i angakovi ko iá, ‘ikai na‘a nau ‘emo ‘a e fanga kulií hono totó?”

“Haʻu ki heni kiate au —ʻi heni, ʻi heni,” ko e lea ia ʻa Pelekí, mo ha ʻuhinga ʻi hono matá ʻa ia naʻe meimei ke ne fakaʻohovaleʻi au. “Sio mai, tamasiʻi; ʻoua naʻá ke teitei lea ʻaki ia ʻi he vaka Pequod. ʻOua naʻá ke teitei lea ʻaki ia ʻi ha feituʻu.
