VAHE 4. Ko e Fakafepaki.

ʻI heʻeku ʻā hake ʻi he pongipongi hono hokó ʻi he maama ʻo e ʻahó, ne u ʻiloʻi kuo lī mai ʻa e nima ʻo Kuiki ʻiate au ʻi he founga ʻofa mo ʻofa taha. Naʻá ke meimei fakakaukau ko hono uaifí au. Ko e counterpane naʻe ʻo e patchwork, fonu ʻi he fanga kiʻi tapafā lanu kehekehe mo e tafaʻaki tolu; pea ko e nima ko ʻeni ʻo hono tattoo kotoa ʻaki ha labyrinth Kēliti taʻe ngata ʻo ha fakatātā, ʻoku ʻikai ha ongo konga ʻo ia naʻe ʻi ai ha malumalu pau ʻe taha —ʻoku ou mahalo ʻoku makatuʻunga ʻi heʻene tauhi hono nimá ʻi tahi taʻefakapotopoto ʻi he laʻaá mo e malumalú, naʻe ʻikai ke maau ʻa e ngaahi nima ʻo hono kofú ʻi ha ngaahi taimi kehekehe —ko ʻene sio kotoa ko ʻeni ʻo hangē ko e sa ʻo e sa, quilt fakapipiki. Ko e moʻoni, ʻi heʻeku tokoto ʻi ha konga ʻi ai ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe fai ʻe he nimá ʻi heʻeku fuofua ʻā haké, naʻe ʻikai ke u meimei lava ʻo ʻiloʻi ia mei he kofukofú, naʻa nau fuʻu fakafefiofi fakataha honau ngaahi lanú; pea naʻe toki lava pē ʻi he ongoʻi ʻo e mamafá mo e tenge ʻo ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻuma mai ʻa Queequeg kiate aú.

Naʻe kehe ʻeku ngaahi ongó. Tuku muʻa ke u feinga ke fakamatalaʻi kinautolu. ʻI heʻeku kei siʻí, ʻoku ou manatuʻi lelei ha tūkunga ʻoku kiʻi meimei tatau naʻe hoko kiate au; pe ko ha meʻa moʻoni pe ko ha misi, naʻe ʻikai ke u teitei lava ʻo nofo kakato. Ko e tūkungá naʻe pehē. Naʻá ku ʻosi tuʻusi ha ngaahi kāpa pe ko ha meʻa kehe —ʻoku ou pehē naʻe feinga ke totolo hake ʻi he ʻaá, ʻo hangē ko ia ne u mamata ki ha kiʻi tafi ʻi ha ngaahi ʻaho siʻi kimuʻá; pea mo ʻeku faʻē-ua ʻa ia, ʻi ha faʻahinga founga pē, naʻá ne uipi au ʻi he taimi kotoa pē, pe fekauʻi au ke u mohe ʻo ʻikai haʻaku kai efiafi, —naʻe toho au ʻe heʻeku faʻeé ʻi hoku ongo vaʻé mei he ʻaá ʻo ne fakaheka au ke u mohe, neongo ko e taimi pē ia ʻe ua ʻo e hoʻatā ʻo e ʻaho 21 ʻo Suné, ko e ʻaho lōloa taha ia ʻo e taʻú ʻi homau hemisifiá. Naʻá ku ongoʻi ilifia. Ka naʻe ʻikai ha tokoni ki ai, ko ia ne u ʻalu hake ʻi he sitepú ki hoku kiʻi loki ʻi he fungavaka tolú, ʻo vete hoku valá ʻi he māmālie taha ʻe ala lavá koeʻuhí ke u tāmateʻi e taimí, pea ʻi haʻaku mapuhoi mamahi ne u hū ki he vahaʻa ʻo e ngaahi laʻipepá.

Naʻá ku tokoto fakaloloma ʻi ai ʻo fakafuofuaʻi kuo pau ke ʻosi ha houa kakato ʻe hongofulu mā ono kimuʻa peá u toki ʻamanaki ki ha toetuʻu. Houa ʻe hongofulu mā ono ʻi he mohengá! naʻe mamahi ʻa e kiʻi ʻo hoku tuʻá ʻi heʻeku fakakaukau ki aí. Pea naʻe fuʻu maʻamaʻa foki; ko e laʻā ʻoku ulo mai ʻi he matapā sioʻatá, mo ha longoaʻa lahi ʻa e ngaahi kosí ʻi he ngaahi halá, mo e leʻo ʻo e ngaahi leʻo fakasōtoma ʻi he falé kotoa. Naʻe fakaʻau ke kovi ange ʻeku ongoʻí —naʻe faifai peá u tuʻu hake, teunga, pea ʻalu hifo māmālie ʻi hoku vaʻe kuo ʻosi sitokení, kumi ʻeku faʻē-tangatá, pea fakafokifā pē kuó u lī au ki hono vaʻé, ʻo kole ange kiate ia ko ha ʻofa makehe ke ʻomi haʻaku slipper lelei koeʻuhí ko ʻeku tōʻonga koví; ko ha meʻa pē moʻoni ka ko hono fakahalaiaʻi au ke u tokoto ʻi ha taimi lōloa taʻealakātakiʻi pehē. Ka ko e faʻē-ua lelei taha mo tokanga taha ia, pea ʻi heʻeku foki maí naʻe pau ke u ʻalu ki hoku lokí. Naʻá ku tokoto ʻi ai ʻi ha ngaahi houa lahi ʻo ʻāʻā lahi, ʻo ongoʻi kovi ange ʻaupito ʻi he meʻa kuó u ongoʻi talu mei ai, naʻa mo e ngaahi faingataʻa lahi taha ne hoko atu aí. Naʻe faifai peá u tō ki ha misi fakatupu faingataʻa ʻo ha mohe; pea ʻi heʻeku ʻā māmālie mei aí —ʻo vaeua ʻeku ngaahi misí —naʻá ku fakaava hoku matá, pea ko e loki kimuʻa naʻe maama ʻe he laʻaá kuo takaiʻi he taimí ni ʻe he fakapoʻuli ʻi tuʻá. ʻI he taimi pē ko iá ne u ongoʻi ha ʻohovale ʻoku lele ʻi hoku fōmetí kotoa; na‘e ‘ikai ha me‘a ke sio ki ai, pea na‘e ‘ikai ha me‘a ke ongona; ka naʻe hangē naʻe ʻai ha nima mahulu hake ʻi hoku nimá. Naʻe tautau hoku nimá ʻi he funga ʻo e counterpane, pea ko e fotunga pe phantom taʻehingoa, taʻefakakaukauʻi, fakalongolongo, ʻa ia naʻe kau ki ai ʻa e nimá, naʻe hangē naʻe tangutu ofi ʻi hoku tafaʻaki mohengá. He ko e meʻa naʻe ngali ko e ngaahi kuonga ʻoku fakaputu ʻi he ngaahi kuonga, naʻá ku tokoto ai, ʻo ʻaisi ʻi he ngaahi manavasiʻi fakalilifu tahá, ʻo ʻikai ke u loto-toʻa ke toho hoku nimá; ka ʻoku ou kei fakakaukau pē kapau te u lava pē ʻo ueʻi ia ʻi ha ʻinisi ʻe taha, ʻe motuhi ʻa e sipele fakalilifú. Naʻe ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi e founga naʻe faifai pea ʻalu holo ai ʻa e ʻatamai ko ʻení meiate aú; ka ʻi heʻeku ʻā hake ʻi he pongipongí, ne u manatuʻi kotoa ia ʻi he tetetete, pea ʻi ha ngaahi ʻaho mo ha ngaahi uike mo ha ngaahi māhina hili iá ne u mole ʻi he ngaahi feinga fakatupu puputuʻu ke fakamatalaʻi e meʻa liló. ‘Ikai, ‘o a‘u mai ki he houa tonu ko ení, ‘oku ou fa‘a puzzle au ‘aki ia.

Ko ʻeni, toʻo ʻa e manavasiʻi fakalilifú, pea ko ʻeku ngaahi ongo ʻi heʻeku ongoʻi ʻa e nima fakanatula ʻiate aú naʻe meimei tatau ʻaupito, ʻi honau ngali kehé, mo e ngaahi ongo naʻá ku aʻusia ʻi heʻeku ʻā hake ʻo sio ki he nima fakapangani ʻo Queequeg ʻoku lī takai ʻiate aú. Ka naʻe faifai pea toe hoko fakapotopoto mai ʻa e ngaahi meʻa kotoa pē naʻe hoko ʻi he pō kuo ʻosí, ʻo takitaha, ʻi ha moʻoni tuʻumaʻu, peá u toki tokoto pē ʻo moʻui ki he faingataʻa fakakata. He neongo naʻá ku feinga ke ueʻi hono nimá —fakaava hono meʻa-fakaʻaiʻai ʻo e tangata taʻané —ka ʻi heʻene mohé, naʻá ne kei ʻuma mālohi pē kiate au, ʻo hangē ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa kehe ka ko e maté pē te ne fakamavaheʻi kimaua. Naʻá ku feinga he taimí ni ke fakaakeake ia —“Queequeg!”—ka ko ʻene talí pē ko ha ngungu. Naʻá ku takai holo leva, ʻo ongoʻi ʻe hoku kiá ʻo hangē ʻoku ʻi ha kola-hoosi; pea fakafokifā pē kuó ne ongoʻi ha kiʻi toʻotoʻo. ʻI hono lī ki he tafaʻakí ʻa e counterpane, naʻe tokoto ai ʻa e tomahawk ʻo mohe ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e manu fekaí, ʻo hangē ha pēpē ʻoku fofonga ʻaki ha heletaá. Ko ha pickle fakaʻofoʻofa, moʻoni, naʻá ku fakakaukau; mohe heni ʻi ha fale kehe ʻi he ʻaho lahi, mo ha tokotaha kai tangata mo ha tomahawk! “Kuiki!—ʻi he hingoa ʻo e leleí, Kuiki, ʻā hake!” ʻI he fakaʻosí, ʻi he lahi ʻo e wriggling, mo e expostulations leʻolahi mo taʻetuku ʻi he taʻefeʻunga ʻo ʻene ʻuma ki ha kaungā tangata ʻi he faʻahinga sitaila mali ko iá, naʻá ku lavameʻa ʻi hono toʻo mai ha ngungu; pea ʻi he taimi ko iá, naʻá ne toʻo hono nimá ki mui, ʻo ne lulululu kotoa ʻo hangē ha kuli Niufaunilani mei he vaí pē, peá ne tangutu hake ʻi hono mohengá, ʻo fefeka ʻo hangē ha tokotoko paiká, ʻo ne sio mai kiate au, mo milimili hono matá ʻo hangē ʻoku ʻikai ke ne manatuʻi fakaʻaufuli ʻa e founga naʻá ku ʻi ai aí, neongo naʻe ʻi ai ha meʻa ʻoku ʻikai ke ne ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻasi mai kiate au. Lolotonga iá, naʻá ku tokoto fakalongolongo ʻo siofi ia, ʻo ʻikai haʻaku veiveiua lahi he taimí ni, peá u punou ke siofi fakanounou ha meʻamoʻui ʻoku fuʻu fie ʻilo lahi. ‘I he taimi, na‘e faifai pē ‘o hā ngali kuo fakapapau‘i ai ‘e hono ‘atamaí ke ne uesia ‘a e ‘ulungaanga ‘o hono kaungā mohengá, pea na‘á ne hoko, ‘o hangē ha laú, ‘o fakalelei mo e mo‘oni‘i me‘á; naʻá ne puna ki tuʻa ʻi he falikí, pea ʻi ha ngaahi fakaʻilonga mo ha ngaahi leʻo pau naʻá ne ʻomi ke mahino kiate au, kapau ʻoku ou saiʻia ai, te ne ʻuluaki teunga pea toki tuku au ke u teunga ʻi he hili iá, ʻo tuku ʻa e fale nofoʻangá kotoa kiate au. Fakakaukau au, Queequeg, 'i he ngaahi tukunga, ko e overture sivilaise 'aupito 'eni; ka, ko e moʻoní, ʻoku maʻu ʻe he fanga manu fekai ko ʻení ha ongo fakanatula ʻo e meʻakaí, lea ʻaki ʻa e meʻa te ke loto ki aí; ʻoku fakaofo ʻa e anga ʻo ʻenau anga fakaʻapaʻapá ʻi he tefitó. ʻOku ou fai ʻa e fakamālō makehe ko ʻení kia Queequeg, koeʻuhí he naʻá ne fai mai kiate au ʻa e anga fakafonua mo e fakakaukau lahi, lolotonga ia ʻoku ou halaia ʻi he angakovi lahi; ʻo sio fakamamaʻu kiate ia mei he mohengá, mo siofi ʻene ngaahi ngaue kotoa ʻi he toiletí; mo e taimi 'oku ma'u ai 'e he'eku fie'ilo 'a e lelei ange 'o 'eku fakatupu. Ka neongo ia, ko ha tangata hange ko Queequeg 'oku 'ikai ke ke sio ki ai 'i he 'aho kotoa pe, ko ia mo hono ngaahi founga na'e mahu'inga lelei 'a e ta'e angamaheni fekau'aki mo ia.

Naʻá ne kamata teunga ʻi ʻolunga ʻaki ʻene tui hono hēkesi pivá, ko ha hēkesi māʻolunga ʻaupito, ʻi he tafaʻakí, pea hili iá —ʻo kei ʻikai ke ʻi ai hono talausese —naʻá ne tuli hake hono ngaahi sū. Ko e hā ʻi lalo ʻi he langí naʻá ne fai ai iá, ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo tala, ka ko ʻene ngaʻunu hokó ko ʻene laiki ia —ʻo ʻai ha ngaahi sū ʻi hono nimá, mo ha hēkesi —ʻi lalo he mohengá; ʻi he taimi, mei he ngaahi hanu mo e ngaahi faingataʻaʻia fakamālohi kehekehe, ne u fakaʻuhingaʻi naʻá ne ngāue mālohi ʻo boot ia; neongo ʻoku ʻikai ha lao ʻo e totonu kuó u fanongo ki aí, ʻoku fie maʻu ai ha tangata ke ne fakafūfū ʻi he taimi ʻokú ne tui ai hono ngaahi sū. Ka ko Queequeg, ‘okú ke sio ki ai, ko ha me‘amo‘ui ia ‘i he tu‘unga liliú—‘o ‘ikai ko ha anuhe pe ko ha manupuna. Naʻá ne sivilaise feʻunga pē ke fakahaaʻi ʻene ʻulungaanga ʻi tuʻá ʻi he ngaahi tōʻonga ngali kehe taha ʻe ala lavá. Naʻe teʻeki ke kakato ʻene akó. Ko ha tokotaha ako ʻi he ako māʻolungá ia. Kapau naʻe ʻikai ke ne kiʻi sivilaise, mahalo pē naʻe ʻikai ke ne mei fakahohaʻasi ia ʻaki ha ngaahi sū ʻi ha meʻa; ka ʻi he taimi ko iá, kapau naʻe ʻikai ke ne kei hoko ko ha manu fekai, naʻe ʻikai ke ne teitei misi ke hū ki lalo ʻi he mohengá ʻo ʻai kinaua. Fakaʻosí, naʻá ne ʻasi mai mo hono hēkesí ʻoku fuʻu ʻuʻulu mo holoki hifo ʻi hono matá, pea kamata ke ne ngungu mo luelue holo ʻi he lokí, ʻo hangē, ʻi he ʻikai ke ne fuʻu angamaheni ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi sū, ko hono ongo sū kiliʻi pulú ʻoku sūsū mo ngingila —mahalo naʻe ʻikai ke ngaohi ke ʻota pe —ka naʻe pinched mo fakamamahiʻi ia ʻi ha bitter go cold.

ʻI heʻene sio, he taimí ni, ʻoku ʻikai ha ngaahi puipui ki he matapaá, pea ʻoku fuʻu fāsiʻi ʻa e halá, naʻe fekauʻi ʻe he fale ʻi he tafaʻakí ha sio mahino ki he lokí, pea ʻi heʻene fakatokangaʻi lahi ange ʻa e fakatātā taʻefakaʻapaʻapa naʻe ngaohi ʻe Kuiki, ʻo ne ʻalu holo ʻo ʻikai ha meʻa kehe ka ko hono hēkesi mo e ngaahi sū; Naʻá ku kole ange kiate ia ʻi he lelei taha te u ala lavá, ke ne kiʻi fakavaveʻi ʻene falemālōloó, pea tautautefito ki heʻene hū ki hono ngaahi pantaloon ʻi he vave tahá. Naʻá ne talangofua, peá ne hoko atu leva ʻo fufulu ia. ‘I he taimi ko ia ‘i he pongipongí na‘e mei fufulu ‘e ha Kalisitiane pē hono fofongá; ka ko Queequeg, ‘i he‘eku ofo, na‘á ne fiemālie pē ‘i hono fakangatangata ‘ene ngaahi fufulu‘angá ki hono fatafatá, ongo nimá, mo e ongo nimá. Naʻá ne tui leva hono kofukofú, peá ne toʻo hake ha konga sopu fefeka ʻi he tēpile lotoloto ʻo e tuʻunga fufuluʻangá, ʻo ne lī ia ki he vaí pea kamata ke ne fakaʻauha hono fofongá. Naʻá ku siofi ke sio ki he feituʻu naʻá ne tauhi ai ʻene heletaá, ʻi he taimi naʻá ne toʻo ai ʻa e heletaá mei he tuliki ʻo e mohengá, ʻo ne fakaʻauha ʻa e sitoka ʻakau lōloá, ʻo ne vete ʻa e ʻulú, ʻo ne kiʻi whets ia ʻi hono sū, pea laka hake ki he konga ʻo e sioʻatá ʻi he ʻā, ʻo kamata haʻane toʻotoʻo mālohi, pe ʻoku lelei ange ʻene cheek. Fakakaukau au, Queequeg, ko hono faka'aonga'i 'eni 'o e cutlery lelei taha 'a Rogers mo ha tautea. Hili iá naʻá ku fifili ki he siʻisiʻi ange ʻi he ngāue ko ʻení ʻi heʻeku ʻiloʻi pe ko e hā ʻa e ukamea lelei ʻoku ngaohi ʻaki ʻa e ʻulu ʻo ha foʻi ʻakau, pea mo e fuʻu māsila ʻaupito ʻoku tauhi maʻu pē ʻa e ngaahi tafaʻaki hangatonu lōloá.

Naʻe vave pē ʻene aʻusia ʻa e toenga ʻo ʻene falemālōloó, peá ne laka pōlepole atu mei he lokí, ʻo ne takai ʻi hono kote manuki pailate lahí, mo fakaʻaongaʻi ʻene foʻi ʻaá ʻo hangē ha foʻi ʻakau ʻo e ʻeikitaú.





VAHE 5. Ko e kai pongipongi.

Naʻá ku muimui vave ki ai, pea ʻi heʻeku hifo ki he loki paá naʻá ku accosted ʻa e ʻea ʻoku malimali ʻo fuʻu fakafiefia. Naʻe ʻikai ke u fakaʻamua ha loto-kovi kiate ia, neongo naʻá ne skylarking mo au ʻo ʻikai siʻisiʻi ʻi he meʻa ʻo hoku kaungā mohé.

Kae kehe, ko ha kata lelei ko ha meʻa lelei mālohi, pea ʻoku fuʻu siʻisiʻi ha meʻa lelei; ko e lahi ange 'a e faka'ofa. Ko ia, kapau ʻoku ʻi ai ha tangata ʻe taha, ʻi hono tokotaha totonu pē ʻoʻoná, ʻokú ne lava ʻo fakaʻatā ha ngaahi meʻa ki ha fakakata lelei ki ha taha, ʻoua naʻá ne fakaholomui, ka tuku ke ne fakaʻatā fiefia ia ke ne fakamoleki mo fakamoleki ʻi he founga ko iá. Pea ko e tangata ʻoku ʻi ai haʻane meʻa ʻoku lahi ʻene fakakata fekauʻaki mo iá, fakapapauʻi ʻoku lahi ange ʻa e meʻa ʻi he tangata ko iá ʻi he meʻa ʻokú ke fakakaukau ki aí.

Naʻe fonu ʻa e loki paá he taimí ni ʻi he kau nofo nofoʻanga naʻa nau ʻalu hifo ʻi he pō kimuʻá, pea naʻe teʻeki ai ke u sio lelei kiate kinautolu. Ko e meimei kau tafola kotoa kinautolu; ko e kau hoa pule, mo e kau hoa ua, mo e kau hoa tolu, mo e kau tufunga tahi, mo e kau tufunga ukamea tahi, mo e kau tufunga ukamea tahi, mo e kau tufunga haʻapo, mo e kau tauhi vaka; ko ha kautaha lanu engeenga mo brawny, mo e ngaahi ʻuluʻulu bosky; ko ha seti taʻe-kosi, fulufulua, ʻoku nau tui kotoa ha ngaahi kote manuki ki he ngaahi kofu pongipongí.

Naʻe lava ke ke ʻiloʻi mahino ʻa e fuoloa ʻo e ʻi ʻuta ʻa e tokotaha takitaha. Ko e kouʻahe moʻui lelei ʻo e talavou ko ʻení ʻoku hangē ia ha foʻi pea kuo toʻo ʻi he laʻaá ʻi hono lanú, pea ʻe ngali namu meimei tatau mo e musky; he ʻikai lava ke ne tūʻuta ʻaho ʻe tolu mei heʻene folau ʻInitiá. Ko e tangata ko ia ʻi hono tafaʻakí ʻoku hā ngali maʻamaʻa ange ʻi ha ngaahi lanu siʻi; mahalo te ke pehē ʻoku ʻiate ia ha kiʻi ʻakau satin. 'I he lanu 'o e tolu 'oku kei lingers ha tawn fakatalopiki, ka 'oku ki'i fakahinehina withal; ‘ oku ‘ikai ha veiveiua kuó ne nofo ‘i ha ngaahi uike kakato ‘i ‘uta. Ka ko hai te ne lava ʻo fakahaaʻi ha foʻi fofonga hangē ko Queequeg? ‘a ia, ‘i hono ta‘ofi ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi lanu kehekehe, na‘e hā ngali hangē ha funga fakahihifo ‘o e ‘Anitesí, ke fakahaa‘i ‘i ha fakahokohoko ‘e taha, ‘o kehekehe ‘a e ngaahi ‘eá, ‘i he sone ki he sone.

“Kulupi, ho!” ko ʻeni ko e tangi ia ʻa e ʻeiki ʻapí, ʻo ne fakaava ha matapā, peá ma hū atu ʻo maʻu meʻatokoni pongipongi.

‘Oku nau pehē ko e kau tangata kuo nau sio ki he māmaní, ‘oku nau hoko ai ‘o mātu‘aki nonga ‘i he tō‘ongá, ‘o mātu‘aki ma‘u-kita ‘i he feohi‘angá. Kae kehe, ‘oku ‘ikai ma‘u pē: ko Ledyard, ko e tokotaha fononga lahi ‘i Niu ‘Ingilaní, mo Mungo Park, ko e tokotaha Sikotilaní; ʻi he kakai kotoa pē, naʻa nau maʻu ʻa e fakapapau siʻisiʻi taha ʻi he lokí. Ka mahalo ko e kolosi pē ʻi Saipēlia ʻi ha saliote toho ʻe he fanga kulií ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe fai ʻe Ledyard, pe ko e luelue tokotaha lōloa ʻi ha kete ʻataʻatā, ʻi he loto ʻo e kau negro ʻo ʻAfiliká, ʻa ia ko e fakakātoa ʻo e ngaahi fakaʻaliʻali masiva ʻa Mungo —ko e faʻahinga fononga ko ʻení, ʻoku ou pehē, mahalo ʻoku ʻikai ko e founga fakasōsiale māʻolunga taha ia ke aʻusia ai ha polish. Ka neongo ia, ‘i he konga lahi, ko e fa‘ahinga me‘a ko iá ‘oku totonu ke ma‘u ia ‘i ha feitu‘u pē.

Ko e ngaahi fakakaukau ko eni heni pe 'oku tupu ia mei he tukunga hili 'emau tangutu kotoa 'i he tepile, pea na'a ku teuteu ke fanongo ki ha ngaahi talanoa lelei fekau'aki mo e toutai tafola; naʻe ʻikai siʻisiʻi ʻeku ʻohovalé, ʻi he meimei tangata kotoa pē naʻá ne tauhi ha fakalongolongo loloto. Pea ʻikai ko ia pē, ka naʻa nau hā mā. ‘Io, na‘e ‘i heni ha seti ‘o e fanga kulii-tahí, ko e tokolahi ‘o kinautolú na‘a nau heka ‘i he ngaahi tafola lalahi ‘i he ngaahi tahi mā‘olungá ‘o ‘ikai ha‘anau ki‘i mā—ko e kau muli kakato kiate kinautolu—pea nau tau‘i kinautolu ‘o mate ‘o ‘ikai ke nau kuikui; pea neongo ia, naʻa nau tangutu heni ʻi ha tēpile kai pongipongi fakasōsiale —ʻoku nau uiuiʻi tatau kotoa, ʻoku nau manako fakakāinga kotoa —ʻo nau fesiofaki takai ʻo hangē ha sipi ʻo hangē kuo teʻeki ke nau mavahe mei he sio ki ha ngaahi ʻā sipi ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e Moʻunga Lanu Matalá. Ko ha sio fie‘ilo; ko e fanga pea mā ko ʻení, ko e kau tafola tau mā ko ʻení!

Ka ko e meʻa kia Kuiki —ko e hā, naʻe tangutu ʻa Kuiki ʻi ai ʻi honau lotolotongá —ʻi he ʻulu ʻo e tēpilé foki, naʻe hoko fakatuʻupakē pē ia; ‘oku momoko ‘o hangē ha ‘aisí. Ke fakapapau'i 'oku 'ikai te u lava 'o lea lahi ki hono fakatupu. Naʻe ʻikai mei lava ʻe hono tokotaha fakaʻofoʻofa lahi tahá ʻo fakatonuhiaʻi ʻi he loto-ʻofa ʻene ʻomi ʻene foʻi ʻaá ki he maʻu meʻatokoni pongipongi mo iá, pea fakaʻaongaʻi ia ʻi ai taʻe ʻi ai ha ouau; ʻo aʻu atu ki ʻolunga ʻi he tēpilé mo ia, ki he fakatuʻutāmaki vave ʻo e ngaahi ʻulu tokolahi, pea puke ʻa e ngaahi foʻi pulu ʻo fakahangatonu atu kiate ia. Ka naʻe pau pē naʻe fai fakamāfana ʻaupito ia ʻe ia, pea ʻoku ʻilo ʻe he tokotaha kotoa pē ʻi he fakafuofua ʻa e tokolahi taha ʻo e kakaí, ke fai ha meʻa pē ʻi he mālié ko hono fai ia ʻi he anga-fakaʻeiʻeikí.

He'ikai te tau lea heni ki he ngaahi me'a makehe kotoa pe 'o Queequeg; ʻa e founga naʻá ne fakaʻehiʻehi ai mei he kofí mo e ngaahi foʻi lōloa velá, pea fakaʻaongaʻi ʻene tokanga taʻe vahevahe ki he beefsteaks, ʻoku tātātaha ke fai. Naʻe feʻunga, ʻi he ʻosi ʻa e kai pongipongí naʻá ne mavahe ʻo hangē ko e toengá ki he loki ʻo e kakaí, ʻo ne tutu ʻene tomahawk-pipe, pea naʻá ne tangutu fakalongolongo pē ʻo digest mo ifi mo hono hēkesi taʻe-mavahevahé, ʻi heʻeku lele atu ʻo luelue.





VAHE 6. Koe Hala.

Kapau naʻá ku ofo ʻi he ʻuluaki taimi ʻi heʻeku sio ki ha tokotaha fakafoʻituitui ʻoku fuʻu kehe ʻaupito ʻo hangē ko Queequeg ʻoku takai holo ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e sōsaieti anga fakaʻapaʻapa ʻo ha kolo sivilaise, naʻe vave pē ʻa e mavahe ʻa e ofo ko iá ʻi heʻeku fuofua luelue ʻaho ʻi he ngaahi hala ʻo Niu Petifōtí.

'I he ngaahi hala lalahi ofi ki he ngaahi taulanga, 'e fa'a 'oatu 'e ha taulanga lahi ke vakai ki he queerest sio nondescripts mei he ngaahi konga muli. Na‘a mo e ‘i he ngaahi hala Broadway mo e hala Chestnut, ‘e faka‘ai‘ai ‘e he kau toutai Metiteleniané ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi ‘a e kau fine‘eiki ‘oku nau ilifiá. ʻOku ʻikai taʻeʻiloa ʻa e Hala Regent ki he kau Lascar mo e kau Malaisi; pea ʻi Bombay, ʻi he ʻApollo Green, kuo faʻa fakailifiaʻi ʻe he kau Yankees moʻuí ʻa e kau nofo fonuá. Ka 'oku ikuna'i 'e Niu Petifooti 'a e Hala Vai mo e Wapping kotoa pe. ‘I he ngaahi feitu‘u ko eni ‘oku lave ki ai fakamuimui tahá ‘okú ke sio pē ki he kau folaú; ka ‘i Niu Petifootí, ‘oku tu‘u ai ‘a e kau kai tangata mo‘oní ‘o talanoa ‘i he ngaahi tuliki ‘o e halá; savages fakahangatonu; ko e tokolahi ‘o kinautolu ‘oku nau kei ‘ave ‘i honau ngaahi huí ‘a e kakano ta‘emā‘oni‘oní. ʻOkú ne ʻai ha taha muli ke ne sio fakamamaʻu.

Ka, tukukehe 'a e kau Feegeeans, kau Tongatobooarrs, kau Erromanggoans, kau Pannangians, mo e kau Brighggians, pea, tukukehe 'a e ngaahi fakatata vao 'o e vaka toutai tafola 'a ia na'e ta'etokanga'i 'a e 'alu holo 'i he ngaahi hala, te ke sio ki ha ngaahi me'a kehe 'oku kei fie'ilo ange, 'oku pau 'oku fakakata ange. ʻOku aʻu mai fakauike mai ki he koló ni ha kau tangata lanu mata ʻe laui mano ʻo Veamoní mo Niu Hempsaí, ʻoku nau fieinua kotoa ki ha maʻuʻanga paʻanga mo e lāngilangi ʻi he toutaí. Ko e lahi tahá ‘oku nau kei talavou, ‘o e ngaahi fōtunga fefeka; fellows kuo nau taʻaki ʻa e ngaahi vaotaá, pea ʻoku nau fekumi he taimí ni ke tuku hifo ʻa e tokí pea puke ʻa e tao-tafola. Ko e tokolahi ‘oku nau lanumata ‘o hangē ko e Mo‘unga Lanumata na‘a nau ha‘u mei aí. ʻI ha ngaahi meʻa ʻe niʻihi te ke fakakaukau kiate kinautolu ka ko ha ngaahi houa siʻi pē ʻenau motuʻá. Sio ki ai! ko e chap ko ia 'oku strutting takai holo he tuliki. ‘Okú ne tui ha hēkesi piva mo ha kote hiku ‘o e fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i manupuná, ‘o nono‘o ‘aki ha fusi-folau mo ha heletā-‘a-‘aá. Ko e ha'u eni 'a e taha mo ha sou'-hihifo mo ha kofukofu pomu.

ʻOku ʻikai ha dandy ʻoku tupu ʻi he koló ʻe fakafehoanaki ki ha dandy ʻoku tupu ʻi he fonuá —ʻoku ou ʻuhinga ki ha dandy ʻoku tupu ʻi he fonuá —ko ha tangata ʻa ia, ʻi he ngaahi ʻaho ʻo e kulií, te ne tuʻusi hono ʻeka ʻe uá ʻi ha ngaahi kofunima kiliʻi pulu ʻi heʻene manavasiʻi ke ʻuliʻi hono ongo nimá. Ko 'eni ka 'ave ia 'e ha dandy fonua hange ko 'eni ki hono 'ulu ke fai ha ongoongo makehe, pea kau atu ki he toutai-tafola lahi, 'oku totonu ke ke sio ki he ngaahi me'a fakakata 'oku ne fai 'i he'ene a'u ki he taulanga. ‘I he‘ene lea‘aki ‘a ‘ene teunga tahí, ‘okú ne ‘ota ai ‘a e ngaahi pātene fafangu ki hono ngaahi kofukofú; ngaahi haʻi ki hono talausese kanivá. ʻA, masiva ʻa e Hay-Seed! ko e hā hono mamahi ʻe pa ʻa e ngaahi haʻi ko iá ʻi he ʻuluaki matangi ʻuʻulu, ʻi he taimi ʻe fakaʻauha ai koe, ngaahi haʻi, ngaahi pātene, mo e meʻa kotoa pē, ʻo hifo ki he ngutu ʻo e afā.

Kae ‘oua ‘e fakakaukau ko e kolo ‘iloa ko ení ‘oku ‘i ai pē ‘a e kau ha‘apo, kau kai tangata, mo e kau bumpkins ke fakahaa‘i ki he‘ene kau ‘a‘ahí. Hala'atā. Ko e kei Niu Petifootí ko ha feituʻu queer. Kapau naʻe ʻikai ko kimautolu kau tangata tafola, mahalo naʻe mei ʻi ha tuʻunga ʻohovale ʻa e konga kelekele ko iá ʻi he ʻahó ni ʻo hangē ko e matāfanga ʻo Lapalataá. Hange ko ia, 'Oku fe'unga 'a e ngaahi konga 'o hono fonua mui ke fakailifia'i ha taha, 'Oku nau sio 'oku fu'u hui. Ko e koló tonu mahalo ko e feitu‘u ‘ofeina taha ia ke nofo aí, ‘i he kotoa ‘o Niu ‘Ingilaní. Ko e fonua lolo ia, moʻoni feʻunga: ka ʻoku ʻikai hangē ko Kēnani; ko ha fonua, foki, ʻo e koane mo e uaine. ‘Oku ‘ikai lele ‘a e ngaahi halá ‘i he hu‘akaú; pea ‘i he fa‘ahita‘u failaú ‘oku ‘ikai te nau ‘ai ke nau ‘ai ‘a e fua‘imoa fo‘ou. Ka, neongo ‘a e me‘á ni, ‘oku ‘ikai ha feitu‘u ‘i ‘Amelika kotoa te ke ma‘u ai ha ngaahi fale hangē ha kau pēteliá; paʻake mo e ngoueʻanga ʻoku fakaʻofoʻofa ange, ʻi Niu Petifootí. Na‘a nau ha‘u mei fē? 'oku anga fefe hono to 'i he scoria scraggy tu'o taha ko 'eni 'o ha fonua?

ʻAlu ʻo sio fakamamaʻu ki he ngaahi foʻi ʻakau fakaʻilonga ukamea ʻoku takatakai ʻi he fale māʻolunga ʻi aí, pea ʻe tali hoʻo fehuʻí. ʻIo; ko e ngaahi fale toʻa mo e ngoue matalaʻiʻakau kotoa ko ʻení naʻe haʻu ia mei he ʻoseni ʻAtalanitikí, Pasifikí, mo e ʻInitiá. Ko e taha mo e meʻa kotoa, naʻe ʻohofi kinautolu ʻo toho hake ki heni mei he lalo tahí. ‘E lava ke fakahoko ‘e Herr Alexander ha me‘a fakaofo pehē?

‘I Niu Petifootí, ‘oku pehē ‘e he ngaahi tamaí ‘oku nau ‘oange ‘a e fanga tafola ki he ngaahi me‘a mali ki honau ngaahi ‘ofefiné, pea vahevahe honau ngaahi ‘ofefiné ‘aki ha fanga ki‘i porpoises si‘isi‘i ‘i he konga. Kuo pau ke ke ʻalu ki Niu Petifootí ʻo sio ki ha mali ngingila; he, ‘oku nau pehē, ‘oku ‘i ai ‘enau ngaahi tānaki‘anga lolo ‘i he fale kotoa pē, pea ‘i he pō kotoa pē ‘oku nau tutu ta‘efakakaukau‘i honau ngaahi lōloá ‘i he ngaahi te‘elango spermaceti.

ʻI he taimi māfaná, ʻoku melie ʻa e koló ke sio ki ai; fonu ‘i he ngaahi fu‘u maple lelei—ko e ngaahi hala lōloa ‘o e lanu mata mo e koula. Pea ‘i ‘Aokosi, ‘i he ‘eá ‘i he mā‘olungá, ‘oku ‘oatu ai ‘e he ngaahi fo‘i ‘akau-hoosi faka‘ofo‘ofa mo lahi, ‘o hangē ha kandelá, ki he tokotaha ‘oku fakalaka atu ‘a honau ngaahi fo‘i ‘akau hangatonu ‘oku faka‘au‘auhifo ‘o e ngaahi matala‘i‘akau kuo fakatahataha‘í. Ko ia ‘oku māfimafi-aoniu ‘a e ‘ātí; ‘a ia ‘i he ngaahi vahefonua lahi ‘o Niu Petifōtí kuó ne fakatupu ‘a e ngaahi fungavaka ngingila ‘o e ngaahi matala‘i‘akaú ‘i he ngaahi maka veve ta‘e‘aonga na‘e lī ki he tafa‘akí ‘i he ‘aho faka‘osi ‘o e fakatupú.

Pea ko e kau fefine 'o New Bedford, 'oku nau fisi 'o hange ko 'enau ngaahi lose kulokula 'anautolu. Ka ʻoku toki fisi pē ʻa e losé ʻi he faʻahitaʻu māfaná; ka ko e kānite lelei ‘o honau ongo kou‘ahé ‘oku ta‘engata ia ‘o hangē ko e maama ‘o e la‘aá ‘i he langi hono fitú. ʻI ha feituʻu kehe ʻoku fakatatau ki heʻenau matalaʻiʻakau ko iá, ʻoku ʻikai ke mou lava, tukukehe ʻi Sēlemi, ʻa ia ʻoku nau talamai ai kiate au ʻoku mānava ʻe he fānau fefine kei talavoú ʻa e musie peheé, ʻoku nanamuʻi kinautolu ʻe honau ngaahi ʻofaʻanga folaú ʻi ha ngaahi maile mei he matāfangá, ʻo hangē ʻoku nau fakaofiofi atu ki he ngaahi ʻoneʻone ʻo e Pulitaní.

ʻI he New Bedford tatau ko ʻení ʻoku tuʻu ai ha Falelotu ʻo e Kau Tafola, pea ʻoku tokosiʻi ʻa e kau toutai ʻoku nau loto-mamahi, ʻoku nau ʻalu nounou ki he ʻŌseni ʻInitiá pe Pasifikí, ʻoku ʻikai ke nau ʻaʻahi ʻi he Sāpaté ki he feituʻú. ʻOku ou fakapapauʻi naʻe ʻikai ke u fai ia.

ʻI heʻeku foki mei heʻeku fuofua luelue pongipongí, ne u toe lele atu ʻi he fekau makehe ko ʻení. Naʻe liliu ʻa e langí mei he momoko mahino mo laʻā, ki he ʻuha momoko fakaʻuli mo e kakapu. ʻI heʻeku takaiʻi au ʻi hoku kote fulufulua ʻo e tupenu ʻoku ui ko e kiliʻi peá, ne u tauʻi hoku halá mo e afā fefeká. ‘I he‘eku hū atú, na‘á ku ‘ilo ai ha ki‘i fakataha‘anga movetevete ‘o e kau toutaí, mo e ngaahi uaifi mo e kau uitou ‘o e kau toutaí. Naʻe pule ha fakalongolongo fakalongolongo, ʻo toki maumauʻi pē ʻi ha ngaahi taimi ʻe he kaila ʻa e matangí. Naʻe hangē naʻe tangutu fakataumuʻa ʻa e tokotaha lotu fakalongolongo takitaha ʻo mavahe mei he taha ʻe tahá, ʻo hangē ʻoku ʻinsula mo ʻikai lava ke fetuʻutaki ʻa e loto mamahi fakalongolongo takitaha. Naʻe teʻeki ke aʻu mai ʻa e faifekaú; pea naʻe tangutu maʻu ai ʻa e ngaahi motu fakalongolongo ko ʻeni ʻo e kau tangata mo e kau fafiné ʻo siofi ha ngaahi lauʻi maka māpele lahi, mo ha ngaahi ngataʻanga ʻuliʻuli, naʻe tā ʻi he ʻā ʻi he ongo tafaʻaki ʻo e tuʻunga malangá. ʻI heʻeku lulululu ʻa e ʻuha momokó mei hoku hēkesi mo e kote ʻoku ʻaisí, ne u tangutu ofi ki he matapaá, pea ʻi heʻeku tafoki ʻi he tafaʻakí ne u ʻohovale ʻi heʻeku sio kia Kuiki ʻoku ofi mai kiate au. ʻI hono uesia ʻe he fakaʻeiʻeiki ʻo e meʻa naʻe hokó, naʻe ʻi ai ha sio fakaofo ʻo e fie ʻilo taʻetui ʻi hono fofongá. Ko e manu fekai ko ʻení pē ʻa e tokotaha naʻe ʻi aí ʻa ia naʻe hangē naʻá ne fakatokangaʻi ʻeku hū ki aí; koeʻuhí he ko ia pē naʻe ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo lautohí, pea, ko ia ai, naʻe ʻikai ke ne lau ʻa e ngaahi tohi momoko ko ia ʻi he ʻā. Pe ko ha taha ʻo e kāinga ʻo e kau toutaí ʻa ia naʻe ʻasi honau hingoá ʻi aí ʻoku ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e fakatahaʻangá he taimí ni, naʻe ʻikai ke u ʻilo; ka ʻoku lahi fau ʻa e ngaahi fakatuʻutāmaki taʻe lekooti ʻi he toutaí, pea naʻe fuʻu mahino ʻaupito ʻa e tui ʻe ha kau fefine ʻe niʻihi naʻe ʻi aí ʻa e fofongá kapau ʻoku ʻikai ko e ngaahi tauhele ʻo ha ngaahi loto mamahi taʻetuku, ʻoku ou ongoʻi pau naʻe fakatahatahaʻi heni ʻi muʻa ʻiate aú ʻa kinautolu, ʻa ia ʻi honau loto taʻefakamoʻuí naʻe fakatupu ʻe he sio ki he ngaahi lauʻi papa fakapoʻuli ko iá ʻa e ngaahi lavea fakaʻofaʻia.

Oo! ʻakimoutolu ʻoku tokoto homou kau pekia kuo tanu ʻi he lalo mohuku lanu mata; ko hai ʻoku tuʻu ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi matalaʻiʻakaú ʻe lava ke ne pehē —ʻi heni, ʻoku tokoto ʻi heni hoku ʻofaʻangá; ʻoku ʻikai te mou ʻilo ʻae maumau ʻoku tupu ʻi he ngaahi fatafata hangē ko e ngaahi meʻá ni. Ko e ngaahi fo‘i ‘ata‘atā kona ē ‘i he ngaahi maka māpele ‘uli‘uli ko ia ‘oku ‘ikai ke nau ‘ufi‘ufi ha efuefu! He loto-mo‘ua ē ‘i he ngaahi tohi ta‘e-ue‘ia ko iá! Ko e hā ha ngaahi ʻataʻatā fakatupu mate mo ha ngaahi taʻefaitotonu taʻefakaʻatā ʻi he ngaahi laine ʻoku ngali ʻoku nau gnaw ʻi he Tui kotoa pē, pea fakafisingaʻi ʻa e ngaahi toetuʻu ki he ngaahi meʻamoʻui kuo nau ʻauha taʻeʻiai ha feituʻu ʻo ʻikai ha faʻitoka. Mahalo pē ʻe tuʻu ʻa e ngaahi lauʻi papa ko iá ʻi he ʻana ʻo ʻElefaná ʻo hangē ko ʻení.

Ko e hā ‘a e tohi kakai ‘o e ngaahi me‘amo‘uí, ‘oku kau ai ‘a e kau mate ‘o e fa‘ahinga ‘o e tangatá; ko e hā ʻoku pehē ai ʻe ha palōveepi fakamāmani lahi fekauʻaki mo kinautolú, ʻoku ʻikai ke nau tala ha ngaahi talanoa, neongo ʻoku lahi ange ʻa e ngaahi fakapulipuli ʻoku ʻi aí ʻi he Goodwin Sands; ʻoku anga fēfē ʻa e pehē ki hono hingoa naʻá ne mavahe ʻaneafi ki he māmani ʻe tahá, ʻoku tau fakamuʻomuʻa ai ha foʻi lea ʻoku fuʻu mahuʻinga mo taʻetui, pea neongo ia ʻoku ʻikai ke tau fakaʻatā ia kiate ia, kapau te ne folau pē ki he ʻInitia mamaʻo taha ʻo e māmani moʻui ko ʻení; ko e hā ʻoku totongi ai ʻe he ngaahi Kautaha Maluʻi Moʻuí ʻa e ngaahi totongi mate ki he kau taʻefaʻamaté; ʻi he palalisia taʻengata, taʻe-ueʻi, mo e fakatupu mate, taʻeʻamanaki, ka ʻoku tokoto ai ʻa ʻĀtama motuʻa naʻe pekia ʻi he senituli ʻe onongofulu kuohilí; ʻoku anga fēfē ʻetau kei fakafisi ke fakafiemālieʻi kitautolu he ko kinautolu ʻoku tau tauhi neongo ia ʻoku nau nofo ʻi ha fiefia taʻefaʻalaua; ko e hā ʻoku feinga lahi ai ʻa e kau moʻuí kotoa ke fakalongolongoʻi ʻa e kau pekia kotoa pē; ko ia ko e ongoongo pē ʻo ha tukituki ʻi ha fonualoto te ne fakailifiaʻi ha kolo kotoa. ʻOku ʻikai ke taʻeʻuhinga ʻa e ngaahi meʻá ni kotoa.

Ka ko Tui, ʻo hangē ha siaká, ʻokú ne kai ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi faʻitoká, pea naʻa mo e ngaahi veiveiua mate ko ʻení ʻokú ne tānaki ʻene ʻamanaki mahuʻinga tahá.

ʻOku ʻikai fie maʻu ke talamai, pe ko e hā e ngaahi ongo, ʻi he efiafi kimuʻa ʻi ha folau ʻi Nantucket, ne u vakai ki he ngaahi lauʻi maka māpele ko iá, pea ʻi he maama fakapoʻuli ʻo e ʻaho fakapoʻuli mo fakaloloma ko iá ne u lau ai ʻa e ikuʻanga ʻo e kau tangata tafola naʻa nau ʻalu kimuʻa ʻiate aú. ‘Io, ‘Isime‘eli, ‘e hoko nai ‘a e iku‘anga tatau kiate koe. Ka ʻi ha faʻahinga founga naʻá ku toe tupu ʻo fiefia. Incements fakafiefia ke heka, faingamalie lelei ki he hiki hake, 'oku ngali-'io, 'e ngaohi au 'e ha vaka sitovu ko ha ta'efa'amate 'e he brevet. ʻIo, ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e mate ʻi he pisinisi ko ʻeni ʻo e toutai tafola —ko ha fusi moveuveu vave taʻe-lea ʻo ha tangata ki ʻItāniti. Kae fēfē leva? Methinks kuo tau fu'u fehalaaki lahi 'i he me'a ko 'eni 'o e Mo'ui mo e Mate. Methinks ko e me'a 'oku nau ui ko hoku 'ata heni 'i mamani ko hoku substance mo'oni. Methinks 'i he vakai ki he ngaahi me'a fakalaumalie, 'oku tau fu'u hange ha 'osita 'oku nau siofi 'a e la'a 'i he vai, pea fakakaukau ko e vai matolu ko e manifinifi taha ia 'o e 'ea. Methinks hoku sino ka ko e lees 'o hoku lelei ange. Ko hono moʻoní toʻo hoku sinó ko hai te ne, toʻo ia ʻoku ou pehē, ʻoku ʻikai ko au. Pea ko ia ai ko e ngaahi fakafiefia ʻe tolu maʻa Nantucket; pea ha'u ha vaka sitofu mo e sino sitofu 'i he taimi te nau loto ki ai, he stave hoku laumalie, 'Oku 'ikai lava 'e Jove tonu.





VAHE 8. Koe Tu'unga Malanga.

Naʻe teʻeki fuʻu fuoloa ʻeku tangutu kimuʻa pea toki hū mai ha tangata ʻo ha faʻahinga mālohi fakaʻapaʻapa pau; ʻi he lele mai pē ʻa e matapā naʻe ʻohofi ʻe he matangí ʻo foki mai ʻi hono fakahū iá, ko ha sio fakaʻapaʻapa vave kiate ia ʻe he fakatahaʻangá kotoa, naʻe fakamoʻoniʻi feʻunga ai ko e motuʻa lelei ko ʻení ʻa e faifekaú. ‘Io, ko e Tamai Mapple ‘iloa ia, ‘a ia na‘e ui pehē ‘e he kau tangata tafola, ‘a ia na‘á ne ‘i honau lotolotongá ko ha tokotaha manakoa lahi ‘aupito. Na‘á ne hoko ko ha tangata toutai mo ha tokotaha tā fo‘i ‘akau ‘i he‘ene kei talavoú, ka ‘i he ngaahi ta‘u lahi kuohilí na‘á ne fakatapui ‘ene mo‘uí ki he ngāue fakafaifekaú. ʻI he taimi ʻoku ou tohi atu ai he taimí ni, naʻe ʻi he faʻahitaʻu momoko fefeka ʻo ha taʻu motuʻa moʻui lelei ʻa e Tamai Mapple; ko e faʻahinga motuʻa ko ia ʻoku ngali fakatahaʻi ia ki ha talavou ʻoku fisi hono uá, he ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi movete kotoa ʻo hono ngaahi foʻi ʻuʻuú, naʻe ulo atu ha ngaahi ngingila māmālie pau ʻo ha fisi ʻoku toki tupulaki —ko e lanu mata ʻo e faʻahitaʻu failaú ʻoku sio ki tuʻa naʻa mo e lalo sinou ʻo Fepuelí. Naʻe ʻikai ha taha naʻá ne fanongo kimuʻa ki hono hisitōliá, ʻe lava ke ne fuofua sio kia Patele Mapple ʻo ʻikai haʻane mahuʻingaʻia lahi taha, koeʻuhí he naʻe ʻi ai ha ngaahi meʻa makehe fakakalake pau naʻe fakahū fekauʻaki mo ia, ʻoku lava ke tukuakiʻi ki he moʻui fakatahi fakafiefia ko ia naʻá ne tataki. ʻI heʻene hū maí ne u fakatokangaʻi naʻe ʻikai ke ne ʻave ha fakamalu, pea ʻoku pau pē naʻe ʻikai ke ne haʻu ʻi heʻene salioté, he naʻe lele hifo hono hēkesi tāpauliní ʻi he ʻuha momoko ʻoku vaia, pea naʻe hangē naʻe meimei toho ia ʻe hono kote tupenu pailate lahí ki he falikí ʻi he mamafa ʻo e vai naʻá ne mimisí. Kae kehe, naʻe toʻo takitaha ʻa e hēkesí mo e kote mo e ngaahi sū ʻi ʻolungá, pea tautau ʻi ha kiʻi feituʻu ʻi ha tuliki ofi mai; ʻi heʻene ʻalu fakalongolongo atu ki he tuʻunga malangá, ʻi heʻene teunga ʻi ha suti taau.

Hangē ko e lahi taha ʻo e ngaahi tuʻunga malanga motuʻá, ko ha sitepu māʻolunga ʻaupito ia, pea koeʻuhí ko ha sitepu angamaheni ki ha māʻolunga pehē, ʻe, ʻi hono tuliki lōloa mo e falikí, ʻe fakasiʻisiʻi fakamātoato ʻa e ʻēlia ʻoku ʻosi siʻisiʻi ʻo e falelotú, naʻe ngali, naʻe ngāue ʻa e ʻākiteki ʻi he fakahinohino ʻa e Tamai Mapple, pea ʻosi ʻa e tuʻunga malangá ʻaki ʻa e substitucular a sipende, ladder, hangē ko e ngaahi me‘a ‘oku ngāue‘aki ki hono heka ha vaka mei ha vaka ‘i tahi. Naʻe ʻosi ʻoange ʻe he uaifi ʻo ha kapiteni toutai tafola ki he falelotú ha hoa fakaʻofoʻofa ʻo e ngaahi maea tangata kulokula worsted ki he lueʻanga ko ʻení, ʻa ia, ʻi heʻene ʻulu fakaʻofoʻofá, pea ʻoku ʻuliʻi ʻaki ha lanu mahogany, ko e meʻa fakaʻofoʻofá kotoa, ʻi heʻene fakakaukau ki he faʻahinga falelotú, naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke kovi hono ifo. ʻI heʻene tuʻu ʻi ha kiʻi taimi ʻi he vaʻe ʻo e lēlué, pea ʻi heʻene ongo nimá ʻo puke ʻa e ngaahi foʻi tuʻuʻanga teuteu ʻo e ngaahi maea-tangatá, naʻe sio hake ʻa e Tamai Mapple ki ʻolungá, pea ʻi haʻane pōtoʻi moʻoni ʻo hangē ha kau toutaí ka ʻoku kei fakaʻapaʻapa pē, ʻo ne heka hake ʻi he sitepú ʻo hangē ʻokú ne ʻalu hake ʻi he tumutumu ʻo ʻene vaká.

Ko e ngaahi konga tu‘u hangatonu ‘o e lue‘anga tafa‘aki ko ení, ‘o hangē ko ia ‘oku fa‘a hoko ‘i he ngaahi lue‘anga ta‘alo, na‘e ngaohi ia mei he maea na‘e ‘ufi‘ufi ‘aki ‘a e tupenu, ko e ngaahi takai pē na‘e ngaohi ia mei he ‘akau, ko ia ‘i he sitepu kotoa pē na‘e ‘i ai ha hui. ʻI heʻeku fuofua sio ki he tuʻunga malangá, naʻe ʻikai ke hao meiate au neongo ʻene faingofua ki ha vaká, ka naʻe ngali ʻikai fie maʻu ʻa e ngaahi hui ko ʻeni ʻi he tuʻunga lolotongá. He naʻe ʻikai ke u mateuteu ke sio kia Patele Mapple hili ʻene maʻu ʻa e māʻolungá, ʻo ne tafoki māmālie, pea punou ʻi he tuʻunga malangá, ʻo ne toho ʻosi fakakaukauʻi ʻa e lueʻangá ʻi he sitepu ki he sitepu, kae ʻoua kuo fakatoka kotoa ʻi loto, ʻo tuku ai ia ʻo ʻikai lava ke ʻohofi ia ʻi hono kiʻi Kuepekí.

Naʻá ku fakalaulauloto ʻi ha taimi ʻo ʻikai ke mahino kakato kiate au ʻa e ʻuhinga ʻo e meʻá ni. Naʻe fiefia ʻa e Tamai Mapple ʻi ha ongoongo lahi ʻo e fakamātoato mo e māʻoniʻoní, ʻo ʻikai ke u lava ʻo mahaloʻi ia ʻokú ne fakaʻaiʻai ʻa e ongoongokovi ʻaki ha ngaahi founga kākā noaʻia ʻo e siteisí. ʻIkai, naʻá ku fakakaukau, kuo pau ke ʻi ai ha ʻuhinga fakapotopoto ki he meʻá ni; ‘ikai ngata aí, kuo pau ke ne fakatātaa‘i ha me‘a ‘oku ‘ikai ke hā mai. Ko ia ai, ‘e lava ke pehē ‘i he ngāue ko ia ‘o e fakamavahe‘i fakaesinó, ‘okú ne fakahaa‘i ai ‘a ‘ene mavahe fakalaumālie ki he taimí, mei he ngaahi ha‘i mo e ngaahi fehokotaki‘anga fakamāmani kotoa pē ‘i tu‘á? ʻIo, he ʻi hono fakafonu ʻaki ʻa e kakanoʻi manu mo e uaine ʻo e folofolá, ki he tangata faivelenga ʻo e ʻOtuá, ko e tuʻunga malangá ni, ʻoku ou vakai, ko ha kolotau ʻoku ʻiate ia pē —ko ha Ehrenbreitstein māʻolunga, mo ha vaitupu vai taʻengata ʻi loto ʻi he ngaahi ʻā.

Ka naʻe ʻikai ko e fungavaka tafaʻakí pē ʻa e meʻa ngali kehe ʻo e feituʻú, naʻe nō mei he ngaahi fononga tahi kimuʻa ʻa e faifekaú. ʻI he vahaʻa ʻo e ngaahi cenotaphs māpelu ʻi he ongo nima ʻo e tuʻunga malangá, naʻe teuteuʻi ʻa e ʻā naʻá ne faʻu hono tuʻá ʻaki ha tā valivali lahi ʻokú ne fakafofongaʻi ha vaka toʻa ʻoku taaʻi ha afā fakalilifu ʻi ha matāfanga lee ʻo e ngaahi maka ʻuliʻulí mo e ngaahi vakapuna sinou. Ka ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he scud lele mo e ngaahi ʻao fakapoʻuli ʻoku takai holo, naʻe ʻalu hake ha kiʻi motu ʻo e maama ʻo e laʻaá, ʻa ia naʻe ulo mai mei ai ha fofonga ʻo ha ʻāngelo; pea naʻe lilingi ʻe he fofonga ngingila ko ʻení ha foʻi ngingila makehe ʻi he fungavaka ʻo e vaká kuo lī, ko ha meʻa hangē ko e peleti siliva ko ia ʻoku fakahū he taimí ni ki he laupapa ʻo e Ikuná ʻa ia naʻe tō ai ʻa Nelesoní. “ʻOiauē, vaka fakaʻeiʻeiki,” naʻe hangē naʻe pehē ʻe he ʻāngeló, “taʻi atu, taaʻi atu, ʻa e vaka fakaʻeiʻeiki, pea ke ke ʻave ʻa e taumuʻa fefeka: he vakai, ʻoku ʻasi mai ʻa e laʻaá, ʻoku tafe atu ʻa e ngaahi ʻaó —ʻoku ofi mai ʻa e lanu kulokula nonga tahá.”

Pea naʻe ʻikai foki ke taʻe ʻi ai ha fakaʻilonga ʻo e sea-taste tatau naʻá ne aʻusia ʻa e lēlué mo e fakatātaá ʻi he tuʻunga malangá tonu. Ko hono tafa‘aki ki mu‘a na‘e ‘i ai ‘a e ngaahi pānelí na‘e hangē ia ha ngaahi kaufana bluff ‘o ha vaka, pea na‘e tu‘u ‘a e Tohitapu Mā‘oni‘oní ‘i ha konga takainga tohi na‘e ‘asi hake, ‘a ia na‘e fa‘u ‘o fakatatau ki he ngutu ‘o ha vaka ‘oku ‘ulu fiolo.

Ko e hā ha meʻa ʻe toe fonu ange ʻi he ʻuhingá?—he ko e tuʻunga malangá ʻoku hoko maʻu pē ia ko e konga tuʻukimuʻa taha ʻo e māmani ko ʻení; ko e toenga kotoa ‘oku ha‘u ‘i hono tu‘á; ʻoku taki ʻe he tuʻunga malangá ʻa e māmaní. Mei ai ko e matangi ‘o e houhau vave ‘a e ‘Otua ‘oku ‘uluaki fakamatala’i, pea kuo pau ke fua ‘e he kaufana ‘a e mamafa vave taha. Mei ai ko e 'Otua 'o e ngaahi matangi lelei pe kovi 'oku 'uluaki ui ki he ngaahi matangi lelei. ‘Io, ko e māmaní ko ha vaka ‘i hono ‘alu ki tu‘á, pea ‘oku ‘ikai ko ha folau kakato; pea ko e tuʻunga malangá ko hono muʻá ia.





VAHE 9. Koe Malanga.

Naʻe tuʻu hake ʻa e Tamai Mapple, pea ʻi ha leʻo angavaivai ʻo e mafai taʻefakaʻeiʻeikí naʻá ne fekauʻi ʻa e kakai naʻe moveteveté ke nau fakanounou. “Hala ʻo e tafaʻaki toʻomataʻú, ʻi ai! tafaʻaki mamaʻo ki he tafaʻaki toʻomataʻú —hala ʻo e tafaʻaki toʻomataʻú! Ngaahi vaka lotoloto! vaka lotoloto!”

Naʻe ʻi ai ha longoaʻa maʻulalo ʻo e ngaahi sū tahi mamafá ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi tangutuʻangá, mo ha kei kiʻi feʻauʻauhi ʻo e ngaahi sū ʻo e kakai fefiné, pea naʻe toe fakalongolongo kotoa, pea sio ʻa e mata kotoa pē ki he tokotaha malangá.

Naʻá ne kiʻi tuʻu; peá ne tūʻulutui leva ʻi he ngaahi kaufana ʻo e tuʻunga malangá, ʻo ne fusi hono ongo nima lanu engeenga lalahí ʻi hono fatafatá, ʻo hiki hake hono ongo mata kuo moʻuí, ʻo ne fai ha lotu ʻoku fuʻu loloto ʻene lotu ʻo hangē naʻá ne tūʻulutui mo lotu ʻi he lalo tahí.

Naʻe fakaʻosi ʻeni, ʻi ha ngaahi leʻo fakaʻeiʻeiki lōloa, ʻo hangē ko e hokohoko atu ʻa e tā ʻo ha fafangu ʻi ha vaka ʻoku ʻalu hifo ʻi tahi ʻi ha kakapu —ʻi ha ngaahi leʻo pehe naʻá ne kamata lau ai ʻa e himi ko ʻení; ka ʻi heʻene liliu ʻene founga ki he ngaahi foʻi hiva fakaʻosí, naʻá ne puna mai ʻaki ha fiefia mo e fiefia ʻoku ʻohovale —

“Ko e ngaahi hui mo e ngaahi fakailifia ‘i he tafola, .
Naʻe ʻātakaiʻi au ʻe ha fakapoʻuli fakaloloma,
Lolotonga ‘a e tafe mai ‘a e ngaahi peau la‘ā kotoa ‘a e ‘Otuá, .
Pea hiki hake au ʻo loloto hifo ki he malaʻiá.

“Na‘á ku sio ki he maw fakaava ‘o helí, .
Mo e ngaahi mamahi mo e ngaahi mamahi taʻe ngata ʻi ai;
ʻA ia ʻoku ʻikai ha taha ka ko kinautolu pē ʻoku nau ongoʻí te nau lava ʻo tala—
ʻOiauē, naʻá ku tō ki he loto-moʻuá.

“ʻI he faingataʻaʻia ʻuliʻulí, naʻá ku ui ki hoku ʻOtuá, .
ʻI he taimi naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo tui ai kiate ia ʻeku,
Naʻá ne punou hono telingá ki heʻeku ngaahi lāungá—
ʻIkai toe ʻi ai ha tafola naʻá ku fakaʻatā.

“Na‘á ne lele vave mai ki hoku fakafiemālié, .
Hange ko ha tolofini ngingila 'oku fua;
Fakalilifu, ka ʻoku ngingila, ʻo hangē ko e ulo ʻa e ʻuhilá .
Ko e fofonga 'o hoku 'Otua Fakamo'ui.

“ʻE lekooti ʻeku hiva ʻo taʻengata .
Ko e houa fakalilifu ko ia, fakafiefia ko ia;
‘Oku ou ‘atu ‘a e lāngilangí ki hoku ‘Otuá, .
ʻOkú Ne ʻaloʻofa kotoa pē mo e mālohí.”

Naʻe meimei kau kotoa ʻi hono hivaʻi ʻo e himi ko ʻení, ʻa ia naʻe puna māʻolunga ange ʻi he ʻuʻulu ʻa e matangí. Naʻe hoko atu ai ha kiʻi tuʻu nounou; na‘e fulihi māmālie ‘e he tokotaha malangá ‘a e ngaahi lau‘i Tohi Tapú, pea faifai pē, ‘o ne fusi hifo hono nimá ‘i he peesi totonú, ‘o ne pehē: “Kau kaungā vaka ‘ofeina, puke ‘a e veesi faka‘osi ‘o e ‘uluaki vahe ‘o Sioná —‘Pea na‘e teuteu ‘e he ‘Otuá ha ika lahi ke ne folo ‘a Siona.’”

Naʻá ne tō ʻo tō mamaʻo meiate ia ʻi ha kiʻi taimi; peá ne toe hiki hake hono fofongá kiate kinautolú, naʻá ne fakahaaʻi ha fiefia loloto ʻi hono matá, ʻi heʻene kaila ʻi ha loto vēkeveke fakalangi,—“Ka ʻoiauē, kaungā vaka! ʻi he nima toʻomataʻu ʻo e malaʻia kotoa pē, ʻoku ʻi ai ha fiefia pau; pea ʻoku māʻolunga ange ʻa e tumutumu ʻo e fiefia ko iá, ʻi he lalo ʻo e malaʻiá ʻoku loloto ange ʻa e De ketruck ʻoku loloto ange ʻa e- mama'o, mama'o ki 'olunga, mo e fiefia 'i loto —'a ia 'oku ne fakafepaki'i 'a e ngaahi 'otua polepole mo e kau commodores 'o e mamani ko 'eni, 'oku ne tu'u ma'u pe 'a hono 'ata'ataa 'o'ona 'a ia 'oku kei poupou'i ia 'e hono ngaahi nima malohi, 'i he taimi kuo 'alu hifo ai 'a e vaka 'o e mamani kākā ko 'eni 'i lalo 'iate ia Ko e fiefia 'oku 'ikai kiate ia, 'a ia 'oku 'ikai ke ne foaki 'a e sinstro mo e plulls 'i he,. 'i lalo 'i he ngaahi kofu 'o e kau Senatoa mo e kau Fakamaau. ke fakatokoto hifo ia, ʻe lava ke ne pehē ʻaki ʻene mānava fakaʻosí —ʻE Tamai! —ʻoku ʻiloʻi tefito kiate au ʻe Hoʻo tokotokó —ʻoku matelie pe taʻefaʻamate, ʻi heni kuó u feinga ke hoko ko Hoʻo meʻa, ʻo lahi ange ʻi heʻeku hoko ko ha meʻa ʻaʻaku ʻoʻoná: ʻOku ʻikai ko ha meʻa ʻeni: ʻOku ou tuku ʻa e moʻui ʻa e tangatá kiate Koe;

Naʻe ʻikai ke ne toe lea ʻaki, ka naʻá ne lulululu māmālie ha tāpuaki, ʻo ne ʻufiʻufi hono fofongá ʻaki hono ongo nimá, pea naʻá ne kei tūʻulutui pē, kae ʻoua kuo mavahe ʻa e kakaí kotoa, pea naʻá ne kei tokotaha pē ʻi he feituʻú.
