He ʻi he ngaahi saati ʻo e ngaahi ʻoseni kotoa ʻe fā ʻi muʻa aí, naʻe filo ʻe ʻĒhapi ha foʻi ʻataʻatā mo e ngaahi ʻauʻauha, ʻi ha fakakaukau ki hono fakahoko pau ange ʻo e fakakaukau monomaniac ko ia ʻo hono laumālié.

Naʻe fakatahaʻi ʻa e taimi mo e feituʻu pau ko iá ʻi he kupuʻi lea fakatekinikale ʻe taha —ko e Faʻahitaʻu-ʻi he-Lainé.
