VAHE 124. Ko e Tuitui.

ʻI he pongipongi hono hokó naʻe takai holo ʻa e tahi ʻoku teʻeki ke holo ʻi ha ngaahi peau māmālie lōloa ʻo e fuʻu ʻakau lahi, pea ʻi heʻene feinga ʻi he hala ʻo e Pequod ʻoku ngungu, naʻá ne tekeʻi ia ʻo hangē ha ngaahi nima ʻo e kau tangata lalahí ʻoku mafola atu. Naʻe lahi ʻaupito ʻa e havili mālohi mo taʻe-fakaʻauʻauhifó, ʻo hangē ʻa e langí mo e ʻeá ko ha ngaahi folau ʻeveʻeva lahi ʻi tuʻa; naʻe boom ʻa e māmaní kotoa ʻi he ʻao ʻo e matangí. ʻI hono fakalongolongoʻi ʻe he maama kakato ʻo e pongipongí, naʻe ʻiloʻi pē ʻa e laʻā taʻehāmaí ʻi he mafola ʻa e mālohi ʻo hono feituʻú; ʻa ia naʻe ngaʻunu atu ai ʻene ngaahi huelo ʻo e bayonet ʻi ha ngaahi tuʻunga. Ko e ngaahi faka‘ilonga, ‘o hangē ko e ngaahi tu‘i mo e ngaahi kuini Pāpilone kuo fakakalauni, na‘a nau pule‘i ‘a e me‘a kotoa pē. Na‘e hangē ‘a e tahí ha ipu koula kuo fakamolū, ‘a ia ‘oku puna pupula ‘i he māmá mo e velá.

‘I hono tauhi fuoloa ha fakalongolongo faka‘ofo‘ofá, na‘e tu‘u mavahe ‘a ‘Ēhapi; pea ko e taimi kotoa pē naʻe tuku hifo ai ʻe he vaka teté ʻene bowsprit, naʻá ne tafoki ʻo sio ki he huelo ngingila ʻo e laʻaá ʻoku fakatupu ʻi muʻá; pea ʻi heʻene nofo loloto ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e muiʻi vaká, naʻá ne tafoki ki mui, ʻo ne sio ki he feituʻu ʻo e laʻaá ʻi mui, pea mo e founga ʻoku fefiofi ai ʻa e ngaahi huelo kulokula tatau mo ʻene ʻā taʻe-afe.

"Ha, ha, ko hoku vaka! 'e lava ke 'ave koe he taimi ni ki he saliote tahi 'o e la'a. Ho, ho! 'a kimoutolu kotoa pe 'a e ngaahi pule'anga 'i mu'a 'i hoku mu'a, 'Oku ou 'omi 'a e la'a kiate kimoutolu! 'Ioke 'i he ngaahi peau 'oku toe mama'o ange; hallo! ha tandem, 'Oku ou faka'uli 'a e tahi!"

Ka naʻe fakafokifā pē hono taʻofi ia ʻe ha ngaahi fakakaukau fakafepaki, naʻá ne fakavavevave atu ki he taumuʻá, ʻo ne fie maʻu fakaʻohovale ʻa e founga ʻoku ʻalu ai ʻa e vaká.

“Hahake-tonga-hahake, tangataʻeiki,” ko e lea ia ʻa e tangata fakaʻuli ilifiá.

“ʻOkú ke loi!” ‘o ne taa‘i ia ‘aki hono nima kuo puké. “ʻOku fakahangatonu ki he Hahaké ʻi he houa ko ʻeni ʻo e pongipongí, pea ʻoku fakahangatonu ʻa e laʻaá?”

ʻI he meʻa ni naʻe puputuʻu ai ʻae laumālie kotoa pē; he ko e meʻa fakaofo naʻe toki fakatokangaʻi ʻe ʻĒhapí ʻi he taimi ko iá naʻe hao taʻefakaʻuhingaʻi ia mei he tokotaha kotoa pē; ka ko hono palpableness fakakuihi ‘aupitó kuo pau pē ko e tupu‘angá ia.

‘I hono lī hono ‘ulú ‘i he vaeua‘anga ‘o e binnacle, na‘e ma‘u ai ‘e ‘Ēhapi ha ki‘i sio ‘e taha ki he ngaahi kāpasá; naʻe tō māmālie hono nima naʻe hiki haké; ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti naʻá ne meimei hangē naʻá ne tetetete. Naʻe tuʻu ʻi hono tuʻá ʻa Starbuck ʻo sio, pea vakai! naʻe tuhu ʻa e ongo kāpasá ki he Hahaké, pea naʻe ʻalu taʻehalaia ʻa e Pequod ki he Hihifó.

Ka kimuʻa pea lava ʻa e ʻuluaki fakatokanga vaó ʻo ʻalu atu ʻi muli ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e kau ngāué, naʻe kaila ʻa e motuʻá mo ha kata fefeka, “ʻOku ou maʻu ia! Kuo hoko ia kimuʻa. Misa Sitāpaki, naʻe liliu ʻe he mana ʻaneafí ʻemau ngaahi kāpasá —ko ia pē. Kuó ke fanongo kimuʻa he taimí ni ki ha meʻa pehē, ʻoku ou toʻo ia.”

“ʻIo; ka kuo teʻeki ai ke hoko ia kiate au kimuʻa, tangataʻeiki,” ko e lea ia ʻa e hoa lanu engeengá, ʻi he loto-mamahi.

‘I heni, kuo pau ke fiema‘u ke lea‘aki, ko e ngaahi fakatu‘utāmaki hangē ko ení kuo laka hake ‘i he me‘a ‘e taha kuo hoko ki he ngaahi vaka ‘i he ngaahi afā fakamālohi. Ko e ivi fakamakinito, ‘o hangē ko ia ‘oku fakatupulekina ‘i he tuitui ‘a e tangata toutaí, ‘oku hangē ko ia ‘oku ‘ilo‘i kotoa, ‘oku tefito ‘ene taha mo e ‘uhila ‘oku sio ki ai ‘i he langí; ko ia ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu ofo lahi ʻi he hoko ʻa e ngaahi meʻa peheé. Ko e ngaahi tu‘unga ‘a ia kuo taa‘i mo‘oni ai ‘e he ‘uhilá ‘a e vaká, koe‘uhi ke ne taa‘i hifo ‘a e ni‘ihi ‘o e ngaahi spars mo e rigging, ko e ola ‘i he tuituí kuo kei fakatupu mate ange ia ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi; ʻoku fakaʻauha kotoa hono ʻulungaanga lelei maka kavengá, ko ia naʻe ʻikai toe ʻaonga ange ʻa e ukamea fakamakinito kimuʻá ʻi ha tuitui lalanga ʻa ha uaifi motuʻa. Ka ʻi he ongo meʻá ni, ʻoku ʻikai ke toe maʻu ʻe he tuituí, ʻiate ia pē, ʻa e ʻuluaki ʻulungaanga lelei naʻe maumauʻi pe mole peheé; pea kapau ʻe uesia ʻa e ngaahi kāpasa binnacle, ʻe aʻu ʻa e ikuʻanga tatau ki he niʻihi kehe kotoa pē ʻe ala ʻi he vaká; naʻe aʻu ʻo fakahū ʻa e taha ʻi lalo tahá ki he kelson.

ʻI heʻene tuʻu fakakaukauʻi ʻi muʻa ʻi he binnacle, mo siofi ʻa e ngaahi kāpasa transpointed, naʻe toʻo ʻe he motuʻá, ʻaki ʻa e māsila ʻo hono nima kuo mafao atu, ʻa e ʻasi tonu ʻo e laʻaá, pea fiemālie ʻi he fakafetongi tonu ʻo e ngaahi tuituí, naʻá ne kaila atu ʻene ngaahi tuʻutuʻuni ke liliu ʻa e hala ʻo e vaká ʻo fakatatau ki ai. Naʻe fefeka ʻa e ngaahi ʻiate; pea naʻe toe lī ʻe he Pequod ʻene ngaahi kaufana taʻemanavaheé ki he matangi fakafepakí, he ko e taha ʻoku pehē ʻoku totonú naʻá ne toki fakaʻaiʻai pē ia.

Lolotonga iá, neongo pe ko e hā ʻene ngaahi fakakaukau fakapulipuli ʻaʻaná, naʻe ʻikai ke lea ʻa Starbuck ʻi ha meʻa, ka naʻá ne tuku fakalongolongo pē ʻa e ngaahi tuʻutuʻuni kotoa pē naʻe fie maʻú; lolotonga ia ko Stubb mo Flask —‘a ia ‘i ha ki‘i tu‘unga na‘e hā ngali ‘i he taimi ko iá na‘á na vahevahe ‘ene ngaahi ongó —na‘á na tali ta‘elāunga tatau pē. Ko e meʻa ki he kau tangatá, neongo naʻe ngungu maʻulalo ha niʻihi ʻo kinautolu, ka naʻe lahi ange ʻenau manavahē kia ʻĒhapí ʻi heʻenau manavahē kia Fate. Kae hangē ko ia na‘e hoko ki mu‘á, na‘e meimei ‘ikai ke maongo kakato ‘a e kau tangata fana fakapanganí; pe kapau na‘e maongo, na‘e fai pē ia ‘i ha fa‘ahinga mākiniti pau na‘e fana‘i ki honau loto anga-‘ofá mei he loto ta‘efe‘unga ‘o ‘Ēhapí.

Mo ha taimi naʻe luelue ai ʻa e motuʻá ʻi he funga vakapuná ʻi ha ngaahi fakakaukau fakatakamilo. Ka ʻi heʻene faingamālie ke hē mo hono muli ʻaivolí, naʻá ne sio ki he ngaahi paipa sioʻata kopa kuo holoki ʻo e kuata naʻá ne maʻu ʻi he ʻaho kimuʻá ʻoku nau lele atu ki he funga vaká.

"Ko koe masiva, pōlepole sio langi mo e pailate ʻo e laʻaá! ʻaneafi naʻá ku fakaʻauha koe, pea ʻi he ʻahó ni ʻe fain ʻa e ngaahi kāpasá ke nau fakaʻauha au. Ko ia, ko ia. Ka ʻoku kei ʻeiki ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi he maka kavenga levolo. Misa Sitāpaki —ko ha tao taʻe ʻi ai hano pou; ko ha top-maul, pea ko e siʻisiʻi taha ʻo e ngaahi saʻaá!"

Mahalo pē, ko e tokoni ki he ueʻi naʻá ne fakahoko ʻa e meʻa naʻá ne teu ke fai he taimí ni, ko ha ngaahi taumuʻa fakapotopoto pau, ʻa ia ko ʻene taumuʻá nai ke fakaakeake ʻa e laumālie ʻo ʻene kau ngāué ʻaki haʻane tā ʻo ʻene taukei fakapulipulí, ʻi ha meʻa fakaofo ʻo hangē ko e ngaahi kāpasa kuo fakafetongí. ʻIkai ngata aí, naʻe ʻiloʻi lelei ʻe he motuʻá ko e fakaʻuli ʻaki ha ngaahi tuitui kuo fakaʻilongaʻi, neongo ʻoku lava ke fakahoko fakapikopiko, ka naʻe ʻikai ko ha meʻa ia ke fakalaka atu ʻe he kau folau taʻeʻaonga, ʻo ʻikai ha ngaahi tetetete mo ha ngaahi fakaʻilonga kovi.

“Kau tangata,” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻo ne tafoki maʻu pē ki he kau kauvaká, ʻi hono ʻoange ʻe he hoá ʻa e ngaahi meʻa naʻá ne fie maʻú, “ʻeku kau tangata, naʻe liliu ʻe he manamanaá ʻa e ngaahi tuitui motuʻa ʻa ʻĒhapí; ka mei he konga ukamea ko ʻení ʻe lava ʻe ʻĒhapi ʻo ngaohi haʻane taha ʻaʻana, ʻa ia ʻe tuhu ʻo moʻoni ʻo hangē ko ha toe taha.”

Naʻe fefakatauʻaki ʻe he kau folaú ha ngaahi sio fakamā ʻo e ofo fakasevāniti, ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe lea ʻaki ʻení; pea naʻa nau tatali ʻi he mata fakamānako ki ha faʻahinga meʻa fakaofo pē ʻe hoko mai. Ka naʻe sio mamaʻo atu ʻa Starbuck.

ʻI ha tā mei he top-maul naʻe tukiʻi ai ʻe ʻĒhapi ʻa e ʻulu ukamea ʻo e taó, peá ne toki ʻoange ki he hoá ʻa e vaʻa ukamea lōloa naʻe toé, ʻo ne fekauʻi ia ke ne pukepuke ia ʻo hangatonu, ʻo ʻikai ke ne lave ki he funga vaká. Pea, ʻaki ʻa e maul, hili ʻene toutou taaʻi ʻa e ngataʻanga ʻi ʻolunga ʻo e vaʻa ukamea ko ʻení, naʻá ne ʻai ʻa e tuitui fakaʻauʻauhifo ʻi he ngataʻangá ʻi hono tumuʻakí, pea siʻisiʻi ange hono tā mālohi ʻa e meʻa ko iá, ʻi ha ngaahi taimi lahi, ʻoku kei puke pē ʻe he hoá ʻa e vaʻá ʻo hangē ko ia kimuʻá. Pea ko e fou atu ʻi ha fanga kiʻi ngaʻunu ngali kehe mo iá —pe ʻoku fuʻu fie maʻu ia ki hono fakamākinito ʻo e ukameá, pe ʻoku fakataumuʻa pē ke fakalahi ʻa e manavahē ʻa e kau ngāué, ʻoku ʻikai pau —naʻá ne ui ki ha filo līneni; pea ʻunu ki he binnacle, ʻo ne fakaʻauʻauhifo ʻa e ongo tuitui fakafokifā ʻi aí, pea tautau fakahosite ʻa e tuitui-folau ʻi hono lotomālié, ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he taha ʻo e ngaahi kaati-kāpasá. ‘I he kamatá, na‘e ‘alu takai holo ‘a e ukameá, ‘o tetetete mo ngaueue ‘i he ongo ngata‘angá; ka naʻe faifai peá ne nofo ki hono tuʻungá, ʻi he taimi naʻe holomui fakahangatonu ai ʻa ʻĒhapi, ʻa ia naʻá ne siofi fakamātoato ʻa e ola ko ʻení, mei he ʻaiʻangakai ʻo e manú, ʻo ne tuhu hono nima kuo mafao atu ki aí, ʻo ne kaila,—“Vakai, maʻamoutolu, kapau ʻoku ʻikai ko ʻĒhapi ʻa e ʻeiki ʻo e maka kavenga lēvolo! Ko e laʻaá ʻoku Hahake, pea ko e compass ia !”

Naʻa nau sio hokohoko mai ki loto, he naʻe ʻikai ha meʻa kehe ka ko honau matá pē te ne lava ʻo fakalotoʻi ʻa e taʻeʻilo pehē ʻo hangē ko kinautolú, pea naʻa nau hokohoko atu ʻo luelue atu.

‘I hono mata vela ‘o e manuki mo e ikuna, na‘á ke sio leva kia ‘Ēhapi ‘i he‘ene pōlepole fakatupu mate kotoa pē.





VAHE 125. Koe Loka moe Laine.

Lolotonga ko ʻeni kuo fuoloa ʻa e ʻalu ʻa e Pequod kuo ʻosi ʻi he folau ko ʻení, naʻe ʻikai ke fuʻu faʻa ngāueʻaki ʻa e ʻakaú mo e lainé. Koe‘uhi ko ha falala falala‘anga ki he ngaahi founga kehe ke fakapapau‘i ai ‘a e feitu‘u ‘o e vaká, ‘oku li‘aki faka‘aufuli ai ‘e he kau fakatau ‘e ni‘ihi, mo e kau toutai tafola tokolahi, tautefito ‘i he taimi ‘oku nau folau aí, ke hiki hake ‘a e fu‘u ‘akaú; neongo ʻi he taimi tatau, pea ʻoku faʻa lahi ange koeʻuhí ko e fōtungá ʻi ha toe meʻa kehe, ʻo tuku maʻu pē ki lalo ʻi he ʻaofi ʻo e angamahení ʻa e hala ʻoku fakalele ʻe he vaká, pea pehē ki he ʻavalisi ʻo e fakalakalaka ʻoku fakakaukauʻí ʻi he houa kotoa pē. Naʻe pehē pē ʻa e Pequod. Naʻe tautau ʻa e foʻi ʻakau ʻakau mo e fuʻu ʻakau angular naʻe fakapipikí, ʻo fuoloa ʻene ʻikai ke uesia, ʻi lalo pē ʻi he ʻā ʻo e ngaahi ʻā ʻi muí. Naʻe fakamomoko ia ʻe he ʻuhá mo e spray; naʻe fakapikopikoʻi ia ʻe he laʻaá mo e matangí; kuo fakatahaʻi ʻa e ngaahi ʻelemēniti kotoa pē ke pala ha meʻa naʻe tautau noaʻia ʻaupito. Ka ʻi he ʻikai ke ne tokanga ki he meʻá ni kotoa, naʻe puke ʻe hono lotó ʻa ʻĒhapi, ʻi heʻene hoko ʻo sio ki he foʻi lēlué, ʻo ʻikai ha ngaahi houa lahi mei he fakaʻaliʻali ʻo e mākinití, peá ne manatuʻi ʻa e ʻikai ke toe ʻi ai hono kuataá, peá ne manatuʻi ʻene fuakava fakaʻohovale fekauʻaki mo e ʻakau levolo mo e laine. Naʻe folau fakaʻauʻauhifo ʻa e vaká; astern naʻe takai holo ʻa e ngaahi peau ʻi he ngaahi fakamoveuveu.

“Ki mu‘a, ‘i ai! Hiki hake ‘a e fu‘u ‘akaú!”

Naʻe haʻu ha ongo tangata toutai. Ko e Tahiti lanu koula mo e Manxman lanu koula. “Toʻo ʻa e foʻi lēlué, ko ha taha ʻo kimoutolu, te u hiki hake.”

Naʻá na ʻalu atu ki he tafaʻaki ʻo e mui ʻo e vaká, ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e vaká, ʻa ia naʻe meimei tō ai ʻa e funga vaká, mo e ivi fakaʻuli ʻo e matangí, ʻi he taimí ni ki he tahi kilimi mo lele fakavavevave ʻi he tafaʻakí.

Na'e to'o 'e he Manxman 'a e reel, pea puke ia 'o ma'olunga, 'e he projecting handle-ends 'o e spindle, 'a ia na'e takai 'a e spool 'o e laine, ko ia na'e tu'u mo e 'akau angular 'oku tautau hifo, kae 'oua kuo 'unu atu 'a 'Ehapi kiate ia.

Naʻe tuʻu ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi hono ʻaó, pea naʻá ne vete maʻamaʻa ha ngaahi tafoki ʻe tolungofulu pe fāngofulu nai ke faʻu ha tomuʻa nima-koili ke lī ki ʻolunga, ʻi he taimi naʻe fai ai ʻe he motuʻa Manxman, ʻa ia naʻá ne siofi fakamātoato fakatouʻosi ia mo e lainé, ʻa e loto-toʻa ke lea.

“Tangataʻeiki, ʻoku ʻikai ke u falala ki ai; ʻoku hā ngali ʻosi mamaʻo ʻa e laine ko ʻení, kuo maumauʻi ia ʻe he vela lōloá mo e sūsuú.”

“'Te pukepuke, tangata'eiki motu'a. Vela loloa mo e 'uha, kuo nau maumau'i koe? 'Oku ngali 'oku ke pukepuke. Pe, mo'oni ange mahalo, 'oku pukepuke koe 'e he mo'ui; 'ikai ko koe ia.”

"ʻOku ou puke ʻa e spool, tangataʻeiki. Ka ʻo hangē pē ko e lea ʻa ʻeku ʻeikitaú. ʻI heʻeku ngaahi fulufuluʻi ʻulu hina ko ʻení ʻoku ʻikai ke mahuʻinga ia lolotonga ʻeku fakakikihí, ʻo tautautefito ki ha taha māʻolunga ange, ʻa ia ʻe ʻikai ke ne fakahā.”

"Ko e hā ia? ʻOku ʻi ai he taimí ni ha palōfesa kuo fakapipiki ʻi he Kolisi ʻa Kuini Natulá ʻa ia naʻe fokotuʻu ʻaki ʻa e makamaka; ka ʻoku ou fakakaukau ʻoku fuʻu moʻulaloa ia. Naʻe fāʻeleʻi koe ʻi fē?"

“ʻI he kiʻi Motu makamaka ʻo Maní, tangataʻeiki.”

“Maʻongoʻonga! Kuó ke taaʻi ʻa e māmaní ʻaki ia.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u ʻilo, tangataʻeiki, ka naʻe fāʻeleʻi au ʻi ai.”

“ʻI he ʻOtu Motu ʻo Maní, hei? Sai, ko e founga ʻe tahá, ʻoku lelei. Ko ha tangata ʻeni mei Maní; ko ha tangata naʻe fāʻeleʻi ʻi ha Tangata tauʻatāina ʻi ha taimi, pea ʻoku ʻikai haʻane tangata ʻi he taimí ni; ʻa ia ʻoku mimisi ʻi he —ʻe he hā? ʻO ʻalu hake mo e foʻi lēlué! ʻOku ʻohofi ʻe he ʻā mate mo fakaʻulí ʻa e ngaahi ʻulu fehuʻi kotoa pē ʻi he fakaʻosí. ʻAlu hake mo ia!."

Naʻe hiki hake ʻa e fuʻu ʻakaú. Naʻe vave ʻa e fakahangatonu ʻa e ngaahi foʻi koila veteveté ʻi ha laine toho lōloa ki mui, pea ʻi he taimi pē ko iá, naʻe kamata ke vilo ʻa e foʻi koila. ʻI hono olá, ʻi hono hiki hake mo tuku hifo fakaʻauʻausino ʻe he ngaahi peau ʻoku tafe maí, naʻe fakatupu ʻe he fakafepakiʻi ʻo e toho ʻo e ʻakaú ke tetetete ngali kehe ʻa e motuʻa reelman.

“Puke mālohi!”

Motu! naʻe tō hifo ʻa e laine naʻe fuʻu fakamālohiʻí ʻi ha festoon lōloa ʻe taha; naʻe ʻikai toe ʻi ai ʻa e fuʻu ʻakau toho.

"'Oku ou laiki 'a e kuata, 'oku liliu 'e he manamana 'a e ngaahi tuitui, pea ko 'eni 'oku vahevahe 'e he tahi vale 'a e laine 'akau. Ka 'e lava 'e 'Ehapi 'o fakalelei'i kotoa. Pea ko ʻeni ʻi he taimi mo e feituʻu totonú, hili ha folau fakamuʻomuʻa lōloa mo lahi pehē, naʻe hangē naʻe tuli ʻe ʻĒhapi, —naʻe tafiʻi ʻa e ngaahi vai tafola kehe kotoa pē —ʻa hono filí ki ha ʻā ʻoseni, ke tāmateʻi malu ange ia ʻi ai; ko ʻeni, ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi ʻoku fefeka ia ʻi he latitute mo e longitute tonu naʻe fakahoko ai hono lavea fakamamahí; ko ʻeni kuo leaʻaki ha vaka ʻa ia ʻi he ʻaho tonu kimuʻá naʻá ne fetaulaki moʻoni mo Mopi Tiki;—pea ko ʻeni kuo lototaha kehekehe ʻene ngaahi fakataha hokohoko kotoa pē mo e ngaahi vaka kehekehe ke fakahaaʻi ʻa e taʻetokanga fakatēvolo ʻa ia naʻe haeʻi ʻaki ʻe he tafola hinehina ʻene kau tulimanú, ʻo tatau ai pē pe ʻoku nau faiangahala pe faiangahala ki ai; ko ʻeni naʻe ʻi ai ha meʻa naʻe toitoi ʻi he mata ʻo e motuʻá, ʻa ia naʻe ʻikai ke fuʻu kātakiʻi ʻe he ngaahi laumālie vaivaí ke nau sio ki ai. Hangē ko e fetu‘u polar ta‘efaka‘atu‘í, ‘a ia ‘i he pō ‘o e mo‘ui, ‘ātika, māhina ‘e ono ‘okú ne poupou‘i ‘a ‘ene sio tu‘uma‘u, tu‘uma‘u, mo lotoloto; ko ia na‘e ulo tu‘uma‘u he taimí ni ‘a e taumu‘a ‘a ‘Ēhapí ‘i he vaeua po‘uli ma‘u pē ‘a e kau folau fakapo‘ulí. Naʻe puleʻi ia ʻi ʻolunga ʻiate kinautolu ʻo pehē, ʻo ʻikai ke fufuuʻi ʻenau ngaahi fakaʻilonga kotoa pē, veiveiua, veiveiua, manavahē ʻi lalo ʻi honau laumālié, pea ʻikai ke tupu hake ha tao pe lau ʻe taha.

‘I he vaha‘a taimi faka‘ata ko ení foki, na‘e mole atu ai ‘a e ngaahi fakakata kotoa pē, fakamālohi pe fakanatula. Naʻe ʻikai toe feinga ʻa Situpi ke hiki hake ha malimali; Naʻe ʻikai toe feinga ʻa Starbuck ke vakaiʻi ha taha. ʻI he tatau pē, ko e fiefiá mo e mamahí, ʻamanaki leleí mo e manavaheé, naʻe hangē kuo tuki ia ki he efuefu lelei tahá, pea efuefuʻi, ʻi he taimi ko iá, ʻi he ʻumea kuo fakamaʻu ʻo e laumālie ukamea ʻo ʻĒhapí. Hangē ha ngaahi mīsiní, naʻa nau ngaʻunu fakalongolongo holo ʻi he funga vaká, ʻo nau ʻiloʻi maʻu pē ʻoku ʻiate kinautolu ʻa e mata fakamālohi ʻo e motuʻá.

Ka naʻá ke sikani loloto ia ʻi hono ngaahi houa fakapulipuli fakapulipuli angé; ʻi heʻene fakakaukau ʻoku ʻikai ha sio ka ko e taha ʻoku ʻiate ia; pea naʻá ke mei sio leva neongo ʻa e fuʻu fakaʻohovale ʻa e mata ʻo ʻĒhapí ki he kau folaú, ka naʻe fakaʻohovaleʻi ʻe he sio ʻa e Parsee taʻefakakaukauʻí ʻa ʻene sio; pe ʻi ha faʻahinga founga, ʻi ha founga vao, ʻi ha ngaahi taimi naʻá ne uesia ia. Ko ʻeni, ko e fuofua taimi naʻe tuʻu ai ʻa ʻĒhapi ʻi ʻolungá; kimuʻa peá ne ʻi ai ʻi ha miniti ʻe hongofulu; ko e taha ‘o e fanga ki‘i manupuna tahi fekai ‘oku nau ngutu kulokula ‘a ia ‘oku nau fa‘a lele ta‘efakafiemālie ‘o ofi takai ‘i he ngaahi ‘ulu-pou ‘o e kau tangata tafola ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e kakai ‘i he ngaahi latitūte ko ení; naʻe haʻu ʻa e taha ʻo e fanga manupuna ko ʻení ʻo vili mo kaila takai ʻi hono ʻulú ʻi ha meʻa fakaʻuli ʻo e ngaahi takai vave taʻealafakaʻilongaʻi. Pea naʻá ne lele hangatonu hake ʻi ha fute ʻe taha afe ki he ʻeá; pea spiralized hifo, pea 'alu eddying toe takai 'i hono 'ulu.

Ka ʻi heʻene sio fakamamaʻu ki he ʻataʻatā fakapoʻuli mo mamaʻó, naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai ke fakaʻilongaʻi ʻe ʻĒhapi ʻa e manupuna vao ko ʻení; pea, ko e mo‘oni, na‘e ‘ikai mei faka‘ilonga‘i lahi ia ‘e ha taha kehe, he ‘oku ‘ikai ko ha tu‘unga ta‘e angamaheni ia; ko e taimi pē ko ʻení naʻe meimei sio ʻa e mata ʻoku siʻisiʻi taha ʻene tokangá ki ha faʻahinga ʻuhinga kākā ʻi he meimei sio kotoa pē.

“Ko hoʻo hēkesí, ko hoʻo hēkesí, tangataʻeiki!” ko e kaila fakafokifā ia ʻa e tangata toutai Sisiliá, ʻa ia ʻi hono fakanofo ʻi he ʻulu ʻo e mizen-mast, naʻá ne tuʻu hangatonu ʻi mui ʻia ʻĒhapi, neongo naʻe kiʻi maʻulalo ange ia ʻi hono tuʻunga, pea ʻoku ʻi ai ha ʻea loloto ʻokú ne vahevahe kinaua.

Ka naʻe ʻosi ʻi he ʻao ʻo e motuʻá ʻa e kapakau sable; ko e foʻi ʻuhiki lōloa ʻoku ʻi hono ʻulú: ʻi ha kaila, naʻe lele atu ʻa e ʻuhikiʻi manupuna ʻuliʻulí mo ʻene palé.

Naʻe lele tuʻo tolu ha ʻīkale ʻo takai ʻi he ʻulu ʻo Tākini, ʻo ne toʻo hono kepi ke fetongi ʻaki ia, pea ʻi he taimi ko iá naʻe fakahā ʻe Tanaki, ko hono uaifí, ʻe hoko ʻa Tākini ko e tuʻi ʻo Lomá. Ka ko hono fetongi pē ʻo e kepi naʻe lau lelei ai ʻa e fakaʻilonga ko iá. Na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito ke fakafoki mai ‘a e hēkesi ‘o ‘Ēhapí; naʻe lele atu mo ia ʻa e ʻuhikiʻi manupuna vaó; mamaʻo ʻi muʻa ʻi he ʻao: pea faifai pea pulia; lolotonga ia mei he feituʻu ʻo e puli ko iá, naʻe ʻiloʻi fakapoʻuli ha foʻi ʻuliʻuli miniti ʻe taha, ʻo tō mei he fuʻu māʻolunga ko iá ki he tahí.
