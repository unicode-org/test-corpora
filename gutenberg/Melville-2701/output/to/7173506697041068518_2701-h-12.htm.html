VAHE 58. Ko Pilitania.

‘I he‘emau faka‘uli fakatokelau-hahake mei he Crozetts, na‘a mau tō ai ki he ngaahi vaotā lahi ‘o e brit, ko e me‘a lanu kulokula si‘isi‘i, ‘a ia ‘oku kai lahi ai ‘a e Tafola To‘omata‘ú. Naʻe ʻalu takai holo ia ʻi ha ngaahi liiki mo ha ngaahi liiki, ʻo hangē naʻá ma folau ʻi ha ngaahi ngoueʻanga taʻefakangatangata ʻo e uite motuʻa mo koulá.

'I he 'aho hono ua, na'e sio ki he ngaahi fika 'o e Right Whales, 'a ia, malu mei he 'ohofi 'o ha Sperm Whaler hange ko e Pequod, mo e ngaahi 'aofinima 'oku fakaava sluggishly luelue 'i he brit, 'a ia, 'oku piki ki he ngaahi filo fringing 'o e fakaofo Venetian faka'uli ko ia 'i honau ngutu, na'e 'i he founga ko ia 'a e lipeca 'a e lipd 'oku mavahe.

Hangē ko e kau tuʻusi pongipongí, ʻa ia ʻoku nau tafaʻaki ki he tafaʻaki ʻoku nau ʻunuakiʻi māmālie mo vela ʻenau ngaahi heletaá ʻi he mohuku lōloa momoko ʻo e ngaahi vaotaá; naʻa mo ia naʻe luelue ʻa e fanga manu fekai ko ʻení, ʻo nau fai ha leʻo ngali kehe, mohuku, mo motuhi; pea tuku ‘iate kinautolu ‘a e ngaahi fu‘u ‘akau lanu pulū ta‘e ngata ‘i he tahi kulokulá.*

*Ko e konga ko ia 'o e tahi 'oku 'iloa 'e he kau tangata tafola ko e “Brazil Banks” 'oku 'ikai ke ne ma'u 'a e hingoa ko ia 'o hange ko e ngaahi Banks 'o Niufaunilani, koe'uhi ko e 'i ai 'a e ngaahi loloto mo e ngaahi soundings 'i ai, ka koe'uhi ko e fōtunga fakaofo ko eni 'oku hange ha vao, 'oku fakatupu 'e he ngaahi fu'u tafe lahi 'o e brit la titude 'oku hokohoko atu 'a e Rha, 'oku hokohoko atu 'a e ngaahi chatitude 'o e Rha.

Pea hangē ko ia ‘i he ngaahi fonua tulimanu lalahi ‘o ‘Initiá, ‘e fakalaka atu ‘a e muli ‘i he mama‘ó ‘i he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi ‘i he ngaahi ‘elefānite tokoto ‘i he ngaahi tele‘á ‘o ‘ikai te ne ‘ilo‘i ‘oku nau pehē, ‘o ne ‘ave kinautolu ki he ngaahi feitu‘u mā‘olunga ‘ata‘atā mo ‘uli‘uli ‘o e kelekelé; naʻa mo ia, ʻoku faʻa hoko, mo ia, ʻa ia ʻokú ne fuofua sio ki he faʻahinga ko ʻeni ʻo e fanga levaiatani ʻo e tahí. Pea naʻa mo e taimi ʻoku ʻiloʻi ai ʻi he fakaʻosí, ʻoku ʻai ʻe honau lahi taʻefaʻalauá ke fuʻu faingataʻa moʻoni ke tui ʻe lava ke hoko ʻa e ngaahi fuʻu ʻakau lahi peheé ʻo e tupu ʻo ʻosiʻosingamālié, ʻi he ngaahi konga kotoa pē, ʻaki ʻa e faʻahinga moʻui tatau ʻoku moʻui ʻi ha kuli pe ko ha hoosi.

Ko e mo‘oni, ‘i he ngaahi tafa‘aki kehé, ‘oku ‘ikai meimei lava ke ke vakai ki ha fa‘ahinga me‘amo‘ui ‘o e lolotó ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi ongo‘i tatau ‘okú ke fai ki he ngaahi me‘amo‘ui ‘o e matāfangá. He neongo kuo taukave‘i ‘e he kau mataotao fakanatula motu‘a ‘e ni‘ihi ko e ngaahi me‘amo‘ui kotoa pē ‘o e fonuá ‘oku nau fa‘ahinga ‘i he tahí; pea neongo ʻoku toʻo ha vakai fakalūkufua ʻoku lahi ki he meʻá, ʻe lava ke fuʻu lelei ʻaupito ʻeni; ka ‘i he‘ene ha‘u ki he ngaahi me‘a makehé, ko fē, hangē ko ‘ení, ‘oku ‘omai ai ‘e he ‘osení ha ika ‘a ia ‘i hono ‘ulungāngá ‘okú ne tali ki he anga-‘ofa fakapotopoto ‘a e kulií? Ko e 'anga'anga fakamala'ia'i tokotaha pe 'e lava ke pehe 'i ha fa'ahinga faka'apa'apa fakalukufua 'oku ne fua 'a e fakatata fakafehoanaki kiate ia.

Ka neongo ia, ki he kau tangata fonuá fakalūkufua, kuo ‘osi vakai‘i ‘a e kau nofo tu‘ufonua ‘o e ngaahi tahí ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi ongo‘i ta‘ealafakamatala‘i ‘oku ‘ikai ke fakasōsiale mo fakafepaki; neongo ‘oku tau ‘ilo‘i ko e tahí ko ha terra incognita ta‘engata, ko ia na‘e folau ai ‘a Kolomupi ‘i he ngaahi māmani ta‘e‘iloa ta‘efa‘alaua ke ne ‘ilo‘i ‘a ‘ene māmani fakahihifo ‘e taha ‘oku ‘i ‘olunga; neongo ia, ʻi he ngaahi meʻa fakatuʻutāmaki lahi tahá, ko e fakatamaki fakalilifu taha ʻi he ngaahi fakatamaki fakamatelie kotoa pē kuo hoko taʻefakamanatuʻi mo taʻefakafaikehekeheʻi ia ki he laui hongofulu mo e laungeau afe ʻo kinautolu kuo nau ʻalu ʻi he ngaahi vaí; neongo ko ha fakakaukau pē ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti ʻe akoʻi mai, neongo pe ko e hā hono lahi ʻo e pōlepole ʻa e tangata pēpeé ʻi heʻene saienisí mo ʻene taukeí, pea neongo pe ko e hā hono lahi, ʻi ha kahaʻu fakaʻofoʻofá, ʻe fakalahi ʻe he saienisí mo e taukeí; ka ʻo taʻengata pea taʻengata, ki he mafesifesi ʻo e malaʻiá, ʻe fakaangaʻi mo fakapoongi ia ʻe he tahí, pea ʻe fakaʻauha ʻe he tahí ʻa e vakapuna fakaʻeiʻeiki mo fefeka taha te ne lava ʻo ngaohí; ka neongo ia, ʻi he toutou fai ʻo e ngaahi ongoʻi tonu ko ʻení, kuo mole mei he tangatá ʻa e ongo ko ia ʻo e fakalilifu kakato ʻo e tahí ʻa ia ʻoku ʻaʻana ʻi he ʻaboriginally.

Ko e fuofua vaka naʻa mau lau ki aí, naʻe ʻalu holo ʻi ha ʻoseni, ʻa ia ʻi he tautea ʻa e kau Potukalí naʻá ne whelmed ha māmani kakato ʻo ʻikai ke ne tuku ha meʻa lahi ʻo hangē ha uitou. Ko e ‘oseni tatau pē ‘oku takai holo he taimí ni; ko e ‘oseni tatau pē na‘á ne faka‘auha ‘a e ngaahi vaka maumau ‘o e ta‘u kuo ‘osí. ʻIo, ʻae kakai matelie vale, ʻoku teʻeki ke holo hifo ʻae lōmaki ʻo Noa; ko e vahe tolu ʻe ua ʻo e māmani totonu ʻokú ne kei ʻufiʻufi.

Ko e hā ʻoku faikehekehe ai ʻae tahi mo e fonua, ʻoku ʻikai ko ha mana ʻi he taha ʻoku hoko ko e mana ʻi he taha? Na‘e mālōlō ‘a e ngaahi fakailifia ki mu‘a ‘i he fakanatulá ‘i he kau Hepeluú, ‘i he taimi na‘e fakaava ai ‘e he kelekele mo‘uí ‘i lalo ‘i he va‘e ‘o Kola mo hono kautahá ‘o folo kinautolu ‘o ta‘engata; ka ‘oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ke tō ha la‘ā fakaeonopooni, ka ‘i he founga tatau tofu pē ‘oku folofua ai ‘e he tahi mo‘uí ‘a e ngaahi vaká mo e kau ngāué.

Ka ʻoku ʻikai ngata pē ʻi he hoko ʻa e tahí ko ha fili pehē ki he tangata ʻoku muli kiate iá, ka ʻokú ne toe hoko foki ko ha fili ki hono hako ʻoʻoná; kovi ange ia ‘i he kau tau Pēsia na‘a nau fakapoongi ‘ene kau fakaafé tonu; ʻo ʻikai ke ne fakaʻatā ʻa e ngaahi meʻamoʻui ʻa ia kuo ne fanauʻi ʻe ia. Hangē ko ha taika fefine fekai ‘oku lī ‘i he vaotaá ‘o ne ‘ufi‘ufi ‘a ‘ene fanga ki‘i tafola tonu, ‘oku pehē pē ‘a hono lī ‘e he tahí na‘a mo e fanga tafola mālohi tahá ki he ngaahi maká, pea tuku kinautolu ‘i ai ‘o fehangahangai mo e ngaahi fo‘i vaka kuo mavahevahé. ‘Oku ‘ikai ha ‘alo‘ofa, ‘ikai ha mālohi ka ko hono mālohi pē ‘okú ne pule‘i iá. ‘I he‘ene mānava mo ngungu ‘o hangē ha hoosi tau valevale kuo mole hono tokotaha heka aí, ‘oku ‘ohofi ‘e he ‘oseni ta‘e‘eikí ‘a e kolopé.

Fakakaukau foki ki he ngingila mo e faka‘ofo‘ofa fakatēvolo ‘o e tokolahi taha ‘o hono ngaahi matakali ta‘efakatomala tahá, ‘o hangē ko e fōtunga faka‘ofo‘ofa faka‘ofo‘ofa ‘o e ngaahi fa‘ahinga lahi ‘o e fanga ‘angá. Fakakaukau atu, ‘i ha toe taimi, ki he kai tangata fakamāmani lahi ‘o e tahí; ko kinautolu kotoa pē ʻoku nau feʻauʻauhi ʻenau ngaahi meʻamoʻuí, ʻo nau fakahoko ʻa e tau taʻengatá talu mei he kamataʻanga ʻo e māmaní.

Fakakaukau ki he me‘á ni kotoa; pea tafoki leva ki he māmani lanu mata, angavaivai, mo talangofua taha ko ʻení; fakakaukau kiate kinaua fakatou‘osi, ‘a e tahi mo e fonua; pea ʻikai ʻokú ke maʻu ha fakatatau ngali kehe ki ha meʻa ʻiate koe? Ke tauhi koe ‘e he ‘Otua! ʻOua naʻá ke tekeʻi mei he motu ko iá, he ʻikai te ke lava ʻo foki mai!





VAHE 59. Ko e 'uhiki.

ʻI heʻene luelue māmālie ʻi he ngaahi vaotā ʻo e brit, naʻe kei puke pē ʻe he Pequod ʻene fononga fakatokelau-hahake ʻo fakahangatonu ki he motu ko Java; ko ha ʻea māmālie ʻokú ne ueʻi hono ʻulú, ko ia ʻi he nonga ʻoku takatakaí naʻe lulululu māmālie hono ngaahi pou māʻolunga ʻe tolu ʻoku fakaʻauʻauhifó ki he havili vaivai ko iá, ʻo hangē ha ngaahi paame māmālie ʻe tolu ʻi ha teleʻa. Pea neongo ia, ʻi ha ngaahi vahaʻataimi lahi ʻi he pō silivá, ʻe sio ki he vakapuna tuēnoa mo fakamānakó.

Ko e Tafola Hinehina, ko e Tafola Hinehina!”

Pe naʻe faifai pea ngāue ʻa e ʻaʻahi fakavavevave ʻa e vakapuna ʻe taha ʻoku tuʻu mo nofo tokotahá kia ʻĒhapi, ʻo ne mateuteu ai he taimí ni ke fakafehokotaki ʻa e ngaahi fakakaukau ʻo e angamaluú mo e mālōloó mo e ʻuluaki sio ki he tafola pau naʻá ne tuli ki aí; neongo ia naʻe hoko ʻeni, pe naʻe lavakiʻi ia ʻe heʻene vēkeveké; pe ko e hā pē ʻa e founga naʻe mei hokó, naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuó ne ʻiloʻi mahino ʻa e fuʻu ʻakau hinehina, ka ʻi ha mālohi vave naʻá ne ʻoatu leva ha ngaahi fekau ke tuku hifo.

Ko e hā pē ha ngaahi tui taʻeʻuhinga kuo fehokotaki ʻe he kau sperm whalemen ʻi he tuʻunga fakalūkufuá mo e sio ki he meʻá ni, ʻoku pau, ko ha kiʻi sio ki ai ʻoku fuʻu taʻe angamaheni ʻaupito, kuo ʻalu mamaʻo ʻa e tūkunga ko iá ke ʻinivesi ia ʻaki ʻa e fakaʻilonga. Ko ia ‘oku tātātaha ke sio ki ai, ‘o neongo ‘oku fakahā ‘e he taha mo kinautolu kotoa ko e me‘a mo‘ui lahi taha ia ‘i he ‘oseni, ka ‘oku tokosi‘i ‘aupito ‘a kinautolu ‘oku nau ma‘u ha ngaahi fakakaukau ka ko e ngaahi fakakaukau ta‘emahino taha fekau‘aki mo hono natula mo‘oní mo hono fōtungá; neongo ia, ‘oku nau tui ‘okú ne ‘omai ki he tafola sperm ‘a ‘ene me‘akai pē ‘e tahá. He neongo 'oku ma'u 'e he ngaahi fa'ahinga kehe 'o e tafola 'enau me'akai 'i 'olunga 'i he vai, pea 'e lava ke sio ki ai 'a e tangata 'i he ngaue 'o e kai, 'oku ma'u 'e he tafola spermaceti 'ene me'akai kotoa 'i he ngaahi sone ta'e'iloa 'i lalo 'i he fukahi vai; pea ‘i he faka‘uhinga pē ‘oku lava ai ‘e ha taha pē ‘o tala ‘a e me‘a, tonu, ‘oku kau ki ai ‘a e me‘akai ko iá. ‘I he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi, ‘i he taimi ‘oku tuli ofi ai iá, te ne disgorge ‘a e me‘a ‘oku pehē ko e ngaahi nima mavahe ‘o e squid; naʻe fakaʻaliʻali pehē ʻe ha niʻihi ʻo kinautolu ʻoku laka hake ʻi he fute ʻe uofulu mo e tolungofulu hono lōloá. ‘Oku nau fakakaukau ko e monster na‘e kau ki ai ‘a e ongo nimá ni ‘oku angamaheni ‘aki ‘ene piki ‘iate kinautolu ki he mohenga ‘o e ‘osení; pea ko e sperm whale, ‘i he ‘ikai hangē ko e ngaahi fa‘ahinga kehé, ‘oku ‘oange ki ai ‘a e nifo koe‘uhi ke ne ‘ohofi mo hae‘i ia.

Hange 'oku 'i ai ha ngaahi makatu'unga ke fakakaukau ai 'e lava ke iku 'o fakalelei'i 'e he Kraken lahi 'o Pīsope Pontoppodan ia 'o hoko ko e Squid. Ko e founga ‘oku fakamatala’i ai ‘e he Pīsope, ‘o fetongitongi ‘a e ‘alu hake mo e ngoto, mo e ngaahi fakaikiiki kehe ‘oku ne fakamatala’i, ‘i he me’a kotoa ko ‘eni ‘oku fe’unga ‘a e ongo me’a ni. Ka 'oku fie ma'u 'a e abatement lahi 'i he faka'apa'apa ki he lahi ta'ealafakakaukau'i 'oku ne vahe'i ia.

‘E he kau mataotao fakanatula ‘e ni‘ihi kuo nau fanongo ta‘emahino ki he ngaahi talanoa fekau‘aki mo e me‘amo‘ui fakapulipulí, ‘a ia ‘oku lea‘aki heni, ‘oku fakakau ia ‘i he kalasi ‘o e ika-kutí, ‘a ia, ko e mo‘oni, ‘i ha ngaahi tafa‘aki pau ‘i tu‘a ‘e ngali kau ki ai, ka ko e ‘Anaki pē ‘o e matakalí.





VAHE 60. Koe Laine.

ʻI heʻeku lave ki he sioʻata ʻo e toutai tafola ʻoku teu ke fakamatalaʻí, pea pehē ki he mahino lelei ange ʻo e ngaahi sioʻata tatau kotoa pē ʻi ha feituʻu kehe ʻoku ʻoatu, ʻoku ʻi ai ʻeku lea heni ki he laine tafola fakameaʻa, ʻoku fakalilifu ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi.

Ko e laine na‘e ‘uluaki ngāue‘aki ‘i he toutaí ko e hemp lelei tahá, na‘e ki‘i faka‘au‘auhifo ‘aki ‘a e tā, ‘o ‘ikai ke faka‘ai‘ai ia, ‘o hangē ko e ngaahi maea anga-mahení; he lolotonga ko e tā, hangē ko ia ‘oku ngāue‘aki anga-mahení, ‘okú ne ‘ai ‘a e hemp ke toe pliable ange ki he tokotaha ngaohi maeá, pea ‘okú ne toe ‘ai ‘a e maeá tonu ke faingofua ange ki he tokotaha folaú ki hono ngāue‘aki anga-maheni ‘o e vaká; ka, ‘oku ‘ikai ngata pē ‘i he fu‘u fakafefeka‘i ‘e he lahi anga-mahení ‘a e laine-tafola ki he faka‘au‘auhifo ofi ‘a ia kuo pau ke mo‘ulaloa ki aí; ka ‘i he kamata ke ako ‘e he tokolahi taha ‘o e kau toutaí, ‘oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ke tānaki atu ‘e he tā ‘i he tu‘unga fakalūkufuá ‘a e tu‘uloa pe mālohi ‘o e maeá, neongo pe ko e hā hono lahi ‘o ‘ene ‘oange ki ai ‘a e fuopotopoto mo e ngingilá.

'I he ngaahi ta'u kimui ni mai kuo meimei ke fetongi 'e he maea Manilla 'i he toutai 'Amelika 'a e hemp ko ha naunau ki he ngaahi laine-tafola; he, neongo ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu tolonga ʻo hangē ko e hemp, ka ʻoku mālohi ange ia, pea ʻoku mamaʻo ange ʻene molumalu mo ʻelasitiki; pea te u tānaki atu (koe'uhi 'oku 'i ai ha æsthetics 'i he me'a kotoa pe), 'oku lahi ange 'a e faka'ofo'ofa mo e hoko ki he vaka, 'i he hemp. Ko e Hemp ko ha tokotaha fakapoʻuli, fakapoʻuli, ko ha faʻahinga ʻInitia; ka ko Manilla 'oku hange ha Siasi 'ulu koula ke sio ki ai.

Ko e laine-tafola ko e vahe tolu pē ʻe ua ʻo e ʻinisi hono matolú. ‘I he ‘uluaki vakaí, he‘ikai te ke fakakaukau ‘oku mālohi pehē ia ‘o hangē ko ia ‘oku mo‘oní. ʻI he ʻahiʻahí ʻe takitaha tautau ʻe hono ngaahi filo ʻe taha mo e nimangofulú ha mamafa ʻo e pauni ʻe teau mā uofulu; ko ia ‘e fua ‘e he maea kotoa ha mafasia ‘oku meimei tatau mo e toni ‘e tolu. ‘I hono lōloá, ko e laine sperm whale anga-mahení ‘oku fua ia ‘i ha me‘a ‘oku laka hake ‘i he fute ‘e uangeau. Fakahangatonu ki he mui 'o e vaka 'oku spirally coiled 'o 'alu 'i he 'aofi, 'ikai hange ko e ulofi-paipa 'o ha kei neongo, ka koe'uhi ke fa'u ha takai 'e taha, siisi-fotunga 'o e densely bedded “ngaahi fu'u 'akau,” pe ngaahi la'i 'o e concentric spiralizations, 'o 'ikai ha ava ka ko e “loto,” pe fa'u 'a e miniti 'o e chexi 'a e tube tu'u. Hange ko e si'isi'i taha 'o e tangle pe kink 'i he coiling 'e, 'i he lele ki tu'a, infallibly to'o 'a e nima 'o ha taha, va'e, pe sino kotoa, 'Oku faka'aonga'i 'a e fakatokanga lahi taha 'i hono stowing 'o e laine 'i hono tub. 'E faka'aonga'i 'e he kau harpooneers 'e ni'ihi 'a e meimei ha pongipongi kakato 'i he pisinisi ko 'eni, 'o 'ave 'a e laine 'o ma'olunga 'i 'olunga pea toki reeving ia ki lalo 'o fakafou 'i ha poloka 'o fakahangatonu ki he 'aofi, ko ia 'i he ngaue 'o e coiling ke fakatau'ataina'i ia mei he ngaahi wrinkles mo e twists kotoa pe 'e ala hoko.

‘I he ngaahi vaka ‘Ingilaní ‘oku ngāue‘aki ai ‘a e ongo pā ‘e ua kae ‘ikai ko e taha; ko e laine tatau pē ʻoku hokohoko atu hono fakaʻauʻauhifo ʻi he ongo pātí fakatouʻosi. ‘Oku ‘i ai ha lelei ‘i he me‘á ni; koeʻuhí he ʻoku fuʻu siʻisiʻi ʻa e ngaahi pā māhanga ko ʻení ʻoku nau hū faingofua ange ki he vaká, pea ʻoku ʻikai ke nau fuʻu fakamālohiʻi ia; ka, ko e tēpile ʻAmeliká, ʻoku meimei fute ʻe tolu hono lahí pea ʻoku feʻunga hono lolotó, ʻokú ne ngaohi ha uta ʻoku ʻikai fuʻu lahi ki ha vakapuna ʻoku ʻinisi pē ʻe taha mo e vaeua hono ngaahi laupapa ʻa hono matolú; he ko e lalo vaka-tafola 'oku hange ha 'aisi mahu'inga, 'a ia te ne fua hake ha mamafa lahi 'oku tufaki, ka 'oku 'ikai fu'u lahi 'o ha taha 'oku fakatahataha'i. ʻI he taimi ʻoku pakipaki ai ʻa e ʻufiʻufi kaniva kuo valitaá ʻi he laine-tub ʻo ʻAmeliká, ʻoku hangē ʻoku toho atu ʻa e vaká mo ha keke mali lahi fakaofo ke foaki ki he fanga tafola.

ʻOku fakahaaʻi ʻa e ongo ngataʻanga ʻo e lainé; ko e ngata'anga 'i lalo 'oku faka'osi 'i ha mata-splice pe loop 'oku ha'u hake mei lalo 'o fakafepaki'i 'a e tafa'aki 'o e 'aofi, pea tautau 'i hono tafa'aki 'oku disengaged kakato mei he me'a kotoa pe. Ko e fokotu‘utu‘u ko eni ‘o e ngata‘anga ‘i laló ‘oku fiema‘u ia ‘i ha ongo fakamatala. ‘Uluakí: Koe‘uhi ke fakafaingofua‘i hono fakama‘u ki ai ha laine tānaki atu mei ha vaka kaungā‘api, telia na‘a ‘i ai ha le‘o loloto ‘o e tafola kuo taa‘í ‘o fakamanamana‘i ai ke ne ‘ave ‘a e laine kotoa na‘e ‘uluaki fakapipiki ki he fo‘i ‘aá. ‘I he ngaahi tu‘unga ko ení, ‘oku mo‘oni ‘oku hiki ‘a e tafola ‘o hangē ha ipu ‘aleá, ‘o hangē ko ia ‘oku ‘i aí, mei he vaka ‘e tahá ki he vaka ‘e tahá; neongo ‘oku puna ma‘u pē ‘a e ‘uluaki vaká ‘i hono nimá ke tokoni ki hono hoá. Uá: Ko e fokotu‘utu‘u ko ení ‘oku mātu‘aki fiema‘u ia koe‘uhi ko e malu anga-mahení; he kapau naʻe fakapipiki ʻa e ngataʻanga ki lalo ʻo e lainé ʻi ha faʻahinga founga pē ki he vaká, pea kapau naʻe lele leva ʻa e tafola ʻi he taimi ko iá ʻi he lainé ʻo aʻu ki he ngataʻangá ʻo meimei ʻi ha miniti pē ʻe taha, ʻo kohu ʻo hangē ko ia ʻokú ne fai he taimi ʻe niʻihi, he ʻikai ke ne tuʻu ai, he ʻe toho hifo taʻehalaia ʻa e vaka ʻoku malaʻiá ʻo muimui ʻiate ia ki he loloto ʻo e tahí; pea ‘i he tu‘unga ko iá ‘e ‘ikai toe ma‘u ia ‘e ha taha-kalanga kolo.

Kimuʻa pea tuku hifo ʻa e vaká ki he tulimanú, ʻoku ʻave ʻa e ngataʻanga ʻi ʻolunga ʻo e lainé ʻi mui mei he tāpuní, pea ʻi heʻene fakalaka takai ʻi he ʻuluʻakau ʻi aí, ʻoku toe ʻave ia ki muʻa ʻi he lōloa kotoa ʻo e vaká, ʻo mālōlō fakakolosi ʻi he loom pe meʻa puke ʻo e foʻi ʻaʻalo ʻa e tangata kotoa pē, koeʻuhí ke ne lele holo ʻo fakafepakiʻi hono louhiʻi nimá ʻi he folau; pea mo e fakalaka atu foki ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e kau tangatá, ʻi heʻenau taufetongi ʻi he ngaahi gunwales fehangahangaí, ki he ngaahi chocks pe grooves ʻoku ʻi he prow ʻoku fuʻu tuhuʻi ʻaupito ʻo e vaká, ʻa ia ʻoku taʻofi ai ia ʻe ha pine ʻakau pe skewer ʻoku lahi hangē ha quill angamaheni, mei heʻene hē ki tuʻá. Mei he ngaahi chocks 'oku tautau ia 'i ha ki'i festoon 'i 'olunga 'i he ngaahi kaufana, pea 'oku toe fakalaka leva ia 'i loto vaka; pea ko e ngaahi fute ʻe hongofulu pe uofulu nai (ʻoku ui ko e box-line) ʻoku takaiʻi ʻi he puha ʻi he ngaahi kaufana, ʻoku hoko atu ʻene fononga ki he gunwale ʻoku kei kiʻi mamaʻo ange ʻi mui, pea ʻoku toki fakapipiki ia ki he short-warp —ko e maea ʻoku fehokotaki vave mo e harpoon; ka kimu'a 'i he fehokotaki ko ia, 'oku 'alu 'a e short-warp 'i he ngaahi mystifications kehekehe 'oku fu'u fakafiefia ke fakaikiiki.

Ko ia ‘oku fakafonu ‘e he laine-tafola ‘a e vaká kotoa ‘i hono ngaahi fo‘i koini faingata‘á, ‘o mio‘i mo vilo takai ‘i ai ‘i he meimei tafa‘aki kotoa pē. ‘Oku kau kotoa ‘a e kau folau ‘eve‘evá ‘i hono ngaahi fakapikopiko fakatu‘utāmakí; ko ia ki he mata mā ‘o e tangata fonuá, ‘oku nau hā ngali ko e kau juggler ‘Initia, ‘o faka‘ofo‘ofa‘ia fakasipoti ‘e he fanga ngata fakatupu mate tahá honau ngaahi va‘é. Pea ʻe ʻikai foki ke lava ʻe ha foha ʻo ha fefine fakamatelie, ʻi he fuofua taimí, ʻo tangutu ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi meʻa lavelavea ko ia ʻo e hempen, pea lolotonga ʻene fakamālohiʻi hono lelei tahá ʻi he foʻi ʻaá, fakakaukau kiate ia ʻi ha faʻahinga taimi taʻeʻiloa pē ʻe lava ke lī ʻa e foʻi ʻaá, pea ʻe fakahoko ʻa e ngaahi fakapikopiko fakalilifu kotoa ko ʻení ʻo hangē ha ngaahi ʻuhila ʻoku mamaʻó; he ʻikai lava ke ne tuʻunga pehē taʻekau ai ha tetetete ʻokú ne ʻai ʻa e uho ʻo hono ngaahi huí ke tetetete ʻiate ia ʻo hangē ha jeli ʻoku luluʻí. Ka ko e tō‘onga—me‘a ngali kehe! ko e hā ʻoku ʻikai lava ke fakahoko ʻe he tōʻongá?—Ko e ngaahi sallies Gayer, fiefia lahi ange, ngaahi fakakata lelei ange, mo e ngaahi repartees ngingila ange, kuo teʻeki ai ke ke fanongo ki ai ʻi ʻolunga ʻi hoʻo mahogany, ʻi he meʻa te ke fanongo ki ai ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he sita hinehina ʻinisi vaeua ʻo e vaka-tafola, ʻi he taimi ʻoku tautau pehē ai ʻi he ngaahi noʻo ʻo e tangata tautauʻangá; pea, hangē ko e kau burgher ʻe toko ono ʻo Calais ʻi he ʻao ʻo Tuʻi ʻEtuate, ʻoku toho ʻe he kau tangata ʻe toko ono ʻoku nau faʻu ʻa e kau folaú ki he ngaahi nifo ʻo e maté, mo ha halter ʻi he kia kotoa pē, ʻo hangē ko ia te ke lea ʻakí.

Mahalo ko ha kiʻi fakakaukau siʻisiʻi ʻaupito te ne ʻai ke ke lava he taimí ni ʻo fakamatalaʻi ʻa e ngaahi fakatamaki ko ia ʻoku toutou hoko ʻi he toutai tafola —ʻa ia ko ha niʻihi tokosiʻi ʻoku fakamatalaʻi fakaʻaufuli —ʻo hono ʻave ʻo e tangatá ni pe ko e tangatá ni mei he vaká ʻe he lainé, pea mole. He, ʻi he taimi ʻoku lele atu ai ʻa e lainé ki tuʻa, ke tangutu leva ʻi he vaká, ʻoku hangē ia haʻo tangutu ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi ngingila lahi ʻo ha mīsini-kohu ʻi he vaʻinga kakató, ʻi he taimi ʻoku kai ai koe ʻe he ngaahi ʻakau lele kotoa pē, mo e ʻakau, mo e uili. ʻOku kovi ange ia; he he ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo tangutu taʻe ngaue ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi fakatuʻutāmaki ko ʻení, koeʻuhí he ʻoku lulululu ʻa e vaká ʻo hangē ha mohengá, pea ʻoku lī koe ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe taha mo e tafaʻaki ʻe tahá, ʻo ʻikai ha kiʻi fakatokanga; pea 'i ha buoyancy self-adjusting pau mo e taimi tatau 'o e loto mo e ngaue, te ke lava 'o hao mei hono ngaohi koe ko ha Mazeppa 'o e, pea hola mo e feitu'u 'e 'ikai lava 'e he la'a sio kotoa pe 'iate ia pe 'o tuki'i koe ki tu'a.

Toe: hangē ko e nonga loloto ʻa ia ʻoku ngali pē ʻoku muʻomuʻa mo kikiteʻi ʻa e matangí, mahalo ʻoku fakalilifu ange ia ʻi he matangí tonu; he, ko e moʻoni, ko e nongá ko e takai mo e ʻenivolope pē ia ʻo e matangí; pea ‘oku ‘i ai ia ‘iate ia pē, ‘o hangē ko hono pukepuke ‘e he me‘afana ‘oku ngali ta‘efakatupu maumaú ‘a e efuefu fakatupu maté, mo e fo‘i pulú, mo e pā; ko ia ko e mālōlō fakaʻofoʻofa ʻo e lainé, ʻi heʻene ngata fakalongolongo fekauʻaki mo e kau folau ʻeveʻevá kimuʻa pea toki ʻomi ia ki he vaʻinga moʻoní —ko ha meʻa ʻeni ʻokú ne ʻave ʻa e ilifia moʻoní ʻo lahi ange ʻi ha toe tafaʻaki kehe ʻo e meʻa fakatuʻutāmaki ko ʻení. Ka ko e hā ke lea lahi ange aí? Ko e tangata kotoa pē ‘oku nau mo‘ui ‘o ‘ufi‘ufi ‘e he ngaahi laine tafola. ‘Oku fanau‘i mai kotoa pē mo e ngaahi ha‘i ‘akau ‘i honau kiá; ka ko e taimi pē ʻoku maʻu ai ʻe he kakai fakamatelié ʻa e ngaahi fakatuʻutāmaki fakalongolongo, fakapulipuli, mo ʻi ai maʻu pē ʻo e moʻuí ʻi he liliu vave mo fakafokifā ʻo e maté. Pea kapau ko ha filōsefa koe, neongo ‘okú ke tangutu ‘i he vaka-tafola, ‘e ‘ikai te ke ongo‘i ‘i ho lotó ha ki‘i me‘a ‘e taha ‘oku lahi ange ‘a e ilifiá, ‘i ha‘o tangutu ‘i mu‘a ‘i ho‘o afi efiafí mo ha poker, kae ‘ikai ko ha fo‘i ‘akau, ‘i ho tafa‘akí.
