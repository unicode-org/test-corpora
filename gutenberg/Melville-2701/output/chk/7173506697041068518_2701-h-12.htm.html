SOPUN 55. Ren ekkewe Sasingin Monster ren ekkewe Whale.

Ngang upwe ere long peinit ngenikemi usun chok emon epwe tongeni ese wor kanvas, och mettoch usun ewe ennetin napanapen ewe waimw usun a wesewesen pwa ngeni mesen ewe waimw nupwen non pwisin inisin mi unusoch ewe waimw a nomw unukun ewe waimw-ship pwe epwe tongeni pwungun step won ikewe. Ina minne, epwe tongeni murinno ach sipwe akkomw esilefeili ekkena sasingin ekiek mi amwarar ussun i mi pwal mwo nge tori ikenai ra lukuchar le u ngeni lukuen ewe chon fonu. A tori ewe fansoun sipwe awenechara fonufan lon ei mettoch, ren ach pwarata pwe ekkena sasingin ewe whale meinisin ra mwaal.

Eli ewe aewin popun ekkena ekiek mi mwaal meinisin mi lingoch epwe nom lein ekkewe sasingin chon Hindu, Isip, me Kriis mi pin seni meinisin. Pun seni chok ekkena fansoun mi foruta nge rese wenechar nupwen won ekkewe marble mi for seni ekkewe tempel, won ekkewe puun mi for seni ekkewe uluulun, me won ekkewe tittin maun, medal, kap, me moni, ewe dolphin a mak lon ekkewe skel mi for seni sein maun ussun chok Saladin, me efoch mokuran mi wor helmet ussun chok St. seni ena fansoun a wor och mettoch mi ussun chok ena sokkun liwinin a nemenem, esap chok lon lape-ngeni ekkewe sasing mi itefoulo ussun ewe whale, nge lon chommong porausen science ussun i.

Iei, ren meinisin ekkewe mettoch, ewe sasing mi nom lom lom mi apasa pwe ina ewe sasing ewe waimw, a nom lon ewe imwen fel mi itefoulo lon Elephanta, lon India. Ekkewe Brahmin ra apasa pwe lon ekkewe uluulun arapakan ese much lon ena pagoda mi fen nom ren aramas lom lom, meinisin ekkewe angang me fofforun aramas, meinisin ekkewe angang aramas ra tongeni ekieki, ra fen mak lon ekkewe ier mwen eu me leir epwe wesewesen foruta. Ese mwaal iwe, pwe non ekkoch napanap ach angang mi tekia ren whaling epwe fen nomw ikewe ie a pwano. Ewe waimw chon Hindu mi mak ussun, a fis lon eu kinikin mi sokkolo lon ewe tit, a liosueta ewe inisin Vishnu lon lapalapen lefiathan, ewe aramas ra kaeo pwe ra iteni ewe Matse Avatar. Nge inaamwo ika ei uluulun a esopw aramas me esopw whale, pwe epwe chok ngeni ewe ttit ewe mwirin, nge ena kukkun kinikinin i a mwaal meinisin. A kon ussun chok ewe ttit mi ttit an emon anaconda, lap seni ekkewe ttit mi ttit mi ttit lon ekkewe ttit mi fokkun watte an ewe whale mi enlet.

Nge kopwe feilo ngeni ekkewe Galleries lom, me nengeni iei eu sasingin emon chon Kraist mi lapalap mi fori sasingin ei ik; pun ese sopwoch lap seni ewe chon Hindou mwen ewe noter. Ina ewe sasingin Guido ren an Perseus amanaua Andromeda seni ewe manmwacho are manmwacho. Ia Guido a angei me ie ewe leenien appiru ren ena sokkun monumanau mi amwarar ussun chok ena? Hogarth ese pwal, lon an makkei ewe chok sokkun lapalap lon pwisin an we “Perseus a Feitiu,” a fori pwe eu chok lapalap a murinno seni ena. Ewe watten chochon ena monun Hogarth a fitikoko won ewe fonu, ese kon lien angei eu inis konik. A wor eu sokkun howdah won pechen, me awan mi uren ttit mi ttit ikewe ekkewe ttit ra ttur lon, eli epwe tongeni uwei ngeni ewe Asamalapen Chon Otuputup mi feilo seni ewe Thames ren konik ngeni ewe Imwen Tekia. Iwe, a wor ekkewe waimw itan Prodromus seni ewe man loom itan Scotch Sibbald, me ewe waimw itan Sona, ussun a mak lon ekkewe sasing lon ekkewe Paipel lom lom me ekkewe minen alillis mi mak lon ekkewe puken popun lom lom. Met epwe apasa usun ekkei? Ren ewe whale an ewe chon fori puk mi fitikoko usun efoch iraan wain mi pwellifeili ewe stock an eu angko mi feitiw —usun a mak me gilded won ekkewe pekin me title-peich non chommong puk mi nom me minefo —ina ew monun fonufan mi fokkun amwarar nge mi chok amwarar, mi appiru, ua angei, seni ekkewe sokkun sasing mi amwarar won. Ina mwo ika aramas meinisin ra eita ngeni emon dolphin, nge ua chok eita ngeni ei ik mi fori puk ew sossotun emon whale; pun a ina ussun ekiekin lupwen ewe pisek a keran poputa. A poputa ren emon chon afanafan mi chinnap seni Italy non ew neni non ewe 15th senturi, atun ewe Manausefanin Kaeo; me lon ekkena ran, me pwal mwo nge tori eu fansoun mi kon ssenuk, aramas ra ekieki pwe ekkewe dolphin ir eu sokkun Leviathan.

Non ekkewe sasing me pwan ekkoch pisekin pwarata non ekkoch puken nom kopwe churi fan ekkoch ren ekkewe fitikoko mi fokkun amwarar non ewe waimw, ikewe sokopaten konik mi pwichikar, jet d’eau, konik mi pwichikar me pwichikar, Saratoga me Baden-Baden, ra feito me pupunu seni an ekiek ese uren fitikoko. Non ewe title-peich non ewe aewin kapiin “Awewe ren Kaeo” kopwe kuna ekkoch whale mi mochen sinei.

Nge ren ach sipwe likitalo ekkena sossot meinisin rese pwung, sipwe nengeni ekkena sasingin leviathan mi apasa pwe ir mi mirit, ekkewe sasingin science, ren ekkewe mi silei. Non an Harris we puken uruon sai mi wor ekkoch seleichen ekkewe waimw ra angei seni ew puken Dutch usun ekkewe sai, AD 1671, a iteni “Ew Sai ngeni Wiis ngeni Spitzbergen non ewe sipw Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson seni Friesland, masta.” Lon eu me lein ekkena peleich ekkewe whale, ussun chok ekkewe watteen ttit mi for seni ira, ra liosueta pwe ra nom lein ekkewe ice-isle, me ekkewe bear mi pwechepwech ra ssa won pecher kewe mi manau. Lon pwal eu plate, ewe blunder mi amwarar a for ren an liosuetä ewe whale mi wor föün äsefich mi chöüfetäl.

Iwe, pwal eu, a wor eu quarto mi amwarar, makkeien emon Kapten Colnett, emon Kaptin Post lon ewe mwichen sounfiun England, itelapan “Eu Sai pwe epwe pwellifeili Cape Horn ngeni ekkewe Setin South, fan iten an epwe alapalo ewe leenien attau itan Spermaceti.” Lon ei puk a wor eu kokkot mi apasa pwe ina eu “Sasingin emon waimw itan Physeter are Spermaceti, a mak ren eu scal seni emon mi ninniilo won ewe matauen Mexico, August, 1793, me a tori ewe deck.” Ua tipemwaramwar pwe ewe kaptin ese fori ei sasing mi ennet a kawor fan iten feiochun noun kewe sounfiu. Ren ai upwe apasa chok ew mettoch usun, mut ngeniei upwe apasa pwe a wor mesan minne a fis, anongonong won ewe scal mi fiti, ngeni ew sperm whale mi unusen watte, epwe fori mesen ena waimw epwe ew bow-window ukukun nimu fit ttaman. Ah, nei kaptin mi pwora, pwata kese ngenikem Sona a nenengeni seni ena mesan!

Ekkewe puken Natural History mi kon fiti mirit fan iten feiochun ekkewe sarafo me ekkewe mi tipetekison rese pwal ngaselo seni ewe chok sokkun fofforun mwaal mi fokkun ngau. Nengeni ena puk mi itefoulo itan “Ewe Napanapen Kolt mi Manau.” Lon ewe puk mi mwochomwoch lon London lon 1807, a wor ekkewe taropwe mi mak porausen emon “wa” me emon “naar” mi apasa pwe i emon “wa” me emon “wa.” Use mochen upwe ussun ita pwe use lingoch, nge ei whale mi ngau a fokkun ussun chok emon sow mi fen amputa; me, ren ewe narwhale, eu chok nenengeni a naf an epwe mairu emon, pwe non ei engon me tiuen senturi ena sokkun hippogriff epwe tongeni nom ren emon me emon chon sukun mi tipatchem.

Iwe, pwal lon 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, emon sou kaeo ussun ekkewe man mi lapalap, a fori eu puken science mi kokkotoch ussun ekkewe waimw, iwe, a wor fitu sasingin ekkewe sokkopaten man mi nom lon Leviathan. Ekkena mettoch meinisin rese chok mwaal, nge ewe sasingin ewe Mysticetus are Greenland (weween, ewe man mi pwung), pwal mwo nge Scoresby, emon mwan mi fen silei fansoun langattam pwe a tori ena sokkun man, a pwarata pwe ese wor an lukun lon forutaan.

Nge ewe kokkotun isetiu ewe kap ngeni meinisin ei angang mi mwaal a chok nom ren ewe sou kaeo Frederick Cuvier, pwiin ewe Baron mi itefoulo. Lon 1836, a makkei eu puk mi mak porausen ekkewe man mi poon, iwe, a awora minne a eita ngeni eu sasingin ewe man mi lingoch. Me mwen om kopwe pwarata ena sasing ngeni emon chon Nantucket, epwe murinno om kopwe awora om fansoun mwochomwoch seni Nantucket. Non ew chok kapas, an Frederick Cuvier we Sperm Whale esap emon Sperm Whale, nge emon squash. Pwungun pwe ese mwo wor feiochun an sai ngeni whale (ekkewe mwan rese kan kuna), nge ia a angei me ie ena sasing, io epwe tongeni silei? Neman a angei usun chok ewe chon science mi akkom mwen i non ewe chok pekin, Desmarest, a angei ew me nein an kewe abortion mi ennet; weween, seni eu sasingin chon China. Iwe met sokkun at mi manaman ren ewe pencil ekkena chon China, chommong kap me saucer mi queer ra esinesin ngenikich.

Nge ren ekkewe whale an ekkewe chon fori sain mi kuna lon ekkewe al mi riri won ekkewe sitowa an ekkewe chon amomo oil, met epwe apasa ussun ir? Ra kan iteni Richard III. ekkewe whale, mi wor ppwul mi ppwul, me ra fokkun ppwul; ra mongon neanowas won ulungat are fomon chon sai, weween, ekkewe wa mi ur ren chon sai: ar kewe apwangapwang ra fitikoko lon ekkewe matau mi ur ren chcha me peinit mi pwechepwech.

Nge ekkei chommong mwaken non an pwarata ewe whale rese kon fokkun amwarar mwirin meinisin. Afani! Lape ngeni ekkewe sasingin science ra fen angei seni ekkewe ik mi kkuf; me ekkei ra arapakan chok pung usun ew sasingin ew sipw mi tatakis, ren pechen mi tatakis, epwe pung ne pwarata ewe man mi tekia pwisin non meinisin an namanam tekia ese ataieno ren hull me spars. Inaamwo ika ekkewe elefant ra fen uta fan iten ar repwe unusen fiti, nge ewe Leviathan mi manau ese mwo pwungupwung won pwisin i fan iten an we sasing. Ewe whale mi manau, non unusen an ling me auchean, a chok tongeni kuna non sat non ekkewe konik rese tongeni weweiti; me a pwellifeili ewe watten kinikinin i ese tongeni kuna, ussun chok eu sipw mi fen fiti maun; me seni ena kinikin ina ew mettoch ese tufich feinfeino chok ngeni aramas mi tongeni mano ar repwe uwei i non inisin ngeni ewe asepwan, pwe epwe tumunu meinisin an kewe pochokunen swell me undulations. Iwe, sisap kapas ussun ewe sokkofesenin lapalap mi fokkun amwarar lefilen emon kukkun man mi suuki me emon Leviathan mi unusen watte seni Plato; nge, pwal mwo nge lon ewe poraus ussun emon me lein ekkena kukkun whale mi suuk mi suuk ngeni eu sipw, ina ussun ena fansoun ewe lapalap mi sokkolo, ussun chok eel, limbered, me sokkofesen lapalapan, pwe an we pwungun pwarata ewe tefil ese tongeni pwisin angei.

Nge eli epwe tongeni fis, pwe seni ewe chuun ewe whale mi kkuf, epwe tongeni kawor ekkewe esissin mi pwung mi weneiti wesewesen lapalapan. Ese ekkekisimwo. Pun ina eu me lein ekkewe mettoch mi kon amwarar ussun ei Leviatan, pwe inisin a awora ekis chok ekiek ussun lapalapan meinisin. Inaamwo ika ewe föün Jeremy Bentham, mi föfföri föün äsefich lon ewe imwen puk fän iten emön me lein nöün kewe chon nielo aramas, a pwüng pwe a äweweei ewe ekiek usun emön chinnap mi üföüf üf mi chöüfetäl, nge a pwal wor meinisin napanapen Jeremy kewe mi iteföülo; nge ese wor och mettoch mi ussun ena epwe tongeni fis seni ekkewe chuun leviathan mi ririfengen. Pwungun pwe, ussun ewe chon atake mi lapalap a apasa, ewe chok chuun ewe whale a wor ewe chok riri ngeni ewe man mi unusen lingoch me pad ussun chok ewe inisech a riri ngeni ewe chrysalis mi fokkun lingoch le pwellifeili. Ei sokkofesen a fokkun pwapwalo lon ewe mokuren aramas, ussun chok lon ekkoch kinikinin ei puk epwe pwalo fan eu chok. A pwal fokkun amwarar an epwe pwapwalo lon ewe side fin, ekkewe chuun arapakan chok ra polueni ekkewe chuun poun aramas, nge esap chok ewe thumb. Ei fin a wor föü föün äsefich mi chöüfetäl, ewe föün äsefich mi chöüfetäl, ewe föün äsefich lefilen, ewe föün äsefich mi chöüfetäl, me ewe föün äsefich mi kükkün. Nge ekkena mettoch meinisin ra nonnom lon ar kewe ppwul mi lingoch, ussun chok ekkewe poun aramas lon eu ppwul mi for seni aramas. “Inaamwo ika ewe whale epwe tongeni angang ngenikich fan ekkoch fan tipemwaramwar,” Stubb mi sikesik a apasa lon eu ran, “esap tongeni wesewesen apasa pwe epwe angang ngenikich ika ese wor mittens.”

Ren ekkei popun meinisin, iwe, ese nifinifin ifa usun kopwe nenengeni, mi namwot kopwe apasa pwe ewe Leviathan mi nap ina ena ew monun fonufan mi namwot epwe chok nonnom ese peinit tori nesoponon. Pwungun pwe eli eu sasing epwe tori ewe esissin mi fokkun arap ngeni ewe esissin lap seni pwal eu, nge esor emon epwe tongeni tori ren och ukukun mi fokkun pwung. Ina minne, ese wor eu alen fonufan an epwe tongeni silei wesewesen lapalapen ewe whale. Iwe, ewe chok napanap ka tongeni angei pwal mwo nge eu ekiek mi tongeni likiitu fan usun an manau, ina ren om kopwe pwisin feilo whaling; nge ren om fori ena, ese kukkun om feiengau ren om kopwe stove me sunk ren i feinfeino chok. Ina minne, a ussun ita ngeniei pwe epwe murinno ika kosap kon fitikoko lon om mochen silei ussun ei Leviathan.





SOPUN 56. Ren ekkewe Sasingin Ekkewe Whale rese kon mwaal, me ekkewe sasing mi ennet usun ekkewe nenien whale.

Ren ekkewe sasingin ekkewe waimw mi fokkun ngaw, ua fokkun mochen tonong won ekkewe poraus mi chuen fokkun ngaw usun ir mi nom non ekkoch puk, ekkewe puk me nom me ikenai, akkaewin non Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, me pwan ekkoch.

Ua sinei usun chok fomon poraus mi makketiw usun ewe watten Sperm Whale; Ekkewe ra fiti ewe mwichen soukáé, me ekkewe chón fiti ewe mwichen soupwúngúpwúng. Non ewe sopwun me mwan Colnett me Cuvier ra fen kapas usun. An Huggins a fokkun murinno seni ar kewe; nge, ren watten osukosuk, an Beale ewe mi murinno. Meinisin an Beale kewe sasingin ei waimw mi och, tiwenon chok ewe nenien nom nefinen non ewe sasingin ulumon waimw non sokopaten napanap, a kapunguno an we aruwen sopwun. An we sasing mi mak mwen, ekkewe wa ra maunei ekkewe Sperm Whale, inaamwo ika ese mwaal a ekieki pwe epwe efisi tipemwaramwar lefilen ekkoch mwan mi fiti parlor, nge a fokkun pwung me a ussun chok manau lon an we foffor. Ekkoch ekkewe sasingin ewe sperm whale non J. nge ra fen riaffou. Nge esap ina tipisin.

Seni ewe Waimw mi Pwung, ekkewe sasingin outline mi murinno ra nom non Scoresby; nge ra mak won eu scal mi kon kukkun pwe epwe tongeni efisata eu ekiek mi murinno. A wor eu chok sasingin ekkewe nenien whale, me iei ew osukosuk mi aletipechou, pun ina ren ekkena sasing chok, nupwen a fokkun murinno, pwe ka tongeni angei och mettoch usun ew ekiek mi ennet usun ewe whale mi manau usun ra kuna me ren noun kewe chon atake mi manau.

Nge, ika sipwe angei ren meinisin, ren ukukun ewe mi murinno, ina mwo ika non ekkoch poraus ese kon pung, ekkewe pwarata usun ekkewe whale me ekkewe nenien whale mi tongeni nom non ew neni, ir ruu watten sasingin France, mi murinno ar fori, me ra angei seni ekkewe sasing ren ew Garnery. Iwe, ra wewe ngeni ekkewe atake ngeni ewe Sperm me ewe Whale mi Pwung. Non ewe aewin mak emon Sperm Whale mi tekia a pwa non unusen an pochokun, a chok uta fan ewe wa seni ewe nepoputan ewe sat, me a uwei tekia non ewe asepwan won pechen ewe watten ataieno ekkewe ira mi for seni stoven. Ewe pworun ewe wa a ekis atai, me a suki a chok balance won ewe chuun ewe monukon; me ka uta non ena prow, ren ena ew chok saramen fansoun ese tongeni awewei, ka kuna emon chon sai, esopw a rokopwanieno ren ewe puun mi fifis mi uren ekkei an ewe whale, me non ewe fofforun su, usun ita seni ew chuk mi uren puun. Ewe fofforun unusen ewe mettoch mi amwarar murinno me ennet. Ewe line-tub mi chék chéúfetál a chék chéúfetál wóón ewe matau mi pwechepwech; ekkewe ira mi for seni ekkewe harpun mi ssá ra chék chéúfetál lón; ekkewe mokuren ekkewe chon fiti ewe sai ra toropasfeil won ewe whale lon sokkopaten lapalap mi pwarata niuokkus; nupwen non ewe towaw mi rochopwak ewe sipw a tori ewe neni. Eli epwe wor tipis watte epwe kuna ren ekkewe porausen inisin ei whale, nge mut ngeni ena epwe fis; pokiten, ren manauei, use tongeni draw ew mi fokkun murinno.

Non ewe aruwen sasing, ewe wa a nom non ewe fofforun suki ewe puun mi uren barnacled ren ew watten Right Whale mi fiti, ewe a rokopwanieno an we puun mi pwechepwech mi pwechepwech non ewe sat usun ekkoch fau mi mos-slide seni ekkewe chuuk non Patagonia. An kewe jet ra uta, uren, me ra pwechepwech usun chok soot; pwe seni chommongun ew fifi non ewe chimney, kopwe ekieki pwe epwe wor ew mongon neanowas mi pwora epwe kuk non ekkewe watten puun fan. Ekkewe manmwacho ra kan ässäwa ekkewe kükkün crab, ik mi chöüfetäl, me pwal ekkoch föün äsefich me mackaroni, ewe Right Whale a uwei fän ekkoch woon pechen mi pöchökkül. Iwe, non unusen ewe fansoun ewe leviathan mi uren fitikoko a fitikoko non ewe konik mi alollol, a likiti ton mi fitikoko mi pwechepwech non an fitikoko, me a fori ewe kukkun wa epwe fitikoko non ekkewe fitikoko usun ew wa mi fitikoko arap ngeni ekkewe paddle-wheels non ew waimw mi fitikoko. Ina minne, ewe popun a fis meinisin fitikoko; nge mwirin, non sokofesenin art mi amwarar, ina ewe niwinin glas non ew sat mi kinamwe, ekkewe sail rese wor starch mi turutiw non ewe sipw ese wor pochokun, me ewe inert mass ren ew whale mi mano, ew nenien tumun mi win, ren ewe flag ren katurunong mi fitikoko a riri ngeni ewe whale-pole mi tonong non an we spout-hole.

Ion Garnery ewe chon peinit, ika a fen, use sinei. Nge manauei fan itan i a wesewesen sinei usun an we porous, ika a fokkun amwarar an aiti me ren ekkoch chon whaleman mi sinenap. Ekkewe chon France ir ekkewe at fan iten fofforun peinit. Kopwe feilo me nengeni meinisin ekkewe sasingin Iurop, me ia kopwe kuna ena sokkun leenien urumot mi manau me ngunungun won kanvas, ussun chok lon ena imwen win lon Versailles; ikewe ewe chon nengeni a fiu ngeni an we aan, pell-mell, ren ekkewe maun watte mi fitikoko non France; ikewe ie iteiten ketilas meinisin a ussun ita eu saramen ekkewe Saram lon Notou, me ekkewe king me Emperor mi uwei pisekin maun mi tapwoto mwirin ra ssafetal, ussun chok eu charge ren centaur mi wor ar king? Ese unusen fich ngeni ew neni non ena galeri, ekkei pisekin maun non sat an Garnery.

A ussun ita pwe ewe tufichin chon France le angei ekkewe mettoch mi lingoch a pwapwalo ren met sokkun sasing me engraving ra fori ussun ar kewe leenien nnielo man. Ren ese wor eu ne engonun an England sinenap non ewe angangen attaw, me esap ewe engon kinikinin an chon Merika sinenap, ra fen awora ngeni ekkewe ruu muu ekkewe chok sketch mi wes mi tongeni pwarata ewe ennetin ngunun angangen attaw whale. Ren lape ngeni, ekkewe chon England me Merika mi forata ekkewe whale ra ussun ita unusen pwapwa ren ar pwarata ewe kokkotun mecha ussun ekkewe mettoch, awewe chok ren ewe pworaus mi monomon ussun ewe whale; minne, ren ukukun an epwe fis, a ussun chok ach sipwe sketchini lapalapen eu pyramid. Pwal mwo nge Scoresby, ewe chon fiti ewe mwichen sounfiu mi itefoulo lon pekin Right, mwirin an ngenikich eu minen alillis mi lingoch ussun ekkewe man mi poon lon Greenland, me ulungat are fomon kukkun sasingin ekkewe man mi lingoch me ekkewe man mi lingoch, a fori ngenikich eu mwichen sasingin ekkewe man mi lingoch ussun ekkewe chuk mi fiti wa, ekkewe chuk mi fitikoko, me ekkewe chuk mi fitikoko; me ren ewe tinikken mi kukkun an emon Leuwenhoeck a sufoliiti an epwe nengeni eu fonufan mi chechech engon me wonu famini mi ussun chok ekkewe kristal sno mi watte lon ewe Arctic. Ua weweiti pwe ese wor ai turunufas ngeni ewe chon sai mi murinno (ua asamolu i pokiten emon sounfiu mi fen mano), nge non ew mettoch mi fokkun auchea a wesewesen ew tumunungaw an ese angei fan iten iteiten kristal ew taropwen pwon mi kawor me mwen emon Sou-Kapung non Greenland.

Me lukun ekkena sasing mi murinno seni Garnery, mei wor pwal ruu sasing lon fosun France mi fich ngeni ach sipwe nengeni, ren emon mi pwisin makkei itan “H. Durand.” Eú me leir, inaamwo ika ese fókkun tipeeú ngeni ach we popun iei, nge a fich ngeni an epwe mak lón pwal ekkóch pwóróus. Ina eu leeni mi kinamwe lefilen ekkewe fonu lon ewe Pacific; emon chon France mi fiti whale a anchor, non ewe sat, non ew kinamwe, me a tipemwaramwar ne angei konik won ewe wa; ekkewe sai mi suki ewe sipw, me ekkewe foun ira palm mi langattam non ewe puun, ir me ruemon ra suki fengen non ewe asepwan mi uren asepwan. Ewe feioch a fokkun murinno, nupwen sia ekieki usun an pwarata ekkewe chon attaw mi pochokun fan ew me nein ar kewe kukkun kinikinin ewe asoso non otiw. Ewe pwal eu minen mak a fokkun sokkolo: ewe sipw a ssa won ewe matau mi pwas, me lon ewe wesewesen letipen manauen chon Lefiat, a wor emon Right Whale a nom unukkun; ewe wa (lon ewe fofforun kutta-in) a ssa ngeni ewe monumanau ussun ita ngeni eu quay; me ew wa, a mwitir ne pusin tou seni ei nenien angang, a usun an epwe ngeni chase ngeni ekkewe whale non towaw. Ekkewe harpon me lans ra nom lepekin pwe repwe aea; ulumon chon sai ra chok seti ewe mast lon an we ttit; nge seni eu rokopwanieno ewe sat mi mwitir, ewe kukkun sepenin a uta nesoponon me nukun ewe konik, usun emon oris mi uta. SOPUN 58. Ewe chon Britain.

Aua sai ngeni notou-eor seni ekkewe Crozett, aua tonong non ekkewe watten nenien brit, ewe minich, mettoch mi pwechepwech, minne ewe Right Whale a kan mongo won. Ren fitu league me league a pwellifeilikem, pwe a ussun ita pwe aua saifetal lon ekkewe leenien wiich mi poon me kolt mi lingoch.

Non ewe aruwen ran, ra kuna chommongun ekkewe Whale mi Pwung, ekkewe, ra tumun seni ewe atake an emon Sperm Whaler usun ewe Pequod, ren meser mi suk ra fitikoko ne fetan non ewe brit, minne, a fiti ekkewe fiber mi uren fitikoko an ena chon Venetian mi chun mi amwarar non auer, a nom non ena napanap mi aimufeseni ewe konik.

Ussun chok ekkewe chon atake lekuniol, ekkewe mi fitifengen me ekis me ekis ra kan amwokutu ar kewe scythes lon ewe fetil mi langattam mi pwichikar lon ekkewe leenien ppwul mi poon; ina mwo ika ekkei monun ngun ra su, ra fori ew mongungu mi amwarar, mi uren fetin, mi kutta; me ra likitalo mwirin ir ekkewe ttit mi pwechepwech ese much won ewe matau mi pwechepwech.*

*Ena kinikinin ewe matau mi itefoulo lein ekkewe chon fiti ewe matau pwe “Ekkewe Bankin Brazil” ese wor ena it ussun chok ekkewe Bankin Newfoundland, pokiten a wor ekkewe chuk mi poon me ekkewe sounfiu ikewe ie, nge pokiten ei lapalap mi amwarar mi ussun chok eu leenien ppwul, a fis pokiten ekkewe watteen chuk mi poon mi lingoch lon ekkewe chanpupu Wha.

Ika sipwe kuna seni ekkewe mast-heads, akkaewin nupwen ra asoso me ra uta ekis fansoun, ar kewe watten napanap mi pwechepwech ra usun chok ekkewe fau ese wor manauer nap seni mettoch meinisin. Iwe usun chok non ekkewe fonu mi watte non India, ewe chon ekis mi towaw epwe fan ekkoch fetan won ekkewe elefant mi mwokut won ekkewe fonu pon nge ese sinei pwe ir mi ina usun, a angeiir ngeni ekkewe neni mi tekia mi uren puun mi rochopwak; ina mwo, fan chommong, ren i, ewe a kuna fan eu ei sokkun ekkewe leviathan non ewe sat. Iwe, pwal mwo nge lupwen ra fen esilla le sopwoloon, ar watte watte a fori pwe epwe fokkun weires ach sipwe wesewesen luku pwe ekkena sokkun chochoon ira mi watte ra tongeni fis fan iten inisir, lon kinikin meinisin, ren ewe chok sokkun manau mi manau lon emon kukkun man are emon oris.

Pwungun pwe lon pwal ekkoch lapalap, kosap tongeni ekieki ussun ekkoch man lon ewe matau mi alollol ren ekkewe chok sokkun memmeef ka ekieki ngeni ekkewe man lon ewe matau. Pun inaamwo ika ekkoch sou kaeo uruwoon aramas lom lom ra apasa pwe meinisin ekkewe monumanauen fonu ra ussun chok ar sokkun lon ewe matau; me ina mwo ika a angei ew watten ekiek usun ewe mettoch, ei epwe tongeni fokkun murinno; nge a feito ngeni ekkewe specialties, ia, awewe chok, ewe matau a awora ngeni ekkoch ik mi polueni ewe kirokiroch mi mirit an ewe man? Ewe shark mi anumamau chok epwe tongeni non och napanap mi generic epwe apasa pwe a wor an awewe ngeni i.

Ewe aewin wa aua anneani usun, a fetan won ew sat, pwe ren niwinin Portugal a fen atai unusen fonufan nge ese nikitano chommong mettoch usun emon fefin mi mano punuwan. Ena chok matau a rokopwanieno iei; ena chok matau a atai ekkewe sipw mi tatakis non ewe ier a no. Kot epwe tumunuk! Kosap sü seni ena fönü, kosap tongeni liwinsefäl!





SOPUN 59. Ewe Swit.

Ekis me ekis a fetan non ekkewe fonu pon non brit, ewe Pequod a chuen fiti an sai ngeni notou-eor ngeni ewe fonu Java; eu asepwan mi mosonoson a amwokutu an we keel, pwe non ewe kinamwe mi pwellifeili an kewe ulumon mast mi tekia mi fitikoko ra mosonoson ne fitikoko ngeni ena asepwan mi languid, usun ulumon palm mi mosonoson won eu fonu pon. Iwe nge, lon ekkewe fansoun mi langattam lon ewe pwinin mi silifer, ewe jet mi monomon me amwarar epwe kuna.

Pwan ewe phantom a feitiw, nge nupwen a pwasefan fan ew, ren ew puchor usun stiletto mi eniuokusu emon me emon mwan seni an suki awan, ewe negro a puchor —“Ina! ikewe sefan! ikewe a atai! pwungun me mwen! Ekiselo chok a feitiu, me atun, ren ekkewe oar mi suki, aua witiwiti an epwe pwasefan, nengeni! non ewe chok neni ikewe a turutiw ie, fan ew a pwan ekis uta. Arapakan sia monuki ren ei fansoun meinisin ekiekin Moby Dick, iei sia nenengeni ewe mettoch mi amwarar nap seni meinisin minne ekkewe sat mi monomon ra fen pwarata ngeni aramas tori ikenai. Eú watteen chénúpupu, furlong lón ttaman me chénúpupun, mi chéúfetál wóón ewe konik, chómmóng péún mi langattam rese tongeni álleani ra chéúfetál seni lefilen, me ra chék chéúfetál me chéúfetál usun chék eú chénúpupun anaconda, usun itá ra chék chéúfetál pwe repwe chék chéúfetál wóón och mettóch mi ngaw mi tongeni toriir. Ese wor mesan are mesan mi tongeni kuna; ese wor esissin mi tongeni ekieki usun memmeef are instinct; nge a uren fitikoko ikewe ie won ekkewe chuk, eu esissinnen manau ese nom won fonufan, ese wor lapalapan, ussun chok eu chance.

Usun chok ren ew mongungu mi kukkun a ekis morono sefan, Starbuck a chuen nenengeni ekkewe konik mi uren fitikoko ikewe a tonong ie, ren ew mongungu mi ngaw a puchor —“Arapakan epwe fen murino ai upwe kuna Moby Dick me fiu ngeni, nap seni ai upwe kuna en, en anu mi pwechepwech!”

“Ewe squid mi watte mi manau, ewe, ra apasa, ekkoch chok ekkewe sipw mi fiti whale ra fen kuna, me ra niwin ngeni ar kewe nenien wa pwe repwe aporausa usun.”

Ina minne, ese kan fis pwe inaamwo ika emon me meinisin ra apasa pwe ina ewe mettoch mi manau mi watte seni meinisin lon ewe matau, nge mi chok kukkun leir ra wor ar ekiek mi monomon ussun wesewesen lapalapan me lapalapan; inaamwo ika ina, nge ra luku pwe epwe awora ngeni ewe sperm whale an chok mongo. Ra ekieki pwe ewe manmwacho ekkena poun ra nom ren a kan riri ngeniir ngeni ewe ppwul lon ewe matau; me pwe ewe sperm whale ese usun ekkewe ekkoch sokkun man, a wor ttit pwe epwe tongeni maunei me ataielo.

SOPUN 60. Ewe Line.

Ren ai upwe awewe won ewe nenien whale ekiselo chok epwe awewei, pwan ren ach sipwe weweiti meinisin ekkewe neni mi usun chok non ekkoch neni mi pwa, mi wor ikei ai upwe kapas usun ewe magic, fan ekkoch mi fokkun ngaw whale-line.

Ewe line ra akkomw nöünöü lon ewe angangen attaw, ina ewe hemp mi mürinnö seni meinisin, a ekis chöüfetäl ren tar, ese chöüfetäl ren, usun chök ekkewe föün äsefich mi chöüfetäl; pun atun ewe tar, ussun chok aramas ra kan aea, a fori pwe ewe hemp epwe kon lingoch ngeni ewe chon fori ewe rop, me a pwal fori pwe ewe rop epwe pwisin mecheres ngeni ewe chon sai fan iten an epwe aea ekkewe sipw; nge, esap chok pwe ewe ukukun mi monomon epwe kon apochokkula ewe whale-line fan iten ewe coiling mi arap epwe nom fan; nge ussun lape ngeni ekkewe chon sai ra poputa le kaeo, tar lon unusen fonufan ese fokkun alapalo pochokkulen are pochokkulen ewe rop, inaamwo ika epwe ifa ukukun an epwe ngeni an epwe lingoch me lingoch.

Lon ekkewe ier ra lo, ewe ropwan Manilla a fen lon ewe leenien attaw lon Merika arapakan unusen siwili ewe hemp pwe epwe eu pisekin angang fan iten ekkewe ropwan whale; pun, inaamwo ika ese kon lien ttam ussun hemp, nge a pochokkul, me a fokkun mosonoson me lingoch; iwe upwe apacha (pokiten mi wor ew æsthetics non mettoch meinisin), a fokkun murinno me fich ngeni ewe wa, nap seni hemp. Hemp emon mi rochopwak, emon mi rochopwak, eu sokkun chon India; nge Manilla a usun chök emön chon Sirkasia mi üföüf kolt pwe epwe nennengeni.

Ewe whale-line a chok ruu-ne-engon inis ttaman. Lupwen ka akkomw kúna, kosap ekieki pwe a fókkun péchékkúl usun chék met a wesewesen péchékkúl. Ren sossot an kewe eu me limu föün äsefich repwe eü me eü äeä eü choun ükükün engeröü ruwe paun; pwe unusen ewe rop epwe uwei eu ttit arapakan ukukun ulungat ton. Lon langattaman, ewe sperm whale mi kan fis a ükükün lap seni ruu ngeröü föün äsefich. A tori ewe ttikin ewe wa a chék chéúfetál lón ewe tub, esap usun ewe worm-pipe lón eú chénúpupu, nge pwe epwe féri eú chénúpupu mi chéúfetál, mi usun chék cheese, mi wor “föün” mi chénúpupu, are ekkewe chénúpupu mi chéúfetál, nge ese wor eú chénúpupu nge ewe “letip,” are a ffér lón ewe chénúpupu mi chék chéúfetál. Usun ewe kukkun fitikoko ika kink non ewe coiling epwe, non an epwe su, ese mwaken epwe angei poun emon, pechen, ika unusen inisin, ewe watten tumun a fis non an epwe isoni ewe line non an we tub. Ekkoch chon fiti harpoon repwe aea arapakan unusen eu pwinin lon ei angang, repwe uwei ewe line tekia me mwirin repwe reeve ngeni fan ren eu block ngeni ewe tub, ussun chok lon ewe fofforun coiling pwe epwe angasalo seni meinisin ekkewe ppwul me ppwul mi tongeni fis.

Non ekkewe waen England ruu tub ra aea nap seni ew; ewe chok line a sopweno ne kouk non ekkewe ruu tub. Mei wor ekkoch feioch non ei; pokiten ekkei twin-tub ra fokkun kukkun ra mecheresin tonong non ewe wa, me rese kon achocho ngeni; nge, ewe tub seni Merika, arapakan ülüngät fiit chölapan me a ükükün chölapan, a föri eü pisek mi kon watte fän iten eü sepelin mi äeä föün irä mi chök eü kinikinin inis chölapan; pun ewe puun whale-boat a usun chok ice mi auchea, epwe uwei ew watten choun mi kinikinfesen, nge esap kon watte ew mi kon watte. Nupwen ewe puun kanvas mi peinit a kap won ewe line-tub non Merika, ewe wa a usun ita a pull off ren ew watten cake-apupulu mi amwarar pwe epwe ngeni ekkewe whale.

Iir me ruu kinikinin ewe line ra pwälo; ewe kinikinin fan a muchulo lon eu eye-splice are loop mi feito seni fan ngeni pekin ewe tub, me a riri won pechen we mi unusen katowu seni mettoch meinisin. Ei kokkotun ewe kinikinin fan a lamot won ruu popun. Aewin: Ren an epwe mecheres an epwe riri ngeni eu line mi kapachelong seni eu wa mi nom unukkun, ika ewe whale mi kkuf epwe fokkun ngingioch pwe epwe tongeni uwei unusen ewe line mi akkom riri ngeni ewe harpoon. Lon ekkena lapalap, ewe whale a wesewesen siwil ussun chok eu mug ale, ussun ita nge, seni ewe eu wa ngeni ewe eu; inaamwo ika ewe aewin wa a kan fitikoko fansoun meinisin pwe epwe alisi puluwan we. Oruuan: Ei kokkot a fokkun lamot fan iten tumunun aramas meinisin; pun ika pwe ewe nesoponon ewe line non och napanap a riri ngeni ewe wa, me ika pwe ewe whale epwe mwitir suki ewe line ngeni nesoponon arapakan non ew chok, minich mi fifi usun chok a fori fan ekkoch, esapw uta ikewe, pun ewe wa mi mano epwe chok suki mesan mwirin i non ewe puun non ewe sat; iwe non ena fansoun ese wor emon chon fiti ewe teninimw epwe kuna sefan.

Me mwen an epwe aturalong ewe wa fan iten ewe chassi, ewe kinikinin asan lon ewe line a angei mwirin ewe tub, me a pwellifeili ewe loggerhead ikewe ie, a pwal uwei ngeni mwach unusen ttamen ewe wa, a asoso won ewe loom are ewe ttit an emon me emon mwan we oar, pwe epwe jog ngeni poun lon ewe rowing; me pwal fetal lefilen ekkewe mwan, atun ra siwili ar mwot lon ekkewe gunwales mi ü ngeni, ngeni ekkewe chock are groove mi wor lead lon ewe prow mi fokkun chöüfetäl lon ewe waimw, ikewe ie efoch pin are skewer mi för seni irä mi ükükün watteen efoch quill mi chöüfetäl, a eppeti an epwe chöüfetäl. Seni ekkewe chok a riri non ew kukkun festoon won ekkewe bow, iwe mwirin a passini non ewe wa sefan; me ukukun engon ika ruwe fatom (a iteni box-line) a kouno won ewe pwor non ekkewe bow, a sopweno an sai ngeni ewe gunwale mi chuen ekis towaw mwirin, iwe mwirin a riri ngeni ewe short-warp —ewe sepi mi mwitir riri ngeni ewe harpoon; nge me mwen ena riri, ewe short-warp a fetan non sokopaten monomon mi kon weires an epwe awewei.

Ina usun ewe whale-line a ffér unusen ewe waimw lón an kewe chénúpupu mi chéúfetál, me a chéúfetál me chéúfetál wóón arapakkan meinisin leeni. Meinisin ekkewe chon sai ra fiti an kewe kon mi efeiengaw; ina minne, ngeni mesen ewe chon fonu mi niuokkus, ra ussun ita pwe ir chon India mi fiti urumot, nge ekkewe serepenit mi fokkun nnielo aramas ra kan fiti urumot le fiti urumot. Esap pwan wor emon noun fefin mi tongeni mano, ren ewe aewin fansoun, epwe mwot nefinen ekkena fitikoko mi fis seni hempen, me nupwen a achocho ngeni an epwe fiti ewe oar, epwe ekieki pwe non ew fansoun ese fat ewe harpoon epwe tongeni fis, me meinisin ekkei fitikoko mi ngaw repwe nom non fitikoko usun chok fitikoko mi fitikoko; ese tongeni ina usun napanapan nge ese wor ew ngungures mi fori ewe chok mwongo non inisin epwe ngungures non i usun ew jelly mi ngungures. Nge föfförün —mettoch mi amwarar! met ewe foffor ese tongeni apwonueta?—Ekkewe salli mi kon amwarar, pwapwa mi kon murinno, kapasen urumot mi murinno, me ekkewe reparte mi saramaram, kese mwo rongorong won om mahogany, nap seni om kopwe rongorong won ewe ira setar mi pwechepwech mi ukukun esopw inis non ewe waimw mi fiti whale, nupwen a ina usun a riri ngeni ekkewe noos an chon nnielo aramas; me, usun chok ekkewe wonu chon Calais me mwen King Edward, ekkewe wonu mwan mi forata ewe mwichen chon angang ra suki ngeni ekkewe chuk mi mano, ren ew halter won iteiten nepekin, usun kopwe tongeni apasa.

Meni ew ekiek mi fokkun kukkun epwe atufichuk iei ne awewei usun ekkena feiengawen whale mi fis fan chommong —ekkoch me neir ra chok makketiw —ren ei mwan ika ena mwan a tou seni ewe wa ren ewe line, me a pout seni. Pun, nupwen ewe line a suki, om kopwe mwot mwirin non ewe wa, a usun om kopwe mwot non ewe nefinen ekkewe chommong ngingiin ew steam-engine non unusen fitikoko, nupwen meinisin ekkewe puun mi fiti, me ewe puun, me ewe wiil, ra mongo ngonuk. A kon ngaw; pun ouse tongeni mwokut non netipen ekkei feiengau, pokiten ewe wa a mwokut usun ew cradle, me oua suki ew pekin me ewe pwan ew, ese wor ew kukkun ourour; me ren chok ew sokkun pwisin awenewen me fitikoko ren mochen me foffor, ka tongeni su seni om kopwe fori emon Mazeppa seni, me su seni ikewe ewe akkar mi kuna mettoch meinisin ese tongeni pwisin atai ngonuk.

Pwan: usun ewe kinamwe mi watte minne a chok pwarata me mwen me oesini usun ewe fitikoko, eni epwe kon ngaw nap seni ewe fitikoko pwisin; pun, ennetin, ewe kinamwe a chok ewe rokopwanieno me ewe rokopwanieno ewe fitikoko; me a nom non pwisin i, usun ewe rifle mi usun ita ese efeiengaw a amwochu ewe powder mi fatafatoch, me ewe ball, me ewe fitikoko; ina minne ewe kinamwe mi kirekiroch an ewe line, nupwen a chok serpentines usun ekkewe chon sai me mwen an epwe kawor ngeni wesewesen urumot —iei ew mettoch mi uwei nap seni ennetin niuokus nap seni meinisin kinikinin ei mettoch mi efeiengaw. Nge pwata sipwe apasa pwal ekkoch? Aramas meinisin ra manau lon ekkewe föün whale. Meinisin ra uputiw fän föün äsefich mi pwellifeili mökürer; nge ina chok nupwen ra tori ewe mwitir, mwitir ne siwinin mano, pwe aramas mi tongeni mano ra miritiiti ekkewe feiengawen manau mi monomon, mi monomon, mi nom fansoun meinisin. Iwe, ika en emon sou tipatchem, inaamwo ika ka mwot lon ewe wa mi fiti whale, nge kosap meefi lon letipom eu chok kukkun niuokkus, lap seni om kopwe mwot mwen om ekkei lekuniol ren efoch poker, nge esap efoch harpoon, ren pechen.
