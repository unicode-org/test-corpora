Pwal och, lon eu fansoun, le pwin are ran ekkewe chon sai rese tongeni iei ussun fetal won ewe waimw, ika pwe Ahap ese nom mwen meser; ika a uta non an we pivot-hole, ika a pwungun fetan won ekkewe plank nefinen ruu kinikin rese tongeni siwin, —ewe mast mi nap me ewe mizen; are ra kuna i a uta non ewe nenien fitikoko, —pechen mi manau a feino won ewe deck, usun ita epwe fetan; an we hat a slouch watte won mesan kewe; ina minne ina mwo ika ese mwokut an epwe uta, ina mwo ika ekkewe ran me pwin ra apachanong, pwe ese mwo suki non an we hammock; nge ra monota fan ena hat mi slouching, rese tongeni sinei non tipemwaramwar ika, ren ekkei meinisin, mesan a wesewesen suki fan ekkoch; are ika a chuen achocho ne scan ngenir; ese nifinifin, ina mwo ika a uta ina usun non ewe scuttle ren unusen ew awa won ewe aan, me ewe pwinin-pwichikar ese ausening ngeni a chufengen non ekkewe puun puun won ena kout me hat mi for seni fau. Ekkewe uf ewe pwinin a fen uren konik, ewe akkar non ewe ran mwirin a uren konik won; iwe iei usun, ran me ran, me pwin me pwin; ese chiwen feino fan ekkewe ira; met chok a mochen seni ewe cabin ena mettoch a tinato ren.

A mongo lon ewe chok leeni mi pwas; weween, an kewe ruu chok mongo, —mongon neanowas me mongon neanowas: mongon neanowas ese mwo tori; ese pwal kini an kewe ppwul; mi rochopwak a marita meinisin gnarled, usun ekkewe puun ira rese fen pwa ra pungunong, ekkewe ra chuen marita non ew neni mi uren puun, ina mwo ika ra mano non ewe verdure mi nom asan. Nge ina mwo ika unusen manauan a iei winiti ew chok mas won deck; me inaamwo ika ewe fansoun mi monomon an ewe Parsee ese wor fansoun asoso usun chok pwisin an; nge ekkei ruemon rese mwo usun ita repwe kapas —emon mwan ngeni ewe emon —ika pwe non fansoun nangatam ekkoch mettoch mi fis rese auchea ra fori pwe epwe namwot. Ina mwo ika ena sokkun spell mi pochokun a usun ita pwe epwe monomon an epwe fiti ekkewe ruemon; pwarata, me ngeni ekkewe chon angang mi niuokkus, ra ussun ita pwe ra polufesen. Ika non ran ra tongeni apasa ew chok kapas; non pwin, mwan mi tiparoch ra ir me ruemon, ren ukukun ewe kukkun kapas ekkesiwin. Fan ekkoch, ren awa mi langattam, ese wor eu chok ttit, ra uta towaufesen lon saramen ekkewe fuu; Ahap lon an we waimw, ewe Parse ren ewe waimw mi watte; nge ra chuen chok nenengeni emon me emon; usun ita nge lon ewe Parse Ahap a kuna an we saram mi ffat, lon Ahap ewe Parse an we pisek mi likitalo.

Iwe nge, ekkoch, Ahap a fori —non pwisin napanapan, usun iteiten ran, iteiten awa, me iteiten fansoun, a pwarata ngeni ekkewe chon nom fan nemenian non annuk, —Ahap a usun emon samol mi pwisin nemeni inisin; ewe chon Pars nge noun chon angang. Pwal och, ir me ruuemon ra ussun ita ririfengen, me emon chon nemenem mi kirikiringau rese kuna a emweniir; ewe saram mi lean a siding ewe riri mi pochokkul. Pun epwe ei Parsee met epwe tongeni, meinisin rib me keel ra pochokun Ahap.

Non ewe aewin saramen ewe pwinin mi kukkun, mongungun we mi for seni mecha a rong seni mwirin, —“Man the mast-heads!” —me non unusen ewe ran, tori mwirin tuputiwen ewe akkar me mwirin pwinin, ewe chok mongungu iteiten awa, non ewe fitikoko an ewe chon uwei ewe sepenin, a rong —“Met d’ye see?—sharp!”!

Nge lupwen a tori ülüngät ika rüänü rän, mwirin an churi ewe Rachel mi kütta nöün kewe semirit; iwe ese wor spout a fen kuna; ewe chinnap mwan mi monomon a ussun ita ese lukuluk won tuppwolun noun kewe chon angang; nge, seni arapakan meinisin lukun chok ekkewe chon Pagan; a usun ita a tipemwaramwar, pwan mwo, ika Stubb me Flask resap tongeni tipeew ne poutano ewe nenien nengeni a kutta. Nge ika pwe ekkena tipemwaramwar ra wesewesen an, a tipatchem le tumunu pwe esap apasawu ren kapas, inaamwo ika an kewe foffor repwe ussun ita pwarata.

“Upwe pwisin kuna ewe aewin kuna ewe whale,” —a apasa. “Ewer, Ahap epwe eäni ewe föün äsefich!” me ren pwisin poun kewe a fori eu neni mi uren basket; me a tinato poun ngeni nang, ren ew chok blok mi uren fitikoko, pwe epwe riri ngeni ewe mokuren ewe main-mast, a angei ekkewe ruu nesoponon ewe sepi mi fitikoko ngeni fan; me a apacha eu ngeni an we pasikit a ammonlata eu pin fan iten ewe epek, pwe epwe tongeni apacha ngeni ewe rail. Ei a fis, ren ena nesoponon mi chuen nom non poun me a uta unukun ewe pin, a nenengeni unukun noun kewe chon angang, a suki seni ew ngeni ewe pwan ew; a auku an nenengeni fansoun langattam won Dago, Kike, Tastego; nge a su seni Fetal; me mwirin a anomu mesan mi pochokun ne apinukunuk won ewe chienan mi nap, a apasa, —“Angei ewe sepi, samol —ua ngeni non poum, Starbuck.” Mwirin a akkota inisin non ewe pasikit, a ngeni ewe kapas pwe repwe uwei ngeni an we nenien mwottun king, Starbuck ewe emon a amwokutu ewe sepi non soponon; iwe mwirin a uta arap ngeni. Iwe ina usun, ren efoch poun a kamwoch won ewe mast mi king, Ahap a nenengeni lukun ewe sat ren fite mail me mail, —me mwen, otiw, ei pekin, me ena, —non ewe watten circle mi watte a nemenem won eu neni mi fokkun tekia.

Nupwen a angang ren poun kewe non ekkoch neni mi tekia arapakan towaw non ewe rigging, minne ese tongeni wor nenien fetan, ewe chon sai won sat a suki ngeni ena neni, me a suki ikewe ren ewe sein; fan ekkei napanap, an we famini won deck fansoun meinisin a kawor non nemenem mi pwung ngeni emon emon mwan mi wor an ewe watten mas mi sokkono ren. Pokiten non ena sokkun fonu pon ren rigging mi fiti, ekkewe sokopaten sokkopaten ririfengen won nang rese tongeni fansoun meinisin repwe tongeni weweiti ren met a kuna senir non ewe deck; me nupwen ekkewe deck-ends ren ekkei ropwan ra kan kouno iteiten fitu minich seni ekkewe fastening, epwe chok ew napanap mi mano, ika, ese wor emon chon mas mi kan tumunu, ewe chon sai mi uwei epwe ren ekkoch tumunungaw an ekkewe chon angang epwe kouno me turutiw meinisin ngeni ewe sat. Ina minne, an Ahap kewe foffor lon ei poraus esap eu mettoch mi amwarar; ewe chok mettoch mi amwarar usun ir a usun ita, pwe Starbuck, arapakan ewe emon chok mwan a fen tipepos ne ungeni i ren och mettoch non ewe kukkun ukukun a arap ngeni finata —emon me nein ekkewe pwan, ewe an tuppwol won ewe nenengeni a usun ita a ekis tipemwaramwar; —a amwarar, pwe iei ewe chok noun watchman epwe finata fan itan; a pwisin fang unusen manauan ngeni poun ena sokkun aramas ese lukuluk won.

Iei, ewe aewin fansoun Ahap a nom won; ere a fen nomw non ena neni engon minich; emon me lein ekkena manmwacho mi uren fitikoko mi kan fitikoko fan chommong mi arap ngeni ekkewe mast mi wor aramas lon ekkena latitude; emon me nein ekkei man a feito a wiil me a puchor won mokuran non ew maze ren ekkewe circle mi mwitir ese tongeni fis. Mwirin a suki ew ngerou fit wenechar ngeni ewe asepwan; mwirin a spiral ngeni fan, me a feino eddi sefan won mokuran.

Nge ren an nennengeni ewe leeni mi rochopwak me towau, a ussun ita pwe Ahap ese esissinata ei man mwacho; are, ennetin, emon epwe fen makkei chommong, esap ew napanap mi fis fan chommong; iei chok arapakan ewe mesen ausening mi kisikis a usun ita a kuna och sokkun wewen mi otuputup non arapakan meinisin kuna.

“Om hat, om hat, samol!” mwitir chok a puchor ewe chon sai seni Sicily, ewe a nom won ewe mizen-mast-mokuren, a uta wenechar mwirin Ahap, ina mwo ika a ekis tekison seni an we niwin, me a wor eu ttit mi watte a aimufeseniir.

Nge a fen ewe wing sable a nom mwen mesen ewe chinnap mwan; ewe bill mi langatam mi hook won mokuran: ren ew puchor, ewe hawk mi pwech a suno ren an we niwin.

Emon ikel a fitikoko fan ulungat won mokuren Tarquin, a angei an we kap pwe epwe siwini, iwe mwirin Tanaquil, puluwan we, a apasa pwe Tarquin epwe kingen Rom. Nge ren chok ewe siwinin ewe kap ena omen a ekieki pwe mi och. Esap chüen wor üfen Ahap we hat; ewe manmwacho a fiti me fiti; towaw me mwen ewe prow: me mwirin a morono; nge seni ewe nenien ena morono, ew minich mi pwechepwech a ekis kuna, a turutiw seni ena watten neni ngeni ewe sat.
