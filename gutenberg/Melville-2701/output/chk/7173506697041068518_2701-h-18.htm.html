SOPUN 89. Fast-Ik me Los-Ik.

Ewe kapas ussun ekkewe waif me ekkewe waif-pole lon ewe sopwun le sopwoloon nge eu chok, a lamot ngeni ekkoch poraus ussun ekkewe alluk me alluk fan iten ewe angangen attau, iwe, ewe waif epwe tongeni ekieki pwe ina ewe esissin me badge mi lapalap.

Fan chommong a fis pwe lupwen fitu sipw ra saifetal lon eu mwich, emon whale epwe tongeni kkuf ren eu sipw, mwirin epwe su, me mwirin epwe malo me kkuf ren pwal eu sipw; me ikei ra indirectly kapachelong chommong kukkun mettoch mi fis, meinisin ra fiti ei ew napanap mi nap. Awewe chok, —mwirin an a apwangapwang me efeiengaw an epwe fiti me angei emon whale, ewe inis epwe tongeni ngaselo seni ewe sipw pokiten eu fitikoko mi pochokkul; me a ssa towau ngeni leeward, epwe angei sefani ren emon aruwen whale, ewe, non ew kinamwe, snugly a suki ngeni unukun, ese wor feiengawen manauan ika line. Ina usun ekkewe anini mi kon aletipechou me fitikoko repwe kan fis lefilen ekkewe chon attaw, ika ese wor ekkoch alluk mi mak are rese mak, mi weneiti unusen fonufan, ese wor anini won, mi weneiti ekkewe case meinisin.

Neman ewe chok alluk mi mumuta ren alluk mi mumuta ren alluk, ina ewe alluk lon Holland. A fen affat me ren ekkewe muu lon AD 1695. Nge inaamwo ika ese wor pwal eu muu a fen makkei eu alluk fan iten an epwe nnielo man, nge ekkewe chon attau lon Merika ra pwisin fori alluk me sou kapwung lon ei poraus. Ra awora eu kokkot mi titchik mi lap seni ekkewe Pandect an Justinian me ekkewe Alluk mi Kisikis fan iten ewe Mwichen Sou Pwungupwungun China fan iten an epwe pinei an aramas repwe angolong lon fofforun ekkewe ekkoch aramas. Ewer; ekkena alluk repwe tongeni mak won efoch farthing an Kiwin Anne, are won efoch ttikin efoch ketilas, me repwe ufouf won nepekin, ina minne, ra kukkun.

I. Emon Fast-Ik a nom non ewe pekin fast ngeni.

II. Ew Loose-Ik ina ew minen urumot mi pung ngeni emon mi tongeni mwitir angei.

Nge met a efisata ewe fofforingau ren ei kokkot mi lingoch, ina ewe mwochomwochen poraus mi amwarar, iwe, a lamot ngeni eu watten chochoon aweween poraus pwe epwe awewei.

Aewin: Met weween eu Famini? Emon ik mi manau are malo a fokkun mwittir, lupwen a riri ngeni eu sipw are waa mi wor aramas lon, ren och mettoch mi tongeni nemeni ren ewe chon wawa are ekkewe chon wawa, —eu mast, eu oar, eu kabel mi tiu inis, eu wire mi telegraph, are eu ttit mi fiti cobweb, meinisin ra ussun chok. Pwal ina chök usun emön ik a fokkun ässäw lupwen a uwei efoch waif, are pwal och esissil mi iteföülo pwe a nom ren; ika pwe ewe pekin waifing a affata ar tufichin non fansoun meinisin ar repwe angei, pwan ar ekiekin fori.

Ikkeei ekkewe porausen science; nge ekkewe porausen ekkewe chon fiti ewe whale pwisin fan ekkoch ra masou ren kapas mi pochokkul me kokko mi pochokkul —ewe Kok-won-Littleton ewe fiu. Pwungun pwe lein ekkewe whalemen mi wenechar me itefoulo, fansoun meinisin ra kan awora alillis fan iten ekkewe case mi sokkolo aucheaan, ikewe ie epwe eu foffor ese pwung mi fokkun ngau ika emon epwe apasa pwe a wor ren emon whale mi fen nnielo are nielo me lom ren pwal eu pekin. Nge pwal ekkoch rese fokkun ina ussun.

Ina epwe limu ier lom a wor eu case mi amwarar ussun emon chon atai alluk mi kapwung lon England, ikewe ie ekkewe chon tipis ra apasa pwe mwirin ar achocho le kutta emon man lon ekkewe matauen Notou; me nupwen ennetin ir (ekkewe chon tipis) ra fen sopwosopwono ne harpoon ekkewe ik; ra soposopono, ren feiengawen manauer, ra wisen nikitano esap chok ar kewe line, nge pwisin ar we wa. Ren enletin ekkewe chon tipis (ekkewe chon angangen pwan ew sipw) ra feito ren ewe whale, ra atai, nieno, angei, me soposopono ra angei me mwen mesen ekkewe chon tipis. Iwe nupwen ekkena chon tipis ra apungu, nour we kaptin a suki poun kewe non ekkewe chon tipis, me a anukunuku ngenir pwe ren an epwe doxology ngeni ewe foffor a fori, iei epwe chok amwochu ar line, harpoons, me wa, minne ra chok nom won ewe whale non ewe fansoun ra angei. Ina popun ekkewe chon tipis ra iei usun tingor pwe epwe niwinsefaniti auchean ar kewe whale, line, harpoon, me wa.

Erskine a emon chon aninis ngeni ekkewe chon tipis; Ewe Samol Ellenborough a ewe sou kapung. Lon ewe fansoun a fen apwungalo, ewe Erskine mi tipatchem a sopweilo le awewei an ekiek, ren an kapas ussun eu fofforingau mi keran fis. kon. case, ikewe emon mwan mi murino, mwirin an ese wor namotan an achocho ne nemeni an punuwan we fofforun kirikiringaw, a fen soposopono ne nikitano won ekkewe sat non manau; nge non ekkewe ier, a aier seni ena foffor, a poputani ew foffor pwe epwe angei sefani pisekin neminewe. Erskine a nom won ewe epek; iwe mwirin a anisi ren an apasa, pwe ina mwo ika ewe mwan a akkomw harpoon ngeni ewe fefin, me a fen fori an fast fan ew, me pokiten chok ewe watten osukosuken an fofforungaw mi plunging, a fen soposopono ne nikitano i; nge a likitalo i a fori, pwe a wiliiti emon ik mi lingoch; iwe ina minne nupwen emon mwan mwirin a harpoon sefan ngeni, ewe fefin a mwitir winiti pisekin ena mwan mwirin, fiti met chok harpoon epwe tongeni kuna pwe a fiti.

Iei non ewe case ikenai Erskine a apasa pwe ekkewe nenien appiru ren ewe whale me ewe fefin ra awewefengenni emon me emon.

Ekkei tingor, me ekkewe tingor mi ungeni, ra rongorong pwungun, ewe sou-kapwun mi fokkun tipachem non ekkewe kapas mi kefinita a finata, wewen, —Pwe usun ewe wa, a ngeni ekkewe chon tipis, pokiten ra chok nikitano pwe repwe amanaua manauer; nge pwe ren ewe waimw, ekkewe harpoon, me line mi wor anini won, ir noun ekkewe chon tipis; ewe waimw, pokiten i emon Loos-Ik non ewe fansoun a fis ewe amuchunon fitikoko; me ekkewe harpoon me line pun nupwen ewe ik a fori off ren ir, a (ewe ik) angei ew pisek non ekkena pisek; iwe ina popun emon me emon mwirin a angei ewe ik a wor an pwuung ngenir. Iei ekkewe chon tipis mwirin ra angei ewe ik; ergo, ekkewe poraus mi mak asan ra nom rer.

Emon aramas mi itefoulo mi nengeni ei kefil an ewe Sou Kapwung mi fokkun tipatchem, epwe tongeni u ngeni. Nge a tori ewe aewin fau ren ewe poraus, ekkewe ruu nongonong mi nap mi mak non ekkewe annukun twin whale mi fen mak me mwan, me ra aea me awewei me ren Lord Ellenborough non ewe case mi mak asan; ekkei ruu annuk mi weneiti Fast-Ik me Loose-Ik, ua apasa, epwe, ren ekiek, kuna ekkewe nongonongen meinisin annukun aramas; pun inaamwo ika a weires an epwe forata ekkewe pisekin maun, nge ewe imwen fel fan iten ewe Alluk, ussun chok ewe imwen fel an chon Filistia, a wor ruu chok pisekin alillis epwe uta won.

Esap pwe eu kapas mi nom lon auen emon me emon, ‘Ewe pisek ina esopw ewe alluk,’ weween, ese lifilifil ifa usun ewe pisek a tori aramas? Nge fan chommong pisek ina unusen ewe alluk. Met ekkewe siwin me ngunun ekkewe chon fotek lon Russia me ekkewe chon fotek lon ewe Republican nge ekkewe chok Fast-Fish, nge ar kewe pisek ina unusen ewe alluk? Met ngeni ewe chon imw mi fitikoko ina ewe amuchunon monien ewe fefin mi mano punuwan nge emon Fast-Fish? Met a nom ikewe ie ewe imwen chon koum mi for seni marble mi wor eu asam-plate fan iten emon waif; met ena nge ew Fast-Ik? Met ewe discount mi efeiengaw minne Mortokai, ewe chon amomo, a angei seni Wopekone mi woungaw, ewe mi bankrupt, won ew loan pwe epwe tumunu an Wopekone famini seni echik; met ena discount mi ngaw nge ew Fast-Ik? Met ewe monien Savesoul £100,000 a angei seni ewe pilawa me cheese mi kukkun seni fitepuku ngerou chon angang mi tatakis (ir meinisin ra nukuchar ngeni nang nge ese wor emon an Savesoul aninis) met ena £100,000 mi uren globe nge emon Fast-Fish? Met ekkewe telinimw me sopw mi nom ren ewe Duke Dunder nge Fast-Fish? Met ngeni ena chon fiti ewe mwichen sounfiu mi tipemwaramwar, John Bull, pwe Ireland mi woungau, nge emon chon fiti ewe mwichen sounfiu? Met ngeni ena chon fiti ewe mwichen aposel, Pwiich Jonathan, Texas nge ew Fast-Ik? Iwe usun ekkei meinisin, esap pwe Posses ina unusen ewe annuk?

Nge ika ewe afanafan usun Fast-Fish epwe fokkun fis ngeni aramas meinisin, ewe afanafan mi riri ngeni Loose-Fish a chuen watte an epwe fis. Ina met a fis won unusen fonufan me won unusen fonufan.

Met weween Merika lon 1492 nge esap eu Los-Ik, ikewe ie Columbus a ataielo ewe alluk lon Spain ren an epwe wisen fori ngeni noun we masta me fefin mi king? Met weween Poland ngeni ewe King? Met Kriis ngeni ewe chon Turkey? Met India ngeni England? Met mwirin epwe fis ngeni Mexico ngeni Merika? Meinisin Los-Ik.

Met ekkewe Pwungun Aramas me ekkewe Ngasenon Fonufan nge Loose-Fish? Met meinisin aramas ekiekin me mefier nge Loose-Ik? Met ewe nongonongen nuku non lamalam non ir nge ew Loose-Ik? Met ngeni ekkewe chon apasawu kapasen otuputup mi pwarata ekiekin ekkewe chon ekiek nge Loose-Fish? Met ewe globe mi watte pwisin nge eu Los-Ik? Iwe met en, chon anneani, nge emon Loos-Ik me emon Fast-Ik, pwan?





SOPUN 90. Ekkewe mokuren ika tailin.

“A naf ekewe mönükol, nge ewe ät a nom fän nemenian, nge ewe fefin a nom fän nemenian.” Brakton, l. 3, c. 3.

Latin seni ekkewe puken Alluk lon England, ewe a fitifengen me ewe poraus, a wewe ngeni, pwe seni meinisin ekkewe whale mi nninniilo ren emon mi nom won ewe matau lon ena fonu, ewe King, ussun chok ewe Chon Angang mi Lapalap, epwe wor mokuran, me ewe Kiwin epwe sufoliiti an epwe pwarata ewe ttit. Ew kinikin minne, non ewe whale, a fokkun usun chok kinikinin ew apel; ese wor eu minen apwapwa mi nom lefilen. Iei usun ei annuk, fan ew napanap mi siwin, a tori ikenai a pochokun non England; me usun a awora non sokopaten napanap ew anomaly mi amwarar mi tori ewe annukun Fast me Loose-Fish, a mak ikei ie non ew sopwun mi sokkono, won ewe chok nongonongen kirekiroch mi amwokutu ekkewe chon England ar repwe nomw non ewe niwinin ew mota mi sokkono, mi kefinita fan iten nenien ekkewe chon king. Non ewe aewin kinikin, non ew pisekin pwarata mi amwarar pwe ewe annuk mi mak asan a chuen pochokun, ua sopweno ne apasa me mwen ami ew napanap a fis non ekkewe ruu ier ra no.

A ussun ita pwe ekkoch chon sai mi wenechar lon Dover, are Sandwich, are ekkoch eu me lein ekkewe leenien sai lon Cinque, ra fen mwirin eu fitikoko mi weires le nnielo me ataielo efoch waimw mi murinno minne ra akkom awewei towau seni ewe matau. Iei ekkewe Cinque Ports ra nom fan nemenien eu sokkun polis are beadle, mi iteni emon Samol mi Lapalap. A amwochu ewe ofes seni ewe muu, ua luku pwe meinisin ekkewe liwinin king mi fis ngeni ekkewe fonuen Cinque Port ra wiliiti ren wisan an. Ren ekkoch soumak ei ofes a iteni sinecure. Nge esap ina usun. Pokiten ewe Samon Warten a angang weires fan ekkoch ren an fopun an kewe perkisi; minne ir an kewe mi nap ren pochokunen ena chok fobbing ngenir.

Iei nupwen ekkei chon sai mi woungaw mi keni ewe akkar, rese ufouf pecher, me ren ar kewe trowser ra rokopwanieno tekian won pecher kewe mi eely, ra fen menemenoch ne uwei nour kewe ik mi fat tekia me pwas, ra pwon ngeni pwisin ir ew £150 mi och seni ewe oil me futuk mi auchea; me non ar ekiek ra unumi tea mi rare ren punuwer kewe, me ale mi murinno ren chiener kewe, won pochokunen ar kewe kinikin; a feito emon mwan mi fokkun tipachem me a fokkun chon Kraist me kirekiroch, a wor eu kapiin Blackstone fan poun; me a isenalong won mokuren ewe whale, a apasa —“Oupwe tou! Ei ik, nei kewe masta, emon Fast-Fish. Ua angei pwe an ewe Samon Warden.” Won ei ekkewe chon sai mi woungaw non ar niuokus mi sufon —ina wesewesen chon England —rese sinei met repwe apasa, ra turutiw ngeni ar pochokun ne suki mokurer unukun; me mwen ena a nenengeni ren netipengaw seni ewe whale ngeni ewe chon ekis. Nge ena ese fokkun awenechara ewe osukosuk, are ese fokkun amoielo letipen ewe mwan mi silelap mi pochokkul ren ewe kapiin Blackstone. Mwirin och fansoun, emon leir, mwirin an fansoun langattam a ekieki ussun an kewe ekiek, a pwora le kapas, .

Ren ach ekieki pwe ika sipwe nengeni non ekkoch napanap, ewe case epwe tongeni ren ew chok tufich non ekkoch kukkun napanap epwe ekieki, fan ekkewe napanap, ew mi kon weires, emon nouwis mi wenechar non ewe teninimw a makkei ew taropwe ngeni an we Grace non sufon, a tingor ngeni an epwe angei ewe case usun ekkena chon sai mi osukosuk non unusen ekiek. Nge pwata ewe King epwe wor mokuran, me ewe Kiwin epwe wor uwan? Ew popun ren ena, ami sou annuk!

Nge ewe Kiwin emon chon fiti ewe mwichen sounfiu, pwe epwe pwarata eu ttit? Eú weween kapas áwewe epwe tongeni nóm ikei ie.

Mei wor ruu ik mi king mi fokkun style ren ekkewe sou makkei alluk lon England —ewe whale me ewe sturgeon; ir me ruu pisekin king fan ekkoch aukukun, me noun kewe ra awora ewe engonun kinikinin ewe monien muun aramas meinisin. Use sinei pwe emon soumak a fen hint usun ewe poraus; nge ren ai ekieki pwe a ussun ita ngeniei pwe ewe sturgeon epwe kinikinfesen lon ewe chok lapalap ussun chok ewe whale, ewe King epwe angei ewe mokuran mi fokkun lingoch me lingoch mi sokkolo ngeni ena ik, ewe, ika sipwe ekieki ussun lon kapas monomon, epwe tongeni fis pwe epwe longolong won ekkoch mettoch aramas ra ekieki pwe ra fiti tong. Iwe ina usun a usun ita pwe mi wor popun non mettoch meinisin, pwan mwo non annuk.

Ewe niuokus ese mochen non ewe fansoun a fori an epwe su, paddle non poun, seni ewe wa; me non ew napanap, pwe kinikinin ewe linein whale mi slack a feito ngeni pechen, a suki ngeni ewe sat ren, pwe epwe fitikoko non, nupwen a soposopono ne turutiw non ewe konik. Non ena fansoun ewe whale mi tori a poputa won ew ran mi pochokun, ewe line a mwitir ne wenechar; me presto! Pip mi woungaw a feito meinisin a foummong ngeni ekkewe chochon ewe wa, ese niwinsefan a suki ikewe ren ewe line, minne a fen angei fitu turn won pechen me nepekin.

Tasteko a ütä lon ekewe lenien ässäw. A uren ekkeiin ewe hunt. A oput Pip ren emon chon pol. A angei ewe niwinin wa seni an we ttit, a suki ewe ttit mi fitikoko won ewe line, me a suki ngeni Stubb, a puchor non kapas eis, “Kut?” Non ena fansoun Pip a blue, chok mesan a pwarata, For, fan iten Kot! Meinisin ra pass lon eu chok fansoun. Non ukukun esopw minich, unusen ei mettoch a fis.

“Kopwe atai i, kopwe!” a rokopwanieno Stubb; iwe ina usun ewe whale a pout seni Pip a manau.

Ina minne, lupwen a chok pwisin chikar, ewe kukkun negro mi woungau a kkuf ren puchor me kapasen turunufas seni ekkewe chon angang. Non kinamwe a mut ngeni ekkei kapasen turunufas ese pwung ar repwe morono, Stubb iwe non ew napanap mi fatafatoch, usun chok business, nge mi chuen kinikinin urumot, a turunufasei Pip non ofes; me ena mettoch a fori, a ngeni i chommong fon mi murinno. Ewe mettoch a apasa, Kosapw mwo su seni ew wa, Pip, tiwenon chok —nge meinisin ekkewe ekkoch ese much, usun ewe aurour mi murinno seni meinisin. Iei, non unusan, Kopwe fiti ewe wa , ina om ennetin kapasen pesepes non whaling; nge case epwe fis fan ekkoch nupwen Leap seni ewe wa , a chuen murinno. Pwal och, ussun ita nge a miritiiti le sopwoloon pwe ika epwe ngeni Pip kapasen fon mi popu seni mwelien letipan, epwe likiti ngeni eu leeni mi kon watte pwe epwe tongeni fiti fan iten mwach kkan; Stubb a mwitir nikitano meinisin aurour, me a awesi ren ew annuk mi pochokun, “Kopwe chok fiti ewe wa, Pip, ika ren ewe Samon, usap angei en ika kopwe su; ekieki ena. kopwe pwal fiti.” Iei eni Stubb a apasa pwe inaamwo ika aramas ra tongei chiener kewe, nge aramas ir emon man mi kutta moni, iwe, ewe mochen a kan eppeti an kirokiroch.

Nge kich meinisin sia nom fan nemenien ekkewe Kot; me Pip a suki sefan. A nom fan ekkewe lapalap mi fokkun ussun chok ewe aewin urumot; nge non ei fansoun ese suki ewe line; iwe ina popun, nupwen ewe whale a poputa ne su, Pip a nom won ewe sat, usun chok emon chon sai mi mwitir. Feiengau! Stubb a nge a kon ennet ngeni an kapas. Ina eu ran mi ling, uren pisek, me blue; ewe sat mi ling a kinamwe me pwichikar, me a ititiw, unukun, ngeni ewe puun, usun chok inisin emon chon for kolt mi hammer ngeni ewe neni mi fokkun watte. A bobbing won me fan non ena sat, mokuren Pip mi ebon a pwarata usun mokuren cloves. Ese wor efoch wa-knife a suki nupwen a fokkun mwitir turutiw ngeni aster. Stubb a fiti ewe mwich ese tongeni awesi; iwe ewe waimw a fiti. Non unungat minich, ew unusen mwail ewe sat ese wor sat a nom nefinen Pip me Stubb. Seni ewe nefinen ewe sat, Pip mi woungaw a siwini mokuran mi uren fitikoko, uren fitikoko, mi pwechepwech ngeni ewe akkar, pwan emon castaway mi chok pwisin nonnom, ina mwo ika ewe mi tekia me saramaram.

Iei, lon fansoun kinamwe, an epwe ssa lon ewe matau mi pwas a mecheres ngeni ewe chon ssa mi fen kaeo ussun chok an epwe wawa eu spring-carriage ngeni matau. Nge ewe lolilen mi fokkun ngau ese tongeni likiitu fan. Ewe watten ekiekin pwisin kich non nefinen ena sokkun ekiek ese wor netip, ai Kot! io epwe tongeni apasa? Mark, ifa usun nupwen chon sai non ew kinamwe mi mano ra tonong non ewe sat mi pwas —mark ifa usun ra arap ngeni ar we sipw me chok coast won pechen kewe.

Nge Stubb a wesewesen likitalo ewe kukkun negro mi woungau ngeni an epwe kuna feioch? Aapw; ese mochen, ese lifilifil. Pokiten a wor ruu wa non an we wake, me a ekieki, ese wor tipemwaramwar, pwe repwe wesewesen feito ngeni Pip mi fokkun mwitir, me angei i; inaamwo ika, enlet, ekkena sokkun ekiek ngeni ekkewe chon sai mi feiengau ren pwisin ar niuokkus, ese kan pwapwalo ren ekkewe chon atake lon meinisin ekkewe lapalap mi ussun chok ena; me ekkena sokkun fansoun rese kan fis fan chommong; arapakan fansoun meinisin lon ewe angangen attaw, emon mi niuokkus, ina ussun an iteni, a esissin ren ewe chok sokkun oput mi kirikiringau mi fis ngeni ekkewe sounfiu mi fiti maun me ekkewe sounfiu mi fiti maun.

Nge a fis, pwe ekkena wa, rese kuna Pip, ra mwitir ne spai ekkewe whale mi arap ngenir won ew pekin, ra suki, me ra ngeni chase; me an Stubb we wa iei a fokkun towaw, me i me meinisin noun kewe chon angang ra fokkun ekieki usun noun kewe ik, pwe ewe ringen Pip a poputa ne watteno unukun non napanap mi ngaw. Ren chok ewe fansoun ewe sipw pwisin a soposopono ne amanaua i; nge seni ena awa ewe kukkun negro a fetan won ewe deck emon chon tiparoch; sokkun, ese nifinifin, ra apasa pwe i. Ewe sat a fen jeerioch ne amwochu inisin mi finioch, nge a atai ewe ese much an ngun. Ese unusen nninniilo, nge. Nge a uwei ngeni manau ngeni ekkewe leeni mi alollol mi amwarar, ikewe ie ekkewe lapalap mi amwarar lon ewe fonufan mi akkomw rese siwil ra glide ngeni me mwen mesan kewe mi passive; me ewe chon pisekisek, Tipatchem, a pwarata an kewe pisek mi ionifengeni; me nein ekkewe fansoun ese much mi pwapwa, ese wor netiper, mi chok kukkun fansoun meinisin, Pip a kuna ekkewe chommong, Kot-mi nom ekis meinisin, ekkewe inis mi koral, pwe seni ewe nang konik ra suki ekkewe orbs mi watte. A kuna pechen Kot won ewe puun, a apasa; iwe ina popun chienan kewe chon sai ra eita ngeni mad. Ina minne, an aramas tiparoch ina ekiekin lang; me ra rikino seni meinisin popun mi tongeni mano, aramas ra feito soposopono ngeni ena ekiekin celestial, minne, ren ekiek, mi umwes me uren fitikoko; me pisekisek are riaffou, a mefi mwirin ese tongeni tipemwaramwar, ese ekieki usun an Kot.

Ren ekkewe ekkoch, tipi ngeni Stubb pwe ese kon watte. Ewe mettoch a fis non ena nenien attaw; me non ewe mwirin ewe poraus, epwe mwitir kuna met usun abandon a fis ngeniei.
