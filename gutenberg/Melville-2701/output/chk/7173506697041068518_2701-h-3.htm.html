SOPUN 10. Emon Chiechi mi Bosom.

Nupwen ua niwin ngeni ewe Spouter-Inn seni ewe imwen fel, ua kuna Queequeg ikewe ie mi fokkun chok pwisin i; i a fen tou seni ewe Chapel me mwen ewe feioch ekkoch fansoun. A mwot won ew pet me mwen ewe ekkei, me pechen kewe ra nom won ewe rumwen stove, me non ew poun a amwochu arap ngeni mesan ena kukkun uluulun negro an; a nenengeni pochokun non mesan, me ren ew jack-knife a mosonoson ne suki ngeni noun, non ena fansoun a ngungures ngeni pwisin i non an napanapen chon koum.

Nge pokiten iei a fis, a isetiu ewe uluulun; me ekis chok fansoun, a feino ngeni ewe tepwe, a angei ew watten puk ikewe, me a isoni won pechen a poputa ne anneani ekkewe peich ren an ekiekin fori iteitan; non iteiten fisu peich —usun ua ekieki —a uta ekis, a nenengeni ese wor namotan unukun, me a awora kapas ngeni ew whistle mi gurgling mi fis fansoun nangatam ren an mairu. Iwe epwe poputa sefan non ekkewe fitepuku mwirin; usun ita a poputa non nampa ew iteitan, usun ita ese tongeni anneani nap seni fitepuku, me a chok ren ena ukukun chommongun fitepuku ra kuna fengen, pwe an mairu ren chommongun ekkewe peich a fis.

Ren watten mochen ua mwot ne nenengeni i. Savage ina mwo ika i, me a fokkun uren ngaw usun mesan —ren ai mochen —mesan a wor och mettoch non minne ese fokkun ngaw. Kosap tongeni opano ewe ngun. Ren meinisin an kewe tattoo rese nom won fonufan, ua ekieki pwe ua kuna ekkewe esisinen ew netip mi wenechar mi mecheres; me non mesan kewe mi watte, mi uren fitikoko, mi pwechepwech me pwora, a usun esisinen ew ngun epwe pworacho ngeni ew ngerou tefin. Iwe me nukun ekkei meinisin, a wor ew napanap mi tekia usun ewe Pagan, minne pwan mwo an ese tongeni unusen ataieno. A usun emon mwan ese mwo cring me ese mwo wor emon chon liwinimmang. Ika pwe ina, pwan, pwe mokuran a suki, mokuran a suki non ew napanap mi ngaseno me saramaram, me a nikinikin watte nap seni met epwe fis ika esap ina, ei usapw tipemwaramwar ne finata; nge mi enlet pwe mokuran a fokkun murinno. Meni epwe usun ita mi mwaken, nge a achema ngeniei usun mokuren General Washington, usun a pwa non ekkewe bust mi itefouno usun i. A wor ewe chok sokkun chuk mi langattam mi fitikoko seni won ekkewe chuk, ekkewe ra pwal fokkun pwapwalo, ussun chok ruu chuk mi langattam mi uren ira won. Kuweek a föfföri ewe föfförün lisowu mwääl.

Nupwen ua ina usun ai arap ngeni ai upwe scan i, ua kan fori pwe ua chok nenengeni ewe storm seni ewe casement, ese mwo ausening ngeni ai nomw, ese mwo osukosuk ren ukukun ew chok nenengeni; nge a ussun ita pwe a unusen osukosuk ren an alleani ekkewe pekin taropwe lon ewe puk mi amwarar. Nupwen ua ekieki usun am aua fen annut fengen non ewe pwinin me mwen, me akkaewin ekieki usun ewe poun tong ua kuna a aturanong won ngang nupwen ua nennelo non ewe pwinin, ua ekieki pwe ei ese ekieki usun an fokkun amwarar. Nge ekkewe manmwacho ir aramas mi sokkolo; fan ekkoch kese fokkun sinei ifa usun kopwe angeiir. Akkomw ra chék chéúfetál; ar kinamwe le pwisin ionifengenni ar mecheresin manau a ussun ita eu tipatchemen Socrates. Ua fen kuna pwan pwe Queequeg ese fokkun chiechi ngeni, ika nge a fokkun kukkun, ren ekkewe ekkoch chon sai non ewe imwen chon sai. Ese fori och mettoch mi murinno; a ussun ita pwe ese mochen alapalo ewe mwichen aramas mi silei. Ekkei meinisin ra toriei usun emon mi pochokun; nge, atun ua ekieki, a wor och mettoch mi arapakkan tekia lon. Iei emon mwan ukukun ruwe ngerou mwail seni imwan, ren ewe aanen Cape Horn, wewen —ina ewe chok aan a tongeni tori ikewe —a kouno nein aramas mi sokkono ngeni usun ita a nom non ewe fonufan Jupiter; iwe inamo a usun ita a unusen kinamwe; tumunu ewe kinamwe mi fokkun watte; pwapwa ren pwisin an chiechi ngeni; fansoun meinisin a wewe ngeni pwisin i. Pwungun pwe ei a eu minen apwapwa mi murinno; inaamwo ika ese mwaal ese mwo rongorong pwe a wor ena sokkun mettoch. Nge, eni, pwe sipwe ennetin chon tipatchem, kich aramas mi tongeni mano sisapw mefi pwe sipwe fokkun manau are fokkun achocho. Ina minne, lupwen ua chok rongorong pwe ena sokkun mwan a fangelo pwisin i fan iten emon sou tipatchem, ua ekieki pwe, ussun chok ewe fefin chinnap mi semmwen ren an mongo, epwe fen “atai an we digester.”

Nupwen ua mwot ikewe non ena rum mi monomon iei; ewe ekkei a ngetenget fan eu, non ena kinikin mi mosonoson nupwen, mwirin an we aewin pochokun a fen apwapwa ewe asepwan, mwirin a chok saramaram pwe epwe nenengeni; ekkewe saram me ekkewe saram non ewe pwinin ra chufengen unukun ekkewe casement, me ra nenengenikem me ruemon mi monomon, mi chok pwisin nonnom; ewe fitikoko a fis nge ese wor non solemn; Ua poputa ne mefi ekkewe memef mi amwarar. Ua mefi ew menemenoch non ngang. Ese chiwen wor netipei mi tatakis me pei mi mad ra siwin ngeni ewe fonufan mi wolf. Ei safei mi kinamwe a fen amanaua. Ikenai a mwot, an fokkun tipemwaramwar a kapas usun eu napanap ese wor fofforun otuputup mi siwil me fofforun otuputup mi monomon. Wild i a; ew fokkun saram ren saram pwe epwe kuna; nge ua poputa ne mefi pwe ua monomon ne arap ngeni i. Iwe ekkena chok mettoch repwe fen akkufu lape ngeni ekkewe ekkoch, ir ekkewe chok magnet mi ina ussun a amwokutuei. Ngang upwe sotuni emon chienei chon lukun, ua ekieki, pokiten kirekirochun chon Kraist a fen pwarata nge ese wor tipetekison. Ua amwokutu ai we pet arap ngeni, me fori ekkoch esisin me kapasen pesepes mi chiechioch, ua fori ai tufich ne fos ngeni non ena fansoun. Akkom ese kon lien kuna ekkena feffeita; nge iei, nupwen ua kapas usun an kewe awasola aramas non ewe pwinin a no, a fori pwe epwe eisiniei ika am aupwe chiwen chiechi fengen. Ua ereni pwe ewer; whereat ua ekieki pwe a nikinikin pwapwa, eni epwe ekis mwareiti.

Mwirin aua suki ewe puk fengen, me ua achocho ne awewei ngeni ewe popun a for, me wewen ekkewe ekkoch sasing mi nom non. Iei usun ua mwitir angei an mochen; me seni ena aua feino ngeni jabbering ewe mi murino aua tongeni usun ekkewe sokopaten nenien nengeni me nukun epwe kuna non ei teninimw mi itefouno. Ekiselo chok ua propose ngeni eu sopai; me, a fori an we poun me tomahawk, a ekis ngeniei ew puch. Iwe mwirin aua mwot ne siwini puff seni ena paip mi mwacho an, me tumunu pwe epwe fetan iteitan nefinem.

Ika pwe mi chuen wor och ice ese ekieki usun ngang non ewe ngunun Pagan, ei fitikoko mi apwapwa, mi kirekiroch aua nom ren, a mwitir chok atai, me a likiti ngenikem chon angang. A usun ita a angei ngeniei non napanap mi fokkun fis me ese wor tingor usun chok ngang ngeni i; iwe nupwen am we fitikoko a wes, a apachanong mokuran ngeni mesei, a amwochuei won pechei, me a apasa pwe seni ei fansoun am aua pupunu; wewen, non kapasen fonuan we, pwe am aua chiechioch; epwe pwapwa le mäla fän itei, ika epwe lamot. Non emon chon fonu, ei ekkein chiechioch mi mwitir epwe usun ita a fokkun mwitir, ew mettoch epwe fokkun nukuchar; nge non ei safei mi mecheres ekkena annuk mi nom resapw fis.

Mwirin mongon neanowas, me pwan ew porous fengen me fitikoko, aua feino ngeni am rum fengen. A föri ngeniei eu lifang seni möküran mi limelimoch; a angei noun we watten wallet mi uren sakaw, me a suki fan ewe sakaw, a angei ukukun ipuku dollar non silifer; mwirin a apachanong won ewe tepwe, me a mecheresin kinikin ngeni ruu kinikin mi ew chok, a pusin suki ew me neir ngeniei, me a apasa pwe ina ai. Ua fen mochen upwe apungu; nge a aúcheaniei ren an ninatiw lón ekkewe chénúpupun ai kewe trowser. Ua mut ngeniir ar repwe nom. Mwirin a fori an kewe iotek lekuniol, a angei noun we uluulun, me a angei ewe taropwen ekkei. Ren ekkoch esisin me napanap, ua ekieki pwe a usun ita a osukosuk ren ai upwe fiti i; nge pokiten ua sinei met epwe fis mwirin, ua ekieki ekis ika, ika epwe etiwaei, upwe aneasochisi ika esap ina.

Ngang emon chon Kraist mi murinno; a uputiw me uputiw non ewe nepoputan ewe Mwichefenin Presbyterian ese tongeni mwaken. Iwe, ifa usun, üpwe tongeni tipeeufengen me ei chon fel ngeni uluulun mi ngaw le fel ngeni an we kinikinin irä? Nge met weween fel? ekieki I. Ka ekieki iei, Ishmael, pwe ewe Koten nang me fonufan —chon lukun me meinisin ra kapachelong —a tongeni tongeni song ren eu kinikinin ira mi pwechepwech ese auchea? Ese tufich! Nge met weween fel? —ach sipwe fori letipen Kot —ina fel . Iwe met mochenin Kot?—pwe upwe fori ngeni chienei aramas met ngang upwe mochen chienei aramas repwe fori ngeniei —ina mochenin Kot. Iei, Queequeg ina chienei aramas. Iwe met ngang mei mochen pwe ei Queequeg epwe fori ngeniei? Pwata, kopwe tipeeufengen me ngang lon ai we sokkun fel ngeni Presbyterian. Ina popun, upwe mwitir tipeeu ngeni i lon an we; ergo, mi namwot upwe wiliiti chon fel ngeni uluulun. Iei mine üa keni ekewe föün äsefich; a anisi ne apochokuna ewe kukkun uluulun ese wor tipisin; a ngeni i ewe bisikit mi keni me Queequeg; salam mwen mesan fan ruu are fan ulungat; a kis ngeni noun we; me ena a fis, aua suki uf me feino ngeni nenien annut, non kinamwe ren pwisin mwelien letipem me unusen fonufan. Nge aua kan annut ika ese wor ekkoch kukkun chat.

Ifa usun a fis use sinei; nge ese wor eu leeni mi ussun eu pet fan iten poraus mi monomon lefilen chiechi. Mwan me fefin, ra apasa, ikewe ie ra suuki ewe wesewesen nepoputan ngunur ngeni emon me emon; me ekkoch pupulu mi chinnap ra kan kapas chofona me porausfengen won ekkewe fansoun lom tori arapakan lekuniol. Ina minne, non netipem, ngang me Queequeg aua nom —ew pean pupunu mi kinamwe me tong.





SOPUN 11. Ewe pwinin.

Aua fen annut ina usun non pet, aua porausfengen me annut non fansoun mwochomwoch, me Queequeg iei me mwirin a tongnei an epwe aturanong pechen kewe mi tattoo mi brown won pechei kewe, me mwirin a angei sefanir; ina minne unusen chiechioch me ngaseno me mecheres am; nupwen, soposopono, pokiten am kewe kon, met kukkun nappishness a nom rem a unusen tou, me aua mefi pwe aupwe uta sefan, ina mwo ika ewe fansoun asoso a chuen ekis towaw non fansoun mwach.

Ewer, aua fokkun mammasa; ina popun pwe am nenien mwottun king a poputa ne watteno, me ekis me ekis aua kuna pwe aua mwokut; ekkewe uf mi murinno ne suki ngenikem, ra anongonong won ewe head-board ren am kewe fomon pechen ra suki ngenikem, me am kewe ruemon noum ra suki won, usun ita am kewe pechen ra warm-pan. Aua mefi pwe aua fokkun murinno me snug, ewe nap seni pokiten a fokkun pwichikar me nukun asam; ennetin seni ekkewe ufoufen pet pwan, kuna pwe ese wor ekkei non ewe rum. Ewe nap seni, ua apasa, pokiten ennetin ren om kopwe pwapwaiti pwichikaren inis, ekkoch kukkun kinikinin inisimi epwe pwichikar, pun ese wor ew napanap non ei fonufan esapw met a nom ren chok sokofesenin. Ese wor och mettoch mi nom lon pwisin i. Ika ka apasa pwe ka fen kinamwe meinisin, me ka fen ina usun fansoun nangatam, iwe kosap tongeni apasa pwe ka kinamwe sefan. Nge ika, usun chok Queequeg me ngang non ewe pet, ewe nepoputan noum ika ewe nepoputan mokurom epwe ekis chikar, pwata iwe, ennetin, non ewe ekiek mi nap ka mefi pwe mi kon pwapwa me ese wor tipemwaramwar ren ewe pwichikar. Ren ei popun eu imw mi annut esap fokkun wor ekkei lon, nge ina eu me lein ekkewe mettoch mi ling ngeni ekkewe mi pisekisek. Pun tekian ei sokkun pwapwa pwe esap wor och mettoch nge ewe blanket lefilemi me om snugness me ewe pwichikar seni ewe asepwal lukun. Iwe, ikewe ie ka nom ie ussun ewe eu chok saram mi pwichikar lon letipen eu kristal mi lingoch lon ewe arctic.

Aua fen mwot non ei napanap mi uren ngungures ren ekkoch fansoun, nupwen non ew chok fansoun ua ekieki pwe upwe suuki mesei; pun nupwen nefinen ekkewe sepi, ika ran ika pwin, me ika ua annut ika nennelo, mi wor ai napanap ne tumunu mesei fansoun meinisin, pwe upwe tongeni kon ekieki usun ewe snugness ne nom non pet. Pokiten ese wor emon epwe tongeni mefi pwisin an esisin mi pung tiwenon chok ika mesan epwe suki; usun ita rochopwak mi wesewesen ewe kinikin mi fich ngeni ach kewe essences, ina mwo ika saram epwe kon kon fich ngeni ach kinikin mi clayey. Nupwen ua suuki mesei non ena fansoun, me tou seni ai pwisin rochopwak mi apwapwa me pwisin forata ngeni ewe rochopwak mi uren me chou non ewe engon me ruu awa-non-pwin ese saram, ua mefi ew nipwakingaw use tipeew ngeni. Use pwal fokkun u ngeni ewe kapasen pesepes seni Queequeg pwe eni epwe murinno ach sipwe fiti eu saram, pokiten am aua fokkun uren pwapwa; me nukun a mefi ew pochokunen mochen an epwe wor ekkoch puch mi kinamwe seni an we Tomahawk. Epwe apasa, pwe ina mwo ika ua fen mefi ena sokkun pochokunen oput ngeni an sakaw non ewe pet non ewe pwinin me mwen, nge nengeni ifa usun elastic ach kewe ekiek mi pochokun ra watteno nupwen tong fan ew a feito pwe epwe apungu ir. Pun iei use sani och mettoch mi murino nap seni ai upwe wor Queequeg epwe fitiei, pwan mwo non pet, pun a usun ita a uren ena sokkun pwapwa non imw mi kinamwe non ena fansoun. Use chúen meefi pwe ua kon osukosuk ren án ewe chón imw we allúkún insurance. Ua chok manau ngeni ewe kinamwe mi monomon mi kon watte ren ai upwe aporausa eu paip me eu blanket ren emon wesewesen chienei. Ren am kewe jacket mi uren fitikoko ra suki won pechem, iei aua fetan won ewe Tomahawk seni ew ngeni ewe pwan ew, tori ekis me ekis a marita won am ew tester mi uren fitikoko mi pwechepwech, a saramaram ren ewe ekkei an ewe lamp mi fo mi saramaram.

Ika pwe ei chon sotuni mi uren fitikoko a rokopwanieno ewe manmwacho ngeni ekkewe neni mi towaw, use sinei, nge iei a kapas usun fonuan we; me, ren ai mochen rongorong uruon we, ua tingor ngeni an epwe sopweno ne aporausa. A pwapwa le álleasochisi. Ina mwo non ewe fansoun ua chok weweiti esap ekkoch an kewe kapas, nge ekkewe pwarata mwirin, nupwen ua fen kon sinei usun an kewe kapas mi tatakis, iei a atufichiei ai upwe pwarata unusen ewe poraus usun met epwe tongeni pwarata non ewe chok skeleton ua ngeni.





SOPUN 12. Pworaus usun manauen aramas.

Queequeg emon chon Rokovoko, eu fonu mi towau ngeni otiu me otiu. Ese nom non ew map; ekkewe neni mi ennet rese kan fis.

Lupwen emon manmwacho mi keran uputiw a ssafetal won an kewe leenien ira lon eu leeni mi poon, iwe, ekkewe siip mi ninnila ra tapwelo mwirin, ussun ita nge i emon ira mi pwechepwech; pwal mwo nge lon ena atun, lon ngunun Queequeg mi mochen nemenem, a nom ren eu mochen mi pochokkul le kuna och mettoch mi lap seni Kristentom lap seni emon are ruuemon chon masen siip. Seman we emon Samol mi Tekia, emon King; noun we uncle emon Souasor mi Lap; me non ewe pekin in a mwareiti ekkewe aunt ir punuwen ekkewe sounfiu rese tongeni akkufu. A wor chcha mi murinno lon an kewe ppwul —pisekin king; ina mwo ika a netipengaw, ua nuokus, ren ewe mochenin kannibal a nounou non an kukkun ese kaeo.

Ew sipw Sag Harbor a churi ewe nenien seman we, iwe Queequeg a kutta ew aan ngeni fonuen chon Kraist. Nge ewe sipw, pokiten a wor unusen noun kewe chon sai, a poutano an we suit; me esap meinisin ewe King seman we a tongeni akkufu. Nge Kuweek a pwonei eu pwon. A chok nom non an we wa, a sai ngeni ew chuk mi towaw, minne a sinei pwe ewe sipw epwe fetan non nupwen a tou seni ewe fonu. Lon eu pekin a wor eu ttit mi koral; won ewe epek eu tongen fonu mi tekison, mi ur ren ekkewe ira mangrove mi mar ngeni ewe konik. A opano an we wa, mi chuen nom won konik, nein ekkei chuk, ren an we prow ngeni ewe sat, a mwot non ewe stern, paddle mi tekison non poun; iwe nupwen ewe sipw a suno, usun ew saram a suno; a angei an we pekin; ren ew dash mi niwinsefan pechen a turutiw me a suki an we wa; a fiti ekkewe sein; me a pwisin aturalong inisin won ewe deck, a amwochu eu ring-bolt ikewe, me a pwon pwe esap mut ngeni an epwe feilo, inaamwo ika a fen ataielo.

Ese wor lamotan ewe kaptin a erä pwe epwe aturalong lon ewe matau; a suki efoch cutlass won poun kewe mi uren uren; Kuek noun emon King, me Kuek ese budge. Pokiten a mairu ren an fokkun niuokkus, me an mochen feilo ngeni Christendom, ewe kaptin a mwittir ükütiu, me a ereni pwe eli epwe tongeni föri pwisin i lon imwan. Nge ei kukkun man mi murino —ei Princen Wales mi nom non ewe sat, ese mwo kuna ewe imwen Kapten. Ra anomu i lein ekewe chon sai, ra föri pwe i eman chon fiti ewe waimw. Nge ussun chok Czar Peter mi pwapwa le angang weires lon ekkewe leenien for sipw lon ekkewe telinimw lukun, Queequeg ese turunufasei och mettoch mi ussun ita pwe a turunufas, ika ren ena epwe tongeni pwapwa le angei ewe manamanen asarama chon fonuan kewe rese kaeo. Pun non ewe fansoun —ina usun a ereniei —a amwokutu ren ew watten mochenin kaeo nein ekkewe chon Kraist, ekkewe art ren an epwe fori noun kewe aramas repwe chuen pwapwa nap seni ir; me nap seni ena, mi chuen murinno nap seni ir. Nge, iei! fofforun ekkewe chon whale a muttir alukuluku i pwe pwal mwo nge chon Kraist ra tongeni riaffou me fofforingau; ese wor soponon, nap seni meinisin chon lukun seman we. A tori ewe leeni mi pin lon ewe leenien Sag; me kuna met ekkewe chon sai ra fori ikewe; iwe mwirin a feino ngeni Nantucket, me kuna ifa usun ra aea ar moni tonong non ena neni pwan, Queequeg mi woungaw a nikitano ren an pout seni. Ekieki i, pwe ew fonufan mi ngaw non meinisin meridian; Ngang upwe mano emon chon lukun.

Iwe, ina ussun emon chon fel ngeni uluulun mi chinnap lon letipan, a chuen nonnom lein ekkena chon Kraist, a ufouf ufer kewe, me a sotun apasa ar kewe kapas mi mwaal. Ina popun ekkewe napanap mi sokkolo usun i, inaamwo ika iei ekkoch fansoun seni imwan.

Ren ekkewe kapasen pesepes, ua eisini ika ese ekiekin niwin, me fori ew korona; pokiten epwe tongeni iei ekieki pwe seman we a mano me a no, a fokkun chinnap me apwangapwang non ekkewe amuchunon poraus. A ponuweni ewer, ese mwo; me a pwal apasa pwe i emon chon Kraist mi niuokkus, are sipwe apasa pwe chon Kraist, a fen ataielo i ren an epwe feita won ewe leenien motun king mi limoch me ese limengau ren ekkewe tiu King chon lukun mi nom mwen i. Nge ekis me ekis, a apasa, epwe niwin, —non chok fansoun a mefi pwe a papatais sefan. Nge ren ewe nonce, a ekiekin saifetal, me fotuki an kewe oats mi mwacho lon meinisin ekkewe fomon matau. Ra fen fori efoch harpoon seni i, me ena mecha mi poon a siwili efoch wokun king iei.

Ua eisini ika met epwe tongeni an kokkot mi mwitir, a tori an kewe mokutukut non fansoun mwach. A ponuweni, pwe epwe feino ngeni sat sefan, non an koko me nom. Non ei, ua ereni pwe ewe angangen whale ina pwisin ai kokkot, me ua esinesin ngeni usun ai ekiekin sai seni Nantucket, pwe ina ewe nenien sai mi murinno ngeni emon chon whale mi mochen sai seni. A mwitir finata pwe epwe fitiei ngeni ena fonu, sipw won ewe chok wa, tonong non ewe chok watch, ewe chok wa, ewe chok mess ngeniei, non mwochomwochen ai upwe aporausa ai meinisin hap; ren pei kewe ruepek non poun, pwora ne tonong non ewe Potluck non ekkewe ruu fonufan. Ngeni meinisin ekkei ua pwapwa ne etiwa; pun me nukun ewe tong ua mefi iei ngeni Queequeg, i emon chon fiti harpoone mi sinenap, me ren ena, ese tongeni epwe fokkun auchea ngeni emon, ewe, usun chok ngang, a unusen sinei usun ekkewe monomonen whale, ina mwo ika a sineioch usun ewe sat, usun met ekkewe chon fiti sepenin mi kamo ra sinei.

An poraus a muchuno ren an we paip amuchunon puff mi mano, Queequeg a etiwaei, a apachanong mokuran ngeni mesei, me a suki ewe saram, aua rokopwanieno seni emon me emon, ei napanap me ena, me fokkun mwitir aua annut.





SOPUN 13. Ewe wiis.
