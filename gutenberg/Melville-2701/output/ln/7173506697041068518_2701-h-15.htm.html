MOKAPO 74. Motó ya Baleine spermatozoïde —Botali oyo ekeseni.

Tala, sikawa, baleine minene mibale, etandami mitó na yango esika moko; tosangana na bango, mpe tolalisa elongo oyo ya biso.

Na molɔngɔ monene ya ba leviathans ya folio, Baleine Spermique mpe Baleine Droite ezali mosika mpenza oyo eleki koyebana. Ezali kaka baleines oyo moto alukaka mbala na mbala. Mpo na Nantucketer, bamonisaka makambo mibale oyo eleki ndelo ya mitindo nyonso ya baleine oyo eyebani. Lokola bokeseni ya libanda kati na bango emonanaka mingimingi na mitó na bango; mpe lokola motó ya moko na moko ezali ngonga oyo ekangami na mopanzi ya Pequod; mpe lokola tokoki kokende na bonsomi nyonso longwa na moko kino na mosusu, na komatisaka kaka na pont: —epai wapi, nalingi koyeba, okozwa libaku malamu ya koyekola cétologie ya mosala koleka awa?

Na esika ya liboso, ozali kobɛtama na bokeseni monene oyo ezali kati na mitó yango. Bango mibale bazali minene mpenza na lisosoli nyonso; kasi ezali na symétrie moko boye ya matematiki na oyo ya Baleine ya Sperm oyo ya Baleine Droite ezangi na mawa nyonso. Ezali na bizaleli mingi na motó ya Baleine Spermique. Wana ozali kotala yango, ozali kopesa na bolingi te koleka ye monene, na esika ya lokumu oyo epalangani mingi. Na likambo ya lelo, mpe, lokumu yango ebakisami na langi ya pilipili mpe mungwa ya motó na ye na nsɔngɛ, kopesaka elembo ya mbula ya mibange mpe eksperiansi monene. Na mokuse, azali oyo balobi-mbisi babengaka na mayele “baleine ya motó ya motane.”

Sikoyo tótalela oyo ekokani mingi te na mitó yango​—elingi koloba binama mibale ya ntina mingi, liso mpe litoi. Mosika na nsima na mopanzi ya motó, mpe na nse, pene na angle ya mbanga ya baleine moko to mosusu, soki oluki na moke, nsukansuka okomona liso oyo ezangi nsuki, oyo okokanisa ete ezali liso ya mwana ya mpunda; yango wana na proportion nyonso ezali na bonene ya motó.

Sikawa, uta na esika oyo ya kokamwa ya mopanzi ya miso ya baleine, ezali polele ete akoki ata moke te komona eloko oyo ezali mpenza liboso, koleka te lokola akoki komona mpenza na nsima. Na liloba moko, etɛlɛmɛlo ya miso ya baleine ekokani na oyo ya matoi ya moto; mpe okoki kokanisa, mpo na yo moko, lolenge nini ekozala na yo, otalaki na mopanzi biloko na matoyi na yo. Okomona ete okokaki bobele kopesa mitindo ya degré soki ntuku misato ya komona liboso ya nzela ya semba ya mopanzi ya komona; mpe soko ntuku misato mosusu nsima na yango. Soki monguna na yo oyo aleki bololo azalaki kotambola mbala moko epai na yo, na dague oyo etombolami na mokolo ya monene, olingaki komona ye te, ndenge moko te soki azalaki koyiba yo na nsima. Na liloba moko, olingaki kozala na mokɔngɔ mibale, soki tokoki koloba bongo; kasi, na ntango moko, lisusu, liboso mibale (ba liboso ya mopanzi): mpo nini ezali oyo esalaka liboso ya moto —nini, ya solo, longola se miso na ye?

Lisusu, atako na banyama mosusu mingi oyo nakoki kokanisa sikoyo, miso elonamaki na ndenge oyo ekoki komonana te mpo na kosangisa nguya na yango ya komona, na boye ete ebimisa elilingi moko kasi mibale te na bɔɔngɔ; esika ya kokamwa ya miso ya baleine, oyo ekabwani mpenza ndenge ezali na makolo cube mingi ya motó ya makasi, oyo etɛlɛmi na katikati na yango lokola ngomba monene oyo ekaboli mabeke mibale na mabwaku; yango, ya solo, esengeli kokabola mobimba makanisi oyo enama mokomoko oyo ezali na lipanda epesaka. Na yango, baleine esengeli komona elilingi moko ekeseni na ngámbo oyo, mpe elilingi mosusu ekeseni na ngámbo wana; nzokande nyonso kati na yango esengeli kozala molili mozindo mpe eloko te mpo na ye. Tokoki koloba ete moto azali kotala mokili na kati ya sanduku ya bakɛngɛli oyo ezali na bansinga mibale oyo esangisami mpo na lininisa na ye. Kasi na baleine, ba sashes mibale wana ekɔtisami na bokeseni, kosala maninisa mibale ekeseni, kasi na mawa nyonso ebebisaka kotala. Bokeseni wana ya miso ya baleine ezali likambo oyo esengeli ntango nyonso kobosana te na mosala ya koboma mbisi; mpe mpo motángi akanisa yango na bililingi mosusu oyo elandi.

Motuna moko ya kokamwa mpe ya kobulunganisa mingi ekoki kobanda na ntina na likambo oyo ya komona lokola esimbaki Leviathane. Kasi nasengeli kosepela na mwa likanisi moko. Soki kaka miso ya moto efungwami na pole, mosala ya komona ezali na bolingi te; elingi koloba ete na nsima akoki te kosalisa na masini komona biloko nyonso oyo ezali liboso na ye. Atako bongo, likambo oyo moto nyonso akutanaki na yango ekoteya ye, ete atako akoki kozwa na kati ya kopɛtola makambo kozanga bokeseni na mbala moko, ekoki mpenza te mpo na ye, na likebi, mpe na mobimba, kotalela makambo mibale nyonso —ata soki ezali monene to ata moke ndenge nini —na mbala moko mpe mbala moko ya ntango; ata moke te soki balali pembenipembeni mpe basimbanaki. Kasi soki boyei sikawa kokabola biloko oyo mibale, mpe kozinga moko na moko na sɛrklɛ ya molili mozindo; na nsima, mpo na komona moko na yango, na ndenge oyo ekosala ete makanisi na yo ekɔta na likambo yango, mosusu akolongolama mpenza na makanisi na yo ya ntango na biso. Na bongo, ezali ndenge nini mpo na baleine? Ya solo, miso na ye nyonso mibale, na yango moko, esengeli kosala na mbala moko; kasi bɔɔngɔ na ye ezali mpenza na makambo mingi, oyo esangisaka, mpe ya mayele mabe koleka oyo ya moto, na boye ete akoki na ntango moko kotalela na likebi nyonso bilikya mibale ekeseni, moko na ngámbo moko na ye, mpe mosusu na ngámbo oyo ekeseni mpenza? Soki akoki, boye ezali likambo ya kokamwa lokola kati na ye, lokola nde moto azali na makoki ya koleka na mbala moko na bilakiseli ya mikakatano mibale ekeseni na Euclide. To, soki totali malamu, ezali na boyokani te na bokokanisi oyo.

Ekoki kozala kaka mposa ya mpambampamba, kasi emonanaki ntango nyonso epai na ngai, ete koningana ya kokamwa ya kotambola oyo baleine mosusu emonisaka ntango masuwa misato to minei ezingami; nsɔninsɔni mpe mokumba ya kobangisa makambo ya kokamwa, oyo emonanaka mingi na baleines ya ndenge wana; Nakanisi ete nyonso wana euti na ndenge ya semba uta na mobulungano ya bolingi oyo ezangi lisungi, oyo kati na yango nguya na bango ya bomoni oyo ekabwani mpe oyo ekeseni mpenza esengeli kosangisa bango.

Kasi litoi ya baleine etondi na mposa ya koyeba makambo lokola liso. Soki ozali mopaya mpenza na loposo na bango, okoki kobundisa mitó yango mibale na boumeli ya bangonga mingi, mpe okomona ata moke te enama yango. Litoi ezali na nkasa ya libándá ata moke te; mpe na libulu yango moko okoki mpenza te kokɔtisa libenga, yango wana ezali miniti ya kokamwa. Efandi mwa moke nsima ya liso. Na oyo etali matoi na bango, bokeseni wana ya ntina esengeli komonana kati na baleine spermatozoïde mpe ya mobali. Atako litoi ya oyo ya liboso ezali na esika oyo efungwami na libándá, oyo ya oyo ya nsuka ezipami mobimba mpe ndenge moko na nsinga, na boye ete soki ozali na libándá, emonanaka mpenza te.

Ezali likambo ya kokamwa te, ete ekelamu monene lokola baleine amona mokili na liso ya moke boye, mpe ayoka nkake na litoi oyo ezali moke koleka oyo ya lièvre? Kasi soki miso na ye ezalaki minene lokola lente ya télescope monene ya Herschel; mpe matoi na ye manene lokola ba veranda ya bandakonzambe; yango ekosala ete amona lisusu, to ayoka malamu koleka? Ata moke te.​—Bongo mpo na nini ozali koluka “kokómisa” makanisi na yo? Subtiliser yango.

Tika biso sikoyo na ba leviers nionso mpe ba moteurs-vapeur oyo tozali na yango na maboko, cant likolo ya motó ya baleine spermatozoïde, mpo ete elala na nse likolo; na nsima, soki ozali komata na eskalye tii na nsɔngɛ, salá na nse ya monɔkɔ; mpe soki ezalaki te ete nzoto ekabwani sikawa mobimba na yango, na lanterne tokokaki kokita na kati ya Libulu monene ya Kentucky Mammoth ya libumu na ye. Kasi tosimba awa na lino oyo, mpe totala zingazinga na biso esika tozali. Oyo nde monɔkɔ kitoko mpenza mpe oyo ezali komonana pɛto! kobanda na nse tii na plafɔ, batyá yango na kati, to koloba malamu, batye yango na papye na membrane ya mpɛmbɛ oyo ezali kongɛnga, oyo ezali kongɛnga lokola ba satin ya libala.

Kasi bima sikawa, mpe tala mbanga oyo ya nse ya portentous, oyo emonani lokola ezipeli molai ya moke ya sanduku monene ya komela, na charnière na nsuka moko, na esika ya ngambo moko. Soki ozwi yango, mpo na kozwa yango likoló ya motó, mpe kobimisa milɔngɔ ya mino na yango, emonani lokola portcullis moko ya kokamwa; mpe ya ndenge wana, mawa! yango ezali komonisa epai na bato mingi lokola wight mobola na mosala ya koboma mbisi, oyo likoló na ye bansɔngɛ yango ekweaka na makasi ya kobaka na nzete. Kasi ezali nsɔmɔ mingi koleka mpo na kotala, ntango ozali kokita na nse na mbu, omoni mwa baleine sulky, kopumbwa kuna ekangami, na mbanga na ye ya kokamwa, penepene na bolai ya makolo zomi na mitano, ekangami semba na nse na ba angles droits na nzoto na ye, mpo na nyonso mokili lokola jib-boom ya masuwa. Baleine oyo ekufi te; azali kaka kolɛmba nzoto; libanda ya mitindo, mbala mosusu; hypochondrie oyo ezali na kati; mpe na nse mpenza, na boye ete ba charnières ya mbanga na ye ekiti, kotikaka ye kuna na lolenge wana ya mpasi ya kozanga bosepeli, kofinga mpo na libota na ye mobimba, oyo basengeli, na ntembe te, kofinga ye manzaka ya kokanga.

Mbala mingi, mbanga oyo ya nse —oyo elongolamaka na pɛtɛɛ nyonso na moyemi oyo amesá na mosala —elongolamaka mpe etombolamaka na pont na ntina ya kobimisa mino ya mpɛmbɛ ya nzoku, mpe kopesa biloko ya mokuwa wana ya makasi ya mpɛmbɛ ya baleine oyo babomi-mbisi basalaka na yango biloko ya ndenge nyonso ya mposa ya koyeba, bakisa mpe banzete ya mbila, biloko ya parapluie, mpe manzaka ya fimbo ya komata.

Na kotombola molai mpe oyo elɛmbi mbanga ebendamaka na masuwa, lokola nde ezali longo; mpe ntango ntango oyo ebongi ekomi —mwa mikolo moke nsima ya mosala mosusu —Queequeg, Daggoo, mpe Tashtego, lokola bango nyonso minganga ya mino oyo bayebi mosala malamu, bamibongisi mpo na kobenda mino. Na mbeli ya kokata ya makasi, Queequeg azali kobɛtabɛta mino; na nsima, bakangisaka mbanga na nse tii na ba ringbolts, mpe lokola ba rigged tackle moko longwa na likoló, babendaka mino yango, lokola bangɔmbɛ ya Michigan babendaka matiti ya banzete ya kala ya chêne na mabele ya zamba ya zamba. Mingimingi ezali na mino ntuku minei na mibale nyonso; na baleines ya kala, oyo elɛmbi mingi, kasi epɔli te; to mpe etondisami nsima ya mode na biso ya artificiel. Na nsima, bakataka mbanga na mabaya, mpe batyaka yango lokola makonzí mpo na kotonga bandako.





MOKAPO 75. Motó ya Baleine Droite —Likanisi oyo ekeseni.

Kokatisa pont, tika sikawa totala malamu na molai na motó ya Baleine Droite.

Lokola na lolenge ya monene motó ya Baleine Spermique ya lokumu ekoki kokokanisama na likalo ya etumba ya Loma (mingimingi liboso, epai ezali na monene mpenza ya zingazinga); yango wana, soki totaleli yango na monene, motó ya Baleine Droite ekokani mpenza te na sapato monene oyo ezali na misapi ya galliot. Mibu nkama mibale mileki, mokumbi mobembo moko ya kala ya Pays-Bas akokanisaki lolenge na yango na oyo ya nsuka ya mosali sapato. Mpe na kaka ya nsuka to sapato oyo, mwasi wana ya kala ya lisapo ya bana ya bana, elongo na bana oyo bazali kosopana, akokaki mpenza kolala malamu, ye mpe bana na ye nyonso.

Kasi lokola ozali kopusana penepene na motó monene oyo ebandi kozwa makambo ndenge na ndenge, engebene likanisi na yo. Soki otelemi na nsɔngɛ na yango mpe otali mabulu oyo mibale oyo ezali na lolenge ya F, olingaki kozwa motó mobimba mpo na bass-viol moko monene, mpe ba spiracles wana, ba ouvertures oyo ezali na tableau de sondage na yango. Na nsima, lisusu, soki otye liso na yo na esika wana ya kokamwa, ya nsɔngɛ, oyo ezali lokola peigne na likoló ya masse —eloko oyo ya vert, ya barnacle, oyo bato ya Groenland babengaka “motole,” mpe balobi mbisi ya Sudi “bonnet” na yango Baleine ya Droite; kotya miso na yo kaka na likambo yango, olingaki kozwa motó mpo na mokuwa ya nzete moko ya chêne monene, oyo ezali na zumbu ya ndɛkɛ na kati ya libumu na yango. Ata ndenge nini, ntango ozali kotala ba crabes wana ya bomoi oyo ekɔtaka awa na bonnet oyo, likanisi ya ndenge wana ekozala pene na koyela yo na makanisi; longola se soki, ya solo, makanisi na yo esili kobongisama na liloba ya tekiniki “motole” oyo epesami mpe na yango; na likambo yango okosepela mingi kokanisa lolenge nini nyama mabe wana ya nguya azali mpenza mokonzi ya mbu oyo alati diade, oyo motole na ye ya vert esanganaki mpo na ye na lolenge wana ya kokamwa. Kasi soki baleine oyo azali mokonzi, azali moninga oyo azali komonana na sulky mingi mpo na kopesa ngolu na diademe. Talá mbɛbu wana ya nse oyo ekangami! nini sulk na pout ya munene eza wana! sulk mpe pout, na mezire ya mosali-mabaya, bolai ya mɛtrɛ soki ntuku mibale mpe bozindo ya mɛtrɛ mitano; sulk mpe pout oyo ekopesa yo soki 500 gallons ya mafuta mpe koleka.

Mawa monene, sikawa, ete baleine oyo ya mawa esengeli kozala na mbɛbu ya lièvre. Fissure yango ezali na bolai ya mɛtrɛ soki moko. Mbala mosusu mama yango na eleko moko ya ntina mingi azalaki kokita na masuwa na libongo ya Pérou, ntango koningana ya mabele esalaki ete libongo efungwama. Likolo ya mbɛbu oyo, lokola likoló ya esika oyo ezali kogumbama, tozali sikawa kokita na monɔkɔ. Likolo ya liloba na ngai nazalaki na Mackinaw, nasengeli kozwa oyo mpo na kozala kati ya wigwam moko ya mindele. Nkolo malamu! oyo ezali nzela oyo Yona akendaki? Toit ezali na bolai ya mɛtrɛ soki zomi na mibale, mpe ekei na angle moko kitoko ya makasi, lokola nde ezalaki na nzete ya nsɔngɛ ya mbala na mbala kuna; nzokande mipanzi oyo ya mikuwa ya mokɔngɔ, oyo ezali na arc, oyo ezali na nsuki, ezali kolakisa biso ba-plates wana ya kokamwa, oyo ezali na ndambo ya semba, oyo ezali lokola scimetar ya mokuwa ya baleine, tóloba nkama misato na ngámbo moko, oyo engebene na eteni ya likoló ya motó to mokuwa ya motole, esalaka ba blinds wana ya Vénétie oyo balobelami na bisika mosusu na mbalakaka. Nsuka ya mikuwa yango ezali na bansinga ya nsuki, oyo na nzela na yango Baleine Droite epɛtolaka mai, mpe na makambo na yango ya mindɔndɔmindɔndɔ abatelaka mbisi ya mikemike, ntango efungwami na monɔkɔ elekaka na mbu ya brit na ntango ya kolya. Na ba blinds centrales ya mikuwa, ndenge etɛlɛmi na molɔngɔ na yango ya bozalisi, ezali na bilembo mosusu ya kokamwa, ba curves, mabulu, mpe ba ridges, oyo bato mosusu oyo balukaka baleine ba calculer mbula ya ekelamu yango, lokola mbula ya chêne na ba rings na yango ya sɛrklɛ. Atako bosolo ya critère oyo ezali mosika na oyo ekoki kolakisa, nzokande ezali na savour ya probabilité analogique. Ata ndenge nini, soki tondimi yango, tosengeli kopesa Baleine Droite mbula monene koleka oyo na ebandeli ekomonanaka ete ezali malamu.

Na ntango ya kala, emonani ete ezalaki na makanisi ya kokamwa mingi oyo etali ba blindages wana. Moto moko oyo asalaka mobembo na Purchas abengi yango “mipingi” ya kokamwa oyo ezali na kati ya monɔkɔ ya baleine;* mosusu, “nsu ya ngulu”; mobange moko ya misato ya mobange na Hackluyt asaleli elobeli oyo ya kitoko: “Ezali na nsɔngɛ soki nkama mibale na ntuku mitano oyo ezali kokola na ngámbo mokomoko ya chop na ye ya likoló , oyo ebalukaka likoló ya lolemo na ye na ngámbo mokomoko ya monɔkɔ na ye.”

*Oyo ekundolisi biso ete Baleine Droite ezali mpenza na lolenge moko ya mipende, to koloba malamu moustache, oyo ezali na mwa nsuki ya mpɛmbɛ oyo epalangani na eteni ya likoló ya nsuka ya libándá ya mbanga ya nse. Tango mosusu ba tufts wana epesaka expression moko ya plutôt brigande na elongi na ye soki te solemne.

Lokola moto nyonso ayebi yango, kaka “nsu ya ngulu,” “naile,” “mipepe,” “ba blinds,” to eloko nyonso oyo olingi, epesaka basi yango ba busks na bango mpe mayele mosusu oyo ekómisaka bango makasi. Kasi na particulier oyo, demande ebandaki kokita banda kala. Ezalaki na ntango ya Mokonzi-mwasi Anne nde mokuwa ezalaki na nkembo na yango, farthingale ezalaki na ntango wana mode nyonso. Mpe lokola ba dames wana ya kala bazalaki kotambolatambola na esengo, atako na mbanga ya baleine, lokola okoki koloba; ata bongo, na douche, na kozanga kokanisa lokola, tozali lelo oyo topumbwaka na nse ya mbanga moko mpo na kobatelama; parapluie ezali hema oyo epalangani likoló ya mokuwa yango moko.

Kasi sikawa kobosana nyonso na ntina ya ba blinds mpe mipende mpo na mwa ntango, mpe, kotelema na monoko ya Baleine Droite, tala zingazinga na yo lisusu. Komona makonzí oyo nyonso ya mikuwa oyo ezalaki kotambola na ndenge ya mayele mpenza, okokanisa te ete ozalaki na kati ya orgue monene ya Haarlem, mpe kotalatala na nkóto ya bapiblisite na yango? Mpo na tapis na orgue tozali na tapis ya Turquie oyo eleki pete —monoko, oyo ekangami, lokola, na nse ya monoko. Ezali mafuta mingi mpe tendre, mpe ebongi kopasola na biteni na kotombola yango na pont. Monoko oyo ya sikisiki sikawa liboso na biso; na mbala moko nasengeli koloba ete ezalaki motuka ya bitunga motoba; elingi koloba, ekopesa yo pene na motuya wana ya mafuta.

Ere oyo, bosengeli komona polele bosolo ya oyo nabandaki na yango —ete Baleine Spermique mpe Baleine Droite bazali na mitó pene na oyo ekeseni mpenza. Na yango, mpo na koloba na mokuse: na oyo ya Baleine Droite ezali na libulu monene ya spermatozoïde te; mino ya mpɛmbɛ ya nzoku ata moke te; mandibule ya molai mpe ya mikemike te ya mbanga ya nse, lokola oyo ya Baleine Spermique. Na Baleine Spermique ezali mpe te na ba blindages wana ya mikuwa; mbɛbu monene ya nse te; mpe na mpasi eloko moko ya monoko. Na likambo oyo mpe, Baleine Droite ezali na mabulu mibale ya libanda, Baleine Sperm kaka moko.

Tala ya nsuka na yo, sikawa, na mitó oyo ya lokumu oyo ezali na kapoti, ntango bazali naino kolala esika moko; mpo moko ekozinda mosika te, oyo ekomami te, na mbu; mosusu ekozala molai mingi te na kolanda.

Okoki kokanga expression ya Baleine Spermique ezali kuna? Ezali ndenge moko akufaki na yango, kaka mwa ba rides milai na elongi emonani sikawa esili. Nakanisi ete elongi na ye ya monene etondi na placidité lokola prairie, ebotami na indifférence spéculative na oyo etali liwa. Kasi elembo na elongi ya motó mosusu. Talá mbɛbu wana ya nse ya kokamwa, oyo efinamaki na mbalakaka na mopanzi ya masuwa, mpo eyamba makasi mbanga. Emonani te ete motó oyo mobimba ezali kolobela ekateli monene mpenza oyo ekoki kosalelama mpo na kokutana na liwa? Baleine Droite oyo nazuaka que ezalaki Stoïque; Baleine Spermique, moto ya Platon, oyo akoki kozala ete azwaki Spinoza na bambula na ye ya nsuka.





MOKAPO 76. Ram ya kobɛtabɛta.

Ere kotika, mpo na mbala moko, motó ya Baleine Spermique, nakolinga ete yo, lokola physiologiste ya mayele, kaka —mingimingi kozongela likambo na yango ya liboso, na bosangisi na yango nyonso ya compacted. Nakolinga ete oluka yango sikawa na likanisi se moko ya kosala mpo na yo moko mwa ekanisami ya kolekisa ndelo te, ya mayele ya nguya nyonso ya battering-ram ekoki kozala lodged kuna. Talá likambo moko ya ntina mingi; mpo ete osengeli kosilisa likambo oyo na ndenge oyo esepelisaka yo moko, to mpo na libela kotikala moto oyo azali kondima te mpo na moko ya makambo oyo eleki nsɔmɔ, kasi oyo ezali mpenza solo mingi te, mbala mosusu esika nyonso oyo ekoki kozwama na lisolo nyonso oyo ekomami.

Omoni ete na position ordinaire ya ko nager ya Baleine Spermique, liboso ya mutu na ye e présenter plan presque entièrement verticale na mayi; omoni ete eteni ya nse ya liboso wana ebalukaka mingi na nsima, mpo na kopesa mingi esika ya kozonga nsima mpo na socket molai oyo eyambaka mbanga ya nse oyo ezali lokola boom; omoni ete monɔkɔ ezali mobimba na nse ya motó, mpenza ndenge moko, ya solo, lokola nde monɔkɔ na yo moko ezali mobimba na nse ya nsuki ya motó na yo. CHAPTER 79. The Prairie.

To scan the lines of his face, or feel the bumps on the head of this Leviathan; this is a thing which no Physiognomist or Phrenologist has as yet undertaken. Such an enterprise would seem almost as hopeful as for Lavater to have scrutinized the wrinkles on the Rock of Gibraltar, or for Gall to have mounted a ladder and manipulated the Dome of the Pantheon. Still, in that famous work of his, Lavater not only treats of the various faces of men, but also attentively studies the faces of horses, birds, serpents, and fish; and dwells in detail upon the modifications of expression discernible therein. Nor have Gall and his disciple Spurzheim failed to throw out some hints touching the phrenological characteristics of other beings than man. Therefore, though I am but ill qualified for a pioneer, in the application of these two semi-sciences to the whale, I will do my endeavor. I try all things; I achieve what I can.

Physiognomically regarded, the Sperm Whale is an anomalous creature. He has no proper nose. And since the nose is the central and most conspicuous of the features; and since it perhaps most modifies and finally controls their combined expression; hence it would seem that its entire absence, as an external appendage, must very largely affect the countenance of the whale. For as in landscape gardening, a spire, cupola, monument, or tower of some sort, is deemed almost indispensable to the completion of the scene; so no face can be physiognomically in keeping without the elevated open-work belfry of the nose. Dash the nose from Phidias's marble Jove, and what a sorry remainder! Nevertheless, Leviathan is of so mighty a magnitude, all his proportions are so stately, that the same deficiency which in the sculptured Jove were hideous, in him is no blemish at all. Nay, it is an added grandeur. A nose to the whale would have been impertinent. As on your physiognomical voyage you sail round his vast head in your jolly-boat, your noble conceptions of him are never insulted by the reflection that he has a nose to be pulled. A pestilent conceit, which so often will insist upon obtruding even when beholding the mightiest royal beadle on his throne.

In some particulars, perhaps the most imposing physiognomical view to be had of the Sperm Whale, is that of the full front of his head. This aspect is sublime.

In thought, a fine human brow is like the East when troubled with the morning. In the repose of the pasture, the curled brow of the bull has a touch of the grand in it. Pushing heavy cannon up mountain defiles, the elephant's brow is majestic. Human or animal, the mystical brow is as that great golden seal affixed by the German emperors to their decrees. It signifies—“God: done this day by my hand.” But in most creatures, nay in man himself, very often the brow is but a mere strip of alpine land lying along the snow line. Few are the foreheads which like Shakespeare's or Melancthon's rise so high, and descend so low, that the eyes themselves seem clear, eternal, tideless mountain lakes; and all above them in the forehead's wrinkles, you seem to track the antlered thoughts descending there to drink, as the Highland hunters track the snow prints of the deer. But in the great Sperm Whale, this high and mighty god-like dignity inherent in the brow is so immensely amplified, that gazing on it, in that full front view, you feel the Deity and the dread powers more forcibly than in beholding any other object in living nature. For you see no one point precisely; not one distinct feature is revealed; no nose, eyes, ears, or mouth; no face; he has none, proper; nothing but that one broad firmament of a forehead, pleated with riddles; dumbly lowering with the doom of boats, and ships, and men. Nor, in profile, does this wondrous brow diminish; though that way viewed its grandeur does not domineer upon you so. In profile, you plainly perceive that horizontal, semi-crescentic depression in the forehead's middle, which, in man, is Lavater's mark of genius.

But how? Genius in the Sperm Whale? Has the Sperm Whale ever written a book, spoken a speech? No, his great genius is declared in his doing nothing particular to prove it. It is moreover declared in his pyramidical silence. And this reminds me that had the great Sperm Whale been known to the young Orient World, he would have been deified by their child-magian thoughts. They deified the crocodile of the Nile, because the crocodile is tongueless; and the Sperm Whale has no tongue, or at least it is so exceedingly small, as to be incapable of protrusion. If hereafter any highly cultured, poetical nation shall lure back to their birth-right, the merry May-day gods of old; and livingly enthrone them again in the now egotistical sky; in the now unhaunted hill; then be sure, exalted to Jove's high seat, the great Sperm Whale shall lord it.

Champollion deciphered the wrinkled granite hieroglyphics. But there is no Champollion to decipher the Egypt of every man's and every being's face. Physiognomy, like every other human science, is but a passing fable. If then, Sir William Jones, who read in thirty languages, could not read the simplest peasant's face in its profounder and more subtle meanings, how may unlettered Ishmael hope to read the awful Chaldee of the Sperm Whale's brow? I but put that brow before you. Read it if you can.





CHAPTER 80. The Nut.

If the Sperm Whale be physiognomically a Sphinx, to the phrenologist his brain seems that geometrical circle which it is impossible to square.

In the full-grown creature the skull will measure at least twenty feet in length. Unhinge the lower jaw, and the side view of this skull is as the side of a moderately inclined plane resting throughout on a level base. But in life—as we have elsewhere seen—this inclined plane is angularly filled up, and almost squared by the enormous superincumbent mass of the junk and sperm. At the high end the skull forms a crater to bed that part of the mass; while under the long floor of this crater—in another cavity seldom exceeding ten inches in length and as many in depth—reposes the mere handful of this monster's brain. The brain is at least twenty feet from his apparent forehead in life; it is hidden away behind its vast outworks, like the innermost citadel within the amplified fortifications of Quebec. So like a choice casket is it secreted in him, that I have known some whalemen who peremptorily deny that the Sperm Whale has any other brain than that palpable semblance of one formed by the cubic-yards of his sperm magazine. Lying in strange folds, courses, and convolutions, to their apprehensions, it seems more in keeping with the idea of his general might to regard that mystic part of him as the seat of his intelligence.

It is plain, then, that phrenologically the head of this Leviathan, in the creature's living intact state, is an entire delusion. As for his true brain, you can then see no indications of it, nor feel any. The whale, like all things that are mighty, wears a false brow to the common world.

If you unload his skull of its spermy heaps and then take a rear view of its rear end, which is the high end, you will be struck by its resemblance to the human skull, beheld in the same situation, and from the same point of view. Indeed, place this reversed skull (scaled down to the human magnitude) among a plate of men's skulls, and you would involuntarily confound it with them; and remarking the depressions on one part of its summit, in phrenological phrase you would say—This man had no self-esteem, and no veneration. And by those negations, considered along with the affirmative fact of his prodigious bulk and power, you can best form to yourself the truest, though not the most exhilarating conception of what the most exalted potency is.

But if from the comparative dimensions of the whale's proper brain, you deem it incapable of being adequately charted, then I have another idea for you. If you attentively regard almost any quadruped's spine, you will be struck with the resemblance of its vertebræ to a strung necklace of dwarfed skulls, all bearing rudimental resemblance to the skull proper. It is a German conceit, that the vertebræ are absolutely undeveloped skulls. But the curious external resemblance, I take it the Germans were not the first men to perceive. A foreign friend once pointed it out to me, in the skeleton of a foe he had slain, and with the vertebræ of which he was inlaying, in a sort of basso-relievo, the beaked prow of his canoe. Now, I consider that the phrenologists have omitted an important thing in not pushing their investigations from the cerebellum through the spinal canal. For I believe that much of a man's character will be found betokened in his backbone. I would rather feel your spine than your skull, whoever you are. A thin joist of a spine never yet upheld a full and noble soul. I rejoice in my spine, as in the firm audacious staff of that flag which I fling half out to the world.

Apply this spinal branch of phrenology to the Sperm Whale. His cranial cavity is continuous with the first neck-vertebra; and in that vertebra the bottom of the spinal canal will measure ten inches across, being eight in height, and of a triangular figure with the base downwards. As it passes through the remaining vertebræ the canal tapers in size, but for a considerable distance remains of large capacity. Now, of course, this canal is filled with much the same strangely fibrous substance—the spinal cord—as the brain; and directly communicates with the brain. And what is still more, for many feet after emerging from the brain's cavity, the spinal cord remains of an undecreasing girth, almost equal to that of the brain. Under all these circumstances, would it be unreasonable to survey and map out the whale's spine phrenologically? For, viewed in this light, the wonderful comparative smallness of his brain proper is more than compensated by the wonderful comparative magnitude of his spinal cord.

But leaving this hint to operate as it may with the phrenologists, I would merely assume the spinal theory for a moment, in reference to the Sperm Whale's hump. This august hump, if I mistake not, rises over one of the larger vertebræ, and is, therefore, in some sort, the outer convex mould of it. From its relative situation then, I should call this high hump the organ of firmness or indomitableness in the Sperm Whale. And that the great monster is indomitable, you will yet have reason to know.
