MOKAPO 55. Ya Bilili ya Nsɔmɔ ya Baleines.

Nako ere long paint na bino lokola malamu lokola moto akoki kozanga toile, eloko lokola lolenge ya solo ya baleine lokola ye mpenza komonana na liso ya baleineman ntango na nzoto na ye moko absolu baleine ekangami pembeni ya baleine-masuwa mpo ete bakoki konyata ye na bosembo kuna. Na yango, ekoki kozala na ntina liboso koyebisa bililingi wana ya makanisi ya kokamwa ya ye oyo ata kino lelo ezali kotya ntembe na elikya nyonso kondima ya moto oyo azali na mabele. Ezali ntango ya kobongisa mokili na likambo oyo, na komonisáká ete bililingi ya ndenge wana ya baleine ezali mabe mpenza.

Ekoki kozala ete liziba ya libosoliboso ya makanisi wana nyonso ya lokuta ya bililingi ekozwama kati na mayemi ya kala koleka ya Bahindu, ya Ezipito mpe ya Grèce. Mpo uta ntango wana ya bokeli kasi ya kozanga bokebi ntango na ba panneaux ya marbre ya batempelo, na ba piédestals ya bikeko, mpe na nguba, ba médailles, ba kopo, mpe na mbongo ya bibende, dauphin ezalaki kobendama na ba échelles ya bibundeli ya minyɔlɔlɔ lokola oyo ya Saladin, mpe motó oyo ezali lokola kashɛ Esika ya Santu George; banda wana, eloko moko ya lolenge moko ya ndingisa esili kolonga, bobele na bililingi mingi oyo bato mingi balingaka ya baleine te, kasi na makambo mingi ya siansi oyo balobelaki ye.

Sikawa, na ndenge nyonso, elilingi ya kala koleka oyo ezali tii lelo oyo ata ndenge nini oyo elobaka ete ezali ya baleine, esengeli kozwama na pagoda ya mabulu oyo eyebani mingi ya Elephanta, na Inde. Ba Brahmans balobaka ete na mayemi oyo ezalaki pene na kozanga nsuka ya pagoda wana ya kala, misala mpe misala nyonso oyo moto akoki kosala, mosala nyonso oyo moto akoki kokanisa, ezalaki na elilingi ya bambula liboso ete moko na yango ebima mpenza. Na bongo, likambo ya kokamwa te, ete na lolenge moko to mosusu mosala na biso ya lokumu ya kokanga baleine esengelaki kozala wana elili. Baleine Hindoo oyo balobeli, ezwami na departema ekeseni ya efelo, oyo ezali komonisa bomoto ya Vishnu na lolenge ya leviathan, oyo eyebani na nkombo Matse Avatar. Kasi atako ekeko oyo ezali ndambo ya moto mpe ndambo baleine, mpo na kopesa kaka mokila ya oyo ya nsuka, nzokande eteni wana ya moke ya ye ezali nyonso mabe. Ezali komonana mingi lokola mokila oyo ezali kokita ya anaconda, koleka mabɔkɔ ya minene ya ba flukes ya lokumu ya baleine ya solosolo.

Kasi kende na ba Galleries ya kala, mpe tala sikoyo elilingi ya mosali monene ya moklisto ya mbisi oyo; mpo alongi malamu te koleka Hindou oyo ezalaki liboso ya mpela. Ezali elilingi ya Guido oyo ezali komonisa Perseus azali kobikisa Androméda na mabɔkɔ ya nyama mabe ya mbu to baleine. Guido azuaki modèle ya ekelamu moko ya somo boye wapi? Hogarth mpe te, na kosala mayemi ya esika moko na “Perseus Descending” na ye moko, azali kosala mwa moke malamu koleka. Corpulence monene ya monstre wana ya Hogarthien ebalukaka na likolo, ebendaka na mpasi santimetele moko ya mayi. Ezali na lolenge moko ya howdah na mokɔngɔ na yango, mpe monɔkɔ na yango oyo epanzani oyo ezali na mino oyo mabanga ya ntalo ezali kobalubaluka na kati, ekoki kozwama mpo na Porte ya Batɛki oyo ezali komema longwa na Tamise na nzela ya mai mpo na kokɔta na Linɔ́ngi. Na nsima, ezali na baleines Prodromus ya Sibbald ya kala ya Écossais, mpe baleine ya Jonah, ndenge emonisami na mikanda oyo enyatami na Babiblia ya kala mpe na biteni ya ba primers ya kala. Nini ekolobama mpo na baoyo? Mpo na oyo etali baleine ya mokangami ya mikanda oyo ezali kobalusabalusa lokola lititi ya vinyo zingazinga ya stock ya longo oyo ezali kokita —lokola etyami tembe mpe na wolo na mokɔngɔ mpe na nkasa ya motó ya likambo ya mikanda mingi ya kala mpe ya sika —yango ezali ekelamu moko ya kitoko mpenza kasi ya lisapo mpamba, bamekoli, nazwi yango, uta na bililingi ya ndenge wana na ba vases ya kala. Atako na mokili mobimba babengaka yango dauphin, atako bongo nabengaka mbisi oyo ya mokangami ya mikanda lokola komeka baleine; mpo ete ekanamaki bongo ntango babimisaki aparɛyi yango mpo na mbala ya liboso. Ekɔtisamaki na mobimisi moko ya kala ya Italie esika moko boye soki na ekeke ya 15, na ntango ya Bozongisi ya Boyekoli; mpe na mikolo wana, mpe ata kino na eleko moko ya nsuka, bato mingi bazalaki kokanisa ete ba dauphin ezali lolenge moko ya Leviathan.

Na ba vignettes mpe ba embellissements mosusu ya ba livres mosusu ya kala okokutana na tango mosusu na ba touches très curieuses na baleine, esika ba spouts ya ndenge nionso, ba jets d’eau, ba sources chauds mpe malili, Saratoga na Baden-Baden, eyaka ko bubbler likolo uta na oyo ya ye bɔɔngɔ oyo elɛmbi te. Na lokasa ya motó ya likambo ya ebimeli ya ebandeli ya “Kokende liboso ya boyekoli” okokuta mwa baleines oyo ezali na mposa ya koyeba makambo mingi.

Kasi kotika komeka nyonso wana oyo ezali ya mosala te, tótala bililingi wana ya leviathan oyo elobaka ete ezali bilembo ya mayele, oyo esalemi na siansi, na bato oyo bayebi. Na liboke ya mibembo ya Harris ya kala ezali na mwa bapalaki ya baleine oyo ezwamaki na buku ya mibembo ya Pays-Bas, 1671 T.B., oyo ezali na motó ya likambo “Mobembo ya kokanga baleine na Spitzbergen na masuwa Jonas na baleine, Peter Peterson ya Friesland, nkolo.” Na moko ya bapalaki yango, baleines, lokola ba raft minene ya mabaya, emonisami ete etandami na kati ya bisanga ya zɛlo, na ba ours ya mpɛmbɛ oyo ezali kopota mbangu likoló ya mokɔngɔ na yango ya bomoi. Na plaque mosusu, libunga ya kokamwa esalemi mpo na komonisa baleine na ba flukes perpendicules.

Na nsima lisusu, ezali na quarto moko ya kokamwa, oyo ekomamaki na Kapiteni moko Colnett, Kapiteni ya Poste na basoda ya mai ya Angleterre, oyo ezali na motó ya likambo “Mobembo ya zingazinga ya Cape Horn na kati ya Mbu ya Sudi, mpo na koyeisa monene esika ya koboma mbisi ya baleines ya Spermaceti.” Na buku oyo ezali na plan moko oyo elobaka ete ezali “Elilingi ya baleine Physeter to Spermaceti, oyo ezwami na échelle na oyo ebomamaki na libongo ya Mexique, na sanza ya mwambe, na mobu 1793, mpe etombolamaki na pont.” Nazali na ntembe te ete kapitɛni azwaki fɔtɔ́ oyo ya solosolo mpo na bolamu ya basoda na ye ya mai. Mpo na kolobela kaka likambo moko na ntina na yango, tika naloba ete ezali na liso oyo esalemi, engebene na échelle oyo elandi, na baleine spermatozoïde oyo ekoli mobimba, ekosala ete liso ya baleine wana ezala fenetre ya arc na bolai ya mɛtrɛ soki mitano. Ah, kapiteni na ngai ya mpiko, mpo na nini opesaki biso te Yona oyo azali kotala na liso wana!

Ba compilations ya histoire naturelle oyo eleki conscience te mpo na bolamu ya bilenge mpe ya tendre, ezali te sans même hainousness ya erreur. Talá buku wana oyo bato mingi balingaka “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature.” Na ebimeli mokuse ya Londres ya 1807, ezali na bapalaki ya “baleine” oyo balobaka ete ezali “baleine” mpe “baleine narwhale.” Nalingi te komonana lokola moto ya kitoko te, kasi baleine oyo ya kitoko te ezali lokola ngulu oyo bakatá biteni; mpe, mpo na oyo etali baleine narwhale, kotala yango mwa moke ekoki mpo na kokamwa moto, ete na ekeke oyo ya zomi na libwa hippogrife ya ndenge wana ekokaki kozwama na mabɔkɔ mpo na kozala solo likoló ya bato nyonso ya mayele ya bana mibali ya kelasi.

Na nsima, lisusu, na 1825, Bernard Germain, Comte de Lacépède, moto monene oyo ayekolaka makambo ya bomoi, abimisaki buku moko ya siansi oyo ebongisami mpo na baleines, oyo kati na yango ezali na bililingi mingi ya mitindo ndenge na ndenge ya Leviathan. Nyonso wana ezali bobele ya mabe te, kasi elilingi ya baleine Mysticetus to Groenland (elingi koloba, baleine Droite), ata Scoresby, moto oyo ayebi mosala yango banda kala mpo na kosimba lolenge wana ya nyama, asakoli ete azali na moninga na yango te na bozalisi.

Kasi kotiya cap-sheaf na mombongo oyo nyonso oyo ezalaki kosala mabunga ezalaki kaka mpo na Frederick Cuvier, moto ya siansi, ndeko ya Baron oyo ayebani mingi. Na 1836, abimisaki buku moko (Histoire naturelle des baleines), oyo apesi oyo ye abengi elilingi ya Baleine sperme. Yambo ya kolakisa elilingi wana na Nantucketer nyonso, esengelaki malamu kopesa mpo na bozongisi na yo ya mokuse uta na Nantucket. Na liloba moko, Baleine Spermique ya Frederick Cuvier ezali Baleine Spermique te, kasi squash. Ya solo, azwaki ata mokolo moko te litomba ya mobembo ya kokanga baleine (mibali ya ndenge wana bazwaka yango mingi te), kasi epai wapi azwaki elilingi yango, nani akoki koyeba? Mbala mosusu azwaki yango lokola moto oyo azalaki liboso na ye ya siansi na mosala yango moko, Desmarest, azwaki moko ya kosopa zemi na ye ya solosolo; elingi koloba, euti na dessin moko ya Chinois. Mpe lolenge nini ya mibali ya bomoi na crayon oyo Chinois wana bazali, ba kopo mpe ba saucers mingi ya queer eyebisaka biso.

Mpo na baleines ya bato oyo basalaka mayemi na bilembo oyo emonanaka na babalabala ekangami likoló ya bamagazini ya bato oyo batɛkaka petrole, bakoloba nini mpo na yango? Mingimingi bazali Richard III. baleines, oyo ezali na ba humps dromédiaires, mpe ya zamba mingi; kolya bilei ya ntɔ́ngɔ́ na ba tartes misato to minei ya bato ya masuwa, elingi koloba bamasuwa ya baleines oyo etondi na bato oyo batambolaka na masuwa: mbeba na yango ezali kotɛngatɛnga na mbu ya makila mpe ya langi ya bule.

Kasi mabunga wana ya ndenge na ndenge na oyo etali komonisa baleine ezali mpenza likambo ya kokamwa te nsima ya nyonso. Kotalela! Mayemi mingi ya siansi ezwami na mbisi oyo ekangami na nsinga; mpe yango ezali pene na sembo lokola elilingi ya masuwa oyo ebebi, oyo mokɔngɔ ebukani, ekokaki komonisa malamu nyama ya lokumu yango moko na lolendo na yango nyonso oyo epasukaka te ya coque mpe ya ba spars. Atako bangando etelemi mpo na bolai na yango mobimba, Leviathane ya bomoi amitombolaki naino na bosembo te mpo na elilingi na ye. Baleine ya bomoi, na bonene mpe na ntina na yango mobimba, esengeli komonana kaka na mbu na mai oyo ekoki kososolama te; mpe na mai eteni monene na ye ezali mosika na miso, lokola masuwa ya molongo ya etumba oyo ebandi; mpe libanda ya eloko wana ezali likambo oyo ekoki kosalema libela te mpo na moto ya kufa kotombola ye na nzoto na mopepe, mpo na kobatela kovimba mpe ba ondulations na ye nyonso ya nguya. Mpe, koloba te bokeseni oyo ekanisami mingi ya contour kati na elenge baleine oyo ezali komela mpe Leviathan ya Platon oyo ekoli mobimba; nzokande, ata na likambo ya moko ya bana wana ya baleines oyo ezali komɛla oyo etombolami na esika ya masuwa, na ntango wana ezali na lolenge na ye ya kokamwa, oyo ezali lokola anguille, ya kolɛmba, oyo ekeseni, na boye ete elongi na ye ya sikisiki oyo zabolo ye moko akokaki kokanga te.

Kasi ekoki kokanisama, ete uta na mikuwa ya bolumbu ya baleine oyo ekangami, makanisi ya sikisiki ekoki kozwama oyo esimbaki lolenge na ye ya solosolo. Ata moke te. Mpo ezali moko ya makambo oyo ezali kobenda likebi mingi na Leviathan oyo, ete mikuwa na ye epesi likanisi moke mpenza ya lolenge na ye ya monene. Atako mikuwa ya Jeremy Bentham, oyo ekangami mpo na candelabre na bibliothèque ya moko ya ba exécuteurs na ye, ezali kopesa na bosolo likanisi ya monsieur ya kala ya utilitaire ya miso ya nzoto makasi, na bizaleli mosusu nyonso ya Jeremy ya liboso ya moto ye moko; nzokande eloko moko te ya lolenge oyo ekokaki kozwama na mikuwa moko ya leviathan oyo ekangamaki. Kutu, lokola Mobomi-nyama monene alobi yango, mikuwa mpamba ya baleine ezali na boyokani ndenge moko na nyama oyo ekɔtisami mobimba mpe oyo ekɔtisami na biloko lokola nyamankɛkɛ yango na chrysalis oyo ezipaka yango na ndenge ya zingazinga. Bokeseni wana emonisami na lolenge ya kokamwa na motó, lokola na eteni mosusu ya buku oyo ekolakisama na mbalakaka. Ezali mpe na ndenge ya kokamwa mpenza na nsɔngɛ ya mopanzi, oyo mikuwa na yango eyanolaka pene na mpenza na mikuwa ya lobɔkɔ ya moto, longola kaka mosapi monene. Naino yango ezali na misapi minei ya mikuwa oyo esalemaka mbala na mbala, misapi ya index, ya katikati, ya lopɛtɛ mpe ya moke. Kasi nyonso wana efandaka libela na ezipeli na yango ya mosuni, lokola misapi ya moto na ezipeli ya mayele. Mokolo moko Stubb, moto oyo azalaki kosɛkisa, alobaki boye: “Atako na kozanga likebi, baleine ekoki ntango mosusu kosalela biso, tokoki mpenza koloba ete asimbaka biso kozanga mbeli.”

Mpo na bantina oyo nyonso, bongo, lolenge nyonso oyo okoki kotala yango, osengeli kosukisa ete Leviatana monene ezali ekelamu moko wana na mokili oyo esengeli kotikala kozanga langi tii na nsuka. Ya solo, elilingi moko ekoki kobɛta elembo yango penepene mpenza koleka mosusu, kasi moko te akoki kobɛta yango na ndenge ya sikisiki mpenza. Na yango, ezali na lolenge moko te ya mabele mpo na koyeba mpenza ndenge oyo baleine ezali mpenza. Et le seul mode oyo okoki ko dériver même idée tolérable ya contour na ye vivant, ezali na kokende ko baleine yo moko; kasi na kosaláká bongo, ozali na likama moke te ya kozala libela na fulu mpe kozinda na ye. Yango wana, emonani na ngai ete osengelaki kozala na bokasi mingi te na mposa na yo ya koyeba kosimba Leviathan oyo.





MOKAPO 56. Ya Bililingi oyo ezali na mabunga mingi te ya baleines, mpe bilili ya solo ya bisika ya kokanga baleine.

Na boyokani na bililingi ya nsomo ya baleines, namekami makasi awa mpo na kokota likolo ya masolo wana ya nsomo mingi koleka ya bango oyo esengeli kozwama na mikanda mosusu, ezala ya kala mpe ya mikolo oyo, mingimingi na Pline, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, . etc. Kasi nalekisaka likambo wana.

Nayebi bobele ba plans minei oyo ebimisami ya Baleine spermatozoïde monene; Ya Colnett, ya Huggins, ya Frederick Cuvier, mpe ya Beale. Na mokapo oyo eleki balobelaki Colnett mpe Cuvier. Ya Huggins eleki oyo ya bango mosika; kasi, na ba chances minene, ya Beale nde eleki malamu. Mayemi nyonso ya Beale ya baleine oyo ezali malamu, longola kaka elilingi ya katikati na elilingi ya baleine misato oyo ezali na bizaleli ndenge na ndenge, oyo ezali kokanga mokapo na ye ya mibale. Frontispiece na ye, bamasuwa oyo ezali kobundisa ba Baleines spermatozoïdes, atako na ntembe te etangamaki mpo na kolamusa ntembe ya civil ya mibali mosusu ya salon, ezali na lolenge ya kokamwa mpenza mpe ezali lokola bomoi na bopusi na yango ya monene. Ba dessins mosusu ya baleine sperme na J. Ross Browne ezali assez correcte na contour; kasi mayemi na yango na ndenge ya mawa. Yango ezali foti na ye te atako bongo.

Ya Baleine Droite, bililingi ya plan oyo eleki malamu ezali na Scoresby; kasi babendami na échelle ya moke mpenza mpo na kopesa likanisi oyo elingi. Azali kaka na elilingi moko ya bililingi ya kokanga baleine, mpe yango ezali bozangi ya mawa, mpamba te ezali kaka na bililingi ya ndenge wana, ntango esalemi ata moke te, nde okoki kozwa eloko nyonso lokola likanisi ya solo ya baleine ya bomoi ndenge babomi-nyama na ye ya bomoi bamonaka yango.

Kasi, soki totali yango nyonso, mosika mpenza, oyo eleki malamu, atako na makambo mosusu ya mikemike ezali malamu koleka te, oyo ezali kolakisa baleine mpe bililingi ya kokanga baleine oyo ekoki kozwama esika nyonso, ezali mayemi mibale ya minene ya Lifalanse, oyo esalemi malamu, mpe ezwami na mayemi ya Garnery moko. Respectivement, ba représenter ba attaques na ba Sperms na Baleine Droite. Na mayemi ya yambo Baleine Spermique moko ya lokumu elakisami na nkembo mobimba ya nguya, etombolaki kaka na nse ya masuwa uta na bozindo ya mbu, mpe ememi likolo na mopepe na mokɔngɔ na ye biloko ya nsɔmɔ oyo ebebi ya mabaya oyo batyá na fulu. Liboso ya masuwa ebukani ndambo te, mpe ebendami kaka na bokatikati na mokuwa ya mokɔngɔ ya nyama mabe yango; mpe kotelema na liboso wana, mpo na kongenga moko wana ya ntango oyo ekoki kotángama te, omoni mokumbi-masuwa, ndambo ezipami na nsuki ya kotɔka oyo ezali kotɔka ya baleine, mpe na mosala ya kopumbwa, lokola nde autaki na esika oyo ezali na nse ya mabelé. Mosala ya likambo mobimba ezali malamu mpe ya solo na ndenge ya kokamwa. Line-tub oyo ezali na ndambo ya mpamba ezali kopumbwa likoló ya mbu oyo ekómi mpɛmbɛ; makonzí ya mabaya ya ba harpoons oyo esopani ebɛtaka na kati na yango na ndenge ya oblique; mitó ya bato oyo bazali kobɛta mai epalangani zingazinga ya baleine na ndenge oyo ekeseni ya kobanga; nzokande na ntaka ya mopepe makasi ya moindo masuwa ezali kokita na esika ya likambo. Libunga monene ekoki komonana na makambo mikemike ya nzoto ya baleine oyo, kasi tiká ete yango eleka; lokola, mpo na bomoi na ngai, nakokaki te kobenda oyo ya malamu boye.

Na mayemi ya mibale, masuwa ezali na mosala ya kobenda pembeni ya flanque barnacled ya Baleine Droite moko monene oyo ezali kopota mbangu, oyo ebalusaka eteni na ye ya matiti mabe ya moindo na mbu lokola mwa mabanga ya mossy-slide uta na mabanga ya Patagonie. Ba jets na ye ezali semba, etondi, mpe moindo lokola mputulu; na boye ete uta na milinga mingi boye na kati ya cheminée, okokanisa ete esengeli kozala na elambo ya mpokwa ya mpiko oyo ezali kolamba na misɔpɔ minene oyo ezali na nse. Ndɛkɛ ya mbu ezali kobɛtabɛta ba krab ya mikemike, mbisi oyo babengi shell-fish, mpe bonbon mosusu ya mbu mpe maccaroni, oyo Baleine Droite ememaka ntango mosusu na mokɔngɔ na ye oyo ezali na maladi mabe. Mpe ntango nyonso wana leviathan ya mbɛbu minene ezali koleka mbangu na mozindo, kotikaka ba tonnes ya ba curds ya mpɛmbɛ ya mobulu na nsima na ye, mpe kosala ete masuwa moke eningana na bisika oyo evimbaki lokola skiff oyo ekangami pene na ba roues ya paddle ya masuwa ya mopɛpɛ ya mbu. Na yango, esika ya liboso ezali nyonso mobulu makasi; kasi nsima, na bokeseni ya mayele ya kokamwa, ezali na nivo ya vitre ya mbu oyo ekiti, ba voiles oyo ezali na amidon te oyo ezali kokita ya masuwa oyo ezangi nguya, mpe ebele ya baleine oyo ekufá oyo ezali kosala te, esika makasi oyo balongi, na drapo ya bokangami oyo ekangami na paresseux na baleine- nzete oyo batyaki na libulu na ye ya kobimisa mai.

Nani Garnery mosali ya mayemi azali, to azalaki, nayebi te. Kasi bomoi na ngai mpo na yango azalaki soki ayebaki mpenza likambo na ye, to soki te na ndenge ya kokamwa azwaki mateya na mwa mokumbi baleine oyo ayebi mosala malamu. Ba français nde ba garçons pona ko peinture action. Kende kotala mayemi nyonso ya Mpoto, mpe wapi okokuta galeri ya mobulu ya bomoi mpe ya kopema boye na toile, lokola na ndako wana ya elonga na Versailles; epai wapi motali abundi nzela na ye, pell-mell, na nzela ya bitumba minene oyo elandi ya France; epai mopanga nyonso emonani lokola kongɛnga ya miinda ya Nɔrdi, mpe bakonzi mpe Baamperɛrɛ oyo bazali na bibundeli oyo balandaki mbangu, lokola efundeli ya ba centaurs oyo balati motole? Ebongi mpenza te na esika na galerie wana, ezali biteni oyo ya bitumba ya mbu ya Garnery.

Emonani ete makoki oyo bato ya France bazali na yango mpo na kokanga kitoko ya biloko ezali komonana na ndenge ya kokamwa na mayemi mpe mayemi nini bazali na yango mpo na bililingi na bango ya kokanga baleine. Lokola ata moko te na zomi ya mayele ya Angleterre na mosala ya koboma mbisi, mpe ndambo ya nkóto te ya oyo ya bato ya Amerika, atako bongo bapesaki bikólo nyonso mibale basketi bobele moko oyo esilaki oyo ekoki ata moke te kopesa elimo ya solosolo ya koluka baleine. Mingimingi, bato ya Angleterre mpe ya Amerika oyo basalaka mayemi ya baleine bamonani ete basepelaka mpenza na kolakisa ndenge oyo biloko esalaka na masini, na ndakisa ndenge oyo baleine yango ezali mpamba; oyo, mpo na oyo etali kitoko ya bopusi, ekokani na kosala eskeche ya profil ya piramide. Ata Scoresby, mobateli ya baleine Droite oyo ayebani na bosembo, nsima ya kopesa biso bolai mobimba ya makasi ya baleine ya Groenland, mpe bililingi misato to minei ya mikemike ya pɛtɛɛ ya baleines narwhales mpe ba porpoises, asalisaka biso na molɔngɔ ya mayemi ya kala ya ba crochets ya masuwa, bambeli ya kokatakata, mpe ba grapnels; mpe na diligence microscopique ya Leuwenhoeck moko amikitisi na inspection ya mokili oyo ezali koningana ntuku libwa na motoba fac-similes ya ba cristaux ya neige ya Arctique magnifié. Nalingi koloba kotyola te mpo na mobembo malamu mpenza (nakumisaka ye mpo na ancien combattant), kasi na likambo ya ntina mingi ezalaki mpenza bokengeli mpo na kozwa te mpo na cristal nyonso affidavit ya ndai oyo ezwamaki liboso ya Zuzi ya Kimya ya Groenland.

Thus glistening for a moment, as slowly it subsided, and sank. Then once more arose, and silently gleamed. It seemed not a whale; and yet is this Moby Dick? thought Daggoo. Again the phantom went down, but on re-appearing once more, with a stiletto-like cry that startled every man from his nod, the negro yelled out—“There! there again! there she breaches! right ahead! The White Whale, the White Whale!”

Upon this, the seamen rushed to the yard-arms, as in swarming-time the bees rush to the boughs. Bare-headed in the sultry sun, Ahab stood on the bowsprit, and with one hand pushed far behind in readiness to wave his orders to the helmsman, cast his eager glance in the direction indicated aloft by the outstretched motionless arm of Daggoo.

Whether the flitting attendance of the one still and solitary jet had gradually worked upon Ahab, so that he was now prepared to connect the ideas of mildness and repose with the first sight of the particular whale he pursued; however this was, or whether his eagerness betrayed him; whichever way it might have been, no sooner did he distinctly perceive the white mass, than with a quick intensity he instantly gave orders for lowering.

The four boats were soon on the water; Ahab's in advance, and all swiftly pulling towards their prey. Soon it went down, and while, with oars suspended, we were awaiting its reappearance, lo! in the same spot where it sank, once more it slowly rose. Almost forgetting for the moment all thoughts of Moby Dick, we now gazed at the most wondrous phenomenon which the secret seas have hitherto revealed to mankind. A vast pulpy mass, furlongs in length and breadth, of a glancing cream-colour, lay floating on the water, innumerable long arms radiating from its centre, and curling and twisting like a nest of anacondas, as if blindly to clutch at any hapless object within reach. No perceptible face or front did it have; no conceivable token of either sensation or instinct; but undulated there on the billows, an unearthly, formless, chance-like apparition of life.

As with a low sucking sound it slowly disappeared again, Starbuck still gazing at the agitated waters where it had sunk, with a wild voice exclaimed—“Almost rather had I seen Moby Dick and fought him, than to have seen thee, thou white ghost!”

“What was it, Sir?” said Flask.

“The great live squid, which, they say, few whale-ships ever beheld, and returned to their ports to tell of it.”

But Ahab said nothing; turning his boat, he sailed back to the vessel; the rest as silently following.

Whatever superstitions the sperm whalemen in general have connected with the sight of this object, certain it is, that a glimpse of it being so very unusual, that circumstance has gone far to invest it with portentousness. So rarely is it beheld, that though one and all of them declare it to be the largest animated thing in the ocean, yet very few of them have any but the most vague ideas concerning its true nature and form; notwithstanding, they believe it to furnish to the sperm whale his only food. For though other species of whales find their food above water, and may be seen by man in the act of feeding, the spermaceti whale obtains his whole food in unknown zones below the surface; and only by inference is it that any one can tell of what, precisely, that food consists. At times, when closely pursued, he will disgorge what are supposed to be the detached arms of the squid; some of them thus exhibited exceeding twenty and thirty feet in length. They fancy that the monster to which these arms belonged ordinarily clings by them to the bed of the ocean; and that the sperm whale, unlike other species, is supplied with teeth in order to attack and tear it.

There seems some ground to imagine that the great Kraken of Bishop Pontoppodan may ultimately resolve itself into Squid. The manner in which the Bishop describes it, as alternately rising and sinking, with some other particulars he narrates, in all this the two correspond. But much abatement is necessary with respect to the incredible bulk he assigns it.

By some naturalists who have vaguely heard rumors of the mysterious creature, here spoken of, it is included among the class of cuttle-fish, to which, indeed, in certain external respects it would seem to belong, but only as the Anak of the tribe.





CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
