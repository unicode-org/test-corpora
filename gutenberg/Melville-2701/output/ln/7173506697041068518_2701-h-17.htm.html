MOKAPO 86. Mokila.

Bapowɛti mosusu babɛti masanzoli ya liso ya pɛtɛɛ ya antilope, mpe nsala kitoko ya ndɛkɛ oyo ekitaka ata moke te; moke ya likoló, nasepelaka na mokila.

Kokanisaka ete mokila ya Baleine sperme oyo ezali na bonene ya monene koleka mpo ebandi na esika wana ya mokuwa epai ekómaka moke tii na zingazinga ya moto, ezali kaka na likoló na yango, etando ya ata mɛtrɛ-kare ntuku mitano. Nzoto ya zingazinga ya mosuni na yango oyo ezali moke epalanganaka na mabɔkɔ mibale ya minene, ya makasi, ya patatalu to ya ba flukes, mpe mokemoke epumbwaka tii na bonene ya santimɛtrɛ moko te. Na esika oyo libumu to esika oyo esanganaka, ba flukes yango ebalukaka mwa moke, na nsima na mopanzi ezongaka nsima lokola mapapu, mpe etikaka esika monene oyo ezali na eloko te kati na yango. Na ekelamu moko te ya bomoi, milɔngɔ ya kitoko elimbolami malamu koleka na bandelo ya sanza ya sanza ya ba flukes wana. Na bopanzani na yango ya nsuka na baleine oyo ekoli mobimba, mokila na yango ekoleka mpenza na bolai ya mɛtrɛ ntuku mibale.

Membre mobimba emonani lokola mbeto ya webbed dense ya misisa ya soudure; kasi kata na kati na yango, mpe okomona ete biteni misato ekeseni nde esalaka yango: —ya likoló, ya katikati, mpe ya nse. Ba fibres na couches ya likolo na ya se, ezali milayi pe horizontal; oyo ya oyo ya katikati, mokuse mpenza, mpe ezali kotambola na ekulusu kati na ba couches ya libanda. Ebongiseli wana ya bosato, lokola eloko mosusu nyonso, epesaka nguya na mokila. Mpo na moyekoli ya bifelo ya kala ya Loma, couche ya katikati ekopesa parallèle moko ya kokamwa na nzela ya pete ya ba carreaux oyo ebalukaka ntango nyonso na libanga na kati ya biloko wana ya kokamwa ya biloko ya kala, mpe oyo na ntembe te esalisaka mingi na makasi monene ya mabanga.

Kasi lokola soki nguya monene wana ya esika oyo ezali na mokila oyo ezali na misisa ekoki te, eteni monene mobimba ya leviathan ekangami na nsinga mpe nsinga ya misisa mpe bansinga ya misisa, oyo elekaka na ngámbo nyonso mibale ya loketo mpe ezali kokita na kati ya ba flukes, na kozanga mayele kosangana na bango, mpe mingimingi kopesa mabɔkɔ na nguya na bango; na boye ete na mokila nguya oyo esanganaka oyo ekoki komekama te ya baleine mobimba emonani lokola ete ekangami tii na esika moko. Est-ce que annihilation ekokaki kosalema na matériel, oyo ezalaki eloko ya kosala yango.

Yango mpe te —makasi na yango ya kokamwa, ata moke te ezali na momeseno ya kopekisa kogumbama ya kitoko ya koningana na yango; epai wapi bomwana ya pete ebalukaka na nzela ya Titanisme ya nguya. Au contraire, ba mouvements wana ezuaka kitoko na yango ya somo koleka na yango. Bokasi ya solosolo ebebisaka ata moke te kitoko to boyokani, kasi mbala mingi epesaka yango; mpe na eloko nyonso oyo ezali kitoko mpenza, makasi ezali na boyokani mingi na maji. Longola misisa oyo ekangami oyo bisika nyonso emonani lokola ezali kopasuka na marbre oyo ezali na Hercule oyo eyemami, mpe kitoko na yango elingaki kosila. Ntango Eckerman oyo amipesaki mingi atombolaki nkasa ya lini na ebembe ya bolumbu ya Goethe, azindisamaki na ntolo monene ya mobali yango, oyo emonanaki lokola arche ya elonga ya Loma. Tango Angelo azali ko peinture ata Nzambe Tata na forme ya mutu, marquer robusté nini ezali wana. Mpe eloko nyonso oyo bakoki kobimisa na ntina na bolingo ya bonzambe kati na Mwana, bililingi ya pɛtɛɛ, ya kobalusama, ya kosangisa nzoto na mibali ya Italie, oyo kati na yango likanisi na ye ekɔtisamaki na elonga mingi; bililingi yango, oyo ezali mpenza na bozangi ya makasi nyonso, ezali kopesa likanisi ya nguya moko te, kasi oyo ya mabe mpamba, ya mwasi ya komikitisa mpe ya koyika mpiko, oyo na maboko nyonso endimami, ezali kosala bizaleli malamu ya kosalela oyo ekeseni na mateya na ye.

Elasticité subtile ya organe oyo na traite ezali boye, que ezala esalelami na sport, to na sembo, to na kanda, ezala na humeur nini ezali, ba flexions na yango ezali invariablement marquée na ngolu eleki. Na kati na yango loboko ya fée moko te ekoki koleka yango.

Ba mouvements minene mitano ezali particulière na yango. Ya liboso, ntango basaleli yango lokola naino mpo na kokende liboso; Ya mibale, ntango basalelaka yango lokola mace na etumba; Ya misato, na kokɔmbɔla; Ya minei, na makambo ya lobtailing; Ya mitano, na ba flukes oyo ezali na sommet.

Ya liboso: Lokola ezali horizontal na esika na yango, mokila ya Leviathan esalaka na lolenge ekeseni na mikila ya bikelamu mosusu nyonso ya mbu. Ebalukaka ata moke te. Na moto to mbisi, kobalubaluka ezali elembo ya kozala na nse. Mpo na baleine, mokila na ye ezali motindo bobele moko oyo epusaka yango. Scroll-wise coiled liboso na nse ya nzoto, mpe na nsima nokinoki sprung nsima, ezali oyo epesaka wana singulier darting, kopumbwa mouvement na monstre ntango furiously natation. Ba fins na ye ya mipanzi esalaka kaka mpo na ko diriger.

Ya mibale: Ezali mwa ntina, ete atako baleine moko ya spermatozoïde ebundisaka kaka baleine spermatozoïde mosusu na motó mpe na mbanga na ye, atako bongo, na matata na ye na moto, asalelaka mingimingi mpe na kotyola mokila na ye. Na kobɛtaka masuwa, abalukaka nokinoki ba flukes na ye na yango, mpe kobɛtama yango esalemaka kaka mpo na kozonga nsima. Soki esalemi na mopɛpɛ oyo epekisami te, mingimingi soki ekiti tii na elembo na yango, na nsima, AVC yango ekoki kopekisa yango te. Mikuwa ya mokɔngɔ ya moto to masuwa moko te ekoki koyika mpiko na yango. Lobiko na yo bobele moko ezali na kokima yango; kasi soki eyei na mopanzi na nzela ya mai oyo ezali kotɛmɛla, boye ndambo na yango mpo na kopumbwa ya pɛtɛɛ ya masuwa oyo ezali na baleine, mpe ndenge oyo biloko na yango ekómaka makasi, mbala mingi mokuwa ya mokɔngɔ oyo epasuka to etanda oyo epasukaki to mibale, lolenge moko ya kotonga na mopanzi mbuma oyo eleki mabe. Mbala mingi, bazwaka ba coups wana ya mipanzi oyo ezindi na mai na esika ya koboma mbisi, na boye ete batalelaka yango lokola lisano ya bana mike mpamba. Mosusu alongoli elamba, mpe libulu etɛlɛmi.

Ya misato: Nakoki kolakisa yango te, kasi emonani na ngai, ete na baleine sens ya kosimba ezali concentré na mokila; mpo na likambo yango ezali na eloko moko ya kitoko oyo ekokani kaka na kitoko ya mokuwa ya ngando. Elengi wana ya kitoko emonanaka mingimingi na mosala ya kokɔmbɔla, ntango na boboto ya mwana mwasi baleine na malɛmbɛ moko boye ya pɛtɛɛ etambolisaka ba flukes na ye minene longwa na ngámbo moko tii na ngámbo mosusu likoló ya mbu; mpe soki ayoki kaka nsuki ya mokumbi masuwa, mawa mpo na mokumbi masuwa wana, mipende mpe nyonso. Oyo nde boboto ezali na kosimbama wana ya liboso! Soki mokila oyo ezalaki na nguya moko ya kokanga, nasengelaki mbala moko kokanisa ngai na ntina ya ngando ya Darmonodes oyo ezalaki kokende mingi na zando ya bafololo, mpe na mbote ya nse alakisaki ba nosegays na bana basi, mpe na nsima nasimbasimbaki ba zones na bango. Na masolo mingi koleka moko, mawa ezali ete baleine ezali na ezaleli malamu wana ya kokanga na mokila na ye te; mpo nayoki lisusu ngando mosusu, oyo ntango azokaki na etumba, ebalukaki zingazinga ya mokuwa na ye mpe ebimisaki flèche.

Ya minei: Koyiba kozanga koyeba likoló ya baleine na libateli oyo ozali kokanisa ete ezali na katikati ya mbu oyo ezali kaka ye moko, omoni ye agumbami te mpo na nzoto monene ya lokumu na ye, mpe lokola mwana ya mbwa, azali kosakana na mbu lokola nde ezali esika ya kotumba mɔtɔ. Mais pourtant omoni pouvoir na ye na jeu na ye. Maboko ya minene ya mokila na ye ezali kolinga mingi na mopɛpɛ; na nsima kobɛtáká likoló, koningana ya nkake ezali koyokana na boumeli ya bakilomɛtɛlɛ mingi. Okokanisa pene na kokanisa ete mondoki moko monene ebimisamaki; mpe soki omoni guirlande ya pete ya vapeur oyo ewutaki na spiracle na nsuka na ye mosusu, okokanisa ete wana ezalaki milinga oyo ewutaki na libulu ya touch-hole.

Ya mitano: Lokola na etɛlɛmɛlo ya momeseno ya leviathan oyo ezali kopumbwapumbwa, ba flukes elalaka mpenza na nse ya nivo ya mokɔngɔ na ye, na nsima ezalaka mpenza mosika na nse ya likoló; kasi ntango azali pene ya kozinda na mozindo, ba flukes na ye mobimba na ata makolo ntuku misato ya nzoto na ye ebwakama semba na mopepe, mpe bongo etikalaka koningana mwa ntango moke, kino na nse ekobeta masasi libanda ya komonana. Longola kaka kobukana ya lokumu —esika mosusu oyo esengeli kolimbolama —nsɔngɛ wana ya ba flukes ya baleine ezali mbala mosusu likambo oyo eleki monene oyo emonanaka na biloko nyonso oyo ezali na bomoi. Na bozindo oyo ezangi nse mokila monene emonani lokola ezali kobɔtɔla na ndenge ya spasmode na likoló oyo eleki likoló. Na yango, na bandoto, namoni Satana ya lokumu kobimisa libenga na ye monene oyo ezali konyokwama uta na mɔ́tɔ ya Baltique ya Lifelo. Kasi na kotalaka bililingi ya ndenge wana, ezali nyonso na ezalela nini ozali na yango; soki na Dantean, ba zabolo bakoyela bino; soki na oyo ya Yisaya, baanzelu minene. Natelemaki na motó ya mast ya masuwa na ngai na ntango ya kobima ya moi oyo ekómisaki lola mpe mbu motane, mokolo moko namonaki etonga monene ya baleines na ɛsti, bango nyonso bazalaki kokende epai ya moi, mpe mpo na mwa ntango bazalaki koningana na boyokani na ba flukes ya nsonge. Lokola emonanaki na ngai na ntango wana, lolenge monene boye ya losambo ya banzambe emonanaki ata moke te, ata na Perse, ndako ya basambeli ya mɔ́tɔ. Lokola Ptolémée Philopater apesaki litatoli mpo na ngando ya Afrika, na nsima natatolaki mpo na baleine yango, mpe nalobaki ete azali moto oyo amipesaka mingi koleka bikelamu nyonso. Mpo ete engebene Mokonzi Juba, mbala mingi bangando ya basoda ya ntango ya kala bazalaki kokumisa ntɔngɔ na mikuwa na bango oyo etombolama na kimya ya mozindo mpenza.

Kokokanisa ya mbalakaka na mokapo oyo, kati na baleine mpe ngando, na oyo etali makambo mosusu ya mokila ya moko mpe ya mokuwa ya mosusu, esengeli te kozala na momeseno ya kotya binama wana mibale oyo ekeseni na bokokani, mingimingi te bikelamu oyo bango moko bazali na yango. Mpo lokola ngando oyo eleki nguya ezali kaka terrier mpo na Leviatana, ndenge moko mpe, soki tokokanisi yango na mokila ya Leviatana, mokuwa na ye ezali bobele lititi ya lis. Kobɛta ya nsɔmɔ mingi oyo eutaki na mokuwa ya ngando ezalaki lokola kobɛtabɛta ya kosakana ya ventilateur, soki tokokanisi yango na konyata mpe kokwea oyo ezangi mesure ya ba flukes ya kilo ya baleine spermatozoïde, oyo na makambo oyo ezongelami mbala na mbala, moko nsima na mosusu ebwakaki masuwa mobimba na ba remorques na bango nyonso mpe bato oyo basalaka na yango mopɛpɛ, mpenza ndenge mosani moko ya Inde azali kobwaka bale na ye.*

*Atako bokokanisi nyonso na lolenge ya masse générale kati na baleine mpe ngando ezali ya bozoba, na ndenge na likambo wana ya sikisiki ngando etelemi na oyo etali baleine ndenge moko na oyo mbwa esalaka na ngando; atako bongo, ezali na kozanga te mwa makambo oyo ekokani na oyo ya kokamwa; kati na yango ezali na libenga oyo ebimisaka mai. Eyebani malamu ete mbala mingi ngando ekobenda mai to mputulu na kati ya mokuwa na ye, mpe na nsima ekotombola yango, ekobwaka yango na ebale.

Soki nazali kotalela mokila wana ya nguya mingi, nayokaka mawa mingi mpo na kozanga makoki na ngai ya kobimisa yango. Na bantango mosusu ezali na bajɛstɛ kati na yango, oyo, atako ekosepelisa mpenza lobɔkɔ ya moto, etikalaka mpenza oyo ekoki kolimbolama te. Na etonga monene, ya kokamwa mpenza, ntango mosusu, ezali na bajɛstɛ wana ya nkuku, na boye ete nayokaki babomi-nyama oyo basakolaki ete ekokani na bilembo mpe bilembo ya Mason ya Lingomba; ete baleine, ya solo, na nzela ya mayele wana esololaki na mayele na mokili. Ezali mpe te na kozanga koningana mosusu ya baleine na nzoto na ye ya monene, etondi na makambo ya kokamwa, mpe oyo ekoki kopesa mokumba te epai ya mobundi na ye oyo ayebi mingi. Dissect ye ndenge nakoki, bongo, ngai kasi kokende loposo mozindo; Nayebi ye te, mpe nakoyeba ye ata mokolo moko te. Kasi soki nayebi ata mokila ya baleine oyo te, ndenge nini kososola motó na ye? mingi koleka, ndenge nini kososola elongi na ye, nzokande elongi azali na yango te? Okomona biteni na ngai ya mokɔngɔ, mokila na ngai, emonani lokola azali koloba, kasi elongi na ngai ekomonana te. Kasi nakoki te koyeba mpenza biteni na ye ya mokɔngɔ; mpe kopesa likanisi oyo akosala mpo na elongi na ye, nalobi lisusu azali na elongi te.





MOKAPO 87. Armada monene.

Péninsule molai mpe ya moke ya Malacca, oyo etandami na sudi-ɛsti longwa na bateritware ya Birmah, ezali esika oyo eleki na sudi koleka Azia mobimba. Na molɔngɔ oyo ezali kolandana longwa na péninsule wana, bisanga milai ya Sumatra, Java, Bally, mpe Timor etandami; oyo, elongo na mosusu mingi, esalaka mole to efelo monene, oyo na bolai ezali kokangisa Azia na Australie, mpe kokabola mbu ya Inde oyo ebukani te banda kala na bisanga ya ɛsti oyo ezali na biloko minene. Efelo yango etɔbɔlami na mabongo mingi oyo babengi sally-ports mpo masuwa mpe baleines ezala pɛtɛɛ; oyo ezali komonana polele kati na yango ezali na ba détroits ya Sunda mpe ya Malacca. Na ba détroits ya Sunda, mingimingi, masuwa oyo ezali kokende na Chine longwa na wɛsti, ebimaka na mbu ya Chine.

Ba détroits wana ya mikemike ya Sunda ekaboli Sumatra na Java; mpe etɛlɛmaki na katikati ya efelo monene wana ya bisanga, oyo esimbami na nsɔngɛ wana ya vert ya mpiko, oyo bato ya masuwa bayebani na nkombo Java Head; ekokani ata moke te na ekuke ya katikati oyo efungwami na bokonzi moko boye ya monene oyo ezali na bifelo: mpe soki totaleli bozwi oyo esilaka te ya biloko ya nsolo kitoko, mpe soie, mpe biloko ya motuya, mpe wolo, mpe mpɛmbɛ ya nzoku, oyo bisanga nkóto ya mbu wana ya ɛsti ekómi na bozwi na yango, emonani ete ebongiseli moko ya ntina mingi ya biloko bizalisami, ete biloko ya motuya ya ndenge wana, na ndenge oyo mabele yango esalemi mpenza, esengeli ata komonana, ata soki ezali na matomba te, ya kobatelama na mokili ya wɛsti oyo esimbaka makambo nyonso. Mabongo ya détroit ya Sunda ezali na ba forteresse wana ya bokonzi te oyo ebatelaka ba entrées ya Méditerranée, Baltique, mpe Propontis. Na bokeseni na ba Danemark, bato wana ya Orient basengaka te lokumu ya obsequie ya ba voiles ya likolo oyo ekitisami uta na mobembo oyo ezangi nsuka ya masuwa liboso ya mopepe, oyo na boumeli ya bikeke mingi eleki, na butu mpe na moi, elekaki kati na bisanga ya Sumatra mpe Java, oyo ezali na biloko biloko oyo esɛngaka ntalo mingi na ɛsti. Kasi atako batiki na bonsomi nyonso molulu lokola oyo, batika ata moke te likambo oyo balobi ete bakopesa bango mpako ya makasi koleka.

Ntango esili na makanisi ba proas pirates ya ba Malais, oyo ezali kolala na kati ya ba côtes mpe bisanga mike ya Sumatra oyo ezali na elili ya nse, esili koleka na masuwa oyo ezali koleka na kati ya détroit, kosenga na makasi mpako na nsɔngɛ ya makula na bango. Atako na bitumbu ya makila oyo bazwaki mbala na mbala na maboko ya ba cruisers ya Europe, mpiko ya ba corsairs wana esili kozala mwa kopekisa; nzokande, ata na mikolo oyo, toyokaka ntango mosusu nsango ya masuwa ya Angleterre mpe ya Amerika, oyo, na mai wana, ekɔtisamaki na mawa te mpe epunzamaki.

Na mopepe ya malamu mpe ya sika, Pequod ezalaki sikawa kopusana penepene na ba détroits wana; Ahaba na mokano ya koleka na kati na bango na mbu ya Javan, mpe uta kuna, kokendeke na masuwa na nordi, likolo ya mai oyo eyebani ete Baleine Spermique ekendeke mingi awa mpe kuna, kokɔmbɔla libongo na Bisanga ya Philippines, mpe kozwa libongo ya mosika ya Japon, na ntango mpo na eleko monene ya kokanga baleine kuna. Na nzela wana, Pequod oyo ezalaki kobalusabalusa elingaki kokɔmbɔla pene na bisika nyonso oyo eyebani ete Baleines Sperms ezali kotambola na masuwa na mokili mobimba, liboso ya kokita na Ligne na Pasifike; epai wapi Ahaba, atako bisika nyonso mosusu alongaki te na kolanda ye, akanisaki makasi ete akopesa etumba na Moby Dick, na mbu oyo ayebanaki mingi koleka; mpe na eleko oyo bakoki mpenza kokanisa ete azali kotungisa yango.

Kasi, ndenge nini sikoyo? na boluki oyo ya zone, Ahaba asimbaka mabele moko te? bato na ye ya masuwa bamɛlaka mopɛpɛ? Na ntembe te, akotɛlɛma mpo na kozwa mai. Te. Banda kala, sikawa, moi oyo ezali kopota mbangu na cirque esili kopota mbangu na kati ya lopɛtɛ na ye ya mɔ́tɔ, mpe azali na mposa ya bilei te kasi oyo ezali kati na ye moko. Bongo Ahaba. Tyá elembo mpe na likambo oyo, na mobomi-baleine. Ntango ba coque mosusu ekitisaka biloko ya bapaya, mpo na kotinda yango na ba quais ya bapaya; masuwa oyo ezali koyengayenga na mokili mobimba oyo ezali na baleine ememaka biloko te kasi ye moko mpe bato oyo basalaka na yango, bibundeli na bango mpe biloko oyo balingi. Azali na biloko oyo ezali na laki mobimba oyo batye na milangi na esika oyo azali kosimba yango mingi. Azali ballast na ba utilités; mobimba te na ngulu-plomb mpe kentledge oyo ekoki kosalelama te. Amemaka mai ya bambula mingi kati na ye. Mai ya kala ya Nantucket ya kala ya polele; oyo, ntango mbula misato ezali na mai, masuwa Nantucketer, na Pasifike, alingaka komɛla liboso ya mai ya salite, kasi lobi ekendeki na masuwa na kati ya bitunga, longwa na bibale ya Pérou to ya Inde. Yango wana ezali, ete, atako masuwa mosusu ekoki kozala ete ekendaki na Chine longwa na New York, mpe ezongaki lisusu, esimbaki na ebele ya mabongo, masuwa ya baleine, na kati ya ntango wana nyonso, ekoki kozala ete emonaki mbuma moko te ya mabele; bato na ye ya masuwa bamonaki moto te kasi bato ya masuwa oyo bazali kopumbwapumbwa lokola bango. Bongo yango nde omemaki bango nsango ete mpela mosusu eyei; bazalaki koyanola kaka​—“Malamu, bana mibali, talá masuwa!”

Sikawa, lokola Baleines Sperms mingi ekangamaki na libongo ya wɛsti ya Java, na penepene ya Detroits ya Sunda; ya solo, lokola eteni monene ya mabele, rond-point, eyebanaki mingimingi na babomi-mbisi lokola esika malamu mpenza mpo na kotambola na masuwa; yango wana, lokola Pequod ezwaki mingi mpe mingi likolo ya Java Head, batali bazalaki kokumisama mbala na mbala, mpe bapesaki toli ete bálala mpɔngi makasi. Kasi atako mabanga ya vert ya palmier ya mabele eumelaki te kopusana na arc ya loboko ya mobali, mpe na mabulu ya zolo ya esengo cannelle ya sika efungwamaki na mopepe, nzokande ata jet moko te elimbolamaki. Penepene na kotika makanisi nyonso ya kokweya na lisano nyonso awa, masuwa esilaki penepene na kokota na mabulu, ntango kolela ya esengo ya momesano eyokanaki uta likolo, mpe ere long spectacle ya magnificence bobele moko epesaki biso mbote.

Kasi awa ezala premis, ete mpo na mosala ya kolemba te oyo ya mikolo oyo euti koleka ba chassé bango likolo ya ba océans nionso minei, ba Baleines Spermique, na esika ya kosala pene na invariablement kosala mobembo na ba petites compagnies détachées, lokola na tango ya kala, bazali sikoyo mbala mingi kokutana na na bitonga minene, oyo ntango mosusu eyambaki ebele monene mpenza, na boye ete ekozala pene na komonana lokola nde bikólo mingi kati na yango balapi ndai ya boyokani mpe kondimana ya lokumu mpo na kosungana mpe kobatelana. Na bosangisi oyo ya Baleine Spermique na ba caravanes minene boye, ekoki kozala imputé circonstance que ata na ba terrains ya croisière ya malamu koleka, bokoki sikoyo tango mosusu kosala mobembo na masuwa na boumeli ya ba semaines mpe ba mois ensemble, sans que boyambama na spout moko; mpe na nsima kopesama mbote na mbalakaka na oyo ntango mosusu emonani lokola bankóto likoló na bankóto.

“Line! line!” cried Queequeg, looking over the gunwale; “him fast! him fast!—Who line him! Who struck?—Two whale; one big, one little!”

“What ails ye, man?” cried Starbuck.

“Look-e here,” said Queequeg, pointing down.

As when the stricken whale, that from the tub has reeled out hundreds of fathoms of rope; as, after deep sounding, he floats up again, and shows the slackened curling line buoyantly rising and spiralling towards the air; so now, Starbuck saw long coils of the umbilical cord of Madame Leviathan, by which the young cub seemed still tethered to its dam. Not seldom in the rapid vicissitudes of the chase, this natural line, with the maternal end loose, becomes entangled with the hempen one, so that the cub is thereby trapped. Some of the subtlest secrets of the seas seemed divulged to us in this enchanted pond. We saw young Leviathan amours in the deep.*

*The sperm whale, as with all other species of the Leviathan, but unlike most other fish, breeds indifferently at all seasons; after a gestation which may probably be set down at nine months, producing but one at a time; though in some few known instances giving birth to an Esau and Jacob:—a contingency provided for in suckling by two teats, curiously situated, one on each side of the anus; but the breasts themselves extend upwards from that. When by chance these precious parts in a nursing whale are cut by the hunter's lance, the mother's pouring milk and blood rivallingly discolour the sea for rods. The milk is very sweet and rich; it has been tasted by man; it might do well with strawberries. When overflowing with mutual esteem, the whales salute more hominum .

And thus, though surrounded by circle upon circle of consternations and affrights, did these inscrutable creatures at the centre freely and fearlessly indulge in all peaceful concernments; yea, serenely revelled in dalliance and delight. But even so, amid the tornadoed Atlantic of my being, do I myself still for ever centrally disport in mute calm; and while ponderous planets of unwaning woe revolve round me, deep down and deep inland there I still bathe me in eternal mildness of joy.

Meanwhile, as we thus lay entranced, the occasional sudden frantic spectacles in the distance evinced the activity of the other boats, still engaged in drugging the whales on the frontier of the host; or possibly carrying on the war within the first circle, where abundance of room and some convenient retreats were afforded them. But the sight of the enraged drugged whales now and then blindly darting to and fro across the circles, was nothing to what at last met our eyes. It is sometimes the custom when fast to a whale more than commonly powerful and alert, to seek to hamstring him, as it were, by sundering or maiming his gigantic tail-tendon. It is done by darting a short-handled cutting-spade, to which is attached a rope for hauling it back again. A whale wounded (as we afterwards learned) in this part, but not effectually, as it seemed, had broken away from the boat, carrying along with him half of the harpoon line; and in the extraordinary agony of the wound, he was now dashing among the revolving circles like the lone mounted desperado Arnold, at the battle of Saratoga, carrying dismay wherever he went.

But agonizing as was the wound of this whale, and an appalling spectacle enough, any way; yet the peculiar horror with which he seemed to inspire the rest of the herd, was owing to a cause which at first the intervening distance obscured from us. But at length we perceived that by one of the unimaginable accidents of the fishery, this whale had become entangled in the harpoon-line that he towed; he had also run away with the cutting-spade in him; and while the free end of the rope attached to that weapon, had permanently caught in the coils of the harpoon-line round his tail, the cutting-spade itself had worked loose from his flesh. So that tormented to madness, he was now churning through the water, violently flailing with his flexible tail, and tossing the keen spade about him, wounding and murdering his own comrades.

This terrific object seemed to recall the whole herd from their stationary fright. First, the whales forming the margin of our lake began to crowd a little, and tumble against each other, as if lifted by half spent billows from afar; then the lake itself began faintly to heave and swell; the submarine bridal-chambers and nurseries vanished; in more and more contracting orbits the whales in the more central circles began to swim in thickening clusters. Yes, the long calm was departing. A low advancing hum was soon heard; and then like to the tumultuous masses of block-ice when the great river Hudson breaks up in Spring, the entire host of whales came tumbling upon their inner centre, as if to pile themselves up in one common mountain. Instantly Starbuck and Queequeg changed places; Starbuck taking the stern.

“Oars! Oars!” he intensely whispered, seizing the helm—“gripe your oars, and clutch your souls, now! My God, men, stand by! Shove him off, you Queequeg—the whale there!—prick him!—hit him! Stand up—stand up, and stay so! Spring, men—pull, men; never mind their backs—scrape them!—scrape away!”

The boat was now all but jammed between two vast black bulks, leaving a narrow Dardanelles between their long lengths. But by desperate endeavor we at last shot into a temporary opening; then giving way rapidly, and at the same time earnestly watching for another outlet. After many similar hair-breadth escapes, we at last swiftly glided into what had just been one of the outer circles, but now crossed by random whales, all violently making for one centre. This lucky salvation was cheaply purchased by the loss of Queequeg's hat, who, while standing in the bows to prick the fugitive whales, had his hat taken clean from his head by the air-eddy made by the sudden tossing of a pair of broad flukes close by.

Riotous and disordered as the universal commotion now was, it soon resolved itself into what seemed a systematic movement; for having clumped together at last in one dense body, they then renewed their onward flight with augmented fleetness. Further pursuit was useless; but the boats still lingered in their wake to pick up what drugged whales might be dropped astern, and likewise to secure one which Flask had killed and waifed. The waif is a pennoned pole, two or three of which are carried by every boat; and which, when additional game is at hand, are inserted upright into the floating body of a dead whale, both to mark its place on the sea, and also as token of prior possession, should the boats of any other ship draw near.

The result of this lowering was somewhat illustrative of that sagacious saying in the Fishery,—the more whales the less fish. Of all the drugged whales only one was captured. The rest contrived to escape for the time, but only to be taken, as will hereafter be seen, by some other craft than the Pequod.





CHAPTER 88. Schools and Schoolmasters.

The previous chapter gave account of an immense body or herd of Sperm Whales, and there was also then given the probable cause inducing those vast aggregations.

Now, though such great bodies are at times encountered, yet, as must have been seen, even at the present day, small detached bands are occasionally observed, embracing from twenty to fifty individuals each. Such bands are known as schools. They generally are of two sorts; those composed almost entirely of females, and those mustering none but young vigorous males, or bulls, as they are familiarly designated.

In cavalier attendance upon the school of females, you invariably see a male of full grown magnitude, but not old; who, upon any alarm, evinces his gallantry by falling in the rear and covering the flight of his ladies. In truth, this gentleman is a luxurious Ottoman, swimming about over the watery world, surroundingly accompanied by all the solaces and endearments of the harem. The contrast between this Ottoman and his concubines is striking; because, while he is always of the largest leviathanic proportions, the ladies, even at full growth, are not more than one-third of the bulk of an average-sized male. They are comparatively delicate, indeed; I dare say, not to exceed half a dozen yards round the waist. Nevertheless, it cannot be denied, that upon the whole they are hereditarily entitled to en bon point .

It is very curious to watch this harem and its lord in their indolent ramblings. Like fashionables, they are for ever on the move in leisurely search of variety. You meet them on the Line in time for the full flower of the Equatorial feeding season, having just returned, perhaps, from spending the summer in the Northern seas, and so cheating summer of all unpleasant weariness and warmth. By the time they have lounged up and down the promenade of the Equator awhile, they start for the Oriental waters in anticipation of the cool season there, and so evade the other excessive temperature of the year.

When serenely advancing on one of these journeys, if any strange suspicious sights are seen, my lord whale keeps a wary eye on his interesting family. Should any unwarrantably pert young Leviathan coming that way, presume to draw confidentially close to one of the ladies, with what prodigious fury the Bashaw assails him, and chases him away! High times, indeed, if unprincipled young rakes like him are to be permitted to invade the sanctity of domestic bliss; though do what the Bashaw will, he cannot keep the most notorious Lothario out of his bed; for, alas! all fish bed in common. As ashore, the ladies often cause the most terrible duels among their rival admirers; just so with the whales, who sometimes come to deadly battle, and all for love. They fence with their long lower jaws, sometimes locking them together, and so striving for the supremacy like elks that warringly interweave their antlers. Not a few are captured having the deep scars of these encounters,—furrowed heads, broken teeth, scolloped fins; and in some instances, wrenched and dislocated mouths.

But supposing the invader of domestic bliss to betake himself away at the first rush of the harem's lord, then is it very diverting to watch that lord. Gently he insinuates his vast bulk among them again and revels there awhile, still in tantalizing vicinity to young Lothario, like pious Solomon devoutly worshipping among his thousand concubines. Granting other whales to be in sight, the fishermen will seldom give chase to one of these Grand Turks; for these Grand Turks are too lavish of their strength, and hence their unctuousness is small. As for the sons and the daughters they beget, why, those sons and daughters must take care of themselves; at least, with only the maternal help. For like certain other omnivorous roving lovers that might be named, my Lord Whale has no taste for the nursery, however much for the bower; and so, being a great traveller, he leaves his anonymous babies all over the world; every baby an exotic. In good time, nevertheless, as the ardour of youth declines; as years and dumps increase; as reflection lends her solemn pauses; in short, as a general lassitude overtakes the sated Turk; then a love of ease and virtue supplants the love for maidens; our Ottoman enters upon the impotent, repentant, admonitory stage of life, forswears, disbands the harem, and grown to an exemplary, sulky old soul, goes about all alone among the meridians and parallels saying his prayers, and warning each young Leviathan from his amorous errors.

Now, as the harem of whales is called by the fishermen a school, so is the lord and master of that school technically known as the schoolmaster. It is therefore not in strict character, however admirably satirical, that after going to school himself, he should then go abroad inculcating not what he learned there, but the folly of it. His title, schoolmaster, would very naturally seem derived from the name bestowed upon the harem itself, but some have surmised that the man who first thus entitled this sort of Ottoman whale, must have read the memoirs of Vidocq, and informed himself what sort of a country-schoolmaster that famous Frenchman was in his younger days, and what was the nature of those occult lessons he inculcated into some of his pupils.

The same secludedness and isolation to which the schoolmaster whale betakes himself in his advancing years, is true of all aged Sperm Whales. Almost universally, a lone whale—as a solitary Leviathan is called—proves an ancient one. Like venerable moss-bearded Daniel Boone, he will have no one near him but Nature herself; and her he takes to wife in the wilderness of waters, and the best of wives she is, though she keeps so many moody secrets.

The schools composing none but young and vigorous males, previously mentioned, offer a strong contrast to the harem schools. For while those female whales are characteristically timid, the young males, or forty-barrel-bulls, as they call them, are by far the most pugnacious of all Leviathans, and proverbially the most dangerous to encounter; excepting those wondrous grey-headed, grizzled whales, sometimes met, and these will fight you like grim fiends exasperated by a penal gout.

The Forty-barrel-bull schools are larger than the harem schools. Like a mob of young collegians, they are full of fight, fun, and wickedness, tumbling round the world at such a reckless, rollicking rate, that no prudent underwriter would insure them any more than he would a riotous lad at Yale or Harvard. They soon relinquish this turbulence though, and when about three-fourths grown, break up, and separately go about in quest of settlements, that is, harems.

Another point of difference between the male and female schools is still more characteristic of the sexes. Say you strike a Forty-barrel-bull—poor devil! all his comrades quit him. But strike a member of the harem school, and her companions swim around her with every token of concern, sometimes lingering so near her and so long, as themselves to fall a prey.
