Nkigenda, nari nuzuye gutekereza; ibyari byampishuriwe na Kapiteni Ahabu, byanyujuje ibisobanuro bidasobanutse byububabare kuri we. Ariko, ibitekerezo byanjye byari birebire mu bindi byerekezo, ku buryo kuri Ahabu wijimye muri iki gihe yataye umutwe.





UMUTWE WA 17. Ramazani.

Nkuko Ramazani ya Queequeg, cyangwa Kwiyiriza ubusa no Guteterezwa, yagombaga gukomeza umunsi wose, ntabwo nahisemo kumuhungabanya kugeza nimugoroba; kuberako nkunda cyane kubahiriza inshingano z’amadini ya buri wese, sinigeze ntekereza uburyo busekeje, kandi sinigeze mbona mu mutima wanjye guha agaciro ndetse n'itorero ry'ibimonyo bisenga igituba; cyangwa ibyo bindi biremwa byo mu bice bimwe na bimwe byisi yacu, bafite urwego rwo gukandagira ibirenge bitigeze bibaho mu yindi mibumbe, barunama imbere yumurambo wa nyiri nyakwigendera wapfuye bitewe gusa nubutunzi bukabije nyamara butunze kandi bukodeshwa mwizina rye.

Ndabivuze, twe abakristu beza ba Peresibiteriyeni dukwiye kuba abagiraneza muribi bintu, kandi ntitwishime cyane kurenza abandi bantu buntu, abapagani nibitagenda, kuberako ubwirasi bwabo bwasaze kuri izi ngingo. Madamu Hussey! apoplexy! ”- hamwe n'ayo marira, yiruka yerekeza mu gikoni, ndakurikira.

Iruka ku bw'Imana, hanyuma uzane ikintu cyo gukingura urugi - ishoka! - ishoka! yagize ubwonko; bishingikirize! ”- nuko mvuga nti mu buryo butemewe nongeye kwihutira kuzamuka ingazi nambaye ubusa, ubwo Madamu Hussey yateraga inkono ya sinapi na vinegere-cruet, hamwe n'abamuteraga mu maso.

Mu ituze, ariko byihuse muburyo bushoboka, namuhaye kumva ikibazo cyose. Atabishaka akoma vinegere-cruet kuruhande rumwe rw'izuru, yahise avugisha mukanya; hanyuma aratangara ati: “Oya! Sinigeze mbona kuva nashyira aho. ” Yirutse mu kabati gato amanuka ku ngazi, arareba, aragaruka, ambwira ko inanga ya Queequeg yabuze. Yatakambiye ati: “Yiyahuye.” “Ntabwo bibabaje Stiggs yongeye gukorwa - hari indi mpanuka - Imana igirire impuhwe nyina wumukene! - bizaba amatongo yinzu yanjye. Umusore w'umukene yaba mushiki we? Uwo mukobwa ari he? —Hariho, Betty, jya kuri Snarles the Painter, umubwire ko ansiga irangi, hamwe - “nta kwiyahura byemewe hano, kandi nta kunywa itabi muri salle;” - bishobora no guhitana inyoni zombi icyarimwe. Kwica? Uwiteka agirire imbabazi umuzimu we! Urwo rusaku ni uruhe? Wowe, musore, avast hariya! ”

N'urusaku rudasanzwe umuryango urakinguka, ipfundo ryikubita ku rukuta, ryohereza plaster ku gisenge; ngaho, ijuru ryiza! ngaho hicaye Queequeg, rwose ikonje kandi yishyize hamwe; hagati mu cyumba; kwikubita ku ngofero, no gufata Yojo hejuru y'umutwe. Ntiyarebye mu buryo bumwe cyangwa mu bundi buryo, ahubwo yicaye nk'ishusho ishushanyijeho ikimenyetso gike cy'ubuzima bukora.

Ndamubwira nti: "Queequeg, ndamusanga nti:" Queequeg, bikugendekeye bite? "

“Ntiyigeze yicara umunsi wose, si byo?” nyir'inzu.

Ariko ibyo twavuze byose, nta jambo twashoboraga kumukuramo; Njye numvaga nshaka kumusunika hejuru, kugirango mpindure imyanya, kuko byari hafi kutihanganirwa, byasaga nkibibabaza kandi bidasanzwe; cyane, nkuko mubishoboka byose yari yicaye kuburyo hejuru yamasaha umunani cyangwa icumi, agenda nawe adafite amafunguro asanzwe.

Ati: “Madamu Navuze nti: Hussey, ni muzima mu birori byose; nimudusige, niba mubishaka, nanjye ubwanjye nzareba iki kibazo kidasanzwe. ”

Gufunga umuryango kuri nyirinzu, nagerageje gutsinda Queequeg gufata intebe; ariko biba iby'ubusa. Agezeyo, aricara; kandi ibyo yashoboraga gukora byose - kubuhanzi bwanjye bwose bwiyubashye no gutukana - ntabwo yanyeganyega urumogi, cyangwa ngo avuge ijambo na rimwe, cyangwa ngo andeba, cyangwa ngo amenye ko mpari mu buryo bworoheje.

Ndibaza, natekereje I, niba ibi bishoboka ko ari igice cya Ramadhan; ese biyiriza ubusa ingofero zabo muri kirwa kavukire. Bigomba kumera gutya; yego, ni bimwe mubyo yemera, ndakeka; reka rero, reka aruhuke; azahaguruka vuba cyangwa vuba, nta gushidikanya. Ntishobora kuramba, Imana ishimwe, kandi Ramazani yayo iza rimwe gusa mumwaka; kandi sinizera ko byubahiriza igihe.

Namanutse gusangira. Nyuma yo kwicara umwanya munini utegera inkuru ndende za bamwe mubasare bari bavuye mu rugendo rwa plum-pudding, nkuko babyise (ni ukuvuga urugendo rugufi-whale-ingendo muri schooner cyangwa brig, bigarukira mumajyaruguru yumurongo , mu nyanja ya Atalantika gusa); nyuma yo kumva aba plum-puddingers kugeza saa kumi nimwe, nazamutse ingazi kuryama, numva nzi neza ko iki gihe Queequeg agomba kuba yarazanye Ramazani ye. Ariko oya; ngaho yari aho nari namusize; ntabwo yari yazunguye na santimetero imwe. Natangiye kurakara; byasaga naho bidafite ishingiro kandi bidasaze kuba wicaye umunsi wose n'igice cya nijoro ku ngofero ye mu cyumba gikonje, ufashe igiti ku mutwe.

“Ku bw'ijuru, Queequeg, haguruka wikunkumure; haguruka ugire ifunguro rya nimugoroba. Uzicwa n'inzara; uzokwiyahura, Queequeg. ” Ariko nta jambo na rimwe yashubije.

Kumwiheba rero, niyemeje kuryama no kuryama; kandi nta gushidikanya, mbere yigihe kinini, yankurikira. Ariko mbere yo guhindukira, mfata ikoti ryanjye riremereye cyane, ndamujugunya hejuru, kuko byasezeranije ko ari ijoro rikonje cyane; kandi nta kindi yari afite usibye ikoti rye risanzwe. Mugihe runaka, kora ibyo nshaka byose, sinshobora kwinjira muri doze yoroheje. Nari nzimye buji; no gutekereza gusa kuri Queequeg - ntabwo ari metero enye uvuye aho - wicaye aho hantu hatuje, uhagaze wenyine mubukonje n'umwijima; ibi byanteye ubwoba rwose. Bitekerezeho; gusinzira ijoro ryose mucyumba kimwe hamwe n'umupagani mugari ukangutse ku ngofero ye muri Ramazani iteye ubwoba, itabarizwa!

Ariko hari ukuntu nahagaritse amaherezo, kandi nta kindi nari nzi kugeza bwacya; iyo, urebye hejuru yigitanda, hacuramye Queequeg, nkaho yajugunywe hasi. Ariko akimara kubona izuba ryinjiye mu idirishya, arahaguruka, afite ingingo zikomeye kandi zifata, ariko afite akanyamuneza; acumbagira ansanga aho ndyamye; yongeye gukanda uruhanga rwe ku rwanjye; akavuga ko Ramazani ye yarangiye.

Noneho, nkuko nabivuze mbere, ntabwo nanze idini ry'umuntu uwo ari we wese, uko ryaba rimeze kose, igihe cyose uwo muntu atishe cyangwa ngo atuke undi muntu uwo ari we wese, kuko uwo muntu nawe atabyemera. Ariko iyo idini ry'umuntu rihindutse ubwoba; iyo ari umubabaro mwiza kuri we; kandi, neza, ituma iyi si yacu idacumbikirwa neza kubamo; noneho ndatekereza ko igihe kigeze cyo gufata uwo muntu kuruhande tukajya impaka nawe.

Namubwiye kandi, ko kuba mubindi bintu nkumunyamahane ukabije kandi ushyira mu gaciro, byarambabaje, birambabaza cyane, kumubona ubu ari ibicucu biteye isoni kuri Ramazani ye iteye isoni. Usibye, mpaka I, kwiyiriza ubusa bituma ubuvumo bwumubiri; ni yo mpamvu umwuka wijimye; n'ibitekerezo byose byavutse mugisibo bigomba byanze bikunze kuba inzara. Ninimpamvu ituma abanyamadini benshi badakunda cyane bakunda cyane ibitekerezo nkibi byababaje. Mw'ijambo rimwe, Queequeg, yavuze I, ahubwo mu buryo bworoshye; ikuzimu nigitekerezo cyavutse bwa mbere kuri pome idasukuye; kandi kuva icyo gihe yakomezaga binyuze muri heredita dyspepsias yarezwe na Ramadans.

Nabajije Queequeg niba we ubwe yigeze agira ikibazo cya dyspepsia; kwerekana igitekerezo neza, kugirango ashobore kugitwara. Ati oya; gusa mugihe kimwe kitazibagirana. Ni nyuma y'ibirori bikomeye yahawe na se umwami, igihe habaga intambara ikomeye aho mirongo itanu mu banzi bishwe nka saa mbiri za mu gitondo, bose bateka bararya nimugoroba.

UMUTWE WA 18. Ikimenyetso cye.

Ubwo twagendaga tumanuka ku mpera y’ikibuga tugana ku bwato, Queequeg yitwaje inanga ye, Kapiteni Peleg mu ijwi rye riteye ubwoba yadusuhuje cyane na wigwam, avuga ko atigeze akeka ko inshuti yanjye yari umuntu urya abantu, ndetse akanatangaza ko atigeze yemera. abantu barya abantu muri ubwo bukorikori, keretse babanje gukora impapuro zabo.

“Yabaye umunyamuryango kugeza ryari?” hanyuma arambwira ati: “Ntabwo ari ndende cyane, ahubwo ndakeka yuko musore.”

Bildad yatakambiye ati: “Noneho vuga, uyu ni Umufilisitiya ni umwe mu bagize inama isanzwe ya Deacon? Sinigeze mbona ajyayo, kandi ndabinyuza ku munsi wa Nyagasani. ”

Navuze nti: “Ntacyo nzi kuri Deacon Deuteronomie cyangwa inama ye.” “Icyo nzi cyo ni uko Queequeg hano ari umunyamuryango wavutse mu Itorero rya mbere ry'Itorero. Ni umudiyakoni ubwe, Queequeg ni. ”

Bildad ati: "Umusore, uri hejuru yanjye nanjye, sobanura, wa musore wa Heti. Ni irihe torero ushaka kuvuga? Nsubize. ”

Nsanze rero nsunitswe cyane, ndasubiza. “Ndashaka kuvuga, nyakubahwa, Kiliziya Gatolika ya kera aho wowe na njye, na Kapiteni Peleg uhari, na Queequeg hano, kandi twese, kandi umuhungu wa nyina n'ubugingo bwa buri wese; Itorero rya mbere rikomeye kandi rihoraho ryiyi si yose isenga; twese turi abo; gusa bamwe muritwe dukunda amakariso ya queer ntakuntu akora kumyizerere ikomeye; muri ibyo twese dufatanye. ”

Peleg yegera hafi ati: “Gabanya, urashaka kuvuga ko ugabanije amaboko.” “Umusore, byaba byiza wohereje umumisiyoneri, aho kuba ukuboko kwa mbere; Sinigeze numva ikibwiriza cyiza. Umudiyakoni Gutegeka kwa kabiri - impamvu Padiri Mapple ubwe adashobora kuyitsinda, kandi hari icyo yabaze. Injira mu bwato, ngwino; ntuzigere utekereza ku mpapuro. Ndavuga, bwira Quohog ngaho-ibyo wamwita iki? bwira Quohog gutera intambwe. Ninanga nini, mbega inanga yagezeyo! bisa nibintu byiza ibyo; kandi arabyitwaramo neza. Ariko kuri iki kibazo, Queequeg, wari ufite inshuro ebyiri cyangwa eshatu mbere yo kwitabira imihango nk'iyi, yasaga naho atigeze asuzugurwa; ariko gufata ikaramu yatanzwe, yandukuwe ku mpapuro, ahabigenewe, igereranya nyaryo ryerekana ishusho yishushanyijeho ku kuboko; ku buryo binyuze mu makosa akomeye ya Kapiteni Peleg akora ku bujurire bwe, byahagaze nkibi: -

Quohog. ikimenyetso cye X.

Hagati aho, Kapiteni Bildad yicaye ashishikaye kandi ashikamye yitegereza Queequeg, arangije arazamuka yishimye kandi avugiriza mu mufuka munini w'ikote rye rinini cyane yambaye ikote, akuramo agapapuro k'udupapuro, maze ahitamo imwe yise “Umunsi wanyuma uza; cyangwa Nta mwanya wo Gutakaza, ”abishyira mu biganza bya Queequeg, hanyuma abifata hamwe n'igitabo hamwe na we bombi, amureba mu maso, maze avuga ati:“ Mwana w'umwijima, ngomba gukora inshingano zanjye nawe; Ndi nyir'ubwo bwato, kandi numva mpangayikishijwe n'ubugingo bw'abakozi bayo bose; niba ukomeje gutsimbarara ku nzira zawe za gipagani, ibyo ndatinya birababaje, ndagusabye, ntugumane aye umugaragu wa Belial. Hindura ikigirwamana Inzogera, n'ikiyoka gihishe; hindukirira uburakari buzaza; Tekereza ijisho ryawe, ndavuga; yewe! ineza ineza! koresha umwobo ugurumana! ”

Ikintu cyinyanja yumunyu nyamara cyatinze mururimi rwa Bildad rwa kera, bivanze muburyo butandukanye hamwe nibyanditswe byera.

Peleg yatakambiye ati: “Avast hariya, avast hariya, Bildad, avast ubu yonona inanga yacu.” “Abavuza inanga bubaha ntibigera bakora ingendo nziza - ikuramo inyanja muri 'em; nta mucuranga ufite agaciro kicyatsi cyerekana sharkish. Hariho umusore Nat Swaine, wigeze gutinyuka-umutware wubwato muri Nantucket yose na Vineyard; yinjiye mu nama, kandi ntiyigeze agera ku byiza. Yagize ubwoba bwinshi ku bugingo bwe bw’ibyago, ku buryo yagabanutse kandi yitandukanya n’inyanja, kubera gutinya gukubita amashyi, aramutse abonye amashyiga akajya kwa Davy Jones. ”

“Peleg! Peleg! ” yagize ati: uzi, Peleg, icyo ari cyo kugira ubwoba bw'urupfu; nigute rero, ushobora gute gushishoza muri iyi myiyerekano itubaha Imana. Wizeye umutima wawe bwite, Peleg. Mbwira, ubwo iyi Pequod imwe hano yari ifite masta atatu hejuru muri iyo nkubi y'umuyaga mu Buyapani, urwo rugendo rumwe ubwo wajyanaga na Kapiteni Ahabu, ntiwigeze utekereza ku rupfu n'urubanza icyo gihe? ”

Peleg yatakambiye ati: “Umva, umwumve nonaha,” yambukiranya akazu, maze atera amaboko hasi mu mufuka, - “mwumve mwese. Tekereza kuri ibyo! Iyo buri kanya twatekerezaga ko ubwato bwarohama! Urupfu n'urubanza noneho? Niki? Hamwe na masta uko ari atatu akora inkuba iteka kuruhande; ninyanja yose iturenga, imbere na aft. Tekereza ku rupfu n'urubanza noneho? Oya! nta mwanya wo gutekereza ku rupfu icyo gihe. Ubuzima nicyo Kapiteni Ahabu natekerezaga; nuburyo bwo kuzigama amaboko yose-uburyo bwo kwiba joriji-masts - uburyo bwo kwinjira ku cyambu cyegereye; icyo ni cyo natekerezaga. ”

Bildad ntiyongeye kuvuga, ariko akanda ikoti rye, akandagira ku igorofa, aho twamukurikiye. Agezeyo, yarahagaze, acecetse yirengagiza bamwe mu bakora ubwato basanaga ubwato bwo hejuru mu rukenyerero. Noneho noneho arunama kugira ngo atoragure, cyangwa abike iherezo ryimpanga zitemewe, ubundi zishobora kuba zarapfushije ubusa.





UMUTWE WA 19. Intumwa.

“Basangirangendo, mwohereje muri ubwo bwato?”

Jye na Queequeg twari tumaze kuva muri Pequod, kandi twarimo tujya kure y'amazi, mu kanya buri wese ahugiye mu bitekerezo bye, igihe ayo magambo yavuzwe haruguru twashyikirizwaga n'umuntu utazi, waduhagarara imbere yacu, agereranya urutoki rwe runini kuri ubwato buvugwa. Yari yambaye ubusa ariko yambaye ikoti yazimye hamwe na trowsers; igitambaro cy'igitambara cy'umukara gishora ijosi. Ihuriro rito-pox ryari rifite impande zose ryamuzengurutse mu maso, maze risigara rimeze nk'igitanda kitoroshye cy'urubavu rw'umugezi, igihe amazi yihuta yumye.

“Wamwohereje muri we?” arabisubiramo.

Nagerageje kubona umwanya muto wo kumureba nta nkomyi, nti: "Urashaka kuvuga ubwato Pequod, ndakeka".

Ati: "Aye, Pequod - ubwo bwato ngaho."

Navuze nti: “Ego, tumaze gusinya ingingo.”

“Hari ikintu kiri hepfo yerekeye ubugingo bwawe?”

“Bite ho?”

Yahise avuga ati: “Yoo, birashoboka ko utigeze ubona.” "Ntakibazo, nzi ibice byinshi bitigeze bibona, - amahirwe masa kuri 'em; kandi bose ni beza kuri yo. Ubugingo bumeze nk'uruziga rwa gatanu rugana ku igare. ”

“Urasetsa iki, mugenzi wawe mu bwato?” ati I.

Mu buryo butunguranye, uwo muntu atazi yagize ati: " Afite ibihagije, nubwo, kugira ngo akemure ibitagenda neza muri ubwo buryo mu bindi bice ."

Navuze nti: “Queequeg, reka tugende; mugenzi wawe yavunitse ahantu runaka; arimo avuga ikintu runaka n'umuntu tutazi. ”

“Hagarara!” uwo muntu atazi. “Wavuze ukuri - nturabona Inkuba ishaje, si byo?”

“Inkuba ishaje ni nde?” ati njye, nongeye guterura umurava usaze muburyo bwe.

“Kapiteni Ahabu.”

“Bite! umutware w'ubwato bwacu, Pequod? ”

Ati: “Aye, muri bamwe muri twe bashaje basare, agenda yitirirwa. Ntabwo wigeze umubona, si byo? ”

“Oya, ntabwo. Bavuga ko arwaye, ariko agenda amera neza, kandi azongera kumererwa neza bidatinze. ”

“Byose byongeye kandi bidatinze!” yasetse uwo atazi, hamwe no gusetsa bikabije. “Reba; igihe Kapiteni Ahabu ameze neza, noneho ukuboko kwanjye kwi bumoso bizaba byiza; si mbere. ”

“Uzi iki kuri we?”

“ Bakubwiye iki kuri we? Vuga ibyo! ”

“Ntibigeze bamubwira byinshi kuri we; gusa numvise ko ari umuhigi mwiza wa balale, akaba na capitaine mwiza kubakozi be. ”

“Nibyo, nibyo, yego, byombi ni ukuri bihagije. Ariko ugomba gusimbuka mugihe atanze itegeko. Intambwe no gutontoma; gutontoma no kugenda - iryo ni ijambo hamwe na Kapiteni Ahabu. Ariko nta kintu na kimwe kijyanye n'icyo kintu cyamubayeho kuri Cape Horn, kera, igihe yari aryamye nk'uwapfuye iminsi itatu n'amajoro; nta kintu na kimwe kijyanye n'iryo shyano ryica hamwe na Espagne mbere y'urutambiro rwa Santa? - nta kintu na kimwe wigeze wumva kuri ibyo, eh? Ntakintu na kimwe kijyanye na feza calabash yaciriyemo? Kandi ntakintu na kimwe kijyanye no gutakaza ukuguru kwurugendo rwanyuma, nkubuhanuzi. Ntabwo wigeze wumva ijambo kubijyanye nibintu nibindi, eh? Oya, sinkeka ko wabikoze; wabikora ute? Ninde ubizi? Ntabwo ari Nantucket yose, ndakeka. Ariko burya, mayhap, wigeze wumva kuvuga ukuguru, nuburyo yabuze; aye, mwabyumvise, ndatinyuka kubivuga. Yego yego, ko buriwese azi a'most - Ndashaka kuvuga ko bazi ko ari ukuguru kumwe gusa; kandi ko parmacetti yakuyemo indi. ”

Navuze nti: “Mugenzi wanjye, ibyo byose bya gibberish byawe, simbizi, kandi simbyitayeho cyane; kuko kuri njye mbona ugomba kuba wangiritse gato mumutwe. Ariko niba uvuga kuri Kapiteni Ahabu, muri ubwo bwato hariya, Pequod, reka nkubwire, ko nzi byose ku kubura ukuguru. ”

“ Byose , eh - uzi neza ko ubikora? - byose?”

“Ni ukuri.”

Hamwe n'urutoki rwerekejweho ijisho kuri Pequod, umuntu utazi usabiriza yahagaze akanya, nkaho muri reverie ifite ibibazo; hanyuma utangira gato, arahindukira ati: - “Kohereje, ufite? Amazina hasi kumpapuro? Nibyiza, nibyiza, ibyashyizweho umukono, byashyizweho umukono; n'ibizaba, bizaba; hanyuma na none, birashoboka ko bitazaba, nyuma ya byose. Ibyo aribyo byose, byose birakosowe kandi byateguwe neza; n'abasare bamwe cyangwa abandi bagomba kujyana nawe, ndakeka; kimwe nabandi nkabandi bagabo, Imana igirire impuhwe! Igitondo kuri mwebwe, basangirangendo, mugitondo; ijuru ridashobora kuguha imigisha; Mbabajwe no kuba narahagaritse. ”

Navuze nti: “Reba hano, nshuti, niba ufite ikintu cy'ingenzi utubwira, hanze yacyo; ariko niba ugerageza kudutera imigano gusa, uribeshya mumikino yawe; ibyo ni byo ngomba kuvuga. ”

“Kandi byavuzwe neza, kandi nkunda kumva umutwe uvuga muri ubwo buryo; uri umugabo kuri we gusa - nkawe. Igitondo kuri mwebwe, basangirangendo, mugitondo! Yoo! nugerayo, bwira 'em narangije kudakora imwe muri em. ”

“Ah, mugenzi wanjye nkunda, ntushobora kutubeshya muri ubwo buryo - ntushobora kutubeshya. Nicyo kintu cyoroshye ku isi umuntu asa nkaho afite ibanga rikomeye muri we. ”

“Mugitondo kuri mwebwe, basangirangendo, mu gitondo.”

Navuze nti: “Igitondo ni, ngwino, Queequeg, reka dusige uyu musazi. Ariko hagarara, mbwira izina ryawe, uzabikora? ”

“Eliya.”

Eliya! natekereje njye, maze turagenda, twembi dutanga ibitekerezo, nyuma yimyambarire ya mugenzi wawe, kuri uyu musare ushaje; maze yemera ko ntakindi uretse guceceka, kugerageza kuba mubi. Ariko ntitwari twaragiye wenda hejuru ya metero ijana, mugihe duhindukiriye guhindura inguni, tukareba inyuma nkuko nabikoze, ninde wagaragara ariko Eliya adukurikira, nubwo ari kure. Nuburyo, kumubona byarantangaje cyane, ku buryo ntacyo nabwiye Queequeg ko ari inyuma ye, ariko mpita ndi kumwe na mugenzi wanjye, mpangayikishijwe no kureba niba uwo atazi azahindura inguni imwe twakoze. Yarabikoze; hanyuma numvaga ko arimo aduhiga, ariko niyihe ntego ntashobora gukora mubuzima bwanjye. Ibi bintu, bifatanije nubusobanuro bwe budasobanutse, igice-cyerekana, igice-gihishura, ubwoko butandukanye bwibiganiro, ubu byabyaye muri njye ibintu byose bitangaje bidasobanutse no gutinya igice, kandi byose bifitanye isano na Pequod; na Kapiteni Ahabu; ukuguru yari yataye; n'ihembe rya Cape; na feza calabash; n'icyo Kapiteni Peleg yari yaramuvuzeho, ubwo navaga mu bwato ejobundi; no guhanura inkwi Tistig; n'urugendo twari twarahambiriye gufata ubwato; nibindi ijana byigicucu.

Niyemeje kunyurwa niba koko Eliya yarakaye yarimo aduhiga cyangwa atarimo, kandi kubera iyo ntego yambutse inzira na Queequeg, kandi kuruhande rwayo yasubiye inyuma intambwe zacu. Ariko Eliya yararenganye, asa nkaho atatubonye. Ibi byaranduhuye; nubundi, kandi amaherezo nkuko nabonaga, namutangaje mumutima wanjye, humbug.





UMUTWE WA 20. Astir yose.

Umunsi umwe cyangwa ibiri irashize, kandi muri Pequod habaye ibikorwa bikomeye. Ntabwo ubwato bwa kera bwahinduwe gusa, ahubwo ubwato bushya bwazaga mubwato, hamwe na bolts ya canvas, hamwe na coil yo kwiba; muri make, ibintu byose byavugaga ko imyiteguro yubwato yihutiye kurangira. Kapiteni Peleg gake cyangwa ntiyigeze agera ku nkombe, ariko yicaye muri wigwam ye yitegereza neza amaboko: Bildad yakoze ibintu byose byo kugura no gutanga kububiko; n'abagabo bakoreshwaga muri gereza no kuri ruswa bakoraga kugeza nimugoroba.

Bukeye bwaho, Queequeg yashyize umukono ku ngingo, ijambo ryatangiwe mu icumbi ryose aho isosiyete y’ubwato yari ihagaze, ko igituza cyabo kigomba kuba cyinjiye mbere yijoro, kuko nta wabwiraga igihe ubwato bushobora kugenda. Jye na Queequeg rero twamanutse mumitego yacu, twiyemeza, ariko, kuryama ku nkombe kugeza kumperuka. Ariko bisa nkaho bahora batanga integuza ndende muribi bihe, kandi ubwato ntibumara iminsi myinshi. Ariko ntibitangaje; habaye ikintu cyiza cyo gukora, kandi ntawabura kuvuga ibintu byinshi ugomba gutekerezaho, mbere yuko Pequod iba ifite ibikoresho byuzuye.

Buriwese azi icyo ibintu byinshi-ibitanda, isosi-isafuriya, ibyuma nudukoni, amasuka nuduseke, ibitambaro, udukariso, nudukoko, nibitari byo, nibyingenzi mubucuruzi bwo murugo. Gusa rero hamwe na whale, bisaba ko imyaka itatu ikorerwa murugo hejuru yinyanja yagutse, kure yabacuruzi bose, abakora costermongers, abaganga, abatetsi, nabanyamabanki. Kandi nubwo ibi nabyo bifitanye isano nubwato bwabacuruzi, nyamara ntabwo muburyo ubwo aribwo bwose hamwe na baleine. Kuberako usibye uburebure bunini bwurugendo rwa baleine, ingingo nyinshi zihariye zishinzwe gukurikirana uburobyi, kandi ntibishoboka kuzisimbuza ibyambu bya kure ubusanzwe byakunze kugaragara, bigomba kwibukwa, ko mubwato bwose, ubwato bwa baleine aribwo bugaragara cyane ku mpanuka z'ubwoko bwose, cyane cyane kurimbuka no gutakaza ibintu nyine intsinzi y'urugendo rushingiye cyane. Kubwibyo, ubwato bwibikoresho, ibiceri byabigenewe, n'imirongo isigaranye na harponi, hamwe nibikoresho byose, hafi, ariko Kapiteni usanzwe hamwe nubwato bubiri.

Mugihe cyo kugera ku Kirwa, ububiko buremereye bwa Pequod bwari bwarangiye; igizwe n'inka z'inka, umutsima, amazi, lisansi, hamwe n'ibyuma. Ariko, nkuko mbere yabitangaje, mugihe runaka habayeho gukomeza kuzana no gutwara muburyo butandukanye kandi amaherezo yibintu, binini na bito.

Umukuru mu bakoze ibi byo kuzana no gutwara ni mushiki wa Kapiteni Bildad, umukecuru unanutse ufite umutima wiyemeje kandi utarambirwa, ariko ufite umutima mwiza cyane, wasaga nkuwiyemeje ko, aramutse abishoboye, ntakintu nakimwe cyaboneka gikenewe muri Pequod , nyuma yo kugera ku nyanja neza. Igihe kimwe yazaga mu bwato afite ikibindi cy'ibijumba byo mu bubiko bw'igisonga; ikindi gihe hamwe nudusimba twinshi kumeza yumuyobozi mukuru, aho yabikaga igiti cye; a third time with a roll of flannel for the small of some one's rheumatic back. Never did any woman better deserve her name, which was Charity—Aunt Charity, as everybody called her. And like a sister of charity did this charitable Aunt Charity bustle about hither and thither, ready to turn her hand and heart to anything that promised to yield safety, comfort, and consolation to all on board a ship in which her beloved brother Bildad was concerned, and in which she herself owned a score or two of well-saved dollars.

But it was startling to see this excellent hearted Quakeress coming on board, as she did the last day, with a long oil-ladle in one hand, and a still longer whaling lance in the other. Nor was Bildad himself nor Captain Peleg at all backward. As for Bildad, he carried about with him a long list of the articles needed, and at every fresh arrival, down went his mark opposite that article upon the paper. Every once in a while Peleg came hobbling out of his whalebone den, roaring at the men down the hatchways, roaring up to the riggers at the mast-head, and then concluded by roaring back into his wigwam.

During these days of preparation, Queequeg and I often visited the craft, and as often I asked about Captain Ahab, and how he was, and when he was going to come on board his ship. To these questions they would answer, that he was getting better and better, and was expected aboard every day; meantime, the two captains, Peleg and Bildad, could attend to everything necessary to fit the vessel for the voyage. If I had been downright honest with myself, I would have seen very plainly in my heart that I did but half fancy being committed this way to so long a voyage, without once laying my eyes on the man who was to be the absolute dictator of it, so soon as the ship sailed out upon the open sea. But when a man suspects any wrong, it sometimes happens that if he be already involved in the matter, he insensibly strives to cover up his suspicions even from himself. And much this way it was with me. I said nothing, and tried to think nothing.

At last it was given out that some time next day the ship would certainly sail. So next morning, Queequeg and I took a very early start.





CHAPTER 21. Going Aboard.

It was nearly six o'clock, but only grey imperfect misty dawn, when we drew nigh the wharf.

“There are some sailors running ahead there, if I see right,” said I to Queequeg, “it can't be shadows; she's off by sunrise, I guess; come on!”

“Avast!” cried a voice, whose owner at the same time coming close behind us, laid a hand upon both our shoulders, and then insinuating himself between us, stood stooping forward a little, in the uncertain twilight, strangely peering from Queequeg to me. It was Elijah.

“Going aboard?”

“Hands off, will you,” said I.

“Lookee here,” said Queequeg, shaking himself, “go 'way!”

“Ain't going aboard, then?”

“Yes, we are,” said I, “but what business is that of yours? Do you know, Mr. Elijah, that I consider you a little impertinent?”

“No, no, no; I wasn't aware of that,” said Elijah, slowly and wonderingly looking from me to Queequeg, with the most unaccountable glances.

“Elijah,” said I, “you will oblige my friend and me by withdrawing. We are going to the Indian and Pacific Oceans, and would prefer not to be detained.”

“Ye be, be ye? Coming back afore breakfast?”

“He's cracked, Queequeg,” said I, “come on.”

“Holloa!” cried stationary Elijah, hailing us when we had removed a few paces.

“Never mind him,” said I, “Queequeg, come on.”

But he stole up to us again, and suddenly clapping his hand on my shoulder, said—“Did ye see anything looking like men going towards that ship a while ago?”

Struck by this plain matter-of-fact question, I answered, saying, “Yes, I thought I did see four or five men; but it was too dim to be sure.”

“Very dim, very dim,” said Elijah. “Morning to ye.”

Once more we quitted him; but once more he came softly after us; and touching my shoulder again, said, “See if you can find 'em now, will ye?

“Find who?”

“Morning to ye! morning to ye!” he rejoined, again moving off. “Oh! I was going to warn ye against—but never mind, never mind—it's all one, all in the family too;—sharp frost this morning, ain't it? Good-bye to ye. Shan't see ye again very soon, I guess; unless it's before the Grand Jury.” And with these cracked words he finally departed, leaving me, for the moment, in no small wonderment at his frantic impudence.

At last, stepping on board the Pequod, we found everything in profound quiet, not a soul moving. The cabin entrance was locked within; the hatches were all on, and lumbered with coils of rigging. Going forward to the forecastle, we found the slide of the scuttle open. Seeing a light, we went down, and found only an old rigger there, wrapped in a tattered pea-jacket. He was thrown at whole length upon two chests, his face downwards and inclosed in his folded arms. The profoundest slumber slept upon him.

“Those sailors we saw, Queequeg, where can they have gone to?” said I, looking dubiously at the sleeper. But it seemed that, when on the wharf, Queequeg had not at all noticed what I now alluded to; hence I would have thought myself to have been optically deceived in that matter, were it not for Elijah's otherwise inexplicable question. But I beat the thing down; and again marking the sleeper, jocularly hinted to Queequeg that perhaps we had best sit up with the body; telling him to establish himself accordingly. He put his hand upon the sleeper's rear, as though feeling if it was soft enough; and then, without more ado, sat quietly down there.

“Gracious! Queequeg, don't sit there,” said I.

“Oh! perry dood seat,” said Queequeg, “my country way; won't hurt him face.”

“Face!” said I, “call that his face? very benevolent countenance then; but how hard he breathes, he's heaving himself; get off, Queequeg, you are heavy, it's grinding the face of the poor. Get off, Queequeg! Look, he'll twitch you off soon. I wonder he don't wake.”

Queequeg removed himself to just beyond the head of the sleeper, and lighted his tomahawk pipe. I sat at the feet. We kept the pipe passing over the sleeper, from one to the other. Meanwhile, upon questioning him in his broken fashion, Queequeg gave me to understand that, in his land, owing to the absence of settees and sofas of all sorts, the king, chiefs, and great people generally, were in the custom of fattening some of the lower orders for ottomans; and to furnish a house comfortably in that respect, you had only to buy up eight or ten lazy fellows, and lay them round in the piers and alcoves. Besides, it was very convenient on an excursion; much better than those garden-chairs which are convertible into walking-sticks; upon occasion, a chief calling his attendant, and desiring him to make a settee of himself under a spreading tree, perhaps in some damp marshy place.

While narrating these things, every time Queequeg received the tomahawk from me, he flourished the hatchet-side of it over the sleeper's head.

“What's that for, Queequeg?”

“Perry easy, kill-e; oh! perry easy!”

He was going on with some wild reminiscences about his tomahawk-pipe, which, it seemed, had in its two uses both brained his foes and soothed his soul, when we were directly attracted to the sleeping rigger. The strong vapor now completely filling the contracted hole, it began to tell upon him. He breathed with a sort of muffledness; then seemed troubled in the nose; then revolved over once or twice; then sat up and rubbed his eyes.

“Holloa!” he breathed at last, “who be ye smokers?”

“Shipped men,” answered I, “when does she sail?”

“Aye, aye, ye are going in her, be ye? She sails to-day. The Captain came aboard last night.”

“What Captain?—Ahab?”

“Who but him indeed?”

I was going to ask him some further questions concerning Ahab, when we heard a noise on deck.

“Holloa! Starbuck's astir,” said the rigger. “He's a lively chief mate, that; good man, and a pious; but all alive now, I must turn to.” And so saying he went on deck, and we followed.

It was now clear sunrise. Soon the crew came on board in twos and threes; the riggers bestirred themselves; the mates were actively engaged; and several of the shore people were busy in bringing various last things on board. Meanwhile Captain Ahab remained invisibly enshrined within his cabin.
