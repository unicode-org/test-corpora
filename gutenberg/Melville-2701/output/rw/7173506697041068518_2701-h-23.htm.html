Kandi, ariko, hari ukuntu, Ahabu yakoze - muburyo bwe bwite, nkuko buri munsi, buri saha, na buri kanya, abitegeka abayoboye, - Ahabu yasaga nkumutware wigenga; Parsee ariko umugaragu we. Na none kandi byombi byasaga naho bifatanyirijwe hamwe, kandi umunyagitugu utagaragara abirukana; igicucu kijimye kuruhande rwurubavu rukomeye. Kuberako kuba Parsee icyo ashoboye cyose, imbavu zose na keel byari bikomeye Ahabu.

Ku ncuro ya mbere yacitse intege mu museke, ijwi rye ry'icyuma ryumvikanye kuva aft, - “Muntu wa mast-imitwe!” - kandi umunsi wose, kugeza izuba rirenze na nyuma ya nimugoroba, ijwi rimwe buri saha, igihe inzogera y'umuyobozi w'ingoma yumvikanaga - “Ni iki d'ye ubona? —Icyuma!

Ariko iyo iminsi itatu cyangwa ine yatembye, nyuma yo guhura na Rasheli ushaka abana; kandi nta spout yari yabonetse; umusaza wa monomaniac yasaga nkutizera ubudahemuka bwabakozi be; byibuze, muri hafi ya bose usibye inanga ya gipagani; yasaga nkudashidikanya, nubwo, Stubb na Flask bashobora kutirengagiza kubushake yashakaga. Ariko niba koko ibyo gukeka byari ibye, yirinze abigaragaza mu magambo, icyakora ibikorwa bye bisa nkaho bibabangamira.

Ahabu agomba kugira kabiri!” n'amaboko ye bwite yatoboye icyari cy'ibikombe; no kohereza ikiganza hejuru, hamwe n'umutwe umwe wogoshe, kugirango ugere kumutwe-nyamukuru, yakiriye impera zombi z'umugozi wamanutse; no kwomekaho umwe mubiseke bye yateguye pin kurundi ruhande, kugirango abihambire kuri gari ya moshi. Ibi birangiye, afite iherezo nyamara mu ntoki kandi ahagarara iruhande rwa pin, yarebye hirya no hino ku bakozi be, akubura umwe kugeza ku wundi; ahagarika amaso ye igihe kirekire kuri Daggoo, Queequeg, Tashtego; ariko wirinda Fedallah; hanyuma ahanga amaso ye yishimikije uwo bashakanye, ati: - Fata umugozi, nyagasani, ndaguha mu biganza byawe, Starbuck. ” Hanyuma atunganya umuntu we mu gitebo, abaha ijambo ngo bamuzamure ku cyicaro cye, Starbuck niwe wabonye umugozi amaherezo; hanyuma ahagarara hafi yacyo. Ahabu rero, ukuboko kumwe kwiziritse ku bwami bwa cyami, Ahabu yitegereza mu nyanja ibirometero n'ibirometero, - imbere, mu kirere, ku ruhande, kandi, - mu ruziga rwagutse rwategekaga ku burebure cyane.

Iyo mu gukorana n'amaboko ye ahantu hirengeye hafi y’ahantu hitaruye, hashobora kubaho amahirwe yo kutagira ikirenge, umusare mu nyanja yazamuwe kugeza aho hantu, kandi agakomeza ku mugozi; muribi bihe, impera yacyo ifatanye kumurongo ihora itangwa muburyo bukomeye kumuntu umwe ufite isaha yihariye. Kuberako mubutayu nkubu bwo kwiruka, umubano wabo utandukanye ntushobora guhora ubushishozi nibiboneka kuri etage; kandi mugihe igorofa yimigozi yiyi migozi iba buri minota mike ijugunywe kumurongo, byaba ari impfu zisanzwe, niba, bidatanzwe numuzamu uhoraho, umusare yazamuye agomba gutwarwa nubwitonzi bwabakozi bakagwa hasi bakagwa mukinyanja. Ibikorwa bya Ahabu rero muri iki kibazo ntibyari bisanzwe; ikintu kidasanzwe kuri bo wasaga nkaho, kuba Starbuck, hafi yumugabo umwe rukumbi wigeze yiyemeza kumurwanya nikintu icyo aricyo cyose murwego ruto rwegera gufata umwanzuro - umwe muribo, ubudahemuka bwe kumureba yasaga nkudashidikanya; - byari bitangaje, ko uyu ariwe mugabo agomba guhitamo kumurinzi we; kubuntu gutanga ubuzima bwe bwose mumaboko yumuntu utizera.

Ni bwo bwa mbere Ahabu yashizwe hejuru; ere yari amaze iminota icumi; imwe muri izo fagitire zitukura zo mu nyanja-inyoni zikunze kuguruka zidafunze hafi yizengurutse imitwe ya mast-imitwe ya baleine muri ubu burebure; imwe muri izo nyoni yaje izunguruka ivuza induru umutwe mu gihirahiro cyihuta kidasubirwaho. Hanyuma yikaraga metero igihumbi igororotse mu kirere; hanyuma azunguruka epfo, hanyuma yongera gukubita umutwe.

Ariko amaso ye yerekeje kuri horizone yijimye kandi ya kure, Ahabu yasaga nkaho ataranze iyi nyoni yo mu gasozi; ndetse, mubyukuri, ntawundi wabiranga byinshi, ntabwo ari ibintu bidasanzwe; gusa ubu hafi yijisho ryoroheje ryitondewe ryasaga nkubona ubwoko bwamayeri mubisobanuro hafi ya byose.

“Ingofero yawe, ingofero yawe, nyagasani!” mu buryo butunguranye yarize umusare wo muri Sisiliyani, washyizwe ku mutwe wa mizen-mast, ahagarara inyuma ya Ahabu, nubwo yari munsi y'urwego rwe, kandi akayaga gakomeye ko kubagabanya.

Ariko bimaze kuba amababa ya sable yari imbere yumusaza; fagitire ndende yafatiriwe kumutwe: hamwe n'induru, igikona cyirabura cyatwaye igihembo cye.

Inkukuma yazengurutse gatatu mu mutwe wa Tarquin, imukuraho ingofero kugira ngo iyisimbuze, nuko Tanaquil, umugore we, atangaza ko Tarquin azaba umwami wa Roma. Ariko mugusimbuza ingofero gusa ni uko ibimenyetso byari byiza. Ingofero ya Ahabu ntiyigeze isubizwa; inyoni yo mu gasozi yagurukaga hamwe nayo; kure cyane yubuhanga: hanyuma amaherezo arabura; mugihe uhereye aho ibyo byazimiye, umunota umwe wumukara wamenyekanye neza, ugwa muri ubwo burebure bunini ujya mu nyanja.
