UMUTWE WA 43. Hark!

“AMATEKA! Cabaco, wumvise urwo rusaku?”

Byari isaha yo hagati: urumuri rwiza rw'ukwezi; abasare bari bahagaze mu mugozi, bava kuri kamwe mu mazi meza y’amazi mu rukenyerero, kugeza kuri buto-buto hafi ya tafrail. Muri ubu buryo, banyuze mu ndobo kugirango buzuze igituba. Bahagaze, ahanini, ku kibanza cyera cya kimwe cya kane, baritondera kutavuga cyangwa gukandagira ibirenge. Kuva mu ntoki ukuboko, indobo zagiye mu guceceka cyane, gusa zavunaguritse rimwe na rimwe ubwato, hamwe na hum ihoraho ya keel idatera imbere.

Hagati yibi biruhuko, nibwo Archy, umwe mu mugozi, poste ye yari hafi yinyuma, yongorera umuturanyi we, Cholo, amagambo yavuzwe haruguru.

“Amateka! Wumvise urwo rusaku, Cabaco?”

“Fata indobo, uza, Archy? Urusaku d'ye rusobanura iki?”

“Hano harongeye, munsi y'ibyatsi - ntubyumva - inkorora - byumvikanye nk'inkorora.”

“Inkorora iravumwe! Genda unyuze muri iyo ndobo.”

“Hano na none, haraho! —Birasa n'abasinziriye babiri cyangwa batatu bahindutse, ubu!”

"Caramba! Wakoze, mugenzi wawe mu bwato, urabikora? Nibisuguti eshatu zokeje urya kugirango ifunguro rya nimugoroba rihindukire imbere yawe - ntakindi. Reba indobo!"

“Vuga icyo ushaka, mugenzi wawe, mfite amatwi atyaye.”

Ati: "Aye, uri ishapure, si byo, wigeze wumva hum y'urushinge rwa Quakeress rushaje rwo kuboha-ibirometero mirongo itanu ku nyanja uvuye i Nantucket; uri ishapure."

Ati: "Genda, tuzareba uko bizagenda. Hark ye, Cabaco, hari umuntu wamanutse nyuma ya hold-tutaraboneka ku igorofa; kandi ndakeka ko Mogul wa kera na we hari icyo abiziho. Numvise Stubb abwira Flask, isaha imwe yo mu gitondo, ko hari ikintu nk'icyo mu muyaga."

“Tish! Indobo!”





UMUTWE WA 44. Imbonerahamwe.

Uramutse ukurikiye Kapiteni Ahabu kumanuka mu kabari ke nyuma y’igitero cyabaye mu ijoro ryatsindiye iryo shyamba ry’umugambi we hamwe n’abakozi be, wari kumubona yagiye mu kabati muri transom, akazana umuzingo munini wuzuye inkingi z’ibara ry'umuhondo, ukamusakaza imbere ye ku meza ye yuzuye. Noneho yicaye imbere yacyo, wamubonye yiga yitonze yiga imirongo itandukanye nigicucu cyahuye nijisho rye; hamwe na gahoro gahoro ariko ihamye ikurikirana amasomo yinyongera hejuru yimyanya mbere yari irimo ubusa. Rimwe na rimwe, yerekezaga ku birundo by'ibitabo bishaje by'ibiti iruhande rwe, aho byashyizwemo ibihe n'ahantu, mu ngendo zitandukanye zahoze mu mato atandukanye, ifi y'intanga zafashwe cyangwa zabonetse.

Mugihe yakoreshwaga gutya, itara riremereye rya pewter ryahagaritswe muminyururu hejuru yumutwe, ahora atigita hamwe nubwato bwubwato, kandi burigihe yajugunyaga urumuri rwijimye nigicucu cyimirongo kumutwe we wijimye, kugeza ubwo byasaga nkaho mugihe we ubwe yarimo ashyiraho imirongo namasomo kumurongo ushushanyijeho, ikaramu itagaragara nayo yakurikiranaga umurongo n'amasomo kumurongo.

Ariko muri iri joro, ni bwo Ahabu yiherereye mu kazu ke, Ahabu yatekereje ku mbonerahamwe ye. Hafi ya buri joro basohokaga; hafi buri joro ibimenyetso by'ikaramu byasibwe, ibindi birasimburwa. Kuberako igishushanyo mbonera cy'inyanja zose uko ari enye zamubanjirije, Ahabu yarimo atondekanya urujya n'uruza rw'imigezi, hagamijwe kugera ku bikorwa bimwe na bimwe byagezweho n'icyo gitekerezo cya monomaniac ku bugingo bwe.

Noneho, kubantu bose batamenyereye byimazeyo inzira za leviathans, birasa nkigikorwa kidafite ibyiringiro bityo gushaka ikiremwa kimwe rukumbi mu nyanja idafunguye kuri iyi si. Ariko siko byagenze kuri Ahabu, wari uzi imirongo yimigezi yose; bityo kubara ibara ryibiryo byintanga ngabo; kandi, nanone, wibutse ibihe bisanzwe, byamenyekanye kumuhiga byumwihariko; irashobora gushika mubitekerezo bifatika, hafi yo kwegera ibyemezo, bijyanye numunsi wigihe ugomba kuba kuri iki cyangwa aha kugirango ushake umuhigo we.

Mu byukuri rero, ni ukuri kwerekeranye nigihe cyigihe cya baleine yintanga yifashisha amazi yatanzwe, kuburyo abahigi benshi bemeza ko, ashobora gukurikiranirwa hafi no kwigwa kwisi yose; byari ibiti by'urugendo rumwe rw'amato yose ya baleine yakusanyirijwe hamwe yitonze, noneho kwimuka kwa baleine yintanga wasangaga bihuye muburyo budahinduka nubwa herring-shoals cyangwa kuguruka kumira. Kuri iyi ngingo, hageragejwe kubaka igishushanyo mbonera cyimuka cy’intanga ngabo. *

Nubwo, muri ibi bihe, icyerekezo cyafashwe na baleine iyo ari yo yose igororotse nkuburinganire bwabashakashatsi, kandi nubwo umurongo wimbere ugarukira gusa kubyuka byonyine bidashobora kwirindwa, gukanguka kugororotse, nyamara umutsi utabishaka aho muri iki gihe bivugwa ko koga, mubusanzwe urimo ibirometero bike mubugari (byinshi cyangwa bike, kuko imitsi ifatwa ko yagutse cyangwa igasezerana); ariko ntizigera irenza ibintu byose biva mu bwato-bwato-bwato, iyo butembera neza muri kariya gace. Igiteranyo ni, ko mugihe cyihariye muri ubwo bugari no muri iyo nzira, balale yimuka irashobora gushakishwa cyane.

Ni yo mpamvu atari mu bihe bifatika gusa, ahantu hazwi ho kugaburira, Ahabu yashoboraga guhura n'umuhigo we; ariko mu kwambuka amazi yagutse hagati yizo mpamvu yashoboraga, akoresheje ibihangano bye, umwanya rero nigihe cye munzira ye, nkubwo icyo gihe atagomba kuba rwose adategereje inama.

Hariho ibihe byabanje kubona bisa nkaho bihuza gahunda ye yubushake ariko iracyari uburyo. Ariko sibyo mubyukuri, birashoboka. Nubwo intanga ngore ya gregarious ifite ibihe byayo bisanzwe kubwimpamvu zihariye, nyamara muri rusange ntushobora kwemeza ko amashyo yahigaga gutya nubunini nkubu cyangwa uburebure bwuyu mwaka, tuvuge ko bizavamo kimwe kimwe nababonetse hari igihe cyabanjirije iki; nubwo hariho ibihe byihariye kandi bidashidikanywaho aho ibinyuranye nibi byagaragaye ko ari ukuri. Muri rusange, ijambo rimwe, gusa mugihe gito cyane, rireba solitaries na hermits hagati yintangangabo zikuze, zishaje. Rero ko nubwo Moby Dick yari yarabonye mu mwaka ushize, urugero, ku cyiswe ubutaka bwa Seychelle mu nyanja y’Ubuhinde, cyangwa Ikirunga cy’ibirunga ku nkombe y’Ubuyapani; nyamara ntibyakurikiye, iyo niyo Pequod gusura imwe muri iyo myanya mugihe cyakurikiyeho, yari kumusanganira byanze bikunze. Noneho rero, hamwe nizindi mpamvu zo kugaburira, aho yagiye rimwe na rimwe yigaragaza. Ariko ibyo byose byasaga nkaho yahagararaga bisanzwe hamwe n’inyanja-nyanja, mu buryo bw'ikigereranyo, ntabwo ari aho yari amaze igihe kirekire. Kandi aho amahirwe ya Ahabu yo gusohoza umugambi we kugeza ubu yavuzwe, kwerekeza gusa kumpande zose, izabanjirije, ibyiringiro byinyongera byari ibye, mbere yigihe cyagenwe cyangwa ahantu runaka byagerwaho, mugihe ibishoboka byose byahinduka ibishoboka, kandi nkuko Ahabu yabitekerezaga cyane, ibishoboka byose ikintu gikurikiraho kukintu runaka. Icyo gihe cyagenwe cyagenwe cyahujwe ninteruro imwe ya tekiniki - Igihe-ku-murongo. Aho ngaho hanyuma, mumyaka itari mike ikurikiranye, Moby Dick yamanukaga rimwe na rimwe, akatinda muri ayo mazi igihe gito, nkizuba, mugihe cyumwaka waryo, abasahuzi intera iteganijwe mubimenyetso byose bya Zodiac. Aho niho kandi, ibyinshi mu byica byica na baleine yera byari byarabaye; ngaho imiraba yari yuzuye ibikorwa bye; hari kandi aho hantu hateye agahinda aho umusaza wa monomaniac yari yabonye impamvu iteye ubwoba yo kwihorera. Ariko mu bwitonzi bwuzuye no kuba maso atajegajega Ahabu yajugunye roho ye yororoka muri ubwo buhigi butajegajega, ntabwo yanze ko ashingira ibyiringiro bye byose ku kintu cyambitswe ikamba hejuru, nubwo cyaba gishimishije kuri ibyo byiringiro; eka mbere no kudasinzira indahiro yiwe ntiyashobora gutuza umutima wiwe utuje ku buryo wasubika ubushakashatsi bwose bwatabaye.

Noneho, Pequod yari yafashe ubwato i Nantucket mu ntangiriro yigihembwe-ku-murongo. Nta gikorwa na kimwe gishoboka noneho gishobora gutuma umuyobozi we akora inzira nini yerekeza mu majyepfo, inshuro ebyiri Cape Horn, hanyuma akiruka kuri dogere mirongo itandatu z'ubugari akagera muri pasifika ya ekwatoriya mugihe cyo gutembera aho. Kubwibyo, agomba gutegereza igihe gikurikira. Nyamara isaha itaragera yubwato bwa Pequod yari yaratoranijwe neza na Ahabu, hagamijwe kureba ibintu neza. Kuberako, intera yiminsi magana atatu na mirongo itandatu nagatanu ninjoro yari imbere ye; intera, aho kwihanganira kwihangana ku nkombe, yakoresha mu guhiga bitandukanye; niba kubwamahirwe White Whale, kumara ikiruhuko mu nyanja kure y’ahantu ho kugaburira buri gihe, yagombye guhindura umusatsi we wijimye mu kigobe cy’Ubuperesi, cyangwa mu kirwa cya Bengal, cyangwa mu nyanja y’Ubushinwa, cyangwa mu yandi mazi ayo ari yo yose ahigwa n’ubwoko bwe. Ko rero Monsoons, Pampas, Nor'-Westers, Harmattans, Ubucuruzi; umuyaga uwo ariwo wose ariko Levanter na Simoon, birashobora guhuha Moby Dick mumagambo ya zig-zag yisi-izenguruka ya Pequod izenguruka.

Ariko gutanga ibyo byose; nyamara, urebye mubushishozi kandi ukonje, bisa nkaho atari igitekerezo cyasaze, iki; ko mu nyanja yagutse itagira umupaka, igifi kimwe cyonyine, kabone niyo cyaba gihuye nacyo, cyakagombye gutekerezwa ko gishobora kumenyekana ku muntu wamuhigaga, ndetse na Mufti ufite ubwanwa bwera mu nzira nyabagendwa ya Constantinople? Yego. Kubireba umwihariko wa shelegi-yera ya Moby Dick, hamwe na shelegi yera yera, ntibyashobokaga ariko bidashidikanywaho. Kandi sinigeze mbyimba igifi kinini, Ahabu yaritotombeye, nk'igihe amaze kwitegereza ibicapo bye kugeza nyuma ya saa sita z'ijoro yisubije inyuma mu cyubahiro - aramwambura, aratoroka? Amababa ye yagutse ararambiwe, kandi arazunguruka nk'amatwi yatakaye! Kandi hano, ubwenge bwe bwasaze bwakwiruka mumarushanwa adahumeka; kugeza umunaniro no gucika intege byo gutekereza kuri we; no mu kirere cyuguruye yashakaga kugarura imbaraga. Mana! ni ubuhe buryo bwo kubabazwa uwo mugabo yihanganira utwarwa nicyifuzo kimwe cyo kwihorera kitagerwaho. Asinzira afite amaboko afunze; akanguka afite imisumari yamaraso ye mumaboko.

Akenshi, iyo yirukanwe ku nyundo ye ananiwe kandi adashobora kwihanganira inzozi z'ijoro, ibyo, bikongera ibitekerezo bye bwite ku manywa, bikabitwara mu gihe cyo guterana amagambo kwa frenesi, hanyuma akazunguruka hirya no hino mu bwonko bwe bwaka, kugeza ubwo gutitira cyane aho yari ari ubuzima bwe byabaye umubabaro udasanzwe; kandi igihe, nkuko rimwe na rimwe byagenze, iyi mitsi yo mu mwuka muri we yatumaga ahagarara hejuru yacyo, maze umwobo usa nkuwugururiwe muri we, aho inkongi y'umuriro n'umurabyo byarashwe, kandi umuvumo wavumwe amwinginga ngo asimbukire muri bo; iyo ikuzimu muri we yikubise munsi ye, humvikana urusaku rwo mu gasozi; Ahabu n'amaso yijimye, yaturukaga mu cyumba cye cya Leta, nk'aho yahunze igitanda cyari gifite umuriro. Nyamara ibi, ahari, aho kuba ibimenyetso simusiga byintege nke zihishe, cyangwa ubwoba kubwicyemezo cye bwite, byari ibimenyetso byerekana ubukana bwacyo. Kuberako, mubihe nkibi, umusazi Ahabu, umugambi, umuhigi ushikamye uhiga baleine yera; uyu Ahabu wari wagiye ku nyundo ye, ntabwo yari umukozi wamuteye kongera guturika avuye mu bwoba. Iyanyuma yari ihame rihoraho, rizima cyangwa ubugingo muri we; no mu bitotsi, kubera ko igihe cyatandukanijwe n'ubwenge buranga, ikindi gihe kikaba cyarakoreshaga ku modoka yacyo yo hanze cyangwa umukozi wacyo, cyahise gishaka guhunga ibintu byacitse ku kintu giteye ubwoba, muri byo, icyo gihe, ntibyari bikiri ngombwa. Ariko nkuko ubwenge butabaho keretse iyo bushingiye ku bugingo, bigomba rero kuba byarabaye, kubwa Ahabu, gutanga ibitekerezo bye n'ibitekerezo bye byose kumugambi we w'ikirenga; iyo ntego, kubwubushake bwayo bwite, yihatiye kurwanya imana na shitani muburyo bwo kwibwira, kwigenga kwonyine. Oya, yashoboraga kubaho nabi no gutwikwa, mugihe ubuzima busanzwe bwari bufatanije, bwahunze ubwoba bwatewe no kuvuka kutabujijwe kandi kutavutse. Kubwibyo, umwuka wababajwe wamaso mumaso yumubiri, mugihe icyasaga na Ahabu yihutiye kuva mucyumba cye, cyari icyo gihe ariko ikintu cyarimo ubusa, ikiremwa kidafite somnambuliste, urumuri rwumucyo muzima, kugirango ubyemeze, ariko udafite ikintu gifite ibara, bityo rero ubwacyo ni ubusa. Imana igufashe, musaza, ibitekerezo byawe byaremye ikiremwa muriwe; kandi ufite ibitekerezo bikomeye bityo akamugira Prometheus; inkongoro igaburira uwo mutima ubuziraherezo; icyo gisimba ikiremwa ubwacyo arema.





UMUTWE WA 45.

Kugeza ubu uko hashobora kuba inkuru zivuga muri iki gitabo; kandi, mubyukuri, nko gukoraho mu buryo butaziguye ikintu kimwe cyangwa bibiri cyane bishimishije kandi byamatsiko mumico ya baleine yintanga, igice kimaze kuvugwa, mugice cyacyo cyambere, ningirakamaro kimwe nkuko tuzabisanga muri iki gitabo; ariko ikibazo cyambere cyacyo gisaba gukomeza kwaguka no kurushaho kwaguka mumiryango, kugirango byumvikane bihagije, kandi byongeye kandi gukuraho ikintu icyo ari cyo cyose kitemewe, ubujiji bwimbitse bwibintu byose bushobora gutera mubitekerezo bimwe na bimwe, kubijyanye nukuri kwukuri kwingingo zingenzi ziki kibazo.

Nita ku kudakora iki gice cyinshingano zanjye muburyo; ariko azanyurwa no gutanga ibitekerezo byifuzwa ukoresheje imirongo itandukanye yibintu, mubikorwa cyangwa bizwi neza nkanjye nka whaleman; kandi mvuye muri aya magambo, ndabifata - umwanzuro ugamije uza gukurikiza ubwayo.

Icya mbere: Njyewe ubwanjye nzi ingero eshatu aho igifi kinini, nyuma yo kwakira inanga, cyarokotse burundu; kandi, nyuma yigihe gito (murugero rumwe rwimyaka itatu), yongeye gukubitwa ukuboko kumwe, aricwa; mugihe ibyuma byombi, byombi byaranzwe na cypher imwe yihariye, byakuwe mumubiri. Murugero aho imyaka itatu yatabaye hagati yo kuvuza inanga zombi; kandi ndatekereza ko hashobora kuba harikintu kirenze ibyo; umugabo wabashushanyijeho bibaho, mugihe gito, kugirango ajye mubwato bwubucuruzi mu rugendo yerekeza muri Afrika, yagiye ku nkombe aho, yinjira mu birori byo kuvumbura, maze yinjira kure cyane mu gihugu, aho yagenze mu gihe cyimyaka hafi ibiri, akenshi yabangamiwe ninzoka, abanyamahane, ingwe, miyasi yuburozi, hamwe nibindi byago byose bikunze kubaho kugirango azerera mu mutima w'uturere tutazwi. Hagati aho, igifi kinini yakubise kigomba no kuba mu rugendo rwacyo; nta gushidikanya ko yazengurutse isi inshuro eshatu, ikoza impande zayo ku nkombe zose za Afurika; ariko nta ntego. Uyu mugabo niyi baline bongeye guhurira hamwe, umwe aratsinda undi. Ndavuga nti, njye ubwanjye, nzi ingero eshatu zisa n'izi; ibyo biri muri bibiri nabonye inyanja yakubiswe; hanyuma, ku gitero cya kabiri, abona ibyuma bibiri byanditseho ibimenyetso byabigenewe, nyuma bivanwa mu mafi yapfuye. Murugero rwimyaka itatu, byaje kugwa kuburyo nari mubwato inshuro zombi, ubwa mbere nubwa nyuma, kandi ubushize namenye mu buryo butandukanye ubwoko bwihariye bwa mole nini munsi yijisho rya baleine, nari narabibonyeyo imyaka itatu ishize. Ndavuga imyaka itatu, ariko nzi neza ko byari birenze ibyo. Dore ingero eshatu, noneho, njye ubwanjye nzi ukuri kwa; ariko numvise izindi ngero nyinshi kubantu bafite ukuri kubibazo nta mpamvu nziza yo gushinja.

Icya kabiri: Birazwi cyane mu burobyi bwa Sperm Whale, nubwo isi yaba itabizi ku nkombe z’isi, ko habaye amateka menshi atazibagirana aho igifi kinini cyo mu nyanja cyabaye mu bihe bya kure ndetse n’ahantu hazwi hose. Impamvu igifi nk'iki cyamenyekanye bityo nticyari cyuzuye kandi mubyukuri bitewe numwihariko wumubiri we nkuko bitandukanye nizindi nyanja; kuko nubwo ari umwihariko muri urwo rwego amahirwe yose ya baleine yaba afite, bahise bahagarika umwihariko we bamwica, bakamutekesha mumavuta yihariye. Oya: icyabiteye nuko: ko mubyabaye byica uburobyi hamanikwa icyubahiro giteye ubwoba cyikibi nkiki nkuko byagenze kuri Rinaldo Rinaldini, kuburyo abarobyi benshi banyuzwe no kumumenya bakoraho gusa kuri tarpaine zabo mugihe yari gusanga arikumwe nabo mukiyaga, badashaka guhinga kurushaho. Kimwe nabadayimoni bamwe bakennye ku nkombe bibaho kugirango bamenye umuntu ukomeye udashyira mu gaciro, bamusuhuza kumuhanda mumuhanda, kugira ngo nibakomeza gukurikirana uwo baziranye, ntibashobora kubona incamake yibitekerezo byabo.

Ariko ntabwo buri kimwe muri ibyo bine byamamaye cyishimiye ibyamamare byihariye-Oya, urashobora kubyita icyamamare mu nyanja; ntabwo yari azwi cyane mubuzima none akaba adapfa mu nkuru ziteganijwe nyuma y'urupfu, ariko yemerewe uburenganzira bwose, uburenganzira, no gutandukanya izina; yari afite izina nkukuri nka Cambyses cyangwa Cæsar. Ntabwo aribyo, yewe Timorori Tom! wamamaye leviathan, ufite inkovu nka ice ice, ninde umaze igihe kinini atihishe mu bice byo mu burasirazuba bw'iryo zina, spout ye ntiyakunze kugaragara ku mucanga wa Ombay? Ntabwo byari bimeze, yewe Nouvelle-Zélande Jack! wowe ubwoba bwubwato bwose bwambutse ibyuka hafi yigihugu cya Tattoo? Ntabwo byari bimeze, yewe Morquan! Mwami wUbuyapani, ninde ndege ndende bavuga ko rimwe na rimwe yafataga umusaraba wera wera urubura hejuru yijuru? Ntabwo byari bimeze, yewe Don Miguel! wowe baleine ya Chilian, iranga nk'inyenzi ishaje ifite hieroglyphics mystic inyuma! Muri prose isanzwe, hano hari balale enye izwi nabanyeshuri bo mumateka ya Cetacean nka Marius cyangwa Sylla kubumenyi bwa kera.

Ariko ibi sibyose. Nouvelle-Zélande Tom na Don Miguel, nyuma y’ibihe bitandukanye byateje akaduruvayo gakomeye mu bwato bw’amato atandukanye, amaherezo bagiye mu gushaka, guhiga gahunda, guhiga no kwicwa na ba capitaine b'intwari b'inyamanswa, bateraga inanga zabo hamwe n'icyo kintu cyerekanwe cyane, nko mu guca mu bwicanyi bwa Narragansett Woods, Kapiteni Butler wa kera yari afite mu bwicanyi bwo mu bwicanyi bwitwa Anna, Filipo.

Sinzi aho nshobora kubona ahantu heza kuruta hano gusa, kugira ngo mvuge ku kintu kimwe cyangwa bibiri, kuri njye mbona ari ngombwa, nko mu buryo bwanditse bushyiraho mu buryo bwose gushyira mu gaciro inkuru yose y’igiti cyera, cyane cyane ibiza. Jye na Queequeg twakoraga mu buryo bworoheje kuboha icyo bita materi, kugira ngo twongere dukubite ubwato. Biracyaza rero kandi biraceceka nyamara hari ukuntu kubanziriza byari ibintu byose byabayeho, kandi gutondeka reverie byihishe mu kirere, kuburyo buri musare wicecekeye yasaga nkuwiyemeje muburyo bwe butagaragara.

Nari umusuku cyangwa page ya Queequeg, mugihe mpuze cyane kuri matel. Nkomeje kunyura no gusubiramo ibyuzuye cyangwa ubudodo bwa marline hagati yintambara ndende yintambara, nkoresheje ukuboko kwanjye kuri shitingi, kandi nka Queequeg, uhagaze iruhande, burigihe na anon yambuye inkota ye iremereye hagati yudodo, kandi nitegereje amaso hejuru y'amazi, uburangare kandi utabitekereje utwara urugo rwuzuye hejuru yubwato bwose bwarakozwe hejuru yubwato bwakorewe hejuru yubwato bwose. y'inkota, ko byasaga nkaho aribwo buryo bwigihe, kandi nanjye ubwanjye nari shitingi yo kuboha imashini no kuboha kuri Fates. Hano harashyizweho imigozi ihamye yintambara ikurikiza ariko imwe imwe, ihora igaruka, kunyeganyega kudahinduka, kandi uku kunyeganyega gusa bihagije kugirango twemere guhuza inzira zinyuranye nizindi. Iyi ntambara yasaga nkibikenewe; kandi hano, natekereje ko, hamwe n'ukuboko kwanjye nashizeho ubwato bwanjye bwite hanyuma nkaboha ibyanjye muri izi nsanganyamatsiko zidahinduka. Hagati aho, inkota ya Queequeg itabishaka, ititaye kubantu, rimwe na rimwe ikubita ubwoya buhoro, cyangwa bugoramye, cyangwa bikomeye, cyangwa intege nke, nkuko bigenda; kandi niri tandukaniro mugusoza gusohora bitanga itandukaniro rihuye mubice byanyuma byimyenda yuzuye; iyi nkota yubunyamanswa, natekereje I, amaherezo irashushanya kandi ikambara imyenda yombi nubudodo; iyi nkota yoroshye, ititaye kubantu igomba kuba amahirwe-aye, amahirwe, umudendezo wo kwihitiramo ibibanogeye, nibikenewe-ubungubu ntibishobora kubangikana-byose bikorera hamwe. Intambara igororotse ikenewe, ntigomba kuyoberwa inzira yanyuma - ihindagurika ryayo risimburana, mubyukuri, bikunda gusa; ubuntu buracyafite umudendezo wo guhuza ingendo hagati yinsanganyamatsiko yatanzwe; n'amahirwe, nubwo yabujijwe gukina kwayo mumurongo iboneye wibikenewe, no kuruhande rwicyerekezo cyayo iyobowe nubushake bwo kwihitiramo ibibanogeye, nubwo rero byateganijwe na bombi, amahirwe yo guhinduranya amategeko haba, kandi afite iheruka ryerekana ibintu byabaye.

Nguko uko twarimo kuboha no kuboha ubwo natangiraga ku majwi adasanzwe, ashushanyije, kandi yumuziki wo mu gasozi kandi utagaragara, ku buryo umupira w'ubuntu uzamanuka mu kuboko, maze mpagarara nitegereje ibicu aho iryo jwi ryamanutse nk'ibaba. Ahantu hirengeye mu biti byambukiranya ibiti byari umusazi Gay-Header, Tashtego. Umubiri we wagendaga ushishikara imbere, ukuboko kwe kurambuye nk'urubingo, kandi mu kanya gato akomeza gutaka. Kugira ngo tumenye neza ko ijwi rimwe ari ako kanya wenda kumvikana hirya no hino ku nyanja, uhereye ku magana ya baleine bareba hejuru cyane mu kirere; ariko muri bike muribyo bihaha byashobokaga kurira kumenyera bishaje byakuye cadence nziza cyane nka Tashtego wumuhinde.

Kuri ubwo bukorikori budasanzwe rimwe na rimwe bwagiye bugaragazwa na Sperm Whale iyo, yumvikanye n'umutwe we mu cyerekezo kimwe, nyamara, nubwo yihishe munsi yubutaka, urusyo ruzunguruka, kandi ako kanya akoga mu gihembwe gitandukanye - ubu buriganya bwe ntibwashoboraga kuba mubikorwa; kuberako ntampamvu yo gutekereza ko amafi yabonwaga na Tashtego yari afite ubwoba muburyo ubwo aribwo bwose, cyangwa mubyukuri yari azi hafi yacu. Umwe mu bagabo batoranijwe mu batwara ubwato - ni ukuvuga abadashyizweho mu bwato, icyo gihe yorohereje Umuhinde ku mutwe wa mast. Abasare bari imbere na mizzen bari bamanutse; umurongo wumurongo washyizwe mumwanya wabo; crane zajugunywe hanze; mainyard yari ishyigikiwe, kandi amato atatu yazungurutse hejuru yinyanja nkibiseke bitatu bya samphire hejuru yimisozi miremire.
