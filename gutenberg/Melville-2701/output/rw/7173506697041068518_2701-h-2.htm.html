UMUTWE WA 4. Counterpane.

Nkibyuka bukeye bwaho nko ku manywa, nasanze ukuboko kwa Queequeg kuntera hejuru muburyo bwuje urukundo kandi bwuje urukundo. Wari warigeze gutekereza ko nabaye umugore we. Counterpane yari yububiko, yuzuye udusimba duto duto duto duto twinshi na mpandeshatu; kandi uku kuboko kwe kwishushanyijeho hirya no hino hamwe na labyrint ya Cretan ihuza ishusho, nta bice bibiri byayo byari bifite igicucu kimwe - bitewe nuko ndakeka ko kuba yarakomeje ukuboko kwe mu nyanja mu buryo butemewe n'izuba no mu gicucu, amaboko ye y'ishati yazungurutswe bidasanzwe mu bihe bitandukanye - uku kuboko kwe kumwe, ndavuga, yashakishaga isi yose nk'igitambaro kimwe. Mubyukuri, igice kimwe kuryamyeho nkuko ukuboko kwabikoze nkibyuka bwa mbere, sinashoboraga kubivuga kuva mugitanda, nuko bahuza indabyo zabo; kandi numvise uburemere nigitutu gusa nashoboraga kubwira ko Queequeg yampobeye.

Ibyiyumvo byanjye byari bidasanzwe. Reka ngerageze kubisobanura. Nkiri umwana, nibuka neza ibintu bisa nkanjye byambayeho; yaba impamo cyangwa inzozi, ntabwo nigeze nshobora gutuza rwose. Ibintu byari bimeze. Nari narimo gutema caper cyangwa izindi - Ndatekereza ko yagerageje kunyerera hejuru ya chimney, nkuko nabonye siporo nkeya ikora iminsi mike ishize; na mama wa mama wanjye, mu buryo runaka cyangwa ubundi, buri gihe yankubitaga, cyangwa akanyohereza kuryama ku mugoroba, - mama yankuruje amaguru muri chimney anshyira kuryama, nubwo hari saa mbiri gusa nyuma ya saa sita z'amanywa yo ku ya 21 Kamena, umunsi muremure mu mwaka mu isi yacu. Numvise mfite ubwoba. Ariko nta mfashanyo yabyo, nuko hejuru yintambwe njya mucyumba cyanjye gito mu igorofa rya gatatu, niyambura buhoro buhoro kugira ngo nice igihe, maze ndumirwa cyane mbona hagati y'amabati.

Naryamyeyo mbara nabi ko amasaha cumi nagatandatu yose agomba kurangira mbere yuko nizera ko nzuka. Amasaha cumi n'atandatu mu buriri! umuto wumugongo wanjye urababara kubitekereza. Kandi byari byoroshye cyane; izuba rimurika mu idirishya, hamwe no gutontoma gukomeye kw'abatoza mu mihanda, n'ijwi ry'amajwi y'abahuje ibitsina hirya no hino mu rugo. Numvise meze nabi cyane - amaherezo narahagurutse, nambara, kandi njya hasi buhoro buhoro mu birenge byanjye, nshakisha mama wanjye, maze mpita njugunya ku birenge bye, ndamwinginga ngo ampe kunyerera neza kubera imyitwarire yanjye mibi; ikintu cyose rwose ariko kuncira urubanza kubeshya abed igihe kirekire kidashoboka. Ariko yari mwiza kandi witonda cyane mubabyeyi ba mama, kandi inyuma byabaye ngombwa ko njya mucyumba cyanjye. Mumasaha menshi naryamye ndakangutse, numva ibintu bibi cyane kuruta uko nigeze kubikora kuva, ndetse no mubyago bikomeye byakurikiyeho. Ubwanyuma ngomba kuba naguye mubitotsi bibi bya doze; maze kubyuka buhoro buhoro - kimwe cya kabiri cyuzuye mu nzozi - nakinguye amaso, icyumba mbere cyaka izuba cyari cyuzuye umwijima w'inyuma. Ako kanya numvise ihungabana rinyuze kumurongo wanjye wose; nta kintu na kimwe cyagombaga kugaragara, kandi nta kintu na kimwe cyagombaga kumvikana; ariko ikiganza ndengakamere cyasaga nkicyashyizwe mubyanjye. Ukuboko kwanjye kumanitse kuri kaburimbo, kandi izina ritagira izina, ridashobora gutekerezwa, ryicecekeye cyangwa fantom, ukuboko kwarimo, byasaga naho bicaye hafi yigitanda cyanjye. Kubintu byasaga naho imyaka yarundanyije imyaka, naryamyeyo, nkonje ubwoba bwinshi buteye ubwoba, sinatinyuka gukuramo ikiganza; nyamara burigihe utekereza ko niba mbishoboye ariko nkabyutsa santimetero imwe, amarozi ateye ubwoba yari gucika. Ntabwo nari nzi uburyo iyi myumvire amaherezo yaranyobeye; ariko mbyutse mugitondo, mpinda umushyitsi nibuka byose, maze iminsi, ibyumweru n'amezi nyuma yaho nabuze uko ngerageza kugerageza gusobanura ibanga. Oya, kugeza kuriyi saha nyine, akenshi ndumiwe.

Noneho, kura ubwoba buteye ubwoba, kandi ibyiyumvo byanjye byo kumva ukuboko ndengakamere kwanjye byari bisa cyane, mubitangaje, nabiboneye kubyuka nkabona ukuboko kwa gipagani kwa Queequeg kuntera impande zose. Ariko muburebure ibyabaye mwijoro ryakeye byagarutsweho cyane, umwe umwe, mubyukuri, hanyuma ndaryama nzima gusa mubibazo bisekeje. Kuberako nagerageje kunyeganyeza ukuboko - gukingura umukwe umukwe - nyamara, asinziriye uko yari ameze, aracyampobera cyane, nkaho nta kindi uretse urupfu rukwiye kudutandukanya. Ubu nihatiye kumukangura - “Queequeg!” - ariko igisubizo cye cyonyine cyari igituba. Nahise nzunguruka, ijosi ryanjye numva ari nk'ifarashi; hanyuma ahita yumva akantu gato. Kujugunya kuruhande, haryamye tomahawk aryamye kuruhande rwumunyamahane, nkaho ari uruhinja rufite isura. Umutobe mwiza, mubyukuri, natekereje njye; abed hano munzu idasanzwe kumunsi mugari, hamwe numuntu urya umuntu na tomahawk! “Queequeg! —Mu izina ry'ibyiza, Queequeg, kanguka!” Mu burebure, nkoresheje amagambo menshi yo guterana amagambo, hamwe n'amajwi aranguruye kandi adahwema kwerekana ku buryo butagaragara bwo guhobera umugabo mugenzi we muri ubwo buryo bwo gushyingiranwa, nashoboye gukuramo induru; kandi ubungubu, asubiza inyuma ukuboko, yinyeganyeza hirya no hino nk'imbwa ya Newfoundland avuye mu mazi, maze yicara mu buriri, akomeye nk'abakozi ba pike, arandeba, kandi amukubita amaso nkaho atibuka na gato uko naje kuhaba, nubwo ubwenge buke bwo kumenya ikintu kuri njye bwasaga nkumuseke buhoro buhoro. Hagati aho, naryamye ncecetse ndamureba, nta kibazo gikomeye mfite ubu, maze nunama nitegereza gato ikiremwa gifite amatsiko. Igihe, amaherezo, ibitekerezo bye byasaga nkibigize gukora ku miterere ya mugenzi we baryamanye, maze ahinduka, nkaho, yiyunze nukuri; yahise asimbukira hasi, kandi ku bimenyetso n'amajwi amwe yampaye kumva ko, niba binshimishije, yabanza kwambara hanyuma akansiga nambaye nyuma, asiga inzu yose. Tekereza I, Queequeg, mubihe, iyi ni impinduramatwara cyane; ariko, ukuri nukuri, aba banyarugomo bafite imyumvire ivuka yo kuryoherwa, vuga icyo ushaka; biratangaje uburyo mubyukuri bafite ikinyabupfura. Ndashimira byumwihariko Queequeg, kuko yangaragarije ubupfura bwinshi no kubitekerezaho, mugihe nari nagize ikinyabupfura gikomeye; kumwitegereza avuye ku buriri, akareba uko umusarani we wose; kumwanya amatsiko yanjye arushaho kuba mwiza mubworozi bwanjye. Nubwo bimeze bityo, umugabo nka Queequeg utabona burimunsi, we n'inzira ze byari bikwiye rwose bidasanzwe kubijyanye.

Yatangiye kwambara hejuru yambara ingofero ye yinzuki, ndende cyane, hafi ya, hanyuma - aracyagabanya umutego we - ahiga inkweto. Niki munsi y'ijuru yabikoreye, sinshobora kubivuga, ariko urugendo rwe rwakurikiyeho kwari ukwikubita hasi - inkweto mu ntoki, n'ingofero - munsi yigitanda; mugihe, uhereye kumatiku yubugizi bwa nabi bukabije no kunanirwa, nasanze yari umunyamwete mukazi ko kwikorera wenyine; nubwo nta tegeko ryigenga nigeze numva, hari umuntu usabwa kwiherera mugihe yambaye inkweto. Ariko Queequeg, urabona, yari ikiremwa mugihe cyinzibacyuho - yaba inyenzi cyangwa ikinyugunyugu. Yari afite umuco uhagije kugirango yerekane ubwiyandarike bwe muburyo budasanzwe bushoboka. Amashuri ye yari atararangira. Yari umunyeshuri wa kaminuza. Niba atarigeze agira umuco muto, birashoboka cyane ko ataba yarigeze yikanga na gato inkweto; ariko rero, iyo ataba akiri umunyamahane, ntabwo yigeze arota kwinjira munsi yigitanda ngo abambare. Amaherezo, yagaragaye afite ingofero ye yunamye cyane kandi yunamye hejuru y'amaso, atangira kunyeganyega no gucumbagira hafi y'icyumba, nkaho, atamenyereye cyane inkweto, inkweto ze zijimye, zinyeganyeza inka - birashoboka ko zitigeze zitumizwa - ahubwo zaramukubise kandi zimubabaza mugitangira cya mbere cy'ubukonje bukabije.

Kubona, ubu, ko nta mwenda uhari ku idirishya, kandi ko umuhanda ari muto cyane, inzu ihanganye yategetse kureba mu cyumba cyeruye, kandi yitegereza cyane cyane ishusho iteye isoni Queequeg yakoze, yiziritse ku bindi bike uretse ingofero ye n'inkweto; Namusabye uko nshoboye, kwihutisha umusarani mu buryo runaka, cyane cyane kwinjira mu ipantaro ye vuba bishoboka. Yarabyubahirije, hanyuma atangira kwiyuhagira. Icyo gihe mugitondo umukristo wese yaba yarogeje mumaso; ariko Queequeg, natangaye, anyuzwe no kugabanya kwiyuhagira mu gituza, amaboko, n'amaboko. Yahise yambara ikoti rye, afata isabune ikomeye ku meza yo gukaraba, ayijugunya mu mazi atangira kumupfuka mu maso. Narebaga kugira ngo ndebe aho yabitse urwembe, igihe dore, akuramo inanga mu mfuruka yigitanda, asohora ibiti birebire bikozwe mu giti, atiyogoshesha umutwe, abizunguza gato kuri boot, maze agenda yerekeza ku ndorerwamo gato ku rukuta, atangira gusakara cyane, cyangwa se kuvuza imisaya. Tekereza I, Queequeg, iyi ikoresha ibikoresho byiza bya Rogers hamwe no kwihorera. Nyuma yaho, nibajije bike kuriyi mikorere ubwo naje kumenya icyuma cyiza umutwe wa inanga ikozwe, nuburyo bukaze cyane impande ndende zigororotse zihora zibitswe.

Ubwiherero bwe busigaye bwaragezweho bidatinze, maze yishimira gusohoka mu cyumba, yizingira mu ikoti rye rinini ry'indege, kandi akinisha inanga ye nk'ikibando cya marshal.





UMUTWE WA 5. Ifunguro rya mu gitondo.

Nahise nkurikira iyo nzira, maze manuka mu cyumba cyo mu kabari nandika nyir'inzu aseka cyane. Ntabwo nigeze nkunda kumugirira nabi, nubwo yari yaranyiboneye hamwe na gato mu kibazo cya mugenzi wanjye dusangiye.

Ariko, guseka neza nikintu cyiza gikomeye, kandi ni gake cyane ikintu cyiza; byinshi birababaje. Noneho, niba umuntu umwe, muburyo bwe bwite, agura ibintu byurwenya rwiza kumuntu uwo ari we wese, ntasubire inyuma, ahubwo yemere yishimye gukoresha kandi akoreshe muri ubwo buryo. Kandi umugabo ufite ikintu cyose gisekeje kuri we, menya neza ko muri uriya mugabo hari byinshi birenze ibyo utekereza.

Icyumba cy'akabari cyari cyuzuye abicumbikiraga bari baraye mu ijoro ryakeye, kandi uwo nari ntarabona neza. Bose bari hafi ya baleine; abo bashakanye bakuru, hamwe nabandi bashakanye, hamwe nabagatatu, hamwe nababaji bo mu nyanja, hamwe nabafatanyabikorwa bo mu nyanja, hamwe n'abacuzi bo mu nyanja, n'abacuranga inanga, n'abashinzwe ubwato; isosiyete yijimye kandi yikaraga, ifite ubwanwa bwa bosky; ishati idashwanyaguje, shaggy, bose bambaye amakoti yinguge kumyenda ya mugitondo.

Urashobora kuvuga neza igihe buri umwe yari amaze ku nkombe. Uyu musore ukiri muto umusaya muzima ni nkamapera yizuba ryizuba, kandi byasa nkimpumuro nziza nkimitsi; ntashobora kuba amaze iminsi itatu avuye murugendo rwe rwo mubuhinde. Uwo mugabo ukurikiraho asa nigicucu gito; ushobora kuvuga ko gukoraho ibiti bya satin biri muri we. Mu isura ya gatatu iracyafite ubushyuhe bwo mu turere dushyuha, ariko byoroheje gato; ntagushidikanya ko yamaze ibyumweru byose ku nkombe. Ariko ninde washobora kwerekana umusaya nka Queequeg? ibyo, bibujijwemo amabara atandukanye, byasaga nkiburengerazuba bwa Andes, kugirango bigaragare muburyo bumwe, butandukanye nikirere, akarere kuntara.

“Grub, ho!” noneho nyir'inzu yarize, akingura urugi, maze tujya gufata ifunguro rya mu gitondo.

Bavuga ko abagabo babonye isi, bityo bakoroherwa muburyo, bakitunga rwose. Ntabwo buri gihe, nubwo: Ledyard, umugenzi ukomeye wa New England, na Mungo Park, umwe muri Scotch; mubagabo bose, bari bafite ibyiringiro bike muri salle. Ariko birashoboka ko kwambuka gusa muri Siberiya mu muhigo washushanijwe n'imbwa nkuko Ledyard yabigenje, cyangwa gufata urugendo rurerure wenyine ku gifu cyuzuye ubusa, mu mutima wa negro wo muri Afurika, wari igiteranyo cy'imikorere mibi ya Mungo - ubu bwoko bw'urugendo, ndavuga, bushobora kuba atari bwo buryo bwiza cyane bwo kugera kuri polisi yo mu rwego rwo hejuru. Biracyaza, kubice byinshi, ibintu nkibi bigomba kuba ahantu hose.

Ibi bitekerezo hano gusa bigaragazwa nigihe tumaze kwicara kumeza, kandi nkaba nitegura kumva inkuru nziza zijyanye na baleine; Ntibitangaje, hafi ya buri mugabo yakomeje guceceka cyane. Kandi sibyo gusa, ahubwo basaga nisoni. Nibyo, hano hari imbwa-mbwa zo mu nyanja, inyinshi muri zo zitagira isoni na gato zari zinjiye mu nyanja nini ku nyanja ndende - abantu bose batazi kuri bo - maze zibapfira nta guhumbya; kandi nyamara, hano bicaye kumeza yo gusangira ifunguro rya mugitondo - bose bahamagaye kimwe, uburyohe bwa bene wabo - bareba nk'intama zintama nkaho batigeze babona intama zimwe ziri mumisozi yicyatsi. Kubona amatsiko; ibi bidubu biteye isoni, aba barwanyi b'intwari batinyuka!

Ariko kubijyanye na Queequeg - kubera iki, Queequeg yicaye hagati yabo - ku meza yameza, nayo yarahindutse; nkibyiza nkicicle. Kugira ngo mumenye neza ko ntashobora kuvuga byinshi kubworozi bwe. Umukunzi we ukomeye ntiyashoboraga kwerekana neza ko yazanye inanga ye mu gitondo, kandi akayikoresha nta muhango; kugera hejuru yameza hamwe nayo, mukaga kari hafi yimitwe myinshi, no gufata inyama zinka zimugana. Ariko ibyo rwose byakozwe neza na we, kandi buriwese azi ko mubitekerezo byabantu benshi, gukora ikintu cyiza ari ukubikora witonze.

Ntabwo tuzavuga umwihariko wa Queequeg hano; uburyo yirinze ikawa hamwe nudupapuro dushyushye, kandi akoresha ibitekerezo bye bitavangiye kuri beefsteaks, byakozwe bidasanzwe. Birahagije, ko ifunguro rya mu gitondo rirangiye asubira inyuma nk'abandi basigaye mu cyumba rusange, acana umuyoboro we wa tomahawk, maze yicara aho atuje kandi anywa itabi yambaye ingofero ye itandukanijwe, ubwo nashakaga gutembera.





UMUTWE WA 6. Umuhanda.

Iyaba nari natangajwe no kubanza kubona umuntu ku giti cye nka Queequeg yazengurukaga muri societe yubupfura yo mumujyi utuwe, ibyo byahise bitangara nyuma yo gutembera kumanywa yambere mumihanda ya New Bedford.

Mu nzira nyabagendwa yegereye ikivuko, icyambu icyo aricyo cyose gishobora gutanga kenshi kugirango turebe ikibazo cyanditse kidafite inyandiko ziva mu bice by'amahanga. Ndetse no mu mihanda ya Broadway na Chestnut, abasare bo mu nyanja ya Mediterane rimwe na rimwe bazajya basetsa abadamu bafite ubwoba. Umuhanda wa Regent ntabwo uzwi na Lascars na Maleziya; no kuri Bombay, muri Green Apollo, Yankees bazima bakunze gutera ubwoba kavukire. Ariko New Bedford ikubita Street Street yose na Wapping. Muri izi mpigi zavuzwe haruguru urabona abasare gusa; ariko muri New Bedford, abantu barya abantu bahagaze baganira kumihanda; abanyarugomo rwose; benshi muribo nyamara bitwaza amagufwa yabo inyama zanduye. Bituma umuntu utazi yitegereza.

Ariko, usibye Feegeeans, Tongatobooarrs, Erromanggoans, Pannangians, na Brighggians, kandi, usibye ingero zo mwishyamba zubukorikori butitondewe kumihanda, uzabona ahandi hantu hakiri amatsiko, rwose birasekeje. Hano buri cyumweru ugera muri uyu mujyi amanota menshi yicyatsi cya Vermonters nabagabo ba New Hampshire, bose bafite inyota yo kunguka nicyubahiro muburobyi. Ahanini ni bato, ya stalwart frame; bagenzi bacu batemye amashyamba, none bagashaka guta ishoka no kunyaga balale. Benshi ni icyatsi nkimisozi yicyatsi aho baturutse. Mubintu bimwe wabitekereza ariko amasaha make. Reba hano! icyo gice kizunguruka mu mfuruka. Yambaye ingofero yinzuki hamwe namira ikote umurizo, akenyeye umukandara-umukandara nicyuma. Hano haje undi ufite sou'-wester n'umwenda wa bombazine.

Nta dandy zororerwa mu mujyi uzagereranya n’uwororerwa mu gihugu - ndavuga dandy yuzuye igicucu - mugenzi wanjye, mu minsi y’imbwa, azaca hegitari ebyiri mu ntoki za buckskin kubera gutinya kumanika amaboko. Noneho iyo igihugu cyijimye nkibi kimujyanye mumutwe kugirango cyamamare, kandi kifatanije nuburobyi bukomeye bwa baleine, ugomba kubona ibintu bisekeje akora amaze kugera ku cyambu. Mu kwambika imyambaro ye yo mu nyanja, ategeka inzogera-buto ku ikoti rye; imishumi kumurongo wa canvas. Ah, umukene-Imbuto! mbega ukuntu bizaturika cyane iyo mishumi muri gale ya mbere yo gutaka, mugihe utwarwa, imishumi, buto, nibindi byose, kumuhogo wumuyaga.

Ariko ntutekereze ko uyu mujyi uzwi cyane ufite inanga, abantu barya abantu, hamwe nuduseke kugirango bereke abashyitsi be. Ntabwo ari rwose. Biracyaza New Bedford ni ahantu habi. Iyo tutaba abacuzi b'inyamanswa, ako gace k'ubutaka uyu munsi wenda kari kumera nk'inyanja ya Labrador. Nkuko biri, ibice byigihugu cye cyinyuma birahagije kugirango umuntu atere ubwoba, birasa neza. Umujyi ubwawo birashoboka ko ari ahantu heza cyane ho gutura, mu Bwongereza bwose. Nigihugu cyamavuta, cyukuri gihagije: ariko ntameze nka Kanani; igihugu, nacyo, cy'ibigori na vino. Imihanda ntigenda n'amata; eka no mu gihe c'impeshyi ntibayitera amagi mashya. Nyamara, nubwo bimeze gurtyo, ntahantu na hamwe muri Amerika uzasangamo amazu asa na patrician; parike n'ubusitani byiza cyane, kuruta muri New Bedford. Baturutse he? nigute cyatewe kuriyi scoria ya scraggy yigihugu?

Genda witegereze ibyuma byerekana ibimenyetso byicyuma kizengurutse inzu ndende, kandi ikibazo cyawe kizasubizwa. Yego; aya mazu yose yintwari nubusitani bwindabyo byaturutse mu nyanja ya Atalantika, Pasifika, nu Buhinde. Umwe muri bose, bavuzaga inanga bakururwa hano kuva munsi yinyanja. Ese Herr Alexander ashobora gukora ibikorwa nkibyo?

Bavuga ko muri New Bedford, ba se, baha abakobwa babo amafi ya baleine, bakagabana na bishywa babo hamwe na pisine nkeya. Ugomba kujya muri New Bedford kureba ubukwe bwiza; kuberako, bavuga ko bafite ibigega byamavuta muri buri nzu, kandi buri joro utitonze utwika uburebure bwa buji ya spermaceti.

Mu gihe cyizuba, umujyi uraryoshye kubona; yuzuye amakarita meza-inzira ndende yicyatsi na zahabu. Muri Kanama, hejuru cyane mu kirere, amafarashi meza cyane kandi menshi cyane-igituba, ubwenge bwa candelabra, uhindura umuhisi ukoresheje imishino yabo igororotse yuzuye indabyo. Ishoborabyose rero ni ubuhanzi; mu turere twinshi two muri New Bedford yarengeje amaterasi y’indabyo hejuru y’indabyo zitagira ubutayu zajugunywe ku munsi wanyuma w’irema.

N'abagore ba New Bedford, barabya nka roza zabo zitukura. Ariko roza zirabya gusa mu cyi; mugihe karnasi nziza yimisaya yabo ihoraho nkumucyo wizuba mwijuru rya karindwi. Ahandi hose bihuye nuburabyo bwabo, ntushobora, usibye muri Salem, aho bambwira ko abakobwa bakiri bato bahumeka imitsi nkiyi, umukunzi wabo wabasare babahumuriza kubirometero byinshi kuruhande, nkaho bashushanya hafi ya Moluccasi ihumura aho kuba umusenyi wa Puritanic.





UMUTWE WA 7. Chapel.

Muri iyi New Bedford nyine hari Chapel ya Whaleman, kandi bake ni abarobyi bameze neza, bidatinze berekeza mu nyanja y'Ubuhinde cyangwa Pasifika, bananiwe gusura icyumweru aho hantu. Nzi neza ko ntabikoze.

Nsubiye mu rugendo rwanjye rwa mbere mu gitondo, nongeye gusuhuza iki gikorwa kidasanzwe. Ijuru ryari ryahindutse riva mu mbeho ikonje, izuba, rihinduka ibicu n'ibicu. Nipfunyitse mu ikoti ryanjye rya shaggy y'igitambara cyitwa bearskin, narwanye inzira yanjye kurwanya umuyaga winangiye. Ninjiye, nasanze itorero rito ryatatanye ry'abasare, n'abagore b'abasare n'abapfakazi. Guceceka gucecetse byategetse, gusa byacitse rimwe na rimwe n'induru y'umuyaga. Buri wese usenga acecetse yasaga nkuwicaye nkana atandukanijwe nundi, nkaho buri gahinda kicecekeye kari insular kandi ntigishobora kuvugana. Abasare baramuranga; kandi barushijeho gushidikanya ko bamukekaho ukuri, kandi amaherezo, kugira ngo bagerageze ukuri, bakoresheje ikibazo cyose bakerekeza mu ijuru ryinshi, kugira ngo barebe icyaba cyatewe n'umuyaga mwinshi kuri bo. Umwuga wawe ni uwuhe? Uva he?

Arataka ati: '' Ndi Umuheburayo, hanyuma aravuga ati: 'Ndatinya Uwiteka Imana yo mu ijuru yaremye inyanja n'ubutaka bwumutse!' Wamutinye, Yona, Aye, urashobora gutinya Uwiteka Imana icyo gihe , noneho akomeza kwatura byuzuye, aho abasare barushijeho gutungurwa, ariko aracyafite impuhwe. "Noneho dore Yona yafashe nk'inanga maze agwa mu nyanja; mu gihe ako kanya umutuzo w'amavuta ureremba uturutse iburasirazuba, kandi inyanja iracyahari, kuko Yona yatwaraga gale hamwe na we, agasiga amazi meza. Yamanutse mu mutima uzunguruka w'umuvurungano utagira ubuhanga ku buryo atakunze kumvira akanya gato ko kumera nk'urwasaya rwinshi. benshi bera, kuri gereza ye. Hanyuma Yona asenga Uwiteka avuye mu nda. Ariko witegereze isengesho rye, kandi wige isomo riremereye kuko ari we, Yona ntabwo arira kandi araboroga kwihana kwizerwa ntabwo ari ugutakambira imbabazi, ariko ushimira igihano Kandi mbega ukuntu Imana yashimishije iyi myitwarire muri Yona, irerekanwa nyuma yo kumurokoka mu nyanja na baleine. Ntabwo nshyira Yona imbere yawe ngo yandukure icyaha cye, ariko ndabishyira mu mwanya wo kwihana;

Mu gihe yavugaga aya magambo, gutaka kw’umuyaga utontoma, uhuhuta utarinze bisa nkaho byongereye imbaraga umubwiriza, we, igihe yavugaga umuyaga w’umuyaga wa Yona, wasaga nkuwatewe n’umuyaga ubwe. Igituza cye cyimbitse cyane nko kubyimba hasi; amaboko ye yajugunywe yasaga nkibintu birwana kukazi; n'inkuba ziva mu musego we wuzuye, n'umucyo usimbuka mu jisho, bituma abamwumva bose bamureba bamutinya bafite ubwoba bwihuse kuri bo.

Noneho haje gutuza mumaso ye, ubwo ahindukirira bucece amababi yigitabo; hanyuma, amaherezo, uhagaze utanyeganyega, ufite amaso afunze, mukanya, wasaga nkuvugana n'Imana na we ubwayo.

"Basangirangendo, Imana yagushyizeho ikiganza kimwe gusa; amaboko ye yombi arankandaho. Kandi ibyo byari ibiki, abo mu bwato ngo babwirize Ukuri Kubeshya!

"Ibi, basangirangendo, iri ni ryo somo rindi; kandi haragowe uriya muderevu w'Imana nzima uyitwikiriye. Muzabona ishyano uwo isi ikundwa n'inshingano z'Ubutumwa Bwiza! Yaguye hasi yikubita hasi gato; hanyuma yongera kubatura mu maso, yongera kwerekana umunezero mwinshi mumaso ye, ubwo yatakambaga ashishikaye mwijuru, ati: "Ariko yewe, basangirangendo bo ku bwato! ku kiganza cyo ku cyambu cya buri cyago cyose, hari umunezero udashidikanywaho; kandi hejuru y'ibyo byishimo, kuruta munsi y'ibyago birimbitse. Ibyishimo ni umuntu ufite amaboko akomeye nyamara amushyigikiye, mugihe ubwato bwiyi si yahemutse bwamanutse munsi ye, Ibyishimo ni kuri we, udatanga kimwe cya kane mu kuri, kandi akica, agatwika, kandi agasenya ibyaha byose nubwo yabikuye mu mwenda w'Abasenateri n'abacamanza, ariko ntawe ubyishimira, ni we nyiricyubahiro. gukunda igihugu mu ijuru.
