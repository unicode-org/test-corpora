Intumwa ya Anacharsis Clootz iva mu birwa byose byo mu nyanja, no ku mpande zose z’isi, iherekeza Ahabu wa kera muri Pequod kugira ngo ishyire ibibazo by'isi imbere y'akabari aho benshi muri bo batigeze bagaruka. Umuyoboro muto w'umukara - ntabwo yigeze abikora-yewe, oya! yagiye mbere. Umukene Alabama! Ku byerekeranye na Pequod iteye ubwoba, uzatinda kumubona, akubita tambourine; mbere yigihe cyiteka, igihe yatumirwaga, kuri kimwe cya kane kinini hejuru, yasabwe gukubita abamarayika, akubita ingoma ye mu cyubahiro; witwa ikigwari hano, yashimye intwari aho!





UMUTWE WA 28. Ahabu.

Iminsi itari mike nyuma yo kuva muri Nantucket, ntakintu kiri hejuru yicyuma cyagaragaye cya Kapiteni Ahabu. Abashakanye bahoraga baruhura ku masaha, kandi ku kintu cyashoboraga kugaragara ku rundi ruhande, basaga nkaho ari bo bayobozi bonyine b'ubwato; gusa rimwe na rimwe basohokaga mu kabari bafite amabwiriza atunguranye kandi atunguranye, ku buryo nyuma yabyo byari byoroshye ariko bategeka bitandukanye. Nibyo, umutware wabo wikirenga numunyagitugu yari ahari, nubwo kugeza ubu atabonwa namaso ayo ari yo yose atemerewe kwinjira mumwiherero wera wa kabini.

Igihe cyose nazamutse mukibuga mvuye mumasaha yanjye hepfo, nahise nitegereza aft kugirango menye niba hari isura idasanzwe yagaragaye; kuberako ikibazo cyanjye cya mbere kidasobanutse gikora kuri capitaine utazwi, ubu mukigunga cyinyanja, byahindutse hafi. Ibi byariyongereye kuburyo budasanzwe rimwe na rimwe na Eliya yangiritse ya diabolical idahuzagurika kuri njye ntabishaka, nkoresheje imbaraga zoroshye ntari gushobora gutekereza mbere. Ariko nabi sinabashaga kubyihanganira, kimwe no mubindi bihe nari niteguye kumwenyura kubera irari rikomeye ry'uwo muhanuzi wo mu mahanga. Ariko ibyo aribyo byose byari ubwoba cyangwa guhangayika - kubyita gutya - numvaga, nyamara igihe cyose nazaga kundeba mu bwato, byasaga naho binyuranyije na garanti zose guha agaciro amarangamutima nkaya. Kuberako nubwo inanga, hamwe numubiri munini wabakozi, bari abanyamahane, abanyamahanga, na motley bashizeho cyane kuruta ayandi masosiyete yose yamenyereye abadandaza-ubwato bwambere nabimenyereye mbere, ariko nabyanditse - kandi ndabivuze neza - kubwihariye budasanzwe bwimiterere yuwo muhamagaro wa Scandinaviya nari nataye cyane. Ariko byari byibanze cyane cyane ku bayobozi bakuru batatu b'ubwato, abo bashakanye, babaruwe ku gahato kugira ngo bakureho ayo makimbirane atagira ibara, kandi bitera icyizere n'ibyishimo muri buri kiganiro cy'urugendo. Batatu beza, birashoboka cyane ko ari abasirikari bakuru n’abasirikare, buri wese muburyo bwe butandukanye, ntabwo yaboneka byoroshye, kandi bose bari abanyamerika; a Nantucketer, Umuzabibu, umugabo wa Cape. Noneho, ni Noheri ubwo ubwato bwarasaga ku cyambu cye, umwanya muto twarumye ikirere cya Polar, nubwo igihe cyose twahungaga tujya mu majyepfo; kandi kuri buri ntera n'umunota w'uburebure twafashe ubwato, buhoro buhoro dusiga iyo mbeho itagira impuhwe, hamwe nikirere cyayo cyose kidashobora kwihanganira inyuma yacu. Nibimwe mubitagabanutse, ariko biracyafite imvi nijimye mugitondo gihagije cyinzibacyuho, mugihe hamwe numuyaga mwiza ubwato bwanyuraga mumazi nubwoko bwihorere bwo gusimbuka no kwihuta cyane, kuburyo ubwo nageraga mukibuga mpamagaye isaha ya forenoon, nuko nkimara kuringaniza amaso yanjye kuri tafrail, nabanje kunyerera hejuru yanjye. Ukuri kurenze ubwoba; Kapiteni Ahabu yahagaze kuri etage ye.

Byasaga nkaho nta kimenyetso cyindwara zisanzwe zimwerekeyeho, nta nubwo yakize. Yasaga nkumugabo waciwe ku giti, mugihe umuriro wapfushije ubusa ingingo zose utarinze, cyangwa ngo ukureho agace kamwe mubikomere byabo byashaje. Imiterere ye yose miremire, yagutse, yasaga nkuwakozwe mu muringa ukomeye, kandi ikozwe muburyo butahinduka, nkumukinnyi wa Cellini Perseus. Uhinduranya inzira uva mu misatsi ye imeze imvi, hanyuma ukomeza kumanuka uruhande rumwe rwuruhu rwe rwijimye kandi rwijimye, kugeza igihe ruzimiye mu myenda ye, wabonye ikimenyetso kimeze nk'inkoni yoroheje, cyera cyane. Byasaga n'ikidodo cya perpendicular rimwe na rimwe bikozwe mu giti kigororotse, gishyizwe hejuru cy'igiti kinini, iyo umurabyo wo hejuru urira ugabanuka ukamanuka hasi, kandi utarinze gukata igiti kimwe, ibishishwa hamwe n'ibiti biva mu kibabi kuva hejuru kugeza hasi, mbere yuko biruka mu butaka, bigasigara igiti kikiri kizima, ariko kikaba cyanditseho ikimenyetso. Niba icyo kimenyetso cyaravukanye na we, cyangwa niba ari inkovu yasizwe igikomere runaka, ntawabura kuvuga. Kubwumvikane buke, murugendo rwose cyangwa ntanumwe wabigizemo uruhare, cyane cyane kubashakanye. Ariko igihe mukuru wa Tashtego, umusaza w’Abahinde bahuje ibitsina bahuje ibitsina mu bakozi, yemeraga mu miziririzo avuga ko atarageza ku myaka mirongo ine y'amavuko Ahabu yahindutse atyo, hanyuma bikamugeraho, atari mu burakari bw’intambara iyo ari yo yose yapfuye, ahubwo ni amakimbirane ashingiye ku nyanja. Nyamara, aya magambo yo mu gasozi yasaga nkaho ari mubi, bitewe n’ibyo Manxman yumukara yashizemo, umusaza w’imva, utarigeze afata ubwato ava i Nantucket, ntabwo yari yarigeze yitegereza Ahab ishyamba. Nubwo bimeze bityo ariko, imigenzo ya kera yo mu nyanja, kwizerwa kuva kera, yakunze gushora imari ya Manxman ishaje n'imbaraga zidasanzwe zo gushishoza. Kugira ngo rero nta musare w'umuzungu wamuvuguruzaga cyane igihe yavugaga ko niba harigihe Kapiteni Ahabu yagombye gushyirwaho mu mutuzo - bikaba bitashoboka ko abaho, bityo akitotomba - noneho, umuntu wese uzakorera ibyo biro bya nyuma ku bapfuye, yamusangamo ikimenyetso cy'amavuko kuva ku ikamba kugeza ku kindi.

Mu buryo bukomeye, ibintu byose bibi bya Ahabu byangizeho ingaruka, hamwe nikirangantego cyiza cyayikurikiranye, ku buryo mu kanya gato sinigeze mbona ko nta na gato muri ibyo byago birenze urugero byatewe n'ukuguru kwera kwera yari ahagararaho igice. Byari byabanje kunsanga ko ukuguru kwinzovu kwari mu nyanja kwakozwe mu magufwa asukuye yo mu rwasaya rwa baleine. Umusaza Gay-Head Umuhinde yigeze kuvuga ati: "Aye, yasezerewe mu Buyapani." "Ariko nk'ubukorikori bwe bwasenyutse, yohereje ikindi gikoko atagarutse mu rugo. Afite umutiba wa em."

Nakubiswe numwanya wihariye yakomeje. Kuri buri ruhande rw'igorofa ya Pequod, kandi hafi y’igitambaro cya mizzen, hari umwobo wa auger, urambiwe hafi igice cya santimetero cyangwa irenga, ku rubaho. Ukuguru kwe kw'amagufwa guhagarara muri uwo mwobo; ukuboko kumwe kuzamuye, kandi gufashe umwenda; Kapiteni Ahabu yahagaze neza, yitegereza neza hejuru y'ubwato buri gihe. Habaho ubuziraherezo bwo gushikama gukomeye, ubushake, budashidikanywaho, mubushake butajegajega kandi budatinya, ubwitange bwimbere bwiryo jisho. Nta jambo yavuze; eka n'abasirikare biwe ntibigeze bamubwira ikintu na kimwe; nubwo bakoresheje ibimenyetso byabo byose byibuze, bagaragaje neza ko bitagoranye, niba bitababaza, imyumvire yo kuba munsi ya shobuja-jisho rifite ibibazo. Kandi si ibyo gusa, ahubwo Ahabu yari afite ubwoba bwinshi yahagaze imbere yabo afite umusaraba mu maso; muri byose bitagira izina regal birenze icyubahiro cyamahano akomeye.

Ere ndende, avuye mu ruzinduko rwe rwa mbere mu kirere, asubira mu kabari ke. Ariko nyuma yicyo gitondo, buri munsi yabonaga abakozi; yaba ahagaze mu mwobo we, cyangwa yicaye ku ntebe y'inzovu yari afite; cyangwa kugenda cyane muri etage. Igihe ikirere cyakuze kijimye; mubyukuri, yatangiye gukura genial nkeya, yabaye muto kandi ntagabanuka; nkaho, igihe ubwato bwari bumaze kuva murugo, ntakindi uretse umwijima wapfuye winyanja wapfuye wari waramurinze wenyine. Kandi, uko byagenda kose, byaje guhora mu kirere; ariko, kugeza ubu, kubyo yavuze byose, cyangwa byumvikane ko yakoze, kumurongo wanyuma wizuba, yasaga nkibidakenewe hariya nkindi mast. Ariko Pequod yarimo ikora igice gusa; ntibigenda buri gihe; hafi ya byose byateguwe na baleine bakeneye kugenzurwa abo bashakanye bari babishoboye rwose, kuburyo ntakintu gito cyangwa ntakintu, muri we, cyo gukoresha cyangwa gushimisha Ahabu, ubungubu; hanyuma rero wirukane, kuri iyo ntera imwe, ibicu bitondekanye kurwego rwarundarunda ku musego we, nkuko bisanzwe ibicu byose bihitamo impinga ndende kugirango birundarunda.

Nubwo bimeze bityo ariko, mbere yigihe kirekire, ubushyuhe, bushyashya bwo kwemeza ibihe byiza, ibiruhuko twaje, byasaga nkaho bigenda bimushimisha kumutima. Kuberako, nkigihe abakobwa bafite imisaya itukura, babyina, Mata na Gicurasi, basubira murugo mumashyamba ya wintry, misanthropic; ndetse igiti cyiza cyane, gikomeye, inkuba zishaje cyane byibura zohereza ibimera bike byatsi, kugirango twakire abashyitsi bafite umutima wishimye; nuko Ahabu akora, amaherezo, asubiza gato kuri allurings ikinisha yuwo mwuka wumukobwa. Inshuro zirenze imwe yashyize ahagaragara uburabyo butagaragara bwo kureba, mu wundi mugabo uwo ari we wese, bidatinze byari kumera neza amwenyura.
