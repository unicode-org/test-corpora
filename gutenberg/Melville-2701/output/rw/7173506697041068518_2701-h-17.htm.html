Nubwo bimeze bityo ariko, ntidushobora guhakana, ko muri rusange bafite uburenganzira bwo kuragwa kuri bon point .

Biteye amatsiko cyane kureba iyi harem na nyagasani muri rambling zabo zidashaka. Kimwe nimyambarire, bahora murugendo rwo gushakisha bidatinze. Urabahurira kumurongo mugihe cyururabyo rwuzuye rwigihe cyo kugaburira Ekwatoriya, umaze kugaruka, wenda, kuva kumara icyi mumyanyanja yamajyaruguru, bityo rero ugashuka icyi cyumunaniro udashimishije nubushyuhe. Mugihe bamaze kuzamuka no kumanuka mugihe cya Ekwateri mugihe gito, batangira kumazi yuburasirazuba bategereje ibihe bikonje bihari, bityo bakirinda ubundi bushyuhe bukabije bwumwaka.

Iyo utezimbere cyane murimwe mururwo rugendo, niba hari ibintu bitangaje biboneka, databuja whale akomeza kwitondera umuryango we ushimishije. Niba hari umusore udafite ishingiro Leviathan uza gutya, tekereza kwiyegereza rwihishwa umwe mubadamu, nuburakari bukabije Bashaw yamuteye, aramwirukana! Ibihe byinshi, mubyukuri, niba udusimba duto duto duto nka we tugomba kwemererwa gutera ubutagatifu bwibyishimo murugo; nubwo ukora ibyo Bashaw ashaka, ntashobora kurinda Lothario uzwi cyane muburiri bwe; kuko, ishyano! uburiri bwamafi bwose. Nkinkombe, abadamu bakunze gutera amahano ateye ubwoba mubakunzi babo bahanganye; gusa rero hamwe na baleine, rimwe na rimwe baza kurugamba rwica, kandi bose kubwurukundo. Bazitira urwasaya rurerure rwo hasi, rimwe na rimwe bakabafunga hamwe, bityo bagaharanira ubutware nk'inzoka zivanga mu ntambara. Ntabwo ari bake bafashwe bafite inkovu ndende zibyo bahuye, -imitwe yuzuye, amenyo yamenetse, amababa manini; hamwe na hamwe, umunwa wuzuye kandi wimuwe.

Ariko tuvuge ko uwateye umunezero wo mu rugo ngo yishyire mu gihirahiro cya mbere cya shebuja wa harem, ubwo rero biratandukanye cyane kureba uwo mutware. Abigiranye ubwitonzi, yongeye gushiramo igice kinini muri bo maze ahishurirwa igihe gito, akiri mu gutandukanya hafi na Lothario ukiri muto, nka Salomo wubaha Imana wasengaga abihayimana mu nshoreke ze igihumbi. Guha izindi nyanja kugira ngo ziboneke, abarobyi ntibakunze kwirukana umwe muri abo Banyaturukiya Bakuru; kuberako aba Turkiya Nkuru bafite imbaraga nyinshi, bityo rero kutitonda kwabo ni bito. Naho abahungu n'abakobwa babyara, kuki, abo bahungu n'abakobwa bagomba kwiyitaho; byibuze, hamwe nubufasha bwababyeyi gusa. Kuberako nkabandi bakunzi bagenda bashobora byose bashobora kwitwa, Mwami Whale ntabwo afite uburyohe kuri pepiniyeri, nubwo ari bower; nuko rero, kuba umugenzi ukomeye, asize abana be batamenyekanye kwisi yose; buri mwana wese ni exotic. Mugihe cyiza, nonese, nkuko ishyaka ryurubyiruko rigabanuka; uko imyaka n'ibitaka byiyongera; nkuko gutekereza kumuguriza kuruhuka; muri make, nkuko lassitude rusange irenga Turkiya yicaye; noneho gukunda ubworoherane nubupfura bitanga urukundo kubakobwa; Ottoman yacu yinjira mubyiciro bidashoboka, bihannye, byimpanuro zubuzima, areka, asenya harem, kandi akura mubugingo bwintangarugero, sulky, agenda wenyine wenyine muri meridian hamwe na parallel avuga amasengesho ye, kandi aburira buri musore Leviathan kumakosa ye ateye isoni.

Noneho, nkuko harem ya baleine yitwa abarobyi ishuri, niko nyagasani na shebuja w'iryo shuri mubuhanga bizwi nkumuyobozi wishuri. Ntabwo rero ari imico itajenjetse, nubwo ishimishije, ko nyuma yo kujya mwishuri ubwe, agomba noneho kujya mumahanga atabigizemo uruhare aho yize, ahubwo ni ubupfu bwabyo. Izina rye, umuyobozi w’ishuri, risanzwe risa nkaho ryakomotse ku izina ryahawe harem ubwayo, ariko hari abavuze ko uyu mugabo wabanje kwita ubwo bwoko bwa baleine ya Ottoman, agomba kuba yarasomye ibyibutswe na Vidocq, akanamenyesha ubwoko bw’umuyobozi w’ishuri w’umufaransa uzwi cyane mu minsi ye y'ubuto, kandi ni ubuhe bwoko bw'ayo masomo y’ubupfumu yatangizaga.

Kwigunga no kwigunga kimwe na baleine w’ishuri yishakiye mu myaka ye y'ubukure, ni ukuri no ku basaza bose ba Sperm Whales. Hafi yisi yose, balale yonyine-nkuko yitwa Leviathan wenyine yitwa-yerekana ko ari iyakera. Kimwe na Daniel Boone ufite ubwanwa bwogosha bwanwa, nta wundi azagira hafi ye uretse Kamere ubwe; na we amujyana mu butayu bw'amazi, kandi mwiza mu bagore ni we, nubwo abika amabanga menshi atuje.

Amashuri adahimbye usibye abasore n'imbaraga b'igitsina gabo, twavuze haruguru, atanga itandukaniro rikomeye n'amashuri ya harem. Kuberako mugihe izo nyanja zi gore zirangwa no gutinyuka, abasore bato, cyangwa ibimasa mirongo ine, nkuko babita, kugeza ubu ni abanyamurwango cyane muri Leviathans, kandi wa mugani ni bibi cyane guhura nabyo; usibye utwo tuntu twiza twinshi twumutwe, wifi ya baleine, rimwe na rimwe twahuye, kandi ibi bizakurwanya nka grim fiends yarakaye na goute.

Amashuri mirongo ine-ingunguru-nini nini kuruta amashuri ya harem. Kimwe n'agatsiko k'abasore bakorana, buzuye urugamba, kwishimisha, n'ubugome, kuzerera ku isi ku buryo butitondewe, buzunguruka, ku buryo nta mwanditsi w’ubushishozi wigeze abishingira nk'uko yabikora umusore wigometse kuri Yale cyangwa Harvard. Bidatinze baretse iyi mvururu nubwo, kandi iyo hafi bitatu bya kane byakuze, biratandukana, kandi bitandukanye bigenda bishakisha gutura, ni ukuvuga harem.

Indi ngingo itandukanye hagati yishuri ryigitsina gabo nigitsina gore iracyaranga igitsina. Vuga ko ukubise ikimasa-mirongo ine-shitani-shitani ikennye! bagenzi be bose baramuretse. Ariko ukubite umunyeshuri wishuri rya harem, nabagenzi be baroga bamuzengurutsa nibimenyetso byose, rimwe na rimwe bimutinda hafi kandi birebire, nkuko nabo ubwabo bagwa.
