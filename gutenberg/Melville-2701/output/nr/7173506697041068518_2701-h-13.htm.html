ISAHLUKO 61. U-Stubb ubulala umkhomo.

Nangabe ku-Starbuck ukuvela kwe-Squid bekuyinto ebonakalako, ku-Queequeg bekuyinto ehluke khulu.

"Nawumbona 'i-quid," kwatjho isigebengu, aqinisa isibhamu sakhe emnsalo wesikepe sakhe esiphakanyisiweko, "bese umbona msinyana 'umkhomo we-parm."

Ilanga elilandelako belithule khulu begodu livutha, begodu bekunganalitho elikhethekileko elingababamba, isiqhema se-Pequod besingakghoni ukujamelana nokulala okubangelwa lilwandle elinganamuntu kangaka. Ngombana ingcenye yeLwandle le-India ebesikhamba kiyo akusiyo leyo abantu bemikhomo abayibiza ngokuthi yindawo ephilako; okutjho bona, inikela ukubona okuncani kwama-porpoises, ama-dolphin, i-flying-fish, nezinye iinlwana ezihlala emanzini ahlaba umxhwele, kunalezo ezise-Rio de la Plata, nofana ephasini eliseduze ne-Peru.

Kwaba lithuba lami lokujamela phambili; begodu ngamahlombe wami ancike eembathweni zasebukhosini ezikhululekileko, ngapha nangapha ngitjhinga ngingasebenzi kilokho ebekubonakala ngasuthi kungumoya othakazelisako. Asikho isisombululo ebesingajamelana naso; ngaleso simo sokuphupha ngilahlekelwa yikho koke ukwazi, ekugcineni umphefumulo wami waphuma emzimbeni wami; nanyana umzimba wami usaragela phambili nokunyakaza njenge-pendulum, isikhathi eside ngemva kokuthi amandla ebegade awunyakazisa athoma akhutjhwe.

Ngaphambi kokuthi ukukhohlwa kufike kimi, benginanzelele bona abasebenzi bemikhumbi abasemikhumbini ekulu besele balele. Ekugcineni sobathathu sajika ngaphandle kokuphila, begodu ngaso soke isikhathi esikwenzako bekunokuvuma ngehloko okuvela phasi okuvela kumtjhayeli wesikepe oleleko. Amagagasi, nawo, avuma ngehloko; begodu ngaphetjheya kwe-trance ebanzi yelwandle, ipumalanga yatjhingela entshonalanga, begodu ilanga liphezu kwakho koke.

Ngokuphazima kweso kwabonakala amabhamuza aqhuma ngaphasi kwamehlo wami avaliweko; njengemibi izandla zami zabamba izingubo; enye into engabonakaliko, enomusa yangilondoloza; ngokuthuka ngabuyela epilweni. Begodu qala! eduze ngaphasi kwe-lee yethu, ingasi ngama-fathoms amane, i-Sperm Whale ekulu gade igijima emanzini njengesikhumba se-frigate, umhlane wayo obanzi, ocwebezelako, onombala we-Ethiopia, ukhazimula emisebeni yelanga njengesibuko. Kodwana ngokuvilapha atjhinga emgodini welwandle, begodu njalo ngokuthula akhipha i-jet yayo yomhwamuko, umkhomo bewuqaleka njenge-burgher ebhema iphayiphi yayo yemini efuthumeleko. Kodwana iphayiphi leyo, umkhomo omumbi, bekungowokugcina wakho. Njengokungathi utshaywe yinduku yomunye umthakathi, umkhumbi olelekileko nabo boke abalele kiwo bathoma ukuphaphama; begodu amaphimbo angaphezu kwamatjhumi amanengi avela kizo zoke iingcenye zomkhumbi, ngasikhathi sinye namanothi amathathu avela phezulu, arhuwelela ukulila okujayelekileko, njengombana ifesi ekulu beyithumela kancanikancani begodu njalo i-brine ecwebezelako emoyeni.

“Susa iinkepe! Luff!” kwamemeza u-Ahabi. Bese alalela umyalo wakhe, wawisa isikepe phasi ngaphambi kokuthi umtjhayeli akwazi ukuphatha ama-spoke.

Ukurhuwelela kwabasebenzi kufuze bona kwathusa umkhomo; Wahamba wavuka walalala e-19.... 10 ucabanga ukuthi u-ama wazibala e-Amleni. Njeke sihlezi njengamaNdiya we-Ontario phezu kweenkepe, sagwedla msinyana kodwana sithule; ukuthula ukungavumi amaseyila anganawo umsindo ebekiweko. Njenganje, njengombana besigijima, isilo saphaphazela umsila waso ngamamitha amatjhumi amane phezulu emoyeni, bese satjhinga ngaphandle kokubona njengombhoshongo ogwinywe.

“Kukhona ama-fluke!” kwaba kukhala, isimemezelo esalandelwa msinyana ngu-Stubb ukukhiqiza umdlalo wakhe begodu wavutha ipayipi lakhe, ngombana kwanje kwanikelwa ikhefu. Ngemva kokuthi isikhathi esipheleleko sokuhlola kwakhe sidlule, umkhomo waphakama godu, begodu njengombana gade sele uphambi kwesikepe sombhemi, begodu sele useduze khulu naso kunanoma ngisiphi esinye, u-Stubb wathembela ekuthunjweni kokubanjwa. Bekusobala, kwanje, bona umkhomo sele sele wazi abamsukelako. Ngalokho-ke ukuthula kokutjheja bekungasasebenzi. Amagwegwe alahlwa, begodu amagwegwe aqala ukudlala khulu. Begodu asaphefumulela umbhobho wakhe, u-Stubb wathokozela isiqhema sakhe ngokuhlasela.

Iye, itjhuguluko elikhulu belifikile phezu kwefesi. Aphila engozini, bekaphuma "ngehloko"; ingcenye leyo ephuma ngokutjhingako evela emvubelo ehlanyako ayiphekako.*

*Kuzokubonakala kwenye indawo bona ingaphakathi lehloko elikhulu le-sperm whale liyinto elula khulu. Nanyana kubonakala ngasuthi yiyo ekulu khulu, kodwana yinto ehle khulu ngaye. Ngalokho ngokulula uyayiphakamisela emoyeni, begodu enza njalo lokha nakahamba ngebelo elikhulu. Ngaphandle kwalokho, ububanzi bengcenye yangaphezulu yangaphambili yekhanda lakhe bunjalo, begodu nokwakheka kwamanzi okusikiweko kwengcenye engezansi, kangangobana ngokuphakamisa ikhanda lakhe ngokutjhingako, kungatjhiwo bona uzitjhugulula ukusuka esikepeni esibuthakathaka esibukhali saba sikepe esibukhali se-New York.

"Mqaliseni, mthome, madoda wami! Ningazijahi; thathani isikhathi esinengi—kodwana mthome; mthome njengokuduma, lokho kwaphela," kwamemeza u-Stubb, akhupha intuthu njengombana akhuluma. "Mthome, nje; ubanike isifo eside nesiqinileko, Tashtego. Mthome, Tash, mfana wami—mthome, woke; kodwana hlala upholile, hlala upholile—amakhukhamba ligama—kulula, lula—mthome njengokufa okumbi namadimoni abobothekako, begodu uvuse abafileko abangcwatjhweko baphume emathuneni wabo—athoma koke lokho!"

“Woo-hoo! Wa-hee!” kwamemeza u-Gay-Header ngokuphendula, aphakamisela phezulu ipi endala ezulwini; njengombana woke umgwedli esikepeni esigandelelekileko wagxuma phambili ngokungazimiseli ngokushaya okukhulu okudosa phambili okwanikelwa mNdiya onomdlandla.

Kodwana ukurhuwelela kwakhe okumbi kwaphendulwa ngabanye abafana nokurhuwelela. “Kee-hee! Kee-hee!” kwamemeza u-Daggoo, atjhingela phambili nangemuva esihlalweni sakhe, njengengwe ekhambako ekhejini lakhe.

“Ka-la! Koo-loo!” kwamemeza u-Queequeg, kwanga ushaya iindebe zakhe phezu komlomo westeyiki se-Grenadier. Begodu ngaleyo ndlela ngamagwegwe nokurhuwelela ama-keel asika ilwandle. Ngalesosikhathi, u-Stubb agcine indawo yakhe evenini, bekasakhuthaza amadodakhe bona athome, ngaso soke isikhathi afutha intuthu emlonyeni wakhe. Njengabantu abaphelelwa lithemba badonsa begodu baqinisa, bekwaba kulapho kuzwakala isililo sokwamukela—“Sukuma, Tashtego!—mnike!” Isibhamu saphoselwa. “Qiniseka kibo boke!” Abagwedli bebasekela amanzi; ngesikhatjhana esifanako kwaba nento etjhisako begodu ekhwiza kizo zoke iinhlakala zabo. Bekumugqa womlingo. Msinyana ngaphambilini, u-Stubb bekabambe amajika amabili angezelelweko msinyana azungeze i-loggerhead, lapho, ngebanga lokujikeleza kwayo okurhabako, intuthu eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka ye-hemp yaphuma begodu yahlangana nentuthu engaguqukiko evela epayipini lakhe. Njengoba umugqa udlula ujikeleza uzungeza i-loggerhead; godu, ngaphambi kobana ifike lapho, yadlula khulu ezandleni zaka-Stubb, lapho iindwangu zezandla, nofana iinkwere zekhanvasi ebegade zigqokwa ngezinye iinkhathi, beziwele khona ngengozi. Bekufana nokubamba isabula elibukhali lesitha elinamahlangothi amabili ngesabula, begodu isitha leso sihlala silwela ukusikhupha esandleni sakho.

“Manzisa umugqa! manzisa umugqa!” kwamemeza u-Stubb kumgwedli webhati (yena ohlezi eduze nebhati) owathi, ahlwitha isigqoko sakhe, wafaka amanzi welwandle kiwo.* Kwathathwa amajika amanengi, ukwenzela bona umtjhini wathoma ukubamba indawo yawo. Isikepe besele siphaphazela emanzini abilako njengeshaka. U-Stubb no-Tashtego lapha batjhugulula iindawo—isiqu se-stern—ibhizinisi elimangalisako kwamambala kileso siphithiphithi esikhulu.

*Ngokwengcenye ukutjengisa ukuqakatheka kwalesi senzo, kungatjhiwo lapha, bona, ekudobeni kwakade kwe-Dutch, bekusetjenziselwa i-mop ukutjhabalalisa umtjhini wokugijima ngamanzi; kweminye imikhumbi eminengi, ingulube yamaplanka, nofana i-bailer, ibekelwa eceleni umnqopho loyo. Isigqoko sakho, nanyana kunjalo, silula khulu.

Ukusuka emgqeni odlidlizako owenza ubude boke behlangothi eliphezulu lesikepe, begodu njengombana sele siqinile ukudlula intambo yehabhu, bewungacabanga bona isikepe sineenqhema ezimbili—elinye lihlukanisa amanzi, elinye lihlukanisa umoya—njengombana isikepe besiragela phambili ngaphakathi kwezinto zombili eziphikisanako ngasikhathi sinye. I-cascade eragela phambili idlalwa emabhodini; ukuvunguza okungapheliko emva kwakhe; begodu, ngokunyakaza okuncani okuvela ngaphakathi, ngitjho nomunwe omncani, isikepe esithuthumelako, esiqhekekako saphakama phezu kwe-gunwale yakhe evuvukileko saya elwandle. Ngalokho baphuthuma; indoda ngayinye ngamandla namandla anamathele esihlalweni sayo, ukukhandela ukuphoselwa egwebu; kanye nefomu ede ka-Tashtego esigwedlweni sokutjhayela aguqe pheze kabili, ukwenzela bona wehlise isikhungo sakhe sokudonsela phasi. I-Atlantics yoke ne-Pacific begade sele idlule njengombana idubula endleleni yayo, bekwaba kulapho umkhomo wehlisa ukuphapha kwawo.

“Donsa—donsa!” kwamemeza u-Stubb kumdubuli! begodu, aqale umkhomo, zoke izandla zathoma ukudosela isikepe kuye, lokha isikepe sisadonswa. Msinyana wakhuphuka ngehlangothini lakhe, u-Stubb, abeka idolo lakhe ngokuqinileko esikhwameni esingakalungi, waphosa umkhonto ngemva komkhonto phakathi kwefesi endizako; ngelizwi lomyalo, isikepe satjhinga ngaphandle kwendlela yomkhomo omumbi, bese siphakama phezulu ukuya enye i-fling.

Igagasi elibomvu besele litheleka ukusuka kizo zoke iinhlangothi zesilo njengemifudlana ehla entabeni. Umzimba wakhe ohlunguphazako bewungaguquki nge-brine kodwana ngeengazi, ebegade zivuvuka begodu zibila isikhathi eside ngemva kokuvuka kwabo. Ilanga elitjhingako elidlala phezu kwechibi elibomvu elwandle, labuyisela ukukhanya kwalo ebusweni boke, ukwenzela bona boke bakhanye njengamadoda abomvu. Begodu soke isikhathi, intuthu emhlophe beyidutshulwa kabuhlungu ukusuka emlonyeni womkhomo, begodu ukuvuvuka okukhulu ngemva kokuvuvuka okuvela emlonyeni wesikhulu esithabileko; njengawo woke umcibisholo, adonsa umkhonto wakhe ogobileko (ngomtjhini onamathiselwe kiwo), u-Stubb wawulungisa godu godu, ngokubetha okumbalwa okurhabako ngokujamelene ne-gunwale, bese wawuthumela godu godu ngaphakathi komkhomo.

“Dosela phezulu—donsa phezulu!” manje wakhala kumdubuli, njengombana umkhomo ongapheliko wakhululeka ngelaka lakhe. “Dosa phezulu!—eduze!” begodu isikepe sahamba ngehlangothini lenhlanzi. Lokha nakafika kude ngaphezulu komnsalo, u-Stubb wagoqa kancanikancani umkhonto wakhe omude obukhali ngaphakathi kwefesi, wawugcina lapho, ngokutjheja begodu ugoqa, ngasuthi ufuna ukuzwa ngemva kwewatjhi yegolide ukuthi umkhomo kungenzeka bona uyiginye, begodu ebegade asaba ukuyiphula ngaphambi kobana ayikhokhe. Kodwana iwatjhi leyo yegolide ebekayifuna bekuyipilo yangaphakathi yefesi. Nje-ke sele itjhaywe; ngombana, ukuthoma ngokuthula kwakhe ukuya entweni engakhulumekiko ebizwa ngokuthi "ukuthuthumela," isilwane sazigoqa egazini laso, sazigoqa ngesifutho esingenakungena, esihlanyako, esibilako, kangangobana isikepe esisengozini, sawela msinyana ngemva, saba neenkinga ezinengi ngokungaboni ukuphuma kusukela emoyeni okhanyako ukuya ekuseni.

Nje-ke sele athoma ukutjhinga kwakhe, umkhomo waphinda wavele wabonakala; ukukhuphuka ukusuka ehlangothini ukuya kwelinye; ukuvuleka nokunciphisa umgodi wakhe, ngokuphefumula okubukhali, okudabukileko, okubuhlungu. Ekugcineni, ukugeleza ngemva kokugeleza kwegazi elibomvu elihlangeneko, kwanga bekuyi-lees ebubende yewayini elibomvu, ladutshulwa emoyeni othukileko; begodu wabuyela emuva godu, wagijima aconsa phasi emahlangothini wakhe angashukumi elwandle. Ihliziyo yakhe beyithuthumele!

"Uhlongakele, Mnumzana Stubb," kwatjho u-Daggoo.

“Iye; womabili amapayipi aphelile!” begodu wakhipha eyakhe emlonyeni wakhe, u-Stubb wahlakaza umlotha ofileko phezu kwamanzi; begodu, isikhatjhana, wama ngokucabanga aqale isidumbu esikhulu asisenzileko.





ISAHLUKO 62. I-Dart.

Ilizwi eliphathelene nesehlakalo esahlukweni sokugcina.

Ngokuya ngokusetjenziswa okungatjhugulukiko kwezokudoba, isikepe semikhomo siyasuka emkhunjini, umphathi namkha umbulali wemikhomo njengomtjhayeli wesikhatjhana, begodu umtjhayeli wemikhomo namkha umtjhayeli wemikhomo udosa isigwedlo sangaphambili, leso esaziwa ngokuthi sigwedlo sesikepe. Nje idinga ingalo enamandla, ethukileko ukushaya insimbi yokuthoma emfesini; ngombana kanengi, kilokho okubizwa ngokuthi mcibisholo omude, isisetjenziswa esinzima kufanele siphoselwe ibanga elingamamitha amatjhumi amabili nofana amathathu. Kodwana nanyana kuthatha isikhathi eside begodu kukhathaza kangangani ukujaha, umdlali we-harpoon kulindeleke bona adose isigwedlo sakhe ngaleso sikhathi; kwamambala, kulindeleke bona abeke isibonelo somsebenzi ongaphezu kwamandla wabantu kwabanye, ingasi ngokugwedla okumangalisako kwaphela, kodwana ngokurhuwelela okuzwakalako nokungabi nesibindi; nokuthi kuyini ukuragela phambili nokurhuwelela phezulu kwekhampasi yakho, ngesikhathi yoke eminye imisipha igandelelekile begodu iqalile—ukuthi lokho akwaziwa ngaphandle kwalabo abake bazama. Kokuthoma, angeze ngakghona ukukhala khulu begodu ngisebenze ngokunganaki ngesikhathi sinye. Ngalesi simo sokugandeleleka, sokurhuwelela, bese, abheke umhlana wakhe emfesini, msinyana umdlali we-harpoon okhatheleko wezwa ukurhuwelela okuthabisako—“Sukuma, umnike yona!” Nje-ke kufuze awise begodu aqinise isigwedlo sakhe, aphenduke phakathi nendawo, athathe i-harpoon yakhe esifubeni, begodu ngamandla amancani angase aseleko, uzama ukuyiphosa ngandlela thize ngaphakathi komkhomo. Akurari, ukuthatha yoke imikhumbi yama-whalemen emzimbeni, ukuthi emathubeni amatjhumi amahlanu amahle womcibisholo, akusiwo amahlanu aphumelelako; akurari ukuthi abadlali abanengi kangaka abanganathando baqalekiswe begodu baphatheke kumbi; akurari ukuthi abanye babo baqhumisa imithambo yabo yeengazi ngaphakathi kwesikepe; akurari ukuthi ezinye iinkhomo zesidoda azikho iminyaka emine ngemiphongolo emine; akurari ukuthi ebantwini abanengi bemikhumbi, ukudoba imikhomo kukutshwenyeka okuphelelwa mamandla; ngombana mdlali we-harpooner owenza ikhambo, begodu nangabe ukhipha ummoya emzimbeni wakhe ungalindela njani ukuwufumana lapho lokha nawufunwa khulu!

Godu, nangabe umcibisholo uphumelele, khona-ke ngesikhatjhana sesibili esiqakathekileko, okutjho bona, lokha umkhomo nawuthoma ukugijima, umdosiphambili wesikepe nomtjhayeli wesikepe nabo bathoma ukugijima phambili nangemuva, ukuya engozini eseduze kibo nabo boke abanye. Kulapho-ke batjhugulula iindawo; begodu umphathi, umphathi omkhulu wesikepe esincani, uthatha isikhundla sakhe esifaneleko emaphethelweni wesikepe.

Nje-ke, anginandaba nokuthi ngubani ophikisako, kodwana koke lokhu kubudlhadlha begodu akutlhogeki. Umphathi kufanele ahlale emabhodini kusukela ekuthomeni ukuya ekugcineni; kufanele adubule i-harpoon ne-lance, begodu akukafaneli kulindeleke ukugwedla kuye, ngaphandle kwalokha ubujamo obubonakala kiwo woke umthiyiinhlambi. Ngiyazi bona lokhu kwesinye isikhathi kuzokufaka hlangana ukulahlekelwa lijubane elincani ekugijimeni; kodwana isipiliyoni eside ema-whalemen ahlukahlukeneko weentjhaba ezingaphezu kwelilodwa singenze ngakholwa bona ekwehlulekeni okunengi kwezokudoba, bekungakabi lijubane lomkhomo njengokudinwa okukhulunywe ngaphambilini komdobi we-harpooner okubangele bona kube njalo.

Ukuqinisekisa ukusebenza kuhle khulu emcibisholo, ama-harpooners wephasi kufuze athome ukusukuma ngaphandle kokungasebenzi, ingasi ngokuzikhandla.





ISAHLUKO 63. Isitho sangasese.

Ngaphandle kwesiqu, amagatja akhula; ngaphandle kwawo, amagatja. Ngalokho, eenhlokweni ezikhiqizako, khulisa izahluko.

Isitho sangasese esikhulunywe ngaso ekhasini langaphambilini sifanele ukukhulunywa ngokuzijamela. Yinduku eneenhluthu ezihlukileko, engaba ziinyawo ezimbili ubude, efakwe ngokuqondileko ngaphakathi kwe-gunwale ye-starboard eduze ne-bow, ngenhloso yokunikela indawo yokuphumula yesiphetho se-harpoon, esinye isiphetho saso esingenalutho, esinamahlaba esiphuma phambili. Ngaleyo ndlela isikhali sitjhidela msinyana kumphoseli waso, osihlwitha lula njengombana umuntu ohlala ehlathini ajikijela isibhamu sakhe ebodeni. Kuyisiko ukuba nama-harpoon amabili ahlala ngaphakathi kwesitho sangasese, ngokulandelana abizwa ngokuthi yi-iron yokuthoma neyesibili.

Kodwana ama-harpoon amabili la, enye nenye ngentambo yayo, zombili zihlangene nelayini; umnqopho bekukuthi: ukuziphosela bobabili, nangabe kungenzeka, omunye ngemva komunye ungene emkhomeni munye; ukwenzela bona nangabe, ekudonseni okuzako, omunye angakhipha, omunye angagcina ukubamba. Kuphinda kabili amathuba. Kodwana kuvame ukwenzeka bona ngebanga lokugijima komkhomo msinyana, okunobudlova, okuvuvukako lokha nakufumana i-ayini yokuthoma, kuba budisi bona umtjhayeli we-harpoon, nanyana ungaba njengombani kangangani ngokunyakaza kwakhe, aphosele insimbi yesibili kiyo. Nanyana kunjalo, njengombana insimbi yesibili sele ixhumene nelayini, begodu ilayini igijima, kungakho isikhali leso kufanele, kizo zoke izehlakalo, siphoswe ngaphandle kwesikepe, ngandlela thize begodu kwenye indawo; kungenjalo ingozi embi khulu ingabandakanya zoke izandla. Iwele emanzini, ngokufaneleko kuba njalo; amakhoyili webhokisi (akhulunywe esahlukweni esanduleleko) okwenza bona lokhu, esikhathini esinengi, kwenzeke ngokuhlakanipha. Kodwana isenzo esibucayi lesi asihlali singatjhejwa ngabantu abalimeleko abadabukisako nababulalako.

Ukudlula lapho: kufanele wazi bona lokha insimbi yesibili nayiphoselwa ngaphetjheya kwelwandle, kuba yinto elengako, ehlabako, ejikajikako kokubili isikepe nomkhomo, ibhidliza imigqa, nofana iyisike, begodu yenza umuzwa omangalisako kizo zoke iinhlangothi. Begodu, ngokuvamileko, akukghonakali ukuyivikela godu bekube kulapho umkhomo sele ubanjwe begodu ube sidumbu.

Cabanga, nje, bona kufanele kube njani endabeni yeenkepe ezine zoke ezisebenzisana nomkhomo owodwa onamandla ngokungakajayeleki, osebenzako, nokwaziko; lokha nangebanga leemfanelo ezikuye, kanye neengozi eziyinkulungwana ezihlangeneko zebhizinisi elinesibindi kangaka, iinsimbi ezisibhozo nofana ezilitjhumi zingahle zilenga ngasikhathi sinye. Ngombana, vele, isikepe ngasinye sinikelwa ama-harpoon amanengi ukugoba emgqeni nangabe esokuthoma singaphazanyiswa ngaphandle kokubuyiselwa. Zoke imininingwana le ilandiswa ngokuthembeka lapha, ngombana angeze yehluleka ukucacisa iindinyana ezinengi eziqakatheke khulu, nanyana ziyinkimbinkimbi kangangani, eenthombeni ezizokupendwa ngemva kwalokhu.





ISAHLUKO 64. Isidlo sakaStubb.

Umkhomo ka-Stubb bewubulawe kude nomkhumbi. Kwaba nokuthula; ngalokho, sakha isiqhema seenkepe ezintathu, sathoma ibhizinisi elibuthakathaka lokudosa indebe ukuya e-Pequod. Ngombana, emseleni omkhulu we-Hang-Ho, nofana nanyana yini abawubiza ngayo, e-China, abasebenzi abane nofana abahlanu endleleni yeenyawo bazokudosa isila esikhulu esithunyelwe ngebelo lemayela nge-awa; kodwana i-argosy ekulu ebesiyidonsa beyiragela phambili, ngasuthi ithwele umthofu wefarigi ngobunengi.

Kwafika ubumnyama; kodwana amalambu amathathu phezulu naphasi emtjhini omkhulu we-Pequod aqondisa indlela yethu; Kuselili 10 u-aliss 19. Waqala umkhomo ophaphazelako isikhatjhana, wakhipha imiyalo ejayelekileko yokuwuvikela ubusuku, bese wanikela isibani sakhe kumtjhayeli welwandle, wangena ngendlini, begodu azange abuye phambili bekwaba sekuseni.

Msinyana nje bewuzokucabanga ngomsindo emadekeni we-Pequod, bona zoke izandla bezilungiselela ukuphosa isikhonkwane emanzini atjhingako; ngombana amaketango anzima adoswa emkhunjini, begodu aphoselwa ngaphandle kweembobo. Kodwana ngalezo zixhumanisi ezizwakalako, isidumbu esikhulu ngokwaso, ingasi umkhumbi, kuzokubotjhwa. ISAHLUKO 65. Umkhomo njengesitsha.

Umuntu loyo kufanele adle isidalwa esidlisa isibane sakhe, begodu, njengo-Stubb, amdle ngokukhanya kwakhe, njengombana ungatjho; lokhu kubonakala kuyinto engakajayeleki kangangobana umuntu kufuze angene kancani emlandwini nefilosofi yayo.

Kubhalwe phasi, bona eminyakeni emakhulu amathathu eyadlulako ilimi le-Right Whale belihlonitjhwa njengesidlo esihle khulu e-France, begodu belibiza khulu lapho. Godu, ngesikhathi saka-Henry VIIIth, umpheki othileko wekhotho wathola umvuzo omuhle ngokusungula isosi ehle khulu ezokudliwa nama-porpoises aphekiweko, okuthi, uyakhumbula, mhlobo we-whale. Inyama yenziwa ibe ziibholo ezilingana namabholo we-billiard, begodu nayifakwa kuhle begodu ifakwe iinongo kungathathwa njengeebholo ze-turtle-ball nofana i-veal. Izindela zakade ze-Dunfermline bezizithanda khulu. Bebanesibonelelo esikhulu se-porpoise esivela embusweni.

Iqiniso kukuthi, hlangana nabazingeli bakhe okungenani, umkhomo bewuzokuthathwa njengesidlo esihle, ngathana bekungakabi khona; kodwana nawufika ukuhlala phasi phambi kwephayi yenyama engaba ziinyawo ezilikhulu ubude, kuyakususa ukudla. U-Zogranda, omunye wabodorhodere abadumileko, uncoma ama-blubber weentjalo ezincani, njengejusi khulu begodu ezondlako. Begodu lokhu kungikhumbuza bona amanye amaNgisi, egade atjhiywa ngephutha e-Greenland mkhumbi wokudoba imikhomo—ukobana amadoda la bekaphila iinyanga ezimbalwa ngeengcenye ezinesikhunta zemikhomo ebezitjhiywe eligwini ngemva kokuzama ukufumana i-blubber. Hlangana nama-whalemen we-Dutch iinsalela lezi zibizwa ngokuthi "ama-fritters"; okuyinto, kwamambala, efana khulu, ngokuba nsundu begodu eqhekezako, begodu inuka okufana nama-dough-nuts wamakhosikazi we-Amsterdam amadala nofana ama-oly-cooks, lokha nayifretjhi. Zinokubukeka okudliwako kangangobana umuntu ongamaziko oziphika khulu akakghoni ukubamba izandla zakhe.

Kodwana okwenza bona umkhomo ube phasi njengesidlo esiphucukileko, kukunotha kwawo okukhulu. Uyinkomo ekulu yomklomelo welwandle, ononileko khulu bona angaba muhle. Qala i-hump yakhe, ebeyingaba kuhle ukudla njengenyathi (okuthathwa njengesidlo esingakavami), ngathana bekungasi yipiramidi eqinileko yamafutha. Kodwana i-spermaceti ngokwayo, ukuthi imnandi kangangani; njengenyama emhlophe ekhanyako, ehlangeneko, emhlophe ye-coconut ngenyanga yesithathu yokukhula kwayo, kodwana inothile khulu ukobana ingathatha isikhundla sebhotela. Nanyana kunjalo, abantu abanengi abadosa imikhomo banendlela yokuyidosa ibe ngenye into, bese bayayidla. Esikhathini eside sokulinga ebusuku kuyinto ejayelekileko bona abasebenzi bemikhumbi bacwilise ibhisikidi yabo yemikhumbi emabhodweni amakhulu wamafutha bese bayivumela bona ithose lapho isikhatjhana. Ngenze ukudla kwantambama okunengi okuhle.

Endabeni ye-Sperm Whale encani ubuqhopho buthathwa njengesidlo esihle. Ibhokisi lekhanda liyaphulwa ngembazo, bese kuthi ama-lobe amabili amakhulu, amhlophe akhutjhwe (afana namapudingi amabili amakhulu), bese ahlanganiswa neflowuru, bese aphekwa abe yi-mess emnandi khulu, ngefleyiva efana nehloko yamathole, okuyisidlo esihle khulu hlangana nabanye abaphikisi; begodu woke umuntu uyazi bona amanye amadzinyana hlangana nama-epicure, ngokuhlala adla ubuqhopho bamathole, ngokukhamba kwesikhathi athola ubuqhopho bawo obuncani, ukwenzela bona akghone ukuhlukanisa ihloko yethole emahlombe wawo; okuthi, kwamambala, kutlhoga ukubandlululwa okungakajayeleki. Begodu kungakho imbuzi encani enekhanda lethole elibukeka lihlakaniphile phambi kwayo, ngenye yezinto ezidabukisako ongazibona. Ihloko imqala ngokumsola, ithi "Et tu Brute!" ihlathululo.

Mhlamunye, akusi ngombana umkhomo unamafutha khulu kangangobana abantu bendawo babonakala baqala ukudliwa kwawo ngokunyanya; lokho kubonakala kuphumela, ngandlela thize, ekucabangeleni okukhulunywe ngakho ngaphambilini: okutjho bona umuntu kufuze adle into esanda kubulawa yelwandle, begodu ayidle nayo ngokukhanya kwayo. Kodwana ngokungangabazeki umuntu wokuthoma owake wabulala inkabi bekathathwa njengombulali; mhlamunye bekalengiswe; begodu nangabe bekafakwe ekulingweni kwakhe ngeenkomo, nakanjani bekazokuba njalo; begodu bekafanelwe ngilokho nangabe kukhona umbulali okwenzako. Iya emakethe yenyama ngoMgqibelo ebusuku begodu ubone amaqoqo weenlwana eziphilako ezineenyawo ezimbili ziqale phezulu emigqeni emide yeenlwana ezineenyawo ezine ezifileko. Kghani ukubona lokho akukhiphi izinyo emhlathini womuntu odla abantu? Abadla abantu? ngubani ongasimuntu odla abantu? Ngiyakutjela bona kuzokubekezeleleka khulu ku-Fejee owabulala isithunywa sevangeli esincani ngaphakathi kweselula yakhe malungana nendlala ezako; kuzokubekezeleleka khulu ku-Fejee loyo, ngithi, ngelanga lokwahlulela, kunawe, umdlali ophucukileko nokhanyiselweko, obetha amahansi phasi begodu udle isibindi sawo esivuvukileko e-paté-de-foie-gras yakho.

Kodwana u-Stubb, udla umkhomo ngokukhanya kwawo, akunjalo? begodu lokho kungezelela ukuthuka ekulimaleni, akunjalo? Qala isibambo sakho sommese, lapho, umdlali wami ophucukileko nokhanyiselweko odla inyama yenkomo eyosiweko, isibambo leso senziwe ngani?—yini ngaphandle kwamathambo womfowabo wenkomo oyidlako? Begodu ukhetha ngani amazinyo wakho, ngemva kokudla irhansi elikhulupheleko? Ngensiba yenkukhu efanako. Begodu nguNobhala weNhlangano yokuGandelela ukuHlukunyezwa ku-Ganders waveza ngaliphi iphepha lakhe ngokusemthethweni? Kungenyanga edlulileko namkha ezimbili lapho umphakathi loyo udlulise khona isiqunto sokuthi kungabi nokusekelwa litho ngaphandle kwamapeni wensimbi.





ISAHLUKO 66. Ukubulawa kwamaShaka.

Lokha e-Southern Fishery, i-Sperm Whale ethunjiweko, ngemva kokusebenza budisi isikhathi eside, ilethwa ebusuku, akusiyo into evamileko, ukuragela phambili msinyana emsebenzini wokuyisika. Ngombana ibhizinisi lelo lisebenza budisi khulu; ayiqedwa msinyana; begodu kutlhoga bona zoke izandla ziqalise ngakho. Ngalokho-ke, ukusetjenziswa okujayelekileko kuthatha woke amaseyila; tjhatjhalaza i-helm a'lee; bese uthumela woke umuntu ongezansi e-hammock yakhe bekube semini, ngokugodla bona, bekube mhlokho, kuzokugcinwa ama-anchor-watch; okutjho bona, ezimbili nezimbili nge-awa, umbhangqwana ngamunye, isiqhema ngokujikeleza sizokukhwela i-deck ukubona bona koke kuhamba kuhle.

Kodwana kesinye isikhathi, khulukhulu e-Line e-Pacific, ihlelo leli angeze laphendula; ngombana iinqhema ezinengi kangaka zamatjhagi zibuthene zizombeleze isidumbu esibotjhweko, ukuthi ngathana bekatjhiywa njalo ama-awa asithandathu, sithi, ngokunwebeka, okuncani ukudlula ithambo ebelingabonakala ekuseni. Kwezinye iindawo ezinengi zelwandle, nanyana kunjalo, lapho iimfesi lezi zingabi khona khulu, ukudla kwazo okumangalisako ngezinye iinkhathi kungehliswa khulu, ngokuzivuselela ngamandla ngeenkezo ezibukhali zokudoba, ikambiso nanyana kunjalo, okuthi, kwezinye izehlakalo, kubonakala ngasuthi iyazikitaza bona zisebenze khulu. Kodwana bekungasinjalo endabeni yamanje ngamatjhagi we-Pequod; nanyana kunjalo, ukuqiniseka, nanyana ngiyiphi indoda engakajayeleki ukubona izinto ezinjalo, ebegade iqale ngehlangothini lakhe ngalobo busuku, beyizokucabanga bona ilwandle eliyindilinga beliyi-cheese ekulu, begodu amatjhagi lawo bekuyizibungu kiwo.

Nanyana kunjalo, lokha u-Stubb nakabeka isikhonkwane ngemva kokuthi isidlo sakhe sakusihlwa siphelile; begodu lokha, ngokufaneleko, u-Queequeg nomtjhayeli we-forecastle beza emkhunjini, kwaba nethabo elincani hlangana namatjhagi; ngombana msinyana bamisa amagadango wokusika ngapha nangapha, begodu behlisa amalambu amathathu, ukwenzela bona akhanyise ukukhanya okude phezu kwelwandle elimnyama, amatilosi amabili la, aphosela iinkezo zawo ezide zokudoba imikhomo, aragela phambili nokubulala amatjhagi,* ngokushaya insimbi ebukhali ngaphakathi kweenhloko zawo, kubonakala ingcenye yawo eqakathekileko kwaphela. Kodwana ekudidekeni okukhulu kwamabutho wabo ahlangahlangeneko begodu atlhagako, abahlaseli bebangakghoni ukushaya umkhawulo wabo ngaso soke isikhathi; begodu lokhu kwaletha izambulo ezitjha zolaka olumangalisako lwesitha. Baqhaqhazela khulu, ingasi kwaphela ekuphumeni kwamathumbu womunye nomunye, kodwana njengeminsalo eguquguqukako, egobileko, begodu iluma eyabo; bekwaba kulapho amathumbu lawo abonakala agwinywa ngokuphindaphindiweko mlomo munye, ukukhishwa ngokuphambeneko silonda esivulekileko. Bekungasikho koke lokhu. Bekungaphephi ukuzibandakanya nezidumbu nezipoki zezidalwa lezi. Uhlobo lokuphila okuvamileko nofana kwe-Pantheistic bekubonakala kucashe emalungwini wabo namathambo, ngemva kokuthi lokho okungabizwa ngokuthi yipilo yomuntu ngamunye sele kuphelile. Ebulawa begodu aphakanyiswa emkhunjini ngebanga lesikhumba sakhe, enye yamashaka la pheze yathatha isandla saka-Queequeg, lokha nakazama ukuvala isivalo somhlathi wakhe wokubulala.

*I-whale-spede esetjenziselwa ukusika yenziwe ngensimbi ehle khulu; Wazama ukusiza ukuvulala, i-Ama. begodu ngobujamo obujayelekileko, buhambisana nesisetjenziswa sengadi esibizwa ngaso; amahlangothi wayo kwaphela ayisicaba, begodu umphetho wayo ongaphezulu mncani khulu kunalowo ongezansi. Isikhali lesi sihlale sigcinwa sibukhali ngendlela ekungakghoneka ngayo; begodu lokha nayisetjenziswako iyatjhugululwa ngezinye iinkhathi, njengesikere. Ngaphakathi kwesokhethi yayo, isigodo esiqinileko, ukusuka emafidini amatjhumi amabili ukuya kwamatjhumi amathathu ubude, sifakwa isibambo.

"U-Queequeg akanandaba nokuthi ngubani unkulunkulu owenze waba yishaka," kwatjho isilwane, siphakamisa isandla sakhe phezulu naphasi ngokubuhlungu; "uZimu ka-Fejee nofana uZimu we-Nantucket; kodwana uZimu owenza ishakhi kufuze kube yidamu elilodwa Ingin."
