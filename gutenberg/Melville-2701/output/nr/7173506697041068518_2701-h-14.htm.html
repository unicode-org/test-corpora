Nje-ke umsebenzi uyaragela phambili; ama-tackle amabili aphakamisa nokwehlisa ngasikhathi sinye; kokubili umkhomo kanye ne-windlass ukuphakama, abaphakamisi bayahlabelela, amadoda wegumbi le-blubber ayagoqa, abafazi bayagoqa, umkhumbi uyagandeleleka, begodu zoke izandla zithuka ngezinye iinkhathi, ngendlela yokuthoba ukungezwani okujayelekileko.





ISAHLUKO 68. Ingubo.

Nginikele ngetjhejo elincani esihlokweni esingakakhathazeki, isikhumba somkhomo. Ngibe neempikiswano ngakho nabantu abanelwazi elinama-whaleman, kanye nabafundi bezemvelo abaselwandle. Umbono wami wekuthomeni awutjhuguluki; kodwana ngumbono kwaphela.

Umbuzo kukuthi, khuyini begodu kuphi isikhumba somkhomo? Sele uyazi bona i-blubber yakhe iyini. I-blubber leyo yinto efana nenyama yenkomo eqinileko, eneenhlamvu eziseduze, kodwana iqinile, iqinile begodu ihlanganiswe, begodu isukela emasentimitheni abunane nofana alitjhumi ukuya kelitjhumi nambili ukuya kelitjhumi nahlanu ngobukhulu.

Nje, nanyana kungabonakala kungakaqakatheki kangangani ukukhuluma ngesikhumba sanoma ngisiphi isidalwa njengesifana nokuqina, kodwana eqinisweni lokhu akusizimpikiswano eziphikisana nokucabanga okunjalo; ngombana angeze waphakamisa enye isendlalelo esiqinileko esigoqela emzimbeni womkhomo kodwana ifutha elifanako; begodu isendlalelo esingaphandle khulu sanoma ngisiphi isilwana, nangabe siqinile, kungaba yini lokho ngaphandle kwesikhumba? Kuliqiniso, emzimbeni womkhomo ongalimalanga, ungakhuhla ngesandla sakho into encani khulu, ekhanyako, efana neengcezu ezincani khulu ze-isinglass, kodwana ipheze ibe lula begodu ithambile njenge-satin; okutjho bona, ngaphambi kokobana yomiswe, lokha nayingagcini ngokunciphisa begodu iqine, kodwana iba budisi begodu iphuke. Nginezinto ezinengi ezomisiweko, engizisebenzisela amamaki eencwadini zami zemikhomo. Kusobala, njengombana ngitjho ngaphambilini; begodu ngokubekwa ekhasini eligadangisiweko, ngezinye iinkhathi ngizithokozise ngokucabanga bona inomthelela omkhulu. Nanyana kukuphi, kumnandi ukufunda ngemikhomo ngezibuko zayo, njengombana ungatjho. Kodwana engikutjhoko lapha ngilokhu. Into efanako encani engapheliko, eyi-isinglass, ethi, ngiyavuma, ifaka umzimba woke womkhomo, akukafaneli ithathwe njengesikhumba sesidalwa, njengesikhumba sesikhumba, ngaleyo ndlela; ngombana bekungasibulula ukutjho, bona isikhumba esifaneleko somkhomo omkhulu sincani begodu sithambile ukudlula isikhumba somntwana osanda ukubelethwa. Kodwana akusenalokhu.

Ukuthatha bona i-blubber yisikhumba somkhomo; bese, lokha isikhumba lesi, njenge-Sperm Whale ekulu khulu, sizokukhiqiza ubunengi bemiphongolo eyikhulu yamafutha; begodu, lokha nakucatshangelwa bona, ngobunengi, nofana ubudisi, amafutha lawo, ebujameni bawo obuveziweko, angaba ziingcenye ezintathu kwaphela, ingasi yoke into yejazi; umbono othileko ungaba khona ngobukhulu besisindo esiphilako, ingcenye nje yesikhumba sayo esikhiqiza ichibi lamanzi njengalelo. Nawubala imigqomo elitjhumi ngethani, unamathani alitjhumi wesisindo samakota amathathu wesikhumba somkhomo.

Epilweni, ubuso obubonakalako be-Sperm Whale akusikho okuncani hlangana neemangaliso ezinengi azivezako. Pheze ngokungaguqukiko yoke indawo iphambaniswe begodu iphambukelwe godu ngamatshwayo aqondileko amanengi khulu, into efana nalawo asemifanekisweni emihle khulu ye-Italy. Kodwana amatshwayo la awabonakali abhalwe phezu kwento ye-isinglass ekhulunywe ngehla, kodwana abonakala ngathi abonakala kiyo, kube ngasuthi aqoshwe emzimbeni ngokwawo. Begodu akusikho koke lokhu. Kwezinye izehlakalo, emehlweni akhawulezako, aqapheleko, lawo matshwayo asemgqeni, njengokuqopha kwamambala, kodwana anikela indawo yokwenza eminye imidwebo. Lokhu kunama-hieroglyphic; okutjho bona, nangabe ubiza ama-cyphers angazwisisekiko emabodeni wamaphiramidi ngokuthi ma-hieroglyphics, khona-ke lelo ligama elifaneleko lokusebenzisa ekuxhumaneni kwanje. Ngokukhumbula kwami ​​okuqinileko kwama-hieroglyphics phezu kwe-Sperm Whale eyodwa, ngatjhaywa khulu ngepuleti elijamele iinhlamvu zakade zama-India ezitlolwe emaphepheni we-hieroglyphic adumileko emaphethelweni we-Upper Mississippi. Njengamatje lawo angaziwako, nawo, umkhomo onetshwayo eliyimfihlakalo uhlala ungazwisiseki. Lokhu kutjho amatje we-India kungikhumbuza enye into. Ngaphandle kwazo zoke ezinye izehlakalo ezivezwa ngaphandle kwe-Sperm Whale, akavami ukutjengisa umhlane, begodu khulukhulu amahlangothi wakhe, asuswe khulu ukubukeka okuvamileko, ngebanga lemihuzuko eminengi engakalungi, yoke into engakajayeleki, engakahleleki. Ngingatjho bona amadwala lawo we-New England asegwini lelwandle, u-Agassiz acabanga bona anamatshwayo wokuthintana okunobudlova namaqhwa amakhulu antantako—ngingathi, amadwala lawo akukafaneli afane ne-Sperm Whale kilokhu. Kubonakala kimi nokuthi imihuzuko enjalo emkhomeni mhlamunye yenziwa ngokuthintana okumbi neminye imikhomo; ngombana ngizitjhejile khulu eenkunzini ezinkulu, ezikhulileko zomhlobo lo.

Igama elilodwa nofana amabili malungana nendaba le yesikhumba nofana amafutha womkhomo. Sekutjhiwo, bona ikhutjhwe kuye ngeengcezu ezide, ezibizwa ngokuthi ziingcezu zengubo. Njengamagama amanengi we-sea, le ithabile khulu begodu iqakathekile. Ngombana umkhomo ugoqwe ngamafutha wawo njengengubo yamambala nofana isikhumba; nofana, okungcono khulu, i-poncho ye-India yatjhelela phezu kwehloko yakhe, begodu yadlula umphetho wakhe. Kungenxa yokuvala umzimba wakhe, ukuthi umkhomo ukghone ukuzigcina ukhululekile kibo boke ubujamo bezulu, kiwo woke amalwandle, iinkhathi, namagagasi. Bekuzokwenzekani ngomkhomo we-Greenland, sithi, emalwandle lawo athuthumelako, anamaqhwa weTlhagwini, nangabe awunakwa i-surtout yakhe ethokomeleko? Qala iingazi zakhe, begodu uyafa. Kuhle kangangani-ke—ngaphandle kokuchazwa—ukobana isilwane esikhulu, lapho ukufuthumala komzimba kuqakathekile njengombana kunjalo emuntwini; kuhle kangangani bona angatholakala ekhaya, acwiliswe emlonyeni wakhe ipilo yoke emanzini we-Arctic! lapho, lokha abasebenzi bemikhumbi nawela ngaphasi kwelwandle, kwesinye isikhathi batholakala, ngemva kweenyanga, baqhwa ngokuqondileko eenhliziyweni zamasimu weqhwa, njengombana impukane itholakala inamathele ku-amber. Kodwana okurarako khulu kukwazi, njengombana kufakazelwe ngokulinga, bona iingazi ze-Polar whale zifuthumele ukudlula ze-Borneo negro ehlobo.

Kubonakala kimi, ukuthi lapha sibona ubuhle obungakajayeleki bomuntu ngamunye oqinileko, nobuhle obungakajayeleki bamaboda aqinileko, kanye nobuhle obungakajayeleki bokuvuleka kwangaphakathi. O, ndoda! zithokozise begodu uzilingise njengomkhomo! Nawe, hlala ufuthumele hlangana neqhwa. Ingabe nawe, uphila ephasini lo ngaphandle kokuba ngowalo. Yiba pholile e-ikhwetha; gcina uketshezi lwegazi lakho e-Pole. Njenge-dome ekulu ye-St. Peter's, begodu njenge-whale ekulu, gcina, O muntu! kizo zoke iinkhathi zomnyaka amazinga womtjhiso wakho.

Kodwana kulula kangangani begodu kunganathemba kangangani ukufundisa izinto ezihle lezi! Ngokwakhiwa, bambalwa kangangani abafana ne-St. Peter's! yezidalwa, zimbalwa kangangani ezinkulu njengomkhomo!





ISAHLUKO 69. Umngcwabo.

“Dosa amaketani! Vumela isidumbu siye ngemuva!”

Ama-tackle amakhulu sele enze umsebenzi wawo. Umzimba omhlophe ohlutshiweko womkhomo onqunywe ihloko uphazima njengethuna lemabula; nanyana itjhugulukile ngombala, ayikalahlekelwa litho ngobunengi. Iseyinkulu khulu. Kancanikancani iyatjhinga itjhidela kude, amanzi ayizungezileko adatshulwa begodu atjhatjalaliswa mashaka angasuthi, begodu umoya ongaphezulu uhlushwa ziinkukhu ezirhuwelelako, imilomo yazo ifana nama-poniard amanengi ahlambalazako emkhomeni. I-phantom ekulu emhlophe enganahloko intanta iqhubekela phambili ukusuka emkhunjini, begodu yoke induku eyintantako, lokho okubonakala njengeenqhema eziyisikwere zamatjhagi kanye neenqhema zeenkukhu, zikhulisa umsindo wokubulala. Kwaphela ama-awa amanengi ukusuka emkhunjini opheze umile umbono omumbi loyo ubonakala. Ngaphasi kwesibhakabhaka esinganamafu nesithambileko, ebusweni obuhle belwandle elimnandi, eliphephethekiswa mimoya ejabulisako, ubunengi obukhulu bokufa buzulazula, bekube kulapho bulahleka emibonweni engapheliko.

Begodu akusikuphela lokhu. Njengombana umzimba ungcolile, isipoki esiziphindiselako siyasinda begodu siphaphazela phezu kwawo ukuthusa. Ihlolwe yindoda yepi eneenhloni namkha isikepe esiphambukako esivela kude, lokha ibanga elifiphaza iinkukhu ezihlangeneko, nanyana kunjalo lisatjengisa ubukhulu obumhlophe obuphaphazela elangeni, kanye nesifutho esimhlophe esiphakama phezulu ngokujamelana naso; msinyana isidumbu somkhomo esingalimaziko, ngeminwe ethuthumelako sibekwe phasi esihlahleni— amadwala, amadwala, nama-breakers lapha: tjheja! Bekungakafaneli bona kutjhiywe bona ngaphambi kokukhumula umzimba weleviyathani, waqunywa ikhanda. Nje, ukuqunywa kwehloko ye-Sperm Whale kuyinto yezesayensi, lapho abodorhodere abanolwazi be-whale bazikhakhazisa khona: begodu ingasi ngaphandle kwesizathu.

Khumbula, godu, bona udorhodera ohlinzako kufuze ahlinze phezulu, amamitha abunane nofana alitjhumi phakathi kwakhe nesihloko sakhe, begodu umuntu loyo upheze afihleke elwandle olutjhugulukileko, oluguquguqukako, begodu kanengi oluphithizelako noluqhumako. Awumangali na,-ke, ngokuzikhakhazisa kwaka-Stubb, ukuthi wafuna imizuzu elitjhumi kwaphela ukuquma ikhanda lomkhomo wesidoda?

Lokha nayiqunywa kokuthoma, ihloko iyalahlwa ngemuva bese ibanjwa lapho ngekheyibuli bekube kulapho umzimba ukhutjhwa khona. Lokho kwenziwa, nangabe ngeyomkhomo omncani iphakanyiswa emkhunjini ukuze ilahlwe ngamabomu. Kodwana, nge-leviathani ekhulileko lokhu akukghonakali; ngombana ihloko ye-sperm whale ihlanganisa pheze ingcenye eyodwa kwezintathu yobukhulu bayo boke, begodu ukumisa umthwalo onjengalowo, ngitjho nangokugandeleleka okukhulu komdosi we-whale, lokhu bekuyinto enganalitho njengokulinga ukulinganisa isibaya se-Dutch ngesikali sabathengisi bamagugu.

Umkhomo we-Pequod nakaqunywa ikhanda begodu umzimba wawo ukhutjhwe, ihloko yayo yaphakanyiselwa ehlangothini lomkhumbi—pheze ibe yingcenye yokuphuma elwandle, ukwenzela bona ibe yingcenye ekulu yokuphakanyiswa yinto yayo yemvelo. Bese kuthi lapho isikepe esigandelelekileko sitjhinge khulu kiso, ngebanga lokudosa okukhulu okuya phasi okuvela ekhanda le-mast eliphasi, begodu yoke ingalo yeyadi ngaleyo hlangothi iphuma njengekhreyini phezu kwamagagasi; lapho, ihloko leyo econsa iingazi gade ilenga esinqeni saka-Pequod njengo-Holofernes omkhulu ovela ebhandeni laka-Judith.

Lokha umsebenzi wokugcina nawuqediweko bekusemini, begodu abasebenzi bemikhumbi baya phasi ukuya esidlweni sabo sakusihlwa. Kwaba nokuthula phezu kwendawo ebegade inesiphithiphithi kodwana nje sele inganamuntu. Ukuthula okukhulu kwethusi, njenge-lotus ephuzi yendawo yoke, bekuragela phambili nokuvula amakari wayo angenamdumo elwandle.

Kwadlula isikhala esifitjhani, begodu phakathi kwalokhu kungabi nomsindo kwangena u-Ahabi yedwa avela endlini yakhe. Ukuthatha amajika ambalwa emphemeni wekota, wajama ukuqala ngehlangothini, bese wangena kancanikancani ngaphakathi kwamaketango amakhulu wathatha isipeyidi saka-Stubb—esasele lapho ngemva kokuqunywa kwekhanda lomkhomo—bese wasishaya engcenyeni engezansi yesisindo esingalengisiwe, wabeka enye induku yayo ngaphasi komkhondo owodwa, ngamehlo atjhinga ngaphezulu kwamehlo uqale khulu ekhanda leli.

Bekuyikhanda elimnyama nelinesigqoko; begodu ukulenga lapho phakathi kokuthula okukhulu kangaka, bekubonakala ngasuthi i-Sphynx isehlane. Ngaleso sikhathi, u-Fedallah bekaqale ngokuthula ikhanda lomkhomo wesokudla, begodu bekahlala aqale ukusuka emibimbini ejulileko ukuya emigqeni esandleni sakhe. U-Ahabi wajama kangangobana umPharisi wahlala esithunzini sakhe; kuthi, nangabe isithunzi se-Parsee besikhona bekubonakala ngasuthi sihlangana, begodu sikhulise esika-Ahabi. Njengombana isiqhema besisebenza budisi, ukuqagela kwe-Laplandish kwahlanganiswa hlangana nabo, malungana nazo zoke izinto ezidlulako.
