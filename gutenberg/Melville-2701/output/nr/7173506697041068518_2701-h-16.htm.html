ISAHLUKO 81. I-Pequod Ihlangana Nentombi.

Ilanga ebelibekwe ngaphambilini lafika, begodu sahlangana nomkhumbi u-Jungfrau, u-Derick De Deer, umphathi, we-Bremen.

Ngesinye isikhathi bebabantu abakhulu khulu bokudoba imikhomo ephasini, ama-Dutch nama-German njengamanje ahlangana nabantu abancani; kodwana lapha nalaphaya ngeenkhathi ezibanzi ze-latitude ne-longitude, uhlala uhlangana nefulegi labo e-Pacific.

Ngasizathu esithileko, u-Jungfrau bekabonakala afuna ukumhlonipha. Lokha nakasekude ne-Pequod, wajika waya, begodu wawisa isikepe, ukaputeni wakhe watjhidelelwa kithi, ajame ngokuphelelwa sineke emaphethelweni esikhundleni sesikepe.

“Uphethe ini esandleni sakhe lapho?” kwamemeza u-Starbuck, akhomba into ebeyibanjwe mJamani. "Angeze kwenzeka!—umthombo wesibani!"

"Awa lokho," kwatjho u-Stubb, "awa, awa, yimbiza yekhofi, Mnumzana Starbuck; uzakuzosenzela ikofi yethu, ngu-Yarman; awuboni ithini elikhulu lapho eceleni kwakhe?—lawo ngamanzi wakhe abilako. Oh! ulungile, ngu-Yarman."

"Khamba nawe," kwamemeza u-Flask, "ngumtjhini wokuphakela amalambu nekani lamafutha. Uphelelwe mafutha, begodu uze ukuzobawa."

Nanyana kungabonakala kumangalisa kangangani bona umkhumbi we-oli uboleke amafutha ephasini lemikhomo, begodu nanyana kungaphikisana kangangani nesaga esidala sokuthwala amalahle ukuya e-Newcastle, kodwana kesinye isikhathi into enjalo yenzeka kwamambala; begodu endabeni yamanje uKaputeni u-Derick De Deer ngokungathandabuzekiko wenza umtjhini wokuphakela amalambu njengombana u-Flask atjho.

Njengombana akhwela umkhumbi, u-Ahabi wavele wamhlasela, anganaki bona uphetheni; kodwana ngelimi lakhe eliphukileko, umJarimani msinyana watjengisa ukungazi kwakhe ngokupheleleko nge-White Whale; msinyana watjhugulula ingcoco waya esibaneni sakhe sokudla kanye nekani lamafutha, ngamazwana athileko athinta ukuphendukela kwakhe ebusuku ebumnyameni obukhulu—ithonsi lakhe lokugcina lamafutha we-Bremen besele liphelile, begodu akukho ngitjho nenye ifesi endizako ebanjwe ukuvala ukutlhayela; waphetha ngokuthi umkhumbi wakhe bekuyinto e-Fishery ebizwa ngokuthi muhlanzekile (okutjho bona, enganalitho), efanele ibizo elithi Jungfrau nofana iNtombi.

Iintlhogo zakhe zatholwa, u-Derick wasuka; kodwana bekangakazuzi ihlangothi lomkhumbi wakhe, lokha imikhomo nayiphakanyiswa pheze ngasikhathi sinye eenhloko zemikhumbi yomibili; begodu u-Derick bekalangazelela ukujaha, kangangobana ngaphandle kokujama ukufaka ikani lakhe lamafutha ne-lamp-feeder ngaphakathi, wajikeleza isikepe sakhe begodu wenza ngemva kwe-leviathan-lamp-feeders.

Nje, umdlalo sele ukhuphukele emoyeni, yena nezinye iinkepe ezintathu ze-Germany ezamlandela msinyana, bebanesiqalo esikhulu se-Pequod's keels. Bekunemikhomo ebunane, i-pod ephakathi. Njengombana bazi ngobungozi babo, bebakhamba ngebelo elikhulu ngqo phambi komoya, bahlikihla amahlangothi wabo eduze njengamahhashi amanengi ahlanganisiweko. Batjhiya umvuko omkhulu, obanzi, ngasuthi baragela phambili nokuvula isikhumba esikhulu esibanzi elwandle.

Ngokuzeleko ekuvukeni okurhabako, begodu ngama-fathoms amanengi ngemva, kwabhukuda inkunzi endala enkulu, eneenhluthu, eyathi ngokuthuthuka kwayo okubuthakathaka, kanye namathumba angakajayeleki aphuzi egade ikhula phezu kwayo, bekubonakala ngasuthi ihlaselwe yi-jaundice, nofana obunye ubuthakathaka. Ukuthi umkhomo lo bewungowe-pod ngaphambilini, bekubonakala kubuzwa; ngombana akusiyo isiko bona ama-leviathani ahloniphekileko abe nomphakathi. Nanyana kunjalo, wabambelela ekuvukeni kwabo, nanyana amanzi wabo wangemuva bekufuze bona amvimbe, ngombana ithambo elimhlophe nofana ukuvuvuka emlonyeni wakhe obanzi bekuyinto ephukileko, njengokuvuvuka okwakhiwa lokha nakuhlangana imisinga emibili enobutha. Umlomo wakhe bewumfitjhani, ubuthakathaka, begodu usebenza budisi; iphuma ngokugandeleleka, begodu iziqeda ibe ziingcenye ezidabukileko, kulandelwe ziimphithiphithi ezingakajayeleki zangaphasi kwehlabathi kuye, ebezibonakala ziphuma ngakelinye ihlangothi lakhe elingcwatjiweko, okwenza bona amanzi ngemva kwakhe avuvuke.

“Ngubani onesipharego?” Kutjho u-Stubb, "unesisu esibuhlungu, ngiyasaba. Nkosi, cabanga ngokuba nesisu esiyingcenye ye-acre! Imimoya emimbi ibamba uKresmusi kuye, bafana. Ngomoya omumbi wokuthoma engiwaziko bona uvunguza ukusuka e-astern; kodwana qala, ingabe umkhomo wake waphakama kanjalo ngaphambilini, kufuze bona ulahlekelwe yi-tiller yakhe."

Njengomuntu we-India othwele umthwalo omkhulu othwele phasi eligwini le-Hindostan ngeenqhema ezigcwele amahhashi athukiweko, akhalela, angcwaba, agijima, begodu agubha endleleni yakhe; kwaba njalo nomkhomo omdala waphakamisa umzimba wawo omdala, begodu ngaleso sikhathi uphenduka ngeembambo zawo ezinzima, waveza imbangela yokuvuka kwawo okukhohlisako esiqwini esingakajayeleki sesiphephelo sakhe se-starboard. Kwaba budisi ukutjho bona walahlekelwa siphiko leso epini, namkha wabelethwa ngaphandle kwaso.

"Linda kancani, mfana omdala, ngizokunikela isikhonkwane sengalo elimeleko," kwamemeza u-Flask ononya, akhomba emgqeni wemikhomo eduze kwakhe.

"Qaphela bona angakuphoseli ngakho," kwamemeza u-Starbuck. "Nikela, ngaphandle kwalokho umJamani uzomthatha."

Ngenhloso eyodwa zoke iinkepe ezihlanganisiweko beziqondiswe kile mfesi yinye, ngombana bekungasi mkhulu kwaphela, begodu bekumkhomo oqakatheke khulu, kodwana bekaseduze nawo, begodu eminye imikhomo beyikhamba ngebelo elikhulu, ngaphezu kwalokho, pheze beyizokuphikisana nokusukelwa ngesikhatheso. Ngaleso sikhathi ama-keel we-Pequod begade adutshulwe ziinkepe ezintathu ze-German ezagcina ukwehliswa; kodwana kusukela ekuthomeni okuhle, isikepe saka-Derick besisolo sidosa phambili ukujaha, nanyana ngaso soke isikhathi besisondela eembangi zakhe zangaphandle. Into ebegade bayisaba, bekukukuthi, ngombana besele aseduze kangaka, uzokukghona ukudubula insimbi yakhe ngaphambi kobana bamdlule begodu bamdlule. Ngokuphathelene no-Derick, bekabonakala aqiniseka bona lokhu kuzokuba njalo, begodu ngezinye iinkhathi ngesenzo sokuhlekisa bekathintitha isibane sakhe kwezinye iinkepe.

“Inja enganamusa nengathokoziko!” kwamemeza u-Starbuck; "Uyangihleka begodu ungiqinisa ngebhokisi elimbi khulu engimgcwalise lona kungakapheli imizuzu emihlanu eyadlulako!"—bese ngokunyenyeza kwakhe okuqinileko okudala—"Vukelani, zinja! Inja kiyo!"

"Ngiyanitjela bona kuyini, madoda" - kwamemeza u-Stubb esiqhemeni sakhe - "kuphikisana nekolo yami ukuthukuthela; kodwana ngingathanda ukudla u-Yarman okhohlakeleko—Pull—angeze? Nizokuvumela isigebengu leso sikubethe? Niyayithanda na i-brandy? I-hoghead ye-brandy, ke, kumuntu omuhle khulu Wozani, kubayini ningaqhumi igazi? Ngubani loyo obegade aphosela isikhonkwane ngaphasi kwelwandle—asithulile, nansi utjani bukhula ngaphasi kwesikepe—begodu ngeKosi, insika lapho iyahluma Lokhu angeze kwakghonakala, bafana!

“Hawu! qala amagwebu awenzako!” kwamemeza u-Flask, agida phezulu naphasi—“Yiphi i-hump—Oh, yenza inyama yenkomo—ilele njengesigodo! O! bafana bami, yenzani intwasahlobo—ama-slap-jacks nama-quahogs ngesidlo sakusihlwa, niyazi, bafana bami—ama-clams abhakiweko nama-muffin—oh, yenza , yenza , intwasahlobo,—uyikhulu lamabhareli—ungamlahli—oh ! —bona lokho Yarman—Oh , angeze wakghona ukudosa imali yenu , madoda !

Ngaleso sikhathi u-Derick bekasesenzweni sokuphosa isibani sakhe esiphakela iinkepe ebegade ziragela phambili, kanye nekani lakhe lamafutha; mhlamunye ngombono ophindwe kabili wokubuyisela emuva indlela yabaphikisi bakhe, begodu ngesikhathi esifanako akhuphule eyakhe ngokwezomnotho ngomfutho wesikhatjhana wokuphosela emuva.

“Inja ye-Dutch enganasimilo!” kwamemeza u-Stubb. "Donsani nje, madoda, njengeenkulungwana ezimatjhumi amahlanu zemikhumbi yepi yamademoni aneenhluthu ezibomvu. Nithini, Tashtego; ingabe uyindoda ezokuphula umgogodla wakho ube ziingcezu ezingamatjhumi amabili namashumi amabili ukuhlonipha u-Gayhead omdala? Nithini?"

"Ngithi, dosa njengedamu likaZimu,"—kwamemeza umNdiya.

Ngolaka, kodwana ngokulinganako ukukhuthazwa ziinhlamba zamaJalimane, iinkepe ezintathu ze-Pequod zathoma ukutjhinga ndawonye; begodu, azimisele kangaka, watjhidela kuye isikhatjhana. Ngaleso simo esihle, esikhululekileko, sobuqhawe somphathi lokha nakatjhidela eduze nenyamazana yakhe, abalingani abathathu basukuma ngokuzikhakhazisa, ngezinye iinkhathi basekela umgwedli ngemva kwalokho ngokurhuwelela okuthabisako okuthi, "Nanyana uyatjhelela, nje! Hurray ngomlotha omhlophe! Phansi ne-Yarman! Mkhumbi phezu kwakhe!"

Kodwana wathatha isiqunto sokuqala ngathana u-Derick, ukuthi nangaphandle kobuqhawe babo, bekazokuveza umnqobi emncintiswaneni lo, ngathana isahlulelo esilungileko esingakazehlele phezu kwakhe ngekala eyabamba ibhande lomgwedli wakhe wesikepe. Ngesikhathi umtjhayeli lo ongakalungi alwela ukutjhaphulula umlotha wakhe omhlophe, begodu ngesikhathi, umphumela, isikepe saka-Derick besiseduze nokuguquka, begodu waduma emadodeni wakhe ngokuthukuthela okukhulu;—lokho bekusikhathi esihle ku-Starbuck, u-Stubb, no-Flask. Ngokurhuwelela, bathatha isiqalo esihle ukuya phambili, begodu batjhinga phezulu ekoteni ye-German. Kwaba mzuzwana, begodu zoke iinkepe ezine bezitjhinga emkhomeni, lokha nazitjhingela kizo, emahlangothini womabili, bekunomtjhini owenza amagwebu.

Bekuyinto emangalisako, edabukisako, begodu ehlanyako. Umkhomo besele uyaphuma, begodu uthumela umlomo wawo phambi kwawo nge-jet ehlunguphazako; ngesikhathi isiphiko sakhe esisodwa esimbi sishaya ihlangothi lakhe ngokuzwa ubuhlungu obukhulu. Nje-ke esandleni lesi, njeke esandleni sakhe, watjhinga ekuphapheni kwakhe, begodu kiwo woke amagagasi aphukileko, bekatjhinga elwandle, nofana watjhingela esibhakabhakeni. Nje-ke ngike ngabona inyoni enephiko eliqunyiweko yenza imibuthano ephukileko emoyeni, ilwela ukubalekela ama-hawk weenlwanyana. Kodwana inyoni inephimbo, begodu ngokurhuwelela okuzwakalako izokuveza ukwesaba kwayo; kodwana ukwesaba kwesilwane esikhulu esiyisimungulu selwandle, bekubotjhwe ngamaketani begodu kwathandwa kuye; bekanganalo ilizwi, ngaphandle kokuphefumula okugandelelako nge-spiracle yakhe, begodu lokhu kwenza ukubona kwakhe kube buhlungu khulu; ngitjho nanyana, ngobukhulu bakhe obumangalisako, umhlathi omkhulu, nomsila onamandla woke, bekunokwaneleko ukuthusa indoda enamandla khulu eyayizwela kangaka.

Ukubona nje bona kodwana imizuzwana embalwa khulu izokwenza bona iinkepe ze-Pequod zibe nethuba, begodu kunokuthi aphumelele emdlalweni wakhe, u-Derick wakhetha ukufaka engozini lokho kuye okufuze bona bekubonakala njengomcibisholo omude khulu, ngaphambi kobana ithuba lokugcina liphunyuke.

Kodwana kungakapheli sikhathi lapho umdlali wakhe we-harpoone ajame ukuhlaselwa, kwathi zoke iingwe ezintathu—u-Queequeg, u-Tashtego, u-Daggoo—zasukuma ngokuzenzakalelako, begodu zijame emgqeni otjhingako, zakhomba iinhlaba zazo ngasikhathi sinye; begodu bagijimela phezu kwehloko yomtjhayeli we-German, iinsimbi zabo ezintathu ze-Nantucket zangena emkhomeni. Umhwamuko ophuphuthekisako wegwebu nomlilo omhlophe! Iinkepe ezintathu, ngokuthukuthela kokuthoma kokugijima komkhomo, zatjhayisana ne-German ngamandla, kangangobana bobabili u-Derick nomtjhayeli wakhe othukileko bachitheka ngaphandle, begodu bakhamba ngeenkepe ezintathu eziphaphako.

“Ningasabi, amabhokisi wami webhotela,” kwamemeza u-Stubb, abaqala njengombana adlula; "Nizokulandwa msinyana—kulungile—ngibone amatjhagi asemuva—izinja ze-St. Bernard, uyazi—zikhulula abahambi abagandelelekileko. Hurrah! le yindlela yokukhamba ngesikepe nje. I-keel ngayinye ilanga! Hurrah!—Nasi sihamba njengamaketle amathathu emsileni we-madgare ungifake engqondweni! indlovu ethafeni—yenza bona amasondo aphapheme ngaleyondlela;

Kodwana ukugijima kwesilo bekukufitjhani. Waphefumulela phezulu ngokuzumayo, wazwakala ngokuphithizela. Ngokurhaba okukhulu, imigqa emithathu yaphaphazela izungeze iinhloko zemigodo ngamandla kangangobana enza imigodi ejulileko kiyo; ngitjho nanyana abadlali be-harpoon bebasaba khulu ukuthi ukuzwakala okurhabako lokhu kuzokuqeda imigqa msinyana, kangangobana basebenzisa woke amandlabo, babamba amajika wokubhema aphindaphindiweko ngentambo yokubamba; bekwaba sekugcineni—ngenxa yokugandeleleka okujameleko okuvela ema-chocks wesikepe, lapho iintambo ezintathu ziya khona ngqo ehlaza okwesibhakabhaka—ama-gunwales wama-bows bekapheze alingana namanzi, ngesikhathi ama-sterns amathathu atjhinga phezulu emoyeni. Begodu umkhomo msinyana waphela ukuzwakala, isikhathi esithileko bahlala ngaleso simo, besaba ukusebenzisa umugqa omnengi, nanyana ubujamo bebukitaza kancani. Kodwana nanyana iinkepe zithathwe phasi zalahleka ngalendlela, nanyana kunjalo "ukubambelela," njengombana kubizwa njalo; ukubophelela lokhu ngamahlaba abukhali wenyama yakhe ephilako ngemuva; lokhu ngikho okuvame ukuhlunguphaza uLeviyathani bona avuke msinyana godu ukuhlangabezana nomkhonto obukhali wezitha zakhe. Kodwana singakhulumi ngobungozi bento, kufanele kube nokungabaza bona indlela le ihlale ingcono khulu na; ngombana kunengqondo ukucabanga bona, lokha umkhomo ohlasiweko nawuhlala isikhathi eside ngaphasi kwamanzi, uyadinwa khulu. Ngombana, ngebanga lobukhulu bakhe—e-sperm whale ekhule ngokuzeleko into engaphasi kwama-square feet ama-2000—umfutho wamanzi mkhulu khulu. Soke siyazi bona thina ngokwethu sijame ngaphasi kwesisindo esimangalisako kangangani; ngitjho nalapha, ngaphezulu kwephasi, emoyeni; mkhulu kangangani-ke, umthwalo womkhomo, othwele emhlane wakhe ikholomu yama-fathoms amakhulu amabili welwandle! Kufanele okungenani ibe nesisindo sama-atmospheres amatjhumi amahlanu. Omunye umdosi wemikhomo ulinganisele ubudisi bemikhumbi ematjhumi amabili yepi, nazo zoke iinbhamu zayo, neentolo, nabantu abasesikepeni.

Njengoba iinkepe ezintathu zilele lapho elwandle eliguquguqukako, ziqale phasi emini yalo engapheliko eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka; begodu njengombana kungekho ukububula okukodwa namkha ukulila kwananyana ngiliphi ihlobo, awa, kungabi ngitjho negagasi namkha ibhamuza elivela ekujuleni kwalo; yini umuntu wendawo ebekangacabanga ngayo, ukuthi ngaphasi kwakho koke ukuthula nokuthula, isilwane esikhulu selwandle besitjhinga begodu sihlunguphazeka ngobuhlungu! Akukho masentimitha abunane wentambo ejame ngqo ebegade abonakala eemnsalo. Kubonakala kukholeka bona ngemicu emithathu emincani kangaka iLeviyathani ekulu beyilengiswe njengesisindo esikhulu ewatjhini yamalanga abunane. Janyisiweko? begodu kuphi? Ukuya eengcenyeni ezintathu zebhodi. Kghani lesi sidalwa okwakhe kwathiwa ngaso ngokunqoba—“Ungagcwalisa isikhumba sakhe ngeensimbi ezihlabako, namkha ikhanda lakhe ngemikhonto yefesi? Inkemba yomuntu omhlaselako ayikwazi ukubamba, umkhonto, umcibisholo, namkha isikhwama; ngokusikinyeka komkhonto!” Isidalwa lesi? yena? Bababe! ukuthi ukungagcwaliseki kufanele kulandele abaphrofethi. Ngombana ngamandla wamathanga ayikulungwana emsileni wakhe, uLeviyathani bekagijimise ihlokwakhe ngaphasi kweentaba zelwandle, ukumfihla emikhontweni yeenhlambi ye-Pequod!

Ngaleso sikhathi sokukhanya kwelanga, amathunzi ebegade athunyelwa ziinkepe ezintathu ngaphasi kwephasi, kufuze bona begade amade ngokwaneleko begodu abanzi ngokwaneleko ukobana avikele ingcenye yebutho laka-Xerxes. Ngubani ongatjho bona bekumthusa kangangani umkhomo olimeleko bona bekuziinthombe ezikulu kangaka eziphaphazela phezu kwehloko yakhe!

"Yimani, madoda; uyavuvuka," kwamemeza u-Starbuck, njengombana imigqa emithathu yathuthumela emanzini, ngokucacileko idlulisela phezulu kiyo, njengeentambo zamagnethi, ukubetha kwepilo nokufa komkhomo, ukwenzela bona woke umgwedli azizwe esihlalweni sakhe. Ngomzuzu olandelako, sele zikhululekile khulu ekugandelelekeni kwehla emaphethelweni, iinkepe zaphakama msinyana phezulu, njengombana kuzokwenza indawo encani yeqhwa, lokha umhlambi omkhulu wamabhere amhlophe nawuthuswa kiwo ukuya elwandle.

“Ngena! Ngena!” kwamemeza u-Starbuck godu; "Uyavuka."

Imigqa, egade, msinyana ngaphambilini, ububanzi besandla esisodwa bebungatholakala, besele iyimikhoyili emide emsinyana ephoselwa emuva yoke iconsa eenkepeni, begodu msinyana umkhomo waphula amanzi ngaphakathi kobude bemikhumbi emibili yabazingeli.

Ukunyakaza kwakhe bekutjengisa ukudinwa kwakhe okukhulu. Eenlwaneni ezinengi ezihlala ephasini kunamavalvu athileko nofana amasango weenkhukhula emithanjeni yazo eminengi, lapho lokha nazilimeleko, iingazi zivaleka msinyana ngeendlela ezithileko. Akunjalo ngomkhomo; enye yezinto ezihlukileko kukuba nesakhiwo esingesiso se-valvula semithambo yeengazi, ukwenzela bona lokha nayibhobozwa ngitjho nephuzu elincani kangaka njenge-harpoon, ukugeleza okubulalako kuthoma msinyana kiyo yoke imithambo yeengazi; begodu lokha nakuphakanyiswa mfutho ongakajayeleki wamanzi ebangeni elide ngaphasi kwephasi, ipilo yakhe kungathiwa iphuma kuye ngemifudlana engapheliko. Nanyana kunjalo inani leengazi kuye likhulu kangaka, begodu likude begodu linengi imithombo yalo yangaphakathi, kangangobana uzokuragela phambili nokopha begodu ukopha isikhathi eside; ngitjho nanyana kunesomiso kuzokugeleza umlambo, umthombo wawo usemithonjeni yeentaba ezikude nezingabonakaliko. Ngitjho nanje, lokha iinkepe nazidosa phezu komkhomo lo, begodu zidonsa ngokuyingozi phezu kwama-flukes wakhe, begodu amalansi aphoselwa kuye, bezilandelwa ziinqhema ezijameleko ezivela esilondani esitjha, ebegade sidlala njalo, ngesikhathi umgodi wemvelo ekhanda lakhe bewusolo uthumela umswakama wawo othusako emoyeni. Kusukela emgodini wokugcina bekungakaphumi iingazi, ngombana akukho ngcenye yakhe eqakathekileko ebegade ihlaselwe. Ipilo yakhe, njengombana bayibiza njalo, beyingakathintwa.

Njengoba iinkepe besele zimzungezile, yoke ingcenye ephezulu yefomu yakhe, enenengi yayo evamise ukucwiliswa, yavezwa sobala. Amehlo wakhe, nofana iindawo lapho amehlo wakhe gade akhona, abonakala. Njengombana iinqubuthu ezingakajayeleki ezingakakhuli kuhle zibuthene eendaweni ezinamafindo zama-oki amahle khulu lokha naziguqako, ngalokho-ke ukusuka eendaweni lapho amehlo womkhomo egade ahlala khona, kwanje kwaphuma amabhalbhu angaboniko, okumbi khulu ukuwabona. Kodwana ukuzwelana bekunganalitho. Ngobudala bakhe boke, nomkhono wakhe owodwa, namehlo akhe angaboniko, kufuze afe begodu abulawe, ukwenzela bona akhanyise abatjhadikazi abathandana nabobulili obufanako neminye iminyanya yamadoda, begodu akhanyise amabandla ahloniphekileko atjhumayela ukungabi nemibandela ngabo boke abantu. Nakasolo agijima egazini lakhe, ekugcineni waveza isiqubuthu esitjhuguluke umbala ngokungakajayeleki, ubukhulu bebhasheli, phasi ngehlangothini.

"Indawo ehle," kwamemeza u-Flask; "Ngivumele ngimhlabe lapho kanye."
