Kodwana bekubonakala ngasuthi, lokha nakasesikepeni, u-Queequeg bekangakananzeleli lokho ebengikhuluma ngakho nje; ngalokho-ke bengingazicabanga bona ngikhohlisiwe endabeni leyo, ngathana bekungasi ngombuzo ka-Elija ongahlathululekiko. Kodwana ngayitjhabalalisa into leyo; begodu waphinda watshwaya umuntu olele, ngokuhlekisa waveza u-Queequeg bona mhlamunye kungcono sihlale phezulu nomzimba; umtjela bona azimise ngokufaneleko. Wabeka isandla sakhe ngemuva komuntu olele, kwanga uzwa bona ithambile ngokwaneleko; bese, ngaphandle kokukhuluma, wahlala phasi lapho athule.

“Umusa! Queequeg, ungahlali lapho,” kwatjho mina.

"Hawu! isihlalo se-perry dood," kwatjho u-Queequeg, "indlela yami yezwe; angeze yamlimaza ubuso."

"Ukuqalana!" Ngatjho mina, "kubize lokho ngobuso bakhe? ubuso obuhle khulu ke; kodwana uphefumula budisi kangangani, uyaziphakamisa; yehla, Queequeg, uyasinda, kugaya ubuso babantu abatlhagako. Yehla, Queequeg! Qala, uzokutjhiya msinyana. Ngiyazibuza bona akavuki."

U-Queequeg wazisusa ngale kwehloko yomuntu olele, begodu wakhanyisa ipayipi lakhe le-tomahawk. Ngahlala eenyaweni. Besigcina ipayipi idlula phezu komuntu olele, ukusuka komunye ukuya komunye. Ngalesosikhathi, lokha nangimbuza ngendlela yakhe ephukileko, u-Queequeg wangipha ukuzwisisa bona, enarheni yakhe, ngebanga lokungabi khona kwama-sette namasofa wemihlobo yoke, ikosi, iinkosi, nabantu abakhulu ngokujayelekileko, bebanesiko lokunothisa abanye babantu abaphasi bona babe ma-ottoman; begodu ukuhlobisa indlu ngokukhululeka ngalokho, bekufuze uthenge abantu abasibhozo nofana abalitjhumi abavilaphako, bese uwabeka hlangana nama-pier nama-alcove. Ngaphandle kwalokho, bekulula khulu ekhambweni; ngcono khulu kunalezo zihlalo zegadeni ezingatjhugululwa zibe ziintonga zokukhamba; ngezinye iinkhathi, ikosi ibiza umsizi wayo, begodu imfisa bona enze isihlalo sayo ngaphasi komuthi osabaleleko, mhlamunye endaweni emanzi enamaxhaphozi.

Lokha nakalandisa izinto lezi, ngaso soke isikhathi u-Queequeg nakathola i-tomahawk kimi, bekayifaka ihlangothi le-hatchet phezu kwehloko yomuntu olele.

“Kwenzelwani lokho, Queequeg?”

"Perry lula, kill-e; oh! perry lula!"

Bekaragela phambili nokukhumbuza okumbi ngepayipi lakhe le-tomahawk, ebegade kubonakala ngasuthi, begade isetjenziswa kizo zombili iintha zakhe begodu ithoba umphefumulo wakhe, lokha nasikhangwa ngqo mtjhayeli oleleko. Umhwamuko oqinileko sele ugcwalisa umgodi onciphileko, wathoma ukumtjela. Waphefumula ngohlobo lokufiphala; bese wabonakala atshwenyekile ngekhala; bese ujikeleza kanye nofana kabili; bese wahlala phasi wahlikihla amehlo wakhe.

“Hawu!” waphefumulela phezulu ekugcineni, "ngobani nina babhemayo?"

"Amadoda athunyelwe," ngaphendula, "uhamba nini?"

"Iye, iye, niyangena ngaphakathi kwayo, angithi? Ihamba ngesikepe namhlanjesi. UKaputeni ungene emkhunjini izolo ebusuku."

"Khuyini uKaputeni?—U-Ahabi?"

“Ngubani ngaphandle kwakhe?”

Bengizombuza eminye imibuzo malungana no-Ahabi, lokha nasizwa umsindo emkhunjini.

“Hawu! I-Starbuck ithabile,” kwatjho umtjhayeli. "Umlingani omkhulu ophilako, loyo; umuntu olungileko, nohlonipha uZimu; kodwana woke umuntu ophilako nje, kufuze ngiphendukele kuye." Watjho njalo waya emkhunjini, nathi salandela.

Kwasekukhanya ilanga. Msinyana isiqhema sangena ngababili ngabathathu; abatjhayeli bazitjhukumisela; abalingani bebazibandakanya ngokuzikhandla; begodu abantu abanengi abasegwini bebamatasatasa ngokuletha izinto ezihlukahlukeneko zokugcina emkhunjini. Ngalesosikhathi uKaputeni u-Ahabi wahlala angabonakali ngaphakathi endlini yakhe.
