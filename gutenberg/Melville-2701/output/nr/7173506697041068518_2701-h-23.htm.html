ISAHLUKO 124. Inaliti.

Ngelanga elilandelako ekuseni ilwandle ebelingakathomi lagijima ngamagagasi amade abuthakathaka, begodu lilwa emzileni we-Pequod, lamsunduzela phambili njengezandla zeziqhwaga ezivulekileko. Umoya onamandla, ongatjhugulukiko bewugcwele kangangobana isibhakabhaka nomoya bekubonakala njengeseyila ezikulu; umhlaba woke waduma phambi komoya. Ukufiphazwa ekukhanyeni okuzeleko kwekuseni, ilanga elingabonakaliko laziwa kwaphela ngobukhulu bendawo yakhe; lapho imisebe yakhe ye-bayonet beyiragela phambili ngamaqoqo. Imihlobiso, njengamakhosi namakhosikazi weBhabhiloni, beyibusa kikho koke. Ilwandle belifana nesitja segolide encibilikileko, esigxuma ngokukhanya nokutjhisa.

Athule isikhathi eside, u-Ahabi wema ngaphandle; begodu ngaso soke isikhathi umkhumbi ogade utjhinga phasi umnsalo wawo, bekajika aqale imisebe yelanga ekhanyako ebegade ikhiqizwa phambili; begodu lokha nakahlala phasi ngemuva kwesikepe, wajika ngemva, wabona indawo yelanga yangemuva, nokuthi imisebe efanako etjheli beyihlangana njani nokuvuka kwakhe okungaphambukiko.

"Ha, ha, umkhumbi wami! Ungathathwa njengamanje njengenqola yelanga. Ho, ho! zoke nina zizwe phambi kwe-prow yami, ngiletha ilanga kini! Joka phezu kwamagagasi aragela phambili; sawubona! i-tandem, ngiqhuba ilwandle!"

Kodwana msinyana wabuyiselwa emuva mcabango othileko, waphuthuma waya esiqhemeni, abuza ngokurhuwelela bona umkhumbi ukhamba njani.

“Pumalanga-Sewula-Pumalanga, mnumzana,” kwatjho umtjhayeli othukileko.

“Uyaqamba amanga!” amshaya ngesibhakela sakhe esiqinileko. "Uya epumalanga ngaleli-awa ekuseni, begodu ilanga lisempumalanga?"

Ngalokhu woke umphefumulo wadideka; ngombana isenzakalo esanda kubonwa ngu-Ahabi besiphunyuke ngokungaziphenduleli kibo boke abanye abantu; kodwana ukuzwakala kwayo okuphuphuthekisako kufuze bona bekuyimbangela.

U-Ahabi wafaka ihlokwakhe phakathi nendawo, u-Ahabi wabona amakhampasi; ingalo yakhe ephakanyisiweko yawela kancanikancani; isikhatjhana gade abonakala atjhinga. U-Starbuck ajame ngemva kwakhe waqala, begodu khangela! amakhampasi amabili bekakhomba ipumalanga, begodu i-Pequod beyiya eTjingalanga.

Kodwana ngaphambi kokuthi i-alamu yokuthoma iphume phetjheya hlangana nabasebenzi, indoda endala ngokuhleka okuqinileko yababaza, "Nginakho! Kwenzeke ngaphambilini. Mnumzana Starbuck, ukuduma kwezolo ebusuku kuphendule ikhampasi zethu—ngilokho kwaphela. Uke wezwa ngento enjalo, ngiyayithatha."

"Iye; kodwana akukaze kwenzeke kimi, mnumzana," kwatjho umlingani omnyama, ngokudana.

Lapha, kufanele kutjhiwo, bona iingozi ezifana nalezi sele zenzeke eemkhunjini ezineenwuruwuru ezinamandla. Amandla kazibuthe, njengombana akhiwe ngenalithi yomtjhayeli, njengombana boke abantu bazi, munye nogesi obonakala ezulwini; ngalokho-ke akukafaneli kurarwe khulu, ukuthi izinto ezinjalo zingaba njalo. Iinkhathi lapho umbane utjhaye khona umkhumbi, ukwenzela bona utjhaye ezinye zeenqhema kanye nama-rigging, umphumela phezu kwenalithi ngezinye iinkhathi bewusolo ubulala khulu; koke ubuhle bayo be-loadstone butjhatjalaliswa, kangangobana insimbi yangaphambilini yamazibuthe beyingasasebenzi ukudlula inaliti yokunitha yomfazi omdala. Kodwana kizo zombili iimeko, inaliti angeze yaphinde, ngokwayo, ibuyise ubuhle bokuthoma ebegade butjhabalaliswe namkha obulahlekileko; begodu nangabe amakhampasi we-binnacle athinteka, isiphetho esifanako sifinyelela kibo boke abanye abangaba semkhunjini; ngitjho naleyo ephasi khulu yafakwa ngaphakathi kwe-kelson.

Ngokuma ngamabomu phambi kwe-binnacle, begodu aqale amakhampasi atjhugululiweko, indoda endala, ngesandla sayo esibukhali, nje yathatha indlela ilanga elikhambe ngayo, begodu yaneliseka ngokuthi iinalithi beyiphendulwe ngqo, yarhuwelela imiyalo yayo yokuthi indlela yomkhumbi itjhugululwe ngokufaneleko. Amayadi bekaqinile; begodu godu i-Pequod yaphosela iminsalo yayo engasasabiko emoyeni ophikisanako, ngombana loyo okuthiwa muhle bekasolo amdlala.

Khatheso, nanyana ngimiphi imicabango yakhe eyimfihlo, u-Starbuck akhenge atjho litho, kodwana ngokuthula wakhipha woke ama-oda atlhogekako; ngesikhathi u-Stubb no-Flask—ebegade babonakala bahlanganyela amazizo wakhe—nabo bavuma ngaphandle kokukhonona. Mayelana namadoda, nanyana amanye wawo bekaduma, ukwesaba kwawo u-Ahabi bekukukhulu ukudlula ukwesaba kwawo i-Fate. Kodwana njengangaphambilini, abatjhayeli bamahedeni bahlala bangathinteki; nofana nangabe bahlatshwe umxhwele, bekuyindlela ethileko yokuzibuthela eenhliziyweni zabo ezihle ezivela ku-Ahabi ongatjhugulukiko.

Okwesikhatjhana indoda endala yakhamba emkhunjini ngamaphupho. Kodwana nakatjhelela ngesithende sakhe sezinyo lendlovu, wabona amashubhu wethusi aphukileko we-quadrant ebegade anawo ngelanga langaphambilini agijimela emkhunjini.

"Wena ohluphekileko, ozikhakhazisako oqala izulu nomtjhayeli welanga! izolo ngikubhidlize, begodu namhlanjesi amakhampasi angabe angibhidlize. Njeke, njeke. Kodwana u-Ahabi uyinkosi phezu kwelitje lokuthwala elilinganako. Mnumzana Starbuck—umkhonto ongenapali; i-top-maul, kanye nenalithi encani khulu yomenzi weseyila!"

Okungezelelweko, mhlamunye, emfuthweni obegade afuna ukuyenza, bekuneenhloso ezithileko ezihlakaniphileko, ebegade umnqopho wazo bekukuvuselela imimoya yabasebenzi bakhe ngokubetha kwekghono lakhe elifihlakeleko, endabeni emangalisako njengaleyo yamakhampasi aphendukileko. Ngaphandle kwalokho, indoda endala beyazi kuhle bona ukutjhayela ngeenaliti ezitjhugululweko, nanyana bekungasi yinto ekufuze idlule ngamatilosi akholelwa eentweni ezingakholwakaliko, ngaphandle kokuthuthumela nokutjengisa okumbi.

“Madoda,” watjho, aphendukela esiqhemeni, njengombana umlinganakhe amnikela izinto egade azifunile, “madoda wami, ukuduma kwatjhugulula iinalithi zika-Ahabi omdala; kodwana ngensimbi le u-Ahabi angenza eyakhe, ezakuba liqiniso njenganoma ngiyiphi.”

Ukubuka okuhlazisako kokumangala okusebenzako kwatjhentjhana ngamatilosi, njengombana lokhu kwatjhiwo; begodu ngamehlo athakazelisako bebalindele nanyana ngimuphi umlingo ongalandela. Kodwana u-Starbuck waqala kude.

Ngokutshaywa okuvela e-top-maul u-Ahabi wawisa ihloko yensimbi yomkhonto, bese wanikela umlingani induku ende yensimbi eseleko, wamtjela bona ayibambe iqondile, ngaphandle kokuthinta i-deck. Bese, nge-maul, ngemva kokubetha ngokuphindaphindiweko umphetho ongaphezulu wenduku yensimbi, wabeka inaliti ebuthuntu phezulu kwayo, begodu washaya ngamandla kangako, kanengi, umlinganakho asabambe induku njengangaphambilini. Bese kuthi ngemva kokunyakaza okuncani okungakajayeleki ngayo—kungaba yinto eqakathekileko ekwenzeni insimbi ibe yizibuthe, nofana ihloselwe ukwandisa ukusaba kwabasebenzi, akuqiniseki—wabiza intambo yelineni; begodu waya e-binnacle, wakhupha iinalithi ezimbili ezibuyele emuva lapho, begodu walengisa inaliti yeseyila phakathi kwayo, phezu kwelinye lamakhadi wekhamphasi. Ekuthomeni, isimbi beyijikeleza, ithuthumela begodu ithuthumela emaphethelweni womabili; kodwana ekugcineni yahlala endaweni yayo, lokha u-Ahabi, obegade aqale umphumela lo, abuyela emuva ngokungagwegwesi ebhokisini, begodu akhomba ingalo yakhe ekulu kiyo, wababaza,—“Qalani, ngokwakho, nangabe u-Ahabi akasiyo inkosi yelitje lomthwalo eliphakemeko! Ilanga liseMpumalanga, begodu liyafunga!”

Baqala ngaphakathi ngokulandelana, ngombana bekunganalitho ngaphandle kwamehlo wabo ebegade akghona ukuncenga ukungazi okunjengakwabo, begodu ngokulandelana kwabaleka.

Ngamehlo wakhe avuthako wokudelela nokuphumelela, wabona u-Ahabi ngakho koke ukuzikhakhazisa kwakhe.





ISAHLUKO 125. Ilogi nomugqa.

Nanyana nje i-Pequod ebegade sele ihamba isikhathi eside kangaka, isigodo nelayini bekungakavami ukusetjenziswa. Ngebanga lokuthembela ngokuzithemba kwezinye iindlela zokuthola indawo yomkhumbi, abanye abathengisi, nabantu abanengi abathengisa imikhomo, khulukhulu lokha nabakhamba ngesikepe, bayatjhiya ngokupheleleko ukuphakamisa isigodo; nanyana ngesikhathi esifanako, begodu kanengi khulu ngebanga lobujamo ukudlula nanyana yini enye, njalo ukubeka phasi phezu kwesilayidi esijayelekileko indlela eqondiswa mkhumbi, kanye nezinga eliphakathi lokuragela phambili qobe nge-awa. Bekunjalo nge-Pequod. I-reel yamapulangwe kanye nelogo enama-engeli ebegade inamathiselwe gade ilenga, isikhathi eside ingathintwa, ngaphasi kwereyili yama-bulvarks. Izulu nesitjhatjha beziyithobisile; ilanga nomoya bekuyitjhugulule; zoke izinto bezihlangene ukubola into ebegade ilenga kangaka. Kodwana ngaphandle kokutjheja koke lokhu, umuzwa wakhe wabamba u-Ahabi, njengombana kwenzeka bona aqale i-reel, kungakapheli ama-awa amanengi ngemva kwesigcawu samazibuthe, begodu wakhumbula bona i-quadrant yakhe beyingesekho njani, begodu wakhumbula isifungo sakhe esikhulu nge-level log nomugqa. Umkhumbi bewuhamba phasi; ngasemuva amagagasi aphephetha ngemibhikisho.

"Phambili, lapho! Phakamisa isigodo!"

Kwafika abasebenzi bemikhumbi ababili. I-Tahitian enombala wegolide kanye ne-Manxman ene-grizzly. “Thatha i-reel, omunye wenu, ngizokuphakamisa.”

Baya ngasemuva khulu, ngehlangothini lomkhumbi, lapho i-deck, ngamandla wommoya, besele ipheze itjhinga ngaphakathi kwelwandle elinokhilimu, eligijima ngehlangothini.

I-Manxman yathatha i-reel, begodu yayibamba phezulu, ngamaphethelo wesibambo esiphumileko se-spindle, lapho i-spool yelayini beyijikeleza khona, yajama nge-log ene-engele ilenga phasi, bekwaba kulapho u-Ahabi atjhidela khona kuye.

U-Ahabi bekajame phambi kwakhe, begodu bekakhulula kancani amajika angaba matjhumi amathathu nofana amane ukwenza i-hand-coil yokuthoma yokuphosela ngaphetjheya, lokha i-Manxman endala, ebegade imqale yena nomlayini, yenza isibindi sokukhuluma.

“Mnumzana, angiyithembi; umugqa lo ubukeka usuphelile, ukutjhisa okude nokumanzi kuwonakele.”

"'Ngizokubambelela, mnumzana omdala. Ukutjhisa okude nokuthamba, kukumoshile na? Ubonakala ubamba. Nofana, mhlamunye, ipilo iyakubamba; ingasi wena."

"Ngiphethe i-spool, mnumzana. Kodwana njengombana ukaputeni wami atjho. Ngeenhluthu zami ezimpunga akukafaneli ukuphikisana, khulukhulu nomphathi, ongasoze avume."

"Khuyini lokho? Nje-ke kunoprofesa ohlanganisiweko eKholeji ye-Queen Nature eyakhiwe ngamagranite; kodwana ngicabanga bona uzithoba khulu. Wabelethelwaphi?"

“E-Isle of Man enamadwala, mnumzana.”

"Kuhle khulu! Ubethe umhlaba ngalokho."

“Angazi, mnumzana, kodwana ngabelethwa lapho.”

"E-Isle of Man, hey? Ngakelinye ihlangothi, kuhle. Nansi indoda evela ku-Man; indoda ezalwa ku-Muntu egade izijameleko, begodu nje ingasenamuntu e-Muntu; edonswa—yini? Phezulu nge-reel! Umduli ofileko, ongaboniko uhlasela zoke iinhloko ezibuzako ekugcineni. Phezulu ngakho! Nje-ke."

Isigodo saphakanyiswa. Amakhoyili akhululekileko aqonda msinyana emgqeni omude wokurhuga ngemuva, bese kuthi, khonokho, i-reel yathoma ukujikajika. Ngokuphindaphindiweko, iphakanyiswa begodu yehliswa ngamagagasi agijimako, ukujamelana nokudosa kwesigodo kwenza bona umtjhayeli omdala athuthumele ngokungakajayeleki.

“Bamba ngamandla!”

Phahlaka! umugqa ogandelelekileko wehla phasi ngefestoni eyodwa ede; isigodo sokudosa besele siphelile.

"Ngichoboza i-quadrant, ukuduma kujika iinalithi, begodu nje ilwandle elihlanyako lihlukanisa umtjhini weenkuni. Kodwana u-Ahabi angalungisa koke. Ngena lapha, mTahiti; goqa, Manxman. Begodu qala, vumela umbazi enze enye inkuni, bese ulungisa umtjhini. Yenza bona."

"Nanyana-ke nje; kuye akukaze kwenzeke litho; kodwana kimi, isikhonkwane sibonakala sikhululeka phakathi kwephasi. Ngena, ngena, we-Tahitian! Imigqa le igijima iphelele, begodu iyajikajika: ngena uphukile, begodu urhuga kabuthaka. Ha, Pip? woza uzokusiza; eh, Pip?"

“Pip? Ubiza bani uPip? UPip ugxume esikepeni semikhomo. UPip ulahlekile. Akhe sibone nje bona awukamdobi lapha, mdobi. Kuyadosa khulu; ngiyacabanga bona uyabambelela. Mkhuphe, Tahiti! Mkhiphe; asidosi amagwala lapha. Iingalo zakhe ziphula iingalo! siqume!

"Ukuthula, wena sidlhadlha," kwamemeza i-Manxman, imbamba ngengalo. “Kude nekota-dekhi!”

"Isidlhayela esikhulu sihlala sikhuza esincani," kwabubula u-Ahabi, aragela phambili. “Susa izandla ebungcweleni! Uthi u-Pip bekakuphi, mfana?

“Ngemuva lapho, mnumzana, ngemuva! Qala! Qala!”

"Begodu ungubani wena, mfana? Angiboni isithombe sami emehlweni wakho angenalutho. O Zimu! umuntu loyo kufanele abe yinto yemiphefumulo engafiko ukuhlunga! Ungubani, mfana?"

“Mfana wensimbi, mnumzana; ummemeli wesikepe; ding, dong, ding! Pip! Pip! Pip! Umvuzo wamaphawundi alikhulu wobumba kaPip; ukuphakama kwamamitha amahlanu—kubukeka njengegwala—okwaziwa msinyana ngalokho! Ding, dong, ding! Ngubani obone uPip igwala?”

“Angeze kwaba neenhliziyo ezingaphezu komgca weqhwa. O, nina mazulu aqandako! Qala phasi lapha. Nizale umntwana lo ongenatjhudu, begodu nimtjhiye, nina babantu abakhululekileko. Lapha, mfana; indlu ka-Ahabi izokuba likhaya laka-Pip kusukela nje, ngesikhathi u-Ahabi asaphila. Uthinta isikhungo sami sangaphakathi, indodana yami; iintambo zehliziyo. Woza, asiye phasi.”

"Khuyini lokhu? nansi isikhumba se-shark," aqala isandla saka-Ahabi, begodu asizwa. "Ah, nje, u-Pip kodwana bekanomusa onjengalokhu, mhlamunye bekangakaze alahleke! Lokhu kubonakala kimi, mnumzana, njengentambo yomuntu; into imiphefumulo ebuthakathaka engabambelela kiyo. O, mnumzana, vumela u-Perth omdala manje eze ahlanganise izandla ezimbili lezi; omnyama nomhlophe, ngombana angeze ngivumele lokhu."

“Hawu, mfana, nami angeze ngakwenza, ngaphandle kobana ngikudosele eendaweni ezimbi ukudlula ezikhona lapha. Wozani-ke endlini yami. Khangela, nina makholwa kibozimu boke ubuhle, nasebantwini boke abagulako, khangelani! ngizizwe ngizikhakhazisa ngokukudosela ngesandla sakho esimnyama, kunokuthi ngibambe esakoMbusi!”

"Kunabantu ababili abaziindlhayela nje," kwabubula i-Manxman endala. "Omunye uyisithutha ngamandla, omunye uyisithutha ngobuthakathaka. Kodwana nansi isiphetho somzila obolileko—woke uyaconsa, nawo. Lungisa, eh? Ngicabanga bona kungcono sibe nomzila omutjha. Ngizokubona uNom. Stubb ngakho."





ISAHLUKO 126. I-Life-Buoy.

Utjhinga ngesewula-pumalanga ngensimbi ka-Ahabi elinganisiweko, begodu ukuragela phambili kwakhe kunqunywa yi-log nelayini ka-Ahabi kwaphela; i-Pequod yabambelela endleleni yayo eya e-Equator. Ukwenza ikhambo elide kangaka emanzini angakavami, ukungabi nemikhumbi, begodu isikhathi eside, siqhutshwa yimimoya engatjhugulukiko, phezu kwamagagasi alula; zoke lezi bezibonakala zizinto ezithulileko ezingakajayeleki eziqalisa isigcawu esithileko esinesiphithiphithi nesiphelelwa lithemba.

Ekugcineni, lokha umkhumbi nawutjhidela emaphethelweni, njengokungathi, wendawo yokudoba ye-Equatorial, begodu ebumnyameni obudephileko obuhamba ngaphambi kokusa, bewuhamba ngesiqubuthu samatje; iwatjhi—gade ikhokhelwa ngu-Flask—yathuswa ukulila okuhlasimulisa umzimba nokungasikho kwephasi—okufana nokulila okungakhulunywako kwezipokwe zabo boke abantu abanganacala bakaHerodi ababulawa—ukuthi omunye nomunye, bathoma emaphupheni wabo, begodu isikhatjhana esithileko bajama, nofana bahlala, nofana batjhinga, bebalalele, njengesigqila samaRoma ukukhala okukhulu kwasala ngaphakathi kokuzwa. Ingcenye yamaKrestu nofana ephucukileko yesiqhema yathi bekungama-mermaid, begodu yathuthumela; kodwana abatjhayeli bamahedeni bahlala bangathukwa. Nanyana kunjalo i-Manxman empunga—umtjhayeli wesikepe omdala kunabo boke—wamemezela bona imidumo ethokozisako ebeyizwakala, bekumaphimbo wamadoda asanda kuminza elwandle.

Ngezansi embhedeni wakhe, u-Ahabi akhenge akuzwe ngalokhu bekwaba sekuseni, lokha nakafika emkhunjini; kwabe sekulandiswa kuye ngu-Flask, ingasi ngaphandle kweenhlathululo ezimnyama. Wahleka khulu, begodu ngaleyo ndlela wahlathulula isimangaliso.

Iinhlenge ezinamadwala umkhumbi ebegade udlulile kizo beziyindawo yokuphumula yama-seal amanengi, begodu amanye ama-seal amancani ebegade alahlekelwe madamu wawo, nofana amanye amadamu alahlekelwe mazinyane wawo, kufuze bona begade atjhidele eduze nomkhumbi begodu ahlala nawo, akhala begodu akhala ngokulila kwawo kobuntu. Kodwana lokhu kwathinta khulu abanye babo, ngombana inengi labasebenzi bemikhumbi bathanda umuzwa ongakholwako ngama-seal, ongaveli kwaphela ngemizwa yawo engakajayeleki lokha nakasebujameni obubudisi, kodwana nangokubukeka kwabantu kweenhloko zawo eziyindilinga nobuso obuhlakaniphileko, obubonakala buvuka emanzini eceleni. Elwandle, ngaphasi kobujamo obuthileko, ama-seal sele athathwa njengamadoda kanengi.

Kodwana ama-bodings wesiqhema bekazokufumana isiqinisekiso esizwakalako khulu ngesiphetho somunye wabo ekuseni. Ekuphumeni kwelanga indoda le yasuka e-hammock yayo yaya ekhanda layo le-mast ngaphambili; begodu nokuthi bekangakavuki ebuthongweni bakhe (ngombana amatilosi kesinye isikhathi aya phezulu asebujameni bokutjhuguluka), nokuthi bekunjalo na endodeni leyo, akusekho ukutjho; kodwana, nanoma kungaba njalo, bekangakahlali isikhathi eside, lokha nakuzwakala ukukhala—ukukhala nokugijima—begodu baqala phezulu, babona i-phantom iwela emoyeni; begodu uqala phasi, inqwaba encani yamabhamuza amhlophe eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka olwandle.

I-slip-buoy—umgqomo omude omncani—yalahlwa ngemva kwesikepe, lapho gade ihlala ilenga khona ngokuthobela isiphethu esihlakaniphileko; kodwana akukho isandla esaphakama ukuyibamba, begodu ilanga sele lishaya isikhathi eside phezu kwebhokisi leli belinciphile, kangangobana lagcwala kancanikancani, begodu iinkuni ezomileko nazo zagcwala kiyo yoke imigodi yayo; begodu umgqomo obotjhwe ngensimbi oneenhluthu walandela umkhumbi ukuya phasi, kwanga uzomnikela umsamelo wakhe, nanyana bewuthambile kodwana uqinile.

Ngalokho indoda yokuthoma ye-Pequod eyakhwela isikhonkwane ukuqala i-White Whale, endaweni engakajayeleki ye-White Whale; indoda leyo yaginywa phakathi kwamanzi. Kodwana bambalwa, mhlamunye, ebebacabanga ngalokho ngesikhatheso. Njengombana ngesikhathi esifaneleko nendawo, ngemva kokukhamba ngesikepe okude kangaka, u-Ahabi,—woke amanye amanzi wokudoba imikhomo—wabonakala agijimisa isitha sakhe wangena esibayeni selwandle, ukumbulala ngokuphepha lapho; nje, ukuthi wazithola anzima nge-latitude ne-longitude lapho isilonda sakhe esihluphako besibangelwe khona; njeke ngombana bekukhulunywe umkhumbi owahlangana no-Moby Dick ngelanga langaphambilini;—begodu njeke ngombana yoke imihlangano yakhe elandelanako nemikhumbi ehlukahlukeneko beyivumelana ngokuhlukileko ukutjengisa ukunganaki okufana namadimoni umkhomo omhlophe owadabula abazingeli bakhe, kungakhathaliseki bona wona namkha wona; kwanje kwaba yikuthi bekukhona okuthileko emehlweni wendoda endala, ebekungakghonakali bona abantu ababuthakathaka bakubona. Njengenkanyezi ye-polar engapheliko, ebusuku obuphilako, be-arctic, beenyanga ezisithandathu igcina ukuqala kwayo okuhlabako, okuqinileko, okuphakathi; ngalokho umnqopho ka-Ahabi gade sele ukhanya phakathi kobusuku besiqhema esidabukisako. Kwabusa phezu kwabo kangangobana koke ukucabanga kwabo, ukuzaza, ukuzaza, ukwesaba, bekulula ukufihla ngaphasi kwemiphefumulwabo, begodu kungakhimi ngitjho nomkhonto nofana ikari.

Nakulesi sikhathi sokuqagela, woke amahlaya, agandelelweko nofana wemvelo, anyamalala. U-Stubb akhenge asazami ukumomotheka; I-Starbuck azange isazama ukuhlola enye. Ngokufanako, ithabo nokudana, ithemba nokwesaba, bekubonakala kugayiwe kwaba lithuli elihle khulu, begodu kuphulwe, okwesikhatjhana, emgodini oqinileko womphefumulo wensimbi ka-Ahabi. Njengemitjhini, bebakhamba ngokuthula emkhunjini, bebahlala bazi bona iso lendoda endala beliqale kibo.

Kodwana ingabe wamhlolisisa khulu ngesikhathi sakhe esiyimfihlo; lokha nakacabanga bona akekho ukubuka kodwana okukodwa bekukuye; bewuzokubona bona ngitjho nanyana amehlo ka-Ahabi bekathusa khulu abasebenzi, ukuqala kwaka-Parsee okungazwisisekiko kwamangalisa kwakhe; nofana ngandlela thize, okungenani, ngendlela engakalungi, ngezinye iinkhathi yayithinta. Ubujamo obunjalo obungezelelweko, obutjhingako buthome ukutjala i-Fedallah encani nje; ukuthuthumela okungapheliko kwamthukuthelisa; ukuthi amadoda la bekamqala ngokungabaza; bekungaqiniseki, njengombana bekubonakala, bona kwamambala bekayinto efako, namkha isithunzi esithuthumelako esiphoswe emkhunjini ngumzimba wesidalwa esithileko esingabonakaliko. Begodu isithunzi besihlala siphaphazela lapho. Ngombana ingasi ebusuku, ngitjho, u-Fedallah akhe waziwa ngokulala, nofana ukuya phasi. Bekajame ama-awa amanengi: kodwana azange ahlale namkha ancike; amehlo wakhe abuthakathaka kodwana amangalisako bekatjho ngokukhanyako—Thina abalindi ababili asiphumuli.

Begodu, nganoma ngisiphi isikhathi, ebusuku nofana emini abasebenzi bemikhumbi bebangakghoni ukunyathela emkhunjini, ngaphandle kobana u-Ahabi abe phambi kwabo; angaba ajame emgodini wakhe, nofana akhamba ngamapulangwe hlangana nemikhawulo emibili engaphambukiko,—i-main-mast ne-mizen; namkha bambona ajame ngaphakathi kwendlu,—unyawo lwakhe oluphilako lwaqhubekela phambili emkhunjini, kwangathi uyanyathela; isigqoko sakhe besitjhinge khulu emehlweni wakhe; kangangobana nanyana bekajame angasikinyeki, nanyana amalanga nobusuku bebungezelelwe kangangani, bekangakajiki e-hammock yakhe; kodwana befihlekile ngaphasi kwesigqoko esithambileko, bebangakghoni ukutjho bona, ngakho koke lokhu, amehlo wakhe bekavaliwe ngezinye iinkhathi; nofana bekasazihlolisisa na; kungakhathaliseki, nanyana bekajame njalo ngaphakathi kwe-scuttle i-awa loke, begodu ubusuku obumanzi obungakaqalwa bubuthene ngamazolo phezu kwejazi nesigqoko esiqotjhwe ngamatje. Izembatho ebegade zimanzi ebusuku, ilanga lelanga elilandelako lazoma phezu kwakhe; begodu, ilanga nelanga, nobusuku ngemva kobusuku; azange asangena ngaphasi kwamapulangwe; nanyana yini ayifunako endlini leyo into ayithumeleko.

Wadla endaweni evulekileko efanako; okutjho bona, ukudla kwakhe okubili kwaphela,—isidlo sasekuseni nesidlo sakusihlwa: isidlo sakusihlwa azange asithinte; begodu azange avune iindevu zakhe; eyakhula yoke ibe mnyama, njengemirabhu yemithi evumbululwe phezulu, esakhula inganamsebenzi phasi, nanyana sele itjhabalale ehlathini elingaphezulu. Kodwana nanyana ipilo yakhe yoke besele iyiwatjhi yinye emkhunjini; begodu nanyana iwatjhi ka-Parsee beyinganawo umkhawulo njengeyakhe; kodwana laba ababili azange bakhulume—omunye umuntu komunye—ngaphandle kwalokha ngeenkhathi ezide into engakaqakatheki eyenza bona kube nesidingo. Nanyana umlingo onamandla kangaka bewubonakala uhlanganisa bobabili ngasese; tjhatjhalazi, begodu eqenjini elithukileko, bebabonakala bahlukene njengesigodo. Nangabe emini bebanethuba lokukhuluma ilizwi elilodwa; ebusuku, amadoda ayizithutha gade akhulumisana. Ngezinye iinkhathi, ama-awa amade khulu, ngaphandle kwesiwuruwuru, bebajame kude khulu ekukhanyeni kweenkwekwezi; U-Ahabi ngaphakathi kwesikepe sakhe, i-Parsee ngesikepe esikhulu; kodwana basaqalana; kwangathi ku-Parsee u-Ahabi wabona isithunzi sakhe esilahliweko, ku-Ahabi i-Parsee into yakhe elahliweko.

Nanyana kunjalo, ngandlela thize, u-Ahabi—ngokwakhe, njengombana bekavezwa qobe langa, nge-awa, begodu ngaso soke isikhathi, ngokuyalela ebantwini abangaphasi kwakhe,—u-Ahabi bekabonakala ayikosi ezijameleko; i-Parsee kodwana isigqila sakhe. Godu bobabili bebabonakala bahlanganiswe ndawonye, ​​begodu umlawuli ongabonakaliko bewubaqhuba; umthunzi omncani oqalene nembambo eqinileko. Ngombana kungaba yini umPharisi lo, zoke iimbambo nezimbambo bekungu-Ahabi oqinileko.

Ekuseni kwathoma ukukhanya okuncani, ilizwi lakhe lensimbi lazwakala ngemuva,—“Ndoda iinhloko zesikhonkwane!”—begodu ilanga loke, bekwaba ngemva kokutjhinga kwelanga nangemva kokuhlwa, ilizwi elifanako qobe nge-awa, ekubetheni kwensimbi yomtjhayeli, bezwakala—“Yini oyibonako?—ebukhali!!”

Kodwana kwathi nakudlula amalanga amathathu nofana amane, ngemva kokuhlangana noRaheli ofuna abantwana; begodu bekungakabonwa umlomo; indoda endala enomdlandla oyedwa gade ibonakala ingathembi ukuthembeka kwesiqhema sayo; okungenani, pheze kibo boke ngaphandle kwabatjhayeli be-Pagan; bekabonakala angabaza, ngitjho, nokuthi u-Stubb no-Flask bangeze banganaki ngokuzithandela umbono egade awufuna. Kodwana nangabe ukusola lokhu bekungokwakhe kwamambala, wazibamba ngokuhlakanipha ukukuveza ngamazwi, nanyana kunjalo izenzo zakhe bezingabonakala zitjengisa lokho.

"Ngizokuthoma ukubona umkhomo ngokwami," watjho. "Iye! U-Ahabi kufanele bona une-dubloon!" begodu ngezandla zakhe wakha isidleke samabhasikidi; begodu wathumela isandla phezulu, ngebhlogo elilodwa eliqotjhiweko, ukubophelela ekhanda le-main-mast, wamukela amaphethelo amabili wentambo eya phasi; begodu ukunamathisela enye ebhasikidini lakhe walungisa ipini yomunye umkhawulo, ukwenzela bona ayibophelele esitimeleni. Lokhu kwenziwa, aphethe umphetho loyo esandleni sakhe begodu ajame eceleni kwephini, waqala isiqhema sakhe, atjhayela ukusuka komunye ukuya komunye; ukujama ukubuka kwakhe isikhathi eside ku-Daggoo, Queequeg, Tashtego; kodwana ukubalekela u-Fedallah; bese wabeka ilihlo lakhe eliqinileko emlinganini omkhulu, wathi,—“Thatha intambo, mnumzana—ngiyinikela ezandleni zakho, Starbuck.” Bese ahlela umuntu wakhe ngaphakathi kwebhasikidi, wanikela umlayezo wokuthi bamphakamisele esihlalweni sakhe, u-Starbuck nguye owaqinisa intambo ekugcineni; begodu ngemva kwalokho wema eduze kwayo. Begodu, ngesandla esisodwa sibambelele esiqwini sobukhosi, u-Ahabi waqala ngaphandle elwandle amakhilomitha amanengi,—phambili, ngemuva, ngehlangothini, naleya,—ngaphakathi kwesiyingi esibanzi esinabileko esilawulwa ngokuphakama okukhulu kangaka.

Lokha nakasebenza ngezandla zakhe endaweni ephakemeko epheze ibe yodwa ngaphakathi kwe-rigging, okungakghoni ukukghona ukujama, umkhumbi oselwandle uphakanyiselwa phezulu lapho, begodu asekelwe lapho ngentambo; ngaphasi kwalezi zimo, isiphetho sayo esibophekileko emkhunjini sihlala sinikelwa ngokuqinileko endodeni eyodwa enewatjhi ekhethekileko yayo. Ngombana ehlane elinjalo lokugijima kwe-rigging, lapho ubudlelwano obuhlukahlukeneko obuhlukahlukeneko phezulu angeze bahlale bubonakala ngokungenaphutha ngalokho okubonwa ngabo emkhunjini; begodu lokha amaphethelo weentambo la aphoselwa phasi qobe ngemva kwemizuzu embalwa, kungaba yikufa okungokwemvelo, nangabe, ngaphandle kokunikelwa umlindi ohlalako, umkhumbi ophakanyisiweko angaphoselwa phasi begodu awele elwandle. Ngalokho indlela u-Ahabi athatha ngayo endabeni le beyingasi yinto engakajayeleki; into engakajayeleki ngabo bekubonakala kukuthi, u-Starbuck, pheze bekungumuntu oyedwa owakhe waphikisana naye nganoma yini etjhidelene nesiqunto—omunye walabo naye, ukuthembeka kwakhe ekuqapheleni egade abonakala angabaza ngandlela thile;—bekumangalisa, bona lo bekuyiyo yoke indoda ebekufuze ayikhethe bona ibe ngumlindi wakhe; ngokutjhaphuluka ukunikela ipilo yakhe yoke ezandleni zomuntu ongathenjwa.

Nje-ke, kokuthoma u-Ahabi bekahlezi phezulu; ngaphambi kobana abe lapho imizuzu elitjhumi; enye yeenlwanyana ezihlabako ezibomvu ezivame ukuphapha zitjhidele eduze neenhloko zemikhomo ezinabantu eendaweni eziphakemeko; enye yeenyoni lezi yeza irhuwelela begodu irhuwelela ekhanda lakhe emzileni wokujikeleza okurhabako okungalandelekiko. Bese yaphaphama ngamamitha ayikulungwana ngqo emoyeni; bese yatjhinga phasi, yaphinda yajikeleza ikhanda lakhe.

Kodwana njengombana aqale emkhathizweni omnyama nokude, u-Ahabi bekabonakala angayiphawuli inyoni le; begodu, kwamambala, akekho omunye umuntu obekangayiphawula khulu, bekungasi bujamo obungakajayeleki; kwaphela kwanjesi pheze ilihlo elinganaki khulu belibonakala libona ihlathululo ethileko yobuqili pheze kiyo yoke into ebonakalako.

“Isigqoko sakho, isigqoko sakho, mnumzana!” kwamemeza msinyana umtjhayeli wesikepe we-Sicily, ogade abekwe ehloko ye-mizen-mast, ajame ngqo ngemva kuka-Ahabi, nanyana gade angaphasi kwezinga lakhe, begodu anomoya ojulileko ohlukanisako.

Kodwana iphiko le-sable besele liphambi kwamehlo wendoda endala; umlomo omude onomgoqo ekhanda lakhe: ngokurhuwelela, i-hawk emnyama yagijima nomvuzo wayo.

Ukhozi lwaphaphazela kathathu ekhanda laka-Tarquin, lakhipha isigqoko sakhe ukuze sifake esikhundleni salo, begodu u-Tanaquil, umkakhe, wamemezela bona u-Tarquin uzokuba yikosi ye-Roma. Kodwana ngokubuyiselwa kwesigqoko kwaphela lapho isibonakaliso sasihle. Isigqoko saka-Ahabi azange sibuyiselwe emuva; i-hawk yasendle yaphapha yaragela phambili nayo; kude phambili kwe-prow: begodu ekugcineni wanyamalala; lokha ukusuka lapho kunyamalala khona, ibala elimnyama elincani labonakala kancani, liwela ukusuka phezulu khulu liye elwandle.
