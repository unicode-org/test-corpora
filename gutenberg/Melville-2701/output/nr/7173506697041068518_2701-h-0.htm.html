Kuzokubonakala bona umgodi lo osebenza kabuhlungu begodu umhluzi wedeveli ompofu we-Sub-Sub ubonakala adlule ema-Vaticans amade kanye nama-stall wephasi, aqoqa nanyana ngikuphi ukutjho okungakahleleki ngemikhomo angakuthola kiyo nanyana ngiyiphi incwadi, ecwengileko namkha engakalungi. Ngalokho-ke akukafaneli, kizo zoke iimeko okungenani, uthathe iintatimende ze-higgledy-piggledy whale, nanyana zingaba ziqiniso kangangani, kilezi ziqubuthu, ukuze zibe yi-cetology yevangeli yamambala. Kude nalokho. Njengombana kuthinta abalobi bekadeni ngokujayelekileko, kanye neembongi ezivela lapha, iingcaphuno lezi ziqakathekile kwaphela nofana ziyathabisa, njengombana zinikela umbono weenyoni walokho okutjhiwo, okucatjangwe, okuthandwako, nokuhlatshelwa ngoLeviyathani, ziintjhaba ezinengi neenzukulwana, kufaka hlangana nezethu.

Nje-ke hamba kuhle, usathane omumbi we-Sub-Sub, engingumhlaziyi wayo. Ungowesitjhaba esingenathemba, esilula esingakghoni ukufuthumala iwayini lephaseli; begodu ngitjho no-Pale Sherry angaba namandla khulu; kodwana umuntu ngezinye iinkhathi athanda ukuhlala naye, begodu azizwe ahluphekile, naye; begodu ukhule ube mnene phezu kweenyembezi; begodu uthi kibo ngokutjho ngokutjho, ngamehlo agcweleko namagilasi angenalitho, begodu ngokudana okungakalungi ngokupheleleko—Niyekeleni, Ma-Sub-Subs! Ngombana lokha nanizikhakhazisa kangangani ukuthabisa umhlaba, nizakuhlala ningathokozi unomphela! Ngathana ngingakghona ukukuhlanza i-Hampton Court ne-Tuileries! Kodwana gwinya iinyembezi zenu begodu uphakame phezulu esigodlweni sekosi ngeenhliziyo zenu; ngombana abangani bakho abakhe bahamba ngaphambilini bahlanza izulu elinezitezi ezilikhomba, begodu benza ababaleki bakaGabriyeli, uMikayeli, noRafayeli abathathwe isikhathi eside, ngokujamelana nokufika kwakho.
