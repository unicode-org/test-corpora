Kodwana lokha, ngemva kokuqeda isikhathi sakhe seyunifomu lapho ubusuku obunengi obulandelanako ngaphandle kokukhipha itjhada elilodwa; lokha, ngemva kwakho koke ukuthula, ilizwi lakhe elingakajayeleki lazwakala limemezela ijethi leyo ekhanyiswe yinyanga, woke umtjhayeli wesikepe oleleleko wathoma ukusukuma kwanga umoya othileko onamaphiko ukhanyise ngaphakathi kwe-rigging, begodu wadumisa isiqhema esifako. “Nanyana uyavuthela!” Ngathana icilongo lokwahlulela belivuthelwe, bebangeze bathuthumela khulu; kodwana bebangazizwanga ukwesaba; kunalokho ubumnandi. Nanyana bekuyi-awa engakajayeleki, kodwana ukukhala bekukarisa khulu, begodu bekuthabisa khulu, kangangobana pheze woke umphefumulo ogade usemkhunjini bewufisa ukwehliswa.

U-Ahabi akhamba emkhunjini ngeenyawo ezirhabako, eziphaphazelako, u-Ahabi wayala bona amaseyila amakhulu nama-royals abekwe, begodu woke amaseyila we-stunsail arhatjheke. Indoda engcono khulu emkhunjini kufuze ithathe isiquntu. Bese, ngawo woke ama-mast-head-head aphethe abantu, isikepe ebegade siqoqwe sigijima phasi phambi komoya. Ukuthambekela okungakajayeleki, okuvusa umxhwele, okuphakamisako kommoya we-taffrail ozalisa imigodi yamaseyila amanengi kangaka, kwenza bona i-deck ephaphazelako, ephaphazelako ibe njengomoya ngaphasi kweenyawo; ngesikhathi asaphuthuma, kwanga imithelela emibili ephikisanako beyilwisana kuye—enye ukukhwela ngqo ezulwini, enye ukutjhayela ngokutjhinga emnqophweni othileko ovundlileko. Begodu ngathana ubukele ubuso baka-Ahabi ngalobo busuku, bewuzokucabanga bona kuye naye bekunezinto ezimbili ezihlukileko ezilwako. Lokha umlenze wakhe owodwa ophilako wenza umsindo ophilako emkhunjini, woke umtjhapho wesitho sakhe esifileko bewuzwakala njengomtjhini webhokisi lokugcwaba. Ngokuphila nokuhlongakala indoda endala le yahamba. Kodwana nanyana umkhumbi bewugijima msinya kangaka, begodu nanyana amehlo woke, njengemicibisholo, ukuqala okutjhisakalelako kwadubula, nanyana kunjalo ijethi yesiliva beyingasabonakali ngalobo busuku. Woke umkhumbi wafunga bona wayibona kanye, kodwana ingasi okwesibili.

Umlomo lo phakathi kobusuku besele sele ukhule waba yinto ekhohliweko, lokha, ngemva kwamalanga ambalwa, qala! ngesikhathi esifanako sokuthula, kwaphinda kwamenyezelwa: godu kwachazwa ngibo boke; kodwana lokha nayenza iseyilu ukuyidlula, yaphinde yanyamalala kwanga azange ibe khona. Bese kusisebenzela ubusuku nobusuku, bekwaba kulapho kungekho noyedwa owayilalela ngaphandle kokumangala. Ngokungakajayeleki kwatjhiswa ekukhanyeni kwenyanga okukhanyako, nofana ukukhanya kwenkanyezi, njengombana kungaba njalo; ukunyamalala godu ilanga elilodwa loke, nofana amalanga amabili, nofana amathathu; begodu ngandlela thize bekubonakala ngaso soke isikhathi ukubuyelelweko okuhlukileko bona kuragela phambili phambili ngeveni yethu, ijethi le yodwa beyibonakala isiheha.

Nanyana ngeenkolelo-ze zangapheliko zomhlobo wabo, begodu ngokuvumelana nokungabi khona kwemvelo, njengombana bekubonakala, okwatjala i-Pequod ezintweni ezinengi, bekungaba khona abanye babasebenzi bemikhumbi ebebafunga bona nanyana kunini begodu nanyana kukuphi lapho kuchazwa khona; nanyana kunjalo ngeenkhathi ezikude kangangani, nanyana eendaweni ezikude kangangani, umlomo loyo ongafinyelelekiko waphoselwa mkhomo munye; kanye nomkhomo loyo, u-Moby Dick. Okwesikhatjhana, kwaba nomuzwa wokwesaba okungakajayeleki ngalesi sibonakaliso esiphaphazelako, kwangathi besibiza ngokukhohlisa, ukwenzela bona isilwane siphendukele kithi, begodu sisidabule ekugcineni elwandle elikude khulu nelimbi khulu.

Ukutshwenyeka kwesikhatjhana, okungacaciko kodwana okumbi kangaka, kwathola amandla amangalisako ekuthuleni okuphambeneko kwezulu, lapho, ngaphasi kwakho koke ukuthamba kwawo okuhlaza satjani, abanye bebacabanga bona kunethando lobusathane, njengombana amalanga namalanga ebesikhamba ngawo, emalwandle sikhathele kangaka, sisizungu, kangangobana woke umkhathi, ukuziphindiselela, ukuziphindiselela kwethu bekubonakala ngasuthi iyazitjhiya epilweni ngaphambi kwe-prow yethu efana ne-urn.

Kodwana, ekugcineni, lokha nasiphendukela ngepumalanga, imimoya ye-Cape yathoma ukubhonga isizungezile, begodu saphakama sawa phezu kwelwandle olude, oluphithizelako olukhona lapho; lokha i-Pequod enamazinyo wendlovu nayikhothamela ukuqhuma, begodu yahlaba amagagasi amnyama ngokuhlanya kwakhe, bekwaba kulapho, njengeemvula zesiliva, amagwebu aphaphazela phezu kweenqaba zakhe; khona-ke koke lokhu okungenalutho kwepilo kwaphela, kodwana kwanikela indawo yezinto ezimbi ukudlula ngaphambilini.

Eduze kweminsalo yethu, izinto ezingakajayeleki emanzini beziphapha phambi kwethu; lokha ngemva kwethu bekuphaphazela amagwababa angazwisisekiko. Njalo ekuseni, sihlezi eendaweni zethu, imigqa yeenyoni lezi beyibonakala; begodu nangaphandle kokurhuwelela kwethu, isikhathi eside sabambelela ku-hemp, kwanga bebathatha umkhumbi wethu njengomkhumbi ongahlalwako; into ebekelwe ukubhujiswa, begodu ngalokho ifanele indawo yokuhlala yabo abanganamakhaya. Bese kuphakanyiswa begodu kwaphakanyiswa, kwasaphakanyiswa ngokungazinzi ilwandle elimnyama, kwangathi amagagasi walo amakhulu bekungunembeza; begodu umphefumulo omkhulu ongakajayeleki bewusebuhlungwini nokuzisola ngesono eside nokutlhaga ebegade ukwenze.

I-Cape of Good Hope, ingabe bayakubiza? Kunalokho i-Cape Tormentoso, njengombana beyibizwa ngaphambilini; isikhathi eside sithandwa kuthula okumbi ebegade kusiphathele ngaphambilini, sazithola sithunyelwe elwandle elihlunguphazako, lapho abantu abanecala batjhuguluka baba ziinyoni neenhlambi, bebabonakala bagwetjwe ukubhukuda unomphela ngaphandle kokuphephela esitolo, nofana ukushaya umoya omnyama ngaphandle komkhathi. Kodwana uzolile, umhlophe njengeqhwa, begodu ungatjhuguluki; isaqondisa umthombo wayo weensiba esibhakabhakeni; isasibiza kusukela ngaphambilini, i-jet ehlala yodwa ngezinye iinkhathi beyizokuchazwa.

Phakathi kobumnyama bezinto, u-Ahabi, nanyana bekathatha umyalo ohlala ukhona emkhunjini omanzi begodu oyingozi, watjengisa ukuzivikela okumbi khulu; begodu bekangakavami ukukhuluma nabalingani bakhe. Ngeenkhathi ezineenwuruwuru ezifana nalezi, ngemva kobana koke okuphezulu nokuphezulu kuvikelwe, akukho okunye okungenziwa ngaphandle kokulindela indaba yesiwuruwuru. Bese kuthi uKaputeni nesiqhema baba ziindlhayela ezisebenzako. Njeke, ngomlenze wakhe wezinyo lendlovu ufakwe emgodini wawo ojwayelekileko, begodu ngesandla esisodwa abambe isigubuzelo, u-Ahabi bekajame ama-awa amanengi aqale umoya, ngesikhathi isiwuruwuru seqhwa nofana iqhwa begade sihlanganisa iinhluthu zakhe. Khatheso, isiqhema ebegade siqhutjhwe ukusuka engcenyeni yangaphambili yomkhumbi ngelwandle oluyingozi ebegade siphuke phezu kwemikhonto yawo, bejama emgqeni eceleni kweenqabo ekhalweni; begodu ngcono ukuvikela amagagasi agxumako, indoda ngayinye beyizitjhelelele ngaphakathi kwentambo ye-bowline ebotjhelelwe esitimeleni, lapho beyijikijela khona njengebhande elikhululekileko. Kwakhulunywa amagama ambalwa nofana awakhulunywako; begodu umkhumbi othulileko, kwanga uphethwe bamatilosi apendiweko nge-wax, ilanga nelanga bewudabulela kibo boke ubuhlanya oburhabako nethabo lamagagasi wamadimoni. Ebusuku ukuthula okufanako kwabantu ngaphambi kokurhuwelela kwelwandle kwabusa; bekusese kuthule amadoda ajika emigqeni; u-Ahabi bekasolo anganalizwi wasukumela phezulu. Ngitjho nanyana ubujamo obudiniweko bebubonakala bufuna ukuphumula bekangafuni ukuphumula lokho embhedeni wakhe. U-Starbuck bekangakghoni ukukhohlwa ubujamo bendoda endala, lokha ngobunye ubusuku nakangena ngaphakathi endlini ukuyokubona bona i-barometer ijame njani, wambona amehlo avaliweko ahlezi ngqo esihlalweni sakhe esinezikulufu phasi; izulu neqhwa elincibilikileko lesiwuruwuru ebegade aphume kiso isikhathi esithileko ngaphambilini, kusaconsa kancanikancani esigqokweni nejazi elingakasuswa. Etafuleni eceleni kwakhe bekulele elinye lamatjhadi wamagagasi namagagasi ebegade kukhulunywe ngawo ngaphambilini. Isibani sakhe satjhinga esandleni sakhe esiqinileko. Nanyana umzimba bewumile, ihloko beyiphoselwa emuva ukwenzela bona amehlo avalekileko akhombe enaliti ye-tel-tale ebegade ijikeleza emgodini ophahleni.*

*Ikhampasi yekhabini ibizwa ngokuthi yi-tell-tale, ngombana ngaphandle kokuya ekhampasini ephepheni, uKaputeni, lokha nakaphasi, angazisa ngendlela umkhumbi ohamba ngayo.

Indoda endala embi! kwacabanga u-Starbuck ngokuthuthumela, alele ngaphakathi kwesiwuruwuru, kodwana uqale umnqopho wakho.





ISAHLUKO 52. I-Albatross.

Ngaseningizimu-mpumalanga ukusuka e-Cape, eduze ne-Crozetts ekude, indawo ehle yokukhamba ngesikepe se-Right Whalemen, iseyili beyivela phambili, i-Goney (Albatross) ngebizo. Njengombana atjhidela kancanikancani, asesihlalweni sami esiphakemeko esiqwini sangaphambili, ngaba nombono omuhle walokho okumangalisako ku-tyro eendaweni zokudoba elwandle elikude—umdobi wemikhomo elwandle, begodu engekho ekhaya isikhathi eside.

Njengalokha amagagasi begade agcwele, isikepe lesi besihlanjululwe njengethambo le-walrus ebhajwe. Eenhlangothini zakhe zoke, ukubukeka okubukhali bekulandelwe ziindlela ezide zokugqwala okubomvu, lokha woke ama-spars wakhe kanye nama-rigging wakhe bekufana namagatja wemithi adephileko aphethwe yi-hoar-frost. Kwafakwa amaseyila wakhe aphasi kwaphela. Kwaba yinto emangalisako ukubona iintshebe zakhe ezinde ziqala ngaphandle eenhlokweni ezintathu zesikhonkwane. Bebabonakala bembethe isikhumba seenlwana, ngalokho badabukile begodu bakhupha izambatho ebegade zisinde iminyaka emine yokukhamba ngesikepe. Bajame ngama-hoop wensimbi abethelwe esiqwini, batjhinga begodu batjhinga phezu kwelwandle elingapheliko; begodu nanyana, lokha umkhumbi nawutjhelelako kancanikancani ngaphasi kwesiphephelo sethu, thina amadoda asithandathu emoyeni sasondelana khulu kangangobana besingaba pheze sisuka eenhlokweni zesikhonkwane somkhumbi ukuya komunye; kodwana, labo badobi ebegade baqaleka bengaboni, basiqala kancani njengombana bebadlula, akhenge batjho ngitjho litho ebantwini bethu, ngesikhathi isiqhotho se-quarter-deck sizwakala ngezansi.

"Umkhumbi ahoy! Uke wawubona umkhomo omhlophe?"

Kodwana njengombana umphathi ongakajayeleki, ancike phezu kweenqaba eziphaphathekileko, bekasesigabeni sokubeka icilongo lakhe emlonyeni wakhe, ngandlela thize lawela esandleni sakhe waya elwandle; begodu ummoya sele uphakama, walinga ukuzikhakhazisa ngaphandle kwawo. Ngaleso sikhathi umkhumbi wakhe bewusangezelela ibanga phakathi. Ngesikhathi ngeendlela ezihlukahlukeneko ezithulileko abasebenzi be-Pequod bebatjengisa ukutjheja kwabo isehlakalo esimbi lesi ekukhulunyweni kokuthoma kwebizo le-White Whale komunye umkhumbi, u-Ahabi wajama isikhatjhana; bekubonakala ngasuthi bekazokwehlisa isikepe ukobana akhwele umuntu angamaziko, ngathana umoya othusako bewungakavimbeli. Kodwana ngokusebenzisa ubujamo bakhe obuphaphathekako, waphinda wabamba icilongo lakhe, begodu ngokwazi bona umkhumbi ongaziwako bewuyi-Nantucketer begodu msinyana bewuzokubuyela ekhaya, warhuwelela khulu—“Ahoy lapha! Le yi-Pequod, ebotjhwe ephasini loke! Batjele bona bathumele zoke iincwadi zesikhathi esizako elwandle le-Pacific begodu iminyaka emithathu, nangabe ngingasekho ekhaya, ngitjele bona bakhulume nabo! ku --"

Ngaleso sikhathi ama-wake amabili bewawela kuhle, begodu msinyana, ngokuya ngeendlela zawo, iinhlambi ezincani ezinganabungozi, ebegade zibhukuda ngokuthula eduze kwethu amalanga ambalwa ngaphambilini, zabaleka ngeenhluthu ebegade zithuthumela, begodu zazihlukanisa phambili nangemuva ngamahlangothi womfokazi. Nanyana ngesikhathi samakhambo wakhe aragela phambili u-Ahabi kufuze bona kanengi wabona into efanako, kodwana, kiyo nanyana ngiyiphi indoda ethanda ukuzikhakhazisa, izinto ezincani khulu zineenhlathululo.

“Bhukuda usuke kimi, angithi?” kwabubula u-Ahabi, aqale ngaphakathi kwamanzi. Kwabonakala kuncani emagameni, kodwana ithoni beyidlulisa ukudana okukhulu okungasizi ngalitho ukudlula indlela iqhegu elihlanya ebegade lalitjengisa ngaphambilini. Kodwana wajika waya kumtjhayeli, obegade abambe umkhumbi emoyeni ukunciphisa ukuragela phambili kwawo, warhuwelela ngephimbo lakhe lebhubesi elidala,—“Phakamela isikepe! Mvimbele ephasini loke!”

Zizombeleze iphasi loke! Kunengi emsindweni loyo okukhuthaza imizwa yokuzikhakhazisa; kodwana koke lokho kujikeleza kuhamba ngaphi? Kuphela ngokusebenzisa iingozi ezinengi ukufikela lapho sithoma khona, lapho lezo ebesizitjhiya ngemva zivikelekile, bezihlala ziphambi kwethu.

Ngathana umhlaba lo bewulithafa elingapheliko, begodu ngokukhamba ngesikepe ukuya epumalanga besingakghona ukufikelela amabanga amatjha, begodu sithole iindawo ezimnandi nezingakajayeleki ukudlula nanyana ngiyiphi i-Cyclades nofana i-Islands yeKosi uSolomoni, bekuzokuba nesithembiso ekhambweni. Kodwana ngokuphishekela iimfihlakalo ezikude esiziphuphako, nofana ngokujaha okuhlunguphazako kwedimoni elithi, ngesinye isikhathi, libhukuda phambi kweenhliziyo zabantu boke; lokha nasigijimisa izinto ezinjalo phezu kwephasi eliyindilinga, zingasidosela phambili ema-mazeni angenalutho nofana phakathi nendlela zisitjhiye sithukiwe.
