ISAHLUKO 55. Yeenthombe ezimbi khulu zemikhomo.

Ngizokupenda isikhathi eside njengombana umuntu angakwenza ngaphandle kwekhanvasi, into efana nesimo samambala somkhomo njengombana ubonakala emehlweni womkhomo lokha nasemzimbeni wakhe opheleleko umkhomo ubotjhelelwe eceleni komkhumbi womkhomo ukwenzela bona unyathelwe lapho. Kungaba kuhle, ngalokho-ke, ngaphambilini ukukhangisa kilezo zithombe zakhe ezithakazelisako ezicatshangelwako ezitjhijilela ukukholwa komuntu wendawo. Sekuyisikhathi sokulungisa umhlaba endabeni le, ngokufakazela bona iinthombe ezinjalo zomkhomo zingakalungi.

Kungenzeka bona umthombo wokuthoma wazo zoke iinthombe ezikhohlisako uzokufumaneka hlangana nemifanekiso emidala khulu yamaHindu, yamaGibhide, namaGirigi. Solo kusukela eenkhathini zokusungula kodwana ezingakalungi lokha nakumaphaneli wemabula wamathempeli, eenqophelweni zemifanekiso, nasezihlangu, emamedalini, eenkomitjhini, kanye neenhlamvu zemali, idolfini beyidwetjhwe ngeenhluthu zezikhali ezifana neka-Saladin, kanye nehloko enesigqoko esifana neka-St. George; solo kwamhlokho kube nento efanako yelayisense, ingasi kwaphela eenthombeni ezithandwa khulu zomkhomo, kodwana neenthombe ezinengi zesayensi ngaye.

Nje, ngazo zoke iinqabo, isithombe sakade khulu esisekhona nanyana kunjalo esizibiza ngokuthi sisemkhomo, sitholakala emgodini odumileko we-Elephanta, e-India. Ama-Brahmin athi emifanekisweni engapheliko ye-pagoda leyo, yoke imisebenzi nokuphishekela, yoke imisebenzi yomuntu, bekuqalwe ngaphambilini ngaphambi kobana nanyana ngiyiphi yazo ibe khona. Akurari-ke, bona ngandlela thize umsebenzi wethu omuhle wokudoba imikhomo bewuzokuthunyelwa. I-Hindoo whale okukhulunywa ngayo, ivela emnyangweni ohlukileko webodeni, etjengisa ukuzalwa kwaka-Vishnu ngendlela ye-leviathan, eyaziwa ngokuthi yi-Matse Avatar. Kodwana nanyana isithombe esibaziweko siyihhafu yomuntu nehhafu ye-whale, ukwenzela bona kunikelwe umsila wokugcina, kodwana ingcenye encani yakhe ayikalungi. Ifana nomsila we-anaconda, ukudlula iintende ezibanzi zama-flukes we-true whale.

Kodwana iya emagalarini amadala, begodu uqale isithombe somdwebi omkhulu womKrestu wefesi le; ngombana akaphumeleli ngcono kunamaHindu wangaphambi kwesikhukhula. Yisithombe saka-Guido saka-Perseus ahlenga u-Andromeda esilwaneni selwandle nofana umkhomo. U-Guido wayithathaphi imodeli yesidalwa esingakajayeleki kangaka? Begodu u-Hogarth, ngokupenda isigcawu esifanako ku-“Perseus Descending” yakhe, akakwenzi ngcono. Ukukhuluphala okukhulu kwesilwane se-Hogarthian kuphaphatheka ngaphezulu, kungadoseli ngitjho ne-intjhi yamanzi. Inomhlobo we-howdah emhlane wayo, begodu umlomo wayo onamazinyo ovuvukileko lapho amagagasi agijima khona, ungathathwa ukuya eSangweni lamaTrayitor elisuka e-Thames ngamanzi ukuya e-Tower. Bese kuba nemikhomo ye-Prodromus ye-Scotch Sibbald endala, kanye nemikhomo ka-Jonah, njengombana kuvezwe emaphrintini wamaBhayibheli amadala kanye nokusikeka kwama-primers amadala. Kuzakuthiwani ngalokhu? Ngokuphathelene nomkhomo womhlanganisi weencwadi ojikeleza njengesiqu somvini esizungeze isitoko sesikhonkwane esehlako—njengombana ufakwe isitembu begodu ufakwe igolide ngemuva namakhasi weencwadi ezinengi ezindala nezintsha—lokho kuyisidalwa esihle khulu kodwana esimangalisako, esilingiswa, ngiyasithatha, ukusuka emifanekisweni efanako ema-antique vases. Nanyana ibizwa ngokuthi yidolfini, kodwana ngibiza ifesi yokubophelela iincwadi njengomzamo womkhomo; ngombana bekuhloswe njalo lokha idivayisi nayithoma ukwethulwa. Kwethulwe mtjhumayeli omdala we-Italy ngekhulu le-15, ngesikhathi sokuvuselelwa kokufunda; begodu ngalezo zinsuku, begodu bekube sesikhathini esidlulileko, amadolfini bekuthathwa njengomhlobo we-Leviathan.

Emavignethini neminye imihlobiso yeencwadi zakade ngezinye iinkhathi uzokuhlangana nokuthinta okukhulu khulu emkhomeni, lapho yoke imihlobo yama-spouts, ama-jets d'eau, imithombo etjhisako kanye namakhaza, i-Saratoga ne-Baden-Baden, ivela ebuchosheni bakhe obungakadinwa. Ekhasini lesihloko se-edishini yokuthoma ye-“Advancement of Learning” uzokufumana imikhomo efuna ukwazi.

Kodwana ukuyeka yoke imizamo engakalungi, akhe siqale iinthombe ze-leviathan ezithi zingakaphuzi, zihlukaniswe ngokwesayensi, ngalabo abaziko. Eqoqweni lamakhambo ka-Harris omdala kunamapuleti wemikhomo ekhishwe encwadini ye-Dutch yamakhambo, AD 1671, enesihloko esithi "Uhambo lokudoba imikhomo ukuya e-Spitzbergen emkhunjini u-Jonas in the Whale, u-Peter Peterson we-Friesland, umphathi." Kwelinye lamapuleti lawo imikhomo, njengemikhumbi emikhulu yamaplanka, itjengiswa ilele hlangana neenqhingi zeqhwa, namabhere amhlophe agijima phezu kwemihlana yawo. Kwelinye ipuleti, iphutha elikhulu lenziwe ngokujamela umkhomo ngama-fluke aqondileko.

Bese godu, kune-quarto ehle khulu, etlolwe nguKaputeni u-Colnett, uKaputeni wePosi we-England navy, enesihloko esithi "Uhambo olujikeleza i-Cape Horn ukuya eLwandle lweSewula, ngenhloso yokwandisa i-Spermaceti Whale Fisheries." Encwadini le kunohlaka oluthi "Isithombe somkhomo we-Physeter nofana we-Spermaceti, othathwe ngesikali ukusuka komunye owabulawa eligwini le-Mexico, ngoRhoboyi, 1793, begodu waphakanyiswa emkhunjini." Angithandabuzi bona ukaputeni bekathatha isithombe esihle kangaka ukwenzela bona kuzuze amasotja wakhe welwandle. Ukukhuluma ngento yinye ngakho, ake ngithi ineliso elisetjenziswa, ngokuya kwesikali esikhambisanako, kumkhomo wesidoda okhulileko, kungenza iso lalowo mkhomo libe yifasitela elingaba ziinyawo ezihlanu ubude. Hawu, umphathi wami onesibindi, kubayini ungasinikeli uJona aqale ngalelo lihlo!

Begodu akusiwo ukuhlanganiswa okuhle khulu kwe-History yemvelo ukuzuzisa abatjha nabasesebancani, akusiwo amaphutha afanako. Qala umsebenzi odumileko othi "Imvelo ye-Goldsmith's Animated." Emtlolweni omfitjhani we-London wango-1807, kunamapuleti we-“whale” ne-“narwhale.” Angifuni ukubonakala ngingasihle, kodwana umkhomo ongabonakaliko lo ubukeka njengengulube enqunyiweko; begodu, malungana ne-narwhale, ukuyibona nje kwanele ukumangaza umuntu, ukuthi ngekhulu leminyaka elitjhumi nathoba i-hippogriff enjalo ingathathwa njengomuntu wamambala kiwo woke umphakathi ohlakaniphileko wabantwana besikolo.

Bese, godu, ngo-1825, u-Bernard Germain, u-Count de Lacépède, isazi semvelo esikhulu, wagadangisa incwadi yemikhomo ehlelekileko yezesayensi, lapho kuneenthombe ezinengi zemihlobo ehlukileko ye-Leviathan. Konke lokhu akukalungi kwaphela, kodwana isithombe se-Mysticetus nofana i-Greenland whale (okutjho bona, i-Right whale), ngitjho no-Scoresby, indoda enesipiliyoni sokuthinta umhlobo loyo, umemezela bona akanawo umlingani wayo emvelweni.

Kodwana ukubekwa kwe-cap-sheaf kiyo yoke ibhizinisi engakalungi bekubekelwe isazi u-Frederick Cuvier, umfowabo ka-Baron odumileko. Ngo-1836, wagadangisa i-Natural History of Whales, lapho anikela khona lokho akubiza ngokuthi mfanekiso we-Sperm Whale. Ngaphambi kobana utjengise isithombe kibo boke abantu be-Nantucket, kungcono bona ulungiselele ukuphumula kwakho okufingqiweko ukusuka e-Nantucket. Ngamazwi ambalwa, i-Sperm Whale ka-Frederick Cuvier akusiyo i-Sperm Whale, kodwana i-squash. Kuliqiniso, azange abe nenzuzo yokukhamba ngesikepe (amadoda anjalo akakavami ukuba nawo), kodwana usithathephi isithombe leso, ngubani ongatjho? Mhlamunye wayifumana njengomuntu owandulelako wezesayensi emkhakheni ofanako, u-Desmarest, wathola enye yeendlela zakhe zokukhipha isisu eziqinisekileko; okutjho bona, ukusuka emdwebeni we-China. Begodu mhlobobani wamajaha aphilako anepensela lawo maTjhayina, iinkomitjhana ezinengi ezingakajayeleki namasosi asitjela.

Mayelana nemikhomo yabadwebi bamatshwayo ebonwa emigwaqweni ilenga phezu kwezitolo zabathengisi bamafutha, khuyini okuzakuthiwa ngayo? Ngokuvamileko ngu-Richard III. imikhomo, enama-dromedary humps, begodu ihlaza khulu; Ukudla isidlo sasekuseni ngama-tart amathathu nofana amane wemikhumbi, okutjho iinkepe zemikhomo ezizele abasebenzi bemikhumbi: ukukhubazeka kwabo kugcwala elwandle lweengazi nepende eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka.

Kodwana amaphutha amanengi la ekuvezeni umkhomo awamangalisi kangako. Tjheja! Inengi lemidwebo yezesayensi ithathwe emfesini ebhajwe; begodu lokhu kulungile njengomdwebo womkhumbi ophukileko, onomgogodla ophukileko, ungajamela kuhle isilwana esihle ngokwaso ngakho koke ukuzikhakhazisa kwaso okungapheliko kwesikhumba nama-spars. Nanyana iindlovu bezijamele ubude bazo obuzeleko, uLeviyathani ophilako akakaze aziphakamise ngokufaneleko ukwenzela isithombe sakhe. Umkhomo ophilako, ngobukhosi bawo obupheleleko nokuqakatheka, ubonakala kwaphela elwandle emanzini angazwisisekiko; begodu ukuntanta ubunengi bakhe abubonakali, njengomkhumbi wepi; begodu ngaphandle kwaleso sici yinto engekhe yenzeke unomphela bona umuntu ofako amphakamise emoyeni, ukwenzela bona alondoloze woke amagagasi wakhe anamandla. Futhi, singakhulumi ngomehluko omkhulu wekhonta hlangana nomkhomo omncani omunyisako kanye ne-Leviathan ye-Platonian ekhulileko; kodwana, ngitjho nasendabeni yomunye waleyo mikhomo emincani emunyisako ephakanyiselwe emkhunjini, ubujamo bakhe bunjalo obumangalisako, obufana ne-eel, obukhulu, obuhlukahlukeneko, ukuthi ubuso bakhe obuqondileko udeveli ngokwakhe bekangakghoni ukububamba.

Kodwana kungacatshangwa, bona ukusuka emthanjeni onqunu womkhomo ovalelekileko, amacebo anembileko angatholwa athinta ubujamo bakhe bamambala. Nakancani. Ngombana kungenye yezinto ezimangalisako ngeLeviyathani, ukuthi amathambo wakhe anikela umbono omncani khulu ngesimo sakhe. Nanyana amathambo ka-Jeremy Bentham, alengiswe njenge-candelabra elayibhrari yomunye wababulali bakhe, adlulisela ngokufaneleko umbono wendoda endala enamashiya amakhulu, nazo zoke ezinye iimpawu zika-Jeremy; kodwana akukho okunjalo okungaphethwa emathanjeni we-leviyathani ahlangeneko. Kuhlekuhle, njengombana uMzingeli omkhulu atjho, ithambo lomkhomo linobudlelwano obufanako nesilwana esifakwe ngokuzeleko begodu esinamaphedi njengombana isinambuzane senza ne-chrysalis eyigubuzele ngokuzeleko. Ubujamo obuhlukileko bubonakala khulu ekhanda, njengombana kwenye ingcenye yebhuku leli kuzokutjengiswa ngokungakalindeleki. Godu itjengiswa ngokumangalisako esiphepheni sehlangothini, amathambo wawo aphendula ngqo emathanjeni wesandla somuntu, ngaphandle kwesithupha kwaphela. Isiphini lesi sineminwe emine yamathambo, inkomba, phakathi, indandatho, nomunwe omncani. Kodwana zoke lezi zihlala unomphela esimbozweni sazo esinenyama, njengeminwe yomuntu esimbozweni sokufakelwa. "Nanyana umkhomo ungasiphatha kangangani," kwatjho u-Stubb ohlekisako ngelinye ilanga, "angeze kwathiwa uyasiphatha ngaphandle kwama-mittens."

Ngebanga lazo zoke iinzathu, nanyana ngiyiphi indlela ongayiqala ngayo, kufanele uphethe ngokuthi iLeviyathani ekulu yisidalwa sinye ephasini ekufuze sihlale singapendiwe bekube sekugcineni. Kuliqiniso, isithombe esisodwa singafika eduze khulu kunesinye, kodwana akekho ongakghona ukufikelela ngezinga eliphezulu lokunemba. Njeke ayikho indlela yokuthola bona umkhomo ubukeka njani kwamambala. Begodu indlela yodwa ongafumana ngayo ngitjho nomqondo ozwakalako wendlela yakhe yokuphila, kukuthi uyokudoba wena ngokwakho; kodwana ngokwenza njalo, ungekho engozini encani yokuba yisitofu esingapheliko begodu utjhinge nguye. Ngalokho-ke, kubonakala kimi bona kungcono bona ungabi msinyana ekufuneni kwakho ukwazi ngokuthinta iLeviyathani.





ISAHLUKO 56. Yeenthombe Ezingakalungi Zemikhomo, Neenthombe Zeqiniso Zeenthombe Zokudoba Imikhomo.

Malungana neenthombe ezimbi khulu zemikhomo, ngilingwa khulu lapha ukungena eendatjaneni ezimbi khulu zazo ezitholakala eencwadini ezithileko, zombili zakade nezesimanje, khulukhulu ku-Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, njll. Kodwana ngidlula indaba leyo.

Ngiyazi ama-outline amane kwaphela agadangisiweko we-Sperm Whale ekulu; u-Colnett, u-Huggins, u-Frederick Cuvier, no-Beale. Esahlukweni esidlulileko kukhulunywe ngabo u-Colnett no-Cuvier. Eka-Huggins ingcono khulu kuneyabo; kodwana, ngokungakajayeleki, u-Beale's ungcono khulu. Yonke imidwebo ka-Beale yomkhomo lo mihle, ngaphandle komfanekiso ophakathi esithombeni semikhomo emithathu eneendlela ezihlukahlukeneko, ukuvala isahluko sakhe sesibili. Umtlolo wakhe wangaphambili, iinkepe ezihlasela ama-Sperm Whales, nanyana kungathandabuzeki bona uzokuvusa ukungabaza kwabanye amadoda we-parlor, ulungile begodu ufana nokuphila ngomphumela wawo jikelele. Eminye imidwebo ye-Sperm Whale ku-J. Ross Browne ilungile khulu; kodwana ziqotjhwe kumbi. Lokho akusiwo umlandu wakhe.

Nge-Right Whale, iinthombe ezihle khulu ziku-Scoresby; kodwana zidwetshwe ngezinga elincani khulu ukudlulisa umbono omuhle. Unesithombe sinye kwaphela sezigcawu zokudoba imikhomo, begodu lokhu kukutlhayela okudanisako, ngombana kungeenthombe ezinjalo kwaphela, lokha nazenziwe kuhle, lapho ungathola khona nanyana yini efana nomqondo weqiniso womkhomo ophilako njengombana ubonwa bazingeli bakhe abaphilako.

Kodwana, nakuthathwa koke, okuhle khulu, nanyana kweminye imininingwana kungasi kuhle khulu, izethulo zemikhomo neenthombe zokudoba imikhomo ezingatholakala nanyana kukuphi, ziinthombe ezimbili ezinkulu zesiFulentshi, ezenziwe kuhle, begodu zithathwe emidwebeni ye-Garnery eyodwa. Ngokulandelana, zijamele ukuhlaselwa kwe-Sperm ne-Right Whale. Ekuqotjhweni kokuthoma i-Sperm Whale ehloniphekileko ivezwe ngamandla apheleleko, isanda kuphakama ngaphasi kwesikepe ukusuka ekujuleni kwelwandle, begodu ithwele phezulu emoyeni emhlane wakhe ukuphahlazeka okumbi kwamapulangwe wesitofu. I-prow yesikepe ayikaphuki, begodu idonswe ngokulinganisela emgogodleni wesilo; begodu ujame ngaphakathi kwe-prow, isikhathi esisodwa esingabalekiko, ubona umgwedli, uhhafu agubuzelwe mlomo ovuthako womkhomo, begodu usesenzweni sokugxuma, ngasuthi uvela emrhobeni. Isenzo sento yoke sihle begodu siliqiniso. Ibhathi yelayini enganalutho isigamu intanta elwandle elimhlophe; iingodo zamapulangwe ezichithekileko zitjhinga kiyo; iinhloko zesiqhema sokubhukuda zihlakazeke emkhomeni ngendlela ehlukileko yokuthuka; ngesikhathi umkhumbi utjhinga phasi endaweni yesehlakalo. Iphutha elikhulu lingatholakala emininingwaneni yomzimba womkhomo lo, kodwana ake lokho kudlule; ngombana, epilweni yami, bengingakwazi ukudweba okuhle kangaka.

Emtlolweni wesibili, isikepe sidweba eceleni kwe-barnacled flank ye-Right Whale ekulu egijimako, egijima umzimba wayo omnyama onokhula elwandle njengelitje elinama-mossy elivela emaweni we-Patagonian. Iinqhema zakhe zimile, zizele, begodu zimnyama njenge-soot; ukwenzela bona ukusuka entuthuni enengi kangaka etjhimini, ungacabanga bona kufuze kube nesidlo sakusihlwa esinesibindi esiphekwa emathunjini amakhulu ngezansi. Iinkukhu zaselwandle zikhotha amakala amancani, iimfesi zamagobolondo, namanye amaswidi welwandle nama-macaroni, okuyinto i-Right Whale kesinye isikhathi eyithwala emhlane wayo onesifo. Begodu soke isikhathi ileviyathani enemilebe emide igijima phakathi kwemanzini atjhingako, itjhiya amathani we-curds amhlophe anesiphithiphithi emva kwayo, begodu yenza bona isikepe esincani sinyakazise emagagasini njengesikepe esibanjwe eduze namasondo wesikepe se-ocean. Ngalokho, indawo yangaphambili inesiphithiphithi esivuthako; kodwana ngemva, ngokuhlukileko kwezobuciko, kukhona izinga elifana nengilazi lolwandle oluthulileko, amaseyila angafakwa isitatshi womkhumbi onganamandla, kanye nobukhulu obungenzi litho bomkhomo ofileko, inqaba ehluliweko, enefulegi lokubanjwa elilenga ngobuvila esibondeni somkhomo esifakwe ngaphakathi komlomo wayo.

Ngubani u-Garnery umdwebi, nofana bekangubani, angazi. Kodwana ipilo yami bekakwazi kuhle isihloko sakhe, nofana bekafundiswa kuhle khulu mdosi wemikhomo onelwazi. AmaFulentshi ngamajaha wokudweba isenzo. Hamba uyokuqala yoke imidwebo ye-Europe, begodu uzokufumana kuphi igalari enjalo yokuphila nokuphefumula kwephithiphithi phezu kwekhanvasi, njengehholo lokuphumelela e-Versailles; lapho umuntu oqalako alwela khona, ngezipi ezinkulu ezilandelanako ze-France; lapho woke amasabula abonakala njengokukhanya kwe-Northern Lights, begodu amakhosi namaKhosi ahlomileko alandelana agijima, njengehlaselo lama-centaurs ahlomileko? Akukafaneli bona kube nendawo egalari leyo, ziinqhema zepi yelwandle ze-Garnery.

Ubuhlakani bemvelo bamaFrentshi wokuthatha ubuhle bezinto kubonakala bubonakala ngokungakajayeleki emidwebeni nemifanekisweni abanayo yezigcawu zabo zokudoba imikhomo. Njengombana i-England ingasi ingcenye yeshumi yesipiliyoni sokudoba, begodu ingasi ingcenye eyikulungwana yama-American, nanyana kunjalo banikele amazwe womabili imidwebo eqediweko kwaphela ekwazi ukudlulisa umoya wamambala wokuzingela imikhomo. Esikhathini esinengi, abadwebi bemikhomo be-England ne-America babonakala basuthiseka ngokuveza umtlhala wemitjhini wezinto, njengephrofayili ye-whale enganamuntu; okuthi, ngokuya ngobuhle bomphumela, kufana nokudweba iphrofayili yepiramidi. Ngitjho no-Scoresby, umkhomo we-Right odumileko, ngemva kokusinikela ubude obupheleleko bomkhomo we-Greenland, kanye nama-miniature amathathu nofana amane wama-narwhale nama-porpoises, usiphathisa uchungechunge lwemifanekiso yeklasikhi yeengwegwe zesikepe, imimese yokusika, kanye nama-grapnels; begodu ngokuzikhandla okuncani kwe-Leuwenhoeck unikela ekuhlolweni kwephasi elithuthumelako ama-fac-similes amatjhumi alithoba nesithandathu wamakristalu weqhwa we-Arctic akhulisiweko. Angitjho ukuhlambalaza umhambi omuhle khulu (ngiyamhlonipha njengomdlali wesiqhwaga), kodwana endabeni eqakathekileko kangaka bekukungabi nokutjheja ukungatholi i-afidavithi efungelweko ethathwe phambi kwe-Greenland Justice of the Peace.

Ngaphezu kwaleyo mifanekiso emihle evela ku-Garnery, kuneminye imifanekiso emibili yesiFulentshi efanele ukutjhejwa, eyenziwe ngomunye umuntu ozibiza ngokuthi "H. Durand." Elinye lazo, nanyana lingakalungiswa kuhle emnqophweni wethu wamanje, nanyana kunjalo lifanelwe kukhulunywa ngalo kwezinye iindaba. Yindawo ethulileko yemini hlangana neenhlengele ze-Pacific; umdosi wemikhomo we-France ujame, ogwini, ngokuthula, begodu ngobuvila uthatha amanzi ngaphakathi kwesikepe; amaseyila womkhumbi akhululekileko, namakari amade weensundu ngemuva, zombili ziwela ndawonye emoyeni ongenammoya. Umphumela muhle khulu, nawuqalwa ngokuqalisele ekuvezeni kwawo abadobi abaqinileko ngaphasi kwenye yezinto zabo ezimbalwa zokuphumula kwepumalanga. Okunye ukuqotjhwa yindaba ehluke khulu: umkhumbi uphaphazela elwandle elivulekileko, begodu usenhliziyweni yokuphila kwe-Leviyathani, nomkhomo we-Right eceleni; umkhumbi (ngesenzo sokusika) utjhingela esilwaneni sengathi uya esikhumulweni; begodu isikepe, esisuka msinyana esiqhemeni somsebenzi, sizokusukela imikhomo ebudeni. Ngabanye abahlaziyi bemvelo abake bezwa amahemuhemu wesidalwa esingazwisisekiko, esikhulunywa ngaso lapha, sifakwe hlangana nesigaba se-cuttle-fish, lapho, kwamambala, ngeendlela ezithileko zangaphandle kungabonakala ngasuthi singokwaso, kodwana njenge-Anak yesizwe kwaphela.





ISAHLUKO 60. Umugqa.

Ngokuya ngesigcawu sokudoba imikhomo esizokuchazwa msinyana, kanye nokuzwisisa ngcono zoke iinthombe ezifanako kwezinye iindawo ezivezwe, lapha ngizokukhuluma ngomlingo, kwesinye isikhathi omumbi khulu.

Intambo ebegade isetjenziswe ekudobeni beyiyi-hemp engcono khulu, ebegade ifakwe i-tar, ingafakwanga, njengeentambo ezijayelekileko; ngombana ngitjho nanyana itiyela, njengombana isetjenziswa ngokujayelekileko, yenza bona i-hemp ibe lula kumenzi wentambo, begodu yenza bona intambo ngokwayo ibe lula kumtjhayeli wesikepe bona isetjenziswe emkhunjini; kodwana, ingasi kwaphela bona inani elijayelekileko lingaqinisa umugqa womkhomo malungana nokugoqa okuseduze okufanele ube ngaphasi kwawo; kodwana njengombana inengi labasebenzi bemikhumbi sele lithoma ukufunda, itayela ngokuvamileko alingezeleli ukuqina kwentambo nofana amandla, nanyana ingayenza ibe mncani kangangani nokukhanya.

Eminyakeni edlulileko intambo ye-Manilla sele ithatha indawo ye-hemp njengento yokwenza imikhomo; ngombana, nanyana ingasi isikhathi eside njenge-hemp, inamandla, begodu ithambile begodu iyathamba; begodu ngizokungezelela (ngombana kune-æsthetics kizo zoke izinto), muhle khulu begodu ufanele isikepe, kune-hemp. I-Hemp mnyama, mnyama, umhlobo wamaNdiya; kodwana u-Manilla ufana ne-Circassian eneenhluthu ezigolide.

Umtjhini womkhomo ubukhulu be-intshi ezimbili kwezintathu kwaphela. Ekuthomeni, angeze wacabanga bona inamandla kangaka njengombana injalo kwamambala. Ngokulinga iintambo zayo ezili-1 namatjhumi amahlanu zizokuphakamisela isisindo samaphawundi alikhulu namatjhumi amabili; ukwenzela bona intambo yoke ithwale umthwalo olingana namathani amathathu. Ngobude, umugqa we-sperm whale ovamileko ulinganisa into engaphezu kwama-fathoms amakhulu amabili. Ngasemuva kwesikepe igoqwe ngokugoqeka ngaphakathi kwebhadi, ingasi njengepayipi ye-worm-though, kodwana ukwenzela bona yenze isiqubuthu esisodwa esiyindilinga, esibunjwe njenge-cheese se-“sheaves” eziminyeneko, namkha amalayini wokujikeleza okuhlangeneko, ngaphandle komgodi kodwana “ihliziyo,” namkha itjhubhu encani ejame ngqo eyakhiwe e-eksisi ye-cheese. Njengombana ukutjhabalala okuncani namkha ukutjhinga ekugoqeni kungathatha ingalo, umlenze, namkha umzimba woke womuntu, kusetjenziswa ukutjheja okukhulu ekubekeni umugqa ngaphakathi kwebhadi layo. Abanye abadlali be-harpoon bazokuthatha isikhathi eside ekuseni emsebenzini lo, bathwele umugqa phezulu phezulu bese bawugoqa phasi ngebhlogo ukuya ebhadini, ukwenzela bona bawugoqele ukuwutjhaphulula kiwo woke ama-wrinkl kanye nama-twist.

Eenkepeni zamaNgisi kusetjenziswa amabhabha amabili esikhundleni somunye; umugqa ofanako uragela phambili nokugoqwa kiwo womabili amabhati. Kunenzuzo ethize kilokhu; ngombana ama-twin-tubs la mancani kangaka angenela lula ngaphakathi kwesikepe, begodu awasigandeleli kangako; kanti, umbhobho we-American, opheze ube ziinyawo ezintathu ngobubanzi begodu onobude obufanako, wenza umthwalo omkhulu wesikepe esinamapulangwe angaba yi-intshi eyodwa ubukhulu; ngombana iphasi lesikepe somkhomo lifana neqhwa eliqakathekileko, elizokuthwala ubudisi obukhulu, kodwana ingasi obukhulu khulu. Lokha isembozo sekhanvasi esipendiweko nasibekwa phezu kwe-American line-tub, isikepe sibukeka ngasuthi sisuka nekhekhe lomtjhado elihle khulu elizokunikela imikhomo.

Zombili iinphetho zomugqa zivulekile; umphetho ongezansi ophelela nge-eye-splice nofana i-loop evela phezulu ukusuka phasi ngokuya ngehlangothini lebhabha, begodu ulenga phezu komphetho wayo ungahlanganisi ngokupheleleko kikho koke. Ilungiselelo lokuphela okungezansi liyatlhogeka ema-akhawundini amabili. Kokuthoma: Ukuze kube lula ukubophelela kiyo intambo eyengeziweko evela esikepeni esiseduze, nakungenzeka bona umkhomo ohlasiweko ungazwakala ujule khulu kangangobana ungasongela ukuthwala yoke intambo ebegade ihlanganiswe ne-harpoon. Ebujameni obunjalo, umkhomo utjhugululwa njengekomitjhi ye-ale, njengombana kunjalo, ukusuka kwesinye isikepe ukuya kwesinye; nanyana isikepe sokuthoma sihlala siseduze ukusiza umlingani waso. Okwesibili: Ilungiselelo leli liqakathekile ukwenzela ukuphepha kwabantu; ngombana ngabe umphetho ongezansi womzila unamathele nganoma ngiyiphi indlela esikepeni, begodu nangabe umkhomo bewuzokugijima umzila ukuya ekugcineni pheze ngomzuzu owodwa, obhemako njengombana enza kesinye isikhathi, bekangasoze ame lapho, ngombana isikepe esilahlekileko besizokudonswa phasi ngemva kwakhe siye ekujuleni kwelwandle; begodu ngaleso simo akekho ummeli wedorobha ongaphinde amthole.

Ngaphambi kokuthi kwehliswe isikepe ukujaha, umphetho ongaphezulu welayini uthathwa ngemva kwebhadi, bese udlula lapho, uphinde uqhutshwe phambili ubude boke besikepe, uphumule ngokuphambana phezu kwe-loom nofana isibambo segwegwe lomuntu ngamunye, ukwenzela bona sigijime esihlakaleni sakhe lokha nawugwedla; begodu nokudlula hlangana namadoda, njengombana bahlala eendaweni ezihlukeneko, ukuya eendaweni ezine-lead noma ema-groove ngaphakathi kwe-prow yesikepe, lapho ipini yamapulangwe nofana i-skewer elilingana ne-quill evamileko, ivimbela bona ingatjheleli ngaphandle. Kusukela ema-chocks ilenga nge-festoon encani phezu kweminsalo, bese idluliselwa ngaphakathi kwesikepe godu; begodu ama-fathoms alitjhumi nofana amatjhumi amabili (okubizwa ngokuthi yi-box-line) agoqelwe phezu kwebhokisi emabhokisini, iragela phambili nendlela yayo eya e-gunwale esesemuva kancani, bese inamathela ku-short-warp—intambo ehlangene msinyana ne-harpoon; kodwana ngaphambi kokuthintana lokho, ukuguquguquka okufitjhani kudlula eemfihlakalweni ezihlukahlukeneko ezikhathazako ukuhlathulula imininingwana.

Ngalokho umugqa wemikhomo ugoqa isikepe soke ngamakhoyili wawo ahlangahlangeneko, ujikeleza begodu ugoqa pheze kiyo yoke indawo. Boke abagwedli bazibandakanya ekuguqukeni kwayo okuyingozi; ukwenzela bona emehlweni anamahloni womuntu wendawo, babonakala njengabadlali bama-India, ngeenyoka ezibulalako ezihlobisa amalunga wazo. Begodu nanyana ngiyiphi indodana yowesifazana ofako, kokuthoma, angeze yazihlalela hlangana nezinto eziyinkimbinkimbi ze-hemp, begodu ngesikhathi igandelela ngamandla ayo woke ukugwedla, imcabange bona nganoma ngisiphi isikhathi esingaziwako i-harpoon ingadutshulwa, begodu yoke imiraro emimbi le ingafakwa njengemibane eneendandatho; angeze abe nobujamo obunjalo ngaphandle kokuthuthumela okwenza umnkantsha emathanjeni wakhe ukuthuthumela kuye njengejeli ethuthumelako. Kodwana umkhuba—into engakajayeleki! khuyini umkhuba ongakghoni ukuyenza?—Ukuthaba okuhle, ukuthaba okukhulu, amahlaya angcono, nokuphendula okukhanyako, ongakaze ukuzwe phezu kwe-mahogany yakho, kunalokho ozokuzwa phezu kwe-cedar emhlophe eyingcenye ye-intshi yesikepe somkhomo, lokha nayilengiswe ngaleyo ndlela eentanjeni ze-manman; begodu, njengama-burgers asithandathu we-Calais ngaphambi kweKosi u-Edward, amadoda asithandathu akha isiqhema adosela emihlathini yokufa, nge-halter entanyeni yoke, njengombana ungatjho.

Mhlamunye umcabango omncani khulu uzokwenza bona ukghone ukulandisa ngeenhlekelele eziphindaphindiweko zokudoba imikhomo—ezinye zazo ezitlolwa ngokungakaqakatheki—zokuthi indoda le namkha indoda leyo ikhutjhwe esikepeni ngomtjhini, bese ilahleka. Ngombana, lokha umzila nawuphumela ngaphandle, ukuhlala ngaphakathi kwesikepe, kufana nokuhlala hlangana nokuvunguza okunengi kwenjini yomtjhiso edlala ngokuzeleko, lokha yoke imisebe endizako, neshafu, namasondo, kukudla. Kumbi khulu; ngombana angeze wahlala ungashukumi hlangana neentjhijilo lezi, ngombana isikepe siyasikinyeka njengombhede, begodu uphoswe ngakelinye ihlangothi, ngaphandle kwesiyeleliso esincani; begodu ngokuzilungisa okuthileko nokuzimisela kanyekanye kwentando nesenzo, ungaphunyuka ekwenzeni i-Mazeppa, begodu ubaleke lapho ilanga elibona koke ngokwalo lingakghoni ukukubhoboza khona.

Godu: njengombana ukuthula okukhulu okubonakala kwandulela begodu kuphrofetha isiwuruwuru, mhlamunye kumbi ukudlula isiwuruwuru ngokwaso; ngombana, kwamambala, ukuthula kuyisigoqo nesiphephelo sesiwuruwuru; begodu iqukethe kiyo, njengombana isibhamu esibonakala singalimazi siphethe ipuyere ebulalako, nebholo, nokuqhuma; ngalokho ukuphumula okuhle kwelayini, njengombana ithule iphaphatheka ngabagwedli ngaphambi kobana ingeniswe emdlalweni wamambala—lokhu yinto ethwala ukwesaba kwamambala ukudlula nanyana ngiyiphi enye into yendaba eyingozi. Kodwana kubayini kutjho okunengi? Woke amadoda aphila ahlanganiswe ziintambo zemikhomo. Boke babelethwa banamathambo eentanyeni zabo; kodwana kulapho abanjwa msinyana, ngokuzumako kokufa, lapho abantu babona khona ubungozi obuthulileko, obufihlakeleko, obuhlala bukhona bepilo. Begodu nangabe uyifilosofi, nanyana uhlezi esikepeni semikhomo, angeze wezwa ukwesaba okukhulu, kunokuthi uhlezi phambi komlilo wakho wantambama une-poker, ingasi i-harpoon, eceleni kwakho.
