ISAHLUKO 123. I-Musket.

Ngesikhathi sokuthuthumela okunamandla khulu kwe-Typhoon, indoda eyayise-Pequod's jaw-bone tiller gade iphoselwe kanengi emkhunjini ngokunyakaza kwayo okuvuvukako, nanoma-nje ama-preventer tackles anamathiselwe kiyo—ngombana begade atjhinga—ngombana ukudlala okuthileko ku-tiller bekutlhogeka khulu.

Esiwuruwuruni esinamandla esinjengalesi, ngesikhathi umkhumbi uyi-shuttlecock ephoselwe ukuqhuma, akukajayeleki ukubona iinalithi emakhamphasini, ngezikhathi ezithileko, zijikeleza. Amaseyila amatjha amathathu akhambelana nawo besele agobile begodu ahlanganiswa, begodu iseyilu ye-storm-trysail beyibekwe ngemva; kangangobana umkhumbi msinyana wadlula emanzini ngokunemba okuthileko godu; begodu indlela—njenganje, ipumalanga-sewula-mpumalanga—ebekufuze ayiqondise, nangabe kungenzeka, yaphinde yanikelwa umtjhayeli wesikepe.
