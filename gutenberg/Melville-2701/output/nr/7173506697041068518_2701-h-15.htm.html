ISAHLUKO 74. Ikhanda le-Sperm Whale—Umbono Ophikisanako.

Lapha, nje, kunemikhomo emibili emikhulu, ibeke iinhloko zayo ndawonye; asibahlanganise, sihlanganise eyethu.

Ku-oda elikhulu lama-folio leviathan, i-Sperm Whale ne-Right Whale ngizo eziqakatheke khulu. Yibo bodwa imikhomo ehlala izingelwa babantu. Ku-Nantucketer, baveza ubujamo obubili obudluleleko bazo zoke iinhlobo ezaziwako zomkhomo. Njengombana umehluko wangaphandle hlangana nabo ubonakala khulu emahlombe wabo; begodu njengehloko yomunye nomunye umzuzu lo ulenga ehlangothini laka-Pequod; begodu njengombana singakghona ukusuka kwenye ukuya kwenye, ngokudlula nje emkhunjini:—ngingathanda ukwazi, ungafumana kuphi ithuba elingcono lokufunda i-cetology esebenzako kunalapha?

Ekuthomeni, uyamangazwa mahluko hlangana namakhanda la. Bobabili bakhulu ngokwaneleko ngonembeza woke; kodwana kunokulingana okuthileko kweembalo ku-Sperm Whale okuyinto enganakho yi-Right Whale. Kunobuntu obunengi ekhanda le-Sperm Whale. Njengombana uyibona, ngokungazimiseli unikela ubukhulu obukhulu kuye, malungana nesithunzi esibanzi. Esigabeni samanje, nayo, isithunzi lesi siphakanyiswa mbala wepelepele netshwayi wekhanda lakhe esiqongweni, okunikela itshwayo lobudala nesipiliyoni esikhulu. Ngamafuphi, ngilokho abadobi abakubiza ngokuthi "umkhomo onenhloko empunga."

Akhe sitjheje ukuthi khuyini engafani khulu emakhanda la—okutjho, izitho ezimbili eziqakatheke khulu, ilihlo nendlebe. Kude emuva ngehlangothini lekhanda, begodu phasi, eduze kwe-engele yomhlathi womkhomo, nangabe ufuna kancani, uzokugcina ubona iso elingenaenhluthu, ongacabanga bona liliso lethole elincani; njeke ngaphandle kwakho koke ukulingana kuya ngobukhulu bekhanda.

Nje, ukusuka endaweni engakajayeleki yamehlo womkhomo, kuyakhanya bona angeze wakghona ukubona into ephambili ngqo, ngaphandle kokuthi angayibona ngemuva ngqo. Ngamazwi ambalwa, ubujamo bamehlo womkhomo bufana nebujamo beendlebe zomuntu; begodu ungazicabangela, ngokwakho, bona bekungaba njani kuwe, ingabe uhlolisise izinto ngeendlebe zakho. Uzokufumana bona ungakwazi ukulawula amadigri angaba matjhumi amathathu wokubona ngaphambi kokubona okuqondileko; begodu abanye abangaba matjhumi amathathu ngemva kwayo. Nangabe isitha sakho esibuhlungu khulu besikhamba siqonde kuwe, siphakanyiswe inkemba emini, bewungekhe ukwazi ukusibona, njengombana besikweba ngemuva. Ngamazwi ambalwa, ungaba nemihlane emibili, ngaleyo ndlela; kodwana, ngesikhathi esifanako, godu, amaphambili amabili (amaphambili aseceleni): ngombana khuyini eyenza umuntu abe phambili—yini, kwamambala, ngaphandle kwamehlo wakhe?

Ngaphezu kwalokho, ngitjho nanyana kezinye iinlwana ezinengi engicabanga ngazo, amehlo atjalwe ngendlela engabonakaliko ukuhlanganisa amandla wazo wokubona, ukwenzela bona akhiqize isithombe sinye ingasi ezimbili ebuchosheni; ubujamo obungakajayeleki bamehlo womkhomo, ahlukaniswe kuhle ngamamitha amanengi wehloko eqinileko, ephakama hlangana kwawo njengentaba ekulu ehlukanisa amachibi amabili emagodini; lokhu, kunjalo, kufanele kuhlukanise ngokupheleleko imibono isitho ngasinye esizijameleko esinikela ngaso. Ngalokho-ke, umkhomo kufanele ubone isithombe esisodwa esihlukileko ngehlangothini, nesinye isithombe esihlukileko ngehlangothini; ngesikhathi koke okuphakathi kufanele kube mnyama khulu nokungasililitho kuye. Umuntu, eqinisweni, kuthiwa uqala ngaphandle ephasini asebhokisini lokugada elinamabhande amabili ahlanganisiweko efasidereni lakhe. Kodwana ngomkhomo, amabhande amabili la afakwa ngokwehlukana, okwenza amafasitela amabili ahlukileko, kodwana ngokudanisako kuphazamisa ukubona. Ubujamo obuhlukileko bamehlo womkhomo yinto ekufuze ihlale ikhunjulwa emsebenzini wokudoba; begodu ikhunjulwe mfundi kezinye iinthombe ezilandelako.

Umbuzo omangalisako nodida khulu ungathoma malungana nendaba le ebonakalako njengokuthinta iLeviyathani. Kodwana kufanele ngisuthiseke ngesiluleko. Inqobo nje nangabe amehlo womuntu avulekile ekukhanyeni, isenzo sokubona asizithandi; okutjho bona, angeze akghone ukusiza ngokubona nanyana ngiziphi izinto eziphambi kwakhe. Nanyana kunjalo, okuhlangabezanwe nakho komuntu kuzomfundisa, bona nanyana angakghona ukuthatha izinto ngokungakhethi ngokubuka kanye, akukghonakali kuye, ngokutjheja, nangokupheleleko, ukuhlola nanyana ngiziphi izinto ezimbili—loba zikulu kangangani namkha zincani kangangani—ngesikhatjhana sinye; ungazikhathazi nangabe balala eceleni begodu bathintane. Kodwana nangabe uzokuhlukanisa izinto ezimbili lezi, begodu uzungeze enye nenye ngesiyingi sobumnyama obukhulu; khona-ke, ukwenzela bona ubone enye yazo, ngendlela yokuthi ulethe ingqondo yakho bona ibekezele kiyo, enye izokukhishwa ngokupheleleko emqondweni wakho wesikhatheso. Kunjani-ke, ngomkhomo? Kuliqiniso, amehlo wakhe womabili, ngokwawo, kufuze asebenze ngasikhathi sinye; kodwana ingabe ubuqhopho bakhe buphelele khulu, buhlanganisa, begodu bufihlakele ukudlula bomuntu, kangangobana angakwazi ukuhlola ngokutjheja amathemba amabili ahlukileko, enye ngehlangothini lakhe, enye ngehlangothini eliphikisanako? Nangabe angakghona, khona-ke kuyinto emangalisako kuye, njengokungathi umuntu angakghona ukudlula ngesikhathi sinye ukutjengiswa kwemiraro emibili ehlukileko ku-Euclid. Begodu, nakuphenywa ngokuqinileko, akukho ukungavumelani kilokhu kumadanisa.

Kungenzeka kube yinto engasizi ngalitho, kodwana bekusolo kubonakala kimi, ukuthi ukunyakaza okungakajayeleki kokunyakaza okutjengiswa eminye imikhomo lokha nayihlaselwa ziinkepe ezintathu nofana ezine; ukuhlazeka nokuba nesibopho sokwesaba okungakajayeleki, okujayelekileko emikhomeni enjalo; Ngicabanga bona koke lokhu kuvela ngokungakaqondi ekudidekeni okungenasizo kokuzithandela, lapho amandla wabo wokubona ahlukeneko begodu aphikisana khulu kufuze ababandakanye khona.

Kodwana indlebe yomkhomo izele ukufuna ukwazi njengelihlo. Nangabe awusazi isitjhaba sabo, ungazingela phezu kwamakhanda amabili la ama-awa amanengi, begodu ungasoze usithole isitho leso. Indlebe ayinalo iqabunga langaphandle; begodu emgodini ngokwawo awukwazi ukufaka i-quill, ngalokho-ke mncani khulu. Ihlala kancani ngemva kwelihlo. Malungana neendlebe zazo, umehluko oqakathekileko lo kufanele uqalwe hlangana nomkhomo wesidoda nesokudla. Nanyana indlebe yokuthoma inendawo evulekileko yangaphandle, eyokugcina imbozwe ngokulinganako nge-membrane, ukwenzela bona ingabonakali ngaphandle.

Akusiyo into emangalisako na, bona umuntu omkhulu kangaka njengomkhomo ungabona umhlaba ngelihlo elincani kangaka, begodu uzwe ukuduma ngendlebe encani kunemvundla? Kodwana ngathana amehlo wakhe bekabanzi njengelensi yesibonakude esikhulu saka-Herschel; neendlebe zakhe zivuleke njengamaveranda weenkonzo; lokho kungamenza angakwazi ukubona isikhathi eside, nofana ukuzwa okubukhali? Akunjalo.—Kubayini-ke ulinga ‘ukukhulisa’ ingqondo yakho? Yifake ngaphasi.

Nje-ke, nganoma ngiziphi iinleva neenjini zomtjhiso esinazo, sibeke phezu kwehloko yomkhomo wesidoda, ukwenzela bona ilele phasi phezulu; bese, ukhuphuka ngesitebhisi uye esiqongweni, ube nokuhlolisisa phasi emlonyeni; begodu ngathana umzimba sele uhlukaniswe ngokupheleleko kiwo, ngesibane besingasehlela emgedeni omkhulu we-Kentucky Mammoth wesisu sakhe. Kodwana asibambelele lapha ngalizinyo, begodu sikhangele lapho esikhona. Umlomo muhle kangangani begodu ubukeka uhlanzekile! ukusuka phasi ukuya ephahleni, ilayiniwe, nofana iphepha nge-membrane emhlophe ecwebezelayo, ecwebezelayo njengama-satin womlobokazi.

Kodwana phuma nje, uqale umhlathi ongaphasi, obonakala njengesivalo eside esincani sebhokisi elikhulu le-snuff, eline-hinge ngakelinye ihlangothi, kunokobana kube ngehlangothini linye. Nawuyiphakamisa, ukwenzela bona ibe phezulu, begodu uveze imigqa yamazinyo wayo, ibonakala iyi-portcullis ehle khulu; begodu okunjalo, maye! kufakazela ebantwini abanengi abangasebenzi kuhle emsebenzini wokudoba, lapho ama-spike la awela khona ngamandla. Kodwana kumbi khulu ukubona, lokha nakufika phasi elwandle, ubona umkhomo othukutheleko, untanta lapho ulengiswe, ngomhlathi wakhe omkhulu, ubude obungaba ziinyawo ezilitjhumi nahlanu, ulenga ngqo phasi ngokuqondileko nomzimba wakhe, ephasini loke njenge-jib-boom yomkhumbi. Umkhomo lo awukafi; uphelelwa mamandla kwaphela; ngaphandle kwemihlobo, mhlamunye; i-hypochondriac; begodu alele kangaka, kangangobana ama-hinges womhlathi wakhe akhululekile, amtjhiya lapho asebujamweni obungakajayeleki, ukuhlambalaza kibo boke isizwe sakhe, okumele, ngokungangabazeki, bamhlukumeze.

Esikhathini esinengi umhlathi ongaphasi lo—owuvulwa lula mdwebi onelwazi—uyakhululwa begodu uphakanyiswe emkhunjini ngehloso yokukhupha amazinyo wendlovu, begodu unikele ngethambo elimhlophe eliqinileko lapho abadobi benza khona yoke imihlobo yezinto ezimangalisako, okufakahlangana iintonga, izambulela, kanye nezandla.

Ngesiphakamiso eside, esidiniweko umhlathi udoselwa ebhodini, kube ngasuthi yisikhonkwane; begodu lokha isikhathi esifaneleko nasifikako—emva kwamalanga ambalwa ngemva komunye umsebenzi—u-Queequeg, u-Daggoo, no-Tashtego, njengabodorhodera bamazinyo abanekghono, bazokudweba amazinyo. Nge-cutting-spede ebukhali, u-Queequeg ushaya iinsini; bese umhlathi ubotjhwa phasi emabhodini, begodu i-tackle ifakwe phezulu, bakhupha amazinyo la, njengombana iinkabi ze-Michigan zidosa iinqubu zama-oki amadala ngaphandle kwamahlathi weganga. Ngokuvamileko kunamazinyo amatjhumi amane nambili woke; emikhomeni emidala, egugile khulu, kodwana engakaboli; namkha izaliswe ngemva kwefeshini yethu yokufakelwa. Umhlathi ngemva kwalokho uyasahlwa ube ziingcezu, bese uqoqwa njengama-joists wokwakha izindlu.





ISAHLUKO 75. Ihloko yomkhomo wesidla—Umbono ophikisanako.

Nasiwela umkhumbi, ake sibe nokuqala okude ehloko ye-Right Whale.

Njengombana ikhanda le-Sperm Whale elihle lingafaniswa nekoloyi yepi yamaRoma (khulukhulu ngaphambili, lapho iyindilinga khona); njeke, ngokubuka okubanzi, ihloko ye-Right Whale ifana khulu nesicathulo esikhulu esinezinzwane ze-galliot. Eminyakeni emakhulu amabili eyadlulako umhambi omdala we-Dutch wafanisa ukwakheka kwayo nowokugcina womenzi wezicathulo. Begodu ngalesi sicathulo sokugcina, umfazi omdala wendaba ye-nursery, oneenlwanyana ezinengi, angahlaliswa kuhle, yena nabo boke abantwana bakhe.

Kodwana njengombana utjhidela ekhanda elikhulu leli lithoma ukuthatha izinto ezihlukileko, ngokuya ngombono wakho. Nawujame esiqongweni sayo begodu uqale imigodi emibili emise oko-F, ungathatha ihloko yoke njenge-bass-viol ekulu, kanye nama-spiracles, ama-apertures ebhodini layo lokuzwakala. Bese, godu, nangabe uqala ilihlo lakho phezu kwento engakajayeleki, eneenqhema, efana nekama phezulu kwenqwaba—into eluhlaza, ene-barnacled, abantu be-Greenland abayibiza ngokuthi "ngumqhele," begodu abadobi beSewula "ibhonethi" ye-Right Whale; uqale lokhu kwaphela, ungathatha ihloko uye esiquni se-oak ekulu, enesidleke senyoni esifubeni sayo. Nanyana kukuphi, lokha nawubukela iinkala eziphilako ezihlala lapha phezu kwebhonethi, umqondo onjalo uzokufika kuwe; ngaphandle kobana, kwamambala, ukuthanda kwakho kulungiswe ligama lobuthekhnikhi elithi "umqhele" nalo elinikelwe kilo; lapho-ke uzokuthatha ikareko ekulu yokucabanga bona isilo esinamandla siyinkosi yolwandle ene-diadem, umqhele waso oluhlaza ohlanganiswe yona ngendlela emangalisako. Kodwana nangabe umkhomo lo ungaba yikosi, ubukeka njengesithukuthezi khulu. Qala umlomo ongaphasi olengako! yeka ukuthukuthela okukhulu nokuthukuthela lapho! i-sulk ne-pout, ngokulinganisa kombazi, okungaba ziinyawo ezimatjhumi amabili ubude namafidi amahlanu ukujula; ukuthukuthela nokuthukuthela okuzokuletha amalitha angaba ma-500 wamafutha nokunye.

Kuyadabukisa khulu, njeke, bona umkhomo lo ongakalungi kufanele ube nemilebe yomvundla. Umfantu ungaba yinyawo ububanzi. Mhlamunye umma ngesikhatjhana esiqakathekileko bekakhamba ngesikepe ehlela eligwini le-Peru, lokha ukusikinyeka kwephasi kwenza bona ibhishi livuleke. Phezu komlomo lo, njengaphezu komngcele otjhelelako, njeke siyatjhelela emlonyeni. Ngokwelizwi lami ngabe ngise-Mackinaw, bekufuze ngithathe lokhu njengengaphakathi kwe-wigwam ye-India. Nkosi elungileko! ingabe le yindlela uJona akhambe ngayo? Uphahla lungaba ziinyawo ezilitjhumi nambili ukuphakama, begodu luya ekhoneni elibukhali, kube ngasuthi kunesigodo esijayelekileko lapho; lokha iinhlangothi ezineembambo, ezigobileko, ezinoboya, zisinikela ngalezo ezimangalisako, ezijame ngqo, ezifana ne-scimetar zamathambo womkhomo, sithi amakhulu amathathu ngehlangothini, eziya ngehlangothini eliphezulu lekhanda nofana ithambo lomqhele, zakha lezo zimpumputhe ze-Venetian ebezikhulunywe ngazo kwenye indawo. Imiphetho yamathambo la inemicu eneboya, lapho i-Right Whale ihluza khona amanzi, begodu ngobuqili bayo igcina iimfesi ezincani, lokha nayivulekileko idlula emalwandle we-brit ngesikhathi sokudla. Eendaweni eziphakathi zamathambo, njengombana zijame ngokulandelana kwazo, kuneempawu ezithileko ezimangalisako, amajika, imigodi, kanye nama-ridges, lapho abanye abantu abathanda imikhomo babala khona iminyaka yesidalwa, njengeminyaka ye-oki ngamasongo wayo ayindilinga. Nanyana ukuqiniseka kwalendlela kungakghoni ukutjengiswa, kodwana inephunga lokufana. Nanyana ngiliphi izinga, nangabe sivuma kikho, kufuze sinikele iminyaka ekulu khulu ku-Right Whale kunaleyo ekuqaleni okuzokubonakala kunengqondo.

Eenkhathini zakade, kubonakala ngasuthi bekunezinto ezithakazelisako eziphathelene nama-blind la. Omunye umhambi we-Purchas uzibiza ngokuthi “ziindevu” ezimangalisako ngaphakathi komlomo womkhomo;* omunye, “iinhluthu zengulube”; umnumzana wesithathu omdala e-Hackluyt usebenzisa ilimi elihle elilandelako: "Kuneemphiko ezingaba makhulu amabili namatjhumi amahlanu ezimila ngapha nangapha kwe -chop yakhe yangaphezulu, ezigoba phezu kwelimi lakhe ngapha nangapha komlomo wakhe."

*Lokhu kusikhumbuza bona i-Right Whale ineenhluthu, nofana amadevu, aneenhluthu ezimbalwa ezimhlophe ezihlakazekileko engcenyeni ephezulu yesiphetho sangaphandle somhlathi ongaphasi. Kwesinye isikhathi amathuthumbo la anikela ubuso bakhe obukhulu.

Njengombana woke umuntu azi, “iinhluthu zengulube,” “amaphini,” “amadevu,” “ama-blind,” namkha nanyana yini oyithandako, inikela abafazi ama-busk wabo nezinye izinto eziqinisako. Kodwana endabeni le, ukufunwa sele kude kwehla. Kwakungesikhathi seNdlovukazi u-Anne lapho ithambo belisezingeni eliphezulu, i-farthingale beyiyinto ethandwa khulu. Njengoba amakhosikazi lawo asendulo akhamba ngokuthaba, nanyana asemihlathini yomkhomo, njengombana ungatjho; nanyana kunjalo, etjhaweni, ngokungacabangi okufanako, ingabe namhlanjesi siphapha ngaphasi kwemihlathi efanako ukuvikeleka; isambulela siyitende elisakazwe phezu kwethambo elifanako.

Kodwana nje khohlwa koke ngama-blinds namadevu isikhatjhana, begodu, umile emlonyeni we-Right Whale, uqale ngapha nangapha kabutjha. Nawubona zoke iinqhema zamathambo zihlelwe kuhle kangaka, bewungekhe ucabange bona ungaphakathi kwesitho esikhulu se-Haarlem, begodu ubuka amapayipi waso ayikulungwana? Ukwenza ikhaphethe lesitho sinomata we-Turkey ethambileko khulu—ilimi, enamathele, njengokungathi, phasi emlonyeni. Ikhuluphele khulu begodu ithambile, begodu ingadabuka ibe ziinquntu lokha nayiphakanyiswa emkhunjini. Ilimi elithileko eliphambi kwethu; ngokukhangela okudlulako ngingathi bekuyi-six-barrelers; okutjho bona, kuzokukhiqiza inani elinjalo lamafutha.

Ngaphambi kwalokhu, kufanele bona sele ulibonile iqiniso lalokho engiqale ngakho—ukuthi i-Sperm Whale ne-Right Whale ineenhloko ezihlukileko. Ukurhunyeza, ke: e-Right Whale ayikho umthombo omkhulu wesidoda; akunazinyo zendlovu; ingasi umhlathi omude, omncani womhlathi ongaphasi, njenge-Sperm Whale. Begodu ku-Sperm Whale ayikho nanyana ngiyiphi yalezo zimpumputhe zamathambo; ayikho umlomo omkhulu ongezansi; begodu kungabi nalutho lolimi. Godu, i-Right Whale inemigodi emibili yangaphandle, i-Sperm Whale yinye kwaphela.

Qala kokugcina, nje, eenhlokweni ezihlonitjhwako ezine-hood, lokha nazilele ndawonye; ngombana omunye uzokucwila msinyana, angakatlolwa, elwandle; enye angeze yathatha isikhathi eside khulu.

Ungakwazi na ukubona ubuso be-Sperm Whale lapho? Kufana nalokho ahlongakala ngakho, kwaphela eminye imibimbi emide epandla ebonakala sele iphelile. Ngicabanga bona ubuso bakhe obubanzi buzele ukuthula okufana ne-prairie, okubelethwe kukungabi nandaba nokufa. Kodwana phawula ubuso benye ikhanda. Bona umlomo ongezansi omangalisako, ogandelelwe ngengozi ehlangothini lomkhumbi, ukwenzela bona ubambe umhlathi ngokuqinileko. Ingabe ihloko yoke le ayibonakali ikhuluma ngesinqumo esikhulu esisebenzako sokujamelana nokuhlongakala? I-Right Whale le ngiyithatha njenge-Stoic; i-Sperm Whale, u-Platonian, okungenzeka bona wathatha u-Spinoza eminyakeni yakhe yokugcina.





ISAHLUKO 76. I-Battering-Ram.

Ngaphambi kokuyeka, isikhatjhana, ikhanda le-Sperm Whale, ngingathanda bona, njengesazi somzimba esinengqondo, uvele—ikakhulukazi uphawule ubujamo bayo bangaphambili, kikho koke ukubuthelelwa kwayo. Ngingathanda bona uphenye ngakho nje ngombono wokuthi uzenzele isilinganiso esingakaqini, esihlakaniphileko sanoma ngiliphi amandla wokushaya angaba khona lapho. Nali iphuzu eliqakathekileko; ngombana kufanele ulungise indaba le ngendlela eyanelisako, namkha uhlale ungakholwa unomphela malungana nenye yezehlakalo ezimbi khulu, kodwana ingasi ezingekho emthethweni, mhlamunye nanyana kukuphi emlandwini otlolwe phasi.

Uyatjheja bona ebujameni obujayelekileko bokubhukuda kwe-Sperm Whale, ingaphambili lekhanda layo liveza isibhakabhaka esijame ngqo emanzini; uyatjheja bona ingcenye engezansi yangaphambili leyo itjhinga emuva khulu, ukwenzela bona kube nendawo yokubuyela emuva yesokhethi ede eyamukela umhlathi ongaphasi ofana ne-boom; uyatjheja bona umlomo ungaphasi kwehloko, ngendlela efanako, kwamambala, kwanga umlomo wakho ungaphasi kwesilevu sakho. Uyabona bona umkhomo awunakhala langaphandle; begodu nokuthi ngiliphi ikhala analo—umgodi wakhe wempumulo—uphezu kwehloko yakhe; uyatjheja bona amehlo neendlebe zakhe zisemahlangothini wekhanda lakhe, pheze ingcenye eyodwa kwezintathu yobude bakhe boke ukusuka phambili. Ngalokho-ke, kufanele bona sele ubonile bona ihloko yangaphambili ye-Sperm Whale liboda elifileko, elingaboniko, elinganasitho esisodwa namkha ukuvelela okuthambileko kwananyana ngiliphi ihlobo.
