Lokhu kutjelwa u-Queequeg, wavele wavuma, begodu waphinda wangikhomba bona ngilale embhedeni—wajika ngakelinye ihlangothi wathi—“Angekhe ngithinte umlenze wakho.”

“Ulale kuhle, mninimuzi,” ngatjho mina, “ungakhamba.”

Ngavuma, begodu azange ngilale ngcono epilweni yami.
