ISAHLUKO 94. Ukucindezela Isandla.

I-whale ka-Stubb, eyathengwa ngemali enengi, yalethwa ehlangothini le-Pequod, lapho yoke imisebenzi yokusika nokuphakamisa ebegade ihlathululwe ngaphambilini, gade yenziwa njalo, ngitjho nokuvalwa kwe-Heidelburgh Tun, nofana i-Case.

Lokha abanye bebaphithizela ngomsebenzi lo wokugcina, abanye baqatjhwe ekudoseni amabhabha amakhulu, msinyana nje sele azele isidoda; begodu lokha isikhathi esifaneleko nasifikako, isidoda esifanako saphathwa ngokutjheja ngaphambi kobana siye emisebenzini yokulinga, lapho kungakabi khona.

Besele ipholile begodu yaba yikristalu ngezinga elinjalo, kangangobana lokha, nabanye abanengi, nangihlala phasi phambi kokuhlamba okukhulu kwaka-Constantine, ngayifumana ikhonkrithi ngokungakajayeleki yaba ziinqhema, lapha nalaphaya ijikajika engcenyeni yamanzi. Bekumsebenzi wethu ukugandelela amathumba la abuyele emanzini. Umsebenzi omnandi nonganamafutha! Akurari ukuthi eenkhathini zakade isidoda lesi besiyintandokazi yokuzilungisa. Okucacileko! isithako esinjalo! isithambiso esinjalo! isithambiso esimnandi kangaka! Ngemva kokuthi ngifake izandla zami kiyo imizuzu embalwa, iminwe yami yazwakala njenge-eel, begodu yathoma, njengokungathi, ukujikajika nokujikeleza.

Njengombana ngihlezi lapho ngikhululekile, ngiphambanise imilenze emkhunjini; ngemva kokuzikhandla okubabako e-windlass; ngaphasi kwesibhakabhaka esihlaza satjani; umkhumbi ngaphasi kweseyilu engasebenziko, begodu ukhamba ngokuthula; njengombana ngigeza izandla zami hlangana nalawo ma-globules athambileko, athambileko wezicubu ezingenako, ezilukwe pheze kungakapheli ne-awa; njengombana baphula iminwe yami, begodu bakhupha koke ukunotha kwabo, njengamadiribe avuthiweko iwayini labo; njengombana ngihogela iphunga elingakasilaphazeki,—ngokweqiniso, njengephunga lama-violet entwasahlobo; Ngiyakubikezela, bona isikhathi ebengiphila njengedlelweni le-musky; Ngikhohlwe koke ngesifungo sethu esimbi; ngaleso sidoda esingakhulumekiko, ngageza izandla zami nehliziywami ngaso; Ngipheze ngathoma ukuthokoza ngeenkolelo-ze zakade ze-Paracelsan zokuthi isidoda sinobuhle obungakavami ekuqedeni ukutjhisa kwelaka; ngesikhathi ngigeza ngaphakathi kwebhathi leyo, ngazizwa ngikhululekile kikho koke ukugandeleleka, nofana ukuthukuthela, nofana ubumbi, nanyana ngiliphi ihlobo.

Minya! minya! minya! ekuseni koke; Ngacindezela isidoda leso bekwaba kulapho ngipheze ngincibilike khona; Ngacindezela isidoda leso bekwaba kulapho ngihlanya khona; begodu ngazifumana ngigandelela izandla zabasebenzi engisebenza nabo kiyo, ngithatha izandla zabo ngama-globules athambileko. Umuzwa omkhulu kangaka, onothando, onobungani, onothando lowo owazala; ukuthi ekugcineni bengihlala ngibacindezela izandla, begodu ngibaqala emehlweni ngokuzizwa; kangangobana ngithi,—O! bantu bami abathandekako, kubayini singasazithanda nanyana ngibuphi ubujamo bezenhlalakahle, namkha sazi ukuhlekisa okuncani namkha umona! Iza; asiqinise izandla zoke; awa, soke asizigandelele komunye nomunye; asizigandeleleni kiyo yoke indawo ebisini nesidoda somusa.

Ngathana ngingaragela phambili nokucindezela isidoda leso unomphela! Njenganje, ngombana ngokuhlangabezanwe nakho okunengi okuthatha isikhathi eside, okuphindaphindiweko, ngibonile bona kizo zoke iimeko umuntu kufuze ekugcineni wehlise, namkha okungenani atjhugulule, ukuzikhakhazisa kwakhe ngethabo elifinyelelekako; ukungayibeki nanyana kukuphi engqondweni nofana ekuthandweni; kodwana kumfazi, ihliziyo, umbhede, itafula, isihlalo, umlilo, inarha; Njengombana ngikubonile koke lokhu, ngikulungele ukugandelela icala unomphela. Ngicabanga ngemibono yobusuku, ngabona imigqa emide yeengilozi epharadesi, enye nenye inezandla zakhe emgqonyeni we-spermaceti.

Nje, lokha nakukhulunywa ngesidoda, kufanele sikhulume ngezinye izinto ezifana nayo, ebhizinisini lokulungiselela umkhomo wesidoda ukulinga.

Kokuthoma kuza i-white-horse, ebizwa njalo, etholakala engcenyeni enciphileko yefesi, begodu naseengcenyeni eziqinileko zama-flukes wayo. Iqinile ngemithambo eqinileko—i-wad yemisipha—kodwana isaqukethe amafutha. Ngemva kokuhlukaniswa nomkhomo, ihhashi elimhlophe liqale lisikwe libe ziinqhema eziphathekako ngaphambi kobana liye emtjhini wokugaya. Zifana khulu namabhlogo wemabula ye-Berkshire.

I-plum-pudding ligama elisetjenziswa eengcenyeni ezithileko zenyama yomkhomo, lapha nalaphaya zinamathela engutsheni ye-blubber, begodu kanengi zihlanganyela ngezinga elikhulu ekungabi namafutha kwayo. Yinto eqabulako, ethabisako, ehle khulu ukuyibona. Njengombana ibizo layo litjho, inombala ocebileko, onamabalabala, onomhlabathi oneqhwa negolide, onamabala abomvu khulu nophephuli. Ngama-plums wama-rubies, eenthombeni ze-citron. Nangaphandle kwesizathu, kubudisi ukuzikhandela bona ungayidli. Ngiyavuma, ukuthi ngake ngeba ngemva kwe-foremast ukuzama. Kwakunambitheka njengombana bengicabanga bona i-cutlet yasebukhosini evela ethangeni laka-Louis le Gros gade inambitheka, ngicabanga bona wabulawa ngelanga lokuthoma ngemva kwesikhathi senyama yenyamazana, begodu isizini yenyama yenyamazana leyo efana nesikhathi esifanako nesivini esihle khulu se-Champagne.

Kunenye into, begodu eyodwa khulu, evela ngesikhathi sebhizinisi leli, kodwana engibona ngasuthi iyadida ngokwaneleko ukuyihlathulula. Ibizwa ngokuthi yi-slobgollion; ibizo lokuthoma elinama-whalemen, begodu kunjalo nangobunjalo bento. Yinto engatjho litho, eneentambo, evame ukutholakala emabhadini wesidoda, ngemva kokucindezela isikhathi eside, nokukhipha okulandelako. Ngicabanga bona ziinqhema ezincani, ezidabukileko zekheyisi, ezihlangana.

I-Gurry, ebizwa njalo, ligama elifaneleko labantu abadoba imikhomo, kodwana kesinye isikhathi lisetjenziswa babantu abathiya iinhlambi zesidoda. Itjho into emnyama, enamathelako eklwetjhwe ngemva kwe-Greenland nofana i-right whale, begodu okunengi kwayo kumboza amadekhi walezo ziphefumulo eziphasi ezizingela i-Leviathan engakalungi.

Ama-nippers. Ngokuqinisekileko igama leli alisiwo umdabu wesilulumagama somkhomo. Kodwana njengombana kusetjenziswa babantu abathanda imikhomo, kuba njalo. I-nipper ye-whaleman mtjhini omfitjhani oqinileko wezinto ezinemithambo ezisikiweko engcenyeni encani yomsila we-Leviathan: ilinganiselwa ku-intjhi ngobukhulu, begodu koke okuseleko, ilingana nengcenye yensimbi ye-hoe. Ithuthelwa ngaphasi kwe-oily deck, isebenza njenge-squilgee yesikhumba; begodu ngokuhlambalaza okunganabizo, njengomlingo, kudosa kanye nakho koke ukungcola.

Kodwana ukufunda koke ngalezi zindaba ezifihlakeleko, indlela yakho engcono khulu msinyana ukwehlela egumbini le-blubber, bese ukhuluma isikhathi eside neziboshwa zalo. Indawo le sele ikhulunywe ngaphambilini njengendawo yokufaka iinquntu zengubo, lokha nazikhutjhwa begodu ziphakanyiswa emkhomeni. Lokha nakufika isikhathi esifaneleko sokusika okuqukethwe kiyo, ifulethi le iba yindawo yokwesaba kiwo woke ama-tyros, khulukhulu ebusuku. Ngakelinye ihlangothi, kukhanyiswe sibani esifipheleko, isikhala sitjhiyelwe abasebenzi. Zivamise ukukhamba ngababili,—i-pike-and-gaffman kanye ne-spade-man. I-whaling-pike ifana nesikhali sokugibela se-frigate esinebizo elifanako. I-gaff yinto efana ne-boat-hook. Nge-gaff yakhe, i-gaffman ibophelela ephepheni le-blubber, begodu ilwela ukulibamba bona lingatjheleli, njengombana umkhumbi uphakama begodu uphaphatheka. Khatheso, indoda ye-spade ijame ephepheni ngokwalo, ilisika libe ziinqetjhana zehhashi eziphathekako. Ipeyidi le ibukhali njengombana i-hone ingayenza; iinyawo zomtjhayeli azinazicathulo; into ajame kiyo kesinye isikhathi izokutjhelela isuke kuye, njenge-sledge. Nangabe angaquma omunye weenzwane zakhe, namkha omunye wabasizi bakhe, ungamangala khulu? Iinzwane ziyatlhogeka hlangana namadoda we-blubber-room.





ISAHLUKO 95. Ikhasoki.

Ngabe ungene emkhunjini we-Pequod ngesikhathi esithileko sokubulawa komkhomo; begodu ngathana bewukhambe phambili eduze ne-windlass, nginesiqiniseko sokuthi bewuzokuhlolisisa ngaphandle kokufuna ukwazi into engakajayeleki, engazwisisekiko, ebewuzoyibona lapho, ilele ngobude ema-lee scuppers. Ingasi umgodi omangalisako ekhanda elikhulu lomkhomo; ingasi isimangaliso somhlathi wakhe ongaphasi ongahlanganiswa; ingasi isimangaliso somsila wakhe olinganako; akukho nanye kilezi engakumangaza, njengombana ukubona isigamu sekhoni enganakubalwa,—ede ukudlula umuntu we-Kentucky ubude, pheze unyawo ububanzi ngaphasi, begodu mnyama njengo-Yojo, isithombe se-ebony se-Queequeg. Begodu isithombe, kwamambala, sinjalo; namkha, kunalokho, eenkhathini zakade, ukufana kwayo bekunjalo. Isithombe esinjengaleso esitholakala emahlathini afihlekileko weNdlovukazi uMaaka eJudiya; begodu ngokukhonza lokho, iKosi u-Asa, indodanakhe, yamkhipha, begodu yatjhabalalisa isithombe, begodu yayitjhisa yaba sinengiso emfuleni iKedroni, njengombana kuvezwe ngokumnyama esahlukweni se-15 seNcwadi yokuThoma yamaKhosi.

Qala umkhumbi, obizwa nge-mincer, ozako nje, begodu asizwa babantu ababili, asekela i-grandissimus, njengombana abasebenzi bemikhumbi bayibiza njalo, begodu ngamahlombe akhothamileko, uyatjhinga ngawo ngasuthi ungumtjhayeli we-grenadier othwele umngani ohlongakeleko ovela egangeni. Ukuyinweba phezu kwe-forecastle deck, njeke uragela phambili ngokurhaba ukususa isikhumba sayo esimnyama, njengomzingeli we-Afrika isikhumba se-boa. Lokhu kwenziwa uphendula isikhumba ngaphakathi ngaphandle, njengomlenze we-pantaloon; inikela ukulula okuhle, ukwenzela bona pheze iphindwe kabili ububanzi bayo; begodu ekugcineni uyayilengisa, irhatjheke kuhle, ngaphakathi kwe-rigging, ukuze yome. Kungakabi sikhathi, iyathathwa phasi; lokha nakasusa amafidi amathathu wayo, ukuya esiphethweni esiqondileko, bese usika imigodi emibili yemigodi yezandla ngakelinye ihlangothi, uyazitjhelela ngokomzimba ngaphakathi kwayo. Umgayi ujame phambi kwakho bona utjale imali epheleleko yokubizwa kwakhe. Ukukhunjulwa kiyo yoke i-oda lakhe, ukutjala imali lokhu kukodwa kuzomvikela ngokwaneleko, lokha nakaqatjhwe emisebenzini engakajayeleki yehhovisi lakhe.

I-ofisi leyo iqukethe ukugaya iincezu ze-blubber ukuze zibe namabhodlela; umsebenzi owenziwa ehhashini lamaplanka, elitjalwe ngokujamelana neenqaba, begodu linebhati elikhulu ngaphasi kwalo, lapho iinqetjhana ezisikiweko ziwela khona, msinya njengamashidi avela etafuleni lomkhulumi othabileko. Ifakwe ngombala omnyama omuhle; ukuhlala epulpiti ebonakalako; ihloso emaqabungeni webhayibheli; bekayikhethelwa kangangani lokuba mbhishobhi omkhulu, bekayindoda kangangani yokuba nguPapa!*

*Ibhayibheli liyaphuma! Amakhasi weBhayibheli! Lokhu ukulila okungatjhugulukiko okuvela ebantwini abathandanako ukuya egayini. Imyalela bona aqaphele, begodu asike umsebenzi wakhe ube ziinlayi ezincani ngendlela angakghona ngayo, ngombana ngokwenza njalo umsebenzi wokubilisa amafutha uyarhaba khulu, begodu inani lawo likhuphuke khulu, ngaphandle kokuthi mhlamunye lithuthukise ikhwalithi.





ISAHLUKO 96. Imisebenzi yokuzama.

Ngaphandle kwesikepe sakhe esiphakanyisiweko, umdobi wemikhomo we-America uhlukile ngaphandle ngemisebenzi yakhe yokuzama. Uveza ubujamo obumangalisako bokuhlangana kwamatje aqinileko ne-oak ne-hemp ekwakheni umkhumbi oqediweko. Kungasuthi ukusuka egangeni elivulekileko isitofu sezitina sithuthwe emaplankeni wakhe.

I-try-works itjalwe hlangana ne-foremast ne-mainmast, ingcenye evulekileko ye-deck. Iinkuni ezingezansi zinamandla angakajayeleki, zilungiswe ukubamba ubudisi besitina nodaka, ezingaba ziinyawo ezilitjhumi ngesikwere esibunane, kanye namafidi amahlanu ukuphakama. Isisekelo asingeni ngaphakathi emkhunjini, kodwana amatshe aqiniswe ngokuqinileko phezulu ngamadolo wensimbi aqinileko ayiqinisa emahlangothini woke, bese ayibophelela emagodini. Emahlangothini ifakwe ngeengodo, begodu phezulu imbozwe ngokupheleleko yindlela ekulu, etjhingako, eneenqhema. Ukususa i-hatch le siveza ama-try-pot amakhulu, amabili ngenani, begodu enye nenye inamandla wemiphongolo eminengi. Lokha nazingasetjenziswako, zigcinwa zihlanzekile. Kwesinye isikhathi zipholitjhwa ngelitshe lesepha nesihlabathi, bekube kulapho zikhanya ngaphakathi njengeenkezo zesiliva. Ngesikhathi sokugada ebusuku abanye amatilosi amadala azokungena ngaphakathi kiwo bese azigoqa lapho ayokulala kancani. Lokha nakusetjenziswa ukuzipholisa—indoda yinye embizeni ngayinye, eceleni—ukukhulumisana okunengi okuyimfihlo kuqhutshwa, phezu kweendebe zensimbi. Yindawo yokuzindla ngokujulileko ngeembalo. Bekuse-try-pot yesandla sobunxele se-Pequod, nge-soapstone engizungeza ngokuzikhandla, lapho ngathoma khona ukutjhaywa liqiniso eliphawulekako, lokuthi ngejometri yoke imizimba ekhamba nge-cycloid, i-soapstone yami ngokwesibonelo, izokwehla kiyo nanyana ngiyiphi indawo ngesikhathi esifanako.

Ukususa ibhodi lomlilo ngaphambili kwe-try-works, amatje angenalutho wehlangothi lelo ayavezwa, angena ngemilomo emibili yensimbi yama-furnace, ngqo ngaphasi kwamabhodo. Imilomo le ifakwe iminyango ebudisi yensimbi. Umtjhiso omkhulu womlilo uyavinjelwa bona ungazikhulumi emphemeni, ngokusebenzisa indawo yokugcina amanzi engatjhingiko enweba ngaphasi kwayo yoke indawo evalekileko yemisebenzi. Ngomhubhe ofakwe ngemva, idamu leli ligcinwa ligcwele amanzi msinya njengombana lihwamuka. Awekho amatshimini wangaphandle; zivuleka ngqo ebodeni langemuva. Bese kuthi lapha sibuyele emuva kancani.

Kwaba nge-awa lethoba ebusuku lapho ukulinga kwe-Pequod kwathoma khona ekhambweni lamanje. Bekuka-Stubb ukutjheja ibhizinisi.

"Konke ukulungele lapho? Phuma, bese umqalisa. Uyapheka, utjhise imisebenzi." Lokhu bekuyintfo elula, ngombana umbazi bekasolo afaka iinhluthu zakhe esithandweni ngaphakathi kwendlela yoke. Lapha kutjhiwo bona ekhambweni lokudoba imikhomo umlilo wokuthoma emisebenzini yokulinga kufanele uphakelwe isikhatjhana ngeenkuni. Ngemva kwalokho akukho ziinkuni ezisetjenziswako, ngaphandle kobana kube yindlela yokuvutha msinyana emafutheni aqakathekileko. Ngamazwi ambalwa, ngemva kokulingwa, i-blubber eqhekezako, evuvukileko, njengamanje ebizwa ngokuthi ziinsalela nofana ama-fritters, isaqukethe izakhiwo zayo ezinengi ezinganamafutha. Ama-fritter la adlisa amalangabi. Njengomuntu omfela ukholo ovuthako, namkha umuntu oziphatha kumbi, lokha nasele avutha, umkhomo uzinikela amafutha wawo begodu utjhisa ngomzimba wawo. Ngathana bekazidla intuthu yakhe! ngombana intuthu yakhe iyasabisa ukuyihogela, begodu kufanele uyihogele, ingasi lokho kwaphela, kodwana kufanele uphile kiyo isikhatjhana. Inephunga elingakhulumekiko, elingakajayeleki, le-Hindu ngalo, elingaba khona eduze nemililo yomngcwabo. Iphunga njengephiko lesinxele lelanga lokwahlulela; mpikiswano yomgodi.

Phakathi kobusuku imisebenzi besele isebenza ngokuzeleko. Besele siphumile esidumbu; iseyili sele yenziwe; umoya bewupholile; ubumnyama obukhulu belwandle bebukhulu. Kodwana ubumnyama lobo bakhothwa malangabi aqinileko, ebegade aphume ngeenkhathi ezithileko emtjhinini, begodu akhanyise yoke intambo ephakemeko, njengomlilo odumileko wamaGirigi. Umkhumbi ovuthako waragela phambili, kwanga uthunyelwe ngokungazisoli bona wenze isenzo esithileko sokuziphindiselela. Njeke ama-brig we-pitch ne-sulphur we-Hydriote, i-Canaris, aphuma emathekwini wawo phakathi kobusuku, anamalangabi abanzi wokwenza amaseyila, ahlasela phezu kwemikhumbi ye-Turkey, begodu awagoqa abe mlilo.

I-hatch, esuswe phezulu kwemisebenzi, besele inikela ngeziko elibanzi phambi kwabo. Phezu kwalokhu bekujame amajamo we-Tartarean wama-harpooneers wamahedeni, begade bahlala babapheki bemikhumbi yemikhomo. Ngamapali amakhulu anamazinyo bebaphosela amafutha amanengi ngaphakathi kweembiza ezivuthako, nofana bavuse imililo ngaphasi, bekwaba kulapho amalangabi wenyoka aphumela ngaphandle, agoqeka, ngaphandle kweminyango ukubabamba ngeenyawo. Intuthu yagijima yaba ziinqwaba ezimbi. Kiwo woke amaphitjhi womkhumbi bekunephitjhi yamafutha abilako, ebegade abonakala afuna ukugxuma ebusweni babo. Maqondana nomlomo wemisebenzi, ngakelinye ihlangothi leziko elibanzi lamaplanka, bekune-windlass. Lokhu bekusebenzela isofa yelwandle. Lapha kwahlala iwatjhi, lokha nayingasetjenziswa ngenye indlela, iqala ukutjhisa okubomvu komlilo, bekwaba kulapho amehlo wabo atjhiswa emahlombe wabo. Ubujamo babo obubomvu, njenganje boke buhlanjululwe yintuthu nomjuluko, iindevu zabo ezihlangeneko, nokukhanya okuhlukileko kwamazinyo wabo, koke lokhu kwavezwa ngokungakajayeleki ekuhlobiseni okuhle kwemisebenzi. Njengombana bebalandisana ngeenkhathi zabo ezingakalungi, iindaba zabo zokwesaba zikhulunywa ngamezwi wethabo; njengombana ukuhleka kwabo okungakaphucuki kwaphuma phezulu kibo, njengamalangabi avela esithandweni; phambili nangemuva, ngaphambili kwabo, ama-harpoon bekakhuluma ngezandla ngeemfologo zabo ezinkulu ezinama-dippers; njengombana umoya uragela phambili, begodu nelwandle lagxuma, begodu umkhumbi ububula begodu utjhinga, kodwana ngokuqinileko wadubula isihogo sakhe esibomvu phambili nokuya phambili ebumnyameni belwandle nobusuku, begodu ngokudelela washaya ithambo elimhlophe emlonyeni wakhe, begodu wamkhafula ngonya emahlangothini woke; bese-ke i-Pequod ephuthumako, ethwele iinlwanyana, begodu ithwele umlilo, begodu itjhisa isidumbu, begodu itjhingela ebumnyameni bobumnyama, yabonakala ingumlingani wezinto eziphathekako zomphefumulo womlawuli wakhe othanda ukuzikhakhazisa.

Kwabonakala njalo kimi, njengombana ngijame esikhundleni sakhe, begodu ama-awa amade ngiqondisa indlela yomkhumbi womlilo elwandle. Ngigoqwe, ngaleso sikhathi, ebumnyameni ngokwami, kodwana ngcono ngabona ububomvu, ubuhlanya, ubumbi babanye. Ukubona okuragela phambili kwamajamo we-fiend phambi kwami, aphephezela ingcenye yentuthu nehhafu emlilweni, lokhu ekugcineni kwaveza imibono efanako emphefumulweni wami, msinyana ngemva kokuthi ngithome ukuvuma ukulala okungenakuphendulwa okwakuzokwenzeka kimi phakathi kobusuku.

Kodwana ngobusuku lobo, khulukhulu, into engakajayeleki (begodu engahlathululekiko) yeza kimi. Ukuthoma ebuthongweni obufitjhani, bengiqaphele khulu ukuthi kukhona okungakalungi. I-tiller yethambo lomhlathi yashaya ihlangothi lami, elalithembele kilo; eendlebeni zami bekuzwakala ukuduma okuphasi kwamaseyila, athoma ukuthuthumela emoyeni; Bengicabanga bona amehlo wami avulekile; Bengizizwa ngingafaki iminwe yami eemvalweni begodu ngizilule ziqhelelene khulu. Kodwana, nangaphandle kwakho koke lokhu, bengingaboni ikhampasi phambi kwami ​​engingayidlula; nanyana bekubonakala ngasuthi umzuzu nje solo ngiqale ikhadi, ngesibane se-binnacle esilikhanyisako.
