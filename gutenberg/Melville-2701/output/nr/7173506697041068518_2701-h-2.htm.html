ISAHLUKO 4. I-Counterpane.

Nangivuka ekuseni ngelanga elilandelako, ngathola ingalo ka-Queequeg iphoswe phezu kwami ​​ngendlela enethando nethando khulu. Bewupheze ucabange bona bengingumkakhe. I-counterpane beyiyenziwe ngama-patchwork, izele iinkwere ezincani ezinemibala engakajayeleki kanye nama-triangle; begodu ingalo yakhe le ine-tattoo yoke nge-labyrinth engapheliko ye-Cretan yomfanekiso, kungekho iingcenye ezimbili ebegade zinomthunzi owodwa—ngenxa yokuthi ugcine ingalo yakhe elwandle ngokungakafaneli elangeni nomthunzi, imikhono yehembe lakhe igoqwe ngokungakajayeleki ngeenkhathi ezihlukahlukeneko—ingalo yakhe efanako, ngithi yoke, beyibukeka njengomtjhini ofanako wephasi ikhavara yombhede. Kwamambala, ngilele phezu kwayo njengombana ingalo yenza lokha nangithoma ukuvuka, bengingeze ngakghona ukutjho ngengubo, gade ihlanganisa imibala yayo ndawonye; begodu bekungomqondo wesisindo nokugandeleleka kwaphela lapho bengikghona ukutjho bona u-Queequeg uyangigona.

Imizwa yami beyingakajayeleki. Akhe ngizame ukuzihlathulula. Lokha nangisese mntwana, ngikhumbula kuhle ubujamo obufanako obungehlelako; kungakhathaliseki bona bekuyinto yamambala nofana iphupho, azange ngikghone ukuzinza ngokupheleleko. Ubujamo bebunjalo. Bengilokhu ngisika i-caper nofana enye—ngicabanga bona beyizama ukukhwela phezulu etjhimini, njengombana bengibone ukutjhayela okuncani kwenza emalangeni ambalwa adlulileko; kanye nomma wesilisa, ngandlela thize, obegade angitshaya ngaso soke isikhathi, nofana angithumela embhedeni ngingadli isidlo sakusihlwa,—umma wangihudulela ngemilenze ngaphandle kwetshimini begodu wangifaka embhedeni, nanyana bekuli-awa lesibili ntambama mhlazi-21 kuMgwengweni, ilanga elide khulu emnyakeni ephasini lethu. Ngazizwa kumbi khulu. Kodwana bekunganasizo, njeke ngezitebhisi ngaya ekamelweni lami elincani esitezi sesithathu, ngazikhulula kancanikancani ukwenzela bona ngiqede isikhathi, begodu ngokububula okubabako ngangena hlangana namashidi.

Ngalala lapho ngokudanisako ngibala bona kufuze kudlule ama-awa alitjhumi nathandathu ngaphambi kobana ngibe nethemba lokuvuswa kwabafileko. Ama-awa alitjhumi nathandathu embhedeni! umgogodla wami bewubuhlungu ukucabanga ngakho. Begodu bekulula khulu; ilanga likhanya ngaphakathi efasidereni, nokukghwathaza okukhulu kwamakhotjhi emigwaqweni, netjhada lamaphimbo wezitabane kiyo yoke indlu. Ngazizwa kumbi khulu—ekugcineni ngasukuma, ngagqoka, begodu ngehlela phasi ngeenyawo zami ezifakwe amasokisi, ngafuna umma wami wesilisa, begodu msinyana ngazilahlela eenyaweni zakhe, ngimncenga njengomusa othileko bona angipha ukutjhelela okuhle ngebanga lokuziphatha kwami ​​okumbi; nanyana yini ngaphandle kokungilahla bona ngilale isikhathi eside kangaka. Kodwana bekangumzali ophuma phambili begodu onesazela khulu, begodu bekufuze ngiye ekamelweni lami. Kwaphela ama-awa ambalwa ngilale lapho ngiphapheme, ngizizwa kumbi khulu kunangendlela ebengike ngazizwa ngayo solo kwamhlokho, ngitjho nangeenhlekelele ezikulu khulu ezalandelako. Ekugcineni kufanele bona ngiwele ephusheni elimbi lokulala; begodu ngivuka kancanikancani kiwo—ngigcwele amabhudango—ngavula amehlo wami, begodu ikamelo ebelikhanyiswe lilanga besele ligoqelwe bumnyama bangaphandle. Khonokho ngezwa ukuthuthumela okugijima kiyo yoke ifreyimu yami; bekungabonakali litho, begodu bekungazwakali litho; kodwana isandla esingaphezu kwemvelo besifakwe kimi. Ingalo yami yayilenga phezu kwekhawuntara, begodu ifomu elinganabizo, elingacabangekiko, elithulileko nofana i-phantom, ebegade isandla sayo, bekubonakala ngasuthi ihlezi eduze nombhede wami. Ngombana bekubonakala ngasuthi yiminyaka eminengi, bengilele lapho, ngiqhwale ngokwesaba okumbi khulu, ngingazimiseli ukudosa isandla sami; kodwana bengisolo ngicabanga bona nangabe ngingakghona ukuyitjhukumisela isentimitha eyodwa, umlingo omumbi loyo bewuzokuphuka. Bengingazi bona ukwazi lokhu kwagcina kusuka njani kimi; kodwana nangivuka ekuseni, ngakhumbula koke ngokuthuthumela, begodu amalanga namaviki neenyanga ngemva kwalokho ngazilahlekela emizamweni edidako yokuhlathulula imfihlakalo. Awa, bekube nje, ngivame ukuziphazamisa ngakho.

Nje-ke, susa ukwesaba okumbi, begodu imizwa yami yokuzwa isandla esingaphezu kwemvelo kimi beyifana khulu, ngokungakajayeleki kwayo, naleyo engayizwa lokha nangivuka begodu ngibona ingalo ka-Queequeg yobuhedeni iphoswe kimi. Kodwana ekugcineni zoke izehlakalo zobusuku obudlulileko zabuya, ngamunye ngamunye, ngobujamo obuqinileko, bese ngalala ngiphila ebujamweni obuhlekisako. Nanyana ngazama ukunyakazisa ingalo yakhe—ukuvula isibopho sakhe somkhwenyana—kodwana, alele njengombana bekanjalo, bekangigona ngokuqinileko, ngasuthi akukho okunye ngaphandle kokufa okungasihlukanisa sobabili. Nje-ke ngazama ukumvusa—“Queequeg!”—kodwana ipendulo yakhe bekukurhuwelela. Ngabe sengiguqa, intamo yami beyizwakala ngasuthi ifakwe ikhola yehhashi; begodu msinyana wezwa ukuklwebheka okuncani. Ukulahla eceleni ikhawuntara, kwalele i-tomahawk eceleni kwe-savage, kwanga umntwana onobuso obufana ne-hatchet. I-pickle ehle, kwamambala, ngacabanga; ulele lapha endlini engakajayeleki emini, nomuntu odla abantu ne-tomahawk! “Queequeg!—ebizweni lobuhle, Queequeg, vuka!” Ekugcineni, ngokurhuwelela okukhulu, nokukhulumisana okukhulu nokungapheliko ngokungakafaneli kokugona kwakhe omunye umuntu wesilisa ngendlela yomtjhado, ngaphumelela ukukhipha ukububula; begodu msinyana, wadosela ingalo yakhe emuva, wazithintitha njengenja ye-Newfoundland evela emanzini, wahlala embhedeni, aqinile njengesikhonkwane se-pike, angiqala, begodu ahlikihla amehlo wakhe ngasuthi akakhumbuli ngokupheleleko bona ngifike njani lapho, nanyana ukwazi okuthileko ngami kwabonakala kuthoma kancanikancani. Khatheso, ngalala ngithule ngimqale, nginganakho ukuzaza okukhulu nje, begodu ngizimisele ukuqala kancani isidalwa esifuna ukwazi kangaka. Lokha, ekugcineni, ingqondo yakhe nayibonakala yenziwe ngokuthinta ubuntu bomuntu alala naye, begodu waba, njengokungathi, ukubuyisana neqiniso; wagxuma waphuma phasi, begodu ngeempawu ezithileko nemisindo yangenza ngazwisisa bona, nangabe kungithokozisa, uzokugqoka qangi bese angitjhiya ngigqoke ngemva kwalokho, atjhiye yoke ifulethi kimi. Mina, Queequeg, ngicabanga bona ngaphasi kobujamo, lokhu kuvulwa okuphucukileko; kodwana, iqiniso kukuthi, iinlwanyana lezi zinomuzwa wobumnandi, khuluma lokho ofuna ukukwenza; kuyamangalisa ukuthi banesizotha kangangani. Ngikhokha ukuncoma okukhethekileko ku-Queequeg, ngombana wangiphatha ngendlela ehle khulu nangokucabangela, ngesikhathi mina nginecala lokungabi nalunya okukhulu; amgqolozele embhedeni, begodu aqale koke ukunyakaza kwakhe kwethoyilethi; ngesikhatheso ukufuna ukwazi kwami ​​kuthola ngcono ekuzaleni kwami. Nanyana kunjalo, indoda enjengo-Queequeg ongayiboni qobe langa, yena neendlela zakhe bezifanele ukuqalwa ngokungakajayeleki.

Wathoma ukugqoka phezulu ngokugqoka isigqoko sakhe se-beaver, eside khulu, ngaseceleni, bese—enganawo amabhrugu wakhe—wazingela amabhuzu wakhe. Angikghoni ukutjho bona wakwenzani ngaphasi kwamazulu, kodwana ukunyakaza kwakhe okulandelako bekukuzichoboza—amabhuzu esandleni, nesigqoko—ngaphasi kombhede; lokha, ngokuphefumulela phezulu okuhlukahlukeneko nokugandeleleka, ngabona bona bekasebenza budisi ukuzikhakhazisa; nanyana kungekho mthetho wokuziphatha engike ngawuzwa, nanyana ngiyiphi indoda itlhogeka bona ibe yimfihlo lokha nayifaka amabhuzu wayo. Kodwana u-Queequeg, uyabona, bekayisidalwa esisesigabeni sokutjhuguluka—ingasi isibungu namkha ivivane. Bekaphucukile ngokwaneleko ukutjengisa ubujamo bakhe ngendlela engakajayeleki. Imfundo yakhe beyingakapheli. Bekafunda ibanga lokuthoma. Ngathana bekangakaphucuki kancani, kungenzeka bona bekangazikhathazi ngamabhuzu; kodwana-ke, ngathana bekangakabi silwane, bekangasoze aphuphe ukungena ngaphasi kombhede ukuzifaka. Ekugcineni, waphuma ngesigqoko sakhe esigobileko begodu siphukile phezu kwamehlo akhe, wathoma ukukghwathaza nokuxhuzula ekamelweni, kwangathi, bekangakajayeleki khulu amabhuzu, amabhuzu wakhe amanzi, ashwabeneko wesikhumba senkomo—mhlamunye angakenziwa ngokuya kwe-oda—kunalokho amphinya begodu amhlunguphaza ekuseni kokuthoma okubandako.

Ngokubona, kwanje, bona bekunganamakhetheni efasidereni, nokuthi umgwaqo bewumncani khulu, indlu eqalene nayo beyikghona ukubona kuhle ikamelo, begodu iqalisisa khulu isithombe esingakafaneli esenziwa ngu-Queequeg, atjhinga ngaphandle kokunye ngaphandle kwesigqoko namabhuzu wakhe; Ngamncenga ngendlela engingakghona ngayo, bona akghone ukurhabisa ithoyilethi yakhe, begodu khulukhulu bona angene emabhruguni wakhe msinyana. Wavuma, bese waragela phambili nokugeza. Ngaleso sikhathi ekuseni nanyana ngimuphi umKrestu bekangageza ubuso bakhe; kodwana u-Queequeg, ngokumangala kwami, wazisuthisa ngokukhawulela ukugeza kwakhe esifubeni sakhe, emikhonweni, nezandla. Wabe esegqoka ijazi lakhe, wathatha isiqetjhana sesepa esiqinileko etafuleni eliphakathi lokugeza, wasifaka emanzini wathoma ukugcoba ubuso bakhe. Bengiqalile ukubona bona ugcinephi isikere sakhe, lokha nakathatha i-harpoon ekhoneni lombhede, akhuphe isitoko eside seplanka, akhuphe ihloko, ayibophe kancani ebhuthini lakhe, bese akhamba aya esibonibonini eduze neboda, athoma ukuklwebha ngamandla, nofana ukuklwebha isihlathi sakhe. Mina, u-Queequeg, ngicabanga bona lokhu kusebenzisa iinsetjenziswa ezihle khulu zika-Rogers ngokuziphindiselela. Ngemva kwalokho ngazibuza kancani ngomsebenzi lo lokha nangazi bona ikhanda le-harpoon lenziwe ngensimbi ehle kangangani, nokuthi imiphetho emide eqondileko ihlale igcinwa ibukhali kangangani.

Ithoyilethi yakhe yoke yatholakala msinyana, begodu waphuma ngekamelweni ngokuzikhakhazisa, agoqe ngejazi lakhe elikhulu lomtjhayeli weenkawu, begodu afake i-harpoon yakhe njengenduku yomtjhayeli.





ISAHLUKO 5. Isidlo sasekuseni.

Ngalandela msinyana, begodu ngehlela egumbini lebhawa ngathinta umninimuzi omomotheka kuhle khulu. Bengingaziphathi kuhle ngaye, nanyana bekahlala angithanda khulu endabeni yomuntu engilala naye.

Kodwana, ukuhleka okuhle yinto ehle khulu, begodu yinto ehle khulu; kukhulu ubuhlungu. Njeke, nangabe umuntu oyedwa, ngomuntu wakhe, akghona ukukghona ukwenza ihlaya elihle komunye umuntu, akangabi muva, kodwana avumele ngokuthokoza bona asebenzise begodu asetjenziswe ngaleyo ndlela. Begodu indoda enento ehlekisako ngayo, qiniseka bona kunengi endodeni leyo kunalokho okucabangako.

Igumbi lokuphuza besele lizele abantu ebegade bafika ebusuku bangaphambilini, begodu ebengingakaze ngibaqale kuhle. Pheze boke bebabadosi bemikhomo; abasebenzi abakhulu, nabasebenzi besibili, nabasebenzi besithathu, nababazi belwandle, nabasebenzi bethusi belwandle, nabakhandi belwandle, nabakhandi bemikhumbi, nabagcini bemikhumbi; ikhamphani ensundu neqinileko, enendevu eziqinileko; isethi engakagundwa, eneenhluthu, boke bembethe amajakhethi weenkawu weengubo zasekuseni.

Ungakghona ukutjho ngokukhanyako bona omunye nomunye besele ahlala isikhathi esingangani. Isihlathi esiphilileko somuntu omncani sifana nepere elitjhiswe lilanga, begodu lingabonakala linuka njenge-musky; bekungeze kwaba namalanga amathathu afika ephasini ukusuka ekhambweni lakhe le-India. Indoda leyo eseduze kwakhe iqaleka ikhanya ngamathunzi ambalwa; ungathi ukuthinta isigodo se-satin kuye. Esikhumbeni sesithathu kusasele umbala we-tropic, kodwana uhlanjululwe kancani; ngokungangabazeki sele ahlale amaviki woke egwini. Kodwana ngubani ongatjengisa isihlathi esifana no-Queequeg? ebegade ivalwe ngemibala ehlukileko, beyibonakala njenge-Andes' engeTjingalanga, ukutjengisa ngendlela eyodwa, ubujamo bezulu obuhlukileko, indawo ngendawo.

“Dla, ho!” kwanjesi kwamemeza umninimuzi, avula umnyango, begodu sangena saya esidlweni sasekuseni.

Bathi amadoda abone umhlaba, ngaleyo ndlela abakhululekile ngendlela, abaziphatha khulu nabahlangana nabo. Kodwana ingasi ngaso soke isikhathi: u-Ledyard, umhambi omkhulu we-New England, no-Mungo Park, we-Scotch; kibo boke amadoda, bebanesiqiniseko esincani khulu ekamelweni. Kodwana mhlamunye ukuwela i-Siberia nge-sledge edonswa ziinja njengombana kwenza u-Ledyard, nofana ukuthatha ikhambo elide wedwa ungadli lutho, enhliziyweni yama-negro we-Afrika, okwakuyisamba semisebenzi ka-Mungo ehluphekileko—lolu hlobo lokuhamba, ngithi, kungenzeka kungabi yindlela engcono khulu yokuthola izinga eliphezulu lomphakathi. Nanyana kunjalo, ingcenye ekulu, into enjalo ingatholakala nanyana kukuphi.

Lokhu kucabanga lapha kubangelwa bujamo bokuthi ngemva kobana soke sihlezi etafuleni, begodu bengilungiselela ukuzwa iindaba ezihle ngokudoba imikhomo; ngokumangala kwami, pheze woke amadoda athule khulu. Begodu ingasi lokho kwaphela, kodwana bebaqaleka banamahloni. Iye, lapha bekuneenja zelwandle, ezinengi zazo ngaphandle kokuba neenhloni ebegade zikhwele imikhomo emikhulu elwandle eliphezulu—ebezingaziwa—begodu zazilwa zife ngaphandle kokucwayiza; begodu nanyana kunjalo, lapha bebahlezi etafuleni lesidlo sasekuseni somphakathi—boke bebanebizo elifanako, boke bathanda izinto ezifanako—baqalana ngokuzithoba ngasuthi azange khebaphume esibayeni sezimvu hlangana neentaba eziluhlaza. Umbono omangalisako; amabhere la anamahloni, amaqhawe wemikhomo anamahloni!

Kodwana malungana no-Queequeg—kubayini, u-Queequeg bekahlezi lapho hlangana nabo—ekhanda letafula, nayo, kwaba yingozi; kupholile njengeqhwa. Ukuqiniseka angeze ngatjho okunengi ngokuzalanisa kwakhe. Umthandi wakhe omkhulu bekangakghoni ukuzithethelela ngokuzithoba ukuletha isibhamu sakhe esidlweni sasekuseni naye, begodu asisebenzise lapho ngaphandle komnyanya; wafikelela phezu kwetafula ngayo, ukuya engozini eseduze yamakhanda amanengi, begodu wabamba ama-beefsteaks waya kuye. Kodwana lokho kwenziwa nguye ngendlela epholileko, begodu woke umuntu uyazi bona ngokucabanga kwabantu abanengi, ukwenza nanyana yini ngendlela epholileko kutjho ukuyenza ngobumnene.

Angeze sakhuluma ngakho koke okuhlukileko kwe-Queequeg lapha; indlela agwema ngayo ikofi nama-roli atjhisako, begodu wasebenzisa itjhejo lakhe elipheleleko ema-beefsteaks, enziwa ngokungakavami. Kwanele, ukuthi lokha isidlo sasekuseni nasiphelile wahlehla njengabanye egumbini lomphakathi, wakhanyisa ipayipi lakhe le-tomahawk, begodu ahlezi lapho athule agaya begodu abhema ngesigqoko sakhe esingahlukaniswako, lokha nangiphuma ngiyokukhamba.





ISAHLUKO 6. Umgwaqo.

Ngathana bengimangale ekuthomeni ngibona umuntu ongakajayeleki njengo-Queequeg ajikeleza hlangana nomphakathi ohloniphekileko wedorobha eliphucukileko, ukumangala lokho kwaphela msinyana ngemva kokuthatha ikhambo lami lokuthoma emini emigwaqweni ye-New Bedford.

Emigwaqweni emikhulu eduze namadokodo, nanyana ngiliphi idokodo elikhulu lizakuvamise ukunikela ngokubuka izinto ezingakajayeleki ezivela eendaweni zangaphandle. Ngitjho nasemigwaqweni ye-Broadway ne-Chestnut, abasebenzi bemikhumbi be-Mediterranean kesinye isikhathi bazokugandelela abafazi abathukileko. I-Regent Street ayikaziwa ma-Lascars nama-Malays; begodu e-Bombay, e-Apollo Green, ama-Yankees aphilako avamise ukuthusa abantu bomdabu. Kodwana i-New Bedford inqoba yoke i-Water Street ne-Wapping. Eendaweni ezikhulunywe ngazo zokugcina ubona amatilosi kwaphela; kodwana e-New Bedford, abantu abadla abantu bajame bacoca emakhoneni weendlela; iinlwanyana; abanengi babo basathwele amathambo wabo inyama engacwengileko. Yenza umuntu ongamazi aqale.

Kodwana, ngaphandle kwama-Feegeeans, ama-Tongatobooarrs, ama-Erromanggoans, ama-Pannangians, nama-Brighggians, begodu, ngaphandle kweembonelo zeganga ze-whaling-craft ezitjhinga emgwaqweni, uzokubona ezinye izinto ezihlekisako, ngokuqinisekileko ezihlekisako. Njalo ngeveke kufika edorobheni leli amadoda amanengi we-Vermont kanye ne-New Hampshire, woke amile inzuzo nephazimulo emsebenzini wokudoba. Inengi lazo zisese zitjha, ezinamafreyimu aqinileko; abantu abagawule amahlathi, begodu nje bafuna ukulahla izembe begodu bahlwithe umkhonto womkhomo. Ezinengi ziluhlaza njengeentaba eziluhlaza lapho zivela khona. Kwezinye izinto ungacabanga bona zineenkhathi ezimbalwa. Qala lapho! umfana lowo otjhinga ekhoneni. Ufake isigqoko se-beaver nejazi elinomsila we-swallow, ufake ibhande lomkhumbi kanye nommese we-sheath. Nanku omunye ophethe i-sou'-wester nengubo ye-bombazine.

Ayikho i-dandy ekhuliswe edorobheni engamadanisa ne-dandy yasemaphandleni—ngitjho i-dandy ye-bumpkin epheleleko—umuntu, ngesikhathi sezinja, ozokusika ama-acre wakhe amabili ngamagilavu ​​wesikhumba sembuzi ngokwesaba ukutjhwaba izandla zakhe. Nje-ke lokha umdlali wendawo onjengalo nakathatha ekhanda lakhe ukwenza idumela elihle, bese ujoyina ukudoba imikhomo okukhulu, kufanele ubone izinto ezihlekisako azenzako nakafika ethekwini. Ekhuluma ngesambatho sakhe selwandle, u-oda iinkinobho zeensimbi emajazini wakhe; amabhande ebhrugweni lakhe le-canvas. Ah, imbewu ye-Hay embi! kuzokuqhuma kangangani imithambo leyo esiwuruwuruni sokuthoma, lokha nawuqhutshwa, imithambo, amabhathini, nakho koke, phasi emphinjeni wesiwuruwuru.

Kodwana ungacabangi bona idorobha elidumileko lineenqhema, abadla abantu, nama-bumpkins ukutjengisa iimvakatjhi zalo. Nakancani. Nanyana kunjalo i-New Bedford yindawo engakajayeleki. Ngathana bekungasi ngathi amadoda wemikhomo, indawo leyo bekungaba sebujameni obumbi njengeligwini le-Labrador. Njengombana kunjalo, iindawo zelizwe lakhe langemuva zanele ukuthusa umuntu, zibukeka zinamathambo. Idorobha ngokwalo mhlamunye yindawo ethandeka khulu yokuhlala kiyo, kiyo yoke i-New England. Lilizwe lamafutha, kwamambala: kodwana ingasi njengeKhanani; inarha, godu, yamabele newayini. Iintrada azigijimi ngebisi; begodu ngesikhathi sentwasahlobo abazifaki ngamaqanda amatjha. Nanyana kunjalo, nangaphandle kwalokhu, akukho lapho kiyo yoke i-Amerikha ozokufumana khona izindlu ezifana namakhosi; amapaki neengadi ezihle khulu, ukudlula e-New Bedford. Bavelaphi? njani ukutjalwa phezu kwe-scoria yenarha eyake yaba mnyama?

Hamba uyokuqala iinsimbi ezifana nama-harpoon azungeze indlu ephakemeko, begodu umbuzo wakho uzokuphendulwa. Iye; zoke iindlu eziqinileko neengadi ezinamathuthumbo zivela e-Atlantic, e-Pacific, ne-Indian oceans. Omunye nomunye, batjhatjalaliswa begodu bahudulelwa phezulu lapha besuka ngaphasi kwelwandle. Kghani u-Herr Alexander angenza into enje?

E-New Bedford, obaba, bathi, bapha amadodakazi wabo imikhomo, begodu babelane nabazukulu babo ngama-porpoise ambalwa. Kufanele uye e-New Bedford ukuyobona umtjhado omuhle khulu; ngombana, bathi, baneendawo zokugcina amafutha kiyo yoke imizi, begodu ubusuku ngabunye batjhisa ubude babo ngamakhandlela we-spermaceti.

“Isibane esijikelezako sitjhinga ngehlangothini, isibani esijikajikako sitjhinga kancani ngekamelweni lakaJona; begodu umkhumbi, utjhinga ngase-wharf ngesisindo samabhayili wokugcina atholiweko, isibani, ilangabi nakho koke, nanyana kunyakaza kancani, kusagcina ukutjhinga okungapheliko malungana nekamelweni nanyana, eqinisweni, kodwana ngokwakho, kwakwenza bona kube ngqo; kusobala amazinga amanga hlangana nawo ebegade alenga kiwo. umzwangedwa ulenga kimi!' uyabubula, 'qonda phezulu, ngalokho kuyatjhisa;

“Njengomuntu ngemva kobusuku bokuzithokozisa ngokudakwa, atjhidela embhedeni wakhe, asatjhinga, kodwana unembeza umhlaba, njengombana ukutjhinga kwehhashi lomjarho lamaRoma kodwana kutjhayela khulu amathegi wakhe wensimbi; njengomuntu osebujamweni obubuhlungu obusaphenduka begodu aphenduke ngobuhlungu obukhulu, athandaza uZimu bona atjhabalalise bekube kulapho ukuhlupheka kuphelile; ngosizi azizwako, ukuthuthumela okujulileko kumtshontsha, njengendoda eyopha igazi ukuya ekufeni, ngombana umzwangedwa uyisilonda, begodu akukho okungasiqinisa;

“Nje-ke isikhathi samagagasi sesifikile; umkhumbi ulahlela iintambo zawo; begodu ukusuka esikhumulweni esinganamuntu umkhumbi ongakajayeleki oya eTarshishi, woke uphaphatheka, utjhelela elwandle. “Nje-ke qala uJona athathwe njengesikhonkwane waphoselwa elwandle, kwathi msinyana ukuthula okunamafutha kuvela empumalanga, begodu ilwandle lithulile, njengombana uJona athwele isiwuruwuru, atjhiya amanzi abutjhelelezi ngemva. ulindele yena; begodu umkhomo utjhinga kiwo woke amazinyo wakhe wendlovu, njengeenhluthu ezinengi ezimhlophe, phezu kwejele lakhe. Isifuba sakhe esijulileko saphakama njengokuvuvuka kwephasi; iingalo zakhe eziphakanyiswako bezibonakala njengezinto ezilwako ezisebenzako; begodu ukuduma okwavela ebunzini lakhe elimnyama, nokukhanya okuvela emehlweni wakhe, kwenza boke abalaleli bakhe abalula bamqale ngokwesaba okurhabako ebekungakajayeleki kibo.
