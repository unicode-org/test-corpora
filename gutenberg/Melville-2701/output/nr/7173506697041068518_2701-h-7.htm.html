Endabeni yinye, u-Ahabi bekangabonakali angafani nabaphathi beenkhomo be-America, ebegade, njengesethi, bathambekele embonweni wokobana ngamalungelo indlu yomkhumbi ngeyabo; begodu nokuthi kungesizotha kwaphela ukuthi omunye umuntu, nganoma ngisiphi isikhathi, avunyelwe lapho. Ngalokho, eqinisweni, abangani nabadlali be-Pequod kungatjhiwo bona bebahlala ngaphandle kwekhabini kunokobana babe ngaphakathi kwayo. Ngombana lokha nabangena kiyo, bekuyinto efana nomnyango womgwaqo ongena endlini; ukuphendukela ngaphakathi isikhatjhana, bese uphendukela ngaphandle kwesikhatjhana; begodu, njengento ehlala njalo, ihlala emoyeni ovulekileko. Begodu azange balahlekelwe khulu ngalokhu; ngaphakathi kwendlu bekunganabungani; ngokwehlalakuhle, u-Ahabi bekangafinyeleleki. Nanyana gade afakwe ekubalweni kweLobukholwa, bekasese mfokazi kikho. Bekaphila ephasini, njengombana i-Grisly Bears yokugcina gade ihlala e-Missouri. Njengombana iNtwasahlobo neHlobo sele zihambile, uLogan loyo wehlathi, azifihle emgodini wesihlahla, waphila lapho ubusika, amunya iinyawo zakhe; ngalokho, ebudaleni bakhe obumbi, obubhongako, umphefumulo ka-Ahabi, ovalelwe ngaphakathi kwesiqu somzimba wakhe, lapho wawudla ngeenyawo ezidabukileko zokufiphala kwawo!





ISAHLUKO 35. Ihloko ye-Mast.

Kwaba ngesikhathi sezulu esihle khulu, lapho ngokujikeleza okufaneleko nabanye abasebenzi bemikhumbi ikhanda lami lokuthoma le-mast lafika khona.

Ema-whalemen amanengi we-America iinhloko ze-mast ziphethwe pheze ngesikhathi sinye nomkhumbi nawutjhiya idokodo lakhe; nanyana angaba namamayela ayizinkulungwane ezilitjhumi nahlanu, nokunye, ukuhamba ngesikepe ngaphambi kobana afike endaweni yakhe yokukhamba ngesikepe. Begodu nangabe, ngemva kwekhambo leminyaka emithathu, emine, nofana emihlanu sele atjhidela ekhaya anento enganalitho kiyo—sithi, isitja esinganalitho—khona-ke, iinhloko zakhe zesikhonkwane zigcinwa zinabantu bekube sekugcineni; begodu ingasi bekube kulapho ama-skysail-poles wakhe angena phakathi kweenqhema zetheku, alahleke ngokupheleleko ithemba lokubamba umkhomo owodwa.

Nje-ke, njengombana ibhizinisi lokuma iinhloko ze-mast, eligwini nofana elimanzini, liyinto yakade khulu nethakazelisako, ake sikhulume lapha. Ngiyakuthatha, bona abantu bokuthoma abajame iinhloko zama-mast bekumaGibhide amadala; ngombana, kikho koke ukurhubhulula kwami, angifumani litho ngaphambi kwabo. Ngombana nanyana abokhokho babo, abakhi beBhabheli, ngokungangabazeki, ngombhoshongo wabo, bebahlose ukukhulisa ikhanda eliphezulu khulu kunazo zoke i-Asia, nofana i-Afrika; kodwana (ngaphambi kobana iloli yokugcina ifakwe kiyo) njengombana insika yabo ekulu yamatshe kungatjhiwo bona yadlula ebhodini, ngesiwuruwuru esikhulu selaka lakaZimu; ngalokho-ke, angeze sakwazi ukunikela abakhi beBhabheli laba phambili ukudlula amaGibhide. Nokuthi amaGibhide bekusitjhaba esijame ngeenhloko ze-mast, kukugandelela okusekelwe ekolweni ejwayelekileko hlangana nezazi zezinto zakade, kobana amaphiramidi wokuthoma asungulwa ngeenhloso zeenkwekwezi: ithiyori esekelwa ngokukhethekileko ukwakheka okufana nezitebhisi kwawo woke amahlangothi amane wezakhiwo lezo; lapho, ngokuphakama okude kwemilenze yabo, izazi zeenkwekwezi ezindala bezijayele ukukhuphuka ziye esicongweni, begodu zivumele iinkwekwezi ezitjha; ngitjho nanyana ukuqalisa komkhumbi wesimanjemanje kuvuma iseyila, nofana umkhomo obonakalako. Ku-Saint Stylites, umKrestu odumileko weenkhathi zakade, owamakhela insika ephakemeko yamatshe erhalawumbeni begodu waqeda ingcenye yoke yokugcina yokuphila kwakhe esiqongweni sayo, aphakamisa ukudla kwakhe phasi nge-tackle; kuye sinesibonelo esimangalisako somuntu onganakwesaba; obekangasoze aqothwe endaweni yakhe ziinkungu namkha amaqhwa, izulu, isichotho, namkha iqhwa; kodwana ngesibindi aqalene nakho koke bekube sekugcineni, wafela esikhundleni sakhe. Kwama-stander-we-mast-heads wesimanjemanje sinesethi engaphiliko; amadoda wamatje, insimbi, nethusi; labo, nanyana bakghona ukujamelana nesiwuruwuru esinamandla, basangakakwazi ukusebenza ngokuhlabelela lokha nabathola nanyana ngikuphi ukubona okungakajayeleki. Kukhona u-Napoleon; ngubani, phezu kwekholomu ye-Vendome, ajame izandla ezigoqene, ngamamitha alikhulu namatjhumi amahlanu emoyeni; ukunganaki, nje, ngubani obusa amadekhi angenzasi; kungaba ngu-Louis Philippe, u-Louis Blanc, nofana u-Louis the Devil. I-Great Washington, nayo, ijame phezulu e-main-mast yayo ephakemeko e-Baltimore, begodu njengenye yeensika zika-Hercules, ikholomu yakhe itshwaya iphuzu lobukhulu bomuntu lapho abantu abambalwa abazokudlula khona. U-Admiral Nelson, naye, phezu kwe-capstan yensimbi ye-gun, ujame ihloko yakhe ye-mast e-Trafalgar Square; begodu nanyana kunini lokha nakufihlwa msizi we-London, kunikelwa itshwayo lokuthi iqhawe elifihlekileko likhona; ngombana lapho kunentuthu, kufanele kube nomlilo. Kodwana i-Washington ekulu, nanyana u-Napoleon, nanyana u-Nelson, angeze waphendula isichotho esisodwa esivela phasi, nanyana bangabizwa kangangani bona babe mngani ngeziluleko zabo namadekhi aphazamisekileko abaqale kiwo; nanyana kunjalo kungaqagelwa, bona imimoya yabo ingena ngaphakathi kwenkungu eqinileko yesikhathi esizako, begodu ihlathulula bona ngiziphi iinqhema namatje okufuze agwenywe.

Kungabonakala kungakalungi ukuhlanganisa nganoma ngiyiphi indlela iinqhema zesikhonkwane sezwe nalezo zaselwandle; kodwana ukuthi eqinisweni akunjalo, kufakazelwa ngokukhanyako yinto u-Obed Macy, umlando oyedwa we-Nantucket, aphendula ngayo. U-Obed ohloniphekileko usitjela bona, ngesikhathi sokuthoma sokudoba imikhomo, ngaphambi kokuthi imikhumbi ihlale ithunyelwa ukulandela umdlalo lo, abantu besiqhingi leso bebakha ama-spar aphakemeko ngasegwini lelwandle, lapho iinqhema ebezikhuphukela khona ngeenqhema ezibethelwe ngezipikili, into efana neenkukhu ezikhuphuka ngaphakathi kwendlu. Eminyakeni embalwa eyadlulako ihlelo elifanako lamukelwa babantu be-Bay whalemen be-New Zealand, ebegade, lokha nabahlambalaza umdlalo lo, banikela isaziso ngeenkepe ezilungele abantu eduze nelwandle. Kodwana isiko leli seliphelile; sibe sesijika siye ehloko eyodwa efaneleko, leyo yomkhumbi wemikhomo elwandle. Iinhloko ezintathu ze-mast zigcinwa zinabantu ukusuka ekuphumeni kwelanga ukuya ekutjhingeni kwelanga; abasebenzi bemikhumbi bathatha amatjhuguluko wabo avamileko (njengasesiqhemeni), begodu bakhulula omunye nomunye qobe ngemva kwama-awa amabili. Ebujameni bezulu obuthulileko beendawo ezitjhisako kumnandi khulu ukutjhinga kwe-mast-head; awa, endodeni enephupho elizindla kuyathabisa. Ujame lapho, ngamamitha alikhulu ngaphezulu kwamadekhi athulileko, ukhamba ngeenyawo ezijulileko, kwangathi iinsika ziziinsika ezikulu, lokha ngaphasi kwakho begodu hlangana nemilenze yakho, njengokungathi, kubhukuda iinlwanyana ezinkulu khulu zelwandle, njengombana imikhumbi gade ihamba hlangana namabhuzu we-Colossus edumileko e-Rhodes endala. Ujame lapho, ulahlekile emlandweni ongapheliko welwandle, kunganalitho eliguquguqukako ngaphandle kwamagagasi. Umkhumbi othulileko ugijima ngokunganaki; imimoya yokurhweba evuthako; yoke into ikwenza bona ube mraro. Ngokwengcenye ekulu, epilweni yokudoba imikhomo etjhisako, ukungabi nezehlakalo okuphakemeko kukutjala; awuzwa iindaba; ungafundi amagazethi; izinto ezingezelelweko ezinama-akhawunti amangalisako wezinto ezijayelekileko azisoze zikukhohlise ekuthabeni okungakafaneli; awuzwa ngeenhlupheko zasekhaya; izibambiso ezibhidlikako; ukuwa kwamasheya; awukaze utshwenyeke ngomcabango wokuthi uzokudla ini ngesidlo sakusihlwa—ngombana koke ukudla kwakho kweminyaka emithathu nangaphezulu kugcinwe kuhle emabhokisini, begodu imali yakho yokubhadela ayitjhuguluki.

Komunye wabantu abasebenza ngemikhomo, ekhambweni elide leminyaka emithathu nofana emine, njengombana kuvamise ukwenzeka, inani lama-awa ahlukahlukeneko owachitha ekhanda le-mast lingaba ziinyanga ezimbalwa ezipheleleko. Begodu kuyadabukisa khulu ukuthi indawo ozinikela kiyo ingcenye ekulu yesikhathi soke sepilo yakho yemvelo, kufanele ibe yinto edabukisako enganakho okusondela ekuhlaleni okufuthumeleko, nofana ilungiselelwe ukuzalanisa umuzwa okhululekileko wendawo, njengombhede, i-hammock, ikoloyi yezidumbu, ibhokisi lokulinda, ipulpiti, nanyana ngikuphi okunye okuncani amaqhinga lapho amadoda azihlukanisa khona okwesikhatjhana. Indawo yakho ejayelekileko yokuhlala yihloko ye-t' gallant-mast, lapho ujame khona phezu kweentonga ezimbili ezincani eziphambeneko (ezingakajayeleki ema-whalemen) ezibizwa nge-t' gallant cross-trees. Lapha, aphoselwa lilwandle, umuntu osathomako uzizwa athokomele njengombana bekangajame phezu kweempondo zenkunzi. Ukuqiniseka, ebujameni bezulu obubandako ungathwala indlu yakho phezulu nawe, ngendlela yejazi lokugada; kodwana ngokukhuluma kuhle ijazi lokulinda elidephileko alisiyo indlu ngaphandle komzimba ongakagqokwa; ngombana njengombana umphefumulo unamathele ngaphakathi kwetabernakele yawo enenyama, begodu ungakghoni ukukhamba ngokutjhaphuluka kiwo, namkha uphume kiwo, ngaphandle kokuba sengozini ekulu yokutjhabalala (njengehambi elinganalwazi eliwela i-Alps eneqhwa ebusika); njeke ijazi lewatjhi akusilo indlu kangako njengombana liyimvilophu nje, nofana isikhumba esingezelelweko esikugubuzelako. Angeze wafaka ishelufu nofana ibhokisi lamakhabethe emzimbeni wakho, begodu angeze wakghona ukwenza ikhabethe elilula lejazi lakho lewashi.

Malungana nakho koke lokhu, kuyadanisa khulu ukuthi iinhloko zemikhumbi yemikhomo yesewula ayinawo amatende amancani namkha amapulpithi, abizwa ngokuthi ziindleke zamagwababa , lapho ukuqapha komdosi wemikhomo we-Greenland kuvikelwe khona ebujameni bezulu obumbi bamalwandle aqandako. Emlandwini kaKaputeni Sleet, onesihloko esithi "Uhambo hlangana nama-Iceberg, ukufuna i-Greenland Whale, begodu ngokuya ngokutholakala kabutjha kwama-Lost Icelandic Colonies we-Old Greenland;" kilomqulu omuhle khulu, woke ama-mast-heads anikelwe i-akhawunti ekhangako ye- crow's-nest ye-Glacier, ebegade ibizo lesikepe esihle sakaKaputeni Sleet. Wayibiza ngokuthi yi- Sleet's crow's-nest , ukuzihlonipha; yena njengomsunguli wokuthoma nomnikazi welungelo lobunikazi, begodu ukhululekile kibo boke ubumnandi bamala, begodu uthi nangabe sibiza abantwabethu ngamagama wethu (thina obaba sibasunguli bokuthoma nabanelungelo lobunikazi), ngokufanako kufuze sibize ngemuva kwethu nanyana ngiyiphi enye into esingayizala. Ngokwejamo, isidleke se-Sleet's crow's-nest sifana ne-tierce ekulu nofana ipayipi; ivulekile ngaphezulu, nanyana kunjalo, lapho inikelwe khona isikrini esiseceleni esithuthekako ukugcina ikhanda lakho libheke emmoyeni lokha nakunesiwuruwuru esinamandla. Njengombana ujame esiqongweni sesikhonkwane, ukhuphukela kiso nge-hatch encani yokubamba ngaphasi. Ngehlangothini langemuva, nofana ngehlangothini eliseduze nemuva lomkhumbi, kunesihlalo esikhululekileko, esine-locker ngaphasi kwezambulela, iinduduzo, namajazi. Ngaphambili kune-rack yesikhumba, lapho ungabeka khona icilongo lakho lokukhuluma, ipayipi, isibonakude, nezinye izinto ezilula zelwandle. Lokha uKaputeni u-Sleet nakajame ihloko yakhe esidlekeni sakhe, usitjela bona bekahlala anesibhamu (naso ebegade sibekwe ngaphakathi kwe-rack), kanye neflaski yepuyere begodu adubule, ngenhloso yokukhipha ama-narwhale alahlekileko, nofana ama-unicorn welwandle ahambahambako ahlasela amanzi lawo; ngombana angeze wakghona ukuzidubula ngepumelelo usuka emkhunjini ngebanga lokujamelana kwamanzi, kodwana ukuzidubula phasi yinto ehluke khulu. Nje-ke, bekungumsebenzi wethando kuKaputeni Sleet ukuhlathulula, njengombana enza, zoke izinto ezincani ezilula zesidleke sakhe segwababa; kodwana nanyana akhulisa okunengi kwalokhu, begodu nanyana asiphatha ngendaba yezesayensi yokuhlola kwakhe esidlekeni segwababa, ngekhamphasi encani ayigcine lapho ngenhloso yokulwisana namaphutha abangelwa yilokho okubizwa ngokuthi "ukukhanga kwalapho" kwawo woke amazibuthe we-binnacle; iphutha elibangelwa kutjhidela okuvundlileko kwensimbi emapulangweni womkhumbi, begodu endabeni ye-Glacier, mhlamunye, ngokuthi bekuneenkhandi ezinengi kangaka eziphukileko hlangana nesiqhema sakhe; Ngithi, nanyana uKaputeni ahlakaniphile khulu begodu anesayensi lapha, kodwana, kikho koke ukufunda kwakhe “ukuphambuka kwe-binnacle,” “ukutjheja kwekhamphasi ye-azimuth,” kanye “namaphutha alinganiselweko,” uyazi kuhle, uKaputeni Sleet, bona bekangazibandakanyi kangako kilokho kuzindla okujulileko kwamazibuthe, njengombana bekahluleka ukudoselwa ngezinye iinkhathi ezintweni ezincani, kangaka efihlekile ngakelinye ihlangothi lesidleke sakhe segwababa, endaweni elula yokufinyelela ngesandla sakhe. Nanyana kunjalo, ngiyamthanda khulu begodu ngiyamthanda uKaputeni onesibindi, othembekileko nofundileko; kodwana ngiyakuthatha kumbi ngaye ukuthi angayinaki ibhodlelo lekheyisi, ngokubona bona bekungumngani othembekileko nomduduzi, ngesikhathi ngeminwe efakwe izandla nehloko enesigqoko bekafunda iimbalo phezulu lapho esidlekeni senyoni ngaphakathi kweendawo ezintathu nofana ezine zepali.

Kodwana nangabe thina abadobi bemikhomo beSewula asihlali kuhle phezulu njengoKaputeni Sleet nabantu bakhe be-Greenland; kodwana ukungalungi lokho kuphikiswa khulu ngokuthula okuhlukileko kwalawo malwandle ahlaba umxhwele lapho thina abadobi beSewula sizintanta khona khulu. Kokuthoma, bengivame ukuphumula phezulu kwe-rigging ngokukhululeka, ngiphumule phezulu ukucoca no-Queequeg, nofana nanyana ngubani omunye ongasebenziko engingamthola lapho; bese ngikhuphuka kancani, bese ngiphosa umlenze onganamandla phezu kwegceke eliphezulu, ngibone amadlelo anamanzi, bese ekugcineni ngikhamba ngiye lapho ngiya khona.

Akhe ngenze isifuba esihlanzekileko ngakho lapha, begodu ngivume ngokutjho ngokutjho bona ngigcine kodwana ngiyacolisa umlindi. Njengombana umraro wendawo yoke ujikeleza kimi, ngingakghona njani—ngokutjhiywa ngedwa ngokupheleleko endaweni ephakeme kangaka—ngingakghona njani ukubamba iimbopho zami zokulandela imiyalo yoke yemikhumbi yemikhomo, “Gcina amehlo wakho wobujamo bezulu avulekile, begodu uhlabelele ngaso soke isikhathi.”

Begodu ake nginiyelelise endaweni le, nina banikazi bemikhumbi ye-Nantucket! Tjheja ukubhalisa emisebenzini yakho yokutjheja iimfesi nanyana ngimuphi umsana oneenhluthu ezincani nelihlo elivulekileko; unikelwe ukuzindla okungakafaneli; begodu ngubani onikela ngokuthumela nge-Phædon esikhundleni se-Bowditch ekhanda lakhe. Qaphela umuntu onjalo, ngithi; imikhomo yakho kufanele ibonwe ngaphambi kobana ibulawe; begodu u-Plato omncani onamehlo atjhingako uzokudosela amavuko alitjhumi ephasini loke, begodu angeze akwenze ube mnothi khulu. Begodu iinqophiso lezi azitlhogeki. Njenganje, ukudoba imikhomo kunikela indawo yokukhosela emajaheni amanengi anethando, adanileko, begodu angacabangi, anengekile ngokukhathazeka kwephasi, begodu afuna umuzwa nge-tar ne-blubber. U-Childe Harold akavami ukuzihlalela phezu kwehloko ye-mast-whale-whale-whale, begodu ngamazwi ahlasimulisa umzimba ukhipha amanzi:—

“Ragela phambili, wena lwandle oluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka olujulileko, gijima!
Abazingeli be-blubber abaziinkulungwana ezilitjhumi bakutjhayela ngeze.”

Kanengi khulu abokaputeni bemikhumbi enjalo bathatha izazi zefilosofi ezisese zitjha ezinganandaba, zibakhuza ngokungabi "nekareko" eyaneleko ngekhambo; ukutjho bona balahlekelwe lithemba kizo zoke iinhloso ezihloniphekileko, njengombana emphefumulweni wabo oyimfihlo bangakhetha ukungaboni imikhomo kunokuthi bangaboni ngenye indlela. Kodwana koke kwaba lilize; labo baPlato abasesebatjha banombono wokobana umbono wabo awuphelele; ababoni kude; kusetjenziselwani-ke, ukugandelela ithambo lokubona? Batjhiye iingilazi zabo ze-opera ekhaya.

"Kubayini, wena mkawu," kwatjho umdlali we-harpoon komunye wabafana, "sele sikhambe budisi iminyaka emithathu, begodu awukafuyi umkhomo. Imikhomo iyatlhogeka njengamazinyo wenkukhu nanyana kunini lokha nawuphezulu lapha." Mhlamunye bebanjalo; namkha mhlamunye bekuneenqhema zazo emkhathizweni omude; kodwana ufakwe ebujamweni obufana ne-opium bokucabanga okunganalitho, okungaziko mfana lo ongakacabangi ngokuhlanganiswa kwamagagasi nemicabango, ekugcineni ulahlekelwa buwena bakhe; uthatha ilwandle eliyimfihlakalo ngaphasi kweenyawo zakhe ukuthola isithombe esibonakalako somphefumulo ojulileko, ohlaza satjani, ongenamkhawulo, ogcwele abantu nemvelo; nayo yoke into engakajayeleki, ebonakalako, ephaphazelako, ehle emtjhiyako; woke amaphiko atholakala kancani, aphaphamako wendlela ethileko engabonakaliko, kubonakala kuye njengesibonakaliso saleyo micabango engazwisisekiko eyenza abantu umphefumulo ngokuragela phambili nokuphaphazela kiwo. Ngalesi simo sokuthakazelisa, umoya wakho uyatjhinga lapho uvela khona; iyasabalala ngesikhathi nangendawo; njenge-Cranmer's sprinkled Pantheistic ashes, ekugcineni yakha ingcenye yawo woke ama-shore ephasini loke.

Akukho kuphila kuwe, nje, ngaphandle kokuphila okuzamazamako okunikelwe mkhumbi ogijima kancanikancani; nguye, ebolekwe elwandle; ngaselwandle, ukusuka emagagasini angazwisisekiko kaZimu. Kodwana ngesikhathi ulele, iphupho leli liphezu kwakho, tjhugulula unyawo lwakho nofana isandla sakho; tjhiya ukubamba kwakho; begodu ubuwena bubuya ngokuthusa. Phezu kwama-vortices we-Descartian uyaphapha. Mhlamunye, phakathi nemini, ebujameni bezulu obuhle khulu, ngokurhuwelela okukodwa okuqinileko uwela emoyeni okhanyako uye elwandle lehlobo, ungasakghoni ukuvuka unomphela. Yilalelisise kuhle, nina maPhentheist!





ISAHLUKO 36. Ikota-Dekhi.

( Kungena u-Ahabi: Bese, koke. )

Bekungasi sikhathi eside ngemva kwendaba yepayipi, ukuthi ngelinye ilanga ekuseni ngemva kwesidlo sasekuseni, u-Ahabi, njengombana bekajayelekile, wakhuphukela emgwaqweni wendlu.
