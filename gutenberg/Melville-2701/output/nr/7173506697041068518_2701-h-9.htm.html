ISAHLUKO 44. Itjhadi.

Ngathana bewulandele uKaputeni u-Ahabi wangena ngendlini yakhe ngemva kwesiwuruwuru esenzeka ngobusuku obulandela ukuqinisekiswa okumbi kwehloso yakhe nesiqhema sakhe, bewuzombona aya e-locker ngaphakathi kwe-transom, begodu akhupha irolo elikhulu lamatjhadi welwandle aphuzi, awasabalalise phambi kwakhe etafuleni lakhe elibomvu. Bese ahlale phambi kwayo, bewuzombona afunda ngokucophelela imigqa ehlukahlukeneko nemithunzi eyahlangana nelihlo lakhe; begodu ngepensela ebuthakathaka kodwana eqinileko landelela ezinye iinkhathi eendaweni ebezinganalitho ngaphambilini. Ngeenkhathi ezithileko, bekakhuluma ngeenqwaba zamabhuku amadala eceleni kwakhe, lapho bekubekwe khona amasizini neendawo lapho, emakhambweni ahlukahlukeneko wangaphambilini wemikhumbi ehlukahlukeneko, imikhomo yesidoda beyibanjwe namkha ibonwe.

Lokha nakasetjenziswe njalo, isibani esinzima se-pewter esilengiswe ngamaketani phezu kwehloko yakhe, besiragela phambili nokusikinyeka ngokunyakaza komkhumbi, begodu sihlala siphosa ukukhanya okutjhugulukako namathunzi wemigqa phezu kwebunzi lakhe elishwabeneko, bekwaba kulapho kubonakala ngasuthi ngesikhathi yena ngokwakhe atshwaya imigqa namakhosi ematjhadeni ashwabeneko, bekubuye kube neminye imigqa ebonakalako phezu kwetjhadi elimakwe ngokujulileko lebunzi lakhe.

Kodwana bekungasibo ubusuku lobu khulukhulu lapho, eyedwa endlini yakhe, u-Ahabi wazindla ngamatjhadi wakhe. Pheze qobe ebusuku bebakhutjhwa ngaphandle; pheze qobe ebusuku amanye amatshwayo wepensela bekasuswa, bese kuthi amanye afakwe esikhundleni sawo. Ngombana ngamatjhadi wawo woke amalwandle amane phambi kwakhe, u-Ahabi bekakhamba ngendlela yemisinga, ngombono wokufezeka okuqinisekileko komcabango womphefumulo wakhe.

Nje-ke, kibo boke abangazi kuhle iindlela zama-leviathan, kungabonakala kungumsebenzi ongenathemba ukufuna isidalwa sinye esisodwa emalwandle angakavunyelwa weplanethi le. Kodwana bekungabonakali njalo ku-Ahabi, obekazi amasethi wawo woke amagagasi namagagasi; begodu ngaleyo ndlela kubalwe ukutjhinga kokudla kwe-sperm whale; begodu, godu, ukukhumbula iinkhathi ezijayelekileko, eziqinisekisiweko zokuzingela yena eendaweni ezithileko; angafika ekuqageleni okuzwakalako, pheze atjhidele ekuqinisekeni, malungana nelanga elifaneleko lokuba sendaweni le namkha leyo ayokufuna inyamazana yakhe.

Kwaqinisekiswa, kwamambala, iqiniso eliphathelene nokukhamba kwesikhathi kwe-sperm whale emanzini anikelweko, kangangobana abazingeli abanengi bakholelwa bona, ingabe ingatjhejwa begodu ifundwe ephasini loke; nangabe amalogi wohambo olulodwa lwemikhumbi yoke yemikhomo ahlanganiswe ngokutjheja, khona-ke ukufuduka komkhomo wesidoda kuzokufumaneka bona kuhambelana ngokungatjhugulukiko nalawo we-herring-shoals nofana ukuphapha kwama-swallows. Ngalokhu, kwenziwe imizamo yokwakha amatjhadi wokufuduka we-sperm whale.*

Ngaphandle kwalokho, lokha nayenza umzila ukusuka kwenye indawo yokudla ukuya kwenye, imikhomo yesidoda, eqondiswa mzwa othileko ongaphosekiko—sithi, kunalokho, ubuhlakani obuyimfihlo obuvela kuZimu—kanengi ibhukuda emithanjeni , njengombana ibizwa njalo; baragela phambili ngendlela yabo emgqeni welwandle ngokuqondileko okungapheliko, kangangobana akukho mkhumbi owakhe wahamba ngendlela yawo, nganoma ngiliphi itjhadi, ngetjhumi elilodwa lokunemba okumangalisa kangaka. Nanyana, emacaleni la, indlela ethathwa mkhomo munye kufanele iqonde njengomhloli, begodu nanyana umugqa wokuragela phambili ungavalelwa khulu ekuvukeni kwawo okungabalekiko, okuqondileko, kodwana umthambo ongakhethiko lapho ngalezi zikhathi kuthiwa uyabhukuda khona, ngokuvamileko uhlanganisa amakhilomitha ambalwa ngobubanzi (umthambo omncani nofana omncani, njengoba kucatshangwa bona umthambo unwetshwa); kodwana angeze yadlula ukukhanya okuvela eenhlokweni zomkhumbi wemikhomo, lokha nayitjhelela ngokucophelela endaweni yomlingo. Isamba kukuthi, ngeenkhathi ezithileko ngaphakathi kobubanzi obunjalo begodu ngaleyondlela, imikhomo efudukako ingafunwa ngokuzithemba okukhulu.

Ngalokho-ke ingasi ngeenkhathi eziqinisekisiweko kwaphela, eendaweni zokudla ezihlukileko ezaziwako, u-Ahabi bekangathemba ukuhlangana nenyamazana yakhe; kodwana ekuweleni iindawo ezibanzi khulu zamanzi hlangana nalawo mabala bekangakghona, ngobukghwari bakhe, ukuzibeka begodu azibeke isikhathi endleleni yakhe, ukwenzela bona ngitjho nangaleso sikhathi angabi nethemba lokuhlangana.

Kwaba nobujamo ekuthomeni ebegade bubonakala buphazamisa ihlelo lakhe elingakalungi kodwana elisese lihle. Kodwana akunjalo eqinisweni, mhlamunye. Nanyana imikhomo ye-sperm gregarious inesikhathi sayo esijayelekileko sendawo ethileko, kodwana ngokuvamileko angeze waphetha ngokuthi imihlambi ebegade ihlasela i-latitude nofana i-longitude enjalo nomnyaka lo, sithi, izokufana naleyo eyatholakala lapho ngesikhathi esidlulileko; nanyana kuneenkhathi ezingakajayeleki nezingathandabuzekiko lapho okuphambene nalokhu kufakazele kuliqiniso. Ngokuvamileko, ukuphawula okufanako, ngaphakathi komkhawulo ongaphasi, kusebenza ebantwini abahlala bodwa nabahlala bodwa hlangana nemikhomo yesidoda evuthiweko, endala. Ngalokho nanyana i-Moby Dick gade ibonwe emnyakeni odlulileko, ngokwesibonelo, endaweni ebizwa ngokuthi yi-Seychelle ground e-Indian ocean, nofana i-Volcano Bay e-Japanese Coast; kodwana azange kulandele, bona nangabe i-Pequod izokuvakatjhela enye yalezo zindawo nganoma ngisiphi isizini elandelako, bekazokuhlangana naye lapho. Kunjalo, nangezinye iindawo zokudla, lapho bekaziveza khona ngezinye iinkhathi. Kodwana koke lokhu bekubonakala njengeendawo zakhe zokuma ezijayelekileko neendawo zokuhlala elwandle, singatjho njalo, ingasi iindawo zakhe zokuhlala isikhathi eside. Begodu lapho amathuba ka-Ahabi wokufeza umnqopho wakhe bekukhulunywa ngawo, kukhulunywa ngawo nanyana ngiyiphi indlela, ukuqala, amathemba angezelelweko ebegade akhe, ngaphambi kobana kufinyelelwe isikhathi esithileko nofana indawo, lapho woke amathuba azokuba mathuba, begodu, njengombana u-Ahabi bekacabanga, yoke into engenzeka ibe yinto elandelako. Isikhathi esithileko nendawo ebekiweko bezihlanganiswe emtjhweni owodwa wezobuchwepheshe—Isikhathi-esigabeni. Ngombana lapho, iminyaka eminengi ilandelana, u-Moby Dick bekasolo achazwa, ahlale emanzini lawo isikhatjhana, njengombana ilanga, emzuliswaneni walo wonyaka, lihlala isikhathi esithileko esibikezelweko kinoma ngiliphi itshwayo le-Zodiac. Kwaba khona, godu, lapho inengi lokuhlangana okubulalako nomkhomo omhlophe kwenzeke khona; lapho amagagasi bekakhulunywa ngezenzo zakhe; bekunendawo edabukisako lapho indoda endala ethanda ukuziphindiselela ithole khona umnqopho omumbi wokuziphindiselela kwayo. Kodwana ngokutjheja ngokutjheja nokutjheja okungapheliko u-Ahabi aphosa ngakho umphefumulo wakhe ocabangako ekuzingeleni okungapheliko, azange azivumele bona abeke woke amathemba wakhe eqinisweni elilodwa elikhulunywe ngalo ngehla, nanyana kungaba lihle kangangani lawo mathemba; begodu ngokungalali kwesifungo sakhe bekangakghoni ukuthulisa ihliziywakhe engathulileko kangangobana ahlehlise koke ukufuna ukungenelela.

Nje, i-Pequod beyisuka e-Nantucket ekuthomeni kwe-Season-on-the-Line. Akukho mzamo ongakwenza bona umlawuli wakhe akghone ukwenza indlela ekulu eya eningizimu, kabili i-Cape Horn, bese agijima phasi ngamadigri amatjhumi asithandathu we-latitude afike e-equatorial Pacific ngesikhathi sokukhamba ngesikepe lapho. Ngalokho-ke, kufuze alinde isizini elandelako. Nanyana kunjalo i-awa engakafiki yokuhamba kwe-Pequod, mhlamunye, beyikhethwe kuhle ngu-Ahabi, ngombono wombala wezinto. Ngombana, isikhatjhana samalanga namatjhumi amathathu namatjhumi asithandathu nahlanu besiphambi kwakhe; isikhatjhana, esikhundleni sokuthi abekezelele egwini ngokuphelelwa sineke, bekangasisebenzisa ekuzingeleni okuhlukahlukeneko; nangabe ngengozi i-White Whale, ichitha ikhefu layo elwandle elikude khulu nendawo yayo yokudla, ingaveza ubuso bayo obuhlangeneko e-Persian Gulf, nofana e-Bengal Bay, nofana e-China Seas, nofana kiwo woke amanye amanzi ahlushwa mhlobo wayo. Ngalokho ama-Monsoons, ama-Pampas, ama-Nor'-Westers, ama-Harmattans, ama-Trades; nanyana ngimuphi umoya ngaphandle kwe-Levanter ne-Simoon, ungavuthela u-Moby Dick ngaphakathi komjikelezo wephasi we-Pequod's circumnavigating wake.

Kodwana ukunikela koke lokhu; kodwana, nakuqalwa ngokuhlakanipha nangokupholileko, kubonakala kungasi mqondo ohlanyako, lokhu; ukuthi elwandle olubanzi olungenamkhawulo, umkhomo owodwa, ngitjho nanyana ungahlangabezana nawo, kufanele ucatjangwe bona ungakghona ukubonwa mzingeli wawo, ngitjho njengoMufti oneentshebe ezimhlophe emigwaqweni emikhulu ye-Constantinople? Iye. Ngombana ubuso obumhlophe obufana neqhwa buka-Moby Dick, kanye ne-hump yakhe emhlophe njengeqhwa, bekungeze kwaphazanyiswa. Begodu angizange ngiyibale umkhomo, u-Ahabi bekazibubula, njengombana ngemva kokuhlolisisa amatjhadi wakhe bekwaba ngemva kwesikhathi eside phakathi kobusuku bekaziphosela emuva emaphupheni—wambala, begodu uzokuphunyuka na? Iimphiko zakhe ezibanzi zibhorekile, begodu ziphume njengendlebe yemvu elahlekileko! Begodu lapha, ingqondo yakhe ehlanyako beyizokugijima emjarhweni ongaphelelwa mumoya; bekwaba kulapho ukudinwa nokuphelelwa mamandla kokuzindla kumfikela; begodu emoyeni ovulekileko wedekhi bekafuna ukubuyisa amandlakhe. Hawu, Zimu! ngiziphi iinhlupheko ezibekezelela indoda leyo ephethwe sifiso sokuziphindiselela esingakafezeki. Ulala ngezandla eziqinileko; begodu uvuka aneenzipho zakhe ezineengazi ezandleni zakhe.

Kanengi, lokha nakagandelelwa bona aphume embhedeni wakhe ngamabhudango wobusuku akhathazako begodu angabekezelelekiko, ebegade, athoma imicabango yakhe emini, aragela phambili hlangana nokutjhayisana kwama-phrensies, begodu awajikelezisa ebuchosheni bakhe obuvuthako, bekwaba kulapho ukubetha kwebala lakhe lokuphila kungabekezeleleki ukuthukuthela; begodu lokha, njengombana bekunjalo ngezinye iinkhathi, ukugandeleleka okungokomoya kuye kwaphakamisa ubuntu bakhe ukusuka esizibeni sayo, begodu kwabonakala kuvuleka umgodi kuye, lapho amalangabi ahlukeneko nemibane kwavela khona, begodu amadimoni aqalekisiweko amkhomba bona weqe phasi hlangana nawo; lokha isihogo ngokwaso sizamula ngaphasi kwakhe, ukukhala okukhulu bekuzwakala ngaphakathi komkhumbi; begodu ngamehlo akhazimulako u-Ahabi bekaphuma egumbini lakhe lobukhosi, kube ngasuthi uphunyuka embhedeni ovutha umlilo. Kodwana lokhu, mhlamunye, kunokobana kube ziimpawu ezingagandeleleki zobuthakathaka obuthileko obufihlakeleko, nofana ukwesaba ngokuzimisela kwakhe, bekuziimpawu ezicacileko zokuqina kwawo. Ngombana, ngeenkhathi ezinjalo, u-Ahabi ohlanyako, umzingeli womkhomo omhlophe onobuqili, oqinileko; u-Ahabi lo obekaye e-hammock yakhe, bekangasimenzeli owamenza bona aphume kiwo ngokuthuka godu. Lokhu kokugcina bekungumthetho ongapheliko, ophilako nofana umphefumulo kuye; begodu ebuthongweni, ngokuba okwesikhatjhana uhlukaniswe nengqondo ehlukileko, ebeyisebenzisa ngezinye iinkhathi njengesithuthi sayo sangaphandle nofana i-ejenti, ngokuzenzakalelako yafuna ukubalekela ukutjhidelana okutjhisako kwento ehlanyako, okwathi, ngesikhatheso, bekungakabi yinto eqakathekileko. Kodwana njengombana ingqondo ingekho ngaphandle kobana ihlanganiswe nomphefumulo, ngalokho-ke kufanele bona, endabeni ka-Ahabi, anikela yoke imicabango yakhe neenkanuko zakhe emnqophweni wakhe ophakemeko; umnqopho loyo, ngokuzimisela kwawo, wazigandelela ukujamelana nabozimu namadimoni waba mhlobo wokuzicabangela, ukuzijamela. Awa, bekungaphila begodu kutjhiswe, lokha amandla ajayelekileko ebegade ahlanganiswe nawo, abaleka ngokuthuswa kuzalwa okungakabizwa nokungabi ngubaba. Ngalokho-ke, umoya ohlunguphaziweko owawuphuma emehlweni womzimba, lokha lokho okwakubonakala ngasuthi ngu-Ahabi aphuthuma ekamelweni lakhe, bekuyinto enganamuntu, isidalwa esingenabujamo esileleko, umsebe wokukhanya okuphilako, ukuqiniseka, kodwana ngaphandle kwento yokufaka umbala, begodu ngalokho bekuyinto engenalutho ngokwayo. ISAHLUKO 45. I-Affidavithi.

Ngokuya ngokuthi kungaba yini ukulandisa encwadini le; begodu, kwamambala, njengombana kuthinta ngokungakaqondi imininingwana eyodwa nofana emibili ethakazelisako nemangalisako emikhubeni yama-sperm whale, isahluko esidlulileko, engcenyeni yaso yokuthoma, siqakathekile njengombana sizokufumaneka emqulwini lo; kodwana indaba ephambili kiyo itlhoga bona ikhuliswe ngokuragela phambili, ukwenzela bona izwisiswe ngokwaneleko, begodu ngaphezu kwalokho kususwe nanyana ngikuphi ukungakholwa okungabangela ukungazi okudephileko kwayo yoke indaba kezinye iingqondo, malungana neqiniso lemvelo lamaphuzu aqakathekileko wendaba le.

Anginandaba nokwenza ingcenye yomsebenzi wami ngendlela efaneleko; kodwana uzokwaneliseka ngokukhiqiza umbono ofunekako ngokudzubhula izinto ezihlukileko, ezaziwa kimi njengomuntu wemikhomo; begodu kilokhu okucaphunako, ngiyakuthatha—isiphetho esihloselwe sizozilandela ngokwemvelo.

Kokuthoma: Mina ngokwami ​​ngiyazi izehlakalo ezintathu lapho umkhomo, ngemva kokuthola i-harpoon, ubaleke ngokupheleleko; begodu, ngemva kwesikhatjhana (kwesinye isikhathi seminyaka emithathu), sele atjhaywe ngesandla esifanako, begodu wabulawa; lokha iinsimbi ezimbili, zombili ezitshwaywe nge-cypher efanako, zithathwe emzimbeni. Esigabeni lapho iminyaka emithathu yangenelela khona hlangana nokuphoselwa kwama-harpoon amabili; begodu ngicabanga bona kungenzeka bekuyinto engaphezu kwalokho; indoda eyabatjhayela, ngesikhathi esithileko, ukuya emkhunjini wokurhweba ukuya e-Afrika, yaya lapho, yajoyina isiqhema sokutholwa, begodu yangena kude ngaphakathi, lapho yakhamba khona isikhathi esingaba minyaka emibili, kanengi isengozini ziinyoka, iinyamazana, iingwe, iimfesi ezinobuthi, nazo zoke ezinye izehlakalo zokuzulazula ehliziyweni iindawo ezingaziwako. Khatheso, umkhomo egade awutjhaye nawo bewusendleleni; ngokungangabazeki beyijikeleze iphasi loke kathathu, ibhulasha ngamahlangothi wayo woke amagu we-Afrika; kodwana kungasizi ngalitho. Indoda le nomkhomo baphinde bahlangana, begodu omunye wahlula omunye. Ngithi mina, ngokwami, ngazi izehlakalo ezintathu ezifana nalezi; lokho kutjho bona emabili wawo ngabona imikhomo itjhaywa; begodu, ekuhlaselweni kwesibili, wabona iinsimbi ezimbili ezinamatshwayo ahlukene kizo, ngemva kwalokho zathathwa efesini efileko. Eminyakeni emithathu, kwavela bona bengisesikepeni kokubili, kokuthoma nekokugcina, begodu kokugcina ngabona ngokucacileko umhlobo ongakajayeleki wemvukuzane ekulu ngaphasi kwelihlo lomkhomo, ebengiyibone lapho eminyakeni emithathu eyadlulako. Ngithi iminyaka emithathu, kodwana nginesiqiniseko sokobana bekungaphezu kwalokho. Nanzi izehlakalo ezintathu, ke, engikwaziko iqiniso ngazo; kodwana ngizwe ngezinye izehlakalo ezinengi ezivela ebantwini abanesizathu esihle sokumangalela iqiniso labo endabeni le.

Kwesibili: Kwaziwa khulu e-Sperm Whale Fishery, nanyana iphasi elisemaphethelweni lingakwazi kangangani, bona kube nezehlakalo ezinengi ezikhumbulekako zomlando lapho umkhomo othileko elwandle gade ubonakala khona eenkhathini ezikude neendawo ezithandekako.
