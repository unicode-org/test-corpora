ISAHLUKO 53. I-Gam.

Isizathu esibonakalako sokuthi kubayini u-Ahabi angazange angene emkhunjini ebesikhulume ngawo bekukuthi: umoya nelwandle bekutjho iinwuruwuru. Kodwana ngitjho nanyana lokhu bekungasinjalo, bekangasoze amkhwele—ngokwahlulela ngokuziphatha kwakhe okulandelako ngezikhathi ezifanako—nangabe bekunjalo, ngendlela yokubiza, bekafumene ipendulo engakalungi yombuzo awubuzileko. Ngombana, njengombana kwagcina kuvele, bekangakhathali ukuhlangana, ngitjho nemizuzu emihlanu, nanyana ngimuphi ukaputeni ongaziwako, ngaphandle kobana anganikela ngeminye imininingwana egade ayifuna khulu. Kodwana koke lokhu kungahlala kungalinganiselwa ngokwaneleko, ngathana bekungatjhiwo lapha ngokusetjenziswa okungakajayeleki kwemikhumbi yokudoba imikhomo lokha nayihlangana elwandle langaphandle, begodu khulukhulu endaweni ejayelekileko yokukhamba ngesikepe.

Nangabe abantu ababili abangabaziko bawela i-Pine Barrens e-New York State, nofana i-Salisbury Plain e-England; nangabe bahlangana ngokungakaqakatheki eenhlathini ezimbi kangaka, laba ababili, ngepilo yabo, angeze babalekela ukubingelelana; begodu ukuma isikhatjhana ukutjhugulula iindaba; begodu, mhlamunye, ukuhlala phasi isikhatjhana begodu uphumule ngokuvumelana: khona-ke, kungaba yinto engokwemvelo kangangani ukuthi phezu kwe-Pine Barrens engapheliko ne-Salisbury Plains yelwandle, imikhumbi emibili yokudoba imikhomo ehlaselana emaphethelweni wephasi—e-Fanning's Island, nofana i-King's Mills ekude; ngithi, kungokwemvelo kangangani bona ngaphasi kobujamo obunjalo imikhumbi le akukafaneli itjhugulule isichotho kwaphela, kodwana ibe nokuthintana okutjhideleneko, okunobungani nokuhlanganyela. Begodu khulukhulu lokhu kungabonakala njengento ejayelekileko, endabeni yemikhumbi ephethwe yitheku elilodwa, begodu abaphathi bayo, iimphathiswa, begodu ingasi ambalwa wamadoda aziwa mathupha; begodu ngokulandelako, ube nayo yoke imihlobo yezinto zasekhaya ezithandekako zokukhuluma ngazo.

Ngomkhumbi ongasekho isikhathi eside, umkhawulo wangaphandle, mhlamunye, uneencwadi emkhunjini; nanyana kukuphi, uzokuqiniseka bona umvumela bona abe namaphepha wedethi emnyakeni namkha emibili ngemva kokugcina emafayeleni wakhe angabonakaliko begodu afakwe isithupha. Begodu ngokubuyisela ngesizotha leso, umkhumbi oya ngaphandle uzokufumana ilwazi lakamuva lokudoba imikhomo elivela endaweni yokukhamba ngesikepe lapho bekungaba khona, into eqakatheke khulu kuye. Begodu ngezinga, koke lokhu kuzokuba liqiniso malungana nemikhumbi yokudoba imikhomo ewela umzila womunye nomunye endaweni yokuvakatjha ngokwayo, nanoma sele ingekho ekhaya isikhathi eside. Ngombana omunye wabo kungenzeka bona wathola ukudluliselwa kweencwadi ezivela komunye umkhumbi wesithathu, begodu nje ukude; begodu ezinye zeencwadi lezo zingaba ngezabantu bomkhumbi ahlangana nawo nje. Ngaphandle kwalokho, bebazokukhulumisana ngeendaba zokudoba imikhomo, begodu babe nengcoco evumelanako. Ngombana bebangeze bahlangabezana nakho koke ukuzwelana kwamatilosi kwaphela, kodwana ngokufanako nakho koke ukuthandwa okungakajayeleki okuvela ekuphishekeleni okufanako nokuhlupheka nobungozi ababelana ngabo bobabili.

Begodu umehluko wenarha bewungekhe wenze umehluko oqakatheke khulu; okutjho bona, inqobo nje nangabe bobabili bakhuluma ilimi linye, njengombana kunjalo ngamaMelika nesiNgisi. Nanyana kunjalo, ukuqiniseka, ukusuka enanini elincani labadobi bemikhomo be-England, imihlangano enjalo ayikavami ukwenzeka, begodu lokha nayenzekako kungenzeka bona kube namahloni hlangana nabo; ngombana isiNgisi sakho sizifihlile, begodu isiNgisi sakho, akathandi into enjalo kibo boke abantu ngaphandle kwakhe. Ngaphandle kwalokho, abadobi bemikhomo be-England kesinye isikhathi bathinta uhlobo lokuphakama kwedorobha elikhulu phezu kwabadobi bemikhomo be-America; malungana no-Nantucketer omude, onciphileko, ngokuziphatha kwakhe okungakacaci, njengomlimi welwandle. Kodwana lapho ukuphakama kwama-whalemen wamaNgisi kukhona khona, kungaba budisi ukutjho, ngokubona bona ama-Yankees ngelanga elilodwa, ngokuhlanganyela, abulala imikhomo eminengi ukudlula woke amaNgisi, ngokuhlanganyela, eminyakeni elitjhumi. Kodwana lokhu kubuthakathaka obuncani obunganabungozi ebantwini abazingeli bemikhomo be-England, okuyinto i-Nantucketer engayithathi kangako; mhlamunye, ngombana uyazi bona unabo ubuthakathaka obumbalwa.

Njeke-ke, siyabona bona kiyo yoke imikhumbi ekhamba ngokuhlukana elwandle, abadobi bemikhomo baneenzathu ezinengi zokuba nobungani—begodu banjalo. Nanyana, eminye imikhumbi yezokurhweba ewela phakathi kwe-Atlantic, kanengi izokudlula ngaphandle kwegama elilodwa lokuvuma, isika omunye nomunye elwandle oluphakemeko, njengesiqhema sama-dandies e-Broadway; begodu ngaso soke isikhathi bazitika, mhlamunye, ngokugxeka okupheleleko phezu kwe-rig yomunye nomunye. Malungana nama-Men-of-War, lokha nabahlangana elwandle, bathoma ngokudlula emtjhini wokukhothama nokukhuhla, ukufihla amaflegi, kangangobana kungabonakali ngasuthi kunothando oluhle nothando lobuzalwane ngakho. Njengombana imikhumbi yeengqila ihlangana, kubayini, iphuthuma khulu, ibalekelana msinyana. Begodu malungana nama-Pirates, lokha nabathola ithuba lokuwela amathambo womunye nomunye, isichotho sokuthoma sithi—“Zingaki amakhakhasi?”—ngendlela efanako naleyo abadosi bemikhomo abathi—“Zingaki imiphongolo?” Begodu umbuzo loyo nasele uphendulwe, amapirate ayahlukana msinyana, ngombana aziindlhayela ezimbi emahlangothini womabili, begodu azithandi ukubona ukufana okukhulu komunye nomunye.

Kodwana qala umdobi wemikhomo ohlonipha uZimu, othembekileko, ongazikhakhazisiko, onokwamukela iinvakatjhi, othanda ukuhlanganyela nabantu, okhululekileko nolula! Une-“ Gam ,” into engaziwa kiyo yoke eminye imikhumbi kangangobana azange kheyizwe ngebizo; begodu nangabe ngengozi bezwa ngakho, bayamomotheka kwaphela, begodu babuyelele izinto ezihlekisako "ngama-spouters" kanye "nama-blubber-boilers," nokunye okufana nokubabaza okuhle. Kubayini boke abathengisi bemikhumbi, begodu nabo boke amadoda wePirates neMan-of-War, kanye namatilosi wemikhumbi yezigqila, bathanda umuzwa onjalo wokudelela imikhumbi ye-Whale; umbuzo lo kungaba budisi ukuwuphendula. Ngombana, endabeni yamapirate, sithi, ngingathanda ukwazi bona umsebenzi wabo unephazimulo engakajayeleki ngawo. Kwesinye isikhathi iphela ngokuphakama okungakajayeleki, kwamambala; kodwana kwaphela esigxotsheni. Begodu ngaphandle kwalokho, lokha umuntu nakaphakanyiswa ngendlela engakajayeleki, akanasisekelo esifaneleko sokuphakama kwakhe. Ngalokho-ke, ngiphetha ngokuthi, ngokuzikhakhazisa ngokuthi uphakanyiswe phezulu kune-whaleman, kilokho kugomela umphangi akanasisekelo esiqinileko sokuma kiso.

Ngalokho-ke ugoqwe ngokupheleleko ngaphambili nangemuva, begodu angazivulela eceleni ngokuhlala phasi emilenzeni yakhe elula; kodwana ukutjhinga kwesikepe okungakalindeleki, okunolaka kuzokuvame ukuya kude ukumwisa, ngombana ubude besisekelo abusililitho ngaphandle kobubanzi obufanako. Yenza i-engele yokurhatjheka yamapali amabili, begodu angeze wakwazi ukuwajamela.
