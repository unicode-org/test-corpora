As a de waka go, a bin de tink gud wan; wetin bin dɔn apin to mi fɔ Kpɛntin Eab, ful mi wit wan patikyula wayl vagueness of painfulness concerning am. Ɛn sɔm kayn we, da tɛm de, a bin fil sɔri fɔ am ɛn sɔri fɔ am, bɔt fɔ a nɔ no wetin, pas nɔmɔ na di kruk we i lɔs in leg. Ɛn pan ɔl dat, a bin fil bak wan strenj fred fɔ am; bɔt da kayn fred de, we a nɔ ebul fɔ tɔk bɔt atɔl, nɔ bin rili fred; A nɔ no wetin i bin bi. Bɔt a bin fil am; ɛn i nɔ bin mek a nɔ want fɔ tɔk to am; pan ɔl we a nɔ bin gɛt peshɛnt fɔ wetin bin tan lɛk sikrit insay am, so i nɔ pafɛkt lɛk aw a bin sabi am da tɛm de. Bɔt, afta lɔng tɛm, dɛn bin kɛr mi tinkin go ɔda say dɛn, so dat fɔ di dak daknɛs naw Eab bin slip mi maynd.





CHAPTER 17. Di Ramadan.

As Queequeg in Ramadan, ɔ Fasting and Humiliation, bin fɔ kɔntinyu ɔl di de, a nɔ bin disayd fɔ distɔb am te towards nait-foll; bikɔs a valyu di big big rɛspɛkt fɔ ɔlman in rilijɔn ɔbligayshɔn, a nɔ ɛva tink bɔt aw i kɔmik, ɛn a nɔ bin ebul fɔ si am na mi at fɔ ɔndavalyu ivin wan kɔngrigeshɔn we gɛt ant dɛn we de wɔship tod-stɔl; ɔ dɛn ɔda tin dɛn we de na sɔm pat dɛn na wi wɔl, we wit wan digri fɔ futman we nɔ ɛva bi na ɔda planɛt dɛn, de butu bifo di bɔdi fɔ wan pɔsin we dɔn day we gɛt land jɔs bikɔs ɔf di bɔku bɔku prɔpati dɛn we dɛn stil gɛt ɛn rɛnt insay in nem.

A se, wi gud Presbyterian Kristian dɛn fɔ bi charitable pan dɛn tin ya, ɛn nɔ fansi wisɛf so vastly superior to ɔda mɔtalman, pegan ɛn wetin nɔto, bikɔs ɔf dɛn af-af krayzi conceits pan dɛn tɔpik ya. Kwik bin de, naw, we rili de ɛnjɔy di tin dɛn we nɔ mek sɛns pas ɔl bɔt Yojo ɛn in Ramadan;—bɔt wetin bɔt dat? Kwik bin tink se i no wetin i de tɔk bɔt, a tink se; i bin tan lɛk se i satisfay; ɛn na de lɛ i rɛst. Ɔl di agyumɛnt we wi bin de agyu wit am nɔ bin fɔ ɛp; lɛ i bi, a se: ɛn Ɛvin sɔri fɔ wi ɔl—Prɛsbitirian ɛn Pegan dɛn ɔl—bikɔs wi ɔl sɔm kayn we de krak bad bad wan bɔt di ed, ɛn i sɔri fɔ no se wi nid fɔ mek wi ed.

Tuws ivintɛm, we a fil se a dɔn shɔ se ɔl in pɔfɔmɛnshɔn ɛn ritual dɛn fɔ dɔn, a go ɔp na in rum ɛn nak na di domɔt; bɔt nɔto ansa. A tray fɔ opin am, bɔt dɛn bin tay am insay. “Kwik,” a se saful wan tru di ki-ol:—ɔl dɛn nɔ tɔk natin. “A se, Kwik! wetin mek yu nɔ de tɔk? Na mi—Ishmayl.” Bɔt ɔltin bin stil de lɛk aw dɛn bin de du trade. A bigin fɔ fred bad bad wan. A bin dɔn alaw am fɔ gɛt da kayn bɔku tɛm de; A bin tink se sɔntɛm i bin gɛt wan sik we dɛn kɔl apoplectic fit. A luk tru di ki-ol; bɔt di domɔt we opin insay wan ɔda kɔna na di rum, di ki-ol we dɛn bin de op fɔ gɛt na bin jɔs wan kruk ɛn bad bad wan. A bin jɔs ebul fɔ si wan pat pan di fut-bɔd na di bed ɛn wan layn na di wɔl, bɔt natin nɔ bin de pas dat. A bin sɔprayz fɔ si di wud shaft fɔ Kwik in harpun, we di uman we gɛt di os di ivintɛm bifo bin tek am frɔm am, de rɛst pan di wɔl, bifo wi go ɔp di rum. Dat na strenj tin, a bin tink; bɔt pan ɔl we i bi, bikɔs di harpun tinap de, ɛn i nɔ kin go ɔ i nɔ kin ɛva go ɔda kɔntri if i nɔ gɛt am, na dat mek i fɔ de insay ya, ɛn i nɔ fɔ mek mistek.

“Kwik!—Kwik!”—ɔl dis stil. Sɔntin go dɔn apin. Apopleksi we pɔsin kin gɛt! A tray fɔ bɔs opin di domɔt; bɔt i bin trangayes fɔ tinap tranga wan. We a rɔn go dɔŋ di stej, a tɛl di fɔs pɔsin we a mit kwik kwik wan wetin a de tink, dat na di uman we de wok na di chamba. “La!la!” i kray se, "A bin tink se sɔntin fɔ bi di mata. A go mek di bed afta brɛf, ɛn dɛn lɔk di domɔt; ɛn nɔto maws fɔ yɛri; ɛn i jɔs so kwayɛt frɔm da tɛm de. Bɔt a bin tink se, sɔntɛm, una ɔl tu dɔn go ɔf ɛn lɔk una bag insay fɔ mek dɛn kip am sef wan. La! la, ma'am!—Mistress! murder! Misis Hussey! apoplexy!”—ɛn wit dɛn kray ya, i rɔn go na di kichin, a de fala am.

I nɔ tu te, Misis Husi kam, wit wan mɔstad-pɔt na wan an ɛn wan vinega-krut na di ɔda an, i jɔs dɔn brok away frɔm di wok we i bin de du fɔ atɛnd di kastɔ dɛn, ɛn i bin de kɔl in smɔl blak bɔbɔ da tɛm de.

“Wud-os!” a kray, “us we fɔ go de? Rɔn fɔ Gɔd in sek, ɛn kam wit sɔntin fɔ pry opin di domɔt—di aks!—di aks! i dɔn gɛt strok; dipen pan am!”—ɛn so a bin de se a bin de rɔsh ɔp stej bak ɛmti-an we nɔ gɛt wan we, we Misis Hussey put di mɔstad-pɔt ɛn vinega-kruet insay, ɛn di wan ol kastɔ we i gɛt fes-to-fes.

“Wetin na di prɔblɛm wit yu, yɔŋ man?”

“Gɛt di aks! Fɔ Gɔd in sek, rɔn fɔ di dɔktɔ, sɔm wan, we a de pry am opin!”

Di uman we gɛt di os se: “Luk ya,” ɛn i put di vinega-krut dɔŋ kwik kwik wan, so dat i go gɛt wan an fri; “luk ya, yu de tɔk bɔt fɔ opin ɛni wan pan mi domɔt dɛn?”—ɛn wit dat i ol mi an. “Wetin na di prɔblɛm wit yu?Wetin na di prɔblɛm wit yu, shipmɛt?”

A bin gi am fɔ ɔndastand di wan ol kes, bɔt a bin de du am kwik kwik wan. I bin de klap di vinega-kru na wan say na in nos we i nɔ bin no, ɛn i bin de luk fɔ smɔl tɛm; dɔn i ala—“Nɔ!, a nɔ si am frɔm we a put am de.” We i rɔn go na wan smɔl kloz ɔnda di landin na di stej, i glan insay, ɛn kam bak, tɛl mi se Kwik in hapun nɔ de. I kray se: “I dɔn kil insɛf.” "I nɔ fayn fɔ mek Stiggs du am bak—ɔda kɔntrapen de go—Gɔd sɔri fɔ in po mama!—i go bi di ruin fɔ mi os. Di po bɔbɔ gɛt sista? Usay da gyal de?—de, Bɛtɛl, go to Snarles di Peinta, ɛn tɛl am fɔ peint mi wan sayn, wit—“nɔ alaw pɔsin fɔ kil insɛf ya, ɛn nɔ fɔ smok na di parlor;”—might as well kill both birds at once. Kil? Di Masta sɔri fɔ in gɔst! Wetin na da nɔys de de? Yu, yɔŋ man, avast de!”

Ɛn we i rɔn go afta mi, i kech mi as a de tray bak fɔ fos fɔ opin di domɔt.

"A nɔ alaw am; a nɔ go mek mi ples pwɛl. Go fɔ di pɔsin we de mek lɔk, wan de we de lɛk wan mayl frɔm ya. Bɔt avast!” put in an na in sayd-pɔkit, “na wan ki we go fit, a tink se; lɛ wi si.” Ɛn wit dat, i tɔn am na di lɔk; bɔt, alas! Queequeg in supliment bolt bin stil de we dɛn nɔ pul am insay.

“Have to burst it open,” a se, ɛn a bin de rɔn dɔŋ di ɛntrɛ smɔl, fɔ wan gud stat, we di uman we gɛt di os kech mi, bak i prɔmis se a nɔ fɔ brok in ples; bɔt a chɛr kɔmɔt pan am, ɛn wit wan bɔdi rɔsh wantɛm wantɛm, a rɔsh misɛf ful wan agens di mak.

Wit wan prodigious nois di doa flay opin, en di knob slam pan di wol, sen di plasta to di siling; ɛn de, gud ɛvin! na de Kwik bin sidɔm, ɔltogɛda kol ɛn insɛf bin gɛda; rayt na di midul pat na di rum; skwatin pan in ham, ɛn ol Yojo pan tap in ed. I nɔ bin luk wan we ɔ di ɔda we, bɔt i bin sidɔm lɛk imej we dɛn kɔt wit smɔl sayn fɔ aktif layf.

“Kwik,” a se, ɛn go nia am, “Kwik, wetin na di prɔblɛm wit yu?”

“I nɔ bin sidɔm so ɔl di de, nɔto so?” na so di pɔsin we gɛt di os bin tɔk.

Bɔt ɔl wetin wi bin tɔk, wi nɔ bin ebul fɔ drɛg wan wɔd kɔmɔt pan am; I bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ a fil fɔ push am ova, so dat a go chenj in pozishɔn, bikɔs i bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ a nɔ ebul fɔ bia, i bin tan lɛk se i de mek i fil pen ɛn i nɔ bin de mek i nɔ ebul fɔ du am; espeshali, as in ol probabiliti i bin don sidon so fo opwards of et or ten awa, go tu witout in regular it.

A se, “Misis Hussey, i de alayv pan ɔl di tin dɛn we apin, so lɛf wi, if yu want, ɛn a go si dis strenj afɛkt misɛf.”

We a lɔk di domɔt pan di pɔsin we gɛt di os, a tray tranga wan fɔ win Kwik fɔ tek chia; bɔt na fɔ natin. Na de i sidɔm; ɛn ɔl wetin i bin ebul fɔ du—fɔ ɔl mi pɔlitiks art ɛn blandishmɛnt dɛn—i nɔ bin de muv wan peg, ɔ tɔk wan wɔd, ɔ ivin luk mi, ɔ notis mi prezɛns pan smɔl we.

A bin de wɔnda, a bin tink, if dis kin pɔsibul fɔ bi pat pan in Ramadan; du dem fast pan dem ham dat we na in yon ayland. I fɔ bi so; yes, na pat pan in kried, a tink se; wɛl, so, lɛ i rɛst; i go grap kwik ɔ leta, i nɔ gɛt wan dawt. I nɔ go ebul fɔ las sote go, tɛl Gɔd tɛnki, ɛn in Ramadan kin jɔs kam wan tɛm insay di ia; ɛn a nɔ biliv se i rili de na di rayt tɛm da tɛm de.

A bin go dɔŋ fɔ it ivintɛm it. Afta we dɛn sidɔm fɔ lɔng tɛm de lisin to di lɔng stori dɛn bɔt sɔm selɔ dɛn we jɔs kɔmɔt frɔm wan plen-pudding voyage, as dɛn kɔl am (dat na, shɔt wɛl-voyage insay skun ɔ brig, we dɛn kɔnfyus na di nɔt pat na di layn, na di Atlantik Oshɔn nɔmɔ); afta a lisin to dɛn plum-puddingers ya te klos ilevin oklɔk, a go ɔp stej fɔ go slip, a fil rili shɔ bay dis tɛm Kwik go mɔs dɔn briŋ in Ramadan to tɛmnɛshɔn. Bɔt nɔto so; na de i bin de jɔs usay a bin dɔn lɛf am; i nɔ bin dɔn stɔp wan inch. A bigin fɔ vɛks pan am; i bin tan lɛk se i nɔ gɛt sɛns ɛn i kray fɔ sidɔm de ɔl di de ɛn af nɛt pan in ham dɛn na kol rum, ɛn ol wan wud na in ed.

"Fɔ ɛvin in sek, Kwik, grap ɛn shek yusɛf; grap ɛn it sɔm it. Yu go angri; yu go kil yusɛf, Kwik." Bɔt nɔto wan wɔd i ansa.

So bikɔs a nɔ bin gɛt op igen, a bin disayd fɔ go slip ɛn slip; ɛn wi nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se, bifo lɔng tɛm, i go fala mi. Bɔt bifo a tɔn insay, a tek mi ebi bea skin jakit, ɛn trowe am oba am, as i prɔmis fɔ bi wan nɛt we rili kol; ɛn i nɔ bin gɛt natin pas in ɔdinari rawnd jakit. Fɔ sɔm tɛm, du ɔl wetin a go du, a nɔ bin ebul fɔ go insay di faintest doze. A bin dɔn blo di kandul; ɛn di jɔs tink bɔt Kwik—we nɔ de fa fɔ 4 fit—we sidɔm de na da say de we nɔ izi fɔ du, we i de in wan na di kol ɛn dak; dis bin mek a rili sɔri. Tink bɔt dis; slip ɔl nɛt na di sem rum wit wan big wek pegan pan in ham dɛn insay dis dreary, unaccountable Ramadan!

Bɔt sɔm kayn we a bin drɔp las las, ɛn a nɔ bin no natin mɔ te di de kɔmɔt; we, we i de luk oba di bedsayd, na de Kwik bin skwatin, lɛk se dɛn dɔn skru am dɔŋ na grɔn. Bɔt jɔs lɛk aw di fɔs tin we i si di san go insay di winda, i go ɔp, wit jɔyn dɛn we stif ɛn we de grit, bɔt wit gladi gladi luk; limped towards mi usay a ledɔm; pres in fɔɛd bak pan mi yon; ɛn se in Ramadan dɔn dɔn.

Naw, as a bin don hint bifo, a no get eni objekshon to eni pesin rilijon, i bi wetin e bi, so long as dat pesin no kil o insul eni oda pesin, bikos dat oda pesin no biliv am tu. Bɔt we man in rilijɔn kin rili fred; we i de mek i sɔfa fayn fayn wan; ɛn, fɔ dɔn, i de mek dis wɔl we wi gɛt bi ples fɔ slip we nɔ fayn fɔ slip insay; dɔn a tink se na ay tɛm fɔ tek da pɔsin de na sayd ɛn agyu di pɔynt wit am.

En jis so I naw du wit Queequeg. A se: “Kwik, go na bed naw, ɛn lay ɛn lisin to mi.” Dɔn a go bifo, bigin wit di rayz ɛn prɔgrɛs we di fɔstɛm rilijɔn dɛn de rayz ɛn prɔgrɛs, ɛn kam dɔŋ to di difrɛn rilijɔn dɛn we de naw, insay da tɛm de a bin wok tranga wan fɔ sho Kwik se ɔl dɛn Lɛnt, Ramadan, ɛn ham-skwatin fɔ lɔng tɛm na kol, rum dɛn we nɔ gɛt gladi at na bin stark nɔsɛns; bad fɔ di wɛlbɔdi biznɛs; yuslɛs fɔ di sol; agens, in shot, to di klia lɔ dɛn fɔ Hajin ɛn kɔmɔn sɛns. A tɛl am bak se bikɔs i bi insay ɔda tin dɛn so wan ekstrim sɛns ɛn sagacious savage, i bin de pen mi, rili bad fɔ mi, fɔ si am naw so deplorably fulish bɔt dis ridikul Ramadan we i gɛt. Apat frɔm dat, a bin agyu se, fast de mek di bɔdi kev insay; na dat mek di spirit de kev insay; ɛn ɔl di tin dɛn we pɔsin kin tink bɔt we pɔsin fast, fɔ rili angri af-af. Dis na di rizin we mek bɔku pan di rilijɔn dɛn we nɔ gɛt bɛtɛ trɛnk kin valyu dɛn kayn pwɛl at we dɛn de tink bɔt dɛn layf afta dɛn dɔn day. Insay wan wɔd, Kwik, a se, rather digressively; ɛl na aidia we dɛn bɔn fɔs pan apul-dɔmpling we dɛn nɔ dayjɛst; ɛn frɔm da tɛm de, i dɔn kɔntinyu fɔ de tru di ɛriditi dispɛpsia we Ramadan dɛn bin de mɛn.

Dɔn a aks Kwik if insɛf bin ɛva gɛt prɔblɛm wit dispepsia; fɔ tɔk di aidia klia wan, so dat i go tek am insay.I se nɔ; na wan tɛm nɔmɔ we wi nɔ go ɛva fɔgɛt. Na afta wan big pati we in papa di kiŋ bin gi, we i gɛt wan big fɛt usay dɛn bin dɔn kil fifti pan di ɛnimi dɛn bay lɛk tu oklɔk aftanun, ɛn dɛn ɔl kuk ɛn it da sem ivintɛm de.

A bin de shek shek se: “Nɔ igen, Kwik; “dat go du;” bikɔs a bin no di infɔmeshɔn dɛn we i nɔ bin de hint dɛn mɔ. A bin dɔn si wan man we de wok na di bot we bin dɔn go na da sem ayland de, ɛn i tɛl mi se na di kɔstɔm, we dɛn dɔn gɛt big fɛt de, fɔ barbecue ɔl di wan dɛn we dɛn kil na di yad ɔ gadin fɔ di pɔsin we win; ɛn afta dat, wan bay wan, dɛn kin put dɛn na big big wud trencha dɛn, ɛn mek dɛn fayn rawnd lɛk pilau, wit bredfrut ɛn kokonat; ɛn wit sɔm pasli na dɛn mɔt, dɛn bin de sɛn dɛn rawnd wit di wan we win in kɔmplimɛnt to ɔl in padi dɛn, jɔs lɛk se dɛn prɛzɛnt ya na bɔku bɔku Krismas tik.

Afta ɔl, a nɔ tink se di tin dɛn we a bin tɔk bɔt rilijɔn bin mek pipul dɛn rili kɔle Kwik. Bikɔs, fɔs, i bin tan lɛk se i nɔ de yɛri bɔt da impɔtant tɔpik de sɔm kayn we, pas nɔmɔ dɛn tink bɔt am frɔm in yon we; ɛn, insay di sɛkɔn ples, i nɔ bin ɔndastand mi pas wan pat pan tri pat, i bin kɔch mi aydia dɛn jɔs lɛk aw a go du; ɛn, fɔ dɔn, i go mɔs bi se i bin tink se i no bɔku tin bɔt di tru rilijɔn pas mi. I bin de luk mi wit wan kayn kɔndɛm ɛn sɔri-at, lɛk se i tink se i rili sɔri fɔ mek dis kayn yɔŋ man we gɛt sɛns lɔs so oplɛs to ivanjelikal pegan piety.

Las las wi grap ɛn drɛs; ɛn Queequeg, tek wan prodigiously hearty breakfast of chowders of all sorts, so dat di landlady nɔ go mek bɔku prɔfit bikɔs ɔf in Ramadan, wi sallied out fɔ go insay di Pequod, sauntering along, ɛn pik wi tit wit halibut bon.





CHAPTER 18. In Mak.

As wi bin de waka dɔŋ di ɛnd pan di wɔf go na di ship, Kwik we de kɛr in harpun, Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg wit in grɔf vɔys bin de prez wi lawd wan frɔm in wigwam, se i nɔ bin saspek se mi padi na pɔsin we de it pipul dɛn, ɛn mɔ i bin de anɔys se i nɔ mek ɛni kanibal kam insay da kraft de, pas nɔmɔ dɛn bin dɔn prodyuz dɛn pepa dɛn bifo.

“Wetin yu min wit dat, Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg?” a se, naw a de jomp pan di bulwarks, ɛn lɛf mi kɔmpin tinap na di wɔf.

I ansa se: “A min se i fɔ sho in pepa dɛn.”

“Yɛs,” Kpɛntin Bildad se wit in olo vɔys, i stik in ed frɔm biɛn Pɛlɛg in yon, kɔmɔt na di wigwam. I tɔn to Kwik, i tɔk bak se: “I fɔ sho se i dɔn chenj. Daknɛs pikin, yu de kɔmyuniɔn wit ɛni Kristian chɔch naw?”

A se: “Wetin mek, na mɛmba na di fɔs Kɔngrigeshɔn Chɔch.” Na ya dɛn se, bɔku savayj dɛn we gɛt tatu we de sel na Nantucket ship dɛn kin kam las fɔ chenj to di chɔch dɛn.

Bildad kray se: “Fɔs Kɔngrigeshɔn Chɔch, wetin! dat de wɔship na Dikon Ditarɔnɔmi Kolman in mitin os?” ɛn so i de se, i pul in spɛktaklɔs, i rɔb dɛn wit in big yɔlɔ bandana hankɛf, ɛn put dɛn fayn fayn wan, kɔmɔt na di wigwam, ɛn ledɔm stif wan oba di bulwɔk dɛn, tek wan gud lɔng luk pan Kwik.

“Aw lɔng i dɔn bi mɛmba?” i den se, i tɔn to mi; “nɔto tu lɔng, a lɛk fɔ gɛs, yɔŋ man.”

Pɛlɛg se: “Nɔ, ɛn i nɔ baptayz rayt bak, ɔ i bin fɔ dɔn was sɔm pan da dɛbul in blu kɔmɔt na in fes.”

Bildad kray se: “Naw, dis Filistin na mɛmba ɔltɛm na Dikon Ditarɔnɔmi in mitin? A nɔ ɛva si am de go de, ɛn a de pas am ɛvri Masta in de.”

A se: “A nɔ no natin bɔt Dikon Ditarɔnɔmi ɔ in mitin; "ɔl wetin a no na, se Kwik we de ya na mɛmba we dɛn bɔn na di Fɔs Kɔngrigeshɔn Chɔch. Insɛf na dikon, Kwik na."

I bin dɔn nia siks oklɔk, bɔt na grey imperfect misty dawn nɔmɔ, we wi kam nia di wɛf.

“Avast!” kray wan vɔys, we in ɔna di sem tɛm kam nia wi, le in an pan wi tu sholda, ɛn afta dat i insinuate insɛf bitwin wi, tinap fɔ butu bifo smɔl, insay di twilayt we nɔ shɔ, strenj wan de luk frɔm Kwik to mi. Na Ilayja bin de.

A se, “I dɔn krak, Kwik, kam.”
