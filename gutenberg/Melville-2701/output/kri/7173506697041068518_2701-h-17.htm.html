*Pan ɔl we ɔl kɔmpiashɔn na di we aw jenɛral bɔlk de bitwin di wɛl ɛn di ɛlifant na tin we nɔ mek sɛns, bikɔs insay da patikyula de di ɛlifant tinap insay bɔku di sem rɛspɛkt fɔ di wɛl we dɔg de du to di ɛlifant; pan ɔl dat, nɔto sɔm pɔynt dɛn de we pɔsin kin want fɔ no bɔt we fiba; wan pan dɛn tin ya na di spɔt. Di mɔ we a de tink bɔt dis pawaful tel, na di mɔ a de sɔri fɔ we a nɔ ebul fɔ tɔk am. Sɔntɛnde, dɛn kin mek sayn dɛn insay de, we pan ɔl we dɛn go mek mɔtalman in an fayn, dɛn nɔ kin ebul fɔ ɛksplen am atɔl. Insay wan big grup, we so wɔndaful, wan wan tɛm, dɛn mistik jes ya de, dat a dɔn yɛri ɔntinman dɛn we dɔn deklare se dɛn tan lɛk Fri-Mason sayn ɛn simbul dɛn; dat di wɛl, fɔ tru, bay dɛn we ya bin tɔk wit di wɔl wit sɛns. Nɔto bak fɔ want ɔda muvmɛnt dɛn fɔ di wɛl na in jenɛral bɔdi, we ful-ɔp wit strenj tin dɛn, ɛn we nɔ go ebul fɔ ansa to di pɔsin we gɛt ɛkspiriɛns pas ɔlman fɔ atak am. Dissect am aw a kin, den, a bɔt go skin dip; A nɔ no am, ɛn a nɔ go ɛva no am. Bɔt if a nɔ ivin no di tel fɔ dis wɛl, aw a go ɔndastand in ed? bɔku mɔ, aw fɔ ɔndastand in fes, we fes i nɔ gɛt? Yu go si mi bak pat, mi tel, i tan lɛk se i de se, bɔt dɛn nɔ go si mi fes. Bɔt a nɔ ebul fɔ no in bak pat dɛn kpatakpata; ɛn hint wetin i go du bɔt in fes, a se bak i nɔ gɛt fes.





CHAPTER 87. Di Grɛnd Armada.

Di lɔng ɛn smɔl peninsula na Malaka, we de go na di sawt-ist frɔm di say dɛn we dɛn kɔl Bama, na in de mek di say we de na di sawt pas ɔl di ɔda say dɛn na Eshia. Insay wan layn we de kɔntinyu frɔm da peninsula de, di lɔng ayland dɛn we nem Sumatra, Java, Bali, ɛn Timor de; we, wit bɔku ɔda wan dɛn, de mek wan big big mol, ɔ rampart, we de kɔnɛkt Eshia wit Ɔstrelia fɔ lɔng tɛm, ɛn sheb di Indian si we nɔ brok fɔ lɔng tɛm frɔm di tik tik tik stɔd dɛn na di oriental akipelago dɛn. Dis rampart gɛt bɔku sally-port dɛn fɔ mek i izi fɔ ship ɛn wɛl dɛn; we pipul dɛn kin si pan dɛn na di strayt dɛn na Sunda ɛn Malaka. Bay di strayt dɛm na Sunda, mɔ, bot dɛm we de go na Chaina frɔm di wɛst, de kɔmɔt na di Chaina si dɛm.

Dɛn smɔl smɔl strayt dɛn de na Sunda de sheb Sumatra frɔm Java; ɛn tinap midul na da big big wɔl we gɛt ayland dɛn, we da bold grin mawnten de we di siman dɛn sabi as Java Hɛd, bin de sɔpɔt am; dɛn nɔ kɔrɛkt smɔl to di sɛntral get we de opin insay sɔm big big ɛmpaya we gɛt wɔl: ɛn we yu tink bɔt di bɔku bɔku spays, ɛn silk, ɛn jɔlɔs, ɛn gold, ɛn ayvri, we di tawzin ayland dɛn na da oriental si de gɛt mɔni wit, i tan lɛk se na impɔtant prɔvishɔn fɔ di nature, se dɛn kayn trɔs dɛn de, bay di we aw dɛn mek di land, at ɔl fɔ bia di apinɛns, bɔt ineffectual, fɔ bi gayd frɔm di wɛst wɔl we de ɔndastand ɔltin. Di shore dɛn na di Straits of Sunda nɔ gɛt dɛn tin dɛn de we gɛt pawaful fɔt dɛn we de gayd di say dɛn we dɛn kin go insay di Mɛditarenian, di Baltik, ɛn di Propontis. Dɛn nɔ tan lɛk di pipul dɛn na Denmak, dɛn pipul ya we kɔmɔt na di Ist nɔ de aks fɔ di obsequious homage of lowered top-sails frɔm di endless procession of ships bifo di briz, we fɔ lɔng lɔng tɛm pas, na nɛt ɛn na de, dɔn pas bitwin di ayland dɛn na Sumatra ɛn Java, we dɛn kin kɛr wit di kago dɛn we dia pas ɔl na di ist. Bɔt pan ɔl we dɛn kin fri fɔ lɛf fɔ du sɛrimɔni lɛk dis, dɛn nɔ kin lɛf fɔ du wetin dɛn se fɔ pe mɔ strɔng tribut.

Taym aut of maind di piratical proas of di Malays, lurking bitwin di low shaded coves and islets of Sumatra, don sallied out pan di vessels seiling through di straits, faya faya de aks tribute at di point of their spiars. Pan ɔl we bay di blɔd kɔshɔ we dɛn dɔn gɛt bɔku bɔku tɛm na di an dɛn fɔ di Yuropian kruza dɛn, di audacity fɔ dɛn kɔsa dɛn ya dɔn stɔp smɔl; bɔt stil, ivin naw, wan wan tɛm wi kin yɛri bɔt Inglish ɛn Amɛrikan bot dɛn, we, insay dɛn wata dɛn de, dɛn dɔn put dɛn ɛn tif dɛn ɛn nɔ rigrɛt.

Wit wan fayn, fresh briz, di Pequod bin de kam nia dis straits naw; Eab bin want fɔ pas tru dɛn go insay di Javan si, ɛn frɔm de, i de krɔs go na di nɔt, oba wata we dɛn no se di Spɛm Wɛl kin go bɔku tɛm ya ɛn de, i swip in sho na di Filipin Ayland dɛn, ɛn gɛt di fa kɔst na Jepan, insay di tɛm fɔ di big sizin fɔ kech wɛl de. Bay dɛn we ya, di Pequod we de rawnd go swip ɔlmost ɔl di ples dɛn we dɛn sabi fɔ kruz Spɛm Wɛl na di wɔl, bifo i kam dɔŋ pan di Layn na di Pasifik; usay Eab, pan ɔl we ɔl ɔda say dɛn nɔ bin ebul fɔ rɔnata am, i bin kɔnt tranga wan fɔ gi fɛt to Mobi Dik, na di si we dɛn bin sabi am fɔ go bɔku tɛm; ɛn na sizin we dɛn go tink se i de mɔna am.

Bɔt aw naw? insay dis zoned quest, Eab nɔ tɔch ɛni land? in kru de drink briz? Fɔ tru, i go stɔp fɔ gɛt wata. Nay. Fɔ lɔng tɛm, naw, di san we de rɔn na di sɛrks dɔn de rɔn insay in faya ring, ɛn i nɔ nid ɛnitin fɔ it pas wetin de insay insɛf. So Eab. Mak dis, tu, insay di wɛl. Wail ɔda hul dɛn kin lod dɔŋ wit alien tin dɛn, fɔ transfa dɛn to fɔrina wɔv dɛn; di wɛl-ship we de waka waka na di wɔl nɔ de kɛr kago pas insɛf ɛn di wan dɛn we de wok de, dɛn wɛpɔn dɛn ɛn wetin dɛn want. I gɛt wan ol lek insay bɔtul insay in bɔku bɔku ol. I de balast wit yutiliti dɛn; nɔto ɔltogɛda wit pig-lid ɛn kentledge we yu nɔ go yuz. I kin kɛr wata we dɔn de fɔ lɔng tɛm insay am. Klia ol praymɛnt Nantucket wata; we, we tri ia de flot, di Nantucketer, na di Pasifik, kin lɛk fɔ drink bifo di brak wata, bɔt yestede rafted off in casks, frɔm di Peruvian ɔ Indian streams. If dɛn alaw ɔda wɛl dɛn fɔ de na di say we dɛn de si, di fishaman dɛn nɔ go rili rɔnata wan pan dɛn Grɔn Turk ya; bikɔs dɛn Grand Turks ya tu lavish fɔ dɛn trɛnk, ɛn na dat mek dɛn unctuousness smɔl. As fɔ di bɔy pikin ɛn gyal pikin dɛn we dɛn bɔn, wetin mek, dɛn bɔy pikin ɛn gyal pikin dɛn de fɔ kia fɔ dɛnsɛf; at ɔl, na di mama in ɛp nɔmɔ. Bikɔs lɛk sɔm ɔda omnivorous roving lovers we dɛn kin kɔl, mi Lord Whale nɔ gɛt ɛni teist fɔ di nɔsri, ilɛksɛf i lɛk di bower; ɛn so, bikɔs i na big travulman, i kin lɛf in bebi dɛn we i nɔ no dɛn nem ɔlsay na di wɔl; evri bebi wan ekzotik. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, insay gud tɛm, as di wam we yɔŋ pipul dɛn kin gɛt kin go dɔŋ; as di ia ɛn di dɔmp dɛn de bɔku; as riflekshɔn de lɛnt am solemn stɔp dɛn; in shot, as wan jenɛral lassitude de ɔvatak di sated Turk; dɔn fɔ lɛk fɔ izi ɛn fɔ du gud tin dɛn kin tek di ples fɔ di lɔv fɔ titi dɛn; wi Ottoman enta pan di impotent, repentant, admonitory stej na layf, forswear, disbands di harem, en grow to wan exampulary, sulky old sol, go arawnd ol wan bitwin di meridians en parallels de se in preya, en won each yong Leviathan from in amorous errors.

Naw, as di fishaman dɛn kin kɔl di harem fɔ wɛl skul, na so di masta ɛn masta fɔ da skul de we dɛn sabi as di skulmasta. So i nɔ de insay strikt kɔlchɔ, ilɛksɛf i admirably satirical, se afta i go skul insɛf, i fɔ dɔn go ɔda kɔntri fɔ inculcate nɔto wetin i lan de, bɔt di fulish fɔ am. In taytul, skulmasta, go rili tan lɛk se i kɔmɔt frɔm di nem we dɛn gi di harem sɛf, bɔt sɔm pipul dɛn dɔn tink se di man we fɔs gi dis kayn Ottoman wɛl taytul so, go mɔs dɔn rid di mɛmua dɛn we Vidocq rayt, ɛn tɛl insɛf us kayn kɔntri-skul masta da fambul Frɛnchman de bin bi we i bin yɔŋ, ɛn wetin na bin di kayn we aw dɛn majik lɛsin dɛn de bin de inkult insay sɔm pan in studɛnt dɛn.

Di sem we aw di skulmasta wɛl kin betak insɛf to pipul dɛn we nɔ de nia ɔda pipul dɛn ɛn we i de fa frɔm ɔda pipul dɛn we i de ol, na tru fɔ ɔl di Spɛm Wɛl dɛn we dɔn ol. Klosap ɔlsay, wan wɛl we de in wan—as dɛn kin kɔl Livaytan we de in wan—de pruv se na wan we bin de trade trade. Lɛk venerable moss-bearded Daniel Buone, i nɔ go gɛt ɛnibɔdi nia am pas Nature insɛf; ɛn i kin tek am fɔ mared na di wildanɛs we wata de, ɛn na di bɛst uman dɛn, pan ɔl we i kin kip bɔku sikrit dɛn we kin mek pɔsin fil bad.

Di skul dɛm we nɔ de kɔmpoz ɛni wan pas yɔŋ ɛn trɛnk man dɛm, we wi bin dɔn tɔk bɔt, de gi wan strɔng difrɛns frɔm di harem skul dɛm. Bikɔs pan ɔl we dɛn uman wɛl dɛn de kin shem, di yɔŋ man dɛn, ɔ fɔti barɛl-bul dɛn, as dɛn kin kɔl dɛn, na dɛn kin fɛt pas ɔl di Livaytan dɛn, ɛn dɛn kin tɔk se dɛn denja pas ɔl fɔ mit dɛn; pas dɛn wɔndaful grey-hed, grizzled wɛl dɛn de, sɔntɛnde dɛn kin mit, ɛn dɛn wan ya go fɛt yu lɛk grim fiends we dɛn vɛks pan penal gout.

Di Fɔti-barɛl-bul skul dɛn big pas di harem skul dɛn. Lɛk bɔku bɔku yɔŋ kɔleji pipul dɛn, dɛn ful-ɔp wit fɛt-fɛt, ɛnjɔymɛnt, ɛn wikɛd tin dɛn, dɛn de tumbl rawnd di wɔl wit so rɛklɛs, rolik rit, dat no prudent ɔndarayta nɔ go inshɔr dɛn pas aw i go inshɔr wan bɔbɔ we de mek fɛt-fɛt na Yel ɔ Havad. Bɔt i nɔ tu te, dɛn kin lɛf dis trɔbul, ɛn we lɛk tri pat pan 4 pipul dɛn dɔn big, dɛn kin brok, ɛn dɛn kin go sɛpret fɔ go fɛn say dɛn fɔ de, dat na, harems.

Wan ɔda pɔynt we difrɛn bitwin di man ɛn uman skul dɛn stil gɛt mɔ kwaliti fɔ di man ɛn uman. Se yu slap wan Fɔti-barɛl-bul—poor dɛbul! ɔl in kɔmpin dɛn bin lɛf am. Bɔt strayk wan mɛmba na di harem skul, ɛn in kɔmpin dɛn swim rawnd am wit ɛvri sayn fɔ wɔri, sɔntɛnde dɛn kin de so nia am ɛn so lɔng, lɛk dɛnsɛf fɔ fɔdɔm pan animal.
