CHAPTER 43. Hark!

“HIST!Yu yɛri da nɔys de, Kabako?”

Na bin di midul-watch: wan fayn mun layt; di siman dɛn bin tinap na wan kɔdon, we bin de kɔmɔt frɔm wan pan di fresh wata bɔt dɛn we de na di wes, te to di skata-bɔt nia di tafrail. Dis we ya, dɛn pas di bɔkit dɛn fɔ ful-ɔp di skata-bɔt. We dɛn bin tinap, bɔku tɛm, na di oli ples dɛn na di kwata-dɛk, dɛn bin de tek tɛm mek dɛn nɔ tɔk ɔ rɔsh dɛn fut. Frɔm wan an to an, di bɔkit dɛn bin de go insay di dip saylɛns, dɛn bin jɔs de brok bay di flap we wan sel de flap wan wan tɛm, ɛn di stedi hum we di kil we de go bifo nɔ de stɔp.

Na insay dis rɛst, na in Archy, we na wan pan di kɔdɔn, we in post bin de nia di afta-hatch, wispa to in neba, wan Cholo, di wɔd dɛn we de ɔp.

“Hist!, yu yɛri da nɔys de, Kabako?”

“Tek di bɔkit, yu go du am, Archy? wetin nɔys d’ye min?”

“Na de i de bak—ɔnda di hatch dɛn—yu nɔ yɛri am—kɔf—i bin tan lɛk kɔf.”

“Kɔf bi damn! Pas along dat ritɔn bɔkit.”

“Na de bak—na de!—i tan lɛk se tu ɔ tri pipul dɛn we de slip de tɔn, naw!”

“Caramba! dɔn du, shipmet, yu go du am? Na di tri sok biskit dɛn we una de it fɔ sup de tɔn ova insay una—natin nɔ de. Luk to di bɔkit!”

“Say wetin yu want, shipmet, a gɛt shap yes.”

“Aye, yu na di chap, nɔto so, we yɛri di hum we di ol Kwakeress in knitting-needles de hum fifti mayl na si frɔm Nantucket; yu na di chap.”

"Grin away; wi go si wetin tɔn op. Hark ye, Cabaco, sɔmbɔdi de dɔŋ na di afta-hold we dɛn nɔ si yet na dɛk; ɛn a saspek se wi ol Mogul no sɔntin bɔt am bak. A yɛri Stubb tɛl Flask, wan mɔnin wach, se sɔntin lɛk dat bin de na di briz."

“Tish! di bɔkit!”





CHAPTER 44. Di Chat.

If yu bin fala Kpɛntin Eab dɔŋ insay in kebin afta di big big briz we bin apin di nɛt we bin apin afta da wayl ratifyeshɔn de fɔ in purpose wit in kru, yu fɔ dɔn si am go na wan lɔka na di transom, ɛn briŋ wan big wrinkled rol we gɛt yɔlɔsh si chɛt dɛn, spre dɛn bifo am pan in skru-dɔwn tebul. Dɔn we i sidɔm bifo am, yu fɔ dɔn si am gud gud wan de stɔdi di difrɛn layn dɛn ɛn shed dɛn we de mit in yay; ɛn wit slo bɔt stedi pensil tray ɔda kɔs dɛn oba spɛs dɛn we bifo bin blank. Wan wan tɛm, i kin tɔk bɔt bɔku bɔku ol lɔg buk dɛn we de nia am, usay dɛn kin put di sizin ɛn ples dɛn usay, pan difrɛn difrɛn ship dɛn we dɛn bin de travul trade, dɛn bin dɔn kech ɔ si sperm wɛl dɛn.

We dɛn bin de yuz am so, di ebi ebi piɔta lamp we dɛn bin de suspɛnd insay chen oba in ed, bin de rɔk ɔltɛm wit di muv we di ship de muv, ɛn fɔ ɔltɛm i bin de trowe di shado dɛn we de chenj chenj ɛn shado dɛn we gɛt layn dɛn na in brɔw we bin dɔn rɔtin, te i bin tan lɛk se we insɛf bin de mak layn ɛn kɔs dɛn na di chɛt dɛn we gɛt wrinkled, sɔm pensil dɛn we wi nɔ de si bin de tray layn ɛn kɔs dɛn bak di chɔt we dɛn mak dip dip wan na in fɔɛd.

Bɔt nɔto dis nɛt mɔ, we Eab bin de in wan, i bin de tink gud wan bɔt in chɛt dɛn. Klosap ɛvri nɛt, dɛn bin de briŋ dɛn kɔmɔt na do; klos to ɛvri nɛt, dɛn bin de pul sɔm pensil mak dɛn, ɛn dɛn bin de chenj ɔda wan dɛn. Bikɔs wit di chɛt dɛn fɔ ɔl di 4 si dɛn we bin de bifo am, Eab bin de thread wan mazi we gɛt kɔrɛnt ɛn ed, wit di atɛnshɔn fɔ di mɔ shɔ se i go ebul fɔ du da monomaniac tink bɔt in sol de.

Naw, to ɛnibɔdi we nɔ rili no di we aw di livatan dɛn de du, i go tan lɛk se na wok we nɔ mek sɛns fɔ fɛn wan tin we de in wan na di si dɛn we nɔ gɛt hop na dis planɛt. Bɔt nɔto so i bin tan lɛk to Eab, we bin no aw ɔl di tayd ɛn kɔrɛnt dɛn kin kam; ɛn bay dat, dɛn kin kɔl di drɛf we di sperm wɛl in it de drɛf; ɛn, bak, fɔ mɛmba di rɛgyula, ascertained sizin dɛn fɔ hunt am insay patikyula latitud dɛn; kin rich to rizin fɔ tink, i kin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ i kam nia sɔntin we i fɔ no, bɔt di de we i fɔ de na dis ɔ da grɔn de fɔ go fɛn in animal dɛn we i want fɔ it.

Fɔ tru, wi rili biliv di tru tin bɔt di tɛm we di sperm wɛl kin yuz wata we dɛn gi am, dat bɔku pipul dɛn we de ɔntin biliv se, dɛn go ebul fɔ wach am gud gud wan ɛn stɔdi am ɔlsay na di wɔl; if dɛn bin tek tɛm kɔl di lɔg dɛn fɔ wan trip fɔ di wan ol wɛl grup, dɔn dɛn go si se di we aw di sperm wɛl de muf go ɔdasay, nɔ go chenj to di wan dɛn we di hɛri-shɔl ɔ di flay we di swɛlin dɛn de flay. Na dis hint, dɛn dɔn tray fɔ mek fayn fayn chɔt dɛn fɔ di sperm wɛl we de muf go ɔdasay.*

Apat frɔm dat, we dɛn de mek rod frɔm wan ples fɔ it to ɔda ples, di sperm wɛl dɛn, we sɔm instinkt we nɔ de mek mistek de gayd dɛn—lɛ wi se, sikrit intɛlijɛns frɔm di Gɔd—bɔku pan dɛn kin swim insay veins , lɛk aw dɛn kin kɔl dɛn; kɔntinyu fɔ waka along wan givɛn ocean-line wit so undeviating exactitude, dat no ship nɔ ɛva sel in kɔs, bay ɛni chɔt, wit wan tɛn pan dis kayn wɔndaful prɛsishɔn. Pan ɔl we, insay dɛn kes ya, di dairekshɔn we ɛni wan wɛl tek fɔ bi stret as sɔvayva in paralel, ɛn pan ɔl we di layn fɔ go bifo fɔ bi strikt wan fɔ in yon wek we nɔ go ebul fɔ avɔyd, we stret, yet di arbitri vein we dɛn se i de swim insay dɛn tɛm dɛn ya, jɔs de embras sɔm mayl in waid (mɔ ɔ smɔl, as dɛn tink se di vein go pan ɔ kɔntrakt); bɔt i nɔ de ɛva pas di swip we pɔsin kin si frɔm di wɛl-ship in mast-hɛd dɛn, we i de glid fayn fayn wan along dis majik zon. Di sɔm na, se na patikyula sizin dɛn we de insay da brayt de ɛn along da rod de, dɛn kin luk fɔ wɛl dɛn we de muf go ɔdasay wit big kɔnfidɛns.

Ɛn dat mek nɔto jɔs di tɛm dɛn we dɛn gɛt pruf, na difrɛn say dɛn we pipul dɛn sabi fɔ it, Eab bin ebul fɔ op fɔ mit di animal we i want fɔ it; bɔt we i de krɔs di wata we big pas ɔl bitwin dɛn grɔn dɛn de, i bin ebul, bay in art, so ples ɛn tɛm insɛf na in rod, as ivin da tɛm de nɔ fɔ bi ɔltin we nɔ gɛt op fɔ mit.

Wan sikɔstɛms bin de we we yu si am fɔs, i bin tan lɛk se i de miks in delirious bɔt stil mɛtɔdikal skim. Bɔt nɔto so insay di rial tin, sɔntɛm. Pan ɔl we di gregarious sperm whales gɛt dɛn rɛgyula sizin fɔ patikyula grɔn, yet in jɔnal yu nɔ go ebul fɔ kɔnklud se di herds we bin de haunt so ɛn so latitude ɔ longitude dis ia, lɛ wi se, go tɔn aut fɔ bi di sem wit di wan dɛn we dɛn bin fɛn de di sizin bifo; pan ɔl we sɔm patikyula tin dɛn de we nɔ gɛt wan dawt usay dis dɔn sho se na tru. In jɛnɛral, di sem tɔk, jɔs insay wan less waid limit, de aplay to di solitary ɛn hermits bitwin di machɔ, ol sperm wɛl. So dat pan ɔl we dɛn bin dɔn si Mobi Dik insay wan ia trade, fɔ ɛgzampul, na wetin dɛn kɔl di Sishɛl grɔn na di Indian si, ɔ Vɔlkɛnɔ Bay na di Japan Kɔst; yet i nɔ bin fala, se if di Pequod go visit ɛni wan pan dɛn ples dɛn de na ɛni kɔrɛspɔndɛns sizin we go kam afta dat, i go mit am de we nɔ go mek mistek. So, tu, wit sɔm ɔda ples dɛn fɔ it, usay i bin dɔn sho insɛf sɔntɛnde. Bɔt ɔl dɛn tin ya bin tan lɛk se na di say dɛn nɔmɔ we i kin stɔp fɔ stɔp ɛn di say dɛn we i kin slip na di si, so fɔ se, nɔto di say dɛn we i kin de fɔ lɔng tɛm. Ɛn usay dɛn dɔn tɔk bɔt Eab in chans fɔ akɔmplit in ɔbjɛkt te naw, dɛn dɔn jɔs mek allusion to ɛni we-sayd, antecedent, ɛkstra prɔspɛkt dɛn we na in yon, ere wan patikyula sɛt tɛm ɔ ples bin atɛnd, we ɔl di pɔsibul dɛn go bi prɔbabiliti, ɛn, as Eab bin lɛk fɔ tink, ɛvri pɔsibul di nɛks tin to wan shɔt tin. Dɛn bin jɔyn da patikyula tɛm ɛn ples de we dɛn bin dɔn sɛt insay di wan tɛknik frayz—di Sizin-ɔn-di-Layn. Fɔ de ɛn afta dat, fɔ sɔm ia kɔnsɛkutiv, dɛn bin dɔn de tɔk bɔt Mobi Dik wan wan tɛm, i bin de de na dɛn wata dɛn de fɔ sɔm tɛm, as di san, insay in ɛni ia rawnd, de loit fɔ wan prɛdikted intaval insay ɛni wan sayn fɔ di Zodiac. Na de bak, bɔku pan di bad bad tin dɛn we dɛn bin de mit wit di wayt wɛl bin dɔn apin; na de dɛn bin de tɔk bɔt di wef dɛn wit di tin dɛn we i du; na de bak da trayjik ples de usay di monomaniac ol man bin dɔn fɛn di bad bad rizin fɔ in blem. Bɔt insay di tek tɛm we Eab bin tek tɛm fɔ ɔndastand ɛn nɔ bin de wach in sol we bin de tink gud wan insay dis ɔntin we nɔ bin de chenj, i nɔ bin fɔ alaw insɛf fɔ put ɔl in op dɛn pan di wan krawn tru tin we wi bin dɔn tɔk bɔt ɔp, ilɛksɛf i fayn fɔ dɛn op dɛn de; ɛn insay di vaw we i nɔ bin ebul fɔ slip, i nɔ bin ebul fɔ mek in at we nɔ kwayɛt so kol dat i go put ɔl di tin dɛn we i bin de du fɔ go bifo.

Naw, di Pequod bin dɔn kɔmɔt na Nantucket di tɛm we di Sizin-ɔn-di-Layn bigin. No pɔsibul tray da tɛm de nɔ bin ebul fɔ mek in kɔmanda ebul fɔ mek di big pasej we de go na di sawt, we de dubl Kep Hɔn, ɛn afta dat we i rɔn dɔŋ siksti digri latitud rich na di ekwetorial Pasifik insay di tɛm fɔ kruz de. So, i fɔ wet fɔ di nɛks sizin we go kam. Bɔt stil, sɔntɛm Eab bin dɔn pik di tɛm we di Pɛkud bin de go bifo tɛm, ɛn i bin dɔn pik am kɔrɛkt wan, wit di atɛnshɔn fɔ dis kayn we aw tin dɛn tan. Bikɔs, wan intaval we na tri ɔndrɛd ɛn siksti fayv de ɛn nɛt bin de bifo am; wan intaval we, instead fɔ bia we i nɔ peshɛnt fɔ bia na di si, i go spɛn fɔ fɛn difrɛn difrɛn tin dɛn; if bay chans di Wait Wɛl, we de spɛn in vaykashun na si dɛn we de fa frɔm di say dɛn we i de it ɔltɛm, fɔ tɔn in wrinkled brɔw ɔp ɔf di Pashian Galf, ɔ na di Bɛngal Bay, ɔ Chaina Si, ɔ na ɛni ɔda wata we in rays de haunt. So dat Monsɔn, Pampas, Nɔ’-Wɛsta, Hamatan, Tred; ɛni briz pas di Levanter ɛn Simoon, kin blo Moby Dick insay di devious zig-zag wɔl-sɛklɔ na di Pequod in sirkumnavigating wek.

Bɔt fɔ gi ɔl dis; yet, we yu tek am wit sɛns ɛn kol, i tan lɛk se nɔto bɔt na mad aidia, dis; dat insay di brayt si we nɔ gɛt bɔda, dɛn fɔ tink se wan wɛl we de in wan, ilɛksɛf dɛn mit am, i go ebul fɔ no wan wan pɔsin frɔm in ɔntinman, ivin lɛk Mufti we gɛt wayt biad na di rod dɛn we bɔku pipul dɛn kin pas na Kɔnstantinopl? Yɛs. Bikɔs di pekul wayt sno brɔw we Mobi Dik gɛt, ɛn in sno wayt hump, nɔ bin ebul fɔ bi tin we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ mistek. Ɛn a nɔ tink se a dɔn kɔnt di wɛl, Eab go de mɔna to insɛf, lɛk se afta we i dɔn luk in chɛt dɛn te lɔng afta midnayt i go trowe insɛf bak wit drɛg—i go kɔnt am, ɛn i go rɔnawe? In brayt fin dɛn dɔn bost, ɛn skel kɔmɔt lɛk ship in yes we dɔn lɔs! Ɛn na ya, in mad maynd go rɔn go bifo pan wan res we nɔ de blo; te wan taya ɛn taya fɔ tink gud wan kam oba am; ɛn na di opin ɛj na di dɛk i go tray fɔ gɛt in trɛnk bak. A, Gɔd! wetin trances of torments dat man de endure we de konsum wit wan unachieved revengeful desire. I kin slip wit in an dɛn we dɛn tay; ɛn wek wit in yon blɔd nel dɛn na in an.

Bɔku tɛm, we dɛn kin fos am fɔ kɔmɔt na in hamak bay drim dɛn we de mek i taya ɛn we nɔ go ebul fɔ bia wit drim dɛn bɔt di nɛt, we, we i bigin fɔ tink bak bɔt in yon strɔng tink dɛn tru di de, de kɛr dɛn go bifo bitwin wan krash we dɛn de kɔl frensi, ɛn swɛ dɛn rawnd ɛn rawnd ɛn rawnd insay in bren we de bɔn, te di rili bit we in layf-spɔt bin de blo bin bi pen we i nɔ go ebul fɔ bia; ɛn we, lɛk aw i kin bi sɔntɛnde, dɛn spiritual trɔbul dɛn ya we bin de insay am bin de es in bɔdi ɔp frɔm in bays, ɛn i bin tan lɛk se wan chasm de opin insay am, usay fɔk faya ɛn laytin dɛn bin de shot ɔp, ɛn swɛ dɛbul dɛn bin de mek sayn fɔ am fɔ jomp dɔŋ bitwin dɛn; we dis ɛl insɛf de yawn ɔnda am, dɛn go yɛri wan wayl kray tru di ship; ɛn wit yay we de shayn Eab go bɔs kɔmɔt na in stet rum, lɛk se i de rɔnawe kɔmɔt na bed we gɛt faya. Bɔt stil, dɛn tin ya, sɔntɛm, instead fɔ bi di sayn dɛn we nɔ go ebul fɔ stɔp fɔ sɔm kayn wikɛdnɛs we ayd, ɔ fɔ fred fɔ insɛf, na bin jɔs di klia sayn dɛn fɔ di kayn we aw i bin rili tranga. Bikɔs, na dɛn kayn tɛm dɛn de, krayzi Eab, di wan we de fɛn di wayt wɛl we nɔ de chenj ɛn we nɔ de mek i gladi; dis Eab we bin dɔn go na in hamak, nɔto di ɛjɛn we so mek i bɔs kɔmɔt de wit fred bak. Di las wan na bin di prinsipul ɔ sol we de sote go, we de alayv insay am; ɛn insay slip, bikɔs fɔ di tɛm i nɔ bin de pan di maynd we de sho di pɔsin in kwaliti, we ɔda tɛm dɛn bin de yuz am fɔ in ɔda motoka ɔ ɛjɛn, i bin de tray fɔ rɔnawe bay insɛf frɔm di skɔching kɔntigyushɔn fɔ di frantik tin, we, fɔ di tɛm, i nɔ bin bi wan impɔtant tin igen. Bɔt as di maynd nɔ de pas i lig wit di sol, so i fɔ dɔn bi dat, insay Eab in kes, fɔ gi ɔl in tink ɛn fansi to in wan impɔtant tin we i want fɔ du; da rizin de, bay in yon sheer inveteracy of will, fos insɛf agens gɔd ɛn dɛbul fɔ bi wan kayn self-assumed, indipɛndɛnt biin fɔ in yon. Nay, kin grimly liv ɛn bɔn, we di kɔmɔn vitality we i bin jɔyn to, rɔnawe horror-stricken frɔm di unbidden ɛn unfathered bɔn. So, di spirit we dɛn bin de sɔfa we bin de shayn kɔmɔt na in bɔdi in yay, we wetin bin tan lɛk Eab bin rɔsh kɔmɔt na in rum, na fɔ di tɛm bɔt na tin we nɔ gɛt pɔsin, na bin somnambulist biin we nɔ gɛt fɔm, wan rayt we gɛt layt we gɛt layf, fɔ tru, bɔt we nɔ gɛt ɛnitin fɔ kɔla, ɛn dat mek i bin bi blank insɛf. Gɔd ɛp yu, ol man, yu tinkin dɔn mek wan krichɔ insay yu; ɛn di wan we in strɔng tinkin so mek i bi Prometheus; wan vayl de it da at de sote go; dat vulture di very krichɔ we i mek.





CHAPTER 45. Di Afidavit.

So fa as wetin kin de pan wan narrative in dis buk; ɛn, fɔ tru, as i tɔch wan ɔ tu patikyula tin dɛn we rili intrestin ɛn we kin mek pɔsin want fɔ no mɔ bɔt di abit dɛn we sperm wɛl gɛt, di chapta we wi bin dɔn tɔk bɔt, insay in fɔs pat, impɔtant wan lɛk aw wi go si insay dis volyum; bɔt di lida tin bɔt am nid fɔ mek dɛn stil big pan am mɔ ɛn mɔ, fɔ mek dɛn ɔndastand am fayn fayn wan, ɛn pan ɔl dat, fɔ pul ɛnitin we nɔ biliv we wan dip ignɔrans bɔt di ɔl tɔpik kin mek sɔm pipul dɛn maynd, as to di natura l tru tin bɔt di men pɔynt dɛn na dis afɛkt.

A bisin fɔ nɔ du dis pat pan mi wok di rayt we; bɔt i fɔ satisfay fɔ prodyuz di impreshɔn we i want bay we i de sayt difrɛn say dɛn fɔ tin dɛn, we a sabi ɔ we a kin abop pan as wɛlman; ɛn frɔm dɛn saytayshɔn ya, a tek am—di kɔnklushɔn we dɛn aim fɔ go fala insɛf.

Fɔs: Mi sɛf dɔn no tri tɛm dɛn we wɛl, afta i dɔn gɛt harpun, dɔn mek i rɔnawe kpatakpata; ɛn, afta wan intaval (insay wan instans fɔ tri ia), dɛn dɔn bit am bak wit di sem an, ɛn kil am; we dɛn dɔn pul di tu ayɛn dɛn, we dɛn ɔl tu mak wit di sem prayvet sayfa, na di bɔdi. Bɔt dis impɔtant tɛm, dɛn yɛri wan ala ala wantɛm wantɛm we tek ɔl di yay pan di wɛl. Wit wan stat ol glared at dak Eab, we bin de roun wit faiv dusky phantoms we bin luk laik se fresh fom out of air.
