Fɔ di big big ɔda we dɛn kɔl folio leviathans, di Sperm Whale ɛn di Right Whale na dɛn wan dɛn we wi fɔ notis pas ɔl. Na dɛn nɔmɔ na di wɛl we mɔtalman kin fɛn ɔltɛm. To di Nantucketer, dɛn de sho di tu ekstrem dɛn fɔ ɔl di kayn wɛl dɛn we dɛn sabi. Fɔs, yu kin sɔprayz fɔ si di difrɛns bitwin dɛn ed dɛn ya. Dɛn ɔl tu big inof insay ɔl kɔnshɛns; bכt wan sכm mεtematכk simetri de insay di Sεm Wεl dεm we di Rayt Wεl in wan dεm sad wan nכ de. Insay di prɛsɛn instans bak, dis rɛspɛkt de ɔp bikɔs ɔf di pepa ɛn sɔl kɔlɔ we de na in ed na di sɔmt, we de gi token fɔ ol ej ɛn big ɛkspiriɛns. Fɔ tɔk smɔl, na in di fishaman dɛn kin kɔl “grey-headed whale.”

Naw lɛ wi no wetin nɔ difrɛn bɛtɛ na dɛn ed ya—dat na di tu pat dɛn we impɔtant pas ɔl, we na di yay ɛn di yes. Fa bak na di sayd we di ed de, ɛn dɔŋ, nia di angul we ɛni wan pan di wɛl dɛn jaw de, if yu luk smɔl, las las yu go si wan yay we nɔ gɛt lash, we yu go tink se na yɔŋ kɔlt in yay; so aut pan ɔl di prɔpɔshɔn i to di magnitude fɔ di ed.

Naw, frɔm dis pekul sayd we di wɛl in yay de, i klia se i nɔ go ɛva ebul fɔ si sɔntin we de bifo, nɔto pas aw i kin si pɔsin we de biɛn. Fɔ tɔk wan wɔd, di say we di wɛl in yay de kɔrɛkt wit di we aw man in yes de; ɛn yu kin fansi, fɔ yusɛf, aw i go du wit yu, yu bin de sɔv tin dɛn na sayd tru yu yes. Yu go si se yu kin jɔs kɔmand lɛk tati digri fɔ si bifo di stret sayd-layn fɔ si; ɛn lɛk tati ɔda wan dɛn biɛn am. If yu ɛnimi we bita pas ɔl bin de waka stret to yu, wit daga we dɛn es ɔp insay brayt de, yu nɔ go ebul fɔ si am, lɛk aw i de tif pan yu frɔm biɛn. Insay wan wɔd, yu go gɛt tu bak, so fɔ se; bɔt, di sem tɛm, bak, tu frɔnt (sayd frɔnt): bikɔs wetin de mek man in frɔnt—wetin, fɔ tru, pas in yay?

Pantap dat, pan ɔl we insay bɔku ɔda animal dɛn we a kin tink bɔt naw, dɛn kin plant di yay dɛn so dat dɛn nɔ kin no se dɛn go miks di pawa we dɛn gɛt fɔ si, so dat dɛn go mek wan pikchɔ ɛn nɔto tu to di bren; di spɛshal pozishɔn we di wɛl in yay dɛn de, we dɛn sheb fayn fayn wan lɛk aw dɛn de bay bɔku kubik fit we gɛt sɔlid ed, we de ɔp bitwin dɛn lɛk big mawnten we de sheb tu lek dɛn na vali dɛn; dis, fכ tru, fכ separet di impreshכn dεm we εvri indipεndεnt כgan de gi. So, di wɛl fɔ si wan difrɛn pikchɔ na dis say, ɛn ɔda difrɛn pikchɔ na da say de; pan ɔl we ɔltin we de bitwin dɛn fɔ bi dip daknɛs ɛn natin fɔ am. I go tan lɛk se mɔtalman de luk di wɔl frɔm wan sɛnt-bɔks we gɛt tu sash dɛn we dɛn jɔyn fɔ in winda. Bɔt wit di wɛl, dɛn kin put dɛn tu sash dɛn ya apat, ɛn dis kin mek tu difrɛn winda dɛn, bɔt i sɔri fɔ no se i nɔ kin ebul fɔ si. Dis pekul we di wɛl in yay gɛt na sɔntin we wi fɔ mɛmba ɔltɛm we dɛn de fishin; ɛn fɔ mek di pɔsin we de rid mɛmba am na sɔm tin dɛn we apin afta dat.

Wi kin bigin wan kwɛstyɔn we kin mek pɔsin want fɔ no mɔ ɛn we kin mek pɔsin kɔnfyus pas ɔl bɔt dis tin we pɔsin kin si as i tɔch di Livaytan. Bɔt a fɔ satisfay wit wan hint. So lɔng as man in yay opin na di layt, di akt fɔ si nɔto fɔ insɛf; dat min se, i nɔ go ebul fɔ ɛp da tɛm de fɔ si ɛnitin we de bifo am wit mashin. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, ɛnibɔdi in ɛkspiriɛns go tich am, se pan ɔl we i kin ebul fɔ swip tin dɛn wan tɛm we i nɔ de tek wan pɔsin bɛtɛ pas ɔda pɔsin, i nɔ pɔsibul fɔ mek i, tek tɛm, ɛn kɔmplit wan, chɛk ɛni tu tin—ilɛksɛf i big ɔ ilɛksɛf i smɔl—wan tɛm nɔmɔ; nɔ ɛva tink if dɛn ledɔm sayd to sayd ɛn tɔch dɛnsɛf. Bɔt if yu kam naw fɔ separet dɛn tu tin ya, ɛn rawnd ɛni wan pan dɛn wit wan sɛklɔ we gɛt dip daknɛs; dɔn, fɔ mek yu si wan pan dɛn, di kayn we we go mek yu maynd bia pan am, di ɔda wan go kɔmɔt kpatakpata pan yu kɔntempɔral kɔnshɛns. So aw i kin bi wit di wɛl? Na tru se in tu yay dɛn, insɛf, fɔ du sɔntin wan tɛm; bɔt yu tink se in bren so bɔku, we de jɔyn togɛda, ɛn we nɔ klia pas mɔtalman in bren, dat na di sem tɛm i kin tek tɛm luk tu difrɛn tin dɛn we i go gɛt, wan de na wan say pan am, ɛn di ɔda wan de na di ɔda say? If i ebul, dat min se na wɔndaful tin insay am, lɛk se man ebul fɔ go tru di demonstreshɔn dɛn we de sho tu difrɛn prɔblɛm dɛn na Yuklid wan tɛm. Nɔto, we dɛn strikt wan invɛstigat, ɛnitin nɔ de we nɔ gri wit dis kɔmpiashɔn.

I kin bi se na jɔs wan idle whim, bɔt i dɔn tan lɛk to mi ɔltɛm, se di ɛkstra ɔdinari vacillations of muvmɛnt we sɔm wɛl dɛn kin sho we tri ɔ 4 bot dɛn de kam mit dɛn; di shem ɛn layabiliti fɔ queer frayd, so kɔmɔn to dɛn kayn wɛl dɛn de; A tink se ɔl dis indaykt wan de kɔmɔt frɔm di ɛplɛs kɔnfyushɔn fɔ volishɔn, we dɛn divayd ɛn dayamɛtrik ɔpɔzit pawa fɔ vishɔn fɔ involv dɛn.

Bɔt di wɛl in yes ful-ɔp fɔ want fɔ no mɔ lɛk aw in yay kin want fɔ no mɔ. If yu na wan ol strenja to dɛn rays, yu kin ɔntin oba dɛn tu ed ya fɔ lɔng tɛm, ɛn nɔ ɛva fɛn da ɔgan de. Di yes nɔ gɛt ɛni ɔda lif we de na do; ɛn insay di ol sɛf yu nɔ go ebul fɔ put kwil, so wɔndaful minit na am. I de lod smɔl biɛn di yay. Wit rispek fɔ dɛn yes, dis impɔtant difrɛns na fɔ si bitwin di sperm wɛl ɛn di rayt. pan ɔl we di fɔs wan in yes gɛt wan opin we de na do, di sɛkɔn wan in yes kin kɔba ɔlsay ɛn ivin wit wan mɛmbran, so dat i nɔ go rili no am frɔm do.

Yu nɔ tink se i rili want fɔ no mɔ, se pɔsin we big lɛk di wɛl fɔ si di wɔl tru smɔl yay, ɛn yɛri di tɛnda tru wan yes we smɔl pas di hare in yon? Bɔt if in yay dɛn bin brayt lɛk di lens fɔ di big tɛliskɔp we Ɛshɛl bin mek; ɛn in yes dɛn bin big lɛk di pɔch dɛn na katidral dɛn; dat go mek i ebul fɔ si igen, ɔ i go shap fɔ yɛri? Nɔto so atɔl.—Wetin mek yu de tray fɔ “mek yu maynd big”? Subtilize am.

Lɛ wi naw wit ɛni lev ɛn stim-ɛnjin we wi gɛt na an, kant oba di sperm wɛl in ed, so dat i go ledɔm bɔt ɔp; dɔn, we yu de go ɔp wit lada fɔ go na di mawnten, mek yu luk dɔŋ di mɔt; ɛn if nɔto se di bɔdi dɔn separet ɔl naw frɔm am, wit lantan wi kin kam dɔŋ insay di big Kɛntaki Mamɔt Kev we de na in bɛlɛ. Bɔt lɛ wi ol ya wit dis tut, ɛn luk rawnd wi usay wi de. Na rili fayn ɛn klin mɔt! frɔm flo to siling, layn, ɔ rayt, pepa wit wan wayt mɛmbran we de shayn, we de shayn lɛk bridal satin.

Bɔt kam na do naw, ɛn luk dis portentous lower jaw, we tan lɛk di lɔng smɔl lid fɔ wan big big snuff-box, wit di hinj na wan ɛnd, instead of wan sayd. If yu pry am op, so dat yu go gɛt am oba ed, ɛn sho in row dɛn we gɛt tit, i tan lɛk se na terrific portcullis; ɛn dɛn kayn tin ya, alas! i pruv to bɔku pipul dɛn se na po wayt na di fisha wok, we dɛn spayk dɛn ya kin fɔdɔm pan wit pawa fɔ tik. Bɔt i rili bad fɔ si, we yu fathoms dɔŋ na di si, yu si sɔm sulky wɛl, we de flɔt de we dɛn suspɛnd, wit in prodigious jaw, lɛk fayvtin fit lɔng, we de hang stret dɔŋ rayt-angul wit in bɔdi, fɔ ɔl di wɔl lɛk ship in jib-boom. Dis wɛl nɔ day; i jɔs de fil bad; aut ɔf sɔm, sɔntɛm; di haypokɔndria; ɛn so supin, dat di hinj dɛn na in jaw dɔn rilaks, lɛf am de insay da kayn prɔblɛm de we nɔ fayn, we na kɔs fɔ ɔl in trayb, we fɔ, no dawt, fɔ kɔndɛm am fɔ lɔk-jaw.

Bɔku tɛm, dis jaw we de dɔŋ—we i izi fɔ mek pɔsin we sabi fɔ mek atis nɔ ebul fɔ pul am—dɛn kin pul am ɛn es am ɔp na di dɛk fɔ di rizin fɔ pul di ayvri tit dɛn, ɛn fɔ gi wan sapɔt fɔ da had wayt wɛlbɔn de we di fishaman dɛn kin yuz fɔ mek ɔlkayn tin dɛn we kin mek pipul dɛn want fɔ no mɔ, lɛk ken, ɔmbrela-stɔk, ɛn handel fɔ rayd-wip.

Wit wan lɔng, taya ɔs, dɛn kin drɛg di jaw pan di bot, lɛk se na anka; ɛn we di rayt tɛm rich—sɔm sɔm dez afta di ɔda wok—Queequeg, Daggoo, ɛn Tashtego, bikɔs dɛn ɔl na dɛntist dɛn we sabi du dɛnt, dɛn kin rɛdi fɔ drɔ dɛn tit. Wit wan keen cutting-spade, Queequeg de lans di gam dɛm; dɔn dɛn kin lash di jaw dɔŋ to ringbɔlt dɛn, ɛn we dɛn de rig wan takɛl frɔm ɔp, dɛn kin drɛg dɛn tit ya, lɛk aw Michigan ɔks dɛn kin drɛg stɔmp dɛn we gɛt ol ɔk tik dɛn kɔmɔt na wayl wud land dɛn. Jɛnɛral wan, fɔti tu tit dɛn de ɔl togɛda; insay ol wɛl dɛn, we dɔn pwɛl bad bad wan, bɔt we nɔ rɔtin; ɔ ful-ɔp afta wi atifishal fashɔn. Afta dat, dɛn kin saw di jaw fɔ mek i bi slab, ɛn dɛn kin gɛda am lɛk joist fɔ bil os.





CHAPTER 75. Di Rayt Wɛl in ed—Difrɛn we fɔ si tin.

We wi de krɔs di dɛk, lɛ wi naw gɛt gud lɔng luk pan di Rayt Wɛl in ed.

As in jenɛral shep di nobul Spɛm Wɛl in ed kin kɔmpia to wan Roman wɔ-chariot (especially in front, usay i so brayt rawnd); so, we yu si am brayt wan, di Rayt Wɛl in ed gɛt sɔntin we nɔ fayn we tan lɛk wan big big sus we gɛt galiot fut. Tu ɔndrɛd ia bifo dis tɛm, wan ol Dɔch man we bin de travul wit bot bin kɔmpia in shep to di las wan we pɔsin we de mek sus kin gɛt. Ɛn insay dis sem las ɔ shuz, da ol uman de na di nɔsri stori, wit di smɔl smɔl pikin dɛn we de swɛla, kin rili kɔmfyut fɔ lod, in ɛn ɔl in pikin dɛn.

Bɔt as yu de kam nia dis big ed i bigin fɔ tek difrɛn aspek dɛn, akɔdin to yu pɔynt. If yu tinap na in sɔmt ɛn luk dɛn tu F-shaped spoutholes ya, yu go tek di wan ol ed fɔ wan big big bas-viol, ɛn dɛn spiracles ya, di apertures in in sounding-board. Dɔn, bak, if yu fiks yu yay pan dis strenj, kres, we tan lɛk kɔm we de ɔp di mas—dis grɔn, barnacled tin, we di Grinland pipul dɛn kɔl di “krawn,” ɛn di Sawt fishaman dɛn kɔl di “bɔnet” fɔ di Rayt Wɛl; we yu fiks yu yay pan dis nɔmɔ, yu go tek di ed fɔ di trɔnk fɔ sɔm big big ɔk, wit bɔd in nɛst na in kroch. Ɛni we aw i bi, we yu wach dɛn layf krab dɛn we de nest ya na dis bɔnɛt, i go lɛf smɔl fɔ mek yu tink se dis kayn aidia go kam na yu maynd; pas nɔmɔ, fɔ tru, dɛn dɔn fiks yu fansi bay di tɛknikal wɔd “krawn” we dɛn gi am bak; if na so i bi, yu go rili intres fɔ tink bɔt aw dis pawaful monsta rili bi kiŋ na di si we gɛt dayad, we dɛn dɔn put in grɔn krawn togɛda fɔ am dis wɔndaful we. Bɔt if dis wɛl bi kiŋ, i rili sulky luk felo fɔ gras wan dayadɛm. Luk da lip we de dɔŋ we de hang! wetin na big big sulk ɛn pout de! wan sulk ɛn pout, bay kapɛnta in mɛzhɔ, lɛk twɛnti fit lɔng ɛn fayv fit dip; wan sulk ɛn pout we go gi yu lɛk 500 galɔn ɔyl ɛn mɔ.

Wan big sɔri-at, naw, se dis ɔnaful wɛl fɔ gɛt hare-lip. Di fis na lɛk wan fut akɔdin. Sɔntɛm di mama we bin de insay wan impɔtant tɛm bin de tek bot fɔ go dɔŋ di si na Pɛru, we atkwek bin mek di bich opin. Ova dis lip, lεk ova wan slip trεshold, wi de slayv naw insay di mכt. Upon mai wod were I at Mackinaw, a shud tek dis tu bi di insaid of wan Indian wigwam. Gud Masta! dis na di rod we Jona bin go? Di ruf ay lɛk twɛlv fit, ɛn i de rɔn to wan fayn shap angle, lɛk se wan rɛgyula rij-pol de de; we dɛn sayd ya we gɛt rib, arch, we gɛt ia, de prɛzɛnt wi wit dɛn wɔndaful, af vertikal, scimetar-shep slat dɛn we gɛt wɛlbon, se tri ɔndrɛd pan wan sayd, we dipen frɔm di ɔpa pat pan di ed ɔ krawn bon, de mek dɛn Venetian blaynd dɛn we dɛn dɔn tɔk bɔt ɔdasay. Di ed dɛn fɔ dɛn bon ya gɛt fayv dɛn we gɛt ia, we di Rayt Wɛl kin yuz fɔ strɛch di wata, ɛn insay in intrikas dɛn i kin kip di smɔl fish dɛn, we i opin in mɔt i kin go tru di si dɛn we brit de insay di tɛm we i de it. Insay di sɛntral blaynd dɛn na di bon, as dɛn tinap insay dɛn natura ɔda, sɔm mak dɛn de we kin mek pɔsin want fɔ no mɔ, kɔba, ol, ɛn rij dɛn, usay sɔm pipul dɛn we de fɛt wɛl kin kɔl di ej we di krichɔ gɛt, lɛk di ej we ɔk gɛt bay in sɛkɔndari ring dɛn. Though di surety of dis krayteria de fa frɔm demonstrable, yet i gɛt di savour of analogical probability. Ɛni we aw i bi, if wi gri wit am, wi fɔ gi di Rayt Wɛl ej we pas fa fawe pas aw yu go si am fɔs.

Trade trade, i tan lɛk se pipul dɛn bin de tink bɔt dɛn blaynd dɛn ya we kin mek pipul dɛn want fɔ no mɔ. Wan man we de travul na Purchas kɔl dɛn di wɔndaful “wiskers” we de insay di wɛl in mɔt;* wan ɔda wan, “hogs’ bristles”; wan tɔd ol jentlman na Hackluyt yuz dis fayn fayn langwej: “Na lɛk tu ɔndrɛd ɛn fifti fin dɛn de gro na ɛni say na in ɔpa chop , we de arch oba in tong na ɛni say na in mɔt.”

*Dis de mɛmba wi se di Rayt Wɛl rili gɛt wan kayn wisk, ɔ fɔ se i gɛt mɔstaz, we gɛt sɔm wayt ia dɛn we skata na di ɔp pat na di ɔda ɛnd na di ɔda jaw. Sɔntɛnde, dɛn tuft dɛn ya kin mek in fes we nɔ bin rili impɔtant.

As ɔlman no, dɛn sem “hɔg dɛn bristles,” “fin,” “wiskers,” “blinds,” ɔ ɛnitin we yu want, de gi di uman dɛn dɛn bɔks ɛn ɔda tin dɛn we de mek dɛn stif. Bɔt insay dis patikyula, di dimand dɔn de go dɔŋ fɔ lɔng tɛm. Na insay Kwin An in tɛm di bon bin de insay in glori, di farthingale na bin ɔl di fashɔn da tɛm de. Ɛn as dɛn ol damɛ dɛn de bin de muf wit gladi at, pan ɔl we dɛn bin de na di wɛl in jaw, lɛk aw yu go se; ivin so, insay shawa, wit di kayn we aw wi nɔ de tink, wi de flay tide ɔnda di sem jaw fɔ protɛkshɔn; di ambɔla na tɛnt we dɛn spred oba di sem bon.

Bɔt naw fɔgɛt ɔltin bɔt blaynd ɛn wisk fɔ smɔl tɛm, ɛn, tinap na di Rayt Wɛl in mɔt, luk rawnd yu bak. We yu si ɔl dɛn kɔlɔna dɛn ya we gɛt bon dɛn we dɛn de rɛnj so, yu nɔ go tink se yu de insay di big Haarlem ɔgan, ɛn de luk in tawzin paip dɛn? Fɔ wan kap to di ɔgan wi gɛt wan rug we na di Turkey we soft pas ɔl—di tɔŋ, we dɛn kin glu, lɛk se, na di flɔ na di mɔt. I rili fat ɛn i tan lɛk, ɛn i kin te we i de es am ɔp na di dɛk. Dis patikyula tong naw bifo wi; we a luk pas a fɔ se na siks-barɛl; dat min se i go gi yu lɛk da kayn ɔyl de.

Ere dis, yu go dɔn si klia wan di tru tin bɔt wetin a bigin wit—dat di Spɛm Wɛl ɛn di Rayt Wɛl gɛt ɔlmost difrɛn ed dɛn. So fɔ sɔm ɔp: na di Rayt Wɛl in yon big wɛl nɔ de we gɛt swɛlin; nɔ gɛt ayvri tit atɔl; nכ lכng, slεnd mandibul fכ wan lכw jaw, lεk di Sεm Wεl in yon. Nɔto insay di Spɛm Wɛl, ɛni wan pan dɛn blaynd dɛn de we gɛt bon; nɔ gɛt big big lip we de dɔŋ; ɛn i nɔ kin gɛt ɛnitin fɔ du wit wan langwej.
