CHAPTER 55. Fɔ di Monstrous Pikchɔ dɛn bɔt Wɛl.

I shall ere long paint to you as well as one can without canvas, sɔntin lɛk di tru fɔm fɔ di wɛl as i rili de apia to di wɛl man in yay we insay in yon absolyut bɔdi di wɛl de mɔr alongsay di wɛl-ship so dat dɛn go ebul fɔ step am fayn fayn wan de. So, i kin fayn fɔ advatayz bifo to dɛn kɔriɔs imajinari pikchɔ dɛn de bɔt am we ivin te tide wit kɔnfidɛns chalenj di fet we di landman gɛt. Na tɛm fɔ mek di wɔl rayt pan dis, bay we wi pruv se dɛn kayn pikchɔ dɛn ya bɔt di wɛl nɔ rayt.

I kin bi se di men say we ɔl dɛn pikchɔ dɛn de we pipul dɛn kin tink bɔt, go de pan di ol wan dɛn we dɛn mek wit Indu, Ijipshian, ɛn Gris. Fɔ frɔm da tɛm de we pipul dɛn bin de mek tin dɛn bɔt we nɔ bin gɛt sɛns, we dɛn bin de mek pan di mabul panɛl dɛn na di tɛmpul dɛn, di say dɛn we dɛn mek statu dɛn, ɛn pan shild dɛn, mɛdaliɔn dɛn, kɔp dɛn, ɛn kɔyn dɛn, dɛn bin de drɔ di dɔlfin wit skel dɛn we dɛn mek wit chen we tan lɛk Saladin in yon, ɛn wan ed we gɛt ɛlmɛt lɛk Sɛnt Jɔj in yon; frɔm da tɛm de, sɔntin we gɛt di sem kayn laysens dɔn de, nɔto jɔs pan bɔku pan di pikchɔ dɛn we pipul dɛn lɛk bɔt di wɛl, bɔt na bɔku sayɛns prɛzɛntɛnshɔn dɛn bɔt am.

Naw, bay ɔl di ɔda tin dɛn, di pikchɔ we ol pas ɔl we stil de ɛni we we dɛn se na di wɛl in yon, na fɔ si am na di famɔs kev-pagoda we dɛn kɔl Ɛlifanta, insay India. Di Brahmin dɛn kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se insay di skapul dɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnd na da pagoda de we nɔ bin dɔn ɛva de, ɔl di wok dɛn ɛn di tin dɛn we pɔsin kin du, ɛvri wok we pɔsin kin tink bɔt, bin dɔn de fɔ lɔng lɔng tɛm bifo ɛni wan pan dɛn rili kam. So, i nɔ sɔprayz fɔ no se insay sɔm kayn we wi nobul wok fɔ kech wɛl fɔ dɔn de de shado. Di Hindoo wɛl we dɛn tɔk bɔt, de apin na wan sɛpret dipatmɛnt na di wɔl, we de sho di bɔdi we Vishnu bɔn insay di fɔm we dɛn kɔl leviathan, we pipul dɛn lan as di Matse Avatar. Bɔt pan ɔl we dis skapul na af man ɛn af wɛl, so dat i go jɔs gi di las wan in tel, bɔt stil da smɔl pat de pan am ɔl nɔ rayt. I tan lɛk di tel we de tan lɛk anakonda, pas di brayt brayt an dɛn we di tru wɛl in majestic flukes gɛt.

Bɔt go na di ol Galeries, ɛn luk naw wan big Kristian peinta in pikchɔ bɔt dis fish; bikɔs i nɔ ebul fɔ win bɛtɛ pas di Hindu we bin de bifo di wata we bin de rɔn. Na Guido in pikchɔ we Pɛsiɔs de sev Andrɔmɛda frɔm di si-mɔnta ɔ wɛl. Usay Guido bin gɛt di mɔdel fɔ dis kayn strenj krichɔ lɛk dat? Ɛn Hogarth, we i de peint di sem scene insay in yon “Perseus Descending,” nɔ mek wan smɔl bɛtɛ bɛtɛ wan. Di big big bɔdi we da Hogarthian monsta de gɛt de ɔnda di wata, ɛn i nɔ kin izi fɔ pul wan inch wata. I gɛt wan kayn howdah na in bak, ɛn in mɔt we gɛt tusk we dɔn big we di bilɔ dɛn de rɔl insay, dɛn kin tek am fɔ di Treytors’ Gate we de kɔmɔt na di Temz bay wata ɛn go insay di Tawa. Dɔn, di Prodromus wɛl dɛn de we dɛn bin de yuz fɔ ol Skɔtish Sibbald, ɛn Jona in wɛl, lɛk aw dɛn sho am na di print dɛn we dɛn print na ol Baybul dɛn ɛn di kɔt dɛn we dɛn kɔt ol prayma dɛn. Wetin dɛn go tɔk bɔt dɛn tin ya? As fɔ di buk-baynda in wɛl we de wind lɛk vayn-stɔl rawnd di stok fɔ wan anka we de kam dɔŋ—as dɛn stamp am ɛn gild am na di bak ɛn taytul-pej dɛn fɔ bɔku buk dɛn we ol ɛn nyu—dat na wan rili pikchɔ bɔt klin fabul krichɔ, we dɛn falamakata, a tek am, frɔm di kayn figa dɛn we de pan antik vays dɛn. Pan ɔl we ɔlsay dɛn kin kɔl am dɔlfin, pan ɔl dat, a kin kɔl dis fish we dɛn kin yuz fɔ tay buk, fɔ tray fɔ kil wɛl; bikɔs na so dɛn bin want am we dɛn bin de yuz di divays fɔ di fɔstɛm. Na wan ol Italiyan pɔblisha bin introduks am sɔmsay lɛk di 15t sɛntinari, di tɛm we dɛn bin de Rivayval fɔ Lanin; ɛn insay dɛn tɛm dɛn de, ɛn ivin te to di tɛm we dɛn kɔmpia am, pipul dɛn bin de tink se dɔlfin dɛn na wan kayn Livaytan.

Insay di vignet ɛn ɔda ɛmbelishmɛnt dɛn fɔ sɔm ol buk dɛn yu go mit sɔntɛnde wit rili kɔriɔs tɔch dɛn na di wɛl, usay ɔl kayn spɔt, jet d’eau, ɔt spring ɛn kol, Saratoga ɛn Baden-Baden, kin kam bɔbbul ɔp frɔm in bren we nɔ taya. Insay di taytul-pej fɔ di fɔs edishɔn fɔ di “Advancement of Learning” yu go si sɔm wɛl dɛn we want fɔ no mɔ.

Bɔt we wi lɛf ɔl dɛn tray ya we nɔto pɔsin we sabi du in wok, lɛ wi luk dɛn pikchɔ dɛn de bɔt leviathan we dɛn se na soba, sayɛns delineations, we di wan dɛn we no. Insay di ol Haris in kɔlekɛshɔn fɔ travul, sɔm plet dɛn we gɛt wɛl dɛn we dɛn pul frɔm wan Dutch buk we de tɔk bɔt travul, AD 1671, we gɛt di taytul “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson of Friesland, master.” Insay wan pan dɛn plet dɛn de, dɛn sho di wɛl dɛn, lɛk big big raf dɛn we dɛn mek wit tik dɛn, we de ledɔm bitwin ays ayland dɛn, ɛn wayt bea dɛn de rɔn oba dɛn bak we de alayv. Insay wan ɔda plet, dɛn mek di prodigious blunder fɔ ripresent di wɛl wit perpendicular flukes.

Dɔn bak, wan impɔtant kwata de, we wan Kpɛntin Kɔlnɛt, we na Post Kpɛntin na di Inglish nevi, rayt, we gɛt di taytul “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, for the purpose of extended the Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Insay dis buk, dɛn rayt wan ɔutlayn we dɛn se na “Picture of a Physeter or Spermaceti whale, we dɛn pul bay skel frɔm wan we dɛn kil na di si na Mɛksiko, Ɔgɔst, 1793, ɛn we dɛn es am ɔp na di dɛk.” A dawt if di kapten nɔ bin mek dɛn tek dis tru tru pikchɔ fɔ bɛnifit in marin sojaman dɛn. Fɔ tɔk bɔt wan tin bɔt am, lɛ a se i gɛt yay we dɛn aplay, akɔdin to di skel we de wit am, to wan ful-grɔn sperm wɛl, go mek di yay fɔ da wɛl de bi bo-windo we lɔng lɛk fayv fit. Ah, mi gallant kapten, wetin mek yu nɔ gi wi Jona we de luk kɔmɔt na da yay de!

Ɛn di wan dɛn we dɛn dɔn kɔmpilayt Natural History we gɛt kɔnshɛns pas ɔl fɔ bɛnifit di yɔŋ wan dɛn ɛn di wan dɛn we gɛt sɛns, nɔ gɛt di sem bad bad mistek we dɛn mek. Luk da buk de we pipul dɛn lɛk we nem “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature.” Insay di shɔt shɔt edishɔn we dɛn mek na Lɔndɔn insay 1807, dɛn put plet dɛn we gɛt wan “wɛl” ɛn wan “narwɛl.” A nɔ want fɔ mek i tan lɛk se a nɔ fayn, bɔt dis wɛl we nɔ fayn, tan lɛk sow we dɛn kɔt kɔt kɔt; ɛn, as fɔ di narwhale, wan smɔl luk pan am na inf fɔ mek pɔsin sɔprayz, se insay dis nayntin sɛntiwɔd dɛn kin palm dis kayn hipogrif fɔ tru pan ɛni intɛligent pɔblik we skul bɔy dɛn gɛt.

Dɔn bak, insay 1825, Bɛnard Jɛmɛn, we na Kaɔnt di Lasɛpɛd, we na wan bigman we sabi bɔt di wɔl, bin pul wan buk we gɛt sayɛns we dɛn kɔl wɛl, we gɛt bɔku pikchɔ dɛn bɔt di difrɛn difrɛn kayn Livaytan dɛn. Ɔl dɛn tin ya nɔto jɔs kɔrɛkt, bɔt di pikchɔ bɔt di Mysticetus ɔ Grinland wɛl (dat na fɔ se, di Rayt wɛl), ivin Skɔresbi, we dɔn gɛt ɛkspiriɛns fɔ lɔng tɛm fɔ tɔch da kayn animal de, de tɔk se i nɔ gɛt in kɔmpin insay di nature.

Bɔt di ples we dɛn put di kap-shif fɔ ɔl dis blundering biznɛs, na fɔ di sayɛnsman we nem Frɛdrik Kuvia, we na di brɔda fɔ di fambul Barɔn. Insay 1836, i bin pul wan buk we dɛn kɔl Natural History of Whales, ɛn i bin gi wetin i kɔl wan pikchɔ bɔt di Sperm Whale. Bifo yu sho da pikchɔ de to ɛni Nantucketer, i bin bɛtɛ fɔ mek yu gi yu sɔmari ritrit frɔm Nantucket. Fɔ tɔk wan wɔd, Frɛdrik Kuvia in Spɛm Wɛl nɔto Spɛm Wɛl, bɔt na skwash. Na tru se i nɔ ɛva gɛt di bɛnifit fɔ go kech wɛl (dɛn kayn man ya nɔ kin gɛt bɛtɛ bɛnifit), bɔt usay i pul da pikchɔ de, udat go no? Sɔntɛm i bin gɛt am as sayɛnsman we bin de bifo am na di sem wok, we nem Desmarest, bin gɛt wan pan in tru tru bɛlɛ; dat na, frɔm wan Chaynish drɔin. Ɛn us kayn layfful ​​lads wit di pensil dɛn Chaynish pipul dɛn de, bɔku queer kɔp ɛn sosa dɛn kin tɛl wi.

As fɔ di wɛl dɛn we pipul dɛn we de peint sayn dɛn de si na strit we dɛn de hang oba di shɔp dɛn we pipul dɛn we de sɛl ɔyl de mek, wetin dɛn go tɔk bɔt dɛn? Dɛn kin jɔs bi Richad di Tɔd. wɛl, wit dromedary humps, ɛn rili savayj; brɛkfas pan tri ɔ 4 selɔ tart, dat na wɛl bot dɛn we ful-ɔp wit pipul dɛn we de wok na di si: dɛn difɔmiti dɛn de flɔd na si we gɛt blɔd ɛn blu pent.

Bɔt dɛn bɔku bɔku mistek dɛn ya we dɛn mek we dɛn de sho di wɛl nɔ rili sɔprayz afta ɔl. Tink bɔt! Bɔku pan di sayɛns drɔin dɛn we dɛn tek frɔm di fish dɛn we dɔn stɔp; ɛn dɛn tin ya kɔrɛkt lɛk aw fɔ drɔ ship we dɔn pwɛl, we gɛt bak brok, go kɔrɛkt wan fɔ sho di fayn animal insɛf pan ɔl in prayz we nɔ gɛt wan skata we gɛt in bɔdi ɛn spars. Pan ɔl we ɛlifant dɛn dɔn tinap fɔ dɛn ful-lɛng, di Livaytan we de alayv nɔ ɛva flɔt insɛf fayn fayn wan yet fɔ in pikchɔ. Di wɛl we gɛt layf, insay in ful majesty ɛn minin, na fɔ jɔs si am na si insay wata we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ ɔndastand; ɛn we i de flɔt, di big big pat pan am nɔ de si am, lɛk ship we dɛn dɔn lans we de fɛt wɔ; ɛn frɔm da ɛlimɛnt de na tin we nɔ pɔsibul sote go fɔ mɔtalman fɔ es am ɔp in bɔdi na di ays, so dat i go kip ɔl in pawaful swɛlin ɛn ɔnda. Ɛn, nɔ fɔ tɔk bɔt di difrɛns we dɛn kin tink se go difrɛn bitwin wan yɔŋ wɛl we de sok ɛn wan ful-grɔn Platonian Livaytan; yet, ivin insay wan pan dɛn yɔŋ wɛl dɛn we de sok we dɛn es am ɔp to ship in dɛk, na so di ɔda kayn we aw i tan, we tan lɛk il, we gɛt smɔl smɔl shep, we gɛt difrɛn shep, so dat di dɛbul insɛf nɔ bin ebul fɔ kech di rayt we aw i de tɔk.

Bɔt dɛn kin tink se frɔm di nekɛd skel we di wɛl we dɔn stɔp, dɛn kin gɛt kɔrɛkt hint dɛn we de tɔch in tru tru fɔm. Atɔl. Bikɔs na wan pan di tin dɛn we kin mek pipul dɛn want fɔ no mɔ bɔt dis Livaytan, na dat in skel nɔ de gi bɛtɛ aidia bɔt in jenɛral shep. Pan ɔl we Jɛri Bɛntam in skel, we de hang fɔ kandul na wan pan in ɛgzikitɔ dɛn laybri, kɔrɛkt wan de sho di aidia fɔ wan ol jentlman we gɛt bɔdi we gɛt bɔku bɔku brɔda, wit ɔl di ɔda big big pɔsin dɛn we Jɛri gɛt; yet dɛn nɔ bin ebul fɔ no natin lɛk dis frɔm ɛni leviathan in artikul bon dɛn. Infakt, lɛk aw di big Hɔnta se, di jɔs skel we di wɛl gɛt gɛt di sem rilayshɔn to di animal we dɛn dɔn put ɔl in mɔni ɛn we gɛt pad lɛk aw di insɛkt gɛt fɔ du wit di krysalis we de rawnd am so rawnd. Dis peculiarity de strikingly evinced in di ed, as in som pat of dis buk go sho in incidentally. I de sho bak wit rili kɔriɔs na di sayd fin, we di bon dɛn kin ansa ɔlmost ɛksaktɔli to di bon dɛn na mɔtalman an, we dɛn pul di big an nɔmɔ. Dis fin gɛt 4 bon-finga dɛn we de ɔltɛm, di indeks, midul, ring, ɛn smɔl finga. Bɔt ɔl dɛn tin ya kin lod sote go insay dɛn bɔdi we dɛn kɔba, lɛk mɔtalman finga dɛn we de insay atifishal kɔva. Wan de, Stub we bin de laf bin tɔk se: “If di wɛl kin sav wi sɔntɛnde, dɛn nɔ go ɛva rili se i de ol wi if i nɔ gɛt mit.”

So, fɔ ɔl dɛn rizin ya, ɛni we we yu luk am, yu fɔ nid fɔ dɔn se di big Livaytan na da wan tin we Gɔd mek na di wɔl we nɔ fɔ peint te to di las wan. Na tru se wan pikchɔ kin hit di mak fa fawe pas ɔda wan, bɔt nɔbɔdi nɔ kin hit am wit ɛni rili big digri fɔ ɛksaktɔ. So no we nɔ de na di wɔl fɔ no ustɛm di wɛl rili tan. Ɛn di wangren we aw yu kin pul ivin wan tolerable aidia bɔt in liv kɔntɔ, na bay we yu go wɛl yusɛf; bɔt we yu du dat, yu nɔ go gɛt ɛni smɔl risk fɔ bi stov ɛn sink bay am sote go. So, i tan lɛk to mi se i bin bɛtɛ fɔ mek yu nɔ tu fastidious insay yu curiosity fɔ tɔch dis Leviathan.





CHAPTER 56. Of di Less Erroneous Pictures of Whales, ɛn di Tru Pikchɔ dɛn bɔt di Wel.

In kɔnekshɔn wit di monstrous pikchɔ dɛm fɔ wɛl, a de strong wan tɛmpt ya fɔ go insay pan dɛn stil mɔ monstrous stori dɛm bɔt dɛm we fɔ de na sɔm buk dɛm, ɔl tu di ol ɛn di mɔdan, mɔ na Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, ɛn ɔda wan dɛm Bɔt a pas dat mata.

Na 4 autlayn nɔmɔ we dɛn dɔn pablish bɔt di big Spɛm Wɛl nɔmɔ a no; Na Kɔlnet, Hugins, Frɛdrik Kuvia, ɛn Bil. Insay di chapta we dɔn pas, dɛn bin tɔk bɔt Kɔlnɛt ɛn Kuviɛ. Huggins in yon bɛtɛ fa fawe pas dɛn yon; bɔt, bay big ods, Beale in yon na di bɛst. Ɔl di drɔin dɛn we Beale drɔ bɔt dis wɛl na gud, pas di midul figa na di pikchɔ fɔ tri wɛl dɛn we gɛt difrɛn abit dɛn, we de kapchɔ in sɛkɔn chapta. In frontispiece, bot dɛn we de atak Sperm Whales, pan ɔl we i go mɔs bi se dɛn kɔl am fɔ mek sɔm parlor man dɛn gɛt di sivil skepticism, na admirably kɔrɛkt ɛn layf-layk in in jenɛral ifɛkt. Sɔm pan di Sperm Whale drɔin dɛn na J. Ross Browne rili kɔrɛkt pan kɔnto; bɔt dɛn rayt dɛn bad bad wan. Bɔt nɔto in fɔlt dat.

Fɔ di Rayt Wɛl, di bɛst ɔtlayn pikchɔ dɛn de na Skɔrsbi; bɔt dɛn drɔ dɛn pan tu smɔl skel fɔ mek pɔsin fil fayn. I gɛt wan pikchɔ nɔmɔ we de sho aw dɛn de kech wɛl, ɛn dis na sɔri tin we i nɔ ebul fɔ du, bikɔs na bay dɛn kayn pikchɔ dɛn de nɔmɔ, we dɛn du am fayn atɔl, yu kin gɛt ɛnitin lɛk tru tru aidia bɔt di wɛl we de alayv lɛk aw in ɔntinman dɛn we de alayv de si am.

Bɔt, if dɛn tek am ɔl togɛda, di bɛst, pan ɔl we insay sɔm ditil dɛn nɔto di kɔrɛkt pas ɔl, prɛzɛntɛnshɔn dɛn bɔt wɛl ɛn wɛl dɛn we dɛn go si ɛnisay, na tu big Frɛnch engraving dɛn, we dɛn dɔn ɛksɛkutiv fayn fayn wan, ɛn we dɛn tek frɔm di pikchɔ dɛn we wan Garnery bin peint. Rispɛktful wan, dɛn ripresent atak pan di Spɛm ɛn Rayt Wɛl. Insay di fɔs engraving dɛn sho wan nobul Sperm Whale wit ful majesty of might, jɔs rayz ɔnda di bot frɔm di profundities of di ocean, ɛn bia ay na di ays pan in bak di terrific wreck of di stoven planks. Di fɔs pat pan di bot nɔ brok, ɛn dɛn drɔ am jɔs balans pan di monsta in spayna; ɛn we yu tinap na da prow de, fɔ da wan flash we yu nɔ go ebul fɔ kɔmpia, yu si wan man we de rayd ɔs, we di wɛl in bɔyl spɔt we gɛt insɛns dɔn kɔba am, ɛn we de jomp, lɛk se i kɔmɔt na wan ples we nɔ gɛt wata. Di akshɔn fɔ di wan ol tin na wɔndaful gud ɛn tru. Di layn-tɔb we dɔn ɛmti af-af de flɔt na di si we dɔn wayt; di wud tik dɛn we di harpuon dɛn we dɔn rɔsh de yuz obliquely bob in it; di ed dɛn fɔ di wan dɛn we de swim de skata rawnd di wɛl insay difrɛn we dɛn we de sho se dɛn de fred; we di blak stormy distans di ship de bear down pan di scene. Dɛn kin si siriɔs fɔlt wit di anatomical ditel dɛm fɔ dis wɛl, bɔt lɛ dat pas; bikɔs, fɔ mi layf, a nɔ bin ebul fɔ drɔ so gud wan.

Insay di sɛkɔn engraving, di bot de insay di akt fɔ drɔ nia di barnacled flank fɔ wan big Right Whale we de rɔn, we de rol in blak weedy bulk na di si lɛk sɔm mossy rock-slide frɔm di Patagonian klifs. In jet dɛn tinap stret, ful-ɔp, ɛn blak lɛk sɔt; so dat frɔm so bɔku smok na di chimini, yu go tink se i go mɔs bi se wan brayt sup de kuk na di big bɔdi we de dɔŋ. Si fɔl dɛn de pik di smɔl smɔl krab dɛn, shɔɛl-fish, ɛn ɔda si kandy ɛn makaroni, we di Rayt Wɛl kin kɛr sɔntɛnde na in bak we gɛt sik.
