CHAPTER 22. Meri Krismas.

Fɔ lɔng tɛm, nia midulnɛt, we dɛn dɔn pul di ship in riga dɛn las wok, ɛn afta dɛn dɔn pul di Pequod kɔmɔt na di wɔf, ɛn afta di Charity we bin de tink ɔltɛm dɔn kam ɔf insay wan wɛl-bot, wit in las gift—na nɛt-kap fɔ Stubb, di sɛkɔn patna, in brɔda-in-law, ɛn wan spay Baybul fɔ di stiwɔd—afta ɔl dis, di tu Kaptɛn dɛn, Pɛlɛg ɛn Bildad, we kɔmɔt na di kebin, ɛn tɔn to di chif mɛt, Pɛlɛg se:

“Naw, Mista Stabɔk, yu shɔ se ɔltin rayt? Kpɛntin Eab dɔn rɛdi ɔl—jɔs tɔk to am—natin nɔ de fɔ gɛt frɔm di sho, eh? Wɛl, kɔl ɔl yu an, den. Muster ’em aft here—blast ’em!”

Bildad bin tɔk se: “Nɔ nid fɔ tɔk bad bad wɔd dɛn, ilɛksɛf i rɔsh, Pɛlɛg, bɔt i nɔ nid fɔ de wit yu, padi Stabɔk, ɛn du wetin wi tɛl wi fɔ du.”

Aw naw! Na ya we dɛn bigin fɔ travul, Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg ɛn Kpɛntin Bildad bin de go am wit ay an na di kwata-dɛk, jɔs lɛk se dɛn fɔ bi jɔyn kɔmanda dɛn na si, ɛn bak to ɔl di wan dɛn we de apia na di pɔt. Ɛn, as fɔ Kpɛntin Eab, dɛn nɔ bin si ɛni sayn fɔ am yet; nain, dem se i de na di kabin. Bɔt afta dat, di aidia na bin, se in prezɛns nɔ bin nid ɛni we fɔ mek di ship wej, ɛn stiar am fayn fayn wan fɔ go na di si. Fɔ tru, as dat nɔto bin in rayt biznɛs atɔl, bɔt na di payɔnia in yon; ɛn as i nɔ bin dɔn wɛl yet—na so dɛn se—so, Kpɛntin Eab bin de dɔŋ. Ɛn ɔl dis bin tan lɛk se na nɔmal tin; mɔ as insay di biznɛs savis bɔku kapten dɛn nɔ kin ɛva sho dɛnsɛf na di dɛk fɔ lɔng tɛm afta dɛn dɔn es di anka, bɔt dɛn kin de oba di kabin tebul, gɛt fayvɛl gladi gladi wit dɛn shore padi dɛn, bifo dɛn kɔmɔt na di ship fɔ gud wit di payɔt.

Bɔt bɔku chans nɔ bin de fɔ tink bɔt di tin, bikɔs Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg bin dɔn gɛt layf naw. I bin tan lɛk se na in de du bɔku pan di tɔk ɛn kɔmand, ɛn nɔto Bildad.

As di man dɛn we de wok na di bot bin de te na di men mast, i ala se: “Aft here, you sons of bachelors.” “Mista Stabɔk, drayv dɛn biɛn.”

“Nak di tɛnt de!”—na di nɛks ɔda. As a bin hint bifo, dis wɛlbon mak nɔ bin ɛva pitch pas na pɔt; ɛn we dɛn bin de na di Pequod, fɔ tati ia, dɛn bin no gud gud wan se di ɔda fɔ nak di tɛnt na di nɛks tin fɔ es di anka ɔp.

“Man di kapstan! Blɔd ɛn tɛnda!—jɔmp!”—na di nɛks kɔmand, ɛn di kru bin rɔn fɔ di hanspayk dɛn.

Naw we dɛn de gɛt ɔnda wej, di steshɔn we di payɔnia kin jɔs de na di fɔs pat pan di ship. Ɛn na ya Bildad, we, wit Pɛlɛg, ilɛksɛf dɛn no am, apat frɔm in ɔda ɔfisa dɛn, na bin wan pan di laysens payɔt dɛn na di pɔt—dɛn bin de saspek am se i mek insɛf bi payɔt fɔ mek i go sev di Nantucket payɔt-fi to ɔl di ship dɛn we i bin de wɔri bɔt, bikɔs i nɔ ɛva payɔt ɛni ɔda kraft—a se, dɛn go si Bildad naw aktif wan fɔ luk oba di bɔw fɔ di ship we de kam nia am ankɔ, ɛn wan wan tɛm dɛn de siŋ wetin bin tan lɛk wan bad bad tik we dɛn mek wit sam, fɔ mek di an dɛn gladi fɔ di windlas, we bin de ala sɔm kayn kɔrɛkt wan bɔt di gyal pikin dɛn na Bubul Ali, wit ɔl in at. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, nɔto tri dez bifo dat, Bildad bin dɔn tɛl dɛn se dɛn nɔ go alaw ɛni dɔti siŋ dɛn na di Pequod, mɔ fɔ mek dɛn nɔ wej; ɛn Charity, in sista, bin dɔn put wan smɔl kɔpi we dɛn pik fɔ Watts na ɛni siman in bet.

Insay da tɛm de, we Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg bin de oba di ɔda pat na di ship, i bin rip ɛn swɛ fɔ go biɛn di we we bin de mek i fred pas ɔl. I bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ a tink se i go sink di ship bifo dɛn ebul fɔ grap di anka; a nɔ bin want a stɔp na mi anspayk, ɛn tɛl Kwik fɔ du di sem tin, tink bɔt di prɔblɛm dɛn we wi ɔl tu bin de rɔn, we wi bigin fɔ travul wit da kayn dɛbul de fɔ payɔt. Bɔt a bin de kɔrej misɛf wit di tink se insay Bildad we de wɔship Gɔd, dɛn go fɛn sɔm sev, pan ɔl we in sɛvin ɔndrɛd ɛn sɛvinti sɛvin lay; we a fil wan shap shap pɔk na mi biɛn, ɛn tɔn rawnd, a bin fred bad bad wan fɔ di aparishin we Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg bin de apia we i de pul in leg kɔmɔt nia mi wantɛm wantɛm. Dat na bin mi fɔs kik.

“Na so dɛn de hev insay di machɛnt savis?” i bin de ala. “Spring, yu ship-ed;spring, ɛn brok yu bakbon! Wetin mek una nɔ de spring, a se, una ɔl—spring! Quohog! spring, yu chap wit di rɛd wisk; spring de, Skɔtish-kap; spring, yu grɛn pant. Spring, a se, una ɔl, ɛn spring una yay kɔmɔt!” Ɛn we i de tɔk so, i bin de muf along di windlas, ya ɛn de yuz in leg rili fri wan, we Bildad we nɔ bin de ambɔg bin de kɔntinyu fɔ lid go wit in sam. Tink se mi, Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg go mɔs dɔn de drink sɔntin tide.

Las las di anka bin dɔn ɔp, dɛn bin dɔn sɛt di sel dɛn, ɛn wi bin de glid fɔ go. Na bin shɔt Krismas we kol; ɛn as di shɔt de we de na di nɔt bin de miks to nɛt, wi bin si se wi bin dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ wi brayt pan di si we kin kol, we in sprɛy we kin friz kin mek wi insay ays, lɛk aw wi kin wɛr klos we dɛn dɔn polish. Di lɔng lɔng row dɛn we gɛt tit dɛn we bin de na di wɔl dɛn bin de shayn pan di mun layt; ɛn lɛk di wayt ayvri tut dɛn we sɔm big big ɛlifant dɛn kin gɛt, big big ays dɛn we kin kɔba bin de dipen frɔm di bɔw dɛn.

Lank Bildad, as payɔt, bin de bifo di fɔs wach, ɛn ɛva ɛn anon, as di ol kraft de dip dip insay di grɔn si, ɛn sɛn di frɔst we de shek shek ɔlsay oba am, ɛn di briz dɛn de ala, ɛn di kɔd de ring, dɛn yɛri in stedi not dɛn,—

      “Swit fil dɛn pas di wata we de swel, .
Stand dres wit liv grin.
So to di Ju pipul dɛn, di ol Kenan bin tinap, .
We Jɔdan bin de rol bitwin.”

Dɛn swit wɔd dɛn de nɔ bin ɛva saund swit to mi pas da tɛm de. Dɛn bin ful-ɔp wit op ɛn frut. Pan ɔl dis frigid winta nɛt na di Atlantik we bin de mek bɔku bɔku pipul dɛn, pan ɔl we mi fut wet ɛn wet wet jakit, i bin stil, i bin tan lɛk to mi, bɔku fayn fayn ples dɛn we a bin dɔn kip; ɛn mead ɛn glades so vernal sote go, dat di gras we di spring shot ɔp, we nɔ trowe, we nɔ wilt, de kɔntinyu fɔ de na midul sɔm.

Las las wi bin gɛt da kayn chans de, dat dɛn nɔ bin nid di tu payɔnia dɛn igen. Di stɔt sel-bot we bin de wit wi bigin fɔ rɔn nia wi.

I bin rili want fɔ no mɔ ɛn i nɔ bin mek i nɔ gladi, aw Pɛlɛg ɛn Bildad bin afɛkt dis tɛm, mɔ Kpɛntin Bildad. Bikɔs a nɔ lɛk fɔ kɔmɔt, bɔt stil; rili et fɔ kɔmɔt, fɔ gud, wan ship we de go na so lɔng ɛn denja trip—bifo di tu Kep dɛn we gɛt big big briz; wan ship we dɛn put sɔm tawzin dɔla dɛn we i bin dɔn wok tranga wan fɔ du; wan ship, we wan ol pɔsin we bin de wok wit am na di ship bin de go as kapten; wan man we dɔn ol lɛk am, wan tɛm bak we i bigin fɔ mit ɔl di fred dɛn we di jaw we nɔ gɛt sɔri-at de mek; nɔ lɛk fɔ se gud-bay to sɔntin so ɔlsay we ful-ɔp wit ɛvri intres to am,—poor ol Bildad bin de fɔ lɔng tɛm; bin de waka na di dɛk wit strid dɛn we bin de wɔri; rɔn go dɔŋ insay di kebin fɔ tɔk ɔda fayvɛl wɔd de; i kam bak na di dɛk, ɛn luk to di say we di briz de blo; bin de luk to di wata we big ɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnd, we na di Istan Kɔntinɛnt dɛn we de fa fawe we pɔsin nɔ de si nɔmɔ de rawnd am; luk to di land; luk ɔp; luk rayt ɛn lɛft; luk ɔlsay ɛn nɔsay; ɛn las las, i kɔyl wan rop wit mashin pan in pin, i ol wan strɔng Pɛlɛg in an wit kɔnvulsiv wan, ɛn ol wan lantan ɔp, fɔ smɔl tɛm i tinap de luk in fes wit hero, as i se, “Bɔt pan ɔl dat, padi Pɛlɛg, a kin tinap fɔ am; yes, a ebul.”

As fɔ Pɛlɛg insɛf, i tek am mɔ lɛk pɔsin we sabi bɔt sayɛns; bɔt fɔ ɔl in filɔsofi, wan kray wata bin de shayn na in yay, we di lantan kam tu nia. Ɛn insɛf nɔ bin de rɔn smɔl frɔm wan kebin to di ɔda dɛk—naw wan wɔd de dɔŋ, ɛn naw wan wɔd wit Starbuck, di chif mɛt.

Bɔt, las las, i tɔn to in kɔmpin, wit wan las kayn luk bɔt am,—“Kpɛntin Bildad—kam, ol shipmet, wi fɔ go. Bak di men-yad de! Bot ahoy! Stand bay fɔ kam nia nia, naw! Tek tɛm, tek tɛm!—kam, Bildad, bɔbɔ—say yu las. Lak to yu, Stabɔk—lak to yu, Mista Stɔb—lak to yu, yu, lak to yu, yu, laki to yu, laki to yu, laki to yu, Mista Stubb—lak to yu, Mista Stubb—lak to yu, Mista Stubb—lak to yu, Mista Stubb—lak to ye, Mista Flask—gud-bay ɛn gud lak to una ɔl—ɛn dis de tri ia a go gɛt wan hot sup fɔ smok fɔ una na ol Nantucket ɛn away!”

“Gɔd blɛs una, ɛn mek una de kip in oli ples, man dɛm,” na so ol Bildad bin de grɔmbul, i bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ i nɔ gɛt wanwɔd. “A op se una go gɛt fayn wɛda naw, so dat i nɔ go te igen, Kpɛntin Eab go de muf midul una—na fayn san na ɔl wetin i nid, ɛn una go gɛt bɔku pan dɛn na di tropik voyage we una de go. Una tek tɛm we una de ɔnt, una mates. Una nɔ stav di bot dɛn we nɔ nid, una harpooneers; gud wayt sida plank de rayz ful tri pasɛnt. insay di ia. Nɔ fɔgɛt una prea, either tink.

“Kam, kam, Kpɛntin Bildad, stɔp fɔ palav,—we!” ɛn wit dat, Pɛlɛg mek i rɔn go na di sayd kwik kwik wan, ɛn dɛn ɔl tu drɔp insay di bot.

Ship ɛn bot bin difrɛn; di kol, damp briz we bin de blo na nɛt bin de blo bitwin; wan gɔl we de ala bin flay oba di ed; di tu hul dɛn bin de rɔl bad bad wan; wi gi tri ebi ebi gladi gladi, ɛn blaynd wan fɔdɔm lɛk fate insay di wan ol Atlantik.





CHAPTER 23. Di Li Shoa.

Sɔm chapta dɛn bifo, dɛn bin tɔk bɔt wan Bulkington, wan lɔng, nyuland mariner, we dɛn mit na Nyu Bɛdfɔd na di in.

We pan da winta nɛt de we bin de shek shek, di Pɛkud bin push in blɛsin bɔw dɛn insay di kol bad bad wef dɛn, udat a fɔ si tinap na in ed pas Bulkington! A bin luk wit sɔri-at ɛn fred pan di man, we insay midul winta jɔs land frɔm 4 ia denja voyage, kin so nɔ rɛst fɔ push ɔf bak fɔ stil ɔda tɛm we big big briz. I bin tan lɛk se di land de bɔn na in fut. Di tin dɛn we wɔndaful pas ɔl na di tin dɛn we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ tɔk bɔt; dip mɛmori dɛn nɔ de gi ɛni epitaf; dis chapta we gɛt siks inch na di grev we nɔ gɛt ston na Bulkington. Lɛ a jɔs se i bin du wit am lɛk di ship we di big big briz bin de trowe, we de drayv bad bad wan along di land we gɛt briz. Di pɔt go fain gi sɔpɔt; di pɔt de sɔri fɔ wi; na di pɔt na sef, kɔmfɔt, hearthstone, sup, wam blanket, padi, ɔl dat na kain to wi mortalities. Bɔt insay da big big briz de, di pɔt, di land, na di bad bad tin we da ship de pan denja; i fɔ flay ɔl di ɔspitul dɛn; wan tɔch land, pan ɔl we i bin jɔs de it di kil, go mek i shek tru ɛn tru. Wit ol her might she krawd ol seil off shore; we i du dat, i kin fɛt ‘gens di sem briz we fain go blo am fɔ go na os; de luk fɔ ɔl di lashed si in landlɛsnɛs bak; fɔ rɔnawe fɔ rɔnawe fɔ rɔsh fɔ go pan denja; in wangren padi in ɛnimi we pas ɔlman!

Yu no naw, Bulkington? I tan lɛk se una de si smɔl smɔl bɔt da trut de we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ bia wit; dat ɔl di dip, rial tinkin na jɔs di sol we de tray tranga wan fɔ kip di opin indipɛndɛns fɔ in si; we di briz we de blo pas ɔl na ɛvin ɛn di wɔl de plan fɔ trowe am na di si we de mek pipul dɛn de trit am ɛn we tan lɛk slev?

Bɔt jɔs lɛk aw insay landlɛs nɔmɔ, di trut we ay pas ɔl de, we nɔ gɛt shore, we nɔ gɛt ɛnd lɛk Gɔd—so, i bɛtɛ fɔ lɛ pɔsin day insay da ala ala we nɔ gɛt ɛnd, pas fɔ rɔn pan di wata we nɔ gɛt glori, ilɛksɛf dat na bin sef! Fɔ we tan lɛk wom, so, oh! we go kray fɔ kray fɔ land! Terrors of di bad bad tin dɛn! ɔl dis pen we pɔsin de fil na fɔ natin? Tek at, tek at, O Bulkington! Bia yu grimly, demigod! Up frɔm di sprɛy fɔ yu ocean-peshing—stret ɔp, de jomp yu apotheosis!





CHAPTER 24. Di Advoketi.

As mi ɛn Kwik dɔn bigin fɔ du dis biznɛs fɔ kech wɛl naw fayn fayn wan; ɛn as dis biznɛs fɔ kech wɛl dɔn kam fɔ bi sɔm kayn we we pipul dɛn we de wok na di land dɔn tek am as sɔntin we nɔ gɛt poem ɛn we nɔ gɛt gud nem; so, a ɔl de wɔri fɔ mek una, una we de wok na di land, biliv di injɔstis we dɛn de du to wi we de fɛn wɛl.

Fɔs, dɛn kin tek am se i nɔ impɔtant fɔ mek wi no di tru tin, se pan pipul dɛn at ɔl, dɛn nɔ kin tek di biznɛs fɔ kech wɛl pan wan lɛvul wit wetin dɛn kɔl di fri wok dɛn. If dɛn introduks wan strenja insay ɛni difrɛn mɛtropɔliti sosayti, i go bɔt smɔl advans di jenɛral opinion bɔt in mɛrit, if dɛn prɛzɛnt am to di kɔmni as harpooneer, lɛ wi se; ɛn if insay ɛmulashɔn fɔ di nevl ɔfisa dɛn i fɔ ad di initial dɛn SWF (Sperm Whale Fishery) to in visitin kad, dɛn go tek da kayn we de as prɛ-eminently presuming ɛn ridiculous.

Wi nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se wan men rizin we mek di wɔl nɔ de gri fɔ ɔnɔ wi wɛlman dɛn, na dis: dɛn tink se, if i fayn, wi wok tan lɛk wan kayn biznɛs we de kil animal; ɛn we wi de du am tranga wan, ɔlkayn dɔti tin dɛn de rawnd wi. Butchers wi bi, dat na tru. Bɔt bɔsa dɛn, bak, ɛn bɔsa dɛn we gɛt di badge we gɛt blɔd pas ɔl, dɔn bi ɔl di Mashal Kɔmanda dɛn we di wɔl kin gladi ɔltɛm fɔ ɔnɔ. Ɛn as fɔ di tin we dɛn se wi biznɛs nɔ klin, i nɔ go te igen una go bigin fɔ tink bɔt sɔm tin dɛn we wi nɔ no te naw, ɛn we, pan ɔl, go win di sperm wɛl-ship at ɔl bitwin di tin dɛn we klin pas ɔl na dis tidi wɔl. Bɔt ivin fɔ gi di chaj we dɛn de tɔk bɔt fɔ bi tru; us disɔda slip dɛk dɛn na wɛl-ship we dɛn kin kɔmpia to di kariɔn we nɔ pɔsibul fɔ tɔk bɔt na dɛn fɛt-fɛt ples dɛn we bɔku sojaman dɛn kin kam bak fɔ drink insay ɔl di uman dɛn prez? Ɛn if di aidia fɔ peril so mɔ de mek di pɔpul kɔnsetin fɔ di sojaman in wok bɛtɛ; lɛ a mek una no se bɔku pan di vetɛna dɛn we dɔn fri fɔ mach te i rich na batri, go bak kwik kwik wan we di sperm wɛl in big tel apia, ɛn i go mek di briz we de oba in ed, tɔn to ɛdi. Fɔ wetin na di fred we mɔtalman kin ɔndastand we yu kɔmpia am to di fred ɛn wɔndaful tin dɛn we Gɔd kin mek we gɛt sɔntin fɔ du wit dɛnsɛf!

Bɔt, pan ɔl we di wɔl de skaut wi we de fɛn wɛl, bɔt stil i de gi wi di dip ɔma we wi nɔ no; yes, wan ɔl-bɔku adoreshɔn! bikɔs klos to ɔl di taper, lamp, ɛn kandul dɛn we de bɔn rawnd di wɔl, de bɔn, lɛk aw i bin de bifo bɔku bɔku oli ples dɛn, fɔ mek wi gɛt glori!

Bɔt luk dis tin insay ɔda layt dɛn; wej am wit ɔlkayn skel; si wetin wi wɛlman dɛn bi, ɛn wi dɔn bi.

Wetin mek di Dɔch pipul dɛn we bin de insay De Witt in tɛm bin gɛt admiral dɛn we dɛn bin de yuz fɔ kech wɛl? Wetin mek Luis di Sɛvin bin du dat. na Frans, pan in yon mɔni, fit ship dɛn we de kech wɛl frɔm Dunkirk, ɛn invayt sɔm ɔ tu famili dɛn we kɔmɔt na wi yon ayland we nem Nantucket fɔ kam na da tɔŋ de wit ɔl in at? Wetin mek Briten bitwin di ia 1750 ɛn 1788 bin pe to in wɛlman dɛn wit bɔku bɔku mɔni we pas £1,000,000? Ɛn las wan, aw i kam se wi wɛlman dɛn na Amɛrika naw pas ɔl di ɔda wɛlman dɛn we gɛt band na di wɔl; sel wan nevi we gɛt pas sɛvin ɔndrɛd bot dɛn; ettin tawzin man dɛn bin de wok fɔ dɛn; i kin yuz 4,000,000 dɔla ɛvri ia; di ship dɛn we gɛt valyu, di tɛm we dɛn de go, $20,000,000! ɛn ɛvri ia we wi de import insay wi haba dɛn wan avɛst we dɛn dɔn avɛst fayn fayn wan we na $7,000,000. Aw ɔl dis kin kam, if sɔntin nɔ de we de mek pɔsin tink bɔt wɛl?

Bɔt dis nɔto di af; luk bak.

A fri fɔ tɔk, se di kɔsmopɔlit filɔsofa nɔ go ebul, fɔ in layf, pɔynt wan singl pisful inflɔɛns, we insay di las siksti ia dɔn ɔpreshɔn mɔ pɔtnɛshɛl pan di wan ol brayt wɔl, we dɛn tek insay wan agrɛgat, pas di ay ɛn pawaful biznɛs fɔ kech wɛl. Fɔ di rɛspɛkt we pɔsin gɛt fɔ kech wɛl, a go fain advans natin pas tru tru tru tin dɛn. Bɔt afta we i dɔn fɛt in tru tin dɛn, wan advatayz we fɔ stɔp ɔltin fɔ tink bɔt sɔntin we nɔto fɔ mek i nɔ mek sɛns, we go tɛl am fayn fayn wan bɔt in kɔz—da kayn advatayz de, yu nɔ tink se dɛn go blem am?

CHAPTER 26. Nayt ɛn Skwaya.

Di men pɔsin we di Pequod bin de wit na bin Starbuck, we kɔmɔt na Nantucket, ɛn we kɔmɔt na Kwayɛt. I bin lɔng ɛn i bin rili lɛk am, ɛn pan ɔl we dɛn bɔn am na say we gɛt ays, i bin tan lɛk se i dɔn adap fayn fɔ bia we di ples wam, ɛn in bɔdi bin at lɛk biskit we dɛn dɔn bek tu tɛm. If dɛn kɛr am go na di Indiz, in layf blɔd nɔ go pwɛl lɛk bɔtul ale. I go mɔs dɔn bɔn insay sɔm tɛm we dray ɛn angri bin de, ɔ pan wan pan dɛn fast de dɛn we in stet gɛt nem fɔ. Yu go si am de shayn na di an we de yuz pik ɔ drayv spayk; dat dimokrasi digniti we, pan ɔl an, de shayn we nɔ gɛt ɛnd frɔm Gɔd; Na insɛf sɛf! Di big Gɔd absoliut! Di sɛnta ɛn di say we ɔl di dimokrasi de! In ɔlsay, wi divayn ikwal!

So, if a min se pipul dɛn we de wok na di si pas ɔlman, ɛn pipul dɛn we de tɔn agens di gɔvmɛnt ɛn pipul dɛn we dɔn lɛf fɔ wok, afta dis a go se a gɛt ay kwaliti dɛn, pan ɔl we dɛn dak; wev rawnd dɛn trajik gudnɛs; if ivin di wan we de kray pas ɔl, sɔntɛm di wan we nɔ gɛt wan rɛspɛkt, pan dɛn ɔl, go es insɛf ɔp sɔntɛnde to di mawnten dɛn we ay pas ɔl; if a go tɔch da wokman in an wit sɔm ethereal layt; if a go spre renbo oba in bad bad san we de dɔn; den agens ɔl mɔtalman kritik dɛn bia mi aut insay am, yu jɔs Spirit fɔ Ikwalti, we dɔn spre wan royal mantle fɔ mɔtalman oba ɔl mi kayn! Bia mi insay am, yu big dimokrasi Gɔd! we nɔ bin gri fɔ di swart kɔvikt, Bunyan, di pale, poɛtik pal; Yu we bin de klos wit lif dɛn we dɛn dɔn hama tu tɛm we dɛn mek wit fayn fayn gold, di an we bin stɔp ɛn po we ol Sɛvantis bin gɛt; Yu we bin pik Andru Jakson frɔm di ston dɛn; we bin trowe am pan wɔ-ɔs; we bin mek tɛnda am ay pas tron! Yu we, insay ɔl Yu pawaful, wɔl mach dɛn, de ɛva kul Yu sɛlɛkɛt champion dɛn frɔm di kiŋ kɔmɔn; bia mi insay de, O Gɔd!





CHAPTER 27. Nayt ɛn Skwaya.

Stubb na bin di sɛkɔn patna. I bin kɔmɔt na Kep Kɔd; ɛn na dat mek, akɔdin to di lokal yus, dɛn bin de kɔl am Kep-Kɔd-man. Wan pɔsin we gladi-go-laki; nɔto pɔsin we want fɔ du sɔntin ɛn we gɛt maynd; tek perils as dɛn de kam wit wan indipɛndɛnt ɛya; ɛn we i bin de insay di kraysis we bin de kam nia di chase, i bin de wok tranga wan, i bin de kol ɛn i bin de gɛda as joynman jɔynman we bin de ɛnjɔy fɔ di ia. I bin de laf gud, izi, ɛn i nɔ bin de tek tɛm, i bin de bifo in wɛl-bot lɛk se di tin we dɛn kin mit we kin kil pas ɔl na jɔs dina, ɛn in kru ɔl bin invayt pipul dɛn fɔ kam. I bin patikyula bɔt di kɔmfyut arenjmɛnt fɔ in pat pan di bot, jɔs lɛk aw wan ol stej-draiva kin tɔk bɔt di fayn fayn we aw in bɔks de. We i bin de nia di wɛl, insay di day-lɔk we di fɛt bin de, i bin de ol in lans we nɔ gɛt sɔri-at kol ɛn ɔf-han, as pɔsin we de wisɛl de tink in hama. I go hum oba in ol rigadig tiun dɛn we i de flank ɛn flank wit di monsta we vɛks pas ɔl. Lɔng yus bin dɔn, fɔ dis Stubb, chenj di jaw fɔ day to wan izi chia. Wetin i bin tink bɔt day sɛf, nɔbɔdi nɔ ebul fɔ tɛl am. If i ɛva tink bɔt am atɔl, kin bi kwɛstyɔn; bɔt, if i ɛva du chans fɔ trowe in maynd da we de afta i dɔn it kɔmfyut dina, i nɔ gɛt wan dawt, lɛk gud selɔ, i tek am fɔ bi wan kayn kɔl fɔ di wach fɔ fɔdɔm ɔp, ɛn bestir dɛnsɛf de, bɔt sɔntin we i go fɛnɔt we i obe di ɔda, ɛn nɔto kwik.

Wetin, sɔntɛm, wit ɔda tin dɛn, mek Stubb bi so izi-go, man we nɔ de fred, so gladi gladi fɔ trud go wit di lod fɔ layf na wan wɔl we ful-ɔp wit grev pedla dɛn, ɔl butu na grɔn wit dɛn pak; wetin ɛp fɔ briŋ da gud kɔmɛnt de we i bin de mek lɛk se i nɔ de wɔship Gɔd; dat tin go mɔs dɔn bi in paip. Bikɔs, lɛk in nos, in shɔt blak smɔl paip na bin wan pan di tin dɛn we i bin de du ɔltɛm na in fes. Yu go dɔn tink se i go kɔmɔt na in bɔnk if i nɔ gɛt in nos jɔs lɛk aw if i nɔ gɛt in paip. I bin de kip wan ol row paip dɛn de rɛdi fɔ lod, we dɛn stɔp na wan rɛk, we i izi fɔ mek in an ebul fɔ rich; ɛn, ɛnitɛm we i tɔn insay, i kin smok dɛn ɔl wan afta di ɔda, ɛn layt wan frɔm di ɔda wan te di chapta dɔn; dɔn dɛn de lod dɛn bak fɔ mek dɛn rɛdi nyu wan. Bikɔs, we Stubb drɛs, instead fɔ put in leg fɔs insay in trayz, i put in paip insay in mɔt.

A se dis kɔntinyu fɔ smok fɔ dɔn bi wan rizin, at ɔl, fɔ in pekul dispɔzishɔn; bikɔs ɔlman no se dis briz we de na dis wɔl, ilɛksɛf i de na di si ɔ i de na di wata, i rili gɛt di bad bad tin dɛn we di pipul dɛn we nɔ gɛt nɔmba fɔ day we dɛn de blo am, de sɔfa; ɛn lɛk insay di tɛm we kɔlera bin de, sɔm pipul dɛn kin go rawnd wit wan hankɛf we gɛt kafɔt na dɛn mɔt; so, semweso, agens ɔl di prɔblɛm dɛn we kin apin we pɔsin kin day, di smok we Stub bin de smok bin fɔ dɔn de wok lɛk wan kayn tin we de kil di sik.

Di tɔd man we dɛn bin mared na bin Flask, we kɔmɔt na Tisbɔri, we de na Mata in Vayn Gad. Wan shɔt, stout, ruddy yɔŋ felo, we bin rili fɛt wit wɛl, we sɔm kayn we bin tan lɛk se i tink se di big big liviathan dɛn bin dɔn ambɔg am insɛf ɛn frɔm dɛn gret gret granpa dɛn; ɛn na dat mek i bin bi wan kayn pɔynt fɔ ɔnɔ wit am, fɔ pwɛl dɛn ɛnitɛm we dɛn mit dɛn. So i bin lɔs kpatakpata fɔ ɔl di sɛns fɔ rɛspɛkt fɔ di bɔku wɔndaful tin dɛn we dɛn gɛt fɔ du wit dɛn majestic bulk ɛn mystic ways; ɛn so day to ɛnitin lɛk fɔ fred ɛni pɔsibul denja we yu mit dɛn; dat insay in po opinion, di wɔndaful wɛl na bin jɔs wan spɛshal maws we dɛn dɔn mek big, ɔ at ɔl wata-rat, we i bin nid fɔ jɔs rɔn smɔl ɛn yuz sɔm tɛm ɛn trɔbul smɔl fɔ mek i ebul fɔ kil ɛn bɔyl. Dis ignorant, unconscious fredlessness of his bin mek i waggish smɔl pan di mata bɔt wɛl; i bin de fala dɛn fish ya fɔ ɛnjɔy am; ɛn fɔ travul fɔ tri ia rawnd Kep Hɔn na bin jɔs wan fayn fayn jok we bin las fɔ da lɔng tɛm de. Jɔs lɛk aw dɛn kin sheb kapɛnta in nel dɛn to nel dɛn we dɛn mek ɛn nel dɛn we dɛn kɔt; so mɔtalman kin sheb di sem we. Lil Flask na bin wan pan di wan dɛn we dɛn bin mek; mek fɔ klin tayt ɛn las fɔ lɔng tɛm. Dɛn kɔl am King-Post we bin de na di Pequod; bikɔs, pan in fɔm, dɛn kin kɔmpia am fayn fayn wan to di shɔt, skwea tik dɛn we pipul dɛn we de kech wɛl na Aktik sabi wit da nem de; ɛn we bay we dɛn yuz bɔku sayd timba dɛn we de shayn we dɛn put insay am, de sav fɔ mek di ship nɔ gɛt di ays kɔnkyushɔn dɛn we dɛn si dɛn de we de bit dɛn kin gɛt.

Naw dɛn tri man dɛn ya—Stabuck, Stubb, ɛn Flask, na bin impɔtant man dɛn. Na dɛn it bin we bay universal prɛskrishɔn kɔmand tri pan di Pequod in bot dɛn as edman dɛn. Insay da big big ɔda fɛt we go mɔs bi se Kpɛntin Eab go gɛda in sojaman dɛn fɔ kam dɔŋ pan di wɛl dɛn, dɛn tri edman dɛn ya bin tan lɛk kapten dɛn fɔ di kɔmpin dɛn. Ɔ, bikɔs dɛn bin gɛt dɛn lɔng lɔng spia dɛn we dɛn kin yuz fɔ kech wɛl, dɛn bin tan lɛk tri pipul dɛn we dɛn kin pik fɔ lansa; ivin lɛk aw di wan dɛn we de ple hapun na bin pipul dɛn we de flay jawlin.

Ɛn bikɔs insay dis fishaman we bɔku pipul dɛn sabi, ɛni patna ɔ edman, lɛk wan Gotik Nayt we bin de trade, kin de wit in bot-stia ɔ harpuoneer ɔltɛm, we insay sɔm kɔnjɔkt dɛn kin gi am fresh lans, we di fɔs wan dɔn twist bad bad wan, ɔ ɛlb we dɛn atak am; ɛn pan ɔl dat, as i jɔs de bitwin di tu, wan tayt padi biznɛs ɛn padi biznɛs; so i jɔs mit, se na dis ples wi sɛt dɔŋ udat na di Pequod in harpooneers, ɛn to us edman ɛni wan pan dɛn bi.

Fɔs, na Kwik, we Stabɔk, we na di chif patna, bin dɔn pik fɔ in skwaya. Bɔt pipul dɛn dɔn ɔlrɛdi no Kwik.

Di nɛks wan na bin Tashtego, we na wan Indian we nɔ miks ɛn we kɔmɔt na Gay Head, we na di ples we de na di wɛst pat na Mata in Vayn Gad, usay di las wan we lɛf pan wan vilej we gɛt rɛd man dɛn stil de, we dɔn de gi bɔku pan in ayland we de nia Nantucket fɔ lɔng tɛm wit bɔku pan in harpun dɛn we gɛt maynd pas ɔlman. Insay di fisha, dɛn kin go wit di jenɛrik nem we na Gay-Headers. Tashtego in lɔng, slim, sable ia, in ay chɛst bon dɛn, ɛn blak rawnd yay dɛn—fɔ wan Indian, we kɔmɔt na di Ist pan dɛn big big wan, bɔt Antaktik insay dɛn fes we de shayn—ɔl dis bin inof fɔ proklaym am as inheritor fɔ di unvitiated blɔd fɔ dɛn praud wɔriɔ ɔnta dɛn, we, fɔ fɛn di big Nyu Ingland mus, bin dɔn skɔ, bo in an, di aborijinal fɔrɛst dɛn we de na di men wan. Bɔt i nɔ bin de snuf igen na di tray we di wayl animal dɛn we de na di bush bin de waka, Tashtego bin de ɔntin naw insay di we aw di big big wɛl dɛn we de na di si bin de waka; di harpun we nɔ de mek mistek fɔ di pikin fit fɔ tek di ples fɔ di aro we nɔ de mek mistek we di sires dɛn gɛt. Fɔ luk di tawny brawn na in lithe snaky limbs, yu go dɔn klos fɔ gi kredit to di lay lay biliv dɛn we sɔm pan di Puritan dɛn we bin de trade bin gɛt, ɛn af-af biliv se dis wayl Indian na di pikin fɔ di Prins fɔ di Pawa dɛn na di Eya. Tashtego na bin Stubb di sɛkɔn mate in skwaya.

Di tɔd pan di wan dɛn we bin de mek harpun na bin Daggoo, we na wan big big nigro-savage we bin blak lɛk kol, ɛn i bin gɛt wan tred we tan lɛk layɔn—na Ahasuerus fɔ si. Tu gold hoop dɛn bin de na in yes, we so big dat di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot bin de kɔl dɛn ring-bolt, ɛn dɛn go tɔk bɔt fɔ mek di halyard dɛn we de ɔp di sel tayt to dɛn. We Daggoo bin yɔŋ, i bin dɔn gri fɔ sɛn ship pan wan we we de kech wɛl, ɛn i bin de ledɔm na wan bay we pipul dɛn nɔ de na in yon si na in yon si. Ɛn bikɔs dɛn nɔ ɛva go ɛnisay na di wɔl pas na Afrika, Nantucket, ɛn di pegan haba dɛn we pipul dɛn we de fɛt wɛl kin go; ɛn naw dɛn dɔn de lid fɔ bɔku ia di bold layf fɔ di fishaman dɛn na di ship dɛn we di ɔna dɛn de we nɔ kɔmɔn fɔ pe atɛnshɔn to us kayn man dɛn dɛn de ship; Daggoo bin kip ɔl in barbaric gud kwaliti dɛn, ɛn tinap lɛk jrafa, i bin de muv rawnd di dɛk dɛn wit ɔl di pomp we siks fit fayv insay in sɔks. Wan bɔdi bin ɔmbul we i luk am ɔp; ɛn wan wayt man we tinap bifo am bin tan lɛk se wan wayt flag kam fɔ beg fɔ mek dɛn nɔ gri fɔ fɛt wan fɔt. Dis impyɔrial nigro, Ahasuerus Daggoo, bin want fɔ no mɔ, na bin di Skwaya fɔ smɔl Flask, we bin tan lɛk chɛs-man nia am. As fɔ di rɛsɛdyu fɔ di Pequod in kɔmni, lɛ dɛn se, na di tɛm naw nɔto wan pan tu pan di bɔku bɔku tawzin man dɛn bifo di mast we dɛn bin de yuz fɔ di Amɛrikan wɛl fisha, na Amɛrika pipul dɛn bɔn, pan ɔl we na lɛk ɔl di ɔfisa dɛn bɔn. Na ya na di sem tin wit di Amɛrikan wɛl fisha biznɛs lɛk di Amɛrikan ami ɛn di sojaman ɛn biznɛs nevi dɛn, ɛn di injinɛri fɔs dɛn we dɛn yuz fɔ bil di Amɛrikan Kanal ɛn Rel Rod dɛn. Na di sem tin, a se, bikɔs insay ɔl dɛn kes ya di indiɛn Amɛrikin fri wan de gi di bren, di ɔda pat na di wɔl as fri wan de gi di mɔsul dɛn. Nɔto smɔl nɔmba pan dɛn wɛl siman dɛn ya de na di Azores, usay di wan dɛn we de kech wɛl na Nantucket we de go na do kin tɔch bɔku tɛm fɔ mek dɛn kru dɛn bɔku frɔm di pipul dɛn we nɔ de wok tranga wan na dɛn sho dɛn de we gɛt rɔk. Semweso, di Grinland wɛlman dɛn we bin de kɔmɔt na Hul ɔ Lɔndɔn, bin put insay di Shetland Ayland dɛn, fɔ gɛt di ful kɔmplimɛnt fɔ dɛn kru. We dɛn pas fɔ go na os, dɛn kin drɔp dɛn de bak. Aw i bi, notin nɔ de fɔ tɛl, bɔt i tan lɛk se di pipul dɛn na Ayland de mek di bɛst wɛlman dɛn. Dɛn na bin klos to ɔl Ayland pipul dɛn na di Pequod, Isolatoes bak, a kɔl dɛn kayn pipul ya, nɔ gri wit di kɔmɔn kɔntinɛnt fɔ man dɛn, bɔt ɛni Isolato we de liv na wan sɛpret kɔntinɛnt fɔ in yon. Yet naw, federated along wan kil, na bin big set dis Isolatoes bin bi! Wan Anacharsis Clootz deputeshɔn frɔm ɔl di ayland dɛn na di si, ɛn ɔl di ɛnd dɛn na di wɔl, we bin de wit Ol Eab na di Pequod fɔ lay di wɔl in grɔj bifo da bar de usay nɔto tumɔs pan dɛn ɛva kɔmɔt bak. Blak Lil Pip—i nɔ ɛva du am—o, nɔ! i bin go bifo. Po Alabama bɔbɔ! Na di grim Pequod in fɔkas, una go ere long si am, de bit in tamburin; bifo di tɛm we go de sote go, we dɛn sɛn am fɔ go na di big kwata-dɛk we de ɔp, dɛn bin tɛl am fɔ atak wit enjɛl dɛn, ɛn bit in tamburin wit glori; kɔl wan kɔvayd ya, hail wan hiro de!





CHAPTER 28. Eab.

Fɔ sɔm dez afta dɛn kɔmɔt na Nantucket, dɛn nɔ bin si natin ɔp di hatch dɛn bɔt Kpɛntin Eab. Di mared pipul dɛn bin de fil fri dɛnsɛf ɔltɛm na di wach dɛn, ɛn fɔ ɛnitin we dɛn go si se difrɛn frɔm dat, i bin tan lɛk se na dɛn nɔmɔ na di kɔmanda dɛn na di ship; na dɛn nɔmɔ sɔm tɛm dɛn kin kɔmɔt frɔm di kabin wit ɔda ɔda tin dɛn we kin apin wantɛm wantɛm ɛn we kin mek pipul dɛn want fɔ du sɔntin, dat afta ɔl i bin klia se dɛn bɔt dɛn kin kɔmand fɔ bi pɔsin we dɛn want. Yɛs, dɛn suprɛm masta ɛn dikteta bin de de, pan ɔl we te naw nɔbɔdi nɔ bin de si am wit ɛni yay we dɛn nɔ alaw fɔ go insay di say we oli naw na di kebin.

Ɛnitɛm we a de go ɔp di dɛk frɔm mi wach dɛn we de dɔŋ, a kin luk biɛn wantɛm wantɛm fɔ mak if ɛni strenj fes de; bikɔs mi fɔs vayg wɔri we a bin de tɔch di kapten we a nɔ bin no, we naw de na di say we nɔbɔdi nɔ de na di si, bin kam fɔ bi lɛk se a de ambɔg am. Dis bin strenj wan heightened by di ragged Ilayja in diabolical incoherences we bin de kam bak to mi we a nɔ invayt, wit wan subtil ɛnaji we a nɔ bin ebul fɔ tink bɔt bifo. Bɔt a nɔ bin ebul fɔ bia wit dɛn, jɔs lɛk aw a kin bia wit ɔda we dɛn, i bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ a rɛdi fɔ smayl to di solemn whimsicalities of dat outlandish prɔfɛt na di wharves. Bɔt ilɛk wetin na fɔ fred ɔ nɔ izi fɔ mi—fɔ kɔl am so—we a bin de fil, bɔt stil ɛnitɛm we a kam fɔ luk rawnd mi na di ship, i bin tan lɛk se i nɔ gri wit ɔl di tin dɛn we a fɔ du fɔ valyu dɛn kayn filin dɛn de. Bikɔs pan ɔl we di harpuoneers, wit di big bɔdi fɔ di kru, na bin fa fawe mɔ barbaric, heathenish, ɛn motley sɛt pas ɛni wan pan di tame mɛrchant-ship kɔmni dɛn we mi fɔs ɛkspiriɛns dɛn bin dɔn mek a no, stil a askrib dis—ɛn rayt fɔ askrib am—to di faya faya uniqueness of di very nature of dat wild Skandinavian vocation in we a bin dɔn sodonedly embarked. Bɔt na di aspek we di tri chif ɔfisa dɛn na di ship, di mates, bin gɛt mɔ, we dɛn bin de kɔl am wit pawa pas ɔl fɔ mek dɛn nɔ gɛt wanwɔd we nɔ gɛt kɔlɔ, ɛn fɔ mek pipul dɛn gɛt kɔnfidɛns ɛn gladi at pan ɛnitin we dɛn de sho na di trip. Tri beta, we go mɔs bi se-ɔfisa ɛn man dɛn, we ɛni wan pan dɛn difrɛn we, dɛn nɔ bin ebul fɔ fɛn wantɛm wantɛm, ɛn dɛn ɔl na bin Amɛrikin; wan Nantucketer, wan Vineyarder, wan Cape man. Naw, na Krismas we di ship shot frɔm aut in haba, fɔ wan spes wi bin gɛt bit Polar wɛda, pan ɔl we ɔltɛm wi bin de rɔn away frɔm am to di sawt; ɛn bay ɛvri digri ɛn minit we wi de travul wit bot, ɛn smɔl smɔl wi de lɛf da winta de we nɔ gɛt sɔri-at, ɛn ɔl di wɛda we wi nɔ go ebul fɔ bia wit. Na bin wan pan dɛn mɔnin dɛn we nɔ bin de dɔŋ, bɔt stil grey ɛn dak inof mɔnin dɛn fɔ di chenj, we wit wan fayn briz di ship bin de rɔsh tru di wata wit wan vindictive sɔrt fɔ jomp ɛn melancholy rapidity, dat as a de mawnt to di dɛk na di kɔl fɔ di fɔnin wach, so jɔs as a lɛvɛl mi glans to di taffrail, foreboding shivers bin rɔn oba mi. Rial tin pas di we aw pipul dɛn de fred; Kpɛntin Eab bin tinap na in kwata-dɛk.

I bin tan lɛk se no sayn nɔ bin de fɔ kɔmɔn bɔdi sik bɔt am, ɔ fɔ se i dɔn wɛl frɔm ɛni wan. I bin tan lɛk man we dɛn kɔt kɔmɔt na di tik, we di faya dɔn west ɔl di an ɛn fut dɛn pasmak we i nɔ bɔn dɛn, ɔ tek wan patikyula tin frɔm dɛn kɔmpakt ol robustnɛs. In wan ol ay, brayt fɔm, i bin tan lɛk se dɛn mek am wit sɔlid brɔnz, ɛn i shep lɛk wan mol we nɔ go chenj, lɛk Pɛsiɔs we Sɛlini bin mek. We yu de trɛd in we fɔ kɔmɔt midul in grey ia dɛn, ɛn kɔntinyu rayt dɔŋ wan say na in fes ɛn nɛk we dɔn bɔn wit tawny, te i lɔs insay in klos, yu si wan slɛnd mak we tan lɛk stik, we wayt smɔl. I bin tan lɛk da pɔrpindikul sim we dɛn kin mek sɔntɛnde na di stret, ay ay trɔnk na wan big tik, we di laytin we de ɔp kin tear dart dɔŋ am, ɛn we i nɔ wrench wan tik, i kin pul ɛn grov di bark frɔm ɔp to dɔŋ, bifo i rɔn go na di grɔn, ɛn lɛf di tik stil grin layf, bɔt i gɛt brand. If dɛn bɔn da mak de wit am, ɔ if na di skata we sɔm wund we bin rili pwɛl bin lɛf, nɔbɔdi nɔ bin rili ebul fɔ tɔk. Bay sɔm tacit consent, insay di trip, dɛn nɔ bin tɔk bɛtɛ ɔ nɔ tɔk bɔt am, mɔ di mared pipul dɛn. Bɔt wan tɛm, Tashtego in sinia, we na bin ol Gey-Hɛd Indian we bin de pan di kru, bin tɔk wit lay lay biliv se nɔto te i ful fɔti ia, Eab bin bi da we de brand, ɛn afta dat i kam pan am, nɔto insay di wamat we ɛni mɔtalman fɛt, bɔt insay wan ɛlimɛntal fɛt-fɛt na si. Yet, dis wayl hint bin tan lɛk inferentially negatif, bay wetin wan grey Manxman insinuated, wan ol grev man, we, we nɔ ɛva bifo bifo sel kɔmɔt na Nantucket, nɔ ɛva ere dis layt ay pan wayl Eab. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, di ol si-tradishɔn dɛn, di immemorial credulities, pɔpul invayt dis ol Manxman wit prɛternatural pawa fɔ no. So dat no wayt selɔ nɔ bin siriɔs wan agens am we i se if ɛva dɛn fɔ layt Kpɛntin Eab kwayɛt wan—we nɔ go izi fɔ apin, so i bin de mɔna—dɛn, ɛnibɔdi we fɔ du da las ɔfis de fɔ di wan dɛn we dɔn day, go fɛn bɔn mak pan am frɔm krawn to in fut.

So pawaful wan di ɔl grim aspek fɔ Eab bin afɛkt mi, ɛn di livid brand we bin streak am, dat fɔ di fɔs fɔs mɔnt dɛn a nɔ bin rili notis se nɔto smɔl pan dis ɔva-big grimnɛs na bikɔs ɔf di barbaric wayt leg we i bin tinap pat pan. I bin dɔn kam to mi bifo tɛm se dɛn bin dɔn mek dis ayvri leg na si frɔm di bon we dɛn dɔn polish na di sperm wɛl in jaw. Wan tɛm, di ol Gey-Hɛd Indian bin tɔk se: “Aye, dɛn bin dismast am na Jepan; “bɔt lɛk in kraft we dɔn pwɛl, i ship ɔda mast we i nɔ kam bak na os fɔ am. I gɛt wan quiver of ’em.”

A bin sɔprayz wit di singul posture we i bin de mentɛn. Na ɛni say na di Pequod in kwata dɛk, ɛn fayn fayn wan nia di mizzen shroud dɛn, wan ɔga ol bin de, we dɛn bored lɛk af inch ɔ so, insay di plank. In bon leg bin tinap tranga wan na da ol de; wan an we dɛn es ɔp, ɛn we dɛn ol am wit shroud; Kpɛntin Eab bin tinap stret, ɛn i bin de luk stret pas di ship in prɔw we bin de ɔltɛm. Wan infinity of firmest fortitude bin de, wan determinate, unsurrenderable wilfulness, in di fiks ɛn nɔ fred, fɔdɔm dedikeshɔn fɔ da glans de. Nɔto wan wɔd we i tɔk; ɛn in ɔfisa dɛn nɔ bin tɔk natin to am; pan ɔl we bay ɔl di smɔl smɔl tin dɛn we dɛn bin de du ɛn di we aw dɛn bin de sho dɛnsɛf, dɛn bin de sho klia wan se dɛn nɔ izi fɔ dɛn, if nɔto fɔ mek dɛn fil pen, fɔ no se dɛn de ɔnda masta-ay we gɛt prɔblɛm. Ɛn nɔto dat nɔmɔ, bɔt Eab we bin de fil bad bin tinap bifo dɛn wit wan krɔs na in fes; in ɔl di nemlɛs rigal ɔvabearing dignity fɔ sɔm pawaful woe.

I nɔ tu te, frɔm di fɔs tɛm we i go na di ays, i pul insɛf kɔmɔt na in kebin. Bɔt afta da mɔnin de, di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot bin de si am ɛvride; ɔ tinap na in pivot-hol, ɔ sidɔm pan ayvri stɔl we i bin gɛt; ɔ fɔ waka bad bad wan na di dɛk. As di skay nɔ bin de dak igen; fɔ tru, bigin fɔ gro smɔl genial, i bin stil de smɔl smɔl fɔ de nia insɛf; lɛk se, we di ship dɔn kɔmɔt na os, natin nɔ bin dɔn mek i nɔ de nia ɔda pipul dɛn da tɛm de pas di day we di si bin dak insay di kol sizin. Ɛn, smɔl smɔl, i kam fɔ bi se i bin de na di ays ɔltɛm; bɔt, as yet, fɔ ɔl wetin i tɔk, ɔ we i no se i du, na di las dek we gɛt san, i bin tan lɛk se i nɔ nid fɔ de de lɛk ɔda mast. Bɔt di Pequod bin jɔs de mek wan pasej naw; nɔ fɔ de krɔs ɔltɛm; klos to ɔl di wɛl prɛparativ dɛn we nid fɔ bi supavayshɔn di mates bin ful-ɔp fɔ kɔmpɛt, so dat na smɔl ɔ natin nɔ bin de, kɔmɔt pan insɛf, fɔ wok ɔ mek Eab gladi, naw; ɛn so drɛb am, fɔ da wan intaval de, di klawd dɛn we de layt pan layt bin dɔn pila pan in brɔda, lɛk aw ɔltɛm ɔl klawd dɛn kin pik di ay ay mawnten dɛn fɔ pila dɛnsɛf pan.

Bɔt pan ɔl dat, ere long, di wam, warbling persuasiveness of di pleasant, ɔlide wɛda we wi kam to, bin tan lɛk se i de charm am smɔl smɔl frɔm in mud. Bikɔs, lɛk we di gyal pikin dɛn we gɛt rɛd chɛst, we de dans, Epril ɛn Me, kin trip go na os na di winta, misanthropic wud dɛn; ivin di ol ɔk we nɔ gɛt natin, we rɔf pas ɔl, we gɛt tɛnda, at ɔl go sɛn sɔm smɔl grɔn sprot dɛn, fɔ wɛlkɔm dɛn kayn pipul dɛn ya we gladi fɔ kam de; so Eab bin du, na di ɛnd, smɔl ansa to di ple ple ple ple we dɛn bin de mek da gyal pikin de. I pas wan tɛm we i put di smɔl smɔl flawa we i de luk, we, pan ɛni ɔda man, i nɔ go te igen, i go dɔn flawa wit smayl.
