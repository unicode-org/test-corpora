CHAPTER 10. Wan Bɔs Padi.

We a go bak na di Spouter-Inn frɔm di Chapel, a fɛn Queequeg de kwik kwik wan; i dɔn kɔmɔt na di Chapel bifo di blɛsin sɔm tɛm. I bin sidɔm na wan bɛnch bifo di faya, wit in fut pan di stov faya, ɛn wit wan an i bin ol klos klos to in fes da smɔl nigro aydɔl we i gɛt; i de luk tranga wan na in fes, ɛn wit jak-naif we de smɔl smɔl na in nos, ɛn da tɛm de i de hum to insɛf insay in hεtin we.

Bɔt bikɔs dɛn bin de ambɔg am naw, i put di imej; ɛn i nɔ tu te, we i go na di tebul, tek wan big buk de, ɛn put am na in lap bigin fɔ kɔnt di pej dɛn ɔltɛm wit wilful; na ɛvri fifti pej—as a bin de tink—a de stɔp fɔ smɔl tɛm, luk ɛmti wan rawnd am, ɛn gi wɔd to wan lɔng lɔng gurgling wisɛl we de mek a sɔprayz. Dɔn i go bigin bak na di nɛks fifti; i tan lɛk se i bigin na nɔmba wan ɛvri tɛm, lɛk se i nɔ ebul fɔ kɔnt pas fifti, ɛn na we dɛn fɛn bɔku bɔku fifti pipul dɛn togɛda, na in i bin sɔprayz fɔ si di bɔku bɔku pej dɛn.

Wit plenti intres a sidon wach am. Savage though i bin, ɛn hideously marred bɔt di fes—at least to mi teist—in fes yet bin gɛt sɔntin insay we nɔ bin de we nɔ gri wit ɛnitin. Yu nɔ go ebul fɔ ayd di sol. Tru ɔl in tatu dɛn we nɔ de na di wɔl, a bin tink se a si di trays dɛn fɔ wan simpul ɔnɛs at; ɛn insay in big, dip yay, faya blak ɛn bold, i bin tan lɛk sayn dɛn fɔ wan spirit we go gɛt maynd fɔ tawzin dɛbul dɛn. Ɛn apat frɔm ɔl dis, wan patikyula ay ay tin bin de bɔt di Pegan, we ivin di we aw i nɔ bin de trit fayn nɔ bin ebul fɔ mek i nɔ ebul fɔ du am atɔl. I bin tan lɛk man we nɔ ɛva kray ɛn we nɔ ɛva gɛt pɔsin we gɛt fɔ pe am. If na so bak, dɛn bin de sheb in ed, dɛn drɔ in fɔɛd kɔmɔt na fri ɛn brayt rilif, ɛn i bin luk mɔ pas aw i go bi, dis a nɔ go venchɔ fɔ disayd; bɔt shɔ se na in ed bin phrenologically wan fayn fayn wan. I kin tan lɛk se i rili laf, bɔt i mek a mɛmba Jɛnɛral Washinton in ed, lɛk aw a si am na di pɔpul bɔst dɛn we i gɛt. I bin gɛt di sem lɔng gred ɔltɛm we de tɔn bak frɔm ɔp di brɔw dɛn, we dɛnsɛf bin rili projɛkt, lɛk tu lɔng promontori dɛn we gɛt tik tik tik tik dɛn pan tap. Queequeg na bin Jɔj Washinton we dɛn bin de divɛlɔp wit pipul dɛn we de it mɔtalman bɔdi.

Wail a bin so klos skan am, af-af pretend meanwhile se a de luk out pan di storm frɔm di casement, i nɔ ɛva lisin to mi prezɛns, nɔ ɛva trobul insɛf wit so mɔ as wan glans; bɔt i bin tan lɛk se i bin rili bizi fɔ kɔnt di pej dɛn na di wɔndaful buk. We a tink bɔt aw wi bin de slip togɛda di nɛt bifo dis tɛm, ɛn mɔ we a tink bɔt di an we a bin dɔn si se dɛn trowe oba mi we a wek na mɔnin, a bin tink se dis nɔ bisin bɔt in rili strenj. Bɔt savayj dɛn na strenj pipul dɛn; sɔntɛnde yu nɔ kin no bɛtɛ aw fɔ tek dɛn. Fɔs, dɛn kin fred pasmak; dɛn kol kol sɛlf-kɔlɛkshɔn fɔ simpul tin tan lɛk se na Sɔkratis sɛns. A bin dɔn notis bak se Kwik nɔ bin ɛva kɔnsɔrt atɔl, ɔ bɔt i nɔ bin rili kɔnsɔt wit di ɔda siman dɛn we bin de na di in. I nɔ bin mek ɛni advans ɛnitin; i bin tan lɛk se i nɔ bin want fɔ mek di sɛklɔ fɔ di wan dɛn we i sabi big. Ɔl dis bin slap mi as pawaful singul; bɔt stil, we a tink bɔt am bak, sɔntin bin de we bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ i fayn. Na ya wan man bin de we de lɛk twɛnti tawzin mayl frɔm os, bay di rod we de na Kep Hɔn, dat na—we na di wangren we we i go ebul fɔ rich de—dɛn trowe am bitwin pipul dɛn we strenj to am lɛk se i de na di planɛt Jupita; ɛn pan ɔl dat, i bin tan lɛk se i bin rili fil fri; fɔ kip di kol at we pas ɔl; satisfay wit in yon kɔmpin; ɔltɛm i kin ikwal to insɛf. Fɔ tru, dis na bin tɔch fɔ fayn fayn sɛns; pan ɔl we i nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se i nɔ bin ɛva yɛri se sɔntin lɛk dat de. Bɔt, sɔntɛm, fɔ bi tru tru sayɛnsman dɛn, wi mɔtalman nɔ fɔ no se wi de liv so ɔ tray so. So jɔs lɛk aw a yɛri se da kayn man de ɔ da kayn man de gi insɛf fɔ bi pɔsin we sabi bɔt sayɛns, a kin kam fɔ no se, lɛk di ol uman we gɛt dispɛptik, i go mɔs dɔn “brɔk in dayjɛsta.”

As a sidɔm de na da rum de we naw na mi wan; di faya we de bɔn smɔl, insay da mild stej de we, afta in fɔs intensiti dɔn wam di briz, i kin jɔs shayn fɔ mek dɛn luk am; di ivintɛm shed ɛn phantom dɛn we de gɛda rawnd di kesmɛnt dɛn, ɛn de luk insay wi tu we nɔ tɔk natin ɛn we de wansay; di big big briz we de blo witout in solemn swells; A bigin fɔ tink bɔt strenj filin dɛn. A bin fil wan mɛlt insay mi. Nɔ mɔ mi at we bin skata ɛn mi an we bin dɔn mek a kray nɔ bin tɔn agens di wulf wɔl. Dis savayj we de mek pɔsin fil fayn bin dɔn fri am. Na de i sidɔm, in yon indipɛndɛns bin de tɔk wan kayn we we nɔ sivil ipokrit ɛn bland lay lay tɔk dɛn nɔ bin de. Wild i bin de; wan rili sight of sights fɔ si; yet a bigin fɔ fil se dɛn dɔn drɔ misɛf to am we nɔ izi fɔ ɔndastand. Ɛn dɛn sem tin dɛn de we bin fɔ dɔn drɛb bɔku ɔda wan dɛn, na di sem magnet dɛn we bin de drɔ mi so. A go tray wan pegan padi, a bin tink, bikɔs Kristian gudnɛs dɔn pruv bɔt olo kɔtisti. A drɔ mi bɛnch nia am, ɛn mek sɔm fayn fayn sayn dɛn ɛn hint dɛn, du mi bɛst fɔ tɔk wit am da tɛm de. Fɔs, i nɔ bin notis dɛn tin ya we dɛn dɔn go bifo; bɔt naw, we a de tɔk bɔt di ɔspitul dɛn we i bin gɛt las nɛt, i mek fɔ aks mi if wi fɔ bi bedfɛlɔ dɛn bak. A bin tɛl am se yɛs; usay a bin tink se i luk gladi, sɔntɛm dɛn bin kɔmplimɛnt am smɔl.

Dɔn wi tɔn di buk togɛda, ɛn a bin tray fɔ ɛksplen to am wetin mek dɛn print am, ɛn wetin di smɔl smɔl pikchɔ dɛn we bin de insay de min. Na so i nɔ tu te a bigin fɔ intres; ɛn frɔm dat wi bin go fɔ jab di bɛst we wi ebul bɔt di difrɛn ɔda tin dɛn we wi kin si na do we wi kin si na dis tɔŋ we bɔku pipul dɛn sabi. I nɔ tu te, a prɔmis fɔ mek pipul dɛn smok; ɛn, we i de prodyuz in paus ɛn tomahawk, i kwayɛt wan fɔ gi mi wan pɔf. Ɛn afta dat, wi sidɔm de chenj puf frɔm da wayl paip we in yon, ɛn kip am de pas bitwin wi ɔltɛm.

If ɛni ays we nɔ bisin bɔt mi bin stil de na di Pegan in brɔst, dis fayn, genial smok we wi bin gɛt, i nɔ tu te, i tɔn am, ɛn lɛf wi kroni. I bin tan lɛk se i tek to mi kwik kwik wan ɛn we a nɔ kɔl am lɛk aw a tek am to am; ɛn we wi smok dɔn, i prɛs in fɔɛd pan mi yon, tay mi rawnd mi wes, ɛn se frɔm naw wi dɔn mared; min, insay in kɔntri in wɔd, se wi na bin bosom padi; i go gladi fɔ day fɔ mi, if nid de. Insay pɔsin we de na di kɔntri, dis faya we kin bɔn wantɛm wantɛm fɔ padi biznɛs go dɔn tan lɛk se i nɔ tu te yet, ɛn na tin we pɔsin nɔ fɔ abop pan; bɔt insay dis simpul savayj dɛn ol lɔ dɛn de nɔ go aplay.

Afta wi it, ɛn wan ɔda soshal chat ɛn smok, wi go na wi rum togɛda. I mek mi prɛzɛnt fɔ in ed we dɛn dɔn ɛmbas; pul in big big tabak walet, ɛn grop ɔnda di tabak, pul lɛk tati dɔla silva; dɔn i spre dɛn na di tebul, ɛn mek dɛn sheb dɛn to tu ikwal pat dɛn wit mashin, i push wan pan dɛn to mi, ɛn se na mi yon. A bin de go fɔ rimonstret; bɔt i bin mek a nɔ tɔk natin bay we i tɔn dɛn na mi trayz poket. A mek dɛn de de. Dɔn i bin de pre fɔ ivintɛm, pul in aydɔl, ɛn pul di pepa fayabɔd. Bay sɔm sayn ɛn sayn dɛn, a bin tink se i bin tan lɛk se i de wɔri fɔ mek a jɔyn am; bɔt bikɔs a bin no gud gud wan wetin fɔ fala, a bin tink fɔ smɔl tɛm if, if i invayt mi, a go fala ɔ ɔda we.

Mi na bin gud Kristian; dɛn bɔn am ɛn mɛn am na di Presbitirian Chɔch we nɔ de mek mistek. So aw a go gɛt wanwɔd wit dis wayl aydɔl man fɔ wɔship in wud? Bɔt wetin na wɔship? tink I. Yu tink naw, Ishmayl, se di big big Gɔd we de na ɛvin ɛn di wɔl—pegan dɛn ɛn ɔlman inklud—go ebul fɔ jɛlɔs wan smɔl blak wud we nɔ impɔtant? Nɔ pɔsibul! Bɔt wetin na wɔship?—fɔ du wetin Gɔd want— dat na wɔship. Ɛn wetin Gɔd want?—fɔ du to mi kɔmpin mɔtalman wetin a go want mi kɔmpin mɔtalman fɔ du to mi— na dat na Gɔd in wil. Naw, Queequeg na mi kɔmpin man. En wetin a wish se dis Queequeg go du to mi? Wetin mek, yunayt wit mi na mi patikyula Presbyterian we fɔ wɔship. So, a fɔ dɔn gɛt wanwɔd wit am insay in yon; ergo, a fɔ tɔn aydɔl. So a kin bɔn di shavings dɛn; ɛp fɔ sɔpɔt di smɔl aydɔl we nɔ du natin; bin gi am biskit we dɛn dɔn bɔn wit Kwik; salamed bifo am tu ɔ tri tɛm; kis in nos; ɛn dat dɔn, wi pul klos ɛn go slip, wit pis wit wi yon kɔnshɛns ɛn ɔl di wɔl. Bɔt wi nɔ bin de slip we wi nɔ bin de tɔk smɔl.

Aw i de a nɔ no; bɔt no ples nɔ de lɛk bed fɔ mek padi dɛn tɔk bɔt kɔnfidɛns. Man ɛn wɛf, dɛn se, na de dɛn de opin di bottom na dɛn sol to dɛnsɛf; ɛn sɔm ol mared pipul dɛn kin lay ɛn tɔk bɔt ol tɛm te i rich nia mɔnin. So, so, insay wi at in onimun, mi ɛn Kwik bin de—na tu pipul dɛn we bin fayn ɛn we lɛk dɛnsɛf.





CHAPTER 11. Nayt klos.

Wi bin dɔn ledɔm so na bed, de tɔk ɛn slip fɔ shɔt tɛm, ɛn Kwik bin de trowe in brawn tatu leg dɛn wit lɔv oba mi yon, dɔn i de drɔ dɛn bak; so wi bin rili ebul fɔ spɛn tɛm wit ɔda pipul dɛn ɛn fri ɛn izi; we, las las, bikɔs ɔf wi kɔnfabuleshɔn, wetin smɔl nappishnɛs bin lɛf insay wi ɔltogɛda bin kɔmɔt, ɛn wi bin fil lɛk fɔ grap bak, pan ɔl we de-brɛk bin stil sɔm we dɔŋ di fyuchu.

Yɛs, wi bin rili wek; sote wi sidɔm ples bigin fɔ taya, ɛn smɔl smɔl wi bin si wisɛf sidɔm; di klos dɛn bin tuck fayn fayn wan rawnd wi, dɛn bin de ledɔm pan di ed-bɔd wit wi 4 ni dɛn we dɛn drɔ klos togɛda, ɛn wi tu nos dɛn bin bɛn oba dɛn, lɛk se wi ni-pan dɛn na wam-pan. Wi bin fil rili fayn ɛn snug, di mɔ bikɔs i bin rili kol na do; fɔ tru, kɔmɔt na bed-klos bak, we i si se faya nɔ bin de na di rum. Di mɔ, a se, bikɔs fɔ rili ɛnjɔy fɔ wam yu bɔdi, sɔm smɔl pat pan yu fɔ kol, bikɔs no kwaliti nɔ de na dis wɔl we nɔto wetin i bi jɔs difrɛns. Natin nɔ de fɔ insɛf. If yu flat yusɛf se yu ɔl ova kɔmfyut, ɛn dɔn so lɔng tɛm, den dɛn nɔ go se yu kɔmfyut igen. Bɔt if, lɛk mi ɛn Kwik we de na di bed, di tip na yu nos ɔ di krawn na yu ed kol smɔl, wetin mek den, fɔ tru, insay di jenɛral kɔnshɛns yu kin fil mɔs gladi ɛn yu nɔ go mistek wam. Fɔ dis rizin, dɛn nɔ fɔ ɛva gɛt faya na ples usay dɛn kin slip, ɛn dis na wan pan di fayn fayn tin dɛn we di jɛntriman dɛn nɔ kin fil fayn. Bikɔs di ayt fɔ dis kayn swit na fɔ gɛt natin pas di blankit bitwin yu ɛn yu snugnɛs ɛn di kol we di ɔdasay de kol. Dɔn na de yu de ledɔm lɛk di wan wam spak we de na di at fɔ wan aktik kristal.

Wi bin dɔn sidɔm dis kayn we fɔ kray fɔ sɔm tɛm, we wan tɛm a tink se a go opin mi yay; bikɔs we a de bitwin shit, ilɛksɛf na de ɔ na nɛt, ɛn ilɛksɛf a de slip ɔ we a de wek, a kin gɛt we fɔ kip mi yay ɔltɛm, fɔ mek a ebul fɔ pe atɛnshɔn mɔ pan di fayn fayn tin dɛn we a kin du we a de na bed. Bikɔs no man nɔ go ɛva fil in yon aydentiti rayt pas in yay lɔk; lɛk se daknɛs na rili di rayt tin fɔ wi ɛsin, pan ɔl we layt kin fayn mɔ to wi pat we gɛt kle. We a opin mi yay da tɛm de, ɛn kɔmɔt na mi yon daknɛs we fayn ɛn we a mek misɛf go insay di impoz ɛn kɔs ɔda daknɛs we de na di twɛlv oklɔk-nayt we nɔ gɛt layt, a bin gɛt wan bad bad tin we a nɔ gri wit. A nɔ bin agens atɔl di hint frɔm Kwik se sɔntɛm i bɛtɛ fɔ slap layt, bikɔs wi si se wi dɔn wek so; ɛn apat frɔm dat i bin fil wan strɔng want fɔ gɛt sɔm kwayɛt puf frɔm in Tomahawk. I bi se, pan ɔl we a bin dɔn fil da kayn strɔng repugnance de fɔ in smok na bed di nɛt bifo, yet si aw elastik wi stiff prejudices de gro we lɔv wans kam fɔ bɛn dɛn. Fɔ naw a nɔ bin lɛk natin pas fɔ mek Kwik de smok nia mi, ivin na bed, bikɔs i bin tan lɛk se i ful-ɔp wit da kayn gladi at we de na os we gɛt kol at da tɛm de. A nɔ bin de fil bad igen fɔ di inshɔrans polisi we di pɔsin we gɛt di os bin gɛt. A bin jɔs de alayv to di kɔndens kɔnfidɛns kɔmfyut we a bin de sheb wan paip ɛn blankit wit mi rial padi. Wit wi shaggy jakit dɛn we dɛn drɔ rawnd wi sholda, naw wi pas di Tomahawk frɔm wan to di ɔda wan, te smɔl smɔl wan blu hanging testa fɔ smok gro oba wi, we di flawa we di nyu layt lamp de layt bin de shayn.

If na dis wan we de tɛst di ɔndalayt bin rol di savayj go fa fa say dɛn, a nɔ no, bɔt naw i de tɔk bɔt in yon ayland; ɛn bikɔs a bin rili want fɔ yɛri in istri, a beg am fɔ go bifo ɛn tɛl am. I bin gladi fɔ du wetin dɛn se. Pan ɔl we da tɛm de a nɔ bin ɔndastand sɔm pan in wɔd dɛn, bɔt di tin dɛn we i tɔk afta dat, we a bin dɔn kam fɔ no mɔ bɔt in brok brok wɔd dɛn, naw de mek a ebul fɔ prizent di wan ol stori lɛk aw i kin pruv insay di jɔs skel we a gi.





CHAPTER 12. Bayografik.

Kwik na bin Rokovoko in yon, we na wan ayland we de fa fawe na di Wɛst ɛn Sawt. I nɔ de dɔŋ na ɛni map; tru tru ples dɛn nɔ de ɛva de.

We wan nyu savayj we dɔn bɔn de rɔn wayl rawnd in yon wudland dɛn insay wan gras klot, we di got dɛn we de swɛla de fala am, lɛk se na grɔn sapling; ivin da tɛm de, insay Kwik in sol we bin want fɔ gɛt bɔku prɔpati, wan strɔng want bin de fɔ si sɔntin we tan lɛk Kristian rilijɔn pas wan ɔ tu spɛshal wɛl. In papa na bin Ay Chif, na bin Kiŋ; in ɔnkul na bin Ay Prist; ɛn na di mama in sayd i bin de bost bɔt anti dɛn we na di wɛf dɛn fɔ fɛtman dɛn we dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ win. Blɔd bin de na in vein dɛn we bin rili fayn—na tin dɛn we kiŋ bin de mek; though sadly vitiated, a de fred, bay di kanibal propensity we i nourished in in untutored youth.

Wan ship we dɛn kɔl Sag Haba bin go na in papa in bay, ɛn Kwik bin de luk fɔ rod fɔ go na Kristian land dɛn. Bɔt di ship, we bin gɛt in ful kɔmplimɛnt fɔ siman dɛn, bin spurn in sus; ɛn nɔto ɔl di Kiŋ in papa in pawa bin ebul fɔ win. Bɔt Kwik bin prɔmis fɔ mek i prɔmis. In wan insay in kenu, i padul go na wan ples we de fa, we i no se di ship fɔ pas we i kɔmɔt na di ayland. Na wan say, wan kɔral rif bin de; na di ɔda say na wan land we nɔ gɛt bɛtɛ grɔn, we dɛn kɔba wit mangro tik tik dɛn we de gro na di wata. I ayd in kenu, we stil de flɔt, bitwin dɛn tik tik tik dɛn ya, wit in prow we de go na di si, i sidɔm na di stɛp, i padul smɔl na in an; ɛn we di ship bin de glid pas, lɛk flash i rɔn kɔmɔt; bin gɛt in sayd; wit wan baksay dash na in fut kapsay ɛn sink in kenu; klaym ɔp di chen dɛn; ɛn trowe insɛf ful-ɔp na di dɛk, i ol wan ring-bɔlt de, ɛn swɛ se i nɔ go lɛf am, pan ɔl we i kɔt am smɔl smɔl.

Na fɔ natin di kapten bin trɛtin am fɔ trowe am na di wata; i bin de suspɛnd wan kɔtlas oba in nekɛd an dɛn; Kwik na bin wan Kiŋ in pikin, ɛn Kwik nɔ bin de chenj. Bikɔs di kapten bin sɔprayz bikɔs i nɔ bin de fred bad bad wan, ɛn i bin rili want fɔ go na Kristian rilijɔn, i bin dɔn lɛf fɔ du am, ɛn i tɛl am se sɔntɛm i go mek insɛf na os. Bɔt dis fayn yɔŋ savayj—dis si Prins fɔ Wɛls, nɔ ɛva si di Kpɛntin in kebin. Dɛn put am dɔŋ midul di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot, ɛn mek i bi wɛlman. Bɔt jɔs lɛk aw Za Pita bin satisfay fɔ wok tranga wan na di say dɛn we dɛn de mek ship dɛn na ɔda siti dɛn, Kwik nɔ bin de rɛspɛkt ɛnibɔdi we tan lɛk se i de shem, if dat go mek i gladi fɔ gɛt di pawa fɔ mek in kɔntri pipul dɛn we nɔ lan buk gɛt sɛns. Bikɔs na di bɔt—na so i tɛl mi—i bin mek i rili want fɔ lan bitwin di Kristian dɛn, di art dɛn we i go yuz fɔ mek in pipul dɛn stil gladi pas dɛn; ɛn pas dat, stil bɛtɛ pas aw dɛn bin de. Bɔt, i sɔri fɔ no se! i nɔ tu te, di tin dɛn we pipul dɛn we de fɛt wɛl bin de du bin mek i biliv se ivin Kristian dɛn kin sɔfa ɛn wikɛd pipul dɛn; i nɔ gɛt ɛnd, pas ɔl in papa in neshɔn dɛn. A rich las las na ol Sag Aba; ɛn si wetin di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot de du de; ɛn afta dat, i go na Nantucket, ɛn si aw dɛn spɛn dɛn pe na da ples de bak, po Kwik giv am fɔ lɔs. Tink se, na wikɛd wɔl insay ɔl di mɛridian dɛn; A go day as pegan.

Ɛn so na ol aydɔl wɔship in at, i stil de liv wit dɛn Kristian ya, wɛr dɛn klos, ɛn tray fɔ tɔk dɛn gibberish. Na dat mek di queer ways bɔt am, though naw sɔm tɛm frɔm os.

Bay hint, a aks am if i nɔ prɔmis fɔ go bak, ɛn gɛt korona; bikɔs naw i go tek in papa se i dɔn day ɛn i nɔ de igen, i bin dɔn ol bad bad wan ɛn i nɔ bin gɛt bɛtɛ trɛnk we i kam pan di las stori dɛn. I ansa se nɔ, nɔto yet; ɛn i bin tɔk bak se i bin de fred se Kristianiti, ɔ fɔ se Kristian dɛn, nɔ bin fit am fɔ go ɔp di klin ɛn nɔ dɔti tron ​​we gɛt tati pegan Kiŋ dɛn we bin de bifo am. Bɔt smɔl smɔl, i bin se, i go kam bak,—as soon as i fil se i dɔn baptayz bak. Bɔt fɔ di nɔs, i bin prɔmis fɔ sel rawnd, ɛn plant in wayl ɔt na ɔl di 4 si dɛn. Dɛn bin dɔn mek am wan harpuoneer, ɛn da barbed ayɛn de bin de insay stik in ples naw.

A aks am wetin go bi in men tin we i want fɔ du wantɛm wantɛm, ɛn tɔch di we aw i go muv tumara bambay. I ansa, fɔ go na si bak, insay in ol vokeshɔn. We a du dis, a tɛl am se fɔ kech wɛl na mi yon dizayn, ɛn a tɛl am se a want fɔ tek bot kɔmɔt na Nantucket, as na di pɔt we prɔmis pas ɔl fɔ mek pɔsin we lɛk wɛlman fɔ go insay. I bin disayd wantɛm wantɛm fɔ go wit mi na da ayland de, ship insay di sem bot, go insay di sem wach, di sem bot, di sem mɛs wit mi, fɔ tɔk smɔl fɔ sheb mi ɛvri hap; wit mi tu an dɛn na in yon, boldly dip insay di Potluck fɔ ɔl tu di wɔl dɛn. A bin gri wit ɔl dɛn tin ya; bikɔs apat frɔm di lɔv we a bin de fil naw fɔ Kwik, i bin sabi fɔ ple hapun, ɛn as i bi so, i nɔ bin ebul fɔ fɔgɛt fɔ bi big yus to pɔsin, we, lɛk mi, nɔ bin no atɔl bɔt di sikrit dɛn bɔt wɛl, pan ɔl we i bin sabi di si gud gud wan, lɛk aw di biznɛsman dɛn we de wok na di si no.

In stori we i dɔn wit in paip in las day puf, Kwik embras mi, prɛs in fɔɛd pan mi yon, ɛn blo di layt, wi rol ova frɔm wisɛf, dis we ɛn dat, ɛn i nɔ tu te, wi bin de slip.





CHAPTER 13. Wilbɔro.

Neks mɔnin, Mɔnde, afta a dɔn trowe di ed we dɛn dɔn ɛmbam to wan barba, fɔ wan blɔk, a sɛtul mi yon ɛn kɔmrɛd bil; bɔt we a de yuz mi kɔmpin in mɔni. Di landlɔd we bin de grin, ɛn di wan dɛn we bin de na di bɔda, bin tan lɛk se dɛn bin de tikl am wɔndaful wan pan di padi biznɛs we bin dɔn kam wantɛm wantɛm bitwin mi ɛn Kwik—espɛshali as Pita Kɔfin in kɔk ɛn kaw stori dɛn bɔt am bin dɔn mek a fred bad bad wan bifo bɔt di sem pɔsin we a de kɔmpin wit naw.

Wi borrow wan wilbaro, ɛn embark wi tin dɛm, inklud mi yon po kap-bag, ɛn Queequeg in kanvas sak ɛn hamak, away wi go dɔŋ to “di Moss,” di smɔl Nantucket paket skun we dɛn bin dɔn muv na di wɔf. As wi de go, di pipul dɛn bin de luk wi; nɔto na Kwik so—bikɔs dɛn bin dɔn yus fɔ si pipul dɛn we de it pipul dɛn lɛk am na dɛn strit,—bɔt fɔ si mi ɛn am pan dɛn kayn kɔnfidɛns tɛm dɛn de. Bɔt wi nɔ bin lisin to dɛn, wi bin de go wit wil di barɔ bay tɔn, ɛn Kwik bin de stɔp wan wan tɛm fɔ ajɔst di swɛt pan in harpun barb dɛn. A aks am wetin mek i kin kɛr dis kayn tin we kin mek i gɛt prɔblɛm wit am na di si, ɛn if ɔl di ship dɛn we kin kech wɛl nɔ kin fɛn dɛn yon hapun. To dis, in substance, i ansa, se pan ɔl we wetin a hint na tru inof, yet i bin gɛt patikyula afɛkt fɔ in yon harpun, bikɔs na tin we dɛn mek shɔ se, dɛn tray am fayn fayn wan pan bɔku fɛt-fɛt we de kil pipul dɛn, ɛn i bin rili gɛt tayt padi biznɛs wit di at dɛn fɔ wɛl. Fɔ tɔk smɔl, lɛk bɔku pipul dɛn we de rip ɛn kɔt tik we de insay di land, we kin go na di fama dɛn mɛdɔ wit dɛn yon swɛt—pan ɔl we dɛn nɔ gɛt ɛnitin fɔ du wit fɔ gi dɛn tin dɛn—ivin so, Kwik, fɔ in yon prayvet rizin dɛn, bin lɛk in yon harpun.

We i shift di barɔ frɔm mi an to in yon, i tɛl mi wan fani stori bɔt di fɔs wilbaro we i ɛva si. I bin de na Sag Aba. I tan lɛk se di wan dɛn we gɛt in ship bin dɔn lɛnt am wan, we i go kɛr in ebi ebi chɛst go na in bɔding os. Nɔ fɔ mek i tan lɛk se i nɔ no bɔt di tin—pan ɔl we fɔ tru, i bin rili so, bɔt di rayt we fɔ manej di barɔ—Kwik put in chɛst pan am; lash am fast fast; ɛn afta dat, i sholda di barɔ ɛn mach ɔp di watawɛl. “Wetin mek,” a se, “Kwik, yu go dɔn no bɛtɛ pas dat, pɔsin go tink.Di pipul dɛn nɔ bin laf?”

We i kam pan dis, i tɛl mi wan ɔda stori. I tan lɛk se di pipul dɛn na in ayland we nem Rokovoko, na dɛn mared fɛstival dɛn, dɛn kin pul di fayn fayn wata we de kɔmɔt na yɔŋ kokonat dɛn insay wan big kalabash we gɛt dɔti lɛk panchbɔl; ɛn dis panchbɔl kin mek di big sɛntral ɔnamɛnt ɔltɛm na di breyd mat usay dɛn kin gɛt di fɛstival. Naw wan patikyula big big biznɛs ship bin tɔch wan tɛm na Rokovoko, ɛn in kɔmanda—frɔm ɔl di stori dɛn, na bin wan rili stet pɔnktiliɔs jentlman, at ɔl fɔ wan si kapten—dɛn bin invayt dis kɔmanda fɔ di mared fɛstival fɔ Kwik in sista, wan fayn yɔŋ prinses we jɔs dɔn rich tɛn ia. Wɛl; we ɔl di mared pipul dɛn gɛda na di yawo in bambu kɔtjɛ, dis Kpɛntin de mach insay, ɛn we dɛn gi am di post fɔ ɔnɔ, i put insɛf oba di panchbɔl, ɛn bitwin di Ay Prist ɛn in majesty di Kiŋ, we na Kwik in papa. Grɛs we dɛn se,—bikɔs dɛn pipul dɛn de gɛt dɛn grɛs lɛk wi—pan ɔl we Kwik tɛl mi se nɔ lɛk wi, we da kayn tɛm de kin luk dɔŋ to wi plet dɛn, dɛn, difrɛn frɔm dat, we dɛn de kɔpi di dɔk dɛn, de luk ɔp to di big Giva fɔ ɔl di fɛstival dɛn—Grɛs, a se, we dɛn se, di Ay Prist de opin di pati bay di sɛrimɔni we nɔ gɛt wan bɔt na di ayland; dat na fɔ dip in finga dɛn we i dɔn kɔnsakret ɛn we i dɔn kɔnsakret insay di bol bifo di blɛsin drink go skata. We i si se dɛn put insɛf nia di Prist, ɛn notis di sɛrimɔni, ɛn tink se insɛf—bi Kpɛntin fɔ wan ship—lɛk se i gɛt klia fɔs pas wan ayland Kiŋ nɔmɔ, mɔ na di Kiŋ in yon os—di Kpɛntin kol kol wan fɔ was in an dɛn na di panchbɔl;—tek am a tink se fɔ wan big finga-glas. Kwik bin se: “Naw, wetin yu tink naw?—Wi pipul dɛn nɔ bin de laf?”

Las las, we dɛn pe fɔ pas, ɛn sef bag, wi tinap na di skun. We i bin de es di sel, i bin de glid dɔŋ di Akushnɛt riva. Na wan say, Nyu Bɛdfɔd bin de rayz na di tɛras dɛn na strit, dɛn tik dɛn we ays kɔba ɔl bin de shayn na di klia ɛn kol briz. Big big il dɛn ɛn mawnten dɛn we gɛt bɔtul dɛn we de pan bɔtul dɛn bin dɔn gɛda na in watawɛl dɛn, ɛn sayd to sayd di wɛl ship dɛn we bin de waka waka na di wɔl bin ledɔm kwayɛt wan ɛn dɛn bin dɔn muv dɛn sef wan las las; we frɔm ɔda wan dɛn bin de mek sawnd we kapɛnta ɛn kɔpa dɛn de mek, wit faya ɛn fɔj dɛn we dɛn miks fɔ mek di pitch mɛlt, ɛn ɔl dis de sho se nyu kruz dɛn de bigin; dat wan trip we bin rili denja ɛn we bin lɔng bin dɔn, na wan sɛkɔn nɔmɔ i bigin; ɛn sɛkɔn ɛnd, jɔs bigin wan tɔd, ɛn so de go, fɔ sote go ɛn fɔ aye. Na so di tin dɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnd, yes, di we aw ɔl di tray we dɛn de tray na dis wɔl nɔ go ebul fɔ bia.

We di wata we bin opin mɔ, di briz we bin de blo bin de blo fresh; di smɔl Mɔs bin de tos di kwik fom frɔm in bɔw, lɛk aw yɔŋ kɔlt de snor. Aw a bin de snuf da Tartar ɛya de!—aw a bin de spurn da tɔnpayk wɔl de!—da kɔmɔn aywe de ɔlsay we bin dɔn dent wit di mak dɛn fɔ slev il ɛn fut; ɛn tɔn mi fɔ admaya di big big si we nɔ go alaw ɛni rikodɔ.

Na di sem fom-fɔnt, i bin tan lɛk se Kwik de drink ɛn riɛl wit mi. In nos dɛn we bin dak bin swel apat; i sho in fayl ɛn pɔynt tit dɛn. On, on wi bin de flay; ɛn wi ɔfing bin gɛt, di Mɔs bin du ɔmajɛ to di blast; ducked en dive in bows as slev bifo di Sultan. Saydways lean, wi saydways dart; ɛvri rop yarn de tingling lɛk waya; di tu lɔng mast dɛn we de bɔk lɛk Indian ken dɛn na land big big briz. Wi bin so ful-ɔp wit dis reeling scene, as wi tinap nia di plunging bowsprit, dat fɔ sɔm tɛm wi nɔ bin notis di jeering glances of di pasenja dɛn, wan asɛmbli we tan lɛk lubber, we bin de wɔnda se tu kɔmpin pipul dɛn fɔ bi so kɔmpin; lɛk se wayt man na ɛnitin we gɛt rɛspɛkt pas nigro we dɛn wayt. Bɔt sɔm bɔbi ɛn bɔmpkin dɛn bin de de, we, bay dɛn tranga grɛn, go mɔs dɔn kɔmɔt na di at ɛn sɛnt fɔ ɔl di grɔn. Kwik bin kech wan pan dɛn yɔŋ tik dɛn ya we de falamakata am biɛn in bak. A bin tink se di bumpkin in awa fɔ doom dɔn kam. We i drɔp in harpun, di brawn savayj kech am na in an, ɛn bay wan ɔlmost mirekul dɛkstri ɛn trɛnk, i sɛn am ay ɔp bɔdi na di ɛj; dɔn i tap in stɛp smɔl na mid-sɔmset, di kɔmpin land wit bɔs lɔng dɛn na in fut, we Kwik, we tɔn in bak pan am, layt in tomahawk paip ɛn pas am to mi fɔ mek a puf.

“Kaptin! Kaptin!” di bɔmpkin ala, i rɔn go to da ɔfisa de; “Kaptin, Kaptin, na di dɛbul de ya.”

“Halo, yu sa,” di Kpɛntin kray, we na wan gaunt rib na di si, we de stalk go nia Kwik, “wetin insay tɛnda yu min wit dat?Yu nɔ no se yu go dɔn kil da chap de?”

“Wetin i se?” Kwik se, as i de tɔn to mi saful wan.

“I se,” a se, “se yu kam nia kil-e da man de,” i pɔynt to di grɛnhɔn we stil de shek shek.

“Kil-e,” Kwik kray, i twist in fes we gɛt tatu to wan ɛksprɛshɔn we nɔ de na di wɔl fɔ sho se i nɔ rɛspɛkt am, “ah! am bevy smɔl-e fish-e; Kwik nɔ kil-e so smɔl-e fish-e; Kwik kil-e big wɛl!”

“Luk yu,” di Kpɛntin ala, “A go kil-e yu , yu kanibal, if yu tray ɛni mɔ pan yu trik dɛn na di bot ya; so maynd yu yay.”

Bɔt i bin apin jɔs da tɛm de, dat na bin ay tɛm fɔ mek di Kpɛntin tink bɔt in yon yay. Di wɔndaful strɛs we bin de pan di men-sɛl bin dɔn sheb di wɛda-shit, ɛn di big big bɔm bin de flay naw frɔm wan say to di ɔda say, ɛn i bin de swip di wan ol afta pat pan di dɛk kpatakpata. Di po man we Kwik bin dɔn handle so roughly, dɛn bin swip am ova di wata; ɔl dɛn an dɛn bin de fred; ɛn fɔ tray fɔ snɛp di bɔm fɔ mek i nɔ de, i bin tan lɛk se na madnɛs. I bin de flay frɔm rayt to lɛft, ɛn bak bak, klos to wan tik we wan wach de tik, ɛn ɛvri instant i bin tan lɛk se i de snɛp to split. Dɛn nɔ bin du natin, ɛn i bin tan lɛk se natin nɔ go ebul fɔ du; di wan dɛn we bin de na di dɛk bin rɔsh go nia di bɔw dɛn, ɛn tinap de luk di bɔm lɛk se na di ɔnda jaw fɔ wan wɛl we vɛks. Insay dis kɔnstɛnshɔn, Kwik bin drɔp wit sɛns to in ni, ɛn kray ɔnda di rod we di bɔm de pas, i wip ol wan rop, sikyurɛt wan ɛnd to di bulwark dɛn, dɔn i fling di ɔda wan lɛk laso, kech am rawnd di bɔm as i de swip oba in ed, ɛn na di nɛks jɔk, di spar bin dɔn trɔp da we de, ɛn ɔltin bin sef. Dɛn rɔn di skun insay di briz, ɛn we di an dɛn bin de klin di bot we de na di bot, Kwik, we dɛn pul in klos te i rich na in wes, rɔn kɔmɔt na di sayd wit wan lɔng layf ark we de jomp. Fɔ tri minit ɔ mɔ, dɛn bin si am de swim lɛk dɔg, i de trowe in lɔng an dɛn stret bifo am, ɛn bay tɔn i de sho in brawn sholda dɛn tru di friz fom. A luk di big ɛn gloriful felo, bɔt a nɔ si ɛnibɔdi fɔ sev. Di grɛnhɔn bin dɔn go dɔŋ. We i de shot insɛf perpendicularly frɔm di wata, Queequeg, naw tek wan instant in glans rawnd am, ɛn i tan lɛk se i si jɔs aw tin de, i dayv dɔŋ ɛn disappear. Fɔ sɔm minit mɔ, ɛn i grap bak, wan an stil de kɔmɔt, ɛn wit di ɔda wan de drɛg wan fɔm we nɔ gɛt layf. I nɔ tu te, di bot pik dɛn. Di po bumpkin bin kam bak. Ɔl di an dɛn bin vot Queequeg fɔ bi nobul trɔmp; di kapten beg am fɔ fɔgiv am. Frɔm da awa de a klov to Kwik lɛk barnakul; yea, til poor Queequeg tek in las long dive.

Yu tink se dis kayn pɔsin bin ɛva de we nɔ bin no natin? I bin tan lɛk se i nɔ bin tink se i fɔ gɛt medal atɔl frɔm di Humane and Magnanimous Societies. I bin jɔs aks fɔ wata—frɛsh wata—sɔntin fɔ was di brin; dat dɔn, i wɛr dray klos, layt in paip, ɛn ledɔm pan di bulwark dɛn, ɛn saful wan yay di wan dɛn we de rawnd am, i bin tan lɛk se i de se to insɛf se—“It’s a mutual, joint-stock world, in all meridians. Wi pipul dɛn we de it pipul dɛn fɔ ɛp dɛn Kristian ya.”





CHAPTER 14. Nantucket we de na di wɔl.

Natin nɔ bin apin mɔ pan di pat we fit fɔ mek wi tɔk bɔt; so, afta wi dɔn rɔn fayn fayn wan, wi rich na Nantucket sef wan.

Nantukɛt we de na di wɔl! Tek yu map ɛn luk am. Si wetin na rial kɔna na di wɔl i de; aw i tinap de, fa fawe na di sho, i de in wan pas di Eddystone laythaus. Luk am—na jɔs il, ɛn ɛlb we gɛt san; ɔl di bich, we nɔ gɛt bakgrɔn. San san de de pas aw yu go yuz insay twɛnti ia as substitute fɔ blotting pepa. Sɔm gamesome wights go tɛl yu se dɛn fɔ plant gras de, dɛn nɔ de gro naturally; dat dɛn kin import Canada thistles; dat dɛn fɔ sɛn pas di si fɔ mek wan spil fɔ stɔp lik insay wan ɔyl bɔtul; dat dɛn kin kɛr sɔm wud dɛn na Nantucket lɛk bit dɛn na di tru tru krɔs na Rom; dat pipul dɛn de de plant todstɔl bifo dɛn os, fɔ go ɔnda di shed insay sɔm tɛm; dat wan blad fɔ gras de mek wan oasis, tri bled dɛn insay wan de waka na wan prɛri; dat dɛn kin wɛr kwik sand sus, sɔntin lɛk Laplander sno-shuz; dat dɛn kin so lɔk, bɛlt rawnd, ɔl we dɛn lɔk, rawnd, ɛn mek dɛn bi wan ɔt ayland ɔf bay di si, dat to dɛn yon chia ɛn tebul dɛn, sɔm tɛm dɛn go si smɔl klam dɛn we de tay, lɛk di bak fɔ si tɔl dɛn. Bɔt dɛn ekstravaganza ya jɔs de sho se Nantucket nɔto Ilinois.

Luk naw di wɔndaful tradishɔnal stori bɔt aw di rɛd-man dɛn bin kam de na dis ayland. Na so di lay lay stori de go. Trade trade wan igl bin de swip dɔŋ di Nyu Ingland kɔst, ɛn kɛr wan bebi Indian go na in fut. Wit lawd kray, di mama ɛn papa si dɛn pikin we dɛn de kɛr kɔmɔt na di say we dɛn nɔ go si am oba di big big wata. Dɛn bin disayd fɔ fala di sem say. We dɛn de go insay dɛn kenu dɛn, afta dɛn dɔn pas we denja, dɛn fɛn di ayland, ɛn na de dɛn fɛn wan ɛmti ayvri bɔks,—di po smɔl Indian in skel.

So, i rili wɔndaful fɔ si se dɛn Nantucketers ya, we dɛn bɔn na bich, fɔ go na di si fɔ liv! Dɛn bin fɔs kech krab ɛn quohog na di san; we dɛn bin dɔn gɛt maynd mɔ ɛn mɔ, dɛn bin de waka wit nɛt fɔ mek makrɛl; we dɛn bin gɛt mɔ ɛkspiriɛns, dɛn bin de push go na bot ɛn kech kɔd; ɛn las las, we i lans wan nevi we gɛt big big ship dɛn na di si, i bin fɛn dis wɔl we gɛt wata; put wan bɛlt we nɔ de stɔp we de rawnd am; i bin de luk insay di say we dɛn kɔl Behring’s Straits; ɛn insay ɔl di sizin dɛn ɛn ɔl di si dɛn bin deklare wɔ we go de sote go wit di pawaful animal dɛn we dɔn sev frɔm di wata we bin rɔn; di monstrous pas ɔl ɛn di mawnten pas ɔl! Ɛn na so dɛn nekɛd Nantucketers ya, dɛn si ɛmit ya, we de kɔmɔt na dɛn ant-hil na di si, dɔn ɔva ɛn win di wata wɔl lɛk bɔku bɔku Alɛgzanda dɛn; we dɛn bin de sheb di Atlantik, Pasifik, ɛn Indian si dɛn bitwin dɛn, jɔs lɛk aw di tri pawaful pawa dɛn we dɛn bin de kɔl pairati bin du na Polan. Bikɔs di si na in yon; na in gɛt am, jɔs lɛk aw Emparɔ dɛn gɛt ɛmpaya dɛn; ɔda siman dɛn we gɛt bɔt rayt fɔ pas de. Merchant ship dɛn na jɔs ɛkstenshɔn brij dɛn; di wan dɛn we gɛt wɛpɔn bɔt fɔt dɛn we de flɔt; ivin pairati ɛn prayvet pipul dɛn, pan ɔl we dɛn de fala di si as ayweman dɛn na di rod, dɛn kin jɔs tif ɔda ship dɛn, ɔda pat dɛn na di land lɛk dɛnsɛf, we dɛn nɔ de tray fɔ pul dɛn layf frɔm di dip we nɔ gɛt bottom. Di Nantucketer, na in nɔmɔ de liv ɛn de mek rɔsh na di si; in wan, insay Baybul langwej, de go dɔŋ to am insay ship dɛn; to ɛn fro de plɔg am as in yon spɛshal plantɛshɔn. Na de in os de; na de in biznɛs de, we Noa in wata nɔ go ambɔg, pan ɔl we i bin pas ɔl di bɔku bɔku pipul dɛn na Chaena. Fɔ lɔng tɛm naw, i nɔ no di land; so dat we i kam to am las las, i de smɛl lɛk ɔda wɔl, we strenj pas aw di mun go smɛl to pɔsin we de na di wɔl. Wit di gɔl we nɔ gɛt land, we we di san de go dɔŋ, i kin fold in wing dɛn ɛn rɔk am fɔ slip bitwin big big wata dɛn; so we nɛt dɔn, di Nantucketer, we nɔ de si land, kin ful in sel dɛn, ɛn le am fɔ rɛst, we ɔnda in pilo sɛf kin rɔsh bɔku bɔku walrus ɛn wɛl dɛn.

CHAPTER 16. Di Ship.

Na bed wi bin de mek wi plan fɔ di nɛks tɛm. Bɔt a sɔprayz ɛn nɔto smɔl wɔri, Kwik naw gi mi fɔ ɔndastand, se i bin dɔn de wok tranga wan fɔ aks Yojo—di nem fɔ in blak smɔl gɔd—ɛn Yojo bin dɔn tɛl am tu ɔ tri tɛm pas, ɛn i bin insist am tranga wan ɔltɛm, se instead fɔ mek wi go togɛda bitwin di wɛl-flit dɛn na haba, ɛn insay kɔnsɛt fɔ pik wi kraft; insted of dis, a se, Yojo bin rili enjoin se di selekshon of di ship fo res ol wit mi, inasmuch as Yojo bin purpose fo bi padi; ɛn, fɔ mek a ebul fɔ du dat, a bin dɔn ɔlrɛdi pitch pan wan bot, we, if dɛn lɛf am to misɛf, mi, Ishmayl, go layt pan am we nɔ go mek mistek, fɔ ɔl di wɔl lɛk se i dɔn tɔn aut bay chans; ɛn insay da bot de a fɔ ship misɛf wantɛm wantɛm, fɔ di tɛm naw ilɛksɛf na Kwik.

Fɔ sɔm pan dɛn sem Kwayɛt dɛn ya na di wan dɛn we gɛt blɔd pas ɔl di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot ɛn di wan dɛn we de fɛn wɛl. Dɛn de fɛt di Kwayɛt pipul dɛn; dɛn na Kwayɛt pipul dɛn we gɛt wan blem.

Ɛn we dɛn tin ya jɔyn pan man we gɛt bɔku bɔku natura pawa, we gɛt bren we gɛt glob ɛn at we gɛt bɔku bɔku tin dɛn fɔ du; we dɔn mek bak bay di stil ɛn seklɔshɔn we bɔku lɔng nɛt-watch dɛn de na di wata dɛn we de fa pas ɔl, ɛn ɔnda di sta dɛn we dɛn nɔ ɛva si ya na di nɔt, dɛn dɔn mek i tink di we we nɔto tradishɔnal ɛn fɔ insɛf; we i de gɛt ɔl di swit ɔ savayj impreshɔn dɛn we di nature gɛt fresh frɔm in yon vajin volontia ɛn kɔnfid brɔst, ɛn dat de mɔ, bɔt wit sɔm ɛp frɔm aksidɛnt advantej dɛn, fɔ lan wan bold ɛn nervous lofty langwej—dat man de mek wan insay wan ol neshɔn in sɛns—wan pawaful pejɛnt krichɔ, we dɛn fɔm fɔ nobul trayji. Ɛn i nɔ go pul am atɔl, we dɛn tek am dramatik wan, if na bay we dɛn bɔn am ɔ ɔda tin, i gɛt wetin tan lɛk af-af wilful ɔvruling morbidness na di bɔt ɔf in nature. Bikɔs ɔl man dɛn we rili big, dɛn kin mek dɛn so tru wan patikyula sik. Mek shɔ se dis, O yɔŋ ambishɔn, ɔl mɔtalman big big tin na jɔs sik. Bɔt, as yet wi nɔ gɛt fɔ du wit da kayn wan de, bɔt wi gɛt fɔ du wit wan ɔda wan; ɛn stil na man, we, if i rili spɛshal, i jɔs kɔmɔt bak frɔm ɔda pat pan di Kwayɛt, we dɛn chenj bay wan wan tin dɛn we de apin.

Jɔs lɛk Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg, Kpɛntin Bildad na bin wɛlman we bin dɔn ritaia ɛn we bin gɛt mɔni. Bɔt nɔ lɛk Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg—we nɔ bin bisin bɔt wan rɔsh fɔ wetin dɛn kɔl siriɔs tin dɛn, ɛn fɔ tru, i bin si dɛn sɛf-sɛf siriɔs tin dɛn de as di tin dɛn we rili impɔtant pas ɔl—Kpɛtɛn Bildad nɔ bin jɔs dɔn lan fɔs akɔdin to di strikt sɛkt we na Nantucket Quakerism, bɔt ɔl di layf we i bin de liv afta dat na di si, ɛn di si bɔku tin dɛn we nɔ bin wɛr klos, we dɛn lɛk na di ayland, rawnd di Hɔn—ɔl wetin nɔ bin dɔn muv dis nativ we dɛn bɔn Kwayɛt wan jot, nɔ bin dɔn chenj wan angul pan in vest so. Naw, Bildad, a sɔri fɔ se, i bin gɛt di gudnem fɔ bi ol hunks we nɔ go ɛva chenj, ɛn insay in si-go tɛm, i bin bita, had wok-masta. Dɛn tɛl mi na Nantucket, pan ɔl we i rili tan lɛk wan stori we a want fɔ no mɔ, se we i bin de travul wit di ol Kategut wɛlman, in kru, we dɛn rich na os, dɛn bin de kɛr bɔku pan dɛn ɔl go na di si na di ɔspitul, dɛn bin taya ɛn dɛn bin dɔn taya. Fɔ pɔsin we de wɔship Gɔd, mɔ fɔ wan Kwayɛt, i bin rili gɛt at at, fɔ tɔk di tru. Bɔt i nɔ bin ɛva yus fɔ swɛ to in man dɛn, dɛn bin se; bɔt sɔm kayn we i bin gɛt bɔku bɔku kruk, tranga wok we nɔ bin de stɔp fɔ wok frɔm dɛn. We Bildad na bin chif-mɛt, fɔ mek in yay we gɛt dray kɔlɔ de luk yu gud gud wan, i bin de mek yu fil bad bad wan, te yu ebul fɔ ol sɔntin—hama ɔ marling-spayk, ɛn go wok lɛk mad, pan sɔntin ɔ ɔda tin, nɔ ɛva tink bɔt wetin. Indolence ɛn idleness bin dɔnawe bifo am. In yon pɔsin na bin di rayt we fɔ sho in utilitarian abit. Na in lɔng bɔdi we nɔ gɛt bɛtɛ bɔdi, i nɔ bin de kɛr ɛni spɛshal bɔdi, i nɔ bin de kɛr ɛni biad we nɔ impɔtant, in chin bin gɛt wan saf, ikɔmik nap to am, lɛk di west nap na in hat we gɛt brayt brim.

So, na so di pɔsin we a si sidɔm na di transom we a fala Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg dɔŋ insay di kebin. Di ples bitwin di dɛk dɛn bin smɔl; ɛn de, bolt-aprayt, sidɔm ol Bildad, we bin de sidɔm so ɔltɛm, ɛn nɔ ɛva ledɔm, ɛn dis fɔ sev in kot tel. Dɛn bin put in brayt brim nia am; in leg dɛn bin stif krɔs; dɛn bin de bɔt in klos we nɔ fayn te i rich na in chin; ɛn spɛktaklɔs na in nos, i bin tan lɛk se i bin de rid frɔm wan ponderous volyum.

Kpɛntin Pɛlɛg kray se: “Bildad, pan am bak, Bildad, eh? Yu dɔn de stɔdi dɛn Skripchɔ dɛn de, naw, fɔ di las tati ia, to mi shɔ se a no. Aw fa yu rich, Bildad?”
