CHAPTER 135. Di Chase.—Tɔd De.

Di mɔnin fɔ di tɔd de bin shayn fayn ɛn fresh, ɛn wan tɛm bak di solitary nayt-man we bin de na di fɔs-mast-hɛd bin gɛt fridɔm frɔm krawd dɛn we bin de luk fɔ di de layt, we bin de dot ɛvri mast ɛn ɔlmost ɛvri spar.

“Yu si am?” Eab bin kray; bɔt di wɛl nɔ bin de yet fɔ si.

“Bɔt insay in wek we nɔ gɛt mistek; bɔt fala da wek de, na dat nɔmɔ. Helm de; stedi, as yu de go, ɛn yu dɔn de go. Dis na fayn de bak! if na bin nyu wɔl we dɛn mek, ɛn mek am fɔ sɔm-os to di enjɛl dɛn, ɛn dis mɔnin di fɔs tɛm we dɛn trowe am opin fɔ dɛn, wan de we fayn pas dis nɔ go ebul fɔ kam pan da wɔl de. Na dis na fud fɔ tink, bin gɛt Eab tɛm fɔ tink; bɔt Eab nɔ de ɛva tink; i jɔs de fil, fil, fil; dat na tingling inof fɔ mɔtalman man! fɔ tink na audacity. Na Gɔd nɔmɔ gɛt da rayt ɛn ɔnɔ de. Tink na, ɔ i fɔ bi, kol ɛn kol at; ɛn wi po at dɛn kin bit, ɛn wi po bren kin bit tumɔs fɔ dat. Ɛn pan ɔl dat, sɔntɛnde a dɔn tink se mi bren bin rili kol—frɔzɛn kol, dis ol skel krak so, lɛk glas we di tin dɛn we de insay de tɔn to ays, ɛn shek am. Ɛn stil dis ia de gro naw; dis mɔnt de gro, ɛn ɔt fɔ bɔn am; bɔt nɔ, i tan lɛk da kayn kɔmɔn gras de we go gro ɛnisay, bitwin di dɔti kleft dɛn na Grinland ays ɔ insay Vesuvius lava. Aw di wayl briz de blo am; dɛn de wip am bɔt mi as di swɛt shred dɛn we split sel dɛn de lash di ship we dɛn dɔn tos we dɛn de klin to. Wan wikɛd briz we nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se i dɔn blo bifo dis tru prizin kɔridɔ ɛn sɛl dɛn, ɛn wɔd dɛn na ɔspitul dɛn, ɛn mek briz de blo dɛn, ɛn naw i de kam blo ya as inosɛnt lɛk wul. Out pan it!—i dɔn dɔti. If na mi bin di briz, a nɔ bin fɔ dɔn blo igen pan dis kayn wikɛd, misɛf wɔl. A go kray sɔmsay go na wan kev, ɛn slink de. Ɛn yet, 'tis na nobul ɛn heroic tin, di briz! udat ɛva win am? Insay ɛni fɛt i gɛt di las ɛn bita blo. Rɔn tilt pan am, ɛn yu bɔt rɔn tru am. Ha! wan briz we de fred ɛn we kin atak man dɛn we nekɛd bad bad wan, bɔt i nɔ go tinap fɔ gɛt wan blo. Ivin Eab na tin we gɛt maynd pas am—na tin we gɛt ay pozishɔn pas dat . Would naw di briz bɔt gɛt bɔdi; bɔt ɔl di tin dɛn we de mek mɔtalman vɛks ɛn vɛks pas ɔl, ɔl dɛn tin ya nɔ gɛt bɔdi, bɔt na jɔs tin dɛn we nɔ gɛt bɔdi lɛk tin, nɔto as ɛjɛn. Na wan spɛshal, wan mɔs kɔni, o, wan mɔs malicious difrɛns de! Ɛn yet, a se bak, ɛn swɛ am naw, se sɔntin de we ɔl gɛt glori ɛn we gɛt sɔri-at na di briz. Dɛn wam wam Tred Wind ya, at ɔl, we na di klia ɛvin de blo stret, insay strɔng ɛn stedi, trɛnk saful saful; ɛn nɔ veer frɔm dɛn mak, ɔltogɛda di baser kɔrɛnt dɛm na di si kin tɔn ɛn tak, ɛn di pawaful Misisipi dɛm na di land swift ɛn swɛv arawnd, nɔ shɔ usay fɔ go las. Ɛn bay di Pol dɛn we de sote go! dis sem Trades we so dairekt blo mi gud ship pan; dɛn Tred ya, ɔ sɔntin lɛk dɛn—sɔntin we nɔ de chenj, ɛn we ful-ɔp lɛk we strɔng, de blo mi kil sol along! To am! Aloft dey! Wetin d’ye si?”

“Natin nɔ de, sa.”

"Natin! ɛn midi de nia! Di doublon go a-beg! Si di san! Aye, aye, i fɔ bi so. A don ovasail am. Aw, gɛt di biginin? Aye, i de chase mi naw; nɔto mi, am —dat na bad tin; Sɔntɛm misɛf bin fɔ dɔn no am. Ful! di layn dɛn—di harpun dɛn we i de pul. Aye, aye, a don run am bai las nait. Bɔt! bɔt! Kam dɔŋ, una ɔl, bɔt di rɛgyula luk aut! Man di bres!”

Stia as i bin dɔn du, di briz bin dɔn de sɔm kayn we na di Pequod in kwata, so dat naw we dɛn de pɔynt am na di rivays dairekshɔn, di ship we gɛt brayt ship bin de sel tranga wan pan di briz as i de richɔr di krim insay in yon wayt wek.

“Agenst di briz i de stiar naw fɔ di opin jaw,” Starbuck bin de grɔmbul to insɛf, as i de kɔyl di nyu-hauled men-brace pan di rel. “Gɔd kip wi, bɔt ɔlrɛdi mi bon dɛn de fil wet insay mi, ɛn frɔm insay de wet mi bɔdi. A nɔ de dawt mi se a nɔ de obe mi Gɔd we a de obe am!”

“Dɛn tinap fɔ mek a swɛ mi!” Eab kray, i de go bifo to di baskɛt we gɛt hεmp. “Wi fɔ mit am jisnɔ.”

“Aye, aye, sa,” ɛn wantɛm wantɛm Starbuck du Eab in bid, ɛn wan tɛm mɔ Eab swing ɔp ay.

Wan ol awa dɔn pas naw; gold-bit aut to ages. Taym insɛf naw bin de ol lɔng briz wit keen sɔspɛns. Bɔt las las, lɛk tri pɔynt dɛn frɔm di wɛda bɔw, Eab tɔk bɔt di spɔt bak, ɛn wantɛm wantɛm frɔm di tri mast-ed dɛn, tri kray kray dɛn go ɔp lɛk se faya in tɔng dɛn dɔn mek am vɔys.

“Fɔrɛst to fɔrɛst A mit yu, dis tɔd tɛm, Mobi Dik! Na dɛk de!—bras shap ɔp; krawd am na di briz in yay. I tu fa fɔ lɛ i lɔs yet, Mista Stabɔk. Di sel dɛn de shek! Stand oba da helmsman de wit wan top-maul! So, so, so; i de travul fast, ɛn a fɔ kam dɔŋ. Bɔt lɛ a gɛt wan mɔ gud rawnd luk ɔp ya na di si; tɛm de fɔ dat. Wan ol, ol sight, ɛn yet sɔm kayn we so yɔŋ; aye, ɛn nɔ chenj wan wink sins a fɔs si am, wan bɔbɔ, frɔm di san-hil dɛm na Nantucket! Na di sem tin!—na di sem tin!—na di sem tin to Noa jɔs lɛk aw i to mi. Na wan soft shawa de fɔ leeward. Dɛn kayn fayn fayn leewardings ya! Dɛn fɔ lid sɔmsay—to ɔda tin pas kɔmɔn land, we gɛt palm pas di pam tik dɛn. Liwɔd! di wayt wɛl kin go da we de; luk to di say we di briz de blo, so; di bɛtɛ if di kwata we bita. Bɔt gud bay, gud bay, ol mast-hɛd! Wetin na dis?—grin? aye, smɔl smɔl mɔs dɛn na dɛn krak krak ya we dɔn wɔp. Nɔ da kayn grɔn wɛda stɛyn de na Eab in ed! Di difrɛns de naw bitwin man in ol ej ɛn matta in ol ej. Bɔt aye, ol mast, wi ɔl tu de ol togɛda; sawnd insay wi hul, bɔt, nɔto so, mi ship? Aye, minus wan leg, na ol dat. Bay ɛvin dis day wud gɛt bɛtɛ pas mi layf bɔdi ɔlsay. A nɔ ebul fɔ kɔmpia am wit am; ɛn a dɔn no se sɔm ship dɛn we dɛn mek wit day tik dɛn kin las pas di layf we man dɛn we dɛn mek wit di tin dɛn we impɔtant pas ɔl fɔ papa dɛn we impɔtant pas ɔl. Wetin na dat i tɔk? i fɔ stil go bifo mi, we na mi payɔnia; ɛn yet dɛn fɔ si am bak? Bɔt usay? A go gɛt yay na di bottom na di si, we wi tink se a de kam dɔŋ dɛn stej dɛn de we nɔ gɛt ɛnd? ɛn ɔl nɛt a dɔn de sel frɔm am, ɛnisay we i bin sink go. Aye, aye, laik plenti mo yu tel'st direful trut as toch yusef, O Parsee; bɔt Eab, na de yu shot nɔ bin fɔdɔm. Gud-bay, mast-hɛd—kip yu yay pan di wɛl, di tɛm we a nɔ de igen. Wi go tɔk tumara, nɔto so, tide na nɛt, we di wayt wɛl de ledɔm de, tay am wit ed ɛn tel.”

I gi di wɔd; ɛn stil de luk rawnd am, dɛn bin de put am dɔŋ ɔltɛm tru di blu briz we bin dɔn klin to di dɛk.

Insay di rayt tɛm, dɛn bin put di bot dɛn dɔŋ; bɔt as i tinap na in shalɔp in stɛp, Eab jɔs de flay na di say we i de kam dɔŋ, i wɛf in wɛf,—we ol wan pan di takɛl-rop dɛn na di dɛk—ɛn tɛl am fɔ stɔp.

“Stabɔk!”

"Sa?"

“Fɔ di tɔd tɛm we mi sol in ship bigin pan dis voyage, Starbuck.”

“Aye, sa, yu go gɛt am so.”

“Sɔm ship dɛn kin kɔmɔt na dɛn pɔt, ɛn ɛva afta dat, dɛn nɔ kin de, Starbuck!”

“Tru, sa: trut we rili sɔri.”

“Sɔm man dɛn kin day we di wata de go dɔŋ; sɔm pan dɛn we wata smɔl; sɔm na di ful-ɔp wit di wata;—ɛn a fil naw lɛk wan bilɔ we na ɔl wan kres kɔm, Starbuck. A dɔn ol;—shek an wit mi, man.”

Dɛn an dɛn mit; dɛn yay dɛn bin tay; Starbuck in de tear di glu.

“O, mi kapten, mi kapten!—nobul at—nɔ go—nɔ go!—si, na brayt man we de kray; di pen we dɛn bin de fil we dɛn bin de mek dɛn biliv da tɛm de!”

Eab bin ala se: “Dɔŋ!”—I bin de trowe di man in an pan am. “Dɛn tinap nia di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot!”

Wantɛm wantɛm di bot bin de pul rawnd klos ɔnda di stɛp.

“Di shak dɛn! di shak dɛn!” wan vɔys kray frɔm di lɔw kabin-windo we de de; “O masta, mi masta, kam bak!”

Bɔt Eab nɔ yɛri natin; bikɔs in yon vɔys bin de ɔp da tɛm de; ɛn di bot jomp go bifo.

Bɔt stil, di vɔys bin tɔk tru; bikɔs i nɔ bin dɔn push frɔm di ship, we bɔku bɔku shak dɛn, we tan lɛk se dɛn de kɔmɔt na di dak wata we de ɔnda di ship, bin de snɛp di blad dɛn na di ship dɛn wit bad at, ɛvri tɛm we dɛn de dip insay di wata; ɛn na dis we dɛn bin de go wit di bot wit dɛn bayt dɛn. Na tin we nɔ kin apin to di wɛl-bɔt dɛn we de na dɛn si dɛn de we kin ful-ɔp wit bɔku bɔku wata; sɔntɛnde i tan lɛk se di shak dɛn kin fala dɛn di sem we aw di vayl dɛn kin flay oba di ban dɛn fɔ di rejimɛnt dɛn we de mach na di ist. Bɔt dɛn wan ya na bin di fɔs shak dɛn we di Pequod bin dɔn si frɔm we dɛn bin fɔs tɔk bɔt di Wait Wɛl; ɛn if na se Eab in kru na bin ɔl dɛn kayn tayga-yɔlɔ barbarian dɛn de, ɛn dat mek dɛn bɔdi bin de mɔs mɔs to di sɛns dɛm fɔ di shak dɛm—na tin we pipul dɛn sabi sɔntɛnde fɔ afɛkt dɛn,—bɔt i bin tan lɛk se dɛn de fala da wan bot de we dɛn nɔ de fɔ du mami ɛn dadi biznɛs wit di ɔda wan dɛn.

“At we dɛn mek wit stɛl!” Starbuck bin de grɔmbul we i de luk oba di sayd, ɛn fala wit in yay di bot we de go bak—“yu kin stil ring wit maynd fɔ si da tin de?—i de put yu kil dɔŋ bitwin shak dɛn we de rɔn, ɛn fala dɛn, opin yu mɔt fɔ di chase; ɛn dis na di tɔd de we rili impɔtant?—Bikɔs we tri dez de flɔ togɛda fɔ wan kɔntinyu fɔ tray tranga wan; mek shɔ se di fɔs wan na mɔnin, di sɛkɔn wan na midulnɛt, ɛn di tɔd wan na ivintɛm ɛn di ɛnd fɔ da tin de—bi da ɛnd de wetin i bi. O! mi Gɔd! wetin na dis we de shot tru mi, ɛn lɛf mi so dedly calm, yet expectant,—fixed at the top of a shudder! Tin dɛn we go apin tumara bambay de swim bifo mi, lɛk ɛmti autlayn ɛn skel dɛn; ɔl di tin dɛn we dɔn pas, sɔm kayn we dɔn gro dim. Meri, gyal pikin! yu de fade in pale glori biɛn mi; bɔy! I tan lɛk se a de si bɔt yu yay dɔn gro wɔndaful blu. I tan lɛk se di prɔblɛm dɛn we strenj pas ɔl na layf de klin; bɔt klawd de swip bitwin—Mi joyn in ɛnd de kam? Mi leg dɛn kin fil lɛk se dɛn dɔn wik; lɛk in yon we dɔn fut am ɔl di de. Fil yu at,—bit am yet? Stir yusɛf, Starbuck!—stav am ɔf—muv, muv! tɔk lawd wan!—Mast-hed de! Yu si mi bɔbɔ in an na di il?—Krayz;—aloft de!—kip yu yay we pas ɔl pan di bot dɛn:—mak di wɛl fayn fayn wan!—Ho! bak!—drɛb da hawk de! si! i de pik—i de tear di vane”—i de pɔynt to di rɛd flag we de flay na di men-trak—“Ha! i de flay go wit am!—Usay di ol man de naw? yu de si da tin de, o Eab!—shɛd, shek!”

Di bot dɛn nɔ bin dɔn go fa, we bay wan sayn frɔm di mast-hed dɛn—wan an we pɔynt dɔŋ, Eab bin no se di wɛl dɔn blo; bɔt bikɔs i bin want fɔ de nia am di nɛks tɛm we i go rayz, i bin ol in rod smɔl sayd frɔm di bot; di kru we bin gɛt becharmed bin de kip di profoundest silence, as di ed-bit wev dɛn bin de hama ɛn hama pan di opɔsin bɔw.

“Drayv, draiv in yu nel, oh ye waves! to dɛn ed we pas ɔl drɛb dɛn insay! una de nak sɔntin we nɔ gɛt lid; ɛn no kɔfin ɛn no bɔdi motoka nɔ go ebul fɔ bi mi yon:—ɛn na hεmp nɔmɔ go ebul fɔ kil mi! Ha! ha!”

Wantɛm wantɛm, di wata we bin de rawnd dɛn bin swel smɔl smɔl insay brayt sɛklɔ dɛn; dɔn i kin shek kwik kwik wan, lɛk se i de slayv na sayd frɔm wan ays we de ɔnda wata, ɛn i de go ɔp kwik kwik wan. Dɛn yɛri wan sawnd we de mek smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl smɔl. wan hum we de ɔnda grɔn; ɛn afta dat dɛn ɔl ol dɛn briz; as bedraggled wit rop we de biɛn, ɛn harpun, ɛn lans, wan big fɔm we dɛn shot lɔng, bɔt obliquely frɔm di si. I bin kɔba am wit wan tint vel we de drɔp we gɛt mist, ɛn i bin de flay fɔ smɔl tɛm na di renbo ays; ɛn afta dat i fɔdɔm swamp bak insay di dip. We dɛn krɔs am tati fit ɔp, di wata bin de flash fɔ smɔl tɛm lɛk bɔku bɔku watawɛl dɛn, dɔn i bin brok brok brok insay wan shawa we gɛt flɛks, ɛn lɛf di say we bin de rawnd am krim lɛk nyu milk rawnd di mabul trɔnk na di wɛl.

“Gi we!” Eab kray to di wan dɛn we de rayd bot, ɛn di bot dɛn rɔn go bifo fɔ atak; bɔt i bin dɔn mek Mobi Dik ful wit di fresh ayɛn dɛn we bin de yestede we bin rɔtin insay am, i bin tan lɛk se ɔl di enjɛl dɛn we bin fɔdɔm frɔm ɛvin dɔn jɔyn am. Di wayd taya dɛn we gɛt wɛld tɛndon dɛn we bin de oba in brayt wayt fɔɛd, ɔnda di transparent skin, bin tan lɛk se dɛn nit togɛda; as i de ed, i kam de chuk in tel bitwin di bot dɛn; ɛn wan tɛm mɔ i flay dɛn apat; dɛn bin de trowe di ayɛn ɛn lans dɛn frɔm di tu man dɛn bot dɛn, ɛn rɔn go na wan say na di ɔp pat na dɛn bo, bɔt i lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ Eab in yon nɔ gɛt skata.

Wail Daggoo en Queequeg bin de stop di strained plank dem; ɛn as di wɛl de swim kɔmɔt nia dɛn, tɔn, ɛn sho wan ol flank as i de shot nia dɛn bak; na da tɛm de wan kwik kray bin go ɔp. Lash rawnd ɛn rawnd to di fish in bak; pinioned in di turns upon turns in which, during di past nait, di wɛl bin dɔn riel di involushɔn dɛm fɔ di layn dɛm we de rawnd am, dɛn si di af-af bɔdi fɔ di Parsee; in sable klos bin rɔtin te i brok; in yay dɛn we bin opin bin tɔn ful-ɔp pan ol Eab.

Di harpun bin drɔp frɔm in an.

“Dɛn ful am, ful dɛn!”—i tek lɔng slim briz—“Aye, Parsee! A si yu bak.—Aye, ɛn yu de go bifo; ɛn dis, dis na di bɔdi we yu bin dɔn prɔmis. Bɔt a de ol yu te to di las lɛta na yu wɔd. Usay di sɛkɔn motoka we gɛt bɔdi de? Away, mates, to di ship! dɛn bot dɛn de nɔ gɛt yus naw; ripɛnt dɛn if una ebul insay tɛm, ɛn go bak to mi; if nɔto so, Eab dɔn du fɔ day—Dɔn, man dɛn! di fos tin we bot ofa fo jomp fo dis bot we a tinap in, dat tin we a harpoon. Una nɔto ɔda man, na mi an ɛn mi fut; ɛn so obe mi.—Usay di wɛl de? go dɔŋ bak?”

Bɔt i bin luk tumɔs nia di bot; bikɔs lɛk se i bɛn fɔ rɔnawe wit di bɔdi we i kɛr, ɛn lɛk se di patikyula ples we dɛn bin mit am las tɛm na bin jɔs wan stej na in leeward voyage, Mobi Dick bin de swim bak stedi fɔ go bifo; ɛn i bin dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ i pas di ship,—we te naw, i bin dɔn de go di ɔda say we i de go, pan ɔl we fɔ naw, dɛn dɔn stɔp di we aw i de go bifo. I bin tan lɛk se i de swim wit in pawa pas ɔl, ɛn naw i jɔs gɛt di maynd fɔ fala in yon stret rod na di si.

"O! Eab,” Starbuck kray, “nɔ tu let, ivin naw, di tɔd de, fɔ lɛf fɔ du am. Si! Moby Dick nɔ de luk fɔ yu. Na yu, yu, de luk fɔ am wit mad!”

We di bot we bin de in wan bin de go na di briz we bin de blo, dɛn bin de yuz ɔa ɛn kanvas fɔ mek i go kwik kwik wan fɔ go na di say we di briz de blo. Ɛn las las we Eab bin de slayv nia di bot, so nia am klia wan fɔ no difrɛns bitwin Stabɔk in fes as i de ledɔm oba di rel, i prez am fɔ tɔn di bot rawnd, ɛn fala am, nɔto tu kwik, wan tɛm we i gɛt sɛns. We i luk ɔp, i si Tashtego, Queequeg, ɛn Daggoo, we bin rili want fɔ go ɔp to di tri mast-hɛd dɛn; we di wan dɛn we de ɔs bin de rɔk insay di tu bot dɛn we gɛt stav we dɛn bin jɔs es ɔp na di sayd, ɛn dɛn bin bizi de wok fɔ mek dɛn. Wan afta di ɔda, tru di pɔt-ol dɛn, as i de spid, i bin de si bak we i de flay fɔ si Stubb ɛn Flask, we bin de bizi na di dɛk bitwin bɔndɛl dɛn we gɛt nyu ayɛn ɛn lans dɛn. As i si ɔl dis; as i yɛri di hama dɛn na di bot dɛn we dɔn brok; fa ɔda hama dɛn bin tan lɛk se dɛn de drɛb nel insay in at. Bɔt i bin gɛda. Di Epilogue we di Baybul tɔk bɔt

“ƐN NA MI WƆN WE DE RƐBƐM FƆ TƐL YU” Job.

Di drama dɔn dɔn. So wetin mek na ya ɛnibɔdi step fɔ go bifo?—Bikɔs wan bin sev we di bot pwɛl.

I so chans, se afta di Parsee in disappearance, na mi na di Fates odain fɔ tek di ples fɔ Eab in bowsman, we da bowsman de tek di vacant post; di sem, we, we di las de, dɛn bin trowe di tri man dɛn kɔmɔt na di bot we bin de rɔk, dɛn bin drɔp am biɛn. So, we a bin de flɔt na di margin fɔ di scene we bin de afta dat, ɛn we a de si am ful wan, we di af-af sɔk we di ship we bin dɔn sink bin de sɔk bin rich to mi, a bin de da tɛm de, bɔt sloslo, drɔ to di vortex we bin de klos. We a rich de, i bin dɔn go dɔŋ to wan krim pul. Dɔn, rawnd ɛn rawnd, ɛn ɛva kɔntrakt to di blak bɔbul we tan lɛk bɔtin na di aks fɔ da sɛklɔ we de wil sloslo, lɛk ɔda Ixion we a bin du fɔ rɔn. Till, we i gɛt da impɔtant sɛnt de, di blak bɔbul we de go ɔp bɔs; ɛn naw, we dɛn fri am bikɔs ɔf in kɔni spring, ɛn, bikɔs ɔf in big big buoyancy, we i de rayz wit big pawa, di kɔfin layf-buoy shot lɔng lɔng wan frɔm di si, fɔdɔm oba, ɛn flot nia mi sayd. We da kɔfin de bin de ɛp mi, fɔ lɛk wan ol de ɛn nɛt so, a bin de flɔt pan wan men we saf ɛn we tan lɛk dirge. Di shak dɛn we nɔ bin de du bad, dɛn bin de glid pas lɛk se dɛn gɛt padlɔk na dɛn mɔt; di savayj si-hawk dɛn bin de sel wit dɛn bich dɛn we gɛt sheath. Di sɛkɔn de, wan sel kam nia, kam nia mi, ɛn i kam tek mi las las. Na di devious-cruising Rechɛl, we we i bin de tray bak fɔ fɛn in pikin dɛn we bin dɔn mis, i bin jɔs fɛn ɔda ɔfɛn.
