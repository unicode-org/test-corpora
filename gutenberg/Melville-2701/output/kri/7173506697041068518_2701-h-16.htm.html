CHAPTER 81. Di Pequod Mit Di Vɛjin.

Di de we wi bin dɔn disayd fɔ du bin rich, ɛn wi mit di ship we nem Jungfrau, we na Dɛrik Di Diya, we na masta, we kɔmɔt na Bremen di rayt we.

Wan tɛm, di pipul dɛn we bin de kech wɛl pas ɔlman na di wɔl, di Dɔch ɛn Jamani pipul dɛn naw de pan di wan dɛn we nɔ bɔku; bɔt ya ɛn de na rili wayd intaval dɛn na latitud ɛn lɔnjitud, yu stil de mit wit dɛn flag wan wan tɛm na di Pasifik.

Fɔ sɔm rizin, i bin tan lɛk se di Jungfrau famili bin rili want fɔ pe am rɛspɛkt. We i bin stil de sɔm distans frɔm di Pequod, i bin rawnd to, ɛn drɔp wan bot, dɛn bin push in kapten kam to wi, ɛn i nɔ bin gɛt peshɛnt fɔ tinap na di bɔw dɛn instead na di stɛp.

“Wetin i gɛt na in an de?” Starbuck kray, i pɔynt to sɔntin we di Jamani bin ol wit wɛv. “I nɔ pɔsibul!—na lamp-fida!”

Stubb se, “Nɔto dat, nɔ, nɔ, na kɔfi-pɔt, Mista Starbuck; i de kam ɔf fɔ mek wi wi kɔfi, na di Yarman; yu nɔ si da big tin de de nia am?—dat na in wata we de bɔl. Oh! i fayn, na di Yarman.”

Flask kray se: “Go wit yu, na lamp-fida ɛn ɔyl-kan. I dɔn dɔn wit ɔyl, ɛn i dɔn kam a-beg.”

Ilɛksɛf i tan lɛk se i want fɔ no mɔ fɔ lɛ ɔyl-ship de lɛnt ɔyl na di wɛl-grɔn, ɛn ilɛksɛf i invayt wan agens di ol prɔvab bɔt fɔ kɛr kol go na Nyukasɛl, bɔt stil sɔntɛnde dis kayn tin kin rili apin; ɛn insay di kes we wi de naw, Kpɛntin Dɛrik Di Diya bin rili kɔndɔkt wan lamp-fida lɛk aw Flask bin deklare.

As i de go ɔp di dɛk, Eab bin kam nia am wantɛm wantɛm, ɛn i nɔ bin lisin atɔl wetin i bin gɛt na in an; bɔt insay in brok brok lingo, i nɔ tu te, di Jamani bin sho se i nɔ no bɔt di Wait Wɛl atɔl; wantɛm wantɛm i tɔn di tɔk to in lamp-fida ɛn ɔyl kan, wit sɔm tɔk dɛn we tɔch aw i fɔ tɔn to in hamak na nɛt insay dip daknɛs—in las drɔp Bremen ɔyl nɔ de igen, ɛn nɔto wan flay-fish yet we dɛn dɔn kech fɔ saplae di tin we nɔ de; dɔn bay we i hint se in ship na bin rili wetin na di Fishery dɛn kin kɔl am klin wan (dat na, ɛmti wan), we fit fɔ gɛt di nem Jungfrau ɔ di Vɛjin.

We dɛn gi am di tin dɛn we i nid, Dɛrik bin kɔmɔt; bɔt i nɔ bin dɔn gɛt in ship in sayd, we dɛn bin de es wɛl dɛn ɔp klos wan tɛm frɔm di mast-ed dɛn na di tu ship dɛn; ɛn Dɛrik bin rili want fɔ rɔnata am, dat i nɔ bin stɔp fɔ put in ɔyl-kan ɛn lamp-fida insay di bot, i kil rawnd in bot ɛn mek afta di livaytan lamp-fida dɛn.

Naw, we di gem dɔn go ɔp to leeward, in ɛn di ɔda tri Jaman bot dɛn we nɔ tu te we fala am, bin gɛt bɔku tɛm di stat fɔ di Pequod in kil. Na et wɛl dɛn bin de, we na wan avrej pɔd. Bikɔs dɛn bin no se dɛn denja, dɛn bin de go ɔl togɛda wit big spid stret bifo di briz, ɛn dɛn bin de rɔb dɛn flank dɛn klos klos lɛk bɔku span dɛn we ɔs dɛn gɛt. Dɛn lɛf wan big big wayd wek, lɛk se dɛn de kɔntinyu fɔ opin wan big wayd pepa pan di si.

Ful insay dis wek kwik kwik wan, ɛn bɔku fathoms in di riya, swim wan big, humped ol bul, we bay in kɔmparativ sloslo prɔgrɛs, ɛn bak bay di ɔnusual yɔlɔsh inkrustashɔn dɛn we bin de gro oba am, i bin tan lɛk se i gɛt di janda, ɔ sɔm ɔda sik. If dis wɛl na di pɔd in yon bifo tɛm, i bin tan lɛk se dɛn kin aks kwɛstyɔn bɔt; bikɔs i nɔto kɔstɔm fɔ mek dɛn kayn rɛspɛktful lɛviatan dɛn de sɔshal atɔl. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, i bin stɔp fɔ wek, pan ɔl we fɔ tru, dɛn bak wata go dɔn mek i nɔ ebul fɔ waka, bikɔs di wayt-bɔn ɔ di swɛlin we bin de na in brayt mɔt na bin wan we dɛn bin dɔn dash, lɛk di swɛlin we kin mek we tu kɔrɛnt dɛn we nɔ lɛk dɛn mit. In spɔt bin shɔt, i bin slo, ɛn i bin de wok tranga wan; we de kam wit wan kayn swɛlin we de chok, ɛn spɛn insɛf insay rɔtin swɛt, we de fala strenj sɔbtarenian kɔmɔshɔn dɛn insay am, we bin tan lɛk se i dɔn kɔmɔt na in ɔda bɛrin ɛnd, we de mek di wata we de biɛn am de ɔpbubble.

“Udat gɛt sɔm paregoric?” Stubb se, “i gɛt di bɛlɛ-ak, a de fred. Masta, tink bɔt fɔ gɛt af eka bɛlɛ-aks! Advays briz de ol mad Krismas insay am, bɔy pikin dɛn. Na di fɔs dɔti briz we a ɛva no fɔ blo frɔm astern; bɔt luk, yu dɔn ɛva wɛl yaw so bifo? i fɔ bi, i dɔn lɔs in tila.”

As wan Indiaman we ful-ɔp wit lod we de bia dɔŋ di Hindostan kɔst wit wan dɛk lod we gɛt ɔs dɛn we de fred, careens, bɛri, rol, ɛn wallow we i de go; na so dis ol wɛl bin de es in ol bɔku bɔku wan, ɛn naw ɛn naw i bin de tɔn sɔm pat pan in kɔmbrɔs rib-ɛnd dɛn, i bin de sho di tin we mek i wek we nɔ gɛt wanwɔd na di stɔmp we nɔ natin na in stabɔd fin. If i bin lɔs da fin de na fɛt, ɔ dɛn bɔn am witout am, i bin at fɔ tɔk.

“Na wet smɔl, ol chap, ɛn a go gi yu sling fɔ da an we wund,” kruk Flask kray, ɛn pɔynt to di wɛl-layn nia am.

Starbuck kray se: “Mind se i nɔ de sling yu wit am.” “Gi we, ɔ di Jamani go gɛt am.”

Wit wan intenshɔn ɔl di kɔmbayn rival bot dɛn bin pɔynt fɔ dis wan fish, bikɔs nɔto jɔs in na bin di big wan, ɛn dat mek na di wɛl we gɛt valyu pas ɔl, bɔt i bin de nia dɛn pas ɔlman, ɛn di ɔda wɛl dɛn bin de go wit so big velositi, pan ɔl dat, as i bin lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ dɛn nɔ gri fɔ rɔnata fɔ di tɛm. Na dis junkshɔn di Pequod in kil dɛn bin dɔn shot bay di tri Jaman bot dɛn we dɛn las lɔs; bɔt frɔm di big big stat we i bin dɔn gɛt, Dɛrik in bot bin stil de lid di chase, pan ɔl we ɛvri mɔnt in fɔrina rival dɛn bin de kam nia am. Di onli tin we dɛn bin de fred, na, se, bikɔs i dɔn ɔlrɛdi de nia in mak, i go ebul fɔ dart in ayɛn bifo dɛn ebul fɔ ɔvatak am ɔltogɛda ɛn pas am. As fɔ Dɛrik, i bin tan lɛk se i rili biliv se dis go bi, ɛn wan wan tɛm wit wan we fɔ provok, i bin de shek in lamp-fida pan di ɔda bot dɛn.

“Di dɔg we nɔ gɛt sɔri-at ɛn we nɔ gɛt tɛnki!” Starbuck bin kray; “i de provok ɛn dea mi wit di rili po-bɔks we a ful-ɔp fɔ am nɔto fayv minit bifo!”—dɔn insay in ol tranga wispa—“Gi we, greyhaunds!Dɔg to am!”

“A de tɛl una wetin i bi, man dɛn”—Stɔb kray to in kru—“i de agens mi rilijɔn fɔ mek a vɛks; bɔt a go lɛk fɔ it da wikɛd Yaman de—Pul—nɔto? Una go mek da raskal de bit una? Una lɛk brandi? Wan hogshead of brandy, den, to di bɛst man. Kam, wetin mek sɔm pan una nɔ de bɔs wan blɔd-vessel? Udat in dat bin de drɔp wan anka oba di wata—wi nɔ de muf wan inch—wi dɔn kol, na ya gras de gro na di bot in bottom—ɛn bay di Masta, di mast we de de de bɔd Dis nɔ go du, bɔy pikin dɛn Di shɔt ɛn lɔng pan am na, man dɛn.

“O! si di suds we i de mek!” kray Flask, de dans ɔp ɛn dɔŋ—“Wetin na hump—O, du pail pan di bif—lay lɛk lɔg! Oh! mi bɔbɔ dɛn, du spring—slap-jak ɛn kwahog fɔ sup, yu no, mi bɔbɔ dɛn—bek klam ɛn mafin—oh, du , du , spring,—na wan ɔndrɛd barela—nɔ lɔs am naw—nɔ oh, nɔ! —si dat Yarman—Oh, una nɔ go pul fɔ una duff, mi lads—sog sog Una nɔ lɛk sperm!—a whole bank!—Oh, do , do , do!

Na dis tɛm ya, Dɛrik bin de du di akt fɔ pitch in lamp-fida na di bot dɛn we de go bifo, ɛn bak in ɔyl-kan; sɔntɛm wit di dɛbul we fɔ si am fɔ mek in rival dɛn nɔ ebul fɔ waka, ɛn di sem tɛm fɔ mek in yon we i go ebul fɔ du am kwik kwik wan bay di smɔl smɔl tin we di bak-wɔd tos de mek.

“Di Dɔch dɔg we nɔ gɛt sɛns!” Stubb bin kray. "Pul naw, man dɛm, lɛk fifti tawzin layn-ɔf-bat-ship lod dɛm we gɛt rɛd-hɛd dɛbul dɛm. Wetin d'ye se, Tashtego; yu na di man fɔ snap yu spayna insay tu-ɛn-twɛnti pis fɔ di ɔna fɔ ol Gehɛd? Wetin d'ye se?"

“A se, pul lɛk gɔd-dam,”—di Indian kray.

Di Jaman in provok we di Jaman pipul dɛn bin de provok dɛn wit wamat, bɔt ivin we dɛn bin de mek di Pequod in tri bot dɛn bigin fɔ rɔn klos klos naw; ɛn, so dispɔz, i bin kam nia am fɔ smɔl tɛm. Insay da fayn, lɔs, chivalrous abit de we di edman bin gɛt we i de kam nia in animal we i de it, di tri man dɛn bin tinap wit prawd, wan wan tɛm dɛn bin de bak di afta ɔsman wit wan gladi gladi kray se, “Na de i de slayv, naw! Hurray for the white-ash breeze! Down with the Yarman! Sail over him!”

Bɔt so disayd wan ɔrijinal stat if Dɛrik bin gɛt, se pan ɔl dɛn gallantri, i bin fɔ dɔn pruv se na in win dis res, if nɔto rayt jɔjmɛnt bin kam dɔŋ pan am insay wan krab we kech di bled fɔ in midship ɔrsman. We dis klɔmsi lɔba bin de tray fɔ fri in wayt-ash, ɛn pan ɔl we, bikɔs ɔf dat, Dɛrik in bot bin dɔn nia fɔ kapsay, ɛn i bin de tɛnda go pan in man dɛn wit wan pawaful wamat;—dat na bin gud tɛm fɔ Stabɔk, Stɔb, ɛn Flask. Wit wan ala, dɛn tek wan mɔtal stat fɔ go bifo, ɛn slantingly rɛnj ɔp pan di Jamani in kwata. Wan instant mɔ, ɛn ɔl di 4 bot dɛn bin de dayagɔnik wan na di wɛl in wek wantɛm wantɛm, we dɛn bin de strɛch frɔm dɛn, na ɔl tu di say dɛn, na di fom swel we i mek.

Na bin fayn fayn tin we a bin si, we bin de mek a sɔri pas ɔl, ɛn we bin de mek pɔsin in at pwɛl. Di wɛl bin de go ed naw, ɛn sɛn in spɔt bifo am insay wan jet we dɛn bin de sɔfa ɔltɛm; we in wan po fin bin de bit in sayd wit pen we i bin de fred. Naw to dis an, naw to dat, i yaw in in faltering flayt, ɛn stil na ɛvri billow we i brok, i spasmodically sink in di si, ɔ saydways rol to di skay in wan bit fin. So a dɔn si bɔd we gɛt klip in wing de mek brok brok sɛklɔ dɛn we de fred na di ays, we de tray fɔ natin fɔ rɔnawe pan di pairatik hawk dɛn. Bɔt di bɔd gɛt vɔys, ɛn wit kray kray we i de kray, i go mek pipul dɛn no se i de fred; bɔt di fred we dis big big mumu animal we de na di si bin de fred, dɛn bin chen am ɛn ɛnjɔy am; i nɔ bin gɛt ɛni vɔys, pas da chok briz de tru in spirakl, ɛn dis bin mek di si am sɔri fɔ am we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ tɔk bɔt; pan ɔl we stil, insay in wɔndaful bɔku bɔku, pɔtkul jaw, ɛn ɔltin pawaful tel, inof bin de fɔ mek di man we strɔng pas ɔlman we bin sɔri fɔ am, fred.

We i si naw se bɔt wan rili smɔl tɛm mɔ go gi di Pequod in bot dɛn di advantej, ɛn pas fɔ mek dɛn nɔ ebul fɔ ple in gem so, Dɛrik pik fɔ denja wetin to am go dɔn tan lɛk wan lɔng dart we nɔ kɔmɔn pas ɔl, bifo di las chans go rɔnawe fɔ ɔltɛm.

Bɔt jɔs afta we in harpuoneer tinap fɔ di strok, ɔl di tri tayga dɛn—Queequeg, Tashtego, Daggoo—bin instinkt fɔ jomp na dɛn fut, ɛn tinap na wan dayagɔnal row, dɛn pɔynt dɛn barb dɛn wan tɛm; ɛn dart oba di Jaman harpuoneer in ed, dɛn tri Nantucket ayɛn dɛn go insay di wɛl. Blayndin vapour dɛn we gɛt fom ɛn wayt-faya! Di tri bot dɛn, insay di fɔs wamat we di wɛl bin rɔsh wit ed, bin bɔmp di Jamani wan dɛn na sayd wit so pawa, dat ɔl tu Dɛrik ɛn in kɔnfyus harpuoneer bin tɔn kɔmɔt, ɛn dɛn bin sel oba bay di tri kil dɛn we bin de flay.

“Una nɔ fred, mi bɔta-bɔks dɛn,” Stubb kray, ɛn i de luk dɛn pas wan as i de shot pas; “ye’ll be picked up presently—all right—a si sɔm shak dɛn biɛn—Sɛnt Bɛnard in dɔg dɛn, yu no—relieve distressed travellers. Hurrah! dis na di we fɔ sel naw. Every keel a sanbeam! Hurrah!—Na ya wi go lɛk tri tin ketul dɛn na di tel fɔ wan mad cougar!Dis de put mi na maynd fɔ fast to ɛlifant insay a tilbury on a plain—mek di wil-spokes flay, boys, wen yu fasten to am dat way, wen yu strike a hill dis na di we aw yu felo de fil wen e de go na Davy Jones—all a rush down an endless inclined plane!

Bɔt di rɔn we di monsta bin rɔn na bin shɔt tɛm. We i gi wan gasp wantɛm wantɛm, i mek sawnd wit trɔbul. Wit wan gratin rɔsh, di tri layn dɛn bin de flay rawnd di loggerheads wit so pawa dat fɔ gouge dip grooves insay dɛn; pan ɔl we di hapun dɛn bin de fred so dat dis kwik sawnd go mek di layn dɛn taya i nɔ go te igen, dat yuz ɔl dɛn sɛnsful pawa, dɛn bin de kech ripit smok tɔn wit di rop fɔ ol; te las las—bikɔs ɔf di strɛs we bin de strɛch we di bot dɛn bin de layn wit lid, usay di tri rop dɛn bin de go stret dɔŋ insay di blu—di gɔnwɛl dɛn na di bɔw dɛn bin de klos ivin wit di wata, we di tri stɛp dɛn bin de tilt ay ay wan na di ays. Ɛn i nɔ tu te, di wɛl nɔ bin de blo igen, fɔ sɔm tɛm dɛn bin de da kayn we de, dɛn bin de fred fɔ spɛn mɔ layn, pan ɔl we di pozishɔn bin tik smɔl. Bɔt pan ɔl we dɛn dɔn pul bot dɛn dɔŋ ɛn lɔs dis we, na dis “hold on,” as dɛn kɔl am; dis de huk wit di shap barb dɛn we de na in layf bɔdi frɔm di bak; dis na di wan we kin mek di Livaytan sɔfa bɔku tɛm fɔ mek i nɔ tu te i grap bak fɔ mit in ɛnimi dɛn shap lans. Bɔt stil nɔ fɔ tɔk bɔt di prɔblɛm we di tin gɛt, wi fɔ dawt if dis kɔs na di bɛst ɔltɛm; bikɔs i rili mek sɛns fɔ tink se di mɔ we di wɛl we dɛn dɔn bit de ɔnda wata, na di mɔ i de taya. Bikɔs, bikɔs ɔf di big big say we i de—insay wan sperm wɛl we dɔn ful-ɔp wit sɔntin we nɔ rich 2000 skwea fit—di prɛshɔn we di wata de prɛshɔ rili bɔku. Wi ɔl no wetin na wan wɔndaful atmosfɛre wet we wisɛf tinap ɔnda; ivin ya, ɔp-grɔn, na di ays; so, di lod we wɛl kin gɛt, we kin kɛr wan kɔlɔn we gɛt tu ɔndrɛd fat si na in bak! I fɔ at le ikwal to di wet we fifti atmosfɛs gɛt. Wan wɛlman dɔn tɔk se i wet fɔ twɛnti ship dɛn we de na di layn fɔ fɛt, wit ɔl dɛn gɔn dɛn, ɛn stɔ dɛn, ɛn man dɛn we de insay.

As di tri bot dɛn de ledɔm de na da si de we de rɔl saful wan, ɛn dɛn de luk dɔŋ insay in blu midulnɛt we go de sote go; ɛn as nɔto wan kray ɔ kray ɛni kayn we, nɔto so, lɛk se wan rip ɔ bɔbul kɔmɔt na in dip; wetin landman go dɔn tink, se ɔnda ɔl da kwayɛt ɛn kol at de, di big big animal we de na di si bin de rɔtin ɛn rɔtin bikɔs i de fil pen! Nɔto et inch rop we de pepɛndikul bin de na di bɔw dɛn. I tan lɛk se i pɔsibul fɔ biliv se bay tri dɛn kayn tint trɛd dɛn de, dɛn bin de suspɛnd di big Livaytan lɛk di big wet to et dez klok. Stɔp? ɛn to wetin? To tri bits of bod. Na dis na di krichɔ we dɛn bin de tɔk bɔt wan tɛm wit win so—“Yu kin ful-ɔp in skin wit ayɛn we gɛt tik? ɔ yu kin ful-ɔp in skin wit fish-spia? Di sɔd fɔ di pɔsin we le am nɔ ebul fɔ ol, di spia, di dart, ɔ di habjɛn: i tek ayɛn lɛk straw, di aro nɔ go ebul fɔ mek i rɔnawe, dɛn kin tek dart as stɔbul; de laf we spia de shek!” Dis na di krichɔ? dis na in? O! dat di tin dɛn we nɔ de apin fɔ fala di prɔfɛt dɛn. Bikɔs wit di trɛnk we wan tawzin shɔl gɛt na in tel, Livaytan bin dɔn rɔn in ed ɔnda di mawnten dɛn na di si, fɔ ayd am frɔm di Pɛkud in fish-spia dɛn!

Insay da aftanun san layt we bin de slop, di shado dɛn we di tri bot dɛn bin de sɛn dɔŋ ɔnda di wata, go mɔs dɔn lɔng ɛn brayt fɔ shed af-af Zaksis in ami. Udat go no aw dɛn kayn big big phantom dɛn de we bin de flay oba in ed go dɔn mek di wɛl we wund bin rili fred!

“Una tinap nia, man dɛm; i de stɔp,” na so Starbuck kray, as di tri layn dɛn de shek shek wantɛm wantɛm na di wata, difrɛn we de kɔndɔkt ɔp to dɛn, lɛk bay magnɛtik waya, di layf ɛn day we di wɛl de blo, so dat ɔlman we de ɔs de fil dɛn na in sidɔm ples. Di nɛks tɛm, we dɛn bin rili fri bɔku pan di strɛs we bin de go dɔŋ na di bɔw dɛn, di bot dɛn bin de bounce ɔp wantɛm wantɛm, lɛk aw smɔl ays fil go du, we wan bɔku bɔku wayt bea dɛn de fred frɔm am ɛn go insay di si.

“Haul in! Haul in!” Starbuck kray bak; “i de rayz.”

Di layn dɛn, we, i nɔ bin izi fɔ mek dɛn gɛt wan an brayt, naw dɛn bin de insay lɔng kwik kɔyl dɛn we dɛn bin de trowe bak ɔl de drip insay di bot dɛn, ɛn i nɔ tu te, di wɛl brok wata we bin de tu ship lɔng frɔm di ɔntinman dɛn.

Da wɔndaful oriental stori de naw fɔ rɛdi frɔm di Shaster, we gi wi di dread Vishnoo, wan pan di tri pipul dɛn we de insay di gɔd we di Indu dɛn gɛt; gi wi dis divayn Vishnoo insɛf fɔ wi Masta;—Vishnoo, we, bay di fɔs wan pan in tɛn inkarnɛshɔn dɛn na dis wɔl, dɔn sɛt apat ɛn mek di wɛl oli fɔ ɔltɛm. We Brahma, ɔ di Gɔd fɔ Gɔd dɛn, di Shasta se, bin disayd fɔ mek di wɔl bak afta wan pan di tɛm we i dɔn skata, i bɔn Vishnoo, fɔ bi di prɛsidɛnt fɔ di wok; bɔt di Vedas, ɔ mistik buk dɛn, we i go tan lɛk se Vishnoo bin rili impɔtant fɔ rid dɛn bifo i bigin fɔ mek di tin dɛn, ɛn dat mek dɛn go dɔn gɛt sɔntin we tan lɛk prɛktikal hint to yɔŋ akitɔk dɛn, dɛn Vedas ya bin de ledɔm na di bɔt ɔf di wata; so Vishnoo bin bi bɔdi insay wan wɛl, ɛn we i bin de sawnd dɔŋ insay am te i dip pasmak, i bin sev di oli volyum dɛn. So, yu tink se dis Vishnoo nɔto bin wɛlman? ivin lɛk aw dɛn kin kɔl man we de rayd ɔs man ɔs?

Pɛsiɔs, Sɛnt Jɔj, Ɛkyulis, Jona, ɛn Vishnu! na memba-rol de fo yu! Us klab bɔt di wɛlman in yon kin ed ɔf lɛk dat?





CHAPTER 83. Jona bin de tink bɔt am.

Dɛn bin tɔk bɔt di istri stori bɔt Jona ɛn di wɛl insay di chapta bifo dis wan. Naw sɔm Nantucketers dɛn kin rili nɔ trɔst dis istri stori bɔt Jona ɛn di wɛl. Bɔt afta dat, sɔm Grik ɛn Roman dɛn bin de we nɔ bin biliv, we bikɔs dɛn bin difrɛn frɔm di ɔtodɔks pegan dɛn we bin de insay dɛn tɛm, dɛn bin ikwal dawt di stori bɔt Ɛkiul ɛn di wɛl, ɛn Ariɔn ɛn di dɔlfin; ɛn yet dɛn dawt bɔt dɛn tradishɔn dɛn de nɔ mek dɛn tradishɔn dɛn de wan whit di less facts, fɔ ɔl dat.

Wan ol Sag-Habɔ wɛlman in men rizin we mek i aks kwɛstyɔn bɔt di Ibru stori na dis:—I bin gɛt wan pan dɛn fayn fayn ol Baybul dɛn de, we dɛn bin mek fayn fayn wan wit plet dɛn we pipul dɛn kin want fɔ no mɔ ɛn we nɔ gɛt fɔ du wit sayɛns; wan pan dɛn bin tinap fɔ Jona in wɛl we gɛt tu spɔt dɛn na in ed—na wan patikyula tin we na tru nɔmɔ we i kam pan wan kayn Livaytan (di Rayt Wɛl, ɛn di difrɛn kayn we dɛn we de na da ɔda de), we di fishaman dɛn gɛt dis wɔd bɔt, “Wan peni rol go chok am”; in swɛla so rili smɔl. Bɔt, to dis, Bisop Jɛb in ansa we i bin de tink bɔt dɔn rɛdi. I nɔ nid, di Bisop hint, fɔ mek wi tek Jona as pɔsin we dɛn bɛr insay di wɛl in bɛlɛ, bɔt as pɔsin we dɔn lod fɔ sɔm tɛm na sɔm pat na in mɔt. Ɛn dis tan lɛk se i rizinɔs inof insay di gud Bisop. Fɔ tru, di Rayt Wɛl in mɔt go akɔmod tu-tri wist-tebul dɛn, ɛn kɔmfyut fɔ sidɔm ɔl di pleya dɛn. I go bi bak se Jona go dɔn de insay wan ol tut; bɔt, we yu tink bɔt am bak, di Rayt Wɛl nɔ gɛt tut.

Wan ɔda rizin we Sag-Harbor (i bin go wit da nem de) bin ɛnkɔrej fɔ in nɔ gɛt fet pan dis tin bɔt di prɔfɛt, na sɔntin we nɔ klia we i de tɔk bɔt in bɔdi we dɛn put na jel ɛn di wɛl in gastric jus. Bɔt dis ɔbjɛshɔn sɛf de fɔdɔm na grɔn, bikɔs wan Jamani ɛgzibitist tink se Jona go mɔs dɔn rɔnawe pan di bɔdi we de flɔt we wan day wɛl—ivin as di Frɛnch sojaman dɛn we bin de na di Rɔshian kampen bin tɔn dɛn ɔs dɛn we dɔn day to tɛnt, ɛn kray insay dɛn. Apat frɔm dat, ɔda kɔntinɛntal kɔmɛntatɔ dɛn dɔn tɔk se we dɛn trowe Jona na di wata frɔm di Jopa ship, i bin mek i rɔnawe wantɛm wantɛm go na ɔda bot we bin de nia de, sɔm bot we gɛt wɛl fɔ figa ed; ɛn, a go ad se, sɔntɛm dɛn kin kɔl am “Di Wɛl,” as sɔm kraf dɛn de baptayz tide di “Shak,” di “Gɔl,” di “Igl.” Nɔto pipul dɛn we sabi fɔ ɛksplen di Baybul nɔ bin want fɔ tɔk se di wɛl we dɛn tɔk bɔt na Jona in buk bin jɔs min sɔntin we de protɛkt in layf—na bag we gɛt briz we dɛn dɔn blo—we di prɔfɛt we bin de pan denja bin swim go, ɛn so i bin sev frɔm wan bad bad tin we bin apin to am. So, i tan lɛk se po Sag-Habɔ wɔs ɔlsay. Bɔt i bin stil gɛt ɔda rizin we mek i nɔ gɛt fet. Na dis, if a mɛmba rayt: Jona bin swɛla di wɛl na di Mɛditarenian Si, ɛn afta tri dez, i vɔmit sɔmsay insay tri dez waka frɔm Ninivɛ, wan siti we de na di Taygris, we rili pas tri dez waka fɔ krɔs di say we de nia di Mɛditarenian si. Aw dat kin bi?

Bɔt yu tink se no ɔda we nɔ bin de fɔ mek di wɛl go land di prɔfɛt insay da shɔt say de frɔm Ninivɛ? Yɛs. Sɔntɛm i bin fɔ dɔn kɛr am go rawnd na di rod we de na di Kep fɔ Gud Op. Bɔt nɔ fɔ tɔk bɔt di pas we dɛn pas na di wan ol Mɛditarenian, ɛn wan ɔda pas we go ɔp di Pashian Galf ɛn Rɛd Si, dis kayn we fɔ tink go involv fɔ mek dɛn go ɔlsay na Afrika ɔlsay insay tri dez, nɔto fɔ tɔk bɔt di Taygris wata dɛn, nia di say we Ninivɛ bin de, we tu shalo fɔ ɛni wɛl fɔ swim insay ali wan de go tek di ɔnɔ fɔ fɛn da big edland de frɔm Batɔlɔmi Dayaz, we pipul dɛn sabi fɔ fɛn am, ɛn so i go mek di istri tide bi layman.

Bɔt ɔl dɛn fulish agyumɛnt dɛn ya we ol Sag-Habɔ bin de tɔk, bin jɔs sho se i prawd fɔ rizin—na tin we stil de kɔndɛm am mɔ, bikɔs i si se i nɔ bin lan bɛtɛ tin pas wetin i bin dɔn pik frɔm di san ɛn di si. A se i jɔs de sho in fulish, impious prawd, ɛn abominable, devilish rebellion agens di reverend clergy. Bikɔs na wan Pɔtyugis Katolik prist, dis sem aidia bɔt Jona fɔ go na Ninivɛ tru di Kep fɔ Gud Op, dɛn bin advans am as sayn fɔ mek di jenɛral mirekul big. Ɛn na so i bin bi. Apat frɔm dat, te tide, di Turk dɛn we gɛt bɔku sɛns biliv tranga wan pan Jona in istri stori. Ɛn lɛk tri ɔndrɛd ia so dɔn pas, wan Inglish travulman na wan ol Haris Voyages, tɔk bɔt wan Turkish Moskit we dɛn bil fɔ ɔnɔ Jona, usay Moskit na bin wan mirekul lamp we bin de bɔn we nɔ gɛt ɛni ɔyl.





CHAPTER 84. Pitchpoling we pɔsin kin tɔk.

Fɔ mek dɛn ebul fɔ rɔn izi wan ɛn kwik kwik wan, dɛn kin anɔynt di aks dɛn na di kariɔt dɛn; ɛn fɔ di sem tin, sɔm pipul dɛn we de kech wɛl kin du di sem tin we dɛn de du na dɛn bot; dɛn kin gris di bɔt. Wi nɔ fɔ dawt bak se bikɔs dis kayn we fɔ du tin nɔ go ebul fɔ du ɛni bad tin, i nɔ go gɛt ɛni bɛnifit we pɔsin nɔ de tek mek natin; we yu tink bɔt se ɔyl ɛn wata na ɛnimi; dat ɔyl na tin we de slayv, ɛn di tin we wi de si na fɔ mek di bot slayv wit maynd. Kwik bin biliv tranga wan fɔ anɔynt in bot, ɛn wan mɔnin i nɔ tu te afta we di Jamani ship we nem Jungfrau nɔ bin de igen, i bin tek mɔ pas di pen we i bin de fil we i bin de du da wok de; krawl ɔnda in bɔt, usay i bin de hang oba di sayd, ɛn rɔb insay di unctuousness lɛk se i de tray tranga wan fɔ inshɔr wan krop fɔ ia frɔm di kraft in bold kil. I bin tan lɛk se i bin de wok fɔ obe sɔm patikyula prɛzɛnt. Ɛn i nɔ bin kɔntinyu fɔ de we di tin we apin nɔ mek ɛni rizin.

Towards noon, dɛn bin de mɛn wɛl dɛn; bɔt jɔs afta di ship go dɔŋ to dɛn, dɛn tɔn ɛn rɔnawe wit kwik kwik wan; wan flay we nɔ bin de ɔganayz, lɛk di barj dɛn we Kliopatra bin de flay frɔm Aktiɔm.

Bɔt pan ɔl dat, di bot dɛn bin de rɔnata dɛn, ɛn na Stubb in yon bin de bifo. We Tashtego bin tray tranga wan, las las i bin ebul fɔ plant wan ayɛn; bɔt di wɛl we dɛn bin bit, we nɔ bin de sawnd atɔl, bin stil kɔntinyu fɔ flay ɔrizɔntal, ɛn i bin de flay mɔ. Dɛn kayn we ya we dɛn kin strɛch di ayɛn we dɛn dɔn plant we nɔ kin stɔp, fɔ pul am kwik ɔ leta. I bin bi impɔtant fɔ lans di wɛl we de flay, ɔ fɔ satisfay fɔ lɔs am. Bɔt fɔ pul di bot go ɔp to in flank nɔ bin pɔsibul, i bin swim so fast ɛn vɛks bad bad wan. Wetin bin lɛf afta dat?

Pan ɔl di wɔndaful tin dɛn ɛn di sɛns we dɛn kin yuz, di slɛt dɛn we dɛn kin yuz wit an ɛn di bɔku bɔku tin dɛn we dɛn kin du, we dɛn kin fos di vetɛna wɛlman fɔ du bɔku tɛm, nɔbɔdi nɔ pas da fayn fayn we de wit di lans we dɛn kɔl pitchpoling. Smɔl sɔd, ɔ brayt sɔd, pan ɔl in ɛksesaiz dɛn nɔ de bost natin lɛk am. I jɔs impɔtant wit wan wɛl we de rɔn we dɔn wok fɔ lɔng tɛm; in big big tin ɛn di tin we i gɛt na di wɔndaful distans we dɛn kin dart di lɔng lans kɔrɛkt wan frɔm wan bot we de rɔk bad bad wan ɛn we de jɔk, ɔnda big big edway. If yu put stɛl ɛn wud, di wan ol spia lɔng lɛk tɛn ɔ twɛlv fit; di stik smɔl pas di wan we di harpun gɛt, ɛn bak na wan tin we layt—pain. Dɛn kin put wan smɔl rop we dɛn kɔl wɔp, we lɔng pasmak, we dɛn kin yuz fɔ pul am bak na di an afta dɛn dɔn dart am.

Bɔt bifo wi go bifo, i impɔtant fɔ tɔk ya, se pan ɔl we dɛn kin pitch di harpun di sem we wit di lans, bɔt dɛn nɔ kin du am ɔltɛm; ɛn we dɛn du am, i stil nɔ kin wok fayn, bikɔs di harpun in wet ɛn i nɔ lɔng we yu kɔmpia am wit di lans, we infakt kin bi siriɔs prɔblɛm dɛn. So, as jenɛral tin, yu fɔ fɔs go fast to wɛl, bifo ɛni pitchpoling kam insay.

Luk naw na Stubb; wan man we frɔm in kɔmɛnt, deliberate kol ɛn equanimity in di direst imejensi, bin spɛshal kwalifay fɔ excel in pitchpoling. Luk am; i tinap stret na di bot we de flay in bɔw we dɛn kin tos; we dɛn rap am wit fleecy fom, di towing wɛl de fɔti fit bifo. We i de ol di lɔng lans layt wan, i de luk tu ɔ tri tɛm along in lɔng fɔ si if i rili stret, Stub de wispa gɛda di kɔyl fɔ di wɔp wit wan an, so dat i go mek in fri ɛnd tayt na in an, ɛn lɛf di ɔda wan dɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnitin fɔ ambɔg am. Dɔn i ol di lans ful-ɔp bifo in wesband in midul, ɛn i lɛvul am to di wɛl; we, we i kɔba am wit am, i de prɛs di bɔt-ɛnd na in an ɔltɛm, ɛn dis de mek di pɔynt es ɔp te di wɛpɔn tinap fayn fayn wan pan in an, fayvtin fit na di ays. I de tink bɔt yu lɛk se na jɔg, i de balans wan lɔng stik na in chin. Neks moment wit wan rapid, nemless impulse, in wan superb lofty arch di brayt stɛl span di fom distans, ɛn de shek shek na di layf ples fɔ di wɛl. Bifo i yuz wata we de shayn, naw i de pul rɛd blɔd.

“Dat bin drɛb di spigot kɔmɔt insay am!” Stubb bin kray. “'Tis Julay in imɔtal Fɔs; ɔl di watawɛl dɛn fɔ rɔn wayn tide! Would naw, it was old Orleans wiski, ɔ ol Ohio, ɔ unspeakable old Monongahela! Dɔn, Tashtego, lad, a go mek una ol wan kanakin to di jet, ɛn wi go drink rawnd am! Yea, fɔ tru, at dɛn de alayv, wi bin fɔ dɔn brew choice in di spred of his punch spɔt-ol de, ɛn frɔm da layf panch-bɔl de kwaf di tin dɛn we gɛt layf.”

Bak ɛn bak to dis kayn gem, dɛn kin ripit di dart we gɛt sɛns, di spia kin kam bak to in masta lɛk greyhaund we dɛn ol wit skilful lis. Di wɛl we de sɔfa de go insay in flurry; di tow-layn dɔn slak, ɛn di pitchpoler de drɔp biɛn, fold in an dɛn, ɛn mumu de wach di monsta we de day.





CHAPTER 85. Di Fawndeshɔn.

Dat fɔ siks tawzin ia—ɛn nɔbɔdi nɔ no ɔmɔs milyɔn ej bifo—di big big wɛl dɛn fɔ dɔn de spɔt ɔlsay na di si, ɛn sprink ɛn mistif di gadin dɛn na di dip, lɛk bɔku bɔku pɔt dɛn we de sprink ɔ mistif; ɛn se fɔ sɔm sɛnti ia bak, bɔku bɔku ɔntinman dɛn fɔ dɔn de nia di wɛl in watawɛl, de wach dɛn sprinklin ɛn spɔt ya—se ɔl dis fɔ bi, ɛn yet, se te to dis blɛsin minit (fayvtin ɛn kwata minit pas wan oklɔk PM PM fɔ dis siksti de insay Disɛmba, AD 1851), i fɔ stil de bi prɔblɛm, if dɛn spɔt ya na, afta ɔl, rili wata, ɔ natin pas vapour—fɔ tru, dis na sɔntin we wi fɔ notis.

So, lɛ wi luk dis tin, wit sɔm intrestin tin dɛn we de apin. Ɔlman no se bay di pekul kɔni we dɛn gil dɛn gɛt, di fini trayb dɛn injɔnal de blo di briz we ɔltɛm dɛn kin jɔyn wit di ɛlimɛnt we dɛn kin swim insay; so, ɛring ɔ kɔd kin liv fɔ wan ɔndrɛd ia, ɛn i nɔ go ɛva es in ed ɔp wan tɛm. Bɔt bikɔs ɔf in mak intanɛnt strɔkchɔ we de gi am rɛgyula lɔng, lɛk mɔtalman in yon, di wɛl kin jɔs liv bay we i inhal di disɛngaj ɛya na di opin atmosfɛs. So di nid fɔ in visit dɛn to di ɔpa wɔl wan wan tɛm. Bɔt i nɔ kin ebul fɔ blo tru in mɔt pan ɛni digri, bikɔs, insay in ɔdinari abit, dɛn bɛr di Spɛm Wɛl in mɔt at le et fit ɔnda di wata; ɛn wetin stil pas dat, in winda nɔ gɛt ɛnitin fɔ du wit in mɔt. Nɔ, i de blo tru in spirakul nɔmɔ; ɛn dis de ɔp in ed.

If a se, se insay ɛni krichɔ fɔ blo na jɔs wan wok we impɔtant fɔ mek i gɛt layf, bikɔs i de pul wan patikyula tin kɔmɔt na di ays, we afta dat we dɛn kam wit di blɔd, i de gi di blɔd in prinsipul we de mek i gɛt layf, a nɔ tink se a go mek mistek; pan ɔl we a kin yuz sɔm sayɛns wɔd dɛn we nɔ impɔtant. Lɛ wi tek am se, if dɛn ebul fɔ blo ɔl di blɔd we de na man wit wan briz, i kin sidɔm na in nos ɛn nɔ kam wit ɔda wan fɔ lɔng tɛm. Dat min se, da tɛm de i go liv we i nɔ de blo. Pan ɔl we i tan lɛk se i nɔ kɔmɔn, dis na di kayn tin we kin apin to di wɛl, we kin liv sistamatically, bay intaval, in ful awa ɛn mɔ (we i de dɔŋ) we i nɔ pul wan briz, ɔ so mɔ as in ɛni we i inhal wan patikyula briz; bikɔs, mɛmba se i nɔ gɛt ɛni gil. Aw dis kin bi? Bitwin in rib ɛn na ɛni say na in spayna dɛn de gi am wan wɔndaful Krit labirint we involv we gɛt vessel dɛn we tan lɛk vermicelli, we di vessel dɛn, we i kɔmɔt na di say we i de, kin kɔmɔt kpatakpata wit blɔd we gɛt ɔksijɛn. So dat fɔ wan awa ɔ mɔ, we na wan tawzin pat na di si, i kin kɛr bɔku bɔku trɛnk insay am, jɔs lɛk aw di kamɛl we de krɔs di dɛzat we nɔ gɛt wata kin kɛr bɔku bɔku drink fɔ yuz tumara bambay na in 4 sɔpɔtmɛnt bɛlɛ dɛn. Di anatomical fact of dis labyrinth na tin we pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ agyu bɔt; ɛn se di sɔpɔzishɔn we dɛn bil pan am na rizin ɛn tru, i tan lɛk se i rili kɔgent to mi, we a tink bɔt di ɔda we we da leviathan de nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɛksplen fɔ mek in spɔt dɛn kɔmɔt , lɛk aw di fishaman dɛn kin tɔk am. Dis na wetin a min. If dɛn nɔ ambɔg am, we i rayz na di wata, di Spɛm Wɛl go kɔntinyu fɔ de de fɔ sɔm tɛm we i rili yunifom wit ɔl in ɔda rayz dɛn we i nɔ ambɔg am. Say i de de ilevin minit, ɛn jet sɛvinti tɛm, dat min se i de blo sɛvinti briz; dɔn ɛnitɛm we i grap bak, i go shɔ se i gɛt in sɛvinti briz bak, to wan minit. Naw, if afta i tek sɔm briz yu ala am, so dat i go mek i sawnd, i go de rɔnawe ɔltɛm bak fɔ mek i fayn fɔ di briz we i kin gɛt ɔltɛm. Ɛn nɔto te dɛn tɛl dɛn sɛvinti briz dɛn de, i go dɔn go dɔŋ fɔ de aut in ful tɛm dɔŋ. Bɔt rimark se pan difrɛn pipul dɛn dɛn rit ya difrɛn; bɔt insay ɛni wan, dɛn fiba. Naw, wetin mek di wɛl fɔ insist so fɔ mek in spɔt dɛn kɔmɔt, pas nɔmɔ fɔ ful-ɔp in rizɔva fɔ di briz, bifo i go dɔŋ fɔ gud? Aw i klia bak se dis nid fɔ mek di wɛl rayz de mek i gɛt ɔl di bad bad tin dɛn we kin apin we i de rɔnata am. Bikɔs nɔto huk ɔ nɛt dɛn go ebul fɔ kech dis big big lɛviatan, we i de travul fɔ wan tawzin fat ɔnda di san layt. So, nɔto so yu skil, O ɔntin, as di big big tin dɛn we yu nid we de mek yu win!

Insay man, di briz de go bifo ɔltɛm—wan briz de jɔs sav fɔ tu ɔ tri pulseshɔn; so dat ɛni ɔda biznɛs we i gɛt fɔ atɛnd, we i wek ɔ we i de slip, briz i fɔ, ɔ day i go du am. Bɔt di Spɛm Wɛl jɔs de blo lɛk wan sɛvin ɔ Sɔnde pan in tɛm.

Dɛn dɔn tɔk se di wɛl kin jɔs blo tru in spɔt-ol; if i kin tru tru fɔ ad se in spɔt dɛn miks wit wata, den a opinion se wi fɔ furnish wit di rizin we mek i tan lɛk se in sɛns fɔ smɛl dɔn dɔnawe wit am; bikɔs di wangren tin bɔt am we at ɔl ansa to in nos na da idɛntik spɔt-ol de; ɛn bikɔs i bin so klos wit tu tin dɛn, dɛn nɔ bin ebul fɔ tink se i go gɛt di pawa fɔ smɛl. Bɔt bikɔs ɔf di mistek we di spɔt gɛt—ilɛksɛf na wata ɔ if na vapour—dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ no ɔltin yet pan dis ed. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, i shɔ se di Spɛm Wɛl nɔ gɛt di rayt ɔlfaktɔri. Bɔt wetin i want frɔm dɛn? Nɔ ros, nɔ vayɛl, nɔ Kɔlɔn-wata na di si.

Dɔn bak, as in winda paip jɔs de opin insay di tyub we de na in kanal we de spɔt, ɛn as da lɔng kanal de—lɛk di big Ɛri Kanal—dɛn gɛt wan kayn lɔk (we de opin ɛn lɔk) fɔ mek di briz kɔntinyu fɔ de dɔŋ ɔ fɔ mek wata nɔ de go ɔp, na dat mek di wɛl nɔ gɛt vɔys; pas yu insul am bay we yu se, we i so strenj rumbul, i de tɔk tru in nos. Bɔt afta dat bak, wetin di wɛl gɛt fɔ tɔk? Na smɔl tɛm nɔmɔ a dɔn no ɛni dip pɔsin we gɛt ɛnitin fɔ tɔk to dis wɔl, pas nɔmɔ dɛn fos am fɔ stɔp sɔntin bay we i go gɛt mɔni fɔ liv. O! gladi se di wɔl na pɔsin we sabi lisin gud gud wan!

Naw, di kanal we de kɔmɔt na di Spɛm Wɛl, we dɛn bin mek mɔ lɛk aw i de fɔ mek di briz go, ɛn fɔ sɔm fit dɛn we dɛn le along, ɔrizɔntal wan, jɔs ɔnda di ɔpa say na in ed, ɛn smɔl to wan say; dis kanal we pɔsin kin want fɔ no mɔ, tan lɛk gas-paip we dɛn put na siti na wan say na strit. Bɔt di kwɛstyɔn de kam bak if dis gas-paip na wata-paip bak; in ɔda wɔd, if di spɔt we di Spɛm Wɛl de kɔmɔt na di jɔs vapour we di briz we dɛn pul, ɔ if da briz we dɛn pul de miks wit wata we dɛn tek insay di mɔt, ɛn kɔmɔt tru di spirakul. i sכri se di mכt de kכmכnik indaykt wan wit di kanal we de spכt; bɔt dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ pruv se dis na fɔ di rizin fɔ mek wata kɔmɔt na di spirakl. Bikɔs i go tan lɛk se di tin we impɔtant pas ɔl fɔ du dat na, we we i de gi tin fɔ it, i aksidɛntli tek wata. Bɔt di it we di Spɛm Wɛl de it de fa fawe ɔnda di wata, ɛn na de i nɔ go ebul fɔ spɔt ilɛksɛf i want. Apat frɔm dat, if yu luk am gud gud wan, ɛn taym am wit yu wach, yu go si se we dɛn nɔ de mɔna am, wan raym we nɔ de kɔmɔt biɛn di tɛm dɛn we i de jet ɛn di ɔdinari tɛm dɛn we i de blo.

Bɔt wetin mek fɔ pesh pɔsin wit ɔl dis rizin bɔt di tɔpik? Tɔk klia wan! Yu dɔn si am de swɛt; dɔn deklare wetin na di spɔt; yu noh kin no wata na di briz? Mi dia sa, na dis wɔl i nɔ so izi fɔ sɛtul dɛn klin tin ya. A dɔn ɛva si yu klin tin dɛn we gɛt knot pas ɔl. Ɛn as fɔ dis wɛl spɔt, i go lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ yu tinap insay, ɛn yet yu nɔ go disayd wetin i bi.

Di sɛntral bɔdi pan am ayd insay di sno we de shayn spak mist we de kɔba am; ɛn aw yu go rili no if ɛni wata de fɔdɔm frɔm am, we, ɔltɛm, we yu de nia wɛl fɔ si in spɔt klos, i de insay wan wɔndaful krawd, di wata de rɔn rawnd am. Ɛn if na dɛn kayn tɛm dɛn de yu fɔ tink se yu rili si se di wata we de kɔmɔt na di spɔt de drɔp, aw yu go no se dɛn nɔ jɔs de kɔndens frɔm in vapour; ɔ aw yu no se nɔto dɛn sem kayn drɔp dɛn de we de ɔp ɔp na di spɔt-ol fishɔ, we dɛn kin kɔntrasunk insay di wɛl in ed in ed? Fɔ ivin we i de swim kwayɛt wan tru di mid-de si insay wan ples we kol, wit in ɛlevɛt hump we dɛn dɔn dray wit san lɛk dromedary in na di dɛzat; ivin da tɛm de, di wɛl kin kɛr smɔl wata na in ed ɔltɛm, jɔs lɛk aw ɔnda san we de shayn, sɔntɛnde yu go si wan say we de insay wan rɔk we ful-ɔp wit ren.

Ɛn i nɔ mek sɛns atɔl fɔ mek di ɔntinman want fɔ no mɔ fɔ tɔch di rayt we aw di wɛl spɔt tan. I nɔ go du fɔ am fɔ de luk insay de, ɛn put in fes insay de. Yu nɔ go ebul fɔ go wit yu pitcha na dis watawɛl ɛn ful-ɔp am, ɛn briŋ am go. Bikɔs ivin we yu kam insay smɔl kɔntakt wit di ɔda, vaypa shred dɛn na di jet, we go apin bɔku tɛm, yu skin go fiva smat, frɔm di akrid we di tin so tɔch am. Ɛn a no wan, we de kam insay stil klos kɔntakt wit di spɔt, ilɛksɛf wit sɔm sayɛns tin we i de si, ɔ ɔda we, a nɔ go ebul fɔ se, di skin dɔn pul kɔmɔt na in chɛst ɛn an. So, pan wɛlman dɛn, dɛn kin tek di spɔt as pɔyzin; dɛn kin tray fɔ avɔyd am. Wan ɔda tin; A dɔn yɛri dɛn se, ɛn a nɔ de dawt am bɛtɛ, se if di jet fayn fayn wan spɔt insay yu yay, i go blayn yu. Di tin we mek sɛns pas ɔl we di invɛstigatɔ kin du da tɛm de, i tan lɛk to mi, na fɔ lɛf dis spɔt we de kil pɔsin.

Stil, wi kin hypothesize, ivin if wi nɔ ebul fɔ pruv ɛn establish. Mi hypothesis na dis: dat di spout na natin pas mist. Ɛn apat frɔm ɔda rizin dɛn, fɔ dis kɔnklushɔn a de impɛl, bay kɔnsidareshɔn dɛn we de tɔch di big inhɛrɛnt digniti ɛn sublimity fɔ di Spɛm Wɛl; A nɔ de si am as kɔmɔn, shalo pɔsin, bikɔs na tru tin we nɔ gɛt wan dawt se dɛn nɔ de ɛva si am na sawnd, ɔ nia sho; ɔl di ɔda wɛl dɛn kin bi so sɔntɛnde. I kin tink gud wan ɛn i kin rili dip. Ɛn a biliv se frɔm di ed fɔ ɔl di ponderous profound beings, lɛk Plato, Pyrrho, di Dɛbul, Jupiter, Dante, ɛn ɔda wan dɛn, ɔltɛm wan patikyula semi-visible stim de go ɔp, we i de insay di akt fɔ tink dip tink dɛn. We a bin de rayt smɔl buk bɔt Eternity, a bin gɛt di want fɔ no mɔ fɔ put miro bifo mi; ɛn ere lɔng saw riflɛkt de, wan kɔriɔs involv wom ɛn undulation na di atmosfɛs oba mi ed. Di mɔstɔ we nɔ de chenj na mi ia, we a bin de insay dip tink, afta siks kɔp ɔt ti na mi tint shingled ɔpgarɛt, fɔ wan Ɔgɔst midulnɛt; dis tan lɛk se na ɔda argumɛnt fɔ di sɔpɔzishɔn we de ɔp.

Ɛn aw i de mek wi prawd bɔt di pawaful, mist monsta, fɔ si am we i de travul wit sɔri-at tru wan kol si we kol; in big, saful ed we wan kanopi we gɛt vapour, we i de tink bɔt we i nɔ ebul fɔ tɔk to, we i de tink bɔt, ɛn da vapour de—as yu go si am sɔntɛnde—we renbo de gi glori, lɛk se Ɛvin insɛf dɔn put in sil pan in tinkin. Bikɔs, d’ye si, renbo nɔ kin go na di klia briz; dɛn jɔs de rayt di vapour. Ɛn so, tru ɔl di tik tik mist dɛm fɔ di dim dawt dɛm na mi maynd, divayn intuitions naw ɛn den de shot, enkindling mi fɔg wit wan ɛvin rayt. Ɛn fɔ dis a tɛl Gɔd tɛnki; bikɔs ɔlman gɛt dawt; bɔku pipul dɛn kin dinay; bɔt dawt ɔ dinay, smɔl wan wit dɛn, gɛt intuitions. Doubt fɔ ɔltin na di wɔl, ɛn intuitions fɔ sɔm tin dɛn na ɛvin; dis kɔmbayn nɔ de mek pɔsin biliv ɔ infidul, bɔt i de mek man we de si dɛn ɔl tu wit ikwal yay.
