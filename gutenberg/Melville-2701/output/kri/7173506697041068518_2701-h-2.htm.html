CHAPTER 4. Di Kɔntrapin.

We a wek nɛks mɔnin bɔt de layt, a si se dɛn trowe Kwik in an oba mi di we we gɛt lɔv ɛn lɔv we pas ɔl. Yu bin dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ tink se na mi na in wɛf. Di kɔntapin na bin pat-pat, ful-ɔp wit ɔda smɔl smɔl pati-kɔlɔ skwea ɛn trayangul dɛn; ɛn dis an we i gɛt bin tatu ɔlsay wit wan Krit labirint we nɔ de dɔn we gɛt wan figa, we nɔ tu pat pan am nɔ bin gɛt wan prɛsis shed—bikɔs a tink se i bin de kip in an na si we nɔ gɛt mɛtod insay san ɛn shed, in shit sliv dɛn bin rol ɔp ɔltɛm difrɛn tɛm dɛn—dis sem an in, a se, bin de luk fɔ ɔl di wɔl lɛk wan strip pan da sem patchwɔk quilt de. Fɔ tru, pat pan am bin de ledɔm pan am lɛk aw di an bin de we a fɔs wek, a nɔ bin ebul fɔ no am frɔm di quilt, dɛn bin so miks dɛn kɔlɔ togɛda; ɛn na bay di we aw a bin de fil we a gɛt wet ɛn we a bin de prɛs mi, na in a bin ebul fɔ no se Kwik de ɔg mi.

Di we aw a bin de fil bin strenj. Mek a tray fɔ ɛksplen dɛn. We a bin smɔl, a mɛmba gud gud wan wan tin we bin apin to mi we fiba mi; ilɛksɛf na rial tin ɔ na drim, a nɔ bin ɛva ebul fɔ setul ɔltogɛda. Di tin we bin apin na dis. A bin dɔn de kɔt sɔm kapa ɔ ɔda wan—a tink se i bin de tray fɔ kray ɔp di chimini, lɛk aw a bin dɔn si smɔl swip de du sɔm dez bifo; ɛn mi stɛp-mama we, sɔm kayn we ɔ ɔda we, bin de wip mi ɔltɛm, ɔ sɛn mi fɔ go slip ɔltɛm we a nɔ de it ivintɛm,—mi mama bin drɛg mi mi leg dɛn kɔmɔt na di chimini ɛn pak mi fɔ go slip, pan ɔl we na bin tu oklɔk nɔmɔ aftanun fɔ di 21st Jun, we na di de we lɔng pas ɔl di ɔda de dɛn insay di ia na wi ɛmisfya. A bin fil bad bad wan. Bɔt no ɛp nɔ bin de fɔ am, so a go ɔp stej a go na mi smɔl rum we de na di tɔd flɔ, pul mi klos sloslo as a ebul so dat a go kil tɛm, ɛn wit bita swɛt a go bitwin di sheet dɛn.

A bin ledɔm de wit pwɛl at de kɔlkul se siksti ɔl awa fɔ pas bifo a op fɔ gɛt layf bak. Sikstɛn awa na bed! di smɔl pat na mi bak bin de pen we a tink bɔt am. Ɛn i bin so layt bak; di san we de shayn insay di winda, ɛn wan big big ratling fɔ kɔch dɛn na di strit, ɛn di sawnd fɔ gay vɔys dɛn ɔlsay na di os. A bin de fil wɔs ɛn wɔs—las a grap, drɛs, ɛn go dɔŋ saful saful wit mi fut we gɛt stɔkin, luk fɔ mi stɛp-mama, ɛn wantɛm wantɛm a trowe misɛf na in fut, ɛn beg am as patikyula fayv fɔ gi mi gud slip fɔ di bad we aw a de biev; ɛnitin fɔ tru pas fɔ kɔndɛm mi fɔ lay abed dis kayn lɔng tɛm we nɔ go ebul fɔ bia. Bɔt na in na bin di bɛst ɛn kɔnshɛns pas ɔlman pan stɛp-mama dɛn, ɛn bak a bin gɛt fɔ go na mi rum. Fɔ sɔm awa, a bin de ledɔm de brayt wek, a bin de fil bad bad wan pas aw a dɔn ɛva fil frɔm da tɛm de, ivin frɔm di big big bad tin dɛn we bin apin afta dat. Las las a go dɔn fɔdɔm insay wan trɔbul drim we a dɔn slip; ɛn we a wek frɔm am smɔl smɔl—a bin de drim af-af—a opin mi yay, ɛn di rum we bin de bifo di san bin dɔn rap naw wit daknɛs na do. Wantɛm wantɛm a fil wan shɔk de rɔn ɔlsay na mi freym; natin nɔ bin fɔ de, ɛn natin nɔ bin fɔ yɛri; bɔt i bin tan lɛk se dɛn put wan an we pas mɔtalman na mi yon. Mi an bin de hang oba di kɔntapen, ɛn di fɔm ɔ phantom we nɔ gɛt nem, we a nɔ ebul fɔ imajin, we nɔ de tɔk, we di an gɛt, bin tan lɛk se i sidɔm nia mi bed-sayd. Fɔ wetin bin tan lɛk se dɛn dɔn gɛda bɔku bɔku ia, a bin ledɔm de, a bin dɔn frɔz wit di fred dɛn we rili bad, a nɔ bin de fred fɔ drɛg mi an; yet ɛva tink se if a ebul fɔ jɔs stɔp am wan inch, di bad bad spɛl go brok. A nɔ bin no aw dis kɔnshɛns las las glid kɔmɔt nia mi; bɔt we a wek na mɔnin, a bin de shek shek fɔ mɛmba ɔltin, ɛn fɔ sɔm dez ɛn wik ɛn mɔnt afta dat, a bin lɔs misɛf we a bin de tray fɔ ɛksplen di sikrit we bin de mek a kɔnfyus. Nɔ, te to dis sem awa, bɔku tɛm a kin pazl misɛf wit am.

Naw, tek di bad bad fred, ɛn mi sɛns fɔ fil di supɛnachɔral an na mi yon bin rili fiba, insay dɛn strenj, to di wan dɛn we a bin ɛkspiriɛns we a wek ɛn si Kwik in pegan an trowe rawnd mi. Bɔt as lɔng ɔl di tin dɛn we bin apin di nɛt we dɔn pas bin soba bak, wan bay wan, insay fiks rialiti, ɛn afta dat a bin jɔs ledɔm layf layf to di kɔmik prɔblɛm. Bikɔs pan ɔl we a bin de tray fɔ muv in an—unlɔk in ɔkɔ in klap—bɔt, we a bin de slip lɛk aw i bin de, i bin stil ɔg mi tayt tayt wan, lɛk se natin nɔ fɔ sheb wi tu pas day. Naw a bin de tray tranga wan fɔ wek am—“Kwik!”—bɔt di wangren ansa we i bin gi na fɔ snor. Dɔn a rol oba, mi nɛk fil lɛk se i de insay ɔs-kɔla; ɛn wantɛm wantɛm i fil smɔl skrach. We dɛn trowe di kɔntapin na sayd, na de di tomahawk bin de slip nia di savayj in sayd, lɛk se na bebi we gɛt hatchet fes. Wan fayn pikɛl, fɔ tru, a bin tink; abed ya na wan strenj os insay di brayt de, wit wan kanibal ɛn wan tomahawk! “Kwik!—in di nem fɔ gudnɛs, Kwik, wek!” Fɔ lɔng tɛm, bay dint ɔf bɔku wriggling, ɛn lawd ɛn inscessant exposulations pan di nɔ fayn we i ɔg in kɔmpin man insay da matrimonial sort of style, a bin ebul fɔ pul wan grunt; ɛn naw, i pul in an bak, shek insɛf ɔlsay lɛk Nyufaundlan dɔg jɔs frɔm di wata, ɛn sidɔm ɔp na bed, stif lɛk paik-staf, de luk mi, ɛn rɔb in yay lɛk se i nɔ mɛmba ɔltogɛda aw a kam fɔ de de, pan ɔl we i bin tan lɛk se wan dak kɔnshɛns fɔ no sɔntin bɔt mi bin de kam dɔŋ am smɔl smɔl. Dis tɛm ya, a bin ledɔm kwayɛt wan de luk am, a nɔ gɛt ɛni siriɔs dawt naw, ɛn a bin bɛn fɔ wach wan tin we rili want fɔ no mɔ. We, las las, i bin tan lɛk se in maynd mek i tɔch di abit we in kɔmpin we de na di bed gɛt, ɛn i bin tan lɛk se i gri wit di tru tin; i jomp kɔmɔt na grɔn, ɛn bay sɔm sayn ɛn sawnd dɛn we mek a ɔndastand se, if i gladi fɔ mi, i go drɛs fɔs ɛn lɛf mi fɔ drɛs afta dat, ɛn lɛf di wan ol apatmɛnt fɔ misɛf. Tink mi, Kwik, ɔnda di sikɔstɛms, dis na wan rili siviliz ɔvtɛr; bɔt, di trut na dat, dɛn savayj ya gɛt innate sense of delicacy, se wetin yu want; i wɔndaful aw dɛn rili gɛt pɔlitiks. A de pe dis patikyula kɔmplimɛnt to Kwik, bikɔs i bin trit mi wit bɔku siviliti ɛn kɔnsidareshɔn, we a bin gilti fɔ big rud; de luk am frɔm di bed, ɛn wach ɔl in tɔylɛt muvmɛnt; fɔ di tɛm we a want fɔ no mɔ de bɛtɛ pan mi brid. Bɔt pan ɔl dat, man lɛk Kwik we yu nɔ de si ɛvride, in ɛn in we dɛn bin rili fayn fɔ tink bɔt we nɔ kɔmɔn.

I bigin fɔ drɛs ɔp bay we i wɛr in biv hat, we rili lɔng, nia di bay, dɔn—i stil pul in trayz—i fɛn in but. Wetin ɔnda di ɛvin i du am fɔ, a nɔ ebul fɔ no, bɔt in nɛks muvmɛnt na fɔ krɔs insɛf—but in an, ɛn hat pan—ɔnda di bed; we, frɔm difrɛn kayn vaylɛnt gasping ɛn strɛs, a bin tink se i bin de wok tranga wan fɔ but insɛf; pan ɔl we nɔto ɛni lɔ we de sho se pɔsin fɔ du wetin rayt we a ɛva yɛri bɔt, ɛni man fɔ bi prayvet we i de wɛr in but. Bɔt Kwik, yu si, na bin krichɔ we bin de insay di transishɔn stej—nɔto katapila ɔ bɔtaflay. I bin jɔs sivilizayt fɔ sho ɔf in ɔtlandishnɛs di strenj we pɔsibul. In edyukeshɔn nɔ bin dɔn yet. I bin de ɔnda gradyuet. If i nɔ bin sivilizayt smɔl digri, i go mɔs bi se i nɔ bin fɔ dɔn trɔbul insɛf wit but atɔl; bɔt afta dat, if i nɔ bin stil bi savayj, i nɔ bin fɔ ɛva drim fɔ go ɔnda di bed fɔ put dɛn. Las las, i kɔmɔt wit in hat we dɔn rili dent ɛn krɔs dɔŋ oba in yay, ɛn bigin fɔ krak ɛn limp rawnd di rum, lɛk se, bikɔs i nɔ bin dɔn yus to but, in pe we wet wet, we gɛt wrinkled kaw-ayd wan dɛn—i go mɔs bi se dɛn nɔ mek am fɔ ɔda pipul dɛn bak—bin pinch am ɛn mek i sɔfa di fɔs tɛm we i go ɔf wan bita kol mɔnin.

We dɛn si, naw, se kɔtin nɔ bin de na di winda, ɛn di strit bin rili smɔl, di os we bin de bifo bin kɔmand fɔ si klia wan insay di rum, ɛn we dɛn bin de wach mɔ ɛn mɔ di fayn fayn figa we Kwik bin mek, we bin de rɔn rawnd wit smɔl ɔda tin pas in hat ɛn but; A beg am fayn fayn wan, fɔ aksɛleret in tɔylɛt smɔl, ɛn mɔ fɔ go insay in pantalɔn dɛn kwik kwik wan. I bin du wetin di lɔ se, dɔn i bigin fɔ was insɛf. Da tɛm de na mɔnin ɛni Kristian fɔ dɔn was in fes; bɔt Kwik, we a bin sɔprayz, bin satisfay wit fɔ stɔp in ablushɔn dɛn na in chɛst, in an dɛn, ɛn in an dɛn. Dɔn i wɛr in wɛstkot, ɛn tek wan had sop na di was-stand sɛnt tebul, dip am insay wata ɛn bigin fɔ lather in fes. A bin de wach fɔ si usay i kip in reza, we lo ɛn si, i tek di harpun na di bed kɔna, slip kɔmɔt na di lɔng wud stok, pul di ed, whet am smɔl na in but, ɛn strit go ɔp to di bit we de na di miro we de nia di wɔl, bigin fɔ skrap wit trɛnk, ɔ fɔ se i de harpuon in chɛst. Tink mi, Queequeg, dis na yuz Rogers in bes cutlery wit wan vengeance. Afta dat a bin de wɔnda di less pan dis ɔpreshɔn we a kam fɔ no us fayn stɛl dɛn kin mek di ed fɔ wan hapun, ɛn aw dɛn kin kip di lɔng stret ed dɛn shap pasmak ɔltɛm.

I nɔ tu te, dɛn bin ebul fɔ du di ɔda pat pan in tɔylɛt, ɛn i bin prawd fɔ mach kɔmɔt na di rum, rap am wit in big payɔt mɔnki jakit, ɛn spɔt in harpun lɛk mashɛl in baton.





CHAPTER 5. Brekfas.

A fala mi kwik kwik wan, ɛn we a kam dɔŋ insay di ba-rum, a aks di landlɔd we bin de grin fayn fayn wan. A nɔ bin valyu ɛni bad bad tin to am, pan ɔl we i bin dɔn de skaylark wit mi nɔto smɔl pan di mata bɔt mi bedfɛlɔ.

Bɔt, gud laf na pawaful gud tin, ɛn rather tu scarce na gud tin; di mɔ na di sɔri-at. So, if ɛni wan man, insay in yon prɔpa pɔsin, ebul fɔ pe tin fɔ gud jok to ɛnibɔdi, lɛ i nɔ bak, bɔt mek i gladi fɔ alaw insɛf fɔ spɛn ɛn spɛn am da we de. Ɛn di man we gɛt ɛnitin we yu kin laf bɔku bɔku wan bɔt am, mek shɔ se mɔ tin de insay da man de pas aw yu go tink bɔt.

Di ba-rum bin ful-ɔp naw wit di bɔda dɛn we bin dɔn de drɔp insay di nɛt bifo, ɛn we a nɔ bin dɔn luk gud gud wan yet. Klosap ɔl dɛn na bin wɛlman dɛn; chif patna, ɛn sɛkɔn patna, ɛn tɔd patna, ɛn si kapɛnta, si kupa, si blaksmit, ɛn hapun, ɛn ship kipa; wan brawn ɛn brawn kɔmni, wit bosky biad; wan sɛt we nɔ kɔt, we gɛt shaggy, ɔl dɛn wɛr mɔnki jakit fɔ mɔnin gɔn.

Yu kin no klia wan aw lɔng ɛni wan pan dɛn dɔn de na di si. Dis yɔŋ man in wɛlbɔdi chɛk tan lɛk pia we dɛn dɔn tost wit di san in kɔlɔ, ɛn i go tan lɛk se i go smɛl lɛk di mɔski; i nɔ go ebul fɔ dɔn land fɔ tri dez frɔm in Indian voyage. Dat man we de nia am, luk layt fɔ sɔm shed dɛn; yu kin se wan tohch satin wud de insay am. Insay di kɔmpleks fɔ wan tɔd stil de ling wan tropik tawn, bɔt smɔl blich wital; i nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se i dɔn de na di si fɔ lɔng lɔng wiks. Bɔt udat go sho in chɛst lɛk Kwik? we, we dɛn bar wit difrɛn tint, bin tan lɛk di Andes in wɛst slɔp, fɔ sho bifo insay wan arenjmɛnt, kɔntrast klaymat, zon bay zon.

“Grub, ho!” naw di pɔsin we gɛt di land kray, i fling opin wan domɔt, ɛn insay wi go fɔ it brɛf.

Dɛn se man dɛn we dɔn si di wɔl, dat kin mek dɛn rili fil fri fɔ biev, dɛn kin rili lɛk dɛnsɛf fɔ de wit dɛn. Bɔt nɔto ɔltɛm: Ledyard, di bigman na Nyu Ingland, ɛn Mungo Park, di Skɔtish wan; pan ɔl di man dɛn, na dɛn bin gɛt di smɔl ashurant na di parlor. Bɔt sɔntɛm di jɔs krɔs Sayberia insay wan slɛj we dɔg dɛn drɔ lɛk aw Ledyard bin de du, ɔ di tek wan lɔng solitary waka pan ɛmti bɛlɛ, na di nigro at na Afrika, we na bin di sɔm pan di po Mungo in pefɔmɛns dɛn—dis kayn travul, a se, nɔ go bi di bɛst we fɔ gɛt ay soshal polish. Bɔt stil, bɔku tɛm, da kayn tin de fɔ de ɛnisay.

Dɛn riflekshɔn ya jɔs ya na di sikɔstɛms we afta wi ɔl sidɔm na di tebul, ɛn a bin de rɛdi fɔ yɛri sɔm gud stori dɛn bɔt wɛl; a nɔ bin sɔprayz fɔ no se i nɔ bin izi fɔ lɛ ɔlman nɔ tɔk natin. Ɛn nɔto dat nɔmɔ, bɔt dɛn bin luk lɛk se dɛn shem. Yɛs, na wan sɛt fɔ si-dɔg dɛn bin de, bɔku pan dɛn we nɔ bin shem smɔl, bin dɔn go insay big big wɛl dɛn na di ay si—we na ɔl strenja dɛn to dɛn—ɛn dɛn bin de fɛt wit dɛn day we dɛn nɔ wink; ɛn yet, na ya dɛn sidɔm na wan soshal brɛkfas tebul—ɔl dɛn kɔl dɛn di sem, dɛn ɔl gɛt fambul teist—de luk rawnd as ship shep to dɛnsɛf lɛk se dɛn nɔ ɛva kɔmɔt na di say we dɛn nɔ de si sɔm ship dɛn we de bitwin di Grin Mawnt dɛn. Wan tin we pɔsin kin si we pɔsin want fɔ no mɔ; dɛn bea dɛn ya we de shem, dɛn pipul ya we de shem fɔ fɛt wɛl!

Bɔt as fɔ Kwik—wetin mek, Kwik bin sidɔm de bitwin dɛn—na di ed fɔ di tebul, bak, i bin so chans; as i kol lɛk ayskul. Fɔ mek shɔ se a nɔ go ebul fɔ tɔk bɔku tin fɔ in brid. Di pɔsin we bin rili lɛk am nɔ bin fɔ dɔn ebul fɔ jɔstify wit ɔl in at fɔ briŋ in harpun fɔ it brɛf wit am, ɛn yuz am de we nɔ gɛt sɛrimɔni; i rich oba di tebul wit am, to di denja we bin de kam nia bɔku ed dɛn, ɛn grap di bifstik dɛn to am. Bɔt dat na fɔ tru, i bin rili kɔl am, ɛn ɔlman no se insay bɔku pipul dɛn, fɔ du ɛnitin kol wan na fɔ du am jɛnɛral.

Wi nɔ go tɔk bɔt ɔl di tin dɛn we Queequeg gɛt ya; aw i bin de avɔyd kɔfi ɛn ɔt rol, ɛn put in atɛnshɔn we nɔ sheb to bifstik, we dɛn kin du rare. Inaf, se we brɛf dɔn dɔn i pul insɛf kɔmɔt lɛk di ɔda wan dɛn insay di pɔblik rum, layt in tomahawk-paip, ɛn sidɔm de kwayɛt wan de dayjɛst ɛn smok wit in hat we nɔ go separet pan, we a salli aut fɔ strol.





CHAPTER 6. Di Strit.

If a bin dɔn sɔprayz fɔs we a si wan pɔsin we nɔ lɛk Kwik we de skata bitwin di pɔlitiks sosayti na wan sivilizayt tɔŋ, i nɔ tu te, da sɔprayz de kɔmɔt we a tek mi fɔs de layt waka waka na di strit dɛn na Nyu Bɛdfɔd.

Insay di rod dɛn we de nia di dok dɛn, ɛni bɔku bɔku sipɔt go ɔfrɛd bɔku tɛm fɔ si di queerest lukin nondescripts frɔm fɔrin pat dɛn. Ivin na Brodway ɛn Chɛstnɛt strit, sɔntɛnde, pipul dɛn we de wok na di Mɛditarenian marin go jost di uman dɛn we de fred. Rijɛn Strit nɔto ples we Laskar ɛn Malay pipul dɛn nɔ no; ɛn na Bombay, na di Apollo Green, layv Yankees dɔn mek di pipul dɛn we de de fred bɔku tɛm. Bɔt Nyu Bɛdfɔd win ɔl Wata Strit ɛn Wapin. Insay dɛn ples dɛn ya we wi bin dɔn tɔk bɔt las las, na di wan dɛn nɔmɔ we de wok na di bot yu de si; bɔt na Nyu Bɛdfɔd, rial pipul dɛn we de it pipul dɛn kin tinap de tɔk na kɔna dɛn na strit; savayj dɛn we dɛn kin du wantɛm wantɛm; bɔku pan dɛn stil de kɛr dɛn bon dɛn bɔdi we nɔ oli. I de mek wan strenja de luk am.

Bɔt, apat frɔm di Feegeeans, Tongatobooarrs, Erromanggoans, Pannangians, ɛn Brighggians, ɛn, apat frɔm di wayl spɛshal wan dɛn fɔ di wɛl-kraft we nɔ bin de lisin to riɛl bɔt di strit dɛn, yu go si ɔda say dɛn we yu go si stil mɔ kɔriɔs, fɔ tru, mɔ kɔmik. Evri wik, bɔku bɔku grɔn Vɛmɔnt ɛn Nyu Amsha man dɛn kin kam na dis tɔŋ, dɛn ɔl tɔsti fɔ gɛt bɛnifit ɛn glori na di fisha wok. Bɔku pan dɛn na yɔŋ pipul dɛn, dɛn gɛt strɔng freym dɛn; kɔmpin dɛn we dɔn kɔt fɔrɛst, ɛn naw dɛn de tray fɔ drɔp di aks ɛn tek di wɛl-lans. Bɔku pan dɛn grɔn lɛk di Grin Mawnt dɛn usay dɛn kɔmɔt. CHAPTER 7. Di Chapel.

Insay dis sem Nyu Bɛdfɔd, wan Whaleman’s Chapel tinap, ɛn na smɔl pipul dɛn nɔmɔ gɛt di fishaman dɛn we gɛt maynd, we jɔs de go na di Indian Oshɔn ɔ Pasifik, we nɔ kin go na di ples Sɔnde. A shɔ se a nɔ bin du dat.

We a kam bak frɔm mi fɔs mɔnin waka, a bigin fɔ du dis spɛshal wok bak. Di skay bin dɔn chenj frɔm klia, san kol, to drayvin slit ɛn mist. We a rap misɛf wit mi shaggy jakit we dɛn mek wit di klos we dɛn kɔl bearskin, a fɛt mi we agens di stɛp-an big big briz. We a go insay, a fɛn wan smɔl kɔngrigeshɔn we skata we gɛt pipul dɛn we de wok na di bot, ɛn di wan dɛn we de wok na di bot dɛn wɛf ɛn uman dɛn we dɛn man dɔn day. Wan muf silɛns bin de rul, ɛn sɔntɛnde di big big briz bin de ala pan am nɔmɔ i bin de brok. I bin tan lɛk se ɛni wan pan di wan dɛn we de wɔship kwayɛt wan bin sidɔm apat frɔm di ɔda wan bay wilful, lɛk se ɛni wan pan di wan dɛn we nɔ de tɔk natin na inshɔl ɛn nɔbɔdi nɔ go ebul fɔ tɔk to am. Di chaplain nɔ bin dɔn kam yet; ɛn na de dɛn ayland dɛn ya we nɔ gɛt wan minin we gɛt man ɛn uman dɛn bin sidɔm tranga wan de luk sɔm mabul tablɛt dɛn, we gɛt blak bɔda dɛn, we dɛn mek wit ston na di wɔl na di tu say dɛn na di pulpit. SEKRI TO DI MƐMƆRI ƆF Di layt KAPTƐN IZƐKƐL HARDY, We insay di bɔw dɛn na in bot, wan Spɛm Wɛl bin kil am na di kɔst na Jepan, Ɔgɔst 3 d , 1833. DIS TƐBLƐT Na IN WIDƆ bin bil am fɔ mek i Mɛmori.

We a shek di slit frɔm mi hat ɛn jakit we gɛt ays glaz, a sidɔm nia di domɔt, ɛn we a tɔn na sayd, a sɔprayz fɔ si Kwik nia mi. Di solemnity of di scene bin afɛkt, wan wɔndaful luk bin de na in fes we nɔ biliv se i want fɔ no mɔ. Dis savayj na bin di wangren pɔsin we bin de de we i bin tan lɛk se i notis mi ɛntrɛ; bikɔs na in nɔmɔ nɔ bin ebul fɔ rid, ɛn, bikɔs ɔf dat, i nɔ bin de rid dɛn frigid tin dɛn de we dɛn rayt na di wɔl. If ɛni wan pan di fambul dɛn we di siman dɛn we dɛn nem bin de de, bin de na di kɔngrigeshɔn naw, a nɔ bin no; bɔt bɔku pan di aksidɛnt dɛn we dɛn nɔ rayt na di fisha, ɛn so klia wan se sɔm uman dɛn we bin de de bin wɛr di fes if nɔto di trap dɛn fɔ sɔm sɔri-at we nɔ de stɔp, dat a fil shɔ se na ya bifo mi, dɛn bin gɛda dɛn wan dɛn de, we insay dɛn at we nɔ wɛl we dɛn si dɛn blak tablɛt dɛn de wit sɔri-at, dat bin mek di ol wund dɛn blɔd bak.

O! una we dɛn dɔn bɛr una day we dɛn dɔn bɛr ɔnda di grɔn gras; we tinap midul flawa dɛn kin se—na ya, na ya mi pɔsin we a lɛk de; una nɔ no di pwɛl pwɛl we de bɔn na bɔdi lɛk dɛn wan ya. Na bita blank dɛn de insay dɛn mabul dɛn we gɛt blak bɔda we nɔ de kɔba ɛni ashis! Dis pwɛl at de pan dɛn tin dɛn de we nɔ de muv! Ɔl dɛn tin ya nɔ de we nɔ gɛt minin.

Bɔt Fet, lɛk jakal, de it midul di grev dɛn, ɛn ivin frɔm dɛn day dawt ya i kin gɛda in op we impɔtant pas ɔl.

I nɔ nid fɔ tɛl, wit us filin, di ivintɛm fɔ wan Nantucket voyage, a bin luk dɛn mabul tablɛt dɛn de, ɛn bay di dak layt we bin de dak, de we bin de mek a fil bad, a bin rid di fate fɔ di wɛlman dɛn we bin dɔn go bifo mi. Yɛs, Ismayl, di sem tin kin apin to yu. Bɔt sɔm we dɛn de, a bigin fɔ gladi bak. I tan lɛk se fayn fayn tin dɛn fɔ mek a go insay di bot, fayn chans fɔ gɛt prɔmoshɔn—aye, stov bot go mek a bi pɔsin we nɔ de day bay brevet. Yɛs, day de na dis biznɛs fɔ kech wɛl—na wan chaotic bundling of man in Eternity we nɔ de tɔk kwik kwik wan. Bɔt wetin afta dat? Methinks wi don hugely mistek dis mata of Laif en Day. CHAPTER 8. Di Pɔlpit.

A nɔ bin dɔn sidɔm fɔ lɔng tɛm bifo wan man we gɛt wan patikyula venerable robustness kam insay; wantɛm wantɛm we di domɔt we di big big briz bin blo bin flay bak we dɛn admit am, we ɔl di kɔngrigeshɔn bin de luk am kwik kwik wan, ɛn dis bin sho se na dis fayn ol man na di chaplain. Yɛs, na di fambul Fada Mapple, we di wɛlman dɛn bin de kɔl so, ɛn i bin rili lɛk am. I bin dɔn de wok na bot ɛn i bin de ple hapun we i bin yɔŋ, bɔt fɔ bɔku ia we dɔn pas, i bin dɔn gi in layf fɔ di prichin wok. Di tɛm we a de rayt bɔt naw, Fada Mapple bin de insay di kol sizin we i bin gɛt wan wɛlbɔdi ol ej; da kayn ol ej de we tan lɛk se i de miks to sɛkɔn yɔŋ pɔsin we gɛt flawa, bikɔs pan ɔl di fis dɛn we in rɔtin dɛn bin gɛt, sɔm smɔl smɔl layt dɛn bin de shayn bɔt wan nyu blɔk we bin de divɛlɔp—di spring verdure bin de luk ivin ɔnda di sno we bin de na Fɛbwari. Nɔbɔdi nɔ bin dɔn yɛri in istri bifo, nɔ bin ebul fɔ si Fada Mapple fɔ di fɔs tɛm witout di utmost intres, bikɔs sɔm engrafted klerikal peculiarities bin de bɔt am, imputable to dat adventurous maritime life we ​​i bin liv. We i go insay a si se i nɔ bin de kɛr ambɔla, ɛn fɔ tru, i nɔ bin kam insay in kat, bikɔs in tapul hat bin de rɔn dɔŋ wit slit we de mɛlt, ɛn i bin tan lɛk se in big payɔt klos jakit dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ drɛg am go na grɔn wit di wet we di wata we i dɔn absɔb gɛt. Bɔt, dɛn bin de pul hat ɛn kot ɛn ɔvashɔ wan bay wan, ɛn hang am na smɔl ples na wan kɔna we de nia de; we i wɛr fayn fayn sus, i kwayɛt wan kam nia di pulpit.

Lɛk bɔku pan di ol fashin pulpit dɛn, i bin rili ay, ɛn bikɔs wan rɛgyula stɛp fɔ go ɔp da kayn ayt de go, bay in lɔng angul wit di flɔ, siriɔs wan kɔntrakt di ɔlrɛdi smɔl eria na di chapel, i bin tan lɛk se di akitɔk bin dɔn akt pan di hint we Fada Mapple bin gi am, ɛn dɔn di pulpit we nɔ gɛt stej, ɛn i bin tek wan stɛp we de pepɛndikul sayd, lɛk di wan dɛn we dɛn kin yuz fɔ mawnt ship frɔm a bot na si. Di wɛf fɔ wan kapten we de kech wɛl bin dɔn gi di chapel wan fayn fayn pe fɔ rɛd wɔst man-rop fɔ dis lada, we, bikɔs insɛf gɛt fayn ed, ɛn stɛyn wit mahogani kɔlɔ, di wan ol kɔntrivans, we yu tink bɔt us kayn chapel i bi, i nɔ bin tan lɛk se i nɔ gɛt bad teys. We Fada Mapple stɔp fɔ smɔl tɛm na di fut fɔ di lada, ɛn wit in tu an dɛn we ol di ɔnamɛnt knob dɛn na di man-rop dɛn, i luk ɔp, dɔn wit wan rili sɛns we tan lɛk selɔ bɔt stil gɛt rɛspɛkt, in an oba an, i go ɔp di stɛp dɛn lɛk se i de go ɔp di men-tɔp na in bot.

Di pat dɛn we de pepɛndikul na dis sayd lada, lɛk aw i kin bi wit di wan dɛn we de swing, na bin rop we dɛn kɔba wit klos, na di rawnd dɛn nɔmɔ na wud, so na ɛni stɛp, wan jɔyn bin de. We a si di pulpit fɔ di fɔstɛm, i nɔ bin dɔn rɔnawe pan mi se ilɛksɛf i izi fɔ ship, i bin tan lɛk se dɛn jɔyn dɛn ya we de naw nɔ nid fɔ de. Bikɔs a nɔ bin rɛdi fɔ si Fada Mapple afta i dɔn gɛt di ayt, i tɔn rawnd smɔl smɔl, ɛn butu oba di pulpit, bay wilful fɔ drɛg di lada ɔp stɛp bay stɛp, te di wan ol tin dɔn put insay, ɛn lɛf am we nɔbɔdi nɔ go ebul fɔ win am na in smɔl Kwibɛk.

A bin tink bɔt sɔm tɛm we a nɔ bin ɔndastand gud gud wan di rizin fɔ dis. Fada Mapple bin ɛnjɔy so bɔku nem fɔ sinsi ɛn oli, dat a nɔ bin ebul fɔ sɔprayz se i de kɔt badnem bay ɛni jɔs trik na di stej. Nɔ, a bin tink se sɔm soba rizin fɔ de fɔ dis tin; pan tap dat, i fɔ sho sɔntin we pɔsin nɔ de si. So, yu tink se bay da akt de we i de du fɔ mek i nɔ de nia ɔda pipul dɛn wit in bɔdi, i de sho se i dɔn pul insɛf kɔmɔt pan Gɔd biznɛs fɔ da tɛm de, frɔm ɔl di tayt ɛn kɔnekshɔn dɛn we de na di wɔl we de na do? Yɛs, bikɔs dɛn dɔn ful-ɔp am bak wit di mit ɛn wayn we de insay di wɔd, to di fetful man fɔ Gɔd, dis pulpit, a si, na wan strɔng ples we gɛt insɛf—wan ay ay ples we dɛn kɔl Ehrenbreitstein, we gɛt wata we de ɔltɛm insay di wɔl dɛn.

Bɔt nɔto di sayd lada nɔmɔ na bin di strenj tin we bin de na di ples, we dɛn bin lɛnt frɔm di chaplain in fɔs si-faring dɛn. Bitwin di mabul sɛnotaf dɛn we de na di tu an dɛn na di pulpit, dɛn bin mek di wɔl we mek in bak fayn wit wan big peint we de sho wan gallant ship we de bit agens wan bad bad briz we bin de blo nia wan lee kɔst we gɛt blak rɔk dɛn ɛn sno we de brok brok. Bɔt ay ɔp di skɔd we de flay ɛn di klawd dɛn we de rɔl dak, wan smɔl ayland we gɛt san layt bin de flɔt, ɛn wan enjɛl in fes bin de kɔmɔt; ɛn dis brayt fes bin shed wan difrɛn say we de shayn pan di ship in dɛk we dɛn bin dɔn tos, sɔntin lɛk da silva plet we dɛn dɔn put naw insay di Viktɔri in plank usay Nɛlson bin fɔdɔm. I bin tan lɛk se di enjɛl se: “A, nobul ship, bit, bit, yu nobul ship, ɛn bia wan strɔng ed, bikɔs di san de brok, di klawd de rɔl go—serenest azure de nia.”

Gladi at to am—a fa, fa, de fɔ am—a fa, de fa, i de fa, i de fa, i de fa, i de fa, i de fa, i de fa, i de fa, i de fa. fa go ɔp, ɛn gladi at insay—we de ɛva tinap fɔ in yon gɔd dɛn we nɔ ebul fɔ stɔp, Gladi na to di wan we in strɔng an dɛn stil de sɔpɔt am, we di ship we dis wɔl we de mek pipul dɛn de du bad, dɔn go dɔŋ ɔnda am Gladi na to am, we nɔ de gi ɛni kwata na di trut, ɛn kil, bɔn, ɛn pwɛl ɔl sin pan ɔl we i de pul am kɔmɔt ɔnda di klos fɔ Sɛnatɔ ɛn Jɔj dɛn Gladi,—tɔp-gallant gladi at na to am, we nɔ gri wit ɛni lɔ ɔ masta, ɛn na jɔs pɔsin we lɛk in kɔntri to ɛvin Gladi na to am, we ɔl di wef dɛn we di si we de mek pipul dɛn de kray nɔ go ɛva shek frɔm dis shɔt Kil we de kam le am dɔŋ, kin se wit in las briz—O Papa!—we a sabi mɔ wit Yu stik—a de day ɔ a nɔ de day, na ya a dɔn tray fɔ bi Yu yon, pas fɔ bi dis wɔl in yon, ɔ mi yon yet: A de lɛf to yu sote go;

I nɔ tɔk igen, bɔt i de wev wan blɛsin smɔl smɔl, i kɔba in fes wit in an dɛn, ɛn so i kɔntinyu fɔ nil dɔŋ, te ɔl di pipul dɛn dɔn go, ɛn i lɛf in wan na di ples.
