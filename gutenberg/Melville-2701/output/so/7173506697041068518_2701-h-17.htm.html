CUTUBKA 86. Dabada.

Abwaano kale ayaa ammaanta isha jilicdasan ee habar-dugaag, iyo xuubka quruxda badan ee shimbirka ee aan waligood soo dagin; samada yar, waxaan u dabaal dabo.

Iyadoo la tixgalinayo dabada shahwada ee ugu weyn inay ka bilaabato barta jirridda halkaasoo ay ku dhufanayso ilaa gunta ninka, waxay ka kooban tahay dusha sare oo keliya, dhul ah ugu yaraan konton cagood oo laba jibbaaran. Jidhka wareegsan ee xididkiisu waxa uu u fidiyaa laba ballaadhan, calaacasha adag, fidsan, oo si tartiib ah u ruxaya ilaa in ka yar dhumucdiisuna tahay. Marka la joogo jilbaha ama halka ay isku xiraan, faleebadani wax yar ayay is dulsaaran yihiin, ka dibna dhinacba midba midka kale ayuu uga noqonayaa sida baalal, isaga oo ka tagaya boos ballaadhan. Nool ma jiro xarriiqyada quruxda si ka qurux badan loo qeexay marka loo eego xudduudaha bilaha ah ee faleebooyinkan. Markay ugu badnaan balaadhinta nibiriga koray, dabada ayaa si aad ah uga badan doonta labaatan fiit.

Xubinta oo dhami waxay u muuqataa sariir cufan oo seedaha alxanka ah; laakiin goo, oo waxaad ogaanaysaa in saddex qaybood oo kala duwan ay ka kooban yihiin:- sare, dhexe iyo hoose. Fiilooyinka lakabyada sare iyo kuwa hoose, waa kuwo dheer oo siman; kuwa dhexe, oo aad u gaaban, oo si isdaba joog ah u socda inta u dhaxaysa lakabyada dibadda. Qaab-dhismeedkan saddex-midnimada ah, sida wax kasta oo kale, wuxuu siinayaa awoodda dabada. Ardaygii derbiyadii hore ee Roomaanka, lakabka dhexe wuxuu siin doonaa isbarbardhig xiiso leh oo la mid ah koorsada khafiifka ah ee foornada had iyo jeer waxay ku beddelanayaan dhagaxa ku yaal waxyaalaha cajiibka ah ee qadiimiga ah, oo shaki la'aan wax badan ku kordhinaya xoogga weyn ee dhismaha.

Laakin sida haddii awooddan baaxadda leh ee degaanka ee dabada jilicsani ay ku filnaan weyday, in badan oo leviathan ah ayaa lagu xidhxidhay gariir iyo galmo muruqa ah iyo fiilo, kuwaas oo dhexda labada dhinac uga sii gudba oo hoos ugu sii qulqulaya, si dareen la'aan ah. iyaga ku dhex milma, oo si weyn uga qaybqaata awooddooda; si dabada ku jirta xoogga isku-darka ah ee aan cabbirka lahayn ee nibiriga oo dhan uu u muuqdo mid ku ururay meel. Ma laga yaabaa in dabar-goynta ay ku dhacdo maadada, tan ayaa ahayd waxa la sameeyo.

Mana aha tan-xooggeeda yaabka leh, haba yaraatee waxay u janjeertaa inay curyaamiso dabacsanaanta quruxda badan ee dhaqdhaqaaqeeda; halkaas oo dhallaannimada fududaantu ay ka soo baxdo iyada oo loo marayo Titanism of power. Taa beddelkeeda, dhaqdhaqaaqyadaasi waxay ka soo jeedaan quruxdooda ugu naxdinta badan. Xoogga dhabta ahi marnaba ma wiiqo quruxda ama wada noolaanshaha, laakiin inta badan way siisaa; iyo wax kasta oo qurux badan, xoog wax badan ayay ku leedahay sixirka. Ka saar seedaha xidhxidhan ee dhammaan u muuqda inay ka dillaaceen marmarka Hercules xardhan, oo soo jiidashadeedu way dhammaan doontaa. Sida Eckerman oo cibaado leh uu ka soo qaaday go'ii linenka ee meydka qaawan ee Goethe, wuxuu ka buuxsamay laabta weyn ee ninku, oo u ekaa sidii qaanso guul leh oo Roomaan ah. Marka Angelo uu xitaa Ilaaha Aabbaha ah ku sawiro qaabka aadanaha, calaamadee waxa adag ee jira. Iyo wax kasta oo ay muujin karaan jacaylka rabbaaniga ah ee Wiilka, jilicsan, duuduuban, sawiro hermaphroditical Talyaani, taas oo uu fikradda ayaa si guul leh u qaabaysan; Sawirradan, oo aad u caydhoobay maadaama ay yihiin kuwo caqli-gal ah, wax awood ah kama muuqdaan, laakiin mid xun oo keliya, mid dheddig ah oo is-dhiibin iyo dulqaad leh, kaas oo gacmihiisa oo dhan loo oggolaaday, ayaa ka dhigaya dabeecadaha gaarka ah ee waxtarka leh ee waxbaristiisa.

Kaasi waa dabacsanaanta dahsoon ee xubinta aan kula dhaqmo, taas oo ah in lagu dhaqmo isboortiga, ama si dhab ah, ama xanaaq, si kasta oo ay tahay niyadda ay ku jirto, dabacsanaanteeda ayaa had iyo jeer lagu calaamadiyaa nimco aad u badan. Halkaasna cudud cirfiid ah kama gudbi karto.

Shan mooshin oo waaweyn ayaa u gaar ah. Marka hore, marka loo isticmaalo sida fin horumarka; Midda labaad, marka dagaalka loo isticmaalo sida maanso; Saddex, xagga xaaqidda; Afar, xagga lobtailing; Shanaad, oo ku jira qulqulka ugu sarreeya.

Marka hore: Isagoo jiifa booskeeda, dabada Leviathan waxay u dhaqantaa si ka duwan dabada xayawaannada kale ee badda. Marna ma qalloociso. Nin ama kalluun, qallafsanaantu waa calaamad muujinaysa liidasho. Xagga nibiriga, dabadiisu waa habka kaliya ee kicinta. Duub-xikmad leh ayaa hore u soo duuduubay jidhka hoostiisa, ka dibna si degdeg ah gadaal ugu soo baxay, waa tan tan siinaysa qoob-ka-cayaaraha keli ah, u boodaya dhaq-dhaqaaqa daanyeerka marka uu si cadho leh u dabaalanayo. Dhankiisa dhinac wuxuu kaliya u adeegaa inuu isteeriyo.

Midda labaad: Waa wax yar oo muhiim ah, in halka shahwada nibirigu ay madaxa iyo daanka kula dagaalanto shahwo kale, si kastaba ha ahaatee, colaadaha uu la leeyahay ninka, wuxuu si weyn oo quursi leh u isticmaalo dabada. Intuu ku dhuftey doon, ayuu si degdeg ah uga leexiyey qoolkiisa, oo jugtiina waxaa ku dhacay dib u dhac. Haddii lagu sameeyo hawada aan xannibnayn, gaar ahaan haddii ay ku soo dhaadhacdo calaamadeeda, istaroogga markaa waa mid aan la adkeyn karin. Ma jiro feeraha nin ama doon u adkeysan kara. Badbaadadaada keliyaa waxay ku jirtaa inaad ka cararto. Laakin haddii ay dhinac u timaaddo biyaha ka soo horjeeda, markaas qayb ahaan iyada oo ay ugu wacan tahay guuxa iftiinka doonta nibiriyada, iyo dabacsanaanta alaabteeda, feeraha dillaacsan ama looxa la jeexjeexay ama laba, nooc tol ah oo dhinaca ah, ayaa guud ahaan ah. natiijada ugu daran. Dharbaaxooyinkan hoostooda ah ayaa inta badan laga helaa kalluumeysiga, taas oo lagu xisaabiyo inay yihiin ciyaar carruureed oo keliya. Mid baa marada ka siibay, daloolkiina waa la joojiyey.

Saddexaad: Ma muujin karo, laakiin waxay iila muuqataa, in nibiriga dareenka taabashada uu ku urursan yahay dabada; Waayo, dhinacan waxaa ku jira cunto macaan oo la siman dhadhanka jirridda maroodiga. Nacnacani waxa uu si gaar ah u caddeeyaa ficilka xaaqidda, marka inta badan dabacsanaanta nibiriga oo leh tartiib tartiib tartiib ah uu u dhaqaaqo dhaqdhaqaaqiisa baaxadda leh ee gees ilaa gees korka badda; Haddiise uu dareemo wax aan badmaax ahayn mooyaane, hoog waxaa u sugnaaday badmaaxaas, shabagga iyo dhammaanba. Waa maxay damqasho ku jirta taabashadaas hordhaca ah! Hadday dabadani leedahay awood hor leh, waa inaan isla markiiba iga fikiraa maroodiga Darmonodes ee aad ugu badan suuqa ubaxa, salaanta hoosena waxay u soo bandhigtay sanka gay hablaha, ka dibna salaaxday aagagooda. In ka badan hal xisaab, ayaa laga xumaado waa in nibirigu uusan haysanin dabeecaddan prehensile ee dabadiisa; waayo, waxaan maqlay maroodi kale, oo markii uu dagaalka ku dhaawacmay, uu jirkiisa u qaloociyay, oo uu la soo baxay dabada.

Midda afraad: isagoon ku baraarugsanayn nibiriyada dushiisa oo ku jira ammaankii loo hamuun qabay ee badhtamaha badaha keligiis ah, waxa aad arkaysaa isaga oo aan ka leexan karaamadiisa baaxadda leh, oo bisad u eeg, oo uu badweynta ku dul ciyaarayo sidii in ay tahay xabo. Laakin wali waxaad arkaysaa awoodiisa ciyaartiisa. calaacasha balaadhsan ee dabadiisa ayaa hawada sare loogu haasaawaha; ka dibna ku garaaca dusha sare, gariirkii onkodku wuxuu ku dhawaaqayaa mayl. Waxaad u malaynaysaa in qori weyn laga soo saaray; oo haddii aad dareento ubaxa iftiinka ee uumiga ka imanaya cidhifkiisa kale, waxaad u malaynaysaa inay taasi ahayd qiiqa godka taabashada.

Shanaad: Sida qaabka caadiga ah ee sabaynta leviathan, flukes waxay si aad ah uga hooseeyaan heerka dhabarkiisa, waxay si buuxda uga maqan yihiin indhaha hoostiisa; Laakin marka uu qarka u saaran yahay inuu gudaha u galo moolka, dhammaan wixigiisa oo leh ugu yaraan sodon cagood oo jirkiisa ah ayaa hawada lagu tuurayaa si toos ah, markaasay ku sii jiri karaan gariir ilaa ay hoos u tooganayaan. Marka laga reebo jebinta sare -meel kale oo lagu tilmaami karo - heerkan ugu sarreeya ee hargabka nibiriga ayaa laga yaabaa inay tahay aragtida ugu weyn ee lagu arko dhammaan dabeecadda firfircoon. Xagga hoose ee hoose, dabada weyni waxay u muuqataa mid si xun u dafi karta samada ugu sarreysa. Haddaba riyooyin, miyaan ku arkay Shaydaanka haybadda leh oo ka soo saaraya ciddigiisa weyn ee caddibaadda leh ee ka ololaysa Baltic of Jahannamo. Laakin marka aad u fiirsato muuqaalada noocaan oo kale ah, waa wax walba waxa aad ku jirto; Haddii Dantean-ka, shaydaanku kugu dhici doono; Hadday tii Isaiah, malaa'igaha sare. Aniga oo taagan dabada madaxii markabkayga xilli ay qorraxdu soo baxday cirku iyo badda cas, ayaa waxa aan bari bari ka arkay xayn badan oo nibiriga ah, kuwaas oo dhamaantood u jihaysan dhanka cadceedda, cabbaarna gariiraya bandhig faneed ay la socdaan fiilo heersare ah. Sida ay aniga ila ahayd waagaas, qaab-dhismeedkan weyn ee ilaahyada waligeed laguma arag, xitaa Faaris, oo ah guriga ay deggan yihiin kuwa dabka caabuda. Sida uu Ptolemy Philopater uga markhaati furay maroodiga Afrika, waxaan markaas ka markhaati furay nibiriga, anigoo ku dhawaaqay inuu yahay kan ugu cibaadada badan dhammaan noolaha. Waayo, sida uu sheegay boqor Juba, maroodiyaashii millateriga ahaa ee qadiimiga ahaa ayaa inta badan subaxdii ku ammaani jiray jirkooda oo kor loo qaaday aamusnaanta ugu qoto dheer.

Isbarbardhigga fursada ee cutubkan, ee u dhexeeya nibiriga iyo maroodiga, ilaa iyo inta ay khusayso qaybo ka mid ah dabada mid iyo jiridda kale, waa in aanay u janjeerin in ay labadaas xubnood ee iska soo horjeeda ku dhejiyaan sinnaanta, in ka yar makhluuqa ay ka tirsan yihiin. Waayo, sida maroodiga ugu xoogga badan Lewiiyaataan uu uga itaal roon yahay, sidaas oo kalena marka loo eego dabada Lewiiyaataan, jirriddiisu waa sida jiridda ubaxa. Dhaawacii ugu darnaa ee ka soo gaaray jirridda maroodiga ayaa ahaa sida tuubada cayaarta ee taageeraha, marka la barbar dhigo burburka iyo shilka aan la qiyaasi karin ee ku yimid hargabka nibiriyada shahwada, kuwaas oo marar badan midba midka kale uu ku tuuray doomo dhan oo ay la socdaan dhammaan seeradihii iyo shaqaalihii saarnaa hawada, si aad u badan sida juggler Hindi ah oo tuuraya kubbadaha.

* In kasta oo dhammaan isbarbardhigga habka tirada guud ee u dhexeeya nibiriga iyo maroodiga uu yahay mid been abuur ah, marka la eego in si gaar ah uu maroodigu u taagan yahay si la mid ah nibiriga uu eeygu ku sameeyo maroodiga; si kastaba ha ahaatee, ma jiraan meelo la rabo oo la mid ah kuwa xiisaha leh; Kuwaas waxaa ka mid ah tufaax. Sida la wada ogsoon yahay maroodigu waxa uu inta badan biyo ama boodhka jirkiisa ka soo dhaansan doonaa, ka dibna kor u qaadaya, oo uu la soo baxay tog.

Mar kasta oo aan tixgeliyo dabadan xoogga badan, ayaa aan aad uga xumaadaa awood la'aantayda in aan muujiyo. Mararka qaarkood waxa ku jira tilmaamo, kuwaas oo, in kastoo ay si wanaagsan u nici doonaan gacanta bini'aadamka, aan si buuxda loo fasiri karin. Xoolo ballaadhan, oo aad u cajiib ah, marmar, waa dhaqdhaqaaqyadan qarsoodiga ah, ee aan maqlay ugaarsatada kuwaas oo ku dhawaaqay inay la mid yihiin calaamadaha Free-Mason; in nibiriga, runtii, hababkan si caqli-gal ah ula sheekaystay adduunka. Sidoo kale ma jiraan dhaqdhaqaaqyo kale oo nibiriga ku jira jirkiisa guud, oo ay ka buuxaan qariib, oo aan lala xisaabtami karin weeraryahankiisa ugu khibradda badan. Haddaba isaga kala saar sidaan anigu maqaarka u guntano. Ma garanayo isaga, weligayna ma doonayo. Laakiin haddaanan garanayn xataa dabada nibirigan, sidee baan madaxiisa u garanayaa? Haddaba bal sidee buu wejigiisa u garanayaa, isagoo aan weji lahayn? Waxaad arki doontaa xubnahayga dhabarka iyo dabadayda, ayuu u eg yahay inuu leeyahay, laakiinse wejigayga lama arki doono. Laakiinse anigu dhammaan kama aan caddayn karo xubnihiisa dambe; oo u sheeg waxa uu ka doono wajigiisa, waxaan leeyahay mar kale waji ma leh.





CUTUBKA 87. Armada Weyn.

Jasiiradda dheer iyo cidhiidhiga ah ee Malacca, oo ku fidsan koonfur-bari dhanka dhulka Birmah, ayaa ka dhigaysa barta ugu konfureed Aasiya oo dhan. Xariiq joogto ah oo ka imanaya jasiiradaas fidsan jasiiradaha dheer ee Sumatra, Java, Bally, iyo Timor; Kaas oo, qaar kale oo badan, ay sameysteen bar aad u ballaaran, ama dhufeys, dherer ahaan ku xiraya Aasiya iyo Australia, oo kala qaybisa badweynta Hindiya ee aan go'in ee dheer ee jasiiradaha bari ee dhumuc weyn leh. Dhufayskan waxa daloola dhawr dekedood oo sally ah si ay ugu raaxaystaan ​​maraakiibta iyo nibiriyada; kuwa muuqda oo ay ka mid yihiin cidhiidhiga Sunda iyo Malacca. Marka la eego cidhiidhiga Sunda, gaar ahaan, maraakiibta ku xidha Shiinaha ee galbeedka, ayaa ka soo baxaya badda Shiinaha.

Cidhiidhigaas cidhiidhiga ah ee Sunda waxay kala qaybiyaan Sumatra oo ka socda Java; oo dhex taagan dhufeyskaas ballaadhan ee jasiiradaha, oo ay ka go'an tahay dhiirranaanta cagaaran, ee loo yaqaanno badmaaxiinta sida Madaxa Java; Iyagu in yar kama ay ekaan doonaan iridda dhexe ee ka soo baxda boqortooyo ballaadhan oo deyr leh, oo waxay u ekaayeen inay ka fikiraan maalka aan la koobi karin oo ah dhir udgoon, iyo xariir, iyo jowharaadyo, iyo dahab, iyo foolmaroodi, oo ay ku taajirsan yihiin kun gasiiradood oo badda bari. Bixinta muhiimka ah ee dabiiciga ah, in khasnadaha noocaas ah, marka la samaynayo dhulka, waa in ugu yaraan muuqaal ahaan, si kastaba ha ahaatee aan waxtar lahayn, oo laga ilaaliyo adduunka galbeedka oo dhan. Xeebaha Straits of Sunda kuma jiraan qalcado maamula kuwaas oo ilaaliya albaabada laga soo galo Mediterranean-ka, Baltic-ka, iyo Propontis-ka. Si ka duwan deenishka, Orientals-kani ma dalbanayaan ixtiraamka qarsoon ee shiraacyada sare ee hoos loo dhigay ee ka imanaya socodka aan dhammaadka lahayn ee maraakiibta ka hor dabaysha, kuwaas oo qarniyo hore, habeen iyo maalinba, u dhexeeyay jasiiradaha Sumatra iyo Java, oo lagu raran yahay shixnadaha bariga ugu qaalisan. Laakiin in kasta oo ay si xor ah uga tanaasulaan xaflad sidan oo kale ah, sinaba kama ay tanaasulaan sheegashadooda abaalmarin adag.

Wakhtigii maanka laguma hadhin faallada budhcad-badeedda ee Malaysiya, oo ku dhex dhuuntay godadka hoose ee hadhsan iyo jasiiradaha Sumatra, ayaa ku soo dul baxay maraakiibta cidhiidhiga maraysa, iyaga oo si adag u dalbanaya abaal-marin marka ay warmahoodu joogaan. In kasta oo ciqaabtii soo noqnoqonaysay ee dhiigga ku daatay ee ay kala kulmeen maraakiibta Yurub, dhiirranaanta raggaas ayaa wakhti dambe xoogaa la cabudhiyey; haddana, xataa wakhtigan xaadirka ah, waxaynu mararka qaarkood maqalnaa maraakiib Ingiriis iyo Maraykan ah, kuwaas oo, biyahaas, si hagar la'aan ah loo fuulay, loogana dhacay.

Dabayl cadaalad ah oo cusub, Pequod ayaa hadda u soo dhowaanaysay cidhiidhigan; Ahab wuxuu damcay inuu dhex maro iyaga oo galay badda Javan, ka dibna, isagoo u socdaalay xagga woqooyi, oo dul maray biyaha la og yahay inay halkan iyo halkaas ku tagaan shahwada Whale, oo ku dhufashada xeebaha Filibiin, oo ay helaan xeebta fog ee Japan, wakhtiga xilli nibiriga weyn halkaas. Sidan oo kale, wareegtada Pequod waxay xaaqi doontaa ku dhawaad ​​dhammaan meelaha la yaqaan ee shahwada Whale ee adduunka, ka hor inta aysan ku soo degin khadka Baasifigga; Meesha Ahab, in kasta oo meel kasta oo kale uu ku fashilay daba-galkiisii, haddana wuxuu si adag ugu xisaabtamay inuu la dagaallamo Moby Dick, badda dhexdeeda oo uu caan ku ahaa inuu ku soo noqnoqdo; iyo xilli laga yaabo in si macquul ah loogu maleeyo in uu dhibsanayo.

Laakiin sidee hadda? Axaab miyaanu dhul taaban? shaqaalihiisa ma cabbaan hawada? Hubaal, wuxuu u joogsan doonaa biyo. Maya. Muddo dheer, hadda, cadceedu circus-gu waxay ku dhex yaacday faraantigiisa gubanaysa, wax quud ahna uma baahna ee waxa naftiisa ku jira. Haddaba Axaab. Ku calaamadee kan sidoo kale, gudaha nibiriga. Halka qolof kale ay ku raran yihiin alaab shisheeye, si loogu wareejiyo werwers shisheeye; Markabka nibiriga wareegaya ee adduunku wax rar ah ma qaado ee nafteeda iyo shaqaalaheeda, hubkooda iyo waxa ay rabaan. Waxay haysataa haro dhan oo ku jira dhalo oo ku jira xajin ku filan. Waxaa lagu dahaadhay agabyo; aan la wada isticmaali karin-lead-ka doofaarka iyo kentledge. Waxay ku sidataa biyo sannado ah. Nadiifi biyihii hore ee Nantucket; kaas oo, marka saddex sano ay dul sabeeyaan, Nantucketer, ee Pacific, door bidaan in la cabbo ka hor dareere brackish, laakiin shalay rafted off in qasacadaysan, ka Peruvian ama durdurrada Indian. Sidaa darteed, iyada oo maraakiibta kale laga yaabo in ay aadeen Shiinaha oo ka yimid New York, oo dib u soo laabtay, oo taabanaya tiro dekedo ah, markabka nibiriga, dhammaan muddadaas, laga yaabo in aanu arag hal hadhuudh oo ciid ah; shaqaalaheedu ma arag nin laakiin badmaaxayaal sabbaynaya iyaga oo kale ah. Markaa intaad u xambaartay war daad kale ayaa yimid; Waxay ugu jawaabi jireen oo keliya—“Hagaag, wiilal, waa kan doonnidii!”

Hadda, in badan oo shahwada Whales ah ayaa laga soo qabtay xeebta galbeed ee Java, agagaarka agagaarka Straits of Sunda; Runtii, sida dhulka intiisa badan, hareeraha, waxaa guud ahaan kalluumaysatadu u aqoonsadeen inay tahay meel aad u wanaagsan oo lagu safro; Sidaa darteed, markii Pequod-ku uu aad iyo aad ugu kasbaday Head-ka Java, soo-bandhigyadii si isdaba-joog ah ayaa loo bogay, waxaana lagula dardaarmay inay si weyn u soo jeedaan. Laakin in kasta oo jararkii calaacalaha ahaa ee dhulku ay si degdeg ah ugu soo dheceen qaansada starboard-ka, iyo dulalka sanka ee farxadda leh, qorfaha cusubna waa laga damiyay hawada, haddana hal jet lama sheegin. Ku dhawaadba in uu ka tanaasulo fikirkii ahaa in uu ku dhaco ciyaar kasta oo halkan ku taal, markabku aad buu ugu dhawaaday in uu cidhiidhiga galo, markii qaylo dhaanta caadadu ahayd meel sare laga maqlay, in badanna waxa na soo dhaweeyay muqaal qurux badan.

So that tormented to madness, he was now churning through the water, violently flailing with his flexible tail, and tossing the keen spade about him, wounding and murdering his own comrades.

This terrific object seemed to recall the whole herd from their stationary fright. First, the whales forming the margin of our lake began to crowd a little, and tumble against each other, as if lifted by half spent billows from afar; then the lake itself began faintly to heave and swell; the submarine bridal-chambers and nurseries vanished; in more and more contracting orbits the whales in the more central circles began to swim in thickening clusters. Yes, the long calm was departing. A low advancing hum was soon heard; and then like to the tumultuous masses of block-ice when the great river Hudson breaks up in Spring, the entire host of whales came tumbling upon their inner centre, as if to pile themselves up in one common mountain. Instantly Starbuck and Queequeg changed places; Starbuck taking the stern.

“Oars! Oars!” he intensely whispered, seizing the helm—“gripe your oars, and clutch your souls, now! My God, men, stand by! Shove him off, you Queequeg—the whale there!—prick him!—hit him! Stand up—stand up, and stay so! Spring, men—pull, men; never mind their backs—scrape them!—scrape away!”

The boat was now all but jammed between two vast black bulks, leaving a narrow Dardanelles between their long lengths. But by desperate endeavor we at last shot into a temporary opening; then giving way rapidly, and at the same time earnestly watching for another outlet. After many similar hair-breadth escapes, we at last swiftly glided into what had just been one of the outer circles, but now crossed by random whales, all violently making for one centre. This lucky salvation was cheaply purchased by the loss of Queequeg's hat, who, while standing in the bows to prick the fugitive whales, had his hat taken clean from his head by the air-eddy made by the sudden tossing of a pair of broad flukes close by.

Riotous and disordered as the universal commotion now was, it soon resolved itself into what seemed a systematic movement; for having clumped together at last in one dense body, they then renewed their onward flight with augmented fleetness. Further pursuit was useless; but the boats still lingered in their wake to pick up what drugged whales might be dropped astern, and likewise to secure one which Flask had killed and waifed. The waif is a pennoned pole, two or three of which are carried by every boat; and which, when additional game is at hand, are inserted upright into the floating body of a dead whale, both to mark its place on the sea, and also as token of prior possession, should the boats of any other ship draw near.

The result of this lowering was somewhat illustrative of that sagacious saying in the Fishery,—the more whales the less fish. Of all the drugged whales only one was captured. The rest contrived to escape for the time, but only to be taken, as will hereafter be seen, by some other craft than the Pequod.





CHAPTER 88. Schools and Schoolmasters.

The previous chapter gave account of an immense body or herd of Sperm Whales, and there was also then given the probable cause inducing those vast aggregations.

Now, though such great bodies are at times encountered, yet, as must have been seen, even at the present day, small detached bands are occasionally observed, embracing from twenty to fifty individuals each. Such bands are known as schools. They generally are of two sorts; those composed almost entirely of females, and those mustering none but young vigorous males, or bulls, as they are familiarly designated.

In cavalier attendance upon the school of females, you invariably see a male of full grown magnitude, but not old; who, upon any alarm, evinces his gallantry by falling in the rear and covering the flight of his ladies. In truth, this gentleman is a luxurious Ottoman, swimming about over the watery world, surroundingly accompanied by all the solaces and endearments of the harem. The contrast between this Ottoman and his concubines is striking; because, while he is always of the largest leviathanic proportions, the ladies, even at full growth, are not more than one-third of the bulk of an average-sized male. They are comparatively delicate, indeed; I dare say, not to exceed half a dozen yards round the waist. Nevertheless, it cannot be denied, that upon the whole they are hereditarily entitled to en bon point .

It is very curious to watch this harem and its lord in their indolent ramblings. Like fashionables, they are for ever on the move in leisurely search of variety. You meet them on the Line in time for the full flower of the Equatorial feeding season, having just returned, perhaps, from spending the summer in the Northern seas, and so cheating summer of all unpleasant weariness and warmth. By the time they have lounged up and down the promenade of the Equator awhile, they start for the Oriental waters in anticipation of the cool season there, and so evade the other excessive temperature of the year.

When serenely advancing on one of these journeys, if any strange suspicious sights are seen, my lord whale keeps a wary eye on his interesting family. Should any unwarrantably pert young Leviathan coming that way, presume to draw confidentially close to one of the ladies, with what prodigious fury the Bashaw assails him, and chases him away! High times, indeed, if unprincipled young rakes like him are to be permitted to invade the sanctity of domestic bliss; though do what the Bashaw will, he cannot keep the most notorious Lothario out of his bed; for, alas! all fish bed in common. As ashore, the ladies often cause the most terrible duels among their rival admirers; just so with the whales, who sometimes come to deadly battle, and all for love. They fence with their long lower jaws, sometimes locking them together, and so striving for the supremacy like elks that warringly interweave their antlers. Not a few are captured having the deep scars of these encounters,—furrowed heads, broken teeth, scolloped fins; and in some instances, wrenched and dislocated mouths.

But supposing the invader of domestic bliss to betake himself away at the first rush of the harem's lord, then is it very diverting to watch that lord. Gently he insinuates his vast bulk among them again and revels there awhile, still in tantalizing vicinity to young Lothario, like pious Solomon devoutly worshipping among his thousand concubines. Granting other whales to be in sight, the fishermen will seldom give chase to one of these Grand Turks; for these Grand Turks are too lavish of their strength, and hence their unctuousness is small. As for the sons and the daughters they beget, why, those sons and daughters must take care of themselves; at least, with only the maternal help. For like certain other omnivorous roving lovers that might be named, my Lord Whale has no taste for the nursery, however much for the bower; and so, being a great traveller, he leaves his anonymous babies all over the world; every baby an exotic. In good time, nevertheless, as the ardour of youth declines; as years and dumps increase; as reflection lends her solemn pauses; in short, as a general lassitude overtakes the sated Turk; then a love of ease and virtue supplants the love for maidens; our Ottoman enters upon the impotent, repentant, admonitory stage of life, forswears, disbands the harem, and grown to an exemplary, sulky old soul, goes about all alone among the meridians and parallels saying his prayers, and warning each young Leviathan from his amorous errors.

Now, as the harem of whales is called by the fishermen a school, so is the lord and master of that school technically known as the schoolmaster. It is therefore not in strict character, however admirably satirical, that after going to school himself, he should then go abroad inculcating not what he learned there, but the folly of it. His title, schoolmaster, would very naturally seem derived from the name bestowed upon the harem itself, but some have surmised that the man who first thus entitled this sort of Ottoman whale, must have read the memoirs of Vidocq, and informed himself what sort of a country-schoolmaster that famous Frenchman was in his younger days, and what was the nature of those occult lessons he inculcated into some of his pupils.

The same secludedness and isolation to which the schoolmaster whale betakes himself in his advancing years, is true of all aged Sperm Whales. Almost universally, a lone whale—as a solitary Leviathan is called—proves an ancient one. Like venerable moss-bearded Daniel Boone, he will have no one near him but Nature herself; and her he takes to wife in the wilderness of waters, and the best of wives she is, though she keeps so many moody secrets.

The schools composing none but young and vigorous males, previously mentioned, offer a strong contrast to the harem schools. For while those female whales are characteristically timid, the young males, or forty-barrel-bulls, as they call them, are by far the most pugnacious of all Leviathans, and proverbially the most dangerous to encounter; excepting those wondrous grey-headed, grizzled whales, sometimes met, and these will fight you like grim fiends exasperated by a penal gout.

The Forty-barrel-bull schools are larger than the harem schools. Like a mob of young collegians, they are full of fight, fun, and wickedness, tumbling round the world at such a reckless, rollicking rate, that no prudent underwriter would insure them any more than he would a riotous lad at Yale or Harvard. They soon relinquish this turbulence though, and when about three-fourths grown, break up, and separately go about in quest of settlements, that is, harems.

Another point of difference between the male and female schools is still more characteristic of the sexes. Say you strike a Forty-barrel-bull—poor devil! all his comrades quit him. But strike a member of the harem school, and her companions swim around her with every token of concern, sometimes lingering so near her and so long, as themselves to fall a prey.
