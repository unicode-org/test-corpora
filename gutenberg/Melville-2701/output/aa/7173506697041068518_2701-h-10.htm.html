Inkih tet exxaaxil, ta spectral appearance redden rust lih xer channelitte luk sugteeh, tet spars kee tet rigging inkih hoar-frost lih furred coox ludditteh innal sugte. dubuk tet gubi doonik daffeyteh sugte. garbô mabla toh teetik xer deben wagitto too sidiica masti moyya. oson alluwwe arabit sarteenim ceelan, tonnal qanxixxem kee qanxixxem affara sanatih addat raqteh tan sartan. birti hoopitteh addat soolak mastil, oson meekitaanam kee meekitaanam fathom sinni badih amol; kee takkay immay, doonik lowsis gubat gacte wak, nanu 6 num hawal ittat xayyowneeh, inki doonik mast-heads fanah gexak sugne; away, too forlorn-looking kullumta, lowsis gubat gacta wak, inki qangara nek wagitak, quarter-deck hail lowsis gubat gacteh sugte.

"Markab ahoy! Qado Kullum tublee?"

Laakin, qibnaytiino le kapten, pallid bulwarks fanah culteh sugteeh, isi qanxaffe af fanah culsak sugeeh, usuk isi gabat culteh sugte; kee caacay away fayya iyya, usuk foyya isih is yaabbem faxa toh sinnim. too uddur kay markab uxih ossimak suge fanat yan baxxo. Baxaabaxsale tibba le gurral Pequod baddi maritte tama qawwalaylah addat tan taqabitte qimmoh addah White Whale migaq gersi doonik fanah xayyoosak sugte wak, Ahab inki wakti sooliseh; toh iyyaanam duuduntah usuk huuri oobise qibnaytu, bohoy le caacay waaso alle week. Laakin, usuk qagitak isi trumpet yeyyeeqeh, tet qaynat elle yaaxigennal, qunxaaneyti Nantucketer kinnim kee uxxuk buxah addal geytimam, usuk kaxxaam qimmoh addah yeyyeeqeh—“Ahoy wokkel! Tah Pequod, baadal inkih geytimam! ——”

too uddur namma ugutma qadlinaan luk tabimte, kee too wakaak, lakal, ken dubuk yan gitaak ugutak, shoals qunxa umaane sinni kullumta, too dago ayroorah naharal placidly giirisak suge ni xaqut, darted iroh maca celtam diriirigoh galitteh, kee ranged sinnih fooca kee aft flaks lih qibnaytiino. Faxennal usuk abah yan safar addat Ahab mango adda naharal sitta ceela mabla wagsiisak, uxih, faxe monomaniac numuh, veriest trifles capriciously maqnisso quuka.

"Yok iroh giiris, hinnaa?" Ahab leeh addat wagitak, tutucte. Qangor addat dagoom celta, takkay immay xongolo cabuli idaltuh yan numuk muxxi kaxxa cato sinni rooka tatrusse. Laakin, steersman fanah gaceeh, aham fan doonik lih gexak sugeh yan, usuk isi qunxa lubak xongolot,—“Up helm! Keep her off round the world!”

baad dariifal! too xongolot mangom tan kaxxiini cuso uguugussam; takkay immay ankeey inkih too meeko gexsissa? dubuk ixxima sinni qawwalaylooli kaxxam ximmo mankel qimbisne, too nanu darrel cabneh amaan, inkih yan wakti nek naharal sugte.

Tama baadal gaba kalti sinni booxal sugteeh, ayro mawqah exxal gexak sugteeh, qusba baaxooxal geytimnaah, faxe Cyclades kee Islands of King Solomon fanah gexak, kaxxaam meqeeh, qimmoh addah geytimnaah, tohih taagah, safar addat xagana sugte. Laakin too baxsa le mistiritteh addat nanu soonitnam, hinnay too jinni phantom tormented chase too, inki inki wakti hinnay gersi wakti, inkih tan seehadayti baxi kalbih foocal geytima; too maroh tan baadal diiriyak, oson hinnay nee miraacisan mazes hinnay gudet nee cabeenih whelmed.
