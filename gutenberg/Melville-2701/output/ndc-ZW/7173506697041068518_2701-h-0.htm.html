Zvinozooneka kuti uyu unongorwadza burrower uye grub-worm wadhiabhorosi murombo we Sub-Sub unoita inga wakapfuura ngeVaticans dzakareba uye mumigwagwa-yepashi, kutora chero random allusions kune whales yaaizowana chero zvakadaro mubhuku ripi neripi, rakachena kana rakashata. Hino amufaniri, mundaa imwe ngaimwe, kutora mazwi e higgledy-piggledy whale, kunyazwi ari emene, mune zvikamu izvi, ku cetology yevhangeri yemene. Kuretu ndizvo. Iwewe uri wedzinza iroro risina gonda, rakatsvuka iro risina waini yenyika ino inozombodziya; uye kune uyo kunyange Pale Sherry angave akasimba zvakanyanya rosy; asi naani munhu dzimweni nguva anoda kugara, uye kunzwa murombo-dhiabhorosi, zvakare; uye kukura convivial pamusoro misodzi; uye uvabhuire zvakajeka, nge madziso akadzara nge magirazi asina chiro, uye mukusuwa kusingadakadzi zvachose—Isiyai, Sub-Subs! Ngokuti kudai ngekuti munozotambudzwa kakurutu kudakadza nyika, ndizvo munozoramba mwechibonga mhetsa nariini! Dai ndaikwanisa kuchenesa Hampton Court neTuileries ngendaa yenyu! Asi medzai misodzi yenyu, mukwire mudenga kune chidziro cheumambo ngemwoyo yenyu; ngokuti shamwari dzako dzakaenda pamberi dziri kuchenesa matenga ane madziro manomwe, nekuita vapoteri vaGabrieri, Mikaeri, naRafaeri vakarekeredzwa kwenguva yakareba, kurwisa kuuya kwako.
