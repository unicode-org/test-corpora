CHITSAUKO 10. Shamwari Yepamwoyo.

Kupetuka Spouter-Inn kubva kuChapel, ndakawana Queequeg aripo ari ega; iye achisiya Chapel asati akomborerwa imwe nguva. Wakagara pachigaro pamberi pe mwoto, nge makumbo ake ari pa chitofu, nge nyara imwe wakabata pasinde ne hope yake chidhori chake chidoko che negro; kudongorera zvakasimba muhope yaro, uye nebanga re jack richirova zvinyoro-nyoro kure pamhino yaro, nguva iyoyo achizviungudza ega ngenjira yake yechihedheni.

Asi ngekuti zvino wakavhiringidzwa, wakaisa mufananidzo; uye ngekukasira, kuenda kutafura, wakatora bhuku guru apo, akariisa pachipfuva chake akatanga kuerenga mapeji ngekugara nemaune; pa peji imwe neimwe ye makumi mashanu—sezvandaifunga—kuema kwechinguvana, kutarisa pasina chiro zvakamupoteredza, uye kupa mazwi kune mhere-mhere yakakweverwa kwenguva yakareba yekushamisika. Aizotangazve pamakumi mashanu anoteera; kuita inga wakatanga pa nhamba imwe nguva imwe na imwe, inga kuti aakoni kuerenga zvinodarika makumi mashanu, zve zvakaitika basi nge nhamba yakakura kudaro ye makumi mashanu yakawanikwa pamwepo, kuti kushamisika kwake nge kuwanda kwe mapeji kwakadakadza.

Nekufarira kwakanyanya ndakagara ndakamutarisa. Savage kunyazi aive, uye akasvibiswa zvakashata ngendaa yehope—kunyazwi ngekuda kwangu—hope yake yainge ine chiro chainge chisingadakadzi. Aukoni kufisha mweya. Kubudikidza ngezvinyorwa zvake zveshe zvisiri zvenyika, ndaifunga kuti ndakaona zviratidzo zvemwoyo wakapfava wakatendeseka; uye mumaziso ake akakura, akadzika, akaita semoto akasvipa uye akashinga, kwaiita kunge zviratidzo zvemweya waizoshinga chiuru chemadhimoni. Uye padera pe zveshe izvi, paiya ne kuzvikudza kumweni pamusoro pe muhedheni, kunyazwi kusaremekedza kwake kwaiya kusikakoni kuremara zvachose. Wainga eiita inga mwanarume acizivi kumboita cringe zve acizivi kumboita mundhu waizomukweretesa. Kuti zvainge, zvakare, kuti soro rake richiveurwa, guma rake rakakweverwa kubanze mukusununguka uye rakajeka, uye raioneka rakatambanuka kupfuura zvaizoita, izvi andizoshinga kuzvisarudza; asi chokwadi chaiva musoro wake waiva phrenologically yakanakisa imwe. Zvinokona kuita inga zvinoshekedza, asi zvakandikarakadzisa soro ra General Washington, kudai ngezvinooneka muma busts ake akakurumbira. Yainga no njira imweyo yakareba yainga yakadzokera sure kubve padera po mavhudzi, yainga yakajeka maningi, inga ndau mbiri jakareba jainga jakazara ngo miti padera. Queequeg aiva George Washington akagadzirwa nenzira yekudya vanhu.

Pandainge ndeimuongorora kudaro, ndeiita inga ndeiningira kunze kwedutu kubva mu casement, aazi kumbozwisisa kuvapo kwangu, aazi kumbodzinesa ngekutarisisa kamwe; asi akaoneka akabatikana zvachose nekuverenga mapeji ebhuku rinoshamisa. Kufunga ngezvekubatana kwatainge takaata pamwepo usiku hwakapfuura, uye kunyanya kufunga ruoko rwerudo rwandainge ndawana rwakaposherwa pamusoro pangu pandakamuka mangwanani, ndakafunga kuti kusava nehanya kwake uku kwaishamisa yaamho. Asi zvipuka zvisikwa zvisizi kujeka; dzimwe nguva hauzivi chaizvo kuti unozvitora sei. Pakutanga vanoita inga vanoita zviro zvicikabezo; kudzikama kwavo kuzviunganidza kwekureruka kunoita kunge hungwaru hweSocrates. Ndainge ndaonawo kuti Queequeg haazi kumbobatana zvachose, kana asi zvishoma, nevamwe vafambisi vengarava muimba yevashanyi. Iyena aazivi kuita budiriro iri yese; akaita sekuti asina chido chekukudza denderedzwa revaanozivana navo. Zvese izvi zvakandirova semunhu ane simba; asi, pa kufunga kechipiri, paiva nechimwe chinhu chainge chakanyanya kukwirira mukati mayo. Heino murumbwana wainge nezviuru makumi mairi zvemakiromita kubva kumhatso, ngenjira ye Cape Horn, kureva kuti—iyo ndiyo njira imwe yaaizokwanisa kugumayo—akakandwa pakati pevanhu semusingaziikanwi kwaari inga ari munyika Jupiter; uye asi aiita kunge akasununguka zvachose; kuchengetedza kudzikama kwakanyanya; kugutsikana neshamwari yake pachake; nguva dzose akaenzana naye pachake. Ngo gwinyiso izvi zvainga kubata ko uzivi hwakanaka; kunyange zvazvo pasina mubvunzo kuti akanga asina kumbonzwa kuti pane chinhu chakadai. Asi, pamweni, kuti tiite vazivi vemene, isu vanofa atifaniri kuziva kurarama kudaro kana kuedza kudaro. Ngo kudaro, pandinozwa kuti mundhu wakadaro kana wakadaro anojipa kuti aite muzivi, ndinopejisira ngo kunanga kuti, inga mukaji mukuru wainga no dyspeptic, anodikana kunge “wakapwanya digester yake.”

Pandainge ndakagarapo mukamuri iroro rainge riri rega zvino; mwoto unopisha pashi, mu ndau iyoyo yakapfava apo, pasure po kusimba kwawo kokutanga kwadziyisa mweya, unozopenya basi kuti uonekwe; mumvuri wemadekoni uye zvidhori zvinoungana zvakapoteredza ma casements, uye zvinotarisa mukati mwedu vakanyarara, vaviri vari vega; dutu rinoputika pasina mu kuzvimba kunoremekedzeka; Ndakatanga kuva nepfungwa dzemanzwiro anoshamisa. Ndakanzwa kunyungudika mukati mangu. Hapanazve mwoyo wangu wakatsemuka uye ruoko rwangu rwakapenga rwakatendeuka kunyika yemapere. Savage iyi inozorodza yainge yairidzikinura. Ipopo wakagara, kutama kukhatara kwake kwaibhuya nge njira yainga isina unyepi hwakabudirira no unyepi hwakashata. Ainge ari mushango; chionero chaicho chezvionero zvekuona; asi ndakatanga kuzvinzwa ndichikwezverwa kwaari zvisingazivikanwi. Uye zviro zvimwezvo zvaizodzinga vamweni vakawanda, ndizvona zvainge zviri magnets akandikwevera kudaro. Ndichaedza shamwari yechihedheni, ndakafunga, sezvo mutsa wechiKristu wakaratidza asi kuremekedza kwakashata. Ndakakwevera chigaro changu pedyo naye, ndokuita zviratidzo zveushamwari uye mazano, ndichiedza zvese zvandaikona kuti nditaure naye panguva iyoyo. Pakutanga iyena aazivi kuvona zvishoma kuenderera mberi uku; asi nguva ino, pandakataura nezvekugamuchira kwake kweusiku hwekupedzisira, akaita kuti andibvunze kana tichazorara zvakare here. Ndakamuudza kuti hongu; whereat ndakafunga kuti aitarisa kufara, pamwe zvishoma akarumbidzwa.

Takazopetudza bhuku iri teshe, inini ndakaedza kumutsanangurira chinangwa chekudhindwa, uye zvinorehwa ngemifananidzo mishoma yaive mukati maro. Ngokudaro ndakakasira kuita kuda kwake; uye kubva ipapo takaenda kuno jabbering zvakanakisisa zvatinokwanisa pamusoro pezvinhu zvakasiyana-siyana zvekunze zvinoonekwa mudhorobha iri rakakurumbira. Pasina nguva ndakakurudzira utsi hwemagariro; uye, achibudisa chikwama chake ne tomahawk, akandipa chinyararire puff. Uye takagara teichinjanisa mapuffs kubva mupombi iyoyo yemushango yake, uye teigara teichipfuura nguva ngenguva pakati pedu.

Kana pakanga paine chando chekusava nehanya neni muzamu rePagan, utsi uhu hunodakadza, hwe genial hwatainge tinawo, hwakakurumidza kunyungudika, ndokutisiya tiri shamwari. Wakaita inga wakanditora ngo kujidodokesa zve acikakumbiwi inga inini kwaari; uye apo utsi hwedu hwakapera, akasungira huma yake pane yangu, akandibata muchiuno, akati kubva zvino takaroorana; zvinoreva, mumutsara wenyika yake, kuti taive shamwari dzepamwoyo; aizondifira nemufaro, kana zvichidikanwa. Mu mundhu wo mu nyika, murazvo wo ushamwari wo kukasika uwu waizoita inga wakanonoka maningi, ciro co kutama kugondwa maningi; asi mu savage iyi iri nyore iyo mitemo yekare ingadai isingashandi.

Tapedza kurya zvemadekoni, nekubhuyazve nekuputa, takaenda ku room yedu teshe. Wakandiitira chipo chesoro rake rakazodzwa; akabudisa chikwama chake chikuru chefodya, uye achitsvoda pashi pefodya, akabudisa madhora makumi matatu esirivha; ndokuzozviparadzira patafura, ndokuzviparadzanisa nemashini kuita zvikamu zviviri zvakaenzana, ndokusundire chimwe chazvo kwandiri, ndokuti ndezvangu. Ndainge ndaenda kunotsimbidza; asi akandinyarara nekuzvidira muhomwe dzemabhuruku angu. Ndakavasiya vachigara. Wakazoita minamato yake ye madekoni, wakabudisa chifananidzo chake, akadusa bhodhi remoto remapepa. Nge zvikwangwari zvimweni ne zviratidzo, ndaifunga kuti aiita inga anoshushikana kuti ndimubatanidze; asi ndichiziva zvakanaka zvaizotevera, ndakafunga kwechinguvana kuti, kana akandikoka, ndaizoteerera kana zvimwe.

Ndainge ndiri muKristu akanaka; akaberekwa uye akarerwa pachipfuva cheChechi yePresbyterian isingakanganisi. Hino ndaizobatana sei nemunamati wezvifananidzo uyu wemusango mukunamata chikamu chake chehuni? Asi kunamata chiinyi? ndakafunga I. Unofunga kuti zvino, Ishmaeri, kuti Mwari mukuru we denga ne pashi—vahedheni ne veshe vakabatanidzwa—vanokona kuita godo nge chikamu chisina kukosha che huni yakasvipa? Zvichiyikayitiki! Asi kunamata kunorevei?—kuita kuda kwa Mwari— ndikona kunamata. Uye kuda kwaMwari chiinyi?—kuita kune vamweni vangu zvandinoda kuti vamweni vangu vandiitire— ndizvo zvinoda Mwari. Zvino, Queequeg ndiye munhu wangu. Uye ndinoshuwira kuti Queequeg uyu andiitirei? Ngei, batana neni mu njira yangu yo kunamata yo Presbyterian. Nge ndaa ye izvi, ndinosisira kubatana naye mu zvake; ergo, ndinofanira kutendeuka munamati wezvidhori. Saka ndakatungidza zvicherwa; akabatsira kutsigira chifananidzo chidiki chisina mhosva; akamupa bhisikiti rakapiswa neQueequeg; salamed pamberi pake kaviri kana katatu; akatsvoda mhino yake; uye izvozvo zvakaitwa, takabvisa mbatya ndokuenda kunorara, tiri murugare nehana dzedu pachedu uye nenyika yese. Asi atizi kuenda kunoata ticikabhuyi padoko.

Kuti zviri kudini andizii; asi hapana nzvimbo yakafanana nemubhedha wekubudisa zvakavanzika pakati peshamwari. Murume nemukadzi, vanoti, apo panovhura pashi pemweya yavo kune umwe neumwe; uye vamwe vakaroorana vakura vanowanzonyepa uye kutaura pamusoro penguva dzekare kusvikira pedyo nemangwanani. Ngo kudaro, mu honeymoon yo mwoyo yedu, inini na Queequeg takaata—vaviri vanodakara zve vano rudo.





CHITSAUKO 11. Mucheka weusiku.

Tainge takaata kudaro pamubhedhu, teireketa nekuata nguwa shomani, uye Queequeg nguva ngenguva ngekukanda ngerudo mirenje yake yakanyorwa nge brown pamusoro pangu, uye ngekuzoikwevera sure; saka zvakakwana sociable uye vakasununguka uye nyore taive isu; apo, pakupedzisira, nge ndaa ye confabulations dzedu, chiinyi chiro chidoko chakasara mwetiri chakaenda zvachose, uye takazwa kuda kumurukazve, kunyazwi kuzorora kwe masikati kwainge kuchiri njira imweni kuenda pashi pe mberi.

Eya, takazomuka maningi; zvakanyanya zvekuti nzvimbo yedu yekuata yakatanga kuremba, uye zvishoma nezvishoma takazviwana takagara; ngubo dzakatitendenedza zvakanaka, dzakasendamira pamusoro-bhodhi nemabvi edu mana akakweverwa pedyo, uye mhino dzedu mbiri dzakakotama pamusoro padzo, sekuti mabvi edu aidziya-mapani. Takazwa zvakanaka maningi zve takajizwa zvakanaka, maningi-ningi ngokuti kwaitonhora maningi kubanze; zvirokwazvo kunze kwezvipfeko zvemubhedha zvakare, kuona kuti pakanga pasina mwoto mukamuri. Kunyanya kudaro, ndinoti, ngekuti ngegwinyiso kuti udakarire kudziya kwemuiri, chikamu chimweni chidoko chako chinofanira kutonhora, ngekuti hapana unhu munyika ino husiri sezvauri ngekusiyana kwega. Apana chiro chiripo pachacho. Kana ukadzibata kuti wakasununguka kweshe, uye wakagara nguva yakareba kudaro, hino auzombobhuyiwa kuti wakasunungukazve. Asi kana, kudai ndi Queequeg neni pamubhedhu, mupendero we mhino yako kana korona ye soro rako inotonhora zvishomani, ngenyi hino, zvemene, mu pfungwa dzakawanda unozwa kudakara maningi ne kudziya kusingashaishi. Nge ndaa ye izvi ndau yekuata haifaniri kumboiswa mwoto, unova umwe we zviro zvisingadakadzi zve upfumi zve vapfumi. Ngokuti kukwirira kwe rudzi urwu rwekunakira kusava nechiro asi chifukidzo pakati pako ne snugness yako ne kutonhora kwe mweya we kubanze. Pasurepo, munoata inga n’anga imwe basi inodziya iri pakati po kristaro yo arctic.

Tainge takagara ngenjira iyi yekugwadama kwenguva yakati, apo nguva imweyo ndakafunga kuti ndinozovhura madziso angu; ngokuti apo ndiri pakati pemachira, kunyazwi masikati kana usiku, nekuti ndakaata kana ndakamuka, ndine njira yekugara ndakavhara madziso angu, kuti ndiwedzere kuisa pfungwa pa snugness yekuva pamubhedhu. Ngekuti apana mundhu unozombozwa kuzikana kwake zvakanaka asi madziso ake akavharwa; kunge rima raiva zvemene chikamu chakafanira chezviro zvedu, kunyange chiedza chinonyanya kukodzera chikamu chedu chedhaka. Pandakavhura madziso angu apo, nekubuda muchidima changu chinodakadza uye chandakazvisika ndega kupinda muchidima chekunze chakamanikidzwa uye chakashata chemaawa gumi nemaviri-eusiku asina kuvhenekwa, ndakaona kunyangadzwa kusingadakadzi. Uye andizi kumboramba zvaakabhuiwa ndi Queequeg kuti pamweni zvainga zvakanaka kuti tibate mwenje, ngo kuvona kuti tainga takamuka maningi; uye kunze kwezvo akazwa chido chakasimba chekuva nemapuffs mashoma akanyarara kubva kuTomahawk yake. Ngazvireketwe, kuti kunyazi ndainge ndazwa kunyangadzwa kwakasimba kudaro ngekuputa kwake mumubhedhu usiku hwakapfuura, asi wona kuti kusarura kwedu kwakaoma kunokura sei apo rudo rwunouya kamwe chete kuzovakotamisa. Ngokuti wari andizi kudakarira ciro ciri nane kupinda kuva na Queequeg waiputa padhuze neni, kunyazwi pa mubhedhu, ngokuti waiita inga wakadzara ngo kudakara kwakanyarara kwakadaro ko pamuzi pa nguvayo. Ndainga ndicikakhatari maningi ngo pamusoro po mutemo wo inishuwarenzi wo muridzi wo nyumba. Ndairarama chete kune condensed yakavanzika kusununguka kwekugovana pombi uye gumbeze neshamwari chaiyo. Tiine mabhachi edu ane bvudzi akakweverwa pabendekete redu, zvino takapfuura Tomahawk kubva kune imwe kuenda kune imwe, kuguma zvishomani zvishomani pakakura pamusoro pedu chiedza chebhuruu chakarembera cheutsi, chakajekeswa ngemwoto wemwenje itsva yakatungidzwa.

Kuti zvainge zviri kuti muedzi uyu undulating wakaguqa mhuka iyi kuretu kuenda kuzviro zviri kuretu, andizii, asi zvino wakabhuya ngechitsuwa chake chekubarwa kwake; uye, ndichida kunzwa nhoroondo yake, ndakamukumbira kuti aenderere mberi nekuzvitaura. Iyena wakazwira ngo kudakara. Kunyazi nguwayo inini ndainga ndisikazwisisi mazwi ake mashomani, asi kujekesa kwakazotevera, apo ndainge ndanyatsoziya mazwi ake akatsemuka, zvino zvinonditendera kuti ndipe ndaa yeshe kudai ngezvinokona kukhombise mupfupa rega rega randinopa.





CHITSAUKO 12. Nhoroondo yehupenyu.

Queequeg wainga mundhu wo ku Rokovoko, ciwi cainga kuretu ku Madokero no ku Mabvazuva. Azvizi kudzaka pa mepu iri yeshe; nzvimbo dzechokwadi hadzimbove.

Pano mhuka itsva yakaberekwa yeirumba-rumba mhuka dzemusango dzayo dzekuzvarwa mumasango, ichiteverwa ngembudzi dzinoruma, ingatei i muti wakasvibira; kunyange zvakadaro, mumweya waQueequeg waida zvikuru, wakavanda chido chakasimba chekuona chimwe chinhu chakawanda chechiKristudhomu kupfuura muenzaniso wewhaler kana maviri. Baba vake vainge vari Mukuru Mukuru, Mambo; sekuru vake Muprista Mukuru; uye parutivi rwemadzimai akazvirumbidza tete vaive vakadzi vevarwi vasingakundiki. Painga no ngazi yakanaka maningi mu tsinga jake—zviro zvo umambo; kunyange zvazvo akasvibiswa zvinosuruvarisa, ndinotya, nekuda kwekudya vanhu kwaakadzidza muhuduku hwake husina kudzidziswa.

Ngarava yo Sag Harbour yakavhakacira ndau ya baba vake, zve Queequeg wakatsvaka njira yo kuenda ku nyika jo ciKristu. Asi ngarava, ine vafambisi vengarava vakadzara, yakaramba ngubo yake; uye kwete Mambo ese baba vake kukurudzira aigona kukunda. Asi Queequeg wakapika mhiko. Iyena ari ega mu cikepe cake, wakakhwedera ku ndau iri kuretu, yaaiziva kuti ngarava inodikana kupfuurapo paaizobuda pa ciwi. Kune rumwe rutivi kwaiva nedombo rekorari; kune rumwe rurimi rwenyika yakaderera, yakafukidzwa nemasango emangrove akakura kunze mumvura. Kufisha chikepe chake, chichiri kuyangarara, pakati pemasango aya, nemberi kwacho kwakaenda kugungwa, wakagara pashi kumashure, chikepe chakaderera munyara; uye apo ngarava yacho ichifamba-famba, sechiedza akakurumidza kunze; akawana rutivi rwake; ne dash imwe yekudzokera kumashure yetsoka yake yakapinduka ndokunyudza chikepe chake; akakwira pamusoro pemaketani; uye akadzikanda ngekureba kwakadzara pamusoro pechikepe, akabata mhete-bhaudhi apo, akapika kuti asaisiya, kunyange yakatsemuka zvidimbu.

Pasina maturo mukuru wengarava wakamutyisidzira kuti aizomukanda mugungwa; akasimudza cutlass pamusoro pemaoko ake asina kupfeka; Queequeg wainge mwanakomana wa Mambo, na Queequeg aazi kuvhunduka. Ngo kukhuvazwa ngo kutama kuthya kwake, no cido cake co kushanyira ciKristudhomu, mukuru wo ngarava wakazosiya, womubvunja kuti waizokwanisa kujiita inga ari pamuzi. Asi uyu mujaha wakanaka we savage—uyu Prince we gungwa we Wales, aazi kumboona kamuri ra Kaputeni. Vakamuisa pashi pakati pevafambisi vezvikepe, vakamuita munhu wehowe. Asi inga Czar Peter wakagutsikana ngo kushanda ngo simba mu ndau jinogadzirwa ngarava jo madhorobha o ku nyika jimweni, Queequeg aazivi kunyangajwa ngo kunyangajwa, kudari ngo kudaro waizowana ngo kudakara simba ro kujekesa vandhu vo mu nyika yake vacizivi kujijiswa. Ngokuti pashi—ndizvona zvaakandironzera—wakatungamirirwa ngo cido cikuru co kujija pakati po maKristu, njira jo kuita kuti vandhu vake vachiri kudakara kupinda zvavaiita; uye kupfuura izvozvo, vachiri nani kupfuura zvavaive. Asi, maiwe kani! maitiro e whalemen akakurumidza kumugonesa kuti kunyange maKristu anokwanisa kuva anosuruvarisa uye akaipa; zvisingaperi zvakanyanya kudaro, kupfuura vahedheni vese vababa vake. Ndakaguma pakupedzisira muSag Harbor yekare; uye kuona kuti vafambisi vezvikepe vaiita sei ipapo; uyezve kuenda kuNantucket, uye kuona kuti vakashandisa sei muhoro wavo munzvimbo iyoyowo , Queequeg murombo wakazvisiya ngekuti zvakarashika. Ndakafunga kuti, inyika yakashata mu meridians dzeshe; Ndinozofa ndiri muhedheni.

Uye nge ndaa iyoyo munamati we zvidhori wekare mu mwoyo, wakaramba eigara pakati pe maKristu aya, eipfeka ngubo dzavo, eizama kubhuya mazwi avo e gibbery. Hino njira dzinoshamisa pamusoro pake, kunyange zvino nguva imwe kubva kumba.

Nge mazano, ndakamubvunza kuti aazi kukarakadza kupetuka ere, ne kuita coronation; sezvo zvino aizofunga kuti baba vake vakafa uye vakaenda, iye akanga akwegura zvikuru uye asina simba pazvinyorwa zvekupedzisira. Wakapingura kuti aiwa, azvizi kumboitika; uye akawedzera kuti aitya chiKristu, kana kuti chiKristu, chakanga chisina kukodzera kukwira pachigaro cheumambo chakachena uye chisina tsvina cheMadzimambo makumi matatu evahedheni pamberi pake. Asi ngo kupera ko nguva, wakananga kuti waizopetuka, — paaizojizwazve kuti wabhabhatijwazve. Asi, nge ndaa ye nonce, wakafunga kuhamba nge chikepe, ne kudyara oats yake ye mushango mu gungwa reshe re mana. Vainge vakamuita harpooneer, uye simbi iyo inobaya yaive panzvimbo yetsvimbo zvino.
