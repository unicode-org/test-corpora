Chiinyi chiro cheshe ichi chandainge ndeiita, ndakafunga mumwoyo wangu—muisawo mundhuwo inga inini: une chikonzero chakawanda chekundithya, kudai ngekuti inini ndinofanira kumuthya. Zviri nani kurara nemunhu anodya vanhu akadhakwa pane muKristu akadhakwa.

"Muridzi wendau," ndakadaro inini, "muronzerei kuti aise tomahawk yake apo, kana paipi, kana chero chamunoidaidza; muronzerei kuti arekere kuputa, muchidimbu, uye ini ndinozopinda naye. Esi andidakariri kuva ngemuisa anoputa pamubhedhu neni. Zvine ngozi. Padera peizvozvo, andina inishuwarenzi."

Izvi zvakabhuiwa kuna Queequeg, wakakasira kuzwira, zve wakandikhombijirazve ngo ungwaru kuti ndiende pa mubhedhu—wakazungunuka ku divi rimweni inga zvokuti—“Andizobati murenje wenyu.”

Ndakati: “Usiku hwakanaka, muridzi weimba, unokwanisa kuenda.”

Ndakatendeuka, uye handina kumborara zviri nani muhupenyu hwangu.
