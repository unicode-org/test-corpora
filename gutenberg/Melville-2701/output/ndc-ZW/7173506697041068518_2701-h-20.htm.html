CHITSAUKO 101. Mudziyo wekubika.

Ere ngarava yeChirungu inopera kuona, inga yakaiswa pashi pano, kuti wakarumbidza kubva kuLondon, uye wakapiwa zina ngemufi Samuel Enderby, mutengesi weguta iroro, wekutanga wemhatso yakakurumbira yekubata howe ye Enderby & Sons; mhatso iyo mukufunga kwangu murombo whaleman, inouya isiri kure kumashure kwemhatso dzeumambo dzakabatana dzeTudors neBourbons, mupfungwa yekudakarira kwechokwadi kwenhoroondo. Kuguma rini, gore ra Mambo wedu 1775 risati rasvika, mhatso iyi huru yekubata howe dzainge iripo, magwaro angu akawanda ehowe haaiti kuti zvijeke; asi mugore iroro (1775) yakagadzirira ngarava dzekutanga dzeChirungu dzakambogara dzichivhima Sperm Whale; asi kwemakore makumi maviri apfuura (kubvira muna 1726) mabhokisi edu ane ushingi uye Maceys eNantucket uye Munda weMizambiringa ainge ane zvikepe zvikuru aiteverera Leviathan iyoyo, asi kuNorth neSouth Atlantic chete: kwete kumwe. Ngazvinyorwe zvakajeka pano, kuti vaNantucketers ndivo vakatanga pakati pevanhu kurova ngesimbi yakabudirira Sperm Whale huru; uye kuti kwehafu yezana remakore ndivo vega vanhu vepasi rose vakamurova zvakadaro.

Mugore ra 1778, ngarava yakanaka, Amelia, yakagadzirirwa kuitira chinangwa chakajeka, uye ngekutungamirirwa kwega kweEnderbys yakasimba, yakatenderera Cape Horn ngekushinga kwakanyanya, uye yaive yekutanga pakati pemarudzi kudzikisa chikepe che whale chemhando ipi zvayo muGungwa guru reKumaodzanyemba. Rwendo rwacho rwaiva rweunyanzvi uye rwerombo rakanaka; uye kupetuka paanogara nekubata kwake kwakadzara mbeu inokosha, muenzaniso waAmelia wakakasira kuteererwa ngezvikepe zvimweni, zveChirungu neAmerica, uye ngekudaro ndau yakakura yeSperm Whale yePacific yakaposherwa yakavhurika. Asi asikazi kugutsikana nge chiito ichi chakanaka, mhatso isingarembi yakadzisimudzazve: Samuel neVanakomana vake veshe —vangani, mai avo vanoziya vega —uye pasi pekutungamirirwa kwavo ngekukasira, uye chikamu, ndinofunga, ngekubhadharisa kwavo, hurumende yeBritain yakakurudzirwa kutuma sloop-of-war Rattler pa whaly voyagea ye South disco. Kurairwa nge Post-Captain wengarava, Rattler wakaita rwendo rwekuvhunduka rwaro, uye wakaita mushando; kuti yakawanda sei haina kuoneka. Asi izvi azvisiri zvega. Mu gore ro 1819, nyumba imweyo yakabeka ngarava yo discovery whale yavo, kuti vaende koita rwendo rwo kuravira ku mvura jiri kure jo Japão. Ngarava yondhoyo—inodanwa kamare kuti “Syren”—yakaita rwendo hwo kueja hwo kueja; uye ndizvo zvakaita kuti nzvimbo huru yeJapan Whaling Ground yakatanga kuzivikanwa. Syren mu rwendo urwu rwakakurumbira rwakarairwa nge Kaputeni Coffin, muNantucketer.

Kuremeredzwa kweshe kune Enderbies, ngendaa iyoyo, iyo mhatso yavo, ndinofunga, iripo kuguma nyamashi; kunyange zvazvo pasina kumbozengurira Samueri wekutanga anofanira kunge akatsvedza tambo yake kare kuenda kuGungwa guru reKumaodzanyemba reimwe nyika.

Ngarava yakapiwa zina rake yainga yakasisira kuremekedzwa, ngokuti yainga yecirumba maningi zve yainga yakasisira maningi mu njira jese. Ndakamukwidza kamwe pakati peusiku kumwe kunze kwePatagonian coast, uye ndakamwa flip yakanaka pasi mu forecastle. Yainga gam yakanaka yatainga nayo, zve yese yainga ma trump—mweya wese wainga mu ngarava. Hupenyu hupfupi kwavari, uye rufu runodakadza. Uye gam iyo yakanaka yandainge nayo—nguva yakareba, nguva yakareba maningi mushure mokunge Ahabhi mukuru abata mapuranga ake ngechitsitsinho chake chenyanga yenzou—inondikarakadza ngekugamuchira vaeni kunokudzwa, kwakasimba, kweSaxon kwengarava iyoyo; uye dai mufundisi wangu andikanganwe, uye dhiabhorosi andirangarire, kana ndikazozvishaya. Vindhuja? Ndakati tine flip here? Eya, uye takaitenderedza ngekukurumidza kwemagaloni gumi paawa; uye apo dutu rakaguma (ngekuti riri dutu kurepo nge Patagonia), uye maoko eshe—vaenzi ne veshe—vakadaidzwa ku reef topsails, tainge takarema-pamusoro zvekuti taifanira kuzunguzana mudenga nge bowlines; uye isu tisingazivi takapeta zviketi zvemabhachi edu muzvikepe, zvekuti takaremberapo, takakurumidza kufema mudutu raiungudza, muenzaniso wekunyevera kune matara ese akadhakwa. Asi, masti aazi kuende ku ndau imweni; uye ngekukurumidza takadzika, zvakanyanya kudzikama, zvekuti taifanira kupfuura flip zvakare, kunyange zvazvo munyu wakashata wakaputika pasi forecastle scuttle, pane zvakanyanya kudzikisira uye wakazvibika kune zvandaida.

Nyama yemombe yainge yakanaka—yakaoma, asi ine muviri mukati mayo. Vakati nyama yemombe-yemombe; vamweni, kuti yainge iri nyama yemombe ye dromedary; asi andizii, ngegwinyiso, kuti zvakaitika kudini. Vainge vanewo ma dumpling; zvishoma, asi zvakakura, zvakaenzana globular, uye zvisingaparadziki dumplings. Ndaifunga kuti unokwanisa kuzvizwa, wozviumburudza mukati mwako mushure mokunge zvamedzwa. Kuti wakakotamira mberi maningi, waizoita ngozi yo kukuposha inga mabhora o billiard. Chingwa—asi izvozvo zvaisakwanisa kubatsirwa; kunze kwezvo, yaive inopesana nescorbutic; muchidimbu, chingwa chaive nemari itsva chete yavaive nayo. Asi forecastle yainga isizi kurema maningi, zve zvainga zvicizivi kureruka maningi kupinda mu kona yakasvipa pawainga wairya. Asi zveshe zveshe, kumutora kubve rori kuenda ku helm, eifunga ngezvekukura kwemabhoira emubiki, kusanganisira mabhoira ake e parchment anorarama; mberi nekumashure, ndinoti, Samuel Enderby yaive ngarava inofadza; yekufamba kwakanaka uye zvakawanda; flip yakanaka uye yakasimba; kutsemuka vamwe vose, uye guta guru kubva shangu zvitsitsinho kuti ngowani-bhendi.

Asi nge ndaa yenyi, munokarakadza, kuti Samuel Enderby, ne vamweni vabati ve howe ve Chirungu vendinoziya—azvizi kuita veshe—vainga ngarava dzakakurumbira kudaro, dzinoashira vaeni; iyo yakapfuura ichitenderera nyama yemombe, nechingwa, nechirongo, nejoke; uye havana kukurumidza kuremba nekurya, nekumwa, nekuseka? Ndinozomuronzera. Kudakara kwakanaka kwakawanda kwevanobata howe dzeChirungu inyaya yekutsvakurudza nhoroondo. Uye andizi kumbosiya kutsvakurudza kwe nhoroondo ye whale, apo zvakaita inga zvinodiwa.

VaNgezi vakatangwa ngevaHolland, Zealand, neDanes pakubata howe dze whale; kubva kwavari vakawana mazwi akawanda achiripo muhove; uye chii chimwezve, mafuta avo ekare, anobata zvakawanda zvekurya nekunwa. Ngokuti, inga chiro chakajairika, ngarava yekutengesa yeChirungu ino scrims vashandi vayo; asi kwete kudaro whaler yeChirungu. Hino, muChindau, chiro ichi che whaling good cheer achiri chiro chakajairika uye chechisikigo, asi chinoitika ngekungoerekana; uye, nge ndaa ye izvi, inofanira kuva nge mavambo akakosha, ayo anokombidzwa pano, uye achazojekeswazve.

Munguwa yekutsvakurudza kwangu munhoroondo dzeLeviathan, ndakagumburwa ngebhuku rekare rechiDutch, iro, ngekunhuhwirira kwaro kwemusty whaling, ndaiziya kuti rinofanira kuva re whalers. Musoro wendaa wainge, “Dan Coopman,” ngendaa iyoyo ndakapedzisa kuti izvi zvinofanira kuva zviyeuchidzo zvisingakoshi zveumweni muAmsterdam cooper mubasa rekubata howe, sezvo ngarava imwe neimwe ye whale inofanira kutwara cooper yayo. Ndakasimbiswa mumufungo uyu nekuona kuti kwaiva kugadzirwa kwe “Fitz Swackhammer” imwe. Asi shamwari yangu Dr. Snodhead, mundhu wakafunda maningi, mujijisi wo Low Dutch no High German ku koreji yo Santa Claus no St. “Mutengesi.” Mukupfupikisa, bhuku iri rekare uye rakafunda Low Dutch rakabhuya ngezvekutengeserana kweHolland; uye, pakati pedzimwe nyaya, yaive nenhoroondo inofadza zvikuru yekubata hove dzayo dze whale. Uye mu chikamu ichi chaiva, chine musoro unoti, “Smeer,” kana kuti “Mafuta,” apo ndakawana rondedzero yakareba yakadzara yezvipfeko zvezvitoro nezvitoro zve 180 zvikepe zveDutch whalemen; kubva pane iyo rondedzero, sezvakashandurwa na Dr. Snodhead, ndinonyora zvinotevera:

400,000 mapaundi. yenyama yemombe. 60,000 mapaundi. Nyama yenguruwe yeFriesland. 150,000 mapaundi. ye hove ye stock. 550,000 mapaundi. ye bhisikiti. 72,000 mapaundi. ye chingwa chakapfava. 2,800 mafirkins e butter. 20,000 mapaundi. Texel uye Leyden chizi. 144,000 mapaundi. chizi (zvichida chinyorwa chakaderera). 550 zvikepe zveGenevha. 10,800 midziyo yedoro.

Matafura akawanda ezviverengo akaoma parchingly mukuverenga; asipi kudaro mu ndaa iripo, asi, apo muerengi wakadzara nge mapaipi akazara, midziyo, ma quarts, nema gills e gin yakanaka ne cheer yakanaka.

Nge nguwayo, ndakapedza mazuwa matatu kudzidza kugaya doro iri reshe, nyama ye mombe, ne chingwa, panguwa iyoyo mifungo yakawanda yakadzama yakakurudzirwa nge njira yechiitiko kwandiri, inokwanisa kushandiswa kwe transcendental uye ye Plato; uye,zve, ndakaunganidza matafura ekuwedzera angu, ndichibata huwandu hunokona kunge hwehove-yekuchengeta, nezvimwewo, zvakadyiwa ngemunhu weshe weLow Dutch harpooneer mune iyo yekare Greenland uye Spitzbergen whale hove. Pakutanga, kuwanda kwe butter, ne Texel ne Leyden cheese zvinodyiwa, zvinoita inga zvinoshamisa. Ndinozvipomera, asi, kuzviro zvavo zvisina kunaka, vechiitwa kuti vawedzere kuita zviro zvisina kunaka nge ndaa yekudaidzwa kwavo, uye kunyanya nge kuteerera kwavo mutambo wavo mumakungwa e Polar anotonhora, pamhenderekedzo ye nyika iyoyo ye Esquimaux apo vagari vanodakarirana vanogondisa umwe neumwe mumabhampa e chitima oil.

Kuwanda kwedoro, zvakare, kwakakura zvikuru, 10,800 midziyo. Zvino, sezvo hove idzodzo dze polar dzaikona kusungwa kwega muchirimo chipfupi che mamiriro ekunze iwayo, kuti rwendo rwese rwe umwe wevaDutch whalemen ava, kusanganisira rwendo rupfupi kuenda nekudzoka mugungwa re Spitzbergen, rwakanga rusinganyanyi kudarika mwedzi mitatu, tingabhuya, nekuverenga Lo 30 varume kune imwe neimwe yezvikepe zvavo zve 180 Dutch sail in,40; saka, ndinoti, tine chaizvoizvo midziyo miviri yedoro pamunhu, kwemavhiki gumi nemaviri 'allowance, isingasanganisi chikamu chake chakafanira cheiyo 550 ankers ye gin. Zvino, kana aya ma gin ne doro harpooneers, akafukidzwa zvakanyanya sezvingafungwa ngemunhu kuti aive, aive mhando yakarurama yevarume kuti vamire musoro wechikepe, uye vatore chinangwa chakanaka che whales dzinobhururuka; izvi zvaizoita sezvisingaitike. Asi vakavananga, vakavarovawo. Asi izvi zvainge zviri kuretu maningi ku North, ngazvikarakadzwe, apo doro rinopindirana zvakanaka nge bumbiro remutemo; pamusoro peEquator, muhove yedu yekumaodzanyemba, doro raizokwanisa kuita kuti harpooneer aate pa mast-head uye boozy muchikepe chake; uye kurasikirwa kunorwadza kunogona kuitika kuNantucket neNew Bedford.

Asi hapanazve; zvakakwana zvakareketwa kukhombisa kuti vakuru vekare veDutch whalers vemazana maviri kana matatu emakore apfuura vaive chiropa chakakwirira; uye kuti vachengeti vehove veChirungu havana kuregeredza muenzaniso wakanakisa kudaro. Ngokuti, vanoti, kana uchihamba muchikepe chisina chiro, kana usingakoni kuwana chiro chiri nani kubve munyika, wana kurya kwemadekoni kwakanaka kubva mweiri, zvishoma. Uye izvi zvinobudisa decanter.





CHITSAUKO 102. Mumwe Bower muArsacides.

Kuguma zvino, mukutsanangura kurapa Sperm Whale, ndakagara maningi pazvishamiso zve rutivi rwake rwe kubanze; kana zvakasiyana uye zvakadzama pamusoro pezvimwe zvishoma zvemukati zvakagadzirwa. Asi kuzwisisa kwakakura uye kwakadzama kwekutsvaira kwake, zvinofanira kuti ndimusunungure zvino, uye kusunungura mapointi e hose yake, kusunungura ma garters ake, uye kukanda zvikorekedzo nemaziso emajoini emapfupa ake emukati, kumuisa pamberi penyu mu ultimatum yake; kureva kuti, mupfupa rake risina chikonzero.

Asi zvino zvinodini, Ishmaeri? Zvinoitika kudini, kuti iwewe, mundhu anongoita basa ro kukwasva howe, unoita inga unoziva ciro ngo pamusoro po ndau jo pashi po nyika jo hove huru? Stubb, wakakwira pamusoro pe capstan yako, wakapa hurukuro pamusoro pekugadzirwa kwemuviri weCetacea here; uye ngekudetsera kwe windlass, kubata mbabvu yemuenzaniso kuti ionekwe? Zvitsanangure iwewe, Ishmaeri. Unokona kuisa howe yakakura yakadzara pachikepe chako kuti iongororwe, sezvinoita mubiki anobika nguruve yakabikwa ere? Ngo gwinyiso haiwa. Iwewe wainga uri mufakazi wemene kuguma zvino, Ishmaeri; asi funga kuti unobata sei chikomborero chaJona ega; ropafadzo yekukurukura pamusoro pe joists uye matanda; mapuranga, ridge-pole, vanoata, uye pasi-pinnings, zvinoita furemu-basa rereviathan; uye belike yemafuta-mafuta, makamuri emukaka, mafuta, uye cheeseries mumatumbu ake.

Ndinoreurura, kuti kubvira kuna Jona, vashomani ve whalemen vakapinda kuretu maningi pashi peganda re whale yakakura; zvisinei, ndakakomborerwa nemukana wekumucheka mudiki. Mu ngarava yandainge ndiri, mwana mudoko we Sperm Whale wakambosimudzwa nge muiri ku deck kuti aite poke yake kana bhegi, kuti aite sheaths dze barbs dze harpoons, ne misoro ye lances. Munofunga kuti ndakasiya mukana iwoyo kuenda, ndisingashandise chikepe-hatchet yangu ne jack-knife yangu, nekutsemura chisimbiso nekuerenga zveshe zviri mukati mwemwana mudoko uyo?

Uye nge ndaa ye ruzivo rwangu rwemene rwe mapfupa e leviathan mukukura kwawo kukuru, kwakadzara, nge ndaa ye ruzivo irworwo rusingawanzoitika ndine chikwereti kune shamwari yangu yeumambo yakafa Tranquo, mambo we Tranque, umwe we Arsacides. Kudai ngekuti ndainge ndiri ku Tranque, makore apfuura, apo ndakabatana ne ngarava yekutengesa Dey ye Algiers, ndakakokwa kuti ndipedze chikamu che mazororo e Arsacidean na ishe we Tranque, pa villa yake ye michindwe yakasiya mushando ku Pupella; glen iri parutivi pegungwa isisiri kuretu nezvaidaidzwa ngevafambisi vedu vezvikepe kuti Bamboo-Town, guta guru rake.

Pakati pezviro zvimweni zvakawanda zvakanaka, shamwari yangu yeumambo Tranquo, ngekuti wakapuwa rudo rwakadzipira ngendaa dzeshe dze barbaric vertu, wakanga aunza pamwepo muPupella zviro zvipi nezvipi zvisingawanzoitika zvaizogadzirwa ngevanhu vake; zvikurukuru huni dzakavezwa dzemidziyo inoshamisa, chiselled shells, inlaid mapfumo, zvikepe zvinodhura, zvikepe zvinonhuhwirira; uye zveshe izvi zvakagoverwa pakati pezvishamiso zvese zvechisikigo, masaisai akatakura zvishamiso, anobhadhara mutero akanga akakanda pamhenderekedzo dzake.

Mukuru pakati pezviro izvi zvokupeisira wainga Sperm Whale huru, iyo, sure kwe dutu rakareba risingajairiki, yakanga yawanikwa yakafa uye yakasungirirwa, nge soro rayo rakabatana no muti wa cocoa-nut, uwo wakaita inga minhenga, wakadonha wakaita inga jet yake yakasvibira. Muiri wakakura pawakapeisira kubviswa zvipfeko zvawo zvakadzama, uye mapfupa akaoma guruva muzuwa, ipapo pfupa rakaendeswa ngekungwarira kukwira muPupella glen, apo tembere huru yemichindwe yemadzishe yakaidzivirira zvino.

Mbabvu dzakasungirirwa ngemikombe; musana wakavezwa ngezvinyorwa zveArsacidean, mumazwi anoshamisa; mu hloko, vapristi vaichengeta mwoto unonhuhwirira usikazi kudzima, zvekuti musoro we mystic wakatumirazve muromo wawo we mhepo; asi, yakasungirirwa pa davi, tsaya yepashi inotyisa yakadengenyeka pamusoro pevakazvipira veshe, kudai nge munondo wakasungirirwa ngevhudzi wakathya Damocles maningi.

Kwaiye chiro chinodakadza maningi kuona. Huni dzainge dzakasvibira se mosses ye Icy Glen; miti yakaema yakakwirira uye ichizvikudza, ichinzwa muto wayo unorarama; nyika inoshanda ngo simba pashi yainga inga n’anga yo kuruka, yainga no kapeti yakaseja maningi, iyo nzungu jo muzambiringa jo pashi jainga jeciita n’anga no n’anga, zve maruva anorarama aiita inga mifananidzo. Miti yeshe, nematavi ayo eshe akaremerwa; zvirimwa zveshe, nemaferni, nemashizha; mhepo inotakura mashoko; zveshe izvi zvaishanda zvisingaperi. Kubudikidza nge tambo dze mashizha, zuwa guru raiita inga chikepe chinobhururuka chinoruka maruva asizi kuremba. Oo, muruki wakabatikana! muruki asingaoneki!—mboema!—izwi rimwe!—jira rinoyerera kupi? muzi weumambo upi unokwanisa kudeck? ngenyi mushando weshe uwu usikaperi? Reketa, muruki!—gara nyara yako!—asi izwi rimwe newe! Haiwa—shuttle inobhururuka—mifananidzo inoyangarara kubva mberi kwe loom; iyo freshet-inokurumidza kapeti nokusingaperi inotsvedza kure. Mwari-muruki, anoruka; uye nekuruka ikoko akaita chimumumu, kuti asazwa izwi rinofa; uye ngekuungudza ikoko, isuwo, tinotarisa pamusoro pekuruka tinoita matsi; uye chete patinopukunyuka ndiyo tichazwa mazwi ane chiuru anotaura kuburikidza nayo. Nekuti kunyange zvakadaro mumafekitari ese ezvinhu. Mashoko akareketwa asingazwiki pakati pezvipfeko zvinobhururuka; mazwi aya anozwika zvakajeka pasina madziro, achiputika kubva kuma casements akavhurwa. Ngokudaro, zviro zvakashata zvakavonekwa. Aa, munhu anofa! zvino, ungwarire; ngokuti kudaro, mu mhere-mhere yeshe iyi ye nyika huru, mifungo yako yakafishika inokona kuzwika kuretu.

Ngokudaro, pakati po nzungu yo ruva, isingazorori yo upenyu yo muti wo Arsacidean, nzungu huru, yakacena, inonamatwa yainga yakaata yecizorora—ciro cikuru maningi cicikaiti ciro! Asi, apo nzungu no nzungu zvaigara zvakarukwa zvaisanganiswa no kuvhunduka zvakamutendeneja, mundhu wakasimba waiita inga muruki wakangwara; iye pachake zvose zvakarukwa pamusoro nemizambiringa; mwedzi wega wega uchifunga kuti maruva akasvibira, akachena; asi iye pachake pfupa. Hupenyu hwakapeta Rufu; Rufu rwakadzivirira Upenyu; mwari wakashata akaroorana ne Upenyu hwechidiki, uye akamuberekera kubwinya-soro rakamonana.

Zvino, apo ne royal Tranquo ndakavhakachira whale iyi inoshamisa, ndikaona musoro aritari, uye utsi hwekunasirwa huchikwira kubva apo jet chaiyo yakabuda, ndakashamiswa kuti mambo anofanira kuona chapel sechiro che vertu. Wakasheka. Asi zvakanyanya ndakashamiswa kuti vafundisi vanofanira kupika kuti smoky jet yake yaive yechokwadi. Kuenda ngekuenda ndakahamba pamberi pepfupa iri—rakabvisa mizambiringa parutii—rakatsemura mbabvu—ne bhora re Arsacidean twine, rakadzungaira, rakatenderera nguva yakareba pakati pema colonnades aro akawanda anotenderera, ane mumvuri uye arbours. Asi pasina nguva mutsara wangu wakange wabuda; uye ndichiitevera sure, ndakabuda kubva pakuvhura kwandakapinda. Andizi kuona chiro chinopona mukati; hapana chimwe chaivepo asi mapfupa.

Kundicheka tsvimbo yekuyera ye green, ndakadzika zvakare mukati memapfupa. Kubva pa museve wavo wakavhurika musoro, vapiri vakandivona ndecitora urefu hwo mbabvu yo kupejisira, “Kudini wari!” vakadaidzira kuti; "Dar'st iwe unoyera uyu mwari wedu! Ndizvo zvedu." “Eya, vapristi—zvakanaka, munomuita kuguma rini, hino?” Asi nge ndaa iyi kwakamuka hondo inotyisa pakati pavo, nge ndaa ye makumbo nge masendimita; vakatsemura sconces dzeumweni neumweni nge yard-sticks dzavo—musoro mukuru wakadzokorora—uye kutora mukana iwoyo werombo rakanaka, ndakapedza ngekukasira admeasurements yangu.

Aya admeasurements ini zvino ndinokurudzira kuisa pamberi penyu. Asi kutanga, ngazvinyorwe, kuti, mundaa iyi, andizi kusununguka kureketa chero chiyero chekufungidzira chandinoda. Asi kuchiripo kumwe kubvunzurudza; imwe inowanzovhiringidzwa nevaNantucketers vanonyanya kuzvikudza. Kuti nge ndaa ye kutarisira kunoda kuziya zveshe pa mast-heads e whale-ships, zvino anopinda kunyazwi kuburikidza nge Behring’s straits, ne kuende muzvidhirowa ne zvivharo zvakavanzika zviri kuretu zve nyika; uye chiuru chemapanga nemapfumo akarumbire kumahombekombe ese enyika; pfungwa isina kujeka ndeyekuti, kana Revhiatani inokwanisa kushivirira kwenguva yakareba kudaro kudzingirirwa kwakakura kudaro, uye kuparadza kusina kuzvidemba; kana kuti aafaniri kupeisira kuparadzwa mumvura, uye whale yekupeisira, kudai ngemunhu wekupeisira, inoputa pombi yake yekupeisira, uye iye ega anozoputika mukufema kwekupeisira.

Kuenzanisa mapoka ane mavhunduka e whales nemakwai ane mavhunduka e buffalo, ayo, asipi makore makumi mana apfuura, akapararira nge makumi ezviuru emasango e Illinois ne Missouri, uye akazunungudza mavhudzi avo esimbi uye akatsveruka nge mavhudzi avo akavharwa nge mabanan'ana pamusoro pe ndau dzine vanhu vakawanda no rwizi, guta guru- guta guru. inch; mukuenzanisa kwakadaro nharo isingadzivisiki ingaita inga yakapiwa, kukhombise kuti whale yakavhimwa haikwanisi zvino kutiza kupera ngekukasira.

Asi munofanira kuona nyaya iyi muchiedza chese. Kunyazi nguwa pfupi yakapfuura—asipi nguwa yakanaka yeupenyu—kuverengwa kwe mbudzi mu Illinois kwakadarika kuverengwa kwe anhu anogara mu London zvino, uye kunyazi mazuwa ano akuna nyanga imwe kana nhanho imwe yavo yakasara mundau iyoyo yeshe; uye kunyange chikonzero chekuparadza uku kunoshamisa chaiva pfumo remunhu; asi kusiyana kwazvo kwe whale-hunt kunorambidza zvikuru kupera kusina mbiri kwaRevhiathan. Varume makumi mana vari muchikepe chimwe vechivhima Sperm Whales kwemwedzi makumi mana nesere vanofunga kuti vakaita zvakanaka yaamho, uye vanobonga Mwari, kana pakupedzisira vakatwara kumba mafuta ehowe makumi mana. Kudai ngekuti, mumazuwa evavhimi vekare veCanada neIndia nevabati vekuMadokero, apo kumadokero kwakanyanya (mukuti mazuwa ekuvira achiri kubuda) yainge iri renje uye mhandara, nhamba imweyo yevarume vakapfeka moccasined, kwemwedzi yakafanana, vakatasva bhiza pane kuurawa kwete buffalo for thousalo for more, vaizova; chokwadi chekuti, kana zvichidikanwa, zvaigona kutaurwa nezviverengo.

Uye, zvakafungwa zvakanaka, zvinoita inga pane nharo inotsigira kupera zvishoma nezvishoma kweSperm Whale, semuenzaniso, kuti mumakore apfuura (chikamu chekupedzisira chezana remakore rapfuura, tingabhuya) aya maLeviathan, mumapods madoko, akasangana kazhinji kazhinji kupfuura iye zvino, uye, semugumisiro, rwendo uye voyagetive dzaive dzisiriwo zvakanyanya remu. Ngokuti, inga zvakavoneka kumweni, howe jondhojo, jakakwevejehwa ngo mavonero amweni o kungwarira, wari jinodhuvha mu makungwa mu ma caravans makuru, zvokuti kuguma pa ndau yakakura maningi ma solitaries akapararira, joko, no ma pods, no zvikora zvo mazuwa amweni wari zvinoungana kuva armi yakakura asi yakaparadzana maningi, unfreque. Ndizvona zvega. Uye zvakafanana fallacious zvinoita inga conceit, kuti ngekuti zvinozwi whale-bone whales hazvichatambudzi ndau dzakawanda mumakore akapfuura akawanda nazvo, ndizvo mhando iyoyowo iri kudzikira. Ngokuti vari kungodzingwa kubva ku promontory kuenda ku cape; uye kana imwe nzvimbo yekumahombekombe isisiri kurarama nge jets dzavo, saka, iva nechokwadi, imwe uye kure nharaunda yakavhunduswa nguva pfupi yapfuura ngechiitiko chisingazivikanwi.

Padera peizvozvo: ngo pamusoro po maRevhiyatani aya akapejisira kureketwa, ano nhare mbiri jakagwinya, ijo, mukuvona kwese hwo vandhu, jinozogara jicikakwanisiki kukundwa kwenda no kwenda. Uye kudai ngekupindira kwemipata yavo, vaSwitzerland vane chando vakadzokera kumakomo avo; saka, kuvhimwa kubva kuma savannas nema glades emakungwa epakati, whale-bone whales inokona pakupedzisira kuende ku Polar citadels dzavo, uye kudzika pashi pezvipingamupinyi zvekupedzisira zvegirazi nemadziro apo, kuuya pakati peminda yechando uye floes; uye mudenderedzwa rakashongedzwa raDecember risingaperi, bid kushora kune zvese zvinoteverwa kubva kumunhu.

Asi sezvo zvingangoita makumi mashanu e whale-bone whales aya anosungirirwa nge harpoon kuti aite cachalot imwe, vamweni vazivi ve forecastle vakapedzisa kuti kuparadza uku kwakanaka kwakatoderedza zvikuru ma battalions avo. Asi kunyazi kwe nguwa yakapfuura nhamba ye howe idzi, dzisingapfuuri 13,000, dzakaurawa gore nge gore ku nor' west coast nge vaAmerica vega; asi kune zvifungo zvinoita kuti kunyange mamiriro aya ave mashoma kana asina nhoroondo sekupokana kunopesana munhau iyi.

Kunyazwi zvakasikwa kuti tiite zvimweni zvisingatendi ngendaa yekuwanda kwevanhu vezvipuka zvikuru zvikuru zvenyika, asi tinozoti kudini kuna Harto, munyori wenhoroondo weGoa, paanotibhuyira kuti pakuvhima kumwe Mambo weSiam wakatora nzou 4,000; kuti munharaunda idzodzo nzou dzakawanda semapoka emombe munzvimbo dzinodziya. Uye zvinoita inga pasina chikonzero chekupokana kuti kana nzou idzi, idzo dzave kuvhimwa zvino kwezviuru zvemakore, naSemiramis, naPorus, naHannibal, uye ngemadzimambo ese akateverana eKumabvazuva—kana vachiri kuraramapo ngehuwandu hwakakura, zvikurukuru kuti whale huru inogara kupfuura kuvhima kweshe, sezvo ane mafuro akawanda sezvo lar muAsia, America, Europe ne Africa, New Holland, ne Zvitsuwa zveshe zve gungwa zvakasanganiswa.

CHITSAUKO 106. Murefu waAhabhi.

Njira yekukurumidza iyo Captain Ahab akasiya Samuel Enderby weLondon, aizi kusingatarisirwi ngekuita mhirizhonga shomani kumunthu wake. Iyena wainga wavheneka ngo simba maningi pa kuparadzwa ko cikepe cake zve murenje wake wo nyanga yonzou wakavhunduka ngo kuvhunduka. Uye apo mushure mokuwana chikepe chake, uye pivot-hole yake apo, wakatenderera ngekukurumidza ngekurayira kwekukurumidza kune steersman (kwaive, semazuva ese, chimwe chiro pamusoro pekusatungamira kwake inflexibly zvakakwana); zvino, nyanga yenzou yakatozungunuswa yakaashira kuvhiringika kwakawedzerwa, zvekuti kunyazi yakaramba yakakwana, uye kuzviro zveshe zvaiita inga inodakajisa, asi Ahabhi aazi kuivona inga yakagondeka kamare.

Uye, zvemene, zvaiita inga zviro zvidoko zvo kushamisa, kuti ngo kupararira kwake kwese, kupenga kwake, Ahabhi nguva jimweni waingwarira maningi mugariro wo pfupa rondhoro raainga akaema paari. Ngokuti paiya pasizi kutora nguva yakareba maningi Pequod asati aenda ngechikepe kubva Nantucket, kuti akawanikwa usiku humweni akaata pashi, asizi kuzwa; nge zvimweni zvisikazikanwi, uye zvinoita inga zvisingatsananguriki, zvisingafungidziki kukuvara, mudziyo wake wenyanga yenzou wakabviswa zvine simba, zvekuti wakarohwa nge stake-wise, uye zveshe asi zvakabaya ganda rake; uyewo hazvina kuoma zvakanyanya kuti ronda rairwadza rapora zvachose.

Kana, panguwayo, zvainga zvisizi kukorera kupinda mu ngqondo yake ye monomaniac, kuti kushungurudzika kweshe kwekutambudzika ikoko kwaivapo kwaiva asi ndaa yakananga ye dambudziko rekare; uye iyewo wakaita inga wakaona zvakajeka, kuti kudai nge chipuka chinokambaira chine poison zvikuru che mudhaka chinoenderera mberi nge rudzi rwacho zvisingadzivisiki kudai nge muimbi unotapira maningi we musango; saka, zvakaenzana ne felicity imwe neimwe, zviitiko zvese zvinosuruvarisa zvinobereka zvakasikwa zvavo. Eya, kupfuura zvakaenzana, akafunga Ahabhi; sezvo zveshe madzinza nevana vaKusuwa zvinoenda mberi kudarika madzinza nevana vaMufaro. Ngokuti, haiwa kuronzera izvi: kuti i inference kubva kune dzimweni dzidziso dze canonical, kuti kunyazwi kudakara kumweni kwechisikigo pano akuzovi nge vana vanobarwa ndivo nge ndaa ye nyika imweni, asi, zvakasiyana, zvinozoteverwa nge kudakara-kutama vana kwe kupererwa nge gonda kweshe kwe gehena; asi, dzimweni matambudziko anofa ane mhosva acharamba achizviberekera vana vasingaperi vanoenderera mberi vekusuwa kupfuura guva; kwete zvachose kuronzera izvi, kuchiri kuoneka kusaenzana mukuongorora kwakadzama kwechinhu. Ngokuti, wakarangarira Ahabhi, kunyazwi kudakara kwakanyanya pashi pano kwakambova no zviro zvidoko zvicikavoneki zvakafishika mukati mwazvo, asi, pashi, matambudziko eshe o mwoyo, kukosha kwakavanzika, zve, mu vandhu vamweni, ukuru hwo ngirosi huru; saka vanoshingaira tracings-kunze kwete belie pachena kuderedzwa. Kuteera madzinza emadambudziko aya akakwirira anofa, anotitwara pakupedzisira pakati pemadzinza asina mhedziso evamwari; kuti, pamberi pe mazuwa eshe anodakadza, anoita uswa, ne kurira kwakapfava, mwedzi yekukohwa yakatenderera, tinofanira kuzvipa kune izvi: kuti vamwari vega avadakariri mhetsa nariini. Chiratidzo chekubarwa chisingadzimiki, chinosuruvarisa muhuma yemunhu, asi chidhindo chekusuwa mune vanosayina.

Asikazi kuziya pano chakafishika chakabudiswa, icho chinokona kunyatsoita zvakanaka, ngenjira yakasarudzwa, chakabudiswa kare. Ne zvimweni zviro zvakawanda zvine chekuita na Ahabhi, nguva dzeshe zvainge zvakaramba zviri chiro chisingaziikanwi kune vamweni, nge ndaa yei zvakadaro, kuti kwe nguwa yakati, zveshe zvisati zvaenda ne sure kwe kuhamba kwe Pequod, wakadzifisha kuretu nge kuzvipira kwakadaro kwakafanana ne Grand-Lama; uye, kwenguva imweyo, akatsvaka utiziro husina kutaura, sekuti, pakati pe senate yemarble yevakafa. Chikonzero chaKaputeni Peleg chakakuvadzwa chechiro ichi chakaoneka chisina kukwana; asi, zvemene, inga zvakabata ndau yese yakadzama ya Ahabhi, kuzarurirwa kweshe kwaitora zvakawanda zve rima rinokosha pane chiedza chinopashangura. Asi, pakupedzisira, zveshe zvakabuda; nyaya iyi imwe yakaita, zvishoma. Ngozi iyoyo yakashata maningi yainga iri pashi po kugara kwake ega ko nguva doko. Uye haiwa izvi zvega, asi kune iyo inogara-ichibvumirana, inodonhedza denderedzwa kusina mvura, uyo, ngechikonzero chero chipi, aive nechikomborero chekusvika pasina kurambidzwa kwaari; ku denderedzwa iroro rinothya kukuvara kwakarongwa padera apapo—kwakasara, inga zvakaita, kucikazwisiswi ndi Ahabhi—kwakajiisa mu zviro zvinothya, zvicikazivi kuceneswa kamare kubvira mu nyika yo mweya no yo kucema. Ngokudaro, nge kushingaira kwavo kwaari, veshe vakanga vakaronga, kuguma pavari, kuti vafishe kuziya kwe chiro ichi kubva kune vamweni; uye ndizvo zvakaitika, kuti kwete kusvikira nguva yakareba yapfuura, zvakaitika pamusoro pemadhizaini ePequod.

Asi ngazviite zveshe izvi kudai ngezvingaite; ngazvioneke, zvisizi kujeka sinodhi mumhepo, kana machinda anotsiva uye vane simba remwoto, vane chekuita kana kusaita naAhabhi wepasi pano, asi, munhau iyi yemazuwa ano yemurenje wake, wakatora maitiro akajeka anoshanda;—akadaidza muvezi wemapuranga.

Uye apo mushandi uyo akaoneka pamberi pake, wakamuraira asizi kunonoka kuti aite murenje mutsva, uye wakaraira vamwe vake kuti vamuone akapiwa zviphenduko zveshe uye zviphenduriso zveshaya-ivory (Sperm Whale) zvainge zvaunganidzwa kusvika parizvino parwendo, kuti kusarudzwa kwakanaka kwe stouteine, clearest segra kuve kwakajeka. Izvi zvakaitwa, muvezi wakaashira mutemo wokuti murembe upere usiku wondhowo; uye kupa zviro zveshe zvinodiwa ndizvo, zvisizi kusiyana nezvinoenderana nezvisingagondwa zviri kushandiswa. Uyezve, ndau yekugadzirwa ngarava yakarairwa kuti isimudzwe kubva pakushanda kwayo kwenguva pfupi muchitoro; uye, kuti nyaya ikurumidze, mupfuri wesimbi wakarairwa kuti aenderere mberi pakarepo nekuumba chero zviro zvesimbi zvaizodiwa.
