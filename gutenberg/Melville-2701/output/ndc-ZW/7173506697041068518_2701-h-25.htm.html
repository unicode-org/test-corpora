Kubva pa uta hwo ngarava, phedo no vandhu vese vaifamba mu ngarava wari vakarembera vacikashandi; nyundo, zvimedu zve mapuranga, mapfumo, ne zvibhamu, zvakachengetedzwa nge njira ye michina munyara dzavo, kudai nge zvavainge vakarumba kubva kumishando yavo yakasiyana-siyana; madziso avo eshe akarongwa akatarisa hove huru, iyo kubva kurutivi kuenda kurutivi ichidengenyeka zvinoshamisa soro rake rakagara rakasarudzwa, yakatuma bhande rakafara remapundu akapararira semicircular pamberi pake paairumbire. Kutsiva, kutsiva ngekukasira, kushata kusingaperi kwaiva muchikamu chake cheshe, uye pasinei nezveshe zvaikona kuitwa ngemunhu unofa, chitsigiro chakasimba chakachena chehuma yake chakarova uta hwekurudyi rwengarava, kuguma anhu nematanda akazunguzika. Vamweni vakawa ngehope dzavo. Kufanana nemarori akasunungurwa, misoro yevarume vehondo vainge vari mudenga yaizunguzika pamitsipa yavo yakaita semombe. Kubudikidza ngo ndau yakavhurika, vakazwa mvura yecidurukira, inga makomo anodurukira mu rwizi.

“Ngarava! Muchovha wevakafa!—muchovha wechipiri wekufa!” akashevedzera Ahabhi ari muchikepe; “huni dzayo dzaigona kunge dziri dzeAmerica chete!”

Kudzika pashi pe ngarava yaigara, howe huru yakarumba yeitetemera nge keel yayo; asi wakatendeuka pashi pe mvura, wakakasira kupfurazve pashi, kuretu no uta humweni, asi painga pakaguma pa mamita mashomanana o cikepe ca Ahabhi, apo, ko nguva yakati, wakaata akanyarara.

"Ndinotendeudza muiri wangu kubva kuzuwa. Chiinyi ho, Tashtego! ngandizwe nyundo yako. Oh! imwimwi mitatu isizi kuzvipira yangu; iwewe chikepe chisina kutsemuka; ne chikepe chega chakashungurudzwa ndi mwari; iwewe chikepe chakagwinya, ne chikepe chinozvikudza, ne chikepe chinopinza nge nhanho, - rufu-kubwinya kwe ngarava kubva kwandiri?, uye ndinofanira kuparadzwa nge ngarava ye rufu! fond pride of meanest shipwreck captains? O, zvino ndinozwa kukura kwangu kwepamusoro-soro kuri mukusuwa kwangu kwepamusoro-soro. zvose-zvinoparadza asi zvisingakundi whale; kusvika pakupedzisira ndinorwa newe; kubva mumwoyo wegehena ndinobaya iwe; whale! Ngokudaro , ndinosiya pfumo!”

Harpoon yakapondwa; whale yakarohwa yakabhururuka kuenda mberi; nekupisa velocity mutsara wakamhanya kuburikidza nemakomba;—akamhanya zvakasviba. Ahabhi wakakotama kuti aichenese; akazvichenesa; asi kutendeuka kunobhururuka kwakamubata mumutsipa, uye asina izwi sezvo matsi eTurkey achipfura munhu wavo, akapfurwa kunze kwechikepe, ere vashandi vasati vaziya kuti aenda. Pa mukuvo wakateera, n’anga inorema yo madziso pa mugumo wo kupejisira wo tambo yakabhururuka kubve mu tub yainga isina ciro, yochorova mundhu waikwasva, yochorova gungwa, yonyangarika pa kudzaka kwayo.

Kwechinguvana, vashandi vechikepe chakange chakavhiringika vakaema; ndokuzotendeuka. "Ngarava? Mwari mukuru, ngarava iripi?" Ngekukasira, kuburikidza ngezviro zvisina kujeka, zvinovhiringidza vakaona phantom yake iri kupera, sezvakaitika muFata Morgana; chete masts epamusoro-soro kubva mumvura; apo vakagadziriswa ngekuda, kana kuvimbika, kana kuguma, kuzvigaro zvavo zvaimbova zvakakwirira, vahedheni vanoita zvehondo vairamba veitarisa gungwa rainyura. Uye zvino, zvitenderedzwa zvakabatana zvakabata chikepe chimwe chega, nevashandi vacho veshe, nechikepe chimwe nechimwe chinoyangarara, ne lance-pole imwe neimwe, nekutenderera, zvipenyu nezvisina upenyu, zveshe zvakatenderera nekutenderera mu vortex imwe, zvakatakura chip chidoko yaamho che Pequod kuti chisaoneka.

Asi apo whelmings dzekupeisira dzakasanganiswa dzakazvidurura pamusoro pesoro rakadzika reIndia pa mainmast, dzichisiya masendimita mashomani e spar yakaema asi ichioneka, pamwe chete nemayadhi marefu anoyerera emureza, ayo akadzikama, nekubatana kunosekesa, pamusoro pe alchest a— that billows inoparadza; nyundo yakasimudzwa kumashure yakasimudzwa mumhepo yakasununguka, muchiito chekuisa mureza nekukurumidza uye asi nekukurumidza kune spar inodzikira. Sky-hawk iyo ngekuseka yakateera main-truck kuenda pashi kubva kumhatso yayo yekuzvarwa pakati penyeredzi, ichipenya mureza, uye ichiita kuti Tashtego adzikame apo; shiri iyi zvino yakawana mukana wekuvhara bapiro rayo rakafara rinobhururuka pakati penyundo nehuni; uye nguva imweyo eizwa kuti etherial thrill, savage yakanyudzwa pashi, mukufa kwake-kufema, yakachengeta nyundo yake yakaoma apo; uye nge ndaa ye shiri ye denga, nge kushevedzera kwe ngirosi huru, ne muromo wake we umambo wakasundirwa mudenga, ne chimiro chake cheshe che nhapwa chakapetwa mu mureza wa Ahabhi, wakadzaka nge ngarava yake, iyo, kudai ndi Sathana, aisazonyura ku gehena kuguma apo akakweva chikamu chipenyu che denga pamwepo naye, ne helme with helme.

Zvino shiri duku dzakabhururuka dzichishevedzera pamusoro pegomba richiri kuzeya; sullen chena surf inorova pamusoro pemativi ayo akakwirira; zvino zveshe zvakawa, nechifukidzo chikuru chegungwa chakaenderera mberi sezvakazungunuka makore zviuru zvishanu apfuura.
