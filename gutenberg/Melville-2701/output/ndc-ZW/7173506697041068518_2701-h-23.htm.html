Kudai nge nyeredzi ye polar isingagari, iyo kuburikidza ne livelong, arctic, mwedzi mitanhatu yeusiku inochengetedza kubaya kwayo, yakagadzikana, kutarisa kwepakati; saka chinangwa chaAhabhi zvino chakajeka pamusoro pepakati peusiku nguva dzose yevashandi vakasuruvara. Yakatonga padera pavo kudaro, kuti bodings dzavo dzeshe, kusahadzika, kusava nechokwadi, kutya, zvaida kufishika pashi pemweya yavo, nekusabuda pfumo kana shakani rimwe.

Mu nguva iyi yo kufanovonavo, kushekeja kwese, kwakamanikijwa kana kwakasikwa, kwakapera. Stubb haazi kuzoedzazve kusimudza kushekerera; Starbuck haana kuzoedzazve kuongorora imwe. Kufanana, kudakara no kusuruvara, gonda no kuthya, zvaiita inga zvakagurwa kuita guruva rakanaka maningi, zve zvakagurwa, ngo nguvayo, mu dhaka rakasungirirwa ro mweya wo simbi wa Ahabhi. Kufanana nemichina, vaihamba-hamba vasingabhuyi pachikepe, vechigara veiziva kuti ziso remurume mukuru raivaona.

Asi wakamuongorora zvakadzama ere mumaawa ake akavanzika akavanzika; apo akafunga kuti hapana kutarisa asi imwe yaive paari; zvino waizoona kuti kunyazi madziso aAhabhi aishamisa maningi madziso evashandi, kusota kwaParsee kusingazivikanwi kwakashamisa kwake; kana kuti nenzira imwe, zvishoma, nenzira imwe yesango, dzimwe nguva yakazvikanganisa. Zvakadaro zvakawedzera, gliding zvinoshamisa zvakatanga kuisa Fedallah yakaonda zvino; kudedera kwakadaro kusingaperi kwakamuzunungutsa; kuti varume vakamutarisa zvine chokwadi; hafu isina chokwadi, sezvaiita, kana zvemene aive chiro chinofa, kana kuti mumvuri unodedera wakakandwa pamusoro pedhizaini ngemuviri wechimwe chisikwa chisingaoneki. Uye mumvuri iwoyo waigara uchitendererapo. Ngokuti haiwa usiku, kunyazwi, Fedallah wakamboziikanwa ngegwinyiso kuti anoata, kana kuenda pashi. Aizoema akaema kwemaawa: asi haazi kumbogara kana kusendamira; maziso ake asina simba asi anoshamisa akataura zvakajeka kuti—Isu varindi vaviri hatimbozorori.

Uye, nguva iri yeshe, usiku kana masikati vafambisi vezvikepe vaikona zvino kutsika pamusoro pechikepe, kunze kwekuti Ahabhi ari pamberi pavo; kana akaema mu pivot-hole yake, kana kunyatsohamba mapuranga pakati pemiganhu miviri isingatsauki, — main-mast uye mizen; kana kuti vakamuona akaema mu cabin-scuttle, — rutsoka rwake rwunorarama rwakaenda mberi pamusoro pe deck, sekuti rwunotsika; ngowani yake yakasimudza zvikuru pamusoro pemaziso ake; zvekuti kunyazwi akaema asizi kuhamba, kunyazwi masikati neusiku zvakawedzerwa, kuti aazi kuzunguzika muchibhegi chake; asi vakafishika pashi pe ngowani iyo yakatsvedza, avazi kumbokona kuziye vasingashaishi kuti, nge ndaa ye zveshe izvi, madziso ake akavharwa zvemene nguwa imweni; kana kuti aive achiri kunyatsozviongorora; hazvina nendaa, kunyange akaema kudaro mu scuttle kweawa yeshe pakutambanudza, uye kusingateereri night-damp yakaungana mumabwe edova pamusoro pejasi iroro rakavezwa ngemabwe nengowani. Mucheka wakanyorova usiku, zuva remangwana rakaoma pamusoro pake; uye saizvozvo, zuva nezuva, uye husiku nehusiku; haana kuzoendazve pashi pemapuranga; chero chaaida kubva mu cabin chinhu ichocho chaakatuma.

Wakarya mumhepo imweyo yakavhurika; kureva kuti, zvikafu zvake zviiri zvega, — zvemangwanani ne zvemadekoni: zvemadekoni haazi kumbobata; kana kukohwa ndebvu dzake; izvo zvakakura zvakasvipa zveshe zvakavhunduka, semidzi yakavhurwa yemiti yakavhunduswa, iyo ichiri kukura isina chiro pachidziro chisina ngubo, kunyange yakaparadzwa mu verdure yepamusoro. Asi kunyazwi upenyu hwake hweshe hwainge hwava wandi imwe pachikepe; uye kunyange zvazvo wachi yeParsee yechishamiso yaive isina kumbomira seyake; asi vaviri ava avazivi kumboita inga vanobhuya—mundhu umwe kuno umweni—kusiyapo pa nguva jakareba ciro cimweni cicikasisi maningi caipfuura cakaita kuti zvidikanwe. Kunyazi kuti n’anga yakasimba kudaro yaiita inga yakafishika kuti inobatana nevaviri; pachena, uye kune boka rakavhunduka, vaiita semapuranga akaparadzana. Kana masikati vakawana mukana wekutaura izwi rimwe; usiku, varume mbeveve vaive vese, kusvika pakuchinjana kwemazwi mashoma. Nguva jimweni, ko nguva yakareba maningi, pasina mvuramabwe imwe, vaiema kuretu mu civheneko co nyeredzi; Ahabhi muchikepe chake, Parsee ngechikepe chikuru; asi vachiri kutarisana; sekuti muParsee Ahabhi akaona mumvuri wake wakakandwa mberi, muna Ahabhi muParsee zvinhu zvake zvakasiiwa.

Uyezve, nge njira imweni, Ahabhi wakaita—mu njira yake kamare, inga zvaaivoneswa njiku ngo njiku, pa nguva ngo nguva, no mu nguva jese, ngo kuraira ku vandhu vaishanda pashi pake,—Ahabhi waiita inga ishe wakazviemera; muParsee asi muranda wake. Asizve zveshe zviri zviiri zvaiita inga zvakasungirirwa pamwepo, no mudzvanyiriri usikaoneki waivatungamirira; mumvuri wakaonda siding mbabvu yakasimba. Nekuti aive uyu Parsee chero angave, mbabvu dzose ne keel zvakanga zvakasimba Ahabhi.

Pakupenya kwekutanga kwekupenya kwemangwanani, izwi rake resimbi rakazwika kubva kumashure, —“Murume misoro yemapuranga!”—uye zuwa reshe, kuguma pashure pekuvira kwezuwa uye pashure pekuvira kwemadekoni, izwi rimwe chete awa imwe neimwe, pakurova kwebhero remutungamiri wechikepe, rakazwika—“What d’ye sharp?—sharp!”!

Asi mazuwa matatu kana mana akapfuura, mushure mokusangana naRacheri aitsvaka vana; uye hapana muromo wakange usati waonekwa; murume mukuru we monomaniac aiita kunge asingatendi kuvimbika kwevashandi vake; zvishoma, zvevanenge vese kunze kwevahedheni vanotamba zvibhakera; aiita kunge aisava nechokwadi, kunyange, kana Stubb naFlask vangasazvidira kurekerera kuona kwaaitsvaka. Asi kudari zviro zvaaifungira zvainga zvake kamare, iyena wakarekerera kuzvibhuya ngo mazwi, pikija zviito zvake zvaizoita inga zvinozvikhombija.

“Ndinozotanga kuvona hove huru inini,”—wakadaro. "Eya! Ahabhi anofanira kuva nechibharo chiviri!" uye nemaoko ake akagadzira dendere rezvibhakera zvakaiswa mumabhasiketi; uye kutuma nyara mudenga, nge chidhindo chimwe chakarukwa, kuti asimbise kusoro re main-mast, wakaashira magumo maviri e tambo yakadzika-reved; uye kubatanidza imwe kudengu rake akagadzirira pini kune imwe mugumo, kuti aisungirire pachitima. Izvi zvakaitwa, nge mugumo uwo uchiri munyara yake uye akaema parutii rwe pini, wakaningira kutenderera pachikwata chake, eitsvaira kubva kune umwe kuenda kune umwe; akambomira kutarisa kwake kwenguva yakareba paDaggoo, Queequeg, Tashtego; asi kudzivisa Fedallah; uye akazogara ziso rake rakasimba rakavimba pamusoro pemukuru weshamwari, akati,—“Tora tambo, changamire—ndinoipa mumaoko ako, Starbuck.” Pasurepo wakaronga mundhu wake mudengu, wakapa izwi kuti vamusimudze ku perch yake, Starbuck ndiye wakabatanidza tambo pakupeisira; Bhaibheri rinondiudza kuti ndiuye. Uye ngokudaro, ngo nyara imwe yakabatirira pa nhanho yo umambo, Ahabhi wakaningira kubanze kwe gungwa ko makiromita no makiromita, — mberi, kumavirira, divi iri, no iro, — mukati mo mudenderedzwa wakakura wakatambanuka wakarairwa pa kukwirira kukuru kudaro.

Paanoshanda ngenyara dzake pandau imweni yakakwirira iri kuretu mu rigging, iyo inokona kusakwanisa kugara, mufambisi wechikepe ari mugungwa unosimudzwa kuguma pandau iyoyo, wotsigirwapo ngetambo; pasi pemamiriro ezvinhu aya, mugumo wayo wakasungirirwa pamusoro pedhizaini unogara uchipiwa mukutarisira kwakasimba kune mumwe murume mumwe chete ane wachi yakakosha yayo. Ngekuti murenje rakadaro rekurumba rigging, iro hukama hwaro hwakasiyana-siyana hwakasiyana-siyana mudenga haukoni kugara huchionekwa zvisina kukanganisa ngezvinoonekwa kwavari pachikepe; uye apo deck-ends ye tambo idzi dziri kukandwa pashi mumaminitsi mashomanana ega ega kubva pazvisungo, zvaizova asi kufa kwechisikigo, kana, asina kupiwa ngemurindi anogara, mufambisi wechikepe wakasimudzwa anofanira ngekusachengeteka kwevashandi vechikepe kukandwa uye kuwa veshe vechibhururuka kuenda kugungwa. Ngo kudaro, zvaiitwa ndi Ahabhi pa shwiro iyi zvainga zvicizivi kujairika; chiro chimwe chega chinoshamisa pamusoro pavo chaiita inga, kuti Starbuck, inga ariye ega murumbwana wakamboshinga kumupikisa nge chiro chidoko chidoko chinoswedera kune chisarudzo—umweni weavowo, kugondeka kwake pakutarisa-kunze kwaaiita inga wakapokana zvishomani;—zvaishamisa, kuti uyu ndiye murumbwana wake watchman waaifanira kusarudza; kupa hupenyu hwake hwose zvakasununguka mumaoko emunhu akadaro zvimwe zvisingavimbiki.

Zvino, nguva yekutanga Ahabhi akagara mudenga; ere ainge atovepo kwemaminitsi gumi; imwe ye mhuka dzemugungwa dzine miromo tsvuku dzinowanzobhururuka zvisingaiti zvakanaka dzichitenderera misoro yema whalemen ane vanhu muma latitudes aya; imwe yeshiri idzi yakauya ichitenderera uye ichishevedzera ichitenderera soro rake mu maze yekutenderera kunokurumidza kusingatevedzeki. Pasurepo yakarumba chiuru chemamita yakananga mudenga; ndokuzotenderera pasi, ndokuenda eddying zvakare kutenderera musoro wake.

Asi ngo kusotesesa kwake pa ndau yakajeka zve iri kure, Ahabhi waiita inga kuti aazivi kuvona shiri iyi yo mushango; kana, zvemene, apana umweni waizozvikarakadza maningi, ngokuti azvizi kumboitika; asi zvino anenge ziso risinganyanyi kuteerera raiita kunge rinoona imwe mhando yezvinoreva zvekunyengera mune anenge maonero ese.

“Ngowani yenyu, ngowani yenyu, changamire!” ngekukasira wakachema mufambisi wengarava weSicily, uyo wakaiswa pa mizen-mast-head, wakaema zvakananga sure kwaAhabhi, kunyazi wakaderera zvishomani pane zvaaiita, uye wainge ne ndau yakadzama ye mhepo yaivaparadzanisa.

Asi kare bapiro re sable rainge riri pamberi pemaziso emurume mukuru; bhiri rakareba rakasungirirwa pamusoro pake: nekushevedzera, shiri yakasvipa yakarumbire kure nemubairo wake.

Gondo rakabhururuka katatu rakatenderera soro ra Tarquin, rakadusa chivharo chake kuti riise pandau yacho, ngesurepo Tanaquil, mukadzi wake, wakabhuya kuti Tarquin uchaita mambo we Roma. Asi chete nekutsiviwa kepisi kuti omen akaverenga zvakanaka. Ngowani yaAhabhi haizi kumbodzorerwa; shiri yemusango yakabhururuka ichienderera mberi nayo; kure mberi kwe prow: uye pakupedzisira akanyangarika; asi kubve pa ndau yakanyangarika iyoyo, ndau yakasvipa yo miniti yakavoneka zvishoma, yakawa kubve pa ndau yakakura yondhoyo kuenda mu gungwa.
