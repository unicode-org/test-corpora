Tarisa hump yake, iyo ingadai yakanaka kurya kudai ngeye mbudzi (iyo inoremekedzwa sechikafu chisingawanzowaniki), dai isipi piramidhi yakasimba kudaro yemafuta. Asi spermaceti pachayo, kuti inonaka sei uye yakasvibira; kufanana nenyama yakajeka, hafu-jellied, chena yekokonati mumwedzi wechitatu wekukura kwayo, asi yakapfuma zvikuru kuti ipe chinotsiva butter. Zvakanyadaro, vandhu vazinji vanoita zve whale vano njira yo kuikweva mu ciro cimweni, vozoitora. Mukuedza kwakareba kweusiku chiro chakajairika kuti vafambisi vengarava vanyike ship-biscuit yavo mumafuta-mapoto makuru vozvisiya zvichibikapo kwechinguwa. Kudya kwemanheru kwakawanda kwakanaka ndakaita saizvozvo.

Mu ndaa ye Sperm Whale doko pfungwa dzinoverengwa sendiro yakanaka. Bhokisi remusoro rinopwanywa ngesanhu, uye ma lobes maviri akaoma, machena anobviswa (anonyatsofanana nemapuddings maviri makuru), anozosanganiswa ngegorosi, uye anobikwa kuita chiro chinodakadza yaamho, mukunaka kunofanana ngesoro remhuru, iyo iri epicures imwe; uye munhu weshe unoziya kuti dzimweni mbudzi duku pakati pema epicures, ngekuramba dzeirya pamusoro pepfungwa dzemhuru, ngekukurumidza vanoguma pakuva ngepfungwa shomani dzavo, kuti vakwanise kuziya soro remhuru kubva kumisoro yavo; izvo, zvemene, zvinoda kusarura kusingawanzoitika. Uye ndicho chikonzero nei mwana wemombe ane soro remhuru rinotaridzika rakangwara pamberi pake, ngenjira imwe imwe yezviro zvinosuruvarisa zvaunokona kuona. Musoro unomutarisa kunge unomutuka, une “Et tu Brute!” mazwi.

Azviiti, kangaije, ngo ndava yo kuti hove iyi yakanyanya kushata zve vandhu vo mu nyika vanoita inga vanovona kurya kwayo ngo kunyangajwa; zvinooneka kuti zvinoguma, ngenjira imweni, ngekufunga kwakamboreketwa: kureva kuti munhu unofanira kurya chiro chitsva chakaurawa chegungwa, nekuchiryawo ngechiedza chacho. Asi acikana-kana kuti mundhu wo kutanga wakauraya mombe waivoniwa inga muurayi; pamwe akasungirirwa; uye dai akaiswa pakuedzwa kwake nemombe, zvechokwadi angadai akaiswa; uye zvechokwadi akafanirwa nazvo kana pane mhondi achizviita. Enda kumusika wenyama weMugovera usiku woona mapoka ezvipuka zvine makumbo maviri zvinorarama zvakaningira mudenga mitsara yakareba yezvipuka zvine makumbo mana zvakafa. Kuona ikoko akubudisi zino mutsaya yemunhu anodya vanhu here? Vanodya vanhu ere? ndiani asiri munhu anodya vanhu? Ndinokuronzera kuti zvinozoshiviririka maningi kuna Fejee wakaisa munyu mumishinari wakaonda mu cellar yake kurwisa nzara yaiuya; zvinozoshiviririka zvikuru kune Fejee uyo anopa, ndinoti, pazuwa rekutongwa, kupfuura iwe, gourmand yakabudirira uye yakajekeswa, iyo inopisha madhadha pashi uye inoita mabiko pachiropa chavo chakazvimba mu paté-de-foie-gras yako.

Asi Stubb, unorya whale nge chiedza chayo, ndizvona ere? uye izvozvo zvirikuwedzera kutuka kukuvara, ndizvo here? Tarisa banga rako, apo, gourmand wangu wakabudirira uye wakajekeswa uchidya nyama iyo yakabikwa, mubato iwo wakagadzirwa ngechinyi?—chiinyi asi mapfupa emukoma wemombe iyoyo yemuri kurya? Uye unotora mazino ako ngechii, mushure mekurya gozhe iroro rakafuta? Ne minhenga ye shiri imweyo. Uye nge quill ipi Munyori we Sociedade de Suppression de la Cruelté a Ganders akaratidza zvakajeka ma circulars ake? Mukati mwe mwedzi wekupeisira kana miiri ndiyo apo society iyoyo yakapa chisarudzo chekuti vasatsigira chiro asi mapeni esimbi.





CHITSAUKO 66. Kuurawa kwe Shark.

Pano ku Southern Fishery, Sperm Whale yakabatwa, sure kwekushanda nguva yakareba nekuremba, inounzwa parutii rweusiku, azvizi, sechiro chakajairika zvishoma, tsika yekuenderera mberi kamwe chete kubhizimisi rekumucheka mukati. azvizi kupera ngo kukasika maningi; uye zvinoda maoko ese kuti aite pamusoro pazvo. Ngokudaro, kushandiswa kwakajairika ngo kwo kutora zvikepe zvese; rova chigaro chemusoro a'lee; uye wozotuma umwe naumwe pashi ku hammock yake kuguma masikati, ngekuchengeta kuti, kuguma nguwa iyoyo, anchor-watches inozochengetedzwa; kureva kuti, vaviri nevaviri kweawa, vaviri vega vega, vashandi vari mukutenderera vanozokwira deck kuti vaone kuti zveshe zvinohamba zvakanaka.

Asi dzimweni nguwa, kunyanya pamusoro peMutsara muPacific, chirongwa ichi hachizopinduri zvachose; ngekuti mapoka akadaro asingaverengeki e shark anoungana akatenderedza mutumbi wakasungirirwa, kuti dai akasiiwa kudaro kwemaawa matanhatu, tingati, pakutambanudza, zvishoma kupfuura skeleton zvaizooneka mangwanani. Mundau dzimweni dzakawanda dze gungwa, asi, apo howe idzi dzisinganyanyi kuwanda, kudya kwadzo kunoshamisa kunokona nguwa imweni kudzikira, nge kudzisimudza nge simba nge sharp whaling-spades, maitiro asizi kumboitika, ayo, mune dzimweni nguva, anoita inga anodziita kuti dziite zviro zvikuru. Asi zvainga zvisizi kudaro mu ndaa iripo wari nge shark dze Pequod; asi, kuve nechokwadi, murume weshe asizi kujaira kuona zvakadaro, kuti akaningira kurutivi rwake husiku ihwohwo, angadai akapotsa akafunga kuti gungwa reshe rakatenderera raiva chizi imwe yakakura, uye shark idzodzo makonzo ari mukati mwaro.
