Asi kunyazi ngarava yakarumba kudaro, zve kunyazi kubve mu madziso ese, inga miseve, kusota kunodakarira kwakapfura, asi jet ye sirivha aizi kuzoonekazve usiku wondhowo. Mufambisi wechikepe wega wega akapika kuti akazviona kamwe chete, asi kwete kechipiri.

Muromo uyu we pakati peusiku wakanga wava kuda kukura chiro chakariarwa, apo, mazuwa amweni akateera, wonai! paawa imweyo yakanyarara, yakaziiswazve: zvakare yakatsanangurwa ngeveshe; asi paakaita chikepe kuti aipfuure, yakanyangarika zvakare sekuti haizi kumbovapo. Uye ndizvo zvakatishandira usiku no usiku, kuguma pasina wakazvizwisisa asi kuzvishamisika. Ngo njira isikazwisisiki, yakajekeswa ngo mweji wakajeka, kana civheneko co nyeredzi, inga zvaizoitika; kunyangarikazve kwezuwa rimwe rose, kana mazuwa mairi, kana matatu; uye ngenjira imweni kuoneka pakudzokorora kwega kwega kwakasiyana-siyana kuende mberi kuenderera mberi nekuenderera mberi mumotokari yedu, ndege iyi iri yega yaiita inga inotikwezva.

Kana ngekutenda mashura kusingakanganwiki kwe rudzi rwavo, uye mukuwirirana ne preternaturalness, sezvaioneka, iyo mune zviro zvakawanda yakaisa Pequod, paiya pane kuda vamweni vevafambisi vezvikepe vaipika kuti nguwa yeshe ne peshe pazvinotsanangurwa; pa nguva dziri kuretu, kana mu latitudes ne longitudes dziri kuretu, muromo uwo usikakwanisiki wakaposherwa nge whale imweyo; uye iyo hove huru, Moby Dick. Kwe nguwa yakati, pakatonga, zvakare, kuzwa kutya kunoshamisa pa kuoneka uku kunobhururuka, ingatei kwaitikwezva nge unyengeri kuenda mberi ne mberi, kuti chikara chizotendeuka pamusoro pedu, ne kutitsemura pakupedzisira mumakungwa ari kuretu uye ane mhuka dzakashata maningi.

Izvi zvinothya zve nguva doko, zvisizi kujeka asi zvinotyisa, zvakawana simba rinoshamisa kubve mukusiyana kwekunyarara kwe mamiriro ekunze, umo, pashi pe blandness yayo yeshe yebhuruu, vamweni vaifunga kuti pane chiro chakashata chakashata, kudai nge mazuwa nemazuwa atakahamba pamwepo, kuburikidza nge makungwa takaremba, tiri tega, kuti zveshe zvakaona mu kuzvisiya kwehupenyu pamberi pe urn-yakafanana ne prow yedu.

Asi, pakupeisira, patakatendeukira kumabvazuva, mhepo dze Cape dzakatanga kuungudza dzakatitendenedza, takasimuka towira pamusoro pemakungwa marefu, anotambudzika aripo; apo Pequod ane mazino ane nyanga yenzou akagwadama zvakanyanya kune kuvhuvhuta, uye akarova masaisai ekusvipa mukupenga kwake, kusvikira, kunge mvura inonaya yezvimedu zvesirivha, mapundu-mapundu akabhururuka pamusoro pezvidziviriro zvake; zvino zvose izvi zvakaparadzwa zvehupenyu zvakaenda, asi zvakapa ndau kune zvionero zvinosuruvarisa kupfuura kare.

Pedyo ne uta hwedu, mafomu anoshamisa mumvura airumba apapo pamberi pedu; apo gobvu kumashure kwedu akabhururuka inscrutable gungwa-magunguwo. Uye mangwanani ose, takagara pazvigaro zvedu, mitsara yeshiri idzi yaionekwa; uye pasinei ne hootings dzedu, kwenguva yakareba vakaomesa mwoyo vakabatirira pa hemp, sekuti vaiona ngarava yedu imwe ngarava inofamba, isina vanhu; chiro chakaemeswa kuti chiparadzwe, ngendaa iyoyo yakafanira ndau yekugara yavo vasina pekugara. Uye akasimudzwa uye akasimudzwa, achiri kusingazorori akasimudza gungwa dema, sekuti mafungu aro makuru aive hana; uye mweya mukuru wenyika wakange uri mukushungurudzika nekuzvidemba ngechivi chakareba nekutambudzika kwawakabereka.

Cape ye Tariro Yakanaka, vanokudana ere? Asi Cape Tormentoso, sezvaidaidzwa kare; ngekuti nguva yakareba takakwezverwa ngekunyarara kwekunyepa kwakange kwatiunganidza kare, takazviwana takaposherwa mugungwa iri rinotambudzwa, apo zvipuka zvine mhosva zvakashandurwa kuva shiri idzodzo nehowe idzi, zvaiita inga zvakatongwa kudhuvha mhetsa nariini pasina ndau yekugara, kana kurova mhepo iyo yakasvipa pasina ndau yekuzogara. Asi yakadzikama, yakachena-chando, uye isingashanduki; ichiri kutungamirira chitubu chayo cheminhenga kudenga; achiri kutikombedza kubva pamberi, ndege iri yega dzimwe nguva yaizotsanangurwa.

Munguwa yeshe iyi yekusvipa kwezviro, Ahabhi, kunyazi akafunga kuti ngenguva iyoyo waigara eitonga ndau yakanyorova uye ine ngozi, wakakhombidza kuchengetedza kwakashata maningi; uye kashoma kupfuura kare kutaura nevamwe vake. Munguwa dze dutu dzakadai nge idzi, sure kwe zviro zveshe zviri mudenga nge mudenga zvachengetedzwa, apana chimweni chinokona kuitwa asi kugara vechiemera ndaa ye dutu. Pasurepo Kapiteni nevashandi vanova fatalists vanoshanda. Nguvayo, vashandi vakadzingwa kubva kumberi kwe ngarava nge makungwa ane ngozi akatsemuka ngekuvhunduka pamusoro pe uta hwayo, vakaema mumutsara pamwepo ne nhare dziri mu chiuno; uye zviri nani kungwarira masaisai anosvetuka, murume umwe neumwe ainge azvitsvedza mumhando yebowline yakasungirirwa pachitima, iyo yaaizununguka semubhande rakasunungurwa. CHITSAUKO 52. Chipuka chinozwi Albatross.

Kumaodzanyemba-kumabvazuva kubva kuCape, kure neCrozetts iri kure, ndau yakanaka yekuhamba ngezvikepe yeRight Whalemen, chikepe chaioneka mberi, Goney (Albatross) ngezina. Apo aikwedera pasinde zvishomani, kubva pachigaro changu chakakwirira pamusoro wemberi-mast, ndakaona zvakanaka chiono ichocho chinoshamisa maningi kune tyro muhowe dzemugungwa rekure —whaler mugungwa, uye nguva yakareba asipo pamuzi.

Kudai kuti masaisai ainge akadzara, chikepe ichi chakacheneswa kunge pfupa re walrus yakasungirirwa. Pashi pe mativi ake ese, kuoneka uku kwaioneka nge njira dzakareba dze rust yakatsvuka, apo spars dzake dzeshe ne rigging yake zvaiita inga matavi akaoma emiti akafukidzwa nge hoar-frost. Ndizvo zvega zvakaiswa zvikepe zvake zvepasi. Chiono chemusango chaiva kuona ndebvu dzake dzakareba dzinotarisa-kunze kune misoro iyo mitatu yemast. Vaiita inga vakasimira ganda ro zvipuka, ngo kudaro vakabvaruka no kutsemura nguvo jainga jakapona pa makore anokhwedera marongomuna o kuhamba mu ngarava. Vakaema muzviphenduriso zvesimbi zvakasungirirwa pachitanda, vaizunguzika no kuzunguzika pamusoro pegungwa risingaperi; uye kunyazi, apo ngarava yakahamba zvishomani zvishomani pashi pe sure kwedu, isu varume vatanhatu mudenga takaguma pasinde pedyo zvekuti taigona kunge takapotsa tasvetuka kubva pamisoro ye mast-ye ngarava imwe kuenda kune imwe; asi, vabati vehove vaya vaivoneka inga vakaparajwa, vecitisotesesa ngo kupfava pavaihamba, avazivi kubhuya ciro cimwe basi ku vandhu vedu vaivona, apo mvuramabwe yo quarter-deck yainga yecizwika kubvira pashi.

"Ngarava ahoy! Mwakaona ere Whale Chena?"

Asi apo mukuru wehondo usikazikani, wakasendamira padera pe nhare dzakachena, wakange ari muchiito chekuisa hwamanda yake kumuromo wake, nge njira imweni yakawa kubva munyara yake kuenda mugungwa; uye mhepo zvino yakasimuka ain, akaedza pasina kuti azviite kuti anzwike pasina iyo. Nguva iyoyo ngarava yake yainge ichiwedzera kureba pakati. Apo nge njira dzakasiyana-siyana dzakanyarara vafambisi vengarava ve Pequod vaikhombise kuona kwavo chiitiko ichi chinotyisa pakutanga kungobhuya zina re White Whale kune imweni ngarava, Ahabhi wakamboema kwechinguvana; zvakaita inga kuti waizodzasa chikepe kuti akwire mueni, kudai mhepo inotyisa isikazi kurambidza. Asi echishandisa ndau yake ye mhepo, wakabatazve hwamanda yake, ne kuziya nge ndaa yake kuti ngarava ye mweni yainge ye Nantucketer uye yakasungwa kumhatso nguva pfupi, wakabonga nge izwi guru—“Ahoy apo! Iyi ndiyo Pequod, yakasungwa kutenderera nyika yeshe! ——”

Pa nguwa iyoyo, ma wake maviri akayambuka zvakanaka, uye ponapo, zvino, mukuwirirana nge njira dzavo imwe, mapoka e howe duku dzisina kukuvadza, idzo kwe mazuwa mamweni apfuura dzainge dzichidhuvha parutii rwedu, dzakarumba nge zvaiita inga zvinodedera zviphenduko, ndokuzviparadzanisa mberi nge sure nge zviphenduko zve mweni. Pikija hazvo kuti paaienderera mberi no kwenda, Ahabhi waidikana kumbovona zviro zvakafanana, asi, ku mundhu ari wese anodakarira kuita zviro zvimwe basi, zviro zvidoko maningi zvinoreva zviro.

“Shambirai kuretu neni, munoita ere?” akagunun'una Ahabhi achitarisa mumvura. Zvaioneka inga zvishoma mu mazwi, asi izwi raikhombija kusuruvara kwakadzara kusingadetseri kupinda zvainga zvambokhombiswa ngo mundhu mukuru waipenga. Asi wakatendeukira kumutungamiriri, uwo kuguma wari wakabata ngarava mu mhepo kuti aderedze mberi kwayo, wakadanidzira nge izwi rake re shumba rekare, —“Simudza chikepe!

Kutenderera nyika yeshe! Kune zvakawanda muzwi iroro kukurudzira manzwiro ekuzvikudza; asi kutenderera kweshe ikoko kunoendesa kupi? Kubudikidza nge ngozi dzisingaverengeki kuguma patakatangira, apo avo vetakasiya vakachengeteka, vaigara nguva dzeshe pamberi pedu.

Kuti nyika ino inga iri ndau isingaperi, zve ngo kuhamba ngo cikepe kuenda kumabvazuva taizokwanisa kuguma pa ndau jimweni jimweni, no kuvona zviro zvinotapira maningi no zvinoshamisa kupinda Cyclades kana zvitsuwa zva Mambo Soromona, hino painga no cigondiso mu rwendo hwo ngarava. Asi mukutsvaka zvakavanzika izvozvo zviri kure zvatinorota, kana mukutsvaka ngekutambudzwa kwedhimoni iro phantom iro, nguwa imweni kana imwe, rinoshambira pamberi pemwoyo yeanthu eshe; pavanodzingirira vakadaro pamusoro penyika ino yakatenderera, vanotitungamirira mu maze asina mvura kana pakati penjira vanotisiya takadzara.
