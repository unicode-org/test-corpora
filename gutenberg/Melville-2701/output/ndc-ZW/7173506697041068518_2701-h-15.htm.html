CHITSAUKO 74. Musoro we Sperm Whale—Kuona Kwakasiyana.

Heino, zvino, pane howe mbiri huru, dzakaisa misoro yadzo pamwe chete; ngatizvibatanidze, uye tiise pamwe chete zvedu.

Pakati po zviro zvinozwi folio leviatans, Sperm Whale no Right Whale ndizvona zvinosisira maningi. Ndivo chete whales nguva dzose kuvhimwa munhu. Ku Nantucketer, vanokhombija zviro zviviri zvakanyanya zvo muhlobo wese unozivikanwa wo whale. Sezvo musiyano wekunze pakati pavo unonyanya kuonekwa musoro ravo; uye sesoro reumwe neumwe ndiyo nguva ino yakarembera kubva kurutivi rwaPequod; uye sezvo tinokona kuenda takasununguka kubva kune imwe kuenda kune imwe, ngekungotsika-tsika mhiri kwechikepe:—ndinofanira kuda kuziya, munozowana mukana wakanaka wekufunda cetology inoshanda kupfuura pano ere?

Pakutanga, munoshamisika ngo kusiyana kwakawanda pakati po misoro iyi. Zveshe zviri zviiri zvakakura zvakakwana muhana yeshe; asi pane imwe yekuenzana kwemasvomhu muSperm Whale iyo Right Whale inosuruvarisa inoshayikwa. Kune hunhu hwakawanda mumusoro weSperm Whale. Pamunozvivona, munozvipira ngo kutama kuzvidira kuti muve mukuru maningi kwaari, ngo pamusoro po kureremeja kwakapararira. Muchiitiko chemazuva ano, zvakare, kuremekedzwa uku kunowedzerwa kubudikidza nge ruvara rwe pepper ne munyu rwe soro rake pachikomo, zvinopa chiratidzo che makore akura uye ruzivo rwakakura. Mukupfupikisa, ndiyena unodanwa ngo vabati ve hove ngo njira yo uzivi kuti “hove yo musoro wakasvipa.”

Ngativone wari zviro zvicizivi kusiyana maningi pa misoro iyi—zvinoreva, zviro zviviri zvinosisira maningi, jiso no nzeve. Kuretu sure parutii rwe soro, ne pashi, pasinde ne kona ye shaya imwe neimwe ye whale, kana ukatsvaka zvishoma, unozopeisira kuona ziso risina lash, iro raunozofunga kuti rive ziso re mwana wembongoro; saka kubva pazviyero zvose zviri kuhukuru hwesoro.

Zvino, kubve pa ndau iyi yakasiyana-siyana yo mu divi ro madziso o whale, zvakajeka kuti aakoni kumbovona ciro ciri mberi kamare, kudari inga zvaanokwanisa kuvona ciro ciri kuseri kamare. Mu izwi rimwe, ndau ye madziso e whale inoenderana ne nzee dze mundhu; uye unokona kufungidzira, iwewe, kuti zvaizofamba sei newe, wakaongorora zviro parutii kuburikidza nenzee dzako. Unozowana kuti unokona kuraira basi madhigirii makumi matatu ekuona pamberi pemutsara wakarurama wekuona; uye vamwe vanenge makumi matatu kumashure kwayo. Kudai muvengi wako unovava maningi waihamba wakananga kwauri, no banga rakasimudzwa masikati makuru, auzokwanisi kumuona, kudai nge kudari waikuba nge sure. Mu gama rimwe, waizova no musana miviri, inga zvatinonanga; asi, nguva imweyo,zve, mberi mbiri (mberi dzeparutivi): ngekuti chiinyi chinoita kuti mberi kwemunhu—chiinyi, zvemene, asi madziso ake?

Uyezve, apo mune dzimweni mhuka dzakawanda dzandinokona kufunga zvino, madziso akasimwa zvakanyanya sezvisingaoneki kuti asanganise simba rawo rekuona, kuti abudise mufananidzo umwe uye kwete maviri kupfungwa; ndau yakasiyana-siyana ye madziso e whale, yakaparadzaniswa zvakanaka sezvairi nge cubic feet dzakawanda dze soro rakasimba, iro rinokwira pakati pavo kunge gomo guru rinoparadzanisa nyanza mbiri mumipata; izvi, zvemene, zvinofanira kuparadzanisa zvakakwana zvinooneka izvo nhengo imwe neimwe yakazvimiririra inopa. Ngokudaro, howe huru inofanira kuona mufananidzo umwe wakasiyana nge divi iri, ne mufananidzo umweni wakasiyana nge divi iro; apo zvese zviri pakati zvinofanira kuva rima rakadzama uye kusava nechinhu kwaari. Mundhu angaita inga kuti anonangwa kuti anosotesesa nyika ari mu bhokisi ro kungwarira rine zviphenduko zviviri zvakabatana zvo fafitera yake. Asi nge whale, mabhande aya maviri anoiswa akasiyana, achiita mahwindo maviri akasiyana, asi zvinosuruvarisa kuti zvinokanganisa kuona. Kusiyana uku kwemaziso e whale chiro chinofanira kugara cheikarakadzwa pakubata howe; uye kuti zviyeukwe nemuverengi mune zvimwe zviitiko zvinotevera.

Mubvunjo unoda kuziva zve unoshamisa maningi ungatangwa ngo pamusoro po shwiro iyi inovoneka inga inobata Revhiatani. Asi ndinofanira kugutsikana ngezano. Kudai ngekuti madziso emunhu akavhurika muchiedza, chiito chekuona hachiiti ngekuzvidira; kureva kuti, haakoni ipapo kubatsira mechanically kuona chero zvinhu zviri pamberi pake. Zvakanyadaro, zvaakasongana nazvo mundhu wese zvinozomujijisa, kuti pikija ecikwanisa kutora zviro zvicikasaruri ngo kuvona kamwe basi, azvikwanisiki kamare kuti iyena, ngo kungwarira, zve zvakakwana, aongorore zviro zviviri—kunyazwi zvakakura kana zvidoko—pa nguva imweyo; never mind kana vakarara parutivi vachibatana. Asi kana iwe zvino wauya woparadzanisa zviro zviiri izvi, wotenderedza chimwe nechimwe nge denderedzwa re rima rakadzama; zvino, kuti uone imwe yadzo, nge njira yekuunza pfungwa dzako kuti dzizviite, imwe inozobviswa zvachose kubva mupfungwa dzako dzemazuva ano. Ngo kudaro, zvinoitika kudini ngo hove huru? I cokwadi, madziso ake ese maviri, mukati mwawo, anodikana kuita nguva imweyo; asi ngqondo yake inozwisisa maningi, inosanganisa, zve isizivi kujeka kupinda yo mundhu, zvokuti anokwanisa pa mukuvo umwewo kuvona ngo ungwaru zviro zviviri zvakasiyana, cimweni ciri ku divi rimweni rake, cimweni ciri ku divi rimweni rake? Kana eikwanisa, hino chiro chinoshamisa maari, kudai ngekuti mundhu waikona nguva imweyo kupfuura ngekukhombise matambudziko maviri akasiyana muna Euclid. Kana, zvakanyatsoongororwa, hapana kusaenderana mukuenzanisa uku.

Zvinokona kunge zviri zviro zvisina shwiro, asi zvakagara zveioneka kwandiri, kuti kudengenyeka kunoshamisa kwekuhamba kunokhombidzwa ngedzimweni howe kana dzakapoteredzwa ngezvikepe zvitatu kana zvirongomuna; kunyadziswa uye mutoro kune queer kutya, saka zvakajairika kune whales dzakadaro; Ndinofunga kuti zveshe izvi zvinobuda zvisina kunanga kubva mukuvhiringika kusingadetseri kwekuda, umo simba ravo rakaparadzana uye diametrically rakasiyana rekuona rinofanira kuvabatanidza.

Asi nzeve ye whale yakazara sekuda kuziva seziso. Kana uri mueni weshe ku rudzi rwavo, unokona kuvhima pamusoro pe misoro miviri iyi kwemaawa, uye usazombowana nhengo iyoyo. Nzeve haina shakani rekunze chero chii zvacho; uye mugomba pacharo haukwanisi kuisa quill, saka zvinoshamisa miniti ndiyo. Inogara zvishoma kumashure kweziso. Nge kuremeredza nzee dzavo, musiyano uyu wakakosha unofanira kuonekwa pakati pe sperm whale ne kurudyi. Kunyazi nzee yo kutanga ine ndau yakavhurika yo kubanze, yo yo kutanga yakavharwa kamare zve zvakaenzana ngo ganda, kuitira kuti azvivoneki kamare kubve kubanze.

Azvidakadzi kuziya ere, kuti chipuka chikuru kudai nge howe huru chinokona kuona nyika ngeziso doko kudaro, ne kuzwa mabanan’ana nge nzee doko kupinda ye tsuro? Asi kudai madziso ake akafara kudai nge lens ye teresikopu huru ya Herschel; uye nzeve dzake dzakakura semaveranda emachechi makuru; izvi zvaizomuita kuti avonezve, kana kuzwa zvakapinza here? Haiwa.—Hino ngenyi munoeja “kukura” ngqondo yenyu? Iite kuti zviite zvishoma.

Ngatiite zvino nge levers ne steam-engines dzetiinadzo panyara, cant pamusoro pe sperm whale, kuti idzokere pashi kuenda mudenga; zvino, kukwira nge ladder kuenda pamusoro, uve ne peep pasi muromo; uye dai zvisiri kuti muiri zvino waparadzana zvachose nawo, ne mwenje tingadzika muBako guru re Kentucky Mammoth remudumbu rake. Asi ngatibate pano ngezino iri, titarise kumativi edu apo tiri. Muromo wakacena zve wakacena kamare! kubva pashi kuguma padenga, yakashongedzwa, kana kuti zviri nani pepa rine membrane chena inopenya, inopenya se satins yemwenga.

Asi buda zvino, woona tsaya iyi yepashi inoshamisa, inoita inga chivharo chakareba chakatetepa che snuff-box yakakura, ine hinge kumupendero umwe, pane rutivi rumwe. Kana ukaipry up, kuti uite kuti iende pamusoro, uye wobudisa mitsara yayo yemazino, inoita inga portcullis inotyisa; uye zvakadaro, maiwe! zvinokhombise vakawanda wight murombo mukubata hove, pamusoro pavo spikes aya anowira nesimba rekuroverera. Asi zvinotyisa maningi kuona, apo fathoms pashi mugungwa, unoona imweni sulky whale, yechiyangararapo yakasungirirwapo, nge tsaya dzayo dzinoshamisa, dzinenge makubiti gumi nemashanu kureba, yakasungirirwa yakananga pashi pa right-angles nge muiri wake, kuitira kuti nyika yeshe iite inga jib-boom ye ngarava. Whale iyi haizi kufa; anongoora mwoyo; kubva kune mhando, zvichida; munhu anozvifunga; uye saka supine, kuti zviphenduko zve tsaya dzake zvakasununguka, zvichimusiya ipapo mune iyo ungainly mhando yekutambudzika, kunyadziswa kune rudzi rwake rwese, uyo anofanira, pasina kumbozengurira, imprecate lock-jaws pamusoro pake.

Muzviitiko zvakawanda tsaya iyi yepashi—inosunungurwa nyore nyore ngemunhu anodzidzira—inosunungurwa uye inosimudzwa pamusoro pechikepe ngechinangwa chekubudisa mazino enyanga yenzou, uye kupa zvinhu zveiyo yakaoma ye whalebone chena iyo vabati vehove vanogadzira ndiyo mhando dzeshe dzezviro zvinodakadza, kusanganisira sticks, umbrellastoles.

Ne hoist yakareba, yakaremba tsaya inokwekwetwa pamusoro pechikepe, sekuti iri chikepe; uye apo nguva yakafanira inoguma —mazuwa mashoma mushure mwemushando umweni —Queequeg, Daggoo, na Tashtego, veshe vari madokotera emazino akabudirira, vanogadzirirwa kudhonza mazino. Nekucheka-spade kwakanyanya, Queequeg anorova mazino; zvino tsaya inorohwa pashi kuguma pa ringbolts, ne tackle iri kugadzirwa kubva mudenga, vanokweva mazino aya, kudai nge mombe dze Michigan dzinokweva zvigutsa zve miti ye oak yekare kubve munyika dze miti ye mushango. Kune kazhinji mazino makumi mana nemaviri mune zvose; mu whales dzekare, dzakashata zvikuru, asi dzisina kuora; kana kuzadzwa mushure mefashoni yedu yekunasirwa. Tsaya inozochekwa kuita mapuranga, younganidzwa kuretu inga ma joists ekuaka mhatso.





CHITSAUKO 75. Musoro weWhale Yekurudyi—Kuona Kwakasiyana.

Kuyambuka deck, ngatiite zvino zvakanaka kure kutarisa musoro Right Whale.

Kudai nge maumbirwo akawanda soro re Sperm Whale rinoremekedzeka rinokona kuenzaniswa nengoro yehondo yeRoma (kakurutu mberi, apo rakatenderera zvakanyanya); saka, pakuona kwakakura, soro reRight Whale rinofanana zvisina kunaka neshangu huru ine zvigunwe zve galliot. Makore mazana maviri apfuura mufambi wekare weDutch akafananidza chimiro chayo nechekupedzisira chemugadziri weshangu. Uye mune iyi imweyo yekupeisira kana shangu, mukadzi mukuru uyo ​​we nursery ngano, nge vana vanobhururuka, waikona kugara zvakanaka maningi, iye nge vana vake veshe.

Asi paunoguma pasinde ne soro iri guru rinotanga kutora zviro zvakasiyana-siyana, kudai nge maonero ako. Kuti ukaema pamusoro payo woningira mavhunduka aya maviri akaita inga F, unozotora soro reshe kuti uite bass-viol yakakura maningi, ne spiracles idzi, mavhura ari mu sounding-board yaro. Pasurepo,zve, kudari mukasotesesa jiso renyu pa ciro ici cinoshamisa, cino ganda, cakaita inga muzinga pa musoro po mass—ici ciro cino ruva, cino barnacled, ico vandhu vo ku Greenland vanodana kuti “korona,” zve vabati ve hove vo ku Maodzanyemba vanodana kuti “bonnet” yo Right Whale; uchinasira madziso ako pane izvi zvega, waizotora soro kuti uite muti wemuti mukuru we oak, une dendere reshiri muchipfuva chayo. At any rate, kana ukaona magakanje aya anorarama anogara pano pa bonnet iyi, mufungo wakadaro unozoguma pauri; kunze kwekuti, zvemene, kudakarira kwako kwakagadziriswa ngezwi rekuita “korona” rakapuwawo pairi; muchiitiko ichi uchatora kudakarira kukuru kufunga kuti chikara ichi chine simba chiri mambo wegungwa ane diademed sei, uyo korona yake ye green yakaiswa pamwe chete kwaari nenzira iyi inoshamisa. Asi kana whale iyi iri mambo, iye munhu anotarisa sulky zvikuru kuti aite nyasha diadem. Ningiranyi muromo wo pashi wakarembera! chii chikuru sulk uye pout iripo! sulk ne pout, nge chiyero che muvezi, ingangoita makubiti makumi mairi kureba ne makubiti mashanu kudzaka; sulk uye pout iyo inozokupa mafuta anenge 500 magaloni uye zvimwe.

Kusiririsa kukuru, zvino, kuti iyi whale isina rombo rakanaka inofanira kuva hare-miromo. Fissure iri ingangoita tsoka mhiri. Pamweni mai pa nguva yakasisira maningi vaihamba ngo cikepe vecidzaka ku ndau iri phedo no gungwa ro Peru, apo kudengenyeka ko nyika kwakaita kuti ndau yo gungwa ivhurike. Pamusoro pemuromo uyu, sepamusoro pechikumbiridzo chinotsvedza, isu zvino tinotsvedza mumuromo. Pamusoro peshoko rangu ndainge ndiri kuMackinaw, ndaifanira kutora izvi kuti zvive mukati mewigwam yeIndia. Mambo Mwari vakanaka! ndiyo njira yakafamba naJona here? Denga racho rinenge rakareba makubiti gumi nemaviri, uye rinorumba kuenda kune kona yakapinza yakanaka, kunge pane ridge-pole yenguva dzose apo; apo aya mativi ane mbabvu, akabhenda, ane bvudzi, anotipa mativi aya anoshamisa, hafu akaema, akaita ngatei scimetar e whalebone, anoti mazana matatu parutii, ayo anoenderana kubva kuchikamu chepamusoro chesoro kana korona bone, anoumba mapofu aya eVenetian ayo akambotaurwa kune imwe nzvimbo. Mipendero yemapfupa aya yakashongedzwa ngetambo dzine bvudzi, iyo Right Whale inosefa mvura, uye mukuoma kwayo inochengeta hove duku, kana yakavhurika muromo inohamba ngemumakungwa e brit munguwa yekurya. Mumapofu epakati emapfupa, sezvaakaema mukurongeka kwawo kwechisikigo, kune zviratidzo zvimweni zvinodakadza, zvakabhenda, makomba, uye zviphenduriso, apo vamweni vatambi vehove vanoverenga makore echipuka, semakore e oak ngezvindori zvayo zvakatenderera. Kunyazi kugondeka kwe njira iyi kuri kuretu ne kukhombise, asi kune kunaka kwe analogical probability. Zvakanyadaro, kudari tikazvitendera, tinodikana kupa makore makuru maningi ku Right Whale kupinda pakutanga zvinozoita inga zvinozwisisika.

Kare, zvinoita inga kuti painga no zviro zvaidakarira maningi kuziva ngo pamusoro po mapofu aya. Mundhu umweni waifamba ngo ngarava ku Purchas wakazvidanija kuti “ndebvu” jinoshamisa jiri mukati mo muromo wo whale;* umweni, “ndebvu jo nguruve”; mundhu wo citatu wakakura wo ku Hackluyt wakashandisa mutara unotevera wakaseja: “Kuno zviphenduko zvinokwana mazana maviri no makumi mashanu zvinokura ku divi rimwe nga rimwe ro chop yake yo ndhambo , zvinotenderera pa rurimi rwake ku divi rimwe nga rimwe ro muromo wake.”

*Izvi zvinotikaradza kuti Right Whale ine gwinyiso ine mhando ye ndebvu, kana kuti ndebvu, dzine bvudzi shomani rakapararira rakachena pamusoro pekupedzisira kwe kubanze kwe tsaya yepashi. Nguwa imweni tufts idzi dzinopa kutaridzika kwakanyanya kwe brigandish kune chiso chake chimwe chinoremekedza.

Kudai ngekuziya kwemunhu weshe, “mavhudzi enguruwe” aya, “mafirimu,” “ndebvu,” “mapofu,” kana chero chaunoda, anopa madzimai mabhurukwa avo nezvimweni zviro zvinoita kuti vaome. Asi mune izvi chaizvoizvo, kudiwa kwagara kuri kudzikira. Yainge iri nguwa ya Mambokadzi Anne apo pfupa rainge riri mukubwinya kwaro, farthingale yaive nguva iyoyo yeshe yefashoni. Uye sezvo madzimai aya ekare aihamba-hamba eidakara, kunyange ari mushaya dze whale, sezvaungataura; kunyange zvakadaro, mumvura inoshambidzwa, nekusafunga kwakafanana, isu mazuva ano tinobhururuka pasi petsaya imweyo kuti tidzivirirwe; amburera iri tende yakapararira pamusoro pepfupa rimwechete.

Asi zvino riarwa zveshe zve mapofu ne ndebvu kwechinguvana, uye, wakaema mumuromo we Right Whale, ningira zvakakutendenedza patsva. Kuona ma colonnades aya eshe emapfupa akarongeka nge njira, auzofungi kuti uri mukati mwe chiro chikuru che Haarlem, weiningira mapaipi ayo ane chiuru ere? Kuitira kapeti ku n’anga tino kapeti yo Turquia yakapfava maningi—rurimi, rwunonamatira, inga kuti, pashi po muromo. Inofuta maningi zve yakapfava, zve inokwanisa kutsemuka ngo zvidimbu pa kuisimudza pa deck. Rurimi urwu rwakanyatsojeka zvino pamberi pedu; pakutarisa kunopfuura ndinofanira kutaura kuti yaive midziyo mitanhatu; kureva kuti, zvichakupa mafuta anenge akadaro.

Ere izvi, munofanira kunge mwaona zvakajeka chokwadi chezvandakatanga ndizvo—kuti Sperm Whale ne Right Whale vane misoro inoda kusiyana zvachose. Kupfupikisa, hino: mu Right Whale's akuna tsime guru re mbeu; hapana mazino enyanga yenzou zvachose; hapana yakareba, yakatetepa mandible yetsaya yepasi, kunge yeSperm Whale. Kana muSperm Whale hapana imwe yemapofu aya emapfupa; hapana muromo mukuru wepasi; uye zvishoma chimwe chinhu cherurimi. Zvakare, Whale yekurudyi ine maburi maviri ekunze e-sput, Sperm Whale imwe chete.

Tarisa yekupeisira yako, zvino, pamisoro iyi inoremekedzwa yakavharwa, vachiri kuata pamwepo; nokuti imwe ichakurumidza kunyura, isina kunyorwa, mugungwa; imwe haizove yakareba zvikuru mukutevera.

Unokona kubata kutaridza kweSperm Whale iripo ere? Ndizvona zvaakafa nazvo, asi mamweni mavhinga akareba pahuma anoita inga apera zvino. Ndinofunga kuti chiso chake chakafara kuti chizare nekunyarara kwakaita se prairie, kwakabarwa ngekusaitira hanya kwekufungidzira sekufa. Asi cherechedza kutaridzika kweimwe soro. Ona kuti muromo wepasi unoshamisa, wakasundirwa ngetsaona parutivi rwemudziyo, kuitira kuti zvakasimba kumbundira tsaya. Musoro uwu wese auvoneki inga unobhuya ngo pamusoro po civaringo cikuru cinoshanda co kusongana no rufu here? Iyi Whale Yekurudyi ndinotora kuti yaive Stoic; iyo Sperm Whale, muPlato, uyo angadai akatora Spinoza mumakore ake ekupedzisira.





CHITSAUKO 76. Gondohwe rekurova.

Ere kurega, nge ndaa ye nonce, soro re Sperm Whale, ndinoda kuti iwe, se physiologist wakangwara, zviri nyore-kunyanya kuona chikamu chayo chemberi, mukuunganidzwa kwayo kweshe kwakabatana. Ndinoda kuti muzviongorore zvino nge maonero ega ekuzviumba mwemene zvimweni zvisizi kuwedzeredzwa, zvakangwara zvekufungidzira chero simba rekurova-ram rinokona kuiswapo. Heino pfungwa yakakosha; ngokuti munodikana kupeja ndaa iyi ngo njira inogutsa mwemene, kana kugara muri mutendi mbhera narini inga imwe yo zviro zvinothya maningi, asi haiwa zviro zvishoma zvokadi, zvingaita kuti peshe panowanika mu ndau yese yakanyorwa.

Unovona kuti mu ndau yakajairika yo kudhuvha yo Sperm Whale, mberi kwe soro rake kunokhombija ndege inoda kuita inga yakaema kamare ku mvura; unoona kuti chikamu chepasi chemberi iyo chinotenderera zvikuru kudzokera kumashure, kuti chipe nzvimbo yakawanda yekudzokera kune socket yakareba inogamuchira tsaya yepasi yakaita se boom; unovona kuti muromo uri pasi po soro kamare, ngo njira imweyo, kamare, inga kuti muromo wako uri pasi po cirebvu cenyu kamare. Uyezve unoona kuti whale haina mhino yekunze; uye kuti mhino ipi yaanayo—gomba rake remuromo—iri pamusoro pesoro rake; unovona kuti madziso ake no nzee zviri kumativi o soro rake, zvinoda kuguma pa cikamu cimwe pa zvitatu zvo kureba kwake kwese kubvira ku mberi. Ngokudaro, munodikana kunge mwaona zvino kuti mberi kwe soro re Sperm Whale i rusvingo rwakafa, rwakapofumara, rusina chiro chimwe chete kana kuoneka kwakapfava kwe muhlobo uri weshe.
