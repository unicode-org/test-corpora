Zvakanyadaro, wakabatirira pa kumuka kwavo, kunyazi kamare mvura yavo yo sure inodikana kunge yakamuita kuti anonoke, ngokuti pfupa jena kana kuzvimba pa muromo wake wakafara kwainga kwakatsemuka, kudai nge kuzvimba kunoitwa apo mafungu maviri anovenga anosangana. Muromo wake wainga wakapfupika, wakanonoka, zve waishanda maningi; kubuda ngekuvhunduka kwemhando ye gush, uye kuzvipedza muzvimedu zvakataturwa, zvinoteverwa ngekuvhunduka kunoshamisa kwepasi penyika mukati mwake, kwaiita inga kune kubuda kune imwe mugumo wake wakavigwa, zvichikonzera mvura iri kumashure kwake kuti ibhuye.

Kuti wakarashikirwa nge fin iyoyo muhondo, kana kuti wakabarwa asinayo, zvainga zvakaoma kubhuya.

“Ema zvishoma, chap mukuru, ndinozokupa sling ye ruoko irworwo rwakakuvadzwa,” wakachema Flask une utsinye, weinongedzera ku whale-line yainge iri pasinde naye.

Asi kudaro wakakheta kutanga kwekutanga kudai Derick ainge anazvo, kuti kunyazwi nge ushingi hwavo hweshe, waizokhombise kukunda mumujaho uyu, kudai pasina kutongwa kwakarurama kwakadzaka pamusoro pake nge gakanje rakabata banga re mufambisi wake we pakati pe ngarava. An instant more, uye zvikepe zveshe zvirongomuna zvainge zvakaita diagonically mu whale yakakurumidza kumuka, apo yakatambanuka kubva kwavari, kumativi maviri, kwaiva kuzvimba kunoputika kwaakaita.

Uye whale yakakurumidza kurega kurira, kwenguva yakati vakaramba vari mumafungiro iwayo, vachitya kupedza mutsara wakawanda, kunyange zvazvo nzvimbo yacho yaive ichitsvedza zvishoma. Asi kunyazi zvikepe zvakaderedzwa no kurashika ngo njira iyi, asi ndizvona “kubata” uku, inga zvinodanwa; uku kubatanidzwa ne barbs yakapinza yenyama yake inorarama kubva kumashure; izvi ndizvo zvinowanzotambudza Revhiatani kuti akurumidze kumuka zvakare kuti asangane nepfumo rakapinza revavengi vake. Asi kuti tisabhuya nge ngozi ye chirocho, zvinosisira kukarakadzwa kuti njira iyi ndiyo yakanakisisa nguva dzeshe ere; ngokuti zvinozwisisika basi kurangarira, kuti kudari hove yakarohwa ikagara pashi po mvura nguva yakareba, inozoremba maningi. Ngokuti, ngo ndava yo ndau yake yakakura maningi—mu sperm whale yakakura maningi ciro cicikakwanisi kuguma 2000 square feet—kumanikijwa ko mvura kwakakura maningi. Teshe tinoziya kuti kurema kwemuchadenga kunoshamisa sei isu pachedu tinoema pashi; kunyange pano, pamusoro-pasi, mumhepo; hino, mutoro wo whale, wakatakura pa musana wake mbiru inokwana mamita mazana maviri o gungwa wakakura maningi!
