I kare maber, kadi bed kumeno, ma miti pa bulu tye ka rweny; kit ma mwaka ki jami ma ki bolo tye ka mede kwede; ma calo tam miyo en ling mot; ki lok macek, ma calo goro ma mako Turk ma opong; lacen mar pi yot ki kit maber loko mar pi anyira; Ottoman wa odonyo i kwo ma pe twero timo gin mo, loko cwinye, ki miiyo tam, kwero lok goba, turo harem, ki dongo me doko lanen maber, cwiny ma otii, woto kene i kin meridians ki parallels kun waco lega ne, ki ciko Leviathan acel acel ki i kom bal ne me mar.

Kombedi, kit ma dul pa rec ma ki lwongo ni whales ki lwongo ni gang kwan, kit meno bene ladit ki ladit gang kwan ni ki ngeyo ni ladit kwan. Pi meno pe tye i kit ma rwate, kadi bed ni obedo gin ma yomo cwiny, ni inge cito i gang kwan en kikome, en omyero ocit i lobo mukene kun pwonyo pe gin ma en opwonyo kunu, ento pi mingo ne. Nyinge, lapwony, nen calo oa ki i nying ma kimiyo bot harem kikome, ento jo mogo gu tamo ni lacoo ma okwongo miyo nying rec ma ki lwongo ni Ottoman whale ni, omyera obed ni okwano buk pa Vidocq, ki ngeyo kome kene ni lapwony me gang kwan ma lacoo ni obedo i kare ma en pud tidi, ki ngo ma obedo pwony me tekwaro pa dano meno en opwonyo lutino kwan ne mogo.

Bedo kene ki bedo kene ma rec ma ki lwongo ni schoolmaster whale bedo kwede i kare ma en tye ka dongo, tye ada pi rec ma ki lwongo ni Sperm Whales ma mwaka gi dit. I wilobo weny, rec ma ki lwongo ni lone whale-ma calo Leviathan ma ki lwongo ni solitary Leviathan-nyutu ni obedo gin macon. Calo Daniel Boone ma tye ki yec ma ki lwongo ni moss-bearded, en pe obi bedo ki ngat mo cok kwede ento Nature kikome; ki en otero me bedo dako ne i dye tim me pii, ki en aye dako maber loyo, kadi bed ni en gwoko jami mapol ma kelo lworo.

Gang kwan ma tye ki coo matino ki matek, ma kiloko gii malo ni, miiyo apoka poka madit ki gang kwan ma ki lwongo ni harem. Pien kadi bed ni rec ma ki lwongo ni whales ma mon ni gi lworo, co matino, onyo forty-barrel-bulls, kit ma gi lwongo gi kwede, gin aye gi maro lweny loyo Leviathans weny, ki ma ki waco ni gi rac loyo me rwate kwed gi; ma pat ki rec ma wic gi tar, ma ki lwongo ni grizzled whales, ma pol kare gi rwate kwedi, ki magi gubi lweny kwedi calo jo maraco ma ki cwero cwiny gi pi two ma ki lwongo ni penal gout.

Gang kwan ma ki lwongo ni Forty-barrel-bull dit loyo gang kwan ma ki lwongo ni harem. Calo lwak pa lutino kwan ma mwaka gi tiino, gin gupong ki lweny, yomcwiny, ki tim maraco, ma gi tye ka ngwec i wilobo i rwom ma lamal, ma pe ki ngeyo, ma laco mo ma ryek ma twero gwoko gi ma calo latin awobi ma lakwo i Yale onyo Harvard. Gin cut cut gi weko lok man, ki ka romo adek me angwen gi dongo, gi poko, ki gi cito ka yenyo kabedo, ma en aye, harems.

Gin mukene ma pat i kin gang kwan pa coo ki mon tye ma nyutu kit pa coo ki mon. Waci ni i goyo ler ma ki lwongo ni Forty-barrel-bull- lacan marac! lurem ne weng gu weko en. Ento turo ngat mo ma tye i gang kwan pa harem, ki lurem ne gi kwang i kome ki lanyut me lworo, kare mukene gi bedo cok kwede ki pi kare malac, calo gin kikom gi me poto i kom ler.
