DUL KWAN ME 33. Gin ma ki lwongo ni Specksnyder.

Ma mako lutic ma i yeya ma ki lwongo ni whale-craft, man nen calo kabedo maber me keto jami ma pat pat i kom yeya, ma oa ki i bedo pa lutic ma gi lwongo ni harpooneer, dul ma pe ki ngeyo i kom yeya mukene ma pat ki yeya ma ki lwongo ni whale-fleet.

Kit ma pire tek kwede i kom tic pa latuku ma ki lwongo ni harpooneer ni ki nyutu ki lok ada, ni i acaki i Dutch Fishery macon, cencwari aryo ki ma kato meno, loc me yeya ma ki lwongo ni whale onongo pe tye i cing ngat ma dong ki lwongo ni ladit, ento onongo kipoko i kin en ki ladit ma ki lwongo ni Specksnyder. Lok man tere ni ngat ma kwanyo moo; tic kwede, kadi bed kumeno, i kare ma okato oweko odoko rom ki Chief Harpooneer. I kare meno, twero pa ladit me yeya onongo tye me doro yeya ki gwoko jami ducu; ma nongo tye i kom dul ma kwako mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ki jami ma mako ne weny, Specksnyder onyo Chief Harpooneer aye obedo laloc. I British Greenland Fishery, i te nying ma ki balo ni Specksioneer, ladit macon me Dutch ni pud ki gwoko, ento deyo ne macon ni ki dwoko piny. I kare ni en tye i rwom ma malo calo Harpooneer ma dit; ki ma calo meno, en tye ngat acel i kin lutic pa ladit mony ma rwom gi lapiny. Kadi bed kumeno, ma calo i kom kit maber pa lumony ma gi mako rec ma ki lwongo ni harpooneers, ber pa wot me mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whaling cung i kom, ki kit ma i American Fishery en pe obedo ladit ma pire tek i yeya keken, ento i te jami mogo (gwok i dyewor i kabedo ma ki mako iye rec) loc me yeya bene tye mere; pi meno lok me wibye madit me nam mito, ni en omyero obed pat ki coo ma tye i nyim yeya, ki omyero ki nyute calo ngat ma loyo tic gi; kadi bed ni kare weny, ki bot gi, ki ngeyo calo ngat ma rom ki gii.

Kombedi, apoka poka madit ma tye i kin opici ki dano i nam, tye eni- ngat mukwongo bedo i ngeye, ngat me agiiki anyim. Pi meno, i yeya ma ki lwongo ni whale-whale ki lucat wil, luwil gi bedo ki kabedo gi ki ladit me yeya; ki bene, i pol pa lumony me Amerika ma gi mako rec ma ki lwongo ni harpooneers gi bedo i nge yeya. Man tere ni, gi camo cam gi i ot pa ladit me yeya, ki gi nino i kabedo ma gi lok kwede labongo rwate.

Kadi bed ni kare mabor me wot me mako rec ma ki lwongo ni Southern whaling voyage (ma obedo wot mabor loyo ma dano otimo i kare ni onyo ma dano gu timo), peko ma twero bino iye, ki dul dano ma gi maro jami ma tye i kin kampuni, ma gin weng, ma malo onyo ma piny, gi jenge i kom magoba gi, pe i kom cul ma ki moko, ento i kom gum gi, kacel ki ryeko gi, ki tic matek; kadi bed ni jami eni weng i kare mogo gi kelo pwony ma nok loyo pa lucat wil; ento, pi itam kit ma calo jo me ot macon me Mesopotamian lumony ma ki lwongo ni whalemen ni, i kare mogo macon, gibedo kacel; pi jami eni weng, jami ma woko ma nen maber, ma nok loyo, me dul ma ki lwongo ni quarter-deck ni pe ki dwoko piny, ki pe ki kwanyo gi woko. Ki adaa, pol pa yeya ma ki lwongo ni Nantucket ma in ibi neno ladit me yeya ne tye ka nyutu rwom me yeya ne ki yomcwiny ma pe ki twero poro ki yeya mo keken me mony; ku, kwanyo woro ma nen calo en oruko bongo macol ma nen calo laloc, ki pi bongo ma nen marac loyo.

Ki kadi bed ni i kin dano weny ladit wi yeya ma ki lwongo ni Pequod onwongo obedo ngat ma pe ki miiyo tam ma kit meno; ki kadi bed ni woro acel keken ma en onongo, onongo obedo woro ma pe nen, ma oyot; kadi bed ni en onongo pe mito ngat mo me kwanyo war ki i tyene ma peya odonyo i kom cel; ki kadi bed ni kare mogo onongo tye ma, pi jami mogo ma pat pat ma kubbe ki jami ma bitimme i anyim ma myero ki lok iye matut, en oloko kwedgi i lok ma pe nen, kadi bed ni obedo lok me mwolo nyo i terrorem , nyo kit mukene; ento kadi wa ladit mony Ahab onongo pe ngeyo kit ma nam tye kwede ki kit ma en tiyo kwede.

Dok bene, gwok nyo, pe bibedo me niang i agikkine, ni i nge kit ki tic magi, kit ma onongo tye kwede, en kare mukene onongo oumo kome kene; ki tiyo kwed gi pi jami mukene ki pi jami ma pi ki ngeyo ma pat ki ma onongo ki tamo me cobo ne. Gin ma ki lwongo ni sultanism me obwone, ma onongo tye i rwom maber onongo pe onyute; ki i kin jami ma kit meno, sultanism acel-lu odoko gin ma ki ngeyo i loc ma pe kitwero juku ne. Pi bedo rwom me ryeko pa lacoo gin ma en miito, en pe twero tero rwom me tic, ma tye i kom dano mukene, labongo kony pa jami mogo ma ki ngeyo ki woko, kare weny, i kom gi, ma nok ki ma rwate. Man aye, ma pi naka gwoko luloc me ada pa Lubanga ki i lok me lobo; ki weko deyo ma malo ma yamo ni twero miyo, bot coo ma gi doko dano ma gi ngene i kom rwom me kwo gi ma pe gik i kom dano ma ki yero ma ki kano, ma ka bedo ki rwom ma malo ma pe ki akalakala i kom dano mapol. Kit maber ma dit ni tye i jami ma nok ni ka lok goba me wibye oketo gi, ma i kare mogo me loc kadi wa bot dano ma laming gi miyo teko. Ento ka, ma calo i lok pa Nicholas the Czar, loc ma ki lwongo ni geographic empire orumo wic pa laloc; lacen, dul ler ma ki lwongo ni plebeian gu guro piny ma peya ki keto yub madit. Dok bene, laco lok ma mako lok me arem cwiny ma obi nyutu kwo pa dano ma pe ki twero loyo i yoo ma opore ki rwom ma lamal, pe obi weko lok mo, ma pire tek i tic me goyo cal, ma calo ma kiloko iye i kare ni.

Ento Ahab, latela na, pwod tye ka wot i nyima i Nantucket ma nen marac ki goro; ki i lok man ma mako luloc ki rwodi, pe myero a kan ni atye ka timo gin mo ki lakwo ma lacan ma calo en; ki, pi meno, jami weny ma nen maber ki odi ki kwero bota. Oh, Akab! gin ma obi bedo madit i komi, omyera ki kwany ki i polo, ki ki keto i pii matut, ki ki nyute i yamo ma pe ki kom!

DUL KWAN ME 35. Wii yat.

I kare ma piny onongo tye maber, ma i kare ma atye ka loko ki lutic mukene me yeya, yeya na mukwongo obino.

I kom pol pa rec ma ki lwongo ni whalemen me Amerika, wi yeya ni ki tiyo kwede i kare acel ki yeya ni tye ka aa ki i dog nam; kadi bed ni en tye ki mairo alip apar wiye abic, ki ma kato meno, me ngwec ki yeya ma peya oo i kabedone me ngwec. Kombedi, ma calo tic me cung i wi yeya, i dog nam onyo i pii, obedo gin macon ki ma mit, wek wa lok kany. Atamo ni, jo ma gucung i kom yeya macon ni gubedo jo Ejipt macon; pien, i kwedo na weng, anongo ni pe tye mo ma okato gi. Pien kadi bed ni kwaro gi, lugedo me Babel, omyera obed ni, ki cel gi, onongo tye ki yub me gero yeya mabor loyo i Asia weny, onyo Afrika bene; ento (ma peya ki keto motoka me agiiki) ma calo got madit ni ma ki yubu ki got ni ki twero waco ni okato ki i wi got, i yamo magwar me akemo pa Lubanga; pi meno, pe watwero miyo bot lugedo magi me Babel bedo mukwongo loyo jo Ejipt. Ki ni jo Ejipt onwongo gubedo rok ma gicung i kom yeya, obedo lok ma jenge i kom niyee pa ludiro ma kwano lok kom jami macon, ni pyramid mukwongo ni ki cako pi yub me kwan i kom jami ma tye i dan polo: lok ma ki cwako ki kit ma ki yubu kwede dul angwen me gedo ni; ma, ki tingo tyen gi mabor, ludiro macon ma kwano lok kom lakalatwe ni onongo gi maro cito malo i wi got, ki wero wer pi lakalatwe manyen; kadi bed ni jami ma ki neno i yeya manyen ni gi wer pi yeya, onyo rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ma tye ka nen. I Saint Stylites, lakristo ma ki ngeyo i kare macon, ma ogero got mabor i dye tim ki otero kare me kwo ne weny i wiye, kun tingo cam ne ki i ngom ki jami lweny; i kome wan tye ki labol maber me ngat ma cung matek i kom yeya; ma onongo pe ki bi ryemo ki kama en tye iye pi yamo ma ngiic, kot, pee, onyo pee; ento ki tek cwiny me lweny ki jami weng nio wa i agiki, otoo i gang ticce. I kom jami ma ki keto i kom yat me kare ni wan tye ki jami ma pe kwo; dano ma ki yubu ki got, nyonyo, ki mola; ma, kadi bed ni gitwero lweny ki yamo magwar, pud gitye ki kero me wero wer ka gunongo gin mo ma pe nen.
