DUL KWAN ME 60. Labol.

Ki lok ma mako mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whale ma ki bi tito ne cok cok ni, kacel ki me niang maber i kom jami ma kit meno ma ki nyutu i kabedo mukene, atye kany me lok i kom rec ma ki lwongo ni whale-line ma mako jami me aura, ma pol kare rac.

Gin ma ki tiyo kwede i tic me mako rec onwongo obedo yat maber loyo, ma ki keto iye tar manok, ma pe ki keto iye, ma calo tol ma ki tiyo kwede; pien kadi bed ni tar, kit ma ki tiyo kwede, miyo yat ma ki lwongo ni hemp bedo yot bot layub tol, ki bene miyo tol ni bedo yot bot latic me yeya me tic kwede i yeya; ento, pi ni wel ma rwate keken ni obi medo teko pa ler ma ki lwongo ni whale-line pi rwate ma omyera ki ket iye; ento kit ma pol pa lutic me yeya gi tye ka cako pwonyo kwede, tar pe medo teko pa tol ni, kadi bed ni twero miine bedo ma rwate ki ryeny.

I mwaka manok ma okato ni, tol ma ki lwongo ni Manilla rope ni i tic me mako rec i Amerika oloko hemp ma calo gin ma ki tiyo kwede me mako rec ma ki lwongo ni whale-lines; pien, kadi bed ni pe tek calo hemp, en tek, ki yom ki goro; ki an abi medo (pien tye æsthetics i jami weny), tye maber ki rwate ki yeya, ma loyo hemp. Hemp obedo gin macol, macol, ma calo pa Indian; ento Manilla tye calo Circassian ma tye ki yer wiye ma ryal.

Rec ma ki lwongo ni whale-line ni tye ki dul aryo me adek pa inci acel keken. I neno mukwongo, pi ibi tamo ni tek kit ma tye kwede kikome. Ki temo, dul ne acel ki pyerabic acel acel obi keto pek pa paun miya acel ki pyera aryo; wek tol ni weng obi tingo pek ma rom ki ton adek. Tye marac makato; pien pe itwero bedo labongo ngwec i kin jami ma kelo peko ni, pien yeya tye ka ngwec calo gin ma ki keto iye, ki in ki keto i yoo acel ki mukene, labongo miyo ngec mo manok; ki keken ki rwom me ngwec ma rwate ki miti ki tic, in itwero bwot ki bedo Mazeppa, ki ngwec ki kama ceng ma neno jami weng kikome pe twero turo in woko.

Dok: ma calo ling ma nen calo tye anyim ki tito pi yamo magwar, twero bedo rac loyo yamo magwar kikome; pien, ada, kuc obedo gin ma umu ki umo yamo magwar; ki tye kwede i kome kene, ma calo muduku ma nen calo pe kelo peko ni tye ki buru ma neko dano, ki odilo, ki jami ma obale; ki dong yweyo maber pa dul ma ki lwongo ni line, ma en tye ka ngwec mot mot i kom lutic ma tye ka ngwec ma peya ki kelo i tuku kikome-man obedo gin ma kelo lworo me ada makato gin mo keken ma mako lok man ma kelo peko ni. Ento pingo iwaco lok mukene? Coo weny gi kwo i kin ler ma ki lwongo ni whale-lines. Gin weng ki nywalo gi ki jami ma ki lwongo ni halters i ngut gi; ento ka ki mako gi i lok me too ma oyot, ma oyot oyot, en aye dano gi niang peko me kwo ma ling, ma pe nen, ma tye kare ducu. Ki ka in ibedo ngat ma ngeyo lok ma mako tam, kadi bed ni ibedo i yeya ma ki lwongo ni whale-boat, pi ibi winyo lworo mo keken, ma loyo ka ibedo i nyim mac me otyeno ki poker, ento pi harpoon, i ngeti.
