ESSUULA 32. Ensengekera y’ebika (Cetology).

Edda twasindikibwa n’obuvumu ku buziba; naye mu bbanga ttono tujja kubula mu bunene bwayo obutali ku mwalo, obutaliiko mwalo. Ere ekyo okutuukirira; ere ekisenge kya Pequod ekirimu omuddo nga kiyiringisibwa ku mabbali n’ebisenge eby’ekika kya leviathan ebiriko barnacled; ku ntandikwa kirungi nnyo okufaayo ku nsonga kumpi ezeetaagisa okutegeera obulungi okusiima okubikkulirwa okw’enjawulo ennyo okwa leviathanic n’okujuliza okw’engeri zonna okulina okugoberera.

It is some systematized exhibition of the whale mu kika kye ekigazi, kye nnandibadde kati fain okuteeka mu maaso gammwe. Naye si mulimu mwangu. Ensengeka y’ebintu ebikola akavuyo, tewali kisingako wano essayed. Wuliriza ab’obuyinza abasinga obulungi n’abasembyeyo bye bataddewo.

“Tewali ttabi lya Zoology lyenyigiddemu nnyo ng’eryo eriyitibwa Cetology,” bw’atyo Captain Scoresby, AD 1820 bw’agamba.

“Si kigendererwa kyange, singa kyali mu buyinza bwange, okuyingira mu kubuuliriza ku ngeri entuufu ey’okugabanyaamu ebiwuka ebiyitibwa cetacea mu bibinja n’amaka * * * Okutabulwa okujjuvu kuliwo mu bannabyafaayo b’ensolo eno” (sperm whale), bw’atyo Surgeon Beale, AD 1839 bw’agamba.

“Obutasaana kukola kunoonyereza kwaffe mu mazzi agatategeerekeka.” “Ekibikka ekitayitamu nga kibikka okumanya kwaffe ku biwuka ebiyitibwa cetacea.” “Ennimiro esaasaanidde amaggwa.” “Bino byonna ebiraga nti tebituukiridde naye bikola okututulugunya ffe abakugu mu by’obutonde.”

Bw’otyo yogera ku nnyanja ekika kya whale, Cuvier omukulu, ne John Hunter, ne Lesson, amataala ago ag’eby’ensolo n’ensengekera y’omubiri. Wadde kiri kityo, wadde ng’okumanya okwa nnamaddala kutono, naye ebitabo bingi; era bwe kityo mu ddaala eritali limu, n’eby’obutonde (cetology), oba ssaayansi w’ennyanja ekika kya whale. Bangi be basajja, abato n’abanene, abakadde n’abapya, abakubi b’ettaka n’abavubi, abalina okutwalira awamu oba mu kitono, ebiwandiiko ku nnyanja ekika kya whale. Dduka ku bitonotono: —Abawandiisi ba Baibuli; Aristotle, era nga ye; Pliny, era nga ye; Aldrovandi ne banne; Sir Thomas Browne, omuwandiisi w’ebitabo; Gesner, ne banne; Ray; Linnæus, era nga ye; Rondeletius nga ye; Willoughby, ne banne; Kiragala; Artedi, ne banne; Sibbald, ne banne; Brisson, ne banne; Marten, ne banne; Lacépède ow’ekika kya Lacépède; Bonneterre, ne banne; Desmarest, omusajja omulala; Baron Cuvier ne banne; Frederick Cuvier ne munne; John Hunter, omuyimbi; Owen, ne banne; Scoresby nga; Beale; Bennett, ne banne; J. Ross Browne, omuwandiisi w’ebitabo; Omuwandiisi w’ekitabo Miriam Coffin; Olmstead, ne banne; ne Rev. T. Cheever. Naye ku kigendererwa ekisembayo eky’okugatta ebintu bino byonna bye bawandiise, ebitundu ebijuliziddwa waggulu bijja kulaga.

Ku mannya agali ku lukalala luno olw’abawandiisi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale, abo bokka abagoberera Owen be baalaba ennyanja ekika kya whale enamu; era naye omu ku bo yali muvuzi wa harpooneer ow’ekikugu ddala era nga muvuzi wa whale. Ntegeeza Kapiteeni Scoresby. Ku nsonga ey’enjawulo eya Greenland oba right-whale, ye mukulu asinga obulungi aliwo. Naye Scoresby yali talina ky’amanyi era talina ky’ayogera ku nnyonyi ennene ey’ensigo, bw’ogigeraageranya n’ennyanja Greenland whale kumpi tekisaanira kwogerwako. Era wano kigambibwa, nti Greenland whale muwambi ku ntebe y’ennyanja. Mu ngeri yonna si y’asinga obunene mu nnyanja ekika kya whale. Naye, olw’okukulembeza okumala ebbanga eddene kw’ebyo bye yayogera, n’obutamanya obw’amaanyi obwali, okutuusa emyaka nga nsanvu emabega, obwateekamu ennyanja sperm-whale eyali emanyiddwa ennyo oba eyali temanyiddwa ddala mu kiseera ekyo, era obutamanya obwo n’okutuusa leero bukyafuga mu byonna okuggyako ebitonotono ebya ssaayansi eby’okuwummulamu n’emyalo gy’ennyanja whale; okuwamba kuno kubadde kujjuvu mu buli ngeri. Okwogera kumpi ku bigambo byonna ebya leviathanic allusions mu bayimbi abakulu ab’ennaku eziyise, kijja kukumatiza nti Greenland whale, nga terina muvuganya omu, gye bali kabaka w’ennyanja. Naye ku nkomerero ekiseera kituuse eky’okulangirira okupya. Ono ye Charing Cross; muwulire mmwe! abantu abalungi bonna, —ennyanja Greenland whale egobeddwa mu ntebe, —kati sperm whale ennene efugira!

Waliwo ebitabo bibiri byokka mu kubeerawo nga n’akatono byefuula ebiteeka sperm whale enamu mu maaso go, ate mu kiseera kye kimu, mu ddaala erisinga ewala ne bituuka ku buwanguzi mu kugezaako. Ebitabo ebyo bya Beale ne Bennett; bombi mu kiseera kyabwe baali abalongoosa ku mmeeri z’e Bungereza eza South-Sea whale-ships, era bombi abasajja abatuufu era abeesigika. Ekintu ekisooka ekikwata ku sperm whale okusangibwa mu voliyumu zaabwe kiteekwa okuba nga kitono; naye okutuuka we kigenda, kya mutindo mulungi nnyo, wadde ng’okusinga kikoma ku kunnyonnyola kwa ssaayansi. Kyokka, n’okutuusa kati, sperm whale, eya ssaayansi oba ey’ebitontome, ewangaala nga tetuukiridde mu bitabo byonna. Okusinga wala ennyanja ekika kya whale endala zonna eziyiggibwa, eyiye bulamu obutawandiikiddwa.

Kati ebika by’ennyanja ekika kya whale eby’enjawulo byetaaga ekika ky’okusengeka okujjuvu okumanyiddwa ennyo, bwe kiba nga kyangu okulambika mu kiseera kino, oluvannyuma okujjibwamu mu bitongole byayo byonna abakozi abaddako. Nga tewali musajja asinga agenda mu maaso kutwala nsonga eno mu ngalo, wano nwaayo kaweefube wange omubi. Sisuubiza kintu kyonna kijjuvu; kubanga ekintu kyonna eky’obuntu ekiteeberezebwa okuba ekijjuvu, olw’ensonga eyo yennyini kiteekwa okuba nga kikyamu awatali nsobi. Sijja kwefuula nga nnina ennyonyola ya ddakiika ey’omubiri ku bika eby’enjawulo, oba —mu kifo kino waakiri —ku nnyonyola yonna. Ekigendererwa kyange wano kwe kukola pulojekiti yokka ebbago ly’ensengeka y’eby’obutonde (systematization of cetology). Nze mukubi wa pulaani, so si muzimbi.

Waliwo ebimu ebisookerwako okusenga.

Ekisooka: Embeera etali nkakasa, etali nnywevu eya ssaayansi ono ow’Eby’Ensi (Cetology) eri mu kisenge kyennyini ekikakasibwa ensonga, nti mu bitundu ebimu kikyali nsonga ya kukubaganya birowoozo oba ennyanja ekika kya whale eba kyennyanja. Mu kitabo kye ekiyitibwa System of Nature, AD 1776, Linnæus alangirira nti, “Njawula ennyanja ekika kya whale ku byennyanja.” Naye okusinziira ku kye mmanyi, nkimanyi nti okutuukira ddala mu mwaka gwa 1850, shark ne shad, alewives ne herring, nga zimenya ekiragiro kya Linnæus ekitegeerekeka, zaasangibwa nga zigabanya obuyinza bw’ennyanja ze zimu ne Leviathan.

Ensonga Linnæus kwe yandifudde yandigobye ennyanja ekika kya whale mu mazzi, agamba bw’ati: “Olw’omutima gwazo ogubuguma ogw’amaaso abiri, amawuggwe gazo, ebikowe by’amaaso ebitambula, amatu gaabwe agalimu ebituli, penem intrantem feminam mammis lactantem,” n’okusembayo, “ex lege naturæ jure meritoque.” Bino byonna nabiwaayo eri mikwano gyange Simeon Macey ne Charley Coffin, ab’e Nantucket, bombi baali ba messmate zange mu lugendo olumu, era ne beegatta mu ndowooza nti ensonga ezaateekebwawo zonna zaali tezimala. Charley mu ngeri ey’obugwenyufu yalaga nti baali ba humbug.

Kimanyiddwa nti, nga nvaako okuyomba kwonna, nkwata ettaka eddungi ery’omulembe nti ennyanja ekika kya whale, ne mpita Yona omutukuvu okumpagira. Ekintu kino ekikulu kyatereera, ensonga eddako eri nti, mu ngeri ki ey’omunda ennyanja ekika kya whale gye yawukana ku byennyanja ebirala. Waggulu, Linnæus akuwadde ebintu ebyo. Naye mu bufunze, ze zino: amawuggwe n’omusaayi ogubuguma; so nga, ebyennyanja ebirala byonna tebirina mawuggwe era nga birimu omusaayi omunnyogovu.

Ekiddako: tunaannyonnyola tutya ennyanja ekika kya whale, nga tuyita mu bintu bye eby’ebweru ebyeyoleka, bwe tutyo mu ngeri eyeeyolese okugiwandiikako akabonero ak’ebiseera byonna ebijja? N’olwekyo, okubeera omumpi, ennyanja ekika kya whale kye kyennyanja ekifulumya amazzi nga kirina omukira ogw’okwebungulula . Eyo gy’olina. Wadde nga ekoleddwa, ennyonyola eyo eva mu kufumiitiriza okugaziyiziddwa. Walrus efuuwa nnyo ng’ennyanja ekika kya whale, naye walrus si kyenyanja, kubanga ebeera mu mazzi n’omu ttaka. Naye ekigambo ekisembayo mu nnyonyola kikyali kikakafu nnyo, nga bwe kigattibwako n’ekisooka. Kumpi omuntu yenna ateekwa okuba nga yakiraba nti ebyennyanja byonna abakugu mu by’ettaka bye bamanyi tebirina mukira gwa fulaati, wabula omukira ogwesimbye, oba ogugenda waggulu ne wansi. So nga mu byennyanja ebifuuwa omukira, wadde nga guyinza okuba n’enkula y’emu, bulijjo gutwala ekifo ekiwanvu.

Olw’ennyonnyola eyo waggulu ku kiki ennyanja ekika kya whale, siggyako n’akatono mu sseruganda wa leviathanic ekitonde kyonna eky’omu nnyanja okutuusa kati ekimanyiddwa n’ennyanja whale aba Nantucketers abasinga okumanya; wadde, ku ludda olulala, okuyunga nayo ebyennyanja byonna okutuusa kati ebitunuulirwa mu buyinza ng’eby’amawanga amalala.* N’olwekyo, ebyennyanja byonna ebitono, ebifuuwa, era ebirina omukira ogw’okwebungulula birina okuteekebwa mu pulaani eno ey’oku ttaka ey’Eby’Ensi. Kati, n’olwekyo, wajja enjawulo ennene ez’ekifo kyonna ekikyaza ennyanja ekika kya whale.

*Nkimanyi nti okutuukira ddala mu kiseera kino, ebyennyanja ebiyitibwa Lamatins ne Dugongs (Pig-fish and Sow-fish of the Coffins of Nantucket) biyingizibwamu abakugu mu by’obutonde bangi mu nnyanja ekika kya whale. Naye engeri ebyennyanja bino eby’embizzi gye biri seti ezikola amaloboozi, ezinyoomebwa, ng’okusinga zikweka mu mimwa gy’emigga, era nga zirya omuddo omubisi, n’okusingira ddala nga tezifuuwa mafuta, nneegaana ebiwandiiko byabwe nga ennyanja ekika kya whale; era nga babawadde paasipooti zaabwe okuva mu Bwakabaka bwa Cetology.

Ekisooka: Okusinziira ku bunene ngabanya ennyanja ekika kya whale mu EBITABO bisatu ebisookerwako (ebigabanyizibwamu ESSUULA), era bino bijja kubitegeera byonna, ebitono n’ebinene.

I. ENNYONYI YA FOLIO WHALE; II. ennyonyi ekika kya OCTAVO WHALE; III. ekika kya DUODECIMO WHALE.

Nga ekika kya FOLIO nyanjula Sperm Whale ; wa OCTAVO, aba Grampus ; wa DUODECIMO, ekika kya Porpoise .

EBIKWATA KU BIKOZESEBWA. Mu bino nze wano mulimu essuula zino wammanga: —I. Ensolo ekika kya Sperm Whale ; II. ennyonyi ya Right Whale ; III. ennyonyi ekika kya Fin-Back Whale ; IV. ennyonyi ekika kya Hump-backed Whale ; V. ennyonyi ekika kya Razor Back Whale ; VI. ennyonyi ekika kya Sulphur Bottom Whale .

EKITABO I. ( Folio ), ESSUULA I. ( Sperm Whale ).—Whale eno, mu Lungereza olw’edda emanyiddwa mu ngeri etategeerekeka nga Trumpa whale, ne Physeter whale, ne Anvil Headed whale, ye Cachalot y’Abafaransa mu kiseera kino, ne Pottsfich y’Abagirimaani, ne Macrocephalus y’Ebigambo Ebiwanvu. Awatali kubuusabuusa, ye muntu asinga obunene mu nsi yonna; ekisinga okutiisa mu nnyanja ekika kya whale zonna okusisinkana; ekisinga obukulu mu nsonga; era ekisembayo, ewala nnyo ekisinga okuba eky’omuwendo mu by’obusuubuzi; ye nga ye kitonde kyokka ekintu ekyo eky’omuwendo, spermaceti, mwe kifunibwa. Ebintu bye byonna eby’enjawulo, mu bifo ebirala bingi, bijja kugaziwa. Okusinga ku linnya lye kati kye nnina okukola. Mu by’ennimi, tekiba kya busirusiru. Ebyasa ebimu emabega, nga Sperm whale kumpi temanyiddwa ddala mu ngeri yaayo entuufu, era ng’amafuta ge gafunibwa mu butanwa okuva mu byennyanja ebyasibirwa; mu biseera ebyo, kyandirabise ng’ensigo (spermaceti) abantu baali bateekwa okuba nga zisibuka mu kitonde ekifaanagana n’ekyo ekyali kimanyiddwa mu Bungereza mu kiseera ekyo nga Greenland oba Right Whale. Yali kirowoozo era, nti spermaceti eno yennyini ye yali enseko eyo eyanguya eya Greenland Whale ennukuta esooka ey’ekigambo ky’eyogera butereevu. Mu biseera ebyo, era, spermaceti yali ntono nnyo, nga tekozesebwa ku kitangaala, wabula ng’ekizigo n’eddagala lyokka. Yali ya kubeera na ddagala lyokka nga ensangi zino ogula ounce ya rhubarb. Nga bwe ndowooza, mu bbanga, obutonde obw’amazima obwa spermaceti bwe bwamanyibwa, erinnya lyayo eryasooka lyali likyakuumibwa abasuubuzi; awatali kubuusabuusa okwongera ku muwendo gwayo olw’endowooza ey’amakulu ennyo mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa olw’ebbula lyayo. Era bwe kityo erinnya eryo ku nkomerero liteekwa okuba nga lyatuuka okuweebwa ennyanja ekika kya whale mwe yava ddala spermaceti eno.

EKITABO I. ( Folio ), ESSUULA II. ( Right Whale ).—Mu ngeri emu eno y’esinga okuweebwa ekitiibwa mu leviathans, nga ye yasooka okuyigganyizibwa buli kiseera abantu. Kivaamu ekintu ekimanyiddwa ennyo nga whalebone oba baleen; n’amafuta agamanyiddwa mu ngeri ey’enjawulo nga “amafuta g’ennyanja ekika kya whale,” ekintu eky’omutindo ogwa wansi mu by’obusuubuzi. Mu bavubi, ayitibwa awatali kusosola n’ebitiibwa bino byonna: The Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Greenland Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Black Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Great Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Whale Entuufu; ennyonyi ya Right Whale. Waliwo okuzibuwalirwa okunene ku bikwata ku bika by’ebisolo ebibatizibwa bwe bityo mu bungi. Kati olwo ennyanja ekika kya whale kye ki, kye nteka mu kika eky’okubiri ekya Folios zange? Ye Mysticetus Omukulu ow’abakugu mu by’obutonde Abazungu; ennyonyi ekika kya Greenland Whale ey’abavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale Abangereza; Baleine Ordinaire ow’abavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale mu Bufalansa; envubu ya Growlands Walfish ey’Abasweden. Ye whale okumala ebyasa ebisukka mu bibiri emabega ebadde eyiggibwa Abadaaki n’Abangereza mu nnyanja za Arctic; ye whale abavubi b’Abamerika gye baludde nga bagoberera mu nnyanja Buyindi, ku Brazil Banks, ku Nor’ West Coast, n’ebitundu ebirala eby’enjawulo eby’ensi, bye baalonda Right Whale Cruising Grounds.

Abamu beefuula abalaba enjawulo wakati w’ennyanja Greenland whale ey’Abangereza n’ey’Abamerika eya ddyo. Naye bakkiriziganya ddala mu bintu byabwe byonna ebikulu; era tewannaba kwanjulwa nsonga emu enkakafu kwe tuyinza okusinziirako enjawulo ey’amaanyi. Okuyita mu kugabanyaamu okutaggwaawo nga kwesigamiziddwa ku njawulo ezisinga obutakoma, ebitongole ebimu eby’ebyafaayo by’obutonde mwe bifuuka ebizibu ennyo mu ngeri egoba. Whale eya ddyo ejja kujjanjabwa awalala mu buwanvu obumu, nga twogera ku kunnyonnyola sperm whale.

EKITABO I. ( Folio ), ESSUULA III. ( Fin-Back ).—Wansi w’omutwe guno mbalirira ekisolo ekinene ennyo, nga kituumiddwa amannya ag’enjawulo aga Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, ne Long-John, ekibadde kirabibwa kumpi mu buli nnyanja era nga kitera okuba ennyanja ekika kya whale ennyonyi yaayo ey’ewala etera ennyo okunnyonnyolwa abasaabaze abasala Atlantic, mu New York packet-tracks. Mu buwanvu bw’atuukako, ne mu baleen ye, Fin-back eringa ennyanja ekika kya right whale, naye nga ya less portly girth, ate nga ya langi enzirugavu, esemberera omuzeyituuni. Emimwa gye eminene giraga ekifaananyi ekiringa waya, ekikolebwa ebizimba ebiwanvu ebiserengese eby’enviiri ennene. Ekintu ekinene eky’enjawulo kye, ekiwaawaatiro, mw’aggya erinnya lye, kitera okuba ekintu ekyeyoleka. Ekiwaawaatiro kino kiwanvu ffuuti nga ssatu oba nnya, nga kikula mu vertikal okuva mu kitundu eky’emabega eky’omugongo, nga kya ngeri ya nkoona, era nga kiriko enkomerero ensongovu ennyo. Ne bwe kiba nti ekitundu ekirala ekitono eky’ekitonde ekyo tekirabika, ekiwaawaatiro kino ekyetongodde, oluusi, kijja kulabibwa nga kifuluma bulungi okuva kungulu. Ennyanja bw’eba ekkakkamu mu kigero, era ng’eriko akabonero katono n’amayengo ag’enkulungo, era ng’ekiwawaatiro kino ekiringa gnomon kiyimirira ne kisuula ebisiikirize ku ngulu eriko ennyiriri, kiyinza bulungi okulowoozebwa nti enzirugavu ey’amazzi egyetoolodde eringa ekintu ekifaananako ennamba, nga kuliko sitayiro yaayo n’ennyiriri z’essaawa eziwunya amayengo. Ku Akazi-dial eyo ekisiikirize kitera okudda emabega. Fin-Back si ya mukwano. Alabika nga mukyayi wa whale, anti abasajja abamu bwe bakyawa abantu. Ensonyi nnyo; bulijjo okugenda nga bali bokka; okulinnya ku mazzi mu ngeri etasuubirwa mu mazzi agasinga ewala era agasinga okubeera amazibu; ennyonyi ye egolokofu era emu empanvu ng’esituka ng’effumu eddene erya misanthropic ku lusenyi olutaliiko bimera; ekirabo ky’amaanyi ag’ekitalo n’embiro mu kuwuga, okusomooza byonna ebiriwo kati okuva eri omuntu; leviathan ono alabika nga Kayini eyagobebwa era atawangulwa ow’ekika kye, ng’alina akabonero ke sitayiro eyo ku mugongo gwe. Okuva ku kuba ne baleen mu kamwa ke, Fin-Back oluusi eyingizibwa ne whale eya ddyo, mu kika eky’enzikiriziganya ekiyitibwa Whalebone whales , kwe kugamba, whale ezirina baleen. Ku nnyanja zino eziyitibwa Whalebone whale, kyandirabise nga waliwo ebika ebiwerako, naye ebisinga obungi ku byo tebimanyiddwa nnyo. Ennyonyi ekika kya whale ez’ennyindo empanvu n’ennyanja ekika kya beaked whales; ennyanja ekika kya pike-headed whale; ennyanja ekika kya whale ezikubiddwa mu bibinja; under-jawed whales ne rostrated whales, ge mannya g’abavubi abalina ebika ebitonotono.

Mu bikwatagana n’ekigambo kino eky’okuyita “Whalebone whales,” kikulu nnyo okwogera, nti wadde ng’entuuma ng’eyo eyinza okuba ennyangu mu kwanguyiza okwogera ku kika ky’ennyanja ekika kya whale, naye nga bwereere okugezaako okugabanya mu ngeri entegeerekeka eya Leviathan, nga yeesigamiziddwa ku baleen ye, oba hump, oba fin, oba amannyo; wadde ng’ebitundu ebyo ebiteekeddwako akabonero oba ebifaananyi byeyoleka bulungi nnyo birabika nga bituukiridde bulungi okusobola okusasula omusingi gw’enkola eya bulijjo ey’Eby’Ensi okusinga enjawulo endala zonna ez’omubiri ezitali zimu, envubu, mu bika byayo, z’eyanjula. Kati olwo otya? Ensigo za baleen, hump, back-fin, n’amannyo; bino bye bintu eby’enjawulo byabyo ebibunye awatali kusosola mu bika byonna eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale, awatali kulowooza kwonna ku kiki ekiyinza okuba obutonde bw’ensengekera yazo mu bintu ebirala era ebikulu ennyo. Bwe kityo, sperm whale ne humpbacked whale, buli emu erina ekiwujjo; naye eyo okufaanagana we kukoma. Olwo, eno y’emu ekika kya humpbacked whale ne Greenland whale, buli emu ku zino erina baleen; naye eyo nate okufaanagana kukoma. Era kye kimu n’ebitundu ebirala ebyogeddwako waggulu. Mu bika by’ennyanja ekika kya whale ez’enjawulo, zikola okugatta ng’okwo okutali kwa bulijjo; oba, mu mbeera y’omu ku bo eyeekutudde, okweyawula okutali kwa bulijjo ng’okwo; nga okusoomooza ddala enkola zonna ez’awamu ezikoleddwa ku musingi ng’ogwo. Ku lwazi luno buli omu ku ba whale-naturalists yayawukanye.

Naye kiyinza okulowoozebwa nti, mu bitundu eby’omunda eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale, mu nsengeka ye ey’omubiri —ewo, waakiri, tujja kusobola okukuba ensengeka entuufu. Nedda; okugeza, kintu ki ekiri mu nsengeka y’ennyanja Greenland whale ekisinga okukwata ennyo okusinga baleen ye? Naye tulabye nti okusinziira ku baleen ye tekisoboka kugabanya bulungi nnyonyi ya Greenland whale. Era bw’okka mu byenda bya leviathans ez’enjawulo, lwaki eyo tojja kusanga njawulo kitundu kya makumi ataano nga bwe kiri eri omutegesi ng’ebyo eby’ebweru ebyabalibwa edda. Olwo kiki ekisigaddewo? tewali kirala wabula okukwata ennyanja ekika kya whale mu mubiri, mu voliyumu yazo yonna ey’eddembe, n’okuzisunsulamu n’obuvumu bwe zityo. Era eno y’enkola ya Bibliographical wano ekwatibwa; era ye yokka eyinza okutuuka ku buwanguzi, kubanga yokka y’esobola okukolebwa. Okugenda mu maaso.

EKITABO I. ( Folio ) ESSUULA IV. ( Hump Back ).—Envubu eno etera okulabibwa ku lubalama lw’ennyanja mu bukiikakkono bwa Amerika. Abadde atera okukwatibwayo, n’asimbulwa n’atwalibwa ku mwalo. Alina pack ennene ku ye ng’omusuubuzi; oba oyinza okumuyita Enjovu ne Castle whale. Mu ngeri yonna, erinnya lye erimanyiddwa ennyo terimwawula kimala, okuva bwe kiri nti sperm whale nayo erina ekiwujjo wadde nga ntono. Amafuta ge si ga muwendo nnyo. Alina baleen. Ye asinga okuzannya emizannyo n’okubeera omuweweevu mu nnyanja zonna, ng’akola foam y’abasajja n’amazzi amazungu okutwalira awamu okusinga endala zonna.

EKITABO I. ( Folio ), ESSUULA V. ( Razor Back ).—Ku nnyanja eno kitono ekimanyiddwa okuggyako erinnya lye. Nze mmulabye ng’ali wala nnyo okuva e Cape Horn. Olw’engeri y’okuwummula, yeewala abayizzi n’abafirosoofo. Wadde nga talina mukodo, tannaba kulaga kitundu kye okuggyako omugongo gwe, ogusituka mu luwonko oluwanvu olusongovu. Muleke agende. Nze mmanyi kitono nnyo ku ye, era n’omuntu omulala yenna tamanyi.

EKITABO I. ( Folio ), ESSUULA VI. ( Sulphur Bottom ).—Omusajja omulala eyali awummudde, ng’alina olubuto olw’ekibiriiti, awatali kubuusabuusa yafuna ng’asenya ku tile za Tartarian mu bimu ku bifo bye eby’okubbira mu mazzi ebiwanvu. Tatera kulabibwa; waakiri simulabangako okuggyako mu nnyanja ez’ewala ez’obugwanjuba, ate olwo bulijjo mu bbanga eddene ennyo okusoma ffeesi ye. Tagoberwa n’akatono; yaddukanga n’emiguwa-okutambula kwa layini. Prodigies bamubuulirwako. Adieu, Wansi wa Sulphur! Siyinza kwogera kintu kirala kisingawo ku mmwe, era n’omu Nantucketer asinga obukulu tasobola.

Bwatyo bwe kiggwa EKITBO I. ( Folio ), era kati kitandika EKITBO II. ( Octavo ) nga bwe kiri.

OCTAVOES.*—Bino bikwata ennyanja ekika kya whale ez’obunene obw’omu makkati, nga mu zo eziriwo ziyinza okubalibwa:—I., the Grampus ; II., Ekyennyanja Ekiddugavu ; III., ekika kya Narwhale ; IV., Omutemu ; V., Omutemu .

*Lwaki ekitabo kino ekya whales tekiyitibwa Quarto kyeyoleka bulungi nnyo. Kubanga, wadde nga ennyanja ekika kya whale ez’omutendera guno, wadde nga ntono okusinga ez’omutendera ogw’edda, wadde kiri kityo zisigaza okufaanagana okugeraageranye nazo mu kifaananyi, naye omuzingo gwa Quarto ogw’omusiba ebitabo mu ngeri yaago ey’ebipimo tegukuuma nkula ya muzingo gwa Folio, naye omuzingo gwa Octavo gukuuma.

EKITABO II. ( Octavo ), ESSUULA I. ( Grampus ).—Wadde ng’ekyennyanja kino, okussa kwakyo okw’amaanyi okw’amaloboozi, oba okusingawo okufuuwa, kufudde abatuuze b’ettaka olugero, kimanyiddwa nnyo ng’omutuuze w’omu buziba, naye tekitwalibwa mu kibinja ky’ennyanja ekika kya whale. Naye olw’okuba alina ebintu byonna ebinene eby’enjawulo ebiri mu leviathan, abakugu mu by’obutonde abasinga obungi bamutegedde nti kimu. Ya sayizi ya octavo ey’ekigero, eyawukana okuva ku ffuuti kkumi na ttaano okutuuka ku abiri mu ttaano mu buwanvu, era ng’alina ebipimo ebikwatagana okwetooloola ekiwato. Awuga mu bisibo; tayiggibwangako bulijjo, wadde ng’amafuta ge manene nnyo mu bungi, era malungi nnyo eri ekitangaala. Abavubi abamu enkola ye etwalibwa ng’eraga nti ennyonyi ennene ekika kya sperm whale egenda mu maaso.

EKITABO II. ( Octavo ), ESSUULA II. ( Black Fish ).—Nziwa amannya g’abavubi abamanyiddwa ennyo ku byennyanja bino byonna, kubanga okutwalira awamu bye bisinga obulungi. Erinnya lyonna we lituukira nga teritegeerekeka oba nga teririna kwogera, nja kwogera bwe ntyo, era nteese ku ndala. Nkikola kati, nga nkwata ku Black Fish, kye bayita, kubanga obuddugavu bwe tteeka kumpi mu nnyanja zonna eziyitibwa whales. Kale, muyite Hyena Whale, bw’oba ​​oyagala. Obuwoomi bwe bumanyiddwa bulungi, era okusinziira ku mbeera nti enkoona z’emimwa gye ez’omunda zikoona waggulu, asitudde akamwenyumwenyu aka Mephistophelean akataggwaawo ku maaso ge. Whale eno eweza ffuuti nga kkumi na mukaaga oba kkumi na munaana. Asangibwa kumpi mu latitude zonna. Alina engeri ey’enjawulo gy’alagamu ekiwaawaatiro kye eky’omugongo ekiyitibwa dorsal hooked fin mu kuwuga, ekiringa ennyindo y’Abaruumi. Bwe batakozesebwa mu ngeri esingako, abayizzi b’ennyanja ekika kya sperm whale oluusi bakwata ennyanja Hyena whale, okusobola okusigala nga bafuna amafuta ag’ebbeeyi entono okukola emirimu gy’awaka —ng’abamu ku balabirira awaka abakekkereza, nga tebalina kkampuni, era nga bokka bokka, bookya omusulo ogutawooma mu kifo ky’okuwunya. Wadde ng’ekikuta kyazo kigonvu nnyo, ezimu ku nnyanja zino zijja kukuvaamu ggaloni z’amafuta ezisukka mu makumi asatu.

EKITABO II. ( Octavo ), ESSUULA III. ( Narwhale ), kwe kugamba, Nostril whale .—Ekyokulabirako ekirala eky’ennyanja ekika kya whale erinnya ery’okwewuunya, eryatuumibwa erinnya eryo nsuubira okuva ku jjembe lye ery’enjawulo mu kusooka okukyamulwa nti lyali nnyindo eriko entikko. Ekitonde kino kiweza ffuuti nga kkumi na mukaaga, ate ejjembe lyakyo liri wakati wa ffuuti ttaano, wadde ng’ezimu zisukka ffuuti kkumi, era zituuka n’okutuuka ku ffuuti kkumi na ttaano. Mu ngeri enkakali, ejjembe lino liri bbugumu lyokka eriwanvuye, nga likula okuva mu bbwa mu layini enyigiriziddwa katono okuva ku nneekulungirivu. Naye kisangibwa ku ludda olubi lwokka, olulina ekikolwa ekibi, nga luwa nnannyini yo ekintu ekifaananako n’oludda lw’omusajja ow’omukono ogwa kkono omukodo. Kigendererwa ki ekituufu ejjembe oba effumu lino ery’amasanga ly’eddamu, kyandibadde kizibu okugamba. Tekirabika nga kikozesebwa ng’ekiso ky’ekyennyanja ekitala n’ekyennyanja ekiyitibwa bill-fish; wadde ng’abalunnyanja abamu bang’amba nti Narwhale bagikozesa okukola rake mu kukyusa wansi w’ennyanja okufuna emmere. Charley Coffin yagambye nti yakozesebwa mu kufumita ice; kubanga Narwhale, ng’asituka ku mabbali g’ennyanja Polar, n’esanga ng’ejjudde ice, n’esika ejjembe lye waggulu, era bwe kityo n’emenya. Naye tosobola kukakasa nti ku kuteebereza kuno kwonna kutuufu. Endowooza yange eri nti, wadde ejjembe lino ery’oludda olumu ddala liyinza okukozesebwa Narwhale —wadde nga ekyo kiyinza okuba —mazima ddala kyandibadde kyangu nnyo gy’ali olw’ekitabo mu kusoma obupapula. Ennyonyi eyitibwa Narwhale gye mpulidde eyitibwa Tusked whale, Horned whale ne Unicorn whale. Mazima ddala kyakulabirako ekyewuunyisa eky’Obufuzi bwa Unicornism obusangibwa kumpi mu buli bwakabaka obw’obutonde obulina obulamu. Okuva mu bawandiisi abamu ab’edda abakuŋŋaanyiziddwa nkuŋŋaanyizza nti ejjembe lino lye limu erya sea-unicorn mu biseera eby’edda lyali litwalibwa ng’eddagala eddene eritta obutwa, era nga bwe kiri, okuteekateeka kwalyo kwaleeta emiwendo egy’amaanyi ennyo. Era yafuumuulwa okutuuka ku minnyo ewunya eri abakyala abazirika, mu ngeri y’emu ng’amayembe g’empologoma ensajja bwe gakolebwa ne gafuuka hartshorn. Mu kusooka ku bwakyo kyali kitwalibwa ng’ekintu eky’okwegomba ennyo. Black Letter eηηamba nti Sir Martin Frobisher bwe yakomawo okuva ku lugendo olwo, Nnabagereka Bess bwe yamuwuuba n’obuzira omukono gwe ogw’amayinja ag’omuwendo okuva mu ddirisa ly’Olubiri lwa Greenwich, ng’emmeeri ye ey’obuvumu egenda wansi ku mugga Thames; “Sir Martin bwe yakomawo okuva ku lugendo olwo,” bw’atyo Black Letter bw’agamba, “ku maviivi agafukamidde yayanjula eri omukulu we ejjembe eddene ery’ekitalo erya Narwhale, eryamala ebbanga eddene oluvannyuma lw’okuwanikibwa mu lubiri e Windsor.” Omuwandiisi Omuyindi avers nti Earl of Leicester, ku maviivi agafukamidde, mu ngeri y’emu yayanjula obukulu bwe ejjembe eddala, erikwata ku nsolo ey’oku lukalu ey’obutonde bwa unicorn.

Narwhale erina ekifaananyi ekirabika obulungi ennyo, ng’engo, nga ya langi ya ttaka enjeru ng’amata, ng’eriko ebitundu ebiddugavu ebyetooloovu n’ebiwanvu. Amafuta ge gasinga nnyo, matangaavu era malungi; naye kitono ku kyo, era tatera kuyigganyizibwa. Asinga kusangibwa mu nnyanja eziyitibwa circumpolar seas.

EKITABO II. ( Octavo ), ESSUULA IV. ( Killer ).—Ku whale eno kitono ddala kimanyiddwa bulungi eri Nantucketer, era tewali kintu kyonna eri oyo eyeeyita omukugu mu butonde. Okusinziira ku bye ndabye ku wala, nsaana okugamba nti yali akwata ku bunene bwa grampus. Mukambwe nnyo —ekika ky’ebyennyanja Feegee. Oluusi akwata envubu ennene eza Folio whales ku mimwa, n’awanirira awo ng’enseenene, okutuusa ensolo ey’amaanyi lwe yeeraliikirira okufa. Omutemu tayiggibwangako. Siwulirangako mafuta ga ngeri ki gy’alina. Okujjako kuyinza okutwalibwa ku linnya eryaweebwa ennyanja eno ey’ennyanja ekika kya whale, olw’obutategeerekeka bwayo. Kubanga ffenna tuli batemu, ku lukalu ne ku nnyanja; Bonapartes ne Sharks nga muno mwe muli.

EKITABO II. ( Octavo ), ESSUULA V. ( Thrasher ).—Omusajja ono omulungi amanyiddwa nnyo olw’omukira gwe, gw’akozesa okukola ferule mu kukuba abalabe be. Alinnya omugongo gwa Folio whale, era bw’awuga, akola ekkubo lye ng’amukuba emiggo; nga abakulu b’amasomero abamu bwe bakwatagana mu nsi nga bayita mu nkola efaananako bwetyo. Still kitono ekimanyiddwa ku Thrasher okusinga ku Killer. Bombi bamenya mateeka, ne mu nnyanja ezitaliimu mateeka.

Bwatyo bwe kiggwa EKITABO II. ( Octavo ), era n’atandika EKITABO III. ( Duodecimo ) nga bwe kiri.

DUODECIMOES.—Mu bino mulimu ennyanja ekika kya whale entonotono. I. Ensigo ya Huzza Porpoise. II. Ennyonyi ekika kya Porpoise ey’omu Algery. III. Ennyonyi ekika kya Mealy-mouthed Porpoise.

Eri abo abatafunye mukisa mu ngeri ey’enjawulo okusoma ensonga eyo, kiyinza okulabika ng’ekyewuunyisa, nti ebyennyanja ebitatera kusukka ffuuti nnya oba ttaano birina okukuŋŋaanyizibwa wakati mu WHALES —ekigambo, mu makulu agamanyiddwa ennyo, bulijjo kituusa endowooza y’obunene. Naye ebitonde ebiteekeddwa wansi nga Duodecimoes biba whales ezitasobya, okusinziira ku bigambo by’ennyonnyola yange ku whale kye ki — kwe kugamba, ekyennyanja ekifuuwa, nga kirina omukira ogw’okwebungulula.

EKITABO III. ( Duodecimo ), ESSUULA 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Eno ye nvubu eya bulijjo esangibwa kumpi mu nsi yonna. Erinnya lyange lyange; kubanga waliwo ebika by’enseenene ebisukka mu kimu, era waliwo ekiteekwa okukolebwa okuzaawulamu. Nze mmuyita bwe ntyo, kubanga bulijjo awuga mu biwonvu ebisesa, ku nnyanja engazi ebisigala nga byesuula mu ggulu ng’enkoofiira mu kibiina ky’abantu eky’okuna mu July. Okutwalira awamu endabika yazo etenderezebwa nnyo omuvubi w’oku nnyanja. Nga zijjudde emyoyo emirungi, bulijjo ziva mu biwujjo ebifuuwa empewo okutuuka mu mpewo. Bano be ba lads ababeera bulijjo nga empewo tennabaawo. Babalibwa ng’akabonero k’omukisa. Bw’oba ​​ggwe kennyini osobola okugumira essanyu ssatu ng’olaba ebyennyanja bino ebiramu, olwo eggulu muyambe; omwoyo gw’okuzannya emizannyo egy’okutya Katonda teguli mu mmwe. Huzza Porpoise erimu ebiriisa obulungi era omugejjo ejja kukuwa ggaloni emu ennungi ey’amafuta amalungi. Naye amazzi amalungi era amagonvu agaggibwa mu nsaya ze ga muwendo nnyo. Kiri mu kusabibwa mu basuubuzi b’amajolobero n’abakola essaawa. Abalunnyanja bagiteeka ku hones zaabwe. Ennyama ya porpoise nnungi okulya, omanyi. Kiyinza okuba nga tekikujjirangako nti envubu efuumuuka. Mazima ddala, ekifuba kye kitono nnyo ne kiba nti tekitegeerekeka mangu nnyo. Naye omulundi oguddako bw’ofuna omukisa, mutunuulire; era olwo ojja kulaba envubu ennene eya Sperm whale yennyini mu butonotono.

EKITABO III. ( Duodecimo ), ESSUULA II. ( Algerine Porpoise ). —Omubbi w’oku nnyanja. Savage nnyo. Asangibwa mu Pacific yokka, ndowooza. Ye munene katono okusinga Huzza Porpoise, naye bingi bya general make y’emu. Munyiize, era ajja kwesiba ku shark. Nze mmukka wansi emirundi mingi, naye sinnaba kumulaba ng’awambibwa.

EKITABO III. ( Duodecimo ), ESSUULA III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Ekika kya Porpoise ekisinga obunene; era esangibwa mu Pacific yokka, okutuuka we kimanyiddwa. Erinnya lyokka ery’Olungereza, okutuusa kati lye yatuumibwa, lye lya bavubi —Right-Whale Porpoise, okusinziira ku mbeera nti okusinga asangibwa mu bitundu ebiriraanye Folio eyo. Mu nkula, yawukana mu ddaala eritali limu ku Huzza Porpoise, olw’okuba nga si wa bbugumu nnyo ate nga ya ssanyu; mazima ddala, wa ffiga nnungi nnyo era ng’eya ssebo. Talina biwaawaatiro ku mugongo (envubu endala ezisinga zirina), alina omukira omulungi, n’amaaso g’Abayindi aga sentimental aga langi ya hazel. Naye akamwa ke ak’emmwaanyi kwonoona byonna. Wadde ng’omugongo gwe gwonna okutuuka ku biwaawaatiro bye eby’ebbali gwa sable enzito, naye layini y’ensalo, eyawukana ng’akabonero akali mu kisenge ky’emmeeri, akayitibwa “ekiwato ekitangalijja,” layini eyo emuyitamu okuva ku kikolo okutuuka emabega, nga erina langi bbiri ez’enjawulo, waggulu enjeru ate wansi enjeru. Enjeru erimu ekitundu ky’omutwe gwe, n’akamwa ke konna, ekimuleetera okulabika ng’alinga eyaakasimattuka okukyalira ensawo y’emmere mu ngeri ey’obumenyi bw’amateeka. Ensonga esinga obubi era ey’obuwunga! Amafuta ge gafaananako nnyo ag’omuti gwa porpoise ogwa bulijjo.

Okusukka DUODECIMO, enkola eno tegenda mu maaso, kubanga Porpoise y’esinga obutono mu nnyanja ekika kya whale. Waggulu, olina Abaleeviyaani bonna ab’amaanyi. Naye waliwo ekibinja ky’ennyanja ekika kya whale ezitakakasa, ezidduka, ezitali za kitundu, nga, ng’omuvuzi w’ennyanja Omumerika, mmanyi olw’erinnya, naye si nze kennyini. Nja kuzibala okusinziira ku mannya gaabwe ag’olubiri olw’omu maaso; kubanga mpozzi olukalala ng’olwo luyinza okuba olw’omuwendo eri bambega ab’omu maaso, abayinza okumaliriza bye nnina wano naye nga ntandise. Singa emu ku nnyanja zino wammanga, oluvannyuma ejja kukwatibwa era n’eteekebwako akabonero, olwo asobola mangu okuyingizibwa mu Nkola eno, okusinziira ku bunene bwe obwa Folio, Octavo, oba Duodecimo:—The Bottle-Nose Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Junk Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Pudding-Headed Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Cape Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Leading Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Cannon Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Scragg Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Coppered Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Elephant Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Iceberg Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Quog Whale; ennyonyi ekika kya Blue Whale; etc. Okuva mu bakulu b’e Iceland, Abadaaki, n’Abangereza ab’edda, wayinza okujulizibwa enkalala endala ez’ennyanja ekika kya whale ezitali nkakasa, eziweereddwa omukisa n’amannya ag’engeri zonna agatali ga kitiibwa. Naye nzilekayo nga ezitaliiko mugaso gwonna; era tayinza kuyamba kuziteebereza olw’amaloboozi gokka, agajjudde Obuleeviyaani, naye nga tezitegeeza kintu kyonna.

N’ekisembayo: Kyagambibwa ku ntandikwa, nti enkola eno tegenda kuba wano, era omulundi gumu, etuukiridde. Toyinza butalaba bulungi nti nkuumye ekigambo kyange. Naye kati ndeka Enkola yange ey’eby’obutonde (cetological System) ng’eyimiridde bwetyo nga tennaggwa, ne bwe yali esigaddewo Lutikko ennene eya Cologne, nga crane ekyayimiridde waggulu ku munaala ogutannaggwa. Kubanga obuzimbi obutono buyinza okumalirizibwa abakubi b’ebifaananyi baabwe abasooka; grand ones, abatuufu, bulijjo baleka copestone eri abazzukulu. Katonda ankuume obutamaliriza kintu kyonna. Ekitabo kino kyonna kirimu kiwandiiko kyokka —nedda, wabula bbago ly’ekiwandiiko. Oh, Ebiseera, Amaanyi, Ssente Enkalu, n’Obugumiikiriza!
