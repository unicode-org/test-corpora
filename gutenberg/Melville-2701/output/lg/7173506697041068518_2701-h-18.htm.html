ESSUULA 89. Ebyennyanja eby’amangu n’ebyennyanja ebikalu.

Okwogerwako ku waif ne waif-poles mu ssuula esembayo naye emu, kyetaagisa okunnyonnyola okutonotono ku mateeka n’ebiragiro by’envuba y’ennyanja ekika kya whale, nga waif eyinza okutwalibwa ng’akabonero akakulu ne badge.

Kitera okubaawo nti emmeeri eziwerako bwe ziba zitambulira mu kkampuni, ennyanja ekika kya whale eyinza okukubwa emmeeri emu, n’etoloka, n’esembayo okuttibwa n’ewambibwa emmeeri endala; era wano mu ngeri etali butereevu mulimu ebintu ebitonotono bingi ebiyinza okugwawo, byonna nga byetabamu ku kintu kino ekimu ekinene. Ng’ekyokulabirako, —oluvannyuma lw’okugoba n’okukwata ennyanja ekika kya whale ng’okooye era mu ngeri ey’akabi, omulambo guyinza okusumululwa okuva ku mmeeri olw’omuyaga ogw’amaanyi; era ng’ewuguka ewala okutuuka mu bbanga, okuddamu okukwatibwa omuvubi ow’okubiri ow’ennyanja, nga mu bukkakkamu, mu ngeri ennungi n’agisika ku mabbali, awatali bulabe bwa bulamu oba layini. Bwe kityo enkaayana ezisinga okunyiiza era ez’effujjo zandibadde zitera okubaawo wakati w’abavubi, singa tewaaliwo tteeka erimu eriwandiikiddwa oba eritali liwandiikiddwa, erya bonna, eritaliiko kuwakana erikola ku misango gyonna.

Mpozzi enkola yokka entongole ey’okuvuba envubu eyakkirizibwa okuteekebwawo kw’amateeka, yali ya Holland. Kyalagirwa States-General mu AD 1695. Naye newankubadde nga tewali ggwanga kirala libadde na tteeka lyonna eriwandiikiddwa ku kuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale, naye abavubi b’Amerika babadde bateesa baabwe era bannamateeka baabwe mu nsonga eno. Bawadde enkola nga mu bufunze esukkulumye ku Justinian’s Pandects ne By-laws of the Chinese Society for the Suppression of Meddling with other People’s Business. Yee; amateeka gano gayinza okuba nga gaayoleddwa ku farthing ya Queen Anne, oba ku barb ya harpoon, ne gayambalibwa mu bulago, kale matono nnyo.

I. A Fast-Fish ya kibiina ekisiiba ku kyo.

II. Loose-Fish muzannyo gwa bwenkanya eri omuntu yenna asobola okugukwata amangu.

Naye ekizannya obubi n’enkola eno ey’obukugu bwe bufunze bwayo obusiimibwa, obwetaagisa omuwendo omunene ennyo ogw’okunnyonnyola okusobola okuginnyonnyola.

Ekisooka: Fast-Fish kye ki? Ekyennyanja ekiramu oba ekifudde kiba kyangu mu by’ekikugu, bwe kiyungibwa ku mmeeri oba eryato eritudde, n’ekintu kyonna ekiyinza okufugibwa n’akatono oyo akibeeramu oba abakirimu, —ekikondo, ekiwujjo, waya ya yinsi mwenda, waya y’amasimu, oba oluwuzi lw’omuti gw’enseenene, byonna biba bye bimu. Mu ngeri y’emu ekyennyanja kiba kyangu mu by’ekikugu bwe kiba nga kikutte waif, oba akabonero akalala konna akamanyiddwa ak’okubeera nakyo; kasita ekibiina ekigiwaayo kiraga bulungi obusobozi bwabwe ekiseera kyonna okukitwala ku bbali, awamu n’ekigendererwa kyabwe okukikola.

Bino bye binnyonnyola bya ssaayansi; naye ennyonyola z’abavuzi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale bennyini oluusi zibeera mu bigambo ebikalu n’okukoona okusingawo —Coke-upon-Littleton y’ekikonde. Kituufu nti mu bavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale abasinga okubeera abagolokofu era ab’ekitiibwa bulijjo ensako ekolebwa ku misango egy’enjawulo, nga kyandibadde butali bwenkanya obw’empisa obw’ekitalo oludda olumu okugamba nti lulina ennyanja ekika kya whale eyagoberwa oba eyattibwa ekibiina ekirala. Naye abalala si beegendereza n’akatono bwe batyo.

Emyaka nga ataano egiyise waaliwo omusango ogw’okwewuunya ogwa whale-trover ogwawozesebwa mu Bungereza, abawawaabira mwe baalaga nti oluvannyuma lw’okuyigga ennyo whale mu nnyanja z’Obukiikakkono; era nga ddala bo (abawawaabira) basobodde okukuba ebyennyanja ebyo; ku nkomerero, okuyita mu kabi ak’obulamu bwabwe, baali bavunaanyizibwa okuleka si layini zaabwe zokka, naye n’eryato lyabwe lyenyini. Mu nkomerero abawawaabirwa (abakozi b’emmeeri endala) bajja n’ennyanja eno, ne bagikuba, ne bagitta, ne bagiwamba, era okukkakkana nga bagitwala mu maaso g’abawawaabira. Era abawawaabirwa abo bwe baawakanyibwa, kapiteeni waabwe yasika engalo ze mu mannyo g’abawawaabira, n’abakakasa nti mu ngeri y’okudoxology ku kiwandiiko kye yali akoze, kati yali agenda kusigaza layini yaabwe, harpoons, n’eryato, ebyali bisigadde nga byesibye ku whale mu kiseera we yakwatiddwa. Nolwekyo kati abawawaabira baawawaabira okuddizibwa omuwendo gwa whale yaabwe, line, harpoons, n’eryato.

Erskine yali ku ludda olulala; era n’alyoka akiwagira ng’agamba nti, newankubadde nga mu kusooka omwami oyo yali akubye omukyala oyo ensambaggere, era nga lumu yali asiibye, era olw’okunyigirizibwa okunene okw’obukambwe bwe obwali bunywera, ku nkomerero yali amusudde; naye n’amuleka n’akola, n’afuuka ekyennyanja ekisumuluddwa; era n’olwekyo omwami eyaddirira bwe yaddamu okumukuba ensambaggere, omukyala oyo olwo n’afuuka eby’obugagga by’omusajja oyo eyaddirira, awamu n’omusota gwonna ogwali guyinza okusangibwa nga gumunywereddemu.

Kati mu musango guno Erskine yagamba nti ebyokulabirako by’ennyanja ekika kya whale ne lady byali biragagana.

Okwewozaako kuno, n’okwewozaako okw’okuddamu, bwe kwawulirwa obulungi, omulamuzi omuyivu ennyo mu bigambo ebiteekeddwawo yasalawo, okugamba,—Nti ku lyato, yaliwa abawawaabira, kubanga baali bamaze okulisuula okutaasa obulamu bwabwe; naye nti ku bikwata ku whale eyali ekaayanirwa, harpoons, ne line, byali bya bawawaabirwa; whale, kubanga yali Loose-Fish mu kiseera we yasembayo okukwatibwa; ne harpoons ne line kubanga ebyennyanja bwe byagenda nabyo, (ebyennyanja) byafuna eby’obugagga mu bintu ebyo; era nga kye kivaako omuntu yenna oluvannyuma eyakwata ebyennyanja ebyo yalina eddembe ku byo. Kati abawawaabirwa oluvannyuma baatwala ebyennyanja; ergo, emiko egyogeddwako waggulu gyali gyabwe.

Omuntu wa bulijjo ng’atunuulira okusalawo kuno okw’Omulamuzi omuyivu ennyo, ayinza okukiwakanya. Naye nga zirimiddwa okutuuka ku lwazi olusookerwako olw’ensonga, emisingi emikulu ebiri egyateekebwawo mu mateeka g’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya twin whale agaajuliziddwa emabegako, era ne gakozesebwa era ne gannyonnyolwa Lord Ellenborough mu musango ogwogeddwako waggulu; amateeka gano abiri agakwata ku Fast-Fish ne Loose-Fish, ngamba nti, bwe gafumiitiriza, gajja kusangibwa nga ge mateeka gonna ag’obuntu; kubanga wadde nga waliwo ebibumbe ebizibu, Yeekaalu y’Amateeka, okufaananako Yeekaalu y’Abafirisuuti, erina ebintu bibiri byokka by’eyinza okuyimirirako.

Ssaabalabirizi wa Savesoul ssente ki eza pawundi 100,000 ze yakwatibwa okuva mu mugaati ne kkeeki ebitonotono eby’abakozi abamenyese emigongo (bonna bakakasa eggulu nga tewali buyambi bwa Savesoul) pawundi 100,000 ezo ez’ensi yonna naye nga Fast-Fish? Ebibuga n’obubuga obusikira Duke of Dunder bye biruwa okuggyako Fast-Fish? Kiki eri omukubi w’ennyonyi oyo amanyiddwa ennyo, John Bull, Ireland omwavu, naye nga Fast-Fish? ESSUULA 90. Emitwe oba Emikira.

“De balena vero emala, si rex habeat caput, ne regina caudam.” Bracton, omuwandiisi w’ebitabo. 3, c. 3. 3. .

Olulattini okuva mu bitabo by’Amateeka ga Bungereza, nga bitwalibwa wamu n’ensonga, kitegeeza, nti ku nnyanja zonna ezikwatibwa ennyanja ekika kya whale ezikwatibwa omuntu yenna ku lubalama lw’ensi eyo, Kabaka, nga Honorary Grand Harpooneer, alina okuba n’omutwe, era Nnabagereka n’aweebwa omukira mu ngeri ey’ekitiibwa. Enjawukana nga, mu nnyanja ekika kya whale, eringa nnyo okusala obulo ekitundu; tewali kisigalira kya wakati. Kati ng’etteeka lino, wansi w’engeri ekyusiddwa, n’okutuusa leero bwe likola mu Bungereza; era nga bwe kiwa mu ngeri ez’enjawulo obutafaanagana obw’ekyewuunyo obukwata ku tteeka erya bulijjo erya Fast and Loose-Fish, wano kikwatibwako mu ssuula ey’enjawulo, ku musingi gwe gumu ogw’empisa oguleetera eggaali y’omukka ey’e Bungereza okubeera ku ssente z’emmotoka ey’enjawulo, ey’enjawulo eterekeddwa okusuza ab’obwakabaka. Mu kusooka, mu bukakafu obw’okwewuunya nti etteeka eryogeddwako waggulu likyakola, ngenda mu maaso n’okuteeka mu maaso gammwe embeera eyagwawo mu myaka ebiri egiyise.

Kirabika abavubi abamu abeesimbu ab’e Dover, oba Sandwich, oba omu ku mwalo gw’e Cinque, oluvannyuma lw’okuyigga ennyo baali basobodde okutta n’okutuusa ku bbiici envubu ennungi gye baali boogedde mu kusooka nga bali wala okuva ku lubalama lw’ennyanja. Kati Cinque Ports ziri wansi wa kitundu oba mu ngeri emu oba endala wansi w’obuyinza bw’ekika ky’omupoliisi oba beadle, ayitibwa Lord Warden. Okukwata ofiisi butereevu okuva ku ngule, ndowooza, byonna eby’obwakabaka ebibaddewo okutuuka ku bitundu by’omwalo gwa Cinque bifuuka by assignment ye. Abawandiisi abamu ofiisi eno eyitibwa sinecure. Naye si bwe kiri. Kubanga Lord Warden akozesebwa nnyo ebiseera ebimu mu kufoba perquisites ze; ezibeera ze okusinga olw’okufuba okwo kwe kumu okwazo.

Kati abavubi bano abaavu abaali bayokeddwa omusana, nga tebalina bigere, era nga balina bbulawuzi zaabwe nga ziyiringisiddwa waggulu ku magulu gaabwe amazibu, bwe baali bakooye okusika ebyennyanja byabwe ebisavu waggulu era nga bikalu, nga beesuubiza £150 ennungi okuva mu mafuta n’amagumba ag’omuwendo; era mu fantasy nga banywa caayi atatera kulabika ne bakyala baabwe, ne good ale ne cronies zaabwe, ku maanyi g’emigabo gyabwe; up steps a very learned and most Christian and charitable gentleman, nga alina kkopi ya Blackstone wansi w’omukono gwe; era ng’agiteeka ku mutwe gw’ennyanja ekika kya whale, agamba nti —“Emikono give!, ekyennyanja kino, bakama bange, kya Fast-Fish. Nkikwata nga ekya Lord Warden.” Ku kino abavubi abaavu mu kuwuniikirira kwabwe okw’ekitiibwa —bwe kityo Olungereza olw’amazima —nga tebamanyi kya kwogera, bagwa mu kusika emitwe gyabwe n’amaanyi okwetooloola; mu kiseera kino ng’atunudde mu nnaku okuva ku nnyanja ekika kya whale okutuuka ku muntu gw’atamanyi. Naye ekyo mu ngeri yonna tekyalongoosa nsonga, oba n’akatono ne kigonza omutima omukalu ogw’omusajja omulungi omuyivu n’kkopi ya Blackstone. Mu bbanga ttono omu ku bo, oluvannyuma lw’okumala ebbanga ng’asikambula ebirowoozo bye, yakola obuvumu okwogera, .

“Nkwegayiridde ssebo, Lord Warden y’ani?”

“Omulangira.”

“Kye kikye.”

“Kye kikye.”

“Nnalowooza okuwummuza maama wange omukadde eyali ku kitanda nga nkozesa ekitundu ku mugabo gwange ku nnyanja eno ey’ennyanja ekika kya whale.”

Mu kigambo kimu, whale eno yakwatibwa n’etundibwa, era Grace we Duke of Wellington ye yafuna ssente ezo. Ng’alowooza nti bwe yatunuulirwa mu bitaala ebimu, omusango guno mu ngeri ey’ekibogwe mu ddaala ettono, guyinza okutwalibwa ng’omukalu ennyo, munnaddiini omwesimbu ow’omu kibuga mu ngeri ey’ekitiibwa yawandiika akawandiiko eri Grace we, ng’amusaba atwale ensonga y’abavubi abo ab’omukisa omubi mu bujjuvu. Ekyo Lord Duke wange mu makulu n’addamu (ebbaluwa zombi zaafulumizibwa) nti yali yakikola dda, era ssente yazifuna, era yandibadde alina okukakasibwa eri omwami ow’ekitiibwa singa olw’ebiseera eby’omu maaso ye (omusajja omulungi ow’ekitiibwa) yandigaanyi okweyingiza mu bizinensi z’abantu abalala. Ono ye mukadde akyali mulwanyi, ng’ayimiridde ku nsonda z’obwakabaka obusatu, ku mikono gyonna ng’akaka sadaaka z’abasabiriza?

Kijja kulabibwa mangu nti mu musango guno eddembe erigambibwa nti Duke lyalina ku nnyanja ekika kya whale lyali lyaweebwayo okuva eri Sovereign. Tulina okwetaaga okwebuuza olwo ku musingi ki Omufuzi w’asooka okuteekebwamu eddembe eryo. Etteeka lyenyini lyateekebwawo dda. Naye Plowdon atuwa ensonga lwaki tukikola. Plowdon agamba nti, whale ekwatiddwa bwetyo ya Kabaka ne Nnabagereka, “olw’obulungi bwayo obw’oku ntikko.” Era abakomentinga abasinga obutuufu kino kibadde kikwatibwako ensonga entuufu mu nsonga ng’ezo.

Naye lwaki Kabaka yandibadde n’omutwe, ate Nnabagereka omukira? Ensonga lwaki ekyo, mmwe bannamateeka!

Mu kitabo kye ekikwata ku “Queen-Gold,” oba Queen-pinmoney, omuwandiisi omukadde ow’e King’s Bench, omu William Prynne, bw’atyo ayogera bw’ati: “Ye tail is you Queen’s, mmwe woduloomu ya Queen musobole okuweebwa eggumba ly’ennyanja whale.” Kati kino kyawandiikibwa mu kiseera ng’eggumba erya black limber erya Greenland oba Right whale lisinga kukozesebwa mu bodices z’abakyala. Naye eggumba lino lye limu teri mu mukira; kiri mu mutwe, nga eno nsobi ya nnaku eri munnamateeka omugezi nga Prynne. Naye Nnabagereka ye mermaid, okuweebwa omukira? Amakulu ag’olugero gayinza okwekweka wano.

Waliwo ebyennyanja bibiri eby’obwakabaka ebyakolebwa mu ngeri eyo abawandiisi b’amateeka mu Bungereza — whale ne sturgeon; byombi eby’obwakabaka wansi w’obukwakkulizo obumu, n’okugabira mu linnya ettabi ery’ekkumi ery’ensimbi eza bulijjo ez’engule. Simanyi nti waliwo omuwandiisi omulala yenna alina akabonero ku nsonga eno; naye okusinziira ku kuteebereza kirabika gyendi nti ennyonyi ekika kya sturgeon erina okwawulwamu mu ngeri y’emu n’ennyanja ekika kya whale, Kabaka ng’afuna omutwe omunene ennyo era ogw’obugumu ogw’enjawulo eri ekyennyanja ekyo, nga bwe kitunuulirwa mu ngeri ey’akabonero, kiyinza okuba nga kyasibuka mu ngeri ey’okusekerera ku bintu ebimu ebiteeberezebwa okuba eby’okusanyusa. Era bwe kityo kirabika waliwo ensonga mu bintu byonna, ne mu mateeka.





ESSUULA 91. Pequod Asisinkana Rose-Bud.

“Mu bwereere kyali ku rake for Ambergriese mu paunch ya Leviathan eno, insufferable fetor nga yeegaana obutabuuza.” Sir T. Browne, V. E., era nga ye muwandiisi w’ebitabo

Waali wayise wiiki emu oba bbiri ng’ekifo ekisembayo eky’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale kinyumiddwa, era bwe twali tusaabala mpola ku nnyanja eyali ezeebaka, eyali efuuse omukka, ey’omu ttuntu, ennyindo ennyingi ezaali ku ddeeke ya Pequod zaalaga nti zaali bulindaala nnyo ezizuula okusinga amaaso asatu agaali waggulu. Akawoowo ak’enjawulo era akatasanyusa nnyo kaali kawunyiriza mu nnyanja.

Stubb yagamba nti: “Nja kuteeka bbeeti kati nti waliwo wano we wali ezimu ku nnyanja ekika kya whale ezo ezaali zikozesa ebiragalalagala ze twasikambula olunaku olulala. Nnalowooza nti mu bbanga ttono zandibadde zigenda waggulu.”

Mu kiseera kino, omukka ogwali gusoose gwaseeyeeya ku bbali; era awo ewala we waali waaliwo emmeeri, ng’amayengo gaayo agafuukuuse galaga nti ekika ky’ennyanja ekika kya whale kiteekwa okuba nga kiri ku mabbali. Bwe twasemberera, omugenyi oyo yalaga langi z’Olufaransa okuva ku ntikko ye; era olw’ekire ekiwujjo eky’ebinyonyi by’omu nnyanja eby’enkima ebyali byetooloola, ne biwuubaala, ne biwuubaala okumwetooloola, kyali kyeyoleka lwatu nti ennyanja ekika kya whale eyali ku mabbali eteekwa okuba nga y’eyo abavubi gye bayita ennyanja ekika kya blasted whale, kwe kugamba, ennyanja ekika kya whale efudde nga tetulugunyizibwa ku nnyanja, era bwe kityo n’etengejja omulambo ogutasaana. Kiyinza bulungi okulowoozebwa, nga akawoowo akatali kawooma nnyo ekibi ng’ekyo kye kiteekwa okufulumya; obubi okusinga ekibuga ky’e Bwasuli mu kawumpuli, ng’abalamu tebalina busobozi kuziika bafu. Mazima ddala abamu bakitwala nga tekigumiikiriza nnyo, ne kiba nti tewali kwegomba kwonna kuyinza kubasikiriza kusiba ku mabbali gaakyo. Naye waliwo abakyagenda okukikola; wadde nga amafuta agafunibwa okuva mu bintu ng’ago ga mutindo gwa wansi nnyo, era mu ngeri yonna si ga butonde bwa attar-of-rose.

Nga tukyasemberera n’empewo eyali egenda okuggwaako, twalaba ng’Omufaransa yalina ennyanja ekika kya whale eyookubiri ku mabbali; era ennyanja eno ey’okubiri yalabika ng’esinga n’okubeera ennyindo okusinga eyasooka. Mu butuufu, kyazuuka nga y’emu ku nnyanja ekika kya whale ezo ezirina obuzibu ezirabika ng’ekala ne zifa nga zirina ekika ky’obulwadde bw’okubulwa emmere mu ngeri ey’ekitalo, oba obutagaaya mmere; okuleka emirambo gyabwe egyali tegikola kumpi nga gifuuse bankrupt ya kintu kyonna ng’amafuta. ESSUULA 92. Ambergris.

Kati ambergris eno kintu kya kwewuunya nnyo, era kikulu nnyo ng’ekiwandiiko ky’ebyobusuubuzi, ne kiba nti mu 1791 Captain Coffin omu enzaalwa y’e Nantucket yakeberebwa mu bbaala y’olukiiko lwa Bungereza olwa House of Commons ku nsonga eyo. Kubanga mu kiseera ekyo, era ddala okutuusa ku lunaku olukeerezi bw’ogeraageranya, ensibuko entuufu eya ambergris yasigala, okufaananako amber yennyini, ng’ekizibu eri abayivu. Wadde ng’ekigambo ambergris kigambo kya Lufalansa ekitegeeza amber enzirugavu, naye ebintu bino byombi byawukana nnyo. Kubanga amber, wadde ng’oluusi esangibwa ku lubalama lw’ennyanja, era asimibwa mu ttaka erimu ery’ewala ery’omunda, so ng’ate ambergris tesangibwako okuggyako ku nnyanja. Ng’oggyeeko ekyo, amber kintu ekikalu, ekitangaavu, ekiwunya, ekitaliimu kawoowo, ekikozesebwa mu kamwa okutuuka ku payipu, okukola obululu n’eby’okwewunda; naye ambergris nnyogovu, ya wax, era ewunya nnyo era erimu eby’akawoowo, ne kiba nti ekozesebwa nnyo mu kukola obuwoowo, mu pastile, mu mimuli egy’omuwendo, mu butto w’enviiri, ne mu pomatum. Abatuluuki bagikozesa mu kufumba, era era bagitwala e Mecca, olw’ekigendererwa kye kimu ng’obubaane bwe butwalibwa mu St. Peter’s e Rooma. Abasuubuzi ba wayini abamu basuula empeke ntono mu claret, okugiwooma.

N’olwekyo, ani yandirowoozezza nti bassebo n’abasajja abalungi bwe batyo balina okwesanyusaamu n’ekintu ekisangibwa mu byenda ebitali bya kitiibwa eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale omulwadde! Naye bwe kityo bwe kiri. Abamu, ambergris eteeberezebwa okuba nga y’evaako, ate abalala n’ekivaamu, obulwadde bw’okubulwa emmere mu nnyanja ekika kya whale. Engeri y’okuwonya obulwadde bw’okubulwa emmere ng’obwo kyali kizibu okugamba, okuggyako ng’ogaba emigugu esatu oba ena egy’amaato egy’empeke za Brandreth, n’oluvannyuma n’eggwaamu obulabe, ng’abakozi bwe bakola mu kutulisa amayinja.

Nze nerabira okugamba nti mu ambergris eno mwasangibwamu, ebipande ebimu ebikalu, ebyetooloovu, eby’amagumba, mu kusooka Stubb bye yalowooza nti biyinza okuba obutambi bw’empale z’abalunnyanja; naye oluvannyuma kyazuulibwa nti tezaali kirala okuggyako ebitundutundu by’amagumba amatono aga squid agaali gasiigibwa mu ngeri eyo.

Kati ng’obutavunda bwa ambergris ono asinga okuwunya obulungi busaana okusangibwa mu mutima gw’okuvunda ng’okwo; kino si kintu? Lowooza ku kigambo ekyo ekya Pawulo Omutukuvu mu Bakkolinso, ekikwata ku kuvunda n’obutavunda; nga bwe twasigibwa mu kitiibwa, naye ne tuzuukizibwa mu kitiibwa. Era mu ngeri y’emu jjukira enjogera eyo eya Paracelsus ku kiki ekikola musk asinga obulungi. Era tewerabira nsonga eyeewuunyisa nti mu bintu byonna eby’obubi, amazzi ga Cologne, mu mitendera gyago egy’okukola egy’olubereberye, ge gasinga obubi.

Njagala okumaliriza essuula n’okujulira okwo waggulu, naye sisobola, olw’okweraliikirira kwange okugoba omusango ogutera okukolebwa ku bavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale, era nga, mu kuteebereza kw’ebirowoozo abamu abaali bamaze okusosola, guyinza okutwalibwa ng’ogukakasibwa mu ngeri etali ya butereevu olw’ebyo ebibadde byogerwa ku nnyanja ebbiri ez’Omufaransa. Awalala mu muzingo guno okuvuma okuvuma kubadde kuwakanya, nti omulanga gw’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale mu kiseera kyonna bizinensi ya slatternly, etali nnongoofu. Naye waliwo ekintu ekirala eky’okusambajja. Balaga nti ennyanja zonna eziyitibwa whale bulijjo ziwunya bubi. Kati okuvumwavumwa kuno okw’omuzizo kwasibuka kutya?

Nze ndowooza, nti kigobererwa bulungi okutuuka ku kutuuka kw’emmeeri za Greenland ezivuba envubu ezaasooka mu London, ebyasa ebisukka mu bibiri emabega. Kubanga abo abaali bavuba ennyanja ekika kya whale mu kiseera ekyo, era kati tebagezesa mafuta gaabwe ku nnyanja ng’emmeeri z’omu Bugwanjuba bwe zibadde zikola bulijjo; naye ng’osalako ekikuta ekipya mu butundutundu obutonotono, n’okisuula mu bituli bya bung eby’ebidomola ebinene, n’okitwala awaka mu ngeri eyo; obumpi bwa sizoni mu Nnyanja ezo ez’omuzira, n’embuyaga ez’amangu era ez’amaanyi ze bafuna, nga zigaana ekkubo eddala lyonna. Ekivaamu kiri nti, bwe bamenya mu kifo ekikuumirwamu, n’okutikkula emu ku masabo gano ag’ennyanja ekika kya whale, mu mwalo gw’e Greenland, akawoowo kaweebwayo ekintu ekifaananako n’ekyo ekiva mu kusima entaana y’ekibuga ekikadde, olw’emisingi gy’Eddwaaliro erya Lying-in.

Ekitundu nteebereza era, nti omusango guno omubi oguvunaanibwa abavubi ba whale mu ngeri y’emu guyinza okuteekebwa ku kubeerawo ku lubalama lw’ennyanja Greenland, mu biseera eby’edda, ekyalo ky’Abadaaki ekiyitibwa Schmerenburgh oba Smeerenberg, erinnya eryo erisembayo lye likozesebwa omuyivu Fogo Von Slack, mu mulimu gwe omunene ku Smells, ekitabo ekisomesebwa ku nsonga eyo. Nga erinnya lyayo bwe liyingizibwa mu ggwanga (smeer, fat; berg, okuteeka waggulu), ekyalo kino kyatandikibwawo okusobola okwetuusaako ekifo eky’okugezesebwamu blubber y’ekibinja ky’ennyanja ekika kya Dutch whale, awatali kutwalibwa waka e Holland olw’ekigendererwa ekyo.
