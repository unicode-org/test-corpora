Essuula 67. Okusala mu.

Lwali kiro kya Lwamukaaga, era Ssabbiiti ng’eyo bwe yaddirira! Ex officio Professors of Ssabbiiti okumenya bonna bavubi ba nnyanja. Pequod ey’amasanga yafuulibwa ekintu ekyalabika ng’ekifuuse eky’obuseegu; buli mulunnyanja omukanyama. Wandirowoozezza nti tuwaayo ente emmyuufu emitwalo kkumi eri bakatonda b’ennyanja.

Mu kifo ekisooka, ebikozesebwa ebinene ennyo eby’okusala, mu bintu ebirala ebizitowa ebirimu ekibinja kya bulooka okutwalira awamu ezisiigiddwa langi ya kiragala, era nga tewali musajja n’omu ayinza kusitula —ekibinja kino ekinene eky’emizabbibu kyawuubaala okutuuka waggulu ku main-top ne kinywezebwa nnyo ku lower mast-head, ekifo ekisinga amaanyi wonna waggulu w’ekisenge ky’emmeeri. Enkomerero y’omuguwa ogw’ekika kya hawser ogwazingulula nga guyita mu bintu bino ebizibu, olwo n’etwalibwa eri ekyuma ekikuba empewo, era bbulooka ennene eya wansi ey’ebikozesebwa n’ewuubaala waggulu w’ennyanja ekika kya whale; ku bulooka eno yassibwako akaguwa akanene aka blubber, akazitowa pawundi nga kikumi. Era kati nga bawaniriddwa mu mitendera waggulu w’ebbali, Starbuck ne Stubb, abafumbo, nga bakutte ebiso byabwe ebiwanvu, baatandika okusala ekituli mu mubiri olw’okuyingiza enkoba waggulu ddala ku biwaawaatiro by’ebbali ebibiri ebisinga okumpi. Kino kikoleddwa, layini egazi, eriko ekitundu ekigazi nga yeetooloola ekinnya, ekikonde kiyingizibwamu, era omubiri omukulu ogw’abakozi nga gukuba chorus ey’omu nsiko, kati gutandika okuzitowa mu bantu omu omunene ku Windlass. Mu kaseera ako, emmeeri yonna bwe yeekulukuunya ku ludda lwe; buli bbuutu mu ye etandika ng’emisumaali gy’ennyumba enkadde mu budde obw’omuzira; akankana, akankana, era n’anyenya emitwe gye egy’amasasi egyali gitidde okutuuka mu bbanga. Yeeyongera okwesigamira ku nnyanja ekika kya whale, ate nga buli kusitula omukka okw’ekyuma ekikuba empewo kuddibwamu okusituka okuyamba okuva mu biwujjo; okutuusa ku nkomerero, eddoboozi ery’amangu, eryewuunyisa lwe liwulirwa; n’okusika omuguwa okunene emmeeri yeekulukuunya waggulu n’emabega okuva ku nnyanja ekika kya whale, era ekyuma ekiwangula ne kisituka mu maaso nga kisika oluvannyuma lwayo enkomerero ya semicircular etali ya nneekulungirivu ey’olutimbe olusooka olwa blubber. Kati nga blubber bw’ezinga ennyanja whale ddala ng’olususu bwe lukola emicungwa, bwe kityo bwe kiggyibwa ku mubiri ddala ng’emicungwa oluusi bwe giggyibwako nga bagifuula spiralizing. Kubanga okunyigirizibwa okukuumibwa buli kiseera olw’omuyaga ogw’empewo bulijjo kukuuma envubu ng’eyiringisibwa emirundi n’emirundi mu mazzi, era ng’omuguwa mu lugoye lumu gusekula mu ngeri y’emu ku layini eyitibwa “envubu,” mu kiseera kye kimu nga kisaliddwako ebiso bya Starbuck ne Stubb, . abafumbo; era nga bwe kiri bwe kityo bwe kisekulwa bwe kityo, era ddala olw’ekikolwa ekyo kyennyini, buli kiseera kiwanirirwa waggulu n’okudda waggulu okutuuka ku nkomerero yaakyo eya waggulu kirundidde waggulu-waggulu; abasajja abali ku ekyuma ekikuba empewo olwo ne balekera awo okusitula, era okumala akaseera katono oba abiri ekibinja eky’ekitalo ekitonnya omusaayi ne kiwuubaala okuva mu bbanga, era buli muntu aliwo alina okufaayo ennyo okukyewala nga kiwuubaala, bwe kitaba ekyo ayinza okukukuba ebikonde mu matu n’amusuula omutwe mu nnyanja.

Omu ku ba harpooneers ababeerawo kati agenda mu maaso n’ekyokulwanyisa ekiwanvu era ekinene ekiyitibwa boarding-sword, era bw’alaba omukisa gwe mu ngeri ey’obukugu asala ekituli ekinene mu kitundu ekya wansi eky’ekibinja ekiwuubaala. Mu kinnya kino, enkomerero y’ekyuma ekinene eky’okubiri ekikyukakyuka olwo n’ekwatibwa enkoba okusobola okusigala ng’ekwata ku bbugumu, okusobola okwetegekera ekiddako. Awo, omukubi w’ebitala ono omukugu, ng’alabula emikono gyonna okuyimirira, addamu okukola ekkubo lya ssaayansi ku mmisa, era n’okusalasala okutono ku mabbali, okuggwaamu essuubi, okuwuuma, n’agisalako ddala mu bibiri; bwe kityo nga ekitundu ekimpi ekya wansi kikyali kya mangu, ekitundu ekiwanvu ekya waggulu, ekiyitibwa blanket-piece, kiwuubaala nga kitangaavu, era nga byonna byetegefu okukka. Abasitula mu maaso kati baddamu okuyimba, era nga tackle emu esekula n’okusitula strip eyookubiri okuva ku whale, endala ekendeezebwa mpola, era wansi egenda strip esooka okuyita mu main hatchway wansi ddala, mu parlor etaliimu bintu eyitibwa the ekisenge kya blubber. Mu muzigo guno ogw’ekizikiza emikono egy’enjawulo egy’amaloboozi gisigala nga giyiringisibwa wala ekitundu ekiwanvu eky’omufaliso nga kiringa ekibinja ekinene ekiramu eky’emisota egyalukibwa. Era bwe kityo omulimu bwe gugenda mu maaso; tackles ebbiri ezisitula n’okukka omulundi gumu; bombi whale ne windlass heaving, heavers okuyimba, blubber-room gentlemen coiling, mates scarfing, emmeeri okunyigirizibwa, n’emikono gyonna okulayira oluusi n’oluusi, mu ngeri y’okukkakkanya okusika omuguwa okwa bulijjo.





ESSUULA 68. Omufaliso.

Nze nfuddeyo nnyo ku nsonga eyo etali ya buzibu, olususu lw’ennyanja ekika kya whale. Nfunye okusika omuguwa ku nsonga eno n’abavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale abalina obumanyirivu nga batengejja, era abakugu mu by’obutonde abayize ku lukalu. Endowooza yange eyasooka tekyuse; naye ndowooza yokka.

Ekyebuuzibwa, olususu lw’ennyanja ekika kya whale luli ki era luli ludda wa? Omanyi dda blubber ye kye ki. Okwo okufulumira ku bbuutu kintu kya nnyama y’ente ennywevu, ey’empeke okumpi, naye nga nkalu, nga kigonvu ate nga kikwatagana, era nga kiva ku yinsi munaana oba kkumi n’ebiri n’ekkumi n’etaano mu buwanvu.

Kati, wadde nga mu kusooka kiyinza okulabika ng’eky’obusiru okwogera ku lususu lw’ekitonde kyonna ng’olw’ekika ekyo ekikwatagana n’obuwanvu, naye mu butuufu zino si nsonga eziwakanya okuteebereza ng’okwo; kubanga tosobola kusitula layeri ndala yonna enzito ezibikka okuva mu mubiri gwa whale wabula blubber eyo yennyini; era oluwuzi olusinga okuzingiramu ensolo yonna, bwe luba nga lunene nnyo, ekyo kiyinza okuba kiki okuggyako olususu? Kituufu, okuva ku mulambo gw’ennyanja ogutafumbiddwa ogw’ennyanja ekika kya whale, oyinza okusenya n’omukono gwo ekintu ekigonvu ekitaliiko kkomo, ekitangalijja, ekintu ekifaananako ebitundutundu ebisinga obugonvu ebya Isinglass, kyokka kumpi kibeera kigonvu era kigonvu nga satin; kwe kugamba, nga tennakalizibwa, bwe kitakoma ku kukwatagana na kugonvuwa, wabula ne kifuuka kikaluba nnyo era nga kikutuka. Nnina ebitundu ebikalu ng’ebyo ebiwerako, bye nkozesa okukola obubonero mu bitabo byange eby’ennyanja whale. Kiba kitangaavu, nga bwe nnagambye emabegako; era olw’okuteekebwa ku lupapula olukubiddwa, oluusi nsanyuse nnyo olw’okulowooza nti lwalina obuyinza obugulumiza. Mu ngeri yonna, kinyuma okusoma ebikwata ku nnyanja ekika kya whale ng’oyita mu ndabirwamu zaabwe, nga bw’oyinza okugamba. Naye kye nvuga wano kiri kino. Ekintu ekyo kye kimu ekigonvu ekitaliiko kkomo, ekya isinglass, nga, nkiriza, kiteeka omubiri gwonna ogwa whale, si nnyo okutwalibwa ng’olususu lw’ekitonde, ng’olususu lw’olususu, bwe tuyinza okugamba; kubanga kyali kya kusaaga kwokka okugamba, nti olususu olutuufu olw’ennyanja ekika kya whale ennene ennyo lugonvu era lugonvu okusinga olususu lw’omwana eyaakazaalibwa. Naye tewali kisingawo ku bino.

nga tufudde nti blubber ye lususu lwa whale; awo, olususu luno, nga bwe kiri mu mbeera ya Sperm Whale ennene ennyo, bwe lunaavaamu ekitundu ekinene eky’ebipipa kikumi eby’amafuta; era, bwe kitunuulirwa nti, mu bungi, oba okusingawo obuzito, amafuta ago, mu mbeera yaago eraga, gaba bitundu bisatu byokka eby’okuna, so si kintu kyonna eky’ekkanzu; n’olwekyo endowooza ezimu eyinza okubaawo ku bunene bw’ekizito ekyo ekirimu obulamu, ekitundu kyokka ku kisenge kyakyo kyokka kivaamu ennyanja ey’amazzi ng’eyo. Bw’obalirira ebipipa kkumi ku ttani, olina ttani kkumi ku buzito obutuufu obw’ebitundu bisatu ku bina byokka eby’ebintu eby’olususu lw’ennyanja ekika kya whale.

Mu bulamu, ekitundu ekirabika ekya Sperm Whale si kye kisinga obutono mu byewuunyo bingi by’alaga. Kumpi bulijjo yonna esaliddwa mu ngeri ey’ekyekulungirivu era n’eddamu okusalako n’obubonero obugolokofu obutaliiko muwendo mu nsengeka enzito, ekintu ekiringa ebyo ebiri mu biwandiiko ebisinga obulungi eby’ennyiriri eby’e Yitale. Naye obubonero buno tebulabika nga bwasiimibwa ku kintu kya Isinglass waggulu ekyogeddwako, naye bulabika nga bulabibwa okuyita mu kyo, nga bwe bwayobwa ku mubiri gwennyini. Era bino byonna si. Mu mbeera ezimu, eri eriiso ery’amangu, eritunula, obubonero obwo obw’ennyiriri, nga mu kifaananyi ekituufu, naye nga busobola okuzimba ensengeka endala ewala ennyo. Bino biwandiikiddwa mu ngeri ya hieroglyphical; kwe kugamba, bw’oyita cyphers ezo ez’ekyama ku bisenge bya piramids hieroglyphics, olwo ekyo kye kigambo ekituufu eky’okukozesa mu nkolagana eriwo kati. Olw’okujjukira ennyo ebiwandiiko ebiwandiikiddwa ku Sperm Whale emu naddala, nnakwatibwako nnyo n’essowaani ekiikirira ennukuta z’Abayindi enkadde ezaali zisaliddwa ku bipande ebimanyiddwa ennyo eby’empandiika ku lubalama lw’omugga Upper Mississippi. Okufaananako n’amayinja ago ag’ekyama, nago, envubu ekika kya mystic-marked whale esigala nga tesobola kutegeerekeka. Okwogera kuno ku njazi z’Abayindi kinzijukiza ekintu ekirala. Ng’oggyeeko ebintu ebirala byonna eby’ebweru eby’ennyanja y’ensigo by’alaga, tatera kwolesa mugongo, n’okusingira ddala ku mabbali ge, agassibwamu mu kitundu ekinene eky’endabika eya bulijjo ey’ennyiriri, olw’okukutuka okungi okw’obugwenyufu, okutwalira awamu okw’ekitundu ekitali kya bulijjo, ekitali kigenderere ekifo ekilondemu kukintu. Nsaana okugamba nti enjazi ezo eza New England eziri ku lubalama lw’ennyanja, Agassiz z’alowooza nti zirina obubonero bw’okukwatagana okw’amaanyi okw’okusenya n’ensozi ennene ennyo ezitengejja —nsaana okugamba, nti enjazi ezo tezirina kufaanana katono ku Sperm Whale mu kino entongole. Era kirabika gyendi nti okukunya ng’okwo mu nnyanja ekika kya whale oboolyawo kukolebwa olw’okukwatagana okw’obulabe n’ennyanja ekika kya whale endala; kubanga nsinga kuzirabira mu nte ennene, ezikuze mu bujjuvu ez’ekika ekyo.

Ekigambo ekirala oba bibiri ebikwata ku nsonga eno ey’olususu oba olususu lw’ennyanja ekika kya whale. Kyagambibwa dda, nti kimuggyibwako mu bitundu ebiwanvu, ebiyitibwa blanket-pieces. Okufaananako n’ebigambo ebisinga eby’ennyanja, kino kya ssanyu nnyo era kya makulu. Kubanga ddala whale ezingibwa mu blubber ye nga mu bulangiti oba counterpane entuufu; oba, ekisinga obulungi, poncho y’Omuyindi yaseerera ku mutwe gwe, n’esala enkomerero ye. Olw’okubikka omubiri gwe mu ngeri ennungi, ennyanja eno esobozesa okwekuuma obulungi mu mbeera zonna ez’obudde, mu nnyanja zonna, mu biseera byonna, n’amayengo gonna. Kiki ekyandifuuse ennyonyi ekika kya Greenland whale, tugambe, mu nnyanja ezo ezikankana, ezirimu omuzira ez’omu Bukiikakkono, singa teziweebwa surtout ye ennungi? Kituufu nti ebyennyanja ebirala bisangibwa nga biyitiridde mu mazzi ago aga Hyperborean; naye bino, ka bibeere byetegereza, bye byennyanja byo eby’omusaayi omunnyogovu, ebitaliiko mawuggwe, ng’olubuto lwabyo lwennyini firiigi; ebitonde, ebibuguma wansi w’omusana gw’olusozi lwa ice, ng’omutambuze mu kiseera eky’obutiti bw’awummulira mu maaso g’omuliro gw’ekiyumba ky’abagenyi; so ng’ate okufaananako omuntu, ennyanja ekika kya whale erina amawuggwe n’omusaayi ogubuguma. Freeze omusaayi gwe, n’afa. Nga kya kitalo olwo —okuggyako oluvannyuma lw’okunnyonnyolwa —nti ekisolo kino ekinene, gy’ali ebbugumu ery’omubiri lye tekyetaagisa nga bwe liri eri omuntu; nga kya kitalo nnyo okusangibwa awaka, ng’annyikiddwa ku mimwa gye obulamu bwe bwonna mu mazzi ago ag’omu Arctic! awali, abalunnyanja bwe bagwa mu nnyanja, oluusi basangibwa, oluvannyuma lw’emyezi, nga bafuuse bbugumu nga beesimbye mu mitima gy’ennimiro za bbalaafu, ng’enseenene bw’esangibwa nga esibiddwa mu amber. Naye ekisinga okwewuunyisa kwe kumanya, nga bwe kibadde kikakasibwa okugezesa, nti omusaayi gw’ennyanja ekika kya polar whale gubuguma okusinga ogwa Borneo Negro mu kyeya.

Kirabika gyendi, nti wano tulaba empisa ennungi etali ya bulijjo ey’obulamu obw’amaanyi obw’omuntu kinnoomu, n’empisa ennungi etali ya bulijjo ey’ebisenge ebinene, n’empisa ennungi etali ya bulijjo ey’obugazi obw’omunda. Oh, musajja wattu! weegomba era weekolere ekyokulabirako ky’ennyanja ekika kya whale! Naawe, sigala ng’obuguma wakati mu bbalaafu. Naawe beera mu nsi eno nga toli wa yo. Beera cool ku equator; amazzi go gakuume ku Pole. Nga dome ennene eya Petero Omutukuvu, era nga whale ennene, kuuma, Ayi musajja! mu biseera byonna ebbugumu lyo.

Naye nga kyangu era nga tekirina ssuubi okuyigiriza ebintu bino ebirungi! Ku kusituka, nga batono nnyo aba domed nga St. Peter’s! wa bitonde, nga bitono nnyo ng’ennyanja ekika kya whale!





ESSUULA 69. Okuziika.

“Sika enjegere! Omulambo gugende emabega!”

Tackles ennene kati zikoze omulimu gwazo. Omubiri omweru ogwasekuddwa ogw’ennyanja ekika kya whale eyasaliddwako omutwe guyakaayakana ng’entaana ey’amayinja amabajje; wadde nga kyakyusiddwa mu langi, mu ngeri etegeerekeka tekifiiriddwa kintu kyonna mu bungi. Kikyali kinene nnyo. Mpola mpola yeeyongera okulengejja, amazzi agagyetoolodde nga gakutuse era ne gamansira shark ezitakkuta, era empewo waggulu n’enyiiga olw’ennyonyi ezikuba enduulu ez’obukambwe, emimwa gyazo nga giringa embalaasi nnyingi ezivuma mu nnyanja ekika kya whale. Phantom ennene ennyo enjeru etaliiko mutwe yeeyongera okulengejja okuva ku mmeeri, era buli muggo gwe gulengejja bwe gutyo, ebirabika ng’ebiwujjo ebya square ebya shark n’ebinyonyi ebiyitibwa cubic roods, byongera ku ddoboozi ery’ettemu. Okumala essaawa n’essaawa okuva ku mmeeri kumpi etaliiko kye yeetooloovu okulaba okw’ekivve. Wansi w’eggulu erya azure eritaliiko bire era erigonvu, ku maaso amalungi ag’ennyanja ennyuvu, ewuubaala empewo ez’essanyu, ekibinja ekyo ekinene eky’okufa kitengejja ne kigenda, okutuusa lwe kibula mu ndowooza ezitaliiko kkomo.

Waliwo okuziika okusinga okunakuwala n'okusekerera! Envubu z’omu nnyanja zonna mu kukungubaga okw’okutya Katonda, enseenene z’omu bbanga zonna mu kiseera ekituufu mu langi enjeru oba ez’amabala. Mu bulamu naye batono ku bo abandiyambye whale, I ween, singa peradventure yali akyetaaga; naye ku kijjulo ky’okuziika kwe basinga okutya Katonda. Oh, vultureism ey’entiisa ey’ensi! okuva mu ekyo si whale esinga amaanyi gye ya bwereere.

Era eno si nkomerero. Wadde ng’omulambo gufuuse ogwonoonebwa, omuzimu ogw’okwesasuza guwona ne guwuubaala ku gwo okutiisa. Espied by some timid man-of-war or blundering discovery-vessel from afar, nga ebanga erizibikira ebinyonyi ebiwunya, wadde kiri kityo, likyalaga ekibinja ekyeru nga kitengejja mu musana, n’ekifuuyira ekyeru nga kisitula waggulu ku kyo; amangu ago omulambo gwa whale ogutaliiko bulabe, nga guliko engalo ezikankana guteekebwa wansi mu kikondo — ebiwonvu, amayinja, n’ebimenya wano: weegendereze! Era okumala emyaka oluvannyuma, mpozzi, emmeeri zeewala ekifo ekyo; okukibuukako ng’endiga ezisirusiru bwe zibuuka ku vacuum, kubanga mu kusooka omukulembeze waabwe yabuuka eyo ng’omuggo gukwatiddwa. Waliwo etteeka lyo ery'ebintu ebisookerwako; Waliwo omugaso gwo ogw'ennono; waliwo emboozi y’okuwona kwo okw’obukakanyavu okw’enzikiriza enkadde ezitakka wansi ku nsi, era kati nga teziwuguka wadde mu bbanga! Waliwo obusodokisi!

Bwe kityo, wadde nga mu bulamu omubiri gwa Whale omunene guyinza okuba nga gwali ntiisa ya ddala eri abalabe be, mu kufa kwe omuzimu gwe gufuuka okutya okutaliimu maanyi eri ensi.

Oli mukkiriza mu mizimu, mukwano gwange? Waliwo emizimu emirala okuggyako egya Cock-Lane, n’abasajja abazito ennyo okusinga Dokita Johnson abagikkiririzaamu.





ESSUULA 70. Ensigo ya Sphynx.

Tekyandibadde kulekebwawo nti nga tannayambula ddala mulambo gwa leviathan, yatemebwako omutwe. Kati, okutema omutwe gwa Sperm Whale kikolwa kya ssaayansi mu mubiri, abasawo abalina obumanyirivu mu kulongoosa whale kye benyumirizaamu ennyo: era si awatali nsonga.

Lowooza nti ennyanja ekika kya whale terina kintu kyonna kiyinza kuyitibwa bulungi nsingo; okwawukana ku ekyo, omutwe gwe n’omubiri gwe we birabika nga byegatta, awo, mu kifo ekyo kyennyini, we wali ekitundu kye ekisinga obunene. Era jjukira nti omusawo alongoosa alina okulongoosa ng’asinziira waggulu, ng’ayingirira ffuuti nga munaana oba kkumi wakati we n’omuntu gw’agenda okukozesa, era ng’omuntu oyo kumpi yeekwese mu nnyanja eyaka langi, eyeekulukuunya, era emirundi mingi erimu akajagalalo n’okubutuka. Era kijjukire nti mu mbeera zino embi alina okusala ebigere bingi wansi mu mubiri; era mu ngeri eyo ey’okunsi, awatali kufuna nnyo n’akatono akatunula mu kiwujjo ekikonziba buli kiseera ekikoleddwa bw’atyo, alina mu ngeri ey’obukugu okwewala ebitundu byonna ebiriraanyewo, ebikugirwa, era n’agabanya ddala omugongo mu kifo ekikulu ennyo nga guyingizibwa mu ekiwanga. N’olwekyo, teweewuunya Stubb okwewaana, nti yasaba eddakiika kkumi zokka okusalako omutwe gw’ennyonyi ekika kya sperm whale?

Bwe basooka okusazibwamu, omutwe gusuulibwa emabega ne gukwatibwa eyo ku waya okutuusa omubiri lwe gwaggyibwamu. Ekyo bwe kikolebwa, bwe kiba kya whale entono kisitulwa ku ddeeke okusuulibwa mu bugenderevu. Naye, nga Leviathan akuze mu bujjuvu kino tekisoboka; kubanga omutwe gwa sperm whale gukwatira ddala kumpi kimu kya kusatu eky’ekitundu kyayo kyonna ekinene, era okuyimiriza ddala omugugu ng’ogwo, ne bwe gukolebwa mu bikolwa ebinene ennyo eby’omuvubi w’ennyanja, kino kyali kintu kya bwereere ng’okugezaako okupima ekiraalo ky’Abadaaki mu minzaani z’abasuubuzi b’amajolobero .

Envubu ya Pequod bwe yali esaliddwako omutwe era omulambo ne guggyibwako, omutwe gwasitulwa ku ludda lw’emmeeri —nga kitundu kya kkubo okuva mu nnyanja, olwo ekitundu ekinene esobole okusitulwa ekintu kyayo enzaalwa. era awo n’emikono egy’emikono egy’okunyigirizibwa nga ginywezeddwa bulungi ku yo, olw’okusika okunene okunene okuva ku mutwe gw’omuti ogwa wansi, ne buli mukono gw’oluggya ku ludda olwo nga gufuluma ng’ekiwujjo ku mayengo; eyo, omutwe ogwo ogwatonnya omusaayi gwali guwanikiddwa mu kiwato kya Pequod ng’ogwa Holofune omunene ennyo okuva ku musipi gwa Yudith.

Omulimu guno ogwasembayo bwe gwatuuka ku ssaawa 12 ez’emisana, era abavubi ne bagenda wansi ku kijjulo kyabwe. Akasiriikiriro kaafuga ddeeke eyali ebaddemu akajagalalo naye kati nga tewali muntu yenna. Obukkakkamu obw’amaanyi obw’ekikomo, ng’ekimuli kya kiragala ekisangibwa mu nsi yonna, kyali kyeyongera okubikkula ebikoola byakyo ebitaliimu maloboozi ebitaliiko kipimo ku nnyanja.

CHAPTER 72. The Monkey-Rope.

In the tumultuous business of cutting-in and attending to a whale, there is much running backwards and forwards among the crew. Now hands are wanted here, and then again hands are wanted there. There is no staying in any one place; for at one and the same time everything has to be done everywhere. It is much the same with him who endeavors the description of the scene. We must now retrace our way a little. It was mentioned that upon first breaking ground in the whale's back, the blubber-hook was inserted into the original hole there cut by the spades of the mates. But how did so clumsy and weighty a mass as that same hook get fixed in that hole? It was inserted there by my particular friend Queequeg, whose duty it was, as harpooneer, to descend upon the monster's back for the special purpose referred to. But in very many cases, circumstances require that the harpooneer shall remain on the whale till the whole flensing or stripping operation is concluded. The whale, be it observed, lies almost entirely submerged, excepting the immediate parts operated upon. So down there, some ten feet below the level of the deck, the poor harpooneer flounders about, half on the whale and half in the water, as the vast mass revolves like a tread-mill beneath him. On the occasion in question, Queequeg figured in the Highland costume—a shirt and socks—in which to my eyes, at least, he appeared to uncommon advantage; and no one had a better chance to observe him, as will presently be seen.

Being the savage's bowsman, that is, the person who pulled the bow-oar in his boat (the second one from forward), it was my cheerful duty to attend upon him while taking that hard-scrabble scramble upon the dead whale's back. You have seen Italian organ-boys holding a dancing-ape by a long cord. Just so, from the ship's steep side, did I hold Queequeg down there in the sea, by what is technically called in the fishery a monkey-rope, attached to a strong strip of canvas belted round his waist.

It was a humorously perilous business for both of us. For, before we proceed further, it must be said that the monkey-rope was fast at both ends; fast to Queequeg's broad canvas belt, and fast to my narrow leather one. So that for better or for worse, we two, for the time, were wedded; and should poor Queequeg sink to rise no more, then both usage and honor demanded, that instead of cutting the cord, it should drag me down in his wake. So, then, an elongated Siamese ligature united us. Queequeg was my own inseparable twin brother; nor could I any way get rid of the dangerous liabilities which the hempen bond entailed.

So strongly and metaphysically did I conceive of my situation then, that while earnestly watching his motions, I seemed distinctly to perceive that my own individuality was now merged in a joint stock company of two; that my free will had received a mortal wound; and that another's mistake or misfortune might plunge innocent me into unmerited disaster and death. Therefore, I saw that here was a sort of interregnum in Providence; for its even-handed equity never could have so gross an injustice. And yet still further pondering—while I jerked him now and then from between the whale and ship, which would threaten to jam him—still further pondering, I say, I saw that this situation of mine was the precise situation of every mortal that breathes; only, in most cases, he, one way or other, has this Siamese connexion with a plurality of other mortals. If your banker breaks, you snap; if your apothecary by mistake sends you poison in your pills, you die. True, you may say that, by exceeding caution, you may possibly escape these and the multitudinous other evil chances of life. But handle Queequeg's monkey-rope heedfully as I would, sometimes he jerked it so, that I came very near sliding overboard. Nor could I possibly forget that, do what I would, I only had the management of one end of it.*

*The monkey-rope is found in all whalers; but it was only in the Pequod that the monkey and his holder were ever tied together. This improvement upon the original usage was introduced by no less a man than Stubb, in order to afford the imperilled harpooneer the strongest possible guarantee for the faithfulness and vigilance of his monkey-rope holder.

I have hinted that I would often jerk poor Queequeg from between the whale and the ship—where he would occasionally fall, from the incessant rolling and swaying of both. But this was not the only jamming jeopardy he was exposed to. Unappalled by the massacre made upon them during the night, the sharks now freshly and more keenly allured by the before pent blood which began to flow from the carcass—the rabid creatures swarmed round it like bees in a beehive.

And right in among those sharks was Queequeg; who often pushed them aside with his floundering feet. A thing altogether incredible were it not that attracted by such prey as a dead whale, the otherwise miscellaneously carnivorous shark will seldom touch a man.

Nevertheless, it may well be believed that since they have such a ravenous finger in the pie, it is deemed but wise to look sharp to them. Accordingly, besides the monkey-rope, with which I now and then jerked the poor fellow from too close a vicinity to the maw of what seemed a peculiarly ferocious shark—he was provided with still another protection. Suspended over the side in one of the stages, Tashtego and Daggoo continually flourished over his head a couple of keen whale-spades, wherewith they slaughtered as many sharks as they could reach. This procedure of theirs, to be sure, was very disinterested and benevolent of them. They meant Queequeg's best happiness, I admit; but in their hasty zeal to befriend him, and from the circumstance that both he and the sharks were at times half hidden by the blood-muddled water, those indiscreet spades of theirs would come nearer amputating a leg than a tail. But poor Queequeg, I suppose, straining and gasping there with that great iron hook—poor Queequeg, I suppose, only prayed to his Yojo, and gave up his life into the hands of his gods.

Well, well, my dear comrade and twin-brother, thought I, as I drew in and then slacked off the rope to every swell of the sea—what matters it, after all? Are you not the precious image of each and all of us men in this whaling world? That unsounded ocean you gasp in, is Life; those sharks, your foes; those spades, your friends; and what between sharks and spades you are in a sad pickle and peril, poor lad.

But courage! there is good cheer in store for you, Queequeg. For now, as with blue lips and blood-shot eyes the exhausted savage at last climbs up the chains and stands all dripping and involuntarily trembling over the side; the steward advances, and with a benevolent, consolatory glance hands him—what? Some hot Cognac? No! hands him, ye gods! hands him a cup of tepid ginger and water!

“Ginger? Do I smell ginger?” suspiciously asked Stubb, coming near. “Yes, this must be ginger,” peering into the as yet untasted cup. Then standing as if incredulous for a while, he calmly walked towards the astonished steward slowly saying, “Ginger? ginger? and will you have the goodness to tell me, Mr. Dough-Boy, where lies the virtue of ginger? Ginger! is ginger the sort of fuel you use, Dough-boy, to kindle a fire in this shivering cannibal? Ginger!—what the devil is ginger? Sea-coal? firewood?—lucifer matches?—tinder?—gunpowder?—what the devil is ginger, I say, that you offer this cup to our poor Queequeg here.”

“There is some sneaking Temperance Society movement about this business,” he suddenly added, now approaching Starbuck, who had just come from forward. “Will you look at that kannakin, sir: smell of it, if you please.” Then watching the mate's countenance, he added, “The steward, Mr. Starbuck, had the face to offer that calomel and jalap to Queequeg, there, this instant off the whale. Is the steward an apothecary, sir? and may I ask whether this is the sort of bitters by which he blows back the life into a half-drowned man?”

“I trust not,” said Starbuck, “it is poor stuff enough.”

“Aye, aye, steward,” cried Stubb, “we'll teach you to drug a harpooneer; none of your apothecary's medicine here; you want to poison us, do ye? You have got out insurances on our lives and want to murder us all, and pocket the proceeds, do ye?”

“It was not me,” cried Dough-Boy, “it was Aunt Charity that brought the ginger on board; and bade me never give the harpooneers any spirits, but only this ginger-jub—so she called it.”

“Ginger-jub! you gingerly rascal! take that! and run along with ye to the lockers, and get something better. I hope I do no wrong, Mr. Starbuck. It is the captain's orders—grog for the harpooneer on a whale.”

“Enough,” replied Starbuck, “only don't hit him again, but—”

“Oh, I never hurt when I hit, except when I hit a whale or something of that sort; and this fellow's a weazel. What were you about saying, sir?”

“Only this: go down with him, and get what thou wantest thyself.”

When Stubb reappeared, he came with a dark flask in one hand, and a sort of tea-caddy in the other. The first contained strong spirits, and was handed to Queequeg; the second was Aunt Charity's gift, and that was freely given to the waves.





CHAPTER 73. Stubb and Flask kill a Right Whale; and Then Have a Talk over Him.

It must be borne in mind that all this time we have a Sperm Whale's prodigious head hanging to the Pequod's side. But we must let it continue hanging there a while till we can get a chance to attend to it. For the present other matters press, and the best we can do now for the head, is to pray heaven the tackles may hold.

Now, during the past night and forenoon, the Pequod had gradually drifted into a sea, which, by its occasional patches of yellow brit, gave unusual tokens of the vicinity of Right Whales, a species of the Leviathan that but few supposed to be at this particular time lurking anywhere near. And though all hands commonly disdained the capture of those inferior creatures; and though the Pequod was not commissioned to cruise for them at all, and though she had passed numbers of them near the Crozetts without lowering a boat; yet now that a Sperm Whale had been brought alongside and beheaded, to the surprise of all, the announcement was made that a Right Whale should be captured that day, if opportunity offered.

Nor was this long wanting. Tall spouts were seen to leeward; and two boats, Stubb's and Flask's, were detached in pursuit. Pulling further and further away, they at last became almost invisible to the men at the mast-head. But suddenly in the distance, they saw a great heap of tumultuous white water, and soon after news came from aloft that one or both the boats must be fast. An interval passed and the boats were in plain sight, in the act of being dragged right towards the ship by the towing whale. So close did the monster come to the hull, that at first it seemed as if he meant it malice; but suddenly going down in a maelstrom, within three rods of the planks, he wholly disappeared from view, as if diving under the keel. “Cut, cut!” was the cry from the ship to the boats, which, for one instant, seemed on the point of being brought with a deadly dash against the vessel's side. But having plenty of line yet in the tubs, and the whale not sounding very rapidly, they paid out abundance of rope, and at the same time pulled with all their might so as to get ahead of the ship. For a few minutes the struggle was intensely critical; for while they still slacked out the tightened line in one direction, and still plied their oars in another, the contending strain threatened to take them under. But it was only a few feet advance they sought to gain. And they stuck to it till they did gain it; when instantly, a swift tremor was felt running like lightning along the keel, as the strained line, scraping beneath the ship, suddenly rose to view under her bows, snapping and quivering; and so flinging off its drippings, that the drops fell like bits of broken glass on the water, while the whale beyond also rose to sight, and once more the boats were free to fly. But the fagged whale abated his speed, and blindly altering his course, went round the stern of the ship towing the two boats after him, so that they performed a complete circuit.

Meantime, they hauled more and more upon their lines, till close flanking him on both sides, Stubb answered Flask with lance for lance; and thus round and round the Pequod the battle went, while the multitudes of sharks that had before swum round the Sperm Whale's body, rushed to the fresh blood that was spilled, thirstily drinking at every new gash, as the eager Israelites did at the new bursting fountains that poured from the smitten rock.

At last his spout grew thick, and with a frightful roll and vomit, he turned upon his back a corpse.

While the two headsmen were engaged in making fast cords to his flukes, and in other ways getting the mass in readiness for towing, some conversation ensued between them.

“I wonder what the old man wants with this lump of foul lard,” said Stubb, not without some disgust at the thought of having to do with so ignoble a leviathan.

“Wants with it?” said Flask, coiling some spare line in the boat's bow, “did you never hear that the ship which but once has a Sperm Whale's head hoisted on her starboard side, and at the same time a Right Whale's on the larboard; did you never hear, Stubb, that that ship can never afterwards capsize?”

“I don't know, but I heard that gamboge ghost of a Fedallah saying so, and he seems to know all about ships' charms. But I sometimes think he'll charm the ship to no good at last. I don't half like that chap, Stubb. Did you ever notice how that tusk of his is a sort of carved into a snake's head, Stubb?”

“Sink him! I never look at him at all; but if ever I get a chance of a dark night, and he standing hard by the bulwarks, and no one by; look down there, Flask”—pointing into the sea with a peculiar motion of both hands—“Aye, will I! Flask, I take that Fedallah to be the devil in disguise. Do you believe that cock and bull story about his having been stowed away on board ship? He's the devil, I say. The reason why you don't see his tail, is because he tucks it up out of sight; he carries it coiled away in his pocket, I guess. Why, they say as how he went a sauntering into the old flag-ship once, switching his tail about devilish easy and gentlemanlike, and inquiring if the old governor was at home. Well, he was at home, and asked the devil what he wanted. The devil, switching his hoofs, up and says, 'I want John.' 'What for?' says the old governor. 'What business is that of yours,' says the devil, getting mad,—'I want to use him.' “I think I remember some such story as you were telling,” said Flask, when at last the two boats were slowly advancing with their burden towards the ship, “but I can't remember where.”

“Now, do you mean what you say, and have been saying all along, Stubb?”

The boats were here hailed, to tow the whale on the larboard side, where fluke chains and other necessaries were already prepared for securing him.

“Didn't I tell you so?” said Flask; “yes, you'll soon see this right whale's head hoisted up opposite that parmacetti's.”

In good time, Flask's saying proved true. As before, the Pequod steeply leaned over towards the sperm whale's head, now, by the counterpoise of both heads, she regained her even keel; though sorely strained, you may well believe. So, when on one side you hoist in Locke's head, you go over that way; but now, on the other side, hoist in Kant's and you come back again; but in very poor plight. Thus, some minds for ever keep trimming boat. Oh, ye foolish! throw all these thunder-heads overboard, and then you will float light and right.

In disposing of the body of a right whale, when brought alongside the ship, the same preliminary proceedings commonly take place as in the case of a sperm whale; only, in the latter instance, the head is cut off whole, but in the former the lips and tongue are separately removed and hoisted on deck, with all the well known black bone attached to what is called the crown-piece. But nothing like this, in the present case, had been done. The carcases of both whales had dropped astern; and the head-laden ship not a little resembled a mule carrying a pair of overburdening panniers.

Meantime, Fedallah was calmly eyeing the right whale's head, and ever and anon glancing from the deep wrinkles there to the lines in his own hand. And Ahab chanced so to stand, that the Parsee occupied his shadow; while, if the Parsee's shadow was there at all it seemed only to blend with, and lengthen Ahab's. As the crew toiled on, Laplandish speculations were bandied among them, concerning all these passing things.
