ESSUULA 55. Eby’ebifaananyi eby’ekitalo eby’ennyanja ekika kya Whales.

I shall ere long paint to you as well as one can without canvas, ekintu nga ekifaananyi ekituufu eky’ennyanja whale nga mu butuufu bw’alabika eri eriiso ly’omusajja whale nga mu mubiri gwe ogw’enkomeredde whale esimbiddwa ku mabbali g’emmeeri ya whale bwe kityo asobola okulinnyirira mu bwenkanya eyo. N’olwekyo, kiyinza okuba eky’omugaso emabegako okulangirira ebifaananyi ebyo eby’okulowoozaako eby’okwewuunya ebimukwatako ebisomooza n’obwesige okukkiriza kw’omusajja ow’oku ttaka. Kye kiseera okutereeza ensi mu nsonga eno, nga tukakasa ebifaananyi ng’ebyo eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale byonna bikyamu.

Kiyinzika okuba nti ensibuko enkulu ey’obulimba obwo bwonna obw’ebifaananyi ejja kusangibwa mu bibumbe ebisinga obukadde eby’Abahindu, Abamisiri, n’Abayonaani. Kubanga okuva mu biseera ebyo eby’obuyiiya naye nga tebirina mpisa nga ku bipande by’amayinja amabajje ebya yeekaalu, ku bituuti by’ebibumbe, ne ku ngabo, emidaali, ebikopo, n’effeeza, ennyonyi eno ekika kya dolphin yali ekubiddwa mu minzaani ez’ebyokulwanyisa eby’enjegere ng’eya Saladin, n’omutwe ogufaanana nga guliko enkoofiira Omutukuvu George's; okuva olwo waliwo ekintu eky’ekika kye kimu ekya layisinsi ekifuga, si mu bifaananyi ebisinga obungi ebimanyiddwa ennyo eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale yokka, naye ne mu ngeri nnyingi eza ssaayansi ze baamulaga.

Kati, mu ngeri yonna, ekifaananyi ekisinga okuba eky’edda ekikyaliyo mu ngeri yonna egamba nti kya whale, kisangibwa mu cavern-pagoda emanyiddwa ennyo eya Elephanta, mu Buyindi. Aba Brahmin bagamba nti mu bibumbe kumpi ebitaggwaawo ebya pagoda eyo etajjukirwa, emirimu gyonna n’okugoberera, buli mulimu gw’omuntu oguyinza okulowoozebwako, byali bitegeezeddwa emyaka mingi nga tebinnabaawo ddala. Olwo tekyewuunyisa nti mu ngeri emu oba endala omulimu gwaffe ogw’ekitiibwa ogw’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale gwandibadde awo nga gusiikiridde. Envubu ya Hindoo eyogerwako, esangibwa mu kitundu eky’enjawulo ekya bbugwe, ng’eraga okufuuka omuntu kwa Vishnu mu ngeri ya leviathan, mu kuyiga emanyiddwa nga Matse Avatar. Naye newankubadde ekibumbe kino kitundu kya muntu ate kitundu kya whale, bwe kityo okuwa omukira gw’oyo asembayo gwokka, naye ekitundu ekyo ekitono eky’ekyo kyonna kikyamu. Kifaanana nnyo omukira ogugonvu ogwa anaconda, okusinga engalo empanvu ez’ebiwuka ebinene eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale entuufu.

Naye genda mu Galleries enkadde, era laba kati ekifaananyi ky’omusiizi w’ebifaananyi omukulu Omukristaayo eky’ekyennyanja kino; kubanga tasobola bulungi okusinga Abahindu abaaliwo ng’amataba tegannabaawo. Kye kifaananyi kya Guido ekya Perseus ng’ataasa Andromeda okuva ku kisolo ekiyitibwa sea-monster oba whale. Guido yaggya wa model y’ekitonde ekyewuunyisa ng’ekyo? Era Hogarth, mu kusiiga ekifaananyi kye kimu mu “Perseus Descending” ye, takola kisingako katono. Obugumu obunene ennyo obw’ekisolo ekyo ekya Hogarthian buwuuma ku ngulu, nga tebusika yinsi emu ey’amazzi. Kirina ekika kya howdah ku mugongo gwayo, era akamwa kaakyo akagaziye nga kalimu amayinja amayengo mwe gayiringisibwa, kayinza okutwalibwa olw’Omulyango gw’Abawemu ogugenda okuva ku mugga Thames nga guyita mu mazzi okuyingira mu Munaala. Ate era, waliwo ennyanja Prodromus whales eza Scotch Sibbald enkadde, ne Jonah’s whale, nga bwe kiragibwa mu biwandiiko bya Baibuli enkadde n’ebitundu ebisaliddwako ebiwandiiko eby’edda ebisookerwako. Kiki ekinaayogerwa ku bano? Ate ku whale y’omusiba ebitabo eyeekulukuunya ng’ekikolo ky’emizabbibu okwetooloola sitokisi y’ennanga ekka —nga bwe yateekebwako sitampu era nga ya zaabu ku migongo n’emitwe gy’ebitabo bingi ebikadde n’ebipya —ekyo kitonde kya kifaananyi nnyo naye nga kya kitalo, okukoppa, nkitwala, okuva mu bifaananyi ebifaananako bwe bityo ku bibya eby’edda. Wadde nga mu nsi yonna eyitibwa dolphin, wadde kiri kityo, ekyennyanja kino eky’omusiba ebitabo nkiyita okugezaako okukuba ennyanja ekika kya whale; kubanga kyali kigendereddwa bwe kityo ng’ekyuma kino kyasooka kuleetebwa. Kyatongozebwa omubuulizi omukadde Omuyitale awalala nga mu kyasa eky’ekkumi n’ettaano, mu kiseera ky’Okuzuukusa Okuyiga; era mu biseera ebyo, era ne mu kiseera ekisembayo, abantu bangi baali balowooza nti ennyonyi eziyitibwa dolphins zaali kika kya Leviathan.

Mu vignettes n’ebintu ebirala eby’okuyooyoota eby’ebitabo ebimu eby’edda oluusi ojja kusisinkana n’okukwata okw’okwewuunya ennyo ku whale, nga buli ngeri ya spouts, jets d’eau, hot springs and cold, Saratoga ne Baden-Baden, zijja nga zifuumuuka okuva ku ze obwongo obutakoowa. Mu lupapula lw’omutwe olw’ekitabo ekyasooka ekya “Advancement of Learning” ojja kusangamu ennyanja ekika kya whale eziwuniikiriza.

Naye nga tulekera awo okugezaako kuno kwonna okutali kwa kikugu, ka tutunule mu bifaananyi ebyo ebya leviathan ebyeyita nti bya ssober, scientific delineations, by abo abamanyi. Mu kukuŋŋaanyizibwa kw’olugendo lwa Harris omukadde mulimu ebipande ebimu eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale ebyaggibwa mu kitabo ky’olugendo eky’Oludaaki, AD 1671, ekyatuumiddwa “A Whaling Voyage to Spitzbergen in the ship Jonas in the Whale, Peter Peterson of Friesland, master.” Mu kimu ku bipande ebyo ennyanja ekika kya whale, ng’ebikondo ebinene, zikiikirira nga zigalamidde wakati mu bizinga bya ice, nga n’eddubu eryeru lidduka ku migongo gyazo emilamu. Mu pulati endala, ensobi ey’ekitalo ekolebwa nga ekiikirira ennyanja ekika kya whale n’ebiwujjo ebiwanvuye (perpendicular flukes).

Ate era, waliwo quarto enkulu ennyo, eyawandiikibwa Captain Colnett omu, Post Captain mu ggye ly’oku mazzi erya Bungereza, eriko omutwe “A Voyage round Cape Horn into the South Seas, for the purpose of extending the Spermaceti Whale Fisheries.” Mu kitabo kino mulimu ensengeka eyeeyita “Ekifaananyi ky’ennyanja ekika kya Physeter oba Spermaceti whale, ekyakubiddwa ku minzaani okuva ku emu eyattibwa ku lubalama lw’ennyanja Mexico, August, 1793, era n’ewanirirwa ku ddeeke.” Sibuusabuusa nti kapiteeni yalina ekifaananyi kino ekituufu ekyakwatibwa olw’okuganyula abaserikale be ab’oku mazzi. Okwogera ekintu kimu kyokka ku kyo, ka njogere nti erina eriiso eryasiigibwa, okusinziira ku minzaani ewerekedde, ku sperm whale ekuze mu bujjuvu, kyandifudde eriiso lya whale eyo eddirisa ly’obutaasa obuwanvu bwa ffuuti nga ttaano. Ah, kapiteeni wange omuzira, lwaki tewatuwa Yona ng’atunudde mu liiso eryo!

Era n’ebitabo ebisinga okuba eby’omunda eby’Ebyafaayo by’Obutonde okuganyula abato n’abagonvu, tebirina nsobi y’emu ey’ekivve. Laba omulimu ogwo ogumanyiddwa ennyo “Goldsmith’s Animated Nature.” Mu lupapula lwa London olufunze olwa 1807, mulimu ebipande ebiraga nti yali “whale” ne “narwhale.” Saagala kulabika nga si wa kitiibwa, naye ennyanja eno etalabika bulungi eringa nnyo enkoko esaliddwako ebitundu by’omubiri; era, nga ku narwhale, okugitunuulira omulundi gumu kumala okuwuniikiriza omuntu, nti mu kyasa kino eky’ekkumi n’omwenda hippogriff ng’eyo yali esobola okukwatibwa engalo olw’amazima ku bantu bonna abagezi ab’abalenzi b’amasomero.

Awo, nate, mu 1825, Bernard Germain, Count de Lacépède, omukugu mu by’obutonde omukulu, yafulumya ekitabo kya ssaayansi ekitegekeddwa obulungi ku nnyanja whale, nga muno mulimu ebifaananyi ebiwerako eby’ebika by’ennyanja Leviathan eby’enjawulo. Bino byonna si bikyamu byokka, naye ekifaananyi kya Mysticetus oba Greenland whale (kwe kugamba, Right whale), ne Scoresby, omusajja aludde ng’amanyi ku kukwata ku kika ekyo, alangirira nti talina munne mu butonde.

Naye okuteeka cap-sheaf ku bizinensi eno yonna eyali efuuse ensobi kwali kuterekeddwa munnasayansi Frederick Cuvier, muganda wa Baron omututumufu. Mu 1836, yafulumya ekitabo ekiyitibwa Natural History of Whales, mwe yawa ekifaananyi ky’ayita ekya Sperm Whale. Nga tonnalaga kifaananyi ekyo eri Omunantucket yenna, kyandibadde kirungi okuteekateeka ekifo kyo eky’okuwummulamu mu bufunze okuva e Nantucket. Mu kigambo kimu, Frederick Cuvier’s Sperm Whale si Sperm Whale, wabula squash. Kya lwatu nti teyafunangako mugaso gwa lugendo lwa kuvuba nnyanja (abasajja ng’abo tebatera kufuna mugaso), naye ekifaananyi ekyo gye yakiggya, ani ayinza okukimanya? Mpozzi yakifuna nga eyamusooka mu bya ssaayansi mu mulimu gwe gumu, Desmarest, bwe yafuna emu ku embuto ze entuufu; kwe kugamba, okuva mu kifaananyi ky’Abachina. Era nga ba lads abalamu abalina ekkalaamu Abachina abo bwe bali, ebikopo bingi ebya queer ne saucers bitutegeeza.

Ate ku nnyanja ekika kya whale z’abasiiga ebipande ezirabibwa mu nguudo nga ziwanikiddwa ku maduuka g’abasuubuzi b’amafuta, kiki ekinaayogerwako? Okutwalira awamu be Richard III. whales, nga zirina dromedary humps, era nga za nsolo nnyo; ekyenkya ku sailor tarts ssatu oba nnya, kwe kugamba amaato g’ennyanja whale agajjudde abavubi: obulema bwago nga buwuguka mu nnyanja ez’omusaayi ne langi ya bbululu.

Naye ensobi zino ez’enjawulo mu kulaga ennyanja ekika kya whale tezeewuunyisa nnyo nnyo oluvannyuma lw’ebyo byonna. Fumiitiriza! Ebifaananyi bya ssaayansi ebisinga biggyiddwa mu byennyanja ebisibiddwa; era bino bituufu ng’ekifaananyi ky’emmeeri eyamenyese, ng’erina omugongo ogumenyese, kyandikiikiridde bulungi ensolo ey’ekitiibwa yennyini mu amalala gaayo gonna agatali gakutuse ag’ekisenge ky’emmeeri n’ebiwujjo. Wadde ng’enjovu ziyimiridde ku buwanvu bwazo obujjuvu, Leviathan omulamu tannaba kwetengejja mu bwenkanya olw’ekifaananyi kye. Ennyanja enamu, mu bukulu bwayo obujjuvu n’amakulu gaayo, egenda kulabibwa ku nnyanja mu mazzi agatategeerekeka; era ku mazzi ekitundu ekinene ennyo kye tekirabika, ng’emmeeri essiddwa mu layini y’olutalo; era okuva mu kintu ekyo kintu ekitasoboka emirembe gyonna omuntu afa okumusitula mu mubiri mu bbanga, asobole okukuuma ebizimba bye byonna eby’amaanyi n’amayengo. Era, nga tetulina kwogera ku njawulo eteeberezebwa ennyo ey’enkula wakati w’ennyanja ento esonseka ne Leviathan ow’e Platonia akuze mu bujjuvu; naye, ne mu mbeera y’emu ku nvubu ezo ento ezisonseka ezisituddwa ku ddeeke y’emmeeri, olwo bwe kityo bwe kiri enkula ye ey’ekyewuunyo, eringa enseenene, ewunya, ey’enjawulo, ne kiba nti enjogera ye entuufu sitaani yennyini teyasobola kukwata.

Naye kiyinza okulowoozebwa, nti okuva mu magumba ag’obwereere ag’ennyanja ekika kya whale ekwatiddwa, ebigambo ebituufu biyinza okufunibwa nga bikwata ku kifaananyi kye ekituufu. Nedda. Kubanga kye kimu ku bintu ebisinga okwewuunyisa ku Leeviyani ono, nti amagumba ge gawa endowooza ntono nnyo ku nkula ye ey’awamu. Wadde ng’amagumba ga Jeremy Bentham, agawaniriddwa olw’ettaala mu tterekero ly’omu ku baamutuukiriza, gatuusa bulungi ekirowoozo ky’omusajja omukadde ow’omugaso ow’amaanyi, n’engeri endala zonna eza Jeremy ezikulembedde ez’obuntu; naye tewali kintu kya ngeri eno ekyali kiyinza kutegeerekeka okuva mu magumba gonna aga leviathan agaali gakwatagana. Mu butuufu, ng’Omuyizzi omukulu bw’agamba, amagumba gokka ag’ennyanja ekika kya whale galina enkolagana y’emu n’ensolo essiddwamu ssente mu bujjuvu era eriko ebipande ng’ekiwuka bwe kikola ku chrysalis ekigizingako mu ngeri eyeetooloovu ennyo. Okwewunyisa kuno kweyolekera mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa mu mutwe, nga mu kitundu ekimu eky’ekitabo kino bwe kijja okulagibwa mu butanwa. Era eraga mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa ennyo mu kiwaawaatiro eky’ebbali, amagumba gaakyo kumpi gaddamu bulungi amagumba g’omukono gw’omuntu, ng’oggyeeko engalo ensajja yokka. Ekiwaawaatiro kino kirina engalo nnya ez’amagumba eza bulijjo, engalo ey’omukono, eya wakati, ey’empeta, n’engalo entono. Naye bino byonna bibeera mu kibikka kyabyo eky’ennyama enkalakkalira, ng’engalo z’omuntu mu kibikka eky’obutonde. Stubb omusezi bwe yagamba olunaku lumu nti: “Kyokka mu ngeri ey’obulagajjavu oluusi ennyanja ekika kya whale eyinza okutuweereza, mu butuufu tekiyinza kugambibwa nti atukwata nga tetulina mitto.”

Olw’ensonga zino zonna, n’olwekyo, engeri yonna gy’oyinza okukitunuuliramu, olina okwetaaga okumaliriza nti Leviyasani omukulu kye kitonde ekyo ekimu mu nsi ekiteekwa okusigala nga tekisiigiddwa langi okutuuka ku nkomerero. Kyo kituufu nti ekifaananyi ekimu kiyinza okukuba akabonero ako okumpi ennyo okusinga ekirala, naye tewali kiyinza kukikuba mu ngeri yonna ey’obutuufu. N’olwekyo tewali ngeri yonna ey’oku nsi ey’okuzuula ddala ennyonyi eno ekika kya whale gy’efaanana. Era mode yokka mw’osobola okuggya wadde endowooza egumiikiriza ku contour ye enamu, kwe kugenda okuvuba whale ggwe kennyini; naye bw’okola bw’otyo, tolina kabi katono ak’okubeera sitoovu emirembe gyonna n’okubbira ye. N’olwekyo, kirabika gyendi wandibadde osinga obutaba muyitirivu nnyo mu kwegomba kwo ng’okwata ku Leeviyasaani ono.





ESSUULA 56. Eby’Ebifaananyi Ebitali Bikyamu Ebitono Eby’Envubu, n’Ebifaananyi Ebituufu eby’Ebifo eby’Okuvuba Envubu.

Mu kukwatagana n’ebifaananyi eby’ekitalo eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale, nkemebwa nnyo wano okuyingira ku mboozi ezo ezikyali ez’entiisa ennyo ku zo ezisangibwa mu bitabo ebimu, eby’edda n’eby’omulembe, naddala mu Pliny, Purchas, Hackluyt, Harris, Cuvier, . etc. Naye ensonga eyo ngiyitako.

Nze mmanyi ensengeka nnya zokka ezifulumiziddwa ez’ennyonyi ennene ey’ekika kya Sperm Whale; Ebya Colnett, Huggins, ebya Frederick Cuvier ne Beale. Mu ssuula emabega Colnett ne Cuvier babadde boogerwako. Eya Huggins esinga wala eyabwe; naye, mu ngeri ennene, eya Beale y’esinga. Ebifaananyi byonna Beale bye yakubye ku nnyanja eno birungi, okuggyako ekifaananyi ekiri wakati mu kifaananyi ky’ennyanja ssatu eziri mu ndowooza ez’enjawulo, ng’akwata essuula ye eyookubiri. Ekifaananyi kye eky’omu maaso, amaato agalumba Sperm Whales, wadde nga awatali kubuusabuusa gabalirirwa okucamula okubuusabuusa kw’abantu abamu okw’abasajja abamu mu parlor, kituufu mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa era kiringa obulamu mu ngeri gye kikwatamu abantu bonna. Ebimu ku bifaananyi bya Sperm Whale mu J. Ross Browne bituufu nnyo mu contour; naye ziyooleddwa mu ngeri ey’ennaku. Ogwo si musango gwe wadde.

Ku Right Whale, ebifaananyi ebisinga obulungi mu nsengeka biri mu Scoresby; naye zikubiddwa ku minzaani entono ennyo okusobola okulaga endowooza eyeegombebwa. Alina ekifaananyi kimu kyokka eky’ebifo eby’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale, era kino kibulamu eky’ennaku, kubanga mu bifaananyi ng’ebyo byokka, bwe biba bikoleddwa bulungi, mw’osobola okuggya ekintu kyonna ng’endowooza ey’amazima ku nnyanja ekika kya whale enamu nga bwe kirabibwa abayizzi be abalamu.

Naye, bwe kitwalibwa byonna awamu, okutuuka wala ebisinga obulungi, wadde nga mu bintu ebimu si bituufu, ennyanjula z’ennyanja ekika kya whale n’ebifaananyi by’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale ebiyinza okusangibwa wonna, bye bifaananyi bibiri ebinene eby’Abafaransa, ebikoleddwa obulungi, era nga biggyiddwa mu bifaananyi ebyakolebwa Garnery omu. Mu kulondako, zikiikirira okulumba Ensigo ne Right Whale. Mu kifaananyi ekisooka ekika kya Sperm Whale eky’ekitiibwa kiragibwa mu kitiibwa ekijjuvu eky’amaanyi, nga yaakasituka wansi w’eryato okuva mu buziba bw’ennyanja, era ng’asitula waggulu mu bbanga ku mugongo gwe ekifunfugu eky’entiisa eky’embaawo ezisiigibwa. Ekyenyi ky’eryato tekikutuse ekitundu, era kikubiddwa nga kitebenkedde ku mugongo gw’ekisolo ekinene; era ng’oyimiridde mu bbanga eryo, olw’okumasamasa okwo okumu okutali kubalirira okw’ekiseera, olaba omuvuzi w’amaato, ng’ekitundu kibikkiddwa ekifuba ekibuguma eky’ennyanja ekika kya whale, era ng’ali mu kikolwa eky’okubuuka, ng’alinga ava mu kiwonvu. Ekikolwa ky’ekintu kyonna kirungi mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa era kituufu. Layini-bbaafu efuuse njereere ekitundu etengejja ku nnyanja eyeeru; ebikondo by’embaawo eby’ebikondo ebiyiiriddwa biba mu kyo mu ngeri ey’ekyekulungirivu; emitwe gy’abakozi abawuga gisaasaanidde ku nnyanja ekika kya whale mu ngeri ez’enjawulo ez’okutya; ate mu bbanga ery’omuyaga omuddugavu emmeeri egenda wansi ku kifo kino. Ensobi ey’amaanyi eyinza okusangibwa mu bintu ebikwata ku nsengeka y’ennyanja eno, naye ekyo kiyite; okuva, olw’obulamu bwange, bwe nnali sisobola kukuba mulungi bwe butyo.

Mu kuyoola okw’okubiri, eryato liri mu kikolwa ky’okukuba ku mabbali g’oludda oluliko barnacled olw’ennyonyi ennene edduka Right Whale, eyiringisiza ekitundu kye ekinene eky’omuddo omuddugavu mu nnyanja ng’amayinja agamu agasereba ag’ebiwuka okuva ku njazi z’e Patagonia. Jeti ze zigoloddwa, zijjudde era ziddugavu ng’enfuufu; bwe kityo okuva mu mukka ogwali guyitiridde mu ssigiri, wandirowoozezza nti wateekwa okubaawo ekyeggulo eky’obuzira ekifumba mu byenda ebinene wansi. Ennyonyi z’omu nnyanja zinyiga enseenene entonotono, ebisusunku, ne ssweeta endala ez’omu nnyanja ne maccaroni, oluusi Right Whale z’etwala ku mugongo gwe ogulimu kawumpuli. Era ebbanga eryo lyonna ennyonyi ekika kya leviathan ey’emimwa eminene efubutuka mu buziba, ng’asigaza ttani z’ebikuta ebyeru ebitabuse mu kuzuukuka kwe, era ng’ereetera eryato ettono okukankana mu bizimba ng’ekiwujjo ekikwatiddwa okumpi ne nnamuziga z’amaato ag’omu nnyanja. Bwe kityo, eby’omu maaso byonna biba bya kavuyo akakulu; naye emabega, mu njawulo ey’ekikugu eyesiimibwa, waliwo eddaala ery’endabirwamu ery’ennyanja erikkakkana, amaato g’emmeeri etaliimu sitaagi agagwa wansi, n’ekibinja ekitaliiko kye gakola eky’ennyanja ekika kya whale enfu, ekigo ekyawangulwa, nga bendera y’okukwatibwa ewaniridde mu bugayaavu ku nnyanja ekika kya whale- ekikondo ekyayingizibwa mu kinnya kye eky’okufuuwa.

Ani Garnery omusiizi w’ebifaananyi y’ani, oba yali, simanyi. Naye obulamu bwange olw’ekyo yali oba practically conversant n’essomo lye, oba else nga yasomesebwa mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa omusajja omu ow’obumanyirivu mu whaleman. Abafaransa be ba lads for painting action. Genda otunule mu bifaananyi byonna ebya Bulaaya, era ojja kusanga wa galagi ng’eyo ey’akajagalalo akalamu n’okussa ku kanvaasi, nga mu kisenge ekyo eky’obuwanguzi e Versailles; omulabi gy’alwana ekkubo lye, pell-mell, okuyita mu ntalo ennene eziddiring’ana eza Bufalansa; buli kitala we kirabika ng’ekitangaala ky’Ebitaala by’Obukiikakkono, era bakabaka ne Empula abaddiring’ana abalina emmundu ne badduka ne bayitawo, ng’omusango gw’aba centaurs abatikkiddwa engule? Si nti tezisaanira ddala kifo mu galagi eyo, bino bye bitundu by’olutalo by’ennyanja ebya Garnery.

Obusobozi obw’obutonde bw’Abafaransa obw’okuwamba ebifaananyi by’ebintu bulabika nga bweyolekera mu ngeri ey’enjawulo mu bifaananyi n’ebiwandiiko bye balina ku bifo byabwe eby’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale. Olw’okuba tewali kimu kya kkumi eky’obumanyirivu bwa Bungereza mu kuvuba, era nga si kitundu kya lukumi eky’eky’Abamerika, wadde kiri kityo, bawadde amawanga gombi sketch zokka eziwedde n’akatono ezisobola okutuusa omwoyo omutuufu ogw’okuyigga ennyanja ekika kya whale. Durand.” Ekimu ku byo, wadde nga tekituukagana bulungi na kigendererwa kyaffe ekiriwo kati, wadde kiri kityo, kisaana okwogerwako ku biwandiiko ebirala. Kati, ekimu ku bifaananyi eby’enjawulo eby’omusajja omukambwe mu ssaawa ze ez’awaka, kwe kugumiikiriza kwe okw’ekitalo mu makolero. Ekibiina ky’olutalo oba eky’amafumu eky’edda eky’e Hawaii, mu bungi bwakyo obujjuvu n’obulungi bwakyo obw’okubumba, kikopo kinene eky’okugumiikiriza kw’abantu ng’enkuluze y’Olulattini. Kubanga, nga waliwo akaseko k’ennyanja akamenyese oba erinnyo lya shark, obuzibu obwo obw’ekyamagero obw’omutimba gw’embaawo butuukiddwaako; era ebadde emaze emyaka egitakyukakyuka egy’okugikozesa nga tekyukakyuka.

Envubu ez’embaawo, oba ennyanja ekika kya whale ezisaliddwa mu ngeri ya profile okuva mu bupande obutono obuddugavu obw’enku ez’olutalo ez’ekika kya South Sea ez’ekitiibwa, zitera okusangibwa mu bifo eby’okuteebereza eby’abavubi b’ennyanja mu Amerika. Ebimu ku byo bikolebwa mu butuufu bungi.

ESSUULA 58. Omuzungu.

Nga tuvuga mu bukiika kkono-buvanjuba okuva ku Crozetts, twagwa mu nnimiro ennene ennyo eza brit, ekintu ekitono ennyo ekya kyenvu, Right Whale kw’esinga okuliisa. Okumala liigi ne liigi zaatuyitamu amayengo, ne tulabika nga tusaabala mu nnimiro ezitaliiko kkomo ez’eŋŋaano enkungu era eya zaabu.

Ku lunaku olwokubiri, omuwendo gwa Right Whales gwalabibwa, nga, nga gukuumibwa okuva ku kulumbibwa kwa Sperm Whaler nga Pequod, nga zirina ensaya eziggule, zaawuga mu ngeri ey’obugayaavu okuyita mu brit, nga, nga zinyweredde ku biwuzi ebiwanvu eby’omuzibe w’amaaso oyo ow’e Venetian ow’ekitalo mu kamwa kazo , yali mu ngeri eyo eyawuddwa ku mazzi agaafulumanga ku mimwa.

Ng’abasala eby’oku makya, abagenda ku mabbali mpola era nga babuguma, bagenda mu maaso n’ebisero byabwe nga bayita mu muddo omuwanvu omubisi ogw’ebiwuka ebiyitibwa marshy meads; wadde bwe kityo ebisolo bino ebikambwe byawuga, nga bikola eddoboozi ery’ekyewuunyo, ery’omuddo, erisala; ne balekawo ebitundu bya bbululu ebitaggwa ku nnyanja eya kyenvu.*

*Ekitundu ekyo eky’ennyanja ekimanyiddwa mu bavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale nga “Brazil Banks” tekiyitibwa linnya eryo nga Banks of Newfoundland bwe zikola, olw’okuba waliwo ebifo ebitali biwanvu n’ebiwulikika eyo, wabula olw’endabika eno eyeewuunyisa eringa omuddo, evudde ku bunene drifts of brit ezitengejja buli kiseera mu latitudes ezo, nga Right Whale etera okugobererwa.

Naye eddoboozi lyokka lye baakola nga bwe baawulamu brit eryajjukiza n’akatono omuntu abasala. Bwe zitunuulirwa okuva ku mitwe gy’amayinja naddala nga ziyimiriddeko katono era nga ziyimiridde okumala akaseera, ebifaananyi byabwe ebinene ebiddugavu byali bifaanana nnyo amayinja agataliiko bulamu okusinga ekintu ekirala kyonna. Era nga bwe kiri mu nsi ennene ez’okuyigga eza Buyindi, omugenyi ali ewala oluusi ajja kuyita ku biwonvu enjovu ezigalamidde nga tazimanyi nti bwe zityo, ng’azitwala ng’obugulumivu obw’ettaka obw’obwereere, obuddugavu; wadde bwe kityo, emirundi mingi, naye, omulundi gwe ogusooka okulaba ekika kino eky’ebiwuka ebiyitibwa leviathans eby’omu nnyanja. Era ne bwe zitegeerwa ku nkomerero, obunene bwazo obw’ekitalo bufuula ekizibu ennyo ddala okukkiriza nti ebibinja ebinene bwe bityo eby’okukula okusukkiridde biyinza okuba nga bya butonde, mu bitundu byonna, nga birina ekika ky’obulamu kye kimu ekibeera mu mbwa oba embalaasi.

Mazima ddala, mu bintu ebirala, toyinza kutunuulira bitonde byonna eby’omu buziba n’enneewulira y’emu ng’eyo ey’oku lubalama lw’ennyanja. Kubanga newankubadde nga abamu ku bakugu mu by’obutonde ab’edda bagamba nti ebitonde byonna eby’omu nsi bya kika kyabyo mu nnyanja; era newankubadde nga tukwata endowooza engazi ey’awamu ku kintu, kino kiyinza bulungi nnyo okuba; naye nga tujja ku by’enjawulo, ng’ekyokulabirako, ennyanja w’ewa ebyennyanja byonna ebiddamu ekisa eky’amagezi eky’embwa? Shark akolimiddwa yokka mu ngeri yonna ey’enjawulo esobola okugambibwa nti alina okugeraageranya okugeraageranyizibwa ku ye.

Naye wadde, eri bannansi okutwalira awamu, abantu enzaalwa z’ennyanja babadde batunuulirwa mu nneewulira ezitayinza kwogerwako ezitali za mu bantu era ezigoba; newankubadde nga tumanyi ennyanja nti terra incognita etaggwaawo, ne kiba nti Columbus yasaabala ku nsi ezitabalika ezitamanyiddwa okuzuula ye emu ey’amaserengeta ey’okungulu; wadde, mu ngeri enzibu ennyo, obutyabaga obusinga okuba obw’entiisa mu buzibu bwonna obw’okufa butuuse mu ngeri etajjukirwa era awatali kusosola mu makumi n’ebikumi n’ebikumi by’abo abagenze ku mazzi; newankubadde naye okulowooza akaseera katono kujja kuyigiriza, nti wadde omwana omuwere ayinza okwewaana ne ssaayansi we n’obukugu bwe, era ne bwe biba bingi, mu biseera eby’omu maaso ebinyuvu, ssaayansi n’obukugu bwe biyinza okweyongera; naye emirembe gyonna n’emirembe gyonna, okutuuka ku nnyatika y’okuzikirira, ennyanja ejja kumuvuma n’okumutta, era efuumuule emmeeri esinga okubeera ey’ekitiibwa, esinga okukaluba gy’asobola okukola; wadde kiri kityo, olw’okuddiŋŋana okutambula obutasalako kw’ebifaananyi bino byennyini, omuntu afiiriddwa okutegeera okwo okw’entiisa enzijuvu ey’ennyanja mu buzaale bw’eri.

Eryato eryasooka lye twasomako, lyalengejja ku nnyanja, nti n’okwesasuza kw’Abapotugali lyali lizinze ensi yonna nga terulese nnyo wadde nnamwandu. Ennyanja eyo y’emu yeekulukuunya kati; ennyanja eyo yennyini yasaanyaawo emmeeri ezaali zimenyese omwaka oguwedde. Weewaawo, abantu abafa abasirusiru, amataba ga Nuuwa tegannakka; ebitundu bibiri ku bisatu eby’ensi ey’obwenkanya gye kikyabikka.

Ennyanja n’ensi bye byawukana, nti ekyamagero ku omu si kyamagero ku munne? Entiisa ezitali za butonde zaali ku Abebbulaniya, wansi w’ebigere bya Koola n’ekibinja kye ettaka ennamu bwe lyagguka ne libamira emirembe gyonna; naye ate si njuba ya mulembe egwa, naye mu ngeri y’emu ddala ennyanja ennamu gy’emira emmeeri n’abakozi.

Naye ennyanja si mulabe nnyo eri omuntu omugwira gy’ali, naye era muzimu eri ezzadde lyayo; obubi okusinga omugenyi Omuperusi eyatta abagenyi be bennyini; nga tosonyiwa bitonde byennyini ebizaala. Ng’ensowera enkambwe ng’okusuula mu nsiko kubikka abaana baayo, bwe kityo ennyanja n’ekuba n’ennyanja ekika kya whale ezisinga amaanyi ku njazi, n’ezireka awo nga ziri ku mabbali n’ebifunfugu by’emmeeri ebyawuddwamu. Tewali kusaasira, tewali maanyi wabula gaago ge gakifuga. Nga efuuwa n’okufuuwa ng’embalaasi ey’olutalo eddalu efiiriddwa omuvuzi waayo, ennyanja etaliimu kafulu ebuutikidde ensi yonna.

Lowooza ku bukodyo bw’ennyanja; engeri ebitonde byayo ebisinga okutiisibwa gye biseeyeeya wansi w’amazzi, nga tebirabika okusinga obungi, era nga bikwekeddwa mu ngeri ey’enkwe wansi w’amabala agasinga okulabika obulungi aga langi ya azure. Lowooza n’okumasamasa n’obulungi bwa sitaani obw’ebika byayo bingi ebisinga obuteejjusa, ng’enkula ennungi ennyo ey’ebika bya shark bingi. Lowooza, omulundi omulala, okulya abantu bonna mu nnyanja; byonna ebitonde byabwe bye biyiggagana, nga bigenda mu maaso n’olutalo olutaggwaawo okuva ensi lwe yatandika.

Lowooza ku bino byonna; n’oluvannyuma n’okyukira ku nsi eno eya kiragala, ennyogovu, era esinga okugondera; birowoozeeko byombi, ennyanja n'ensi; era tosanga kugeraageranya kwa kyewuunyo ku kintu mu ggwe kennyini? Kubanga ng’ennyanja eno ey’entiisa bwe yeetoolodde ensi enkalu, bwe kityo mu mwoyo gw’omuntu mulimu Tahiti emu ey’ekizinga, ejjudde emirembe n’essanyu, naye nga yeetooloddwa ebitiisa byonna eby’obulamu obumanyiddwa ekitundu. Katonda akuume! Tosika kuva ku kizinga ekyo, tosobola kuddayo!





ESSUULA 59. Squid.

Ng’ekulukuta mpola mu ddundiro ly’e brit, Pequod yali ekyakwata mu kkubo lyayo ery’obukiikakkono-buvanjuba ng’eyolekera ekizinga Java; empewo ennyogovu ng’esika ekifuba kye, ne kiba nti mu mirembe egyali gyetoolodde emisinde gye esatu emiwanvu egyali gifuukuuse ne giwuuba mu ngeri ey’obukkakkamu eri empewo eyo eyali ewunya, ng’engalo ssatu ennyogovu ku lusenyi. Era wadde kiri kityo, mu biseera ebigazi mu kiro ekya ffeeza, ennyonyi ekika kya jet eyali yokka era esikiriza yandirabibwa.

Naye enkya emu eya bbululu entangaavu, ng’obusirise kumpi obutali bwa butonde busaasaana ku nnyanja, wadde nga tebulabirirwa n’obukkakkamu bwonna obuyimiridde; omusana omuwanvu ogwayakaayakana ku mazzi bwe gwalabika ng’engalo eya zaabu eyateekebwa ku mazzi, ng’eragira okubeera n’ekyama ekimu; amayengo agaali gaseerera bwe gawuuba wamu nga bwe gadduka mpola; mu kusirika kuno okw’amaanyi okw’enkulungo erabika omuzimu ogw’ekyewuunyo gwalabibwa Daggoo okuva ku mutwe gw’omuggo omukulu.

Ewala, ekibinja ekinene ekyeru kyasituka mu bugayaavu, era nga kyeyongera waggulu, era nga kyekutula ku azure, ku nkomerero kyayakaayakana mu maaso gaffe ng’omuzira ogusereba, omupya ne guseerera okuva ku busozi. Thus glistening for a moment, as slowly it subsided, and sank. Then once more arose, and silently gleamed. It seemed not a whale; and yet is this Moby Dick? thought Daggoo. Again the phantom went down, but on re-appearing once more, with a stiletto-like cry that startled every man from his nod, the negro yelled out—“There! there again! there she breaches! right ahead! The White Whale, the White Whale!”

Upon this, the seamen rushed to the yard-arms, as in swarming-time the bees rush to the boughs. Bare-headed in the sultry sun, Ahab stood on the bowsprit, and with one hand pushed far behind in readiness to wave his orders to the helmsman, cast his eager glance in the direction indicated aloft by the outstretched motionless arm of Daggoo.

Whether the flitting attendance of the one still and solitary jet had gradually worked upon Ahab, so that he was now prepared to connect the ideas of mildness and repose with the first sight of the particular whale he pursued; however this was, or whether his eagerness betrayed him; whichever way it might have been, no sooner did he distinctly perceive the white mass, than with a quick intensity he instantly gave orders for lowering.

The four boats were soon on the water; Ahab's in advance, and all swiftly pulling towards their prey. Soon it went down, and while, with oars suspended, we were awaiting its reappearance, lo! in the same spot where it sank, once more it slowly rose. Almost forgetting for the moment all thoughts of Moby Dick, we now gazed at the most wondrous phenomenon which the secret seas have hitherto revealed to mankind. A vast pulpy mass, furlongs in length and breadth, of a glancing cream-colour, lay floating on the water, innumerable long arms radiating from its centre, and curling and twisting like a nest of anacondas, as if blindly to clutch at any hapless object within reach. No perceptible face or front did it have; no conceivable token of either sensation or instinct; but undulated there on the billows, an unearthly, formless, chance-like apparition of life.

As with a low sucking sound it slowly disappeared again, Starbuck still gazing at the agitated waters where it had sunk, with a wild voice exclaimed—“Almost rather had I seen Moby Dick and fought him, than to have seen thee, thou white ghost!”

“What was it, Sir?” said Flask.

“The great live squid, which, they say, few whale-ships ever beheld, and returned to their ports to tell of it.”

But Ahab said nothing; turning his boat, he sailed back to the vessel; the rest as silently following.

Whatever superstitions the sperm whalemen in general have connected with the sight of this object, certain it is, that a glimpse of it being so very unusual, that circumstance has gone far to invest it with portentousness. So rarely is it beheld, that though one and all of them declare it to be the largest animated thing in the ocean, yet very few of them have any but the most vague ideas concerning its true nature and form; notwithstanding, they believe it to furnish to the sperm whale his only food. For though other species of whales find their food above water, and may be seen by man in the act of feeding, the spermaceti whale obtains his whole food in unknown zones below the surface; and only by inference is it that any one can tell of what, precisely, that food consists. At times, when closely pursued, he will disgorge what are supposed to be the detached arms of the squid; some of them thus exhibited exceeding twenty and thirty feet in length. They fancy that the monster to which these arms belonged ordinarily clings by them to the bed of the ocean; and that the sperm whale, unlike other species, is supplied with teeth in order to attack and tear it.

There seems some ground to imagine that the great Kraken of Bishop Pontoppodan may ultimately resolve itself into Squid. The manner in which the Bishop describes it, as alternately rising and sinking, with some other particulars he narrates, in all this the two correspond. But much abatement is necessary with respect to the incredible bulk he assigns it.

By some naturalists who have vaguely heard rumors of the mysterious creature, here spoken of, it is included among the class of cuttle-fish, to which, indeed, in certain external respects it would seem to belong, but only as the Anak of the tribe.





CHAPTER 60. The Line.

With reference to the whaling scene shortly to be described, as well as for the better understanding of all similar scenes elsewhere presented, I have here to speak of the magical, sometimes horrible whale-line.

The line originally used in the fishery was of the best hemp, slightly vapored with tar, not impregnated with it, as in the case of ordinary ropes; for while tar, as ordinarily used, makes the hemp more pliable to the rope-maker, and also renders the rope itself more convenient to the sailor for common ship use; yet, not only would the ordinary quantity too much stiffen the whale-line for the close coiling to which it must be subjected; but as most seamen are beginning to learn, tar in general by no means adds to the rope's durability or strength, however much it may give it compactness and gloss.

Of late years the Manilla rope has in the American fishery almost entirely superseded hemp as a material for whale-lines; for, though not so durable as hemp, it is stronger, and far more soft and elastic; and I will add (since there is an æsthetics in all things), is much more handsome and becoming to the boat, than hemp. Hemp is a dusky, dark fellow, a sort of Indian; but Manilla is as a golden-haired Circassian to behold.

The whale-line is only two-thirds of an inch in thickness. At first sight, you would not think it so strong as it really is. By experiment its one and fifty yarns will each suspend a weight of one hundred and twenty pounds; so that the whole rope will bear a strain nearly equal to three tons. In length, the common sperm whale-line measures something over two hundred fathoms. Towards the stern of the boat it is spirally coiled away in the tub, not like the worm-pipe of a still though, but so as to form one round, cheese-shaped mass of densely bedded “sheaves,” or layers of concentric spiralizations, without any hollow but the “heart,” or minute vertical tube formed at the axis of the cheese. As the least tangle or kink in the coiling would, in running out, infallibly take somebody's arm, leg, or entire body off, the utmost precaution is used in stowing the line in its tub. Some harpooneers will consume almost an entire morning in this business, carrying the line high aloft and then reeving it downwards through a block towards the tub, so as in the act of coiling to free it from all possible wrinkles and twists.

In the English boats two tubs are used instead of one; the same line being continuously coiled in both tubs. There is some advantage in this; because these twin-tubs being so small they fit more readily into the boat, and do not strain it so much; whereas, the American tub, nearly three feet in diameter and of proportionate depth, makes a rather bulky freight for a craft whose planks are but one half-inch in thickness; for the bottom of the whale-boat is like critical ice, which will bear up a considerable distributed weight, but not very much of a concentrated one. When the painted canvas cover is clapped on the American line-tub, the boat looks as if it were pulling off with a prodigious great wedding-cake to present to the whales.

Both ends of the line are exposed; the lower end terminating in an eye-splice or loop coming up from the bottom against the side of the tub, and hanging over its edge completely disengaged from everything. This arrangement of the lower end is necessary on two accounts. First: In order to facilitate the fastening to it of an additional line from a neighboring boat, in case the stricken whale should sound so deep as to threaten to carry off the entire line originally attached to the harpoon. In these instances, the whale of course is shifted like a mug of ale, as it were, from the one boat to the other; though the first boat always hovers at hand to assist its consort. Second: This arrangement is indispensable for common safety's sake; for were the lower end of the line in any way attached to the boat, and were the whale then to run the line out to the end almost in a single, smoking minute as he sometimes does, he would not stop there, for the doomed boat would infallibly be dragged down after him into the profundity of the sea; and in that case no town-crier would ever find her again.

Before lowering the boat for the chase, the upper end of the line is taken aft from the tub, and passing round the loggerhead there, is again carried forward the entire length of the boat, resting crosswise upon the loom or handle of every man's oar, so that it jogs against his wrist in rowing; and also passing between the men, as they alternately sit at the opposite gunwales, to the leaded chocks or grooves in the extreme pointed prow of the boat, where a wooden pin or skewer the size of a common quill, prevents it from slipping out. From the chocks it hangs in a slight festoon over the bows, and is then passed inside the boat again; and some ten or twenty fathoms (called box-line) being coiled upon the box in the bows, it continues its way to the gunwale still a little further aft, and is then attached to the short-warp—the rope which is immediately connected with the harpoon; but previous to that connexion, the short-warp goes through sundry mystifications too tedious to detail.

Thus the whale-line folds the whole boat in its complicated coils, twisting and writhing around it in almost every direction. All the oarsmen are involved in its perilous contortions; so that to the timid eye of the landsman, they seem as Indian jugglers, with the deadliest snakes sportively festooning their limbs. Nor can any son of mortal woman, for the first time, seat himself amid those hempen intricacies, and while straining his utmost at the oar, bethink him that at any unknown instant the harpoon may be darted, and all these horrible contortions be put in play like ringed lightnings; he cannot be thus circumstanced without a shudder that makes the very marrow in his bones to quiver in him like a shaken jelly. Yet habit—strange thing! what cannot habit accomplish?—Gayer sallies, more merry mirth, better jokes, and brighter repartees, you never heard over your mahogany, than you will hear over the half-inch white cedar of the whale-boat, when thus hung in hangman's nooses; and, like the six burghers of Calais before King Edward, the six men composing the crew pull into the jaws of death, with a halter around every neck, as you may say.

Perhaps a very little thought will now enable you to account for those repeated whaling disasters—some few of which are casually chronicled—of this man or that man being taken out of the boat by the line, and lost. For, when the line is darting out, to be seated then in the boat, is like being seated in the midst of the manifold whizzings of a steam-engine in full play, when every flying beam, and shaft, and wheel, is grazing you. It is worse; for you cannot sit motionless in the heart of these perils, because the boat is rocking like a cradle, and you are pitched one way and the other, without the slightest warning; and only by a certain self-adjusting buoyancy and simultaneousness of volition and action, can you escape being made a Mazeppa of, and run away with where the all-seeing sun himself could never pierce you out.

Again: as the profound calm which only apparently precedes and prophesies of the storm, is perhaps more awful than the storm itself; for, indeed, the calm is but the wrapper and envelope of the storm; and contains it in itself, as the seemingly harmless rifle holds the fatal powder, and the ball, and the explosion; so the graceful repose of the line, as it silently serpentines about the oarsmen before being brought into actual play—this is a thing which carries more of true terror than any other aspect of this dangerous affair. But why say more? All men live enveloped in whale-lines. All are born with halters round their necks; but it is only when caught in the swift, sudden turn of death, that mortals realize the silent, subtle, ever-present perils of life. And if you be a philosopher, though seated in the whale-boat, you would not at heart feel one whit more of terror, than though seated before your evening fire with a poker, and not a harpoon, by your side.
