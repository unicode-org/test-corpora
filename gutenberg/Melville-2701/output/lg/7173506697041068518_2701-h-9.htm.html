ESSUULA 43. Hark!

“EBYAFAAYO! Owulidde eddoboozi eryo, Cabaco?”

Yali ssaawa ya wakati: omusana gw’omwezi omulungi; abavubi baali bayimiridde mu kkoodi, nga bava ku kimu ku bikuta by’amazzi amayonjo mu kiwato, okutuuka ku scuttle-butt okumpi ne taffrail. Mu ngeri eno, baayisaamu ebibbo okujjuza scuttle-butt. Nga bayimiridde, okusinga obungi, ku bifo ebitukuvu ebya ddeeke ya kwata, baali beegendereza obutayogera oba okukuba ebigere byabwe. Okuva ku mukono okudda ku mulala, ebibbo byagenda mu kasirise akasinga obuziba, nga bimenyeka kwokka olw’okuwuuma kw’amayengo oluusi n’oluusi, n’okuwuuma okutambula obutasalako okw’ekiwujjo ekyali kigenda mu maaso obutasalako.

Wakati mu kuwummula kuno, Archy, omu ku ba cordon, ng’ekikondo kye kyali okumpi n’ebifo ebiyitibwa after-hatches, yayomba muliraanwa we, Omucholo, ebigambo waggulu.

“Ebyafaayo! owulidde eddoboozi eryo, Cabaco?”

“Twala ekibbo, ojja kukikola, Archy? noise d’ye etegeeza ki?”

“Eyo nate —wansi w’ebikuta —tokiwulira —okusesema —kwawulikika ng’okusesema.”

“Cough be damned! Yitamu mu baketi eyo ey’okudda.”

“Eyo nate —waliwo!—kiwulikika ng’abasula babiri oba basatu abakyuka, kati!”

“Caramba! okoze, munno ku mmeeri, munaakikola? Ye bisikiiti essatu ezinywezeddwa ze mulya ku kijjulo nga zikyuka munda mu mmwe —tewali kirala. Tunuulira ekibbo!”

“Yogera ky’oyagala, munno ku mmeeri; Nnina amatu amasongovu.”

“Aye, ggwe chap, si mmwe, eyawulira okuwuuma kw’empiso z’okuluka ez’omu Quakeress omukadde mayiro ataano ku nnyanja okuva e Nantucket; ggwe chap.”

“Nnyenya mugende; tujja kulaba ekigenda okukyuka. Hark ye, Cabaco, waliwo omuntu wansi mu after-hold nga tannalabibwa ku deck; era nteebereza nti ne Mogul waffe omukadde alina ky’amanyi. Nawulira Stubb ng’agamba Flask, essaawa emu ey’oku makya, nti waliwo ekintu eky’engeri eyo mu mpewo.”

“Tish! ekibbo!”





ESSUULA 44. Ekipande.

Singa wali ogoberedde Kapiteeni Akabu wansi mu kabina ye oluvannyuma lw’omuyaga ogwaliwo mu kiro ekyaddirira okukakasa okwo okw’ensiko okw’ekigendererwa kye n’abakozi be, wandimulabye ng’agenda mu loogi mu kisenge ekiyitibwa transom, n’afulumya omuzingo omunene ogw’ennyiriri ogwa chati z’ennyanja eza kyenvu, zibunye mu maaso ge ku mmeeza ye eyali efuukuuse. Olwo nga yeetudde mu maaso gaayo, wandimulabye ng’asoma nnyo layini n’ebisiikirize eby’enjawulo ebyasisinkana awo eriiso lye; era nga olina ekkalaamu empola naye nga tekyukakyuka, londoola emisomo emirala ku bifo ebyali edda nga tebiriiko kintu kyonna. Mu biseera ebitali bimu, yayogeranga ku ntuumu z’ebitabo eby’edda ebiri ku mabbali ge, nga muno mwe mwateekebwa sizoni n’ebifo, mu lugendo olw’enjawulo olw’edda olw’emmeeri ez’enjawulo, ennyanja ekika kya sperm whales mwe zaali zikwatiddwa oba ezaalabibwa.

Nga akozesebwa bw’atyo, ettaala ya pewter enzito eyali ewaniriddwa mu njegere ku mutwe gwe, ng’ekankana buli kiseera olw’entambula y’emmeeri, era emirembe gyonna yasuula ebimasamasa ebikyukakyuka n’ebisiikirize by’ennyiriri ku kyenyi kye ekyali kikutte, okutuusa kumpi lwe kyalabika ng’ekyo nga ye kennyini akuba akabonero layini n’amasomo ku chati eziriko ennyindo, ekkalaamu ezimu ezitalabika nazo zaali zilondoola layini n’emisomo ku chati eyali ewandiikiddwa ennyo ey’ekyenyi kye.

Naye ekiro kino naddala, mu kasenge ke, Akabu bwe yafumiitiriza bwe yafumiitiriza ku chati ze. Kumpi buli kiro baafulumizibwanga; kumpi buli kiro obubonero obumu obwa sipensulo bwasazibwamu, ate obulala ne bukyusibwa. Kubanga ng’alina ebipande by’ennyanja zonna ennya ezaali mu maaso ge, Akabu yali akuba emisinde egy’amaanyi n’enkulungo, n’ekigendererwa eky’okutuukiriza endowooza eyo ey’omuntu omu ku mmeeme ye.

Kati, eri omuntu yenna atamanyidde ddala makubo ga ba leviathans, kiyinza okulabika ng’omulimu ogutaliimu ssuubi mu ngeri etaliimu nsa bwe kityo okunoonya ekitonde kimu ekyetongodde mu nnyanja ezitaliiko bbuutu za pulaneti eno. Naye bwe kityo bwe kyalabika eri Akabu, eyali amanyi ensengeka z’amayengo gonna n’amazzi gonna; era bwe batyo okubala okuwuguka kw’emmere ya sperm whale; era, era, okujjukiza sizoni eza bulijjo, ezikakasibwa ez’okumuyigga mu latitudes ezenjawulo; yali asobola okutuuka ku kuteebereza okutuufu, kumpi ng’asemberera ebikakafu, ebikwata ku lunaku olusinga okubeera mu budde okubeera ku ttaka lino oba eryo ng’anoonya omuyiggo gwe.

Mazima ddala, ekakasiddwa nnyo ensonga ekwata ku buli luvannyuma lwa kiseera ng’ennyanja ekika kya sperm whale eddukira mu mazzi agaweereddwa, ne kiba nti abayizzi bangi bakkiriza nti, yandisobodde okwetegereza ennyo n’okusomesebwa mu nsi yonna; singa ebikondo by’olugendo olumu olw’ekibinja kyonna eky’ennyanja ekika kya whale byakuŋŋaanyizibwa n’obwegendereza, olwo okusenguka kw’ennyanja ekika kya sperm whale kwandisangiddwa nga kukwatagana mu ngeri etakyukakyuka n’okwo okw’ebinyonyi ebiyitibwa herring-shoals oba okubuuka kw’ennyonyi ezimira. Ku nsonga eno, wabaddewo okugezaako okuzimba ebipande ebirabika obulungi eby’okusenguka kw’ennyanja ekika kya sperm whale.*

Ng’oggyeeko ekyo, bwe zikola ekkubo okuva mu kifo ekimu eky’okulya okudda mu kirala, ennyanja ekika kya sperm whales, nga zikulemberwa obutonde obumu obutasobya —tugambe, okusinga, amagezi ag’ekyama okuva eri Katonda —okusinga ziwuga mu misuwa , nga bwe ziyitibwa; nga bagenda mu maaso n’ekkubo lyabwe nga bayita mu layini y’ennyanja eweereddwa n’obutuufu obutakyuka, ne kiba nti tewali mmeeri yonna ebadde etambulira mu kkubo lyayo, ku kipande kyonna, n’ekitundu kimu eky’ekkumi eky’obutuufu obw’ekitalo bwe butyo. Wadde, mu mbeera zino, obulagirizi obutwalibwa ennyanja ekika kya whale emu yonna bubeere bugolokofu ng’okufaanana kw’omupunta, era newankubadde nga layini y’okugenda mu maaso ekoma nnyo ku kuzuukuka kwayo okuteewalika, okugolokofu, naye omusuwa ogw’ekimpatiira mu biseera bino mw’agambibwa okuwuga, okutwalira awamu kukwata mayiro nga ntono mu bugazi (okusinga oba okutono, ng’omusuwa bwe guteeberezebwa okugaziwa oba okukendeera); naye tesukkangako kusenya kulaba okuva ku mitwe gy’emmeeri ya whale, bwe giseeyeeya mu ngeri ey’obwegendereza mu kitundu kino eky’amagezi. Omugatte guli nti, mu sizoni ezitali zimu mu bugazi obwo ne mu kkubo eryo, ennyanja ekika kya whale ezisenguka ziyinza okunoonyezebwa n’obwesige bungi.

Era n’olwekyo si mu biseera ebikakafu byokka, ku bifo ebimanyiddwa ennyo eby’okulya eby’enjawulo, Akabu yali asobola okusuubira okusisinkana omuyiggo gwe; naye mu kusala ebifo eby’amazzi ebisinga obugazi wakati w’ebibangirizi ebyo yali asobola, olw’obuyiiya bwe, bw’atyo okuteeka n’obudde bwe ye kennyini mu kkubo lye, nga ne mu kiseera ekyo obutaba ddala nga talina ssuubi lya kusisinkana.

Waaliwo embeera nga ku kusooka okulaba ng’ezing’amya enteekateeka ye ey’okuwuubaala naye ng’ekyali ya nkola. Naye si bwe kiri mu butuufu, mpozzi. Newankubadde nga ennyanja ekika kya sperm whales ezikwatagana zirina sizoni zazo eza bulijjo olw’ebifo ebimu, naye okutwalira awamu toyinza kumaliriza nti ebisibo ebyabadde biwunya latitude oba longitude bwetyo omwaka guno, tugambe, bijja kufuuka bye bimu n’ebyo ebyasangibwayo sizoni ewedde; wadde nga waliwo emirundi egy’enjawulo era egitabuusibwabuusibwa nga kino kizuuliddwa nti ekikontana n’ekyo. Okutwalira awamu, ekigambo kye kimu, mu kkomo eritali ddene lyokka, kikwata ku bantu abali bokka n’abaweereza mu nnyanja ekika kya sperm whale ezikuze era ezikaddiye. Kale newankubadde nga Moby Dick yali mu mwaka ogw’emabega alabiddwako, okugeza, ku kifo ekiyitibwa Seychelle ground mu nnyanja Buyindi, oba Volcano Bay ku lubalama lw’ennyanja Japan; naye tekyagoberera, nti singa Pequod yagenda okukyalira ekimu ku bifo ebyo mu sizoni yonna ekwatagana eddako, yandimusisinkanye eyo awatali nsobi. Bwe kityo era n’ebifo ebirala eby’okulya, oluusi gye yali yeeyolekedde. Naye bino byonna byalabika ng’ebifo bye eby’okuyimiriramu n’ebifo eby’okusulamu eby’omu nnyanja byokka, bwe tuyinza okugamba, so si bifo bye yamala ebbanga eddene. Era emikisa gya Akabu egy’okutuukiriza ekigendererwa kye gye gyayogerwako okutuusa kati, allusion ebadde ekoleddwa ku buli ludda lw’ekkubo, antecedent, extra essuubi yonna gye yali eyiye, ere a particular set time or place were attained, when all possibilities would become probabilities, and , nga Akabu bwe yalowooza n’okwagala, buli kisoboka ekintu ekiddako okutuuka ku kikakafu. Ekiseera ekyo n’ekifo ekyo ekigere ekyateekebwawo byagattibwa mu kigambo kimu eky’ekikugu —Sizoni-ku-Line. Kubanga eyo n’oluvannyuma, okumala emyaka egiwerako egy’omuddiring’anwa, Moby Dick yali ayogerwako buli luvannyuma lwa kiseera, ng’asigala mu mazzi ago okumala akaseera, ng’enjuba, mu nkulungo yaayo ey’omwaka, ewuubaala okumala ekiseera ekiteeberezebwa mu kabonero konna akamu aka Zodiac. Awo era we waali nti ensisinkano ezisinga obungi ezatta abantu n’ennyanja enjeru zaali zibaddewo; eyo amayengo gaayogerwako n’ebikolwa bye; era waaliwo ekifo ekyo eky’ekikangabwa omusajja omukadde omuyiiya omu gye yali azudde ekigendererwa eky’entiisa eky’okwesasuza kwe. Naye mu kwegendereza okujjuvu n’obulindaala obutasalako Akabu bwe yasuula emmeeme ye eyali ewunyiriza mu kuyigga kuno okutakyukakyuka, teyandikkirizza kussa ssuubi lye lyonna ku nsonga emu enkulu eyogeddwako waggulu, ne bwe yandibadde enyumiza essuubi eryo; era mu buteebaka bw’obweyamo bwe yali tasobola kukkakkanya mutima gwe ogutali musirise n’atuuka n’okwongezaayo okunoonya kwonna okwali kuyingirira.

Kati, emmeeri ya Pequod yali evudde e Nantucket ku ntandikwa yennyini eya Season-on-the-Line. Tewali kaweefube yenna asoboka mu kiseera ekyo yali asobola kusobozesa omuduumizi we okukola ekkubo eddene erigenda ebugwanjuba, emirundi ebiri Cape Horn, n’oluvannyuma ng’adduka wansi diguli nkaaga eza latitude okutuuka mu equatorial Pacific mu budde okusaabala eyo. N’olwekyo, alina okulinda sizoni eddako eddako. Naye essaawa eyasooka ey’okusaabala Pequod, oboolyawo, yali esunsuddwa bulungi Akabu, ng’atunuulira langi eno yennyini ey’ebintu. Kubanga, ebbanga ery’ennaku n’ekiro ebikumi bisatu mu nkaaga mu ttaano lyali mu maaso ge; ekiseera ekigere, mu kifo ky’okugumira ku lukalu mu butagumiikiriza, kye yandimaze mu kuyigga okw’enjawulo; singa mu butanwa White Whale, ng’amala oluwummula lwe mu nnyanja eziri ewala ennyo okuva mu bifo by’alya buli luvannyuma lwa kiseera, yandikyusizza waggulu ekibatu kye ekinyirira okuva ku Persian Gulf, oba mu Bengal Bay, oba China Seas, oba mu mazzi amalala gonna agalimu ebizibu bye okusindana. Olwo Monsoons, Pampas, Nor'-Westers, Harmattans, Eby'obusuubuzi; empewo yonna okuggyako Levanter ne Simoon, eyinza okufuuwa Moby Dick mu nkulungo y’ensi ey’obulimba eya zig-zag ey’okuzuukuka kwa Pequod okwetooloola.

Naye okukkiriza bino byonna; naye, bwe kitunuulirwa mu ngeri ey’amagezi era mu ngeri ennyogovu, kirabika si wabula kirowoozo kya ddalu, kino; nti mu nnyanja engazi etaliiko nsalo, whale emu yokka, ne bw’eba esangibwa, erina okulowoozebwa nti esobola okumanyibwa ssekinnoomu okuva eri omuyizzi waayo, ne bwe yali Mufti ow’ekirevu ekyeru mu nguudo ezijjudde abantu mu Constantinople? Yee. Kubanga ekibatu ekyewunyisa ekyeru ng’omuzira ekya Moby Dick, n’ekifuba kye ekyeru ng’omuzira, tebyayinza butabuusibwabuusibwa. Era sibala ennyanja ekika kya whale, Akabu yandigugumbudde mu mutima gwe, ng’oluvannyuma lw’okutunula mu bipande bye okutuusa nga wayiseewo ekiseera ekiwanvu mu ttumbi lwe yeesuula emabega mu birooto —n’amubala, era anaatoloka? Ebiwaawaatiro bye ebigazi biboola, era bifuuse ebiwujjo ng’okutu kw’endiga ebuze! Era wano, ebirowoozo bye eby’eddalu byandidduse mu misinde egy’okussa; okutuusa obukoowu n’okuzirika olw’okufumiitiriza lwe byamutuukako; era mu bbanga erya ddeeke yali anoonya okuddamu amaanyi ge. Ah, Katonda! bizibu ki eby’okubonyaabonyezebwa omuntu oyo by’agumira amalibwa n’obwagazi bumu obw’okwesasuza obutatuukiddwaako. Asula ng’anywedde emikono; era azuukuka ng’alina emisumaali gye egy’omusaayi mu ngalo.

Emirundi mingi, bwe yawalirizibwa okuva mu hammock ye olw’ebirooto ebikooya era ebirabika obulungi mu ngeri etagumiikiriza eby’ekiro, ebyaddamu okutandika ebirowoozo bye eby’amaanyi okuyita mu lunaku, ne bibitwala mu maaso wakati mu kulwanagana kw’ebigambo ebisongovu, ne biziwuuta okwetoloola n’okwetooloola n’okwetooloola mu bwongo bwe obwali buyaka, . okutuusa okukuba kwennyini okw’ekifo kye eky’obulamu lwe kwafuuka ennaku etagumiikiriza; era nga, nga bwe kyali oluusi, ebizibu bino eby’omwoyo mu ye bwe byasitula obulamu bwe okuva ku musingi gwabwo, era ekinnya ne kirabika nga kigguka mu ye, ennimi z’omuliro ezaali zifuukuuse n’okumyansa ne bimukuba waggulu, era emizimu egyakolimirwa ne gimukola akabonero okubuuka wansi wakati waabwe; ggeyeena eno ku yennyini bwe yawuuma wansi we, eddoboozi ery’omu nsiko lyandiwuliddwa nga liyita mu mmeeri; era n’amaaso agayakaayakana Akabu yabanga akutuse okuva mu kisenge kye eky’eggwanga, ng’alinga atolose ku kitanda ekyali kikutte omuliro. Naye bino, mpozzi, mu kifo ky’okuba obubonero obutaziyizibwa obw’obunafu obumu obukusike, oba okutya olw’obumalirivu bwe, bwali bubonero obusingayo obweyoleka obw’amaanyi gabwo. Kubanga, mu biseera ng’ebyo, Akabu omugwa eddalu, omuyizzi w’ennyanja enjeru eyali akola enkwe, omunywevu mu ngeri etasanyusa; ono Akabu eyali agenze mu hammock ye, si ye agent eyamuleetera bwetyo okubutuka okuva mu yo mu ntiisa nate. Ekisembayo kyali musingi oba emmeeme ey’olubeerera, ennamu mu ye; era mu tulo, olw’okuba okumala ekiseera nga yeekutudde ku birowoozo ebiraga, ebiseera ebirala ebyabikozesanga olw’ekidduka kyayo eky’ebweru oba ekikola, mu ngeri eyeetongodde yanoonya okutoloka okuva mu kukwatagana okwokya okw’ekintu ekiwuniikiriza, nga, mu kiseera ekyo, kye kyali nedda longer an integral. Naye nga ebirowoozo bwe bitaliiwo okuggyako nga bikwatagana n’omwoyo, n’olwekyo kiteekwa okuba nga kyali ekyo, mu mbeera ya Akabu, okuwaayo ebirowoozo bye byonna n’ebirooto bye eri ekigendererwa kye ekimu eky’oku ntikko; ekigendererwa ekyo, olw’obutafaayo bwakyo obw’okwagala, kyekaka okulwanyisa bakatonda ne sitaani ne kifuuka ekika ky’ekitonde ekyetwala, ekyetongodde ku kyakyo. Nedda, yali esobola okuwangaala mu ngeri ey’ennaku n’okwokya, ate ng’obulamu obw’awamu bwe bwagattibwa, bwadduka nga butiisibwatiisibwa okuva mu kuzaalibwa okutayitibwa era okutali kwa kitaawe. N’olwekyo, omwoyo ogwabonyaabonyezebwa ogwayakaayakana okuva mu maaso g’omubiri, ekyalabika nga Akabu bwe kyafubutuka okuva mu kisenge kye, kyali kya kiseera ekyo naye ekintu ekitaliimu, ekitonde eky’okwebaka ekitaliiko kifaananyi, omusinde gw’ekitangaala ekiramu, okukakasa, naye nga tekirina kintu kyonna langi, n’olwekyo ekifo ekitaliimu kintu kyonna ku bwakyo. Katonda akuyambe, omukadde, ebirowoozo byo bitonze ekitonde mu ggwe; era oyo endowooza ye ey’amaanyi bwetyo emufuula Prometheus; envubu eriisa omutima ogwo emirembe gyonna; envubu eyo ekitonde kyennyini ky’atonda.





ESSUULA 45. Ekirayiro.

Okutuuka ku kiki ekiyinza okubaawo eky’ennyonnyola mu kitabo kino; era, mazima ddala, nga bwe kikwata mu ngeri etali butereevu ku kintu kimu oba bibiri ebinyuvu ennyo era ebyewuunyisa mu mize gy’ennyanja ekika kya sperm whales, essuula eyo waggulu, mu kitundu kyayo ekyasooka, nkulu nnyo nga bwe kinaasangibwa mu muzingo guno; naye ensonga ekulembera ku kyo yeetaaga okwongera okugaziwa ennyo era mu ngeri emanyiddwa ennyo, okusobola okutegeerwa obulungi, n’okusingawo okuggyawo obutakkiriza bwonna obutamanya obw’amaanyi ku nsonga yonna bwe buyinza okuleeta mu birowoozo ebimu, ku mazima ag’obutonde ku nsonga enkulu ezikwata ku nsonga eno.

Nfaayo obutakola kitundu kino eky’omulimu gwange mu ngeri ey’enkola; naye ajja kuba mumativu okufulumya endowooza eyagala nga ajuliza ebintu eby’enjawulo, ebimanyiddwa mu nkola oba mu ngeri eyeesigika gyendi ng’omuvuzi w’ennyanja ekika kya whale; era okuva mu biwandiiko bino ebijuliziddwa, nkitwala —enkomerero egendereddwamu mu butonde ejja kugoberera ku bwayo.

Ekisooka: Nze ku lwange mmanyi emirundi esatu ng’ennyanja ekika kya whale, oluvannyuma lw’okufuna ekyuma ekiyitibwa harpoon, evuddeko okutoloka ddala; era, oluvannyuma lw’ekiseera (mu kiseera kimu eky’emyaka esatu), azzeemu okukubwa omukono gwe gumu, n’attibwa; ebyuma ebibiri, byombi nga biwandiikiddwako cypher y’emu ey’obwannannyini, bwe biba biggyiddwa ku mulambo. Mu mbeera nga emyaka esatu giyingiddewo wakati w’okusuula ebyuma ebibiri; era ndowooza nti kiyinza okuba nga kyali kintu ekisingako awo; omusajja eyaziddusa nga kigenda mu maaso, mu kiseera ekyo, okugenda mu mmeeri y’obusuubuzi ku lugendo lw’e Afirika, n’agenda ku lukalu eyo, ne yeegatta ku kibiina ky’abazuula, n’ayingira wala munda mu nsi, gye yatambulira okumala ebbanga lya kumpi emyaka ebiri, . ebiseera ebisinga zibeera mu kabi olw’emisota, ensolo enkambwe, enjovu, miasmas ez’obutwa, n’akabi akalala konna akatera okubaawo mu kutaayaaya mu mutima gw’ebitundu ebitamanyiddwa. Mu kiseera ekyo, ennyanja ekika kya whale gye yali akubye nayo eteekwa okuba nga yali ku ntambula zaayo; awatali kubuusabuusa yali yeetoolodde ensi emirundi esatu, ng’esiimuula n’enjuyi zaayo ku lubalama lw’ennyanja zonna eza Afirika; naye nga tewali kigendererwa kyonna. Omusajja ono n’ennyanja eno ekika kya whale nate ne bakwatagana, era omu n’awangula munne. Nze ngamba nze, nze kennyini, mmanyi emirundi esatu egifaanagana ne gino; kwe kugamba mu bibiri ku byo nnalaba ennyanja ekika kya whale nga zikubiddwa; era, ku kulumba okw’okubiri, yalaba ebyuma ebibiri nga bisaliddwamu obubonero obwo, oluvannyuma ne biggyibwa mu byennyanja ebifudde. Mu mbeera ey’emyaka esatu, kyagwa nnyo ne kiba nti nnali mu lyato emirundi gyombi, okusooka n’okusembayo, era omulundi ogwasembayo nnategeera bulungi ekika eky’enjawulo eky’ekiwujjo ekinene ennyo wansi w’eriiso ly’ennyanja ekika kya whale, kye nnali ndabye eyo emyaka esatu emabega. Nate, kitera nnyo okwetegereza nti, singa sperm whale, bw’emala okukubwa, ekkirizibwa obudde okukuŋŋaana, olwo n’ekola, si mirundi mingi nnyo n’obusungu obutazibe, nga n’enteekateeka z’okuzikiriza mu bugenderevu, mu bugenderevu eri abagigoberera; nor is it without conveying some eloquent indication of his character, that upon being attacked he will frequently open his mouth, and retain it in that dread expansion for several consecutive minutes. But I must be content with only one more and a concluding illustration; a remarkable and most significant one, by which you will not fail to see, that not only is the most marvellous event in this book corroborated by plain facts of the present day, but that these marvels (like all marvels) are mere repetitions of the ages; so that for the millionth time we say amen with Solomon—Verily there is nothing new under the sun.

In the sixth Christian century lived Procopius, a Christian magistrate of Constantinople, in the days when Justinian was Emperor and Belisarius general. As many know, he wrote the history of his own times, a work every way of uncommon value. By the best authorities, he has always been considered a most trustworthy and unexaggerating historian, except in some one or two particulars, not at all affecting the matter presently to be mentioned.

Now, in this history of his, Procopius mentions that, during the term of his prefecture at Constantinople, a great sea-monster was captured in the neighboring Propontis, or Sea of Marmora, after having destroyed vessels at intervals in those waters for a period of more than fifty years. A fact thus set down in substantial history cannot easily be gainsaid. Nor is there any reason it should be. Of what precise species this sea-monster was, is not mentioned. But as he destroyed ships, as well as for other reasons, he must have been a whale; and I am strongly inclined to think a sperm whale. And I will tell you why. For a long time I fancied that the sperm whale had been always unknown in the Mediterranean and the deep waters connecting with it. Even now I am certain that those seas are not, and perhaps never can be, in the present constitution of things, a place for his habitual gregarious resort. But further investigations have recently proved to me, that in modern times there have been isolated instances of the presence of the sperm whale in the Mediterranean. I am told, on good authority, that on the Barbary coast, a Commodore Davis of the British navy found the skeleton of a sperm whale. Now, as a vessel of war readily passes through the Dardanelles, hence a sperm whale could, by the same route, pass out of the Mediterranean into the Propontis.

In the Propontis, as far as I can learn, none of that peculiar substance called brit is to be found, the aliment of the right whale. But I have every reason to believe that the food of the sperm whale—squid or cuttle-fish—lurks at the bottom of that sea, because large creatures, but by no means the largest of that sort, have been found at its surface. If, then, you properly put these statements together, and reason upon them a bit, you will clearly perceive that, according to all human reasoning, Procopius's sea-monster, that for half a century stove the ships of a Roman Emperor, must in all probability have been a sperm whale.





CHAPTER 46. Surmises.

Though, consumed with the hot fire of his purpose, Ahab in all his thoughts and actions ever had in view the ultimate capture of Moby Dick; though he seemed ready to sacrifice all mortal interests to that one passion; nevertheless it may have been that he was by nature and long habituation far too wedded to a fiery whaleman's ways, altogether to abandon the collateral prosecution of the voyage. Or at least if this were otherwise, there were not wanting other motives much more influential with him. It would be refining too much, perhaps, even considering his monomania, to hint that his vindictiveness towards the White Whale might have possibly extended itself in some degree to all sperm whales, and that the more monsters he slew by so much the more he multiplied the chances that each subsequently encountered whale would prove to be the hated one he hunted. But if such an hypothesis be indeed exceptionable, there were still additional considerations which, though not so strictly according with the wildness of his ruling passion, yet were by no means incapable of swaying him.

To accomplish his object Ahab must use tools; and of all tools used in the shadow of the moon, men are most apt to get out of order. He knew, for example, that however magnetic his ascendency in some respects was over Starbuck, yet that ascendency did not cover the complete spiritual man any more than mere corporeal superiority involves intellectual mastership; for to the purely spiritual, the intellectual but stand in a sort of corporeal relation. Starbuck's body and Starbuck's coerced will were Ahab's, so long as Ahab kept his magnet at Starbuck's brain; still he knew that for all this the chief mate, in his soul, abhorred his captain's quest, and could he, would joyfully disintegrate himself from it, or even frustrate it. It might be that a long interval would elapse ere the White Whale was seen. During that long interval Starbuck would ever be apt to fall into open relapses of rebellion against his captain's leadership, unless some ordinary, prudential, circumstantial influences were brought to bear upon him. Not only that, but the subtle insanity of Ahab respecting Moby Dick was noways more significantly manifested than in his superlative sense and shrewdness in foreseeing that, for the present, the hunt should in some way be stripped of that strange imaginative impiousness which naturally invested it; that the full terror of the voyage must be kept withdrawn into the obscure background (for few men's courage is proof against protracted meditation unrelieved by action); that when they stood their long night watches, his officers and men must have some nearer things to think of than Moby Dick. For however eagerly and impetuously the savage crew had hailed the announcement of his quest; yet all sailors of all sorts are more or less capricious and unreliable—they live in the varying outer weather, and they inhale its fickleness—and when retained for any object remote and blank in the pursuit, however promissory of life and passion in the end, it is above all things requisite that temporary interests and employments should intervene and hold them healthily suspended for the final dash.

Nor was Ahab unmindful of another thing. In times of strong emotion mankind disdain all base considerations; but such times are evanescent. The permanent constitutional condition of the manufactured man, thought Ahab, is sordidness. Granting that the White Whale fully incites the hearts of this my savage crew, and playing round their savageness even breeds a certain generous knight-errantism in them, still, while for the love of it they give chase to Moby Dick, they must also have food for their more common, daily appetites. For even the high lifted and chivalric Crusaders of old times were not content to traverse two thousand miles of land to fight for their holy sepulchre, without committing burglaries, picking pockets, and gaining other pious perquisites by the way. Had they been strictly held to their one final and romantic object—that final and romantic object, too many would have turned from in disgust. I will not strip these men, thought Ahab, of all hopes of cash—aye, cash. They may scorn cash now; but let some months go by, and no perspective promise of it to them, and then this same quiescent cash all at once mutinying in them, this same cash would soon cashier Ahab.

Nor was there wanting still another precautionary motive more related to Ahab personally. Having impulsively, it is probable, and perhaps somewhat prematurely revealed the prime but private purpose of the Pequod's voyage, Ahab was now entirely conscious that, in so doing, he had indirectly laid himself open to the unanswerable charge of usurpation; and with perfect impunity, both moral and legal, his crew if so disposed, and to that end competent, could refuse all further obedience to him, and even violently wrest from him the command. From even the barely hinted imputation of usurpation, and the possible consequences of such a suppressed impression gaining ground, Ahab must of course have been most anxious to protect himself. That protection could only consist in his own predominating brain and heart and hand, backed by a heedful, closely calculating attention to every minute atmospheric influence which it was possible for his crew to be subjected to.

For all these reasons then, and others perhaps too analytic to be verbally developed here, Ahab plainly saw that he must still in a good degree continue true to the natural, nominal purpose of the Pequod's voyage; observe all customary usages; and not only that, but force himself to evince all his well known passionate interest in the general pursuit of his profession.

Be all this as it may, his voice was now often heard hailing the three mast-heads and admonishing them to keep a bright look-out, and not omit reporting even a porpoise. This vigilance was not long without reward.





CHAPTER 47. The Mat-Maker.

It was a cloudy, sultry afternoon; the seamen were lazily lounging about the decks, or vacantly gazing over into the lead-coloured waters. Queequeg and I were mildly employed weaving what is called a sword-mat, for an additional lashing to our boat. So still and subdued and yet somehow preluding was all the scene, and such an incantation of reverie lurked in the air, that each silent sailor seemed resolved into his own invisible self.

I was the attendant or page of Queequeg, while busy at the mat. As I kept passing and repassing the filling or woof of marline between the long yarns of the warp, using my own hand for the shuttle, and as Queequeg, standing sideways, ever and anon slid his heavy oaken sword between the threads, and idly looking off upon the water, carelessly and unthinkingly drove home every yarn: I say so strange a dreaminess did there then reign all over the ship and all over the sea, only broken by the intermitting dull sound of the sword, that it seemed as if this were the Loom of Time, and I myself were a shuttle mechanically weaving and weaving away at the Fates. There lay the fixed threads of the warp subject to but one single, ever returning, unchanging vibration, and that vibration merely enough to admit of the crosswise interblending of other threads with its own. This warp seemed necessity; and here, thought I, with my own hand I ply my own shuttle and weave my own destiny into these unalterable threads. Meantime, Queequeg's impulsive, indifferent sword, sometimes hitting the woof slantingly, or crookedly, or strongly, or weakly, as the case might be; and by this difference in the concluding blow producing a corresponding contrast in the final aspect of the completed fabric; this savage's sword, thought I, which thus finally shapes and fashions both warp and woof; this easy, indifferent sword must be chance—aye, chance, free will, and necessity—nowise incompatible—all interweavingly working together. The straight warp of necessity, not to be swerved from its ultimate course—its every alternating vibration, indeed, only tending to that; free will still free to ply her shuttle between given threads; and chance, though restrained in its play within the right lines of necessity, and sideways in its motions directed by free will, though thus prescribed to by both, chance by turns rules either, and has the last featuring blow at events.

Thus we were weaving and weaving away when I started at a sound so strange, long drawn, and musically wild and unearthly, that the ball of free will dropped from my hand, and I stood gazing up at the clouds whence that voice dropped like a wing. High aloft in the cross-trees was that mad Gay-Header, Tashtego. His body was reaching eagerly forward, his hand stretched out like a wand, and at brief sudden intervals he continued his cries. To be sure the same sound was that very moment perhaps being heard all over the seas, from hundreds of whalemen's look-outs perched as high in the air; but from few of those lungs could that accustomed old cry have derived such a marvellous cadence as from Tashtego the Indian's.

As he stood hovering over you half suspended in air, so wildly and eagerly peering towards the horizon, you would have thought him some prophet or seer beholding the shadows of Fate, and by those wild cries announcing their coming.

“There she blows! there! there! there! she blows! she blows!”

“Where-away?”

“On the lee-beam, about two miles off! a school of them!”

Instantly all was commotion.

The Sperm Whale blows as a clock ticks, with the same undeviating and reliable uniformity. And thereby whalemen distinguish this fish from other tribes of his genus.

“There go flukes!” was now the cry from Tashtego; and the whales disappeared.

“Quick, steward!” cried Ahab. “Time! time!”

Dough-Boy hurried below, glanced at the watch, and reported the exact minute to Ahab.

The ship was now kept away from the wind, and she went gently rolling before it. Tashtego reporting that the whales had gone down heading to leeward, we confidently looked to see them again directly in advance of our bows. For that singular craft at times evinced by the Sperm Whale when, sounding with his head in one direction, he nevertheless, while concealed beneath the surface, mills round, and swiftly swims off in the opposite quarter—this deceitfulness of his could not now be in action; for there was no reason to suppose that the fish seen by Tashtego had been in any way alarmed, or indeed knew at all of our vicinity. One of the men selected for shipkeepers—that is, those not appointed to the boats, by this time relieved the Indian at the main-mast head. The sailors at the fore and mizzen had come down; the line tubs were fixed in their places; the cranes were thrust out; the mainyard was backed, and the three boats swung over the sea like three samphire baskets over high cliffs. Outside of the bulwarks their eager crews with one hand clung to the rail, while one foot was expectantly poised on the gunwale. So look the long line of man-of-war's men about to throw themselves on board an enemy's ship.

But at this critical instant a sudden exclamation was heard that took every eye from the whale. With a start all glared at dark Ahab, who was surrounded by five dusky phantoms that seemed fresh formed out of air.
