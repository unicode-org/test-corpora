ESSUULA 21. Okugenda ku lyato.

Zaali kumpi ssaawa mukaaga, naye nga ziri enzirugavu enzirugavu ezitatuukiridde nga zirimu enkuba, bwe twasemberera omwalo.

“Waliwo abalunnyanja abamu abadduka mu maaso eyo, bwe mba ndaba bulungi,” bwe nnagamba Queequeg, “tekiyinza kuba bisiikirize; she’s off by sunrise, I guess; come on!”

“Avast!” eddoboozi lyakaaba, nnannyini lyo mu kiseera kye kimu ng’asemberera emabega waffe, n’ateeka omukono ku bibegabega byaffe byombi, n’oluvannyuma n’ayingiza wakati waffe, ne liyimirira nga lifukamidde mu maaso katono, mu budde obw’ekizikizo obutali bukakafu, nga litunula mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa okuva e Queequeg gye ndi. Yali Eriya.

“Okugenda ku lyato?”

“Emikono giggye, ojja,” bwe nnagamba.

“Lookee wano,” Queequeg bwe yagamba ng’akankana, “genda ‘way!”

“Kale togenda kulinnya?”

“Yee, ffe bwe tuli,” bwe nnagamba, “naye omulimu gwo guli gutya?

“Nedda, nedda, nedda;ekyo nnali sikimanyi,” Elijah bwe yagamba, ng’atunudde mpola mpola era nga yeewuunya okuva gyendi okutuuka e Queequeg, n’amaaso agasinga obutavunaanyizibwa.

Nze ne mmugamba nti: “Eliya, ojja kukaka nze ne mukwano gwange ng’oggyayo. Tugenda mu nnyanja Buyindi ne Pacific, era twandiyagadde obutasibibwa.”

“Mubeere, mubeere?Okomawo nga tonnalya kyankya?”

“Akutuse, Queequeg,” bwe nnagamba, “jjangu.”

“Holloa!” Eriya eyali ayimiridde bwe yakaaba, ng’atutendereza nga tumaze okuggyawo amadaala matono.

“Temufaako,” bwe nnagamba, “Queequeg, jangu.”

Naye n’addamu okutubba, era amangu ago n’akuba omukono gwe ku kibegabega kyange, n’agamba nti — “Mulabye ekintu kyonna ekifaanana ng’abasajja nga bagenda okwolekera emmeeri eyo akaseera katono emabega?”

Olw’okukubwa ekibuuzo kino eky’amazima, nnaddamu, nga mmugamba nti, “Yee, nnalowooza nti ddala ndabye abasajja bana oba bataano; naye kyali kizikidde nnyo okukakasa.”

Eriya yagamba nti: “Ekizikiza nnyo, kizikidde nnyo.” “Enkya gye muli.”

Nate twamulekawo; naye omulundi omulala n’atugoberera mpola; era n’addamu okukwata ku kibegabega kyange, n’agamba nti, “Laba oba osobola okubasanga kati, nedda?

“Funa ani?”

“Enkya gye muli, enkya gye muli!” yaddamu okwegatta, n’addamu okusenguka. “Oo! Nnali hhenda kubalabula ku —naye sifaayo, sifaayo — byonna bimu, byonna mu maka nabyo;—omuzira ogw’amaanyi enkya ya leero, si bwe kiri? Musiibule. Sijja kuddamu kubalaba mangu nnyo, nteebereza; okuggyako nga biri mu maaso g’Abalamuzi Abakulu.” Era n’ebigambo bino ebyatika yatuuka n’okugenda, n’andeka, mu kiseera kino, nga siwuniikirira nnyo olw’obugwenyufu bwe obw’ekitalo.

Mu nkomerero, nga tulinnya ku lyato erya Pequod, twasanga buli kimu mu kasirise ak’amaanyi, so si mwoyo nga gutambula. Omulyango oguyingira mu kabina gwali gusibiddwa munda; ebisenge byonna byali ku, era nga bikutte koyilo za rigging. Nga tugenda mu maaso ku forecastle, twasanga slide ya scuttle nga eggule. Nga tulaba ettaala, twakka, ne tusangayo rigger enkadde yokka, ng’ezingiddwa mu jaketi y’entangawuuzi eyali ekutuse. Yasuulibwa mu buwanvu bwonna ku bifuba bibiri, ffeesi ye wansi era ng’asibiddwa mu mikono gye egyali gikutte. Otulo otusinga obunene bwamusulako.

“Abalunnyanja abo be twalaba, Queequeg, bayinza okuba nga baagenda wa?” bwe nnagamba nga ntunuulira eyali yeebase mu ngeri ey’okubuusabuusa. Naye kyalabika nti, bwe yali ku mwalo, Queequeg yali tannalaba n’akatono kye nnali njogerako kati; n’olwekyo nnandirowoozezza nti nnalimbibwa mu ngeri ey’amaaso mu nsonga eyo, singa si kibuuzo kya Eriya ekyali tekiyinza kunnyonnyolwa. Naye ekintu nakikuba wansi; era nate ng’assaako akabonero ku muntu eyeebase, mu ngeri ey’okusaaga n’alaga Queequeg nti osanga twandibadde kirungi okutuula waggulu n’omulambo; ng’amugamba okwenyweza okusinziira ku ekyo. Yassa omukono gwe emabega w’oyo eyeebase, ng’alinga awulira oba nga gugonvu ekimala; n’oluvannyuma, awatali kwongera kuyomba, n’atuula wansi awo mu kasirise.

“Gracious! Queequeg, totuula awo,” bwe nnagamba.

“Oh! perry dood seat,” Queequeg bwe yagamba, “my country way; sijja kumulumya face.”

"Feesi!" nagamba nti, “ekyo kiyite ffeesi ye? ffeesi ey’ekisa ennyo olwo; naye ng’assa nnyo, yeesitula;va, Queequeg, oli muzito, kisenya ffeesi y’abaavu.Vuka, Queequeg! Laba, ajja kukunyiga mangu.Nneewuunya tazuukuka.”

Queequeg yeeggyawo okutuuka okusukka omutwe gw’omuntu eyeebase, n’akoleeza payipu ye eya tomahawk. Natuula ku bigere. Twakuuma payipu ng’eyita waggulu w’ekisulo, okuva ku kimu okudda ku kirala. Mu kiseera ekyo, bwe yamubuuza mu ngeri ye eyamenyese, Queequeg yampa okutegeera nti, mu nsi ye, olw’obutabaawo settee ne sofa eza buli ngeri, kabaka, abaami, n’abantu abakulu okutwalira awamu, baali mu mpisa y’okugejja abamu ku ba wansi eri aba ottoman; era okusobola okufunira ennyumba mu ngeri ennungi mu nsonga eyo, walina okugula bannaffe munaana oba kkumi abagayaavu, n’obagalamiza okwetooloola mu mwalo ne mu biyumba. Ng’oggyeeko ekyo, kyali kirungi nnyo ku lugendo; esinga nnyo entebe ezo ez’omu lusuku ezikyusibwa ne zifuuka emiggo; oluusi, omukulu ng’ayita omuweereza we, era ng’ayagala yeekole settee wansi w’omuti ogubunye, oboolyawo mu kifo ekimu ekinyogovu ekirimu ebitosi.

Nga anyumya ebintu bino, buli Queequeg lwe yafunanga tomahawk okuva gyendi, yafuumuuka ku ludda lwayo olwa hatchet-side ku mutwe gw’omuntu eyeebase.

“Ekyo kya ki, Queequeg?”

“Perry kyangu, kitta-e;oh! perry kyangu!”

Yali agenda mu maaso n’ebimu ku bijjukizo eby’omu nsiko ku payipu ye eya tomahawk, nga kirabika, mu nkozesa yaayo ebbiri yalina byombi obwongo bw’abalabe be n’okukkakkanya emmeeme ye, bwe twasikiriza butereevu omuvubi eyali yeebase. Omukka ogw’amaanyi kati nga gujjuza ddala ekituli ekyali kikonziba, gwatandika okumugamba. Yassa n’ekika ky’okuzirika; olwo n’alabika ng’atabuse mu nnyindo; oluvannyuma ne yeetooloola omulundi gumu oba ebiri; olwo n’atuula n’asika amaaso ge.

“Holloa!” yassa omukka ku nkomerero, “mmwe abanywa sigala muli baani?”

Nnamuddamu nti: “Abasajja abali ku mmeeri, asaabala ddi?”

“Aye, ye, mugenda mu ye, muli? Asaabala leero. Kapiteeni yazze ku lyato ekiro ekikeesezza olwaleero.”

“Kapiteeni ki? — Akabu?”

“Ddala ani okuggyako ye?”

Nnali hhenda okumubuuza ebibuuzo ebirala ebikwata ku Akabu, ne tuwulira eddoboozi ku ddeeke.
