ESSUULA 48. Okukka Okusooka.

Phantoms, kubanga bwe zityo bwe zaali zirabika, zaali ziwuubaala ku ludda olulala olwa ddeeke, era, n’embiro ezitaliimu maloboozi, zaali zisumulula ebikozesebwa n’emiguwa gy’eryato eryali liwuubaala eyo. Eryato lino bulijjo lyali litwalibwa ng’erimu ku lyato erya sipeeya, wadde nga mu by’ekikugu lyali liyitibwa lya kapiteeni, olw’okuwanirira ku ddyo. Ekifaananyi kati ekyali kiyimiridde ku butaasa bwayo kyali kiwanvu era nga kiwunya, ng’erinnyo erimu eryeru lifulumye mu bubi okuva ku mimwa gyayo egyali giringa ekyuma. Jaketi y’Abachina eyali efuukuuse eya ppamba omuddugavu yamuteekamu ssente mu ngeri ey’okuziika, ng’alina bbulawuzi enjeru empanvu ez’ebintu bye bimu ebiddugavu. Naye mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa eyali etikkira engule eno ey’ekika kya ebonness yali ekitambaala ekyeru ekimasamasa nga kilukibwa, enviiri ennamu nga zilukibwa era nga zeetooloola ku mutwe gwe. Abaali batono nnyo mu ndabika, banne b’ekifaananyi kino baali ba langi eyo eyakaayakana, eya kyenvu ng’engo eyeetongodde eri abamu ku bantu enzaalwa z’e Manillas; abakessi abasasulwa n’abakungu ab’ekyama ab’ekyama ku mazzi ga sitaani, mukama waabwe, ekisenge kye eky’okubala ge balowooza nti kiri awalala.

Nga n’ekibinja ky’emmeeri ekyewuunyisa kitunudde mu bantu bano be batamanyi, Akabu n’akaabira omukadde ow’ekitambaala enjeru eyali ku mutwe gwabwe nti, “Bonna beetegese eyo, Fedallah?”

“Weetegefu,” bwe kyali eky’okuddamu nga kitundu.

“Mukka wansi n’olwekyo; d'ye owulidde?” nga baleekaana emitala wa ddeeke. “Mukka awo, ngamba.”

Okubwatuka kw’eddoboozi lye bwe kwali bwe kutyo, ne kiba nti wadde nga beewuunya abasajja ne babuuka ku ggaali y’omukka; ebinywa byali byetooloola mu bulooka; nga balina ekiwujjo, amaato asatu ne gagwa mu nnyanja; ate nga, n’obuvumu obw’amagezi, obutali bwa maanyi, obutamanyiddwa mu mulimu mulala gwonna, abalunnyanja, ng’embuzi, ne babuuka wansi ku ludda lw’emmeeri eyali yeekulukuunya ne bayingira mu maato agaali gasuuliddwa wansi.

Nga tebannaba kuva wansi w’omusulo gw’emmeeri, ekiwujjo eky’okuna, ekyava ku ludda olw’empewo, ne kisika okwetooloola wansi w’emabega w’emmeeri, ne kiraga abantu abataano abaali batamanyi nga bavugira Akabu, ng’ayimiridde nga yeegolodde emabega w’emmeeri, n’atendereza nnyo Starbuck, Stubb, . ne Flask, okwesaasaana ennyo, okusobola okubikka amazzi amangi. Naye ng’amaaso gaabwe gonna gazzeemu okusiba ku Fedallah omusuffu n’abakozi be, abasibe b’amaato amalala tebaagondera kiragiro.

Starbuck yagamba nti: “Kapiteeni Akabu?—.”

Akabu yayomba nti: “Mwebunye; “muwe ekkubo, amaato gonna ana. Ggwe, Flask, ggyayo ebisingawo ku leeward!”

“Aye, ye, ssebo,” akaaba akatono aka King-Post bwe kaakaaba mu ssanyu, nga kasenya okwetooloola ekyuma kye ekinene ekya siteeringi. “Sula emabega!” ng’ayogera eri abakozi be. “Eyo!—eyo!—eyo nate! Eyo gy’afuuwa mu maaso ddala, abalenzi! —galamira emabega!”

“Tofaayo ku balenzi ba kyenvu eyo, Archy.”

“Oo, sirina buzibu ’em, ssebo,” Archy bwe yagamba; “Byonna nnali mbimanyi nga kati tennabaawo. Ssaawulira 'em mu hold? Era wano saakibuulira Cabaco? Mugamba ki, Cabaco? Bano ba stowaways, Mwami Flask.”

“Sika, sika, emitima gyange emirungi-nga giramu; musika, abaana bange; pull, my little ones,” Stubb bwe yasiiba mu ngeri ey’okusikambula era ey’okukkakkanya eri abakozi be, abamu ku bo nga bakyalaga obubonero obw’obutabeera bulungi. “Lwaki temumenya migongo gyammwe, abalenzi bange? Kiki ky’otunuulira butunuzi? Abo chaps mu lyato eryo? Tut! Zino mikono emirala etaano gyokka egijja okutuyamba —never mind okuva wa —gy’okoma okusanyuka. Sika, n’olwekyo, kola okusika; never mind the brimstone — sitaani bannaabwe balungi ekimala. Kale, bwe kityo; eyo gy’oli kati; eyo ye stroke ya pawundi lukumi; eyo ye stroke okusenya emiggo! Hurrar ku kikopo kya zaabu eky'amafuta g'ensigo, abazira bange! Enjagala ssatu, abasajja —emitima gyonna miramu! Kyangu, kyangu; tobeera mu bwangu —tobeera mu bwangu. Lwaki temukuba biwujjo byammwe, mmwe abafere? Muluma ekintu, mmwe embwa! Kale, bwe kityo, bwe kityo, olwo:—mu mpola, mpola! Ekyo kye kiri —ekyo kye kiri! empanvu era nga za maanyi. Muwe ekkubo eyo, muwe ekkubo! Sitaani muleete mmwe, mmwe ragamuffin rapscallions; mwenna muli mu tulo. Mulekere awo okusinda, mmwe abeebase, musika. Sika, mujja kukikola? okusika, temusobola? okusika, si bwe kiri? Lwaki mu linnya lya gudgeons ne ginger-cakes temusika? —sika era mumenya ekintu! ssika, era otandike amaaso go okuvaayo! Wano!" ng’akuba ekiso ekisongovu okuva mu musipi gwe; “buli mutabani wa maama ku mmwe asika ekiso kye, n’okusika n’ekiso wakati w’amannyo ge. Ekyo kye kiri — ekyo kye kiri. Kaakano mulina kye mukola; ekyo kifaanana, ebyuma-bits zange. Mutandike —mutandike, ebijiiko byange ebya ffeeza! Mutandike, aba marling-spikes!”

Stubb’s exordium to his crew is given here at large, kubanga yalina engeri ey’enjawulo ennyo ey’okwogera nabo okutwaliza awamu, n’okusingira ddala mu kusiiga eddiini y’okuvuba. Naye tolina kulowooza okusinziira ku kyokulabirako kino eky’okubuulira kwe nti yabuukako mu kwegomba okw’amazima n’ekibiina kye. Nedda; era mu ekyo mwe mwalimu eky’enjawulo kye ekikulu. Yandigambye ebintu ebisinga okutiisa eri abakozi be, mu ddoboozi ery’ekyewuunyo ery’okusanyuka n’obusungu, era obusungu bwalabika nga bubaliriddwa nnyo ng’akawoowo kwokka eri okusanyuka, ne kiba nti tewali muvuzi wa mazzi yali asobola kuwulira kukoowoola kwa njawulo ng’okwo nga tasika kufuna bulamu bwa muwendo, . era naye nga basika olw’okusaaga kwokka okw’ekintu ekyo. Ng’oggyeeko ekyo buli kiseera yali alabika nga mwangu nnyo era nga mugayaavu ye kennyini, ng’addukanya siteeringi ye mu ngeri ey’okuwummulamu, era ng’azibula nnyo — ng’akamwa akaggule oluusi —ne kiba nti okulaba kwokka omuduumizi ng’awunya, olw’amaanyi amangi ag’enjawulo, kyakola ng’a charm ku bavuzi b’ennyonyi. Ate era, Stubb yali omu ku ba odd sort of humorists, nga jollity ye oluusi so curriously ambiguous, nga okuteeka bonna aba wansi ku bunkenke bwabwe mu nsonga y’okubagondera.

Mu kugondera akabonero okuva ewa Akabu, Starbuck kati yali asika mu ngeri ey’ekyekulungirivu okubuna obutaasa bwa Stubb; era okumala eddakiika emu oba bbiri amaato gombi bwe gaali gali kumpi nnyo, Stubb yasiima munne.

“Mw. Starbuck yaayo! eryato erya larboard awo, ahoy! ekigambo naawe, ssebo, bwe munaayagala!”

“Halloa!” yakomawo Starbuck, nga yeetooloola si yinsi emu nga bw’ayogera; akyakubiriza n’obunyiikivu naye ng’akuba enduulu abakozi be; ffeesi ye yateekebwa ng’amayinja amanene agava mu Stubb’s.

“Mulowooza ki ku balenzi abo aba kyenvu ssebo!”

“Emmeeri yakukusibwa, mu ngeri emu oba endala, nga emmeeri tennatambula. (Ba maanyi, ba maanyi, abalenzi!)” mu ngeri ey’okuwuubaala eri abakozi be, olwo n’addamu okwogera mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka nti: “Bizineesi ey’ennaku, Mwami Stubb! (seethe her, seethe her, my lads!) naye tofaayo, Mwami Stubb, byonna olw’obulungi. Leka abakozi bo bonna basike amaanyi, jjangu ekijja. (Ensulo, basajja bange, ensulo!) Waliwo embizzi z’ensigo mu maaso, Mwami Stubb, era ekyo kye mwajja. (Sika, abalenzi bange!) Ensigo, ensigo gwe muzannyo! Kino waakiri mulimu; omusolo n’amagoba nga bikwatagana.”

“Aye, aye, nalowooza nnyo,” Stubb bwe yayogera yekka, amaato bwe gaawukana, “amangu ddala nga nnakuba mu ngalo eriiso ku ’em, nnalowooza bwentyo. Aye, era ekyo kye yagenda mu after hold for, so often, nga Dough-Boy bwe yali amaze ebbanga ng’ateebereza. Baali bakwese wansi awo. White Whale eri wansi waayo. Wamma, bulungi, bwe kityo bwe kibeere! Tesobola kuyambibwa! Kaale! Muwe ekkubo, abasajja! Si ye White Whale leero! Muwe ekkubo!”

Kati okujja kw’abantu bano ab’enjawulo abatamanyi mu kaseera akazibu ng’okukka amaato okuva ku ddeeke, kino kyali tekizuukusizza mu ngeri etasaana kwewuunya kwonna okw’enzikiriza enkyamu mu bamu ku kkampuni y’emmeeri; naye Archy’s fancied discovery having some time previous got ebweru w’eggwanga mu bo, wadde nga ddala tekyaweebwa kitiibwa mu kiseera ekyo, kino mu kigero ekitono kyali kibategese omukolo. Kyaggyako ekyewuunyo kyabwe ekisukkiridde; era bwe kityo kiki ne bino byonna n’engeri Stubb gye yeesigamu ey’okubalirira endabika yaabwe, baali basumuluddwa mu kiseera ekyo okuva mu kuteebereza okw’enzikiriza enkyamu; wadde ng’ensonga eyo yali ekyaleka ekifo ekinene eky’okuteebereza okw’ensiko okwa buli ngeri ku kizikiza ekitongole ekituufu ekya Akabu mu nsonga eyo okuva ku ntandikwa. Nze, mu kasirise nnajjukira ebisiikirize eby’ekyama bye nnali ndabye nga byekulukuunya ku mmeeri ya Pequod mu kiseera ky’oku makya ennyo mu Nantucket, awamu n’ebigambo eby’ekyama ebya Elijah atavunaanyizibwa.

Mu kiseera ekyo, Akabu, olw’okuwulira abaserikale be, ng’avudde ewala ennyo okutuuka ku mpewo, yali akyagenda mu maaso n’amaato amalala; embeera espeaking engeri potent a crew yali emusika. Ebitonde bye ebyo ebya kyenvu eby’enjovu byali birabika nga byonna bya kyuma n’amagumba g’ennyanja ekika kya whale; nga ennyondo ttaano eziyitibwa trip-hammers zaasituka ne zigwa nga zikuba amaanyi aga bulijjo, nga buli luvannyuma lwa kiseera kyatandika eryato ku mabbali ng’ekyuma ekibuguma mu bbanga (horizontal burst boiler) okuva mu mmeeri y’omukka eya Mississippi. Ate ye Fedallah, eyalabibwa ng’asika ekyuma ekiyitibwa harpooneer, yali asudde ebbali jaketi ye enjeru, n’ayolesa ekifuba kye eky’obwereere ng’ekitundu ky’omubiri gwe kyonna kiri waggulu w’emmundu, nga kisaliddwa bulungi ku bifo ebikyukakyuka eby’amazzi; ate ku nkomerero endala ey’eryato Akabu, ng’omukono gumu, ng’ogw’omuzibizi, ng’asuuliddwa ekitundu emabega mu bbanga, ng’alinga agenda okuziyiza omuze gwonna ogw’okugwa; Akabu yalabiddwako ng’addukanya siteeringi ye nga mu lukumi lw’amaato okukka nga White Whale tennamukutula. Omulundi gumu omukono ogwagoloddwa gwawa entambula ey’enjawulo n’oluvannyuma ne gusigala nga gunywevu, ate nga n’amaloboozi agataano ag’eryato gaalabibwa nga gatuuse ku ntikko mu kiseera kye kimu. Eryato n’abakozi baatuula ku nnyanja nga tebatambula. Amangu ago amaato asatu agaali gasaasaanye emabega gaayimirirako katono nga gagenda. Envubu zaali zituuse mu mubiri mu ngeri etaali ya bulijjo wansi mu bbululu, bwe kityo nga teziwa kabonero konna akategeerekeka ewala akalaga entambula eyo, wadde ng’okuva mu kitundu gye yali ali okumpi Akabu yali akirabye.

“Buli musajja atunuulidde ku mabbali ge!” Starbuck bwe yakaaba. “Ggwe Queequeg, yimirira!”

Ng’asituka mu ngeri ey’obwegendereza ku bbokisi eyali ewaniriddwa mu ngeri ey’enjuyi essatu mu butaasa, ensolo enkambwe yayimirira awo nga yeegolodde, era n’amaaso ag’okwagala ennyo n’atunuulira ekifo we baali basembyeyo okunnyonnyolwa okuyigga. Mu ngeri y’emu ku mugongo ogusukkiridde ogw’eryato nga nalyo lyali liteekeddwa mu njuyi essatu nga likwatagana n’emmundu, Starbuck yennyini yalabibwa mu ngeri ennyogovu era ey’amagezi ng’akwatagana n’okuwuuma okuwuuma kwa chip ye ey’ekyuma ky’emikono, era mu kasirise ng’atunuulira eriiso eddene erya bbululu ery’ennyanja.

Tewali wala nnyo eryato lya Flask nalyo lyali ligalamidde nga liremesezza omukka; omuduumizi waayo ng’ayimiridde mu ngeri ey’obulagajjavu waggulu ku mutwe gw’emmeeri, ekika ky’ekikondo ekigumu nga kisimbye emirandira mu kisenge ky’emmeeri, era ng’asituka ffuuti nga bbiri waggulu w’eddaala ly’ekifo eky’emabega. Kikozesebwa okukwata enkyukakyuka ne layini ya whale. Waggulu waayo si mugazi okusinga engalo y’omusajja, era ng’ayimiridde ku musingi ng’ogwo, Flask yalabika ng’atudde ku mutwe gw’emmeeri ezimu ezaali zibbidde eri bonna okuggyako loole ze. Naye King-Post omuto yali mutono ate nga mumpi, ate mu kiseera kye kimu King-Post omuto yali ajjudde ebirowoozo ebinene era ebiwanvu, ne kiba nti ennyiriri ye eno ey’okusika omuguwa teyamatiza King-Post n’akatono.

“Sisobola kulaba nnyanja ssatu nga zivuddeko; tutunuulire amaaso awo, era ka ngenda ku ekyo.”

Ku kino, Daggoo, ng’omukono gwonna guli ku mmundu okunyweza ekkubo lye, yaserengeta mangu emabega, n’oluvannyuma n’ayimirira n’awaayo ebibegabega bye ebigulumivu n’awaayo ekituuti.

“Good a mast-head nga yonna, ssebo. Onoolinnya?”

“Nti njagala, era neebaza nnyo, munnange omulungi; kyokka nkwagaliza obuwanvu bwa ffuuti amakumi ataano.”

Awo bwe yasimbye ebigere bye bulungi ku bipande bibiri eby’eryato ebikontana, omuddugavu omunene ennyo, ng’afukamidde katono, n’ayanjula engalo ye empanvu eri ekigere kya Flask, n’oluvannyuma n’ateeka omukono gwa Flask ku mutwe gwe ogwali gujjudde eggaali y’omulambo n’amulagira ensulo nga ye kennyini bw’alina okusuula, n’okusuula okumu okw’amagezi, omusajja omuto yakka waggulu era nga nkalu ku bibegabega bye. Era wuuno Flask kati ng’ayimiridde, Daggoo ng’awanise omukono gumu ng’amuwa omuguwa gw’oku kifuba gw’ayinza okwesigamira n’okwenyweza.

Mu kiseera kyonna kiba kya kyewuunyo eri tyro okulaba n’omuze ogw’ekitalo ogw’obukugu obutategeera omuvuzi w’ennyanja whale gw’anaakuuma ng’ayimiridde mu lyato lye, ne bw’aba asuuliddwa ennyanja ezisinga okubeera ez’akajagalalo era ezisalasala. Ekyewuunyisa ennyo okumulaba ng’atudde ku mutwe gwennyini ng’azirika, mu mbeera ng’ezo. Naye okulaba Flask entono ng’esimbye ku Daggoo ennene ennyo kwali kwongera okwewuunyisa; olw’okweyimirizaawo n’obukulu obunnyogovu, obutafaayo, obwangu, obutalowoozebwako, obw’obukambwe, omuddugavu ow’ekitiibwa eri buli muzingo gw’ennyanja mu kukwatagana yayiringisiza ekifaananyi kye ekirungi. Ku mugongo gwe omugazi, Flask ow’enviiri za flaxen yalabika ng’omuzira. Omusitula yali alabika nga wa kitiibwa okusinga omuvuzi. Wadde nga ddala Flask omuto alina obulamu, akajagalalo, akalaga nti alina obulamu, kati n’oluusi yandikoze sitampu n’obutagumiikiriza; naye tewali n’emu yayongeddeko heave mu ngeri eyo gye yawa ekifuba ky’omuddugavu eky’obwakabaka. Bwentyo ndabye Passion ne Vanity nga zikuba sitampu ku nsi enamu ey’amaanyi, naye ensi teyakyusa mayengo gaayo ne sizoni zaayo olw’ekyo.

Mu kiseera kye kimu Stubb, munne owookusatu, teyalyamu lukwe mu ngeri yonna ey’okusaba ewala ng’eyo. Envubu zino zandibadde zikola ekimu ku biwulikika byabwe bulijjo, so si kubbira mu mazzi okumala akaseera olw’okutya kwokka; era bwe kiba bwe kityo, Stubb, nga bwe yali atera okukola mu mbeera ng’ezo, kirabika, yali amaliridde okubudaabuda ekiseera ekizibu ennyo n’ payipu ye. Yagiggye ku bbandi ye ey’enkoofiira, bulijjo gy’agiyambalanga ng’eserengese ng’amaliba. Yagitikka, n’akuba awaka okutikka n’engalo ye ensajja; naye yali tannaba kukoleeza mupiira gwe okuyita mu lupapula lw’omusenyu olukalu olw’omukono gwe, Tashtego, omukubi we ow’engoye, amaaso ge agaali gatunudde mu mpewo ng’emmunyeenye bbiri ezitakyuka, n’agwa mangu ng’ekitangaala okuva mu ndowooza ye ey’okugolola okutuuka ku ntebe ye, ng’akaaba mu bwangu phrensy of hurry, “Wansi, wansi byonna, era muwe ekkubo!—awo we bali!”

Eri omukozi w’ettaka, tewali whale, wadde akabonero konna akalaga nti ennyonyi ekika kya herring, kandibadde kalabika mu kaseera ako; tewali kirala wabula ekitundu ekizibu eky’amazzi amazungu aga kiragala, n’ebiwujjo ebigonvu ebisaasaanidde eby’omukka ebiwuubaala waggulu waago, era nga bifuuwa nnyo okutuuka mu bbanga, ng’ekiwujjo ekitabuddwatabuddwa okuva mu biwujjo ebyeru ebiyiringisibwa. Empewo eyali yeetoolodde yakankana mangu era n’ewunya, nga bwe tuyinza okugamba, ng’empewo eri ku bipande by’ekyuma ebyabuguma ennyo. Wansi w’okuwuuba kuno okw’empewo okuwuubaala n’okuzingulula, n’ekitundu wansi w’oluwuzi lw’amazzi olugonvu, era, ennyanja ekika kya whale zaali ziwuga. Bwe balabibwa nga bukyali ebiraga ebirala byonna, omukka ogw’omukka gwe baafuuwa, gwalabika ng’ababaka baabwe abaali bagenda mu maaso n’ababuuka abaali babuuka ebweru.

Amaato gonna ana kati gaali gagoberera nnyo ekifo ekyo ekimu eky’amazzi n’empewo ebizibu. Naye kyali kya bwenkanya okubasinga; yabuuka ne yeeyongerayo, ng’ekibinja ky’ebiwujjo ebitabulagana nga bisitulibwa wansi mu mugga ogw’amangu okuva ku nsozi.

“Sika, musika, abalenzi bange abalungi,” Starbuck bwe yagamba, mu ngeri eya wansi ennyo naye nga ya maanyi nnyo eri basajja be; ate ng’okutunula okusongovu okunywevu okuva mu maaso ge kwadduka butereevu mu maaso g’obutaasa, kumpi nga kulabika ng’empiso bbiri ezirabika mu kkampasi bbiri eza binnacle ezitasobya. Kyokka, teyayogera bingi eri abakozi be, era n’abakozi be tebaamugamba kintu kyonna. Obusirise bw’eryato bwokka mu biseera ebitali bimu bwafumibwa mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa olw’ekimu ku biwuubaalo bye eby’enjawulo, kati nga bikambwe olw’okulagira, kati nga bigonvu olw’okwegayirira.

Nga kya njawulo nnyo akatono aka King-Post akawulikika. “Muyimba era mwogere ekintu, my hearties. Muwuuma era musika, okubwatuka kwange! Beach me, beach me ku migongo gyabwe emiddugavu, abalenzi; ekyo kyokka munkolere, era nja kubassa omukono ku nnimiro yange ey’Olusuku lw’Emizabbibu olwa Maliza, abalenzi; omuli omukyala n’abaana, abalenzi. Ngalamire ku —ngalamiza! Ayi Mukama, Mukama! naye nja kugenda nnyo, nga ntunula eddalu! Okulaba! laba amazzi ago amazungu!” Era bwe yaleekaana, n’aggya enkoofiira ye ku mutwe gwe, n’agiteekako sitampu waggulu ne wansi; oluvannyuma n’agisitula, n’agipepeya ewala ku nnyanja; era okukkakkana ng’agwa mu kukuza n’okubbira mu mugongo gw’eryato ng’omwana gw’endogoyi ogugwa eddalu okuva mu kibira.

“Laba chap oyo kati,” Stubb bwe yasika mu ngeri ey’obufirosoofo, nga, ne payipu ye ennyimpi etali ya musana, ng’asimbiddwa mu makanika wakati w’amannyo ge, mu bbanga ettono, n’agoberera —“Alina ebizimba, Flask by’alina. Kituukira ddala? yee, muwe fits —ekyo kye kigambo kyennyini —pitch fits into 'em. Mu ssanyu, mu ssanyu, emitima-emiramu. Puddingi y’ekyeggulo, omanyi;—merry’s the word. Sika, babes —sika, abayonka —sika, byonna. Naye sitaani ki gwe mwanguwa? Mpola, mpola, era mu ngeri etakyukakyuka, basajja bange. Sika yokka, era sigala ng’osika; tewali kisingako awo. Yatika emigongo gyo gyonna, era oluma ebiso byo mu bibiri —ekyo kyokka. Kitwale — lwaki temukitwala nga kyangu, ngamba, ne mukutula ebibumba byammwe byonna n’amawuggwe!”

Naye kye kyali Akabu oyo atategeerekeka kye yagamba ekibinja kye ekyo ekya kyenvu ng’engo —bino bye bigambo ebisinga okulekebwa wano; kubanga mubeera wansi w’ekitangaala eky’omukisa eky’ensi y’enjiri. Shark ezitakkiriza zokka eziri mu nnyanja ez’obuvumu ze ziyinza okuwuliriza ebigambo ng’ebyo, bwe, ng’alina ekibatu eky’omuyaga, n’amaaso ag’ettemu emmyuufu, n’emimwa egyasiigibwako ekikuta, Akabu n’abuuka ng’agoberera omuyiggo gwe.

Mu kiseera ekyo, amaato gonna ne gakutuka. Ebigambo ebitongole ebiddiŋŋanwa Flask bye yayogera ku “whale eyo,” nga bwe yayita ekisolo ekitali kya bulijjo kye yalangirira nti kyali kiwuniikiriza obutasalako obutasalako bw’eryato lye n’omukira gwakyo —ebigambo bino eby’oluusi byali bitangaavu nnyo era nga bifaanana ng’obulamu, ne kiba nti byandireese omu oba babiri ku basajja be okunyaga okutunula okw’entiisa ku kibegabega. Naye kino kyali kimenya obufuzi bwonna; kubanga abavuzi b'amaato banaaggyayo amaaso gaabwe, ne bakuba ekikuta mu bulago bwabwe; enkozesa nga ziraga nti zirina okuba nga tezirina bitundu by’omubiri wabula amatu, era nga tezirina bitundu bya mikono wabula emikono, mu biseera bino ebikulu.

Kyali kifaananyi ekijjudde okwewuunya okw’amangu n’okutya! Ebizimba ebinene eby’ennyanja eyinza byonna; okuwuluguma okw’amaanyi, okw’ebituli kwe baakola, nga bwe beekulukuunya ku bifo omunaana eby’emmundu, ng’ebbakuli ennene ennyo mu langi ya bowling-green etaliiko kkomo; obulumi obwali bumpimpi obwali buyimiriziddwa obw’eryato, nga bwe bwali bugenda okuwuuba okumala akaseera katono ku mabbali g’amayengo agaali gasongovu agalinga ekiso, kumpi nga galabika ng’agatiisa okulisalako ebitundu bibiri; okunnyika okw’amaanyi okw’amangu mu biwonvu n’ebinnya ebirimu amazzi; ebisiba ebisongovu n’ebikondo okusobola okufuna entikko y’olusozi olulala; okusereba okuwanvuwa omutwe, okulinga sled wansi ku ludda lwayo olulala;—bino byonna, n’okukaaba kw’abakubi b’emitwe n’abavubi, n’okuwuuma okukankana kw’abavuzi b’amaato, n’okulaba okwewuunyisa okwa Pequod ey’amasanga ng’esitula wansi ku maato gaayo ng’erina amaato agagoloddwa, ng’enkoko ey’omu nsiko oluvannyuma lw’abaana baayo abakuba enduulu;—bino byonna byali bicamula.

Si muyimbi omubisi, ng’atambula okuva mu kifuba kya mukyala we n’ayingira mu bbugumu ly’omusujja ery’olutalo lwe olwasooka; si muzimu gw’omufu nga gusisinkana ekifaananyi ekisookera ddala ekitali kimanyiddwa mu nsi endala;—tewali n’emu ku zino ayinza kuwulira nneewulira za kitalo era ez’amaanyi okusinga omusajja oyo, omulundi gwe ogusooka yeesanga ng’asika mu nkulungo ewunyiriza, ewunyiriza ey’ennyanja ekika kya sperm whale eyiggibwa.

Amazzi amazungu agaali gazina agaakolebwanga okuyigga kati gaali geeyongera okulabika, olw’ekizikiza ekyeyongera eky’ebisiikirize by’ebire ebitali bimu ebyasuulibwa ku nnyanja. Ebiwujjo by’omukka tebyakyatabula, wabula byalengejja buli wamu okudda ku ddyo ne ku kkono; ennyanja ekika kya whale zaali zirabika nga zaawulamu ebizuukuka byabwe. Amaato gaayongera okusimbulwa; Starbuck nga ewa chase eri whale ssatu ezidduka nga zifudde okugenda mu leeward. Kati emmeeri yaffe yali esimbye, era, olw’empewo eyali ekyali egenda waggulu, twafubutuka ne tugenda; eryato nga ligenda n’eddalu nnyo nga liyita mu mazzi, ne kiba nti ebiwujjo ebiyitibwa lee oars byali tebisobola kukolebwa mangu nnyo okusobola okuwona okukutulwa okuva mu bizibiti by’ennyiriri.

Mu bbanga ttono twali tudduka mu lugoye olugazi olwali lujjudde enfuufu; wadde emmeeri wadde eryato okulabibwa.

“Muwe ekkubo, abasajja,” Starbuck bwe yayomba, ng’ayongera okusika emabega w’ekipande ky’emmeeri ye; “waliwo obudde okutta ekyennyanja n’okutuusa kati ng’omuyaga tegunnatuuka. Waliwo amazzi amazungu nate! —okumpi ne! Sepulingi!"

Amangu ddala nga wayiseewo akaseera katono, emiranga ebiri egy’amangu egy’omuddiring’anwa ku buli ludda lwaffe gyali giraga nti amaato amalala gaali gafunye sipiidi; naye tebaawuliddwa bulungi, Starbuck bwe yagamba nti: “Yimiridde!” era Queequeg, ng’akutte engoye, n’abuuka n’ayimirira.

Wadde nga mu kiseera ekyo tewali n’omu ku bavuzi b’amaato yali ayolekedde akabi ak’obulamu n’okufa akali kumpi nnyo nabo mu maaso, naye ng’amaaso gaabwe gatunudde mu maaso ag’amaanyi aga munne eyali emabega w’eryato, baali bakimanyi nti akaseera akaali kaali kagenda okutuuka; nabo baawulira eddoboozi eddene ennyo ery’okuwuubaala ng’enjovu amakumi ataano eziwuubaala mu kasasiro waabwe. Mu kiseera ekyo eryato lyali likyawuuma mu nfuufu, ng’amayengo gatwetooloola era nga gawuuma ng’ebikonge ebisimbye eby’emisota egyali gisunguwadde.

“Eyo ye hump ye. Eyo , eyo , muwe!” Starbuck bwe yayomba.

Eddoboozi ettono eryafubutuka lyabuuka okuva mu lyato; yali kyuma ekyali kifumbiddwa mu Queequeg. Awo byonna mu kavuyo kamu aka welded ne wajja okusika okutalabika okuva emabega, ate mu maaso eryato lyalabika nga likuba ku ledge; emmeeri yagwa n’ebwatuka; omuyaga gw’omukka ogwokya gwakubwa okumpi awo; ekintu kyayiringisibwa ne kigwa nga musisi wansi waffe. Abakozi bonna baali bazibye ekitundu nga bwe basuulibwa helter-skelter mu kizigo ekyeru ekifuuwa ekizimbulukusa eky’omuyaga. Squall, whale, ne harpoon byonna byali bitabuddwa wamu; era ennyanja ekika kya whale, eyabadde erundibwa ekyuma kyokka, yasimattuse.

Wadde ng’eryato lyali litonnya ddala, kumpi lyali terina bulabe bwonna. Nga tuwuga okwetooloola twasitula ebiwujjo ebitengejja, ne tubikuba emiggo emitala w’emmundu, ne tugwa ne tudda mu bifo byaffe. Eyo twatuula okutuuka ku maviivi gaffe mu nnyanja, ng’amazzi gabikka buli mbavu n’olubaawo, ne kiba nti eri amaaso gaffe agatunudde wansi emmeeri ey’emikono eyali ewaniriddwa yalabika ng’eryato ery’amasanga eryatukula okuva wansi mu nnyanja.

Empewo yeeyongera n’efuuka eddoboozi ery’omwanguka; amayengo gaamenya ebisiba byabwe wamu; omuyaga gwonna gwawuluguma, ne gufuumuuka, era ne guwuuma nga gutwetooloola ng’omuliro omweru ku kiwonvu, nga mu byo, nga tetunnayokebwa, twali twokya; abatafa mu nsaya zino ez’okufa! Mu bwereere twatendereza amaato amalala; nga kwotadde n’okuwuluguma okutuuka ku amanda amalamu wansi mu ssigiri y’ekikoomi ekiyaka ng’omuzira amaato ago mu kibuyaga ago. Mu kiseera ekyo ekiwujjo ekivuga, rack, n’enfuufu, byagenda byeyongera okuddugala olw’ebisiikirize by’ekiro; tewali kabonero ka mmeeri kaali kasobola kulabibwa. Ennyanja eyali egenda ekula yagaana okugezaako kwonna okuggya eryato eryo. Ebiwujjo byali tebirina mugaso ng’ebiwujjo, nga kati bikola ofiisi y’ebikuuma obulamu. Kale, nga basala lashing y’ekibbo ky’omupiira ekiziyiza amazzi, oluvannyuma lw’okulemererwa kungi Starbuck yayiiya okukuma omuliro mu ttaala mu ttaala; oluvannyuma ng’agigolola ku kikondo kya waif, n’agikwasa Queequeg ng’omusitula bendera w’essuubi lino eryali liweddewo. N’olwekyo, eyo gye yatuula, ng’akutte omumuli ogwo ogw’obusiru mu mutima gw’okuggwaamu okwo okw’amaanyi. N’olwekyo, eyo gye yatuula, akabonero n’akabonero k’omusajja atalina kukkiriza, ng’akutte essuubi nga talina ssuubi wakati mu kuggwaamu essuubi.

Nga tufuuse nnyogovu, nga tutonnya, era nga tukankana nga nnyogovu, nga tuggwaamu essuubi olw’emmeeri oba eryato, twasitula amaaso gaffe ng’obudde bukya. Enfuufu yali ekyasaasaana ku nnyanja, ettaala etaliimu kintu kyonna yali egalamidde nga efuukuuse wansi mu lyato. Amangu ago Queequeg yatandika okuyimirira, ng’asika omukono gwe mu kutu kwe. Ffenna twawulira okuwuuma okutono, ng’okuwuuma kw’emiguwa n’oluggya okutuusa kati ebyazibiddwa omuyaga. Eddoboozi lyagenda lyeyongera okusemberera; enfuufu enzito zaali zaawuddwamu mu ngeri etali ya maanyi nnyo olw’ekifaananyi ekinene ennyo ekitali kitegeerekeka. Olw’okutya, ffenna twabuuka mu nnyanja ng’emmeeri ku nkomerero etunudde mu maaso, ng’etutuukako wansi mu bbanga eritasukka nnyo buwanvu bwayo.

Nga tulengejja ku mayengo twalaba eryato eryali lisuuliddwa, nga okumala akaseera kamu liwuubaala era ne liyaka wansi w’obutaasa bw’emmeeri ng’ekikuta ku musingi gw’ekizimba ky’amaaso; era awo ekisenge ekinene ennyo ne kikyekulukuunyaako, ne kitaddamu kulabibwa okutuusa lwe kyava waggulu nga kiwuguka emabega. Nate twawuga okugifuna, ennyanja ne tugikuba, era ku nkomerero ne tusitulwa ne tutuuka bulungi ku lyato. Ere omuyaga nga gusemberera, amaato amalala gaali gavudde ku byennyanja gaago ne gadda ku mmeeri mu budde obulungi. Emmeeri yali etuwaddeyo, naye yali ekyatambula, bwe kiba nti eyinza okwaka ku kabonero akalaga nti tugenda kuzikirizibwa, — emmeeri oba ekikondo ky’effumu.





ESSUULA 49. Ensigo ya Hyena.

Waliwo ebiseera n’emikolo egimu egy’ekyewuunyo mu nsonga eno ey’ekyewuunyo etabuddwamu gye tuyita obulamu omuntu bw’atwala obutonde buno bwonna ng’okusaaga okunene ennyo okw’omugaso, wadde ng’amagezi gaakyo naye ategeera bulungi, era n’okusinga okuteebereza nti essanyu eryo si ku muwendo gwa muntu yenna wabula ye obwa nannyini. Kyokka, tewali kintu kyonna kimalamu maanyi, era tewali kirabika nga kya mugaso. Akuba wansi ebibaddewo byonna, enzikiriza zonna, n’enzikiriza, n’okusikiriza, ebintu byonna ebikalu ebirabika n’ebitalabika, nga tofuddeeyo ku ngeri ya knobby; ng’ensowera ey’okugaaya emmere ey’amaanyi bw’emira amasasi n’amayinja ag’emmundu. Era ku bizibu ebitonotono n’okweraliikirira, essuubi ly’akatyabaga ak’amangu, akabi k’obulamu n’ekitundu ky’omubiri; bino byonna, n’okufa kwennyini, bimulabika ng’okukuba kwokka okw’obukuusa, okw’empisa ennungi, n’ebikonde eby’essanyu mu ludda ebiweebwa omujoozi omukadde atalabika era atavunaanyizibwa. Oyo odd sort of wayward mood gwe njogerako, ejja ku musajja mu kiseera ekimu kyokka eky’ekibonyoobonyo ekiyitiridde; kijja wakati ddala mu bunyiikivu bwe, ne kiba nti ekyo ekyali kimaze okulabika ng’ekintu ekisinga obukulu gy’ali, kati kirabika ng’ekitundu kyokka eky’okusaaga okwa bulijjo. Tewali kiringa kabi akali mu kuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale okuzaala ekika kino eky’eddembe era eky’angu eky’obufirosoofo obw’amagezi, obw’okuggwaamu essuubi; era nayo kati nnatwala olugendo luno lwonna olwa Pequod, n’ennyonyi ennene White Whale ng’ekigendererwa kyayo.

“Queequeg,” bwe nnagamba, bwe baali bansikambula, omusajja asembayo, okutuuka ku ddeeke, era nga nkyakankana mu jaketi yange okusuula amazzi; “Queequeg, mukwano gwange omulungi, ekintu eky’ekika kino kitera okubaawo?” Nga talina nnyo nneewulira, wadde nga yannyika nga nze, yampa okutegeera nti ebintu ng’ebyo byatera okubaawo.

“Mw. Stubb,” bwe nnagamba nga nkyukidde omusaanira oyo, eyali asibye obukomo mu jaketi ye ey’amafuta, kati yali afuuwa payipu ye mu nkuba mu bukkakkamu; “Mw. Stubb, ndowooza mpulidde ng’ogamba nti mu bavubi bonna b’obadde osanga, munnaffe omukulu, Mwami Starbuck, y’asinga okwegendereza era ow’amagezi. Ndowooza olwo, nti okugenda nga oli mugejjo ku nnyanja ekika kya whale ebuuka ng’ekyuma kyo kiteekeddwa mu kibuyaga ow’ekifu, bwe buwanvu bw’okutegeera kw’omuvuzi w’ennyanja ekika kya whale?”

"Okuba n'obukakafu. Nserengese okunoonya ennyanja ekika kya whale okuva ku mmeeri eyali ekulukuta mu kibuyaga ow’amaanyi ku nnyanja Cape Horn.”

“Mw. Flask,” bwe nnagamba nga nkyuka ne ntunuulira King-Post omuto, eyali ayimiridde okumpi awo; “ggwe olina obumanyirivu mu bintu bino, nange si bwe kiri. Onoombuulira oba tteeka eritakyusibwa mu nvuba eno, Mwami Flask, omuvubi okumenya omugongo gwe yeesika emabega-mu maaso mu nvuba z’okufa?”

“Ekyo ekitono tosobola kukikyusa?” Flask bwe yategeezezza. “Yee, eryo lye tteeka. Njagala okulaba abakozi b’eryato nga badda emabega amazzi okutuuka ku ffeesi ya whale foremost. Ha, ha! whale yandibawadde squint for squint, mind that!”

Wano olwo, okuva ku bajulizi basatu abatalina kyekubiira, nnalina sitatimenti mu bugenderevu ku musango gwonna. N’olwekyo, okulowooza nti omuyaga n’okukulugguka mu mazzi n’ebifo ebiyitibwa bivouacks ebiddirira ku buziba, byali bintu bya bulijjo mu bulamu obw’ekika kino; okulowooza nti mu kaseera akazibu ennyo ak’okugenda ku nnyanja ekika kya whale nnina okulekulira obulamu bwange mu mikono gy’oyo eyavuga eryato —emirundi mingi munnange mu kaseera ako kennyini ali mu bwangu bwe ku ssa ly’okusaanyawo emmeeri n’erye okukuba sitampu mu ngeri ey’ekibogwe; okulowooza nti akatyabaga ak’enjawulo eri eryato lyaffe eryennyini okusinga lyali lya kuteekebwa ku Starbuck okuvuga ku nnyanja ye kumpi mu mannyo g’omuyaga, era nga tulowooza nti Starbuck, wadde kyali kityo, yali amanyiddwa nnyo olw’okufaayo kwe okw’amaanyi mu by’obuvubi; okusinziira ku kuba nti nnali wa lyato lino erya Starbuck erya magezi eritali lya bulijjo; era okusembayo nga ndowooza mu kuyigga kwa sitaani kwe nnali nnyigiddemu, nga nkwata ku White Whale: okutwala ebintu byonna awamu, ngamba, nnalowooza nti nnyinza okugenda wansi ne nkola ebbago eritali ddene ery’ekiraamo kyange. “Queequeg,” bwe nnagamba, “jjangu, ojja kuba munnamateeka wange, omutuukiriza, era omusika wange.”

Kiyinza okulabika ng’ekyewuunyisa nti mu basajja bonna abalunnyanja balina okuba nga bakola ku biraamo byabwe ebisembayo n’endagaano zaabwe, naye mu nsi tewali bantu basinga kwagala kuwugula okwo. Guno gwali mulundi gwakuna mu bulamu bwange obw’oku nnyanja nga nkola ekintu kye kimu. Omukolo bwe gwaggwa ku mukolo guno, nnawulira nga nnyangu nnyo; ejjinja lyayiringisibwa okuva ku mutima gwange. N’ekirala, ennaku zonna ze nsaanidde okubeera kati zandibadde nnungi ng’ennaku Lazaalo gye yabeeranga oluvannyuma lw’okuzuukira kwe; amagoba amayonjo ag’okugattako ag’emyezi oba wiiki nnyingi nnyo nga bwe kiyinza okuba. Nze kennyini nnasimattuse; okufa kwange n’okuziika kwange byasibirwa mu kifuba kyange. Natunula okwetooloola mu mirembe era nga ndi mumativu, ng’omuzimu omusirise ogulina omuntu ow’omunda omuyonjo ogutudde munda mu bbaala z’ekisenge ky’amaka ekirabika obulungi.

Kati olwo, bwe nnalowooza, nga simanyi nga nzinga emikono gya ffulaayi yange, wano agenda ku dive ennyogovu, ekuŋŋaanyiziddwa ku kufa n’okuzikirizibwa, era sitaani n’aleeta eky’emabega.





ESSUULA 50. Eryato lya Akabu n’abakozi baayo. Fedallah.

“Ani yandikirowoozezzaako, Flask!” Stubb bwe yakaaba; “singa nnalina ekigere kimu kyokka tewandikwatidde mu lyato, okuggyako mpozzi okuyimiriza ekituli kya pulagi n’ekigere kyange eky’embaawo. Oh! musajja mukulu wa kitalo!”

“Silowooza nti kyewuunyisa nnyo, oluvannyuma lw’ebyo byonna, ku akawunti eyo,” Flask bwe yategeezezza. “Singa okugulu kwe kwali kuvudde ku kisambi, kati, kyandibadde kintu kya njawulo. Ekyo kyandimulemaza; Naye alina okugulu okumu, n’ekitundu ekirungi ku kkono omulala, omanyi.”

“Ekyo sikimanyi, musajja wange omuto; Nze n’okutuusa kati simulabangako ng’afukamira.”

Mu bantu abamanyi ennyanja ekika kya whale kibadde kitera okukaayanirwa oba, okusinziira ku bukulu obusinga obunene obw’obulamu bwe eri obuwanguzi bw’olugendo, kituufu kapiteeni avuba ennyanja ekika kya whale okuteeka obulamu obwo mu matigga mu kabi ak’amaanyi ak’okuyigga. Kale abaserikale ba Tamerlane batera okukaayana nga amaziga gamuyitamu, oba obulamu bwe obwo obw’omuwendo ennyo bwalina okutwalibwa mu lutalo olusinga obunene.

Naye ne Akabu ekibuuzo kyatwala engeri ekyusiddwa. Okulowooza nti omuntu alina amagulu abiri ali but a hobbling wight mu biseera byonna eby’akabi; okulowooza nti okuyigga ennyanja ekika kya whale bulijjo kubeera wansi w’ebizibu ebinene era eby’ekitalo; nti buli kaseera ssekinnoomu, ddala, olwo kalimu akabi; Mu mbeera zino kiba kya magezi omusajja yenna alema okuyingira mu lyato erya whale-boat mu kuyigga? Nga ekintu eky’awamu, bannannyini Pequod ab’awamu bateekwa okuba nga baali balowooza bulungi nti si bwe kiri.

Akabu yali akimanyi bulungi nti wadde nga mikwano gye awaka tebandirowoozezza kitono ku kuyingira kwe mu lyato mu nkyukakyuka ezimu ezitali za bulabe bwe zigeraageranyizibwa ku kuyigga, olw’okwagala okubeera okumpi n’ekifo we kikolebwa n’okuwa ebiragiro bye mu buntu, naye olw’okuba Kapiteeni Akabu alina a eryato mu butuufu lyamugabibwa ng’omukulu wa bulijjo mu kuyigga —okusinga byonna Kapiteeni Akabu okuweebwa abasajja bataano ab’enjawulo, ng’abakozi b’eryato eryo lye limu, yali akimanyi bulungi nti okwegulumiza okw’obugabi bwe kutyo tekwayingirangako mu mitwe gya bannannyini lyato lya Pequod. N’olwekyo yali tannabasaba bakozi ba lyato, era mu ngeri yonna yali talaga kwegomba kwe ku mutwe ogwo. Wadde kyali kityo yali akoze eby’ekyama ebibye ku nsonga ezo zonna. Okutuusa Cabaco lwe yazuula ekyafulumizibwa, abalunnyanja baali tebakiraba nnyo, wadde nga nga bakakasa ddi, oluvannyuma lw’okumala akaseera katono nga tebali ku mwalo, emikono gyonna gyali gimalirizza bizinensi ey’ennono ey’okuteeka amaato g’ennyanja ekika kya whale okusobola okuweereza; bwe yamala ekiseera oluvannyuma lwa kino Akabu kati n’oluvannyuma yasanga nga yeenyiiza mu nsonga y’okukola thole-pins n’emikono gye ku ekyo ekyali kilowoozebwa okuba ekimu ku maato aga sipeeya, era n’atuuka n’okusala mu ngeri ey’okusaba obusike obutono obw’embaawo, nga bwe layini is running out are pinned over the groove in the bow: nga bino byonna byatunuulirwa mu ye, n’okusingira ddala solicitude ye mu kuba n’ekkanzu ey’okubikka ey’enjawulo wansi w’eryato, ng’alinga alifuula okugumira obulungi puleesa ensongovu ey’eye ekitundu ky’omubiri eky’amasanga; era n’okweraliikirira kwe yalaga mu kubumba ddala olubaawo lw’ekisambi, oba clumsy cleat, nga bwe kiyitibwa oluusi, ekitundu ekiwanvu mu butaasa bw’eryato olw’okunyweza okugulu mu kudduka oba okufumita ennyanja ekika kya whale; bwe kyatunuulirwa emirundi gye yayimiriranga mu lyato eryo ng’okugulu kwe okw’okwetongola kunywedde mu kifo ekiwanvu ekyekulungirivu mu cleat, era ng’ekisero ky’omubazzi kigogola katono wano n’akigolola katono awo; ebintu bino byonna, ngamba, byali bizuukusizza okwagala kungi n’okwegomba okumanya mu kiseera ekyo. Naye kumpi buli omu yalowooza nti okufaayo kuno okw’enjawulo okw’okwetegeka mu Akabu kulina okuba nga kulina ekigendererwa ky’okugoba Moby Dick okw’enkomeredde kwokka; kubanga yali yalaga dda ekigendererwa kye eky’okuyigga ekisolo ekyo ekifa mu buntu. But such a supposition did by no means involve the remotest suspicion as to any boat's crew being assigned to that boat.

Now, with the subordinate phantoms, what wonder remained soon waned away; for in a whaler wonders soon wane. Besides, now and then such unaccountable odds and ends of strange nations come up from the unknown nooks and ash-holes of the earth to man these floating outlaws of whalers; and the ships themselves often pick up such queer castaway creatures found tossing about the open sea on planks, bits of wreck, oars, whaleboats, canoes, blown-off Japanese junks, and what not; that Beelzebub himself might climb up the side and step down into the cabin to chat with the captain, and it would not create any unsubduable excitement in the forecastle.

But be all this as it may, certain it is that while the subordinate phantoms soon found their place among the crew, though still as it were somehow distinct from them, yet that hair-turbaned Fedallah remained a muffled mystery to the last. Whence he came in a mannerly world like this, by what sort of unaccountable tie he soon evinced himself to be linked with Ahab's peculiar fortunes; nay, so far as to have some sort of a half-hinted influence; Heaven knows, but it might have been even authority over him; all this none knew. But one cannot sustain an indifferent air concerning Fedallah. He was such a creature as civilized, domestic people in the temperate zone only see in their dreams, and that but dimly; but the like of whom now and then glide among the unchanging Asiatic communities, especially the Oriental isles to the east of the continent—those insulated, immemorial, unalterable countries, which even in these modern days still preserve much of the ghostly aboriginalness of earth's primal generations, when the memory of the first man was a distinct recollection, and all men his descendants, unknowing whence he came, eyed each other as real phantoms, and asked of the sun and the moon why they were created and to what end; when though, according to Genesis, the angels indeed consorted with the daughters of men, the devils also, add the uncanonical Rabbins, indulged in mundane amours.





CHAPTER 51. The Spirit-Spout.

Days, weeks passed, and under easy sail, the ivory Pequod had slowly swept across four several cruising-grounds; that off the Azores; off the Cape de Verdes; on the Plate (so called), being off the mouth of the Rio de la Plata; and the Carrol Ground, an unstaked, watery locality, southerly from St. Helena.

It was while gliding through these latter waters that one serene and moonlight night, when all the waves rolled by like scrolls of silver; and, by their soft, suffusing seethings, made what seemed a silvery silence, not a solitude; on such a silent night a silvery jet was seen far in advance of the white bubbles at the bow. Lit up by the moon, it looked celestial; seemed some plumed and glittering god uprising from the sea. Fedallah first descried this jet. For of these moonlight nights, it was his wont to mount to the main-mast head, and stand a look-out there, with the same precision as if it had been day. And yet, though herds of whales were seen by night, not one whaleman in a hundred would venture a lowering for them. You may think with what emotions, then, the seamen beheld this old Oriental perched aloft at such unusual hours; his turban and the moon, companions in one sky. But when, after spending his uniform interval there for several successive nights without uttering a single sound; when, after all this silence, his unearthly voice was heard announcing that silvery, moon-lit jet, every reclining mariner started to his feet as if some winged spirit had lighted in the rigging, and hailed the mortal crew. “There she blows!” Had the trump of judgment blown, they could not have quivered more; yet still they felt no terror; rather pleasure. For though it was a most unwonted hour, yet so impressive was the cry, and so deliriously exciting, that almost every soul on board instinctively desired a lowering.

Walking the deck with quick, side-lunging strides, Ahab commanded the t'gallant sails and royals to be set, and every stunsail spread. The best man in the ship must take the helm. Then, with every mast-head manned, the piled-up craft rolled down before the wind. The strange, upheaving, lifting tendency of the taffrail breeze filling the hollows of so many sails, made the buoyant, hovering deck to feel like air beneath the feet; while still she rushed along, as if two antagonistic influences were struggling in her—one to mount direct to heaven, the other to drive yawingly to some horizontal goal. And had you watched Ahab's face that night, you would have thought that in him also two different things were warring. While his one live leg made lively echoes along the deck, every stroke of his dead limb sounded like a coffin-tap. On life and death this old man walked. But though the ship so swiftly sped, and though from every eye, like arrows, the eager glances shot, yet the silvery jet was no more seen that night. Every sailor swore he saw it once, but not a second time.

This midnight-spout had almost grown a forgotten thing, when, some days after, lo! at the same silent hour, it was again announced: again it was descried by all; but upon making sail to overtake it, once more it disappeared as if it had never been. And so it served us night after night, till no one heeded it but to wonder at it. Mysteriously jetted into the clear moonlight, or starlight, as the case might be; disappearing again for one whole day, or two days, or three; and somehow seeming at every distinct repetition to be advancing still further and further in our van, this solitary jet seemed for ever alluring us on.

Nor with the immemorial superstition of their race, and in accordance with the preternaturalness, as it seemed, which in many things invested the Pequod, were there wanting some of the seamen who swore that whenever and wherever descried; at however remote times, or in however far apart latitudes and longitudes, that unnearable spout was cast by one self-same whale; and that whale, Moby Dick. For a time, there reigned, too, a sense of peculiar dread at this flitting apparition, as if it were treacherously beckoning us on and on, in order that the monster might turn round upon us, and rend us at last in the remotest and most savage seas.

These temporary apprehensions, so vague but so awful, derived a wondrous potency from the contrasting serenity of the weather, in which, beneath all its blue blandness, some thought there lurked a devilish charm, as for days and days we voyaged along, through seas so wearily, lonesomely mild, that all space, in repugnance to our vengeful errand, seemed vacating itself of life before our urn-like prow.

But, at last, when turning to the eastward, the Cape winds began howling around us, and we rose and fell upon the long, troubled seas that are there; when the ivory-tusked Pequod sharply bowed to the blast, and gored the dark waves in her madness, till, like showers of silver chips, the foam-flakes flew over her bulwarks; then all this desolate vacuity of life went away, but gave place to sights more dismal than before.

Close to our bows, strange forms in the water darted hither and thither before us; while thick in our rear flew the inscrutable sea-ravens. And every morning, perched on our stays, rows of these birds were seen; and spite of our hootings, for a long time obstinately clung to the hemp, as though they deemed our ship some drifting, uninhabited craft; a thing appointed to desolation, and therefore fit roosting-place for their homeless selves. And heaved and heaved, still unrestingly heaved the black sea, as if its vast tides were a conscience; and the great mundane soul were in anguish and remorse for the long sin and suffering it had bred.

Cape of Good Hope, do they call ye? Rather Cape Tormentoso, as called of yore; for long allured by the perfidious silences that before had attended us, we found ourselves launched into this tormented sea, where guilty beings transformed into those fowls and these fish, seemed condemned to swim on everlastingly without any haven in store, or beat that black air without any horizon. But calm, snow-white, and unvarying; still directing its fountain of feathers to the sky; still beckoning us on from before, the solitary jet would at times be descried.

During all this blackness of the elements, Ahab, though assuming for the time the almost continual command of the drenched and dangerous deck, manifested the gloomiest reserve; and more seldom than ever addressed his mates. In tempestuous times like these, after everything above and aloft has been secured, nothing more can be done but passively to await the issue of the gale. Then Captain and crew become practical fatalists. So, with his ivory leg inserted into its accustomed hole, and with one hand firmly grasping a shroud, Ahab for hours and hours would stand gazing dead to windward, while an occasional squall of sleet or snow would all but congeal his very eyelashes together. Meantime, the crew driven from the forward part of the ship by the perilous seas that burstingly broke over its bows, stood in a line along the bulwarks in the waist; and the better to guard against the leaping waves, each man had slipped himself into a sort of bowline secured to the rail, in which he swung as in a loosened belt. Few or no words were spoken; and the silent ship, as if manned by painted sailors in wax, day after day tore on through all the swift madness and gladness of the demoniac waves. By night the same muteness of humanity before the shrieks of the ocean prevailed; still in silence the men swung in the bowlines; still wordless Ahab stood up to the blast. Even when wearied nature seemed demanding repose he would not seek that repose in his hammock. Never could Starbuck forget the old man's aspect, when one night going down into the cabin to mark how the barometer stood, he saw him with closed eyes sitting straight in his floor-screwed chair; the rain and half-melted sleet of the storm from which he had some time before emerged, still slowly dripping from the unremoved hat and coat. On the table beside him lay unrolled one of those charts of tides and currents which have previously been spoken of. His lantern swung from his tightly clenched hand. Though the body was erect, the head was thrown back so that the closed eyes were pointed towards the needle of the tell-tale that swung from a beam in the ceiling.*

*The cabin-compass is called the tell-tale, because without going to the compass at the helm, the Captain, while below, can inform himself of the course of the ship.

Terrible old man! thought Starbuck with a shudder, sleeping in this gale, still thou steadfastly eyest thy purpose.





CHAPTER 52. The Albatross.

South-eastward from the Cape, off the distant Crozetts, a good cruising ground for Right Whalemen, a sail loomed ahead, the Goney (Albatross) by name. As she slowly drew nigh, from my lofty perch at the fore-mast-head, I had a good view of that sight so remarkable to a tyro in the far ocean fisheries—a whaler at sea, and long absent from home.

As if the waves had been fullers, this craft was bleached like the skeleton of a stranded walrus. All down her sides, this spectral appearance was traced with long channels of reddened rust, while all her spars and her rigging were like the thick branches of trees furred over with hoar-frost. Only her lower sails were set. A wild sight it was to see her long-bearded look-outs at those three mast-heads. They seemed clad in the skins of beasts, so torn and bepatched the raiment that had survived nearly four years of cruising. Standing in iron hoops nailed to the mast, they swayed and swung over a fathomless sea; and though, when the ship slowly glided close under our stern, we six men in the air came so nigh to each other that we might almost have leaped from the mast-heads of one ship to those of the other; yet, those forlorn-looking fishermen, mildly eyeing us as they passed, said not one word to our own look-outs, while the quarter-deck hail was being heard from below.

“Ship ahoy! Have ye seen the White Whale?”

But as the strange captain, leaning over the pallid bulwarks, was in the act of putting his trumpet to his mouth, it somehow fell from his hand into the sea; and the wind now rising amain, he in vain strove to make himself heard without it. Meantime his ship was still increasing the distance between. While in various silent ways the seamen of the Pequod were evincing their observance of this ominous incident at the first mere mention of the White Whale's name to another ship, Ahab for a moment paused; it almost seemed as though he would have lowered a boat to board the stranger, had not the threatening wind forbade. But taking advantage of his windward position, he again seized his trumpet, and knowing by her aspect that the stranger vessel was a Nantucketer and shortly bound home, he loudly hailed—“Ahoy there! This is the Pequod, bound round the world! Tell them to address all future letters to the Pacific ocean! and this time three years, if I am not at home, tell them to address them to ——”

At that moment the two wakes were fairly crossed, and instantly, then, in accordance with their singular ways, shoals of small harmless fish, that for some days before had been placidly swimming by our side, darted away with what seemed shuddering fins, and ranged themselves fore and aft with the stranger's flanks. Though in the course of his continual voyagings Ahab must often before have noticed a similar sight, yet, to any monomaniac man, the veriest trifles capriciously carry meanings.

“Swim away from me, do ye?” murmured Ahab, gazing over into the water. There seemed but little in the words, but the tone conveyed more of deep helpless sadness than the insane old man had ever before evinced. But turning to the steersman, who thus far had been holding the ship in the wind to diminish her headway, he cried out in his old lion voice,—“Up helm! Keep her off round the world!”

Round the world! There is much in that sound to inspire proud feelings; but whereto does all that circumnavigation conduct? Only through numberless perils to the very point whence we started, where those that we left behind secure, were all the time before us.

Were this world an endless plain, and by sailing eastward we could for ever reach new distances, and discover sights more sweet and strange than any Cyclades or Islands of King Solomon, then there were promise in the voyage. But in pursuit of those far mysteries we dream of, or in tormented chase of that demon phantom that, some time or other, swims before all human hearts; while chasing such over this round globe, they either lead us on in barren mazes or midway leave us whelmed.
