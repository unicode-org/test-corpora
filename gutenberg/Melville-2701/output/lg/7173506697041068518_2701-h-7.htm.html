ESSUULA 33. Aba Specksnyder.

Ku bikwata ku baserikale b’ennyonyi ekika kya whale-craft, kino kirabika ekifo ekirungi nga kyonna okuteeka wansi akatono ak’enjawulo ak’awaka ku mmeeri-board, okuva ku kubeerawo kw’ekibiina ky’abaserikale aba harpooneer, ekibiina ekitamanyiddwa ddala mu mazzi amalala gonna okuggyako ekibinja ky’ennyonyi eziyitibwa whale-fleet.

Obukulu obunene obussibwa ku mulimu gw’omuvubi w’ennyonyi (harpooneer) bweyolekera mu nsonga, nti mu kusooka mu Nvuba y’Abadaaki enkadde, ebyasa bibiri n’okusingawo emabega, obuduumizi bw’emmeeri ya whale tebwali busudde ddala mu muntu kati ayitibwa kapiteeni, wabula bwagabanyizibwamu wakati ye n’omuserikale eyali ayitibwa Specksnyder. Mu bufunze ekigambo kino kitegeeza Fat-Cutter; enkozesa, naye, ekiseera bwe kyagenda kiyitawo kyagifuula eyenkanankana ne Chief Harpooneer. Mu biseera ebyo, obuyinza bwa kapiteeni bwali bukoma ku kutambuza emmeeri n’okuddukanya emmeeri mu bulambalamba; ate nga ku kitongole ky’okuyigga ennyanja ekika kya whale n’ebintu byonna ebikyeraliikiriza, Specksnyder oba Chief Harpooneer ye yafuga. Mu British Greenland Fishery, wansi w’erinnya eryonoonese erya Specksioneer, omukungu ono omukadde Omudaaki akyasigaddewo, naye ekitiibwa kye eky’edda kifunze mu ngeri ey’ennaku. Mu kiseera kino akwata ekifo kyokka nga Harpooneer omukulu; era nga bwe kiri, y’omu ku ba subalterns ba kapiteeni abasinga wansi. Wadde kiri kityo, nga bwe kiri ku mpisa ennungi ey’abavubi b’ennyonyi obuwanguzi bw’olugendo lw’okuvuba envubu okusinga bwesigamye, era okuva mu Buvubi bw’Amerika si muserikale omukulu yekka mu lyato, naye mu mbeera ezimu (okukuuma ekiro ku kisaawe ky’okuvuba ennyanja) ekiduumizi wa ddeeke y’emmeeri nakyo kikye; n’olwekyo enjogera enkulu ey’ebyobufuzi ey’ennyanja esaba, nti mu linnya alina okubeera nga yeetongodde ku basajja abaali mu maaso g’ekikondo, era mu ngeri emu oba endala ayawulwamu ng’omukulu waabwe ow’ekikugu; wadde nga bulijjo, gye bali, mu ngeri emanyiddwa nga batwalibwa ng’okwenkanankana kwabwe mu mbeera z’abantu.

Kati, enjawulo ennene essiddwa wakati w’omuserikale n’omuntu ku nnyanja, ye eno —asooka abeera emabega, asembayo mu maaso. N’olwekyo, mu mmeeri ezivuga ennyanja ekika kya whale n’abasuubuzi, abafumbo bano baba n’ebifo byabwe ne kapiteeni; era bwe kityo, era, mu bavubi b’ennyanja abasinga obungi ab’Amerika abavubi ba harpooneer basula mu kitundu eky’oluvannyuma lw’emmeeri. Kwe kugamba, emmere yaabwe bagitwala mu kabina ya kapiteeni, ne basula mu kifo nga bawuliziganya nayo mu ngeri etali ya butereevu.

Newankubadde ekiseera ekiwanvu eky’olugendo lw’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya Southern whale (nga lwe lusinga obuwanvu mu lugendo lwonna kati oba olubadde lukoleddwa omuntu), akabi ak’enjawulo akali mu lugendo lwalwo, n’ekibiina ky’amagoba ekifuga mu kkampuni, nga byonna, ebya waggulu oba ebya wansi, byesigamye olw’amagoba gaabwe, si ku musaala oguteereddwawo, wabula ku mukisa gwabwe ogw’awamu, awamu n’obulindaala bwabwe obw’awamu, obuzira, n’okukola ennyo; newankubadde ebintu bino byonna mu mbeera ezimu bitera okuzaala empisa ezitali nkakali okusinga mu basuubuzi okutwalira awamu; naye, tofaayo ngeri ki okufaananako amaka amakadde ag’e Mesopotamiya, abavubi bano abavuba ennyanja ekika kya whale, mu mbeera ezimu ez’edda, bayinza okubeera awamu; olw’ebyo byonna, eby’ebweru ebituufu, waakiri, ebya ddeeke ya kwata tebitera kuwummuzibwa mu bintu, era mu mbeera yonna tebiggwaawo. Mazima ddala, nnyingi ze mmeeri za Nantucket mw’onoolaba skipper ng’alaga quarter-deck ye n’obukulu obw’essanyu obutasukkulumye mu ggye lyonna ery’oku mazzi ery’amagye; nedda, okuggya ssente kumpi ekitiibwa eky’okungulu ng’alinga ayambadde engoye za kakobe ez’obwakabaka, so si olugoye olusinga okubeera olufuuse oluvundu.

Era newankubadde nga mu bantu bonna kapiteeni w’emmeeri ya Pequod eyali alina embeera y’omuntu ye yali asinga obutono okuweebwa ekika ekyo eky’okulowooza okusinga obuwanvu; era wadde ng’ekitiibwa kyokka kye yasaba, kwe kugondera okutegeerekeka, okw’akaseera katono; newankubadde nga teyalina muntu yenna kuggya ngatto ku bigere bye nga tannalinnya ku ddeeke ya kwata; era newankubadde nga waliwo ebiseera, olw’embeera ez’enjawulo ezikwatagana n’ebintu eby’oluvannyuma ebirina okunnyonnyolwa mu bujjuvu, yabyogerako mu bigambo ebitali bya bulijjo, ka bibeere eby’okunyooma oba mu terrorem , oba ebirala; naye ne Kapiteeni Akabu teyafaayo n’akatono ku ngeri n’enkozesa y’ennyanja ezisinga obukulu.

Era, mpozzi, tekijja kulemererwa ku nkomerero okutegeerwa, nti emabega w’engeri n’enkozesa ezo, nga bwe tuyinza okugamba, oluusi yeekweka; mu ngeri ey’akabenje nga bazikozesa ku bigendererwa ebirala era eby’obwannannyini okusinga bwe byali bigendereddwamu mu mateeka okuweereza. Obu sultanism obumu obw’obwongo bwe, obwali mu ngeri endala mu ddaala eddungi bwasigala nga tebulabika; okuyita mu ngeri ezo obusultan obwo bwe bumu bwafuuka obw’omubiri mu bwannakyemalira obutaziyizibwa. Kubanga okuba obusukkulumu bw’omusajja mu magezi kye kinaaba, tekiyinza nate kutwala bukulu bwa nkola, obuliwo ku basajja abalala, awatali buyambi bwa kika kya buyiiya obw’ebweru n’okusimba emirandira, bulijjo, mu byo, ekitono oba ekinene ekitono era nga kya musingi. Kino kye kiri, emirembe gyonna ekikuuma abalangira ba Katonda ab’amazima ab’Obwakabaka okuva mu kugwa kw’ensi; era aleka ekitiibwa ekisinga obunene empewo eno ky’esobola okuwa, eri abasajja abo abafuuka ab’ettutumu ennyo okuyita mu butono bwabwe obutakoma ku mukono ogw’okulonda ogwekwekeddwa ogw’Obutakola obw’Obwakatonda, okusinga okuyita mu busukkulumu bwabwe obutabuusibwabuusibwa ku ddaala erifu ery’ekibiina ekinene. Empisa ennungi ennene bwe zityo zikweka mu bintu bino ebitonotono ng’enzikiriza enkyamu ez’ebyobufuzi ezisukkiridde ziziteekamu, ne kiba nti mu mbeera ezimu ez’obwakabaka ne ku busirusiru obusiru zigabye amaanyi. Naye bwe, nga bwe kyali ku Nicholas the Czar, engule ey’empeta ey’obwakabaka obw’ebitundu yeetoolodde obwongo bw’obwakabaka; awo, ebisibo by’abantu abangi (plebeian herds) ne bifukamira nga tebinnabaawo nga tekunnabaawo kugatta kwa maanyi nnyo. Era, omuzannyi wa katemba ow’ekikangabwa eyandiraze obutafugibwa obufa mu kusenya kwabwo okujjuvu n’okuwuubaala obutereevu, tajja kwerabira akabonero, mu ngeri y’emu akakulu ennyo mu by’emikono bye, ng’oyo kati ayogerwako.

Naye Akabu, Kapiteeni wange, akyatambula mu maaso gange mu Nantucket ye yonna grimness ne shagginess; era mu kitundu kino ekikwata ku Empula ne Bakabaka, silina kukweka nti nnina akakwate ku muyizzi omwavu omukadde ow’ennyanja whale nga ye yekka; era, n’olwekyo, emitego gyonna egy’ekitiibwa egy’okungulu n’amayumba bingaanyi. Oh, Akabu! ekijja okuba ekinene mu ggwe, kiteekwa okwetaaga okusimbulwa okuva mu bbanga, n’okubbira mu buziba, n’okulagibwa mu mpewo etaliiko mubiri!





ESSUULA 34. Emmeeza ya Kabina.

Buli ssaawa 12 ez’emisana; era Dough-Boy, omuwanika, ng’asika ffeesi ye ey’omugaati omumyufu okuva mu kabina-scuttle, alangirira ekyeggulo eri mukama we ne mukama we; nga, ng’atudde mu lyato erya lee quarter-boat, yaakamala okutwala okwetegereza enjuba; era kati mu busirise abala latitude ku kipande ekiseeneekerevu, ekiringa omudaali, ekyaterekebwa ekigendererwa ekyo ekya buli lunaku ku kitundu eky’okungulu eky’ekigere kye eky’amasanga. Okusinziira ku butafaayo ddala ku mawulire ago, wandirowoozezza nti Akabu eyali ow’omutima yali tawulidde buwombeefu bwe. Naye mu kiseera kino, ng’akwata ebibikka bya mizen, yeewuuba okutuuka ku ddeeke, era mu ddoboozi ery’ekigero, eritali lya ssanyu, ng’agamba nti, “Ekyeggulo, Mwami Starbuck,” n’abulawo mu kabina.

Eddoboozi erisembayo ery’eddaala lya sultan we bwe lifa, era nga Starbuck, Emir eyasooka, alina ensonga yonna okulowooza nti atudde, olwo Starbuck n’azuukuka okuva mu kusirika kwe, n’akyuka ntono ku bibaawo, era, oluvannyuma lw’okutunula mu ntaana mu binnacle, agamba, n’okukwata ku ssanyu, “Ekyeggulo, Mwami Stubb,” era n’akka ku scuttle. Emir owookubiri awummulako ku rigging akaseera katono, n’oluvannyuma n’akankana katono ku bbulaasi enkulu, okulaba oba kinaaba bulungi n’omuguwa ogwo omukulu, naye mu ngeri y’emu akwata omugugu omukadde, era n’amangu “Ekyeggulo, Mwami Flask,” . agoberera oluvannyuma lw’abaamusooka.

Naye Emir owookusatu, kati nga yeelaba yekka ku ddeeke ya kwata, alabika awulira ng’awummudde okuva mu kuziyiza okulala okw’okwewuunya; kubanga, ng’akuba enduulu ez’engeri zonna ez’okumanya mu njuyi zonna, n’okusamba engatto ze, akuba mu muyaga ogw’amaanyi naye nga teguliiko maloboozi ogw’engoye z’amayembe waggulu ddala ku mutwe gwa Grand Turk; n’oluvannyuma, ng’ayita mu sleight ey’ekikugu, ng’asuula enkoofiira ye waggulu mu mizentop okusobola okufuna shelf, agenda wansi ng’asanyuka okutuuka wala waakiri nga bw’asigala nga alabika okuva ku ddeeke, ng’azza emabega emikolo emirala gyonna, ng’aleeta waggulu emabega n’omuziki. Naye ere okulinnya mu mulyango gw’akayumba wansi, ayimirirako katono, n’asindika ffeesi empya yonna, era, olwo, akatono aka Flask eyeetongodde, akasesa kayingira mu maaso ga Kabaka Akabu, mu mpisa ya Abjectus, oba Omuddu.

Si kye kisinga obutono mu bintu ebyewuunyisa ebizaalibwa obukozi obw’amaanyi obw’enkozesa y’ennyanja, nti bwe baba mu bbanga eriggule erya ddeeke abaserikale abamu, bwe banyiize, bajja kwegumiikiriza n’obuvumu n’okujeemera ekimala eri omuduumizi waabwe; naye, kkumi ku kimu, abaserikale abo bennyini akaseera akaddako baserengete ku kijjulo kyabwe eky’ennono mu kabina y’omuduumizi oyo yennyini, era amangu ago nga tebalina musango, so si kugamba mpewo enyooma era enwombeefu gy’ali, ng’atudde ku mutwe gw’emmeeza; kino kyewuunyisa, oluusi kisinga kusekererwa. Lwaki enjawulo eno? Ekizibu? Mpozzi si bwe kiri. Okuba nga yali Berusazza, Kabaka w’e Babulooni; era olw’okuba yali Berusazza, si mu malala wabula mu ngeri ey’empisa, mazima ddala mu ekyo kiteekwa okuba nga kyalimu obukulu obw’olubeerera. Naye oyo mu mwoyo omutuufu ogw’obwakabaka era ogw’amagezi akubiriza emmeeza ye ey’ekyeggulo ey’ekyama ey’abagenyi abayitiddwa, amaanyi g’omusajja oyo agatasoomoozebwa n’obufuzi bw’obuyinza obw’omuntu kinnoomu obw’ekiseera ekyo; obwakabaka bw’omuntu oyo busukkulumye ku bwa Berusazza, kubanga Berusazza si ye yali asinga obukulu. Oyo naye omulundi gumu alya mikwano gye, awoomye kye kitegeeza okubeera Cæsar. Bulogo bwa social czarship nga tewali kugumira. Kati, singa ku kulowooza kuno oyongerako obukulu obutongole obw’omukulu w’emmeeri, olwo, nga oyita mu kuteebereza, ojja kuggya ekivaako eky’enjawulo ekyo eky’ebiramu eby’omu nnyanja ekyaakagambibwa.

Ku mmeeza ye eyali ekoleddwa mu masanga, Akabu yakubiriza ng’empologoma ey’omu nnyanja esirise era eriko enviiri ku mwalo gw’amasanga amazungu, nga yeetooloddwa abaana be abaali baagala entalo naye nga bakyali ba kitiibwa. Mu kkubo lye erituufu, buli muserikale yalinda okuweereza. Baali ng’abaana abato mu maaso ga Akabu; era naye, mu Akabu, kyalabika nga tewaaliwo kwekweka kwegulumiza okutono ennyo mu bantu. Nga balina endowooza emu, amaaso gaabwe agagenderera gonna gaali gasibye ku kiso ky’omusajja omukadde, ng’ayoola essowaani enkulu mu maaso ge. Sirowooza nti eri ensi bandyonoonye akaseera ako nga beetegereza katono, ne ku mulamwa ogutaliimu ludda ng’obudde. Nedda! Era bwe yagolola ekiso kye ne fooro, wakati wabyo ekitundu ky’ennyama y’ente we kyasibirwa, Akabu bw’atyo n’akola akabonero ku ssowaani ya Starbuck gy’ali, munne n’afuna ennyama ye ng’alinga afuna sadaaka; era ogiteme bulungi; era katono ne kitandikira singa, mpozzi, ekiso kyaliira ku ssowaani; n’agikamula nga tewali maloboozi; n’agimira, si awatali kwegendereza. Kubanga, okufaananako n’ekijjulo ky’okutikkirwa ku ntebe e Frankfort, Empula wa Girimaani gy’alya ennyo n’Abalonzi b’Obwakabaka omusanvu, bwe kityo emmere eno ey’omu kabina mu ngeri emu oba endala yali mmere ya kitiibwa, ng’eriibwa mu kasirise ak’entiisa; era naye ku mmeeza Akabu omukadde teyagaana kwogera; ye kennyini yekka yali musiru. Nga kyali kiwummuza nnyo okuziyira Stubb, envunza bwe yakola racket ey’amangu mu hold wansi. Era Flask omuto omwavu, ye yali omwana omuto, era omulenzi omuto ow’akabaga kano ak’amaka akakooye. Ebibye byali magumba g’ennyama y’ente ey’omunnyo; eyiye yandibadde endongo. Flask okusobola okwetwala nti yeeyamba, kino kiteekwa okuba nga kyamulabika ng’ekifaanagana n’obubbi mu ddaala erisooka. Singa yeeyamba ku mmeeza eyo, awatali kubuusabuusa, teyandisobodde kukwata mutwe gwe waggulu mu nsi eno ey’obwesimbu; wadde kiri kityo, ekyewuunyisa okugamba nti, Akabu teyamugaana. Era singa Flask yeeyambye, emikisa gyali nti Akabu yali takyetegereza nnyo. Ekitono ennyo, Flask yeetwala nti yeeyamba okukola butto. Oba yali alowooza nti bannannyini mmeeri baagigaana, olw’okugizimba langi ye entangaavu era ey’omusana; oba oba yalowooza nti, ku lugendo oluwanvu bwe lityo mu mazzi ago agataliiko katale, butto yali ku mutindo gwa waggulu, era n’olwekyo si ye, subaltern; wabula bwe kyali, Flask, woowe! yali musajja atalina butto!

Ekirala. Flask ye muntu eyasembyeyo okukka wansi ku kijjulo, ate Flask ye musajja asoose okulinnya. Fumiitiriza! Kubanga wano ekyeggulo kya Flask kyali kijjudde bubi mu kiseera. Starbuck ne Stubb bombi be baalina entandikwa ye; era naye era balina enkizo ey’okuwummulamu emabega. Singa Stubb wadde, ali waggulu peg yokka okusinga Flask, atuuka n’okuba n’apetite entono, era mu bbanga ttono n’alaga obubonero bw’okumaliriza emmere ye ey’okulya, olwo Flask alina okwesanyusa, tajja kufuna mimwa egisukka mu ssatu ku lunaku olwo; kubanga kimenya enkozesa entukuvu Stubb okukulembera Flask okutuuka ku ddeeke. N’olwekyo kyali nti Flask lumu yakkiriza mu kyama, nti okuva lwe yasituka okutuuka ku kitiibwa ky’omuserikale, okuva mu kaseera ako yali tamanyirangako kiki kye kyali okubeera ekirala okuggyako enjala, okusinga oba okutono. Kubanga ebyo bye yalya tebyamumalamu nnyo njala, wabula ne bimukuuma nga tebifa. Emirembe n’okumatizibwa, Flask bwe yalowooza, bivudde mu lubuto lwange emirembe gyonna. Ndi muserikale; naye, nga njagala nnyo okuvuba akatono ku nnyama y’ente ey’omulembe mu forecastle, nga bwe nnali nkola nga ndi mu maaso g’ekikondo. Waliwo ebibala by'okukuzibwa kati; waliwo obutaliimu bw'ekitiibwa: waliwo eddalu ly'obulamu! Ng’oggyeeko ekyo, singa kyali bwe kityo nti omuvubi yenna omunyafu owa Pequod yalina ensaalwa ku Flask mu butongole bwa Flask, omulunnyanja oyo kye yalina okukola, okusobola okufuna okwesasuza okungi, kwe kugenda emabega mu kiseera ky’ekyeggulo, n’afuna akafaananyi ku Flask ng’ayita mu kabina sky-light, ng’atudde musiru era nga atabuse mu maaso ga Akabu ow’entiisa.

Kati, Akabu ne banne abasatu baakola emmeeza eyinza okuyitibwa emmeeza esooka mu kabina ya Pequod. Oluvannyuma lw’okugenda kwabwe, nga bigenda mu maaso mu nsengeka ekyusiddwa okutuuka ku kutuuka kwabwe, olugoye olwa kanvaasi lwalongoosebwa, oba okusingawo ne luzzibwa mu nsengeka emu ey’amangu olw’omuwanika eyali omumyufu. Era awo abavuzi ba harpoone abasatu ne bayitibwa okugenda ku mbaga, nga be ba legatees baakyo abasigaddewo. Baakola ekika ky’ekisenge ky’abaweereza eky’ekiseera eky’akayumba akawanvu era ak’amaanyi.

Mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa eyawukana ku kuziyizibwa okutagumiikiriza nnyo n’okufuga okutalabika okutaliimu mannya okw’emmeeza ya kapiteeni, yali layisinsi yonna ey’obutafaayo n’obwangu, demokulasiya kumpi ow’ekibogwe eya bannaffe abo ab’omutindo ogwa wansi abaali bakuba enduulu. Nga bakama baabwe, abafumbo, balabika nga batya eddoboozi ly’ebikondo by’ensaya zaabwe, abavubi ba harpooneer baali bakamula emmere yaabwe n’essanyu lingi nnyo ne kiba nti waliwo lipoota gye baali baagiwadde. Baalya nga bakama; bajjuza olubuto lwabwe ng’emmeeri z’Abayindi olunaku lwonna nga zitikka eby’akaloosa. Appetites bwetyo portentous yalina Queequeg ne Tashtego, nti okujjuza ebifo ebikalu ebyakolebwa repast emabega, emirundi mingi pale Dough-Boy yali fain okuleeta ku great baron of salt-junk, nga kirabika yasimibwa okuva mu nte enkalu. Era bw’aba nga teyabadde mulamu ku nsonga eyo, bw’aba nga teyagenda na ‘nimble hop-skip-and-jump’, olwo Tashtego yalina engeri etali ya kitiibwa ey’okumusitula amangu ng’akuba fooro ku mugongo gwe, mu ngeri ya harpoon. Era lumu Daggoo, eyakwatibwa n’okusesa okw’amangu, yayamba Dough-Boy okujjukira ng’amunyakula mu mubiri, n’asika omutwe gwe mu mudumu omunene ogw’embaawo ekyerere, ate Tashtego, ng’akutte ekiso mu ngalo, n’atandika okuteeka enzirugavu ng’asooka okumusala ku mutwe. Mu butonde yali musajja muto alina obunkenke nnyo, ng’akankana, omuwanika ono ow’amaaso ag’omugaati; ezzadde ly’omufumbi w’emigaati eyagwa ne nnansi w’eddwaaliro. Era kiki n’ekyewuunyo ekiyimiridde ekya Akabu omuddugavu ow’entiisa, n’okukyalira abantu bano abasatu mu ngeri ey’akajagalalo buli luvannyuma lwa kiseera, obulamu bwa Dough-Boy bwonna bwali bumu obw’okukankana emimwa obutasalako. Ebiseera ebisinga, oluvannyuma lw’okulaba abavuzi b’engoye nga bateekeddwamu ebintu byonna bye baali baagala, yatolokanga mu mikono gyabwe n’ayingira mu kasenge ke akatono akaali kaliraanyewo, n’abatunula mu kutya ng’ayita mu bizibiti by’oluggi lwayo, okutuusa byonna lwe byaggwa.

Kyabadde kifaananyi okulaba Queequeg ng’atudde ku Tashtego, ng’awakanya amannyo ge agaali gateekeddwa mu fayiro n’ag’Omuyindi: mu musalaba gye bali, Daggoo ng’atudde wansi, kubanga entebe yandireese omutwe gwe ogwali gujjudde eggaali y’abafu ku carlines eza wansi; ku buli ntambula y’ebitundu bye ebinene ennyo, ekifuula ekisenge ky’akayumba ekya wansi okukankana, ng’enjovu ya Afirika bw’egenda ng’egenda musaabaze mu mmeeri. Naye olw’ebyo byonna, omuddugavu omukulu yali yeewuunyisa okwewala, so si kugamba nti dainty. Kyalabika nga tekisoboka nti olw’akamwa akatono bwe katyo bw’ogeraageranya asobola okukuuma amaanyi agasaasaanyizibwa mu muntu omugazi ennyo, ow’ekika kya baronial, era ow’ekitalo. Naye, awatali kubuusabuusa, omusajja ono omukambwe ow’ekitiibwa yaliisibwa nnyo era n’anywa nnyo ekintu eky’empewo ekyali kiyitiridde; era okuyita mu nnyindo ze ezigaziye nga zifuuwa mu bulamu obw’ekika ekya waggulu obw’ensi. Si nnyama ya nte oba mu mugaati, ebinene bikolebwa oba biriisibwa. Naye Queequeg, yalina akabonero akafa, ak’obukambwe ak’omumwa mu kulya — eddoboozi eribi ekimala — nnyo, ne kiba nti Dough-Boy eyali akankana katono yatunula okulaba oba waliwo obubonero bwonna obw’amannyo obwali bukwese mu mikono gye egyali gigonvu. Era bwe yawuliranga Tashtego ng’amuyimba asobole okwefulumya, amagumba ge gasobole okulondebwa, omuwanika eyali ow’amagezi amangu byonna n’amenyaamenya ebibya ebyali bimwetoolodde mu dduuka ly’emmere, olw’okusannyalala kwe okw’amangu. ESSUULA 35. Omutwe gw’Ekikondo.

Mu kiseera ky’obudde ekyasinga okusanyusa, mu kukyukakyuka okutuufu n’abalunnyanja abalala omutwe gwange ogusooka ogw’omuti ne gujja okwetooloola.

Mu bavuzi b’ennyanja abasinga obungi ab’Amerika abavuzi b’ennyanja mast-heads zibeera n’abantu kumpi mu kiseera kye kimu ng’eryato liva ku mwalo gwayo; newankubadde nga ayinza okuba ne mayiro enkumi kkumi na ttaano, n’okusingawo, okusaabala nga tannatuuka mu kifo kye ekituufu eky’okusaabala. Era singa, oluvannyuma lw’olugendo lw’emyaka esatu, ena, oba etaano aba asemberera awaka ng’alina ekintu kyonna ekyerere mu ye — tugambe, ekidomola ekyerere wadde —olwo, emitwe gye egy’amayinja gikuumibwa nga girimu abantu okutuuka ku nkomerero; era si okutuusa ng’ebikondo bye eby’omu bbanga biyingidde wakati mu bikondo by’omwalo, talekulira ddala essuubi ly’okukwata ennyanja ekika kya whale endala emu.

Kati, nga bizinensi y’okuyimirira emitwe gy’amasasi, ku lukalu oba ku mazzi, bw’eri ey’edda ennyo era ey’enjawulo, ka tugaziye mu kigero ekimu wano. Nkitwala, nti abaasooka okuyimirira ku mitwe gy’amasasi baali Bamisiri ab’edda; kubanga, mu kunoonyereza kwange kwonna, sisangayo n’emu nga tennabaawo. Kubanga newankubadde nga bajjajjaabwe, abazimbi ba Baberi, awatali kubuusabuusa, ku munaala gwabwe, bateekwa okuba nga baali bagenderera okukuza omutwe gw’omuti ogusinga obugulumivu mu Asiya yonna, oba mu Afirika oba; naye (nga loole esembayo tennateekebwa ku yo) ng’ekikondo ekyo ekinene eky’amayinja ekyabwe bwe kiyinza okugambibwa nti kyagenda ku lubaawo, mu muyaga ogw’entiisa ogw’obusungu bwa Katonda; n’olwekyo, tetusobola kuwa bazimbi bano ab’e Babulooni okukulembeza okusinga Abamisiri. Era nti Abamisiri baali ggwanga lya bayimiridde ku mitwe gy’amadaala, kwe kukakasa okwesigamiziddwa ku nzikiriza ey’awamu mu bakugu mu by’okunoonyereza ku by’edda, nti piramidi ezaasooka zaatandikibwawo olw’ebigendererwa by’eby’emmunyeenye: endowooza ewagirwa mu ngeri ey’enjawulo entonde ey’enjawulo eringa amadaala ey’enjuyi zonna ennya eza ebizimbe ebyo; mu ngeri eyo, n’okusitula amagulu gaabwe amawanvu mu ngeri ey’ekitalo, abakugu abo ab’emmunyeenye ab’edda baali bamanyidde okulinnya okutuuka ku ntikko, ne bayimbira emmunyeenye empya; ne bwe kiba nti ebifo ebitunuulirwamu emmeeri ey’omulembe biyimba olw’okusaabala, oba ennyanja ekika kya whale emala okulabika. Mu Saint Stylites, omusirusiru Omukristaayo omututumufu ow’edda, eyamuzimbira empagi ey’amayinja empanvu mu ddungu era ekitundu kyonna eky’obulamu bwe eky’oluvannyuma n’amala ku ntikko yaayo, ng’asitula emmere ye okuva wansi ng’akozesa ekintu ekiyitibwa tackle; mu ye tulina ekyokulabirako ekyewuunyisa eky’omuntu ayimiridde-emitwe gy’amasasi atalina ntiisa; eyali tagenda kugobebwa mu kifo kye kifu oba omuzira, enkuba, laddu oba omuzira; naye mu buzira ng’ayolekedde buli kimu okutuuka ku nkomerero, ddala yafiira ku kifo kye. Ku standers-of-mast-heads ez’omulembe tulina naye set etalina bulamu; abasajja ab’amayinja, ekyuma n’ekikomo bokka; wadde nga basobola bulungi okwolekagana n’omuyaga omukambwe, bakyataliiko busobozi bwonna mu bizinensi y’okuyimba nga bazudde ekintu kyonna ekyewuunyisa. Waliwo Napoleon; oyo, waggulu ku mpagi ya Vendome, ayimiridde ng’azingidde emikono, nga ffuuti kikumi mu ataano mu bbanga; careless, kati, ani afuga ddeeke wansi; oba Louis Philippe, Louis Blanc, oba Louis Omulyolyomi. Great Washington naye, ayimiridde waggulu waggulu ku kikondo kye ekikulu ekiwanvu mu Baltimore, era okufaananako emu ku mpagi za Hercules, empagi ye eraga ensonga eyo ey’obukulu bw’omuntu abantu abatono abafa gye bajja okusukka. Admiral Nelson naye, ku capstan ey’ekyuma ky’emmundu, ayimiridde omutwe gwe ogw’omuggo mu Trafalgar Square; era buli lwe kisinga okuzikizibwa omukka ogwo ogwa London, akabonero kakyaweebwa nti omuzira omukwese aliwo; kubanga awali omukka, wateekwa okubaawo omuliro. Naye wadde Washington omukulu, wadde Napoleon, wadde Nelson, tajja kuddamu laddu n’emu okuva wansi, wadde nga bayitibwa mu ddalu okukola omukwano n’abawabuzi baabwe ddeeke eziwuguliddwa ze batunuulira; wabula kiyinza okuteeberezebwa, nti emyoyo gyazo giyingira mu kifu ekinene eky’omu maaso, ne ginnyonnyola ebiwujjo ki n’amayinja ki ebirina okwewalibwa.

Kiyinza okulabika ng’ekitali kya bwenkanya okugatta mu ngeri yonna ebiyimiridde ku mutwe gw’omuggo ogw’oku lukalu n’eby’ennyanja; naye nti mu mazima si bwe kiri, kyeyoleka bulungi n’ekintu Obed Macy, munnabyafaayo yekka owa Nantucket, ky’avunaanibwa. Obed asaanira atugamba, nti mu biseera eby’olubereberye eby’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale, ere emmeeri tezinnaba kusindikibwa bulijjo nga zigoberera omuzannyo guno, abantu b’oku kizinga ekyo baazimba ebisiba ebigulumivu ku lubalama lw’ennyanja, abatunuulizi gye baalinnyanga nga bayita mu ngeri wa cleats eziriko emisumaali, ekintu ng’ebinyonyi bwe bigenda waggulu mu nnyumba y’enkoko. Emyaka mitono emabega enteekateeka eno y’emu yassibwawo abavuzi b’ennyanja Bay whalemen ab’e New Zealand, era bwe baayogera ku muzannyo guno, ne bawa ekiwandiiko eri amaato agaali geetegefu okutambulirako abantu agaali okumpi ne bbiici. Naye empisa eno kati efuuse ya mugaso; tukyuse olwo ne tudda ku mutwe gumu omutuufu ogw’omuggo, ogw’emmeeri ya whale ku nnyanja. Emitwe esatu egy’amayinja gikuumibwa nga girimu abantu okuva enjuba lw’evaayo okutuuka ng’egwa; abalunnyanja nga bakwata enkyukakyuka zaabwe eza bulijjo (nga bwe bali ku muvuzi), era nga buli omu awummuza munne buli luvannyuma lwa ssaawa bbiri. Mu budde obw’obutebenkevu obw’omu bitundu eby’obutiti kinyuma nnyo omutwe gw’amasasi; nedda, eri omusajja afumiitiriza ow’ekirooto kisanyusa. Eyo gy’oyimiridde, ffuuti kikumi waggulu ku ddeeke ezisirise, ng’otambula mu buziba, ng’olinga agamba nti ebikondo biba bikondo ebinene ennyo, ate wansi wo ne wakati w’amagulu go, nga bwe tuyinza okugamba, owuga ebisolo ebinene ennyo eby’ennyanja, wadde ng’emmeeri bwe zaasaabala edda wakati w’engatto z’ekisolo ekimanyiddwa ennyo ekya Colossus e Rodes enkadde. Eyo gy’oyimiridde, ng’obuze mu lunyiriri lw’ennyanja olutaliiko kkomo, nga tolina kintu kyonna kiwuguddwa okuggyako amayengo. Emmeeri efuuse ekyekulungirivu yeekulukuunya mu ngeri ey’obugayaavu; empewo z’obusuubuzi ezirimu otulo zifuuwa; buli kimu kikugonjoola mu languor. Okusinga obungi, mu bulamu buno obw’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale mu bitundu eby’obutiti, ekintu ekitali kya bulijjo ekisukkiridde kikuteeka ssente; towulira mawulire gonna; soma tewali gazeeti yonna; extras with startling accounts of commonplaces tezikulimba mu ssanyu eriteetaagisa; temuwulira kubonaabona kwonna mu maka; emigabo egy’okugwa; okugwa kwa sitokisi; tebatawaanyizibwangako kulowooza ku ky’onoolya ekyeggulo —kubanga emmere yo yonna ey’emyaka esatu n’okusingawo eterekebwa bulungi mu bidomola, era ssente z’ogenda okusasula tezikyuka.

Mu omu ku bavubi abo ab’omu bukiikaddyo, ku lugendo oluwanvu olw’emyaka esatu oba ena, nga bwe kitera okubaawo, omugatte gw’essaawa ez’enjawulo z’omala ku mutwe gw’omuti gwandibadde guwera emyezi egiwerako egy’enjawulo. Era kinene nnyo eky’ennaku nti ekifo ky’owaayo ekitundu ekinene ennyo eky’ekiseera kyonna eky’obulamu bwo obw’obutonde, kirina okuba nga kya nnaku nnyo nga tekirina kintu kyonna ekisemberera ekifo ekinyuvu, oba ekituukiridde okuzaala ekifo ekinyuma eky’okuwulira, . gamba ng’ekwata ku kitanda, hammock, mmotoka y’abafu, bbokisi y’abakuumi, empuku, kkoosi, oba ebirala byonna ku bikozesebwa ebyo ebitonotono era ebinyuma abasajja mwe beeyawula okumala akaseera. Ekifo kyo ekisinga okutera okutuula gwe mutwe gwa t’ gallant-mast, gy’oyimiridde ku miggo ebiri emigonvu egy’enjawulo (kumpi egy’enjawulo eri abavubi b’ennyanja whale) egiyitibwa t’ gallant cross-trees. Wano, ng’asuuliddwa ku nnyanja, omutandisi awulira ng’awummudde nga bw’awulira ng’ayimiridde ku mayembe g’ente ennume. Okukakasa, mu budde obw’obutiti oyinza okusitula ennyumba yo waggulu, mu ngeri y’ekkooti y’essaawa; naye mu ngeri entuufu ekkooti y’essaawa esinga obunene si nnyumba okusinga omubiri ogutambadde; kubanga ng’omwoyo bwe gusibiddwa munda mu weema yaayo ey’ennyama, era nga tegutasobola kutambula mu yo mu ddembe, wadde okutambula okuva mu yo, nga tegudduse mu kabi kanene ak’okuzikirizibwa (ng’omulamazi atamanyi bw’asala ensozi za Alps ezirimu omuzira mu kiseera eky’obutiti); kale ekkooti y’essaawa si nnyumba nnyo wabula nvulopu yokka, oba olususu olw’okwongerako olukubikka. Tosobola kuteeka shelf oba ssanduuko mu mubiri gwo, era tosobola kukola kabada nnyangu ya kkanzu yo ey’essaawa.

Ku bikwata ku bino byonna, kya nnaku nnyo nti emitwe gy’emmeeri y’ennyanja ekika kya whale ey’obugwanjuba tegirina weema oba empula ezo entonotono ezisikiriza, eziyitibwa crow’s-nests , nga muno ebifo ebitunuulirwa omuvubi w’ennyanja mu Greenland bikuumibwa okuva ku bubi embeera y’obudde bw’ennyanja ezirimu omuzira. Mu nnyiriri ezaali ku mabbali g’omuliro eza Kapiteeni Sleet, ezaatuumibwa “Olugendo mu nsozi za Icebergs, nga banoonya Greenland Whale, era mu ngeri y’emu olw’okuddamu okuzuula Amatwale ga Iceland agabula mu Greenland Enkadde;” mu muzingo guno ogw’okusiimibwa, abayimiridde bonna ab’emitwe gy’amayinja baweereddwa n’ennyiriri ezisikiriza ezikwata ku kisu ky’enkovu ekya Glacier ekyayiiya mu kiseera ekyo gye buvuddeko awo, nga lino lye lyali erinnya ly’omulimu omulungi ogwa Kapiteeni Sleet. Yakiyita ekisu ky’enkookooma ya Sleet , mu kitiibwa kye; ye nga ye muyiiya eyasooka era nga ye patent, era nga tetulina biwoomerera byonna eby’obulimba ebisesa, era nga tukwata nti bwe tuyita abaana baffe bennyini amannya gaffe (ffe bataata nga tuli abayiiya n’aba patent abasooka), bwe tutyo bwe tulina okutuuma erinnya lyaffe ekyuma ekirala kyonna tuyinza okuzaala. Mu nkula, ekisu ky’enkookooma ya Sleet kintu ekiringa tierce oba payipu ennene; wabula waggulu kiggule nga kiriko ekyuma ekitambula ku mabbali okusobola okukuuma empewo okuva ku mutwe gwo mu muyaga omukambwe. Olw’okuba ng’onywedde ku ntikko y’ekikondo, olinnya mu kyo ng’oyita mu kasenge akatono akayitibwa trap-hatch wansi. Ku ludda olw’oluvannyuma oba oludda oluddako emabega w’emmeeri, waliwo entebe ennungi, nga wansi waliwo loogi ey’okuteeka amaliba, ebibudaabuda n’amakanzu. Mu maaso waliwo ekifo eky’amaliba, mw’oyinza okukuuma ekkondeere lyo ery’okwogera, payipu, ewala, n’ebintu ebirala ebikozesebwa mu by’ennyanja. Kapiteeni Sleet mu buntu bwe yayimiriza omutwe gwe ogw’omuggo mu kisu kino eky’enkookooma eky’ekye, atugamba nti bulijjo yalina emmundu (nayo ng’etereezeddwa mu kkeeki), awamu n’eccupa ya pawuda n’amasasi, n’ekigendererwa eky’okupopa okuva ku narwhales ezitaayaaya, oba enseenene z’omu nnyanja ezitambulatambula eziyingira mu mazzi ago; kubanga tosobola kuzikuba masasi bulungi ng’oli ku ddeeke olw’okuziyiza kw’amazzi, naye okuzikuba wansi kintu kya njawulo nnyo. Kati, kya lwatu nti gwali mulimu gwa kwagala eri Kapiteeni Sleet okunnyonnyola, nga bw’akola, ebintu byonna ebitonotono ebikwata ku nsonga z’ekisu kye eky’enkookooma; naye newankubadde nga bw’agaziwa ku bingi ku bino, era newankubadde nga atujjanjaba n’ennyiriri za ssaayansi ennyo ez’okugezesa kwe mu kisu kino eky’enkookooma, ne kkampasi entono gye yakuuma awo n’ekigendererwa eky’okuziyiza ensobi ezaava mu ekyo ekiyitibwa “ . okusikiriza okw’omu kitundu” okwa magineeti zonna eza binnacle; ensobi eyinza okuva ku kitundu ekiwanvu eky’ekyuma mu bipande by’emmeeri, era mu mbeera ya Glacier, mpozzi, olw’okuba waaliwo abaweesi bangi nnyo abamenyese mu bakozi baayo; Nze ngamba, nti newankubadde nga Kapiteeni mugezi nnyo era wa ssaayansi wano, naye, olw’okuyiga kwe kwonna “okukyama kwa binnacle,” “okwetegereza kwa azimuth compass,” ne “ensobi ezibalirirwa,” akimanyi bulungi nnyo, Kapiteeni Sleet, nti teyabadde bwe kityo nnyo nnyikiddwa mu kufumiitiriza okwo okw’amaanyi okwa magineeti, ng’okulemererwa okusikiriza oluusi n’oluusi eri akacupa ako akatono akajjuza obulungi, akaali kakubiddwa bulungi nnyo ku ludda olumu olw’ekisu ky’enkookooma ye, nga kyangu okutuukako omukono gwe. Wadde, okutwaliza awamu, nneegomba nnyo era n’okwagala Kapiteeni omuzira, omwesimbu, era omuyivu; naye mmutwala nga kibi nnyo nti yandibadde abuusa amaaso ddala eccupa eyo ey’ekibonerezo, ng’alaba nga mukwano gwe omwesigwa era omubudaabuda bwe guteekwa okuba nga yali, ate ng’engalo eziriko emitto n’omutwe oguliko enkoofiira yali asoma okubala waggulu awo mu kisu ky’ekinyonyi ekyo munda ebifo bisatu oba bina eby’omuti.

Naye bwe kiba nti ffe abavubi b’ennyanja ekika kya Southern whale tetuteekebwa waggulu nnyo nga Captain Sleet n’abantu be aba Greenland bwe baali; naye ate obuzibu obwo bukontana nnyo olw’obutebenkevu obw’enjawulo ennyo obw’ennyanja ezo ezisikiriza ffe abavubi b’omu Bugwanjuba mwe tusinga okulengejja. Ku kimu, nnawummulanga ku rigging nga nwummudde nnyo, nga mpummula waggulu okunyumya ne Queequeg, oba omuntu omulala yenna atali ku mulimu gwe nnyinza okusangayo; olwo ne nnyongerako katono, n’okusuula ekigere ekigayaavu ku luggya olw’amaato ag’okungulu, n’okusooka okulaba amalundiro ag’amazzi, era bwe ntyo ku nkomerero n’okulinnya okutuuka gye nsinga okugenda.

Ka nkole ebbeere eriyonjo wano, era mu bwesimbu nkkirize nti nakuuma naye bambi guard. Ng’ekizibu ky’obutonde bwonna kyetooloola mu nze, nnali nsobola ntya —nga nlekeddwa ddala nzekka ku buwanvu obw’engeri eyo obuleeta ebirowoozo —nyinza ntya okukwata obuvunaanyizibwa bwange obw’okukuuma ebiragiro byonna eby’emmeeri z’ennyanja ekika kya whale eziyimiridde, “Kuuma eriiso lyo ery’obudde ggulawo, era muyimbe buli kiseera.”

Era ka mu kifo kino mbabuulirire mu ngeri ekwata ku mutima, mmwe bannannyini mmeeri aba Nantucket! Weegendereze okwewandiisa mu buvubi bwo obulindaala omulenzi yenna alina ekibatu ekigonvu n’eriiso eririmu ebituli; okuweebwa okufumiitiriza okutali kwa sizoni; era eyeewaayo okusindika ne Phædon mu kifo kya Bowditch mu mutwe gwe. Mwegendereze omuntu ng’oyo, ngamba nti; ennyanja zo ekika kya whale zirina okulabibwa nga tezinnaba kuttibwa; era omuvubuka ono ow’amaaso agabbira mu Platon ajja kukusika wake kkumi okwetoloola ensi yonna, era takufuula pint emu ey’ensigo gy’asinga okugaggawala. Era ne monitions zino tezeetaagisa n’akatono. Kubanga ennaku zino, envuba y’ennyanja ekika kya whale ewa obubudamu eri abavubuka bangi ab’omukwano, ab’ennaku, era abatalina birowoozo, abeetamwa okufaayo okw’amaanyi okw’ensi, era nga banoonya ebirowoozo mu ttaala ne blubber. Childe Harold not unfrequently perches himself upon the mast-head of some luckless disappointed whale-ship, and in moody phrase ejaculates:—

“Roll on, thou deep and dark blue ocean, roll!
Ten thousand blubber-hunters sweep over thee in vain.”

Very often do the captains of such ships take those absent-minded young philosophers to task, upbraiding them with not feeling sufficient “interest” in the voyage; half-hinting that they are so hopelessly lost to all honorable ambition, as that in their secret souls they would rather not see whales than otherwise. But all in vain; those young Platonists have a notion that their vision is imperfect; they are short-sighted; what use, then, to strain the visual nerve? They have left their opera-glasses at home.

“Why, thou monkey,” said a harpooneer to one of these lads, “we've been cruising now hard upon three years, and thou hast not raised a whale yet. Whales are scarce as hen's teeth whenever thou art up here.” Perhaps they were; or perhaps there might have been shoals of them in the far horizon; but lulled into such an opium-like listlessness of vacant, unconscious reverie is this absent-minded youth by the blending cadence of waves with thoughts, that at last he loses his identity; takes the mystic ocean at his feet for the visible image of that deep, blue, bottomless soul, pervading mankind and nature; and every strange, half-seen, gliding, beautiful thing that eludes him; every dimly-discovered, uprising fin of some undiscernible form, seems to him the embodiment of those elusive thoughts that only people the soul by continually flitting through it. In this enchanted mood, thy spirit ebbs away to whence it came; becomes diffused through time and space; like Cranmer's sprinkled Pantheistic ashes, forming at last a part of every shore the round globe over.

There is no life in thee, now, except that rocking life imparted by a gently rolling ship; by her, borrowed from the sea; by the sea, from the inscrutable tides of God. But while this sleep, this dream is on ye, move your foot or hand an inch; slip your hold at all; and your identity comes back in horror. Over Descartian vortices you hover. And perhaps, at mid-day, in the fairest weather, with one half-throttled shriek you drop through that transparent air into the summer sea, no more to rise for ever. Heed it well, ye Pantheists!





CHAPTER 36. The Quarter-Deck.

( Enter Ahab: Then, all. )

It was not a great while after the affair of the pipe, that one morning shortly after breakfast, Ahab, as was his wont, ascended the cabin-gangway to the deck. There most sea-captains usually walk at that hour, as country gentlemen, after the same meal, take a few turns in the garden.

Soon his steady, ivory stride was heard, as to and fro he paced his old rounds, upon planks so familiar to his tread, that they were all over dented, like geological stones, with the peculiar mark of his walk. Did you fixedly gaze, too, upon that ribbed and dented brow; there also, you would see still stranger foot-prints—the foot-prints of his one unsleeping, ever-pacing thought.

But on the occasion in question, those dents looked deeper, even as his nervous step that morning left a deeper mark. And, so full of his thought was Ahab, that at every uniform turn that he made, now at the main-mast and now at the binnacle, you could almost see that thought turn in him as he turned, and pace in him as he paced; so completely possessing him, indeed, that it all but seemed the inward mould of every outer movement.

“D'ye mark him, Flask?” whispered Stubb; “the chick that's in him pecks the shell. 'Twill soon be out.”

The hours wore on;—Ahab now shut up within his cabin; anon, pacing the deck, with the same intense bigotry of purpose in his aspect.

It drew near the close of day. Suddenly he came to a halt by the bulwarks, and inserting his bone leg into the auger-hole there, and with one hand grasping a shroud, he ordered Starbuck to send everybody aft.

“Sir!” said the mate, astonished at an order seldom or never given on ship-board except in some extraordinary case.

“Send everybody aft,” repeated Ahab. “Mast-heads, there! come down!”

When the entire ship's company were assembled, and with curious and not wholly unapprehensive faces, were eyeing him, for he looked not unlike the weather horizon when a storm is coming up, Ahab, after rapidly glancing over the bulwarks, and then darting his eyes among the crew, started from his standpoint; and as though not a soul were nigh him resumed his heavy turns upon the deck. With bent head and half-slouched hat he continued to pace, unmindful of the wondering whispering among the men; till Stubb cautiously whispered to Flask, that Ahab must have summoned them there for the purpose of witnessing a pedestrian feat. But this did not last long. Vehemently pausing, he cried:—

“What do ye do when ye see a whale, men?”

“Sing out for him!” was the impulsive rejoinder from a score of clubbed voices.

“Good!” cried Ahab, with a wild approval in his tones; observing the hearty animation into which his unexpected question had so magnetically thrown them.

“And what do ye next, men?”

“Lower away, and after him!”

“And what tune is it ye pull to, men?”

“A dead whale or a stove boat!”

More and more strangely and fiercely glad and approving, grew the countenance of the old man at every shout; while the mariners began to gaze curiously at each other, as if marvelling how it was that they themselves became so excited at such seemingly purposeless questions.

But, they were all eagerness again, as Ahab, now half-revolving in his pivot-hole, with one hand reaching high up a shroud, and tightly, almost convulsively grasping it, addressed them thus:—

“All ye mast-headers have before now heard me give orders about a white whale. Look ye! d'ye see this Spanish ounce of gold?”—holding up a broad bright coin to the sun—“it is a sixteen dollar piece, men. D'ye see it? Mr. Starbuck, hand me yon top-maul.”

While the mate was getting the hammer, Ahab, without speaking, was slowly rubbing the gold piece against the skirts of his jacket, as if to heighten its lustre, and without using any words was meanwhile lowly humming to himself, producing a sound so strangely muffled and inarticulate that it seemed the mechanical humming of the wheels of his vitality in him.

Receiving the top-maul from Starbuck, he advanced towards the main-mast with the hammer uplifted in one hand, exhibiting the gold with the other, and with a high raised voice exclaiming: “Whosoever of ye raises me a white-headed whale with a wrinkled brow and a crooked jaw; whosoever of ye raises me that white-headed whale, with three holes punctured in his starboard fluke—look ye, whosoever of ye raises me that same white whale, he shall have this gold ounce, my boys!”

“Huzza! huzza!” cried the seamen, as with swinging tarpaulins they hailed the act of nailing the gold to the mast.

“It's a white whale, I say,” resumed Ahab, as he threw down the topmaul: “a white whale. Skin your eyes for him, men; look sharp for white water; if ye see but a bubble, sing out.”

All this while Tashtego, Daggoo, and Queequeg had looked on with even more intense interest and surprise than the rest, and at the mention of the wrinkled brow and crooked jaw they had started as if each was separately touched by some specific recollection.

“Captain Ahab,” said Tashtego, “that white whale must be the same that some call Moby Dick.”

“Moby Dick?” shouted Ahab. “Do ye know the white whale then, Tash?”

“Does he fan-tail a little curious, sir, before he goes down?” said the Gay-Header deliberately.

“And has he a curious spout, too,” said Daggoo, “very bushy, even for a parmacetty, and mighty quick, Captain Ahab?”

“And he have one, two, three—oh! good many iron in him hide, too, Captain,” cried Queequeg disjointedly, “all twiske-tee be-twisk, like him—him—” faltering hard for a word, and screwing his hand round and round as though uncorking a bottle—“like him—him—”

“Corkscrew!” cried Ahab, “aye, Queequeg, the harpoons lie all twisted and wrenched in him; aye, Daggoo, his spout is a big one, like a whole shock of wheat, and white as a pile of our Nantucket wool after the great annual sheep-shearing; aye, Tashtego, and he fan-tails like a split jib in a squall. Death and devils! men, it is Moby Dick ye have seen—Moby Dick—Moby Dick!”

“Captain Ahab,” said Starbuck, who, with Stubb and Flask, had thus far been eyeing his superior with increasing surprise, but at last seemed struck with a thought which somewhat explained all the wonder. “Captain Ahab, I have heard of Moby Dick—but it was not Moby Dick that took off thy leg?”

“Who told thee that?” cried Ahab; then pausing, “Aye, Starbuck; aye, my hearties all round; it was Moby Dick that dismasted me; Moby Dick that brought me to this dead stump I stand on now. Aye, aye,” he shouted with a terrific, loud, animal sob, like that of a heart-stricken moose; “Aye, aye! it was that accursed white whale that razed me; made a poor pegging lubber of me for ever and a day!” Then tossing both arms, with measureless imprecations he shouted out: “Aye, aye! and I'll chase him round Good Hope, and round the Horn, and round the Norway Maelstrom, and round perdition's flames before I give him up. And this is what ye have shipped for, men! to chase that white whale on both sides of land, and over all sides of earth, till he spouts black blood and rolls fin out. What say ye, men, will ye splice hands on it, now? I think ye do look brave.”

“Aye, aye!” shouted the harpooneers and seamen, running closer to the excited old man: “A sharp eye for the white whale; a sharp lance for Moby Dick!”

“God bless ye,” he seemed to half sob and half shout. “God bless ye, men. Steward! go draw the great measure of grog. But what's this long face about, Mr. Starbuck; wilt thou not chase the white whale? art not game for Moby Dick?”

“I am game for his crooked jaw, and for the jaws of Death too, Captain Ahab, if it fairly comes in the way of the business we follow; but I came here to hunt whales, not my commander's vengeance. How many barrels will thy vengeance yield thee even if thou gettest it, Captain Ahab? it will not fetch thee much in our Nantucket market.”

“Nantucket market! Hoot! But come closer, Starbuck; thou requirest a little lower layer. If money's to be the measurer, man, and the accountants have computed their great counting-house the globe, by girdling it with guineas, one to every three parts of an inch; then, let me tell thee, that my vengeance will fetch a great premium here! ”

“He smites his chest,” whispered Stubb, “what's that for? methinks it rings most vast, but hollow.”

“Vengeance on a dumb brute!” cried Starbuck, “that simply smote thee from blindest instinct! Madness! To be enraged with a dumb thing, Captain Ahab, seems blasphemous.”

“Hark ye yet again—the little lower layer. All visible objects, man, are but as pasteboard masks. But in each event—in the living act, the undoubted deed—there, some unknown but still reasoning thing puts forth the mouldings of its features from behind the unreasoning mask. If man will strike, strike through the mask! How can the prisoner reach outside except by thrusting through the wall? To me, the white whale is that wall, shoved near to me. Sometimes I think there's naught beyond. But 'tis enough. He tasks me; he heaps me; I see in him outrageous strength, with an inscrutable malice sinewing it. That inscrutable thing is chiefly what I hate; and be the white whale agent, or be the white whale principal, I will wreak that hate upon him. Talk not to me of blasphemy, man; I'd strike the sun if it insulted me. For could the sun do that, then could I do the other; since there is ever a sort of fair play herein, jealousy presiding over all creations. But not my master, man, is even that fair play. Who's over me? Truth hath no confines. Take off thine eye! more intolerable than fiends' glarings is a doltish stare! So, so; thou reddenest and palest; my heat has melted thee to anger-glow. But look ye, Starbuck, what is said in heat, that thing unsays itself. There are men from whom warm words are small indignity. I meant not to incense thee. Let it go. Look! see yonder Turkish cheeks of spotted tawn—living, breathing pictures painted by the sun. The Pagan leopards—the unrecking and unworshipping things, that live; and seek, and give no reasons for the torrid life they feel! The crew, man, the crew! Are they not one and all with Ahab, in this matter of the whale? See Stubb! he laughs! See yonder Chilian! he snorts to think of it. Stand up amid the general hurricane, thy one tost sapling cannot, Starbuck! And what is it? Reckon it. 'Tis but to help strike a fin; no wondrous feat for Starbuck. What is it more? From this one poor hunt, then, the best lance out of all Nantucket, surely he will not hang back, when every foremast-hand has clutched a whetstone? Ah! constrainings seize thee; I see! the billow lifts thee! Speak, but speak!—Aye, aye! thy silence, then, that voices thee. ( Aside ) Something shot from my dilated nostrils, he has inhaled it in his lungs. Starbuck now is mine; cannot oppose me now, without rebellion.”

“God keep me!—keep us all!” murmured Starbuck, lowly.

But in his joy at the enchanted, tacit acquiescence of the mate, Ahab did not hear his foreboding invocation; nor yet the low laugh from the hold; nor yet the presaging vibrations of the winds in the cordage; nor yet the hollow flap of the sails against the masts, as for a moment their hearts sank in. For again Starbuck's downcast eyes lighted up with the stubbornness of life; the subterranean laugh died away; the winds blew on; the sails filled out; the ship heaved and rolled as before. Ah, ye admonitions and warnings! why stay ye not when ye come? But rather are ye predictions than warnings, ye shadows! Yet not so much predictions from without, as verifications of the foregoing things within. For with little external to constrain us, the innermost necessities in our being, these still drive us on.

“The measure! the measure!” cried Ahab.

Receiving the brimming pewter, and turning to the harpooneers, he ordered them to produce their weapons. Then ranging them before him near the capstan, with their harpoons in their hands, while his three mates stood at his side with their lances, and the rest of the ship's company formed a circle round the group; he stood for an instant searchingly eyeing every man of his crew. But those wild eyes met his, as the bloodshot eyes of the prairie wolves meet the eye of their leader, ere he rushes on at their head in the trail of the bison; but, alas! only to fall into the hidden snare of the Indian.

“Drink and pass!” he cried, handing the heavy charged flagon to the nearest seaman. “The crew alone now drink. Round with it, round! Short draughts—long swallows, men; 'tis hot as Satan's hoof. So, so; it goes round excellently. It spiralizes in ye; forks out at the serpent-snapping eye. Well done; almost drained. That way it went, this way it comes. Hand it me—here's a hollow! Men, ye seem the years; so brimming life is gulped and gone. Steward, refill!

“Attend now, my braves. I have mustered ye all round this capstan; and ye mates, flank me with your lances; and ye harpooneers, stand there with your irons; and ye, stout mariners, ring me in, that I may in some sort revive a noble custom of my fisherman fathers before me. O men, you will yet see that—Ha! boy, come back? bad pennies come not sooner. Hand it me. Why, now, this pewter had run brimming again, wer't not thou St. Vitus' imp—away, thou ague!

“Advance, ye mates! Cross your lances full before me. Well done! Let me touch the axis.” So saying, with extended arm, he grasped the three level, radiating lances at their crossed centre; while so doing, suddenly and nervously twitched them; meanwhile, glancing intently from Starbuck to Stubb; from Stubb to Flask. It seemed as though, by some nameless, interior volition, he would fain have shocked into them the same fiery emotion accumulated within the Leyden jar of his own magnetic life. The three mates quailed before his strong, sustained, and mystic aspect. Stubb and Flask looked sideways from him; the honest eye of Starbuck fell downright.

“In vain!” cried Ahab; “but, maybe, 'tis well. For did ye three but once take the full-forced shock, then mine own electric thing, that had perhaps expired from out me. Perchance, too, it would have dropped ye dead. Perchance ye need it not. Down lances! And now, ye mates, I do appoint ye three cupbearers to my three pagan kinsmen there—yon three most honorable gentlemen and noblemen, my valiant harpooneers. Disdain the task? What, when the great Pope washes the feet of beggars, using his tiara for ewer? Oh, my sweet cardinals! your own condescension, that shall bend ye to it. I do not order ye; ye will it. Cut your seizings and draw the poles, ye harpooneers!”

Silently obeying the order, the three harpooneers now stood with the detached iron part of their harpoons, some three feet long, held, barbs up, before him.

“Stab me not with that keen steel! Cant them; cant them over! know ye not the goblet end? Turn up the socket! So, so; now, ye cup-bearers, advance. The irons! take them; hold them while I fill!” Forthwith, slowly going from one officer to the other, he brimmed the harpoon sockets with the fiery waters from the pewter.

“Now, three to three, ye stand. Commend the murderous chalices! Bestow them, ye who are now made parties to this indissoluble league. Ha! Starbuck! but the deed is done! Yon ratifying sun now waits to sit upon it. Drink, ye harpooneers! drink and swear, ye men that man the deathful whaleboat's bow—Death to Moby Dick! God hunt us all, if we do not hunt Moby Dick to his death!” The long, barbed steel goblets were lifted; and to cries and maledictions against the white whale, the spirits were simultaneously quaffed down with a hiss. Starbuck paled, and turned, and shivered. Once more, and finally, the replenished pewter went the rounds among the frantic crew; when, waving his free hand to them, they all dispersed; and Ahab retired within his cabin.
