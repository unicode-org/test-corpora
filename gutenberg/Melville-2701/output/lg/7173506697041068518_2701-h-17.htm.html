ESSUULA 86. Omukira.

Abawandiisi b’ebitontome abalala bawuubaala nga batendereza eriiso erigonvu ery’enseenene, n’ebyoya ebirabika obulungi eby’ekinyonyi ekitakka; less celestial, njaguza omukira.

Nga tubalirira omukira gwa Sperm Whale ogusinga obunene okutandikira mu kifo ekyo eky’omugongo we gukendeera okutuuka ku buwanvu bw’omuntu, gulimu ku ngulu kwagwo kwokka, obuwanvu bwa square feet ezitakka wansi wa ataano. Omubiri ogwetooloovu ogw’ekikolo kyakyo gugaziwa ne gufuuka engalo oba ebiwujjo bibiri ebigazi, ebinywevu, ebipapajjo, nga bigenda bikulukuta mpolampola okutuuka ku buwanvu obutawera yinsi emu. Ku kisambi oba ku nkulungo, ebiwujjo bino bikwatagana katono, olwo ku mabbali ne bidda emabega okuva ku birala ng’ebiwaawaatiro, ne bisigalawo ekifo ekigazi wakati. Mu kiramu kyonna ennyiriri z’obulungi tezinnyonnyolwa bulungi okusinga mu nsalo z’omwezi ez’enjuba ez’ebiwuka bino ebiyitibwa flukes. Ku kugaziwa kwayo okusinga mu whale ekuze mu bujjuvu, omukira gujja kusukka nnyo ffuuti amakumi abiri mu buwanvu.

Ekitundu kyonna kirabika nga kitanda kya webbed ekinene eky’emisuwa egya welded; naye ogisalamu, n’osanga nti ebitundu bisatu eby’enjawulo bye bigikola: —waggulu, wakati, ne wansi. Ebiwuzi ebiri mu layeri eya waggulu n’eya wansi, biwanvu era nga biwanvu; ezo ez’omu makkati, nnyimpi nnyo, era nga zidduka mu musalaba wakati wa layers ez’ebweru. Ensengekera eno ey’obusatu, ng’ekintu ekirala kyonna, egaba amaanyi eri omukira. Eri omuyizi wa bbugwe ow’edda ow’Abaruumi, oluwuzi olw’omu makkati lujja kuwa okufaanagana okwewuunyisa n’omusomo omugonvu ogwa tile bulijjo ogukyukakyuka n’ejjinja mu bisigadde ebyo ebyewuunyisa eby’edda, era awatali kubuusabuusa biyamba nnyo mu maanyi amangi ag’amayinja.

Naye ng’amaanyi gano amanene ag’ekitundu mu mukira ogw’emisuwa tegamala, ekitundu ekinene kyonna ekya leviathan kilukibwa waggulu n’ekiwujjo n’oluwuzi lw’ebinywa n’obuwuzi, ebiyita ku buli ludda lw’ekiwato ne bidduka wansi mu biwujjo, mu ngeri etategeerekeka okwegatta nabo, era okusinga okuyamba mu maanyi gaabwe; bwe kityo mu mukira amaanyi agatali gapima agakwatagana ag’ennyanja ekika kya whale yonna galabika nga gakuŋŋaanyiziddwa okutuuka ku ssa. Could annihilation occur to matter, kino kye kyali ekintu okukikola.

Era kino —amaanyi gaayo ag’ekyewuunyo, n’akatono gatera okulemesa okufukamira okw’ekitiibwa okw’entambula zaayo; awali obuwere obw’obwangu obukulukuta okuyita mu Titanism ey’amaanyi. Okwawukana ku ekyo, entambula ezo zifuna obulungi bwazo obusinga okutiisa okuva mu kyo. Amaanyi amatuufu tegakosa bulungi oba enkolagana, naye gatera okubuwa; era mu buli kintu ekirabika obulungi ennyo, amaanyi galina kinene kye gakola n’obulogo. Ggyako emisuwa egyasibiddwa nga wonna girabika nga gyakutuka okuva ku mabbaale mu Hercules eyayolwa, era okusikiriza kwayo kwandibadde kuweddewo. Eckerman omunyiikivu bwe yali asitula ekitambaala kya bafuta okuva ku mulambo gwa Goethe ogwali obwereere, yazitoowererwa ekifuba ekinene ennyo eky’omusajja, ekyali kirabika ng’ekisenge ky’obuwanguzi bw’Abaruumi. Angelo bw’asiiga ne Katonda Kitaffe mu ngeri y’omuntu, ssaako akabonero ku bugumu bwe buliwo. Era kyonna kye bayinza okubikkula ku kwagala okw’obwakatonda mu Mwana, ebifaananyi by’Abayitale ebigonvu, ebizinguluddwa, eby’ekika kya hermaphroditical, endowooza ye mwe ebadde esinga okuteekebwamu obulungi; ebifaananyi bino, nga bwe bibulamu amaanyi gonna, tebirina kye biraga maanyi gonna, wabula ekitali kirungi kyokka, eky’ekikazi eky’okugondera n’okugumiikiriza, nga ku mikono gyonna kikkirizibwa, bikola empisa ennungi ez’enjawulo ez’omugaso ez’enjigiriza ze.

Bw’etyo bw’obutakyukakyuka obutonotono obw’ekitundu ky’omubiri kye nkwata, ne kiba nti oba nga bukozesebwa mu mizannyo, oba mu bunyiikivu, oba mu busungu, ka kibeere mu muudu ki, okufukamira kwakyo tekukyuka kulagibwa ekisa ekisukkiridde. Mu yo tewali mukono gwa fairy guyinza kugusukkuluma.

Entambula ennene ttaano za njawulo ku kyo. Ekisooka, bwe kikozesebwa ng’ekiwaawaatiro okusobola okugenda mu maaso; Ekyokubiri, bwe kikozesebwa ng’omuggo mu lutalo; Ekyokusatu, mu kusenya; Eky’okuna, mu kukola lobtailing; Eky’okutaano, mu peaking flukes.

Ekisooka: Olw’okuba omukira gwa Leviathan gubeera nga guyimiridde mu kifo we gubeera, gukola mu ngeri ya njawulo ku mikira gy’ebitonde ebirala byonna eby’omu nnyanja. Tekiwuguka n’akatono. Mu muntu oba mu byennyanja, okuwuubaala kabonero akalaga nti omuntu ali wansi. Eri ennyanja ekika kya whale, omukira gwayo gwe gwokka gwe gusitula. Scroll-wise coiled forwards beneath the body, and then rapidly sprung backwards, kino kye kiwa nti singular darting, leaping motion eri ekisolo ekinene nga kiwuga n’obusungu. Ebiwaawaatiro bye eby’ebbali bikola ku kuvuga bwokka.

Ekyokubiri: Kikulu katono, nti wadde nga sperm whale emu erwanyisa sperm whale endala yokka n’omutwe n’akawanga, wadde kiri kityo, mu ntalo ze n’omuntu, okusinga era mu ngeri ey’okunyooma akozesa omukira gwe. Mu kukuba eryato, akoona mangu ebiwujjo bye okuva ku lyo, era ekikonde ekyo kikubwa kudda mabega kwokka. Singa ekolebwa mu mpewo etaziyizibwa naddala singa ekka okutuuka ku kabonero kaayo, olwo stroke eba tesobola kuziyizibwa. Tewali mbavu za muntu oba lyato zisobola kugumira. Obulokozi bwo bwokka buli mu kukyewala; naye bwe kiba nga kijja ku mabbali nga kiyita mu mazzi agavuganya, olwo ekitundu kiva ku kuwuuma okutono okw’eryato erya whale-boat, n’obugumu bw’ebintu byalyo, olubavu olwatika oba olubaawo olukutuse oba bbiri, ekika ky’omusono mu mabbali, okutwalira awamu kiba ekivaamu ekisinga okuba eky’amaanyi. Ebikonde bino eby’ebbali ebinyweredde mu mazzi bitera nnyo okufunibwa mu by’obuvubi, ne kiba nti bitwalibwa ng’okuzannya kw’omwana omuto. Omu ayambula ffulaayi, ekituli ne kiyimirira.

Ekyokusatu: Sisobola kukiraga, naye kirabika gyendi, nti mu whale okutegeera kw’okukwata kubeera mu mukira; kubanga mu nsonga eno waliwo ekintu ekiwooma mu kyo ekyenkana n’obulungi bw’ekikolo ky’enjovu kyokka. Obuwoomi buno okusinga bweyolekera mu kikolwa ky’okusenya, nga mu buwombeefu obw’obuwala ennyanja ekika kya whale n’empola ezimu ennyogovu etambuza ebiwujjo bye ebinene ennyo okuva ku ludda olumu okudda ku lulala ku ngulu w’ennyanja; era bw’awulira ng’enviiri z’omuvubi, zisanze omuvubi oyo, enviiri ne byonna. Nga mugonvu nnyo mu kukwata okwo okusooka! Singa omukira guno yalina amaanyi gonna ag’okukwata, nnandibadde nzirowooza butereevu ku njovu ya Darmonodes eyatera ennyo okugenda mu katale k’ebimuli, era n’okulamusa okutono n’eyanjula ennyindo eri abawala, n’oluvannyuma ne nweeweeta zoni zaabwe. Ku nsonga ezisukka mu emu, kya kusaasira nti ennyanja ekika kya whale terina mpisa eno ennungi ekwata mu mukira gwayo; kubanga mpulidde enjovu endala, bwe yafuna ebisago mu lutalo, n’ekoona okwetooloola omugogo gwayo n’eggyayo omusipi.

Eky’okuna: Ng’abba nga tomanyi ku nnyanja ekika kya whale mu bukuumi obw’okulowooza wakati mu nnyanja ezitali za bulijjo, omusanga nga tafukamidde olw’obuzito obunene obw’ekitiibwa kye, era ng’alinga embuzi y’embuzi, azannyira ku nnyanja ng’alinga omuliro. Naye still olaba amaanyi ge mu muzannyo gwe. Engalo ezigazi ez’omukira gwe zipepeya waggulu mu bbanga; olwo ng’ekuba ku ngulu, okukonziba okw’okubwatuka kuwulikika okumala mayiro eziwera. Kumpi wandirowoozezza nti emmundu ennene yali efulumye; era singa weetegereza ekimuli ekitangaavu eky’omukka okuva mu spiracle ku nkomerero ye endala, wandirowoozezza nti ogwo gwe mukka oguva mu touch-hole.

Eky’okutaano: Nga bwe kiri mu mbeera eya bulijjo ey’okutengejja eya leviathan ebiwujjo bwe bigalamira wansi nnyo ku ddaala ly’omugongo gwe, olwo ne biva ddala wansi w’amazzi; naye bw’aba anaatera okunnyika mu buziba, ebiwujjo bye byonna ebirina waakiri ffuuti amakumi asatu ez’omubiri gwe bisuulibwa nga bigoloddwa mu bbanga, era bwe bityo ne bisigala nga bikankana akaseera katono, okutuusa lwe bikuba wansi nga tebirabika. Ng’oggyeeko okumenya okw’amaanyi —awalala wonna okunnyonnyolwa —entikko eno ey’ebiwuka ebiyitibwa whale’s flukes oboolyawo kye kifo ekisinga obukulu ekirabibwa mu butonde bwonna obulina obulamu. Mu buziba obutali wansi omukira omunene ennyo gulabika nga gunyaga mu ngeri ey’okusannyalala ku ggulu erisinga okuba waggulu. Kale mu birooto, ndabye Sitaani ow’ekitiibwa ng’asika enjala ye ennene ennyo eyali ebonyaabonyezebwa okuva mu nnimi z’omuliro eza Baltic of Hell. Naye mu kutunuulira ebifo ng’ebyo, byonna biba mu muudu gy’olimu; bwe muba mu Dantean, sitaani zijja kukutuukako; bwe kiba nga mu ekyo ekya Isaaya, bamalayika abakulu. Nga nnyimiridde ku mutwe gw’emmeeri yange ng’enjuba evaayo eyakaayakana eggulu n’ennyanja, lumu nnalaba ekisibo ekinene eky’ennyanja ekika kya whale mu buvanjuba, nga zonna zoolekera enjuba, era okumala akaseera nga zikankana mu kivvulu n’amaloboozi ag’entikko. Nga bwe kyalabika gyendi mu kiseera ekyo, ekifaananyi ekinene bwe kiti eky’okusinza bakatonda tekirabibwangako, ne mu Buperusi, amaka g’abasinza omuliro. Nga Ptolemy Philopater bwe yawa obujulizi ku njovu ya Afirika, olwo nange ne nzijulira ku nnyanja ekika kya whale, nga mmugamba nti y’asinga okwewaayo mu bitonde byonna. Kubanga okusinziira ku Kabaka Juba, enjovu z’amagye ez’edda zaateranga okutendereza enkya nga zisituddwa emigogo gyazo mu kasirise akasinga obunene.

Okugerageranya okw’omukisa mu ssuula eno, wakati w’ennyanja ekika kya whale n’enjovu, okutuuka ku nsonga ezimu ez’omukira gw’ekimu n’ekikolo ky’endala, tekirina kutera kuteeka bitundu ebyo ebibiri ebikontana ku bwenkanya, n’okusingawo ebitonde bye bibeera mu kulondako. Kubanga ng’enjovu esinga amaanyi bw’eba ensolo ekika kya terrier eri Leviathan, bwe kityo bwe kigeraageranyizibwa ku mukira gwa Leviathan, omugogo gwayo guba kikolo kya lily. Ekikonde ekyasinga okuba eky’entiisa okuva ku kikonge ky’enjovu kyali nga ttaapu ey’okuzannya ey’omuwagizi, bw’ogeraageranya n’okunyiga n’okugwa okutali kwa kipimo okw’ebiwujjo ebizitowa eby’ennyonyi ekika kya sperm whale, nga mu mirundi egiddiŋŋana, emu oluvannyuma lw’endala zisuula amaato gonna n’amaloboozi gaago gonna n’abakozi empewo, nnyo ng’Omuyindi omuzannyi wa juggler bw’akuba emipiira gye.*

*Wadde ng’okugeraageranya kwonna mu ngeri y’obungi obw’enjawulo wakati w’ennyanja ekika kya whale n’enjovu kwa busirusiru, okuva mu ngeri eyo enjovu gy’eyimiridde mu ngeri y’emu ennyo eri enjovu ng’embwa gy’ekola ku njovu; wadde kiri kityo, tewali kwagala nsonga ezimu ezifaanagana ez’okwewuunya; mu bino mwe muli n’omusulo. Kimanyiddwa bulungi nti enjovu ejja kutera okusika amazzi oba enfuufu mu kikonge kyayo, n’oluvannyuma ng’agisitula, n’egifuuwa mu mugga.

Gy’okoma okulowooza ku mukira guno ogw’amaanyi, gye nkoma okweraliikirira obutasobola kugyogera. Oluusi wabaawo obubonero mu kyo, wadde nga bwandisiimye bulungi omukono gw’omuntu, busigala nga tebunnyonnyolwa ddala. Mu kisibo ekinene, ekyewuunyisa ennyo, oluusi n’oluusi, mulimu obubonero buno obw’ekyama, ne mpulira abayizzi abalangirira nti bufaanagana n’obubonero n’obubonero bwa Free-Mason; nti ennyanja ekika kya whale, ddala, mu nkola zino yayogera n’ensi mu magezi. Era tewali kwagala ntambula ndala za whale mu mubiri gwe okutwalira awamu, nga zijjudde ebyewuunyisa, era nga tezivunaanyizibwa eri oyo asinga obumanyirivu mu kumulumba. Dissect him how I may, olwo, nze naye ngenda skin deep; Simumanyi, era sijja kumumanya. Naye bwemba nga simanyi wadde omukira gwa whale eno, omutwe gwe gutegeera ntya? ebisingawo nnyo, okutegeera otya ffeesi ye, so nga ffeesi talina? Ojja kulaba ebitundu byange eby’emabega, omukira gwange, alabika ng’agamba, naye amaaso gange tegalirabibwa. Naye sisobola kutegeera ddala bitundu bye eby’emabega; and hint what he will about his face, nddamu ngamba nti talina maaso.





ESSUULA 87. Ekibinja Ekinene (Grand Armada).

Ekizinga ekiwanvu era ekifunda ekya Malacca, ekigenda mu bukiikaddyo-buvanjuba okuva mu bitundu bya Birmah, kye kisinga okubeera mu bukiikaddyo bwa Asiya yonna. Mu layini egenda mu maaso okuva ku kizinga ekyo, biwanvuwa ebizinga ebiwanvu ebya Sumatra, Java, Bally, ne Timor; nga zino, n’endala nnyingi, zikola ekiwujjo ekinene oba bbugwe, mu buwanvu nga zigatta Asiya ne Australia, era nga zaawulamu ennyanja ya Buyindi empanvu etamenyeddwa okuva ku bizinga eby’omu buvanjuba ebirimu ebikondo ebinene. Bbugwe ono afumitiddwa emyalo egiwerako egy’ekika kya sally-ports okusobola okwanguyiza emmeeri n’ennyanja ekika kya whale; okweyoleka mu byo mulimu ebiwonvu bya Sunda ne Malacca. Ku mugga Sunda, okusinga, amaato agagenda e China okuva mu maserengeta, gafuluma mu nnyanja za China.

Emigga egyo emifunda egya Sunda gyawula Sumatra ne Java; era ng’eyimiridde wakati mu bbugwe oyo ennene ennyo ey’ebizinga, ng’ewaniriddwa olusozi olwo olwa kiragala olugumu, olwamanyibwa abavubi nga Java Head; tezikwatagana katono n’omulyango ogw’omu makkati oguggukira mu bwakabaka obumu obunene obuliko bbugwe: era bw’olowooza ku bugagga obutaggwaawo obw’eby’akaloosa, ne silika, n’amayinja ag’omuwendo, ne zaabu, n’amasanga, ebizinga lukumi eby’ennyanja eyo ey’obuvanjuba bye bigaggawaza, kirabika enteekateeka ey’amaanyi ey’obutonde, nti eby’obugagga ng’ebyo, olw’okutondebwa kw’ensi yennyini, waakiri birina okubeera n’endabika, wadde nga tebikola, ey’okukuumibwa okuva mu nsi ey’amaserengeta ekwata byonna. Emyalo gy’omugga Sunda tegirina bigo ebyo ebifuga ebikuuma emiryango egiyingira mu Mediterranean, Baltic, ne Propontis. Okwawukanako n’Abadan, abantu bano ab’omu Buvanjuba tebaagala kussa kitiibwa mu ngeri ya kimpowooze eri emmeeri ezikka waggulu okuva mu kutambula okutaggwaawo okw’emmeeri nga empewo tennabaawo, okumala ebyasa bingi ebiyise, ekiro n’emisana, nga ziyise wakati w’ebizinga bya Sumatra ne Java, nga zitikkiddwa emigugu emigugu egisinga okubeera egy’ebbeeyi mu buvanjuba. Naye wadde nga balekawo mu ddembe omukolo nga guno, tebakyegaana n’akatono okwewozaako kwabwe ku musolo ogusingako obunywevu.

Time out of mind the piratical proas of the Malay, nga beekukumye wakati mu bizinga ebitono ebisiikirize n’ebizinga ebitonotono eby’e Sumatra, bafubutuse ku mmeeri eziyita mu biwonvu, nga zisaba nnyo omusolo ku nsonga y’amafumu gaabwe. Wadde ng’okubonerezebwa okw’omusaayi okuddiŋŋana kwe bafunye mu mikono gy’emmeeri z’Abazungu ezitambulira ku nnyanja, obuvumu bw’abavuzi bano aba corsairs ennaku zino bubadde bunyigirizibwako katono; naye, ne mu kiseera kino, oluusi n’oluusi tuwulira ku mmeeri z’Abangereza n’ez’Abamerika, nga, mu mazzi ago, zibadde zirinnya era ne zinyagibwa awatali kwejjusa.

Olw’empewo ennungi era empya, kati emmeeri ya Pequod yali esemberera emigga gino; Akabu ng’agenderera okuyita mu zo mu nnyanja Javan, n’ava awo, ng’agenda mu bukiikakkono, ng’agenda mu mazzi agamanyiddwa nti Sperm Whale gatera okutambulira wano ne wali, n’aseenya ku lukalu olw’ebizinga by’e Philippines, n’afuna olubalama lw’ennyanja ewala ennyo olwa Japan, mu budde sizoni ennene ey’okuvuba ennyanja ekika kya whale eyo. Mu ngeri zino, Pequod eyeetooloola yandibadde esenya kumpi ebifo byonna ebimanyiddwa eby’ensi yonna eby’okusaabala Sperm Whale, nga tennakka ku Line mu Pacific; Akabu, wadde nga buli wamu yalemererwa okumuyigga, n’abalirira nnyo okuwa Moby Dick olutalo, mu nnyanja gye yali asinga okumanyibwa okugendako; era mu sizoni gy’ayinza okusinga okulowoozebwa nti agiwunya.

Naye kati otya? mu kunoonya kuno okwa zooni, Akabu takwata ku ttaka lyonna? abakozi be banywa empewo? Mazima ddala, ajja kuyimirira ku mazzi. Nedda. Okumala ebbanga ddene, kati, enjuba eddukira mu circus ebadde eddukira munda mu mpeta ye ey’omuliro, era tekyetaaga kuyimirizaawo wabula ekiri mu ye. Bwe kityo Akabu. Kino nakyo ssaako akabonero mu muvubi w’ennyanja ekika kya whaler. So nga ebisenge ebirala bitikkibwa wansi ebintu eby’ebweru, okukyusibwa ne bitwalibwa ku mwalo gw’amawanga amalala; emmeeri ya whale etaayaaya mu nsi yonna tetwala migugu wabula ye kennyini n’abakozi, ebyokulwanyisa byabwe n’ebyo bye baagala. Alina ebirimu ennyanja yonna ebikubiddwa mu bucupa mu kifo kye ekimala. Akubwa ballast n’ebintu ebikozesebwa mu bulamu obwa bulijjo; si wamu n’embizzi-lead ne kentledge ezitakozesebwa. Asitula amazzi ag’emyaka mingi mu ye. Amazzi amayonjo amakadde aga prime Nantucket; nga kino, bwe kiba nga kitengejja emyaka esatu, Nantucketer, mu Pacific, ayagala nnyo okunywa nga amazzi ag’omunnyo tegannabaawo, naye eggulo yasitudde mu bidomola, okuva mu nzizi z’e Peru oba Buyindi. Kyova olaba nti, wadde ng’emmeeri endala ziyinza okuba nga zagenda e China okuva e New York, ne ziddayo nate, nga zikwata ku myalo egiwera, emmeeri ya whale, mu bbanga eryo lyonna, eyinza okuba nga teyalaba mpeke emu ey’ettaka; abakozi be nga tebalaba musajja wabula abavubi abatengejja nga bo. Kale ekyo wabatwala amawulire nti amataba amalala gazze; baali bazzeemu kyokka —“Kale, abalenzi, laato lye liri!”

Kati, nga Sperm Whales nnyingi bwe zaali ziwambiddwa ku lubalama lw’amaserengeta ga Java, mu kitundu ekiriraanyewo okumpi ne Straits of Sunda; mazima ddala, ng’ettaka erisinga obungi, eryetooloola, okutwalira awamu abavubi baali bamanyi ng’ekifo ekirungi ennyo eky’okusaabala; n’olwekyo, Pequod bwe yeeyongera okufuna ku Java Head, abatunuulizi baali batenderezebwa enfunda n’enfunda, era ne babuulirirwa okusigala nga batunula nnyo. Naye wadde ng’enjazi eza kiragala ez’enkindu ez’ensi mu bbanga ttono zaali ziyimiridde ku mutaasa ogwa ddyo, era n’ennyindo ezisanyuse sinamoni omuggya yafuumuulwa mu bbanga, naye tewali n’emu eyayogerwako. Kumpi nga yeegaana ebirowoozo byonna eby’okugwa mu muzannyo gwonna wano, emmeeri yali enaatera okuyingira mu biwonvu, okukaaba okw’ennono n’okusanyuka bwe kwawulirwa okuva waggulu, era ere long a spectacle of singular magnificence us saluted us.

Naye wano kibeere premised, nti olw’omulimu ogutakooye gwe babadde bayigganyizibwako mu nnyanja zonna ennya, Sperm Whales, mu kifo ky’okusaabala kumpi bulijjo mu kkampuni entonotono ezeetongodde, nga mu biseera eby’edda, kati zitera okusisinkana mu ebisibo ebinene, oluusi nga bizingiramu ekibiina ekinene ennyo, ne kiba nti kumpi kyandirabise ng’amawanga amangi ku go gaalayidde okukolagana n’endagaano ey’ekitiibwa olw’okuyambagana n’okukuumagana. Ku kugatta kuno okwa Sperm Whale mu kabangali ennene ennyo bwe zityo, kiyinza okuteekebwako embeera nti ne mu bifo ebisinga obulungi eby’okusaabala, kati oluusi muyinza okusaabala okumala wiiki n’emyezi nga muli wamu, nga temuyaniriziddwa n’akatono; n’oluvannyuma n’alamusibwa mu bwangu n’ekyo oluusi ekirabika ng’enkumi n’enkumi.

Not seldom in the rapid vicissitudes of the chase, this natural line, with the maternal end loose, becomes entangled with the hempen one, so that the cub is thereby trapped. Some of the subtlest secrets of the seas seemed divulged to us in this enchanted pond. We saw young Leviathan amours in the deep.*

*The sperm whale, as with all other species of the Leviathan, but unlike most other fish, breeds indifferently at all seasons; after a gestation which may probably be set down at nine months, producing but one at a time; though in some few known instances giving birth to an Esau and Jacob:—a contingency provided for in suckling by two teats, curiously situated, one on each side of the anus; but the breasts themselves extend upwards from that. When by chance these precious parts in a nursing whale are cut by the hunter's lance, the mother's pouring milk and blood rivallingly discolour the sea for rods. The milk is very sweet and rich; it has been tasted by man; it might do well with strawberries. When overflowing with mutual esteem, the whales salute more hominum .

And thus, though surrounded by circle upon circle of consternations and affrights, did these inscrutable creatures at the centre freely and fearlessly indulge in all peaceful concernments; yea, serenely revelled in dalliance and delight. But even so, amid the tornadoed Atlantic of my being, do I myself still for ever centrally disport in mute calm; and while ponderous planets of unwaning woe revolve round me, deep down and deep inland there I still bathe me in eternal mildness of joy.

Meanwhile, as we thus lay entranced, the occasional sudden frantic spectacles in the distance evinced the activity of the other boats, still engaged in drugging the whales on the frontier of the host; or possibly carrying on the war within the first circle, where abundance of room and some convenient retreats were afforded them. But the sight of the enraged drugged whales now and then blindly darting to and fro across the circles, was nothing to what at last met our eyes. It is sometimes the custom when fast to a whale more than commonly powerful and alert, to seek to hamstring him, as it were, by sundering or maiming his gigantic tail-tendon. It is done by darting a short-handled cutting-spade, to which is attached a rope for hauling it back again. A whale wounded (as we afterwards learned) in this part, but not effectually, as it seemed, had broken away from the boat, carrying along with him half of the harpoon line; and in the extraordinary agony of the wound, he was now dashing among the revolving circles like the lone mounted desperado Arnold, at the battle of Saratoga, carrying dismay wherever he went.

But agonizing as was the wound of this whale, and an appalling spectacle enough, any way; yet the peculiar horror with which he seemed to inspire the rest of the herd, was owing to a cause which at first the intervening distance obscured from us. But at length we perceived that by one of the unimaginable accidents of the fishery, this whale had become entangled in the harpoon-line that he towed; he had also run away with the cutting-spade in him; and while the free end of the rope attached to that weapon, had permanently caught in the coils of the harpoon-line round his tail, the cutting-spade itself had worked loose from his flesh. So that tormented to madness, he was now churning through the water, violently flailing with his flexible tail, and tossing the keen spade about him, wounding and murdering his own comrades.

This terrific object seemed to recall the whole herd from their stationary fright. First, the whales forming the margin of our lake began to crowd a little, and tumble against each other, as if lifted by half spent billows from afar; then the lake itself began faintly to heave and swell; the submarine bridal-chambers and nurseries vanished; in more and more contracting orbits the whales in the more central circles began to swim in thickening clusters. Yes, the long calm was departing. A low advancing hum was soon heard; and then like to the tumultuous masses of block-ice when the great river Hudson breaks up in Spring, the entire host of whales came tumbling upon their inner centre, as if to pile themselves up in one common mountain. Instantly Starbuck and Queequeg changed places; Starbuck taking the stern.

“Oars! Oars!” he intensely whispered, seizing the helm—“gripe your oars, and clutch your souls, now! My God, men, stand by! Shove him off, you Queequeg—the whale there!—prick him!—hit him! Stand up—stand up, and stay so! Spring, men—pull, men; never mind their backs—scrape them!—scrape away!”

The boat was now all but jammed between two vast black bulks, leaving a narrow Dardanelles between their long lengths. But by desperate endeavor we at last shot into a temporary opening; then giving way rapidly, and at the same time earnestly watching for another outlet. After many similar hair-breadth escapes, we at last swiftly glided into what had just been one of the outer circles, but now crossed by random whales, all violently making for one centre. This lucky salvation was cheaply purchased by the loss of Queequeg's hat, who, while standing in the bows to prick the fugitive whales, had his hat taken clean from his head by the air-eddy made by the sudden tossing of a pair of broad flukes close by.

Riotous and disordered as the universal commotion now was, it soon resolved itself into what seemed a systematic movement; for having clumped together at last in one dense body, they then renewed their onward flight with augmented fleetness. Further pursuit was useless; but the boats still lingered in their wake to pick up what drugged whales might be dropped astern, and likewise to secure one which Flask had killed and waifed. The waif is a pennoned pole, two or three of which are carried by every boat; and which, when additional game is at hand, are inserted upright into the floating body of a dead whale, both to mark its place on the sea, and also as token of prior possession, should the boats of any other ship draw near.

The result of this lowering was somewhat illustrative of that sagacious saying in the Fishery,—the more whales the less fish. Of all the drugged whales only one was captured. The rest contrived to escape for the time, but only to be taken, as will hereafter be seen, by some other craft than the Pequod.





CHAPTER 88. Schools and Schoolmasters.

The previous chapter gave account of an immense body or herd of Sperm Whales, and there was also then given the probable cause inducing those vast aggregations.

Now, though such great bodies are at times encountered, yet, as must have been seen, even at the present day, small detached bands are occasionally observed, embracing from twenty to fifty individuals each. Such bands are known as schools. They generally are of two sorts; those composed almost entirely of females, and those mustering none but young vigorous males, or bulls, as they are familiarly designated.

In cavalier attendance upon the school of females, you invariably see a male of full grown magnitude, but not old; who, upon any alarm, evinces his gallantry by falling in the rear and covering the flight of his ladies. In truth, this gentleman is a luxurious Ottoman, swimming about over the watery world, surroundingly accompanied by all the solaces and endearments of the harem. The contrast between this Ottoman and his concubines is striking; because, while he is always of the largest leviathanic proportions, the ladies, even at full growth, are not more than one-third of the bulk of an average-sized male. They are comparatively delicate, indeed; I dare say, not to exceed half a dozen yards round the waist. Nevertheless, it cannot be denied, that upon the whole they are hereditarily entitled to en bon point .

It is very curious to watch this harem and its lord in their indolent ramblings. Like fashionables, they are for ever on the move in leisurely search of variety. You meet them on the Line in time for the full flower of the Equatorial feeding season, having just returned, perhaps, from spending the summer in the Northern seas, and so cheating summer of all unpleasant weariness and warmth. By the time they have lounged up and down the promenade of the Equator awhile, they start for the Oriental waters in anticipation of the cool season there, and so evade the other excessive temperature of the year.

When serenely advancing on one of these journeys, if any strange suspicious sights are seen, my lord whale keeps a wary eye on his interesting family. Should any unwarrantably pert young Leviathan coming that way, presume to draw confidentially close to one of the ladies, with what prodigious fury the Bashaw assails him, and chases him away! High times, indeed, if unprincipled young rakes like him are to be permitted to invade the sanctity of domestic bliss; though do what the Bashaw will, he cannot keep the most notorious Lothario out of his bed; for, alas! all fish bed in common. As ashore, the ladies often cause the most terrible duels among their rival admirers; just so with the whales, who sometimes come to deadly battle, and all for love. They fence with their long lower jaws, sometimes locking them together, and so striving for the supremacy like elks that warringly interweave their antlers. Not a few are captured having the deep scars of these encounters,—furrowed heads, broken teeth, scolloped fins; and in some instances, wrenched and dislocated mouths.

But supposing the invader of domestic bliss to betake himself away at the first rush of the harem's lord, then is it very diverting to watch that lord. Gently he insinuates his vast bulk among them again and revels there awhile, still in tantalizing vicinity to young Lothario, like pious Solomon devoutly worshipping among his thousand concubines. Granting other whales to be in sight, the fishermen will seldom give chase to one of these Grand Turks; for these Grand Turks are too lavish of their strength, and hence their unctuousness is small. As for the sons and the daughters they beget, why, those sons and daughters must take care of themselves; at least, with only the maternal help. For like certain other omnivorous roving lovers that might be named, my Lord Whale has no taste for the nursery, however much for the bower; and so, being a great traveller, he leaves his anonymous babies all over the world; every baby an exotic. In good time, nevertheless, as the ardour of youth declines; as years and dumps increase; as reflection lends her solemn pauses; in short, as a general lassitude overtakes the sated Turk; then a love of ease and virtue supplants the love for maidens; our Ottoman enters upon the impotent, repentant, admonitory stage of life, forswears, disbands the harem, and grown to an exemplary, sulky old soul, goes about all alone among the meridians and parallels saying his prayers, and warning each young Leviathan from his amorous errors.

Now, as the harem of whales is called by the fishermen a school, so is the lord and master of that school technically known as the schoolmaster. It is therefore not in strict character, however admirably satirical, that after going to school himself, he should then go abroad inculcating not what he learned there, but the folly of it. His title, schoolmaster, would very naturally seem derived from the name bestowed upon the harem itself, but some have surmised that the man who first thus entitled this sort of Ottoman whale, must have read the memoirs of Vidocq, and informed himself what sort of a country-schoolmaster that famous Frenchman was in his younger days, and what was the nature of those occult lessons he inculcated into some of his pupils.

The same secludedness and isolation to which the schoolmaster whale betakes himself in his advancing years, is true of all aged Sperm Whales. Almost universally, a lone whale—as a solitary Leviathan is called—proves an ancient one. Like venerable moss-bearded Daniel Boone, he will have no one near him but Nature herself; and her he takes to wife in the wilderness of waters, and the best of wives she is, though she keeps so many moody secrets.

The schools composing none but young and vigorous males, previously mentioned, offer a strong contrast to the harem schools. For while those female whales are characteristically timid, the young males, or forty-barrel-bulls, as they call them, are by far the most pugnacious of all Leviathans, and proverbially the most dangerous to encounter; excepting those wondrous grey-headed, grizzled whales, sometimes met, and these will fight you like grim fiends exasperated by a penal gout.

The Forty-barrel-bull schools are larger than the harem schools. Like a mob of young collegians, they are full of fight, fun, and wickedness, tumbling round the world at such a reckless, rollicking rate, that no prudent underwriter would insure them any more than he would a riotous lad at Yale or Harvard. They soon relinquish this turbulence though, and when about three-fourths grown, break up, and separately go about in quest of settlements, that is, harems.

Another point of difference between the male and female schools is still more characteristic of the sexes. Say you strike a Forty-barrel-bull—poor devil! all his comrades quit him. But strike a member of the harem school, and her companions swim around her with every token of concern, sometimes lingering so near her and so long, as themselves to fall a prey.
