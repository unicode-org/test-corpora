ESSUULA 74. Omutwe gwa Sperm Whale —Endowooza ey’enjawulo.

Wano, kati, waliwo envubu ennene bbiri, nga ziteeka emitwe gyazo wamu; tubegatteko, tuteeke wamu ebyaffe.

Ku kibinja ekinene eky’ebiwuka ebiyitibwa folio leviathans, Sperm Whale ne Right Whale bye bisinga okweyoleka. Zino ze whales zokka abantu z’ayigga bulijjo. Eri Nantucketer, ziraga ebisukkiridde ebibiri eby’ebika byonna ebimanyiddwa eby’ennyanja ekika kya whale. Nga enjawulo ey’ebweru wakati waabwe bw’esinga okweyoleka mu mitwe gyabwe; era ng’omutwe gwa buli emu akaseera kano kawanikiddwa ku ludda lwa Pequod; era nga bwe tuyinza okugenda mu ddembe okuva ku kimu okudda ku kirala, nga tumala kulinnya ku ddeeke:—wa, njagala okumanya, w’onoofuna omukisa omulungi okusoma eby’obutonde eby’omugaso okusinga wano?

Mu kusooka, ekubwa olw’enjawulo ey’awamu eriwo wakati w’emitwe gino. Bombi ba massive ekimala mu muntu yenna ow’omunda; naye waliwo ekigerageranyo ky’okubala ekimu mu Sperm Whale’s ekya Right Whale’s kye kibulamu eky’ennaku. Waliwo empisa ezisingawo mu mutwe gwa Sperm Whale. Nga bw’okiraba, mu butayagala muwaayo obusukkulumu obw’ekitalo gy’ali, mu ngeri y’ekitiibwa ekibunye. Mu mbeera eriwo kati, era, ekitiibwa kino kyongerwako langi y’entungo n’omunnyo ey’omutwe gwe ku ntikko, n’awa akabonero k’emyaka egy’obukulu n’obumanyirivu obunene. Mu bufunze, ye mu by’ekikugu abavubi gwe bayita “envubu ey’omutwe enzirugavu.”

Kati ka twetegereze ekitali kya njawulo nnyo mu mitwe gino —kwe kugamba, ebitundu ebibiri ebisinga obukulu, eriiso n’okutu. Ewala emabega ku ludda lw’omutwe, era wansi wansi, okumpi n’enkoona y’akawanga k’ennyanja ekika kya whale, bw’onoonya mu bufunda, ku nkomerero ojja kulaba eriiso eritaliiko nviiri, lye wandirowoozezza okuba eriiso ly’endogoyi ento; kale okuva mu kigerageranyo kyonna kiri ku bunene bw’omutwe.

Kati, okuva mu mbeera eno ey’enjawulo ey’amaaso ga whale ku mabbali, kyeyoleka lwatu nti tayinza kulaba kintu ekiri mu maaso ddala, nga tekisukka ku ky’asobola kulaba ddala emabega. Mu kigambo kimu, ekifo amaaso ga whale we gali kikwatagana n’amatu g’omuntu; era oyinza okwerowoozaako, ku bubwo, engeri gye kyandikuyiseemu, wapima ebintu ku mabbali ng’oyita mu matu go. Wandikizudde nti osobola okulagira diguli nga amakumi asatu zokka ez’okulaba nga bukyali ku ludda olugolokofu olw’okulaba; n’endala nga amakumi asatu emabega waakyo. Singa omulabe wo asinga okukaawa yali atambula butereevu gy’oli, ng’awanise ekiso mu lunaku olugazi, tewandisobodde kumulaba, okusinga singa yali akubba okuva emabega. Mu kigambo kimu, wandibadde n’emigongo ebiri, bwe tuyinza okugamba; naye, mu kiseera kye kimu, era, enjuyi bbiri (emmanju ez’ebbali): kubanga kiki ekifuula emmanju w’omuntu — kiki, ddala, okuggyako amaaso ge?

Ate era, wadde nga mu bisolo ebirala ebisinga obungi bye nsobola okulowoozaako kati, amaaso gasimbibwa nnyo mu ngeri etategeerekeka okusobola okutabula amaanyi gaago ag’okulaba, ne gavaamu ekifaananyi kimu so si bibiri eri obwongo; ekifo eky’enjawulo eky’amaaso ga whale, nga gaawuddwamu mu ngeri entuufu nga bwe gali ku kiyuubi ffuuti nnyingi ez’omutwe omugumu, oguwanirira wakati waago ng’olusozi olunene olwawula ennyanja bbiri mu biwonvu; kino, kya lwatu, kirina okwawula ddala endowooza buli kitundu ekyetongodde kye kiwa. N’olwekyo, ennyanja ekika kya whale erina okulaba ekifaananyi kimu eky’enjawulo ku ludda luno, n’ekifaananyi ekirala eky’enjawulo ku ludda olwo; so nga byonna ebiri wakati birina okuba ekizikiza ekinene era nga tewali kintu gy’ali. Mu butuufu, omuntu ayinza okugambibwa nti atunuulira ensi ng’asinziira mu bbokisi y’omukuumi ng’erina emiguwa ebiri egy’eddirisa lye. Naye ku whale, emiguwa gino ebbiri giyingizibwa mu ngeri ya njawulo, ne gikola amadirisa abiri ag’enjawulo, naye eky’ennaku ne kikosa okulaba. Okwewunyisa kuno okw’amaaso ga whale kintu bulijjo ekirina okulowoozebwako mu nvuba; era okujjukirwa omusomi mu bifaananyi ebimu ebiddirira.

Ekibuuzo ekyewuunyisa era ekisinga okusoberwa kiyinza okutandikibwawo ekikwata ku kintu kino ekirabika ng’ekikwata ku Leeviyasani. Naye nteekwa okuba omumativu n’akabonero akalaga. Kasita amaaso g’omuntu gazibuka mu kitangaala, ekikolwa eky’okulaba tekiba kya kyeyagalire; kwe kugamba, olwo tasobola kuyamba kulaba mu ngeri ya makanika ebintu byonna ebiri mu maaso ge. Wadde kiri kityo, omuntu yenna by’ayitamu bijja kumuyigiriza, nti wadde ng’asobola okutwala mu kusenya ebintu awatali kusosola mu kutunuulira okumu, tekisoboka nnyo gy’ali, n’obwegendereza, era mu bujjuvu, okwekenneenya ebintu byonna ebibiri —ka bibe binene bitya oba bitono bitya —ku kimu n’akaseera ke kamu ak’ekiseera; never mind singa bagalamira ku mabbali ne bakwatagana. Naye bwe mujja kati okwawula ebintu bino ebibiri, era nga buli kimu weetooloole enzirugavu ey’ekizikiza ekinene; olwo, okusobola okulaba ekimu ku byo, mu ngeri ereeta ebirowoozo byo okugugumira, omulala ajja kuggyibwako ddala mu kutegeera kwo okw’omulembe guno. Kati olwo kiba kitya ku nnyanja ekika kya whale? Kituufu nti amaaso ge gombi, ku go gonna, galina okukola mu kiseera kye kimu; naye obwongo bwe bukwata nnyo, bugatta, era butegeerekeka okusinga obw’omuntu, ne kiba nti mu kiseera kye kimu asobola okwekenneenya n’obwegendereza ebisuubirwa bibiri eby’enjawulo, ekimu ku ludda lwe olumu, ate ekirala mu kkubo ery’enjawulo ddala? Bw’aba asobola, olwo kiba kintu kya kitalo mu ye, ng’omuntu bw’asobola okuyita mu kiseera kye kimu mu kwolesebwa kw’ebizibu bibiri eby’enjawulo mu Euclid. Era, bwe binoonyerezebwako ennyo, tewali butakwatagana bwonna mu kugeraageranya kuno.

Kiyinza okuba nga kyali kizibu ekitaliimu nsa, naye bulijjo kibadde kirabika gyendi, nti okuwuguka okw’ekitalo okw’entambula okulagibwa ennyanja ezimu eziyitibwa whales bwe zizingibwa amaato asatu oba ana; ensonyi n’obuvunaanyizibwa ku kutiisa okw’ekika kya queer, okutera ennyo ku nnyanja ekika kya whale ng’ezo; Ndowooza nti bino byonna mu ngeri etali butereevu biva mu kusoberwa okutaliimu buyambi okw’okwegomba, nga mu kino amaanyi gaabwe ag’okulaba agaawuddwamu era agakontana ennyo galina okubazingiramu.

Naye okutu kw’ennyanja ekika kya whale kujjudde okwagala okumanya ng’eriiso. Bw’oba ​​omugenyi ddala mu ggwanga lyabwe, oyinza okuyigga ku mitwe gino ebiri okumala essaawa eziwera, n’otozuula kitundu ekyo. Okutu tekulina kikoola kya bweru kyonna; era mu kinnya kyennyini tosobola bulungi kuyingiza kiwujjo, so wonderously minute is it. Kisula katono emabega w’eriiso. Ku bikwata ku matu gaabwe, enjawulo eno enkulu erina okwetegereza wakati w’ennyanja ekika kya sperm whale n’eya ddyo. Wadde ng’okutu kw’eky’olubereberye kulina ekituli eky’ebweru, eky’eky’oluvannyuma kibikkiddwa ddala era kyenkanyi n’oluwuzi, ne kiba nti okuva ebweru tekitegeerekeka nnyo.

Si kya kwewuunya, nti ekitonde ekinene ennyo nga whale okulaba ensi mu liiso ettono bwe lityo, n’okuwulira okubwatuka okuyita mu kutu okutono okusinga okwa hare? Naye singa amaaso ge gaali gagazi ng’endabirwamu y’endabirwamu ennene eya Herschel; n’amatu ge nga gazibye ng’embalaza za Lutikko; ekyo kyandimufudde okwongera okulaba, oba okuwulira obulungi? Si bwe kiri. —Kati olwo lwaki ogezaako ‘okugaziya’ ebirowoozo byo? Kifuule subtilize.

Kati ka tulina levers zonna ne yingini z’omukka ze tulina mu ngalo, cant over the sperm whale’s head, that it may lie bottom up; olwo, ng’olinnya amadaala okutuuka ku ntikko, funa akafaananyi wansi mu kamwa; era singa si nti omulambo kati gwawuddwamu ddala, n’ettaala tuyinza okukka mu mpuku ennene eya Kentucky Mammoth ey’olubuto lwe. Naye ka twekwate wano ku linnyo lino, tutunuulire gye tuli. Nga ddala kamwa kalungi era nga kalabika nga kalongoofu! okuva wansi okutuuka waggulu, nga ziriko layini, oba okusingawo nga ziriko empapula n’oluwuzi olweru olumasamasa, olumasamasa ng’abagole.

Naye fuluma kati, otunuulire akawanga kano aka wansi akalaga, akalabika ng’ekibikka ekiwanvu ekifunda eky’ekibokisi ekinene ennyo eky’okunywa, nga hingi eri ku nkomerero emu, mu kifo ky’oludda olumu. Bw’ogisika waggulu, osobole okugifuna waggulu, n’obikkula ennyiriri z’amannyo gaayo, kirabika nga portcullis ey’entiisa; era nga bwe batyo, woowe! kiraga bangi nti wight omwavu mu by’obuvubi, emisinde gino gye gigwa n’amaanyi ag’okukomerera. Naye ekisingako okutiisa kwe kulaba, bw’otuuka wansi mu nnyanja, olaba ennyanja ekika kya sulky whale, ng’etengejja awo ng’ewaniriddwa, ng’akawanga ke ak’ekitalo, nga ka ffuuti kkumi na ttaano obuwanvu, nga kalengejja butereevu wansi mu nkoona entuufu n’omubiri gwe, olw’ebyo byonna ensi nga jib-boom y’emmeeri. Whale eno tefudde; aggwaamu omwoyo gwokka; out of sorts, mpozzi; okukendeera kw’omumwa gwa nnabaana; era nga yeefukamidde nnyo, ne kiba nti enkokola z’akawanga ke ziwummudde, ne zimuleka awo mu mbeera eyo ey’ekika ekitali kya bulijjo, ekivume eri ekika kye kyonna, abalina, awatali kubuusabuusa, okumuvumirira ensaya ezisiba.

Emirundi egisinga akawanga kano aka wansi —nga kyangu okusumululwa omuyiiya eyeegezaamu —kaggyibwamu ne lisitulwa ku ddeeke n’ekigendererwa eky’okuggya amannyo g’amasanga, n’okuwaayo eby’okukozesa eby’eggumba eryo amakalu eryeru erya whale abavubi lye bakola nalyo ebintu eby’engeri zonna eby’okwegomba, . omuli emiggo, amaliba-stocks, n’emikono okutuuka ku riding-whips.

Nga bakozesa ekintu ekiwanvu ekikooye ekisitula akawanga kasimbulwa ku lyato, ng’alinga ennanga; era ekiseera ekituufu bwe kituuka —oluvannyuma lw’ennaku ntono oluvannyuma lw’omulimu omulala —Queequeg, Daggoo, ne Tashtego, olw’okuba bonna basawo b’amannyo abakugu, bateekebwawo okusenya amannyo. Ng’akozesa ekyuma ekisala, Queequeg akuba ennyindo amasasi; olwo akawanga ne kasibibwa wansi ku ringbolts, era tackle nga rigged okuva waggulu, zisika amannyo gano, ng’ente z’e Michigan zisika ebikonge by’emivule emikadde okuva mu ttaka ly’enku ez’omu nsiko. Okutwalira awamu waliwo amannyo amakumi ana mu abiri gonna; mu nnyanja ekika kya whale enkadde, ezikaddiye ennyo, naye nga tezivunze; wadde okujjula oluvannyuma lw’omulembe gwaffe ogw’ekikugu. Oluvannyuma akawanga kasalibwa mu bipande, ne katumbibwa ng’ebikondo okuzimba amayumba.





ESSUULA 75. Omutwe gwa Right Whale —Endowooza ey’enjawulo.

Nga tusala ddeeke, kati ka tulabe bulungi ekiseera ekiwanvu ku mutwe gwa Right Whale.

Nga bwe kiri mu nkula okutwaliza awamu omutwe gwa Sperm Whale ogw’ekitiibwa guyinza okugeraageranyizibwa ku ggaali ly’olutalo ery’Abaruumi (naddala mu maaso, we guli nga gwetooloovu mu bugazi ennyo); kale, bw’olaba mu bugazi, omutwe gwa Right Whale gulina okufaanagana okutali kwa kitiibwa n’engatto ennene ennyo eriko engalo za galliot. Emyaka ebikumi bibiri emabega omuvubi omukadde Omudaaki yageraageranya enkula yaayo n’ey’omukozi w’engatto asembayo. Era mu kino kye kimu ekisembayo oba engatto, omukazi oyo omukadde ow’olugero lw’abaana abato, n’abaana abakulukuta, ayinza okusula obulungi ennyo, ye n’ezzadde lye lyonna.

Naye bw’osemberera omutwe guno omunene gutandika okutwala ensonga ez’enjawulo, okusinziira ku ndowooza yo. Singa oyimiridde ku ntikko yaayo n’otunuulira ebituli bino ebibiri ebiringa F, wanditutte omutwe gwonna ku bass-viol ennene ennyo, ne spiracles zino, apertures mu sounding-board yaayo. Awo, nate, singa ossa eriiso lyo ku kizimba kino ekyewuunyisa, ekiriko ekikomo, ekiringa ekikomo waggulu w’ekizigo —ekintu kino ekya kiragala, eky’ekika kya barnacle, Aba Greenland kye bayita “engule,” n’abavubi b’omu Bugwanjuba “bonnet” yaakyo ennyonyi ekika kya Right Whale; ng’ossa amaaso go ku kino kyokka, wanditutte omutwe ku kikolo ky’omuti omunene ennyo ogw’omuvule, nga guliko ekisu ky’ekinyonyi mu kisambi kyakyo. Mu ngeri yonna, bw’olaba enseenene ezo enamu ezisula wano ku bonnet eno, ekirowoozo ng’ekyo kumpi kijja kukujjira mu birowoozo; okuggyako nga, ddala, ekyewuunyo kyo kibadde kitereezeddwa ekigambo eky’ekikugu “engule” nakyo ekyakiweebwa; mu mbeera eyo ojja kufaayo nnyo okulowooza engeri ekisolo kino eky’amaanyi gye kiri mu butuufu kabaka w’ennyanja alina engule, ng’engule ye eya kiragala emuteekeddwa wamu mu ngeri eno ey’ekitalo. Naye singa ono whale eba kabaka, aba munne alabika sulky nnyo okusiima engule. Laba omumwa ogwo ogwa wansi oguwaniridde! nga sulk ne pout ennene nnyo eriwo! a sulk and pout, okusinziira ku kipimo ky’omubazzi, obuwanvu bwa ffuuti nga amakumi abiri ate obuziba bwa ffuuti ttaano; a sulk and pout ejja okukuvaamu ggaloni z’amafuta nga 500 n’okusingawo.

Kisaasidde nnyo, kati, nti whale eno ey’omukisa omubi yandibadde ya hare-lipped. Enjatika eno eriko ffuuti nga emu mu buwanvu. Oboolyawo maama mu kiseera ekikulu yali agenda ku nnyanja ng’agenda ku lubalama lw’ennyanja Peru, musisi n’avaako bbiici okuzibuka. Ku mimwa gino, nga bwe tuyita ku mulyango oguseerera, kati tuseerera mu kamwa. Ku kigambo kyange nali ku Mackinaw, kino nsaanidde okukitwala okuba munda mu wigwam y’Abayindi. Mukama mulungi! lino lye kkubo Yona lye yagenda? Akasolya kali mu buwanvu bwa ffuuti nga kkumi na bbiri, era kaddukira mu nkoona ennungi ennyo, ng’olinga alina ekikondo ky’omugongo ekya bulijjo awo; ate nga enjuyi zino eziriko embiriizi, eziriko ebikonde, ez’ebyoya, zitulaga ebipande ebyo ebyewuunyisa, ekitundu ekyesimbye, ebiringa scimetar-shaped slats of whalebone, tugambe ebikumi bisatu ku ludda olumu, okusinziira ku kitundu eky’okungulu eky’omutwe oba eggumba ly’engule, bikola ebyo ebizibe eby’e Venetian ebi awalala zibadde zoogerwako mu ngeri ey’ekibogwe. Empenda z’amagumba gano zirimu ebiwuzi ebirimu ebyoya, Right Whale mw’ayita okusengejja amazzi, era mu buzibu bwayo mw’asigaza obwennyanja obutono, bwe buggule akamwa buyita mu nnyanja za brit mu kiseera ky’okulya. Mu bizibiti by’amagumba ebiri wakati, nga bwe biyimiridde mu nsengeka yaago ey’obutonde, waliwo obubonero obumu obw’okwewuunya, ebikoonagana, ebituli, n’ebiwonvu, abamu ku bavuzi b’ennyanja ekika kya whale mwe babalirira emyaka gy’ekitonde ekyo, ng’emyaka gy’omuti gwa oak olw’empeta zagwo ezeetooloovu. Wadde ng’obukakafu bw’omusingi guno buli wala nnyo okuva ku kulagibwa, naye gulina akawoowo k’obusobozi obw’okugeraageranya. Mu ngeri yonna, bwe tugikkiriza, tulina okuwa Right Whale emyaka mingi nnyo okusinga ku kusooka okulabika ng’ogw’ensonga.

Mu biseera eby’edda, kirabika waaliwo ebirowoozo ebisinga okwewuunyisa ebikwata ku bizibe bino. Omutambuze omu mu Purchas aziyita “enviiri” ezewuunyisa eziri munda mu kamwa k’ennyanja ekika kya whale;* omulala, “enviiri z’embizzi”; omusajja owookusatu omukadde mu Hackluyt akozesa olulimi luno olulungi: “Waliwo ebiwaawaatiro nga ebikumi bibiri mu ataano ebimera ku buli ludda lw’ekikuta kye eky’okungulu , ebiwanvuwa ku lulimi lwe ku buli ludda lw’akamwa ke.”

*Kino kitujjukiza nti ddala Right Whale erina ekika ky’enviiri, oba okusingawo enviiri, ezirimu enviiri enjeru entono ezisaasaanidde ku kitundu eky’okungulu eky’enkomerero ey’ebweru ey’akawanga aka wansi. Oluusi ebikuta bino biwa ffeesi ye ey’ekitiibwa ennyo.

Nga buli omu bw’amanyi, bino bye bimu “ebiwujjo by’embizzi,” “ebiwaawaatiro,” “enviiri,” “ebizibe,” oba kyonna ky’oyagala, biwa abakyala obusiba bwabwe n’ebintu ebirala ebikaluba. Naye mu kino entongole, obwetaavu bumaze ebbanga nga bukendeera. Mu kiseera kya Kkwiini Anne eggumba lino mwe lyali mu kitiibwa kyalyo, nga mu kiseera ekyo farthingale ye yali omusono gwonna. Era nga ba dames abo ab’edda bwe batambulatambula mu ssanyu, newankubadde nga bali mu bbwa lya whale, nga bw’oyinza okugamba; wadde kiri kityo, mu ssaawa, n’obutalowooza obufaananako bwe butyo, ennaku zino tubuuka wansi w’ensaya y’emu olw’obukuumi; amaliba nga weema esaasaanidde ku ggumba lye limu.

Naye kati weerabire byonna ebikwata ku bbugumu n’enviiri okumala akaseera, era, ng’oyimiridde mu kamwa ka Right Whale, tunuulira obuggya. Okulaba empagi zino zonna ez’amagumba nga zitambula mu ngeri ey’enkola, tewandirowoozezza nti oli munda mu kitundu ekinene ekya Haarlem, era ng’otunuulira payipu zaakyo lukumi? Ku kapeti ku organ tulina rug ya Turkey esinga okugonvu —olulimi, olusiigiddwa, nga bwe tuyinza okugamba, wansi w’akamwa. Kiba kigejjo nnyo era kigonvu, era kituukira ddala okukutuka mu bitundutundu mu kukisitula ku ddeeke. Olulimi luno olutongole kati mu maaso gaffe; ku kutunula okumpi nsaana okugamba nti yali ya bipipa mukaaga; kwe kugamba, kijja kukuvaamu amafuta nga bwe gatyo.

Ere kino, oteekwa okuba nga olabye bulungi amazima g’ebyo bye natandika nabyo —nti Sperm Whale ne Right Whale kumpi balina emitwe egy’enjawulo ddala. N’olwekyo mu bufunze: mu Right Whale’s tewali luzzi lunene lwa nsigo; tewali mannyo ga masanga n’akatono; si kitundu kiwanvu, ekigonvu eky’akawanga aka wansi, ng’ogwa Sperm Whale. Era mu Sperm Whale tewali ku bizibe ebyo eby’amagumba; tewali mimwa gya wansi munene nnyo; era nga tewali kintu kyonna kya lulimi. Nate, Right Whale erina ebituli bibiri eby’ebweru ebifulumya amazzi, Sperm Whale kimu kyokka.

Laba ekisembayo, kati, ku mitwe gino egy’ekitiibwa egy’enkoofiira, nga gikyagalamira wamu; kubanga omu ajja kubbira mangu, nga tewandiikiddwa, mu nnyanja; omulala tegujja kuba muwanvu nnyo mu kugoberera.

Osobola okukwata expression ya Sperm Whale's awo? Kye kimu kye yafiira nakyo, enviiri empanvu mu kyenyi ezimu zokka zirabika kati ziweddewo. Ndowooza nti ekibatu kye ekigazi kijjudde obukkakkamu obulinga obw’omu nsiko, obuzaalibwa obutafaayo obuteeberezebwa ku kufa. Naye ssaako akabonero ku ngeri omutwe omulala gye yeeyolekamu. Laba omumwa ogwo ogwa wansi ogwewuunyisa, ogwanyigirizibwa mu butanwa ku ludda lw’ekibya, bwe kityo n’okunyweza okukwatira akawanga. Omutwe guno gwonna tegulabika nga gwogera ku kusalawo okw’omugaso ennyo mu kwolekagana n’okufa? Ono Right Whale mmutwala nti yali Stoic; the Sperm Whale, Omuplaton, ayinza okuba nga yakwata Spinoza mu myaka gye egyasembayo.





ESSUULA 76. Ekyuma ekikuba-Ram.

Ere okulekera awo, for the nonce, omutwe gwa Sperm Whale, nandyagadde ggwe, ng’omukugu mu by’omubiri alina amagezi, mu ngeri ennyangu —naddala yogera ku ludda lwayo olw’omu maaso, mu kukuŋŋaanyizibwa kwayo kwonna okunyigirizibwa. Njagala okinoonyereze kati n’endowooza yokka ey’okwekolera okubalirira okutali kuyitiridde, okw’amagezi okw’amaanyi gonna aga battering-ram agayinza okuteekebwa awo. CHAPTER 79. The Prairie.

To scan the lines of his face, or feel the bumps on the head of this Leviathan; this is a thing which no Physiognomist or Phrenologist has as yet undertaken. Such an enterprise would seem almost as hopeful as for Lavater to have scrutinized the wrinkles on the Rock of Gibraltar, or for Gall to have mounted a ladder and manipulated the Dome of the Pantheon. Still, in that famous work of his, Lavater not only treats of the various faces of men, but also attentively studies the faces of horses, birds, serpents, and fish; and dwells in detail upon the modifications of expression discernible therein. Nor have Gall and his disciple Spurzheim failed to throw out some hints touching the phrenological characteristics of other beings than man. Therefore, though I am but ill qualified for a pioneer, in the application of these two semi-sciences to the whale, I will do my endeavor. I try all things; I achieve what I can.

Physiognomically regarded, the Sperm Whale is an anomalous creature. He has no proper nose. And since the nose is the central and most conspicuous of the features; and since it perhaps most modifies and finally controls their combined expression; hence it would seem that its entire absence, as an external appendage, must very largely affect the countenance of the whale. For as in landscape gardening, a spire, cupola, monument, or tower of some sort, is deemed almost indispensable to the completion of the scene; so no face can be physiognomically in keeping without the elevated open-work belfry of the nose. Dash the nose from Phidias's marble Jove, and what a sorry remainder! Nevertheless, Leviathan is of so mighty a magnitude, all his proportions are so stately, that the same deficiency which in the sculptured Jove were hideous, in him is no blemish at all. Nay, it is an added grandeur. A nose to the whale would have been impertinent. As on your physiognomical voyage you sail round his vast head in your jolly-boat, your noble conceptions of him are never insulted by the reflection that he has a nose to be pulled. A pestilent conceit, which so often will insist upon obtruding even when beholding the mightiest royal beadle on his throne.

In some particulars, perhaps the most imposing physiognomical view to be had of the Sperm Whale, is that of the full front of his head. This aspect is sublime.

In thought, a fine human brow is like the East when troubled with the morning. In the repose of the pasture, the curled brow of the bull has a touch of the grand in it. Pushing heavy cannon up mountain defiles, the elephant's brow is majestic. Human or animal, the mystical brow is as that great golden seal affixed by the German emperors to their decrees. It signifies—“God: done this day by my hand.” But in most creatures, nay in man himself, very often the brow is but a mere strip of alpine land lying along the snow line. Few are the foreheads which like Shakespeare's or Melancthon's rise so high, and descend so low, that the eyes themselves seem clear, eternal, tideless mountain lakes; and all above them in the forehead's wrinkles, you seem to track the antlered thoughts descending there to drink, as the Highland hunters track the snow prints of the deer. But in the great Sperm Whale, this high and mighty god-like dignity inherent in the brow is so immensely amplified, that gazing on it, in that full front view, you feel the Deity and the dread powers more forcibly than in beholding any other object in living nature. For you see no one point precisely; not one distinct feature is revealed; no nose, eyes, ears, or mouth; no face; he has none, proper; nothing but that one broad firmament of a forehead, pleated with riddles; dumbly lowering with the doom of boats, and ships, and men. Nor, in profile, does this wondrous brow diminish; though that way viewed its grandeur does not domineer upon you so. In profile, you plainly perceive that horizontal, semi-crescentic depression in the forehead's middle, which, in man, is Lavater's mark of genius.

But how? Genius in the Sperm Whale? Has the Sperm Whale ever written a book, spoken a speech? No, his great genius is declared in his doing nothing particular to prove it. It is moreover declared in his pyramidical silence. And this reminds me that had the great Sperm Whale been known to the young Orient World, he would have been deified by their child-magian thoughts. They deified the crocodile of the Nile, because the crocodile is tongueless; and the Sperm Whale has no tongue, or at least it is so exceedingly small, as to be incapable of protrusion. If hereafter any highly cultured, poetical nation shall lure back to their birth-right, the merry May-day gods of old; and livingly enthrone them again in the now egotistical sky; in the now unhaunted hill; then be sure, exalted to Jove's high seat, the great Sperm Whale shall lord it.

Champollion deciphered the wrinkled granite hieroglyphics. But there is no Champollion to decipher the Egypt of every man's and every being's face. Physiognomy, like every other human science, is but a passing fable. If then, Sir William Jones, who read in thirty languages, could not read the simplest peasant's face in its profounder and more subtle meanings, how may unlettered Ishmael hope to read the awful Chaldee of the Sperm Whale's brow? I but put that brow before you. Read it if you can.





CHAPTER 80. The Nut.

If the Sperm Whale be physiognomically a Sphinx, to the phrenologist his brain seems that geometrical circle which it is impossible to square.

In the full-grown creature the skull will measure at least twenty feet in length. Unhinge the lower jaw, and the side view of this skull is as the side of a moderately inclined plane resting throughout on a level base. But in life—as we have elsewhere seen—this inclined plane is angularly filled up, and almost squared by the enormous superincumbent mass of the junk and sperm. At the high end the skull forms a crater to bed that part of the mass; while under the long floor of this crater—in another cavity seldom exceeding ten inches in length and as many in depth—reposes the mere handful of this monster's brain. The brain is at least twenty feet from his apparent forehead in life; it is hidden away behind its vast outworks, like the innermost citadel within the amplified fortifications of Quebec. So like a choice casket is it secreted in him, that I have known some whalemen who peremptorily deny that the Sperm Whale has any other brain than that palpable semblance of one formed by the cubic-yards of his sperm magazine. Lying in strange folds, courses, and convolutions, to their apprehensions, it seems more in keeping with the idea of his general might to regard that mystic part of him as the seat of his intelligence.

It is plain, then, that phrenologically the head of this Leviathan, in the creature's living intact state, is an entire delusion. As for his true brain, you can then see no indications of it, nor feel any. The whale, like all things that are mighty, wears a false brow to the common world.

If you unload his skull of its spermy heaps and then take a rear view of its rear end, which is the high end, you will be struck by its resemblance to the human skull, beheld in the same situation, and from the same point of view. Indeed, place this reversed skull (scaled down to the human magnitude) among a plate of men's skulls, and you would involuntarily confound it with them; and remarking the depressions on one part of its summit, in phrenological phrase you would say—This man had no self-esteem, and no veneration. And by those negations, considered along with the affirmative fact of his prodigious bulk and power, you can best form to yourself the truest, though not the most exhilarating conception of what the most exalted potency is.

But if from the comparative dimensions of the whale's proper brain, you deem it incapable of being adequately charted, then I have another idea for you. If you attentively regard almost any quadruped's spine, you will be struck with the resemblance of its vertebræ to a strung necklace of dwarfed skulls, all bearing rudimental resemblance to the skull proper. It is a German conceit, that the vertebræ are absolutely undeveloped skulls. But the curious external resemblance, I take it the Germans were not the first men to perceive. A foreign friend once pointed it out to me, in the skeleton of a foe he had slain, and with the vertebræ of which he was inlaying, in a sort of basso-relievo, the beaked prow of his canoe. Now, I consider that the phrenologists have omitted an important thing in not pushing their investigations from the cerebellum through the spinal canal. For I believe that much of a man's character will be found betokened in his backbone. I would rather feel your spine than your skull, whoever you are. A thin joist of a spine never yet upheld a full and noble soul. I rejoice in my spine, as in the firm audacious staff of that flag which I fling half out to the world.

Apply this spinal branch of phrenology to the Sperm Whale. His cranial cavity is continuous with the first neck-vertebra; and in that vertebra the bottom of the spinal canal will measure ten inches across, being eight in height, and of a triangular figure with the base downwards. As it passes through the remaining vertebræ the canal tapers in size, but for a considerable distance remains of large capacity. Now, of course, this canal is filled with much the same strangely fibrous substance—the spinal cord—as the brain; and directly communicates with the brain. And what is still more, for many feet after emerging from the brain's cavity, the spinal cord remains of an undecreasing girth, almost equal to that of the brain. Under all these circumstances, would it be unreasonable to survey and map out the whale's spine phrenologically? For, viewed in this light, the wonderful comparative smallness of his brain proper is more than compensated by the wonderful comparative magnitude of his spinal cord.

But leaving this hint to operate as it may with the phrenologists, I would merely assume the spinal theory for a moment, in reference to the Sperm Whale's hump. This august hump, if I mistake not, rises over one of the larger vertebræ, and is, therefore, in some sort, the outer convex mould of it. From its relative situation then, I should call this high hump the organ of firmness or indomitableness in the Sperm Whale. And that the great monster is indomitable, you will yet have reason to know.
