Noako katha lại tuluć, tinre Queequeg do iń ṭhen khon tomahawk ńam lekhan, uni do jạpit́ akan hoṛaḱ bohok cetanre ona reaḱ hatet́-seć doe jạpit́et́ kan tãhẽkana.

“Ona do cet́ lạgit́ kana, Kuiku?”

“Peri alga, goć-e; oh! peri alga!”

Uni do acren tomahawk-pipe babot te thora j^rur disạm kạmi calao idiyena, oka do, nonka bujhauḱ kana, uniaḱ bar beohar re acren bạiri koaḱ bạṛić kạmi ko kạmi ocoet́ koa ar uniaḱ jion hõe rạskạ ocoet́ koa, tinre abo do direct jạpit́ rigger seć bon aṭkar ena. Keṭec bhap do nit do purạte kạṭić kạṭić kạṭić kạṭić teye pereć ena, ona do uni cetan re lại lạgit́́ e ehoṕ ena. Uni do mit́́ ṭen ḍạṅguạ kuṛi sãote saṕhae ńam keda; ona tayom mọhọk̕ re ạḍi bhabna ńelok̕kan tahẽkana; ona tayom mit́ bar dhao cetanreye ạcur calaoena; ona tayom duṛuṕ kate met́́hãe ạcurkeda.

“Họlọa!” uni do mucạt́reye saṕha keda, “ape do okoe kanape ḍuṅgạko?”

“Jahaj ren hoṛko,” iń doń roṛ ruạṛ keda, “uni do tinreye calaḱa?”

“Hẽ, hẽ, ape do uni repe calaḱ kana, cet́? Uni do tihiń jahaj reye calaḱ kana. Captain do teheń nindhạn jahaj reye heć akana.”

“Oka Kapṭan?—Ahab?”

“Uni chada okoe do sạri ge?”

Iń do̠ Ahab babot́ arhõ thoṛagan kukliń kuli lạgit́ iń calaḱ kan tahẽkana, unre deck reaḱ ạḍi ãṭ rạskạń ańjom keda.

“Họlọa!Sṭarbak reak’ asṭir,” rigọr doe menkeda. “Uni do mit́́ ṭen jion reaḱ maraṅ gate kanae, ona; bhage hoṛ, ar dhorom; menkhan joto jivạt́ nit́́ do, iń do iń ṭhen calaḱ jạruṛa.” Ar onka menkate uni do deck reye calaoena ar ale dole pańja kedea.

Nit do̠ sạri surjo̠m ạgu akana. Ạḍi usạrate kạmiạko do bar-pe hoṛko jahajre ko heć ena; rigger ko do apnar tege ko udgạuena; mate ko do̠ ạḍi ãṭ ko kạmi kan tahẽkana; ar shore ren aema hoṛko do aema lekan mucạt́ jinis ko jahaj re ạgu reaḱ ko kạmi kan tahẽkana. Ona talare Kapṭan Ahab do acʼakʼ cabin bhitri re bae ńel ńam ocoyena.
