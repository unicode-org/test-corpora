Ar eken noa do baṅ, menkhan ona ạḍi ghạṛić kạmi lạgit́te kạmikan, ḍạrkore ḍoboḱkan circle ṭhen, okoe do, jahan karonte, uni ṭhen kạṭic̕ bandhao ạgu reaḱ ạidạri menak̕taea; ona botor circle re cetan reaḱ cinhạ ńam akan hạnḍi-ko taheyena, ona lekage, Ahab hotete mo̠j ạkil te begor hisạbte tahena- ona do̠ ạḍi bạṛić kạmi ko sãoteye kạmi keda, atma ar rạskạ reaḱ disom khon do̠ purạte baṅ ḍahar ạgu akana. Onate uni lạgit́́ te ạḍi uskur te onko do joto koge noa kạmi reaḱ baḍae do eṭaḱ ko ṭhen khon ạḍi jạruṛ hạbić ko kạmi keda; ar onate noa do hoe ena je, ạḍi ghạṛić hạbić ona do Pequod reaḱ deck cetanre baṅ hoelena.

Menkhan noa joto do cetʼleka hoyokʼma; ạḍi jạruṛ gea, ona do hawa reaḱ baṅ ńel ńam akan, bạṛić ạkil reaḱ synod, se ạḍi bạṛić kạmiạ rajko ar seṅgel reaḱ daṛeanko do dhạrtiren Ahab sãote kạmi hoyoḱ takoa se baṅ, enhõ, uniaḱ jaṅga reaḱ noa nit́aḱ bisoe re uni do sạriaḱ kạmiạ kạmi koye hatao keda;—uni do kaṭkoe hohoadea.

Ar tinre uni kạmiạ hoṛ do uni samaṅreye hećlena, unre uni do begor ghạṛićte nãwã jaṅga benao lạgit́e hukumat́koa ar sãotenkoe hukumat́koa jemon uni do nit hạbić jahaj reaḱ ḍahare jạmạu akan jaw-ivory (Sperm Whale) reaḱ joto ḍahar ar joist ko em ocoye, jemon ạḍi gạḍi, ạḍi sạri jaṅga reaḱ ạḍi jạruṛte bachao daṛeaḱ. Noa kạmi purạu lenkhan, uni kaṭko do ona nindhạnre ona jaṅga purạu lạgit́ hukume ńamkeda; ar ona lạgit́ joto fittings em lạgit́, beohar reaḱ bepạtiạr hoṛ sãote jopoṛao akanko khon sạdhin. Ar ona chaḍa jahaj reaḱ forge do hold reaḱ ạḍi thoṛa ghạṛić kạmi khon oḍok lạgit́ hukum emlena; ar ona kạmi usạrate calao lạgit́te lohar doe hukumadea jemon jãhãn lo̠so̠t́ kạmi lạgit́ jạruṛ menaḱ khan ona kạmi lạgit́ mit́ dhao ge laha idiḱ.
