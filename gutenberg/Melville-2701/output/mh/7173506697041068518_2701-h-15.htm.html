JAPTER 74. Bōran Sperm Whale eo—Contrasted View.

Ijin, kiõ, ewōr ruo whale ko relap, rej likit bõrair ibben don; jen kobaik ir, im kobaik ad make.

Jen laajrak in folio leviathan ko, Sperm Whale im Right Whale rej lukkun aorōk. Rej wōt whale ko armij rej mōn ir. Ñan Nantucketer ro, rej kwalok kin ruo kain whale ko. Einwot an oktak eo ikõtan ir ej alikar ilo bōraer; im einwot juon bōran kajjojo ej ien in ej dāpij jen turin Pequod; im einwot ad maroñ in etal jen juon ñan bar juon, ilo ad baj etal wōt ilo deck eo:—ia, ij kōnan jelā, kwonaj ke bōk juon ien emmanlok ñan katak kin cetology jen ijin?

Ilo jinoin, kwoj bwilōñ kin oktak eo ikõtan bõra kein. Ruo iaer rej lukkun lap ilo aolep lemnok; ak ewōr juon joñan joñan ilo Sperm Whale eo me Right Whale ejjab wōr ie. Ewōr bar juon kadkad ilo bōran Sperm Whale eo. Ilo am loe, kwoj jab kōnan lelok utiej eo elap ñan e, ilo joñan kautiej eo elap. Ilo ien in, bareinwõt, kautiej in ej laplok kin pepper im salt color in bõran ilo tol eo, ej kwalok kin an rūtto im elap an jelã. Ilo kakobaba, ej ta eo ri ek ko rej naetan juon “grey-headed whale.”

Jen kiiō lale ta eo ejjab lukkun oktak ilo bōran kein—einwot, mōttan ko ruo raorōk tata, mej im lojilñin. Ettolok ilo turear in bōran, im lal, iturin turin mejān whale eo, elañe kwoj lukkun kappok, kwonaj lo juon mejen ejjelok ļash ie, eo kwonaj kar lemnok ej mejen juon colt edik; innem jen aolep joñan ej ñan joñan an lap bōran.

Kiiō, jen jikin in mejen whale eo ej bed iturin, ealikar bwe ejjab maroñ lo juon men eo ej bed iman, jab laplok jen an maroñ lo juon eo ej bed iman. Ilo juon nan, jikin mejen whale eo ej einlok wōt ñan lojilñin juon armij; im kwomaroñ lemnok, ñan kwe make, ewi wawein an kar emman ñan yuk, ke kwar lale men ko ikijen lojilñūm. Kwonaaj kar lo bwe kwomaroñ wōt kōmman jet jiljinoñoul degree in vision mokta jen line in vision eo ejimwe; im enañin jiljinoñoul bar jet itulikin. Elañe ri kijirãt eo am elaptata an nana ej etetal tok ñan kwe, ippāñ juon jāje eo ej utiej ilo ran, kwoban kar maroñ in loe, elaplok jen elañe ej bōk yuk jen likio. Ilo juon nan, kwonaj kar wor ruo likio, einwot am ba; ak, ilo ejja ien eo wot, bareinwõt, ruo maan (side fronts): bwe ta eo ej kōmman maan in juon armij—ta, emool, ak mejen?

Bareinwõt, ilo enañin aolep menin mour ko jet me imaroñ lemnak kaki kiõ, mejen rej lukkun eddek einwõt juõn men eo ejjab alikar ñan kobaik kajur in loe, bwe en maroñ walok juõn pija im jab ruo ñan bõklikit eo; jikin eo ejenolok an mejen whale eo, emoj an jebel kin elōn cubic feet in bōran eo ebin, eo ej jutak ibwiljier einwot juon tol elap ej kōjepel ruo reba ko ilo valley ko; men in, emool, ej aikuj in lukkun kōjepel melele ko me kajjojo mōttan ko rej make iaer rej lelok. Whale eo, kin menin, ej aikuj lo juon pija eo ejenolok ilo turear in, im bar juon pija eo ejenolok ilo turear eo; ilo ien eo aolep men ko ikõtan rej aikuj in marok im ejelok tokjen ñan e. Armej emaron, ilo mol, ba bwe ej kalimjōk lal in jen juon sentry-box ippāñ ruo sash ko rej koba ñan window eo an. Ak ippān whale eo, m̧ōttan kein ruo rej likūt er make, kōm̧m̧an ruo wintō ko reoktak, bōtaab ekaburom̧ōjm̧ōj ej kōm̧m̧an bwe en jab em̧m̧an an armej loe. Men in mejen whale ej juon men eo jej aikuj in kememej ilo ien ek ko; im bwe ri riit eo en kememej ilo jet pija ko tokelik.

Juon kajitok eo ekabwilōñlōñ im elap an kabwilōñlōñ emaron jino kin men in einwot an jelet Leviathan eo. Ak ij aikuj in mõnõnõ kin juon nan in kakabilek. Joñan wōt an mejān juon armej peļļo̧k ilo meram, jerbal in loe ej jab kōņaan; melelen, ejjab maroñ in jibañ ilo an lo jabdrewot men ko rej bed iman mejen. Meñe āindein, en̄jake eo an jabdewōt armej enaaj katakin e, bwe men̄e emaron̄ bōk juon m̧ōttan eo ejjab kalijekļo̧k an men ko ilo juon wōt an kalimjōk, ej lukkuun pen n̄an e, ilo lukkuun, im lukkuun, n̄an etale jabdewōt ruo men ko —jekdo̧o̧n ewi jon̄an aer ļap ak dik —ilo juon wōt iien; jab inebata elañe rej babu iturin don im jibwe don. Ak elañe kwoj itok in kōjepel men kein ruo, im kōjepel kajjojo kin juon circle in marok eo elap; innem, ñan am maroñ lo juon iaer, ilo juon wewein eo enaj bōktok lemnok eo am ñan an bōke, eo juon enaj lukkun jako jen lemnok eo am ilo ien in. Ewi wāween, innem, ippān whale eo? Emool, mejen ruo, ilo ir make, rej aikuj in jerbal ilo juon wot ien; ak ke bōro eo an elaplok an melele, kobaik, im mōkaj jen an armij, bwe emaron ilo ejja ien eo wot lukkun etale ruo men ko reoktak, juon ilo juon ian, im eo juon ilo juon ial eo eoktak? Elañe emaron, innem ej juon men eo elap an kabwilōñlōñ ilo e, einwot elañe juon armij emaron ilo juon wot ien etal ilo kwalok ko an ruo abañ ko reoktak jen dron ilo Euclid. Ak, ilo lukkun etale, ejelok jabdrewot men ko rejjab errā ilo keidi in.

Emaroñ juon men eo ejelok tokjen, ak ekar iien otemjej walok ñan ña, bwe m̧akūtkūt ko rekabwilōn̄lōn̄ rej kwaļo̧k jān jet whale ko n̄e rej pād ioon jilu ak emān wa ko; mijak im eddo ñan mijak ko reoktak, eo ekkã an walok ñan kain whale rot kein; Ij lemnok bwe men kein otemjej rej walok jen an jab maroñ jibañ kin an kōnan, eo ilo an kajoor in loe eo air ej aikuj in koba ir.

Ak lojilñin whale eo ej obrak kin an kōnan jelā einwot mejen. Elañe kwoj juon eo ejjab jelā kin bwij eo air, kwomaroñ in etale bōran rein ruo iumwin elõñ awa ko, im jab loe menin mour eo. Lojilñin ejjelok leen ilikin; im ilo hole eo make kwojjab maroñ in likit juon quill, elap an dik. Ej bed jidik itulikin mejen. Ilo kautiej ñan lojilñier, oktak in eaorōk ej aikuj in lale ikõtan sperm whale eo im eo anmoon. Ke lojilñin eo moktata ewōr juon an peļļo̧k itulik, eo an eo āliktata ej lukkuun im em̧m̧an an kalibubuik kōn juon membrane, bwe en jab lukkuun alikar jān itulik.

Ejjab ke juon men in bwilōñ, bwe juon menin mour eo elap einwot whale eo en maroñ in lo lal in kin juon mejen edik, im roñjake jourur eo kin juon lojilñin eo ediklok jen lojilñin juon hare? Ak elañe mejen rar depakpak einwot lens in telescope eo an Herschel; im lojilñin rebin einwot porch ko an cathedral ko; men in enaj ke kōmman an maroñ in lo, ak maroñ in roñjake? Jab.—Innem etke kwoj kajeoñ in “kalaplok” lemnok eo am? Kōmman bwe en diklok.

Jen kiõ ilo jabdrewot lever ko im steam-engine ko ewōr ippād, cant ioon bōran sperm whale eo, bwe en maroñ in babu jen lal; innem, wanlōñlok ilo juon ladder ñan tōrerein eo, lale ilo loñin; im elañe en kar jab bwe enbwin eo ej lukkun jenolok jen e, ippāñ juon lamp jemaroñ kar wanlaḷtak ilo Kentucky Mammoth Cave eo an lojien. Ak jen dāpij wōt ijin kin menin mour in, im lale ijo jej bed ie. Ej juon lonin eaiboojoj im erreo! jen lal ñan lal, line, ak kar kōmmane kin juon membrane mouj ej romaak, ej romaak einwot nuknuk in belele.

Ak diwōjtok kiõ, im lale lojen in, eo ej einwõt lid eo edik an juon snuff-box elap, ippāñ hinge eo ilo juon jemlokin, ijellokin juon turin. Elañe kwoj jibwe, bwe en maroñ in bōk ion bōram, im kwalok ļain in ñi ko an, ej einwot juon portcullis eo elap an emman; im men kein, alas! ej kalikar ñan elōn juon wight eo ejjab emman ilo jerbal in ek, eo ej wōtlo̧k ion spike kein ilo an kajoor. Ak elaplok an nana ñan loe, ñe ej to lal ilo lojet, kwoj lo jet whale ko rebin, rej ettōr ijo im dāpij, ippāñ loñin eo elap, jet joñoul lalem ne aitokan, ej dāpij wōt ilo right-angle ko ibben enbwinin, ñan aolepen lal in einwot jib-boom in juon wa. Whale in ejjab mij; ej baj ebbweer wōt; jen kain, bōlen; hypochondriac; im lukkun babu, bwe menin mour ko an rar mōjno, im likit e ijo ilo kain jorrāān eo, juon naan in kajirere ñan aolep bwij eo an, ro rej aikuj, ejelok bere, kōmman nana ñan e.

Ilo elōn ien, lower lower in—ebin an juon ri-kōmman pija—ej jolok im likit ion deck eo ñan an maroñ in bōk niñniñ ko, im lelok juon kein jerbal in whalebone eo ekilmeej eo me ri-eoñ ko rej kōmmane aolep kain men ko rebin, ekoba cane ko, umbrella-stock ko, im hand-hip ko.

Ilo juon kein jibwe eo elap im ebin, ej jibwe lojen eo ion wa eo, einwot ñe ej juon ankōr; im ñe ien eo ejejjet ej itok—jet raan ko elikin jerbal eo juon—Queequeg, Daggoo, im Tashtego, rej aolep taktō ro remāletlet, rej pojak in kōmman menin mour ko. Ilo juon kein mōttan eo ebin, Queequeg ej mwijbar mōttan ko; innem ej dāpij loñin ñan ringbolt ko, im juon tackle ej rigged jen lañ, rej jibwe ñi kein, einwot an kau ko an Michigan jibwe wōjke oak ko remootlok jen bwidej ko rebin. Ewōr jiljinon̄oul-ruo dānin kōmjaaļaļ ilo aolepān; ilo whale ko remootlok, elap air jorrāān, ak rejjab jorrāān; ak kobrak elikin wāween ad kōmmane. Innem rej m̧wijm̧wij m̧ōttan eo n̄an slab ko, im likūt e āinwōt joist ko n̄an kalōk m̧ōko.





JAPTER 75. Bōran Whale eo ejimwe—Lemnok eo eoktak.

Ilo ad etal jen deck eo, jen kiiō lale bōran Right Whale eo.

Einwot ilo jekjek eo an bōran Sperm Whale eo eutiej emaron keidi ñan juon jariot in tarinae an Rome (elaptata ilo maan, ijo ej lukkun rup); innem, ilo juon wāween eo elap, bōran Right Whale ej āinwōt juon juuj eo elap an galliot-toed. Ruo bukwi yio ko remootlok juon ri ito-itak in Dutch eo etto ear keidi jekjek eo an ñan jekjek eo an juon ri kōmman juuj. Im ilo ejja tōre in wōt, kōrā eo edik ilo bwebwenato in niñniñ eo, ippān ļaddik ro nejin, emaron̄ kar lukkuun em̧m̧an an jokwe, e im aolep ro nejin.

Ak ilo am epaaklok ñan bōran in elap ej jino an bōk jet wāween ko, ekkar ñan lemnok eo am. Elañe kwoj jutak ion tōrerein im kalimjōk ruo F-shaped spoutholes kein, kwonaj bōk aolepen bōran ñan juon bass-viol elap, im spiracles kein, aperture ko ilo sounding-board eo an. Innem, bar juon alen, elañe kwoj kalimjōk mejōm ion men in ekabwilōñlōñ, ewōr bōran, āinwōt juon mōttan ioon m̧ōttan eo—men in em̧ōrā, barnacled, eo armej in Greenland rej ņa etan “crown,” im ri-eo̧n̄ōd ro ilo Southern rej ņa etan “bonnet” eo an Right Whale eo; ilo am kalimjōk wōt men in, kwonaj kar bōk bōran ñan wōjke oak eo elap, ippāñ juon mweo ilo bōran. Ilo jabdewōt ien, ñe kwoj kalimjōk crab ko rej jokwe ijin ion bonnet in, kain lemnok rot in enaj lukkun walok ñan yuk; ijellokin wōt elañe, emool, am kōnan emoj an kar kōmmane kin nan in “crown” eo kar bar lewoj ñan e; ilo ien in kwonaj bōk elap itok limo in lemnok kin ewi wawein an mennin mour in ekajur ej juon irooj in lojet eo ewōr an diadem, eo me crown eo an ekilmeej kar likite ñan e ilo wāween in ekabwilōñlōñ. Ak elañe whale in ej juon irooj, ej juon eo elap an illu ñan an maroñ in bōk juon diadem. Lale lip eo iumwin eo ej dāpij wōt! ta juon sulk im pout elap ej bed ie! juon sulk im pout, ekkar ñan joñan eo an ri jerbal, enañin 20 ne depakpakin im lalem ne depakpakin; juon sulk im pout eo enaj letok ñan yuk 500 gallon in oil im elaplok.

Juon menin būromõj elap, kiõ, bwe whale in ej aikuj in kar hare-lipped. Mōttan eo ej juon ne depakpakin. Bōlen jinen ilo juon ien eo eaorōk ekar ettōr ilo wa eo ilo Peru, ke m̧akūtkūt in laļ ko raar kōm̧m̧an bwe āne eo en peļļo̧k. Ion loñin in, einwot ion juon kōjām eo ej mōrā, jej kiiō mōkaj ilo loñin. Ilo aō kar kallimur elañe ikar bed ilo Mackinaw, inaj kar bōk menin bwe en ilowaan juon wigwam an Indian. Irooj eo emman! enin ial eo Jona ear etal ie ke? Wōr eo ej joñoul ruo ne utiej, im ej ettōr ñan juon jikin eo ebin, einwot ñe ewōr juon ridge-pole ijo; ilo ien eo turin kein rebin, rebin, im koot, rej kwalok ñan kij men ko rekabwilōnlōn, jimattan rej jutak, scimetar-shaped slats in whalebone, ba jilu bukwi ilo juon side, ko rej pedped wōt jen mõttan eo ilōñ in bōran ak crown bone, rej ejaak Venetian blind ko me kar kwalok kaki ilo bar juon jikin. Ijo in di ko rej obrak kin menin mour ko, eo Right Whale ej dāpij den eo, im ilo menin ej dāpij ek ko redik, ñe ej bellok lonin ej etal ilo lojet in brit ilo ien an mōñā. Ilo mōttan ko ilo di ko, ilo aer jutak ilo wāween eo aer make, ewōr jet kakōļļe ko rekabwilōnlōn, mōttan ko, mōttan ko, im mōttan ko, ijo jet whalemen rej bwine iiō in menin mour eo, āinwōt iiō in juon oak kōn ring ko an. Meñe mol eo an joñan in ejjab maroñ in kwalok, ak ewōr an maroñ in analogical probability. Ilo jabdewōt jekjek, elañe jej errā kake, jej aikuj lelok juon iiō eo elaplok ñan Right Whale eo jen an kar walok moktata.

Ilo ien ko etto, ej einwõt kar wõr lemnak ko rebintata kin blind kein. Juon ri ito-itak ilo Purchas ej naetan ir “whiskers” ko rekabwilōnlōn ilowaan loñin whale eo;* bar juon, “hogs’ bristles”; juon em̧m̧aan eo kein kajilu ilo Hackluyt ej kōjerbal kajin in em̧m̧an: “Ewōr enan̄in ruo bukwi im lemn̄oul m̧ōttan ko rej eddek ilo kajjojo m̧ōttan in upper chop eo an, ko rej arch ioon lo̧n̄iin ilo kajjojo m̧ōttan in lo̧n̄iin.”

Men in ej kakememej kij bwe Right Whale ewōr juon kain bōran, ak juon bōran, eo ewōr jet bōran ko rekilmeej ilo tulik in bōran. Jet ien mōttan kein rej letok juon kalikar eo ejjab emman ñan turin mejen eo ejjab lukkun utiej.

Einwot an aolep jelā, ejja “bōran pig,” “fins,” “whiskers,” “blinds,” ak jabdewōt men kwoj kōnaan, rej lelok ñan kōrā ro busk ko aer im men ko jet rej kōmman bwe en bin. Ak ilo men in, aikuj eo ekar diklok. Ilo ien eo an Queen Anne ke di ko rar bed ilo aiboojoj eo an, farthingale eo ekar aolep men ko rar walok ilo ien eo. Im ke kōrā ro etto rar etetal ilo mõnõnõ, meñe ilo loñin whale eo, einwõt am maroñ ba; meñe eindrein, ilo juon mōñā, ippāñ jab lemnok, jej ke kijoñ ilo raan kein ej ettōr iumwin ejja mōñā ko ñan kōjparok; umbrella eo ej juon m̧ōn kōppād eo ej pād ioon ejja di eo wōt.

Ak kiõ meloklok kin blind ko im whiskers ko ilo jidik ien, im, jutak ilo loñin Right Whale eo, bar kalimjōk iturin yuk. Ilo am loe aolep colonnade kein an di ko rej lukkun jejjet, kwoban kar lemnok bwe kwoj bed ilowaan Haarlem organ eo elap, im kalimjōk thousand pipe ko an? Ñan juon carpet ñan organ eo ewōr ippād juon rug in Turkey eo emōrā tata—loñ, eo ej glue, einwot an ba, ñan lal in loñin. Elukkun mōrā im mōrā, im emaron in rup ilo mōttan ko ñe rej jibwe ion deck eo. Lo in kiõ ej bed iman kij; ilo juon kalimjōk eo edik ij aikuj ba bwe ekar juon six-barreler; melelen, enaj letok ñan yuk joñan oil eo.

Mokta jen men in, kwoj aikuj in kar lo mol eo kin ta eo ikar jinoe—bwe Sperm Whale im Right Whale ewōr bōraer reoktak jen don. Ñan kōllaajrake, innem: ilo Right Whale eo ejjelok juon aebōj in sperm; ejelok niñniñ in ivory; ejjab aitok, edik loñin loñin, einwot an Sperm Whale. Ak ilo Sperm Whale ejelok jabdrewot ian blind in bone ko; ejelok lip eo elap; im ejjab lukkun wōr jabdrewot in juon lo. Bar juon alen, Right Whale ewōr ruo an spout-hole ko itulik, Sperm Whale ewōr wōt juon an.

Lale āliktata, kiiō, ioon bōran rein reutiej, ke rej babu ippān doon; bwe juon enaj mōkaj an tulo, ejelok melele, ilo lojet; eo juon ejamin to an ļoor.

Kwomaroñ ke loe an Sperm Whale eo kwalok turin mejen ijo? Ej einwot an kar mij kake, jet wōt ian mōttan ko ilo turin mejen rej einwõt emwij air jakolok. Ij lemnok bwe bōran eo elap ej obrak kin juon menin kalmenlokjen einwot juon melaj, ej lotak jen juon an jab kea kin mij. Ak kakōlkōl mejān bōran eo juon. Lale lip eo iumwin eo ekabwilōñlōñ, kar dāpij ilo jab kōtmene ņae turin wa eo, bwe en pen an maroñ in jibwe loñin. Ejjab ke bwe aolepān bōran in ej kōnono kōn juon kōttōpar eļap ilo jelm̧ae mej? Right Whale in ij bōk bwe ekar juon Stoic; Sperm Whale eo, juon Platonian, eo emaron kar bōk Spinoza ilo yio ko an eliktata.





JAPTER 76. Battering-Ram eo.

Mokta jen an bōjrak, ñan jidik ien, bõran Sperm Whale eo, Ikõnan bwe kwon, einwõt juõn ri meletlet kin enbwinin armij, baj wõt—elaptata lale mõttan eo iman, ilo aolep an kobalok. Ij kōnaan bwe kwon etale kiõ kin lemnak eo wōt ñan kōmmane ñan kwe make jet melele ko rejjab ļap, im māletlet kin jabdewōt kajoor in battering-ram emaron bed ie. Enin ej juon men eaorōk; bwe kwoj aikuj in kōmman bwe en emman am kōmmane men in ippaṃ make, ak ñan indeeo pād wōt juon ri jab tōmak āinwōt juon iaan men ko rekabūrom̧ōjm̧ōj tata, bōtaab jab men ko redikļo̧k aer m̧ool, bōlen jabdewōt jikin jemaron̄ loe ilo aolep bwebwenato ko kar je.

Kwoj lo bwe ilo jikin ikkure eo an Sperm Whale, maan in bōran ej kwalok juon plane eo ej lukkun jutak ñan den eo; kwoj lo bwe mõttan eo iumwin ej lukkun liklok, bwe en laplok an maroñ in jeblak ñan socket eo elap eo ej bōk boom-einwõt lower lower; kwoj lo bwe loñim ej bed iumwin bōram, ilo ejja wāween in wōt, emool, einwot ñe loñim make ej bed iumin bōram. Bareinwõt kwoj lo bwe whale eo ejelok an loñin ilikin; im bwe ta bōran ewōr ippāer—bōro in lo̧n̄iin—ej pād ioon bōran; kwoj lo bwe mejen im lojilñin rej bed iturin bōran, enañin juon mõttan jilu in aolepen depakpakin jen maan. Kin menin, kwoj aikuj in kar kile bwe maan in bōran Sperm Whale ej juon worwor eo emej, ejelok juon mõttan ak juon men eo ebin. Bar juon, kwoj aikuj kiiō ļōmņak bwe ilo wōt m̧ōttan eo eļap, edikļo̧k, im ej likūt likļo̧k ilo m̧aan bōram̧, ewōr jidik wōt m̧ōttan di ko; im jab mae ien eo kwoj epaake 20 ne jen turin mejōm kwoj itok ñan eddeklok eo an bōram. Bwe aolep men in elap ejjelok di ko ej einwot juon wad. Āliktata, meñe, einwot an naaj walok tokelik, men ko ie rej koba oil eo ediktata; ak, kwoj aikuj in jelā kin jekjek in men eo ej lukkun kalikar aolep men ko rej walok.
