Lemnak, kiõ, ewi wawein an aikuj in walok ilo jekjek in emen wa ko rej jerbal ibben juon whale eo elap an kajoor, jerbal, im jelā; ñe kin kadkad kein ilo e, im bareinwõt kin taujin jorrāān ko rej walok ilo kain jerbal rot in, jiljino ak joñoul iron ko rebin remaroñ in bed iturin. JAPTER 65. Whale eo einwot juon mōñā.

Armej eo enaj mōñā menin mour eo ej mōñā lamp eo an, im, einwot Stubb, mōñā e kin meram eo an make, einwot am maroñ ba; men in ej einwot juon men eo elap an oktak bwe juon ej aikuj in etal jidik ilo bwebwenato im lemnok eo an.

Ej bed ilo bwebwenato, bwe jilu buki yio ko remootlok lonin Right Whale kar watõke einwõt juõn men in mõñã elap ilo France, im ear lap wõnãn ie. Bareinwõt, ilo ien eo an Henry VIIIth, juon ri kōmat ilo jikin ekajet eo ear bōk juon wōnan eo emman kin an kar kōmmane juon sauce eo emman ñan mõñā ibben porpoises ko rekar barbacued, ko, kwoj kememej, rej juon kain whale. Porpoises, emool, rej ñan rainin watōki bwe rej mōñā ko remman. Kanniōk eo ej kōmmane ñan ball ko einwot ball in billiard, im elañe emman an mōñā im spice emaron in bōk ñan turtle-ball ak veal ball. Mol eo ej, bwe ibwiljin ri-kattōr ro an, whale eo enaj kar watōke juon mõñā eo emman, elañe ear jab lõñ an; ak ñe kwoj itok im jijet iman juon meat-pie eo enañin juon bukwi ne aitokan, ej bōklok am kõnan mõñã. Ro wōt rejjab kalijeklok armij einwot Stubb, rainin rej mōñā whale ko rekar kōmat; ak Esquimaux ro rejjab lukkun mōkaj. Jej ao jelā kilen aer mour ioon whale ko, im ewōr ippāer oil in tūrein eo etto. Zogranda, juon ian taktō ro air rebuñbuñ tata, ej kōmman bwe en wor blubber ñan niñniñ ro, einwot an lukkun emman im emman. Im men in ej kakememej iõ bwe jet ri England ro, ro etto rar likit ir ilo Greenland jen juon wa in whale—bwe armij rein rar mour iumin elõñ alliñ ko ion whale ko rar likiti ilo ene elikin air kar kajeoñ blubber eo. Ilubwiljin Dutch whalemen kein rej naetan “fritters”; eo, emool, rej lukkun einlok wõt, kin air brown im mōrā, im ej bwiin einwot dough-nuts ak oly-cooks an Amsterdam housewives ro etto, ñe rekãl. Ewōr aer juon wāween eo em̧m̧an n̄an m̧ōn̄ā bwe rioktak eo eļap an make kajekdo̧o̧n e make ejjab maron̄ kōjparok pein.

Ak ta eo ej bar kalijeklok whale eo einwot juon mōñā eo emman, ej an lukkun mweie. Ej ox eo elap an wōnan ilo lojet, elap an mōrā bwe en maroñ in emman. Lale bōran, eo enaj kar emman an mōñā einwot an buffalo eo (eo ej juon mõñā eo ejjab lukkun alikar), elañe en kar jab juon pyramid in bōtōktōk eo ebin. Ak spermaceti eo make, ewi joñan an mōrā im creamy; einwot kanniōk eo ealikar, jimattan-jellied, ekilmeej an juon cocoanut ilo allōñ eo kein kajilu in an eddek, ak elap an mweie ñan an maroñ in bōk jikin butter. Meñe āindein, elōn whalemen ro ewōr aer wāween n̄an bōk e n̄an bar juon men, innām bōk m̧ōttan. Ilo ien ko reaitok ilo boñ ej juon men eo ekkā an walok ñan ri-wa ko ñan air likit biscuit in wa ko air ilo oil-pot ko relap im kōtlok bwe ren kōmat ie jidik ien. Elōñ mõñā in jibboñ ko remman iar kōmmane.

Ilo jekjek in juon Sperm Whale edik, bōro ko rej watōki juon mōñā emman. Bok in bōran ej rupe kin juon axe, innem ruo mōttan ko rebin im rekilmeej rej bōklok (einwot ruo pudding ko relap), innem rej kobaiki ippāñ pilawā, im kōmat ilo juon men eo elap an emman, ilo an āinwōt bōran kau ko, eo ej juon mōñā ibwiljin jet epicures; im aolep rejelā bwe jet kau ko redik ibwiljin epicures ko, ilo aer mōñā bōro ko an kau ko, rej bōk jidik bōro ko aer make, bwe ren maroñ in kile bōran kau ko jen bōraer make; eo, emool, ej aikuj kalijeklok eo ejjab ekkā. Im enin unin an juon kau edik ippāñ juon bōran kau eo emālōtlōt iman mejen, ej juon ian men ko rekabūromõjmõj tata kwomaroñ loe. Bōran ej kalimjōk e ilo an kajjirere, ippān juon “Et tu Brute!” kwalok lomnak.

Ejjab, bōlen, kinke whale eo elap an mōñā bwe armij in āne ko rej lemnok in mōñā e kin dike; ej walok, ilo jet wewein, jen lemnok eo kar kwalok kake mokta: einwõt bwe juon armij en mõñã juõn men in lojet eo ej kab mõn e, im mõñã e bareinwõt ilo meram eo an make. Ak ejelok bere bwe armij eo moktata ear mõn juon ox kar watõke einwõt juõn ri uror; bōlen kar dāpij e; im elañe kar likūt e ilo mālijjon̄ eo an kōn kau ko, em̧ool enaaj kar; im ekar tellokin bōk e elañe jabdrewot ri uror ej kōmmane. Etal ñan jikin wia kanniōk ilo juon boñ in Jabōt im lo jarlepju in menin mour ko rej mour rej kalimjōk laajrak in menin mour ko emān neer remej. Ejjab ke bwe men in ej bōk juon menin mour jen lonin ri mõñã armij eo? Ri mōñā kanniōk? wōn eo ejjab juon ri mōñā kanniōk? Ij jiron yuk bwe enaj emmanlok ñan Fejee eo ear mōn juon mijenede eo edik ilo cellar eo an nae juon ñūta eo ej itok; enaj emmanlok ñan Fejee eo ej lale, ij ba, ilo ran in ekajet eo, jen kwe, juon ri mōñā eo elap an meletlet, eo ej nail geese ko ñan bwidej im mōñā kin liver ko air ilo paté-de-foie-gras eo am.

Ak Stubb, ej mõñā whale eo kin meram eo an make, ejjab ke? im men in ej kobaiktok kajjirere ñan jorrāān, ejjab ke? Lale dāpij knife eo am, ijo, gourmand eo aõ eo emman im meletlet ej mõñã jen roast beef eo, ta eo handle eo ej kōmmane jen e?—ta ak di ko an būratin ox eo kwoj mõñā? Im ta eo kwoj kōjerbale, elikin am kar mōñā goose eo ebin? Ippān juon bōran ejja bao eo wōt. Im kin ta quill eo Secretary eo an Society for the Suppression of Cruelty to Ganders ear kwalok kin circular ko an? Ej wot ilo juon ak ruo allōñ ko remotlok ke jukjuk in pād eo ear kōmman juon pepe ñan jab kōtlok jabdewōt men ijellokin steel pen ko.





JAPTER 66. M̧an Shark ko.

Ñe ilo Southern Fishery, juon Sperm Whale eo emwij jibwe, elikin an kar jerbal elap im ebin, ej bōktok ilo boñ, ejjab, einwot juon men eo elap, manit ñan jino ilo ien eo wot ñan jerbal in mwijbar e. Bwe jerbal in ej juon jerbal eo elap an bin; ejjab lukkun mõkaj an dedelok; im ej aikuj aolep pein ñan kōmmane. Therefore, the common usage is to take in all sail; lash the helm a'lee; innem jilkinlok aolep ro rej bed iumwin ñan jikin kiki eo air mae ien eo ej walok, ippāñ joñan eo bwe, mae ien eo, renaj lale anchor-watch ko; melelen, ruo im ruo ilo juon awa, kajjojo ripālele, rijerbal ro ilo rotation renaj uwe ion deck eo ñan lale bwe aolep men rej emman.

Ak jet ien, elaptata ilo Line eo ilo Pacific, karōk in ejamin uwaak; kinke elōñ shark ko rej kobatok iturin enbwin eo, bwe elañe ear likite iumin jiljino awa, ba, ilo juon ial, jidik wōt jen an skeleton eo enaj kar alikar ilo jibboñ. Ilo elap ian jikin ko jet ilo lojet, ijo ek kein rejjab lukkun lõñ, air maroñ in mõñã emaroñ diklok, ilo air lukkun kamakit ir kin whale-spades ko rebin, juõn wãwen meñe, eo, ilo jet ien, ej einwõt ej kõmman bwe ren laplok air jerbal. Ak ear jab āindein ilo jekjek in kiiō ippān shark ko an Pequod; meñe, ñan mol, jabdewōt em̧m̧aan eo ejjab jelā kin men kein, ñan an kar kalimjōk turin bon̄ōn eo, enaaj kar ļōmņak bwe aolepān lo̧jet eo ej juon wōt cheese eļap, im shark ko rej maggot ko ie.

Meñe eindrein, ke Stubb ear likit anchor-watch eo elikin an kar jemlok mõñā in jota eo an; im ke, ekkar ñan men in, Queequeg im juon ri-tōl ilo forecastle rar itok ñan deck eo, ejelok juon menin kamõnõnõ edik ear walok ibwiljin shark ko; kin an kar mōkaj in likit jikin mōttan ko ion turin, im kōttāik jilu lamp ko, bwe ren maroñ in kōmman meram ko reutiej ion lojet eo ebin, ri wa rein ruo, rej jolok whale-spade ko air reaitok, rar wõnmanlok wõt im mõn shark ko,* ilo air kar m̦an steel eo ebin ilo bõraer, im ej loe wõt mõttan eo eaorõk ilo bõraer. Ak ilo inebata eo an ri tarinae ro air, ri tarinae ro rar jab maroñ in tōpar joñan eo air; im men in ear bōktok revelesōn ko rekãl kin joñan an nana an ri kijirãt eo. Rar lukkun mōñā, jab wōt ilo aer mōjno, ak einwot bow ko rebin, mōjno, im mōñā ko air make; mae ien eo mōttan ko rar einwõt kar mōñā bar juon alen im bar juon alen jen ejja loñin eo wot, ñan an kar mōñā jen kinej eo elap. Ejjab men in wōt. Ear jab emman ñan bōk kunair ilo enbwinin im jitõb ko an menin mour kein. Juon kain kajoor eo ej āinwōt ej pād ilo m̧ōttan ko im di ko aer, ālkin an ta eo jemaron̄ kar ņa etan mour eo an kajjojo armej kar jako. Kar mõn e im likit ion deck kin kilin, juon ian shark kein ear epaak an bōk pein Queequeg eo ejeramel, ke ear kajeoñ in kilōk kilin loñin eo ear mij.

*Whaling-spade eo rej kajerbale ñan mwijbar ej kōmmane jen steel eo emman tata; ej kin joñan an lap pein juon armij; im ilo jekjek eo an, ej ekkar ñan kein jerbal in jikin kallib eo me kar ņa etan; turin wōt rej lukkun rup, im jemlokin ilōñ elaplok an dik jen eo iumwin. Kein tarinae in ej iien otemjej lukkun mōrā; im ñe rej kajerbale ej jet ien kōmman, einwot juon razor. Ilo jikin eo an, juon wōjke ebin, jen rualitōk ñan jiljinoñoul ne aitokan, ej likūt ñan juon dāpij.

“Queequeg ejjab kea kin ta anij eo ear kōmmane bwe en shark,” savage eo ear ba, ilo an eñtan im kotak pein lañ im lal; “wedder Fejee god ak Nantucket god; ak anij eo ej kōmmane shark eo ej aikuj in juon dam Ingin.”
