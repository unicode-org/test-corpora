JAPTER 32. Cetology.

Em̧ōj ad peran in ettōrļo̧k ioon m̧wilaļ; ak ejjab to jenaaj jako ilo jikin ko an ejelok tōrerein, im ejelok jikin kōttar ie. Ej jet menin kwalok ko an whale eo ilo kain eo an, eo ij kōnan likit iman mejōmi. Ak ejjab juon jerbal epidodo. Kōjenolok men ko ilo juon chaos, ejelok men ediklok ej walok ijin. Roñjake ta eo ri utiej ro remman im rekãl rar likiti.

“Ejjelok branch in Zoology ej lukkun bōk koņaan einwot eo etan Cetology,” Captain Scoresby ej ba, AD 1820.

“Ejjab aō lemnok, elañe ekar ilo maroñ eo aõ, ñan deloñ ilo etale eo kin wāween eo emool ñan kōjepel cetacea ñan kumi ko im baamle ko. * * * Elap an jab melele ibwiljin ri bwebwenato ro kin menin mour in” (sperm whale), ej ba Surgeon Beale, AD 1839.

“Jab maroñ in kōmmane ekkatak ko ad ilo dren ko rejjab maroñ in melele kaki.” “Vil eo ejjab maroñ deloñ ej kalibubu jelālokjen eo ad kin cetacea.” “Juon jikin kallib eobrak kin wijki ko.” “Aolep men kein rejjab jejjet ak rej jerbal ñan kaeñtan kij ri meletlet ro kin menin mour ko.”

Kin menin konono kin whale eo, Cuvier eo elap, im John Hunter, im Lesson, meram ko an zoology im anatomy. Mekarta, meñe edik jelālokjen eo emool, ak elōñ bok ko; im āindein ilo jet joñan ko redik, ippān cetology, ak science eo an whale ko. Elōñ armij ro, redik im relap, rerūtto im rekãl, ri aelõñ im ri lojet, ro rar jeje kin whale eo. Lale jet ian:—Ro rar jeje Bible eo; Aristotle; Pliny; Aldrovandi; Sir Thomas Browne; Gesner; Ray; Linnæus; Rondeletius; Willoughby; Māroro; Artedi; Sibbald; Brisson; Marten; Lacépède; Bonneterre; Desmarest; Baron Cuvier; Frederick Cuvier; John Hunter; Owen; Scoresby; Beale; Bennett; J. Ross Browne; ri jeje eo an Miriam Coffin; Olmstead; im Rev. T. Cheever. Ak ñan ta unin an aolep men kein kar jeje, men ko kar kwalok kaki ilõñ renaj kwalok.

Jen āt ko ilo laajrak in ri jeje ro kin whale, ro wōt rej lor Owen rar lo whale ko remour; im ak juon iaer ekar juon ri tarinae im ri tarinae eo emol. Ij ba Captain Scoresby. Ak, kin an kar lukkun aurõk men ko an, im an kar jab jelā eo elap, mae jiljilimjuonñoul yio ko remootlok, ear likit sperm-whale eo ekabwilōñlōñ ak ejelok an jelā kake ilo ien eo, im eo jaje eo ñan rainin ej bed wot ilo aolep ak jejjo jikin ko an scientific im whale-port ko; bōk men in ekar lukkun dedelok. Ilo am lale enañin aolep naan in leviathanic ilo ri jeje al ro ilo ien ko remotlok, enaj kamolol yuk bwe Greenland whale, ejelok juon ri jumae, ear ñan ir monarch in lojet ko. Men in eaorōk ej dedelok, point eo tok juon ej, ilo ta kautiej eo ej oktak jen ek ko jet. Ilōñ, Linnæus ear lewoj men kein ñan yuk. Tok juon: ewi wawein ad naaj melele kin whale eo, ikijen an alikar an alikar, bwe en alikar ad naetan ñan aolep ien ko rej itok? Ñan kōmman bwe en dik, innem, juon whale ej juon ek eo ej mōñā im ewōr juon an bōran ej jimwe . Ewōr ippam̧ e. Meñe ej diklok, melele in ej tokjen eo elaplok an kalmenlokjen. Juon walrus ej konono einwot juon whale, ak walrus eo ejjab juon ek, kinke ej mour ilo dren. Ak naan eo eliktata ilo melele eo ej lukkun kalikar, einwot an kobalok ibben eo moktata. Enañin aolep armij rej aikuj in kar lo bwe aolep ek ko rej jelā kaki ñan ri-lale ro rejjab wōr juon aer flat, ak juon aer ej jutak, ak ej utiej-im-laļ. Ilo ien eo, ibwiljin ek ko rej mōñā, bōran, meñe emaron in einlok wōt juon, ej bōk juon jikin eo ej jutak.

Ilo melele eo ilōñ kin ta eo juon whale, Ijjab jolok jen leviathanic brotherhood eo jabdrewot menin mour in lojet eo kar kile kin whale eo jen Nantucketers ro rejelā; ak, ilo bar juon wāween, kobaik ippān jabdewōt ek ko kar watōki āinwōt rioktak.* Kin menin, aolep ek ko reddikļo̧k, rej m̧ōn̄ā, im ewōr bōraer rej aikuj in pād ilo ground-plan in an Cetology. ( Fin-Back ).—Iumin bōran in ij ļōmņak juon mennin mour eo, kōn etan ko reoktak Fin-Back, Tall-Spout, im Long-John, kar loe ilo aolep lo̧jet im ekkā an whale eo me jet eo an ettoļo̧k ekkā an armej ro rej eļļāļo̧k jān Atlantic, ilo New York packet-tracks. Fin in ej jilu ak emen ne aitokan, ej eddeklok jen turin likio, ej juon wāween eo ej āinwōt juon āin, im ewōr juon an jemlokin eo ebin. Ñe lojet ej lukkun kalmenlakjān, im jidik wōt an wōr mōttan ko rebin, im fin in āinwōt gnomon ej jutak im likit meram ko ioon mejān eo em̧ōrā, emaron̄ in kar ļōmņak bwe circle in dān eo ej jepooļe ej āinwōt juon dial, kōn style eo an im wavy hour-lines ko kar je ioon. Ilo Ahaz-dial eo, anōk eo ekkã an jeblak. Fin-Back ejjab lukkun mōñā. Ej einwot juon eo ej dike whale, einwot jet armij rej dike armij. Elukkuun jook; aolep ien etal make iaer; ilo jab kōtmene ej wanlōñtak ñan mejatoto ilo dren ko rettolok im rebin tata; jet eo an ejimwe im ej make wōt ej wanlōñtak einwot juon m̧ade eo eutiej ioon juon āne ejjeļo̧k armej ie; kar lelok ñan e kajoor im mōkaj in ettōr, bwe en maroñ in jume aolep men ko armij rej kajeoñ kaki; leviathan in ej einwot Cain eo emoj an jako im jab maroñ anjo ion bwij eo an, im ej bōk kakōlkōl eo an ilo likio. Jen an kar wōr baleen eo ilo loñin, Fin-Back ej jet ien kobalok ibben whale eo anmoon, ibwiljin juon kain menin mour eo etan Whalebone whales , melelen, whale ko ewōr baleen ibbeir. Jen men kein rej naetan Whalebone whale, enaj kar wor jet kain, elaptata, mekarta, rejjab lukkun jelā kaki. Ilo an wōr jemjerā ippān naan in “Whalebone whales,” ej juon men eaorōk n̄an kwaļo̧k, bwe jekdo̧o̧n ta kain āt rot in emaron̄ in em̧m̧an ilo an kōm̧m̧an bwe en pidodo an armej ro kōmeļeļeik jet kain whale ko, ak ej ejjeļo̧k tokjān n̄an kajjieon̄ juon kōllaajrak eo ealikkar kōn Leviathan eo, pedped ioon baleen eo an, ak hump, ak fin, ak teeth; jekdoon bwe mõttan ko ak kadkad ko rej alikar air emmanlok air maroñ in lelok bedbed eo ñan juon jukjuk in bed eo an Cetology jen jabdrewot oktak ko jet ilo enbwin, eo whale eo, ilo kain ko an, ej kwalok. Ekōjkan innem? Baleen eo, hump eo, back-fin eo, im niñ ko; men kein rej men ko me oktak ko air rej bed ilo jab kalijeklok ibwiljin aolep kain whale ko, ilo ejelok lemnok kin ta eo emaron in kar jekjek in jekjek eo air ilo men ko jet im elaplok air aorōk. Ilo kain whale ko reoktak jen dron, rej ejaak kain koba ko rejjab jimwe; ak, ilo jekjek eo an jabdewōt iaer ej jepel, kain kōjepel eo ejjab jimwe; einwot ñan lukkun jumae aolep wāween ko rej ejaak ion juon pedped rot in. Ion dekā in aolep ri meletlet ro an whale-naturalist ro rar jepel.

Jaab; ta men eo, einwõt juõn wanjoñok, ej bed ilo enbwinnin Greenland whale eo elaplok an kabwilõñlõñ jen baleen eo an? Im elañe kwoj wanlaļtak ilo lojen leviathan ko reoktak jen dron, etke ijo kwojjab naaj lo oktak ko juon mõttan eo kein ka lemnoul einwot an maroñ in bed ñan systematizer eo einwot ko ilikin emoj an kar kwalok kaki. Ta eo ej bed wot? ejelok men ak ñan jibwe whale ko ilo enbwin, ilo aolepen joñan eo air, im peran in kōjenolok ir ilo wewein in. Im menin ej Bibliographical system eo kar bōk ijin; im ej juon wōt eo emaron in tōbrak, bwe ej make wōt emaron in jerbal. ñan wōnmanlok.

Ñan ro rejjab maroñ in katak kin men in, emaron in einwot juon men ekabwilōñlōñ, bwe ek ko rejjab laplok jen jiljino ak lalem ne rej aikuj in bed ibwiljin WHALES ko—juon nan, eo, ilo melele eo elap, ej iien otemjej kwalok juon lemnak kin joñan an lap. Ak menin mour ko kar likiti ilōñ einwot Duodecimoes rej whale ko rejjab bōd, ekkar ñan melele eo aõ kin ta eo whale ej— einwot juon ek ej konono, ippāñ juon loñin eo ej jutak.

BOOK III. ( Duodecimo ), JAPTER 1. ( Huzza Porpoise ).—Eñin ej porpoise eo ekkā an walok ibelakin lal. Etan ej jen aō make lewoj; bwe elaplok jen juon kain porpoises, im ewōr juon men ej aikuj in kōmmane ñan kōjenolok ir. Ij naetan āindein, bwe ej iien otemjej ettōr ilo dān ko rekōmetak, ko ioon lo̧jet eo eļap rej dāpij wōt aer ettōrļo̧k n̄an lan̄ āinwōt kap ko ilo juon jarlepju in armej ilo raan eo kein kajilu in Juļae. Wāween aer walok ej lukkun nebar e kin lañliñ jen ri wa eo. Eobrak kin jitōb ko remman, rej itok jen kōto ko rebin ñan kōto. Rej ļaddik ro rej iien otemjej mour im̧aan kōto. Rej watōki juon kakōļļe in jeraam̧m̧an. Elañe kwe make kwomaroñ in jutak bin ilo am lo ek kein rekajur, innem lañ ej jibañ yuk; jetōb in ikkure an Anij e jab ilo koṃ. Juon Huzza Porpoise eo emman an mōñā, im emōrā enaj letok juon gallon in oil eo emman. Ak dren eo emman im ebin kar bōk jen lonin ej lukkun aorōk. Ej juon menin aikuj ibben ri wia ro im ri kōmman awa ko. Ri wa ko rej likite ion hone ko air. Kanniōk in porpoise ej emman ñan mōñā, kwojelā ke. Emaroñ kar jab walok ilo lemnok eo am bwe juon porpoise ej mōñā. Emol, mōñā eo an edik bwe ejjab lukkun alikar. Ak ien eo tok juon ñe ewōr am ien, lale e; im kwonaj loe Sperm whale eo elap make ilo an dik.

BOOK III. ( Duodecimo ), JAPTER II. ( Algerine Porpoise ).—Juon ri-kōmman jorrāān. Elukkuun nana. Ej baj walok wōt, ij lemnok, ilo Pacific. Ej diklok jen Huzza Porpoise, ak ej einwot juon wōt. Kwōn kainepataik e, im enaaj buckle ñan juon shark. Ikar kajirere kin e elōn alen, ak ikar jab loe an kar jibwe.

BOOK III. ( Duodecimo ), JAPTER III. ( Mealy-mouthed Porpoise ).—Kain Porpoise eo elaptata; im ej walok wōt ilo Pacific, joñan an jelā kake. Etan eo wōt ilo English, eo kar ņa etan, ej etan ri-eo̧n̄ōd ro—Right-Whale Porpoise, jān jekjek eo bwe ej lukkuun waļo̧k ilo jikin eo iturin Folio eo. Ilo jekjek, ej oktak ilo jet joñan jen Huzza Porpoise, kin an jab lukkun rup im ejjab lukkun mōrā; emool, ej juon eo emman im einwot juon emaan. Ejelok an pein ilo likio (enañin aolep porpoise ko jet ewōr ippāer), ewōr juon an bōran eaiboojoj, im mejān Indian eo ej juon hazel hue. Ak loñin eo ebin ej kokkure aolep. Meñe aolepān likio ñan turin ej juon sable eo em̧wilaļ, ak juon ļain in tōrerein, eoktak āinwōt kakōļļe eo ilo m̧ōttan wa eo, etan “bright waist,” ļain eo ej kōjepel e jān stem n̄an stern, kōn ruo colour ko reoktak, marok ilōn̄ im mouj ium̧win. Emouj ej bōk jikin bōran, im aolepān lo̧n̄iin, eo ej kōm̧m̧an bwe en āinwōt n̄e eaar m̧ōkaj an ko jān juon loļo̧k eo enana n̄an juon jikin m̧ōn̄ā. Juon men eo elap an nana im ejjab emman! Oil eo an ej einwot eo an porpoise eo ekkā an walok.

* * * * * *

Ijellokin DUODECIMO, jukjuk in bed in ejjab wōnmanlok, kinke Porpoise ej diktata ian whale ko. Ilōñ, ewōr ippam aolep Leviathan ko rebuñbuñ. Ak ewōr juon kumi in whale ko rejjab jelā, rej ko, im rejjab lukkun jelā, eo, einwot juon ri-Amedka, ijelā kin etan, ak jab make. Inaaj kōllaajrake er kōn etan fore-castle ko aer; bwe bōlen kain laajrak rot in emaron in aorōk ñan ri etale ro ilju jeklaj, ro remaroñ kadedelok ta eo ikar jinoe ijin. Elañe jabdrewot ian whale kein, renaj bōk im kakōlkōl, innem emaron in kobalok ilo System in, ekkar ñan Folio, Octavo, ak Duodecimo magnitude eo an:—The Bottle-Nose Whale; Junk Whale eo; Pudding-Headed Whale eo; Cape Whale eo; Leading Whale eo; Cannon Whale eo; Scragg Whale eo; Coppered Whale eo; Elephant Whale eo; Iceberg Whale eo; Quog Whale eo; Blue Whale eo; Jen Icelandic, Dutch, im old English ri utiej ro, emaron kar wor bar jet laajrak in whale ko rejjab alikar, kar jerammōn kin etan ko renana. Ak ij jolok ir einwot rejjab jerbal; im ejjab maroñ jibañ lemnok kin ir kin ainikien ko, eobrak kin Leviathanism, ak rejjab melelen jabdrewot.

Āliktata: Kar ba ilo jinoin, bwe jukjuk in pād in eban kar pād ijin, im ilo juon wōt iien, kar weeppān. Kwojjab maroñ in jab lo bwe io kar kejbãrok nan eo aõ. Ak kiõ ij likit cetological System eo aõ ej jutak im jab dedelok, einwõt an kar Cathedral eo elap an Cologne bed wõt, ippāñ crane eo ej jutak wõt ion tower eo ejjab dedelok. Bwe jikin kalek ko redik remaroñ dedelok jen ri kalōk ro moktata; ro relap, ro remol, rej likit copestone eo ñan ro rej itok. Anij en kejbãrok io jen aō kadedelok jabdrewot. Aolepān bok in ej juon wōt pija—jaab, ak ej juon pija in juon pija. O, Iien, Kajoor, Jāān, im Kijenmej!
