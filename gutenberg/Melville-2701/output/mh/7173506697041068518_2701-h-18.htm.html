JAPTER 89. Ek ko re mōkaj im ek ko re mōkaj.

Kōmeļeļe eo ñan waif im waif-poles ilo jebta eo āliktata bōtaab juon, ej aikuj jet kōmeļeļe ko kōn kien ko im kakien ko an whale fishery, eo waif emaron̄ in watōke āinwōt kakōļļe eo eļap im badge eo.

Ekkā an walok bwe ñe elōn wa ko rej ito-itak ippān doon, juon whale emaron in jorrāān jān juon wa, innem ko, im āliktata mej im jibwe jān bar juon wa; im ijin rej koba elōn men ko redik, aolep rej bōk koņaer ilo juon wōt menin aorōk. Einwõt juõn wanjoñok,—elikin juõn ien ekauwatata im ekauwatata in lukwarkware im jibwe juõn whale, enbwinin emaroñ kar diojlok jen wa eo kin juõn kõto elap; im ej ettōr ettolok ñan mejatoto, bar bōk e jen juon ri-whale eo kein karuo, eo, ilo juon jikin eo ebin, ej jibwe e iturin, ilo ejelok jorrāān in mour ak line. Kin menin, aitwerõk ko relap im rekajur renaj kar walok ikõtan ri eñwõd ro, elañe ear ejelok jet kien ko rekar jeje ak jab jeje, kien ko rejejjet, im ejelok akwāāl ie im rej jerbal ñan aolep case ko.

Bōlen kakien eo wot kin whale eo kar kōtlok jen kien, kar eo an Holland. Kar kōmmane jen States-General ilo AD 1695. Ak meñe ejelok bar juon ailiñ ear wor an kien kin whale, ak ri-eo̦ñõd ro an Amedka rar make ri-kaki im ri-ekajet ro ilo men in. Rar letok juon jukjuk in bed eo ej laplok jen Justinian's Pandects im By-laws ko an Chinese Society ñan bōbrae an armij ro jet bōk koņaer ilo jerbal ko an armij ro jet. Aet; kien kein remaroñ kar je ion juon farthing an Queen Anne, ak barb in juon harpoon, im kar likiti iturin bōran, joñan air dik.

I. Juon Fast-Fish ej an kumi eo emōkaj ñan e.

II. Juon Loose-Fish ej juon ikkure eo emman ñan jabdewōt eo emaron jibwe ilo ien eo emōkaj tata.

Ak ta eo ej kōmman an nana kin kakien in ej joñan an dik, eo ej aikuji elap melele ko ñan kwalok kake.

Mokta: Ta ej juon Fast-Fish? Juon ek ej mour ak mij ej lukkun mõkaj, ñe ej kobalok ibben juon wa ak wa eo ewōr armij ie, ilo jabdrewot kein jerbal eo emaron in kōmmane jen armij eo ej bed ie ak ro rej bed ie,—juon mast, juon oar, juon nine-inch cable, juon telegraph wire, ak juon strand in cobweb, ej einwot juon wōt. Bareinwõt juon ek ej mõkaj an mõkaj ñe ej bõk juõn waif, ak jabdewõt kakõlle ko jet rej kile kin an bõk; joñan wōt an party eo ej kōtlok ej kwalok air maroñ ilo jabdewōt ien ñan bōk e, im barāinwōt aer kōnan kōmmane.

Men kein rej melele ko jen science; ak kōmeļeļe ko an whalemen ro make jet iien rej pād ilo naan ko reppen im kōjan̄jan̄ ko reppenļo̧k—Coke-upon-Littleton eo an fist eo. Emool, ibwiljin ro rejimwe im kautiej whalemen ro rej iien otemjej kōmman mālim ñan case ko reoktak, ijo enaj kar juon men eo enana ñan juon armij ñan an bōk juon whale eo kar lukwarkware ak mij jen bar juon armij. Ak ro jet rejjab lukkun lukkun lale.

Enañin 50 yio ko remootlok ekar wor juon case in whale-trover kar litigate ilo England, ijo ri-kamool ro rar kwalok bwe elikin an kar lukkun lukwarkware juon whale ilo Northern seas; im ke emol (ri-kamool ro) rar tōbrak ilo aer kar mōn ek eo; rar āliktata, kōn kauwōtata in mour ko aer, kar aikuj in joļo̧k ejjab wōt ļain ko aer, ak wa eo waer make. Ilo jemlokin ri-kamool ro (ri-jerbal ro ilo bar juon wa) rar itok ippāñ whale eo, kar mōn, mõn, bōk, im āliktata bōk e iman mejen ri-kamool ro. Im ke ri-kamool ro rar naruon ir, kapen eo air ear jibwe pein ilo ñi ri-kamool ro, im kamool ñan ir bwe ilo an kwalok kin jerbal eo ear kōmmane, enaj kiõ kejbãrok line eo air, harpoon ko, im wa eo, eo ear bedwõt ilo whale eo ilo ien eo ear bõke. Kin menin ri-kamool ro rar kiiō bōk eddo in bōk tokjen whale eo air, line, harpoon ko, im wa eo air.

Kiiō ilo case in Erskine ear ba bwe waanjonok ko an whale eo im kōrā eo rar kwalok kin don.

Akweļap kein, im akweļap ko ņae, ke kar ron̄jaki, riekajet eo eļap an ekkatak ilo naan ko kar kōmālim, n̄an ba, —Bwe kōn wa eo, eaar leļo̧k n̄an rikōņaan ro, kōnke raar baj likūt wōt e n̄an lo̧mo̧o̧ren mour ko aer; ak bwe ikijen whale eo, harpoon ko, im line eo, rar an ri-kamool ro; whale eo, kinke ekar juon Loose-Fish ilo ien eo kar jibwe e; im harpoon ko im line ko kinke ke ek ko rar etal ibbeir, (ek ko) rar bōk juon jikin ilo men kein; im kin menin jabdewōt eo ear bōk ek eo ear wōr an maroñ ñan ir. JAPTER 90. Bōran ak Tail ko.

“De balena vero sufficit, si rex habeat caput, et regina caudam.” Bracton, l. 3, c. 3.

Latin jen bok in Kien ko an England, eo ej bōk ibben melele eo, ej melelen, bwe jen aolep whale ko kar jibwe jen jabdewōt armij ilo tōrerein āne eo, King eo, einwot Honorary Grand Harpooneer, ej aikuj in bōk bōran, im Queen eo ej aikuj in bōk bōran ilo kautiej. Kiiō ke kien in, iumin juon wewein eo emōj an oktak, ej jerbal ñan rainin ilo England; im einwot an letok ilo elōn wāween ko juon men eo eoktak kin kien eo an Fast im Loose-Fish, ijin ej kwalok kake ilo juon chapter eo ejenolok, ilo ejja pedped in kautiej eo wōt me ej kōmman bwe railway ko an England ren bōk juon wa eo ejenolok, eo kar kōmmane ñan royalty ro. Ilo an bōk opiij eo jen crown eo, ij tõmak, aolep royal emoluments ko rej walok ñan Cinque Port territories rej erom an. Jet ri jeje ro rej naetan opiij in juon sinecure. Ak ejjab āindein. “Emmon, innem, Bouton-de-Rose-bud eo aõ, kwar ke loe White Whale eo?”

“ Whale ta ?”

“Whale eo White —juon Sperm Whale—Moby Dick, kwar loe ke?

“Ikar jab roñ kin kain whale rot in. Cachalot Blanche! White Whale—jaab.”

“Emmōn, innem; jouj im etal kiō, im inaj bar call ilo juon minit.”

Innem ej mōkaj an jeblak ñan Pequod eo, im lo Ahab ej babu ion quarter-deck rail eo im kõttar report eo an, ear kōmman pein ruo ñan juon trumpet im lamõj—“Jaab, Sir! Jaab!” Ilo ien eo Ahab ear kakkije, im Stubb ear jeblak ñan Frenchman eo.

Kiiō ej kile bwe Guernsey-man eo, eo ej kab deloñ ilo chain ko, im ej kajerbal juon cutting-spade, ekar likit loñin ilo juon kain bag.

“Ta eo ej walok ñan loñim, ijo?” Stubb ear ba. “Ekar rupe ke?”

“Ikõnan bwe en kar rup, ak bwe en kar ejelok aõ loñ!” uwaak Guernsey-man eo, eo ear jab lukkun mõnõnõ kin jerbal eo ear bed ie. “Ak ta eo kwoj dāpij am ?”

“O, ejelok men! Ej juon bōran wax; ij aikuj in jibwe. Emmōn raan eo, ejjab ke? Air ej juon jikin kallib, ij aikuj ba; jolok ñan kim juon bunch in posies, kwonaj ke, Bouton-de-Rose?”

“Ta eo kwoj kōnaan ijin?” Guernseyman eo ekar lamõj, ej ettōr ilo juon an kijejeto.

“O! kejbarok—kōmman? aet, ej nan eo! etke kwojjab likit whale kein ilo ice ilo ien eo kwoj jerbal ñan ir? Ak joke, meñe; kwojelā ke, Rose-bud, bwe ej juon men in bwebwe ñan kajeoñ in bōk oil jen whale rot kein? Kin eo emōrā, ijo, ejjelok an kar bōk juon oil ilo wa eo waan.”

“Ijelā menin; ak, kwoj loe, Kapen eo ijin ejamin tõmak; enin ej ito-itak eo an moktata; ekar juon ri-kōmman Cologne mokta. Ak itok ilo wa eo, im bōlen enaj tõmak eok, elañe ejjab kōnan io; innem inaj diwōjtok jen jorrāān in.”

“Jabdewōt men ñan kōmmane ñan kwe, m̧ōtta eo em̧m̧an im em̧m̧an,” Stubb eaar bar kobatok, im kōn menin eaar m̧ōkaj an wanlōn̄ļo̧k n̄an deck eo. Ijo juon pija in oktak ear walok. Ri wa ko, ilo nuknuk in red worsted, rar bōk kein jerbal ko rebin ñan air bojak ñan whale ko. Ak rar jerbal jidik kin jidik im konono ilo mõkaj, im rar einwõt ilo jabdewõt men ak juõn men in ettõñ. ao loñier rej walok jen turin mejeir einwot elōn jib-boom ko. Kiiō im tokālik ruo iaer renaaj kar joļo̧k jerbal ko aer, im ettōrļo̧k n̄an bōran mast eo n̄an bōk jet kōto em̧m̧an. Jet rar lemnok bwe renaj kar bōk nañinmij in, rar likit oakum ilo coal-tar, im jet ien rar jibwe ñan loñier. Ro jet ke rar rupe tōrerein paip ko aer ilo bowl eo, rar lukkun idaak tobacco-smoke, bwe en kobrak jikin bwiin ko air.

Stubb ear būromõj kin juon lañliñ im nana ko rar walok jen mweo imõn Kapen eo; im ke ej kalimjōk ijo ej lo juon turin mejān kijeek ej itok jen turin kejem eo, eo ekar bellok jen lowaan. Eñin ej taktō eo ekar eñtan, eo, elikin an kar kalijeklok nae jerbal ko ilo raan eo, ekar bōk e make ñan mweo imõn Kapen eo ( cabinet eo ekar naetan) ñan bõprae nañinmij eo; ak ejjab maroñ in bōjrak an kwalok an akwelap im illu jet ien.

Ilo an kar lale men kein otemjej, Stubb ear aitwerõk kin an karōk, im oktaklok ñan Guernsey-man eo ear bwebwenato jidik ibben, ilo ien eo eo ear jab jelã kake ear kwalok an dike Kapen eo an einwõt juõn ri jab jelã, eo ear bõktok ir aolep ñan juõn pickle eo ejjab emõn im ejjab wõr tokjen. Ilo an kar lukkun roñjake, Stubb ear bar kile bwe Guernsey-man eo ear ejelok an lemnok kin ambergris eo. Kin menin ear jab konono kin men eo, ak ear lukkun kwalok im kwalok men ko jet ñan e, bwe erro jimor rar mõkaj im kõmman juõn karõk jidikdik ñan air kar jolok im kajjirere kake Kapen eo, ilo an jab lemnak kin air jab liki mol eo air. Ekkar ñan karōk jidikdik in aer, Guernsey-man eo, iumin kōjparok in juon opiij in ri ukok, ekar aikuj in jiron Kapen eo ta eo ekar kōnan, ak einwot ej itok jen Stubb; im ñan Stubb, ej aikuj in kwalok jabdrewot men ko rejjab mol me renaj kar walok ilo e ilo ien intōrpiu eo.

Ilo ien in ri jumae eo air ear walok jen mweo imõn. Ekar juon em̧m̧aan edik im marok, ak ekar lukkuun em̧m̧an an waļo̧k n̄an juon kapen in lo̧jet, kōn bōran ko reļļap im bōran, mekarta; im ekar kōnak juon nuknuk emera im ewōr watch-seal ko iturin. Ñan em̧m̧aan in, Stubb kar kiiō kwaļo̧k ilo jouj jān Guernsey-man eo, eo ilo iien eo wōt eaar kōm̧m̧ane m̧akūtkūt in kōmeļeļe eo ikōtaan erro.

“Ta eo in ba ñan e mokta?” ekar ba.

“Etke,” Stubb eaar ba, ej kalimjōk velvet vest eo im watch eo im seal ko, “kwomaroñ jino ilo am ba ñan e bwe ej einwot juon niñniñ ñan ña, meñe ijjab kōmman einwõt juon ri ekajet.”

“Ej ba, Monsieur,” Guernsey-man eo eaar ba, ilo kajin French, oktakļo̧k n̄an kapen eo an, “bwe inne wōt wa eo waan eaar kōnono juon wa, eo kapen eo an im chief-mate eo an, ippān ļalem ri-wa ko, raar aolep mej jān juon fever kar bōk jān juon whale eo eaar bōktok.”

Ilo ien in kapen eo ear jino, im ear lukkun kōnan jelā elaplok.

“Ta kiiō?” Guernsey-man eo ear ba ñan Stubb.

“Etke, kinke ej lukkun inebata, ba ñan e bwe kiõ emoj aō lukkun kalimjeke, ij lukkun jelā bwe ejjab maroñ in iroij juon wa in whale jen juon St. Jago monkey. Ilo mol, ba ñan e jen ña bwe ej juon baboon.”

“Ej kallimur im kwalok, Monsieur, bwe whale eo juon, eo emōrā, elaplok an nana jen eo emōrā; ilo jemlokin, Monsieur, ej kōketak kij, ilo ad kautiej mour ko ad, bwe jen jolok jen ek kein.”

Ilo ien eo wot kapen eo ear ettōr lok iman, im ilo juon ainikien elap ear jiroñ rijerbal ro an bwe ren bōjrak jen air jibwe kein tarinae ko, im ilo ien eo wot ear jolok cable ko im chain ko rej likit whale ko ñan wa eo.

“Ta kiiō?” ekar ba Guernsey-man eo, ke Kapen eo ekar jeblak ñan ibbeir.

“Etke, kwon kōtlok bwe in lale; aet, kwomaroñ ba ñan e kiõ bwe—bwe—ilo mol, ba ñan e bwe iar diddle e, im (ilo e make) bōlen bar juon armij.”

“Ej ba, Monsieur, bwe ej lukkun mõnõnõ in kar jibañ kim.”

Ke ear roñ men in, kapen eo ear kallimur bwe rej armij ro rej kamolol (melelen e make im eo mōttan) im ear kajemlok ilo an kirtok Stubb ñan lowaan cabin eo an bwe en idaak juon bo̦o̦l in Bordeaux.

“Ekōnaan bwe kwon bōk juon kab in wain ippān,” ri ukok eo eaar ba.

“Kamolol e kin aolepen buruõm; ak ba ñan e bwe ej jumae kien ko aõ bwe in idaak ibben emaan eo iar idaak. Ilo mol, ba ñan e bwe ij aikuj etal.”

“Ej ba, Monsieur, bwe pedped ko an reban kōtlok an idaak; ak bwe elañe Monsieur ekōnaan mour bar juon raan ñan idaak, innem Monsieur enaj kar jolok aolep wa ko emen, im jibwe wa eo jen whale kein, bwe elap an kalmenlokjen im rejamin maroñ in idaak.”

Ilo ien in Stubb ear bed iturin wa eo, im ke ear uwe ilo wa eo waan, ear kamolol Guernsey-man eo ñan men in,—bwe kin an wor juon tow-line eo elap ilo wa eo an, enaj kar kōmmane men eo emaron ñan jibañ ir, ilo an bōklok whale eo ediklok jen turear in wa eo. Ke wa ko an ri-France eo, innem, rar jerbal in jibwe wa eo ilo juon ial, Stubb ear jouj im jibwe whale eo an ilo ial eo juon, ilo an kar kōmman juon tow-line eo elap an aitok.

Ilo ien eo wot juon kōto ear walok; Stubb ear kōmman einwot ej jolok jen whale eo; ilo an kar kōttāik wa ko waan, ri-France eo ear mōkaj an kalaplok an ettol, ilo ien eo Pequod eo ear mōkaj an etal ikōtaan e im whale eo an Stubb. Innem Stubb ear mõkaj an jibwe enbwin eo ej ettõr, im ear kirtok Pequod eo ñan lelok melele kin an kar kõnan, ilo ien eo wõt ear jino bõk leen leen eo an ejjab jimwe. Ilo an jibwe kein dāpij wa eo an, ekar jino an ekkatak ilo enbwinin, jidik itulikin side fin eo. Kwonaj kar lemnok bwe ej kalbwin juon cellar ijo ilo lojet; im ke spade eo an ear mōjno ilo rib ko redik, ear einwot an kar ukōt tile ko an Rome im pottery ko kar kalbwini ilo English loam eo ebin. Rijerbal ro ilo wa eo waan rar aolep lukkun mõnõnõ, im rar kijejeto in jibañ iroij eo air, im rar einwõt air inebata einwõt ri kappok gold ro.

Im aolep ien bao ko ejelok oran rar tulo, im dāpij, im lamõj, im lamõj, im tarinae iturin ir. Stubb ear jino an buromõj, elaptata ke ear laplok an nana, ke ilo ien eo wot jen buruen nañinmij in, ear walok juon reba in perfume edik, eo ear toor ilo juon river eo enana ilo an jab bōk jikin, einwot an juon river naaj toor im innem bar juon, ilo an jab maroñ in kobalok ibben iumwin jidik ien.

“Ewōr ippa, ewōr ippa,” Stubb eaar lam̧ōj, kōn m̧ōņōņō, ej m̧akūtkūt juon men ilo jikin ko ium̧win laļ, “juon pāāk! juon pāāk!”

Ilo an kar jolok spade eo an, ear likit pein ruo ilowaan, im kar bōk juon men eo ej einwot soap in Windsor eo emōrā, ak cheese eo emōrā; elap an mōñā im emman ippān. Kwomaroñ in mōkaj am mōjno kin peim; ej juon kilaaj ikōtaan kilaaj ekilmeej im kilaaj in ash. Im men in, ro mōtta ro remman, ej ambergris, ej joñan wōnan juon gold guinea juon ounce ñan jabdewōt ri wia uno. Jet jiljino mōttan ko kar bōk; ak elaplok men ko rar jako ilo lojet, im elaplok, bōlen, remaroñ kar bōk elañe en kar jab kin an Ahab eo ejjab kijenmij jiroñ Stubb bwe en bōjrak, im uwe ion wa eo, elañe jab wa eo enaj kar ba ñan ir jouj.





JAPTER 92. Ambergris.

Kiiō ambergris in ej juon men eo elap an kabwilōñlōñ, im elap an aorōk einwot juon menin wia, bwe ilo 1791 juon Captain Coffin eo ear lotak ilo Nantucket kar etale ilo bar eo an English House of Commons kin men in. Bwe ilo ien eo, im emool mae juon raan eo eliktata, jinoin ambergris ear bed wot, einwot amber make, juon apañ ñan ro rar katak. Meñe nan eo ambergris ej nan in French ñan gray amber, ak men kein ruo rej lukkun oktak. Bwe amber, meñe jet ien ej walok ilo tōrerein lojet, ej barāinwōt kalbwini ilo jet bwidej ko rettolok, ak ambergris ejjab walok ijellokin wōt ion lojet. Bareinwõt, amber ej juon men ekajur, ealikar, ebin, ejelok bwiin, rej kajerbale ñan mōñā ko ñan paip ko, ñan bead ko im ornament ko; ak ambergris ej mōrā, waxy, im elap an bwiin im spicy, bwe elap an kajerbale ilo perfumery, ilo pastiles, candle ko raorōk, hair-powder, im pomatum. Ri Turks ro rej kōjerbale ilo kōmat, im barāinwōt bōkļo̧k n̄an Mecca, n̄an ejja un eo wōt āinwōt an frankincense bōkļo̧k n̄an St. Peter's ilo Rome. Jet ri wia wine rej jolok jet grain ko ilo claret, ñan kōmman an emman.

Wōn enaj kar lemnok, innem, bwe kain kōrā im maan ro remman rej aikuj in lelok ñan ir make kin juon men eo ej walok ilo lojen juon whale eo enañinmej! Ak ej āindein. Jet, ambergris ej unin, im jet ej tokjen, an dyspepsia ilo whale eo. Ewi wawein an maroñ in kemour kain nañinmij in mōñā rot in ear bin ñan ba, ijellokin wōt ilo an kar lelok jilu ak emen boat load in pill ko an Brandreth, innem ettōr jen ial in jorrāān, einwot an rijerbal ro kōmmane ilo aer kōjepel dekā ko.

Ikar meloklok in ba bwe ekar wor ilo ambergris in, jet pileij ko rebin, rebin, im ewōr di ko, ko ilo jinoin Stubb ekar lemnok bwe remaroñ kar bōtōktōk in nuknuk in wa ko; ak tokelik ear walok bwe rar jab bar juon men jen mōttan ko redik in squid ko rar embalm ilo wewein in.

Kiiō ke jab jorrāān in ambergris in em̧m̧an tata ej aikuj in pād ilo būruwōn kain jorrāān rot in; ejjelok men in ke? Lemnak kin nan eo an St. Paul ilo Korint, kin jorrāān im jab jorrāān; ekijkan ar jeor kij ilo jok, a kejerkakbiji kij ilo aibujuij. Im bareinwõt kememej nan eo an Paracelsus kin ta eo ej kōmman musk eo emman tata. Bareinwõt jab meloklok mol eo ekabwilõñlõñ bwe jen aolep men ko rejjab emõn, den in Cologne, ilo ien an kar jino kõmmane, ej men eo enana tata.

Ikõnan kajemlok jebta in kin apil eo ilõñ, ak ijjab maroñ, kin aõ inebata in jolok juõn nan in kinaak eo ekkã an walok nae ri wia ko, im eo, ilo lemnak eo an jet ro emwij air lemnak, emaroñ kar watõke einwõt juõn men eo ejjab alikar kin ta eo kar ba kin whale ko ruo an Frenchman eo. Ilo bar juon jikin ilo bok in, naan in riab eo emōj an kar kajekdon, bwe jerbal in whale ej ilo juon jerbal eo ejjab erreo. Ak ewōr bar juon men ñan bōbrae. Rej kwalok bwe aolep whale ko rej iien otemjej bwiin nana. Kiiō ewi wawein an kar walok stigma in?

Ij lemnok, bwe ej alikar an walok jen ien eo moktata an wa in wia ko an Greenland kar itok ilo London, elaplok jen ruo buki yio ko remootlok. Bwe ri wia ro rar jab ilo ien eo, im rejjab kiõ, kajeoñ oil eo air ilo lojet einwõt an wa ko ilo Southern kar kõmmane ien otemjej; ak mwijbar blubber eo ekãl ilo mõttan ko redik, jolok e ilo bung hole ko ilo cask ko relap, im bõklok ñan mweo ilo wãwen in; joñan an dik ien eo ilo Icy Seas ko, im jorrāān ko rekajur im rekajur rej bed ie, im bōbrae jabdrewot ial ko jet. Tokjen eo ej, bwe ilo ien eo ej deloñ ilo hold eo, im jolok juon ian jikin lũp ko an whale kein, ilo Greenland dock eo, juon menin mour ej walok jen an kar kalbwin juon lũp ilo jikin kwelok eo etto, ñan pedped in juon Lying-in Hospital.

Ij barāinwōt ļōmņak, bwe naan in nana in ņae ri-whale ro emaron̄ barāinwōt kar ļōmņak kōn an kar pād ilo tōrerein Greenland, ilo iien ko etto, juon bukwōn in Dutch etan Schmerenburgh ak Smeerenberg, eo etan āliktata ej eo kar kōjerbale jān rimāletlet eo Fogo Von Slack, ilo jerbal eo an eļap kōn Smells, juon bok in katak kōn menin. Einwot an etan kwalok (smeer, fat; berg, to put up), bukwōn in kar ejaak bwe en maroñ in wōr juon jikin ñan blubber eo an Dutch whale fleet eo ñan an maroñ in māllejoñ, ilo an jab bōklok ñan Holland kin un in. Ear juon jikin kōmat kijeek, kein kōmat ko, im jikin oil ko; im ke jerbal ko rar lukkun jerbal rar jab kwalok juon bwiin emman. Ak men kein rej lukkun oktak jen juon South Sea Sperm Whaler; eo ilo juon ito itak in emen yio bōlen, elikin an kar lukkun kobrak jikin eo an kin oil, ejjab, bōlen, bōk lemñoul raan ilo jerbal in kōmat; im ilo jekjek eo ej bed ilo cask, oil eo ej epaak an ejelok bwiin. Mol eo ej, bwe mour ak mej, elañe kar kōmmane ilo jimwe, whale ko einwot juon kain rejjab menin mour ko renana; ak armij in whalemen rejjab maroñ in kile, einwot armij ro ilo ien ko etto kar jelet ir ñan lo juon ri Jew ilo kumi eo, ikijen loñin. Bareinwõt, whale eo ejjab maroñ in bar juon men jen an bwiin, ñe, einwõt juõn men eo elap, ej mõnõnõ kin ejmour eo elap; bōk elap ikkure; aolep ien ilo mweo; mekarta, ej mol, ejjab lukkun walok ilo mejatoto. Ij ba, bwe mōkaj in an juon Sperm Whale mōñā ion den ej kōmman juon perfume, einwot ñe juon kōrā eo ewōr an musk-scented ej mōñā nuknuk eo an ilo juon jikin kiki eo ebwil. Ta eo inaj keidi ñan Sperm Whale kin an bwiin, ñe jej lemnok kin joñan an lap? Ejjab ke ñan elephant eo elap an jelā kake, kin menin mour ko rebin, im ewor myrrh ie, eo kar bōk e jen juon jikin kwelok in India ñan kautiej Alexander the Great?





JAPTER 93. Ri jolok eo.

Ear jejjo wōt raan ko elikin an kar iion ri France eo, ke juon men eo elap an aorōk ear walok ñan eo ediktata an aorōk ian rijerbal ro an Pequod; juon men eo ekauwatata; im eo ear jemlok ilo an kar lelok ñan wa eo jet ien elap an mõnõnõ im kar karõke mokta kin juõn kanan eo emour im ej bed ibben don kin jabdewõt men eo ear walok tokelik emaroñ kar kalikar an make.

Kiiō, ilo wa in whale, ejjab aolep rej etal ilo wa ko. Jet armij rej bed ie rej naetan ship-keepers, ro rej jerbal ilo wa eo ilo ien eo wa ko rej lor whale eo. Einwot juon men eo elap, ri lale wa rein rej lukkun kajoor einwot armij ro rej jerbal ilo wa ko. Ak elañe ewōr juon eo edik, ejjab emman, ak ej mijak ilo wa eo, armij eo enaj lukkun kōmmane juon ri lale wa. Ear āindein ilo Pequod ippān negro eo edik Pippin kōn etan eo edik, Pip kōn etan eo edik. Pip eo ejeramel! kom ar roñ kin e mokta; komij aikuj kememej tamborin eo an ilo lukwon boñ eo, elap an mōņōņō.

Ilo turin, Pip im Dough-Boy rar kōmman juon ikkure, einwot juon kidia ebōrōrō im juon eo ekilmeej, jen eddōklok ko rejimwe, meñe rejjab einlok wōt kilin, kar kōmmane ilo juon wōt span eo eoktak. Ak ke Dough-Boy eo ejjab jerammōn ekar lukkun mōjno im mōjņo ilo lemnok ko an, Pip, meñe ekar lukkun jouj, ekar lukkun meram, ippāñ meram eo emman, joij, im jouj eo ej make wōt ñan bwij eo an; juon bwij, eo ej mõnõnõ kin aolep raan in kakkije ko im kwojkwoj ko kin mõnõnõ eo emõnlok, im anemkwoj jen jabdewõt bwij ko jet. Ñan armij ro rekilmeej, calendar in yio eo ej aikuj kwalok jabdrewot ak jilu bukwi im jiljinoñoul-lalem raan in allōñ in Juļae im raan in yio ko rekāāl. Jab ettōñ dikdik, ilo ien eo ij jeje bwe black in edik ear lukkun emman, bwe meñe marok ewōr an meram; lo ebony eo emera, kar kōmmane ilo cabinet ko an king. Ak Pip ear yokwe mour, im aolep men ko rejimwe ilo mour; bwe jerbal eo ekar kōmman an armij inebata eo ekar bed ie, ekar lukkun kalijeklok meram eo an; meñe, einwot an kar to an naaj walok, ta eo kar kōmmane ilo e, ilo jemlokin kar kōmmane bwe en meramlok kin kijeek ko reoktak jen dron, ko rar kwalok e ñan joñoul alen joñan an meram ilo Tolland County eo an ilo Connecticut, ear juon ien kōmman bwe en mour elōn fiddler ro ilo greenfrolic ko; im ilo ien eo emman, ippāñ an gay ha-ha! ear ukōt lal in mejatoto eo ñan juon tambourine eo ewōr iju ko ie. Innem, meñe ilo mejatoto eo ealikar ilo raan eo, ej dāpij juon bōran eo ebōrōrō, diamond eo erreo enaj meram; ak, ke rikōmman jewel eo ej kwalok ñan yuk diamond eo ilo an lukkun meram, ej likite ion juon bwidej eo emarok, innem ej kōmeramlok, jab kin al, ak kin jet gas ko rejjab menin mour. Innem rej walok meram in kijeek ko, relukkun emman; innem diamond eo ej urur, juon ien ej kakōlkōl eo elaptata an lañ ko, ej einwot juon crown-jewel kar bōk jen King in Hell. Ak jen etal ñan bwebwenato eo.

Ear walok, bwe ilo ambergris affair eo Stubb’s after-oarsman ear maroñ in rup pein, einwot an kar lukkun mōjno; im, jidik ien, kar likit Pip ilo jikin eo an.

Iien eo moktata Stubb ear wanlaḷḷọk ippān, Pip ear kwaḷọk an inepata; ak ilo mõnõnõ, ilo ien eo, ear ko jen an epaak ibben whale eo; im kin menin ear jab lukkun kalijeklok; meñe Stubb ej kalimjōk e, ear lale, tokelik, ñan kōketak e bwe en kautiej peran eo an ñan joñan eo elaptata, bwe emaron kar loe bwe ej aikuj.

Kiiō ilo an kar wanlaļļo̧k kein karuo, wa eo eaar ettōr ioon whale eo; im ke ek eo ear bōk māāl eo, ear lelok rap eo an, eo ear walok, ilo ien in, bwe en bed iumin jikin jijet eo an Pip. Inebata eo ear walok ilo ien eo ear kōmman an ettōr, ilo pein, jen wa eo; im ilo juon wewein, bwe mõttan in slack whale line eo ej itok nae lojen, ear bōk e ilo lojet ippān, bwe en maroñ in dāpij e, ke ear āliktata wōtlo̧k ilo dān eo. Ilo ien eo wōt whale eo ear jorrāān ear jino an ettōr, line eo ear mõkaj an jimwe; im presto! Pip eo ejeramel ear itok im mōñā ñan tōrerein wa eo, ilo an jab būromõj kar jibwe e ñan ijo kin line eo, eo ear bōk elōn an oktak ko iturin lojen im bōran.

Tashtego ear jutak ilo bow ko. Ekar obrak kin kijeek in mōñā eo. Ear dike Pip kin an kar juon poltroon. Ilo an kar bōk boat-knife eo jen kilin, ear likit tōrerein eo an ebin ion line eo, im oktaklok ñan Stubb, im ba ilo an kajitõk, “Cut?” Ilo ien in turin mejen Pip eo ebūrōrō im ekar mōrā ekar alikar an kalimjōk, Do, kin Anij! Aolep rar eļļā ilo juon wōt iien. Ilo diklok jen jimettan minit, men in ear walok.

“Damn him, cut!” Stubb ekar lamij; innem whale eo ear jako im Pip ear lomoren.

Ilo ien eo wot ke ear bar mour, negro eo edik ejeramel ear bōk jorrāān jen lamõj im kajirere jen ri jerbal ro. Ilo an kar kōtlok bwe naan in kajjirere kein ren jako, Stubb innem ilo juon wāween eo ealikar, einwot business, ak ejjab lukkun ettōn, ear kajjirere kake Pip ilo kien; im men in ear walok, ear lewoj ñan e elap nan in kakabilek ko remman. Men eo ej, Jab ettōr jen juon wa, Pip, ijellokin—ak aolep men ko jet rar jab jemlok, einwot nan in kakabilek eo emman tata. Kiiō, ilo kakobaba, Dāpdep wōt ilo wa eo , ej motto eo am emol ilo ikkure in whaling; ak jet ien enaj walok ñe Leap jen wa eo , ej emmanlok wōt. Bareinwõt, einwõt ñe ej kile ilo jemlokin bwe elañe ej lelok nan in kakabilek ko rejimwe ñan Pip, enaj kar likit e juõn jikin elap ñan ilju im jeklaj; Stubb ear mōkaj an jolok aolep nan in kakabilek ko, im kar kajemlok kin juon kakien eo ekajur, “Kwon bed wot ilo wa eo, Pip, ak ilo Iroij, Ijjab naaj bōk eok elañe kwoj ettōr; kememej men in. Jejjab maroñ in jolok whale ko kin ro einwot kwe; juon whale enaj kar wiakake kin jiljinoñoul alen joñan am kar wiakake, Pip, ilo Alabama Bear ilo lemnok in jab ettōr bar juon alen.” Ilo ijin bōlen Stubb ear kwalok ilo jab mol, bwe meñe armij ej yokwe ro mōttan, ak armij ej juon menin mour eo ej kōmman money, eo ej lukkun bōbrae an jouj.

Ak jej aolep bed ilo pein Anij ro; im Pip ear bar ettōr. Ear bed ilo jekjek ko relukkun einlok wōt juon ñan ikkure eo moktata; ak ilo ien in ear jab jolok line eo; im kin menin, ke whale eo ear jino ettōr, Pip ear bed wōt ilo lojet, einwot juon ri ito-itak eo ej mōkaj. A! Stubb ekar lukkun mol ñan nan eo an. Ekar juon raan eaiboojoj, elap, im blue; lojet eo emera ej kalmenlokjen im molo, im ej eddeklok, aolep jikin, ñan mejatoto, einwot kilin gold-beater eo emōj an rup ñan joñan eo elaptata. Ilo an mōkaj ilo lojet eo, bōran ebon eo an Pip ej walok einwot juon bōran clove. Ejelok wa eo ear bōk jikin ke ear wōtlo̧k ilo m̧ōkaj astern. Stubb ear oktaklok ñan e; im whale eo ekar wōr pein. Ilo jilu minit, juon m̧aiļ in lojet eo ejjeļo̧k tōrerein eaar pād ikōtaan Pip im Stubb. Jen lukkun lojet, Pip eo ejeramel ear ukõt bõran eo ebin, ebin, im ebwil ñan al, bar juon eo ej make iaer, meñe eo eutiejtata im elaptata an meram.

Kiiō, ilo ien ko rejimwe, ñan tutu ilo lojet ej einwot an bin ñan ri ettor eo emōj an katak einwot an uwe ilo juon spring-carriage ñan āne. Ak make ian eo enana ejjab maroñ in kijenmij. Eļap an ļap aō ļōmņak kōn kwe make ilo m̧ōttan eo eļap an ejjeļo̧k būruwōn, aō Anij! wōn emaron in kwalok? Mark, ewi wawein an ri wa ko ilo juon jikin kalmenlakjān tutu ilo lojet—kōmeļeļeik ewi joñan aer lukkun jibwe wa eo waer im baj etal wōt iturin.

Ak Stubb ear ke lukkun jolok negro eo edik ejeramel ñan an mour? Jab; ear jab kōnaan, ilo ien eo ediktata. Bwe ekar wor ruo wa ko ilo ial eo an, im ekar lemnok, ejelok bere, bwe renaj kar itok ñan Pip ilo mõkaj, im bōk e; meñe, emool, kain lemnok rot kein ñan ri-kōmman wa ko rej bed ilo kauwatata kin aer make mijak, ejjab aolep ien walok jen ri-kattōr ro ilo aolep ien ko einlok wõt; im kain jekjek rot kein rejjab walok ilo ien otemjej; enañin aolep ien ilo jikin ek ko, juon ri mijak, rej naetan, ej bōk ejja dike eo ejjab tiriamokake eo ej walok ñan ri tarinae ro im jar in tarinae ko.

Ak ekar walok, bwe wa ko, ilo air jab lo Pip, rar lukkun lale whale ko iturin ir ilo juon ian, rar oktak, im lukwarkwar; im wa eo an Stubb ekar lukkun ettolok, im e im aolep rijerbal ro an ekar lukkun kōnan ek ko an, bwe Pip ekar jino an eddeklok iturin ilo an nana. Ilo ien eo wōt wa eo make ear lomoren e; ak jen awa eo negro eo edik ear etal ilo deck eo juon ri bwebwe; āindein, ediktata, raar ba eaar. Lojet eo ear dāpij enbwinnin eo edik, ak ear jolok jetōb eo an. Ejjab lukkun tulo, mekarta. Kar bōklok e mour ñan jikin ko rebin, ijo jekjek ko reoktak jen lal eo etto rar etal im itok iman mejen ko an; im armij eo ejeramel, Mālōtlōt, ear kwalok men ko an rekar dāpij; im ilubwiljin ien ko remõnõnõ, ejelok burueir, im ejjelok ien, Pip ear lo elõñ, Anij-ebed ijoko otemjej, insect in coral ko, ko jen mejatoto in dren ko rar walok orb ko relap. Ear lo neen Anij ioon tōrerein loom eo, im ear kōnono kake; im kin menin ro mōttan ilo wa eo rar naetan e bwebwe. Innem bwebwe eo an armij ej melele eo an lañ; im etal jen aolep lemnok ko, armij ej itok ñan lemnok eo an lañ, eo, ñan lemnok, ej juon men eo ejjab mol im ebin; im mweie ak jorrāān, ej eñjake an jab bōjrak, jab kea einwot Anij eo an.

Ñan ro jet, jab naruon Stubb elap. Men eo ej walok ilo jikin ek ko; im ilo jemlokin bwebwenato eo, enaj walok ta eo ear walok ñan ña make.
