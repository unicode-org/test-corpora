TEEKATEEKA AHA KITONTOME.

Ekihama ky’ekitontome, ekitari ahansi y’okutunguuka kw’ekigano ky’okushekyerera, kiri omu matu g’ogwo orikukihurira. Yeikiriza okuroga kwayo, oikirize omutima gw’omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo: eki, bwanyima y’ebyo byona, nikyo abanyabwengye barikugarukamu waababuuza omugasho gwayo. N’obu omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo we wenka, omu mbeera ye endiijo, araabe naakugambira ngu eby’obuhangwa bye n’obukodyo bw’emikono kwonka, eby’okurya by’omufumu we w’eby’okurya, kandi akakuhayo kukworeka obukodyo bwabyo,—otamwikiririzamu. Mutegyereze eshaaha ye ey’obunabi; reero oyeheereze eki arikukunda munonga, okushemererwa kwe nari obusaasi bwe. "Turi omu mikono ya Rukundo erizooba!" Gautier naayeshongora, omu kushoboorora kwa Swinburne okurikushemeza,—kandi kuruga omu kasheeshe kuhika omu kugwa kw’eizooba nitugyendera aha nyanja y’obushaarizi: hariho rukundo emwe yonka, May emwe, orukoba rumwe orw’ebimuri. Rukundo n’ey’ebiro byona, ebindi byona ti by’amazima kandi biteirwe aha rubaju. Okworekwa kwine enkomerero, ekishushani nikihinduka; kwonka twine eki twatunga, okwijuka ekirikushemeza. Nk’abahandiiki b’ebitebyo baingi, n’ebishushani bingi. Hariho okushemeza kwa Evanescence, okurikuha oburungi oburikukirayo n’embabazi aureole ya Pathos. Gaaniira na Landor "ekiro kye kimwe eky'okwijuka n'okuhuuta" ahabwa Rose Aylmer, kandi eki oine omu bwijwire.

Kandi hati kwata omukono gw'omuteezi w'ebyeshongoro by'ensi ensya, owahabire kuruga omu mwanya gumwe oguhikire gw'okutuuramu kuza omu America egyo ey'obushema kandi etarikuhikaana, nk'oku Baudelaire yaareebire, "ekaba eri ahari Poe ekihome kihango kyonka eki yaabaire naaza kwirukamu, aha n'aha, n'okushoberwa kw'omushwija kw'ekihangirwe ekyahangirwe kuhuuta omu nsi eshemeire," kandi ahu pofe, his e in, omusinzi, kikaba kiri okuteeraho kumwe okutarikuhwaho okuhunga okukwatwaho kw'embeera egi ey'okuhakanisa." Kwata omukono gw’omweshongozi oine oburemeezi orikuhurira nk’ogwo, kandi omugambire aha mbeera y’obwire ei yaashangiremu, — hatariho bwire bwona omu izooba ryona, obwire bwona omu kiro, — ku kiraabe kitari Atlantis ahu yaabaire azengyereire, haakiri omwanya gw’okwehungiramu kuruga omu nshonda ezi yaabaire akingirwe omu ihangwe.

Omu nsi emwe yonka aine obushoboorozi bw’okukutwara, kandi ekiro kimwe kyonka noobaasa kugaaniira aha kirooto kye. Turakiti etaine Nsi nari Iguru: "Ensi y'omuntu weena," kuruga omu Mwanya, kuruga omu Bwiire. Aha hariho abarikuteganisibwa, kurabira omu maisho gaabo, nk’aga Cenci, omutima nigutunga amadirisa n’obu ebiteekateeko biraabe bitaine maani; aha emyoyo, erikuteera aha muhanda ogurikuhitsya omu Butahwaho, neeyemerezibwa kandi ekyerererwa. Ni limbo ya "emyoyo y'enyonyoozi," ahu hariho obutamanya bwona obw'omushana gw'okwezi, rukundo zoona ezibuzire n'ebishuba. Monodies zaayo n’ebitontome ikumi na bibiri, ebi ebyeshongoro byabyo birikuteeraho kuhinduka kwonka ever nibishushana. Kimwe aha kimwe nibihurirwa, nk'okuteera kw'eshaha y'omuringa n'ey'omuti gw'omusheenyi, omu "The Masque of the Red Death," ekyareeteire aba waltzers kuhuumuraho n'"okushoberwa n'okutetema n'okuteekateeka," emirundi mingi nk'oku eshaaha yaabaire neehika.

Omuri ebi byona eby’amazima, okwetomboita kwa The Raven , okurikutengeeta kurabira omu muryango, okukira munonga kushemeize ensi ey’aheeru. Ni bintu ki ebirikuza omu kukora ekitontome,—kandi nibihikiirira bita omuri eki, nk'omu bindi bingi, oruganda orwo orwayetsire abanyabwengye barwo "abakozi"? Omurimo nigwetwa kuruga omu busha. Aho haabaire hatariho kintu kyona, nikigumaho,—ekihangirwe kisya, ekicweka ky’obugaiga bw’abantu. Kandi ebigambo bikye eby’omutaano, okukira ebindi ebiine obuhanguzi oburikushushana, nibitugyema kuteekateeka busya oku akacumu k’omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo karikuhindura ebintu ebitarikumanywa n’obumanzi .

Buri kimwe nikireebeka nk’ekitaine ky’okureeberaho, kwonka byona nibitushemeza n’ebintu ebiihirwe omu kicuucu ky’Obuhangwa. Hati okuteekateeka kw’ahaiguru munonga nikufayo aha mwoyo gw’ebintu; nikibaasa nari kitarikubaasa kureetaho Fantasy ngu abantu baine ebishushani by’omunda y’okwezi ebitarikwetegyerezibwa gye. Kwonka nabwo, kimwe aha bigambo ebi, omu muringo gwakyo mukye, nikitukwataho n'okuhurira ngu turi ab'omutaano, nk'oku emirimo y'omutaano ey'abazaani b'omutaano eri, - "Faustus" ya Marlowe, "Faust" ya Goethe, "Manfred," nari nangwa n'ebyo bishushani by'omutaano, "The Mimmersure" na "The Mimmersure." Ebirikukira ahari kimwe, n’obu biraabe biri eby’omutaano, biine ebimwe . omugugu nari okwetantara okurikuteganisa okwijuka,—kuhurira, kutakyebwa, nk’eiraka ry’ekicweka ky’omubiri ekitarikukira kukoresibwa kwonka ekirikutaanisaho. Ekikuru omurizo n'ekyeshongoro kya Bürger ekya "Lenore," ekyo kitambi ky'emizimu kandi ekirikuhurirwa, ekirikuteganisa n'ekirikuteganisa abavunuuzi. Bakye nibo barikwehakana ngu "Rime y'omuvugi w'enyanja owa ira" eya Coleridge ey'okutangaaza eyemereire aha mutwe gwabo gwonka. "Le Juif-Errant" akaba naija kuba aine ebi arikugira, kuri Beranger yaabaire ari omuhandiiki w'ebitebyo mukuru; kandi, kwonka ahabw'okugira ngu bari hare n'okusaasira abantu, "Omukazi wa Shalott" na "Damozel Oine Omugisha" nibibaasa kwongyerwa aha runyiriri. Kikaheebwa Edgar Allan Poe kukora ebigambo bibiri, "The Bells" na The Raven , buri kimwe, n'obu kiraabe nikibaasa kuba kiine oburungi bukye kukira ebya Tennyson na Rossetti, n'eky'omutaano. Kwonka The Raven , nka "Endongo" na "Annabel Lee," neeyehimbisa baingi n'abakye. Nkagamba ahandi ngu ebihandiiko bya Poe ebitarikukira kureebwa bikareebeka ahariinye "ebyo ebirimu okureebeka, haakiri, kw'okwetunguura,-omu arikwikiriza enyehurira ze, obwo arikufa arikugwa kandi akateera ebyeshongoro bingi, ebirikukirayo obusaasi, nibiruga ahariwe atamanyire." Eki n’obunaku obu nkirikwikiriza; kandi obwo, ku ndikugaruka kwega ekitontome eki, nikinshemeza munonga okukira obwire bwona kuruga omu buto bwangye. Okumanyana kwa haihi nikugambira omu kushagika buri murimo gw’obuhangwa ogw’amazima. Reeba omushaija, otarikumanya eby’obuhangwa nari okubifaho, kushwijuma ekitontome nari ekishushani ekimwe, kandi oku amaani gaakyo garikumukwata juba! Nangwa, naija kurenga aha mugasho gw’omurimo murungi ogw’okubanza ogu omuteireho omutima gwe omu muringo gw’oburingaaniza. Raven , nayo, eine emitwarize etarikuhinduka ei n’omuhangu aine kukunda. Tihariho omu bitontoma ebindi eby’omuhandiiki waayo ahu ekigyendererwa kirikushoboororwa gye nk’oku kiri. "The Haunted Palace" n'eky'amazima nk'oku kiri aha mushomi otoorainwe, kwonka Poe akaba atarikubaasa kutwara enfumu egyo etarikwetegyerezibwa ahabw'okushwijuma ekiharaani. Ekinyonyi ekirikwetwa Raven kyona kiine omutwe gw’omuhandiiki ogurikukirayo—okufeerwa okutarikubaasa kugarukaho kw’omukazi orikuramibwa kandi omurungi; kwonka aha nshonga ezindi, nazo, obuhangwa bw’abantu boona nibuhikiirira omu kuteekateeka ngu n’ekitontome kye ekirikujwekyera.

Omuntu w’obwengye nakira kutunga ekigyere ahabw’obusinguzi bw’okuteeraho kumwe, kandi eki tikirikuba kiri ekikuru ahariwe obwire bwona. Okumanyirwa nikwo kurikukirayo ahonaaho ahabw’okuyongyerwayo. Tarikukitunga, nk'obugaiga bw'omunyabwengye, ekicweka ky'esente aha kindi, kwonka "tihariho na kakye nari byona omuri byona." Kandi nikwo n’ebindi bigyendererwa: omugabe, omuserukare, omuzaani,—n’obu baakuba baine ebicweka ki,—buri omwe nabara obusinguzi bwe kuruga omu kuteera kw’omugisha gumwe. Poe's Raven, n'obu araabe yaabaire ari augury, ahariwe ekaba eri "ekinyonyi ekyareeteire omuyaga kuhunga." Omuhandiiki w'ebitebyo akatuura omuri New-York, omu bwire bw'obutiti bwa 1844-'45, yaatunga omurimo aha rupapura rwa Willis, "The Evening Mirror," kandi yaatunga sente ze arikuhayo sente ahandi. Akaba amazire emyaka mukaaga ari omuhandiiki orikukora munonga, kandi aine eiziina ry’obukugu; akakwatwa omu kitiinisa n’omu kubangaanisa, kandi akaba ataine ki yaafeerwa ahabw’omugabo gwe ogw’amakuru agatarikushashurwa gye eizooba eryo. Kandi akaba akozire emirimo ye mirungi munonga, nk'ebitebyo nka "Ligeia" na "Okugwa kw'enju ya Usher," (eky'aha muheru kirimu ekigambo ky'amazima, omu runyiriri, ekya Elihu Vedder "A Lost Mind,") nk'ebikorwa by'okucondooza nka "The Gold Bug" na "Ekihama kya Marie Roget." Akaba ahindukire abantu aheeru y’ihanga, kandi akatunga obushoboorozi bw’obutwire omu biteekateeko by’Abafaransa. Akaba ayegire amaani ge n’obugobya bwe, kandi akaba ari omu bwire bwe bw’okutunguuka, kandi ataine iziina rimwe. Kwonka akaba ataine ki yaahandiikire ekyabaire kiri aha rurimi rwa buri omwe. Aha mirimo etarikukira kureebwa kandi egumire, okukwataho okumwe okurikumanywa munonga kushemerire kwongyerwaho kubaasa kukwata abantu boona ab‘obwire bw‘omuntu.

Kurabira omu nkora y’abahandiiki b’ebitebyo bya Poe abakuratana, ekigano ekyakozirwe The Raven kihindukire ekigano ekirikukira kugambwa. Omukazi w’omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo omuto, Virginia, akaba naahwaho omu maisho ge, kwonka akagumaho omwaka ogundi omu kicuucu ky’okufa. Ekishengye kiraingwa kandi eky’ahansi omu nju eri haihi n’omuhanda gwa Bloomingdale kiri nk’oku . ekimanyirwe nk’ekishengye eki Rouget de l’Isle yaahandiikiremu ebyeshongoro bya Marseillaise. Boona bahuriire ngu ekitontome eki, ekihandiikirweho omukono gwa "Quarles," kikaza omu "American Review," kiine ekigambo ky'okuhandiika eky'ebishuba aha muringo gwakyo; ngu Poe akatunga doola ikumi ahabw’ekyo; ngu Willis, owaabaire aine embabazi kandi otaine eihari aha baramuzi b’omurembe, akagaruka yaakihandiika n’ebigambo by’okuhimbisa ebyabaire nibigambwaho ahonaaho omu tauni. Okubanganisa kwona aha buhandiiki bwayo kukahwaho obu Poe yaakishoma we wenka omu ruteerane rw’ebihandiiko, kandi kumara obwire akaba ariwe orikukirayo kumanyirwa aha bahandiiki ba America. Okuteerwa kukamugaruramu amaani kandi kwamugaruramu amaani. Nk'ondiijo kandi omunyabwengye orikwehimbisa, nawe akashanga "ekintu ky'omushana" nangwa n'omu "kuhurira kw'ebinyonyi." Eky’obusaasi, kwonka emyaka eshatu ekahinguraho, — obwire bw’okutegyeka, okwehimbisa, obusaasi, kwonka buriijo obw’okuteekateeka nari okukora munonga, — okusingurwa okwahamuheru kutakatandikire, omukingo gutakagwire aha magara agaabaire gari omu mazima ag’obusaasi, kandi akaikiriza “Ekicuncu Omusinguzi.”

"The American Review: A Whig Journal" ekaba eri magaziini erikuheebwa ekitiinisa omu bwire obwo, erimu enyuguta ibiri, ehandiikirwe aha mpapura nungi n'ebigambo ebirikwetegyerezibwa gye, kandi erimu ebishushani bya mezzotint. Omu bintu bingi ebiri ahansi y’omutindo oguriho hati, kikaba kirimu ebimwe ebi omuhandiiki weena yaabaire naija kushemererwa kutunga. Omuzingo gw’okubanza, ogw’omwaka 1845, gurimu ebihandiiko ebyahandiikirwe Horace Greeley, Donald Mitchell, Walter Whitman, Marsh, Tuckerman, na Whipple. Ekitontome kya Ralph Hoyt eky'omutaano, "Old," kikareebeka omu kitabo eki. Kandi hanu hariho ebigambo bishatu ebyahandiikirwe Poe: "Orurembo omu Nyanja," "Oruhanga rw'obutabanguko," na The Raven . Ebibiri ahari ebi bikaba byombekirwe, — nikwo omuringo gwe gwabaire guri, — kuruga omu kucondooza okwabandize, kwonka ekyahererukireyo kikarugayo nk’ekihandiikirwe busya, kandi haihi nk’oku tukiine hati. Ekigambo ekirikugira ngu bwanyima tikiragarukiremu n’ekigwire. Empindahinduka ikumi n’emwe ezitari nkuru kuruga omu bihandiiko bya magaziini nizireebeka omu kitabo The Raven and Other Poems , 1845, ekitabo eki omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo yaahuriire ngu omu bwire bukye akahigwa kuha abantu. Ebi nibikira kuba biri empindahinduka z’obubonero, nari ez’ebigambo bimwe, omuringo gw’aha muheru ogurikukorwa kwongyera aha nkora y’okuteerana. Omu nvunuura nungi eya Mr. Lang ey'orunyiriri rwa Poe, eyaareetsirwe omu "Parchment Library," ayorekire obuhanguzi bw'omunyabwengye, kandi akozire n'obwengye, omu kugaruka omu bihandiiko ebiri omu kitabo eki twaheza kugambaho, nk'oku kyaheebwa omu rupapura rwa London orwa 1846. Omurimo "gw'omutindo" ogwa Griswold posauet' collection. Ebi bikagarukwamu abahandiiki abakuratsireho, nabo bakakora enshobe zaabo. Kwonka ebihandiiko bya The Raven , ahabw’okushaba okwakozirwe omuhandiiki ngu akore kopi z’ebihandiiko, bikaba bikyebeirwe hare munonga; omu mazima, akagiteera omuri Richmond, atakafiire, n'okuhindura omu muringo gw'ebitebyo "seraphim ou ebigyere bye birikugwa" omu mwanya gwa "bamaraika abaine ebigyere birikugwa ebikye," omu nshonga ya ikumi n'ena. Nahabwekyo, ekihandiiko kyaitu ekya hati, n’obu kiraabe kiri eky’omu mwaka gwa 1845, kihindwirwemu kakye ahabw’okushoma kw’omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo bwanyima, kandi ninteekateeka ngu nikyo kihikire kandi ekirikukorera gye ekitontome eki kimwe. Empindahinduka erikukirayo obuhango kuruga omu nvunuura eyabandize ekareebeka, kwonka, omu kitabo ekyahandiikirwe omu mwaka gwa 1845; omurundi gwa ikumi n’ogumwe omu kubanza gukaba gurimu enyuguta ezi, zirimu obuzibu omu kugamba n’ebindi biine akamogo aha kitontoma:

"Akwatsirwe kuruga ahari mukama omwe otashemereirwe, ou Ekihikiirizi ekitaine saasi .
 Akakuratira juba kandi yaakuratira juba—n’ahabw’ekyo, Hope ku yaabaire arahira, .
 Stern Despair akagaruka, omu mwanya gw’Amatsiko marungi agu yaabaire aine obumanzi bw’okurahira—
 Ekyo kyokugarukamu ekyobusaasi, 'Tihariho!'"

Nikiba kiri kirungi abandi, kandi abamanyirwe, abahandiiki b’ebitebyo baaba nibabaasa kuhamya ngu empindahinduka zaabo niziija kuba ez’okutunguuka obwire bwona. Poe akaba aguma naagaruka omu kworeka kwe okukye okw’enyuguta, kandi obwire obwingi akaba agiboneza. Ekicuncu nikyo kyabaire eky’okubanza omu bitontome bikye ebi yaabaire ari haihi kuheza atakahandiikire. Nikibaasa kuba ngu abo abarikufayo aha ntambi bakafeerwa bikye ahabw’okufa kwe. Okumanya gye ebigambo by’ebigambo, tarahandiikire runyiriri ahabw’okwenda kukora ekitontome. Refrain y’ekishushani ku yaabaire neemuteganisa, ebigambo ebyarugiremu bikaba biri okuhiga, nk’oku we wenka yaagambire, okw’okukunda munonga, beitu kutari kw’ekigyendererwa. Eki kikaba kiri omu bwire bukye munonga nk‘oku kyabaire kiri haihi kuhakanisa enteekateeka ya Fairfield ngu buri emwe ekaba eri ekirugire omu buremeezi bw‘obusaasi.

Mbwenu shi niki ekyahaire omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo ekishushani kye ekya The Raven ? Ni musingi ki ogurimu oruho ogu kyaimukire mpora mpora kuhika aha muziki ogurikuhuuta mpora mpora? Nk’oku kyabaire kiri butoosha, ebintu ebirikukira ahari kimwe bikaza omu kwombeka ekitontome ekirikumanyirwa munonga. Okuteekateeka aha kubuurira okuraingwa okurikukira kubuurirwa aha bihandiiko bikye kandi ebitarikureetaho ebiteekateeko bingi, nyine amatsiko ngu tindikwija kujunaanizibwa ahabw’okugaaniira oku aha ntambi emwe, —na munonga emwe ei omunyabwengye nka Doré akozire nk’eky’okureeberaho ky’okuburabura. Okuhitsya obu nkaba ninteekateeka ngu ekicweka eki, n’ebikye ebi omuhandiiki waakyo yaahandiikire bwanyima y’okureebeka, bikaba biri eby’omutaano omu kutakura kuruga omu bukooko omu runyiriri rwe orw’omwojo. Kwonka Mr. Fearing Gill anyorekire ebicweka bimwe ebitakashohoziibwe ebya Poe, ebyahandiikirwe omu mwaka gwe ogwa ikumi n'omunaana, kandi ebiine omutwe, "Omuzimu w'omuriro." Ekihandiiko eki nikireebeka nk’ekihandiikirwe omu ngaro z’omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo omu kubanza, kandi obuhame bwakyo nibuhamibwa eka ei kigumireho kumara ekicweka ky’ekyasha. Oyihireho obukooko oburikworeka gye "The Bells" na "The Haunted Palace" harimu emiringo mikye erikworeka okutandika n'okukinga kwa The Raven . Aha bikwatiraine n'omuhimbo gw'ekitontome kyaitu, okugyeragyeranisa ebiro nikworeka ngu eki kikakwatwaho omuhimbo gwa "Lady Geraldine's Courtship." Poe akaba ari omwe aha baabandize kuha ekitiinisa obuhanguzi bwa Miss Barrett; akamuhandiikira ebitontome bye ebi yaabaire ateeraine nk'ogwo "orikukirayo ekitiinisa omu kihama kye," kandi akaba ari omu 1914. okusaasirana n’omuringo gwe gw’okuhandiika ebyeshongoro. Enyuguta kuruga omu kitontoma kye kya rukundo,

"N'okutongana okutarikuhamibwa, omu mwanya, omukingo gwa papo
 Okuzimba omunda kandi kuzimba aheeru okwehinguririza ebishuba bye ebitarikutambura,"

akashanga eco omuri ebi:

"Kandi okuhurira okutarikwetegyerezibwa okwa bujwa okwa buri mukingo gwa papo
 Kikanshemeza—kinyijuza okutiina oku ntakahuriraga."

Aha Poe akatwara omugisha ogu yaabaire naajumirira Longfellow, kandi ogu hatariho n’omwe owaabaire naasherura omu mwanya gwe wenka ataine kukyebera ahonaaho n’okufubirwa. Omu muringo gw’okuhimbisa n’omu ntambi, nitwija kureeba ngu ebitontome ebi byombi tibirikushushana, kwonka omu kigyendererwa nibitaanira kimwe. Omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo omugabi tarahuriire kintu kyona kureka obukomooko bw’ebitebyo obwabuzima. "Eki kihandiiko ekirikworeka gye!" akagira ati,—"amaani aga agarikuhurirwa!... Omuhandiiki waitu omukuru, Mr. Browning, omuhandiiki wa 'Paracelsus,' &c., aine ekihika omu kukunda kwe omuhimbo." Mr. Ingram, bwanyima y'okugamba ahari "Lady Geraldine," omu bwengye naayoreka enshonga endiijo ei Poe arikubaasa kuba yaakwatsireho omutima. Omu mwaka gwa 1843, Albert Pike, omuhandiiki w'ebitebyo kuruga Arkansas, akaba ahandiikire omu "The New Mirror," ei Poe yaabaire naahandiika, enyuguta ezimwe eziine omutwe "Isadore," kwonka kuruga obwo zikagarurwamu omuhandiiki kandi zikeetwa "Omutima gw'omufaakazi." Nintoorana omu kugarukamu kwa Mr. Pike orunyiriri oru, orurimu ebikuru ebirikuhikaana n’envunuura y’okubanza:

"Ntaine busingye ningyendera omu bishengye byaitu eby'okwehereera, tindikugaruka kuteera ebyeshongoro byaitu,
 Eizooba eririkwaka gye nirijwara gye ahansi etaine nshonga;
 Ekinyonyi ekirikushekyerera nikiguma nikishutama kyeshongora, Iwe nshonga y'obusaasi!
 Ahabw'okuba omutima gwangye nigushusha nk'ekicu ky'omu bwire bw'enjura;
 Okabura ahariinye ebiro byoona, Isadore!"

Aha twine ekipimo ky’obwire buraingwa, okushushana kw’okugaruka enyima, n’okutandika kw’ekinyonyi eki ekyeshongoro kyakyo kirikwongera obusaasi. Hariho emihanda endiijo erikubaasa kukuratirwa abarikwenda kumanya; okukira munonga, ekicweka eki Mr. Ingram arikutoorana kuruga omu kucondooza kwa Poe okwahamuheru okwa "Barnaby Rudge":

"Ekicuncu, nakyo, * * * nikibaasa kuba kyakozirwe, okukira oku turikukireeba hati, ekicweka ky'okuteekateeka kwa Barnaby ow'omutaano. * * * Emitwarize yaakyo neebaasa kuba yaakozire, aha bikwatiraine n'ey'omushema, ekicweka kimwe nk'oku kirikukora, omu byeshongoro, okuhererukayo omu kuha ekitiinisa empeho."

Nangwa n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo, bwanyima y’okworeka obukooko obu n’okushushana, omugasho gw’ekitontome eki nigukishangwa omu kutandika kwakyo. Ebyeshongoro ebirikugyenda omu maisho, ebicweka ebirikureebeka gye n’omushana n’ekicuucu ebirikutaana, —okukira byona, okukunda eby’omwoyo eby’ekiro, nibibihindura omurimo gw’omunyabwengye orikumanyisa. Aha bikwatiraine n'ekinyonyi ekirikutiinisa, tari nk'ebinyonyi ebindi byona eby'omu ruganda rwe, kuruga ahari "twa corbies" na "ebinyonyi bishatu" eby'abateezi b'ebyeshongoro kuhika ahari "Grip" ya Barnaby erimu ebicupuri. Aha tihariho kushushana n’enkongoro erikwetwa cawing rook erikuteganisa enju z’abanyabuzaare kandi erikuteera omusiri gw’obumanyiso; tihariho kishushani ekirikumanyisa eki Tickell yaayeshongoire ngu, obu,

"okuyomba aha dirisa ye emirundi eshatu,
 Ekicuncu kikateera eipapa ryakyo,
 Too well omwishiki owaabaire aine rukundo akaba naamanya
 Eiraka ry'okurabura."

Ekinyonyi kya Poe n’entekateeka y’omutaano; okuhinduka kw’omuntu orikucurira obusaasi n’okubura amatsiko; a sable embodied Memory, omuhandiiki w’ebyafaayo by’okucwekyerezibwa orikugumaho, ekika ky’Ekitarikugarurwaho. Okwijuka kwa Styx, kuruga “aha rubaju rw’ekiro rwa Plutonian,” nareebeka nk’omwoyo ogurikushushanisibwa ogw’omubuuzi we wenka, — ogw’ogwo otarikubaasa, otarikwija, quaff the kind nepenthe, ahabw’okuba okwijuka Lenore nikyo kyonka ekimusigaireho, kandi n’okuhwaho kw’obusaasi bwe nangwa n’ekyo kikaba nikiija kuteebwa aha rubaju.

Ekicuncu nakyo nikibaasa kutwarwa nk’ekitontome ekirikujwekyera omuhandiiki waakyo, ahabw’eky’okureeberaho kyakyo aha miteekateekyere ye yoona aha ki ekitontome kiine kuba. Ebi nibishanga omu bihandiiko bye ebirikukwata ahari "Omusingye gw'ebitebyo," "Enshonga y'orunyiriri," na "Enteekateeka y'okuhandiika." Poe akarangirira ngu "omu Byeshongoro, obundi, omwoyo nigukirayo kuhika haihi n'enkomerero mpango ei, ku gurikuhuumurizibwa Poetic Sentiment, gurikurwanira—okuhangaho Oburungi bw'ahaiguru.... Orunyiriri tirurikubaasa kutooranwa gye kukira nk'ebyeshongoro by'ahansi nari ebitarikubaasa kukora gye"; kwonka kandi, orunyiriri oru buzima ruri "Ebitontome by'Ebigambo" ni "Okuhanga kw'Oburungi omu Muhimbo,"—eki kandi tihariho ekindi. Eiraka ry’Oburungi oburikukirayo n’ery’Obusaasi. Emitwe erikukirayo kureeta obusaasi n’Okufa. Eki kiine kuba kiine akakwate n’Oburungi. "Okufa, n'ahabw'ekyo, kw'omukazi murungi, hatariho kubangaanisa, n'omutwe gw'ebitebyo ogurikukirayo omu nsi yoona,—kandi nigwo gumwe tihariho kubangaanisa ngu eminywa erikukirayo kuhikaana n'omutwe nk'ogwo n'ey'omukundwa ofiire." Ebigambo ebi eby’aha muheru nibijurizibwa omu kucondooza kwa Poe okw’obushema okw’ekitontome eki kyonka, kwonka nibyoreka gye omubaro gw’orunyiriri rwe omu bwijwire. Enshonga y'ahaiguru eya "The Bells" n'eiraka ry'ebinyonyi ebitarikuhurirwa, ebirikwehimbisa ahabw'okuremeera omutima gw'omuntu. "Lenore," The Raven , "Ogwejegyeire," "Kuza ahari Omwe omuri Paradiso," na "Ulalume" nibikora ebyeshongoro by'omutaano,—kandi "Annabel Lee," ebyahandiikirwe ebirikuhererukayo ahari byona, nibyoreka ngu omutwe gumwe gukamugira kuhitsya aha muheru. Kandi, ebi byona tihariho kintu kyaba kitari ky’ebyeshongoro, kandi ebimwe nibikwatwaho omutwarize ogwo ogwa Fantastic ogurikusisimura omutima gw’okutiina, kandi gukayongyera aha kutiina okusya aha busaasi bwonka. Kurabira omu byona nibishoboororwa gye, ahansi y’ekicuucu, rukundo y’omuhandiiki w’ekyevugo ehikire, —n’ahabw’ekyo emirundi mingi neeshushanisibwa kimwe kya kabiri ahandi, —omukazi wa Usher owaabaire aziikirwe, Lady Ligeia, omu 1914. amazima ekirikushushana n’obuhangwa bwe. Ninteekateeka ngu rukundo y'omunyabwengye ahari omwe "omu muringo" terikubaasa kuhakanisa okwehayo kwe kwona aha kiteekateeko kimwe. Omukazi ori haihi nawe aine kukoresa emibazi ye, abe weena ari omu nshonda zoona kandi ataine ki arikumara, amushemeze n’ebintu bitari bimwe na bimwe ebitarikuhwaho, nari ashemererwe kureka omugabo gw’obwesigwa bwe. Aine kuhabisa ebitarikubaasa kuhikirizibwa, kandi eki nikiguma nikimurengaho omu kukunda kwe eby’obuhangwa.

Poe, nka Hawthorne, akaija n’okugwa kw’eishomero rya Romantic, kandi tihaine owaashemereirwe kukira we okusheka akabi karyo. Kwonka omutima gwe gukaba guri aha bakundana n’ebintu byabo eby’omu burugwa-izooba nari eby’omu Gothic. Eki yaahangire, ekyabaire kiine obugaiga bwingi omu nfumu z’ebigambo, kikaba nikireebeka nk’ekimutsigireho obu yaahandiika orunyiriri; kandi okucwamu kwe kukamugira ngu ebitontome by’omubonano ebiraingwa nibirugiirira munonga aha byabaho okukira okuhimbisibwa, —kandi ngu, okugamba ebitontome by’omubonano, ebigambo by’ebitontome nibiija kumara. Nahabwekyo enteekateeka ye, ehikire n'obugarukiro bwe, ngu "'ekitontome kiraingwa' nikihakanisa omu bigambo." Ebicweka bya The Raven ni bikye kandi byorobi: omushaija, ekinyonyi, n’okwijuka kw’omutaano aha mukazi. Kwonka ekicweka ekyo nikiha okworeka kurungi kw’ebintu by’omubonano. Twine ki? Ekiro kya kabiri; ekishengye ky’ekicuucu n’ebitabo byakyo eby’ebitebyo ebyabaire byebirwe; omwegi,—Heronymus ow’obunaku obu; taapu y’ekicuncu aha rubaju rw’ekitanda; ekiro ky’obutiti n’omuriro ogurikufa; ebipande bya siriki ebirikuhanikwa omuyaga; ebirooto n’obuteesigana obutarikwetegyerezibwa obw’omwirima ogurikugarukamu; ekinyonyi ekirikwiragura, ekitari ky’obuhangwa aha kifuba ekirikwiragura; ebikwato by’okukoresa omu violet velvet n’etabaaza erikumurinkana. Ebi byona ebirikuruga omu mbeera, omushana, erangi, eiraka, n'eby'omubonano, kandi n'eby'okushemeza, kwonka eby'omutindo gw'ebitebyo ebi eby'okushemeza bitarikworekwa munonga, kandi nibyoreka gye "okukunda kw'ebiro byona, obusaasi obutahwaho" bw'omuhandiiki w'ebitebyo.

Omuringo gw’okuteera akaari ogwa The Raven gukaba guhamire ngu nigwija kushemeza abantu. Enyuguta, okuteerana, omugugu, omuringo gw’enyuguta, bikateekateekwaho n’obwengye bw’omuntu omwe. Hatariho kubangaanisa omuhandiiki w'ebitebyo akakwatwaho okuhikiirira kw'ebyeshongoro bya Miss Barrett ebya trochaics, omu "munaana," kandi na munonga ahabw'entebeekanisa eyaatwairwe haihi n'okuhendera kwa "Lady Geraldine":

"'Amaisho,' akagira, 'hati nigateera omunda yangye! Nimwe amaisho agaanshohoize?
 Amaisho garikumurinkana, nk’amabaare g’omuhendo agateirwe omu kishushani-eibaare rya Parian!
 Ahansi y'obuso obwo obucureire oburikwera, nimuguma nimuyotsya munonga
 O'er omushenyi-eihamba ry'omutima gwangye n'amagara gangye bihindwirwemu?'"

Okutandika kwe okw’obuhanguzi okw’orunyiriri rwa kashatu omu nyuguta zombi eza kabiri n’eza kana, n’okwongyeraho orunyiriri rwa kataano n’enyuguta y’aha muheru, bikakora orunyiriri rwa The Raven . Okuteerana okurikugumizamu nikureebeka nk’okurikwija hatariho kuteeraho, kandi obwire obwingi ebigambo ebiri omu nyuguta nibihabisa; obwo refrain nari omugugu gurikutegyeka omurimo gwona. Aha nawe akaba atungire omugasho omu kwega kwa Miss Barrett okwa ballads na romants omu ndimi ze n’ezindi. "Refrain" n'omutego ogu omuhandiiki w'ebitebyo nari omweshongozi arikukwatiraho okutu okurikuzengyerera,—okwetenga okurikugarukagaruka kw'Obuhangwa ahabw'okutandika. Butoosha nkunda munonga ebigambo birungi by’omukazi w’ebyeshongoro by’Orungyereza,—“Ebinyonyi by’omu kiro, ebinyonyi by’omu kiro,” “Margret, Margret,” “Omutima gwangye nanye,” “Toll mpora,” “Omugyera nigugumizamu,” “Pan, Pan afiire,” n’ebindi. akakigaruraho:

"Imwe ebihangirwe ebiine amagara ebiine okushemererwa! tihaine rurimi
 Oburungi bwabo nibubaasa kurangirira:
 Entuura ya rukundo ekaruga omu mutima gwangye, .
 Kandi nkabashabira omugisha ntakimanyire;
 Buzima omuhikiriire wangye w’embabazi akangirira embabazi,
 Kandi nkabaha omugisha ntarikumanya."

Poe akahanga omurongooti gwa kataano gw’ekigambo kye ahabw’amahano g’okugarukamu. Akakyesiga kuhika aha muheru omu bitontoma bikye ebyakuratsireho,—“Lenore,” “Annabel Lee,” “Ulalume,” na “Aha Annie.” Kikatunga ebyeshongoro by'omukibira kandi eby'obusaasi, nkateekateeka, kuruga omu "sweet influences," z'emigugu n'okugarukamu kwa Afirika ebyabaire byeshongorerwa omu buto, birikuhikaanisa n'omuziki gwabo gw'obuzaarwa eiraka ry'omuhandiiki waitu w'omumashuma.

"Enteekateeka y'okuhandiika," okweshwijuma kwe okwa The Raven , n'okucondooza kw'eby'obuhangwa aha muringo gwayo n'omutindo gwayo. Tihariho kwehakana kwe okw’obuhanguzi obutaine shagama, nari okwehakana kwe okwakuratsireho ahari banywani be ngu okushohoza obuhanguzi obu n’obundi bushuma bwe bwonka obw’obwengye, oburikwetenga kuheebwa ekitiinisa kyona. Kuri yaatebeekaniise omurimo gwona kare, akaba atarikubaasa kukora entandikwa y’ebigambo by’okuteera ebigambo ebigumire munonga kuhika aha muziki ogurikushemeza ogw’omu ntambi ya kabiri, —n’obu yaakuba aha nteekateeka ye ey’eby’okutangaaza. Buzima omunyabwengye, ku arikuheza kukora omurimo, naareeba, nk’Omunyabwengye w’okubanza, ngu ni murungi, kandi areeba ahabw’enki ni murungi. Okweshwijuma okurikukuratiraho, hamwe n’okwehakana omuriro gwona ogurikwera, nikubaasa kukorwa ahonaaho. Enyikiriza yangye eri ngu okuteekateeka kw’okubanza n’okuhandiika okugumire kw’ekitontome eki kukaija nk’oku okuhiga kurikwija obwire bwona; ngu omuhandiiki waayo bwanyima akareeba oku kirikubaasa kuhikiirizibwa, akakiha ebicweka by’aha muheru ebishobooroirwe omu shuura ye erikugamba aha Kuhandiika, kandi ngu eky’aha muheru, n’ahabw’ekyo, ti kishuba kyona nari amazima goona. Obubi oburi omu kweshwijuma nk’okwo n’okugira ngu nikureetera omuntu otakatandikire kwega kuteekateeka ngu emiringo y’okukora ebintu neebaasa kusinga okuteekateeka. Ekigyendererwa ky’obwengye n’okurinda ebihama by’eshaaha yaayo ey’okuhanga. Ebishushani ebitungirwe aha kukora n’amaani, okugyezesa okushoberwa, okurikubanza obusinguzi, nk’oku kiri omu murimo gw’okuteera ebishushani ogwa Hawthorne ogwaherize kureetwa omu mushana, niguha obuhabuzi n’okugarurwamu amaani kw’omuhendo aha munyabwengye omwesigwa. Kwonka kimwe . orikwekundira kworeka Muse we omu maisho g’abantu boona abaizire ashemerire kwijuka ebyahikire aha Mugabe Candaules.

Ensi n'okuhitsya hati neeteekateeka ahari Poe nk'omushaija "otaine mugisha w'obwengye." Juba aha nkahurira naagambwaho nka "omwe ou buri omwe arikushusha ngu akaheebwa orusa rw'okuhakanisa, okujumirira nari okubeihabeiha." Kwonka nikireebeka ahariinye ngu obubi bwe bukahwaho n’okufa kwe okurikutiinisa, kandi ngu kuruga obwo okurwanirira kwe kukaba nikugumizamu, n’eiziina rye ery’okukura okuhamire nk’oku omuhandiiki orikubonabona kandi oine ekiconco yaabaire naabaasa kushaba omu shaaha ye ey’amatsiko. Okujuma n’okushekyerera kwa Griswold bikahindura embeera zaamuhagira. Abacondoozi n’abahandiiki b’ebitebyo bye baizire omu maisho n’okuhakanisa okurikukuratana, n’okusiima emitwarize ye, n’ebihandiiko bisya by’ebihandiiko bye. Amabaruha ge ahabwe n’ebintu ebikye ebyabaireho omu murimo gwe biri omu maisho gaitu; ebihandiiko, ebirungi n’ebibi, nibimanyirwa munonga. Tihariho orikujurira owatungire ensharamu erikukiraho ey’obucureezi kandi erikusaasira. Ebizibu bye omu magara bikaba biri eby’ekicweka kye n’obwire bwe, obundi n’obugobya bwe obw’okwenda. Hatariho kubangaanisa, omwanya gwe gukaba gutari gw’okurinda obuhangwa oburimu obujuma burungi kandi obutarikuhikaana gye akabi akari aheeru n’omunda. Okwenda kwe okw'amaani, okuba mukama we wenka, kukamutunga "obuhunguzi obwo obw'obusaasi." Akeetonganira mukama w’ekinyonyi otashemereirwe, omubonabonesi orikuteerwa ekitontome eki. Kwonka ekye kikaba kiri omugabo gwona ogw’ekiniga ekyo eky’omutaano ekirikushemererwa omu kumanyisa okucwekyerezibwa kwakyo. Hariho okushemererwa omu kuzaana ekicweka ky’omuntu eky’ahaiguru: twena turi abashaija b’amaani, turikurira Ave Imperator! Okujuriza enshonga ya Burke: "Omu busaasi okushemererwa nikuguma kuri ahaiguru, kandi okubonabona oku turikutunga tikurikushushana n'obusaasi obuhikire, obutoosha nibushiisha, kandi obu turikuteeraho kwihaho ahonaaho nk'oku kirikubaasika." Poe akaza hare, kandi akaba ari omunyabwengye nangwa n’omu kabi k’omurimo gwe. Ku kiraabe ngu, kurugiirira aha kwikiriza kwe, obusaasi n’okubura kw’oburungi nizo mitwe y’ebitontome ey’ahaiguru munonga, kandi okuhurira kw’ebitontome n’okushemererwa kw’ensi okurikukirayo obuhango, obwo obusaasi n’omwirima gw’amagara ge agacwekire bikaba bitarikubura okurihirirwa kwabyo emirundi mingi.

Omu mpapura ezirikukurataho, twine eky’okureeberaho kisya ky’obwengye bw’omunyabwengye oburikworeka oku arikushoboorora ekitontome ekimanyirwe munonga. Gustave Doré, omurimo gw’aha muheru ogu ekaramu ye eri omu maisho gaitu, akaba atari mutambi w’ebishushani, nari nangwa n’omuhandiiki w’ebishushani, ahabw’abanyabwengye abarikushaba amazima g’eiraka, ekishushani, n’okuhikiirira. Enshonga nk’ezo zikaba zitarikumukwataho munonga kukira okukora ekishushani n’oburaingwa, omushana n’ekicuucu, kuhwera ekigyendererwa kye, — ekyabaire kiri eky’okushemeza omutima, okuteekateeka, kw’orikureeba. Eki akakikora arikutureetera okutiina, kurabira omu kuteekateeka kw’okutangaaza kandi okurikukira kuba okw’ahaiguru aha bintu ebitarikugambwa kandi ebiijwire ekizibu nari ekitiinisa. Nikigambwa gye ngu emirimo ye ku eraabe etari bishushani bikuru, nk’ebishushani ni bikuru buzima. Nk'omunyabwengye "omuhandiiki w'ebihandiiko," kandi nk'oku yaabaire ari, amaani ge gakaba gari omu nkoragana n'okuhanga kw'omuhandiiki we okw'omutaano, n'obumanzi bw'omwoyo obwabaire nibureetaho obwe. Nikyo obusinguzi bwe na Rabelais, na “Le Juif-Errant,” “Les Contes Drolatiques,” na “Don Quixote,” kandi nikyo, ekirikuhinduka, okuremwa kwe kworeka oburungi bw’ebitabo bya Tennyson ebya Idyls, ebya “Il Paradiso,” eby’obureeberezi bw’Abayudaaya, n’ebindi bihandiiko ebirikwetenga okushohoza nari okushemeza,. Akaba ari omuhangu omu by’okutangaaza, kandi ahabw’okwetegyereza kw’omutaano akaba naabaasa kushoboorora ebishushani by’obwongo oburimu emizimu. Nitureeba ebintu nk’oku abantu be baabaire babireeba, kandi n’amaisho g’Omuyudaaya Orikuzengyerera, Don owaabaire ashoberwa, nari muhara w’omuheisi w’ezaabu ou okukunda kwe kurikuhimbisa munonga Omugabe omu duuka eri aha Pont-au-Change. Omu buhangwa bw’ebintu nibwo yaabaire aine kukwatwaho buri kihandiiko ky’omutaano eky’orunyiriri nari ebigambo ebi nyetsire eby’omutaano. Obugabe bwa hansi bukeetwa omu buheereza bwe; amabaare ge, emiti n’ensozi, eiguru ryonka, biine amagara n’ekigyendererwa na diablerie. Kuri yaabaire ariho kworeka Shakespeare, tukaba tushemerire kureeba oku Caliban, Abafumu, omuyaga gw'amaani gwatwaziibwe omu muringo gw'okutangaaza omu "Lear"; kwonka hatariho kubangaanisa akaba aine kubuuzabuuza ebiteekateeko bye ahari Imogen nari Miranda. Oburungi obushemeire kandi bworobi, n’oburungi bwabwo obuhikire, akaba atarikukira kureebeka nk’orikwetegyereza.

Kwonka hariho oburungi omu biteekateeko bye ebya "Ancient Mariner," ebitari by'amazima nk'oku biri, n'okukuratana okutarikukira kubaho omu mubaro gwa Doré. The Rime ekamuha ekitebyo kiraingwa, n’okuhindura bingi eby’okureeberaho. Omuri The Raven eiraka n’erangi nibigumya eiraka ryabyo rimwe kandi tituine mpinduka y’omwanya nari omukoro. Niki ekirikurugamu? Doré naaha entaaniso nyingi aha mutwe nk'oku yaagiteekateekire, "ekibuuzo ky'okufa n'okuteekateeka kw'omutima ogutarikuhuumurizibwa." Omu bimwe aha bishushani ebi enshobi ze nizireebeka; ebindi nibyoreka obukomooko bwe obw’amaani, n’emitwarize mirungi ei yaabaire ayemereiremu nk’omuhandiiki w’ebishushani. Omu bwijwire hakaba hariho ekirikushushana ahagati y’emiteekateekyere y’okukora eya Poe na Doré. Eki kikaba nikiza kureebeka gye kuri owa bwanyima yaagyezaho omukono gwe aha "Ebitebyo bya Grotesque na Arabesque." Bombi bakaba bakira kuza omu nsi y’abazimu ey’okuteekateeka n’okukundana. Omu bikorwa by’okuteera akaari bombi, kurabira omu kuragiira kwabo okw’ebintu ebitari by’obuhangwa, bakeetantara akabi akari ahagati y’ekirungi n’ekitahikire. Poe akaba ari omuramya w’amazima ow’Omurungi; rukundo ye aharikyo ekaba eri ekihika ekirikuhayo, kandi aha naataanisa n’omuteezi we w’ekishushani. Omuhandiiki w’ebitebyo nari omunyabwengye, Okufa aha muheru nikuhindura byona: omu kicuucu ky’omuhandiiki we w’omunyabwengye, akabonero ka Vedder akarikubataho omu muringo oguhikire amaisho n’amaisho, kwonka nikabazingirana n’ekijwaro ky’obwengye n’amaani agatarikufa. Omukazi w'omumerika akozire ekishushani ky'omunyabwengye oine amaisho g'enyonyoozi aine etaara y'aha muheru, ekishushani kirungi ky'omuntu orikureeba, kwonka eminywa ye etarikubaasa kugamba, ebihama by'ensi eri aheeru ya "omuryango gw'ekituuro ky'omutaano."

Edmund C. Omutegyeki.
