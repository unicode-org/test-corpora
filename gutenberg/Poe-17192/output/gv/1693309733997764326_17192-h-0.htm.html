COMMENT ER YN BARD.

Ta secret bardaght, cha nee less na prosperity jest, ayns cluain y fer ta cluster eh. Yield to e spell, glow rish mooad y bard: shoh, er-y-fa shen, t’eh freggyrt ny saggyrtyn tra t’ou goaill rish e vleeantyn. C'raad erbee ta'n bard hene, ayns e mooad elley, gra rhyt, nagh vel e art agh sleight of hand, bieys y enchanter-bio, as dy vel eh er ny choyrt dy hoilshaghey dhyt y trick jeh, — nagh cred-jee eh. Feaysaghey son e oor prophetagh; as eisht cur oo hene da’n passion echey, da’n ghloyr ny da’n pian. "Ta shin ayns laue Love y laa shoh!" t’eh sheeyney Gautier, ayns paraphrase buoyant Swinburne,—as veih’n voghrey gys y ghrian-shaghey ta shin ry-hoi ’sy marrey violen: cha nel agh un ghraih, un May, un sniaghtey bleaystagh. Ta’n ghraih eternal, ooilley ny reddyn elley neu-real as currit er-chea. Ta jerrey ec y vision, ta’n scene caghlaa; agh ta shin er ghoaill red erbee, cooinaghtyn charm. Choud as ta bardaghyn, choud as ta charmyn. Ta charm Evanescence, shen ta cur da’n ard-chaslys as y ghraih aureole jeh Pathos. Shassoo rish Landor e un "oie jeh cooinaghtyn as jeh sniaghtey" son Rose Aylmer, as ta shoh ayd gys y chooid smoo.

As nish, gow laue minstrel jeh'n theihll noa, er n'gholl er-shaghryn veih ard-valley ry-hoi gys America rude as dissonant shen, myr honnick Baudelaire, "cha row son Poe ny-lomarcan ny phryssoon mooar trooid ren eh reih, shaghey as shaghey, lesh y ghraih feoilt jeh'n vea v'er ny chroo dy 'naght ayns y theihll s'glenney," as raad v'eh er ny chroo dy 'naght ayns y theihll s'glenney.' drunkard, cha row eh agh un ymmyd dy bragh dy scarrey veih yn aght s'anmey shoh." Clash laue sensitive y chantoor trome dreeing myr shen e weird, as share rish y climate ayns v'eh er gheddyn, - riamh trooid y laa, dy bragh 'sy noid, - mannagh nee'n Atlantis veih v'eh er n'gholl, er-y-fa shen, ard-chreeagh veih ny cliaghtey v'eh er n'immeeaght 'sy laa.

Gys un thalloo ny lomarcan ta pooar echey dy dty ghiarey, as son un oie ny lomarcan oddys oo y choardail rish e hrooid. Tract jeh'n Talloo ny jeh'n Niau: "Trooid-ny-dooinney," ass Space, ass Time. Ta’n red shoh ny perturbed, trooid nyn sooillyn, myr ny sooillyn jeh’n Cenci, ta’n annym geddyn fuinneig agh ta’n spyrryd s’daze; ec y traa t’ayn, ta spyrrydyn, glenney son y raad ta goaill gys Eternity, staydys as slaynt. Ta'n limbo jeh "anmeenyn planetary," ayn ta ooilley ny n'annymyn soilshey geay, ooilley ny ghraih as ny illusionyn caillt. Ta cuid jeu ry-gheddyn ayns trance, y red s’odjey; feallagh elley.

Ta’n cheer shen myr shen, as son un oie t’ayn, — oie dy phaseyn astral as chimes ry-cheilley. Ta e monodies daa-eed as jeih bard, as ta'n cheer oc streeu dy chaghlaa agh t'eh ymmyrchagh. Un lurg y jeh t'ad cliaghtey, myr chimmey y chloan brasnagh as ebony, ayns "The Masque of the Red Death," t'er n'yannoo y waltzers staydys lesh "disconcert as treishteil as meditation," choud as v'an oor er-gerrey.

As jeh ooilley ny cadensyn mystickagh shoh, ta'n plaint The Raven , ta vibrate trooid y phortal, er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh'n theihll shey. Cre'n red ta goll gys jannoo bardaght, — as cre'n aght ta'n raad shen ry-hoi, myr ayns y chooid smoo elley, y raad shen ren enmyssey e bardyn "the makers"? Ta obbyr er ny enmyssey ass y void. As raad nagh row nhee erbee, t’eh er-mayrn,—cruinney noa, part jeh’n treealtys jeh’n dooinney. As ta beggan lirickyn ec y traa t’ayn, ny smoo na feallagh elley ta ry-hoi, cur orrin smooinaghtyn er-dy-henney cho brasnagh ta pen y bard cur reddyn nagh vel fys orroo .

"da cummey, as cha vel eh cur da aer nhee erbee
 Ta'n thie 'sy valley, as ennym."

Ta’n chooid smoo jeh’n daa prototype, agh t’ad ooilley fascinating shin lesh eilynnyn ry-gheddyn ass sjeayl y Supernatural. Agh nish ta’n smaoinee s’ardjey er ny choyrt gys annym ny reddyn; t'eh might ny might eh inspire y Fantasy ta pobble lesh ymmydyn y vague interlunar. Agh, ta un jeh ny lirickyn shoh, ayns e aght ny s'beggey, cur ymmyd jeh'n aght s'beggan, cho shickyr as ta'n obbyr sublimer jeh cast ard-natural, - "Faustus" Marlowe, "Faust" Goethe, "Manfred," ny eddyr y obbyr s'ethereal shen, "The Mimpedsuam as N'Tempedsure." Ta ny smoo na un, agh er y chooish elley, ta cuid burden ny refrain ta shleaystey yn cooinaghtyn,—un uair cliaghtey, chamoo er ny vee-chraueeaght, myr tonn organ-stop nagh vel ry-chlashtyn agh ry-chlashtyn. Ta "Lenore" ec Bürger, y ballad ghostly as resonant shen, y lure as y foil jeh'n aittaghyn, ry-akin jeu. Ta'n "Rime of the Ancient Mariner" ymmyrchagh ec Coleridge er nyn skyn. "Le Juif-Errant" v'eh er n'yannoo clashtyn, my v'eh Beranger bard ny s'mooar; as, agh son nyn 'faarkey' veih sympathy pobble, might "Y Lady of Shalott" as "Y Damozel Beane" ve currit stiagh 'sy liosta. Va'n obbyr er ny choyrt gys Edgar Allan Poe dy yannoo daa lirick, "The Bells" as The Raven , as ta'n daa lirick, agh s'cosoylagh dy vel ad ny sloo 'sy ghraih, 'sy ghraih 'sy ghraih. "Ulalume," agh s'cosoylagh as s'cosoylagh, cha nel y caghlaa universal echey ta 'sy test ain.

Ta'n Raven ayns sheeanyn bardaght tuittym fo skeealyn myr "Yn Palace Haunted," "Y Chashtal 'sy Mharrey," "Yn Cooidjagh," as "Israfel." By lhisagh eh ooilley ve er ny mharkey, ta mee credjal, liorish leuee cast fastidious, son rannagyn myr shoh:

Agh The Raven , myr "Ny Cloan" as "Annabel Lee," t'eh cur booise da'n chooid smoo as ny beggan. Dy row mee er ghra ayns ard elley dy row ny h-obbraghyn s'raare ec Poe myr "ny h-obbraghyn ayn ta'n aght, er-y-fa shen, jeh spontaneity, - ayn ta'n aght t'eh geddyn rish e ghraih, tra ta'n baase tuittym as cadences ta'n chooid smoo jeh'n cheer, y chooid smoo jeh'n melancholy, cheet veih'n chione echey." Ta shoh my chredjue foast; as foast, er y scansh jeh’n bardaght shoh, t’eh m’y ghra ny s’mooar na ec traa erbee rish my vleeantyn. Ta co-earrooder dwoaiagh loayrt ayns favour gagh obbyr ellan ghraih. Insh da’n dooinney, nagh vel fys echey er ellan ny s’cosoylagh rish, dy scanshaghey red erbee jeh’n bardaght ny jeh’n pictoor, as cre’n aght ta’n phooar echey glenney eh! Ayns y fa, nee eh overrate y value relative jeh’n chied obbyr vie liorish t’eh er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh’n aght cheddin. The Raven , myrgeddin, ta aghtyn coontit ec y traa t'ayn, as t'eh ry-hoi ec y traa t'ayn. Nish ayns bardaghyn y screeudeyr echey cha nel y motive er ny mharkey ny s'palpably. "The Haunted Palace" t'eh ry-hoi ry-hoi rish y leighder taghyrtagh, agh cha row Poe er n'yannoo rish y ghraih shen son analys baljagh. Ta'n Raven ry-hoi ry-hoi rish y theme typical jeh'n screeudeyr—y chaillteen nagh vod ymmyd jeh ben idolit as s'beggan; agh er reddyn elley, myrgeddin, ta'n instinct pobble cooie ayns smooinaghtyn er e bardaght reiht.

Ta dooinney dy ghraih ry-gheddyn ry-cheilley lesh soilshey jeh un obbyr, as cha nee shoh dagh ooilley traa y s’mooar echey. Ta'n aignaghey ny s'instant son dy vel eh er ny chur stiagh. Cha vel eh goaill eh, myr fortune miser, coinney er coinney, agh "cha nel eh erbee ny ooilley ayns ooilley." As myr shen lesh ambitions elley: y courtier, y saighdeyr, y actor, — cre-erbee t'ad ry-hoi, — ta dagh unnane jeu coontit e triomf veih stroke erbee fortunate. Raven Poe, dys augury, v'eh da "y eayst ren y ghrian y sniaghtey." Va'n bard er n'eiyrt gys New-York, 'sy gheimmey 1844-'45, as v'eh er gheddyn obbyr er paper Willis, "The Evening Mirror," as v'eh er n'eiyrt magh e 'argid' liorish co-obbraghyn ayns ardjyn elley. Erreish da shey bleeaney v'eh screeudeyr glen, as v'eh er n'yannoo cliaghtey proffeshonail; v'eh er ny reayll ayns scansh as ayns mraane, as cha row eh er-mayrn son e rannaghyn jeh'n obbyr-gazetteyrys gyn-paid y laa shen. Va'n obbyr echey er ny yannoo myrgeddin, myr sampleyr, s'cosoylagh rish "Ligeia" as "Y Tuittym jeh'n Thie Usher," (y s'cosoylagh rish y s'cosoylagh shen, ayns verse, jeh "A Lost Mind" Elihu Vedder) s'cosoylagh rish s'cosoylagh rish "The Gold Bug" as "The Mystery of Marie Roget." Va'n proselyte er ny yannoo er-chea, as v'eh er ghoaill stiagh stiagh 'sy ghraih Frangagh. Va eshyn er n’ynsaghey e phooar as e vee-chairys hene, as v’eh ec e h-eash, as cha nee fegooish cliaghtey erbee. Agh cha row eh er scrieu nhee erbee va er teihll dy chooilley ghooinney. As rish obbyr rare as delicate, shegin da cuid jeh'n touch pobble ve currit rish dy ghlackey y lught-thie coontinagh jeh'n traa echey hene.

Trooid y ghleashtan jeh biographeyryn Poe er-dy-henney, t'eh er jeet dy ve scansh t'er ny ghra ec The Raven . Va ben y bard, Virginia, ry-hoi ry-hoi, agh v'ee ry-hoi son bleïn elley ayns sjeayl y baase. Ta'n seihll ard, low 'sy thie kiongoyrt rish Raad Bloomingdale myr cliaghtey myr yn seihll raad v'eh er n'yannoo y Marseillaise. Ta ooilley er chlashtyn dy vel y bardaght, s'cosoylagh rish "Quarles", er ny choyrt magh ayns y "American Review," lesh ymmyd jeh'n aght t'eh er n'yannoo; dy row Poe er gheddyn deig dollaryn son; dy row Willis, y fer s’kinjagh as s’leshtal jeh arbiteryn fashionagh, er n’yannoo eh reesht lesh eulogy ren eh y yannoo town-talk dy kiart. Va ooilley ny ammyssyn jeh'n screeudeyr echey er nyn stroie tra ren Poe eh hene y ghra ayns coonrey litreehaghtyragh, as son traa v'eh y screeudeyr smoo markee jeh screeudeyryn Americk. Yn hit stiurey as stiurey eh. Cooidjagh rish satirist elley as ny s'boalltagh, dynsee eh myrgeddin "ruddyr jeh'n samhradh" eddyr y "hum jeh'n insect." Ta'n red s'trimmid, agh t'eh er n'gholl shaghey trimshey bleeaney, - traa dy ghraih, dy ghraih, dy ghraih, agh dy bragh dy obbyr scanshoil ny criticagh, - roish y chlashtyn derrey, roish my daink y curtain er bea v'er e hon ayns firrinys tragedy, as hug eh eh hene da "the Conqueror Worm."

"Y Review American: A Whig Journal" v'eh ny magazine creditagh son y traa, lesh daa cholught, cloit er paper mie lesh screeuyn soilshey, as ry-hoilley lesh portraittyn mezzotint. Ayns mraane reddyn fo'n staid t'ayn nish, va ayn cuid jeh'n red v'eh booiagh dy gheddyn. Ta'n chied lioar, son 1845, ry-akin ayns y chied lioar, as t'eh ry-akin ayns y chied lioar. Va'n bardaght quaint Ralph Hoyt, "Old," er ny choyrt magh 'sy lioar shoh. As shoh trimmid lirickyn ec Poe: "Yn Chashtal ayns y Mharrey," "Yn Ghleann jeh'n Unnaneys," as Yn Raven . Va daa jeu shoh er nyn skeaylley, — myr shen va e aght, — veih staydyssyn roish, agh haink y chied-ennym magh myr veagh eh ry-hoi, as cha row eh ry-hoi myr ta shin nish. Ta'n stashoon dy row eh er ny ath-reih erreish da, er-drogh-spyrryd. Ta un-jeig ymmyd jeh'n text-magazine ry-akin ayns The Raven and Other Poems , 1845, lioar va'n bard er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh'n aght shoh dy chur eh da'n phobble. Ta'n chooid smoo jeu shoh caghlaaghyn jeh'n ghrian, ny jeh focklyn shingil, y sorch s'jerree jeant dy h-arrey effeit y alliteration. Ayns y lioar s'brinnagh ec Mr. Lang jeh verse Poe, v'er ny choyrt magh ayns y "Parchment Library," t'eh er hoilshaghey yn instinct jeh scholar, as t'eh er n'yannoo glen, ayns goll stiagh 'sy text 'sy lioar t'er ny ghra nish, myr t'eh er ny choyrt 'sy lioar Londonagh 'sy vlein 1846. Ta'n red shoh er ny h-addey reesht liorish screeudeyryn ny yei, as t'ad er n'yannoo mraane jeh'n aght oc hene myrgeddin. Agh va'n teisht jeh The Raven , er y fa dy row ny h-iartyssyn er ny yannoo rish y screeudeyr son copy'n lioar-screeuyn, er ny ath-reih ec y screeudeyr foast ny s'faggys da; ayns firrinys, ren eh eh y phrintaghey ayns Richmond, ry-cheilley rish e baase, lesh y ghraih bardaght jeh "seraphim ta e chosh-fakin" son "angelyn ta e chosh-faint faint," ayns y cheyoo stanza. Ta'n text ain nish, myr shen, agh t'eh ry-akin ayns y 1845, er ny ghra ry-cheilley liorish leigh y bard er-dy-henney, as t'eh, ta mee credjal, y version s'cowrey as s'effective jeh'n un bard shoh. Va'n aghtar bun t'ayns y ghraih s'anmey er ny hoilshaghey, agh, 'sy lioar 'sy vlein 1845; yn un-jeig stanza v'ayns y chied cheayrt ny lineenyn shoh, faulty ayns rimmey as myr shen blemish er y varrey:

"Glait veih masteyr neu-lowal, ta Disaster unmercious
 Lesh s’fast as lesh s’fast—myr shen, tra v’eh Hope adjure,
 Stern Despair dy row eh er-gerrey, ayns ynnyd y Hope sweet v'eh er n'yannoo adjure—
 Yn freggyrt trimshagh shen, 'Chamoo!'"

By vie lhiam dy beagh bardoonee elley, as cliaghtey, cho shickyr dy yannoo nyn aashaghyn dagh ooilley traa feaysley. Va Poe dy kinjagh ry-reayll e scansh scanshoil jeh verse, as ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi. Yn Raven v'eh y chiad jeh'n red beggan bardaghyn v'eh er chur gys jerrey roish my v'eh er ny phrintaghey. Ta’n red s’cosoylagh dy vel adsyn ta cur kiarail jeh bardaght er choayl beggan rish e baase. Fluent ayns prose, cha row eh rieau er scrieu verse son y chooid smoo dy yannoo bardaght. Nush va refrain jeh'n image er n'eiyrt rish, va'n lyric v'er ny h-eiyrt magh yn inspiration, myr dooyrt eh hene, jeh passion, cha nee jeh purpose. Va shoh ec eaddagh cho ard as dy row eh er-y-fa shen dy row eh ry-hoi y theory Fairfield dy row gagh un red ry-hoi y nervous crisis.

Cre, eisht, hug da'n bard y cliaghtey echey son The Raven ? Veih cre’n bun-sthie misty v’eh er n’irree s’leaie gys ceayrt s’leaie? As myr t’eh ry-akin, hie ny smoo na un red gys toiggal bardaght cho notable. As mee smooinaghtyn er ny preacheilyn ny s’hiare ta’n lught-thie preacheil dy mennick er textyn gearr as ny s’leoi, ta mee credjal nagh bee’m er ny choyrt gys peccah son y ghraih shoh jeh un lyric, —goaill stiagh un lyric ta’n artist myr Doré er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh’n lyric prodigal. Ta mee er n’yannoo rish y traa t’er n’gholl shaghey dy row y skeeal shoh, as beggan jeh’n screeudeyr echey er n’yannoo rish erreish da ve er ny h-earroo, er-yn-oyr nagh row ad er n’gholl seose veih germidyn ayns e verse boyish. Agh ta'n Chiarn Fearing Gill er hoilshaghey dou stanzas Poe nagh vel er nyn follaghey, s'cosoylagh rish e hoghtoo vleïn jeig, as t'ad er nyn goyrt, "Yn Demon y Teih." Ta'n lioar-screeuyn myr dy vel eh ayns scriptyr t'er-dy-hoshiaght y bard, as ta'n ghrinney echey er ny choyrt stiagh liorish y theay ta'n lioar-laa er ve ayn son leshtal y cheead bleïn. Ta'n ghrian ry-akin ayns y ghrian, as t'eh ry-akin ayns y ghrian, as t'eh ry-akin ayns y ghrian . As rish rythm y varrey ain, ta co-choardail rish daateyn cur fys dy row shoh er ny ymmyd liorish rythm "Lady Geraldine's Courtship." Va Poe un jeh ny h-erree dy choyrt onnor da ghraih Miss Barrett; s'cosoylagh eh rish e bardaghyn coardail rish myr "y noblest jeh'n ghine echey," as v'eh ayns sympathy rish e moddey lyrical. Ny lineenyn veih e bard-ghraih,

"Lesh stir murmuragh neu-chiaralagh, 'syn aer, y curtain purple
 Swellt stiagh as swellt magh mygeayrt e shliawnyn pale gyn mow."

lhoyrt echo ayns ny reddyn shoh:

"As y rustling trimshagh as neu-chiaralagh jeh gagh curtain purple
 Thrilled mee—liooar eh mee lesh aggleyn ard-ghooghyssagh nagh row mee er n’yannoo riamh roimhe."

Ta Poe er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh priviledge son y row eh er n'yannoo censure rish Longfellow, as nagh row dooinney erbee er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh er e h-earroo hene fegooish ymmyd jeh s'cosoylagh as s'cosoylagh. Ayns form melody as stanzaic, ver mayd tastey da nagh vel ny daa varrant ry-cheilley, agh ayns motive t'ad dy chooilley red. Cha row y bard ghraihagh er n’yannoo red erbee agh y ghraih ghraih y bard. "Yn scriptyr beayn shoh!" 'Sy stayd shen, t'eh gra, - "y phooar shoh ta ry-akin!... Ta'n bard mooar ain, Mr. Browning, screeudeyr 'Paracelsus,' &c., ry-akin ayns e ghraih er y rythm." Ta'n Chiarn Ingram, erreish da jannoo ymmyd jeh "Lady Geraldine," soilshaghey magh s'cleayney s'cosoylagh rish s'cosoylagh dy row Poe er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh. Ayns 1843, va Albert Pike, yn leshtal-Greagagh, leshtal-frontiersman, bard Arkansas, er screeu ayns "The New Mirror," va Poe ry-screeu, cuid jeh'n verseyn va'n ennym "Isadore" er, agh va'n screeudeyr er n'yannoo rish y traa shen as v'ad er nyn goyrt "The Widowed Heart." Ta mee roshtyn veih revision Mr. Pike y stanza shoh, as ta ny prìomh-feaysley jeu correspondaghey rish y version t'er-dy-hoshiaght:

"Shegin dou sheeyney er nyn seihll, cha nel mee cloie nyn sheeanyn ny smoo,
 Ta'n greysh garish sheeyney flaunyssagh er y lhooar nagh vel sniessey;
 Ta'n eayst-mollaghtagh foast ny hoie as sheeyney, O strain melancholy!
 Son ta my chree myr cloan-folley, ta ry-hoi rish y fliaghey;
 T'ou er choayl dou son dy bragh, Isadore!"

Ta shin ry-akin ec y traa t’ayn, co-chosney refrain, as introduction jeh eayst ta’n chanan echey ymmyrkey y ghraih. Ta slieauyn elley t’ad y curious y leih; 'sy ghraih, rannagh ta'n Chiarn Ingram roshtyn veih'n rieughid derrey Poe er "Barnaby Rudge":

"Yn raven, myrgeddin, * * * v'eh er ny yannoo, ny smoo na ta shin fakin eh nish, myr rannagh jeh'n ghrinney jeh'n Barnaby fantastic. * * * Oddagh e character er n'yannoo, mychione rannagh y idiot, y rannagh cheddin myr ta, ayns ceayrt, y cho-chowrey mychione an aer."

Agh, erreish da ny germyn as ny co-chosney shoh y hoilshaghey, ta’n vlein shoh ry-gheddyn foast ayns e ghraih. Ta'n cheer progressive, y mhuinney scenic as y soilshey as y sfeer contrast, — erskyn ooilley, y ghraih spioradagh jeh'n oie, jannoo jeh obbyr genius informing. As cour y eayst glen, cha nel eh ry-akin rish ooilley ny cronkaghyn elley jeh e chlann, veih ny "twa corbies" as "three cronk" ny balladistyn gys "Grip" rumpled Barnaby. Nish cha nel ymmyd erbee jeh’n rook cawing ta shleaystey turretyn shenn-ayraghyn as treishteil er y field heraldry; cha row phantom boding jeh va Tickell er ghra dy row, tra,

"Screeu er e uinnag three keayrtyn,
 Va'n corbey flap'd e skian,
 Roish y ven-loar v'ayns graih
 Yn soilshey solemn."

Ta raven Poe ny s'diuish; yn incarnation jeh’n aggle as y vee-chraueeaght jeh’n dooinney; sable corporal Memory, y chronicler bishagh jeh doom, sorch jeh'n Irreparable. Er-e-skyn rish y Styx, veih "clieau Plutonian y Noid," t'eh myr yn annym imaged jeh'n cheshaght-chaggee hene, — jeh'n dooinney shen nagh vod, nagh vod, quaff y ghraih nepenthe, son ta cooinaghtyn jeh Lenore ooilley ny ta er-mayrn da, as lesh y surcease jeh e ghraih eddyr y red shen vees er ny choyrt stiagh.

Faagym y Raven myrgeddin myr bardaght reiht jeh'n screeudeyr echey, son e sampleyr jeh ooilley e smooinaghtyn mychione cre'n aght dy beagh bardaght. Ta'n red shoh ry-gheddyn ayns e essays mychione "Yn Principal Bardaght," "Yn Rationale jeh'n Verse," as "Yn Philosophy jeh'n Chompoosit." Poe er ghra dy nee "ayns Ceayrt, mraane, ta'n annym ny s'crooie er y chronk mooar ta, tra t'eh er ny h-insh liorish y Sentiment Poetic, t'eh streeu—y chroo jeh'n Beauty supernal.... Cha nel Verse ry-hoi ry-hoi ny share na myr ceayrt inferior ny less capable"; agh reesht, verse ta dy jarroo "Bardaght ny Goan" eh "Yn Chroo Rhythmical jeh'n Beauty,"—she shoh as ny smoo. Tone y Beauty s’ardjey t’eh un Sadness. Yn rud s'meancholy jeh'n towse t'ayn, she Baase. Feme dy bee shoh co-cheangailt rish Beauty. "Ta'n baase, eisht, jeh ben s'brinnagh, fegooish ymmyd, y theme s'bardaght er y theihll, - as myr shen cha nel eh ry-hoi, dy vel ny lippyn s'cosoylagh son theme myr shen, ny lippyn jeh'n ghraihagh t'er ny stroie." Ta ny s'jerree jeh ny s'jerree shoh er nyn ghleiy jeh'n analys whimsical ec Poe jeh'n bard shoh hene, agh t'ad soilshaghey dy shickyr y rang-oardagh coontit jeh'n verse echey. Ta'n climax jeh "The Bells" y monotone muffled jeh ghouls, ta gloyraghey y veeal jeh'n chree dooinney. "Lenore," Yn Corbe , "Yn Cooidjagh," "Gys Unnane ayns Paradys," as "Ulalume" t'ad jannoo symfony tenebrose, — as "Annabel Lee," s'cosoylagh rish ooilley ny reddyn, ta'n chied red t'ayn, soilshaghey dy row un theme er n'yannoo rish gys y jerrey. Agh reesht, cha nel ad shoh ooilley ny red erbee mannagh vel ad musical, as ta cuid jeu er nyn stroie liorish y chaghlaa shen jeh’n Fantastic ta ymmyrkey yn aght dy ghra, as ta cur aggle noa rish y ghraih hene. Trooid ooilley ta’n ghraih ideal jeh’n bard er ny skeealaghey dy s’diuney, fo’n pall s’diuney, — myr shen t’eh er ny skeealaghey dy mennick lesh leshtal ayns ard elley, — ben Usher, y Lady Ligeia, ayns truth y co-earrooder jeh e nature hene. Ta mee credjal nagh vod graih artist son un "in the form" rieau ry-cheilley rish e devotion da red erbee ideal. Ta’n ven ta kiongoyrt rish, fegooish ymmyd y yannoo jeh e ghraih, ve ooilley er y ghraih as ny s’faggys da, charm eh lesh ymmyd gyn jerrey, ny ve content dy scarrey rish ayrn jeh e ghraih. Feddyn eh ve lured ec y Unattainable, as t’eh shoh ry-hoi ry-hoi ayns e ghraih son ellan cruinnaghey.

Poe, myr Hawthorne, haink eh stiagh lesh y skool Romantagh, as cha row dooinney erbee ny s'mish na eh dy ghra rish y ghraih echey. Agh nish va e chree rish ny romanseyryn as ny effeityn Oriental ny Gothic oc. Va e h-invention, cho baiht ayns ny scanshyn prose, myr dy row eh er n’eiyrt rish tra s’cosoylagh eh rish verse; as dooyrt e vriwnys rish dy row bardaghyn romansagh fadagh dependit ny smoo er incident na er inspiration, —as dy row, dy ghra bardaght romansagh, lirickyn doaltattym. As shen-y-fa e teory, ta soilshaghey magh rish e ymmyd hene, dy vel "bardaght ard ny cho-chosney flat ayns termyn." Ta ny h-earrooyn jeh The Raven beggan as simply: dooinney, eayst, as y cuirraghyn phantasmal ec ben. Agh ta'n piece cur stiagh taishbyney fine jeh studeyrys romansagh. Cre t'ain? Yn mean-oie; y seihll s’smooar lesh e tomeyn dy lore er-diu; y scholar,—Hieronymus y traa t'ayn; y tappee 'sy chassey; yn oie gheayrt as yn aile dy marroo; ny cronkaghyn silkyn sniessey rish y gheay; ny aigney as ny mraane vague jeh’n dorraghys echo; yn eayst doo, uncanny er y bust pallid; ny h-ashoonyn velvet violet as y lamp gloyroil. Ta'n effeit stage shoh ooilley jeh'n stayd, soilshey, colour, sound, glen romansagh, as fiu melodramatic, agh jeh'n aght bardaght nagh vel melodrama ry-akin, as t'eh ry-akin dy kiaralagh "passion eternal, pain eternal" y bard.

Va structuur rythmagh The Raven sure dy yannoo ymmyd jeh. Va'n rimmey, y alliteration, y bharrant, y form stanzaic, er nyn skeaylley lesh ard-chraueeaght. Cha nel ymmyd erbee jeh'n bard dy row eh s'cosoylagh rish y ghraih t'ayn son trochaickyn ceaut Miss Barrett, ayns "eights," as goaill stiagh y rang-oardagh v'er ny ghoaill stiagh rish jerrey "Lady Geraldine":

"'Suoillyn,' dooyrt eh, 'ta ad nish throbbing trooid mee! Vel shiuish sooillyn ren mee y ghlenney?
 Sushtal gloyroil, myr jewels antique set ayns cloie-stashoon Parian!
 Fo'n front geal calm shen, vel shiu dy bragh lostey torrid
 O'er y ghrian-faarkey s'diuney jeh my chree as my vea er ny stroie?'"

Ta'n rol-artyssagh echey jeh treeoo rimmey ayns y daa as y cheyoo lieh, as y chiaghtoo lieh as refrain derrey, er n'yannoo stanza jeh The Raven . Ta'n alliteration persistent myr dy vel eh cheet fegooish ymmyd, as dy mennick ta'n rimmey ta'n lineenyn seductive; tra ta'n refrain ny'n burden ry-hoi 'sy obbyr ooilley. Ta shen myrgeddin er ghoaill profit jeh scansh Miss Barrett er balladyn as romantyn ayns e teihll hene as ayns teihll elley. "Refrain" t'eh y lure lesh ta bard ny soilsheyder y chleaysh ry-hoi,—y ghraih ry-hoi jeh Nature son y strain toshiaght. Ta mee er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh'n red s'brinnagh jeh'n ven-chynney Saggyrt, - "Ny Nightingales, ny Nightingales," "Margret, Margret," "My Chree as Mish," "Toll slowly," "T'an Aarkey s'brinnagh," "Pan, Pan marroo," etc. ath-bheaghey eh:

"O nheeghyn beo maynrey! cha nel teihll erbee .
 Faagys yn ard-chraueeaght oc:
 T'eh er n'eiyrt magh ass my chree,
 As vannee mee ad fegooish toiggal:
 Sure, ghow my naboo mie trust orrym,
 As bentyn mee ad fegooish toiggal."

Cree Poe y chiaghtoo line jeh'n stanza echey son y ghraih jeh'n repetent. Va'n 'Lenore', 'Annabel Lee', 'Ulalume', as "For Annie." T'eh er gheddyn ceayrt fieau as melancholy, ta mee er smaoiney, jeh'n "sweet influences," jeh'n burdens as repets Affrican v'er ny channoo da tra v'eh jeh'n lhiannooid, as v'ad ry-chlashtyn rish nyn melody t'ayn rish y ghlare jeh'n bard ain jeh'n Cheayn Sheear.

"Yn Filosofyn jeh'n Cho-chowrey," e analys jeh'n Raven , she skeeal tecnicagh jeh'n aght as y structuur echey. Nish cha nel feme er e ghraih er artifice cold-blooded, ny e ghraih er-dy-henney da e chaarjyn dy row y ghraih shoh ny s'odjey jeh e hoaxes intellectual, dy ve credited dy kiaralagh. Mannagh row eh er n’yannoo y obbyr immeeaght ro-laue, cha row eh er n’yannoo prelude cho harsh jeh rimmey rattle-pan gys y melody blastagh jeh’n daa stanza, — chamoo er e theory jeh’n fantastic. Ta’n artist, tra t’eh er n’yannoo obbyr perfect, cur-my-ner, myr y chiad Artist, dy vel eh mie, as cur-my-ner cre’n-fa t’eh mie. Faggys da mishaghey er-dy-hoshiaght, cooidjagh lesh disavowal jeh'n aile casherick, ve jeant dy h-oikoil. Ta my chredjue dy daink y chiad skeeal as y chied skeeal rowl jeh’n bardaght shoh myr ta’n inspiration cheet dagh ooilley traa; dy honnick y screeudeyr echey eisht myr v’eh er ny choyrt gys kione, as dy row eh er choyrt da ny s’jerree t’er ny ghra ayns e chabdil er Composition, as dy vel y s’jerree, shen-y-fa, cha nee dy chooilley red baghey ny dy chooilley ghraih. Ta'n doghan jeh'n analys shoh dy vel eh ymmyrkey novice dy smooinaghtyn dy vod prosessyn artificial ymmyrkey yn scansh. Yn impulse jeh genius eh dy choadey secretyn e oor chruinney. Ta glimpsyn ta ry-gheddyn jeh’n obbyr, jeh’n obbyr baffled, ta ry-gheddyn roish triomf, myr t’eh ayns y sketch-work jeh Hawthorne er ny chur magh rish y traa t’er n’gholl shaghey, cur stiagh ynsagh as stiurey gyn price da’n artyst sincere. Agh un ta’n Muse echey y hroggal seose gys sooillyn ooilley ny ta cheet, dy beagh eh cooinaghtyn jeh’n stayd jeh’n ree Candaules.

Ta'n theihll foast smooinaghtyn er Poe myr "dooinney gyn shee." Chleaysh mee eh er ny ghra rish y traa t'er n'gholl shaghey myr "un t'eh ry-akin dy vel ooilley ny h-oik er ny chartey dy mraaney, dy mraaney ny dy mraaney." Agh ta’n red shen myr shen dy row e vee-chraueeaght er jeet gys jerrey lesh e baase threealtagh, as dy vel e ghraih er ny choyrt gys jerrey rish y traa shen, as e cliaghtey dy row eh er n’gholl seose cho staydoil as v’eh ry-hoi y screeudeyr dy hrooid as dy hrooid y ghra ayns e oor dy hrooid. Va y ghraih as y ghraih ec Griswold er n'yannoo y stream ayns e laue. Ta critickeyryn as biographeryn er jeet magh lesh ymmyd jeh scansh, lesh ymmyd jeh e character, lesh ymmyd jeh e obbraghyn. Ta e litreeyn echey hene as ny taghyrtyn minuteagh jeh e chorree roish; y record, mie as olk, t'eh ry-akin. Nish cha vel appellor erbee er gheddyn breearrey ny s’tendreil as ny s’pardoonagh. Va e mishaps ayns bea jeh'n rheynn as jeh'n traa echey, as mraane jeh'n vee-chraueeaght echey hene. Cha row e thie-thie erbee dy choadey nature fine-grained, ill-balanced veih perillyn stiagh as stiagh. E ghraih s'creeney, dy ve ree er e hene, ghow eh "y oighreaght shen dy vee-chraueeaght." Va eshyn er n’addey eh hene myr ard-oik y eayst, y strooan s’goanney jeh’n bardaght shoh. Agh v’eh echey laghyn laghyn jeh’n temper dramagh shen ta boggyssagh ayns y ghraih jeh’n dooghys echey hene. Ta boggey ec y rol ard: ta shin ooilley gladiatoryn, cliaghtey Ave Imperator! As t'eh ry-akin rish Burke: "Ayns y ghraih ta'n sheean ry-akin foast, as cha vel y ghraih ta shin goaill rish cha nel eh ry-akin rish pian absolute, ta dagh ooilley traa odious, as ta shin shirrey dy shlieeney ersooyl cho t'eh ry-akin." Poe hie eh er-gerrey, as v'eh ny artiste son y tragedy jeh e chorree. Mannagh vel, cordail rish e chredjue hene, y ghraih as y vee-chraueeaght ny themeyn bardaght s’ardjey, as y ghraih bardaght y ghraih thallooinagh s’keenney, eisht cha row y ghraih as y dorraghys jeh e bhea brishey fegooish nyn ghraihagh dy mennick.

Ayns ny duillagyn ta ry-hoi, ta shin er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh'n aght t'eh ry-hoi jeh'n aght t'eh ry-hoi jeh'n aght t'eh ry-hoi jeh'n aght t'eh ry-hoi jeh'n aght t'eh ry-hoi. Gustave Doré, y obbyr s'jerree jeh'n pencil echey t'er-dy-hoshiaght, cha nee'n peintyr, ny eddyr y skeeal-tarroo, son realistee v'er n'eiyrt rish firrinys tonn, figure, as folliaght. Va ny reddyn shoh ny s’leaie na dy yannoo form as distance, soilshey as shadow, dy chur cooney da’n obbyr echey, — va’n obbyr shen dy hroggal seose yn annym, y smaoinee, y dooinney va jeeaghyn er. Ren eh shoh liorish ymmyd y yannoo jeh’n ghrayse ainyn, trooid smooinaghtyn ard-vooaralagh as dy mennick sublime jeh niart erbee nagh vod ad y ghra as lane dy ghloyr no’n ghloyr. Ta’n aght dy ghra dy mie, my nagh row e obbraghyn tallooyn mooarey, myr pictuyryn, t’ad mooar dy jarroo. As "artist litterature," as myr shen v'eh, va e phooar ayns co-chaslys direct rish y ghraih dramagh jeh'n screeudeyr echey, rish y ghraih braew jeh'n spyrryd v'er n'yannoo rish e ghraih hene. As shen y raad v'eh er n'yannoo rish Rabelais, rish "Le Juif-Errant", "Les Contes Drolatiques," as "Don Quixote," as shen-y-fa, er-y-fa shen, rish y ghraih echey rish y ghraih echey rish Idylyn Tennyson, rish "Il Paradiso," rish ny pastoralyn Hebrew, as rish textyn elley ta feme er ghraih. Va'n grotesque er ny ruggey, as liorish scansh s'speciale v'eh ry-akin specteryn 'sy 'brain' 'haunted'. Ta shin cur-my-ner reddyn myr v’eh er n’yannoo rish e phersoonyn, as lesh ny sooillyn hene jeh’n Iudeeagh Wandering, y Don s’goanney, ny niar y smid-oor ta’n fancy echey myr shen y Ree ayns y shop er y Pont-au-Change. Va’n nature jeh ny reddyn dy beagh eh er ny h-arrey da gagh obbyr-chaggee verse ny prose ta mee er n’enmyssey unique. Va ny reeriaghtyn s’inshley er nyn goyrt gys e hirveish; e chreagyn, e chreeaghyn as e beinn, y skyn hene, t'ad beo lesh motive as diablerie. My v'eh beo dy ghra Shakespeare, v'eh ry-akin rish Caliban, ny Witches, y stoarm ayns "Lear"; agh cha nel teyrey erbee dy beagh eh er cheau question er e idealyn jeh Imogen ny Miranda. Beauty glen as simply, as y ghraih perfect jeh, cha row eh ry-akin dy row eh ry-hoi.

Agh t'eh ry-akin ayns e skeealaghyn son y "Ancient Mariner," nagh vel ry-akin myr t'ad, as ry-akin ry-akin ayns sreeh liorish Doré. Va'n Rime er chur da stôrys ard, lesh mooarane ymmyd jeh'n scene. Ayns The Raven ta'n soun as y dath y choadey jeh'n monotone oc as cha nel ymmyd jeh'n 'place' ny 'occasion' ain. Cre'n towse t'ayn? Ta Doré cur sreeh ymmyd jeh'n theme myr v'eh er n'yannoo rish, "y ghraih jeh'n baase as jeh'n hallucination jeh'n annym nagh vod ymmyd jeh." Ayns cuid jeh ny tarraaghyn shoh ta e vee-chraueeaght follaghtagh; ta feallagh elley soilshaghey magh e ghraih cummaltagh, as ny s’mishrey jeh’n aght v’eh, myr skeeal-draue, v’eh sheese echey hene. Ta shen soilshaghey magh dy row red erbee cooidjagh eddyr mooads obbree Poe as Doré. By lhisagh shoh soilshaghey magh my row'n derrey yeh er n'yannoo e laue er "Screeuyn y Grotesque as y Arabesque." Va'n daa elley ry-gheddyn ayns y thalloo elf jeh fantasy as romans. Er feats melodramatic v'ad daa-eed, trooid nyn goyrle jeh'n ard-natural, sheiltynys y line-danger eddyr y ideal as y absurd. Poe v'eh yn ard-addyr smoo jeh'n Beautiful; va e ghraih er son y ghraih shen, as ayns shoh t'eh ry-cheilley rish e illustrator. Bard ny artist, Ta'n baase er-y-fa shen ymmyrkey ooilley: ayns sjeayl e harbinger sable, ta'n crayon symbolic Vedder cur ad ry-cheilley, agh t'eh ry-cheilley rish y mantel jeh glenney as pooar dy bragh farraghtyn. Ta ben American er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh'n skeeal jeh Genius lesh sooillyn stereotypagh rish y torch derrey, y skeeal s'brinnagh jeh fer ta'n vision echey cur-my-ner, agh nagh vel e laghyn ry-hoi, mysteriyn thallooin er-dy-hoshiaght "door boayl legended."

Edmund C. Stedman.

YN CORRAN.

Ayns mean-oie, tra v'ee er smooinaghtyn er, lag as staydoil,
 Over mooarane voluum quaint as curious jeh lore er-diu,
 As mish glenney my chione, cha row mee er-gerrey dy ghoaill coyrle, haink tapping dy tappee,
 As jeh quoi-erbee ry-rap, ry-rap ec dorrys y chamber aym.
 "T'eh red erbee dy veeal," dooyrt mee, "tappee er dorrys y chamber aym—
 Agh shoh, as ny smoo."
Ah, ta mee cooinaghtyn dy row eh ayns Mee ny Nollick doaltattym,
 As ren gagh ember dy marroo e ghost er y lhoo.
 Va mee dy mooar booiagh y laa er-dy-henney:—vainly v'eh er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh
 Veih my leighyn surcease dy ghraih—graih son y Lenore caillt—
 Son y ven-chaggee rare as radiant ta'n ainleyn enmyssit Lenore—
 Nameless ec shoh son dy bragh.
As y rustling trimshagh as neu-chiaralagh jeh gach curtain purple
 Thrilled mee—liooar mee lesh aggleyn fantastic nagh row riamh er n’yannoo roimhe;
 As shen-y-fa nish, dy scughey y chree, sheese mee reesht
 "T'eh red erbee dy chur stiagh ec dorrys y seihll aym
 Roish y traa t'er n'gholl shaghey, t'eh gobbraghey rish dorrys y chamber aym;—
