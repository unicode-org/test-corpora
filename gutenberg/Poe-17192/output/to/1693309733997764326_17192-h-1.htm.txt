Fakatātā 4

"'A, mahino 'oku ou manatu'i na'e 'i he fakapo'uli 'o Tisema, Pea naʻe ngaohi ʻe he ʻember takitaha ʻoku mate ʻa hono laumālié ʻi he falikí."

Fakatātā 5

"Na'a ku faka'amu vēkeveke ki he 'apongipongi;—ta'e'aonga 'eku feinga ke nō . Mei heʻeku ngaahi tohí ʻoku ʻasi mai ʻa e loto mamahí —loto mamahi kia Lenore kuo molé."

Fakatātā 6

"Loto mamahi ki he Lenore kuo mole."

Fakatātā 7

"Mo e ta'ahine 'oku 'ikai ke fa'a ma'u mo ngingila 'oku fakahingoa 'e he kau 'angelo ko Lenore— Taʻehingoa ʻi heni ʻo taʻengata."

Fakatātā 8

"'T ko ha kau 'a'ahi 'oku nau kole mai ke hū mai ki hoku matapā 'o e lokí— Ko ha kau ʻaʻahi tōmui ʻoku nau kole mai ke hū mai ki hoku matapā lokí."

Fakatātā 9

"Ko 'eni na'a ku fakaava lahi 'a e matapa;— Fakapoʻuli ʻi ai, pea ʻikai toe ʻi ai ha meʻa kehe."

Fakatātā 10

"Ko e veiveiua, misi misi 'oku 'ikai ha matelie 'e lotolahi ke misi kimu'a."

Fakatātā 11

"'Ko e mo'oni,' ko 'eku lea ia, 'ko e mo'oni ko e me'a ia 'i hoku lattice 'o e matapa sio'ata; Tuku ke u sio leva, pe ko e hā ʻa e meʻa ko iá, pea fakatotoloʻi ʻa e misiteli ko ʻení.'"

Fakatātā 12

"Fakaava heni na'a ku lī 'a e shutter."

Fakatātā 13

. . . . . . . . "Ko ha Leveni faka'ei'eiki 'o e ngaahi 'aho tapu 'o e kuohili." Naʻe ʻikai ke ne fai ha kiʻi punou siʻisiʻi; naʻe ʻikai ke ne tuʻu pe nofo ʻi ha miniti."

Fakatātā 14

"Naʻá ku tangutu ʻi ha fuʻu ʻakau ʻo Pallas ʻi ʻolunga pē ʻi he matapā ʻo hoku lokí— Naʻe tuʻu, mo tangutu, pea ʻikai toe ʻi ai ha meʻa kehe."

Fakatātā 15

"'Oku 'alu holo mei he 'uta 'o e Po."

Fakatātā 16

"Kae ʻoua kuó u meimei lahi ange ʻi heʻeku lāungá, 'Kuo lele ha ngaahi kaungāmeʻa kehe kimuʻa— ʻI he ʻapongipongí te ne mavahe meiate au, ʻo hangē ko ʻeku ʻamanaki lele kimuʻá.'"

Fakatātā 17

"Pea, 'i he ngoto 'a e velvet, Na'a ku betook au ke fakafehokotaki . Faka'ofo'ofa ki he faka'ofo'ofa."

Fakatātā 18

"Ka ko hai 'oku veliveli violet 'a e 'aofi mo e maama 'o e maama gloating o'er . Te ne lomiʻi, ʻā, ʻikai toe!"

Fakatātā 19

"'Faka'ofa,' ko 'eku kalanga ia, 'kuo 'oatu 'e ho 'Otua 'a e no kiate koe —'o fakafou 'i he kau 'angelo ko 'eni na'a ne fekau'i atu koe . Mālōlō—mālōlō mo e nepenthe mei hoʻo ngaahi manatu kia Lenore!'"

Fakatātā 20

Fakatātā 22

"Talaange ki he laumalie ko 'eni 'oku kavenga 'a e loto mamahi kapau, 'i loto 'i he Aidenn mama'o, . Te ne puke ha taʻahine māʻoniʻoni ʻa ia ʻoku fakahingoa ʻe he kau ʻāngeló ko Lenore."

Fakatātā 23

"'Ke hoko ko e fo'i lea ko ia ko hotau faka'ilonga 'o e mavahe, manupuna pe fiend!' Naʻá ku kaila, ʻo kamata hake."
