Ndemanga PA NDAKATULO.

Cinsinsi ca ndakatulo, sipang’ono kuposa kupambana kwa mseru, cimakhala m’khutu la woimva. Perekani ku spell yake, vomerezani maganizo a ndakatulo: izi, pambuyo pa zonse, ndi zomwe anzeru amayankha mukawafunsa za mtengo wake. Ngakhale wolemba ndakatulo mwiniyo, mumkhalidwe wake wina, akukuuzani kuti luso lake ndi luso lamanja, chakudya chake chamatsenga, ndikupereka kuti akuwonetseni chinyengo chake, musamukhulupirire. Yembekezerani ora lake launeneri; ndiye dziperekeni ku chilakolako chake, chisangalalo chake kapena zowawa zake. "Tili m'manja mwa Chikondi lero!" akuimba Gautier, m'mawu omveka bwino a Swinburne, - ndipo kuyambira m'mawa mpaka kulowa kwadzuwa timagwedezeka panyanja yachiwawa: pali chikondi chimodzi, May 1, chingwe chamaluwa. Chikondi ndi chamuyaya, zina zonse ndi zopanda pake ndipo zimayikidwa pambali. Masomphenya ali ndi mapeto, zochitika zikusintha; koma tapindula kanthu, kukumbukira chithumwa. Monga olemba ndakatulo ambiri, zithumwa zambiri. Pali kukongola kwa Evanescence, komwe kumapereka kukongola kwakukulu ndi chisomo cha aureole ya Pathos. Gawani ndi Landor "usiku wake umodzi wokumbukira ndi kuusa moyo" wa Rose Aylmer, ndipo muli ndi izi mokwanira.

Ndipo tsopano gwirani dzanja la woyimba nyimbo wadziko latsopano, wosokera ku malo ena abwino kupita ku America wamwano ndi wosagwirizana yemwe, monga Baudelaire adawonera, "inali ya Poe yekha ndende yayikulu yomwe adathamangiramo, uku ndi uku, ndi chiwopsezo cha thupi lopangidwa kuti lipume m'dziko loyera," komanso komwe "moyo wake wamkati, wolimbikira wauzimu, ngakhale wauzimu. thawa chisonkhezero cha mkhalidwe wosamvera chisoni umenewu.” Gwirani dzanja lomvera la woyimba yemwe ali ndi vuto lomwe amasewera modabwitsa, ndikugawana naye zomwe adapeza, - masana onse, nthawi zonse usiku, - ngati sichoncho Atlantis komwe adasokera, malo othawirako malire omwe adasungidwa masana.

Ku dziko limodzi lokha ali ndi mphamvu yakukutsogolerani, ndipo usiku umodzi wokha mukhoza kugawana maloto ake. Kapepala ka dziko lapansi kapena Kumwamba: "No-man's-land," kunja kwa Space, kunja kwa Nthawi. Pano pali osokonezeka, omwe kudzera m'maso mwawo, monga a Cenci, moyo umapeza mazenera ngakhale malingaliro ali osokonezeka; apa mizimu, ikusakasaka njira yopita ku Muyaya, imayimitsidwa ndi kuchedwa. Ndilo limbo la "miyoyo ya mapulaneti," momwe muli kusatsimikizika konse kwa mwezi, chikondi chonse chotayika ndi chinyengo. Apa ena akhazikika m'maganizo, kupuma kokhako kotheka; ena

"kuyenda modabwitsa
 Kwa nyimbo yosagwirizana:

pamene kulikonse kuli

"Zokumbukira Zakale Zakale-
 Mawonekedwe ophimbidwa omwe amayamba ndi kuusa moyo
 Pamene akudutsa woyendayendayo."

Ndilo dzikolo, ndipo kwa usiku umodzi timalowamo, usiku wa zochitika zakuthambo ndi kulira kobwerezabwereza. Ma monodies ake ndi ndakatulo khumi ndi ziwiri, zomwe nyimbo zake zimayesetsa kusintha koma zimakhala zofanana. Imodzi ndi imodzi imamveka, ngati kulira kwa wotchi ya brazen ndi ebony, mu "Masque of the Red Death," yomwe inapangitsa kuti mawaltzer ayime ndi "kusokonezeka ndi kunjenjemera ndi kusinkhasinkha," nthawi zonse pamene ola linkafika.

Pazinthu zonsezi zachinsinsi, kudandaula kwa Raven , kugwedezeka kudzera pa portal, makamaka kwachititsa chidwi dziko lakunja. Ndi zinthu ziti zomwe zimapita popanga ndakatulo, -ndipo zili zoona bwanji mu izi, monganso zina zambiri, mpikisano womwe umatcha zibonga zake "opanga"? Ntchito imayitanidwa kuti ichoke pamalowo. Kumene kunalibe kalikonse, kumakhalabe,—cholengedwa chatsopano, mbali ya chuma cha anthu. Ndipo nyimbo zingapo zapadera, zochulukirapo kuposa zina zomwe zimapangidwanso mofanana, zimatikakamiza kulingaliranso momwe cholembera cha wolemba ndakatulo molimba mtima chimasinthira zinthu zosadziwika.

"Kupanga mawonekedwe, ndikupereka ku airy kanthu
 Malo okhalamo, ndi dzina.

Iliyonse imawoneka yopanda choyimira, komabe zonse zimatisangalatsa ndi zinthu zomwe zachotsedwa mumthunzi wa Zauzimu. Tsopano malingaliro apamwamba akukhudzidwa ndi moyo wa zinthu; zikhoza kapena sizingalimbikitse Zongopeka kuti anthu omwe ali ndi zithunzi zosamveka bwino zapakati pa mwezi. Komabe, imodzi mwa nyimbozi, m’njira yake yaying’ono, imatikhudza ndi lingaliro lapadera, monga momwemonso ndi ntchito zapamwamba kwambiri za ochita zauzimu—“Faustus” ya Marlowe, “Faust” ya Goethe, “Manfred,” kapena ngakhale zida zaluso za ethereal, “Mkuntho” ndi “Loto la Usiku Wapakati pa Summer”. Zoposa imodzi, ngakhale zosiyana, zili ndi zina kulemedwa kapena kudziletsa komwe kumadetsa chikumbukiro, -kumveka kamodzi, kosaiwalika, monga kamvekedwe ka kuyimitsidwa kwa chiwalo chomwe sichimagwiritsidwa ntchito kawirikawiri koma chodziwika. Chodziŵika kwambiri pakati pawo ndi “Lenore” wa Bürger, woimba wamatsenga ndi womveka bwino, nyambo ndi zojambula za omasulira. Ochepa angakane kuti Coleridge's "Rime of the Ancient Mariner" waima pamutu pawo. "Le Juif-Errant" akanakhala ndi zonena, Beranger akanakhala wolemba ndakatulo wamkulu; ndipo, koma chifukwa chakutali kwawo ndi chifundo chodziwika, "Dona wa Shalott" ndi "Damozel Wodala" akhoza kuwonjezeredwa pamndandanda. Zinaperekedwa kwa Edgar Allan Poe kuti apange nyimbo ziwiri, "The Bells" ndi Raven , iliyonse yomwe, ngakhale kuti mwina ndi yokongola kwambiri kuposa ya Tennyson ndi Rossetti, ndi yapadera. "Ulalume," ngakhale ili yodabwitsa komanso yongoganizira, ilibe mawonekedwe achilengedwe omwe ndi gawo la mayeso athu.

Mbalame yotchedwa Raven yomwe ili m'ndakatulo yeniyeni imagwera pansi pa zidutswa monga "Haunted Palace," "The City in the Sea," "The Sleeper," ndi "Israfel." Zonsezi zingasinthidwe, ndikukayikira, ndi owerenga okonda masewera, ndi ndime monga izi:

"Kuzungulira, ndikukweza mphepo kuyiwala,
 Mosasamala pansi pa thambo
 Madzi osungunuka amakhala.
Palibe kuwala kochokera kumwamba koyera komwe kumabwera pansi
 Pa nthawi yayitali yausiku ya tawuniyo;
 Koma kuwala kuchokera kunyanja yowuma
 Amayendetsa ma turrets mwakachetechete-
 · · · · · · ·
 Pamwamba kachisi wambiri komanso wodabwitsa
 Yemwe amawomba friezes amalumikizana
 Viol, violet, ndi mpesa.
 · · · · · · ·
 Palibe zotupa zomwe zimanena kuti mphepo ingakhale
 Panyanja yosangalatsa kwambiri -
 Palibe umboni wotsimikiza kuti mphepo yakhalapo
 Panyanja pamakhala bata kwambiri."

Ilibe nyimbo yapamlengalenga ya wolemba ndakatulo yemwe zingwe zake zapamtima zimakhala zoyimba:

“Ndipo amati (kwaya ya nyenyezi
 Ndi zinthu zina zomvetsera)
 moto wa Israeli
 Ndi chifukwa cha zeze
 Momwe amakhala ndi kuyimba -
 Waya wamoyo wonjenjemera
 Za zingwe zosazolowereka zimenezo."

Koma Raven , monga "Mabelu" ndi "Annabel Lee," amadziyamikira okha kwa ambiri ndi ochepa. Ndanena kwina kuti zopanga za Poe zomwe sizinachitikepo zimawoneka kwa ine "zomwe zimawonekera, modzidzimutsa, - momwe amamvera zomwe amamvera, pomwe akugwa ndikuyimba nyimbo zambiri, zonyozeka kwambiri, zimachokera kwa iye mosazindikira." Ichi akadali chikhulupiriro changa; ndipo komabe, pakuwerenga kwatsopano ndakatulo iyi, imandisangalatsa kwambiri kuposa nthawi ina iliyonse kuyambira ubwana wanga. Kudziwana kwapafupi kumanena mokomera ntchito zonse zaluso. Limbikitsani munthu, yemwe sadziwa luso kapena kusamala nazo, kuti apende ndakatulo kapena kujambula, ndipo posakhalitsa mphamvu zake zimamugwira! M'malo mwake, adzakweza mtengo wocheperako wa ntchito yabwino yoyamba yomwe chidwi chake chakopeka nacho. Raven , nayenso, ali ndi mikhalidwe yosasinthika yomwe ngakhale katswiri ayenera kusilira. Mu ndakatulo zina za mlembi wake mulibe cholinga chofotokozedwa momveka bwino. "The Haunted Palace" ndi yotsimikizika kwa owerenga osankhidwa, koma Poe sakanatenga fanizo losawoneka bwino la kusanthula dazi. Khwangwala ali wotanganidwa kwambiri ndi mutu wa mlembi wake—kutaya kosatheka kwa mkazi wopembedzedwa ndi wokongola; koma pazifukwa zina, komanso, chibadwa cha anthu ndicholondola poganiza kuti ndi ndakatulo yake yomuyimira.

Munthu wanzeru nthawi zambiri amatsogola akachita bwino, ndipo izi sizikhala zazikulu nthawi zonse. Kuzindikiridwa ndi nthawi yomweyo chifukwa choimitsidwa. Iye sachipeza, monga mwa chuma cha wosokera, ndalama pambuyo pa khobidi, koma “alibe konse kapena konse konse. Ndipo motero ndi zokhumba zina: msilikali, msilikali, wosewera, - zirizonse zomwe ali nazo, - aliyense amawerengera kupambana kwake kuchokera ku sitiroko yamwayi. Poe's Raven, ngakhale kuti anali wodabwitsa, anali kwa iye "mbalame yomwe inapangitsa mphepo kuwomba." Wolemba ndakatuloyo anakhazikika ku New-York, m'nyengo yozizira ya 1844-'45, kupeza ntchito pa pepala la Willis, "The Evening Mirror," ndikupeza ndalama zake popereka zopereka kwina. Kwa zaka zisanu ndi chimodzi anali wolemba wokangalika, ndipo anali ndi mbiri yabwino; adachitidwa mwaulemu komanso molakwika, ndipo sanataye gawo lake la utolankhani wosalipidwa wa tsikulo. Anachitanso zambiri mwa ntchito zake zabwino kwambiri, monga "Ligeia" ndi "Kugwa kwa Nyumba ya Usher," (yotsirizirayi ili ndi mnzake wodabwitsayo, m'vesi, la Elihu Vedder's "A Lost Mind,") zowunikira monga "The Gold Bug" ndi "Mystery of Marie Roget." Anapanga anthu otembenukira ku Chiyuda kumayiko ena, ndipo adagwira mwamphamvu malingaliro achi French. Iye anali ataphunzira mphamvu zake ndi kufooka kwake, ndipo anali pa msinkhu wake, ndipo osati wopanda mbiri inayake. Koma sanalembe chilichonse chimene chinali pa lilime la aliyense. Pantchito yosowa komanso yofewa, kukhudza kotchuka kuyenera kuwonjezeredwa kuti mukope anthu ambiri pa nthawi yake.

Kudzera m'makampani omwe adalemba mbiri ya Poe motsatizana, kugunda kopangidwa ndi The Raven kwakhala nkhani yosimbidwa nthawi zambiri. Mkazi wamng'ono wa ndakatuloyo, Virginia, anali kuzimiririka pamaso pake, koma anakhalabe kwa chaka china mkati mwa mthunzi wa imfa. Chipinda chachitali, chotsika mnyumbamo pafupi ndi Bloomingdale Road ndi Chodziwika bwino ngati chipinda chomwe Rouget de l'Isle adapanga Marseillaise. Onse amva kuti ndakatuloyo, yolembedwa "Quarles," inawonekera mu "American Review," ndi ndemanga yachinyengo pa mawonekedwe ake; kuti Poe analandira madola khumi pa izo; Willis, yemwe anali wokoma mtima kwambiri komanso wansanje kwambiri mwa ochita masewero apamwamba, adasindikizanso ndi mawu olimbikitsa omwe adapangitsa kuti kuyankhulana kwatawuni. Kukayika konse za kulembedwa kwake kunathetsedwa pamene Poe adadziwerengera yekha pamsonkhano wazolemba, ndipo kwa nthawi yayitali anali wolemba wodziwika kwambiri mwa olemba aku America. Kugundako kunamulimbikitsa ndi kumulimbikitsa. Monga satirist wina komanso wonyada, nayenso adapeza "chinachake chachilimwe" ngakhale "munthaka ya tizilombo." Mwachisoni kwambiri, koma zaka zitatu zidadutsa, -nthawi yachikoka, kunyada, kuzunzika, komabe nthawi zonse yantchito yongoganizira kapena yovuta, chigonjetso chomaliza chisanachitike, chinsalu chisanagwe pa moyo womwe kwa iye unalidi tsoka, ndipo adadzipereka kwa "Wogonjetsa Nyongolotsi."

"The American Review: A Whig Journal" inali magazini yodalirika panthawiyo, yokhala ndi mizere iwiri, yosindikizidwa pamapepala abwino okhala ndi zilembo zomveka bwino, ndipo ikuwonetsedwa ndi zithunzi za mezzotint. Pakati pa zinthu zambiri zomwe zili pansi pa muyezo wapano, munali zina zomwe mkonzi aliyense angasangalale kuzilandira. Voliyumu yoyamba, ya 1845, ili ndi nkhani za Horace Greeley, Donald Mitchell, Walter Whitman, Marsh, Tuckerman, ndi Whipple. Ndakatulo yodziwika bwino ya Ralph Hoyt, "Old," idawonekera m'bukuli. Ndipo apa pali mawu atatu a Poe: "Mzinda wa M'nyanja," "Chigwa cha Zipolowe," ndi Raven . Awiri mwa awa adamangidwa, - ndi njira yake, - kuchokera ku maphunziro oyambirira, koma omaliza adatuluka ngati opangidwa mwatsopano, ndipo pafupifupi monga ife tiri nawo tsopano. Mawu akuti sanakonzedwenso pambuyo pake ndi olakwika. Zosintha zazing’ono khumi ndi chimodzi za m’magazini a magazini zinapezeka m’buku lakuti Raven and Other Poems , 1845, buku limene wolemba ndakatuloyo posakhalitsa analimbikitsidwa kugaŵira anthu. Izi nthawi zambiri zimakhala zosintha za m'kalembedwe, kapena mawu amodzi, mtundu womaliza womwe umapangidwa kuti akweze tanthauzo la liwu laling'ono. M'buku lokongola la Bambo Lang la vesi la Poe, lomwe linatulutsidwa mu "Parchment Library," wasonyeza chibadwa cha katswiri wamaphunziro, ndipo wachita mwanzeru, pobwerera ku malemba omwe atchulidwa m'buku la 1846 la London. Izi zabwerezedwanso ndi akonzi apatsogolo pake, omwenso apanga zolakwika zawozawo. Koma lemba la The Raven , chifukwa cha pempho loperekedwa kwa wolemba makope a malembo apamanja, linasinthidwabe ndi iye; m'malo mwake, adasindikiza ku Richmond, atatsala pang'ono kufa, ndikulowetsa m'ndakatulo ya "aserafi amene phazi lawo limagwa" m'malo mwa "angelo omwe phazi lawo lofooka limagwa," mu gawo lakhumi ndi chinayi. Zolemba zathu zamakono, kotero, ngakhale kuti za 1845, zasinthidwa pang'ono ndi kuwerenga kwa ndakatulo pambuyo pake, ndipo ndikuganiza, ndilolondola komanso lothandiza kwambiri la ndakatulo imodziyi. Kusintha kwakukulu kwambiri kuchokera ku Baibulo loyambirira kunawonekera, komabe, mu voliyumu mu 1845; chigawo chakhumi ndi chimodzi poyambirira chinali ndi mizere iyi, yolakwika mu ndakatulo ndipo mwanjira ina inali ndi chilema pa ndakatuloyo:

"Kutengedwa kuchokera kwa mbuye wina wosasangalala, yemwe Tsoka lopanda chifundo
 Anatsatira mofulumira ndikutsata mofulumira - kotero, pamene Hope amalumbirira,
 Stern Despair adabwerera, m'malo mwa Hope wokoma yemwe adayembekeza kudzudzula-
 Yankho lomvetsa chisoni limenelo, 'Sipadzakhalanso!'

Zingakhale bwino ngati ena, ndi otchuka, olemba ndakatulo angakhale otsimikiza kuti kusintha kwawo kumasintha nthawi zonse. Poe nthawi zonse ankabwereza masewero ake ochepa a ndime, ndipo nthawi zambiri amawongolera. Raven inali yoyamba mwa ndakatulo zochepa zomwe adatsala pang'ono kumaliza asanasindikizidwe. Zingakhale kuti amene amasamalira ndakatulo anataya pang'ono ndi imfa yake. Podziwa bwino ma prose, sanalembepo vesi pofuna kupanga ndakatulo. Pamene kukana kwa chithunzi kumamuvutitsa, mawu omwe adatulukawo anali kudzoza, monga momwe iye mwini adanenera, za chilakolako, osati cha cholinga. Izi zinali pakanthawi kochepa kwambiri mpaka kutsimikizira chiphunzitso cha Fairfield kuti chilichonse chidachitika chifukwa cha vuto lamanjenje.

Nanga n’chiyani chinapatsa wolemba ndakatuloyo kuti adziŵe bwino kwa The Raven ? Kuchokera ku maziko otani omwe adakwera pang'onopang'ono mpaka nyimbo yomwe imapuma pang'onopang'ono? Monga mwachizolowezi, kuposa chinthu chimodzi chinapita ku nyumba ya ndakatulo yotchuka kwambiri. Poganizira maulaliki aatali omwe amalalikidwa nthawi zambiri pamalemba achidule komanso osalimbikitsa, ndikuyembekeza kuti sindidzaimbidwa mlandu pakukambitsirana kwa liwu limodzi, makamaka lomwe wojambula ngati Doré wapanga mutu wa fanizo lolowerera. Mpaka posachedwapa ndimaganiza kuti kachidutswachi, ndi zochepa zomwe wolemba wake adazilemba atawonekera, zinali zapadera chifukwa chosakula kuchokera ku majeremusi mu ndime yake yachinyamata. Koma Bambo Fearing Gill wandisonyeza zina zomwe sizinasindikizidwe ndi Poe, zomwe zinalembedwa m'chaka chake chakhumi ndi zisanu ndi zitatu, ndi mutu wakuti, "Chiwanda cha Moto." Mipukutuyi ikuwoneka kuti ili m'malemba oyambirira a ndakatuloyo, ndipo kutsimikizika kwake kumatsimikiziridwa ndi banja lomwe lakhala nalo kwa zaka theka. Kupatula majeremusi omveka bwino a "The Bells" ndi "The Haunted Palace" ili ndi mizere ingapo yomwe ikuwonetsa kutsegulira ndi kutseka kwa The Raven . Ponena za kayimbidwe ka ndakatulo yathu, kuyerekeza kwa masiku kumasonyeza kuti izi zinakhudzidwa ndi kamvekedwe ka "Chibwenzi cha Lady Geraldine." Poe anali mmodzi mwa oyamba kulemekeza luso la Abiti Barrett; adalemba ndakatulo zake zosonkhanitsidwa kwa iye ngati "wolemekezeka pakati pa amuna ake," ndipo adalowamo chifundo ndi njira yake yanyimbo. Mizere ya ndakatulo yake yachikondi,

"Ndi kung'ung'udza kosadziwika bwino, mumlengalenga, nsalu yofiirira
 Imatuluka mkati ndikutuluka mozungulira mphuno zake zosasuntha, "

adapeza echo mu izi:

"Ndipo silika wachisoni akumveka phokoso losatsimikizika pa nsalu iliyonse yofiirira
 Zinandisangalatsa - zidandidzaza ndi zoopsa zomwe sindinamvepo."

Apa Poe adatenga mwayi womwe adadzudzula Longfellow, womwe palibe amene adaufunafuna yekha popanda kuzindikira komanso kulanga. Mu nyimbo ndi ma stanzaic, tidzawona kuti ndakatulo ziwirizi sizili zosiyana, koma zolinga zake ndi zosiyana. Wandakatulo wowolowa manja sanamve kalikonse koma chiyambi chenicheni cha ndakatuloyo. "Zolemba zomveka bwino izi!" iye anafuula kuti,—“mphamvu imeneyi imene imamveka!... Wolemba ndakatulo wathu wamkulu, Bambo Browning, wolemba buku la ‘Paracelsus,’ & c., ali ndi chidwi chosirira kanyimboko.” Bambo Ingram, atatha kunena za "Lady Geraldine," mochenjera akulozera gwero lina lomwe Poe angakhale adagwirapo chidwi. Mu 1843, Albert Pike, theka la Greek, theka-frontiersman, wolemba ndakatulo wa Arkansas, adasindikiza mu "Galasi Latsopano," lomwe Poe anali kulembapo, mavesi ena otchedwa "Isadore," koma atasinthidwa ndi wolemba ndikutchedwa "The Widowed Heart." Ndimasankha kuchokera pakusintha kwa Bambo Pike mawu otsatirawa, omwe mbali zake zazikulu zimagwirizana ndi mtundu woyamba:

"Mopanda bata ndimayendetsa zipinda zathu zosungulumwa, sindimaseweranso nyimbo zathu,
 Dzuwa lonyezimira limawala monyadira pansi osaseseredwa;
 Mbalame yonyodola ikukhalabe ndi kuyimba, O, kupsinjika kwachisoni!
 Pakuti mtima wanga uli ngati mtambo wa m'dzinja wosefukira mvula;
 Watayika kwa ine mpaka kalekale, Isadore!”

Pano tili ndi muyeso wautali, kufanana kwa kukana, ndi mawu oyamba a mbalame imene kuimba kwake kumawonjezera chisoni. Pali njira zina zomwe zitha kutsatiridwa ndi chidwi; makamaka, ndime yomwe Bambo Ingram amasankha kuchokera ku ndemanga yomaliza ya Poe ya "Barnaby Rudge":

"Khwangwala, nayenso, * * * ayenera kuti anapangidwa, kuposa momwe tikuwonera tsopano, gawo la lingaliro la Barnaby wodabwitsa. * * * Khalidwe lake liyenera kuti linachita, ponena za chitsiru, mbali yofanana ndi yomwe imachitira, mu nyimbo, kutsagana ndi mpweya."

Komabe, pambuyo pofotokoza majeremusi ndi kufanana kwake, phindu la ndakatuloyi likupezekabe mu chiyambi chake. Nyimbo zopita patsogolo, tsatanetsatane wowoneka bwino ndi kuwala kosiyana ndi mthunzi, - koposa zonse, chilakolako chauzimu cha usiku, chimapanga ntchito ya katswiri wodziwitsa. Ponena za mbalame yowopsyayo, iye ndi wosiyana ndi makungubwi ena onse a fuko lake, kuchokera ku "twa corbies" ndi "makungubwi atatu" a balladist mpaka Barnaby's rumpled "Grip." Pano palibe chooneka ngati nkhwangwa yomwe imavutitsa akamba akale ndi kuponda m'munda wa heraldry; palibe phantom yomwe Tickell adayimba kuti, pamene,

"anakuwa pawindo lake katatu,
 Khwangwala anatambasula mapiko ake,
 Bwino kwambiri mtsikana wachikondi ankadziwa
 Phokoso lamphamvu kwambiri. "

Khwangwala wa Poe ndi lingaliro losiyana; kubadwa kwa wolira maliro ndi wopanda chiyembekezo; chojambula chophatikizidwa ndi Memory, wolemba mbiri wa chiwonongeko, mtundu wa Zosasinthika. Atathawa kudutsa Styx, kuchokera ku "gombe la Night Plutonian," akuwoneka ngati mzimu wa wofunsayo mwiniwake, - wa iye amene sangathe, sangatero, quaff the kind nepenthe, chifukwa kukumbukira Lenore ndizo zonse zomwe zatsala kwa iye, ndipo chifukwa cha chisoni chake ngakhale icho chidzayikidwa pambali.

A Raven nawonso atha kutengedwa ngati ndakatulo yoyimilira wolemba wake, chifukwa chowonetsera malingaliro ake onse pazomwe ndakatulo iyenera kukhala. Izi zimapezeka m'nkhani zake za "Mfundo Yandakatulo," "Rationale of Verse," ndi "Philosophy of Composition." Poe adalengeza kuti "Mu Nyimbo, mwinamwake, moyo umakhala pafupi kwambiri ndi mapeto aakulu omwe, pamene auziridwa ndi Poetic Sentiment, amavutika-kulengedwa kwa Kukongola kopambana .... Vesi silingatchulidwe bwino kuposa nyimbo yotsika kapena yocheperako"; koma kachiwiri, vesi limene kwenikweni liri "ndakatulo ya Mawu" ndilo "Chilengedwe Chomveka cha Kukongola," - izi ndipo palibenso china. Kamvekedwe ka Kukongola kopambana ndi chimodzi mwa Chisoni. Nkhani yodetsa nkhawa kwambiri ndi Imfa. Izi ziyenera kugwirizana ndi Kukongola. "Chotero, imfa ya mkazi wokongola ndi mutu wandakatulo kwambiri padziko lonse lapansi, ndipo mofananamo n'zosakayikitsa kuti milomo yoyenera kwambiri pamutu wotero ndi ya wokondedwa woferedwa." Mawu omalizirawa agwidwa kuchokera ku kusanthula kwachipongwe kwa Poe pa ndakatulo yomweyi, koma akusonyeza ndendende kuchuluka kwa vesi lake. Chimake cha "Mabelu" ndi mawu osamveka a ghouls, omwe amadzitamandira poyesa mtima wa munthu. "Lenore," The Raven , "The Sleeper," "To One in Paradise," ndi "Ulalume" amapanga symphony ya tenebrose, -ndipo "Annabel Lee," yolembedwa pomalizira pake, imasonyeza kuti mutu umodzi unali naye mpaka mapeto. Apanso, zonsezi sizili kanthu ngati sizili nyimbo, ndipo ena amakhudzidwa ndi khalidwe la Fantastic lomwe limadzutsa chidwi, ndikuwonjezera mantha atsopano ku zowawa zokha. Kupyolera mu zonse kufotokozedwa moperewera, pansi pa mthunzi, chikondi choyenera cha wolemba ndakatulo, - nthawi zambiri chimawonetsedwa kwina kulikonse, - mkazi woikidwa wa Usher, Lady Ligeia, mu chowonadi chofanana ndi chikhalidwe chake. Ndikuganiza kuti chikondi cha wojambula pa "m'mawonekedwe" sichingafanane ndi kudzipereka kwake kuzinthu zina. Mkazi wapafupi ndi iye ayenera kuchita matsenga ake, mosinthana mosinthana komanso motalika, kumusangalatsa mosiyanasiyana, kapena kukhutitsidwa kusiya kukhulupirika kwake. Ayenera kukopedwa ndi Zosatheka Kupeza, ndipo izi zimangomupitilira muzokonda zake zaluso lopanga.

Poe, monga Hawthorne, adabwera ndi kuchepa kwa sukulu ya Romantic, ndipo palibe amene adakondwera kuposa iye kuseka tsoka lake. Komabe mtima wake unali ndi okonda zachikondi ndi zotsatira zawo za Kum'maŵa kapena Gothic. Kupanga kwake, kolemera kwambiri mu nthano za prose, kumawoneka ngati kumusiya pamene adalemba vesi; ndipo chiweruzo chake chinamuuza kuti ndakatulo zazitali zachikondi zimadalira kwambiri zochitika osati kudzoza, ndipo kuti, kunena ndakatulo zachikondi, mawu angakwane. Chifukwa chake chiphunzitso chake, chogwirizana momveka bwino ndi malire ake, kuti "ndakatulo yayitali" ndi kutsutsana kopanda pake. Zigawo za The Raven ndizochepa komanso zosavuta: mwamuna, mbalame, ndi kukumbukira kwa phantasmal pa mkazi. Koma chidutswacho chimapereka chiwonetsero chabwino cha zinthu zachikondi. Tili ndi chiyani? Pakati pausiku; chipinda chamthunzi ndi tome zake zankhani zoiwalika; wophunzira,—Hieronymus wamakono; mpopi wa khwangwala pa bwalo; usiku wachisanu ndi moto wakufa; zotchingira za silika zosesedwa ndi mphepo; maloto ndi kusakhulupirirana kosadziwika bwino kwa mdima womwe ukukulirakulira; mbalame yakuda, yamatsenga pamphuno yotuwa; zowonjezera za violet velvet ndi nyali yowala. Zonsezi siteji zotsatira za mkhalidwe, kuwala, mtundu, phokoso, mwangwiro chikondi, ndipo ngakhale melodramatic, koma khalidwe ndakatulo kuti melodrama kawirikawiri amasonyeza, ndi bwino kusinkhasinkha ndakatulo "chilakolako chamuyaya, ululu wosatha."

Kapangidwe kake ka The Raven kunali kochititsa chidwi. Kalankhulidwe, kamvekedwe, kulemedwa, mawonekedwe a stanzaic, adapangidwa ndi luso limodzi. Mosakayikira wolemba ndakatuloyo adachita chidwi ndi luso la nyimbo za Abiti Barrett, mu "eights," makamaka ndi dongosolo lomwe linakhazikitsidwa pafupi ndi kumapeto kwa "Lady Geraldine":

“Maso,” iye anatero, ‘akutuluka mwa ine tsopano!
 Maso owala, ngati miyala yamtengo wapatali yakale yoyikidwa mumwala wa chifaniziro cha Parian!
 Pansi pa mphumi yoyera yodekhayo, mukuyaka moto nthawi zonse
 O'er chipululu cha mchenga wabwinja wa mtima wanga ndi moyo kuthetsedwa?'

Chidziwitso chake chaluso cha nyimbo yachitatu m'mizere yachiwiri ndi yachinayi, ndi kuwonjezera kwa mzere wachisanu ndi kukana komaliza, anapanga stanza ya Raven . Kubwerezabwereza kosalekeza kumawoneka kuti kumabwera popanda kuyesayesa, ndipo nthawi zambiri nyimbo zoimbidwa m'mizere zimakhala zokopa; pamene kuletsa kapena kulemedwa kumalamulira ntchito yonse. Apanso adapindula ndi maphunziro a Abiti Barrett a ballads ndi zilankhulo zawo ndi malirime ena. "Kudziletsa" ndiko kukopa komwe wolemba ndakatulo kapena woimba amanyamula khutu loyendayenda, - chilakolako chokhazikika cha Chilengedwe cha kupsyinjika koyamba. Nthaŵi zonse ndimasirira mawu omveka bwino a woimba nyimbo wa Chingelezi, — "Nightingales, the Nightingales," "Margret, Margret," "Mtima Wanga ndi Ine," "Toll pang'onopang'ono," "Mtsinje ukuyenda," "Pan, Pan wafa," ndi zina zotero. Anagwiritsanso ntchito zomwe ndimazitcha Repetend, pogwiritsa ntchito ndakatulo zomwe ndakatulo zonse zakhala zikuchita bwino kwambiri:

“E inu zamoyo zokondwa!
 Kukongola kwawo kunganene kuti:
 Kasupe wa chikondi anasefukira mu mtima mwanga,
 Ndipo ndinawadalitsa mosadziwa:
 Ndithudi woyera mtima wanga anandichitira chifundo,
 Ndipo ndidawadalitsa mosadziwa."

Poe adapanga mzere wachisanu wa stanza yake yamatsenga obwerezabwereza. Anadalira kwambiri ndakatulo zingapo pambuyo pake, "Lenore," "Annabel Lee," "Ulalume," ndi "For Annie." Idapeza nyimbo zakutchire komanso zonyowa, ndaganiza, kuchokera ku "zokoma," za zolemetsa za Afric ndikubwereza zomwe zidayimbidwa ali mwana, zikugwirizana ndi nyimbo yawo yachibadwidwe mawu a wolemba ndakatulo wathu wakumwera.

"Philosophy of Composition," kusanthula kwake The Raven , ndiko kugawanika kwaukadaulo kwa njira yake ndi kapangidwe kake. Sitiyenera kulemekezedwa kotheratu chifukwa chonena za luso lake lopanda nzeru, kapena kuululira kwa anzake kuti kuonetsa luso limeneli kunali zina mwa chinyengo chake. Ngati akanakonza ntchito yonseyo pasadakhale, sakanapanga mawu oyamba anthabwala ya nyimbo zomveka bwino za ndime yachiwiriyi, ngakhale pa chiphunzitso chake chodabwitsacho. Zoonadi, wojambula, atamaliza ntchitoyo, amaona, monga Wojambula woyambayo, kuti ndi yabwino, ndipo amawona chifukwa chake ili yabwino. Kusanthula kotsatira, pamodzi ndi kukana moto uliwonse wopatulika, kungathe kupangidwa mosavuta. Chikhulupiriro changa ndi chakuti lingaliro loyamba ndi zolemba zovuta za ndakatuloyi zinadza monga kudzoza kumabwera nthawi zonse; kuti mlembi wake ndiye anawona m’mene angakwaniritsidwire, akumaupereka kukhudza komalizira kolongosoledwa m’mutu wake wa Kupanga, ndi kuti chotsiriziracho, chotero, sichabodza kotheratu kapena chowona kotheratu. Vuto la kusanthula koteroko ndilakuti kumapangitsa munthu wongoyamba kumene kuganiza kuti njira zopangapanga zimatha kuposa malingaliro. Chikhumbo cha namatetule ndi kuteteza zinsinsi za nthawi yake yolenga. Zowona zomwe zidapezedwa pakugwira ntchito movutikira, zoyeserera zododometsa, zomwe zidatsogolera kupambana, monga momwe tawonera posachedwa za Hawthorne, zimapereka malangizo amtengo wapatali ndi chilimbikitso kwa wojambula wowona mtima. Koma mmodzi amene mwaufulu amawulula Muse wake pamaso pa onse obwera ayenera kukumbukira tsogolo la King Candaules.

Dziko lapansi limaganizirabe za Poe ngati "munthu wopanda mwayi wanzeru." Posachedwapa ndinamumva akutchulidwa kuti "amene aliyense akuwoneka kuti amamuneneza molakwika, kumunyoza kapena kumuneneza." Koma zikuwoneka kwa ine kuti tsoka lake lidatha ndi imfa yake yomvetsa chisoni, ndikuti kuyambira pamenepo chitetezo chake chakhala cholimbikira, ndipo kutchuka kwake kwakukula mokhazikika monga wolemba wovutika komanso waluso amatha kupempherera mu ola lake lachiyembekezo. Kudzudzula kwa Griswold ndi miseche zidasinthiratu zomwe zikuchitika. Otsutsa ndi olemba mbiri ya anthu abwera kutsogolo ndi zotsutsa zotsatizana, ndi msonkho ku khalidwe lake, ndi kumasulira kwatsopano kwa ntchito zake. Makalata ake omwe ndi zochitika zazing'ono za ntchito yake zili patsogolo pathu; mbiri, yabwino ndi yoipa, imadziwika kwambiri. Palibe wodandaula yemwe walandira chigamulo chachifundo komanso chokhululuka. Zovuta zake m'moyo zinali za dera lake komanso nthawi yake, mwinanso kufooka kwa kufuna kwake. Mosakayikira malo ake sanali oti atetezere khalidwe losakhwima bwino, losalinganizika ku zowopsa kunja ndi mkati. Chifuniro chake champhamvu, kukhala mbuye wa iyemwini, cholandira “cholowa cha tsoka”cho. Anaulula yekha mbuye wosasangalala wa mbalameyo, yemwe anali kudwala ndakatulo imeneyi. Koma iye anali gawo lonse la mkwiyo waukulu umene umakondwera ndi chiwonongeko chake. Pali chisangalalo pakusewera gawo lalikulu: tonse ndife ochita masewera olimbitsa thupi, tikulira Ave Imperator! Kugwira mawu nkhani ya Burke: "Pachisoni chisangalalo chikadali chachikulu, ndipo masautso omwe timakumana nawo alibe chofanana ndi ululu wamphumphu, womwe nthawi zonse umakhala wonyansa, ndipo timayesetsa kugwedeza mwamsanga." Poe anapita patali, ndipo anali wojambula ngakhale pamavuto a ntchito yake. Ngati, malinga ndi chikhulupiliro chake, chisoni ndi kutha kwa kukongola ndizo mitu yandakatulo yapamwamba kwambiri, ndi kumverera kwa ndakatulo chisangalalo chapadziko lapansi, ndiye kuti chisoni ndi mdima wa moyo wake wosweka sizinali zopanda malipiro awo kawirikawiri.

M'masamba otsatirawa, tili ndi chitsanzo chatsopano cha luso la wojambula yemwe amatanthauzira ndakatulo yotchuka. Gustave Doré, ntchito yomaliza ya amene pensulo yake ili patsogolo pathu, sanali wojambula, kapena wojambula, chifukwa cha zenizeni zomwe zimafuna choonadi cha kamvekedwe, mawonekedwe, ndi ungwiro. Zinthu zoterozo sizinkamukhudza kwambiri kuposa kupanga mawonekedwe ndi mtunda, kuwala ndi mthunzi, kuthandiza cholinga chake, chomwe chinali kusangalatsa moyo, malingaliro, ndi malingaliro a wopenya. Iye anachita zimenezi mwa kudzutsa chidwi chathu, kupyolera mu malingaliro odabwitsa ndi ochititsa kaŵirikaŵiri a zinthu zosaneneka ndi zodzaza ndi mdima kapena ulemerero. Zimanenedwa bwino kuti ngati ntchito zake sizinali zojambula zazikulu, monga zithunzi ndi zazikulu ndithu. Monga "wojambula wolemba," ndipo anali wotero, mphamvu yake inali yofanana ndi kupangidwa kwakukulu kwa wolemba wake, ndi kulimba mtima kwa mzimu umene unayatsa zake. Chifukwa chake kupambana kwake ndi Rabelais, ndi "Le Juif-Errant," "Les Contes Drolatiques," ndi "Don Quixote," motero, mosiyana, kulephera kwake kufotokoza kukongola kwa Idyls ya Tennyson, ya "Il Paradiso," ya abusa Achihebri, ndi malemba ena omwe amafunikira kukwezedwa, kapena kukoma ndi kupuma. Iye anali wobadwa mbuye wa grotesque, ndipo mwa kuzindikira kwapadera akanakhoza kuwonetsera maonekedwe a ubongo wosauka. Timaona zinthu monga mmene anthu ake ankazionera, ndiponso ndi maso a Myuda woyendayenda, Don wozunguliridwa, kapena mwana wamkazi wa osula golide amene kukongola kwake kumalemekeza kwambiri Mfumu m’sitolo ya ku Pont-au-Change. Zinali mu chikhalidwe cha zinthu kuti iye ayenera kukopeka ndi mbambande iliyonse ya vesi kapena zolemba zomwe ine ndazitcha zapadera. Maufumu apansi anaitanidwa kumtumikira; miyala yake, mitengo ndi mapiri, thambo palokha, ndi moyo ndi zolinga ndi diablerie. Akadakhala kuti afotokoze Shakespeare, tikanayenera kuwona chithandizo chodabwitsa cha Caliban, Afiti, mkuntho mu "Lear"; koma mosakayika akanayenera kukayikira malingaliro ake a Imogen kapena Miranda. Kukongola koyera ndi kophweka, ndi kupambana kwake kwangwiro, iye sankawoneka kuti akumvetsa.

Komabe pali kukongola m'mapangidwe ake a "Ancient Mariner," osawona monga momwe aliri, komanso kutsatizana kosowa mu mndandanda wa Doré. The Rime adamupatsa nkhani yayitali, ndikusintha kosiyanasiyana kwa zochitikazo. Mu Raven phokoso ndi mtundu zimasunga mawu awo ndipo tilibe kusintha kwa malo kapena zochitika. Chotsatira chake nchiyani? Doré akupereka masiyanidwe angapo pamutuwo monga momwe adauyambira, "chosamvetsetseka cha imfa ndi kuwona ziwonetsero za mzimu wosatonthozedwa." Zina mwa zojambulazi zolakwa zake zimawonekera; ena amavumbula chiyambi chake champhamvu, ndi mikhalidwe yabwino koposa imene, monga wojambula zithunzi, anaima yekha. Mwachiwonekere panali chinachake chofanana pakati pa machitidwe a Poe ndi Doré. Izi zitha kuwoneka bwino ngati womalizayo adayesa dzanja lake pa "Tales of the Grotesque and Arabesque." Onsewa nthawi zambiri amapita ku malo ongopeka komanso achikondi. M'zochita zanyimbo iwo onse, kupyolera mu kulamulira kwawo kwa zauzimu, anapewa mzere wangozi pakati pa zabwino ndi zopanda pake. Poe anali wopembedza moona wa Chokongola; chikondi chake pa icho chinali chikhumbo chodzipatulira, ndipo apa amagawana ndi wojambula wake. Wolemba ndakatulo kapena wojambula, Imfa pamapeto pake imasandulika onse: mkati mwa mthunzi wa cholembera chake, krayoni yophiphiritsa ya Vedder imawayika bwino maso ndi maso, koma amawaphimba ndi chovala cha nzeru zosakhoza kufa ndi mphamvu. Mayi wina wa ku America wapanga chithunzi cha Genius wa maso a nyenyezi ndi nyali yomaliza, mawonekedwe okongola a munthu amene masomphenya ake amawona, koma milomo yake siingathe kunena, zinsinsi za dziko lomwe liri pamwamba pa "khomo la manda a nthano."

Edmund C. Stedman.

Ndakatulo.

Khwangwala.

Kamodzi pa usiku pakati pa dreary, pamene ine ndinali kusinkhasinkha, wofooka ndi wotopa,
 Pazambiri zodziwika bwino komanso zochititsa chidwi zankhani zoiwalika,
 Ndikangogwedeza mutu, ndikungotsala pang'ono kugona, mwadzidzidzi kunabwera kugogoda,
 Monga wina akukwapula modekha, ndikugogoda pakhomo lachipinda changa.
 "'Ndi mlendo," ndinadandaula, "kugogoda pakhomo la chipinda changa-
 Izi zokha, ndipo palibenso china."
