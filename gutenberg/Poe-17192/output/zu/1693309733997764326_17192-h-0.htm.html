PHAWULA NGENKONDLO.

Imfihlo yenkondlo, engengaphansi kokuchuma kwenhlekisa, ilele endlebeni yalowo oyizwayo. Zinikeni ekulobeni kwayo, yamukeleni imbongi: lokhu phela izazi ziphendula uma uzibuza ukubaluleka kwayo. Ngisho noma imbongi ngokwayo, kwesinye isimo sayo, ikutshela ukuthi ubuciko bayo buwukukhohlisa nje, ukudla kwayo komthakathi wokudla, futhi ithembisa ukukubonisa iqhinga lakho, - ungamkholwa. Lindela ihora lakhe lesiprofetho; bese uzinikela othandweni lwakhe, injabulo noma ubuhlungu bakhe. "Sisesandleni sikaLove namuhla!" kucula u-Gautier, ku-Swinburne's buoyant paraphrapse, -futhi kusukela ekuseni kuya ekushoneni kwelanga sinyakaziswa olwandle olunobudlova: kukhona uthando olulodwa, ngoMeyi owodwa, umucu owodwa oqhakazile. Uthando lungunaphakade, konke okunye akunaqiniso futhi kubekwe eceleni. Umbono unesiphelo, indawo iyashintsha; kodwa sizuze okuthile, inkumbulo yentelezi. Izimbongi eziningi, izintelezi eziningi. Kukhona ubuhle be-Evanescence, obuboleka ebuhleni obuphakeme nomusa i-aureole yePathos. Yabelana no-Landor "ubusuku bakhe bezinkumbulo nokububula" obubodwa ka-Rose Aylmer, futhi unalokhu ngokugcwele.

Futhi manje thatha isandla somculi wezwe elisha, owaduka endaweni ethile efanele waya kuleyo Melika ehlambalazayo nengavumelani, njengoba uBaudelaire ebona, "kwakungenxa yejele elikhulu likaPoe agijima kulo, ngapha nangapha, nokushisa okushisayo kwesidalwa esidalwe ukuba siphefumule emhlabeni ohlanzekile," nalapho "impilo yakhe yangaphakathi, umzamo womoya, ungokomoya, njengomzamo wokuphila, umphefumulo wakhe, umphefumulo wakhe, umphefumulo wakhe, umphefumulo wakhe, umphefumulo wakhe, umphefumulo wakhe, ubuthakathaka. ukubalekela ithonya lalo mkhathi wokungakhathaleli." Bamba isandla esibucayi somculi okhathazekile edree ngale ndlela yakhe eyinqaba, futhi wabelane naye ngesimo sezulu athola kuso, - hhayi usuku lonke, njalo ebusuku, - uma kungeyona i-Atlantis lapho ayezulazule khona, okungenani indawo yokuphephela emingceleni lapho ayehlatshwa khona emini.

Ezweni elilodwa kuphela unamandla okukuhola, futhi ngobusuku obubodwa kuphela ungabelana ngephupho lakhe. Ipheshana elingelona uMhlaba noma iZulu: "No-man's-land," liphumile emkhathini, liphelelwe yisikhathi. Naba abakhungathekile, ngamehlo abo, njengawaseCenci, umphefumulo uthola amafasitela nakuba ingqondo iphithene; lapha imimoya, iphumputha indlela eholela ePhakadeni, iyamiswa futhi ibambezelekile. Kuyi-limbo "yemiphefumulo yeplanethi," lapho kukhona konke ukungaqiniseki kokukhanya kwenyanga, lonke uthando olulahlekile kanye nokukhohlisa. Lapha abanye bagxilile ekucabangeni, ukuphela kokuphumula okungafinyeleleka; abanye

"hamba ngendlela emangalisayo
 Emculweni ongavumelani:

kuyilapho zikhona yonke indawo

"Izinkumbulo Zesikhathi Esidlule-
 Amafomu ahlanganisiwe aqala futhi abubule
 Njengoba bedlula ozulazulayo."

Linjalo izwe, futhi singena kulo ubusuku obubodwa—ubusuku bezigaba zezinkanyezi nokukhala okuphindaphindekayo. Ama-monodies ayo ayizinkondlo eziyishumi nambili, umculo wazo olwela ukushintsha nokho usafana. Zizwakala ngamunye ngamunye, njengokukhala kwewashi le-brazen ne-ebony, kokuthi "I-Masque of the Red Death," eyenza ama-waltzer ame kancane "ngokudideka nokuthuthumela nokuzindla," njalo lapho ihora lifika.

Kuwo wonke lawa ma-cadence angaqondakali, isimangalo se -Raven , esidlidliza ngengosi, sihlabe umxhwele kakhulu umhlaba wangaphandle. Yiziphi izinto eziya ekwakhiweni kwenkondlo,—futhi kuyiqiniso kangakanani kulokhu, njengakwezinye eziningi, lowo mjaho owaqamba amabhodi awo ngokuthi “abenzi”? Umsebenzi ubizwa uphume esikhaleni. Lapho kwakungekho lutho, ihlala,—indalo entsha, ingxenye yengcebo yesintu. Futhi izingoma ezimbalwa ezihlukile, ezingaphezu kwamanye anobuciko obufanayo, zisiphoqa ukuthi sicabange kabusha ukuthi ipeni lembongi liziphendulela kanjani izinto ezingaziwa.

"ukuma, futhi ayinikezi lutho emoyeni
 Indawo yokuhlala, negama."

Ngayinye ibonakala ingenaso isibonelo, nokho zonke zisihlaba umxhwele ngezinto ezithathwe ethunzini Lokungaphezu Kwemvelo. Manje umcabango ophakeme kakhulu ukhathazekile ngomphefumulo wezinto; kungase kukhuthaze noma kungayikhuthazi i-Fantasy ukuthi abantu abanezithombe ezingacacile phakathi kwenyanga. Noma kunjalo, enye yalezi zosha zosha ingoma, ngendlela yayo encane, iyasithinta ngomuzwa wokuhluka, njengoba ngokuqinisekile njengemisebenzi esezingeni eliphansi yomlingisi ongaphezu kwemvelo—ingoma kaMarlowe ethi “Faustus,” “Faust” kaGoethe, “Manfred,” noma ngisho naleyo ngoma yobuciko obuyisimangaliso, “Isiphepho” kanye “Nephupho Lobusuku Baphakathi Nehlobo.” Ezingaphezu kweyodwa, nakuba zihlukile, zinezinye umthwalo noma ukugwema okukhathaza inkumbulo,—uma sekuzwiwe, akusoze kwalibaleka, njengephimbo lokuma kwesitho esingavamile ukusetshenziswa kodwa esihlukile. Okuphawulekayo phakathi kwazo “uLenore” kaBürger, lelo culo eliyisipoki nelinomsindo, ukuheha ne-foil yabahumushi. Bambalwa abazophika ukuthi ingoma ka-Coleridge ethi "Rime of the Ancient Mariner" ime ekhanda labo. "Le Juif-Errant" wayezoba nezimangalo, ukube uBeranger ubeyimbongi enkulu; futhi, kodwa ngenxa yokuba kude nozwelo oludumile, "Inkosikazi yaseShalott" kanye "neDamozel Ebusisiwe" ingase yengezwe ohlwini. Inikezwe u-Edgar Allan Poe ukuze akhiqize izingoma ezimbili, "Izinsimbi" kanye neRaven , ngayinye yazo, nakuba mhlawumbe inobuhle obuncane kunaleyo kaTennyson noRossetti, iyingqayizivele. I-"Ulalume," nakuba ixakile futhi iwumcabango, ayinayo ikhwalithi yendawo yonke eyingxenye yokuhlolwa kwethu.

Igwababa elisezindaweni ezisankondlo liwela ngaphansi kwezingcezu ezinjengokuthi "I-Haunted Palace," "Idolobha Lasolwandle," "I-Sleeper," kanye "no-Israfel." Konke kungashintshaniswa, ngiyasola, ngabafundi bomdlalo osheshayo, ngezindima ezinjengalezi:

"Mazungeza, ngokuphakamisa imimoya ukhohliwe,
 Ngokuzidela ngaphansi kwesibhakabhaka
 Amanzi e-melancholy amanga.
Ayikho imisebe evela ezulwini elingcwele eyehlayo
 Ebusuku obude balelo dolobha;
 Kodwa ukukhanya okuvela olwandle olugubhayo
 Usakaza ama-turrets buthule—
 · · · · · · · · ·
 Up eziningi futhi eziningi ezingcwele emangalisayo
 Okukabani ama-frieze ama-wreathed ahlanganayo
 I-viol, i-violet, nomvini.
 · · · · · · · · ·
 Akukho ukuvuvukala okutshela ukuthi imimoya ingase ibe
 Phezu kolwandle olukude olujabulisayo—
 Akukho ukusikisela kokuthi imimoya ibikhona
 Olwandle akuzolile kangako."

Ayinayo ingoma yasemoyeni yembongi enezintambo zenhliziyo eziwumbhobho:

"Futhi bathi (i-starry choir
 Nezinye izinto ezilalelayo)
 Lowo mlilo wakwa-Israel
 Kungenxa yalelohabhu
 Ahlala futhi acule ngayo—
 Intambo ephilayo evevezelayo
 Kulezo zintambo ezingajwayelekile."

Kodwa i-Raven , njengokuthi "Izinsimbi" kanye "no-Annabel Lee," izincoma kwabaningi nabambalwa. Ngike ngasho kwenye indawo ukuthi imikhiqizo kaPoe engavamile kimi ibonakala sengathi "leyo lapho kubonakala khona, okungenani, ukuzenzela, - lapho evumela khona imizwa yakhe, ngenkathi efa ewa futhi ecula iningi lomculo, elibuhlungu kakhulu, livela kuye engazi." Lokhu kuseyinkolelo yami; futhi nokho, ekucwaningeni okusha kwale nkondlo, kungihlaba umxhwele kakhulu kunanoma yisiphi isikhathi kusukela ngisemncane. Ukujwayelana eduze kusho ngokuvuna yonke imisebenzi yeqiniso yobuciko. Yenga indoda, engayazi ubuciko noma engazikhathaleli, ukuba ihlole inkondlo ethile noma umdwebo, futhi yeka ukuthi amandla ako ayibamba ngokushesha kangakanani! Eqinisweni, uzokweqa inani elihlobene nomsebenzi wokuqala omuhle lapho ukunaka kwakhe kubanjwe khona kahle. Igwababa , nalo, linezimfanelo ezingaguquguquki okufanele zibabazeke ngisho nochwepheshe. Azikho ezinye izinkondlo zombhali wayo lapho isisusa sichazwa ngokucace kakhudlwana. "I-Haunted Palace" icacile kumfundi okhethiwe, kodwa u-Poe wayengeke athathe lowo mfanekiso ocashile wokuhlaziya impandla. Igwababa limatasatasa ngendikimba evamile yombhali—ukulahlekelwa okungenakulungiseka kowesifazane okhulekelwe isithixo nomuhle; kodwa kwezinye izizathu, futhi, umuzwa wemvelo womphakathi ulungile ekucabangeni ukuthi yinkondlo yakhe emele.

Indoda ehlakaniphile ngokuvamile izuza unyawo ngempumelelo yomzamo othile, futhi lokhu akuhlali kukhulu kunayo yonke. Ukuqashelwa kushesha kakhulu ukuhlehliswa. Akayitholi, njengenhlanhla ye-miser, uhlamvu lwemali ngemva kohlamvu lwemali, kodwa "alutholi nhlobo noma alukho konke." Ngakho-ke nezinye izifiso: i-court, isosha, umlingisi, - noma ngabe yiziphi izingxenye, - ngamunye ubala ukunqoba kwakhe kusukela ekushayweni kwenhlanhla. I-Poe's Raven, naphezu kokudumazeka, kwakungenxa yakhe "inyoni eyenza umoya uphephethe." Imbongi yazinza eNew-York, ebusika bango-1844-'45, yathola umsebenzi ephepheni likaWillis, elithi "The Evening Mirror," futhi ihola imali yayo ngeminikelo kwenye indawo. Iminyaka eyisithupha wayekade engumbhali okhuthele, futhi ejabulela idumela lobungcweti; waphathwa ngenhlonipho nangokungabaza, futhi akazange alahlekelwe ingxenye yakhe yobuntatheli obungakhokhelwanga ngaleso sikhathi. Futhi wayenze umsebenzi wakhe omningi omuhle kakhulu,—njengezinganekwane ezithi “Ligeia” kanye “Ukuwa Kwendlu Ka-Usher,” (owokugcina uqukethe lowo uzakwabo oyimfihlakalo, evesini, lencwadi ka-Elihu Vedder ethi “A Lost Mind,”) izigigaba ezihlaziya njengokuthi “The Gold Bug” kanye “The Mystery of Marie Roget.” Wayenze amaproselithe phesheya, futhi wazuza ukubamba okuhlala njalo engqondweni yesiFulentshi. Wayewafundile amandla akhe kanye nobuthakathaka bakhe, futhi wayesesezingeni eliphezulu, futhi engenalo idumela elithile. Kodwa wayengabhalanga lutho olwalusolimini lwawo wonke umuntu. Emsebenzini ongavamile futhi obucayi kufanele kungezwe ukuthintwa okudumile ukuze kuthwebule izethameli ezijwayelekile ngesikhathi somuntu.

Ngomkhakha wababhali bempilo kaPoe abalandelanayo, ingoma eshisa izikhotha eyenziwe yiThe Raven isiphenduke indaba evame ukuxoxwa. Unkosikazi omncane wale mbongi, uVirginia, ubefiphala phambi kwamehlo akhe, kodwa wahlala omunye unyaka ethunzini lokufa. Igumbi elide, eliphansi endlini eliseduze nomgwaqo uBloomingdale linjengo edume njengegumbi lapho uRouget de l'Isle aqamba khona iMarseillaise. Bonke bezwile ukuthi inkondlo, esayiniwe "Quarles," ivele "ku-American Review," ngamazwana-mbumbulu wokuhlela ngesimo sayo; ukuthi uPoe wathola amadola ayishumi ngakho; ukuthi u-Willis, onomusa futhi ongenamona kakhulu ngabaxazululi bemfashini, wayiphrinta kabusha nge-eulogy eyayenza ngokushesha yaba inkulumo-dolobha. Konke ukungabaza ngobunikazi bayo kwaqedwa lapho uPoe ezisho ngokwakhe embuthanweni wezincwadi, futhi isikhathi esithile wayemakwe kakhulu kubabhali baseMelika. Ihithi yamvuselela futhi yamkhuthaza. Njengomunye futhi u-satirist oziqhenyayo, naye wathola "into yasehlobo" ngisho "nasendaweni yezinambuzane." Ngokudabukisayo, kodwa kwadlula iminyaka emithathu,—inkathi yethonya, ukuzidla, usizi, nokho njalo kuwumsebenzi ocabangelayo noma ogxekayo,—ngaphambi kokunqotshwa kokugcina, ngaphambi kokuba ikhethini lehlisele ukuphila okwaba yinhlekelele ngempela kuye, futhi wavumela “uMnqobi Wombungu.”

"I-American Review: I-Whig Journal" yayiwumagazini othembekile ngaleso sikhathi, onamakholomu aphindwe kabili, ophrintiwe ephepheni elihle elinohlobo olucacile, futhi waboniswa ngezithombe ze-mezzotint. Phakathi kwezinto eziningi ezingaphansi kwezinga elikhona manje, yayiqukethe okuthile noma yimuphi umhleli angakujabulela ukukuthola. Umqulu wokuqala, wango-1845, unezihloko zikaHorace Greeley, uDonald Mitchell, uWalter Whitman, uMarsh, uTuckerman, noWhipple. Inkondlo kaRalph Hoyt ethi, "Old," ivele kulo mqulu. Futhi nazi izingoma ezintathu zikaPoe: "Idolobha Olwandle," "Isigodi Sokungahlaliseki," kanye neRaven . Okubili kwalokhu kwakhiwe,—kwakunjalo ngendlela yakhe,—kusukela ezifundweni zangaphambili, kodwa isigcino saphuma njengokungathi saqanjwa kabusha, futhi cishe njengoba sinakho manje. Isitatimende sokuthi asizange sibuyekezwe kamuva sinephutha. Izinguquko eziyishumi nanye ezincane zombhalo kamagazini zivela encwadini ethi The Raven and Other Poems , 1845, incwadi imbongi eyazizwa ikhuthazwa ngokushesha ukuba iyihambise emphakathini. Lezi izikhathi eziningi izinguquko zezimpawu zokuloba, noma zamagama angawodwa, uhlobo lwakamuva olwenzelwe ukukhulisa umthelela wophiko-fana nofana. Encwadini enhle kaMnu Lang yevesi likaPoe, elikhishwe ku-"Parchment Library," uye wabonisa umuzwa wemvelo wesazi, futhi wenze ngokuhlakanipha, ngokubuyela embhalweni osencwadini esanda kushiwo, njengoba kunikezwe kumagazini waseLondon ka-1846. Iqoqo "elijwayelekile" likaGriswold lemisebenzi yembongi ligcwele amaphutha. Lokhu kuye kwaphindwa ngabahleli bakamuva, nabo abenze awabo amaphutha. Kodwa umbhalo weThe Raven , ngenxa yezicelo ezenziwa kumlobi zamakhophi emibhalo yesandla, wawusabuyekezwa nguye; empeleni, wayinyathelisa e-Richmond, ngaphambi nje kokufa kwakhe, esikhundleni senkondlo esithi "serafimi onyawo lwazo luwa" esikhundleni salo "izingelosi eziwa phansi," esigabeni seshumi nane. Ngakho-ke, umbhalo wethu wamanje, nakuba ungoka-1845, ushintshwe ngandlela thize ngokufundwa kamuva kwembongi, futhi, ngicabanga, inguqulo elungile nephumelelayo yale nkondlo eyodwa. Ushintsho olukhulu kakhulu oluvela enguqulweni yokuqala lwavela, nokho, emqulwini ka-1845; Isigaba seshumi nanye ekuqaleni sinale migqa, sinephutha emlolozelweni futhi ngenye indlela sinesici enkondlweni:

"Uthathwe inkosi ethile engajabule, iNhlekelele engenasihe
 Walandela ngokushesha futhi walandela ngokushesha—ngakho-ke, lapho uHope wayefungisa,
 Ukuphelelwa ithemba okuqinile kwabuya, esikhundleni seThemba elimnandi alokotha ukulifungisa—
 Leyo mpendulo edabukisayo, 'Ngeke ngiphinde!'

Kungaba kuhle uma ezinye, nezidumile, izimbongi zingaqiniseka ukuthi zenza izinguquko zazo zihlale zithuthuka. U-Poe wayelokhu ewuphendula njalo uhlelo lwakhe oluncane lwevesi, futhi ngokuvamile wayeluthuthukisa. Igwababa kwaba ngeyokuqala kwezimbalwa izinkondlo acishe wayiqeda ngaphambi kokuyiphrinta. Kungenzeka ukuthi labo abanakekela izinkondlo balahlekelwa kancane ngokufa kwakhe. Njengoba eyazi kahle iphrozi, akazange abhale ivesi ukuze enze inkondlo. Lapho ukunqanyulwa kwesithombe kumkhathaza, ingoma eyaba umphumela yaba ugqozi, njengoba yena ngokwakhe esho, lwentshiseko, hhayi injongo. Lokhu bekuyizikhawu ezingavamile kangangokuthi kucishe kuthethelele inkolelo-mbono ye-Fairfield yokuthi ngayinye yayiwumkhiqizo wenkinga yezinzwa.

Yini-ke eyanika imbongi inkomba yayo ethi The Raven ? Isuka kusiphi isisekelo esiyinkungu ikhuphuke kancane yaya emculweni ophefumulelwe kancane? Njengenjwayelo, kunento engaphezu kweyodwa eya ekwakhiweni kwenkondlo ephawuleka kangaka. Ngokucabangela izintshumayelo ezinde ezivame ukushunyayelwa ngemibhalo emifushane nengasikiseli kangako, ngethemba ukuthi ngeke ngisolwe ngale ngxoxo yeculo elilodwa,—ikakhulukazi leyo ingcweti efana noDoré eyenze isihloko somfanekiso wolahleko. Kuze kube muva nje bengicabanga ukuthi lesi siqeshana, kanye nezimbalwa ezaqanjwa umlobi waso ngemva kokuvela kwaso, zazikhethekile ngokungakhuli emagciwaneni evesini lakhe eliwumfana. Kodwa uMnumzane Esaba uGill ungibonise izitanza ezingashicilelwe zikaPoe, ezibhalwe ngonyaka wakhe weshumi nesishiyagalombili, ezinesihloko esithi, "Idemoni Lomlilo." Umbhalo wesandla ubonakala ubhalwe ngesandla sokuqala sembongi, futhi ubuqiniso bawo bufakazelwa umndeni osekuphele iminyaka engamashumi amahlanu uphethe. Ngaphandle kwamagciwane asobala okuthi "The Bells" kanye "The Haunted Palace" iqukethe imigqa embalwa esikisela ukuvulwa nokuvalwa kweRaven . Ngokuqondene nesigqi senkondlo yethu, ukuqhathaniswa kwezinsuku kubonisa ukuthi lokhu kwakuthonywe yisigqi esithi "Ukuthandana kukaLady Geraldine." UPoe wayengomunye wabokuqala ukuhlonipha ubuhlakani bukaMiss Barrett; wamloba izinkondlo zakhe aziqoqile ngokuthi “owayeyinkosi ehlonishwayo ngobulili bakhe,” wayesengena uzwela ngendlela yakhe ye-lyrical. Imigqa evela enkondlweni yakhe yothando,

"Ngokunyakaza okukhonondayo okungaqinisekile, emoyeni, ikhethini elinsomi
 Iyakhukhumala futhi ikhukhumale izungeza izibunzi zakhe eziphaphathekile ezinganyakazi,"

uthole i-echo kulokhu:

"Futhi ukukhenceza kukasilika okudabukisayo okungaqinisekile kwekhethini ngalinye elinsomi
 Kwangijabulisa—kungigcwalise ngezinto ezesabekayo engingakaze ngizizwe ngaphambili.”

Lapha uPoe wathola ilungelo asola ngalo uLongfellow, futhi okungekho muntu owake walifuna endaweni yakhe ngaphandle kokutholwa nokujeziswa ngokushesha. Ngesimo somculo nesitanza, sizobona ukuthi lezi zinkondlo ezimbili azifani, kodwa ngokwenhloso zihluke ngokuphelele. Imbongi ephanayo ayizwanga lutho ngaphandle koqobo lwangempela lwembongi. "Lo mbhalo ocacile!" wababaza,—"la mandla azwakalayo!... Imbongi yethu enkulu, uMnu. Browning, umbhali wencwadi ethi 'Paracelsus,' & c., inomdlandla ekusithakaseleni kwayo isigqi." UMnu Ingram, ngemva kokubhekisela ku-"Lady Geraldine," ngobuhlakani ukhomba omunye umthombo okungenzeka ukuthi uPoe ubambe umfutho kuwo. Ngo-1843, u-Albert Pike, i-half-Greek, i-half-frontiersman, imbongi yase-Arkansas, wayephrinte ku- "The New Mirror," lapho uPoe ayebhala khona, amavesi athile anesihloko esithi "Isadore," kodwa kusukela abuyekezwa ngumlobi futhi abiza ngokuthi "Inhliziyo Engumfelokazi." Ngikhetha enguqulweni kaMnu. Pike isigaba esilandelayo, izici eziyinhloko ezihambisana nenguqulo yokuqala:

"Angiphumuli ngihamba emakamelweni ethu anesizungu, angisawadlali izingoma zethu,
 Ilanga elicwebezelayo likhanya ngokukhazimulayo endaweni engashaneliwe;
 Inyoni ebhuqayo isahlezi futhi icula, O bunzima obudabukisayo!
 Ngokuba inhliziyo yami injengefu lasekwindla elichichima imvula;
 Ulahlekile kimi kuze kube phakade, Isadore!"

Lapha sinesilinganiso eside, ukufana kokuzibamba, kanye nokwethulwa kwenyoni ecula ngokukhulisa usizi. Kukhona ezinye izindlela ezingase zilandelwe yilabo abafuna ukwazi; ikakhulukazi, indima uMnu. Ingram ayikhethayo ekubuyekezweni kokugcina kuka-Poe kwe-"Barnaby Rudge":

"Igwababa, futhi, * * * kungenzeka ukuthi lenziwe, ngaphezu kwalokho esikubona manje, ingxenye yokukhulelwa kwe-Barnaby emangalisayo. * * * Umlingiswa walo kungenzeka ukuthi wenza, ngokuphathelene naleso siphukuphuku, ingxenye efanayo neyenziwa, emculweni, ukuhambisana maqondana nomoya."

Noma kunjalo, ngemva kokuveza la magciwane kanye nokufana, ukubaluleka kwale nkondlo kusatholakala ekuqanjweni kwayo. Umculo oqhubekayo, imininingwane ebabazekayo kanye nokukhanya okuphambene nomthunzi, - ngaphezu kwakho konke, uthando olungokomoya lwasebusuku, luwenza umsebenzi wengcweti efundisayo. Ngokuqondene nenyoni esabekayo, akafani nawo wonke amanye amagwababa omndeni wakhe, kusukela ku-"twa corbies" kanye "namagwababa amathathu" ama-balladists kuya ku-"Grip" ka-Barnaby. Lapha akukho mfanekiso we-cawing rook edla izimpungushe zokhokho futhi inyathela insimu ye-heraldry; ayikho i-boding phantom u-Tickell acula ngayo ukuthi, lapho,

"ememeza efasiteleni lakhe kathathu,
 Igwababa lashaya iphiko lalo,
 Kahle kahle intombi yazi
 Umsindo wokukhala opholile."

Igwababa likaPoe liwumqondo ohlukile; ukuzalwa kobuhlungu nokuphelelwa ithemba komuntu olilayo; i-sable efakwe ku-Memory, umlandi we-doom, uhlobo Lokungenakulungiseka. Uphunyuke wawela i-Styx, esuka "ogwini lwe-Night's Plutonian," ubonakala ewumphefumulo ofanekiselwa umuntu obuza umbuzo ngokwakhe, - walowo ongakwazi, ngeke, aphendule i-nepenthe enomusa, ngoba inkumbulo kaLenore yiyona yonke into esele kuye, futhi ngokulandela usizi lwakhe ngisho nalokho kuzobekwa eceleni.

Igwababa nalo lingathathwa njengenkondlo emele umbhali walo, ukuze libe yisibonelo sayo yonke imibono yakhe yokuthi inkondlo kufanele ibe yini. Lokhu kutholakala ezindabeni zakhe ezithi “The Poetic Principle,” “Rationale of Verse,” kanye nethi “The Philosophy of Composition.” U-Poe wamemezela ukuthi "Emculweni, mhlawumbe, umphefumulo ucishe ufinyelele isiphetho esikhulu lapho, lapho uphefumulelwe i-Poetic Sentiment, uzabalaza-ukwakhiwa kobuhle obumangalisayo .... Ivesi alikwazi ukuqokwa kangcono kunomculo ophansi noma onekhono elincane"; kodwa futhi, ivesi elithi “Inkondlo Yamazwi” ngempela lithi “Indalo Enesigqi Sobuhle,”—lokhu futhi akukho okunye. Iphimbo likaNobuhle eliphakeme kakhulu elokudabuka. Isihloko esibuhlungu kakhulu esokufa. Lokhu kumele kuhlangene noBeauty. "Khona-ke, ukufa kowesifazane omuhle, ngokungangabazeki, kuyisihloko esinkondlo kakhulu emhlabeni, - futhi akungabazeki ngokulinganayo ukuthi izindebe ezisifanelekela kakhulu isihloko esinjalo yilezo zomuntu oshonelwe." Lezi zinkulumo zokugcina zicashunwe ekuhlaziyeni okungaqondakali kukaPoe ngayo kanye le nkondlo, kodwa zikhombisa ngokunembile ububanzi bevesi lakhe. Umvuthwandaba othi "The Bells" uyizwi elilodwa elingacacile lama-ghoul, aziqhenya ngokukala inhliziyo yomuntu. "Lenore," The Raven , "The Sleeper," "To One in Paradise," nelithi "Ulalume" zakha i-symphony ye-tenebrose,—kanye nethi "Annabel Lee," ebhalwe ekugcineni kukho konke, ibonisa ukuthi itimu eyodwa yaba naye kwaze kwaba sekugcineni. Futhi, konke lokhu akuyona into uma kungewona umculo, futhi abanye bathintwa ngaleyo khwalithi ye-Fantastic evusa umuzwa wokwesaba, futhi yengeza ukwesaba okusha ebuhlungwini uqobo. Kukho konke kuchazwe kalufifi, ngaphansi kwethunzi elimnyama, uthando olufanele lwembongi,—okuvame ukuvezwa ingxenye encane kwenye indawo,—unkosikazi ongcwatshwe u-Usher, uLady Ligeia, iqiniso elihambisana nemvelo yakhe. Ngicabanga ukuthi uthando lweciko ngomunye "osesimweni" alusoze lwamelana ngokuphelele nokuzinikela kwakhe kokuhle. Owesifazane oseduze kwakhe kufanele asebenzise iziphonso zakhe, ashintshane futhi angabi nasikhathi eside, amthande ngezinhlobonhlobo ezingapheli, noma aneliswe ukudela ingxenye yokwethembeka kwakhe. Kumele akhangwe yi-Unattainable, futhi lokhu kungaphezu kwakhe ngothando lwakhe lobuciko bokudala.

U-Poe, njengo-Hawthorne, wangena ngokuwohloka kwesikole se-Romantic, futhi akekho owajabulela ukwedlula yena ukuhleka inhlekelele yaso. Nokho inhliziyo yakhe yayinabathandi bothando kanye nemiphumela yabo yaseMpumalanga noma yesiGothic. Ukusungula kwakhe, okucebile kakhulu ezindabeni ze-prose, kubonakala sengathi kuyamlahla lapho ebhala ivesi; futhi ukwahlulela kwakhe kwamtshela ukuthi izinkondlo ezinde zothando zincike kakhulu esenzakalweni kunokuphefumulelwa,—nokuthi, ukusho izinkondlo zothando, amagama azokwanela. Ngakho-ke inkolelo-mbono yakhe, ihambisana ngokucacile nokulinganiselwa kwakhe, ukuthi "inkondlo ende" iwukuphikisana okuphelele ngokwemibandela." Izingxenye zeThe Raven zimbalwa futhi zilula: indoda, inyoni, kanye nenkumbulo ye-phantasmal kowesifazane. Kodwa ucezu lunikeza ukuboniswa okuhle kwezinto zothando. Yini esinayo? Phakathi kwamabili; igumbi elinomthunzi namathomu alo ezindaba ezikhohliwe; umfundi,—i-Hieronymus yesimanje; umpompi wegwababa phezu kwecasement; ubusuku basebusika nomlilo obulalayo; izilenge ezishaywa umoya kasilika; amaphupho nokungathembani okungacacile kobumnyama obunanelayo; inyoni emnyama, engaqondakali ekuqhumeni okuphaphathekile; izesekeli ze-violet velvet nesibani esikhazimulayo. Wonke lo mphumela wesiteji wesimo, ukukhanya, umbala, umsindo, uluthando kuphela, futhi ngisho nomculo, kodwa wekhwalithi yenkondlo i-melodrama engavamile ukuyibonisa, futhi ikhombisa ngokugcwele "intshiseko yaphakade, ubuhlungu baphakade" bembongi.

Isakhiwo esinesigqi seThe Raven sasiqinisekile ukuthi sizokwenza umbono. Imvumelwano, imfanozanyana, umthwalo, isimo sestanzaic, zakhiwe ngobunyoninco obubodwa. Ngokungangabazeki imbongi yahlatshwa umxhwele ukufaneleka kwezingoma zomculo zikaMiss Barrett, "eziyisishiyagalombili," futhi ikakhulukazi ngohlelo olwamukelwa ngasekupheleni kuka "Lady Geraldine":

“Amehlo,” esho, ‘aselokhu engigqolozela!
 Amehlo acwebezelayo, njengamatshe ayigugu asendulo abekwe etsheni lesithombe seParian!
 Ngaphansi kwalelo siphongo elimhlophe elizolile, ingabe ulokhu uvutha amalangabi
 Ingabe ugwadule oluyincithakalo lwenhliziyo yami nokuphila kwami?''

Isethulo sakhe sobuciko semvumelwano yesithathu emigqeni wesibili nowesine, kanye nokwengezwa komugqa wesihlanu kanye nokuphindaphinda kokugcina, kwenza isiqephu seThe Raven . Imfanozandi ephikelelayo ibonakala iza ngaphandle komzamo, futhi ngokuvamile imilolozelo engaphakathi kwemigqa iyayenga; kuyilapho ukubanjwa noma umthwalo ubusa wonke umsebenzi. Lapha futhi wayezuze ngokufunda kukaMiss Barrett kwama-ballads kanye nabathandi bothando ngezilimi zakhe nezinye. "I-refrain" iwukuyenga lapho imbongi noma umculi ebamba indlebe ezulazulayo, - ukulangazelela okuphindaphindiwe kweMvelo kohlobo lokuqala. Bengilokhu ngiwathanda amazwi amnandi engoma yesiNgisi, ethi, “The Nightingales, the Nightingales,” “Margret, Margret,” “My Heart and,” “Toll slowly,” “The River flows on,” “Pan, Pan is dead,” njll. Waphinde wasebenzisa engikubiza ngokuthi i-Repetend, lapho oPoe asebephumelele ngale ndlela zonke izinkondlo:

"O zidalwa eziphilayo! alukho ulimi
 Ubuhle babo bungase busho:
 Kwaphuma isiphethu sothando enhliziyweni yami,
 Futhi ngababusisa ngingazi:
 Impela ongcwele wami onomusa wangihawukela,
 Futhi ngababusisa bengazi."

U-Poe udale umugqa wesihlanu westanza sakhe ngomlingo wokuphindaphinda. Wathembela kuyo ngokuphelele ezinkondlweni ezimbalwa zakamuva,—“uLenore,” “Annabel Lee,” “Ulalume,” kanye “Neka-Annie.” Ithole umculo ongalawuleki futhi opholile, ngiye ngacabanga, kusukela "emathonyeni amnandi," wemithwalo ye-Afric kanye nokuphindaphinda ayeculwa kuye ebuntwaneni, ehambisana nomculo wabo wendabuko izwi lembongi yethu yaseNingizimu.

"I-Philosophy of Composition," ukuhlaziya kwakhe I-Raven , i-dissection yobuchwepheshe yendlela yayo nesakhiwo. Akudingeki ukuba kunikezwe udumo ngokuphelele lobu buciko bakhe bokuqamba amanga, noma ukuvuma kwakhe kubangane ukuthi ukuvezwa kwalobu buciko kwakungezinye zenkohliso zakhe. Ukube wayeklame umsebenzi ophelele kusenesikhathi, wayengeke enze isandulelo esinokhahlo esingaka semilolozelo ye-rattle-pan yomculo omnandi westanza sesibili, ngisho naphezu kwenkolelo-mbono yakhe yokumnandi. Yebo, umdwebi, lapho esewuphelelisile umsebenzi, ubona, njengomdwebi wokuqala, ukuthi muhle, futhi abone ukuthi kungani muhle. Ukuhlaziya okulandelayo, okuhambisana nokuchithwa kwanoma yimuphi umlilo ongcwele, kungenziwa kalula. Inkolelo yami ukuthi umbono wokuqala kanye nohlaka olubi lwale nkondlo lweza njengoba ugqozi lufika njalo; ukuthi umbhali wayo wabe esebona ukuthi ingapheleliswa kanjani, eyinikeza ukuthinta kokugcina okuchazwe esahlukweni sakhe esithi Ukubunjwa, nokuthi lokhu kwakamuva akulona iqiniso ngokuphelele futhi akulona iqiniso ngokuphelele. Ukulimala kokuhlaziywa okunjalo ukuthi kulinga oqalayo ukuba acabange ukuthi izinqubo zokwenziwa zingadlula umcabango. Umfutho wobuhlakani uwukugada izimfihlo zehora layo lokudala. Amazwibela atholwe ngokukhandleka, izivivinyo ezididayo, ezandulela ukunqoba, njengoba kumdwebo-msebenzi we-Hawthorne osanda kuvezwa, zinikeza imfundo eyigugu nesikhuthazo kumculi oqotho. Kodwa eyodwa odalula ngokuzithandela uMnyuziyamu wakhe emehlweni abo bonke abezayo kufanele akhumbule isiphetho seNkosi uCandaules.

Umhlaba usacabanga ngoPoe "njengendoda ehlakaniphile engenanhlanhla." Ngisanda kumuzwa kukhulunywa ngaye ngokuthi "yilowo wonke umuntu obonakala eqokelwe ukummelela kabi, ukumgxeka noma ukunyundela." Kodwa kimi kubonakala sengathi ishwa lakhe laphela ngokufa kwakhe okudabukisayo, nokuthi kusukela lapho ukuzivikela kwakhe bekulokhu kuphikelela, nodumo lwakhe lokukhula okuqinile njengombhali ohluphekayo nonesiphiwo angathandazela ngehora lakhe lethemba. Ukunyundela nokunyundela kukaGriswold kwaguqula isimo savuna yena. Abagxeki nababhali bomlando wokuphila kwakhe beze phambili ngokuphikiswa okulandelanayo, ngokuhlonipha umlingiswa wakhe, nezinhlelo ezintsha zemisebenzi yakhe. Izincwadi zakhe kanye nezigameko ezincane zomsebenzi wakhe ziphambi kwethu; irekhodi, elihle nelibi, laziwa kabanzi. Akekho ofake isicelo sokubuyekeza othole ithenda eyengeziwe nesinqumo esixolelayo. Amaphutha akhe empilweni ayengowesifunda nesikhathi sakhe, okungenzeka ukuthi kwakungenxa yobuthakathaka bakhe bentando. Ngokungangabazeki indawo ayehlala kuyo yayingeyona eyokuqapha imvelo eqinile, engalingani kahle ezingozini ngaphandle nangaphakathi. Intando yakhe enamandla kakhulu, yokuba yinkosi yakhe, ayizuzele “lelofa likamaye.” Wazisholo yena umnumzane wenyoni ongajabulile, ohlaselwe yile nkondlo. Kodwa eyakhe yayiyingxenye egcwele yalowo msindo omangalisayo owethaba ekuboniseni ukubhujiswa kwawo. Kukhona intokozo ngokudlala ingxenye ephezulu yomuntu: sonke singama-gladiators, sikhala i-Ave Imperator! Ukucaphuna iqiniso likaBurke: "Osizini injabulo isahamba phambili, futhi usizi esibhekene nalo alufani nobuhlungu obuphelele, obuhlala bucasula, futhi esizama ukubunyakazisa ngokushesha ngangokunokwenzeka." UPoe waya phambili, futhi wayengumculi ngisho nasenhlekeleleni yomsebenzi wakhe. Uma, ngokwenkolelo yakhe, ukudabuka kanye nokushabalala kobuhle kuyizindikimba eziphakeme kakhulu zezinkondlo, kanye nomuzwa wenkondlo ubumnandi obuhlaba umxhwele kakhulu basemhlabeni, khona-ke usizi nobumnyama bempilo yakhe ephukile bekungekho ngaphandle kwesinxephezelo sabo esivamile.

Emakhasini alandelayo, sinesibonelo esisha sobuhlakani bomculi ochaza indlela achaza ngayo inkondlo edumile. U-Gustave Doré, umsebenzi wokugcina wepensela lakhe eliphambi kwethu, wayengeyena umdwebi, noma ngisho nomdwebi wokudweba, kumaqiniso afuna iqiniso lephimbo, isibalo, nokuphelela. Izinto ezinjalo zazingamkhathazi kangako kunokuba enze isimo nebanga, ukukhanya nomthunzi, zisize injongo yakhe,—okwakuwukuvusa umphefumulo, umcabango, womuntu obukele. Lokhu wakwenza ngokuvusa umuzwa wethu wokwesaba, ngemibono emangalisayo futhi ngokuvamile evelele yezinto ezingenakukhulumeka nezigcwele ubumnyama noma inkazimulo. Kushiwo kahle ukuthi ukube imisebenzi yakhe bekungeyona imidwebo emihle, njengezithombe mikhulu impela. "Njengomculi wezincwadi," futhi wayenjalo, amandla akhe ayelingana ngqo nokusungulwa okumangalisayo kombhali wakhe, ngesibindi somoya owavutha owakhe. Ngakho-ke impumelelo yakhe noRabelais, nge "Le Juif-Errant," "Les Contes Drolatiques," kanye "Don Quixote," ngakho-ke, ngokuphambene, ukwehluleka kwakhe ukuveza ubuhle be-Idyls kaTennyson, ye "Il Paradiso," yabelusi besiHeberu, neminye imibhalo edinga ukuphakanyiswa, noma ubumnandi nokuphumula. Wayeyingcweti yokuzalwa eyisimangaliso, futhi ngokuqondisisa okukhethekile wayekwazi ukuveza imibono yobuchopho obuhanjelwe yinhliziyo. Sibona izinto ngendlela abantu bakhe ababezibona ngayo, nangawo kanye amehlo omJuda Ozulazulayo, uDon odidekile, noma indodakazi yomkhandi wegolide obuhle bayo bukhazimulisa iNkosi esitolo ePont-au-Change. Kwakusesimweni sezinto lapho kufanele ahehe khona evesini ngalinye lobuciko noma iphrozi engiyibize njengeyingqayizivele. Imibuso ephansi yabizelwa enkonzweni yakhe; amadwala akhe, izihlahla nezintaba, isibhakabhaka ngokwaso, siphila ngesisusa kanye ne-diablerie. Ukube waphila ukufanekisa Shakespeare, bekufanele sibone impatho ephawulekayo Caliban, Abathakathi, isiphepho in "Lear"; kodwa ngokungangabazeki kufanele ngabe wayingabaza imibono yakhe ka-Imogen noma uMiranda. Ubuhle obumsulwa nobulula, kanye nobuhle obuphelele babo, wayengavamile ukubuqonda.

Nokho kunobuhle emiklamo yakhe "yeMarine Yasendulo," engelona iqiniso njengoba injalo, kanye nokulandelana okungajwayelekile ochungechungeni luka-Doré. I-Rime imnike indaba ende, nokushintshashintsha okuningi kwesigcawu. Ku -Raven umsindo nombala kugcina i-monotone yabo futhi asinakho ukushintsha kwendawo noma umcimbi. Uyini umphumela? U-Doré unikeza uchungechunge lokuhlukahluka ngendikimba njengoba ayicabanga, "impicabadala yokufa kanye nokubona izinto ezingekho zomphefumulo ongaduduzi." Kweminye yale midwebo amaphutha akhe ayabonakala; ezinye zembula imvelaphi yakhe enamandla, kanye nezimfanelo ezinhle kakhulu lapho, njengomdwebi wama-draughter, ame yedwa. Ngokusobala kwakukhona okuthile okufanayo phakathi kwezimo zokusebenza kukaPoe noDoré. Lokhu bekuzovela ngokucace kakhudlwana ukube lona wakamuva uzame isandla sakhe ku-"Tales of the Grotesque and Arabesque." Bobabili bavame ukuphendukela ku-elf-land yenganekwane nezothando. Emigilingwaneni emnandi bobabili, ngokuyala kwabo kwamandla angaphezu kwawemvelo, bagwema umugqa wengozi phakathi kokuhle nokungenangqondo. U-Poe wayengumkhulekeli weqiniso we-Enhle; uthando lwakhe ngalo kwakuwukungcwelisa, futhi lapha uhlukanisa nomdwebi wakhe. Imbongi noma umdwebi, Ukufa ekugcineni kuguqula konke: ngaphakathi komthunzi we-sable harbinger, ikhrayoni engokomfanekiso kaVedder iwabeka ngokufanele ubuso nobuso, kodwa abembesa ngengubo yokuhlakanipha namandla angenakufa.
