Kiʻi 5

Mai kaʻu mau puke mai ke kaumaha o ke kaumaha - ke kaumaha no Lenore i nalowale."


Kii 6

Kii 7


"No ke kaikamahine kakaikahi a me ka nani i kapa ʻia e nā ʻānela ʻo Lenore—
He inoa ʻole ma ʻaneʻi a mau loa aku."


Kii 8


"'O kekahi malihini e komo mai ana ma ko'u puka keena—
Ke komo nei kekahi malihini kipa ma ka puka o ko'u keena."


Kii 9


"Eia au i wehe nui aku ai i ka puka;—
Pouli ma laila, ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe."


Kii 10


"Ke kanalua, moeʻuhane ʻaʻohe kanaka i ʻaʻa e moeʻuhane ma mua."


Kii 11


"ʻOiaʻiʻo," wahi aʻu, 'ʻoiaʻiʻo he mea ia ma koʻu puka makani;
E ʻae mai iaʻu e ʻike i ke ʻano o ia mea, a me kēia mea pohihihi e ʻimi.'"


Kii 12


"E wehe ma ʻaneʻi ua hoʻolei au i ka pani."


Kii 13


. . . . . . . . "He Koraka nani o nā lā haipule o ka wā kahiko.
ʻAʻole ʻo ia i hoʻomaikaʻi iki; ʻaʻole hoʻokahi minute i kū a noho ʻo ia."


Kii 14


"E kau ana ma luna o ka ʻūhā o Pallas ma luna pono o koʻu puka keʻena—
Kau, a noho, ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe."


Kii 15


"E auwana ana mai ke kapakai Po."


Kii 16


"A hiki i koʻu wā i ʻōhumu ai, 'Ua lele mua nā hoaaloha ʻē aʻe—
I ka lā aʻe e haʻalele ʻo ia iaʻu, ʻoiai ua lele mua koʻu manaʻolana.'"


Kii 17


"A laila, i ka hāʻule ʻana o ka velvet, hoʻomaka wau e hoʻopili
Manaʻo i ka nani."


Kii 18


"Akā, nona ka velvet violet e uhi ana me ke kukui e ʻā ana
E kaomi ʻo ia , ʻaʻole loa!"


Kii 19


"'Auwe,' kahea aku la au, 'ua haawi mai kou Akua ia oe—ma keia mau anela i hoouna mai ai oia ia oe.
Hoomaha—hoomaha a nepenthe mai kou hoomanao ana ia Lenore!'"


Kii 20


"Ma kēia home e Horror haunted."


Kii 21


. . . . . . . . . "E hai mai oe ia'u, ke noi aku nei au—
Aia anei—he balsama anei ma Gileada?—e hai mai—e hai mai ia'u, ke noi aku nei au!"


Kii 22


"E haʻi aku i kēia ʻuhane me ke kaumaha kaumaha inā, i loko o Aidenn mamao,
E paʻi ia i kahi kaikamahine hemolele i kapa ʻia e nā ʻānela ʻo Lenore."


Kii 23


"'E lilo kēlā huaʻōlelo i hōʻailona o ka hemo ʻana, manu a i ʻole fiend!' Ua hoʻōho au, me ka hauʻoli."


Kii 24

Kii 25

E hoʻokiʻekiʻe ʻia - ʻaʻole loa!"
