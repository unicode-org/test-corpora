MANAʻO NO KA POEM.

ʻO ka mea huna o ka mele, ʻaʻole i emi iho ka pōmaikaʻi o ka mea hoʻohenehene, aia ma ka pepeiao o ka mea lohe. E ʻae i kāna ʻōlelo, e ʻae i ke ʻano o ka haku mele: ʻo ia ka mea a ka poʻe naʻauao e pane ai ke nīnau ʻoe iā lākou i kona waiwai. ʻOiai ʻo ka mea haku mele ponoʻī, ma kāna ʻano ʻē aʻe, e haʻi iā ʻoe he lima maʻalahi kāna ʻoihana, ʻo kāna ʻai a ka mea hoʻokalakupua, a hāʻawi ʻo ia e hōʻike iā ʻoe i ke ʻano o ia mea, - mai manaʻoʻiʻo iā ia. E kali i kona hora wanana; a laila hāʻawi iā ʻoe iho i kona makemake, kona hauʻoli a i ʻole ka ʻeha. "Aia mākou i ka lima o ke aloha i kēia lā!" mele ʻo Gautier, ma ka ʻōlelo ʻoluʻolu a Swinburne,—a mai ke kakahiaka a i ka napoʻo ʻana o ke kai, ua hoʻolele ʻia mākou i ke kai ʻino: hoʻokahi wale nō aloha, hoʻokahi Mei, hoʻokahi kaula pua. He mau loa ke aloha, he mea oiaio ole na mea a pau a waihoia. He hopena ka hihio, hoʻololi ke ʻano; akā, ua loaʻa iā mākou kekahi mea, ʻo ka hoʻomanaʻo o kahi leʻaleʻa. E like me ka nui o nā haku mele, nui nā mea hoʻohiwahiwa. Aia ka nani o Evanescence, ka mea e hāʻawi ai i ka nani kiʻekiʻe a me ka nani i kahi aureole o Pathos. E kaʻana like me Landor i kāna "pō hoʻomanaʻo a me nā kanikau" no Rose Aylmer, a loaʻa iā ʻoe kēia i ka piha.

Ano, e lawe aku i ka lima o kekahi mea hookani pila o ka honua hou, i auwana mai kahi noho kupono a hiki i kela Amerika kolohe a me ke kuewa, e like me ka Baudelaire i ike ai, "no Poe he halepaahao nui wale no ia i holo aku ai, ma o a maanei, me ka huhu wela o ka mea i hanaia e hanu i loko o ka honua maemae," a ma kahi o "kona ola kino, uhane e like me ka hooikaika ana. pakele i ka mana o keia lewa antipathetical." E paʻa i ka lima ʻoluʻolu o kahi mea mele pilikia e ʻoliʻoli nei i kāna ʻano ʻano ʻē, a e haʻi aku iā ia i ke ʻano i loaʻa iā ia, - ʻaʻole loa i ka lā a pau, i ka pō mau, - inā ʻaʻole ʻo Atlantis kahi āna i auwana ai, ma kahi liʻiliʻi o kahi puʻuhonua mai nā palena i mālama ʻia ai ʻo ia i ke ao.

I ka ʻāina hoʻokahi wale nō kona mana e alakaʻi iā ʻoe, a no ka pō hoʻokahi wale nō e hiki ai iā ʻoe ke haʻi i kāna moeʻuhane. He ʻāpana ʻaʻole o ka Honua a me ka Lani: "No-man's-land," ma waho o Space, ma waho o ka manawa. Eia ka poʻe pilikia, ma ko lākou mau maka, e like me ko ka Cenci, e ʻike ai ka ʻuhane i nā puka makani, ʻoiai ua haʻalulu ka manaʻo; eia na uhane e imi ana i ke ala e hiki aku ai i ke ao pau ole, ua oki a lohi. ʻO ia ka limbo o "nā ʻuhane honua," kahi i maopopo ʻole ai ka mahina mahina, nā aloha a me nā hoʻopunipuni a pau. Maanei ua paa kekahi poe ma ka trance, o ka maha wale no e hiki ke loaa; nā mea ʻē aʻe

"neʻe maikaʻi
 I kahi mele kū'ē:"

oiai ma na wahi apau

"He mau mea hoʻomanaʻo o ka wā i hala—
 ʻO nā ʻano i uhi ʻia e hoʻomaka a kanikau
 I ko lakou maalo ana i ka mea auwana."

ʻO ia ka ʻāina, a no ka pō hoʻokahi mākou e komo ai, he pō o nā astral phases a me nā leo kani. ʻO kāna monodies he ʻumikūmālua mau mele, nona ka mele e hoʻoikaika nei e hoʻololi e like naʻe. Hookani pakahi lakou, e like me ke kani ana o ka uaki keleawe a me ka eboni, ma "The Masque of the Red Death," i hoomaha ai ka poe waltzers me ka "disconcert and tremulousness and meditation," i na manawa a pau e hiki mai ai ka hora.

ʻO kēia mau cadences pohihihi a pau, ʻo ka plaint of The Raven , e haʻalulu ana ma ka puka, ua hoʻohauʻoli nui i ka honua o waho. He aha nā mea e pili ana i ka hana ʻana i ka mele, - a pehea ka ʻoiaʻiʻo o kēia, e like me ka hapa nui, kēlā lāhui i kapa i kona mau papa "ka mea hana"? Kāhea ʻia kahi hana ma waho o ka hakahaka. Ma kahi ʻaʻohe mea, koe nō ia, he hana hou, ʻāpana o ka waiwai o ke kanaka. A ʻo kekahi mau huaʻōlelo kūʻokoʻa, ʻoi aku ma mua o nā mea ʻē aʻe i like like, koi iā mākou e noʻonoʻo hou i ka wiwo ʻole o ka peni a ka haku mele e hoʻohuli ai i nā mea ʻike ʻole ʻia.

"e hoʻohālike, a hāʻawi i ka lewa ʻole
 He wahi noho kūloko, a he inoa."

Me he mea lā ʻaʻohe prototype, akā hoʻohiwahiwa nā mea āpau iā mākou me nā mea i hoʻokaʻawale ʻia mai ke aka o ka Supernatural. I kēia manawa, pili ka manaʻo kiʻekiʻe e pili ana i ka ʻuhane o nā mea; hiki paha ke hoʻoulu i ka Fantasy i nā kānaka me nā kiʻi i ka interlunar vague. Eia nō naʻe, ʻo kekahi o kēia mau mele, ma kona ʻano liʻiliʻi, e pili ana iā mākou me ke ʻano o ke ʻano ʻokoʻa, e like me nā hana haʻahaʻa o kahi mea hoʻokalakupua, ʻo Marlowe's "Faustus," ka "Faust" o Goethe, "Manfred," a i ʻole kēlā mau mea hana ethereal, "The Tempest" a me "A Midsummer Night's Dream." ʻOi aku ma mua o hoʻokahi, ʻoiai ʻokoʻa, loaʻa kekahi ʻO ke kaumaha a i ʻole ka hoʻopaʻa ʻana i ka hoʻomanaʻo, i lohe ʻia, ʻaʻole poina ʻia, e like me ka leo o kahi mea hoʻohana ʻole ʻia akā ʻokoʻa. ʻO ka mea kaulana i waena o lākou ʻo "Lenore" a Bürger, ʻo ia ka puʻupuʻu ʻuhane a me ka leo kani, ka lure a me ka foil o ka poʻe unuhi. He kakaikahi ka poe e hoole ana e ku ana ka "Rime of the Ancient Mariner" kupanaha o Coleridge ma ko lakou poo. He mau koi ko "Le Juif-Errant", inā he haku mele ʻoi aku ʻo Beranger; a, akā, no ko lākou mamao ʻana mai ke aloha kaulana, hiki ke hoʻohui ʻia ʻo "The Lady of Shalott" a me "The Blessed Damozel" i ka papa inoa. Ua hāʻawi ʻia iā Edgar Allan Poe e hana i ʻelua mau mele, "The Bells" a me The Raven , ʻo kēlā me kēia, ʻoiai ʻoi aku ka liʻiliʻi o ka nani ma mua o ko Tennyson lāua ʻo Rossetti, he mea kū hoʻokahi. ʻO "Ulalume," ʻoiai he ʻano ʻano ʻē a noʻonoʻo, ʻaʻole i loaʻa ke ʻano o ke ao holoʻokoʻa kahi ʻāpana o kā mākou hoʻāʻo.

ʻO ka Raven i loko o nā mele mele ma lalo o nā ʻāpana e like me "The Haunted Palace," "The City in the Sea," "The Sleeper," a me "Israfel." Ke manaʻo nei au e hoʻololi ʻia ka mea a pau e ka poʻe heluhelu o kahi hoʻolei wikiwiki, no nā pauku e like me kēia:

"A puni, ma ka hāpai ʻana i ka makani poina,
 Haʻalele i lalo o ka lani
 Ke moe nei ka wai melancholy.
ʻAʻole iho mai nā kukuna mai ka lani hemolele mai
 I ka po lōʻihi o ia kūlanakauhale;
 Aka, mai ke kai lurid
 E kahe malie ana i na hale kupapau—
 · · · ·
 E piʻi aʻe i kahi heiau kupanaha
 ʻO wai nā friezes lei e hui pū
 ʻO ka viole, ka viole, a me ke kumu waina.
 · · · ·
 ʻAʻohe ʻōhū e haʻi mai he makani paha
 Ma luna o kekahi kai hauʻoli mamao loa—
 ʻAʻohe manaʻo he nui ka makani
 Ma nā kai i emi iki ka mālie."

Ua nele i ke mele lewa o ka haku mele nona ka puuwai he lute.

"A ke ʻōlelo nei lākou (the starry choir
 A me nā mea hoʻolohe ʻē aʻe)
 Ke ahi o ka Israfeli
 No ia lira
 Noho ʻo ia a hīmeni—
 Ka uwea ola haalulu
 ʻO kēlā mau kaula maʻamau."

Akā ʻo ka Raven , e like me "The Bells" a me "Annabel Lee," e hoʻomaikaʻi iā ia iho i ka nui a me ka liʻiliʻi. Ua ʻōlelo au ma nā wahi ʻē aʻe, ʻo nā mea hana ʻē aʻe a Poe me he mea lā iaʻu "ʻo nā mea i ʻike ʻia, ma ka liʻiliʻi loa, o ka spontaneity, - kahi i hāʻawi ai ʻo ia i kona mau manaʻo, ʻoiai ʻo ka make ʻana a hāʻule i ka hapa nui o nā mele, melancholy, hele mai iā ia me ka ʻike ʻole." ʻO kēia koʻu manaʻoʻiʻo; aka nae, ma ka noonoo hou ana i keia poem, ua oi aku ka mahalo ia'u mamua o ko'u wa kamalii. Ua ʻōlelo ka ʻohana pili i ke aloha i kēlā me kēia hana ʻoiaʻiʻo. E hoʻowalewale i ke kanaka ʻike ʻole a mālama ʻole hoʻi iā ia, e nānā i kekahi mele a i ʻole nā ​​​​pena kiʻi, a pehea lā e paʻa koke ai kona ikaika iā ia! ʻOiaʻiʻo, e hoʻonui ʻo ia i ka waiwai pili o ka hana maikaʻi mua i ʻike pono ʻia ai kona nānā. ʻO ka Raven hoʻi, he mau ʻano like ʻole e pono ai ka mea akamai e mahalo. ʻAʻohe mea ʻē aʻe o kāna mau mele a ka mea kākau i wehewehe ʻia ke kumu. ʻO "The Haunted Palace" e like me ka maopopo i ka mea heluhelu i koho ʻia, akā ʻaʻole hiki iā Poe ke lawe i kēlā ʻōlelo hoʻohālike maʻalahi no ka nānā ʻana i ka ʻōhule. Ua paʻa loa ka Raven i ke kumumanaʻo maʻamau o ka mea kākau—ka nalowale hiki ʻole ke hoʻihoʻi ʻia o kahi wahine hoʻomana kiʻi a nani; akā, ma nā kumu ʻē aʻe, ua pololei ka manaʻo o ka lehulehu ma ka noʻonoʻo ʻana i kāna mele ʻelele.

Loaʻa i ke kanaka akamai ke kūlana me ka kūleʻa o kekahi hoʻoikaika ʻana, ʻaʻole ʻo ia ka mea nui loa. ʻOi aku ka wikiwiki o ka hoʻomaopopo ʻana no ka hoʻopanee ʻana. ʻAʻole loaʻa iā ia, e like me ka waiwai o ka pōʻino, ke kālā ma hope o ke kālā, akā "ʻaʻole loa a ʻaʻole loa." A pela no me na makemake e ae: o ke aloalii, koa, keaka,—he aha ko lakou mau wahi,—e helu ana kela mea keia mea i kona lanakila ana mai kekahi hahau laki. ʻOiai ʻo Poe's Raven, ʻoiai ʻo ia hoʻi, ʻo ia ka "manu nāna i puhi ka makani." Ua noho ka haku mele ma New-York, i ka hooilo o ka makahiki 1844-'45, e imi ana i ka hana ma ka pepa a Willis, "The Evening Mirror," a e imi ana i kana loaa ma na haawina ma kahi e. No nā makahiki ʻeono, ua lilo ʻo ia i mea kākau ʻeleu, a ua hauʻoli ʻo ia i ke kūlana ʻoihana; Ua mālama ʻia ʻo ia ma ka mahalo a me ke kānalua ʻole, a ʻaʻole ia he pohō no kāna ʻāpana o ka nūpepa uku ʻole ʻia o ia lā. Ua hana pū ʻo ia i ka hapa nui o kāna mau hana maikaʻi loa, ʻo ia hoʻi nā moʻolelo e like me "Ligeia" a me "The Fall of the House of Usher," (ʻo ia ka mea i loaʻa i kēlā me kēia mea pohihihi, ma ka pauku, ʻo "A Lost Mind" a Elihu Vedder,) e like me "The Gold Bug" a me "The Mystery of Marie Roget." Ua hana oia i ka poe haipule ma na aina e, a ua paa mau i ka manao Farani. Ua aʻo ʻo ia i kona mana ponoʻī a me kona nāwaliwali, a ua noho ʻo ia i kona kūlana kiʻekiʻe, ʻaʻole me ka ʻole o ka inoa. ʻAʻole naʻe ʻo ia i kākau i kekahi mea ma ka ʻōlelo a nā kānaka a pau. No ka hana kakaʻikahi a paʻakikī, pono e hoʻohui ʻia kekahi paʻi kaulana e hopu i ka lehulehu o ka manawa ponoʻī.

Ma o ka ʻoihana o nā mea kākau moʻolelo o Poe, ua lilo ka pā i hana ʻia e The Raven i moʻolelo i haʻi pinepine ʻia. ʻO ka wahine ʻōpio a ka haku mele ʻo Vireginia, e mae ana i mua o kona mau maka, akā ua hoʻomau ʻo ia no kekahi makahiki hou i loko o ka malu o ka make. ʻO ke keʻena lōʻihi a haʻahaʻa ma ka hale kokoke i ke alanui Bloomingdale e like me kaulana e like me ka lumi kahi i haku ai ʻo Rouget de l'Isle i ka Marseillaise. Ua lohe na mea a pau ua puka mai ka poem, i kakau inoa ia "Quarles," ma ka "American Review," me ka olelo pseudo-edittorial ma kona ano; ua loaa ia Poe he umi dala no ia mea; ʻO Willis, ka mea ʻoluʻolu a lili loa o nā mea hoʻoponopono hoʻoponopono, paʻi hou iā ia me kahi eulogy i lilo koke ia i kamaʻilio kūlanakauhale. Ua hoʻopau ʻia nā kānalua a pau o kāna mea kākau i ka wā i heluhelu ai ʻo Poe iā ia iho ma kahi hui palapala, a no kekahi manawa ʻo ia ka mea kākau ʻoi loa o nā mea kākau ʻAmelika. Hoʻoulu a hoʻoikaika ka paʻi iā ia. E like me kekahi mea haʻaheo a haʻaheo, ua loaʻa ʻo ia i "kekahi mea o ke kauwela" ʻoiai "i loko o ka hum o nā iniseti." Me ke kaumaha, aka, ua hala na makahiki ekolu,—he manawa o ka mana, ka haaheo, ka ehaeha, aka nae o ka hana noonoo a koʻikoʻi paha,—ma mua o ka lanakila hope, ma mua o ka hāʻule ʻana o ka pākū ma luna o ke ola nona ka ʻoiaʻiʻo he pōʻino, a hāʻawi ʻo ia i "ka Ilo Naʻi."

ʻO "The American Review: A Whig Journal" he makasini i hōʻaiʻē ʻia no ia manawa, ʻelua kolamu, paʻi ʻia ma ka pepa maikaʻi me ke ʻano maopopo, a i hōʻike ʻia e nā kiʻi mezzotint. Ma waena o nā mea nui ma lalo o ke kūlana o kēia manawa, aia kekahi mau mea e hauʻoli ai kekahi mea hoʻoponopono. ʻO ka puke mua, no 1845, aia nā ʻatikala na Horace Greeley, Donald Mitchell, Walter Whitman, Marsh, Tuckerman, a me Whipple. Ua ʻike ʻia ma kēia puke ka mele kuʻuna a Ralph Hoyt, "Old." A eia nā mele ʻekolu na Poe: "The City in the Sea," "The Valley of Unrest," and The Raven . ʻElua o kēia mau mea i kūkulu ʻia, - ʻo ia kāna ala, - mai nā haʻawina mua, akā ʻo ka inoa hope i puka mai me he mea lā i haku hou ʻia, a kokoke e like me kā mākou i kēia manawa. He hewa ka ʻōlelo ʻaʻole i hoʻoponopono hou ʻia. He ʻumikūmākahi mau hoʻololi liʻiliʻi mai ka puke makasini i ʻike ʻia ma The Raven and Other Poems , 1845, he puke i manaʻo ʻia e ka haku mele e hāʻawi i ka lehulehu. He mau hoʻololi kēia o nā kaha kikoʻī, a i ʻole nā ​​huaʻōlelo hoʻokahi, ke ʻano hope i hana ʻia e hoʻonui ai i ka hopena o ka alliteration. Ma ka puke nani a Poe a Mr. Lang, i lawe ʻia mai i loko o ka "Parchment Library," ua hōʻike ʻo ia i ka manaʻo o kahi haumāna, a ua hana naʻauao, i ka hoʻi ʻana i ke kikokikona ma ka leo i ʻōlelo ʻia, e like me ka mea i hāʻawi ʻia ma ka pukana London o 1846. ʻO ka "standard" Griswold ohi o nā hana a ka haku mele he nui nā hewa. Ua hana hou ʻia kēia e nā mea hoʻoponopono hope, a ua hana hewa lākou. Akā ʻo ka kikokikona o The Raven , ma muli o nā noi i hana ʻia i ka mea kākau no nā kope manuscript, ua hoʻoponopono hou ʻia e ia; ʻoiaʻiʻo, ua paʻi ʻo ia ma Richmond, ma mua o kona make ʻana, me ka hoʻololi ʻana i ke mele o "seraphim nona ka wāwae" no nā "anela i hāʻule mau wāwae," ma ka paukū ʻumikumamāhā. No laila, ʻo kā mākou kikokikona i kēia manawa, ʻoiai ʻo ka makahiki 1845, ua hoʻololi ʻia e ka heluhelu ʻana a ka haku mele ma hope, a ke manaʻo nei au, ʻo ia ka mana kūpono a maikaʻi loa o kēia mele hoʻokahi. Ua ʻike ʻia ka hoʻololi nui loa mai ka mana mua loa, akā naʻe, ma ka leo i ka makahiki 1845; ʻO ka paukū ʻumikūmākahi i loaʻa i kēia mau lālani, he hewa i ka mele a i ʻole he kīnā ma ka mele:

"Loaʻa mai kekahi haku hauʻoli, nona ka pōʻino aloha ʻole
 Hahai wikiwiki a hahai wikiwiki - no laila, ke hoʻohiki ʻo Hope,
 Ua hoʻi mai ʻo Stern Despair, ma kahi o ka Manaʻolana maikaʻi āna i ʻaʻa ai e hoʻohiki—
 ʻO kēlā pane kaumaha, 'Aʻole loa!'"

He mea maikaʻi inā hiki i nā poʻe ʻē aʻe, a kaulana hoʻi, ke hōʻoiaʻiʻo e hoʻomaikaʻi mau i kā lākou mau loli. Hoʻomaʻamaʻa mau ʻo Poe i kāna hōʻike liʻiliʻi o ka paukū, a ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi. ʻO ka Raven ka mua o nā mele liʻiliʻi āna i hoʻopau ai ma mua o ka paʻi ʻana. Malia paha ua nalowale ka poʻe mālama mele i kona make ʻana. ʻAʻole ʻo ia i kākau i ka paukū no ka hana ʻana i ka mele. I ka wā i hoʻopiʻi ai ke kiʻi iā ia, ʻo ke mele i loaʻa mai ai, ʻo ia ka hoʻoikaika ʻana, e like me kāna i ʻōlelo ai, ʻo ke kuko, ʻaʻole o ke kumu. He mea kakaʻikahi kēia e like me ka mea e hōʻoiaʻiʻo ai i ka manaʻo Fairfield he hua kēlā me kēia o kahi pilikia hopohopo.

No laila, he aha ka mea i hāʻawi i ka haku mele i kāna ʻike iā The Raven ? Mai ke kumu noe hea i piʻi mālie ai i kahi mele i hanu mālie? E like me ka mea maʻamau, ʻoi aku ma mua o hoʻokahi mea i hele i ke kūkulu ʻana i kahi mele kaulana. Ma ka noʻonoʻo ʻana i nā haʻi ʻōlelo lōʻihi i haʻi pinepine ʻia ma nā kikokikona pōkole a me ka liʻiliʻi o ka manaʻo, ke manaʻo nei wau ʻaʻole e hoʻopiʻi ʻia no kēia kūkākūkā ʻana o kahi mele hoʻokahi, ʻo ia hoʻi ka mea a kahi mea pena e like me Doré i hana ai i ke kumuhana o ke kiʻi hoʻohaunaele. A hiki i kēia mau lā, ua manaʻo wau ʻo kēia ʻāpana, a me kekahi mau mea i haku ʻia e kona mea kākau ma hope o kona ʻike ʻia ʻana, he mea kūʻokoʻa i ka ulu ʻole ʻana mai nā germs i kāna paukū keiki. Akā, ua hōʻike mai ʻo Mr. Fearing Gill iaʻu i kekahi mau pauku i hoʻopuka ʻole ʻia e Poe, i kākau ʻia i kona makahiki ʻumikumamāwalu, a ua kapa ʻia ʻo "The Demon of the Fire." Ua ʻike ʻia ka manuscript ma ka lima kākau mua a ka haku mele, a ʻo kona ʻoiaʻiʻo e hōʻoiaʻiʻo ʻia e ka ʻohana nona ka mea i noho ai no ka hapalua haneli. Ma waho aʻe o nā germs maʻalahi o "The Bells" a me "The Haunted Palace" aia kekahi mau laina i manaʻo ʻia no ka wehe ʻana a me ka pani ʻana o The Raven . E pili ana i ka puolo o kā mākou mele, ʻo ka hoʻohālikelike ʻana i nā lā e hōʻike ana ua hoʻohuli ʻia kēia e ke mele o "Lady Geraldine's Courtship." ʻO Poe kekahi o nā mea mua i hoʻohanohano i ke akamai o Miss Barrett; Ua kākau ʻo ia i kāna mau mele i hōʻiliʻili ʻia iā ia ma ke ʻano he "ʻoi aku ka hanohano o kāna wahine," a i loko aloha me kona ʻano mele. ʻO nā laina mai kāna mele aloha,

"Me ka ʻōhumu ʻōhumu i maopopo ʻole, i ka lewa, ka pale poni
 Pehu a pehu a puni kona mau lae haikea ole,"

ua loaʻa kahi leo i kēia mau mea:

"A ʻo ka ʻūlū ʻana o kēlā me kēia pale poni ʻulaʻula
 Hoʻohauʻoli iaʻu - hoʻopiha iaʻu me nā weliweli weliweli ʻaʻole i ʻike ma mua. "

Ma ʻaneʻi ua loaʻa ʻo Poe i kahi kuleana i hoʻopaʻi ʻia ʻo ia iā Longfellow, a ʻaʻohe mea i ʻimi ma kona wahi ponoʻī me ka ʻike ʻole a me ka hoʻopaʻi ʻana. Ma ke ʻano mele a me ke ʻano stanzaic, e ʻike mākou ʻaʻole like nā mele ʻelua, akā ma ke kumu he ʻokoʻa loa. ʻO ka haku mele lokomaikaʻi ʻaʻohe mea i manaʻo i ke ʻano maoli o ka haku mele. "Keia palapala maopopo!" i hooho ae ai oia,—"keia mana i manaoia!... O ko kakou haku mele nui, o Mr. Browning, ka mea kakau o 'Paracelsus,' &c., ua ohohia loa ia i ka puolo." ʻO Mr. Ingram, ma hope o ka haʻi ʻana iā "Lady Geraldine," kuhikuhi ʻo ia i kahi kumu ʻē aʻe i loaʻa ai iā Poe kahi manaʻo. I ka makahiki 1843, ua paʻi ʻo Albert Pike, ka hapa Helene, hapa palena, haku mele o Arkansas, ma "The New Mirror," kahi a Poe i kākau ai i ia manawa, kekahi mau pauku i kapa ʻia ʻo "Isadore," akā mai ka manawa i hoʻoponopono ʻia e ka mea kākau a kapa ʻia ʻo "The Widowed Heart." Koho au mai ka hooponopono hou ana a Mr. Pike i keia mau pauku, a o na hiohiona nui e like me ka manao mua.

"Me ka hoʻomaha ʻole wau e hele nei i ko mākou mau keʻena mehameha, ʻaʻole wau e hoʻokani hou i kā mākou mau mele,
 Puhi nui mai ka lā ʻulaʻula ma luna o ka papahele i holoi ʻole ʻia;
 Noho mau ka manu hoohenehene a oli, E ke kaumaha e!
 No ka mea, ua like koʻu naʻau me ke ao hāʻule i piha i ka ua;
 Ua nalowale ʻoe iaʻu a mau loa aku, e Isadore!"

Eia kekahi ana lōʻihi, he ʻano like o ka hoʻopaʻa ʻana, a me ka hoʻolauna ʻana o ka manu nona ke mele e hoʻonui ai i ke kaumaha. Aia kekahi mau alahele ʻē aʻe i hahai ʻia e ka poʻe ʻimi; ʻoiai, he paukū a Mr. Ingram i koho ai mai ka loiloi hope a Poe no "Barnaby Rudge":

"O ka manu koraka hoi, * * * ua hanaia paha, oi aku mamua o ka kakou e ike nei, he hapa o ka manao o ka Barenaby fantastic.

Eia nō naʻe, ma hope o ke kuhikuhi ʻana i kēia mau germs a me nā ʻano like, ʻike ʻia ka waiwai o kēia mele ma kona ʻano kumu. ʻO ke mele holomua, nā hiʻohiʻona hiʻohiʻona a me ka mālamalama like ʻole a me ka malu, - ma luna aʻe o nā mea a pau, ʻo ke aloha ʻuhane o ka pō, e hana ia i ka hana a ka mea akamai hoʻomaopopo. ʻO ka manu weliweli, ʻaʻole ia e like me nā manu koraka ʻē aʻe o kona ʻohana, mai ka "twa corbies" a me "ʻekolu manu koraka" o ka poʻe hoʻokani pila a hiki i ka "Grip" rumpled a Barnaby. ʻAʻole kēia he ʻano o ka puʻu cawing e kau nei i nā hale kupapaʻu kupuna a hehi i ke kahua o ka heraldry; ʻaʻohe phantom hoʻohenehene a Tickell i mele ai, i ka wā,

"E hooho ana ma kona pukaaniani ekolu,
 Hoʻopā ka manu koraka i kona ʻēheu,
 Ua maopopo loa i ke kaikamahine aloha
 ʻO ke kani hoʻopaʻapaʻa koʻikoʻi."

He manaʻo ʻokoʻa ke koraka a Poe; ka hoʻokomo ʻana i ka ʻeha a me ka manaʻolana ʻole o ke kanaka kanikau; he sable embodied Memory, the abiding chronicler of doom, a type of the Irreparable. Pakele i ka Styx, mai "ka Po o Plutonian shore," me he mea lā ʻo ia ka ʻuhane kiʻi o ka mea nīnau iā ia iho, - ʻo ka mea hiki ʻole, ʻaʻole hiki ke hoʻokaʻawale i ke ʻano nepenthe, no ka mea, ʻo ka hoʻomanaʻo ʻana iā Lenore ʻo ia wale nō ka mea i koe, a me ka nui o kona kaumaha e waiho ʻia.

Hiki ke lawe ʻia ʻo Raven ma ke ʻano he mele no kāna mea kākau, no ka hoʻohālikelike ʻana i kāna mau manaʻo āpau i ke ʻano o ka mele. Loaʻa kēia ma kāna mau moʻolelo ma "The Poetic Principle," "The Rationale of Verse," a me "The Philosophy of Composition." Ua ʻōlelo ʻo Poe, "ma ka Music, malia paha, ʻaneʻane loaʻa ka ʻuhane i ka hopena nui, i ka wā i hoʻoulu ʻia e ka Poetic Sentiment, e hakakā ʻo ia-ka hana ʻana i ka nani kiʻekiʻe. aka, o ka pauku maoli ka "Poetry of Words" "The Rhythmical Creation of Beauty,"—ʻo kēia a ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe. ʻO ka leo o ka nani kiʻekiʻe kekahi o ke kaumaha. ʻO ka melancholy o nā kumuhana he make. Pono kēia e pili i ka nani. "No laila, ʻo ka make ʻana o ka wahine uʻi, ʻaʻole ia he mea kānalua, ʻo ia ke kumuhana poʻomanaʻo loa o ka honua, - a pēlā pū me ke kānalua ʻole ʻo nā lehelehe kūpono loa no ia kumuhana, ʻo ia nā lehelehe o ka ipo i hoʻonele ʻia." Hoʻopuka ʻia kēia mau ʻōlelo hope mai kā Poe kānana ʻana i kēia mele, akā hōʻike pololei lākou i ka laulā nui o kāna pauku. ʻO ka piko o "The Bells" ʻo ia ka monotone muffled o nā ghouls, ka mea e kaena nei i ke kaumaha ʻana i ka puʻuwai kanaka. ʻO "Lenore," The Raven , "The Sleeper," "To One in Paradise," a me "Ulalume" i hana i kahi symphony tenebrose, - a ʻo "Annabel Lee," i kākau ʻia ma hope o nā mea a pau, e hōʻike ana i hoʻokahi kumuhana i loaʻa iā ia a hiki i ka hopena. Eia hou, he mea ʻole kēia inā ʻaʻole mele, a ua hoʻopā ʻia kekahi i kēlā ʻano o ka Fantastic e hoʻāla ai i ke ʻano o ka weliweli, a hoʻohui i kahi makaʻu hou i ka ʻeha. Ma nā mea a pau, ua hōʻike pohihihi ʻia, ma lalo o kahi pale malu, ke aloha maikaʻi loa o ka haku mele, - i hōʻike pinepine ʻia ma kahi ʻē aʻe, - ka wahine i kanu ʻia a Usher, ka Lede Ligeia, ma ʻoiaʻiʻo ke ʻano o kona ʻano ponoʻī. Manaʻo wau ʻaʻole hiki i ke aloha o kahi mea pena kiʻi "ma ke ʻano" ke hoʻokūkū piha i kona kūpaʻa i kekahi mea kūpono. Pono ka wahine e kokoke ana iā ia e hoʻomaʻamaʻa i kāna mau ʻano, e hoʻohuli a ʻaʻohe lōʻihi, e hoʻohiwahiwa iā ia me nā ʻano like ʻole, a i ʻole e ʻoluʻolu e haʻalele i kāna kuleana. Pono ʻo ia e hoʻowalewale ʻia e ka Unattainable, a ʻoi aku kēia ma mua o kona makemake i ka hana noʻeau.

Ua hele mai ʻo Poe, e like me Hawthorne, me ka emi ʻana o ke kula Romantic, a ʻaʻohe mea i hauʻoli i ka ʻakaʻaka i kona pōʻino. Akā naʻe, aia kona puʻuwai me ka poʻe aloha a me kā lākou hopena Oriental a i ʻole Gothic. ʻO kāna mea i hana ai, me ka waiwai nui o nā moʻolelo moʻolelo, me he mea lā ua haʻalele ʻo ia iā ia i kāna kākau ʻana i ka paukū; a ʻo kāna hoʻoholo ʻana i haʻi aku iā ia, ʻoi aku ka hilinaʻi o nā mele aloha lōʻihi i ka mea i hana ʻia ma mua o ka hoʻoikaika ʻana, - a ʻo ka hoʻopuka ʻana i ka mele o ke aloha, ua lawa nā mele. No laila ʻo kāna kumumanaʻo, ua kūpono i kona mau palena ponoʻī, ʻo "ʻo ka 'pom lōʻihi' he kūʻē paʻa i nā huaʻōlelo." He liʻiliʻi a maʻalahi nā ʻāpana o The Raven : he kāne, he manu, a me ka hoʻomanaʻo phantasmal i kahi wahine. Akā hāʻawi ka ʻāpana i kahi hōʻike maikaʻi o nā mea aloha. He aha kā mākou? ʻO ke aumoe; ke keena malu me kona mau palapala moolelo poina; ka haumana,—he Hieronymus hou; ka paʻi ʻana o ka manu koraka i ka pahu; ka po hooilo a me ke ahi e make ana; nā pale kilika i kāhili ʻia e ka makani; nā moeʻuhane a me ka hilinaʻi ʻole o ka pōʻeleʻele; ʻo ka manu ʻeleʻele, ʻano ʻino ma luna o ka ʻāʻī ʻeleʻele; nā mea komo o ka violet velvet a me ke kukui hoʻonani. A pau keia pae hopena o ke kulana, malamalama, kala, kani, he purely aloha, a hiki melodramatic, akā, o ka poetic maikaʻi i melodrama kakaʻikahi hōʻike, a pono reflective o ka haku mele "aloha mau loa, eha mau loa."

ʻO ke ʻano rhythmical o The Raven ua maopopo ka manaʻo. Rhyme, alliteration, the burden, the stanzaic form, were devided with singular adroitness. Me ke kanalua ole, ua hahauia ka haku mele i ka pono o na mele mele a Miss Barrett, ma "eights," a oi loa aku hoi ma ka hoonohonoho i laweia kokoke i ka pau ana o "Lady Geraldine":

"'E na maka,' i mai la oia, 'ano e hahau ana iloko o'u! O oukou anei na maka nana au i hoopau?
 ʻO nā maka ʻālohilohi, e like me nā mea kahiko kahiko i hoʻonohonoho ʻia ma ke kiʻi-pōhaku Parian!
 Ma lalo o kēlā lae keʻokeʻo mālie, e ʻā mau ana ʻoe
 O'er ka waonahele one neoneo o koʻu puʻuwai a me ke ola i hoʻopau ʻia?'"

ʻO kāna hoʻolauna ʻana i ke kolu o ka mele ma ka laina ʻelua a me ka ʻehā, a me ka hoʻohui ʻana i ka laina ʻelima a me ka hoʻopaʻa hope ʻana, ua lilo ka stanza o The Raven . Me he mea lā e hele mai ana ka ʻōlelo hoʻomau ʻana me ka hoʻoikaika ʻole ʻia, a he mea hoʻowalewale pinepine nā mele i loko o nā laina; ʻoiai ke kāohi a i ʻole ke kaumaha ma luna o ka hana holoʻokoʻa. Ma ʻaneʻi nō hoʻi i pōmaikaʻi ai ʻo Miss Barrett i ke aʻo ʻana i nā mele pila a me nā mele ma kāna mau ʻōlelo ponoʻī a me nā ʻōlelo ʻē aʻe. ʻO ka "pale" ʻo ia ka lure a kahi haku mele a mea hoʻokani pila paha e hoʻopaʻa ai i ka pepeiao auwana, -ʻo ka makemake mau o Nature no ke kaumaha mua. Ua mahalo mau au i na puana maikai a ka wahine mele Pelekane,—"The Nightingales, the Nightingales," "Margret, Margret," "My Heart and I," "Toll slow," "The River is dead," "Pan, Pan is dead," etc.

"E nā mea ola hauʻoli! ʻaʻohe alelo
 E haʻi paha ko lākou nani:
 He punawai o ke aloha i hū mai koʻu puʻuwai,
 A hoʻomaikaʻi aku au iā lākou me ka ʻike ʻole:
 ʻOiaʻiʻo, ua aloha mai koʻu haipule aloha iaʻu,
 A hoʻomaikaʻi au iā lākou me ka ʻike ʻole.

Ua haku ʻo Poe i ka lālani ʻelima o kāna stanza no ke kilokilo o ka mea hou. Ua hilinaʻi nui ʻo ia iā ia i loko o kekahi mau mele ma hope,—"Lenore," "Annabel Lee," "Ulalume," a "No Annie." Ua loaʻa iā ia he mele ʻāhiu a me ka melancholy, i koʻu manaʻo, mai nā "ʻoluʻolu ʻoluʻolu," o nā haʻahaʻa a me nā haʻalulu o ʻApelika i mele ʻia iā ia i ka wā kamaliʻi, e hoʻopili pū ana me kā lākou mele kamaʻāina i ka leo o kā mākou haku mele Hema.

ʻO "The Philosophy of Composition," kāna kānana ʻana o The Raven , he wehewehe ʻenehana o kāna ʻano a me kāna ʻano. ʻAʻole pono e hoʻopiʻi ʻia ʻo kāna ʻōlelo ʻana i ke koko anuanu, a ʻo kāna ʻōlelo hoʻi i nā hoaaloha ʻo ka hōʻike ʻana i kēia mea hana i kekahi o kāna mau hoʻopunipuni naʻauao. Inā ua hoʻolālā mua ʻo ia i ka hana holoʻokoʻa, ʻaʻole ʻo ia e hana i kahi ʻano paʻakikī o ka hoʻomaka ʻana o nā mele rattle-pan i ka leo ʻono o ka pauku ʻelua, ʻaʻole hoʻi ma luna o kona manaʻo o ka fantastic. ʻOiaʻiʻo ka mea pena kiʻi, ua hoʻopau i kahi hana, ʻike, e like me ka Artist mua, he maikaʻi ia, a ʻike i ke kumu he maikaʻi. Hiki ke hana maʻalahi ka nānā ʻana, me ka hōʻole ʻana i kekahi ahi kapu. ʻO koʻu manaʻoʻiʻo, ʻo ka manaʻo mua a me ke kiʻi paʻakiki o kēia mele i hele mai e like me ka hoʻoikaika mau ʻana; ua ʻike ka mea kākau i ke ʻano o ka hoʻokō ʻia ʻana, me ka hāʻawi ʻana iā ia i nā manaʻo hope loa i wehewehe ʻia ma kāna mokuna ma ka Composition, a no laila, ʻaʻole he wahaheʻe a ʻaʻole ʻoiaʻiʻo loa ka mea hope. ʻO ka pōʻino o ia ʻano loiloi ʻo ia ka hoʻowalewale ʻana i ka mea novice e manaʻo e hiki i nā kaʻina hana hana ke hoʻololi i ka noʻonoʻo. ʻO ka manaʻo o ke akamai ke kiaʻi i nā mea huna o kāna hola hana. ʻO nā hiʻohiʻona i loaʻa i ka hana, nā hoʻokolohua hoʻoweliweli, i mua o ka lanakila, e like me ka hana kiʻi kiʻi a Hawthorne i hōʻike ʻia mai nei i ka mālamalama, hāʻawi i ke aʻo kumu kūʻai a me ka paipai ʻana i ka mea pena kiʻi. Akā hoʻokahi ʻO ka mea nāna e hōʻike i kāna Muse i ka maka o nā mea hele mai e hoʻomanaʻo i ka hopena o King Candaules.

Ke noʻonoʻo nei ka honua iā Poe ma ke ʻano he "kanaka maikaʻi ʻole o ke akamai." Ua lohe koke au iā ia i ʻōlelo ʻia ʻo "ka mea i manaʻo ʻia e nā kānaka āpau e kuhi hewa, hoʻohewa a hōʻino paha." Akā, i koʻu manaʻo, ua pau kāna pōʻino me kona make ʻino, a mai ia manawa mai ua hoʻomau ʻia kāna pale ʻana, a ʻo kona kaulana ʻo ka ulu paʻa ʻana e like me ka mea kākau ʻeha a maikaʻi e hiki ai ke pule no kāna hola manaolana. ʻO ka ʻōlelo ʻino a me ka ʻōlelo hōʻino a Griswold i hoʻohuli i ke au i kona makemake. Ua hele mai nā mea hoʻohewa a me nā mea kākau moʻolelo me nā hoʻopiʻi kūleʻa, me ka mahalo i kona ʻano, me nā paʻi hou o kāna mau hana. Aia i mua o mākou kāna mau leka ponoʻī a me nā minuke o kāna ʻoihana; ua ike nui ia ka moolelo, he maikai a he ino. ʻAʻohe mea hoʻopiʻi i loaʻa i ka manaʻo palupalu a kala. ʻO kāna mau pōʻino i ke ola no kona ʻāina a me kona manawa, ʻoi aku paha ma muli o kona nāwaliwali ponoʻī. ʻO ke kanalua ʻaʻole ʻo kona wahi kaiapuni kahi e mālama ai i kahi ʻano maikaʻi a maikaʻi ʻole mai nā pilikia i waho a i loko. ʻO kona manaʻo ikaika loa, e lilo i haku nona iho, ua loaʻa iā ia ka "hoʻoilina o ka ʻino." Ua haʻi ʻo ia iā ia iho ʻo ia ka haku hauʻoli ʻole o ka manu, ka mea i ʻeha i kēia mele. Akā ʻo kāna ʻāpana piha o kēlā huhū nui e hauʻoli nei i ka presage o kona make ponoʻī. He leʻaleʻa i ka pāʻani ʻana i ke ʻano kiʻekiʻe: he poʻe koa mākou a pau, e uē ana iā Ave Imperator! No ka haʻi ʻana i ka mea ʻoiaʻiʻo a Burke: "Ma ke kaumaha ka leʻaleʻa i luna, a ʻo ka ʻeha a mākou e ʻeha nei, ʻaʻole like ia me ka ʻeha loa, he mea ʻino mau, a ke hoʻoikaika nei mākou e hoʻoluliluli i ka wā hiki." Ua hele ʻoi aku ʻo Poe, a he mea pena i loko o ka pōʻino o kāna ʻoihana. Inā, e like me kona manaʻoʻiʻo, ʻo ke kaumaha a me ka nalo ʻana o ka nani nā poʻomanaʻo mele kiʻekiʻe loa, a ʻo ka manaʻo mele i ka leʻaleʻa honua ʻoi loa, a laila ʻo ke kaumaha a me ka pōʻeleʻele o kona ola haʻihaʻi ʻaʻole i nele i kā lākou uku pinepine.

Ma nā ʻaoʻao aʻe, loaʻa iā mākou kahi hiʻohiʻona hou o ke akamai o kahi mea pena e hōʻike ana i kāna wehewehe ʻana i kahi mele kaulana. ʻO Gustave Doré, ka hana hope loa o kāna penikala i mua o mākou, ʻaʻole ia ka mea pena, a i ʻole ka mea pena kiʻi, no ka poʻe ʻike maoli e koi ana i ka ʻoiaʻiʻo o ka leo, kiʻi, a me ka hemolele. ʻO ia mau mea e pili ana iā ia ma mua o ka hana ʻana i ke ʻano a me ka mamao, ka mālamalama a me ka malu, e kōkua i kāna kumu, - ʻo ia ka mea e hoʻoulu ai i ka ʻuhane, ka noʻonoʻo, o ka mea nānā. Ua hana ʻo ia i kēia ma ka hoʻāla ʻana i ko mākou manaʻo hopohopo, ma o nā manaʻo kupanaha a haʻahaʻa pinepine i nā mea hiki ʻole ke haʻi ʻia a piha i ka pouli a nani. Ua ʻōlelo maikaʻi ʻia inā ʻaʻole kāna mau hana kiʻi kiʻi maikaʻi, ʻoiai nā kiʻi maikaʻi loa. Ma ke ʻano he "mea kākau moʻokalaleo," a ʻo ia hoʻi, ua pili pololei kona ikaika me ka mea i haku ʻia e kāna mea kākau, me nā manaʻo wiwo ʻole o ka ʻuhane i hoʻā aʻe iā ia iho. No laila kona lanakila ʻana me Rabelais, me "Le Juif-Errant," "Les Contes Drolatiques," a me "Don Quixote," a no laila, ʻaʻole i hōʻike i ka nani o Tennyson's Idyls, o "Il Paradiso," o nā kahu hipa Hebera, a me nā palapala ʻē aʻe e koi ana i ka hoʻokiʻekiʻe, a me ka ʻono a me ka hoʻomaha. He haku hānau ʻo ia o ka grotesque, a ma kahi ʻike kūikawā hiki ke hōʻike i nā hiʻohiʻona o ka lolo haunted. ʻIke mākou i nā mea e like me kona ʻike ʻana iā lākou, a me nā maka ponoʻī o ka Iudaio Wandering, ʻo Don piʻoloke, a i ʻole ke kaikamahine a ka mea hana gula i hoʻonani ʻia i ka Mōʻī ma ka hale kūʻai ma ka Pont-au-Change. ʻO ia ke ʻano o nā mea e pono ai ʻo ia e hoʻokipa i kēlā me kēia haku o ka pauku a i ʻole ka moʻolelo aʻu i kapa ai he ʻokoʻa. Ua kahea ia na aupuni haahaa i kana oihana; ʻO kona mau pōhaku, nā lāʻau a me nā mauna, ʻo ka lani ponoʻī, he animate me ka motive a me ka diablerie. Ina ua ola oia e hoike ia Shakespeare, ina ua ike kakou i ka hana kupanaha o Caliban, na Witches, ka ino ma "Lear"; akā naʻe, ua nīnau ʻo ia i kāna mau manaʻo no Imogen a i ʻole Miranda. ʻO ka nani maʻemaʻe a me ka maʻalahi, a me ka maikaʻi maikaʻi o ia mea, ʻaʻole ʻo ia i hoʻomaopopo.

Eia nō naʻe, aia ka nani i kāna mau hoʻolālā no ka "Ancient Mariner," ʻaʻole maoli e like me lākou, a ʻaʻole i loaʻa kahi ʻano like ʻole i kahi moʻolelo na Doré. Hāʻawi ka Rime iā ia i kahi moʻolelo lōʻihi, me nā loli he nui o ke ʻano. Ma The Raven leo a me ka waihoʻoluʻu e mālama i kā lākou monotone a ʻaʻohe o mākou hoʻololi i kahi a i ʻole manawa. He aha ka hopena? Hāʻawi ʻo Doré i nā ʻano like ʻole i ke kumumanaʻo e like me kāna i hāpai ʻia ai, "ka enigma o ka make a me ka hallucination o kahi ʻuhane ʻole." Ma kekahi o keia mau kii, ua akaka kona mau hemahema; hōʻike nā mea ʻē aʻe i kona ʻano kumu ikaika, a me nā ʻano maikaʻi loa i kū hoʻokahi ai, ma ke ʻano he mea pena kiʻi. He mea maʻamau ma waena o nā ʻano hana o Poe a me Doré. E ʻike maopopo ʻia kēia inā hoʻāʻo ka hope i kona lima ma luna o "Tales of the Grotesque and Arabesque." Ua hele pinepine lāua i ka ʻāina elf o ka noʻonoʻo a me ke aloha. Ma nā mele melodramatic, ua pale lāua ʻelua, ma o kā lāua kauoha ʻana i ke kupua, i ka laina pilikia ma waena o ka mea kūpono a me ka mea lapuwale. O Poe ka haipule oiaio o ka Nani; ʻO kona aloha no ia mea he kuko hoʻolaʻa, a ma laila ʻo ia i hui pū ai me kāna mea kākau kiʻi. ʻO ka Poet a i ʻole ka mea pena, ʻo ka make i ka hope e hoʻololi i nā mea a pau: i loko o ke aka o kāna harbinger sable, ua hoʻonohonoho pono ka penikala hōʻailona a Vedder iā lākou he alo a he alo, akā hoʻopili iā lākou me ka ʻaʻahu o ka naʻauao a me ka mana make ʻole. Ua hana kekahi wahine ʻAmelika i ke kiʻi o kahi Genius maka hōkū me ka lama hope, ʻo ke ʻano nani o ka mea nona ka ʻike maka, akā ʻaʻole hiki i kona mau lehelehe ke hoʻopuka i nā mea pohihihi o kahi ʻāina ma waho o "ka puka o ka lua kupapaʻu kaʻao."

Edmund C. Stedman.

Kiʻi 1


KA KORAKA.

I kekahi manawa i ke aumoe me ka luuluu, i ko'u noonoo ana, nawaliwali a luhi.
 Ma luna o ka nui o ka puʻuwai a me ka pīhoihoi o ka poina lore,
 Ia'u e kunou ana, kokoke e hiamoe, hikiwawe mai la ke kani ana,
 E like me ke kani mālie ʻana o kekahi, e kīkī ana ma ka puka o koʻu keʻena.
 "ʻO kahi malihini kipa," i ʻōhumu aku ai au, "e paʻi ana i ka puka o koʻu keʻena—
 ʻO kēia wale nō, ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe. "
ʻAe, ke hoʻomanaʻo nei au i ka pō ʻino o Dekemaba,
 A ʻo kēlā me kēia ember make kaʻawale i hana i kona ʻuhane ma luna o ka papahele.
 Ua makemake au i ka lā ʻapōpō:—makemake wau i ʻimi e ʻaiʻē
 Mai kaʻu mau puke i hoʻopau i ke kaumaha - kaumaha no Lenore nalowale -
 No ke kaikamahine liʻiliʻi a nani hoʻi i kapa ʻia e nā ʻānela ʻo Lenore—
 He inoa ole maanei no ka wa pau ole.
A ʻo ka ʻenehe silika kaumaha ʻole o kēlā me kēia pale poni
 Hoʻohauʻoli iaʻu - hoʻopiha iaʻu i nā mea weliweli weliweli ʻaʻole i ʻike mua ʻia;
 No laila, i kēia manawa, no ka hoʻomaha ʻana o ka puʻuwai o koʻu naʻau, ua kū hou wau
 "ʻO T kekahi malihini e noi ana i ke komo ʻana ma ka puka o koʻu keʻena
 Ke komo nei kekahi malihini kipa ma ka puka o ko'u keena;—
 ʻO kēia, ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe. "
I kēia manawa ua ikaika koʻu ʻuhane; kanalua ole,
 "E ka haku," wahi a'u, "a e Madam, ke noi aku nei au i kou kala mai;
 Akā ʻo ka mea ʻoiaʻiʻo e hiamoe ana wau, a no laila ua hele mālie ʻoe i ka rap,
 A hele mai ʻoe me ka nawaliwali, e paʻi ana i ka puka o koʻu keʻena,
 Aole au i maopopo ua lohe au ia oe"—eia au i wehe nui aku ai i ka puka;—
 ʻO ka pouli ma laila, ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe.
I loko o kēlā pōʻeleʻele e nānā ana, lōʻihi koʻu kū ʻana ma laila me ka noʻonoʻo, hopohopo,
 Ke kanalua, moeʻuhane, ʻaʻohe kanaka i ʻaʻa e moeʻuhane ma mua;
 Akā, ʻaʻole i hoʻopau ʻia ka hamau, ʻaʻole i hōʻike mai ka pouli.
 A ʻo ka huaʻōlelo wale nō i ʻōlelo ʻia ma laila ʻo ka ʻōlelo hawanawana, "Lenore!"
 ʻO kēia kaʻu i hāwanawana ai, a ua ʻōhumu ka leo i ka huaʻōlelo, "Lenore!"
 ʻO kēia wale nō a ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe.
E hoʻi hou i loko o ke keʻena, e ʻaʻā ana koʻu ʻuhane a pau i loko oʻu,
 ʻAʻole i liʻuliʻu, lohe hou au i ka paʻi ʻana, ʻoi aʻe ka leo ma mua.
 "ʻOiaʻiʻo," wahi aʻu, "ʻoiaʻiʻo he mea ia ma koʻu puka makani;
 E ʻae mai iaʻu e ʻike i ke ʻano o ia mea, a me kēia mea pohihihi e ʻimi—
 E noho mālie koʻu puʻuwai a ʻimi kēia mea pohihihi;—
 'O ka makani 'a'ohe mea e ae!"
Wehe maʻaneʻi ua hoʻolei au i ka pani, i ka wā, me ka nui o ka lele a me ka lele.
 Ma laila i kuʻe aku ai kahi Koraka nani o nā lā haipule o ka wā kahiko.
 ʻAʻole ʻo ia i hoʻomaikaʻi iki; ʻaʻole hoʻokahi minute i kū a noho ʻo ia;
 Aka, me kuu haku a lede paha, e kau ana maluna o ka puka o ko'u keena—
 Kau ʻia ma luna o ka ʻūhā o Pallas ma luna pono o koʻu puka keʻena—
 Kau, a noho, ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe.
A laila hoʻopunipuni kēia manu ʻeboni i koʻu manaʻo kaumaha i ka ʻakaʻaka.
 Ma ka nani a me ke kupapaʻu o kona helehelena,
 "Inā i ʻako ʻia a kahi ʻia kou ʻāʻī, ʻo ʻoe," wahi aʻu, "ʻaʻole ʻoe he makemake.
 ʻO Raven weliweli a kahiko e auwana ana mai ke kahakai ʻo Nightly,—
 E haʻi mai ʻoe iaʻu ʻo wai kou inoa haku ma kahakai ʻo Plutonian Night!"
 Quoth the Raven, "Aole loa."
Mahalo nui wau i kēia moa ʻino i ka lohe ʻana i ka ʻōlelo akaka loa.
 ʻOiai he liʻiliʻi ka manaʻo o kāna pane - ʻaʻohe mea kūpono;
 No ka mea, ʻaʻole hiki iā mākou ke ʻae aku ʻaʻohe kanaka ola
 Ua hoʻopōmaikaʻi ʻia i ka ʻike ʻana i ka manu ma luna o ka puka o kona keʻena—
 ʻO ka manu a i ʻole ka holoholona ma luna o ka ʻūhā i kālai ʻia ma luna o kona puka keʻena,
 Me ka inoa e like me "Nevermore."
Akā, ʻo ka Raven, e noho mehameha ana ma luna o ka puʻu palaka, ʻōlelo wale nō
 ʻO kēlā ʻōlelo hoʻokahi, me he mea lā i loko o kēlā huaʻōlelo āna i hoʻopuka ai.
 ʻAʻohe mea ʻē aʻe āna i ʻōlelo ai—ʻaʻole he hulu, a laila lele ʻo ia—
 A hiki i koʻu ʻōlelo ʻana, "Ua lele mua nā hoaaloha ʻē aʻe—
 I ka lā aʻe e haʻalele ʻo ia iaʻu, e like me koʻu manaʻolana ma mua."
 A laila, ʻōlelo ka manu, "ʻAʻole loa."
Pihoihoi i ka noho malie i haʻi ʻia e ka pane pololei ʻana.
 "He kanalua ole," wahi a'u, "o kana mea i olelo mai ai, oia wale no kana waiwai a me ka halekuai.
 Loaʻa ʻia mai kekahi haku hauʻoli nona ke aloha ʻole
 Hahai wikiwiki a hahai wikiwiki a hiki i kāna mau mele hoʻokahi kaumaha—
 A hiki i ka leo kanikau o kona Manaolana i kaumaha ai ka haawe melancholy
 ʻO 'Aʻole—ʻaʻole loa.'"
Akā, e hoʻopunipuni ana ka Raven i koʻu ʻuhane kaumaha a pau i ka ʻakaʻaka,
 Ua huila pololei au i kahi noho cushioned i mua o ka manu a me ka ʻūhā a me ka puka;
 A laila, i ka hāʻule ʻana o ka velvet, hoʻomaka wau e hoʻopili
 Manaʻo i ka manaʻo, e noʻonoʻo ana i kēia manu ʻino o ka wā kahiko—
 He aha kēia manu ʻino, ʻinoʻino, ʻinoʻino, ʻeleʻele a weliweli o ka wā kahiko
 Mean in croaking "Nevermore."
ʻO kēia kaʻu i noho ai me ka noʻonoʻo ʻana, akā ʻaʻohe syllable hōʻike
 I ka moa nona na maka ahi e aa mai ana iloko o ko'u poli;
 ʻO kēia a me nā mea hou aʻe aʻu i noho ai e kilokilo, me koʻu poʻo e noho mālie
 Ma luna o ka ʻili ʻeleʻele o ka cushion i ʻōlinolino ai ke kukui,
 Akā, nona ka velvet violet lining me ke kukui e ʻā ana
 E kaomi ʻo ia , ʻaʻole loa!
A laila, manaʻo ʻia, ua māhuahua aʻe ka ea, ua ʻala ʻia mai kahi mea ʻala ʻike ʻole ʻia
 Koli ʻia e nā seraphim nona ka hāʻule wāwae ma luna o ka papahele ʻulaʻula.
 "Auwe," i kahea aku ai au, "ua haawi mai kou Akua ia oe—ma keia mau anela i hoouna mai ai oia ia oe.
 Hoʻomaha—hoʻomaha a nepenthe mai kou hoʻomanaʻo ʻana iā Lenore!
 Quaff, oh quaff this kind nepenthe, a poina i keia nalowale Lenore!"
 Quoth the Raven, "Aole loa."
"Kaula!" wahi a'u, "he mea ino!—ke kaula, ina he manu a he diabolo paha!—
 Ina paha i hoounaia e ka Hoowalewale, a ina paha i hoolei mai ka ino ia oe i uka.
 Ua neoneo nae ka makau ole, ma keia aina waonahele, ua hookamaniia—
 Ma keia home e ka Horror haunted—e hai mai oe ia'u, ke noi aku nei au—
 Aia anei—he balsama anei ma Gileada?—e hai mai—e hai mai ia'u, ke noi aku nei au!"
 Quoth the Raven, "Aole loa."
"Kaula!" ʻĪ akula au, "He mea ʻino—ke kāula, inā he manu a he diabolo paha!
 Ma ka lani e piʻo mai nei, ʻo kākou—ma ia Akua kākou e hoʻomana nei—
 E haʻi aku i kēia ʻuhane me ke kaumaha kaumaha inā, i loko o Aidenn mamao,
 E puliki ia i ke kaikamahine hemolele i kapaia e na anela o Lenore—
 E hoʻopaʻa i kahi kaikamahine liʻiliʻi a nani hoʻi i kapa ʻia e nā ʻānela ʻo Lenore.
 Quoth the Raven, "Aole loa."
"E lilo kēlā huaʻōlelo i hōʻailona o ka hemo ʻana, manu a i ʻole fiend!" Ua hoʻōho au, e hoʻomaka ana—
 "E hoʻi ʻoe i loko o ka ʻino a me ke kahakai ʻo Plutonian Night!
 Mai waiho ʻoe i ka hulu ʻeleʻele i hōʻailona o kēlā wahaheʻe a kou ʻuhane i ʻōlelo ai!
 E haʻalele i koʻu mehameha!—e haʻalele i ka ʻūhā ma luna o koʻu puka!
 E lawe oe i kou nuku mai ko'u naau aku, a e lawe i kou ano mai ko'u puka aku."
 Quoth the Raven, "Aole loa."
A ʻo ka Raven, ʻaʻole lele, ke noho nei, noho nō
 Ma ka ʻūhā pallid o Pallas ma luna pono o koʻu puka keʻena;
 A ua like kona mau maka me ko ka daimonio e moeuhane ana.
 A ʻo ke kukui o luna ona e hoʻolei ana i kona aka i ka papahele;
 A ʻo koʻu ʻuhane mai kēlā aka e lana ana ma ka papahele
 E hāpai ʻia—ʻaʻole loa!
