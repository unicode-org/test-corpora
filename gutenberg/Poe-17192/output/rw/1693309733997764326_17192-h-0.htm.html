IGITEKEREZO KU CYivugo.

Ibanga ryigisigo, kitari munsi yiterambere ryurwenya, kiri mumatwi yuwumva. Iyemeze kuraguza, wemere umusizi: ibi, nyuma ya byose, nibyo abanyabwenge basubiza iyo ubabajije agaciro kacyo. Nubwo umusizi ubwe, mubindi byiyumvo bye, akubwira ko ibihangano bye ariko byoroshye amaboko, ibiryo byabashitsi, akakwemerera kukwereka amayeri yabyo, - ntukamwemere. Tegereza isaha ye yo guhanura; noneho witange kubyifuzo bye, umunezero cyangwa ububabare. "Turi mu maboko y'urukundo uyu munsi!" aririmba Gautier, mu magambo ahinnye ya Swinburne, -kandi kuva mu gitondo kugeza izuba rirenze turazunguruka ku nyanja ikaze: hariho urukundo rumwe, Gicurasi, umurongo umwe w’indabyo. Urukundo ruhoraho, ibindi byose ntibishoboka kandi bishyize kuruhande. Iyerekwa rifite iherezo, ibiboneka birahinduka; ariko hari icyo twungutse, kwibuka igikundiro. Nkabasizi benshi, igikundiro cyinshi. Hano hari igikundiro cya Evanescence, itanga ubwiza buhebuje nubuntu aureole ya Pathos. Sangira na Landor "ijoro ryo kwibuka no kwishongora" kuri Rose Aylmer, kandi ufite ibi byuzuye.

Noneho fata ukuboko k'umucuranzi mushya w'isi, wateshutse aho utuye ukagera muri Amerika itagira ikinyabupfura kandi idahwitse nk'uko Baudelaire yabibonye, ​​"yari iya Poe gereza nini yanyuzemo, aha n'aha, hamwe n'umuvuduko ukabije w'ikiremwa cyaremwe kugira ngo uhumeke mu isi itanduye," kandi aho "ubuzima bwe bw'imbere, bwo mu mwuka nk'umusizi, mu mwuka ndetse no mu mwuka nk'umusizi, mu mwuka. ikirere kirwanya antipathetique. " Kata ukuboko kwunvikana k'umuririmbyi ufite ibibazo arimo kurira bityo adasanzwe, kandi musangire nawe ibihe yasanze, - igihe cyose umunsi wose, buri gihe nijoro, - niba atari Atlantis aho yari yarazerereye, byibuze ahantu h'ubuhungiro ku mipaka aho yinjizaga.

Mu gihugu kimwe gusa afite imbaraga zo kukuyobora, kandi ijoro rimwe gusa ushobora gusangira inzozi ze. Agace k'isi cyangwa Ijuru: "Nta-butaka-muntu," bivuye mu kirere, mu gihe. Dore abahagaritse umutima, binyuze mumaso yabo, nkaya Cenci, roho ibona amadirishya nubwo ubwenge bwasaze; hano imyuka, ifata inzira iganisha Iteka, irahagarara kandi iratinda. Nibisambo by "ubugingo bwimibumbe," aho usanga ukwezi kutamenyekana, urukundo rwatakaye. Hano bamwe bakosowe mubitekerezo, ikiruhuko cyonyine cyagerwaho; abandi

"genda mu buryo butangaje
 Ku ndirimbo idahuye: "

mugihe hose

"Urupapuro rwibutsa ibyahise -
 Impuzu zitwikiriye zitangira kandi zishongora
 Mugihe banyuze inzererezi. "

Ngiyo igihugu, kandi ijoro rimwe turaryinjiramo, -ijoro ryibice bya astral na chimes zisubiramo. Monodies zayo ni ibisigo cumi na bibiri, umuziki uharanira guhinduka nyamara burigihe ni kimwe. Umwe umwe umwe bumvikana, nko gutontoma kw'isaha ya brazen na ebony, muri "Masque y'urupfu rutukura," yatumye waltzers ihagarara hamwe "kutanyurwa no guhinda umushyitsi no gutekereza", igihe cyose isaha yazengurukaga.

Muri aba kadadeni bose b'amayobera, ikibaya cy'igikona , kinyeganyeza binyuze kumurongo, ahanini cyashimishije isi. Ni ibihe bintu bijya mu gukora igisigo, -kandi ni ukuri muri ibi, kimwe no mu bindi byinshi, iryo siganwa ryitaga bard "ababikora"? Igikorwa cyahamagariwe kuva mu cyuho. Ahatariho ikintu, gisigaye, - icyaremwe gishya, igice cyubutunzi bwabantu. Kandi amagambo make adasanzwe, kurenza ayandi arema kimwe, aduhatira gutekereza bundi bushya ubutwari ikaramu yumusizi ihindura ibintu bitazwi

"ku miterere, kandi nta kintu ihumeka
 Ubuturo bwaho, n'izina. "

Buri kimwe gisa na prototype, nyamara byose biradushimisha nibintu byakuwe mu gicucu cyindengakamere. Noneho igitekerezo cyo hejuru kireba ubugingo bwibintu; irashobora cyangwa ntishobora gutera Fantasy abantu bafite amashusho interlunar idasobanutse. Nubwo bimeze bityo ariko, imwe muri aya magambo, muburyo bwayo buto, iratugiraho ingaruka no kumva ko idasanzwe, nkukuri nkibikorwa bya sublimer byabakinnyi ndengakamere, -Marlowe "Faustus", "Faust" ya Goethe, "Manfred," cyangwa ndetse nibi bihangano bya ethereal, "The Tempest" na "Inzozi za Midsummer Night." Birenze umwe, mugihe ubundi bidasanzwe, bifite bimwe umutwaro cyangwa wirinde guhiga kwibuka, -kumva, ntiwibagirwe, nkijwi ryikoreshwa gake ariko ryihariye-guhagarara. Ikigaragara muri bo harimo "Lenore" ya Bürger, iyo ballade yizimu kandi yumvikana, umutego numwuka wabasemuzi. Bake bazahakana ko Coleridge itangaje "Rime of the Mariner Mariner" ihagaze kumutwe wabo. "Le Juif-Errant" yaba afite ibyo avuga, iyaba Beranger yari umusizi ukomeye; kandi, ariko kubera kure yabo yimpuhwe zizwi, "Umukecuru wa Shalott" na "Blessed Damozel" barashobora kongerwaho kurutonde. Yahawe Edgar Allan Poe kugirango akore amagambo abiri, "Inzogera" na Igikona , buri imwe muri zo, nubwo wenda ifite ubwiza buke ugereranije n'iya Tennyson na Rossetti, irihariye. "Ulalume," nubwo bidasanzwe kandi bitekereza, ntabwo bifite ireme rusange mubice bigize ikizamini cyacu.

Igikona mu bice by'imivugo bigwa munsi y'ibice nka "Ingoro ihiga," "Umujyi wo mu nyanja," "Umusinzira," na "Israfel." Byose byahindurwa, ndakeka, nabasomyi bumukinnyi wihuta, kubice nkibi:

"Hirya no hino, mu kuzamura umuyaga wibagiwe,
 Yeguye munsi yijuru
 Amazi meza arabeshya.
Nta mirase iva mwijuru ryera
 Mu ijoro rirerire ry'uwo mujyi;
 Ariko urumuri ruva mu nyanja ya lurid
 Kuzamuka hejuru ya bucece bucece -
 · · · · · · · ·
 Hejuru na benshi murusengero rwiza
 Ninde wreathéd frezes intertwine
 Inanga, inanga, n'umuzabibu.
 · · · · · · · ·
 Nta kubyimba bivuga ko umuyaga ushobora kuba
 Ku nyanja ya kure yishimye -
 Nta buremere bwerekana ko umuyaga wabaye
 Ku nyanja nta mutuzo uhishe. "

Ntibuze injyana yindege yumusizi ufite imitima yumutima ni lute:

"Kandi baravuga (korari yinyenyeri
 Ibindi byo gutega amatwi)
 Uwo muriro wa Isiraheli
 Ni ukubera iyo ndirimbo
 Iyo yicaye akanaririmba -
 Umugozi muzima uhinda umushyitsi
 Muri iyo migozi idasanzwe. "

Ariko igikona , nka "Inzogera" na "Annabel Lee," kirashimira benshi na bake. Ahandi navuze ko ibihangano bya Poe bidasanzwe kuri njye nabonaga "aho usanga harimo isura, byibura, yo kwizana, - aho yiyemerera ibyiyumvo bye, mugihe apfa kugwa na cadence umuziki cyane, wishimye cyane, uturuka kuri we atabizi." Ibi biracyari imyizerere yanjye; kandi nyamara, ku bushakashatsi bushya bwiki gisigo, birantangaza kuruta ikindi gihe cyose kuva nkiri muto. Kumenyana hafi bivugako ibikorwa byose byubuhanzi. Shishikariza umugabo, utazi ibihangano cyangwa ngo abitayeho, asuzume igisigo cyangwa igishushanyo, nuburyo imbaraga zacyo zimufata vuba! Mubyukuri, azagereranya agaciro kagereranijwe kumurimo wambere mwiza yakunzwe cyane. Igikona , nacyo, gifite imico ihamye niyo umuhanga agomba kwishimira. Ntayindi mivugo yumwanditsi wayo niyo mpamvu isobanuwe neza. "Ingoro ihiga" irasobanutse neza kubasomyi batoranijwe, ariko Poe birashoboka ko yaba yarafashe iyo mvugo yoroheje yo gusesengura uruhara. Igikona kirimo rwose insanganyamatsiko yumwanditsi - igihombo kidasubirwaho cyumugore usenga ibigirwamana kandi mwiza; ariko ku zindi mpamvu, nanone, instinzi ya rubanda nukuri mugutekereza ko ari igisigo cye.

Umugabo wumuhanga mubusanzwe yunguka intsinzi hamwe nimbaraga zimwe, kandi ntabwo buri gihe arikomeye. Kumenyekana nuburyo bwihuse bwo gusubikwa. Ntabwo ayigura, nkamahirwe yumubabaro, igiceri nyuma yigiceri, ariko "ntabwo aribyose cyangwa byose muri byose." Kandi rero hamwe nibindi byifuzo: umunyacyubahiro, umusirikare, umukinnyi, - ibice byabo byose, - buriwese abara intsinzi ye kubwamahirwe amwe. Igikona cya Poe, nubwo cyinshi, kuri we "inyoni yatumaga umuyaga uhuha." Umusizi yatuye i New-York, mu itumba ryo mu 1844 -45, abona akazi ku rupapuro rwa Willis, "Umugoroba w’umugoroba," maze ashakisha amafaranga yinjije mu misanzu ahandi. Amaze imyaka itandatu yari umwanditsi ukora, kandi yari azwiho ubuhanga; yafatwaga mu cyubahiro no gushidikanya, kandi nta gihombo yari afite kubera uruhare rwe mu itangazamakuru ridahembwa uwo munsi. Yari yaranakoze byinshi mu bikorwa bye byiza cyane, - imigani nka "Ligeia" na "Kugwa kwa Inzu ya Usher," (icya nyuma kirimo uwo mugenzi we w'amayobera, ku murongo wa Elihu Vedder "Ubwenge bwazimiye,") ibikorwa by'isesengura nka "Zahabu ya Bug" na "Amayobera ya Marie Roget." Yari yarahinduye abayoboke mu mahanga, kandi yigarurira ubwenge bw'Abafaransa. Yari yarize imbaraga n'intege nke ze, kandi yari ku isonga rye, kandi ntabwo yari afite izina runaka. Ariko nta kintu yari yanditse cyari ku rurimi rwa buri wese. Kubikorwa bidasanzwe kandi byoroshye gukoraho gukundwa bigomba kongerwaho kugirango ufate abumva muri rusange igihe cyabo.

Binyuze mu nganda z’abanditsi bakurikirana ubuzima bwa Poe, ibyakozwe na Raveni byabaye inkuru ikunze kuvugwa. Umugore ukiri muto w’umusizi, Virijiniya, yazimye imbere ye, ariko amara undi mwaka mu gicucu cyurupfu. Icyumba kirekire, gito mucyumba hafi yumuhanda wa Bloomingdale ni nk uzwi nk'icyumba Rouget de l'Isle yahimbye Marseillaise. Bose bumvise ko igisigo cyashyizweho umukono "Quarles," cyagaragaye muri "American Review", gifite ibisobanuro byanditse kuri pseudo-editor; ko Poe yakiriye amadorari icumi kuri yo; ko Willis, umugwaneza kandi utagirira ishyari abakemurampaka berekana imideli, yongeye kubisubiramo hamwe na eulogy yahise ibigira umujyi. Gushidikanya ku bwanditsi bwayo byavanyweho igihe Poe yabisomaga ubwe mu giterane cy’abanditsi, kandi mu gihe runaka ni we wabaye ikirangirire mu banditsi b'Abanyamerika. Gukubita byamuteye inkunga kandi bimutera inkunga. Kimwe n'undi muntu usebanya kandi wishimye, na we yasanze "ikintu cy'impeshyi" ndetse "muri hum y'udukoko." Birababaje bihagije, ariko hashize imyaka itatu, -igihe cyingaruka, ubwibone, umubabaro, nyamara buri gihe cyimirimo itekereza cyangwa ikomeye, - mbere yo gutsindwa bwa nyuma, mbere yuko umwenda utangira ubuzima ko kuri we byari amahano, maze akemera "Inzoka Yatsinze."

"The American Review: A Whig Journal" cyari ikinyamakuru cyizewe muri kiriya gihe, gifite inkingi ebyiri, cyanditswe ku mpapuro nziza gifite ubwoko busobanutse, kandi cyerekanwa n'amashusho ya mezzotint. Hagati yibintu byinshi biri munsi yubu, byari bikubiyemo bimwe umwanditsi wese yakwishimira kwakira. Igitabo cya mbere, cyo mu 1845, gifite ingingo za Horace Greeley, Donald Mitchell, Walter Whitman, Marsh, Tuckerman, na Whipple. Igisigo cya Ralph Hoyt, "Kera", cyagaragaye muri iki gitabo. Kandi hano hari amagambo atatu ya Poe: "Umujyi mu nyanja," "Ikibaya cy'imvururu," n'igikona . Babiri muri bo barubatswe, -kuko yari inzira ye, -hereye kubushakashatsi bwambere, ariko izina ryanyuma ryasohotse nkaho ryahimbwe vuba, kandi hafi nkuko tubifite ubu. Amagambo avuga ko atavuguruwe nyuma arikosa. Impinduka 11 zidasanzwe ziva mu kinyamakuru-inyandiko zigaragara mu gikona no mu bindi bisigo , 1845, igitabo umusizi yahise yumva ashishikajwe no gutanga rubanda. Izi nizo mpinduka cyane kuruhuka, cyangwa yamagambo amwe, ubwoko bwa nyuma bwakozwe kugirango uzamure ingaruka za alliteration. Mu gitabo cyiza cya Bwana Lang cy’umurongo wa Poe, cyazanywe mu "Isomero ry’impu," yerekanye ubushake bw’intiti, kandi yakoze neza, mu gusubira mu nyandiko iri mu gitabo kimaze kuvugwa, nk'uko byatanzwe mu nomero y'i Londres yo mu 1846. Icyegeranyo "gisanzwe" Griswold cy’ibikorwa by’umusizi cyuzuyemo amakosa. Ibi byagarutsweho nabanditsi nyuma, nabo bakoze amakosa yabo. Ariko inyandiko y'Igikona , bitewe n'ibyifuzo byasabwe n'umwanditsi kuri kopi yandikishijwe intoki, yari akivugurura cyane; mubyukuri, yabicapiye i Richmond, mbere gato y'urupfu rwe, asimbuza ibisigo "serafimu waguye ikirenge" ngo "abamarayika bafite ibirenge byacitse intege," mu murongo wa cumi na kane. Inyandiko yacu y'ubu rero, nubwo, cyane cyane iy'umwaka wa 1845, yahinduwe mu buryo runaka n’umusizi nyuma yo gusoma, kandi ndatekereza ko ari verisiyo yukuri kandi ifatika y’iki gisigo kimwe. Impinduka zikomeye cyane kuva verisiyo yambere yagaragaye, ariko, mubunini muri 1845; umurongo wa cumi na rimwe ubanza urimo iyi mirongo, ikosa mu njyana naho ubundi inenge ku gisigo:

"Yafashwe na shobuja utishimye, we Impanuka itagira impuhwe
 Yakurikiranye byihuse kandi akurikira byihuse - bityo, iyo Byiringiro yakwemeza,
 Stern Kwiheba yagarutse, aho kuba Ibyiringiro byiza yatinyutse kubyemeza -
 Igisubizo kibabaje, 'Nta na rimwe!' "

Byaba byiza abandi, kandi bazwi, abasizi bashoboraga kumenya neza ko impinduka zabo zihora zitera imbere. Poe yahoraga asubiramo imirongo ye yerekana umurongo, kandi ubusanzwe yarayitezimbere. Igikona nicyo cyambere mubisigo bike yari hafi kurangiza mbere yo gucapa. Birashoboka ko abita ku mivugo babuze bike kubera urupfu rwe. Kuba azi kuvuga neza, ntabwo yigeze yandika umurongo agamije gukora igisigo. Iyo kwanga ishusho byamuhigaga, amagambo yavuyemo ni ihumure, nkuko we ubwe yabivuze, ku bushake, ntabwo ari intego. Ibi byari umwanya muto cyane kuburyo byasobanuraga ko Fairfield yerekana ko buriwese yari umusaruro wibibazo byubwoba.

None se, ni iki cyahaye umusizi ibisobanuro ku Gikona ? Nuwuhe musingi utari mwiza wazamutse gahoro gahoro umuziki uhumeka buhoro? Nkibisanzwe, ibintu birenze kimwe byagiye mukubaka igisigo kizwi cyane. Urebye inyigisho ndende zikunze kubwirizwa ku masomo magufi kandi adatanga ibitekerezo, ndizera ko ntazaryozwa iki kiganiro cy'indirimbo imwe, - cyane cyane imwe umuhanzi nka Doré yatanze ingingo yo gushushanya. Kugeza vuba aha natekerezaga ko iki gice, na bike umwanditsi wacyo yahimbye nyuma yo kugaragara, byari bidasanzwe mu kuba bitarakuze muri mikorobe mu murongo we w'ubuhungu. Ariko Bwana Fearing Gill anyeretse imirongo itarasohoka yanditswe na Poe, yanditswe mu mwaka wa cumi n'umunani, kandi yise "Umudayimoni wumuriro." Inyandiko yandikishijwe intoki isa nkaho iri mu nyandiko y’umusizi yandikishijwe intoki, kandi ubunyangamugayo bwayo burashimangirwa n’umuryango wari umaze igice cy'ikinyejana. Usibye mikorobe zisobanutse za "Inzogera" na "Ingoro ihiga" ikubiyemo imirongo mike bimwe byerekana gufungura no gufunga igikona . Ku bijyanye n'injyana y'igisigo cyacu, kugereranya amatariki byerekana ko ibyo byatewe n'injyana ya "Urukundo rwa Lady Geraldine." Poe ni umwe mu bambere bubahirije ubuhanga bwa Miss Barrett; amwandikira ibisigo bye yakusanyije nk "" umunyacyubahiro mu gitsina cye, "kandi yari arimo impuhwe nuburyo bwe bw'indirimbo. Imirongo iva mu rukundo-igisigo cye,

"Hamwe no kwitotomba bitazwi, mu kirere, umwenda w'umuhengeri
 Yabyimbye kandi arabyimba hirya no hino ye yijimye. "

habonetse urusaku muri ibi:

"Kandi ubudodo bubabaje budashidikanywaho kuri buri mwenda wijimye
 Byaranshimishije - byuzuye ubwoba buteye ubwoba ntigeze numva mbere. "

Hano Poe yagize amahirwe yamaganaga Longfellow, kandi ntamuntu numwe wigeze yishakira mu nzu ye atabanje gutahura no guhanwa. Muburyo bwa melody na stanzaic, tuzareba ko ibisigo byombi bidasa, ariko kubwimpamvu biratandukanye rwose. Umusizi utanga ubuntu ntakindi yumvaga usibye umwimerere nyawo wumusizi. "Iyi nyandiko ishimishije!" Yatangaye ati: - "izo mbaraga zumvikana! ... Umusizi wacu ukomeye, Bwana Browning, umwanditsi wa 'Paracelsus,' & c., ashishikajwe no kwishimira injyana." Bwana Ingram, nyuma yo kuvuga kuri "Lady Geraldine," abigiranye ubuhanga yerekanye indi soko Poe ashobora kuba yaratewe imbaraga. Mu 1843, Albert Pike, igice cy’Abagereki, igice cy’umupaka, umusizi wa Arkansas, yari yaracapye muri "The New Mirror", Poe icyo gihe yandikaga, imirongo imwe n'imwe yise "Isadore," ariko kuva yasubirwamo n’umwanditsi akita "Umutima wapfakaye." Nahisemo mubisubiramo Bwana Pike imirongo ikurikira, muribintu nyamukuru bihuye na verisiyo yumwimerere:

"Ntaruhuka ndahuta ibyumba byacu byigunze, sinzongera gucuranga indirimbo zacu,
 Izuba ryinshi ryaka cyane hejuru yubutaka;
 Inyoni isebanya iracyicara iraririmba, yewe mubyishimo!
 Erega umutima wanjye umeze nk'igicu-gicu cyuzuyemo imvura;
 Uranyobeye ubuziraherezo, Isadore! "

Hano dufite igipimo kirekire, guhuza kwifata, no kumenyekanisha inyoni indirimbo yayo yongera intimba. Hariho izindi nzira zishobora gukurikirwa namatsiko; icyitonderwa, igice Bwana Ingram yahisemo mu isuzuma rya nyuma rya Poe rya "Barnaby Rudge":

"Igikona nacyo, * * * gishobora kuba cyarakozwe, kuruta uko tubibona ubu, igice cyo gusama kwa Barnaby fantastique.

Nubwo bimeze bityo ariko, nyuma yo kwerekana izo mikorobe nizisa, agaciro kiki gisigo kiracyaboneka mwumwimerere. Umuziki ugenda utera imbere, ibisobanuro nyaburanga kandi bitandukanye n'umucyo n'igicucu, - hejuru ya byose, ishyaka ryo mu mwuka rya nocturn, bigira umurimo wubwenge butanga amakuru. Naho inyoni iteye ubwoba, ntameze nkizindi gikona zose zo mu muryango we, kuva kuri "twa corbies" na "ibikona bitatu" bya balladiste kugeza Barnaby yavuzweho "Grip." Hano ntaho bihuriye n'inkoni ya cawing ihiga imitwe ya basekuruza kandi ikandagira umurima w'ubutumwa; nta fantom fantom ya Tickell yaririmbye ko, iyo,

"gutaka ku idirishya rye gatatu,
 Igikona gikubita ibaba,
 Nibyiza cyane inkumi yambaye urukundo yari abizi
 Ijwi rikomeye. "

Igikona cya Poe ni imyumvire itandukanye; kwigira umuntu ububabare bw'icyunamo no kwiheba; umugozi ugizwe na Memory, uhoraho amateka yamakuba, ubwoko bwa Irreparable. Yahungiye hakurya ya Styx, avuye kuri "Nijoro ya Plutoniya," asa nubugingo bwishusho bwuwabajije ubwe, -umuntu utabishoboye, ntazabikora, yanga nepenthe mwiza, kuko kwibuka Lenore aribyo byose asigaranye, kandi hamwe nububabare bwakababaro kabone niyo byaba byashyizwe kuruhande.

Igikona gishobora kandi gufatwa nkigisigo gihagarariye umwanditsi wacyo, kugirango kigaragaze ibitekerezo bye byose byerekeranye nigisigo. Ibi tubisanga mu nyandiko yanditse kuri "Ihame ry'Ubusizi," "Impamvu y'umurongo," na "Filozofiya yo guhimba." Poe yatangaje ko "muri Muzika, ahari, roho yenda kugera ku ndunduro ikomeye aho, iyo ihumekewe na Poetic Sentiment, irwana - kurema Ubwiza buhebuje .... Umurongo ntushobora kugenwa neza kuruta umuziki uri hasi cyangwa udashoboye"; ariko na none, umurongo nukuri "Imivugo yamagambo" ni "Kurema Rythmical Creation of Beauty," - ibi ntakindi. Ijwi ryubwiza buhebuje nimwe mububabare. Umubabaro mwinshi mu ngingo ni Urupfu. Ibi bigomba guhuzwa nubwiza. "Urupfu rw'umugore mwiza rero, nta gushidikanya, ni ingingo y’imivugo kurusha izindi ku isi, -kandi nta gushidikanya ko iminwa ikwiranye n'iyi ngingo ari iy'umukunzi wapfushije." Iyi mvugo yanyuma yavuzwe mu isesengura rya Poe ryasesenguye iki gisigo nyine, ariko ryerekana neza umurongo rusange wumurongo we. Indunduro ya "Inzogera" ni monotone ivanze ya ghouls, yishimira gupima umutima w'umuntu. "Lenore," Igikona , "Umusinzira," "Kuri Umwe muri paradizo," na "Ulalume" bigize simfoni ya tenebrose, - na "Annabel Lee," byanditswe nyuma ya byose, byerekana ko insanganyamatsiko imwe yamutunze kugeza imperuka. Na none, ibyo byose ntakintu niba atari umuziki, kandi bamwe bakoraho nubwiza bwa Fantastic ikangura ubwoba, kandi ikongeramo ubwoba bushya kububabare ubwabwo. Muri byose hagaragajwe neza, munsi yikicucu, urukundo rwiza rwumusizi, -kenshi bikunze kugaragazwa kimwe cya kabiri ahandi, -umugore wa Usher, umudamu Ligeia, muri ukuri mugenzi we wa kamere ye. Ndakeka ko urukundo rwumuhanzi kuri umwe "muburyo" rudashobora na rimwe guhangana rwose nubwitange bwe kubintu byiza. Umugore uri hafi ye agomba gukoresha amarozi, kuba kumurongo kandi ntakintu kirekire, kumukunda muburyo butandukanye, cyangwa kunyurwa no kureka umugabane we. Agomba gushukwa na Ntagerwaho, kandi ibi biramurenze kumukunda wo guhanga ibihangano.

Poe, kimwe na Hawthorne, yaje agabanuka kw'ishuri rya Romantike, kandi nta numwe wishimiye kuruta guseka ibyago byayo. Nyamara umutima we wari kumwe nabakundana ningaruka zabo zo muburasirazuba cyangwa Gothique. Ibyo yahimbye, bikungahaye cyane ku migani ya prose, byasaga naho byamutereranye igihe yandikaga umurongo; kandi urubanza rwe rwamubwiye ko ibisigo birebire byurukundo biterwa nibyabaye kuruta guhumekwa, - kandi ko, kuvuga ibisigo byurukundo, amagambo yaba ahagije. Niyo mpamvu, igitekerezo cye, gihuye neza n’aho ubushobozi bwe bugarukira, ko "igisigo kirekire" kivuguruzanya mu magambo. " Ibigize Igikona ni bike kandi byoroshye: umugabo, inyoni, hamwe na fantasmal yibuka kumugore. Ariko igice gitanga kwerekana neza ibintu byurukundo. Dufite iki? Mu gicuku; icyumba cyigicucu hamwe na tomes yacyo yibagiwe; umunyeshuri, - Hieronymus igezweho; igikona cy'igikona kuri casement; ijoro rya wintry n'umuriro upfa; kumanika umuyaga wumuyaga; inzozi no kutizerana bidasobanutse umwijima ugaruka; inyoni yirabura, idasanzwe kuri bust pallid; ibikoresho bya veleti ya violet n'itara ryaka. Izi ngaruka zose zerekana uko ibintu bimeze, urumuri, ibara, amajwi, ni urukundo gusa, ndetse na melodramatike, ariko ni ireme ry'ubusizi melodrama idakunze kwerekana, kandi ikagaragaza neza "umushyitsi w'iteka, ububabare bw'iteka."

Imiterere yinjyana yikona yari yizeye neza. Injyana, alliteration, umutwaro, imiterere ya stanzaic, yatunganijwe hamwe no kwiyemera. Nta gushidikanya, umusizi yatangajwe n'ubuhanga bwa muzika ya Miss Barrett, mu "munani", cyane cyane kubera gahunda yemejwe hafi yo gusoza "Lady Geraldine":

"Ati:" Amaso, ubu arimo kuntontomera! Ese uri amaso yankuyeho?
 Amaso amurika, nkibintu bya kera byashizwe mubishusho bya Parian!
 Munsi yuru ruhanga rwera rutuje, uhora utwika torrid
 O'er umusenyi-ubutayu bwumutima wanjye nubuzima bwarangiye? '"

Ubuhanzi bwe bwatangije injyana ya gatatu haba kumurongo wa kabiri nuwa kane, no kongeramo umurongo wa gatanu no kwirinda bwa nyuma, byatumye umurongo wigikona . Gukomeza kugabana bisa nkaho biza nta mbaraga, kandi akenshi injyana iri mumirongo irareshya; mugihe kwirinda cyangwa umutwaro biganje kumurimo wose. Hano kandi yari yarungukiwe nubushakashatsi bwa Miss Barrett kubyerekeranye na ballad na romaunts mu rurimi rwe no mu zindi ndimi. "Kwirinda" ni amayeri umusizi cyangwa umucuranzi afata ugutwi kuzerera, -kwifuza kenshi kwa Kamere kubibazo bya mbere. Nahoraga nishimira kwirinda kwiza kwindirimbo zicyongereza, - "The Nightingales, Nightingales," "Margret, Margret," "Umutima wanjye na njye," "Tora gahoro gahoro," "Uruzi rutemba," "Pan, Pan yarapfuye," nibindi yakoresheje kandi ibyo nise Repetend, mugukoresha Poe yarushijeho kuba mwiza abasizi bose kuva Coler

"Yemwe ibinyabuzima byishimye! Nta rurimi
 Ubwiza bwabo bushobora gutangaza:
 Isoko y'urukundo yatembye mu mutima wanjye,
 Kandi nabahaye umugisha ntabizi:
 Nukuri umutagatifu wanjye mwiza yangiriye impuhwe,
 Kandi nabahaye umugisha ntabizi. "

Poe yaremye umurongo wa gatanu wumurongo we kuburozi bwo gusubiramo. Yayishingikirije cyane mu bisigo bike nyuma, - "Lenore," "Annabel Lee," "Ulalume," na "Kuri Annie." Yabonye umuziki wo mu gasozi kandi wishimye, natekereje, nkurikije "ingaruka nziza," imitwaro nyafurika yisubiramo yaririmbwe akiri umwana, ihuza injyana yabo kavukire ijwi ryumusizi wamajyepfo.

"Filozofiya yo guhimba," isesengura rye ku Gikona , ni ugutandukanya tekiniki uburyo n'imiterere. Yaba ibihangano bye byuzuye amaraso akonje, cyangwa kuba yarakurikiranye inshuti avuga ko kwerekana iki gihangano ari ikindi kintu cyamuriganya mu bwenge, ntibikwiye gushimirwa byimazeyo. Iyaba yari yarateguye umurimo wuzuye mbere, birashoboka ko yaba yarabaye intangiriro yindirimbo zivuza injyana yindirimbo nziza yumurongo wa kabiri, - ndetse no mubitekerezo bye bya fantastique. Nibyo, umuhanzi, amaze gutunganya umurimo, abona, nkumuhanzi wambere, ko ari byiza, akabona impamvu ari nziza. Isesengura ryakurikiyeho, rifatanije no kutemera umuriro wera wose, byoroshye birashobora gukorwa. Icyo nizera nuko igitekerezo cya mbere nigishushanyo mbonera cyiki gisigo cyaje nkuko guhumeka biza; ko umwanditsi wacyo noneho yabonye uburyo bishobora gutunganywa, akabiha ibisobanuro bya nyuma byasobanuwe mu gice cye cyerekeye Ibigize, kandi ko ibya nyuma rero, atari ibinyoma rwose cyangwa ni ukuri rwose. Ingaruka zisesengura ni uko igerageza abashya gutekereza ko inzira zubukorikori zishobora gusimbuza ibitekerezo. Imbaraga zubwenge nugukingira amabanga yisaha yo guhanga. Glimpses yabonye ku murimo, ubushakashatsi bwataye umutwe, bubanziriza intsinzi, nko mu gishushanyo-mbonera cya Hawthorne giherutse gushyirwa ahagaragara, gitanga inyigisho zitagereranywa no gutera inkunga umuhanzi ubikuye ku mutima. Ariko imwe abishaka kubushake Muse we kubireba abaje bose agomba kwibuka ibyabaye kumwami Candaules.

Isi iracyatekereza kuri Poe "umuntu utagira amahirwe." Mperutse kumwumva avugwa nk "umwe abantu bose basa nkabashinzwe kuvuga nabi, kubeshya cyangwa gusebanya." Ariko kuri njye mbona amahirwe ye yarangiranye n'urupfu rwe rubabaje, kandi ko kuva icyo gihe ubwunganizi bwe bwakomeje, kandi kuba icyamamare cye cyo gukura gushikamye nk'umwanditsi ubabaye kandi ufite impano yashoboraga gusengera mu masaha ye yizeye. Griswold gucibwa no gusebanya byahinduye ikigezweho. Abakenguzamateka hamwe n'abanditsi ku mibereho yabo bazanye ibyiyumvo bikurikiranye, bubahiriza imico ye, hamwe n'ibitabo bishya by'ibikorwa bye. Amabaruwa ye bwite niminota yibyabaye mu mwuga we biri imbere yacu; inyandiko, nziza n'ibibi, irazwi cyane. Nta wajuriye yakiriye urubanza rwiza kandi rubabarira. Amakosa ye mu buzima yari ay'akarere ke n'ibihe bye, birashoboka cyane ko afite ubushake buke. Nta gushidikanya ko ibidukikije bye bitari ibyo kurinda imiterere-karemano, itaringanijwe nabi mu kaga nta n'imbere. Ubushake bwe bukomeye, bwo kuba umutware we, bwamugiriye "uwo murage w'amagorwa." Yiyemereye ko umutware w'inyoni atishimye, urwaye iki gisigo. Ariko ibye byari umugabane wuzuye kuri ubwo burakari butangaje bwishimira kurimbuka kwabwo. Hariho umunezero wo gukina igice kinini: twese turi gladiator, turira Ave Imperator! Kugira ngo dusubiremo ikibazo cya Burke: "Mu kababaro umunezero uracyari hejuru, kandi imibabaro duhura na yo ntaho ihuriye n'ububabare bukabije, burigihe burigihe, kandi twihatira guhungabana vuba bishoboka." Poe yagiye kure, kandi yari umuhanzi ndetse no mubyago byumwuga we. Niba, ukurikije imyizerere ye bwite, agahinda no kubura ubwiza ninsanganyamatsiko zisumba izindi zose, hamwe nubusizi ukumva umunezero mwinshi kwisi, ubwo rero umubabaro numwijima mubuzima bwe bwacitse ntabwo byari bifite indishyi zabo kenshi.

Mu mpapuro zikurikira, dufite urugero rushya rwubwenge bwumuhanzi buranga gusobanura igisigo kizwi. Gustave Doré, umurimo wanyuma w'ikaramu ye imbere yacu, ntabwo yari umurangi, cyangwa se umunyabukorikori, kubantu ba realiste basaba ukuri kw'ijwi, ishusho, no gutungana. Ibintu nkibi ntibyamuhangayikishije cyane kuruta gukora imiterere nintera, umucyo nigicucu, bifasha umugambi we, - ibyo byari ugushimisha ubugingo, ibitekerezo, byabareba. Ibi yabikoze adukangura ubwoba, binyuze mubitekerezo bitangaje kandi akenshi bihebuje byibintu bidasubirwaho kandi byuzuye umwijima cyangwa icyubahiro. Biravugwa neza ko niba ibihangano bye bitari amashusho akomeye, nkamashusho nibyiza rwose. Nk "umuhanzi wubuvanganzo," kandi nkuriya, imbaraga ze zari zihuye neza nubuvumbuzi butangaje bwumwanditsi we, hamwe nubutwari bwintwari bwumwuka watwitse ibye. Niyo mpamvu intsinzi ye na Rabelais, hamwe na "Le Juif-Errant," "Les Contes Drolatiques," na "Don Quixote,", bityo rero, kuba yarananiwe kwerekana ubwiza bwa Idyls ya Tennyson, ya "Il Paradiso," y'abashumba b'Abaheburayo, n'andi masomo asaba gushyirwa hejuru, cyangwa kuryoherwa no gutuza. Yavutse ari umutware wa groteque, kandi mubushishozi budasanzwe yashoboraga kwerekana ibitekerezo byubwonko bwahigwaga. Turabona ibintu nkuko abantu be babibonye, ​​kandi n'amaso yumuyahudi uzerera, Don yatangaye, cyangwa umukobwa wumucuzi wa zahabu, ubwiza bwe bukuza Umwami mumaduka kuri Pont-au-Impinduka. Mu miterere y'ibintu niho agomba gukururwa na buri gihangano cyumurongo cyangwa prose navuze ko kidasanzwe. Ingoma zo hasi zahamagawe mu murimo we; urutare rwe, ibiti n'imisozi, ikirere ubwacyo, kizima gifite intego na diablerie. Iyaba yarabayeho yerekana Shakespeare, twakagombye kubona uburyo budasanzwe bwa Caliban, Abarozi, umuyaga muri "Lear"; ariko ntagushidikanya yari akwiye kwibaza ku bitekerezo bye bya Imogen cyangwa Miranda. Ubwiza bwera kandi bworoshye, nubwiza buhebuje, gake wasaga nkuwabyumva.

Nyamara hariho ubwiza mubishushanyo bye kuri "Kera Mariner," bidashoboka nkuko biri, kandi bikurikiranye bidasanzwe mubukurikirane bwa Doré. Rime yamuhaye inkuru ndende, hamwe no guhinduranya ibintu byinshi. Mu gikona amajwi n'ibara bibika monotone yabo kandi ntiduhindura ahantu cyangwa umwanya. Ingaruka ni izihe? Doré avuga ibintu byinshi bitandukanye ku nsanganyamatsiko igihe yatekerezaga, "enigma y'urupfu no kwiyumvisha ubugingo budashoboka." Muri bimwe muri ibyo bishushanyo amakosa ye aragaragara; abandi bagaragaza umwimerere we ukomeye, nimico myiza aho, nkumukorikori, yahagaze wenyine. Mubyukuri hari ikintu gihuriweho hagati yimyitwarire yakazi ya Poe na Doré. Ibi byagaragara neza mugihe aba nyuma bagerageje ukuboko kuri "Umugani wa Grotesque na Arabesque." Bombi bitabaje kenshi kuri elf-land of fantasy and romance. Mubikorwa bya melodramatike bombi, binyuze mubuyobozi bwabo bwindengakamere, birinze umurongo-akaga uri hagati yicyiza nibidasobanutse. Poe yari asenga byukuri Byiza; urukundo yakundaga byari ubushake bwo kwiyegurira Imana, kandi hano agabana n'abashushanya. Umusizi cyangwa umuhanzi, Urupfu amaherezo ruhindura byose: mu gicucu cyururimi rwe rwiza, Vedder yikigereranyo cyikimenyetso kibashyira imbona nkubone, ariko ikabashyiraho umwambaro wubwenge nimbaraga zidapfa. Umugore w’umunyamerika yakoze ishusho ya Genius ufite amaso yinyenyeri hamwe n’itara rya nyuma, risa neza cyane n’umuntu ufite iyerekwa rireba, ariko iminwa ye idashobora kuvuga, amayobera y’igihugu kirenze "umuryango w’imva."

Edmund C. Stedman.

Ariko igikona, cyicaye wenyine kuri busti ya plaque, kivuga gusa
 Iri jambo rimwe, nkaho roho ye muri iryo jambo rimwe yakoze isohoka.

 Inyoni iravuga iti: "Nta na rimwe."
Watangajwe no gutuza kumenetse kubisubizo byavuzwe neza,
 Navuze nti: "Nta gushidikanya, ibyo ivuga ni ububiko bwayo n'ububiko byonyine,
 Yafashwe na shobuja utishimye ufite Impanuka itagira impuhwe
 Yakurikiranye vuba kandi akurikira byihuse kugeza indirimbo ze umutwaro umwe uremereye -
 Kugeza kumurongo wibyiringiro bye umutwaro mubi uremereye
 Bya 'Nta na rimwe - nta na rimwe.' "
Ariko igikona kiracyashuka umutima wanjye wose ubabaye kumwenyura,
 Ugororotse Nazungurutse intebe yegeranye imbere yinyoni na bust n'inzugi;
 Noneho, kuri mahame arohama, niyemeje guhuza
 Ibyiza kubwiza, utekereza icyo iyi nyoni iteye ubwoba ya yore -
 Niki iyi iteye ubwoba, idashidikanywaho, iteye ubwoba, inyoni ninyoni ya yore
 Bivuze mu gutontoma "Nta na rimwe."
Ibi naricaye nshishikajwe no gukeka, ariko nta jambo ryerekana
 Ku nyoni amaso yumuriro ubu yatwitse mu gituza cyanjye;
 Ibi nibindi byinshi nicaye kuragura, n'umutwe wanjye byoroshye kuryama
 Kuri velheti ya cushion yerekana ko itara ryaka o'er,
 Ariko ninde mahame ya violet yatondekanye n'amatara yaka o'er
 Azakanda , ah, nta na rimwe!
Hanyuma, methought, umwuka warushijeho kwiyongera, uhumura neza uhereye kuri censer itagaragara
 Swung by seraphim ibirenge-bigwa byikubita hasi hasi.
 Ndatakamba nti: "Ndagowe, Imana yawe yagurije - abo bamarayika yagutumye.
 Kuruhuka - kuruhuka na nepenthe uhereye kubyo wibutse kuri Lenore!
 Quaff, yewe quaff ubu bwoko nepenthe, kandi wibagirwe iyi Lenore yazimiye! "
 Quoth igikona, "Nta na rimwe."
"Umuhanuzi!" naravuze nti, "ikintu kibi! -umuhanuzi aracyariho, niba inyoni cyangwa shitani! -
 Niba Umuyaga yoherejwe, cyangwa niba umuyaga wagutaye hano ku nkombe,
 Ubutayu nyamara butagira ubwoba, kuri ubu butayu bwarogejwe -
 Kuri uru rugo na Horror arahiga-mbwira rwose, ndinginga -
 Hariho i Galeyadi hari amavuta? - Mbwira - mbwira, ndakwinginze! "
 Quoth igikona, "Nta na rimwe."
"Umuhanuzi!" naravuze nti, "ikintu kibi - umuhanuzi aracyariho, niba inyoni cyangwa shitani!
 Ukoresheje Ijuru ryunamye hejuru, twe - kubw'Imana twembi dusenga -
 Bwira ubu bugingo umubabaro uremereye niba, muri Aidenn ya kure,
 Izaba ifashe inkumi yera abamarayika bita Lenore -
 Kata umukobwa udasanzwe kandi urabagirana abamarayika bita Lenore. "
 Quoth igikona, "Nta na rimwe."
"Ba iryo jambo ikimenyetso cyacu cyo gutandukana, inyoni cyangwa fiend!" Nakomye induru, ndatangira -
 "Subiza mu muyaga mwinshi no ku nkombe za Plutoniya!
 Ntugasige ibara ryirabura nkikimenyetso cyikinyoma roho yawe yavuze!
 Reka ubwigunge bwanjye butavunitse! —Kureka bust hejuru yumuryango wanjye!
 Kura umunwa wawe mu mutima wanjye, ukure ishusho yawe ku muryango wanjye! "
 Quoth igikona, "Nta na rimwe."
Kandi igikona, ntigishobora guhindagurika, iracyicaye, iracyicaye
 Kuri busti ya Pallas hejuru yumuryango wicyumba cyanjye;
 Kandi amaso ye afite ibintu byose bisa nabadayimoni barota,
 Kandi itara rimutemba ritera igicucu cye hasi;
 Kandi roho yanjye ivuye muri kiriya gicucu kiryamye hejuru
 Ntibizakurwaho!
