KƆMƐNT PAN DI POEM.

Di sikrit fɔ wan poem, we nɔ less dan wan jest’s prosperity, de na di yes fɔ di wan we yɛri am. Yield to its spell, accept the poet’s mood: dis, afta ɔl, na wetin di sages dɛn kin ansa we yu aks dɛn bɔt in valyu. Ivin if di pɔyt insɛf, insay in ɔda mud, tɛl yu se in art na jɔs sleight of hand, in fud enchanter in it, ɛn ɔf fɔ sho yu di trik fɔ am,—nɔ biliv am. Wet fɔ in prɔfɛt awa; dɔn gi yusɛf to in pawa, in gladi ɔ in pen. "Wi de na Lɔv in an tide!" sing Gautier, in Swinburne’s buoyant paraphrase,—ɛn frɔm mɔnin te di san go dɔŋ wi de waf na di vaylɛnt si: na wan lɔv nɔmɔ de, wan May, wan flawa strɛch. Lɔv de sote go, ɔl ɔda tin nɔ rial ɛn put am na sayd. Di vishɔn gɛt ɛnd, di tin we de apin de chenj; bɔt wi dɔn gɛt sɔntin, di mɛmori fɔ wan charm. As bɔku poet dɛn, so bɔku charms. Na di charm fɔ Evanescence de, dat we de lend to suprɛm biuti ɛn gras wan aureole fɔ Pathos. Sheb wit Landor in wan "nayt of memories and of sighs" fo Rose Aylmer, en yu get dis to di ful.

Ɛn naw tek di an fɔ wan nyu-wɔl minstrel, we dɔn stray frɔm sɔm prɔpa habitat to da rud ɛn dissonant Amɛrika we, as Baudelaire bin si, "na bin fɔ Poe na wan big prizin nɔmɔ we i bin de rɔn tru, ya ɛn de, wit di fiva agiteshɔn fɔ wan pɔsin we dɛn mek fɔ blo insay wan wɔl we klin pas am," ɛn usay "in intɛriɔ layf, spiritual as pɔyt, spiritual ivin as drunkard, bin de bɔt wan ɛfɔt we go de sote go fɔ rɔnawe pan di inflɛns we dis antipathetical atmosphere gɛt." Klasp di sɛnsitiv an fɔ wan trɔbul siŋda we de dree so in weda, ɛn sheb wit am di klaym we i fɛn,—nɔ ɛva ɔlsay na di de, ɔltɛm na nɛt,—if nɔto di Atlantis usay i bin dɔn waka waka, at ɔl na ples fɔ rɔnawe frɔm di bɔda dɛn we bay de i bin de immured.

Na wan land nɔmɔ i gɛt pawa fɔ lid yu, ɛn fɔ wan nɛt nɔmɔ yu go ebul fɔ sheb in drim. Wan trakt we nɔto di Wɔl ɔ Ɛvin: "No-man's-land," kɔmɔt na Spays, kɔmɔt na Taym. Na di wan dɛn we de ambɔg, we tru dɛn yay, lɛk di wan dɛn we de na di Sɛnsi, di sol de fɛn winda dɛn pan ɔl we di maynd dɔn daz; na ya spirit dɛn, we de grop fɔ di rod we de go na Eternity, dɛn kin stɔp ɛn delay. Na di limbo fɔ "planetary souls," usay ɔl di mun layt nɔ shɔ, ɔl lɔv lɔv ɛn ilyushɔn dɛn we dɔn lɔs. Na ya sɔm dɛn fiks insay trance, di wangren rɛst we pɔsin kin gɛt; ɔda wan dɛn

"muv fantastik wan."

"Sheeted Mɛmori dɛn bɔt di Past—

Na so di land tan, ɛn fɔ wan nɛt wi kin go insay de,—na nɛt we astral faz ɛn chimes we de kam bak. I monodies na twɛlv poem, we in myuzik de tray fɔ chenj yet ɛva na di sem. Wan wan dɛn de sawnd, lɛk di chiming we di brazen ɛn ebony klok de mek, insay "The Masque of the Red Death," we mek di waltzers dɛn stɔp wit "disconcert ɛn tremulousness ɛn meditation," as ɔltɛm di awa kam rawnd.

Fɔ ɔl dɛn mistik kadɛns ya, di plet we dɛn kɔl The Raven , we de shek shek tru di pɔtal, mɔ dɔn mek di ɔda wɔl kɔle. Us tin dɛn kin go fɔ mek wan poem,—ɛn aw tru insay dis, lɛk bɔku ɔda wan dɛn, da rays we bin gi in bard dɛn nem "di wan dɛn we mek am"? Dɛn kin kɔl wan wok fɔ kɔmɔt na di say we nɔ gɛt natin. Usay natin nɔ bin de, i stil de,—na nyu tin we Gɔd mek, we na pat pan mɔtalman jɛntri. Ɛn sɔm ɛksepshɔnal liriks, pas ɔda wan dɛn we ikwal kriyaytiv, de kɔmpɛl wi fɔ tink nyu wan aw brayt wan di pɔyt in pen de tɔn tin dɛn we wi nɔ no

"to shep, ɛn gi to airy natin."
 Wan lokal ples fɔ de, ɛn wan nem."

I tan lɛk se ɛni wan pan dɛn nɔ gɛt ɛni protɔtayp, bɔt stil ɔl de mek wi kɔle wit ɛlimɛnt dɛn we dɛn pul frɔm di shado fɔ di Supanɛchɔral. Naw di imajineshɔn we ay pas ɔl de wɔri bɔt di sol fɔ tin dɛn; i kin ɔ nɔ kin inspɛkt di Fɛntasi se pipul dɛn wit imej dɛn di intalunar vayg. Stil, wan pan dɛn liriks ya, insay in smɔl we, de afɛkt wi wit wan sɛns fɔ spɛshal, as shɔ se di sublaym wok dɛn we wan supanɛchɔral kast de du,—Marlowe in "Faustus," di "Faust" fɔ Goethe, "Manfred," ɔ ivin dɛn ethereal masta wok dɛn de, "The Tempest" ɛn "A Midsummer Night's Dream." Mɔ pas wan, pan ɔl we ɔda we dɛn difrɛn, gɛt sɔm lod ɔ refrain we de haunt di mɛmori,—wans yu yɛri, nɔ ɛva fɔgɛt, lɛk di tɔyn fɔ wan ɔgan-stɔp we dɛn nɔ kin yuz ɔltɛm bɔt we difrɛn. Notis pan dɛn na Bürger in "Lenore," dat ghostly ɛn rezonant balad, di lure ɛn fɔyl fɔ di transletɔ dɛn. Na smɔl pipul dɛn nɔmɔ go dinay se Coleridge in wɔndaful "Rime of the Ancient Mariner" tinap na dɛn yon ed. "Le Juif-Errant" bin fɔ dɔn gɛt klem, if Beranger na bin big pɔyt; ɛn, bɔt fɔ dɛn rimot frɔm pɔpul sɔri-at, "Di Ledi ɔf Shalɔt" ɛn "Di Blɛsin Damozel" kin ad to di list. Dɛn bin gi am to Edgar Allan Poe fɔ mek tu liriks, "The Bells" ɛn The Raven , ɛni wan pan dɛn, pan ɔl we sɔntɛm dɛn nɔ fayn pas di wan dɛn we Tennyson ɛn Rossetti rayt, na wan spɛshal wan. "Ulalume," pan ɔl we i ikwal strenj ɛn imajinari, i nɔ gɛt di yunivasal kwaliti we na pat pan wi tɛst.

Di Raven in sheer poetical constituents de fɔdɔm dɔŋ dɛn kayn pies dɛn lɛk "Di Haunted Palace," "Di Siti in di Si," "Di Slipa," ɛn "Israfel." Di wan ol tin go chenj, a saspek, bay rida dɛm fɔ wan fastidious kast, fɔ dɛn kayn pasej dɛm lɛk dis:

Bɔt The Raven , lɛk "The Bells" ɛn "Annabel Lee," de kɔmɛnt insɛf to di bɔku pipul dɛn ɛn di smɔl wan dɛn. A dɔn tɔk ɔdasay se Poe in rarer prodakshɔn dɛn bin tan lɛk to mi "dɛn wan dɛn we de insay di apinɛns, at ɔl, fɔ spontan,—insay i de giv-ɔp to in filin dɛn, we di day fɔdɔm ɛn kadɛns dɛn we mɔs myuzik, mɔs mɛlankɔli, kɔmɔt frɔm am we i nɔ no." Dis na tin we a stil biliv; ɛn pan ɔl dat, we a stɔdi dis poem bak, i kin mek a kɔle pas ɛni ɔda tɛm frɔm we a bin smɔl. We yu sabi am gud gud wan, dat de tɛl yu fɔ du ɛni tru tru wok we dɛn mek wit at. Mek di man, we nɔ no art ɛn we nɔ bisin bɔt am, chɛk sɔm poem ɔ peint, ɛn aw i nɔ tu te, in pawa tek am! Infakt, i go tɔk pasmak bɔt di valyu we di fɔs gud wok we i dɔn pe atɛnshɔn pan fayn fayn wan gɛt. Di Raven , bak, gɛt kɔnsistɛns kwaliti dɛn we ivin pɔsin we sabi bɔt di tin fɔ admaya. Insay no ɔda poem dɛn we di pɔsin we rayt am rayt, dɛn nɔ ɛksplen di rizin we mek dɛn du am mɔ. "The Haunted Palace" na jɔs lɛk difinit to di sɛlɛkɛt rida, bɔt Poe nɔ bin fɔ dɔn tek da subtil alɛgrɔri de fɔ bold analisis. Di Revin de ful-ɔp wit di tipik tim we di pɔsin we rayt am gɛt—di lɔs we i nɔ go ebul fɔ gɛt bak fɔ wan uman we dɛn de wɔship aydɔl ɛn we fayn; bɔt pan ɔda grɔn, bak, di pɔblik instinkt kɔrɛkt fɔ tink se na in ripɔt poem.

Bɔku tɛm, pɔsin we gɛt sɛns kin gɛt fut wit di sakrifays we sɔmbɔdi kin tray fɔ du, ɛn nɔto ɔltɛm dis kin bi di big tin we i kin du. Rikɔgnishɔn na di mɔ instant fɔ we dɛn dɔn put am bifo. I nɔ de gɛt am, lɛk misɛf in prɔpati, kɔyn afta kɔyn, bɔt "nɔto atɔl ɔ ɔl in ɔl." Ɛn so wit ɔda ambishɔn dɛn: di kɔt, sojaman, aktɔ,—ilɛk wetin na dɛn pat,—ɛvri wan de kɔnt in win frɔm sɔm laki laki strok. Poe in Raven, pan ɔl we augury, na bin fɔ am "di bɔd we mek di briz blo." Di pɔyt bin setul na Nyu-Yɔk, insay di winta insay 1844-'45, i fɛn wok pan Wilis in pepa, "Di Ivin Miro," ɛn eking in inkɔm bay kɔntribyushɔn ɔdasay. Fɔ siks ia i bin dɔn bi wan aktif rayta, ɛn ɛnjɔy wan prɔfɛshɔnal gudnem; bin de ol am wit rɛspɛkt ɛn misdoubt, ɛn i nɔ bin lɔs fɔ in shea pan di jɔnalistik we dɛn nɔ bin de pe fayn da de de. I bin dɔn du bɔku pan in bɛst wok bak,—lɛk stori dɛn lɛk "Ligeia" ɛn "The Fall of the House of Usher," (di las wan gɛt da mistik kɔmpin de, insay vas, fɔ Elihu Vedder in "A Lost Mind,") lɛk "The Gold Bug" ɛn "The Mystery of Marie Roget." I bin dɔn mek pipul dɛn we dɔn tɔn to Jiova na ɔda kɔntri, ɛn i bin gɛt wan pawa fɔ lɔng tɛm pan di Frɛnch maynd. I bin dɔn lan in yon pawa ɛn wikɛdnɛs, ɛn i bin de na in praymɛnt, ɛn nɔto we i nɔ gɛt wan patikyula gudnem. Bɔt i nɔ bin dɔn rayt ɛnitin we de na ɔlman in tɔŋ. Fɔ rare ɛn diliket wok sɔm pɔpul tɔch fɔ ad fɔ kech di jenɛral ɔdiɛns fɔ pɔsin in yon tɛm.

Tru di industri fɔ Poe in saksesiv biyografi dɛm, di hit we The Raven mek dɔn bi wan stori we dɛn kin tɔk bɔku tɛm. Di poet in yɔŋ wɛf, Virginia, bin de fade bifo in yay, bɔt i bin de fɔ ɔda ia insay day in shado. Di lɔng, lɔw chɛmba na di os nia di Blɔmingdel Rod na as we pipul dɛn sabi as di rum usay Rouget de l’Isle bin mek di Marseillaise. Ɔlman dɔn yɛri se di poem, we dɛn sayn "Quarles," bin apia na di "American Review," wit wan pseudo-ɛditorial kɔmɛnt bɔt in fɔm; dat Poe bin gɛt tɛn dɔla fɔ am; dat Wilis, we na di wan we gud pas ɔlman ɛn we nɔ bin de jɛlɔs pas ɔlman pan di arbit dɛn we gɛt fashɔn, bin print am bak wit wan prez we mek i tɔk to pipul dɛn na di tɔŋ wantɛm wantɛm. Ɔl di dawt we pipul dɛn bin gɛt bɔt udat rayt am bin kɔmɔt we Poe insɛf bin de rid am na wan mitin fɔ rayt buk, ɛn fɔ sɔm tɛm, na in na bin di wan we dɛn bin mak pas ɔlman pan di wan dɛn we rayt am na Amɛrika. Di hit bin mek i want fɔ du sɔntin ɛn ɛnkɔrej am. Lɛk ɔda ɛn praud satirist, insɛf bin fɛn "sɔntin fɔ sɔm" ivin "in di hum fɔ insɛkt dɛn." I sɔri fɔ no se, bɔt tri ia pas,—wan tɛm we i bin de inflɔws, prawd, sɔri, bɔt stil i bin de wok fɔ imajin ɔ we i bin de wok tranga wan ɔltɛm,—bifo di las win, bifo di kɔtin fɔdɔm pan layf we fɔ am fɔ tru, na bin bad bad tin, ɛn i gri fɔ du wetin “di Kɔnkɔra Wɔm.”

"The American Review: A Whig Journal" na bin wan magazin we dɛn kin gi kredit fɔ da tɛm de, i bin gɛt tu kɔlɔm, dɛn bin print am pan gud pepa wit klia tayp, ɛn dɛn bin de sho am wit mɛzotint pɔtrɛt dɛn. Amid bɔku tin dɛn we nɔ bin de ɔnda di standad we de naw, i bin gɛt sɔm tin dɛn we ɛni ɛditɔ go gladi fɔ gɛt. Di fɔs volyum, fɔ 1845, gɛt atikul dɛn we Ɔras Grili, Dɔnal Mitchell, Walta Witman, Mash, Tuckerman, ɛn Whipple rayt. Ralph Hoyt in kwayɛt poem, "Old," bin de insay dis volyum. Ɛn na tri wɔd dɛn ya we Poe rayt: “Di Siti na di Si,” “Di Vali we Nɔ Rɛst,” ɛn Di Revin . Tu pan dɛn tin ya bin bil,—na so i bin de du,—frɔm di stɔdi dɛn we dɛn bin dɔn du trade, bɔt di las nem kɔmɔt lɛk se dɛn jɔs mek am, ɛn i dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ wi gɛt am naw. Di tin we dɛn tɔk se dɛn nɔ bin rivays am afta dat, na mistek. Ilɛvin smɔl chenj dɛn frɔm di magazin-tɛks de insay The Raven and Other Poems , 1845, wan buk we di pɔyt bin fil shɔt tɛm se dɛn ɛnkɔrej am fɔ gi di pipul dɛn. Dis na mɔs chenj dɛn pan pɔntueshɔn, ɔ fɔ wan wɔd dɛn, di las kayn we dɛn mek fɔ mek di ifɛkt we alitɛreshɔn gɛt go ɔp. Insay Mista Lang in fayn fayn edishɔn fɔ Poe in vas, we dɛn briŋ kam na di "Parchment Library," i dɔn sho di instinkt we wan masta sabi bukman gɛt, ɛn i dɔn du wit sɛns, fɔ go bak to di tɛks we de insay di volyum we wi jɔs tɔk bɔt, as dɛn gi am insay di Lɔndɔn isɔ fɔ 1846. Di "standad" Griswold kɔllɛkshɔn fɔ di poet in wok dɛn bɔku wit mistek. Dɛn ɛditɔ dɛn we leta dɔn ripit dɛn tin ya, ɛn dɛnsɛf dɔn mek mistek fɔ dɛnsɛf. Bɔt di tɛks fɔ di buk we nem The Raven , bikɔs dɛn bin aks di pɔsin we rayt di buk fɔ mek dɛn gi am di manuskrip kɔpi dɛn, na in bin stil rivays am fa fawe; infakt, i bin print am na Richmɔnd, jɔs bifo i day, wit di poɛtik sɔbstityushɔn fɔ "sɛrafim we in fut-fɔl" fɔ "enjɛl dɛn we in fayn fut-fɔl," insay di fɔtin stanza. So, wi prɛsɛn tɛks, pan ɔl we i rili bi di wan we dɛn rayt insay 1845, dɛn dɔn chenj am smɔl bay di we aw di pɔyt rid am leta, ɛn na, a tink se, na di kɔrɛkt ɛn ifɛktiv vɛshɔn fɔ dis wan poem. Bɔt di chenj we bin rili chenj frɔm di fɔs wan bin apin insay di volyum insay 1845; di ilevin stanza we bin dɔn gɛt dɛn layn ya fɔs, we nɔ fayn pan raym ɛn ɔda we na wan blemish pan di poem:

"Kɛch frɔm sɔm masta we nɔ gladi, we nɔ gɛt sɔri-at Disasta."
 Fɔ fala fast ɛn fala fast—so, we Hope go adjure, .
 Stern Despair kam bak, insted of di swit Hope we i bin dea adjure—
 Dat sɔri ansa, ‘Nɔ ɛva!’"

I go fayn if ɔda, ɛn fambul, poet dɛn kin bi as shɔ fɔ mek dɛn chenj dɛn ɔltɛm impɔtant. Poe bin de rihand in smɔl sho fɔ vas ɔltɛm, ɛn bɔku tɛm i bin de bɛtɛ am. Di Revin na bin di fɔs wan pan di smɔl poem dɛn we i bin dɔn nia fɔ dɔn bifo dɛn print am. I kin bi se di wan dɛn we de kia fɔ poem nɔ bin lɔs bɛtɛ we i day. I bin sabi fɔ rayt proz fayn fayn wan, ɛn i nɔ ɛva rayt vas fɔ mek i mek wan poem. We wan refrain of image bin de haunt am, di lyric we bin de rizulyt na bin di inspɛkshɔn, as insɛf bin se, fɔ wan pawa, nɔto fɔ wan rizin. Dis bin de pan intaval dɛm we so rare as i bin lɛf smɔl fɔ mek i jɔstify di Fairfield tiori se ɛni wan pan dɛn na di prodakt fɔ wan nervɔs kraysis.

So, wetin bin gi di pɔsin we rayt di poem in clue to The Raven ? Frɔm us misti fawndeshɔn i bin rayz sloslo to myuzik we dɛn de blo smɔl smɔl? As yushul, mo dan wan tin go fo di bildin of so notable poem. We a tink bɔt di lɔng sɛmin dɛn we dɛn kin prich bɔku tɛm pan shɔt ɛn smɔl tɛks dɛn we nɔ kin mek pɔsin tink gud wan, a op se dɛn nɔ go blem mi fɔ dis tɔk bɔt wan layrik,—espɛshali wan we wan atis lɛk Dɔre dɔn mek di tɔpik fɔ prodigal ɛgzampul. Te to dis biɛn tɛm a bin dɔn tink se dis pies, ɛn sɔm we di pɔsin we rayt am bin kɔmpoz afta i apia, na bin ɛksepshɔnal we i nɔ bin gro frɔm jem dɛn na in bɔysh vas. Bɔt Mista Fearing Gill dɔn sho mi sɔm stanzas dɛn we Poe rayt we i nɔ pablish yet, we i rayt insay in ettin ia, ɛn we gɛt di taytul, "Di Dɛbul fɔ di Faya." I tan lɛk se di manuskrip na di fɔstɛm we di pɔsin we rayt di poem bin rayt am, ɛn di famili we i dɔn de fɔ af sɛnti ia de sho se i rili tru. Apat frɔm di plainest jem dɛm fɔ "The Bells" ɛn "The Haunted Palace" i gɛt sɔm layn dɛm we sɔm kayn we de sho aw di opin ɛn klos fɔ The Raven . As fɔ di ritm fɔ wi poem, we wi kɔmpia di de dɛn, i sho se dis bin afɛkt di ritm fɔ "Lady Geraldine's Courtship." Poe na bin wan pan di fɔs pipul dɛn we bin ɔnɔ Mis Barrett in sɛns; i rayt in poem dɛn we i dɔn gɛda to am as "di nobul pas ɔlman pan in sɛks," ɛn i bin de insay sɔri-at wit in layrik we i de yuz. Di layn dɛn frɔm in lɔv-poem, .

"Wit wan murmurous stir we nɔ shɔ, na di ays, di pepul kɔtin."
 Swɛl insay ɛn swɛl aut rawnd in motionless pale brows,"

bin fɛn wan ɛko insay dɛn tin ya:

"Ɛn di silk sad uncertain rustling fɔ ɛni pepul kɔtin."
 Thrilled mi—ful mi wit fantastic terrors we a nɔ ɛva fil bifo."

Na ya Poe bin tek wan prɛvilɛj we i bin roughly censured Longfellow, ɛn we nɔbɔdi nɔ ɛva luk fɔ na in yon ples we i nɔ bin no kwik kwik wan ɛn pɔnish am. Insay myuzik ɛn stanzaik fɔm, wi go si se di tu poem dɛn nɔ difrɛn, bɔt pan wetin mek dɛn du dɛn, dɛn difrɛn atɔl. Di uman we de rayt poem we gɛt fri-an nɔ bin fil natin pas di tru tru ɔrijinal we di pɔsin we rayt di poem bin gɛt. "Dis raytin we klia!" i ala se,—"dis pawa we dɛn de fil!... Wi big pɔyt, Mista Brawn, we rayt 'Paracelsus,' &c., gɛt zil fɔ in admaya fɔ di ritm." Mista Ingram, afta i tɔk bɔt "Lady Geraldine," i tek sɛns fɔ pɔynt ɔda sɔs we Poe go dɔn kech wan impuls frɔm. Insay 1843, Albɛrt Payk, di af-af Grik, af-frɔntiman, pɔyt na Akansas, bin dɔn print insay "The New Mirror," fɔ we Poe bin de rayt fɔ da tɛm de, sɔm vas dɛn we gɛt di taytul "Isadore," bɔt frɔm da tɛm de, di pɔsin we rayt am rivays am ɛn kɔl am "The Widowed Heart." A pik frɔm Mista Payk in rivishɔn di stanza we de dɔŋ ya, we di men tin dɛn we de insay de kɔrɛkt wit di ɔrijinal vɛshɔn:

"Rɛstlɛs a de paz wi wan rum dɛn, a nɔ de ple wi siŋ dɛn igen,
 Di san we de shayn de shayn fayn fayn wan na di flɔ we dɛn nɔ swip;
 Di bɔd we de provok stil sidɔm ɛn siŋ, O melancholy strain!
 Bikɔs mi at tan lɛk ɔtom klawd we ren kin ful-ɔp;
 Yu dɔn lɔs to mi sote go, Isadore!"

Na ya wi gɛt wan we fɔ mɛzhɔ fɔ lɔng tɛm, wan we fiba di refrain, ɛn di introdukshɔn fɔ wan bɔd we in siŋ de mek pɔsin fil bad mɔ. Ɔda rod dɛn de we di wan dɛn we want fɔ no mɔ kin fala; notably, wan pasej we Mista Ingram pik frɔm Poe in las rivyu fɔ "Barnaby Rudge":

"Di revɛn, sɛf, * * * kin dɔn mek am, pas aw wi de si am naw, wan pat pan di kɔnsɛpshɔn fɔ di fantastik Banabi. * * * In karakta kin dɔn du, we i kam pan di wan we di idiot gɛt, bɔku di sem pat we i de du, insay myuzik, di akɔmpanyin we i kam pan di ays."

Bɔt pan ɔl dat, afta dɛn dɔn sho dɛn jem dɛn ya ɛn di tin dɛn we fiba dɛn, dɛn stil de si di valyu we dis poem gɛt na di we aw i bigin. Di myuzik we de go bifo, di fayn fayn tin dɛn we de apin ɛn di layt ɛn shed we difrɛn,—di tin we pas ɔl, di spiritual pawa we di nɛt kin gɛt, de mek i bi di wok we wan man we sabi fɔ no bɔt sɔntin kin du. As fɔ di bɔd we de mek pɔsin fred, i nɔ tan lɛk ɔl di ɔda revɛn dɛn na in klen, frɔm di "twa corbies" ɛn "tri revɛn" dɛn we di baladist dɛn gɛt to Barnaby in rumpled "Grip." Na ya, no semblance nɔ de fɔ di cawing rook we de haunt ancestral turrets ɛn tred di fil fɔ heraldry; no boding phantom we Tickell bin siŋ se, we, .

"i de ala na in winda tri tɛm, .
 Di revɛn flap in wing, .
 Tu gud di lov-lorn maiden bin no
 Di solemn boding sawnd."

Poe in revɛn na difrɛn kɔnsɛpshɔn; di bɔdi we pɔsin we de kray kin fil bad ɛn we i nɔ gɛt op; wan sable embodied Memory, di abiding chronicler of doom, wan kayn di Irreparable. We i rɔnawe krɔs di Styx, frɔm "di Nayt in Plutonian sho," i tan lɛk di imej sol fɔ di pɔsin we de aks insɛf,—fɔ di wan we nɔ ebul, nɔ go, quaff di kayn nepenthe, bikɔs di mɛmori fɔ Lenore na ɔl wetin lɛf am, ɛn wit di surcease fɔ in sɔri ivin dat go put am na sayd.

Dɛn kin tek di Revin bak as ripɔt poem fɔ di pɔsin we rayt am, fɔ in ɛgzampul fɔ ɔl in noshɔn dɛn bɔt wetin poem fɔ bi. Dɛn tin ya de insay in ɛsay dɛn we i rayt bɔt "Di Poɛtik Prinsipul," "Di Rashɔnal fɔ Vas," ɛn "Di Filɔsofi fɔ Kɔmpozishɔn." Poe bin deklare se "insay Myuzik, sɔntɛm, di sol de mɔs nia fɔ rich di big ɛnd fɔ we, we dɛn inspɛkt am frɔm di Poetic Sentiment, i de strɛch—di krieshɔn fɔ supana Biuti.... Vas nɔ kin bɛtɛ fɔ pik pas as myuzik we nɔ impɔtant ɔ we nɔ ebul fɔ du bɛtɛ"; bɔt bak, vas we rili bi di "Poetry of Words" na "The Rhythmical Creation of Beauty,"—dis ​​ɛn nɔto mɔ. Di tone of di highest Beauty na wan of Sadness. Di tɔpik we de mek pɔsin fil bad pas ɔl na Day. Dis fɔ bi alay to Biuti. "So, di day we fayn uman day na, we nɔ gɛt wan dawt, di tɔpik we gɛt poem pas ɔl na di wɔl,—ɛn ikwal, i nɔ go gɛt wan dawt se di lip dɛn we fit pas ɔl fɔ dis kayn tɔpik na di wan dɛn we pɔsin we in lɔv dɔn day." Dɛn kot dɛn las ɛksplɛshɔn ya frɔm Poe in whimsical analysis of dis very poem, bɔt dɛn sho prɛsishɔn di jenɛral rɛnj fɔ in vas. Di klimaks fɔ "Di Bɛl dɛm" na di muffled monotone fɔ ghouls, we de glori fɔ wej di mɔtalman at. "Lenore," The Raven , "The Sleeper," "To One in Paradise," ɛn "Ulalume" de mek wan tenebrose simfɔni,—ɛn "Annabel Lee," we dɛn rayt las wan, sho se wan tim bin posɛs am te di ɛnd. Agen, dis ɔl na natin if nɔto myuzik, ɛn sɔm de tɔch wit da kwaliti de fɔ di Fantastik we de wek di sɛns fɔ fred, ɛn ad nyu fred to pen insɛf. Tru ɔl na dimly autlayn, ɔnda wan shadowy pall, di pɔyt in ideal lɔv,—so bɔku tɛm dɛn kin sho am af-af ɔdasay,—di bɛrbɔd wɛf fɔ Usher, di Ledi Ligeia, in trut di kɔmpin fɔ in yon nature. A tink se di lɔv we atis gɛt fɔ wan "in di fɔm" nɔ go ɛva ebul fɔ rili rival in devoshɔn to sɔm ideal. Di uman we de nia am fɔ ɛksesaiz in spɛl, i fɔ bi ɔl bay tɔn ɛn nɔ lɔng, i fɔ charm am wit difrɛn difrɛn tin dɛn we nɔ gɛt ɛnd, ɔ i fɔ satisfay fɔ lɛf fɔ gɛt pat pan in fet. I fɔ mek di Unattainable ful am, ɛn dis kin ɛva jɔs pas am insay in pawa fɔ kriyaytiv art.

Poe, lɛk Hawthorne, bin kam insay wit di we aw di Romantik skul bin de go dɔŋ, ɛn nɔbɔdi nɔ bin gladi pas am fɔ laf di bad tin we bin apin to am. Yet in at bin de wit di romancers ɛn dɛn Oriental ɔ Gothic effects. Di tin we i mek, we bɔku pan di proz stori dɛn, bin tan lɛk se i lɛf am we i de rayt vas; ɛn in jɔjmɛnt bin tɛl am se lɔng lɔv poem dɛn dipen mɔ pan tin we apin pas inspɛkshɔn,—ɛn fɔ tɔk di poem dɛn bɔt lɔv, di liriks dɛn go du fɔ am. Na dat mek in tiori, we fit klia wan to in yon limiteshɔn, se "'lɔng poem' na flat kɔntradikshɔn pan wɔd dɛn." Di tin dɛn we de insay di Revin nɔ bɔku ɛn simpul: man, bɔd, ɛn di fantasmal mɛmori we uman gɛt. Bɔt di pies de gi fayn fayn displei fɔ romantik matirial. Wetin wi gɛt? Di midulnɛt; di shado chamba wit in tomes of foget lore; di studɛnt,—wan Hironimus we de naw; di revɛn in tap pan di kesmɛnt; di nɛt we di kol sizin kin de ɛn faya we de day; di silk we dɛn kin hang we di briz kin swip; di drim dɛn ɛn di vague mistrust fɔ di echoing daknɛs; di blak, uncanny bɔd we de pan di pallid bust; di aksesɔri dɛn we dɛn mek wit vayɛl velvet ɛn di lamp we de shayn. Ɔl dis stej ifɛkt fɔ sityueshɔn, layt, kɔlɔ, sawnd, na klin romantik, ɛn ivin melodramatik, bɔt na wan poɛtik kwaliti we melodrama nɔ kin sho, ɛn i rili sho di poet in "eternal passion, eternal pain."

Di ritmik we aw dɛn mek di fim we nem The Raven bin shɔ se i go mek pipul dɛn no bɔt am. Raym, alitereshɔn, di lod, di stanzaik fɔm, dɛn bin devis wit singul adroitness. I nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se di pɔyt bin sɔprayz wit di aptnɛs fɔ Mis Barɛt in myuzik trochaics, insay "ɛit," ɛn mɔ bay di arenjmɛnt we dɛn adopt nia di klos fɔ "Ledi Jɛraldin":

"'Ay,' i se, 'naw de throb tru mi! Na ye yay we bin undo mi?
 Yay dɛn we de shayn, lɛk antik jɔlɔs dɛn we dɛn put insay Parian statu-ston!
 Ɔnda da kol wayt fɔrɛst de, una de ɛva bɔn torrid
 O'er di desolate san-desert of mai hat en laif undone?'"

In atis introdukshɔn fɔ wan tɔd raym insay ɔl tu di sɛkɔn ɛn 4 layn, ɛn di ad we i ad fayv layn ɛn wan las rɛfrɛn, bin mek di stanza fɔ Di Revin . I tan lɛk se di alitɛreshɔn we de kɔntinyu fɔ de kam witout ɛfɔt, ɛn bɔku tɛm di raym dɛn we de insay layn dɛn kin mek pɔsin want fɔ du sɔntin; we di refrain ɔ di lod de domin di wan ol wok. Na ya bak i bin dɔn prɔfit bay we Mis Barɛt bin de stɔdi bɔt balad ɛn romaunt insay in yon ɛn ɔda langwej dɛn. "Refrain" na di lure we poet ɔ myusishan de ol di wandering yes,—di rikɔrɛkt longing of Nature fɔ di initial strain. A bin ɔlwayz de admaya di fayn fayn rɛfrɛns dɛm fɔ di Inglish siŋman,—"Di Naytin, di Naytin," "Magrɛt, Magrɛt," "Mi At ɛn mi," "Tɔl sloslo," "Di Riva de flɔd on," "Pan, Pan dɔn day," ɛn ɔda wan dɛm.

"O gladi layf tin dɛm! nɔ tong."
 Dɛn fayn fayn tin dɛn kin tɔk se:
 Wan spring we gɛt lɔv bin kɔmɔt na mi at, .
 Ɛn a bin blɛs dɛn we a nɔ bin no se:
 Fɔ tru, mi gud sɛnt bin sɔri fɔ mi, .
 Ɛn a bin blɛs dɛn we a nɔ bin no."

Poe mek di fayv layn na in stanza fɔ di majik fɔ di ripit. I bin abop pan am te i rili insay sɔm poem dɛn we i rayt leta,—“Lenore,” “Annabel Lee,” “Ulalume,” ɛn “For Annie.” I bin gɛt wan wayl ɛn mɛlankɔli myuzik, a dɔn tink, frɔm di "swit inflɔwɛns dɛm," fɔ di Afrikan lod ɛn ripit dɛm we dɛn bin de siŋ to am we i bin smɔl, we de attuning wit dɛn yon myuzik di vɔys fɔ wi Sawt pɔyt.

"Di Filɔsofi ɔf Kɔmpɔzishɔn," in analisis fɔ Di Revin , na tɛknikal disɛkshɔn fɔ in we ɛn strɔkchɔ. Nɔto di we aw i bin tɔk bɔt kol-blɔd atifishin, ɔ di we aw i bin tɔk afta dat to in padi dɛn se fɔ mek dɛn no bɔt dis atifishin na jɔs ɔda tin we i bin de lay pan in sɛns, nɔ nid fɔ gɛt ɔl di kredit. If i bin dɔn disayn di kɔmplit wok bifo tɛm, i nɔ bin fɔ dɔn mek so harsh fɔs fɔ di rat-pan raym dɛn to di fayn fayn myuzik we de na di sɛkɔn stanza,—ivin pan in tiori bɔt di fantastik. Na tru se atis, we dɔn pafɛkt wan wok, de si, lɛk di fɔs Atis, se i gud, ɛn si wetin mek i gud. Wan analisis we go kam afta dat, kɔpl wit di disavowal fɔ ɛni oli faya, i kin izi fɔ mek. Mi biliv na se di fɔs kɔnsɛpshɔn ɛn rɔf draft fɔ dis poem kam as inspɛkshɔn de kam ɔltɛm; dat di pɔsin we rayt am bin si da tɛm de aw i go pafɛkt, ɛn i gi am di las tɔch dɛn we i tɔk bɔt insay in chapta we de tɔk bɔt Kɔmpozishɔn, ɛn dat mek di las wan nɔto lay lay tin ɛn nɔto tru ɔl. Di bad tin we dis kayn analisis kin du na dat i kin tɛmpt pɔsin we jɔs bigin fɔ tink se atifishal prɔses kin pas di imajineshɔn. Di impuls fɔ genius na fɔ gayd di sikrit dɛn fɔ in kriet awa. Glimpses obtained of di toil, di baffled experiments, we de bifo wan triumph, as in di sketch-work of Hawthorne we dɛn jɔs bring to layt, afford priceless instrɔkshɔn ɛn ɛnkɔrejmɛnt to di sincere artist. Bɔt wan we volontia fɔ mek ɔlman we de kam luk in Muse, fɔ mɛmba di tin we bin apin to Kiŋ Kandaul.

Di wɔl stil tink bɔt Poe as "laklɛs man we gɛt sɛns." I nɔ tu te yet we a yɛri dɛn tɔk bɔt am se "wan we ɔlman tan lɛk se dɛn chata fɔ misrɛpresent, decry ɔ slander."
