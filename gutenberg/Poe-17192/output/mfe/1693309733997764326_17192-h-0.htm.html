KOMANTER LOR POEM-LA.

Sekre enn poem, pa mwins ki prosperite enn jok, trouv dan zorey seki tann li. Sede devan so sor, aksepte lanbians poet-la: se seki bann saz reponn kan ou demann zot so valer. Mem si poet-la limem, dan so lot lanbians, dir ou ki so lar se zis enn trik, manze so ensanser manze, e ofer pou montre ou so trik, — pa kwar li. Atann so ler profetik; lerla donn twa so pasion, so lazwa ousa so douler. "Nou dan lame Lamour zordi!" sante Gautier, dan parafraz bien flotan Swinburne,—e depi gramatin ziska soley kouse nou pe anvole lor lamer violan: ena zis enn lamour, enn Me, enn sel brin fleri. Lamour li eternel, tou lezot zafer ireel e met de kote. Vizion la ena so bout, senn la sanze; me nou finn gagn kiksoz, memwar enn sarm. Otan poet, otan sarm. Ena sarm Evanesans, seki donn bote ek lagras siprem enn aureol Pathos. Partaz avek Landor so enn sel "lanwit souvenir ek soupir" pou Rose Aylmer, e ou ena sa net.

E asterla pran lame enn minstrel nouvo lemond, ki finn elwagn li depi enn abita apropriye pou al dan sa Lamerik grosie ek disonan ki, kouma Baudelaire ti trouve, "ti pou Poe zis enn gran prizon atraver ki li ti pe galoupe, isi-laba, avek azitasion febril enn etre ki finn kree pou respir dan enn lemond pli pir," e kot "so lavi interyer, spiritiel kouma enn poet, spiritiel kouma enn sou, ti mem sou zefor pou sape depi linflians sa latmosfer antipatik-la." Trap lame sansib enn santer boulverse ki pe dres so bizar koumsa, e partaz avek li klima dan lekel li finn trouve, — zame pandan lazourne, touletan dan lanwit, — si pa Atlantis kot li ti pe erle, omwin enn plas refiz depi bann limit kot li ti anferme lizour.

Zis dan enn sel pei li ena pouvwar pou amenn twa, e zis pou enn lanwit to kapav partaz so rev. Enn bout ni Later ni Lesiel: "Later personn," andeor Lespas, andeor Letan. Ala bann ki perturbe, atraver ki zot lizie, kouma seki bann Cenci, lam trouv lafnet mem si lespri etonnen; isi bann lespri, ki pe rod sime ki amenn ver Leternite, arete ek retarde. Se limbo bann "nam planeter," kot ena tou bann insertitid dan lalimier lalinn, tou bann lamour ek ilizion perdi. Isi ena ki fixe dan trans, sel repo ki kapav gagne; lezot

"bouz dan enn fason fantastik
 Pou enn melodi diskordan:"

alor ki partou ena

"Bann souvenir lor fey lepase—
 Bann form anvlope ki koumanse ek soupire
 Kouma zot pas par sa vagabon-la."

Telman pei-la ete, e pou enn lanwit nou rant ladan, — enn lanwit kot ena bann faz astral ek bann sonet ki revini. So bann monodi se douz poem, ki zot lamizik pe fer zefor pou sanze me ki touzour parey. Enn par enn zot sone, kouma son lorloz an kwiv ek an eben, dan "The Masque of the Red Death," ki finn fer bann vals fer enn poz avek "dekonserte ek tranbleman ek meditasion," osi souvan ki ler finn arive.

Parmi tou sa bann kadans mistik-la, plent Korbo , ki pe vibre atraver portay, finn prinsipalman inpresionn lemond andeor. Ki bann zafer al dan kreasion enn poem,—e kouma li vre dan sa, kouma dan laplipar lezot zafer, sa ras ki finn nom so bann bard "bann fabrikan"? Enn travay finn apele depi vid. Kot pa ti ena nanye, li res la, — enn nouvo kreasion, enn parti trezor limanite. Ek de-trwa parol exsepsionel, plis ki lezot ki egalman kreatif, obliz nou reflesi ankor kouma avek kouraz plim poet-la tourn bann zafer inkoni

"a bann form, e pa donn aere nanye
 Enn abitasion lokal, ek enn nom."

Sakenn paret san enn prototip, pourtan tou fasinn nou avek bann eleman ki finn tire depi lonbraz Sirnatirel. Aster limazinasion pli ot konsern lam bann kiksoz; li kapav ou pa kapav inspir Fantazi ki bann dimounn avek bann zimaz vag interliner. Pourtan, enn sa bann parol-la, dan so pli tipti fason, afekte nou avek enn santiman inik, osi sirman ki bann travay siblim enn lekip sirnatirel, — "Faustus" Marlowe, "Faust" Goethe, "Manfred," ou mem sa bann sef-d-ouvraz eterik, "The Tempest" ek "A MidSummers'." Plis ki enn, mem si li inik, ena inpe fardo ou refren ki antet memwar,—enn fwa tande, zame bliye, kouma ton enn lorg-stop ki rar servi me ki distinktif. Remarkab parmi se "Lenore" Bürger, sa balad fantom ek rezonan, latraksion ek foil bann tradikter. Bien tigit pou nie ki Coleridge so extraordiner "Rime of the Ancient Mariner" trouv dan zot latet. "Le Juif-Errant" ti pou ena revandikasion, si Beranger ti enn pli gran poet; e, me akoz zot elwagne depi sinpati popiler, "The Lady of Shalott" ek "The Blessed Damozel" kapav azoute a lalis. Sa finn donn Edgar Allan Poe pou prodir de parol, "The Bells" ek The Raven , sakenn ladan, mem si kitfwa li mwins zoli ki seki pou Tennyson ek Rossetti, li inik. "Ulalume," mem si li egalman drol ek imazinatif, li pena kalite iniversel ki form parti nou tes.

Korbo dan bann konstitian poetik tom anba bann morso kouma "The Haunted Palace," "The City in the Sea," "The Sleeper," ek "Israfel." Tousala pou esanz, mo soupsonne, par bann lekter enn lekip fastidie, pou bann pasaz kouma sa:

"Otour, par lev divan finn bliye,
 Demisione anba lesiel
 Bann dilo melankolik manti.
Okenn reyon depi lesiel sin pa desann
 Pandan long lanwit sa lavil-la;
 Me lalimier ki sorti depi lamer bien terib
 Bann kouran mont lor bann tourel an silans—
 · · · · · · ·
 Anler boukou ek boukou enn sanktyer merveye
 Ki so bann friz kouronne antrene
 Viol, violet, ek rezin.
 · · · · · · ·
 Okenn gonfleman pa dir ki divan kapav
 Lor enn lamer pli ere bien lwin—
 Pena okenn lever ki indike ki bann divan finn
 Lor bann lamer mwins sere."

Li mank melodi aerien poet ki so lakord leker se enn lyt:

« Ek zot dir (la lakoral zetwal
 Ek bann lezot zafer ki pe ekoute)
 Sa dife Israfeli-la
 Eski akoz sa lyre-la
 Par ki li asize ek sante—
 Difil vivan ki pe tranble
 Sa bann lakord insolit-la."

Me The Raven , kouma "The Bells" ek "Annabel Lee," felisit limem a boukou ek tigit. Mo finn dir enn lot plas ki bann prodiksion pli rar Poe finn paret mwa "bann prodiksion kot ena laparans, omwin, spontaneite, — kot li sede devan so bann santiman, pandan ki li pe mor ek bann kadans pli mizikal, pli melankolik, sorti depi li san ki li kone." Sa li touzour mo krwayans; e pourtan, lor enn nouvo letid sa poem-la, li inpresionn mwa plis ki ninport ki moman depi mo lanfans. Konesans pros rakont an faver sak vre travay lar. Insit sa misie-la, ki ni konn lar ni gagn traka, pou examinn enn poem ouswa enn tablo, e kouma so lafors pran li bien vit! Anfet, li pou tro estim valer relatif premie bon travay par ki finn ase atir so latansion. Korbo-la , li osi, ena bann kalite konsistan ki mem enn exper bizin admire. Dan okenn lezot poem so oter pena motif ki pli palpabman defini. "The Haunted Palace" li osi defini pou enn lekter selekte, me Poe pa ti pou pran sa alegori sibtil-la pou enn analiz bal. Korbo-la okipe net avek tem tipik loter-la—pert ireparab enn fam idole ek zoli; me lor lezot baz, osi, linstin piblik korek pou pans li so poem reprezantatif.

Enn zom zeni normalman gagn enn pied avek sikse enn sel zefor, e sa pa touletan so pli gran. Rekonesans li pli instantane pou finn ranvwaye. Li pa gagn li, kouma fortinn enn avar, pies apre pies, me "pa ditou ou tou an tou." Ek alor avek lezot anbision: kourtien, solda, akter, — ninport ki zot rol, — sakenn kont so triyonf apartir enn kout sans. Poe so Korbo, malgre li ti ena boner, ti pou li "zwazo ki ti fer labriz soufle." Poet-la finn etablir li dan New-York, dan liver 1844-'45, li finn rod travay lor papie Willis, "The Evening Mirror," e li finn gagn so reveni par bann kontribision lezot plas. Pandan sis an li ti enn ekrivin aktif, e li ti ena enn repitasion profesionel; ti ena respe ek mal dout, e li pa ti perdi so par dan zournalism mal peye sa lepok-la. Li finn osi fer boukou so bann meyer travay,—bann zistwar kouma "Ligeia" ek "The Fall of the House of Usher," (dernie-la kontenir sa omolog mistik, an verse, "A Lost Mind" par Elihu Vedder,) bann fe analitikal kouma "The Gold Bug" ek "The Mystery of Marie Roget." Li finn fer bann prozelit deor, e finn gagn enn kontrol dirab lor lespri bann Franse. Li ti finn aprann so prop pouvwar ek febles, e li ti dan so meyer moman, e pa san enn sertenn repitasion. Me li pa finn ekrir nanye ki ti lor lalang tou dimounn. A bann travay rar ek delika bizin azout enn ti tous popiler pou kapte lodians zeneral so prop lepok.

Atraver lindistri bann biograf siksesif Poe, sikse ki The Raven finn fer finn vinn enn zistwar ki souvan rakonte. Zenn fam poet-la, Virginia, ti pe disparet devan so lizie, me li finn res ankor enn an dan lonbraz lamor. Long lasam ba dan lakaz pre kot Bloomingdale Road li kouma seleb kouma lasam kot Rouget de l'Isle finn konpoz Marseillaise. Tou dimounn finn tande ki poem-la, avek sign "Quarles," finn aparet dan "American Review," avek enn komanter pseudo-editoryal lor so form; ki Poe finn gagn dis dolar pou sa; ki Willis, pli zanti ek mwin zalou parmi bann arbit lamod, finn re-inprim li avek enn eloz ki finn fer li vinn enn koze dan lavil deswit. Tou dout lor so loter finn disparet kan Poe finn resit li limem dan enn rasanbleman literer, e pandan enn tan li ti pli marke parmi bann oter Amerikin. Sa frap-la finn stimil li ek finn ankouraz li. Kouma enn lot satiris ek pli fier, li osi li finn trouv "enn kiksoz lete" mem "dan ronfleman bann insek." Malerezman, me trwa-z-an finn pase,—enn peryod linflians, lorgey, langwas, me touzour travay imazinatif ou kritik,—avan defet final, avan ki rido tom lor enn lavi ki pou li ti anverite enn trazedi, e li finn sede devan "Ver Konkerir."

"The American Review: A Whig Journal" ti enn magazinn kredib pou sa lepok-la, avek de kolonn, inprime lor bon papie avek tip kler, e ilistre par bann portre mezzotint. Parmi boukou zafer anba standar aktyel, li ti ena sertin ki ninport ki editer ti pou kontan resevwar. Volim inisial, pou 1845, ena bann lartik par Horace Greeley, Donald Mitchell, Walter Whitman, Marsh, Tuckerman, ek Whipple. Poem pittoresk Ralph Hoyt, "Old," finn aparet dan sa volim-la. Ek ala trwa parol par Poe: "Lavil dan Lamer," "Vale Dezord," ek Korbo . De ladan finn konstrir,—koumsamem so manier,—depi bann letid avan, me dernie-nom-la finn sorti koumadir finn fek konpoze, e preske kouma nou ena li aster. Deklarasion ki li pa finn revize apre li fos. Onz bann ti sanzman depi text magazinn-la aparet dan The Raven and Other Poems , 1845, enn liv ki poet-la finn bien vit santi li ankouraze pou ofer piblik. Sa se laplipar bann sanzman ponktiasion, ouswa bann mo tousel, dernie kalite-la finn fer pou ogmant lefe aliterasion. Dan zoli edision verse Poe par Misie Lang, ki finn sorti dan "Parchment Library," li finn montre linstin enn savan, e li finn fer avek sazes, kan li finn retourn lor text dan volim ki nou finn fek mansione, kouma finn done dan nimero Lond an 1846. Koleksion "standar" Griswold bann travay poet-la ranpli avek bann erer. Sa finn repete par bann editer plitar, ki finn osi fer zot prop erer. Me text The Raven , akoz bann demann ki finn fer a loter pou bann kopi maniskri, finn ankor plis revize par li; anfet, li finn inprim li dan Richmond, zis avan so lamor, avek ranplasman poetik "serafim ki so lipie tonbe" par "bann anz ki so lipie tonbe feb," dan katorziem strof. Nou text prezan, donk, mem si li sibstansielman text 1845, li inpe modifie par lektir poet-la plitar, e li, mo panse, version pli korek ek efikas sa sel poem-la. Sanzman pli radikal depi premie version finn aparet, sepandan, dan volim-la an 1845; onziem strof ti orizinalman kontenir sa bann lalinn-la, defo dan rim e otreman enn defo lor poem-la:

"Trape depi enn met malere, ki finn ena enn katastrof san pitye
 Swiv vit ek swiv pli vit—alor, kan Lespwar li ti pou fer serman,
 Dezespwar sever finn retourne, olie Lespwar dou ki li finn oze fer serman—
 Sa repons tris-la, 'Zame ankor!'"

Li ti pou bon si lezot, ek bann poet seleb, ti kapav osi sir ki zot bann sanzman touletan bann ameliorasion. Poe finn konstaman re-manier so ti spektak verse, e normalman li finn amelior li. Korbo ti premie parmi bann tigit poem ki li finn preske fini avan inprim li. Li posib ki bann ki gagn traka poezi finn perdi tigit par so lamor. Li ti bien konn proz, zame li finn ekrir bann verse zis pou fer enn poem. Kan enn refren zimaz ti anbet li, lirik ki ti rezilte ti linspirasion, kouma limem li ti dir, enn pasion, pa enn bi. Sa ti a bann interval telman rar ki preske pou zistifie teori Fairfield ki sakenn ti prodwi enn kriz nerve.

Ki, alor, finn donn poet-la so indis lor Korbo ? Depi ki fondasion brouyar li finn monte dousman-dousman ziska enn lamizik ki finn respire dousman-dousman? Kouma dabitid, plis ki enn zafer finn al dan konstriksion enn poem sitan notab. Kan nou konsider bann sermon pli long ki souvan prese lor bann text bref ek mwin sigzetif, mo espere ki pa pou blanm mwa pou sa diskision lor enn sel lirik-la, — sirtou enn ki enn artis kouma Doré finn fer size enn ilistrasion prodig. Ziska resaman mo ti sipoze ki sa morso-la, ek de-trwa ki so oter finn konpoze apre so laparision, ti exsepsionel parski zot pa finn grandi depi bann mikrob dan so verse garson. Me Misie Fearing Gill finn montre mwa bann strof ki pankor pibliye par Poe, ki finn ekrir dan so dizwitiem lane, e ki ti ena tit, "The Demon of the Fire." Li paret ki maniskri-la dan lekritir poet-la dan koumansman, e so otantisite finn garanti par lafami ki li finn res dan so posesion pandan enn demi-siek. Apart bann mikrob pli sinp "The Bells" ek "The Haunted Palace" li kontenir de-trwa lalinn ki inpe sigzestif lor louvertir ek fermtir The Raven . Konsernan ritm nou poem, enn konparezon bann dat indike ki sa finn inflianse par ritm "Lady Geraldine's Courtship." Poe ti enn parmi bann premie pou onor zeni Mis Barrett; li finn inskrir so bann poem kolekte pou li kouma "pli nob dan so sex," e li ti dan sinpati avek so metod lirik. Bann lalinn depi so poem-lamour,

"Ar enn boulversman mirmire enserten, dan ler, rido mov..."
 Gonfle ek gonfle otour so sousi pal ki pa bouze,"

finn trouv enn eko dan sa bann-la:

"Ek enn tapaz tris inserten kouma laswa sak rido mov
 Finn kontan mwa—finn ranpli mwa avek bann teroris fantastik ki zame mo finn resanti avan."

Isi Poe finn asim enn privilez pou ki li finn sansir Longfellow, e ki zame personn pa finn rode lor so prop landrwa san deteksion ek pinision rapid. Dan form melodi ek strof, nou pou trouve ki sa de poem-la pa diferan, me dan motif zot totalman distink. Sa poetes zenere-la pa finn resanti nanye apart vre orizinalite poet-la. "Sa lekritir bien vivan la!" li finn kriye,—"sa pouvwar ki nou resanti la!... Nou gran poet, Misie Browning, oter 'Paracelsus,' &c., li antouzias dan so ladmirasion pou ritm-la." Misie Ingram, apre ki li finn fer referans a "Lady Geraldine," avek malen fer resorti enn lot sours kot Poe kapav finn gagn enn inpilsion. An 1843, Albert Pike, poet demi-Grek, demi-frontier, depi Arkansas, ti inprim dan "The New Mirror," pou ki Poe ti pe ekrir sa lepok-la, sertin verse ki ti apel "Isadore," me depi sa loter-la finn revize e finn apel "The Widowed Heart." Mo finn swazir depi revizion Misie Pike sa strof-la, kot bann karakteristik prinsipal koresponn avek version orizinal:

"Enkyet mo mars dan nou lasam tousel, mo nepli zwe nou bann sante,
 Soley briyan pe briye dan enn fason flat lor plans ki pa finn balye;
 Sa zwazo moker-la ankor pe asize e pe sante, O souch melankolik!
 Parski mo leker kouma enn niaz lotonn ki deborde ar lapli;
 To finn perdi pou mwa pou toultan, Isador!"

La nou ena enn mezir prolonze, enn similarite refren, ek lintrodiksion enn zwazo ki so sante ogmant lapenn. Ena lezot santie ki kapav swiv par bann kirye; notaman, enn pasaz ki Misie Ingram swazir depi dernie revizion Poe lor "Barnaby Rudge":

"Korbo-la, li osi, * * * ti kapav finn fer, plis ki nou trouv li aster, enn parti konsepsion fantastik Barnaby. * * * So personaz ti kapav zwe, par rapor a seki enn bourik, preske mem par ki, dan lamizik, akonpagnman par rapor a ler."

Malgre sa, apre ki finn fer resorti sa bann mikrob ek resanblans-la, valer sa poem-la ankor trouve dan so orizinalite. Lamizik progresis, detay senbolik ek lalimier ek lonbraz kontraste,—sirtou, pasion spiritiel lanwit, fer li vinn travay enn zeni informatif. Konsernan sa zwazo terib-la, li pa kouma tou bann lezot korbo dan so klan, depi "twa corbies" ek "three ravens" bann baladis ziska "Grip" Barnaby. Ala okenn resanblans sa ronfleman ki anbet bann tourel anset e ki pe mars dan domenn heraldik; pena okenn fantom ki Tickell finn sante, kan,

"kriye lor so lafnet trwa fwa,
 Korbo-la finn bat so lezel,
 Tro bien sa zennfi ki ti pe perdi lamour-la ti kone
 Son sinal solanel."

Korbo Poe se enn konsepsion distink; inkarnasion enn soufrans ek dezespwar enn dimounn ki dan dey; enn sab ki ti inkarn Memwar, kroniker permanan destriksion, enn tip Ireparab. Sove atraver Styx, depi "laplaz Plutonien Lanwit," li paret nam imazine sa kestioner-la limem, — seki pa kapav, pa pou, bwar sa kalite nepenthe-la, parski memwar Lenore se tou seki finn res pou li, e avek surcease so sagrin mem sa pou met de kote.

Kapav osi pran Korbo kouma enn poem reprezantatif so oter, pou so lexanp tou so bann nosion lor seki enn poem bizin ete. Nou trouv sa dan so bann redaksion lor "Prinsip Poetik," "Rasional Verse," ek "Filozofi Konpozision." Poe finn deklare ki "dan Lamizik, kitfwa, lam preske ariv gran bi pou ki, kan li inspire par Santiman Poetik, li lite—kreasion Bote sipernal.... Vers pa kapav pli bien dezigne ki kouma enn lamizik inferyer ou mwin kapab"; me ankor enn fwa, verse ki vremem "Poezi bann Mo" se "Kreasion Ritmik Labote,"—sa ek nanye plis. Ton pli ot Bote se enn ton Tristes. Size pli melankolik se Lamor. Sa bizin alye ar Bote. "Lamor, alor, enn zoli fam se, san dout, size pli poetik dan lemond, — e egalman li andeor dout ki bann lalev ki pli adapte pou enn tel size se bann lalev enn amoure ki dan dey." Sa bann dernie expresion-la finn site depi analiz kaprisyez Poe lor sa poem-la mem, me zot indik presizeman ranze zeneral so verse. Klimax "The Bells" se monotonn moufle bann ghoul, ki glorifie pou pez leker imin. "Lenore," The Raven , "The Sleeper," "To One in Paradise," ek "Ulalume" form enn senfoni tenebroz,—ek "Annabel Lee," ki finn ekrir dernie, montre ki enn sel tem finn posed li ziska lafin. Ankor enn fwa, tousala pa nanye si pa mizikal, e ena ki touse par sa kalite Fantastik ki evey sans admirasion, e azout enn nouvo laper a lagoni limem. Atraver tou finn delinee dan enn fason feb, anba enn pal lonbraz, lamour ideal poet-la, — ki souvan demi-portre enn lot plas, — fam Usher ki finn antere, Lady Ligeia, dan laverite kontrepar so prop natir. Mo sipoze ki lamour enn artis pou enn "dan form" zame pa pou kapav rivaliz so devosion pou enn ideal. Fam ki pre ar li bizin exerse so bann sor, res tou par tour e pa long, sarm li avek enn varyete enfini, ouswa kontant li pou abandonn enn par so fidelite. Li bizin atire par seki pa kapav atenn, e sa li touzour andeor so pasion pou lar kreatif.

Poe, parey kouma Hawthorne, finn rant avek dekalaz lekol Romantik, e personn pa finn pli kontan ki li pou riy so maler. Pourtan so leker ti avek bann romanter ek zot bann lefe oriantal ouswa gotik. So linvansion, telman ris dan bann zistwar proz, ti paret abandonn li kan li ti ekrir bann verse; e so zizman ti dir li ki bann long poem romantik depann plis lor insidan ki lor linspirasion, — e ki, pou pronons poezi romantik, bann parol pou sifi. Alor so teori, ki klerman adapte ar so prop limit, ki "enn 'long poem' se enn kontradiksion plat an term." Bann konpozan The Raven bien tigit ek sinp: enn zom, enn zwazo, ek memwar fantastik enn fam. Me sa pies-la ofer enn zoli prezantasion materyel romantik. Ki nou ena? Minwi; lasam lonbraz avek so bann liv tradision ki finn bliye; etidian-la,—enn Hieronymus modern; robine korbo lor kazot; lanwit liver ek dife ki pe mor; bann pankart an laswa ki finn balye par divan; bann rev ek mefians vag dan marenwar ki pe fer echo; zwazo nwar, mazik lor so pwatrin pal; bann akseswar an velour violet ek lalanp ki pe vante. Tou sa lefe lasenn sitiasion, lalimier, kouler, son, li pirman romantik, e mem melodramatik, me li ena enn kalite poetik ki melodram bien rar expoze, e li reflet afon "pasion eternel, douler eternel" poet-la.

Striktir ritmik The Raven ti sirman pou fer enn linpresion. Rim, aliterasion, fardo, form strof, finn devlope avek enn abilite inik. Pena dout ki poet-la finn etone par apropriye bann trokaik mizikal Mis Barrett, dan "wit," e sirtou par laranzman ki finn adopte ver lafin "Lady Geraldine":

"'Lizie,' li finn dir, 'asterla pe bat dan mwa! Eski zot bann lizie ki finn detrir mwa?
 Lizie briyan, kouma bizou antik met dan ros-stati Parian!
 Anba sa fron blan kalm-la, eski zot touzour pe brile bien for
 O'er dezer disab dezole mo leker ek lavi debrouye?'"

So lintrodiksion artistik enn trwaziem rim dan deziem ek katriem lalinn toulede, ek azoutman enn sinkiem lalinn ek enn dernie refren, finn fer strof The Raven . Aliterasion persistan paret vinn san zefor, e souvan bann rim dan bann lalinn sedwir; alor ki refren ou fardo dominn travay-la an antie. Isi osi li finn profit letid Mis Barrett lor bann balad ek bann romant dan so prop lang ek lezot lang. Enn "refren" se lasenn avek ki enn poet ouswa enn mizisien tini so zorey ki pe vagabonde, — lanvi rekiran Lanatir pou so tension inisial. Mo finn touzour admir bann zoli refren sa santez Angle-la,—"The Nightingales, the Nightingales," "Margret, Margret," "My Heart and I," "Toll slowly," "The River floweth on," "Pan, Pan is dead," etc. Li finn osi itiliz seki mo apel Repetend, dan itilizasion Poece ki finn reviv tou pois Coleri sa:

"O bann vivan ere! pena lalang
 Zot bote kapav deklare:
 Enn lasours lamour ti fane depi mo leker,
 Mo finn beni zot san kone;
 Sirman mo sin zanti finn pran pitye pou mwa,
 Ek mo finn beni zot san kone."

Poe finn kre sinkiem lalinn so strof pou mazik repete. Li finn fie lor la ziska lafin dan de-trwa poem plitar,—"Lenore," "Annabel Lee," "Ulalume," ek "For Annie." Li finn gagn enn lamizik sovaz ek melankolik, mo finn panse, apartir bann "linflians dou," bann fardo ek bann repetision Afriken ki ti sant pou li dan lanfans, ki ti pe akord avek zot melodi natif lavwa nou poet Sid.

"The Philosophy of Composition," so analiz lor The Raven , se enn diseksion teknik so metod ek so striktir. Ni so deklarasion artifis san fre, ni so deklarasion ki finn swiv avek bann kamarad ki enn expozision sa artifis-la ti zis enn lot so bann fos intelektyel, bizin donn kredi net. Si li ti finn konsevwar travay-la an-antie alavans, li ti pou apenn fer enn preludi sitan dir avek bann rim rattle-pan pou melodi delisie deziem strof-la, — mem lor so teori lor fantastik. Biensir enn artis, kan li finn perfeksionn enn travay, li trouve, kouma premie Artis, ki li bon, e li trouve kifer li bon. Enn analiz ki swiv, ansam avek enn rezet ninport ki dife sakre, kapav fer ase fasilman. Mo krwayans se ki premie konsepsion ek proze brit sa poem-la finn vini kouma linspirasion touletan vini; ki so loter lerla finn trouve kouma li kapav perfeksione, donn li bann dernie tous ki finn dekrir dan so sapit lor Konpozision, e ki sa dernie-la, donk, li pa ni fos ni vre. Ditor enn tel analiz se ki li tant enn debitan pou panse ki bann prosesis artifisiel kapav ranplas limazinasion. Linpilsion zeni se pou vey bann sekre so ler kreatif. Bann apersi ki finn gagne lor travay dir, bann experimantasion dekonserte, ki presed enn triyonf, kouma dan travay kroki Hawthorne ki finn fek met an evidans, donn enn instriksion ek enn lankourazman inestimab pou sa artis sinser-la. Me enn ki volonterman expoz so Mize ar regar tou dimoun ki vini bizen rapel sor Lerwa Kandaules.

Lemond ankor pans Poe kouma enn "zom zeni malsans." Resaman mo finn tann li mansione kouma "enn ki tou dimounn paret anvi mal reprezante, kritike ouswa kalonmni." Me li paret mwa ki so maler finn fini par so lamor pitiab, e ki depi sa so defans finn persistan, e so selebrite finn grandi osi ferm ki enn oter soufer ek doue ti kapav priye pou li dan so ler lespwar. Griswold so kritik ek so kalonmni finn tourn kouran dan so faver. Bann kritik ek biograf finn vinn devan avek bann refutasion siksesif, avek bann omaz a so personaz, avek bann nouvo edision so bann travay. So bann prop let ek bann ti insidan so karyer devan nou; rikord-la, bon kouma move, bien koni. Okenn apeler pa finn gagn enn zizman pli tandres ek pardonan. So bann maler dan lavi ti apartenir a so rezion ek so peryod, kitfwa ankor plis a so prop febles volonte. Pena dout ki so lanvironnman pa ti enn lanvironnman pou protez enn natir bien fine ek mal ekilibre kont bann danze andeor ek andan. So volonte pli for, pou vinn sef limem, finn gagn pou li "sa leritaz maler-la." Li finn konfes limem met malere sa zwazo-la, soufer frape sa poem-la. Me so ti ena enn par konple sa tanperaman dramatik ki rezwir dan presaz so prop destriksion. Ena enn plezir zwe so gran rol: nou tou nou bann gladiater, pe kriye Ave Imperator! Pou sit fe Burke: "Dan lapenn plezir li ankor pli ot, e soufrans ki nou soufer pena okenn resanblans avek douler absoli, ki touzour degoutan, e ki nou fer zefor pou retire o pli vit posib." Poe finn al pli lwin, e li ti enn artis mem dan trazedi so karyer. Si, dapre so prop krwayans, tristes ek disparision bote se bann tem poetik pli ot, e santiman poetik se plezir teres pli for, alor lapenn ek marenwar so lavi kase pa ti san zot konpansasion souvan.

Dan bann paz ki swiv, nou ena enn nouvo lexanp zeni enn artis ki karakteriz so interpretasion enn poem seleb. Gustave Doré, dernie travay ki so kreyon devan nou, pa ti pint, ni mem desinn, pou bann realis ki ti pe dimann laverite lor ton, figir, ek perfeksion. Sa bann zafer-la ti konsern li mwins ki pou fer form ek distans, lalimier ek lonbraz, ed so bi, — ki ti pou exsit lam, limazinasion, dimounn ki pe gete. Sa li finn fer kan li finn lev nou santiman admirasion, atraver bann konsepsion extraordiner ek souvan siblim lor bann kiksoz ki pa kapav dir ek ranpli ar marenwar ouswa laglwar. Li bien dir ki si so bann travay pa ti bann gran tablo, kouma bann zimaz zot vremem gran. Antan ki enn "artis literer," e tel ki li ti ete, so lafors ti an rapor direk avek invansion dramatik so oter, avek bann odas brav lespri ki finn alim so prop lespri. Alor so sikse avek Rabelais, avek "Le Juif-Errant," "Les Contes Drolatiques," ek "Don Quixote," e alor, lekontrer, so defayans pou exprim bote bann Idyl Tennyson, "Il Paradiso," bann pastoral Ebre, ek lezot text ki demann exaltasion, ouswa douser ek repoz. Li ti enn met grotesk ne, e par enn lintelizans spesial li ti kapav portret bann spektak enn servo ante. Nou trouv bann obze kouma so bann personaz finn trouv zot, e avek lizie Zwif Erran, Don etonne, ouswa tifi forzron ki so lanvi telman magnifie Lerwa dan laboutik lor Pont-au-Change. Se dan natir bann zafer ki li ti bizin atire par sak sef-d-ouvraz verse ou proz ki mo finn apel inik. Bann rwayom pli ba ti apele dan so servis; so bann ros, pie ek montagn, lesiel limem, zot anime avek motif ek diablerie. Si li ti viv pou ilistre Shakespeare, nou ti pou trouv enn tretman remarkab Caliban, bann Sorsier, siklonn dan "Lear"; me sirman li ti bizin kestionn so bann ideal lor Imogen ouswa Miranda. Bote pir ek sinp, ek so exselans parfe, li ti paret bien rar konpran.

Pourtan ena bote dan so bann konsepsion pou "Ansien Marin," ireel mem si zot ete, ek enn konsekitivite rar dan enn seri par Doré. Rime-la finn donn li enn zistwar prolonze, avek boukou sanzman dan senn. Dan The Raven son ek kouler prezerv zot monotonn e nou pena okenn sanzman landrwa ou lokazion. Ki rezilta sa ena? Doré ofer enn seri varyasion lor tem-la kouma li finn konsevwar li, "enigm lamor ek alisinasion enn nam inkonsolab." Dan sertin sa bann desin-la so bann defo evidan; lezot revel so orizinalite pwisan, ek bann meyer kalite dan lekel, antan ki enn desiner, li ti debout tousel. Li kler ki ti ena kiksoz an komin ant lanbians travay Poe ek Doré. Sa ti pou paret pli kler si sa dernie-la ti sey so lame lor "Bann Kont Grotesk ek Arabesk." Toulede ti souvan al ver pei-elf fantastik ek romans. Dan bann fe melodramatik zot toulede, atraver zot komann sirnatirel, finn evit laliyn danze ant ideal ek absird. Poe ti pli vre adorater Zoli; so lamour pou li ti enn pasion konsakratif, e la li separe avek so ilistrater. Poet ou artis, Lamor finalman transfigir tou: dan lonbraz so presedan, Vedder so kreyon sinbolik met zot fas-a-fas, me anvlop zot avek manto sazes ek pouvwar imortel. Enn fam Amerikin finn fabrik zimaz enn Zeni avek lizie zetwal avek flanbo final, resanblans ekskiz enn kikenn ki so vizion trouv, me ki so lalev pa kapav pronons, bann mister enn pei andeor "laport enn tom lezand."

Edmund C. Stedman.

POEM-LA.


Ilistrasion 1


KORBO-LA.

Enn fwa dan enn minwi tris, letan mo ti pe reflesi, feb ek fatige,
 Lor boukou volim pittoresk ek kirye bann tradision ki finn bliye,
 Letan mo ti pe fer enn sign ar mo latet, pre pou dormi, enn kout ti ena enn tapaz,
 Kouma enn kikenn ki pe tap dousma, pe tap laport mo lasam.
 "Se enn viziter," mo finn mirmire, "pe tap laport mo lasam—
 Zis sa, ek nanye anplis."
Ah, mo rapel bien ki li ti dan Desam som,
 Ek sak labrez separe ki ti pe mor ti forz so fantom lor plans.
 Avek inpasians mo ti pe swete ki landime:—dan vid mo finn rod prete
 Depi mo bann liv ena sagrin ki sorti—sagrin pou Lenore ki finn perdi—
 Pou sa zennfi rar ek briyan ki bann anz apel Lenore—
 San nom isi pou toultan.
Ek enn tapaz tris inserten kouma laswa sak rido mov
 Finn kontan mwa—finn ranpli mwa avek bann teroris fantastik ki zame mo finn resanti avan;
 Alor ki aster, pou kalme batman mo leker, mo finn debout pe repete
 "Se enn viziter ki pe demann rant devan laport mo lasam
 Enn viziter an retar pe sipliy pou rant dan laport mo lasam;—
 Samem, ek nanye plis."
Deswit mo nam finn vinn pli for; ezite lerla nepli,
 “Misie,” mo ti dir, “ou Madam, vremem ou pardon mo sipliye;
 Me lefet se ki mo ti pe dormi, e telman dousman to finn vinn rape,
 Ek telman feb-feb ou finn vinn tap, tap laport mo lasam,
 Ki mo ti apenn sir mo finn tann twa"—ala mo finn ouver laport gran;—
 Nwar laba, ek nanye plis.
Dan fon sa marenwar-la pe gete, lontan mo finn debout laba pe demann momem, pe per,
 Doute, reve bann rev ki okenn mortel pa finn oze reve avan;
 Me silans-la pa ti kase, e marenwar pa ti donn okenn sign,
 Ek sel mo ki finn dir laba se mo ki finn mirmire, "Lenore!"
 Sa mo finn mirmire, e enn eko finn reponn mo-la, "Lenore!"
 Zis sa ek nanye plis.
Retourn dan lasam tourne, tou mo nam andan mwa pe brile,
 Bien vit ankor enn fwa mo finn tann enn tapaz, inpe pli for ki avan.
 "Sirman," mo finn dir, "sirman sa se kiksoz kot mo lasenn lafnet;
 Les mwa gete, alor, ki ete ladan, e sa mister-la explore—
 Les mo leker res kalm enn moman e sa mister-la explore;—
 'T se divan ek nanye plis!"
Ouver isi mo finn zet volan, kan, avek boukou flert ek flote,
 Laba ti rant enn Korbo detase bann zour sin lontan.
 Li pa finn fer enn tipti lazenes; pa enn minit finn arete ouswa res li;
 Me, avek enn figir senyer ou madam, poz lao laport mo lasam—
 Apoz lor enn bis Pallas zis lao laport mo lasam—
 Asize, ek asize, ek nanye plis.
Lerla sa zwazo eben-la pe atir mo fantazi tris pou fer enn sourir,
 Par dekor grav ek sever so figir ki li ti ena,
 "Mem si to krest finn koupe ek raze, twa," mo finn dir, "to sir to pa enn fou,
 Enn Korbo terib ek ansien ki pe vagabonde depi laplaz Nightly,—
 Dir mwa kouma to nom lor laplaz Plutonien Lanwit!"
 Korbo-la dir, "Zame ankor."
Mo finn bien etone pou sa zwazo malprop-la pou tann enn diskour sitan kler,
 Mem si so repons ti ena tigit sinifikasion—ti ena tigit pertinans;
 Parski nou pa kapav pa dakor ki okenn imin vivan
 Zame finn beni pou trouv zwazo lao laport so lasam—
 Zwazo ou bebet lor bust skilte lao laport so lasam,
 Avek enn nom kouma "Zame ankor."
Me Korbo, ki ti pe asize tousel lor so lestoman kalm, ti koz zis
 Sa enn sel mo-la, koumadir so nam dan sa enn sel mo-la li finn devers li.
 Nanye pli lwin ki li finn prononse—pa enn plim ki li finn flote—
 Ziska ki mo finn apenn plis ki mirmire, "Lezot kamarad finn anvole avan—
 Lelandime li pou kit mwa, kouma mo ti pe espere avan."
 Lerla zwazo-la ti dir, "Zame ankor."
Etone par silans ki finn kase par enn repons ki finn koze dan enn fason apropriye,
 "San dout," mo finn dir, "seki li prononse se so sel stok ek magazin,
 Finn trape par enn met malere ki finn ena enn katastrof san pitye
 Swiv vit ek swiv pli vit ziska ki so bann sante enn fardo finn sarye—
 Ziska bann dey so Lespwar ki fardo melankolik finn sarye
 Depi 'Zame—zame ankor.'"
Me Korbo-la ankor pe atir tou mo nam tris pou fer enn sourir,
 Drwat mo finn roul enn sez kousen divan zwazo ek pwatrinn ek laport;
 Lerla, kan velours-la finn koule, mo finn koumans fer lien
 Fantezi a fantezi, panse ki sa zwazo menasan lontan-la—
 Ki sa zwazo som, degoutan, terib, meg ek menasan lontan-la
 Savedir "Zame ankor."
Sa mo finn asize pe devine, me pena okenn silab ki pe exprim
 Pou zwazo ki so lizie dife aster finn brile dan nwayo mo lestoma;
 Sa ek plis ankor mo finn asize pe devine, avek mo latet alez pe alonze
 Lor doublir velours kousen ki lalimier lalanp ti pe briye lor la,
 Me ki so revwetman violet velours avek lalimier lalanp ki pe vante par lao
 Li pou pez, ah, zame ankor!
Lerla, mo finn panse, ler finn vinn pli danse, parfime depi enn sanser invizib
 Balans par bann serafin ki zot lipie ti pe tonbe lor plans touf.
 "Malere," mo finn kriye, "to Bondie finn pret twa — par sa bann anz-la li finn avoy twa
 Repo—repo ek nepenthe depi to bann souvenir Lenore!
 Quaff, oh quaff sa kalite nepenthe la, e bliye sa Lenore perdi la!"
 Korbo-la dir, "Zame ankor."
"Profet!" mo finn dir, "zafer lemal!—profet ankor, si zwazo ou diab!—
 Ki Tanter finn avoy twa, ouswa ki siklonn finn zet twa isi lor laplaz;
 Dezole me tou san per, lor sa later dezer ansense-la—
 Lor sa lakaz par Laper ante—dir mwa laverite, mo sipliye—
 Eski ena— eski ena balm dan Galaad?—dir mwa—dir mwa, mo sipliye!”
 Korbo-la dir, "Zame ankor."
"Profet!" mo finn dir, "zafer lemal—profet ankor, si zwazo ou diab!
 Par sa Lesiel ki kourbe lao, nou—par sa Bondie ki nou toulede adore—
 Dir sa nam-la avek lapenn sarze si, dan Aidenn bien lwin,
 Li pou trap enn zennfi sin ki bann anz apel Lenore—
 Atrap enn zennfi rar ek briyan ki bann anz apel Lenore."
 Korbo-la dir, "Zame ankor."
"Sa mo-la vinn nou sign separasion, zwazo ou demon!" Mo finn kriye, ankoler—
 "Retourn twa dan siklonn ek laplaz Plutonien Lanwit!
 Pa les enn plim nwar kouma enn sign sa mansonz ki to nam finn dir la!
 Les mo solitid san kase!—kit sa kas lao mo laport!
 Tir to labek depi mo leker, e tir to form depi mo laport!"
 Korbo-la dir, "Zame ankor."
Ek Korbo-la, zame li pe anvole, ankor pe asize, ankor pe asize
 Lor bis pal Pallas zis lao laport mo lasam;
 Ek so lizie paret koumadir enn demon ki pe reve;
 Ek lalimier lalanp lor li ki pe koule zet so lonbraz lor plans;
 Ek mo nam depi sa lonbraz ki pe flote lor plans-la
 Pou leve—zame ankor!
