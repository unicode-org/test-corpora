Yɛ eŋmalɔ lɛ lalai krokomɛi amli lɛ, atsɔɔ yiŋtoo ni yɔɔ mli lɛ mli jogbaŋŋ fe enɛ. "The Haunted Palace" lɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ kɛha nikanelɔ ni ahala lɛ, shi kulɛ Poe kɛ nakai abɛbua ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ baatsu nii kɛpɛi mli. Raven lɛ kɛ ehe fɛɛ ewo woloŋmalɔ lɛ saneyitso ni afɔɔ namɔ lɛ mli—yoo ko ni ajáa lɛ amagai ni he yɔɔ fɛo ni elaaje ni anyɛɛɛ aku sɛɛ ajie lɛ; shi yɛ yiŋtoi krokomɛi anɔ lɛ, maŋbii lɛ asusumɔi lɛ ja yɛ susumɔ ni amɛsusuɔ akɛ eji enajiaŋdamɔlɔ lala lɛ mli.

Bei pii lɛ, gbɔmɔ ni yɔɔ nilee lɛ naa shidaamɔ kɛtsɔ mɔdɛŋbɔɔ kome ko omanyeyeli nɔ, ni jeee be fɛɛ be enɛ ji nɔ ni fe fɛɛ ni eyɔɔ. Nɔ ni ayɔseɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni baa amrɔ nɔŋŋ fe fɛɛ akɛ ajie kɛtee sɛɛ. Enaaa, tamɔ mɔ ko ni bɛ shika, shika yɛ shika sɛɛ, shi "jeee kwraa loo fɛɛ." Ni nakai eji yɛ hiɛdɔɔi krokomɛi ahe: maŋtsɛwe lɛ mli onukpa lɛ, asraafonyo lɛ, sini mli nifeelɔ lɛ, —bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni amɛgbɛfaŋnɔi ji lɛ, —mɔ fɛɛ mɔ kaneɔ e-kunimyeli lɛ kɛjɛɔ shweremɔ ko mli. Poe Raven, yɛ augury lɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, eji kɛha lɛ "okpo ni haa kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ tswaa." Lalafolɔ lɛ tee New-York, yɛ fɛ̃i be mli yɛ afi 1844-'45, ni ena nitsumɔ yɛ Willis wolo, "The Evening Mirror," ni ekɛ shika ni ená lɛ tsu nii kɛtsɔ onia ni ekɛhaa yɛ hei krokomɛi lɛ nɔ. Afii enumɔ nɛ ni ekɛfee niŋmalɔ ni tsuɔ nii waa, ni ená gbɛi kpakpa yɛ nitsumɔ mli; afee lɛ yɛ bulɛ kɛ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ ni ejaaa fɛɛ mli, ni ebɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko yɛ egbɛfaŋnɔ yɛ adafitswaa nitsumɔ ni awooo lɛ nyɔmɔ jogbaŋŋ yɛ nakai gbi lɛ mli lɛ hewɔ. Etsu enitsumɔi ni hi fe fɛɛ lɛ hu, —tamɔ saji tamɔ "Ligeia" kɛ "Usher Shia lɛ Shigbeemɔ," (naagbee nɔ lɛ hiɛ nakai teemɔŋ sane lɛ, yɛ kuku mli, ni ji Elihu Vedder "Jwɛŋmɔ ni Elaaje,") nibii amlipɛimɔ nitsumɔi tamɔ "Shika Bug" kɛ "Marie Roget Teemɔŋ Sane lɛ." Efee mɛi ni etsake amɛhe yɛ maŋsɛɛ, ni ená hewalɛ ni hiɔ shi daa yɛ Frenchbii lɛ ajwɛŋmɔ mli. Ekase lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehewalɛ kɛ egbɔjɔmɔ, ni eyɛ ehiɛyaa mli, ni jeee akɛ ebɛ gbɛi pɔtɛɛ ko. Shi eŋmako nɔ ko ni yɔɔ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ lilɛi nɔ. Esa akɛ akɛ nɔ ko ni ehe gbɛi afata nitsumɔ ni anáaa waa ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ he koni ekɛmɔ toibolɔi babaoo ni yɔɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ebeaŋ lɛ.

Kɛtsɔ Poe wala he saji aŋmalalɔi ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ anitsumɔ nɔ lɛ, lala ni The Raven lɛ fee lɛ ebatsɔ sane ko ni afɔɔ he wiemɔ. Lalafolɔ lɛ ŋa oblayoo fioo, Virginia, miiho yɛ ehiŋmɛii ahiɛ, shi ehi shi afi kroko yɛ gbele ŋmɔlɔ mli. Tsu kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ ni yɔɔ shĩa ni bɛŋkɛ Bloomingdale Gbɛ lɛ mli lɛ ji tamɔ ni ehe gbɛi waa akɛ tsu ni Rouget de l’Isle ŋma Marseillaise lala lɛ yɛ mli lɛ. Mɛi fɛɛ enu akɛ lala lɛ, ni akɛ enine wo shishi akɛ "Quarles," lɛ je kpo yɛ "American Review" lɛ mli, ni amale niŋmalɔ wiemɔ ko yɛ bɔ ni eyɔɔ ha lɛ he; akɛ Poe nine shɛ dɔlai nyɔŋma nɔ yɛ he; akɛ Willis, ni ji mɔ ni mli hi fe fɛɛ ni ehiɛɛɛ kojolɔi ni yɔɔ fashion lɛ ahe awuŋa lɛ, kala lɛ ekoŋŋ kɛ yijiemɔ wiemɔ ko ni ha maŋ lɛ mli wiemɔ amrɔ nɔŋŋ. Yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ fɛɛ ni kɔɔ mɔ ni ŋma lɛ he lɛ je kpo beni Poe diɛŋtsɛ kane yɛ woloŋlee he kpee ko shishi lɛ, ni yɛ be ko mli lɛ, lɛ ji mɔ ni akadi lɛ fe fɛɛ yɛ Amerika woloŋmalɔi ateŋ. Hit lɛ kanya lɛ ni ewo lɛ hewalɛ. Tamɔ mɔ kroko ni kɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ nɔ lɛ, lɛ hu ena "hulutsoo be mli nɔ ko" yɛ "kooloi agbɛɛmɔ mli." Awrɛho sane ji akɛ, shi afii etɛ ho, — hewalɛ be, henɔwomɔ, piŋmɔ, shi be fɛɛ be lɛ, susumɔ loo nitsumɔ ni mli wa, — dani naagbee kunimyeli lɛ, dani muhɔɔ lɛ ba shi yɛ shihilɛ ko ni yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ eji awerɛho sane kɛha lɛ lɛ nɔ, ni ekɛ ehe ha "Kunimyelɔ Worm lɛ."

"The American Review: A Whig Journal" ji wolo tɛtrɛɛ ko ni ajieɔ yi yɛ nakai beaŋ, ni yɔɔ ŋwɛigbɛi enyɔ, ni akala yɛ wolo kpakpa nɔ ni niŋmaa ni mli ka shi faŋŋ yɔɔ nɔ, ni akɛ mezzotint mfoniri fee he mfoniri. Yɛ saji babaoo ni baa shi fe ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ shishitoo mla lɛ ateŋ lɛ, ekomɛi yɛ mli ni woloŋmalɔ fɛɛ woloŋmalɔ baaná he miishɛɛ akɛ enine ashɛ nɔ. Shishijee wolokpo lɛ, kɛha afi 1845 lɛ yɛ saji ni Horace Greeley, Donald Mitchell, Walter Whitman, Marsh, Tuckerman, kɛ Whipple ŋma. Ralph Hoyt lala ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni ji, "Old," lɛ je kpo yɛ wolo nɛɛ mli. Ni Poe lalai etɛ nɛ: "Maŋ ni yɔɔ Ŋshɔ lɛ Mli," "Basabasafeemɔ Jɔɔ lɛ," kɛ The Raven . Atswa enɛɛmɛi ateŋ enyɔ ama shi, —tamɔ bɔ ni efee lɛ, —kɛjɛ tsutsu nikasemɔi amli, shi naagbee nɔ ni atsɛɔ lɛ lɛ je kpo tamɔ nɔ ni aŋma ehee, ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaafee tamɔ bɔ ni wɔyɔɔ amrɔ nɛɛ. Wiemɔ ni akɛɛ akɛ asaaa mli yɛ no sɛɛ lɛ ejaaa. Tsakemɔi bibii nyɔŋma kɛ ekome ni jɛ wolo tɛtrɛɛ lɛ mli lɛ je kpo yɛ The Raven and Other Poems , 1845, wolo ko ni etsɛɛɛ ni lalafolɔ lɛ nu he akɛ awo lɛ hewalɛ ni ekɛha maŋbii lɛ mli. Enɛɛmɛi babaoo ji tsakemɔi ni afeɔ yɛ wiemɔ mli okadii amli, loo wiemɔi komekomei amli, ni ji naagbee nɔ ni afee koni akɛha wiemɔi ni akɛtoɔ wiemɔi ahe lɛ aya hiɛ. Yɛ Mr. Lang Poe kuku lɛ fɛfɛo lɛ mli, ni ajie lɛ kpo yɛ "Parchment Library" lɛ mli lɛ, ejie woloŋlelɔ ko su kpo, ni ekɛ nilee tsu nii, yɛ niŋmaa ni yɔɔ wolo ni atsĩ tã kɛkɛ nɛɛ mli lɛ mli ni eku esɛɛ kɛtee mli lɛ mli, taakɛ akɛha yɛ London wolo ni je kpo yɛ afi 1846 lɛ mli lɛ. Sɛɛ mli woji aŋmalalɔi lɛ etĩ enɛɛmɛi amli, ni amɛ hu amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛtɔ̃. Shi The Raven , lɛ mli niŋmaa lɛ, yɛ nibimɔi ni abi woloŋmalɔ lɛ koni ekɛ niji ŋmala lɛ ekomɛi aha lɛ hewɔ lɛ, lɛ ji mɔ ni tsake mli lolo; yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, ekala yɛ Richmond, dani egbo, ni ekɛ lalafoo wiemɔ ni ji "seraphim ni enaji-shwieɔ shi" lɛ to "ŋwɛibɔfoi ni enaji-shwieɔ shi ni bɛ hewalɛ" lɛ najiaŋ, yɛ kuku ni ji nyɔŋma kɛ ejwɛ lɛ mli. No hewɔ lɛ, wɔ ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ niŋmaa lɛ, yɛ be mli ni eji 1845 nɔ lɛ, lalafolɔ lɛ sɛɛ mli nikanemɔ lɛ tsake lɛ yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ, ni eji, misusuɔ akɛ, lala kome nɛɛ shishitsɔɔmɔ ni ja ni tsuɔ nii jogbaŋŋ fe fɛɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, tsakemɔ ni fe fɛɛ ni jɛ shishijee shishitsɔɔmɔ lɛ mli lɛ je kpo yɛ wolokpo lɛ mli yɛ afi 1845; stanza ni ji nyɔŋma kɛ ekome lɛ shishijee mli lɛ, nɛkɛ kukuji nɛɛ yɔɔ mli, ni etɔ̃ yɛ rhyme mli, ni kɛ jeee nakai lɛ, eyɛ tɔmɔ ko yɛ lala lɛ he:

"Amɔ nuŋtsɔ ko ni bɛ miishɛɛ, ni mɔbɔnalɛ bɛ ehe Oshara."
 Enyiɛ sɛɛ oya ni enyiɛ sɛɛ oya—no hewɔ lɛ, beni Hope baakã kitã lɛ, .
 Stern Despair ku esɛɛ eba, yɛ Hiɛnɔkamɔ ni ŋɔɔ ni ekɛ ekãa wo kitã lɛ najiaŋ—
 Hetoo ni yɔɔ awerɛho lɛ, 'Dabi dɔŋŋ!'"

Ebaafee nɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ kɛji lalafolɔi krokomɛi, ni ehe gbɛi lɛ baanyɛ aná nɔmimaa akɛ amɛtsakemɔi lɛ baaha amɛya hiɛ be fɛɛ be. Poe tsakeɔ kukuji fioo ni ejieɔ lɛ kpo lɛ mli ekoŋŋ be fɛɛ be, ni bei pii lɛ, ehaa efeɔ nɔ ni hi fe tsutsu lɛ. The Raven ji lalai fioo ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaagbe naa dani ekala lɛ ateŋ klɛŋklɛŋ nɔ. Ekolɛ mɛi ni kwɛɔ lalafoo wiemɔi anɔ lɛ laaje nɔ ko fioo yɛ egbele lɛ hewɔ. Akɛni ewieɔ wiemɔi jogbaŋŋ hewɔ lɛ, eŋmaaa kukuji kɔkɔɔkɔ koni ekɛfee lala. Beni mfoniri ko ni ajieɔ lɛ kpo lɛ haoɔ lɛ lɛ, lala ni jɛ mli ba lɛ ji mumɔ ni tsirɛɔ lɛ, taakɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ekɛɛ lɛ, suɔmɔ ko, shi jeee yiŋtoo ko. Enɛ baaa shi yɛ be kɛ bei amli bɔ ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaabu Fairfield susumɔ lɛ ja akɛ eko fɛɛ eko jɛ shoofeemɔ mli haomɔ ko mli ba.

Belɛ, mɛni ha lalafolɔ lɛ ná The Raven ? Mɛɛ shishitoo ni mli ewo afua lɛ mli ejɛ etee ŋwɛi fiofio kɛtee lala ko ni amuɔ fiofio mli? Taakɛ eji daa lɛ, nɔ ni fe ekome tee lala ni sa kadimɔ waa lɛ maa mli. Kɛ misusu shiɛmɔi kakadaji ni bei pii lɛ ashiɛɔ yɛ niŋmai kukuji ni bɛ susumɔi babaoo lɛ anɔ lɛ he lɛ, mihiɛ ka nɔ akɛ akafolɔ mi fɔ yɛ lala kome he sanegbaa nɛɛ he, —titri lɛ nɔ ni nitɛŋlɔ ko tamɔ Doré kɛfee nɔkwɛmɔnɔ ni fiteɔ nii lɛ he sane lɛ. Kɛbashi nyɛsɛɛ nɛɛ misusu akɛ nɛkɛ lala nɛɛ, kɛ fioo komɛi ni eŋmalɔ lɛ ŋma beni eje kpo lɛ sɛɛ lɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ srɔto yɛ bɔ ni amɛjɛɛɛ helatsɛmɛi amli helai ni gbeɔ mɔ lɛ mli yɛ ekuku ni ji gbekɛ nuu lɛ mli lɛ hewɔ. Shi Mr. Fearing Gill etsɔɔ mi Poe lalai komɛi ni akalako, ni eŋma yɛ e-afii nyɔŋma kɛ kpaanyɔ mli, ni eyitso ji, "La Daimonio lɛ." Etamɔ nɔ ni wolo ni akɛ niji ŋmala lɛ yɛ lalafolɔ lɛ mra be mli niŋmaa lɛ mli, ni weku ni ehi amɛdɛŋ afii ohai fã lɛ ji nɔ ni maa bɔ ni eji anɔkwale lɛ nɔ mi. Kɛfata helai ni yɔɔ faŋŋ ni yɔɔ "The Bells" kɛ "The Haunted Palace" lɛ he lɛ, eyɛ wiemɔi fioo komɛi ni tsɔɔ The Raven lɛ shishijee kɛ naagbee. Yɛ wɔ lala lɛ lala lɛ he lɛ, gbii ni akɛtoɔ he lɛ tsɔɔ akɛ "Lady Geraldine's Courtship" lala lɛ ná enɛ nɔ hewalɛ. Poe ji mɛi klɛŋklɛŋ ni wo Miss Barrett nilee lɛ hiɛ nyam lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome; eŋmala elalai ni ebua naa lɛ ewo lɛ akɛ "ebɔlɛnamɔ mli nɔ ni nɔ kwɔ fe fɛɛ," ni eyɛ mli musuŋtsɔlɛ kɛha e-lyrical gbɛ lɛ. Wiemɔi ni yɔɔ esuɔmɔ-lala lɛ mli lɛ,

"Kɛ huhuuhu gbee ni bɛ faŋŋ, yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli, purple muhɔɔ lɛ."
 Efuu kɛbote mli ni efu kɛje kpo yɛ ehiŋmɛi tsuru ni tsiii ehe lɛ he,"

na echo yɛ enɛɛmɛi amli:

"Kɛ siliki awerɛho ni bɛ shiŋŋ ni yɔɔ purple muhɔɔ lɛ eko fɛɛ eko mli lɛ."
 Eha mi miishɛɛ—ekɛ gbeyeishemɔ ni minuko he dã eyi mi obɔ."

Biɛ ji he ni Poe ŋɔ hegbɛ ko ni ekɛ mlifu wie eshi Longfellow yɛ he, ni mɔ ko mɔ ko etaooo yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ eshikpɔŋ nɔ pɛŋ ni anaaa lɛ oya ni atsɔseee lɛ. Yɛ lala kɛ stanzaic gbɛ nɔ lɛ, wɔbaana akɛ lalai enyɔ lɛ jeee nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ amɛhe, shi yɛ yiŋtoo hewɔ lɛ, amɛsoro amɛhe kwraa. Lalafolɔ yoo ni feɔ mɛi ejurɔ lɛ nuuu nɔ ko nɔ ko he fe lalafolɔ lɛ shishijee diɛŋtsɛ. "Nɛkɛ niŋmaa ni mli ka shi faŋŋ nɛɛ!" ebolɔ akɛ,—"hewalɛ ni anuɔ he nɛɛ!... Wɔ lalafolɔ kpeteŋkpele, Mr. Browning, ni ŋma 'Paracelsus,' &c., yɛ miishɛɛ yɛ lala lɛ he miishɛɛ mli." Mr. Ingram, beni ewie "Lady Geraldine" he sɛɛ lɛ, ekɛ ŋaa tsɔɔ jɛɛhe kroko ni ekolɛ Poe ná henumɔ ko kɛjɛ mli. Yɛ afi 1843 lɛ, Albert Pike, Helanyo fã, husu-naanyo fã, lalafolɔ ni jɛ Arkansas lɛ, ekala yɛ "The New Mirror," ni Poe ŋmaa lɛ mli, kukuji komɛi ni yitso ji "Isadore," shi kɛjɛ nakai beaŋ kɛbaa nɛɛ niŋmalɔ lɛ tsake ni etsɛ lɛ "Okulafo Tsui." Mihalaa stanza ni nyiɛ sɛɛ nɛɛ kɛjɛɔ Mr. Pike tsakemɔ lɛ mli, ni emli nibii titrii lɛ kɛ shishijee nɔ lɛ kpãa gbee:

"Mibɛ hejɔɔmɔ minyiɛ wɔ tsuji amli, mi tswaaa wɔ lalai lɛ dɔŋŋ,
 Hulu ni tsoɔ waa lɛ kpɛɔ yɛ shikpɔŋ ni atsumɔko nɔ lɛ nɔ kɛ hiɛdɔɔ;
 Okpo ni yeɔ mɔ he fɛo lɛ ta shi lolo ni eláa, O awerɛhoyeli tsɔne!
 Ejaakɛ mitsui tamɔ fɛ̃i be mli atatu ni nugbɔ nɛɔ waa;
 Olaaje mi kɛya naanɔ, Isadore!"

Wɔyɛ susumɔ ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ, lala ni akɛtswaa lɛ ni tamɔ amɛhe, kɛ okpo ko ni elala lɛ haa awerɛhoyeli yaa hiɛ lɛ hiɛkpamɔ yɛ biɛ. Gbɛjegbɛi krokomɛi yɛ ni mɛi ni taoɔ amɛle nɔ ni yaa nɔ lɛ baanyɛ atsɔ nɔ; nɔ ni sa kadimɔ ji, kuku ni Mr. Ingram hala kɛjɛ Poe naagbee niiamlipɛimɔ ni kɔɔ "Barnaby Rudge" he lɛ mli:

"Akpãa lɛ hu, * * * ekolɛ afee, fe bɔ ni wɔnaa amrɔ nɛɛ, Barnaby naakpɛɛ lɛ susumɔ lɛ fã ko. * * * Esubaŋ lɛ baanyɛ afee, yɛ kwashiai ahe, fã kome too lɛ nɔŋŋ ni etsuɔ, yɛ lala mli, ni fataa he yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ he."

Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, beni atsɔɔ nɛkɛ helai ni gbeɔ mɔ kɛ nibii ni tamɔ amɛhe nɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, anaa sɛɛnamɔ ni yɔɔ lala nɛɛ he lɛ lolo yɛ bɔ ni ejɛ eshishijee mli eha lɛ mli. Lalai ni yaa hiɛ, nibii ni yɔɔ fɛo lɛ amli fitsofitso kɛ la kɛ hulutsoo ni yɔɔ srɔto lɛ, —nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, mumɔŋ suɔmɔ ni yɔɔ gbɛkɛ mli lɛ haa efeɔ nilelɔ ko ni haa aleɔ nii lɛ nitsumɔ. Yɛ okpo ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ gbɛfaŋ lɛ, esoro lɛ kwraa yɛ eweku lɛ mli okpoi krokomɛi lɛ fɛɛ ahe, kɛjɛ "twa corbies" kɛ "okpoji etɛ" ni ji balladists lɛ anɔ kɛyashi Barnaby "Grip" ni egbɔjɔ lɛ nɔ. Biɛ ji he ko ni tamɔ cawing rook ni shwieɔ blematsɛmɛi atsuji lɛ anɔ ni enyiɛɔ heraldry ŋmɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ; nɔ ko bɛ ni tsɔɔ akɛ Tickell la akɛ, beni,

"ebolɔ yɛ esamflɛ mli shii etɛ,
 Kpokpaa lɛ tswa etsitsi,
 Oblayoo ni suɔmɔ efite lɛ lɛ le jogbaŋŋ tsɔ
 Gbɛɛmɔ ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ."

Poe ŋshɔŋloo lɛ ji susumɔ ko ni yɔɔ srɔto; mɔ ni yeɔ awerɛho lɛ piŋmɔ kɛ hiɛnɔkamɔ ni bɛ lɛ he gbɔmɔtso; a sable embodied Memory, ni ji hiɛkpatamɔ lɛ he saji aŋmalalɔ ni hiɔ shi daa lɛ, ni ji Nɔ ni Anyɛɛɛ Atsake lɛ henɔ. Ejo foi kɛtsɔ Styx lɛ nɔ, kɛjɛ “Gbi lɛ Plutonian ŋshɔ lɛ naa,” etamɔ nɔ ni eji mɔ ni biɔ lɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ susuma ni afee lɛ mfoniri lɛ, —mɔ ni nyɛŋ, enyɛŋ, ekɛ nepenthe ni mli hi lɛ atsu nii, ejaakɛ Lenore kaimɔ ji nɔ pɛ ni eshwɛ lɛ, ni kɛ awerɛhoyeli lɛ sɛɛ fo lɛ, abaawo no po awo he kroko.

Abaanyɛ aŋɔ Raven lɛ hu akɛ eji lala ni damɔ shi kɛha eŋmalɔ lɛ, yɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ ni etsɔɔ esusumɔi fɛɛ ni kɔɔ nɔ ni esa akɛ lala afee lɛ he lɛ hewɔ. Anaa enɛɛmɛi yɛ ewolo ni kɔɔ "Lala Shishitoo Mla lɛ," "Kuku Yiŋtoo," kɛ "Nilee ni kɔɔ lala he lɛ mli." Poe jaje akɛ "yɛ Lala mli lɛ, ekolɛ, susuma lɛ bɛŋkɛɔ naagbee kpeteŋkpele ni, kɛji akɛ Poetic Sentiment lɛ tsirɛ lɛ lɛ, ebɔɔ mɔdɛŋ—ŋwɛi Hefɛo lɛ bɔɔ.... Anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ kuku lɛ jogbaŋŋ fe akɛ eji lala ni baa shi loo ni nyɛɛɛ atsu nii jogbaŋŋ"; shi ekoŋŋ lɛ, kuku ni ji "Wiemɔi Ahe Lala" diɛŋtsɛ lɛ ji "Hefɛo ni Abɔ yɛ Rhythm nɔ,"—enɛ kɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ fe nakai. Hefɛo ni nɔ kwɔ fe fɛɛ lɛ gbɛɛmɔ ji Awrɛhoyeli nɔ. Saji otii lɛ ateŋ nɔ ni yɔɔ awerɛho fe fɛɛ ji Gbele. Esa akɛ enɛ kɛ Hefɛo afee ekome. "No hewɔ lɛ, yoo fɛfɛo ko gbele ji, ŋwanejee ko bɛ he, lala saneyitso ni fe fɛɛ yɛ je lɛŋ, - ni ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ naabu ni sa jogbaŋŋ kɛha saneyitso ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ji suɔlɔ ni egbo lɛ nɔ." Atsɛ naagbee wiemɔi nɛɛ asɛɛ kɛjɛ Poe lala nɛɛ diɛŋtsɛ mlipɛimɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ mli, shi amɛtsɔɔ bɔ ni ekuku lɛ mli saji lɛ fa ha pɛpɛɛpɛ. Nɔ ni fe fɛɛ yɛ "The Bells" lɛ mli ji ghouls lɛ agbɛɛmɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo, ni woɔ amɛhe hiɛ nyam yɛ adesa tsui ni tsiimɔ yɔɔ mli lɛ mli. "Lenore," The Raven , "Mɔ ni Wɔɔ," "Kɛha Mɔ Ko yɛ Paradeiso," kɛ "Ulalume" feɔ tenebrose lala, —ni "Annabel Lee," ni aŋma naagbee lɛ tsɔɔ akɛ saneyitso kome hiɛ lɛ kɛyashi naagbee. Shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ jeee nɔ ko kɛji jeee lala, ni nakai su ni ji Fantastic lɛ ni teɔ gbeyeishemɔ henumɔ shi lɛ saa mɛi komɛi ahe, ni ekɛ gbeyeishemɔ hee fataa piŋmɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ he. Atsɔɔ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ mli fioo, yɛ ŋmɔtɔ ko shishi, lalafolɔ lɛ suɔmɔ ni sa jogbaŋŋ lɛ, —ni bei pii lɛ ajieɔ lɛ kpo yɛ fã kroko mli lɛ, —Usher ŋa ni afu lɛ, Lady Ligeia, yɛ 1914 mli. anɔkwale lɛ ji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ esubaŋ lɛ he nɔkwɛmɔnɔ. Misusuɔ akɛ suɔmɔ ni nitɛŋlɔ yɔɔ kɛha mɔ ko "ni yɔɔ su mli" lɛ nyɛŋ ekɛ ehetuu-kɛhamɔ ni eyɔɔ kɛha susumɔ ko lɛ aye akaŋ kwraa kɔkɔɔkɔ. Esa akɛ yoo ni bɛŋkɛ lɛ lɛ atsu e-spɛɛji lɛ ahe nii, ni ekɛ ehe awo mli ni ekɛ nɔ ko akafee nɔ ko ni sɛɛ tsɛɔ, ekɛ srɔtoi ni bɛ naagbee afee lɛ fɛo, loo etsui anyɔ emli akɛ ebaaŋmɛ e-anɔkwayeli lɛ fã ko gbɛ. Esa akɛ Nɔ ni Anyɛɛɛ Ashɛ He lɛ alaka lɛ, ni enɛ fe lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ yɛ suɔmɔ ni eyɔɔ kɛha nibii ahe nitsumɔ mli.

Poe, tamɔ Hawthorne, ba kɛ Romantic skul lɛ shigbeemɔ, ni mɔ ko mɔ ko náaa miishɛɛ fe lɛ akɛ ebaaŋmɔ eshara lɛ he. Shi kɛlɛ etsui yɛ suɔmɔ he nilelɔi lɛ kɛ amɛ Bokagbɛ loo Gothic nibii ni amɛfeɔ lɛ ahe. Etamɔ nɔ ni nɔ ni efee, ni saji ni aŋmala ashwie shi lɛ eyi mli obɔ lɛ, shi lɛ beni eŋmala kukuji lɛ; ni ekojomɔ lɛ kɛɛ lɛ akɛ suɔmɔ he lalai kakadaji damɔ nibii ni ba lɛ anɔ fe mumɔ tsirɛmɔ, —ni akɛ, koni awie suɔmɔ he lalai lɛ, lalai lɛ amli wiemɔi lɛ baafee nɔ ni fa. No hewɔ lɛ esusumɔ lɛ, ni kɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe husui lɛ kpãa gbee, akɛ "'lala kakadaŋŋ' ji nɔ ni teɔ shi ewoɔ ehe yɛ wiemɔi amli." Nibii ni yɔɔ The Raven lɛ mli lɛ faaa ni amɛhe ehiaaa: nuu, okpo, kɛ yoo ko he kaimɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ. Shi fã lɛ haa ajieɔ suɔmɔ he saji akpo jogbaŋŋ. Mɛni wɔyɔɔ? Gbi teŋ; tsu ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ kɛ e-tomes ni ji lore ni hiɛ ekpa nɔ lɛ; nikaselɔ lɛ, —ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ Hieronymus ko; ŋshɔŋloo lɛ pɔmpi ni yɔɔ casement lɛ nɔ lɛ; fɛ̃i be mli gbɛkɛ kɛ la ni miigbo; siliki ni kɔɔyɔɔ shwieɔ nɔ lɛ; lamɔi kɛ hekɛnɔfɔɔ ni bɛ faŋŋ ni yɔɔ duŋ ni miitswa lɛ mli lɛ; okpo diŋ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni yɔɔ tsitsi ni mli tse lɛ nɔ lɛ; nibii ni akɛwoɔ mli ni akɛ violet velvet fee kɛ kane ni tsoɔ lɛ. Shihilɛ mli nibii nɛɛ fɛɛ, la, su, gbee, ji suɔmɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ, kɛ melodramatic po, shi eji lala su ni melodrama ejieee lɛ kpo, ni ejieɔ lalafolɔ lɛ "naanɔ suɔmɔ, naanɔ piŋmɔ" lɛ kpo jogbaŋŋ.

Bɔ ni ato The Raven lɛ he gbɛjianɔ aha lɛ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ faŋŋ akɛ ebaaha mɛi aná he miishɛɛ. Akɛ ŋaa kome pɛ fee rhyme, alliteration, jatsu lɛ, stanzaic henɔ lɛ. Ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ lalafolɔ lɛ naa kpɛ ehe yɛ bɔ ni Miss Barrett lalai ahe saji lɛ sa ha lɛ he, yɛ "ekpaa mli," ni titri lɛ gbɛjianɔtoo ni akɛtsu nii beni ebɛŋkɛ "Lady Geraldine" naagbee lɛ:

"'Hiŋmɛii,' ekɛɛ, 'amrɔ nɛɛ miitswa kɛmiitsɔ mimli! Ani nyɛji hiŋmɛii ni jie mi?
 Hiŋmɛii ni kpɛɔ, tamɔ blema jwetrii ni akɛwo Parian amaga-tɛ mli!
 Yɛ nakai hiɛ yɛŋ ni yɔɔ kpoo lɛ shishi lɛ, nyɛshãa la waa daa
 O'er mitsui kɛ wala ŋã ni efee amaŋfɔ lɛ efo mli?'"

Ekɛ ŋaa nitsumɔ ni ji rhyme ni ji etɛ ni yɔɔ line ni ji enyɔ kɛ ejwɛ lɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ je shishi, kɛ line ni ji enumɔ kɛ naagbee lala ni ekɛfata he lɛ, ha The Raven lala lɛ bafee stanza . Etamɔ nɔ ni wiemɔi ni akɛtoɔ he ni yaa nɔ lɛ baa ni mɔdɛŋbɔɔ ko bɛ he, ni bei pii lɛ, wiemɔi ni yɔɔ wiemɔi amli lɛ lakaa mɔ; yɛ be mli ni refrain loo jatsu lɛ yeɔ nitsumɔ muu lɛ fɛɛ nɔ. Biɛ hu ji he ni ená Miss Barrett ballads kɛ romanuts nikasemɔ yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ewiemɔi kɛ ewiemɔi krokomɛi amli lɛ he sɛɛ. "Refrain" ji lakamɔ ni lalafolɔ loo lalafolɔ kɛmɔɔ toi ni nyiɛɔ lɛ, —Adebɔɔ shwelɛ ni yaa nɔ shii abɔ kɛha shishijee tsɔne lɛ. Mikɛ Blɔfo lalafolɔ lɛ lalai fɛfɛji lɛ ahe miishɛɛ be fɛɛ be,—"Nightingales, Nightingales," "Margret, Margret," "Mi kɛ Mitsui," "Toll blɛoo," "Faa lɛ hoɔ kɛyaa nɔ," "Pan, Pan egbo," kɛ ekrokomɛi. tee lɛ shi ekoŋŋ:

"O wala yibii ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ! lilɛi bɛ."
 Amɛhefɛo lɛ baanyɛ ajaje akɛ:
 Suɔmɔ nubu ko jɛ mitsui mli, .
 Ni mijɔɔ amɛ ni mileee;
 Eji anɔkwale akɛ mi krɔŋkrɔŋ ni mli hi lɛ na mi mɔbɔ,
 Ni mijɔɔ amɛ ni mileee."

Poe fee e-stanza lɛ mli kuku ni ji enumɔ kɛha majik ni ji sɛɛkuu lɛ. Ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ nɔ kɛyashi naagbee yɛ sɛɛ mli lalai fioo komɛi amli,—"Lenore," "Annabel Lee," "Ulalume," kɛ "Kɛha Annie." Ená lala ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli, ni misusu akɛ, kɛjɛ "hewalɛi ni ŋɔɔ," ni ji Afrika jatsui kɛ lalai ni afɔɔ feemɔ ni ala aha lɛ yɛ egbekɛbiiashi lɛ mli, ni ekɛ amɛmaŋ lala lɛ kɛ wɔ Anaigbɛ lalafolɔ lɛ gbee lɛ kpãa gbee.

"Nilee ni kɔɔ niŋmaa he lɛ," epɛimɔ ni efee yɛ The Raven , ji ŋaa gbɛ ni atsɔɔ nɔ atsuɔ he nii kɛ bɔ ni ato he gbɛjianɔ aha lɛ mligbalamɔ. Ehe ehiaaa ni ajie yijiemɔ akɛ ejie ehe kpo akɛ ekɛ lá ni mli ejɔ tsuuu nii, loo esɛɛ ni ekɛɛ enanemɛi akɛ nɛkɛ ŋaa nɛɛ kpojiemɔ ji enilee mli amalei lɛ ateŋ ekome pɛ. Eji etsɔ hiɛ eto nitsumɔ muu lɛ fɛɛ he gbɛjianɔ kulɛ, ewaaa akɛ ebaafee rattle-pan rhymes ni kɛ lala ni ŋɔɔ ni yɔɔ stanza ni ji enyɔ lɛ mli lɛ hiɛkpamɔ ni mli wa tamɔ nɛkɛ, —ni ekɛ etsɔɔmɔ ni kɔɔ naakpɛɛ nii lɛ he lɛ po tsuuu nii. Eji anɔkwale akɛ nitɛŋmɔ he nilelɔ, ni etsu nitsumɔ ko he nii emuu lɛ, naa, tamɔ klɛŋklɛŋ Niteŋlɔ lɛ, akɛ ehi, ni enaa nɔ hewɔ ni ehi lɛ. Abaanyɛ afee mlipɛimɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ, kɛ la krɔŋkrɔŋ fɛɛ la krɔŋkrɔŋ ni akpooɔ lɛ oya bɔ ni sa. Mihemɔkɛyeli ji akɛ, klɛŋklɛŋ susumɔ kɛ lala nɛɛ he niŋmaa ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ ba tamɔ bɔ ni mumɔ tsirɛmɔ baa be fɛɛ be lɛ; akɛ no sɛɛ lɛ eŋmalɔ lɛ na bɔ ni abaanyɛ aha eye emuu, ni ekɛ naagbee nibii ni atsɔɔ mli yɛ eyitso ni kɔɔ Niŋmaa he lɛ ha lɛ, ni no hewɔ lɛ, naagbee nɔ lɛ jeee amale kwraa loo anɔkwale kwraa. Nɔ ni yeɔ mɔ awui yɛ mlipɛimɔ ni tamɔ nɛkɛ he ji akɛ, ekaa mɔ hee lɛ koni esusu akɛ nibii ni akɛfee lɛ baanyɛ aye susumɔ najiaŋ. Nɔ ni tsirɛɔ nilee ji ni ebu teemɔŋ saji ni kɔɔ ebɔɔ ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ he lɛ he. Nibii ni anáa kɛjɛɔ nitsumɔ ni wa mli, kaai ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli, ni tsɔɔ kunimyeli hiɛ, taakɛ eji yɛ Hawthorne mfonirifeemɔ nitsumɔ ni ajie lɛ kpo nyɛsɛɛ nɛɛ mli lɛ haa gbɛtsɔɔmɔ kɛ hewalɛwoo ni jara wa waa kɛhaa mfonirifeelɔ ni yɔɔ anɔkwayeli lɛ. Shi ekome ni jɛɔ etsui mli ejieɔ e-Muse lɛ kpo etsɔɔ mɛi fɛɛ ni baa lɛ akai nɔ ni ba Maŋtsɛ Candaules nɔ lɛ.

Je lɛ susuɔ Poe he lolo akɛ "gbɔmɔ ni bɛ shweremɔ ni yɔɔ nilee." Etsɛko tsɔ ni minu akɛ atsĩ etã akɛ "mɔ ni etamɔ nɔ ni mɔ fɛɛ mɔ aha lɛ gbɛ koni ekɛ ehe awo mli yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ, efolɔ enaa loo egbe ehe guɔ." Shi etamɔ nɔ ni misusuɔ akɛ ehiɛ ni ehiii lɛ ba naagbee kɛ egbele ni yɔɔ mɔbɔ lɛ, ni akɛ kɛjɛ nakai beaŋ kɛbaa nɛɛ, efãmɔ lɛ etee nɔ ehi shi, ni egbɛi ni ehe gbɛi akɛ edalɛ ni ma shi shiŋŋ tamɔ bɔ ni woloŋmalɔ ni yeɔ amanehulu ni yɔɔ nikeenii lɛ baanyɛ asɔle yɛ ehiɛnɔkamɔ ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ mli lɛ. Griswold heguɔgbee kɛ eheguɔgbee lɛ ha ŋmɛnɛŋmɛnɛ beaŋ shihilɛ lɛ tsake ehiɛ. Mɛi ni wieɔ shiɔ saji kɛ wala he saji aŋmalalɔi eba kɛ shitee-kɛ-woo ni nyiɛɔ sɛɛ, kɛ yijiemɔ kɛha esubaŋ, kɛ enitsumɔi lɛ ahe woji heei. Lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ewoji kɛ nibii bibii ni tee nɔ yɛ eshihilɛ mli lɛ yɛ wɔhiɛ; niŋmaa lɛ, ekpakpa kɛ efɔŋ, ji nɔ ni ale waa. Mɔ ko mɔ ko ni kɛ sane lɛ ba lɛ enine eshɛko kojomɔ ni mli jɔ ni kɛ eshai keɔ nɔ fe enɛ nɔ. Eshihilɛ mli osharai lɛ ji ekpokpaa lɛ kɛ ebe lɛ nɔ, ekolɛ nɔ ni fe nakai ji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ esuɔmɔnaa nii ni bɛ hewalɛ lɛ. Ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ shihilɛ ni eyɔɔ mli lɛ jeee nɔ ni baabu adebɔɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni ejaaa lɛ he kɛjɛ osharai ni yɔɔ kponɔgbɛ kɛ emli lɛ ahe. Esuɔmɔnaa nii ni mli wa fe fɛɛ, ni ji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe nuŋtsɔ lɛ, ná "nakai gboshinii ni ji haomɔ" lɛ eha lɛ. Ejaje ehe akɛ eji okpo lɛ nuŋtsɔ ni bɛ miishɛɛ, mɔ ni lala nɛɛ mli piŋmɔ ni etswa lɛ lɛ. Shi enɔ lɛ ji nakai mlifu ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni miishɛɛ yɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehiɛkpatamɔ lɛ he gbalɛ mli lɛ mli gbɛfaŋnɔ ni yeɔ emuu. Miishɛɛ yɛ mɔ ko gbɛfaŋnɔ ni nɔ kwɔ ni eshwɛɔ lɛ mli: wɔ fɛɛ wɔji gladiators, ni wɔfoɔ Ave Imperator! Kɛ wɔbaatsɛ Burke sane lɛ mli lɛ: "Yɛ awerɛhoyeli mli lɛ, miishɛɛ ji nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lolo, ni piŋmɔ ni wɔnaa lɛ kɛ piŋmɔ ni yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ kpaaa gbee, ni ji nɔ ni yɔɔ nyaŋemɔ be fɛɛ be, ni wɔbɔɔ mɔdɛŋ ni wɔjie kɛjɛ mli oya bɔ ni wɔɔnyɛ." Poe tee shɔŋŋ, ni eji nitɛŋlɔ yɛ eshihilɛ mli awerɛhoyeli lɛ po mli. Kɛji akɛ, taakɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehemɔkɛyeli tsɔɔ lɛ, awerɛhoyeli kɛ fɛo ni laajeɔ lɛ ji lala mli saji otii ni nɔ kwɔlɔ fe fɛɛ, ni lala mli henumɔ ji shikpɔŋ nɔ miishɛɛ ni mli wa fe fɛɛ lɛ, belɛ awerɛhoyeli kɛ duŋ ni yɔɔ eshihilɛ ni ekumɔ lɛ mli lɛ jeee nɔ ni amɛhe nyɔmɔwoo shii abɔ lɛ bɛ mli.

Yɛ baafai ni nyiɛ sɛɛ nɛɛ amli lɛ, wɔyɛ nɔkwɛmɔnɔ hee ko ni tsɔɔ nitɛŋlɔ ko nilee ni tsɔɔ bɔ ni etsɔɔ lala ko ni ehe gbɛi waa lɛ shishi ehaa lɛ mli. Gustave Doré, ni e-pensil naagbee nitsumɔ lɛ kã wɔhiɛ lɛ jeee mfonirifeelɔ, loo mfonirifeelɔ po, kɛha nibii ni yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ ni biɔ anɔkwale ni kɔɔ gbee, mfoniri, kɛ emuuyeli he lɛ. Saji ni tamɔ nɛkɛ kɔɔɔ ehe fe ni eeefee bɔ ni eyɔɔ ha kɛ he ni eyɔɔ lɛ, la kɛ hulutsoo, ni eye ebua eyiŋtoo lɛ, —ni ji ni ekanya mɔ ni kwɛɔ lɛ susuma, ejwɛŋmɔ lɛ. Efee enɛ kɛtsɔ wɔ gbeyeishemɔ henumɔ lɛ ni etee lɛ shi lɛ nɔ, kɛtsɔ naakpɛɛ nibii ni bei pii lɛ amɛsusuɔ nibii ni anyɛɛɛ awie he ni eyi kɛ duŋ loo anunyam lɛ ahe susumɔi anɔ. Akɛɛ jogbaŋŋ akɛ kɛji akɛ enitsumɔi lɛ jeee mfonirii wuji lɛ, akɛ mfonirii lɛ amɛji wuji diɛŋtsɛ. Ákɛ "woji ahe nilelɔ," ni nakai eji lɛ, ehewalɛ lɛ kɛ ewoloŋmalɔ lɛ naakpɛɛ nifeemɔ lɛ kpãa gbee tɛ̃ɛ, kɛ mumɔ ni yɔɔ ekãa ni shã lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enɔ lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ omanye ni eye yɛ Rabelais mli, kɛ "Le Juif-Errant," "Les Contes Drolatiques," kɛ "Don Quixote," kɛ no hewɔ lɛ, nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ enɛ lɛ, enyɛɛɛ ejie Tennyson Idyls, ni ji "Il Paradiso," ni ji Hebri tookwɛlɔi lɛ ahe fɛo lɛ kpo, kɛ niŋmai krokomɛi ni biɔ loo shweremɔ, kɛ shweremɔ. Afɔ lɛ akɛ eji mɔ ko ni he esa waa yɛ nibii ni yɔɔ gbeyei amli, ni kɛtsɔ sɛɛyoomɔ krɛdɛɛ ko nɔ lɛ, ebaanyɛ ejie jwɛŋmɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei ni yɔɔ gbeyei lɛ kpo. Wɔnaa nibii tamɔ bɔ ni egbɔmɛi lɛ na amɛ lɛ, kɛ Yudanyo ni Kpãa Shi lɛ, Don ni eyiŋ efutu lɛ, loo shika tsurufeelɔ lɛ biyoo ni ehiɛdɔɔ haa Maŋtsɛ lɛ hiɛ nyam waa lɛ hiŋmɛii diɛŋtsɛ yɛ shwapo ni yɔɔ Pont-au-Change lɛ mli lɛ. Nibii ahe su mli ji he ni esa akɛ ekɛ ehe awo kuku loo wiemɔ kuku ni mitsɛɔ lɛ akɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ srɔto lɛ eko fɛɛ eko mli. Atsɛ maŋtsɛyelii ni yɔɔ shishigbɛ lɛ koni amɛbasɔmɔ lɛ; etɛi, tsei kɛ gɔji, ŋwɛi diɛŋtsɛ, yɛ wala kɛ yiŋtoo kɛ diablerie. Eji ehi shi koni ekɛfee Shakespeare he mfoniri kulɛ, esa akɛ wɔna bɔ ni akɛ Caliban, Abonsam lɛ, ahum lɛ ye yɛ "Lear" lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ ni sa kadimɔ waa nɔ; shi ŋwanejee ko bɛ he akɛ kulɛ esa akɛ ebi esusumɔi ni kɔɔ Imogen loo Miranda he lɛ he ŋwane. Hefɛo ni he tse ni yɔɔ mlɛo, kɛ emli nɔ ni hi jogbaŋŋ ni yeɔ emuu lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni enuuu shishi tsɔ.
