AMBA PA POEME.

Tshisokomenu tshia tusala, katshiyi tshipuekele ku dipeta dia tuseku, tshidi mu ditshi dia muntu udi utshiteleja. Itaba tshishima tshiatshi, itaba lungenyi lua mufundi wa tusala: etshi, kunyima kua bionso, ke tshidi bena meji bandamuna paudi ubakonka mushinga watshi. Nansha mudi mufundi wa tusala nkayende, mu lungenyi luende lukuabu, ukuambila ne: mudimu wende wa bianza udi anu bu budimu, biakudia bia muena nkindi wende wa biakudia, ne udi ukuambila bua kukuleja budimu bua bualu ebu, —kumuitabuji to. Bindila dîba diende dia buprofete; pashishe udifile ku dijinga diende, ku disanka diende anyi ku kanyinganyinga kende. "Tudi mu tshianza tshia Dinanga lelu!" Gautier udi wimba, mu tshikoso tshia Swinburne,—ne kubangila mu dinda too ne ku dibuela dia dîba tudi tuenda tuendakana pa mbuu wa tshikisu: kudi anu dinanga dimue, ngondo muitanu umue, tshitupa tshimue tshia bilongo. Dinanga didi dia kashidi, bionso bikuabu kabiena bia bushuwa ne biteka ku luseke. Tshikena kumona tshidi ne ndekelu, tshikena kumona tshidi tshishintuluka; kadi tudi bapeta tshintu kampanda, tshivulukilu tshia tshishima. Bu mudi bafundi ba tusala ba bungi, nunku bipedi bia bungi. Kudi tshipedi tshia Evanescence, tshidi tshifila bulengele bua bungi ne ngasa wa aureole wa Pathos. Udi mua kuambila Landor "butuku buende bua kuvuluka ne bua kuela mbila" bua Rose Aylmer, ne udi ne bualu ebu buonso.

Ne mpindieu angata tshianza tshia muena mizike wa mu buloba bupiabupia, mupambuke ku muaba kampanda muimpe wa kusombela muye mu Amerike wa tshikisu ne wa tshikisu uvua, bu muvua Baudelaire mumone, "uvua bua Poe anu buloko bunene buvuaye wenda unyema, apa ne aku, ne tshiji tshikole tshia muntu mufukibue bua kupuyakana mu buloba bukezuke," ne muaba uvua "nsombelu wende wa munda, bu mupele wa mu nyuma, nansha bu muena maluvu wa mu nyuma, kadi uvua mukuatshike kashidi kudienzeja bua kunyema buenzeji bua tshinyangu etshi." Kuata tshianza tshia muimbi udi ne lutatu udi wela meji a mushindu eu, ne umanyishe tshikondo tshivuaye mupete,—kakuyi nansha kakese mu munya, misangu yonso butuku,—bikalabi kabiyi Atlantis uvuaye mupatuke, nansha muaba wa tshinyemenu ku mikalu ivuaye mubuikila mu munya.

Udi ne bukole bua kukulombola anu ku buloba bumue, ne anu butuku bumue ke udi mua kuabanya tshilota tshiende. Tshitupa tshia buloba anyi tshia diulu: "Buloba bua muntu nansha umue," pambelu pa tshibuashibuashi, pambelu pa tshikondo. Mona aba badi ne tunyinganyinga, ku mêsu abu, bu mudi a ba Cenci, anyima udi upeta madidishi nansha mudi lungenyi lunyanguke; apa nyuma, idi ikeba njila udi ufikisha ku tshiendelele, idi mimanyibue ne mipangile. I limbo wa "mioyo ya pa buloba," mudi malu onso adi kaayi malelela a ngondo, dinanga dionso dijimija ne malu a dishima. Apa bamue badi bashindame mu tshinyongopelu, dikisha dimuepele didibu mua kupeta; bakuabu

"kuenda bimpa
 Ku musambu udi kauyi umvuangana:"

padi miaba yonso

"Malu a kale adibu bavuluke mu mabeji—
 Mifuanu mibuikila idi ituadija ne ipuekesha
 Padibu bapita muena tshimuangi."

Ke mudi buloba, ne bua butuku bumue tudi tubuelamu,—butuku bua bikondo bia mitoto ne biombelu bidi bipingana. Mifundu yende idi ne tusala dikumi ne tubidi, idi mijiki yayi idienzeja bua kushintuluka kadi idi anu ya muomumue. Dimue ku dimue didi diumvuika, bu dikuma dia dîba dia tshiamu tshia mitaku ne dia ébène, mu "The Masque of the Red Death," divua dienza bua bena waltz bimane ne "dibungama ne dikankamika ne dielangana meji," misangu yonso pavua dîba difika.

Mu mishindu yonso eyi ya tshishima, tshiona tshia The Raven , tshidi tshienda tshinyunguluka ku tshiibi, tshidi nangananga tshikemeshe bantu ba pambelu. Mmalu kayi adi aya ku dienza dia tusala,—ne mmunyi mudibi bilelela mu bualu ebu, bu mudi mu malu makuabu a bungi, tshisamba atshi tshivua tshibikile ba-bard batshi ne: “benji”? Mudimu udi ubikidibua mu tshitupa tshia tshianana. Muaba uvuaku kakuyi tshintu, tshidi tshishala,—tshifukibua tshipiatshipia, tshitupa tshia tshiuma tshia bantu. Ne mêyi makese a pa buawu, kupita makuabu adi ne lungenyi lua muomumue, adi atusaka bua kuela meji tshiakabidi mudi tshipenyi tshia mufundi wa tusala tshishintulula malu adi kaayi mamanyike ne dikima dionso

"ku mifuanu, ne udi upesha lupepele tshintu nansha tshimue
 Muaba wa kusombela, ne dina."

Tshionso tshidi tshimueneka katshiyi ne tshilejilu, kadi bionso bidi bitusankisha ne bintu biangatshila mu mundidimbi wa malu a pa mutu. Mpindieu lungenyi lua pa mutu ludi lutangila anyima wa bintu; bidi mua kuikala anyi kabiyi mua kufila lungenyi lua ne: bantu badi ne bimfuanyi bia munkatshi mua ngondo bidi kabiyi biumvuika bimpe. Nansha nanku, umue wa ku miaku eyi, mu mushindu wende mukese, udi utulenga ne lungenyi lua pa bualu, anu mudi midimu ya dikema ya tshisumbu tshia bantu ba pa buabu,—“Faustus” wa Marlowe, “Faust” wa Goethe, “Manfred,” anyi mene misambu ya dikema ayi, “The Tempest” ne “A MidSummer Dreams.” Bipite pa tshimue, nansha mudibi bia pa buabi, bidi ne amue bujitu anyi tshiona tshidi tshitamba kuvuluka,—musangu umue mumvue, kayi muanji kupua muoyo, bu dîyi dia tshisanji tshia orgue tshidi katshiyi tshienza mudimu bikole kadi tshidi tshishilangane. Tshidi tshitamba kumueneka munkatshi muabi ntshia "Lenore" wa Bürger, musambu wa nyuma ne wa mbila, udi ukoka ne udi upangisha bakudimunyi. Bantu bakese ke badi mua kubenga ne: "Rime of the Ancient Mariner" wa dikema wa Coleridge udi ku mutu kuabu. "Le Juif-Errant" uvua mua kuikala ne malu, bu Beranger muikale mufundi munene wa tusala; ne, kadi bua mudibu kule ne luse lua bantu, "Mukaji wa Shalott" ne "Damozel mubeneshibue" badi mua kusakidila ku liste. Bavua bapeshe Edgar Allan Poe bua kupatula mêyi abidi, "The Bells" ne The Raven , yonso wa kudiyi, nansha mudiye kayi muimpe kupita wa Tennyson ne Rossetti, udi wa pa buende. "Ulalume," nansha mudiye wa dikema ne wa lungenyi, kena ne ngikadilu wa pa buloba bujima udi tshitupa tshia diteta dietu to.

Mukanda wa Raven mu miaku ya tusala udi muinshi mua bitupa bu mudi "Nzubu wa bukalenge udi ne nyuma mibi," "Musoko udi mu mbuu," "Mulale," ne "Israfel." Bionso ebi bidi mua kushintuluka, ndi ngela meji, kudi babadi ba tshisumbu tshia bantu badi ne lungenyi lua bungi, bua bitupa bu mudi ebi:

"Kunyunguluka, ku dibandisha dia lupepele lua kupua muoyo,
 Kulekela muinshi mua diulu
 Mâyi a kanyinganyinga adi mashimi.
Kakuena munya udi ufuma mu diulu dia tshijila udi upueka to
 Mu tshikondo tshile tshia butuku tshia mu tshimenga atshi;
 Kadi butoke budi bufumina mu mbuu mutoke
 Misulu idi ibanda pa bimanu bia tshibumba mu musokoko—
 · · · · · · ·
 Muulu ba bungi ne ba bungi tshitendelelu tshia dikema
 Bimfuanyi biende bia nsongo bidi bipetangana
 Viol, violet, ne muonji wa tumuma tua mvinyo.
 · · · · · · ·
 Kakuena bipupu bidi bileja ne: lupepele ludi mua kuikala
 Pa mbuu kampanda wa disanka dia kule—
 Kakuena tshipepele tshidi tshileja ne: lupepele ndulue to
 Pa mbuu idi kayiyi ne ditalala dia bungi."

Katshiena ne musambu wa muulu wa mufundi wa tusala udi ne mionji ya mutshima bu lute to:

"Ne badi bamba ne (tshisumbu tshia mitoto
 Ne bintu bikuabu bia kuteleja)
 Mudilu wa Israfeli au
 Bua lyre au
 Kudiye usomba ne wimba—
 Nshinga ya muoyo idi iteketa
 Ku mionji idi kayiyi ya pa tshibidilu."

Kadi The Raven , pamo bwa "The Bells" ne "Annabel Lee," udi uditumbisha kudi ba bungi ne bakese. Ngakamba muaba mukuabu ne: bintu bia Poe bidi kabiyi bitamba kumueneka bivua bimueneka kundi "bivua ne tshimuenekelu, nansha kakese, tshia tshienzedi tshia patupu,—mudiye witaba mudiye udiumvua, eku ufua upona ne bipendu bia bungi bia mijiki, bia kanyinganyinga bia bungi, bifumina kudiye kayi mumanye." Etshi ke tshindi ngitabuja; kadi, ku dilonga dipiadipia dia tshikuma etshi, tshidi tshinkemesha bikole kupita tshikondo kayi tshionso katshia ku buana buanyi. Dimanyangana dia pabuipi didi dileja diakalenga dia mudimu wonso mulelela wa dizola. Ela muntu, udi kayi mumanye malu a dizola anyi uditatshisha bua bualu ebu, bua kukonkonona tusala kampanda anyi tshimfuanyi kampanda, ne mudi bukole buatshi bumukuata lukasa! Mu bulelela, neapite mushinga wa mudimu muimpe wa kumpala uvuaye mukoke ntema yende. Nzolo , kabidi, udi ne ngikadilu mishindame idi nansha mumanyi ne bua kuanyisha. Kakuena mu mifundu mikuabu ya mufundi wende mudi tshipatshila tshileja bimpe menemene to. "Nzubu wa tshilala benyi" udi anu mujalame kudi mubadi musunguluke, kadi Poe kavua mua kuikala muangate tshilejilu etshi tshia tshianana bua kutshikonkonona to. Mukanda wa Raven udi mudifila bikole ku tshiena bualu tshia mufundi—dijimija dia mukaji uvuabu banemeka ne muimpe kumona; kadi pa malu makuabu, kabidi, lungenyi lua bantu ludi lujalame mu diela meji ne: mmulejilu wende wa tusala.

Muntu wa meji utu misangu ya bungi upeta diakalengele ku ditanta dia mudimu kampanda, ne ki mmisangu yonso idiye mutambe buimpe to. Dimanya didi dia lukasa bua kuikala mupingajibua. Kena upeta, bu mudi bubanji bua muena tshinyangu, tshiamu tshia arjan kunyima kua tshiamu tshia arjan, kadi "katshiyi tshipeta nansha kakese anyi bionso." Ne nunku ne majinga makuabu: muena nzubu wa mukalenge, musalayi, muenji wa manaya,—nansha biobi bikale bitupa kayi,—muntu yonso udi ubala ditshimuna diende ku diakalenga kampanda. Poe's Raven, nansha mudiye ne tshijadiki, uvua kudiye "nyunyi uvua wenza bua lupepele lupueke." Mufundi wa tusala wakasombela mu New-York, mu muvu wa mashika mu 1844-'45, ukeba mudimu mu tshikandakanda tshia Willis, "The Evening Mirror," ne upeta makuta ende ku diambuluisha dia mapa avuaye ufila miaba mikuabu. Munkatshi mua bidimu bisambombo uvua mufundi wa tshisumi, ne uvua ne lumu lua mudimu; uvua muangatshibue ne kanemu ne mpata, ne kavua mujimije bua tshitupa tshiende tshia mudimu wa tshikandakanda uvuabu bafuta bibi dituku adi to. Uvua kabidi muenze mudimu wende mutambe buimpe, bu mudi: "Ligeia" ne "Dikuluka dia nzubu wa Usher," (wa ndekelu udi ne tshitupa tshia malu a tshishima, mu mvese, tshia "Lungenyi lujimine" lua Elihu Vedder) bu mudi "The Gold Bug" ne "The Mystery of Marie Roget." Uvua muvuije bantu bavua batendelela Nzambi mu matunga makuabu, ne uvua mupete bukokeshi bua kashidi mu lungenyi lua bena France. Uvua mulonge bukole buende ne butekete buende, ne uvua mu tshibungubungu tshiende, ne kavua kayi ne lumu kampanda to. Kadi kavua mufunde bualu nansha bumue buvua pa ludimi lua muntu yonso to. Ku mudimu udi kauyi umueneka ne mupepele udi ne tshia kusakidila tshintu kampanda tshidi bantu basue bua kukuata bateleji bonso ba mu tshikondo tshia muntu nkayende.

Kupityila ku mudimu wa bafundi ba miyuki ya Poe, lumu luenza kudi The Raven ndulue bualu budibu bakula misangu ya bungi. Mukaji wa mufundi wa tusala, Virginia, uvua wenda ujimina kumpala kuende, kadi wakashala tshidimu tshikuabu mu mundidimbi wa lufu. Nzubu mule, mupuekele mu nzubu pabuipi ne njila wa Bloomingdale udi bu udi mumanyike bu nzubu uvua Rouget de l'Isle mufunde misambu ya Marseillaise. Bantu bonso bumvue ne: tshikuma etshi, tshifunda ne: “Quarles,” tshivua tshipatuke mu “American Review,” ne mêyi a dishima a mufundi pa mushindu uvuatshi; Poe uvua mupeta dolare dikumi bua bualu ebu; ne Willis, muena bulenga ne kayi ne mukawu wa balumbuluishi ba mu tshikondo etshi, wakawupatula kabidi ne musambu wa dianyisha uvua muwuvuije diakamue muyuki wa mu tshimenga. Lungenyi luonso lua mufundi wa mukanda eu luakajika pavua Poe muyibale nkayende mu tshisangilu tshia mikanda, ne bua tshikondo kampanda uvua mufundi mutambe bunene wa mu Amerike. Dikuma diakamusaka ne kumukankamija. Anu bu muena tuseku mukuabu udi ne lutambishi, yeye pende wakapeta "tshintu tshia mu muvu wa été" nansha "mu mutoyi wa bikunyibua." Diakabi, kadi bidimu bisatu biakapita,—tshikondo tshia buenzeji, lutambishi, kanyinganyinga, kadi misangu yonso tshia mudimu wa lungenyi anyi wa dikonkonona,—kumpala kua ditshimuna dia ndekelu, kumpala kua tshilamba tshipueka pa nsombelu uvua kudiye mu bulelela tshipupu, ne wakadifila kudi "Mutshimunyi."

"The American Review: A Whig Journal" uvua tshibejibeji tshia lumu tshia tshikondo atshi, tshivua ne milongo ibidi, tshipatula pa mabeji mimpe ne maleta matoke, ne tshileja ne bimfuanyi bia mezzotint. Munkatshi mua malu a bungi adi muinshi mua tshipiminu tshia mpindieu, avua ne amue malu avua mufundi yonso mua kusanka bua kupeta. Mukanda wa kumpala, wa 1845, udi ne biena-bualu bia Horace Greeley, Donald Mitchell, Walter Whitman, Marsh, Tuckerman, ne Whipple. Mukanda wa tusala wa Ralph Hoyt, "Old," uvua mupatuke mu mukanda eu. Ne monayi mêyi asatu a Poe: "Tshimenga tshidi mu mbuu," "Tshibandabanda tshia tshimvundu," ne Nyunyi . Bibidi bia kudibi bivua bienza,—ke muvua mushindu wende,—ku malonga a kumpala, kadi tshia ndekelu tshiakapatuka bu tshivua tshipiatshipia, ne pabuipi ne mutudi natshi mpindieu. Diamba dia se kabavua bashintulule bualu ebu pashishe ndibi. Mashintuluka dikumi ne umua a mu mukanda wa tshibejibeji adi amueneka mu mukanda wa The Raven and Other Poems , 1845, mukanda uvua mufundi wa tusala mumone ne: uvua ne bua kufila kudi bantu bonso. Ebi bidi nangananga bishintuluka bia tumanyinu tua miaku, anyi bia miaku umue umue, mushindu wa ndekelu eu mmuenza bua kukolesha tshipeta tshia dishintulula dia miaku. Mu tshibejibeji tshilenga tshia Mr. Lang tshia mvese wa Poe, tshipatuka mu "Parchment Library," mmuleje lungenyi lua mumanyi, ne mmuenze malu ne meji, mu dipingana ku mukanda udi mu mukanda utudi batele, bu mudiwu mufila mu tshibejibeji tshia mu Londres tshia mu 1846. Tshisangilu tshia "tshilelela" tshia Griswold tshia mikanda ya mufundi wa tusala tshidi ne bilema bia bungi. Malu aa mmapingajibue kudi bafundi ba pashishe, bavua pabu benze bilema biabu. Ino mukanda wa The Raven , bua dilomba divuabu benze kudi mufundi bua kupeta kopi ya maniskri, uvua anu mushintuludibue bikole kudiye; mu bulelela, wakawupatula mu Richmond, kumpala kua lufu luende, ne dipingaja dia mu tshiena-bualu tshia "seraphim udi makasa apueka" pa muaba wa "banjelu badi makasa apueka," mu tshitupa tshia dikumi ne tshinayi. Nunku, mukanda wetu wa mpindieu, nansha mudiwu wa mu 1845, mmushintuluke ndambu kudi dibala dia pashishe dia mufundi wa tusala, ne ndi ngela meji ne: mmushindu mutambe bululame ne muimpe wa tusala tumue etu. Dishintuluka dinene dia mu mukanda wa kumpala diakamueneka, nansha nanku, mu mukanda wa mu 1845; tshitupa tshia dikumi ne tshimue tshivua ne milongo eyi, tshilema mu mvese ne tshilema pa tshikuma:

"Kukuata kudi mfumu kampanda udi kayi ne disanka, udi kayi ne luse Tshipupu
 Wakalonda lubilu ne kulonda lubilu lukole—nanku, pavua Hope mua kuditshipa,
 Stern Despair wakapingana, pamutu pa Hope muimpe uvuaye muditshipe—
 Diandamuna dia kanyinganyinga edi, 'Kakuyi mpata!'"

Bidi mua kuikala bimpa shi bakuabu, ne bamanyike, bafundi ba tusala badi mua kuikala bajadike bua kuenza mashintuluka abu misangu yonso dilengejibua. Poe uvua misangu yonso ulongolola mvese yende mikese, ne pa tshibidilu uvua uyilengeja. Mukanda wa Raven uvua wa kumpala wa tusala tukese tuvuaye pabuipi ne kujikija kumpala kua kupatula. Bidi mua kuikala ne aba badi banange mifundu ya nsumuinu mbajimije bintu bikese ku lufu luenda. Bu muvuaye mumanye miaku ya bungi, kavua muanji kufunda mvese anu bua kuenza tshikuma to. Pavua tshimfuanyi tshimutonda, meyi avua afuminamu avua mamusaka, anu muvuaye muambe nkayende, a dinanga, kadi ki mbua tshipatshila to. Bino byadi mu bipindi bya pa bula bwa kujadika lungenyi lua Fairfield lua se tshionso tshivua tshipeta tshia lutatu lua tshiji.

Ntshinyi, nunku, tshiakapesha mufundi wa tusala lungenyi luende lua The Raven ? Ku tshishimikidi kayi tshia lupuishi tshivua tshijuka bitekete bitekete too ne ku mijiki ivua mipuyakana bitekete bitekete? Anu bu pa tshibidilu, bintu bipite pa tshimue biakaya ku dibaka dia tusala tua mushinga mukole. Patudi tutangila miyuki mile itu misangu ya bungi bayisha mu mifundu mikese ne kayiyi ne lungenyi lua bungi, ndi mutekemene ne: kabakupeta tshilumbu bua muyuki eu wa musambu umuepele to, nangananga udi muenji wa misambu bu mudi Doré muvuije tshiena bualu tshia tshimfuanyi tshia tshianana. Too ne matuku mashale aa mvua ngela meji ne: tshitupa etshi, ne bikese bivua mufundi watshi mufunde panyima pa dimueneka diatshi, bivua bia pa buabi bualu kabivua bifumine ku tuishi mu mvese wende wa buana to. Ino Mr. Fearing Gill mundeje bimue bifundu bya Poe bidi kabiyi bipatuka, bifunda mu tshidimu tshienda tshia dikumi ne muanda mukulu, ne bidi ne tshiena bualu tshia se: "Demon wa kapia." Maniskri adi amueneka bu adi mu tshianza tshia kumpala tshia mufundi wa tusala, ne bulelela buende budi bujadikibua kudi dîku didibu bashale nadi munkatshi mua tshitupa tshia bidimu lukama. Pa kumbusha tuishi tua "The Bells" ne "The Haunted Palace" tudi ne milongo mikese idi ileja ntuadijilu ne ndekelu wa The Raven . Pa bidi bitangila tshiona tshia tusala etu, difuanyikija dia matuku didi dileja ne: bualu ebu buvua buenzeja kudi tshiona tshia "Lady Geraldine's Courtship." Poe uvua umue wa ku bantu ba kumpala bavua banemeka meji a Miss Barrett; wakamufundila tusala tuvuaye musangishe bu "muntu mutambe bunene wa bumuntu buende," ne uvua mu luse ne mushindu wende wa difunda. Milongo ya mu tusala tuende tua dinanga,

"Ne tshiona tshikole tshidi katshiyi tshijadika, mu lupepele, rido wa violet
 Udi uvula ne uvula pabuipi ne mpala yende mitoke idi kayiyi yenda,"

wakapeta diandamuna mu malu aa:

"Ne mutoyi wa kanyinganyinga udi kauyi mujadike wa rido yonso wa violet
 Bivua binsankisha bikole—bivua binwuja ne buôwa bua dikema bumvua tshiyi muanji kumvua to."

Apa Poe wakangata diakalenga divuaye mubenge Longfellow bikole, ne divua muntu nansha umue kayi muanji kukeba mu nzubu wende nkayende kayi mupeta ne munyoka lukasa. Mu melodie ne mu mushindu wa stanzaic, netumone ne: tusala tubidi etu katuena tushilangane to, kadi mu tshipatshila tudi tushilangane bikole. Mufundi wa tusala uvua ne muoyo wa kupa kavua mumvue tshintu tshikuabu to, anu bumuntu bulelela bua mufundi wa tusala. "Difunda edi dimpe!" wakamba ne,—"bukole ebu budibu bumvua!... Mufundi wetu munene wa tusala, Mr. Browning, mufundi wa 'Paracelsus,' &c., udi ne tshisumi mu dianyisha diende dia tshiona." Mr. Ingram, kunyima kua yeye mumane kuakula bua "Lady Geraldine," udi uleja ne budimu buonso muaba mukuabu uvua Poe mua kuikala mupete lungenyi. Mu 1843, Albert Pike, muena Greke tshitupa, muena mikalu, mufundi wa tusala wa mu Arkansas, uvua mupatule mu "The New Mirror," uvua Poe wenda ufunda, imue mvese ivua ne tshiena bualu tshia ne: "Isadore," kadi katshia anu pinapu mufundi uvua mupingajibue ne mubikile ne: "The Widowed Heart." Ndi nsungula mu tshikebelu tshia Mr. Pike tshiambilu tshidi tshilonda etshi, tshidi ne malu manene adi apetangana ne tshia ku ntuadijilu:

"Ndi ngendakana mu nzubu yetu idi nkayanyi, tshiena ntua kabidi misambu yetu to,
 Dîba didi dikenka bikole pa buloba budi kabuyi bulengeja;
 Nyunyi wa tuseku utshidi anu musombe ne wimba, O tshisumbu tshia kanyinganyinga!
 Bualu mutshima wanyi udi bu ditutu dia mu muvu wa automne didi diuwuja tente ne mvula;
 Udi mujimine kundi kashidi, Isadore!"

Apa tudi ne tshipiminu tshile, difuanangana dia tshiona, ne mbangilu wa nyunyi udi musambu wende uvudija kanyinganyinga. Kudi njila mikuabu idi mua kulonda kudi bantu badi basue kumanya malu; nangananga, tshitupa tshidi Mr. Ingram musungule mu dikonkonona dia ndekelu dia Poe dia "Barnaby Rudge":

"Mukoko, kabidi, * * * uvua mua kuikala muenza, kupita mutudi tumumona mpindieu, tshitupa tshia lungenyi lua Barnaby wa dikema. * * * Ngikadilu wende uvua mua kuikala muenze, pa bidi bitangila tshia tshilema, tshitupa tshia muomumue ne tshidi, mu mizike, dilondangana pa bidi bitangila luya."

Nansha nanku, kunyima kua kuleja tuishi etu ne difuanangana, mushinga wa tshikuma etshi utshidi anu mu ntuadijilu wende. Mijiki idi yenda ikola, malu adi alenga kumona ne butoke ne mundidimbi bidi bishilangane,—ku mutu kua bionso, dinanga dia mu nyuma dia butuku, bidi bivuija mudimu wa muntu wa meji udi ufila ngumu. Pa bidi bitangila nyunyi wa tshikisu, mmushilangane ne nyunyi mikuabu yonso ya mu tshisa tshiende, kubangila ku "twa corbies" ne "three ravens" ya ba-baladiste too ne ku "Grip" wa Barnaby. Kakuena tshimuenekelu tshia tshisuku tshia cawing tshidi tshikengesha nzubu ya bankambua ne tshidi tshienda mu budimi bua malu a lumu; kakuena tshishima tshidi Tickell muimbe ne:, pavua,

"udi wela mbila ku dididishi diende misangu isatu,
 Mukoko wakatuta dipuapua diende,
 Nsongakaji uvua ne dinanga uvua mumanye bimpe
 Mutoyi wa tshijila."

Poe's raven i lungenyi lushilangane; dilua dia kanyinganyinga ne dipanga ditekemena dia muntu udi udila; tshintu tshia sable tshidi ne Memoire, mufundi wa malu a kabutu, mushindu wa Tshidi katshiyi mua kulongolodibua. Padiye munyeme ku Styx, ku "muelelu wa Pluton wa Butuku," udi umueneka bu anyima wa muena lukonko nkayende,—wa yeye udi kayi mua, kayi mua, kunua nepenthe wa mushindu eu, bualu tshivulukilu tshia Lenore ke tshionso tshidi tshimushiye, ne ne dijika dia kanyinganyinga kende nansha abi nebiteke ku luseke.

Mukanda wa Raven udi kabidi mua kuangatshibua bu tshilejelu tshia mufundi wende, bua tshilejilu tshiende tshia ngenyi yende yonso ya tshidi tshilejelu ne bua kuikala. Bionso ebi bidi mu mifundu yende idi yakula bua "Mukenji wa tusala," "Tshilumbu tshia mvese," ne "Lungenyi lua difunda." Poe unena amba "mu mizike, pamua, anyima udi pabuipi ne kufika ku ndekelu munene udi, padiye mufundisha kudi lungenyi lua tusala, udi uluangana—difukibua dia bulengele bua pa mutu.... Mvese kayena mua kuikala mimanyike bimpe kupita bu mizike idi kayiyi ne bukole to"; kadi kabidi, mvese udi bushuwa "Poésie wa miaku" udi "Difukibua dia bulengele mu mushindu wa tshiona,"—etshi ne kakuyi tshikuabu to. Mutoyi wa bulengele bua pa mutu udi wa dibungama. Tshiena bualu tshidi tshitamba kubungamija ntshia Lufu. Bualu ebu budi ne bua kuikala bupetangana ne bulengele. "Lufu, nunku, lua mukaji muimpe udi, kakuyi mpata, tshiena bualu tshia tusala tshitambe bunene pa buloba,—ne kabidi kakuyi mpata, mishiku idi mikumbane bua tshiena bualu tshia nunku mmishindu ya munanga udi mufuishe." Biambilu bia ndekelu ebi mbiangatshila mu dikonkonona dia Poe dia patupu dia tshikuma etshi, kadi bidi bileja menemene tshitupa tshinene tshia mvese yende. Nshikidilu wa "The Bells" udi mutoyi wa tshianana wa ba-ghouls, badi baditumbisha bua kutekesha mutshima wa muntu. "Lenore," The Raven , "The Sleeper," "To One in Paradise," ne "Ulalume" badi benza symphonie wa tenebrose,—ne "Annabel Lee," mufunda ku ndekelu kua bionso, udi uleja ne tshiena bualu tshimue tshivua tshimukuate too ne ku ndekelu. Musangu eu kabidi, bionso ebi kabiena bia mijiki to, ne bamue badi balenga kudi ngikadilu wa Fantastic udi ujula lungenyi lua buôwa, ne usakidila buôwa bupiabupia ku kanyinganyinga nkayaku. Mu bionso bidi bileja mu mushindu mubi, muinshi mua tshilamba tshia mundidimbi, dinanga dimpe dia mufundi wa tusala,—ditu misangu ya bungi dileja muaba mukuabu,—mukaji wa Usher, Lady Ligeia, mu bulelela budi bufuanangane ne bumuntu buende nkayende. Ndi ngela meji ne: dinanga dia muena nkindi kudi muntu kampanda "mu mushindu" kadiena mua kuikala dipetangana ne didifila diende kudi tshintu kampanda tshimpe to. Mukaji udi pabuipi nende udi ne tshia kuenza mudimu ne mayele ende, kuikala mu tshikondo tshionso ne kayi mutantshi mule, kumusankisha ne bintu bishilashilangane bidi kabiyi ne ndekelu, anyi kuikala ne disanka dia kulekela tshitupa tshia lulamatu luende. Udi ne tshia kuikala mukokibua kudi tshidi katshiyi mua kukumbana, ne bualu ebu budi anu bumupita bukole mu dinanga diende dia malu a bufuki.

Poe, bu mudi Hawthorne, wakalua ne dinyanguka dia tshilongelu tshia dinanga, ne kakuvua muntu uvua musanke bu yeye bua kuseka bua dikenga diatshi to. Ino mutshima wandi wadi na ba romance ne bintu byabo bya ku Mputu nansha bya Gothique. Difuka dienda, divule mu miyuki ya mu tshikoso, diakamueneka bu divua dimulekele pavuaye mufunde mvese; ne lungenyi luende luakamuambila ne: tusala tulampe tua dinanga tudi tushindamena pa malu adi menzeke pamutu pa kuikala ne lungenyi, ne bua kuamba tusala tua dinanga, mêyi adi mua kukumbana. Ke bualu kayi lungenyi luende, ludi lupetangana bimpe ne mikalu yende, lua se: "'muaku mule' udi ubengangana bikole mu miaku." Bitupa bia The Raven mbikese ne bipepele: muntu mulume, nyunyi, ne tshivulukilu tshia dikema tshia mukaji. Kadi tshitupa etshi tshidi tshifila dileja dimpe dia malu a dinanga. Tudi ne tshinyi? Munkatshi wa butuku; nzubu wa mundidimbi ne mikanda yende ya malu a kale adibu bapue muoyo; mulongi,—Hieronymus wa matuku etu aa; tshiona tshia nkutshi pa tshibumba; butuku bua mashika ne kapia kadi kafua; bilamba bia soie bidi bikuata kudi lupepele; bilota ne dipanga dieyemena dia mîdima idi imueneka; nyunyi mufike, wa dikema pa tshiadi tshitoke; bintu bia mudimu bia velours violet ne muendu udi ukenka. Bionso ebi bia nsombelu, butoke, dikala, dîyi, bidi anu bia dinanga, ne mene bia melodrama, kadi bia ngikadilu wa tusala udi melodrama kayi uleja misangu ya bungi, ne bidi bileja bimpe menemene "dinanga dia kashidi, kanyinganyinga ka kashidi" ka mufundi wa tusala.

Mushindu uvuabu balongolole misambu ya The Raven uvua bushuwa mua kulenga bantu. Mifundu, dishintulula, bujitu, mushindu wa tshilejelu, bivua bilongolola ne budimu bua pa buabu. Kakuyi mpata, mufundi wa tusala uvua mukemeshibue kudi bujalame bua mijiki ya Miss Barrett, mu "eights," ne nangananga kudi ndongoluelu uvuabu bangate pabuipi ne ndekelu wa "Lady Geraldine":

"'Mêsu,' udi wamba, 'mpindieu adi atuta munda muanyi! Nudi mesu avua manjimije anyi?
 Mêsu adi akenka, bu mabue a kale adibu bateke mu dibue dia lupingu lua Parian!
 Muinshi mua mpala mutoke udi mupole, udi anu utema kapia kakole
 Mu tshipela tshia nsenga tshia mutshima wanyi ne muoyo wanyi munyanguke anyi?'"

Dibueja diende dia nsongo dia tshikuma tshisatu mu milongo ibidi ne inayi, ne dikumbaja dia mulongo muitanu ne tshikuma tshia ndekelu, biakavuija stanza wa The Raven . Dishintulula dia miaku didi dimueneka bu didi dilua kakuyi lutatu, ne misangu ya bungi miaku idi munda mua milongo itu ikoka bantu; padi tshikuma anyi bujitu tshikokesha mudimu wonso. Muaba eu kabidi uvua mupete diakalenga ku dilonga dia Miss Barrett dia misambu ya ballade ne ya romaunts mu miakulu yende ne mikuabu. "Refrain" i tshintu tshidi mufundi wa tusala anyi muena misambu ukuata natshi ditshi didi diendakana,—dijinga dia misangu ne misangu dia bufuki bua tshiona tshia ntuadijilu. Misangu yonso ntu nsanka bua misambu milenga ya muimbi wa misambu wa mu Angleterre,—"The Nightingales, the Nightingales," "Margret, Margret," "My Heart and Me," "Toll slowly," "The River floweth on," "Pan, Pan is dead," ne bikuabu. Uvua kabidi wenza mudimu ne tshindi mbikila ne: Repetend, mu dienza mudimu ne Poece uvua mujule tshisokomenu tshionso tshia Coleri it:

"O bintu bidi ne muoyo bia disanka! kakuyi ludimi
 Bulenga buabu budi mua kuamba ne:
 Mushimi wa dinanga wakapatuka mu mutshima wanyi,
 Ngakababenesha tshiyi mumanye;
 Bushuwa, munsantu wanyi muimpe wakamfuila luse,
 Ngakabebenesha tshiyi mumanye.”

Poe wakafuka mulongo muitanu wa stanza wende bua majimbu a repetend. Wakaeyemena bualu ebu bikole mu tusala tukese tua pashishe,—"Lenore," "Annabel Lee," "Ulalume," ne "For Annie." Ndi ngela meji ne: uvua mupete mizike ya tshikisu ne ya dibungama, ku diambuluisha dia "malu mimpe," a bujitu ne repetinds ya mu Afrike ivuabu bamuimbila ku buana buende, biumvuangana ne melodie wabu wa ku dibele dîyi dia mufundi wetu wa tusala wa ku Sud.

"The Philosophie of Composition," dikonkonona diende dia The Raven , i dikosolola dia malu a tekiniki dia mushindu wende ne mushindu udiye muenza. Nansha dianyisha diende dia malu a dishima, nansha dianyisha diende dia pashishe kudi balunda bende dia se: dimanyisha dia malu aa divua anu dishima diende dia meji, kabiena bikengela kuanyishibua to. Bu yeye mulongolole mudimu wonso kumpala kua dîba, kavua mua kuikala muenze tshibangidilu tshikole tshia misambu ya rattle-pan ku melodie muimpe wa stanza muibidi to, nansha pa lungenyi luende lua malu a kukema. Bushuwa, muenji wa bintu, yeye mumane kujikija mudimu kampanda, udi umona, anu bu muenji wa bintu wa kumpala, ne: mmuimpe, ne udi umona bua tshinyi mmuimpe. Dikonkonona dia pashishe, disangisha ne dibenga dia kapia ka tshijila, didi mua kuenzeka bipepele. Ditabuja dianyi didi ne: lungenyi lua kumpala ne difunda dia tshikoso dia tshiena-bualu etshi biakalua bu mudi lungenyi lulua misangu yonso; ne: mufundi wende wakamona mushindu uvuaye mua kuikala mupuangane, kumupeshaye malu a ndekelu adibu bumvuije mu nshapita wende wa Difunda, ne bualu bua ndekelu ebu, nunku, ki mbua dishima anyi bulelela to. Bubi bua dikonkonona dia nunku budi ne: didi disaka muntu udi kayi mumanye bua kuela meji ne: malu adibu benze adi mua kupita lungenyi. Tshipatshila tshia meji ntshia kulama malu masokoka a dîba diatshi dia bufuki. Bimuenenu bia mudimu mukole, mateta adi kaayi malelela, adi kumpala kua ditshimuna, bu mudibi mu tshimfuanyi tshia Hawthorne tshidibu bapatule matuku mashale aa, adi afila malongesha ne dikankamija dia mushinga mukole kudi muenji wa bintu bia bushuwa. Kadi umue udi uleja Muse wende ku budisuile kudi bantu bonso badi balua udi ne tshia kuvuluka tshivua tshienzekele Mukalenge Candaules.

Bena panu batshidi bela meji ne Poe udi "muntu wa meji udi kayi ne diakalenga." Matuku mashale aa mvua mumvue bamutela bu "muntu udi bantu bonso bamueneka bu badibu banyishile bua kumuleja bibi, kumupenda anyi kumupenda." Kadi bidi bimueneka kundi ne: dikenga diende diakajika ne lufu luende lua luse, ne katshia anu pinapu, dikuba diende divua ditungunuka, ne lumu luende lua dikola dia kashidi bu mudi mufundi udi ukenga ne wa tshipedi mua kusambila mu dîba diende dia ditekemena. Dipenda ne dipenda dia Griswold biakashintulula malu mu diakalenga diende. Bakulumpe ne bafundi ba miyuki ya bantu mbalue ne dibenga dia malu adi alondangana, ne dianyisha dia ngikadilu wende, ne mikanda mipiamipia ya mikanda yende. Mikanda yende ne malu makese a mudimu wende adi kumpala kuetu; malu adibu bafunde, mimpe ne mabi, mmamanyike bikole. Kakuena muena tshilumbu udi mupeta dilumbuluisha dia bulenga ne dia luse dia nunku to. Malu mabi ende mu nsombelu avua a mu tshitupa tshiende ne a mu tshikondo tshiende, pamuapa avua kabidi afumina ku butekete buende bua disua. Kakuyi mpata, muaba uvuaye kauvua mua kukuba bufuki buimpe ne kabuyi ne nkatshinkatshi ku njiwu ya pambelu ne ya munda to. Disua diende dikole, dia kuikala mfumu wende nkayende, diakamupetesha « bumpianyi bua dikenga adi ». Wakadibingisha bu mfumu wa nyunyi udi kayi ne disanka, mukengeshi wa tshikuma etshi. Kadi wende uvua ne tshitupa tshijima tshia tshiji tshikole tshidi tshisankisha mu dimanyisha dia kabutu kende. Kudi disanka dia kuenza mudimu wa muntu munene: tuetu bonso tudi baluanganyi ba mvita, tudila Ave Imperator! Bua kutela bualu bua Burke: "Mu kanyinganyinga, disanka didi anu dia pa mutu, ne makenga atudi tupeta kaena afuanangana ne kanyinganyinga kakole to, kadi katuena anu tuenza bibi, ne tudi tudienzeja bua kumbusha lukasa." Poe wakaya kule, ne uvua muena nkindi nansha mu ntatu ya mudimu wende. Shi, bilondeshile ditabuja diende nkayende, kanyinganyinga ne dijimina dia bulengele bidi biena-bualu bia mushinga mukole bia tusala, ne didiumvua dia tusala ndisanka dia pa buloba, dîba adi kanyinganyinga ne mîdima ya nsombelu wende munyanguke kabivua kabiyi ne difutu dia misangu ne misangu to.

Mu mabeji adi alonda, tudi ne tshilejilu tshipiatshipia tshia meji a muena nkindi adi aleja mudiye umvuija tshiena-bualu tshiende lumu. Gustave Doré, mudimu wa ndekelu wa crayon wende udi kumpala kuetu, kavua mufundi wa bintu, anyi mene mufundi wa bintu, bua bena malu a bushuwa badi balomba bulelela bua dîyi, tshimfuanyi ne bupuangane. Malu a nunku kaavua amutangila to, anu bua kuenza tshimfuanyi ne ntanta, butoke ne mundidimbi, biambuluisha tshipatshila tshiende,—tshivua tshia kusankisha anyima, lungenyi, lua muntu uvua utangila. Wakenza nunku pa kujula lungenyi luetu lua dikema, ku diambuluisha dia lungenyi lua dikema ne misangu ya bungi lua pa mutu lua malu adi kaayi mua kuambibua ne maule tente ne mîdima anyi butumbi. Badi bamba bimpe ne: bikala midimu yende kayivua bimfuanyi binene, bu mudi bimfuanyi bidi binene menemene. Bu muvuaye "mufundi wa mikanda," ne uvua nanku, bukole buende buvua mu diumvuangana buludiludi ne difuka dia dikema dia mufundi wende, ne dikima dia nyuma divua dimuenzeje. Ke bualu kayi wakapeta diakalengele ne Rabelais, ne "Le Juif-Errant," "Les Contes Drolatiques," ne "Don Quixote," ne ke bualu kayi, mu mushindu mukuabu, dipanga diende dia kuleja bulengele bua Tennyson's Idyls, bua "Il Paradiso," bua mifundu ya bena Ebelu, ne mifundu mikuabu idi ilomba dibandisha, anyi dilengeja ne dikisha. Uvua muledibue bu mfumu wa malu a dikema, ne ku diambuluisha dia lungenyi lua pa bualu uvua mua kuleja bintu bia buongo budi ne nyuma mibi. Tudi tumona bintu anu muvua bantu bende babimone, ne mêsu a muena Yuda uvua wendakana, Don uvua mubungame, anyi muana wa bakaji wa muenji wa or uvua ne lungenyi luende lua kutumbisha Mukalenge mu tshisalu tshia ku Pont-au-Change. Bivua mu tshifukilu tshia bintu bivuaye ne bua kukokibua kudi tshintu tshionso tshilenga tshia mvese anyi tshia meyi andi mubikile ne: tshia pa buatshi. Makalenge a muinshi avua mabikidibue mu mudimu wende; mabue ende, mitshi ne mikuna, diulu nkayadi, bidi ne muoyo ne tshipatshila ne diablerie. Bu yeye muikale ne muoyo bua kuleja Shakespeare, tuvua mua kuikala bamone mushindu uvuabu benzele Caliban, balombodi, tshipepele tshikole mu "Lear"; kadi kakuyi mpata uvua ne tshia kuela mpata pa lungenyi luende lua Imogen anyi lua Miranda. Bulengele bukezuke ne bupepele, ne bulenga buabu bupuangane, uvua umueneka kayi umvua bimpe.

Ino kudi bulengele mu mifundu yende ya "Ancient Mariner," nansha mudibi kabiyi bia bushuwa, ne dilondangana didi kadiyi ditamba kumueneka mu mulongolongo wa Doré. Rime wakamupesha muyuki mule, ne mashintuluka a bungi a tshikena kumona. Mu The Raven, mutoyi ne dikala bidi bilama tshimuenekelu tshiabi ne katuena ne dishintuluka dia muaba anyi dia tshikondo to. Tshipeta ntshinyi? Doré udi ufila mishindu mishilashilangane pa tshiena bualu etshi mudiye mutshiele meji, "tshilumbu tshia lufu ne dimona dia anyima udi kayi mua kusamba." Mu bimue bia ku bimfuanyi ebi bilema biende bidi bimueneka patoke; mikuabu idi ileja bumuntu buende bua bukole, ne ngikadilu mimpe ivuaye, bu muenji wa bimfuanyi, muimane nkayende. Bushuwa, kuvua bualu kampanda buvua bufuanangane pankatshi pa lungenyi lua mudimu lua Poe ne Doré. Bualu ebu buvua mua kumueneka patoke bu yeye mutete tshianza tshiende pa "Miyuki ya Grotesque ne Arabesque." Bubidi buabu bavua baya misangu ya bungi mu buloba bua ba-elf bua malu a mu lungenyi ne a dinanga. Mu bienzedi bia dikema, bonso babidi, ku diambuluisha dia bukokeshi buabu bua malu a pa mutu, bavua bepuka njiwu idi pankatshi pa malu mimpe ne malu adi kaayi ne mushinga. Poe uvua mutendeledi mulelela wa Tshilenga; dinanga diende bua bualu ebu divua dinanga dia kudifila, ne muaba eu udi ushiya bulunda ne muenji wende wa tshimfuanyi. Mufundi wa tusala anyi muena nkindi, lufu ludi ndekelu wa bionso lushintulula bionso: mu mundidimbi wa tshimanyishilu tshiende tshia sable, tshimfuanyi tshia Vedder tshidi tshibateka mpala ne mpala bimpe, kadi tshidi tshibabuikila ne tshilamba tshia meji ne bukole bidi kabiyi bifua. Mukaji mukuabu wa mu Amerike mmuenze tshimfuanyi tshia muena meji udi ne mêsu a mitoto ne muendu wa ndekelu, tshimfuanyi tshilenga tshia muntu udi ne tshimuenenu tshidi tshimona, kadi mishiku yende kayiyi mua kuamba, malu masokoka a buloba budi pambelu pa "tshiibi tshia lukita lua mu mianu."

Edmund C. Stedman.

MUSOKO.


Tshimfuanyi 1


MUKOKO.

Dituku dikuabu munkatshi mua butuku, pamvua ngela meji, mutekete ne mutshioke,
 Pa mutu pa mikanda ya bungi ya malu a kale adibu bapue muoyo,
 Pamvua ntua mpala panshi, pabuipi ne kulala tulu, mu tshimpitshimpi, dîyi diakalua dikuma,
 Bu mudi muntu mukuabu wela mbila bitekete bitekete, wela mbila ku tshiibi tshia nzubu wanyi.
 "'T i muenyi kampanda," ngakamba, "udi ukuma ku tshiibi tshia nzubu wanyi—
 Anu etshi, ne kakuyi tshikuabu to."
Ah, ndi mvuluka bimpe ne: bivua mu ngondo wa dikumi ne muibidi,
 Ne luya luonso luvua lufua luakafua panshi.
 Ngakajinga dituku diakalonda ne muoyo mujima:—patupu mvua mukebe bua kusomba
 Mu mikanda yanyi mudi kanyinganyinga kapatuka—kanyinganyinga bua Lenore uvua mujimine—
 Bua nsongakaji udi kayi umueneka ne udi ukenka udi banjelu babikila ne: Lenore—
 Kakuyi dina apa bua kashidi.
Ne mutoyi wa soie udi kauyi mujadike wa rido yonso wa violet
 Bivua binsankisha bikole—bivua binwuja ne buôwa bua dikema bumvua tshiyi muanji kumvua to;
 Nunku mpindieu, bua kujikija dikuma dia mutshima wanyi, mvua muimane ngamba kabidi
 "'T i muenyi kampanda udi ulomba bua kubuela ku tshiibi tshia nzubu wanyi
 Muenyi kampanda uvua mufike panyima pa dîba uvua unlomba bua kubuela ku tshiibi tshia nzubu wanyi;—
 Etshi ke tshidi, ne kakuena tshikuabu to."
Diakamue anyima wanyi wakakola; welakana pashishe kayi kabidi,
 "Mukalenge," ngakamba, "anyi mamu, bulelela luse lueba ndi nkusengelela;
 Kadi bualu bulelela budi ne: mvua mulale tulu, ne uvua mulue ne bupole buonso,
 Ne bitekete bitekete uvua mulue kukuma, kukuma ku tshiibi tshia nzubu wanyi,
 Ne tshivua mujadike ne: mvua mukuteleje to"—apa ngakangula tshiibi bikole;—
 Mîdima idiku, ne kakuena tshikuabu to.
Mu mîdima ayi mutangile, mutantshi mule mvua muimane muaba au ngela meji, ne buôwa,
 Kuela mpata, kulota bilota bidi muntu nansha umue kayi muanji kulota;
 Kadi ditalala kadivua dijika to, ne mîdima kayivua ifila tshimanyishilu to,
 Ne muaku umuapele uvuabu bambe uvua muaku muela mbila ne: “Lenore!”
 Ngakambila bualu ebu mu mutoyi, ne tshiona tshiakapingaja muaku eu ne: “Lenore!”
 Anu etshi ne kakuyi tshikuabu.
Kupingana mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi, anyima wanyi yonso udi munda muanyi utema,
 Mutantshi mukese ngakumvua kabidi mutoyi, ne dîyi dikole kupita kumpala.
 "Bushuwa," ngakamba, "bushuwa etshi ntshintu tshidi ku dididishi dianyi;
 Lekela mmone, nunku, tshidiku, ne bualu ebu busokoka bua kukonkonona—
 Lekela mutshima wanyi wikale mupuwe bua tshitupa tshîpi ne bualu busokoka ebu bukonkonone;—
 'T i lupepele ne kakuena tshikuabu to!"
Kukangula apa, ngakakuma tshiibi, pavua, ne dinanga dia bungi ne dinaya dia nsongo,
 Muaba au wakabuela Nyunyi munene wa mu matuku a tshijila a kale.
 Kavua muinama nansha kakese to; kakuvua minite nansha umue uvua muimane anyi mushale to;
 Kadi, ne tshimuenekelu tshia mfumu anyi mukaji, musombe pa mutu pa tshiibi tshia nzubu wanyi—
 Ndi musombe pa tshimfuanyi tshia Pallas pa mutu pa tshiibi tshia nzubu wanyi—
 Kusomba, ne kusomba, ne kakuyi tshikuabu.
Pashishe nyunyi wa ébène eu udi ukoka lungenyi luanyi lua dibungama bua kuseka,
 Ku bumuntu bua mpala buvuaye muvuale,
 "Nansha tshifulu tshiebe tshikosa ne tshikosa, wewe," ngakamba, "kuena muena tshinyangu to,
 Nyunyi wa tshinyangu ne wa kale udi wenda unyunguluka ufumina ku muelelu wa mâyi wa butuku,—
 Ngambila dina diebe dia mfumu ku muelelu wa Pluton wa Butuku!"
 Quoth Mukoko, "Kakuyi mpata."
Ngakakema bikole bua nyunyi eu uvua kayi ne lungenyi bua kumvua muyuki patoke nunku,
 Nansha mudi diandamuna diende kadiyi ne diumvuija dikese—kadivua ne mushinga mukese to;
 Bualu katuena mua kubenga kuitaba ne: kakuena muntu udi ne muoyo to
 Ever yet uvua mubeneshibue bua kumona nyunyi pa mutu pa tshiibi tshia nzubu wende—
 Nyunyi anyi nyama wa luonji pa tshimfuanyi tshisonga pa mutu pa tshiibi tshia nzubu wende,
 Ne dina bu mudi "Nevermore."
Kadi Nyunyi, musombe nkayende pa tshiadi tshia patupu, uvua wakula anu
 Muaku umue au, bienze anu bu ne: anyima wende mu muaku umue au uvua mupatule.
 Kakuena bualu bukuabu buvuaye muambe to—kakuyi dikasa dimue pashishe wakatuta—
 Too ne pamvua anu muele mbila ne: “Balunda bakuabu bakadi banji kuya mu ndeke—
 Dituku didi dilonda neanshiye , anu muvua ditekemena dianyi dienda dienda kumpala."
 Pashishe nyunyi wakamba ne: “Kakuyi mpata.”
Kukema bua ditalala divua dinyanguka kudi diandamuna diamba bimpe menemene,
 "Kakuyi mpata," ngakamba, "tshidiye wamba ntshia se: tshibutshilu tshiende tshimuepele,
 Kukuata kudi mfumu kampanda udi kayi ne disanka udi kayi ne luse Tshipupu
 Wakalonda lubilu ne kulonda lubilu too ne pavua misambu yende mimane bujitu bumue—
 Too ne ku madilu a Ditekemena diende, bujitu bua kanyinganyinga abu buakambula
 Wa 'Kakuyi nansha kakese.'"
Kadi Nyunyi utshidi anu ukoka anyima wanyi yonso wa kanyinganyinga bua kuseka,
 Bululame ngakambula nkuasa wa tshisuku kumpala kua nyunyi ne tshiadi ne tshiibi;
 Pashishe, pavua tshiamu tshia velours tshienda tshibuela, ngakadifila bua kulamakaja
 Dinanga ku dinanga, wela meji bua tshidi nyunyi wa tshinyangu wa kale eu—
 Tshinyi nyunyi wa kale eu mubi, mubi, mubi, mutekete ne mubi
 Bidi biumvuija ne: "Kakuyi mpata."
Ndi musombe mu diela meji, kadi kakuyi silabe udi uleja
 Ku nyunyi uvua ne mêsu ende a kapia mpindieu atema mu tshiadi tshianyi;
 Bualu ebu ne bikuabu ngakasomba ngela meji, ne mutu wanyi mupole
 Pa tshilamba tshia tshilamba tshia kulala tshivua butoke bua muendu butokesha,
 Kadi udi ne tshilamba tshiende tshia violet tshia velours tshidi ne butoke bua muendu budi buenda butokesha
 Yeye neenze, ah, nansha kakese!
Pashishe, meme kuela meji ne: lupepele luakakola, luvua ne mupuya muimpe wa mu tshibombi tshia manananshi tshivua katshiyi tshimueneka
 Uvua unyunguluka kudi ba seraphim bavua ne maulu abu avua alenga panshi.
 "Mubi," ngakela mbila, "Nzambi webe mmukusombeshe—ku diambuluisha dia banjelu aba mmukutume
 Dikisha—dikisha ne nepenthe ku malu audi uvuluka a Lenore!
 Quaff, oh quaff nepenthe wa mushindu eu, ne upua muoyo Lenore mujimine eu!"
 Quoth Mukoko, "Kakuyi mpata."
"Muprofete!" Ngakamba ne, "tshintu tshibi!—muprofete nansha, nansha nyunyi anyi diabolo!—
 Nansha mudi Mutetshi mutume, anyi tshipepele tshikole tshikuele ku muelelu wa mâyi;
 Mu tshipela kadi kayi ne buôwa, mu buloba ebu bua tshipela budibu balengeje—
 Pa nzubu ewu kudi Horror haunted—ngambila bulelela, ndi nkusengelela—
 Kudiku— kudiku buanga mu Gilada anyi?—Ngambila—Ngambila, ndi nkusengelela!"
 Quoth Mukoko, "Kakuyi mpata."
"Muprofete!" ngakamba ne, "tshintu tshibi—muprofete nansha, nansha nyunyi anyi diabolo!
 Ku diambuluisha dia Diulu didi dikosa mulu, tuetu—kudi Nzambi utudi tutendelela bonso babidi—
 Ambila anyima eu ne kanyinganyinga ka bungi, bikala, munda mua Aidenn wa kule,
 Neakuate nsongakaji wa tshijila udi banjelu babikila ne: Lenore—
 Kuata nsongakaji udi kayi umueneka ne udi ukenka udi banjelu babikila ne: Lenore."
 Quoth Mukoko, "Kakuyi mpata."
"Muaku au wikale tshimanyinu tshietu tshia ditapuluka, nyunyi anyi tshipupu!" Ngakela mbila, ne tshiji—
 "Pingana mu tshipepele tshikole ne ku muelelu wa Pluton wa Butuku!
 Kulekedi tshintu tshifiike bu tshimanyishilu tshia dishima didi anyima webe muambe to!
 Lekela bunkaya buanyi kabuyi bujika!—lekela tshintu tshidi pa mutu pa tshiibi tshianyi!
 Umbusha mukana muebe mu mutshima wanyi, ne umbusha tshimuenekelu tshiebe ku tshiibi tshianyi!"
 Quoth Mukoko, "Kakuyi mpata."
Ne Nyunyi, kayi wenda unyunguluka, utshidi anu musombe, utshidi anu musombe
 Pa tshimfuanyi tshitoke tshia Pallas anu pa mutu pa tshiibi tshia nzubu wanyi;
 Ne mêsu ende adi ne tshimuenekelu tshia demon udi ulota;
 Ne butoke bua muendu budi pa mutu pende budi buenda buenda buela mundidimbi wende panshi;
 Ne anyima wanyi udi upatuka mu mundidimbi udi mulale panshi
 Nebabandishibue—kakuyi mpata!
