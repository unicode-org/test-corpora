MANKOMENTO ED ANUL.

Say sekreto na sakey ya anlong, ya ag-onkulang ed inkaaligwas na sakey a panag-ayew, et walad layag na toon makadngel ed satan. Manpasakop ed anting-anting to, awaten so liknaan na manag-anlong: saya, diad tua, so ebat na saray marundunong no tepetan mo ra no antoy kablian to. Anggano say mismon manag-anlong, diad sananey a kipapasen to, et ibaga tod sika ya say arte to et singa labat panagpalikdo, say kakanen na manag-anito to, tan iyopresi ton ipanengneng ed sika so trick na satan, —agmo panisiaan. Alagar yo so mapropetikon oras to; insan mo iter so sarilim ed pilalek to, say liket o ot-ot to. "Wala itayo ed lima na Aro natan!" inkansion nen Gautier, diad makapaliket ya paraphrase nen Swinburne,—tan manlapud kabuasan anggad iselek na banwa et nababalang itayo ed mabiskeg a dayat: saksakey so aro, sakey a Mayo, sakey a bulaklak a bulaklak. Say aro et andi-anggaan, amin ya arum et aliwan tua tan ipulisay. Say pasingawey et walay anggaan to, say eksena et manguman; balet walay nagamoran tayo, say pakanodnonotan ed sakey ya anting-anting. No panon karakel so manag-anlong, dakel met so anting-anting. Walay anting-anting na Evanescence, satan so mangiiter na sankatageyan a karakpan tan grasya na aureole na Pathos. Ibagam ed si Landor so sakey a "labi na saray pakanodnonotan tan saray pan-ayew" parad si Rose Aylmer, tan nasumpal mo iya.

Tan natan, alaen so lima na sakey a minstrel na balon mundo, ya inmarawi ed sakey a manepeg a panaayaman ya amaarap ed saman ya bastos tan agmakatunongan ya Amerika ya, unong ed anengneng nen Baudelaire, "parad si Poe et sakey labat a baleg a prisoan ya sikatoy binmatik, dia tan dia, tekep na makapasinagem ya inkagulo na sakey a pinalsa a manlinawa ed mas malinis a mundo," tan diman "say bilay to ed loob, bilang espiritual a poet, balet ta buanges ni ingen, singa buanges sagpot ya makaliktar ed impluensya na sayan makapadimlan atmospera." Ibemben mo so sensitibon lima na sakey a managkanta ya manpapaga ya mankukugip na ontan, tan ibagam ed sikato so klima ya aromog to,—agbalot ed interon agew, naynay ed labi,—no aliwan say Atlantis ya nanlapuan to, anggan panon et sakey a pasen a salimbengan manlapud saray ketegan a ditan et sikatoy akakulong ed agew.

Diad sakey a bansa labat so pakayari ton mangitonton ed sika, tan diad sakey labat a labi et nayarian mon inabang so kugip to. Sakey a tract na aliwan Dalin odino Tawen: "Dalin na anggapoy too," ya anggapoy liwang, anggapoy panaon. Nia ray nababaingan, ya diad saray mata ra, singa saramay Cenci, say kamarerwa et makaromog na saray bintana anggaman say kanonotan et nalilingo; dia saray espiritu, ya manaanap na dalan ya mamaarap ed Andi-anggaan, et natunda tan naatrasado. Saya so limbo na "planetary souls," ya ditan et wala ray ag-inkaseguro ed liwawa na bulan, amin ya abalang ya panangaro tan ilusyon. Dia et arum so akapeket ed trance, say saksakey a painawa a nadampot; arum

Ontan so dalin, tan diad sakey a labi et linmoob itayo ditan,—sakey a labi na saray astral phases tan paulit-ulit iran tunog. Saray monodiya to et labinduan anlong, ya say musika ra et mansasagpot ya manguman balet ta pareho. Sakey-sakey so tanol da, a singa say tanol na brasen tan ebony ya relo, diad "The Masque of the Red Death," ya sengegan na itunda na saray waltzers ya walay "disconcerty tan tremolousness tan meditation," kapiganman ya onsabi so oras.

Diad amin na sarayan mistikon kadensia, say reklamo na The Raven , ya onkukurong ed portal, so manunan amakiwas ed paway na mundo. Anto ray bengatla ya ontutulong ed panggawa na sakey ya anlong,—tan panon karakel so tua ed saya, a singa ed karaklan, ed saman a rasa ya angitawag ed saray bard to ya "saray nanggawa"? Say sakey a kimey et tinawag manlapud andi-kakanaan. No iner so kawalaan to, mansiansia itan,—sakey a balon pinalsa, kabiangan na kayamanan na katooan. Tan pigaran nikadkaduman liriko, a mas ni nen say arum ya parehon makapamalsa, so mamakiwas ed sikatayon mannonot lamet no panon katapel so panagsulat na manag-anlong ed saray ag-amta

"ed saray kurti, tan mangiter ed dagem ya anggapoy
 Sakey a lokal a panaayaman, tan sakey a ngaran."

Balang sakey et singa anggapoy prototype to, balet amin da et makapasagyat ed sikatayo ed saray elemento ya inala ed anino na Supernatural. Natan say sankatageyan ya imahinasyon et nipaakar ed kamarerwa na bengatla; nayarin saya so mangipuyan odino andi ed Pantasya ya saray totoon walaan na saray imahen et interlunar ya aliwan malinew. Balet, sakey ed sarayan liriko, diad mas melag a paraan to, so mangaapekta ed sikatayo tekep na pakalikna na nikadkaduman paraan, a singa met ed saray makapadinayew a kimey na sakey a supernatural a cast,—say "Faustus" nen Marlowe, say "Faust" nen Goethe, "Manfred," odino anggan saramay ethereal ya obra maestra, "The Tempest" tan "A MidSummers'." Masulok nen say sakey, anggaman nikadkaduma, so walaan na pigaran pabelat odino refrain ya mansasakit ed pakanodnonotan,—no narengel la, agbalot nalingwanan, a singa say tono na matalag ya nausar balet ta nikadkaduman organ-stop. Say sankatalonggaringan ed saratan et say "Lenore" nen Bürger, satan ya makapataktakot tan makapasagyat ya ballad, ya makapasagyat tan makapasagyat ed saray managpatalos. Pigpigara so mangiburi ya say makapakelkelaw ya "Rime of the Ancient Mariner" nen Coleridge so walad uloan da. Say "Le Juif-Errant" et walaan na saray ibabaga, no si Beranger et mas maong ya manag-anlong; tan, balet lapud arawi ira ed simpatya na karaklan, say "The Lady of Shalott" tan "The Blessed Damozel" so nayarin iyarum ed listaan. Niiter ed si Edgar Allan Poe so duaran liriko, "The Bells" tan The Raven , ya balang sakey ed saratan, anggaman nayarin agtanton marakep nen saray di Tennyson tan Rossetti, et nikadkaduma. Say "Ulalume," anggaman parehon nikadkaduman tan makapasagyat, et walaan na unibersal a kalidad ya kabiangan na subok tayo.

Balet say The Raven , singa say "The Bells" tan "Annabel Lee," et ipapasen toy inkasikato ed dakel tan pigara. Inkuan ko la ed arum a pasen a saray mas matalag a produksyon nen Poe et singa ed siak "saramay walaan na itsura, anggan panon, na ag-inkayadyari,—a ditan et sikatoy onsuko ed saray liknaan to, bangta saray ompatey a napelag tan saray inka-musika, sankaermenan, so manlalapud sikato ya agto amta." Saya so siansia nin sisiaen ko; balet, diad balon panaral ko ed sayan anlong, mas makapadinayew ak nen say kapiganman manlapu nen ugaw ak ni. Say maapit a pikakabat so mangibaga na pabor ed kada tuan kimey na arte. Sagyaten so too, ya agmakaamta na arte odino mangaasikaso ed satan, ya usisaen so sakey a anlong odino painting, tan no panon kapeles ya naala so puersa na satan ed sikato! Diad tua, sikatoy manpasobra ed relatibon kablian na inmunan maong a kimey ya akaalay imano to. Say Raven , et walaan met na saray kalidad ya anggan sakey ya eksperto et nepeg ya pandinayewan to. Anggapo lay arum ya anlong na autor to ya mas malinew so motibo to. Say "The Haunted Palace" et singa met lanlamang ed saray pinilin manbabasa, balet ag-inawat nen Poe itan ya masilib ya alegoriya parad kalbo ya panagsukimat. Say Uwak et sigpot ya okupado ed tipikal a tema na autor—say agnabawi ya inkaandi na sakey ya idolo tan marakep a bii; balet diad arum a basiyan, ontan met, say instinct na publiko et duga ed pangipasen ton saya so representative poem to.

Say sakey a toon makabat et kaslakan ya ontalona ed itatalona na sakey a sagpot, tan aliwan naynay ya saya so sankatalonggaringan to. Say pakabidbiran et mas tampol parad inka-postpone. Agto itan nagamoran, a singa say kayamanan na sakey a makulangkulang, sinsilyo kayari na sinsilyo, balet "agton balot odino amin." Tan ontan met ed arum ya ambisyon: say kortesano, sundalo, aktor,—antokaman so betang da,—balang sakey et manbibilang na itatalona to manlapud sakey a suwertin inkagawa. Say Raven nen Poe, anggaman ed pakapatnagan, et parad sikato et "say manok ya manggagawa na dagem ya onsiplog." Say manag-anlong so nanayam ed New-York, nen panaoy ambetel nen 1844-'45, ya akaromog na trabaho ed papel nen Willis, "The Evening Mirror," tan inala toy sueldo to panamegley na saray kontribusyon ed arum a pasen. Diad loob na anem taon et sikatoy sakey ya aktibon managsulat, tan sikatoy walaan na propesyonal a reputasyon; sikatoy nirespeto tan ag-inmabobon, tan anggapoy naandi ed betang to ed makapuy so sueldo ton dyurnalismo ed saman ya agew. Dakel met so ginawa to ed saray sankaabigan ya kimey to,—singa saray salaysay a singa say "Ligeia" tan "The Fall of the House of Usher," (say unor et walaan na mistikon katumbas, diad bersikulo, na "A Lost Mind" nen Elihu Vedder,) singa saray analytic feats a singa say "The Gold Bug" tan "The Mystery of Marie Roget." Sikatoy akagawa na saray proselita ed arum a bansa, tan sikatoy akagamor na andukey a pakayari ed kanonotan na saray Pranses. Naaralan to so dilin pakayari tan kakapuyan to, tan sikatoy walad sankaabigan a kipapasen to, tan aliwan andian na reputasyon. Balet anggapoy insulat to ya walad dila na amin. Diad matalag tan malimpek a kimey et kaukolan ya iyarum so pigaran popular a bengatla pian naala so inkalapagan a dumerengel ed mismon panaon na sakey.

Diad panamegley na industriya na nantutumbokan iran biograpo nen Poe, say hit ya ginawa na The Raven et nagmaliw ya mabetbet ya istorya. Say malangwer nin asawa na manag-anlong, si Virginia, et onkukupas la ed arap na mata to, balet ta nansiansia ni na sakey taon ed anino na patey. Say andukey tan abeban kuarto ed abung ya asingger ed Bloomingdale Road et singa bantog bilang kuarto ya angisulatan nen Rouget de l'Isle na Marseillaise. Nadngel na amin ya say anlong, ya akapirma na "Quarles," et pinmaway ed "American Review," ya walay pseudo-editorial ya komento ed porma to; ya akaawat si Poe na samploran dolyar parad satan; ya si Willis, say sankamaaroan tan sankaabebaan ya maimon ed saray uso ya arbiter, so angimprinta lamet ed satan tekep na eulogy ya tampol ya nagmaliw ya town-talk. Naandi so amin ya panduaruwa no siopay nansulat ed satan nen si Poe so angibaga ed satan diad sakey a pantitipon na saray literatura, tan diad loob na sakey a panaon et sikato so sankatalonggaringan ed saray autor na Amerika. Say impaka-hit to so amakiwas tan amaseseg ed sikato. Singa met ed sananey tan mas mapaatagey a satirist, sikato met so akaromog na "bengatla na tiagew" anggan "diad ungol na saray insekto." Makapaermen ta taloy taon so linmabas,—sakey a panaon na impluensya, inkamapaatagey, ermen, balet ta naynay a walay imahinasyon odino kritikal a kimey,—sakbay na unor a inkatalo, sakbay ya inmepas so kurtina ed bilay to a parad sikato et diad tua et sakey a trahedya, tan sikatoy inmabuloy ed "say Conqueror Worm."

Say "The American Review: A Whig Journal" et sakey a maong a magasin ed saman a panaon, walay duaran kolum to, inimprinta ed maong a papel ya malinew so insulat to, tan walay litrato ton mezzotint. Diad pegley na dakel a bengatla ya abeba nen say kaplesan ya estandarte, wala ray lugan na satan ya maliket ya awaten na siopaman ya editor. Say inmunan tomo, nen 1845, et walaan na saray artikulo nen Horace Greeley, Donald Mitchell, Walter Whitman, Marsh, Tuckerman, tan Whipple. Say makapadinayew ya anlong nen Ralph Hoyt, "Daan," so pinmaway ed sayan tomo. Tan nia so taloran liriko nen Poe: "Say Syudad ed Dayat," "Say Lawak na Kaguloan," tan Say Uwak . Duara ed saraya so nipaalagey,—ontan so paraan to,—manlapud saray akaunan impanaral, balet say unor a ngaran so pinmaway a singa balon insulat, tan ngalngali singa ed sikatayo natan. Say balikas ya ag-itan nirebisa kayari na satan et lingo. Labinsakey ya angkekelag a pananguman ed teksto na magasin so pinmatnag ed The Raven and Other Poems , 1845, sakey a libro ya ag-abayag et alikna na manag-anlong ya apaseseg ya iyopresi ed publiko. Saraya so maslak a pananguman ed punctuation, odino ed saksakey a salita, say unor a klase et ginawa pian napaaligwas so epekto na alliteration. Diad marakep ya edisyon nen Mr. Lang na bersikulo nen Poe, ya impaway to ed "Parchment Library," impanengneng toy instinct na sakey ya iskolar, tan makabat so ginawa to, diad impawil tod teksto ed tomo ya asalambit la, a singa niiter ed London issue nen 1846. Say "standard" ya koleksion nen Griswold na saray kimey na manag-anlong et dakel so lingo to. Inulit iratan na saray editor ed saginonor, ya akagawa met na saray lingo ra. Balet say teksto na The Raven , lapud saray kerew ed autor parad saray kopya na manuskrito, et lalon nirebisa to; diad tua, inimprinta to itan ed Richmond, sakbay na impatey to, ya insalat toy "seraphim whose foot-falls" parad "angels whose faint foot-falls," diad koma-labinapat ya stanza. Say kaplesan ya teksto tayo, sirin, anggaman singa nen 1845, et medyo inuman na saginonor a panagbasa na manag-anlong, tan saya, iisipen ko, so sankasustoan tan sankaepektiboan a bersion na sayan saksakey a anlong. Say sankagrabian ya pananguman manlapud sankaunaan ya bersion et pinmatnag, balet, diad tomo nen 1845; say koma-labinsakey ya stanza et walaan na sarayan linya, ya walay depekto ed rima tan walay depekto ed anlong:

"Narel ed sakey ya agmaliket a katawan, ya anggapoy panangasi ton Desyang
 Maples ya tinmumbok tan mas maples ya tinmumbok—kanian, nen mansamba si Hope,
 Pinmawil so Stern Despair, imbes ya say masamit ya Ilalo ya insipan to—
 Say makapaermen ya ebat, 'Anggapo la!'"

Maong no arum, tan bantog, iran manag-anlong so makaseguro met a gawaen dan lawas maaligwas iray pananguman da. Si Poe et naynay ya manguuman ed daiset ya palabas na bersikulo to, tan kaslakan et papaaligwasen to itan. Say Raven so sankaunaan ed pigaran anlong ya ngalngali to la nasumpal antis ya inimprinta. Nayarin saramay mangaasikaso ed anlong et daiset so naandi ed impatey to. Lapud marundunong ed prosa, agbalot nansulat na bersikulo pian makagawa na anlong. Nen walay refrain na imahen ya nan-aburido ed sikato, say liriko ya resulta et say inspirasyon, unong ya inkuan ton mismo, na sakey a pilalek, aliwan sakey a gagala. Saya so agawa ed saray panaon ya matalag ya ngalngali la mangiter na rason ed teorya nen Fairfield ya balang sakey et resulta na sakey a krisis ed nerbiyos.

Anto sirin so angiter ed manag-anlong na pakapatnagan ed The Raven ? Manlapud anton malimpek a pundasyon so inmalagey a kalkalna ed sakey a musikan kalkalnan inlinawa? Singa dati, masulok ya sakey a bengatla so linma ed impamaalagey na sakey a makatantandan anlong. No imanoen iray mas andukey a sermon ya mabetbet ya ipupulong ed antikey tan agtanton makapasagyat iran teksto, ilaloan kon ag ak nababalaw ed sayan pantotongtong nipaakar ed sakey a liriko, —lalo la ed samay ginawa na sakey ya artist a singa si Doré a tema na makulangkulang ya ilustrasyon. Anggad agano ni et inisip ko ya sayan piyesa, tan pigara ya insulat na autor to kayari na inkiwala to, et nikadkaduma lapud ag-ira binmaleg manlapud mikrobyo diad bersikulo to nen ugaw ni. Balet impanengneng nen Mr. Fearing Gill ed siak so pigaran agni nipalapag ya estansa nen Poe, ya insulat to nen koma-labinwalon taon to, tan say titulo to et, "Say Demonyo na Apoy." Say manuskrito et ompatnag ya walad inmunan sulat na manag-anlong, tan say inkamatua to et napaneknekan na pamilyan walaan na satan ed loob na kapalduan siglo. Likud ed saray sankalinewan ya mikrobyo na "The Bells" tan "The Haunted Palace" et wala ray pigaran linya ya medyo mangisusuheri na gapoan tan pansara na The Raven . Nipaakar ed ritmo na anlong tayo, say pangikompara ed saray petsa et mangipapanengneng ya saya so naimpluensyaan na ritmo na "Lady Geraldine's Courtship." Si Poe so sakey ed saray inmunan angigalang ed inkadunong nen Miss Barrett; insulat tod sikato iray tinipon ton anlong bilang "say sankamaongan ed sekso to," tan sikatoy walad simpatya ed liriko ton paraan. Saray linya manlapud love-poem to,

"Diad sakey a manngunguronguro ya ag segurado, diad dagem, say purpura a kurtina
 Manpapasnok tan manpapasnok ed kaliberliber na ag-onkikiwas ya amputin kilay to,"

akaromog na echo ed saraya:

"Tan say singa seda ya maermen ya ag seguradon ungol na kada purpuran kurtina
 Aliketan ak—pinano to ak na makapadinayew iran takot ya ag ko nin balot alikna nensaman."

Dia et inala nen Poe so sakey a pribilehyo ya lapud satan et magaspang ton binalaw si Longfellow, tan anggapoy toon nansagpot ed sarili ton pasen ya anggapoy maples ya pakadetektaan tan panagdusa. Diad melodiya tan stanzaic form, nanengneng tayo ya aliwan mandumaan so duaran anlong, balet diad motibo et sigpot iran mandumaan. Say mabunlok ya manag-anlong et anggapoy arum ya alikna to no ag say tuan inka-orihinal na manag-anlong. "Sayan mabilabilay ya insulat!" inkuanto,—"sayan pakayari ya nalilikna!... Say baleg ya manag-anlong tayo, si Mr. Browning, ya autor na 'Paracelsus,' &c., et entusiastiko ed pandinayew to ed ritmo." Si Mr. Ingram, kayari ton tinukoy so "Lady Geraldine," et marundunong ya angipabitar na sananey a lapuan ya nayarin nanlapuan nen Poe. Nen 1843, si Albert Pike, say kapalduan Griego, kapalduan-frontiersman, manag-anlong na Arkansas, so angiimprinta ed "The New Mirror," ya parad satan et mansusulat si Poe, na pigaran bersikulo ya say titulo to et "Isadore," balet ta inuman na autor tan tinawag ton "The Widowed Heart." Pinili ko manlapud rebisyon nen Mr. Pike so onggendan ya estansa, ya saray manunan kabiangan to et mipara ed orihinal a bersion:

"Agko la nareen so panaakar ko ed saray kuarto mi, ag ko la ipatogtog iray kansion mi,
 Say mankirlap ya agew so onsisinag a maong ed ag-alinisan a datal;
 Say mocking-bird et akayurong ni tan mangansion, O maermen ya strain!
 Ta say pusok et singa lurem na otonyo ya onlelereg ed uran;
 Naandi ka ed siak ed andi-anggaan, Isadore!"

Dia et walay andukey a sukat, pampareho na refrain, tan say pangiloob na sakey a manok ya say kansion to et mamapaermen. Wala ni ray arum ya dalan ya nayarin tumboken na saray mausisa; nagkalalo la, sakey a bersikulo ya pinili nen Mr. Ingram manlapud unor ya repaso nen Poe ed "Barnaby Rudge":

"Say uwak, met, * * * et nayarin ginawa, a mas ni nen say nanenengneng tayo natan, a kabiangan na konsepto na makapadinayew a Barnaby. * * * Say karakter to so nayarin nanggawa, nipaakar ed samay makulangkulang, a parehon kabiangan a singa gagawaen, diad musika, say panangibaga nipaakar ed dagem."

Anggaman ontan, kayari na impangipabitar ed sarayan mikrobyo tan pamparehoan, say kablian na sayan anlong et naromog nin siansia ed inka-orihinal to. Say progresibon musika, say detalye na eksena tan say mankontraan a liwawa tan anino,—nagkalalo ed amin, say espiritual a pilalek na labi, so manggagawa ed satan a kimey na sakey a makabat a henyo. Nipaakar ed makapataktakot a manok, sikatoy aliwan singa arum ya uwak ed pamilya to, manlapud "twa corbies" tan "three ravens" na saray balladist anggad say "Grip" nen Barnaby. Anggapoy pakapatnagan na cawing rook ya mansasnit ed saray torre na inmuunan atateng tan manlalapud lawak na heraldry; anggapoy boding phantom ya inkanta nen Tickell ya, nen,

"mankelyaw ed bintana to na mamitlo,
 Inpakpak na uwak so payak to,
 Amta na maong na samay marikit ya naandiay panangaro
 Say solemnen boding sound."

Say uwak nen Poe et sakey a nikadkaduman konsepto; say inkatoo na sakey a maneermen ya walay ot-ot tan inkaandi-ilalo to; sakey a sable ya walaan na memorya, say mansiansian managsulat na kadederal, sakey a klase na ag nauman. Lapud sikatoy akatakas ed Styx, manlapud "the Night's Plutonian shore," sikatoy singa samay mismon kamarerwa na mantetepet,—na samay agmakayari, agto labay, ya oninum na klase na nepenthe, lapud say pakanodnonotan ed si Lenore so atilak ed sikato, tan diad isabi na ermen to et anggan satan et niiyan ed biek.

Say Raven et nayarin ipasen met ya representative ya anlong na autor to, lapud pangipapanengneng tod amin ya kanonotan to no antoy nepeg ya anlong. Naromog iratan ed saray essay to nipaakar ed "The Poetic Principle," "The Rationale of Verse," tan "The Philosophy of Composition." Inkuan nen Poe ya "diad Musika, nayarin, say kamarerwa et ngalngali la makadampot ed baleg a gagala ya no naimpluensyaan na Poetic Sentiment, sikatoy mansasagpot—say pamawala na makapadinayew a Karakpan.... Say bersikulo et ag nayarin iter na mas maabig nen say abeban odino agtanton makayarin musika"; balet lamet, say bersikulo ya talagan "Anlong na Salita" et "Say Ritmikon Panagpalsa na Karakpan,"—saya tan anggapo lay arum. Say tono na sankatageyan a Karakpan et sakey a Kaermenan. Say sankaermenan ed saray topic et say Patey. Saya so nepeg ya mipara ed Karakpan. "Say impatey, sirin, na sakey a marakep a bii so, andi-duaruwan, sanka-poetika a tema ed mundo,—tan ontan met et andi-duaruwan saray bibil a sankaabigan a matukoy ed ontan a tema et saramay bibil na sakey ya inateyan ya inaro." Sarayan unor a balikas et inaon manlapud makapainteres ya impanaral nen Poe ed sayan mismon anlong, balet ta eksakton ipapanengneng da so inkalapagan a laknab na bersikulo to. Say kapantokan na "The Bells" et say ag-ontutundan tono na saray ghoul, ya manpapasirayew ed pangipabelat ed puso na too. Say "Lenore," The Raven , "The Sleeper," "To One in Paradise," tan "Ulalume" et manporma na tenebrose symphony,—tan say "Annabel Lee," ya insulat ya unor ed amin, et mangipapanengneng ya sakey a tema so angitonton ed sikato anggad sampot. Lamet, saraya et anggapoy kana no aliwan musikal, tan arum so naapektoan na satan a kalidad na Fantastic ya mamapalikdo na liknaan na pankelaw, tan mangarum na balon takot ed mismon ot-ot. Diad amin et malimpek ya akasulat, diad silong na anino, say ideyal ya panangaro na manag-anlong,—ya mabetbet ya ag-ipapanengneng ed arum a pasen,—say imponpon ya asawa nen Usher, say Lady Ligeia, nen katuaan so kapara na dilin inkatoo to. Iisipen ko ya say panangaro na sakey ya artist ed sakey ya "walad porma" et agbalot makapipara ed debosyon to ed sakey ya ideyal. Say bii ya asingger ed sikato et nepeg ya manggawa na saray anting-anting to, mansusublayan tan anggapoy andukey, man-akit ed sikato na agnaparaan a pandurumaan, odino mankontento ya itunda so sakey a kabiangan na katooran to. Kaukolan ya sikatoy naakit na ag naabot, tan saya so ag to la nasarag ed panangaro to ed creative art.

Si Poe, singa si Hawthorne, so linmoob ed inkagba na Romantic school, tan anggapo lay mas maliket nen say sikato ya manliket ed desyang to. Balet say puso to et walad saray romancer tan saray Oriental odino Gothic ya epekto ra. Say imbension to, ya mayaman ed saray istoryan prosa, et singa sikatoy tinaynan nen nansulat na bersikulo; tan say panangukom to et angibaga ed sikato ya saray andukey ya romantikon anlong et mas mandedependi ed inkagawa nen say inspirasyon,—tan pian nibalikas so anlong na romansa, magenap la ray liriko. Kanian say teorya to, ya malinew ya mitunosan ed saray limitasyon to, ya "say 'andukey ya anlong' et sakey a patatdang a kontradiksyon ed saray termino." Saray kabiangan na The Raven et daiset tan simpli: sakey a laki, sakey a manok, tan say makapataktakot a pakanodnonotan ed sakey a bii. Balet say piraso so mangiiter na marakep a panangipanengneng na romantikon materyal. Antoy walad sikatayo? Say pegley-labi; say malimpek a silir a walaan na saray libro na alingwanan lan istorya; say estudyante,—sakey a modernon Hieronymus; say gripo na uwak ed kahon; say ambetel a labi tan say ompatey ya apoy; saray singa seda ya akasabit ya inyalis na dagem; saray kugip tan aliwan malinew ya agpanmatalek ed onkukurang a kabilungetan; say andeket, makapakelaw a manok ed tapew na amputin pagew to; saray kagawaan ya gawad violet velvet tan say gloating lamp. Amin na sayan epekto na situasyon, liwawa, kolor, tanol, et puron romantiko, tan melodramatiko ni ingen, balet ta walay kalidad ton anlong ya matalag ya ipapanengneng na melodrama, tan sigpot ya mangipapatnag ed "andi-anggaan a pilalek, andi-anggaan ya ot-ot" na manag-anlong.

Say ritmikon istruktura na The Raven et seguradon makapangiter na impresyon. Say rhyme, aliteration, say burden, say stanzaic form, et ginawa tekep na nikadkaduman inkadunong. Andi-duaruwan say manag-anlong so abigla ed inkasusto na saray musikal a trochaics nen Miss Barrett, diad "eights," tan nagkalalo la ed inkauksoy ya inadapta nen asingger lan manampot so "Lady Geraldine":

"'Saray mata,' inkuanto, 'natan et onkukurong ed siak! Sikayo kasi so mata ya amawala ed siak?
 Saray mankirlap a mata, a singa saray daan ya alahas ya akapuesto ed estatua na Parian!
 Diad silong na satan ya kalmadon amputin moling, kasin sika so lawas manpopoolan na ambetel
 O'er say nisulinek a buer-desierto na pusok tan say bilay ko ya ag-asumpal?'"

Say artistikon pangiloob to na komatlon rima ed komadua tan komapat a linya, tan say inyarum to na komaliman linya tan unor a refrain, so nanggawa ed stanza na The Raven . Say pirmin aliterasyon et singa anggapoy sagpot, tan mabetbet ya makapasagyat iray rima ed loob na saray linya; bangta say refrain odino pabelat so mangokontrol ed interon kimey. Dia met et sikatoy akagunggonaan ed impanaral nen Miss Barrett ed saray ballad tan romaunt ed dilin lenguahe to tan arum niran lenguahe. Say "refrain" et say pan-akit ya uusaren na sakey a manag-anlong odino musikero pian naegnaan so manliliberliber a layag, —say paulit-ulit a panpipilalek na Kalikasan ed inmunan strain. Lanang kon pandinayewan iray mararakep ya kanta na English songtress,—"The Nightingales, the Nightingales," "Margret, Margret," "My Heart and I," "Toll slowly," "The River flowesh on," "Pan, Pan is dead," tan arum nira. Inusar to met so tatawagen kon Repetend, diad impangusar tod Poece ed powet ya amawala lamet na amin a pompis itan:

"O maliket iran mabilay! anggapoy dila
 Say karakpan da et nayarin mangibaga:
 Sakey a subol na panangaro so pinmaway ed pusok,
 Ag ko amta ya benendisyonan ko ra:
 Seguradon kinasian ak na maomaong a santo ko,
 Tan binendisyonan ko ra ya agko amta."

Ginawa nen Poe so komaliman linya na stanza to parad mahiko na paulit-ulit. Nanmatalek ed satan ya maong diad pigaran anlong to ed saginonor,—"Lenore," "Annabel Lee," "Ulalume," tan "For Annie." Nawalaan itan na makapaermen tan makapaermen a musika, inisip ko, manlapud saray "masamit ya impluensya," na saray Afric burdens tan repetends ya kinansion ed sikato nen ugaw ni, ya intunos da ed katutuboan dan melodiya so boses na manag-anlong tayo ed Abalaten.

Say "The Philosophy of Composition," say impanaral to ed The Raven , et sakey a teknikal a panagsukimat ed paraan tan istruktura to. Agla kaukolan ya sigpot ya iter so kredito ed impangibaga to ed saray ag-inkayadyari to, odino say impangibaga to ed saray kakaaro to ya say pangiparungtal to ed sayan panagpalikdo to et sakey labat ed saray intelektual a panagpalikdo to. No indesinyo to lan masakbay so kompleton kimey, ngalngali ag to la nagawaan so ontan lan inka-apekto na prelude na saray rattle-pan rhymes ed mananam a melodiya na komaduan stanza,—anggan diad teorya to nipaakar ed makapakelkelaw. Siempre say sakey ya artist, no asumpal to lay sakey a kimey, et nanengneng to, singa samay inmunan Artista, ya maong itan, tan nanengneng to no akin ya maong itan. Say ontumbok ya panagsukimat, a kaiba so pangipulisay ed antokaman a sagradon apoy, so nayarin gawaen a tampol. Say sisiaen ko et say inmunan konsepto tan magaspang a draft na sayan anlong et sinmabi legan ya naynay ya onsasabi so inspirasyon; ya anengneng na autor to no panon itan ya naperpekto, ya inter tod satan so unor iran nengneng ya adeskribe ed kapitulo to nipaakar ed Komposisyon, tan say unor, sirin, et aliwan sigpot a palso odino sigpot a tua. Say makaderal ed ontan ya panagsukimat et natukso to so sakey a manbabaroy ya manisip ya saray artipisyal a proseso et nayarin onlampas ed imahinasyon. Say gagala na inka-henyo et say panbantay ed saray sekreto na oras na panagpalsa to. Saray pakanengnengan ed sagpot, saray makapabiglan eksperimento, ya akauna nen say pananalo, a singa diad sketch-work nen Hawthorne ya agano nin niparungtal, so mangiter na agnasaliw ya instruksion tan panamaseseg ed masimoon ya artist. Balet sakey ya boluntaryon mangiparungtal ed Muse to ed pakanengneng na amin ya onsasabi et nepeg ton nodnonoten so agawa ed si King Candaules.

Iisipen na mundo ya si Poe et "makulangkulang a toon henyo." Diad agano ni et nadngel kon sikatoy abitla bilang "sakey ya singa amin et labay dan ibalikas, deralen o pabaingan." Balet ompatnag ed siak a say ag-inkayadyari to so anampot ed makapaermen ya impatey to, tan manlapu la’d saman et nantultuloy so depensa to, tan say inkabantog to a singa mapekder ya imbulaslas a nayarin ipikasi na sakey a maniirap tan marundunong ya autor diad oras na ilalo to. Say panagpauges tan panagpauges nen Griswold so amaabig ed sikato. Saray kritiko tan biograpo so linma ed unaan tekep na nantutumbokan a panagsuppiat, tekep na panangigalang ed karakter to, tekep na balon edisyon na saray kimey to. Saray mismon sulat to tan saray angkekelag ya inkagawa ed karera to so walad arap tayo; say rekord, maong man o mauges, et kabkabat na karaklan. Anggapoy apelador ya akaawat na mas maabagey tan mamerdonan panangukom. Saray ag-inkayadyari to ed bilay et nanlapud rehyon tan panaon to, nayarin lalon nanlapud dilin kakapuyan na linawa to. Andi-duaruwan say kaliberliber to et aliwan sakey a manbabantay ed maong tan ag-inkabalanse na kalikasan manlapud saray kapeligroan ed paway tan loob. Say sankabiskegan a linawa to, a magmaliw a katawan ed inkasikato, so akagamor parad sikato na "saman a tawir na kairapan." Inamin to ya sikato so agmaliket ya katawan na manok, say asakitan ya mansasakit ed sayan anlong. Balet say sikato et walaan na sigpot a kabiangan na satan a makapabiglan ugali ya manliliket ed pakapatnagan na dilin kadederal to. Walay liket ed panggawa na atagey a betang na sakey: amin tayo et gladiator, ya onaakis ya Ave Imperator! Pian inaon so katuaan nen Burke: "Diad ermen say liket so sankatageyan nin siansia, tan say kairapan ya sasagmaken tayo et anggapoy piparaan to ed sigpot ya ot-ot, ya lanang ya makapadimla, tan panggugunaetan tayon ekalen a tampol." Mas arawi ni so inyakar nen Poe, tan sikatoy sakey ya artist anggan diad trahedya na karera to. No, unong ed dilin sisiaen to, say ermen tan say pakaandi na karakpan so sankatageyan a tema na anlong, tan say liknaan na anlong so sankagrabian a liket ed dalin, sirin say ermen tan kabilungetan na abuyak a bilay to so ag-onkulang ed mabetbet a bayad da.

Diad ontumbok iran pahina, walay balon alimbawa na inkadunong na sakey ya artist ya mangipapanengneng ed interpretasyon to ed sakey a bantog ya anlong. Si Gustave Doré, say unor ya kimey na lapis to et walad arap tayo, et aliwan say pintor, odino anggan say draughtsman, parad saray realista ya mankekerew na katuaan na tono, pigura, tan inkayadyari. Saray ontan a bengatla et ag to tanton pampapagaan nen say panggawa na kurti tan distansya, liwawa tan anino, ya ontulong ed gagala to, —ya say pamakiwas ed kamarerwa, say imahinasyon, na toon onnenengneng. Ginawa to iya diad impamabiskeg tod sikatayo, diad panamegley na makapakelkelaw tan mabetbet ya makapadinayew iran konsepto na saray bengatlan agnasabi tan napno na ermen odino gloria. Nibaga ya no saray kimey to et aliwan balibalin painting, bilang litrato et talagan balibali iratan. Bilang sakey ya "literary artist," tan ontan so inkatoo to, say puersa to et direktan misiglaotan ed dramatikon imbension na autor to, tan say makpel ya inkakpel na espiritu ya angigapo ed sikato. Kanian say intalona to ed si Rabelais, diad "Le Juif-Errant," "Les Contes Drolatiques," tan "Don Quixote," tan lapud saya, diad kasunian, say agto pangibalikas ed karakpan na Tennyson's Idyls, na "Il Paradiso," na saray Hebreon pastoral, tan arum niran teksto ya mankaukolan na panangitandoro, odino inka-sweet tan repose. Sikatoy nianak ya marundunong ed saray makapataktakot, tan diad panamegley na espisyal a pakatalos et sarag ton ipanengneng iray multo na sakey ya utek ya walay aswang. Nanengneng tayo ray bengatla a singa ed pakanengneng na saray personahe to, tan diad mismon mata na Wandering Jew, say mankelaw a Don, odino say anak na managpanday na balitok ya say panlabayan to so mamapaatagey ed Ari diad tindaan ed Pont-au-Change. Diad inkagawa na saray bengatla et sikatoy nepeg ya naakit ed kada obra maestra na bersikulo odino prosa ya tinawag kon nikadkaduma. Saray abeban panarian so tinawag ed serbisyo to; saray bato, kakiewan tan palandey to, say katawenan a mismo, et mabilabilay ya walay motibo tan diablerie to. No sikatoy nambilay pian mangiyilustra ed si Shakespeare, anengneng tayo komon so makapadinayew ya impantrato ed si Caliban, saray Witches, say bagyo ed "Lear"; balet seguradon nepeg ton kuestionen iray ideyal to nipaakar ed si Imogen odino si Miranda. Say karakpan ya puro tan simpli, tan say perpekton inkaekselente na satan, et matalag ton natalosan.

Balet walay karakpan ed saray desinyo to parad "Ancient Mariner," anggano aliwan tua iratan, tan say pantutumbokan da et matalag ed sakey a serye nen Doré. Say Rime so angiter ed sikato na andukey ya istorya, a walaan na dakel a pananguman ed eksena. Diad The Raven say tanol tan kolor et mansiansian monotono tan anggapoy panguman na pasen o okasyon. Antoy resulta? Inyopresi nen Doré so nantutumbokan a pandurumaan ed tema unong ed inisip to, "say enigma na patey tan say halusinasyon na sakey ya ag naligliwa a kamarerwa." Diad arum ed sarayan drowing et napatnagan iray lingo to; arum so mangipapatnag na mabiskeg ya inka-orihinal to, tan saray sankaabigan a kalidad to ya, bilang sakey a manag-drowing, et sikatoy akaalagey a bukor. Malinew ya walay pamparehoan na pankikimey nen Poe tan Doré. Mas malinew ya ompatnag iya no sinali na unor so "Tales of the Grotesque and Arabesque." Parehon mabetbet ya onla ed elf-land na pantasya tan romansa. Diad saray melodramatikon agawaan da, diad panamegley na pakayari ra ed supernatural, et apaliisan da so peligro ed baetan na ideyal tan andi-kakanaan. Si Poe so mas tuan managdayew ed Marakep; say panangaro to ed satan et sakey a makapasagyat a pilalek, tan dia et sikatoy akiulop ed managdrowing to. Poeta o artist, say patey et diad kaunoran et manguuman ed amin: diad loob na anino na sable harbinger to, say simbolikon krayola nen Vedder so matukoy ya mangipapaarap ed sikara, balet ta binalkot to ra na manto na imortal a kakabatan tan pakayari. Sakey ya Amerikanon bii so nanggawa na imahen na sakey a Henyo ya singa bitewen so mata to ya walay unor a silew to, say marakep ya itsura na sakey ya say pakanengneng to et nanenengneng to, balet ta ag nayarin ibalikas na saray bibil to, iray misteryo na dalin ya labas ni nen say "puerta na sakey a bantog a lubok."

Edmund C. Stedman.

SAY UWAK.

Aminsan ed pegley-labi et makapaermen, legan kon mannononot, makapuy tan naksawan,
 Diad loob na dakel a makapadinayew tan makapainteres a tomo na alingwanan lan istorya,
 Legan kon ontatalindeg, ngalngali ak la onugip, biglan walay tinmawag,
 Singa sakey a toon kalmadon manpapasnok, manpapasnok ed puerta na kuartok.
 "Walay bisita," inkuan ko, "manpapaknol ed puerta na kuartok—
 Saya labat, tan anggapo lay arum."
Ah, malinew kon nanonotan nen makapaermen ya Disyembre,
 Tan kada mandumaan ya ompapatey ya apoy et nanggawa na aswang to ed datal.
 Magunaet kon pinirawat so kabuasan:—andi-kakanaan so impananap kon mangutang
 Manlapud saray librok et ompaway so ermen—ermen parad abalang ya si Lenore—
 Parad matalag tan mankirlap a marikit ya inngaran na saray anghel ya Lenore—
 Anggapoy ngaran dia ed andi-anggaan.
Tan say singa seda ya maermen ya ag seguradon ungol na kada purpuran kurtina
 Aliketan ak—pinano to ak na makapadinayew iran takot ya ag ko nin balot alikna nensaman;
 Kanian natan, pian napatunda so pitik na pusok, inmalagey ak ya manguulit
 "'T so sakey a bisita ya onkekerew na iloob ed puerta na kuartok
 Walay sakey ya atrasadon bisita ya onkekerew ya onloob ed puerta na kuartok;—
 Saya la, tan anggapo lay arum."
Diad kaplesan et binmiskeg so kamarerwak; manduaruwa insan ag la,
 "Katawan," inkuan ko, "odino Madam, kerewen ko so panamerdona yo;
 Balet say tua et manpapainawa ak, tan maalwar kan linma ya man-rap,
 Tan makapuy kan sinmabi a manpapaknol, manpapaknol ed puerta na kuartok,
 Agko segurado ya nadngel ta ka"—dia et nilukasan kon maong so puerta;—
 Bilungetan diman, tan anggapo lay arum.
Diad aralem na kabilungetan ya onnenengneng, abayag ak ya akaalagey diman ya mankelaw, natatakot,
 Manduaruwa, mankukugip na saray kugip ya anggapo nin balot so too ya akapangikugip nensaman;
 Balet ag-asumpal so kareenan, tan say kabilungetan et ag-angiter na tanda,
 Tan say salitan nibaga labat diman et say inbulong ya salitan, "Lenore!"
 Saya so inbulong ko, tan walay echo ya inmebat ed salitan, "Lenore!"
 Saya labat tan anggapo lay arum.
Pawil ed silir ya onliliber, say amin a kamarerwak ed loob ko et manpopoolan,
 Agnambayag et walay nadngel ko lamet ya panag-tap, ya medyo mas maksil nen say dati.
 "Tua," inkuan ko, "tua ya walay walad bintana ko;
 Nengnengen ko pa, sirin, no antoy wadtan, tan sayan misteryo so mansukimat—
 Komon ta ontunda so pusok tan sayan misteryo so nasukimat;—
 'T so dagem tan anggapo lay arum!"
Lukas dia inbantak ko so shutter, nen, tekep na dakel a panliligaw tan pan-flirt,
 Linmoob diman so sakey a makapadinayew ya Uwak nen saman ya agew.
 Anggapoy anggan daiset a panagdayew to; anggapoy anggan sakey a minuton tinmunda odino tinmunda;
 Balet, tekep na itsura na katawan odino bii, ya akayurong ed tagey na puerta na kuartok—
 Akayurong ed sakey a bust nen Pallas ed tagey na puerta na kuartok—
 Akayurong, tan akayurong, tan anggapo lay arum.
Insan sayan manok ya ebony so mamapalikdo ed maermen kon panliket,
 Diad grabe tan maruksan dekorasyon na lupa to,
 "Anggano say pagew mo et naputot tan nakalkal, sika," inkuan ko, "seguradon aliwan makulangkulang,
 Makapataktakot tan kadaanan ya Uwak ya manlalapud gilig na Labi,—
 Ibagam ed siak no antoy ngaran mo ed Night's Plutonian shore!"
 Inkuan na Uwak, "Anggapo lan balot."
Mankelaw ak ed sayan makapabaing a manok ya makadngel na paliwawa ya malinew,
 Anggaman daiset so kabaliksan na ebat to—agtanton makanakana;
 Ta agtayo naiwasan ya onabobon ya anggapoy mabilay a too
 Si Ever yet et abendisyonan ya akanengneng na manok ed tagey na puerta na kuarto to—
 Manok odino ayep ed tapew na inukit a busto ed tagey na puerta na kuarto to,
 Diad ngaran ya "Nevermore."
Balet say Uwak, ya akayurong a bukor ed mareen a bust, so nansalita labat
 Say sakey a salita, singa say kamarerwa to ed satan a sakey a salita so inkalbo to.
 Anggapo lay arum ya imbaga to—anggapo lay sakey a buek insan to inmepas—
 Anggad agko la inmesel, "Saray arum ya kakaarok et akapanbiahe la nensaman—
 Diad ontumbok ya agew et taynanan to ak, a singa saray ilalo ko nensaman."
 Insan inkuan na manok, "Ag lan balot."
Abigbigla ed ag-inkayadyari na ebat ya duga so inbalikas to,
 "Anggapoy duaruwa," inkuan ko, "say ibabaga to et say stock tan tindaan to labat,
 Narel ed sakey ya agmaliket a katawan ya anggapoy panangasi ton Desyang
 Tinmumbok a maples tan tinmumbok a maples anggad saray kansion to so sakey a pabelat ya inawit to—
 Anggad saray ermen na Ilalo to et naandi so makapaermen a pabelat
 Na 'Agbalot—agbalot.'"
Balet say Uwak et siansia nin mamapalikdo ed amin ya maermen a kamarerwak ya onimis,
 Diretso kon inlugan so sakey a yurongan ya walay unan to diad arap na manok tan pagew tan puerta;
 Insan, nen linmereg so pelus, ginawak so inkasiak ed panag-link
 Magarbo anggad magarbo, no isipen no antoy makapataktakot a manok nensaman—
 Anto iyan makapasinagem, makapadimla, makapataktakot, malimpek tan makapataktakot a manok nensaman
 Say labay ton ibaga et say "Anggapo lan balot."
Saya so akayurong ak ya manhuhula, balet anggapoy silaba ya mangibabalikas
 Diad manok ya saray mandarlang a mata to et manpopoolan la natan ed puso na pagew ko;
 Saya tan dakel ni et akayurong ak ya manhuhula, ya say ulok et akayurong
 Diad velvet ya lining na unan ya onsisinag so liwawa na lamparaan,
 Balet siopay velvet violet ya lining to ya walay liwawa na lamparaan ya mankirlap ed tagey to
 Sikatoy mangipilit, ah, ag lan balot!
Insan, inisip ko, say dagem et lalon inmawet, ya nan-amot manlapud ag-anengneng ya insenso
 Inyugyog na saray seraphim ya say sali ra et mankirlap ed malimpek a datal.
 "Makapaermen," inkelyaw ko, "say Dios mo so angipautang ed sika—diad panamegley na sarayan anghel et imbaki to ka
 Painawa—painawa tan painawa manlapud saray pakanodnonotan mo ed si Lenore!
 Quaff, oh quaff sayan klase na nepenthe, tan lingwanan mo yan abalang ya Lenore!"
 Inkuan na Uwak, "Anggapo lan balot."
"Propeta!" inkuan ko, "bengatlan mauges!—propeta ni, no manok odino demonyo!—
 Balanglan say Managtukso so angibaki, odino say bagyo so angibantak ed sika dia ed gilig na dayat;
 Maermen balet ag-ontakot, diad sayan desierto a dalin ya enkantado—
 Diad sayan abung na Horror haunted—ibaga yod siak so tua, mikakasi ak—
 Wala kasi— wala kasi balsamo ed Gilead?—ibagam ed siak—ibagam ed siak, kerewen ko!"
 Inkuan na Uwak, "Anggapo lan balot."
"Propeta!" inkuan ko, "bengatlan mauges—propeta ni, no manok o demonyo!
 Diad panamegley na satan a Langit ya onkukurong ed tagey, sikatayo—diad satan a Dios ya pareho tayon dadayewen—
 Ibagam ed sayan kamarerwa ya napno na ermen no, diad loob na arawin Aidenn,
 Saya so mangawit ed sakey a santo a marikit ya inngaran na saray anghel a Lenore—
 Igaton mo so sakey a matalag tan mankirlap a marikit ya inngaran na saray anghel ya Lenore."
 Inkuan na Uwak, "Anggapo lan balot."
"Saman a salita so tanda na pansian tayo, manok man odino demonyo!" Inmeyag ak, a manpapasnok—
 "Pawil ka ed bagyo tan ed gilig na Plutonian na Labi!
 Ag ka mangitilak na andeket a buek bilang tanda na katilaan ya imbaga na kamarerwam!
 Agmo naandi so panbukbukor ko!—itundam so bust ed tagey na puertak!
 Ekal moy sangi mo ed pusok, tan ekalen moy itsuram ed puertak!"
 Inkuan na Uwak, "Anggapo lan balot."
Tan say Uwak, ya agbalot ontitikyab, akayurong ni, akayurong ni
 Diad amputin bust nen Pallas ya walad tagey na puerta na kuartok;
 Tan saray mata to et singa saray mata na demonyo a mankukugip,
 Tan say liwawa na lamparaan ed sikato ya onlelereg et mangibubuntok ed anino to ed datal;
 Tan say kamarerwak manlapud satan ya anino ya onleletaw ed datal
 Natagey—ag lan balot!
