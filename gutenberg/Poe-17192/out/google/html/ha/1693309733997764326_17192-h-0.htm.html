SHARHI AKAN WAKAR.

Sirrin waka, bai gaza wadatar izgili ba, tana cikin kunnen wanda ya ji ta. Ka yi watsi da sihirinsa, ka yarda da yanayin mawaƙin: wannan, bayan haka, shine abin da masu hikima suka amsa lokacin da ka tambaye su darajarsa. Ko da yake shi kansa mawaƙin, a cikin wani yanayi nasa, ya gaya maka cewa fasaharsa ba ta daɗe ba, abincin mai sihirinsa ne, kuma ya ba da shi don ya nuna maka dabarar ta, - kar ka yarda da shi. Jira sa'ar annabcinsa; sannan ka ba da kanka ga sha'awarsa, farin ciki ko zafi. "Muna hannun Soyayya yau!" Gautier ya rera waƙa, a cikin fassarar fassarar Swinburne, - kuma daga safiya zuwa faɗuwar rana muna tafe akan tekun tashin hankali: akwai ƙauna ɗaya kaɗai, Mayu ɗaya, fure ɗaya. Ƙauna madawwamiya ce, duk abin da ba gaskiya ba ne kuma a ajiye shi a gefe. Ganin yana da ƙarewa, yanayin ya canza; amma mun sami wani abu, ƙwaƙwalwar fara'a. Kamar yadda mawaka da yawa, da yawa laya. Akwai fara'a na Evanescence, wanda ke ba da rance ga mafi girman kyau da alherin aureole na Pathos. Raba wa Landor "daren abin tunawa da nishi" na Rose Aylmer, kuma kuna da wannan a cikakke.

Kuma yanzu dauki hannun wani sabon-duniya minstrel, bace daga wasu dace mazauninsu zuwa waccan m da dissonant Amurka wanda, kamar yadda Baudelaire ya gani, "ya kasance ga Poe kawai wani babban kurkuku ta hanyar da ya gudu, nan da can, tare da zazzaɓi tashin hankali na wani halitta don numfasawa a cikin mafi tsarki duniya," da kuma inda "ya ciki rai, na ruhaniya a matsayin mawaƙi, ko da wani tasiri na ruhaniya a matsayin mawaƙi, ko da wani tasiri na ruhaniya a matsayin mawaƙi. wannan yanayi mara kyau”. Maƙe hannun mawaƙin da ke cike da damuwa don haka abin ban mamaki ne, kuma ku raba tare da shi yanayin da ya samo, - ba a cikin yini ba, ko da yaushe a cikin dare, - idan ba Atlantis inda ya yi yawo ba, aƙalla wani wurin mafaka daga iyakokin da rana ta yi masa rauni.

Zuwa wata ƙasa kaɗai ke da ikon jagorantar ku, kuma dare ɗaya kawai za ku iya raba mafarkinsa. Wani yanki na duniya ko sama: "Babu-man-kasa," daga sararin samaniya, daga lokaci. Ga masu rugujewa, wadanda ta idanunsu, kamar na Cenci, rai yana samun tagogi duk da hankali ya dugunzuma; a nan ruhohi, masu neman hanyar da za ta kai ga dawwama, an dakatar da su kuma suna jinkiri. Ita ce ginshiƙin “rayuka na duniya,” a cikinsa akwai duk rashin tabbas na hasken wata, duk ɓataccen ƙauna da ruɗi. Anan wasu an gyara su cikin hayyacinsu, kawai jinkirin da ake samu; wasu

Daya bayan daya suna kara, kamar kukan agogon tagulla da agogon ebony, a cikin "Masaukin Jajayen Mutuwa," wanda ya sa masu walza suka dakata da "rashin hankali da rawar jiki da tunani," a duk lokacin da sa'a ta zagayo.

Daga cikin duk waɗannan abubuwan ban mamaki, filin Raven , yana girgiza ta hanyar tashar, musamman ya burge duniyar waje. Waɗanne abubuwa ne ke zuwa wajen yin waƙa, kuma yaya gaskiya a cikin wannan, kamar yadda yake a yawancin sauran, waccan jinsin da ya sa wa barayinta suna “masu yi”? Ana kiran wani aiki daga cikin wofi. Inda babu kome, sai ya kasance,—sabuwar halitta, sashen taska na ’yan Adam. Kuma ƴan waƙoƙi na musamman, fiye da sauran waɗanda ke da ƙirƙira daidai, suna tilasta mana mu sake yin tunanin yadda bajintar alƙalamin mawaƙi ke juya abubuwan da ba a san su ba.

Kowanne kamar ba shi da wani samfuri, duk da haka duk suna burge mu da abubuwan da aka komo daga inuwar Allahntaka. Yanzu mafi girman tunanin yana damuwa game da ruhin abubuwa; yana iya ko a'a ya haifar da Fantasy cewa mutanen da ke da hotuna ba su da fa'ida. Duk da haka, ɗaya daga cikin waɗannan waƙoƙin, a cikin ƙananan hanyarsa, yana rinjayar mu tare da ma'anar bambanci, kamar yadda aikin sublimer na simintin gyare-gyare na allahntaka, - Marlowe's "Faustus," "Faust" na Goethe, "Manfred," ko ma waɗancan ƙwararrun ƙwararru, "The Tempest" da "Mafarkin dare na dare." Fiye da ɗaya, yayin da in ba haka ba na musamman, yana da wasu nauyi ko hana abin da ke damun ƙwaƙwalwar ajiya, - sau ɗaya an ji, ba a manta da shi ba, kamar sautin da ba kasafai ake amfani da shi ba amma tsayayyen gaɓa. Sanannen daga cikinsu shine "Lenore" na Bürger, wannan balladi mai ban tsoro da resonant, rugujewar mafassara. Kadan za su musun cewa abin ban mamaki na Coleridge "Rime of the Ancient Mariner" yana tsaye a kansu. "Le Juif-Errant" zai yi iƙirari, da Beranger ya kasance babban mawaƙi; kuma, amma saboda nisantarsu daga mashahurin tausayi, "The Lady of Shalott" da "The Albarka Damozel" za a iya ƙara zuwa cikin jerin. An ba Edgar Allan Poe don samar da kalmomi guda biyu, "The Bells" da Raven , kowannensu, ko da yake watakila ba shi da kyau fiye da na Tennyson da Rossetti, na musamman. "Ulalume," duk da yake baƙon abu ne kuma mai tunani, ba shi da ingancin duniya wanda shine ɓangaren gwajin mu.

Amma Raven , kamar "The Bells" da "Annabel Lee," ya yaba da kansa ga mutane da yawa da 'yan. Na fada a wani wuri cewa abubuwan da ba su da yawa na Poe sun yi kama da ni "waɗanda a cikin su akwai bayyanar, aƙalla, na spontaneity, - wanda ya ba da gudummawa ga ji, yayin da yake mutuwa ya fadi kuma mafi yawan kida, mafi yawan melancholy, ya fito daga gare shi ba da sani ba." Wannan har yanzu imanina ne; amma duk da haka, a wani sabon nazari na wannan waƙa, ta fi burge ni fiye da kowane lokaci tun lokacin da nake yaro. Kusan saninsa yana nuna goyon baya ga kowane aikin fasaha na gaskiya. Ka sa mutumin, wanda bai san fasaha ba, kuma bai kula da ita ba, ya bincika wasu waƙa ko zane, da sauri da ƙarfinsa ya kama shi! Haƙiƙa, zai ƙasƙantar da darajar aikin kirki na farko da aka kama hankalinsa sosai. Raven , kuma, yana da daidaiton halaye waɗanda ko da gwani dole ne ya yaba. A cikin waqoqin mawallafinsa babu wanda ya fi fayyace dalilinsa. "The Haunted Palace" daidai yake da zaɓaɓɓen mai karatu, amma da kyar Poe ya ɗauki wannan ƙazamin ƙazamin don nazarin sanƙo. Hankaka ya shagaltu da ainihin jigon marubucin—rashin da ba za a iya warwarewa ba na mace mai tsafi kuma kyakkyawa; amma a wasu dalilai, kuma, hankalin jama'a daidai ne a tunaninsa waƙar wakilcinsa.

Mutum mai hazaka yakan samu gindin zama tare da nasarar wani yunƙuri guda ɗaya, kuma wannan ba koyaushe ba ne mafi girmansa. Ganewa shine mafi saurin jinkiri don jinkiri. Ba ya samun ta, kamar dukiyar zullumi, tsabar kuɗi bayan tsabar kuɗi, amma "ba ko kaɗan ba." Sabili da haka tare da sauran buri: kotu, soja, ɗan wasan kwaikwayo, - ko wane ɓangaren su, - kowannensu yana ƙididdige nasararsa daga wasu bugun jini. Poe's Raven, duk da augury, ya kasance a gare shi "tsuntsaye wanda ya sa iska ta busa." Mawaƙin ya zauna a New York, a cikin hunturu na 1844-'45, yana neman aiki akan takarda Willis, "The Evening Mirror," da kuma fitar da kuɗin shiga ta hanyar gudunmawar wasu wurare. Shekaru shida ya kasance marubuci mai himma, kuma yana jin daɗin ƙwararrun suna; an gudanar da shi cikin mutuntawa da rashin kokwanto, kuma bai rasa nasaba da rabon sa na aikin jarida da ba a biya shi albashi ba a wannan rana. Ya kuma yi mafi kyawun aikinsa, kamar tatsuniyoyi irin su "Ligeia" da "Faɗuwar House of Usher," (na ƙarshen yana ɗauke da takwaransa na sufanci, a cikin ayar, na Elihu Vedder's "A Lost Mind,") irin waɗannan gwaje-gwajen nazari kamar "The Gold Bug" da "Asirin Marie Roget." Ya yi masu shiga addini a ƙasashen waje, kuma ya sami dawwama a zuciyar Faransa. Ya koyi ikonsa da rauninsa, kuma ya kasance a farkonsa, ba tare da wani suna ba. Amma bai rubuta kome a harshen kowa ba. Don aiki mai wuyar gaske, dole ne a ƙara wasu shahararriyar taɓawa don kama jama'a gabaɗaya na lokacinsa.

Ta hanyar masana'antar marubutan tarihin rayuwar Poe, bugun da The Raven ya yi ya zama tatsuniya. Matashiyar matar mawaƙin, Virginia, tana dushewa a idonsa, amma ta daɗe har tsawon shekara guda a cikin inuwar mutuwa. Dogon, ƙaramin ɗakin a cikin gidan kusa da titin Bloomingdale shine kamar sananne kamar dakin da Rouget de l'Isle ya hada Marseillaise. Duk sun ji cewa waƙar, wacce ta sanya hannu a kan "Quarles," ta fito a cikin "Bita na Amurka," tare da sharhin edita na ƙarya game da siffarsa; cewa Poe ya karbi dala goma a gare shi; cewa Willis, mafi alheri kuma mafi ƙarancin hassada na masu sasantawa na zamani, ya sake buga shi tare da yabo wanda nan take ya mai da shi zancen gari. An kawar da duk shakkar marubucin sa lokacin da Poe ya karanta shi da kansa a wurin taron adabi, kuma na wani lokaci shi ne ya fi fice a cikin marubutan Amurka. Bugawa ya zaburar da shi da karfafa masa gwiwa. Kamar wani kuma mai girman kai satirist, shi ma ya sami "wani abu na rani" har ma "a cikin ƙwan kwari." Bakin ciki ya isa, amma shekaru uku suka shude,-lokacin tasiri, girman kai, bacin rai, duk da haka koyaushe na tunani ko aiki mai mahimmanci, -kafin rashin nasara na ƙarshe, kafin labule ya faɗo a kan rayuwar da ta kasance a gare shi gaskiya ta zama bala'i, kuma ya ba da kansa ga "Tsarin Nasara."

"The American Review: A Whig Journal" ya kasance mujallu mai daraja don lokacin, mai nau'i biyu, an buga shi a kan takarda mai kyau tare da nau'i mai mahimmanci, kuma an kwatanta shi da hotuna na mezzoint. A cikin abubuwa da yawa da ke ƙasa da ƙa'idodin yanzu, ya ƙunshi wasu waɗanda kowane edita zai yi farin cikin karɓa. Ƙarfin farko, na 1845, yana da labarai na Horace Greeley, Donald Mitchell, Walter Whitman, Marsh, Tuckerman, da Whipple. Waƙar Ralph Hoyt, "Tsohuwar," ta bayyana a cikin wannan kundin. Kuma a nan akwai waƙoƙi guda uku na Poe: "Birnin cikin Teku," "Kwarin Tashin hankali," da Raven . Biyu daga cikin waɗannan an gina su, - irin wannan hanyarsa, - daga karatun farko, amma mai suna na ƙarshe ya fito kamar wanda aka tsara, kuma kusan kamar yadda muke da shi a yanzu. Maganar cewa ba a sake gyara ta ba kuskure ne. Canje-canje guda goma sha ɗaya daga rubutun mujallu sun bayyana a cikin Raven da Sauran Waƙoƙi , 1845, littafin da mawaƙin ya ji an ƙarfafa shi ya ba jama'a. Waɗannan galibin canje-canje ne na alamomin rubutu, ko na kalmomi ɗaya, nau'in na ƙarshe da aka yi don haɓaka tasirin rubutu. A cikin kyawawan bugu na Mr. Lang na ayar Poe, wanda aka fito da shi a cikin "Laburare na Parchment," ya nuna dabi'ar malami, kuma ya yi hikima, a cikin komawa ga rubutun a cikin juzu'in da aka ambata, kamar yadda aka ba da shi a cikin fitowar ta London na 1846. Tarin "misali" Griswold na ayyukan mawaƙa yana da yawa da kurakurai. Masu gyara na baya sun sake maimaita waɗannan, waɗanda kuma sun yi kurakurai na kansu. Amma rubutun Raven , saboda buƙatun da aka yi wa marubucin don kwafin rubutun, har yanzu ya ci gaba da bitar da shi; a gaskiya, ya buga shi a Richmond, kafin mutuwarsa, tare da maye gurbin "seraphim wanda ƙafarsa ta faɗo" don "mala'iku waɗanda ƙafafunsu suka fadi," a cikin sha huɗu na sha huɗu. Rubutunmu na yanzu, don haka, duk da cewa na 1845, an ɗan gyara shi ta hanyar karatun mawaƙin daga baya, kuma, ina tsammanin, shine mafi daidai kuma mafi inganci na wannan waƙa guda ɗaya. Mafi m canji daga farkon version ya bayyana, duk da haka, a cikin girma a 1845; juzu'i na goma sha ɗaya asalinsa yana ɗauke da waɗannan layukan, kuskure a cikin waƙar kuma in ba haka ba akwai lahani ga waƙar:

"An kama shi daga wani ubangida mara dadi, wanda Bala'i mara tausayi
 An bi da sauri kuma a bi da sauri-don haka, lokacin da fatan zai yi rantsuwa.
 Stern Despair ya dawo, maimakon kyakkyawan fatan da ya kuskura ya lallaba-
 Wannan amsa mai ban tausayi, 'Kada!'"

Zai yi kyau idan wasu, kuma shahararrun, mawaƙa za su iya tabbatar da yin canje-canjen su koyaushe. Poe ya ci gaba da sake sarrafa ƙaƙƙarfan ayarsa, kuma yakan inganta ta. Hankaka shi ne farkon waqoqin waqoqin da ya kusa kammalawa kafin bugu. Wataƙila waɗanda ke kula da waƙa sun rasa kaɗan da mutuwarsa. Mai ƙware a cikin larura, bai taɓa rubuta baiti don yin waƙa ba. Lokacin da dena hoton ya ratsa shi, waƙar da ta haifar ita ce ilhami, kamar yadda shi da kansa ya faɗa, na sha'awa, ba na manufa ba. Wannan ya kasance a cikin tsaka-tsaki da wuya kamar yadda kusan tabbatar da ka'idar Fairfield cewa kowanne ya samo asali ne na tashin hankali.

To, menene ya ba mawaƙin mawaƙinsa alamar Hankaka ? Daga wace gidauniyar hazo ta tashi a hankali zuwa kida a hankali? Kamar yadda aka saba, abubuwa fiye da ɗaya sun tafi ginin waka ta shahara. Idan aka yi la’akari da tsawon wa’azin da ake yi a kan gajerun nassosi da ba su da hankali, ina fata ba za a zarge ni ba a kan wannan tattaunawa ta waka guda, musamman wadda wani mai fasaha kamar Doré ya yi batun kwatanci na ɓarna. Har kwanan nan na yi tsammanin cewa wannan yanki, da wasu kaɗan waɗanda marubucinsa ya tsara bayan bayyanarsa, sun yi fice wajen rashin girma daga ƙwayoyin cuta a cikin ayarsa ta yara. Amma Mista Tsoron Gill ya nuna mini wasu tatsuniyoyin da Poe ya rubuta ba a buga ba, wanda aka rubuta a cikin shekara ta goma sha takwas, kuma mai taken, "Aljanin Wuta." Poe, kamar Hawthorne, ya shigo tare da raguwar makarantar Romantic, kuma babu wanda ya yi farin ciki fiye da yadda ya yi dariya game da bala'i. Amma duk da haka zuciyarsa tana tare da masu soyayya da tasirinsu na Gabas ko Gothic. Ƙirƙirar da ya ƙirƙira, mai wadata a cikin tatsuniyoyi, kamar ya yi watsi da shi lokacin da ya rubuta aya; kuma hukuncin da ya yanke ya gaya masa cewa dogayen waqoqin soyayya sun dogara ne akan abin da ya faru fiye da ilhami, kuma, don furta waƙar soyayya, waƙoƙin ya isa. Don haka ka'idarsa, wacce ta dace da iyakokinsa a fili, cewa "dogon waka" sabani ne a cikin sharuddan." Haɗin kai na dagewa yana zuwa ba tare da ƙoƙari ba, kuma sau da yawa waƙoƙin da ke cikin layi suna lalata; yayin da hani ko nauyi ya mamaye dukkan aikin. Anan kuma ya sami riba daga binciken Miss Barrett na ballads da romant a cikin nata da sauran harsuna. “Tsarin” ita ce abin da mawaƙi ko mawaƙa ke riƙe da kunnuwan da ke yawo, — buƙatun Nature na yau da kullun na nau'in farko. Poe ya halicci layi na biyar na stanza nasa don sihiri na maimaituwa. Ya dogara da shi gaba daya a cikin wasu wakoki na baya, - "Lenore," "Annabel Lee," "Ulalume," da "Ga Annie." Ya sami kidan daji da ban sha'awa, na yi tunani, daga "tasiri mai dadi," na nauyin Afirka da maimaitawa waɗanda aka rera masa tun yana ƙuruciya, tare da waƙarsu ta asali muryar mawaƙin Kudancinmu.

"The Philosophy of Composition," nazarinsa na Raven , rarrabuwar fasaha ce ta hanyarsa da tsarinsa. Ba za a yarda da shi gaba ɗaya ba, ko rashin amincewarsa na kayan fasaha mai sanyin jini, ko kuma furucin da ya yi ga abokai cewa fallasa wannan kayan aikin wata dabara ce kawai. Idan da ya tsara cikakken aikin tun da farko, da kyar ya yi kakkausar murya mai tsaurin ra'ayi na kade-kade da wake-wake mai dadi na wakoki na biyu, ba ma bisa ka'idarsa mai ban mamaki ba. Tabbas mai fasaha, bayan kammala aikin, yana gani, kamar Mawaƙin Farko, cewa yana da kyau, kuma yana ganin dalilin da yasa yake da kyau. Ana iya yin nazari na gaba, haɗe tare da ƙin yarda da kowane wuta mai tsarki, da sauri. Imanina shi ne cewa farkon tunani da tsattsauran ra'ayi na wannan waka ya zo ne kamar yadda kullun ke zuwa; cewa marubucin ya ga yadda za a iya kamala, yana ba shi tabo ta ƙarshe da aka kwatanta a cikin babinsa na Haɗawa, kuma cewa na ƙarshe, ba ƙarya ba ne kuma ba gaskiya ba ne. Lalacewar irin wannan bincike shine yana jarabtar novice don yin tunanin cewa hanyoyin wucin gadi na iya ƙetare hasashe. Burin hazaka shine kiyaye sirrin sa'ar halittar sa. Hasashen da aka samu na wahalar aiki, gwaje-gwajen ban mamaki, waɗanda ke gaban nasara, kamar yadda a cikin zane-zane na Hawthorne kwanan nan ya kawo haske, yana ba da umarni mai ƙima da ƙarfafawa ga mai fasaha na gaskiya. Amma daya wanda da son rai ya tona asirin Musa ga duk masu zuwa ya tuna da makomar Sarki Candaules.

Duniya har yanzu tana tunanin Poe a matsayin "mutumin da ba shi da sa'a mai hazaka." Kwanan nan na ji an ambaci shi a matsayin "wanda kowa ya zama kamar an yi hayarsa don yin kuskure, ko kuma ya zarge shi." Amma ni a ganina rashin sa'ar tasa ta ƙare da mutuwarsa mai ban tausayi, kuma tun daga lokacin kariyarsa ta dage, kuma shahararsa na tsayin daka a matsayin marubuci mai wahala da hazaka zai iya yin addu'a a cikin sa'ar fatansa. Girgizar kasa da cin mutuncin Griswold ya juya halin yanzu cikin yardarsa. Masu suka da tarihin rayuwa sun zo gaba tare da karyatawa, tare da girmamawa ga halinsa, tare da sababbin bugu na ayyukansa. Wasiƙunsa da abubuwan da suka faru na ɗan lokaci na aikinsa suna gabanmu; rikodin, mai kyau da mara kyau, sananne ne. Babu wani mai ƙara da ya sami ƙarin hukunci mai taushi da gafartawa. Matsalolinsa na rayuwa sun kasance na yankinsa da zamaninsa, watakila har yanzu ya fi nasa rauni na nufinsa. Babu shakka mahallinsa ba shine wanda zai kiyaye kyakkyawan yanayi, rashin daidaiton yanayi daga haɗari ba tare da ciki ba. Ƙarfin nufinsa, ya zama ubangijin kansa, ya same shi "gadon bala'i." Ya furta kansa a matsayin ubangidan tsuntsu, wanda ya sha fama da wannan waka. Amma nasa cikakken rabo ne na wannan zafin na ban mamaki wanda ke jin daɗin halakar da kansa. Akwai ni'ima a cikin yin babban ɓangaren mutum: mu duka masu farin ciki ne, muna kuka Ave Imperator! Don faɗi al'amarin Burke na gaskiya: "A cikin baƙin ciki jin daɗin har yanzu shine mafi girma, kuma wahalar da muke sha ba ta da kama da cikakken ciwo, wanda koyaushe yana da banƙyama, kuma muna ƙoƙarin girgiza da wuri-wuri." Poe ya yi nisa, kuma ya kasance mai fasaha har ma a cikin bala'i na aikinsa. Idan a bisa ga imaninsa, bakin ciki da gushewar kyawun su ne jigogin waqoqi mafi girma, kuma mawaqi suna jin jin dadin duniya, to baqin ciki da duhun rayuwarsa ta karye ba su kasance ba tare da biyan diyya akai-akai ba.

A cikin shafuffuka masu zuwa, muna da sabon misali na hazakar mai fasaha da ke bayyana fassararsa na wata shahararriyar waƙa. Gustave Doré, aikin ƙarshe na wanda fensir yake gabanmu, ba mai zane ba ne, ko ma mai ɗorawa, ga masu gaskiya waɗanda ke neman gaskiyar sautin, adadi, da kamala. Irin waɗannan al'amura sun shafe shi ƙasa da yin siffa da nisa, haske da inuwa, taimakawa manufarsa, - wanda shine ya faranta ran rai, tunanin, mai kallo. Ya yi haka ta wurin tada hankalinmu, ta wurin ra’ayoyi masu ban al’ajabi da sau da yawa na abubuwan da ba za a iya furtawa ba kuma masu cike da duhu ko ɗaukaka. An ce cewa idan ayyukansa ba manyan zane-zane ba ne, a matsayin hotuna suna da girma da gaske. A matsayinsa na “mawaƙin adabi,” kuma ya kasance, ƙarfinsa ya yi daidai da ƙaƙƙarfan ƙirƙirar marubucinsa, tare da jajircewar ruhun da ya kunna nasa. Saboda haka nasarar da ya samu tare da Rabelais, tare da "Le Juif-Errant," "Les Contes Drolatiques," da "Don Quixote," sabili da haka, akasin haka, rashin nasararsa don bayyana kyawun Tennyson's Idyls, na "Il Paradiso," na Fastoci na Ibrananci, da sauran rubutun da ke buƙatar ɗaukaka, ko zaƙi da hutawa. Haifaffen ƙwararren ƙwaƙƙwal ne, kuma ta hanyar hangen nesa na musamman zai iya kwatanta masu kallon kwakwalwar da ke haɗe. Muna ganin abubuwa kamar yadda mutanensa suka gan su, kuma da idon Bayahude mai yawo, Don ruɗewa, ko ɗiyar maƙerin zinari waɗanda sha'awarsu ta ɗaukaka Sarki a shagon da ke kan Pont-au-Change. A cikin yanayin abubuwa ne ya kamata a shagaltu da shi ga kowace ƙwararriyar aya ko ilimantarwa da na kira na musamman. An kira ƙananan masarautu cikin hidimarsa; Duwatsunsa, bishiyunsa da tsaunukansa, sararin sama da kanta, suna da rayayye da motsin rai. Idan da ya rayu don ya kwatanta Shakespeare, da mun ga wani abin ban mamaki game da Caliban, Bokaye, guguwa a cikin "Lear"; amma ko shakka babu yakamata yayi tambaya akan manufofinsa na Imogen ko Miranda. Kyakykyawan tsantsar tsafta da saukin kai, da cikakkiyar kyawunta, da kyar ya yi kamar ya fahimta.

Duk da haka akwai kyakkyawa a cikin ƙirarsa don "Tsohon Mariner," wanda ba na gaske ba ne kamar yadda suke, da kuma rashin daidaituwa a cikin jerin Doré. Rime ya ba shi labari mai tsawo, tare da sauye-sauye da yawa na yanayin. A cikin Raven sauti da launi suna adana sautin su kuma ba mu da canjin wuri ko yanayi. Menene sakamakon? Doré ya ba da jerin bambance-bambance a kan jigon kamar yadda ya ɗauka, "masanin mutuwa da ruhin rai marar natsuwa." A wasu daga cikin wadannan zane-zanen kurakuransa sun bayyana; wasu kuma suna bayyana ainihin asalinsa mai ƙarfi, da kyawawan halaye waɗanda, a matsayinsa na ɗan wasan ruwa, ya tsaya shi kaɗai. A bayyane akwai wani abu gama gari tsakanin yanayin aiki na Poe da Doré. Wannan zai bayyana a fili idan na karshen ya gwada hannunsa a kan "Tales of the Grotesque and Arabesque." Dukansu sun koma sau da yawa zuwa ga ƙasa mai fantasy da soyayya. A cikin wasan kwaikwayo na melodramatic su biyun, ta hanyar umarninsu na allahntaka, sun guje wa layin haɗari tsakanin manufa da rashin hankali. Poe ya kasance mai gaskiya ga Kyawun; Ƙaunar da yake yi wa abin sha'awa ce mai tsarkakewa, kuma a nan ya raba haɗin gwiwa tare da mai kwatanta shi. Mawaƙi ko mai zane, Mutuwa a ƙarshe ta canza kama: a cikin inuwar sable harbinger, alamar alama ta Vedder ta daidaita su fuska da fuska, amma ya lulluɓe su da rigar hikima da iko marar mutuwa. Wata Ba’amurke ta yi hoton Genius mai ido tauraro tare da fitilar ƙarshe, kyakkyawan kamannin wanda hangen nesansa ke gani, amma wanda leɓunsa ba za su faɗi ba, asirin ƙasar da ta wuce “kofar wani kabari mai almara.”

Edmund C. Stedman.
