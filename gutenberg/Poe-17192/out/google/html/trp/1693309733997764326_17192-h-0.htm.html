Hakhebo "Kwchamni Mariner" ni bagwi bini designrokni bisingo naithokma tongo, abo hai kheno kubui ya, tei Doréni series-o kwrwi wngwi tongmani. Result tamo mankha? Doré bini uansukma hai khe a kokno twiwi kaisa swlaimung rwkha, "kwthwimani enigma tei khá kwchangya pholani hallucination." O drawingrokni bisingo bini chayarok nukjago; buini borokrok bini phan gwnang originality no phunukgo, tei aboni kahamkuk samungrokno phunukgo, aboni bisingo, drawing khlainai wngwi, bo sak sak bachaui tongmani. Bohrok khoroknwi kwbangma jorao khá kháchangma tei hamjakmani elf-ha-o tháng lai kha. Melodramatic samungrokni bisingo bórok khoroknwi’-no, bórokni phan gwnangrokni dagimungni bising tẃi, kaham tei khá hamyarokni bisingo tongnai hamya-lineno mwthangwi man kha. Poe wng kha Naithokmarokno kubui khe khulumnai; bini hamjakma aboni bagwi kaisa kwthar hamjakma wng kha, tei oro bo bini swngcharni swngcharni borok bai kwrwngwi tong kha.
