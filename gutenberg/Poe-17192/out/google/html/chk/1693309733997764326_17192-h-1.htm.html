Awewe 3
"Ew fansoun non ew pwinin ua netipengaw, nupwen ua ekieki, apwangapwang me apwangapwang,
Won chommong ew puk mi amwarar me amwarar usun ekkewe uruwo mi monuki."


Awewe 4


"Ah, mi ffat pwe ua chechemeni pwe a fis non ewe fansoun Tisempa mi rochopwak,
Iwe ew me ew ekkewe ember mi mano a fori an ngun won ewe puun."


Awewe 5 .


"Ua fokkun mochen ewe ran mwirin;—ese wor namotan ua fen kutta ai upwe borrow .
Seni ai kewe puk a fis netipengaw —netipengaw ren ewe Lenore mi pout seni."


Awewe 6 .


"Netipengaw ren ewe Lenore mi mano."


Awewe 7


"Fan iten ewe fopun mi uren tong me saramaram ekkewe chon nang ra eita ngeni Lenore—
Ese wor itan ikei feinfeino chok."


Awewe 8 .


"'T emon chon kuno a tingor pwe epwe tonong non ai we asam—
Ekkoch chon kuno ra tingor pwe repwe tonong non asamen ai we rum."


Awewe 9 .


"Iei ua suuki ewe asam;—
Rochopwak ikewe ie, me ese wor och mettoch mi nap."


Awewe 10 .


"Tipemwaramwar, tan ese wor emon mi tongeni mano a tongeni tan me nom."


Awewe 11 .


"'Enlet,' ua apasa, 'enlet ina ew mettoch non ai we winnach lattice;
Iwe, mut ngeniei upwe kuna met ena, me ei monomon upwe kutta.'"


Awewe 12 .


"Open ikei ua suki ewe asam."


Awewe 13 .


. . . . . . . . "Emon Raven mi itefouno seni ekkewe ran mi pin me nom."
Esap ewe kukkun sufon a fori; ese wor ew minich a uta ika nomw i."


Awewe 14 .


"A nom won eu bust an Pallas mi chok nom won asamen ai we ruumwen —
A mwokut, me mwot, me ese wor och mettoch."


Awewe 15 .


"A fetan seni ewe sat Night."


Awewe 16 .


"Tori ua chok apasa, 'Ekkoch chienei ra fen fiti sepenin me mwan —
Lon ewe rän mwirin epwe likitieila, usun ai apilukuluk a fen chöüfetäl me mwan.’”


Awewe 17 .


"Iwe, nupwen ewe velvet a tori, ua pwisin angei pwe upwe riri ngeni .
Fansoun ngeni fansoun."


Awewe 18 .


"Nge iö a üföüf üf mi üföüf üf mi pwechepwech me saramen ewe lamp mi saramaram woon .
Epwe pres, ah, esap chiwen wor!"


Awewe 19 .


"'Kopwe riaffou,' ua puchor, 'om Kot a fen liwini ngonuk —ren ekkei chon nang a tinato ngonuk .
Asoso—asoso me nepen seni om chechemeni Lenore!'"


Awewe 20 .


"Won ei imw ren Horror a fis."


Awewe 21 .


. . . . . . . . . "Kopwe ereni ei ennetin, ua tingor—
A wor— a wor balm lon Kiliat?—kopwe ereni ei—kopwe ereni ei, ua tingor!"


Awewe 22 .


"Kopwe ereni ei ngun mi uren netipengaw ika, non ewe Aidenn mi towaw,
Epwe angei emon nengin mi pin ekkewe chon nang ra eita ngeni Lenore."


Awewe 23 .


"'Epwe ena kapas ach esisinen kinikin, man ika fiend!' Ua puchor, ua poputa."


Awewe 24 .


"'Kopwe niwin ngeni ewe fitikoko me ewe sat Plutonian non ewe Pwin!'"


Awewe 25 .


"Me nguni seni lukun ena saram mi nom won ewe puun .
Epwe fis —esap chiwen wor fansoun!"
