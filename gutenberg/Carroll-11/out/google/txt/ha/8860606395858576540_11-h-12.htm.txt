"Nan!" Kuka Alice, ta manta cikin tashin hankali na lokacin girman girmanta a cikin 'yan mintuna kaɗan, kuma ta yi tsalle cikin sauri har ta haye kan akwatin jury ɗin tare da gefen siket ɗinta, ta hargitsa duk masu shari'a a kan shugabannin taron da ke ƙasa, can suka kwanta suna yawo, suna tuno mata da yawa game da duniyar kifin zinare da ta tashi a makon da ya gabata ba da gangan ba.

"Oh, ina neman afuwar ku!" Ta fad'a cikin tsananin bacin rai, ta fara d'aukarsu da sauri da sauri, domin had'in kifin zinare ya ci gaba da gudana a cikinta, ita kuma tana da wani ra'ayi mara kyau cewa dole ne a tattara su nan da nan a mayar da su cikin akwatin juri, ko su mutu.

"Ba za a iya ci gaba da shari'ar ba," in ji Sarkin a cikin babbar murya, "har sai duk alkalan sun dawo a wuraren da suka dace - duka ," ya sake maimaitawa tare da mai da hankali, yana kallon Alice kamar yadda ya fada.

Alice ta kalli akwatin juri, sai ta ga, cikin gaggawar ta, ta sanya Lizard a kai zuwa ƙasa, kuma ɗan ƙaramin abu mara nauyi yana kaɗa wutsiyarsa a cikin wani yanayi mai banƙyama, kasancewar ba ta iya motsawa. Sai da alkalan suka dan farfado daga kaduwa na bacin rai, aka samu slats da fensir dinsu aka mayar musu, sai suka himmatu wajen rubuta tarihin hatsarin, sai Lizard, wanda da alama ya fi karfin yin komai, sai dai ya zauna bakinsa a bude, yana kallon rufin kotun.

"Me kuka sani game da wannan kasuwancin?" Sarki ya ce wa Alice.

"Ba komai," in ji Alice.

"Ba komai? " nace Sarki.

"Ba komai," in ji Alice.

"Wannan yana da mahimmanci," in ji Sarkin, yana juya ga alkali. Suna fara rubuta wannan a kan slate ɗinsu, sai farin Zomo ya katse: “ Babu mahimmanci, Mai Martaba yana nufin, ba shakka,” in ji shi cikin muryar girmamawa sosai, amma ya murtuke fuska da fuska yayin da yake magana.

" Ba mahimmanci, ba shakka, ina nufin," Sarkin ya ce da sauri, ya ci gaba da kansa a cikin ajiyar zuciya,

"mahimmanci - mara mahimmanci - mara mahimmanci - mahimmanci - "kamar yana gwada wace kalma ce mafi kyau.

Wasu daga cikin juri sun rubuta shi "mahimmanci," wasu kuma "marasa mahimmanci." Alice na iya ganin haka, yayin da ta kusa isa ta kalli slates ɗin su; "Amma ba komai," a ranta ta yi tunani.

A wannan lokacin ne Sarkin, wanda ya daɗe yana aiki a rubuce a cikin littafinsa na rubutu, ya ce "Shiru!" kuma ya karanta daga littafinsa, “Dokar Arba’in da Biyu. Dukan mutanen da suka fi tsayin mil ɗaya su bar kotu .”

Kowa ya kalli Alice.

"Ba ni da tsayin mil ɗaya," in ji Alice.

"Kai ne," in ji Sarki.

Sarauniyar ta kara da cewa "Kusan mil biyu mai tsayi."

"To, ba zan tafi ba, ko ta yaya," in ji Alice: "ban da haka, wannan ba ka'ida ba ce: ka ƙirƙira shi a yanzu."

“Wannan ita ce doka mafi tsufa a cikin littafin,” in ji Sarkin.

"Sa'an nan ya kamata ya zama lamba ɗaya," in ji Alice.

Sarki ya koma fari, ya rufe littafinsa da gaggawa. "Ku yi la'akari da hukuncinku," ya ce wa alkalan, a cikin sassauyar murya mai rawar jiki.

“Akwai ƙarin shaidar da za ta zo tukuna, don Allah Mai Martaba,” in ji Farin Zomo, yana tsalle cikin sauri; "Wannan takarda an ɗauko."

"Me ke ciki?" In ji Sarauniya.

"Ban buɗe shi ba tukuna," in ji White Rabbit, "amma da alama wasiƙa ce, wanda fursunonin ya rubuta wa-ga wani."

"Tabbas haka ne," in ji Sarkin, "sai dai idan an rubuta wa kowa, wanda ba a saba ba, ka sani."

"Wa aka nufa?" In ji daya daga cikin alkalan.

“Ba a ba da umarni ko kaɗan ba,” in ji Farin Zomo; "a gaskiya, babu wani abu da aka rubuta a waje ." Ya buɗe takardar yayin da yake magana, ya ƙara da cewa “Ba wasiƙa ba ce, bayan haka: ayoyi ne.”

"Shin suna cikin rubutun hannun fursuna?" ya tambayi wani daga cikin alkalan.

"A'a, ba su ba," in ji White Rabbit, "kuma wannan shine abin da ya fi dacewa game da shi." (Jury din duk sun yi mamaki.)

“Tabbas ya yi koyi da hannun wani,” in ji Sarkin. (Jury din duk sun sake haskakawa.)

"Don Allah Mai Martaba," in ji Knave, "Ban rubuta shi ba, kuma ba za su iya tabbatar da cewa na yi ba: babu sunan da aka sanya hannu a ƙarshe."

Sarki ya ce: “Idan ba ka sa hannu ba, hakan ya daɗa daɗaɗa al’amarin, da ma kana nufin wani ɓarna ne, ko kuwa ka sa hannu a sunanka kamar mai gaskiya.”

Aka yi ta tafawa gaba ɗaya: wannan shi ne abin da sarki ya faɗa a wayo a ranar.

"Hakan ya tabbatar da laifinsa," in ji Sarauniyar.

"Ba ya tabbatar da komai na irin!" in ji Alice. "Me ya sa, ba ku ma san abin da suke ba!"

“Karanta su,” in ji Sarkin.

Fararen zomo ya sa adon kallonsa. "Don Allah a ina zan fara?" Ya tambaya.

Sarki ya ce da gaske, “Ku fara tun farko, ku ci gaba har sai kun zo ƙarshe, sai ku tsaya.”

Ga ayoyin da Farin Zomo ya karanta:-

"Sun ce min ka kasance wurinta, Kuma ya ambace ni da shi: Ta bani hali mai kyau, Amma na ce ba zan iya yin iyo ba. Ya aiko musu da cewa ban tafi ba (Mun san gaskiya ne): Idan ta ture lamarin. Me zai same ku? Na ba ta daya, suka ba shi biyu, Ka ba mu uku ko fiye; Daga gare shi duka suka dawo gare ku. Ko da yake sun kasance nawa a da. Idan ni ko ita ya kamata damar zama Acikin wannan al'amari. Ya amince muku da ku 'yanta su. Daidai kamar yadda muke. Tunanina shine kun kasance (Kafin ta sami wannan dacewa) Wani cikas da ya zo tsakanin Shi, da kanmu, da shi. Kar ka bari ya san ta fi son su, Domin wannan dole ne ya kasance Wani sirri, kiyaye shi daga sauran duka. Tsakanin kai da ni."

"Wannan ita ce mafi mahimmancin shaidar da muka ji har yanzu," in ji Sarkin, yana shafa hannayensa; "To yanzu bari juri-"

Alice ta ce: “Idan ɗaya daga cikinsu zai iya bayyana hakan, (ta yi girma sosai a cikin ‘yan mintoci kaɗan da suka wuce har ba ta ji tsoron katse shi ba), “Zan ba shi fam shida. Ban yi imani da akwai ma’ana a cikinsa ba.”

Alkalan kotun sun rubuta a kan tallar su, “Ba ta yarda akwai kwayar zarra mai ma’ana a ciki ba,” amma babu daya daga cikinsu da ya yi yunkurin bayyana takardar.

“Idan babu ma’ana a cikinta,” in ji Sarki, “wanda zai ceci duniyar wahala, ka sani, kamar yadda ba ma bukatar mu yi ƙoƙari mu nemo ko ɗaya. Amma duk da haka ban sani ba,” ya ci gaba da baje ayoyin a gwiwarsa, yana kallon su da ido ɗaya; "Ina ganin ina ganin wasu ma'ana a cikinsu, bayan haka." - ya ce ba zan iya yin iyo ba - "ba za ku iya yin iyo ba, za ku iya?" Ya kara da cewa, ya juya ga Knave.

Knave ya girgiza kai cikin bacin rai. "Ina kama shi?" Yace. (Wanda lalle bai yi ba , an yi shi gaba ɗaya da kwali.)

“Lafiya, ya zuwa yanzu,” in ji Sarkin, ya ci gaba da yin tagumi a kan ayoyin a cikin ransa: “‘ Mun san gaskiya ne— ’ wato alkalan shari’a, ba shakka—’ Na ba ta ɗaya, suka ba shi guda biyu —’me ya sa, abin da ya yi da tart, ka sani—’.

"Amma, yana ci gaba' duk sun dawo daga gare shi zuwa gare ku ," in ji Alice.

"Me yasa, akwai su!" In ji Sarki cikin nasara, yana nuna kwallar da ke kan teburin. "Babu wani abu da zai iya bayyana fiye da wannan . Sannan kuma -' kafin ta sami wannan dacewa -' ba ku taɓa samun dacewa ba, masoyi, ina tsammani?" sai ya ce da Sarauniya.

"Kada!" In ji Sarauniya a fusace, ta jefa tawada ga Lizard tana magana. (Bill ɗin ɗan ƙaramin abin baƙin ciki ya daina rubutawa a kan slate ɗinsa da yatsa ɗaya, saboda ya ga ba shi da wata alama; amma yanzu ya sake farawa cikin gaggawa, yana amfani da tawada, wanda ke ruɓe a fuskarsa, muddin ya daɗe.)

"To maganar ba ta dace da ku ba," in ji Sarki, yana kallon kotun da murmushi. Shiru ya mutu.

"Yana da pun!" Sarkin ya kara da cewa cikin bacin rai, kowa ya yi dariya, "Bari alkalai su duba hukuncin da suka yanke." Sarki ya ce, kusan karo na ashirin a wannan rana.

"A'a, ba!" In ji Sarauniya. "Hukunci na farko - hukunci daga baya."

"Kaya da shirme!" In ji Alice da karfi. "Ra'ayin samun jumlar farko!"

"Ka rike harshenka!" In ji Sarauniya, ta koma purple.

"Ba zan yi ba!" in ji Alice.

"Kashe da kai!" Sarauniya ta yi tsawa a saman muryarta. Babu wanda ya motsa.

"Waye ke damunki?" in ji Alice, (ta girma zuwa girmanta a wannan lokacin). "Ba komai bane illa fakitin katunan!"

A haka duk kayan suka tashi sama sama, suka taho da ita: ta dan yi kururuwa, rabi na tsoro da rabin fushi, ta yi kokarin doke su, ta samu kanta a kwance a banki, da kanta a cinyar 'yar uwarta, a hankali tana goge wasu matattun ganyen da suka gangaro daga itatuwan da ke fuskarta.

"Tashi, Alice masoyi!" 'Yar uwarta ta ce; "Me yasa, dogon barcin da kuka yi!"

"Oh, na yi irin wannan mafarki mai ban sha'awa!" In ji Alice, kuma ta gaya wa ’yar’uwarta, kamar yadda za ta iya tuna su, duk waɗannan baƙon Kasuwar nata da kuke karantawa yanzu; Sa'ad da ta gama, 'yar'uwarta ta sumbace ta, ta ce, "Mafarki ne mai ban sha'awa, masoyi, hakika: amma yanzu ki shiga cikin shayin ki, yana gaba." Don haka Alice ta tashi da gudu, tana tunani yayin da take gudu, haka ma zata iya, wane irin mafarki ne mai ban sha'awa.

Amma 'yar uwarta ta zauna a daidai lokacin da ta bar ta, ta jingina kanta a hannunta, tana kallon faɗuwar rana, tana tunanin ƙaramar Alice da duk abubuwan da suka faru na ban mamaki, har ita ma ta fara mafarki bayan wani salon, kuma wannan shine mafarkinta:-

Na farko, ta yi mafarkin ƙaramar Alice da kanta, kuma ƙananan hannayenta sun haɗa kan gwiwa, kuma idanu masu haske suna kallon cikin nata - tana iya jin sautin muryarta, kuma ta ga wannan ɗan ƙaramin ƙanƙara na kanta don mayar da gashin da ke yawo wanda koyaushe zai shiga cikin idanunta - kuma har yanzu tana saurare, ko kuma ta zama kamar ta saurari, wani wuri mai ban mamaki da 'yar'uwarta.

Dogon ciyawar ta yi karo da kafafunta yayin da Farin zomo ya yi sauri ya bi ta — a tsorace Mouse ya fantsama ta cikin tafkin da ke makwabtaka da ita - tana jin kururuwar shayin yayin da March Hare da abokansa ke cin abincinsu da ba a gama yankewa ba, sai muryar Sarauniya ta ba da umarnin kashe bakinta da suka yi rashin sa'a - saura kadan alade-jaririn da ke kan kwanon abinci a kan farantin da duchess din ke durkushewa. shi-saka kara kukan Gryphon, da kukan fensir na Lizard, da kuma shakewar aladun da aka danne, suka cika iska, suka hade da kukan mai nisa na Mugun Kunkuru.

Don haka ta zauna, tare da rufe idanu, rabi kuma ta yarda da kanta a Wonderland, ko da yake ta san cewa tana da amma ta sake buɗe su, kuma duk za su canza zuwa gaskiya - ciyawar za ta kasance kawai tana ruri a cikin iska, da tafkin ruwa yana ratsawa ga kaɗawar ciyayi - teacups masu rarrafe za su canza zuwa kararrawa na tumaki, kuma Sarauniyar Sarauniya - kukan yaron, muryar yaron ta yi kuka. kukan Gryphon, da duk sauran surutu, za su canza (ta sani) zuwa ruɗewar ruɗani na filin gona mai cike da aiki-yayin da saukar shanu daga nesa zai ɗauki wurin kukan Mock Kunkuru.
