KAPITULO| |III|.
Sarong| |Karera|-|Kaukus| |asin| |Sarong| |Halawig| |na| |Istorya
Sinda| |talagang| |sarong| |grupong| |garo| |baga| |kakaiba| |an| |itsura| |na| |nagtiripon| |sa| |pangpang|—|an| |mga| |gamgam| |na| |may| |mga| |barahibo| |na| |naguguyod|,| |an| |mga| |hayop| |na| |an| |saindang| |barahibo| |nakadukot| |harani| |sa| |sainda|,| |asin| |gabos| |nagtuturo| |na| |basa|,| |krus|,| |asin| |bakong| |komportable|.
An| |enot| |na| |hapot| |siyempre| |iyo|,| |kun| |pano| |magigin| |matuyo| |giraray|:| |nagkaigwa| |sinda| |nin| |konsultasyon| |manungod| |digdi|,| |asin| |pakalihis| |nin| |pirang| |minuto| |garo| |baga| |natural| |para| |ki| |Alice| |na| |manompongan| |an| |saiyang| |sadiri| |na| |pamilyar| |na| |nakikipag|-|olay| |sa| |sainda|,| |na| |garo| |baga| |midbid| |niya| |sinda| |sa| |bilog| |niyang| |buhay|.
Tunay| |nanggad|,| |nagkaigwa| |siya| |nin| |halawig| |na| |argumento| |sa| |Lory|,| |na| |sa| |katapustapusi| |nagin| |maringis|,| |asin| |masabi| |sana|,| |"|Mas| |gurang| |ako| |saimo|,| |asin| |dapat| |mas| |aram| |ko|;|"

“|Ahem|!|”

"|Arog| |kan| |basang| |dati|,|"| |sabi| |ni| |Alice| |sa| |sarong| |mamundong| |tono|:| |"|garo| |dai| |man| |ako| |pinapamara| |kaini|.|"

"|An| |sasabihon| |ko|,|"| |sabi| |kan| |Dodo| |sa| |sarong| |tono| |na| |naanggot|,| |"|iyo|,| |na| |an| |pinakamarahay| |na| |bagay| |tanganing| |mapamara| |kita| |iyo| |an| |sarong| |karera| |sa| |Caucus|.|"
“|Ano| |an| |sarong| |Caucus|-|race|?|”

bako| |na| |gusto| |niyang| |dakul| |an| |maaraman|,| |alagad| |an| |Dodo| |nagpondo| |na| |garo| |baga| |iniisip| |na| |may| |dapat| |na| |magtaram|,| |asin| |mayo| |nang| |ibang| |garo| |baga| |gustong| |magsabi| |nin| |ano| |man|.
“|Taano|,|”| |an| |sabi| |kan| |Dodo|,| |“|an| |pinakamarahay| |na| |paagi| |tanganing| |ipaliwanag| |iyan| |iyo| |an| |paggibo| |kaiyan|.|”
(|Asin|,| |ta| |tibaad| |gusto| |mong| |probaran| |an| |bagay| |na| |iyan| |sa| |sadiri| |mo|,| |sa| |sarong| |aldaw| |nin| |tiglipot|,| |sasabihon| |ko| |saimo| |kun| |pano| |ini| |naasikaso| |kan| |Dodo|.|)
Enot|,| |nagmarka| |ini| |nin| |sarong| |kurso| |nin| |karera|,| |sa| |sarong| |klase| |nin| |bilog|,| |(|"|an| |eksaktong| |porma| |dai| |man| |importante|,|"| |sabi| |kaini|,|)| |dangan| |an| |gabos| |na| |grupo| |ibinugtak| |sa| |kurso|,| |digdi| |asin| |duman|.
Mayo| |nin| |“|Saro|,| |duwa|,| |tolo|,| |asin| |harayo|,|”| |alagad| |nagpoon| |sindang| |magdalagan| |kun| |gusto| |ninda|,| |asin| |minaontok| |kun| |gusto| |ninda|,| |kaya| |bakong| |madaling| |maaraman| |kun| |noarin| |tapos| |na| |an| |karera|.

Sa| |katapustapusi| |an| |Dodo| |nagsabi|,| |"|An| |gabos| |nanggana|,| |asin| |an| |gabos| |dapat| |magkaigwa| |nin| |mga| |premyo|.|"
