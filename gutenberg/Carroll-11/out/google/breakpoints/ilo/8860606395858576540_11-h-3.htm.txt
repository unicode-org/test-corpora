Maysa| |a| |Caucus|-|Race| |ken| |Atiddog| |a| |Sarita

Ti| |umuna| |a| |saludsod| |siempre| |ket|,| |no| |kasano| |manen| |ti| |agmaga|:| |adda| |konsultasionda| |maipapan| |iti| |daytoy|,| |ket| |kalpasan| |ti| |sumagmamano| |a| |minuto| |kasla| |medio| |natural| |ken| |ni| |Alice| |a| |masarakanna| |ti| |bagina| |a| |makisarsarita| |a| |pamiliar| |kadakuada|,| |a| |kasla| |am|-|ammona| |ida| |iti| |intero| |a| |panagbiagna|.
Kinapudnona|,| |medio| |napaut| |ti| |panagsusupiatna| |ken| |ni| |Lory|,| |a| |kamaudiananna| |nagbalin| |a| |natangken| |ti| |ulona|,| |ket| |kunaenna| |laeng|,| |“|Intataenganak| |ngem| |sika|,| |ket| |masapul| |nga| |ad|-|adda| |nga| |ammona|;|”

“|No| |kasta|,|”| |sipapasnek| |a| |kinuna| |ti| |Dodo|,| |a| |timmakder|,| |“|itignayko| |a| |maitantan| |ti| |gimong|,| |para| |iti| |dagus| |a| |pannakaampon| |dagiti| |ad|-|adda| |a| |nasikap| |a| |remedyo—|”

“|Diak| |ammo| |ti| |kaipapanan| |ti| |kagudua| |dagita| |nga| |atiddog| |a| |sasao|,| |ket|,| |ania| |pay|,| |diak| |met| |patien| |nga| |aramidem|!|”
Ket| |inkurba| |ti| |Eaglet| |ti| |ulona| |tapno| |mailemmengna| |ti| |isem|:| |dadduma| |kadagiti| |dadduma| |a| |tumatayab| |ti| |nagkigtot| |a| |mangngeg|.
“|Ti| |ibagak|,|”| |kinuna| |ti| |Dodo| |iti| |makasair| |a| |tono|,| |“|ket|,| |ti| |kasayaatan| |a| |banag| |a| |mangpamaga| |kadakami| |ket| |ti| |lumba| |ti| |Caucus|.|”

“|Apay|,|”| |kinuna| |ti| |Dodo|,| |“|ti| |kasayaatan| |a| |pamay|-|an| |a| |panangilawlawag| |iti| |dayta| |ket| |ti| |panangaramid| |iti| |dayta|.|”
(|Ket|,| |kas| |mabalin| |a| |kayatmo| |a| |padasen| |a| |mismo| |ti| |banag|,| |iti| |sumagmamano| |nga| |aldaw| |ti| |kalam|-|ekna|,| |ibagak| |kenka| |no| |kasano| |a| |tinaming| |dayta| |ti| |Dodo|.|)
Umuna| |a| |nangmarka| |iti| |maysa| |a| |race|-|course|,| |iti| |maysa| |a| |kita| |ti| |sirkulo|,| |(|“|saan| |a| |napateg| |ti| |eksakto| |a| |sukog|,|”| |kinunana|,|)| |ket| |kalpasanna| |amin| |a| |partido| |ket| |naikabil| |iti| |igid| |ti| |kurso|,| |ditoy| |ken| |sadiay|.
Awan| |ti| |“|Maysa|,| |dua|,| |tallo|,| |ken| |adayo|,|”| |ngem| |nangrugida| |nga| |agtaray| |no| |kayatda|,| |ken| |insardengda| |no| |kayatda|,| |isu| |a| |saan| |a| |nalaka| |a| |maammuan| |no| |kaano| |a| |malpas| |ti| |lumba|.
Nupay| |kasta|,| |idi| |kagudua| |nga| |orasen| |ti| |panagtarayda|,| |ken| |medio| |namaga| |manen|,| |kellaat| |nga| |impukkaw| |ti| |Dodo| |“|Nalpasen| |ti| |lumba|!|”
