(|I|,| |komo| |ku| |bo| |por| |gusta| |purba| |e| |kos| |bo| |mes|,| |algun| |dia| |di| |wenter|,| |lo| |mi| |konta| |bo| |kon| |e| |Dodo| |a| |manehá| |esaki|.|)
