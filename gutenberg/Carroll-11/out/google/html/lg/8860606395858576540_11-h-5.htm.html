ESSUULA V.
Amagezi okuva mu Caterpillar

Enseenene ne Alice baatunuuliragana okumala ekiseera mu kasirise: ku nkomerero Enkwale n’eggyayo enkovu mu kamwa kaayo, n’emuyogerako mu ddoboozi eriwunya, ery’otulo.

Ensigo yagamba nti: “Ggwe ani ? ”

Kino tekyali kya kuggulawo mboozi ezzaamu amaanyi. Alice yaddamu, ng’alina ensonyi, “Nze —simanyi bulungi, ssebo, mu kiseera kino kyokka —waakiri mmanyi kye nnali bwe nnazuukuse enkya ya leero, naye ndowooza nti okuva olwo nteekwa okuba nga nnakyusibwa emirundi egiwerako.”

“Ekyo otegeeza ki?” Enseenene bwe yagamba mu ngeri ey’obukambwe. “Weennyonnyole!”

“Sisobola kwennyonnyola , ntya ssebo,” Alice bwe yagamba, “kubanga siri nze kennyini, olaba.”

“Silaba,” Enkwale bwe yagamba.

Alice bwe yaddamu mu ngeri ey’empisa ennyo: “Ntya nti sisobola kukitegeera bulungi nnyo, kubanga nze kennyini sisobola kukitegeera okutandika;

“Si bwe kiri,” Enkwale bwe yagamba.

“Kale, mpozzi tonnakizuula bwe kityo,” Alice bwe yagamba; “naye bw’oba olina okufuuka chrysalis — olunaku lumu ojja kukikola, omanyi —n’oluvannyuma oluvannyuma lw’ekyo n’ofuuka ekiwujjo, nsaanidde okulowooza nti ojja kukiwulira nga kiwuniikiriza katono, si bwe kiri?”

“Si katono,” Enkwale bwe yagamba.

Alice yagamba nti: “Kale, oboolyawo enneewulira zo ziyinza okuba ez’enjawulo; “kye mmanyi kiri nti, kyandiwulidde nga kya njawulo nnyo gyendi .”

"Ggwe!" Enseenene bwe yagamba mu ngeri ey’okunyooma. "Gwe ani? "

Ekyabazzaayo nate ku ntandikwa y’emboozi. Alice yawulira ng’anyiize katono olw’Ensowera okwogera ebigambo ebimpi ennyo bwe bityo, era n’asitula n’agamba, mu ngeri ey’obukambwe ennyo, “Ndowooza, wandibadde ombuulira ky’oli , okusooka.”

"Lwaaki?" Enseenene bwe yagamba.

Wano waliwo ekibuuzo ekirala ekyali kisobera; era nga Alice bwe yali tasobola kulowooza ku nsonga yonna nnungi, era nga Caterpillar bwe yalabika ng’eri mu mbeera etali nnungi nnyo mu birowoozo, yakyuka.

“Komawo!” Enkwale yamuyita. “Nnina ekintu ekikulu kye njagala okwogera!”

Kino kyawulikika nga kisuubiza, mazima ddala: Alice yakyuka n’akomawo nate.

“Kuuma obusungu bwo,” Enkwale bwe yagamba.

“Ebyo byokka?” Alice bwe yagamba ng’amira obusungu bwe nga bw’asobola.

“Nedda,” Enseenene bwe yagamba.

Alice yalowooza nti ayinza okulinda, nga bwe yali talina kirala kya kukola, era mpozzi oluvannyuma lw’ebyo byonna kiyinza okumugamba ekintu ekisaanira okuwulira. Okumala eddakiika ezimu yafuuwa nga teyogedde, naye ku nkomerero yasumulula emikono, n’eddamu okuggya enkoba mu kamwa kaayo, n’egamba nti, “Kale olowooza okyusiddwa, nedda?”

“Ntya nti bwe ndi, ssebo,” Alice bwe yagamba; “Sisobola kujjukira bintu nga bwe nnakozesanga —era sibikuuma sayizi y’emu okumala eddakiika kkumi nga tuli wamu!”

“Tosobola kujjukira bintu ki ?” Enseenene bwe yagamba.

“Kale, ngezezzaako okugamba nti “Aka njuki akatono akakola ennyo kakola katya,” naye byonna byajja bya njawulo!” Alice yaddamu mu ddoboozi ery’ennaku ennyo.

“Ddamu nti, ‘ Okaddiye, Faaza William ,’” Enkwale bwe yagamba.

Alice yakwata emikono gye, n’atandika:—

“Okaddiye, Faaza William,” omuvubuka bwe yagamba, .
“N’enviiri zo zifuuse njeru nnyo;
Era naye oyimirira ku mutwe gwo obutasalako—
Olowooza, ku myaka gyo, kituufu?”

Faaza William yaddamu mutabani we nti, “Mu buto bwange, .
“Nnali ntya nti kiyinza okulumya obwongo;
Naye, kati nga bwe nkakasa bulungi nti sirina, .
Lwaki, nkikola enfunda n’enfunda.”

Omuvubuka oyo yagamba nti: “Okaddiye, nga bwe nnagambye emabegako, .
Era bakuze okusinga okugejja mu ngeri etaali ya bulijjo;
Naye wakyusa back-somersault ku mulyango—
Saba, ensonga lwaki ekyo?”

“Mu buvubuka bwange,” omugezigezi bwe yagamba, ng’asikasika ebizibiti bye enzirugavu, .
“Nnakuuma ebitundu byange byonna nga bigonvu nnyo
Nga bakozesa ekizigo kino —siringi emu mu bbokisi — .
Kkiriza nkuguze abafumbo?”

Omuvubuka oyo yagamba nti: “Okaddiye, era ensaya zo zinafu nnyo
Ku kintu kyonna ekikaluba okusinga suet;
Naye n’omaliriza embuzi, n’amagumba n’omumwa—
Saba, wasobola otya okukikola?”

Kitaawe yagamba nti: “Mu buto bwange, nnagenda mu mateeka, .
Era buli musango yayomba ne mukyala wange;
N’amaanyi g’ebinywa, ge gaawa akawanga kange, .
Amaze obulamu bwange bwonna.”

Omuvubuka oyo yagamba nti: “Okaddiye, omuntu tayinza kulowooza
Nti eriiso lyo lyali linywevu nga bulijjo;
Naye watebenkeza eel ku nkomerero y’ennyindo yo—
Kiki ekyakuleetera okuba omugezi ennyo?”

“Nzizzeemu ebibuuzo bisatu, era ekyo kimala,”
Kitaawe bwe yagamba nti; “towa mpewo!
Olowooza nsobola okuwuliriza olunaku lwonna ebintu ng’ebyo?
Muveeko, oba si ekyo nja kukusamba wansi ku madaala!”

“Ekyo tekigambibwa bulungi,” Enkwale bwe yagamba.

“Si kituufu nnyo , ntya,” Alice bwe yagamba ng’akwatibwa ensonyi; “ebimu ku bigambo bikyusiddwa.”

“Kikyamu okuva ku ntandikwa okutuuka ku nkomerero,” Enkwale bwe yagamba mu ngeri ey’okusalawo, era ne wabaawo okusirika okumala eddakiika ezimu.

Enseenene ye yasooka okwogera.

“Oyagala kubeera sayizi ki?” bwe kyabuuzizza.

“Oo, siri particular ku sayizi,” Alice bwe yaddamu mu bwangu; “omu yekka atayagala kukyuka emirundi mingi bwe gityo, omanyi.”

“ Simanyi ,” Enkwale bwe yagamba.

Alice teyayogera kintu kyonna: yali takontanangako nnyo mu bulamu bwe, era yawulira nti yali abuze obusungu.

“Oli mumativu kati?” Enseenene bwe yagamba.

“Kale, nandyagadde okubeera omunene katono , ssebo, bw’oba ​​tolina buzibu,” Alice bwe yagamba: “yinsi ssatu buwanvu bwa nnaku bwe butyo okubeera.”

“Mazima ddala buwanvu bulungi nnyo!” Enseenene bwe yagamba mu busungu, nga yeekuza nga yeegolodde nga bw’eyogera (yali yinsi ssatu ddala obuwanvu).

“Naye simanyidde!” bwe yeegayirira Alice omwavu mu ddoboozi ery’okusaasira. Era n’alowooza ku ye nti, “Njagala ebitonde tebyandinyiize mangu bwe bityo!”

“Ojja kukimanyiira mu budde,” Enkwale bwe yagamba; n’eyingiza enkoba mu kamwa kaayo n’eddamu okufuuwa sigala.

Ku mulundi guno Alice yalinda n’obugumiikiriza okutuusa lwe yasalawo okuddamu okwogera. Mu ddakiika emu oba bbiri Enseenene yaggyayo hookah mu kamwa kaayo n’ewuuba omulundi gumu oba ebiri, ne yeekankanya. Olwo n’ekka ku ffene, n’eseeyeeya n’egenda mu muddo, ng’eyogera bugambo nga bw’egenda nti, “Oludda olumu lujja kukufuula omuwanvu, ate oludda olulala lujja kukufuula omumpi.”

“Oludda olumu lwa kiki? Oludda olulala lwa ki? ” Alice bwe yalowooza mu mutima gwe.

“Ku ffene,” Enkwale bwe yagamba, ng’alinga eyagibuuzizza mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka; era mu kaseera akalala ne kiva mu maaso.

Alice yasigala ng’atunuulira ffene n’okulowooza okumala eddakiika emu, ng’agezaako okuzuula enjuyi zombi ezaali; era nga bwe kyali kyetoolodde ddala, kino yakisanga nga kibuuzo kizibu nnyo. Kyokka, ku nkomerero yagolola emikono gye okugyetooloola we gyali gigenda, era n’amenya akatono ku bbali buli mukono.

“Era kaakano ani ki?” yagamba mu mutima gwe, n’anyiga akatono ku mukono ogwa ddyo okugezaako ekikolwa: akaseera akaddako yawulira ekikonde eky’amaanyi wansi w’akalevu ke: kyali kimukubye ekigere!

Yatya nnyo enkyukakyuka eno ey’amangu ennyo, naye yawulira nga tewali budde bubula, anti yali akendeera mangu; bwatyo n’atandika okukola omulundi gumu okulya ku kitundu ekirala. Ekirevu kye kyali kinyigiriziddwa nnyo ku kigere kye, nga tewali kifo kya kuggulawo kamwa ke; naye yakikola ku nkomerero, n’asobola okumira akatundu k’ekitundu ky’omukono ogwa kkono.

“Jjangu, omutwe gwange gwa ddembe ku nkomerero!” Alice bwe yagamba mu ddoboozi ery’essanyu, eryakyuka ne lifuuka ery’okutya mu kaseera akalala, bwe yakizuula nti ebibegabega bye tebirina we bisangibwa: kyokka kye yali asobola okulaba, bwe yatunula wansi, bwe bulago obuwanvu ennyo, obwali bulabika ng’obusituka ng’ekikolo okuva mu nnyanja y’ebikoola ebya kiragala ebyagalamira wansi we wala.

“Ebintu ebyo byonna ebya kiragala biyinza kuba ki?” Alice bwe yagamba. “Era ebibegabega byange bituuse wa? Era oh, emikono gyange egyavu, kibeera kitya sisobola kukulaba?” Yali azitambuza nga bw’ayogera, naye nga tewali kivaamu kirabika nga kigoberera, okuggyako okukankana katono mu bikoola ebya kiragala eby’ewala.

Nga bwe kirabika nga tewali mukisa gwonna kutuusa mikono gye waggulu ku mutwe gwe, yagezaako okukkakkanya omutwe gwe, era n’asanyuka nnyo okukizuula nti ensingo ye yali egenda kufukamira mangu mu ludda lwonna, ng’omusota. Yali yaakasobola okugikoona wansi mu zigzag ennungi, era yali agenda kubbira mu bikoola, bye yasanga nga tebirina kirala wabula entikko z’emiti gye yali ataayaaya wansi, eddoboozi ery’amaanyi ne limuleetera okudda emabega mu bwangu: ejjiba eddene lyali libuuse mu maaso ge, era nga limukuba nnyo n’ebiwaawaatiro byalyo.

“Omusota!” Enjiibwa bwe yaleekaana.

“Nze siri musota!” Alice bwe yagamba mu busungu. “Ndeka nze!”

“Omusota, nate ŋŋamba!” yaddiŋŋana Ejjiba, naye mu ddoboozi erisingako obukkakkamu, n’agattako n’ekika ky’okusinda nti, “Ngezezzaako buli ngeri, era tewali kirabika kibasaanira!”

Alice yagamba nti: “Sirina n’akatono ke mmanyi ku ky’oyogerako.”

“Ngezezzaako emirandira gy’emiti, era ngezezzaako ku bbanka, era ngezezzaako n’ebikomera,” Ejjiba bwe yagenda mu maaso, nga temufaako; “naye emisota egyo!Tewali gibasanyusa!”

Alice yeeyongera okusoberwa, naye yalowooza nti tewali mugaso gwonna mu kwogera kintu kyonna okutuusa ng’Ejjiba liwedde.

“Nga bwe kitaali buzibu bumala kuzaala magi,” Ejjiba bwe lyagamba; “naye nteekwa okuba nga ntunuulidde emisota ekiro n’emisana! Lwaki, wiiki zino essatu sifunangako katono ka tulo!”

Alice eyali atandise okulaba amakulu gaakyo yagamba nti: “Nsonyiwa nnyo okunyiiga.”

“Era nga bwe nnali nkwata omuti ogusinga obuwanvu mu nsiko,” Ejjiba bwe lyagenda mu maaso, ng’eyimusa eddoboozi lyalyo okutuuka ku kuleekaana, “era nga bwe nnali ndowooza nti ku nkomerero nsaanidde okuba ddembe, bateekwa okwetaaga okujja nga bawuubaala wansi okuva mu bbanga! Ugh, Omusota!”

“Naye siri musota, nkugamba!” Alice bwe yagamba. “Nze ndi—Nze ndi—”

“Kale! Oli ki?” Ejjiba bwe lyagamba. “Ndaba ng’ogezaako okuyiiya ekintu!”

“Nze —ndi muwala muto,” Alice bwe yagamba, ng’abuusabuusa nnyo, ng’ajjukira omuwendo gw’enkyukakyuka ze yali ayiseemu ku lunaku olwo.

“Emboozi eyinza okuba nga ddala!” Ejjiba bwe lyagamba mu ddoboozi ery’okunyooma ennyo. “Nze ndabye abawala abato bangi nnyo mu kiseera kyange, naye sibangako n’omu alina ensingo ng’eyo! Nedda, nedda! Oli musota;era tewali mugaso gwonna kukyegaana. Nsuubira nti ojja kuba oddako okuntegeeza nti towoomerangako ggi!”

Alice eyali omwana ow’amazima ennyo yagamba nti: “ Nwoomerwa amagi, mazima ddala; “naye abawala abato balya amagi nnyo ng’emisota bwe girya, omanyi.”

“Sikikkiriza,” Ejjiba bwe lyagamba; “naye bwe bakola, lwaki olwo bali kika kya musota, ekyo kyokka kye nsobola okwogera.”

Kino kyali kirowoozo kipya nnyo eri Alice, nti yasirika nnyo okumala eddakiika emu oba bbiri, ekyawa Ejjiba omukisa okugattako nti, “Onoonya amagi, ekyo nkimanyi bulungi;era kinkwatako oba oli muwala muto oba musota?”

“Kinkwatako nnyo gyendi ,” Alice bwe yagamba mu bwangu; “naye sinoonya magi, nga bwe kibeera;era bwemba nali, sandibadde njagala gammwe : sigagala nga mbisi.”

“Kale, beera off, olwo!” Ejjiba bwe lyagamba mu ddoboozi ery’ekibogwe, nga bwe liddamu okusenga mu kisu kyalyo. Alice yafukamira wakati mu miti nga bw’asobola, kubanga ensingo ye yasigala yeenyigira mu matabi, era buli luvannyuma lwa kaseera yalina okuyimirira n’agisumulula. Oluvannyuma lw’akaseera yajjukira nti yali akyakwata ebitundu bya ffene mu ngalo ze, era n’atandika okukola n’obwegendereza ennyo, ng’asooka kunyiga ku emu n’oluvannyuma ku ndala, n’okukula oluusi nga muwanvu ate oluusi n’afuuka omumpi, okutuusa lwe yasobola okwezza wansi ku buwanvu bwe obwa bulijjo.

Kyabanga kiwanvu nnyo nga tannaba kintu kyonna kumpi sayizi entuufu, ne kiba nti mu kusooka kyawulikika nga kyewuunyisa nnyo; naye yakimanyiira mu ddakiika ntono, n’atandika okwogera naye, nga bulijjo. "Jjangu, waliwo ekitundu ky'enteekateeka yange ekikoleddwa kati! Enkyukakyuka zino zonna nga zisobera! Sikakasa kye ngenda kuba, okuva ku ddakiika emu okudda ku ndala! Kyokka, nkomyewo mu sayizi yange entuufu: ekiddako kiri nti, okuyingira mu lusuku olwo olulungi —ekyo kikolebwa kitya , nneebuuza?" Bwe yali ayogera bino, yatuuka mangu ku kifo ekiggule, nga mu kyo waliwo akayumba akatono obuwanvu ffuuti nga nnya. Alice yalowooza nti: “Oyo yenna abeera eyo, tekijja kukola kubatuukako sayizi eno : lwaki, mbatiisizza okuva mu magezi gaabwe!” Bwatyo n’atandika okuddamu okunyiga ku mukono ogwa ddyo, era teyafuba kusemberera nnyumba okutuusa lwe yeezza wansi okutuuka ku yinsi mwenda obuwanvu.
