ESSUULA VIII.
Ekifo kya Nnabagereka Croquet-Ground

Omuti omunene ogwa rose gwali guyimiridde okumpi n’omulyango oguyingira mu lusuku: roses ezaali zimerako zaali njeru, naye nga kuliko abalimi basatu, nga bazisiiga langi emmyufu nga bakola nnyo. Alice kino yakilowooza nti kintu kya kwewuunya nnyo, era n’asemberera okubatunuulira, era bwe yatuuka gye bali n’awulira omu ku bo ng’agamba nti, “Mutunuulire kati, Abataano! Togenda kunfuka langi bw’atyo!”

“Saasobola butamanya,” Five bwe yagamba, mu ddoboozi ery’ekibogwe; “Musanvu yadduka enkokola yange.”

Ku ekyo Seven n’atunula waggulu n’agamba nti, “Kyo kituufu, Five! Bulijjo omusango guteeke ku balala!”

“ Kirungi toyogera!” Five bwe yategeezezza. “Nnawulidde Nnabagereka ng’agamba jjo lyokka ggwe osaana okutemebwako omutwe!”

“Kiki lwa?” oyo eyali asoose okwogera bwe yagamba.

“Ekyo si nsonga yo , Babiri!” Omusanvu bwe yagamba.

“Yee, bizinensi ye !” Five bwe yagamba, “era nja kumugamba — kyali kya kuleeta mufumbi tulip-roots mu kifo ky’obutungulu.”

Musanvu yasuula wansi bbulawuzi ye, era yali yaakatandika “Kale, ku bintu byonna ebitali bya bwenkanya—” eriiso lye ne ligwa ku Alice, ng’ayimiridde ng’abatunuulira, era n’akebera mu bwangu: abalala nabo ne batunula okwetooloola, era bonna ne bafukamira wansi.

Alice bwe yagamba ng’alina ensonyi katono nti: “Oyinza okumbuulira lwaki osiiga langi za roses ezo?”

Five ne Seven tebaalina kye boogera, wabula ne batunuulira Two. Ababiri baatandika mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka, “Lwaki ekituufu kiri nti, olaba, Nnabo, kino wano kyandibadde muti gwa rose- omumyufu , ne tussaamu omweru mu nsobi;era singa Nnabagereka yali agenda kukizuula, ffenna twandibadde tusaliddwako emitwe gyaffe, omanyi. Kale olaba, Nnalulungi, tukola ekisoboka, nga tannajja, oku—” Mu kaseera kano Five, eyali abadde atunudde emitala w’olusuku n’okweraliikirira, yayita out “Nnabagereka! Nnabagereka!” era amangu ago abalimi abasatu ne beesuula ku maaso gaabwe. Waaliwo eddoboozi ly’ebigere bingi, era Alice n’atunula okwetooloola, ng’ayagala nnyo okulaba Nnabagereka.

Okusooka kwajja abaserikale kkumi nga basitudde emiggo; bano bonna baali bafaanana ng’abalimi b’ensuku abasatu, abawanvu era nga bapapajjo, ng’emikono n’ebigere byabwe biri ku nsonda: ekiddako n’abaami b’omu lubiri ekkumi; bino byali biyooyooteddwa wonna ne dayimanda, era nga bitambula bibiri na bibiri, ng’abaserikale bwe baakola. Oluvannyuma lw’ebyo ne wajja abaana b’obwakabaka; baali kkumi, era abaagalwa abato ne bajja nga babuuka mu ssanyu nga bakwatagana mu ngalo, mu bafumbo: bonna baali bayooyooteddwa n’emitima. Ekiddako kwe kujja abagenyi, ng’abasinga baali Bassekabaka ne Bannabagereka, era mu bo Alice mwe yategedde Olukalu Oluzungu: lwali lwogera mu ngeri ey’okutya okw’amangu, nga lumwenya ku buli kyogerwa, era n’eyitawo nga temutegedde. Awo n’agoberera Knave of Hearts, ng’asitudde engule ya Kabaka ku mutto gwa velvet omumyufu; era, ekisembayo mu kukuŋŋaana kuno okunene, kwajja KABAKA NE NNAABANSI W’OMUTIMA.

Alice yali abuusabuusa nnyo oba nga tasaanidde kugalamira ku maaso ge ng’abalimi b’ensuku abasatu, naye yali tajjukira nti yali awuliddeko ku tteeka ng’eryo mu kukuŋŋaana; “era n’ekirala, omukolo gwandibadde gugasa ki,” bwe yalowooza, “ssinga abantu baalina byonna okugalamira ku maaso gaabwe, ne batasobola kukiraba?” Bwatyo n’ayimirira we yali, n’alinda.

Omukolo bwe gwajja okwolekera Alice, bonna ne bayimirira ne bamutunuulira, era Nnabagereka n’agamba mu bukambwe nti “Ono y’ani?” Yakigamba Knave of Hearts, eyafukamira yekka n’amwenya ng’amuddamu.

"Siru!" Nnabagereka bwe yagamba ng’akuba omutwe nga talina bugumiikiriza; era, ng’akyuka n’atunuulira Alice, n’agenda mu maaso n’agamba nti, “Omwana erinnya lyo otya?”

“Amannya gange nze Alice, kale nsaba Ssaabasajja,” Alice bwe yagamba mu mpisa nnyingi; naye n’agattako, mu mutima gwe nti, “Lwaki, zibeera ppaaka ya kaadi zokka, oluvannyuma lw’ebyo byonna. Sikyetaaga kuzitya!”

“Era bano baani? ” Nnabagereka bwe yagamba ng’asonga ku balimi abasatu abaali bagalamidde okwetooloola omuti gwa rose; kubanga, olaba, nga bwe baali bagalamidde mu maaso gaabwe, era ng’omusono ku migongo gyabwe gwe gumu n’abalala abaali mu kibinja, yali tasobola kumanya oba baali ba nnimiro, oba baserikale, oba ba lubiri, oba abaana be basatu bennyini.

“ Mmanye ntya?” Alice bwe yagamba nga yeewuunya obuvumu bwe. “Si bizinensi yange .”

Nnabagereka yafuuka kiragala olw’obusungu, era, oluvannyuma lw’okumutunuulira n’amaaso ag’amaaso okumala akaseera ng’ensolo ey’omu nsiko, n’akuba enduulu nti “Off with her head! Off—”

“Eby’obusiru!” Alice bwe yagamba, mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka ennyo era ng’asalawo, era Nnabagereka n’asirika.

Kabaka n’amuteeka omukono ku mukono gwe, n’agamba mu nsonyi nti “Lowooza, omwagalwa wange: mwana muto yekka!”

Nnabagereka n’amukyuka n’obusungu, n’agamba Omusota “Muzikyuse!”

Omuknave yakikola, n’obwegendereza bungi, n’ekigere kimu.

"Okuyimuka!" Nnabagereka bwe yagamba, mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka era ery’amaanyi, era abalimi abasatu amangu ago ne babuuka waggulu, ne batandika okufukamira eri Kabaka, Nnabagereka, abaana b’obwakabaka, n’abantu abalala bonna.

“Ekyo kireke!” Nnabagereka bwe yayomba. “Onfuula omuzira.” Era awo, ng’akyuka n’atunuulira omuti gwa rose, n’agenda mu maaso n’agamba nti, “Kiki ky’obadde okola wano?”

“Ka kisanyusa Ssaabasajja,” bwatyo Two bwe yagamba, mu ddoboozi ery’obwetoowaze ennyo, ng’akka wansi ku kugulu okumu nga bw’ayogera, “twali tugezaako—”

" Ndaba !" bwatyo Nnabagereka bwe yagamba, mu kiseera ekyo eyali abadde yeekenneenya roses. “Bagenda n’emitwe gyabwe!” era omukolo ne gugenda mu maaso, basatu ku bajaasi ne basigala emabega okutta abalimi b’ensuku abaali batalina mukisa, abaddukira ewa Alice okufuna obukuumi.

“Tojja kutemebwako mutwe!” Alice bwe yagamba, n’abiteeka mu kiyungu ekinene eky’ebimuli ekyali kiyimiridde okumpi. Abaserikale abasatu baatambulatambula okumala eddakiika emu oba bbiri, nga babanoonya, oluvannyuma ne batambula mu kasirise nga bagoberera abalala.

“Emitwe gyabwe givuddeko?” Nnabagereka bwe yaleekaana.

“Emitwe gyabwe giweddewo, bwe kiba nga kisanyusa Ssaabasajja!” abaserikale ne baleekaana nga baddamu.

“Kyo kituufu!” Nnabagereka bwe yaleekaana. “Osobola okuzannya croquet?”

Abaserikale baasirika, ne batunuulira Alice, anti kirabika ekibuuzo ekyo kyali kigendereddwamu ye.

"Yee!" Alice bwe yaleekaana.

“Jjangu olwo!” Nnabagereka bwe yawuluguma, era Alice ne yeegatta ku mukolo, nga yeebuuza nnyo ekiddako.

“Lu—lunaku lulungi nnyo!” eddoboozi ery’ensonyi eryali ku ludda lwe bwe lyagamba. Yali atambula kumpi n’Olubuto Oluzungu, olwali lumutunula mu maaso n’okweraliikirira.

“Nnyo,” Alice bwe yagamba: “—Duchess ali ludda wa?”

“Sisirika! Siriika!” Enkazaluggya bwe yagamba mu ddoboozi erya wansi era ery’amangu. Yatunula ku kibegabega kye n’okweraliikirira ng’ayogera, n’oluvannyuma ne yeesitula ku bigere, n’ateeka akamwa ke okumpi n’okutu kwe, n’amuwuuba nti “Ali wansi w’ekibonerezo ky’okuttibwa.”

“Kiki lwa?” Alice bwe yagamba.

“Wagamba nti ‘Nga kisaasira!’?” Omulalu bwe yabuuzizza.

Alice yagamba nti: “Nedda, saakikola: “Silowooza nti kyakusaasira n’akatono, nnamugamba nti ‘Kiki?’”

“Yakuba ebikonde mu matu ga Nnabagereka —” Omulalu bwe yatandika. Alice yawa enduulu entono ey’okuseka. “Oo, sirika!” Omulalu yayomba mu ddoboozi ery’entiisa. “Nnabagereka ajja kukuwulira!Olaba yajja kikeerezi nnyo, era Nnabagereka n’agamba nti—”

“Mutuuke mu bifo byammwe!” Nnabagereka yaleekaana mu ddoboozi ery’okubwatuka, abantu ne batandika okudduka mu njuyi zonna, nga bwe bakubagana; wabula baatereera mu ddakiika emu oba bbiri, era omupiira ne gutandika. Alice yalowooza nti talabangako kifo kya croquet eky’okwewuunya bwe kiti mu bulamu bwe; byonna byali biwujjo na mifulejje; emipiira gyali gya hedgehogs nnamu, mallets zaali flamingo nga zilamu, era abaserikale baalina okwekubisaamu emirundi ebiri n’okuyimirira ku mikono n’ebigere, okukola ebisenge ebiwanvu.

Obuzibu obukulu Alice bwe yasooka okufuna bwali mu kuddukanya flamingo ye: yasobola okufuna omubiri gwagwo nga gukwekeddwa, mu ngeri eyeeyagaza ekimala, wansi w’omukono gwe, ng’amagulu gaagwo gawanikiddwa wansi, naye okutwalira awamu, nga bwe yali afunye ensingo yaayo nga egoloddwa bulungi, era ng’egenda okuwa enseenene ekikonde n’omutwe gwayo, yandibadde yeekyusakyusa n’atunula waggulu mu maaso ge, ng’alina ekifaananyi ekisoberwa ennyo teyasobola butabutuka kuseka: era bwe yali amaze okussa omutwe gwayo wansi, era ng’agenda okuddamu okutandika, kyali kinyiiza nnyo okusanga nti enseenene yali yeesumulula, era ng’eri mu kikolwa eky’okuseeyeeya: ng’oggyeeko bino byonna, okutwalira awamu waaliwo omuggo oba omukutu mu kkubo buli we yali ayagala okusindika enseenene, era, ng’abaserikale abaali bakubisaamu emirundi ebiri bulijjo basituka ne batambula okugenda eri abalala ebitundu by’ettaka, mu bbanga ttono Alice yatuuka ku kigambo nti ddala gwali muzannyo muzibu nnyo.

Abazannyi bonna baazannya omulundi gumu nga tebalinze kukyusa, nga bayomba ebbanga eryo lyonna, era nga balwanirira ebiwuka ebiyitibwa hedgehogs; era mu bbanga ttono ddala Nnabagereka yali mu bwagazi obw’amaanyi, n’agenda ng’akuba enduulu, n’aleekaana nti “Off with his head!” oba “Off n’omutwe gwe!” nga omulundi gumu mu ddakiika.

Alice yatandika okuwulira nga tateredde nnyo: okukakasa, yali tannafuna nkaayana yonna ne Nnabagereka, naye yali akimanyi nti kiyinza okubaawo eddakiika yonna, “n’oluvannyuma,” bwe yalowooza, “kiki ekyandifuuse? Basinga kwagala nnyo okusala abantu emitwe wano; ekyewuunyisa ekinene kiri nti, waliwo omuntu yenna asigadde nga mulamu!”

Yali anoonya engeri gy’ayinza okuddukamu, era nga yeebuuza oba asobola okuwona nga talabiddwa, n’alaba endabika ey’okwewuunya mu bbanga: yamusobera nnyo mu kusooka, naye, oluvannyuma lw’okugiraba eddakiika emu oba bbiri, yakifuula eky’okunyenya, era n’agamba mu mutima gwe nti “It’s the Cheshire Cat: now I shall have somebody to talk to.”

“Olinnya otya?” Embwa bwe yagamba, kasita wabaddewo akamwa akamala okwogera nakyo.

Alice yalinda okutuusa amaaso lwe galabika, n’oluvannyuma n’anyenya omutwe. Yalowooza nti: “Tekirina mugaso kwogera nayo okutuusa ng’amatu gaayo gazze, oba waakiri emu ku go.” Mu ddakiika endala omutwe gwonna ne gulabika, olwo Alice n’ateeka wansi flamingo ye, n’atandika okuwandiika ku muzannyo, ng’awulira nga musanyufu nnyo nti alina omuntu amuwuliriza. Embwa yalabika ng’elowooza nti kati kyali kimala, era nga tewali kisingawo ku kirabika.

“Silowooza nti bazannya n’akatono mu bwenkanya,” Alice bwe yatandika, mu ddoboozi erisinga okwemulugunya, “era bonna bayomba mu ngeri ey’entiisa ennyo omuntu tasobola kwewulira ng’ayogera —era balabika nga tebalina mateeka gonna naddala;waakiri, bwe wabaawo, tewali abifaako —era tolina kirowoozo ku ngeri gye kitabuzaabuzaamu ebintu byonna okuba ebiramu; okugeza, waliwo arch gye ngenda okuyitamu ekiddako nga ntambula ku nkomerero endala ey’ettaka —era nnandibadde nkuba enkovu ya Nnabagereka kati, yokka yadduka bwe yalaba eyange ng’ejja!”

“Oyagala otya Nnabagereka?” Embwa bwe yagamba mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka.

“Si bwe kiri,” Alice bwe yagamba: “she’s so extremely—” Mu kiseera ekyo kyennyini yakiraba nti Nnabagereka yali kumpi emabega we, ng’awuliriza: bw’atyo n’agenda mu maaso, “—ayinza okuwangula, nti tekiba kya mugaso nnyo ng’amaliriza omuzannyo.”

Nnabagereka yamwenya n’ayitawo.

“ Oyogera ne ani?” Kabaka bwe yagamba, ng’alinnya eri Alice, n’atunuulira omutwe gw’Embwa n’okwegomba okungi.

Alice yagamba nti: “Mukwano gwange —Embwa ya Cheshire: kiriza ngiyanjule.”

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Endabika yaayo sinjagala n’akatono: “kyokka, eyinza okunnywegera omukono singa eyagala.”

“Nsinga butabeera,” Embwa bwe yagamba.

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Tobeera mujoozi, era tontunuulira bw’otyo!” Yatuuka emabega wa Alice nga bw’ayogera.

Alice yagamba nti: “Embwa eyinza okutunuulira kabaka. “Ekyo nkisomye mu kitabo ekimu, naye sijjukira wa.”

“Kale, kiteekwa okuggyibwawo,” Kabaka bwe yagamba mu bumalirivu ennyo, era n’ayita Nnabagereka, eyali ayitawo mu kaseera ako, “Omwagalwa wange! Njagala n’embwa eno eggiddwawo!”

Nnabagereka yalina engeri emu yokka gye yagonjoolamu ebizibu byonna, ebinene oba ebitono. “Agenda n’omutwe gwe!” bwe yagamba, nga tatunudde wadde okwetooloola.

“Nze kennyini nja kuleeta omutemu,” Kabaka bwe yagamba n’obwagazi, era n’ayanguwa okugenda.

Alice yalowooza nti ayinza n’okudda emabega, n’alaba omuzannyo bwe gugenda mu maaso, nga bw’awulira eddoboozi lya Nnabagereka mu bbanga, nga likuba enduulu olw’obwagazi. Yali yawulira dda ekibonerezo kye eky’abazannyi basatu ku bagenda okuttibwa olw’okusubwa eddaala lyabwe, era nga tayagala nnyo ndabika y’ebintu, anti omuzannyo gwali mu kutabulwatabulwa nnyo nga tamanyi oba ddagala lye oba nedda. Bwatyo n’agenda okunoonya enseenene ye.

Enseenene yali yeenyigira mu lutalo n’enseenene endala, ekyalabika ng’omukisa omulungi ennyo eri Alice ogw’okusalako emu ku zo n’endala: ekizibu kyokka kyali nti, flamingo ye yali egenze okusomoka okutuuka ku ludda olulala olw’olusuku, Alice gye yali asobola okugiraba ng’egezaako mu ngeri etaliimu buyambi okubuuka waggulu mu muti.

We yakwatidde flamingo n’agikomyawo, olutalo lwali luwedde, era enseenene zombi zaali tezirabika: “naye si kikulu nnyo,” Alice bwe yalowooza, “nga ebikondo byonna bwe biweddewo okuva ku ludda luno olw’ettaka.” Bwatyo yagikweka wansi w’omukono gwe, ereme kuddamu kutoloka, n’addayo okwogerako katono ne mukwano gwe.

Bwe yakomawo ku Cheshire Cat, yeewuunya okusanga ekibiina ekinene ennyo nga kikuŋŋaanyiziddwa okwetooloola: waaliwo okusika omuguwa okugenda mu maaso wakati w’omutemu, Kabaka, ne Nnabagereka, bonna nga boogera omulundi gumu, ate abalala bonna nga basirise nnyo, era nga balabika nga tebanyuma nnyo.

Akaseera Alice we yalabikira, bonna abasatu baamulaajana okugonjoola ekibuuzo ekyo, era ne bamuddiŋŋana ensonga zaabwe, naye, nga bonna boogera omulundi gumu, yakisanga nga kizibu nnyo ddala okuzuula ekyo kyennyini kye baayogera.

Ensonga y’omutemu yali, nti tosobola kusala mutwe okuggyako nga waliwo omulambo gw’oyinza okugusalako: nti yali talina kukola kintu ng’ekyo, era yali tagenda kutandika mu kiseera ky’obulamu bwe .

Ensonga ya Kabaka yali, nti ekintu kyonna ekirina omutwe kisobola okutemebwako omutwe, era nti tolina kwogera bya busirusiru.

Ensonga ya Nnabagereka yali, nti singa ekintu tekikolebwa ku nsonga eno mu bbanga eritakka wansi yandibadde buli muntu attiddwa, all round. (Ekigambo kino ekyasembayo kye kyali kifudde ekibiina kyonna okulabika ng’eky’amaanyi era nga kyeraliikirira.)

Alice yali tayinza kulowooza ku kintu kirala ky’ayinza kwogera okuggyako “Kya Duchess: kyandibadde kirungi omubuuze ku nsonga eyo.”

“Ali mu kkomera,” Nnabagereka bwe yagamba omutemu: “muleete wano.” Omutemu n’agenda ng’akasaale.

Omutwe gw’Embwa gwatandika okuzikira mu kaseera ke yali takyaliwo, era, we yakomawo ne Duchess, gwali guweddewo ddala; bwe batyo Kabaka n’omutemu ne badduka mu ngeri ey’ekigwenyufu waggulu ne wansi nga baginoonya, ate ng’ekibiina ekisigadde ne badda mu muzannyo.
