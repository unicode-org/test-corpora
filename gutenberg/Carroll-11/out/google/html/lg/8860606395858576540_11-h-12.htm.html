ESSUULA XII.
Obujulizi bwa Alice

"Wano!" Alice bwe yakaaba, nga yeerabira nnyo mu bbugumu ly’akaseera ako engeri gye yali akuze mu ddakiika ezisembayo, era n’abuuka waggulu mu bwangu nnyo ne kiba nti n’akuba ekibokisi ky’abalamuzi n’empenda za sikaati ye, n’anyiiza abalamuzi bonna ku mitwe gy’abantu abaali wansi, era awo ne bagalamira nga babunye, nga bamujjukiza nnyo ensi y’ebyennyanja ebya zaabu bye yali atabuse mu butanwa wiiki ewedde.

“Oo, nkwegayiridde okusonyiyibwa!” yayomba mu ddoboozi ery’okunyiiga ennyo, n’atandika okuddamu okuzisitula mu bwangu nga bw’asobola, kubanga akabenje k’ebyennyanja ebya zaabu kaasigala nga kadduka mu mutwe gwe, era yalina ekirowoozo ekitali kitegeerekeka nti birina okukunganyizibwa omulundi gumu ne biddizibwa mu kibokisi ky’abalamuzi, oba si ekyo byandifudde.

“Omusango teguyinza kugenda mu maaso,” Kabaka bwe yagamba mu ddoboozi ery’amaanyi ennyo, “okutuusa ng’abalamuzi bonna bakomyewo mu bifo byabwe ebituufu — bonna ,” bwe yaddiŋŋana n’okuggumiza ennyo, ng’atunuulira nnyo Alice ng’ayogera bw’atyo.

Alice yatunuulira ekibokisi ky’abalamuzi, n’alaba nti, mu bwangu bwe, yali atadde Enzige mu mutwe wansi, era akatono akaavu kaali kawuuba omukira gwako mu ngeri ey’ennaku, nga tesobola nnyo kutambula. Mu bbanga ttono yaddamu okugifulumya, n’agitereeza; “si nti kitegeeza bingi,” bwe yagamba mu mutima gwe; “Nsaana okulowooza nti kyandibadde kya mugaso nnyo mu kugezesebwa ekkubo erimu okulinnya ng’endala.”

Amangu ddala ng’abalamuzi bateredde katono okuva mu nsisi ey’okunyiiga, era nga n’ebipande byabwe n’ekkalaamu bizuuliddwa ne bibaddizibwa, ne batandika okukola n’obunyiikivu ennyo okuwandiika ebyafaayo by’akabenje kano, bonna okuggyako Enzige, eyalabika ng’ewanguddwa nnyo okukola ekintu kyonna okuggyako okutuula ng’akamwa kaayo kaggule, ng’etunudde waggulu mu kasolya ka kkooti.

“Kiki ky’omanyi ku bizinensi eno?” Kabaka bwe yagamba Alice.

Alice yagamba nti: “Tewali kintu kyonna.

“Tewali kintu kyonna? ” Kabaka bwe yagumiikiriza.

Alice yagamba nti: “Tewali kintu kyonna.”

“Ekyo kikulu nnyo,” Kabaka bwe yagamba ng’akyuka n’atunuulira abalamuzi. Kino baali baakatandika okuwandiika ku bipande byabwe, Olukalu Enjeru n’asalako nti: “ Un important, your Majesty means, of course,” bwe yagamba mu ddoboozi ery’ekitiibwa ennyo, naye ng’akuba ennyindo era ng’amukola amaaso ng’ayogera.

“ Un important, of course, I meant,” Kabaka bwe yagamba mu bwangu, n’agenda mu maaso n’okwebuuza mu ddoboozi erya wansi, .

“ekikulu —ekitali kikulu —ekitali kikulu —ekikulu —” ng’alinga agezaako ekigambo ki ekisinga okuwulikika.

Abamu ku balamuzi baawandiika nti “kikulu,” ate abalala “tekiri kikulu.” Kino Alice yali asobola okukiraba, anti yali kumpi nnyo okusobola okutunula waggulu w’ebipande byabwe; “naye si kikulu n’akatono,” bwe yalowooza mu mutima gwe.

Mu kaseera kano Kabaka eyali amaze ebbanga ng’awandiika mu katabo ke, n’akuba enduulu nti “Silence!” era n’asoma okuva mu kitabo kye, “Etteeka Amakumi Ana mu Bbiri. Abantu bonna obuwanvu obusukka mu mayiro emu okuva mu kkooti .”

Buli omu yatunuulira Alice.

Alice yagamba nti: “ Siri mayiro emu.

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Ggwe oli.”

“Kumpi mayiro bbiri obuwanvu,” Nnabagereka bwe yagattako.

“Kale, sijja kugenda, mu ngeri yonna,” Alice bwe yagamba: “ng’oggyeeko ekyo, eryo si tteeka lya bulijjo: waliyiiya kati.”

“Etteeka erisinga obukadde mu kitabo,” Kabaka bwe yagamba.

“Awo yandibadde Nnamba emu,” Alice bwe yagamba.

Kabaka yafuuka enzirugavu, n’aggalawo akatabo ke mu bwangu. “Mulowooze ku nsala yammwe,” bwe yagamba abalamuzi, mu ddoboozi erya wansi era nga likankana.

“Waliwo obujulizi obulala obugenda okujja, nkwegayiridde Ssaabasajja,” Enkazaluggya Enjeru bwe yagamba ng’abuuka mu bwangu bungi; “olupapula luno lwakalondebwa.”

“Kiki ekirimu?” Nnabagereka bwe yategeezezza.

“Nze sinnagiggulawo,” Enkazaluggya Enjeru bwe yagamba, “naye kirabika ebbaluwa, eyawandiikibwa omusibe eri —eri omuntu.”

“Kiteekwa okuba nga kyali bwe kityo,” Kabaka bwe yagamba, “okuggyako nga tewali muntu yenna kyawandiikibwa, ekintu ekitali kya bulijjo, omanyi.”

“Erambikibwa ani?” Omu ku balamuzi bwe yategeezezza.

“Tekiragiddwa n’akatono,” Enkazaluggya Enjeru bwe yagamba; “mu butuufu, tewali kiwandiikiddwa kungulu .” Yabikkula olupapula nga bw’ayogera, n’agattako nti “Si bbaluwa, oluvannyuma lw’ebyo byonna: nnyiriri za nnyiriri.”

“Zi mu mpandiika y’omusibe?” omulala ku balamuzi bwe yabuuzizza.

“Nedda, si bwe bali,” Enkazaluggya Enjeru bwe yagamba, “era ekyo kye kintu ekisinga okuwuniikiriza ku kyo.” (Abalamuzi bonna baali balabika nga basobeddwa.)

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Ateekwa okuba nga yakoppa omukono gw’omuntu omulala.” (Abalamuzi bonna bazzeemu okumasamasa.)

“Nkwegayiridde Ssaabasajja,” Omuknave bwe yagamba, “si nze nnakiwandiika, era tebasobola kukakasa nti nnakiwandiika: tewali linnya lissiddwaako mukono ku nkomerero.”

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Singa tewagiteeka mukono, ekyo kyongera okusajjula ensonga. Oteekwa okuba ng’otegeeza bubi, oba si ekyo wandissa omukono ku linnya lyo ng’omusajja omwesimbu.”

Waaliwo okukuba mu ngalo okwa bulijjo ku kino: kye kintu eky’amagezi ddala Kabaka kye yali asoose okwogera ku lunaku olwo.

“Ekyo kiraga omusango gwe,” Nnabagereka bwe yagamba.

“Tekikakasa kintu kyonna kya ngeri eyo!” Alice bwe yagamba. “Lwaki, tomanyi na kye boogera!”

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Bisoma.”

Enkazaluggya Enjeru yayambala endabirwamu ze. “Ntandikira wa, nkwegayiridde Ssaabasajja?” bwe yabuuzizza.

“Tandikira ku ntandikwa,” Kabaka bwe yagamba mu bukambwe, “ogende mu maaso okutuusa lw’onootuuka ku nkomerero: olwo olekere awo.”

Zino ze nnyiriri Enkazaluggya Enjeru ze yasoma:—

“Bang’amba nti obaddeko gy’ali, .
Era n’amyogerako nti:
Yampa empisa ennungi, .
Naye yagamba nti sisobola kuwuga.

Yabaweereza ekigambo nti nnali sigenze
(Tukimanyi nti kituufu):
Bw’aba alina okusindiikiriza ensonga, .
Kiki ekyandikutuuseeko?

Namuwa emu, ne bamuwa bbiri, .
Watuwa basatu oba okusingawo;
Bonna ne bakomawo okuva gy’ali gy’oli, .
Wadde nga zaali zange emabegako.

Singa nze oba ye yandibadde chance okubeera
Abeenyigira mu nsonga eno,
Akwesiga okubasumulula, .
Nga bwe twali ddala.

Endowooza yange yali nti wali obadde
(Nga tannafuna fit eno)
Ekizibu ekyajja wakati
Ye, ne ffe kennyini, ne kyo.

Tomutegeeza nti ye yasinga okubaagala,
Kubanga kino kiteekwa okuba nga bwe kiri
Ekyama, ekikuumibwa abalala bonna, .
Wakati wo nange.”

“Obwo bwe bujulizi obusinga obukulu bwe tuwulidde,” Kabaka bwe yagamba ng’asikasika emikono; “kale kati leka abalamuzi—”

“Bwe kiba nti waliwo omu ku bo asobola okukinnyonnyola,” Alice bwe yagamba, (yali akuze nnyo mu ddakiika ezisembyeyo nga tatya katono kumusalako), “Nja kumuwa sixpence. Sikkiririza nti waliwo atomu y’amakulu mu kyo.”

Abalamuzi bonna baawandiika ku slates zaabwe nti, “ Takkiriza nti waliwo atomu y’amakulu mu kyo,” naye tewali n’omu ku bo yagezaako kunnyonnyola lupapula luno.

“Bwe kiba nga tewali makulu mu kyo,” Kabaka bwe yagamba, “ekyo kitaasa ensi ya buzibu, omanyi, nga bwe tetwetaaga kugezaako kufunayo. Era naye simanyi,” bwe yagenda mu maaso, ng’ayanjuluza ennyiriri ku kugulu kwe, n’azitunuulira n’eriiso erimu; “Nlabika ndaba amakulu mu bo, oluvannyuma lw’ebyo byonna.“— yagamba nti sisobola kuwuga —” tosobola kuwuga, nedda?” yagasseeko ng’akyuka n’atunuulira Knave.

Omuknave yanyeenya omutwe mu nnaku. “Nze nfaanana?” bwe yagambye. (Kye mazima ddala teyakikola , nga kikoleddwa ddala mu bbaasa.)

“Kale, okutuuka wano,” Kabaka bwe yagamba, era n’agenda mu maaso n’okugugumbula ku nnyiriri mu mutima gwe: “’ Tukimanyi nti kituufu —’ eyo ye balamuzi, ddala—’ Namuwa emu, ne bamuwa bbiri —’ lwaki, ekyo kiteekwa okuba nga kye yakola ku tarts, omanyi—”

“Naye, kigenda mu maaso ' bonna bakomawo okuva gy’ali gy’oli ,’” Alice bwe yagamba.

“Lwaki, awo we bali!” Kabaka bwe yagamba mu buwanguzi ng’asonga ku tart ezaali ku mmeeza. “Tewali kiyinza kutegeerekeka bulungi okusinga ekyo .Awo nate —’ nga tannafuna fiiti eno —’ tofunangako fiiti, omwagalwa wange, ndowooza?” bwe yagamba Nnabagereka.

"Obutasoboka!" Nnabagereka bwe yagamba mu busungu, ng’asuula ekikondo kya yinki ku Nzige nga bw’ayogera. (Bill omuto ow’omukisa omubi yali alese okuwandiika ku slate ye n’olugalo lumu, nga bwe yasanga nga tekola kabonero konna; naye kati mu bwangu yaddamu okutandika, ng’akozesa yinki, eyali ekulukuta wansi mu maaso ge, kasita yali ewangaala.)

“Awo ebigambo tebikukwatagana ,” Kabaka bwe yagamba ng’atunudde mu kkooti ng’amwenya. Waaliwo akasiriikiriro akafudde.

“Kino kigambo!” Kabaka yayongerako mu ddoboozi ery’okunyiiga, era buli omu n’aseka nti, “Abalamuzi ka balowooze ku nsala yaabwe.” Kabaka bwe yagamba, omulundi nga ogw’amakumi abiri ku lunaku olwo.

“Nedda, nedda!” Nnabagereka bwe yategeezezza. “Okusooka kusalirwa kibonerezo —ensala oluvannyuma.”

“Ebintu n’obusiru!” Alice bwe yagamba mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka. “Ekirowoozo ky’okusooka okuba n’ekibonerezo!”

“Kwata olulimi lwo!” Nnabagereka bwe yagamba ng’afuuka kakobe.

“Sijja kukikola!” Alice bwe yagamba.

“Ogenda n’omutwe gwe!” Nnabagereka yaleekaana ku ntikko y’eddoboozi lye. Tewali yasenguka.

“Ani akufaako?” Alice bwe yagamba, (mu kiseera kino yali akuze okutuuka ku sayizi ye enzijuvu). “Ggwe toli kirala wabula ekipapula kya kaadi!”

Ku kino ekipapula kyonna kyasituka waggulu mu bbanga, ne kijja nga kibuuka wansi ku ye: yawa enduulu entono, ekitundu ky’okutya n’ekitundu eky’obusungu, n’agezaako okubakuba, n’asanga ng’agalamidde ku lubalama, ng’omutwe gwe guli mu kifuba kya mwannyina, eyali asika mpola ebikoola ebimu ebifu ebyali bifuukuuse wansi okuva ku miti ku maaso ge.

“Zuukuka, Alice omwagalwa!” mwannyina bwe yagamba nti; “Lwaki, nga otulo otuwanvuye!”

“Oo, nfunye ekirooto eky’okwewuunya bwe kiti!” Alice bwe yagamba, n’ategeeza mwannyina, nga bwe yali asobola okubijjukira, Adventures ze zonna ez’ekyewuunyo z’obadde waakasoma; era bwe yamala, mwannyina n’amunywegera, n’amugamba nti, “ Kyali kirooto kya kwewuunya, omwagalwa, mazima: naye kati dduka oyingire mu caayi wo; Bwatyo Alice n’asituka n’adduka, ng’alowooza ng’adduka, nga bw’ayinza, nga kirooto kya kitalo nnyo.

Naye mwannyina yatuula nga takyaliwo nga bw’amuleka, ng’agalamira omutwe gwe ku mukono gwe, ng’atunuulira enjuba ng’egwa, era ng’alowooza ku Alice omuto ne Adventures ze zonna ez’ekitalo, okutuusa naye lwe yatandika okuloota oluvannyuma lw’omulembe, era kino kye kyali ekirooto kye:—

Okusooka, yaloota Alice omuto yennyini, era nate emikono emitonotono ne gikwatiddwa ku kugulu kwe, era amaaso agayakaayakana ag’okwagala ennyo gaali gatunudde waggulu mu ge — yali asobola okuwulira amaloboozi gennyini ag’eddoboozi lye, n’alaba akawundo ako akatono ak’ekyewuunyo ak’omutwe gwe okukuuma enviiri ezitaayaaya ezaali zigenda okuyingira mu maaso ge bulijjo — era nga bwe yali awuliriza, oba ng’alinga awuliriza, ekifo kyonna ekimwetoolodde kyafuuka nga mulamu n’ebitonde ebyewuunyisa eby’ekirooto kya mwannyina omuto.

Omuddo omuwanvu gwawuuma ku bigere bye nga Enkazaluggya Enjeru eyanguwa okuyitawo — Ekibe ekyali kitidde n’amansira mu kidiba ekyali kiriraanyewo — yali awulira okuwuuma kw’ebikopo bya caayi nga March Hare ne mikwano gye bagabana emmere yaabwe etakoma, n’eddoboozi ery’amaanyi erya Nnabagereka nga lilagira abagenyi be ab’omukisa omubi okuttibwa —omulundi omulala omwana w’embizzi yali asesema okugulu kwa Duchess, ng’essowaani n’amasowaani bikuba okugyetooloola —nate okuleekaana kwa Gryphon, okuwuuma kw’ekkalaamu ya Lizard eya slate-pencil, n’okuziyira kw’embizzi za guinea-e binyigirizibwa, byajjula empewo, nga bitabuddwamu n’okusinda okw’ewala okw’enkima ey’ekika kya Mock Turtle ey’ennaku.

Bwatyo yatuula, ng’amaaso gazibye, era ekitundu ne yeekkiririza mu Wonderland, wadde nga yali akimanyi nti yalina wabula okuddamu okugaggulawo, era byonna byandikyuse ne bifuuka ebituufu ebizibu —omuddo gwandibadde guwuuma mu mpewo gwokka, n’ekidiba nga kiwuuma okutuuka ku kuwuubaala kw’emivule — ebikopo bya caayi ebiwuuma byandikyuse ne bifuuka ebide by’endiga ebiwuuma, n’okukaaba okw’amaanyi okwa Kkwiini ku ddoboozi ly’omusumba omulenzi — n’okusesema kw’omwana, okuleekaana kwa Gryphon, n’amaloboozi amalala gonna ag’ekyewuunyo, byandikyuse (yali akimanyi) ne bifuuka enduulu etabuddwatabuddwa ey’oluggya lw’ennimiro olwali lujjudde abantu —ate ng’okuwuuma kw’ente ewala kwanditwala ekifo ky’okusinda okw’amaanyi okwa Mock Turtle.

Ekisembayo, yeekuba ekifaananyi mu mutima gwe engeri mwannyina ono yennyini gye yandibadde, mu kiseera eky’oluvannyuma, ye kennyini omukazi omukulu; n’engeri gye yandikuumye, okuyita mu myaka gye gyonna egy’enkungu, omutima omungu era ogw’okwagala ogw’obuto bwe: n’engeri gye yandikuŋŋaanyizza ku baana be abalala abato, n’okufuula amaaso gaabwe okumasamasa n’okwagala ennyo n’olugero olw’ekyewuunyo bangi, mpozzi n’ekirooto ky’Ensi ey’Ebyewuunyo ey’edda: n’engeri gye yandiwuliddemu n’ennaku zaabwe zonna ennyangu, n’afuna essanyu mu ssanyu lyabwe lyonna eryangu, ng’ajjukira obulamu bwe obw’omwana omuto, n’ennaku ez’essanyu ez’omusana.
