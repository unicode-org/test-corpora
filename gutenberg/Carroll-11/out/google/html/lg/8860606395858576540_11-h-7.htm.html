ESSUULA VII.
Ekivvulu kya Caayi eky'eddalu

Waaliwo emmeeza eyateekebwa wansi w’omuti mu maaso g’ennyumba, era March Hare ne Hatter baali baginywa caayi: Dormouse yali etudde wakati waabwe, nga yeebase nnyo, ate abalala ababiri nga bagikozesa ng’omutto, nga bagiwummuzaako ebikoona, era nga boogera ku mutwe gwayo. “Tekinyuma nnyo eri Dormouse,” Alice bwe yalowooza; “yokka, nga bwe yeebase, ndowooza nti tekirina buzibu.”

Emmeeza yali nnene, naye abasatu bonna baali bakuŋŋaanye wamu ku nsonda emu: “Tewali kifo!Tewali kifo!” baakaaba nga balaba Alice ng’ajja. “Ekifo kinene !” Alice bwe yagamba mu busungu, n’atuula wansi mu ntebe ennene ey’omu ngalo ku nkomerero emu ey’emmeeza.

“Munywa omwenge,” March Hare bwe yagamba mu ddoboozi erizzaamu amaanyi.

Alice yatunula mu mmeeza yonna, naye nga tewali kintu kyonna ku yo okuggyako caayi. Yagamba nti: “Silaba wayini yonna.”

“Tewaliwo,” March Hare bwe yagamba.

“Awo tekyali kya buwangwa nnyo gy’oli okugiwaayo,” Alice bwe yagamba mu busungu.

“Tekyali kya buwangwa nnyo gy’oli okutuula wansi nga toyitiddwa,” March Hare bwe yagamba.

Alice yagamba nti: “Nnali simanyi nti ye mmeeza yo ; “kiteekeddwawo abantu bangi nnyo abasukka mu basatu.”

“Enviiri zo zaagala kusala,” Omukofiira bwe yagamba. Yali amaze ebbanga ng’atunuulira Alice ng’ayagala nnyo okumanya, era guno gwe gwali okwogera kwe okwasooka.

“Olina okuyiga obutayogera bigambo bya muntu ku bubwe,” Alice bwe yagamba mu bukambwe obumu; “kiba kya mpisa nnyo.”

Omukuumi w’enkoofiira yazibula amaaso ge nnyo bwe yawulira bino; naye kyokka kye yayogera kyali nti, “Lwaki enkovu eringa emmeeza ey’okuwandiika?”

“Jjangu, tujja kusanyuka kati!” Alice bwe yalowooza. Yagattako mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka nti: “Ndi musanyufu nti batandise okubuuza ebisoko. —Nzikiriza nti ekyo nsobola okukiteebereza.”

“Otegeeza nti olowooza osobola okuzuula eky’okuddamu mu kyo?” bwe yategeezezza March Hare.

Alice yagamba nti: “Bwe kityo ddala.”

“Awo wandibadde oyogera ky’otegeeza,” March Hare bwe yagenda mu maaso.

Alice yamuddamu mu bwangu nti: “Nze nkola; “waakiri —waakiri ntegeeza kye njogera —ekyo kintu kye kimu, omanyi.”

“Si kintu kye kimu katono!” Omukwasi w’enkoofiira bwe yagamba. “Oyinza okugamba nti ‘Ndaba bye ndya’ kye kimu ne ‘Ndya bye ndaba’!”

March Hare yagattako nti: “Oyinza okugamba nti ‘Njagala bye nfuna’ kintu kye kimu ne ‘Nfuna bye njagala’!”

Dormouse, eyalabika ng’ayogera mu tulo bwe yagattako nti: “Oyinza okugamba nti ‘Nfuuwa nga nneebase’ kintu kye kimu ne ‘Nneebaka nga nssa’!”

“ Kye kimu naawe,” Omukwasi w’enkoofiira bwe yagamba, era wano emboozi n’egwa, era ekibiina ne kituula nga kisirise okumala eddakiika emu, nga Alice alowooza ku byonna bye yali asobola okujjukira ku nkwaso n’emmeeza z’okuwandiika, ekintu ekyali si kinene.

Omukuumi w’enkoofiira ye yasooka okumenya akasiriikiriro. “Lu lunaku ki mu mwezi?” yagamba ng’akyuka n’atunuulira Alice: yali aggye essaawa ye mu nsawo ye, era ng’agitunuulira mu ngeri etaali nnyangu, ng’agikankanya buli luvannyuma lwa kaseera, era ng’agikwata ku kutu.

Alice yalowoozaako katono, n’oluvannyuma n’agamba nti “Ow’okuna.”

“Ennaku bbiri enkyamu!” Omukofiira bwe yasiiba. “Nkugambye butto tajja kukwatagana na mirimu!” yagasseeko ng’atunuulira March Hare n’obusungu.

March Hare yaddamu mu ngeri ey’obuwombeefu nti: “Yali butto asinga obulungi .”

“Yee, naye ebikuta ebimu nabyo biteekwa okuba nga byayingira,” Omukofiira bwe yeemulugunya: “tewandibadde okiteekamu n’akambe k’omugaati.”

March Hare yakwata essaawa n’agitunuulira mu ngeri ey’ennaku: olwo n’aginnyika mu kikopo kye ekya caayi, n’addamu okugitunuulira: naye nga tayinza kulowooza ku kintu kirungi ky’ayinza kwogera okusinga ekigambo kye ekyasooka nti, “Yali butto asinga , omanyi.”

Alice yali abadde atunudde waggulu w’ekibegabega kye ng’alina ky’ayagala okumanya. “Nga ssaawa ya nseko!” bwe yategeezezza. “Kyogera olunaku lw’omwezi, era tekitegeeza ssaawa mmeka!”

“Lwaki kisaanidde?” Omukofiira bwe yayomba. “Essaawa yo ekubuulira omwaka gwe guli?”

“Mazima ddala si bwe kiri,” Alice yaddamu mangu nnyo: “naye ekyo kiri bwe kityo kubanga kibeera omwaka gwe gumu okumala ebbanga eddene bwe lityo nga tuli wamu.”

“Ekyo kiri bwe kityo ne ku kyange ,” Omusajja Omukozi w’Enkoofiira bwe yagamba.

Alice yawulira nga asobeddwa mu ngeri ey’entiisa, ekigambo kya The Hatter kyalabika nga tekirina makulu gonna mu kyo, era naye mazima ddala kyali kya Lungereza. Yagamba mu mpisa nga bwe yali asobola nti: “Sikutegedde bulungi.”

“Dormouse ezzeemu okwebaka,” Omukofiira bwe yagamba, n’ayiwa aka caayi ayokya akatono ku nnyindo yaayo.

Dormouse yanyeenya omutwe nga talina bugumiikiriza, n’egamba, nga teyazibula maaso nti, “Mazima ddala, kye nnali hhenda okweyogerako.”

“Oteberezza ekisoko n’okutuusa kati?” omukuumi w’enkoofiira bwe yagamba ng’akyuka nate n’atunuulira Alice.

Alice n’addamu nti: “Nedda, nkivaako: “eky’okuddamu kiri ki?”

“Sirina n’ekirowoozo kyonna,” Omukofiira bwe yagamba.

“Nedda nze,” March Hare bwe yagamba.

Alice yasiiba ng’akooye. Yagamba nti: “Ndowooza oyinza okukola ekintu ekirungi ng’okozesa obudde, okusinga okubwonoona ng’obuuza ebisoko ebitaliimu by’oddamu.”

Omukwasi w’enkoofiira yagamba nti: “Singa omanyi Ekiseera nga nze, tewandiyogedde ku kyonoona .Ye . ”

Alice yagamba nti: “Simanyi ky’otegeeza.

“Mazima ddala ggwe tokikola!” omukuumi w’enkoofiira bwe yagamba ng’asuula omutwe gwe mu ngeri ey’okunyooma. “Ngumiikiriza okugamba nti toyogerangako wadde ne Time!”

Alice yamuddamu n’obwegendereza nti: “Oboolyawo si bwe kiri: “naye nkimanyi nti nnina okukuba obudde bwe njiga ennyimba.”

“Ah!, ekyo kye kibiviirako,” Omukwasi w’Enkoofiira bwe yagamba. "Tajja kugumiikiriza kukuba. Kati, singa obeera n'enkolagana ennungi yokka naye, yandikoze kumpi buli kintu kyonna ky'oyagala n'essaawa. Okugeza, ka tugambe nti zaali ssaawa mwenda ez'oku makya, obudde bwokka okutandika emisomo: wandibadde olina okuwuuba akabonero kwokka eri Obudde, era essaawa egenda mu kaseera katono! Ekitundu ekiyise, ekiseera ky'ekyeggulo!”

(“Njagala kyokka bwe kiba,” March Hare bwe yagamba mu mutima gwe mu ngeri ey’okuwuubaala.)

“Ekyo kyandibadde kya kitiibwa, mazima ddala,” Alice bwe yagamba ng’alowooza: “naye olwo —sisaanye kukiluma njala, omanyi.”

“Si mu kusooka, mpozzi,” Omukopi yagamba: “naye oyinza okugikuuma okutuuka ku kitundu n’ekitundu kasita oba ng’oyagala.”

“Eyo y’engeri gy’oddukanyaamu ?” Alice bwe yabuuzizza.

Omukofiira yanyeenya omutwe mu ngeri ey’okukungubaga. “Si nze!” bwe yaddamu. “Twayomba mu March w’omwaka oguwedde —nga tannagwa eddalu, omanyi —” (ng’asonga n’ekijiiko kye ekya caayi ku March Hare,) “—yali mu kivvulu ekinene ekyaweebwa Nnabagereka w’Emitima, era nnalina okuyimba

‘Twinkle, twinkle, akaana akatono!
Nga nneebuuza nti oli mu ki!’.

Oluyimba olumanyi mpozzi?”

Alice yagamba nti: “Nze mpulidde ekintu ekiringa ekyo.

“Kigenda mu maaso, omanyi,” Omukuumi w’enkoofiira bwe yayongera, “mu ngeri eno:—

‘Waggulu waggulu w’ensi obuuka, .
Nga tea-tray mu bbanga.
Okumasamasa, okumasamasa —’”

Wano Dormouse yeekankanya, n’etandika okuyimba mu tulo twayo “ Twinkle, twinkle, twinkle, twinkle —” era n’egenda mu maaso okumala ebbanga ddene ne balina okuginyiga okusobola okugiyimiriza.

“Kale, nnali sinnamaliriza lunyiriri olusooka,” Omukwasi w’enkoofiira bwe yagamba, “Nnabagereka bwe yabuuka n’akuba enduulu nti, ‘Atta obudde! Agenda n’omutwe gwe!’”

“Nga kika kya ntiisa nnyo!” Alice bwe yayomba.

“Era okuva olwo,” Omukwasi w’enkoofiira bwe yagenda mu maaso mu ddoboozi ery’okukungubaga, “tajja kukola kintu kye nsaba! Bulijjo kati ssaawa mukaaga.”

Ekirowoozo ekitangaavu kyajja mu mutwe gwa Alice. “Eyo y’ensonga lwaki ebintu bya caayi bingi ebiteekebwa ebweru wano?” bwe yabuuzizza.

“Yee, ekyo kye kiri,” Omukofiira bwe yagamba ng’asinda: “bulijjo kiseera kya caayi, era tetulina budde kunaaba bintu wakati w’ebiseera.”

“Awo osigala weetooloola, ndowooza?” Alice bwe yagamba.

Omukwasi w’enkoofiira yagamba nti: “Bwe kityo ddala: ng’ebintu biweddewo.”

“Naye kiki ekibaawo ng’ozzeemu okujja ku ntandikwa?” Alice yafuba okubuuza.

“Ka tugambe nti tukyusizza ensonga,” March Hare bwe yasalako ng’ewuubaala. “Kino nkikooye. Nze nkuba akalulu omuwala atunyumiza emboozi.”

“Ntya nti simanyi emu,” Alice bwe yagamba, ng’asinga okutya olw’ekiteeso ekyo.

“Awo Dormouse ajja kujja!” bombi bakaaba. “Zukuka, Dormouse!” Era ne baginyiga ku njuyi zombi omulundi gumu.

Dormouse yazibula amaaso mpola. Mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka era enafu yagamba nti: “Nnali sikyebaka,” n’agamba nti: “Nnawulira buli kigambo bannaffe kye mwayogeranga.”

“Tubuulire emboozi!” bwe yategeezezza March Hare.

“Yee, nsaba okole!” Alice bwe yeegayirira.

Omukwasi w’enkoofiira n’agattako nti: “Era beera mangu ku nsonga eyo, oba si ekyo ojja kuddamu okwebaka nga tekinnaggwa.”

“Edda waaliwo bannyinaffe abato basatu,” Dormouse bwe yatandika mu bwangu bungi; “era amannya gaabwe baali Elsie, Lacie, ne Tillie, era nga babeera wansi w’oluzzi —”

“Baali babeera ku ki?” Alice bwe yagamba, bulijjo eyafaayo ennyo ku bibuuzo ebikwata ku kulya n’okunywa.

“Baali babeera ku treacle,” Dormouse bwe yagamba, oluvannyuma lw’okulowooza eddakiika emu oba bbiri.

“Ekyo tebayinza kukikola, omanyi,” Alice bwe yagamba mpola; “bandibadde balwadde.”

“Bwe batyo bwe baali,” bw’atyo Omudormouse bwe yagamba; “ mulwadde nnyo .”

Alice yagezaako okwerowooza nti engeri ey’obulamu ey’ekitalo bwe yandibadde, naye kyamusobera nnyo, n’agenda mu maaso n’agamba nti: “Naye lwaki baali babeera wansi mu luzzi?”

“Twala caayi omulala,” March Hare bwe yagamba Alice, mu bujjuvu ennyo.

Alice yamuddamu mu ddoboozi ery’okunyiiga nti: “N’okutuusa kati sirina kye nfuna, n’olwekyo sisobola kutwala birala.”

“Otegeeza nti tosobola kutwala kitono ,” bwatyo Omukofiira bwe yagamba: “kyangu nnyo okutwala ebisingawo okusinga obutaba na kintu kyonna.”

Alice yagamba nti: “Tewali yakubuuza ndowooza yo .

“Ani akola ebigambo eby’obuntu kati?” omukofiira yabuuza n’obuwanguzi.

Alice yali tamanyi bulungi kya kwogera ku kino: bwe kityo yeeyamba okunywa caayi ne butto, n’oluvannyuma n’akyuka n’atunuulira Dormouse, n’addamu ekibuuzo kye. “Lwaki baali babeera wansi mu luzzi?”

Dormouse yaddamu okutwala eddakiika emu oba bbiri okukirowoozaako, n’oluvannyuma n’agamba nti, “Kyali treacle-well.”

“Tewali kintu ng’ekyo!” Alice yali atandise n’obusungu bungi, naye Omukuumi w’Enkoofiira ne March Hare ne bagenda “Sh! sh!” era Dormouse n’agamba mu ngeri ey’obusungu nti, “Bw’oba tosobola kuba wa civil, wandibadde omaliriza emboozi ku bubwo.”

“Nedda, nkwegayiridde genda mu maaso!” Alice yagamba mu bwetoowaze ennyo nti; "Sijja kuddamu kusalako.Ngumiikiriza okugamba nti wayinza okubaawo ."

“Omu, ddala!” Dormouse bwe yagamba mu busungu. Kyokka yakkiriza okugenda mu maaso. “Era bwe batyo bannyinaffe bano abasatu abato — baali bayiga okukuba ebifaananyi, omanyi —”

“Baakubye ki?” Alice bwe yagamba nga yeerabira nnyo ekisuubizo kye.

“Treacle,” Dormouse bwe yagamba, nga tafuddeeyo n’akatono mu kiseera kino.

“Njagala ekikopo ekiyonjo,” Omukopi bwe yasalako: “ffenna tusenguke ekifo kimu.”

Yagenda mu maaso nga bw’ayogera, era Dormouse n’amugoberera: March Hare yasenguka mu kifo kya Dormouse, era Alice okusinga nga tayagala n’atwala ekifo kya March Hare. Omukwasi w’enkoofiira ye yekka eyafuna enkizo yonna okuva mu nkyukakyuka eno: era Alice yali mu mbeera nnungi nnyo okusinga edda, anti March Hare yali yaakanyiiza ekibbo ky’amata mu ssowaani ye.

Alice teyayagala kuddamu kunyiiza Dormouse, kwe kutandika n’obwegendereza bungi: “Naye sitegeera.Treacle baagiggya wa?”

“Osobola okusena amazzi mu luzzi lw’amazzi,” bw’atyo Omukozi w’Enkoofiira bwe yagamba; “kale nsaanidde okulowooza nti oyinza okuggya treacle okuva mu treacle-well —eh, musiru?”

“Naye baali mu luzzi,” Alice bwe yagamba Dormouse, nga tasalawo kwetegereza kigambo kino ekisembayo.

“Mazima ddala baali,” Dormouse bwe yagamba; “—bulungi mu.”

Eky’okuddamu kino kyatabula nnyo Alice omwavu, n’aleka Dormouse okugenda mu maaso okumala ekiseera nga tagisalako.

“Baali bayiga okukuba ebifaananyi,” Dormouse bwe yagenda mu maaso, ng’ewuuma n’okusika amaaso, kubanga yali egenda yeebaka nnyo; “ne bakuba ebifaananyi eby’engeri zonna —buli kintu ekitandikira ku M—”

“Lwaki ng’olina M?” Alice bwe yagamba.

"Lwaaki nedda?" bwe yategeezezza March Hare.

Alice yasirika.

Dormouse yali ezibye amaaso mu kiseera kino, era yali egenda mu tulo; naye, bwe kyanywezebwa Omukofiira, kyazuukuka nate nga kikuba enduulu entono, ne kigenda mu maaso: “ —ekyo kitandikira ku M, gamba ng’emitego gy’ebibe, n’omwezi, n’okujjukira, n’obungi —omanyi ogamba nti ebintu “bingi nnyo” —wali olabye ekintu ng’ekyo ng’ekifaananyi ky’obungi?”

“Mazima ddala, kati ombuuza,” Alice bwe yagamba, ng’asobeddwa nnyo, “sirowooza nti—”

“Awo tolina kwogera,” Omukofiira bwe yagamba.

Ekitundu kino eky’obugwenyufu kyali kisinga Alice kye yali asobola okugumira: yasituka mu kwetamwa okungi, n’atambula; Dormouse yeebaka mangu, era tewali n’omu ku balala yakwata n’akatono ku kugenda kwe, wadde nga yatunula emabega omulundi gumu oba ebiri, ekitundu ng’asuubira nti bajja kumuyita: omulundi gwe yasembayo okubalaba, baali bagezaako okuteeka Dormouse mu kibbo kya caayi.

“Mu ngeri yonna sijja kuddamu kugendayo !” Alice bwe yagamba ng’alonda ekkubo lye mu nku. “Kino kye kivvulu kya caayi ekisinga obusiru kye nnabaddeko mu bulamu bwange bwonna!”

Bwe yali ayogedde bino, yakiraba ng’omu ku miti gulina oluggi oluyingira ddala. “Ekyo kyewuunyisa nnyo!” bwe yalowooza. "Naye buli kimu kya kwewuunya leero. Ndowooza nnyinza okuyingira omulundi gumu." Era mu yagenda.

Nate yeesanga mu kisenge ekiwanvu, era nga kumpi n’akameeza akatono ak’endabirwamu. “Kati, ku mulundi guno nja kuddukanya bulungi,” bwe yagamba mu mutima gwe, n’atandika ng’akwata akasumuluzo akatono aka zaabu, n’asumulula oluggi olwali luyingira mu lusuku. Awo n’agenda okukola ng’anyiga ffene (yali akuumye ekitundu kyayo mu nsawo ye) okutuusa lwe yatuuka ku ffuuti emu obuwanvu: olwo n’atambula wansi mu kkubo ettono: n’oluvannyuma —yesanga ku nkomerero mu lusuku olulungi, wakati mu bimuli ebimasamasa n’ensulo ennyogovu.
