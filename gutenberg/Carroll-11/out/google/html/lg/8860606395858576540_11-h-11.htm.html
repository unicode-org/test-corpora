ESSUULA XI.
Ani Yabba Tarts?

Kabaka ne Nnabagereka w’Emitima baali batudde ku ntebe yaabwe ey’obwakabaka nga batuuse, ng’ekibiina ekinene kikuŋŋaanye okubeetoolodde — obunyonyi obutono n’ensolo eza buli ngeri, awamu n’ekibinja kyonna ekya kaadi: Omukwana yali ayimiridde mu maaso gaabwe, ng’asibiddwa enjegere, ng’alina omuserikale ku buli ludda okumukuuma; era okumpi ne Kabaka waaliwo Olubuto oluzungu, nga luliko ekkondeere mu mukono gumu, n’omuzingo ogw’amaliba mu mulala. Wakati ddala mu kkooti mwalimu emmeeza, nga kuliko essowaani ennene eya tarts: zaali zirabika bulungi nnyo, ne kiba nti kyaleetera Alice enjala ennyo okuzitunuulira —“Njagala bamaze okuwozesebwa,” bwe yalowooza, “n’okukwata ebiwoomerera!” Naye kino kyalabika nga tewali mukisa gwonna, kwe kutandika okutunuulira buli kimu ekimukwatako, okuyita mu kiseera ekyo.

Alice yali tabangako mu kkooti ya bwenkanya, naye yali abisomako mu bitabo, era yasanyuka nnyo okukizuula nti yali amanyi erinnya lya kumpi buli kintu ekiri eyo. Yagamba mu mutima gwe nti: “Oyo ye mulamuzi, olw’okuba wiigi ye ennene.”

Omulamuzi, by the way, yali Kabaka; era nga bwe yayambala engule ye ku wiigi, (tunuulira ekifaananyi eky’omu maaso bw’oba oyagala okulaba engeri gye yakikolamu,) teyalabika nga yeeyagaza n’akatono, era mazima ddala kyali tekifuuka.

“Era ekyo kye kibokisi ky’abalamuzi,” Alice bwe yalowooza, “n’ebitonde ebyo ekkumi n’ebibiri,” (yalina okugamba nti “ebitonde,” olaba, kubanga ebimu ku byo byali bisolo, ate ebirala byali binyonyi,) “Nsuubira nti be balamuzi.” Yayogera ekigambo kino ekisembayo emirundi ebiri oba esatu mu yekka, ng’asinga kwenyumirizaamu: kubanga yalowooza, era mu butuufu era, nti abawala abato abatono ennyo ab’emyaka gye baali bamanyi amakulu gaakyo n’akatono. Kyokka, “abasajja abalamuzi” nabo bandikoze bulungi nnyo.

Abalamuzi ekkumi n’ababiri bonna baali bawandiika nga bakola nnyo ku slates. “Bakola ki?” Alice yayomba eri Gryphon. “Tebasobola kuba na kintu kyonna kye bateeka wansi n’okutuusa kati, ng’omusango tegunnatandika.”

“Bassa wansi amannya gaabwe,” Gryphon bwe yayomba ng’addamu, “olw’okutya nti balina okuzeerabira ng’omusango tegunnaggwa.”

“Ebintu eby’obusiru!” Alice yatandika mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka era ery’obusungu, naye n’ayimirira mu bwangu, kubanga Enkazaluggya Enjeru yaleekaana nti, “Sirika mu kkooti!” era Kabaka n’ayambala endabirwamu ze n’atunula n’okweraliikirira okwetooloola, okumanya ani ayogera.

Alice yali asobola okulaba, era nga bwe yali atunudde waggulu ku bibegabega byabwe, nti abalamuzi bonna baali bawandiika “ebintu eby’obusiru!” ku bipande byabwe, era yali asobola n’okutegeera nti omu ku bo yali tamanyi kuwandiika “musiru,” era nti yalina okusaba muliraanwa we amubuulire. “A nice muddle their slates’ll be in nga omusango tegunnaggwa!” Alice bwe yalowooza.

Omu ku balamuzi yalina ekkalaamu eyabadde ewuuma. Kino kyamazima, Alice yali tasobola kuyimirira, era yeetooloola kkooti n’atuuka emabega we, era mu bbanga ttono nnyo n’afuna omukisa okugitwala. Yakikola mangu nnyo ne kiba nti omulamuzi omuto omwavu (yali Bill, Lizard) teyasobola kutegeera n’akatono kiki ekyali kifuuse ku kyo; kale, oluvannyuma lw’okuyigga byonna, yalina okuwandiika n’olugalo lumu olunaku lwonna; era kino kyali kya mugaso mutono nnyo, kubanga tekyaleka kabonero konna ku slate.

“Herald, soma okulumiriza!” Kabaka bwe yategeezezza.

Ku kino Enkazaluggya Enjeru yafuuwa enduulu ssatu ku kkondeere, n’oluvannyuma n’asumulula omuzingo ogw’amaliba, n’asoma bweti:—

“Nnabagereka w’emitima, yakola tart ezimu, .
Byonna ku lunaku lw’omusana:
The Knave of Hearts, yabba tart ezo, .
Era n’abatwala ddala!”

Kabaka bwe yagamba abalamuzi nti: “Mulowooze ku nsala yammwe.”

“Tennaba, si n’okutuusa!” Omulalu yayanguwa okusalako. “Waliwo bingi ebigenda okujja nga ekyo tekinnatuuka!”

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Muyite omujulizi asooka; era Enkazaluggya Enjeru n’efuuwa amaloboozi asatu ku kkondeere, n’akoowoola nti, “Omujulirwa asooka!”

Omujulizi eyasooka yali Mukwasi w’Enkoofiira. Yayingira ng’akutte ekikopo kya caayi mu mukono gumu ate mu mulala ng’akutte omugaati ne butto. “Nsaba okusonyiyibwa, Ssaabasajja,” bwe yatandika, “olw’okuleeta bino: naye nga sinnamaliriza bulungi caayi wange we bansindikibwa.”

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Wandibadde omalirizza.” “Watandika ddi?”

Omukuumi w’enkoofiira yatunuulira March Hare, eyali amugoberedde okuyingira mu kkooti, ng’akwataganye ne Dormouse. “Olunaku lw’ekkumi n’ena mu mwezi gw’okusatu, ndowooza bwe kyali,” bwe yagambye.

“Eky’ekkumi n’ettaano,” bwatyo March Hare bwe yagamba.

“Ekkumi n’omukaaga,” Dormouse bwe yagattako.

“Ekyo mukiwandiike,” Kabaka bwe yagamba abalamuzi, era abalamuzi ne bawandiika ennaku zonna essatu n’obwagazi ku bipande byabwe, oluvannyuma ne bazigatta, ne bakendeeza ku ky’okuddamu okutuuka ku siringi ne ssente.

“Ggyako enkoofiira yo,” Kabaka bwe yagamba Omukofiira.

Omukuumi w’enkoofiira yagamba nti: “Si yange.”

“ Yabbibwa! ” Kabaka bwe yayomba ng’akyuka n’atunuulira abalamuzi, amangu ago ne bakola ekiwandiiko ekiraga ensonga eyo.

“Nzikuuma okutunda,” Omukuumi w’enkoofiira bwe yagasseeko ng’okunnyonnyola; "Nze sirina kyange. Ndi mukwasi wa nkofiira."

Wano Nnabagereka yayambala endabirwamu ze, n’atandika okutunuulira Omukofiira, eyafuuka omuddugavu n’akyukakyuka.

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Muwa obujulizi bwo; “era tobeera ku bunkenke, oba si ekyo nja kukutta mu kifo ekyo.”

Kino kyalabika nga tekizzaamu mujulizi amaanyi n’akatono: yasigala akyuka okuva ku kigere ekimu okudda ku kirala, ng’atunuulira Nnabagereka mu ngeri etaali nnyangu, era mu kusoberwa kwe yaluma ekitundu ekinene okuva mu kikopo kye ekya caayi mu kifo ky’omugaati ne butto.

Mu kaseera kano kennyini Alice yawulira ekintu eky’okwegomba ennyo, ekyamusobera ennyo okutuusa lwe yategeera kye kyali: yali atandise okuddamu okukula, era yasooka kulowooza nti ajja kusituka ave mu kkooti; naye ku birowoozo eby’okubiri yasalawo okusigala w’ali kasita wabaawo ekifo we yali.

“Njagala tosika bw’otyo,” Dormouse eyali atudde okumpi naye bwe yagamba. “Nze nsobola bulungi okussa.”

Alice yagamba mu ngeri ey’obuwombeefu ennyo nti: “Siyinza kukikola: “Nkula.”

“Tolina ddembe kukula wano ,” Dormouse bwe yagamba.

Alice yayongera obuvumu nti: “Toyogera bigambo bya busirusiru: “okimanyi nti naawe okula.”

“Yee, naye nkula ku sipiidi ensaamusaamu,” Dormouse bwe yagamba: “si mu ngeri eyo ey’okusaaga.” Era n’asituka mu ngeri ey’obusungu ennyo n’asomoka n’agenda ku ludda olulala olw’oluggya.

Ebbanga lino lyonna Nnabagereka yali talekera awo kutunula butunuzi eri Omukofiira, era, nga Dormouse esomoka kkooti, ​​n’agamba omu ku baserikale ba kkooti nti, “Mundeete olukalala lw’abayimbi abaali mu kivvulu ekyasembayo!” ku ekyo Omukofiira ow’ennaku yakankana nnyo, n’akankana engatto ze zombi.

Kabaka n’addiŋŋana mu busungu, “Wa obujulizi bwo, oba si ekyo nja kukutta, oba olina obunkenke oba nedda.”

“Ndi musajja mwavu, Ssaabasajja,” Omukwasi w’enkoofiira bwe yatandika, mu ddoboozi erikankana, “ — era nnali sinnatandika kunywa caayi wange —si kusukka wiiki emu oba bwe zityo —era kiki ng’omugaati ne butto bigonvu nnyo —n’okumasamasa kwa caayi —”

“Okumasamasa kwa kiki? ” Kabaka bwe yagamba.

“ Kyatandikira ku caayi,” Omukuumi w’Enkoofiira bwe yaddamu.

“Mazima ddala okumyansa kutandikira ku T!” Kabaka bwe yagamba mu ngeri ey’obukambwe. “Ontwala ku dunce? Genda mu maaso!”

“Ndi musajja mwavu,” Omukuumi w’enkoofiira bwe yagenda mu maaso, “era ebintu ebisinga byayakaayakana oluvannyuma lw’ekyo — March Hare yokka ye yagamba —”

“Nze saakikola!” envubu ya March Hare yasalako mu bwangu bungi.

“Ggwe wakikola!” Omukwasi w’enkoofiira bwe yagamba.

“Nze nkyegaana!” bwe yategeezezza March Hare.

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Akyegaana: ekitundu ekyo kiveeko.”

“Kale, mu ngeri yonna, Dormouse yagamba—” Omukwasi w’enkoofiira yagenda mu maaso, ng’atunula n’okweraliikirira okwetooloola okulaba oba naye yandikyegaana: naye Dormouse teyalina kye yeegaana, nga yeebase nnyo.

“Oluvannyuma lw’ekyo,” Omukuumi w’Enkoofiira bwe yayongera, “nasala omugaati ne butto omulala—”

“Naye Dormouse yagamba ki?” omu ku balamuzi bwe yabuuzizza.

“Ekyo sisobola kukijjukira,” Omukwasi w’Enkoofiira bwe yagamba.

Kabaka yagamba nti: “ Olina okujjukira, oba si ekyo nja kukutta.”

Omukopi ow’ennaku yasuula ekikopo kye ekya caayi n’omugaati ne butto, n’akka wansi ku kugulu okumu. “Ndi musajja mwavu, Ssaabasajja,” bwe yatandika.

“Oli mwogezi mwavu nnyo ,” Kabaka bwe yagamba.

Wano omu ku ba guinea-pigs yasanyuka, era amangu ago n’anyigirizibwa abaserikale ba kkooti. (Nga ekyo bwe kiri ekigambo ekikaluba, nja kumala kukunnyonnyola engeri gye kyakolebwamu. Baali balina ensawo ennene eya kanvaasi, eyasiba ku mumwa n’emiguwa: mu kino ne baseerera embizzi ya guinea-pig, omutwe gwe basooka, n’oluvannyuma ne bagutuulako.)

Alice yalowooza nti: “Ndi musanyufu nti ndabye ekyo nga kikoleddwa.” “Nze ntera okusoma mu mpapula z’amawulire, ku nkomerero y’emisango, “Waaliwo okugezaako okukuba mu ngalo, okwanyigirizibwa amangu ddala abaserikale ba kkooti,” era n’okutuusa kati sitegeerangako kye kyali kitegeeza.”

“Bw’oba ekyo kyokka ky’omanyi ku nsonga eno, oyinza okuyimirira wansi,” Kabaka bwe yayongeddeko.

“Siyinza kugenda wansi,” Omukopi bwe yagamba: “Ndi wansi, nga bwe kiri.”

Kabaka n’addamu nti: “Awo oyinza okutuula .”

Wano embizzi endala eya guinea-pig yasanyuka, era n’anyigirizibwa.

“Jjangu, ekyo kyamaliriza embizzi za guinea!” Alice bwe yalowooza. “Kati tujja kutambula obulungi.”

“Nsinga kumaliriza caayi wange,” Omukofiira bwe yagamba ng’atunuulira Nnabagereka mu bweraliikirivu, eyali asoma olukalala lw’abayimbi.

“Oyinza okugenda,” Kabaka bwe yagamba, Omukofiira n’ayanguwa n’ava mu lubiri, nga talinze wadde okwambala engatto.

“—era amala kuggyamu mutwe gwe wabweru,” Nnabagereka bwe yagattako omu ku baserikale: naye Omukuumi w’enkoofiira yali talabika ng’omuserikale tannatuuka ku mulyango.

“Muyite omujulizi addako!” Kabaka bwe yategeezezza.

Omujulizi eyaddako ye mufumbi wa Duchess. Yasitula ekibokisi ky’entungo mu ngalo, era Alice n’ateebereza ani, ne bwe yali tannayingira mu kkooti, engeri abantu abaali okumpi n’oluggi gye baatandika okusesema omulundi gumu.

Kabaka yagamba nti: “Muwa obujulizi bwo.”

Omufumbi yagamba nti: “Shan’t.”

Kabaka yatunuulira n’obweraliikirivu mu Kalulu Enjeru, eyagamba mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka nti, “Ssaabasajja ateekwa okubuuza omujulizi ono ebibuuzo ebisalasala.”

“Kale, bwe kiba nga nnina, nteekwa,” Kabaka bwe yagamba, ng’alina empewo ey’ennaku, era, oluvannyuma lw’okuzinga emikono gye n’okufukamira omufumbi okutuusa amaaso ge lwe gaatera okuva mu maaso, n’agamba mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka nti, “Tarts zikolebwa mu ki?”

“Entungo, okusinga,” omufumbi bwe yagamba.

“Treacle,” eddoboozi ery’otulo bwe lyagamba emabega we.

“Collar that Dormouse,” Nnabagereka bwe yaleekaana. “Muteme omutwe gwa Dormouse oyo! Mukyuse Dormouse oyo mu kkooti! Munyigirize! Munyige! Off with his whiskers!”

Okumala eddakiika ezimu kkooti yonna yali etabuddwatabuddwa, n’efuna Dormouse okufuluma, era, we baaddamu okusenga, omufumbi yali abuze.

"Tokifaako!" Kabaka bwe yagamba ng’alina empewo ey’obuweerero obw’amaanyi. “Muyite omujulizi addako.” Era n’agattako mu ddoboozi ery’omwanguka eri Nnabagereka nti, “Ddala, omwagalwa wange, olina okubuuza omujulizi addako ebibuuzo ebisalasala.Kinnuma nnyo ekyenyi!”

Alice yatunuulira Enkazaluggya Enjeru nga bw’akuba enduulu ku lukalala, ng’awulira ng’ayagala nnyo okulaba omujulizi addako bw’anaaba, “—kubanga tebannafuna bujulizi bungi ,” bwe yagamba mu mutima gwe. Teebereza okwewuunya kwe, Enkazaluggya Enjeru bwe yasoma, waggulu ku ddoboozi lye ettono eriwuuma, erinnya “Alice!”
