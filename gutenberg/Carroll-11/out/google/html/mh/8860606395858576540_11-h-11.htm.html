JAPTER XI.
Wōn eo ear bōk tart ko?

Kiin im Kwin in Būruwōd raar jijet ioon tūroon eo aer ke raar itok, ippān juon jarlepju eļap raar kobatok iturin er—aolep kain bao ko reddik im mennin mour ko, barāinwōt aolepān pack in kaat ko: Knave eo eaar jutak im̧aan mejāer, ilo chain ko, ippān juon ritariņae ilo kajjojo turear n̄an kōjparok e; im iturin Kiin̄ eo eaar wōr Rabbit eo Em̧ōrā, ippān juon jilel ilo juon pein, im juon bok in kilin mennin mour ilo pein eo juon. Ilo lukwon jikin ekajet eo ekar wor juon tebōl, ippāñ juon dish in tart ko ion: rekar lukkun emman, bwe ekar kōmman an Alice lukkun kwuli in kalimjōk ir—“Ikõnan bwe ren kadedelok ekajet eo,” ekar lemnok, “im lelok mōñā ko!” Ak ej einwõt ejelok an maroñ in walok men in, innem ear jino lale aolep men ko kin e, ñan jolok ien eo.

Alice ear jab bed ilo juon jikin ekajet mokta, ak ear riit kake ir ilo bok ko, im ear lukkun mõnõnõ in lo bwe ear jelã etan enañin aolep men ko ie. “En̄in ej riekajet eo,” liin eaar ba n̄an e make, “kōnke wig eo an em̧m̧an.”

Ri ekajet eo, ilo ia in, ekar Kiin eo; im ke ekar kōņak crown eo an ioon wig eo, (lale frontispiece eo elañe kwoj kōnaan lale ewi wawein an kar kōmmane,) ekar jab lukkun emman, im ekar jab lukkun emman.

“Im ej jury-box eo,” Alice ear lemnok, “im joñoul ruo menin mour ko,” (ekar aikuj in ba “menin mour ko,” kwoj loe, kinke jet iaer rar menin mour, im jet rar bao ko,) “Ij lemnok rej jury ro.” Ear ba nan in eliktata ruo ak jilu alen ñan e make, im ear lukkun utiej buruen kake: bwe ear lemnok, im ejimwe bareinwõt, bwe jejjo wõt leddik ro reddik ilo yio eo an rar jelã melelein. Ak, “jury-men” renaj kar kōmmane einwot an emman.

Ri ekajet ro joñoul ruo rar aolep jeje ilo slate ko. Ta eo rej kōmmane? Alice ear konono ñan Gryphon eo. “Ejjab maroñ in wor men ko ren likiti kiõ, mokta jen an jino ekajet eo.”

“Rej likit etaer,” Gryphon eo ekar uwaak, “kin mijak bwe renaj meloklok ir mokta jen an jemlok ien ekajet eo.”

“Men ko rebwebwe!” Alice ear jino ilo juon ainikien elap, im illu, ak ear bōjrak ilo mõkaj, bwe White Rabbit eo ear lamõj, “Ejjelok ainikien ilo jikin ekajet eo!” im Kiiñ eo ear kōņak nuknuk ko an im kalimjōk ilo an inepata, ñan an jelā wōn eo ej kōnono.

Alice ear maroñ loe, im bareinwõt elañe ear kalimjōk ion peir, bwe aolep ri ekajet ro rar jeje “men ko rebwebwe!” ilo slate ko aer, im ear maroñ in kile bwe juon iaer ear jab jelā kilen jeje “stupid,” im bwe ear aikuj in kajjitōk ippān eo m̧ōttan bwe en jiron̄ e. “Juon jorrāān emman slate ko aer renaj bed ie mokta jen an jemlok ekajet eo!” Alice ear lemnok.

Juon ian ri ekajet ro ekar wor juon an pencil eo ekar mōñā. Alice ear jab maroñ in jutak, im ear etal iturin jikin ekajet eo im bed ilikin, im ejjab to ear lo juon ien emman ñan an bōke. Ear kōmmane ilo mõkaj bwe ri ekajet eo edik (ekar Bill, Lizard eo) ear jab maroñ in melele kin ta eo ear walok; innem, elikin an kar kappok e, ear aikuj in jeje kin juon wōt pein ñan aolepen raan eo; im men in ear dik tokjen, kinke ear jab likit kakōlkōl ion slate eo.

“Herald, riiti naan in kinaak eo!” Kiiñ eo ekar ba.

Ilo ien in White Rabbit eo ear kōjan̄jan̄ jilu ainikien jilel eo, innām kar kōpeļļo̧k bok in jeje eo, im riiti āinwōt in:—

“Kwin in Būruwōd, ear kōmman jet tart ko,
Aolep ilo juon raan in summer:
Knave of Hearts, ear bōk tart kein,
Im ear bōk ir ettolok!”

“Lemnok kin am ekajet,” King eo ear ba ñan ri ekajet ro.

“Ejjab kiõ, ejjab kiõ!” Rabbit eo ear mōkaj an bōjrak. “Ewōr juon men eļap ej itok m̧okta jān menin!”

“Kūrtok ri-kam̧ool eo m̧oktata,” Kiin̄ eo eaar ba; im White Rabbit eo ear kōjan̄jan̄ jilu alen ilo jilel eo, im kar kūr, “Rikam̧ool eo m̧oktata!”

Ri kennan eo moktata kar Rikōmman nuknuk eo. Ear deļo̧n̄tok kōn juon kap in tii ilo juon pein im juon m̧ōttan pilawā-im-bōta ilo pein eo juon. “Ij kajjitōk jeorlok bwid, am Irooj,” ekar jino, “kin am kar bōktok men kein: ak ikar jab lukkun kadedelok aō idaak tii ke rar jilkintok io.”

“Kwoj aikuj kar kadedelok,” Kiin̄ eo eaar ba. “N̄āāt eo kwaar jinoe?”

Rikōmman nuknuk eo ear kalimjōk March Hare eo, eo ear lor e ilo jikin ekajet eo, pein ippān Dormouse eo. “Jonoul emān in Māe, ij lemnok ekar,” ekar ba.

“Kein kajoñoul lalem,” March Hare eo ear ba.

“Kein ka joñoul lalem,” Dormouse eo ear kobaiktok.

“Je men in,” Kiin eo ear ba ñan ri ekajet ro, im ri ekajet ro rar lukkun kijoñ in jeje aolep jilu raan ko ilo slate ko air, innem rar kobaiki, im kadiklok uak eo ñan shillings im pence.

“Kwōn jolok hat eo am,” Kiin eo ear ba ñan Ri kōmman nuknuk eo.

“Ejjab aō,” Rikōmman Hat eo eaar ba.

“ Ekar bōk! ” Kiin eo ekar lamõj, im oktaklok ñan ri ekajet ro, ro rar mōkaj air kōmmane juon melele kin mol eo.

“Ij kejbãrok ir ñan wiakaki,” ri wia eo ear kobaiktok einwõt juõn kamelele; “Ejjelok aō make. Ij juon ri kōmman hat.”

Ilo ijin Kwin eo ear kōņak spectacles eo an, im jino kalimjōk Rikōmman nuknuk eo, eo ear oktak im mōrā.

“Kwōn letok am kein kamool,” Kiin̄ eo eaar ba; “im jab inebata, ak inaj kōmman bwe ren mõn yuk ilo ien eo wot.”

Men in ear jab kōketak ri kennan eo: ear etal wōt jen juon neen ñan eo juon, kalimjōk Queen eo ilo an jab inebata, im ilo an inebata ear mwij juon mōttan elap jen teacup eo an ijellokin bread-and-butter eo.

Ilo ien in wōt Alice ear eñjake juon men eo elap an kabwilõñlõñ, eo ear kabwilõñ e mae ien eo ear kile ta eo: ear jino an laplok bar juon alen, im ear lemnok ilo jinoin enaj kar jerkak im etal jen jikin ekajet eo; ak ilo an kar lemnok kake ear pepe bwe en bed wōt ijo ear bed ie joñan wōt an wor jikin ñan e.

“Ikõnan bwe kwon jab mōkaj,” Dormouse eo ear ba, eo ear jijet iturin. “Ejjab maroñ in menono.”

“Ijjab maroñ in bōbrae,” Alice eaar ba ilo ettā: “Ij eddeklok.”

“Ejjelok am maroñ in eddek ijin ,” Dormouse eo ear ba.

“Jab konono men ko rejjab mol,” Alice ear ba ilo an peran: “kwojelā ke kwoj eddeklok bareinwõt.”

“Aet, ak ij eddeklok ilo juon joñan eo emman,” Dormouse eo ear ba: “jab ilo wewein eo ebwid.” Im ear jutak ilo an lukkun illu im etal ñan bar juon turear in jikin ekajet eo.

Ilo ien in Queen eo ear jab bōjrak an kalimjōk Hatter eo, im, ke Dormouse eo ear deblọk jikin ekajet eo, ear ba ñan juon iaan opiija ro ilo jikin ekajet eo, “Bōktok laajrak in rikōjan̄jan̄ ro ilo kōjan̄jan̄ eo āliktata!” ilo eo Hatter eo enana ear lukkun mōk, bwe ear jolok juuj ko an ruo.

“Letok am kein kamool,” Kiin eo ear bar ba ilo an illu, “ak inaj kōmman bwe ren mõn yuk, meñe kwoj inebata ak jaab.”

“Ij juon armij ejeramel, am Iroij,” Rikōmman nuknuk eo ear jino, ilo juon ainikien ej mōkaj, “—im ikar jab jino idaak tii eo aõ—ejjab laplok jen juon wiik ak āindein—im ta kin pilawā-im-butter eo ej lukkun mōrā—im mōrā in tii eo—”

“Meram in ta eo? ” Kiin̄ eo eaar ba.

“Ekar jino jen tii eo,” Rikōmman nuknuk eo ekar uwaak.

“Emool bwe twinkling ej jino kin juon T!” Kiin eo ekar ba ilo an kajoor. “Kwoj ke bōk io einwot juon ri bwebwe? Wōnm̧aanlok wōt!”

“Ij juon armij ejeramel,” Hatter eo ear wōnmanlok, “im elaptata men ko rar walok elikin men in—March Hare eo wōt ear ba—”

“Ikar jab!” March Hare eo ear bōjrak ilo an mōkaj.

“Kwar kōmmane!” rikōmman nuknuk eo ear ba.

“Ij kariab!” March Hare eo ear ba.

“Ej kariab,” Kiin̄ eo eaar ba: “likūt m̧ōttan eo.”

“Em̧m̧an, ilo jabdewōt jekjek, Dormouse eo eaar ba—” Hatter eo eaar wōnm̧aanļo̧k wōt, ej kalimjōkļo̧k ilo an inepata n̄an lale eļan̄n̄e enaaj kar kariab barāinwōt: ak Dormouse eo eaar jab kariab jabdewōt, kōnke eaar lukkuun kiki.

“Elikin men in,” ri wia eo ear wōnmanlok, “Iar mōt jet pilawā-im-bōta—”

“Ak ta eo Dormouse eo ear ba?” juon ian ri ekajet ro ear kajitok.

“Men eo ijjab maroñ kememej,” rikōmman nuknuk eo ear ba.

“Kwoj aikuj kememej,” Kiin̄ eo eaar ba, “ak inaaj kōm̧m̧an bwe ren m̧an eok.”

Hatter eo ebin ear jolok teacup eo an im bread-and-butter eo an, im buñpedo ion juon neen. “Ij juon armij ejeramel, am Iroij,” ekar jino.

“Kwoj juon ri konono eo elap an nana,” Kiin eo ear ba.

Ilo ijin juon ian guinea-pig ko ear lañliñ, im kar mōkaj an kar bōjrak jen ri utiej ro an jikin ekajet eo. (Kōnke ej juon naan ekabūrom̧ōjm̧ōj, inaaj baj kōmeļeļeik wōt n̄an eok ekōjkan an kar kōm̧m̧ane. Ekar wōr juon aer bag in canvas eļap, eo kar lukwōje ilo lo̧n̄iin kōn to ko: ilo menin raar likūt guinea-pig eo, bōran m̧okta, innām jijet ioon.)

“Ij mõnõnõ bwe iar loe an kar kõmmane,” Alice ear lemnok. “Ikar lukkun riit ilo nuujpeba ko, ilo jemlokin ekajet ko, “Ekar wor jet armij ro rar kajeoñ in kōjan̄jan̄, eo rar mōkaj an bōjrak jen ri utiej ro an jikin ekajet eo,” im ikar jab melele ta melelein mae ien in.”

“Eļan̄n̄e ej men eo wōt kwoj jeļā kake, kwomaron̄ jutak,” Kiin̄ eo eaar wōnm̧aanļo̧k wōt.

“Ijjab maroñ in etal lallok,” Rikōmman nuknuk eo ear ba: “Ij bed ion lal, einwot an bed.”

“Innem kwomaroñ jijet ,” Kiiñ eo ear uwaak.

Ilo ijin guinea-pig eo juon ear lañliñ, im kar bōbrae.

“Itok, emoj an jemlok guinea-pig ko!” Alice ear lemnok. “Kiō jenaaj emmanlok.”

“Em̧m̧anļo̧k aō kadedeļo̧k tii eo aō,” Hatter eo eaar ba, kōn juon kalimjōk in inepata n̄an Kwin eo, eo eaar riiti laajrak in rikōjan̄jan̄ ro.

“Kwomaroñ etal,” Kiin eo ear ba, im Rikōmman nuknuk eo ear mōkaj an etal jen jikin ekajet eo, ilo an jab kõttar ñan an kōņak juuj ko an.

“—im jolok bōran jen ilikin,” Kwin eo ear kobaiktok ñan juon ian opiija ro: ak Hatter eo ear jab loe mokta jen an opiija eo maroñ in tōpar kejem eo.

“Kūr ri kennan eo tok juon!” Kiiñ eo ekar ba.

Ri kennan eo tok juon ej ri kōmat eo an Duchess eo. Ear jibwe pepper-box eo ilo pein, im Alice ear jelā wōn eo, mokta jen an kar deloñ ilo jikin ekajet eo, ilo an armij ro iturin kejem eo kar jino mōñā ilo juon wōt ien.

“Letok am kein kamool,” Kiin eo ear ba.

“Jab,” ri kōmat eo eaar ba.

Kiin eo ear kalimjōk White Rabbit eo ilo an inebata, eo ear ba ilo juon ainikien edik, “Aṃ Irooj ej aikuj in kajjitōk ippān ri-kam̧ool in .”

“Em̧m̧an, eļan̄n̄e ij aikuj, ij aikuj,” Kiin̄ eo eaar ba, kōn juon kōto in būrom̧ōj, im, ālkin an kar kōpeļļo̧k pein im m̧ōjņo̧ n̄an rikōmat eo m̧ae iien mejān eaar epaak an jako, eaar ba ilo juon ainikien em̧wilaļ, “Ta ko rej kōm̧m̧an jān tart ko?”

“Pepper, elaptata,” ri kōmat eo eaar ba.

“Treacle,” juon ainikien kiki ekar ba itulikin.

“Collar Dormouse eo,” Queen eo ear lamõj. “Kōtlok bōran Dormouse eo! Kōjepel Dormouse eo jen jikin ekajet! Kōm̧akūt e! Pinch e! Kōm̧akūt bōran!”

Iumin jet minit ko aolepen jikin ekajet eo ear inebata, kōmman bwe Dormouse eo en oktak, im, ilo ien eo rar bar jijet, ri kōmat eo ear jako.

"Einwōt juon!" Kiin eo ekar ba, ippāñ juon mejatoto in kaenōmōn elap. “Kūr ri kennan eo tok juon.” Im ear kobaiktok ilo juon ainikien edik ñan Kwin eo, “Emool, jitōnbōro, kwoj aikuj in etale ri kennan eo tok juon. Ej lukkun kōmman an metak bōra!”

Alice ear kalimjōk White Rabbit eo ke ej etale laajrak eo, im ear lukkun kōnan lale ta eo enaj kar ri kennan eo tok juon, “—bwe ejelok air kein kamool ko,” ear ba ñan e make. Baj lemnok kin an bwilōñ, ke White Rabbit eo ear riiti, ilo ainikien edik eo an, etan “Alice!”
