JAPTER VIII.
Jikin ikkure eo an Kwin eo

Juon wijki in rose elap ej jutak iturin kōjām in jikin kallib eo: rose ko rej eddek ion rar mouj, ak ekar wor jilu ri kallib ro ie, im rar lukkun peint ir bwe ren būrōrō. Alice ear lemnok bwe men in ej juon men eo elap an kabwilōñlōñ, im ear epaaklok ñan an lale ir, im ke ear itok ñan ir ear roñ juon iaer ej ba, “Lale kiō, Lalem! Jab etal im kōtlok peint ioō einwot in!”

“Ikar jab maroñ in bōbrae,” Five ekar ba, ilo juon ainikien ejjab emman; “Jiljilimjuon ear ettōr elbow eo aō.”

Ke Seven ear reilōñ im ba, “Emool, Five! Iien otemjej likit bōd eo ion ro jet!”

“ Emmanlok am jab konono!” Lalem eaar ba. “Ikar roñ an Queen eo ba bwe inne wōt kwoj tellokin bwe ren bukwe bōram!”

“Ta ñan?” ekar ba eo ekar konono mokta.

“Ejjab am jerbal, ruo!” Jiljilimjuon eaar ba.

“Aet, ej an jerbal!” Five ekar ba, “im inaj jiron e—ekar kin an kar bōktok tulip-roots ñan ri kōmat eo ijellokin onion ko.”

Jiljilimjuon ear jolok brush eo an, im ear jino “Emmon, jen aolep men ko rejjab jimwe—” ke mejen ear lo Alice, ke ear jutak im kalimjōk er, im ear lale e make ilo jidimkij: ro jet rar bar kalimjōk, im aolep rar bukwelōlō.

“Kwomaroñ ke jiron io,” Alice ear ba, jidik an mijak, “etke kwoj peint rose kein?”

Lalem im Jiljilimjuon rar jab ba jabdrewot, ak rar kalimjōk Ruo. Ruo rar jino ilo juon ainikien edik, "Etke mol eo ej, kwoj loe, Miss, menin ijin ej aikuj kar juon wijki in rose emera , im jaar likit juon eo mouj ilo bōd; im elañe Queen eo enaj kar loe, jej aikuj in kar bukwe bōram, kwojelā. Innem kwoj lo, Miss, jej kōmmane joñan wōt ad maroñ, mokta jen an itok"—Lalem, juon eo ear kalimjōk menin iturin jikin kallib eo, kar kir “Kwin eo! Kwin eo!” im ri kallib ro jilu rar mōkaj aer buñ ion turin mejeir. Ekar wor juon ainikien elōn ne ko, im Alice ekar kalimjōk turin, ekōnaan lo Queen eo.

Mokta rar itok joñoul ri tarinae ro rej bōk kein joñjoñ ko; men kein rar āinwōt jilu ri kallib ro, rekar rup im rup, ippāñ peir im neir ilo kona ko: tokelik joñoul ri utiej ro; men kein rar kōmman aer nuknuk kin diamond, im rar etetal ruo im ruo, einwot an ri tarinae ro kōmmane. Elikin men kein rar itok ajiri ro nejin king; ekar wor joñoul iaer, im ļaddik ro reddik rar itok im ettōr im ettōr ippān doon ilo pein doon, ilo m̧ōttan ko: raar aolep kōm̧m̧an kōn bōro. Tokālik rar itok rilotok ro, elaptata King im Queen ro, im ibwiljier Alice ear kile White Rabbit eo: ear konono ilo juon wāween eo emõkaj im inebata, ettõñdikdik kin men otemjej kar ba, im ear etal ilo an jab loe. Innem ekar lor Knave in Hearts, ej bōk crown eo an King eo ion juon cushion in velvet emera; im, eliktata ilo ial in elap, ear itok KIN IM KIN IN BÕRO.

Alice ear lukkun pere elañe ejjab aikuj in babu ion turin mejen einwot ri kallib ro jilu, ak ear jab maroñ kememej an kar roñ kin kain kakien rot in ilo ien kwelok ko; “im bar juon, ta tokjen juon ial,” ear lemnok, “elañe armij ro renaj babu ion turin mejeir, bwe ren jab maroñ in loe?” Innem ear jutak wōt ijo ear bed ie, im kõttar.

Ke armij ro rar itok ñan Alice, rar aolep bōjrak im kalimjōk e, innem Queen eo ear ba ilo an kajoor “Wōn in?” Ear ba ñan Knave in Būruwōd, eo ear baj bukwelōlō wōt im ettōn̄ dikdik ilo an uwaak.

"Bwebwe!" Kwin eo ear ba, im mōkaj an mōkaj an jab kijenmij; im, oktakļo̧k n̄an Alice, eaar wōnm̧aanļo̧k wōt, “Ta etam̧, ajri?”

“Eta ej Alice, innem jouj im jouj im Iroij,” Alice ear ba ilo jouj; ak ear kobaiktok, ñan e make, “Etke, rej baj juon pack in kaat ko, elikin men otemjej. Ijjab aikuj in mijak ir!”

“Im wōn rein? ” Kwin eo eaar ba, im jitōn̄ļo̧k n̄an ri-kallib ro jilu me raar babu iturin wōjke rose eo; bwe, kwoj loe, ke rej babu ion turin mejeir, im pattern eo ion likiir ej einwot ro jet ilo kumi eo, ear jab maroñ in jella elañe rej ri kallib ro, ak ri tarinae ro, ak ri utiej ro, ak jilu ian ajiri ro nejin make.

“Ekōjkan aō aikuj in jelā?” Alice eaar ba, bwilōñ kin an make peran. Ejjab aō jerbal.

Kwin eo ear oktak im būrōrō kin an illu, im, elikin an kar kalimjōk e ilo jidik ien einwot juon kidu, ear lamõj “Kōtlok bōran! Bōjrak—”

“Ejjelok tokjen!” Alice ekar ba, elap an ļap ainikien im ekar pepe, im Kwin eo ekar binej jenkwan.

Kiiñ eo ear likit pein ion pein, im ilo an mijak ear ba “Lemnok, jitōnbōro: ej juon wōt ajiri!”

Kwin eo ear oktak jen e ilo an illu, im ba ñan Knave eo “Kwōn ukōt er!”

Knave eo ear kōmmane, ilo lukkun lale, kin juon neen.

"Jutak!" Kwin eo eaar ba, ilo juon ainikien ekajoor, im ri kallib ro jilu raar m̧ōkaj aer jutak, im jino bukwelōlō n̄an Kiin eo, Kwin eo, ajri ro nejin kiin̄ eo, im aolep armej ro jet.

“Kwōn jolok men in!” Kwin eo ekar lamij. “Kwoj kōmman ao mōk.” Innem, oktaklok ñan wijki rose eo, lio ear wōnmanlok, “Ta eo kwar kōmmane ijin?”

“En kamõnõnõik am Iroij,” Ruo ear ba, ilo juõn ainikien ettã buru, im bukwelõlõ ion juõn neen ke ej konono, “jekar kajeoñ—”

" Ij loe!" Kwin eo ekar ba, eo ekar etale rose ko. “Kōtlok ippāñ bōraer!” im ial in armij ro rar wōnmanlok, jilu ian ri tarinae ro rar bed wot ñan mõn ri kallib ro, ro rar ettõr ñan Alice ñan kejbãrok ir.

“Ejjab naaj m̧wijm̧wij bōram̧!” Alice ear ba, im ear likiti ilo juon pot in wōjke elap eo ear jutak iturin. Ri tarinae ro jilu rar etetal iumwin juon ak ruo minit, kappok ir, innem rar etetal ilo jidimkij ālkin ro jet.

“Ej ke bōjrak bōraer?” Kwin eo ekar lamij.

“Bōrair rej jako, elañe ej buñburuen Aṃ Irooj!” ri tarinae ro rar lamij ilo uwaak.

“Emool!” Kwin eo ekar lamij. “Kwomaroñ ke ikkure croquet?”

Ri tarinae ro rar jab konono, im kalimjōk Alice, kinke kajitok eo ear alikar an kar kōmmane ñan e.

"Aet!" Alice ekar lamij.

“Itok, innem!” Kwin eo ekar lamij, im Alice ekar kobatok ilo ial eo, im ekar lukkun lemnok kin ta eo enaj kar walok tokelik.

“Ej—ej juon raan eo elap an emman!” ekar ba juon ainikien ebin iturin. Ear etetal iturin White Rabbit eo, eo ear kalimjōk turin mejān ilo an inepata.

“Elap,” Alice ekar ba: “—ia eo Duchess ej bed ie?”

“Kōjekdọọn! Kōjekdọọn!” Rabbit eo ekar ba ilo juon ainikien edik im mōkaj. Ear kalimjōk ilo an inebata ioon bōran ke ej kōnono, innem ear jerkak ioon neen, likūt lo̧n̄iin epaake lo̧jiln̄in, im m̧ōkaj an ba “Ej pād ium̧win ekajet in mej.”

“Ta ñan?” Alice eaar ba.

“Kwar ba ke 'Ej juon men in buromõj!'?” Rabbit eo ekar kajitok.

“Jaab, iar jab,” Alice ear ba: “Ijjab lemnok ej juon men in buromõj. Iar ba ‘Ta?’”

“Ekar dāpij lojilñin Kwin eo—” Rabbit eo ekar jino. Alice ear lamij jidik kin ettōn. “O, bōjrak!” Rabbit eo ekar mōñā ilo juon ainikien mijak. “Lerooj eo enaj roñjake eok! Kwoj lo, ekar itok ilo ien eo ejjab to, im lerooj eo ekar ba—”

“Komin etal ñan jikin ko ami!” Kwin eo ekar lamõj ilo juon ainikien jourur, im armij ro rar jino ettōr ilo aolep jikin, im ettōr nae don; mekarta, rar jijet ilo juon ak ruo minit, innem ikkure eo ear jino. Alice ear lemnok bwe ear jab loe juon jikin ikkure rot in ilo mour eo an; ekar aolep ial ko im mōttan ko; ball ko rar hedgehog ko remour, mallet ko rar flamingo ko remour, im ri tarinae ro rar aikuj in double ir make im jutak ion peir im neir, ñan kōmman arch ko.

Apañ eo elaptata Alice ear loe ilo jinoin ej ilo an maroñ in lale flamingo eo an: ear maroñ in likit enbwinin, ilo an emman, iumin pein, im neir rej dāpij lal, ak ilo mol, einwot an kar lukkun jimwe bōran, im ear kōnan lelok juon mōttan ñan hedgehog eo kin bōran, enaj kar mōkaj im kalimjōk e make ilo an jab bwilōñ bwe ear jab maroñ in bōjrak an ettōn: im ke ear likit bõran, im ear bar jino, ear lukkun inebata ñan lo bwe hedgehog eo ear make rup, im ear etal: ijellokin men kein otemjej, ear wor juon ridge ak furrow ilo ial eo ñan ijo ear kõnan jilkinlok hedgehog eo ñane, im, einwõt an double-up soldiers ro rar iien otemjej etal ñan air jerkak im etal jet mõttan ko ilo jikin eo, Alice ear mõkaj an itok ñan jemlok eo bwe ear juon ikkure eo elap an bin.

Ri ikkure ro rar aolep ikkure ilo juon ien ilo ejelok air kõttar ien, akwāāl aolep ien, im tarinae kin hedgehog ko; im ilo juon ien eo edik, Kwin eo ear lukkun illu, im ear etal im ettōr, im lamõj “Kōtlok bōran!” ak “Kōtlok bōran!” enañin juon alen ilo juon minit.

Alice ear jino an lukkun inebata: ñan mol, ear jab wōr an akwāāl ippān Kwin eo, ak ear jelā bwe emaron kar walok jabdewōt minit, “im innem,” ear lemnok, “ta eo enaj walok ñan ña? Rej lukkun mõnõnõ in bukwe bõran armij ijin; men eo elap an kabwilõñlõñ ej, bwe ewõr juõn ej mour wõt!”

Ear lale juon ial in ko, im lemnok elañe emaron kar ko ilo an jab loe, ke ear lo juon men eo ekabwilōñlōñ ilo mejatoto: ear lukkun kabwilõñ e ilo jinoin, ak, elikin an kar lale juon ak ruo minit, ear kōmmane bwe en juon ettōñ dikdik, im ear ba ñan e make “Ej Cheshire Cat eo: kiõ enaj wor juon armij in konono ibben.”

“Ewi wāween am mour?” Pusi eo ear ba, ilo ien eo wot ear wor loñin bwe en maroñ in konono ibben.

Alice ear kõttar mae ien eo mejen ko rar walok, innem ear mōkaj. “Ejjelok tokjen konono ñan e,” lio ear lemnok, “mae ien eo ej itok lojilñin, ak juon iaer.” Ilo bar juon minit aolepān bōran eaar waļo̧k, innām Alice eaar likūt flamingo eo an, im jino juon bwebwenato kōn ikkure eo, en̄jake eļap an m̧ōņōņō bwe ewōr juon armej ej ron̄jake e. Pusi eo ekar lemnok bwe ekar wōr an kiõ ilo mejen, im ejelok bar ekar walok.

“Ijjab lemnok rej ikkure ilo jimwe,” Alice ekar jino, ilo juon ainikien inebata, “im rej aolep akwāāl ilo juon wāween eo elap an kauwōtata juon ejjab maroñ roñ an make konono—im ej einwõt ejelok air kakien ko; elañe ewor, ejelok armij ej lale ir—im kwojjab jelā ewi joñan an bin aolep men ko rej mour ie; etetal tok ilo jemlokin bwidej eo—im io kar aikuj in kar mōñā hedgehog eo an Queen eo kiõ, ak ear ettõrlok ke ear lo aō itok!”

“Ewi wāween am iakwe Kwin eo?” Pusi eo ekar ba ilo juon ainikien edik.

“Ejjab lukkun,” Alice ear ba: “elukkun—” Ilo ien eo wot ear lo bwe Kwin eo ear bed iturin, roñjake: innem ear wõnmanlok, “—emaroñ in wiin, bwe ejjab lukkun wõr tokjen ilo ien eo ej kadedelok ikkure eo.”

Kwin eo ear ettōn im etal.

“Wōn eo kwoj konono ibben?” King eo ekar ba, etal ñan ippān Alice, im kalimjōk bōran Pusi eo kōn eļap an kōņaan jeļā.

“Ej juon m̧ōtta—juon Cheshire Cat,” Alice eaar ba: “kōtļo̧k bwe in kwaļo̧k e.”

“Ijjab mõnõnõ kin wãwen an walok,” King eo ear ba: “ak, emaroñ jibwe peio elañe ekõnan.”

“Ijjab kōnan,” Pusi eo ekar ba.

“Jab inebata,” Kiiñ eo eaar ba, “im jab kalimjōk eō āinwōt in!” Ear jutak itulikin Alice ke ej konono.

“Juon pusi emaron kalimjōk juon king,” Alice eaar ba. “Ikar riiti ilo jet bok, ak Ijjab kememej ia eo.”

“Emmon, ej aikuj in jolok,” King eo ear ba ilo an lukkun kate, im ear kir Kwin eo, eo ear eļļā ilo ien eo, “Jitōnbōro! Ij kōnaan bwe kwon jolok pusi in!”

Kwin eo ekar wōr wōt juon an wāween n̄an kōm̧m̧ane aolep apan̄ ko, reļļap ak reddik. “Kōtlok bōran!” ej ba, ilo an jab bar kalimjōk.

“Inaaj make bōktok ri mij eo,” Kiin̄ eo eaar ba ilo kijejeto, im eaar m̧ōkaj an etal.

Alice ear lemnok bwe emaron kar jeblak, im lale ewi wawein an ikkure eo etal, ke ear roñ ainikien Queen eo ilo ettolok, ej lamõj kin an kijejeto. Emoj an kar roñ nan in ekajet eo an jilu ian ri ikkure ro bwe renaj kar mõn ir kin air kar likjab in bōk ien eo air, im ear jab mõnõnõ kin wãwen an men ko walok, kinke ikkure eo ear lukkun inebata bwe ear jab jelã elañe ej ien eo an ak jaab. Innem lio ear etal im kappok hedgehog eo an.

Hedgehog eo ear tarinae ippān bar juon hedgehog, eo ear einwot juon ien emman ñan Alice ñan an maroñ in tarinae juon iaer ippān eo juon: men eo ear bin wōt ej, bwe flamingo eo an ear etal ñan turear in jikin kallib eo, ijo Alice ear maroñ loe an kajeoñ ilo juon wāween eo ejelok an maroñ ñan ettōr ñan juon wijki.

Ilo ien eo ear jibwe flamingo eo im bōktok, tarinae eo ear jemlok, im hedgehog ko ruo rar jab walok: “ak ejjab lukkun aorōk,” Alice ear lemnok, “bwe aolep arch ko rar jako jen turear in bwidej in.” Innem ear likite iumwin pein, bwe en jab bar ko, innem ear jeblak ñan bar bwebwenato ibben eo mōttan.

Ke ear jeblak ñan Cheshire Cat eo, ear bwilōñ ke ear lo juon jarlepju in armij ro rar kobatok iturin: ear wor juon aitwerõk ej walok ikõtan ri mij eo, King eo, im Queen eo, ro rar konono ilo juõn wõt ien, ilo ien eo ro jet rar jab konono, im rar lukkun jab mõnõnõ.

Ilo ien eo Alice ear walok, ear akwelap ñan e jen aolep jilu bwe ren kajimwe kajitok eo, im rar bar kwalok air aitwerõk ñan e, meñe, ke rar aolep konono ilo juõn wõt ien, ear lo bwe ear lukkun bin an melele kin ta eo rar ba.

Argument eo an ri mij eo ej, bwe kwojjab maroñ in bukwe juon bōran elañe ejelok juon enbwin ñan bukwe jen e: bwe ejañin kar aikuj in kōmmane kain men rot in mokta, im ejamin kar jinoe ilo ien eo an mour.

Akwāāl eo an Kiin̄ eo eaar, bwe jabdewōt men eo ewōr bōran emaron̄ in m̧wijiti bōran, im bwe kwojjab aikuj kōnono men ko rebōd.

Aitwerõk eo an Kwin eo kar, bwe elañe ejelok men ear jab kōmmane ilo jidik ien enaj kar kōmman bwe ren mõn aolep armij, aolep. (Ekar nan in eliktata eo ear kōmman bwe aolep armij ro ren lukkun inebata im inebata.)

Alice ear jab maroñ lemnok kin bar juon men en ba ak “Ej an Duchess eo: emmanlok am kajitõk ippān kake.”

“Ej bed ilo kalbuj,” Kwin eo ear ba ñan ri mij eo: “bōktok e ñan ijin.” Im ri ṃan armej eo ear etal āinwōt juon ṃade.

Bōran Pusi eo ear jino jakolok ilo ien eo ear jako, im, ilo ien eo ear jeblak ibben Duchess eo, ear jakolok; innem Kiin eo im ri mõn eo rar ettõr im bukõt e, ilo ien eo ro jet ilo kumi eo rar bar jeblak ñan ikkure eo.
