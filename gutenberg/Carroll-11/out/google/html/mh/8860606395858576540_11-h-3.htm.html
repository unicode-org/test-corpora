JAPTER III.
Juon Caucus-Race im juon bwebwenato eo elap

Rekar lukkun juon kumi in armij ro reoktak jen dron im rar kobatok ilo reba eo—bao ko ewōr aer bōraer, menin mour ko ewōr aer bōraer rej dāpij er, im aolep rej mōrā, jorrāān, im jab em̧m̧an.

Kajitōk eo moktata ej, ewi wawein bar mōrā: rar kōmman juon aer kōnono kin men in, innem elikin jejjo minit ko ekar einwot juon men eo ekkar ñan Alice bwe en lo e make ej konono ibbeir, einwot ke ekar jelā kajjier aolepen mour eo an. Emol, ekar wōr an aitwerõk ippān Lory eo, eo ekar oktak im illu, im ekar ba wōt, “Ij rūttoļo̧k jān kwe, im ij aikuj in jeļā em̧m̧anļo̧k;” im men in Alice ejamin kar kōtlok ilo an jab jelā ewi joñan an rūtto, im, ke Lory eo ear lukkun makoko in kwalok joñan an rūtto, ejelok bar men en kar ba.

Āliktata Mouse eo, eo ej einwõt juon armij eo ewõr an maroñ ibwiljier, ear lamij, “Komin jijet, kom aolep, im roñjake iõ! Inaj mōkaj in kõmman bwe komin maroñ in mōrā!” Rej jijet aolep ilo juon ien, ilo juon ring elap, im Mouse eo ilo lukkun. Alice ear kalimjeke ilo an inebata, bwe ear eñjake bwe enaj kar bōk juon nañinmij in bwil elañe ear jab mōkaj an mōrā.

“Ahem!” Mouse eo ear ba ilo juon ainikien eaorōk, “kom̧ij pojak ke? Men in ej men eo em̧ōrā tata ijeļā. Jab kōnono aolep jikin, eļan̄n̄e kom̧ kōņaan! 'William the Conqueror, eo me pope eo eaar m̧ōņōņō kake, eaar m̧ōkaj an kar kōtļo̧k jān riIngland ro, ro raar kōņaan ritōl ro, im eaar lukkuun m̧ōņōņō in bōk im bōk m̧weiuk ko im anjo̧ ioon Morwin, im Morcarls, im Morbrician ro an North.'”

“Ugh!” Lory eo ear ba, ilo an mōk.

“Ij akwelap ñan yuk jeorlok bwid!” Mouse eo ear ba, ej būromōj, ak ilo jouj: “Kwar konono ke?”

Jab io! Lory eo ear ba ilo mõkaj.

“Ikar lemnok ke kwar kōmmane,” Mouse eo ekar ba. “—Ij wōnmanlok. 'Edwin im Morcar, earl ro an Mercia im Northumbria, rar kwalok kin e: im bareinwõt Stigand, archbishop eo an Canterbury, ear lo bwe emman—'”

“Kar loe ta eo ?” Duck eo ear ba.

“Iar loe ,” Mouse eo ear uwaak ilo an jab errā: “em̧ool kwojeļā ta meļeļein ‘ej’.”

“Ijelā ta melelein ‘ej’, ñe ij lo juon men,” Duck eo eaar ba: “ekkā an juon frog ak juon worm. Kajjitōk eo ej, ta eo archbishop eo eaar loe?”

Mouse eo ear jab kile kajitok in, ak ear mõkaj an wõnmanlok, “’—ear lo bwe emman in etal ibben Edgar Atheling ñan iion William im lelok ñan e crown eo. Jerbal eo an William ilo jinoin ear jimwe. Ak an kar jab jimwe an Norman ro an—’ Ewi wãwen am bed kiõ, jitõb?” ej wōnmanlok, oktaklok ñan Alice ke ej konono.

“Einwot an mōrā,” Alice eaar ba ilo juon ainikien būromõj: “ejjab einwot ej kōmman ao mōrā.”

“Ilo jekjek in,” Dodo eo eaar ba ilo kautiej, im jutak, “Ij kajjitōk bwe kweilo̧k eo en bōjrak, n̄an m̧ōkaj in bōk wāween ko rekajoorļo̧k—”

“Kōnono kajin English!” Eaglet eo ekar ba. “Ijjab jelā meļeļein jimettan in naan ko reļļap, im, ta eo eļapļo̧k, Ijjab tōmak kwoj barāinwōt jeļā!” Im Eaglet eo ear bukwelōlō bōran bwe en dāpij an ettōn̄ dikdik: jet iaan bao ko jet raar m̧akūtkūt ilo ainikien.

“Ta eo ikar kōnaan ba,” Dodo eo ekar ba ilo juon ainikien ekabūromōjmōj, “kar, bwe men eo emman tata ñan kōmman bwe jen mōrā enaj kar juon Caucus-race.”

Ta ej juon Caucus-race? Alice eaar ba; ejjab bwe ekar kōnan jelā, ak Dodo ekar bōjrak einwot ekar lemnok bwe ewōr juon ej aikuj in konono, im ejelok bar juon ekar kōnan ba jabdrewot.

“Etke,” Dodo eo eaar ba, “wāween eo em̧m̧an tata n̄an kōmeļeļeik ej n̄an kōm̧m̧ane.” (Im, einwot am make kōnan kajeoñ men in, jet raan in winter, inaj kwalok ñan yuk ewi wawein an Dodo kar kōmmane.)

Mokta ear kakōlkōl juon ial in ettōr, ilo juon kain circle, (“ejjab aorōk jekjek eo ejimwe,” ear ba,) innem aolep kumi eo kar likiti ilo ial eo, ijin im ijin. Ejelok “Juon, ruo, jilu, im etal,” ak rar jino ettōr ñe rar kōnan, im bōjrak ñe rar kōnan, bwe en jab pidodo ñan jelā ñe ettor eo ej jemlok. Ak, ke rar ettor iumwin jimettan awa, im rar bar lukkun mōrā, Dodo eo ear mōkaj an lamõj “Emōj an jemlok ettōr eo!” im rar aolep kobalok iturin, im mōñā, im kajitōk, “Ak wōn eo ear wiin?”

Kajjitōk in Dodo eo ear jab maroñ uwaake ilo an jab lukkun lemnok, im ear jijet iumwin juon ien aitok kin juon pein ej jibwe turin mejen (jikin eo kwoj loe Shakespeare ie, ilo pija ko an), ilo ien eo ro jet rar kõttar ilo air jab konono. Āliktata Dodo eo eaar ba, “ Aolep armej raar wiin, im aolep rej aikuj in wōr aer menin letok.”

“Ak wōn eo enaj lewoj menin letok ko?” juon jar in ainikien ko rar kajitok.

“Etke, lio , emool,” Dodo eo eaar ba, im kwalok ñan Alice kin juon pein; im aolep armij ro rar itok iturin, im kir ilo juon wewein eo ebwid, “Prize ko! Prize ko!”

Alice ear ejelok an jella ta eo en kōmmane, im ilo an kar buromõj ear likit pein ilo bōjo eo an, im bōk juon box in comfits, (emman bwe den in salt eo ear jab deloñ ie), im lelok ir einwot prize ko. Ekar wor juon wōt mōttan, aolep ibelakin.

“Ak ej aikuj in wōr juon an menin letok make, kwojeļā,” Mouse eo eaar ba.

“Emool,” Dodo eo ear uwaak ilo an lukkun inebata. “Ta ko jet ewōr ippam ilo pāāk eo am?” ekar wōnmanlok, im oktaklok ñan Alice.

“Juon wōt thimble,” Alice eaar ba ilo būrom̧ōj.

“Kwōn letok ijin,” Dodo eo eaar ba.

Innem rar bar kobalok ibben bar juon alen, ke Dodo eo ear kwalok thimble eo, im ba “Kimij akwelap ñan yuk bwe kwon bōk thimble in eaiboojoj;” im, ke ekar dedelok an konono edik, rar aolep lañliñ.

Alice ear lemnok bwe men eo ear lukkun jab mol, ak rar aolep lukkun nana bwe ear jab peran in ettōn; im, ke ear jab maroñ lemnok kin jabdrewot men en ba, ear baj bukwelōlō wōt, im bōk thimble eo, im ej lukkun kautiej.

Men eo tok juon ej ñan mōñā comfit ko: men in ear kōmman jet ainikien im inebata, ke bao ko relap rar inebata bwe rejjab maroñ in mōñā eo air, im bao ko redik rar mōñā im aikuj in mōñā ilo likio. Ak, ekar jemlok, im rar bar jijet ilo juon ring, im akwelap ñan Mouse eo bwe en kwalok ñan ir bar juon men.

“Kwar kallimur bwe kwonaj kwalok bwebwenato eo am, kwojelā,” Alice eaar ba, “im etke kwoj dike—C im D,” eaar kobaiktok ilo juon ainikien edik, jimettan an mijak bwe enaj bar jorrāān.

“Aō ej juon bwebwenato eo elap im ekaburomõjmõj!” Mouse eo eaar ba, oktakļo̧k n̄an Alice, im m̧ōņōņō.

“ Ej juon tōrerein eo elap, emool,” Alice eaar ba, im kalimjōk lal ilo bwilōñ ñan tōrerein Mouse eo; “ak etke kwoj naetan menin buromõj?” Im ear wōnmanlok wōt im lemnok kake ke Mouse eo ear konono, innem lemnok eo an kin bwebwenato eo ear einwot in:—

“Kwojjab bed!” Mouse eo ear ba ñan Alice ilo an kajoor. Ta eo kwoj lemnok kake?

“Ij akwelap ñan yuk jeorlok bwid,” Alice ear ba ilo ettā: “kwaar tōpar jikin eo kein ka lemnōn, ij lemnok?”

“Ikar jab! ” Mouse eo ekar lamij, ilo an lukkun illu.

“Juon knot!” Alice ekar ba, aolep ien ej pojak in kōmman bwe en wōr tokjen, im ej kalimjōk e ilo an inebata. “O, kwon kōtlok bwe in jibañ kōmmane!”

“Ijjab kōmmane jabdrewot men rot in,” Mouse eo ear ba, im jutak im etal. “Kwoj kajjirere kake io ilo am konono men ko rejjab mol!”

“Ikar jab melele kake!” Alice ej akwelap. “Ak kwoj lukkun mōkaj am illu, kwojelā ke!”

Mouse eo ear baj mōñā wōt ilo an uwaak.

“Jouj im bar jeblak im kadedelok bwebwenato eo am!” Alice ear call tokelik; im ro jet rar kobatok ilo al, “Aet, jouj im kōmmane!” ak Mouse eo ear baj mōkaj an mōkaj an jab kijenmij, im etetal jidik mōkajlok.

“Ej juon menin būromõj bwe enaj kar jab bed wot!” Lory eo ear mōk, ke ear lukkun jako jen mejen armij; im juon Crab eo eritto ear bōk ien eo emman im ba ñan leddik eo nejin “Ah, jitōnbōro! Men in en juon katak ñan kwe bwe kwon jab illu !” “Kwōn dāpij wōt lo̧n̄iūm̧, Ma!” Crab eo edik ekar ba, jidik an mōkaj. “Kwoj maroñ in kajeoñ kijenmij eo an juon oyster!”

“Ikõnan bwe en wor Dinah ijin, ijelā ke ewōr ippa!” Alice eaar ba ilo ainikien eļap, im jab kōnono n̄an jabdewōt armej. “Enaaj kar mōkaj an bōktok!”

“Im wōn Dinah, elañe imaroñ in kajjitōk kajitok eo?” Lory eo ear ba.

Alice ear uwaak ilo kijejeto, bwe ear aolep ien pojak in konono kin menin mour eo an: “Dinah ej pusi eo ad. Im ej juon eo elap an maroñ in jibwe kidu ko kwojjab maroñ lemnok! Im oh, ij kōnaan bwe kwon maroñ in loe elikin bao ko! Etke, enaj mõñã juon bao edik ilo ien eo wōt kwoj kalimjeke!”

Kōnono in ear kōmman juon menin bwilōñ ibwiljin kumi eo. Jet ian bao ko rar mõkaj im etal ilo ien eo wot: juon Magpie eo eritto ear jino an dāpij e make ilo an lukkun lale, im ba, “Ij lukkun aikuj in tōpar mweo; mejatoto in boñ ejjab ekkar ñan loñio!” im juon Canary ej kirtok ilo juon ainikien ebwil ñan ajiri ro nejin, “Komin itok, ro rejitōnbōro ippa! Emoj ien eo bwe komin aolep bed ilo bed!” Ilo elōn kain un ko rar aolep etal, im Alice ear make iaer.

“Ikar kōnaan bwe in kar jab kwalok kin Dinah!” ej ba ñan e make ilo juon ainikien būromõj. “Ejjelok armij ej yokwe e, ijin, im ijelā bwe ej pusi eo emman tata ilo lal in! O, Dinah eo aõ! Ij lemnok elañe inaj bar lo eok!” Im ijin Alice ejeramel ear jino bar jañ, bwe ear eñjake an make iaan im ebbweer. Ilo jidik ien, mekarta, ear bar roñ jidik ainikien ne ko ilo ettolok, im ear reilōñlok ilo kijejeto, jimettan an kejatrikrik bwe Mouse eo ear oktak lemnok eo an, im ej bar jeblak ñan kadedelok bwebwenato eo an.
