SOPUN III.
Eú Kaús-Ras me eú Pwóróus mi Lamot .

Ra wesewesen ew mwichen aramas mi queer-looking ra chufengen won ewe chanpupu —ekkewe man mi wor uwan mi fitikoko, ekkewe man mi wor uwan mi fitikoko ngenir, me meinisin ra uren konik, cross, me rese kinamwe.

Ewe aewin kapas eis ennetin, ifa usun repwe niwinsefan: ra fori ew kokkot usun ei, me mwirin fitu minich a usun ita mi fokkun fis ngeni Alice an epwe kuna pwe a fos ngenir non napanap mi sinei, usun ita a sinei ir non unusen manauan. Ennetin, a wor ew anini mi fokkun nangatam ren ewe Lory, ewe a soposopono ne siwin ngeni sulky, me epwe chok apasa, “Ngang mi watte seni en, me upwe sinei nap seni;” me ei Alice esap mut ngeni nge ese sinei fite ierin, me, usun ewe Lory a fokkun mochen apasa ierin, ese chiwen wor met epwe apasa.

Ren enletin ewe Mous, ewe a usun ita emon aramas mi wor an nemenem me neir, a koko, “Oupwe mwot, ami meinisin, me ausening ngeniei! Upwe mwitir fori pwe oupwe naf pwas!” Ir meinisin ra mwot non ew chok fansoun, non ew watten ring, nge ewe Mouse a nom nefinen. Alice a chék nennengeni mesan kewe fán lólileniesini, pún a meefi pwe epwe úri eú semmwen mi ngaw ika esap mwittir chék chéúfetál.

“Em!” a apasa ewe Mouse ren ew asepwan mi auchea, "ami meinisin oua fen ammonata? Iei ewe mettoch mi kon pwichikar ua sinei. Oupwe chok kinamwe unukun, ika oua mochen! 'William ewe Chon Angang, ewe popun an we popun a fis ren ewe popun, a mwitir chok nom fan nemenien ekkewe chon England, ekkewe ra mochen souemwen, me ra fen non fansoun nangatam ra fokkun sani ar repwe akkufu Morcar me Mercia me ar repwe angei fonuer. Notou —’”

“Uw!” a apasa ewe Lory, ren an ngungures.

“Ua tingor om omusomus!” a apasa ewe Mouse, a uren netipengaw, nge a fokkun tipetekison: “Ka kapas?”

“Esap ngang!” a apasa ewe Lory non mwitir.

“Ua ekieki pwe ka fori,” ewe Mous a apasa. “—Ua sopweilo. ‘Edwin me Morcar, ekkewe chon nemenem lon Mercia me Northumbria, ra pwarata fan itan: me pwal mwo nge Stigand, ewe pisop mi lap mi tongei fonuan lon Canterbury, a kuna pwe mi murinno —’”

“Ka kuna met ?” a apasa ewe Duk.

“Ua kuna ,” ewe Mouse a ponuweni ren an ekis song: “enlet ka sinei met wewen ‘a’.”

“Ua sinei met ‘a’ wewen mi naf, nupwen ua kuna ew mettoch,” ewe Duck a apasa: “a kan ew frog ika ew worm. Ewe kapas eis, met ewe archbishop a kuna?”

Ewe Mous ese kuna ei kapas eis, nge a mwitir ne sopweno, “’—a kuna pwe mi och an epwe fiti Edgar Atheling ne churi William me ngeni i ewe king. a sopweno, a suki ngeni Alice nupwen a kapas.

“Usun chok me nom,” Alice a apasa non ew mongungu mi netipengaw: “ese fokkun usun ita epwe suki ngeniei.”

“Non ena fansoun,” ewe Dodo a apasa non tipepos, a uta won pechen, “Ua pesei pwe ewe mwich epwe much, ren ewe mwitir ne angei ekkewe safei mi pochokun —”

“Kopwe kapas non fosun Merika!” a apasa ewe Ewin. “Use sinei wewen esopw ekkena kapas mi langatam, me, met a nap seni ena, use nuku pwe ka pwan sinei!” Iwe ewe Eaglet a chapetiw won mokuran pwe epwe opano ew smile: ekkoch ekkewe ekkoch man ra titter non mongungu.

“Met upwe apasa,” ewe Dodo a apasa non ew mongungu mi song, “pwe, ewe mettoch mi murino ne fori kich sipwe pwas epwe ew Caucus-race.”

“Met weween eu rasen Kapas?” a apasa Alice; esap pwe a mochen sinei chommong mettoch, nge ewe Dodo a fen asoso usun ita a ekieki pwe emon epwe kapas, me ese wor emon a usun ita epwe mochen apasa och mettoch.

“Pwata,” ewe Dodo a apasa, “ewe aan mi murinno ne awewei ina ach sipwe fori.” (Me, usun kopwe tongeni pwisin sotuni ewe mettoch, ekkoch ran non fansoun pwichikar, upwe erenuk ifa usun ewe Dodo a manaweni.)

Akkom a makkei ew nenien ras, non ew sokkun circle, (“ewe ennetin napanap ese auchea,” a apasa,) iwe mwirin meinisin ewe mwich ra nom won ewe nenien ras, ikei me ikewe. Ese wor “Emon, ruu, ulumon, me towaw,” nge ra poputa ne fiti nupwen ra mochen, me ra ukutiw nupwen ra mochen, ina minne ese mecheres ar repwe sinei inet ewe kitir a wes. Iwe nge, lupwen ra fen fiti esopw awa are lap seni, me ra fokkun pwichikar sefan, ewe Dodo a muttir kokko “Ewe kitir a wes!” iwe ir meinisin ra chufengen unukun, ra ngungures, me ra eisini, “Nge io a win?”

Ei kapas eis ewe Dodo ese tongeni ponuweni ika ese watte an ekiek, me a mwot fansoun nangatam ren ew poun a suki won mokuran (ewe napanap ka kan kuna Shakespeare non, non ekkewe sasingin i), nupwen ekkewe ekkoch ra witiwit non kinamwe. Ren enletin ewe Dodo a apasa, “ Meinisin ra fen win, me meinisin epwe wor ar niwin.”

“Nge io epwe ngeni ekkewe liwin?” fokkun eu mwichen mongungu ra eisini.

“Pwata, i , ennetin,” a apasa ewe Dodo, a aiti ngeni Alice ren ew poun; me unusen ewe mwich ra mwitir chok fiti, ra kokko non ew napanap mi osukosuk, “Prais!Prais!”

Alice ese wor an ekiek ren met epwe fori, me non an netipengaw a wanong poun non an we pocket, me a angei ew pworun comfits, (feioch pwe ewe konik mi salt ese tonong non), me a ngenir round pwe repwe niwinin. A wor eu chok a-piis, meinisin ra rokopwanieno.

“Nge epwe wor an pwisin niwin, ka sinei,” a apasa ewe Mouse.

“Ewer,” ewe Dodo a ponuweni non fokkun song. “Met ka pwan angei non noumw we pocket?” a sopweno, a suki ngeni Alice.

Alice a apasa fán letipechou: “Eú chék chénúpupu.”

“Kopwe ngeni ikei,” ewe Dodo a apasa.

Iwe ir meinisin ra chufengen unukun fan eu, nupwen ewe Dodo a sopweno ne awora ewe ttikin, a apasa “Am aua tingor ngonuk om kopwe etiwa ei ttikin mi ling;” iwe, nupwen a wes ei kukkun kapas, ir meinisin ra pwapwa.

Alice a ekieki pwe unusen ewe mettoch a fokkun umwes, nge ir meinisin ra fokkun ngaw pwe ese pwora ne takiri; me, pokiten ese tongeni ekieki och mettoch epwe apasa, a chok chapetiw, me angei ewe thimble, a nikinikin solemn usun chok a tongeni.

Ewe mettoch mwirin ina ar repwe mongo ekkewe comfits: ei a efisata ekkoch ngingi me tipemwaramwar, pun ekkewe man watte ra ngungures pwe rese tongeni tapweno mwirin ar kewe, me ekkewe kukkun ra chok menemenoch me ra kan pat won pecher. Nge, a wes non soponon, iwe ra mwot sefan non ew ring, me tingormau ngeni ewe Mouse an epwe ereniir och mettoch mi nap.

“Ka pwon pwe kopwe ereniei om uruwo, ka sinei,” Alice a apasa, “me pwata ina ka oput —C me D,” a apasa sefan non ew mongungu, arapakan a nuokus pwe epwe song sefan.

“Ai we poraus eu poraus mi langattam me aletipechou!” a apasa ewe Mous, a suki ngeni Alice, me a ngungures.

“ Iei ew chuk mi langatam, ennetin,” Alice a apasa, a nenengeni fan ren mairu ren chuk ewe Mouse; “nge pwata ka eita ngeni netipengaw?” Iwe a chok sopweno ne osukosuk ren nupwen ewe Mouse a kapas, ina minne an ekiek usun ewe poraus a usun ei:—

“Ousap fiti!” a apasa ewe Mous ngeni Alice non an song. “Met ka ekieki usun?”

“Ua tingor om omusomus,” Alice a apasa fan fokkun tipetekison: “ka fen tori ewe alimuen bend, ua ekieki?”

“Use mwo! ” a puchor ewe Mouse, a fokkun song me a fokkun song.

“Ew knot!” a apasa Alice, fansoun meinisin a ammonata an epwe fori pwe epwe auchea, me a nenengeni ren osukosuk usun i. “O, kopwe mut ngeniei ai upwe aninis ne awesi!”

“Usap fori och mettoch usun ena,” a apasa ewe Mous, a uta me fetan. “Ka turunufaseiei ren om kapas usun ekkena kapas mi umwes!”

“Use mochen!” a tingormaú ngeni Alice mi woungaw. “Nge ka fokkun mecheresin song, ka sinei!”

Ewe Mouse a chok ngungures non an ponuweni.

“Kose mochen kopwe niwin me awesi om poraus!” Alice a kokori mwirin ena; me ekkewe ekkoch meinisin ra fiti ne köl, “Ewer, kose mochen kopwe föri!” nge ewe Mouse a chok suki awan ese mosonoson, me a ekis mwitir fetan.

“A ifa me watten riaffou pwe esap nom!” a ngungures ewe Lory, usun chok a fokkun tou seni mesan; me emon chinnap Crab a angei ewe tufichin apasa ngeni noun we nengin “Ah, ai tong!Mut ngeni ei epwe ew lesson ngonuk pwe kosapw chiwen song!” “Kopwe amwochu om tong, Ma!” a apasa ewe kukkun Crab, a ekis sniff. “A naf om kopwe sotuni mosonoson an emon ois!”

“Ua mochen pwe epwe wor ach Dinah ikei, ua sinei pwe mi wor!” a apasa Alice non mongungu, ese kapas ngeni emon aramas. “Epwe mwittir angei sefani!”

“Nge io ena Dina, ika upwe tongeni pwora le eisini ewe kapas eis?” a apasa ewe Lori.

Alice a ponuweni ren mochen, pun a kan mmonleta ne kapas usun noun we pet fansoun meinisin: "Dinah ina nouch we pusi. Iwe i emon mi fokkun kapital ren an epwe angei miis kose tongeni ekieki! Iwe oh, ua mochen kopwe tongeni kuna i mwirin ekkewe man! Pwata, epwe mongo emon kukkun man nupwen chok ka nenengeni!"

Ei kapas a efisata eu minen apwapwa mi amwarar lein ewe pekin. Ekkoch ekkewe man ra mwitir feino non ew chok fansoun: emon Magpie mi chinnap a poputa ne rokopwanieno inisin non fokkun tumunoch, a apasa, “Ua wesewesen mochen upwe tori imwei; ewe asepwan non pwin ese fich ngeni ai ngungu!” me emon Kanari a kokko non mongungu mi chechech ngeni noun kewe, “Oupwe feito, nei kewe achengicheng! A tori fansoun ami meinisin oupwe nom non pet!” Ren sokkopaten popun ir meinisin ra feilo, me ekiselo chok Alice a chok nonnom.

“Amwo use fen menlapei Tina!” a apasa ngeni pwisin i non ew mongungu mi netipengaw. “Ese wor emon a usun ita a sani i, ikei fan, me ua nuku pwe i ewe pusi mi murinno seni meinisin won fonufan! O, ai achengicheng Dinah! Ua ekieki ika upwe chiwen kuna en!" Iwe ikei ie Alice mi woungaw a poputa ne kechiw sefan, pun a mefi pwe a fokkun chok pwisin i me a tekison. Nge non ekis fansoun, a pwan rongorong ekis patapaten pechen aramas non towaw, me a nenengeni ren mochen, nesoponon an apinukunuk pwe ewe Mouse a siwini an ekiek, me a niwinsefan pwe epwe awesi an poraus.
