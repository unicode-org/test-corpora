SOPUN II.
Ewe Pool mi Uren Chon Masou .

“Kuri me kukkun!” a puchor Alice (a fokkun mairu, pwe ren ewe fansoun a fokkun monuki ifa usun epwe kapas non fosun England mi murinno); “iei ua suki usun ewe teleskop mi watte seni meinisin mi fen nom!Kopwe feioch, pechen!” (pun nupwen a nenengeni pechen kewe, ra usun ita pwe ra arapakan ngeni ar repwe tou seni mesan, ra fokkun towaw). “O, ai kewe kukkun pechen mi woungaw, ua ekieki io epwe ufouf ami kewe sus me stockings fan itemi iei, pwipwi? Ua nuku pwe usap tongeni!Upwe fokkun towaw ai upwe osukosuk ren ami: oupwe nemeni ewe aan mi murinno oua tongeni;—nge upwe chok kirekiroch ngenir,” a ekieki Alice pwe repwe feino, “ika I epwe feino! nengeni: Upwe ngenir ew minafon pekin boot iteiten Kirisimas.”

Iwe, a sopwela le ekieki ngeni pwisin i ifa usun epwe nemeni. “Repwe feino ren ewe chon uwei,” a ekieki; “me epwe ifa me amwararen, an epwe tinato liffang ngeni pwisin pechen emon!Me epwe ifa me amwararen ekkewe emmwen!

Ewe Pechen Alice mi Pwungun, Esq.,
Pekin letip,
arap ngeni ewe Fen, ( ren an Alice tong ).

O pwipwi, met sokkun kapasen umwes ua apasa!”

Non chok ena fansoun mokuran a tori ewe asamwachon ewe imw: ennetin iei a nap seni tiu fit tekian, me a mwitir angei ewe kukkun kii mi gold me a mwitir feino ngeni ewe asamen ewe atake.

Alice mi osupwang! A ukukun an tongeni fori, a mwokut won ew pekin, an epwe nenengeni non ewe atake ren ew chok mesan; nge an epwe tonong non a kon wor apinukunuk nap seni me nom: a mwot me a poputa ne kechiw sefan.

“Mi namwot kopwe niuokus ren pwisin en,” Alice a apasa, “emon nengin mi nap usun chok en,” (epwe tongeni apasa ei), “kopwe sopweno ne kechiw non ei napanap! Kopwe ukutiw non ei fansoun, ua erenuk!” Nge a chok sopweno, a aturanong gallon ren chonun mesan, tori a wor ew watten konik unukun, ukukun fomon inis watten me a tori nesoponon ewe asam.

Mwirin och fansoun a rongorong ekis ngingiin pechen aramas non towaw, me a mwitir ne suki mesan pwe epwe kuna met epwe fis. Iei ewe Rapin mi pwechepwech a niwin, a ufouf mi amwarar, a wor ew pekin glove mi pwechepwech non ew poun me ew watten fan non ewe pwan ew: a feito a fetan non ew watten mwitir, a ngungures ngeni pwisin i nupwen a feito, “O! Alice a mefi pwe a fokkun netipengaw pwe a mmonleta ne tingor aninis seni emon; ina minne, nupwen ewe Rapin a feito arap ngeni, a poputa, non ew mongungu mi tekison, me niuokus, “Ika kopwe tingor, samol—” Ewe Rapin a poputa ne pochokun, a aturanong ekkewe glove mi pwechepwech me ewe fan, me a skurri ngeni ewe rochopwak usun chok an tongeni feino.

Alice a angei ewe fan me ekkewe glove, me, nupwen ewe nenien mwich a fokkun pwichikar, a chok fan pwisin i fansoun meinisin a sopweno ne kapas: “Achengicheng, achengicheng! Ifa usun mettoch meinisin mi weires ikenai! Iwe ikenai mettoch ra chok fis usun chok me nom. ekis sokkono Nge ika use usun chok, ewe kapas eis mwirin, Io non ewe fonufan ngang Ah, ina ewe puzzle mi nap!” Iwe a poputa ne ekieki usun meinisin ekkewe semirit a sinei pwe ir mi chok ierir usun chok i, pwe epwe kuna ika epwe tongeni siwin ngeni emon me neir.

“Ua nuku pwe esap ngang Ada,” a apasa, “pun uwan ​​kewe ra feino non ekkena sokkun chuk mi chou, me uwan ​​kewe rese fokkun chuk; me ua nuku pwe use tongeni Mabel, pun ua sinei sokopaten mettoch meinisin, me i , oh! ika pwe ua sinei meinisin ekkewe mettoch ua fen sinei me nom.Mut ngeniei upwe kuna: fomon fan nimu ina engon me ruemon, me fomon fan fisu ina —oh achengicheng usapw chiwen tori ruwemon non ena ukukun Nge, ewe Tepulen Achocho ese wewe ngeni pwe Rome ewe kapital non Paris, me— mwaal , ua nuku pwe upwe fen siwin fan iten Mabel!

“Ifa usun ewe kukkun krokodil a .
Kopwe amürinnölo an föün äsefich mi saramaram, .
Iwe, a ninätiu kolukun ewe chanpupu Nil .
Lon meinisin ekkewe skel mi for seni kolt!

“A ifa me pwapwaan an usun ita a ngingioch, .
Ifa usun, a ffat an kewe claws, .
Iwe etiwa ekkewe kukkun ik non .
Ren ekkewe ngingi mi mosonoson!”

“Ua nuku pwe esapw ekkena kapas mi pung,” a apasa Alice mi woungaw, me mesan a uren kechiw sefan nupwen a sopweno, “Upwe chok Mabel mwirin meinisin, me upwe feino me nonnom non ena kukkun imw mi poky, me ese wor ai kewe pisekin urumot upwe urumot ren, me oh! kopwe chok nom fan ikei Esap wor namotan ar repwe suki meser me apasa ‘Kopwe feito sefan, achengicheng!’. Ngang upwe chok nenengeni me apasa ‘Io ngang iwe? Kopwe ereni ei akkom, iwe mwirin, ika ua sani ai upwe ena emon, upwe feito: ika esap ina, upwe nom fan ikei tori ai upwe pwan emon’ —nge, oh dear!” a puchor Alice, ren an mwitir ne kechiw, “Ua wesewesen mochen pwe repwe wanong mokurer! Ua fokkun netipengaw ren ai upwe chok nomw ikei!”

Nupwen a apasa ei a nenengeni poun kewe, me a mairu an kuna pwe a ufouf ew me nein ekkewe kukkun gloves mi pwechepwech an ewe Rabbit nupwen a kapas. “Ifa usun upwe tongeni fori ena?” a ekieki. “Mi namwot upwe kukkununo sefan.” A uta me feino ngeni ewe tepwe pwe epwe auku inisin ren, me a kuna pwe, usun chok a tongeni ekieki, iei a ukukun ruu fit tekian, me a sopweno ne suki me a mwitir: a mwitir kuna pwe ewe popun ei ina ewe fan a kamwoch, me a mwitir ne aturanong, non chok ewe fansoun pwe esapw suki altother.

“ Ina eu minen eppet mi fitikoko!” a apasa Alice, a fokkun niuokus ren ewe siwin mi mwitir, nge a fokkun pwapwa ne kuna pwe a chuen nom; “iwe iei ren ewe atake!” iwe a su ren unusen mwitir ngeni ewe kukkun asam: nge, riaffou! ewe kukkun asam a suk sefan, me ewe kukkun kii gold a nom won ewe tepwenin glass usun chok me nom, “me mettoch ra kon ngaw nap seni me nom,” ewe semirit mi woungaw a ekieki, “pun use mwo kon kukkun usun ei me nom, ese mwo! Iwe ua pwarata pwe a kon ngaw, pwe ina!”

Nupwen a apasa ekkei kapas pechen a suki, me non pwan ew fansoun, a suki! a tori an chinnap non konik mi salt. An we aewin ekiek pwe a fen ekkoch napanap a turutiw non ewe sat, “me non ena fansoun upwe tongeni niwin ren railway,” a apasa ngeni pwisin i. (Alice a fen feino ngeni ewe sat fan ew non manauan, me a fen tori ewe ekiek mi nap, pwe ia chok ka feilo ngeni won ewe sat England ka kuna chommong pisekin konik non ewe sat, ekkoch semirit ra suki non ewe sand ren ekkewe spade mi for seni ira, mwirin ew row ren imwen chon nomw, me mwirin ir ew nenien fiti sepenin nupwen a pool non ewe pool wears. a ükükün tiu fiit tekian.

“Amwo use kon kechiw!” a apasa Alice, nupwen a suki, a achocho ne kutta an epwe tou. "Upwe kuna kapung ren iei, ua ekieki, ren ai upwe tonong non pwisin ai kechiw! Epwe ew mettoch mi amwarar, pwe epwe ennet! Iwe nge, mettoch meinisin mi weires ikenai."

Non chok ena fansoun a rongorong och mettoch a fifis non ewe konik ekis towaw, me a suki arap ngeni pwe epwe sinei met ena: akkom a ekieki pwe epwe ne ew walrus ika hippopotamus, nge mwirin a chechemeni ifa usun a kukkun iei, me a mwitir chok sinei pwe ew chok mouse a slip non usun chok i.

“Epwe wor lamotan, iei,” Alice a ekieki, “ai upwe kapas ngeni ei mouse? Mettoch meinisin mi fokkun tou seni ewe aan fan ikei, pwe upwe ekieki pwe mi fokkun tufich pwe epwe tongeni kapas: ese nifinifin, ese wor ngawen ai upwe achocho.” Ina minne, a poputa le apasa: “O Mous, ka silei ewe alen towu seni ei konik? Ua fokkun letipechou ren ai upwe fiti ikei, O Mous!” (Alice a ekieki pwe epwe iei ewe napanap mi pung an epwe kapas ngeni emon monukon: ese mwo fori ena sokkun mettoch me nom, nge a chechemeni an kuna non an pwin we Latin Grammar, “Emon monukon —ren emon monukon —emon monukon —O monukon!”) Ewe Monukon a nenengeni neminewe ren an ekis mochen sinei, me a apasa pwe ese wor och mettoch ngeni nge a usun ita a win ren eu.

“Eli ese weweiti fosun Merika,” Alice a ekieki; “Ua tipemwaramwar pwe ina emon mouse seni France, kopwe feito ren William ewe Chon Angei.” (Pun, ren meinisin an sinei usun uruwo, Alice ese fokkun fatafatoch an ekiek ifa ukukun fansoun a fis och mettoch.) Ina minne a poputa sefan: “Ifa usun?” ina ewe aewin kapas non noun we puken kaeo non fosun France. Ewe Mouse a mwitir ne su seni ewe konik, me a usun ita a chechech unusan ren niuokus. “O, ua tingor om omusomus!” a puchor Alice fan mosonoson, a nuokus pwe a fen asonga memefin ewe man mi woungaw. “Ua fokkun monuki pwe kese sani pusi.”

“Esap usun pusi!” a puchor ewe Mouse, non ew mongungu mi uren tong, me mochen. “ Ka mochen pusi ika pwe en ngang?”

"Ewer, eni esap ina," Alice a apasa non ew mongungu mi kinamwe: "kosapw song ren. Nge inamo ua mochen upwe tongeni pwari ngonuk ach we pusi Dinah: Ua ekieki pwe kopwe angei ew fansoun ngeni pusi ika kopwe chok tongeni kuna i. I emon mettoch mi kinamwe mi fokkun achengicheng," Alice a sopweno, nesoponon ngeni pwisin i, nupwen a fiti ewe konik. ewe ekkei, a suki poun kewe me a suki mesan —me i ew mettoch mi mosonoson mi murinno ne nounou —me i emon mi fokkun kapital ren an epwe angei miis —o, ua tingor om omusomus!” a puchor sefan Alice, pun non ei fansoun ewe Mouse a uren netipengaw unusan, me a mefi pwe mi ennet pwe epwe wesewesen song. “Sisap chúen fós usun i ika ka mochen kosap fós usun.”

“Kich wesewesen!” a puchor ewe Mouse, ewe a chechech tori nesoponon an we chuk. "Ussun ita upwe kapas won ena sokkun poraus! Fansoun meinisin am famini aua oput pusi: mettoch mi ngaw, tekison, me turunufas! Kosap mut ngeniei ai upwe rongorong sefan ewe it!"

“Usap wesewesen fori!” a apasa Alice, non an fokkun mwitir ne siwini ewe porousen poraus. “Ka —ka sani —ekkewe man?” Ewe Mouse ese ponuweni, iwe Alice a sopweno ren mochen: “Mi wor emon kukkun man mi fokkun murinno arap ngeni imwach upwe mochen pwari ngonuk! Emon kukkun terrier mi saramaram mesan, ka sinei, ren oh, ena sokkun uwan mi uren uren uren! me neir —me a nom ren emon chon atake, ka sinei, me a apasa pwe mi fokkun auchea, a ukukun fitepuku paun A apasa pwe a nieno ekkewe rat meinisin me —oh achengicheng!” a puchor Alice non ew mongungu mi netipengaw, “Ua nuokus pwe ua fen osukosuka sefan!” Pun ewe Mouse a su seni i usun chok an tongeni feino, me a fori ew watten fitikoko non ewe konik nupwen a feino.

Ina minne a kokori mosonoson mwirin, “Mouse mi achengicheng! Kopwe niwin sefan, me sisap pwan kapas usun pusi ika man, ika kese sani ir!” Nupwen ewe Mouse a rongorong ei, a suki me a suki ekis sefan ngeni: mesan a fokkun pwechepwech (ren mochen, Alice a ekieki), me a apasa non ew mongungu mi tekison mi chechech, “Sipwe tori ewe sat, iwe mwirin upwe erenuk uruwoi, iwe kopwe weweiti pwata ina ngang mi oput pusi me man.”

A tori fansoun an epwe feino, pun ewe konik a fokkun uren ekkewe man me man ra turutiw non: mi wor emon Duck me emon Dodo, emon Lory me emon Eaglet, me ekkoch pwan ekkoch man mi mochen sinei. Alice a emweni ewe aan, me unusen ewe mwich ra su ngeni ewe sat.
