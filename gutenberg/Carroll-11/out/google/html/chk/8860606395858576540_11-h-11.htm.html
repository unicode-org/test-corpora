SOPUN XI.
Ié A Solá Ekkewe Tart?

Ewe King me Kiwinin Letip ra mwot won ar we nenien mwottun king nupwen ra tori, ren ew watten mwichen aramas ra chufengen unukur —sokopaten kukkun man me manmwacho meinisin, pwan unusen ewe paken katon: ewe Knave a uta me mwen meser, non sein, ren emon sounfiu won ew me ew pekin pwe epwe tumunu i; iwe arap ngeni ewe King a wor ewe Rapin mi pwechepwech, a wor ew rappwa non ew poun, me ew taropwe mi mak non ewe pwan ew poun. Non ewe chok nefinen ewe imwen kapung mi wor ew tepwe, mi wor ew watten sepien tart won: ra fokkun murinno, pwe a fori Alice epwe fokkun echik ne nenengeniir —“Ua mochen repwe fori ewe kapung,” a ekieki, “me ngeni poun ekkewe mongon apwapwa!” Nge usun ita ese wor tufichin ei, iwe a poputa ne nenengeni mettoch meinisin usun i, pwe epwe passini ewe fansoun.

Alice ese mwo fiti eu imwen kapwung me lom, nge a fen alleani porauser lon ekkewe puk, me a fokkun pwapwa le kuna pwe a silei iten arapakan meinisin mettoch mi nom ikewe ie. “Ina ewe sou-kapwun,” a apasa non netipan, “pokiten an we wig mi watte.”

Ewe soukapwüng, ren enletin, ina ewe King; me nupwen a ufouf an we crown won ewe wig, (nengeni ewe pekin taropwe ika ka mochen kuna ifa usun a fori,) ese fokkun nikinikin kinamwe, me a ennetin ese fich.

“Me ina ewe jury-box,” Alice a ekieki, “me ekkena engon me ruemon monumanau,” (a wisen apasa “monumanau,” ka kuna, pokiten ekkoch me neir ir man, me ekkoch ir man,) “Ua ekieki pwe ir ekkewe jury.” A apasa ei amuchunon kapas fan ruu ika fan unungat ngeni pwisin i, a ekis namanam tekia ren: pun a ekieki, me mi pung pwan, pwe mi chok kukkun kukkun nengin non ierin ra sinei wewen non meinisin. Iwe nge, “ekkewe mwan jury” repwe pwal ina ussun.

Ekkewe engol me ruuemon juror meinisin ra fokkun makkei poraus won ekkewe slate. “Met ra fori?” Alice a apasa ngeni ewe Grifon. “Rese tongeni wor met repwe isoni iei, me mwen ewe kapung epwe poputa.”

“Ra kan makkei iter,” ewe Gryphon a ngungures ne ponuweni, “pokiten ar nuokus pwe repwe monuki ir me mwen nesoponon ewe kapung.”

“Mettoch mi umwes!” Alice a poputa non ew mongungu mi pochokun, me song, nge a mwitir ne ukutiw, pun ewe Rapin mi pwechepwech a puchor, “Kopwe kinamwe non ewe imwen kapung!” iwe ewe King a ufouf an kewe mas me a nenengeni ren osukosuk, pwe epwe sinei io a kapas.

Alice a tongeni kuna, usun chok ika a nenengeni won pecher, pwe meinisin ekkewe juror ra makkei “mettoch mi umwes!” won ar kewe slate, me a tongeni pwan tongeni kuna pwe emon me neir ese sinei ifa usun epwe makkei “tiparoch,” me a namwot epwe tingorei chon orun we an epwe ereni. “Ew muddle mi murino ar kewe slate repwe nom non me mwen ewe kapung epwe much!” a ekieki Alice.

Emon me nein ekkewe juror a wor noun pencil mi sikesik. Iei ennetin, Alice ese tongeni uta, me a feino won ewe nenien kapung me a tori mwirin, me a fokkun mwitir kuna ew tufichin angei seni. A fori non mwitir pwe ewe kukkun juror mi woungaw (ina Bill, ewe Lizard) ese tongeni weweiti met a fis ngeni; ina minne, mwirin an kutta meinisin ren, a wisen mak ren ew chok poun ren unusen ewe ran; iwe ei a fokkun kukkun auchean, pun ese nikitano ew esisin won ewe slate.

“Herald, kopwe alleani ewe kapasen tipimwaal!” a apasa ewe King.

Non ei ewe Rapin mi pwechepwech a takiri ülüngät köl won ewe rappwa, iwe mwirin a suuki ewe taropwe mi mak seni silin man, me a älleani usun ei:—

“Ewe Kiwinin Letip, a fori ekkoch tart, .
Meinisin non ew ran summer:
Ewe Chon Angei Letip, a soläni ekkena tart, .
Iwe, a fokkun angeiir!”

Ewe King a ereni ewe mwichen soukapwüng: “Oupwe ekieki usun ämi kapwüng.”

“Ese mwo, ese mwo!” ewe Rapin a mwitir apasa. “Mi wor ew watten mettoch epwe fis me mwen ena!”

“Kopwe kori ewe aewin chon pwarata,” ewe King a apasa; iwe ewe Rapin mi pwechepwech a fiti unungat fitikoko won ewe rappwa, me a kokko, “Aewin chon pwarata!”

Ewe aewin chon pwarata ina ewe Chon Hatter. A tonong ren ew teacup non ew poun me ew kinikinin pilawa-me-butter non ewe pwan ew. “Ua tingor omusomus, om Majesty,” a poputa, “ren om uweato ekkei: nge use mwo unusen awesi ai tea nupwen ra titiei.”

Ewe King a apasa: “A fich ngeni om kopwe fen fini.” “Ineet ka poputá?”

Ewe Chon Angang a nenengeni ewe March Hare, ewe a tapweno mwirin non ewe imwen kapung, a fiti ewe Dormouse. “Ewe Foun March, ua ekieki pwe ina,” a apasa.

“Engol me limu,” a apasa ewe Hare Mars.

“Engol me wonu,” ewe Dormouse a apasa sefan.

“Makkei ena,” ewe King a apasa ngeni ewe mwichen soukapwüng, iwe, ewe mwichen soukapwüng a fokkun mochen makkei meinisin ekkewe ülüngät rän woon nöür kewe slate, iwe mwirin ra apachafengenniir, me ra aukuuku pölüwan ngeni shilling me pence.

“Kopwe angei om we hat,” ewe King a apasa ngeni ewe chon fori hat.

“Esap ai,” ewe Chon Hatter a apasa.

“ A sola! ” ewe King a puchor, me a suki ngeni ewe jury, iwe, a muttir fori eu taropween chechemeni ewe poraus.

“Ua tumunur pwe repwe amomolo,” ewe Hatter a apasa pwe epwe awewei; “Ese wor ai pwisin. Ngang emon chon fori hat.”

Iei ewe Kiwin a ufouf an kewe mas, me a poputa ne nenengeni ewe Hatter, ewe a siwin me uren fitikoko.

“Kopwe awora om pisekin pwarata,” ewe King a apasa; “me kosap niuokkus, are upwe fori pwe kopwe nielo lon ewe chok leeni.”

Ei ese usun ita epwe fokkun apochokuna ewe chon pwarata: a chok siwin seni ew pechen ngeni ewe pwan ew, a nenengeni ewe Kiwin non an ese kinamwe, me non an tipemwaramwar a atai ew watten kinikin seni an we teacup nap seni ewe pilawa-me-butter.

Non chok ei fansoun Alice a mefi ew memefin fokkun mochen sinei, minne a osukosuka i non ew napanap mi och tori an a fori met ena: a poputa ne watteno sefan, me a ekieki akkomw pwe epwe uta me tou seni ewe imwen kapung; nge non aruwen ekiek a finata pwe epwe chok nom ikewe a nom ie ukukun an epwe wor neni fan itan.

“Ua mochen pwe kosapw sikesik ina usun,” a apasa ewe Dormouse, ewe a mwot unukun. “Use tongeni asoso.”

Alice a fokkun mosonoson le apasa: “Use tongeni älisi: “Ua fen mämmääritä.”

“Ese wor om pwúúng le mááritá ikei ,” ewe Dormouse a apasa.

Alice a apasa fan pwora, “Kosap kapas chofona: ka silei pwe ka pwal watteta.”

“Ewer, nge ua maarita lon eu lapalap mi fich,” ewe Dormouse a apasa: “esap lon ena lapalap mi mwaal.” Iwe, a ütä fän song o feila ngeni ewe epek ewe imwenfel.

Non ei fansoun meinisin ewe Kiwin ese chiwen ukutiw ne nenengeni ewe Hatter, me, nupwen chok ewe Dormouse a pwerelong non ewe imwen kapung, a apasa ngeni emon ekkewe ofesin ewe imwen kapung, “Kopwe uwei ngeniei ewe list ren ekkewe chon kon non ewe amuchunon kon!” won minne ewe Hatter mi riaffou a fokkun chechech, pwe a suki an kewe ruu sus.

Ewe King a apasasefäli fän song, “Kopwe awora om pisekin ännet, ika esap ina, upwe ninniilo, ese lifilifil ika ka niuokkus are kese niuokkus.”

“Ngang emon mwan mi woungaw, om Majesty,” ewe Chon Fori Hatter a poputa, non ew mongungu mi chechech, “—me use mwo poputa ai tea —ese nap seni ew wiik ika nap seni —me met ren ewe pilawa-me-butter a fokkun ititiw —me ewe fifiin ewe tea —”

“Ewe twin ewe met? ” a apasa ewe King.

“A poputa ren ewe tea,” ewe Hatter a ponuweni.

“Ewer, ngingi a poputa ren eu T!” a apasa ewe King fan tipemwaramwar. "Ka ekieki pwe ngang emon chon dunce? Kopwe sopweno!"

“Ngang emon mwan mi woungaw,” ewe Chon Fori Hatter a sopweno, “me chommong mettoch ra ttur mwirin ena —ewe chok March Hare a apasa —”

“Use fen fori!” ewe Hare March a apasa non ew watten mwitir.

“Ka fen fori!” a apasa ewe Chon Angang.

“Ua amam!” a apasa ewe Hare Mars.

“A amam,” ewe King a apasa: “kopwe likitalo ena kinikin.”

“Ewer, ese nifinifin, ewe Dormouse a apasa—” ewe Hatter a sopweno, a nenengeni ren osukosuk pwe epwe kuna ika epwe pwan amam: nge ewe Dormouse ese amam och mettoch, a fokkun annut.

“Mwirin ena,” ewe Hatter a sopweno, “ua pwan suki ekkoch pilawa-me-butter —”

“Nge met ewe Dom a apasa?” emon me nein ekkewe jury a eisini.

“Pwe use tongeni chechemeni,” ewe Hatter a apasa.

Ewe King a apasa: “ Kopwe chechemeni, ika esap ina, upwe fori pwe kopwe ninniilo.”

Ewe Hatter mi osukosuk a aturanong an we kap tea me pilawa-me-butter, me a tonong won ew pechen. A poputa ne apasa, “Ngang emon mwan mi woungaw, om Samol.”

Ewe King a apasa: “En emon chon kapas mi fokkun woungaw .”

Iei emon me nein ekkewe guinea-pig a cheer, me a mwitir ne suki me ren ekkewe ofesin ewe imwen kapung. (Pokiten ina ew kapas mi kon weires, upwe chok awewei ngonuk ifa usun a fis. A wor rer ew watten pachen kanvas, minne a riri ngeni ewe puun ren rong: non ei ra suki ewe guinea-pig, mokuran akkom, me mwirin ra mwot won.)

Alice a ekieki: “Ua pwapwa pwe ua kúna ena mettóch.” “Ua fen fokkun anneani non ekkewe chassi, non nesoponon ekkewe kapung, “A wor ekkoch sossotun applause, minne a mwitir ne pinepin me ren ekkewe ofesin ewe imwen kapung,” me use mwo weweiti met wewen tori ikenai.”

“Ika ina chok met ka sinei usun, kopwe tongeni uta fan,” ewe King a sopweno.

“Use tongeni feino ese wor,” ewe Hatter a apasa: “Ua nom won ewe puun, usun chok a nom.”

Ewe King a pölüeni, “Iwe, kopwe tongeni mottiu .”

Iei ewe emon guinea-pig a cheer, me a suki.

“Kopwe feito, pwe a wes ekkewe chon fiti!” a ekieki Alice. “Iei sipwe tongeni murinnolo.”

“Upwe fen awesi ai tea,” ewe Hatter a apasa, ren an nenengeni ewe Kiwin mi osukosuk, ewe a anneani ewe list ren ekkewe chon kon.

“Ka tongeni feino,” ewe King a apasa, me ewe Hatter a mwitir tou seni ewe imwen kapung, ese mwo witiwit an epwe ufouf an kewe sus.

“—me kopwe chok angei mokuran me nukun,” ewe Kiwin a apasa ngeni emon ekkewe ofes: nge ewe Hatter a tou seni mesen aramas me mwen ewe ofes epwe tongeni tori ewe asam.

“Kopwe kori ewe chon pwarata mwirin!” a apasa ewe King.

Ewe chon pwarata mwirin ina ewe chon kuk an ewe Duchess. A uwei ewe pepper-box non poun, me Alice a ekieki ika io ena, pwan mwo me mwen an epwe tonong non ewe nenien kapung, ren ewe napanap ekkewe aramas unukun ewe asam ra poputa ne sniff non ew chok fansoun.

Ewe King a apasa: “Kopwe awora om pisekin pwarata.”

“Esapw,” ewe chon kuk a apasa.

Ewe King a nenengeni ewe Rapin mi pwechepwech ren an osukosuk, ewe a apasa non mongungu mi tekison, “Om we Majesty epwe kapas eis ngeni ei chon pwarata.”

“Ewer, ika pwe mi namwot, mi namwot,” ewe King a apasa, ren ew ngunun netipengaw, me, mwirin an a suki poun me a suki mesan ngeni ewe chon kuk tori mesan a arapakan epwe tou seni mesan, a apasa non ew mongungu mi uren netipengaw, “Met ekkewe tart ra for seni?”

“Peper, lap ngeni,” ewe chon kuk a apasa.

“Treacle,” a apasa eu mongungu mi annut mwirin.

“Kola ena Dormouse,” ewe Kiwin a puchor. "Kopwe atai mokuren ena Dormouse! Kopwe angei ena Dormouse seni ewe imwen kapung! Kopwe akkufu! Kopwe atai! Kopwe angeiano ren an kewe uwan!"

Ren ekkoch minich unusen ewe imwen kapung a osukosuk, a fori ewe Dormouse epwe tou, me, non ewe fansoun ra fen mwokut sefan, ewe chon kuk a fen morono.

"Ina chek!" a apasa ewe King, ren ew memefin watten kinamwe. “Kopwe kori ewe chon pwarata mwirin.” Iwe a apasa sefan non ew mongungu mi tekison ngeni ewe Kiwin, "Ennet, ai achengicheng, kopwe kapas eis ngeni ewe chon pwarata mwirin. A fokkun fori ai upwe metek mokurei!"

Alice a nenengeni ewe Rapin mi pwechepwech nupwen a futuk won ewe list, a mefi pwe a fokkun mochen kuna met ewe chon pwarata mwirin epwe usun, “—pun rese mwo angei chommong pisekin pwarata ,” a apasa ngeni pwisin i. Ekieki mwo an mairu, nupwen ewe Rapin mi pwechepwech a anneani, non ewe nesoponon an mongungu mi kukkun mi ngingioch, ewe it “Alice!”
