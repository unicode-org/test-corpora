SOPUN IV.
Ewe Rapin a tinato ew kukkun bill .

Ina ewe Rapin mi pwechepwech, a trot ekis sefan sefan, me a nenengeni ren osukosuk nupwen a feino, usun ita a pout seni och mettoch; me a rongorong an a ngungures ngeni pwisin i “Ewe Duchess! Ewe Duchess! O ai kewe paws mi achengicheng! O ai kewe puun me uwan! Epwe fori ai upwe nieno, usun chok ekkewe manmwacho ir manmwacho! Ia upwe tongeni aturanong ir ia, ua ekieki?” Alice a ekieki non ew fansoun pwe a kutta ewe fan me ewe pekin glove mi pwechepwech, me a fokkun murino ne poputa ne kutta ir, nge rese nomw ia repwe kuna —mettoch meinisin ra usun ita ra siwin seni an a su non ewe konik, me ewe watten imw, ren ewe tepwe mi for seni glass me ewe kukkun asam mi unusoch, a fen siwin.

Ekiselo chok ewe Rapin a kuna Alice, nupwen a feino kutta man, me a kokori non ew mongungu mi song, "Pwata, Mary Ann, met ka fori me nukun ikei? Kopwe su ngeni imwom non ei fansoun, me uwei ngeniei ew pekin glove me ew fan! Mwitir, iei!" Iwe, Alice a fokkun niuokkus pwe a su seni fan eu chok lon ewe leeni a aiti ngeni, nge ese sotun awewei ewe mwaal a fori.

“A angeiei pwe upwe noun chon angang fefin,” a apasa ngeni pwisin i nupwen a su. “Epwe fokkun mairu lupwen epwe silei pwe ngang io!Nge epwe fen murinno ai upwe uwei ngeni fan me an kewe glove —weween, ika upwe tongeni kuna.” Nupwen a apasa ei, a tori ew kukkun imw mi nimenimoch, won asamen a wor ew seleich mi for seni piras mi saramaram mi mak ewe it “W. RABBIT,” won. A tonong nge ese fichifich, me a mwitir feita won ewe asam, non watten nuokus pwe epwe churi ewe wesewesen Mary Ann, me epwe niwinsefan seni ewe imw me mwen an epwe kuna ewe fan me ekkewe glove.

Alice a apasa ngeni pwisin i, “A ifa me amwararen ai upwe feilo poraus fan iten emon rabbit! Ua ekieki pwe Dinah epwe tinato poraus ngeniei mwirin!” Iwe a poputa ne ekieki usun ewe sokkun mettoch epwe fis: “‘Miss Alice! ‘Kopwe feito non ew minich, nurse! Nge upwe kuna pwe ewe mouse ese tou.’ Nge ngang use ekieki,” Alice a sopweno, “pwe repwe mut ngeni Dinah an epwe uta non ewe imw ika epwe poputa ne order aramas usun ena!”

Non ei fansoun a fen kuna an aan ngeni ew kukkun rum mi nimenimoch ren ew tepwe non ewe asam, me won (usun met a apinukunuk) ew fan me ruu ika unungat pekin kukkun glove mi pwechepwech: a angei ewe fan me ew pekin glove, me a chok mochen tou seni ewe rum, nupwen mesan a turutiw won ew kukkun bottle mi nom unukun ewe look-glass mi uta. Ese wor ew taropwe non ei fansoun mi mak ekkewe kapas “UNU ME,” nge ina mwo a suuki ewe cork me a apacha ngeni awan. “Ua sinei pwe och mettoch mi amwarar epwe wesewesen fis,” a apasa ngeni pwisin i, “iteiten fansoun ua mongo are unumi och mettoch; ina minne upwe chok kuna met ei bottle a fori. Ua apinukunuk pwe epwe fori ai upwe watte sefan, pun ennetin ua fokkun netipengaw ren ai upwe emon kukkun mettoch mi kukkun!”

A wesewesen fori, me a fokkun mwitir nap seni met a ekieki: me mwen an epwe unumi esopw ewe bottle, a kuna pwe mokuran a suki ngeni ewe siling, me a namwot epwe chapetiw pwe epwe amanaua nepekin seni an epwe turutiw. A mwitir ne isoni ewe bottle, a apasa ngeni pwisin i “A fokkun naf —Ua nuku pwe usapw chiwen watteno —Usun met a fis, use tongeni tou non ewe asam —Ua mochen pwe use fokkun unumi sakaw!”

Feiengau! a fen tori fansoun an epwe mochen ena! A sopweno ne watteno, me watteno, me a fokkun mwitir ne chapetiw won ewe puun: non pwan ew minich ese mwo wor nenien ei, me a sotuni ewe efeiengawen an epwe mwokut ren ew elbow ngeni ewe asam, me ewe pwan ew poun a kurufat won mokuran. Nge a chok sopweno ne watteno, me, usun ew amuchunon pisekin aninis, a wanong ew poun seni ewe asam, me ew pechen won ewe chimney, me a apasa ngeni pwisin i “Iei use chiwen tongeni fori, met chok epwe fis. Met epwe fis ngeniei?”

Luckily ren Alice, ewe kukkun bottle mi magic a fen iei unusen an pochokun, me ese watteno: ina mwo a fokkun ese kinamwe, me, usun ese wor sokkun tufichin an epwe tongeni tou seni ewe rum sefan, ese mwaal a mefi ese pwapwa.

“A fokkun apwapwa non imw,” a ekieki Alice mi woungaw, “nupwen emon ese kan watteno me kukkun, me a kan annuku ren miis me rabbit. Ua arapakan mochen pwe usapw tonong non ena rabbit-hole —me inamo —me inamo —a kon amwarar ngeniei, ka sinei met upwe tongeni fori! a kan anneani ekkewe porausen uruwo, ua ekieki pwe ena sokkun mettoch ese mwo fis, me iei ua nom non ew! “ese wor nenien an epwe chiwen watteta ikei .”

“Nge mwirin,” Alice a ekieki, “usapw chiwen chinnapeno nap seni ai upwe iei? Ina epwe ew aururu, ew aan —usapw chiwen emon fefin chinnap —nge mwirin —fansoun meinisin epwe wor ai kewe lesen upwe kaeo!O, usapw sani ena! ”

“O, en mi tiparoch Alice!” a pwisin pölüweni. “Ifa usun kopwe tongeni kaeo lesen lon ei leeni? Pwata, ese kon lien wor leeni fan itom , me ese fokkun wor leeni fan iten och puken lesen!”

Iwe, a sopweilo, a akkomw angei eu pekin me mwirin ewe epek, me a fori eu porausfengen mi fokkun watte; nge mwirin fitu minich a rongorong ew mongungu me nukun, me a uta pwe epwe ausening.

“Meri Ann!Meri Ann!” a apasa ewe mongungu. “Kopwe uwei ngeniei ai kewe glove non ei fansoun!” Mwirin a feito ekis ngingiin pechen aramas won ewe step. Alice a sinei pwe ina ewe Rapin a feito kutta i, me a chechech tori a suki ewe imw, a fokkun monuki pwe iei a ukukun fite puku fansoun watte seni ewe Rapin, me ese wor popun an epwe nuokus ren.

Iei ewe Rapin a feito ren ewe asam, me a achocho ne suuki; nge, nupwen ewe asam a suk ngeni non, me an Alice we elbow a pochokun ne suki ngeni, ena achocho a pwarata pwe ese fis. Alice a rongorong an apasa ngeni pwisin i “Iwe upwe round me tonong non ewe asam.”

“ Pwe kosap mochen!” ekieki Alice, me, mwirin an witiwit tori a fangeta pwe a rongorong ewe Rapin chok fan ewe asam, a mwitir aitieta poun, me a fori ew sniff non ewe asepwan. Ese angei och mettoch, nge a rongorong ew kukkun puchor me ew turutiw, me ew turutiwen glass mi tatakis, seni minne a ekieki pwe mi chok tufich pwe a turutiw non ew cucumber-frame, ika och mettoch mi usun chok.

Mwirin a feito ew mongungu mi song﻿—an ewe Rapin﻿—“Pat!Pat!Ia ka nom ia?” Iwe mwirin ew mongungu ese mwo rongorong me nom, “Ennet iwe ngang mi nom ikei!

“A wor apel, enlet!” a apasa ewe Rapin non song. “Iei! Kopwe feito me anisiei seni ei! ” (A wor mongungun pwan ekkoch glass mi tatakis.)

“Iei kopwe ereniei, Pat, met ena non ewe asam?”

“Ennet, ina ew arm, yer sufon!” (A apasa “arrum.”)

"Ew poun, en goose! Io a fen kuna ew mi ina ukukun? Pwata, a uren unusen ewe asam!"

“Ennet, a fori, yer sufon: nge ina ew poun ren meinisin ena.”

“Ewer, ese wor an business ikewe, ese nifinifin: kopwe feino me angei!”

A wor ew fansoun kinamwe mi chou mwirin ei, me Alice a chok tongeni rongorong kapasen ngungures iei me mwirin; usun, “Enlet, use sani, yer sufon, ese wor, ese wor!” “Föri usun üa ürenuk, en chon niuokus!” iwe mwirin a aitieta poun sefan, me a fori pwan ew sniff non ewe asepwan. Ei fansoun a wor ruu kukkun ngingi, me pwan chommong mongungun ekkewe glass mi tatakis. “A ifa me chommongun ekkewe kukun-fraim epwe wor!” a ekieki Alice. "Ua ekieki met repwe fori mwirin! Ren ar repwe angeiei seni ewe asam, ua chok mochen pwe repwe tongeni! Ua nuku pwe use chiwen mochen nom non ei!"

A witiwit ren och fansoun nge ese rongorong och mettoch: soposopono a feito ew ngungures ren kukkun wiil, me mongungun chommong mongungu mi murinno meinisin ra kapas fengen: a fori ekkewe kapas: “Ia ewe pwan ew ladder?—Pwata, use mochen upwe uwei nge ew; konak —Ewer, ririfengenniir akkom —rese mwo tori esopw tekia —Oupwe fori och mettoch mi murinno —Iei, Bill epwe amwochu ei sepi? —Ekieki pwe ewe slate mi loos epwe feitiw! (ew mongungu mi pochokun)—“Iei, io a fori ena?—Iei Bill, ua ekieki—Io epwe tonong non ewe nenien fitikoko?—Ewer, usapw ! En kopwe fori!— Pwe ngang usapw, iwe!—Bill epwe tonong non ewe nenien fitikoko!”Iei, Bill! ewe masta a apasa pwe kopwe tonong non ewe nenien fitikoko!”

“O!Iwe Bill epwe ne feitiw won ewe chim, ika met?” a apasa Alice ngeni pwisin i. “Sy, ra usun ita ra wanong mettoch meinisin won Bill!Usap nom non nenien Bill ren ew angang mi och: ei nenien ekkei mi kukkun, pwe upwe nukuchar; nge ua ekieki pwe upwe tongeni ekis kick!"

A suki pechen ngeni ewe chimney ukukun an tongeni, me a witiwit tori a rongorong ew kukkun man (ese tongeni ekieki ika met sokkun) a suki me a suki me a suki non ewe chimney mi arap ngeni: mwirin, a apasa ngeni pwisin i “Iei Bill,” a ngeni ew kick mi fitikoko, me epwe witiwit pwe epwe kuna met epwe fis mwirin.

Ewe aewin mettoch a rongorong ina ew chorus mi nap “A wor Bill!” mwirin mongungun ewe Rapin a fiti —“Kopwe angei i, en ren ewe chuk!” mwirin kinamwe, me mwirin pwan ew osukosuken mongungu —“Kopwe amwochu mokuran —Brandy iei —Kosapw chok suki —Ifa usun, chinnap? Met a fis ngonuk? Kopwe erenikem meinisin usun!”

Mwirin a feito ew mongungu mi apwangapwang, mi ngungures, (“Ina Bill,” Alice a ekieki,) “Ewer, use kon sinei —Ese chiwen wor, kinisou; Ua fen murino iei —nge ngang mi kon menemenoch ai upwe erenuk —met chok ua sinei, och mettoch a feito rei usun emon Jack-non-e-we-box usun ew skyro-!”

“Iwe ka fori, chinnap!” ra apasa ekkewe ekkoch.

“Sipwe keni ewe imw!” a apasa mongungun ewe Rapin; me Alice a kokko ren ukukun an tongeni, “Ika kopwe fori, upwe seti Dinah ngonuk!”

A wor ew kinamwe mi mano non chok ena fansoun, me Alice a ekieki non netipan, “Ua ekieki met repwe fori mwirin!Ika pwe mi wor ar ekiek, repwe angei ewe roof.” Mwirin ew ika ruu minich, ra poputa ne mwokut sefan, me Alice a rongorong ewe Ranin apasa, “Ew barrowful epwe fori, ne poputa.”

Alice a ekieki: “Eú chénúpupu mi chéúfetál? nge ese mwo fansoun nangatam an epwe tipemwaramwar, pun non ewe fansoun mwirin ew pwichikar ren kukkun fau a feito ra rat non ewe asam, me ekkoch me neir ra tori mesan. “Upwe awesi ei,” a apasa ngeni pwisin i, me a puchor, “Epwe murinno ika kosap fori ena sefan!” minne a efisata pwal eu kinamwe mi malo.

Alice a kuna ren ekkoch mairu pwe ekkewe fau mi kukkun meinisin ra siwin ngeni kukkun kek nupwen ra nom won ewe puun, me ew ekiek mi saramaram a feito non mokuran. “Ika upwe mongo ew me nein ekkei kek,” a ekieki, “epwe fokkun fori och siwinin ai sais; me pokiten ese tongeni fori ai upwe watte, epwe fori ai upwe kukkun, ua ekieki.”

Ina popun a únúmi eú me lein ekkewe kek, me a pwapwa le kúna pwe a poputá le chék chéúfetál. Nupwen a chok kukkun an epwe tonong non ewe asam, a su seni ewe imw, me a kuna chommong kukkun man me man ra witiwit lukun. Ewe kukkun Lizard mi woungaw, Bill, a nom non ewe nesen, a suki me ren ruemon guinea-pig, ra ngeni och mettoch seni ew bottle. Ir meinisin ra fori ew rush ngeni Alice non ewe fansoun a pwato; nge a su seni ukukun an tongeni, me mwitir chok a kuna pwe a tumun non ew chuk mi uren puun.

“Ewe aewin mettoch upwe fori,” Alice a apasa ngeni pwisin i, nupwen a fetan fetan non ewe ira, “pwe upwe marita sefan ngeni ukukun mi fich ngeniei; me ewe aruwen mettoch upwe kutta ai aan ngeni ena atake mi amwarar. Ua ekieki pwe epwe ina ewe kokkot mi murino.”

A usun ew kokkot mi murinno, ese wor tipemwaramwar, me a fokkun nimenimoch me mecheres an kokkot; ewe chok weires, pwe ese wor an ekiek mi kukkun ifa usun epwe poputa ne fori; me nupwen a nenengeni ren osukosuk nein ekkewe ira, ew kukkun chuk mi fitikoko mi chok nom won mokuran a fori an epwe nenengeni non ew watten mwitir.

Emon kukkun kukkun man a nenengeni fan ren mesan kewe mi watte mi rokopwanieno, me a apwangapwang a aitieta ew paw, a achocho ne atapa. “Ewe kukkun mettoch!” a apasa Alice, non ew mongungu mi kouk, me a achocho ne fiti ngeni; nge a fokkun niuokus fansoun meinisin ren ewe ekiek pwe epwe tongeni echik, non ena fansoun epwe fokkun tongeni mongo i ina mwo ika meinisin an kewe kouk.

Ese kon sinei met a fori, a angei ew kukkun chuk, me a aiti ngeni ewe kukkun; iwe ewe kukkun a su ngeni ewe asepwan seni pechen kewe meinisin non ew chok fansoun, ren ew ngungures ren pwapwa, me a suki ewe chuk, me a fori an epwe nuku pwe epwe osukosuk ren; mwirin Alice a su seni mwirin ew watten chuk, pwe epwe tumunu pwe esapw tori; me ewe fansoun a pwa non ewe pwan ew pekin, ewe kukkun a fori pwan ew rush ngeni ewe chuk, me a turutiw mokuran won pechen non an mwitir ne angei; iwe Alice, a ekieki pwe a fokkun usun an epwe wor ew minen urumot ren emon cart-horse, me a ekieki pwe iteitan fansoun epwe turutiw fan pechen, a suki ewe ira sefan; iwe ewe kukkun a poputa ew mwichen charges mi mwochomwoch won ewe chuk, a suno ew aan mi fokkun kukkun ngeni mwach iteitan fansoun me ew aan mi towaw ngeni mwirin, me a ngungures hoarsely non unusen ewe fansoun, tori soposopono a mwot ew neni mi och, a ngungures, ren an tong a suki seni awan, me mesan kewe mi watte ra nesoponon.

Ei a usun ngeni Alice ew fansoun mi och ren an epwe fori an epwe su; ina minne a poputa non ew chok fansoun, me a su tori an a fokkun apwangapwang me ese chiwen wor an ngasangas, me tori an ewe puppy bark a fokkun ngingioch non towaw.

“Nge inamo, ifa usun ew kukkun puppy mi achengicheng!” a apasa Alice, nupwen a anongonong won ew buttercup pwe epwe asoso, me a fan inisin ren ew me nein ekkewe foun ira: “Upwe fen fokkun sani ai upwe aiti ngeni tricks, ika —ika pwe ngang mi chok ewe ukukun mi fich ai upwe fori! mongo ika unumi och mettoch ika pwan ekkoch nge ewe kapas eis mi nap, met?”

Ewe kapas eis mi nap mi ennet pwe, met? Alice a nenengeni unukun ekkewe foun ira me ekkewe foun ira, nge ese kuna och mettoch mi usun chok ewe mettoch mi fich an epwe mongo are unumi fan ekkewe napanap. A wor eu watten mushroom a mar arap ngeni, arapakan chok tekian usun chok pwisin i; iwe nupwen a nenengeni fan, me won pekin me pekin, me mwirin, a fis ngeni ekiekin pwe epwe tongeni pwan nenengeni me kuna met mi nom won unungen.

A aitieta inisin won tiptoe, me a nenengeni won ewe puun, me mesan a mwitir churi mesen emon watten puun mi pwechepwech, ewe a mwot won ewe puun ren poun kewe mi suki, a kinamwe ne fiti ew hookah mi langatam, me ese angei och kukkun ekiek usun i ika och mettoch.
