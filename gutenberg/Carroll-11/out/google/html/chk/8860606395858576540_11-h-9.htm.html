SOPUN IX.
Pworaus usun ewe Mokkot .

“Kose tongeni ekieki ifa usun ai pwapwa ne kuna sefan en, en mettochun chinnap mi achengicheng!” a apasa ewe Duchess, nupwen a suki poun ren tong ngeni poun Alice, me ra fetan fengen.

Alice a fokkun pwapwa ne kuna i non ena sokkun song mi apwapwa, me a ekieki non netipan pwe eni ina chok ewe pekin a fori an epwe fokkun safei nupwen ra chufengen non ewe nenien mongo.

“Nupwen ngang emon Duchess,” a apasa ngeni pwisin i, (esap non ew mongungu mi fokkun apinukunuk ina mwo ), “Esapw wor ai pepper non ai we kichen . camomile mi fori ar repwe uren song —me —me barley-sugar me ekkena sokkun mettoch mi fori semirit mi uren song ua chok mochen aramas repwe sinei ena : iwe resap kon sikesik usun, ka sinei —”

A fen fokkun monuki ewe Duchess non ei fansoun, me a ekis mairu nupwen a rongorong mongungun a arap ngeni selingan. “Ka ekieki usun och mettoch, pwipwi, me ena a fori om kopwe monuki om kopwe kapas.Use tongeni erenuk iei chok met ewe moral ren ena, nge upwe chechemeni non ekis fansoun.”

Alice a pwora le apasa: “Eli ese wor eü.”

“Tut, tut, semirit!” a apasa ewe Duches. “Mettoch meinisin mi wor ar moral, ika chok ka tongeni kuna.” Iwe a suki inisin pwe epwe arap ngeni pechen Alice nupwen a kapas.

Alice ese kon sani an epwe fokkun arap ngeni: akkom, pokiten ewe Duchess a fokkun ngaw; me oruwan, pokiten a fokkun pwung tekian an epwe asoso an we chinnap won pechen Alice, me ina ew chinnap mi fitikoko ese kinamwe. Iwe nge, ese sani an epwe chék chéúfetál, ina popun a likiitú fán úkúkún an tufich.

“Ewe game a kon murinno iei,” a apasa, ren an epwe ekis sopweno ewe poraus.

“‘Iei usun,” ewe Duchess a apasa: “me ewe nongonongen ena ina —‘O, ‘iei tong, ‘iei tong, minne a fori ewe fonufan epwe pwellifeili!’”

“Emon a apasa,” Alice a apasa, “pwe a fis ren aramas meinisin ra ekieki pwisin ar kewe angang!”

“Ah, iei! A wewe ngeni usun chok,” ewe Duchess a apasa, a suki an we kukkun chinnap mi fitikoko ngeni pechen Alice nupwen a apasa, “me ewe nongonongen ena ina —‘Kopwe tumunu ewe memef, me ekkewe mongungu repwe pwisin tumunur.’”

“A ifa me watteen an pwapwaiti an epwe kuna foffor mi murinno lon mettoch!” Alice a ekieki lon letipan.

“Ua pwora le apasa pwe ka ekieki pwata use äeä pöüi le äeä pöüm,” ewe Duchess a apasa mwirin an asoso: “ewe popun, pwe ua tipemwaramwar usun songün noum we flamingo. Upwe sotuni ewe sossot?”

“Epwe tongeni fiti,” Alice a polueni fan tumunuoch, ese fokkun mefi osukosuk pwe epwe sotuni ewe sossot.

“A fokkun enlet,” ewe Duchess a apasa: “ekkewe flamingo me mustard ra kan fitikoko.

Alice a apasa: “Ewe chok mustard esap emon man.”

“Pwungun, usun chok fansoun meinisin,” ewe Duchess a apasa: “a ifa me fatafatochun om napanap ne isetiw mettoch!”

Alice a apasa: “Ina eú minen álillis, ua ekieki .”

“Ewer mi ina,” a apasa ewe Duchess, ewe a usun ita a mmonleta le tipeeu ngeni mettoch meinisin Alice a apasa; “mi wor ew watten nenien mustard arap ngeni ikei. Iwe ewe nongonongen ena ina —‘Ika a chommong ai kewe, epwe kisino om kewe.’”

“O, ua sinei!” a apasa Alice, ewe ese fiti ei amuchunon kapas, “ina ew vegetable.Ese usun ew, nge ina.”

“Ua fokkun tipeeu ngonuk,” ewe Duchess a apasa; “me ewe nongonongen ena ina —‘Kopwe usun met ka usun ita kopwe winiti’ —ika ika ka mochen epwe kon mecheres —‘Kosapw ekieki pwe kosapw usun chok met epwe tongeni pwa ngeni ekkoch pwe met ka nomw non ika kopwe tongeni winiti ese pwan usun met ka fen usun epwe pwa ngenir pwe kopwe usun chok.’”

“Ua ekieki pwe upwe weweiti ena,” Alice a apasa non fokkun sufon, “ika pwe upwe makkei: nge use tongeni unusen tapweno mwirin usun ka apasa.”

“Ese wor met upwe tongeni apasa ika upwe finata,” ewe Duchess a ponuweni, non ew mongungu mi pwapwa.

“Iotek kosap osukosuk ren om kopwe apasa nap seni ena,” Alice a apasa.

“O, kosap kapas usun osukosuk!” a apasa ewe Duches. “Ua fori ngonuk ew niffang ren meinisin met ua fen apasa tori ikenai.”

“Eú sókkun liffang mi chepetek!” a ekieki Alice. “Ua pwapwa pwe rese kan ngeni aramas liffang fan iten ranin uputiu ussun chok ena!” Nge ese pwora le apasa ena fän leüwommong.

“Ka ekieki sefan?” ewe Duchess a eisini, ren pwan ew suki an we kukkun chinnap mi fitikoko.

“Mi wor ai pwúúng le ekieki,” Alice a apasa fán péchékkúl, pún a poputá le ekis meefi osukosuk.

“A chok usun chok pwungun,” ewe Duchess a apasa, “usun chok ekkewe pik repwe fiti; me ewe m—”

Nge ikei, ren an Alice fokkun mairu, mongungun ewe Duchess a mano, pwan mwo non ewe nefinen an kapas mi sani ‘moral,’ me ewe poun mi riri ngeni poun a poputa ne chechech. Alice a nenengeni, me ikewe ie ewe Kiwin a uta me mwen meser, a suki poun kewe, a uren netipengaw usun ew fitikoko.

“Eu ran mi murinno, om we Samol!” ewe Duchess a poputa non ew mongungu mi tekison me apwangapwang.

“Iei, ua ngonuk kapasen ourour mi pung,” a puchor ewe Kiwin, a suki ewe puun nupwen a kapas; “ika en are mokurom epwe tou, me ena non ukukun esopw fansoun! Kopwe finata!”

Ewe Duchess a angei an finata, me a feino non ew chok fansoun.

“Sipwe sopweno ren ewe urumot,” ewe Kiwin a apasa ngeni Alice; me Alice a fokkun nuokus an epwe apasa ew kapas, nge ekis me ekis a tapweno mwirin ngeni ewe nenien kroket.

Ekkewe ekkoch chon kuno ra fen angei feiochun an ewe Kiwin ese nom, me ra asoso non ewe saram: nge, ewe fansoun ra kuna i, ra mwitir niwin ngeni ewe urumot, ewe Kiwin a chok apasa pwe ekis chok fansoun epwe atai manauer.

Non unusen ewe fansoun ra fiti ewe Queen ese chiwen ukutiw ne anini ngeni ekkewe ekkoch chon fiti, me a puchor “Off ren mokuran!” are “Off ren mokuran!” Ekkewe a apungu ra uwei ngeni imwen fotek ren ekkewe sounfiu, ekkewe mi ennet pwe repwe nikitano ar repwe arch pwe repwe fori ei, pwe non nesoponon esopw awa ika nap seni ese chiwen wor arch, me meinisin ekkewe chon urumot, tiwenon chok ewe King, ewe Kiwin, me Alice, ra nom non imwen kanapus me fan kapung ren ninnino.

Mwirin ewe Kiwin a ukutiw, a fokkun uren ngasangas, me a apasa ngeni Alice, “Ka fen kuna ewe Mokutukut?”

Alice a apasa: “Ewer. “Use mwo nge sinei met ew Mokutukut.”

“Iei ewe mettoch ewe Soup Mok Turtle a for seni,” ewe Kiwin a apasa.

“Use mwo kuna emon, are rongorong usun emon,” Alice a apasa.

“Iwe, kopwe feito,” ewe Kiwin a apasa, “iwe epwe erenuk uruon we.”

Nupwen ra fetan fengen, Alice a rongorong ewe King a apasa non mongungu mi tekison, ngeni ewe kompani unusan, “Ami meinisin oua omusano.” “Kopwe feito, ina ew mettoch mi och!” a apasa ngeni pwisin i, pun a fen mefi pwe ese fokkun pwapwa ren chochon ekkewe chon nieno aramas ewe Kiwin a annuku.

Ra fokkun mwitir tori emon Gryphon, a annut non ewe akkar. (Ika kese sinei met ew Gryphon, nengeni ewe sasing.) “Up, mettoch mi lamalam tekia!” a apasa ewe Kiwin, “me uwei ei fopun pwe epwe kuna ewe Mock Turtle, me rongorong uruon we. Upwe niwin sefan me kuna mwirin ekkoch ninnino ua fen annuku ngeni;” me a feino, a nikitano Alice chok ren ewe Grifon. Alice ese fokkun sani napanapen ewe monun nom, nge non unusan a ekieki pwe epwe fokkun safe an epwe nom ren usun an epwe feino mwirin ena Queen mi ngaw: ina minne a witiwit.

Ewe Gryphon a mwot me a suki mesan kewe: iwe a nenengeni ewe Kiwin tori a tou seni mesan: iwe a chuchchuri. “A ifa me pwapwa!” a apasa ewe Grifon, esopw ngeni pwisin i, esopw ngeni Alice.

“ Met ewe pwapwa?” a apasa Alice.

“Pwata, i ,” a apasa ewe Grifon. “Iei meinisin an fansoun, pwe: rese kan nieno emon, ka sinei. Kopwe feito!”

“Meinisin ra apasa ‘kopwe feito!’. ikei,” a ekieki Alice, nupwen a feino ekis mwirin: “Use mwo kuna ew fansoun ua fokkun order usun non unusen manauei, use mwo!”

Rese mwo feino towaw me mwen ra kuna ewe Mock Turtle non towaw, a mwot non netipengaw me pwisin nom won ew kukkun chuk mi uren fau, me, nupwen ra arap ngeni, Alice a tongeni rongorong an ngungures usun ita netipan epwe tatakis. A fókkun kirekiréch ngeni. “Met an netipengaw?” a eisini ewe Grifon, me ewe Grifon a ponuweni, a fokkun arapakan ngeni ekkewe chok kapas usun chok me nom, “Iei meinisin an fansoun, pwe: ese wor an netipengaw, ka sinei. Kopwe feito!”

Ina minne ra feitä ren ewe Mock Turtle, ewe a nennengeniir ren mesan kewe mi watte mi ur ren chönün mas, nge ese apasa och mettoch.

“Ei fopun ikei,” ewe Gryphon a apasa, “a mochen pwe epwe sinei uruwom, a fori.”

“Upwe ereni i,” ewe Mock Turtle a apasa non ew mongungu mi fatafatoch, me uren fitikoko: “oupwe mwot, ami me ruemon, me ousapw apasa ew kapas tori ai upwe wes.”

Ina popun ra mwot, me ese wor emon a kapas ren fitu minich. Alice a ekieki non netipan, “Use kuna ifa usun epwe tongeni awesi, ika ese poputa.” Nge a witiwit fán mosonottam.

“Fan ew,” ewe Mokutukut a apasa non soponon, ren ew watten ngungures, “Ngang emon wesewesen Mokutukut.”

Ekkei kapas ra fiti ew kinamwe mi fokkun nangatam, a chok ataieno ren ew puchor fan ekkoch “Hjckrrh!” seni ewe Grifon, me ewe mongungu mi chou fansoun meinisin an ewe Mok. Alice a fokkun arapakan ne uta me apasa, “Kilisou, samol, ren om poraus mi amwarar,” nge ese tongeni an epwe ekieki pwe epwe wor pwan ekkoch repwe feito, ina minne a chok mwokut me ese apasa och mettoch.

“Nupwen am aua kukkun,” ewe Mokutukut a sopweno ne soposopono, a kon kinamwe, ina mwo ika a chuen ekis kechiw iei me mwirin, “aua fiti sukun non ewe sat.

“Pwata ka eita ngeni Torto, ika esap i emon?” Alice a eisini.

“Am aua eita ngeni Torta pokiten a aiti ngenikem,” ewe Mokutukut a apasa ren song: “enletin ka fokkun uren sikesik!”

“Mi namwot kopwe niuokus ren om kopwe eisini ena sokkun kapas eis mi mecheres,” ewe Gryphon a apasa sefan; iwe mwirin ir me ruemon ra mwot non kinamwe me nenengeni Alice mi woungaw, ewe a mefi pwe a ammonata an epwe tonong non ewe fonufan. Ren enletin ewe Grifon a apasa ngeni ewe Mokutukut, “Kopwe sopweno, chinnap! Kosapw unusen ran usun!” iwe a sopweno non ekkei kapas:

“Ewer, aua fiti sukun non ewe sat, ina mwo ika kose nuku —”

“Use mwo apasa pwe use fori!” a apasa Alice.

“Ka fori,” ewe Mokutukut a apasa.

“Kopwe amwochu om tong!” a apasa ewe Grifon, me mwen an Alice epwe tongeni kapas sefan. Ewe Mokutukut a feino.

“A wor am kewe sukun mi murinno —non ennet, aua fiti sukun iteiten ran —”

“ Ua fen fiti eu sukulen ran, pwal,” Alice a apasa; “ese namwot kopwe fokkun namanam tekia usun meinisin ekkena.”

“Me ekkewe ekkoch?” a eisini ewe Mokutukut ren an ekis osukosuk.

Alice a erá: “Ewer, aua káé fósun France me kél.”

“Me wasona?” a apasa ewe Mokutukut.

“Ese mwaal esap ina!” a apasa Alice ren an song.

“Ah!, iwe om we ese wesewesen ew sukun mi murinno,” ewe Mock Turtle a apasa non ew mongungu mi fokkun menemenoch. “Iei non imwem we ra wor non nesoponon ewe bill, ‘France, nikáttik, me waséla —ekstra.’”

“Kose tongeni kopwe fokkun mochen,” Alice a apasa; “mi nonnom lon ewe matau.”

“Use tongeni ai upwe kaeo.” a apasa ewe Mokutukut ren an ngungures. “Ua chék fiti ewe kókkót iteitan.”

“Met ena?” a eisini Alice.

“Ren me Wur, ennet, pwe epwe poputa,” ewe Mokutukut a ponuweni; “me mwirin ekkewe sokkopaten kinikinin Arithmetic —Achocho, Achocho, Achocho, me Achocho.”

Alice a pwora le apasa: “Use mwo rongorong usun ‘Uf.’” “Met ena?”

Ewe Gryphon a aitieta poun kewe ruu ren an mairu. "Met! Use mwo rongorong usun uglifying!" a apasa. “Ka sinei met kopwe fori, ua ekieki?”

“Ewer,” Alice a apasa non tipemwaramwar: “a wewe ngeni —pwe —a fori —mettoch meinisin —mi murino.”

“Ewer, iwe,” ewe Gryphon a sopweno, “ika kese sinei met kopwe uglify, en emon chon tiparoch.”

Alice ese mefi pesepes ne eisini pwan ekkoch kapas eis usun, iwe a suki ngeni ewe Mokutukut, me a apasa “Met pwan ew mettoch kopwe kaeo?”

“Ewer, a wor Mister,” ewe Mock Turtle a ponuweni, a anneani ekkewe porous won an kewe flapper, “—Monomon, me nom me ikenai, ren Seaography: mwirin ewe Drawling —ewe masta mi monomon i emon conger-eel mi nom, ewe a kan feito fan ew non ew wiik: a aitikem non Drawling me Stretch.”

“Ifa usun ena ?” a apasa Alice.

“Ewer, use tongeni pwisin pwari ngonuk,” ewe Mokutukut a apasa: “Ua kon tipemwaramwar. Iwe ewe Gryphon ese mwo kaeo.”

“Ese wor fansoun,” ewe Gryphon a apasa: “Ua feino ren ewe masta Classics, nge. I emon krab mi chinnap, i .”

“Use mwo feino ren,” ewe Mock Turtle a apasa ren an ngungures: “a aiti ngeni ewe Laun me Netipengaw, ra kan apasa.”

“Iei usun a fori, ina usun a fori,” a apasa ewe Gryphon, a ngungures non an fansoun; iwe, ekkewe ruu man ra opano meser lon ar kewe paw.

“Me fite awa iteiten ran ka fori ekkewe lesen?” a apasa Alice, a mwitir ne siwini ewe poraus.

“Engon awa non ewe aewin ran,” ewe Mokutukut a apasa: “tiwin non ewe ran mwirin, me ina usun.”

“A ifa me watten ewe kokkot!” a apasa Alice.

“Ina ewe popun ra iteni lesen,” ewe Gryphon a apasa: “pun ra kisikisilo seni ran ngeni ran.”

Iei eu minefoon ekiek ngeni Alice, me a ekis ekieki ussun me mwen an epwe apasa an we kapas mwirin. “Iwe, ewe engol me eu ran epwe fen eu ranin kunou?”

“Ewer a ina,” ewe Mokutukut a apasa.

“Me ifa usun ka manaweni ewe engon me ruu?” Alice a sopweilo ren an mochen.

“A naf usun ekkewe lesen,” ewe Gryphon a apasa non ew mongungu mi fokkun finata: “kopwe ereni och mettoch usun ekkewe urumot iei.”
