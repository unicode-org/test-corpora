Comhairle veih Caterpillar

Va’n Caterpillar as Alice er nyn shilley er y çheshvean son ymmyd ayns siyll: er-jerrey ghow’n Caterpillar y hookah ass e bee, as loayr eh r’ee lesh coraa languid, sleepy.

“Cre’n aght t’ou? ”, dooyrt y Caarjyn.

Cha row shoh aarloo ymmyrchagh son cowrey. Dreggyr Alice, red beggan sheeyney, “Ta mee—cha nel mee fys aym, sir, ry-hoi—er-y-fa shen ta fys aym c’raad v’ee tra v’ee er n’irree yn oie shoh, agh ta mee credjal dy row mee er ny chaghlaa mee-chrauee rish y traa shen.”

“Cre’n aght t’ou dy ghra rish shen?” dooyrt y Caater sterrym. “Soilshee oo hene!”

“Cha vod mee my-hene y m’ymmyrkey , ta mee aggle, sir,” dooyrt Alice, “son cha nee mish my-hene, ta shiu er vakin.”

“Cha nel mee cur tastey da,” dooyrt y Caarjyn.

“Ta aggle orrym nagh voddym eh y chur ny sodjey,” dreggyr Alice dy mooar, “son cha voddym eh y hoiggal my-hene dy toshiaght; as ta’n chooid smoo dy mheanyn dy-heshaght ayns un laa feer cheeayllagh.”

“Cha nel,” dooyrt y Caarjyn.

“Well, mraane nagh vel oo er gheddyn eh myr shen foast,” as Alice; “agh tra t’ou dy ghra dy vel oo dy ghra dy vel oo dy chrysalis—ver oo laa erbee, ta fys ayd—as eisht lurg shen dy ghra dy vel oo dy ghra dy vel oo dy ghra dy vel oo beggan queer, nagh vel?”

“Cha nee red erbee,” as y Caterpillar.

“Well, mraane t’ou uss geddyn rish,” as Alice; “Yn red ta mee toiggal, dy beagh eh feer queer dou .”

"Oo!" dooyrt y Caterpillar dy ghraih. "Quoishiu ? "

Ta shen er chur ad ry-cheilley reesht gys toshiaght y chaglym. Va Alice feer cheeayllagh rish y Caterpillar dy row eh er n’yannoo reddyn ard -ghooie, as t’ee er n’yannoo reddyn ard-ghooie as dooyrt ee, ard-ghraih, “Ta mee credjal, dy beagh oo er n’insh dou c’raad t’ou , er-dy-hoshiaght.”

"Cre'n fa?" dooyrt y Caater.

Va cowrey puzzling elley; as myr nagh voddagh Alice smooinaghtyn er red erbee mie, as myr va'n Caterpillar myr dy beagh eh ayns stayd dy mooar ymmyrchagh, t'ee er n'gholl ersooyl.

“Thig ry-cheilley!” ’ dy ghra y Caterpillar er y ghraih. “Ta red mooar aym dy ghra!”

Va shoh cliaghtey dy row eh promising, dy shickyr: t’ee Alice er n’irree as t’ee er n’irree reesht.

“Cur dty veeal,” dooyrt y Caterpillar.

“Jean shen ooilley?” dooyrt Alice, slym sheese e ghraih cho mie as v’ee able.

“Cha nel,” dooyrt y Caarjyn.

Smooinee Alice dy beagh ee er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen, son cha row red erbee elley y yannoo, as mraane er-dy-henney dy beagh eh er n’insh da red erbee va feeu dy chlashtyn. Son cuid jeh ny minuteyn ren eh puffy ersooyl fegooish loayrt, agh er-jerrey hrog eh e laueyn, ghow eh y hookah ass e beeal reesht, as dooyrt eh, “Shinney, t’ou credjal dy vel oo er chaghlaa, nagh vel?”

“Ta mee aggle orrym, sir,” dooyrt Alice; “Cha nel mee coontey jeh ny reddyn myr v’eh ry-yannoo—as cha nel mee geddyn yr un mhean son deig minuteyn cooidjagh!”

“Cha nel mee cooinaghtyn cre ny reddyn?” dooyrt y Caater.

“Cha nel shen er ny ghra dy kiart,” as y Caterpillar.

“Cha nel eh dy jarroo, ta mee aggle,” dooyrt Alice, s’treih; “ta cuid jeh ny goan er nyn astyrt.”

“T’eh corree veih’n toshiaght gys y jerrey,” as y Caterpillar dy row, as va’n stayd ry-hoi ry-hoi.

Yn Caterpillar v'eh y chied er loayrt.

“Cre’n mean t’ou dy ve?” v'eh er cheau.

“Oh, cha nel mee s’special mychione y mhean,” dreggyr Alice dy hrooid; “cha nel agh un red ymmyrchagh dy chaghlaa cho fliugh, ta fys ayd.”

“ Cha nel fys aym,” dooyrt y Caarjyn.

Cha dooyrt Alice nhee erbee: cha row ee er ve choud as shen contrary ayns e vioys roimhe, as v’ee feer cheeayllagh dy row ee er choayl y ghraih.

“As vel oo boggyssagh anoish?” dooyrt y Caater.

“Well, by vie lhiam ve beggan ny s’mraane, sir, my nagh row oo geddyn rish,” as Alice: “tree inchid t’eh cho ard-chreeagh dy ve.”

“T’eh ard-vooaralagh dy jarroo!” dooyrt y Caterpillar lesh fear, as hrog eh eh-hene seose myr v’eh loayrt (va’n ghrian ry-hoi ry-hoi).

“Agh cha nel mee cliaghtey rish!” ’s t’eh er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh Alice bochill ayns tonn threealtagh. As smooinee ee er ee hene, “By vie lhiam nagh beagh ny cretooryn cho aashagh er nyn stroie!”

“Vees oo cliaghtey rish ayns traa,” dooyrt y Caterpillar; as hug eh yn hookah ayns e bee, as t'eh toshiaght dy smooinaghtyn reesht.

Yn traa shoh ren Alice geaishtagh lesh sheean derrey ren eh roshtyn dy loayrt reesht. Ayns un mhinid ny daa mhinid ghow y Caterpillar y hookah ass e bee as hrog eh eh-hene urree ny daa cheayrt, as hrog eh eh-hene. As hie eh sheese veih’n mushroom, as crash eh ersooyl ayns y feaysh, as v’eh ny-lomarcan gra myr v’eh goll, “Ta’n taobh shen jannoo oo dy ve ny s’ardjey, as ta’n taobh elley dy dty yannoo ny s’giare.”

“Un rannagh jeh cre? Yn rannagh elley jeh cre? ” smooinee Alice r’ee hene.

“Jeh’n mushroom,” dooyrt y Caterpillar, myr dy beagh ee er n’eiyrt eh dy ard; as ayns y traa elley v'eh er-mayrn.

D’eaisht Alice rish y mushroom son un minnid, as v’ee shirrey dy hoiggal cre’n daa taobh v’ayn; as myr v'eh round perfect, honnick ee shoh dy ve cowrey dy liooar. Agh, er-jerrey, sniee ee e laueyn mygeayrt echey cho fadagh as v’ad, as brishey ee red beggan jeh’n rimmey lesh gagh laue.

“As nish cre’n red ta?” ’s t’ee gra r’ee hene, as t’ee glenney beggan jeh’n right-hand bit dy phrowal y effect: y traa er-gerrey t’ee er chlashtyn strooan violent fo’n strooan: v’eh er n’yannoo strooan er e chosh!

Va’n aght s’taitnyssagh shoh er ny scanshoil, agh v’ee feer cheeayllagh nagh row traa erbee dy ve er ny choayl, son v’ee shrinkey dy hrooid; as shen-y-fa hie ee er-gerrey dy obbraghey dy ee cuid jeh'n bit elley. Va'n skean eck ry-hoi ry-hoi rish e chosh, nagh row room erbee dy foshlit e beeal; agh ren ee eh er-jerrey, as ren ee glenney sniaghtey jeh'n 'lefthand bit'.
