“Oh, ta mee guee erriu!” ’s t’ee gra lesh tonn dy ghraih, as t’ee er n’yannoo ad y hroggal reesht cho s’lesh as v’ee, son v’an tuittym jeh’n eshyn-oor ry-hoi ry-hoi ayns e ben, as v’ee ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi.

“Cha vod y vriwnys goll er,” dooyrt y Ree lesh coraa dy liooar, “derrey vees ooilley ny jurymen er-gerrey ayns nyn ’ellanyn cooie— ooilley ,” as eshyn reesht lesh mooar y vee-chraueeaght, as eh jeeaghyn dy ard er Alice myr v’eh gra.

Smooinee Alice er y jury-box, as honnick ee, ayns e hrooid, dy row ee er chur y Lizard ayns e ben sheese, as dy row y red beg bochill ry-hoi ry-hoi ayns aght melancholy, er-yn-oyr nagh row eh ry-hoi ry-hoi. Gow ee eh magh reesht, as hug ee eh ry-cheilley; “cha nee dy vel eh soilshaghey mooar,” dooyrt ee r’ee hene; “Shegin dou credjal dy beagh eh cho mooar as t’eh ry-chlashtyn ayns y vriaght un raad seose as y raad elley.”

Choud as va'n jury er n'gholl stiagh beggan jeh'n chorree v'ad er n'yannoo, as v'ad er n'yannoo ny slateyn as ny pencilyn oc as v'ad er nyn goyrt reesht daue, ghow ad toshiaght dy obbraghey dy jeean dy scrieu tashtey jeh'n tuittym, ooilley agh y Lizard, v'eh myr v'eh ry-hoi ry-hoi dy yannoo red erbee agh dy suitch lesh e beeal er-skyn y chrosh.

“Cre’n aght ta fys eu mychione y ghleashtan shoh?” dooyrt y Ree rish Alice.

“Ny-yeih,” as Alice.

“Ny- yeih, ” as y Ree.

“Cha nel red erbee,” as Alice.

“T’eh feer p’oor,” as y Ree, as eh geddyn rish y jury. Va’d ry-hoi ry-scrudey shoh er nyn slattyn, tra v’eh er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh’n Coonney Ghiarn: “ Un important, ta’n Ard-reill echey, s’cosoylagh,” as eh ry-hoi, agh v’eh ry-hoi ry-hoi as ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi.

“ Cha nee red erbee, s’cosoylagh, v’eh,” dooyrt y Ree dy hrooid, as hie eh er-gerrey da hene lesh fo-chowrey,

“pooar—nagh vel foddey—pooar—pooar—” myr dy beagh eh feddyn cre’n fockle v’er ny chlashtyn ny share.

Screeu cuid jeh’n jury eh “pooar,” as cuid “nagh-pooar.” Va Alice goaill rish shoh, son v’ee er-gerrey dy row ee er n’eaishtagh rish ny slattyn oc; “agh cha nel eh red erbee,” dooyrt ee r’ee hene.

Ayns y traa shoh, ghow y Ree, v’er n’yannoo rish y traa t’er n’gholl shaghey s’cosoylagh rish y lioar-note echey, “Silence!” as laghyn eh ass e lioar, “Reeal Da-eed as Feed. Gagh dooinney erskyn mile erskyn dy row eh er n’eiyrt magh ass y chooish .”

Va ooilley ny h-oik er Alice.

“ Cha nel mee mile erskyn,” dooyrt Alice.

“T’ou,” dooyrt y Ree.

“Cha nel agh daa mile er yrjid,” dooyrt y Bannister.

“Well, cha jean-ym goll, er-y-fa shen,” dooyrt Alice: “er-y-fa shen, cha nel shen recortys reiltagh: t’ou er n’insh eh nish.”

“She’n recortys s’anmey ’sy lioar,” dooyrt y Ree.

“Shen-y-fa, shegin da ve Number One,” as Alice.

Va'n Ree pale, as hug eh e lioar-note stiagh dy hrooid. “Smooinee er dty vriwnys,” dooyrt eh rish y jury, lesh coraa low, creeoil.

“Ta ny smoo toshiaght ry-heet foast, my t’ou uss,” dooyrt y Coonney Ghiarn, lesh y ghraih mooar; “ta’n paper shoh er ny ghoaill.”

“Cre’n red t’ayn?” dooyrt y Bannister.

“Cha nel mee er foshlit eh foast,” dooyrt y Coonney Ghiarn, “agh shegin da ve lioar, s’cosoylagh rish y phryssoonagh da—da dooinney.”

“Shegin da ve shen,” dooyrt y Ree, “myr nagh row eh scruit da dooinney erbee, shen nagh vel ry-akin, ta fys ayd.”

“Cre’n dooinney t’eh stiurey?” dooyrt fer jeh ny jury.

“Cha nel eh er ny stiurey er chor erbee,” dooyrt y Choonney Ghien; “er-y-fa shen, cha nel red erbee scruit er y thalloo .” Va’n paper er ny h-offrey tra v’eh loayrt, as dooyrt eh rish “Cha nee lieh-litrey eh, er-y-fa shen: she set verseyn eh.”

“As vel ad ayns scriptyr y phryssoonagh?” ’ ass fer elley jeh’n jury.

“Cha nel, cha nel,” dooyrt y Coonney Ghien, “as shen y red s’queer jeh.” (Va'n jury ooilley boggyssagh.)

“Shegin da ymmyd y yannoo jeh laue dooinney elley,” dooyrt y Ree. (Ren y jury ooilley s'brishey reesht.)

“Ta mee cur booise da dty Ard-nieu,” dooyrt y Knave, “cha nee mish s’scruit, as cha vod ad prowal dy s’scruit: cha nel ennym signit ec y jerrey.”

"Myr nagh row oo er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh," dooyrt y Ree, "cha nel shen agh jannoo y chooish ny s'mish. Shegin dhyt ve er n'yannoo red erbee dy mraane, ny s'cosoylagh, v'ou er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh dty ennym myr dooinney onnoroil."

Va clashtyn laueyn coontit ec shoh: she’n chied red cleaynagh v’er ny ghra ec y Ree yn laa shen.

“T’eh shen prowal e chionnaghey,” dooyrt y Bannister.

“Cha nel eh prowal red erbee jeh’n sorch!” dooyrt Alice. “Cre’n-fa, cha nel oo fys ayd’s cre’n red t’ad er n’yannoo!”

“Lesh ad,” dooyrt y Ree.

Cur y Coonney Ghilley er e speeckys. “Cre’n raad t’ou uss goaill toshiaght, myr t’ou uss?” denee eh.

“Toshiaght ec y toshiaght,” dooyrt y Ree dy ghraih, “as gow er-gerrey derrey t’ou er jeet gys y jerrey: eisht stay.”

Shoh ny verseyn va'n Coonney Ghiarn er ghra:—

“Shen y toshiaght s’pooaral t’ain er chlashtyn foast,” as y Ree, s’cosoylagh rish e laueyn; “as nish lhig da’n jury—”

"My vod fer jeu eh y mharkey," as Alice, (va'n ven shen er n'gholl seose cho mooar ayns ny minuteyn shoh chaie as cha row ee erbee aggle er y ghraih echey), "verym eh sixpence. Cha chreid mee dy vel atom dy ghraih ayn."

Screeu y jury ooilley er nyn slattyn, “ Cha nel ee credjal dy vel atom dy ghraih ayn,” agh cha row fer jeu er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh’n paper.

"Mannagh vel ymmyd erbee ayn," dooyrt y Ree, "ta shen slaynt y theihll dy threeish, ta fys ayd, myr nagh vel feme orrin feddyn red erbee. As foast cha nel fys aym," hie eh er-gerrey, spreih eh ny verseyn er e ghlinney, as jeeaghyn orroo lesh un sooill; “T’eh myr dy vel mee cur ymmyd jeu, erreish da ooilley ny reddyn. “— dooyrt eh nagh row mee ry-snaue —” cha row shiu ry-snaue, nagh row?” ’s eh gra, as t’eh geddyn stiagh ’sy Knave.

Chreck y Knave e chree dy brynnagh. “Vel mee gollrish eh?” dooyrt eh. (Ny ren eh dy shickyr, er-yn-oyr dy row eh jeant dy kiaralagh jeh cartoon.)

“Shegin dou, derrey shoh,” dooyrt y Ree, as hie eh er-gerrey dy mraaney er ny verseyn rish hene: “’ Ta fys ain dy vel eh firrinagh —’ shen y jury, s’cosoylagh—’ Hug mee un r’ee, hug ad daa da —’ cre’n-fa, shegin da shen ve ny ren eh rish ny tartanyn, ta fys ayd—”

“Agh, t’eh goll er ’ t’ad ooilley er jeet reesht veih eshyn gys oo ,’” dooyrt Alice.

“Cre’n-fa, shen t’ad!” dooyrt y Ree triomfagh, as eh cur stiagh ny tartanyn va er y vooar. “Cha nel nhee erbee ny sodjey soilshey na shen . As reesht—’ roish my row y fit shoh eck —’ cha row fit ec oo riamh, my ghraih, ta mee credjal?” dooyrt eh rish y Bannister.

"Chioee!" dooyrt y Bannister furiously, as t’ee thrown inkstand er y Lizard tra v’ee loayrt. (Va'n Bill beg s'anmey er n'eiyrt jeh scriptyr er e slate lesh un veeal, son cha row eh er n'yannoo mark erbee; agh t'eh er n'eiyrt rish y ink, v'er n'eiyrt sheese er e veeal, choud as v'eh er n'eiyrt.)

“Shen-y-fa cha nel ny goan ry-hoi ,” as y Ree, as eh jeeaghyn mygeayrt y chorree lesh sniaghtey. Va silence marroo.

“She pun eh!” ’s t’eh er n’yannoo rish y Ree ayns tonn offense, as t’eh er n’yannoo rish, “Lhig da’n jury smooinaghtyn er nyn breearrey.” dooyrt y Ree, son mysh yn feed cheayrt yn laa shen.

“Cha nel, cha nel!” dooyrt y Bannister. “Sheer y chied cheayrt—breearrey er-dy-henney.”

“Stuff as gyn-yss!” dooyrt Alice dy ard. “Yn tushtey dy vel y fraue er-dy-hoshiaght!”

“Curm dty veeal!” dooyrt y Bannister, as v’ee purple.

“Cha jean-ym!” dooyrt Alice.

“Off lesh e ben!” ’s eh cheet er-gerrey da’n Bannister. Cha row dooinney erbee er ghluaisey.

“Cre’n fer ta cur kiarail dhyt?” dooyrt Alice, (va ee er n'gholl stiagh ayns e ghlioon 'sy traa shoh). “Cha nel oo red erbee agh pack caartyn!”

As ec shoh, hie yn pack ooilley seose gys an aer, as haink ad dy fliaghey sheese er: hug ee screeu beggan, lesh lesh aggle as lesh lesh fear, as ren ee feddyn ad y stroie, as honnick ee ee hene ny lhie er y rank, lesh e ben ayns y ghlioon jeh e chreear, v'er n'yannoo sniaghtey jeh duillagyn marroo er e veeal v'er n'yannoo fliaghey.

“Dreggyr, Alice ghraih!” dooyrt ee; “Cre’n-fa, cre’n coayl fadagh t’ou er ghoaill!”

“Oh, ta bunnys cho curious aym!” ’ as Alice, as dooyrt ee rish e chreear, choud as v’ee ry-hoi, ooilley ny Adventures s’diuney shoh t’ou er n’leih jeu; as tra v'ee er chlashtyn, hug ee sheean urree, as dooyrt ee, " B'eh aash curious, s'cosoylagh, dy shickyr: agh nish reill stiagh gys dty tee; t'eh er n'gholl stiagh." As hie Alice seose as ren ee rheynn, as smooinee ee tra v’ee rheynn, cho mie as v’ee, cre’n aght v’eh ry-hoi.

Agh v’ee ny soie stiagh myr v’ee er n’eiyrt r’ee, as v’ee croymmey e ben er e laue, as v’ee jeeaghyn er y ghrian v’er n’eiyrt, as v’ee smooinaghtyn er Alice beg as ooilley e Adventures ard-vooaralagh, derrey t’ee hene myrgeddin er n’eiyrt rish y ghraih, as shoh v’ee er n’eiyrt rish:—

Ard-oik, v'ee son Alice beg hene, as ec y traa cheddin v'ad ny laueyn beggey clashtyn er e ghlinney, as v'ad ny sooillyn glen ard-chreeagh jeeaghyn seose gys e sooillyn - v'ee cluster y tonn jeh'n voylley eck, as v'ee cluster y toss beg queer shen jeh'n ben echey dy chur stiagh y ghruaie ry-cheilley v'er ny listaghey as v'ee er n'yannoo rish ny sooillyn echey. laghyn mygeayrt ee v’er ny ve beo rish ny cretooryn eccleshagh jeh’n aigney jeh’n chreear beg.

Va'n feo ard rustle ec e chosh tra v'eh er n'gholl er-gerrey - va'n Lugh s'scanshoil er n'gholl trooid y lhong s'jerree - v'ee cluster y rattle jeh'n tea-cup tra v'eh y Lion March as e chaarjyn ry-choardail rish nyn veeal nagh row jerrey erbee, as va'n coraa shrill jeh'n Bannister er n'ordail da'n pixeo s'annooinee. sniaghtey er glinney y Duchess, tra v’ad plateyn as plateyn shliaghtey mygeayrt-y-moom—un uair as feed y shliaghtey jeh’n Gryphon, y shliaghtey jeh’n slate-pencil jeh’n Lizard, as y shliaghtey jeh’n slate-pencil jeh’n Lizard, as y shliaghtey jeh’n guinea-pig s’diuney, lionney yn aer, mestit lesh shliaghteyn miser y Turt.

As shen-y-fa hie ee er, lesh sooillyn cloie, as chred ee hene ayns Wonderland, agh v'ee fys echey nagh row agh dy foshlit ad reesht, as cha row ooilley ny reddyn ry-chasley rish y red s'duillagh - cha row'n feoish agh rustling 'sy gheay, as y lhong ry-rippling rish y shliaghtey jeh'n reed - v'eh ry-chasley rish y shliaghtey rish y shliaghtey. y lhiannoo shepherd—as sniaghtey y lhiannoo, shrieu y Gryphon, as ooilley ny soilshaghyn queer elley, v’ad caghlaa (va fys echey) gys y chlaare t’er ny ghleiy jeh’n chlaare-feeyney glen—as v’ee lowing y veeal er-dy-hoshiaght y ghoaill ynnyd sobbyn trome y Mock Turtle.

Er-jerrey, ren ee skeeal y yannoo r’ee hene cre’n aght vees y sheshey beg shoh jeh, ayns y traa er-dy-henney, ee hene ben-oast; as kys v’ee dy choadey, trooid ooilley e vleeantyn riper, y chree simply as graihagh jeh’n lhiannooid eck: as kys v’ee dy choamrey mygeayrt e chloan beggey elley, as dy yannoo nyn sooillyn soilshey as ard-chreeagh lesh ymmodee scansh s’diuney, mraane ec y traa t’er n’gholl shaghey: as kys v’ee dy ghra rish ooilley nyn doghanyn simply, as dy gheddyn ooilley ny s’simpley jeh’n ghloyr oc hene, as dy gheddyn ooilley ny s’diuney jeh’n ghloyr oc hene, bea-leigh, as ny laayn sheean jeh’n shamyr.
