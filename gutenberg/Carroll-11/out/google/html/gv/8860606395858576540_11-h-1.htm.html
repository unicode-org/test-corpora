CAPPYL I.
Sheese y Towl-Coonney

Va Alice toshiaght dy ve feer staghnagh jeh’n aght v’ee ny soie rish e chreear er y rank, as jeh’n aght nagh row red erbee dy yannoo: un uair ny daa cheayrt v’ee er n’eaishtagh rish y lioar v’ee er n’leih, agh cha row pictuyryn ny cowraghyn ayn, “as cre’n aght ta’n lioar,” smooinee Alice “ga pictuyryn ny cowraghyn?”

As shen-y-fa v’ee smooinaghtyn er e h-insh hene (choud as v’ee ry-hoi, son v’eh ry-hoi y laa t’er n’yannoo jeh’n laa t’er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo.

Cha row nhee erbee cho ard -ooasle ayns shen; as chamoo va Alice er smooinaghtyn er choud as shen dy chlashtyn yn Coonney gra rish hene, "Oh ghraih! Oh ghraih! Bee'm er-gerrey!" (tra v'ee er smooinaghtyn er y red shen erreish da, haink eh gys e chree dy beagh ee er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh shoh, agh ec y traa shen v'eh ooilley myr v'eh dy jarroo najooragh); agh tra ghow y Coonney urree ass e phoosey , as honnick eh eh, as eisht hie eh er-gerrey, t'ee Alice er n'eiyrt seose, son v'eh fliaghey er e h-insh nagh row ee rieau er vaik coonney lesh urree dy chur magh ass e phoosey, ny urree dy chur magh ass, as ren ee cur curicross lesh, fortunately v'eh ry-hoi 'sy traa dy akin eh pop sheese rabbit-hole mooar fo'n hedge.

Ayns stayd elley hie Alice sheese er y ghraih, cha row ee erbee er smooinaghtyn er cre'n aght v'ee er y theihll dy gholl magh reesht.

Va’n rabbit-hole goll dy kiart er-gerrey myr tunnel son red erbee, as eisht hie eh sheese dy h-oik, myr shen dy h-oik nagh row Alice erbee dy smooinaghtyn er staydys ee hene roish my honnick ee ee hene dy hrooid y hrooid.

Either va’n towse dy liooar, ny dy row ee dy liooar, son va’n traa echey dy liooar tra v’ee goll sheese dy yeeaghyn mygeayrt as dy smooinaghtyn cre’n red v’er-gerrey. Ard-oik, ren ee shirrey dy yeeaghyn sheese as dy hoiggal cre’n red v’ee cheet, agh v’eh ro-dooghyssagh dy akin red erbee; as eisht honnick ee er tallooyn y veeal, as hug ee tastey da dy row ad lionnit lesh cupboardyn as shelpyn lioaryn; ec y traa shen as ec y traa shen honnick ee mappyn as pictuyryn croghey er peggyn. T’ee er ghoaill jarrood sheese ass un jeh ny shelfyn tra v’ee er n’gholl shaghey; v’eh scruit “ORANGE MARMALADE”, agh son e ghraih mooar v’eh foshlit: cha row ee boggyssagh dy scughey y jarrood son aggle dy row dooinney erbee foddey, as shen-y-fa ren ee eh y chur ayns un jeh ny cupboardyn tra v’ee tuittym er-gerrey da.

"S’aaitnit!" smooinee Alice r'ee hene, "ny lurg tuittym myr shoh, cha jean-ym smooinaghtyn erbee dy tuittym sheese er stairs! Cre'n aght t'ad ooilley bragh smooinaghtyn orrym 'sy thie! Cre'n-fa, cha row mee er n'insh red erbee jeh, agh my tuittym veih barr y thie!" (Cre'n aght v'eh glen.)

Sheese, sheese, sheese. Nagh row y tuittym erbee er jeet gys jerrey? “S’maynrey lhiam cre’n aght ta mee er choayl y traa shoh?” dooyrt ee dy ard. "Shegin dou goll er-gerrey da mean y thalloo. Lhig dou fakin: veagh shen ceithir thousand mile sheese, ta mee credjal—" (son, ta shiu er vakin, v'ee Alice er n'ynsaghey ymmodee reddyn jeh'n sorch shoh ayns e lessonyn ayns y skool, as geddyn rish nagh row shoh cowrey ard -ghooie dy hoilshaghey magh e eddin, myr nagh row dooinney erbee dy ghra rish, ta shen mychione y raad cheddin—agh eisht ta mee goaill aggle cre’n Latitude ny Longitude t’aym?” (Cha row fys ec Alice cre'n red v'ayn Latitude, ny Longitude, agh s'cosoylagh dy row ad goan s'goaill rish.)

Ayns y laa t'ayn, t'ee er toshiaght reesht. "Ta mee smooinaghtyn my t'eh ry - hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry- hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi. Maam, t’eh shoh Seelan Noa ny Austrail?” (as ren ee shirrey curtsey tra v’ee loayrt— curtsey fancy tra t’ou uss tuittym trooid an aer! Cre’n aght t’ou credjal dy vod oo eh y reayll?) “As cre’n niau-chloan ignorant t’ee dy smooinaghtyn orrym son goaill! Cha nee, cha jean eh rieau goaill rish: mraane ver-yms eh scruit seose ayns ard erbee.”

Sheese, sheese, sheese. Cha row red erbee elley dy yannoo, as cha b’echey traa dy row Alice er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh. “T’ee Dinah goaill aggle orrym dy mooar yn oie shoh, lhisagh mee smooinaghtyn er!” (Dinah v'eh y cat.) "Ta mee credjal dy bee ad cooinaghtyn jeh'n saucer bainney echey ec traa-tee. Dinah my ghraih! Ta mee booiagh dy beagh oo sheese shoh mârym! Cha nel luchtyn ayns an aer, ta mee aggle, agh might oo glenney bat, as t'eh shen feer myr luch, ta fys ayd. Agh vel cats gee bat, ta mee goaill aggle?" As ec y traa t’ayn, t’eh er n’yannoo s’coan, as t’ee er n’insh r’ee hene, ayns aght s’ashoonagh, “Ta cats ec y traa t’ayn? Ta cats ec y traa t’ayn?” as ry-cheilley, “Bee ny battyn gee cats?” son, ta shiu er vakin, myr nagh row ee ry-hoi freggyrt da'n daa chosht, cha row eh s'cosoylagh cre'n raad v'ee er chur eh. Va’n aght echey dy row ee ry-hoi, as v’ee er n’yannoo rish dy row ee goll laue-ry-laue rish Dinah, as dy row ee gra r’ee dy ard-chreeagh, “Agh, Dinah, insh dou yn firrinys: row oo er n’ee bat?” tra dy tappee, thump! thump! sheese haink ee er staarte stiagh as duillagyn tiugh, as va'n tuittym er-gerrey.

Cha row Alice erbee glen, as hrog ee seose er e chassyn ayns un ghraih: sheese ee seose, agh v’eh ooilley dorragh erskyn; roish ee va shliaghtey ard elley, as va'n Coonney Ghiarn foast ayns y sight, ry-hoi ry-hoi. Cha row un ghraih erbee dy ve caillt: hie Alice ersooyl myr y gheay, as v’ee ry-hoi ’sy traa dy chlashtyn ee gra, tra v’ee ry-hoi ’sy chorneil, “Oh my chleayshyn as my veeal, cre’n aght t’eh ry-hoi!” Va’n ghrian ry-hoi tra t’ee er n’eiyrt y corneil, agh cha row’n Coonney er ny hoiggal ny s’anmey: v’ee er n’eiyrt stiagh ayns hall ard, low, v’er ny ghlenney lesh row lampadyn v’er nyn skeaylley er y chronk.

Va dorryssyn ooilley mygeayrt y halley, agh v’ad ooilley glast; as tra v'ee Alice er n'gholl ooilley ny raaidyn sheese er y taobh as sheese er y taobh elley, trymmey gagh dorrys, hie ee trimshagh sheese er y mean, gobbraghey myr v'ee er-dy-henney dy gholl magh reesht.

As dy tappee haink ee er boayrd beg three-cosh, ooilley jeant jeh glass solid; cha row nhee erbee er agh alcaghyn oor beg, as va'n chied smooinaght er Alice dy beagh eh jeh un dorrys y halley; agh, atreih! ny v'ad ny lockyn ro-mhor, ny v'an alcaghyn ro-beg, agh er-y-fa shen cha row eh gobbraghey erbee jeu. Agh, er y daa cheayrt, haink ee er curtain low nagh row ee er n’yannoo aarloo roish, as er y çheu jeh va dorrys beg mysh clowan jeig erskyn towse: t’ee er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh’n alcaghyn beg oor ayns y lock, as gys e ghraih mooar v’eh ry-hoi!

As hrog Alice y dorrys er-gerrey as honnick ee dy row eh geddyn stiagh ayns slyst beg, cha nee s’mooar na rat-hole: glioonee ee sheese as sheeyney er y slyst gys y ghard smoo ghraih t’ou er vaik. Cre’n aght v’ee dy ghra dy row ee er n’gholl magh ass y halley dorragh shen, as dy row ee er n’gholl mygeayrt mastey ny laghyn shen dy blaghyn glen as ny s’cowrey shen, agh cha row ee ry-hoi geddyn e ben trooid y dorry; "as ga dy beagh my chree goll trooid," smooinee Alice bochill, "cha bee eh jeh monney ymmyd gyn my ghlioonyn. Oh, cre'n aght v'ee dy voddagh mee cloie myr telescope! Ta mee credjal dy beagh mee, my row mee dy toshiaght." Son, ta shiu er vakin, va choud as v’ad er n’yannoo rish y traa t’er n’gholl shaghey, dy row Alice er n’yannoo rish dy row reddyn ry-hoi ry-hoi.

Va’n red shen myr nagh row feme erbee ec y dorrys beg, as hie ee ry-cheilley gys y table, lesh lesh y scansh dy row ee er gheddyn alcaghyn elley er, ny er-y-fa shen lioar recortyssyn son cloie dooinney myr teleskopyn: y traa shoh honnick ee bottle beg er, (“nagh row ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi,” dooyrt Alice,) as ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi. “DRINK ME,” s’cosoylagh rish y lioar ayns litreeyn m’oie.

Va’n chooid smoo dy ghra “Drink me,” agh cha row’n Alice beg glen gobbraghey shen ayns y ghraih. “Cha nee, verym tastey da’n chied cheayrt,” as ee, “as cur-my-ner my t’eh markit ‘ poison ’ ny nagh vel”; son v’ee er lhaih ymmodee scanshyn beggey s’niessey mychione cloan v’er nyn lostey, as v’er nyn ee liorish beishtyn-feeyney as reddyn elley nagh row mee-chrauee, ooilley son nagh row ad cooinaghtyn jeh ny recortyssyn simply v’ad er n’ynsaghey daue ec nyn caraidey: myr, dy lostey poker red-hot oo my t’ou uss geddyn eh ro-hickyr; as dy vel eh geddyn fuill dy mennick, my t’ou gearrey dty veeal dy dowin lesh knife; as cha row ee er-diu dy row, my t’ou uss goaill mooarane ass bottle markit “poison,” cha row eh ry-hoi ry-hoi, ry-hoi.

Agh cha row y bottle shoh markit “poison,” as shen-y-fa ren Alice ymmyd jeh’n blass, as tra v’eh er ny gheddyn dy row eh feer nice, (va, ayns firrinys, sorch blass measgagh jeh tart cherry, custard, pine-apple, turkey roast, toffee, as toast buttered tey,) ren ee eh y chlashtyn dy s’goan.

“Cre’n aght curious!” dooyrt Alice; “Shegin dou ve ry-chlashtyn myr teleskop.”

As myr shen v’eh dy jarroo: cha row ee er-y-fa shen agh deig inch erskyn, as v’ee er-y-fa shen glen rish y smooinaghtyn er-y-fa shen dy row ee er-y-fa shen y mhean cheddin dy gholl trooid y dorrys beg gys y gharddyn ghraihagh shen. Agh ec y toshiaght, ren ee geaishtagh rish beggan minnid dy akin my row ee dy shrinkey s’leaie: v’ee feer nervous jeh’n red shoh; "son might eh crommey, ta fys ayd," dooyrt Alice r'ee hene, "ayns my heet magh dy chooilley red, myr coinney. Ta mee goaill aggle cre'n aght v'ee myr shen?" As ren ee shirrey dy smooinaghtyn er cre'n aght ta flammey y ghrian tra v'eh er ny s'leaie, son cha row ee ry-hoi coontey dy row ee er vaik red erbee myr shen.

As lurg y traa, tra honnick ee nagh row red erbee er-dy-henney, ren ee ymmyd jeh’n aght dy gholl stiagh ayns y ghard dy h-oikoil; agh, atreish da Alice bochill! tra haink ee gys y dorry, honnick ee dy row ee er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh’n alcaghyn beg oor, as tra hie ee reesht gys y table son eh, honnick ee nagh row ee ry-gheddyn eh: v’ee ry-akin eh dy soilshey trooid y ghlare, as ren ee ymmyd jeh’n aght dy hroggal seose un jeh cosh y table, agh v’eh ry-sliaghtey; as tra v'ee er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh'n aght shen, hie yn red beg bochill sheese as ghow ee y ghraih.

“Thig, cha nel ymmyd erbee jeh cliaghtey myr shen!” dooyrt Alice r’ee hene, raad s’sharroo; “Ta mee cur comhairle dhyt dy n’eiyrt magh ’sy mhinid shoh!” Va’n coyrle ard-ghooie ec y chooid smoo, (ga dy row ee ry-hoi ry-hoi), as ry-hoi, v’ee ry-hoi ry-hoi, as v’ee ry-hoi, as v’ee ry-hoi, as v’ee ry-hoi, as v’ee ry-hoi, as v’ee ry-hoi, as v’ee ry-hoi, as v’ee ry-hoi, as v’ee ry-hoi. as tra hug ee coontey dy row ee er n’yannoo box jeh’n chleaysh echey hene son v’ee er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh’n chleaysh croquet v’ee cloie noi ee hene, son va’n lhiannoo curious shoh feer glen dy yannoo myr daa pheiagh. "Agh cha nel ymmyd erbee ayn nish," smooinee Alice bochill, "dy yannoo myr daa pheiagh! Cre'n-fa, cha nel monney doaltattym jeem er-mayrn dy yannoo un pheiagh urree!"

Gow ee sheese er boggey beg glass v’er ny lhoobaghey fo’n table: as hrog ee eh, as honnick ee cake glen-beg ayn, er va ny goan “EAT ME” markit s’cosoylagh rish currantyn. "Well, gow-ym eh," as Alice, "as my nee eh mee y yannoo ny smoo, foddym reach y lhong; as my nee eh mee y yannoo ny smoo, foddym croo fo'n dorrys; shen-y-fa ayns dagh aght hig-ym stiagh 'sy ghard, as cha nel eh currit dou cre'n red ta ry-yannoo!"

She ee beggan, as dooyrt ee r’ee hene, “Cre’n raad? Cre’n raad?”, as hug ee e laue er barr e ben dy ghra cre’n raad v’eh crooit, as v’ee feer cheeayllagh dy gheddyn magh dy row ee er-mayrn yr un mhean: dy ve shickyr, t’eh shoh goll er y chooid smoo tra ta’n dooinney gee cake, agh cha row Alice er n’eiyrt magh. happen, dy row eh myr dy beagh eh s’duillagh as s’goanney son bea dy gholl er-soylley er y raad coontit.

As t'ee er n'obbraghey, as t'ee er n'yannoo y cake.
