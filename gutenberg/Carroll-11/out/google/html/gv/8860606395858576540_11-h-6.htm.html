CAPPYL VI.
Muc as Piper

Son un minute ny daa sheese sheese sheese er y thie, as gobbraghey cre’n red y yannoo er-gerrey, tra haink dy tappee dooinney-coshee ayns livery ry-reih ass y chosh—(va’n dooinney shen er-y-fa shen myr dooinney-coshee son v’eh ayns livery: myr shen, my v’ee breearrey liorish e veeal ny lomarcan, v’ee er n’enmys eh iasg)—as rap ee dock or e k at loud. Va'n dooinney-coshee elley er ny hoshaghey ayns livery, lesh y veeal cronk, as sooillyn mooarey myr rannag; as va'n daa footman, hug Alice tastey da, lesh ghruaie powderagh currit stiagh er ooilley nyn ben. Va ee feer curious dy hoiggal cre v’ayn, as chreck ee beggan stiagh ass y chosh dy chlashtyn.

T'eh er n'yannoo rish y Fish-Footman liorish cur magh ass fo e laue lieh-littagh mooar, cha row eh cho mooar as eh hene, as hug eh eh shoh da'n jeh elley, gra, ayns tonn solemn, "Foddey y Duchess. Currym veih'n Bannister dy chloie croquet." As dooyrt y Frog-Footman reesht, ayns yr un tonney solemn, agh cha row eh er chaghlaa ordre ny goan beggan, "Veih'n Bannister. Currym da'n Duchess dy chloie croquet."

As chroym ad ooilley ny s’leaie, as v’ad nyn curlyn ry-cheilley.

Ghlare Alice cho mooar rish shoh, dy row ee dy reayll stiagh 'sy chosh son aggle dy beagh ad er chlashtyn ee; as tra v'ee er-gerrey peep out va'n Fish-Footman er-mayrn, as va'n jeh elley ny hoie er y talloo kiongoyrt rish y dorry, stayrt stupid seose gys y skyn.

Gow Alice sheese gys y dorry, as chloie ee.

"Cha nel ymmyd erbee jeh cloie," dooyrt y Footman, "as shen son daa red. Ard-oik, son dy vel mee er y çheu cheddin jeh'n dorrys as oo; yn nah red, son dy vel ad jannoo cho soinney stiagh, cha row dooinney erbee ry-chlashtyn oo." As dy firrinagh va soilshey s’odjey-oaylleeagh gobbraghey ayns shen—goaill-rish as sniaghtey dy kinjagh, as gagh-laa as gagh-laa crash mooar, myr veagh dish ny kettle er ny bhrisey gys p’art.

“T’ou uss, eisht,” dooyrt Alice, “kys t’ou uss goaill stiagh?”

"S'maynrey dy beagh red erbee ry-chlashtyn ayns dty ghleashtan," hie yn Footman er-gerrey fegooish cur tastey da, "my va'n dorrys eddyr ain. Son y chooid smoo, my v'ou stiagh , m'aigney dy ghleashtan, as m'aigney dy chur oo magh, ta fys ayd." Va’n skyn ooilley’n traa v’eh loayrt, as va’n Alice shoh smooinaghtyn dy row eh dy shickyr uncivil. “Agh mraane nagh vod eh y yannoo,” dooyrt ee r’ee hene; "Ta e sooillyn cho ard -chreeagh er barr e chree. Agh er-y-fa shen might eh freggyrt ceashlyn.-Cre'n aght t'eh dy gholl stiagh?" ’s t’ee er n’addey, ard.

“Shegin dou soie shoh,” dooyrt y Footman, “derrey astyr—”

Ayns y traa shoh foshlit dorrys y thie, as haink plate mooar skimmee magh, dy kiart er ben y Footman: v'eh ny s'cosoylagh rish e shnuddey, as v'eh brishey gys p'artaghyn noi un jeh ny craaghyn er e ghlioon.

“—ny yn laa er-giyn, mraane,” hie yn Footman er-gerrey rish y red cheddin, dy jarroo myr nagh row veg er-ny-yannoo.

“Cre’n aght t’ou dy gholl stiagh?” ’ as Alice reesht, ayns tonn ny s’ardjey.

“ Vel oo dy gholl stiagh erbee?” dooyrt y Footman. “Shen y chied cheesh, ta fys ayd.”

Va shen, cha nel teyrey erbee: agh cha row Alice booiagh dy row eh er ny ghra r’ee. "T'eh feer agglagh," dooyrt ee r'ee hene, "y raad ta ooilley ny cretooryn dy ghra. T'eh doaltattym dy chur dooinney er-chea!"

Va'n Footman myr dy row eh son cowrey mie dy row eh er n'yannoo reesht e ghraih, lesh ymmyd. “Shegin dou soie shoh,” dooyrt eh, “er-y-fa shen, son laa as laa.”

“Agh cre’n obbyr t’aym dy yannoo?” dooyrt Alice.

“Agh red erbee ta’d ymmyrchagh,” as y Footman, as t’eh toshiaght dy shleaystey.

“Oh, cha nel feme erbee dy loayrt rish,” dooyrt Alice dy row: “t’eh foalsey idioot!” As doshil ee yn dorrys as hie ee stiagh.

Va’n dorrys er n’gholl stiagh ’sy chidney mooar, va lliurit lesh smooinaght veih’n chione gys y chione elley: va’n Duchess ny soie er stool three-cosh ’sy mhean, as v’ee ’sy mhean ’sy chidney; va'n cook croymmey er y lossey, stiurey cauldron mooar va myr dy beagh eh lliurit lesh soup.

“Ta ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi!” Dooyrt Alice r’ee hene, choud as v’ee ry-hoi son sniaghtey.

Va ry-hoi ry-hoi ayns an aer. Eddyr y Duchess sniee eh ry-cheilley; as mychione y leigh, v'eh sniaghtey as glooish ry-cheilley fegooish ymmyd jeh'n aght. Yn un red v'ayns y chidney nagh row sniaghtey, v'eh y chochiart, as cat mooar v'er ny hoiaghey er y chorneil as glenney veih cluain gys cluain.

“Jeagh oo gra rhym,” as Alice, beggan sheeyney, son cha row ee dy jarroo shickyr row eh s’mie t’ee dy loayrt ec y toshiaght, “cre’n-fa ta dty chatt grinnaghey myr shen?”

"She cat Cheshire eh," dooyrt y Duchess, "as shen y chione. Muc!"

Dy row ee er n’insh y fockle s’jerree lesh y violency shen dy row Alice er n’immeeaght; agh honnick ee ayns y traa elley dy row eh er ny ghra rish y lhiannoo, as cha nee rish, as ghow ee courage, as hie ee er-gerrey reesht:—

“Cha row fys aym dy row cats Cheshire dy bragh glenney; ayns firrinys, cha row fys aym dy row cats glenney .”

“Ta’d ooilley,” dooyrt y Duchess; “as ta’n chooid smoo jeu jannoo.”

“Cha nel fys aym er red erbee ta jannoo,” as Alice dy mooar politely, as v’ee feer boggyssagh dy row ee er n’gholl stiagh ayns cowrey.

“Cha nel monney fys ayd,” dooyrt y Duchess; “as shen red firrinagh.”

Cha row Alice erbee ry-hoi rish tonn y ghraih shoh, as smooinee ee dy beagh eh cho mie dy chur stiagh red erbee elley jeh cowrey. As v’ee geddyn rish un red, ghow y cook y cauldron soup veih’n aile, as t’ee er n’yannoo obbyr dy row eh er n’yannoo rish y Duchess as rish y lhiannoo—haink ny iarnyn aile er-dy-hoshiaght; as eisht haink sniaghtey jeh saucepanyn, plateyn, as plateyn. Cha ghow y Duchess tastey jeu ny-yeih tra v’ad er n’yannoo rish; as va'n lhiannoo glooisht cho mooar er-dy-henney, dy row eh dy bragh doaltattym dy ghra row ny s'goanney rish ny s'goanney.

“Oh, cur-my -ner cre t’ou jannoo!” ’ dy ghra Alice, as t’ee shooyl seose as sheese ayns aggle dy ghraih. “Oh, t’eh goll er e shnuddey preeoil !” myr v’eh er n’eiyrt rish saucepan mooar neu-ghlare, as v’eh er-gerrey dy ghoaill eh ersooyl.

“Myr shen, my row dy chooilley ghooinney tastey jeh’n obbyr echey hene,” dooyrt y Duchess ayns growl hoarse, “by lhisagh y theihll goll mygeayrt y ghraih ny s’faggys da.”

“Cre’n aght nagh bee eh bunnys,” as Alice, v’ee feer glen dy gheddyn cowrey dy hoilshaghey beggan jeh’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo. "Smooinee-jee er cre'n obbyr v'eh dy yannoo rish y laa as y noid! Ta shiu er vakin dy vel y thalloo goaill ceautyn as feed oor dy ghleashaghey er e axe—"

“T’eh loayrt jeh axes,” dooyrt y Duchess, “geayrt jeh’n cheu!”

S’cosoylagh dy row Alice er n’eaishtagh rish y chook, dy akin my v’ee dy ghoaill y hint; agh va’n cook busy tastey y soup, as cha row eh myr nagh row eh cluster, as hie ee er-gerrey reesht: “Ceayrt-feed as feed oor, ta mee credjal ; ny t’eh daa-eed? I—”

“Oh, nagh jean oo m’y ghra ,” dooyrt y Duchess; “Cha row mee rieau ry-hoi figureyn!” As lesh shen t’ee er n’yannoo ymmyd jeh’n lhiannoo eck reesht, as t’ee sheeyney sorch jeh lullaby da myr t’ee jannoo shen, as t’ee cur shliaghtey violent da ec jerrey gagh line:

“Labhairt rish dty vac beg,
As beat eh tra t'eh sniaghtey:
T'eh jannoo eh ny-lomarcan dy yannoo ymmyd jeh,
Son ta fys echey dy vel eh tease.”

CORYS.
(Ayns haink y cook as y leigh stiagh):

"Wau! Wau! Wau!"

As tra v’ee y Duchess sheeyney yn daa verse jeh’n chanan, v’ee geddyn stiagh y lhiannoo dy violent er-skyn as sheese, as v’an red beg bochill glooish myr shen, nagh row Alice ry-chlashtyn ny goan:—

“Ta mee loayrt dy trome rish my vac,
Beat mee eh tra t’eh sniaghtey;
Son t’eh uss geddyn booise dy kiaralagh .
Yn piper tra ta’n chione echey!”

CORYS.

"Waa! Waa! Waa!"

"She shoh! Faag oo eh beggan, my shegin dhyt!" dooyrt y Duchess rish Alice, as t’ee fliaghey y lhiannoo er tra v’ee loayrt. “Shegin dou goll as jannoo aarloo dy chloie croquet rish y Bannister,” as hie ee magh ass y seihll. Throw y cook frying-pan er y ghraih echey tra hie ee magh, agh cha row eh ry-hoi.

Va Alice er n’yannoo y leigh lesh beggan obbyr, son v’eh ny chreeagh beg queer-shaped, as hrog eh e laueyn as e coshaghyn er ooilley ny h-ellanyn, “dy jarroo myr stereo-fish,” smooinee Alice. Va’n red beg bochill sniaghtey myr steam-engine tra v’ee er n’yannoo eh, as v’ee geddyn double as shleaystey eh hene reesht, myr shen, dy chooilley red, son y chiad minute ny daa, v’eh choud as v’ee ry-yannoo dy ghra rish.

As choud as v’ee er n’yannoo magh y raad cheddin dy n’yannoo rish, (va’n aght shen dy hroggal eh seose gys sorch dy ghraih, as eisht dy choyrt stiagh e chleaysh deiney as y chosh chleaysh, dy vod eh y stroie eh hene,) hug ee eh magh gys y ghrian foshlit. “Myr nagh jean mee y lhiannoo shoh y ghoaill ersooyl,” smooinee Alice, “ta’d shickyr dy vod ad eh y varroo ayns laa ny daa laa: nagh beagh eh murder dy lhiattee eh er-gerrey?” Dy jarroo, dooyrt ee ny goan s’jerree, as ghrunt y red beg ayns freggyrt (va’n red beg er n’eiyrt rish y sniaghtey ec y traa shoh). “Nagh jean oo grunt,” dooyrt Alice; “cha nel shen dy bragh aght cooie dy chur magh oo hene.”

Grunt y leigh reesht, as honnick Alice dy mooar ymmyrchagh er e veeal dy akin cre'n red v'ayn. Cha row ymmyd erbee jeh dy row sniaghtey dy -turn-up echey, mooar ny s’cosoylagh rish sniaghtey na sniaghtey glen; as va e sooillyn gobbraghey ard-beg son leshtal: dy chooilley red cha row Alice ry-hoi rish y red erbee. “Agh mraane cha row eh agh glenney,” dooyrt ee, as honnick ee stiagh ayns e sooillyn reesht, dy akin my va deayrtey erbee ayn.

Nish, cha row deayrtey. "Myr shen, my shegin dhyt cheet dy ve moddey, my ghraih," as Alice, dy firrinagh, "cha bee monney erbee aym dy yannoo rhyt. Mind now!" Va’n red beg bochill glooisht reesht (ny glooisht, cha row eh ry-hoi dy ghra cre’n red), as hie ad er-gerrey son ymmyd ayns siyll.

Va Alice ry-hoi ry-hoi dy smooinaghtyn r’ee hene, “Agh, cre’n obbyr t’aym dy yannoo rish y chreeagh shoh tra t’eh er n’gholl stiagh?” tra v’eh grunt reesht, cho violent, dy row ee er n’eaishtagh sheese ayns e veeal ayns red erbee dy ghra. Yn traa shoh cha row mee-chraueeaght erbee er: cha row eh ny s’leaie na ny s’leaie na muc, as va’n aght shen ry-akin dy beagh eh ry-hoi ry-ghoaill eh er-gerrey.

As hug ee yn cretoor beg sheese, as v’ee feer sheeyney tra honnick ee eh troggal ersooyl dy soit stiagh ayns y chosh. “My v’eh er n’gholl seose,” dooyrt ee r’ee hene, “veagh eh er n’yannoo leshtal s’glenney: agh t’eh jannoo muc s’glenney, ta mee credjal.” As t’ee er n’yannoo smooinaghtyn er cloan elley v’ee er n’yannoo, oddys jannoo dy mie myr muckyn, as v’ee dy jarroo gra r’ee hene, “my row dooinney erbee er n’yannoo y raad cheddin dy chaghlaa ad—” tra v’ee er n’yannoo beggan skeeal tra honnick ee yn Cat Cheshire ny hoie er brass craid beggan yard jeh.

Cha row y Cat agh glenney tra honnick eh Alice. Va’n ghrian ry-akin, dooyrt ee: agh v’eh ry-akin dy row clawnyn ard -hiar as mooar dy veeal, shen-y-fa va’n ghrian ry-akin dy beagh eh ry-akin lesh ooashley.

“Cheshire Puss,” t’ee er n’yannoo, s’cosoylagh, son cha row fys eck erbee row’n ennym echey: agh cha row eh agh glenney beggan ny s’leaie. “Ty, t’eh boggyssagh choud as shoh,” smooinee Alice, as hie ee er-soylley. “Jeagh oo gra rhym, my t’ou uss, cre’n raad lhisagh mee goll veih shoh?”

“T’eh dependit dy mie er c’raad t’ou uss dy gholl,” as y Cat.

“Cha nel mee s’cosoylagh rish c’raad—” as Alice.

“Shen-y-fa cha nel eh red erbee cre’n raad t’ou goll,” dooyrt y Cat.

“—choud as ta mee ry-gheddyn ayns ard-valley ,” as Alice myr mraane.

“Oh, t’ou shickyr dy jean oo shen,” dooyrt y Cat, “my t’ou goll fadagh dy liooar.”

Va Alice er n’yannoo rish nagh row eh ry-hoi, as shen-y-fa ren ee feddyn cowrey elley. “Cre’n sorch dy leih ta cummal mygeayrt shoh?”

"Sy çheu shen ," as y Cat, croymmey e laue yesh mygeayrt, "ta Hatter beaghey: as 'sy cheu shen ," croghey yn laue elley, "ta Lionney March beaghey. Taitnys jeh'n derrey yeh ta dty aigney: t'ad ooilley nyn 'oï."

“Agh cha nel mee erreish dy gholl mastey sleih craid,” as Alice.

"Oh, cha vod oo jannoo rish shen," dooyrt y Cat: "ta shin ooilley boggyssagh 'sy cheer shoh. Ta mee boggyssagh. Ta shiu boggyssagh."

“Cre’n aght ta fys ayd dy vel mee mee-chrauee?” dooyrt Alice.

“Shegin dhyt ve,” dooyrt y Cat, “ny cha beagh oo er jeet stiagh.”

Cha row Alice creeney dy row shen prowal eh; agh, hie ee er “As kys ta fys ayd dy vel oo boggyssagh?”

"Dy toshiaght," dooyrt y Cat, "cha nel cow foalsey. T'ou cur da shen?"

“Ta mee credjal dy vel,” as Alice.

"Well, then," hie yn Cat er-soylley, "ta shiu er vakin, ta cow glooish tra ta'n fear, as t'eh croghey e shlieau tra t'eh booiagh. Nish ta mee glooish tra ta mee booiagh, as croo my shlieau tra ta mee fear. Shen-y-fa ta mee mee-chrauee."

“ Ta mee gra purring, cha nee grow,” as Alice.

“Call eh shen ta’n aght t’ou uss,” dooyrt y Cat. “Bee oo cloie croquet rish y Bannister y laa shoh?”

“Shegin dou eh y ghoaill rish,” dooyrt Alice, “agh cha nel mee er ny choyrt cuirrey foast.”

“Shegin dhyt mee y akin shen,” as y Cat, as hie eh ersooyl.

Cha row Alice s’odjey jeh’n red shoh, v’ee er n’yannoo rish reddyn queer ta goll er. As tra v’ee jeeaghyn er y raad v’eh, haink eh magh dy tappee reesht.

“By-the-bye, cre’n red haink er y leigh?” dooyrt y Cat. “Cha row mee er-diu dy ghoaill.”

“T’eh er n’gholl stiagh ayns moddey,” as Alice dy s’smooar, myr dy beagh eh er jeet stiagh reesht ayns aght najooragh.

“S’doyrt mee dy beagh eh,” as y Cat, as hie eh ersooyl reesht.

Va Alice erreish beggan, lesh leshtal dy row ee er ny akin reesht, agh cha daink eh er-ash, as lurg minute ny daa minute hie ee er-gerrey gys y raad raad v’eh er n’yannoo y March Hare. “Ta mee er vakin hatters roish,” dooyrt ee r’ee hene; “by-hickyr dy bee y March Hare ny s’inshley, as mraane er-yn-oyr dy vel shoh May cha bee eh raving mad—er-y-fa shen cha bee eh cho mad as v’eh ayns March.” As tra dooyrt ee shoh, honnick ee seose, as va'n Cat reesht, ny hoie er brass craid.

“Dy row oo gra muc, ny fig?” dooyrt y Cat.

“Dow mee moddey,” dreggyr Alice; “as ta mee booiagh nagh jean oo cummal er-gerrey as goll ersooyl cho tappee: t’ou jannoo un red dy liooar.”

“S’goan,” as y Cat; as y traa shoh hie eh ersooyl dy moal, toshiaght lesh y ghrinney, as crommey lesh y ghrinney, v'er-mayrn ry-hoi tra v'eh er n'gholl ersooyl.

“Well! Ta mee er vakin cat gyn grian dy mennick,” smooinee Alice; "agh grian gyn cat! She'n rud s'curious t'aym er vaik ayns my vea!"

Cha row ee er n’gholl s’faggys da’n thie jeh’n March Hare: s’cosoylagh dy row eh y thie cheddin, son va’n chimney ry-hoi ry-hoi as va’n mullagh ry-hoi ry-hoi. Va'n tie cho mooar, nagh row ee booiagh dy gholl er-gerrey derrey v'ee er n'eiyrt red erbee jeh'n mushroom chleaysh, as hrog ee ee hene gys mysh daa three-feed erskyn: eer ec y traa shen hie ee seose huggey, as dooyrt ee r'ee hene, "S'cosoylagh dy beagh eh er n'gholl stiagh er-gerrey!"
