CAPPYL III.
Race-Caucus as Story Fadagh

Va’d dy jarroo partid queer-looking v’er nyn jaglym cooidjagh er y rank—ny eaystyn lesh cleayshyn dragged, ny beiyn lesh nyn fur cliaghtey rish, as ooilley drippey fliugh, crosh, as neu-chosney.

Yn chiad cheesh s’cosoylagh v’eh, kys dy row ad er n’gholl stiagh reesht: v’ad er n’yannoo co-choyrle mychione shoh, as lurg beggan minutyn v’eh myr red s’natural da Alice dy row ee er n’yannoo rish dy row ee er n’yannoo rish, myr dy beagh ee er n’yannoo rish ooilley e vee. Gooidsave lhiam dy row ee er n’yannoo arrey ard rish y Lory, as v’eh er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen, as cha row eh er-y-fa shen gra, “Ta mee ny s’odjey na oo, as shegin dou fys ’ghoaill ny share;” as cha row Alice shoh er lesh gyn fys cre'n aght v'eh, as, myr v'eh er n'yannoo rish y Lory dy ghra rish e vleeantyn, cha row monney erbee dy ghra.

Er-jerrey, ghow y Lugh, va myr v'eh dooinney dy ard-reill nyn mast'oc, gra, "Sheign-jee sheese, shiu ooilley, as clasht-jee rhym! Nee'm shiu y yannoo sheeyney dy leah!" Shuigh ad ooilley sheese ec y traa cheddin, ayns ring mooar, as y Lugh ayns y mean. Va Alice geddyn e sooillyn stiagh er, son v’ee shickyr dy row ee er gheddyn drogh-chreeagh mannagh row ee er n’gholl stiagh dy h-oik.

“Ah!” ’ as y Lugh lesh aer p’oor, “vees shiu ooilley er nyn yannoo aarloo? She shoh y red s’trimmid ta fys aym er. Silence ooilley mygeayrt, my shegin dhyt! ‘Villiam y Choonceil, va’n cause echey er ny choyrt rish y phapa, cha b’echey traa v’eh er ny choyrt fo’n Sauish, v’eh er ny choyrt fo’n chiarn, as v’eh er ve ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi. Yn Tuath-Twoaie—'”

“Ugh!” dooyrt y Lory, lesh y ghraih.

“Ta mee guee ort pardoon!” dooyrt y Lugh, s’cosoylagh, agh dy mooar politely: “V’ou er loayrt?”

“Cha nee mish!” dooyrt y Lory dy hrooid.

“S’cosoylagh dy row oo er n’yannoo,” as y Lugh. "-Ta mee goll er-gerrey. 'Dy row Edwin as Morcar, earlyn Mercia as Northumbria, er n'insh da: as va Stigand hene, ard-easbaghyn ard-valley Canterbury, er n'insh da.

“Foddey cre ?” dooyrt y Duck.

“Found it ,” dreggyr y Lugh raad crossagh: “dy jarroo ta fys ayd cre ta ‘it’ dy ghra.”

"Ta fys aym cre ta 'eh' dy ghra, tra ta mee feddyn red," dooyrt y Duck: "she frog ny clowan t'ayn ayns y chooid smoo. Yn cheesh, cre'n red honnick y ard-easb?"

Cha dug y Lugh tastey da'n cheesh shoh, agh hie eh er-gerrey dy hrooid, "'—fakin dy row eh cooie dy gholl marish Edgar Atheling dy chosney William as dy chur da'n coron. t’eh er n’eiyrt, as t’eh er n’eiyrt gys Alice myr v’eh loayrt.

“Choud as v’eh fliugh,” dooyrt Alice ayns tonn melancholy: “cha nel eh myr dy vel eh drymmey mee dy bragh.”

“Ayns y chooish shen,” dooyrt y Dodo solemn, ry-hoi, “ta mee cur stiagh dy bee’n cho-choyrle er ny choyrt gys jerrey, son y ghraih dy h-oikoil jeh remedyyn ny s’energeagh—”

“Labhairt Bearla!” dooyrt yn Eayn. “Cha nel fys aym er y chiall jeh leshtal ny goan ard shen, as, cre’n red s’mooar, cha nel mee credjal dy vel oo toiggal myrgeddin!” As chroym y Eaglet sheese e ben dy scughey smile: t’ad cuid jeh’n eayst elley titterit cliaghtey.

“Yn red v’aym dy ghra,” dooyrt y Dodo ayns tonn offense, “va, dy beagh y red s’mish dy chur shin drym, v’eh Caucus-race.”

“Cre’n red ta’n Caucus-race?” dooyrt Alice; cha nee dy row ee er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen, agh v'eh er n'yannoo staydys myr dy beagh eh credjal dy beagh dooinney erbee er loayrt, as cha row dooinney erbee elley er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen er-y-fa shen.

“Cre’n-fa,” dooyrt y Dodo, “y raad smoo dy m’ymmyrkey eh dy yannoo eh.” (As, myr t'ou uss goaill rish y red oo hene, laa erbee 'sy gheimmey, verym fys dhyt kys ren y Dodo eh y rheynn.)

Ard-eash markee eh magh race-course, ayns sorch dy chiark, (“cha nel y form exact ymmyrchagh,” dooyrt eh,) as eisht v’ad ooilley ny partaghyn er nyn skeaylley er y raad, ec y traa t’ayn. Cha row “Un, daa, three, as ersooyl,” agh t’ad toshiaght dy reayll tra v’ad booiagh, as t’ad geddyn stiagh tra v’ad booiagh, myr shen cha row eh erbee dy hoiggal tra v’eh er-gerrey. Agh, tra v’ad er reih lesh oor ny s’anmey, as v’ad ry-hoi ry-hoi, ghow y Dodo magh dy h-oik “T’eh er-gerrey!” as v’ad ooilley crooit mygeayrt, geddyn shee, as goaill rish, “Agh quoi t’er ghoaill?”

Yn cheesh shoh cha row y Dodo ry-hoi freggyrt gyn mooarane smooinaghtyn, as hie eh seose son y chooid smoo lesh un veeal ry-hoi er e eddin (y stayd ayn ta shiu ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi Shakespeare, ayns ny pictuyryn echey), tra v’adsyn elley ry-hoi ry-hoi. Er-jerrey dooyrt y Dodo, “ Ta ooilley ny h-oik er ghoaill, as shegin da ooilley ny h-oik y ghoaill.”

“Agh quoi eh dy choyrt ny prizeyn?” t’eh ry-hoi chorus dy voylley.

“Cre’n-fa, she , s’cosoylagh,” dooyrt y Dodo, as eh cur stiagh Alice lesh un veeal; as va'n chooid smoo jeh'n chloan ry-cheilley ry-cheilley, as ad gra ayns aght s'cosoylagh, "Prizes! Prizes!"

Cha row fys ec Alice cre y yannoo, as ayns y vee-chraueeaght hug ee e laue ayns e phoose, as t'ee er n'yannoo box comfits, (luckyly cha row an ushtey sallagh er n'gholl stiagh ayn), as hug ee ad mygeayrt myr prizeyn. Va un a-piece dy jarroo, ooilley round.

“Agh shegin da prize y ghoaill urree hene, ta fys ayd,” as y Lugh.

“S’cosoylagh,” dreggyr y Dodo dy mooar. “Cre elley t’ayd ayns dty phoosey?” t’eh er n’gholl er-gerrey, as t’eh er n’irree gys Alice.

“Cha nel agh thimble,” as Alice dy brynnagh.

“Cur eh stiagh shoh,” dooyrt y Dodo.

As hrog ad ooilley mygeayrt urree reesht, tra hug y Dodo solemnly y thimble, gra “Ta shin guee ort dy ghoaill y thimble elegant shoh;” as, tra v'eh er chlashtyn rish y ghlare shoh, ghow ad ooilley boggey.

Va Alice er smaoiney dy row y chooid smoo jeh’n red shen feer absurd, agh v’ad ooilley cho grave as nagh row ee geddyn greme; as, myr nagh row ee ry-hoi red erbee dy ghra, chroym ee sheese, as ghow ee y thimble, as v’ee sheeyney cho solemn as v’ee.

Yn rud er-gerrey v’eh dy ee ny comfits: ren shoh ymmyd jeh reddyn ry-chlashtyn as ry-chlashtyn, son v’ad ny eaystyn mooarey geamrey nagh row ad ry-chlashtyn rish nyn eaystyn, as v’ad ny eaystyn beggey ry-chlashtyn as v’ad ry-chlashtyn rish nyn eaystyn beggey. Agh, va'n red er-gerrey, as hie ad sheese reesht ayns ring, as ghow ad arrane rish y Lugh dy ghra roo red erbee elley.

“Ghow oo geill dy ghra rhym dty tashtey, ta fys ayd,” as Alice, “as cre’n-fa t’ou uss glenney—C as D,” as t’ee gra rish ayns shliaghtey, lesh lesh aggle dy beagh eh er ny ghra rish reesht.

“Ta’n skeeal aym’s skeeal ard as trimshagh!” dooyrt y Lugh, as t’eh geddyn stiagh ayns Alice, as t’eh glenney.

“ T’eh ny s’faggys da, dy shickyr,” as Alice, as ee jeeaghyn sheese lesh ymmyd er s’faggys da’n Lugh; “agh cre’n-fa t’ou gra trimshagh?” As hie ee er-gerrey dy ghra mychione jeh tra v’eh loayrt, myr shen va’n tushtey eck jeh’n skeeal myr shoh:—

“Cha nel oo ry-gheddyn!” dooyrt y Lugh rish Alice dy ard. “Cre’n red t’ou smooinaghtyn er?”

“Ta mee guee ort,” dooyrt Alice dy mooar ymmyrchagh: “va’d er jeet gys y chiaghtoo cronk, ta mee credjal?”

“ Cha row mee! ”, ghra y Lugh, s’creeney as s’feer.

“Gnoid!” dooyrt Alice, dy bragh aarloo dy yannoo ee hene feohdagh, as jeeaghyn er-gerrey urree. “Oh, lhig dou cooney dy ghra rish!”

“Cha jean-ym red erbee jeh’n sorch,” as y Lugh, as eh er n’irree as er n’gholl ersooyl. “T’ou uss mee y ymmyrkey lesh loayrt myr shen!”

“Cha row mee er n’yannoo eh!” ’ dy ghra Alice bochill. “Agh t’ou cho s’easyl dy ghra, ta fys ayd!”

Yn Lugh cha row eh agh growlt ayns freggyrtagh.

“Tarree, t’ou uss ry-hoi, as jean dty scansh y chlashtyn!” Ghlare Alice er y ghraih; as haink ny laghyn elley cooidjagh ayns chorus, “Ta, jean-jee!” agh cha row y Lugh agh shleaystey e ben dy s’anmey, as hie eh beggan ny s’quickey.

“Cre’n aght nagh beagh eh er n’eiyrt!” ’s eh y Lory, choud as v’eh er-mayrn jeh’n sight; as ghow sean Crab y chowrey dy ghra rish e niart "Ah, my ghraih! Lhig da shoh ve lesson dhyt dy chaill oo dty ghraih!" “Cur dty veeal, Ma!” dooyrt y Crab aeg, beggan snappish. “T’ou doaltattym dy phrowal sheean oyster!”

“S’maynrey lhiam dy beagh ar Dinah ec y traa t’ayn, ta fys aym dy vel!” dooyrt Alice dy ard, as cha row ee loayrt rish dooinney erbee. “Cha row ee er n’yannoo eh ry-cheilley!”

“As quoi eh Dinah, my shegin dou y cheesh y ghoaill?” dooyrt y Lory.

Dreggyr Alice dy h-earroo, son v'ee dy bragh er-gerrey dy loayrt mychione e phet: "Ta'n cat ain Dinah. As t'ee cho capital son ymmyd jeh luchtyn nagh vod oo smooinaghtyn er! As oh, baillim dy beagh oo er ny akin erreish da'n eayst! Cre'n-fa, nee'm eayst beg y ee choud as t'ee jeeaghyn er!"

Va’n obbyr shoh er n’yannoo scansh ard-vooaralagh mastey’n phartid. Va cuid jeh'n eayst er n'gholl ersooyl dy h-oikoil: t'un sean Magpie er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh'n aght shoh dy ghra, "S'cosoylagh dy vel mee er n'gholl stiagh 'sy thie; cha nel an aer-oie cordail rish my throat!" as ghow Canary rish e chloan lesh coraa creeoil, "Tree-jee ersooyl, my chloan! T'eh er-dy-henney dy row shiu ooilley ayns nyn lhiabbee!" Er y ghraih dy row ad ooilley er n'gholl ersooyl, as cha b'echey traa dy row Alice er-mayrn.

“S’mish nagh row mee er n’yannoo rish Dinah!” dooyrt ee r’ee hene ayns tonn melancholy. "Cha nel dooinney erbee ry-akin r'ee, sheese shoh, as ta mee shickyr dy nee'n cat share er y theihll t'ayn! Oh, my ghraih Dinah! Ta mee goaill aggle nagh jean-ym oo y akin ny s'anmey!" As ec y traa shoh t’ee Alice bochill toshiaght dy ghra reesht, son v’ee feer sheeyney as feer sheeyney. Ayns red beg, agh, chlashtyn ee reesht beggan pattern jeh steatyn-coshee er-dy-henney, as hug ee sheese er-gerrey, lesh leshtal dy row y Lugh er chaghlaa e mean, as dy row eh er-gerrey dy chur seose e skeeal.
