Dreggys ec y traa shen s’cosoylagh dy row e ben ry-hoi rish mullagh y halley: ayns firrinys v’ee nish ny s’yrjey na jeih trooid, as ghow ee y lhong beg oor dy kiart as hie ee er-gerrey gys dorrys y ghard.

Alice bochill! Va’n red shen choud as v’ee ry-yannoo, ry-lowal er y laue, dy yeeaghyn trooid y ghard lesh un sooill; agh dy gholl trooid v'eh ny s'doaltattym na rish traa erbee: hie ee sheese as t'ee er n'gholl stiagh reesht.

"Dy beagh oo boggyssagh jeh dty hene," dooyrt Alice, "nieu mooar myr oo," (shegin da shoh y ghra), "dy gholl er-gerrey dy ghra ayns y raad shoh! Stop y traa shoh, ta mee gra rhyt!" Agh hie ee er-gerrey ooilley myr shen, as sheeyney gallonyn dy ghraih, derrey v’eh ry-hoi ’sy lhong mooar mygeayrt, mysh ceaurt inchid dy dowin as ry-hoi ’sy lhong.

As lurg y traa shen chlashtyn ee beggan pattern cosh er-gerrey, as hrog ee sheese e sooillyn dy hrooid dy akin cre v'er-gerrey. Va'n Coonney Ghilley er-dy-heet, dressit dy s'smooar, lesh paart gloves kid geal ayns un laue as fan mooar ayns y laue elley: haink eh trotting er-gerrey ayns ard-chree, as v'eh mraane rish hene tra v'eh cheet, "Oh! y Duchess, y Duchess! Oh! cha bee ee er ny choadey myr shen!" Va Alice feer glen as dy row ee er-gerrey dy ghoaill cooney veih dooinney erbee; as shen-y-fa, tra haink y Coonnee er-gerrey da, t’ee er n’yannoo, lesh coraa low, s’goanney, “My shegin dhyt, sir—” T’eh er n’yannoo y Coonney dy violent, t’eh er n’yannoo y gloves kid geal as y fan, as t’eh er n’yannoo skurrit ersooyl gys y dorraghys cho hard as v’eh uss.

Ghow Alice yn fan as ny gloves, as, myr va'n hall feer t'er n'eiyrt, v'ee er n'eiyrt ee hene ooilley'n traa v'ee goll er loayrt: "Chaarj, chaarj! Cre'n queer ta ooilley ny reddyn 'sy laa jiu! “Ta mee shickyr nagh nee ny goan shen ny goan cooie,” dooyrt Alice bochill, as va e sooillyn lionnit lesh deayrtey reesht tra hie ee er-gerrey, “feddym ve Mabel erreish da ooilley ny reddyn, as bee’m dy gholl as dy chummal ayns y thie beg poky shen, as cha bee’m erbee jeh’n jaghyn dy chloie, as oh! stay down here! Cha bee feme orroo dy chur nyn ben sheese as dy ghra 'Thig seose reesht, s'cosoylagh!' Cha jean-ym agh seaghney seose as gra 'Cre'n dooinney t'aym eisht? Ta mee cho staydoil dy ve ooilley my lomarcan ’sy cheer shoh!”

As t'ee gra shoh, honnick ee sheese er e laueyn, as v'ee boggyssagh dy hoiggal dy row ee er chur urree un jeh gloves beg geal y Rabbit tra v'ee loayrt. “Cre’n aght oddys mee shen y yannoo?” smooinee ee. “Shegin dou ve beg reesht.” Dreggyr ee as hie ee gys y table dy tommey ee hene liorish, as honnick ee dy row ee, cho s’faggys as v’ee dy ghra, mysh daa three-feed erskyn, as v’ee goll er shrinkey dy hrooid: cha b’echey traa dy hoig ee dy nee’n aght v’ee er n’yannoo shen v’eh y fan v’ee geddyn, as hug ee eh sheese dy hrooid, ry-hoi ry-hoi dy row ee er shliaghtey ersooyl altother.

“ B’eh shen s’cummey!” dooyrt Alice, feer agglagh jeh’n chaghlaa taitnyssagh, agh feer glen dy gheddyn ee hene foast ayns bea; “as nish son y ghard!” as ren ee rheynn rish ooilley ny sniessey rish y dorrys beg: agh, alas! va'n dorrys beg er ny ghleie reesht, as va'n alkey beg oor er ny lhoobey er y board glass myr roish, "as ta ny reddyn ny s'mish na rish," smooinee'n lhiannoo bochill, "son cha row mee rieau cho beg as shoh roish, riamh! As ta mee cur fys dy vel eh ro-doaltattym, dy vel eh!"

As t’ee gra ny goan shoh, sliee eh e chosh, as ayns y traa elley, splash! v'ee gys e shlieau ayns ushtey salagh. Yn chiad smooinaghtyn echey v’eh dy row ee er n’gholl sheese ayns y marrey ayns aght erbee, “as ayns y chooish shen, m’aigney dy gholl ry-cheilley lesh raad-iarail,” dooyrt ee r’ee hene. (Va Alice er ve ec y marrey un uair ayns e vioys, as v'ee er jeet gys y cho-choyrle coontit, dy row c'raad erbee dy row oo er y chost Sauish, dy row ymmodee mraane-bath ayns y marrey, cuid jeh'n lught-thie ry-chlashtyn ayns y ghainney lesh spadeyn cowrey, as ry-cheilley row thieyn-losht, as ry-cheilley stashoon raad-iarail. v'ee jeih feed erskyn.

“By vie lhiam nagh row mee er ghra choud!” dooyrt Alice, tra v’ee shleaystey mygeayrt, as v’ee shirrey y raad y gheddyn magh. "Bee'm er ny stroie er-y-fa shen nish, ta mee credjal, liorish ve er my stroie ayns my deayrtey hene! Bee shen red queer, dy shickyr! Agh, ta ooilley ny reddyn queer y laa shoh."

Dreggys ec y traa shen chlashtyn ee red erbee splashey mygeayrt 'sy lhong beggan jeh'n raad, as s'cosoylagh dy row ee er n'gholl stiagh dy hoiggal cre'n red v'ayn: 'sy toshiaght smooinee ee dy beagh eh walrus ny hippopotamus, agh eisht hug ee coontey cho beg v'ee nish, as cha b'echey traa dy hoiggal nagh row agh lugh v'er shliaghtey stiagh myr ee hene.

"Veagh eh jeh feme erbee, nish," smooinee Alice, "dy loayrt rish y lugh shoh? “Ta’n aght nagh vel eh tushtey Bearlagh,” smooinee Alice; “Ta mee credjal dy nee lugh Frangagh eh, tar stiagh marish William y Choonceil.” (Gan, lesh ooilley e ghraih er tashtey, cha row Alice ry-akin dy liooar cre’n aght v’eh er n’yannoo red erbee.) As shen-y-fa t’ee toshiaght reesht: “Où est ma chatte?” va'n chied obbyr ayns e lioar-lesson Frangagh. Va'n Lugh er n'eiyrt magh ass y ushtey, as v'eh myr dy row eh cliaghtey ooilley e chree lesh aggle. “Oh, ta mee guee erriu!” ’ dy ghra Alice dy hrooid, aggle dy row ee er ghoaill rish y veeal bochill. “Cha row mee er-diu dy row cats ry-hoi.”

“Cha nee myr cats!” ’ dy ghra y Lugh, ayns coraa shliaghtey, s’paasjagh. “Veagh oo booiagh rish cats my v’ou mish?”

"Well, maybe not," as Alice ayns tonney soothing: "na bee fearg jeh. As foast baillim dy beagh mee er hoilshaghey dhyt yn cat ain Dinah: Ta mee credjal dy beagh oo er n'yannoo ymmyd jeh cats my shegin dhyt y akin. y lossey, leckey e laghyn as glenney e veeal—as she red bog s’niessey t’ayn dy n’yannoo—as t’ee cho capital son glenney luchtyn—oh, ta mee guee ort!” ’ dy ghra Alice reesht, son y traa shoh va’n Lugh bristling ooilley, as v’ee feer glen dy row eh dy jarroo er ny ghra. “Cha nel shin loayrt jeh ny smoo my shegin dhyt.”

“Shin dy jarroo!” ’ dy ghra y Lugh, v’er n’eiyrt sheese gys jerrey e shlieau. "Myr dy beagh mee loayrt mychione y chooish shoh! Va'n teihll ain dy bragh glenney cats: ruddyn vee-chrauee, low, vulgar! Na lhig dou clashtyn yn ennym reesht!"

“Cha jean-ym dy jarroo!” dooyrt Alice, ayns ard-chree dy chaghlaa yn theay jeh’n cho-chowrey. “As vel oo—vees oo boggyssagh—er—cooin?” Cha dug y Lugh freggyrt da, as hie Alice er-ash dy h-earroo: "Ta cow beg cho mie ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi ry-hoi! Terrier beg lesh sooillyn glen, ta fys ayd, lesh oh, cho ard as grunt brown! As bee eh ry-hoi ry-hoi tra t'ou uss ry-hoi, as bee eh ry-hoi ry-hoi as ry-hoi ry-hoi. jeu—as t’eh jeh farmer, ta fys ayd, as t’eh gra dy vel eh cho feohdagh, t’eh gra dy vel eh marrey ooilley ny rattyn as—oh ghraih!” ghra Alice ayns tonney trimshagh, “Ta aggle orrym dy vel mee er n’offrey eh reesht!” Son va'n Lugh sniemmey ersooyl veih'n ven cho hard as v'eh able dy gholl, as dy yannoo ard-vooinjer ayns y lhong myr v'eh goll.

As shen-y-fa ghow ee y ghlare softly erreish da, "Mouse dear! Tar reesht, as cha jean shin loayrt mychione cats ny cooin myrgeddin, mannagh vel oo boggyssagh orroo!" As tra chlashtyn y Lugh shoh, t'eh er n'irree as t'eh er n'irree reesht huggey: va'n veeal echey feer pale (lesh paart, smooinee Alice), as dooyrt eh lesh boght creoi, "Lhig dooin cheet gys y tram, as eisht verym fys dhyt my stayr, as bee oo tushtey cre'n-fa ta mee cur aggle da cats as cooin."

Va’n traa er-dy-henney dy gholl, son va’n lhong ry-hoi ry-hoi rish ny eayst as ny beiyn v’er n’immeeaght ayn: va Duck as Dodo, Lory as Eaglet, as ymmodee crooaghyn curious elley. Va Alice er y raad, as sniee yn chloan ooilley gys y tram.
