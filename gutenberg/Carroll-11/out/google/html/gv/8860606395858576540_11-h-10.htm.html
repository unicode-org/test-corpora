Yn Quadrill Lobster

Va'n Mock Turtle glenney dy doaltattym, as t'eh tarroogh y dhrogh-chree jeh un flapper er e sooillyn. Smooinee eh er Alice, as ren eh shirrey dy loayrt, agh son un minute ny daa mhinid v’eh er n’yannoo sniaghtey er e ghoo. “Shegin da cramp y ghoaill ayns e ghlioon,” dooyrt y Gryphon: as t’eh er n’obbraghey dy ghraih eh as dy ghraih eh ayns e ghlioon.
