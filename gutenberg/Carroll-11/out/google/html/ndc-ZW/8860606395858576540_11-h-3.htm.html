CHITSAUKO III.
Mujaho weCaucus uye Nyaya Refu

Ivona vakaita inga boka rinovoneka inga rinoshamisa raiungana pa ndau inogara mhepo—shiri jainga no minhenga yakakwevehwa, zvipuka zvainga no makushe azvo zvakabatirira phedo nazvo, zve zvese zvaidonha zvakanyorova, zvakaphambana, zve zvicikakhatari.

Mubvunzo wekutanga wemene waiva wekuti, vanozoomazve sei: vakaita kubvunzurudza ngendaa iyi, uye mushure mwemaminitsi mashomani zvakaita inga zvakajairika yaamho kuna Alice kuzviwana eibhuya zvakajairika navo, ingatei waivaziya hupenyu hwake hweshe. Igwinyiso, wakaita nharo yakareba maningi na Lory, uwo pakupeisira wakazoita nharo, zve waizobhuya basi kuti, “Ndinokura kukudarika, zve ndinodikana kuziva zviri nane;” uye izvi Alice aisazotendera asikazii kuti zvainge zvakadini, uye, sezvo Lory yakaramba zvakanaka kuti ibhuye makore ayo, pakanga pasina chimweni chaizobhuyiwa.

Pakupeisira Mbeva, iyo yaiita inga munhu une simba pakati pavo, yakadaidzira, "Garai pashi, imwimwi mweshe, mundizwe! Ndichakasira kumuoma zvakakwana!" Veshe vakagara pashi nguva imweyo, mumhete yakakura, Mbeva iri pakati. Alice wakaramba akazvisotesesa ngo kuthya, ngokuti waijizwa kuti waizobatwa ngo ndhendha yakashata maningi kudari acikazivi kuoma ngo kukasika.

“Eya!” akadaro Mbeva ngemhepo yakakosha, "mwakagadzirira mweshe ere? Ichi ndicho chiro chakaoma zvikuru chandinoziya. Kunyarara kweshe, kana uchida! 'William Mukunda, uyo chikonzero chake chakadakadzwa ndi papa, akakurumidza kuzviisa pasi pevaNgezi, avo vaida vatungamiri, uye vainge vajaira zvikuru kukunda Edcar naMercia neMercia uye usurpation. Northumbria—’”

"Haiwa!" vakadaro vaLory vachidedera.

“Ndinokumbira ruregerero!” akadaro Mbeva, achitsveruka, asi ane hunhu hwakanyanya: “Wakataura here?”

“Haiwa inini!” vakadaro vaLory vachikurumidza.

“Ndaifunga kuti wakazviita,” wakadaro Mbeva. “—Ndinoenderera mberi. ‘Edwin na Morcar, vatungamiriri ve Mercia ne Northumbria, vakamuzivisa: uye kunyange Stigand, mukuru we bhishopi we Canterbury anoda nyika yake, wakaona kuti zvakanaka—’”

“Wawana chii ?” akadaro Dhadha.

“ Ndakazviwana ,” Mbeva yakapindura zvishoma: “chokwadi munoziva kuti ‘izvo’ zvinorevei.”

"Ndinoziya zvakanaka kuti 'izvo' zvinoronza chiinyi, pandinowana chiro," wakadaro Dhadha: "kazhinji kacho i jerhye kana gondo. Mubvunzo ndewekuti, chiinyi chakawanikwa nge archbishop?"

Mbeva haizi kuona mubvunzo uyu, asi yakaenderera mberi ngekukasira, "'—yakaona zvakanaka kuenda naEdgar Atheling kunosangana naWilliam uye kumupa korona. Kuzvibata kwaWilliam pakutanga kwaive pakati nepakati. Asi kusazvikudza kwevaNorman vake -' Uri kuenderera mberi sei zvino, mudiwa wangu?" yakaenderera mberi, ichitendeukira kuna Alice sezvayaitaura.

Alice wakananga ngo izwi ro kusuruvara kuti: “Kudai ngo zvainga zvakanyorova, zvinovoneka inga kuti azvindiomisi kamare.”

“Mu mugariro wondhowo,” wakadaro Dodo ngo kureremeja, wakasimuka ngo makumbo ake, “ndinokurudzira kuti musongano uenderere mberi, kuti pave no kukasika kuashira mutombo uno simba maningi—”

“Reketa Chirungu!” akadaro Gondo. “Andizivi zvinoreva hafu ye mazwi aya akareba, uye, chiinyi chimweni, andigondi kuti iwewe unozviziya!” Uye Gondo rakakotamisa soro raro kuti rifishe kushekerera: dzimweni shiri dzimweni dzakachemerera zvinozwika.

“Zvendaizobhuya,” wakadaro Dodo ngezwi rakanyangadzwa, “ndekuti, chiro chakanaka maningi chekutiita kuti tiome kwaizova Caucus-race.”

“Mujaho weCaucus unozwi chiinyi?” akadaro Alice; kwete kuti aida kuziya zvakawanda, asi Dodo rakanga ramboema sekuti raifunga kuti munhu anofanira kubhuya, uye hapana umweni waiita inga wakada kubhuya chiro.

Dodo wakati: “Ngenyi, njira yakanakisisa yo kuzvisanangura ngo yo kuzviita.” (Na, inga zvaungada kuzama chirocho wega, rimweni zuwa remuchando, ndinozokuronzera kuti Dodo wakazvibata sei.)

Kutanga yakakombidza ndau yekukwikwidzana, mumhando ye denderedzwa, (“chimiro chaicho hachina ndaa,” yakati,) uyezve mabiko eshe akaiswa parutii rwemujaho, pano nepapo. Painga pasina “Umwe, vaviri, vatatu, no kure,” asi vaitanga kurumba pavaida, vosiya pavaida, ngo kudaro zvainga zvicizivi kureruka kuziva kuti mujaho wainga wapera rini. Asi, pavainge varumba hafu yeawa kana kudaro, uye vakaoma maningizve, Dodo wakakasira kudaidza kuti “Mujaho wapera!” vose vakaungana vakaritendenedza, vachifema, vachibvunza kuti, “Asi ndiani akakunda?”

Mubvunzo uyu Dodo aazi kukona kudaira asina kufunga kakurutu, wakagara nguwa yakareba ngemunwe umwe wakasungirirwa pahuma yake (chigaro chaunowanzoona Shakespeare, mumifananidzo yake), vamweni veshe vakaemera vakanyarara. Pakupeisira Dodo wakati, “ Munthu weshe wakunda, neveshe vanofanira kuva nemibairo.”

“Esi ndiani anozopa mibairo?” chaizvoizvo chorus yemazwi akabvunza.

“Ngei, iyena , zvemene,” wakadaro Dodo, ecikhombija Alice ngo mundhu umwe; uye boka rese pakarepo rakamupoteredza, richidaidzira nenzira yakavhiringika, "Mibairo! Mibairo!"

Alice wakanga asizi kuziya kuti aitenyi, ngekupererwa kwegonda wakaisa nyara yake muhomwe yake, wakabudisa bhokisi rema comfits, (rombo rakanaka mvura yemunyu yainga isizi kupinda mweiri), akazvipe round se prizes. Pakanga pane imwe chete a-piece, yakapoteredza yeshe.

“Esi anofanira kuva nemubairo iye pachake, munoziya,” akadaro Mbeva.

Dodo wakadavira ngo kushata maningi kuti: “Eya.” “Chii chimwe chauinacho muhomwe yako?” akaenderera mberi, achitendeukira kuna Alice.

Alice wakananga ngo kusuruvara kuti: “I n’anga basi.”

“Ipei pano,” wakadaro Dodo.

Pasurepo veshe vakaungana vakamutendenedza kamwezve, apo Dodo wakape chibhakera ngekuremekedza, eiti “Tinokumbira kuti muashire chibhakera ichi chakanaka;” uye, payakapedza kubhuya uku kupfupi, veshe vakapemberera.

Alice wakafunga kuti zveshe izvi zvainga zvisizi kujeka, asi zveshe zvaiita inga zvakaoma maningi zvekuti aazi kuzoshinga kuseka; uye, sezvo akanga asingakoni kufunga chekutaura, akangogwadama, ndokutora chigunwe, achitarisa sezvaaigona.

Chiro chinoteera inga chiri chekurya ma comfits: izvi zvakakonzera ruzha nekuvhiringika, sezvo shiri huru dzaigunun'una kuti hadzikoni kuravira dzadzo, uye duku dzaivhunduka uye dzaifanira kubatwa pamushana. Asi, zvakapera pakupeisira, vakagarazve pashi mumhete, vakakumbira kuti Mbeva ivabhuire zvimweni.

Alice wakati: “Mwakagondisa kundibvunja nhoroondo yenyu, munoziva, zve ngenyi munovenga—C na D,” wakaengejera ngo kuzevezera, hafu yo kuthya kuti zvaizokhugunyikazve.

“Yangu inyaya yakareba uye inosuruvarisa!” akadaro Mbeva, achitendeukira kuna Alice, uye achitura befu.

“ I muswe wakareba, zvemene,” wakadaro Alice, einingira pashi ngekushamisika kumuswe we Mbeva; “asi why uchizvidaidza kuti zvinosuruvarisa?” Uye wakaramba eizvishamisika ngendaa iyoyo Mbeva yeireketa, zvekuti mufungo wake we ndaa iyi wakaita inga uyu:—

“Amuri kuenda kumisangano!” akadaro Mbeva kuna Alice zvakanyanya. “Uri kufunga nezvei?”

Alice wakananga ngo kujidodokesa maningi kuti: “Ndinokumbira kuti mundirekerere, ndinorangarira kuti mwainga mwaguma pa ndau yo kushanu?”

“Ndainge ndisina! ” yakachema Mbeva, yakapinza uye yakatsamwa zvikuru.

“Mfundo!” akadaro Alice, nguva dzose akagadzirira kuzviita anoshanda, uye achitarisa nekushushikana pamusoro pake. “O, regai ndidetsere kuzvibvisa!”

“Andizoiti chiro chakadai,” wakadaro Mbeva, wakasimuka woenda. “Unondituka ngekutaura zvinhu zvisina maturo zvakadaro!”

“Ndainge ndisingazvireve!” akakumbirisa murombo Alice. “Esi unokasira kugumbuka, unoziva!”

Mbeva yakangoungudza ichipindura.

“Ndapota dzokai mupedze nyaya yenyu!” Alice wakadaidza sure kwayo; uye vamwe vese vakabatana mukuimba, “Eya, ndapota ita!” asi Mbeva yakangodzungudza musoro isingashiviriri, ndokufamba nekukurumidza zvishoma.

“Zvinosiririsa sei kuti zvaisazogara!” akazevezera Lory, pakarepo payakange yanyatsooneka; uye Gakanje rakura rakatora mukana wekuti kumwanasikana wake "Aa, mudikani wangu!Ngazviite chidzidzo kwauri kuti usambotsamwa !" “Bata rurimi rwako, Mai!” akadaro Gakanje wechidiki, achinyatsotsvedza zvishoma. “Wakakwana kuedza kushivirira kweoyster!”

“Dai ndainge ndiine Dina wedu pano, ndinoziva kuti ndinozviita!” akadaro Alice nenzwi guru, asina kutaura nemumwe munhu zvakanyanya. “Aizokurumidza kuitora!”

“Na Dina ndiani, kudari ndingashinga kubvunjisa mubvunjowo?” vakadaro vaLory.

Alice wakapingura ngekudakara, ngekuti waigara akagadzirira kureketa ngendaa yechipuka chake chepamusha: "Dinah chimangowe chedu. Uye iye capital imwe yekubata mbeva dzausingakoni kufunga! Uye oh, ndinoshuva kuti waizomuona mushure mweshiri! Ngei, acharya shiri doko ngekukasira kuiona!"

Kubhuya uku kwakakonzera kushamisika kunoshamisa pakati pepati. Shiri dzimweni dzakakurumidza kuenda nguva imweyo: Magpie imwe yakakura yakatanga kuzviputira ngekungwarira yaamho, ichiti, “Ndinofanira kuguma kumhatso zvemene; mhepo yeusiku haienderani nepahuro yangu!” uye Canary yakadaidzira ngezwi rinodedera kuvana vayo, "Uyai kure, vadikani vangu! Yava nguva yekuti mose muve pamubhedha!" Ngo zviro zvakasiyana-siyana vese vakaenda, zve Alice wakakasira kusara ega.

“Dai ndisina kutaura nezvaDina!” akadaro achizviudza ega nezwi rekusuwa. "Apana unoita inga unomuda, pashi pano, uye ndine gonda rekuti ndiye chimangowe chakanaka maningi munyika! Haiwa, Dinah wangu wandinoda! Ndinokarakadza kuti ndichazombokuonazve ere!" Uye pano murombo Alice wakatanga kuchemazve, ngekuti wakazwa ari ega maningi uye akadzikira. Mukuwo mudoko, asi, wakazwazve kupopota kwetsoka shomani kuretu, wakaningira mudenga ngekudakara, hafu yegonda kuti Mbeva yakachicha pfungwa dzayo, uye yainga yeipetuka kuti ipedze ndaa yake.
