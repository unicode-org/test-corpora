CHITSAUKO VII.
Mutambo weTea-Mapenzi

Pakanga pane tafura yakaiswa pashi pemuti pamberi pemhatso, uye March Hare ne Hatter vaimwa tii pairi: Dormouse yainge yakagara pakati pavo, yakaata zvakanyanya, uye vamweni vairi vaiishandisa semubhedha, vakazorora makona avo pairi, uye veireketa pamusoro payo. Alice wakarangarira kuti: “Azvizivi kugajika maningi ku Dormouse; “chete, sezvo yakarara, ndinofunga kuti haina basa.”

Tafura yainga yakakura, asi vatatu vese vainga vakadzara pa kona imweni: “Apana ndau! vakadaidzira kudaro pavakaona Alice achiuya. “Kune nzvimbo yakawanda !” akadaro Alice akatsamwa, uye akagara pachigaro chikuru chemaoko pane imwe mugumo wetafura.

March Hare wakabhuya ngezwi rinokurudzira kuti: “Ivai ne waini.”

Alice wakaningira kutenderera kweshe kwetafura, asi paiya pasina chiro pairi asi tii. Iyena wakananga kuti: “Andivoni waini.”

“Hapana,” akadaro Tsuro yaKurume.

Alice wakananga ngo kunyangajwa kuti: “Hino, zvainga zvicizivi kujidodokesa maningi kuti muzvipe.”

“Zvainge zvisizi kunyanya kunaka kwauri kugara pashi usikazi kukokwa,” wakadaro March Hare.

Alice wakati: “Ndainga ndicikazivi kuti inga tafura yenyu ; “yakaiswa nokuda kwevakawanda vakawanda kudarika vatatu.”

“Bvudzi rako rinoda kuchekwa,” akadaro Mugadziri weNgowani. Ainge einingira Alice kwenguva yakati ngekuda kuziya zvikuru, uye iyi yaive hurukuro yake yekutanga.

Alice wakananga ngo kujidodokesa kuti: “Munodikana kujija kutama kubhuya zviro zvo mundhu ega; “zvine unhu hwakanyanya.”

Muiti we Ngowani wakavhura madziso ake maningi paakazwa izvi; asi zvese zvaakataura ndezvekuti, “Ngei gunguwo rakafanana netafura yekunyora?”

“Uyai, tinozodakara zvino!” akafunga kudaro Alice. “Ndakadakara kuti vakatanga kubvunjisa zvirevo.—Ndinotenda kuti ndinokwanisa kuzvifembera,” wakaengejera ngo izwi guru.

“Unoreva kuti unofunga kuti unokwanisa kuwana mhinduro yaro here?” akadaro Tsuro yaKurume.

Alice wakati: “Ndizvona kamare.

“Saka unofanira kutaura zvaunoreva,” March Hare akaenderera mberi.

Alice wakadavira ngo kukasika kuti: “Ndinozviziva; “zvishoma—zvishoma ndinoreva zvandinotaura—ndizvo zvakafanana, munoziva.”

“Azvizi kufanana zvishoma!” akadaro Mugadziri weNgowani. “Munganangavo kuti ‘Ndinovona zvandinoha’ zvinoreva kuti ‘Ndinoha zvandinovona’!”

March Hare wakaengejera eciti: “Munganangavo kuti ‘Ndinodakarira zvandinowana’ zvinoreva kuti ‘Ndinowana zvandinodakarira’!”

“Munokona kunangavo kudaro,” wakaengejera kudaro Dormouse, uwo waiita inga ari kubhuya mu hope jake, “kuti ‘Ndinofema pandinoata’ zvinofanana no ‘Ndinoata pandinofema’!”

“ Zvakafanana newe,” wakadaro Mupfeki weNgowani, pano utaurwi hwakapera, nepati yakagara yakanyarara kweminiti imwe, apo Alice eifunga zveshe zvaaikarakadza ngendaa yemagunguwo nemadhesiki ekunyora, zvainga zvisizi kunyanya.

Hatter ndiye akatanga kuputsa runyararo. “Izuwa ripi remwedzi?” akadaro, achitendeukira kuna Alice: akanga abudisa wachi yake muhomwe yake, uye aiiona asina kugadzikana, achiizunguza nguva ngenguva, uye aiibata nzee yake.

Alice wakarangarira zvishoma, wobva ati “Wechina.”

“Mazuwa mairi akashata!” akazevezera Mugadziri weNgowani. “Ndakuudza kuti butter haizoenderani nemabasa!” akawedzera achitarisa March Hare ane hasha.

March Hare wakadavira ngo kupfava kuti: “Inga iri butter yakanaka maningi .”

“Eya, asi zvimweni zvimedu zvinodikana kunge zvakapindawo,” Mugadziri we Ngowani wakagunun’una: “auzi kufanira kuzviisa nge banga re chingwa.”

March Hare wakatora wachi akainingira ngekusuwa: wakazoinyika mukapu yake ye tea, akainingirazve: asi wakanga asingakoni kufunga chiro chiri nani kuronza kupfuura kubhuya kwake kwekutanga, “Inga iri butter yakanakisisa , munoziya.”

Alice ainge akatarisa pamusoro pebendekete rake achida kuziva. “I wachi inoshekedza yaamho!” akataura kudaro. “Inobhuya zuwa re mwedzi, haibhuyi kuti nguwa yapi!”

“Ngei zvinofanira kudaro?” akabvunza kudaro Mugadziri weNgowani. “Wachi yako inokuudza kuti gore ripi here?”

Alice wakadavira ngo kukasika maningi kuti: “Eya haiwa, asi izvi zvinoitika ngo kuti zvinogara pa gore rimwe basi ko nguva yakareba kudaro tiri pamwepo.”

“Izvo zvinongoitika nezvangu ,” akadaro Mugadziri weNgowani.

Alice wakazwa kushamisika maningi, zvaakabhuya The Hatter zvaiita inga zvisina zvazvinoronza, asi zvemene zvainga zviri zve Chirungu. “Andikuzwisisi kamare,” wakananga ngo ungwaru inga zvaaikwanisa.

“Dormouse yaatazve,” wakadaro Mugadziri weNgowani, wakadurura tii shomani inopisha pamhino yayo.

Dormouse yakadzungudze soro rayo isizi kugadzikana, yochobhuya, isizi kuvhura madziso ayo, “Eya, eya; ndizvona zvandaizozvikarakadza.”

“Wakambofembera chirahwe here?” muHatter akadaro achitendeukira kuna Alice zvakare.

Alice wakadavira kudari: “Haiwa, ndinozvisiya, mupinguro ndiyani?”

“Andizi kumboite chiro chidoko,” wakadaro Mugadziri we Ngowani.

“Kana ini,” akadaro Tsuro yaKurume.

Alice wakazeezera ngekuremba. Iyena wakati: “Ndinorangarira kuti ungaita ciro ciri nane ngo nguvayo, pane kuipeja ngo kubvunjisa zvirevo zvicina mupinguro.”

Muiti we Ngowani wakati: “Kudari mwaiziva Nguva inga inini, mwaizotama kubhuya ngo pamusoro po kuipedza . Ndiyena .”

Alice wakati: “Andizivi kuti munoreva kudini.”

“Ehe hazviiti!” mugadziri wengowani akadaro achikanda musoro wake achizvidza. “Ndinoshinga kuronza kuti auzi kumbobhuya na Time!”

Alice wakadavira ngo kungwarira kuti: “Kangaije haiwa, asi ndinozviziva kuti ndinodikana kurova nguva pandinojija zvokuimba.”

“Aa!, ndizvona zvinozvipa,” wakadaro Muiti wo Ngowani. "Aazoemeri kurohwa. Zvino, kudai wakaramba weizwirana naye, waizoita zveshe zvaunodakarira nge nguwa. Ngekudaro, ngatiti yainge iri nguwa ye mapfumbamwe mangwanani, nguwa yekutanga zvidzidzo: waizofanira kungozevezera nguwa, nekutenderera nguwa inoenda nguwa imwe, half twink!"

(“Ndinoshuva kuti dai zvakadaro,” Tsuro yaKurume yakazvibhuyira ngekuzevezera.)

Alice wakananga ngo kurangarira kuti: “Izvo zvaizova zvakanaka maningi, kamare, asi pasurepo—andidikani kuzvizwa ngo nzara, munozviziva.”

“Azviiti pa kutanga, kangaije,” wakadaro Muiti wo Ngowani, “asi munokwanisa kuzviita kuguma pa hafu yo nguva yo nguva yamunoda.”

“Ndiyo njira yaunozvibata ndiyo here?” Alice wakabvunza.

Mugadziri wengowani akadzungudza musoro wake achichema. “Haiwa inini!” akapindura kudaro. “Takapopotedzana muna March wapera—asati apenga , munoziya—” (achinongedzera ngechipunu chake chetii pa March Hare,) “—kwaiva pa concert huru yakapiwa na Queen of Hearts, uye ndaifanira kuimba .

'Kupenya, kupenya, chiremwaremwa chidoko!
Ndinoshamisika kuti uri pachii!’

Unoziya rwiyo urwu, pamwe?”

Alice wakati: “Ndakazwa ciro cakadaro.

“Zvinoenderera mberi, munoziya,” Mugadziri weNgowani wakaenderera mberi, “nenjira iyi:—

'Pamusoro penyika unobhururuka, .
Kudai nge tea-trey iri mudenga.
Kupenya, kupenya—’”

Pano Dormouse yakadzizunguza, yotanga kuimba mukuata kwayo “ Kupenya, kupenya, kupenya, kupenya —” yakaenderera mberi kwenguva yakareba zvekuti vaifanira kuipinza kuti ieme.

“Zvakanaka, ndainga ndicito ndapeja ndima yo kutanga,” wakadaro Muiti wo Ngowani, “apo Mambokadzi wakarumba wobhuya kuti, ‘Ari kuuraya nguva!

“Izvo zvinotyisa maningi!” akadaro Alice achishevedzera.

"Uye kubvira ipapo," Mugadziri weNgowani akaenderera mberi ngezwi rekuchema, "haazoiti chiro chandinokumbira! Nguva dzeshe nguwa dzechitanhatu zvino."

Pfungwa yakajeka yakauya musoro waAlice. “Ndicho chikonzero here kuti zviro zvakawanda zve tea-zviro zviiswe kubanze kuno?” akabvunza.

“Eya, ndizvona,” wakadaro Muiti we Ngowani ecizeezera: “nguva jese nguva yo tea, zve atina nguva yo kusuka zviro pakati po nguva.”

“Saka unoramba uchitenderera, ndinofunga?” akadaro Alice.

“Ndizvona kamare,” wakadaro Muiti wo Ngowani, “sezvo zviro zvinopera.”

“Esi chii chinoitika kana ukasvika pakutanga zvakare?” Alice wakashinga kubvunza.

“Ngatiti tinocinja shwiro,” March Hare wakavhiringija, ecizeya. "Ndirikuneta nenyaya iyi. Ndinovhota musikana anotiudza nyaya."

“Ndinothya kuti andizivi umwe,” wakananga Alice, kusiyapo kuthya ngo pamusoro po cinangwaco.

“Ipapo Mbeva yeKurara inozoita!” vakachema vese vari vaviri. “Muka, Mbeva yeKurara!” Uye vakaipinza kumativi maviri kamwe chete.

Dormouse akavhura maziso ake zvishoma nezvishoma. “Ndainga ndicikaata,” wakananga ngo izwi rinozwika, rinoremba: “Ndakazwa gama rimwe ngarimwe raibhuya imwimwi.”

“Tibhuyire ndaa!” akadaro Tsuro yaKurume.

“Eya, ndapota itai!” akakumbirisa kudaro Alice.

“Uye kurumidza nezvazvo,” akawedzera Mugadziri weNgowani, “kana kuti ucharata zvakare zvisati zvapera.”

“Kare kare kwaiva nehanzvadzi nhatu duku,” Dormouse yakatanga ngekukurumidza zvikuru; “uye mazita avo aiva Elsie, Lacie, naTillie; uye vaigara pashi petsime—”

“Vairarama ngechii?” akadaro Alice, uyo aigara achifarira zvikuru mibvunzo yekurya nekumwa.

“Vairarama nge treacle,” wakadaro Dormouse, paakafunga miniti imwe kana mbiri.

Alice wakananga ngo kupfava kuti: “Vainga vacikakwanisi kuita kudaro, munoziva; “vaizodai vakarwara.”

“Ndizvo zvavaiita,” wakadaro Dormouse; “ kurwara zvikuru .”

Alice wakaeja kujirangarira ega kuti mararamiro anoshamisa kudaro aizova akadini, asi zvakamushamisa maningi, ngo kudaro wakaenderera mberi eciti: “Esi ngenyi vaigara pashi po tsime?”

“Tora imweni tii,” March Hare wakabhuya kuna Alice, ngo mwoyo wese.

Alice wakadavira ngo izwi ro kunyangajwa kuti: “Andizivi kumbova no ciro, ngo kudaro andingakwanisi kutora zvimweni.”

“Unoreva kuti haugoni kutora zvishoma ,” akadaro Mugadziri weNgowani: “zviri nyore zvikuru kutora zvakawanda pane kusava nechiro.”

Alice wakati: “Apana wakabvunjisa mavonero ako .

“Ndiani ari kuita zvirevo zvemunhu iye zvino?” muHatter akabvunza achikunda.

Alice aazi kunyatsoziya kuti anozoti kudii kune izvi: saka wakadzidetsera nge tea ne chingwa-ne-butter, ochozotendeukira kuna Dormouse, ochozopetudza mubvunzo wake. “Ngenyi vaigara pashi po tsime?”

Dormouse yakatorazve miniti imwe kana mbiri kuti afunge ngezvazvo, ndokuzoti, “Inga iri tsime re treacle.”

“Apana chiro chakadaro!” Alice wakatanga kunyangadzwa maningi, asi Muiti we Ngowani no Tsuro yo March vakaenda “Sh! sh!” uye Dormouse sulkily akataura, “Kana usingakwanisi kuva civil, zvirinani upedze nyaya iwe pachako.”

“Haiwa, ndapota endai mberi!” Alice wakabhuya ngekuzvininipisa maningi; "Handizovhiringidzi zvakare.Ndinoshinga kutaura kuti panogona kunge paine imwe ."

“Imwe, zvemene!” akadaro Dormouse achitsamwiswa. Asi, wakatendera kuti aenderere mberi. “Nazvo kudaro hanzvadzi nhatu idzi duku—vaifunda kudhirowa, munoziya—”

“Vakadhirowa chii?” akadaro Alice, achinyatsokanganwa chivimbiso chake.

“Treacle,” wakadaro Dormouse, asizi kumbofunga nguwa yeshe iyi.

“Ndinoda kapu yakachena,” akavhiringidza Mugadziri weNgowani: “ngatiende teshe panzvimbo imwe chete.”

Wakaenderera mberi ngekubhuya, Dormouse wakamuteera: March Hare wakaenda kundau ye Dormouse, Alice asizi kuda kutora ndau ye March Hare. MuHatter ndiye ega wakawana chiro chinodetsera ngekuchicha: na Alice wakaita zviro zvakashata maningi kupinda kare, ngekuti March Hare wakange achangobva kutsamwisa mukaka-jug mundiro yake.

Alice aazivi kuda kukhuvazazve Dormouse, ngo kudaro wakatanga ngo kungwarira maningi: “Esi andizwisisi. Vakabudisa kupi treacle?”

“Unokona kudhonza mvura kubve mutsime remvura,” wakadaro Mugadziri we Ngowani; “saka ndinofanira kufunga kuti unokwanisa kudhonza treacle kubva mutsime re treacle—eh, benzi?”

“Esi vainge vari mutsime,” Alice wakabhuya ku Dormouse, asizi kusarudza kuona mazwi aya ekupedzisira.

“Eya vainge vari,” wakadaro Dormouse; “—zvakanaka mukati.”

Mhinduro iyi yakavhiringidza murombo Alice, zvekuti wakasiya Dormouse ichienderera mberi kwenguva yakati asizi kuivhiringidza.

“Vaifunda kudhirowa,” Dormouse yakaenderera mberi, yeizeya ngekutsvoda madziso ayo, ngekuti yainga yotanga kuata maningi; “vakadhirowa zviro zveshe—zviro zveshe zvinotanga nge M—”

“Ngei nge M?” akadaro Alice.

"Ngenyi hayiwa?" akadaro Tsuro yaKurume.

Alice wakanyarara.

Dormouse yainge yavhara madziso ayo nguwa iyi, yainge yoenda kunoata; asi, pakupinzwa nge Mugadziri we Ngowani, yakamukazve nge kushevedzera kadoko, yoenderera mberi: “—izvo zvinotanga nge M, kudai nge misungo ye mbeva, ne mwedzi, ne kurangarira, ne kuwanda—munoziya munoti zviro “zvakawanda zve kuwanda”—mwakamboona ere chiro chakadai nge kuwanda kudai nge mufananidzo we?”

“Zvokadi, wari munondibvunjisa,” wakadaro Alice, wakavhiringika maningi, “andifungi kuti—”

“Saka haufaniri kutaura,” akadaro Mugadziri weNgowani.

Chikamu ichi chekusadzikama chainge chakanyanya kupfuura zvaikwanisa kuitwa naAlice: wakamiruka ngekunyangadzwa kukuru, akaenda; Dormouse wakaata ponapo, hapana ne umwe wevamweni wakatora chiro chidoko chekuenda kwake, kunyazwi wakaningirazve sure kamwe kana kairi, hafu ye gonda rekuti vanozodaidza sure kwake: nguwa yekupeisira yaakavaona, vaizama kuisa Dormouse mu teapot.

“Kunyazwi zvakadini andizomboendeyozve !” akadaro Alice achisarudza nzira yake kuburikidza nemapuranga. “Ndiyo tea-party yemapenzi kupfuura yese yandakamboita muhupenyu hwangu hwese!”

Paainga ecibhuya kudaro, wakavona kuti muti umweni wainga no musuvo waipinda mukati mwawo. “Izvozvo zvinoda kuziva zvikuru!” akafunga kudaro. "Esi zviro zveshe zviri kuda kuziya nyamashi. Ndinokarakadza kuti ndinokona kupinda kamwe chete." Uye mukati akapinda.

Kamwezve wakadziona ari muhoro yakareba, uye pedyo netafura diki yegirazi. “Zvino, ndinozokwanisa kuita zviri nane nguwa ino,” wakabhuya ega, akatanga ngekutora kiyi doko yegoridhe, nekuvhura musuwo waipinda mumunda. Pasurepo wakaenda kumushando eiruma-ruma mushroom (wakachengeta chikamu chayo muhomwe yake) kuguma paakakwira ingaite tsoka imwe: pasurepo wakahamba eidzaka ngenjira doko: pasurepo —wakadziwana pakupeisira ari mubindu rakanaka, pakati pemaruva anopenya ngezvitubu zvinotonhorera.
