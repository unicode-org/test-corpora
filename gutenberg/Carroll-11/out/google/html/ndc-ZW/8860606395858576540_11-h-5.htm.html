CHITSAUKO V.
Zano rinobva kune Mhuka .

Mhuka na Alice vakatarisana kwenguva yakati vakanyarara: pakupeisira Mhuka yakabudisa hookah mumuromo mwayo, yochobhuya naye ngezwi rakashata, rakaata.

“ Ndiwe ani? ” wakadaro Mhuka.

Izvi azvicainga njira inokurudzira yo kuvhura bhuyo. Alice wakadaira, asi wakanyangadzwa, “Ini—andizivi kuziya, changamire, nguwa ino—zvishoma ndinoziya kuti ndiri ani pandakamuka mangwanani ano, asi ndinofunga kuti ndinofanira kunge ndakachinjwa nguva dzakawanda kubvira ipapo.”

“Munorevei ngezve izvi?” akadaro Mhuka yakasimba. “Zvitsanangure iwewe!”

“Andikwanisi kuzvipashangura , ndinothya, changamire,” wakadaro Alice, “ngokuti andisi inini, munoona.”

“Andioni,” wakadaro Mhuka.

“Ndinothya kuti andingakoni kuzvironza zvakajeka maningi,” Alice wakadavira ngo ungwaru maningi, “ngokuti inini andingakwanisi kuzvizwisisa ndega kutanga; zve kuva no masaizi akawanda akasiyana-siyana pa zuwa rimwe zvinovhiringija maningi.”

“Hazvisi,” wakadaro Mhuka.

Alice wakati: “Zvakanaka, kangaije amuzivi kuzviwana kudaro.” “asi kana uchifanira kushanduka kuva chrysalis—unozoita rimweni zuwa, unoziya—uye sure kwazvo kuita butterfly, ndinofanira kufunga kuti uchazvizwa zvishoma queer, andizvopi?”

“Hapana zvishoma,” wakadaro Mhuka.

Alice wakati: “Zvakanaka, kangaije mazwiro ako angasiyana; “zvese zvandinoziva ndezvekuti, zvaizoita sezvinoshamisa kwandiri .”

"Iwewe!" akadaro Mhuka inozvidza. " Ndimwi ani? "

Zvakavadzosa zvakare kumavambo ehurukuro. Alice wakazwa kunyangadzwa zvishomani ngekuita kwa Caterpillar mazwi mapfupi maningi kudaro, wakadzikwevera mudenga akati, ngekunyanya, “Ndinofunga, unofanira kundironzera kuti uri ani, kutanga.”

"Ngenyi?" akadaro Mhuka yeMhuka.

Heunoi umweni mubvunzo waishamisa; uye sezvo Alice akanga asingakoni kufunga chikonzero chakanaka, uye sezvo Caterpillar yaiita inga iri muchimiro chisingafadzi maningi chepfungwa, wakatendeuka.

“Hwirirai!” chipuka chakadaidzira mushure make. “Ndine chimwe chinhu chakakosha chandinoda kutaura!”

Izvi zvaizwika inga zvinogondisa, zvemene: Alice wakatendeuka wopetukazve.

“Ramba wakatsamwa,” wakadaro Mhuka.

“Ndizvo zvega ere?” akadaro Alice achimedza kutsamwa kwake zvakanaka sezvaaigona.

“Haiwa,” wakadaro Mhuka.

Alice wakafunga kuti angaemerawo, sezvo asina chimweni chekuita, uye pamwe pashure pezvose zvingamuudza chimwe chinhu chakakodzera kuzwa. Kwemaminitsi amweni yakazvimba isizi kubhuya, asi pakupeisira yakavhura maoko ayo, yakabudisazve hookah mumuromo mwayo, yochoti, “Saka unofunga kuti wakachicha, ndizvona ere?”

“Ndinothya kuti ndiri, changamire,” wakadaro Alice; “Andicainga no cirangarijo co zviro inga zvandaiita—uye andicainga no saizi imweyo ko maminiti gumi tiri pamwepo!”

“Handicharangariri zviro zvipi ?” akadaro Mhuka yeMhuka.

“Zvakanaka, ndaedza kuronza kuti “Nyuchi doko yakabatikana inoita sei,” asi zveshe zvakauya zvakasiyana!” Alice akapindura nezwi rainge rakasuwa zvikuru.

“Dzokorora kuti, ‘ Wakura, Baba William ,’” wakadaro Mhuka.

Alice wakapeta nyara dzake, wotanga:—

Jaha rakati: “Mwakura, Baba William, .
“Uye bvudzi rako rachena kwazvo;
Uye asi iwe unogara wakaema pamusoro wako—
Munofunga kuti, pazera renyu, zvakanaka here?”

Baba William vakadavira mwana wavo kuti: “Muhudiki hwangu, .
“Ndaithya kuti zvingakhuvaza ngqondo;
Asi, zvino ndava nechokwadi chakakwana chekuti handina, .
Ei, ndinozviita kakawanda.”

Mujahawo wakati: “Wakura, inga zvandakabhuya pakutanga, .
Uye vakakura zvikuru zvisingawanzo mafuta;
Asi wakatendeuka nge sure-somersault kupinda pa musuwo—
Namata, chikonzero cheizvozvo ndechei?”

“Muhudiki hwangu,” akadaro sage, paainge achizunungudza bvudzi rake rakachena, .
“Ndakaramba ndakaita kuti mitezo yangu yese ive yakapfava zvikuru .
Ngo kushandisa mafuta aya—shiringi imwe yo bhokisi—
Nditendereiwo kuti ndikutengeserei vaviri?”

Jaha rakati: “Wakura, uye tsaya dzako dzakashata zvikuru .
Kune chero chiro chakaoma kudarika suet;
Asi wakapedza goose, nemapfupa nemuromo—
Pray, wakakwanisa kuzviita sei?”

Baba vake vakati: “Pauduku hwangu, ndakatora mutemo, .
Uye akapokana nyaya imwe neimwe nemukadzi wangu;
Uye simba renyama, iro rakapa tsaya yangu, .
Yakagara upenyu hwangu hwose.”

Jaha rakati: “Wakura, munhu angatombofunga .
Kuti ziso rako rainge rakasimba sekare;
Asi wakaenzanisa eel pamupendero wemhino yako—
Chii chakaita kuti uve wakangwara zvakanyanya?”

“Ndapindura mibvunzo mitatu, uye izvi zvakakwana,”
Vakadaro baba vake; “usazvipa mweya!
Unofunga kuti ndinokwanisa kuteerera zuva rose zvinhu zvakadaro here?
Ibva, kana ndisingakwanisi kukurova pasi pazvikwiriso!”

“Izvozvo hazvina kutaurwa zvakanaka,” akadaro Mhuka.

“Azvizivi kunyatsoita , ndinothya,” wakadaro Alice, ecithya; “mazwi amweni akachinjwa.”

“Zvakashata kubve pakutanga kuguma pa kupera,” wakadaro Mhuka nge kuzvipira, kwakaita runyararo kwe maminitsi akati kuti.

Mhuka yeMhuka ndiyo yakatanga kutaura.

“Unoda kuva saizi yakadini?” yakabvunza.

Alice wakadavira ngo kukasika kuti: “O, andizi kuziva maningi ngo pamusoro po kukura; “mumwe chete haafarire kuchinja kakawanda kudaro, munoziva.”

“ Andizii ,” wakadaro Mhuka.

Alice aazi kubhuya chiro: aazi kumbopokanwa zvakanyanya muhupenyu hwake, uye wakazwa kuti aipera simba.

“Wagutsikana here zvino?” akadaro Mhuka yeMhuka.

Alice wakati: “Zvakanaka, ndinodikana kukura padoko , changamire, kudari mukatama kuzvinesa, mainji matatu anorema maningi.”

“I kureba kwakanaka maningi kamare!” akadaro Mhuka yakatsamwa, ichidzisimudza yakarurama sezvayaireketa (yaive yakareba masendimita matatu chaiwo).

“Asi ini handina kuzvijaira!” akakumbirisa murombo Alice nezwi rinosiririsa. Uye akafunga pamusoro pake, “Dai zvisikwa zvisingagumi nyore nyore kudaro!”

“Unozozvijaera ngekuhamba kwenguva,” wakadaro Mhuka; uye yakaisa hookah mumuromo ndokutanga kuputa zvakare.

Nguva ino Alice wakaemera ngo kusengerera mbhera yakakheta kubhuyazve. Muminiti imwe kana mbiri Caterpillar yakabudisa hookah mumuromo mwayo yochozeya kamwe kana kairi, yochodzizunguza. Pasurepo yakadzaka kubve pa mushroom, yokwakuka mu uswa, yecingobhuya payakaenda, “Rutivi rumwe rwunozoita kuti urebe, divi rimweni rinozoita kuti uite mupfupi.”

“Rutivi rumwe rwechii? Rutivi rumwe rwechii? ” akafunga Alice mumwoyo make.

“Ye mushroom,” wakadaro Mhuka, inga wakazvibvunza ngezwi guru; uye mune imwe nguva yakanga isingaoneki.

Alice wakaramba einingira mushroom ngekufunga kweminiti imwe, eizama kuziya kuti ndeapi mativi maviri ayo; uye sezvo yaive yakatenderera zvakakwana, akawana uyu mubvunzo wakaoma zvikuru. Asi, pakupejisira wakatambanudza maoko ake akaitenderera kuguma pavaizoenda, wogura zvishoma pamupendero ngenyara imwe ngaimwe.

“Uye zvino chii chiri chii?” akadaro mumwoyo mwake, akaruma zvishomani ruoko rwekurudyi kuti aedze mhedzisiro: nguva inoteera wakazwa kurova kwesimba pashi pechirebvu chake: kwainge kwarova rutsoka rwake!

Wainga wakathya maningi ngo kucinja uku kwakaitika ngo kukasika, asi wakazwa inga kuti apana nguva yo kurashika, ngokuti wainga eidodoka ngo kukasika; saka akatanga kushanda kamwe chete kuti adye zvimwe zvimwe zvikamu. Chirebvu chake chainge chakasungirirwa zvikuru parutsoka rwake, zvekuti pakanga pasina nzvimbo yekuvhura muromo wake; asi wakazviita pakupedzisira, akakwanisa kumedza chikamu chekuruboshwe.

* * * * * * *

“Uyai, musoro wangu wakasununguka pakupedzisira!” akadaro Alice ngezwi rekudakara, rakachicha kuita kutya mune imwe nguwa, paakaona kuti mafudzi ake akanga asiri pekuwanikwa: zveshe zvaaiona, paakaningira pashi, kwaiva kureba kukuru kwemutsipa, kwaiita inga wakakwira kunge tsanga kubva mugungwa remashizha e green ainge akarara kuretu pashi pake.

“Zviro zveshe izvi zve green zvingaite chiinyi?” akadaro Alice. "Uye mafudzi angu akasvika kupi? Uye oh, maoko angu murombo, zvinoita sei kuti ndisakuona?" Wainga eizvihambisa-hambisa paainga eibhuya, asi apana zvaizobuda zvaiita inga zvaitevera, asi kuvhunduka padoko pakati po mashakani o ruva ari kuretu.

Sezvo zvaiita inga pasina mukana wekukwidza maoko ake kumusoro wake, wakaedza kukwidza soro rake kudzaka kwavari, uye wakadakara kuona kuti mutsipa wake waizokotama zviri nyore kuende kweshe, kudai ngenyoka. Ainge achangobudirira kuikonya pashi kuita zigzag inodakadza, uye aizodzika mukati memashizha, ayo aakaona asisiri chiro asi misoro yemiti yaainge eidzungaira pashi payo, apo mhere yakapinza yakaita kuti adzokere sure ngekukasira: njiva huru yainge yabhururuka muhope yake, uye yairova ngesimba.

“Nyoka!” akashevedzera Njiva.

“ Andisiri nyoka!” akadaro Alice achitsamwiswa. “Ndisiyani ndega!”

“Nyoka, ndinoti zvakare!” akadzokorora Njiva, asi ngezwi rakadzikama, uye akawedzera ngemhando yekuchema, “Ndakaedza njira dzese, uye hapana chinoita sechinovakodzera!”

Alice wakati: “Andizivi kunyazwi kuziva zvamuri kubhuya.”

“Ndakaedza midzi ye miti, ndakaedza mabhanga, ndakaedza madziro,” Njiva yakaenderera mberi, isingamuitire hanya; "asi nyoka idzodzo! Hapana chinodzifadza!"

Alice wakaramba eishamisika, asi wakafunga kuti apana zvaizodetsera kuti abhuye zvimweni kuguma Njiva yapera.

“Kudai ngekuti azvizi kuoma zvakakwana kuchekerera mazanda,” wakadaro Njiva; "asi ndinofanira kugara ndakangwarira nyoka usiku nemasikati! Ngei, andizi kumboata kunyazwi mavhiki matatu aya!"

Alice, uwo wainga wotanga kuvona zvinoreva, wakananga kudari: “Ndinokumbira ruregerero maningi ngo kunyangajwa.”

"Naapo kudai ngekuti ndainge ndatora muti wakareba maningi musango," wakaenderera mberi Njiva, yechisimudza izwi rayo kuguma pakushevedzera, "naapo kudai ngekuti ndaifunga kuti ndinofanira kusununguka kubva kwavari pakupeisira, vanofanira kuuya vechizunguzika kubva mudenga! Ugh, Nyoka!"

“Asi ini handisi nyoka, ndinokuudzai!” akadaro Alice. “Ndiri—ndiri—”

"Zvakanaka! Ndiri ani ?" yakadaro Njiva. “Ndirikuona kuti uri kuedza kugadzira chimwe chinhu!”

“Ini—ndiri mwanakaji mudoko,” wakadaro Alice, acikakhatari maningi, paainga ecirangarira kuwanda ko cinjo jaainga akasongana najo pa njiku yondhoyo.

“Nyaya ingangove iripo zvemene!” akadaro Njiva nezwi rekuzvidza kwakadzama. "Ndakaona ntombi dzakawanda dzakanaka munguwa yangu, asi andizi kumboona imwe ine mutsipa wakadai! Haiwa, haiwa! Uri nyoka; uye hapana chinodetsera kuzviramba. Ndinofunga kuti uchandironzera zvinoteera kuti auzi kumboravira zai!"

“ Ndakaravira mazanda, zvemene,” wakadaro Alice, wainga mwana wo cokwadi maningi; “asi vasikana vaduku vanodya mazai zvakanyanya sezvinoita nyoka, munoziva.”

“Andizvitendi,” wakadaro Njiva; “asi kana vakadaro, nei zvino vari mhando yenyoka, ndizvo zvega zvandinogona kutaura.”

Iyi yainge iri mufungo mutsva kuna Alice, zvekuti wakanyarara yaamho kweminiti imwe kana mbiri, izvi zvakapa Njiva mukana wekuwedzera, "Uri kutsvaka mazanda, ndinozviziya zvakakwana ; uye zvine ndaa yei kwandiri kuti uri musikana mudoko kana nyoka?"

Alice wakananga ngo kukasika kuti: “Zvinosisira maningi kwandiri .” “asi handisi kutsvaka mazai, sezvinoitika; uye dai ndaive, ndaisafanira kuda ako : Handiadari akasvibira.”

“Zvakanaka, enda, saka!” yakadaro Njiva ngezwi rinonyangadza, apo yakagara zvakare mudendere rayo. Alice wakagwadama pakati pe miti nge njira yeshe yaaikwanisa, ngekuti mutsipa wake wairamba weipinda pakati pe matavi, uye nguva nge nguva waifanira kuema no kuusunungura. Mushure mwechinguvana wakakarakadza kuti achiri kubata zvimedu zvehowa mumaoko ake, akatanga kushanda ngekungwarira yaamho, akatanga kuruma pane imwe uyezve pane imwe, uye akakura dzimweni nguva akareba uye dzimweni nguva akapfupika, kuguma paakabudirira kudzidzasa kusvika pakureba kwake kwenguva dzose.

Zvainge zvakareba maningi kubvira paakamboita chiro chiri pasinde ne saizi yakafanira, zvekuti zvaiita inga zvinoshamisa maningi pakutanga; asi akazvijaira mumaminitsi mashoma, ndokutanga kutaura ega, semazuva ese. "Uyai, pane hafu yeurongwa hwangu hwaitwa zvino! Kushanduka kweshe uku kunoshamisa zvakadini! Andizi kumboziya kuti ndichaita chiinyi, kubve paminiti imwe kuenda kune imwe! Zvisinei, ndapetuka kune saizi yangu yakafanira: chiro chinoteera ngechekuti, kupinda mumunda iwoyo wakanaka— zvinozoitwa sei, ndinoshamisika?" Paainge eibhuya izvi, wakaguma ngekukasika pandau yakavhurika, ine mhatso doko mukati mwayo yakareba makubiti marongomuna. Alice wakarangarira kuti: “Ari nani anogara kondhoyo, azvizomboiti kuti vaende pavari ngo kukura uku : ngenyi, ndinodikana kuvathyaisa kubvira pa ungwaru hwavo!” Hino wakatangazve kuruma ruoko rwekurudyi, aazi kumboshinga kuenda pasinde ngemhatso kuguma paakadzidzasa kuguma pamasentimita mapfumbamwe.
