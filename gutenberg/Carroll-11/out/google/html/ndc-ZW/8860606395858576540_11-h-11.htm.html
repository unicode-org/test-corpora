CHITSAUKO XI.
Ndianani Wakaba Matart?

Mambo na Mambokadzi we Nyoka vakagara pa chigaro chavo cheumambo pavakaguma, nge mbungano huru yakaungana yakavatendenedza—mihlobo yeshe ye shiri duku ne zvipuka, pamwepo ne pakiti yeshe ye makadhi: Knave wakaema pamberi pavo, nge maketani, nge musoja kumativi maviri kuti amurinde; uye pedyo naMambo paiva neTsuro Chena, ine hwamanda munyara imwe, nemupumburu weganda munyara imwe. Pakati po dare painga no tafura, yainga no ndiro huru yo tart: yainga yakajeka maningi, zvokuti zvaiita kuti Alice ave no nzara maningi yo kuivona—“Ndaidakarira kuti vaite kuti kutongwa kuitwe,” wakarangarira kudaro, “vozopetuja zviro zvo kuzorora!” Asi zvaiita inga apana mukana wo izvi, ngo kudaro wakatanga kuvona zveshe zvaaiita, kuti apfuure nguva.

Alice aazivi kumboenda ku dare ro kutonga, asi wainge waerenga ngo pamusoro pavo mu mabhuku, zve wakadakara maningi ngo kuvona kuti waiziva zina ro zviro zvinokhwedera pa zvese zvaingapo. “Ndiye mutongi,” akati mumwoyo make, “nokuda kwewig yake huru.”

Mutongi, ngenjira, wainge Mambo; uye sezvo akapfeka korona yake pamusoro pe wig, (tarisa mberi kana uchida kuona kuti akazviita sei,) haana kutaridzika zvachose akasununguka, uye zvemene zvaisaita.

“Nazvo ndiyo jury-box,” wakafunga Alice, “ne zvisikwa gumi ne zviiri izvi,” (wakasungirwa kuronza kuti “zvisikwa,” munoona, ngekuti zvimweni zvazvo zvaiva mhuka, uye zvimweni zvaiva shiri,) “Ndinofunga kuti ndivo vatongi.” Wakabhuya izwi iri rekupeisira kairi kana katatu pamusoro pake, eizvikudza maningi: ngekuti wakafunga, uye zvakaruramawo, kuti vasikana vashomani maningi vezera rake vaiziya zvinoronza zvachose. Asi, “vandhu vanotonga” vaizoitavo kudaro.

Vatongi gumi nevaviri veshe vainyora zvakanyanya pama slates. “Vari kuitenyi?” Alice akazevezera kuna Gryphon. “Avangava nechiro chekuisa pashi parizvino, kutongwa kusati kwatanga.”

“Vari kuisa mazita avo pasi,” Gryphon akazevezera mukupindura, “nekuthya kuti vanozoakanganwa kusati kwapera kutongwa.”

“Zviro zvemapenzi!” Alice wakatanga ngezwi guru, rakanyangadzwa, asi wakaema ngekukasira, ngekuti Tsuro Chena yakachema, “Nyararai mudare!” uye Mambo akapfeka magirazi ake akatarisa achishushikana, kuti aone kuti ndiani aitaura.

Alice waikwanisa kuvona, zve inga wakasotesesa pa makhadza avo, kuti vatongi vese vaitara “zviro zvo bungumupee!” pa ma slate avo, zve waikwanisa kutovona kuti umweni wavo aacaiziva kupereketa “benzi,” zve waidikana kubvunja mundhu wo padhuzepo kuti amubvunje. “Muddle yakanaka slates dzavo dzichava mukati kusati kwapera kutongwa!” akafunga kudaro Alice.

Umwe wevatongi ainge aine penzura yaitsvedza. Izvi zvemene, Alice aazi kukwanisa kuema, wakatenderera dare akaguma sure kwake, wakakasira kuwana mukana wekuzvitora. Wakazviita ngekukasira zvekuti murombo mudoko juror (wainge ari Bill, Lizard) wakakorera kuzwisisa zvachose kuti chiinyi chainge chaitika; saka, mushure mokuvhima zveshe, wakamanikidzwa kunyora ngemunwe umwe zuva rose; uye izvi zvaishanda zvishoma zvikuru, sezvo zvaisasiya chiratidzo pa slate.

“Herald, verenga zvaanopomerwa!” vakadaro Mambo.

Pane izvi Tsuro Chena yakaridza mazwi matatu pahwamanda, ndokuzovhura mupumburu weganda, ndokuerenga kudai ngezvinoteera:—

“Mambokadzi weNyoka, akaita dzimwe tarts, .
Zveshe pazuwa rekupisa:
Knave weNyoka, akaba ma tarts aya, .
Uye akavatora zvachose!”

Mambo wakabhuya ku vatongi kuti: “Fungiranyi mutongo wenyu.”

“Hazvisati, hazvisati!” Tsuro yakakurumidza kuvhiringidza. “Pane zvakawanda zvichauya zvisati zvaitika!”

Mambo wakati: “Daidzanyi mufakazi wo kutanga; uye Tsuro Chena yakaridza hwamanda katatu, ndokushevedzera, “Chapupu chekutanga!”

Mufakazi wekutanga waiva Mugadziri weNgowani. Wakapinda aine kapu ye tea munyara imweni ne chingwa-ne-butter munyara imweni. “Ndinokumbira ruregerero, Mambo wenyu,” akatanga, “nokuunza izvi: asi ndainge ndisati ndapedza chaizvo tii yangu pandakatumwa.”

Mambo wakati: “Waifanira kunge wakapedza.” “Wakatanga rini?”

Mugadziri we Ngowani wakaningira March Hare, wakamuteera kupinda mudare, wakabata ruoko nge Dormouse. “Gumi nemana yaKurume, ndinofunga kuti yaive,” akadaro.

“Chegumi neshanu,” wakadaro Tsuro yaKurume.

“Chegumi nematanhatu,” akawedzera Dormouse.

“Nyorai izvo,” Mambo wakabhuya ku vatongi, vatongi vakanyora ngo kudakara mazuwa ese matatu pa zvinyorwa zvavo, vozozviwedzera, voderedza mupinguro kuita shiringi no peni.

Mambo wakabhuya ku Muiti we Ngowani kuti: “Bvisa ngowani yako.”

“Haisi yangu,” akadaro Mugadziri weNgowani.

“ Zvakabiwa! ” Mambo wakadanidzira, akatendeukira kune jury, iyo pakarepo yakaita memorandamu yechokwadi.

“Ndinozvichengeta kuti nditengese,” Mugadziri weNgowani wakawedzera se tsanangudzo; "Hapana changu. Ndiri mugadziri we ngowani."

Heino Mambokadzi akapfeka magirazi ake, akatanga kutarisa Mugadziri weNgowani, uyo akashanduka akachena uye akavhunduka.

Mambo wakati: “Ipa umboo hwako; “uye usatya, kana ndisingaiti kuti uurayiwe ipapo ipapo.”

Izvi azvizi kuita inga zvinokurudzira mufakazi zvachose: wakaramba eichinjira kubva parutsoka rumwe kuenda kune rumweni, einingira Mambokadzi asizi kugadzikana, uye mukuvhiringika kwake wakaruma chikamu chikuru kubva mukapu yake yetii pane chingwa-ne-butter.

Pa nguwa iyi yomene Alice wakazwa kuda kuziya maningi, zvakamushamisa maningi kuguma paakazwisisa kuti chiinyi: wakatanga kukurazve, wakafunga pakutanga kuti waizosimuka wobuda mu dare; asi paakafunga kechipiri akafunga kuramba ari paaigara chero paine nzvimbo yake.

“Ndaidai usikazi kutsvedza kudaro,” wakadaro Dormouse, wakagara parutii paye. “Ndinotadza kufema.”

Alice wakananga ngo kupfava maningi kuti: “Andikwanisi kuzviita, ndiri kukura.”

“Auna kodzero yekukura pano ,” akadaro Dormouse.

Alice wakananga ngo kucunga maningi kuti: “Mucabhuya zviro zvisina shwiro, munoziva kuti muri kukuravo.”

“Eya, asi ndinokura ngo kukasika kunozwisisika,” wakadaro Dormouse, “azviiti ngo njira yondhoyo inoshekeja.” Uye wakasimuka akatsamwa zvikuru akayambuka kuenda kune rimweni divi rechivanze.

Nguwa yeshe iyi Mambokadzi aazi kumbosiya einingira Hatter, uye, apo Dormouse payakayambuka dare, wakabhuya kune umwe wevakuru vedare kuti, “Ndiunzire list yevaimbi mu concert yekupeisira!” pamusoro pazvo Hatter anosuruvarisa akadedera zvakanyanya, zvekuti akazunungutsa shangu dzake dzose mbiri.

Mambo wakapetuja ngo kunyangajwa kuti: “Ipanyi umboo hwenyu, kudari ndicikaiti kudaro, ndinozoita kuti muurawe, kunyazwi munothya kana kuti haiwa.”

“Ndiri murombo, Mambo wenyu,” Mugadziri we Ngowani wakatanga, nge izwi rinodedera, “—nekuti ndainga ndisati ndatanga tii yangu—azvizi kupfuura vhiki kana kudaro—nekuti chiinyi nge chingwa-ne-butter zviri kuondoroka—ne kupenya kwe tii—”

“Kupenya kwe chii? ” akadaro Mambo.

Muiti we Ngowani wakadavira kudari: “ Zvakatanga ngo tea.”

“Ehe kupenya kunotanga nge T!” vakadaro Mambo zvakapinza. "Unonditora sechirema ere? Enda mberi!"

“Ndiri murombo,” Mugadziri weNgowani wakaenderera mberi, “uye zviro zvakawanda zvakapenya mushure maizvozvo—March Hare ega ndiye akati—”

“Andizi kuzviziya!” March Hare akavhiringidza nekukurumidza zvikuru.

“Wazviita!” akadaro Mugadziri weNgowani.

“Ndinozviramba!” akadaro Tsuro yaKurume.

Mambo wakati: “Iyena anozviramba, siyanyi divi rondhoro.”

“Zvakanaka, chero zvakadini, Dormouse wakati—” Mugadziri weNgowani wakaenderera mberi, einingira ngekuvhunduka kuti aone kuti unozozvirambawo ere: asi Dormouse haazi kuramba chiro, ngekuti wakaata zvakanyanya.

“Pasure po izvi,” wakaenderera mberi Muiti we Ngowani, “ndakagura zvimweni zvingwa-ne-butter—”

“Asi Dormouse yakati kudii?” mumwe wevatongi akabvunza.

“Izvozvo handichazvirangariri,” akadaro Mugadziri weNgowani.

Mambo wakananga kuti: “ Munodikana kuceukija kudari, kudari ndicikaiti kudaro, ndinozoita kuti muurawe.”

Mufudzi weNgowani anosuruvarisa wakadonhedza kapu yake yetii nechingwa-ne-butter, akadzika ngebvi rimwe. “Ndiri murombo, Mambo wenyu,” akatanga.

Mambo wakati: “Uri murombo maningi wo kubhuya .”

Apa imwe ye guinea-pigs yakapemberera, ndokukurumidza kudzvinyirirwa ngevakuru vedare. (Sezvo iro riri izwi rakaoma, ndichangokutsanangurira kuti rakaitwa sei. Vainge nechikwama chikuru chechidziro, chaisungirirwa pamuromo ngetambo: mune izvi vaitsvedza guinea-pig, soro kutanga, vozogara pamusoro payo.)

Alice wakarangarira kudari: “Ndadakara maningi ngo kuvona izvi zvakaitwa.” “Ndakaerenga kazinji maningi mu maphephabhuku, pa kupera ko kutongwa, “Painga no kueja kurova nyara, kwakakasira kudziviswa ngo vakuru vo dare,” zve andizi kumbozwisisa kuti zvairevei mbhera wari.”

Mambo wakaenderera mberi eciti: “Kudari ndizvona basi zvamunoziva ngo pamusoro pazvo, munokwanisa kuema pashi.”

“Andikoni kuende pashi,” wakadaro Muiti we Ngowani: “Ndiri pashi, inga zvazviri.”

Mambo wakadavira kudari: “Pasurepo, munokwanisa kugara pashi.”

Apa imwe guinea-nguruve yakapemberera, uye yakadziviswa.

“Uyai, izvozvo zvakapedza nguruve dzeguinea!” akafunga kudaro Alice. “Zvino tichaenderera mberi zviri nani.”

“Ndinoda kupedza tii yangu,” wakadaro Mugadziri weNgowani, einingira Mambokadzi ngekushushikana, uyo waierenga rondedzero yevaimbi.

“Unokona kuenda,” wakadaro Mambo, Mugadziri weNgowani wakakasira kubuda muchivanze, asizi kumboemera kupfeka shangu dzake.

“—uye unongobvisa soro rake kubanze,” Mambokadzi wakawedzera kune umwe wevakuru vehondo: asi Mupfeki weNgowani wakange asisiri kuonekwa mukuru wehondo asati asvika pamusiwo.

“Daidzai chapupu chinotevera!” vakadaro Mambo.

Mufakazi anotevera aive mubiki weDuchess. Wakatakura pepper-box munyara yake, Alice wakafembera kuti ndiyani, kunyazi asati apinda mu court, nge njira iyo vandhu vainge pasinde nge musuwo vakatanga kutsveruka nguva imweyo.

Mambo wakati: “Ipai umboo hwenyu.”

“Handiite,” wakadaro mubiki.

Mambo vakatarisa ngekuvhunduka Tsuro Chena, iyo yakati ngezwi rakaderera, “Mambo vanofanira kubvunzurudza chapupu ichi .”

“Zvakanaka, kana ndichifanira, ndinofanira,” Mambo wakadaro, nge mweya wekusuwa, uye, mushure mokupeta maoko ake nekutsveruka kumubiki kuguma madziso ake akanga ava pedyo nekuoneka, wakabhuya nge izwi rakadzaka, “Ma tart anogadzirwa nge chiinyi?”

“Pepper, kakurutu,” wakadaro mubiki.

“Treacle,” rakadaro izwi rakaata sure kwake.

“Kola iyo Dormouse,” Mambokadzi akashevedzera. "Dura musoro we Dormouse uyo! Bvisa Dormouse uyo kubanze kwedare! Mudzvinyirire! Mubate! Bvisa nge ndebvu dzake!"

Kwemaminitsi amweni dare reshe rainge rakavhiringika, kuita kuti Dormouse ibude, uye, nguva yavakagarazve, mubiki wakanga anyangarika.

"Siyayi hako!" akadaro Mambo, aine mhepo yekurerukirwa zvikuru. “Daidza chapupu chinotevera.” Uye wakawedzera ngezwi rakaderera kuna Mambokadzi, "Zvomene, mudikani wangu, unofanira kubvunze mufakazi unoteera. Zvinondipanda pahuma!"

Alice wakaningira Tsuro Chena apo eitamba-tamba pamusoro pezvinyorwa, eizwa kuda kuziya maningi kuona kuti mufakazi unoteera uchaita sei, “—ngekuti avazi kuwana umboo hwakawanda ,” wakabhuya ega. Pinimidza kushamisika kwake, apo Tsuro Chena yakaerenga, pamusoro pezwi rake diki rinoshevedzera, zita rekuti “Alice!”
