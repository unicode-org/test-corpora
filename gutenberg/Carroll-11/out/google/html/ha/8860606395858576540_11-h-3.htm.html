BABI NA III.
Race-Race da Dogon Tatsuniya

Lallai biki ne mai kamanni da suka taru a bankin—tsuntsaye masu gashin fuka-fukan ja, dabbobin da gashin gashinsu manne kusa da su, kuma duk suna jika, ƙetare, da rashin jin daɗi.

Tambaya ta farko ita ce, yadda za a sake bushewa: sun yi shawarwari game da wannan, kuma bayan 'yan mintoci kaɗan, Alice ya zama kamar na halitta don yin magana da su, kamar dai ta san su duk rayuwarta. Hakika, ta yi doguwar gardama da Lory, wanda a ƙarshe ya juya baya, kuma zai ce kawai, "Na girme ku, kuma dole ne ku sani mafi kyau." kuma wannan Alice ba zai ƙyale ba tare da sanin shekarunta nawa ba, kuma, kamar yadda Lory ya ƙi bayyana shekarunsa, babu wani abin da za a ce.

A karshe dai Mouse, wanda da alama shi mai iko ne a cikin su, ya yi kira, “Ku zauna, ku duka, ku saurare ni, da sannu zan sa ku ya bushe! Gaba daya suka zauna, cikin wani katon zobe, da Mouse a tsakiya. Alice ta kafe idanunta cike da tashin hankali, domin ta ji tabbas za ta yi mugun sanyi idan ba ta bushe da wuri ba.

"Ahm!" In ji Mouse tare da wani muhimmin iska, "Shin kun kasance a shirye? Wannan shine mafi bushewar abin da na sani. Yi shiru duk zagaye, idan kuna so! 'William the Conqueror, wanda dalilinsa ya sami tagomashi da Paparoma, ba da daɗewa ba Ingilishi, wanda ke son shugabanni, kuma ya saba da cin hanci da rashawa.

"Ugh!" Inji Lory, da rawar jiki.

"Fada kuma!" Mouse ya ce, a yamutse fuska, amma cikin ladabi: “Ka yi magana?”

"Ba ni!" Inji Lory cikin gaggawa.

"Ina tsammanin kun yi," in ji Mouse. “—Na ci gaba. ‘Edwin da Morcar, ’yan kunne na Mercia da Northumbria, sun bayyana masa cewa: har ma Stigand, babban limamin cocin Canterbury mai kishin ƙasa, ya ga ya dace—’”

"Mene ne ?" In ji duck.

"An samo shi ," Mouse ya amsa a takaice: "hakika ka san abin da 'yana nufi."

Duck ya ce: “Na san abin da ‘shi’ yake nufi da kyau, sa’ad da na sami abu, in ji Duck: “A koyaushe kwaɗo ne ko tsutsa.” Tambayar ita ce, mene ne babban Bishop ya samu?”

Mouse bai lura da wannan tambaya ba, amma ya ci gaba da sauri, "'-ya ga yana da kyau a tafi tare da Edgar Atheling don saduwa da William kuma mu ba shi rawani. Halin William da farko ya kasance tsaka-tsaki. ya ci gaba, ya juya ga Alice yayin da yake magana.

"Kamar yadda aka jika kamar kullum," in ji Alice a cikin wata murya mai raɗaɗi: "da alama baya shanya ni ko kaɗan."

“In haka ne,” Dodo ta ce da gaske, ta tashi tsaye, “Na matsar da cewa a dage taron, domin a dauki karin magunguna masu kuzari cikin gaggawa—”

"Yi magana Turanci!" Inji Eaglet. "Ban san ma'anar rabin waɗannan dogayen kalmomi ba, kuma, menene ƙari, ban yarda cewa ku ma kuna yi ba!" Kuma Eaglet ya sunkuyar da kansa kasa don ɓoye murmushi: wasu daga cikin sauran tsuntsaye sun yi titter a ji.

"Abin da zan ce," Dodo ya ce a cikin muryar rashin tausayi, "shi ne, cewa mafi kyawun abin da zai sa mu bushe shi ne tseren Caucus."

"Mene ne tseren Caucus?" in ji Alice; Ba wai tana son ta sani ba, sai Dodo ya dakata kamar yana tunanin wani ya yi magana, kuma ba wanda ya yi niyyar cewa komai.

"Don me," in ji Dodo, "Hanya mafi kyau don bayyana shi ita ce a yi." (Kuma, kamar yadda za ku so ku gwada abin da kanku, wani lokacin sanyi, zan gaya muku yadda Dodo ya sarrafa shi.)

Da farko ya nuna hanyar tsere, a cikin wani da'irar, ("daidaitaccen siffa ba kome ba," in ji shi,) sa'an nan kuma an sanya duk jam'iyyar tare da hanya, nan da can. Babu “Ɗaya, biyu, uku, da nesa,” amma sun fara gudu sa’ad da suke so, da kuma barin lokacin da suke so, don ba shi da sauƙi a san lokacin da gasar ta ƙare. Duk da haka, da suka yi gudun rabin sa’a ko fiye, kuma suka sake bushewa, sai nan da nan Dodo ya yi kira da “Gasar ta ƙare!” Duk suka taru, suna haki, suna tambaya, “Amma wa ya ci nasara?”

Wannan tambayar Dodo ya kasa amsawa ba tare da wani dogon tunani ba, sai ta zauna ta dade da dafe goshinta da yatsa daya (matsayin da kuke ganin Shakespeare a cikin hotunansa), yayin da sauran suka jira shiru. A karshe Dodo ya ce, “ Kowa ya yi nasara, kuma kowa ya samu kyautuka.

"Amma wa zai ba da kyaututtukan?" muryoyin muryoyi suka tambaya.

"Don me , tabbas," in ji Dodo, yana nuna Alice da yatsa ɗaya; Nan take gaba dayan jama'a suka taru suka zagaye ta, suna kiran a ruɗe, suna cewa, "Prizes! Prizes!"

Alice ba ta da masaniyar abin da za ta yi, a razane ta sa hannunta cikin aljihunta, ta zaro kwalin kwalin kwalin, (sa'a ruwan gishirin bai shiga ba), ta mika musu a matsayin kyaututtuka. Akwai daidai guda ɗaya, duk zagaye.

"Amma lallai ita kanta tana da kyauta, ka sani," in ji Mouse.

“Tabbas,” Dodo ya amsa da gaske. "Me kuma ka samu a aljihunka?" Ya ci gaba, ya juya ga Alice.

"Kawai kawai," in ji Alice cikin baƙin ciki.

Dodo ya ce, “Ka mika shi a nan.

Daga nan sai suka sake cin karo da ita, yayin da Dodo ya gabatar da katon tuwo, yana mai cewa, “Muna rokon ku da ku yarda da wannan katafaren leda; kuma, da ya gama wannan gajeriyar jawabin, sai suka yi ta murna.

Alice ta zaci duk abin ba wauta ne, amma duk sun yi kama da kabari har ba ta kuskura ta yi dariya ba; ita kuma ta kasa tunanin abinda zata ce, sai kawai ta sunkuyar da kanta, ta dauki tuwon, tana kallon yadda ta iya.

Abu na gaba shi ne cin ƙwanƙolin: wannan ya haifar da hayaniya da ruɗani, yayin da manyan tsuntsaye suka koka da cewa ba za su iya ɗanɗano nasu ba, ƙananan kuma sun shaƙe ana shafa bayansu. Duk da haka, daga ƙarshe ya ƙare, kuma suka sake zama cikin zobe, suka roƙi Mouse ya faɗa musu wani abu.

"Kin yi alkawarin gaya mani tarihin ku, kin sani," in ji Alice, "da kuma dalilin da ya sa kike ƙi-C da D," ta ƙara da cewa a cikin raɗaɗi, rabin tsoron kada a sake yin fushi.

"Nawa dogon labari ne mai ban tausayi!" Mouse ya ce, ya juya ga Alice, ya yi ta nishi.

"Hakika doguwar wutsiya ce ," in ji Alice, tana kallon kasa da mamaki kan jelar Mouse; "amma me yasa kike kiransa da bakin ciki?" Ita kuma ta ci gaba da daure kai a lokacin da Mouse ke magana, don tunaninta game da tatsuniya ya kasance kamar haka:-

"Ba ku halarta ba!" In ji Mouse ga Alice mai tsanani. "Me kake tunani?"

"Ina neman afuwar ku," in ji Alice cikin tawali'u: "Kin kai kashi na biyar, ina tsammani?"

"Ban samu ba! " Mouse ya yi kuka, a fusace.

"A kulli!" in ji Alice, ko da yaushe a shirye take don yin amfani da kanta, kuma tana kallon ta cikin damuwa. "Oh, bari in taimaka in gyara shi!"

"Ba zan yi komai ba," in ji Mouse, yana tashi yana tafiya. "Kuna zagina da irin wannan maganar banza!"

"Ban nufi ba!" ya roki talaka Alice. "Amma kana da sauƙin fushi, ka sani!"

Mouse ya yi kara kawai yana amsawa.

"Don Allah ka dawo ka gama labarinka!" Alice ta kira bayansa; da sauran duk suka shiga cikin mawaƙa, "I, don Allah yi!" amma Mouse ya girgiza kai kawai ba tare da hakuri ba, ya yi tafiya kadan da sauri.

"Abin tausayi ba zai tsaya ba!" ya huci Lory, da zaran ba a gani; sai wata tsohuwar Kaguwa ta yi amfani da damar ta ce wa ‘yarta “Ah, masoyina! "Ki rike harshenki, Ma!" In ji matashin Kaguwa, a ɗan zare. "Kin isa ki gwada hakurin kawa!"

"Da ace in sami Dinah a nan, na san ina yi!" Alice ta fada da karfi, ba ta yiwa kowa magana ba. "Da sannu zata dawo da ita!"

"Wacece Dinah, idan zan iya yin tambayar?" in ji Lory.

Alice ta amsa da ƙwazo, domin ta kasance a shirye koyaushe ta yi magana game da dabbar ta: “Dinah cat ɗinmu ce, kuma ita ce babban jari don kama berayen da ba za ku yi tunani ba!

Wannan jawabi ya haifar da wani abin mamaki a cikin jam'iyyar. Wasu tsuntsayen suka yi gaggawar tafiya nan take: wata tsohuwa Magpie ta fara nade kanta a hankali, tana mai cewa, “Da gaske na dawo gida; iskar dare bai dace da makogwarona ba!” sai wani Canary ya kira 'ya'yansa cikin rawar jiki ya ce, "Ku zo, ƙaunatattuna, lokaci ya yi da ku duka kuna kwance!" A kan wasu dalilai daban-daban duk sun tashi, kuma ba da daɗewa ba aka bar Alice ita kaɗai.

"Da ma ban ambaci Dinah ba!" Ta fada a ranta cikin sanyin murya. "Ba wanda yake sonta, a nan, kuma na tabbata ita ce mafi kyawun kyan gani a duniya! Haba Dinah dina! Ina tunanin ko zan ƙara ganinki!" Kuma a nan matalauciya Alice ta sake yin kuka, don ta ji kaɗaici da ƙarancin ruhi. Cikin kankanin lokaci ta sake jin takun takunta daga nesa, sai ta daga kai da kwadayi, rabi tana fatan beran ya canza ra'ayinsa, ya dawo ya karasa labarinsa.
