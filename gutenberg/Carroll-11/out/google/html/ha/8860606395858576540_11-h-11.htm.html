BABI NA XI.
Wanene Ya Saci Tarts?

Sarki da Sarauniyar zukata suna zaune a kan karagarsu lokacin da suka isa, tare da taron jama'a da yawa a kusa da su - kowane nau'in tsuntsaye da dabbobi iri-iri, da kuma dukan fakitin kati: Knave yana tsaye a gabansu, cikin sarƙoƙi, da soja a kowane gefe don kiyaye shi; kuma kusa da sarki akwai farin zomo, da ƙaho a daya hannun, da kuma littafin rubutu a daya hannun. A tsakiyar kotun akwai wani teburi, ɗauke da babban tasa na tarts: sun yi kyau sosai, har ya sa Alice ta ji yunwa ta kalle su—“Ina da a yi shari’ar,” in ji ta, “su kuma miƙa abincin!” Amma da alama babu damar hakan, don haka ta fara duban komai nata, don ta wuce lokaci.

Alice ba ta taɓa shiga kotun shari’a ba, amma ta karanta game da su a cikin littattafai, kuma ta yi farin ciki sosai da ta ga cewa ta san kusan duk wani abu da ke wurin. "Alƙali kenan," in ji ta a cikin ranta, "saboda girman gashin kansa."

Alƙali kuwa, shi ne Sarki; kuma yayin da ya sa kambinsa a kan wig, (duba gaban gaba idan kuna son ganin yadda ya yi,) bai kalli komai ba, kuma tabbas bai zama ba.

"Kuma wannan shi ne akwatin juri," in ji Alice, "da waɗannan halittu goma sha biyu," (wajibi ne ta ce "halittu," ka ga, saboda wasu daga cikinsu dabbobi ne, wasu kuma tsuntsaye ne,) "Ina tsammanin su ne alkalai." Ta faɗi wannan kalma ta ƙarshe sau biyu ko uku a ranta, tana mai alfahari da ita: domin ta yi tunani, da gaskiya ma, cewa ƙananan 'yan matan zamaninta kaɗan ne suka san ma'anarta kwata-kwata. Duk da haka, "jury-maza" sun yi daidai da haka.

Masu shari'a goma sha biyun duk sun yi ta rubuce-rubuce sosai a kan faifai. "Me suke yi?" Alice ta rada wa Gryphon. "Ba za su iya samun abin da za su ajiye ba tukuna, kafin a fara shari'ar."

"Suna ajiye sunayensu," in ji Gryphon a cikin amsa, "don tsoron kada su manta da su kafin karshen shari'ar."

"Abubuwan banza!" Alice ta fara da kakkausar murya, bacin rai, amma ta dakata da sauri, domin Farin zomo ya yi kuka, “Shiru a cikin kotu!” Sarki kuwa ya saka lek'o'insa, ya kalleta cikin tashin hankali, domin ya gano wanda ke magana.

Alice ta iya gani, da kuma idan tana kallon kafaɗunsu, cewa duk alkalai suna rubuta "abubuwan wawa!" a kan slates ɗinsu, har ma ta iya gane cewa ɗayansu bai san yadda ake rubuta “wawa ba,” kuma dole ne ya tambayi maƙwabcinsa ya gaya masa. "Kyakkyawan laka za su kasance a ciki kafin shari'ar ta ƙare!" tunanin Alice.

Daya daga cikin alkalan yana da fensir mai tsuma. Wannan ba shakka, Alice ba za ta iya tsayawa ba, sai ta zagaya kotu ta bi shi a baya, kuma nan da nan ta sami damar cire shi. Ta yi hakan da sauri har dan karamin alkali (Bill, Lizard) ya kasa tantance ko kadan abin da ya faru; don haka, bayan farautarsa duka, sai ya wajaba a kansa ya rubuta da yatsa guda har sauran yini; kuma wannan ba shi da amfani sosai, saboda bai bar wani tasiri a kan slate ba.

"Herald, karanta zargin!" Inji Sarki.

Akan haka ne Farin Zomo ya busa kaho guda uku, sannan ya zare littafin littafin, ya karanta kamar haka:-

"Sarauniyar Zuciya, ta yi wasu tart,
Duk a ranar bazara:
The Knave of Hearts, ya sace wadannan tarts,
Kuma ya tafi da su sosai!”

“Ku yi la’akari da hukuncinku,” Sarkin ya ce wa alkali.

"Ba tukuna, ba tukuna!" Zomo yayi gaggawar katse shi. "Akwai babban abin da zai zo kafin wannan!"

Sarki ya ce, “Ka kira shaida na farko; kuma Farin Zomo ya busa ƙaho uku, ya yi kira, “Shaida ta farko!”

Shaida ta farko ita ce Hatter. Ya shigo da ledar tea a hannu daya da biredi da man shanu a daya hannun. Ya fara cewa: “Na roƙi gafara, Mai Martaba, don kawo waɗannan: amma ban gama gama shayi na ba lokacin da aka aiko ni.”

“Ya kamata ka gama,” in ji Sarkin. "Yaushe ka fara?"

Hatter ya kalli kurewar Maris, wanda ya bi shi cikin kotu, da hannu da Dormouse. "Goma sha huɗu ga Maris, ina tsammanin ya kasance," in ji shi.

"Na goma sha biyar," in ji Maris Kuren.

"Na sha shida," in ji Dormouse.

“Rubuta haka,” Sarki ya ce wa masu shari’a, sai alkalan suka rubuta dukkan kwanukan ukun a kan takardar su, sannan suka hada su, suka rage amsar shilling da pence.

"Cire hular ka," Sarkin ya ce wa Hatter.

"Ba nawa ba ne," in ji Hatter.

“ An sace! ” Sarkin ya ce, ya juya ga alkalan kotun, wanda nan take ya ba da sanarwar gaskiyar.

"Na ajiye su don sayarwa," Hatter ya kara da cewa a matsayin bayani; "Ba ni da nawa, ni mai hula ne."

Anan sarauniya ta saka kayan kallo, ta fara duban Hatter, wanda ya juya ya zama farilla.

“Ka ba da shaidarka,” in ji Sarki; "Kada ki damu, ko kuma a kashe ki nan take."

Wannan ko kadan bai karawa mai shaida kwarin guiwa ba: ya cigaba da juyawa daga kafa daya zuwa wancan, yana kallon sarauniya babu dadi, cikin rudani ya cije wani katon guntun shayin nasa maimakon biredi da man shanu.

A dai-dai wannan lokacin Alice ta ji wani yanayi mai ban sha'awa, wanda ya daure mata kai sosai har sai da ta gano menene: ta fara girma kuma, ta yi tunanin da farko za ta tashi ta bar kotun; amma a tunani na biyu ta yanke shawarar ci gaba da zama a inda take muddin akwai dakinta.

“Da ma ba za ki matse haka ba,” Dormouse da ke zaune kusa da ita ta ce. "Ba zan iya numfashi ba."

"Ba zan iya taimaka ba," in ji Alice cikin tawali'u: "Ina girma."

"Ba ku da ikon yin girma a nan ," in ji Dormouse.

"Kada ku yi maganar banza," in ji Alice da gaba gaɗi: "ka san kai ma kana girma."

"Eh, amma ina girma a cikin taki mai ma'ana," in ji Dormouse: "ba a cikin wannan salon ban dariya ba." Shi kuwa ya tashi a nitse ya haye can gefe na kotun.

Duk wannan lokacin Sarauniyar ba ta daina kallon Hatter ba, kuma, a daidai lokacin da Dormouse ta ketare kotu, sai ta ce wa daya daga cikin jami'an kotun, "Kawo mini jerin mawaka a cikin wasan kwaikwayo na karshe!" a kan abin da shaƙuwar Hatter ya yi rawar jiki, har ya girgiza takalmansa biyu.

“Ka ba da shaidarka,” in ji Sarkin a fusace, “ko kuwa a kashe ka, ko kana cikin damuwa ko a’a.

“Ni talaka ne, Mai Martaba,” Hatter ya fara, cikin rawar jiki, “—kuma ban fara shayi na ba—ba sama da mako guda ba—kuma menene tare da biredi-da-man shanu da ke yin siriri sosai—da kyaftawar shayin—”

"Kwaftawar me? " in ji Sarki.

"Ya fara da shayi," Hatter ya amsa.

"Tabbas kyalkyali yana farawa da T!" Inji Sarki da karfi. "Kina dauke ni da wasa? Ci gaba!"

"Ni talaka ne," Hatter ya ci gaba da cewa, "kuma yawancin abubuwan sun yi kyalkyali bayan haka - Maris kurege ne kawai ya ce -"

"Ban yi ba!" Kuren Maris ya katse cikin gaugawa mai girma.

"Ka yi!" Inji Hatter.

"Na ƙaryata!" In ji kuren Maris.

"Ya ƙaryata," in ji Sarkin: "ku bar wannan ɓangaren."

"To, ko ta yaya, Dormouse ya ce," Hatter ya ci gaba, yana duban damuwa don ganin ko zai musanta hakan: amma Dormouse ya musanta kome, yana barci mai sauri.

"Bayan haka," in ji Hatter, "Na sake yanka gurasa-da-man shanu-"

"Amma me Dormouse ya ce?" daya daga cikin alkalan ya tambaya.

"Wannan ba zan iya tunawa ba," in ji Hatter.

“ Dole ne ka tuna,” in ji Sarkin, “ko kuwa zan kashe ka.”

Hatter mai baƙin ciki ya sauke shayinsa da burodi da man shanu, ya faɗi a kan gwiwa ɗaya. "Ni talaka ne, Mai Martaba," ya fara.

Sarkin ya ce: "Kai talakan mai magana ne sosai ."

Anan daya daga cikin aladun ya yi murna, nan take jami’an kotun suka danne su. (Da yake wannan kalma ce mai wuyar gaske, kawai zan bayyana muku yadda aka yi. Suna da babban jakar zane, wanda aka daure a baki da igiya: a cikin wannan suka zame alade, kai farko, sannan suka zauna a kai).

“Na yi farin ciki da na ga an yi haka,” in ji Alice. "Na sha karanta a jaridu, a ƙarshen shari'a, "An yi ƙoƙari na tafi, wanda jami'an kotu suka danne su nan da nan," kuma ban fahimci abin da ake nufi ba har yanzu."

Sarkin ya ci gaba da cewa: “Idan ka sani ke nan, za ka iya tsayawa.

"Ba zan iya yin ƙasa ba," in ji Hatter: "Ina kan ƙasa, kamar yadda yake."

"Sa'an nan za ku zauna ," Sarkin ya ce.

Anan dayan guinea-pig yayi murna, aka danne shi.

"Ku zo, wanda ya gama cin aladu!" tunanin Alice. "Yanzu za mu ci gaba."

"Na fi son in gama shayi na," in ji Hatter, tare da kallon damuwa ga Sarauniya, wadda ke karanta jerin mawaƙa.

"Za ku iya tafiya," in ji Sarki, kuma Hatter ya yi sauri ya bar kotu, ba tare da ya jira ya sa takalmansa ba.

Sarauniyar ta kara da daya daga cikin jami'an "- kuma kawai cire kansa daga waje," amma Hatter ba ya gani kafin jami'in ya isa bakin kofa.

"Kirawo shaida na gaba!" Inji Sarki.

Shaidu na gaba shine mai girkin Duchess. Ta dauko barkonon tsohuwa a hannunta, Alice ta tsinkayi ko waye, tun kafin ta shiga cikin kotun, ta yadda mutanen da ke kusa da kofar suka fara atishawa gaba daya.

“Ka ba da shaidarka,” in ji Sarkin.

"Shan't," in ji mai dafa abinci.

Sarki ya dubi Farin Zomo cikin tashin hankali, wanda ya ce da murya kasa-kasa, "Mai martaba dole ne ya yi wa wannan shaida tambayoyi."

"To, idan na zama dole, dole ne," in ji Sarkin, da iska mai raɗaɗi, kuma, bayan ya naɗe hannuwansa ya daure a kan mai dafa abinci har idanunsa sun kusa fita daga gani, ya ce a cikin murya mai zurfi, "Me aka yi tarts?"

"Pepper, yawanci," in ji mai dafa.

Muryar bacci taji a bayanta tace "magani."

"Collar that Dormouse," Sarauniyar ta yi ihu. "Ku fille kan wannan Dormouse! Ku mayar da Dormouse daga kotu! Ku danne shi! Ku kama shi! Kashe shi da barasa."

Tsawon mintuna duk kotun ta rude, ganin Dormous din ya fito, har suka sake zama, mai dafa abinci ya bace.

"Kada ku damu!" In ji Sarki, cikin iska mai cike da walwala. "Kirawo shaida na gaba." Kuma ya ƙara da cewa a cikin raɗaɗi ga Sarauniya, "Hakika, masoyi, dole ne ku bincika shaida na gaba, yana sa gabana ya yi zafi!"

Alice ta kalli Farin Zomo yayin da yake birgima kan jerin sunayen, tana mai matuƙar sha'awar ganin yadda mashaidi na gaba zai kasance, "- don har yanzu ba su sami shaida da yawa ba tukuna ," in ji ta a cikin ranta. Ka yi tunanin mamakinta, lokacin da Farin Rabbit ya karanta, a saman ƙaramar muryarsa, sunan "Alice!"
