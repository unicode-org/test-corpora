“Am do̠m ho̠yo̠k̕a,” Pusi menkeda, “baṅkhan no̠ṇde̠ bam he̠c̕ le̠na.”

Alice do bae hudis leda je ona do cet́ hõ sạbit gea; menkhan uni doe laha idikeda “Ar cet lekatem badaea je am dom pagol akana?”

“Etọhọb lạgit́,” Pusi menkeda, “mit’ seta do bae pagol akana. Am do onaem aṅgoceda?”

“Iń doń hudis eda,” Alice doe menkeda.

“Hẽ, tobe,” Pusi doe laha idikeda, “am do ńelme, mit’ṭen seta do rạgi len khan ḍaṅgrae ḍaṅgraet’ kana, ar kusi len khan ḍaṅgra doe ḍaṅgraet’ kana. Nit’ do iń kusi len khan ḍaṅgrae ḍaṅgraet’ kana, ar rạgi len khan ḍaṅgra doe ḍaṅgraet’ kana. Onate iń doń pagol akana.”

“ Iń do̠ purạń metae kana, gạḍi do̠ baṅ,” Alice do̠e menkeda.

“Oka lekape kusiak̕ ona do̠pe hohoa,” Pusi do̠e menkeda. “Tihiń do̠ Rani sãote kroket kheleda cet́?”

Alice doe menkeda, “Iń do ạḍiń kusiḱa, menkhan nit hạbić do bạń neota akana.”

“Onḍe do̠m ńel ńamạńa,” Pusi do̠e menkeda arye ạcur calaoena.

Alice do̠ noa re̠ ạḍi bạṛti do̠ bae hahaṛa lena, uni do̠ queer jinis ho̠yo̠k̕kan re̠ ạḍi ạ̃ṭe ạcu calao̠ idiet̕kan tahẽkana. Ona jaega okareye tahekan ona doe nelkeda, unre ackage arho ona doe ńel ńamkeda.

“Bay-bae, gidrạ cet́ hoe ena?” mente Pusi menkeda. “Iń do̠ ạḍi gạhir geń hiṛiń akada kuli.”

“Noa do̠ sukri lekae pherao akana” Alice do̠ thir hapẽ kate̠ye̠ menkeda, cet́ leka sạriạḱ lekate̠ye̠ ruạṛ heć akana.

“Iń doń hudis leda,” Pusi doe menkeda ar arhõe ạcur calaoena.

Alice do thora dinteye thir hapeyena, adha do arhõ ńel lạgit́e as tahekana, menkhan ona do bae ńel ńamlena, ar mit́́ ṭen bar minit tayom uni do ona sećteye calaoena okaṭaḱre March Hare doe tahen kana menteko lạiet́kan tahẽkana. “Iń do̠ lahare̠ hạṭińkoń ńel akada,” uni do̠ ac̕ak̕ monre̠ye̠ menkeda; “March Hare do ạḍi mońj ge hoyoḱa, ar baṅma noa do May cando kana mente ona do raving pagol do baṅ hoyoḱa- kom se kom do March reaḱ leka do baṅ.” Ona katha menkate uni doe udgạuena, ar onḍe do arhõ Pusi, mit́ṭen dare ḍạrkoreye duṛuṕ akana.

“Am do̠m menkeda sukri, se̠ fig?” mente Pusi menkeda.

“Iń doń menkeda sukri,” Alice doe roṛ ruạṛkeda; “ar iń doń khoj kana je am do nonka acka te bam ńel ocoḱ ar bam ńel ocoḱa: am do mit́ṭen ạḍi giddi em benaoet́ kana.”
