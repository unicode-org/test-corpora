“Unre do̠ baṅ ro̠ṛ jạruṛa,” Haṭar do̠e menkeda.

Noa bạṛić kạmi do Alice sahao daṛeaḱ khon hõ bạṛti ge tahẽkana: uni do ạḍi gạltiteye uṭhạuena ar calaoena; Dormouse do ona ghạṛićgeye jạpit́ena ar eṭaḱko modre okoe hõ uni calaḱ reaḱ do bako hudiskeda, menkhan uni do mit́ bar dhao tayomteye ńelkeda, adha do asgeye ńelkeda je onko do uni tayomte ko hohoaea: mucạt́ dhao uni doe ńelkedea, onko do Dormouse do chapa reaḱ ḍạrkoreko ḍoboḱ lạgit́ko kurumutuet́kan tahẽkana.

“Jaha lekate hõ̠ iń do̠ ar tis hõ̠ onḍe bạń calaoa!” ” Alice do̠ ḍạr talate̠ ạḍi ãṭe̠ ạcur calao̠en khan̠ye̠ menkeda. “Noa do̠ ińaḱ jion reaḱ ạḍi bạṛić tea-party kana!”

Ona katha roṛ kate uni doe ńel keda je mit́́ ṭen dare reaḱ mit́́ ṭen duạr do sạri ge ona bhitri seć calaḱ kana. “Ona do̠ ạḍi kusiaḱ kana!” uni doe hudis keda. "Menkhan tehen do joto ge curious gea. Iń doń hudis eda je iń do mit́ dhao geń bolo daṛeaḱa." Ar uni doe calaoena.

Mit́ dhao do arhõ ḍher ghạṛić ḍạrkoreye ńamkeda, ar kạṭic̕ kaca table ṭhen. “Nitok do, noa dhao do bhageteń managea,” uni do acʼakʼ monreye menkeda ar ona kạṭicʼ sona reakʼ chabi hatao kate, ar bagwan secʼ calaoen duạr doe jhićkeda. Ado uni do mushroom jom katet kạmi lạgit́e calaoena (uni do ona reaḱ mit́ṭen ṭuṭhạu acren jạpit́reye doho akada) mit́́ ṭen ḍạr hạbić jạpit́ kate: ona tayom uni do kạṭić ḍạrkoreye calaoena: ar ona tayom —uni do mucạt́re ạḍi mońj bagwanre, ạḍi jạruṛok̕kan baha ar rạskạ pạurạko talareye ńamkeda.
