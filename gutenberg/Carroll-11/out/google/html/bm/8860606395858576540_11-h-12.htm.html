O waati yɛrɛ la, Masakɛ min tun bolo degunnen don ka sɛbɛnni kɛ a ka kaye kɔnɔ kabini waati jan, o ye sɔgɔsɔgɔ kɛ ni “Makun!” ani k’a ka gafe kalan min tɔgɔ ye ko “Sariya binaani ni fila. Mɔgɔ bɛɛ janya ka ca ni kilomɛtɛrɛ kelen ye ka bɔ kiritigɛso la .”

Knave ko: “N b’i deli, i ka Masakɛ, ne ma o sɛbɛn, wa u tɛ se k’a jira ko ne y’a sɛbɛn: tɔgɔ si ma bolonɔ bila a laban na.”

Masakɛ ko: “N’i ma i bolonɔ bila a la, o bɛ ko juguya dɔrɔn. N’a sɔrɔ i tun b’a fɛ ka juguya dɔ fɔ, n’o tɛ, i tun bɛna i tɔgɔ bolonɔ bila i n’a fɔ mɔgɔ sɛbɛ.”

Boloci caman Kɛra o ko in na: o Kɛra Masakɛ ka hakilitigiya fɔlɔ ye tiɲɛ na o don.

Masamuso ko: “O b’a jira ko a jalakilen don.

Masakɛ ko: “Aw k’u kalan.

Laɲɔ finman y’a ka ɲɛkisɛw don. “Ne bɛna daminɛ min, n b’i deli, i ka Matigi?” a ye ɲininkali kɛ.

Masakɛ y’a fɔ ni kɔnɔnajɛya ye ko: “A daminɛ daminɛ na, i ka taa fo i ka se laban na: o kɔ, i ka jɔ.”

Laɲɔ finman ye tɛmɛsira minnu kalan, olu tun ye ninnu ye:—

“O ye dalilu nafamaba ye an ye min mɛn fɔlɔ,” Masakɛ y’a fɔ k’a bolow magaya; “o la sisan, a to jury—”

Alisi ko: “Ni u dɔ la kelen bɛ se k’a ɲɛfɔ, (a tun bonyalen don kosɛbɛ miniti labanw na fo a tun ma siran dɔɔnin k’a ka kuma tigɛ), “Ne bɛna warijɛ wɔɔrɔ di a ma. Ne dalen t’a la ko kɔrɔ atomi dɔ bɛ o la.”.

Jury (kiritigɛjɛkulu) bɛɛ y’a sɛbɛn u ka slate (sɛbɛnw) kan ko “ A dalen t’a la ko kɔrɔ atomu dɔ bɛ a kɔnɔ,” nka u si m’a ɲini ka sɛbɛn in ɲɛfɔ.

Masakɛ ko: “Ni kɔrɔ t’a la, min bɛ gɛlɛya diɲɛ dɔ kisi, i b’a dɔn, i n’a fɔ an mago t’a la k’a ɲini ka dɔ sɔrɔ cogo min na. Wa hali o bɛɛ la, ne t’a dɔn,” a taara a fɛ, ka tɛmɛsira ninnu sɛnsɛn a gɛnɛgɛnɛ kan, k’u lajɛ ni ɲɛ kelen ye “A bɛ iko ne bɛ kɔrɔ dɔw ye u la, o bɛɛ kɔfɛ.“— ko ne tɛ se ka jigin —” i tɛ se ka jigin, tiɲɛ tɛ?” a y'o Fàra o kan, k'a ɲɛsin Knave ma.

Knave y'a kunkolo wuli ni dusukasi ye. “Yala ne bɛ i n’a fɔ o wa?” a ko ten. (O min siga t’a la, a ma o kɛ , k’a sɔrɔ a bɛɛ dilannen don ni kartɔn ye.)

“A ɲɛna, fo ka na se bi ma,” Masakɛ y’a fɔ, a tora ka ŋunuŋunu tɛmɛsiraw kan a yɛrɛ kɔnɔ: “‘ An b’a dɔn ko tiɲɛ don —’ o ye jury ye, tiɲɛ don—’ Ne ye kelen di a ma, u ye fila di a ma —’ mun na, a ka kan ka kɛ o de ye ni tarɛtiw ye, i b’a dɔn—”

Alice ko: “Nka, a bɛ taa ɲɛ ‘ u bɛɛ seginna ka bɔ a fɛ ka na i fɛ .’

“Mun na, u bɛ yen!” Masakɛ y'o fɔ ni sebaaya ye, k'a bolo sin tarɛtiw ma minnu bɛ tabali kan. “Foyi tɛ se ka jɛya ka tɛmɛ o kan . O kɔfɛ tuguni—’ sanni a ka nin fiɲɛ in sɔrɔ —’ i ma deli ka fiɲɛ sɔrɔ, n terikɛ, ne hakili la?” a y'o fɔ Masamuso ye.

"Abada!" Masamuso y’o fɔ ni dimi ye, k’a ka tintiri dɔ fili Nɛgɛso ma a kumatɔ. (Bil fitinin dusukasilen tun ye sɛbɛnni dabila a ka slate kan ni a bolokɔnincinin kelen ye, k’a sɔrɔ a y’a sɔrɔ ko taamasiyɛn si ma kɛ a la; nka sisan a y’a daminɛ teliya la kokura, ka baara kɛ ni tintiri ye, min tun bɛ ka woyo a ɲɛda la, ni a mɛnna.)

“O tuma na fɛ, kumaw tɛ bɛn i ma,” Masakɛ y’a fɔ k’a ɲɛsin kiritigɛso lamini ma ni nisɔndiya ye. Makun salen dɔ tun bɛ yen.

“O ye pun ye!” Masakɛ y’a fara a kan ni kumakan ye min bɛ mɔgɔ dusu tiɲɛ, bɛɛ ye ŋɛɲɛ kɛ ko “kiritigɛlaw k’u ka kiritigɛ jateminɛ.” masakɛ y' o fɔ , a siɲɛ mugannan ɲɔgɔn o don .

“Ayi, ayi!” Masamuso y'o fɔ. “Kiritigɛ fɔlɔ—kiritigɛ kɔfɛ.”

“Fɛnw ni kumakolonw!” Alisi y’o fɔ ni kanba ye. “Hakilina min ye ka kumasen sɔrɔ fɔlɔ!”

“I nɛnkun minɛ!” Masamuso y'o fɔ k'a kɛ bulama ye.

“Ne tɛna o kɛ!” Alisi ko ten.

“Ka taa n’a kunkolo ye!” Masamuso pɛrɛnna a kan sanfɛ. Mɔgɔ si ma wuli.

“Jɔn b’a janto i la?” Alisi ko, (a tun bonyalen don ka se a bonya dafalen ma nin waati in na). “I tɛ fosi ye ni karti pake tɛ!”

O kɛlen, pake bɛɛ wulila fiɲɛ na, ka pan ka jigin a kan: a ye kule dɔɔnin kɛ, siran tilancɛ ni dimi tilancɛ, k’a ɲini k’u bugɔ, k’a sɔrɔ a dalen bɛ banki la, a kunkolo bɛ a balimamuso kɔ, min tun bɛ ka furabulu salen dɔw sɔgɔ nɔgɔya la, minnu tun wulila ka bɔ jiriw la ka jigin a ɲɛda kan.

“Kununna, Alisi kanulen!” a balimamuso ko; “Mun na, i ye sunɔgɔ jan kɛ dɛ!”

“E, ne ye o sugo ɲininkali sugu dɔ kɛ!” Alisi ko, a y’a fɔ a balimamuso ye, i n’a fɔ a hakili bɛ se ka to u la cogo min na, a ka nin Aventures kabakoma ninnu bɛɛ, i bɛ ka minnu kalan sisan; a bannen, a balimamuso y’a susu, k’a fɔ ko: “O kɛra sugo kabakoma ye, n terikɛ, tiɲɛ na: nka sisan, i boli ka don i ka te la, a bɛ ka tɛmɛn.” O la sa, Alisi wulila ka boli ka taa, k’a miiri k’a to boli la, i n’a fɔ a bɛ se ka kɛ cogo min na, ko a kɛra sugo dumanba ye dɛ.

Nka a balimamuso sigilen tun bɛ a to yen dɔrɔn, a y’a kunkolo da a bolo kan, ka tile bintɔ filɛ, ka miiri Alisi fitinin n’a ka Aventures kabakomaw bɛɛ la, fo ale fana y’a daminɛ ka sugo kɛ modɛli dɔ kɔfɛ, wa nin tun ye a ka sugo ye:—

Fɔlɔ, a ye siko kɛ Alisi fitinin yɛrɛ la, ani siɲɛ kelen tuguni, bolo misɛnninw sirilen bɛ a gɛnɛgɛnɛ na, ani ɲɛ kɔrɔ jɛlenw tun bɛ ka u ɲɛsin a ta ma — a tun bɛ se k’a kan mankanw yɛrɛ mɛn, ka o a kunkolo wulicogo fitinin kabakoma ye walasa ka kunsigi yaala-yaalalen bali min tun bɛna don a ɲɛw la tuma bɛɛ — wa hali bi, k’a to a lamɛnni na, walima a tun bɛ iko a bɛ lamɛnni kɛ, a lamini yɔrɔ bɛɛ kɛra ɲɛnama ni a balimamuso fitinin ka sugo danfɛn kabakomaw ye.

Bin jan in tun bɛ mankan bɔ a sen na tuma min na Laɲɔ Finman bɛ teliya ka tɛmɛ — Sogo sirannen y’a ka sira sɔgɔ ka tɛmɛ a kɛrɛfɛ jidagayɔrɔ fɛ — a tun bɛ se ka te tasaw mankan mɛn tuma min na Marisikalo wulu n’a teriw tun bɛ u ka dumuni kɛ ɲɔgɔn fɛ min tɛ ban abada, ani Masamuso kan mankanba min tun bɛ yamaruya di a ka dunan bɔnɛnenw ma u ka faga — donsokɛ den tun bɛ ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ siɲɛ kelen kokura Duchess gɛnɛgɛnɛ, k’a sɔrɔ tabali ni minɛnw bɛ ka bin a lamini na — siɲɛ kelen kokura Gryphon ka kulekan, Lizard ka slate-pencil ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ, ani gine-donsokɛw damatɛmɛnenw ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ, olu ye fiɲɛ fa, ka ɲagami ni Mock Turtle jugu ka kasikan ye yɔrɔjan.

O la, a y’i sigi, a ɲɛw datugulen, a tilancɛ dalen b’a yɛrɛ la Kabako jamana na, hali n’a tun b’a dɔn ko a ka kan k’u da wuli tugun, wa fɛn bɛɛ bɛna Changé ka kɛ tiɲɛ ye min tɛ fɛn ye — bin bɛna wuli dɔrɔn fiɲɛ fɛ, ani jidagayɔrɔ bɛna wuli ka kɛ binw lamagakan ye — te tasa mankanw bɛna Changé ka kɛ sagaw ka bɛlɛkisɛw mankan ye, ani Masamuso ka kulekanba bɛ wuli ka kɛ sagagɛnnaw kan ye cɛnin — ani den ka sɔgɔsɔgɔ, Gryphon ka kulekan, ani mankan kabakoma tɔw bɛɛ, tun bɛna Changé (a tun b’a dɔn) ka kɛ foro-la-yɔrɔ min falen bɛ mɔgɔw la, o mankan ɲagaminen ye — k’a sɔrɔ misiw ka sɔgɔsɔgɔninjɛ min bɛ yɔrɔ jan, o tun bɛna Mock Turtle ka kasi gɛlɛnw nɔ bila.

A laban na, a y’a jateminɛ a yɛrɛ kɔnɔ cogo min na nin a balimamuso fitinin kelen in bɛna kɛ, waati tɛmɛnen kɔfɛ, a yɛrɛ ye muso kɔrɔba ye; ani cogo min na a tun bɛna a to, a ka san mɔlenw bɛɛ kɔnɔ, a ka denmisɛnya dusukun nɔgɔman ni kanuya la: ani cogo min na a tun bɛna ɲɔgɔn lajɛ a den fitinin tɔw ko la, k’u ɲɛw yeelen ani k’u kɔrɔtɔ ni maana kabakoma caman ye, laala hali ni Kabako jamana sugo ye kabini tuma jan: ani a tun bɛna a yɛrɛ sɔrɔ cogo min na n’u ka dusukasi nɔgɔmanw bɛɛ ye, ka nisɔndiya sɔrɔ u ka nisɔndiya nɔgɔmanw bɛɛ la, k’a hakili to a yɛrɛ den-ɲɛnamaya la, ani samiyɛ don nisɔndiyalenw na.
