THEK IX.
Lembe pa Mock Turtle

"Icopo paru ngo kite ma cwinya yom kudu ni neno in kendu, in ngati muti ma amaro!" Duchess uwacu, ma eketho cinge i cing Alice, man giwotho karacelu.

Alice ubedu kud anyonga lee dit ni nwange ku kura maber, man eparu i iye kende nya nyalo bedu nya eca kende re ma umiye ngati ma rac ikare ma girombo i ot tedo.

"Kinde ma abedo Duchess," ewacu ire gire, (ku dwand ma gen ungo), "Abi bedu ngo ku pepper i ot tedo para. Sup timo maber manangu epe—Nyo bedu pepper re ma weko dhanu bedo ku ngei cwiny," emediri, kud anyonga madit ni nwangu cik manyen, "man vine ma weko gibedo camile ma rac —man—man barley-sugar man piny ma kumeno ma weko awiya bedo ma leng.

Egam ewil wil pa Duchess i kare eni, man ebedo ku lworo nyanok kinde ma ewinjo dwond pare ceng ku ithe. "Ibeparu pi piny moko, wadi, man eno weko wiya wil ni wecu. Acopo ngo ni wecu iri kawoni ango ma pire tek, ento abipoi i ngeye nyanok."

"Nyo epe," Alice uwacu.

“Tut, tut, nyathin!” Duchess uwacu. “Gin moko ci utiye ku kura maber, tek icopo nwangu.” Man edham ucungo inget Alice ma ebedu kaweco.

Alice emaru ngo nibedo ceng kude: mir acel, kum Duchess ubedu marac akeca ; man mir aryo, kum ebedo ku bor maber mi ketho cinge iwi goke pa Alice, man ebedo cinge ma bit ma ber ongo. Ento, emaru ngo bedo ku kura ma rac, ci eyiyu kudu maber akeca.

"Tugo ubetimere maber kawoni," ewacu, ku yore mi medo wec manok.

"'Eni," Duchess uwacu: "man kura pa lembe eni utiye—'Oh, 'e mar, 'etiye mar, ma weko ng'om lokore!'"

"Ngatu moko uwacu," Alice uwacu, "nya jutimo ku dhanu ceke ma timo lembe migi gigi!"

"Ah, ber! The lembe marom," Duchess uwacu, ma eturo cinge ma nyap i kor Alice ma emedo, "man lembe ma pire tek utiye —'Gwok ikum lembe, man dwand bi gwoko gire.'"

"Emaru nenedi ni nwangu kura maber i piny!" Alice uparu i iye kende.

"Acopo wacu niya ibeparu pirango ma aketho ngo cinga iwi kore peri," Duchess uwacu ingey ling: "the lembe utiye, nia atiye ku jiji ikum ngei pa flamingo peri. Acopo temo timo ne?"

"Ecopu camu," Alice udwoko ku lworo, ma ebedo ngo ku lworo mi temo timo ne.

"Lemandha," Duchess uwacu: "flamingoes ku mustard zo gicamu. Man lembe ma pire tek utiye—'Winy ma rangi migi rom gicokiri karacelu.'"

"Mustard kende epe winyo," Alice uwacu.

"Eyo, calu ma nja," Duchess uwacu: "yore maleng peri mi ketho piny!"

"Ebedo kidi, aparu ," Alice uwacu.

"Eyo etiye," Duchess uwacu, ma nen calu etiye ayika ni yio lembe ceke ma Alice uwacu; "nitie cula madit mi mustard kago. Man lembe ma pire tek utiye—'Ka piny para utiye madwong, piny peri de nok.'"

“Oh, angeyo!” Alice uwacu, ma ugam uwinjo ngo lembe maeni mir ajiki, "etiye dek macol. Nen ungo calu acel, ento etiye."

"Ayie ku lembe peri," Duchess uwacu; "man lembe ma pire tek utiye—'Bed ango ma ineno ni ibedo'--nyo kani imito juketh yot-'Kud ipar nia ibedo ngo ngati mange ma nen ni dhanu mange nia gin ma ibedo nyo ma icopo bedu epe ni ango ma nen ni ibedo ngo.'"

"Aparu niya acopo nyang maber," Alice uwacu ku mwolo, "ka nwango kyewo piny: ento acopo lubo ngo kite ma iwacu kudu."

"Eni epe ikum gin ma acopo wacu kan ayero," Duchess udwoko, ku dwal ma kud anyonga.

Alice uwacu kumae: “Lembe kud ibed ku lworo mi weco pire pi kare malac.”

“Oh, kud iwec ikum peko!” Duchess uwacu. “Amiyu iri mic pa lembe ceke ma awacu podi.”

“Mic ma yot!” Alice uparu. “Cwinya yom ni gimio ngo mic mi ceng' mi nyol ma kumeno!” Ento egam eyiyu ngo ni wacu ku dwal madit.

“Iparu kendu?” Duchess upenju, ku cinge mange ma nyap.

"Atiye ku twero mi paru," Alice uwacu matek, kum ecaku nwangu lworo manok.

"Eyo," Duchess uwacu, "calu opego umitu gituk; man m—"

Ento keni, pi lworo madit pa Alice, dwal pa Duchess utho, kadi ikind wec pare ma emaru 'moral,' man cinge ma ucungo i cinge ucaku myel. Alice uneno malu, man Queen ucungu inyim gi, ku cinge ma ucungo, ma wange ucungo calu koth.

“Nindu maber, Rwoth peri!” Duchess ucaku ku dwal ma piny man ma goro.

"Kawoni, amiyi iri poyo wic," owacu Queen, ma ucungo iwi ngom ma ebeweco; "Nyoke in nyo wiyi ucikere ni bedu woko, man eno i saa manok kende! Keth yer peri!"

Duchess ugamu yero pare, man ecidho pio pio.

"Wek wamedara ku tuko," Queen uwacu ni Alice; man Alice lworo unyai lee dit ma ecopo yero ngo lembe moko, ento elubo tokgi moth moth i kabedo mi tuko croquet.

Welo mange ugam utingo kare pa bedo pa Queen, man gibedu ka yom i tipo: ento, ikare ma gineno, gidok cen i tuko, Queen uwacu kende kende nia bedo moth nyanok biweko kwo migi.

Saa ceke ma gibedu ka tugu, Queen uweko ngo nyayu lwiny ku ju mukende, man ekok kumae "Wek ku wiye!" nyo "Wek ku wiye!" Ju ma en upoko lembe ikumgi ju maku gi ku askari, ma umitu giwek bedo arches pi timo eni, kara i thum pa nusu sawa acel nyo ma kadhu arches moko udong ungo, man jurutugo ceke, muweko Rwoth, Daker, man Alice, gibedo i mabusi man jupoko lembe ikum gi.

I ngeye, rwoth uweko, ma eweko yweyo, man ewacu ni Alice, "Ilar ineno Mock Turtle podi?"

"Ungo," Alice uwacu. “Angeyo ngo ango ma Mock Turtle utiye.”

"Eni piny ma Mock Turtle Soup juyike kudu," Queen uwacu.

"Aneno ngo acel de, nyo awinjo ngo pi acel de," Alice uwacu.

"Bin, dong," ewacu Queen, "man ebiwecu iri lembe pare mukadho."

Kinde ma giwotho karacelu, Alice uwinjo rwoth uwacu ku dwal ma piny, ni dhanu ceke, "Juweko dubo peri zo." "Bi, eni piny maber!" ewacu ire gire, pi en ebedo kud anyonga lee dit pi wel pa nek ma Queen ungolo.

Gi wok nyathotho ikum Gryphon, ma enindu i ceng. (Ka ingeyo ngo ango ma Gryphon utiye, nen cal.) “Aa malo, piny ma jamutoro!” Rwoth uwacu, "man tero nyaku eni ni neno Mock Turtle, man ni winjo lembe pare. Umitu adok cen anen ingey tho moko ma angolo;" man ecidho, eweko Alice kende ku Gryphon. Alice umaru ngo kite ma lei ne nen kudu, ento i rwom pare zoo eparu niya ecopo bedu ber ni bedo kude calu ni lubo tok dhaku ma rac ne: ci ekuru.

Gryphon ubedo malu man eyubu wange: man eneno Queen nitundu ewok cen kud i wange: egam enyayu ngyeru. “Anyonga nenedi!” Gryphon uwacu, nusu ire gire, nusu ni Alice.

“Anyonga utiye ango?” Alice uwacu.

"Pirango, en ," Gryphon uwacu. "Ebedo paru pare kende, nia: ginego ngo ngati moko ci, ingeyo. Bi!"

Dhanu ceke wacu 'bin!' kany," Alice uparu, ma ecidho moth i ngeye: "Abedo ngo ku cik ma kumeno i kwo para zo, kadi!"

Gicidho bor ungo mapodi gineno Mock Turtle ku bor, mubedu ku can man kende iwi got moko, man, kani gibino ceng, Alice winje ka ywak calu adunde pare ubetur. Ebedo ku kisa lee i kume. “Cwic pare utiye ango?” epenju Gryphon, man Gryphon udwoko, ku wec marom ku macon, "Eni paru pare zo, niya: epe ku can, ingeyo. Bi!"

E gicidho ibang Mock Turtle, ma eneno gi ku wange malac ma upong ku ywak, ento ewacu ngo lembe moko.

"Nyaku ma nyathin eni," Gryphon uwacu, "emito ngeyo lembe peri mukadho, emito."

"Abiwecu ire," Mock Turtle uwacu ku dwal ma thuc man ma oling: "bed piny, wun ario zoo, man kud wuwec lembe moko nitundo athum."

Ci gibedo piny, man ngati moko uweco pi dakika moko. Alice uparu i iye gire kumae, “Aneno ngo kite ma ecopo daru ko, tek ecaku ngo.” Ento ekuru moth.

"I kare moko," Mock Turtle uwacu i ajikine, ku yuyu ma thuc, "Abedo Turtle mandha."

Wec eni ulubre ku ling mabor, ma juturo kende ku koko ma nya "Hjckrrh!" kud i Gryphon, man ywak ma tek pa Mock Turtle. Alice ubedu ka ay malu man ewacu, "Afoyo, jadit, pi lembe peri maber," ento ecopo ngo ni juku paru niya lembe mukende bino bino, ci ebedo ling man ewacu ngo.

"Kinde ma wabedu nyathin," Mock Turtle umedire i ajikine, ku mwolo, kadi nangu podi ebe ywak nyanok, "wabecidho i somo i nam. Jatel wi tic ubedo Turtle ma oti-wabed walwonge ni Tortoise-"

"Pirango ma ilwonge ni Tortoise, tek ebedo ngo acel?" Alice upenju.

"Walwonge Tortoise pi en re ma eponjo wa," Mock Turtle uwacu ku ngei: "andha ibedo ngati ma timo lembe marac!"

"Umitu ibed ku lewic pi penji ma yot ma kumeno," Gryphon umedo; man i ngeye gin ario zoo gibedo ling man gineno Alice ma jacan, ma nwango ebemito etho i ngom. I ajiki Gryphon uwacu ni Mock Turtle kumae, "Dhi, jadwong! Kud ibed iwi lembe eca ceng' zo!" man emediri ku wec maeni:

“Eyo, wacidho i somu i nam, kadi nang ibe yio ngo—”

“Awacu ngo niya atimo ngo!” Alice uturo wec pare.

"Itimo," Mock Turtle uwacu.

“Mak leb peri!” Gryphon umedo, mapodi Alice uweco kendu. Mock Turtle umediri.

"Wabedo ku somo maber loyo-i andha, wabedu ka cidho i somu kubang ceng-"

" Abedo i somu mi dieceng de," Alice uwacu; “Umitu ngo ibed kud anyonga calu lembe eni zo.”

“Ku piny mukende?” penjo Mock Turtle ku lworo manok.

"Eyo," Alice uwacu, "waponjo French man wer."

“Man lwoko?” Mock Turtle uwacu.

“Eyo ngo!” Alice uwacu ku ngei cwiny.

"Ah! dong sukul peri ubedu ngo maber mandha," Mock Turtle uwacu ku dwal mi yom cwiny madit. "Kawoni i ot mwa gibedo ku i thum pa wel piny, 'French, wer, man lwoko bongo — mukende.'"

"Icopo bedu ngo ma imito," Alice uwacu; "kwo i the nam."

“Acopo ngo ni ponjo.” Mock Turtle uwacu ku yuyu. “Atimo kende kende somo ma nja.”

“Eni ubedo ango?” Alice upenju.

"Reeling man Writhing, andha, ni caku," Mock Turtle udwoko; "man i ngeye dul matung tung mi kwan-Ambition, Distraction, Uglification, man Derision."

"Awinjo ngo pi 'Uglification,'" Alice uwacu. “Eni ango?”

Gryphon utingo tyende ario malu ku lworo. "Ango! Awinjo ngo pi timo marac!" ewacu. "Ingeyo ango ma ber, aparu?"

"Eyo," Alice uwacu ku jiji: "thelembene—ni—timo—gin moko ci—ber.”

"Ber, dong," Gryphon umediri, "ka ingeyo ngo ango ma nyayu rac, ibedo ngati ma timo lembe marac."

Alice ugam unwangu ngo amora mi penjo penji mange ikum lembene, eloko wange ibang Mock Turtle, man epenje niya "Ango mange ma umitu iponj?"

"Ber, lembe moko ubedu tiye," Mock Turtle udwoko, ma kwanu lembe iwi flappers pare, "-Mystery, macon man manyen, ku Seaography: i ngeye Drawling-Drawling-master ubedo conger-eel ma con, ma bino wang acel i yenga acel: eponjowa Drawling, Stretching, man Fainting in Coils."

“ Eni ubedo nenedi?” Alice uwacu.

"Ber, acopo nyutho iri ngo," Mock Turtle uwacu: "Atiye ngati ma tek. Man Gryphon uponjo ngo."

"Abedo ungo ku saa," Gryphon uwacu: "Acidho i Classics master, kadi. Ebedo rec ma dit, ebedo ."

"Acidho i bange ngo," Mock Turtle uwacu ku yuyu: "eponjo ngyeru man can, gibed giwacu."

"Etimo, etimo," Gryphon uwacu, ma eyubu i kare pare; man lei ario zoo umu wang gi i tyend gi.

“Man saa adi ma itimo ponji kubang ceng?” Alice uwacu, ku ngwec mi loko wi lembe.

"Sawa apar inindu mir acel," Mock Turtle uwacu: "sawa abungwen inindu mukende, man kumeno."

“Eni yub maber mandha!” Alice uwacu.

"Eni the lembe ma uketho julwongo gi ni ponji," Gryphon uwacu: "pien gi dwoko piny kubang ceng."

Eni ubedu paru manyen ni Alice, man eparu ikume nyanok mapodi eweco ngo. “Ka dong ceng mir apar wiye acel ucikere ni bedu ceng yom?”

"Eyo ubedu," Mock Turtle uwacu.

“Man itimo nenedi i nindo mir apar wi aryo?” Alice umediri ku miti.

"Eni oromo ikum ponji," Gryphon uloko ku dwal ma tek: "wac ire lembe moku ikum tuku kawoni."
