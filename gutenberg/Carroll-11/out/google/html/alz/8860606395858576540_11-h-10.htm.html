THE X. X.
Lobster Quadrille

Mock Turtle oywak matek, man etingo ngeye pa flapper acel iwi wange. Eneno Alice, man etembo wecu, ento pi dakika acel nyo aryo ywak ugam ucungo i dwal pare. "Calu nya ekadho ku cogo i dhoge," Gryphon uwacu: man ecaku tic mi ywaye man egoye i ngeye. I ajiki, Mock Turtle udwoko dwal pare, man, ku pig wange ma mol iwi wiye, emediri kendu:—

"Icopo bedu ngo ibekwo lee i the nam—" ("Abedo ngo," Alice uwacu)-"man nyalo bedu nya jular ungiyu ngo iri lobster—" (Alice ucaku wacu "Abed atemo—" ento eneno kume pio pio, man ewacu "Ungo, ungo") "-pi kumeno icopo bedu ngo ku paru moko ikum gin maber ma Lobster Quadrille utiye!"

"Ungo, andha," Alice uwacu. “Eni kit myel ango?”

"Pirango," ewacu Gryphon, "ilar icungo i dhu nam—"

“Lembe aryo!” owacu Mock Turtle. “Seals, turtles, salmon, man mukende mapol; dong, ka dong igolo jelly-fish zo kud i yo—”

" Eni tero kare moko," Gryphon uwacu.

“—icidho anyim wang aryo—”

“Ng’ato ka ng’ato nigi lobster kaka jahera!” Gryphon owacu.

"Eyo," Mock Turtle uwacu: "cidhi anyim wang aryo, keth ni juwadi—"

"-loko lobsters, man idok i rwom marom," Gryphon umediri.

"I ngeye, ingeyo," Mock Turtle umediri, "ibolo—"

“Lobsters!” Gryphon owacu, ku cinge iwi polo.

“—bor ku nam ma icopo—”

“Dwok ingey gi!” Gryphon owacu koko.

“Lok somersault i nam!” owacu Mock Turtle, ma ucungo iwi cinge.

“Lok lobsters kendu!” Gryphon owacu ku dwal pare ma malu.

"Dwog i ngom kendu, man eni kende re ma ubedo wel mir acel," Mock Turtle uwacu, ma edwoko dwond pare piny pio pio; man lei ario eno, ma ubedu ka ngwec calu piny ma wic kare eni zo, gibedo piny kendu ku can man ling, man gineno Alice.

"Umitu bedu myel maber mandha," Alice uwacu ku lworo.

"Imito inen piny moko manok?" Mock Turtle uwacu.

"Andha," Alice uwacu.

“Bi, wek watem cal mir acel!” Mock Turtle uwacu ni Gryphon. "Wacopo timo labongo lobsters, ingeyo. Ma kani ma bi wer?"

"Oh, iwero ," Gryphon uwacu. “Awilo wec ne.”

"Foyo, etiye myel maber sana mi nenu," Alice uwacu, ma cwinye yom nia edaru: "man atimo kumeno calu wer ma nyayu anyonga ikum whiting!"

"Oh, ikum whiting," Mock Turtle uwacu, "gi—ineno gi, andha?"

"Eyo," Alice uwacu, "Abedo ka neno gi kare ku kare i dinn—" eneno gire pio pio.

"Angeyo ngo kama Dinn copo bedu i iye," Mock Turtle uwacu, "ento kan ineno gi kare ku kare, andha ingeyo kite ma gitiye kudu."

"Ayiyu," Alice udwoko ku paru. “Gitiye ku yibe migi i dhoge—man gitiye iwi lum.”

"The lembe utiye," Gryphon uwacu, "nya gicidho ku rec ma julwongo ni lobsters i myel. Pieno jubologi woko i nam. Pieno gipodho yo mabor. Pieno gicoko yibe migi pio pio i dhoge. Pieno gicopo wodhu gi woko kendu ngo. Eni kende."

Alice uwacu kumae: “Afoyo, etie lembe ma ber dit. Nangu ang’eyo ngo lembe mapol iwi whiting con.”

"Acopo wacu iri lembe madwong uloyu, kan imito," Gryphon uwacu. “Ingeyo pirango ma julwongo ni whiting?”

"Aparu ngo ikum lembene," Alice uwacu. "Pir'ango?"

" Etimo wara man wara ," Gryphon udwoko ku mwolo.

Alice ubedo ku jiji ma lee. “Timu wara man wara!” edwoko kendo ku dwal mi wur.

“Pirango, wara peri jutimu ku ango?” Gryphon uwacu. "Abewecu, ango ma weko gibedo maleng?"

Alice uneno piny ikum gi, man eparu nyanok mapodi emiyu lagam pare. “Gidaru ku rangi macol, ageno.”

"Wara man wara ithe nam," Gryphon umediri ku dwal ma tek, "jutimo ku whiting. Kawoni dong ingeyo."

“Man juyike ku ango?” Alice upenju ku dwal mi mitu ngeyo lembe.

"Soles ku eels, andha," Gryphon udwoko ku lworo: "shrimp moku ci copo wacu iri lembe eni."

"Ka nangu abedo whiting," Alice uwacu, ma paru pare podi ubewothu iwi wer, "nangu awacu ni porpoise, 'Bed cen, akwayi: wamito ngo ibed kudwa!'"

"Gibedo ku twero mi bedo kugi," Mock Turtle uwacu: "rec moko mbe ma rieko ma copo cidhu kaka moko ci manangu epe ku porpoise."

“Ecopo bedu andha ngo?” Alice uwacu ku dwal mi lworo madit.

"Eyo epe," Mock Turtle uwacu: "pirango, ka rec ubinu ira , man ewacu ira nia ebecidho i wotho, awacu 'Ku porpoise ango?'"

"Ibeweco ngo 'lembe'?" Alice uwacu.

"Amito wacu ango ma awacu," Mock Turtle udwoko ku dwal ma nyayu can. Man Gryphon umedo kumae “Bi, wek wawinj lembe peri moko.”

"Acopo titu iri lembe ma anwango- nicaku tin uthieno," Alice uwacu ku lworo manok: "ento epe ku kony mi dok cen i ceng kawoni, kum nangu abedo dhanu mange i kare meno."

"Nyuth lembe eni zo," Mock Turtle uwacu.

"Ungo, ungo! Woth mir acel," Gryphon uwacu ku dwal ma ling: "wec tero kare ma lee dit."

Alice ucaku titu igi lembe ma enwang kudu nicaku ikare ma eneno White Rabbit.
