THE VII.
A Mad Tea-Party

Meza moko ubedu nuti ma juketho ithe yen i nyim ot, man March Hare ku Hatter ubedu ka madhu chai i iye: Dormouse ubedu ikind gi, onindo matek, man ju aryo mukende ubedu katiyo kudu calu kom, ubedu ka ketho cing gi iwie, man ubedu kaweco iwi wiye. "Ebedo ber ungo ni Dormouse," Alice uparu; “kende, calu ma enindo, aparu epe ku peko.”

Meza ne ubedo malac, ento dhanu adek ne gicokiri karacelu i thenge acel: "Kabedo epe! Kabedo epe!" gikok kinde ma gineno Alice ubebino. “Kabedo utiye madwong !” Alice uwacu ku ngei cwiny, man ebedo piny i kom madit ma juketho i thenge acel mi meza.

"Madh kongo," March Hare uwacu ku dwal ma cwalu cwiny.

Alice uneno iwi meza zo, ento piny moko epe iwi meza ento chai kende. "Aneno ngo kongo moko," ewacu.

"Epe," March Hare uwacu.

"Eka ebedo ngo lembe maber iri ni miyo," Alice uwacu ku ngei cwiny.

"Ebedo ngo lembe maber iri ni bedo piny manangu ju lwongi ngo," March Hare uwacu.

Alice uwacu kumae: “Ang’eyo ngo nia etie meza peri ; “juketho pi dhanu madwong makadhu adek.”

"Yik wic peri mitu jwigo," ewacu jatel yuk wic. Ebed eneno Alice pi kare moko ku miti madit mi ngeyo lembe, man eni ubedu wec pare mir acel.

"Umitu iponjri ni kud itim lembe peri giri," Alice uwacu ku tego cwiny moko; “ebedo rac mandha.”

Jagor oyabo wange malac ikare ma ewinjo lembe eni; ento lembe ma ewacu kende ubedu, "Pirango ma winyo ubedo calu kom mi kyewo?"

"Bi, wabibedo kud anyonga kawoni!" Alice uparu. "Cwinya yom ni gicaku penjo lembe ma nya wange," emedo ku dwal madit.

"Imito wacu niya iparu niya icopo nwangu lagam pa penji eni?" March Hare uwacu.

"Eyo andha," Alice uwacu.

"I ngeye umitu iwac ango ma imito wacu," March Hare umediri.

"Atimo," Alice udwoko pio pio; “at least—at least amito wacu ango ma awacu—eni lembe marom, ingeyo.”

"Epe piny marom nyanok!" ewacu jagor wic. "Icopo wacu niya 'aneno ango ma acamu' ubedo lembe marom ku 'aneno ango ma aneno'!"

"Icopo yero maber," March Hare umedo, "nya 'amaru gin ma anwango' utiye lembe marom ku 'amaru gin ma anwango'!"

"Icopo yero bende," umedo Dormouse, ma nen calu ebeweco i nindo pare, "nia 'Ayuyo ka anindu' utiye lembe marom ku 'Anindo ka anindu'!"

" Ebedo lembe marom ku in," ewacu jatel wi bongo, man keni wec ne uthum, man ju ma gibedo ling pi dakika acel, ma Alice ubeparu pi lembe ceke ma ecopu poi ikum winyo man kom mi kyewo, ma ubedu piny madwong ungo.

Hatter ubedo ngati mir acel mi turo ling. “Nindu adi mi dwi eni?” ewacu, eloko wange ibang Alice: edaru kwanyo sawa pare kud i cinge, man ebedo ka nenu ku lworo, ebe yenge kare ku kare, man ebed etingo i ithe.

Alice uparu nyanok, man ewacu "Mir angwen."

“Nindu aryo marac!” Hatter oywak. “Awecu iri nia moo copo bedu ber ungo pi tic!” emedo kun eneno March Hare ku ngei cwiny.

"Ebedo moo maber loyo ," March Hare udwoko ku mwolo.

"Eyo, ento moko moko ucikere ni bedo i iye de," Hatter uwacu: "icopo bedu ngo iket i iye ku pala mi mugati."

March Hare utingo sawa ne man enene ku can: man ebolo i kopo pare mi chai, man enene kendu: ento ecopo paru ngo lembe maber mi wacu ma sagu wec pare mir acel, "Ebedo moo maber loyo , ingeyo."

Alice ubedu ka neno iwi bege ku miti moko mi ngeyo lembe. “Saa ma nyayu ngyeru nenedi!” ewacu. “Enyutho ceng pa dwi, ​​man enyutho ngo sawa adi!”

"Pirango ma umitu etim?" Hatter uwacu. "Saa peri nyuthu iri nia oro adi?"

"Eyo epe," Alice udwoko yot yot: "ento eni pilembe ebedo oro acel pi kare malac karacelu."

"Ma en e lembe para kende," Hatter uwacu.

Alice uwinjo marac akeca, wec pa The Hatter nen calu epe ku the lembe moku cii, man podi ubedu lemandha. "Angeyo ngo lembe peri," ewacu, ku mwolo ma ecopu.

"Dormouse unindo kendu," Hatter uwacu, man ebolo chai ma lyet manok iwi ume.

Dormouse utingo wiye ku lworo, man ewacu, manangu eyabu wange ngo, "Eyo, andha; kende kende ango ma abino wacu pira kena."

“Ilar nyang ikum penji eca podi?” Hatter uwacu, eloko wange ibang Alice kendu.

"Ungo, aweko," Alice udwoko: "dwoko ango?"

"Atie ku paru moko ope," Jaradiri uwacu.

"An de ngo," March Hare uwacu.

Alice uyuyu yuyu ku olo. "Aparu niya icopo timo piny moko maber ku sawa," ewacu, "ma ka rwenyo sawa ni penjo penji ma lagam epe."

"Ka nwang ingeyo sawa maber calu an," ewacu jatel wi bongo, "icopo weco ngo ikum nyayu sawa marac . En e ."

"Angeyo ngo ango ma imito wacu," Alice uwacu.

“Eyo, ipe!” Hatter uwacu, ma etingo wiye ku lewic. "Acopo wacu niya iweco ngo ku Time!"

"Copu bedu epe," Alice udwoko ku lworo: "ento angeyo niya umitu alok kare ka abeponjo wer."

"Ah! eno nyutho," ewacu Jagor. "Ecopo cungo ngo. Kawoni, tek ibedo ku winjiri maber kude, ecopu timo piny moko ci ma imaru ku sawa. Ku lapor, ket nya eca ubedo sawa abungwen mi diewor, sawa mi caku ponji: icopo weco kende kende ikum sawa, man sawa ucidhi nyathotho- nusu- nusu pi cam mi diewor acel!"

("Amito kende nya ebed," March Hare uwacu ire gire ku dwand ma yot.)

"Eni copo bedu ber, andha," Alice uwacu ku paru: "ento i ngeye—omyero ngo abed ku kec pi lembene, ingeyo."

"I acaki epe," jagor owacu: "ento icopo gwoko ni saa acel ku nusu pi kare ma imito."

"Eni yore ma itiyo kudu?" Alice upenju.

Jagor utingo wiye ku can. “An ungo!” edwoko. "Wabedo ka pyem i dwi mir adek mukadho- ma podi edaru ngo, ingeyo-" (enyutho ku kijiko pare mi chai i March Hare,) "-ubedo i wer madit ma Queen of Hearts umio, man umitu awer

'Twinkle, twinkle, bat ma nok!
Abeparu ikum ango ma itiye!'

Ingeyo wer ne, nyo?”

"Awinjo lembe moko ma kumeno," Alice uwacu.

"Emediri, ingeyo," Hatter umediri, "i yo maeni:—

'I wi ng'om ibe ngwec,
Calu chai-tray i polo.
Twinkle, twinkle—'”

Kany Dormouse utingo kume gire, man ucaku wer i nindu pare " Twinkle, twinkle, twinkle, twinkle —" man umedire pi kare malac ma umitu gi juku cinge kara ejuk.

"Ber, nangu adaru ngo kyewo wer mir acel," jagor owacu, "ka Queen ucungu malu man ukok kumae, 'Ebe nego sawa! Wek wiye!'"

“Kit ma rac mandha!” Alice uwacu.

"Man nicaku eca," Hatter umediri ku dwal mi can, "ebitimo ngo piny moko ma apenje! Saa abusiel kawoni utiye."

Paru moko maber wok i wic pa Alice. "Eni the lembe ma uketho ju ketho piny madwong mi chai woko kany?" epenju.

"Eyo, eni kende," Jaradiri uwacu ku yuyu: "sawa mi madhu chai nja, man wabedo ungo ku saa mi lwoko piny ikind sawa moko."

“Idong imediri kawothu, aparu?” Alice uwacu.

"Eyo andha," Jagor owacu: "calu piny ubetimere."

"Ento ango ma timere ka idok i acaki kendu?" Alice ugam upenjo.

"Ka waparu niya waloko wi lembe," March Hare uturo wec pare, ku yeng. "Aol ku lem eni. Abolo kwir nyaku ma nyathin ni tito iwa lembe moko."

"Abedo ku lworo nia angeyo acel de ngo," Alice uwacu, ma ebedo ku lworo ikum paru ne.

"I ngeye Dormouse bibedo!" gin ario zoo gi ywak. "Acer, Dormouse!" Man gi goye ku thenge ario zoo wang acel.

Dormouse oyabo wange moth moth. "Abedo ka nindu ngo," ewacu ku dwal ma nyap man ma nyap: "Awinjo lembe ceke ma wubeweco."

“Wec iwa lembe moko!” March Hare uwacu.

“Eyo, akwayi itim!” Alice ukwayu.

"Man bed pio pio," umedo jatel wi bongo, "kadi ibi nindo kendu ma podi ethum ungo."

"I kare moko, nyamego adek ma thindho ubedu nuti," Dormouse ucaku ku ngwec madit; "man nying gi ubedo Elsie, Lacie, man Tillie; man gikwo i the pii—"

"Gikwo ku ango?" Alice uwacu, ma kare ku kare ebedo ku miti madit ikum penji mi camu man madhu.

"Gikwo iwi treacle," Dormouse uwacu, ingey paru pi dakika acel nyo aryo.

"Gicopo timo ngo eno, ingeyo," Alice uwacu moth moth; "gicopo bedu ju ma two."

"Eyo gibedo," Dormouse uwacu; “ jaratwo mandha .”

Alice utemo paru kudu gire kite ma kwo maber eca bibedo kudu, ento eketho ebedo ku jiji lee, ci emediri kumae: "Ento pirango ma gikwo i the pii?"

"Madh chai moko," March Hare uwacu ni Alice, ku cwiny ma tek.

"Abedo ungo ku piny moko podi," Alice udwoko ku dwal ma nyayu can, "pi meno acopu tingo piny mukende ngo."

"Imito wacu niya icopo tingo piny manok ungo ," Jagor owacu: "e yot mandha ni tingo piny madwong uloyu ma epe."

"Ngatu moko ope ma upenju paru peri ," Alice uwacu.

“Nga ma timo lembe pare gire kawoni?” Hatter upenju ku tego cwiny.

Alice ung'eyo ngo ango ma umitu ewacu ikum lembe eni: egam emadhu chai man mugati-ku-moo, man eloko wange ibang Dormouse, man edwoko penji pare kendu. "Pirango ma gikwo i the pii?"

Dormouse utingo dakika acel nyo aryo mi paru ikume, man ewacu, "Ebedo treacle-well."

“Lembe ma kumeno epe!” Alice ucaku ku ngei madit, ento Hatter ku March Hare giwacu "Sh! sh!" man Dormouse uwacu ku ngei cwiny kumae, “Ka icopo bedo ngo ku kura maber, ber ka idaru lembe peri giri.”

“Ungo, akwayi mediri!” Alice uwacu ku mwolo; "Abi turo wec kendu ngo. Acopo wacu nya ecopu bedu acel ."

“Acel, andha!” Dormouse uwacu ku ngei cwiny. Ento, eyio ni mediri. "Man nyamego adek eni—gibeponjo goyo cal, ingeyo—"

“Gi goyo cal ango?” Alice uwacu, ma wiye wil ku lembangola pare.

"Treacle," Dormouse uwacu, manangu eparu ngo pi kare eni.

"Amitu kopo maleng," Hatter ugam uturo: "wacidhi kama acel."

Ecidho anyim ma ebeweco, man Dormouse ulubo toke: March Hare ucidho i kabedo pa Dormouse, man Alice ugam utingo kabedo pa March Hare. Hatter ubedo ngati acel kende ma unwangu bero kud i aloka loka ne: man Alice ubedu marac uloyu ma sagu macon, calu March Hare ubedu ka ketho jamb cak i sahan pare.

Alice umitu ngo nyayu can i Dormouse kendu, ci ecaku ku lworo: "Ento angeyu ngo. Giwodho treacle kud kama kani?"

"Icopo kwanyo pii kud i vur mi pii," ewacu jatel wi bongo; "dong aparu niya icopo goyo treacle kud i treacle-well-eh, ming?"

"Ento gibedu i vur," Alice uwacu ni Dormouse, ma eyiyu ngo ni neno lembe ma tok cen.

"Eyo, gibedu," Dormouse uwacu; “—ber i iye.”

Lagam eni uketho Alice ma jacan ung'eyo ngo, uketho eweko Dormouse umediri pi kare moko manangu engeyo ngo.

"Gibedu ka ponjo goyo cal," Dormouse umediri, ma eyubu man eyubu wange, kum ebedo ka nindu lee; "man gi goyo piny matung tung- piny ceke ma caku ku M-"

"Pirango ku M?" Alice uwacu.

"Pir'ango ngo?" March Hare uwacu.

Alice ubedo moth.

Dormouse ugam umu wange i kare eni, man ubedu ka cidho i nindu; ento, kinde ma jayub moko utinge, ecer kendu ku koko manok, man ewacu: "-ma caku ku M, calu mouse-traps, man dwe, man poyo wic, man muchness-ingeyo nia iwacu nia piny utiye "much of a muchness"-ibedo ka neno piny ma kumeno calu cal pa muchness?"

"Andha, kawoni ipenja," Alice uwacu, ma wic pare unyai, "Aparu ngo—"

"Ka kumeno, umitu kud iwec," jaradiri uwacu.

Lembe ma rac eni ubedo madwong ma Alice copo yio ngo: e ay malu ku ngei madit, man ecidho; Dormouse ne onindo pio pio, man ngati moko mbe m'ikind ju mukende ma ungiyu ikum wotho pare, kadi nang eneno ngeye wang acel nyo aryo, ku gen ma nusu nia gibilwonge: kare ma tokcen ma eneno gi, gibedu ka tembo ni ketho Dormouse i teapot.

“I yore moku ci, abecidho keca kendu ngo!” Alice uwacu ma enyayu yo pare ikind yen. "Eni ubedo foc mi chai ma rac uloyu ma abedo i iye i kwo para zo!"

Kinde ma ewacu lembe eni, eneno nya yen acel utiye ku dhugola ma cidho i iye. “Eni lembe ma nyayu anyonga lee!” eparu. "Ento piny ceke utiye ma nyayu anyonga tin. Aparu niya acopo cidho i iye kawoni." Man emondo.

Egam enwange kendu i ot mabor, man ceng ku meza ma nok mi gilasi. "Kawoni, abitimo maber i saa maeni," ewacu ire gire, man ecaku ku maku cwiyu ma nok mi mola ma kwar, man eyabu dhugola ma tero i podho. I ngeye ecidho i tic ka camo lum (ebedo ku dul acel i cinge) nitundo ma edoko futi acel: edok ewotho piny i yo ma nok: man i ngeye —enwange i podho maber, ikind lum ma leng man pii ma ngic.
