THEK III.
Caucus-Race man lembe mabor

Gibedu dul pa dhanu ma nen ungo ma gicokiri i dhu nam—winy ku yuk ma juku, lei ku yuk migi ma ucungo ikum gi, man zo ubedu ka mol pii, ucungo, man ubedu maber ongo.

Penji mir acel ubedu, kite mi dwoko piny ma muthwo kendu: gibedo ku wec ikum lem eni, man ingey dakika manok nen ni eber ni Alice ni nwangu nia ebeweco kudgi maber, calu nangu engeyo gi kwo pare zo. Andha, ebedo ku laro lembe ma lee ku Lory, ma i ajikine edoko ngati ma rac, man ewacu kende kumae, "Atiye dit muloyi, man umitu angey maber;" man eni Alice copo yio ngo manangu engeyo ngo oro pare, man, calu Lory okwero tito oro pare, epe lembe mange ma jucopo wacu.

I ajiki, Oyo, ma nen calu ngati ma pire tek ikind gi, olwongo kumae, "Bed piny, wun ceke, man wuwinj ira! Abiketho wu muth ma romo!" Gi zo gibedo piny acel, i dul madit, ku oyo i dyere. Alice uketho wange ikume ku lworo, pi en engeyo nia ecopo nwangu two ma rac tek ebedo ngo muthwo pio pio.

“Ahem!” Mouse uwacu ku yamo ma pire tek, "wube ayika? Eni ubedo piny ma muth uloyu ma angeyo. Bed ling, kani ibemito! 'William the Conqueror, ma lembe pare ubedu ber pa papa, ugam ujolo cinge pio pio ni ju England, ma ubedu ka mito judongo, man ubedu ku rwom ma malu mi kwalu man loyo ngom, Edwin man Morbriciam, man North'"

“Ugh!” Lory uwacu, ku lworo.

"Akwayi kisa peri!" Mouse uwacu, ku wange ma nyai, ento ku mwolo: "Iweco?"

“An ungo!” Lory uwacu nyathotho.

"Aparu niya itimo," Mouse uwacu. “—Amediri. 'Edwin ku Morcar, earls pa Mercia ku Northumbria, giwacu pire: man kadi Stigand, jatel wi kanisa mi Canterbury, unwangu niya ber—'”

“Inwango ango ?” Duck uwacu.

" Anwange ," Mouse udwoko ku lworo: "andha ingeyo ango ma 'en' thee."

"Angeyo ango ma 'e' thee maber, ka anwango piny moko," Duck uwacu: "ebedo ungwal nyo thwol. Penji utiye, archbishop unwangu ango?"

Oyo ungiyo ngo penji eni, ento ewacu nyathotho, "'—anwangu niya ber ni cidho ku Edgar Atheling ni rombu ku William man ni mio ire rwoth. Kura pa William mir acel ubedu maber. Ento timo pa Normans pare—' Ibekwo nenedi kawoni, wadi?" emediri, eloko wange ibang Alice ma ebeweco.

"Macalu ma nja," Alice uwacu ku dwal mi can: "enen ni epe ku pii kadi nyanok."

"I lembe ma kumeno," Dodo uwacu ku cwiny ma tek, ma ucungu iwi tyende, "akwayu nia rombo ne uthum, pi nwangu yore mi cobo peko ma tek-"

“Wec dhok mi ingresa!” Eaglet uwacu. “Angeyo ngo the lembe pa nusu pa wec mabor eni, man, ango ma pire tek uloyu, angeyo ngo nya in de ingeyo!” Man Eaglet ugoyo wiye piny pi kano vunju: moko pa winy mukende gi koko matek.

"Lembe ma abino wacu," Dodo uwacu ku dwal ma nyayu can, "ubedo, nia piny maber uloyu ma copo ketho wabedo ku lworo ubedo ngwec mi Caucus."

“Caucus-race utiye ango?” Alice uwacu; epe nya emito ngeyo lembe madwong, ento Dodo ubedo moth calu nya eparu niya ngati moku umitu ewec, man ngati moko ope ma nen ni emito ewacu lembe moko.

"Pirango," Dodo uwacu, "yo maber loyo mi gonyo lembe eni utiye ni timu." (Man, calu ma icopo temo giri, ceng moko mi ngico, abewecu iri kite ma Dodo utimo kudu.)

Mir acel eketho lanyuth pa ka ngwec, i dul moko, ("kit ma nen kudu pire tek ungo," ewacu,) man i ngeye ju ketho dhanu ceke i ka ngwec, keca ku keca. "Acel, aryo, adek, man cen" ubedu pee, ento gicaku ringo kani ma gimaru, man giweko kani ma gimaru, kara ubedu yot ungo ni ngeyo ka ngwec uthum. Ento, kinde ma gibedu ka ringo pi nusu sawa acel nyo ma kadhu, man gibedu muthwo kendu, Dodo ugam ulwongo nya "Ng'wec uthum!" man gi zo gicokiri i ngete, ma giywak, man gipenjo kumae, “Ento nga ma uloyo?”

Penji eni Dodo copo gamu ngo labongo paru madwong, man ebedo pi kare malac ku cinge acel ucungo iwi wiye (kabedo ma inenu Shakespeare, i cal pare), ma ju mukende gibedo kakuru ling. I ajiki Dodo uwacu, " Dhanu ceke udaru loyo, man dhanu ceke umaku gibed ku mic."

"Ento nga ma bi mio foc?" dwand madwong upenju.

"Pirango, en , andha," Dodo uwacu, ma enyutho Alice ku cinge acel; man dul pa dhanu ceke gicokiri ikume, gilwongo i yo ma nyang ikumgi tek, "Mic! Mic!"

Alice ung'eyo ngo ango ma umitu etim, man ku lworo eketho cinge i cinge, man ewodho sanduku mi comfits, (ku berocwiny pii ma kadu umondo ngo i iye), man emio gi calu mic. Ubedo ku acel kende a-piece, zoo.

"Ento umitu ebed ku foc en gire, ingeyo," Mouse uwacu.

"Eyo," Dodo udwoko ku lworo. “Ango mange ma itiye kudu i cingi?” emediri, eloko wange ibang Alice.

"Thimble kende," Alice uwacu ku can.

"Miye kany," Dodo uwacu.

I ngeye gi zo gicokiri kendu kendu, ma Dodo unyutho thimble ku woro, ewacu ni "Wakwayi yio thimble maber eni;" man, kinde ma edaru wec ma nok eni, gi zo gi mor.

Alice upenu nia lembe eca zo ubedu lembe ma rwinyo, ento lembe eca zo nen ni piny ma rac ma ecopo ngo ni ngiero; man, calu ma ecopo paru ngo pi lembe moku ma ewacu, edwoko piny kende, man etingo cinge, nen calu ngati ma cwinye tek.

Piny mukende ubedu camu comfits: eni ukelo koko man lworo, calu winy madongo ubedu ka koko nia gicopo ngo ni camu wil migi, man winy ma nok ugam ucungo man umitu ju goy gi i ngeye. Ento, edaru, man gibedo piny kendu i ring, man gikwayo Mouse ni ewacu igi lembe mange.

"Ikwongiri ni wacu ira lembe peri mukadho, ingeyo," Alice uwacu, "man pirango ma idagu—C ku D," emedu ku dwal ma yot, lworo nya ecopo nyayu can i iye kendu.

“M'a ubedo lembe mabor man ma nyayu can!” Mouse uwacu, eloko wange ibang Alice, man eywak.

" Ebedo yibe mabor, andha," Alice uwacu, ma eneno piny ku wur ikum yibe pa oyo; "ento pirango ma ilwongo ni can?" Man emediri ku paru ikum lembene podi Mouse ubeweco, uketho paru pare ikum lembe ne ubedo calu maeni:—

"Ibecidho ngo!" Mouse uwacu ni Alice matek. “Ibeparu ango?”

"Akwayi kisa peri," Alice uwacu ku mwolo: "ibedo i dhugola mir abic, aparu?"

"Abed atimo ngo! " Mouse owacu, matek man ku ngei madit.

“A knot!” Alice uwacu, kare ku kare etiye atera mi bedo ber, man enen ku lworo ikume. “Oh, wek akonyi ni dwoko piny!”

"Abitimo ngo piny moko ma kumeno," Mouse uwacu, ucungu man ecidho. "Ibecayu ira ku wecu lembe ma rwinyo!"

“Abed amito ngo!” Alice ma jacan ukwayu. “Ento ibedo ku lworo nyathotho, ingeyo!”

Oyo ugam ugoy kende kende.

"Akwayi idok kendo man ithum lembe peri!" Alice olwongo ingeye; man ju mukende gicokiri i wer, "Eyo, akwayi itim!" ento oyo utingo wiye kende ku lworo, man ewotho pio pio nyanok.

“Lembe marac nia ecopo bedo ngo!” Lory oywak, ikare ma edaru nen ungo; man Crab moko ma jadit ugamu kare mi wacu ni nyare "Ah, nyamira! Wek eni obed ponji iri kara kud iwek ngei peri !" "Mak leb peri, Ma!" Crab ma nyathin uwacu, ku lworo manok. “Iromo temo bedo moth pa oyster!”

"Amito abed ku Dinah mwa keni, angeyo atiye!" Alice uwacu ku dwal ma tek, ma eweco ngo ku ngati moko kende. “Ebi dwoko cen pio pio!”

"Man Dinah ubedo ngati makani, tek acopo penjo penji ne?" Lory uwacu.

Alice udwoko ku cwiny ma tek, pi en ebedo ayika ni weco ikum lei pare: "Dinah ubedo pusi mwa. Man etiye pusi ma pire tek pi mako oyo ma icopo paru ngo! Man oh, amito nya ibed inene ingey winy! Pirango, ecopo camu winy matidi kani inene!"

Wec eni ukelo lworo madit ikind ju dul ne. Winy moko udham wok pio pio: Magpie acel ma dit ucaku umu kume maber, ewacu kumae, "Amito adok i pacu; yamu mi diewor uromo ngo ku dhoga!" man Canary olwongo ku dwal ma yeng yeng ni awiya pare, "Wubin cen, jumara! Saa dong uromo ma wubedo i kavutu!" I kum lembe matung tung, gi zo gi ay kud, man Alice udong kende kende.

“Amito nya nangu aloko ngo pi Dinah!” ewacu ire gire ku dwal mi can. "Ngatu moko ope ma maru en, piny kany, man angeyu niya etiye pusi maber loyo i ng'om! Oh, Dinah ma amaro! Aparu ka acopo nenu kendu!" Man keni Alice ma jacan ucaku ywak kendu, kum ewinjo nia etiye kende man cwinye lith. Ikind kare manok, kadi kudu, ewinjo kendu dwand tyend dhanu mabor, man eneno malo ku miti madit, ku genogen nya oyo uloko paru pare, man ebedu ka dwogo pi daru wec pare.
