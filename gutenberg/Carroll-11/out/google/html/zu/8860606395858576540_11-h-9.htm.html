ISAHLUKO IX.
Indaba YoFudu Oluhlekisayo

“Awucabangi ukuthi ngijabule kanjani ukuphinde ngiphinde ngikubone, sithandwa sami!” Kusho umaNdlovu ehlohla ingalo yakhe ngothando ku-Alice, bahambe bobabili.

Wajabula kakhulu u-Alice emuthola esenomoya omnandi kangaka, wazitshela ukuthi mhlawumbe upelepele kuphela omenze waba nonya uma behlangana ekhishini.

“Lapho nginguDuchess ,” ekhuluma yedwa, (nokho, hhayi ngezwi elibonisa ithemba kakhulu), “Ngeke ngibe nopelepele ekhishini lami nhlobo . Isobho lenza kahle kakhulu ngaphandle—Mhlawumbe uhlala upelepele owenza abantu bathukuthele,” waqhubeka, ejabule kakhulu ngokuthola uhlobo olusha lomthetho, “noviniga obenza babe muncu—kanye nebhali—kanye nezinto ezifana nebhali—kanye nekamoyidi. ngifisa nje ukuthi abantu bazi lokho : khona-ke bebengeke bancisheke ngakho, uyazi—”

Ubesekhohliwe impela umaDushe ngalesi sikhathi, wethuka kancane lapho ezwa izwi lakhe eduze kwendlebe yakhe. “Kunento oyicabangayo sthandwa sami, futhi lokho kukwenza ukhohlwe ukukhuluma. Ngeke ngikwazi ukukutshela njengamanje ukuthi yini isimilo salokho, kodwa ngizokukhumbula kancane.”

“Mhlawumbe ayinayo,” kusho u-Alice.

"Hawu, ntombi, ngane!" Kusho umaNdlovu. "Yonke into inokuziphatha, uma nje ungayithola." Futhi wazicindezela eduze kuka-Alice njengoba ekhuluma.

U-Alice akathandanga kakhulu ukusondelana naye: okokuqala, ngoba amaDuchess ayemubi kakhulu ; futhi okwesibili, ngoba wayengobude obufanele ncamashi ukuze abeke isilevu sakhe phezu kwehlombe lika-Alice, futhi kwakuyisilevu esibukhali ngendlela engathandeki. Nokho, akakuthandanga ukuba luhlaza, ngakho wabekezelela ngokusemandleni akhe.

“Umdlalo uqhubeka kangcono manje,” kusho yena, ngendlela yokuqhubeka nengxoxo kancane.

"'Kunjalo," kusho abakwaDuches: "futhi isimilo salokho ngu-'O,' uthando, 'luthando, olwenza umhlaba uzungeze!'"

“Umuntu othile uthe,” kunyenyeza u-Alice, “ukuthi lokhu kwenziwa yiwo wonke umuntu unake ezakhe izindaba!”

"Hhayi-ke! Kusho into efanayo," kusho abakwaDuchess, bemba isilevu sakhe esincane esibukhali ehlombe lika-Alice njengoba enezela, "futhi isimilo salokho ukuthi —'Nakekela umqondo, futhi imisindo izozinakekela ngokwayo.'

“Yeka indlela akuthanda ngayo ukuthola ukuziphatha okuhle ezintweni!” U-Alice ecabanga.

"Ngike ngithi uyazibuza ukuthi kungani ngingabeki ingalo yami okhalweni lwakho," kusho abakwaDuchess ngemuva kokuthula isikhashana: "Isizathu siwukuthi, ngiyangabaza ulaka lweflamingo yakho. Ngizame ukuhlolwa?"

“Angase alume,” kuphendula u-Alice ngokuqapha, engafuni nakancane ukuthi kuzanywe ukuhlola.

"Kuyiqiniso impela," kusho abakwaDuchess: "ama-flamingo kanye nesinaphi kokubili kuluma. Futhi isimilo salokho ukuthi-'Izinyoni zophaphe zibuthana ndawonye.'

“Isinaphi kuphela okungeyona inyoni,” kuphawula u-Alice.

"Kulungile, njengenjwayelo," kusho abakwaDuchess, "yeka indlela ecacile onayo yokubeka izinto!"

“Kuyiminerali, ngicabanga ,” kusho u-Alice.

"Impela kunjalo," kusho abakwaDuchess, obebonakala bekulungele ukuvuma konke okushiwo u-Alice; “kukhona imayini enkulu yesinaphi eduze, futhi isimilo salokho sithi—'Uma kuba nokwami kuningi, kuyancipha okwakho.'

“O, ngiyazi!” kubabaza u-Alice, owayengazange akulalele lokhu okushiwo okokugcina, “kuyimifino.

"Ngivumelana nawe impela," kusho uDuchess; “Futhi isimilo salokho siwukuthi—‘Yiba yilokho obungase ubonakale uyikho’—noma uma ungathanda kube lula nakakhulu—‘Ungalokothi uzicabange ungehlukile kunalokho okungase kubonakale kwabanye ukuthi lokho owawuyikho noma owawuyikho kwakungenjalo kunalokho owawuyikho kwakuyobonakala kubo njengokuhlukile.’”

"Ngicabanga ukuthi kufanele ngikuqonde kangcono lokho," kusho u-Alice ngesizotha, "uma bengiyibhale phansi: kodwa ngeke ngikwazi ukuyilandela njengoba usho."

"Akukho lutho engingakusho uma ngikhetha," kuphendula abakwaDuchess, ngezwi elijabulisayo.

“Thandaza ungazihluphi ngokukusho isikhathi eside kunalokho,” kusho u-Alice.

“O, ungakhulumi ngezinkinga!” Kusho umaNdlovu. “Ngikwenzela isipho sakho konke engikushilo okwamanje.”

“Isipho esishibhile!” kucabanga u-Alice. “Ngiyajabula ukuthi abazinikezi izipho zosuku lokuzalwa kanjalo!” Kodwa akazange azame ukukusho ngokuzwakalayo.

“Uyacabanga futhi?” Kubuza umaDuchess ephinde emba isilevu sakhe esincane esicijile.

“Nginelungelo lokucabanga,” kusho u-Alice ngesankahlu, ngoba wayeseqala ukukhathazeka kancane.

“Cishe kulungile,” kusho abakwaDuchess, “njengoba izingulube kufanele zindiza; kanye ne-m—”

Kodwa lapha, u-Alice wamangala kakhulu, izwi likaDuchess lafa, ngisho naphakathi kwegama lakhe alithandayo elithi 'moral,' futhi ingalo eyayixhunywe kweyakhe yaqala ukuvevezela. U-Alice wabheka phezulu, nangu uNdlovukazi emi phambi kwabo, esonge izandla, ehwaqabala kuhle kokuduma kwezulu.

“Usuku oluhle, Nkosi!” aqale phansi umaNdlovu ngezwi eliphansi.

“Manje, ngikunikeza isexwayiso esifanele,” kumemeza iNdlovukazi, igxoba phansi lapho ikhuluma; "Kuphakathi kwakho noma ikhanda lakho kufanele livaliwe, futhi lokho cishe ingxenye yesikhathi! Thatha ukukhetha kwakho!"

U-Duchess wathatha ukukhetha kwakhe, futhi wahamba ngomzuzwana.

“Asiqhubeke nomdlalo,” kusho iNdlovukazi ku-Alice; futhi u-Alice wayethuke kakhulu ukuthi angasho izwi, kodwa wamlandela kancane ebuyela endaweni yezingwenya.

Ezinye izimenywa zazisebenzise ithuba lokungabikho kweNdlovukazi, futhi zaziphumule emthunzini: nokho, ngesikhathi ziyibona, zaphuthuma zibuyela emdlalweni, iNdlovukazi imane iphawula ukuthi ukubambezeleka kwesikhashana kuzozilahlekisela ukuphila kwazo.

Ngaso sonke isikhathi bedlala i-Queen ayizange iyeke ukuxabana nabanye abadlali, futhi imemeza ithi "Ayisuse ngekhanda!" noma “Ayivale ngekhanda lakhe!” Labo abagwetshiwe baboshwa ngamasosha, okwakudingeka bayeke ukuba ngamakhothamo ukwenza lokhu, ukuze kuthi ekupheleni kwesigamu sehora noma ngaphezulu kungabi namakhothamo asele, futhi bonke abadlali, ngaphandle kweNkosi, iNdlovukazi, no-Alice, babeboshiwe futhi ngaphansi kwesigwebo sokubulawa.

Khona-ke iNdlovukazi yahamba, iphelelwa umoya, yathi ku-Alice, “Usulubonile uFudu elinguMbusi?”

“Cha,” kusho u-Alice. “Angazi nokuthi yini i-Mock Turtle.”

“Into eyenziwa ngayo iMock Turtle Soup,” kusho iNdlovukazi.

“Angikaze ngimbone, noma ngizwe ngomunye,” kusho u-Alice.

"Woza-ke," kusho iNdlovukazi, "futhi izokutshela umlando wayo."

Njengoba behamba ndawonye, u-Alice wezwa iNkosi isho ngezwi eliphansi, enkampanini ngokuvamile, "Nonke nixolelwe." "Woza, into enhle leyo !" ekhuluma yedwa, phela ubezizwa engajabule ngenani lokubulawa kukaNdlovukazi.

Ngokushesha bazithela phezu kweGryphon, ilele zwi nelanga. (Uma ungazi ukuthi iyini iGryphon, buka isithombe.) “Phakamani, vila! kusho iNdlovukazi, "bese thatha le ntokazi iyobona uFudu elinguMhlengi, futhi sizwe umlando wayo. Kumele ngibuyele emuva ngiyobona ngemva kokubulawa engikuyalile;" wahamba eshiya u-Alice yedwa noGryphon. U-Alice akakuthandanga neze ukubukeka kwesidalwa, kodwa kukho konke wayecabanga ukuthi kwakuzophepheka kakhulu ukuhlala naso njengoba elandela leyo ndlovukazi enonya: ngakho walinda.

I-Gryphon yahlala futhi yacikica amehlo ayo: yabe isibuka iNdlovukazi yaze yangabe isabonwa: yase ihleka. “Yeka injabulo!” Kusho iGryphon, ingxenye yodwa, ingxenye ku-Alice.

“ Kuyini ubumnandi?” Kusho u-Alice.

“Kungani, yena ,” kusho uGryphon. “Kuyintandokazi yakhe, ukuthi: abasoze babulala muntu, uyazi. Woza!”

Wonke umuntu uthi 'wozani!' lapha,” kucabanga u-Alice, njengoba eyilandela kancane: “Angikaze ngihleleke ngale ndlela kukho konke ukuphila kwami, angikaze!”

Abazange bahambe ibanga elide ngaphambi kokuba babone uFudu mbumbulu buqamama, ehlezi edangele futhi enesizungu onqenqemeni oluncane lwetshe, kwathi lapho besondela, u-Alice wayemuzwa ephefumula sengathi inhliziyo yakhe izophuka. Wamdabukela kakhulu. “Luyini usizi lwakhe?” wabuza iGryphon, futhi iGryphon yaphendula, cishe ngamagama afanayo nangaphambili, "Kuyinto yakhe yonke, ukuthi: akanalo usizi, uyazi. Woza!"

Ngakho bakhuphukela kuFudu elinguMhlengi, owababuka ngamehlo amakhulu agcwele izinyembezi, kodwa wathula.

“Le ntokazi elapha,” kusho uGryphon, “ifuna ukwazi umlando wakho, ifuna ukwazi.”

“Ngizomtshela,” kusho uFudu elinguMhlengi ngezwi elijulile nelingenalutho: “hlalani phansi nobabili, futhi ningakhulumi lutho ngize ngiqede.”

Bahlala-ke phansi, akwaba khona okhulumayo imizuzu ethile. U-Alice wacabanga, “Angiboni ukuthi angaqeda kanjani, uma engaqali.” Kodwa walinda ngesineke.

“Kwake kwathi,” kusho uFudu elinguMhlengi ekugcineni, ngokuphefumulela phezulu, “ngangiwuFudu lwangempela.”

Lawa magama alandelwa wukuthula okude kakhulu, ephulwa kuphela isibabazo sezikhathi ezithile esithi “Hjckrrh!” kusukela eGryphon, kanye nokukhala okungapheli kweMock Turtle. U-Alice wayesecishe esukuma ethi, “Ngiyabonga, mnumzane, ngendaba yakho ethokozisayo,” kodwa akazange akwazi ukuzibamba ecabanga ukuthi kusazodingeka okwengeziwe, ngakho wahlala wathula wangasho lutho.

“Ngesikhathi sisebancane,” uFudu elinguMhlengi lwaqhubeka ekugcineni, ngomoya ophansi, nakuba lwalusakhala kancane njalo, “sasifunda esikoleni olwandle.

“Kungani umbize ngoFudu, uma bekungeyena?” Kubuza uAlice.

“Sambiza ngoFudu ngoba wasifundisa,” kusho uFudu elinguMhlengi ngokucasuka: “ubuthuntu ngempela!”

“Kufanele ube namahloni ngawe ngokubuza umbuzo olula,” kwenezela iGryphon; base behlala bobabili bathula babuka u-Alice ndini, owayezizwa ekulungele ukucwila emhlabeni. Ekugcineni uGryphon wathi kuFudu elinguMhlengi, “Shayela, mfo omdala! waseqhubeka ngalamazwi.

“Yebo, safunda olwandle, nakuba ungakukholwa—”

“Angikaze ngithi angishongo! ephazanyiswe u-Alice.

“Ukwenzile,” kusho uFudu elinguMhlekisi.

“Bamba ulimi lwakho!” kwengeza iGryphon, ngaphambi kokuthi u-Alice akhulume futhi. Waqhubeka uFudu.

“Sasinemfundo engcono kakhulu—empeleni, sasisiya esikoleni nsuku zonke—”

“ Nami ngike ngaya esikoleni sasemini,” kusho u-Alice; “Akumele uziqhenye njengakho konke lokho.”

“Nezinye?” kubuza uFudu mbumbulu ngokukhathazeka kancane.

“Yebo,” kusho u-Alice, “sifunde isiFulentshi nomculo.”

“Nokugeza?” Kusho uFudu Qhawe.

“Akunjalo!” Kusho u-Alice ngokucasuka.

Khona-ke esakho bekungesona isikole esihle ngempela,” kwasho uFudu elinguMock ngezwi lokukhululeka okukhulu. Manje ekhaya lethu base bephethe incwadi ethi, 'isiFulentshi, umculo nokuwashwa —okungeziwe.'

“Wawungeke uyifune kakhulu,” kusho u-Alice; "ehlala phansi kolwandle."

“Ngangingeke ngikwazi ukukufunda.” Kusho uFudu Buhle ehefuzela. "Ngithathe izifundo ezijwayelekile kuphela."

"Bekuyini leyo?" kubuza uAlice.

“Ukudlikizela kanye Nokubhimba, yebo, okokuqala nje,” kuphendula uFudu elinguMbusi; “bese kuba namagatsha ahlukene e-Arithmetic—Ambition, Distraction, Uglification, kanye Nokuhlekwa.”

“Angikaze ngizwe ‘nge-Uglification,’” u-Alice wazama ukusho. "Kwenzenjani?"

I-Gryphon yaphakamisa izidladla zayo zombili ngokumangala. Angikaze ngizwe kuthiwa kubi! kubabaza. “Uyazi ukuthi yini okufanele uyenze ibenhle, ngicabanga?”

“Yebo,” kusho u-Alice ngokungabaza: “kusho—ukwenza—noma yini—yinhle kakhulu.”

“Hhayi-ke,” kuqhuba uGryphon, “uma ungazi ukuthi kuyini ukwenza okubi, ungumuntu ongenalwazi.”

U-Alice akazange azizwe ekhuthazekile ukuba abuze eminye imibuzo ngakho, ngakho waphendukela kuFudu Mock, wathi “Yini enye obekufanele uyifunde?”

“Hhayi-ke, bekukhona Imfihlakalo,” kuphendula uFudu elinguMbusi, libala izifundo ezikubadlali bakhe, “—Imfihlakalo, yasendulo nesimanje, ne-Seaography: bese kuthi i-Drawling—u-Drawling-master wayengumbongolo omdala, owawufika kanye ngesonto: wayesifundisa Ukudwengula, Ukwelula, Nokuquleka Ngamakhoyili.”

"Kwakunjani lokho ?" Kusho u-Alice.

"Hhayi-ke, angikwazi ukukukhombisa lokho mina," kusho uFudu elinguMhlengi: "Ngiqine kakhulu. Futhi uGryphon akazange akufunde."

“Angizange ngibe nesikhathi,” kusho uGryphon: “Ngaya ku-Classics master, wayeyinkalankala endala.

“Angikaze ngiye kuye,” kusho uFudu elinguMock ephefumula: “wayefundisa Ukuhleka Nosizi, babevame ukusho.”

“Wenze njalo, wenze njalo,” kusho uGryphon, ephefumulela phezulu; zombili izidalwa zazifihle ubuso bazo ezidladleni zazo.

“Kanti wenza izifundo ngamahora amangaki ngosuku?” Kusho uAlice ejahe ukushintsha inkulumo.

“Amahora ayishumi ngosuku lokuqala,” kwasho uFudu elinguMock: “amahora ayisishiyagalolunye ngosuku olulandelayo, njalo njalo.”

“Yeka icebo elimangalisayo!” kubabaza u-Alice.

“Yingakho zibizwa ngezifundo,” kuphawula abakwaGryphon: “ngoba ziyancipha usuku nosuku.”

Lona kwakungumbono omusha ku-Alice, futhi wakucabangisisa kancane ngaphambi kokuba akhulume ngokulandelayo. “Khona-ke kufanele ukuthi usuku lweshumi nanye bekuyiholide?”

“Impela kwakunjalo,” kusho uFudu elinguMhlekisi.

"Kanti wakwazi kanjani ngolweshumi nambili?" U-Alice waqhubeka ngokulangazela.

"Kwanele ngezifundo," uGryphon waphazamisa ngezwi elinqunyiwe: "mtshele okuthile mayelana nemidlalo manje."
