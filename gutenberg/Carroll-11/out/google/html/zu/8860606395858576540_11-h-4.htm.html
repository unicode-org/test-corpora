ISAHLUKO IV.
Unogwaja Uthumela Imali Encane

KwakunguNogwaja Omhlophe, ephindela emuva kancane kancane, ebuka ngokukhathazeka njengoba ehamba, sengathi ulahlekelwe okuthile; futhi wayizwa ibubula yodwa ithi, “The Duchess! The duchess! Oh my dear paws! Oh my boya namadevu! Uzongibulala, njengoba ama-ferrets eyi-ferret! Ngabe ngiwawise kuphi, ngiyamangala?” Ngokuphazima kweso u-Alice waqagela ukuthi ifuna isiphephetha-moya kanye namagilavu ezingane ezimhlophe, wayeseqala ukubazingela, kodwa ayengabonakali—konke kwabonakala kushintshile kusukela ebhukuda echibini, nehholo elikhulu, elinetafula lengilazi nomnyango omncane, lase linyamalale ngokuphelele.

Ngokushesha uNogwaja waqaphela u-Alice, njengoba ehamba ezingela, futhi wambiza ngezwi elithukuthele, “Kungani, Mary Ann, wenzani lapha? Gijima uye ekhaya ngalo mzuzu, futhi ungiphathele amagilavu kanye nesiphephetha-moya! Shesha, manje!” Futhi u-Alice wethuka kakhulu kangangokuthi wabaleka ngaso leso sikhathi waya ngalapho ekhombe khona, ngaphandle kokuzama ukuchaza iphutha elenzile.

“Wangithatha njengencekukazi yakhe,” ekhuluma yedwa egijima. “Yeka indlela ayomangala ngayo lapho ethola ukuthi ngingubani! Kodwa kungcono ngimthathele ifeni namagilavu akhe—okungukuthi, uma ngingawathola.” Lapho esho lokhu, wazithela phezu kwendlu encane ecocekile, emnyango eyayinocwecwe lwethusi olukhanyayo olunegama elithi “W. RABBI,” oluqoshwe kuso. Wangena ngaphandle kokungqongqoza, futhi waphuthuma waya phezulu, ngokwesaba okukhulu funa ahlangane noMary Ann wangempela, akhishwe endlini engakayitholi ifeni namagilavu.

“Yeka ukuthi kubonakala kuwubuwula kanjani,” u-Alice ekhuluma yedwa, “ukuthumela imiyalezo ngonogwaja! Futhi waqala ukucabanga ukuthi kuzokwenzekani: “'Nkosazana Alice, woza lapha, ulungisele uhambo lwakho!' 'Ngena umzuzu, nesi! Kuphela angicabangi,” kuqhuba u-Alice, “ukuthi bebengamyeka uDina ame endlini uma eqala ukuyala abantu kanjalo!”

Ngalesi sikhathi wayesethole indlela yakhe yokungena endlini encane ehlelekile enetafula efasiteleni, futhi kulo (njengoba ayethemba) isiphephetha-moya namapheya amabili noma amathathu amagilavu amancane amhlophe: wathatha isiphephetha-moya nepheya lamagilavu, futhi wayesezophuma ekamelweni, lapho iso lakhe lawela phezu kwebhodlela elincane elalimi eduze kwengilazi yokubuka. Ngalesi sikhathi yayingekho ilebula enamagama athi “NGIPHUZA,” kodwa nokho waliqaqa walibeka ezindebeni zakhe. “Ngiyazi ukuthi kukhona okuthakazelisayo kuzokwenzeka nakanjani,” ekhuluma yedwa, “noma nini lapho ngidla noma ngiphuza noma yini; ngakho ngizovele ngibone ukuthi leli bhodlela lenzani.

Kwenze njalo ngempela, futhi ngokushesha okukhulu kunalokho ayekulindele: ngaphambi kokuba aphuze ingxenye yebhodlela, wathola ikhanda lakhe licindezela ophahleni, futhi kwadingeka aqonde ukuze asindise intamo yakhe ekuphukeni. Ngokushesha walibeka phansi ibhodlela, ezitshela ukuthi “Sekwanele—ngithemba ukuthi ngeke ngisakhula—Njengoba kunjalo, angikwazi ukuphuma emnyango—ngifisa sengathi ngabe angiphuzanga kangako!”

Maye! kwase kwephuze kakhulu ukufisa lokho! Waqhubeka ekhula, futhi ekhula, futhi ngokushesha kwadingeka aguqe phansi: komunye umzuzu kwakungekho ngisho nendawo yalokhu, futhi wazama umphumela wokulala ngendololwane eyodwa emnyango, futhi enye ingalo yagoqa ikhanda lakhe. Noma kunjalo waqhubeka ekhula, futhi, njengensiza yokugcina, wakhipha ingalo eyodwa ngefasitela, nolunye unyawo phezulu kushimula, futhi wathi enhliziyweni yakhe “Manje ngeke ngisakwazi ukwenza, noma yini eyenzekayo. Kuyoba njani ngami?”

Ngenhlanhla ka-Alice, ibhodlela elincane lomlingo lase lisebenze ngokugcwele, futhi alizange libe likhudlwana: noma kunjalo lalingakhululekile, futhi, njengoba kwakubonakala sengathi alikho ithuba lokuthi aphinde aphume ekamelweni, akumangalisi ukuthi wazizwa engajabule.

“Kwakumnandi kakhulu ekhaya,” kucabanga u-Alice ompofu, “lapho umuntu wayengahlali eba mkhulu futhi eba mncane, futhi eqondiswa amagundane nonogwaja. Ngicishe ngifise sengathi ngabe angizange ngiwele kulowo mgodi onogwaja—kodwa nokho—kodwa nokho—kunelukuluku lokufuna ukwazi, niyazi, lolu hlobo lwempilo! futhi manje sengiphakathi kweyodwa! Kufanele kube khona incwadi ebhalwe ngami, okufanele ngiyibhale—kodwa sengikhulile manje,” enezela ngezwi elidabukisayo; “okungenani ayisekho indawo yokukhulela lapha .”

“Kodwa-ke,” kucabanga u-Alice, “ngeke yini ngibe mdala kunami manje ?

“Hawu, u-Alice oyisiwula! aziphendule. “Ungafunda kanjani izifundo lapha? Phela, ayikho indawo yakho , futhi ayikho nhlobo indawo yanoma yiziphi izincwadi zokufunda!”

Futhi ngakho waqhubeka, ethatha uhlangothi olulodwa kuqala futhi kamuva nolunye, futhi wenza ingxoxo impela ngakho ngokuphelele; kodwa emva kwemizuzu embalwa wezwa izwi ngaphandle, wema walalela.

“Mary Ann! Mary Ann!” Kusho izwi. “Ngilande amagilavu ami manje!” Kwabe sekufika ukubhampa kwezinyawo kancane ezitebhisini. U-Alice wayazi ukuthi kwakunguNogwaja owayezombheka, futhi waqhaqhazela waze wanyakazisa indlu, wakhohlwa impela ukuthi wayesemkhulu ngokuphindwe kayinkulungwane kunoNogwaja, futhi wayengenasizathu sokwesaba lokho.

Njengamanje uMvundla wasondela emnyango, wazama ukuwuvula; kodwa, njengoba umnyango uvuleka ngaphakathi, futhi indololwane ka-Alice yayicindezelwe kanzima kuyo, lowo mzamo wabonakala uhlulekile. U-Alice wezwa izitshela ukuthi “Ngizojikeleza ngingene efasiteleni.”

“ Ukuthi ngeke!” kucabanga u-Alice, futhi, ngemva kokulinda waze wakitaza, wezwa uNogwaja ngaphansi kwefasitela, wavele welula isandla sakhe, wahlwitha emoyeni. Akazange abambe lutho, kodwa wezwa kukhala kancane nokuwa, nokuphahlazeka kwengilazi ephukile, aphetha ngokuthi kungenzeka ukuthi iwele ohlakeni lwekhukhamba, noma into enjalo.

Kwalandela izwi elithukuthele—elikaNogwaja—“Pat! Pat! Ukuphi?” Kwase kuba nezwi ayengakaze alizwe ngaphambili, “Impela sengikhona!

“Ukumba ama-apula, impela!” Kusho uMvundla ngolaka. “Here! Come and help me out of this! ” (Kuzwakala ingilazi ephukile.)

“Manje ngitshele, Pat, yini leyo esefasiteleni?”

“Impela, ingalo, yebo! (Wayibiza ngokuthi “arrum.”)

“Ingalo, hansi, ubani owake wabona eyodwa engaka, igcwele iwindi lonke!”

"Impela, kunjalo, udumo: kodwa kuyingalo yakho konke lokho."

“Hhayi-ke, akunamsebenzi lapho, noma yikanjani: hamba uyoyithatha!”

Kwathuleka isikhathi eside emva kwalokhu, u-Alice wayesezwa amazwi anyenyezayo kuphela; njengokuthi, “Impela, angikuthandi, yebo, nhlobo, nhlobo!” "Yenza njengoba ngikutshela, wena igwala!" kwathi ekugcineni welula isandla sakhe, wenza enye ihlwili emoyeni. Kulokhu kwaba nokukhala okuncane okubili , nemisindo eyengeziwe yengilazi ephukile. “Yeka inani lezinhlaka zamakhukhamba okumelwe zibe khona!” kucabanga u-Alice. “Kazi bazokwenzani ngokulandelayo! Mayelana nokungikhipha ngefasitela, ngifisa sengathi bangangenza !

Walinda isikhathi eside engasezwa lutho: ekugcineni kwavela ukuduma kwamasondo enqola, nomsindo wamazwi amaningi amaningi ekhuluma ndawonye: washo la mazwi: “Liphi elinye iladi? nokho—Oh, bazokwenza kahle ngokwanele—Lapha, uBill bamba le ntambo—Ingabe uphahla luzothwala—Awu, luyehla! (umsindo omkhulu)—“Manje, ubani owenze lokho?—BekunguBill, ngiyafisa— Ubani ozokwehla kushimula?—Cha, ngeke !

Ngakho uBill kuzomele ehle ngoshimula, akunjalo?” ekhuluma yedwa u-Alice. “Mahloni, babonakala bebeka yonke into phezu kukaBill!

Wadonsa unyawo lwakhe waze wafika phansi kushimula ngangokunokwenzeka, futhi walinda kwaze kwaba yilapho ezwa isilwane esincane (wayengakwazi ukuqagela ukuthi kwakuluhlobo luni) sinwaya futhi sinyakaza kushimula oseduze kwakhe: wabe esezitshela ukuthi “Lo nguBill,” wakhahlela okukodwa okubukhali, futhi walinda ukubona ukuthi kwakuzokwenzekani ngokulandelayo.

Into yokuqala ayizwayo kwakuyikhorasi evamile ethi “There goes Bill!” kwase kuba nezwi likaNogwaja—“Mbambe, wena ngothango!” bese kuthi cwaka, bese kuba nokunye ukudideka kwamazwi—“Phakamisa ikhanda lakhe—uBrandy manje—Ungamklinya—Bekunjani, khehla? Kwenzekeni kuwe? Sitshele ngakho konke!”

Kugcine ukuzwakala izwi elincane, elinswininizayo, (“NguBill lowo,” kucabanga u-Alice,) “Hhayi-ke, angisakwazi-ke, ngiyabonga; sengingcono manje—kodwa ngikhathazekile kakhulu ukuthi ngingakutshela—engikwaziyo nje ukuthi, kukhona okuza kimi njengoJack-in-the-box, bese ngikhuphuka njengerokhethi yasezulwini!”

“Wenze njalo, mfo omdala!” Kusho abanye.

“Kumele siyishise indlu!” lathi izwi likaNogwaja; futhi u-Alice wamemeza kakhulu ngangokunokwenzeka, “Uma wenza, ngizobeka uDina kuwena!”

Kwathuleka ngaso leso sikhathi, u-Alice wacabanga, “Kazi bazokwenzenjani ngokulandelayo! Ngemva komzuzu noma emibili, baqala futhi ukuhambahamba, futhi u-Alice wezwa uNogwaja ethi, "I-barrowful izokwenza, ukuqala nje."

“I-barrowful yani? ” kucabanga u-Alice; kodwa akabange esaba nokungabaza, ngomzuzu olandelayo kwatheleka imvula yamatshe amancane efastela, amanye amshaya ebusweni. “Ngizoyimisa le nto,” ekhuluma yedwa, futhi ememeza, “Kungcono ungaphinde ukwenze lokho!” okwaveza okunye ukuthula.

U-Alice waqaphela ngokumangala ukuthi amatshe amatshe ayesephenduka amaqebelengwane amancane njengoba elele phansi, kwase kufika umqondo okhanyayo ekhanda lakhe. “Uma ngidla elinye lalawa maqebelengwane,” ecabanga, “ lizoshintsha usayizi wami; futhi njengoba lingeke lingenze ngibe mkhulu, kufanele lingenze ngibe mncane, ngicabanga ukuthi.”

Ngakho wagwinya elinye lamaqebelengwane, futhi wajabula lapho ethola ukuthi waqala ukuncipha ngqo. Wathi esemncane ngokwanele ukuthi angangena emnyango, waphuma egijima endlini, wathola isixuku esikhulu sezilwane nezinyoni silindile ngaphandle. ULiza omncane ompofu, uBill, wayephakathi, ephakanyiswe amagwinya amabili, ayeyinika okuthile ebhodleleni. Bonke baphuthuma ku-Alice ngesikhathi eqhamuka; kodwa wabaleka kakhulu ngangokunokwenzeka, futhi ngokushesha wazithola ephephile okhunini oluwugqinsi.

“Into yokuqala okufanele ngiyenze,” kusho u-Alice ezitshela ukuthi, njengoba ezulazula ehlathini, “ukuthi ngikhule ngibe sezingeni lami elifanele futhi; futhi into yesibili iwukuba ngithole indlela yami yokungena kuleyongadi enhle. Ngicabanga ukuthi lelo kuzoba isu elingcono kakhulu.”

Kuzwakale kuyisu elihle kakhulu, ngokungangabazeki, futhi ngobunono obukhulu futhi obuhlelwe kalula; okwakuwubunzima kuphela, ukuthi wayengenawo nomcabango omncane wokuthi angaqala kanjani ngakho; kwathi esalunguza phakathi kwezihlahla ngokukhathazeka, igxolo elincane elibukhali phezu kwekhanda lakhe lamenza wabheka phezulu ngokukhulu ukuphuthuma.

Umdlwane omkhulu wawumbheke phansi ngamehlo amakhulu ayindilinga, futhi welula udladla olulodwa, uzama ukuwuthinta. “Into encane empofu!” kusho u-Alice, ngezwi elincengayo, futhi wazama kanzima ukuyishayela ikhwela; kodwa wayethuke kakhulu ngaso sonke isikhathi ecabanga ukuthi kungenzeka ukuthi ilambile, okungenzeka ukuthi yayizomudla naphezu kokuncenga kwakhe konke.

Engazi ukuthi wenzeni, wacosha induku encane, wayibhekisa kumdlwane; lapho umdlwane wagxumela emoyeni wasuka ezinyaweni zawo zonke ngesikhathi esisodwa, ngokukhala kwenjabulo, futhi waphuthuma endukwini, futhi wenza ukuba ukholwe ukuba ukhathazeke; wabe-ke u-Alice evika ngemva kwekhakhasi elikhulu, ukuze azivikele ekugalweni; kwathi lapho uqhamuka ngakolunye uhlangothi, umdlwane waphinda wagijima endukwini, wagingqika ezithendeni ngokuphuthuma ukuwubamba; khona-ke u-Alice, ecabanga ukuthi kwakufana nokuba nomdlalo wokudlala nenqola-ihhashi, futhi elindele umzuzu ngamunye ukuba anyathelwe ngaphansi kwezinyawo zawo, wagijima wazungeza ikhakhasi futhi; khona-ke umdlwane waqala uchungechunge lwamacala amafushane endukwini, ugijima kancane kakhulu uya phambili isikhathi ngasinye futhi ibanga elide ubuyela emuva, futhi ukhonkotha uhayiza ngaso sonke isikhathi, waze wagcina usuhlala kahle kude, uyahefuzela, ulimi lwawo lulenga emlonyeni, futhi amehlo awo amakhulu avaleke kancane.

Lokhu kwabonakala ku-Alice njengethuba elihle lokumenza abaleke; ngakho wasuka khona lapho, wagijima waze wadinwa waphelelwa umoya, kwaze kwazwakala ukukhonkotha komdlwane kuzwakala kufiphele kude.

“Kodwa bekuwumdlwane omncane othandekayo kangaka!” kusho u-Alice, lapho encike ku-buttercup ukuze aziphumulele, wabe eseziphephetha ngelinye lamahlamvu: "Bekufanele ngithande kakhulu ukufundisa amaqhinga, ukube - ukube bengingaba usayizi ofanele ukukwenza! O sthandwa! Bengizokhohlwa ukuthi kufanele ngikhule futhi! Ake ngibone- kuzophathwa kanjani?

Umbuzo omkhulu wawuwukuthi, yini? U-Alice wambheka nxazonke ebuka izimbali nezindwani zotshani, kodwa akazange abone lutho olufana nokufanele ukudliwa noma ukuphuza ngaphansi kwezimo. Kwakukhona ikhowe elikhulu eduze kwakhe, elicishe lilingane naye ngobude; kwathi lapho eqalaza ngaphansi kwawo, nasezinhlangothini zawo zombili, nangemva kwawo, kwafika engqondweni yakhe ukuthi angase abuke futhi abone ukuthi yini ephezu kwawo.

Wazelula ngomunwe, walunguza onqenqemeni lwekhowa, futhi amehlo akhe ahlangana ngokushesha nalawo esibungu esikhulu esiluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, esasihlezi phezulu sisonge izingalo, sithule sibhema ihuka ende, futhi singamnaki ngisho nakancane noma yini enye.
