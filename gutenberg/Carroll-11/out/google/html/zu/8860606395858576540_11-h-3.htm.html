ISAHLUKO III.
I-Caucus-Race kanye Nenganekwane ende

Ngempela kwakuwumcimbi obukeka umbi owawubuthene ogwini—izinyoni ezinezimpaphe ezihudulwayo, izilwane ezinoboya bazo obunamathele eduze kwazo, zonke ziconsa zimanzi, iziphambano, futhi zingakhululekile.

Umbuzo wokuqala wawuwukuthi, uzokoma kanjani futhi: babe nokubonisana ngalokhu, futhi ngemva kwemizuzu embalwa kwabonakala kungokwemvelo ku-Alice ukuzithola ekhuluma ngendlela evamile nabo, njengokungathi wayebazi impilo yakhe yonke. Ngempela, waba nengxabano ende noLori, owagcina ecasukile, futhi wayemane athi, “Ngimdala kunawe, futhi kufanele ngazi kangcono;” futhi lo-Alice wayengeke akuvumele engazi ukuthi uneminyaka emingaki, futhi, njengoba iLory yenqaba ngokuqinisekile ukutshela iminyaka yayo, kwakungasekho okwakuzoshiwo.

Ekugcineni iGundane elalibonakala linegunya phakathi kwabo lamemeza lathi: “Hlalani phansi nonke, ningilalele, ngizonenza nome ngokwanele! Bahlala bonke kanye kanye, indandatho enkulu, iGundane liphakathi. U-Alice wayelokhu ethe njo kuwo ngokukhathazeka, ngoba wayeqiniseka ukuthi uzongenwa umkhuhlane omubi uma engomi ngokushesha.

“Ayem!” kusho iGundane ngomoya obalulekile, “ingabe nonke nimi ngomumo? Yinto eyome kunazo zonke engiyazi lena.” Thula nxazonke, uma uthanda!’ UWilliam uMnqobi, injongo yakhe yavunywa upapa, ngokushesha wanikezwa amaNgisi, ayefuna abaholi, futhi wayesekujwayele kakhulu ukuqhwaga nokunqoba.” U-Edwin noMorcar, izindlebe zaseNyakatho yeMercia’—u-Edwin noMorcar, kanye nezindlebe zaseNyakatho yeMercia.

“Hhawu!” Kusho uLori eqhaqhazela.

"Ngicela uphinde!" kusho uGundane, ehwaqabala, kodwa ngesizotha: “Ukhulumile?”

“Hhayi mina!” Kusho uLori eshesha.

“Bengicabanga ukuthi ukwenzile,” kusho iGundane. “—Ngiyaqhubeka. ‘U-Edwin noMorcar, izindlebe zikaMercia noNorthumbria, zamenyezelwa: ngisho noStigand, umbhishobhi omkhulu oshisekela izwe laseCanterbury, wakuthola kufaneleka—’”

“Uthole ini ?” Kusho uDada.

“ Ngiyitholile ,” iGundane laphendula ngokungananazi: “Yebo uyazi ukuthi lisho ukuthini.”

“Ngiyazi ukuthi kusho ukuthini ngokwanele, lapho ngithola okuthile,” kusho uDada: “imvamisa kuba ixoxo noma isibungu.” Umbuzo uwukuthi, yini eyatholwa umbhishobhi omkhulu?”

IGundane alizange liwubone lombuzo, kodwa ngokushesha laqhubeka lithi, “‘—wakuthola kuwukuhlakanipha ukuhamba no-Edgar Atheling ukuze ahlangane noWilliam futhi amnike umqhele.” Ekuqaleni ukuziphatha kukaWilliam kwakulinganisene. kuqhuba u-Alice esakhuluma.

“Njengoba kumanzi njengakuqala,” kusho u-Alice ngezwi elidabukisayo: “akubonakali kungimisa nhlobo.”

“Uma kunjalo,” kusho uDodo ngokuzithoba, esukuma, “ngicela uhlehlise umhlangano, ukuze kutholwe amakhambi anamandla ngokushesha—”

"Khuluma isingisi!" Kusho uKhozi. “Angiyazi incazelo yalawo magama amade, futhi, ngaphezu kwalokho, angikholwa ukuthi nawe uyazi!” Futhi i-Eaglet yagobisa ikhanda layo ukuze ifihle ukumamatheka: ezinye zezinyoni zakhala ngokuzwakalayo.

"Engizokusho," kusho uDodo ngezwi elicasukile, "ukuthi into engcono kakhulu yokusehlisa kungaba umjaho weCaucus."

“ Uyini umjaho weCaucus?” kusho u-Alice; hhayi ukuthi wayefuna ukwazi okuningi, kodwa uDodo wayesethule sengathi ucabanga ukuthi kukhona okumele akhulume, futhi akekho omunye owayebonakala efuna ukusho okuthile.

"Kungani," kusho uDodo, "indlela engcono kakhulu yokukuchaza ukukwenza." (Futhi, njengoba ungathanda ukuzama into ngokwakho, ngolunye usuku lwasebusika, ngizokutshela ukuthi i-Dodo yakwazi kanjani.)

Kwaqala kwaphawula umjaho, ngohlobo lwendilinga, (“ukwakheka kwayo akunandaba,” yasho,) kwase kuthi wonke amaqembu abekwe eceleni komzila, lapha nalaphaya. Kwakungekho “Oyedwa, ababili, abathathu, noma kude,” kodwa baqala ukugijima lapho bethanda, futhi bayeke lapho bethanda, ukuze kungabi lula ukwazi ukuthi umncintiswano usuphelile. Nokho, lapho sebegijima isigamu sehora noma ngaphezulu, futhi bomile futhi, i-Dodo kungazelelwe yamemeza “Umjaho usuphelile!” basebewubuthela bonke behefuzela bebuza besithi: Kodwa ngubani onqobileyo?

Lo mbuzo uDodo akakwazanga ukuwuphendula ngaphandle kokucabanga okukhulu, futhi wahlala isikhathi eside umunwe owodwa ucindezelwe ebunzini lakhe (indawo ojwayele ukubona kuyo uShakespeare, ezithombeni zakhe), kuyilapho abanye belindile bethule. Ekugcineni uDodo wathi, " Wonke umuntu uwinile, futhi bonke kufanele babe nemiklomelo."

“Kodwa ubani ozonikeza imiklomelo?” kubuza amaphimbo impela.

"Kungani, kunjalo ," kusho uDodo, ekhomba u-Alice ngomunwe owodwa; futhi iphathi yonke ngaleso sikhathi yagcwala kuye, imemeza ngendlela edidekile, "Imiklomelo!

U-Alice wayengazi ukuthi enzeni, futhi ngokudangala wafaka isandla sakhe ephaketheni lakhe, wakhipha ibhokisi le-comfits, (ngenhlanhla amanzi anosawoti ayengakangeni kulo), wawanikeza njengemiklomelo. Kwakukhona ucezu olulodwa impela, nxazonke.

“Kodwa kufanele athole umklomelo ngokwakhe, uyazi,” kusho iGundane.

“Kunjalo,” kuphendula uDodo ngokudabukisayo. “Yini enye onayo ephaketheni lakho?” aqhubeke, aphendukele ku-Alice.

“Ithinjana kuphela,” kusho u-Alice ngokudabuka.

“Nikeza lapha,” kusho uDodo.

Base bephinda futhi bambuthanisa bonke, lapho uDodo wayeselethe ithunga, ethi: “Siyakucela ukuba uwamukele le thinjane elihle; kwathi lapho isiqedile lenkulumo emfushane, bajabula bonke.

U-Alice wacabanga ukuthi yonke le nto yayingenangqondo, kodwa bonke babebukeka bethuna kangangokuthi akazange alokothe ahleke; futhi, njengoba ayengacabangi lutho angakusho, wavele wakhothama, wathatha ithoni, ebukeka enesizotha ngangokunokwenzeka.

Okulandelayo kwakuwukudla ama-comfits: lokhu kwadala umsindo nokudideka, njengoba izinyoni ezinkulu zazikhononda ngokuthi azikwazi ukunambitha ezazo, futhi ezincane zaklinywa futhi kwadingeka ziphucwe emhlane. Nokho-ke kwagcina kuphelile, baphinde bahlala phansi eringini, bancenga uGundane ukuthi abatshele okunye.

“Uthembise ukungitshela umlando wakho, uyazi,” kusho u-Alice, “nokuthi kungani nizonda—u-C no-D,” enezela ngokuhleba, esaba ukuthi uzophinde acasuke.

“Eyami indaba ende futhi edabukisayo!” Kusho uGundane ephendukela ku-Alice, abubule.

“ Umsila omude, impela,” kusho u-Alice, ebuka phansi ngokumangala umsila weGundane; "kodwa kungani ukubiza ngokudabukisayo?" Futhi wayelokhu edidekile ngakho ngenkathi uGundane ekhuluma, kangangokuthi umqondo wakhe wenganekwane waba yinto enjengale:-

“Awungeni!” Kusho uGundane kuAlice. “Ucabangani?”

“Ngicela uxolo,” kusho u-Alice ngokuzithoba okukhulu: “uyacabanga ukuthi usufike ejikeni lesihlanu?”

“Bengingakaze ! ” kukhala iGundane, ngokucijile nangokucasuka okukhulu.

“Ifindo!” kusho u-Alice, ehlale ekulungele ukuzenza abe wusizo, futhi ebuka ngokukhathazeka ngaye. “O, ake ngisize ukukuhlehlisa!”

“Ngeke ngenze lutho olunjalo,” kusho iGundane, lisukuma lihamba. “Uyangithuka ngokukhuluma umbhedo onjalo!”

“Bengingaqondile!” kuncenga u-Alice ndini. “Kodwa ucasuka kalula, yazi!”

UGundane wabhodla kuphela ephendula.

“Ngicela ubuye uzoqedela indaba yakho!” U-Alice wabiza emva kwayo; nabanye bonke bahlanganyela ekuhlabeleleni, "Yebo, ngicela wenze!" kodwa iGundane lanikina ikhanda ngokuphelelwa yisineke, lahamba ngokushesha.

“Yeka usizi ukuthi bekungeke kuhlale!” wabubula uLori, lapho nje wayesengasabonakali; futhi inkalankala endala yathola ithuba lokuthi endodakazini yayo “Awu, sithandwa sami! Lesi akube isifundo kuwe ukuthi ungalokothi uthukuthele !” “Bamba ulimi lwakho, Ma!” Kusho uNkalankala osemncane, ngokukhulu ukuhwalala. “Wanele ukuzama isineke se-oyster!”

“Ngifisa sengathi ngabe nginoDina wethu lapha, ngiyazi ukuthi nginaye!” Kusho u-Alice ephimisela, engakhulumi namuntu. “Uzoyilanda maduze nje!”

“Ubani uDina, uma ngingase ngibuze lo mbuzo?” Kusho uLori.

U-Alice waphendula ngokulangazela, ngoba wayehlale ekulungele ukukhuluma ngesilwane sakhe esifuywayo: “UDina uyikati lethu.

Le nkulumo idale ukushuba okumangalisayo eqenjini. Ezinye zezinyoni zashesha zasuka lapho: omunye uMagpie omdala waqala ukuzisonga ngokucophelela, ephawula, “Kumelwe ngibe ngifika ekhaya ngempela; umoya wasebusuku awuhambisani nomphimbo wami!” umKhenani wamemeza ebantwaneni bawo ngezwi eliqhaqhazelayo, wathi: “Wozani, zithandwa zami, isikhathi sokulala kwenu nonke! Ngezizathu ezihlukahlukene bahamba bonke, futhi u-Alice wasala yedwa.

“Ngifisa sengathi ngabe angizange ngikhulume ngoDina!” ekhuluma yedwa ekhuluma yedwa. “Akekho obonakala emthanda, lapha ezansi, futhi ngiyaqiniseka ukuthi uyikati elidlula zonke emhlabeni! O, Dinah wami othandekayo! Futhi lapha u-Alice ompofu waqala ukukhala futhi, ngoba wazizwa enesizungu futhi enomoya ophansi. Nokho, ngemva kwesikhashana, waphinda wezwa ukushaywa kwezinyawo buqamama, wabheka phezulu ngokulangazela, enethemba lokuthi uGundane usewushintshile umqondo, wayesebuya ezoqedela indaba yakhe.
