Ubufakazi buka-Alice

"Lapha!" kumemeza u-Alice, esekhohliwe ngokuphithizela kwaleso sikhathi ukuthi ukhule kangakanani emizuzwini embalwa edlule, futhi wagxuma ngokukhulu ukushesha waze waguqa phezu kwebhokisi lamajaji ngomphetho wesiketi sakhe, ecasula wonke amajaji emakhanda esixuku esingezansi, futhi lapho balele bebhacaza, bemkhumbuza kakhulu nge-globe ye-goldfish ngaphambi kwesonto ngengozi.

“O, ngicela uxolo!” wababaza ngezwi lokwethuka okukhulu, waqala waphinda wawacosha ngokukhulu ukushesha, ngoba ingozi yenhlanzi yegolide yayilokhu igijima ekhanda lakhe, futhi wayenomqondo ongacacile wokuthi kufanele ziqoqwe ngokushesha futhi zibuyiselwe ebhokisini lejaji, kungenjalo zizofa.

"Icala ngeke liqhubeke," kusho iNkosi ngezwi elinzima, "kuze kube yilapho wonke amajaji ebuyela ezindaweni zawo ezifanele - bonke ," waphinda ngokugcizelela okukhulu, embuka kanzima u-Alice njengoba esho.

U-Alice wabheka ibhokisi lamajaji, futhi wabona ukuthi, ngokuxhamazela kwakhe, wayebeke uNtuli ekhanda phansi, futhi into encane empofu yayinyakazisa umsila wayo ngendlela edabukisayo, ingakwazi nhlobo ukunyakaza. Washeshe wayikhipha futhi, wayilungisa kahle; “hhayi ukuthi kusho lukhulu,” ekhuluma yedwa; "Kufanele ngicabange ukuthi kuzosetshenziswa kakhulu ekuqulweni kwecala ngendlela eyodwa njengokunye."

Kuthe uma abahluleli sebeluleme kancane ekwethukeni kokucasuka, sekutholakele izileti namapensela abo base bebuyiselwa kubo, baqala ukusebenza ngokukhulu ukukhuthala ukubhala umlando wengozi, bonke ngaphandle kukaLizi, owayebonakala ehluleke ukwenza noma yini ngaphandle kokuhlala evule umlomo, egqolozele ophahleni lwenkantolo.

“Wazini ngaleli bhizinisi?” Kusho iNkosi ku-Alice.

“Lutho,” kusho u-Alice.

“Akukho lutho? ” Kuphikelela uNkosi.

“Akukho lutho,” kusho u-Alice.

“Kubaluleke kakhulu lokho,” kusho iNkosi, iphendukela kumajaji. Babeqala ukubhala phansi lokhu ezitsheni zabo, lapho uMvundla oMhlophe ephazamisa: “ Akubalulekile , Nkosi yami kusho ukuthi, yebo,” esho ngezwi elibonisa inhlonipho, kodwa eshwabene futhi ebhekise ubuso kuye njengoba ekhuluma.

“Bengiqonde ukuthi akubalulekile ,” isho ngokukhulu ukushesha iNkosi, yazihambela ngezwi eliphansi,

“okubalulekile—okungabalulekile—okungabalulekile—okubalulekile—” njengokungathi uzama ukuthi yiliphi igama elizwakala kangcono.

Amanye amajaji abhala phansi “okubalulekile,” kanti amanye “akubalulekile.” U-Alice wayekubona lokhu, njengoba ayeseduze ngokwanele ukuba abheke izileti zabo; “kodwa akunandaba nakancane,” ezicabangela.

Ngaleso sikhathi iSilo, obekunesikhathi simatasa sibhala ebhukwini laso, samemeza sithi “Thula!” futhi wafunda encwadini yakhe ethi, “Umthetho Wamashumi Amane Nambili. Bonke abantu abangaphezulu kwekhilomitha ukuphakama ukuba baphume enkantolo .

Wonke umuntu wabuka u-Alice.

“ Angikho imayela ukuya phezulu,” kusho u-Alice.

“Nguwe,” kusho iNkosi.

“Cishe amamayela amabili ukuphakama,” kwengeza iNdlovukazi.

"Hhayi-ke, ngeke ngihambe, noma yikuphi," kusho u-Alice: "ngaphandle kwalokho, lokho akuwona umthetho ojwayelekile: usungule khona manje."

“Ngumthetho omdala kunayo yonke kule ncwadi,” kusho iNkosi.

“Khona-ke kufanele kube iNombolo yokuqala,” kusho u-Alice.

INkosi yaphaphatheka, yavala incwadi yayo ngokushesha. “Cabanga ngesinqumo sakho,” esho emajajini, ngezwi eliphansi eliqhaqhazelayo.

“Kunobunye ubufakazi obuzayo, ngiyakucela Nkosi,” kusho uNogwaja oMhlophe, egxuma ngokukhulu ukuphuthuma; "leli phepha lisanda kuthathwa."

“Yini ekhona?” Kusho uNdlovukazi.

“Angikakayivuli,” kusho uNogwaja Omhlophe, “kodwa kubonakala kuyincwadi, ebhalwe yisiboshwa eya—kumuntu othile.”

“Kufanele ukuthi kube yilokho,” kusho iSilo, “ngaphandle kokuba kubhalelwe muntu, okungajwayelekile, niyazi.”

“Iqondiswe kubani?” Kusho omunye wamajaji.

“Ayiqondiswe nhlobo,” kusho uNogwaja Omhlophe; “Eqinisweni, akukho okubhaliwe ngaphandle . Wembula iphepha esakhuluma, wengeza ngokuthi “Akuyona nje uhlamvu, iqoqo lamavesi.”

“Ingabe zibhalwe ngesandla sesiboshwa?” kubuza elinye lamajaji.

“Cha, abanjalo,” kusho uNogwaja Omhlophe, “futhi leyo yinto exakile ngakho.” (Ijaji lalibukeka lididekile.)

“Kumelwe ukuba ulingise isandla somunye umuntu,” kusho iNkosi. (Ijaji laphinde lakhanya futhi.)

“Ngiyakucela Nkosi,” kusho uKnave, “angiyibhalanga, futhi ngeke bakwazi ukufakazela ukuthi ngiyibhalile: alikho igama elisayinwe ekugcineni.”

“Ukube awusayinanga,” kusho iSilo, “lokho kwenza isimo sibe sibi kakhulu, kufanele ngabe ushilo ububi obuthile, kungenjalo ubuzosayina igama lakho njengendoda eqotho.

Kwaba nokushaya izandla okuvamile kulokhu: kwakuyinto yokuqala ehlakaniphe ngempela iNkosi eyakusho ngalolo suku.

“Lokho kufakazela ukuthi unecala,” kusho uNdlovukazi.

“Akufakazeli lutho olunjalo!” Kusho u-Alice. “Ngani, awazi nokuthi bamayelana nani!”

“Zifunde,” kusho iNkosi.

Unogwaja Omhlophe wafaka izibuko zakhe. "Ngizoqala ngaphi, ngiyakucela Nkosi?" wabuza.

“Qala ekuqaleni,” kusho iNkosi ngokujulile, “futhi uqhubeke uze ufike ekugcineni: bese ume.”

Lawa kwakungamavesi afundwa nguNogwaja Omhlophe:—

“Bangitshela ukuthi uke waba kuye,
Futhi wangitshela kuye:
Unginike isimilo esihle,
Kodwa wathi angikwazi ukubhukuda.

Wabathumelela ukuthi angizange ngihambe
(Siyazi ukuthi kuyiqiniso):
Uma kufanele aluqhubekisele phambili udaba,
Kungaba yini ngawe?

Ngamnika eyodwa, bamnika ezimbili,
Usinike ezintathu noma ngaphezulu;
Bonke babuya kuye beza kini.
Yize bebengabami phambilini.

Uma kufanele ngithole ithuba
Uhileleke kulolu daba,
Uthembele kuwe ukuthi uzobakhulula,
Impela njengoba sasinjalo.

Umbono wami wawuwukuthi wawukhona
(Ngaphambi kokuba abe nalo mkhuba)
Isithiyo esaqhamuka phakathi
Yena, nathi uqobo, nakho.

Ungamazisi ukuthi ubathanda kakhulu,
Ngoba lokhu kumele kube njalo
Imfihlo, egcinwe kubo bonke abanye,
Phakathi kwami nawe."

“Lobo bufakazi obubalulekile esesibuzwile,” kusho iNkosi ihlikihla izandla; "Manje vumela ijaji-"

“Uma noma yimuphi kubo engakuchaza,” kusho u-Alice, (wayekhule kakhulu emizuzwini embalwa edlule kangangokuthi wayengesabi nakancane ukumphazamisa), “ngizomnika u-sixpence. Angikholwa ukuthi kune-athomu yencazelo kuyo.”

Amajaji wonke abhala phansi eziletini zawo, “ Akakholelwa ukuthi kune-athomu yencazelo kuwo,” kodwa akekho kubo owazama ukuchaza iphepha.

“Uma ingekho incazelo kuyo,” kusho iNkosi, “okusindisa izwe elinobunzima, niyazi, njengoba kungadingeki sizame ukukuthola.” Nokho-ke angazi,” yaqhubeka, yendlala amavesi edolweni, iwabuka ngelinye iso; “Kubukeka sengathi ngibona incazelo ethile kuzo, phela.” “— ngathi angikwazi ukubhukuda— ” awukwazi ukubhukuda, akunjalo?” wanezela, ephendukela kuQobo.

Anikine ikhanda ngokudabukisa uKnave. “Ngabe ngifana nayo?” wathi. (Okuyiqiniso ukuthi akazange , enziwe ngekhadibhodi ngokuphelele.)

“Kulungile, kuze kube manje,” kusho iNkosi, futhi yaqhubeka ibubula ngala mavesi: “’ Siyazi ukuthi kuyiqiniso— ’ lelo yijaji, kunjalo—’ Ngimnike eyodwa, bamnika amabili— ’ kungani, kufanele kube yilokho eyakwenza ngama-tarts, niyazi—”

"Kodwa, kuyaqhubeka ' bonke babuya kuye beza kuwe ," kusho u-Alice.

“Ngani, nampa!” Kusho iNkosi ngokunqoba ikhomba amapheshana aphezu kwetafula. “Akukho okungacaca ngaphezu kwalokho . Bese futhi—’ ngaphambi kokuba alingane —’ awukaze ube nokulingana, sithandwa sami, ngiyacabanga?” Kusho uNdlovukazi.

“Ungalokothi!” Kusho uQueen ngokucasuka ephose inkinki kuNtuli ekhuluma. (UBill omncane onebhadi wayeyekile ukubhala esileyitini sakhe ngomunwe owodwa, njengoba engazange aphawule; kodwa manje waqala ngokuphangisa futhi, esebenzisa uyinki, owawuconsa ebusweni bakhe, inqobo nje uma uqhubeka.)

“Khona-ke amagama awakuphathi kahle ,” kusho iNkosi, iqalaza inkundla imamatheka. Kwathula kwathi cwaka.

“Kuyi-pun!” Inkosi yanezela ngezwi elicasukile, futhi wonke umuntu wahleka, "Ijaji alicabangele isinqumo sabo." kusho iNkosi, cishe isikhathi samashumi amabili ngalolo suku.

“Cha, cha!” Kusho uNdlovukazi. "Isigwebo kuqala - isinqumo kamuva."

“Izinto nombhedo!” Kusho u-Alice ekhulumela phezulu. "Umqondo wokuba nesigwebo kuqala!"

“Bamba ulimi lwakho!” Kusho uQueen ephenduka onsomi.

“Ngeke!” Kusho u-Alice.

“Ahlukane nekhanda lakhe!” kumemeza iNdlovukazi ngelizwi eliphezulu. Akekho onyakazayo.

“Ubani okukhathalelayo?” kusho u-Alice, (wayesekhule waze wagcwala ngalesi sikhathi). “Awulutho ngaphandle kwephakethe lamakhadi!”

Kulokhu iphakethe lonke lenyukela emoyeni, leza phezu kwakhe: wamemeza kancane, ingxenye yokwethuka nengxenye yentukuthelo, futhi wazama ukubashaya, wazithola elele ogwini, ikhanda lakhe lisemathangeni kadadewabo, owayebhulabhula kancane amaqabunga afile ayephaphazele phansi ezihlahleni ebusweni bakhe.

"Vuka, Alice sithandwa!" kusho udadewabo; “Ngani, ubuthongo obude kangakanani!”

“O, ngibe nephupho elimangalisayo!” kusho u-Alice, wabe esetshela udadewabo, njengoba wayebakhumbula, zonke lezi Zigigaba zakhe ezixakile obukade ufunda ngazo; kwathi lapho eseqedile, udadewabo wamanga, futhi wathi, “ Bekuyiphupho elimangalisayo, sithandwa, impela: kodwa manje gijima uye etiyeni lakho; Ngakho u-Alice wasukuma wabaleka, ecabanga ngenkathi egijima, futhi kungenzeka, yeka ukuthi bekuyiphupho elimangalisayo kangakanani.

Kepha udadewabo wahlala wathula nje lapho emshiya, encikise ikhanda lakhe esandleni sakhe, ebuka ukushona kwelanga, futhi ecabanga ngo-Alice omncane nazo zonke izigigaba zakhe ezimangalisayo, waze naye waqala ukuphupha imfashini, futhi leli kwakuyiphupho lakhe:—

Okokuqala, waphupha u-Alice omncane ngokwakhe, futhi izandla ezincanyana zaphinde zagaxwa edolweni lakhe, futhi amehlo akhanyayo alangazelele ayebheke phezulu kuye—wayewezwa wona kanye amaphimbo ezwi lakhe, futhi abone lokho kuzulazula okuncane kwekhanda lakhe ukuze avimbe izinwele ezizulazulayo ezazihlala zingena emehlweni akhe—futhi njengoba elalele, noma ebonakala elalele, yonke indawo ezungeza iphupho likadadewabo omncane ephila.

Utshani obude bahuquza ezinyaweni zakhe njengoba uNogwaja Omhlophe edlula edlula—iGundane elethukile lagxaza echibini elingumakhelwane—lalizwa ukukhala kwezinkomishi zetiye njengoba uMashi Hare nabangane bakhe behlanganyela isidlo sabo esingapheli, nezwi elihlabayo leNdlovukazi liyala izivakashi zakhe ezineshwa ukuba zibulawe—futhi kuqhume idolo kwabakwaDuchess. kwaphahlazeka izitsha nxazonke kuyo—kwaphinda futhi ukuhayiza kweGryphon, ukunswininiza kwe-slate pensela kaLizi, kanye nokuklinywa kwamagwinya acindezelwe, kwagcwala umoya, kuhlangene nokukhala okukude koFudu olulusizi.

Ngakho-ke wahlala, enamehlo avaliwe, futhi ingxenye yakholelwa ukuthi i-Wonderland, nakuba ayazi ukuthi wayezowavula futhi, futhi konke kwakuzoshintsha kube yiqiniso elifiphele - utshani buzobe bunyakaza kuphela emoyeni, futhi ichibi ligeleza linyakaze umhlanga - izinkomishi zetiye ezikhencezayo zazizoshintsha zibe izinsimbi zezimvu ezikhencezayo, futhi izwi leNdlovukazi lizwakalisa izwi lomfana. umntwana, ukuklabalasa kweGryphon, kanye nayo yonke eminye imisindo engajwayelekile, yayizoshintsha (yayazi) ibe umsindo ophitheneyo wegceke lepulazi elimatasa—ngenkathi ukukhala kwezinkomo kude kuthathe indawo yokukhala okunzima koFudu.

Okokugcina, uzibonele ngeso lengqondo ukuthi lo dade wabo omncane wayezoba kanjani, ngokuhamba kwesikhathi, abe ngowesifazane osekhulile; nokuthi wayezoyigcina kanjani, kuyo yonke iminyaka yakhe evuthiwe, inhliziyo elula nenothando yobuntwana bakhe: nokuthi wayezoqoqa kanjani mayelana nabanye abantwana bakhe abancane, futhi enze amehlo abo akhanye futhi alangazelele ngezindaba eziningi eziyinqaba, mhlawumbe ngisho nephupho le-Wonderland lakudala: nokuthi wayezozizwa kanjani ngalo lonke usizi lwabo olulula, futhi athole injabulo kuyo yonke intokozo yabo elula, ekhumbula izinsuku zakhe zehlobo ezijabulisayo, kanye nezinsuku ezijabulisayo zasehlobo.
