ISAHLUKO I.
Phansi Kwembobo Yogwaja

U-Alice wayeseqala ukukhathala ngokuhlala nodadewabo ebhange, nokungabi nalutho alwenza: kanye noma kabili wayeke walunguza encwadini udadewabo ayeyifunda, kodwa yayingenazo izithombe noma izingxoxo kuyo, “futhi isiza ngani incwadi,” kucabanga u-Alice “ngaphandle kwezithombe noma izingxoxo?”

Ngakho wayecabanga engqondweni yakhe (njengoba ayengakwenza, ngoba usuku olushisayo lwamenza wazizwa elele futhi eyisiphukuphuku), ukuthi ubumnandi bokwenza iketango le-daisy-daisy kungaba yini inkinga yokusukuma futhi acoshe ama-daisy, lapho kungazelelwe unogwaja oMhlophe onamehlo apinki ugijima eduze kwakhe.

Kwakungekho lutho olwaluphawuleka kakhulu kulokho; futhi u-Alice akazange acabange ukuthi kwakukude kakhulu ukuzwa uMvundla ekhuluma yedwa, “Oh sthandwa! (lapho ecabanga kamuva, kwafika kuye ukuthi bekufanele amangale ngalokhu, kodwa ngaleso sikhathi konke kwakubonakala kungokwemvelo); kodwa ngesikhathi uNogwaja ekhipha iwashi ekhukhwini lejazi lakhe , walibuka, wase egijima, u-Alice waqala ukuma ngezinyawo, ngoba kwakhanya engqondweni yakhe ukuthi wayengakaze abone unogwaja ephethe isikhwama sejazi, noma iwashi azolikhipha, futhi evutha ilukuluku lokufuna ukwazi, wagijima wanqamula inkundla ukuze awubone. imbobo kanogwaja ngaphansi kothango.

Ngomunye umzuzu wehla u-Alice wayilandela, akakaze acabange ukuthi uzophinde aphume kanjani emhlabeni.

Imbobo kanogwaja yaqonda ngqo njengomhubhe ngandlela thize, yabe isicwilisa phansi ngokuzumayo, ngokuphazima kweso kangangokuthi u-Alice akabanga naso isikhathi sokucabanga ukuzimisa ngaphambi kokuba azithole ewela phansi emthonjeni ojule kakhulu.

Kuphakathi kokuthi umthombo wawujule kakhulu, noma wawa kancane kancane, ngoba wayenesikhathi esiningi ehla ebheka ngakuye futhi ezibuza ukuthi kuzokwenzekani ngokulandelayo. Okokuqala, wazama ukubheka phansi ukuze abone ukuthi uyeza, kodwa kwakumnyama kakhulu angaboni lutho; wabe esebheka izinhlangothi zomthombo, futhi waqaphela ukuthi ayegcwele amakhabethe namashalofu ezincwadi; lapha nalaphaya wabona amamephu nezithombe kulenga ezikhonkwaneni. Wehlisa imbiza kwelinye lamashalofu edlula; yayibhalwe ukuthi “ORANGE MARMALADE”, kodwa ngokudumala kwakhe yayingenalutho: wayengathandi ukuwisa imbiza ngoba esaba ukubulala umuntu ngaphansi, ngakho wakwazi ukuyibeka kwelinye lamakhabethe ngesikhathi ewela eduze kwayo.

“Kuhle!” kucabanga u-Alice ezitshela ukuthi, “Ngemva kokuwa okungaka, ngeke ngicabange ukuwela phansi ezitebhisini! (Okungenzeka ukuthi kwakuyiqiniso.)

Phansi, phansi, phansi. Ingabe ukuwa kwakungeke kuphele? “Kazi mangaki amamayela esengwile ngalesi sikhathi?” esho ephimisela. “Kumelwe ngibe ngifika endaweni ethile enkabeni yomhlaba. Ake ngibone: lokho bekungaba amamayela ayizinkulungwane ezine phansi, ngicabanga—” (ngoba, uyabona, u-Alice wayefunde izinto eziningana zalolu hlobo ezifundweni zakhe esikoleni, futhi nakuba leli kwakungelona ithuba elihle kakhulu lokubonisa ulwazi lwakhe, njengoba kwakungekho muntu owayezomlalela, noma kunjalo kwakuwumkhuba omuhle ukusho njalo) “—yebo, i-Latitude engangiyithola ngaleso sikhathi noma i-Longitude—ngaleso sikhathi kwakungeyona yini iLatitude?” (U-Alice wayengazi ukuthi iyini i-Latitude, noma i-Longitude, kodwa wacabanga ukuthi kungamagama amahle angawasho.)

Manje waqala futhi. “Kazi noma ngizowela phansi emhlabeni! Yeka ukuthi kuzobonakala kuhlekisa kanjani ukuphuma phakathi kwabantu abahamba bebhekise amakhanda abo phansi! Ama-Antipathies, ngiyacabanga—” (wayejabule ngokuthi kwakungekho noyedwa owayelalele, kulokhu, njengoba kwakungezwakali nhlobo igama elifanele) “—kodwa kuzodingeka ngibabuze ukuthi liyini igama lezwe, uyazi. Ngiyacela, Mama noma i-Australia, iyiNew Zealand?” (futhi wazama ukuzigwegwisa njengoba ekhuluma—esho amazwi ahlabayo njengoba uwela emoyeni! Ucabanga ukuthi ungakwazi ukuwulawula?) “Futhi yeka ukuthi intombazanyana engazi lutho izongicabangela ngokungibuza yona!

Phansi, phansi, phansi. Kwakungekho okunye ayengakwenza, ngakho u-Alice ngokushesha waqala ukukhuluma futhi. “UDinah uzongikhumbula kakhulu ebusuku, kufanele ngicabange!” (UDinah wayeyikati.) “Ngiyethemba bazolikhumbula isoso lakhe lobisi ngesikhathi setiye. Dinah sthandwa sami! Ngifisa sengathi ngabe ubunami lapha phansi! Awekho amagundane emoyeni, ngiyesaba, kodwa ungase ubambe ilulwane, futhi lokho kufana negundane, uyazi. Kodwa ingabe amakati adla amalulwane, ngiyamangala? Futhi lapha u-Alice waqala ukuba nobuthongo, futhi waqhubeka ezitshela, ngendlela ephuphayo, "Ingabe amakati adla amalulwane? Ingabe amakati adla amalulwane?" futhi ngezinye izikhathi, “Ingabe amalulwane ayawadla amakati?” ngokuba, niyabo, njengoba ayengenakuwuphendula noma yimuphi umbuzo, akubanga nandaba kangako ukuthi wawubeka ngaphi. Wezwa ukuthi uyozela, futhi wayesanda kuqala ukuphupha ehamba noDina bebambene ngesandla, futhi ethi kuye ngokuqiniseka okukhulu, “Manje, Dina, ngitshele iqiniso: Wake wadla ilulwane na? lapho kungazelelwe, qhuma! thupha! phansi lafika phezu kwenqwaba yezinti namaqabunga omile, futhi ukuwa kwase kuphelile.

U-Alice akazange alimale nakancane, futhi wagxuma wema ngezinyawo zakhe ngomzuzwana: wabheka phezulu, kodwa konke kwakumnyama phezulu; phambi kwakhe kwakunelinye iphasiji elide, kanti uNogwaja oMhlophe wayesabonakala, ehla eshesha. Kwakungekho mzuzu wokulahleka: u-Alice wahamba njengomoya, futhi wayesesikhathini sokuyizwa ithi, lapho ijika ekhoneni, "Oh izindlebe zami namadevu, yeka ukuthi sekuhlwile!" Wayeseseduze ngemva kwawo lapho ejika ekhoneni, kodwa uMvundla wayengasabonwa: wazithola esehholo elide, eliphansi, elalikhanyiswe uhide lwamalambu ayelenga ophahleni.

Kwakukhona iminyango nxazonke zehholo, kodwa yonke yayihluthulelwe; kwathi lapho u-Alice elokhu ehle njalo ngapha nangapha, ezama yonke iminyango, wahamba ngokudabukisayo wehla phakathi nendawo, ezibuza ukuthi uzophinde aphume kanjani futhi.

Kuthe kusenjalo wazithela phezu kwetafula elincane elinemilenze emithathu, elakhiwe ngengilazi eqinile; kwakungekho lutho kuyo ngaphandle kokhiye omncane wegolide, futhi umcabango ka-Alice wokuqala wawuwukuthi ingase ingeyomunye weminyango yehholo; kodwa, maye! noma izingidi zazinkulu kakhulu, noma ukhiye wawumncane kakhulu, kodwa noma yikuphi wawungeke uvule noma iyiphi yazo. Nokho, emzuliswaneni wesibili, wazithela phezu kwekhethini eliphansi ayengazange aliqaphele ngaphambili, futhi ngemva kwalo kwakunomnyango omncane ongaba amasentimitha ayishumi nanhlanu ukuphakama: wazama ukhiye omncane wegolide kukhiye, futhi ngokujabula kwakhe wawungena!

U-Alice wavula umnyango futhi wathola ukuthi wawuholela ephasejini elincane, elingelikhulu kangako kunembobo yamagundane: waguqa wabheka ephasejini elingena engadini enhle kunazo zonke owake wazibona. Wayekulangazelela kanjani ukuphuma kulelohholo elimnyama, futhi azulazule phakathi kwaleyo mibhede yezimbali ezikhanyayo naleyo mithombo epholile, kodwa wayengakwazi ngisho nokukhipha ikhanda lakhe emnyango; U-Alice ompofu wacabanga: “Futhi noma ikhanda lami belingase lidlule, bekungaba usizo oluncane kakhulu ngaphandle kwamahlombe ami.” O, yeka ukuthi ngifisa kanjani ukuthi ngithule njengesibonakude! Ngoba, uyabona, izinto eziningi eziphuma endleleni ezenzekile kamuva nje, kangangokuthi u-Alice wayeseqale ukucabanga ukuthi zimbalwa kakhulu izinto ezingenakwenzeka ngempela.

Kwakubonakala kungenamsebenzi wokulinda ngasemnyango omncane, ngakho waphindela etafuleni, ingxenye yethemba lokuthi angase athole omunye ukhiye kuwo, noma nakanjani incwadi yemithetho yokuvala abantu njengezibonakude: kulokhu wathola ibhodlela elincane kuwo, (“owayengekho ngempela ngaphambili,” kusho u-Alice,) futhi entanyeni yebhodlela kwakukhona ilebula yephepha, enamagama amahle athi “DRINK” ngombhalo omkhulu othi “DRINK”.

Kwakukuhle kakhulu ukuthi “Ngiphuzeni,” kodwa u-Alice omncane ohlakaniphile wayengeke akwenze lokho ngokuxhamazela. “Cha, ngizoqala ngibheke,” kusho yena, “futhi ngibone ukuthi kubhalwe ukuthi ' ushevu ' noma qha”; ngoba wayefunde imilando eminingana emihle emincane ephathelene nezingane ezazishisiwe, futhi zadliwa yizilwane zasendle nezinye izinto ezingemnandi, konke ngenxa yokuthi zazingeke ziyikhumbule imithetho elula ababeyifundiswe abangane babo: njengokuthi, ukuthi i-red-hot poker izokushisa uma uyibamba isikhathi eside; nokuthi uma usike umunwe wakho ujule kakhulu ngommese, ngokuvamile wopha; futhi wayengakaze akhohlwe ukuthi, uma uphuza kakhulu ebhodleleni elibhalwe “ushevu,” cishe nakanjani ngeke uvumelane nawe, ngokushesha noma kamuva.

Nokho, leli bhodlela lalingaphawulwanga ngokuthi “ubuthi,” ngakho u-Alice wazama ukulinambitha, futhi walithola limnandi kakhulu, (empeleni lalinohlobo oluthile lwephunga elixubile le-cherry-tart, i-custard, uphayina-apula, i-turkey eyosiwe, ithofi, nesinkwa esishisayo esifakwe ibhotela,) ngokushesha waqeda.

“Yeka umuzwa wokufuna ukwazi!” kusho u-Alice; “Kufanele ngivale umlomo njengesibona-kude.”

Kwaba njalo ngempela: manje wayesengama-intshi ayishumi kuphela ukuphakama, futhi ubuso bakhe bukhazimula lapho ecabanga ukuthi wayeselingana nomnyango omncane ongena kuleya nsimu ethandekayo. Okokuqala, nokho, walinda imizuzu embalwa ukuze abone ukuthi wayezoncipha yini ngokuqhubekayo: wezwa enovalo oluncane ngalokhu; “Ngoba ingase iphele, uyazi,” kusho u-Alice ekhuluma yedwa, “ekuphumeni kwami ngokuphelele, njengekhandlela. Wazama nokufisa ukuthi ilangabi lekhandlela linjani ngemva kokucinywa kwekhandlela, ngoba wayengakhumbuli ukuthi wake wayibona into enjalo.

Ngemva kwesikhashana, ethola ukuthi akwenzekanga lutho, wanquma ukungena engadini ngaso leso sikhathi; kodwa, maye ngo-Alice ompofu! uma efika emnyango, wathola ukuthi ukhohliwe ukhiye omncane wegolide, wathi uma ebuyela etafuleni ewufuna, wathola ukuthi ngeke akwazi ukuwufinyelela: wawubona kahle ngengilazi, futhi wazama ngayo yonke indlela ukukhuphuka omunye wemilenze yetafula, kodwa wawushelela kakhulu; kwathi lapho esekhathele ngokuzama, umfokazana wahlala phansi wakhala.

“Woza, akusizi ukukhala kanjalo!” kusho u-Alice ekhuluma yedwa, ngokucijile; “Ngikweluleka ukuthi uhambe ngalomzuzu!” Ngokuvamile wayezinikeza iseluleko esihle kakhulu, (nakuba ayengavamile ukusilandela), futhi ngezinye izikhathi wayezithethisa kabuhlungu aze akhiphe izinyembezi emehlweni akhe; wake wakhumbula ezama ukuvala izindlebe zakhe ukuthi uzikhohlise emdlalweni wengwenya ayezidlalela, phela lengane ethanda ukwazi yayikuthanda kakhulu ukuzenza abantu ababili. “Kodwa akusizi manje,” kucabanga u-Alice ompofu, “ukuzenza abantu ababili !

Ngokushesha iso lakhe lawela ebhokisini elincane lengilazi elalingaphansi kwetafula: walivula, futhi wathola kulo ikhekhe elincane kakhulu, lapho amagama athi "NGIDLA" ayebhalwe kahle ngama-currants. “Hhayi-ke, ngizoyidla,” kusho u-Alice, “futhi uma ingenza ngikhule ngikhudlwana, ngingafinyelela ukhiye; futhi uma ingenza ngikhule ngibe mncane, ngingangena ngaphansi komnyango; ngakho-ke noma ngayiphi indlela ngizongena engadini, futhi anginendaba ukuthi kwenzekani!

Wadla kancane, futhi wakhuluma yedwa ngokukhathazeka, "Iyiphi indlela? Ngayiphi indlela?", ebambe isandla sakhe phezu kwekhanda lakhe ukuze ezwe ukuthi ikhula ngayiphi indlela, futhi wamangala kakhulu ukuthola ukuthi uhlala enobukhulu obufanayo: ngokuqinisekile, lokhu ngokuvamile kwenzeka lapho umuntu edla ikhekhe, kodwa u-Alice wayengene kakhulu endleleni yokulindela lutho ngaphandle kokuphuma-endleleni okwakubonakala sengathi izinto eziwubuwula zenzeka ekuphileni okujwayelekile.

Ngakho waqala ukusebenza, futhi ngokushesha waqeda ikhekhe.
