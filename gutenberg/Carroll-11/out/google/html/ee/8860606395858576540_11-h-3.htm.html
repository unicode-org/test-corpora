TA III.
Caucus-Duɖimekeke Kple Ŋutinya Legbe aɖe

Wonye kplɔ̃ɖoƒe si ƒe dzedzeme to vovo, si ƒoa ƒu ɖe ƒuta vavã—xevi siwo ƒe fuwo le hehem, lã siwo ƒe fuwo lé ɖe wo ŋu kplikplikpli, eye wo katã wole tsi ƒum le tsi, atitsoga, eye woƒe dzi mefa o.

Nyabiase gbãtɔe nye nyateƒe be, aleke woawɔ agaƒu ake: wowɔ aɖaŋuɖoɖo tso esia ŋu, eye le aɖabaƒoƒo ʋɛ aɖewo megbe la, edze abe ɖe wòsɔ le dzɔdzɔme nu na Alice be wòakpɔe be yele nu ƒom kpli wo nyuie ene, abe ɖe wònya wo le eƒe agbemeŋkekewo katã me ene. Nyateƒee, eʋli nya kple Lory la ɣeyiɣi didi aɖe ŋutɔ, amesi trɔ zu amesi ƒe dzi gbã mlɔeba, eye ɖeko wòagblɔ be, “Metsi wu wò, eye ele be manya nyuie wu;” eye Alice sia maɖe mɔ ne menya ƒe si wòxɔ o, eye esi Lory gbe le mɔ nyuitɔ nu be yemagblɔ ƒe si wòxɔ o ta la, nya bubu aɖeke megali woagblɔ o.

Mlɔeba la, Sisi, si dze abe ŋusẽtɔ le wo dome ene la, do ɣli be, “Mi katã mibɔbɔ nɔ anyi, eye miɖo tom! Eteƒe madidi o mana miaƒu nyuie!” Wo katã wobɔbɔ nɔ anyi zi ɖeka, le asigɛ gã aɖe me, eye Sisi le titina. Alice tsɔ dzitsitsi nɔ ŋku lém ɖe eŋu, elabena ese le eɖokui me be yeka ɖe edzi be yeaxɔ dzamezã vɔ̃ɖi ne yemeƒu kaba kura o.

“Ahem!” Mouse la gblɔ kple ya vevi aɖe be, “mi katã miele klaloa? Esiae nye nu ƒuƒuitɔ kekeake si menya. Ðoɖoezizi le goawo katã me, ne mielɔ̃! ‘Eteƒe medidi o Eŋlisiawo tsɔ William Aʋadziɖula, amesi ƒe nya la dze papa la ŋu, eye wòzu numame nɛ ŋutɔ be woaxɔ amewo le wo si ahaxɔ wo le wo si nyitsɔ laa. Edwin kple Morcar, Mercia kple Northumbria ƒe towo—’”

“Ugh!” Lory la gblɔ kple ʋuʋudedi.

“Meɖe kuku na wò be nàtsɔe ake!” Sisi la gblɔ kple movidzɔdzɔ, gake bubutɔe ŋutɔ be: “Èƒo nua?”

“Menye nyee o!” Lory la gblɔ kabakaba.

Sisi la gblɔ be: “Mesusu be èwɔe.” “—Meyi edzi. ‘Edwin kple Morcar, siwo nye Mercia kple Northumbria ƒe fiagãwo, ɖe gbeƒãe nɛ: eye Stigand, si nye Canterbury ƒe bisiɔpgã si lɔ̃a dulɔlɔ̃ gɔ̃ hã kpɔe be enyo—’”

“Woke ɖe nuka ŋu ?” Dada la gblɔ.

Mouse la ɖo eŋu kple atitsoga boŋ be: “Found it ,” “nyateƒee, ènya nusi ‘eƒe’ fia.”

Dadi la gblɔ be: “Menya nusi ‘eƒe’ fia nyuie, ne meke ɖe nane ŋu: zi geɖe la, akpɔkplɔ alo nudzodzoe ye wònye. Biabia lae nye be, nukae bisiɔpgã la ke ɖo?”

Mouse la mede dzesi biabia sia o, gake ewɔ kaba yi edzi be, “’—ekpɔe be enyo be yeayi kple Edgar Atheling aɖado go William atsɔ fiakukua anae. William ƒe agbenɔnɔ le gɔmedzedzea me sɔ. Gake eƒe Normantɔwo ƒe amemabumabu—’ Aleke nèle edzi yim fifia, lɔlɔ̃tɔ?” eyi edzi, eye wòtrɔ ɖe Alice ŋu esime wònɔ nu ƒom.

Alice gblɔ kple gbe si me nuxaxa le be: “Abe alesi wòtsi abe tsã ene: edze abe meƒuna kura o ene.”

Dodo la tso tsitre ɖe eƒe afɔwo dzi bubutɔe gblɔ be: “Le nɔnɔme ma me la, meɖe kuku be woahe kpekpea ɖe megbe, be woaxɔ atike siwo me ŋusẽ le wu enumake—”

“Do Eŋlisigbe!” Eaglet la gblɔe alea. “Nyemenya gɔmesese si le nya didi mawo ƒe afã ŋu o, eye, nusi gavloe wu la, nyemexɔe se be wò hã ènyae o!” Eye Hɔ̃ la bɔbɔ ta be yeaɣla alɔgbɔnukoko: xevi bubuawo dometɔ aɖewo nɔ ʋuʋum sesĩe.

Dodotɔwo gblɔ kple gbe si do dziku be: “Nya si magblɔe nye be, nu nyuitɔ si ana míaƒu kplakplakplae nye Caucus-duɖimekeke.”

“Nukae nye Caucus-duɖimekeke?” Alice gblɔ be; menye be edi be yeanya nu geɖe o, gake Dodo la tɔ vie abe ɖe wòsusu be ele be ame aɖe naƒo nu ene, eye edze abe ame bubu aɖeke medi be yeagblɔ nya aɖeke o ene.

Dodo la gblɔ be: “Nukatae, mɔ nyuitɔ si dzi woato aɖe emee nye be woawɔe.” (Eye, abe alesi wò ŋutɔ nàdi be yeate nu la kpɔ ene la, dzomeŋɔli ƒe ŋkeke aɖe dzi la, magblɔ alesi Dodo la te ŋu wɔe na wò.)

Gbã la, ede dzesi duɖimekekeƒe aɖe, le gotagome ƒomevi aɖe me, (“egblɔ be nɔnɔme si tututu le eme la mele vevie o,” eye emegbe wotsɔ kplɔ̃ɖoƒea katã da ɖe duɖimekekea to, le afisia kple afima. “Ame ɖeka, eve, etɔ̃, kple adzɔge” aɖeke menɔ anyi o, gake wodzea duƒuƒu gɔme ne wolɔ̃, eye wodzudzɔna ne wolɔ̃, ale be menɔa bɔbɔe be woanya ne duɖimekekea wu enu o. Ke hã, esi woƒu du gaƒoƒo afã alo esi wu nenema, eye wogaku kura ake la, kasia Dodotɔwo do ɣli be “Duɖimekekea wu enu!” eye wo katã woƒo xlãe henɔ gbɔgbɔm henɔ biabiam be, “Gake amekae ɖu dzi?”

Nyabiase sia mete ŋu ɖo eŋu ne womebu eŋu nyuie o, eye wòbɔbɔ nɔ anyi ɣeyiɣi didi aɖe eye wòtsɔ asibidɛ ɖeka te eƒe ŋgonu (nɔnɔme si me nèkpɔa Shakespeare le zi geɖe, le eƒe nɔnɔmetatawo me), esime ame mamlɛawo nɔ lalam le ɖoɖoezizi me. Mlɔeba Dodo la gblɔ be, “ Ame sia ame ɖu dzi, eye ele be fetu nanɔ wo katã si.”

“Gake amekae ana fetuawo?” gbewo ƒe haƒoha aɖe ŋutɔ bia.

Dodo la tsɔ asibidɛ ɖeka fia asi Alice be: “Nukatae, eya , nyateƒee; eye kplɔ̃ɖoƒe bliboa ƒo xlãe zi ɖeka, henɔ ɣli dom le mɔ tɔtɔ nu be, “Fetu! Fetu!”

Alice menya nusi wòawɔ o, eye le mɔkpɔkpɔbuɖeame ta la, etsɔ eƒe asi de eƒe kotoku me, eye wòhe kɔmitiwo ƒe aɖaka aɖe do goe, (dzɔgbenyuietɔe la, dzetsi la mege ɖe eme o), eye wòtsɔ wo ƒo xlãe abe fetu ene. A-piece ɖeka pɛpɛpɛ nɔ anyi, le goawo katã me.

Sisi la gblɔ be: “Gake ele be fetu nanɔ eya ŋutɔ si, ènya.”

Dodo la ɖo eŋu vevie ŋutɔ be: “Nyateƒee.” “Nukae gade wò kotoku me?” eyi edzi, eye wòtrɔ ɖe Alice ŋu.

Alice gblɔ nublanuitɔe be: “Abɔta ɖeka koe.”

Dodo la gblɔ be: “Tsɔe de asi na afisia.”

Emegbe wo katã wogaƒo zi ɖe eŋu zi ɖeka ake, esime Dodo la tsɔ asibidɛ la ɖo ŋkume bubutɔe, henɔ gbɔgblɔm be “Míele kuku ɖem na mi be mialɔ̃ ɖe asibidɛ dzeani sia dzi;” eye esi wòwu nuƒo kpui sia nu la, wo katã wodo dzidzɔɣli.

Alice bu nya bliboa be susu mele eme kura o, gake wo katã wodze abe ɖe wole vevie dom nu ene ale gbegbe be metsɔ dzideƒo ko nu o; eye esi mete ŋu bu nya aɖeke ŋu be yeagblɔ o ta la, ɖeko wòbɔbɔ, eye wòtsɔ asibidɛa, eye wòdze bubutɔe alesi wòate ŋui.

Nusi kplɔe ɖoe nye be woaɖu kɔmewuawo: esia he toɣliɖeɖe kple tɔtɔ aɖewo vɛ, esime xe gãawo nɔ nyatoƒoe tom be yewomate ŋu aɖɔ yewo tɔ o, eye sueawo ƒe nu ƒona eye wòhiã be woaƒo akɔta. Ke hã, ewu enu mlɔeba, eye wogabɔbɔ nɔ kɔsɔkɔsɔ me, eye woɖe kuku na Sisi la be wòagblɔ nya bubu aɖe na yewo.

Alice gblɔ be: “Èdo ŋugbe be yeagblɔ yeƒe ŋutinya nam, ènya, kple nusitae wònye nèlé fui—C kple D,” egblɔ kpee le ɣlidodo me, eye wònɔ vɔvɔ̃m afã kple afã be agado dziku nɛ ake.

“Tɔnye nye ŋutinya didi aɖe si wɔ nublanui!” Sisi la gblɔ, eye wòtrɔ ɖe Alice ŋu, eye wònɔ hũ ɖem.

Sisi la do ɣli sesĩe eye wòdo dziku vevie be: “ Nyemewɔe o!

“Kpo aɖe!” Alice gblɔ esi wònɔ klalo ɣesiaɣi be yeana ye ɖokui naɖe vi, eye wònɔ ŋku lém ɖe eŋu dzitsitsitɔe. “Oo, na makpe asi ɖe eŋu be maɖee ɖa!”

Sisi la tso hedzo yi ɖagblɔ be: “Nyemawɔ naneke ma tɔgbe o.” “Èdoa vlom to nya dzodzro mawo gbɔgblɔ me!”

“Menye ɖe meɖoe be magblɔe o!” ɖe kuku na Alice dahe la. “Gake edoa dziku na wò bɔbɔe ale gbegbe, ènya!”

Ðeko Sisi la ƒo nu kple ɣli tsɔ ɖo eŋu nɛ.

“Meɖe kuku, trɔ va nàwu wò ŋutinyaa nu!” Alice yɔ eyome; eye ame mamlɛawo katã wɔ ɖeka le haƒoƒo me be, “Ẽ, taflatse wɔe!” gake ɖeko Sisi la ʋuʋu ta dzimagbɔɖitɔe, eye wòzɔ kabakaba vie.

“Nublanui ka gbegbee nye si manɔ anyi o!” Lory la ƒo nu kple hũɖeɖe, esi wònya do le ame ŋkume kura ko; eye Kpakpaluʋui tsitsi aɖe tsɔ mɔnukpɔkpɔa gblɔ na vianyɔnua be “Ah, nye lɔlɔ̃tɔ! Na esia nanye nusɔsrɔ̃ na wò be màgado dziku gbeɖe o!” “Lé wò aɖe, Ma!” Crab-vi la gblɔ kple dzitsitsi vie. “Èsɔ gbɔ be nàte oyster ƒe dzigbɔɖi kpɔ!”

“Ne ɖe míaƒe Dina nanɔ afisia hafi, menyae be mele afisia!” Alice gblɔ sesĩe, eye meƒo nu na ame aɖeke koŋ o. “Eteƒe madidi o agbugbɔe vɛ!”

“Eye amekae nye Dina, ne mate ŋu atsɔ dzideƒo abia nya la?” Lory la gblɔe alea.

Alice ɖo eŋu kple didi vevie, elabena enɔa klalo ɣesiaɣi be yeaƒo nu tso yeƒe aƒemelã ŋu be: “Dinah nye míaƒe avu. Eye enye fiadu ale gbegbe le sisiblisi léle si màte ŋu abu o ta! Eye oo, edzrom be nàkpɔe le xeviawo megbe! Nukatae, aɖu xevi sue aɖe ne ekpɔe ko!”

Nuƒo sia he seselelãme ɖedzesi aɖe va kplɔ̃ɖoƒea me tɔwo. Xeviawo dometɔ aɖewo ƒu du dzo zi ɖeka: Magpie tsitsi ɖeka te eɖokui xatsa nyuie ŋutɔ, hede dzesii be, “Le nyateƒe me la, anɔ eme be maɖo aƒeme; zã me ya mesɔ na nye ŋɔti o!” eye Canary aɖe do ɣli kple gbe si le ʋuʋum na viawo be, “Midzo, nye lɔlɔ̃tɔwo! Ɣeyiɣia de be mi katã mianɔ aba dzi!” Wo katã woʋu le aʋatsonya vovovowo ta, eye eteƒe medidi o Alice ɖeka koe susɔ.

“Ne ɖe nyemeƒo nu tso Dina ŋu o la, anye ne!” egblɔ na eɖokui kple gbe si me nuxaxa le. “Edze abe ame aɖeke melɔ̃nɛ o ene, le anyigba le afisia, eye meka ɖe edzi be eyae nye avu nyuitɔ kekeake le xexeame! Oo, nye lɔlɔ̃tɔ Dina! Mebiaa ɖokuinye be ɖe magakpɔ wò azɔ hã!” Eye le afisia la, Alice dahe la gadze avifafa gɔme ake, elabena ese le eɖokui me be yetsi akogo ŋutɔ eye yeƒe dzi bɔbɔ. Gake le ɣeyiɣi kpui aɖe megbe la, egase afɔɖeɖewo ƒe ɖiɖi vie le adzɔge, eye wòfɔ mo dzi kple didi vevie, kple mɔkpɔkpɔ afã be Sisi la trɔ eƒe susu, eye wòtrɔ gbɔna be yeawu yeƒe ŋutinyaa nu.
