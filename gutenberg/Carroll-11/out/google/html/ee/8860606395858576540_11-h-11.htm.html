TA XI.
Amekae Fi Tart-awo?

Dziwo ƒe Fia kple Fianyɔnu nɔ anyi ɖe woƒe fiazikpui dzi esi wova ɖo, eye ameha gã aɖe ƒo ƒu ɖe wo ŋu—xevi suewo kple lã ƒomevi ɖesiaɖe, kpakple agbalẽvi bliboa: Knave la nɔ tsitre ɖe wo ŋgɔ, le kɔsɔkɔsɔ me, eye asrafo aɖe nɔ eƒe axa ɖesiaɖe be wòadzɔ edzi; eye Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la te ɖe Fia la ŋu, eye kpẽ le asi ɖeka me, eye lãgbalẽ xatsaxatsa le asi evelia me. Kplɔ̃ aɖe nɔ ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea titina tututu, eye wotsɔ tart-gba gã aɖe ɖo edzi: woƒe dzedzeme nyo ale gbegbe, be dɔ wu Alice ŋutɔ be wòalé ŋku ɖe wo ŋu—esusu be: “Ne ɖe woawu ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃a nu hafi, eye woatsɔ nuɖuɖu siwo naa nuɖuɖu viviwo la aƒo xlãe!” Gake edze abe mɔnukpɔkpɔ aɖeke meli be esia nadzɔ o ene, eyata edze nusianu si ku ɖe eŋu la kpɔkpɔ gɔme, be yeato ɣeyiɣia me.

Alice meyi ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒe kpɔ o, gake exlẽ nu tso wo ŋu le agbalẽwo me, eye edzɔ dzi nɛ ŋutɔ esi wòkpɔe be yenya nusianu kloe si le afima ƒe ŋkɔ. Egblɔ na eɖokui be: “Ʋɔnudrɔ̃la lae nye ema, le eƒe wig gã la ta.”

Le mɔa nu la, ʋɔnudrɔ̃la lae nye Fia la; eye esi wòdo eƒe fiakuku ɖe wig la dzi la, (kpɔ ŋgɔgbekpa dzi ne èdi be yeakpɔ alesi wòwɔe,) medze abe ɖe wòfa kura o ene, eye kakaɖedzitɔe la, menɔ dzedzem o.

Alice bu eŋu be: “Eye emae nye adaŋudeha la, kple nuwɔwɔ wuieve mawo,” (enye agbanɔamedzi nɛ be wòagblɔ be “nuwɔwɔwo,” èkpɔe, elabena wo dometɔ aɖewo nye lãwo, eye ɖewo nye xewo,) “Mesusu be woawoe nye atamkalawo.” Egblɔ nya mamlɛtɔ sia zi eve alo zi etɔ̃ na eɖokui, eye wònye dada nɛ boŋ: elabena esusui, eye wòsɔ hã, be nyɔnuvi sue ʋɛ aɖewo koe le yeƒe ƒemetɔwo me nya gɔmesese si le eŋu kura. Gake “aʋatsoɖasefowo” hã awɔe nyuie nenema ke.

Atamkala wuieveawo katã nɔ nu ŋlɔm vevie le kpe gbadzɛwo dzi. “Nuka wɔm wole?” Alice ƒo nu na Gryphon la le ɣlidodo me. “Naneke mate ŋu anɔ wo si woatsɔ aɖo anyi haɖe o, hafi ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃a nadze egɔme.”

Gryphon la ɖo eŋu nɛ le ɣlidodo me be: “Wole woƒe ŋkɔwo ŋlɔm ɖi, le vɔvɔ̃ be woaŋlɔ wo be hafi ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃a nawu enu ta.”

“Bometsinyawo!” Alice dze egɔme kple gbe sesẽ si do dziku, gake etɔ kabakaba, elabena Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la do ɣli be, “Zi ɖoɖoe le xɔxɔnu!” eye Fia la do eƒe ŋkumewu eye wòtsɔ dzitsitsi kpɔ godoo, be yeanya amesi le nu ƒom.

Alice te ŋu kpɔe, abe ɖe wònɔ ŋku lém ɖe woƒe abɔta ene, be atamkalawo katã nɔ “bometsinyawo!” le woƒe xɔgbakpewo dzi, eye wòte ŋu kpɔe gɔ̃ hã be wo dometɔ ɖeka menya “bometsila” ŋɔŋlɔ o, eye be ele be wòabia tso eƒe aƒelika si be wòagblɔe nɛ. “Tɔtɔ nyui aɖe si me woƒe slatewo anɔ hafi ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃a nawu enu!” Alice susui.

Pensil si nɔ ɣli dom nɔ atamkalawo dometɔ ɖeka si. Esia ya, Alice mete ŋu nɔ te o , eye wòƒo xlã ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea hege ɖe megbe nɛ, eye eteƒe medidi kura o, ekpɔ mɔnukpɔkpɔ be yeaxɔe le esi. Ewɔe kaba ale gbegbe be atamkala sue dahe la (eyae nye Bill, Akpɔkplɔ la) mete ŋu de dzesi nusi va zu eŋu kura o; eyata esi wòde adelãe vɔ la, ele nɛ be wòatsɔ asibidɛ ɖeka aŋlɔ nu ŋkekea ƒe akpa mamlɛa; eye viɖe boo aɖeke menɔ esia ŋu o, elabena megblẽ dzesi aɖeke ɖe ʋuƒoa dzi o.

“Herald, xlẽ nutsotso la!” Fia la gblɔ.

Le esia dzi la, Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la ku kpẽa zi etɔ̃, eye emegbe wòʋu agbalẽ xatsaxatsa si wotsɔ lãgbalẽ wɔe la, eye wòxlẽ ale:—

“Dziwo ƒe Fianyɔnu, ewɔ tart aɖewo, .
Wo katã le dzomeŋɔli ŋkeke aɖe dzi:
Dziwo ƒe Adzodala la, efi tart mawo, .
Eye wòkplɔ wo dzoe keŋkeŋ!”

Fia la gblɔ na adaŋudeha la be: “Mibu miaƒe ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃a ŋu kpɔ.”

“Menye haɖe o, haɖe o!” Akpɔkplɔa tsɔ kaba tso nya me. “Nu gã aɖe gali si ava do ŋgɔ na ema!”

Fia la gblɔ be: “Miyɔ ɖasefo gbãtɔ; eye Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la ku kpẽa zi etɔ̃, eye wòdo ɣli be, “Ðasefo gbãtɔ!”

Ðasefo gbãtɔe nye Kpovitɔ. Etsɔ tiikplu ɖe asi ɖeka me eye wòtsɔ abolo kple bɔta kakɛ ɖe asi evelia me ge ɖe eme. Edze egɔme be: “Meɖe kuku, wò Aƒetɔ, le esi nètsɔ nusiawo vae ta: gake nyemewu nye tii nono nu tututu esime wodɔm vɛ o.”

Fia la gblɔ be: “Ðe wòle be nàwu enu hafi.” “Ɣekaɣie nèdze egɔme?”

Kpovitɔa lé ŋku ɖe March Hare si kplɔe ɖo yi ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea, eye wòtsɔ alɔnu kple Dormouse la lé ŋku ɖe eŋu. Egblɔ be: “March ƒe ŋkeke wuienelia, mesusu be nenemae wònɔ.”

March Hare gblɔ be: “Wuiatɔ̃lia.”

Dormouse la gblɔ kpee be: “Wuiadelia.”

Fia la gblɔ na adaŋudeha la be: “Miŋlɔ ema ɖi,” eye adaŋudeha la tsɔ didi vevie ŋlɔ ŋkeke etɔ̃awo katã ɖe woƒe agbalẽviwo dzi, eye emegbe woƒo wo nu ƒu, eye woɖe ŋuɖoɖoa dzi kpɔtɔ va ɖo shilling kple pence.

Fia la gblɔ na Kpovitɔ la be: “Ðe wò kposɔ.”

Kpovitɔ la gblɔ be: “Menye tɔnyee o.”

Fia la do ɣli be: “ Wofie!

Kpovitɔa gblɔ kpee be: “Medzraa wo ɖo be madzra; "Nyemekpɔ nye ŋutɔ tɔ aɖeke o. Nyee nye kpovitɔ."

Le afisia la, Fianyɔnua do eƒe ŋkumeɖobolo, eye wòdze ŋkuléle ɖe Kpovitɔ la ŋu, amesi ƒe mo biã eye wònɔ ʋuʋum.

Fia la gblɔ be: “Na wò ɖaseɖiɖi; “eye mègatsi dzodzodzoe o, ne menye nenema o la, mana woawu wò le teƒea.”

Edze abe esia mede dzi ƒo na ɖasefoa kura o ene: enɔ tɔtrɔm tso afɔ ɖeka dzi yi evelia dzi, nɔ Fianyɔnua kpɔm le dziɖeleameƒo me, eye le eƒe tɔtɔ me la, eɖu kakɛ gã aɖe le eƒe tiikplu me ɖe abolo kple bɔta teƒe.

Le ɣeyiɣi sia me tututu la, Alice se le eɖokui me be yedi be yeanya nu geɖe ŋutɔ, si tɔtɔe ŋutɔ vaseɖe esime wòkpɔ nusi wònye: egadze tsitsi gɔme ake, eye wòsusui gbã be yeafɔ adzo le ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea; gake esi wòbu eŋu zi evelia la, eɖoe be yeanɔ afisi yele zi alesi teƒe li nɛ ko.

Dormouse si bɔbɔ nɔ egbɔ gblɔ be: “Ne ɖe màmimie nenema o la, anye ne enyo ŋutɔ.” “Mete ŋu gbɔa ya kura o.”

Alice gblɔ tufafatɔe be: “Nyemate ŋu awɔ naneke o: “Mele tsitsim.”

Dormouse la gblɔ be: “Gome mele asiwò be nàtsi le afisia o .”

Alice gblɔ dzideƒotɔe wu be: “Mègagblɔ nya ƒaƒãwo o: “ènya be wò hã èle tsitsim.”

Dormouse la gblɔ be: “Ẽ, gake metsina le ablaɖeɖe si sɔ me: menye le fewuɖunya ma nu o.” Eye wòtso kple dziku ŋutɔ eye wòtso yi xɔxɔnua ƒe go kemɛ dzi.

Le ɣeyiɣi siawo katã me la, Fianyɔnua medzudzɔ ŋkuléle ɖe Kpovitɔa ŋu kpɔ o, eye esi Dormouse la tso ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea teti la, egblɔ na fiasãa ƒe amegãwo dometɔ ɖeka be, “Mitsɔ hadzila siwo le kɔnsɛt mamlɛtɔ me ƒe ŋkɔwo vɛ nam!” si dzi Kpovitɔ nublanuitɔ la ʋuʋu ale gbegbe be wòʋuʋu eƒe afɔkpa eveawo siaa.

Fia la gbugbɔ gblɔ kple dziku be: “Na wò ɖaseɖiɖi, ne menye nenema o la, mana woawu wò, eɖanye be ètsi dzodzodzoe alo mètsi dzodzodzoe o.”

Kpovitɔ la dze egɔme kple gbe si le ʋuʋum be: “Ame dahee menye, wò Aƒetɔ,—eye nyemedze nye tii nono gɔme haɖe o—menye kwasiɖa ɖeka alo esi wu nenema o—eye nukae nye esi abolo kple bɔta le sue alea gbegbe—kple tiia ƒe keklẽ—”

Fia la gblɔ be: “ Nuka ƒe keklẽ?

Kpovitɔa ɖo eŋu be: “Edze egɔme tso tiia dzi.”

“Nyateƒee, flitete dzea egɔme kple T!” Fia la gblɔ sesĩe. “Èxɔm abe dunce enea? Yi edzi!”

Kpovitɔ la yi edzi be: “Ame dahee menye, eye nu akpa gãtɔ klẽ le ema megbe—March Hare koe gblɔ—”

“Nyemewɔe o!” March Hare la tsɔ ablaɖeɖe gã aɖe tso nyaa me.

“Èwɔe!” Kpovitɔ la gblɔ.

“Megbee!” March Hare la gblɔe alea.

Fia la gblɔ be: “Egbee,” “gblẽ akpa ma ɖi.”

“Enyo, aleke kee wòɖale o, Dormouse la gblɔ be—” Kpovitɔ la yi edzi, eye wòtsɔ dzitsitsi nɔ ŋku lém ɖe eŋu godoo be yeakpɔe ɖa be ye hã yegbee hã: gake Dormouse la gbe naneke o, elabena edɔ alɔ̃ vevie.

Kpovitɔ la yi edzi be: “Le ema megbe la, megatso abolo kple bɔta—”

“Gake nya kae Dormouse la gblɔ?” adaŋudeha la dometɔ ɖeka bia be.

Kpovitɔ la gblɔ be: “Ema dzi nyemate ŋu aɖo ŋku edzi o.”

Fia la de dzesii be: “ Ele be nàɖo ŋku edzi, ne menye nenema o la, mana woawu wò.”

Kpovitɔ nublanuitɔ la tsɔ eƒe tiikplu kple abolo kple bɔta da ɖi, eye wòɖiɖi ɖe klo ɖeka dzi. Edze egɔme be: “Ame dahee menye, wò Aƒetɔ.”

Fia la gblɔ be: “ Nuƒola dahe ŋutɔe nènye .”

Le afisia la, guinea-haawo dometɔ ɖeka do dzidzɔɣli, eye ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea ƒe amegãwo te enu enumake. (Esi wònye nya sesẽ aɖe boŋue nye ema ta la, ɖeko maɖe alesi wowɔe me na mi. Akplo gã aɖe si wotsɔ avɔ wɔe nɔ wo si, si wotsɔ ka bla ɖe nu me: wotsɔ guinea-ha la ƒu gbe ɖe esia me, ta gbã, eye emegbe wobɔbɔ nɔ edzi.)

Alice bu eŋu be: “Edzɔ dzi nam be mekpɔe be wowɔ esia.” “Mexlẽe zi geɖe le nyadzɔdzɔgbalẽwo me, le ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃wo ƒe nuwuwu be, “Wodze agbagba aɖewo be woaƒo asikpe, si ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea ƒe amegãwo te ɖe to enumake,” eye nyemese nusi wòfia gɔme kpɔ vaseɖe fifia o.”

Fia la yi edzi be: “Ne ema koe nènya tso eŋu la, àte ŋu atsi tre.”

Kpovitɔ la gblɔ be: “Nyemate ŋu ayi anyime o: “Mele anyigba, abe alesi wòle ene.”

Fia la ɖo eŋu be: “Ekema àte ŋu anɔ anyi.”

Le afisia la, guinea-ha kemɛa do dzidzɔɣli, eye wozi edzi.

“Va, ema wu guinea-hawo nu!” Alice susui. “Azɔ míava nɔ anyi nyuie wu.”

Kpovitɔ la tsɔ dzitsitsi kpɔ Fianyɔnu si nɔ hadzilawo ƒe ŋkɔwo xlẽm la gblɔ be: “Anyo nam wu be mawu nye tii nu.”

Fia la gblɔ be: “Àte ŋu ayi,” eye Kpovitɔ la do le ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea kaba, eye melala gɔ̃ hã be yeado yeƒe afɔkpa o.

“—eye ɖeko wòɖe ta le gota,” Fianyɔnua gblɔ kpee na kpovitɔawo dometɔ ɖeka: gake Kpovitɔ la meganɔ ŋkume o hafi kpovitɔa te ŋu ɖo ʋɔtrua nu.

“Yɔ ɖasefo si kplɔe ɖo!” Fia la gblɔ.

Ðasefo si kplɔe ɖoe nye Fiavinyɔnua ƒe nuɖala. Etsɔ atadi-ɖaka la ɖe asi, eye Alice bu amesi wònye, hafi wòge ɖe ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea gɔ̃ hã, to alesi ame siwo te ɖe ʋɔtrua ŋu la te aɖuɖɔɖɔɖɔ zi ɖeka.

Fia la gblɔ be: “Na wò ɖaseɖiɖi.”

Nuɖala la gblɔ be: “Mawɔe o.”

Fia la tsɔ dzitsitsi kpɔ Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la, amesi gblɔ kple gbe bɔbɔe be, “Ele be Wò Aƒetɔ nabia gbe ɖasefo sia .”

Fia la gblɔ kple blanuiléle ƒe ya be: “Enyo, ne ele be mawɔe la, ele be mawɔe,” eye esi wòbla eƒe abɔwo hetsɔ moveviɖoɖo ɖe nuɖala la ŋu vaseɖe esime eƒe ŋkuwo megadze o kloe vɔ la, egblɔ kple gbe deto be, “Nukae wotsɔ wɔa tart?”

Nuɖala la gblɔ be: “Atadi, akpa gãtɔ.”

Gbe aɖe si le alɔ̃ dɔm le megbe nɛ gblɔ be: “Treacle.”

Fianyɔnua do ɣli be: “Collar that Dormouse.” “Tsɔ ta na Dormouse ma! Trɔ Dormouse ma do goe le ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒe! Tee ɖe to! Miƒoe! Mitsɔ eƒe ɖa!”

Ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea katã tɔtɔ aɖabaƒoƒo aɖewo, na Dormouse la trɔ, eye kaka woagava nɔ anyi ake la, nuɖala la bu.

"Megadee tame o!" Fia la gblɔ kple gbɔdzɔe gã aɖe. “Yɔ ɖasefo si kplɔe ɖo.” Eye wògblɔ kpee kple gbe si le ete na Fianyɔnua be, “Nyateƒee, lɔlɔ̃tɔ, ele be nàbia gbe ɖasefo si kplɔe ɖo. Ena nye ŋgonu le veve sem ŋutɔ!”

Alice nɔ Akpɔkplɔ Yeyea kpɔm esime wònɔ xexlẽdzesiawo me dzrom, eye wòdi vevie be yeanya alesi ɖasefo si akplɔe ɖo anɔ, “—elabena womekpɔ kpeɖodzi boo aɖeke haɖe o ,” egblɔ na eɖokui. Bu alesi wòwɔ nuku nɛ ŋu kpɔ, esime Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la xlẽ ŋkɔ “Alice!”
