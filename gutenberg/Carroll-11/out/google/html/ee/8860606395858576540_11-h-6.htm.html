TA VI.
Ha kple Atadi

Etsi tre aɖabaƒoƒo ɖeka alo eve nɔ aƒea kpɔm, eye wònɔ nusi wòawɔ kplɔe ɖo ŋu bum, esi kasia afɔzɔla aɖe si do awu ʋlaya la va nɔ du dzi tso atiawo me—(ebui be enye afɔzɔla elabena edo awu ʋlaya: ne menye nenema o la, ne míebu eƒe mo ɖeɖeko ŋu la, anye ne ayɔe be tɔmelã)—eye wòtsɔ eƒe abɔta ƒo ʋɔtrua sesĩe. Afɔzɔla bubu si do awu ʋlaya, si ƒe mo le goglo, eye eƒe ŋkuwo lolo abe akpɔkplɔ ene ye ʋui; eye Alice de dzesii be afɔzɔla eveawo siaa ƒe taɖa si wotsɔ aŋɔ wɔe si ƒo xlã woƒe ta katã. Ese le eɖokui me be yedi vevie be yeanya nusi ŋu wòku ɖo, eye wòge ɖe ati me vie be yeaɖo to.

Tɔmelã-Afɔzɔla la dze egɔme kple lɛta gã aɖe si lolo abe eya ŋutɔ ene kloe la wɔwɔ tso eƒe alɔnu, eye wòtsɔ esia de asi na ame kemɛa, hegblɔ, kple gbe bubutɔe be, “Na Fiavinyɔnua. Amekpekpe tso Fianyɔnua gbɔ be wòaƒo croquet.” Akpɔkplɔ-Afɔzɔla la gbugbɔ gblɔ, le gbe bubu ma ke me, ɖeko wòtrɔ nyawo ƒe ɖoɖo vie be, “Tso Fianyɔnua gbɔ. Amekpekpe na Fiavinyɔnua be wòaƒo croquet.”

Emegbe wo ame evea siaa bɔbɔ, eye woƒe ɖa ƒo ɖe wo nɔewo ŋu.

Alice ko nu le esia ta ale gbegbe be eva hiã be wòagaƒu du ayi avea me le vɔvɔ̃ be yewoasee; eye esi wògaɖe ŋku ɖe gota la, Tɔmelã-Afɔzɔla la megali o, eye evelia bɔbɔ nɔ anyigba le ʋɔtrua gbɔ, nɔ ŋku lém ɖe yame bometsitsitɔe.

Alice yi ʋɔa gbɔ vɔvɔ̃tɔe, eye wòƒo ʋɔa.

Afɔzɔla la gblɔ be: “Viɖe aɖeke mele ʋɔƒoƒo ŋu o, eye esia le susu eve ta. Gbã la, esi mele ʋɔtrua ƒe akpa ɖeka kple wò ta; evelia, esi wole toɣliɖeɖe ma tɔgbe wɔm le eme ta la, ame aɖeke mate ŋu ase wò o.” Eye kakaɖedzitɔe la, toɣliɖeɖe tɔxɛ aɖe ŋutɔ nɔ edzi yim le ememe—ɣlidodo kple aɖuɖɔtoe ɣesiaɣi, eye ɣeaɖewoɣi la, ʋuʋudedi gã aɖe nɔ edzi yim, abe ɖe wogbã nuɖugba alo tsize aɖe kakɛkakɛ ene.

Alice gblɔ be: “Ekema meɖe kuku, aleke mawɔ age ɖe eme?”

Afɔzɔla la yi edzi be: “Ate ŋu anye be gɔmesese aɖe anɔ wò ʋɔƒoƒoa me evɔ melé be nɛ o, nenye be ʋɔa le mía dome. Le kpɔɖeŋu me, ne èle eme , àte ŋu aƒo ʋɔa, eye mate ŋu aɖe asi le ŋuwò nàdo go, ènya.” Enɔ ŋku lém ɖe yame ɣesiaɣi si wònɔ nu ƒom, eye Alice sia susui be menye dukɔmevinyenye o. Egblɔ na eɖokui be: “Gake ɖewohĩ mate ŋu awɔ naneke o; “eƒe ŋkuwo gogo eƒe ta tame ale gbegbe . Gake aleke kee wòɖale o, ate ŋu aɖo nyabiasewo ŋu.—Aleke mawɔ age ɖe eme?” egbugbɔ gblɔe ake, sesĩe.

Afɔzɔla la de dzesii be: “Manɔ afisia vaseɖe etsɔ—”

Le ɣeyiɣi sia me la, aƒea ƒe ʋɔtru ʋu, eye agba gã aɖe va nɔ ʋuʋum do go, tẽe ɖe Afɔzɔla la ƒe ta ŋu: ɖeko wòɖu eƒe ŋɔti, eye wògbã ɖe ati siwo le megbe nɛ dometɔ ɖeka ŋu.

“—alo le ŋufɔke, ɖewohĩ,” Afɔzɔla la yi edzi kple gbe ma ke, abe ɖe naneke medzɔ tututu o ene.

“Aleke mawɔ age ɖe eme?” Alice gabiae ake, kple gbe si nu sẽ wu.

“ Ðe wòle be nàge ɖe eme kuraa?” Afɔzɔla la gblɔ. “Nyabiase gbãtɔe nye ema, ènya.”

Ðikeke mele eme o be enye: Alice koe melɔ̃ be woagblɔe na ye o. Egblɔ le eɖokui me be: “Edzi ŋɔ ŋutɔŋutɔ, alesi nuwɔwɔwo katã ʋlia nyae. Esɔ gbɔ be wòana ame ɖeka nazu tagbɔsesẽ!”

Edze abe Afɔzɔla la bu esia be enye mɔnukpɔkpɔ nyui aɖe be yeagbugbɔ yeƒe nya la agblɔ ene, eye wòto vovo. Egblɔ be: “Manɔ anyi ɖe afisia, ŋkeke kple ŋkeke.”

“Gake nukae mawɔ ?” Alice gblɔe alea.

Afɔzɔla la gblɔ be: “Nusianu si dze ŋuwò,” eye wòdze kpẽkuku gɔme.

Alice gblɔ kple mɔkpɔkpɔbuɖeame be: “Oo, viɖe aɖeke mele nuƒoƒo kplii me o: “enye bometsitsi blibo!” Eye wòʋu ʋɔa hege ɖe eme.

Ʋɔtrua kplɔa ame yia nuɖaƒe gã aɖe si me dzudzɔ yɔ fũu tso eƒe nuwuwu ɖeka yi bubu me tututu: Fiavinyɔnua bɔbɔ nɔ afɔdzideƒe si ƒe afɔ etɔ̃ le titina, nɔ no nam vidzĩ aɖe; nuɖala la nɔ dzoa dzi nɔ ze gã aɖe si dze abe mɔli yɔ eme ene la ʋuʋum.

“Atadi sɔ gbɔ akpa le mɔli ma me godoo!” Alice gblɔ na eɖokui, abe alesi wòate ŋui ene le aɖuɖɔtoe ta.

Kakaɖedzitɔe la, eƒe akpa gãtɔ nɔ yame. Fiavinyɔnua gɔ̃ hã ƒoa aɖuɖɔ ɣeaɖewoɣi; eye le vidzĩa gome la, enɔ aɖuɖɔ ƒom henɔ ɣli dom ɖe wo nɔewo yome evɔ metɔ vie o. Nusiwo ko le nuɖaƒea si meɖe aɖuɖɔ o, enye nuɖala, kple avu gã aɖe si bɔbɔ nɔ dzodoƒea henɔ nu kom tso to me yi to me.

Alice gblɔ kple vɔvɔ̃ vie be: “Meɖe kuku, àgblɔe nam,” elabena meka ɖe edzi tututu nenye be enye nɔnɔme nyui be yeaƒo nu gbã o, “nukatae wò avu ko alɔgbɔnu nenema?”

Fiavinyɔnua gblɔ be: “Cheshire avu aɖee, eye esia tae. Ha!”

Egblɔ nya mamlɛtɔ kple ŋutasẽnuwɔwɔ kpata ale gbegbe be Alice ti kpo kura; gake ekpɔe le ɣeyiɣi bubu me be vidzĩae woŋlɔe ɖo, ke menye ye o, eyata dzi ɖo eƒo, eye wògayi edzi ake be:—

“Nyemenya be Cheshire avuwo ko alɔgbɔnu ɣesiaɣi o, le nyateƒe me la, nyemenya be avuwo te ŋu koa nu o.”

Fiavinyɔnua gblɔ be: “Wo katã woate ŋui; “eye wo dometɔ akpa gãtɔ wɔnɛ.”

Alice gblɔ bubutɔe ŋutɔ, eye wòse le eɖokui me be edzɔ dzi nɛ ŋutɔ be yege ɖe dzeɖoɖo aɖe me be: “Nyemenya ɖeke si wɔa esia o.”

Fiavinyɔnua gblɔ be: “Mènya nu geɖe o; “eye nyateƒenyae nye ema.”

Alice melɔ̃ nya sia ƒe gbeɖiɖi kura o, eye wòsusu be anyo nenema ke be yeato dzeɖoɖo ƒe nyati bubu aɖe vɛ. Esi wònɔ agbagba dzem be yeaɖɔ ɖeka ɖo la, nuɖala la ɖe mɔli ƒe zea le dzoa dzi, eye enumake wòdze dɔ gɔme tsɔ nusianu si wòate ŋui la ƒu gbe ɖe Fiavinyɔnua kple vidzĩa ŋu—dzo-gawoe va gbã; emegbe wotsɔa tsigoewo, agbawo, kple nuɖugbawo ƒe tsilele kplɔe ɖo. Fiavinyɔnua mede dzesi wo o esime woƒoe gɔ̃ hã; eye vidzĩa nɔ ɣli dom ale gbegbe xoxo, ale gbegbe be manya wɔ kura be woagblɔ nenye be ƒoƒoawo vee alo mevee o.

“Oo, meɖe kuku, tsɔ ɖe le nu si wɔm nèle la me!” Alice do ɣli, eye wònɔ titim nɔ dzi yim nɔ anyime le vɔvɔ̃ ƒe vevesese me. “Oo, afi mae eƒe ŋɔti xɔasi la yina!” esime ze gã aɖe si mebɔ o nɔ dzodzom te ɖe eŋu, eye wògogoe ŋutɔ be wòtsɔe dzoe.

Fiavinyɔnua gblɔ kple ɣlidodo sesĩe be: “Ne ame sia ame tsia dzi ɖe eya ŋutɔ ƒe nyawo ŋu la, anye ne xexeame aƒo xlã nubabla aɖe kabakaba wu alesi wòwɔna.”

Alice, amesi se le eɖokui me be edzɔ dzi nɛ ŋutɔ be mɔnukpɔkpɔ su ye si yeaɖe yeƒe sidzedze vi aɖe afia la gblɔ be: “Esi manye viɖe aɖeke o .” “Bu dɔ si wòawɔ kple zã kple keli ŋu kpɔ ko! Èkpɔe be anyigba xɔa gaƒoƒo blaeve vɔ ene hafi wòtrɔna le eƒe axadzi—”

Fiavinyɔnua gblɔ be: “Ne èle nu ƒom tso fíawo ŋu la, lã eƒe ta!”

Alice tsɔ dzitsitsi tsɔ ŋku ɖe nuɖala la ŋu vie, be yeakpɔe ɖa be yedi be yeaxɔ aɖaŋuɖoɖoa hã; gake nuɖala la nɔ vovom nɔ mɔli la ʋuʋum, eye edze abe menɔ to ɖom o ene, eyata egayi edzi be: “Gaƒoƒo blaeve vɔ ene, mesusui ;alo wuievee? Nye—”

Fiavinyɔnua gblɔ be: “Oo, mègaɖe fu nam o .” “Nyemete ŋu nɔ te ɖe xexlẽdzesiwo nu gbeɖe o!” Eye esi wògblɔ esia la, egadze no nana via gɔme ake, nɔ alɔ̃dɔdɔ ƒe ha ƒomevi aɖe dzim nɛ esime wònɔ ewɔm, eye wònɔ ʋuʋum sesĩe le fli ɖesiaɖe ƒe nuwuwu:

“Ƒo nu ƒuƒlu na viwòŋutsuvi sue la, .
Eye nàƒoe ne eɖe aɖuɖɔ:
Ðeko wòwɔa esia be wòado dziku, .
Elabena enya be eɖua fewu le ame ŋu.”

CHORUS ƑE ƑOMEDODO.
(Ame si me nuɖala kple vidzĩa wɔ ɖeka le):

“Wow! wow! wow!”

Esi Fiavinyɔnua nɔ hadzidzia ƒe kpukpui evelia dzim la, enɔ vidzĩa ƒom sesĩe yi dzi kple anyime, eye nu sue dahe la nɔ ɣli dom ale gbegbe be Alice mete ŋu se nya siawo kura o be:—

“Meƒoa nu vevie na vinyeŋutsuvi, .
Meƒonɛ ne eɖe aɖuɖɔ;
Elabena ate ŋu ase vivi bliboe
Atadi la ne elɔ̃!”

CHORUS ƑE ƑOMEDODO.

“Wow! wow! wow!”

“Afi sia!, àte ŋu ana noe vie, ne èlɔ̃!” Fiavinyɔnua gblɔ na Alice, eye wòtsɔ vidzĩa ƒu gbe ɖe eŋu esime wònɔ nu ƒom. “Ele be mayi aɖadzra ɖo be maƒo croquet kple Fianyɔnua,” eye wòdo le xɔa me kaba. Nuɖala la tsɔ nuɖaze aɖe ƒu gbe ɖe eyome esime wònɔ dodom, gake ɖeko wòto eŋu.

Alice lé vidzĩa sesẽ aɖe, elabena enye nuwɔwɔ sue aɖe si ƒe nɔnɔme to vovo, eye wòdo eƒe abɔwo kple afɔwo ɖe goawo katã me, “abe ɣletivimefakala ene,” Alice susui. Nu sue dahe la nɔ hũ ɖem abe mɔ̃ si me dzoxɔxɔ le ene esi wòlée, eye wòyi edzi nɔ eɖokui dzim zi gbɔ zi eve henɔ eɖokui ɖɔm ake, ale be le wo katã me la, le aɖabaƒoƒo ɖeka alo eve gbãtɔ me la, esɔ gbɔ wu alesi wòate ŋui be wòalée.

Esi wònya kpɔ mɔ nyuitɔ si dzi wòato ana noe ko, (si nye be wòatrɔe wòazu ka ƒomevi aɖe, eye emegbe wòalé eƒe ɖusito kple miamefɔ ɖe asi sesĩe, ale be wòagaɖee le eɖokui si o,) etsɔe do goe yi yame. Alice bu eŋu be: “Ne nyemekplɔ ɖevi sia ɖe asi o la, kakaɖedzi li be woawui le ŋkeke ɖeka alo eve megbe: ɖe manye amewuwu be woagblẽe ɖi oa?” Egblɔ nya mamlɛawo sesĩe, eye nu suea ƒo nu kple ɣli tsɔ ɖo eŋu nɛ (edzudzɔ aɖuɖɔɖɔɖɔ le ɣeyiɣi sia me). Alice gblɔ be: “Mèganɔ ɣli dom o; “ema menye mɔ nyuitɔ si dzi nàto aɖe wò susu agblɔ kura o.”

Vidzĩa gaƒo nu kple ɣli ake, eye Alice tsɔ dzitsitsi kpɔ eƒe mo be yeakpɔ nusi le fu ɖem nɛ. Ðikeke aɖeke mate ŋu anɔ eme be eƒe ŋɔti si trɔna yia dzi ŋutɔ , si le abe ŋɔti ene wu ŋɔti ŋutɔŋutɔ; hã eƒe ŋkuwo nɔ sue ŋutɔ na vidzĩ: le wo katã me la, Alice melɔ̃ nu la ƒe dzedzeme kura o. Esusu be: “Gake ɖewohĩ avifafa koe wònye,” eye wògakpɔ eƒe ŋkume ake, be yeakpɔe ɖa be aɖatsi aɖe le dodom hã.

Ao, aɖatsi aɖeke menɔ eme o. Alice gblɔ moveviɖoɖotɔe be: “Ne àtrɔ azu ha la, lɔlɔ̃tɔ la, nyemagawɔ naneke kpli wò wu ema o. Bubu eŋu azɔ!” Nu sue dahe la gafa avi ake (alo ƒo nu kple ɣlidodo, manya wɔ be woagblɔ esi o), eye woyi edzi hena ɣeyiɣi aɖe le ɖoɖoezizi me.

Ðeko Alice dze egɔme nɔ eŋu bum le eɖokui me be, “Azɔ, nukae mawɔ kple nuwɔwɔ sia ne meva ɖo aƒeme?” esi wògaƒo ɣli ake, sesĩe ale gbegbe be wòɖiɖi ŋku ɖe eƒe mo le vɔvɔ̃ aɖe me. Fifia ya la, vodada aɖeke mate ŋu adzɔ le eŋu o : mewu ha alo mede ha o, eye wòse le eɖokui me be susu mele eme kura be yeatsɔe ayi ŋgɔe o.

Eyata etsɔ nuwɔwɔ sue la da ɖe anyi, eye wòse le eɖokui me be yeƒe dzi dze eme ŋutɔ esi wòkpɔe wònɔ dzodzom le avea me kpoo. Egblɔ na eɖokui be: “Ne ɖe wòtsi la, anye ne ewɔ ɖevi nyɔŋu dziŋɔ aɖe: gake mesusu be ewɔa ha dzetugbe boŋ.” Eye wòdze ɖevi bubu siwo wònya, siwo ate ŋu awɔ dɔ nyuie ŋutɔ abe ha ene ŋu bubu gɔme, eye ɖeko wònɔ gbɔgblɔm na eɖokui be, “ne ɖeko ame aɖe nya mɔ nyuitɔ si dzi wòato atrɔ wo—” esime wòvɔ̃ vie esi wòkpɔ Cheshire Avũ si bɔbɔ nɔ ati aɖe ƒe alɔ aɖe si didi meta ʋɛ aɖewo tso afima.

Ðeko Avũa ko alɔgbɔnu esi wòkpɔ Alice. Esusu be edze abe ɖe wònyo ene: ke hã eƒe asike didi ŋutɔ eye aɖu gbogbo aɖewo le eŋu, eyata ese le eɖokui me be ele be woawɔ nu ɖe eŋu bubutɔe.

Edze egɔme kple vɔvɔ̃ vie be: “Cheshire Puss,” elabena menya kura nenye be ŋkɔa adzɔ dzi nɛ o: gake ɖeko wòko alɔgbɔnu wòkeke vie. Alice bu eŋu be: “Va, edzɔ dzi nam vaseɖe fifia.” “Meɖe kuku, àgblɔ mɔ si wòle be mato tso afisia nam?”

Avũa gblɔ be: “Ema nɔ te ɖe afisi nèdi be yeaɖo dzi ŋutɔ.”

Alice gblɔ be: “Nyemetsɔ ɖeke le afi si wòle o—.”

Avũa gblɔ be: “Ekema mɔ si dzi nàto la mehiã o.”

“—zi alesi meɖo afi aɖe ko ,” Alice gblɔ kpee abe numeɖeɖe ene.

Avũa gblɔ be: “Oo, àwɔe nenema godoo, ne èzɔ eteƒe didi ko.”

Alice se le eɖokui me be womate ŋu agbe nya sia o, eyata ete biabia bubu kpɔ. “Ame ka ƒomevie nɔa agbe le afisia?”

Avũa gblɔ esi wònɔ eƒe ɖusifɔ ʋuʋum ƒo xlãe be: “Le mɔ ma nu la, Kpovitɔ aɖe nɔa agbe: eye le mɔ ma nu,” esi wònɔ afɔkpodzi kemɛa ʋuʋum la, “March Hare aɖe nɔa agbe. Tsa le wo dometɔ ɖesiaɖe si nèlɔ̃ gbɔ: wo ame evea siaa ƒe tagbɔ gblẽ.”

Alice de dzesii be: “Gake nyemedi be mayi ame siwo ƒe tagbɔ gblẽ dome o.

Avũa gblɔ be: “Oo, màte ŋu awɔ naneke le ema ŋu o: “mí katã míele tagbɔ gblẽm le afisia. Nye tagbɔ gblẽ. Wò tagbɔ gblẽ.”

“Aleke nèwɔ nya be nye tagbɔ gblẽ?” Alice gblɔe alea.

Avũa gblɔ be: “Ele be nànɔ nenema, ne menye nenema o la, màva afisia o.”

Alice mesusu be ema ɖo kpe edzi kura o; ke hã, eyi edzi be “Eye aleke nèwɔ nya be yeƒe tagbɔ gblẽ?”

Avũa gblɔ be: “Ne míadze egɔme la, avu ƒe tagbɔ gblẽ o. Èlɔ̃ ɖe edzia?”

Alice gblɔ be: “Mesusu be nenemae.

Avũa yi edzi be: “Enyo, ekema èkpɔe, avu aɖe ƒoa ɣli ne edo dziku, eye wòʋuʋua eƒe asike ne edzɔ dzi nam. Fifia meƒoa ɣli ne edzɔ dzi nam, eye meʋuʋua nye asike ne medo dziku. Eyata nye tagbɔ gblẽ.”

Alice gblɔ be: “ Meyɔnae be ɣlidodo, ke menye ɣlidodo o.”

Avũa gblɔ be: “Yɔe be nusi nèlɔ̃.” “Ðe nèƒoa croquet kple Fianyɔnua egbea?”

Alice gblɔ be: “Ele be wòadzɔ dzi nam ŋutɔ, gake womekpem haɖe o.”

Avũa gblɔ be: “Àkpɔm le afima,” eye wòbu.

Esia mewɔ nuku na Alice boo o, enɔ nu manyatalenu siwo nɔ dzɔdzɔm la va zu numame nɛ ale gbegbe. Esi wònɔ teƒe si wònɔ tsã la kpɔm la, kasia egadze ake.

“By-the-bye, nukae va zu vidzĩa?” Avũa gblɔ. “Meŋlɔ biabia be kloe.”

Alice gblɔ kpoo be: “Etrɔ zu ha,” abe ɖe wòtrɔ gbɔ le dzɔdzɔme nu ene.

Avũa gblɔ be: “Mesusu be ava eme,” eye wògabu ake.

Alice lala vie, afã nɔ mɔ kpɔm be yeagakpɔe ake, gake medze o, eye le aɖabaƒoƒo ɖeka alo eve megbe la, ezɔ yi mɔ si dzi wogblɔ be March Hare la le la dzi. Egblɔ na eɖokui be: “Mekpɔ kposɔdɔwɔlawo kpɔ; “March Hare lae anye esi doa dzidzɔ na ame wu, eye ɖewohĩ esi wònye May nye esia ta la, mawɔ aɖaʋa o—ne mede ɖeke o la, manye tagbɔsesẽ abe alesi wònɔ le March me ene o.” Esi wònɔ nya sia gblɔm la, efɔ mo dzi, eye Avũ la ganɔ afima ake, ebɔbɔ nɔ ati aɖe ƒe alɔ aɖe dzi.

“Ègblɔ be ha, alo gbotsetse?” Avũa gblɔ.

Alice ɖo eŋu be: “Megblɔ be ha; “eye medi be màyi edzi anɔ dzedzem henɔ bubum kpata alea o: èna ame ƒe ta ʋuʋu kura.”

Avũa gblɔ be: “Enyo; eye fifia ya ebu blewu ŋutɔ, edze egɔme tso asike ƒe nuwuwu dzi, eye wòwu enu kple alɔgbɔnukoko, si tsi anyi ɣeyiɣi aɖe le eƒe akpa mamlɛa ƒe dzodzo megbe.

Alice bu eŋu be: “Enyo! Mekpɔ avu si meko alɔgbɔnu o zi geɖe; “gake alɔgbɔnukoko si me avu mele o! Enye nusi doa dzidzɔ nam wu ɖesiaɖe si mekpɔ le nye agbe me!”

Meyi ŋgɔ boo hafi va kpɔ March Hare ƒe aƒea o: esusu be anye aƒe nyuitɔe, elabena dzodoƒea ƒe nɔnɔme le abe to ene eye wotsɔ lãfu ƒo xɔa tame. Aƒe gã aɖee wònye, ale gbegbe be melɔ̃a tete ɖe eŋu o vaseɖe esime wògaɖu lãmi si le miame la ƒe ɖe, eye wòkɔ eɖokui ɖe dzi wòkɔ abe afɔ eve ene: ɣemaɣi gɔ̃ hã la, ezɔ yi egbɔ kple vɔvɔ̃ vie, henɔ gbɔgblɔm na eɖokui be “Tsɔe be ele be wòanye tagbɔsesẽtɔe le nyateƒe me! Medi kloe be mayi aɖakpɔ Kpovitɔ la boŋ!”
