TA I.
Akpɔkplɔ-Doa ƒe anyime

Ðeɖi te Alice ŋu vevie be wòanɔ nɔvianyɔnu gbɔ le gadzraɖoƒe, eye naneke meganɔ esi wòawɔ o: zi ɖeka alo zi eve la, eɖe ŋku ɖe agbalẽ si nɔvianyɔnu xlẽm la me, gake nɔnɔmetata alo dzeɖoɖo aɖeke menɔ eme o, “eye viɖe kae le agbalẽ ŋu,” Alice susui be “ne nɔnɔmetatawo alo dzeɖoɖowo mele eme o?”

Eyata enɔ eŋu bum le eya ŋutɔ ƒe susu me (abe alesi wòate ŋui ene, elabena ŋkekea ƒe dzoxɔxɔ na wòse le eɖokui me be yedɔ alɔ̃ ŋutɔ eye yezu bometsitsi), nenye be dzidzɔ si wòakpɔ le daisy-kɔsɔkɔsɔ wɔwɔ me asɔ na kuxi si wòatsɔ atso aɖafɔ daisy-tiawo, esime kasia Akpɔkplɔ Yeye aɖe si ƒe ŋkuwo le pink la ƒu du va te ɖe eŋu.

Naneke menɔ ema me si ɖe dzesi ŋutɔ nenema gbegbe o; eye Alice hã mesusui be edo le mɔ dzi nenema gbegbe be wòase Akpɔkplɔ la nagblɔ na eɖokui be, “Oo lɔlɔ̃tɔ! Oo lɔlɔ̃tɔ! Matsi megbe!” (esi wòbu eŋu emegbe la, eva susu me nɛ be ele be esia nawɔ nuku nɛ hafi, gake ɣemaɣi la, nusianu dze abe ɖe wòle dzɔdzɔme nu ene); gake esi Akpɔkplɔ la ɖe gaƒoɖokui aɖe do goe le eƒe akɔtawu kotoku me ŋutɔŋutɔ , eye wòlé ŋku ɖe eŋu, eye emegbe wòwɔ kaba yi edzi la, Alice dze eƒe afɔwo dzi, elabena enɔ keklẽm le eƒe susu me be yemekpɔ avugbɔ̃e si si akɔtawu kotoku le, alo gaƒoɖokui si yeaɖe le eme kpɔ o, eye wònɔ dzo dam le didi be yeanya nu me ta la, eƒu du to agblea dzi le eyome, eye dzɔgbenyuietɔe la, eɖo ɣeyiɣi nyuitɔ dzi be yeakpɔe do ɖe do gã aɖe si me avugbɔ̃e le le ʋɔtrua te.

Le ɣeyiɣi kpui bubu me la, Alice ɖiɖi dze eyome, eye mebu alesi wòle be wòado go le xexeame ŋu zi ɖeka pɛ hã o.

Avugbɔ̃e ƒe doa yi edzi tẽ abe mɔ̃ aɖe ene hena mɔ aɖe, eye emegbe wònyrɔ ɖe anyi kasia, kasia ale gbegbe be ɣeyiɣi kpui aɖeke meganɔ Alice ŋu bu eŋu be yeatɔ te ye ɖokui hafi akpɔe be yege ɖe vudo si goglo ŋutɔ aɖe me o.

Vudoa goglo ŋutɔ, alo edze anyi blewu ŋutɔ, elabena ɣeyiɣi geɖe nɔ esi esi wònɔ ɖiɖim be yealé ŋku ɖe ye ŋu ahanɔ nusi gbɔna dzɔdzɔ ge ŋu bum. Gbã la, edze agbagba be yeaɖiɖi ŋkume akpɔ nusi gbɔ gbɔna, gake viviti do akpa be yeate ŋu akpɔ naneke o; emegbe elé ŋku ɖe vudoa ƒe axawo ŋu, eye wòde dzesii be nudzraɖoƒewo kple agbalẽdzraɖoƒewo yɔ wo me fũ; afisia kple afima la, ekpɔ anyigbatatawo kple nɔnɔmetata siwo wotsɔ ƒo ɖe atikpowo dzi. Eɖe ze aɖe le agbalẽdzraɖoƒeawo dometɔ ɖeka esime wònɔ yiyim; woŋlɔe ɖe edzi be “ORANGE MARMALADE”, gake nusi te ɖe edzi ŋutɔ la, enɔ ƒuƒlu: melɔ̃a zea tsɔtsɔ ƒu gbe o le vɔvɔ̃ be yeawu ame aɖe le ete ta, eyata ete ŋu tsɔe de nudzraɖoƒeawo dometɔ ɖeka me esi wònɔ gegem to eŋu.

"Vudo!" Alice gblɔ le eɖokui me be, “le anyidzedze sia tɔgbe megbe la, nyemabu naneke ŋu le atrakpuiwo ɖiɖi ŋu o! Aleke gbegbe dzi le wo katã bum le aƒemee nye si! Nukatae, nyemagblɔ nya aɖeke tso eŋu o, ne mege tso aƒea tame gɔ̃ hã!” (Esi anya nye nyateƒe ŋutɔ.)

Yi anyime, anyime, anyime. Ðe anyidzedzea mawu enu gbeɖe oa ? “Mebiaa ɖokuinye be kilometa nenie meɖiɖi va ɖo ɣeyiɣi sia me?” egblɔe sesĩe. "Anye be mele afi aɖe si te ɖe anyigba ƒe titina ŋu. Mina makpɔe ɖa: ema anye kilometa akpe ene le anyime, mesusu be—" (elabena, èkpɔe, Alice srɔ̃ nu sia ƒomevi geɖe le eƒe nusɔsrɔ̃wo me le sukuxɔa me, eye togbɔ be esia menye mɔnukpɔkpɔ nyui aɖeke ŋutɔ be wòaɖe eƒe sidzedze afia o, elabena ame aɖeke meli si aɖo toe o, ke hã, enye nuwɔna nyui aɖe be magblɔe wòagbɔ eme) “—ẽ, ema ku ɖe didiƒe si sɔ ŋu—gake ekema Mebiaa ɖokuinye be Latitude alo Longitude kae meɖo?” (Alice menya nusi Latitude nye, alo Longitude hã o, gake esusu be wonye nya gã nyuiwo be yeagblɔ.)

Fifia egadze egɔme ake. “Mebia ɖokuinye be ɖe madze anyi to anyigba dzi tututu hã! Aleke gbegbe wòadze nukokoe enye si wòado le ame siwo zɔna kple ta ɖe anyi domee! Mesusu be Antipathiestɔwoe nye—” (edzɔ dzi nɛ boŋ be ame aɖeke menɔ to ɖom o, fifia ya, elabena meɖi nya si sɔ kura o) “—gake ahiã be mabia wo be ŋkɔ kae nye dukɔa, ènya. Taflatse, Aƒenɔ, New Zealand alo Australia ye nye esia?” (eye wòdze agbagba be yeawɔ curtsey esime wònɔ nu ƒom— curtseying dzeani esime nèle gegem to yame! Èsusu be yeate ŋu akpɔ edzia?) “Eye nyɔnuvi sue manyamanya ka gbegbee nye si wòabu be mebiae! Ao, mawɔe gbeɖe be mabia o: ɖewohĩ makpɔe be woŋlɔe ɖe afi aɖe.”

Yi anyime, anyime, anyime. Nu bubu aɖeke megali woawɔ o, eyata eteƒe medidi o Alice gadze nuƒoƒo gɔme ake. “Dinah asusum vevie le zã sia me, ele be mabu eŋu!” (Dinah ye nye avu la.) "Mele mɔ kpɔm be woaɖo ŋku eƒe notsi kplu dzi le tii nonoɣi. Dina nye lɔlɔ̃tɔ! Edzrom be nànɔ anyigba afisia kplim! Akpɔkplɔ aɖeke mele yame o, mele vɔvɔ̃m, gake àte ŋu alé ʋetsuvi, eye ema le abe sisiblisi ene ŋutɔ, ènya. Gake ɖe avuwo ɖua ʋetsuviwoa, mebia ɖokuinyea?" Eye le afisia la, Alice dze alɔ̃dɔdɔ gɔme vie, eye wòyi edzi nɔ gbɔgblɔm na eɖokui, le drɔ̃eƒe mɔ aɖe nu be, “Ðe avuwo ɖua ʋetsuviwoa? Ðe avuwo ɖua ʋetsuviwoa?” eye ɣeaɖewoɣi la, “Ðe ʋetsuviwo ɖua avuwoa?” elabena, èkpɔe, esi mete ŋu ɖo nyabiase eveawo dometɔ aɖeke ŋu o ta la, mɔ si dzi wòto gblɔe la mehiã boo o. Ese le eɖokui me be yedɔ alɔ̃, eye ɖeko wòdze drɔ̃ekuku gɔme be ye kple Dina le zɔzɔm asi le asi me, eye wònɔ gbɔgblɔm nɛ vevie ŋutɔ be, “Azɔ, Dina, gblɔ nyateƒea nam: ɖe nèɖu ʋetsuvi kpɔa?” ne kasia, thump! thump! eɖiɖi va ɖo atiwo kple aŋgba ƒuƒuwo ƒe ƒuƒoƒo aɖe dzi, eye eƒe gege wu enu.

Alice mexɔ abi vie o, eye wòti kpo tso eƒe afɔwo dzi le ɣeyiɣi kpui aɖe me: efɔ mo dzi, gake viviti do le ta dzi katã; mɔ didi bubu aɖe hã nɔ ŋgɔ nɛ, eye Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la gakpɔtɔ nɔ dzedzem, nɔ du dzi nɔ edzi yim. Ɣeyiɣi kpui aɖeke menɔ anyi si bu o: Alice dzo abe ya ene, eye wòva ɖo ɣeyiɣi nyuitɔ dzi be wòasee wòagblɔ, esi wòtrɔ dzogoe aɖe be, “Oo nye towo kple nye ɖawo, aleke gbegbe wòtsi megbe enye si!” Enɔ megbe nɛ tututu esi wòtrɔ dzogoea, gake womegakpɔ Akpɔkplɔa o: ekpɔ eɖokui le akpata didi aɖe si bɔbɔ me, si akaɖi siwo le fli me siwo le xɔta la klẽ ɖo.

Ʋɔtruwo ƒo xlã akpataa, gake wotu wo katã; eye esi Alice ɖiɖi va akpa ɖeka eye wònɔ dzi yim le akpa kemɛ, henɔ ʋɔtru ɖesiaɖe tem kpɔ la, ezɔ to titina nublanuitɔe, henɔ alesi wòawɔ ado go ake la ŋu bum.

Kasia eva ɖo kplɔ̃ sue aɖe si dzi afɔ etɔ̃ le, eye wotsɔ ahuhɔ̃e sesẽ wɔ wo katã; naneke menɔ edzi o negbe sika safui sue aɖe ko, eye Alice ƒe susu gbãtɔe nye be ɖewohĩ akpataa ƒe ʋɔtruwo dometɔ ɖeka tɔ; gake, baba! alo gaƒoɖokuiawo lolo akpa, alo safuia le sue akpa, gake aleke kee wòɖale o, maʋu wo dometɔ aɖeke o. Gake le zi evelia si wòƒoe la, eva ke ɖe xɔmetsovɔ bɔbɔe aɖe si mede dzesii kpɔ o ŋu, eye ʋɔtru sue aɖe si kɔkɔ abe sentimeta wuiatɔ̃ ene nɔ megbe nɛ: ete sika safui sue si le gaxɔa me la kpɔ, eye edzɔ dzi nɛ ŋutɔ be esɔ!

Alice ʋu ʋɔa eye wòkpɔe be ekplɔa ame yia mɔ sue aɖe si lolo wu sisiblisi ƒe do boo o me: edze klo hekpɔ mɔa dzi yi abɔ dzeanitɔ kekeake si nèkpɔ kpɔ la me. Aleke gbegbe wòdi vevie be yeado le akpata doviviti ma me, eye yeatsa le seƒoƒo siwo le keklẽm ƒe aba mawo kple tsidzɔƒe fafɛ mawo dome, gake mete ŋu to eƒe ta gɔ̃ hã to ʋɔtrua nu o; Alice dahe la bu eŋu be: “eye ne nye ta ato eme hã la, viɖe boo aɖeke manɔ eŋu nye abɔta manɔmee. Oo, aleke gbegbe medi be mazi ɖoɖoe abe didiƒekpɔmɔ̃ ene! Mesusu be mate ŋui, ne ɖe menya alesi madze egɔmee.” Elabena, èkpɔe, nu geɖe siwo to vovo na mɔ dzi dzɔ nyitsɔ laa, ale gbegbe be Alice va nɔ esusum be nu ʋɛ aɖewo koe manya wɔ ŋutɔŋutɔ o vavã.

Edze abe viɖe aɖeke mele lala le ʋɔtru suea nu me o ene, eyata etrɔ yi kplɔ̃a ŋu, kple afã kple afã kple mɔkpɔkpɔ be ɖewohĩ yeakpɔ safui bubu le edzi, alo aleke kee wòɖale o, sewo ƒe agbalẽ aɖe si dzi woato axe mɔ na amewo abe didiƒekpɔmɔ̃wo ene: fifia ya ekpɔ aŋetu sue aɖe le edzi, (“si kakaɖedzitɔe la, menɔ afisia tsã o,” Alice gblɔ,) eye wotsɔ pepa ƒe nuŋɔŋlɔ aɖe ƒo xlã aŋetu la ƒe kɔ, si dzi woŋlɔ nya siwo nye “DRINK ME,” si woŋlɔ ɖe edzi wòlolo nyuie lɛtawo.

Enyo ŋutɔ be woagblɔ be “Nom,” gake Alice sue nyanu la menɔ ewɔ ge nenema le ablaɖeɖe me o. Egblɔ be: “Ao, malé ŋku ɖe eŋu gbã, eye makpɔe ɖa be woŋlɔe ɖe edzi be ‘ aɖi ’ alo womede dzesii o”; elabena exlẽ ŋutinya sue nyui geɖewo tso ɖevi siwo wotɔ dzoe, eye lã wɔadãwo kple nu bubu siwo medzea ame ŋu o ɖu wo ŋu, esiawo katã le esi womaɖo ŋku se bɔbɔe siwo wo xɔlɔ̃wo fia wo dzi o ta: abe, be poker si me dzo le bibim la atɔ dzo wò ne èlée ɖe asi ɣeyiɣi didi akpa ene; eye be ne ètsɔ hɛ lã wò asibidɛ wòde to ŋutɔ la, zi geɖe la, eɖea ʋu; eye meŋlɔ be kpɔ be, ne èno nu geɖe tso aŋetu si woŋlɔ ɖe edzi be “aɖi” me la, kakaɖedzi li kloe be malɔ̃ ɖe wò nya dzi o, kaba alo emegbe.

Gake womede dzesi aŋetu sia be “aɖi” o , eyata Alice tsɔ eɖokui de afɔku me be yeɖɔe kpɔ, eye esi wòkpɔe be evivi ŋutɔ ta la, (le nyateƒe me la, cherry-tart, custard, pine-apple, koklozi si wotɔ dzoe, toffee, kple toast dzodzoe si wotsɔ bɔta wɔe ƒe vivi tsaka ƒomevi aɖe nɔ eme,) eteƒe medidi hafi wòwu enu o.

“Seselelãme si nye didi be yeanya nu ka gbegbee nye si!” Alice gblɔ be; “Anye be mele nu ƒom abe didiƒekpɔmɔ̃ ene.”

Eye aleae wònɔ vavã: sentimeta ewo koe wòkɔ fifia, eye eƒe mo klẽ le susu be yeƒe lolome sɔ azɔ be yeato ʋɔtru suea me ayi abɔ dzeani ma me ta. Gake gbã la, elala aɖabaƒoƒo ʋɛ aɖewo be yeakpɔe ɖa be yeagaɖe ɖe aga hã: etsi dzodzodzoe vie le esia ta; Alice gblɔ na eɖokui be, “elabena ate ŋu awu enu, ènya, le nye dodo keŋkeŋ me, abe bosomikaɖi ene. Mebiaa ɖokuinye be aleke wòle be manɔ ɣemaɣi?” Eye edze agbagba be yeakpɔ alesi bosomikaɖi ƒe dzo le le bosomikaɖia tutu vɔ megbe le susu me, elabena mete ŋu ɖo ŋku edzi be yekpɔ nu ma tɔgbe kpɔ o.

Le ɣeyiɣi aɖe megbe esi wòkpɔe be naneke megadzɔ o ta la, eɖoe be yeayi abɔa me zi ɖeka; gake, baba na Alice dahe la! esi wòɖo ʋɔtrua nu la, ekpɔe be yeŋlɔ sika safui sue la be, eye esi wòtrɔ yi kplɔ̃a ŋu hena esia la, ekpɔe be yemate ŋu aɖo egbɔ o: ete ŋu kpɔe nyuie to ahuhɔ̃ea me, eye wòdze agbagba ɖesiaɖe be yelia kplɔ̃a ƒe afɔ ɖeka, gake egli akpa; eye esi ɖeɖi te eŋu le agbagbadzedze me la, nu sue dahe la bɔbɔ nɔ anyi hefa avi.

“Va, viɖe aɖeke mele avifafa alea me o!” Alice gblɔ na eɖokui, kple gbe sesẽ boŋ; “Meɖo aɖaŋu na wò be nàdzudzɔ aɖabaƒoƒo sia!” Zi geɖe la, eɖoa aɖaŋu nyui ŋutɔ na eɖokui, (togbɔ be ƒã hafi wòwɔna ɖe edzi hã), eye ɣeaɖewoɣi la, eƒoa fi dea eɖokui vevie ale gbegbe be aɖatsi gena ɖe mo nɛ; eye ɣeaɖeɣi wòɖo ŋku edzi be yedze agbagba be yeawɔ aɖaka na ye ŋutɔ yeƒe to le esi yeble ye ɖokui le croquet fefe aɖe me ta la, enɔ fefem kple eɖokui, elabena ɖevi sia si di be yeanya nu geɖe la lɔ̃a eɖokui wɔwɔ abe ame eve ene ŋutɔ. Alice dahe la bu eŋu be: “Gake viɖe aɖeke mele eŋu fifia be mawɔ abe ame eve ene o! Nukatae, nyemegasɔ gbɔ kura be mawɔ ame ɖeka si ŋu bubu le o!”

Eteƒe medidi o eƒe ŋku va dze ahuhɔ̃eɖaka sue aɖe si mlɔ kplɔ̃a te dzi: eʋui, eye wòkpɔ abolo sue aɖe ŋutɔ le eme, si dzi woŋlɔ nya siwo nye “ÐU NYE” ɖo nyuie le akɔbli me. Alice gblɔ be: “Enyo, maɖui, eye ne ena melolo la, mate ŋu aɖo safuia gbɔ;

Eɖu nu vie, eye wògblɔ na eɖokui dzitsitsitɔe be, “Mɔ ka dzi? Mɔ ka dzi?”, eye wòlé eƒe asi ɖe ta tame be yease le ye ɖokui me be mɔ ka dzie wòle tsitsim ɖo, eye ewɔ nuku nɛ ŋutɔ esi wòkpɔe be yeƒe lolome gakpɔtɔ le nenema: kakaɖedzitɔe la, zi geɖe la, esia dzɔna ne ame ɖu abolo, gake Alice ge ɖe mɔ dzi ale gbegbe be wòanɔ mɔ kpɔm be naneke madzɔ o negbe nusiwo to vovo na mɔ dzi la adzɔ, be edze abe ɖe wòku ɖe eŋu ŋutɔ ene eye wònye bometsitsi be agbea nayi edzi le mɔ si bɔ nu.

Eyata edze dɔ gɔme, eye eteƒe medidi kura o, ewu aboloa nu.
