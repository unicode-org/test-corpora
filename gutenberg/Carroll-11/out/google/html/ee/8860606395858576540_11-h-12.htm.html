"Afi sia!" Alice do ɣli, eye wòŋlɔ alesi gbegbe wòloloe le aɖabaƒoƒo ʋɛ mamlɛawo me be le ɣeyiɣia ƒe ahomya me, eye wòti kpo tso dzi kabakaba ale gbegbe be wòtsɔ eƒe awudziwui ƒe nugbɔ ƒo adaŋudeha la ƒe aɖaka la, si do dziku na adaŋudeha la katã ɖe ameha si le ete ƒe ta dzi, eye afimae womlɔ anyi le, eye wòɖo ŋku sikatɔmelã gbogbo aɖe si wòdo dziku le vo me kwasiɖa si do ŋgɔ la dzi nɛ ŋutɔ.

Fia la gblɔ kple gbe sesẽ ŋutɔ be: “Ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃a mate ŋu ayi edzi o, vaseɖe esime adaŋudeha la katã trɔ gbɔ va woƒe nɔƒewo— wo katã ,” egbugbɔ gblɔe kple gbeteteɖedzi gã aɖe, eye wòlé ŋku ɖe Alice ŋu vevie esime wònɔ egblɔm.

Alice lé ŋku ɖe adaŋudeha la ŋu, eye wòkpɔ be, le eƒe ablaɖeɖe me la, yetsɔ Akpɔkplɔa de ta ɖe anyi, eye nu sue dahe la nɔ eƒe asike ʋuʋum le nuxaxa me, eye mete ŋu le ʋuʋum kura o. Eteƒe medidi o egaɖee do goe ake, eye wòɖɔe ɖo; egblɔ na eɖokui be: “menye be efia nu geɖe o; “Ele be masusui be anye viɖe geɖe ŋutɔ le dodokpɔa me le mɔ ɖeka dzi abe alesi wòzãe le mɔ bubu nu ene.”

Esi adaŋudeha la haya vie tso dziku si wovɔ̃na na wo me ko, eye woke ɖe woƒe slatewo kple pɛnsilwo ŋu hegbugbɔ wo de asi na wo ko la, wodze dɔ gɔme vevie ŋutɔ be yewoaŋlɔ afɔkua ƒe ŋutinya, wo katã negbe Akpɔkplɔ si dze abe ɖe wòɖu edzi akpa be wòate ŋu awɔ naneke wu be wòanɔ anyi kple eƒe nu ʋuʋu, anɔ ŋku lém ɖe ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea tame o ene.

“Nukae nènya tso asitsatsa sia ŋu?” Fia la gblɔ na Alice.

Alice gblɔ be: “Naneke meli o.

Fia la te tɔ ɖe edzi be: “Naneke meli oa? ”

Fia la gblɔ kabakaba be: “ Un important, of course, I meant,” eye wòyi eɖokui dzi kple gbe si le ete, .

“vevietɔ—mele vevie o—mele vevie o—vevietɔ—” abe ɖe wòle nya si ɖi nyuie wu la tem kpɔ ene.

Adaŋudeha la dometɔ aɖewo ŋlɔe be “ele vevie,” eye ɖewo hã “mele vevie o.” Alice te ŋu kpɔ esia, esi wòte ɖe eŋu ale gbegbe be wòate ŋu akpɔ woƒe kpe gbadzɛwo; egblɔ le eɖokui me be: “gake mehiã vie o.”

Le ɣeyiɣi sia me la, Fia la, si nɔ nu ŋlɔm ɖe eƒe nuŋlɔɖigbalẽa me ɣeyiɣi aɖe la, do ɣli be “Zi ɖoɖoe!” eye wòxlẽe tso eƒe agbalẽ si nye, “Se Blaene vɔ eve. Ame siwo katã ƒe kɔkɔme wu kilometa ɖeka nadzo le ʋɔnudrɔ̃ƒea .”

Amesiame lé ŋku ɖe Alice ŋu.

Alice gblɔ be: “ Nyemekɔ kilometa ɖeka o.

Fia la gblɔ be: “Wòe nye ema.”

Fianyɔnua gblɔ kpee be: “Ekɔ kilometa eve kloe.”

Alice gblɔ be: “Enyo, nyemayi o, aleke kee wòɖale o, hekpe ɖe eŋu la, menye se si wowɔna edziedzie nye ema o: fifia koe nètoe vɛ.”

Fia la gblɔ be: “Enye se xoxotɔ kekeake le agbalẽa me.”

Alice gblɔ be: “Ekema ele be wòanye Xexlẽdzesi Gbãtɔ.”

Fia la ƒe mo biã, eye wòdo eƒe nuŋlɔɖigbalẽa kabakaba. Egblɔ kple gbe bɔbɔe si le ʋuʋum be: “Mibu miaƒe ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃a ŋu kpɔ.”

Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la ti kpo tso dzi kple ablaɖeɖe gã aɖe gblɔ be: “Kpeɖodzi bubuwo gale ŋgɔ gbɔna, taflatse wò Aƒetɔ.” “ɖeko wofɔ pepa sia teti koe nye ema.”

“Nukae le eme?” Fianyɔnua gblɔ.

Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la gblɔ be: “Nyemeʋui haɖe o, gake edze abe lɛta aɖee wònye, si gamenɔla la ŋlɔ na—na ame aɖe.”

Fia la gblɔ be: “Anye nenemae, negbe ɖe womeŋlɔe na ame aɖeke o, si mebɔ o, ènya.”

“Ameka gbɔe woɖoe ɖo?” adaŋudeha la dometɔ ɖeka gblɔ.

Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la gblɔ be: “Womefia mɔe kura o; “le nyateƒe me la, womeŋlɔ naneke ɖe gota o .” Eʋu pepaa esime wònɔ nu ƒom, eye wògblɔ kpee be “Menye lɛtae wònye o, le nyateƒe me la: kpukpuiwo ƒe hatsotso aɖee wònye.”

“Ðe wole gamenɔla la ƒe asinuŋɔŋlɔ mea?” bia adaŋudeha la dometɔ bubu.

Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la gblɔ be: “Ao, menye nenemae o, eye emae nye nusi wɔ nuku wu le eŋu.” (Edze abe adaŋudeha la katã tɔtɔ ene.)

Fia la gblɔ be: “Anye be esrɔ̃ ame bubu ƒe asi.” (Adaŋudeha la katã gakɔ ɖe edzi ake.)

Knave la gblɔ be: “Taflatse wò Aƒetɔ, menye nyee ŋlɔe o, eye womate ŋu aɖo kpe edzi be meŋlɔe o: womede asi ŋkɔ aɖeke te le eƒe nuwuwu o.”

Fia la gblɔ be: “Ne mède asi ete o la, ɖeko ema gagblẽ nyaa ɖe edzi. Anɔ eme be nu gbegblẽ aɖee nèdi be yeawɔ, ne menye nenema o la, àde asi wò ŋkɔ te abe anukwareɖila ene.”

Woƒo asikpe le afisiafi le esia ta: enye nya gbãtɔ si Fia la gblɔ le aɖaŋu me ŋutɔŋutɔ gbemagbe.

Fianyɔnua gblɔ be: “Ema ɖo kpe eƒe fɔɖiɖi dzi.”

“Meɖo kpe naneke ma tɔgbe dzi o!” Alice gblɔe alea. “Nukata, mènya nusi ŋu wole nu ƒom tsoe gɔ̃ hã o!”

Fia la gblɔ be: “Xlẽ wo.”

Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la do eƒe ŋkumeɖobolo. “Afikae madze egɔme tsoe, meɖe kuku, wò Aƒetɔ?” ebia nya sia.

Fia la gblɔ moveviɖoɖotɔe be: “Dze egɔme tso gɔmedzedzea me, eye nàyi edzi va se ɖe esime nàɖo nuwuwu: emegbe nàdzudzɔ.”

Esiawoe nye kpukpui siwo Akpɔkplɔ Yeye la xlẽ:—

“Wogblɔ nam be èyi egbɔ kpɔ, .

Fia la nɔ asiwo ƒom gblɔ be: “Emae nye kpeɖodzi vevitɔ kekeake si míese vaseɖe fifia; “eyata azɔ na adaŋudeha la—”

Alice gblɔ be: “Ne wo dometɔ aɖe ate ŋu aɖe eme,” (etsi le aɖabaƒoƒo mamlɛawo me ale gbegbe be mevɔ̃ vie be yeatso nya me nɛ o), “Manae sixpence. Nyemexɔe se be gɔmesese ƒe atɔm aɖeke le eme o.”

Adaŋudeha la katã ŋlɔ ɖe woƒe slatewo dzi be, “ Mexɔe se be gɔmesese ƒe atɔm aɖeke le eme o,” gake wo dometɔ aɖeke mete kpɔ be yeaɖe pepaa me o.

Fia la gblɔ be: “Ne gɔmesese aɖeke mele eme o la, si ɖea xaxa ƒe xexe aɖe ɖe agbe la, ènya, abe alesi mehiã be míadze agbagba ake ɖe ɖeke ŋu o ene. Eye ke hã nyemenya o,” eyi edzi, eye wòkeke kpukpuiawo ɖe eƒe klo dzi, eye wòtsɔ ŋku ɖeka kpɔ wo; “Edze abe gɔmesese aɖe le wo ŋu kpɔm ene, le nyateƒe me.“— gblɔ be nyemate ŋu aƒu tsi o —” màte ŋu aƒu tsi o, alo?” egblɔ kpee, eye wòtrɔ ɖe Knave la ŋu.

Knave la ʋuʋu ta nublanuitɔe. “Ðe nye dzedzeme ɖiia?” egblɔ be. (Esi mewɔe o godoo , esi wotsɔ kakɛ wɔe keŋkeŋ.)

Fia la gblɔ be: “Enyo, vaseɖe fifia,” eye wòyi edzi nɔ ɣli dom le kpukpuiawo ŋu na eɖokui be: “‘ Míenya be enye nyateƒe —’ adaŋudeha lae nye ema, nyateƒee—’ Metsɔ ɖeka nɛ, wotsɔ eve nɛ —’ nukatae, nenemae wòanya wɔ kple tartawo, ènya—”

Alice gblɔ be: “Gake, eyi edzi be ‘ wo katã wotrɔ tso egbɔ va gbɔwò .’”

“Nukata, afimae wole!” Fia la gblɔ aʋadziɖuɖutɔe, eye wòfia asi tart siwo le kplɔ̃a dzi. “Naneke mate ŋu adze ƒã wu ema o . Ekema agate ŋu adzɔ be—’ hafi wòaxɔ lãmetutudɔ sia —’ mèxɔ lãmetutudɔ kpɔ o, lɔlɔ̃tɔ, mesusui?” egblɔ na Fianyɔnua.

"Gbɛɖɛ!" Fianyɔnua gblɔ dzikutɔe, eye wòtsɔ nuŋlɔti aɖe ƒu gbe ɖe Akpɔkplɔa ŋu esime wònɔ nu ƒom. (Bill sue dzɔgbevɔ̃etɔ la dzudzɔ nuŋɔŋlɔ ɖe eƒe slate dzi kple asibidɛ ɖeka, esi wòkpɔe be dzesi aɖeke mele eŋu o; gake azɔ egadze egɔme kabakaba ake, zã nuŋlɔtsi si nɔ sisim le eƒe mo, zi alesi wònɔ anyi.)

Fia la nɔ ŋku lém ɖe xɔxɔnua ŋu kple alɔgbɔnukoko gblɔ be: “Ekema nyawo mesɔ na wò o.” Ðoɖoezizi kuku aɖe nɔ anyi.

“Enye nyakpakpa!” Fia la gblɔ kpee kple gbe si do dziku, eye amesiame ko nu be, “Na adaŋudeha la nabu woƒe ʋɔnudɔdrɔ̃a ŋu.” Fia la gblɔ, abe zi blaevelia ene gbemagbe.

“Ao, ao!” Fianyɔnua gblɔ. “Tohehe gbã—tso afia le ema megbe.”

“Nuwo kple nya dzodzrowo!” Alice gblɔ sesĩe. “Susua si nye be nyagbea nanɔ ame si gbã!”

“Lé wò aɖe ɖe asi!” Fianyɔnua gblɔ esi wòtrɔ zu aɖabɛ.

“Nyemawɔe o!” Alice gblɔe alea.

“Midzo kple eƒe ta!” Fianyɔnua do ɣli kple eƒe gbe sesẽ. Ame aɖeke meʋuʋu o.

“Amekae tsɔa ɖe le eme na wò?” Alice gblɔ, (etsi va ɖo eƒe lolome blibo le ɣeyiɣi sia me). “Menye nanekee nènye o negbe agbalẽvi babla ɖeka ko!”

Le esia ta la, lãha bliboa fɔ ɖe yame, eye wòva nɔ dzodzom ɖe edzi: edo ɣli vi aɖe, vɔvɔ̃ ƒe afã kple dziku ƒe afã, eye wòdze agbagba be yeaƒo wo, eye wòkpɔ eɖokui mlɔ ƒuta, eye eƒe ta le nɔvianyɔnu ƒe akɔnu, amesi nɔ aŋgba kuku aɖewo siwo nɔ ʋuʋum tso atiawo dzi le eƒe mo la ƒom blewuu.

“Nyɔ, Alice lɔlɔ̃tɔ!” nɔvianyɔnu gblɔ be; “Nukata, alɔ̃ didi kae nye si nèdɔ!”

“Oo, meku drɔ̃e si me didi vevie ale gbegbe!” Alice gblɔ, eye wògblɔ na nɔvianyɔnu, abe alesi wòte ŋu ɖoa ŋku wo dzii ene, eƒe Adventures wɔnuku siawo katã siwo ŋu nèle nu xlẽm tsoe teti koe nye ema; eye esi wòwu enu la, nɔvianyɔnu gbugbɔ nu nɛ, eye wògblɔ be, “Drɔ̃e didi aɖee wònye , lɔlɔ̃tɔ, godoo: gake azɔ ƒu du yi ɖe wò tii gbɔ; Eyata Alice tso heƒu du dzo, henɔ susum esime wònɔ du dzi, abe alesi wòate ŋui ene la, drɔ̃e wɔnuku ka gbegbee nye si wònye.

Gake nɔvianyɔnu nɔ anyi kpoo esime wògblẽe ɖi teti, etsɔ eƒe ta ɖo eƒe asi dzi, nɔ ɣe ƒe toɖoɖo kpɔm, eye wònɔ Alice sue la kple eƒe Adventures wɔnukuwo katã ŋu bum, vaseɖe esime eya hã dze drɔ̃ekuku gɔme le atsyã aɖe yome, eye esiae nye eƒe drɔ̃e:—

Gbã la, eku drɔ̃e le Alice sue la ŋutɔ ŋu, eye wògatsɔ asi sueawo gabla ɖe eƒe klo ŋu ake, eye ŋku siwo le dzo dam siwo le keklẽm la nɔ ŋku lém ɖe etɔ ŋu—ete ŋu se eƒe gbe ƒe ɖiɖi ŋutɔŋutɔ, eye wòkpɔ eƒe ta ƒe ʋuʋu sue wɔnuku ma be yeatsɔ axe mɔ na taɖa si le tsaglãla tsam si age ɖe eƒe ŋkuwo me ɣesiaɣi la—eye kokoko esi wònɔ to ɖom, alo wòdze abe ɖe wòle to ɖom ene la, teƒe bliboa si ƒo xlãe la va zu nɔ agbe kple nɔvianyɔnu sue ƒe drɔ̃e me nuwɔwɔ wɔnukuwo.

Gbe didiawo nɔ ɣli dom le eƒe afɔwo dzi esime Akpɔkplɔ Yeyea nɔ du dzi va yina—Akpɔkplɔ si vɔvɔ̃ ɖo la nɔ tsi ƒum to ta si te ɖe eŋu me—ete ŋu se tii kpluawo ƒe ɖiɖi esime March Hare kple exɔlɔ̃wo nɔ woƒe nuɖuɖu si nuwuwu meli na o ɖum, kple Fianyɔnua ƒe gbe sesẽ si nɔ se dem be woaɖe asi le eƒe amedzro dzɔgbevɔ̃etɔawo ŋu be woawu wo—gavi si nye havi la ganɔ aɖuɖɔ ƒom ɖe edzi Fiavinyɔnua ƒe klo, esime agbawo kple nuɖugbawo nɔ ƒoƒom ƒo xlãe—Gryphon ƒe ɣlidodo, Akpɔkplɔ ƒe slate-pencil ƒe ɣlidodo, kple guinea-ha siwo wotu nutete ƒe ʋuʋu, yɔ ya me fũ, eye wòtsaka kple Mock Turtle nublanuitɔ la ƒe avifafa le didiƒe.

Eyata enɔ anyi ɖe edzi, kple ŋkuwo ʋuʋu, eye afã xɔ eɖokui dzi se be Nukunyigba dzi, togbɔ be enya be ɖeko wòle be yeagaʋu wo ake hã, eye nuwo katã atrɔ azu nu ŋutɔŋutɔ si me mekɔ o—ɖeko gbeawo anɔ ɣli dom le ya me, eye ta la anɔ ʋuʋum ɖe aŋɔwo ƒe ʋuʋu ŋu—tiikplu siwo le ɖiɖim la atrɔ azu alẽwo ƒe gaƒoƒo siwo le ɖiɖim, eye Fianyɔnua ƒe ɣlidodo sesẽwo azu alẽkplɔla ƒe gbe ŋutsuvi—kple vidzĩa ƒe aɖuɖɔtoe, Gryphon ƒe ɣlidodo, kple toɣliɖeɖe bubu siwo katã to vovo, atrɔ (enya) azu agbledeƒe si me amewo sɔ gbɔ ɖo ƒe ɣlidodo si tɔtɔ—evɔ nyi siwo le adzɔge ʋĩ ƒe ɣlidodo axɔ ɖe Mock Turtle ƒe avifafa sesẽ teƒe.

Mlɔeba la, ekpɔ alesi nɔvianyɔnu sue sia ke, le ɣeyiɣi si kplɔe ɖo me, anye nyɔnu tsitsi ŋutɔe le susu me; kple alesi wòalé, le eƒe ƒe siwo katã tsi wu me la, eƒe ɖevime dzi bɔbɔe kple lɔlɔ̃ la me ɖe asi: kple alesi wòaƒo ƒu tso via sue bubuawo ŋu, eye wòana woƒe ŋkuwo naklẽ eye woadi vevie kple ŋutinya wɔnuku geɖe, ɖewohĩ kple drɔ̃e si ku le Nukunyigba si nɔ anyi ɣeyiɣi didi aɖee nye sia gɔ̃ hã: kple alesi wòase le eɖokui me kple woƒe nuxaxa bɔbɔewo katã, eye wòakpɔ dzidzɔ le woƒe dzidzɔkpɔkpɔ bɔbɔewo katã me, aɖo ŋku eya ŋutɔ ƒe vi-agbenɔnɔ dzi, kple dzomeŋɔli ŋkeke siwo me dzidzɔ le dzi.
