BAB IX.
Carita Kura-kura Palusan

“Tena nukkulle appikkiri antekamma rannuna pa’maikku anciniki pole, ikau tau toa!” nakanamo Duchess, ri wattu na antama limanna siagang pangngamaseang ri limanna Alice, nampa alampa ke'nanga sipa'rua.

Alice sanna rannuna nabuntulu ia sanna baji'na pa'mai'na, siagang na pikkiri kalenna angkanaya gassingka anjo ladaji bawang ampa'jari sanna' liara'na ri wattu assibuntulu'na ke'nanga ri dapo'.

“Ri wattu inakke Duchess,” nakanamo ri kalenna, (nampa tena na sanna' sanna' panrannuanna), “Inakke tena todong na nia' lada ri dapo'ku. Sup sanna' baji'na punna tena—Mungkin tuli lada ampa'jari tau sanna' larrona,” nakanamo, sanna' rannuna nasaba' narapi'mi atorang beru, “siagang cuka ampa'jari ke'nanga a'jari sanna' sanna'na pait—siagang—siagang jelai-gula siagang apa-apa mamo nu ampa'jari ana'-anaka sanna' baji'na pa'maikku.

Ia sanna'mi nakaluppai Duchess ri hattunna, siagang sike'de'mi lannasa'na ri wattu nalangngere'na sa'ranna ri ampi'na tolinna. “Ikau appikkiri se're passala, saribattangku, siagang anjo ampa'jari kaluppako a'bicara. Inakke tena kukkulle ampauangko kamma kammayya anne apa moralna anjo, mingka laku u'rangi sallang.”

“Mungkin tena,” Alice barani a'pau.

“Tut, tut, ana!” nakanamo Duchess. “Sikontu apa-apa nia moralna, punna akkulleji nu gappa.” siagang ia ansikkoki kalenna mange ri ampi'na alice ri wattu a'bicarana.

Alice tena nasannang nyawana a'reppese' mange ri ia: uru-uruna, nasaba' anjo Duchess sanna' ja'dala'na; siagang maka rua, nasaba ia memang tojeng tinggina untu ampadongkoki dagu na ri bangkenna alice, siagang anjo dagu tarang tena na nyamanga. Mingka, ia tena nasannang a'gau kasar, jari na tahangi baji'-baji'.

“Anne pa'geo'a sanna' baji'na kamma kammayya anne,” nakanamo, lanri na pa'tarrusui pa'bicaraanga sike'de'.

"'Iamintu," nakanamo Duchess: "siagang moralna anjo iamintu—'Oh, 'iamintu pangngamaseang, 'iaminjo pangngamaseang, iaminjo ampa'jari linoa a'lette'!'"

“Nia tau angkana,” Alice a'bisik, “anjo nigaukangi ri sikontu tau anggaukangi urusanna kalenna!”

"Ah, baji'mi! battuanna sangkamma to'ji," nakanamo Duchess, na nakali dagu ca'di tarang ri bangkenna Alice ri wattu na tambai, "siagang moralna anjo iamintu—'Jagai indra, siagang sa'ra-sa'raya la'jagai kalenna.'"

“Antekamma sanna'na ero'na a'boya moral ri se'rea barang-barang!” Alice na pikkiri kalenna.

“Inakke barani angkana ikatte sanna' lannasa'na angngapa natena ku padongko' limanku ri pinggangnu,” nakanamo Duchess ribokoangna ammari-mari: “alasanna, inakke ragu ri sesena larrona flamingonu. Maka akkullei kucobai anjo paccoba?”

“Akkullei nakanre,” Alice appiali siagang ati-ati, tena nasannang nyawana untu’ ancobai anjo paccoba.

“Sanna' tojengna,” nakanamo Duchess: “flamingo siagang sawi rua-ruanna a'giling. Na moralna anjo iamintu—'Jangang-jangang bulu a'rappungang.'”

“Sawiji bawang teai jangang-jangang,” nakanamo Alice.

“Baji, sangkamma biasa,” nakanamo Duchess: “apa cara nu je’ne’ ampadongkoki barang-baranga!”

“Iamintu mineral, inakke kupikkiriki ,” nakanamo Alice.

“Tantu memang,” nakanamo Duchess, ia sanna' sadia untu' a'samaturu' ri sikontu apa napaua Alice; “nia tambang sawi lompo ri ampi'na anne. siagang moralna iamintu—'pila' jai barang-barangku, pila' kurangi barang-barangnu.'”

“Oh, ku isse'i!” nakanamo Alice, ia tenaya na hadere ri anne kana-kana kala’busanga, “anne sayur. Tena nasangkamma, mingka memang.”

“Inakke sanna situju siagang ikatte,” nakanamo Duchess; “siagang moralna anjo iamintu—’A’jari apa nucini’—yareka punna ero’jako nipa’jari la’bi sederhana—’Teako bayang-bayangi kalennu tena nu’jari maraeng battu ri apa nukkullea nucini’ ri tau maraenga angkanaya apa nu’jaria yareka nukkullea nu’jari tena na’jari maraeng battu ri apa nu’jaria nucini’ ri ke’nanga.’”

“Kupikkiriki angkanaya parallui kupahang baji’-baji’ anjo,” nakanamo Alice siagang sanna’ sopanna, “punna nia’mo kutulisiki: mingka tena nakukkulle anturuki sangkamma nupaua.”

“Anjo teai apa-apa akkulle kupau punna kupilei,” appialimi Duchess, siagang nada sannang.

“Appala doang teako susai kalennu ampaui labbi sallo na anjo,” nakanamo Alice.

“Oh, teako a'bicarai ri sesena kasusaang!” nakanamo Duchess. “Kusareko hadia battu ri sikontu apa lebaka kukana kamma kammayya anne.”

“Hadiah yang murah!” nakanamo Alice. “Sannang nyawaku nasaba tena na assarei hadia ulang tahun kamma anjo!” Mingka ia tena na barani ampaui siagang sa'ra lompo.

“Appikkiri pole?” akkuta'nammi Duchess, siagang se'reang kali'bong dagu ca'di tarang.

“Inakke nia hakku untu appikkiri,” nakanamo alice sanna, nasaba ia appakkaramulami sanna linguna.

“Sangkammaji,” nakanamo Duchess, “sangkamma bawi musti alette; siagang m—”

Mingka anrinni, sanna'mi lannasa'na Alice, sa'ra Duchess matemi, manna pole ri tangnga-tangngana kana pa'risi'na 'moral,' siagang limanna ia niaka nipasisambung siagang limanna appakkaramulami a'gio'. Alice accini nai, siagang anjo Ratu ammenteng ri dallekanna ke'nanga, siagang limanna a'lipat, a'rurung-rurung sangkamma anging puttusu'.

“allo baji, karaeng!” Duchess appakkaramulami siagang sa'ra rannu siagang lammai.

“Kamma kammayya anne, kusareangko pappakainga adil,” ammarrang Ratu, antunrungi buttaya ri wattu a’bicarana; “baik ikatte yareka ulunnu musti assulu, siagang anjo kira-kira sitangnga wattu! Allei pappilenu!”

Duchess angngallei pappileanna, siagang alampami ri se'reang wattu.

“Maeko tulusu siagang pa'geo'a,” nakanamo Ratu ri Alice; siagang Alice sanna' malla'na untu' a'bicara, mingka lambat-lambatmi amminawang ammotere' mange ri tampa' kroket.

Sikamma tau battua maraenga angngallei kasampatang ri wattu tenana Ratu, siagang ammari-mari ri tampa pa'bangngiang: mingka, ri wattu nacini'na, akkaro-karomi ke'nanga ammotere' mange ri pa'geo'na, Ratu poro napauangi angkanaya punna sike'de'ji sallona la'busu'mi nyawana ke'nanga.

Sikontu wattu ke’nanga a’balle-balle anjo Ratu tena le’bakai ammari a’gea’-gea’ siagang pa’balle-balle maraenga, siagang akkiok-kio’ “Off with his head!” yareka “Appasulu siagang ulunna!” Tau ni hukkunga ni tahangi ri tantara, ia memang musti ambokoi a'jari lengkung untu' anggaukangi anne, sa'genna ri pa'le'ba'na sitangnga jang iareka kira-kira tenamo lengkung tassesa, siagang sikontu pa'maina, pantaranganna Karaeng, Ratu, siagang Alice, nipantama'mi ri tahanang siagang nihukkung mate.

Nampa anjo Ratu ammari, sanna'mi sanna'na, siagang nakanamo ri Alice, “Maka nucini'mi anjo Mock Turtle?”

“Tena,” nakanamo Alice. “Inakke tena todong ku isse'i apa anjo Mock Turtle.”

“Iamintu barang Mock Turtle Soup nipare battu ri,” nakanamo Ratu.

“Inakke tena lebba kucini, yareka kulangngere se’re,” nakanamo Alice.

“Maeko, punna kamma,” nakanamo Ratu, “siagang ia lanapabattu ri kau sejarahna.”

Ri wattu alampana ke’nanga sipa’rua, Alice nalangngere’mi Karaenga angkana siagang sa’ra rannu, mae ri perusahaanga umumna, “Ikau ngaseng nipammopporangi.” “Maeko, anjo bajiki!” nakanamo ri kalenna, nasaba ia sanna tena nasannang nyawana ri jaina eksekusi naparentakangi ratu.

Tena nasallo battumi ke'nanga mange ri Gryphon, attinro sanna' ri alloa. (Punna tena nu isse'i apa anjo Gryphon, cini'mi gambara.) “Naik, malasa!” nakanamo karaenga, "siagang angngerangi anne tuloloa mange anciniki kura-kura palsu, siagang ampilangngeri sejarahna. Inakke musti ammotere siagang anciniki ribokoangna siapa are eksekusi lebaka kuparentakang;" siagang alampami, ambokoi Alice kale-kalenna siagang Gryphon. Alice tena nasannang ri tanja'na anjo olo'-oloka, mingka secara keseluruhan na pikkiri' sanna' amangna punna ammantang siagang anjo olo'-oloka sangkamma a'lampa mange ri anjo Ratu liara: jari attayangi.

Gryphon ammempo siagang na gosok matanna: nampa na cini'mi Ratu sanggenna ta'lappasa' battu ri paccini'na: nampa a'kakkali. “Apa sanna rannuna!” nakanamo Gryphon, sitangnga mange ri kalenna, sitangnga mange ri Alice.

“Apa anjo kasannangang?” nakanamo alice.

“Angngapa, ia ,” nakanamo Gryphon. “Ikatte ngaseng sanna' ero'na, iamintu: tena le'bakai ke'nanga ambunoi tau maraenga, nuassengi. Maeko!”

“Sikontu taua angkana 'maeko!' anrinni,” nakanamo Alice, ri wattu alampa lambat-lambat ribokoangna: “Inakke tena lebba ni parentai ri sikontu tallasa ku, tena lebba!”

Tena na bellai ke'nanga riolo nacini'mi anjo Mock Turtle ri bellaya, ammempo susa siagang se'reang ri se'reang batu ca'di, siagang, ri wattu a'reppese'na ke'nanga, akkullei nalangngere' Alice a'dakka-dakka sangkamma ero'na ancuru' atinna. Ia sanna kasi-asina ri ia. “Apa kasusaanna?” akkuta'nammi ri Gryphon, siagang Gryphon appiali, sanna' sanna'na sangkamma kana-kana riolo, “Ikatte ngaseng sanna' ero'na, iamintu: ia tena na nia' pa'risi pa'mai'na, nuassengi. Maeko!”

Jari nai'mi ke'nanga mange ri Mock Turtle, ia anciniki ke'nanga siagang mata lompo rassia je'ne' mata, mingka tena apa-apa napau.

“Iaminne anne tuloloa,” nakanamo Gryphon, “ero’ tongi naasseng sejarahnu, ero’ tongi.”

“Lakupauangi,” nakanamo Mock Turtle siagang suara lantang siagang kosong: “ammempo mako, ikatte rua, siagang teako a’bicara manna sike’de’ punna tenapa nale’ba’.”

Jari ammempomi ke'nanga, siagang tena manna sitau a'bicara lalang siapa are menit. Alice na pikkiri kalenna, “Inakke tena ku cini antekamma batena akkulle a'le'basa, punna tena na appakkaramula.” Mingka ia attayang siagang sabar.

“Sikali,” nakanamo Kura-kura Tiru ri kala’busang, siagang angngarru’ lompo, “Inakke Kura-kura sitojennaya.”

Anne kana-kana nipinawang ri se'reang sa'ra sanna' la'buna, nipantama'ji ri se'reang pa'pau “Hjckrrh!” battu ri Gryphon, siagang angngarru sanna battala battu ri Mock Turtle. Alice sanna'mi ero'na ambangung siagang angkana, “Tarima kasih, karaeng, ri carita nu menarik,” mingka takkulleai natahang pikkiranna angkanaya musti rie' ija sallang, jari ammempomi siagang tena apa-apa napau.

“Ri wattu caddi-caddita,” anjo Mock Turtle a'tarrusui, labbi sannang, manna mamo sanna' angngarru'na sike'de' kamma-kamma anne, “ikatte a'sikola ri tamparanga. Gurunna iamintu Kura-kura toa—biasa niarengi Kura-kura—”

“Angngapa nu arengi ia Kura-kura, punna ia teai se’re?” Akkuta'nammi Alice.

“Ikatte angkioki kura-kura nasaba ia angngajaraki ikatte,” nakanamo kura-kura palsu siagang larrona: “sikontutojenna ikatte sanna bodona!”

“Ikau parallui siri ri kalennu lanri akkuta'nang kamma anne,” tambai Gryphon; nampa ke'nanga sipa'rua ammempo a'mereki siagang anciniki Alice kasi-asia, ia sanna' ero'na antama' ri buttaya. Kala’busanna nakanamo Gryphon ri Mock Turtle, “A’jappa mako, tau toa! Teako allo-alloi anjo passalaka!” siagang ia tulusu akkana kamma anne:

“Iyo, a'sikola maki ri tamparanga, manna mamo tena nu tappaka—”

“Inakke tena kukana tena!” na ganggu Alice.

“Ikau anggaukangi,” nakanamo kura-kura palsu.

“Tahang lilannu!” tambai Gryphon, riolo Alice akkulle a'bicara pole. Kura-kura palsu alampa mange.

“Ikatte nia pendidikan kaminang baji—sikontutojenna, ikatte alampa mange ri sikola allo-allo—”

“ Inakke to'ji a'lampa mange ri sikola allo-allo,” nakanamo Alice; “tena nuparallu sanna banggana sangkamma anjo.”

“siagang tambahan?” akkuta'nammi anjo kura-kura palsu sike'de' cemas.

“Iyo,” nakanamo Alice, “ikatte appilajara bahasa perancis siagang musik.”

“siagang a'je'ne?” nakanamo kura-kura palsu.

“Tantu tena!” nakanamo alice siagang larrona.

“Ah! nampa anjo sikolanu teai sipato baji’na,” nakanamo Mock Turtle siagang nada sanna’ rannuna. “Kamma kammayya anne ri katte rie' ri cappa'na tagilanga, 'Perancis, musik, siagang a'je'ne ' — ekstra.'”

“Tena nukkulle sanna ero,” nakanamo Alice; “ammantang ri rawanganna tamparanga.”

“Inakke tena kukkulle angngassengi anjo.” nakanamo kura-kura palsu siagang angngarru. “Inakke angngalleji bawang kursus biasa.”

“Apa anjo?” akkuta'nang Alice.

“A'goyang siagang a'goyang, tantu, untu' appakkaramula,” appialimi Kura-kura palsu; “nampa pole cabang-cabang Aritmatika yang berbeda—Ambisi, Distraksi, Uglifikasi, dan Derision.”

“Inakke tena kulangngere 'Uglification,'” Alice barani angkana. “Apa anjo?”

Gryphon naangkaki ruaya bangkenna lanri lannasa. “Apa! Tena lebba kulangngere kodi!” nakanamo. “Nuasseng ngasenji apa anjo ni pa bajiki, kamma kammayya anne?”

“Iyo,” nakanamo Alice ragu-ragu: “battuanna—untu’—appare’—apa-apa mamo—la’bi baji’.”

“Baji, punna kamma,” Gryphon tulusu, “punna tena nu isse'i apa nu pa'risi, ikatte tau sederhana.”

Alice tena na sanna' ero'na akkuta'nang pole ri sesena anjo, jari a'lette'mi mange ri Mock Turtle, nampa nakana “Apa pole nuparallu nupilajari?”

“Eh, rie' misteri,” appialimi kura-kura palsu, nabilangi subjek ri flapperna, “—misteri, kuno siagang modern, siagang Seaography: nampa Drawling—Drawling-master iami antu conger-el toa, biasana battu sikali saminggu: ia angngajaraki ikatte Drawling, Stretching, siagang Fainting in Coils.”

“Antekamma anjo ?” nakanamo alice.

“Eh, inakke tena kukkulle ampa'piitteang kalengku,” nakanamo Kura-kura Tiru: “Inakke sanna' kassa'na. Na anjo Gryphon tena le'bakai angngassengi.”

“Tena wattu,” nakanamo Gryphon: “Inakke alampa mange ri master klasik, manna mamo. Ia kepiting toa, ia .”

“Inakke tena lebba mange ri ia,” nakanamo kura-kura palsu siagang angngarru: “ia angngajaraki akakkali siagang susa, biasana ke’nanga angkana.”

“Jari ia anggaukangi, jari ia anggaukangi,” nakanamo Gryphon, angngarru ri giliranna; siagang anjo ruaya olo'-olo' ancokkoi rupanna ri bangkenna.

“siagang pirang jammu allo-allo nugaukangi pappilajarang?” nakanamo alice, sanna' linta'na ero' a'pinra topik.

“Sampulo jammi allo maka se’re,” nakanamo Kura-kura Tiru: “salapang allo ribokoanganna, siagang kamma tomminjo.”

“Apa rancana yang menarik!” nakanamo Alice.

“Iaminjo sabakna na nikana pappilajarang,” nakanamo Gryphon: “nasaba’ kurangi battu ri allo mange ri allo.”

anne iami antu pikkirang beru mange ri alice, siagang na pikkiriki sike'de' riolo ri anre'napa na pa'pau ribokoangna. “nampa allo maka sampulo anse're musti allo lompo?”

“Tantu memang,” nakanamo Kura-kura Tiru.

“siagang antekamma batenu anggaukangi ri tanggala sampulo anrua?” Alice tulusu siagang sanna ero.

“Injo sukku'mi ri sesena pangngajarang,” Gryphon anseroi siagang nada yang sanna' sanna'na: “pauangi apa-apa ri sesena pa'geo'a kamma-kamma anne.”
