BAB VI.
Bawi siagang lada

Siapa are menit sallona ammenteng anciniki balla, siagang akkuta'nang apa musti nagaukang ribokoangna, ri wattu nia'mo se're tau a'jappa bangkeng ammakeang livery lari assulu' battu ri romanga—(naanggappai a'jari tau a'jappa bangkeng nasaba' ammakeang livery: punna tena, punna niparessa battu ri rupanna bawang, ia la'kioki juku')—siagang a'dakka lompo ri pakke'bu'a siagang buku-bukunna. Anjo ni sungkei ri tau maraeng ammakeang, siagang rupanna bulaeng, siagang matanna lompo sangkamma kongkong; siagang ruaya tau a'jappa bangkeng, na cini'mi Alice, nia' uu'na bubuk a'lekko-lekko ri sikontu ulunna. Ia sanna' ero'na angngassengi apa anjo, siagang a'dakka sike'de' assulu' battu ri romanga untu' ampilangngeri.

Fish-Footman appakkaramulami siagang appasulu battu ri rawanganna limanna se're sura' lompo, sanna' lompona sangkamma kalenna, siagang anne napassareang mange ri tau maraenga, nakana, siagang suara suci, "Untuk Duchess. Undangan battu ri Ratu untu' a'balle croquet." Frog-Footman na ulangi, siagang nada yang sama, napinra urutan kana-kana sike'de', "Battu ri Ratu. Undangan untuk Duchess a'balle croquet."

Nampa ke'nanga massing su'ju', siagang ikalna ke'nanga a'samaturu'.

Alice sanna'mi a'kakkali ri anne passalaka, sa'genna musti lari ammotere' mange ri romanga lanri malla'na ke'nanga allangngere'i; siagang ri wattu ia ammumba assulu anjo Fish-Footman tenamo, siagang anjo maraenga ammempo ri buttaya ri ampi'na pakke'bu', accini' bodo nai' ri langika.

Alice alampa siagang malla mange ri pakkebuka, siagang antukkoki.

"Tena matu-matunna antunrungi," nakanamo Footman, "siagang anjo nasaba rua passabakkang. maka se're, nasaba inakke ri se'reang sa'ri pakke'bu' siagang ikatte; maka rua, nasaba' ke'nanga appa'nia' sa'ra sanna' ri tangnga, tena manna sitau akkulle allangngere'ko." siagang tantu rie ' sa'ra luar biasa kajariang ri tangnga-tangngana—sa'ra angngarru' siagang bersin, siagang tunggala' wattu rie' sa'ra lompo, sangkamma piring iareka ceret ancuru'.

“Tolong, punna kamma,” nakanamo Alice, “antekamma bateku antama?”

“Akkullei nia’ battuanna ri pa’tokko’nu,” anjo Footman tulusu’mi tena naparhatikangi, “punna nia’ pakke’bu’ ri alla’na ikatte. Pa’pada, punna nia’ko lalang , akkulleko a’tokko’, siagang akkullei kupasulu’, nuassengi.” Ia accini nai ri langika lalang sikontu wattu a'bicara, siagang anne Alice na pikkiri tena na baji. “mingka gassingka takkulleai nabantu,” nakanamo ri kalenna; “mata-matanna sanna ' rapi'na ri coppo' ulunna. Mingka manna antekamma akkullei appiali pakkuta'nang.—Antekamma bateku antama'?” nakanamo pole, sa'ra lompo.

“Inakke la ammempo anrinni,” nakanamo Footman, “sanggenna ammuko—”

Ri wattu kammaya anne tassungkemi pakkebuka balla, siagang nia se're piring lompo assulu, langsung ri ulunna Footman: anjo piringa anseroi idongna, nampa ancuru' ri se'reang poko' kayu ri bokoangna.

“—yareka ammukona, gassingka,” Footman tulusu siagang nada yang sama, sangkamma tena apa-apa kajariang.

“Antekamma bateku antama?” akkuta'nang pole Alice, siagang suara lompo.

“ Maka erokki antama?” nakanamo Footman. “Iaminjo pakkuta’nang maka se’rea, nuassengi.”

Iamintu, tena ragu-ragu: Alice bawang tena nasannang punna ni suroi kamma anjo. “Anne sanna' appakamalla'na,” nakanamo ri kalenna, “carana sikontu olo'-oloka a'gea'-gea'. Sukku'mi untu' ampa'jari tau gilo!”

Footman sanna' na pikkiri' anne kesempatang baji' untu' ampa'nassaang pole kana-kananna, siagang variasi. “Inakke la ammempo anrinni,” nakanamo, “on siagang off, allo-allo siagang allo-allo.”

“mingka apa lakugaukang ?” nakanamo alice.

“Apa-apa mamo nu ero,” nakanamo Footman, nampa appakkaramulami a siul.

“Oh, tena matu-matunna a'bicara siagang ia,” nakanamo Alice putus asa: “ia sanna' tolona!” Nampa nasungke pakkebuka nampa antama.

Anjo pakkebuka antama tojeng mange ri dapo lompo, ia rassia asep battu ri se'reang cappa mange ri cappa maraengannaya: Duchess ammempo ri bangku tallu bangkeng ri tangnga-tangngana, ansusui ana' lolo; tukang masak a'dongko' ri pepeka, a'goccang panci lompo sanna' rassi sup.

“Tantu jai sikali lada ri anjo suppaya!” Alice angkana ri kalenna, kamma to'ji akkulle nagaukang untu' a'sing.

sanna' jaina anjo ri udara. Ba'lalo anjo Duchess a'singka' sikali-kali; siagang ri sesena ana loloa, ia akkiok-kio siagang akkiok-kio tena na ammari-mari. Se'reji bawang barang-barang ri dapura yang tena na bersin, iami antu tukang masak, siagang kucing lompo ammempo ri perapian siagang accillaki battu ri tolinna mange ri tolinna.

“Tolong akkulleko ampauanga,” nakanamo Alice, sike’de’ malla’, nasaba’ tena nasanna’ tappa’na angkanaya bajiki batena a’bicara riolo, “angngapa na anjo kucingnu a’ringis kamma anjo?”

“Iamintu kucing Cheshire,” nakanamo Duchess, “siagang iaminjo sabakna. Bawi!”

Nakana kana kala’busang siagang sanna’ gassinga sa’genna alice sanna’mi lompona; mingka nacini'mi ri wattu maraeng angkua anjo nitujuangi mange ri ana' loloa, teai mange ri ia, jari a'baranimi, nampa a'lampa pole:—

“Inakke tena ku isse'i angkua kucing Cheshire tuli a'ringis; sitoje'-toje'na, inakke tena ku isse'i angkua kucing akkullei a'senyum.”

“Ikatte ngaseng akkullei,” nakanamo Duchess; “siagang loheangna ke’nanga anggaukangi.”

“Inakke tena ku isse'i rie' tau anggaukangi,” nakanamo Alice sanna' sopanna, sanna' rannuna nyawana lanri akkullena a'bicara.

“Ikau tena nu isse' jai,” nakanamo Duchess; “siagang anjo fakta.”

Alice tena sikali nasannang nyawana ri anne kana-kanaya, siagang napikkiriki angkanaya bajikangngangi punna napa'nassaang poleang pa'bicaraang maraeng. Ri wattu ia ancobai ampakabajiki se'rea, tukang masak naallemi panci sup battu ri pepeka, nampa appakkaramulami anjama ampasambilai sikontu apa-apa niaka ri ampi'na mange ri Duchess siagang ana' loloa—bosi-bosi api riolo; nampa nipinawangi je'ne' panci, piring, siagang piring. Duchess tena na parhatikangi ke'nanga manna pole na serang ke'nanga; siagang anjo anaka sanna'mi sanna'na akkiok-kio'na, sa'genna takkulleai nipau angkanaya anjo pa'bakkaka sanna'mi pa'risi'na yareka tena.

“Oh, tolong parhatikangi apa nu pare!” ammarrang Alice, a'lumpa' nai' naung ri pa'risi'na pa'risi'na. “Oh, anjoremmi idongna sannaka anggaranna !” sangkamma panci lompo luar biasa a'lette' ri ampi'na, siagang sanna' a'reppese'na angngerangi.

“Punna massing-massing tau angngukkuruki urusanna kalenna,” nakanamo Duchess siagang sa’ra serak, “linoa la’bi linta’mi a’lampa mange ri se’reang pa’janjiang.”

“Ikatte anre' na'kulle a'jari wassele',” angkua Alice, ia sanna' rannuna punna rie' kasampatang ampa'piitteang sike'de' pangngassengangna. "Pikkiri'mi apa jama-jamanna anjo alloa siagang bangngia! Nucini'mi buttaya angngalle wattu ruampulo appa' jam untu' a'pinra ri porosna—"

“Abicara kapak,” nakanamo Duchess, “potong ulunna!”

Alice anciniki sanna' linguna ri tukang masak, untu' anciniki punna ero'i angngalle pappakainga'; mingka anjo tukang masakka sibukki a'goccang sup, siagang sanna' tena nalangngere, jari napatulusu'mi pole: “Duampulo appa' jam, kupikkiriki ; iareka sampulo anrua? Inakke—”

“Oh, teako susai inakke ,” nakanamo Duchess; “Inakke tena kukkulle antahangi angkana!” siagang anjo appakkaramulami ansusui ana'na pole, akkelong se're lagu lullaby ri wattu nagaukanga, siagang assarei goyang sanna' ri cappa'na massing-massing barisang:

“A’bicarako kasar mae ri ana’ lolonu,
siagang pa'risi punna a'rinra:
Ia anggaukangi anjo untu' ampa'risi pa'mai',
Nasaba ia naisse'i anjo a'goda.

CHORUS.
(Ri kere-keremae anjo tukang masak siagang ana' loloa amminawang):

“Wah! wah! wah!”

Ri wattu Duchess akkelong bait maka ruana anjo lagua, ia tuli na pa'letteki anjo ana' loloa nai' siagang naung, siagang anjo ana' lolo kasi-asia akkiok-kio' sanna', sa'genna Alice tena nakkulle nalangngere' kana-kana:—

“Inakke a'bicara kodi mange ri ana' bura'neku,
Inakke antunrungi punna a'rinra;
Nasaba ia akkullei sanna' rannuna
Lada punna ia ero!

CHORUS.

“Wah! wah! wah!”

“Anne! akkullei nuparakai sike'de', punna ero'jako!” nakanamo Duchess mange ri Alice, na napasambila ana loloa mange ri ia ri wattu a'bicarana. “Inakke musti alampa siagang appasadia untu a'balle croquet siagang ratu,” nampa akkaro-karo assulu battu ri kamaraka. Tukang masak na pa'le'ba'mi panci ri bokoangna ri wattu assulu'na, mingka anre' na'kulle.

Alice anjakkalaki anjo anaka siagang susa, nasaba iami antu olo'-olo' ca'di berbentuk aneh, siagang napajonjoi limanna siagang bangkenna mange ri kere-keremae, “sangkamma juku' bintoeng,” nakanamo Alice. Anjo ana lolo kasi-asia akkiok-kio sangkamma mesin uap ri wattu najakkala, siagang tuli na gandai kalenna siagang na luruskan pole, sa'genna sanna', untu' se're yareka rua menit, iami antu sanna' jaina akkulle nagaukang untu' anjagai.

Punna lebaki naasseng cara baji'na untu' anjagai, (iamintu a'pinra a'jari se're simpul, nampa natahang baji'-baji' tolinna kanang siagang bangkenna kairi, sollanna tena na'jari ancuru' kalenna,) naerangmi assulu' mange ri udara terbuka. “Punna tena kuerang anne anaka mange ri nakke,” nakanamo Alice, “pasti nabunoi ke’nanga lalang se’re yareka rua allo: maka tena na’jari pammunoang punna nibokoi?” Nakana kana-kana kala’busang siagang sa’ra lompo, siagang anjo apa ca’dia akkiok-kio’mi appiali (le’ba’mi ammari a’rinra ri wattu kammaya anne). “Teako akkiok-kio,” nakanamo Alice; “anjo teai cara yang cocok untu ampa'nyatai kalennu.”

Anjo ana' loloa akkiok-kio' pole, siagang Alice sanna' linguna anciniki rupanna untu' anciniki apa kajariangna. Tena nakkulle ni ragukan angkua anjo idongna sanna'i sanna'na, sanna'i sangkamma moncongna naia idongna sitojennaya; kammayatompa matanna a'jari sanna' ca'dina untu' ana' lolo: sikontuna Alice tena nasannang ri tanja'na anjo baranga. “mingka gassingka anjo angngarruji bawang,” nakanamo lalang nyawana, nampa nacini’ pole matanna, untu’ anciniki punna nia’ je’ne’ matanna.

Tena, tena je'ne' matanna. “Punna erokki ajari bawi, saribattang,” nakanamo Alice, serius, “tenamo apa-apa lakugaukang siagang ikatte. Pikkiri kamma kammayya anne!” Anjo ana lolo kasi-asia angngarru poleang (yareka akkiok-kio, tena nakkulle nipau kereanga), nampa a'lampa ke'nanga sallo-sallo anre' na'bicara.

Alice appakkaramulami appikkiri ri kalenna, “Kamma-kamma anne, apa lakugaukang siagang anne olo’-oloka punna battuma’ ri balla’?” ri wattu akkiok-kio'na pole, sanna' gassinga, sa'genna nacini'mi rupanna siagang malla'. Anne wattua tena nakkulle sala ri sesena: anjo teai labbi yareka kurang battu ri bawi, siagang ia napikkiri angkua sanna' absurdna punna ia angngerangi mange ri dallekang.

Jari napadongko'mi anjo olo'-olo' ca'dia, siagang sanna'mi rannuna nyawana anciniki anjo olo'-olo' ca'dia a'dakka-dakka antama' ri romanga. “Punna lompomi,” nakanamo ri kalenna, “la’jari ana’ sanna’ ja’dala’na: mingka a’jari bawi sanna’ ga’gana, kupikkiriki.” Nampa appakkaramulami appikkiri ana'-ana' maraeng naisse'a, yang akkulle sanna' baji'na salaku bawi, siagang nakanamo ri kalenna, “punna rie' tau angngassengi cara annabaya untu' a'pinra—” ri wattu sanna'mi lannasa'na nacini' Cheshire Cat ammempo ri tangke poko' kayu pirangalloang bellana.

Anjo kucinga akkiok-kioji punna nacini alice. Anjo sanna baji'na, nakana pikkiranna: mingka sanna' la'buna cakarna siagang jai giginna, jari napikkiri'mi angkanaya parallui nipakalompo.

“Cheshire Puss,” appakkaramulami, sanna’ malla’na, nasaba’ tena sikali naasseng angkanaya ero’ji anjo arenga: mingka, sanna’mi la’buna. “Maeko, sanna' rannuna sa'genna kamma-kamma anne,” nakanamo Alice, nampa a'lampa. “Maka akkulleko ampauanga, tolong, kemae oloang musti ku lampa battu anrinni?”

“Anjo sanna'i ta'ganttung ri kemae ero'nu mange,” nakanamo Kucing.

“Inakke tena ku peduli kemae—” nakanamo Alice.

“Nampa tena naparallu kemae oloang nu lampa,” nakanamo Kucing.

“—asalkan inakke anrapi ri se’reang tampa’ ,” tambai Alice salaku pa’pau.

“Oh, ikatte pasti anggaukangi anjo,” nakanamo Kucing, “punna ikatte ajjappa salloji.”

Alice napikkiri angkua anne anre' na'kulle ni sangkal, jari nacobai pakkuta'nang maraeng. “Apa anjo tau ammantang anrinni?”

“Ri anjo arah,” nakanamo Kucing, na lambai bangkenna kanangna, “ammantang se’re Tukang Topi: siagang ri anjo arah,” na lambai bangkenna maraenga, “ammantangi se’re Kelinci Maret. Kunjungi manna pole apa nueroka: rua-ruanna sanna’ linguna.”

“mingka inakke tena naku ero mange ri tangnga-tangngana tau gila,” nakanamo alice.

“Oh, tena nukkulle antulungi anjo,” nakanamo Kucing: “ikatte ngaseng sanna larrona anrinni. Inakke larrona. Ikau larrona.”

“Antekamma batenu angngassengi angkanaya inakke larro?” nakanamo alice.

“Ikau musti,” nakanamo Kucing, “punna tena nu battu anrinni.”

Alice tena na pikkiri anjo ampa'butti anjo; manna mamo kamma, ia tulusu “siagang antekamma batenu angngassengi angkanaya ikatte sanna larrona?”

“Untuk appakkaramula,” nakanamo Kucing, “anjo kongkonga tena na gila. Nutarimai anjo?”

“Kupikkiriki,” nakanamo Alice.

“Baji, punna kamma,” anjo kucinga tulusu, “nucini, kongkong akkiok-kio punna larroi, siagang akkiok-kio ikkona punna sannang nyawaku. Kamma-kamma anne akkiok- kiokka punna sannang nyawaku, siagang akkiok-kioki ikkokku punna larroi. Lanri kammana anjo inakke sanna larrona.”

“ Inakke areng anjo akkiok-kio, teai akkiok-kio,” nakanamo Alice.

“Areng apa nu ero,” nakanamo kucing. “Maka a'balle croquet siagang ratu anne alloa?”

“Inakke musti sanna kukaerokia,” nakanamo Alice, “mingka inakke tenapa niundang.”

“Lanuciniki inakke anjoreng,” nakanamo Kucing, nampa lanynya.

Alice tena nasanna' lannasa'na ri anne passalaka, ia sanna'mi biasana ri passala'na apa-apa aneh kajariang. Ri wattu nacini'na anjo tampaka riolo, ti'ring ammumba poleang.

“By-the-bye, apa kajariangna anjo anaka?” nakanamo anjo kucinga. “Inakke sanna kukaluppai akkuta’nang.”

“Anjo a’jari bawi,” nakanamo Alice sa’ra sannang, sangkamma ammotere’mi lalang cara alami.

“Kupikkiriki angkanaya la’jari,” nakanamo Kucing, nampa lanynya’ pole.

Alice antayangi sike'de', sitangnga sanna' ero'na anciniki pole, mingka anre' na'kulle ammumba, siagang punna le'baki se're yareka rua menit a'jappa tulusu'mi mange ri arah na anjo March Hare nikana ammantang. “Inakke lebaki kucini tukang topi riolo,” nakanamo ri kalenna; “March Hare iami antu kaminang menarik, siagang gassingka anne bulang Mei tena na sanna gilana—paling kurang tena na sanna gilana sangkamma ri bulang Maret.” Ri wattu akkana kamma anjo, nacini'mi nai', nampa nia'mo pole Kucing, ammempo ri tangke poko' kayua.

“Apa nukana bawi, iareka ara?” nakanamo anjo kucinga.

“Kukana bawi,” appialimi Alice; “siagang erokka tena nu tuli ammumba siagang lanynya sanna' tiba-tiba: nu pa'jari se're tau sanna' lingu.”

“Baji,” nakanamo Kucing; siagang anne hattua lanynya sanna' lambatna, appakkaramula ri cappa'na ikko'na, siagang a'le'basa' ri accilla'na, ia niaka ija sike'de' ribokoangna sisa'la'na a'lampa.

“Eh! Inakke biasana kucini kucing tena na sanna,” nakanamo Alice; “mingka accillaki tena kucing! iami antu hal yang paling menarik kucini ri tallasa ku!”

Ia anre'pa na'lampa bella riolo ri anre'napa nacini' balla'na March Hare: na pikkiri'mi angkua anjo balla'na sanna' tojengna, nasaba' cerobong asapna sangkamma tolinna siagang pattongko'na nitongko' siagang bulu. Anjo ballaka sanna' lompona, sa'genna tena naero' a'reppese' sa'genna nakanre pole sike'de' jamur ri sa'ri kairinna, nampa naangka' kalenna kira-kira rua bangkeng tinggina: manna mamo kamma anjo a'jappa mange ri anjo ballaka sanna' malla'na, nakana ri kalenna “Ebara' angkana anjo balla'a sanna' linguna! Ero' dudua' a'lampa mange ri jamur!”
