BAB II.
Kolam je'ne mata

“Labbi sanna' ero'na siagang sanna' sanna'na!” ammarrang Alice (sanna'mi lannasa'na, sa'genna kamma-kamma anne sanna'mi nakaluppai antekamma batena a'bicara Inggris baji'); “kamma-kamma anne kusungkei sangkamma teleskop kaminang lompo le’baka nia’! Salama’, bangkeng!” (nasaba ri wattu nacini'na bangkenna, sanna'mi sanna'na, sanna'mi bellana). "Oh, bangkengku ca'di kasi-asia, inakke sanna' kukaerokia inai la'pakei sapatunu siagang stokingnu untu' ikatte kamma-kamma anne, saribattang? Inakke tappaka tena nakukkulle! Inakke la'bi bellai untu' ampa'risi pa'maikku ri sesena ikatte: ikatte musti angngatoro' cara kaminang baji' nukkullea;—mingka inakke musti baji' pa'mai' mange ri ke'nanga," nakanamo Alice, "yareka gassingka tena nala'jappa ri oloang kueroka! ke’nanga sepasang sapatu beru tunggala allo natal.

Nampa tulusu'mi a'rancana ri kalenna antekamma batena la'pa'sadia. “Ikatte musti alampa ri karetaya,” nakanamo; “siagang antekamma lucuna anjo, akkiring hadia mange ri bangkenna tonji! siagang antekamma sanna'na anjo arah-arah!

bangkeng kanangna alice, esq.,
Hearthrug,
ri ampi'na Fender, ( siagang pangngamaseanna Alice ).

Oh sayang, apa omong kosong ku bicaraya!”

Anjo wattua ulunna antama ri pattongko aula: sitoje'-toje'na ia kamma-kamma anne labbi salapang bangkeng tinggina, nampa ia memang anjo wattua naallemi kunci bulaeng ca'di nampa a'lampa linta' mange ri pakke'bu' kokoa.

Alice kasi-asi! anjo sanna' jaina akkulle nagaukang, attinro ri se'reang sa'ri, anciniki ri kokoa siagang se're matanna; mingka untu' allalo sanna'mi tena panrannuanna na riolo: ammempomi siagang appakkaramulami angngarru' pole.

"Ikau musti siri'-siri' ri kalennu," nakanamo Alice, "ana' baine hebaka sangkamma ikau," (akkullei nakana kamma anne), "untu' tulusu' angngarru' kamma anne! Pa'le'baki anne wattua, kukana ri kau!" Mingka ia tulusu'ji, a'lo'loro je'ne' matanna, sanggenna nia'mo se're kolam lompo ri tammulilina, kira-kira appa' inci la'buna siagang narapi' bageang rawanganna lorong.

Ribokoangna wattu nalangngere sike'de' bangkeng ri bellaya, nampa akkaro-karo a'pa'je'ne' matanna untu' anciniki apa lakajarianga. Iamintu Kelinci Puti ammotere, ammakeang sanna baji, siagang sarung tangan pute ri se'reang limanna siagang kipas lompo ri limanna maraenga: battui a'dakka-dakka siagang sanna' linta'na, a'mere'-mere' ri kalenna ri wattu battuna, “Oh! Duchess, Duchess! Oh! tena na'jari liar punna inakke ampa'tarrusui antayangi!” Alice sanna'mi pa'risi'na pa'mai'na sa'genna sadiami appala' bantuang ri tau maraenga; jari, ri wattu battuna kelinci mange ri ia, appakkaramulami, siagang sa’ra rannu siagang malla’, “Punna ero’jako, karaeng—” Kelinci appakkaramulami sanna’ gassinga, napela’mi sarung ana’ loloa siagang kipas anginna, nampa a’lampa mange ri sassanga sa’genna akkulle.

Alice angngallei kipas siagang sarung tangan, siagang, nasaba aula sanna bambangna, ia tuli nakipas kalenna sanna' sanna'na a'bicara: “Sayang, sayang! Antekamma sanna'na sanna'na anne alloa! Na ri allo rioloa sanna'mi sanna'na. Ero' dudua' angngassengi punna le'ba'ma' nipinra ri bangngia? Ero' dudua' appikkiri': maka sangkammaji ri wattungku ambangung bari'basa' anne? Mingka akkullei kupikkiriki sike'de' maraeng. Inakke tena nasangkamma, pakkuta’nang ribokoangna iamintu, inai ri lino inakke? Ah, anjo teka-teki lompoa!” Nampa appakkaramulami appikkiri ngaseng ana'-ana' naisse'a ia umuru'na sangkamma kalenna, untu' anciniki punna akkullei nipinra untu' manna sitau battu ri ke'nanga.

"Inakke tappaka inakke teai ada," nakanamo, "nasaba uu'na sanna' la'buna, siagang inakke tena na'jari sanna' la'buna; siagang inakke tappaka tena nakukkulle a'jari Mabel, nasaba' kuasseng ngase'mi apa-apa, siagang ia, oh! ia sanna' sike'de' naasseng! Nampa pole, iami ia, siagang inakke inakke, siagang—oh, antekamma sanna'na punna kuasseng ngasengi! apa-apa yang biasa ku isse'i: appa kali lima iamintu sampulo anrua, siagang appa kali annang iamintu sampulo antallu, siagang appa kali tuju iamintu —oh dear! Inakke musti nipinra mange ri Mabel! Inakke lakucobai siagang angkana ' Antekamma batena anjo ana' loloa —'” nampa napasilangngeri limanna ri bangkenna sangkamma angkana a'pau pappilajarang, nampa appakkaramulami a'pau poleang, mingka sa'ranna sanna' sanna'na siagang sanna' appakalannasa'na, siagang anjo kana-kanaya tena nasangkamma biasaya:—

“Antekamma batena anjo buaya ca’dia
Pa bajiki ikko na accillaka,
siagang tumpa je'ne'na binangaya
Ri tunggala timbangang bulaeng!

“Antekamma rannuna ia sanna' sanna'na,
Antekamma baji'na ampa'la'bangi cakarna,
siagang tarimai juku cadia ri
siagang rahang accillaki!

“Inakke tappaka anjo teai kana-kana annabaya,” nakanamo Alice kasi-asia, siagang matanna rassi siagang je’ne’ matanna pole ri wattu napatulusu’na, “Inakke musti Mabel ribokoangna, siagang inakke musti a’lampa siagang ammantang ri anjo balla’ ca’dia, siagang tena sikali pa’maina untu’ a’balle-balle, siagang oh! sanna’ jaina pappilajarang lakupilajari! Tena, le’ba’mi kupattantu punna nia’ja’ anrinni Mabel! Tena matu-matunna ke'nanga ampadongkoki ulunna siagang angkana 'Maeko pole, saribattang!' Inakke lakuciniki bawang siagang angkana 'Inai inakke punna kamma? Pauanga' anjo rolong, nampa, punna erokka a'jari anjo taua, inakke la'lampa: punna tena, inakke la'bangngi anrinni sa'genna a'jari tau maraeng'—mingka, oh sayang!” ammarrang Alice, siagang je'ne' mata ta'bangka, "Kuero' dudui ke'nanga ampadongkoki ulunna! Inakke sanna'mi roso'na punna kale-kalengku anrinni!"

Ri wattu akkana kamma anjo nacini'mi limanna, siagang lannasa'mi nacini' angkanaya napakemi sala se're sarung ana' lolo kebo'na Kelinci ri wattu a'bicarana. “Antekamma bateku akkulle anggaukangi anjo?” nakanamo. “Inakke musti a'jari ca'di pole.” Ambangummi nampa mange ri meja untu angngukuri kalenna, siagang narapi'mi, sanna'mi tinggina, kamma-kamma anne kira-kira rua bangkeng tinggina, siagang a'la'bangmi sanna' linta'na: tena nasallo naasseng tommi angkanaya anjo saba'na iamintu kipas anginna na pegang, nampa napela' linta', ri wattu-wattu tojeng-tojenga sollanna tena na'jari ca'di sanna'.

“ Anjo pelarian sempit!” nakanamo Alice, sanna' malla'na ri anjo pa'pinraang tiba-tiba, mingka sanna' rannuna nasaba' nia' ija; siagang kamma kammayya anne untuk kokoa! siagang ia lari siagang sanna' linta'na ammotere' mange ri pakke'bu' ca'dia: mingka, alas! anjo pakkebuka cadia nitongko pole, siagang kunci bulaeng cadia niaki irate ri mejang kaca sangkamma riolo, “siagang anjo passalaka labbi kodia na riolo,” nakanamo anjo ana’ kasi-asia, “nasaba’ inakke tena le’bakai sanna’ ca’dina kamma anne riolo, tena!

Ri wattu napau anjo kana-kanaya bangkenna ta'lappasa', siagang ri wattu maraeng, ta'lappasa'! ia sa'genna dagu ri je'ne' asinna. Pikkirang uru-uruna iamintu ia anre' na'kulle tu'guru' ri tamparanga, "siagang punna kamma anjo akkullei ammotere' siagang kareta api," nakanamo ri kalenna. (Alice lebba'mi mange ri biring tamparanga sikali lalang tallasa'na, siagang battumi ri kesimpulang umum, angkua kemae-kemae mange ri biring tamparanga Inggris rie'mo nubuntulu' siapa are mesin pa'je'ne' ri tamparanga, siapa are ana'-ana' a'kali ri pasi'a siagang sekop kayu, nampa se're barisang balla' pa'bangngiang, siagang ri bokoangna ke'nanga stasiun kareta api.) Mingka, tena nasallo na'kulle assulu' angkanaya nia'mi ri kolam renang ia le'bakamo a'je'ne' ri wattunta a'je'ne'. tinggina salapang bangkeng.

“Erokka tena na sanna angngarru!” nakanamo alice, ri wattu a'lange-lange, ancobai amboyai oloang assulu. "Inakke lanihukkungi kamma kammayya anne, inakke sanna', lanri ancuru'na ri je'ne' matangku! Anjo la'jari se're passala' aneh, untu' ampa'nassai! Mingka, yangasenna aneh kamma-kamma anne."

Anjo wattua nalangngere nia apa-apa a'cidong ri kolam renang sike'de' bellana, nampa a'lange mange ri ampi'na untu' angngassengi apa anjo: ri uru-uruna napikkiriki angkanaya anjo walrus yareka kuda nil, mingka nampami nau'rangi antekamma ca'dina kamma-kamma anne, siagang tena nasallo naasseng angkanaya anjo tikusji bawang antama' sangkamma kalenna.

"Maka nia' matu-matunna, kamma-kamma anne," nakanamo Alice, "a'bicara siagang anne tikus? Sikontu apa-apaya sanna' sanna'na anrinni, sa'genna kupikkiriki angkanaya akkullei a'bicara: manna antekamma, tena apa-apa salana punna ancobai." Jari appakkaramulami: "O Tikus, maka nu isse'i oloang assulu battu ri anne kolam? Inakke sanna'mi roso'na a'lange-lange anrinni, O Tikus!" (Alice na pikkiri anne musti cara annaba a'bicara mange ri tikus: ia anre'pa nale'ba' anggaukangi anjo kammaya riolo, mingka nau'rangimi nacini' ri tata bahasa latinna saribattangna, “Se're tikus—se're tikus—ri se're tikus—se're tikus—o tikus!”) Tikus anciniki ia sanna' sanna' ero'na angngassengi, siagang sanna' sanna'na sanna'na sanna'na, mingka tena apa-apa napau.

“Mungkin tena napahangi bahasa inggris,” nakanamo Alice; “Inakke barani angkana iami antu tikus perancis, battu mako siagang William the Conqueror.” (Nasaba, siagang sikontu pangngassengangna ri sesena sejarah, Alice tena na sanna' singara'na antekamma sallona apa-apa kajariang.) Jari appakkaramulami pole: “Où est ma chatte?” iami antu kalimat maka se're ri buku pangngajarang bahasa perancisna. Tikus a'lumpa' ta'bangka assulu' battu ri je'neka, siagang sanna'mi linguna lanri malla'na. “Oh, appala’ doanga’ ri kau!” ammarrang Alice sanna' linta'na, malla'na nasaba' na pa'risi pa'mai'na anjo olo'-olo' kasi-asia. “Inakke sanna kukaluppai angkanaya tena nu sanna ri kucing.”

“Tena nasangkamma kucing!” ammarrang Tikus, siagang sa'ra sanna' sanna'na. “Maka erokki kucing punna ikatte antu inakke?”

“Eh, gassingka tena,” nakanamo Alice siagang nada sannaka sannanna: “teako larroi anjo passalaka. Na manna mamo kamma erokka’ ampappicinikangi mae ri kau anjo kucingta Dinah: Kupikkiriki angkanaya la’bi nangai anjo kucinga punna akkulleji nucini’. Ia sanna’ sannanna nyawana,” Alice tulusu’mi, sitangnga ri kalenna, ri wattu a’lange-lange malasa’na ri kolam renang, “siagang sanna’ baji’na, sanna’ baji’na akkiok-kio' bangkenna siagang a'je'ne' rupanna—siagang ia sanna' baji'na untu' niparakai—siagang ia sanna' baji'na untu' anjakkalaki tikus—oh, appala' doanga' ri kau!” ammarrang pole Alice, nasaba anne wattua anjo Tikus a'bulu ngasengi, siagang ia sanna'mi sanna'na. “Tenamo na la'bicara pole ri sesena ia punna tena nu ero.”

“Ikatte memang!” akkiok-kio'mi tikus, ia a'gio'-gio' sa'genna ri cappa'na ikko'na. “Sanrapang inakke a'bicara ri sesena anjo! Kaluarga ikatte tuli na benci kucing: ja'dala, rannu, siagang vulgar! Teako pa'biangi inakke allangngere pole anjo arenga!”

“Inakke tena memang!” nakanamo alice, sanna' linta'na ero' a'pinra topik pa'bicaraanga. “Apa nu—nu sanna—ri—kongkong?” Tikus tena na appiali, jari Alice tulusu'mi siagang sanna' ero'na: “Nia' kongkong ca'di sanna' baji'na ri ampi'na balla'ta ero' kupappicinikang mae ri kau! Se're kongkong ca'di mata accilla', nuassengi, siagang oh, sanna' la'buna uu'na a'ra'ra'! Na ia la'allei barang-barang punna nupela', siagang ia la'dongko' siagang appala' doang untu' kanre bangngi, siagang sangka' rupaya barang-barang—siagang tena kukkulle angngu'rangi bageruanna—siagang iami antu bageang battu ri ke'nanga mange ri pajama koko, nu isse', siagang nakana sanna'i matu-matunna, anggaranna sibilangang pon! ammarrang Alice siagang suara susa, “Mallakka’ punna ku pa’risi pa’maikku pole!” Nasaba anjo Tikus a'lange-lange bellai battu ri ia sanna' gassinga, siagang appa'nia' karicuang ri kolam ri wattu a'jappa.

Jari akkiok sanna' sanna'na ribokoangna, "Tikus sayang! Ammotere' mako pole, siagang tena todong na la'bicaraki' ri sesena kucing yareka kongkong, punna tena nuero' ri ke'nanga!" Ri wattu nalangngere anjo Tikus, a'gilimmi siagang a'lange-lange ammotere' mange ri ia: rupanna sanna'mi kebo'na (siagang sanna'mi ero'na, nakanamo Alice), nampa nakanamo siagang sa'ra sanna' rannuna, “Maeko mange ri biring tamparanga, nampa lakupabattuangko sejarahku, nampa nupahang angkana angngapa na kukabirisi kucing siagang kongkong.”

Anne wattua sanna'mi sallona a'lampa, nasaba' anjo kolam renang sanna'mi rassi siagang jangang-jangang siagang olo'-olo' antama' ri anjo kolam: rie' se're bembe siagang se're dodo, se're lory siagang se're elang, siagang siapa are olo'-olo' maraeng. Alice ampimpingi, siagang sikontu rombonganga a'lange mange ri biring tamparanga.
