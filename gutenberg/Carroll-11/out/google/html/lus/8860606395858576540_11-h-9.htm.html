BUNG IX.
The Mock Turtle chanchin

“Ka hmuh leh che ka lawm zia hi i ngaihtuah thei lo, nang putar duhtak!” Duchess chuan a kut chu hmangaihna takin Alice-i kutah a thun a, an kal dun ta a.

Alice chuan chutiang thinrimna nuam tak a hmuh chu a lawm hle a, kitchen-a an inhmuh lai khan pepper chauh hian a savawm hle mai thei tih a ngaihtuah a.

“Duchess ka nih lai chuan,” a ti a, (beiseina rilru pu takin ni lo mah se), “Ka kitchen-ah hian pepper ka nei lo tak tak ang bitter—leh—leh barley-sugar leh naupangte rilru ti na thei thilte chu chu chu miten an hriat ka duh chauh a ni : chuti a nih chuan chutiang chuan an stingy lo ang, i hria em—”

Hetih lai hian Duchess chu a theihnghilh tawh hle a, a beng bulah a aw a hriat chuan a mangang deuh hlek a ni. “Thil engemaw i ngaihtuah a, ka hmangaih, chu chuan thusawi a theihnghilh tir che a ni. Tunah chauh hian chutiang moral chu eng nge ni tih ka hrilh thei lo che a, mahse rei lo teah ka hrechhuak leh ang.”

“A nei lo pawh a ni mai thei,” Alice chuan sawi chhuah a tum a.

“Tut, tut, naupang!” Duchess chuan a ti a. “Engkim hian moral a nei vek a, i hmuh theih phawt chuan.” Tin, a thusawi lai chuan Alice bulah chuan a inhnukdawk hnai hle bawk.

Alice-i chuan a hnaih lutuk chu a duh vak lo: pakhatnaah chuan, Duchess chu a hmelchhiat em avangin; tin, pahnihnaah chuan, Alice-i kekawr ipte chunga a hmui nghah tur khawpa san zawng a nih vang a ni a, chu chu hmui hrual nuam lo tak a nih vang a ni. Mahse, zahmawh taka awm chu a duh lo va, a theih ang tawkin a bore a ni.

“Tun dinhmunah chuan game hi a kal tha zawk deuh a ni,” a ti a, inbiakna tlem a tih chhunzawm zelna tur a ni.

“‘Tis so,” Duchess chuan a ti a: “chumi nungchang chu—‘Aw, ‘tis love, ‘tis love, that makes the world go round!’” a ti a.

“Mi pakhatin a sawi a,” Alice chuan a ti sap a, “mi zawng zawngin mahni thil ngaihtuahin an ti vek a ni!”

“Ah, a tha e! A awmzia chu a inang hle mai,” Duchess chuan a ti leh a, a hmui te tak te hrual tak chu Alice-i kekawr ipteah chuan a lai lut a, “ chuta nungchang chu—‘Sense chu enkawl la, aw te chuan anmahni an in enkawl ang.’” tiin a sawi belh a.

“Thiltea nungchang hmuh chu a va duh tak em!” Alice chuan a ti rilru a.

“Engvangin nge i kawr chhungah ka kut ka dah loh tih i ngaihtuah ngam tih ka sawi ngam,” Duchess chuan a chawlh hnu chuan a ti a: “a chhan chu, i flamingo thinrimna hi ka ringhlel vang a ni. Experiment hi ka lo enchhin dawn em ni?”

“A seh mai thei,” tiin Alice chuan fimkhur takin a chhang a, experiment chu tih tum chu a lungkham lo tak zet a ni.

Duchess-i chuan: “A dik hle mai,” tiin Duchess-i chuan a sawi: “flamingo leh mustard-te hian an seh ve ve a ni. Chumi nungchang chu—‘Sava chi khata savate chu an pungkhawm khawm a ni.’” a ti a.

“Mustard chauh hi sava a ni lo,” tiin Alice chuan a sawi.

“A dik e, a hma ang bawkin,” Duchess chuan a ti a: “thil dah dan chiang tak i nei em!”

“Mineral a ni, ka ti ,” Alice chuan a ti a.

“A ni ngei ang,” Duchess chuan a ti a, Alice-i thusawi zawng zawng chu pawm vek ang maiin a lang a; “he lai bulah hian mustard-mine lian tak a awm a. Chumi nungchang chu—‘Ka ta tam poh leh nangmah ta pawh a tlem zawk.’” tih hi a ni.

“Aw, ka hria!” He thusawi hnuhnung ber ngaihtuah lo Alice-i chuan, “Thlai a ni. Pakhat angin a lang lo, mahse a ni,” tiin a au chhuak a.

“I thusawi hi ka pawm khawp mai,” Duchess chuan a ti a; “chu mi nungchang chu—‘I nih ang ang kha ni rawh’—a nih loh leh a awlsam zawka sawi i duh chuan—‘Mi dangte tâna i nihna emaw, i nih theih dân emaw chu i nihna ang lo takin an lo lang ang tih loh chu thil dang a ni lo tih mi dangte tâna thil dang ni lo tûrin inngaihtuah ngai suh.’”

“Chu chu ka hrethiam zawk tur niin ka hria,” Alice chuan zahawm takin a ti a, “ziak ka neih chuan: mahse i sawi angin ka zawm chiang thei lo.”

“Chu chu ka duh chuan ka sawi theih tur chu engmah a ni lo,” Duchess chuan lawm takin a chhang a.

“Tawngtai rawh chu aia rei sawi tur chuan buai suh,” Alice chuan a ti a.

“Aw, buaina thu sawi suh!” Duchess chuan a ti a. “Tuna ka sawi tawh zawng zawng chu present atan ka siam che a ni.”

“Pregen chi khat man tlawm tak!” Alice chuan a ngaihtuah a. “Chutiang birthday present an pek loh avangin ka lawm e!” Mahse, a aw ring taka sawi chu a tum lo.

“Ngaihtuah nawn leh maw?” Duchess chuan a ti a, a hmui te tak te hrual chu a laih leh bawk.

“Ngaihtuah theihna ka nei a ni,” Alice chuan na takin a ti a, a chhan chu a lungkham deuh tan tawh a ni.

Duchess chuan, “Vawk an thlawh a ngaih ang chiah chiah a dik a;leh m—” a ti a.

Mahse hetah hian Alice-i mak tih em em chu Duchess aw chu a thi ta vek a, a thumal duh ber ‘moral’ tih lai takah pawh a thi ta a, a kut nena inzawm tlat chu a khur ta a ni. Alice chuan a han en a, an hmaah chuan Lalnu chu a ding a, a kut a beng a, thlipui tleh ang maiin a hmui a ti hup a.

“Ni tha tak, i Lalber!” Duchess chuan aw hniam leh chak lo zet hian a ti tan a.

“Tûnah chuan, vaukhânna dik tak ka pe che a ni,” tiin Lalnu chuan a au chhuak a, a thusawi lai chuan leiah a chil a; “nang emaw i lu emaw chu a off tawh ngei ang, chu chu hun chanve vel hnuah!I duhthlanna la rawh!”

Duchess chuan a duhthlanna chu a la a, rei lo te chhungin a bo ta a ni.

“Inkhel hi i kalpui zel ang u,” Lalnu chuan Alice hnenah chuan a ti a; tin, Alice chu a hlau lutuk a, thumal pakhat mah a sawi thei lo va, mahse zawi zawiin croquet-ground lam pan chuan a zui leh ta a.

Khualzin dangte chuan Lalnu awm lohna chu an hmang tangkai a, thlamuang takin an chawl a: mahse, an hmuh meuh chuan inkhelh lam pan chuan an tlan let nghal vat a, Lalnu chuan rei vak lo tihkhawtlai chuan an nunna a chan phah dawn tih a sawi mai a ni.

An inkhelh chhung zawng khan Queen hian player dangte nena inhnialna chu a chawlhsan ngai lo va, “A lu nen off!” emaw, “A lu nen off!” A hremte chu sipaite chuan an man a, chutiang ti tur chuan arch nihna chu an bansan a ngai tih chu a chiang a, chuvang chuan darkar chanve vel a ral meuh chuan arch a awm tawh lo va, Lal, Lalnu leh Alice tih loh chu player zawng zawng chu man an ni a, tihhlum tura hremna hnuaiah an awm ta a ni.

Tichuan Lalnu chuan thawk a la chhuak lo hle a, Alice hnenah chuan, “Mock Turtle hi i la hmu tawh em?”

“Ni lo,” Alice chuan a ti a. “Mock Turtle hi eng nge a nih pawh ka hre lo.”

“Mock Turtle Soup siamna thil a ni,” Lalnu chuan a ti a.

“Pakhat ka hmu ngai lo va, pakhat mah ka hre ngai lo,” tiin Alice-i chuan a sawi.

“Chuti a nih chuan lo kal rawh,” Lalnu chuan a ti a, “a chanchin a hrilh ang che,” a ti a.

An kal dun lai chuan Alice chuan Lal chuan aw hniam takin, company tlangpui hnenah, “In ngaidam vek tawh,” a tih chu a hre ta a. “Lo kal rawh, chu chu thil tha tak a ni!” a ti a, Lalnuin thah tura thupek a pek zat chu a hlim lo hle tih a hriat avangin.

Rei lo teah Gryphon pakhat, ni chhuahna hmuna muhil nghet tak chu an hmu ta a. (Gryphon chu eng nge a nih i hriat loh chuan thlalak hi han en teh.) “Up, lazy thing!” Lalnu chuan, “leh he hmeichhe naupang hi Mock Turtle hmu turin hruai la, a chanchin pawh hre turin hruai rawh. Ka thupek ka tihhlumna thenkhat hnuah ka kir leh a ngai a ni;” tin, a kal ta a, Alice chu Gryphon-a nen chuan a awm dun ta a. Alice chuan chu thilsiam hmel chu a duh vak lo va, mahse a pum puiah chuan chu Lalnu savawm tak hnung zui ang maia a bula awm chu a him khawp ang tih a ngaihtuah a: chuvang chuan a nghak ta a ni.

Gryphon chu a thu a, a mit a chhing a: chutah Lalnu chu a hmuh theih loh thlengin a en reng a: chutah a nui suk a. “A va nuam em!” Gryphon chuan a ti a, a chanve chu amah leh a chanve chu Alice hnenah.

“Eng nge nuam chu?” Alice chuan a ti a.

“Engvangin nge, ani ,” Gryphon chuan a ti a. “A fancy vek a ni, chu chu: tumah an execute ngai lo, i hria em. Lo kal rawh!”

“Mi zawng zawng hian ‘lo kal rawh!’ an ti vek a. hetah hian,” tiin Alice-i chuan a hnungah zawi zawiin a ngaihtuah a: “Ka dam chhung zawng hian hetiang hian thupek ka nei ngai lo, engtikah mah!”

Hmun hla takah Mock Turtle chu lungngai leh mahni chauha thu, lungpui hrual tlemte chungah chuan an hmu hla vak lo va, an hnaih deuh deuh chuan Alice chuan a thinlung a na ang maia a thaw ri chu a hre thei ta a ni. A khawngaih hle mai. “Eng nge a lungngaihna?” Gryphon-a chu a zawt a, Gryphon-a chuan a hma ang bawka thumal inang tak maiin a chhang a, “A fancy zawng zawng a ni, chu chu: lungngaihna a nei lo, i hria em. Lo kal rawh!”

Chuvang chuan Mock Turtle bulah chuan an chho a, ani chuan mit lian tak tak mittui tla chungin a en a, mahse engmah a sawi lo.

“He hmuna hmeichhe naupang hian,” Gryphon chuan a ti a, “i history hriat a duh a ni, a hre ve tho.”

“Ka hrilh ang che,” Mock Turtle chuan aw thuk tak, hollow takin a ti a: “in pahnih hian thu rawh u, ka sawi zawh hma loh chuan thu pakhat mah sawi suh u.”

Chuvang chuan an thu a, minute engemaw zat chu tumah an tawng lo. Alice-i chuan a rilruin, “Engtin nge a tih zawh theih ka hmu lo, a bul a tan loh chuan,” a ti a. Mahse, dawhthei takin a nghak a.

“Vawikhat chu,” a tawpah chuan Mock Turtle chuan a ti a, thaw halh chung hian, “Turtle tak tak ka ni.”

Heng thute hi rei tak ngawi rengin a zui a, a chang chuan “Hjckrrh!” Gryphon atang chuan, leh Mock Turtle-a tap nasat reng reng chu. Alice chu a tho hnai hle a, “Ka pu, i thawnthu ngaihnawm tak avangin ka lawm e,” a ti a, mahse thil dang a la awm ngei ang tih a ngaihtuah thei lo va, chuvang chuan a thu reng a, engmah a sawi lo.

“Kan naupan lai khan,” Mock Turtle chuan a tawpah chuan a ti chhunzawm a, a rilru hahdam zawkin, a chang chuan tlem a la tap rih a, “tuipuiah chuan sikul kan kal a, a pu chu Turtle upa tak a ni—Tortoise kan ti thin—”

“Engvangin nge Tortoise i tih, pakhat a nih loh chuan?” Alice chuan a zawt a.

“Min zirtir avangin Tortoise kan ti a,” Mock Turtle chuan thinrim zet hian a ti a: “dik tak chuan i dull hle mai!”

“Chutiang zawhna awlsam tak i zawh avangin i zak tur a ni,” tiin Gryphon-a chuan a sawi belh a; tichuan, an pahnih chuan ngawi rengin an thu a, Alice rethei tak, leia pil tura inpeih tawh nia inhria chu an en a. A tawpah chuan Gryphon chuan Mock Turtle hnenah chuan, “Drive on, old fellow! Nilengin chutiang thil chu awm suh!” tin, hêng thu hian a sawi chhunzawm zêl a ni.

“Ni e, tuipuiah sikul kan kal a, i ring lo mai thei—”

“Ka ti lo ka ti ngai lo!” Alice chuan a ti tawp a.

“I ti a ni,” Mock Turtle chuan a ti a.

“I lei kha vawn tlat rawh!” Gryphon chuan a ti leh a, Alice-i’n thu a sawi leh hmain. Mock Turtle chu a kal zel a.

“Zirna ṭha ber berte kan nei a—a nihna takah chuan, ni tin sikul kan kal ṭhin—”

“ Day-school-ah pawh ka kal tawh bawk,” Alice-i chuan a ti a; “chutiang zawng zawng ang chuan i chhuang a ngai lo.”

“Extra te nen?” Mock Turtle chuan lungkham deuh hian a zawt a.

Alice-i chuan, “Ni e, French leh music kan zir a ni,” a ti a.

“A nih leh inbual?” Mock Turtle chuan a ti a.

“Ni lo tak zet!” Alice chuan thinrim zet hian a ti a.

“Ah!chuti a nih chuan i school kha chu school tha tak a ni lo,” Mock Turtle chuan rilru hahdamna aw nen a ti a. “Tûnah chuan kan taah chuan bill tâwp lamah, ‘French, music, leh washing —extra’ tih an nei a ni.”

“I duh vak lo thei lo,” Alice-i chuan a ti a; “tuifinriat hnuai lama awmte.”

“Ka zir thei lo.” Mock Turtle chuan thaw halh chung hian a ti a. “Regular course chauh ka zir a ni.”

“Eng nge chu chu?” Alice chuan a zawt a.

“Reeling leh Writhing, a bul tanna atan chuan,” tiin Mock Turtle chuan a chhang a; “chutah chuan Arithmetic branch hrang hrang—Ambition, Distraction, Uglification, leh Derision—te pawh a ni.”

“‘Uglification’ tih hi ka hre ngai lo,” tiin Alice chuan a sawi ngam a. “Eng nge ni?”

Gryphon chuan mak ti zet hian a ke pahnih chu a han chawi sang a. “Eng nge! Uglifying tih hi ka hre ngai lo!” a ti chhuak a. “I hmeltha tur chu i hria em, ka ring?”

“Ni e,” ringhlel takin Alice-i chuan a ti a: “a awmzia chu—eng thil pawh—mawi zawka siam—a awmzia a ni.”

“Awle, chuti a nih chuan,” Gryphon chuan a ti chhunzawm a, “I uglify tur chu eng nge ni tih i hriat loh chuan simpleton i ni .”

Alice-i chuan chu mi chungchâng zawhna zawh leh tûrin fuih a ni tawh lo va, Mock Turtle lam chu a hawi a, “Eng thil dang nge zir tûr i neih?”

“Awle, Mystery a awm a ni,” tiin Mock Turtle chuan a flapper-a thupui awmte chu a chhiar chhuak a, “—Mystery, hmanlai leh tunlai, Seaography nen: chutah chuan Drawling—Drawling-master chu conger-eel hlui tak a ni a, kar khatah vawi khat a lo kal thin: Drawling, Stretching, leh Fainting in Coils te min zirtir thin.”

“ Chu chu eng ang nge a nih?” Alice chuan a ti a.

“Awle, keimah ngei pawhin ka entir thei lo che,” Mock Turtle chuan a ti a: “Ka khauh lutuk. Tin, Gryphon hian a zir ngai lo.”

“Hun ka nei lo,” Gryphon chuan a ti a: “Classics master hnenah chuan ka kal a, mahse, crab hlui a ni a, a ni.”

“A hnenah ka kal ngai lo,” Mock Turtle chuan thaw halh chungin a ti a: “Nui leh Lungngaihna a zirtir thin, an ti thin.”

“Chutiang chuan a ti a, chutiang bawk chuan a ti ta a ni,” Gryphon chuan a ti leh a; tin, thilsiam pahnihte chuan an hmai chu an kekawrteah an thup a.

“A nih leh ni khatah darkar engzat nge lesson i tih thin?” Thupui thlak hmanhmawh zet hian Alice chuan a ti a.

“Ni hmasa berah darkar sawm,” Mock Turtle chuan a ti a: “a tuk zingah darkar 9, chutiang chuan a kal zel a ni.”

“Thil hriat duh tak a va ni em!” Alice chuan a ti a.

“Chu chu zirlai tia koh an nih chhan chu a ni,” tiin Gryphon-a chuan a sawi: “nitin an tihtlem avangin.”

Hei hi Alice tan chuan ngaihtuahna thar tak a ni a, a thusawi tur a sawi leh hmain tlem a ngaihtuah leh ta a ni. “A nih leh ni sawm leh pakhatna chu chawlhni a ni ngei ang?”

“A ni ngei ang,” Mock Turtle chuan a ti a.

“A nih leh ni sawm leh pahnihnaah engtin nge i lo enkawl?” Alice chu duhthawh takin a kal zel a.

“Chu chu zirlai chungchangah chuan a tawk tawh,” Gryphon chuan thutlukna siam tak zetin a ti tawp a: “tunah chuan inkhel chungchang eng emaw sawi rawh.”
