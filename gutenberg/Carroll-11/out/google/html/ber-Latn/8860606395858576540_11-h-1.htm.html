IXEF AMEZRU.
Ddaw n Taɣect n Uwtul

Alice tebda tettḥulfu s lḥif aṭas imi teqqim ɣer tama n weltma-s ɣef tbanka, yerna ur tesɛi ara d acu ara teg : yiwet neɣ snat n tikkal tettmuqul deg wedlis-nni ay teɣra weltma-s, maca ur yesɛi ara deg-s tugniwin neɣ timliliyin, « yerna d acu n lfayda n wedlis », ay d-tenna Alice deg wul-nnes « war tugniwin neɣ timliliyin ?

Ihi tella tettxemmim deg wallaɣ-is (akken tezmer, imi ass-nni yeḥma yeǧǧa-tt ad tḥulfu s yiḍes aṭas yerna d taɛebbuḍt), ma yella lfeṛḥ n uxeddim n tmeẓẓuɣt n tmeẓẓuɣt ad yesɛu azal n lḥif n tnekra d tmeẓẓuɣt n tmeẓẓuɣt, mi d-yeɣli yiwen n Uqcic Amellal s wallen tizegzawin qrib-is.

Ur yelli kra d ayen igerrzen aṭas deg waya; ula d Alice ur t-texsi ara akken ur telli ara deg webrid mi ara tesli i Uqcic-nni yenna-as i yiman-is: "A ḥbib! A ḥbib! Ad ɛeḍḍleɣ!" (mi t-texdem sdeffir-s, yusa-d deg wallaɣ-is belli ilaq ad testeqsi iman-is deg waya, maca di tallit-nni, kullec iban-d d ayen ibanen s tɣawla); maca mi d-yekkes Uqcic-nni s tidett tasaɛet seg lǧib-is n tceṭṭiḍt , yerna imuqel-itt, syin tkemmel s tɣawla, tebda Alice s yiḍarren-is, imi i d-yeffeɣ deg wallaɣ-is belli ur teẓri ara zik yiwen n uɛekkaz ama d tasaɛet n tceṭṭiḍt, neɣ d tasaɛet ara d-tessufeɣ seg-s, yerna tezger-d s, s lxir yella kan deg wakud akken ad t-iwali yettfeggiḍ-d yiwen n lḥebs n tmeẓyant ameqqran ddaw n tẓegwa.

Deg yiwet n teswiɛt nniḍen teɣli-d Alice deffir-s, ula d yiwet n tikkelt ur tettxemmim ara amek deg umaḍal ara d-teffeɣ tikkelt nniḍen.

Taɣerɣert n tmeẓyant tedda srid am tɣerɣert ɣef kra n webrid, sakkin teɣli-d s tɣawla ɣer ddaw, s tɣawla armi Alice ur tesɛi ara kra n wakud akken ad teḥbes iman-is uqbel ad taf iman-is teɣli-d ɣer ddaw n yiwen n lbenyan lqayen aṭas.

Ya d lqut-nni lqayen aṭas, neɣ teɣli-d s tɣawla, imi tesɛa aṭas n wakud mi ara d-teɣli akken ad tmuqel deg-s, ad testeqsi iman-is d acu ara d-yeḍrun sdat-s. Tazwara, teɛreḍ ad tmuqel ɣer ddaw ad teẓr d acu i d-tettaweḍ, maca d aḍu ur tezmir ara ad twali kra; syin tmuqel $er yidis n lqut, tger tamawt belli ččuren d iḍebsiyen d yiḍebsiyen n yedlisen; dagi d din teẓra tikarḍiwin d tugniwin ttwaɛellqent ɣef tmeẓẓuɣin. Tessufeɣ-d yiwen n lqahwa seg yiwen n lḥebs mi tedda; llan ttwarun-as “ORANGE MARMALADE”, maca s lqella n tmussni-s, yella d ayen ur nesɛi lmeɛna : ur tḥemmel ara ad tessers aɣerrabu-nni seg tugdi n tmenɣiwt n yiwen ddaw-as, dɣa tessaweḍ ad t-tessekcem deg yiwen seg yiḍebsiyen-nni mi teɣli zdat-s.

"Dɣa!" » i d-tenna Alice deg yiman-is, « deffir n tɣellist am tagi, ur ttxemmimeɣ ara ɣef tɣellist n tɣerɣert! Amek ara yi-d-xemmen akk s tebɣest deg wexxam! Ayɣer, ur d-qqareɣ ara kra fell-as, ula ma ɣliɣ seg tqacuct n wexxam ! (Ayen yellan d tidett aṭas.)

Ddaw, d wadda, d wadda. Ma yella ur d -tettfakka ara tɣellist-nni? “Wissen acḥal n yikilumitren i ɣliɣ armi d tallit-agi?” Tenna-d s ṣṣut ɛlayen. "Ilaq ad awḍeɣ anda-nniḍen qrib n tlemmast n tmurt. Eǧǧ-iyi ad waliɣ: aya ad yili d ṛebɛa alef n yikilumitren ɣer ddaw, ttwaliɣ..." (imi, twaliḍ, Alice telmed aṭas n tɣawsiwin n ṣṣenf-agi deg temsirin-is deg uɣerbaz, yerna ɣas akken aya ur yelli ara d tagnit yelhan aṭas i wakken ad d-tessken tamussni-s, imi ulac win ara s-d-yinin lxir ɣef listi-nni mazal-itt, ad...") d ayen icudden ɣer tɣerɣert iwulmen—maca syen ttxemmimeɣ d acu n Latitude neɣ n Longitude ay sɛiɣ?” (Alice ur tesɛi ara tikti ɣef wayen ay d Latitude, neɣ ula d Longitude, maca tḥess-d d awalen imeqranen yelhan ara d-tini.)

Ar tura tebda-d tikkelt nniḍen. « Wissen ma ad ɣliɣ srid s lqaɛa ! Amek ara yili d ayen yessewhamen ma ffɣeɣ-d gar yemdanen iteddun s yiqerra-nsen ɣer ddaw ! I Antipathies, ttxemmimeɣ—» (tefreḥ mliḥ imi ulac win i s-d-yeslan, tikkelt-a, imi ur d-yesli ara akk awal iwulmen) « tok as you the Please have is. Massa, d Ziland Tamaynut neɣ d Ustralya ?” (yerna teɛreḍ ad t-id-tessufeɣ akken tettmeslay-d — tcuddeḍ s lḥir mi ara teɣliḍ deg yigenni! Tɣileḍ tzemreḍ ad t-tḥekmeḍ?) “U d acu n tmeṭṭut tamecṭuḥt ijehliyen ara yi-d-tḥess imi steqsaɣ! Ala, ur t-id-yettaf ara lḥal ad steqsiɣ: ahat ad t-waliɣ yettwaru anda-nniḍen.”

Ddaw, d wadda, d wadda. Ur yelli wayen nniḍen ara yexdem, ihi ur tɛeṭṭel ara tebda Alice tettmeslay-d tikkelt nniḍen. “Dinah ad iyi-texṣer aṭas iḍ-a, ilaq ad xemmeɣ!” (Dinah d amcic.) "Ssarameɣ ad d-mektin tasekkirt-is n uyefki deg wakud n teyyita. Dinah a ḥbib-iw! Ssarameɣ ad tiliḍ yid-i dagi ddaw! Ur llin ara yiɣersiwen deg yigenni, ugadeɣ, maca ahat ad tḍefreḍ aɣersiw, yerna aya yecba aṭas aɣersiw, teẓriḍ. Maca imcac ttetten iɣersiwen, steqsaɣ iman-iw?" Yerna dagi tebda Alice tettḥulfu s yiḍes cwiṭ, tkemmel teqqar i yiman-is, s tmuɣli n targit: “Imcac ttetten iɣersiwen? Imcac ttetten iɣersiwen?” yerna tikwal, “Iɣersiwen ttetten imcac?” acku, twalaḍ, imi ur tezmir ara ad d-terr i sin n yisteqsiyen, ur yelli ara d ayen yesɛan azal ameqqran anwa abrid i t-id-tessers. Teḥsa belli tettḥulfu s tmeẓẓuɣt, yerna tebda kan tettnadi belli tetteddu afus deg ufus d Dina, yerna tenna-yas s lqerb aṭas: “Tura, Dina, ini-yi-d tidet: teččiḍ kra n wass lbaṭaṭa?” mi ara t-id-teḍru, thump! aɣebbar! ddaw tewweḍ-d ɣef yiwen n lḥebs n tẓegwa d yiferrawen iqerḥen, dɣa tfuk tɣellist.

Alice ur tettwagdel ara cwiṭ, yerna tneggez ɣef yiḍarren-is deg yiwet n teswiɛt : tmuqel ɣer sdat, maca akk d aḍu ɣef uqerruy-is ; zdat-s yella yiwen n ubrid nniḍen ɣezzifen, yerna mazal-it yettwaẓer Uqcic Amellal, yettɛeggiḍ-it. Ur yelli ara lawan ad t-yesfeḍ : s lbeɛd tedda Alice am waḍu, yerna tella kan deg wakud akken ad s-tesleḍ teqqar, akken tettmuqul deg yiwet n tɣerɣert, “A imeẓẓuɣen-iw d tmeẓẓuɣt-iw, amek i d-yeɣli lḥal!” Tella qrib deffir-s mi d-tezzi s tlemmast, maca Aqcic ur t-id-yettwali ara : tufa iman-is deg yiwet n tzeqqa ɣezzifen, d tameẓyant, i d-yesfeḍḍen yiwen n lḥebs n tmeẓdiyin i d-yeqqnen ɣer tqacuct.

Llant tewwura deg tlemmast n tzeqqa, maca ttwaqqnent akk; yerna mi tella Alice akk abrid-is ddaw n yiwet n tama d sdat n tama nniḍen, teɛreḍ yal tawwurt, tedda s lḥif ɣer tlemmast, tettxemmim amek ara d-teffeɣ tikkelt nniḍen.

Deg yiwet n tikkelt tufa-d yiwet n tmeqbert tamecṭuḥt yesɛan kraḍ n yiḍarren, akk s lgaz iǧehden; ulac fell-as anagar yiwet n tmeẓẓuɣt n wureɣ tamecṭuḥt, yerna axemmem amezwaru n Alice d akken yezmer ad yili d ayla n yiwet seg tewwura n tzeqqa; Maca, aḥi! ama d iqecwal meqqren aṭas, ama d tasarut tamecṭuḥt aṭas, maca akken yebɣu yili lḥal ur teldi ula d yiwet deg-sent. D acu kan, di tikkelt tis snat, tufa-d yiwet n tkurt n uḍar ur t-teẓri ara zik, deffir-s tella yiwet n tewwurt tamecṭuḥt s lqedd n wazal n xemsa n tmerwin n yiḍarren : teɛreḍ tasarut tamecṭuḥt n wureɣ yellan deg tmeẓẓuɣt, yerna s lferḥ-is ameqqran, telha-d !

Alice teldi tawwurt, tufa-d belli tettawi-d ɣer yiwen n ubrid ameẓyan, ur meqqer ara aṭas ɣef yiwen n lḥebs n yiɣersiwen : teɣli-d ɣef yiḍarren-is, tmuqel ɣef ubrid-nni ɣer tẓegwa n tmeṭṭut i tḥemmleḍ aṭas. Amek i tettḥulfu ad teffeɣ seg tzeqqa-nni n tlemmast, ad tettḥewwis gar wusuten-nni n yijeǧǧigen icebḥen d tfekkiwin-nni isemmaḍen, maca ur tezmir ara ad tessufeɣ ula d aqerruy-is seg tewwurt ; "U ula ma yella aqerruy-iw ad yezri", i d-tenna deg wul-is Alice tameɣbunt, "ad yili drus n lfayda mebla tuyat-iw. Ah, amek ttmenniɣ ad ssusmeɣ am tteleskubt! Ɣileɣ zemreɣ, lemmer ad ẓreɣ kan amek ara bduɣ." Imi, twalaḍ, aṭas n tɣawsiwin ur nelli ara deg webrid ay d-yeḍran taggara-a, dɣa Alice tebda tettxemmim d akken drus n tɣawsiwin s tidet ur nezmir ara ad tent-nexdem.

Tban-d amzun ur yelli ara lfayda deg ttraǧu ɣer tama n tewwurt tamecṭuḥt, dɣa tuɣal ɣer ṭṭabla, azgen n usirem ad taf fell-as tasarut nniḍen, neɣ akken yebɣu yili lḥal, adlis n lqanun n uḥebbus n yemdanen am tteleskubt : tikkelt-a tufa-d fell-as yiwet n tbuciḍant tamecṭuḥt, (“ay yellan s tidet ur telli ara dagi uqbel,” i d-tenna Alice,) d round the laper the words . “SW-IYI”, i d-yeţţunefk s ccbaḥa s yisekkilen imeqqranen.

Yella kullec d ayen yelhan aṭas ma tenniḍ-d “Ssew-iyi”, maca Alice tamecṭuḥt n tmusni ur telli ara ad teg aya s tɣawla. Tenna-yas: “Ala, ad ẓreɣ uqbel, ad waliɣ ma yella yettwasmekta-d ‘ ssem ’ neɣ ala”; acku teɣra-d aṭas n yimezruyen imeẓyanen yelhan ɣef yigerdan i d-yesɣermen, yerna ččan-ten yiɣersiwen n tẓegwa d wayen nniḍen ur nḥemmel ara, ayagi akk imi ur ttmektayen ara ilugan isehlen i ten-slemden yimeddukal-nsen : am, belli lpoker n tmes taberkant ad k-yesreɣ ma teṭṭfeḍ-t aṭas ; yerna ma tgeẓmeḍ aḍad-ik s lqedd aṭas s tmeẓẓuɣt, s umata yettfeggiḍ-d idammen; yerna ur tettut ara belli, ma teswiḍ aṭas seg tbuciḍant iɣef yura “ssem”, qrib ad t-id-teqbel, s tɣawla neɣ s tɣawla.

D acu kan, taɣerɣert-a ur tesɛi ara lḥeqq n “ssem”, dɣa Alice tessaweḍ ad tt-teɛreḍ, yerna mi tufa-tt telha aṭas, (tesɛa, deg tidet, ṣṣenf n tɣawsa yexḍan n tceṛṛiḍt, n tɣawsa n tɣawsa, n tɣawsa n tẓegwa, n tɣawsa n tɣawsa, n tɣawsa n tɣawsa, ed tɣawsa n tɣawsa n tɣawsa,) ur tɛeṭṭel ara ad tt-tkemmel.

“Acu n tḥulfa n tmusni!” » i d-tenna Alis; “Ilaq ad ssusmeɣ am tteleskubt.”

Yerna akka i tella s tidet : tura tesɛa kan mraw n tmitar deg teɣzi, yerna udem-is ifeǧǧiǧ mi d-tenna belli tura d lqis iwulmen i wakken ad teddu seg tewwurt tamecṭuḥt ɣer tebḥirt-nni n tmeṭṭut. D acu kan, deg tazwara, terǧa kra n tedqiqin akken ad twali ma ad t-id-tessiweḍ ad t-id-tessiweḍ : teḥsa cwiṭ n lḥir ɣef waya ; "imi yezmer ad yekfu, teẓriḍ", i d-tenna Alice i yiman-is, "deg tuffɣa-inu s lekmal-is, am lqeḍra. Wissen amek ilaq ad iliɣ imir-nni?" Yerna teɛreḍ ad t-id-tefhem amek i d-tettili tmes n lqeḍyan deffir ma tettwakkes lqeḍyan, imi ur tezmir ara ad d-tecfu belli teẓra kra am wa.

Send kra n lweqt, mi tufa ur d-yeḍri ara wayen nniḍen, tɣtes ad tekcem ɣer tebḥirt s tɣawla; maca, aḥi i Alice tameɣbunt! mi d-tewweḍ ɣer tewwurt, tufa-d tettu tasarut-nni tamecṭuḥt n wureɣ, mi d-tuɣal ɣer ṭṭabla fell-as, tufa-d ur tezmir ara ad tt-taweḍ : tettwali-tt s lbaḍna s lgaz, yerna teɛreḍ akk ayen umi tezmer akken ad t-id-tessufeɣ ɣef yiwen seg yiḍarren n ṭṭabla, maca tella tettcewwil aṭas ; mi tesɛedda iman-is s tmeɣra, teqqim tmeṭṭut-nni tameẓyant, tettrun.

“As-d, ulac lfayda ma tettruḍ akka!” » i d-tenna Alice i yiman-is, s tɣawla ; “Nekk ttweṣṣiɣ-k ad teǧǧeḍ deg tedqiqt-agi!” S umata, tettafk i yiman-is lewṣayat yelhan aṭas, (ɣas akken qlil aṭas ay ten-teḍfer), yerna tikwal tettḥeqriq iman-nnes s lqerb armi ay d-tettaweḍ imeṭṭawen deg wallen-nnes; yerna yiwet n tikkelt tecfa-d mi teɛreḍ ad tessexsi imeẓẓuɣen-is imi ay tessexṣer iman-nnes deg yiwen n wurar n tkerrust ay tella tetturar mgal yiman-nnes, imi aɣrud-a ay d-yellan d aɛewwiq, yella yettḥemmil aṭas ad d-yerr iman-nnes d sin n yemdanen. “Maca ur yesɛi ara lfayda tura,” i d-tenna deg wul-is Alice tameɣbunt, “ad t-id-tessewjedḍ amzun d sin n yemdanen!

Cwi kan, teɣli-d wallen-is ɣef yiwet n tkeṛṛust n lgaz tamecṭuḥt i d-yeqqimen ddaw n ṭṭabla : teldi-tt, tufa deg-s yiwet n tkeṛṛust tamecṭuḥt aṭas, fell-as ttwaseknen-d s ccbaḥa awalen “ECT-IYI” s lkurs. "Ihi, ad t-ččeɣ", i d-tenna Alice, "yerna ma yella yessemɣar-iyi, zemreɣ ad awḍeɣ ɣer tasarut; ma yella yessemɣar-iyi, zemreɣ ad sfeḍḍeɣ ddaw tewwurt; ihi akken yebɣu yili lḥal ad kecmeɣ ɣer tebḥirt, yerna ur iyi-yehwi ara anwa ara d-yeḍrun!"

Tečča cwiṭ, tenna-d s lḥir i yiman-is: “Anwa abrid? Anwa abrid?”, teṭṭef afus-is ɣef tqacuct n uqerruy-is akken ad tḥulfu anwa abrid i d-yettnernin, yerna tewhem aṭas mi d-tufa belli teqqim s lqis-is : akken ad tt-id-sfehmeɣ s umata, aya yettili-d s umata mi ara yečč yiwen aɣrum n tɣawsiwin ur t-id-yettṛaǧu ara, maca Alice teffeɣ-d s waṭas n tɣawsiwin ɣer- yeḍra, belli iban-d d akken d ayen ixuṣṣen aṭas yerna d aɛewwiq i tudert ad teddu s ubrid ucrik.

Ihi tebda ccɣel-is, ur tɛeṭṭel ara tfuk lgaṭu-nni.
