IXEF V.
Aswelleh seg yiwen n uɣersiw

Caterpillar d Alice ttmuqulen deg wayeḍ kra n lweqt deg tsusmi : taggara, Caterpillar yekkes-d lḥemmeẓ seg yimi-s, yerna yessawel-as s ṣṣut n tmeẓẓuɣt, n tmeẓẓuɣt.

“Anwa i telliḍ ? ” i d-yenna Uqcic.

Wagi mačči d lqeḍyan ara yesbeɛden i tmesliwt. Terra-yas-d Alice, s tmuɣli n tmeṭṭut, “Nekk – qrib ur ẓriɣ ara, Mass, akka kan akka – ma ulac ẓriɣ anwa i lliɣ mi d-kkseɣ taṣebḥit-agi, maca ttwaliɣ ahat ttwabeddleɣ acḥal n tikkal seg wasmi i d-yeffeɣ.”

“D acu i tebɣiḍ ad d-tiniḍ s waya?” Yenna-d Uqcic s lḥir. “Ssefhem-d iman-ik!”

“Ur zmireɣ ara ad sfehmeɣ iman-iw , ugadeɣ, Mass,” i d-tenna Alice, “acku ur lliɣ ara d iman-iw, twaliḍ.”

“Ur ẓriɣ ara”, i d-yenna Uqcic.

“Ugadeɣ ur zmireɣ ara ad t-id-iniɣ s tɣawla ugar,” i s-terra Alice s lḥeṛma tameqrant, “imi ur zmireɣ ara ad t-fehmeɣ s timmad-iw seg tazwara; yerna imi llant aṭas n tɣawsiwin yemgaraden deg yiwen wass, aya yessewham aṭas.”

“Mačči akka”, i d-yenna Uqcic.

“Aha, ahat ur t-tufiḍ ara akka ar ass-a”, i d-tenna Alice; “maca mi ara tesɛuḍ ad tbeddleḍ d aɣersiw n tkerrust (chrysalis) – ad t-tuɣaleḍ kra n wass, teẓriḍ – syin deffir waya d aferṭaṭṭu, yessefk ad tt-id-iniɣ ad tḥulfuḍ d akken d ayen ixuṣṣen cwiṭ, mačči akka?”

“Mačči d cwiṭ”, i d-yenna Uqcic.

“Aha, ahat lḥir- ik yezmer ad yili yemgarad,” i d- tenna Alice; “ayen akk ẓriɣ d akken, ad iyi -d-iḥulfu d akken d ayen ixuṣṣen aṭas .”

"Kečč!" Yenna-d Uqcic s lḥeq. "Anwa-k ? "

Anwa i ten-id-yerran tikkelt nniḍen ɣer tazwara n uskasi. Alice teḥsa cwiṭ s lḥir imi ay d-yenna Caterpillar awal-a iqesḥen aṭas , yerna tessufeɣ-d iman-nnes, tenna-d, s lqerb aṭas, “Ɣileɣ, yessefk ad iyi-d-tiniḍ anwa ay telliḍ , d amezwaru.”

"I wacu?" Yenna-d Uqcic.

Hatan yiwen n usteqsi nniḍen i d-yesḥissifen; yerna imi Alice ur tezmir ara ad d-texdem kra n ssebba yelhan, yerna imi Caterpillar iban-d amzun yella deg yiwen n lḥal ur nḥemmel ara aṭas , tezzi-d ɣer zdat.

“Uɣal-d!” » i d-yessawel Uqcic deffir-s. “Sɛiɣ kra yesɛan azal ara d-iniɣ!”

Aya yella-d s lbaḍna, s tidet : Alice tezzi-d, tuɣal-d tikkelt nniḍen.

“Ḥrez lḥir-ik,” i d-yenna Uqcic.

“D aya kan?” » i d-tenna Alice, tesseɣli lɣiḍa-s akken tezmer.

“Ala”, i d-yenna Uqcic.

Alice tettxemmim ahat ad terǧu akken ilaq, imi ur tesɛi ara ayen nniḍen ara teg, yerna ahat deffir waya yezmer ad as-d-yini kra s wazal-is ad t-tesleḍ. Kra n tedqiqin tettfeggiḍ mebla ma tessawel, maca taggara tefser-d ifassen-is, tekkes-d tikkelt nniḍen lḥemmeẓ seg yimi-s, tenna-yas: “Ihi tɣileḍ tbeddleḍ, neɣ ala?”

“Ugadeɣ d nekk, Mass,” i d-tenna Alice; “Ur zmireɣ ara ad cfuɣ ɣef tɣawsiwin akken llant zik—yerna ur ţţaǧǧaɣ ara yiwen n lqis mraw n ddqayeq akk!”

“Ur zmireɣ ara ad d-mmektiɣ d acu n tɣawsiwin?” Yenna-d Uqcic.

“Aha, ɛerḍeɣ ad d-iniɣ “Amek i tella tmeẓẓuɣt tamecṭuḥt n tmeẓẓuɣt”, maca kullec yusa-d s tɣawsiwin nniḍen!” Terra-as-d Alice s ṣṣut n tmeṭṭut n tmeṭṭut.

“Iɛawed-d, ‘ Kečč d amɣar, a baba William ’”, i d-yenna Uqcic-nni.

Alice tger ifassen-is, tebda :—

“Kečč d amɣar, a baba William,” i d-yenna ilemẓi-nni,
“U acebbub-ik yuɣal d amellal aṭas;
U ɣas akken tebdedḍ s uqerruy-ik war ma tḥebseḍ—
Tettwaliḍ, deg leɛmeṛ-ik, d ayen yelhan?”

“Di temẓi-w,” i s-d-yerra Baba William i mmi-s, .
« Ugadeɣ ad yejreḥ allaɣ;
Maca, tura imi ttwaliɣ ur sɛiɣ ula d yiwen,
Acuɣer, ttgen-t-id tikkelt ɣef tikkelt.”

“Kečč d ameqqran,” i d-yenna yilemẓi-nni, “akken i d-nniɣ zik, .
Yerna ssawḍen ad sneqsen aṭas n tfekka ur nesɛi lqima;
Maca tgiḍ-d s tɣawla s tɣawla ɣer tewwurt—
Ẓẓal, d acu i d ssebba n waya?”

“Di temẓi-w,” i d-yenna uɛeggal-nni, mi d-yesḥissif iɣil-is aẓiḍan,
“Ḥerzeɣ akk iḍarren-iw s lḥir .
S useqdec n lḥemla-agi—yiwen n ṣṣilin n tkeṛṛust—
Eǧǧ-iyi ad ak-zzenzeɣ sin?”

“Kečč d ameqqran,” i d-yenna ilemẓi-nni, “u iɣes-ik d aɛewwiq aṭas .
I kra n wayen yugaren suet;
Maca tkemleḍ lbaz, s yiɣsan d tmeẓẓuɣt—
Ẓẓal, amek i tessawḍeḍ ad t-txedmeḍ ?”

Baba-s yenna-yas: “Di temẓi-w, ḍefren-iyi-d ccariɛa, .
Yerna ttnaɣen yal taluft d tmeṭṭut-iw;
Yerna tazmert n tfekka, i d-tefka i tmeẓẓuɣt-iw,
Teḍra-d akk tudert-iw.”

“Kečč d ameqqran,” i d-yenna yilemẓi-nni, “ur yezmir yiwen ad d-yini .
D akken allen-ik tella tettkemmil am zik;
Maca tqeḍɛeḍ yiwen n lbaṭaṭa deg taggara n anzaren-ik—
D acu i k-yeǧǧan ad tiliḍ d aḥeqqi aṭas?”

“Rriɣ-d kraḍ n yisestanen, yerna ayagi d ayen iwatan,”
Yenna-d baba-s; “ur ttak ara i yiman-ik lḥaǧa!
Tettwaliḍ zemreɣ ad sleɣ ass kamel i tɣawsiwin am tigi?
Be off, neɣ ad k-id-ssufɣeɣ seg tesraft!”

“Ayagi ur d-yenni ara akken ilaq”, i d-yenna Uqcic.

“Ur t-id-yefki ara akk , ugadeɣ”, i d-tenna Alice s tmeṭṭut-is; “kra n wawalen ttwabeddlen.”

“D ayen ur nelli ara d ayen yelhan seg tazwara ar taggara”, i d-yenna Uqcic s lqedd, yerna tella-d tsusmi kra n tedqiqin.

D Caterpillar i d amezwaru i d-yemmeslayen.

“D acu n lqis i tebɣiḍ ad tiliḍ?” Testeqsa-t.

“Ah, ur lliɣ ara d aḥric seg wayen yerzan lqis,” i s-terra Alice s tɣawla; “ala yiwen ur nḥemmel ara ad ibeddel aṭas, teẓriḍ.”

“ Ur ẓriɣ ara”, i d-yenna Uqcic.

Alice ur d-tenni kra : ur telli ara tettwakkes-as lxir am wa deg tudert-is uqbel, yerna teḥsa belli tettḥulfu s lḥir.

“Tqebleḍ tura?” Yenna-d Uqcic.

“Aha, ilaq ad ḥemmleɣ ad iliɣ meqqer cwiṭ , Mass, ma yella ur k-iɛeṭṭel ara”, i d-tenna Alice: “tlata n tmiqwa d lqedd n lḥif am wa.”

“D lqedd yelhan aṭas s tidet!” » i d-yenna Uqcic-nni s lferḥ, yerna yerfed iman-is s lḥeqq akken i d-yettmeslay (yella s lqedd n kraḍ n yisantimetren akken iwata).

“Maca ur tt-ɛemmleɣ ara!” » i d-tessuter Alice tameɣbunt s ṣṣut n tmeṭṭut. Yerna tettxemmim deg yiman-is, “Ahat ur ttwaɛeqlen ara yimdanen-nni s lfeɛl akka!”

“Ad t-tmenɛeḍ deg wakud”, i d-yenna Uqcic; yerna tessers lqut deg yimi-s, tebda tettkessi tikkelt nniḍen.

Tikkelt-a Alice teṛǧa s ṣṣber alamma tefren ad d-tmeslay tikkelt nniḍen. Deg yiwet n tedqiqt neɣ snat, yekkes-d Uqcic-nni lḥemla-nni seg yimi-s yerna yeɛwej-d tikkelt neɣ snat, yerna yeḥbes iman-is. Syin teɣli-d seg tfekka, teffeɣ-d deg tẓegwa, tettmuqul kan akken tedda, “Yiwet n tama ad k-terr ad t-teɣliḍ, ma d tama nniḍen ad k-terr ad t-teɣzeḍ.”

“Yiwet tama n wacu? Tama nniḍen n wacu? ” i d-tenna Alice deg yiman-is.

“N tmeẓẓuɣt”, i d-tenna Tmeṭṭut, amzun d nettat i tt-yesteqsan s ṣṣut ɛlayen; yerna deg yiwet n teswiɛt nniḍen ur t-id-yettwali ara.

Alice teqqim tettmuqul s tmuɣli deg tmeẓẓuɣt-nni kra n tedqiqt, teɛreḍ ad teẓr anwi i d sin yidisan-is; yerna imi tella tkemmel tkemmel, tufa-d aya d asteqsi iweɛren aṭas. D acu kan, taggara tessewjed-d ifassen-is fell-as akken ara ddun, terẓa-d cwiṭ n tlemmast s yal afus.

“U tura anwa i d anwa?” » i d-tenna i yiman-is, yerna tesseqneɛ cwiṭ seg tmeẓẓuɣt n ufus ayeffus akken ad tɛerḍ lḥeqq-nni : taswiɛt-nniḍen teḥsa s yiwet n tmeẓẓuɣt n lǧehd ddaw n tɣenja-s : teɣli-d ɣef uḍar-is !

Tella tettwagdel aṭas s ubeddel-agi n tɣawsa s tɣawla, maca teḥsa belli ulac akud ara texṣer, imi tettnerni s tɣawla; ihi tebda txeddem deg yiwet n tikkelt akken ad tečč kra seg lḥemla-nniḍen. Aqerruy-is yettwaqqen aṭas ɣer uḍar-is, armi qrib ur yelli ara wemkan akken ad teldi aqemmuc-is; maca tga-t taggara, tessaweḍ ad tečč yiwen n lḥemla n tmeẓẓuɣt n uzelmaḍ.

“As-d, aqerruy-iw d ilelli taggara!” » i d-tenna Alice s ṣṣut n lferḥ, i ibeddlen d lḥir deg teswiɛt nniḍen, mi tufa belli ur llin ara yiḍarren-is anda ara ten-naf : ayen akk tezmer ad twali, mi tmuqel ɣer ddaw, d yiwet n teɣzi n tɣerɣert ur nesɛi lqima, i d-yeffɣen amzun d aɣersiw i d-yeffɣen seg yilel n yiferrawen izewwaɣen i d-yezgan ddaw-as s waṭas.

“D acu i izemren ad yili akk lḥaǧa-nni tazegzawt?” Yenna-d Alis. "U anda wwḍen yiḍarren-iw? U ay ifassen-iw imeɣnasen, amek ur k-ẓriɣ ara?" Tella tettmuqul-iten akken tettmeslay, maca ulac agerruj amzun d win i d-yeḍfer, anagar cwiṭ n tḥemla gar yiferran izewwaɣen ibeɛden.

Akken iban ur yelli ara lḥal ad tessiweḍ ifassen-is ɣer uqerruy-is, teɛreḍ ad tessiweḍ aqerruy-is ɣer-sen, yerna tefṛeḥ imi tufa belli aqerruy-is ad iɛeqleḍ s lfeɛl deg yal tama, am tmeẓẓuɣt. Tessaweḍ kan ad t-teɛreḍ ad t-id-tessufeɣ ɣer ddaw s yiwen n zigzag n tmeẓẓuɣt, yerna tella ad t-teɣli gar yiferran, ay tufa ur llin ara d kra nniḍen anagar tiqcicin n yisekla aydeg tella tettḥewwis, mi tt-id-yeǧǧa yiwen n usḥissef s tɣawla ad d-terr ɣer deffir s tɣawla : yiwen n uɣersiw ameqran yeffeɣ-d deg wudem-nnes, yerna yella yettḍurru-tt s tɣawla.

“Azrem!” » i d-yesɣer Uqcic.

“Nekk mačči d azrem!” » i d-tenna Alice s lferḥ. “Eǧǧ-iyi ad qqimeɣ!”

“Azrem, qqareɣ-d tikkelt nniḍen!” » i d-yerra Uqcic-nni, maca s ṣṣut i d-yesfeḍḍen ugar, yerna-d s ṣṣenf n tmeɣra, « ɛerḍeɣ akk abrid, yerna ulac d acu i ten-iɛeǧben amzun !»

“Ur sɛiɣ ara ula d yiwet n tmuɣli ɣef wayen ay d-teqqareḍ,” ay d-tenna Alice.

« Ḥarebɣ iẓuran n yisekla, ɛerḍeɣ lbankat, ɛerḍeɣ tiḥuna », i d-tkemmel Tmeṭṭut, war ma teḥḍer-itt ; "maca izerman-nni! Ulac ayen ara ten-iɛeǧben!"

Alice tettnerni s lxuf, maca tettwali ur yelli ara lfayda ad d-tini kra nniḍen alamma yekfa Uqcic-nni.

“Amzun ur yelli ara d ugur ilaqen ad d-yesfeḍ timellalin”, i d-yenna Uqcic-nni; “maca ilaq ad qqimeɣ ttḥadarɣ ɣef yizerfan iḍ d wass! Acuɣer, ur sɛiɣ ara ula d yiwen n yiḍes deg tlata n ddurt-agi!”

“Sseḥḥasfeɣ aṭas imi i k-tesɛeddaḍ s lqella n tmussni”, i d-tenna Alice, i yebdan tettwali anamek-is.

"U akken i d-wwiɣ aseklu-nni yugaren akk deg tẓegwa," ikemmel Uqcic, yerfed taɣect-is ɣer tmeɣra, "u akken lliɣ ttxemmimeɣ ad iliɣ d ilelli seg-sen taggara, ilaq ad d-asen ttqerriben seg yigenni! Ugh, Azrem!"

“Maca nekk mačči d azrem, nniɣ-ak!” Yenna-d Alis. “Nekk d—nekk d—”

"Aha! D acu- k?" Yenna-d Uqcic. “Zmerɣ ad waliɣ tettnadiḍ ad d-tesnulfuḍ kra!”

“Nekk—nekk d taqcict tamecṭuḥt”, i d-tenna Alice, s ccek cwiṭ, mi d-tecfa ɣef umḍan n yibeddilen i d-teḍra ass-nni.

“D tamacahut izemren ad tili s tidet!” » i d-yenna Uqcic s ṣṣut n usḥissef lqayen akk. "Ẓriɣ aṭas n teqcicin timecṭaḥ deg tallit-iw, maca werǧin yiwet s lqedd am wa! Ala, ala! Kečč d azrem; yerna ulac lfayda ad t-nekṛeḍ. Ɣileɣ ad iyi-d-tiniḍ sdat-i belli ur tesɛiḍ ara tamellalt!"

“ Ssexdameɣ timellalin, s tidet”, i d-tenna Alice, yellan d tameṭṭut n tidett aṭas; “maca tiḥdayin timecṭaḥ ttettent timellalin akken ttettent yizerfan, teẓriḍ.”

“Ur t-ttamneɣ ara”, i d-yenna Uqcic; “maca ma llan, ayɣer ihi d ṣṣenf n yizerfan, d aya kan i zemreɣ ad d-iniɣ.”

Tagi d tikti tamaynut i Alice, armi tessusem aṭas kra n tedqiqt neɣ snat, dɣa d aya ay yefkan tagnit i Uqcic-nni akken ad yernu : « Tettnadiḍ ɣef tmellalin, ssneɣ aya mliḥ ; yerna d acu ay iyi-iɛawnen ma yella d taqcict tamecṭuḥt neɣ d azrem ?

“Tesɛa azal ameqqran ɣur-i ,” i d-tenna Alice s tɣawla; “maca ur ttnadiɣ ara ɣef tmellalin, akken i d-teḍra; yerna lemmer d nekk, ur ilaq ara ad bɣuɣ tin-ik : ur tent-ḥemmleɣ ara d tiḥemmalin.”

“Aha, ffɣeḍ, ihi!” » i d-yenna Uqcic s ṣṣut n tmeṭṭut, mi d-yerra iman-is deg lqaɛa-s. Alice tettqerrib gar yisekla akken tezmer, imi aqerruy-is yezga yettkemmil yettqerrib gar yiferdisen, yerna yal tikkelt ilaq-as ad teḥbes ad t-tefru. Deffir kra n lweqt tecfa-d belli mazal-itt teṭṭef deg ifassen-is iferdisen n tmeẓẓuɣt, tebda tettqeddim s lḥir meqqren, tettqerrib di tazwara ɣer yiwet, syin ɣer tayeḍ, yerna tettnerni tikwal tettnerni, tikwal tettnerni, armi tessaweḍ ad d-tessiweḍ iman-is ɣer tɣerɣert-is n zik.

Aṭas aya segmi ur telli ara d kra iqerben ɣer lqis ilaqen, dɣa tḥulfa-d d akken d ayen igerrzen aṭas deg tazwara; maca teɛlem-itt deg kra n tedqiqin, tebda tettmeslay d yiman-is, am yal tikkelt. "As-d, yella azgen n wahil-iw yettwaxdem tura! Amek ttcewwilen akk ibeddalen-agi! Ur ẓriɣ ara d acu ara iliɣ, seg tedqiqt ɣer tayeḍ! D acu kan, uɣaleɣ ɣer lqis-iw ilaqen: ayen i d-iteddun d, ad kecmeɣ ɣer tebḥirt-nni icebḥen – amek ara d-yili waya, ttxemmimeɣ?" Akken i d-tenna akka, tewweḍ-d s tɣawla ɣer yiwen wemkan yeldin, deg-s yiwen uxxam ameẓyan s wazal n ṛebɛa n yiḍarren deg teɣzi. “Win yeddren dinna”, i d-tenna Alice deg wul-is, “ur t-id-yettaf ara ad d-yaweḍ fell-asen s lqis -agi : ayɣer, ilaq ad ten-sxeṣreɣ seg lbal-nsen!” Ihi tebda tettqerrib tikkelt nniḍen ɣer tmeẓẓuɣt n ufus ayeffus, yerna ur tessaweḍ ara ad teqreb ɣer wexxam alamma tessaweḍ iman-is ɣer tẓa n lmitrat deg teɣzi.
