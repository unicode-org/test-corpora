Tagzirt n imeṭṭawen

“Ugar n tmusni d tmusni!” » i d-tenna Alice (tewhem aṭas, armi i teswiɛt-nni tettu akk amek ara tessiwel taglizit yelhan); "tura ttldiɣ-d am tteleskubt tameqqrant akk yellan! Ar tufat, iḍarren!" (imi mi ara tmuqel ɣer yiḍarren-is, amzun qrib ad ffɣen seg wallen, ttbeɛden aṭas). "A iḍarren-iw imeẓyanen imeɣnasen, ttxemmimeɣ anwa ara wen-d-yessersen tiqendyar-nwen d tcekkaṛin-nwen tura, a ɛzizen? Ssuref-iyi ur zmireɣ ara! Ad iliɣ s waṭas s lbeɛd akken ad sqeɛdeɣ iman-iw fell-awen : ilaq ad tḍefren abrid yelhan akk ay tzemreḍ ;—maca ilaq ad iliɣ d lxir fell-asen," i d-tenna Alice deg wul-nsen, "or ur zmireɣ ara ad dduɣ! ẓer: Ad asen-fkeɣ yiwen n lbuṭ amaynut yal lɛid.”

Yerna tedda tettxemmim i yiman-is amek ara tt-tesseddu. “Ilaq ad ddun s tkerrust,” i d-tenna deg wul-is; « yerna amek ara d-yeffeɣ d ayen yessewhamen, ad yessiweḍ yiwen tiwlafin ɣer yiḍarren-is !

Aḍar ayeffus n Alis, Esq.,
Tasarut n wul,
qrib n Fender, ( s tayri n Alice ).

A ḥbib, d acu n lbaṭel i d-qqareɣ!”

Imiren kan aqerruy-is iwet $ef sqef n tzeqqa : di tidett tura tewweḍ ugar n tẓa n yiḍarren, yerna deg yiwet n tikkelt teṭṭef tasarut tamecṭuḥt n wureɣ, tkemmel s tɣawla ɣer tewwurt n tẓegwa.

Alis tameɣbunt! D akken tezmer, tettḍeggiɛ iman-is ɣef yiwet n tama, ad tmuqel s yiwet n tiṭ deg tebḥirt; maca akken ad tesɛeddi, ur tesɛi ara asirem ugar n zik : teqqim, tebda tettru tikkelt nniḍen.

“Ilaq ad tesḥissifḍ ɣef yiman-ik”, i d-tenna Alice, “taqcict tameqrant am kečč,” (tezmer ad d-tini akka s lxir), “akken ad tkemleḍ ad tettrun akka! Ḥbes taswiɛt-agi, ad ak-iniɣ!” Maca tedda akk akken, tessufuɣ-d lgallun n imeṭṭawen, armi d asmi yella yiwen n lbabur ameqqran i s-d-yezzin, azal n ṛebɛa n lmitrat deg lqaɛa yerna yewweḍ ɣer uzgen n tzeqqa.

Deffir kra n lweqt tesla i cwiṭ n tmeẓẓuɣt n yiḍarren deg lbeɛd, s tɣawla tesseqɛed allen-is akken ad twali d acu i d-iteddun. D Awtul Amellal i d-yuɣalen, yelsa s lqecc igerrzen, s yiwen ufus s lgaz n tmeṭṭut tamellalt, deg wayeḍ d aferdis ameqqran : yusa-d yettmuqul s tɣawla, yettmuqul deg yiman-is akken i d-yusa: “Ah! Alice teḥsa s lḥir armi tella tettḥezzib ad tessuter lemɛawna seg yal yiwen; ihi, mi s-d-iqerreb Uqcic, tebda, s ṣṣut n tmeṭṭut, s ṣṣut n tmeṭṭut, “Ma tḥemmleḍ, a mass...” Yebda Uqcic s lǧehd, yessers-d lgaz n tmeṭṭut tamellalt d tfekka n tmeṭṭut, yerna yezger-d s tɣawla ɣer ṭṭlam akken izemren ad yeddu.

Alice teṭṭef-d taferka d lgaz, yerna, imi teḥma tzeqqa-nni aṭas, teqqim tettfeggiḍ iman-is akk lweqt aydeg tettkemmil tettmeslay: "A ḥbib, a ḥbib! Amek ay d-yella kullec ass-a! Yerna iḍelli llant tɣawsiwin ttkemmilent am yal tikkelt. Ttxemmimeɣ ma yella ttwabeddleɣ deg yiḍ? cwiṭ mxallafen Maca ma yella ur lliɣ ara d yiwen, asteqsi i d-iteddun d winna, Anwa i d nekk deg umaḍal ? Yerna tebda tettxemmim $ef warrac akk i tessen llan deg leâmer-is, akken ad twali ma yella tezmer ad tt-tbeddel $ef yiwen deg-sen.

"Ssuref-iyi ur lliɣ ara d Ada," i d-tenna, "acku acebbub - is yettkemmil s tmeẓẓuɣin ɣezzifen am tigi, ma d win-iw ur yettkemmil ara s tmeẓẓuɣin akk; yerna ttwaliɣ ur zmireɣ ara ad iliɣ d Mabel, acku ssneɣ akk ṣṣenf n tɣawsiwin, yerna nettat , oh! ma yella ssneɣ akk tiɣawsiwin lliɣ ssneɣ zik.Eǧǧ-iyi ad waliɣ : arbaɛ n tikkal xemsa d mraw d sin, yerna arbaɛ n tikkal sebɛa d mraw d kraḍ, ay ḥemmleɣ ur ttaweḍeɣ ara ɣer ɛecrin s lqidar-nni Maca, Tafelwit n Ussemɣer ur d-tesskanay ara tamaneɣt n Paris, Rome d akk -n ! lxilaf, ttwaliɣ s lqerb ad iliɣ ttwabeddleɣ ɣef Mabel !

« Amek i t-id-yesnulfa uqeclaw ameẓyan .
Sselhu taɣect-is yettceɛciɛen,
U ssizzel aman n Nil .
Deg yal lmizan n wureɣ!

“Amek i d-yettban s lferḥ yettmuqul, .
Amek i d-yessers tifednin-is s lḥir,
U snemren iselman imeẓyanen deg
S yiɣes yettmuqulen s lḥir!”

« Ssuref-iyi, ur llin ara d awalen yelhan », i d-tenna Alice tameɣbunt, yerna allen-is ččurent d imeṭṭawen tikkelt nniḍen mi tettkemmil, « ilaq ad iliɣ d Mabel sdeffir n waya, yerna ad dduɣ ad ddreɣ deg wexxam-nni ameẓyan n tmeṭṭut, yerna ur sɛiɣ ara zdat-iyi kra n tɣawsiwin n wurar ara s-d-urareɣ, yerna oh! qqim dagi ddaw ur d-yettili ara lfayda ma ssersen iqerra-nsen ddaw, nnan-d ‘As-d tikkelt nniḍen, a ḥbib!’. Ad smuqleɣ kan ɣer sdat, ad d-iniɣ ‘Anwa i lliɣ ihi? Ini-yi-d aya d amezwaru, sakkin, ma yella ḥemmleɣ ad iliɣ d amdan-nni, ad d-aseɣ ɣer sdat : ma ulac, ad qqimeɣ dagi ddaw alamma lliɣ d wayeḍ’ — maca, ay ḥbib!” » i d-tenna Alice, s yiwen n imeṭṭawen s tɣawla, « ttmenniɣ ad ssersen iqerra-nsen !

Akken i d-tenna akka tmuqel ɣer ifassen-is, tewhem mi teẓra belli tessers yiwet seg tmeẓyanin n tmeẓyant n tmeẓyant n tmeẓyant n tmeẓyant n tmeẓyant mi tella tettmeslay. “Amek ara xedmeɣ akka?” Tenna deg wul-is. “Ilaq ad tt-id-rreɣ d tameẓyant.” Tekker-d, truḥ ɣer ṭṭabla akken ad t-tqeḍɛed iman-is s waya, tufa-d belli, akken qrib ad t-id-tḥess, tura tesɛa azal n sin n yiḍarren deg teɣzi, yerna tettkemmil tettnerni s tɣawla : ur tɛeṭṭel ara tufa belli ssebba n waya d taferka ay teṭṭef, yerna tessers-itt s tɣawla, deg wakud kan akken ur tettnerni ara altother.

“D ayen i d-yellan d rrwel ẓẓayen!” » i d-tenna Alice, teggugem aṭas seg ubeddel-nni n tɣawsa, maca tefreḥ aṭas imi tufa iman-is mazal-itt tella ; “u tura i tẓegwa!” yerna tezger s tɣawla ɣer tewwurt tamecṭuḥt : maca, aḥi ! tawwurt tamecṭuḥt tettwagdel tikkelt nniḍen, yerna tasarut tamecṭuḥt n wureɣ tella tettɣimi ɣef ṭṭabla n lgaz am zik, “yerna lḥala tgerrez ugar n zik”, i d-yenna deg wul-is weqcic-nni ameɣbun, “imi ur lliɣ ara d ameẓyan am wa zik, werǧin!

Akken i d-tenna awalen-agi aḍar-is yezger, deg teswiɛt nniḍen, sfeḍ ! Tella armi d aqerruy-is deg waman n tmes. Tikti-s tamezwarut d akken teɣli-d s kra n tɣawsa deg yilel, “yerna deg tegnit-nni zemreɣ ad uɣaleɣ s webrid n wuzzal”, i d-tenna i yiman-is. (Alice tella tedda ɣer tlemmast n yilel yiwet n tikkelt deg tudert-is, yerna tewweḍ ɣer taggara tamatut, belli anda ma telliḍ ɣer tlemmast n tmurt n Langliz ad tafeḍ aṭas n tmacinin n usgunfu deg yilel, kra n yigerdan ttɣezzifen deg lqaɛa s tmeẓẓuɣin n useklu, sakkin yiwen n lḥebs n yixxamen n tmeɣra, yerna deffir-sen yiwet n tɣiwant n tmacint mi ara teffeɣ s tɣawla. Tesɛa tẓa n yiḍarren deg teɣzi.

“Amer ur ttrun ara aṭas!” » i d-tenna Alice, mi tettɣummu, tettnadi ad d-taf abrid n tuffɣa. "Ad ttwaɛaqbeɣ fell-as tura, ttwaliɣ, s uɣerrabu deg imeṭṭawen-iw! D ayen ara yilin d ayen ur nesɛi lmeɛna, ad tt-id-iniɣ s lḥeqq! D acu kan, kullec d ayen ur nesɛi lmeɛna ass-a."

Imiren kan tesla i kra yettfeggiḍen deg tmeqbert s lbeɛd cwiṭ, yerna teɛum qrib akken ad teẓr d acu-t : deg tazwara tettwali d akken ilaq d aɣersiw n tẓegwa neɣ d aɣersiw n tẓegwa, maca sakkin tecfa-d amek ay tella tura d tameẓyant, yerna ur tɛeṭṭel ara teẓra d akken d aɣersiw kan ay d-yesnekren am nettat.

"Ad yili kra n nnfeɛ, tura", i d-tenna Alice, "ma ssawleɣ i uɣersiw-agi? Kullec yeffeɣ-d seg ubrid dagi ddaw, dɣa yessefk ad ttxemmimeɣ d akken yezmer lḥal ad d-yemmeslay : akken yebɣu yili lḥal, ulac lxir ma tɛerḍeḍ." Ihi tebda-d: “A Aɣersiw, tessneḍ abrid n tuffɣa seg lbabur-agi, ɛyiɣ aṭas seg uɛummu dagi, a Aɣersiw!” (Alice tettxemmim belli ayagi ilaq ad yili d abrid yelhan n tmesliwt i uɣersiw : ur texdim ara aya zik, maca tecfa-d belli teẓra deg Tjerrumt Talatinit n gma-s, “Aɣersiw—n uɣersiw—ɣer uɣersiw—aɣersiw—A uɣersiw!”) Aɣersiw-nni imuqel-itt s tmuɣli n tmuɣli s tmuɣli n tmuɣli, yerna yenna-as amzun ur t-id-yefki ara, yerna yenna-yas-d amzun ur t-id-yefki ara.

“Ahat ur tefhim ara taglizit,” i d-tenna Alice deg wul-is; “Nekk ttnadiɣ ad d-iniɣ d aɣersiw afransis, ssiwḍ-d ɣer-i d William Aɣella.” (Axaṭer, s tmusni-ines akk n umezruy, Alice ur tesɛi ara tikti ibanen aṭas acḥal aya seg wasmi ay d-yeḍra kra.) Ihi, tebda-d tikkelt niḍen: “Où est ma chatte?” d tafyirt tamezwarut deg udlis-is n temsirin n tefransist. Aɣersiw-nni yefka-d yiwen n usgunfu s tɣawla seg waman, yerna iban-d amzun yettḥezzib akk s tugdi. “Ah, ssutreɣ-ak ssmaḥ!” » i d-tenna Alice s tɣawla, tugad ad teḍḍef lḥir n uɣersiw-nni ameɣbun. “Ttuɣ akk ur tḥemmleḍ ara imcac.”

“Mačči am yimcac!” » i d-yessuɣ Uɣersiw, s ṣṣut n tmeẓẓuɣt, n tmeẓẓuɣt. “Ad tḥemmleḍ imcac lemmer d kečči i d nekk?”

"Aha, ahat ala," i d-tenna Alice s ṣṣut yessefsayen : "ur ttcekkiṛeḍ ara fell-as. U ɣas akken, ttmenniɣ ad ak-d-ssekneɣ amcic-nneɣ Dinah: ttwaliɣ ad tḥemmleḍ imcac ma yella tzemreḍ kan ad tt-twaliḍ. D taɣawsa tasussant ḥemmleɣ aṭas," i d-tenna Alice, azgen i yiman-is, akken tettɛummu s tɣawla. timest, tettḥezzib iḍarren-is yerna tessirid udem-is – yerna d taɣawsa n tmeẓẓuɣt yelhan akken ad tt-tessufeɣeḍ – yerna d taɣawsa n tmaneɣt akken ad teṭṭef iɣersiwen – ay, ssutreɣ-ak ssmaḥ!” » i d-tenna Alice tikkelt nniḍen, imi tikkelt-agi Aɣersiw-nni yella yettqerrib akk, yerna teḥsa s lḥeqq belli ilaq ad t-id-yesfeḍ s tidet. “Ur tt-nettmeslay ara s waṭas ma yella tḥemmleḍ ur tt-nettmeslay ara.”

“Nekni s tidet!” » i d-yessuɣ Uɣersiw, yellan yettnerni armi d taggara n tmeẓẓuɣt-is. "Amzun ad d -mmeslayeɣ ɣef usentel am wa! Tawacult-nneɣ dima tekṛeh imcac: tiɣawsiwin n tkerkas, n tkerkas, n tkerkas! Ur iyi-ttaǧǧa ara ad sleɣ i yisem-nni tikkelt nniḍen!"

“Ur t-id-ttaɣeɣ ara s tidet!” » i d-tenna Alice, s tɣawla tameqqrant akken ad tbeddel asentel n tmesliwt. “Tesɛiḍ—teḥemmleḍ—iqjan?” Aɣersiw ur d-yerri ara, ihi Alice tkemmel s lferḥ : « Yella yiwen n uqjun ameẓyan yelhan am wa qrib n wexxam-nneɣ, bɣiɣ ad ak-d-ssekneɣ ! Yiwen n uqjun ameẓyan s wallen icebḥen, teẓriḍ, s yiɣes n tmeẓẓuɣt ɣezzifen am wa ! deg-sen—yerna d ayla n yiwen n ufellaḥ, teẓriḍ, yerna yenna-d d akken yesɛa aṭas n nnfeɛ, yesɛa azal n mya n yidinaren Yenna-d d akken yenɣa akk iɣersiwen yerna—ay ḥbib-iw!” » i d-tenna Alice s ṣṣut n lḥif, “Ugadeɣ ad tt-ɛeqleɣ tikkelt nniḍen!” Imi Aɣersiw-nni yella yettɛummu fell-as akken izemren, yerna ixeddem aṭas n ccwal deg tmeqbert akken iteddu.

Ihi tessawel-d s lḥir deffir-s, "Aɣersiw aɛziz! Ɣerr-d tikkelt nniḍen, yerna ula d nekni ur nettmeslay ara ɣef yimcac neɣ ɣef yiqjan, ma yella ur ten-teḥemmleḍ ara!" Mi yesla Uɣersiw-nni s waya, yezzi-d yerna yeɛum s lemɣawla ɣer-s : udem-is yella d aẓiḍan aṭas (s lḥir, i d-tenna Alice deg wul-is), yerna yenna-d s ṣṣut n tmeẓẓuɣt n tmeẓẓuɣt, “Eǧǧ-aɣ ad naweḍ ɣer tlisa, sakkin ad ak-d-iniɣ amezruy-iw, ad tfehmeḍ ayɣer ay ḥemmleɣ imcac d yiqjan.”

Yewweḍ-d wakud ad nruḥ, imi lbabur-nni yella yettnerni s waṭas s yigḍaḍ d yiɣersiwen i d-yeɣlin deg-s : yella yiwen n Uḍebsi d yiwen n Dodo, yiwen n Lory d yiwen n Uɣersiw, d waṭas n yimdanen nniḍen iḥemmlen ad ẓren. Alice teḍfer abrid, yerna tarbaɛt-nni akk teɛum ɣer tlemmast n yilel.
