Anwa i yukren tiẓegwa?

Agellid d Tgellidt n Wulawen qqimen ɣef tgelda-nsen mi d-wwḍen, s lɣaci ameqqran i d-yesnejmaɛen fell-asen – akk ṣṣenf n yigḍaḍ d yiɣersiwen imeẓyanen, am wakken d akk lbaṭaṭa n tkarḍiwin : Knave yella ibedd zdat-sen, s snasel, s yiwen n lɛeskeṛ di yal tama akken ad t-iḥudd ; zdat n Ugellid yella Uqcic Amellal, deg yiwen ufus s lbuq, deg wayeḍ s yiwen n wedlis n lqecc. Deg tlemmast n ccṛeɛ s timmad-is, tella yiwet n ṭṭabla, s yiwen n uḍebsi ameqran n ttart : ttbanent-d s lxir, armi d ayen i t-yeǧǧan Alice ad telluẓ aṭas mi ara tent-tmuqel — « Ahat ad ssiwḍen ccṛeɛ », i d-tenna deg wul-is, « yerna ad s-d-fken afus ɣef tɣawsiwin n tmeɣra !» Maca iban-d ur yelli ara lḥal n waya, dɣa tebda tettmuqul deg wayen akk yellan fell-as, akken ad tesɛeddi lweqt.

Alice ur telli ara zik deg ccṛeɛ n lḥeqq, maca teɣra fell-asen deg yedlisen, yerna tefṛeḥ aṭas imi ay d-tufa tessen isem n qrib yal taɣawsa ay yellan din. “D wagi i d lqaḍi”, i d-tenna i yiman-is, “ɣef lǧal n tmeṭṭut-is tameqqrant.”

Lqaḍi, s tɣawla, d Agellid; yerna akken yelsa taɛkemt-is ɣef tmeqbert, (muqel ɣer tqacuct ma tebɣiḍ ad twaliḍ amek i t-yexdem,) ur imuqel ara akk s lxir, yerna s tidet ur yelli ara yettuɣal.

“U d aya ay d lḥebs n lḥukuma”, ay d-tenna Alice deg wul-nnes, “d tnac n yimdanen-nni,” (tella tettḥettim ad d-tini “imdanen”, twaliḍ, imi kra seg-sen d iɣersiwen, ma d kra d igḍaḍ,) “Ɣileɣ d lḥukuma.” Tenna-d awal-agi aneggaru snat neɣ tlata n tikkal i yiman-is, tettḥulfu s waya s lferḥ : imi tettxemmim, yerna s lḥeqq ula d nettat, belli drus aṭas n teqcicin timecṭaḥ n leɛmer-is i yessnen anamek-is akk. D acu kan, “irmen n lḥukuma” ad xedmen akken ilaq.

Tnac n yijurjiyen llan ttarun akk s lxedma tameqqrant ef tfelwiyin. “D acu i xeddmen?” Alice tessusem i Grifun. “Ur zmiren ara ad sɛun kra ara ssersen ar ass-a, uqbel ad tebdu ccṛeɛ.”

“Ssersen ismawen-nsen”, i d-yerra Grifun s tmeẓẓuɣt, “seg tugdi ad ten-ttun uqbel ad tfak ccṛeɛ.”

“Tiɣawsiwin tibuhalin!” Tebda Alice s ṣṣut ɛlayen, s lḥir, maca teḥbes s tɣawla, imi Aqcic Amellal yessuɣu-d: “Ssusmet deg ccṛeɛ!” yerna Agellid yerra l’écran-is, imuqel s lxiq, akken ad iẓer anwa i d-yettmeslayen.

Alice tezmer ad twali, am wakken tettmuqul ɣef yiḍarren-nsen, belli akk iɛeggalen n lḥukuma ttarun-d “tiɣawsiwin n tkerkas!” ɣef tfelwiyin-nsen, yerna tezmer ula ad tfehmeḍ belli yiwen seg-sen ur yessin ara ad d-yesnulfu “aɛewwiq”, yerna yessefk ad yesteqsi lǧar-is ad as-d-yini. “A nice muddle ad yili deg-s slates-nsen uqbel ad tfak ccṛeɛ!” Tenna-d Alis.

Yiwen seg yijuṛṛiyen yesɛa aqerruy n tmeẓẓuɣt i d-yettqerriben. Aya d tidet, Alice ur tezmir ara ad tebded, yerna tezzi-d i lḥebs, tewweḍ deffir-s, yerna ur tɛeṭṭel ara tufa tagnit ad tt-tekkes. Texdem-it s tɣawla armi ur yezmir ara ad d-yefhem akk d acu i t-id-yeḍran ; ihi, deffir ma yeḥḍer akk fell-as, yella yettwaḥettem fell-as ad yaru s yiwen n uḍad akk ass-nni; yerna aya ur yesɛi ara aṭas n lfayda, imi ur yeǧǧi ara lḥeqq deg tfelwit.

“Herald, ɣri-d ccek-nni!” Yenna-d Ugellid.

« Tagellidt n wulawen, tga kra n ttart, .

“Ssiwlet i unagi amezwaru,” i d-yenna Ugellid; u Aqcic Amellal yessufeɣ-d kraḍ n tmeẓẓuɣin ɣef lbuq, yerna yessawel-d: “Aceggeɛ amezwaru!”

Anagi amezwaru d Hatter. Yekcem-d s tmeqbert n tesraft deg yiwen ufus, deg wayeḍ s tmeqbert n uɣrum d lḥemla. « Ssuref-iyi, a Sidi », i d-yebda, « imi d-tessekcemḍ wigi : maca ur kcimeɣ ara akk lgaz-iw mi iyi-d-ssawḍen ».

“Ilaq ad tkemleḍ,” i d-yenna ugellid. “Melmi i tebdiḍ?”

Hatter imuqel ɣer Uqcic n Meɣres, i t-id-yeḍfer ɣer ccṛeɛ, s ufus deg ufus d Uqcic n Dormouse. « Arbaɛ n meɣres, ttwaliɣ d akken yella », i d-yenna.

“Xemsa d mraw”, i d-yenna Uqcic n Maɣres.

“Wis mraw d setta”, i d-yerna Dormouse.

“Kkes-d aqerruy-ik”, i d-yenna Ugellid i Uqecwal.

“Ttḥerriqeɣ-tent akken ad tent-zzenzeɣ”, i d-yerna Uqcic-nni d asefhem; "Ur sɛiɣ ula d yiwen seg yiman-iw. Nekk d aḥeqqani."

Dagi Tagellidt tessers-d tiqendyar-is, tebda tettmuqul deg Uqcic-nni, yuɣal d aɛebbuḍ yerna yettḥulfu s lḥir.

Aya iban-d ur yessebɣas ara akk anagi-nni : yeqqim yettbeddil seg uḍar ɣer wayeḍ, yettmuqul s lqella n lḥir ɣer Tgellidt, yerna deg lxuf-is, yečča-d yiwen n lḥebs ameqran seg tkeṛṛust-is n tesraft deg umkan n weɣrum-nni n lḥemla.

Deg teswiɛt-agi kan, Alice teḥsa s yiwen n usḥulfu n tkerkas aṭas, d ayen i tt-yesḥissifen aṭas armi d asmi ay d-tefhem d acu-t : tebda tettnerni tikkelt niḍen, yerna tettxemmim deg tazwara ad d-tekker ad teffeɣ seg ccṛeɛ ; maca deg tmuɣli tis snat tɣtes ad teqqim anda tella skud yella wemkan i-s.

“Ahat ur tettqerribeḍ ara akka”, i d-yenna Uqcic n Dormouse, yellan yeqqim ɣer tama-s. “Aql-iyi ur zmireɣ ara ad snefseɣ.”

“Ur zmireɣ ara ad t- ḥebseɣ,” i d- tenna Alice s lḥir meqqren: “Nekk ttnerniɣ.”

“Ur tesɛiḍ ara azref ad tnerniḍ dagi ,” i d-yenna Dormouse.

“Ur ttmeslay ara s lbaṭel,” i d-tenna Alice s tebɣest ugar: “teẓriḍ ula d kečč tettnerniḍ.”

“Ih, maca ttnernin s tɣawla s lmeɛqul”, i d-yenna Dormouse: “mačči s tɣawla-nni n tmeḥqranit.” Yerna yekker s lḥir ameqqran, iɛedda ɣer tama nniḍen n lḥebs.

Tikkelt-agi akk Tagellidt ur teqqim ara tettmuqul deg Hatter, yerna, akken kan i d-tezger Dormouse lḥebs, tenna-as i yiwen seg yifesyanen n lḥebs: “Awi-yi-d listi n yicennayen n tmeɣra taneggarut!” ɣef wacu i d-yesfeḍ Hatter aɛdaw-nni armi d asmi i d-yesfeḍ sin n yiḍarren-is.

“Efk-d ttbut-ik,” i d-yenna Ugellid s lɣiḍa, “ma ulac ad k-nɣeɣ, ama tḥemmleḍ neɣ ala.”

“Nekk d ameɣbun, a Sidi-inek,” i d-yebda Uqcic-nni, s ṣṣut yettreggizen, “—yerna ur bdiɣ ara lqahwa-inu – mačči nnig n ddurt neɣ azal-is – d acu ara d-yini s weɣrum-nni d lbaṭaṭa i d-yettnernin aṭas – d tmeẓẓuɣt n lqahwa-nni...”

« Acu kan, akken yebɣu yili lḥal, yenna-d Dormouse... » ikemmel Hatter, yettmuqul s lḥir deg tlemmast akken ad iẓer ma yella ula d netta ad t-yenker : maca Dormouse ur yenker kra, imi yella yeṭṭes s lḥir.

Dagi yiwen seg yiqjan n tmurt n Guinea yessewham, yerna ssawḍen ad t-ḥebsen yifesyanen n ccṛeɛ. (Imi aya d awal iqesden, ad ak-d-sfehmeɣ kan amek ay t-xedmen. Ɣur-sen yiwet n tkeṛṛust tameqrant n lkanvas, ay qqnen ɣer yimi-nnes s tɣawsiwin n tɣawsiwin : deg waya ay d-sliḍen lbaṭaṭa n lbaṭaṭa, aqerruy-nnes d amezwaru, sakkin qqimen fell-as.)

“Freḥeɣ imi walaɣ ayagi yettwaxdem,” i d-tenna Alice deg wul-is. “Qriɣ-d aṭas deg yeɣmisen, deg tgara n ccṛeɛ, “Yella-d kra n ɛewwel n tmeɣra, d win ay sqedcen yifesyanen n ccṛeɛ imiren kan”, yerna ur fhimeɣ ara d acu ay d anamek-nnes ar ass-a.”

Alice tettwali Aqcic Amellal mi yettmuqul deg listiɛmar, teḥsa s lḥir ameqqran akken ad twali amek ara yili unagi i d-iteddun, “—imi ur sɛin ara aṭas n ttbut ,” i d-tenna i yiman-is. Xayel-d amek testeɛref, mi d-yeɣra Uqcic Amellal, s tqacuct n ṣṣut-is ameẓyan i d-yesɣerḍen, isem “Alice!”
