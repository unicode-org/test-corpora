KGAOLO IX.
Kgang ya Khudu e e Tshwanelang

“Ga o ka ke wa akanya gore ke itumela jang go go bona gape, wena selo sa bogologolo se se rategang!” Ga rialo Duchess, fa a ntse a tsenya letsogo la gagwe ka lorato mo go la ga Alice, mme ba tsamaya mmogo.

Alice o ne a itumela thata go mo fitlhela a le mo bogale jo bo monate jaana, mme a ipolelela gore gongwe ke pepere fela e e mo dirileng gore a nne setlhogo jaana fa ba kopana mo ntlwaneng ya boapeelo.

“Fa ke le Duchess,” o ne a bua jalo mo pelong, (e seng ka lentswe le le nang le tsholofelo thata le fa go ntse jalo), “Ga nkitla ke nna le pepere epe mo kitšhineng ya me gotlhelele . Sopo e dira sentle thata kwantle ga—Gongwe ka metlha ke pepere e e dirang gore batho ba galefe,” o ne a tswelela jalo, a itumeletse thata go bo a lemogile rugar mofuta o mosha wa viur, makedne camomile e e dirang gore di nne botlhoko—le—le barley-sugar le dilo tse di ntseng jalo tse di dirang gore bana ba nne le bogale jo bo monate Ke eletsa fela gore batho ba ka bo ba itse seo : foo ba ka bo ba sa nne botlhofo jalo ka gone, o a itse—”

O ne a setse a lebetse Duchess gotlhelele ka nako eno, mme a tshoga go sekae fa a utlwa lentswe la gagwe le le gaufi le tsebe ya gagwe. "O akanya ka sengwe moratiwa wa me, mme seo se dira gore o lebale go bua. Ga ke kgone go go bolelela gone jaanong fela gore boitsholo jwa seo ke eng, mme ke tla se gakologelwa moragonyana."

Alice o ne a itlhaganelela go akgela jaana: “Gongwe ga e na epe.”

“Tut, tut ngwana!” Ga rialo Duchess. “Sengwe le sengwe se na le boitsholo, fa fela o ka bo bona.” Mme a ipitla gaufi le letlhakore la ga Alice fa a ntse a bua.

Alice o ne a sa rate thata go nna gaufi le ene jalo: sa ntlha, ka gonne Duchess o ne a le maswe thata ; mme sa bobedi, ka gonne o ne a le boleele jo bo siameng tota go baya seledu sa gagwe mo legetleng la ga Alice, mme e ne e le seledu se se bogale se se sa phuthologang. Le fa go ntse jalo, o ne a sa rate go nna makgwakgwa, ka jalo o ne a go itshokela kafa a neng a ka kgona ka gone.

“Motshameko o tswelela botoka jaanong,” o ne a rialo, ka tsela ya go tswelela ka motlotlo go sekae.

“‘Go ntse jalo,” ga rialo Duchess: “mme boitsholo jwa seo ke—‘Oh, ‘ke lorato, ‘ke lorato, lo lo dirang gore lefatshe le dikologe!’”

“Mongwe o ne a re,” Alice o ne a seba, “gore go dirwa ke gore mongwe le mongwe a tlhokomele dilo tsa gagwe!”

“Ah, go siame! Go raya selo se se tshwanang thata,” ga rialo Duchess, a epa seledu sa gagwe se sennye se se bogale mo legetleng la ga Alice fa a ntse a oketsa jaana, “mme boitsholo jwa seo ke—‘Tlhokomela tlhaloganyo, mme medumo e tla itlhokomela.’”

“A bo a rata jang ne go bona boitsholo mo dilong!” Alice a akanya jalo mo pelong.

“Ke na le sebete sa go re o a ipotsa gore ke eng fa ke sa go tshwara letsogo mo lethekeng,” Duchess o ne a rialo morago ga go ema go sekae: “lebaka ke gore, ke belaela ka bogale jwa flamingo ya gago. A ke leke go dira tekeletso?”

“A ka nna a loma,” Alice o ne a araba jalo ka kelotlhoko, a sa ikutlwe a tlhobaela gotlhelele gore go lekwe tekeletso eo.

“Ke boammaaruri tota,” ga rialo Duchess: “di- flamingo le mustard ka bobedi di a loma. Mme boitsholo jwa seo ke gore—‘Dinonyane tsa masiba di kokoana mmogo.’”

Alice o ne a akgela jaana: “Ke mustard fela e e seng nonyane.”

“Go siame, jaaka gale,” ga rialo Duchess: “a bo o na le tsela e e tlhamaletseng jang ne ya go baya dilo!”

“Ke diminerale, ke akanya jalo ,” ga rialo Alice.

“Ee ruri go ntse jalo,” ga rialo Duchess, yo o neng a lebega a siametse go dumalana le sengwe le sengwe se Alice a neng a se bua; “go na le moepo o mogolo wa masetate gaufi le fano. Mme boitsholo jwa seo ke gore—‘Fa go na le tsa me tse dintsi, go na le tsa gago tse dinnye.’”

“Ao, ke a itse!” ga goa Alice, yo o neng a sa tlhokomela kakgelo eno ya bofelo, "ke morogo. Ga o lebege jaaka one, mme ke one."

“Ke dumalana le wena gotlhelele,” ga rialo Duchess; “mme boitsholo jwa seo ke gore—‘Nna se o neng o tla lebega o le sone’—kgotsa fa o ka rata gore go tlhalosiwe ka tsela e e bonolo—‘Le ka motlha o se ka wa akanya gore ga o ka tsela e sele go na le se go ka lebegang mo go ba bangwe gore se o neng o le sone kgotsa se o ka bong o le sone se ne se se ka tsela e sele go na le se o neng o le sone se ka bo se bonale mo go bone se le ka tsela e sele.’”

“Ke akanya gore ke ne ke tshwanetse go tlhaloganya seo botoka,” Alice o ne a bua jalo ka maitseo thata, “fa ke ne ke ka se kwala: mme ga ke kgone go se latela sentle jaaka o se bua.”

“Seo ga se sepe mo go se nka se buang fa nka tlhopha,” Duchess a araba, ka lentswe le le itumetseng.

“Rapela o se ka wa itshwenyetsa go e bua lobaka lo loleele go feta foo,” ga rialo Alice.

“Ao, o se ka wa bua ka mathata!” Ga rialo Duchess. “Ke go direla mpho ya sengwe le sengwe se ke se buileng go fitlha jaanong.”

“Mofuta wa mpho e e tlhwatlhwatlase!” Alice o ne a akanya jalo. “Ke itumelela gore ga ba ntshe dimpho tsa letsatsi la matsalo jalo!” Mme ga a ka a itetla go e buela kwa godimo.

“O akanya gape?” Duchess a botsa, a epa gape seledu sa gagwe se sennye se se bogale.

“Ke na le tshwanelo ya go akanya,” Alice o ne a bua jalo ka bogale, ka gonne o ne a simolola go ikutlwa a tshwenyegile go sekae.

“Go siame fela jalo,” ga rialo Duchess, “jaaka dikolobe di tshwanetse go fofa; mme m—”

Mme fano, Alice o ne a gakgamala thata fa lentswe la ga Duchess le ne la swa, tota le mo gare ga lefoko le a le ratang la ‘boitsholo,’ mme letsogo le le neng le golagantswe le la gagwe le ne la simolola go roroma. Alice o ile a sheba hodimo, mme moo ho ne ho eme Kgosigadi pela bona, a phuthetse matsoho, a sosobantse sefahleho jwalo ka sefefo sa maru.

“Letsatsi le lentle, Morena wa gago!” Duchess a simolola ka lentswe le le kwa tlase le le bokoa.

“Jaanong, ke go naya tlhagiso e e siameng,” ga goa Kgosigadi, a gata fa fatshe fa a ntse a bua; “wena kgotsa tlhogo ya gago e tshwanetse ya bo e dule, mme seo mo nakong e e ka nnang halofo! Tsaya tlhopho ya gago!”

Duchess o ile a nka kgetho ya hae, mme a tsamaile ka motsotsoana.

“A re tsweleleng ka motshameko,” Kgosigadi a raya Alice a rialo; mme Alice o ne a tshogile thata go ka bua lefoko, mme a mo sala morago ka iketlo a boela kwa lebaleng la croquet.

Baeng ba bangwe ba ne ba dirisitse go tlhokafala ga ga Kgosigadi, mme ba ne ba ikhutsa mo moriting: le fa go ntse jalo, ka nako e ba neng ba mmona ka yone, ba ne ba boela kwa motshamekong ka bonako, Kgosigadi a tshwaela fela gore go diega ka motsotso go tla ba jela matshelo.

Nako yotlhe e ba neng ba tshameka ka yone Kgosigadi ga a ise a ke a tlogele go omana le batshameki ba bangwe, le go goa a re “Off with his head!” kgotsa “Tlogela tlhogo ya gagwe!” Ba a neng a ba atlhola ba ne ba tshwarwa ke masole, a eleruri a neng a tshwanelwa ke go tlogela go nna di-arch go dira seno, gore kwa bokhutlong jwa halofo ya ura kgotsa go feta go bo go se na di-arch tse di setseng, mme batshameki botlhe, kwantle ga Kgosi, Kgosigadi le Alice, ba ne ba le mo kgolegelong e bile ba le ka fa tlase ga katlholo ya polao.

Ke fa Kgosigadi a tlogela, a feditswe ke mowa gotlhelele, mme a raya Alice a re, “A o setse o bone Khudu ya Mock?”

Alice o ne a re: “Nnyaa.” “Ga ke itse le gore Mock Turtle ke eng.”

“Ke selo se Mock Turtle Soup e dirilweng ka sone,” ga rialo Kgosigadi.

Alice o ne a re: “Ga ke ise ke tsamaye ke bone epe, kgotsa ke utlwe ka yone.

“Tlaya, he,” ga rialo Kgosigadi, “mme o tla go bolelela hisitori ya gagwe.”

Fa ba ntse ba tsamaya mmogo, Alice o ne a utlwa Kgosi a bua ka lentswe le le kwa tlase, mo khamphaning ka kakaretso, “Lo itshwaretswe lotlhe.” “Tla, seo ke selo se se molemo!” a bua jalo mo pelong, ka a ne a ikutlwa a sa itumela gotlhelele ka palo ya dipolao tse Kgosigadi a neng a di laetse.

Go ise go ye kae ba ne ba kopana le Gryphon, e robetse thata mo letsatsing. (Haeba o sa tsebe hore Gryphon ke eng, sheba setshwantsho.) “Hodimo, ntho ya botshwakga!” ga rialo Kgosigadi, “mme o tseye lekgarebe leno go ya go bona Khudu ya Motshego, le go utlwa hisitori ya gagwe. Ke tshwanetse go boela morago go ya go bona morago ga dipolao dingwe tse ke di laetseng;” mme a tsamaya, a tlogela Alice a le esi le Gryphon. Alice o ne a sa rate gotlhelele tebego ya setshedi seno, mme ka kakaretso o ne a akanya gore go tla babalesega go nna le sone fela jaaka go latela Kgosigadi eo e e setlhogo: ka jalo o ne a leta.

Gryphon ya nna fa fatshe ya itlotsa matlho: ya bo e lebile Kgosigadi go fitlhela a sa tlhole a bonala: ya bo e nyenya. “A bo go le monate jang ne!” Ga rialo Gryphon, halofo e le nosi, halofo e le Alice.

“Monate ke eng?” ” Alice o boletse.

“Ee, ene ,” ga rialo Gryphon. "Ke bontle jwa gagwe fela, gore: ga ba ke ba bolaya ope, o a itse. Tlaya!"

“Mongwe le mongwe a re ‘tla!’ fano,” Alice o ne a akanya jalo, fa a ntse a e latela ka iketlo: “Ga ke ise ke tsamaye ke laelwe jaana mo botshelong jwa me jotlhe, le ka motlha!”

Ba ne ba ise ba tsamaye kgakala pele ga ba bona Khudu ya Motshego kgakala, e ntse e hutsafetse e bile e jewa ke bodutu mo godimo ga leje le lennye la letlapa, mme, fa ba ntse ba atamela, Alice o ne a kgona go mo utlwa a fegelwa jaaka e kete pelo ya gagwe e tla thubega. O ne a mo utlwela botlhoko thata. “Kutlobotlhoko ya gagwe ke eng?” a botsa Gryphon, mme Gryphon a araba, a le gaufi thata ka mafoko a a tshwanang le a pele, “Ke fancy ya gagwe yotlhe, gore: ga a na kutlobotlhoko epe, o a itse. Tlaya!”

Ka jalo ba tlhatlogela kwa go Mock Turtle, yo o neng a ba leba ka matlho a magolo a a tletseng dikeledi, mme a sa bue sepe.

“Lekgarebe le le fano,” ga rialo Gryphon, “o batla go itse hisitori ya gago, o a itse.”

“Ke tla e mmolelela,” ga rialo Khudu ya Motshegi ka lentswe le le tseneletseng, le le khuti: “dulang fa fatshe, lona ka bobedi, mme lo se ka lwa bua lefoko go fitlha ke fetsa.”

Ka jalo ba ne ba nna fa fatshe, mme ga go ope yo o neng a bua metsotso e le mmalwa. Alice o ne a ipolelela jaana, “Ga ke bone gore a ka tsamaya a fetsa jang, fa a sa simolole.” Mme o ne a leta ka bopelotelele.

“Nako nngwe,” ga rialo Khudu ya Motshegi kwa bofelong, ka go fegelwa kwa teng, “Ke ne ke le Khudu ya mmatota.”

Mafoko ano a ne a latelwa ke tidimalo e telele thata, e e neng e thubiwa fela ke go goa ga nako le nako ga “Hjckrrh!” go tswa go Gryphon, le go lela go go bokete go go sa kgaotseng ga Mock Turtle. Alice o ne a le gaufi thata le go tsoga mme a re, “Ke a leboga, rra, ka kgang ya gago e e kgatlhang,” mme o ne a sa kgone go itshwara a akanya gore go tshwanetse ga bo go na le tse dingwe tse di tlang, ka jalo o ne a nna fela a sa bue sepe.

“Fa re ne re le bannye,” Khudu ya Motshegi ya tswelela kwa bofelong, e ritibetse thata, le fa e ne e santse e lela go sekae nako le nako, “re ne re tsena sekolo kwa lewatleng. Mong wa yone e ne e le Khudu ya bogologolo—re ne re tle re mmitse Khudu—”

“Ke eng o ne o mmitsa Khudu, fa e ne e se mongwe wa bone?” Alice o ne a botsa.

“Re ne ra mmitsa Khudu ka gonne o ne a re ruta,” ga rialo Khudu ya Motshego e galefile: “tota o bodutu thata!”

“O tshwanetse go tlhabiwa ke ditlhong ka go botsa potso e e motlhofo jaana,” ga oketsa jalo Gryphon; mme morago ga moo bobedi jwa bone ba nna ba didimetse mme ba leba Alice yo o humanegileng, yo o neng a ikutlwa a siametse go nwela mo lefatsheng. La bofelo Gryphon a raya Mock Turtle a re, "Kgweetsa pele, monnamogolo! O se ka wa nna letsatsi lotlhe ka gone!" mme a tswelela ka mafoko a:

“Ee, re tsene sekolo kwa lewatleng, le fa o ka tswa o sa dumele—”

“Ga ke ise ke tsamaye ke re ga ke itse!” Alice o ne a mo kgaola.

“O dirile jalo,” ga rialo Khudu ya Mock.

“Tshwara leleme!” ga oketsa Gryphon, pele ga Alice a ka bua gape. Khudu ya Mock ya tswelela.

“Re ne re na le dithuto tse di molemolemo—tota e bile, re ne re tsena sekolo letsatsi le letsatsi—”

“ Le nna ke kile ka tsena sekolo sa motshegare,” ga rialo Alice; “ga o tlhoke go nna mabela jaaka dilo tseo tsotlhe.”

“Ka ditlaleletso?” ga botsa Khudu ya Mock a tlhobaela go se kae.

Alice o ne a re: “Ee, re ne ra ithuta Sefora le mmino.”

“Le go tlhapa?” Ga rialo Khudu ya Mock.

“Ruri ga go a nna jalo!” Ga rialo Alice a galefile.

“Ah!he ya gago e ne e se sekolo se se siameng tota,” ga rialo Khudu ya Motshegi ka lentswe la go imologa thata. “Jaanong kwa go rona ba ne ba na le kwa bokhutlong jwa sekoloto, ‘Sefora, mmino le go tlhapa —tse di oketsegileng.’”

Alice o ne a re: “O ka bo o sa e batla thata; “ba ba nnang kwa tlase ga lewatle.”

“Ke ne ke sa kgone go e ithuta.” Ga bua Khudu ya Mock a fegelwa. “Ke ne ke tsaya fela khoso e e tlwaelegileng.”

“E ne e le eng seo?” Alice o ne a botsa jalo.

“Go roroma le go tlhotlhorega, e le ruri, go simolola,” Khudu ya Mock e ne ya araba jalo; “mme morago ga moo makala a a farologaneng a Dipalo—Go Batla Maemo a a kwa Godimo, Go Iteka Tseba, Go Nna Maswe le go Sotlwa.”

Alice o ne a itlhaganelela go bua jaana: “Ga ke ise ke tsamaye ke utlwe ka ‘Uglification.’” “Ke eng?”

Gryphon ya tsholetsa maroo a yone ka bobedi ka kgakgamalo. "Eng! Ga ke ise ke tsamaye ke utlwe ka go maswe!" e ne ya goa. “O itse gore go ntlafatsa ke eng, ke akanya jalo?”

“Ee,” Alice o ne a bua jalo a belaela: “go raya—go—dira—sengwe le sengwe—sentle go feta.”

“Ee, he,” Gryphon a tswelela, “fa o sa itse gore go dira gore go nne maswe ke eng, o seeleele.”

Alice ga a ka a ikutlwa a rotloetsega go botsa dipotso tse dingwe ka ga yone, ka jalo o ne a retologela kwa Khudu ya Motšhini, mme a re “Ke eng se sengwe se o neng o tshwanetse go se ithuta?”

“Ebu, go ne go na le Masaitsiweng,” Khudu ya Motshego e ne ya araba jalo, e bala ditlhogo tse di neng di le mo di-flapper tsa yone, “—Masaitsiweng, a bogologolo le a segompieno, ka Seaography: morago ga moo Go Goga—mong wa go Goga e ne e le conger-eel ya bogologolo, e e neng e tle e tle gangwe ka beke: o ne a re ruta go Goga le go Otlolla, go Goga.”

“ Seo se ne se ntse jang?” ” Alice o boletse.

"Ee, ga ke kgone go go bontsha ka bonna," Khudu ya Motshego ya rialo: "Ke gagametse thata. Mme Gryphon ga e ise e ko e e ithute."

"Ke ne ke se na nako," ga rialo Gryphon: "Ke ne ka ya kwa go mong wa Classics, le fa go ntse jalo. E ne e le lekakaie la bogologolo, o ne a le jalo."

“Ga ke ise ke ko ke ye kwa go ene,” Khudu ya Motshego ya rialo e fegelwa: “o ne a ruta Go Tshega le Khutsafalo, ba ne ba tle ba rialo.”

“O ne a dira jalo, o ne a dira jalo,” ga rialo Gryphon, a fegelwa ka nako ya gagwe; mme dibopiwa tseno ka bobedi tsa fitlha difatlhego tsa tsone mo maroong a tsone.

“Mme o ne o dira dithuto diura di le kae ka letsatsi?” Ga rialo Alice, a itlhaganelela go fetola kgang.

“Diura di le lesome letsatsi la ntlha,” ga rialo Khudu ya Motshegi: “diura di le robongwe letsatsi le le latelang, jalo le jalo.”

“A leano la go batla go itse jang ne!” Alice o ne a goa jalo.

“Ke lone lebaka la go bo di bidiwa dithuto,” Gryphon o ne a akgela jalo: “ka gonne di fokotsega letsatsi le letsatsi.”

Eno e ne e le kgopolo e ntšha tota mo go Alice, mme o ne a e akanya go sekae pele ga a bua kakgelo ya gagwe e e latelang. “Mme letsatsi la bolesome le bongwe le tshwanetse la bo le ne le le letsatsi la boikhutso?”

“Ee, e ne e le teng,” ga rialo Khudu ya Mock.

“Mme o kgonne jang ka la bolesomepedi?” Alice o ne a tswelela ka tlhoafalo.

“Go lekane ka dithuto,” Gryphon a mo kgaola ka lentswe le le tlhomameng thata: “mmolelele sengwe ka metshameko jaanong.”
