KGAOLO V.
Kgakololo go tswa go Seboko

Seboko le Alice ba ne ba lebana ka nako e e rileng ba didimetse: kgabagare Seboko sa ntsha hookah mo molomong wa sone, mme sa mo bua ka lentswe le le bodutu, le le robetseng.

“O mang? ” ga rialo Seboko.

Seno e ne e se tsela e e kgothatsang ya go simolola motlotlo. Alice o ne a araba, go na le moo a le ditlhong, “Nna—ga ke itse gotlhelele, rra, fela mo nakong eno—bobotlana ke itse gore ke ne ke le mang fa ke tsoga mosong ono, mme ke akanya gore ke tshwanetse ka bo ke fetotswe makgetlo a le mmalwa fa e sa le ka nako eo.”

“O raya jang ka seo?” Ga bua Seboko ka bogale. “Itlhalose!”

“Ga ke kgone go itlhalosa , ke a tshoga, rra,” ga rialo Alice, “ka gonne ga ke nna, o a bona.”

“Ga ke bone,” ga rialo Seboko.

“Ke tshaba gore nka se kgone go e tlhalosa sentle go feta foo,” Alice o ne a araba jalo ka maitseo thata, “gonne nna ga ke kgone go e tlhaloganya go simolola; mme go nna le bogolo jo bontsi jo bo farologaneng mo letsatsing le le lengwe go tlhakanya tlhogo tota.”

“Ga go a nna jalo,” ga rialo Seboko.

“Ebu, gongwe ga o ise o e fitlhele jalo,” ga rialo Alice; “mme fa o tshwanelwa ke go fetoga go nna chrysalis—o tla dira jalo letsatsi lengwe, o a itse—mme morago ga moo o fetoge serurubele, ke tshwanetse go akanya gore o tla e utlwa e sa tlwaelega go sekae, a ga go jalo?”

“E seng go le gonnye,” ga rialo Seboko.

Alice o ne a re: “Ebu, gongwe maikutlo a gago a ka tswa a farologane; “se ke se itseng fela ke gore, e ne e tla utlwala e sa tlwaelega mo go nna .”

"Wena!" Ga bua Seboko ka lenyatso. "O mang ? "

Se se neng sa ba busetsa gape kwa tshimologong ya motlotlo. Alice o ne a ikutlwa a tenegile go sekae ka ntlha ya go dira ditshwaelo tse dikhutshwane thata jaana tsa ga Seboko, mme a ikgoga mme a re, ka masisi thata, “Ke akanya gore, o tshwanetse go mpolelela gore o mang, pele.”

"Goreng?" Ga rialo Seboko.

Potso e nngwe e e neng e tlhakanya tlhogo e ne e le eno; mme ka Alice a ne a sa kgone go akanya ka lebaka lepe le le utlwalang, le ka Seboko se ne se lebega se le mo seemong se se sa itumediseng thata sa mogopolo, o ne a fapoga.

“Boa!” Seboko sa mmitsa morago ga gagwe. “Ke na le sengwe sa botlhokwa se ke batlang go se bua!”

Seno se ne se utlwala se solofetsa, eleruri: Alice o ne a retologa mme a boa gape.

“Nna o galefile,” ga rialo Seboko.

“A ke gone fela?” Ga rialo Alice, a kometsa bogale jwa gagwe ka fa a neng a ka kgona ka teng.

“Nnyaa,” ga rialo Seboko.

Alice o ne a akanya gore a ka leta fela jalo, ka a ne a sena sepe se sengwe se a ka se dirang, mme gongwe morago ga tsotlhe e ka nna ya mmolelela sengwe se se tshwanetseng go utlwiwa. Ka metsotso e itseng e ile ya buduloa e sa bue, empa qetellong ya phutholola matsoho a yona, ya ntsha hookah molomong wa yona hape, mme ya re, “Kahoo o nahana hore o fetohile, na?”

“Ke tshaba gore ke nna, rra,” ga rialo Alice; “Ga ke kgone go gakologelwa dilo jaaka ke ne ke di tlwaetse—e bile ga ke boloke bogolo jo bo tshwanang ka metsotso e le lesome mmogo!”

“Ga o gakologelwe gore ke dilo dife ?” Ga rialo Seboko.

“Ebu, ke lekile go re “Notshi e nnye e e tshwaregileng e dira jang,” mme tsotlhe di tlile ka go farologana!” Alice o ne a araba ka lentswe le le hutsafetseng thata.

“Boeletsa, ‘ O tsofetse, Rre William ,’” ga rialo Seboko.

Alice a phutha diatla tsa gagwe, mme a simolola:—

“O tsofetse, Rre William,” lekawana leo le ne la rialo, .
“Mme moriri wa gago o sweufetse thata;
Mme le fa go ntse jalo o eme ka tlhogo o sa kgaotse—
A o akanya gore, mo dingwageng tsa gago, go siame?”

“Mo bosheng jwa me,” Rre William o ne a araba morwawe jalo, .
“Ke ne ke tshaba gore e ka nna ya gobatsa boboko;
Mme, jaanong ka ke tlhomamisegile sentle gore ga ke na epe,
Ebu, ke dira jalo gangwe le gape.”

Mosha yoo o ne a re: “O tsofetse, jaaka ke umakile pelenyana, .
Mme ba godile thata ka tsela e e sa tlwaelegang;
Le fa go ntse jalo o ne wa retologa ka morago kwa kgorong—
Rapela, lebaka la seo ke eng?”

“Mo bosheng jwa me,” ga rialo motlhalefi, fa a ntse a tshikinya diloko tsa gagwe tse ditala, .
“Ke ne ke boloka ditokololo tsa me tsotlhe di le bonolo thata .
Ka go dirisa setlolo seno—sheleng e le nngwe lebokoso—
Ntetle go go rekisetsa banyalani?”

Mosha yoo o ne a re: “O tsofetse, mme ditlhaa tsa gago di bokoa thata .
Go sengwe le sengwe se se thata go feta suet;
Le fa go ntse jalo o feditse ganse, ka marapo le molomo—
Rapela, o kgonne jang go e dira?”

Rraagwe o ne a re: “Mo bosheng jwa me, ke ne ka tsena mo molaong, .
Mme ke ne ke ngangisana ka kgetsi nngwe le nngwe le mosadi wa me;
Mme maatla a mesifa, a a neng a a naya motlhagare wa me, .
E nnile botshelo jwa me jotlhe.”

Mosha yono o ne a re: “O tsofetse, motho o ne a ka se akanye .
Gore leitlho la gago le ne le tlhomame jaaka gale;
Le fa go ntse jalo o ne o lekalekanya tlhapi ya eel mo ntlheng ya nko ya gago—
Ke eng se se dirileng gore o nne botlhale jaana?”

“Ke arabile dipotso di le tharo, mme seo se lekane,”
Ga rialo rraagwe; “o seka wa ipha diphefo!
A o akanya gore nka reetsa dilo tse di ntseng jalo letsatsi lotlhe?
Tloga, kgotsa ke tla go raga ka ditepisi!”

“Seo ga se a buiwa sentle,” ga rialo Seboko.

“Ga go a siama gotlhelele , ke a tshoga,” ga rialo Alice, a tshogile; “mafoko mangwe a fetotswe.”

“Go phoso go tswa kwa tshimologong go ya kwa bokhutlong,” ga rialo Seboko ka tshwetso, mme ga nna tidimalo metsotso e le mmalwa.

Seboko e ne e le sone sa ntlha go bua.

“O batla go nna bogolo jo bo kana kang?” e ne ya botsa.

“Oh, ga ke tlhomamisege ka bogolo,” Alice o ne a araba jalo ka bonako; “ke mongwe fela yo o sa rateng go fetoga gantsi jaana, o a itse.”

“ Ga ke itse,” ga rialo Seboko.

Alice ga a ka a bua sepe: ga a ise a ke a ganediwe thata jaana mo botshelong jwa gagwe, mme o ne a ikutlwa gore o a latlhegelwa ke bogale.

“A o kgotsofetse jaanong?” Ga rialo Seboko.

“Ebu, nka rata go nna mogolonyana , rra, fa o ka se nne le bothata,” ga rialo Alice: “disentimetara di le tharo ke bogodimo jo bo utlwisang botlhoko thata go nna jone.”

“Ke bogodimo jo bo siameng thata eleruri!” ga rialo Seboko se galefile, se ikgodisa se tlhamaletse fa se ntse se bua (se ne se le bogodimo jwa disentimetara di le tharo fela).

“Mme ga ke a tlwaela!” ga kopa Alice yo o humanegileng ka lentswe le le utlwisang botlhoko. Mme a akanya ka ene, “Ke eletsa e kete dibopiwa di ka bo di sa kgopisege bonolo jaana!”

“O tla e tlwaela fa nako e ntse e tsamaya,” ga rialo Seboko; mme ya tsenya hookah mo molomong wa yone mme ya simolola go goga gape.

Mo lekgetlong leno Alice o ne a leta ka bopelotelele go fitlha e tlhopha go bua gape. Mo motsotsong kgotsa metsotso e mebedi Seboko sa ntsha hookah mo molomong wa sone mme sa tlhotlhora gangwe kgotsa gabedi, mme sa itlhotlhora. Mme ya fologa mo mushroom, mme ya gagaba mo bojannyeng, e tshwaela fela fa e ntse e tsamaya, “Letlhakore le lengwe le tla go dira gore o nne moleele, mme letlhakore le lengwe le tla dira gore o nne mokhutshwane.”

“Letlhakore le lengwe la eng? Letlhakore le lengwe la eng? ” Alice o ne a akanya jalo mo pelong.

“Ka ga mushroom,” ga rialo Seboko, fela jaaka e kete o ne a se boditse kwa godimo; mme mo motsotsong o mongwe e ne e sa tlhole e bonala.

Alice o ne a nna a lebile mushroom ka motsotso a akantse, a leka go bona gore ke matlhakore afe a mabedi a one; mme ka e ne e le kgolokwe ka botlalo, o ne a fitlhela seno e le potso e e thata tota. Le fa go ntse jalo, kgabagare o ne a otlolla matsogo a gagwe go e dikologa go ya bokgakaleng jo ba neng ba tla ya ka jone, mme a roba bonnye jwa ntlha ka seatla sengwe le sengwe.

“Mme jaanong ke efe e e leng efe?” a bua jalo mo pelong, mme a tlhotlhora go le gonnye karolo ya letsogo le le jang go leka phelelo: motsotso o o latelang o ne a utlwa go itewa ka maatla ka fa tlase ga seledu sa gagwe: go ne go mo iteile mo leotong!

O ne a tshositswe thata ke phetogo eno e e neng ya direga ka tshoganyetso fela, mme o ne a ikutlwa gore ga go na nako e a ka e latlhegang, ka a ne a ngotlega ka bonako; ka jalo o ne a simolola go bereka ka yone nako eo go ja karolo e nngwe. Seledu sa gagwe se ne se gateletswe thata mo leotong la gagwe, mo e leng gore go ne go se na sebaka sa go bula molomo wa gagwe; mme o ne a dira jalo kgabagare, mme a kgona go kometsa karolo ya letsogo la molema.

* * * * * * *

* * * * * *

* * * * * * *

“Tla, tlhogo ya me e gololesegile kgabagare!” ga rialo Alice ka lentswe la boitumelo, le le neng la fetoga go nna letshogo mo motsotsong o mongwe, fa a fitlhela gore magetla a gagwe ga a bonwe gope: se a neng a se bona fela, fa a leba kwa tlase, e ne e le boleele jo bogolo jwa molala, jo bo neng bo lebega bo tlhatloga jaaka tlhaka go tswa mo lewatleng la matlhare a matala a a neng a le kgakala kwa tlase ga gagwe.

“Dilo tseo tsotlhe tse ditala e ka nna eng?” ” Alice o boletse. “Mme magetla a me a ile kae? Mme ao, diatla tsa me tse di humanegileng, go tla jang gore ke se ka ka go bona?” O ne a di tsamaisa fa a ntse a bua, mme go ne go sa lebege go na le phelelo epe e e latelang, fa e se go roroma go sekae mo gare ga matlhare a matala a a kgakala.

Ka go ne go lebega go sena tshono ya go isa diatla tsa gagwe kwa godimo kwa tlhogong, o ne a leka go isa tlhogo ya gagwe kwa tlase kwa go tsone, mme a itumelela go fitlhela gore molala wa gagwe o ne o tla obega bonolo go ya kwa ntlheng epe fela, jaaka noga. O ne a sa tswa go atlega go e koba go ya kwa tlase go nna zigzag e e kgatlhang, mme o ne a tlile go thuma mo gare ga matlhare, a a neng a fitlhela e se sepe fa e se ditlhoa tsa ditlhare tse a neng a tlhola a tlhola a tlhola ka fa tlase ga tsone, fa go lela go go bogale go dira gore a boe morago ka bonako: leeba le legolo le ne le fofetse mo sefatlhegong sa gagwe ka dikgoka, mme le ne le mo itaya ka dikgoka.

“Noga!” ga goa Lephoi.

“ Ga ke noga!” Ga rialo Alice a galefile. “Ntlogeleng!”

“Noga, ke bua gape!” ga boeletsa Lephoi, mme ka lentswe le le kokobeditsweng thata, mme la oketsa ka mofuta mongwe wa go lela, “Ke lekile ka ditsela tsotlhe, mme go lebega go se na sepe se se ba tshwanelang!”

Alice o ne a re: “Ga ke itse le e seng gore o bua ka eng.”

“Ke lekile medi ya ditlhare, mme ke lekile mabopo, mme ke lekile dithako,” Lephoi la tswelela, le sa mo tlhokomele; “mme dinoga tseo! Ga go na go di itumedisa!”

Alice o ne a ntse a tlhakana tlhogo le go feta, mme o ne a akanya gore ga go na mosola go bua sepe se sengwe go fitlha Lephoi le fetsa.

“E kete e ne e se mathata a a lekaneng go thuthupa mae,” ga rialo Lephoi; “mme ke tshwanetse go nna ke disitse dinoga bosigo le motshegare! Ebu, ga ke ise ke robale le fa e le go tshoba matlho mo dibekeng tse tharo tseno!”

Alice yo o neng a simolola go bona bokao jwa yone o ne a re: “Ke maswabi tota go bo o ile wa tena.”

“Mme fela jaaka ke ne ke tsere setlhare se se kwa godimo go gaisa tsotlhe mo sekgweng,” ga tswelela Lephoi, le tsholetsa lentswe la lone go goa, “mme fela jaaka ke ne ke akanya gore ke tshwanetse go gololesega mo go tsone kwa bofelong, di tshwanetse tsa tlhoka go tla di tlhotlhorega go tswa kwa loaping! Ugh, Noga!”

“Mme nna ga ke noga, ke a go bolelela!” ” Alice o boletse. “Ke—ke—”

"Mme! O mang ?" Ga rialo Lephoi. “Ke a bona gore o leka go itlhamela sengwe!”

“Ke—ke mosetsanyana,” ga rialo Alice, go na le moo a belaela, fa a gakologelwa palo ya diphetogo tse a neng a feta mo go tsone letsatsi leo.

“Kgang e e ka tswang e le teng eleruri!” Ga bua Lephoi ka lentswe la lenyatso le le tseneletseng. "Ke bone basetsanyana ba le bantsi ba ba siameng mo nakong ya me, mme ga go ise go ke go nne le ope yo o nang le molala o o ntseng jalo! Nnyaa, nnyaa! O noga; mme ga go na mosola go e ganela. Ke akanya gore o tla bo o mpolelela morago ga moo gore ga o ise o ko o latswe lee!"

“Ke latswitse mae, eleruri,” ga rialo Alice, yo e neng e le ngwana yo o buang boammaaruri thata; “mme basetsanyana ba ja mae fela jaaka dinoga, o a itse.”

“Ga ke dumele,” ga rialo Lephoi; “mme fa ba dira jalo, ke eng fa e le mofuta wa noga, ke sone fela se nka se buang.”

Se e ne e le kakanyo e ntšhwa mo go Alice, mo e leng gore o ne a didimala gotlhelele motsotso kgotsa metsotso e mebedi, se se neng sa naya Lephoi tshono ya go oketsa jaana, “O batla mae, ke itse seo sentle; mme go botlhokwa eng mo go nna gore a o mosetsanyana kgotsa o noga?”

“Go botlhokwa thata mo go nna ,” Alice o ne a rialo a itlhaganelela; “mme ga ke batle mae, jaaka go diragala; mme fa ke ne ke le jalo, ke ne ke sa tshwanela go batla a gago : ga ke a rate a le tala.”

“Ebu, tsamaya, he!” Ga rialo Lephoi ka lentswe le le tlhomolang pelo, fa le ntse le tlhomama gape mo sentlhageng sa lone. Alice o ne a khubama mo gare ga ditlhare ka fa a neng a ka kgona ka teng, ka gonne molala wa gagwe o ne o nna o tlhakatlhakane mo gare ga dikala, mme nako le nako o ne a tshwanelwa ke go ema le go o phutholola. Morago ga nako o ne a gakologelwa gore o ne a santse a tshwere dikarolwana tsa mushroom mo diatleng tsa gagwe, mme a simolola go dira ka kelotlhoko thata, a tlhotlhora pele mo go e nngwe mme morago a tlhotlhora mo go e nngwe, mme a gola ka dinako tse dingwe a nna moleele mme ka dinako tse dingwe a nna mokhutshwane, go fitlhela a atlega go itlisa kwa bogodimong jwa gagwe jo bo tlwaelegileng.

Go ne go setse go le kgale a sa tlhole a le sepe se se gaufi le bogolo jo bo siameng, mo e leng gore go ne go utlwala go sa tlwaelega tota kwa tshimologong; mme o ne a e tlwaela mo metsotsong e se kae fela, mme a simolola go bua ka boene, jaaka gale. “Tlang, go na le halofo ya leano la me e e dirilweng jaanong! A bo diphetogo tseno tsotlhe di gakgamatsa jang ne! Ga ke nke ke tlhomamisega gore ke tlile go nna eng, go tswa mo motsotsong o le mongwe go ya kwa go o mongwe! Le fa go ntse jalo, ke boetse kwa bogolong jwa me jo bo siameng: selo se se latelang ke gore, go tsena mo tshingwaneng eo e ntle—seo se tshwanetse go dirwa jang, ke a ipotsa?” Fa a ntse a bua jalo, o ne a tla ka tshoganetso mo lefelong le le bulegileng, le le nang le ntlonyana mo go lone e e ka nnang dikgato di le nne bogodimo. Alice o ne a akanya jaana: “Le fa e le mang yo o nnang koo, ga go kitla go dira gore ba tle ka bogolo jono : ebu, ke tshwanetse go ba tshosa gore ba se ka ba tlhaloganya dilo!” Ka jalo o ne a simolola go tlhotlhora mo letsogong le le jang gape, mme a se ka a itlhaganelela go atamela ntlo go fitlha a iphokotsa go nna bogodimo jwa disentimetara di le robongwe.
