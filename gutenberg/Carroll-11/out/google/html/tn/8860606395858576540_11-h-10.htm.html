KGAOLO X.
Di-Lobster

Mock Turtle a fegelwa kwa teng, mme a goga mokwatla wa flapper e le nngwe go kgabaganya matlho a gagwe. A leba Alice, mme a leka go bua, mme ka motsotso kgotsa dilelo tse pedi tsa hupetsa lentswe la gagwe. “Go tshwana le fa a ne a na le lerapo mo kgokgotsong,” ga rialo Gryphon: mme ya simolola go dira e mo tshikinya le go mo itaya mo mokwatleng. Kgabagare Mock Turtle a boela a utlwa lentswe la gagwe, mme, ka dikeledi di elela mo marameng a gagwe, a tswelela gape:—

“O ka tswa o sa tshela thata ka fa tlase ga lewatle—” (“Ga ke ise,” ga rialo Alice)—“mme gongwe ga o ise o ko o itsisiwe le lobster—” (Alice o ne a simolola go re “Ke kile ka latswa—” mme a itlhatlhoba ka bonako, mme a re “Nnyaa, le ka motlha”) “—ka jalo ga o ka ke wa itse sepe gore Quadrille e monate jang!”

Alice o ne a re: “Nnyaa, eleruri. “Ke tantshe ya mofuta ofe?”

“Ke ka ntlha yang,” ga rialo Gryphon, “o bopa pele mola go bapa le lebopo la lewatle—”

“Mela e mebedi!” ga goa Khudu ya Mock. “Di-seal, dikhudu, di-salmon, jalo le jalo; go tswa foo, fa o sena go tlosa di-jelly-fish tsotlhe mo tseleng—”

“ Seo ka kakaretso se tsaya nako,” ga kgaola Gryphon.

“—o gatela pele gabedi—”

“Mongwe le mongwe a na le lobster e le molekane!” ga goa Kerifone.

“Ee,” Mock Turtle o ne a rialo: “gatela pele gabedi, o beele balekane—”

“—fetola di-lobster, mme o role tiro ka tatelano e e tshwanang,” Gryphon o ne a tswelela jalo.

“Mme, o a itse,” Khudu ya Motshego ya tswelela, “o latlhela—”

“Di-lobster!” ga goa Gryphon, a bofilwe mo moweng.

“—kgakala kwa ntle ga lewatle ka moo o ka kgonang ka teng—”

“Thuma morago ga bone!” ga goa Griphon.

“Retologa mo lewatleng!” ga goa Khudu ya Mock, e ntse e tlhotlhora ka bogale.

“Fetola di-lobster gape!” ga goa Gryphon ka lentswe le le kwa godimo.

“Go boela kwa lefatsheng gape, mme ke sone sotlhe setshwantsho sa ntlha,” ga rialo Khudu ya Mock, ka tshoganyetso a folosa lentswe la gagwe; mme dibopuwa tse pedi, tse neng di ntse di tlolatlola jwalo ka dintho tse tlwaelehileng nako ena kaofela, tsa dula fatshe hape ka kutlobotlhoko e kgolo le ka tidimalo, mme tsa sheba Alice.

“E tshwanetse ya bo e le tantshe e ntle thata,” ga rialo Alice a tshogile.

“A o ka rata go bona bonnye jwa yone?” Ga rialo Khudu ya Mock.

Alice o ne a re: “Eleruri go le gontsi thata.

“Tlang, a re lekeng palo ya ntlha!” ga rialo Khudu ya Motsogo go Gryphon. "Re ka dira ntle le di-lobster, o a itse. Ke efe e e tla opelang?"

“Oho, o a bina,” ha rialo Gryphon. “Ke lebetse mantswe.”

Ka jalo ba ne ba simolola go bina ka masisi go dikologa Alice, nako le nako ba gata menwana ya gagwe fa ba feta gaufi thata, mme ba tshikinya maoto a bone a kwa pele go tshwaya nako, fa Mock Turtle e ne e opela seno, ka iketlo thata le ka kutlobotlhoko:—

“A o tla tsamaya ka lobelonyana?” ga rialo mosweu go kgopa.
“Go na le tshephe e e gaufi ka fa morago ga rona, mme e gata mo mogatleng wa me.

“Ke a leboga, ke tantshe e e kgatlhang thata go e lebelela,” ga rialo Alice, a ikutlwa a itumetse thata gore kgabagare e fedile: “mme ke dira jalo ke rata pina eo ya go batla go itse ka di- whiting!”

“Oh, mabapi le bosweu,” ga rialo Khudu ya Mock, “bone—o ba bone, ee?”

“Ee,” ga rialo Alice, “Ke ba bone gantsi kwa dinn—” a itlhatlhoba ka bonako.

“Ga ke itse gore Dinn a ka tswa a le kae,” ga rialo Mock Turtle, “mme fa e le gore o kile wa di bona gantsi jaana, gone ke boammaaruri gore o itse gore di ntse jang.”

“Ke dumela jalo,” Alice o ne a araba jalo a akantse. “Di na le megatla ya tsone mo melomong ya tsone—mme di tletse mo makungwana otlhe.”

“O fositse ka mafofane,” ga rialo Khudu ya Motshegiso: “makhudu a ne a tla tlhatswa otlhe mo lewatleng. Mme di na le megatla ya tsone mo melomong ya tsone; mme lebaka ke gore—” fano Khudu ya Motshegiso e ne ya tlhotlhora mme ya tswala matlho.—“Mmolelele ka lebaka le tsotlhe tseo,” a raya Gryphon.

"Lebaka ke gore," ga rialo Gryphon, "gore di ne di tla tsamaya le di-lobster go ya go bina. Ka jalo di ne tsa latlhelwa kwa ntle kwa lewatleng. Ka jalo di ne tsa tshwanelwa ke go wela kgakala. Ka jalo di ne tsa tsenya megatla ya tsone ka bonako mo melomong ya tsone. Ka jalo di ne di sa kgone go di ntsha gape. Ke gone fela."

Alice o ne a re: “Ke a leboga, e a kgatlha tota. Ga ke ise ke tsamaye ke itse go le gontsi jaana ka whiting.”

“Ke ka go bolelela go feta foo, fa o rata,” ga rialo Gryphon. “A o itse gore ke eng fa e bidiwa whiting?”

Alice o ne a re: “Ga ke ise ke tsamaye ke akanye ka gone. "Goreng?"

“ E dira ditlhako le ditlhako ,” Gryphon o ne a araba ka masisi thata.

Alice o ne a tlhakane tlhogo tota. “A diboots le ditlhako!” A boeletsa ka lentswe le le gakgamatsang.

“Ee, ditlhako tsa gago di dirilwe ka eng?” Ga rialo Kerifone. “Ke raya gore ke eng se se dirang gore di phatsime jaana?”

Alice a ba leba fa fatshe, mme a akanyetsa go sekae pele a neela karabo ya gagwe. “Ba feditse ka go ntshofatsa, ke a dumela.”

"Ditlhako le ditlhako ka fa tlase ga lewatle," Gryphon a tswelela ka lentswe le le tseneletseng, "di dirilwe ka go sweufatsa. Jaanong o a itse."

“Mme di dirilwe ka eng?” Alice o ne a botsa ka lentswe le le bontshang go batla go itse thata.

“Di- sole le di- eel, e le ruri,” Gryphon o ne a araba jalo a sa fele pelo: “shirimpe epe fela e ka bo e go boleletse jalo.”

“Fa nkabo ke le whiting,” ga rialo Alice, yo dikakanyo tsa gagwe di neng di sa ntse di taboga ka pina, “nkabo ke raya porpoise ke re, ‘Ikgogone, tsweetswee: ga re batle gore o nne le rona!’”

“Ba ne ba patelesega go nna le ene,” Mock Turtle o ne a rialo: “ga go na tlhapi epe e e botlhale e e neng e ka ya gope fa e se na porpoise.”

“A tota go ne go ka se nne jalo?” Ga rialo Alice ka lentswe la go gakgamala thata.

“Ee, nnyaa,” ga rialo Khudu ya Mock: “ke eng fa, fa tlhapi e ka tla kwa go nna , mme ya mpolelela gore e ya loetong, ke tshwanetse go re ‘Ka porpoise efe?’”

“A ga o reye ‘boikaelelo’?” ” Alice o boletse.

“Ke raya se ke se buang,” Mock Turtle a araba ka lentswe le le kgopisitsweng. Mme Gryphon a eketsa “Tla, a re utlwe tse ding tsa diphihlelo tsa hao .”

“Ke ne ke ka go bolelela ditiragalo tsa me tse di sa tlwaelegang—go simolola mo mosong ono,” Alice o ne a rialo a tshogile go sekae: “mme ga go thuse sepe go boela kwa maabaneng, ka gonne ke ne ke le motho yo o farologaneng ka nako eo.”

“Tlhalosa tsotlhe tseo,” ga rialo Khudu ya Mock.

“Nnyaa, nnyaa! Ditiragalo tse di sa tlwaelegang pele,” ga rialo Gryphon ka lentswe le le sa feleng pelo: “ditlhaloso di tsaya nako e e boitshegang jaana.”

Ka jalo Alice o ne a simolola go ba bolelela ditiragalo tsa gagwe go tloga ka nako ya fa a ne a bona Mmutla o Mosweu la ntlha. O ne a tshogile go se kae ka gone kwa tshimologong fela, dibopiwa tse pedi di ne tsa mo atamela thata, e le nngwe mo letlhakoreng lengwe le lengwe, mme tsa bula matlho le melomo ya tsone thata , mme o ne a nna le sebete fa a ntse a tswelela. Bareetsi ba gagwe ba ne ba didimetse ka botlalo go fitlhela a fitlha kwa karolong e e neng e bua ka go boeletsa ga gagwe “ O tsofetse, Rre William ,” go Seboko, le mafoko otlhe a ne a tla ka go farologana, mme morago ga moo Khudu ya Motshegi ya hema ka boleele, mme ya re “Seo se batla go itse thata.”

“Gotlhe go ka ga go batla go itse ka fa go ka kgonegang ka teng,” ga rialo Gryphon.

“Gotlhe go ne ga tla ka tsela e e farologaneng!” Khudu ya Mock a boeletsa a akantse. "Ke ka rata go mo utlwa a leka go boeletsa sengwe jaanong. Mmolelele gore a simolole." A leba Gryphon jaaka e kete o akanya gore e na le mofuta mongwe wa taolo mo go Alice.

“Ema o boeletse '' Ke lentswe la setshwakga ,'” ga rialo Gryphon.

“A bo dibopiwa di laela motho jang ne, mme di dira gore motho a boeletse dithuto!” ga akanya Alice; “Ke ka nna ka bo ke le kwa sekolong ka nako e le nngwe fela.” Le fa go ntse jalo, o ne a ema, mme a simolola go e boeletsa, mme tlhogo ya gagwe e ne e tletse ka Lobster Quadrille, mo e leng gore o ne a sa itse sentle gore o reng, mme mafoko a ne a tla a sa tlwaelega tota:—
