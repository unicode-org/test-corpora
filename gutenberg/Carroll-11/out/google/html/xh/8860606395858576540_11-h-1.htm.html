ISAHLUKO I.
Phantsi koMngxunya woMvundla

UAlice wayesele eqalisa ukudinwa kukuhlala nodadewabo ebhankini, engenanto yakwenza: kanye okanye kabini wakroba kwincwadi eyayifundwa ngudade wabo, kodwa ingenayo imifanekiso okanye iincoko kuyo, “kwaye kusetyenziswa ntoni incwadi,” wacinga njalo uAlice “ngaphandle kwemifanekiso okanye iincoko?”

Ngoko wayecinga ngengqondo yakhe (kanye njengoko wayenako, kuba imini eshushu yamenza waziva elele kakhulu kwaye usisidenge), ukuba ubumnandi bokwenza i-daisy-chain yayiza kuba yingxaki yokuvuka kunye nokuchola ama-daisies, xa ngequbuliso uMvundla oMhlophe onamehlo apinki wabaleka kufuphi naye.

Kwakungekho nto iphawuleka kakhulu kuloo nto; engazange kananjalo no-Alice acinge ukuba ikude kakhulu ukuva uMvundla usithi, “Owu sithandwa! (wathi akucinga emva koko, kwathi qatha kuye ukuba ngewayekhe amangale koku, kodwa ngelo xesha konke kwabonakala kungokwemvelo); kodwa wathi uMvundla xa eneneni wakhupha iwotshi epokothweni yebhatyi , wayijonga, waza wakhawuleza ukuya phambili, u-Alice wema ngeenyawo, kuba kwadanyaza engqondweni yakhe ukuba wayengazange awubone ngaphambili umvundla unepokotho yesinqe, okanye iwotshi ukuba uyikhuphe, yaye evutha ngokufun’ ukwazi, wabaleka wanqumla ebaleni ukuze awubone. umvundla-umngxuma phantsi kothango.

Ngomnye umzuzu wehla u-Alice emva kwayo, akazange nakanye acinge ukuba uza kuphinda aphume njani emhlabeni.

Umngxuma womvundla waya ngqo phambili njengetonela ngandlel’ ithile, emva koko wantywila phantsi ngesiquphe, kangangokuba u-Alice wayengenalo nomzuzu wokucinga ngokuzinqanda ngaphambi kokuba azifumane esiwa phantsi kwequla elinzulu kakhulu.

Mhlawumbi iqula lalinzulu kakhulu, okanye wawa ngokucothayo, kuba wayenexesha elininzi njengoko wayesihla esiya kukhangela kulo nokuba yintoni eyayiza kwenzeka emva koko. Kuqala, wazama ukujonga ezantsi afumanise ukuba uza kuba yintoni na, kodwa kwakumnyama kakhulu ukuba angaboni nto; emva koko wajonga emacaleni equla, waqaphela ukuba azele ziikhabhathi kunye neeshelufu zeencwadi; apha naphaya wabona iimephu nemifanekiso ixhonywe kwizikhonkwane. Wathula ingqayi kwenye yeeshelufu njengokuba edlula; yayibhalwe “ORANGE MARMALADE”, kodwa ngokudana kwakhe yayingenanto: wayengathandi ukuwisa ingqayi kuba esoyika ukubulala umntu ngaphantsi, ngoko ke ukwazile ukuyibeka kwenye yeekhabhathi njengoko wayewela ngaphaya kwayo.

“Njalo!” wacinga njalo uAlice wathi: “Emva kokuwa ngolu hlobo, andiyi kucinga nto ngokuwa phantsi kwezinyuko! (Ekunokwenzeka ukuba yayiyinyani.)

Phantsi, phantsi, phantsi. Ngaba ukuwa kwakungayi kuze kuphele? “Ndiyazibuza ukuba zingaphi iimayile esele ndiwile ngeli xesha?” watsho ekhwaza. “Kumele ukuba ndisondele ndaweni ithile kumbindi womhlaba. Makhe ndibone: oko bekuya kuba ngamawaka amane eekhilomitha ukuhla, ndiyacinga—” (uyabona, uAlice wayefunde izinto ezininzi ezilolu hlobo kwizifundo zakhe kwigumbi lesikolo, yaye nangona eli yayingelothuba lihle kakhulu lokubonisa ulwazi analo, njengoko kwakungekho mntu wokumamela, sekunjalo yayisisenzo esihle ukuyichaza le nto) “—ewe, ndizibuza ukuba ungakanani na loo mgama—kodwa i<em>Longitude yayingeyomfuneko? (U-Alice wayengazi ukuba iLatitude yayiyintoni, okanye i-Longitude, kodwa wayecinga ukuba angamagama amnandi anokuwathetha.)

Ngoku waqala kwakhona. Ndiyazibuza ukuba ndiya kuwa kanye emhlabeni! Hayi indlela ekuya kubonakala kuhlekisa ngayo ukuphuma phakathi kwabantu abahamba ngeentloko zabo bejonge phantsi! I-Antipathies, ndiyacinga- "(wayevuya kakhulu ukuba kwakungekho mntu uphulaphule, ngeli xesha, njengoko kwakungavakali kwaphela igama elifanelekileyo) "-kodwa kuya kufuneka ndibabuze ukuba yintoni igama lelizwe, uyazi. Nceda, Ma'am okanye i-Australia, le yiNew Zealand?" (yaye wazama ukushwaqa njengoko wayethetha—ethetha amazwi ashwabeneyo njengoko uwela emoyeni! Ngaba ucinga ukuba unokuwulawula?) “Yaye yenjani intombazana engazi nto eya kundicinga ngokundibuza yona!

Phantsi, phantsi, phantsi. Kwakungekho enye into yokwenza, ngoko uAlice waqalisa ukuthetha kwakhona. “UDinah uzakundikhumbula kakhulu ngobu busuku, ndingacinga njalo!” (uDina wayeyikati.) “Ndiyathemba ukuba baya kuyikhumbula isosi yakhe yobisi ngexesha leti. Dinah sithandwa sam! Akwaba ubulapha nam apha ezantsi! Akukho zimpuku emoyeni ndiyoyika, kodwa usenokulibamba ilulwane, yaye oko kufana nempuku kakhulu, uyazi. Kodwa ingaba iikati ziyawadla amalulwane, ndiyazibuza?” Kwaye apha u-Alice waqala ukozela ngakumbi, kwaye waqhubeka ethetha yedwa, ngendlela ephuphayo, "Ngaba iikati ziyawadla amalulwane? Ngaba iikati zidla amalulwane?" kwaye ngamanye amaxesha, "Ngaba amalulwane atya iikati?" kuba, uyabona, njengoko wayengenakuwuphendula nawuphi na umbuzo, kwakungakhathaliseki kangako ukuba wayewubeka ngaphi na. Waziva eyozela, yaye wayesandul’ ukuphupha ehamba noDina ebambene ngesandla, esithi kuye ngokunyanisekileyo, “Ngoku, Dina, thetha inyaniso, wakha wadla ilulwane na? xa ngequbuliso, kubetha! qhwaba! Wehla phezu kwemfumba yeenkuni namagqabi omileyo, kwaye ukuwa kwakuphela.

U-Alice akonzakanga noko, watsiba wema ezinyaweni zakhe emzuzwini: wajonga phezulu, kodwa konke kwakumnyama ngaphezulu; phambi kwakhe kwakukho enye ipaseji ende, kwaye uMvundla oMhlophe wawusabonakala, ungxamile ukuhla ngayo. Kwakungekho nomzuzu wokulahleka: wahamba u-Alice njengomoya, kwaye kwafika ixesha lokuyiva isithi, xa ijika ikona, "Owu iindlebe zam kunye namabhovu, kuhlwile kangakanani!" Wayekufuphi emva kwawo xa ejika ikona, kodwa uMvundla wayengasabonwa: wazibona sele ekwiholo ende, ephantsi, eyayikhanyiswe zizibane ezijinga eluphahleni.

Kwakukho iingcango macala onke kwiholo, kodwa zonke zazitshixiwe; kwaye xa u-Alice wayesehla ezantsi kwelinye icala enyuka kwelinye, ezama ucango ngalunye, wahamba kalusizi esihla embindini, ecinga ukuba uya kuphinda aphume njani kwakhona.

Ngequbuliso weza phezu kwetafile encinci enemilenze emithathu, yonke yenziwe ngeglasi eqinileyo; kwakungekho nto kuyo ngaphandle kwesitshixo esincinci segolide, kwaye ingcinga yokuqala ka-Alice yayikukuba inokuba yeyomnye weminyango yeholo; kodwa, hayi! mhlawumbi izitshixo zazinkulu kakhulu, okanye isitshixo sasincinane kakhulu, kodwa nangaliphi na izinga sasingayi kuvula nanye kuzo. Nangona kunjalo, kumjikelo wesibini, wafika kumkhusane ophantsi awayengazange awuqaphele ngaphambili, kwaye emva kwawo kwakukho ucango oluncinane olumalunga nee-intshi ezilishumi elinesihlanu ukuphakama: wazama isitshixo esincinane segolide kwisitshixo, waza ngovuyo lwakhe olukhulu wasifaka!

U-Alice wavula ucango wafumanisa ukuba ukhokelela kwipaseji encinane, engenkulu kangako kunomngxuma weempuku: waguqa wajonga ecaleni kwendlela eya kweyona gadi ithandekayo wakha wayibona. Indlela awayekulangazelela ngayo ukuphuma kuloo holo imnyama, azulazule phakathi kwezo bhedi zineentyatyambo eziqaqambileyo naloo mithombo ipholileyo, kodwa wayengenako nokungena intloko yakhe emnyango; UAlice osweleyo wacinga oku: “Kwaye nokuba intloko yam ibinokudlula, ibingayi kuba luncedo kangako ngaphandle kwamagxa am.” Owu, hayi indlela ebendithanda ngayo ukuba ndivale umlomo njengeteleskopu! Kuba, uyabona, zininzi izinto eziphume endleleni ezenzekileyo mva nje, kangangokuba uAlice waqala ukucinga ukuba zimbalwa kakhulu izinto ezingenakwenzeka ngokwenene.

Kwakubonakala kungekho ncedo lokulinda ngocango oluncinane, ngoko wabuyela etafileni, enethemba lokuba uya kufumana esinye isitshixo kuyo, okanye nangayiphi na indlela incwadi yemithetho yokuvala abantu njengeteleskopu: ngeli xesha wafumana ibhotile encinane kuyo, (“eyayingekho ngokuqinisekileyo ngaphambili,” watsho uAlice,) kwaye entanyeni yebhotile kwakukho ileyibhile yephepha, enombhalo omhle othi “DRINK” ngombhalo omhle othi “DRINK”.

Kwakulungile ukuthi “Sela,” kodwa uAlice omncinane osisilumko wayengazukuyenza loo nto ngokungxama. “Hayi, ndiza kukhangela kuqala,” watsho, “ndize ndibone ukuba iphawulwe ' yityhefu ' okanye hayi”; kuba wayefunde iimbali ezimbalwa ezintle malunga nabantwana abaye batshiswa, kwaye badliwa ngamarhamncwa kunye nezinye izinto ezingathandekiyo, zonke ngenxa yokuba babengayi kukhumbula imithetho elula ababeyifundiswe ngabahlobo babo: njengokuba, ukuba i-poker ebomvu iya kukutshisa ukuba uyibambe ixesha elide; kwaye ukuba usike umnwe wakho ngokunzulu kakhulu ngemela, ngokuqhelekileyo uyopha; kwaye akazange alibale ukuba, ukuba usela kakhulu kwibhotile ephawulwe "ityhefu," ngokuqinisekileyo ngokuqinisekileyo ukungavumelani nawe, ngokukhawuleza okanye kamva.

Nangona kunjalo, le bhotile yayingaphawulwanga “ityhefu,” ngoko ke u-Alice wazama ukuyingcamla, waza wayifumana imnandi kakhulu, (eneneni, yayinohlobo lwencasa exutyiweyo yecherry-tart, custard, pine-apple, turkey egcadiweyo, toffee, kunye nebhotolo eshushu ethowuniweyo), wayigqiba ngokukhawuleza.

“Enjani yona imvakalelo enomdla!” watsho uAlice; "Kufuneka ndivale umlomo njengetelescope."

Kwaye kwaba njalo ngenene: ngoku wayezii-intshi ezilishumi kuphela ukuphakama, kwaye ubuso bakhe bakhazimla kwingcinga yokuba ngoku wayelingana nocango oluncinane ukuya kula myezo uthandekayo. Okokuqala, nangona kunjalo, walinda imizuzu embalwa ukuze abone ukuba wayeza kucutheka na ngakumbi: waziva enoloyiko oluncinane malunga noku; “Kuba inokuphela, uyazi,” watsho u-Alice ezithethela, “ekuphumeni kwam ngokupheleleyo, njengekhandlela. Ndiyazibuza ukuba bendifanele ukuba njani ngoko?” Kwaye wazama ukuphupha ukuba linjani ilangatye lekhandlela emva kokuba ikhandlela licinyiwe, kuba wayengakhumbuli ukuba wakha wayibona into enjalo.

Emva kwexesha elithile, efumanisa ukuba akukho nto yenzekayo, wagqiba kwelokuba angene kwaoko emyezweni; kodwa, maye ngoAlice olihlwempu! xa efika emnyango, wafumanisa ukuba ulibele isitshixo esincinane segolide, wathi xa ebuyela etafileni ukuze afune kuso, wafumanisa ukuba akanakukwazi ukufikelela kulo: wayesibona ngokucacileyo ngeglasi, waza wazama ngako konke okusemandleni akhe ukunyuka omnye wemilenze yetafile, kodwa umtyibilizi kakhulu; yathi yakuba idiniwe kukuzama, ihlwempu lahlala phantsi lalila.

“Yiza, akuncedi nto ukulila ngolo hlobo!” watsho uAlice kuye, kancinci kancinci; “Ndikucebisa ukuba uyeke kulo mzuzu!” Ngokuqhelekileyo wayezinika icebiso elihle kakhulu, (nangona wayengafane alilandele), yaye maxa wambi wayezithethisa kanobom ngokokude akhuphe iinyembezi emehlweni akhe; kwaye ukhe wakhumbula ezama ukuvala iindlebe zakhe ngokuziqhatha kumdlalo wecroquet awayezidlalela, kuba lo mntwana unomdla wayekuthanda kakhulu ukuzenza abantu ababini. UAlice olihlwempu wathi: “Kodwa akuncedi nto ngoku, ukuzenza abantu ababini !

Kungekudala iliso lakhe lawela kwibhokisi yeglasi encinane ephantsi kwetafile: wayivula, waza wafumana kuyo ikhekhe elincinci kakhulu, apho amagama athi "NDIDLA" abhalwe kakuhle kwi-currants. “Ewe, ndiza kuyitya,” watsho u-Alice, “kwaye ukuba indenza ndikhule ngakumbi, ndingafikelela kwisitshixo; kwaye ukuba indenza ndikhule mncinci, ndingarhubuluza phantsi kocango; ke nangayiphi na indlela ndiya kungena egadini, kwaye andikhathali nokuba kwenzeka ntoni na!

Watya kancinci, wathi ngexhala kuye, "Yeyiphi indlela? Yeyiphi?", Ebambe isandla sakhe phezu kwentloko yakhe ukuze azive ukuba ikhula ngayiphi na indlela, kwaye wothuka kakhulu ukufumanisa ukuba uhlala elingana: ngokuqinisekileyo, oku kwenzeka xa umntu esitya ikeyiki, kodwa u-Alice wayenokulindela nto ngaphandle kokuphuma-endleleni kwakubonakala ngathi izinto ezingaqhelekanga ziya kwenzeka ebomini.

Ngoko waqala ukusebenza, kwaye ngokukhawuleza wayigqiba ikeyiki.
