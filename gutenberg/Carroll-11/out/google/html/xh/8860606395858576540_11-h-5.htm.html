ISAHLUKO V.
Ingcebiso evela kuCaterpillar

I-Caterpillar kunye no-Alice bajongana ixesha elithile bethule: ekugqibeleni iCaterpillar yakhupha i-hookah emlonyeni wayo, yathetha naye ngelizwi elibi, nelobuthongo.

“Ungubani wena? ” watsho uMbungu.

Esi yayingelothuba likhuthazayo lencoko. UAlice waphendula, eneentloni, wathi, “Mhlekazi, andiyazi okwangoku—kodwa ndiyazi ukuba bendingubani ukuvuka ngale ntsasa, kodwa ndicinga ukuba ndinokutshintshwa izihlandlo eziliqela ukususela ngoko.”

"Uthetha ukuthini ngalo nto?" Watsho erhabaxa uMbungu. “Zicacise!”

“Andikwazi ukuzichaza , ndiyoyika, mhlekazi,” watsho u-Alice, “kuba andindim, uyabona.”

“Andiboni,” watsho uMbungu.

“Ndiyoyika ukuba andikwazi ukuyibeka ngokucacileyo,” u-Alice waphendula ngokuthobeke kakhulu, “kuba andiyiqondi mna kwakuqala; kwaye ukuba nobukhulu obahlukeneyo ngosuku kuyabhidisa kakhulu.”

“Akunjalo,” watsho uMbungu.

“Ewe, mhlawumbi awukayifumani okwangoku,” watsho uAlice; “Kodwa xa kufuneka ujike ube yichrysalis—uya kuthi ngenye imini, uyazi—kwaye emva koko ube libhabhathane, ndingacinga ukuba uya kuliva lincinci, akunjalo?”

“Hayi kancinci,” watsho uMbungu.

Ewe, mhlawumbi iimvakalelo zakho zisenokwahluka,” watsho uAlice; "Into endiyaziyo kukuba, iya kuziva imnandi kakhulu kum ."

“Wena!” Watsho ngendelelo uMbungu. "Ungubani ? "

Oko kwababuyisela kwakhona ekuqaleni kwencoko. U-Alice waziva ecaphukile kancinci xa iCaterpillar isenza amagqabantshintshi amafutshane , kwaye wazisondeza wathi, ebuhlungu kakhulu, "Ndicinga ukuba, kufuneka undixelele ukuba ungubani , kuqala."

"Ngoba?" watsho uMbungu.

Nanku omnye umbuzo odidayo; kwaye njengoko uAlice wayengenakucinga ngaso nasiphi na isizathu esilungileyo, kwaye njengoko iCaterpillar ibonakala ikwimo engathandekiyo yengqondo, wajika.

"Buya!" wakhwaza uMbungu emva kwakhe. “Ndinento ebalulekileyo endifuna ukuyithetha!”

Oku kuvakala kuthembisa, ngokuqinisekileyo: u-Alice wajika wabuya kwakhona.

“Khawuqumbe,” watsho uMbungu.

"Ingaba kuko konke?" watsho uAlice ewuginya umsindo wakhe kangangoko ebenako.

“Hayi,” watsho uMbungu.

UAlice wacinga ukuba angalinda, kuba akukho nto yimbi anokuyenza, mhlawumbi emva kwayo yonke loo nto inokumxelela into afanele ayive. Kangangemizuzu ethile yakhukhumala ingathethi, kodwa ekugqibeleni yasolula iingalo zayo, yaphinda yakhupha ihuka emlonyeni, yathi, “Ngoku ucinga ukuba utshintshile, akunjalo?”

“Ndiyoyika, mhlekazi,” watsho uAlice; “Andizikhumbuli izinto endandizikhumbula—kwaye andihlali ndilingana kangangemizuzu elishumi ndikunye!”

"Awukhumbuli ukuba zeziphi izinto?" watsho uMbungu.

“Ewe, ndizamile ukuthi “Injani inyosi encinci ixakekileyo,” kodwa konke kuye kwahluka!” U-Alice waphendula ngelizwi elibi kakhulu.

“Phinda uthi, ‘ Umdala, Tata uWilliam ,’” watsho uCaterpillar.

U-Alice wasonga izandla zakhe, waqala:-

“Umdala, Tata uWilliam,” watsho umfana,
Neenwele zakho zisuke zaba mhlophe kakhulu;
Kwaye uhlala ume phezu kwentloko yakho-
Ngaba ucinga ukuba, kwiminyaka yakho, kulungile?"

“Ebutsheni bam,” waphendula utata uWilliam kunyana wakhe,
Ndandisoyikela ukuba kusenokwenzakalisa ingqondo;
Kodwa, ngoku ndiqinisekile ukuba andinayo,
Kutheni, ndiyenza ngokuphindaphindiweyo.”

“Nibadala,” watsho ulutsha, “njengoko benditshilo ngaphambili,
Kwaye batyebe kakhulu ngokungaqhelekanga;
Nangona kunjalo ujike umva-somersault emnyango-
Thandaza, siyintoni isizathu soko?"

“Ebutsheni bam,” satsho isilumko, njengoko sasishukumisa izikhiya zaso ezingwevu,
“Imilenze yam bendiyigcine ithambile
Ngokusetyenziswa kwesi sithambiso—isheleni enye yebhokisi—
Ndivumele ndikuthengisele isibini?”

“Nibadala,” lwatsho olu lutsha, “kwaye imihlathi yakho ibuthathaka kakhulu
Kuba nantoni na enzima kune-suet;
Kanti uligqibile irhanisi, ngamathambo nomlomo—
Thandaza, ukwazile njani ukuyenza loo nto?”

“Ebutsheni bam,” watsho uyise, “Ndaya emthethweni;
Kwaye saxoxa ityala ngalinye nomfazi wam;
Kwaye amandla emisipha, awawanika emhlathini wam,
Ibuthathe ubomi bam bonke.”

“Nibadala,” lwatsho olu lutsha, “umntu akanakucinga ukuba
Laqina iliso lakho njengakuqala;
Kodwa ulinganise i-eel ekupheleni kwempumlo yakho-
Yintoni ekwenze wahlakanipha kangaka?”

"Ndiphendule imibuzo emithathu, kwaye kwanele,"
Wathi uyise; “musa ukuba nomsindo!
Ucinga ukuba ndingamamela imini yonke kwizinto ezinjalo?
Hamba, okanye ndiza kukukhabela ezantsi izinyuko!”

“Ayithethwa kakuhle loo nto,” watsho uCaterpillar.

“Ayilunganga ncam , ndiyoyika,” watsho uAlice, esoyika; "Amanye amagama atshintshiwe."

“Ayilunganga ukusuka ekuqaleni ukuya ekugqibeleni,” watsho uCaterpillar wagqiba, kwathi cwaka kangangemizuzu ethile.

UMbungu waba ngowokuqala ukuthetha.

"Ufuna ukuba yintoni isayizi?" yabuza.

“Owu, andikhange ndikhethe ubukhulu,” waphendula uAlice engxamile; "Ngunye kuphela ongathandi ukutshintsha rhoqo, uyazi."

“ Andazi ,” watsho uMbungu.

UAlice akazange athethe nto: wayengazange aphikise kangaka ebomini bakhe ngaphambili, kwaye wayeziva ephelelwa ngumsindo.

“Wanelisekile ngoku?” watsho uMbungu.

“Ewe, ndingathanda ukuba mkhulu kancinane , mhlekazi, ukuba awunangxaki,” watsho u-Alice: “ii-intshi ezintathu bubude obumbi kakhulu.”

“Yimpakamo entle ngokwenene!” Watsho ngomsindo uCaterpillar, eziphakamisa eme nkqo xa ethetha (ibiyi-intshi ezintathu ukuphakama).

“Kodwa andiyiqhelanga!” ucenge uAlice ongamahlwempu ngelizwi elibi. Kwaye wazicingela, “Akwaba izidalwa bezingenakukhubeka msinya ngolu hlobo!”

“Uya kuqhela ekuhambeni kwexesha,” watsho uMbungu; yaza yafaka ihuka emlonyeni wayo yaza yatshaya kwakhona.

Ngesi sihlandlo uAlice walinda ngomonde de wakhetha ukuthetha kwakhona. Ngomzuzu okanye emibini iCaterpillar yakhupha ihuka emlonyeni yazamla kanye okanye kabini, yazivuthulula. Emva koko yehla kwikhowa, yaza yarhubuluza yemka engceni, isithi nje njengoko isiya, “Elinye icala liya kukwenza ube mde, kwaye elinye liya kukwenza ube mfutshane.”

“Elinye icala lantoni? Elinye icala lantoni? ” wazicingela uAlice.

“Ngekhowa,” watsho uMbungu, ngokungathi ulibuze ngokuvakalayo; kwaza kwathi ngomzuzwana yatshatyalaliswa.

U-Alice wasala ejonge ngengcinga kwi-mushroom ngomzuzu, ezama ukuqonda ukuba ngawaphi amacala ayo amabini; kwaye njengoko yayijikeleza ngokugqibeleleyo, wafumana lo mbuzo unzima kakhulu. Nangona kunjalo, ekugqibeleni wolula iingalo zakhe zayijikeleza ukuya kuthi ga apho baya kuhamba khona, waza wophula kancinane umphetho ngesandla ngasinye.

"Kwaye ngoku yeyiphi?" yatsho ngaphakathi, yancanca intwana yesandla sasekunene ukuzama isiphumo: kumzuzu olandelayo weva isibetho esinobundlobongela ngaphantsi kwesilevu sakhe: ibethe unyawo lwakhe!

Wayesoyika kakhulu le nguquko yesiquphe, kodwa wayevakalelwa kukuba lalingekho ixesha lokulahlekelwa, njengoko wayencipha ngokukhawuleza; ngoko nangoko waqalisa ukusebenza ukuze atye enye intwana. Isilevu sakhe sasicinezelwe ngokusondeleyo elunyaweni, kangangokuba kwakungekho ndawo yokuvula umlomo; kodwa uyenzile ekugqibeleni, kwaye wakwazi ukuginya iqhekeza lentwana yasekhohlo.

“Yiza, intloko yam ikhululekile ekugqibeleni!” watsho u-Alice ngethoni yolonwabo, eyatshintsha yaba yi-alarm komnye umzuzu, xa wafumanisa ukuba amagxa akhe akafumaneki ndawo: ekuphela kwento awayeyibona, xa ejonga phantsi, yayibubude obukhulu bentamo, ebonakala iphakama njengesiqu kulwandle lwamagqabi aluhlaza alele kude ngaphantsi kwakhe.

" Inokuba yintoni yonke into eluhlaza?" watsho uAlice. Kwaye amagxa am afikelele phi? Kwaye owu, zizandla zam ezihluphekileyo, kunjani ukuba ndingakuboni?" Wayebashukumisa njengoko wayethetha, kodwa akuzange kubekho siphumo silandelayo, ngaphandle nje kokungcangcazela okuncinane phakathi kwamagqabi aluhlaza akude.

Njengoko kwakubonakala ngathi alikho ithuba lokuziphakamisa izandla zakhe entloko, wazama ukuthobisa intloko yakhe kubo, yaye wavuya akufumanisa ukuba intamo yakhe yayinokugoba ngokulula kulo naliphi na icala, njengenyoka. Wayesandula ukuphumelela ukugoba ukuba abe ligoso elihle, kwaye wayeza kuntywila phakathi kwamagqabi, awafumanisa ukuba akayonto ngaphandle kwemiphezulu yemithi eyayibhadula phantsi kwayo, xa umlozi obukhali wambuyisela umva ngokukhawuleza: ihobe elikhulu laphaphazela ebusweni balo, kwaye lalibetha ngamandla ngamaphiko alo.

“Inyoka!” Wakhwaza uHobe.

“ Andiyonyoka mna!” Watsho ecaphuka uAlice. “Ndiyeke!”

“Nyoka, ndiyatsho kwakhona!” waphinda iHobe, kodwa ngelizwi eliphantsi, wongeza ngohlobo oluthile lokulila, “Ndizame ngandlela zonke, kwaye akukho nto ibonakala ifaneleka kubo!”

“Andazi nokuba uthetha ngantoni,” watsho uAlice.

“Ndizamile iingcambu zemithi, kwaye ndizamile iindonga, kwaye ndizamile iindonga,” laqhubela phambili iHobe, lingamhoyanga; kodwa ezo nyoka azikholisi!

U-Alice wayesiya ebhideka, kodwa wayecinga ukuba akukho ncedo lokuphinda athethe ade agqibe iHobe.

“Ngokungathi bekungeyongxaki ngokwaneleyo ukuqandusela amaqanda,” latsho iHobe; Kodwa ndimele ndilumkele iinyoka ubusuku nemini!

“Ndiyaxolisa kakhulu ngokuba ucaphukile,” watsho uAlice, owayeqalisa ukuyibona intsingiselo yayo.

“Kwaye njengokuba ndandithatha owona mthi uphakamileyo ehlathini,” laqhubeka iHobe, liphakamisa ilizwi lalo likhwaza, “kwaye kanye njengokuba ndandicinga ukuba mandikhululeke kubo ekugqibeleni, kufuneka behle begxadazela besuka esibhakabhakeni!

“Kodwa andiyonyoka , ndiyakuxelela!” watsho uAlice. "Ndi-ndi-ndi-"

"Kulungile! Uyintoni ?" watsho uHobe. “Ndiyabona ukuba uzama ukuyila into ethile!”

“Mna—ndiyintombazana encinane,” watsho uAlice, ethandabuza, njengoko wayekhumbula inani lotshintsho awayenalo ngaloo mini.

“Ibali elinokwenzeka ngokwenene!” Watsho ngelizwi lendelelo uHobe. “Ndikhe ndawabona amantombazana amaninzi amancinane ngexesha lam, kodwa andizange ndibone nenye intamo enjengaleyo! Hayi, hayi!

“ Ndiwangcamla amaqanda, ngokuqinisekileyo,” watsho uAlice, owayengumntwana onenyaniso kakhulu; "Kodwa amantombazana amancinci atya amaqanda kakhulu njengoko iinyoka zisenza, uyazi."

“Andikholelwa,” latsho iHobe; “Kodwa ukuba bayayenza, kutheni ke beluhlobo lwenyoka, yiyo kuphela into endinokuyithetha.”

Yayilicebo elitsha eli kuAlice, kangangokuba wathula umzuzu okanye emibini, nto leyo yanika iHobe ithuba lokongeza, “Ukhangela amaqanda, ndiwazi kakuhle ; kwaye ibaluleke ngantoni kum nokuba uyintombazana okanye uyinyoka?

“Ibalulekile into kum ,” watsho ngokukhawuleza uAlice; "Kodwa andifuni amaqanda, njengoko kwenzeka; kwaye ukuba bendinjalo, akufanele ndifune eyakho : andiwathandi ekrwada."

“Kulungile, khawuhambe!” latsho iHobe ngelizwi elirhabaxa, liphinda lizinza kwindlwane yalo. U-Alice wagoba phakathi kwemithi kangangoko anako, kuba intamo yakhe yayihlala ibambene phakathi kwamasebe, kwaye ngamanye amaxesha kwakufuneka ayimise ayivule. Emva kwethuba wakhumbula ukuba wayesawabambe ezandleni zakhe amaqhekeza amakhowa, waza waqalisa ukusebenza ngocoselelo olukhulu, edlakaza kuqala kwelinye aze kwelinye, yaye ekhula ngamanye amaxesha abe mde ngamanye amaxesha abe mfutshane, de waphumelela ukuzithoba kubude bakhe besiqhelo.

Kwakukudala wayeyinto ekufutshane nobukhulu obufanelekileyo, kangangokuba yavakala ingaqhelekanga ekuqaleni; kodwa waqhela kwimizuzu embalwa, waqalisa ukuthetha yedwa, njengesiqhelo. “Yiza, sele ndenze isiqingatha sesicwangciso sam ngoku!Hayi indlela ezidida ngayo zonke ezi nguqulelo!Andiqinisekanga ukuba ndiya kuba yintoni, ukusuka komnye umzuzu ukuya komnye!Noko ke, ndibuyele kumlinganiselo wam ofanelekileyo: into elandelayo, kukungena kweso sitiya sihle— kuya kwenziwa njani oko, ndiyazibuza?” Uthe ke esathetha, wafika ngesiquphe indawo evulekileyo, enendlu encinane kuyo, emalunga neemitha zozithathu ukuphakama. "Nabani na ohlala apho," wacinga njalo u-Alice, "akunakuze kufike kubo ngolu hlobo: kutheni, ndifanele ndiboyikise baphume kwiingqondo zabo!" Waqala ke ukuncwina ngasekunene kwakhona, kwaye akazange alinge ukusondela endlwini ade azithobe ukuya kutsho kwi-intshi ezisithoba ukuphakama.
