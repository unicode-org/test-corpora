ISAHLUKO XII.
Ubungqina bukaAlice

"Apha!" wakhala u-Alice, elibele ngokungxamayo ngelo xesha ukuba ukhule kangakanani kule mizuzu imbalwa idlulileyo, kwaye watsiba engxamile kangangokuba watsiba ibhokisi yejury ngomphetho wesiketi sakhe, ecaphukisa onke amadoda kwiintloko zesihlwele esingezantsi, kwaye apho balala bexhaphake, bemkhumbuza kakhulu ngeglowubhu yegolide awayeyifumene ngengozi kwiveki ephelileyo.

“Owu, ndicela uxolo!” wadanduluka ngethoni yonxunguphalo olukhulu, waqalisa ukuzichola kwakhona ngokukhawuleza kangangoko anako, kuba ingozi yentlanzi yegolide yayiqhubeka ibaleka entlokweni yakhe, kwaye wayenoluvo olungacacanga lokuba kufuneka ziqokelelwe ngoko nangoko kwaye zibuyiselwe kwibhokisi yejaji, kungenjalo ziya kufa.

“Ityala alinakuqhubeka,” watsho uKumkani ngelizwi elibi kakhulu, “de abe bonke abagwebi babuyele kwiindawo zabo ezifanelekileyo— bonke ,” waphinda egxininisa kakhulu, ejonge kabukhali kuAlice njengoko wayesitsho.

U-Alice wajonga kwibhokisi ye-jury, wabona ukuba, ngokungxama kwakhe, wayebeke iLizard entloko ezantsi, kwaye into encinci yayishukumisa umsila wayo ngendlela ebuhlungu, ingakwazi nokushukuma. Kungekudala wayikhupha kwakhona, wayilungisa; “hayi ukuba ithetha lukhulu,” watsho ngaphakathi; "Kufuneka ndicinge ukuba ingasetyenziswa kakhulu kwilingo enye indlela ukuya phezulu."

Kwakamsinyane nje ukuba ijaji lakuba liphilile kumothuko wokucaphuka, kwaye izileyiti neepensile zabo zifunyenwe zaza zabuyiselwa kubo, baqalisa ukusebenza ngenkuthalo enkulu ukubhala imbali yale ngozi, bonke ngaphandle kukaLizard, owayebonakala woyisiwe kakhulu ukuba enze nantoni na ngaphandle kokuhlala evule umlomo, ejonge eluphahleni lwenkundla.

“Wazi ntoni ngeli shishini?” watsho uKumkani kuAlice.

“Akukho nto,” watsho uAlice.

“Akukho nto? ” wazingisa uKumkani.

“Akukho nto,” watsho uAlice.

“Oko kubaluleke kakhulu,” watsho uKumkani, eguqukela kwiqela labagwebi. Babeqala ukubhala oku kwizileyiti zabo, xa uMvundla oMhlophe waphazamisa: “ Akubalulekanga , Mhlekazi uthetha, kakade,” watsho ngelizwi elihloniphekileyo, kodwa efinge iintshiya ejonge ubuso kuye njengoko wayethetha.

" Akubalulekanga , kunjalo, bendithetha," watsho uKumkani ngokukhawuleza, kwaye waya kuye ngesandi esiphantsi,

“ibalulekile—ingabalulekanga—ingabalulekanga—ibalulekile—” ngokungathi uzama ukuba leliphi igama elivakala lifanelekile.

Abanye abachopheli-matyala bayibhala phantsi “ibalulekile,” yaye “ingabalulekanga.” UAlice wayekubona oku, njengoko wayekufuphi ngokwaneleyo ukuba ajonge izileyiti zabo; “kodwa akunamsebenzi kancinane,” wazicingela.

Ngeli xesha uKumkani, owayexakekile ixesha elide ebhala kwincwadi yakhe yamanqaku, wakhwaza esithi “Thula!” waza wafunda kwincwadi yakhe ethi, “Umthetho wamashumi amane anesibini.

Wonke umntu wajonga uAlice.

“ Andiyiyo imayile phezulu,” watsho u-Alice.

“Nguwe,” watsho uKumkani.

“Phantse iimayile ezimbini ukuphakama,” wongeze watsho uKumkanikazi.

“Ewe, andizukuhamba, nangaliphi na ixesha,” watsho u-Alice: “ngaphandle koko, ayingomgaqo oqhelekileyo: uyiqambile ngoku.”

“Lona ngowona mthetho mdala kule ncwadi,” watsho uKumkani.

“Ke kufanele ukuba ibeyiNombolo yokuQala,” utshilo u-Alice.

UKumkani waguquka waluthuthu, wavala incwadi yakhe ngokukhawuleza. “Sithathele ingqalelo isigwebo sakho,” watsho kwijaji, ngelizwi eliphantsi nelingcangcazelayo.

“Kukho obunye ubungqina obuzayo, khawuncede Mhlekazi,” watsho uMvundla oMhlophe, utsiba phezulu ngokungxama okukhulu; “Eli phepha lisandul’ ukucholwa.”

“Yintoni ekuyo?” watsho uQueen.

“Andikayivuli okwangoku,” watsho uMvundla oMhlophe, “kodwa kubonakala ngathi yileta, ebhalwe libanjwa isiya—kumntu othile.”

“Kumele ukuba kube njalo,” watsho uKumkani, “ngaphandle kokuba ibingabhalwanga mntu, nto leyo engaqhelekanga, uyazi.”

“Ibhekiswe kubani?” yatsho enye ijury.

“Ayibhekiswanga konke konke,” watsho uMvundla oMhlophe; “Enyanisweni, akukho nto ibhaliweyo ngaphandle . Wasolula iphepha njengoko wayethetha, waza wongeza ngelithi “Asingonobumba, ngapha koko: liqela leevesi.”

“Ingaba zibhalwe ngesandla sebanjwa?” yabuza enye ijury.

“Hayi, azinjalo,” watsho uMvundla oMhlophe, “kwaye yeyona nto ingaqhelekanga ngayo leyo.” (Iqela labagwebi lalibonakala lididekile.)

“Umele ukuba wayexelisa isandla somnye umntu,” watsho uKumkani. (I-jury yaphinda yaqaqamba kwakhona.)

“Nceda Mhlekazi,” watsho uKnave, “andiyibhalanga, kwaye abanakungqina ukuba ndiyibhalile: akukho gama lityikityiweyo ekugqibeleni.”

UKumkani wathi: “Ukuba ubungasityobelanga, loo nto iyenza ibe mandundu ngakumbi loo nto.

Kwabakho ukuqhwatywa kwezandla ngokubanzi koku: yayiyinto yokuqala ekrelekrele ngokwenene eyathethwa nguKumkani ngaloo mini.

“Oko kubonisa ukuba netyala kwakhe,” watsho uKumkanikazi.

“Akunabungqina bunjalo!” watsho uAlice. “Kutheni, awuzazi nokuba zingantoni na!”

“Zifundeni,” watsho uKumkani.

UMvundla oMhlophe wanxiba izibuko zakhe. “Ndiza kuqala ngaphi, Mhlekazi?” wabuza.

"Qala ekuqaleni," watsho uKumkani ngokukrakra, "uhambe ude ufike esiphelweni: uze uyeke."

Ezi yayizivesi ezazifundeka nguMvundla oMhlophe:—

“Bandixelele ukuba ubukhe waya kuye,
Uye wandikhankanya kuye:
Undinike isimilo esihle,
Kodwa ndathi andikwazi ukuqubha.

Ubathumelele ukuba khange ndiye
(Siyazi ukuba yinyani):
Ukuba unokuwutyhalela phambili umcimbi,
Bekuya kwenzeka ntoni kuwe?

Ndamnika enye, bamnika ezimbini,
Usinike ezintathu nangaphezulu;
Bonke babuya kuye beza kuwe;
Nangona babengabam ngaphambili.

Ukuba ndithe ndafumana ithuba lokuba
Ubandakanyeka kulo mcimbi,
Uthembele kuwe ukuba ubakhulule,
Kanye njengokuba sasinjalo.

Uluvo lwam yayikukuba wawukho
(Phambi kokuba afumane oku)
Kwabakho umqobo phakathi
Yena, kunye nathi, kunye nalo.

Ungamazisi ukuba ubathanda kakhulu,
Kuba oku kufuneka kube njalo
Imfihlo, egcinwe kubo bonke abanye,
Phakathi kwam nawe.”

“Obona bungqina bubalulekileyo esibuvileyo,” watsho uKumkani ephulula izandla zakhe; "Ke ngoku vumela ijaji-"

“Ukuba nabani na kubo unokukucacisa oko,” watsho uAlice, (wayekhule kakhulu kwimizuzu embalwa edluleyo kangangokuba wayengoyiki nakancinane ukumphazamisa), “Ndiza kumnika i-sixpence. Andikholelwa ukuba kukho iathom yentsingiselo kuyo.

Bonke abagwebi babhala phantsi kwizileyiti zabo, “ Akakholelwa ukuba kukho iathom yentsingiselo kuyo,” kodwa akukho namnye kubo owazama ukulicacisa eli phepha.

“Ukuba akukho ntsingiselo kuyo,” watsho uKumkani, “okusindisa ihlabathi lenkathazo, uyazi, njengoko kungeyomfuneko ukuba sizame ukuyifumana.” Noko ke, andazi,” wahlabela mgama ezolula iindinyana edolweni, waza wazijonga ngeliso elinye; “Ndibonakala ndibona intsingiselo ethile kuzo, emva kwayo yonke loo nto. wongeze watsho ejikela eKnave.

Wanikina intloko uKnave kalusizi. "Ngaba ndifana nayo?" uthe. (Yena ngokuqinisekileyo akazange ayenze , yenziwe ngokupheleleyo ngekhadibhodi.)

“Kulungile ke, ukuza kuthi ga ngoku,” watsho uKumkani, waza waqhubeka embombozela ezi ndinyana esithi: “’ Siyazi ukuba yinyani— ’ lilo elo ke, lilo ke—’ Ndimnike elinye, bamnika ezimbini— ’ kuba, kumele ukuba yiloo nto awayenzayo ngeetarti, uyazi—”

“Kodwa, kuyaqhubeka, ' bonke babuyile beza kuwe bevela kuye ,' utshilo uAlice.

“Ngoba, nazo!” Watsho ngoloyiso uKumkani, ekhomba kwii tati eziphezu kwetafile. “Ayikho into ecace ngaphezu kwaleyo . Emva koko—’ ngaphambi kokuba alingane —’ awuzange ufane, sithandwa sam, ndiyacinga?” Watsho uQueen.

“Nakanye!” Watsho evutha ngumsindo uQueen, ejula i-inki kuNcinci xa ethetha. (UBill omncinane onelishwa wayekile ukubhala kwisileyiti sakhe ngomnwe omnye, njengoko wayengafumani phawu; kodwa ngoku wakhawuleza waqalisa kwakhona, esebenzisa i-inki, eyayisehla ebusweni bakhe, okoko kwakunjalo.)

“Ke amagama awakufanelanga ,” yatsho iKumkani, ijonge inkundla ngoncumo. Kwathi cwaka.

“Ngumdlalo lo!” uKumkani wongeze ngelizwi elikhubekileyo, kwaye wonke umntu wahleka, "Ijaji malisithathele ingqalelo isigwebo sabo." Wathi ukumkani, malunga nexesha lamashumi amabini ngaloo mini.

"Hayi hayi!" watsho uQueen. "Isigwebo kuqala-isigqibo emva koko."

"Izinto kunye nobuvuvu!" Watsho ekhwaza uAlice. "Umbono wokuba nesivakalisi kuqala!"

“Bamba ulwimi lwakho!” Watsho uQueen ejike wamfusa.

"Andizukwazi!" watsho uAlice.

“Myeke ngentloko!” uKumkanikazi wakhwaza ngelizwi lakhe. Akukho mntu ushukumayo.

“Ngubani okukhathaleleyo?” watsho u-Alice, (wayesele ekhule ngokugqibeleleyo ngeli xesha). “Awuyonto ngaphandle kwepakethe yamakhadi!”

Ngale nto yonke ipakethe yenyuka yaya emoyeni, yeza phezu kwakhe: wakhwaza kancinci, isiqingatha soloyiko nesiqingatha somsindo, wazama ukubabetha, wazifumana engqengqe elunxwemeni, intloko yakhe isemathangeni kadade wabo, owayebhula ngobunono amagqabi afileyo awayephaphazele esuka emithini ebusweni bakhe.

"Vuka, Alice sithandwa!" watsho udade wabo; “Kaloku, yintoni oye walala ixesha elide ngayo!”

“Owu, bendinephupha elimangalisayo!” Wathi uAlice, waza waxelela udade wabo, njengoko wayekwazi ukuzikhumbula, zonke ezi Adventures ingaqhelekanga yakhe ubufunda malunga; kwaye akugqiba, udade wabo wamanga, wathi, " Liphupha elimangalisayo, sithandwa, ngokuqinisekileyo: kodwa ngoku baleka uye etini yakho; kuyahlwa." Waphakama ke u-Alice wabaleka, ecinga ngeli xesha ebalekayo, enokuba liphupha elimnandi eli.

Kodwa udade wabo wahlala kanye xa wayemshiya, engqiyame entloko esandleni sakhe, ejonge ukutshona kwelanga, kwaye ecinga ngo-Alice omncinci kunye nazo zonke iiAdventures zakhe ezimangalisayo, de naye waqala ukuphupha emva kwefashoni, kwaye eli yayiliphupha lakhe: -

Okokuqala, waphupha u-Alice omncinci ngokwakhe, kwaye kwakhona izandla ezincinci zibambene edolweni lakhe, kwaye amehlo aqaqambileyo ajonge kuye-wayeva iithowuni zelizwi lakhe, kwaye abone loo nto incinci yentloko yakhe ukuze abambe iinwele ezibhadulayo ezazihlala zingena emehlweni akhe-kwaye nangoku njengoko wayemamele, okanye ebonakala emamele, yonke indawo ejikeleze iphupha likadade wabo iyaphila.

Ingca ende irhorhozela ezinyaweni zakhe njengoko uMvundla oMhlophe udlula-iMpuku eyothukileyo yatshiza indlela yayo kwichibi elingummelwane—yayiva inkxwaleko yeekomityi zeti njengoko uMvundla kaMatshi kunye nabahlobo bakhe babesabelana ngesidlo sabo esingenasiphelo, nelizwi elirhabaxa likaKumkanikazi liyalela ukuba iindwendwe zakhe zilishwa zibulawe, ngeli lixa iipleyiti ze-Duchess zaziqhubeka ngedolo. kwaye izitya zangqubeka ngeenxa zonke kuyo-kwaphinda kwakhala i-Gryphon, ukuncwina kwe-slate-pensile yeLizard, kunye nokuminxeka kweehagu ezicinezelweyo, zazalisa umoya, zixutywe kunye nokukhala okukude koMfudo olusizi.

Wahlala ke, enamehlo avaliweyo, kwaye isiqingatha sakholelwa kwi-Wonderland, nangona wayesazi ukuba kufuneka ayivule kwakhona, kwaye yonke into yayiza kutshintsha ibe yinyani emdaka-ingca yayiza kugquma emoyeni kuphela, kwaye idama lirhwashazela kukushukuma kweengcongolo-iicuphe zeti ezikhenkcezayo ziya kutshintsha zibe ziintsimbi zegusha, kwaye ilizwi likaKumkanikazi likhwaza ilizwi lenkwenkwe. usana, ukuncwina kweGryphon, kunye nazo zonke ezinye ingxolo engaqhelekanga, yayiza kutshintsha (eyazi) ibe yingxokozelo ebhidekileyo yeyadi yefama exakekileyo-ngelixa ukuthotywa kweenkomo kumgama kwakuthatha indawo yokulila kukaMock Turtle.

Okokugqibela, wazicingela ngokwakhe ukuba lo dade wabo mncinane wayeya kuthi, emva kwexesha, abe ngumfazi okhulileyo; kunye nendlela awayeza kuyigcina ngayo yonke iminyaka yakhe evuthiweyo, intliziyo elula nenothando yobuntwana bakhe: nendlela awayeza kuhlanganisana ngayo ngabanye abantwana bakhe abancinane, aze enze amehlo abo aqaqambe kwaye alangazelele ngamabali amaninzi angaqhelekanga, mhlawumbi ngephupha le-Wonderland yakudala: nendlela awayeya kuziva ngayo nazo zonke iintsizi zabo ezilula, kwaye afumane uyolo kulo lonke ulonwabo lwabo olulula, ekhumbula iintsuku zakhe zasehlotyeni ezonwabileyo, kunye nolonwabo lwakhe lwasehlotyeni.
