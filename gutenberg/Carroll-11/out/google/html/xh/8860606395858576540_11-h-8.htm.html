ISAHLUKO VIII.
I-Queen's Croquet-Ground

Umthi omkhulu we-rose wema kufuphi nomnyango wegadi: iirozi ezikhula kuyo zazimhlophe, kodwa kwakukho abalimi abathathu kuyo, beyipeyinta ebomvu. U-Alice wacinga le nto inomdla kakhulu, waza wasondela ukuba ababukele, wathi xa esondela kubo weva omnye wabo esithi, “Jonga ngoku, Zintlanu!

“Andikhange ndikwazi ukuzibamba,” watsho uSihlanu, ngelizwi elirhabaxa; Abasixhenxe babaleka ingqiniba yam.

Sathi ke isiXhenxe sajonga phezulu, sathi, Ewe, ziHlanu, hlala ubeka ityala kwabanye.

“ Kungcono ungathethi !” watsho uSihlanu. "Ndive uKumkanikazi esithi izolo ufanelwe kukunqunyulwa intloko!"

"Ukwenzela ntoni?" Watsho lo ebethetha kuqala.

“Asingomsebenzi wakho lowo, Zimbini!” watsho uSixhenxe.

"Ewe, ngumsebenzi wakhe!" watsho uHlanu, “kwaye ndiza kumxelela—ibizele ukuzisa i-tulip roots endaweni yetswele.”

Abasixhenxe baphosa phantsi ibhrashi, kwaye babesandula ukuqalisa “Ke, kuzo zonke izinto ezingalunganga-” xa iliso lakhe lawela ku-Alice, njengoko wayemile ebabukele, kwaye wazijonga ngesiquphe: abanye bajonga ngapha nangapha, kwaye bonke baqubuda.

“Ungandixelela,” watsho u-Alice, esoyika, “kutheni upeyinta ezi rozi?”

UHlanu noSixhenxe abathetha nto, kodwa bajonge kuZimbini. Ababini baqala ngelizwi eliphantsi, "Kutheni inyani, uyabona, Nkosazana, lo ulapha bekufanele ukuba ngumthi obomvu obomvu , kwaye sifake omhlophe ngempazamo; kwaye ukuba uKumkanikazi wayenokuyifumanisa, sonke kufuneka sinqunyulwe iintloko zethu, uyazi. Uyabona, Nkosazana, senza konke okusemandleni ethu, phambi kokuba eze, uku-" Ngalo mzuzu wabiza uKumkanikazi oHlanu, owayenqumle igadi! basuka bawa ngobuso abo bagadi bobathathu. Kwavakala isandi seenyawo ezininzi, kwaye u-Alice wajonga ngapha nangapha, elangazelela ukubona uKumkanikazi.

Kwaqala kwafika amajoni alishumi ephethe iintonga; zonke ezo nto zazimilise okweegadi zontathu, zingqukuva, zisicaba, zinezandla neenyawo embombeni; aba bebehonjiswe ngedayimani yonke indawo, bahamba ngababini-babini, njengoko enzayo amajoni. Emveni koko kwaphuma oonyana bokumkani; kwakukho abalishumi kubo, kwaye abathandekayo abancinci beza bexhuma ngovuyo kunye nesandla ngesandla, kwizibini: zonke zazihonjiswe ngeentliziyo. Kwalandela iindwendwe, uninzi lwazo, iiKumkani neeKumkanikazi, kwaye phakathi kwabo u-Alice waqonda uMvundla oMhlophe: wawuthetha ngokungxama koloyiko, uncuma ngayo yonke into ethethiweyo, kwaye wadlula ngaphandle kokumqaphela. Emva koko kwalandela iKnave yeeNtliziyo, ethwele isithsaba sikaKumkani kumqamelo wevelvet obomvu; yaye, ekugqibeleni kuwo wonke lo mngcelele uzukileyo, kwafika UKUMKANI NOMNKOSI WEENTLIZIYO.

U-Alice wayethandabuza ukuba akufuneki angqengqe ngobuso njengabagcini-gadi abathathu, kodwa wayengakhumbuli ekhe weva ngalo mthetho kwimingcelele; “Kwaye ngaphandle koko, bekuya kunceda ntoni na umngcelele,” wacinga njalo, “ukuba bonke abantu bebengqengqe ngobuso, ukuze bangaboni? Wema ngxi apho abekhona, walinda.

Xa umngcelele wawujongene no-Alice, bema bonke bajonga kuye, kwaye uKumkanikazi wathi kabukhali, "Ngubani lo?" Watsho ebhekisa kwiKnave yeentliziyo, waqubuda kuphela wancuma ephendula.

“Isidenge!” Watsho uKumkanikazi, ejula intloko ngokungenamonde; kwaye, eguqukela ku-Alice, waqhubeka, "Ungubani igama lakho, mntwana?"

“Igama lam ndinguAlice, ndiyacela ke Mhlekazi,” watsho uAlice ngembeko; kodwa wongeza, ngaphakathi kuye, “Kuba, bayipakethe nje yamakhadi, emva kwayo yonke loo nto.

“Kwaye ngoobani aba? ” watsho uKumkanikazi, ekhomba abagadi abathathu ababengqengqe bengqonge umthi werozi; kuba, uyabona, njengoko babelele ngobuso babo, kwaye ipateni emqolo yabo yayifana neyonke ipakethe, wayengenakukwazi ukuba babengabagadi, okanye amajoni, okanye amagosa, okanye abathathu babantwana bakhe.

" Ndifanele ndazi njani?" watsho uAlice, emangaliswe sisibindi sakhe. “Asiyongxaki yam leyo .

UKumkanikazi wajika wabomvu bubomvurha ngumsindo, yaye, emva kokuba emthe krwaqu okomzuzwana okwerhamncwa lasendle, wakhwaza esithi “Khawucime ngentloko!

“Imbudane!” watsho uAlice, ekhwaza kakhulu kwaye egqibe, kwaye uKumkanikazi wathi cwaka.

Ukumkani wabeka isandla sakhe phezu kwengalo yakhe, waza ngoloyiko wathi, “Qwalasela, sithandwa sam: ngumntwana kuphela!”

UKumkanikazi wajika ngomsindo kuye, wathi kuKnave, "Bajike!"

I-Knave yenza njalo, ngononophelo olukhulu, ngonyawo olunye.

"Phakama!" watsho uKumkanikazi, ngelizwi elirhabaxa, elikhwazayo, baza abagadi abathathu ngoko nangoko baxhuma phezulu, baqubuda kuKumkani, uKumkanikazi, abantwana basebukhosini, kunye naye wonke umntu.

“Yiyeke loo nto!” Wakhwaza uQueen. “Uyandenza isithukuthezi.” Kwaye emva koko, wajika waya kumthi werozi, waqhubeka, “ Ufuna ntoni apha?”

“Yanga ingakukholisa uMhlekazi,” watsho uMbini, ngelizwi elithobekileyo, esihla ngedolo elinye njengoko wayethetha, “besizama—”

" Ndiyabona !" Watsho uQueen, ngeloxesha ebephonononga iirozi. “Bayeke neentloko zabo!” kwaye umngcelele waqhubela phambili, amajoni amathathu asala ngasemva ukuze abulale abalimi abanelishwa, ababalekela ku-Alice ukuze bakhuseleke.

“Awuna kunqunyulwa intloko!” watsho uAlice, wazifaka kwimbiza enkulu yeentyatyambo eyayimi kufutshane. La majoni mathathu abhadula kangangomzuzu okanye emibini, ewafuna, aza emva koko ethe cwaka elandela amanye.

"Ngaba iintloko zabo zisusiwe?" wakhwaza uQueen.

“Iintloko zabo zimkile, ukuba kuthe kwalunga kuMhlekazi!” aphendula akhwaza.

"Ilungile lo nto!" wakhwaza uQueen. "Ungakwazi ukudlala i-croquet?"

Amajoni athi cwaka, ajonga u-Alice, njengoko lo mbuzo ngokucacileyo wawubhekiselwe kuye.

"Ewe!" wakhwaza uAlice.

Yiza ke! Wabhonga ukumkanikazi, kwaye u-Alice wajoyina umngcelele, ezibuza kakhulu ukuba kuya kwenzeka ntoni emva koko.

“Lusuku oluhle kakhulu!” kwatsho ilizwi elineentloni ecaleni kwakhe. Wayehamba ngakuMvundla oMhlophe, owawukrobe ebusweni bakhe unexhala.

“Kakhulu,” watsho u-Alice: “Ziphi iiDuchess?”

"Ngokukhawuleza kakhulu!" Watsho ngelizwi eliphantsi, elikhawulezayo uMvundla. Wajonga egxalabeni lakhe enexhala njengoko wayethetha, emva koko waziphakamisa ngencanca, wabeka umlomo wakhe endlebeni yakhe, waza wasebeza “Uphantsi kwesigwebo sokubulawa.”

"Ukwenzela ntoni?" watsho uAlice.

“Ngaba uthe, ‘Ndilusizi olunjani!’?” wabuza uMvundla.

UAlice wathi: “Hayi, andikhange ndiyenze loo nto, andiqondi ukuba lusizi kwaphela.

“Wavala iindlebe zikaKumkanikazi—” waqala watsho uMvundla. U-Alice wakhwaza ehleka. “Owu, cwaka!” Wasebeza uMvundla ngethoni eyoyikayo. "Ukumkanikazi uyakuva! Uyabona, ufike emva kwexesha, kwaye uKumkanikazi wathi-"

“Yiya kwiindawo zakho!” wakhwaza uKumkanikazi ngelizwi lendudumo, baza abantu baqalisa ukubaleka macala onke, bengquzulana; nangona kunjalo, bazinza ngomzuzu okanye emibini, kwaye umdlalo waqala. U-Alice wayecinga ukuba akazange awubone umhlaba onomdla ngolu hlobo ebomini bakhe; zonke yayiziingqengqelo neefolo; iibhola yayizihedgehogs eziphilayo, iimallets live flamingo, kwaye amajoni kwafuneka ukuba kabini phezulu kwaye eme ngezandla neenyawo, ukwenza iarches.

Obona bunzima u-Alice abufumana ekuqaleni yayikukulawula i-flamingo yakhe: wakwazi ukukhupha umzimba wakhe, ngokukhululeka ngokwaneleyo, phantsi kwengalo yakhe, imilenze yakhe ijinga phantsi, kodwa ngokubanzi, kanye njengoko wayeyilungisile intamo yakhe kakuhle, kwaye wayeza kubetha i-hedgehog ngentloko yayo, yayizijijile kwaye ijonge phezulu ebusweni bakhe, kwaye ididekile. phantsi, kwaye yayiza kuphinda iqale kwakhona, yayicaphukisa kakhulu ukufumanisa ukuba i-hedgehog yayizityhilile, kwaye yayisenza isenzo sokukhasa kude: ngaphandle kwayo yonke le nto, kwakukho umqolo okanye umsele kwindlela naphi na apho wayefuna ukuthumela khona i-hedgehog, kwaye, njengoko amajoni aphindwe kabini ayesoloko ephakama kwaye ehamba kwezinye iindawo zomhlaba, u-Alice wafikelela kwisigqibo esinzima ngokwenene.

Abadlali badlale bonke ngaxeshanye ngaphandle kokulinda ukujika, bexambulisana ngalo lonke elo xesha, belwela iihedgehogs; kwaye ngexesha elifutshane kakhulu uKumkanikazi wayevutha ngumsindo, kwaye wahamba enyathela, edanduluka esithi, "Cima ngentloko!" okanye “Umcimile ngentloko!” malunga nokuba kanye ngomzuzu.

U-Alice waqala ukuziva engonwabanga kakhulu: ngokuqinisekileyo, wayengekabinayo ingxabano noKumkanikazi, kodwa wayesazi ukuba ingenzeka nangawuphi na umzuzu, “kwaye emva koko,” wacinga ukuba, “kuya kwenzeka ntoni kum?

Wayejonge indlela yokusaba, kwaye ezibuza ukuba angakwazi na ukubaleka engakhange abonwe, xa wabona inkangeleko enomdla emoyeni: yamothusa kakhulu ekuqaleni, kodwa, emva kokuyibukela umzuzu okanye emibini, wayenza yabuhlungu, kwaye wazixelela ukuba “YiKati yaseCheshire: ngoku ndiza kuba nomntu endinokuthetha naye.”

"Ingaba uqhubeka njani?" Yatsho iKati, yatsho yanele ukuba ithethe.

UAlice walinda de kwavela amehlo, wanqwala. “Akuncedi nto ukuthetha nayo,” wacinga njalo, “zide zifike iindlebe zayo, okanye nokuba ibe nye kuzo.” Ngomnye umzuzu kwavela intloko yonke, emva koko u-Alice wabeka i-flamingo yakhe, waqala ingxelo yomdlalo, eziva evuya kakhulu ukuba unomntu wokumamela. Ikati yayibonakala icinga ukuba yanele ngoku ibonwayo, kwaye akusekho nto ibonakalayo.

“Andicingi ukuba badlala ngokufanelekileyo konke konke,” waqala u-Alice, ngelizwi elikhalazayo, “kwaye bonke bayaxabana ngokoyikeka kangangokuba umntu angakwazi ukuziva ethetha-kwaye kubonakala ngathi abanayo nayiphi na imigaqo; ubuncinci, ukuba kukho, akukho mntu ubahoyayo-kwaye awuyazi ukuba zibhidanisa kangakanani na zonke izinto eziphilayo; umzekelo, ekuhambeni kwexesha, ndiza kudlula. emhlabeni—kwaye ngendiyinqumle ngoku ihedgehog kaKumkanikazi, kodwa yabaleka yakubona eyam isiza!”

"Umthanda njani uKumkanikazi?" Watsho ngelizwi eliphantsi uKati.

“Akunjalo konke konke,” watsho u-Alice: “ugqithise kakhulu—” Kanye ngelo xesha waqaphela ukuba uKumkanikazi wayekufuphi emva kwakhe, emamele: ke waqhubeka, “- kusenokwenzeka ukuba aphumelele, ukuba akufanelanga ukuba ugqibezele umdlalo.”

UKumkanikazi wancuma wagqitha.

“ Uthetha nabani?” Watsho uKumkani esiya kuAlice, ejonge entloko yeKati ngomdla omkhulu.

“Ngumhlobo wam—ikati yaseCheshire,” watsho uAlice: “Ndivumele ndimazise ngayo.”

“Andiyithandi tu inkangeleko yayo,” watsho uKumkani: “Noko ke, isengaphuza isandla sam ukuba iyathanda.”

“Ndikhetha ukungakwenzi,” yatsho iKati.

“Musani ukuba ngabangenambeko,” watsho uKumkani, “kwaye musani ukundijonga ngolo hlobo! Ufike emva ko Alice esathetha.

“Ikati isenokujonga enkosini,” watsho uAlice. “Ndikufundile oko kwenye incwadi, kodwa andikhumbuli ukuba kuphi na.”

“Ewe, kufuneka isuswe,” watsho uKumkani eqinisekile, waza wabiza uKumkanikazi owayedlula ngelo xesha, “Sithandwa sam!

UKumkanikazi wayenendlela enye kuphela yokusombulula zonke iingxaki, ezinkulu okanye ezincinci. “Myeke ngentloko!” watsho engakhange ajonge macala.

“Ndiza kumlanda ngokwam umbulali,” watsho ngomdla uKumkani, waza wemka ngokukhawuleza.

U-Alice wacinga ukuba angaphinda abuyele umva, abone ukuba uqhubeka njani lo mdlalo, njengoko weva ilizwi likaKumkanikazi mgama, ekhwaza ngomdla. Wayesele esivile isigwebo sakhe kubadlali abathathu ukuba bagwetywe ngenxa yokuphoswa kwamathuba abo, kwaye wayengayithandi tu inkangeleko yezinto, njengoko umdlalo wawubhidekile kangangokuba wayengazi nokuba lithuba lakhe okanye akunjalo. Ngoko waya kukhangela i-hedgehog yakhe.

I-hedgehog yayibambene nomlo nenye i-hedgehog, ebonakala ngathi ku-Alice lithuba elihle lokugoba enye yazo kunye nenye: ubunzima kuphela kukuba, i-flamingo yakhe yawela ngaphesheya kwegadi, apho u-Alice wayeyibona izama ngendlela engenakuzinceda ukubhabha emthini.

Ngexesha wayeyibambile i-flamingo waza wayibuyisa, umlo wawusele uphelile, kwaye zombini ezi hedgehogs zazingabonakali: “kodwa akunamsebenzi kangako,” wacinga njalo u-Alice, “njengoko zonke iintsika zimkile kweli cala lomhlaba.” Ngoko walifaka ekhwapheni, ukuze lingaphuncuki kwakhona, wabuyela emva kwincoko encinane nomhlobo wakhe.

Ukubuyela kwakhe kwiKati yaseCheshire, wothuka kukufumana isihlwele esikhulu siqokelelene ngeenxa zonke kuyo: kwakukho ingxabano phakathi kombulali, uKumkani, kunye noKumkanikazi, ababethetha bonke ngaxeshanye, ngelixa bonke abanye bathe cwaka, kwaye babonakala bengakhululekanga.

Ukuvela kuka-Alice, wabhenwa ngabo bobathathu ukuba bawuphendule lo mbuzo, kwaye baphinda iingxoxo zabo kuye, nangona, njengoko babethetha bonke ngaxeshanye, wakufumanisa kunzima ngokwenene ukuyiqonda le nto bayithethayo.

Ingxoxo yombulali yayikukuba, awunakunqumla intloko ngaphandle kokuba kukho umzimba onokuyinqumla: ukuba wayengazange enze into enjalo ngaphambili, yaye wayengayi kuqalisa ngexesha lakhe lobomi.

Ingxoxo kaKumkani yayisithi, nantoni na enentloko inokunqunyulwa intloko, uze ungathethi ububhanxa.

Ingxoxo kaKumkanikazi yayikukuba, ukuba into ayenziwanga malunga naloo nto ngexesha elingaphantsi kwexesha wayeya kuthi wonke umntu abulawe, ngeenxa zonke. (Yayingamagqabantshintshi okugqibela alenze iqela lonke labonakala libuhlungu kwaye linexhala.)

UAlice wayengenakucinga enye into anokuyithetha ngaphandle kokuba "yeyeduchess: kungcono umbuze ngayo."

“Usentolongweni,” uKumkanikazi wathi kumbulali: “mthabathe apha.” Umbulali wahamba njengotolo.

Intloko yeCati yaqala ukunyamalala ngexesha awayehamba ngalo, kwaye, ngelixa ebuyile kunye ne-Duchess, yaphela ngokupheleleyo; ngoko uKumkani kunye nomgwebi babaleka benyuka benyuka beyikhangela, ngelixa elinye iqela labuyela kumdlalo.
