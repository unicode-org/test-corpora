KO E VAHE V.
Fale'i mei ha 'Unu'u'ulu .

Naʻe fesiofaki ʻa e ʻUnuá mo ʻAlisi ʻi ha taimi ʻi he fakalongolongo: faifai pea toʻo ʻe he ʻAnuá ʻa e hookah mei hono ngutú, ʻo ne lea kiate ia ʻi ha leʻo fakaʻofa mo mohe.

“Ko hai koe? ” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

Na‘e ‘ikai ko ha fakaava fakalototo‘a eni ki ha fetalanoa‘aki. Naʻe tali ange ʻe ʻAlisi, ʻo ʻikai fuʻu mā, “Ko au —ʻoku ʻikai ke u meimei ʻiloʻi, tangataʻeiki, ʻi he taimi ní pē —ko e siʻisiʻi tahá ʻoku ou ʻiloʻi pe ko hai au ʻi heʻeku ʻā hake he pongipongí ni, ka ʻoku ou fakakaukau kuo pau pē kuo tuʻo lahi hono liliu au talu mei ai.”

“Ko e hā hoʻo ʻuhinga ki he meʻa ko iá?” ko e lea fefeka ia ʻa e Katá. “Fakamatalaʻi koe!”

“He ʻikai ke u lava ʻo fakamatalaʻi au , ʻoku ou manavasiʻi, tangataʻeiki,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí, “he ʻoku ʻikai ko au, ʻokú ke sio.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u sio,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

“ʻOku ou manavasiʻi naʻa ʻikai te u lava ʻo fakalea mahino ange ia,” ko e tali anga fakaʻapaʻapa ʻaupito ia ʻa ʻAlisí, “he ʻoku ʻikai lava ke mahino ia kiate au ke u kamata ʻaki;pea ko e lahi fau ʻa e ngaahi lalahi kehekehe ʻi ha ʻaho ʻoku fuʻu fakatupu puputuʻu.”

“ʻOku ʻikai,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

“Sai, mahalo ʻoku teʻeki ke ke maʻu ia ʻo pehē,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí; “ka ʻi he taimi kuo pau ke ke liliu ai ki ha chrysalis —te ke liliu ia ʻi ha ʻaho, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi —pea hili iá ki ha manupuna, ʻoku totonu ke u fakakaukau te ke ongoʻi ia ʻoku kiʻi kehekehe, ʻikai ko ia?”

“ʻIkai ko ha kiʻi meʻa siʻisiʻi,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

“Sai, mahalo ʻe kehe nai hoʻo ngaahi ongó,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí; “ko e meʻa pē ʻoku ou ʻiló, ʻe ongoʻi kehekehe ʻaupito ia kiate au .”

"Ko koe!" ko e lea fakaanga ia ʻa e Katá. "Ko hai koe? "

‘A ia na’e toe fakafoki kinaua ki he kamata’anga ‘o e talanoa. Naʻe kiʻi ongoʻi ʻita ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he fai ʻe he Katepilá ha ngaahi lea nounou ʻaupito peheé, peá ne toʻo hake ia ʻo ne pehē, ʻi he fakamātoato ʻaupito, “ʻOku ou fakakaukau, ʻoku totonu ke ke talamai pe ko hai koe , ʻuluaki.”

"Ko e hā hono ʻuhingá?" ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

Naʻe toe ʻi ai ʻeni ha fehuʻi fakatupu puputuʻu; pea ʻi he ʻikai lava ʻe ʻAlisi ʻo fakakaukau ki ha ʻuhinga lelei, pea ʻi he ngali ʻi ha tuʻunga taʻefakafiefia ʻaupito ʻo e ʻatamaí ʻa e Katá, naʻá ne tafoki ʻo ʻalu.

“Foki mai!” ko e ui ia ʻa e ʻUnuʻuá ʻi mui ʻiate ia. “ʻOku ʻi ai ha meʻa mahuʻinga ke u lea ʻaki!”

Naʻe ongo fakatuʻamelie ʻeni, ʻoku pau: Naʻe tafoki ʻa ʻAlisi ʻo toe foki mai.

“Tauhi hoʻo ʻitá,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

“Ko e meʻa pē ia?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻo ne folo hifo ʻene ʻitá ʻi he lelei taha te ne lavá.

“ʻIkai,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

Naʻe fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi mahalo ʻe sai pē ke ne tatali, he ʻoku ʻikai haʻane meʻa kehe ke fai, pea mahalo ʻi he hili iá ʻe talaange nai ʻe he meʻa ko iá ha meʻa ʻoku taau ke ne fanongo ki ai. Naʻá ne puhiʻi atu ʻi ha ngaahi miniti ʻo ʻikai ke ne lea, ka naʻe faifai peá ne vete hono ongo nimá, ʻo ne toe toʻo ʻa e hookah mei hono ngutú, peá ne pehē mai, “Ko ia ʻokú ke fakakaukau kuó ke liliu, ʻikai ko ia?”

“ʻOku ou manavasiʻi ʻoku ou pehē, tangataʻeiki,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí; “ʻOku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo manatuʻi ha ngaahi meʻa ʻo hangē ko ia ne u faʻa faí﻿—pea ʻoku ʻikai ke u tauhi ha lahi tatau ʻi ha miniti ʻe hongofulu fakataha!”

“ʻIkai lava ke manatuʻi pe ko e hā e ngaahi meʻá?” ko e lau ia ʻa e Katá.

“Sai, kuó u feinga ke pehē “ʻOku fēfē ʻa e kiʻi pi femoʻuekiná,” ka naʻe kehe kotoa ia!” Naʻe tali ange ʻe ʻAlisi ʻi ha leʻo fakaloloma ʻaupito.

“Toe pehē mai, ‘ Kuó ke motuʻa, Tamai Uiliami ,’” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

Naʻe fusi ʻe ʻAlisi hono ongo nimá, ʻo ne kamata:—

“Kuó ke motuʻa, Patele Uiliami,” ko e lea ia ʻa e talavoú, .
“Pea kuo hoko ho lou‘ulú ‘o hinehina ‘aupito;
Pea neongo ia ʻokú ke tuʻu taʻetuku ʻi ho ʻulú—
ʻOkú ke pehē, ʻi ho taʻú, ʻoku totonu ia?”

“ʻI heʻeku kei talavoú,” ko e tali ia ʻa e Tamai Uiliami ki hono fohá, .
“Naʻá ku manavasiʻi naʻa lavea ʻa e ʻutó;
Ka, ko ʻeni kuó u fakapapauʻi haohaoa ʻoku ʻikai haʻaku taha, .
He, ʻoku ou toutou fai ia.”

“Kuó ke motu‘a,” ko e lau ia ‘a e talavoú, “hangē ko ia na‘á ku lave ki ai ki mu‘á, .
Pea kuo tupu ʻo ngako taʻe angamaheni taha;
Ka naʻá ke fakatafoki ʻi ha tuʻa-somersault ʻi he matapaá—
Lotu, ko e hā e ʻuhinga ʻo e meʻa ko iá?”

“ʻI heʻeku kei talavoú,” ko e lea ia ʻa e tangata potó, ʻi heʻene lulululu hono ngaahi loka hina, .
“Na‘á ku tauhi hoku ongo va‘é kotoa ke fu‘u fefeka .
‘I hono ngāue‘aki ‘a e lolo ko ení—ko e silini ‘e taha ‘a e kató—
Tuku mai ke u fakatau atu ha ongo me‘a mali kiate koe?”

“Kuo ke motu‘a,” ko e lau ia ‘a e talavoú, “pea ‘oku fu‘u vaivai ho ngaahi kavá .
Mo ha meʻa ʻoku fefeka ange ʻi he suet;
Ka neongo ia naʻá ke fakaʻosi ʻa e kusí, mo e ngaahi huí mo e ngutú—
Lotu, naʻá ke lava fēfē ʻo fai iá?”

“‘I he‘eku kei talavoú,” ko e lea ia ‘a ‘ene tamaí, “na‘á ku ‘alu ki he laó, .
Pea fakakikihi ʻi he keisi takitaha mo hoku uaifí;
Pea ko e mālohi ‘o e uouá, ‘a ia na‘á ne ‘oange ki hoku kavá, .
Kuo tolonga ‘i he toenga ‘o ‘eku mo‘uí.”

“Kuó ke motu‘a,” ko e lau ia ‘a e talavoú, “‘e ‘ikai meimei ke fakakaukau ha taha .
Ko ho matá naʻe tuʻu maʻu ʻo hangē ko ia ne faʻa hokó;
Ka naʻá ke fakapalanisi ha ʻeiki ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻo ho ihú—
Ko e hā naʻá ne ʻai koe ke ke fuʻu poto fakalilifú?”

“Kuó u tali ha fehuʻi ʻe tolu, pea ʻoku feʻunga ia,”
Naʻe pehē ʻe heʻene tamaí; “ʻoua naʻá ke ʻoatu ha ngaahi ʻea kiate koe!
ʻOkú ke pehē te u lava ʻo fanongo ʻi he ʻaho kakató ki he ngaahi meʻa peheé?
Be off, pe te u teke hifo koe ʻi he sitepú!”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke leaʻaki totonu ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

“ʻOku ʻikai fuʻu tonu, ʻoku ou manavasiʻi,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻi heʻene mā; “kuo liliu ‘a e ni‘ihi ‘o e ngaahi leá.”

“ʻOku hala ia mei he kamataʻangá ki he ngataʻangá,” ko e lea fakapapau ia ʻa e Katá, pea naʻe fakalongolongo ʻi ha ngaahi miniti.

Ko e Katepilá naʻá ne fuofua lea.

“Ko e hā e lahi ʻokú ke fie hoko ki aí?” ko e fehuʻí ia.

“ʻOiauē, ʻoku ʻikai ke u pau ʻi he lahí,” ko e tali fakavavevave ia ʻa ʻAlisí; “ko e tokotaha pē ʻoku ʻikai saiʻia ke faʻa liliú, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi.”

“ ʻOku ʻikai ke u ʻilo,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá.

Naʻe ʻikai ke lea ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi ha meʻa: naʻe teʻeki ai ke fuʻu fepaki lahi pehē ʻi heʻene moʻuí kimuʻa, pea naʻá ne ongoʻi ʻoku mole ʻene ʻitá.

“ʻOkú ke fiemālie he taimí ni?” ko e lau ia ʻa e Katá.

“Sai, ʻoku totonu ke u saiʻia ke kiʻi lahi ange, tangataʻeiki, kapau ʻoku ʻikai ke ke tokanga,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí: “ko e ʻinisi ʻe tolú ko ha māʻolunga fakaʻofa ia ke ʻi ai.”

“Ko ha mā‘olunga lelei mo‘oni ia!” ko e lau ia ʻa e ʻAluʻi ʻItá ʻi heʻene ʻitá, ʻo ne hiki hake ia ʻo tuʻu hangatonu ʻi heʻene leá (naʻe ʻinisi ʻe tolu tonu hono māʻolungá).

“Ka ʻoku ʻikai ke u anga ki ai!” ko e kole ia ʻa ʻAlisi masivá ʻi ha leʻo fakaʻofa. Pea naʻá ne fakakaukau pē kiate ia, “ʻOku ou fakaʻamu ke ʻoua naʻa ʻita faingofua pehē ʻa e ngaahi meʻamoʻuí!”

“Te ke maheni mo ia ʻi ha taimi,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Katá; pea na‘á ne ‘ai ‘a e hookah ki hono ngutú ‘o ne toe kamata ke ifi.

ʻI he taimi ko ʻení naʻe tatali ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he faʻa kātaki kae ʻoua kuó ne fili ke toe lea. ʻI ha miniti ʻe taha pe ua naʻe toʻo ʻe he ʻUnuʻuá ʻa e hookah mei hono ngutú ʻo ne ʻuʻulu tuʻo taha pe tuʻo ua, peá ne lulululu ʻa ia tonu. Pea naʻá ne hifo mei he musié, ʻo totolo atu ʻi he mohukú, ʻo ne fakatokangaʻi pē ʻi heʻene ʻalú, “ʻE ʻai koe ʻe he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá ke ke tupu ʻo māʻolunga ange, pea ʻe ʻai koe ʻe he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá ke ke tupu ʻo nounou ange.”

“Ko e tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo e hā? Ko e tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo e hā? ” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻiate ia pē.

“ʻO e musie,” ko e lea ia ʻa e ʻUnuʻuá, ʻo hangē pē ko ʻene ʻeke leʻolahi iá; pea ʻi ha momeniti ʻe taha naʻe ʻikai ke toe sio ki ai.

Naʻe kei sio fakakaukau pē ʻa ʻAlisi ki he musié ʻi ha miniti ʻe taha, ʻo feinga ke ʻiloʻi pe ko e fē ʻa e ongo tafaʻaki ʻo e musié; pea ʻi heʻene takai haohaoá, naʻá ne ʻiloʻi ko ha fehuʻi faingataʻa ʻaupito ʻeni. Ka neongo ia, naʻe faifai peá ne mafao atu hono ongo nimá ʻo takai ʻi ai ʻo aʻu ki he mamaʻo taha te na ʻalu ki aí, peá ne motuhi ha konga ʻo e tafaʻakí ʻaki hono ongo nimá takitaha.

“Pea ko ʻeni ko fē ʻoku fē?” naʻá ne pehē kiate ia pē, peá ne kiʻi ngungu ʻa e konga ʻo e nima toʻomataʻú ke ʻahiʻahiʻi ʻa e olá: ʻi he momeniti hono hokó naʻá ne ongoʻi ha tā fakamālohi ʻi lalo ʻi hono ʻaofinimá: kuo taaʻi ia ʻe hono vaʻé!

Naʻá ne fuʻu ilifia ʻaupito ʻi he liliu fakafokifā ʻaupito ko ʻení, ka naʻá ne ongoʻi ʻoku ʻikai ha taimi ke mole, he naʻá ne vave ʻene holo hifo; ko ia naʻá ne kamata ngāue leva ke kai ha niʻihi ʻo e konga ʻe tahá. Naʻe fuʻu lomiʻi ofi hono ʻaofinimá ki hono vaʻé, ʻo ʻikai ke ʻi ai ha feituʻu ke fakaava ai hono ngutú; ka naʻá ne fai ia ʻi he fakaʻosí, peá ne lava ʻo folo ha konga ʻo e konga toʻohemá.

“Haʻu, kuo faifai pea tauʻatāina hoku ʻulú!” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi ha leʻo fiefia, ʻa ia naʻe liliu ia ʻo manavasiʻi ʻi ha momeniti ʻe taha, ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻikai ha feituʻu ʻe maʻu ai hono ongo umá: ko e meʻa pē naʻá ne lava ʻo sio ki aí, ʻi heʻene sio hifo, ko ha fuʻu lōloa ʻo e kiá, ʻa ia naʻe hangē naʻe ʻalu hake ʻo hangē ha foʻi ʻakaú mei ha tahi ʻo e ngaahi lauʻiʻakau lanu mata naʻe tokoto mamaʻo ʻi lalo ʻiate ia.

“Ko e hā ʻe lava ke hoko ʻa e meʻa lanu mata kotoa ko iá?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí. “Pea kuo aʻu ki fē hoku ongo umá?Pea ʻoiauē, hoku ongo nima masivá, ʻoku fēfē ʻeku ʻikai lava ʻo sio kiate koé?” Naʻá ne hiki holo kinaua ʻi heʻene leá, ka naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai ha ola ʻe muimui mai, tukukehe pē ha kiʻi lulululu ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi lauʻiʻakau lanu mata mamaʻó.

ʻI he hangē naʻe ʻikai ha faingamālie ke ʻohake hono ongo nimá ki hono ʻulú, naʻá ne feinga ke ʻohake hono ʻulú kiate kinaua, pea naʻá ne fiefia ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi ʻe punou holo faingofua hono kiá ʻi ha faʻahinga tafaʻaki pē, ʻo hangē ha ngatá. Naʻá ne toki lavameʻa ʻi hono fakapiko hifo ia ʻo hoko ko ha zigzag fakaʻofoʻofa, pea naʻá ne ʻalu ke lue atu ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi laú, ʻa ia naʻá ne ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻikai ko ha meʻa kehe ka ko e tumutumu ʻo e ngaahi ʻakau naʻá ne ʻeveʻeva holo aí, ʻi he taimi naʻe ʻai ai ʻe ha ʻuʻulu māsila ke ne toʻo fakavavevave ki mui: naʻe lele mai ha fuʻu lupe lahi ki hono fofongá, pea naʻá ne taaʻi fakamālohi ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi w its.

“Ngata!” ko e kaila ia ʻa e Lupé.

“ʻOku ʻikai ko ha ngata au!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí ʻi he ʻitá. “Tuku pē au!”

“Ngata, ʻoku ou toe lea atu!” ko e toe lea ia ʻa e Lupé, ka ʻi ha leʻo fakavaivai ange, peá ne tānaki atu ʻi ha faʻahinga tangi, “Kuó u feinga ʻi he founga kotoa pē, pea ʻoku hangē ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa ʻe feʻunga mo kinauá!”

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe ʻAlisi, “ʻOku ʻikai haʻaku kiʻi fakakaukau ki he meʻa ʻokú ke talanoa ki aí.”

“Kuó u ʻahiʻahiʻi ʻa e ngaahi aka ʻo e ʻuluʻakaú, pea kuó u ʻahiʻahiʻi ʻa e ngaahi veʻe vaí, pea kuó u ʻahiʻahiʻi ʻa e ngaahi ʻā,” ko e hoko atu ia ʻa e Lupé, ʻo ʻikai ke ne tokanga kiate ia; “ka ko e fanga ngata ko iá! ʻOku ʻikai ha meʻa ke fakafiefiaʻi kinautolu!”

Naʻe fakaʻau ke toe puputuʻu ange ʻa ʻAlisi, ka naʻá ne fakakaukau ʻoku ʻikai ha ʻaonga ia ke toe lea ʻaki ha meʻa kae ʻoua kuo ʻosi ʻa e Lupé.

“Hangē naʻe ʻikai ke faingataʻa feʻunga hono foʻi ʻo e fuaʻimoá,” ko e lau ia ʻa e Lupé; “ka kuo pau ke u ‘ā‘ā ki he fanga ngatá ‘i he pō mo e ‘aho: ‘io, kuo te‘eki ai ke u mohe ‘i he uike ‘e tolu ko ení!”

“ʻOku ou fakameʻapangoʻia ʻaupito ʻi hoʻo fakaʻitaʻí,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisi, ʻa ia naʻe kamata ke ne sio ki hono ʻuhingá.

“Pea hangē pē ko ʻeku toʻo ʻa e ʻakau māʻolunga taha ʻi he vaotaá,” ko e hoko atu ia ʻa e Lupé, ʻo ne hiki hake hono leʻó ki ha kaila, “pea hangē pē ko ʻeku fakakaukau ʻoku totonu ke u tauʻatāina meiate kinautolu ʻi he fakaʻosí, kuo pau pē ʻoku nau fie maʻu ke nau haʻu ʻo luelue hifo mei he langí! ʻUh, Ngata!”

“Ka ʻoku ʻikai ko ha ngata au, ʻoku ou tala atu!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí. “Ko au ko ha—ko au ko ha—”

“Sai! Ko e hā koe?” ko e lau ia ʻa e Lupé. “ʻOku ou lava ʻo sio ʻokú ke feinga ke faʻu ha meʻa!”

“Ko au —ko ha kiʻi taʻahine au,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻo ʻikai fuʻu veiveiua, ʻi heʻene manatuʻi e lahi ʻo e ngaahi liliu naʻá ne foua ʻi he ʻaho ko iá.

“Ko ha talanoa ngalingali moʻoni!” ko e lea ia ʻa e Lupé ʻi ha leʻo ʻo e taʻetokaʻi loloto tahá. “Kuó u sio ki ha fanga kiʻi taʻahine tokolahi lelei ʻi hoku taimí, ka ʻoku teʻeki ai ha taha ʻoku ʻi ai hono kia pehē! ʻIkai, ʻikai! Ko ha ngata koe; pea ʻoku ʻikai ha ʻaonga ke fakaʻikaiʻi ia. ʻOku ou mahalo te ke talamai kiate au ʻi he hoko maí naʻe ʻikai ke ke teitei ʻahiʻahiʻi ha fuaʻimoa!"

“ Kuó u ʻahiʻahiʻi ʻa e fuaʻimoá, ʻoku pau,” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisi, ʻa ia ko ha kiʻi tamasiʻi moʻoni ʻaupito; “ka ʻoku kai fuaʻimoa ʻa e fanga kiʻi taʻahiné ʻo hangē pē ko e fanga ngatá, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u tui ki ai,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Lupé; “ka ʻo kapau te nau fai ia, ko e hā leva ʻoku nau hoko ai ko ha faʻahinga ngata, ko e meʻa pē ia te u lava ʻo lea ʻakí.”

Ko ha fakakaukau foʻou ʻeni kia ʻAlisi, ʻo ne fuʻu fakalongolongo ʻaupito ʻi ha miniti ʻe taha pe ua, ʻa ia naʻe ʻoange ai ki he Lupé ʻa e faingamālie ke ne tānaki atu, “ʻOkú ke kumi fuaʻimoa, ʻoku ou ʻiloʻi lelei feʻunga ia ; pea ko e hā hono mahuʻinga kiate au pe ko ha kiʻi taʻahine koe pe ko ha ngata?”

“ʻOku mahuʻinga lahi ia kiate au ,” ko e lea fakavavevave ia ʻa ʻAlisí; “ka ʻoku ʻikai te u kumi ki ha fuaʻimoa, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku hokó; pea kapau naʻá ku ʻi ai, ʻoku ʻikai totonu ke u fie maʻu hoʻo meʻa : ʻoku ʻikai ke u saiʻia ʻi heʻenau mata.”

“Sai, ke ke ʻalu leva!” ko e lau ia ʻa e Lupé ʻi ha leʻo fakaʻofa, ʻi heʻene toe nofo hifo ki hono ʻuhikí. Naʻe punou hifo ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ʻuluʻakaú ʻi he lelei taha te ne lavá, he naʻe kei lavelavea pē hono kiá ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi vaʻá, pea ʻi he taimi kotoa pē naʻe pau ke ne tuʻu ʻo vete ia. Hili ha taimi nounou mei ai naʻá ne manatuʻi ʻokú ne kei puke pē ʻa e ngaahi konga ʻo e musié ʻi hono ongo nimá, peá ne kamata ngāue fakalelei ʻaupito, ʻo ne ʻuluaki ngungu ʻi he taha pea toki ʻi he taha, pea tupulaki ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻo lōloa ange pea ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻo nounou ange, kae ʻoua kuó ne lavameʻa ʻi hono ʻomi ia ki lalo ki hono māʻolunga angamahení.

Naʻe fuoloa ʻaupito ʻene ʻikai ke ne hoko ko ha meʻa ʻoku ofi ki he lahi totonú, ʻo ongoʻi kehe ʻaupito ʻi he kamataʻangá; ka naʻá ne maheni mo ia ʻi ha ngaahi miniti siʻi pē, pea kamata ke ne talanoa pē kiate ia, ʻo hangē ko e angamahení. “Haʻu, kuo ʻosi ʻa e vaeua ʻo ʻeku palaní he taimí ni! ʻOku fakatupu puputuʻu moʻoni ʻa e ngaahi liliu kotoa ko ʻení! ʻOku ʻikai ke u teitei fakapapauʻi pe ko e hā te u hoko ki aí, mei he miniti ʻe taha ki he miniti ʻe taha! Ka neongo ia, kuó u foki ki hoku lahi totonú: ko e meʻa hono hokó, ke u hū ki he ngoue fakaʻofoʻofa ko iá —ʻe anga fēfē hano fai ia, ʻoku ou fifili?” ʻI heʻene lea ʻaki ʻení, naʻá ne aʻu fakafokifā ki ha feituʻu ʻataʻatā, ʻoku ʻi ai ha kiʻi fale ʻi ai ʻoku fute ʻe fā nai hono māʻolungá. “Ko hai pē ʻoku nofo aí,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, “he ʻikai ʻaupito ke ʻaonga ke hoko mai kiate kinautolu ʻi he lahi ko ʻení : ko e hā, ʻoku totonu ke u fakailifiaʻi kinautolu mei honau ʻatamaí!” Ko ia naʻá ne kamata ke toe ngungu ʻi he foʻi ʻuhikiʻi nima toʻomataʻú, pea naʻe ʻikai ke ne loto-toʻa ke ofi ki he falé kae ʻoua kuó ne ʻomi ia ki lalo ki he ʻinisi ʻe hiva hono māʻolungá.
