KO E VAHE VIII.
Ko e Kelekele-Koloketi 'a e Kuini

Naʻe tuʻu ha fuʻu ʻakau lose ofi ki he hūʻanga ʻo e ngoué: ko e ngaahi lose naʻe tupu aí naʻe hinehina, ka naʻe ʻi ai ha kau ngoue ʻe toko tolu ʻi ai, ʻo nau femoʻuekina ʻi hono vali kulokula kinautolu. Naʻe fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi ko ha meʻa fakatupu ʻilo lahi ʻeni, peá ne ofi ange ʻo siofi kinaua, pea ʻi heʻene haʻu pē kiate kinauá naʻá ne fanongo ki ha taha ʻo kinaua ʻokú ne pehē mai, “Tokanga mai he taimí ni, Nima! ʻOua naʻá ke ʻalu ʻo lulululu vali ʻiate au ʻo pehē!”

“Naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo mapukepuke ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa Nimá, ʻi ha leʻo fakaʻofa; “Naʻe lele ʻa e fitú ʻi hoku tuliki.”

ʻA ia naʻe sio hake ʻa Fitu ʻo pehē ange, “ʻOku moʻoni ia, Nima!Tuku maʻu pē ʻa e tukuakiʻí ki he niʻihi kehé!”

“ Sai ange ke ʻoua naʻá ke talanoa!” ko e lau ia ʻa Nimá. “Na’a ku fanongo ki he lea ‘a e Kuini ‘aneafi pe na’e taau ke tu’usi ho ‘ulu!”

“Ko e hā?” ko e lau ia ‘a e tokotaha na‘e ‘uluaki leá.

“ʻOku ʻikai ko haʻo meʻa ia , Ua!” ko e lau ia ʻa Fitú.

“ʻIo, ko ʻene meʻa ia!” ko e lau ia ʻa Nimá, “pea te u talaange kiate ia —naʻe fai ia ki hono ʻomi ʻo e tulip-roots ʻa e kuká kae ʻikai ko e ʻonioní.”

Naʻe lī hifo ʻe Seveni ʻene polosí, pea naʻá ne toki kamata “Sai, ʻi he ngaahi meʻa taʻetotonu kotoa pē—” ʻi he taimi naʻe tō fakatuʻupakē ai hono matá kia ʻAlisi, ʻi heʻene tuʻu ʻo siofi kinautolú, peá ne vakaiʻi fakafokifā ia: naʻe sio takai foki mo e niʻihi kehé, pea nau punou hifo kotoa.

“Te ke talamai,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisi, ʻi heʻene kiʻi mā, “ko e hā ʻokú ke valivali ai ʻa e ngaahi lose ko iá?”

Naʻe ʻikai ke lea ʻa Nima mo Fitu, ka naʻá na sio pē kia Ua. Naʻe kamata leʻo siʻi ʻa e toko uá, “Ko e hā ʻa e moʻoní, ʻokú ke sio, Misa, naʻe totonu ke hoko ʻeni ʻi heni ko ha fuʻu lose kulokula , pea naʻá ma ʻai ha taha hinehina ʻi ha fehālaaki; pea kapau naʻe ʻiloʻi ia ʻe he Kuinií, ʻoku totonu ke motuhi kotoa homau ʻulú, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi. Ko ia ʻokú ke sio, Misa, ʻoku mau fai homau lelei tahá ʻi he momeniti ko iá, ʻa ia naʻe ʻosi, ʻi heʻene sio hohaʻa ki he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo e ngoué, ʻo ne ui mai “Ko e Kuini! Ko e Kuini!” pea naʻe tō leva ʻa e kau ngoue ʻe toko tolú ʻi honau fofongá. Naʻe ʻi ai ha leʻo ʻo e ngaahi laʻivaʻe lahi, pea naʻe sio takai ʻa ʻAlisi, ʻo vēkeveke ke sio ki he Kuini.

Na‘e ‘uluaki ha‘u ha kau sōtia ‘e toko hongofulu ‘oku nau to‘o ‘a e ngaahi fu‘u ‘akau; na‘e fōtunga kotoa ‘a e ngaahi me‘á ni ‘o hangē ko e kau ngoue ‘e toko tolú, ‘o fōlahi mo fōlahi, ‘o ‘i he ngaahi tuliki honau ongo nimá mo e va‘é: hoko atu ai ‘a e kau ‘eiki ‘e toko hongofulu; naʻe teuteuʻi kotoa ʻa e ngaahi meʻá ni ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi taimani, pea naʻa nau lue ua mo ua, ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe fai ʻe he kau sōtiá. Hili kinautolu naʻe haʻu ʻae fānau fakatuʻi; naʻa nau toko hongofulu, pea naʻe haʻu ʻa e fanga kiʻi ʻofaʻangá ʻo puna fiefia ʻo nima-homo, ʻi he ngaahi hoa mali: naʻa nau teuteuʻi kotoa ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi lotó. Naʻe hoko atu ʻa e kau fakaafé, ko e tokolahi tahá ko e kau Tuʻi mo e kau Kuini, pea ʻi honau lotolotongá naʻe ʻiloʻi ʻe ʻAlisi ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina: naʻá ne talanoa ʻi ha founga fakavavevave ʻo e manavasiʻí, ʻo ne malimali ʻi he meʻa kotoa pē naʻe leaʻakí, pea ʻalu atu ʻo ʻikai ke ne fakatokangaʻi ia. Pea naʻe muimui mai ʻa e Knave of Hearts, ʻo ne ʻave ʻa e kalauni ʻo e Tuʻí ʻi ha funga veliveli kulokula; pea, fakamuimuitaha 'i he fononga lahi ko 'eni, na'e ha'u 'a e TU'I MO E KUINI 'O E NGAAHI LOTO.

Naʻe kiʻi veiveiua ʻa ʻAlisi pe ʻoku ʻikai totonu ke ne tokoto hifo ʻo hangē ko e kau ngoue ʻe toko tolú, ka naʻe ʻikai ke ne manatuʻi haʻane fanongo ki ha tuʻutuʻuni pehē ʻi he ngaahi fonongaʻangá; “pea ʻikai ngata aí, ko e hā ʻa e ʻaonga ʻo ha fonongaʻanga,” ko ʻene fakakaukaú ia, “kapau ʻe tokoto kotoa ʻa e kakaí ʻi honau fofongá, ke ʻoua naʻa nau lava ʻo sio ki ai?” Ko ia naʻa ne tuʻu maʻu ʻi he potu naʻa ne ʻi ai, ʻo tatali.

‘I he ha’u ‘a e fononga’anga ‘o fehangahangai mo ‘Alisi, na’a nau tu’u kotoa ‘o sio kiate ia, pea na’e lea fefeka ‘a e Kuini “Ko hai ‘eni?” Naʻá ne lea ʻaki ia ki he Knave ʻo e Ngaahi Lotó, ʻa ia naʻá ne punou pē mo malimali ʻo tali mai.

"Vale!" ko e lau ia ʻa e Kuinií, ʻo ne luelue taʻekātaki hono ʻulú; pea, ʻi heʻene tafoki kia ʻAlisí, naʻá ne hoko atu, “Ko hai ho hingoá, tamasiʻi?”

“Ko hoku hingoá ko ʻAlisi, ko ia kātaki ʻa hoʻo ʻAfió,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he anga fakaʻapaʻapa ʻaupito; ka naʻá ne tānaki mai, ʻiate ia pē, “Ko e hā, ko ha puha kaati pē kinautolu, hili iá. ʻOku ʻikai fie maʻu ke u manavasiʻi kiate kinautolu!”

“Pea ko hai ‘eni? ” ko e lea ia ‘a e Kuini, ‘o ne tuhu ki he kau ngoue ‘e toko tolu na’a nau tokoto takai ‘i he ‘akau lose; he, ʻoku mou sio, ʻi heʻenau tokoto ʻi honau fofongá, pea ko e sīpinga ʻi honau tuʻá naʻe tatau pē ia mo e toenga ʻo e kulupú, naʻe ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo tala pe ko ha kau ngoue kinautolu, pe ko ha kau sōtia, pe ko ha kau ʻeikitau, pe ko ha toko tolu ʻo ʻene fānau tonu.

“ʻOku totonu ke u ʻilo fēfē?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻo ne ʻohovale ʻi heʻene loto-toʻa pē ʻaʻaná. “ʻOku ʻikai ko haʻaku pisinisi ia .”

Naʻe kulokula ʻa e Kuini ʻi heʻene ʻitá, pea hili ʻene sio fakamamaʻu kiate ia ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti ʻo hangē ha manu fekaí, naʻá ne kaila “ʻOff mo hono ʻulú! ʻOff—”

“Meʻa valea!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻo fuʻu leʻolahi mo fakapapauʻi, pea naʻe fakalongolongo ʻa e Kuinií.

Naʻe hilifaki ʻe he Tuʻí hono nimá ki hono umá, peá ne pehē fakamā “Fakakaukau, ʻeku ʻofaʻanga: ko ha kiʻi tamasiʻi pē ia!”

Na’e tafoki ‘ita ‘a e Kuini meiate ia, ‘o ne pehe ki he Knave “Tafoki kinaua!”

Naʻe fai ia ʻe he Knave, ʻo fuʻu tokanga ʻaupito, ʻaki ʻene vaʻe ʻe taha.

"Tu'u ki 'olunga!" ko e lea ia ʻa e Kuini, ʻi ha leʻo ngingila mo leʻolahi, pea puna hake leva ʻa e kau ngoue ʻe toko tolú, ʻo nau kamata punou ki he Tuʻí, ki he Kuini, mo e fānau fakatuʻí, mo e kakai kotoa pē.

“Tuku pē ia!” ko e kaila ia ʻa e Kuinií. “ʻOkú ke ʻai au ke u ngingila.” Pea hili iá, ʻi heʻene tafoki ki he ʻakau-losé, naʻá ne hoko atu, “Ko e hā naʻá ke fai ʻi hení?”

“ʻOfa ke hōifua ki hoʻo ʻAfió,” ko e lea ia ʻa Tō, ʻi ha leʻo fakatōkilalo ʻaupito, ʻo ne tūʻulutui ʻi he tuliki ʻe taha ʻi heʻene leá, “naʻá ma feinga—”

" Oku ou sio!" ko e lau ia ʻa e Kuini, ʻa ia naʻá ne sivisiviʻi ʻa e ngaahi losé ʻi he taimi ko iá. “Off mo honau ʻulú!” pea hoko atu ʻa e fonongaʻangá, ʻo kei nofo pē ha toko tolu ʻo e kau sōtiá ke fakapoongi ʻa e kau ngoue fakaʻofaʻiá, ʻa ia naʻa nau lele kia ʻAlisi ke maʻu ha maluʻi.

“ʻE ʻikai ke tuʻusi ho ʻulú!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, peá ne ʻai kinaua ki ha fuʻu ipu matalaʻiʻakau naʻe tuʻu ofi mai. Naʻe ʻeveʻeva holo ʻa e kau sōtia ʻe toko tolú ʻi ha miniti ʻe taha pe ua, ʻo kumi kinautolu, pea nau toki laka fakalongolongo atu ʻo muimui ʻi he niʻihi kehé.

“Kuo motu honau ʻulú?” ko e kaila ia ʻa e Kuinií.

“Kuo mole honau ‘ulú, kapau ‘oku hōifua ki ho‘o ‘Afió!” ko e kaila ia ʻa e kau sōtiá ʻo tali.

“ʻOku tonu ia!” ko e kaila ia ʻa e Kuinií. “Te ke lava ʻo vaʻinga koloketi?”

Naʻe fakalongolongo pē ʻa e kau sōtiá, pea nau sio kia ʻAlisi, he ʻoku mahino naʻe ʻuhinga ʻa e fehuʻí kiate ia.

"ʻIo!" ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Haʻu leva!” ko e ngungulu ia ʻa e Kuinií, pea kau atu ʻa ʻAlisi ki he fonongaʻangá, ʻo ne fifili lahi pe ko e hā ʻe hokó.

“Ko e﻿—ko ha ʻaho lelei ʻaupito ia!” ko e lea ia ʻa ha leʻo mā ʻi hono tafaʻakí. Naʻá ne lue atu ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e Lāpisi Hinehina, ʻa ia naʻá ne sio fakamamaʻu hohaʻa ki hono fofongá.

“ʻOku fuʻu lahi,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí: “—ʻoku ʻi fē ʻa e Duchess?”

“Fakalongolongo! Fakalongolongo!” ko e lea ia ʻa e Lāpití ʻi ha leʻo māʻulalo mo fakavavevave. Naʻá ne sio hohaʻa ʻi hono umá ʻi heʻene leá, peá ne toki hiki hake ia ʻi hono tumuʻakí, ʻo ʻai hono ngutú ke ofi ki hono telingá, peá ne muhumuhu mai “ʻOkú ne ʻi he malumalu ʻo e tautea fakapoongi.”

“Ko e hā?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Naʻá ke pehē ‘Ko e meʻa fakaʻofa moʻoni!’?” ko e fehuʻi ia ʻa e Lāpití.

“‘Ikai, na‘e ‘ikai te u fai ia,” ko e lau ia ‘a ‘Alisí: “‘Oku ‘ikai te u tui ‘oku ‘ikai ‘aupito ko ha me‘a faka‘ofa ia. Na‘á ku pehē ange ‘Ko e hā?’”

“Naʻá ne fuhu ʻa e telinga ʻo e Kuinií—” ko e kamata ia ʻa e Lāpití. Naʻe fai ʻe ʻAlisi ha kiʻi kaila ʻo e katá. “ʻOiauē, fakalongolongo!” ko e muhumuhu ia ʻa e Lāpití ʻi ha leʻo ilifia. “ʻE fanongo mai ʻa e Kuini kiate koe! ʻOkú ke sio, naʻá ne haʻu ʻo fuʻu tōmui, pea naʻe pehē ange ʻe he Kuini—”

“Mou aʻu ki homou ngaahi feituʻú!” ko e kaila ia ʻa e Kuini ʻi ha leʻo ʻo e manamana, pea kamata ke lele holo ʻa e kakaí ʻi he ngaahi tafaʻaki kotoa pē, ʻo nau feʻohofaki; ka neongo ia, naʻa nau nofo ʻi ha miniti ʻe taha pe ua, pea kamata leva ʻa e vaʻingá. Naʻe fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi kuo teʻeki ai ke ne sio ʻi ha malaʻe koloketi fie ʻilo pehē ʻi heʻene moʻuí; ko e ngaahi ʻotu mo e ngaahi ʻotu moʻunga kotoa pē; ko e ngaahi fo‘i pulú ko e fanga hedgehog mo‘ui, ko e ngaahi mallets ko e fanga flamingo mo‘ui, pea na‘e pau ke liunga ua ‘a e kau sōtiá pea ke nau tu‘u ‘i honau nimá mo honau va‘é, ke ngaohi ‘a e ngaahi ‘ākesí.

Ko e faingata'a tefito na'e ma'u 'e 'Alisi 'i he kamata'anga ko hono pule'i 'ene flamingo: na'a ne lavame'a 'i hono ma'u hono sino tucked 'o 'alu, fakafiemalie fe'unga, 'i lalo 'i hono nima, mo hono ongo va'e 'oku tautau hifo, ka 'i he angamaheni, hange pe ko ia na'a ne ma'u hono kia nicely straightened ki tu'a, pea na'e ' alu ke 'oange 'a e hedgehog ha fotunga 'o 'olunga mo ia mo e itlf . ko ha fakahaaʻi puputuʻu pehē naʻe ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo taʻofi ʻene kata: pea ʻi heʻene maʻu hono ʻulú ki lalo, pea naʻe ʻalu ke toe kamata, naʻe fakatupu ʻita ʻaupito ke ʻiloʻi kuo vete ʻe he hedgehog ia, pea naʻe ʻi he ngāue ʻo e totolo ʻo ʻalu: tukukehe ʻa e meʻa kotoa ko ʻení, naʻe ʻi ai ha ʻotu pe furrow ʻi he hala ʻi he feituʻu ke, mo e sheg ki he, naʻe tuʻu hake maʻu pē ʻa e kau sōtia ʻoku liunga ua ʻo lue atu ki he ngaahi feituʻu kehe ʻo e kelekelé, naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo aʻu ʻa ʻAlisi ki he fakamulituku ko ha vaʻinga faingataʻa ʻaupito moʻoni ia.

Naʻe vaʻinga kotoa ʻa e kau vaʻingá ʻi he taimi pē ʻe taha ʻo ʻikai ke nau tatali ki ha ngaahi taufetongi, ʻo nau fakakikihi ʻi he taimi kotoa ko iá, mo tauʻi ʻa e fanga kiʻi ʻuhikiʻi ʻuhikiʻi ʻuhikiʻi ʻuhikiʻi ʻuhikiʻi ʻuhikiʻi vaʻingá; pea ‘i ha taimi nounou ‘aupito na’e ‘i ha holi faka’ita ‘a e Kuini, pea ne ‘alu ‘o luelue holo, mo kaila “Off with his head!” pe “Off mo hono ‘ulú!” tuʻo taha nai ʻi he miniti.

Naʻe kamata ke ongoʻi taʻefiemālie ʻaupito ʻa ʻAlisi: ke fakapapauʻi, naʻe teʻeki ke ne maʻu ha fakakikihi mo e Kuini, ka naʻá ne ʻiloʻi ʻe ala hoko ia ʻi ha miniti pē, “pea hili iá,” naʻá ne fakakaukau, “ko e hā ʻe hoko kiate aú? ʻOku nau saiʻia ʻaupito ʻi hono tuʻusi ʻulu ʻo e kakaí ʻi hení; ko e meʻa fakaofo lahí, ʻoku ʻi ai ha taha ʻoku kei moʻui!"

Naʻá ne fekumi holo ki ha founga ke hola ai, pea fifili pe ʻe lava ke ne mavahe ʻo ʻikai ke sio kiate ia, ʻi heʻene fakatokangaʻi ha fōtunga fie ʻilo ʻi he ʻeá: naʻá ne fuʻu puputuʻu ʻaupito ʻi he ʻuluaki taimí, ka, hili ʻene sio ki ai ʻi ha miniti ʻe taha pe ua, naʻá ne ngaohi ia ke hoko ko ha malimali, peá ne pehē kiate ia pē “Ko e nobody Cashire to have tal.”

“ʻOku fēfē hoʻo leleí?” ko e lau ia ʻa e Pusí, ʻi heʻene ʻi ai pē ha ngutu feʻunga ke ne lea ʻakí.

Naʻe tatali ʻa ʻAlisi kae ʻoua kuo ʻasi mai ʻa e ongo matá, peá ne toki luelue atu. “ʻOku ʻikai hano ʻaonga ke lea ki ai,” ko ʻene fakakaukaú ia, “kae ʻoua kuo haʻu hono telingá, pe ʻikai toe siʻi hifo ʻi ha taha ʻo kinautolu.” ʻI ha miniti ʻe taha naʻe ʻasi mai ʻa e ʻulú kotoa, pea hili iá naʻe tuku hifo ʻe ʻAlisi ʻene flamingo, ʻo ne kamata ha fakamatala ki he vaʻingá, ʻo ne ongoʻi fiefia ʻaupito ʻi heʻene maʻu ha taha ke fanongo kiate iá. Naʻe hangē naʻe fakakaukau ʻa e Pusí ʻoku ʻi ai ha meʻa feʻunga ʻoku ʻasi mai he taimí ni, pea ʻoku ʻikai toe ʻasi mai ha meʻa ʻe taha.

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u tui ʻoku nau vaʻinga totonu ʻaupito,” ko e kamata ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻi ha leʻo lāunga, “pea ʻoku nau fakakikihi kotoa ʻo fuʻu fakalilifu ʻaupito ʻo ʻikai lava ʻe ha taha ʻo fanongo ki haʻane lea —pea ʻoku ʻikai ke nau ngali ʻi ai haʻanau ngaahi tuʻutuʻuni tautefito; Kuo pau ke u fou atu ʻi he hoko atu ʻo luelue holo ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻe taha ʻo e kelekelé —pea naʻe totonu ke u kolosi ʻa e ʻuhikiʻi manupuna ʻa e Kuinií he taimí ni pē, ka naʻá ne hola pē ʻi heʻene sio ki heʻeku haʻú!”

“ʻOku anga fēfē hoʻo saiʻia ʻi he Kuinií?” ko e lau ia ʻa e Pusí ʻi ha leʻo siʻi.

“ʻIkai ʻaupito,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí: “ʻokú ne fuʻu fuʻu lahi ʻaupito—” ʻI he taimi pē ko iá naʻá ne fakatokangaʻi ʻoku ofi ʻa e Kuini ʻi hono tuʻá, ʻo fanongo: ko ia naʻá ne hoko atu, “—ngalingali ʻe ikuna, ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu mahuʻinga ia lolotonga hono fakaʻosi ʻo e vaʻingá.”

Naʻe malimali ʻa e Kuinií ʻo ne paasi atu.

“Ko hai ʻokú ke talanoa ki aí?” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, ʻo ne ʻalu hake kia ʻAlisi, ʻo ne sio ki he ʻulu ʻo e Pusí ʻi he fie ʻilo lahi.

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe ʻĀlisi, “Ko haku kaungāmeʻa ia﻿—ko ha Pusi Cheshire: tuku ke u fakafeʻiloaki atu ia.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u fuʻu saiʻia ʻi heʻene fōtungá,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí: “ka neongo ia, te ne ʻuma nai ki hoku nimá kapau ʻokú ne saiʻia ai.”

“ʻOku sai ange ke ʻoua naʻá ku fai ia,” ko e fakatokanga ia ʻa e Pusí.

“ʻOua naʻá ke taʻefakaʻapaʻapa,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, “pea ʻoua naʻá ke sio pehē mai kiate au!” Naʻá ne ʻalu ʻi mui ʻia ʻAlisi ʻi heʻene leá.

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “ʻE sio nai ha pusi ki ha tuʻi.” “Kuó u lau ia ʻi ha tohi ʻe niʻihi, ka ʻoku ʻikai ke u manatuʻi pe naʻe ʻi fē.”

“Sai, kuo pau ke toʻo ia,” ko e lea fakapapau ia ʻa e Tuʻí, peá ne ui ʻa e Kuini, ʻa ia naʻe fakalaka atu ʻi he momeniti ko iá, “ʻE hoku ʻofaʻanga! ʻOku ou fakaʻamu ke ke toʻo ʻa e pusi ko ʻení!”

Na’e taha pe ‘a e founga ‘a e Kuini ke ne fakalelei’i ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi faingata’a kotoa pe, ‘o tatau ai pe pe ‘oku lahi pe si’isi’i. “Off mo hono ʻulú!” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻo ʻikai ke ne sio takai holo.

“Te u ʻomi pē ʻe au ʻa e tokotaha fakapoó,” ko e lea vēkeveke ia ʻa e Tuʻí, peá ne ʻalu fakavavevave atu.

Naʻe fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi mahalo ʻe sai pē ke ne foki, ʻo sio ki he anga ʻo e vaʻingá, ʻi heʻene fanongo ki he leʻo ʻo e Kuini ʻi he mamaʻó, ʻo kaila ʻi heʻene loto-māfaná. Naʻá ne ʻosi fanongo ki heʻene tauteaʻi ʻa e toko tolu ʻo e kau vaʻingá ke fakapoongi koeʻuhí he naʻa nau liʻaki honau taimí, pea naʻe ʻikai ke ne saiʻia ʻaupito ʻi he fōtunga ʻo e ngaahi meʻá, he naʻe fuʻu puputuʻu ʻaupito ʻa e vaʻingá ʻo ʻikai ke ne teitei ʻiloʻi pe ko hono taimí pe ʻikai. Ko ia na‘á ne ‘alu ‘o kumi ‘ene hetí.

Naʻe kau ʻa e hedgehog ʻi ha fuhu mo ha hedgehog ʻe taha, ʻa ia naʻe ngali kia ʻAlisi ko ha faingamālie lelei ʻaupito ia ki hono croqueting ʻo e taha ʻo kinaua mo e taha: ko e faingataʻa pē ʻe taha, ko ʻene flamingo naʻe ʻalu ia ʻo kolosi ki he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo e ngoué, ʻa ia naʻe lava ke sio ʻa ʻAlisi ki ai ʻoku feinga ʻi ha faʻahinga founga taʻe-malava ke lele hake ki ha ʻakau.

ʻI he taimi naʻá ne maʻu ai ʻa e flamingo ʻo fakafoki maí, kuo ʻosi ʻa e fuhú, pea kuo ʻikai ke toe sio ki he ongo hedgehog: “ka ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu mahuʻinga ia,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, “he kuo mole kotoa ʻa e ngaahi ʻākesí mei he tafaʻaki ko ʻeni ʻo e kelekelé.” Ko ia naʻá ne fufū ia ʻi lalo ʻi hono umá, ke ʻoua naʻa toe hola, peá ne foki atu ʻo toe kiʻi talanoa mo hono kaungāmeʻá.

ʻI heʻene foki ki he Pusi Cheshire, naʻá ne ʻohovale ʻi heʻene ʻilo ha fuʻu kakai tokolahi ʻoku nau fakatahataha mai ʻo takatakai ʻi aí: naʻe ʻi ai ha fakakikihi ʻoku lolotonga hoko ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo e tokotaha fakapoó, ko e Tuʻí, mo e Kuini, ʻa ia naʻa nau talanoa kotoa ʻi he taimi pē ʻe taha, ka ko e toenga kotoa pē naʻa nau fuʻu fakalongolongo, pea naʻe hā ngali taʻefiemālie ʻaupito.

Ko e momeniti pē naʻe hā mai ai ʻa ʻAlisí, naʻe tangi kiate ia ʻe he toko tolú kotoa ke ne fakaleleiʻi ʻa e fehuʻí, pea naʻa nau toe fakakikihi kiate ia, neongo ia, ʻi heʻenau lea kotoa ʻi he taimi pē ʻe taha, naʻe faingataʻa ʻaupito moʻoni ke ne ʻiloʻi tonu ʻa e meʻa naʻa nau lea ʻakí.

Ko e fakakikihi 'a e tokotaha fakapoongi, 'oku 'ikai lava ke ke motuhi ha 'ulu tukukehe kapau 'oku 'i ai ha sino ke motuhi mei ai: na'e te'eki ke ne fai ha me'a pehe kimu'a, pea 'oku 'ikai ke ne 'alu ke kamata 'i hono taimi 'o e mo'ui.

Ko e fakakikihi 'a e Tu'i, ko ha me'a pe 'oku 'i ai hono 'ulu 'e lava pe ke tu'usi, pea 'oku 'ikai totonu ke ke talanoa vale.

Ko e fakakikihi 'a e Kuini, kapau 'e 'ikai ke fai ha me'a ki ai 'i ha taimi si'isi'i ange 'e 'ikai ke ne 'ai ke fakapoongi 'a e tokotaha kotoa pe, all round. (Ko e lea fakaʻosi ko ʻení naʻá ne ʻai ʻa e paatí kotoa ke nau fuʻu mamafa mo hohaʻa.)

Naʻe ʻikai ke toe fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi ki ha meʻa kehe ke lea ʻaki ka ko e “ʻOku ʻa e Duchess ia: ʻoku sai ange ke ke ʻeke ange kiate ia fekauʻaki mo ia.”

“ʻOku ʻi he pilīsoné ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Kuini ki he tokotaha fakamāú: “ʻomi ia ki heni.” Pea na‘e ‘alu atu ‘a e tokotaha fakapoó ‘o hangē ha ngahaú.

Naʻe kamata ke mole atu ʻa e ʻulu ʻo e Pusí ʻi he momeniti pē naʻá ne ʻosi aí, pea, ʻi he taimi naʻá ne foki mai ai mo e Duchess, kuo mole fakaʻaufuli; ko ia naʻe lele fakavavevave ʻa e Tuʻí mo e tangata fakapoó ʻo kumi ia, kae foki ʻa e toenga ʻo e paatí ki he vaʻingá.
