KO E VAHE XI.
Ko Hai Naʻá Ne Kaihaʻasi ʻa e Ngaahi Tātí?

Naʻe tangutu ʻa e Tuʻi mo e Kuini ʻo e Ngaahi Lotó ʻi hona taloní ʻi heʻena aʻu maí, mo ha fuʻu kakai tokolahi naʻa nau fakatahataha mai ʻo takatakai ʻiate kinaua —ʻa e fanga kiʻi manupuna mo e fanga manu fekai kehekehe, pea pehē ki he puha kaati kotoa pē: naʻe tuʻu ʻa e Knave ʻi hona ʻaó, ʻi ha ngaahi seini, mo ha sōtia ʻi he ongo tafaʻakí ke leʻohi ia; pea ofi ki he Tuʻí naʻe ʻi ai ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina, mo ha talupite ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá, mo ha takainga tohi kiliʻimanu ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá. Naʻe ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e fakamaauʻangá ha tēpile, mo ha fuʻu ipu tāti ʻi ʻolunga: naʻe fuʻu lelei ʻaupito ʻenau fōtungá, ʻo ne ʻai ʻa ʻAlisi ke fuʻu fiekaia ʻaupito ke nau sio kiate kinautolu —“ʻOku ou fakaʻamu ke nau fakahoko ʻa e fakamaauʻangá,” ko ʻene fakakaukau ia, “pea ʻoatu ʻa e ngaahi meʻatokoni fakafiefiá!” Ka naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai ha faingamālie ʻo e meʻá ni, ko ia naʻá ne kamata ke vakai ki he meʻa kotoa pē fekauʻaki mo iá, ke fakalaka ʻa e taimí.

Naʻe teʻeki ai ke ʻi ha fakamaauʻanga fakamaauʻanga ʻa ʻAlisi kimuʻa, ka naʻá ne lau fekauʻaki mo kinautolu ʻi ha ngaahi tohi, pea naʻá ne fuʻu fiefia ʻaupito ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi naʻá ne ʻiloʻi e hingoa ʻo e meimei meʻa kotoa pē ʻi aí. Naʻá ne pehē loto pē, “Ko e fakamāú ia, koeʻuhí ko ʻene uiki lahí.”

Ko e fakamaau, ‘i he ‘uhinga, ko e Tu‘i; pea ʻi heʻene tui hono kalauní ʻi he ʻolunga ʻo e wig, (vakai ki he frontispiece kapau ʻokú ke fie sio ki he founga naʻá ne fai ai iá,) naʻe ʻikai ke ne sio ʻoku fuʻu fiemālie, pea naʻe pau pē naʻe ʻikai ke hoko ia.

“Pea ko e jury-box ia,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, “pea mo e ngaahi meʻamoʻui ʻe toko hongofulu mā ua ko iá,” (naʻe pau ke ne pehē “ngaahi meʻamoʻui,” ʻokú ke sio, koeʻuhí he ko e niʻihi ʻo kinautolú ko e fanga monumanu, pea ko e niʻihi ko e fanga manupuna,) “ʻOku ou mahalo ko e kau sulá kinautolu.” Naʻá ne leaʻaki tuʻo ua pe tolu ʻa e lea fakaʻosi ko ʻení kiate ia pē, ʻo ne ʻai ke polepole ʻi ai: he naʻá ne fakakaukau, pea ʻoku totonu foki, ʻoku tokosiʻi ʻaupito ʻa e fanga kiʻi taʻahine ʻi hono toʻú ʻoku nau ʻiloʻi ʻa e ʻuhinga ʻo e meʻa ko iá ʻi ha meʻa pē. Kae kehe, na‘e mei fai ‘e he “kau-fakamāú” ‘a e me‘a tatau pē.

Naʻe femoʻuekina ʻaupito kotoa ʻa e kau fakamāu ʻe toko hongofulu mā uá ʻi he ngaahi lauʻipepa. “Ko e hā ʻoku nau faí?” Naʻe muhumuhu ʻa ʻAlisi ki he Kalifoní. “ʻOku teʻeki ke nau lava ʻo maʻu ha meʻa ke tuku hifo, kimuʻa pea kamata ʻa e fakamaauʻangá.”

“ʻOku nau tuku hifo honau hingoá,” ko e muhumuhu ia ʻa e Kalifoní ʻi heʻene talí, “ʻi heʻenau manavasiʻi naʻa ngalo kinautolu kimuʻa pea toki ʻosi ʻa e ʻahiʻahí.”

“Ngaahi meʻa fakavalevale!” Naʻe kamata leʻo lahi mo ʻita ʻa ʻAlisi, ka naʻá ne tuʻu fakavavevave, he naʻe kaila ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina, “Fakalongolongo ʻi he lotoʻaá!” pea naʻe tui ʻe he Tuʻí ʻene sioʻatá ʻo ne sio takai holo ʻi he hohaʻa, ke ʻiloʻi pe ko hai ʻoku leá.

Naʻe lava ke sio ʻa ʻAlisi, pea pehē foki kapau naʻá ne sio ʻi honau umá, ʻoku tohi ʻe he kau fakamāú kotoa ʻa e “ngaahi meʻa fakavalevalé!” ʻi heʻenau ngaahi lauʻi peletí, pea naʻe aʻu ʻo ne lava ʻo ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻikai ʻilo ʻe ha taha ʻo kinaua ʻa e sipela ʻo e “vale,” pea kuo pau ke ne kole ki hono kaungāʻapí ke ne talaange ki ai. “Ko ha muddle malie ʻe ʻi ai ʻenau ngaahi slates kimuʻa pea toki ʻosi ʻa e fakamaauʻangá!” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

Naʻe ʻi ai ha penitala ʻe taha ʻo e kau fakamāú naʻe ngingila. Ko e moʻoni ʻeni, naʻe ʻikai lava ʻe ʻAlisi ʻo tuʻu, pea naʻá ne takai holo ʻi he fakamaauʻangá ʻo ne ʻalu ki hono tuʻá, pea naʻe vave ʻaupito ʻene maʻu ha faingamālie ke ʻave ia. Naʻá ne fai ia ʻo fuʻu vave ʻaupito ʻo ʻikai lava ʻe he kiʻi fakamāu masivá (ko Pili ia, ko e Lisi) ʻo ʻiloʻi ʻaupito ʻa e meʻa naʻe hoko ki aí; ko ia, hili ‘ene tulimanu kotoa ki aí, na‘e pau ke ne tohi ‘aki ‘a e louhi‘i nima ‘e taha ‘i he toenga ‘o e ‘ahó; pea naʻe siʻisiʻi ʻaupito ʻa e ʻaonga ʻo e meʻá ni, he naʻe ʻikai ke ne tuku ha fakaʻilonga ʻi he ʻaá.

“Hela, lau ‘a e tukuaki‘í!” Ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí.

‘I he me‘á ni na‘e ifi ai ‘e he Lāpisi Hinehina ‘a e ngaahi ifi ‘e tolu ‘i he talupité, peá ne toki vete ‘a e takainga tohi kili‘imanu, ‘o ne lau ‘o pehē:—

“Ko e Kuini ‘o e Ngaahi Lotó, na‘á ne ngaohi ha ngaahi tā, .
Ko e kotoa ʻi ha ʻaho ʻo e faʻahitaʻu māfaná:
Ko e Knave 'o e ngaahi Loto, na'a ne kaiha'asi 'a e ngaahi tart ko ia, .
Pea na‘á ne ‘ave kinautolu ‘o ‘osi!”

“Fakakaukau ki ho‘omou tu‘utu‘uní,” ko e lea ia ‘a e Tu‘í ki he kau fakamāú.

“ʻOku teʻeki, ʻoku teʻeki!” ko e fakalavea fakavavevave ia ʻa e Lāpití. “ʻOku ʻi ai ha meʻa lahi ʻe hoko mai kimuʻa ʻi he meʻa ko iá!”

“Ui ʻa e ʻuluaki fakamoʻoní,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí; pea ifi ʻe he Lāpisi Hinehina ʻa e talupite ʻe tolu, ʻo ne kalanga, “ʻUluaki fakamoʻoni!”

Ko e ʻuluaki fakamoʻoní ko e Hatter. Naʻá ne hū mai mo ha ipu tī ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá mo ha konga mā-mo-pata ʻi hono nima ʻe tahá. “ʻOku ou kole fakamolemole atu, hoʻo ʻAfió,” ko ʻene kamata ia, “ʻi hono ʻomi ʻo e ngaahi meʻá ni: ka naʻe teʻeki ke u ʻosi ʻaupito ʻeku tií ʻi he taimi naʻe fekauʻi mai ai aú.”

“Naʻe totonu ke ke ʻosi,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí. “Ko e fē taimi naʻá ke kamata aí?”

Naʻe sio atu ʻa e Tokotaha-Hēlusí ki he Hale ʻo Māʻasí, ʻa ia naʻá ne muimui mai ʻiate ia ki he lotoʻaá, ʻo ne nima-nima mo e Dormouse. Naʻá ne pehē mai, “Ko e ʻaho hongofulu mā fā ʻo Māʻasí, ʻoku ou pehē naʻe pehē.”

“Hongofulu mā nima,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Hale ʻo Māʻasí.

“Hongofulu mā ono,” ko e tānaki atu ia ʻa e Dormouse.

“Tohiʻi ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí ki he kau fakamāú, pea naʻe tohi vēkeveke ʻe he kau fakamāú ʻa e ngaahi ʻaho kotoa ʻe tolú ʻi heʻenau ngaahi lauʻipepa, pea nau toki tānaki atu ia, pea fakasiʻisiʻi ʻa e talí ki he silini mo e peni.

“Toʻo hoʻo hēkesí,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí ki he Tokotaha Ngāué.

“ʻOku ʻikai ʻaʻaku ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tufunga Pulú.

“ Kaiha‘asi! ” ko e kaila ia ‘a e Tu‘í, ‘o ne tafoki ki he kau fakamāú, ‘a ia na‘a nau fai leva ha fakamanatu ‘o e mo‘oní.

“ʻOku ou tauhi kinautolu ke fakatau atu,” ko e tānaki atu ia ʻa e Hatter ko ha fakamatala; “ʻOku ʻikai haʻaku meʻa pē ʻaʻaku. Ko ha tangata tufunga hēkesi au.”

Naʻe tui heni ʻe he Kuinií ʻene sioʻatá, pea kamata ke ne sio fakamamaʻu ki he Tokotaha-Hēlusí, ʻa ia naʻe liliu ʻo ngingila mo ngingila.

“‘Omai ho‘o fakamo‘oní,” ko e lea ia ‘a e Tu‘í; “pea ‘oua ‘e manavasi‘i, pe te u ‘ai ke fakapoongi koe ‘i he feitu‘u pē ko iá.”

Naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke fakalotolahiʻi ʻe he meʻá ni ʻa e fakamoʻoní: naʻá ne kei hiki pē mei he vaʻe ʻe tahá ki he vaʻe ʻe tahá, ʻo sio taʻefiemālie ki he Kuinií, pea ʻi heʻene puputuʻú naʻá ne uʻu ha konga lahi mei heʻene ipu tií kae ʻikai ko e mā-mo-pata.

ʻI he momeniti pē ko ʻení naʻe ongoʻi ʻe ʻAlisi ha ongoʻi fie ʻilo ʻaupito, ʻa ia naʻá ne fuʻu puputuʻu lahi kae ʻoua kuó ne ʻiloʻi pe ko e hā ia: naʻá ne kamata ke toe lahi ange, pea naʻá ne fakakaukau ʻi he ʻuluaki taimí te ne tuʻu hake ʻo mavahe mei he fakamaauʻangá; ka ʻi heʻene fakakaukau hono uá naʻá ne fakakaukau ke nofo pē ʻi he feituʻu naʻá ne ʻi aí ʻo kapau pē ʻe ʻi ai ha feituʻu maʻana.

“ʻOku ou fakaʻamu ke ʻoua naʻá ke fakaʻauʻauhifo pehē,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Dormouse, ʻa ia naʻe tangutu ʻi hono tafaʻakí. “ʻOku ʻikai ke u meimei lava ʻo mānava.”

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi ʻi he angavaivai ʻaupito, “ʻOku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo taʻofi ia: “ʻOku ou tupulaki.”

“ʻOku ʻikai haʻo totonu ke tupu heni ,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Dormouse.

“ʻOua naʻá ke lea noa,” ko e lea loto-toʻa ange ia ʻa ʻĀlisí: “ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi ʻokú ke tupulaki foki.”

“ ‘Io, ka ‘oku ou tupu ‘i ha vave ‘oku fe‘ungamālie,” ko e lau ia ‘a e Dormouse: “‘o ‘ikai ‘i he founga fakakata ko iá.” Pea naʻá ne tuʻu hake ʻo fuʻu sulkily ʻo kolosi atu ki he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo e lotoʻaá.

ʻI he taimi kotoa ko ʻení naʻe ʻikai ke tuku ʻe he Kuinií ʻene sio fakamamaʻu ki he Tokotaha-Hēlusí, pea, ʻi he kolosi pē ʻa e Dormouse ʻi he fakamaauʻangá, naʻá ne pehē ange ki he taha ʻo e kau ʻōfisa ʻo e fakamaauʻangá, “ʻOmi mai ʻa e lisi ʻo e kau hiva ʻi he koniseti fakaʻosí!” ʻa ia naʻe tetetete lahi ai ʻa e Hatter fakaʻofaʻiá, ʻo ne lulululu hono ongo sū fakatouʻosi.

“‘Omai ho‘o fakamo‘oní,” ko e toe ‘ita ia ‘a e Tu‘í, “pe te u ‘ai ke tāmate‘i koe, ‘o tatau ai pē pe ‘okú ke manavasi‘i pe ‘ikai.”

“Ko ha tangata masiva au, hoʻo ʻAfió,” ko e kamata ia ʻa e Tokotaha-Hēlesí, ʻi ha leʻo tetetete, “—pea naʻe teʻeki ai ke u kamata ʻeku tií —ʻikai ke laka hake ʻi ha uike ʻe taha pe lahi ange —pea ko e hā ʻa e meʻa ʻoku fuʻu manifi ʻaupito ʻa e mā-mo-patá —pea mo e ngingila ʻo e tií —”

“Ko e ngingila ʻo e meʻa? ” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí.

“Naʻe kamata ia ʻi he tií,” ko e tali ia ʻa e Tangataʻeikí.

“Ko e moʻoni ʻoku kamata ʻa e ngingilá ʻaki ha T!” ko e lea māsila ia ʻa e Tuʻí. “ʻOkú ke toʻo au ko ha vale? Hoko atu!”

“Ko ha tangata masiva au,” ko e hoko atu ia ʻa e Tangataʻeikí, “pea naʻe ngingila ʻa e lahi taha ʻo e ngaahi meʻá hili iá —ko e Hale pē ʻo Māʻasí naʻá ne pehē —”

“Naʻe ʻikai ke u fai ia!” ko e fakalavea ia ʻa e Hale Maʻasí ʻi ha fakavavevave lahi.

“Naʻá ke fai ia!” ko e lau ia ʻa e Tokotaha-Hulú.

“ʻOku ou fakaʻikaiʻi ia!” ko e lau ia ʻa e Hale ʻo Māʻasí.

“ʻOkú ne fakaʻikaiʻi ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí: “tukuange ʻa e konga ko iá.”

“Sai, ʻi ha faʻahinga tuʻunga pē, naʻe pehē ʻe he Dormouse—” ko e hoko atu ia ʻa e Hatter, ʻo ne sio takai hohaʻa ke vakai pe te ne fakaʻikaiʻi foki ia: ka naʻe ʻikai ke fakaʻikaiʻi ʻe he Dormouse ha meʻa, ʻi heʻene mohe vave.

“Hili iá,” ko e hoko atu ia ʻa e Tokotaha Tufunga Pulú, “naʻá ku toe kosi ha ngaahi mā-mo-pata —”

“Ka ko e hā naʻe lea ʻaki ʻe he Domousí?” ko e fehuʻi ia ʻa e taha ʻo e kau fakamāú.

“Ko e meʻa ko iá ʻoku ʻikai ke u manatuʻi,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tangataʻeikí.

“ Kuo pau ke ke manatu‘i,” ko e fakatokanga ia ‘a e Tu‘í, “pe te u ‘ai ke tāmate‘i koe.”

Naʻe tuku hifo ʻe he Hatter fakaʻofaʻiá ʻene ipu tií mo e mā-mo-pata, peá ne ʻalu hifo ʻi he tuliki ʻe taha. “Ko ha tangata masiva au, hoʻo ʻAfió,” ko ʻene kamata ia.

“Ko ha tokotaha lea masiva ʻaupito koe ,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí.

Na'e fiefia heni 'a e taha 'o e fanga ki'i puaka, pea na'e ta'ofi leva ia 'e he kau 'ofisa 'o e fakamaau'anga. (Koe'uhi ko e lea faingata'a 'aupito ia, te u fakamatala atu pe kiate kimoutolu 'a e founga na'e fai ai. Na'e 'i ai 'enau kato kaniva lahi, 'a ia na'e ha'i 'i he ngutu 'aki 'a e ngaahi afo: ki he me'a ni na'a nau fakahu 'a e puaka-kinini, 'ulu 'uluaki, pea nau toki tangutu ki ai.)

“ʻOku ou fiefia ʻi heʻeku sio ki hono fai iá,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí. “Kuó u faʻa lau ʻi he ngaahi nusipepá, ʻi he ʻosi ʻa e ngaahi fakamaauʻangá, “Naʻe ʻi ai ha feinga ke pakipaki, ʻa ia naʻe taʻofi leva ʻe he kau ʻōfisa ʻo e fakamaauʻangá,” pea naʻe ʻikai pē ke mahino kiate au hono ʻuhingá ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimí ni.”

“Kapau ko e meʻa pē ia ʻokú ke ʻilo ki aí, mahalo te ke tuʻu hifo,” ko e hoko atu ia ʻa e Tuʻí.

“He ʻikai ke u lava ʻo ʻalu ki lalo ange,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tokotaha-Hēlusí: “ʻOku ou ʻi he falikí, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku ʻi aí.”

“Pea ‘e lava ke ke tangutu hifo,” ko e tali ia ‘a e Tu‘í.

Naʻe fiefia heni ʻa e puaka-kiní ʻe tahá, pea naʻe taʻofi ia.

“Haʻu, naʻe ʻosi ʻa e fanga puaka-kiní!” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí. “Ko ʻeni te tau toe lelei ange.”

“ʻOku sai ange ke ʻosi ʻeku tií,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tangataʻeikí, ʻo ne sio hohaʻa ki he Kuinií, ʻa ia naʻá ne lau ʻa e lisi ʻo e kau hivá.

“Te ke lava pē ʻo ʻalu,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, pea naʻe mavahe fakavavevave atu ʻa e Tufunga Pulú mei he lotoʻaá, ʻo ʻikai ke ne toe tatali ke tui hono sū.

“—pea toʻo pē hono ʻulú ʻi tuʻa,” ko e tānaki atu ia ʻa e Kuinií ki he taha ʻo e kau ʻōfisá: ka naʻe ʻikai ke sio ki ai ʻa e Tokotaha-Hēlesí kimuʻa pea toki aʻu atu ʻa e ʻōfisá ki he matapaá.

“Ui ʻa e fakamoʻoni hokó!” Ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí.

Ko e fakamoʻoni hono hokó ko e tokotaha kuki ʻa e Duchess. Naʻá ne ʻave ʻa e puha pepá ʻi hono nimá, pea naʻe mateʻi ʻe ʻAlisi pe ko hai ia, naʻa mo e kimuʻa peá ne hū atu ki he lotoʻaá, ʻi he kamata ke mafu ʻa e kakai ofi ki he matapaá ʻi he taimi pē ʻe taha.

Naʻe pehē ʻe he Tuʻí, “ʻOmai hoʻo fakamoʻoní.”

“ʻE ʻikai,” ko e lea ia ʻa e tokotaha kuká.

Naʻe sio hohaʻa ʻa e Tuʻí ki he Lāpisi Hinehina, ʻa ia naʻá ne pehē mai ʻi ha leʻo siʻisiʻi, “Kuo pau ke fakafehuʻi fakakolosi ʻe hoʻo ʻAfió ʻa e fakamoʻoni ko ʻení .”

“Sai, kapau kuo pau, kuo pau,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, ʻi ha ʻea loto-mamahi, pea hili ʻene fusi hono ongo nimá mo fakaʻiseʻisa ʻi he tokotaha kuká ʻo aʻu ki he meimei ʻikai ke sio ki ai hono matá, naʻá ne pehē mai ʻi ha leʻo loloto, “Ko e hā ʻoku ngaohi ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi tātí?”

“Pepa, lahi taha,” ko e lea ia ʻa e tokotaha kuká.

“Treacle,” ko e lea ia ʻa ha leʻo mohe mei hono tuʻá.

“Kola ʻa e Dormouse ko iá,” ko e kaila ia ʻa e Kuinií. “Tuʻusi ʻa e ʻulu ʻo e Dormouse ko iá! Tafoki ʻa e Dormouse ko iá ki tuʻa mei he fakamaauʻangá! Taʻofi ia! Pinch ia! Off ʻaki hono ʻulú!”

Naʻe puputuʻu ʻa e fakamaauʻangá kotoa ʻi ha ngaahi miniti ʻe niʻihi, ʻo ʻai ke ʻasi mai ʻa e Dormouse, pea, ʻi he taimi naʻá na toe nofo hifo aí, kuo pulia ʻa e tokotaha kuká.

"Oua tokanga kiai!" ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, ʻi ha ʻea ʻo e nonga lahi. “Ui ʻa e fakamoʻoni hokó.” Pea naʻá ne tānaki atu ʻi ha leʻo siʻisiʻi ki he Kuinií, “Ko e moʻoni, ʻeku ʻofaʻangá, kuo pau ke ke fakafehuʻi fakakolosi ʻa e fakamoʻoni hokó. ʻOku ʻai ke mamahi ʻaupito hoku laʻé!"

Naʻe siofi ʻe ʻAlisi ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina ʻi heʻene fufulu ʻi he lisí, ʻo ne ongoʻi fie ʻilo lahi ke sio pe ʻe fēfē ʻa e fakamoʻoni hokó, “—he ʻoku teʻeki ke nau maʻu ha fakamoʻoni lahi ,” ko ʻene lea ia kiate iá. Fakakaukauloto atu ki heʻene ʻohovalé, ʻi hono lau atu ʻe he Lāpisi Hinehina, ʻi he tumutumu ʻo hono kiʻi leʻo ngingilá, ʻa e hingoa ko e “ʻAlisi!”
