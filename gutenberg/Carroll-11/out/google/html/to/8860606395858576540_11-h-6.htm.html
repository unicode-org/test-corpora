KO E VAHE VI.
Puaka mo e Pepa

Naʻá ne tuʻu ʻi ha miniti ʻe taha pe ua ʻo sio ki he falé, mo fifili pe ko e hā ʻe fai hono hokó, pea fakafokifā pē kuo lele mai ha tangata vaʻe ʻi he livery mei he ʻakaú —(naʻá ne lau ia ko ha tangata vaʻe koeʻuhí he naʻá ne ʻi he livery: ka ʻikai, ʻi heʻene fakamāuʻi pē ʻi hono fofongá, naʻá ne mei ui ia ko ha ika) —peá ne rapped leʻolahi ʻa e dock or his k at. Naʻe fakaava ia ʻe ha tangata vaʻe ʻe taha ʻi he livery, mo e fofonga takai, mo e mata lalahi ʻo hangē ha foʻi poto; pea ko e ongo tangata vaʻé fakatouʻosi, naʻe fakatokangaʻi ʻe ʻAlisi, naʻe ʻi ai hona fulufuluʻi ʻulu efuefu ʻo fuopotopoto kotoa ʻi hona ʻulú. Naʻá ne ongoʻi fie ʻilo lahi pe ko e hā ʻa e meʻa naʻe hokó, peá ne kiʻi totolo atu mei he ʻakaú ke fanongo.

Naʻe kamata ʻaki ʻe he Fish-Footman ʻaki ʻene ʻomi mei hono lalo nimá ha tohi lahi, ʻo meimei tatau mo ia, pea naʻá ne ʻoange ʻeni ki he taha ʻe tahá, ʻo ne pehē, ʻi ha leʻo fakaʻeiʻeiki, “Maʻá e Duchess. Ko ha fakaafe mei he Kuini ke vaʻinga koloketi.” Naʻe toe lea ʻaki ʻe he Frog-Footman, ʻi he leʻo fakaʻapaʻapa tatau, ʻo ne kiʻi liliu pē ʻa e hokohoko ʻo e ngaahi leá, “Mei he Kuini. Ko ha fakaafe ki he Duchess ke vaʻinga koloketi.”

Pea naʻá na fakatou punou hifo, pea naʻe lavelavea fakataha hona ongo foʻi fulufuluʻi ʻulú.

Naʻe kata lahi ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he meʻá ni, ʻo pau ai ke ne lele ʻo foki ki he vaotaá ʻi heʻene manavasiʻi naʻa nau fanongo kiate iá; pea ʻi heʻene sio hoko atu ki tuʻá kuo ʻikai ke ʻi ai ʻa e Tangata-Vaʻe-Iká, pea naʻe tangutu ʻa e taha ʻi he kelekelé ofi ki he matapaá, ʻo sio fakavalevale hake ki he langí.

Naʻe ʻalu hake mā ʻa ʻAlisi ki he matapaá, ʻo tukituki mai.

“ʻOku ʻikai ha faʻahinga ʻaonga ʻo e tukitukí,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tangata Vaʻé, “pea ʻoku ʻuhinga ia ʻe ua. ʻUluakí, koeʻuhí he ʻoku ou ʻi he tafaʻaki tatau ʻo e matapaá mo koe; ko hono uá, koeʻuhí he ʻoku nau longoaʻa lahi ʻi lotó, naʻe ʻikai ke lava ʻe ha taha ʻo fanongo kiate koe." Pea ko e moʻoni naʻe ʻi ai ha longoaʻa makehe taha naʻe hoko ʻi loto —ko ha ʻuʻulu mo e mafu maʻu pē, pea ʻi he taimi kotoa pē naʻe ʻi ai ha fuʻu pā lahi, ʻo hangē kuo motuhia ha ipu pe keté ʻo kongokonga.

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe ʻAlisi, “Kātaki leva, ʻe anga fēfē haʻaku hū ki loto?”

“Mahalo ʻe ʻi ai ha ʻuhinga ʻi hoʻo tukitukí,” ko e hoko atu ia ʻa e Tangata Vaʻé ʻo ʻikai ke ne tokanga ki ai, “kapau naʻe ʻi homa vahaʻá ʻa e matapaá. Hangē ko ʻení, kapau naʻá ke ʻi loto , te ke tukituki nai, pea te u lava ʻo tukuange koe ki tuʻa, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi.” Naʻá ne sio hake ki he langí ʻi he taimi kotoa pē naʻá ne lea aí, pea naʻe fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi ko ʻení ʻo fakapapauʻi ʻoku taʻefakasivile. “Ka mahalo he ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo tokoniʻi ia,” ko ʻene lea ia kiate iá; “ʻoku fuʻu ofi ʻaupito hono matá ki he tumutumu ʻo hono ʻulú. Ka ʻi ha faʻahinga tuʻunga pē te ne tali nai ha ngaahi fehuʻi.—ʻE anga fēfē haʻaku hū ki loto?” ko ʻene toe lea ʻakí ia, ʻo leʻolahi.

“Te u tangutu heni,” ko e fakatokanga ia ʻa e Tangata Vaʻé, “ʻo aʻu ki ʻapongipongi—”

ʻI he momeniti ko ʻení naʻe fakaava ʻa e matapā ʻo e falé, pea naʻe ʻasi mai ha peleti lahi ʻo ʻalu hangatonu ki tuʻa, ʻo hangatonu ki he ʻulu ʻo e Tangata Vaʻé: naʻá ne kai pē hono ihú, pea motumotuʻa ʻo fakafepakiʻi ʻa e taha ʻo e ngaahi ʻakau ʻi hono tuʻá.

“—pe ʻaho hono hokó, mahalo,” ko e hoko atu ia ʻa e Tangata Vaʻé ʻi he leʻo tatau, ʻo hangē pē naʻe ʻikai ke hoko ha meʻá.

“ʻE anga fēfē haʻaku hū ki loto?” ko e toe fehuʻi ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻi ha leʻo leʻolahi ange.

“ Te ke hū ki loto ʻi ha meʻa pē?” ko e lau ia ʻa e Tangata Vaʻé. “Ko e ʻuluaki fehuʻí ia, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi.”

Naʻe pehē, ʻoku ʻikai ha veiveiua: ko ʻAlisi pē naʻe ʻikai saiʻia ke talaange pehē kiate iá. “ʻOku fakalilifu moʻoni,” ko ʻene lāunga ia kiate iá, “ʻa e founga ʻoku fakakikihi ai ʻa e ngaahi meʻamoʻui kotoa pē. ʻOku feʻunga ia ke fakavalevaleʻi ha taha!"

Naʻe hangē naʻe fakakaukau ʻa e Footman ko ha faingamālie lelei ʻeni ke toe fai ʻene leá, ʻo ʻi ai ha ngaahi kehekehe. “Te u tangutu heni,” ko ‘ene leá ia, “‘o ‘alu mo ‘alu, ‘i ha ngaahi ‘aho mo e ngaahi ‘aho.”

“Ka ko e hā te u faí?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Ko ha meʻa pē ʻokú ke saiʻia aí,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tangata Vaʻé, peá ne kamata ke ifi.

“ʻOiauē, ʻoku ʻikai ha ʻaonga ʻo e talanoa mo iá,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he loto-moʻuá: “ʻokú ne vale haohaoa!” Pea naʻá ne fakaava ʻa e matapaá ʻo hū ki loto.

Naʻe ʻalu tonu atu ʻa e matapaá ki ha fuʻu loki kuki lahi, ʻa ia naʻe fonu ʻi he kohu mei he ngataʻanga ʻe taha ki he ngataʻanga ʻe tahá: naʻe tangutu ʻa e Duchess ʻi ha sea vaʻe tolu ʻi loto, ʻo ne tauhi ha pēpē; naʻe punou ʻa e tokotaha kuká ʻi he afí, ʻo ne fakafefekaʻi ha fuʻu ipu lahi ʻa ia naʻe hangē naʻe fonu ʻi he supó.

“ʻOku pau pē ʻoku fuʻu lahi ʻa e pepa ʻi he supo ko iá!” Naʻe pehē ange ʻe ʻAlisi kiate ia pē, ʻo hangē pē ko ʻene lavá ki he mafu.

Naʻe fuʻu lahi moʻoni ʻa e meʻa ko iá ʻi he ʻeá. Naʻa mo e Duchess naʻá ne mafu ʻi ha ngaahi taimi; pea ko e pēpeé, naʻá ne mafu mo ʻuʻulu fetongitongi ʻo ʻikai haʻane kiʻi tuʻu ʻi ha momeniti. Ko e me'a pe 'i he 'ai'angakai 'o e manu na'e 'ikai ke 'u'ulu, ko e tokotaha kuki, mo ha pusi lahi 'a ia na'e tangutu 'i he 'ai'angakai 'o e manu 'o malimali mei he telinga ki he telinga.

“Kātaki te ke talamai,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻi haʻane kiʻi mā, he naʻe ʻikai ke ne fuʻu fakapapauʻi pe ko ha ʻulungaanga lelei ia ke ne ʻuluaki lea, “ko e hā ʻoku malimali pehē ai hoʻo pusí?”

“Ko ha pusi Cheshire ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess, “pea ko e ʻuhinga ia.Puaka!”

Naʻá ne lea ʻaki ʻa e lea fakaʻosí ʻi ha fakamālohi fakafokifā ʻo puna ʻaupito ʻa ʻAlisi; ka naʻá ne sio ʻi ha momeniti ʻe taha naʻe fakahangatonu ia ki he pēpeé, kae ʻikai kiate ia, ko ia naʻá ne loto-toʻa, ʻo ne toe hoko atu:—

“Na‘e ‘ikai te u ‘ilo‘i ‘oku malimali ma‘u pē ‘a e fanga pusi Cheshire; ko hono mo‘oní, na‘e ‘ikai te u ‘ilo‘i ‘oku lava ke malimali ‘a e fanga pusí.”

“Te nau lava kotoa,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess; “pea ko e tokolahi taha ʻo kinautolú ʻoku nau fai ia.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u ʻilo ha taha ʻoku nau fai ia,” ko e lea fakaʻapaʻapa ʻaupito ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻo ne ongoʻi fiefia ʻaupito ʻi heʻene hū ki ha fetalanoaʻakí.

“ʻOku ʻikai ke ke ʻilo lahi,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess; “pea ko ha moʻoniʻi meʻa ia.”

Naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke saiʻia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he leʻo ʻo e lea ko ʻení, peá ne fakakaukau ʻe sai pē ke fakafeʻiloaki mai ha kaveinga kehe ʻo e talanoá. Lolotonga ʻene feinga ke fakaleleiʻi ha taha, naʻe toʻo ʻe he tokotaha kuká ʻa e ipu supó mei he afí, peá ne kamata leva ke ngāue ʻo lī ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē naʻá ne lava ʻo aʻú ki he Duchess mo e pēpeé —naʻe ʻuluaki haʻu ʻa e ngaahi ʻaione afí; pea hoko atu leva ha ʻuha ʻo e ngaahi siā, peleti, mo e ipu. Naʻe ʻikai ke tokanga ʻa e Duchess kiate kinaua naʻa mo ʻena taaʻi iá; pea naʻe ʻosi ʻuʻulu lahi ʻaupito ʻa e pēpeé, ʻo ʻikai ʻaupito lava ke tala pe naʻe fakamamahiʻi ia ʻe he ngaahi taá pe ʻikai.

“ʻOiauē, kātaki ʻo fakakaukau ki he meʻa ʻokú ke faí!” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻo ne puna hake mo hifo ʻi ha mamahi ʻo e ilifiá. “ʻOiauē, ʻoku ʻalu atu hono ihu mahuʻingá !” ʻi he lele ofi mai ha foʻi siā lahi taʻe angamaheni ʻo ofi atu ki ai, pea meimei ʻaupito ke ne ʻave ia.

“Kapau naʻe fakakaukau ʻa e tokotaha kotoa pē ki heʻenau meʻa pē ʻanautolú,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess ʻi ha ngungu fakaʻohovale, “ʻe vave ange ʻa e takai holo ʻa e māmaní ʻi ha aleapau ʻi heʻene ʻi aí.”

“ʻA ia ʻe ʻikai ko ha lelei ia,” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisi, ʻa ia naʻá ne ongoʻi fiefia ʻaupito ke maʻu ha faingamālie ke fakahaaʻi ha kiʻi konga ʻo ʻene ʻiló. “Fakakaukau pē ki he ngāue te ne fai ʻaki ʻa e ʻahó mo e poʻulí! ʻOkú ke sio ʻoku fie maʻu ʻe he māmaní ha houa ʻe uofulu mā fā ke vilo takai ʻi hono ʻasisí —”

“Ko e talanoa ki he ngaahi toki,” ko e lau ia ‘a e Duchess, “tu‘usi hono ‘ulú!”

Naʻe sio ʻa ʻAlisi ʻo ʻikai fuʻu hohaʻa ki he tokotaha kuká, ke vakai pe ʻokú ne ʻuhinga ke toʻo ʻa e fakahinohinó; ka naʻe femoʻuekina ʻa e tokotaha kuká ʻi hono fakafefekaʻi ʻo e supó, pea naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai ke ne fanongó, ko ia naʻá ne toe hoko atu: “Ko e houa ʻe uofulu mā fā, ʻoku ou fakakaukau ; pe ko e houa ʻe hongofulu mā ua? ʻOku ou—”

“ʻOiauē, ʻoua naʻá ke fakahohaʻasi au ,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess; “Na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito te u lava ‘o kātaki‘i ‘a e ngaahi fiká!” Pea ʻi he meʻa ko iá naʻá ne toe kamata ke fakahuhu ʻene tamá, ʻo ne hivaʻi ha faʻahinga hiva fakaʻofoʻofa ki ai ʻi heʻene fai iá, pea ʻoange ki ai ha lulululu fakamālohi ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻo e laine kotoa pē:

“Lea fakaanga ki ho‘o ki‘i tamasi‘í, .
Pea taaʻi ia ʻi heʻene mafu:
‘Okú ne fai pē ia ke faka‘ita‘i, .
He ‘okú ne ‘ilo‘i ‘oku fakakata.”

KOLOSI.
(ʻA ia naʻe kau mai ai ʻa e tokotaha kuká mo e pēpeé):

“ʻIo! ʻio! ʻio!”

Lolotonga hono hivaʻi ʻe he Duchess ʻa e veesi hono ua ʻo e hivá, naʻá ne kei lī fakamālohi pē ʻa e pēpeé ki ʻolunga mo lalo, pea naʻe tangi lahi ʻa e kiʻi meʻa masivá, ʻo ʻikai ke meimei fanongo ʻa ʻAlisi ki he ngaahi leá:—

“‘Oku ou lea fefeka ki he‘eku tamasi‘í, .
‘Oku ou taa‘i ia ‘i he‘ene mafu;
He ‘oku lava ke ne fiefia faka‘aufuli .
Ko e pepa ‘i he taimi ‘okú ne loto ki aí!”

KOLOSI.

“ʻIo! ʻio! ʻio!”

“ʻI heni! Te ke lava ʻo kiʻi tauhi ia, kapau ʻokú ke loto ki ai!” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess kia ʻAlisi, ʻo ne lī ʻa e pēpeé kiate ia ʻi heʻene leá. “Kuo pau ke u ʻalu ʻo teuteu ke vaʻinga koloketi mo e Kuinií,” peá ne hū fakavave atu mei he lokí. Naʻe lī ʻe he tokotaha kuká ha pani falai ʻi mui ʻiate ia ʻi heʻene ʻalu ki tuʻá, ka naʻe misi pē ia kiate ia.

Naʻe maʻu ʻe ʻAlisi ʻa e pēpeé ʻi ha kiʻi faingataʻa, he ko ha kiʻi meʻamoʻui fōtunga kehekehe, peá ne mafao atu hono ongo nimá mo hono ongo vaʻé ki he ngaahi tafaʻaki kotoa pē, “ʻo hangē pē ha ika fetuʻú,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí. Naʻe ngingila ʻa e kiʻi meʻa masivá ʻo hangē ha mīsini kohu ʻi heʻene puke iá, pea naʻá ne kei liunga ua pē ia mo toe fakatonutonu ia ki tuʻa, ko ia ʻi hono fakakātoá, ʻi he ʻuluaki miniti ʻe taha pe ua, naʻe lahi pē ia ʻi heʻene lava ʻo puke iá.

Ko ʻene ʻiloʻi pē ʻa e founga totonu ʻo hono tauhi iá, (ʻa ia ko hono mioʻi hake ia ʻo hoko ko ha faʻahinga noʻo, pea toki puke maʻu hono telinga toʻomataʻú mo hono vaʻe toʻohemá, koeʻuhí ke taʻofi ʻene veteki ʻiate iá,) naʻá ne ʻave ia ki tuʻa ki he ʻeá. “Kapau he ʻikai ke u ʻave ʻa e kiʻi tamasiʻi ko ʻení mo au,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, “ʻoku pau pē te nau tāmateʻi ia ʻi ha ʻaho ʻe taha pe ua: ʻikai ko ha fakapō ia ke tuku ia?” Naʻá ne leaʻaki leʻolahi ʻa e ngaahi lea fakaʻosí, pea naʻe ngungu ʻa e kiʻi meʻa ko iá ʻo tali (kuo ʻosi ʻene mafu ʻi he taimi ko ʻení). Naʻe pehē ange ʻe ʻAlisi, “ʻOua naʻá ke ngungu; “ʻoku ʻikai ʻaupito ko ha founga totonu ia ʻo hono fakahaaʻi hoʻo fakakaukaú.”

Naʻe toe ngungu ʻa e pēpeé, pea naʻe sio hohaʻa ʻaupito ʻa ʻAlisi ki hono fofongá ke ʻiloʻi pe ko e hā e meʻa ʻoku hokó. Naʻe ʻikai lava ke veiveiua naʻe ʻi ai hono ihu ʻoku fuʻu liliu hake, ʻo lahi ange ʻene hangē ha ngutu ʻi ha ihu moʻoní; foki naʻe fuʻu siʻisiʻi ʻaupito hono matá ki ha pēpē: fakakātoa naʻe ʻikai ke saiʻia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he fōtunga ʻo e meʻá ʻi ha meʻa. “Ka mahalo naʻe tangi pē,” ko ʻene fakakaukau ia, peá ne toe sio ki hono matá, ke vakai pe ʻoku ʻi ai ha loʻimata.

ʻIkai, naʻe ʻikai ha loʻimata. “Kapau te ke liliu ko ha puaka, ʻofaʻanga,” ko e lea fakamātoato ia ʻa ʻAlisí, “ʻE ʻikai toe ʻi ai haʻaku meʻa ke fai mo koe. Fakakaukau he taimí ni!” Naʻe toe tangi ʻa e kiʻi meʻa masivá (pe ngungu, naʻe ʻikai lava ke tala pe ko e fē), pea naʻá na hoko atu ʻi ha kiʻi taimi ʻi he fakalongolongo.

Naʻe toki kamata pē ke fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi ʻiate ia pē, “Ko ʻeni, ko e hā te u fai ki he meʻamoʻui ko ʻení ʻi heʻeku aʻu ki ʻapí?” ʻi heʻene toe ngungu, ʻo fuʻu fakamālohi, ʻo ne sio hifo ki hono fofongá ʻi ha faʻahinga manavasiʻi. Ko e taimi ko ʻení naʻe ʻikai lava ke ʻi ai ha fehālaaki ʻi ai: naʻe ʻikai ke lahi ange pe siʻisiʻi ange ia ʻi ha puaka, pea naʻá ne ongoʻi ʻe fuʻu fakavalevale ʻaupito ke ne ʻave ia ʻo toe lahi ange.

Ko ia naʻá ne tuku hifo ʻa e kiʻi meʻamoʻuí, peá ne ongoʻi mātuʻaki nonga ʻi heʻene sio ki heʻene lele fakalongolongo atu ʻo ʻalu ki he ʻakaú. “Kapau naʻe tupu hake ia,” ko ʻene leá ia kiate iá, “naʻá ne mei ngaohi ha kiʻi tamasiʻi fakalielia fakalilifu: ka ʻokú ne ngaohi ʻo ʻikai ko ha puaka fakaʻofoʻofa, ʻi heʻeku fakakaukaú.” Pea naʻá ne kamata fakakaukau ki ha fānau kehe naʻá ne ʻiloʻi, ʻa ia mahalo ʻe fuʻu lelei ʻaupito ʻenau hoko ko e fanga puaká, pea naʻá ne pehē pē kiate ia, “kapau ʻe ʻiloʻi pē ʻe ha taha ʻa e founga totonu ke liliu ai kinautolú —” ʻi heʻene kiʻi ʻohovale ʻi heʻene sio ki he Pusi Sesiá ʻoku tangutu ʻi ha vaʻa ʻo ha ʻakau ʻi ha ngaahi ʻiate siʻi mei ai.

Naʻe toki malimali pē ʻa e Pusí ʻi heʻene sio kia ʻAlisí. Naʻe hā ngali lelei, ko ʻene fakakaukau ia: neongo ia naʻe ʻi ai pē hono ngaahi nifo lōloa ʻaupito mo ha ngaahi nifo lahi ʻaupito, ko ia naʻá ne ongoʻi ʻoku totonu ke fakaʻapaʻapaʻi ia.

“Cheshire Puss,” ko ʻene kamata ia, ʻo ʻikai fuʻu mā, he naʻe ʻikai ke ne ʻiloʻi pe ʻe saiʻia ʻi he hingoá: ka neongo ia, naʻá ne kiʻi malimali lahi ange pē. “Haʻu, ʻoku fakafiefia ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimí ni,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, peá ne hoko atu. “Te ke talamai, kātaki, pe ko e fē ʻa e hala ʻoku totonu ke u ʻalu mei hení?”

“ʻOku fakafalala lahi ia ʻi he feituʻu ʻokú ke fie aʻu ki aí,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí.

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “ʻOku ʻikai ke u fuʻu tokanga au pe ko fē—.”

“ʻOku ʻikai leva ke mahuʻinga pe ko e fē ʻa e hala te ke ʻalu aí,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí.

“—ko e taimi pē te u aʻu ai ki ha feituʻu ,” ko e tānaki atu ia ʻa ʻAlisi ko ha fakamatala.

“ʻOiauē, ʻoku pau pē te ke fai ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí, “kapau te ke lue pē ʻo fuoloa feʻunga.”

Na‘e ongo‘i ‘e ‘Alisi he‘ikai lava ke faka‘ikai‘i eni, ko ia na‘á ne ‘ahi‘ahi‘i ai ha fehu‘i ‘e taha. “Ko e hā e faʻahinga kakai ʻoku nau nofo ʻi hení?”

“ʻI he tafaʻaki ko iá ,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí, ʻo ne lulululu takai holo hono vaʻe toʻomataʻú, “ʻoku nofo ai ha Tokotaha Tufunga: pea ʻi he tafaʻaki ko iá ,” ʻo ne lulululu ʻaki ʻa e vaʻe ʻe tahá, “ʻoku nofo ai ha Hale ʻo Māʻasi. ʻAʻahi ki ha taha ʻokú ke saiʻia aí: ʻokú na fakatou vale.”

“Ka ʻoku ʻikai ke u fie ʻalu ki he lotolotonga ʻo e kakai valé,” ko e fakatokanga ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“ʻOiauē, he ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo tokoni ki he meʻa ko iá,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí: “ʻoku mau vale kotoa heni. ʻOku ou vale au. ʻOkú ke vale.”

“ʻOkú ke ʻiloʻi fēfē ʻoku ou vale?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Kuo pau pē ko koe,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí, “ka ne ʻikai te ke mei haʻu ki heni.”

Naʻe ʻikai ke fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi ʻoku fakamoʻoniʻi ia ʻe he meʻa ko iá; ka neongo ia, naʻá ne hoko atu “Pea ʻokú ke ʻilo fēfē ʻokú ke vale?”

“Ke kamataʻaki,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí, “ʻoku ʻikai vale ha kuli. ʻOkú ke fakaʻatā ia?”

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “ʻOku ou mahalo ʻoku pehē.”

“Sai, ko ia,” ko e hoko atu ia ‘a e Pusí, “‘okú ke sio, ‘oku ngungu ‘a e kulií ‘i he‘ene ‘itá, pea ‘oku ne lulululu hono hiku ‘i he‘ene fiefiá. Ko ‘eni ‘ oku ou ngungu ‘i he‘eku fiefiá, pea lulululu hoku hiku ‘i he‘eku ‘itá. Ko ia ai ‘oku ou vale.”

“ ʻOku ou ui ia ko e purring, ʻo ʻikai ko e ngungu,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Ui ia ʻi he meʻa ʻokú ke saiʻia aí,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí. “ʻOkú ke vaʻinga koloketi mo e Kuini he ʻaho ní?”

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “ʻOku totonu ke u saiʻia ʻaupito ai, ka ʻoku teʻeki ai ke fakaafeʻi au.”

“Te ke sio mai kiate au ʻi ai,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí, peá ne puli atu.

Naʻe ʻikai fuʻu ʻohovale ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he meʻá ni, naʻá ne fuʻu maheni mo e ngaahi meʻa fakaofo ʻoku hokó. Lolotonga ʻene sio ki he feituʻu naʻe ʻi aí, naʻe fakafokifā pē ʻene toe ʻasi mai.

“ʻI he māvaé, ko e hā naʻe hoko ki he pēpeé?” ko e lau ia ʻa e Pusí. “Naʻe meimei ngalo ʻiate au ke ʻeke.”

“Naʻe liliu ia ʻo hoko ko ha puaka,” ko e lea fakalongolongo ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻo hangē pē naʻá ne foki mai ʻi ha founga fakanatulá.

“Naʻá ku fakakaukau ʻe hoko ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí, peá ne toe puli atu.

Naʻe kiʻi tatali ʻa ʻAlisi, ʻo ʻamanaki ʻa e vaeua ke toe sio ki aí, ka naʻe ʻikai ke ʻasi mai, pea hili ha miniti ʻe taha pe ua naʻá ne lue atu ʻi he tafaʻaki naʻe pehē ʻoku nofo ai ʻa e Hale ʻo Māʻasí. “Kuó u sio ʻi ha kau hēkesi kimuʻa,” ko ʻene lea ia kiate iá; “ko e March Hare ʻe lahi taha ʻene mālie tahá, pea mahalo ʻi heʻene hoko ko Mē ʻeni he ʻikai ke raving mad —ʻo ʻikai fuʻu vale ʻo hangē ko ia ʻi Maʻasí.” ʻI heʻene lea ʻaki ʻení, naʻá ne sio hake, pea naʻe toe ʻi ai ʻa e Pusí, ʻokú ne tangutu ʻi ha vaʻa ʻo ha fuʻu ʻakau.

“Naʻá ke pehē puaka, pe fiki?” ko e lau ia ʻa e Pusí.

“Naʻá ku pehē puaka,” ko e tali ia ʻa ʻAlisí; “pea ʻoku ou fakaʻamu ke ʻoua naʻá ke kei ʻasi mai mo puli fakafokifā pehē: ʻokú ke ʻai ha taha ke fuʻu ngingila.”

“Sai pē,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Pusí; pea ko e taimi ko ʻení naʻe mole māmālie ʻaupito ia, ʻo kamata ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻo e hiku, pea fakaʻosi ʻaki ʻa e malimali, ʻa ia naʻe kei ʻi ai ha taimi hili ʻa e ʻalu ʻa e toenga ʻo e hiku.

“Sai!Kuó u faʻa sio ki ha pusi ʻoku ʻikai haʻane malimali,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí; “ka ko ha malimali taʻe ʻi ai ha pusi! Ko e meʻa fakatupu ʻilo lahi taha ia kuó u sio ki ai ʻi heʻeku moʻuí!”

Naʻe teʻeki ke ne fuʻu mamaʻo ange kimuʻa peá ne toki sio ki he fale ʻo e Hale ʻo Māʻasí: naʻá ne fakakaukau kuo pau pē ko e fale totonu ia, he naʻe fōtunga ʻa e ngaahi ʻaá ʻo hangē ha telingá pea naʻe ʻufiʻufi ʻaki ʻa e fulufuluʻi ʻakaú ʻa e ʻaá. Ko ha fale lahi ʻaupito ia, ʻo ne ʻikai ke ne saiʻia ke ʻalu ofi ange kae ʻoua kuó ne nibbled ha toe konga ʻo e musie ʻi he nima toʻohemá, pea hiki hake ia ki ha fute ʻe ua nai hono māʻolungá: naʻa mo e taimi ko iá naʻá ne lue hake ki ai ʻo ʻikai fuʻu mā, ʻo ne pehē kiate ia pē “Tau pehē ʻoku totonu ke raving mad gone ʻi he hili ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē ʻoku ʻikai ke u!”
