KO E VAHE I.
Hifo ʻi he Luo-Lāpisi

Naʻe kamata ke fuʻu fiu ʻaupito ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi heʻene tangutu ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo hono tuofefiné ʻi he veʻe vaí, pea ʻi he ʻikai haʻane meʻa ke faí: naʻe tuʻo taha pe tuʻo ua ʻene siofi ʻa e tohi naʻe lau ʻe hono tuofefiné, ka naʻe ʻikai ha ngaahi fakatātā pe ngaahi fetalanoaʻaki ʻi ai, “pea ko e hā hono ʻaonga ʻo ha tohi,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí “ʻo ʻikai ha ngaahi fakatātā pe ngaahi fetalanoaʻaki?”

Ko ia naʻá ne fakakaukau ʻi hono ʻatamaí tonu (ʻo hangē ko ia naʻá ne lavá, he naʻe ʻai ʻe he ʻaho velá ke ne ongoʻi mohe mo vale ʻaupito), pe ʻe ʻaonga ʻa e fiefia ʻi hono ngaohi ha teisi-seini ʻi he faingataʻa ʻo e tuʻu hake ʻo toʻo ʻa e ngaahi teisií, ʻi he fakafokifā pē kuo lele mai ha Lāpisi Hinehina mo hono mata lanu pingikī ʻo ofi mai kiate ia.

Naʻe ʻikai ha meʻa ʻe fuʻu fakaofo ʻaupito ʻi he meʻa ko iá; pea naʻe ʻikai foki ke fuʻu fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi ʻoku fuʻu ʻikai ke ne fanongo ki he pehē ʻe he Lāpití kiate ia pē, “ʻOiauē ʻofaʻanga! ʻOiauē ʻofaʻanga! Te u tōmui!” (ʻi heʻene fakakaukauʻi ia ʻi he hili iá, naʻe haʻu ki hono ʻatamaí naʻe totonu ke ne fifili ki he meʻá ni, ka ʻi he taimi ko iá naʻe ngali fakanatula ʻaupito kotoa ia); ka ʻi he taimi naʻe toʻo moʻoni ai ʻe he Lāpití ha uati mei hono kato-kofukofú , peá ne sio ki ai, peá ne toki fakavavevave atu, naʻe kamata ke tuʻu hake ʻa ʻAlisi, he naʻe ulo mai ʻi hono ʻatamaí naʻe teʻeki ai ke ne sio kimuʻa ki ha lāpisi ʻoku ʻi ai haʻane kato-kofukofu, pe ko ha uati ke toʻo mei ai, pea naʻá ne curicross hili ha, pea naʻá ne curicross mo e . me'a lelei na'e taimi totonu ke sio ki ai 'oku pop hifo ha fu'u rabbit-hole 'i lalo he 'ā.

ʻI ha momeniti ʻe taha naʻe ʻalu hifo ʻa ʻAlisi ʻo muimui ki ai, ʻo ʻikai ke ne teitei fakakaukau ki ha founga ʻi he māmaní ke ne toe ʻalu ki tuʻá.

Naʻe ʻalu hangatonu atu ʻa e luo-lāpití ʻo hangē ha ʻaá ʻi ha faʻahinga founga, peá ne toki tō fakafokifā hifo, ʻo fakafokifā ʻaupito ʻo ʻikai ha momeniti ʻa ʻAlisi ke fakakaukau ke taʻofi ia kimuʻa peá ne toki ʻiloʻi ʻokú ne tō hifo ʻi ha vaitupu loloto ʻaupito.

Pe naʻe loloto ʻaupito ʻa e vaitupú, pe naʻá ne tō māmālie ʻaupito, he naʻá ne maʻu ha taimi lahi ʻi heʻene ʻalu hifo ke sio takai holo ʻiate iá mo fifili pe ko e hā ʻe hoko hokó. ʻUluakí, naʻá ne feinga ke sio hifo ʻo ʻiloʻi ʻa e meʻa ʻokú ne haʻu ki aí, ka naʻe fuʻu fakapoʻuli ke ne sio ki ha meʻa; peá ne sio leva ki he ongo tafaʻaki ʻo e vaitupú, ʻo ne fakatokangaʻi kuo fonu ʻi he ngaahi kato mo e ngaahi fata tohi; naʻá ne sio ʻi heni mo hena ki ha ngaahi mape mo ha ngaahi fakatātā naʻe tautau ʻi ha ngaahi foʻi ʻakau. Naʻá ne toʻo hifo ha hina mei he taha ʻo e ngaahi fata ʻi heʻene fakalaka atu aí; na’e fakahingoa ia ko e “ORANGE MARMALADE”, ka ko ‘ene loto mamahi lahi na’e ‘ikai ha me’a ‘i ai: na’e ‘ikai ke ne sai’ia ke tuku hifo ‘a e hina ‘i he’ene manavasi’i na’a ne tamate’i ha taha ‘i lalo, ko ia na’a ne lava ‘o ‘ai ia ki he taha ‘o e ngaahi kapoti ‘i he’ene tō ‘o fakalaka atu ai.

"Lelei!" ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻiate ia pē, “hili haʻaku tō pehē hangē ko ʻení, he ʻikai ke u fakakaukau ki ha meʻa ke u tō hifo ʻi he sitepú! He loto-toʻa ē te nau fakakaukau kotoa au ʻi ʻapi! Ko e hā, he ʻikai ke u lea ʻaki ha meʻa fekauʻaki mo ia, neongo kapau te u tō mei he tumutumu ʻo e falé!" (ʻA ia naʻe ngalingali ʻaupito naʻe moʻoni.)

Hifo, hifo, hifo. He ʻikai ʻaupito ke ngata ʻa e hingá? “ʻOku ou fifili pe ko e maile ʻe fiha kuó u tō ai ʻi he taimi ko ʻení?” ko ʻene lea leʻolahí ia. “Kuo pau pē ʻoku ou aʻu ki ha feituʻu ʻoku ofi ki he lotolotonga ʻo e māmaní. Tuku ke u sio: ʻe maile ʻe fā afe ia ki lalo, ʻoku ou fakakaukau—” (he, ʻokú ke sio, naʻe ako ʻe ʻAlisi ha ngaahi meʻa lahi ʻo e faʻahinga ko ʻení ʻi heʻene ngaahi lēsoni ʻi he loki akó, pea neongo naʻe ʻikai ko ha faingamālie lelei ʻaupito ʻeni ki hono fakahaaʻi ʻene ʻiló, ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe ʻikai ha taha ʻoku ʻi ai ʻene lisi ʻoku kei lelei ke — ʻoku meimei ko e mamaʻo totonu ia —ka ʻoku ou fifili leva pe ko e hā e Latitūte pe Longitute kuó u aʻu ki aí?” (Naʻe ʻikai ha ʻilo ʻa ʻAlisi pe ko e hā ʻa e Latitude, pe Longitude foki, ka naʻá ne fakakaukau ko ha ngaahi lea fakaʻofoʻofa ia ke lea ʻaki.)

ʻI he taimi ní naʻá ne toe kamata. “ʻOku ou fifili pe te u tō tonu ʻi he māmaní! ʻE ngali malie ʻaupito ke ʻasi mai ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e kakai ʻoku nau luelue ʻo ʻalu hifo honau ʻulú! Ko e kau ʻAnipatiá, ʻoku ou fakakaukau—” (naʻá ne ʻikai ke fuʻu fiefia ʻi he ʻikai ha taha ʻe fanongo, ʻi he taimi ko ʻení, he naʻe ʻikai ke ongo ʻaupito ʻa e foʻi lea totonú) “ to nak as you the Please have. Ma’am, ko Nu’usila pe ko ‘Aositelelia ‘eni?” (pea naʻá ne feinga ke curtsey ʻi heʻene leá —curtsey fakaʻofoʻofa ʻi hoʻo tō ʻi he ʻeá! ʻOkú ke pehē te ke lava ʻo puleʻi ia?) “Pea ko ha kiʻi taʻahine taʻeʻilo lahi ē te ne fakakaukau mai kiate au ʻi heʻeku kolé! ʻIkai, he ʻikai ʻaupito ke ʻaonga ke ʻeke: mahalo te u sio kuo tohiʻi ia ʻi ha feituʻu.”

Hifo, hifo, hifo. Na‘e ‘ikai ha toe me‘a ke fai, ko ia na‘e ‘ikai fuoloa kuo kamata ke toe talanoa ‘a ‘Ālisi. “ʻE misi lahi ʻaupito ʻa Taina kiate au he pō ní, ʻoku totonu ke u fakakaukau!” (Ko Taina ʻa e pusí.) “ʻOku ou ʻamanaki te nau manatuʻi ʻene sosi huʻakaú ʻi he taimi tií. Tiina hoku ʻofaʻangá! ʻOku ou fakaʻamu naʻá ke ʻi lalo heni mo au! ʻOku ʻikai ha fanga kiʻi miusi ʻi he ʻeá, ʻoku ou manavasiʻi, ka mahalo te ke maʻu ha peki, pea ʻoku hangē ʻaupito ia ha kiʻi peká, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi. Ka ʻoku kai ʻe he fanga pusí ʻa e fanga kiʻi peká, ʻoku ou fifili?” Pea ʻi heni naʻe kamata ke kiʻi mohe ʻa ʻAlisi, peá ne hoko atu ʻene pehē kiate ia pē, ʻi ha founga fakaʻofoʻofa, “ʻOku kai ʻe he fanga pusí ʻa e fanga peká? ʻOku kai ʻe he fanga pusí ʻa e fanga peká?” pea taimi ʻe niʻihi, “ʻOku kai ʻe he fanga peká ʻa e fanga pusí?” he, ʻokú ke sio, ʻi he ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo tali ha taha ʻo e ongo fehuʻí, naʻe ʻikai ke fuʻu mahuʻinga pe ko e fē ʻa e founga naʻá ne fakalea ʻaki iá. Naʻá ne ongoʻi ʻokú ne mohe, pea naʻá ne toki kamata ke misi ʻokú ne lue nima-homo mo Taina, peá ne pehē ange kiate ia ʻi he fakamātoato ʻaupito, “Ko ʻeni, Taina, talamai ʻa e moʻoní: naʻá ke kai ha peká?” ‘i he taimi fakafokifā, thump! tuhu! naʻá ne ʻalu hifo ki ha fuʻu ʻakau mo e ngaahi lauʻiʻakau mōmoa, pea naʻe ʻosi ʻa e tō.

Naʻe ʻikai ke kiʻi lavea ʻa ʻAlisi, peá ne puna hake ki hono vaʻé ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti: naʻá ne sio hake, ka naʻe fakapoʻuli kotoa ʻi ʻolunga; naʻe ʻi muʻa ʻiate iá ha toe hala lōloa, pea naʻe kei ʻasi pē ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina, ʻo ne fakavavevave hifo ki ai. Naʻe ʻikai ha momeniti ʻe mole: naʻe ʻalu mamaʻo ʻa ʻAlisi ʻo hangē ko e matangí, pea naʻe taimi tonu pē ke ne fanongo ki heʻene pehē, ʻi heʻene afe ʻi ha tuliki, “ʻOiauē hoku telinga mo e ʻuluʻulu, ʻoku tōmui ʻaupito!” Naʻá ne ofi ʻi mui ai ʻi heʻene afe ʻi he tuliki, ka naʻe ʻikai ke toe ʻasi ʻa e Lāpití: naʻá ne ʻiloʻi ia ʻi ha holo lōloa mo maʻulalo, ʻa ia naʻe fakamaama ʻe ha laine ʻo e ngaahi maama naʻe tautau ʻi he ʻaá.

Naʻe ʻi ai ha ngaahi matapā takatakai ʻi he holó, ka naʻe lokaʻi kotoa kinautolu; pea ʻi he ʻosi ʻa e ʻalu hifo ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá ʻo ʻalu hake ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá, ʻo ne ʻahiʻahiʻi e matapā kotoa pē, naʻá ne lue lotomamahi hifo ʻi he lotomālié, ʻo fifili pe ʻe anga fēfē haʻane toe ʻalu ki tuʻa.

Fakafokifā pē kuó ne aʻu ki ha kiʻi tēpile vaʻe tolu, naʻe ngaohi kotoa ʻaki ha sioʻata fefeka; naʻe ʻikai ha meʻa ʻi ai tukukehe pē ha kiʻi kī koula, pea ko e ʻuluaki fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisí mahalo ʻoku kau ia ki he taha ʻo e ngaahi matapā ʻo e holó; ka, ʻoiauē! pe naʻe fuʻu lahi ʻa e ngaahi loká, pe naʻe fuʻu siʻisiʻi ʻa e kií, ka ʻi ha faʻahinga tuʻunga pē he ʻikai ke ne fakaava ha taha ʻo kinautolu. Ka neongo ia, ʻi heʻene takai hono uá, naʻá ne aʻu atu ki ha puipui māʻulalo naʻe ʻikai ke ne fakatokangaʻi kimuʻa, pea ʻi mui aí naʻe ʻi ai ha kiʻi matapā ʻinisi ʻe hongofulu mā nima nai hono māʻolungá: naʻá ne ʻahiʻahiʻi ʻa e kiʻi kī koula ʻi he loká, pea naʻá ne fiefia lahi naʻe feʻunga ia!

Naʻe fakaava ʻe ʻAlisi ʻa e matapaá ʻo ne ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻalu atu ia ki ha kiʻi hala, ʻoku ʻikai fuʻu lahi ange ʻi ha luo ʻo e ʻikale: naʻá ne tūʻulutui hifo ʻo sio ʻi he halá ki he ngoue fakaʻofoʻofa taha kuó ke sio aí. Ko ʻene fakaʻamu lahi ke mavahe mei he holo fakapoʻuli ko iá, ʻo ʻeveʻeva holo ʻi he ngaahi mohenga ko ia ʻo e ngaahi matalaʻiʻakau ngingilá mo e ngaahi matavai momoko ko iá, ka naʻe ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo ʻai hono ʻulú ʻi he matapaá; “pea neongo kapau ʻe fou atu hoku ʻulú,” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisi masivá, “ʻe siʻisiʻi ʻaupito ʻene ʻaongá ʻo kapau ʻe ʻikai hoku ongo umá. ʻOiauē, ʻoku ou fakaʻamu ke u lava ʻo fakalongolongo ʻo hangē ha telesikopí! ʻOku ou fakakaukau te u lava, kapau te u ʻiloʻi pē ʻa e founga ke kamata aí.” He, ʻokú ke sio, naʻe lahi fau e ngaahi meʻa taʻefakakaukauʻi kuo hoko kimuí ni mai, ʻo kamata ke fakakaukau ʻa ʻAlisi ʻoku siʻisiʻi ʻaupito e ngaahi meʻa moʻoni ʻoku ʻikai ke malava moʻoní.

Naʻe hangē naʻe ʻikai ha ʻaonga ʻo e tatali ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e kiʻi matapaá, ko ia naʻá ne foki ki he tēpilé, ʻo ʻamanaki ʻe vaeua te ne maʻu ha kī ʻe taha ʻi ai, pe ʻi ha faʻahinga tuʻunga pē ko ha tohi ʻo e ngaahi tuʻutuʻuni ki hono tāpuniʻi ʻo e kakaí ʻo hangē ha ngaahi telesikopí: ʻi he taimi ko ʻení naʻá ne maʻu ai ha kiʻi hina ʻi ai, (“ʻa ia naʻe pau pē naʻe ʻikai ke ʻi heni kimuʻa,” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí,) pea takatakai ʻi he laper the words . “INU AU,” na‘e paaki faka‘ofo‘ofa ‘i ai ‘i he ngaahi mata‘itohi lalahi.

Naʻe fuʻu lelei ʻaupito ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē ke pehē “Inu mai au,” ka naʻe ʻikai ke ʻalu ʻa e kiʻi ʻAlisi potó ʻo fai ia ʻi ha fakavavevave. “ʻIkai, te u ʻuluaki sio,” ko ʻene leá ia, “pea vakai pe ʻoku fakaʻilongaʻi ʻaki ʻa e ‘ kona ’ pe ʻikai”; he naʻá ne lau ha fanga kiʻi hisitōlia fakaʻofoʻofa ʻe niʻihi fekauʻaki mo e fānau naʻe vela, pea kai ʻe he fanga manu fekaí mo e ngaahi meʻa taʻefakafiefia kehé, koeʻuhí kotoa he ʻikai ke nau manatuʻi ʻa e ngaahi tuʻutuʻuni faingofua naʻe akoʻi kinautolu ʻe honau ngaahi kaungāmeʻá: hangē ko e, ʻe vela koe ʻe ha foʻi poker kulokula-vela kapau te ke pukepuke ia ʻo fuʻu lōloa; pea kapau te ke motuhi loloto ‘ aupito ho louhi‘i nimá ‘aki ha heletā, ‘oku fa‘a tafe toto; pea naʻe teʻeki ai ke ngalo ʻiate ia, kapau te ke inu lahi mei ha hina ʻoku fakaʻilongaʻi ko e “kona,” ʻoku meimei pau ʻe ʻikai ke ne loto-tatau mo koe, ʻi ha taimi vave pe ʻamui ange.

Kae kehe, naʻe ʻikai ke fakaʻilongaʻi ʻa e hina ko ʻení ko e “kona,” ko ia naʻe feinga ʻa ʻAlisi ke ne ʻahiʻahiʻi ia, pea ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi ʻoku fuʻu fakaʻofoʻofá, (naʻe ʻi ai, ko hono moʻoní, ha faʻahinga ifo fefiofi ʻo e sieli-tāti, kasita, paine-ʻāpele, tēki tunu, tofi, mo e tositi pata vela,) naʻe vave ʻaupito ʻene ʻosi ia.

“Ko ha ongoʻi fie ʻilo moʻoni ia!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí; “Kuo pau pē ʻoku ou fakalongolongo ʻo hangē ha telesikopí.”

Pea naʻe pehē moʻoni: naʻe ʻinisi pē ʻe hongofulu hono māʻolungá he taimí ni, pea naʻe ngingila hono fofongá ʻi heʻene fakakaukau ʻokú ne ʻi he lahi totonu he taimí ni ke ne hū atu ʻi he kiʻi matapaá ki he ngoue fakaʻofoʻofa ko iá. Kae kehe, ʻuluaki, naʻá ne tatali ʻi ha ngaahi miniti siʻi ke vakai pe ʻe toe fakasiʻisiʻi ange: naʻá ne ongoʻi kiʻi manavasiʻi ʻi he meʻá ni; “he mahalo ʻe ngata ia, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisi kiate iá, “ʻi heʻeku ʻalu fakaʻaufuli ki tuʻá, ʻo hangē ha teʻelango. ʻOku ou fifili pe ʻoku totonu ke u fēfē ʻi he taimi ko iá?” Pea naʻá ne feinga ke fakakaukauʻi ʻa e anga ʻo e ulo ʻo ha teʻelango hili hono puhiʻi ʻo e teʻelango, he naʻe ʻikai ke ne manatuʻi haʻane sio ki ha meʻa pehē.

Hili ha taimi nounou mei ai, ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻikai toe hoko ha meʻa, naʻá ne fakakaukau ke ʻalu leva ki he ngoué; ka, ʻoiauē ʻa ʻAlisi masiva! ʻi heʻene aʻu atu ki he matapaá, naʻá ne ʻiloʻi kuo ngalo ʻiate ia ʻa e kiʻi kī koulá, pea ʻi heʻene foki ki he tēpilé ke maʻu iá, naʻá ne ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo aʻu ki ai: naʻá ne lava ʻo sio lelei ʻaupito ki ai ʻi he sioʻatá, pea naʻá ne feinga ʻaki hono lelei tahá ke kaka hake ʻi he taha ʻo e vaʻe ʻo e tēpilé, ka naʻe fuʻu hekeheke; pea ʻi heʻene fiu ʻi he feingá, naʻe tangutu hifo ʻa e kiʻi meʻa masivá ʻo tangi.

“Haʻu, ʻoku ʻikai hano ʻaonga ʻo e tangi peheé!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisi kiate iá, ʻo ʻikai fuʻu māsila; “ʻOku ou faleʻi koe ke ke mavahe ʻi he miniti ko ʻení!” Naʻá ne ʻoange fakalūkufua kiate ia ha faleʻi lelei ʻaupito, (neongo naʻe ʻikai ke ne faʻa muimui ki ai), pea ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi naʻá ne valokiʻi lahi ʻaupito ia ʻo ne ʻomi ʻa e loʻimatá ki hono matá; pea ʻi he taimi pē naʻá ne manatuʻi ai ʻene feinga ke puhaʻi hono telingá tonu ʻi heʻene kākaaʻi ia ʻi ha vaʻinga koloketi naʻá ne vaʻingaʻi ʻaki ia, he naʻe saiʻia ʻaupito ʻa e tamasiʻi fie ʻilo ko ʻení ʻi heʻene fakangalingali ko ha ongo meʻa. “Ka ‘oku ‘ikai hano ‘aonga he taimí ni,” ko e fakakaukau ia ‘a ‘Alisi masivá, “ke fakangalingali ko ha ongo me‘a!

Naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuo tō hono matá ki ha kiʻi puha sioʻata naʻe tokoto ʻi lalo he tēpilé: naʻá ne fakaava ia, ʻo ne ʻilo ʻi ai ha kiʻi keke siʻisiʻi ʻaupito, ʻa ia naʻe fakaʻilongaʻi fakaʻofoʻofa ai ʻa e ngaahi lea “KAI AU” ʻaki ʻa e ngaahi foʻi ʻakau ko e currants. “Sai, te u kai ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí, “pea kapau te ne ʻai au ke u lahi ange, te u lava ʻo aʻu ki he kií; pea kapau te ne ʻai au ke u siʻisiʻi ange, te u lava ʻo totolo ʻi he lalo matapaá; ko ia ʻi ha founga pē ʻe taha te u hū atu ki he ngoué, pea ʻoku ʻikai ke u tokanga pe ko e fē ʻe hokó!”

Naʻá ne kai ha kiʻi meʻa siʻisiʻi, peá ne pehē loto hohaʻa kiate ia pē, “Ko e founga fē? Ko e founga fē?”, ʻo ne puke hono nimá ʻi he tumuʻaki ʻo hono ʻulú ke ongoʻi ʻa e founga ʻoku tupulaki aí, pea naʻá ne fuʻu ʻohovale ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi naʻá ne kei tatau pē hono lahí: ke fakapapauʻi, ʻoku hoko fakalūkufua ʻeni ʻi he taimi ʻoku kai ai ʻe ha taha ʻa e ngaahi meʻa ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa ka ʻoku lahi ʻa e founga- ʻoku ʻamanaki ki tuʻa. hoko, naʻe ngali fuʻu fakapoʻuli mo fakavalevale ke hoko atu ʻa e moʻuí ʻi he founga angamahení.

Ko ia naʻá ne kamata ngāue, pea ʻikai fuoloa kuó ne ʻosi ʻa e keké.
