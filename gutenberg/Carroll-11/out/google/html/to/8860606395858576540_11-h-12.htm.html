KO E VAHE XII.
Ko e Fakamoʻoni ʻa ʻAlisi

"ʻi heni!" ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻAlisi, ʻo ngalo ʻiate ia ʻi he ʻoho ʻo e momenití ʻa e lahi ʻo ʻene tupu ʻi he ngaahi miniti siʻi kuohilí, pea naʻá ne puna hake ʻi ha fakavavevave ʻo ne tipi ʻi he puha fakamāú ʻaki ʻa e tafaʻaki ʻo hono sāketí, ʻo ne fakatupu ʻita ki he kau fakamāú kotoa ʻi he ʻulu ʻo e fuʻu kakai ʻi laló, pea ʻi ai naʻa nau tokoto resprawling ha fuʻu shemishbe ʻo fekauʻaki mo ia, fakatuʻupakē ʻene loto-mamahi ʻi he uike kimuʻá.

“ʻOiauē, ʻoku ou kole fakamolemole atu!” naʻá ne kaila ʻi ha leʻo ʻo e loto-mamahi lahi, peá ne kamata ke toe toʻo hake kinaua ʻi he vave taha te ne lavá, he naʻe kei lele pē ʻa e fakatuʻutāmaki ʻo e ika koulá ʻi hono ʻulú, pea naʻá ne maʻu ha faʻahinga fakakaukau taʻemahino kuo pau ke tānaki kinaua ʻi he taimi pē ko iá pea toe ʻai ki he puha-fakamāú, pe te na mate.

“He ʻikai lava ke hoko atu ʻa e fakamaauʻangá,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí ʻi ha leʻo mamafa ʻaupito, “kae ʻoua kuo foki kotoa ʻa e kau fakamāú ki honau ngaahi feituʻu totonú — kotoa ,” ko ʻene toe lea ia ʻi he fakamamafa lahi, ʻo ne sio mālohi kia ʻAlisi ʻi heʻene lea ʻaki iá.

Naʻe sio ʻa ʻAlisi ki he puha-fakamāú, ʻo ne sio, ʻi heʻene fakavavevavé, naʻá ne ʻai ʻa e Lisi ʻi he ʻulu ki lalo, pea naʻe lulululu holo ʻa e kiʻi meʻa masivá hono hiku ʻi ha founga fakaloloma, ʻo ʻikai ke ne fuʻu lava ʻo ngaʻunu. Naʻe ʻikai fuoloa kuó ne toe ʻomi ia ki tuʻa, ʻo ne fakatonutonu ia; “ʻikai ko ha meʻa lahi ia ʻoku ʻuhinga ki aí,” ko ʻene lea ia kiate iá; “ʻOku totonu ke u fakakaukau ʻe fuʻu lahi ʻene ʻaonga ʻi he ʻahiʻahí ʻi he founga ʻe taha ki ʻolunga ʻo hangē ko e founga ʻe tahá.”

Ko e taimi pē naʻe kiʻi fakaakeake ai ʻa e kau fakamāú mei he ʻohovale ʻi heʻenau loto-mamahi, pea kuo maʻu ʻenau ngaahi lauʻi peni mo e ngaahi penitala pea ʻoange kiate kinautolu, naʻa nau kamata ngāue faivelenga ʻaupito ke tohiʻi ha hisitōlia ʻo e fakatuʻutāmakí, kotoa tukukehe ʻa e Lizard, ʻa ia naʻe ngali fuʻu ikunaʻi ke fai ha meʻa ka ko e tangutu pē mo hono ngutu ʻoku fakaava hake ki he gaof ʻo e,.

“Ko e hā ʻokú ke ʻilo fekauʻaki mo e pisinisi ko ʻení?” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí kia ʻAlisi.

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe ʻAlisi, “ʻIkai ha meʻa.”

“ʻIkai ha meʻa pē? ” ko e fakaʻaiʻai ia ʻe he Tuʻí.

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “ʻIkai ha meʻa ʻe taha.”

“ʻOku mahuʻinga ʻaupito ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, ʻo ne tafoki ki he kau fakamāú. Naʻa nau toki kamata ke tohi ʻeni ʻi heʻenau ngaahi lauʻipepa, ʻi he taimi naʻe fakalaveaʻi ai ʻe he Lāpisi Hinehina: “ ʻOku ʻikai mahuʻinga, ʻoku ʻuhinga hoʻo ʻAfió, ko e moʻoni,” ko ʻene leá ia ʻi ha leʻo fakaʻapaʻapa ʻaupito, ka naʻá ne fakaʻiseʻisa mo fai ha fofonga kiate ia ʻi heʻene leá.

“ Un mahuʻinga, ko e moʻoni, naʻá ku ʻuhinga ki aí,” ko e lea fakavavevave ia ʻa e Tuʻí, peá ne hoko atu kiate ia ʻi ha leʻo siʻisiʻi, .

“mahuʻinga﻿—ʻikai mahuʻinga﻿—ʻikai mahuʻinga﻿—mahuʻinga﻿—” ʻo hangē ʻokú ne feinga pe ko e fē ʻa e foʻi lea ʻoku ongo lelei tahá.

Naʻe tohi ia ʻe ha niʻihi ʻo e kau fakamāú ʻo pehē “mahuʻinga,” pea ko e niʻihi ʻoku “taʻe mahuʻinga.” Naʻe lava ke sio ʻa ʻAlisi ki he meʻá ni, ʻi heʻene ofi feʻunga ke sio ʻi heʻenau ngaahi lauʻi peletí; “ka ʻoku ʻikai ke kiʻi mahuʻinga ia,” ko ʻene fakakaukau ia ʻiate iá.

‘I he momeniti ko ‘ení, na‘e kaila mai ‘a e Tu‘í, ‘a ia na‘e fuoloa ‘ene femo‘uekina ‘i he tohi ‘i he‘ene tohi noutí, ‘o pehē “Fakalongolongo!” pea lau atu mei heʻene tohí, “Tuʻutuʻuni Fangofulu Mā Ua. Ko e kakai kotoa pē ʻoku laka hake ʻi he maile ʻe tahá ke nau mavahe mei he fakamaauʻangá .”

Naʻe sio ʻa e tokotaha kotoa kia ʻAlisi.

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “ ʻOku ʻikai ke u maile ʻe taha ʻeku māʻolungá.”

“Ko koe,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí.

“Meimei maile ʻe ua hono māʻolungá,” ko e tānaki atu ia ʻa e Kuinií.

“Sai, he ʻikai te u ʻalu, ʻi ha faʻahinga tuʻunga pē,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí: “ʻikai ngata aí, ʻoku ʻikai ko ha tuʻutuʻuni angamaheni ia: naʻá ke faʻu ia he taimí ni pē.”

“Ko e tuʻutuʻuni motuʻa taha ia ʻi he tohí,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí.

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “Pea ʻoku totonu ke Fika ʻUluaki ia.”

Naʻe liliu ʻa e Tuʻí ʻo lanu engeenga, peá ne tāpuniʻi fakavavevave ʻene tohi noutí. “Fakakaukau ki hoʻomou tuʻutuʻuní,” ko ʻene lea ia ki he kau fakamāú, ʻi ha leʻo māʻulalo mo tetetete.

“ʻOku kei toe ha ngaahi fakamoʻoni ʻe hoko mai, kātaki hoʻo ʻAfió,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina, ʻo ne puna hake ʻi ha fuʻu fakavavevave; “kuo toki toʻo hake ʻa e pepa ko ʻení.”

“Ko e hā ʻoku ʻi aí?” Ko e lau ia ʻa e Kuini.

“ʻOku teʻeki ai ke u fakaava ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina, “ka ʻoku hangē ko ha tohi, naʻe tohi ʻe he pōpulá ki —ki ha taha.”

“Kuo pau pē naʻe pehē,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, “tukukehe kapau naʻe tohi ia ki ha taha, ʻa ia ʻoku ʻikai ko ha meʻa angamaheni, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi.”

“Ko hai ʻoku fakahangatonu ki aí?” ko e lau ia ʻa e taha ʻo e kau fakamāú.

“ʻOku ʻikai ʻaupito ke fakahinohinoʻi ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina; “ko hono moʻoní, ʻoku ʻikai ha meʻa ʻe tohi ʻi tuʻa .” Naʻá ne vete ʻa e laʻipepá ʻi heʻene leá, peá ne tānaki atu “ʻOku ʻikai ko ha tohi ia, hiliō he meʻa kotoa: ko ha seti ʻo e ngaahi veesi.”

“ʻOku nau ʻi he tohinima ʻo e pōpulá?” ko e fehuʻi ia ʻa e taha ʻo e kau fakamāú.

“ʻIkai, ʻoku ʻikai ke nau pehē,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina, “pea ko e meʻa ngali kehe taha ia ʻi he meʻa ko iá.” (Naʻe sio puputuʻu kotoa ʻa e kau fakamāú.)

“Kuo pau pē naʻá ne faʻifaʻitaki ki he nima ʻo ha taha kehe,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí. (Naʻe toe ngingila kotoa ʻa e kau fakamāú.)

“Kātaki hoʻo ʻAfió,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Knave, “naʻe ʻikai ke u tohi ia, pea ʻoku ʻikai ke nau lava ʻo fakamoʻoniʻi naʻá ku tohi ia: ʻoku ʻikai ha hingoa ʻoku fakamoʻoni hingoa ʻi he ngataʻangá.”

“Kapau naʻe ʻikai ke ke fakamoʻoni hingoa ki ai,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, “ʻoku toe fakakoviʻi ange pē ʻe he meʻa ko iá ʻa e meʻá ni. Kuo pau pē naʻá ke ʻuhinga ki ha ngaahi meʻa fakatupu ʻita, ka ʻikai naʻá ke mei fakamoʻoni hingoa ki ho hingoá ʻo hangē ha tangata faitotonú.”

Naʻe ʻi ai ha pakipaki nima fakalūkufua ʻi he meʻá ni: ko e fuofua meʻa fakapotopoto moʻoni ia naʻe lea ʻaki ʻe he Tuʻí ʻi he ʻaho ko iá.

“ ʻOku fakamoʻoniʻi ʻe he meʻa ko iá ʻene halaiá,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Kuiní.

“ʻOku ʻikai ke ne fakamoʻoniʻi ha meʻa ʻo e faʻahinga ko iá!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí. “ʻĒ, ʻoku ʻikai ke ke ʻiloʻi ʻa e meʻa ʻoku nau fekauʻaki mo iá!”

Naʻe pehē ʻe he Tuʻí, “Lau kinautolu.”

Naʻe tui ʻe he Lāpisi Hinehina ʻene sioʻatá. “Te u kamata mei fē, fakahōifua ki hoʻo ʻAfió?” ko ʻene fehuʻí ia.

“Kamata mei he kamataʻangá,” ko e lea fakamātoato ia ʻa e Tuʻí, “pea hoko atu kae ʻoua kuó ke aʻu ki he ngataʻangá: pea tuʻu leva.”

Ko e ngaahi veesi eni naʻe lau ʻe he Lāpisi Hinehina:—

“Na‘a nau tala mai na‘á ke ‘alu kiate ia, .
Pea lave mai kiate au kiate ia:
Naʻá ne ʻomi kiate au ha ʻulungaanga lelei, .
Ka naʻá ne pehē ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo luelue.

Naʻá ne fekauʻi atu kiate kinautolu ʻa e tala kuo teʻeki ke u ʻalu .
(ʻOku mau ʻiloʻi ʻoku moʻoni):
Kapau ‘oku totonu ke ne teke‘i ‘a e me‘á, .
Ko e hā ʻe hoko kiate koé?

Naʻá ku ʻoange kiate ia ʻa e taha, naʻa nau ʻoange kiate ia ʻa e ua, .
Naʻá ke foaki mai ha toko tolu pe lahi ange;
Na‘a nau foki kotoa meiate ia kiate koe, .
Neongo na'a'aku kinautolu kimu'a.

Kapau 'oku totonu ke u faingamalie ke .
Kau ‘i he me‘á ni, .
‘Okú ne falala kiate koe ke ke fakatau‘atāina‘i kinautolu, .
Hangē pē ko kimautolú.

Ko ʻeku fakakaukau naʻá ke ʻosi .
(Kimu'a pea ne ma'u 'a e fit ko 'eni)
Ko ha fakafe‘ātungia na‘e hoko mai ‘i he vaha‘a ‘o e .
Ko Ia, mo kitautolu, mo ia.

ʻOua naʻá ke ʻiloʻi naʻá ne saiʻia taha ʻiate kinaua, .
He kuo pau ke hoko ʻeni ʻi ha taimi .
Ko ha fakapulipuli, tauhi mei he toenga kotoa, .
ʻI ho vahaʻa mo aú.”

“Ko e fakamoʻoni mahuʻinga taha ia kuo mau fanongo ki aí,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, ʻo ne milimili hono ongo nimá; “ko ia ko ʻeni tuku ʻa e kau fakamāú—”

“Kapau ʻe lava ʻe ha taha ʻo kinaua ʻo fakamatalaʻi ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí, (naʻá ne tupu ʻo lahi ʻaupito ʻi he ngaahi miniti siʻi kuohilí ʻo ʻikai ke ne kiʻi manavasiʻi ke fakalaveaʻi ia), “Te u ʻoange kiate ia ha seniti ʻe ono. ʻOku ʻikai ke u tui ʻoku ʻi ai ha ʻatomi ʻo e ʻuhinga ʻi aí.”

Naʻe tohi kotoa ʻe he kau fakamāú ʻi heʻenau ngaahi lauʻi pepá, “ ʻOku ʻikai ke ne tui ʻoku ʻi ai ha ʻatomi ʻo e ʻuhinga ʻi aí,” ka naʻe ʻikai ha taha ʻo kinautolu ʻe feinga ke fakamatalaʻi e pepá.

“Kapau ʻoku ʻikai ha ʻuhinga ʻi ai,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, “ʻoku fakahaofi ai ha māmani ʻo e faingataʻá, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi, ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku ʻikai fie maʻu ke tau feinga ke kumi ha taha. Pea neongo ia ʻoku ʻikai ke u ʻilo,” ko ʻene hoko atu ia, ʻo ne folahi atu ʻa e ngaahi vēsí ʻi hono tuí, pea sio kiate kinautolu ʻaki ʻa e mata ʻe tahá; “ʻOku hangē ʻoku ou sio ki ha ʻuhinga ʻiate kinautolu, hili iá. “— naʻá ne pehē ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo luelue —” ʻoku ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo luelue, ʻikai ko ia?” ko ʻene tānaki atu ia, ʻo ne tafoki ki he Knave.

Naʻe lulululu lotomamahi ʻa e Knave. “ʻOku ou fōtunga hangē ko iá?” ko ʻene leá ia. (ʻA ia naʻe ʻikai moʻoni ke ne fai ia , ʻi hono ngaohi kotoa pē ʻaki ʻa e kaati.)

“Sai, ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimí ni,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, peá ne hoko atu ʻene lāunga ʻi he ngaahi vēsí kiate ia pē: “‘ ʻOku mau ʻiloʻi ʻoku moʻoni ia —’ ko e kau fakamāú ia, ko e moʻoni —’ Naʻá ku ʻoange kiate ia ha taha, naʻa nau ʻoange kiate ia ha ua —’ ko e hā, kuo pau ko e meʻa ia naʻá ne fai ki he ngaahi tātí, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi—”

“Ka, ‘oku hoko atu ‘ na‘a nau foki kotoa meiate ia kiate koe ,’” ko e lau ia ‘a ‘Alisí.

“ʻIkai, ʻoku nau ʻi ai!” ko e lea ikuna ia ʻa e Tuʻí, ʻo ne tuhu ki he ngaahi tā ʻi he funga tēpilé. “ʻOku ʻikai ha meʻa ʻe toe mahino ange ʻi he meʻa ko iá . Pea toe —‘ kimuʻa peá ne maʻu ʻa e mahaki ko ʻení —’ naʻe ʻikai ke ke teitei maʻu ha ngaahi mahaki, ʻofaʻanga, ʻoku ou fakakaukau?” ko ‘ene lea ia ki he Kuini.

"Oua 'aupito!" ko e lea fakaʻita ia ʻa e Kuinií, ʻo ne lī ha tuʻunga vaitohi ki he Lisi ʻi heʻene leá. (Naʻe tuku ʻe he kiʻi Pili fakaʻofa ʻene tohi ʻi heʻene peletí ʻaki ha louhiʻi nima ʻe taha, ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi naʻe ʻikai haʻane fakaʻilonga; ka naʻá ne toe kamata fakavavevave he taimí ni, ʻo fakaʻaongaʻi ʻa e vaitohí, ʻa ia naʻe tafe hifo ʻi hono fofongá, ʻi heʻene kei ʻi aí.)

“Pea ʻoku ʻikai ke feʻunga ʻa e ngaahi leá mo koe,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Tuʻí, ʻo ne sio takai holo ʻi he lotoʻaá ʻo malimali. Naʻe ʻi ai ha fakalongolongo mate.

“Ko ha pun ia!” naʻe tānaki mai ʻe he Tuʻí ʻi ha leʻo fakaʻita, pea naʻe kata ʻa e tokotaha kotoa, “Tuku ke fakakaukauʻi ʻe he kau fakamāú ʻenau tuʻutuʻuní.” ko e lau ia ʻa e Tuʻí, ko e tuʻo uofulu nai ia ʻi he ʻaho ko iá.

“ʻIkai, ʻikai!” Ko e lau ia ʻa e Kuini. “ʻUluaki sētesí —tuʻutuʻuni ʻi he hili iá.”

“Meʻa mo e ngaahi meʻa vale!” ko e lea leʻolahi ia ʻa ʻAlisí. “Ko e fakakaukau ke ʻuluaki maʻu ʻa e sētesí!”

“Taʻofi ho ʻeleló!” ko e lau ia ʻa e Kuinií, ʻo ne liliu lanu vāleti.

“He ʻikai te u fai ia!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Off mo hono ʻulú!” ko e kaila ia ʻa e Kuini ʻi he tumutumu ʻo hono leʻó. Naʻe ʻikai ke ngaʻunu ha taha.

“Ko hai ʻokú ne tokangaʻi koé?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, (naʻá ne tupu ʻo aʻu ki hono lahi kakató ʻi he taimi ko ʻení). “ʻOku ʻikai ha meʻa koe ka ko ha puha kaati pē!”

ʻI he meʻá ni naʻe tuʻu hake ʻa e fuʻu ʻakaú kotoa ki he ʻeá, ʻo nau lele hifo kiate ia: naʻá ne fai ha kiʻi kaila, ko e vaeua ʻo e manavahē mo e vaeua ʻo e ʻitá, peá ne feinga ke taaʻi kinaua, peá ne ʻiloʻi ʻokú ne tokoto ʻi he veʻe vaí, mo hono ʻulú ʻi he fatafata ʻo hono tuofefiné, ʻa ia naʻá ne fufulu māmālie ha ngaahi lauʻiʻakau mate ʻi hono fofongá ʻa ia naʻe ʻi ai ʻa e fulufuluʻi ʻakaú.

“ʻĀ hake, ʻofaʻanga ʻAlisi!” ko e lau ia ʻa hono tuofefiné; “ʻĒ, ko e mohe fuoloa moʻoni ia kuó ke maʻú!”

“ʻOiauē, kuó u maʻu ha misi fieʻilo pehē!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, peá ne talaange ki hono tuofefiné, ʻi heʻene lava ʻo manatuʻí, ʻa ʻene ngaahi ʻAvenisi ngali kehe kotoa ko ʻeni ʻokú ke toki lau ki aí; pea ʻi heʻene ʻosí, naʻe ʻuma ange hono tuofefiné kiate ia, ʻo ne pehē ange, “ Ko ha misi fakatupu ʻilo ia, ʻofaʻanga, ʻoku pau: ka ko ʻeni lele mai ki hoʻo tií; kuo tōmui.” Ko ia naʻe tuʻu hake ʻa ʻAlisi ʻo lele atu, ʻo ne fakakaukau lolotonga ʻene lelé, ʻo hangē pē ko ʻene leá, ko ha misi fakaʻofoʻofa moʻoni ia.

Ka naʻe tangutu noa pē hono tuofefiné ʻi heʻene mavahe meiate iá, ʻo ne fakafalala hono ʻulú ki hono nimá, ʻo siofi ʻa e tō ʻa e laʻaá, mo fakakaukau ki he kiʻi ʻAlisi mo ʻene ngaahi ʻAveni fakaofo kotoa pē, kae ʻoua kuo kamata ke ne misi foki ʻo fakatatau ki ha founga, pea ko ʻene misí ʻeni:—

ʻUluakí, naʻá ne misi ki he kiʻi ʻAlisi tonu, pea naʻe toe puke ʻa e fanga kiʻi nimá ʻi hono tuí, pea naʻe sio hake ʻa e ongo mata vēkeveke ngingilá ki hono matá —naʻá ne lava ʻo fanongo ki he ngaahi leʻo tonu ʻo hono leʻó, pea sio ki he kiʻi luelue fakaofo ko ia ʻo hono ʻulú ke taʻofi ʻa e fulufuluʻi ʻulu hēhētuʻu ʻa ia ʻe lisi maʻu pē ki hono matá, naʻá ne sio maʻu pē ki hono matá. naʻe hoko ʻo moʻui ʻa e feituʻu takatakai ʻiate iá mo e ngaahi meʻamoʻui ngali kehe ʻo e misi ʻa hono kiʻi tuofefiné.

Naʻe ngungu ʻa e mohuku lōloá ʻi hono vaʻé ʻi he fakavavevave atu ʻa e Lāpisi Hinehina —naʻe puna ʻe he Mouse ilifiá ʻa hono halá ʻi he vaitupu kaungāʻapí —naʻá ne lava ʻo fanongo ki he ngungu ʻa e ngaahi ipu tií ʻi he vahevahe ʻe he Hale ʻo Māʻasí mo hono ngaahi kaungāmeʻá ʻenau meʻakai taʻe ngata, pea ko e leʻo ngingila ʻo e Kuini ʻokú ne fekauʻi ʻa e execu lahi ange — ‘o ne mafu ‘i he tuliki ‘o e Duchess, lolotonga ia ‘oku pā takai holo ‘a e ngaahi peletí mo e ngaahi ipu —na‘e toe ‘asi mai ‘a e kaila ‘a e Gryphon, ko e ngingila ‘a e penitala ‘a e Lizard, mo e ngingila ‘a e fanga ki‘i puaka-kiini kuo ta‘ofi, ‘o fakafonu ‘a e ‘eá, ‘o fefiofi mo e ngaahi tangi mick miser miser mama‘o ‘a e Turt.

Ko ia naʻá ne tangutu ai, ʻo moʻui hono matá, pea naʻá ne tui ʻa e vaeua ʻo ia ʻi he Fonua Fakaofó, neongo naʻá ne ʻiloʻi kuo pau pē ke ne toe fakaava kinaua, pea ʻe liliu kotoa ia ki he moʻoni fakapoʻulí —ʻe ngingila pē ʻa e mohukú ʻi he matangí, pea ʻe ngingila ʻa e vaitupú ki he lulululu ʻa e ngaahi kahoá —ʻe liliu ʻa e ngaahi ipu tī ʻoku ngingilá ki he ngaahi leʻo ʻo e Kuinií ki he ngingilá, ki he ngingilá. ʻo e tamasiʻi tauhi-sipí —pea ko e mafu ʻa e pēpeé, ko e kaila ʻa e Kalifoní, mo e ngaahi longoaʻa fakaofo kehe kotoa pē, ʻe liliu ia (naʻá ne ʻiloʻi) ki he longoaʻa puputuʻu ʻo e lotoʻā-faama femoʻuekiná —lolotonga ia ʻe fetongi ʻe he tangi ʻa e fanga pulú ʻi he mamaʻó ʻa e ngaahi tangi mamafa ʻa e Mock Turtle.

Fakaʻosí, naʻá ne fakakaukauloto pē kiate ia ʻa e founga ʻe hoko ai ʻa hono kiʻi tuofefine tatau pē ko ʻení, ʻi he hili ʻa e taimí, ko ha fefine matuʻotuʻa; pea mo e founga te ne tauhi ai, ʻi hono ngaahi taʻu matuʻotuʻá kotoa, ʻa e loto faingofua mo ʻofa ʻo ʻene kei siʻí: pea mo e founga te ne fakatahatahaʻi ai ʻene fānau iiki kehé, pea ʻai ke ngingila mo vēkeveke honau matá ʻaki ha talanoa ngali kehe lahi, mahalo naʻa mo e misi ʻo e Fonua Fakaofó ʻo e kuonga muʻá: pea mo e founga te ne ongoʻi ai ʻi heʻenau ngaahi mamahi faingofua kotoa pē ʻi heʻenau kei siʻí, pea maʻu ha ngaahi fiefia faingofua kotoa pē ʻi heʻenau manatuʻí. mo‘ui fakatamaiki, mo e ngaahi ‘aho fiefia ‘o e fa‘ahita‘u māfaná.

KO E FAKA'OSI .
