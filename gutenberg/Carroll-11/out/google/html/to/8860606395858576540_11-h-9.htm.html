KO E VAHE IX.
Ko e Talanoa ʻo e Fonua Fakaanga

“He ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo fakakaukau ki heʻeku fiefia ke toe sio kiate koé, ʻa koe meʻa motuʻa ʻofeina!” ko e lau ia ʻa e Duchess, ʻi heʻene fakahū ʻofa hono nimá ki he nima ʻo ʻAlisí, peá na lue atu fakataha.

Naʻe fiefia ʻaupito ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi ia ʻi ha ʻita fakafiefia pehē, peá ne fakakaukau loto pē mahalo ko e pepa pē naʻá ne ʻai ia ke ne fuʻu anga-fakamamahi ʻi heʻena fetaulaki ʻi he loki kuí.

“ʻI he taimi ʻoku ou hoko ai ko ha Duchess,” naʻá ne pehē kiate ia pē, (ʻikai ʻi ha leʻo ʻamanaki lelei ʻaupito neongo ia), “He ʻikai ke u maʻu ha pepa ʻi hoku loki kuí ʻi ha meʻa . ʻOku fuʻu lelei ʻaupito ʻa e supó ʻo ʻikai ha—Mahalo ko e pepa maʻu pē ʻokú ne ʻai ʻa e kakaí ke nau ʻita vela,” ko ʻene hoko atu ia, ʻo fiefia lahi ʻaupito ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi ha faʻahinga foʻou ʻo e rugar, viur, makesdne them . camomile ʻokú ne ʻai ke nau kona —pea —mo e paʻale-suka mo e ngaahi meʻa pehē ʻokú ne ʻai ʻa e fānaú ke nau ʻita melié ʻoku ou fakaʻamu pē ke ʻiloʻi ia ʻe he kakaí : pea ʻe ʻikai leva ke nau fuʻu fakapikopiko ki ai, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi —”

Naʻe ʻosi ngalo ʻaupito ʻiate ia ʻa e Duchess ʻi he taimi ko ʻení, pea naʻá ne kiʻi ʻohovale ʻi heʻene fanongo ki hono leʻó ʻo ofi ki hono telingá. “ʻOkú ke fakakaukau ki ha meʻa, ʻofaʻanga, pea ʻoku ngalo ai ʻiate koe ke ke talanoa. ʻOku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo talaatu he taimí ni pe ko e hā ʻa e ʻulungaanga ʻo e meʻa ko iá, ka te u manatuʻi ia ʻi ha kiʻi taimi.”

“Mahalo pē ‘oku ‘ikai ha‘ane taha,” ko e loto-to‘a ia ‘a ‘Alisi ke fakatokanga‘í.

“Tut, tut, tamasiʻi!” ko e lau ia ʻa e Duchess. “ʻOku ʻi ai ha ʻulungaanga ʻo e meʻa kotoa pē, kapau pē te ke lava ʻo kumi ia.” Pea naʻá ne fakaʻau ke ofi ange ki he tafaʻaki ʻo ʻAlisí ʻi heʻene leá.

Naʻe ʻikai fuʻu saiʻia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi heʻene nofo ofi pehē kiate iá: ʻuluakí, koeʻuhí he naʻe fakalielia ʻaupito ʻa e Duchess; pea ko hono uá, koeʻuhí he naʻá ne māʻolunga totonu tonu ke ne hili hono ʻaofinimá ʻi he uma ʻo ʻAlisí, pea ko ha ʻaofinima māsila taʻefakafiemālie ia. Kae kehe, na‘e ‘ikai te ne sai‘ia ke anga-kovi, ko ia na‘á ne kātaki‘i ia ‘i he lelei taha te ne lavá.

“ʻOku ʻikai ke fuʻu lelei ange ʻa e vaʻingá he taimí ni,” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻi ha founga ʻo ʻene kiʻi hokohoko atu ʻa e fetalanoaʻakí.

“‘Oku pehē ia,” ko e lea ia ‘a e Duchess: “pea ko e ‘ulungaanga ‘o e me‘a ko iá ko e —‘‘Oiauē, ‘ko e ‘ofa, ko e ‘ofa, ‘okú ne ‘ai ‘a e māmaní ke takai holo!’”

“Naʻe pehē ʻe ha taha,” ko e muhumuhu ia ʻa ʻAlisí, “ʻoku fai ia ʻe he tokotaha kotoa pē ʻo tokanga ki heʻene meʻa pē ʻaʻaná!”

“ʻA, sai! ʻOku ʻuhinga tatau pē ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess, ʻo ne keli hono kiʻi ʻaofinima māsila ʻi he uma ʻo ʻAlisí ʻi heʻene tānaki atu, “pea ko e ʻulungaanga ʻo e meʻa ko iá ko e —‘Tokangaʻi ʻa e ʻuhingá, pea ʻe tokangaʻi ʻe he ngaahi leʻó kinautolu pē.'”

“He manako ē ko ia ‘i hono kumi ‘a e ngaahi ‘ulungāanga leleí ‘i he ngaahi me‘á!” Naʻe fakakaukau loto pē ʻa ʻAlisi.

“ʻOku ou loto-toʻa ke pehē ʻokú ke fifili pe ko e hā ʻoku ʻikai ke u ʻai ai hoku nimá ʻi ho kongalotó,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess hili haʻane kiʻi tuʻu: “ko e ʻuhingá, ʻoku ou veiveiua ki he ʻita ʻa hoʻo flamingo. Te u ʻahiʻahiʻi nai ʻa e ʻahiʻahí?”

“Mahalo te ne uʻu,” ko e tali fakatokanga ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻo ʻikai ke ne ongoʻi hohaʻa ʻaupito ke ʻahiʻahiʻi ʻa e ʻahiʻahí.

“Mo‘oni ‘aupito,” ko e lau ia ‘a e Duchess: “‘oku fakatou u‘u ‘a e flamingo mo e mustard. Pea ko e ‘ulungaanga ‘o e me‘a ko iá—‘Oku fakatahataha fakataha ‘a e fanga manupuna ‘o e fulufulu‘i manupuná.’”

“Ko e musita pē ʻoku ʻikai ko ha manupuna,” ko e fakatokanga ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Tonu, hangē ko e angamahení,” ko e lea ia ‘a e Duchess: “ko e founga mahino ē ‘okú ke ma‘u ‘i hono fokotu‘u ‘o e ngaahi me‘á!”

“Ko ha minerale ia, ʻoku ou fakakaukau ,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Ko e moʻoni ʻoku pehē,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess, ʻa ia naʻe ngali mateuteu ke loto ki he meʻa kotoa pē naʻe lea ʻaki ʻe ʻAlisí; “ʻoku ʻi ai ha fuʻu keliʻanga musita ofi mai heni. Pea ko e ʻulungaanga ʻo e meʻa ko iá —‘Ko e lahi ange ʻeku meʻá, ko e siʻisiʻi ange ia ʻa hoʻo meʻá.’”

“ʻOiauē, ʻoku ou ʻilo!” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻAlisi, ʻa ia naʻe ʻikai ke ne kau mai ki he lea fakaʻosi ko ʻení, “ko ha vesitapolo ia. ʻOku ʻikai ke hangē ia ha taha, ka ʻoku pehē.”

“ʻOku ou mātuʻaki loto-tatau mo koe,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess; “pea ko e ʻulungaanga ʻo e meʻa ko iá ko e —‘Ke ke hoko ko e meʻa te ke ngali hoko ki aí’ —pe kapau te ke saiʻia ke fakalea faingofua ange —ʻʻOua naʻá ke teitei fakakaukau ʻoku ʻikai ke ke kehe mei he meʻa ʻe ala hā ki he niʻihi kehé ko e meʻa naʻá ke ʻi aí pe naʻe mei hoko ki aí ʻoku ʻikai ke kehe ia mei he meʻa naʻá ke ʻi aí naʻe mei hā kiate kinautolu ʻoku kehé.’”

“ʻOku ou pehē ʻoku totonu ke mahino lelei ange kiate au ʻa e meʻa ko iá,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he anga fakaʻapaʻapa ʻaupito, “kapau naʻá ku tohiʻi ia: ka ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo muimui kakato ki ai ʻo hangē ko hoʻo leá.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ko ha meʻa ia ki he meʻa te u lava ʻo lea ʻakí kapau te u filí,” ko e tali ia ʻa e Duchess, ʻi ha leʻo fiefia.

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “Lotu ʻoua naʻá ke fakahohaʻasi koe ke ke leaʻaki ia ʻo toe lōloa ange ʻi he meʻa ko iá.”

“ʻOiauē, ʻoua naʻá ke talanoa ki he faingataʻá!” ko e lau ia ʻa e Duchess. “ʻOku ou ʻai koe ko ha meʻaʻofa ʻo e meʻa kotoa pē kuó u lea ʻaki ʻo aʻu mai ki he taimí ni.”

“Ko ha fa‘ahinga me‘a‘ofa ma‘ama‘a!” ko e fakakaukau ia ʻa ʻAlisí. “ʻOku ou fiefia he ʻikai ke nau foaki ha ngaahi meʻaʻofa ʻaho fāʻeleʻi pehē!” Ka na‘e ‘ikai te ne loto-to‘a ke lea‘aki le‘o-lahi ia.

“Toe fakakaukau?” ko e fehuʻi ia ʻa e Duchess, ʻo ne toe keli hono kiʻi ʻaofinima māsila.

“ʻOku ʻi ai ʻeku totonu ke fakakaukau,” ko e lea māsila ia ʻa ʻAlisí, he naʻe kamata ke ne ongoʻi kiʻi hohaʻa.

“ʻOku meimei totonu tatau pē,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Duchess, “ʻo hangē ko e pau ke lele ʻa e fanga puaká; pea ko e m—”

Ka ʻi heni, naʻe ʻohovale lahi ʻa ʻAlisi, ʻi he mate ʻa e leʻo ʻo e Duchess, naʻa mo e lotolotonga ʻo ʻene foʻi lea manakoa taha ko e ‘moral,’ pea kamata ke tetetete ʻa e nima naʻe fehokotaki ki hono nimá. Naʻe sio hake ʻa ʻAlisi, pea naʻe tuʻu ʻa e Kuini ʻi muʻa ʻiate kinaua, ʻo ne fusi hono ongo nimá, ʻo ne fūfū hono matá ʻo hangē ha manamanaá.

“Ko ha ‘aho lelei, ho‘o ‘Afió!” ko e kamata ia ʻa e Duchess ʻi ha leʻo māʻulalo mo vaivai.

“Ko ʻeni, ʻoku ou ʻoatu ha fakatokanga totonu,” ko e kaila ia ʻa e Kuinií, ʻo ne luelue ʻi he kelekelé ʻi heʻene leá; “pe ko koe pe ko ho ‘ulú kuo pau ke ‘osi, pea ko e me‘a ia ‘i he meimei vaeua ‘o e taimi ‘ikai! To‘o ho‘o filí!”

Naʻe toʻo ʻe he Duchess ʻene filí, pea naʻe ʻosi ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou.

“Ta hoko atu ʻa e vaʻingá,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Kuini kia ʻAlisí; pea naʻe fuʻu ilifia ʻaupito ʻa ʻAlisi ke ne lea ʻaki ha foʻi lea, ka naʻá ne muimui māmālie atu ʻo foki ki he malaʻe koloketí.

Na'e ngaue'aki 'e he kau fakaafe kehe 'a e 'ikai ke 'i ai 'a e Kuini, pea na'a nau malolo 'i he malumalu: ka neongo ia, ko e momeniti pe na'a nau sio ai kiate ia, na'a nau foki fakavavevave ki he va'inga, ko e fakatokanga'i pe 'e he Kuini 'e mole 'enau mo'ui 'i ha ki'i toloi.

Ko e taimi kotoa pe na’a nau va’inga ai na’e ‘ikai ke tuku ‘e he Kuini ‘ene fakakikihi mo e kau va’inga kehe, pea kaila “Off with his head!” pe “Off mo hono ‘ulú!” Ko kinautolu naʻá ne tauteá naʻe ʻave kinautolu ki he pilīsoné ʻe he kau sōtiá, ʻa ia ko e moʻoni naʻe pau ke nau tuku ʻenau hoko ko e kau ʻākesí ke nau fai ʻeni, ko ia ʻi he ʻosi ʻa e vaeua houa pe lahi ange naʻe ʻikai toe ʻi ai ha ʻākesí, pea ko e kau vaʻingá kotoa, tukukehe ʻa e Tuʻí, Kuini, mo ʻAlisí, naʻa nau ʻi he pilīsoné pea ʻi he malumalu ʻo e tautea fakapoongi.

Pea naʻe mavahe leva ʻa e Kuinií, ʻo ʻosi ʻene mānavá, peá ne pehē ange kia ʻAlisi, “Kuó ke ʻosi sio ki he Mock Turtle?”

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe ʻAlisi, “ʻIkai.” “ʻOku ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi pe ko e hā ʻa e Mock Turtle.”

“Ko e meʻa ia ʻoku ngaohi mei ai ʻa e Supo Mock Turtle,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Kuini.

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “Naʻe ʻikai ke u teitei sio ki ha taha, pe fanongo ki ha taha.”

“Ko ia, ha‘u,” ko e lea ia ‘a e Kuini, “pea te ne tala atu hono hisitōliá.”

ʻI heʻena lue fakatahá, naʻe fanongo ʻa ʻAlisi ki he lea leʻo siʻi ʻa e Tuʻí, ki he kautaha fakalūkufuá, “Kuo fakamolemoleʻi kotoa kimoutolu.” “Ha’u, ko e me’a lelei ia !” naʻá ne pehē kiate ia pē, he naʻá ne ongoʻi taʻefiefia ʻaupito ʻi he lahi ʻo e ngaahi fakapoongi naʻe fekauʻi ʻe he Kuinií.

Naʻe vave ʻaupito ʻenau aʻu atu ki ha Kilifoni, ʻoku tokoto mohe ʻi he laʻaá. (Kapau ʻoku ʻikai ke ke ʻiloʻi pe ko e hā ʻa e Kalifoní, vakai ki he fakatātaá.) “ʻAlu hake, meʻa fakapikopiko!” ko e lau ia ʻa e Kuini, “pea ʻave ʻa e finemui ko ʻení ke sio ki he Mock Turtle, pea ke fanongo ki hono hisitōliá. Kuo pau ke u foki ʻo sio hili ha ngaahi fakapoongi ʻe niʻihi kuó u fekauʻí;” peá ne ʻalu atu, ʻo tuku tokotaha pē ʻa ʻAlisi mo e Kalifoní. Naʻe ʻikai ke fuʻu saiʻia ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he fōtunga ʻo e meʻamoʻuí, ka ʻi hono fakakātoá naʻá ne fakakaukau ʻe malu ʻaupito ke nofo mo ia ʻo hangē ko ʻene ʻalu ʻo muimui ki he Kuini anga-fakamamahi ko iá: ko ia naʻá ne tatali ai.

Na'e tangutu hake 'a e Kilifoni 'o milimili hono mata: pea ne siofi leva 'a e Kuini 'o a'u ki he'ene 'ikai toe sio ki ai: pea ne kata. “He fakafiefia moʻoni!” ko e lau ia ʻa e Kalifoní, ʻo vaeua kiate ia pē, vaeua kia ʻAlisi.

“Ko e hā ʻa e fakafiefiá?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Ko e hā, ko ia ,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Kalifoní. “Ko ʻene fakaʻofoʻofa kotoa pē, ʻa ia: ʻoku ʻikai ke nau teitei fakapoongi ha taha, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi. Haʻu!"

“ʻOku pehē ʻe he tokotaha kotoa pē ʻhaʻu!' ‘i heni,” ko e fakakaukau ia ‘a ‘Alisí, ‘i he‘ene ‘alu māmālie atu ‘o muimui aí: “Kuo te‘eki ai ke u tu‘utu‘uni pehē ‘i he kotoa ‘o ‘eku mo‘uí, ‘ikai ‘aupito!”

Naʻe teʻeki ke na ʻalu mamaʻo kimuʻa peá na sio ki he Mock Turtle ʻi he mamaʻó, ʻokú ne tangutu lotomamahi mo tuēnoa ʻi ha kiʻi ʻotu maka, pea, ʻi heʻena ofi angé, naʻe lava ke fanongo ʻa ʻAlisi ki heʻene mapuhoi ʻo hangē ʻe mafesifesi hono lotó. Naʻá ne fakaʻofaʻia lahi ʻiate ia. “Ko e hā ‘ene mamahí?” ko ʻene fehuʻi ia ki he Kalifoní, pea tali ange ʻe he Kalifoní, ʻo meimei ʻaupito ʻi he ngaahi lea tatau pē mo ia kimuʻá, “Ko ʻene fakaʻofoʻofa kotoa pē, ʻa ia: ʻoku teʻeki ke ne maʻu ha loto mamahi, ʻokú ke ʻiloʻi. Haʻu!”

Ko ia naʻá na ʻalu hake ki he Mock Turtle, ʻa ia naʻá ne sio kiate kinaua ʻaki ha ongo mata lalahi fonu ʻi he loʻimatá, ka naʻe ʻikai ke ne lea ʻaki ha meʻa.

“Ko e finemui ko ʻeni ʻi hení,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Kalifoní, “ʻokú ne fie maʻu ke ʻiloʻi ho hisitōliá, ʻokú ne ʻiloʻi ia.”

“Te u talaange ia kiate ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Mock Turtle ʻi ha leʻo loloto mo ʻataʻatā: “nofo hifo, kimoua fakatouʻosi, pea ʻoua naʻá mo lea ʻaki ha foʻi lea kae ʻoua kuó u ʻosi.”

Ko ia naʻá na tangutu hifo, pea ʻikai ke lea ha taha ʻi ha ngaahi miniti. Naʻe fakakaukau loto pē ʻa ʻAlisi, “ʻOku ʻikai ke u sio pe ʻe lava fēfē ke ne faifai pea fakaʻosi, kapau he ʻikai ke ne kamata.” Ka naʻá ne tatali ʻi he faʻa kātaki.

“ʻI ha taimi ʻe taha,” ko e lea fakaʻosi ia ʻa e Mock Turtle, mo haʻane mapuhoi loloto, “Ko ha Turtle moʻoni au.”

Naʻe muimui ʻi he ngaahi leá ni ha fakalongolongo fuoloa ʻaupito, ʻo motuhi pē ʻe ha kaila ʻi he taimi ʻe niʻihi ʻo e “Hjckrrh!” mei he Kalifoní, pea mo e tangi mamafa maʻu pē ʻa e Mock Turtle. Naʻe meimei ʻaupito ke tuʻu hake ʻa ʻAlisi ʻo pehē ange, “Mālō, tangataʻeiki, hoʻo talanoa mālié,” ka naʻe ʻikai ke ne lava ʻo taʻofi ʻene fakakaukau kuo pau ke toe ʻi ai ha meʻa ʻe hoko mai, ko ia naʻá ne tangutu pē ʻo ʻikai ke ne lea ʻaki ha meʻa.

“ʻI heʻemau kei siʻí,” ko e hoko atu ia ʻa e Mock Turtle ʻi he fakaʻosí, ʻo ne toe nonga ange, neongo ʻene kei kiʻi tangi ʻi he taimi ko iá, “naʻá ma ʻalu ki he akó ʻi he tahí. Ko e ʻeikí ko ha motuʻa ʻo e Fonuá —naʻá ma faʻa ui ia ko e Fonua —”

“Ko e hā naʻá ke ui ai ia ko Toa, kapau naʻe ʻikai ko ha taha ia?” Ko e fehuʻi ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Naʻá ma ui ia ko e Toa koeʻuhí he naʻá ne akoʻi kimaua,” ko e lea ʻita ia ʻa e Toa Fakakata: “moʻoni ʻokú ke fuʻu fakapikopiko!”

“ʻOku totonu ke ke mā ʻiate koe ʻi hoʻo fai ha fehuʻi faingofua pehē,” ko e tānaki atu ia ʻa e Kalifoní; pea naʻá na fakatou tangutu fakalongolongo pē ʻo sio kia ʻAlisi masivá, ʻa ia naʻá ne ongoʻi mateuteu ke ngoto ki he kelekelé. Naʻe faifai pea pehē ange ʻe he Kalifoní ki he Mock Turtle, “Fakaʻuli atu, motuʻa! ʻOua naʻá ke ʻaho kakato ʻi he meʻa ko iá!" peá ne hoko atu ʻi he ngaahi leá ni:

“ʻIo, naʻá ma ʻalu ki he akó ʻi tahí, neongo mahalo he ʻikai ke ke tui ki ai﻿—”

“Naʻe ʻikai ke u teitei pehē ʻoku ʻikai!” ko e fakalavea ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Naʻá ke fai ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Mock Turtle.

“Taʻofi ho ʻeleló!” ko e tānaki atu ia ʻa e Kalifoní, kimuʻa pea toki lea ʻa ʻAlisí. Naʻe hoko atu ʻa e Mock Turtle.

“Naʻá ma maʻu ʻa e ngaahi ako lelei tahá —ko hono moʻoní, naʻá ma ʻalu ki he akó ʻi he ʻaho kotoa pē —”

“ Kuó u ʻosi ʻalu foki ki ha akoʻanga ʻaho,” ko e lea ia ʻa ʻAlisí; “ʻoku ʻikai fie maʻu ke ke fuʻu pōlepole ʻo hangē ko e meʻa kotoa ko iá.”

“Mo e ngaahi meʻa fakalahi?” ko e fehuʻi ia ʻa e Mock Turtle ʻi ha kiʻi hohaʻa.

Na‘e pehē ‘e Alice: “‘Io, na‘á ma ako ‘a e lea faka-Falanisē mo e mūsiká.”

“Pea mo e fufulu?” ko e lau ia ʻa e Mock Turtle.

“ʻOku pau pē ʻoku ʻikai!” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí ʻi he ʻitá.

“ʻĀ!, pea naʻe ʻikai ko ha ʻapiako lelei moʻoni hoʻo ʻapiakó,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Mock Turtle ʻi ha leʻo ʻo e nonga lahi. “Ko ʻeni ʻi homau ʻapí naʻa nau maʻu ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻo e totongí, ʻFalanisē, mūsika, mo e fufulu —ʻoku toe lahi ange.’”

Naʻe pehē ʻe ʻAlisi, “Naʻe ʻikai mei lava ke ke fuʻu fie maʻu ia; “mo‘ui ‘i he lalo tahí.”

“Naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo ako ia.” ko e lau ia ʻa e Mock Turtle ʻi heʻene mapuhoi. “Naʻá ku toki fai pē ʻa e ako angamahení.”

“Ko e hā ʻa e meʻa ko iá?” ko e fehuʻi ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Ko e Tetetete mo e Tetetete, ko e moʻoni, ke kamata ʻaki,” ko e tali ia ʻa e Mock Turtle; “pea hoko mai leva ‘a e ngaahi va‘a kehekehe ‘o e ‘Atimetiká—Ko e Faka‘amu, Fakahoha‘a, Fakalielia, mo e Manuki.”

“Na‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito te u fanongo ki he ‘Uglification,’” ko e loto-to‘a ia ‘a ‘Alisi ke ne pehē. “Ko e hā ia?”

Naʻe hiki hake ʻe he Kalifoní hono ongo vaʻé ʻi heʻene ʻohovalá. "Ko e hā! Kuo teʻeki ai ke u fanongo ki he fakalieliá!" ko e kaila ia. “ʻOkú ke ʻiloʻi ʻa e meʻa ke fakaʻofoʻofá, ʻoku ou mahalo?”

“ʻIo,” ko e lea veiveiua ia ʻa ʻAlisí: “ʻoku ʻuhinga ia —ke —ngaohi —ha meʻa pē —ke fakaʻofoʻofa ange.”

“Sai, ko ia,” ko e hoko atu ia ‘a e Kalifoní, “kapau ‘oku ‘ikai te ke ‘ilo‘i ‘a e me‘a ke fakalieliá, ko ha tokotaha valevale koe.”

Naʻe ʻikai ke ongoʻi fakalotolahiʻi ʻa ʻAlisi ke ne toe fai ha ngaahi fehuʻi fekauʻaki mo ia, ko ia naʻá ne tafoki ki he Mock Turtle, ʻo ne pehē ange “Ko e hā ha toe meʻa naʻá ke toe ako?”

“Sai, naʻe ʻi ai ʻa e Misiteli,” ko e tali ia ʻa e Mock Turtle, ʻo ne lau ʻa e ngaahi kaveinga ʻi heʻene ngaahi flappers, “—Misiteli, motuʻa mo fakaonopooni, mo e Seaography: pea ko e Drawling —ko e Drawling-master ko ha motuʻa conger-eel, ʻa ia naʻe faʻa haʻu tuʻo taha he uike: naʻá ne akoʻi kimautolu ʻi he Drawling mo e Drails, Stretch.”

“Naʻe fēfē ʻa e meʻa ko iá ?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Sai, he ʻikai ke u lava ʻo fakahaaʻi atu ia kiate koe ʻe au,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Mock Turtle: "ʻOku ou fuʻu fefeka. Pea naʻe ʻikai ʻaupito ke ako ia ʻe he Kalifoní.”

“Naʻe ʻikai ha taimi,” ko e lau ia ʻa e Kalifoní: “Naʻá ku ʻalu ki he ʻeiki ʻo e Kalasiká, neongo ia. Ko ha pā motuʻa ia, naʻe pehē.”

“Naʻe ʻikai ke u teitei ʻalu kiate ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Mock Turtle ʻi heʻene mapuhoi: “naʻá ne akoʻi ʻa e Kata mo e Loto-mamahi, ko ʻenau faʻa leá ia.”

“Ko ia naʻá ne fai, ko ia naʻá ne fai,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Kalifoní, ʻo ne mapuhoi ʻi hono taimí; pea naʻe fufū ʻe he ongo meʻamoʻuí hona fofongá ʻi hona ongo vaʻé.

“Pea ko e houa ʻe fiha he ʻaho naʻá ke fai ai ha ngaahi lēsoní?” ko e lau ia ʻa ʻAlisí, ʻi heʻene fakavavevave ke liliu e kaveingá.

“Houa ‘e hongofulu ‘i he ‘uluaki ‘ahó,” ko e lau ia ‘a e Mock Turtle: “hiva ‘i he ‘aho hono hokó, pea pehē atu pē.”

“Ko ha palani fieʻilo moʻoni ia!” ko e kaila ia ʻa ʻAlisí.

“Ko e ‘uhinga ia ‘oku ui ai kinautolu ko e ngaahi lēsoní,” ko e fakatokanga ia ‘a e Kalifoní: “he ‘oku nau fakasi‘isi‘i mei he ‘aho ki he ‘aho.”

Ko ha fakakaukau foʻou ʻaupito ʻeni kia ʻAlisi, pea naʻá ne kiʻi fakakaukauʻi ia kimuʻa peá ne toki fai ʻene lea hokó. “Pea kuo pau pē ko e ʻaho hono hongofulu mā tahá ko ha ʻaho mālōlō?”

“Ko e moʻoni naʻe pehē,” ko e lea ia ʻa e Mock Turtle.

“Pea naʻe anga fēfē hoʻo lava ʻi he ʻaho hongofulu mā uá?” Naʻe hoko atu ʻa ʻAlisi ʻi he vēkeveke.

“Kuo feʻunga ia fekauʻaki mo e ngaahi lēsoní,” ko e fakalavea ia ʻa e Kalifoní ʻi ha leʻo fakapapauʻi ʻaupito: “talaange kiate ia ha meʻa fekauʻaki mo e ngaahi vaʻingá he taimí ni.”
