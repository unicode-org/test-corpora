BINDU VII.
Pesta Teh na gila

Adong sada meja na ipajongjong i toruh ni sada hayu i lobei ni rumah, anjaha si March Hare pakon si Hatter minum teh ijai: sada Dormouse hundul i holangkolang ni sidea, tarpodom, anjaha na dua nari mamakei ai gabe bantal, iondoskon siku ni sidea hujai, anjaha marsahap i atas uluni. “Seng nyaman tumang bani Dormouse,” nini uhur ni si Alice; “pitah, halani modom do ai, hira lang pala haru uhurni.”

Bolag do meja ai, tapi martumpu do sidea na tolu i sada suhi-suhi: “Seng dong ianan! Seng dong ianan!” marsurak ma sidea sanggah ididah sidea si Alice roh. “ Bahat do ianan ijai!” nini si Alice ibagas ringis, anjaha hundul ma ia bani kursi na banggal i ujung ni meja.

“Inum nasiam ma anggur,” nini March Hare ibagas nada na mendorong.

Ipareksa si Alice ma ganup meja ai, tapi lang dong na legan sobali teh. “Seng dong huidah anggur,” nini.

“Seng dong,” nini Kelinci Maret.

“Anggo sonai lang pala sopan ham mambere ai,” nini si Alice ibagas ringis.

“Seng pala sipil ham hundul lang iontang,” nini Kelinci Maret.

“Lang hubotoh ai mejamu ,” nini si Alice; “ai domma ibahen lobih humbani tolu.”

“Sihol iponggol do jambulanmu,” nini Tukang Topi ai. Domma dokah ia mangidah si Alice ibagas sihol mambotoh, anjaha on ma pidatoni na parlobei.

“Maningon marlajar do ham ase ulang mambahen komentar pribadi,” nini si Alice ibagas na parah; “kasar tumang do ai.”

Ibuka Sitopi ai ma matani bolag tumang dob ibogei ai; tapi na ihatahonni pitah, “Mase ma songon meja panuratan ni gagak?”

“Roh ma, marmalas ni uhur ma hita sonari!” nini uhur ni si Alice. “Malas uhurhu halani domma mulai sidea manungkun teka-teki.—Porsaya do ahu boi do ahu manebak ai,” nini use marsora na gogoh.

“Maksudmu, nini uhurmu boi do ibotoh ham balosni ai?” nini Kelinci Maret.

“Tongon do ai,” nini si Alice.

“Anggo sonai, maningon hatahononmu do aha maksudmu,” nini March Hare ma use.

“Hubotoh do,” nini si Alice podas mambalosi; “paling-paling—paling-paling maksudku aha na huhatahon—ai ma na sarupa, ibotoh ham do.”

“Lang sarupa otik!” nini Tukang Topi ai. “Boi do homa ihatahon ham ‘Huidah do na hupangan’ dos do ai pakon ‘Hupangan do na huidah’!”

“Boi do homa ihatahon ham,” nini March Hare use, “paboa ‘harosuhkon do na hudapot’ dos do ai pakon ‘dapot ahu do na huharosuhkon’!”

“Boi do homa ihatahon ham,” nini Dormouse, na songon na marsahap sanggah modom, “paboa ‘marhosah do ahu sanggah modom’ dos do ai pakon ‘modom do ahu sanggah marhosah’!”

“Sonai do homa pakon ham,” nini Tukang Topi ai, anjaha ijon ma parsahapan ai maronti, anjaha sip ma rombongan ai sadokah saminit, sanggah si Alice mamingkirhon haganup na boi idingatni pasal manuk-manuk nanggordaha pakon meja panulisan, na lang pala buei.

Si Tukang Topi ai ma na parlobei manlanglangi na sip ai. “Ari aha do on bani bulan on?” nini, marhusor hubani si Alice: domma ibuat ia jam tanganni hun kantongni, anjaha ipareksa ia ma ai ibagas na lang tenang, ihurak-hurak ma ai sipata, anjaha ijolom ma ai hu pinggolni.

Ipingkirhon si Alice ma otik, dob ai nini ma “Na paompatkon.”

“Dua ari salah!” marhusip si Tukang Topi ai. “Domma huhatahon bamu anggo mentega lang sosok bani horja ai!” nini use mangkawah ibagas ringis hubani March Hare.

“Ai ma mentega na sidearan ,” nini March Hare mambalosi ibagas na lamlam.

“Eak, tapi dong do homa remah-remah na masuk,” nini Tukang Topi ai: “lang patut ipamasuk ham ai marhitei pisou roti.”

Ibuat March Hare ma jam tangan ai anjaha ipareksa ma ai ibagas na suram: dob ai isornobkon ma ai hubagas cangkir tehni, anjaha ipareksa ma use: tapi lang dong be na boi ipingkirhon ia lobih dear marimbangkon komentarni na parlobei, “Ai ma mentega na sidearan , ibotoh ham do.”

Si Alice domma mangkawah hun abarani ibagas na sihol mambotoh. “Jam tangan na lucu tumang!” nini. “Ipatugah do ari ni bulan, anjaha lang ipatugah pukul piga sonari!”

“Mase ma maningon sonai?” nini Tukang Topi ai marhusip. “Ai ipatugah jam tanganmu do tahun piga ai?”

“Tontu lang,” nini si Alice mambalosi: “tapi halani totap do ai bani tahun na sarupa sadokah dokahni rap.”

“Na songon na masa bangku ,” nini Tukang Topi ai.

Si Alice merasa sangat bingung, komentar ni The Hatter songon na lang dong artini, hape tontu bahasa Inggris do ai. “lang pala huarusi ham,” nini, sesopan na boi.

“Dormouse domma modom use,” nini Sitopi ai, dob ai iuseihon ma otik teh na milas hu igungni.

Dormouse mangunsalhon uluni lang sabar, anjaha mangkatahon, lang pala mambuka matani, “Tontu, tontu; songon na laho huhatahon sandiri.”

“Domma itebak ham teka-teki ai?” nini Tukang Topi ai, marhusor use hubani si Alice.

“Lang, huondoskon ma ai,” nini si Alice mambalosi: “aha do balosni?”

“Seng dong hubotoh age otik pe,” nini Tukang Topi ai.

“Ahu pe lang,” nini Kelinci Maret.

Si Alice marhosah loja. “Huagan ra do ham mambahen na lobih dear bani panorang ai,” nini, “marimbang mansoya-soyahon panorang ai manungkun teka-teki na lang dong balosni.”

“Anggo ibotoh ham do Panorang songon ahu,” nini Sitopi, “lang ra ham marsahap pasal na mansoya-soyahon ai . Ia do ai.”

Nini si Alice ma, “Seng hubotoh aha maksudmu.

“Tontu lang!” nini Tukang Topi ai, manurdukkon uluni songon na menghina. “Barani do ahu mangkatahon lang ongga ham marsahap pakon Time!”

“Ra do lang,” nini si Alice ibagas na jaga: “tapi hubotoh do maningon hutaluhon do panorang sanggah marlajar musik.”

“Ah! ai do na mambahen ai,” nini Tukang Topi ai. “Seng tahan ia hona pukul. Sonari, anggo totap do ham marhasoman pakonsi, ra do ia mambahen aha pe na iharosuhkon ham marhiteihon jam ai. Umpamani, misalni pukul sambilou sogod, panorang mamungkah parlajaran: pitah maningon marhusip do ham bani Panorang, anjaha marputar ma jam ai ibagas na marlinang-linang! Satongah panorang mangan bodari!”

(“Anggo ahu, sihol do uhurhu,” nini Kelinci Maret ai ibagas uhurni ibagas na marhusip.)

“Dear do ai, tontu,” nini si Alice marpingkir: “tapi dob ai—lang pala loheian ahu halani ai, ibotoh ham do.”

“Ra do lang bani mungkahni,” nini Tukang Topi ai: “tapi boi do ijaga ham das pukul satongah sada sadokah harosuhmu.”

“Ai sonai do caramu mangatur?” nini si Alice.

Ihurak si Tukang topi ai ma uluni ibagas pusok ni uhur. “Lang ahu!” nini mambalosi. “Marsalisih do hanami bani bulan Maret na salpu—paima gila ia , ibotoh ham do—” (manunjukkon sendok tehni hubani Kelinci Maret,) “—ai ma bani konser na banggal na ibere Ratu Hati, anjaha maningon mandoding do ahu

'Marlinang, marlinang, kelelawar na etek!
Sungkun-sungkun do ahu aha do na masa bamu!'

Ibotoh ham do doding ai, ra?”

Nini si Alice ma, “Ongga do hubogei na songon ai.

“Torus do ai, ibotoh ham do,” nini Tukang Topi ai use, “sonon:—

'Hu atas dunia on ham habang,
Songon nampan teh i langit.
Marlinang-linang, marlinang-linang—'”

Ijon Dormouse ai manghutur dirini, anjaha mulai mandoding sanggah modom “ Marlinang-linang, marlinang-linang, marlinang-linang, marlinang-linang —” anjaha dokah tumang do ia maningon icubit sidea ase maronti.

“Dear, lape salosei ahu mambasa ayat na parlobei,” nini Tukang Topi ai, “sanggah Ratu ai manlumpat janah marsurak, 'Ia mamunuh panorang! Ipadaoh ma uluni!'"

“Sonaha do parahni!” nini si Alice.

“Anjaha humbani panorang ai,” nini Tukang Topi ai ibagas sora na marpusok ni uhur, “lang ra ia mangkorjahon aha pe na hupangindo! Sai pukul onom do sonari.”

Sada ide na jenges roh hu ulu ni si Alice. “Ai halani ai do ase buei barang-barang teh na ibahen ijon?” nini.

“Eak, sonai ma,” nini Tukang Topi ai marhasoman hosah: “tongtong do panorang minum teh, anjaha lang dong panorang nami laho mamurihi barang-barang ai i tongah-tongah ni panorang ai.”

“Anggo sonai, torus do ham marputar-putar, nini uhurhu?” nini si Alice.

“Sonai do tongon,” nini Tukang Topi ai: “songon na dob somal barang-barang ai.”

“Tapi aha do na masa anggo roh ham use bani mungkahni?” Si Alice barani manungkun.

“Umpamani hita mangubah topik,” nini March Hare manlanglangi, manguap. “Domma loja ahu pasal on. Hupilih ma anak boru ai patugahkon sada sarita hubanta.”

“Mabiar do ahu lang hubotoh sada pe,” nini si Alice, agak tarsonggot bani usulan ai.

“Anggo sonai, Dormouse ma!” tangis ma sidea haduasi. “Puho ma ham, Dormouse!” Jadi icubit sidea ma ai humbani na dua suhi-suhi sahali.

Lambat-lambat ibuka Dormouse ai ma matani. “Lang tarpodom ahu,” nini ibagas sora na serak anjaha galek: “Hubogei do ganup hata na ihatahon nasiam.”

“Patugah ham ma sada sarita!” nini Kelinci Maret.

“Eak, bahen nasiam ma!” nini si Alice.

“Anjaha podas ma ham mambahen ai,” nini Sitopi ai use, “atap modom ma ham use paima salosei ai.”

“Ongga do dong tolu botou na etek,” nini Dormouse ai ibagas na marpodas-podas; “anjaha goran ni sidea Elsie, Lacie, pakon Tillie, anjaha marianan do sidea i toruh ni parigi—”

“Aha do na iharosuhkon sidea?” nini si Alice, na selalu sangat tertarik bani soal mangan pakon minum.

“Manggoluh do sidea bani treacle,” nini Dormouse, dob marpingkir saminit atap dua menit.

“Seng tarbahen sidea sonai, ibotoh ham do,” nini si Alice ibagas na lamlam; “boritan do namin sidea.”

“Sonai do sidea,” nini Dormouse; “ boritan tumang .”

Iusahahon si Alice do marpingkir sonaha do cara hidup na luar biasa ai, tapi mambahen ia tarsonggot tumang, gabe nini ma use: “Tapi mase sidea marianan i toruh ni sumur?”

“Ibuat ham ma use teh,” nini March Hare hubani si Alice, ibagas na sungguh-sungguh.

“Seng dong ope na dong bangku,” nini si Alice mambalosi ibagas sora na tarsinggung, “jadi lang boi be ahu manjalo lobih hunjai.”

“Maksudmu lang boi ham mambuat na hurang ,” nini Tukang Topi ai: “urah tumang do mambuat lobih humbani na lang dong.”

“Seng dong na manungkun pandapotmu ,” nini si Alice.

“Ise do na mambahen komentar pribadi sonari?” nini Tukang Topi ai ibagas hamonangan.

Lang ibotoh si Alice aha na laho hatahononni pasal on: jadi iurupi ma dirini minum teh pakon ruti-pakon-mentega, dob ai marhusor ma ia hubani Dormouse, anjaha iulaki ma sungkun-sungkunni. “Mase ma sidea marianan i toruh ni sumur?”

Dormouse use mambere panorang saminit atap dua menit laho mamingkirhon ai, dob ai nini ma, “Sumur treacle do ai.”

“Seng dong na sonai ai!” Si Alice mulai manggila tumang, tapi si Tukang Topi pakon si March Hare mangkatahon “Sh! sh!” anjaha Dormouse ai mangkatahon, “Anggo lang boi ham sipil, dearan ma saloseihon ham sandiri sarita ai.”

“Lang, toruskon ma!” Nini si Alice ibagas toruh ni uhur; “Lang be huponggolhon. Barani do ahu mangkatahon ra do adong sada .”

“Sada, tongon!” nini Dormouse ibagas ringis. Tapi, setuju do ia laho torus. “Jadi botou na tolu on—marlajar manggambar do sidea, ibotoh ham—”

“Aha do na igambar sidea?” nini si Alice, lupa bani bagah-bagahni.

“Treacle,” nini Dormouse, lang pala mamingkirhon panorang on.

“Sihol do ahu bani sada cangkir na borsih,” nini Tukang Topi ai: “pindah hita ma sada ianan.”

Ia mardalan sanggah ia marsahap, anjaha Dormouse mangirikkonsi: March Hare pindah hu ianan ni Dormouse, anjaha Alice agak lang ra manggantihkon March Hare. Si Tukang Topi ai do hansa na dapotan untung humbani parubahan ai: anjaha si Alice domma lambin parah marimbangkon na hinan, halani March Hare baru ope manrobohkon gusi susu hu pingganni.

Si Alice lang ra be mambahen borit uhur ni Dormouse, jadi ipungkah ia ma ibagas na hati-hati: “Tapi lang huarusi. Hunja do sidea mambuat treacle ai?”

“Boi do ham mambuat bah hun sumur bah,” nini Sitopi ai; “jadi nini uhurhu boi do ham manarik treacle humbani sumur treacle—eh, oto?”

“Tapi sidea i sumur do,” nini si Alice hubani Dormouse, lang ra ia mampardiateihon hata na parpudi on.

“Tontu do sidea,” nini Dormouse; “—dear masuk.”

Balos on mambahen bingung si Alice na miskin ai, gabe ipaturut ma Dormouse ai mardalan piga-piga panorang lang pala manlanglangi.

“Marlajar manggambar do sidea,” nini Dormouse ai, manguap pakon mangusap matani, halani domma modom tumang ia; “anjaha igambar sidea do bagei-bagei—haganup na imulaini humbani M—”

“Mase marhiteihon M?” nini si Alice.

"Mase lang?" nini Kelinci Maret.

Sip do si Alice.

Dormouse domma manutup matani bani panorang ai, anjaha laho tarpodom; tapi, dob i cubit Tukang Topi ai, puho ma use ia marhasoman sora na etek, dob ai ilanjutkon ma: “—na imulaini humbani M, songon perangkap tikus, pakon bulan, pakon pardingatan, pakon buei ni—ibotoh ham do na ihatahon ham do “bahat ni buei ni”—ongga do ididah ham na songon gambar ni buei ni?”

“Tongon, sonari sungkun ham ma ahu,” nini si Alice, bingung tumang, “lang hupingkir—”

“Anggo sonai ulang ma ham marsahap,” nini Sitopi ai.

Hasombuhon na kasar on lobih humbani na boi itaron si Alice: jongjong ma ia ibagas sogam ni uhur, dob ai laho ma ia; mintor tarpodom ma Dormouse ai, anjaha lang dong na legan na mampardiateihon ia laho, age pe mangkawah ia hu pudi sahali atap dua hali, satongah mangarapkon ase idilo sidea ia: parpudi sahali ididah ia sidea, sidea mancuba pamasukkon Dormouse ai hubagas teko.

“Sonaha pe lang ongga be ahu hujai !” nini si Alice sanggah ia mamilih dalanni manlaosi hayu ai. “On ma pesta teh na paling bodoh na ongga hudohori sadokah goluhku!”

Sanggah ihatahon ia sonai, ididah ia ma sada humbani hayu ai dong labah na masuk hujai. “Penasaran tumang do ai!” nini uhurni. “Tapi haganupan ai mambahen penasaran sadari on. Huagan boi do ahu masuk mintor.” Dob ai masuk ma ia.

Sahali nari jumpahsi ma dirini i aula na ganjang, anjaha dohor bani meja kaca na etek ai. “Sonari, lambin dear ma ahu bani panorang on,” nini ibagas uhurni, anjaha ipungkah ma marhitei na mambuat kunci omas na etek ai, anjaha mambuka labah na masuk hu taman ai. Dob ai laho ma ia marhorja mangankon jamur ai (ibahen ia do sada jamur ai ibagas kantongni) das hubani gijangni hira-hira saasta: dob ai mardalan ma ia hu lorong na etek ai: dob ai —ujungni jumpahsi ma dirini i taman na jenges ai, i tongah-tongah ni bunga-bunga na marsinalsal pakon pansur na borgoh.
