YITSƆƆ II.
Yaafo Nu lɛ

“Miitao nibii amli babaoo ni miitao nibii amli babaoo!” Alice bolɔ (enaa kpɛ ehe waa, ni yɛ be kukuoo mli lɛ, ehiɛ kpa bɔ ni awieɔ Blɔfo kpakpa lɛ nɔ kwraa); "amrɔ nɛɛ migbeleɔ mihe tamɔ tɛleskopu ni da fe fɛɛ ni yɔɔ pɛŋ! Shɛɛ, naji!" (ejaakɛ beni ekwɛ enaji lɛ shi lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni eshwɛ fioo ni amɛbaafee mɛi ni enaaa amɛ, amɛmiiya shɔŋŋ waa). “Oh, minaji bibii ohiafoi lɛ, misusuɔ mɔ ni baawo nyɛ tokotai kɛ nyɛstɔki lɛ he bianɛ, suɔlɔi? Miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ minyɛŋ ! Mafee mɔ ko ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ tsɔ ni mikɛ mihe baahao yɛ nyɛhe: esa akɛ okwɛ gbɛ ni hi fe fɛɛ ni obaanyɛ otsɔ nɔ;—shi esa akɛ mafee amɛ ejurɔ,” Alice susu he akɛ, “loo I wont! kwɛmɔ: Mahã amɛ bɔɔtu hee daa Blonya.”

Ni etee nɔ eto gbɛjianɔ eha ehe bɔ ni ebaatsu he nii eha. Esusu akɛ: “Esa akɛ amɛtsɔ tsɔne ni tereɔ nii lɛ nɔ amɛtsɔ; “ni kwɛ bɔ ni ebaafee tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ, ni akɛ nikeenii majeɔ mɔ diɛŋtsɛ enaji! Ni kwɛ bɔ ni gbɛtsɔɔmɔi lɛ baafee nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ha!

Alice ninejurɔ naji, Esq.,
Tsui tsɔne,
ni bɛŋkɛ Fender lɛ, ( kɛ Alice suɔmɔ ).

Oh suɔmɔ, mɛɛ kwashiai asane miwieɔ nɛkɛ!”

No mli lɛ, eyitso tswa asa lɛ yiteŋ: yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, agbɛnɛ eshɛ fe ninetalɔi nɛɛhu, ni amrɔ nɔŋŋ ni eŋɔ shika tsuru saji bibii lɛ ni ekɛ oyaiyeli tee abɔɔ lɛ shinaa lɛ naa.

Alice ohiafo! Eji nɔ ni ebaanyɛ efee, ni ekã shi yɛ efã kome nɔ, ni ekɛ hiŋmɛi kome akwɛ abɔɔ lɛ mli; shi koni etsɔ mli lɛ ji nɔ ni hiɛnɔkamɔ bɛ mli fe tsutsu lɛ: eta shi ni ebɔi yaafo ekoŋŋ.

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “Esa akɛ ohiɛ agbo ohe, gbekɛ yoo wulu ko tamɔ bo,” (ebaanyɛ ekɛɛ enɛ jogbaŋŋ), “koni oya nɔ oyafo yɛ nɛkɛ gbɛ nɔ! Kpa nɛkɛ be nɛɛ mli, mikɛɛ bo!” Shi etee nɔ nakai nɔŋŋ, ni ekɛ yaafo galɔŋi babaoo shwie shi, kɛyashi faa agbo ko bɔle ehe fɛɛ, ni elɛɛmɔ aaafee inchi ejwɛ ni eshɛɔ asa lɛ shishigbɛ fã.

Be ko sɛɛ lɛ enu naji ni miitswa fioo yɛ shɔŋŋ, ni ekɛ oyaiyeli gĩ ehiŋmɛii koni ena nɔ ni miiba. Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ ji mɔ ni miiku esɛɛ eba, ni esaa ehe fɛfɛo, ni ehiɛ gbekɛ glɔvs yɛŋ yɛ enine kome mli, ni ehiɛ fan agbo ko yɛ ekroko lɛ mli: eba ni ekɛ oyaiyeli miiho, ni ekɛ ehe miiwie beni eba lɛ akɛ, “Oh! Duchess lɛ, Duchess lɛ! Oh! ani ebaafee mɔ ni yɔɔ gbeyei kɛji mikwɛ lɛ!” Alice nu he akɛ enijiaŋ eje wui waa aahu akɛ efee klalo akɛ ebaabi mɔ fɛɛ mɔ yelikɛbuamɔ; no hewɔ lɛ, beni Kpɔŋɔ lɛ bɛŋkɛ lɛ lɛ, ebɔi, kɛ gbee ni baa shi ni yɔɔ gbeyei akɛ, “Kɛ́ ofainɛ, onukpa—” Kpɔŋɔ lɛ bɔi yiwalɛ, ekɛ gbekɛ glɔvs yɛŋ lɛ kɛ fan lɛ shwie shi, ni ekɛ oyaiyeli tee duŋ lɛ mli bɔ ni ebaanyɛ eya.

Alice wo fan kɛ glɔvs lɛ, ni, akɛni asa lɛ mli edɔ waa hewɔ lɛ, etee nɔ ekɛ fan lɛ tswa ehe be fɛɛ be ni ewieɔ lɛ: "Suɔmɔ, suɔmɔ! Kwɛ bɔ ni nɔ fɛɛ nɔ yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ha ŋmɛnɛ! Ni nyɛsɛɛ nɛɛ nibii tee nɔ tamɔ bɔ ni afeɔ daa lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ. Misusuɔ akɛ ani atsake mi gbɛkɛ lɛ? Ha masusu he akɛ: ani mi hu mitee shi nakai nɔŋŋ be ni minuɔ he? esoro mi fioo Shi kɛji akɛ mijeee mɔ kome lɛ, sanebimɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ ji, Namɔ ji mi yɛ je lɛŋ, no ji shwɛmɔ kpeteŋkpele lɛ!” Ni ebɔi gbekɛbii fɛɛ ni ele amɛ ni amɛkɛ lɛ eye afii lɛ ahe susumɔ, koni ena kɛji kulɛ abaanyɛ atsake lɛ aha amɛteŋ mɔ ko.

Ekɛɛ akɛ, "Miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ jeee Ada ji mi, ejaakɛ eyitsɔi lɛ yaa ŋwɛi ŋwɛi, ni minɔ lɛ hu eyaaa ŋwɛi ŋwɛi kwraa; ni miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ minyɛŋ mafee Mabel, ejaakɛ mile nibii srɔtoi fɛɛ, ni lɛ, oh! ele nɔ ko fioo ko pɛ! Kɛfata he lɛ, eji bɔ ni mi, kɛ mi —oh, ma! kɛji mile nibii fɛɛ ni mile tsutsu lɛ.Ha mana: shii ejwɛ kɛ enumɔ ji nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ, ni shii ejwɛ kɛ kpawo ji—oh suɔmɔ, mishɛɛɛ nyɔŋmai enyɔ he kɔkɔɔkɔ yɛ nakai oyaiyeli lɛ mli Shi kɛlɛ, Multiplication Table lɛ etsɔɔɔ akɛ Paris maŋtiase lɛ ji Ro, kɛ wɔkaa wɔkwɛ tɔmɔ, miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ esa akɛ atsake mi aha Mabel! Mabɔ mɔdɛŋ ni makɛɛ ' Te efeɔ tɛŋŋ ni bibioo lɛ —’” ni ekɛ eniji shwieɔ etsitsi nɔ tamɔ nɔ ni eewie nikasemɔi, ni ebɔi wiemɔ lɛ ekoŋŋ, shi egbee lɛ gbɛɛmɔ ji nɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni eyɛ naakpɛɛ, ni wiemɔi lɛ baaa tamɔ bɔ ni amɛkɛtsuɔ nii lɛ:

“Te ŋshɔŋloo bibioo lɛ feɔ tɛŋŋ
Ha etsitsi ni kpɛɔ lɛ aya hiɛ, .
Ni ofɔse Nilo nui lɛ ashwie shi
Yɛ shika tsuru sɛkɛl fɛɛ sɛkɛl nɔ!

“Kwɛ bɔ ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛ miishɛɛ miiŋmɔ, .
Kwɛ bɔ ni egbɛ enaji lɛ eshwa pɛpɛɛpɛ, .
Ni nyɛherea loo bibii kɛbaa mli
Kɛ tsitsii ni ŋmɔɔ blɛoo!”

“Miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ nakai wiemɔi lɛ jeee wiemɔi ni ja,” Alice ohiafo lɛ kɛɛ, ni ehiŋmɛii amli eyi kɛ yaafo ekoŋŋ beni eyaa nɔ lɛ, “Esa akɛ mafee Mabel yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ, ni esa akɛ mayahi nakai shĩa bibioo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ mli, ni mibɛ shwɛmɔ nibii ni mikɛshwɛɔ, ni oh! nyɛhi shi yɛ biɛ! Makwɛ ŋwɛi pɛ ni makɛɛ akɛ ‘Namɔ ji mi nakai beaŋ? Kɛɛmɔ mi nakai klɛŋklɛŋ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ, kɛji misumɔɔ akɛ nakai gbɔmɔ lɛ ji lɛ, maba ŋwɛi: kɛ jeee nakai lɛ, mahi shi yɛ biɛ kɛyashi be mli ni matsɔ mɔ kroko’—shi, oh suɔmɔ!” Alice bolɔ, ni ekɛ yaafo shwie shi trukaa akɛ, “Miisumɔ waa akɛ amɛkɛ amɛyitsei aba shi! Etɔ mi waa akɛ mikome too miyɔɔ biɛ!”

Beni ewieɔ enɛ lɛ ekwɛ eniji lɛ shi, ni enaa kpɛ ehe akɛ ewo Kpɔŋɔ gbekɛ glɔvs bibii yɛŋ lɛ eko beni ewieɔ lɛ. “Te mafee tɛŋŋ mafee nakai?” esusu he. “Esa akɛ mifee bibioo ekoŋŋ.” Ete shi ni etee okpɔlɔ lɛ he koni eyasusu ehe, ni ena akɛ, bɔ ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaanyɛ esusu lɛ, amrɔ nɛɛ eshɛ aaafee ninetalɔi enyɔ, ni eyaa nɔ egbɔjɔɔ oya: etsɛɛɛ ni ena akɛ nɔ ni kɛ enɛ ba ji fan ni ehiɛ lɛ, ni ekɛshwie shi oya, yɛ be ni sa mli pɛpɛɛpɛ koni ekagbɔjɔ alto.

“No ji gbɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni atsɔɔ nɔ ajoɔ foi!” Alice kɛɛ, ni eshe gbeyei waa yɛ tsakemɔ ni ba trukaa lɛ hewɔ, shi eyɛ miishɛɛ waa akɛ ena akɛ eyɛ shihilɛ mli lolo; “ni agbɛnɛ kɛha abɔɔ lɛ!” ni ekɛ oyaiyeli fɛɛ ku esɛɛ kɛtee shinaa bibioo lɛ naa: shi, shwane! agbe shinaa bibioo lɛ naa ekoŋŋ, ni shika tsuru saji bibii lɛ kã glase okpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ tamɔ tsutsu lɛ, “ni nibii ehiii fe tsutsu lɛ,” gbekɛ ohiafo lɛ susu he, “ejaakɛ mifeeko bibioo tamɔ nɛkɛ pɛŋ, kɔkɔɔkɔ! Ni mijajeɔ akɛ ehiii kwraa, akɛ nakai eji!”

Beni ewieɔ wiemɔi nɛɛ lɛ, enane shwie shi, ni yɛ be kukuoo mli lɛ, efo! eyɛ nu ni mli edɔ mli kɛyashi etsitsi nɔ. Esusumɔ klɛŋklɛŋ ji akɛ, yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, egbee ŋshɔ lɛ mli, “ni yɛ nakai shihilɛ lɛ mli lɛ, manyɛ matsɔ oketeke nɔ maku misɛɛ,” ekɛɛ ehe. (Alice etee ŋshɔ lɛ naa shikome yɛ eshihilɛ mli, ni ebashɛ mumɔŋ sane naa akɛ, he fɛɛ he ni obaaya yɛ Ŋleshi ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ, onaa hejuu tsɔnei babaoo yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli, gbekɛbii komɛi kɛ tsei ni akɛfoɔ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ miifo ŋã lɛ mli, no sɛɛ lɛ, wɔɔhei ni ato amɛhe gbɛjianɔ, kɛ oketeke shidaamɔhe ko ni yɔɔ amɛsɛɛ lɛ.) Shi kɛlɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni she had te pool of she was ma elɛɛmɔ ji ninetalɔi nɛɛhu.

“Eji kulɛ mifooo nakai!” Alice kɛɛ, beni efoɔ nu lɛ mli, ni ebɔɔ mɔdɛŋ ni ena gbɛ ni etsɔɔ nɔ ejeɔ mli lɛ. "Misusuɔ akɛ abaato mihe gbɛjianɔ yɛ he bianɛ, kɛtsɔ mi diɛŋtsɛ mi yaafo mli ni mikɛbaawo mihe lɛ nɔ! No baafee nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, kɛha nɔmimaa! Shi kɛlɛ, nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ŋmɛnɛ."

No mli lɛ enu nɔ ko ni miiho yɛ faa lɛ mli yɛ he ko ni kɛ jɛmɛ jɛkɛmɔ fioo, ni efo kɛbɛŋkɛ koni ena nɔ ni ji: shishijee lɛ esusu akɛ ekolɛ walrus loo hippopotamus ji lɛ, shi no sɛɛ lɛ ekai bɔ ni eji bibioo amrɔ nɛɛ, ni etsɛɛɛ ni eyɔse akɛ ŋmei ko pɛ ni ebote mli tamɔ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ.

Alice susu akɛ, "Ani sɛɛnamɔ ko baaba, amrɔ nɛɛ, akɛ mikɛ ŋmɔtɔ nɛɛ awie? Nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ejɛ gbɛ nɔ yɛ biɛ shishigbɛ, ni esa akɛ masusu akɛ ekolɛ ebaanyɛ ewie: bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, naagba ko bɛ he akɛ aka mihe akwɛ." No hewɔ lɛ, eje shishi akɛ: “O Kpɔŋɔ, ani ole gbɛ ni oootsɔ nɔ ojɛ nubu nɛɛ mli? Etɔ mi waa akɛ mafo yɛ biɛ, O Kpɔŋɔ!” (Alice susu akɛ esa akɛ enɛ afee gbɛ ni ja ni atsɔɔ nɔ akɛ ŋmei wieɔ: efeko nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ dã, shi ekai akɛ ena yɛ enyɛmi nuu lɛ Latin Wiemɔ He Wolo lɛ mli akɛ, “Kpɔŋɔ—ni ŋmei—kɛha ŋmei—ŋmei—O ŋmei!”) Kpɔŋɔ lɛ kwɛ lɛ kɛ sanebimɔ moŋ, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekɛɛ lɛ akɛ no, shi efee nɔ ko kɛye enɔ.

Alice susu akɛ: “Ekolɛ enuuu Blɔfo shishi; “Mikɛɔ akɛ French ŋmɔtɔ ji lɛ, okɛ William Kunimyelɔ lɛ aba.” (Ejaakɛ, yɛ yinɔsane he nilee fɛɛ ni eyɔɔ lɛ hewɔ lɛ, Alice bɛ jwɛŋmɔ ko ni mli ka shi faŋŋ kwraa yɛ be kakadaŋŋ ni eho ni nɔ ko eba lɛ he.) No hewɔ lɛ, ebɔi ekoŋŋ akɛ: “Où est ma chatte?” ni ji klɛŋklɛŋ wiemɔ kuku ni yɔɔ e-French nikasemɔ-wolo lɛ mli lɛ. Mouse lɛ tu trukaa kɛjɛ nu lɛ mli, ni etamɔ nɔ ni ekpokpo kɛ gbeyeishemɔ. “Oo, mikpaa bo fai!” Alice bolɔ oya, ni eshe gbeyei akɛ eye kooloo ohiafo lɛ henumɔ awui. “Mihiɛ kpa nɔ kwraa akɛ osumɔɔɔ ŋmɔtɔ.”

“Jeee tamɔ ŋmɔji!” Mouse lɛ bolɔ, kɛ gbee ni mli wa ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ. “Ani obaasumɔ ŋmɔji kɛji bo ji bo?”

"Ojogbaŋŋ, ekolɛ daabi," Alice kɛɛ kɛ gbee ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli: "kaamli afu yɛ he. Ni kɛlɛ miisumɔ ni matsɔɔ bo wɔ tsɛji Dinah: Misusuɔ akɛ obaasumɔ tsɛji kɛji obaanyɛ ona lɛ pɛ. Eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ dioo ni asumɔɔ waa," Alice tee nɔ, fã kɛha lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ehe, beni ekɛ ehe miiwo nu lɛ mli lɛ, " la lɛ, ni ekɛ enaji ŋmɔɔ ni ekɛ ehiɛ miifɔ ehe—ni eji nɔ ko ni yɔɔ blɛoo ni hi jogbaŋŋ ni esa akɛ akwɛ lɛ—ni eji nɔ ko ni he hiaa waa kɛha ŋmeii amɔmɔ—oh, mikpaa bo fai!” Alice bolɔ ekoŋŋ, ejaakɛ nɛkɛ be nɛɛ mli lɛ, Mouse lɛ miihoso ehe fɛɛ, ni enu he akɛ esa akɛ emli afu lɛ diɛŋtsɛ. “Wɔwieŋ ehe dɔŋŋ kɛji oosumɔ ni owieee.”

“Wɔ diɛŋtsɛ!” Mouse ni miihoso kɛyashi etsitsi naagbee lɛ bolɔ. "Tamɔ nɔ ni mawie saneyitso ni tamɔ nɛkɛ he! Wɔweku lɛ nyɛɔ ŋmɔji be fɛɛ be: nibii ni yɔɔ gbeyei, ni baa shi, ni yɔɔ taŋ! Kaaha minu gbɛi lɛ ekoŋŋ!"

“Mifeŋ nakai lɛɛlɛŋ!” Alice kɛɛ, ni ekɛ oyaiyeli babaoo tsu nii koni etsake sanegbaa lɛ he sane lɛ. “Ani osumɔɔ—ani osumɔɔ—gbɔi?” Kpɔŋɔ lɛ haaa hetoo, no hewɔ lɛ Alice tee nɔ kɛ miishɛɛ akɛ: “Gbɔmɔ bibioo ko ni hi jogbaŋŋ yɛ wɔshia lɛ masɛi ni esa akɛ matsɔɔ bo! Gbɔmɔ bibioo ko ni hiŋmɛii kpɛɔ, ole, ni eyɛ oh, yitsɔi kakadaŋŋ agbo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ! Ni ebaaŋɔ nibii kɛji oshɛrɛ amɛ ashwie, ni ebaata shi ni ekpa mi fai, ni mikɛ mihe awo mli. amɛteŋ ekome—ni eji okwaafonyo ko nɔ, ole, ni ekɛɛ ehe yɛ sɛɛnamɔ waa, ejara ji pound oha Ekɛɛ egbeɔ ŋmeii lɛ fɛɛ ni—oh suɔmɔ!” Alice kɛ awerɛho gbee bolɔ akɛ, “Misheɔ gbeyei akɛ mikɛ mihe ewo mli ekoŋŋ!” Ejaakɛ Mouse lɛ miiho kɛmiijɛ ehe bɔ ni eeenyɛ, ni efee basabasafeemɔ babaoo yɛ nu lɛ mli beni eyaa lɛ.

No hewɔ lɛ, etsɛ blɛoo yɛ no sɛɛ akɛ, "Mouse suɔmɔ! Kuu osɛɛ oba ekoŋŋ, ni wɔwieŋ ŋmɔji loo ŋmɔji ahe hu, kɛji osumɔɔɔ amɛ!" Beni Kpɔŋɔ lɛ nu enɛ lɛ, etsɔ ehe ni efo blɛoo kɛku esɛɛ kɛba eŋɔɔ: ehiɛ efee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ kwraa (kɛ suɔmɔ, Alice susu he), ni ekɛ gbee ni hosoɔ ni baa shi kɛɛ, “Ha wɔyashɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni no sɛɛ lɛ magba bo mi yinɔsane, ni obaanu nɔ hewɔ ni eji akɛ misumɔɔɔ tsɛji kɛ gbeei lɛ shishi.”

Be eho ni esa akɛ aya, ejaakɛ okpoi kɛ kooloi ni egbee mli lɛ miiyi faa lɛ mli obɔ kwraa: Duck kɛ Dodo, Lory kɛ Eaglet, kɛ bɔɔ nii krokomɛi babaoo ni miitao ni amɛle nɔ ni yaa nɔ lɛ yɛ jɛmɛ. Alice nyiɛ hiɛ, ni kuu muu lɛ fɛɛ tsɔ nu lɛ mli kɛtee ŋshɔ lɛ naa.
