YITSƆƆ XI.
Namɔ Ju Tart lɛ?

Maŋtsɛ kɛ Tsuii A-Maŋnyɛ lɛ ta amɛmaŋtsɛ sɛi lɛ nɔ beni amɛbashɛ jɛmɛ lɛ, ni asafo babaoo ebua amɛhe naa—okpoi bibii kɛ kooloi srɔtoi fɛɛ, kɛ agbɛnɛ kaadii akuu muu lɛ fɛɛ: Knave lɛ damɔ amɛhiɛ, yɛ ŋwɛi ŋtayaai amli, ni asraafonyo ko yɛ efã fɛɛ efã koni ebu ehe; ni Maŋtsɛ lɛ masɛi ji Rabi Eyɛŋ lɛ, ni hiɛ tɛtɛrɛmantɛrɛ yɛ enine kome mli, ni wolokpo ni akɛ kooloo hewolo fee lɛ yɛ enine kroko lɛ mli. Okpɔlɔ ko yɛ kojomɔhe lɛ teŋgbɛ tɔ̃ɔ, ni tarts tsɛnsi agbo ko kã nɔ: amɛhe yɛ fɛo waa, ni ehaa hɔmɔ yeɔ Alice waa kɛji ekwɛ amɛ—esusu akɛ, “Miisumɔ ni amɛfee kojomɔ lɛ,” esusu he, “ni amɛkɛ niyenii ni akɛyeɔ niyenii lɛ abɔle he!” Shi etamɔ nɔ ni hegbɛ ko bɛ ni baaha enɛ aba mli, no hewɔ lɛ ebɔi nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni kɔɔ ehe lɛ kwɛmɔ, koni ekɛho be lɛ he.

Alice eyako kojomɔ he dã, shi ekane amɛhe saji yɛ woji amli, ni ená miishɛɛ waa akɛ ele nɔ ni miihe ashɛ nɔ fɛɛ nɔ ni yɔɔ jɛmɛ lɛ gbɛi. Ekɛɛ ehe akɛ: “Kojolɔ lɛ ji nakai, yɛ ewig kpeteŋkpele lɛ hewɔ.”

Kojolɔ lɛ, yɛ gbɛ kroko nɔ lɛ, ji Maŋtsɛ lɛ; ni beni ewoɔ e-akpo lɛ yɛ wig lɛ nɔ lɛ, (kwɛ hiɛgbɛ wolo lɛ kɛji oosumɔ ni ona bɔ ni efee lɛ eha lɛ,) ekwɛɛɛ akɛ eyɛ hejɔɔmɔ kwraa, ni eji nɔ ni esaaa kwraa.

Alice susu akɛ: “Ni no ji kojolɔi-adeka lɛ, kɛ nakai bɔɔ nii nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ lɛ,” (eji gbɛnaa nii ka enɔ akɛ ekɛɛ “bɔɔ nii,” ona, ejaakɛ amɛteŋ mɛi komɛi ji kooloi, ni ekomɛi hu ji okpoi,) “Misusuɔ akɛ amɛji kojolɔi lɛ.” Ekɛɛ naagbee wiemɔ nɛɛ shii enyɔ loo etɛ fe ehe, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ nɔ moŋ: ejaakɛ esusu, ni eja hu, akɛ gbekɛbii yei bibii fioo ko pɛ ni eye afii tamɔ lɛ lɛ le shishinumɔ kwraa. Shi kɛlɛ, kulɛ “kojolɔi” lɛ hu baafee nakai nɔŋŋ.

Jury nyɔŋma kɛ enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ miiŋmala nii waa yɛ slate nɔ. “Mɛni amɛfeɔ?” Alice kɛ Gryphon lɛ wie ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ. “Amɛnyɛŋ amɛná nɔ ko ni amɛkɛbaawo shi lolo, dani kojomɔ lɛ aje shishi.”

Gryphon lɛ kɛ gbee bibioo ha hetoo akɛ: “Amɛkɛ amɛgbɛi miiwo shi, yɛ gbeyeishemɔ akɛ amɛhiɛ baakpa amɛnɔ dani kojomɔ lɛ aba naagbee lɛ hewɔ.”

“Kwashiai asane!” Alice kɛ gbee ni naa wa ni mlifu je shishi, shi ekpa oya, ejaakɛ Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ bolɔ akɛ, “Afee dioo yɛ kojomɔhe lɛ!” ni Maŋtsɛ lɛ wo ehiŋmɛigbelemɔi lɛ ni ekɛ haomɔ kwɛ ehe kɛkpe, koni ena mɔ ni wieɔ lɛ.

Alice nyɛɔ enaa, kɛ agbɛnɛ hu, kɛji eekwɛ amɛkuɛ nɔ lɛ, akɛ kojolɔi lɛ fɛɛ mii ŋma “kwashiai asane!” yɛ amɛ slate lɛ nɔ, ni enyɛ ena po akɛ amɛteŋ mɔ kome leee bɔ ni aŋmaa “kwashia,” ni esa akɛ ebi ekutsoŋnyo lɛ ni ekɛɛ lɛ. “Amɛ slates lɛ baafee basabasafeemɔ kpakpa dani kojomɔ lɛ aba naagbee!” Alice susu he.

Kojolɔi lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome hiɛ pensil ni shwieɔ shi. Enɛ ji anɔkwale, Alice nyɛɛɛ adamɔ shi, ni ebɔle kojomɔhe lɛ he ni ebote esɛɛ, ni etsɛɛɛ tsɔ ni ena hegbɛ ni ekɛbaajie. Efee oya aahu akɛ kojolɔ bibioo ohiafo lɛ (Bill, ni ji Lizard lɛ ji lɛ) nyɛɛɛ ayoo nɔ ni eba lɛ kwraa; no hewɔ lɛ, beni etao he fɛɛ he sɛɛ lɛ, eji gbɛnaa nii ka enɔ akɛ ekɛ enine kome aŋma yɛ gbi muu lɛ fɛɛ mli; ni enɛ he ehiaaa tsɔ, ejaakɛ eshiuuu okadi ko yɛ slate lɛ nɔ.

“Herald, kanemɔ fɔbuu lɛ!” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ.

Yɛ enɛ nɔ lɛ, Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ tswa tɛtɛrɛmantɛrɛ lɛ shii etɛ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ ejie wolokpo ni akɛ kooloo hewolo fee lɛ, ni ekane bɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ nɛɛ:—

“Tsuii A-Maŋnyɛ lɛ, efee tarts komɛi, .
Fɛɛ yɛ latsaa be mli gbi nɔ:
Tsuii Ahe Knave lɛ, eju nakai tarts lɛ,
Ni ekɛ amɛ tee kwraa!”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ kojolɔi akuu lɛ akɛ: “Nyɛsusua nyɛkojomɔ lɛ he.”

“Dabi lolo, dabi lolo!” Rabi lɛ kɛ oyaiyeli kpa sane lɛ mli. “Nibii babaoo yɛ ni baaba dani nakai be lɛ aba!”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Tsɛ́ odasefonyo klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ; ni Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ tswa tɛtɛrɛmantɛrɛ lɛ shii etɛ, ni etsɛ akɛ, “Klɛŋklɛŋ odasefonyo!”

Klɛŋklɛŋ odasefonyo ji Hatter lɛ. Ebote mli kɛ tii kɔɔpoo yɛ enine kome mli, ni ehiɛ blodoo-kɛ-bɔta kuku yɛ enine kroko mli. Eje shishi akɛ: “Mikpaa bo fai, o-Maŋtsɛyeli, akɛ mikɛ enɛɛmɛi ba: shi migbeko mi-tii lɛ naa kwraa beni atsu ni ayatsɛ mi lɛ.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Kulɛ esa akɛ ogbe naa.” “Mɛɛ be oje shishi?”

Hatter lɛ kwɛ March Hare ni nyiɛ esɛɛ kɛbote kojomɔhe lɛ, ni ekɛ Dormouse lɛ ehiɛ amɛhe lɛ. Ekɛɛ akɛ: “March 14 lɛ, misusuɔ akɛ no mli lɛ.”

March Hare lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Nyɔŋma kɛ enumɔ.”

Dormouse lɛ kɛfata he akɛ: “Nyɔŋma kɛ ekpaa.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ kojolɔi akuu lɛ akɛ: “Ŋmalamɔ nakai,” ni kojolɔi akuu lɛ kɛ miishɛɛ ŋma gbii etɛ lɛ fɛɛ yɛ amɛ slate lɛ nɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ amɛkɛfata he, ni amɛba hetoo lɛ shi kɛtee shilingi kɛ pence nɔ.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ Tsɛŋtsɛ lɛ akɛ: “Jiemɔ otsitsi lɛ.”

Hatter lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Jeee mi nɔ ni.”

“ Aju! ” Maŋtsɛ lɛ bolɔ, ni etsɔ ehe ekwɛ kojolɔi akuu lɛ, ni amɛfee anɔkwale lɛ he wolo amrɔ nɔŋŋ.

Hatter lɛ kɛfata he akɛ: “Mikɛ amɛ toɔ koni mahɔ̃ɔ,” akɛ shishitsɔɔmɔ; "Mibɛ mi diɛŋtsɛ mi nɔ ko. Miji tsɛŋemɔ helatsɛ."

Biɛ ji he ni Maŋnyɛ lɛ wo ehiŋmɛigbelemɔi lɛ, ni ebɔi Hatter lɛ kwɛmɔ, ni etsɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni ekpokpo lɛ.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Okɛ odaseyeli lɛ aha; “ni kaafee shoo, kɛjee nakai lɛ, mahã agbe bo yɛ jɛmɛ nɔŋŋ.”

Etamɔ nɔ ni enɛ wooo odasefonyo lɛ hewalɛ kwraa: etee nɔ eshi enane kome kɛtee ekroko nɔ, ni ekwɛ Maŋnyɛ lɛ kɛ hejɔlɛ, ni yɛ eyiŋfutumɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ fã agbo ko shwie etii kɔɔpoo lɛ mli yɛ blodoo-kɛ-bɔta lɛ najiaŋ.

Yɛ nɛkɛ be nɛɛ mli pɛ kɛkɛ ni Alice nu he akɛ eesumɔ ni ele nɔ ni yaa nɔ waa, ni enɛ ha eyiŋ fee lɛ kɔshikɔshi waa aahu kɛyashi eyɔse nɔ ni ji: ebɔi dalɛ ekoŋŋ, ni esusu akɛ shishijee lɛ ebaate shi ni eshi kojomɔhe lɛ; shi beni esusu he shii enyɔ nɔ lɛ, ekpɛ eyiŋ akɛ ebaahi he ni eyɔɔ lɛ be fɛɛ be ni gbɛ ko baahi aha lɛ lɛ.

Dormouse ni ta emasɛi lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Miisumɔ ni okɛ ohe akawo mli nakai.” “Ewaaa akɛ mamu.”

Alice kɛ heshibaa babaoo kɛɛ akɛ: “Minyɛŋ mafee nɔ ko, miida.”

Dormouse lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Obɛ hegbɛ akɛ oda yɛ biɛ .”

Alice kɛ ekãa babaoo kɛɛ akɛ: “Kaawie kwashiai asane, ole akɛ bo hu ooda.”

Dormouse lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Hɛɛ, shi midaa yɛ oyaiyeli ni sa mli: jeee yɛ nakai ŋmɔlɔ gbɛ nɔ.” Ni ete shi kɛ mlifu waa ni efo kɛtee kpoku lɛ sɛɛgbɛ.

Be nɛɛ fɛɛ mli lɛ, Maŋnyɛ lɛ ekpako Hatter lɛ kwɛmɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ, ni beni Dormouse lɛ foɔ maŋtsɛwe lɛ pɛ kɛkɛ ni ekɛɛ maŋtsɛwe lɛ mli onukpai lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome akɛ, “Okɛ lalafolɔi ni yɔɔ naagbee lalatswaa lɛ mli lɛ agbɛi aba mi!” ni Hatter ni yɔɔ awerɛho lɛ hoso waa yɛ nɔ, ni efolɔ ekotokui enyɔ lɛ fɛɛ.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛ mlifu tee nɔ ekɛɛ akɛ: “Okɛ odaseyeli lɛ aha, kɛjee nakai lɛ, mahã agbe bo, kɛji otsui efã loo otsui efãaa.”

“Ohiafo ji mi, o-Maŋtsɛyeli,” Hatter lɛ je shishi, kɛ gbee ni hosoɔ, “—ni mijeko mi-tii lɛ shishi—eshɛɛɛ otsi kome loo nɔ ni fe nakai—ni mɛni ji blodoo-kɛ-bɔta ni miifee mlɛo waa lɛ—kɛ tii lɛ ni miikpɛ lɛ—”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “ Mɛni lɛ kpɛmɔ?

Hatter lɛ ha hetoo akɛ: “Eje shishi kɛ tii lɛ.”

“Eji anɔkwale akɛ ŋmɔlɔ jeɔ shishi kɛ T!” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ kɛ ŋmɔlɔ. "Ani obuɔ mi akɛ kwashiai asane? Yaa nɔ!"

Hatter lɛ tee nɔ ekɛɛ akɛ: “Ohiafo ji mi, ni nibii babaoo kpɛ yɛ no sɛɛ—March Hare lɛ pɛ kɛɛ—”

“Mifeee nakai!” March Hare lɛ kpa sane lɛ mli kɛ oyaiyeli kpele.

“Ofee nakai!” Hatter lɛ kɛɛ.

“Mikpooɔ!” March Hare lɛ kɛɛ.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Ekpooɔ, shi nakai fã lɛ.”

“Ojogbaŋŋ, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, Dormouse lɛ kɛɛ—” Hatter lɛ tee nɔ, ni ekɛ haomɔ kwɛ ehe kɛkpe koni ena kɛji lɛ hu ebaakpoo: shi Dormouse lɛ ekpooo nɔ ko nɔ ko, ejaakɛ ewɔ oya.

Hatter lɛ tsa nɔ akɛ: “No sɛɛ lɛ, mifo blodoo-kɛ-bɔta krokomɛi—”

“Shi mɛni ji nɔ ni Dormouse lɛ kɛɛ?” kojolɔi akuu lɛ mlinyo kome bi.

Hatter lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Nɔ ni minyɛɛɛ makai.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ wie akɛ: “ Esa akɛ okai, kɛjee nakai lɛ, mahã agbe bo.”

Hatter ni yɔɔ awerɛho lɛ shwie etii kɛ blodo-kɛ-bɔta lɛ shi, ni ekɛ etsitsi kome shwie shi. Eje shishi akɛ: “Ohiafo ji mi, o-Maŋtsɛyeli.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “ Wielɔ ni he esa waa ji bo .”

Biɛ ji he ni guinea-pig lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome tswa miishɛɛ, ni maŋtsɛwe lɛ mli onukpai lɛ tsi enaa amrɔ nɔŋŋ. (Akɛni nakai wiemɔ lɛ ji wiemɔ ni wa moŋ hewɔ lɛ, matsɔɔ bo bɔ ni afee lɛ aha lɛ mli kɛkɛ. Amɛyɛ kanvas kotoku agbo ko, ni akɛ kpãai fĩɔ enaa: amɛwoɔ guinea-pig lɛ mli, eyitso klɛŋklɛŋ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ amɛtaraa nɔ.)

Alice susu akɛ: “Eŋɔɔ mi naa akɛ mina akɛ afee nakai.” “Mikane yɛ adafitswaa woji amli shii abɔ, yɛ kojomɔi lɛ anaagbee akɛ, “Abɔ mɔdɛŋ ko ni akɛtswaa dɛŋdade, ni kojomɔhe lɛ mli onukpai lɛ tsi naa amrɔ nɔŋŋ,” ni minuuu nɔ ni etsɔɔ lɛ shishi kɔkɔɔkɔ kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ tsa nɔ akɛ: “Kɛ́ no pɛ ji nɔ ni ole yɛ he lɛ, obaanyɛ odamɔ shi.”

Hatter lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Minyɛŋ maya shishi kwraa, miyɛ shikpɔŋ, taakɛ eji lɛ.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ ha hetoo akɛ: “Kɛkɛ lɛ, obaanyɛ ota shi.”

Biɛ ji he ni guinea-pig kroko lɛ miishɛɛ, ni atsi enaa.

“Ba, no gbe guinea-kpɔŋɔi lɛ anaa!” Alice susu he. “Amrɔ nɛɛ wɔbaahi shi jogbaŋŋ.”

“Ehi akɛ magbe mi tii lɛ naa moŋ,” ni Hatter lɛ kɛɛ, ni ekɛ haomɔ kwɛ Maŋnyɛ ni kaneɔ lalafolɔi lɛ agbɛi lɛ.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Obaanyɛ oya,” ni Hatter lɛ shi maŋtsɛwe lɛ oya, ni emɛɛɛɛ po ni ewo ekotokui lɛ.

“—ni ejie eyitso kɛkɛ yɛ kpo lɛ nɔ,” Maŋnyɛ lɛ kɛfata he etsɔɔ asraafoi lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome: shi Hatter lɛ bɛ hiŋmɛi nɔ dani asraafonyo lɛ shɛ shinaa lɛ naa.

“Tswaa odasefonyo ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ!” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ.

Odasefonyo ni nyiɛ sɛɛ ji Duchess lɛ niyelɔ lɛ. Ehiɛ pepper-box lɛ yɛ enine mli, ni Alice susu mɔ ni ji lɛ he, dani ebote kojomɔhe lɛ po, kɛtsɔ bɔ ni gbɔmɛi ni bɛŋkɛ shinaa lɛ bɔi ŋmɔlɔ shikome lɛ nɔ.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Okɛ odaseyeli lɛ aha.”

Nihoolɔ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Shan’t.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛ haomɔ kwɛ Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ, ni ekɛ gbee ni baa shi kɛɛ akɛ, “Esa akɛ Omaŋtsɛyeli lɛ abi odasefonyo nɛɛ saji.”

“Ojogbaŋŋ, kɛ esa akɛ mafee lɛ, esa akɛ mafee,” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛ awerɛho kɔɔyɔɔ kɛɛ, ni beni ekɛ eniji shwie shi ni ekɛ ehiɛ tsɔɔ nihoolɔ lɛ aahu kɛyashi eshwɛ fioo ni ehiŋmɛii baafo mli lɛ, ekɛ gbee ni mli kwɔ kɛɛ akɛ, “Mɛni akɛfee tarts?”

Nihoolɔ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Pepper, babaoo.”

Gbee ko ni ewɔ yɛ esɛɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Treacle.”

Maŋnyɛ lɛ bolɔ akɛ: “Kola nakai Dormouse lɛ.” "Fomɔ nakai Dormouse lɛ yitso! Tsɔmɔmɔ nakai Dormouse lɛ kɛjɛ kojomɔhe lɛ! Nyɛtsimɔ enɔ! Kpɔ̃ɔ lɛ! Kɛ etsitsii lɛ ajie lɛ!"

Minitii komɛi nɛ ni kojomɔhe muu lɛ fɛɛ efee basaa, ni amɛha Dormouse lɛ je kpo, ni, beni amɛba shi ekoŋŋ lɛ, no mli lɛ niyelɔ lɛ elaaje.

"Ahiishi!" Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ, kɛ hejɔɔmɔ kpele ko. “Tswaa odasefonyo ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ.” Ni ekɛfata he kɛ gbee ni baa shi etsɔɔ Maŋnyɛ lɛ akɛ, "Anɔkwale, misuɔlɔ, esa akɛ obi odasefonyo ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ saji. Ehaa mihiɛ yeɔ mi awui waa!"

Alice kwɛ White Rabbit lɛ beni ekɛ ehe miiwo nibii ni aŋmala lɛ amli lɛ mli lɛ, ni enu he akɛ eesumɔ waa ni ena bɔ ni odasefonyo ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ baafee aha, “—ejaakɛ amɛnako odaseyeli babaoo lolo ,” ekɛɛ ehe. Susumɔ bɔ ni enaa kpɛ ehe eha lɛ he okwɛ, beni Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ kane gbɛi ni ji “Alice” lɛ, yɛ egbee bibioo ni tswaa lɛ nɔ lɛ nɔ!
