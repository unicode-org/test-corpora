YITSƆƆ III.
Caucus-Hewolonɔ Su kɛ Sane Kɛlɛ

Amɛji kuu ko ni tamɔ nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni bua amɛhe naa yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛɛlɛŋ—okpoi ni amɛhetsɔi lɛ etsɔ mli, kooloi ni amɛhetsɔi lɛ ekpɛtɛ amɛhe, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛmiiho nu, amɛkpã amɛhe, ni amɛhe ejɔɔɔ amɛ.

Klɛŋklɛŋ sanebimɔ lɛ ji, bɔ ni aaafee agbi ekoŋŋ: amɛfee sanegbaa yɛ enɛ he, ni yɛ minitii fioo komɛi asɛɛ lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni eji adebɔɔ naa nɔ kwraa kɛha Alice akɛ ebaana ehe ni ekɛ amɛ miigba sane jogbaŋŋ, tamɔ nɔ ni ele amɛ yɛ eshihilɛ be fɛɛ mli. Lɛlɛŋ, ekɛ Lory lɛ je ŋwane be kakadaŋŋ, ni naagbee lɛ etsɔ mlifu, ni ebaakɛɛ pɛ akɛ, “Mida fe bo, ni esa akɛ male jogbaŋŋ;” ni nɛkɛ Alice nɛɛ eŋmɛŋ gbɛ ni eleee afii enyiɛ eye, ni, taakɛ Lory lɛ kpoo yɛ gbɛ kpakpa nɔ akɛ ebaatsɔɔ afii ni eye lɛ hewɔ lɛ, nɔ ko bɛ ni abaanyɛ awie dɔŋŋ.

Naagbee lɛ, Kpɔŋɔ, ni tamɔ mɔ ko ni yɔɔ hegbɛ yɛ amɛteŋ lɛ tsɛ̃ akɛ, "Nyɛ fɛɛ nyɛta shi, ni nyɛboa mi toi! Etsɛŋ ni mahã nyɛgbi bɔ ni sa!" Amɛ fɛɛ amɛtara shi shikome, yɛ ŋwɛitsu agbo ko mli, ni Mouse lɛ yɔɔ teŋgbɛ. Alice kɛ ehiŋmɛii kwɛ nɔ kɛ haomɔ, ejaakɛ enu he akɛ ebaamɔ lɛ fɛ̃i ni naa wa waa kɛji egbiko mra tsɔ.

“Ahɛm!” Mouse lɛ kɛ kɔɔyɔɔ ko ni he hiaa kɛɛ, "ani nyɛ fɛɛ nyɛfee klalo? Enɛ ji nɔ ni mli egbi fe fɛɛ ni mile. Diŋŋfeemɔ ni yɔɔ he fɛɛ he, kɛji oosumɔ! 'William Kunimyelɔ lɛ, ni paapa lɛ sumɔɔ egbɛjianɔtoo lɛ, etsɛɛɛ ni Ŋleshibii, ni miitao hiɛnyiɛlɔi lɛ kɛ amɛhe wo mli, ni yɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ, amɛtsɔse amɛhe waa yɛ kunimyeli ni yɔɔ Mercia kɛ Morcia nɔ lɛ he. Northumbria—’”

“Ugh!” Lory lɛ kɛɛ, ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ wie.

“Mikpaa bo fai!” Mouse lɛ kɛɛ, ni ekɛ ehiɛ tsɔɔ shi, shi ekɛ bulɛ babaoo wie akɛ: “Ani owie?”

“Jeee mi!” Lory lɛ kɛɛ oya.

“Misusu akɛ ofee nakai,” Kpɔŋɔ lɛ kɛɛ. “—Miyaa nɔ. ‘Edwin kɛ Morcar, ni ji Mercia kɛ Northumbria maŋtsɛmɛi lɛ jaje amɛha lɛ: ni Stigand, ni ji Canterbury osɔfonukpa ni sumɔɔ maŋ lɛ po na akɛ eji ŋaawoo—’”

“Na mɛni ?” Duck lɛ kɛɛ.

“Na lɛ ,” Mouse lɛ ha hetoo moŋ kɛ ŋmɔlɔ: “anɔkwale ole nɔ ni ‘e’ tsɔɔ.”

Duck lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Mile nɔ ni ‘etsɔɔ’ lɛ jogbaŋŋ bɔ ni sa, kɛ́ mina nɔ ko: bei pii lɛ, eji ŋmei loo ŋmei. Sanebimɔ lɛ ji, mɛni ji nɔ ni osɔfonukpa lɛ na?”

Mouse lɛ yɔseee sanebimɔ nɛɛ, shi ekɛ oyaiyeli tee nɔ akɛ, “’—na akɛ eji ŋaawoo akɛ ekɛ Edgar Atheling ayakpe ni ekɛ akekere lɛ aha lɛ. Shishijee lɛ, William jeŋba lɛ ji nɔ ni sa. Shi e-Normanbii lɛ ahe ni amɛfeee basabasa lɛ—’ Te oyaa nɔ ohaa tɛŋŋ bianɛ, misuɔlɔ?” etsa nɔ, ni etsɔ ehe ekwɛ Alice beni ewieɔ lɛ.

Alice kɛ awerɛho gbee kɛɛ akɛ: “Efɔ tamɔ tsutsu lɛ, etamɔ nɔ ni egbiɛɛ mi kwraa.”

Dodo lɛ kɛ hiɛdɔɔ kɛɛ, ni ete shi kɛjɛ enaji anɔ akɛ: “Kɛji nakai shihilɛ lɛ mli lɛ, mikɛɔ akɛ ajie kpee lɛ kɛya sɛɛ, koni akɛ tsamɔi ni yɔɔ hewalɛ babaoo atsu nii amrɔ nɔŋŋ—”

“Wiemɔ Blɔfo!” Okropɔŋ lɛ kɛɛ. “Mileee nakai wiemɔi kakadaji lɛ fã shishinumɔ, ni nɔ ni fe fɛɛ lɛ, miheee miyeee akɛ bo hu ole!” Ni Okpo lɛ shwie eyitso shi koni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ ko ato: okpoi krokomɛi lɛ ekomɛi ŋmɔ ni anuɔ.

Dodo lɛ kɛ mlifu gbee kɛɛ akɛ: “Nɔ ni mibaakɛɛ ji, akɛ nɔ ni hi fe fɛɛ ni baaha wɔgbi ji Caucus-foidamɔ.”

“Mɛni ji Caucus-hewolonɔ su?” Alice kɛɛ akɛ; jeee akɛ eesumɔ ni ele babaoo, shi moŋ Dodo lɛ emɛ tamɔ nɔ ni esusuɔ akɛ esa akɛ mɔ ko awie, ni etamɔ nɔ ni mɔ kroko bɛ ni sumɔɔ ni ewie nɔ ko.

Dodo lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Mɛni hewɔ, gbɛ ni hi fe fɛɛ ni atsɔɔ nɔ atsɔɔ mli ji ni afee.” (Ni, taakɛ ekolɛ obaasumɔ ni bo diɛŋtsɛ oka nɔ lɛ okwɛ lɛ, fɛ̃i be ko mli lɛ, makɛɛ bo bɔ ni Dodobii lɛ tsu he nii amɛha.)

Klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ ekadi foidamɔ he ko, yɛ kooyigbɛ ko mli, (“bɔ ni eyɔɔ pɔtɛɛ lɛ he ehiaaa,” ekɛɛ,) ni no sɛɛ lɛ akɛ kuu lɛ fɛɛ to foidamɔ he lɛ he, yɛ biɛ kɛ biɛ. “Ekome, enyɔ, etɛ, kɛ sɛɛ” ko bɛ, shi amɛjeɔ foidamɔ shishi be ni amɛsumɔɔ, ni amɛkpaa be mli ni amɛsumɔɔ, no hewɔ lɛ, ewaaa akɛ aaale be ni foidamɔ lɛ ba naagbee. Shi, beni amɛjo foi ŋmɛlɛtswaa fã loo nɔ ni fe nakai, ni amɛhe egbi kwraa ekoŋŋ lɛ, Dodo lɛ tsɛ trukaa akɛ “Foidamɔ lɛ eba naagbee!” ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛbɔle he, ni amɛmiihoso, ni amɛmiibi akɛ, “Shi namɔ eye kunim?”

Sanebimɔ nɛɛ Dodo lɛ nyɛɛɛ aha hetoo ni esusuuu he babaoo, ni eta shi be kakadaŋŋ ni ekɛ enine kome miimɔ ehiɛ nɔ (gbɛhe ni ofɔɔ Shakespeare namɔ yɛ mli, yɛ emfonirii amli lɛ), yɛ be mli ni mɛi krokomɛi lɛ mɛɔ dioo. Naagbee lɛ, Dodo lɛ kɛɛ, “ Mɔ fɛɛ mɔ eye kunim, ni esa akɛ mɛi fɛɛ aná jweremɔ nii.”

“Shi namɔ ji mɔ ni baaha jweremɔ nii lɛ?” gbeei babaoo bi.

“Mɛni hewɔ, lɛ , lɛɛlɛŋ,” Dodo lɛ kɛɛ, ni ekɛ enine kome tsɔɔ Alice; ni kuu muu lɛ fɛɛ miibɔle ehe shikome, ni amɛmiibolɔ yɛ gbɛ ni futuɔ mɔ nɔ akɛ, “Jweremɔ nii! Jweremɔ nii!”

Alice leee nɔ ni efee, ni yɛ hiɛnɔkamɔ ni enijiaŋ eje wui lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ enine wo ekotoku lɛ mli, ni ejie comfits adeka ko, (ehi jogbaŋŋ akɛ ŋmei nu lɛ eboteee mli), ni ekɛ amɛ ha ŋwɛibɔfoi akɛ jweremɔ nii. A-piece kome pɛpɛɛpɛ yɛ, ni ebɔle he fɛɛ.

“Shi esa akɛ lɛ diɛŋtsɛ ená jweremɔ nii, ole,” Moo lɛ kɛɛ.

Dodo lɛ ha hetoo kɛ hiɛdɔɔ waa akɛ: “Eji anɔkwale.” “Mɛni kroko oyɔɔ yɛ okotoku lɛ mli?” etee nɔ, ni etsɔ ehe ekwɛ Alice.

Alice kɛ awerɛho kɛɛ akɛ: “Thɔɔmɔ tsɔne ko pɛ.”

Dodo lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Okɛha biɛ.”

Kɛkɛ ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛbɔle ehe shikome ekoŋŋ, yɛ be mli ni Dodo lɛ kɛ hiɛdɔɔ kɛ tsɔne lɛ haa, ni ekɛɛ “Wɔmiikpa bo fai ni okpɛlɛ tsɔne fɛfɛo nɛɛ nɔ;” ni, beni egbe wiemɔ kuku nɛɛ naa lɛ, amɛ fɛɛ amɛjie miishɛɛ kpo.

Alice susu akɛ sane lɛ fɛɛ ji jwɛŋmɔ ni bɛ mli waa, shi amɛ fɛɛ amɛhiɛ yɔɔ amɛhe nɔ aahu akɛ ebɛ ekãa ni ekɛŋmɔɔ; ni, akɛni enyɛɛɛ esusu nɔ ko he ni ekɛɛ hewɔ lɛ, ekoto shi kɛkɛ, ni eŋɔ tsɔne ni akɛwoɔ tsɔne mli lɛ, ni ekwɛ ehe nɔ jogbaŋŋ bɔ ni eeenyɛ.

Nɔ ni nyiɛ sɛɛ ji ni aye comfits lɛ: enɛ kɛ dede kɛ basabasafeemɔ ko ba, ejaakɛ okpoi wuji lɛ wieɔ amɛshiɔ akɛ amɛnyɛŋ amɛka amɛ nɔ lɛ amɛkwɛ, ni bibii lɛ hu shwieɔ shi ni ehe bahiaa ni atswa amɛsɛɛ. Shi kɛlɛ, naagbee lɛ, egbe naa, ni amɛtara shi ekoŋŋ yɛ ring mli, ni amɛkpa Mouse lɛ fai ni ekɛɛ amɛ nɔ ko kroko.

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “Owo mi shi akɛ obaakɛɛ mi oyitsoŋwalɛ, ole, kɛ nɔ hewɔ ni onyɛɔ—C kɛ D lɛ,” ni ekɛfata he yɛ gbee bibioo mli, ni eshe gbeyei fã akɛ ebaagba lɛ naa ekoŋŋ.

“Mi nɔ lɛ ji sane kakadaŋŋ ni yɔɔ awerɛho!” Mouse lɛ kɛɛ, ni etsɔ ehe ekwɛ Alice, ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ ŋmɔ.

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “ Eji tsitsi kakadaŋŋ, lɛɛlɛŋ,” ni ekɛ naakpɛɛ kwɛ Mouse lɛ tsitsi lɛ shi; “shi mɛni hewɔ otsɛɔ lɛ awerɛho?” Ni etee nɔ esusu he beni Mouse lɛ wieɔ lɛ, bɔni afee ni esusumɔ yɛ sane lɛ he lɛ tamɔ nɛkɛ:—

“Oyaaa kpee lɛ!” Mouse lɛ kɛɛ Alice kɛ mlifu. “Mɛni osusuɔ he?”

Alice kɛ heshibaa babaoo kɛɛ akɛ: “Mikpaa bo fai, misusuɔ akɛ oshɛ ŋwɛi ŋta ni ji enumɔ lɛ he?”

“Mibɛ ! ” Mouse lɛ bolɔ, ni ekɛ mlifu waa kɛ mlifu wie.

“Kpoo ko!” Alice kɛɛ, ni efee klalo be fɛɛ be akɛ ebaaha ehe afee mɔ ni he yɔɔ sɛɛnamɔ, ni ekɛ haomɔ miikwɛ lɛ. “Oo, ha maye mabua ni ajie lɛ!”

“Mifeŋ nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ,” Moo lɛ te shi ni enyiɛ. “Ogbeɔ mihe guɔ kɛtsɔ kwashiai asane ni tamɔ nɛkɛ ni owieɔ lɛ nɔ!”

“Mitsɔɔɔ nakai!” Alice ohiafo lɛ kpa fai. “Shi omli fuɔ oya, ole!”

Mouse lɛ ŋmɔ kɛkɛ ni ekɛha hetoo.

“Ofainɛ ku osɛɛ oba ni ogbe osane lɛ naa!” Alice tsɛ yɛ esɛɛ; ni mɛi krokomɛi lɛ fɛɛ kɛ amɛhe wo lala mli akɛ, “Hɛɛ, ofainɛ feemɔ!” shi Mouse lɛ shwie eyitso kɛkɛ kɛ tsuishitoo, ni enyiɛ oya fioo.

“Mɛɛ mɔbɔ sane ji akɛ ehiŋ shi!” Lory lɛ ŋmɔ, beni eje kpo kwraa kɛjɛ hiŋmɛii amli pɛ kɛkɛ; ni Crab onukpa ko kɛ hegbɛ lɛ tsu nii ni ekɛɛ ebiyoo lɛ akɛ “Ah, misuɔlɔ! Ha enɛ afee nikasemɔ aha bo koni omli akafu kɔkɔɔkɔ!” “Tsɔɔmɔ olilɛi, Ma!” oblanyo fioo Crab lɛ kɛɛ, ni ekɛ ŋmɔlɔ fioo wie. “Oshɛ bɔ ni sa ni oooka ŋshɔŋloo ko tsuishitoo okwɛ!”

“Miisumɔ ni maná wɔ Dina lɛ yɛ biɛ, mile akɛ miyɛ!” Alice kɛɛ kɛ gbee ni wa, ni ekɛ mɔ ko mɔ ko pɔtɛɛ ewieee. “Etsɛŋ ni ebaaŋɔ ekoŋŋ!”

“Ni namɔ ji Dina, kɛji manyɛ mikɛ mihe awo mli ni mabi sane lɛ?” Lory lɛ kɛɛ.

Alice kɛ miishɛɛ ha hetoo, ejaakɛ efee klalo be fɛɛ be akɛ ebaawie e-kooloo lɛ he akɛ: "Dinah ji wɔ tsɛji lɛ. Ni eji mɔ ko ni he hiaa waa kɛha tsɛji amɔmɔ ni onyɛɛɛ osusu he! Ni oh, mishweɔ waa akɛ obaana lɛ yɛ okpoi lɛ asɛɛ! Mɛni hewɔ, ebaaye okpo bibioo ko kɛji okwɛ lɛ pɛ!"

Wiemɔ nɛɛ ha mɛi ni yɔɔ kuu lɛ mli lɛ ná henumɔ ni sa kadimɔ waa. Okpoi lɛ ekomɛi tee oya amrɔ nɔŋŋ: Magpie onukpa ko bɔi ehe ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ waa, ni ewie akɛ, “Esa akɛ mishɛ shia diɛŋtsɛ; gbɛkɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ esaaa mitsitsi!” ni Canary ko kɛ gbee ni hosoɔ tsɛ ebii lɛ akɛ, “Nyɛbaa, misuɔlɔi! Be eshɛ ni nyɛ fɛɛ nyɛwɔɔ saatso nɔ!” Amɛ fɛɛ amɛshi yɛ gbɛi srɔtoi anɔ, ni etsɛɛɛ ni Alice pɛ eshwɛ.

“Miná kulɛ mitsĩii Dina tã!” ekɛɛ ehe kɛ awerɛho gbee. "Etamɔ nɔ ni mɔ ko mɔ ko sumɔɔɔ lɛ, yɛ biɛ, ni miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ eji tsɛji ni hi fe fɛɛ yɛ je lɛŋ! Oh, misuɔmɔ Dinah! Misusuɔ akɛ ani mana bo dɔŋŋ!" Ni biɛ ji he ni Alice ohiafo lɛ bɔi yaafo ekoŋŋ, ejaakɛ enu he akɛ efee ekome waa ni egbɔjɔ. Shi kɛlɛ, yɛ be fioo ko sɛɛ lɛ, enu naji ahe gbee fioo ko ekoŋŋ yɛ shɔŋŋ, ni ekwɛ ŋwɛi kɛ miishɛɛ, ni ehiɛ kã nɔ akɛ Mouse lɛ etsake ejwɛŋmɔ, ni eeku esɛɛ eba koni ebagbe esane lɛ naa.
