YITSƆƆ XII.
Alice Odaseyeli lɛ

"Biɛ!" Alice bolɔ, ni ehiɛ kpa bɔ ni eda yɛ minitii fioo ni eho lɛ amli lɛ nɔ kwraa, ni ejo foi oya aahu akɛ ekɛ e-skirt lɛ naa tsɔ kojolɔi adeka lɛ nɔ, ni egba kojolɔi lɛ fɛɛ anaa kɛtee asafo babaoo ni yɔɔ shishi lɛ ayitsei anɔ, ni jɛmɛ amɛkã shi ni amɛmiibɔle amɛhe, lɛ mlifu yɛ oshara mli yɛ otsi ni tsɔ hiɛ lɛ mli.

“Oo, mikpaa bo fai!” ekɛ gbee ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli waa bolɔ, ni ebɔi amɛ shwiemɔ ekoŋŋ oya bɔ ni eeenyɛ, ejaakɛ shika tsuru loo lɛ oshara lɛ tee nɔ efo yɛ eyitsoŋ, ni eyɛ susumɔ ko ni bɛ faŋŋ akɛ esa akɛ abua amɛnaa shikome ni akɛwo kojolɔi akuu lɛ mli ekoŋŋ, kɛjee nakai lɛ amɛbaagboi.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛ gbee ni mli wa waa kɛɛ akɛ: “Kojomɔ lɛ nyɛŋ aya nɔ, kɛyashi kojolɔi akuu lɛ fɛɛ baaku amɛsɛɛ amɛba amɛhe— fɛɛ ,” ekɛ nɔmimaa babaoo etĩ mli, ni ekwɛ Alice waa beni ewieɔ nakai lɛ.

Alice kwɛ jury-adaka lɛ, ni ena akɛ, yɛ oyaiyeli ni eyɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ, ekɛ Lizard lɛ ewo eyitso mli kɛtee shi, ni ohiafo bibioo lɛ miitswa etsitsi kɛmiibɔle ehe yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ awerɛho nɔ, ni enyɛɛɛ efã kwraa. Etsɛɛɛ ni ejie lɛ ekoŋŋ, ni ekɛto he jogbaŋŋ; ekɛɛ ehe akɛ: “jeee akɛ etsɔɔ babaoo; “Esa akɛ masusu akɛ ebaaye ebua waa yɛ kaa lɛ mli yɛ gbɛ kome nɔ kɛyaa ŋwɛi tamɔ gbɛ kroko lɛ nɔŋŋ.”

Beni kojolɔi akuu lɛ he jɔ amɛ fioo kɛjɛ mlifu ni amɛyɔɔ lɛ mli, ni ana amɛ slates kɛ pensil ni akɛ amɛ ku amɛsɛɛ amɛha amɛ lɛ, amɛbɔi nitsumɔ waa koni amɛŋmala oshara lɛ he yinɔsane, amɛ fɛɛ ja Lizard, ni etamɔ nɔ ni eye enɔ kunim tsɔ ni enyɛŋ efee nɔ ko nɔ ko shi moŋ eta shi ni ekɛ enaabu egbele ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ.

“Mɛni ole yɛ jarayeli nitsumɔ nɛɛ he?” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ Alice.

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “Nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ tee nɔ ekɛɛ akɛ: “Nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ? ”

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “Nɔ ko nɔ ko bɛ.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ tsɔ ehe ekwɛ kojolɔi akuu lɛ ni ekɛɛ akɛ: “Enɛ he miihia waa.” No mli lɛ, amɛje enɛ ŋmamɔ shishi yɛ amɛ slate lɛ nɔ pɛŋ, ni Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ kpa sane lɛ mli akɛ: “ Un important, your Majesty means, of course,” ekɛɛ kɛ bulɛ ni mli wa waa, shi ekɛ ehiɛ tsɔɔ lɛ ni ekɛ hiɛ fee lɛ beni ewieɔ lɛ.

“ Nɔ ni he ehiaaa, lɛɛlɛŋ, mitsɔɔ,” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ oya, ni ekɛ gbee ni baa shi tee nɔ ewie ehe, .

“he hia—he ehiaaa—he ehiaaa—he hia—” tamɔ nɔ ni eeka wiemɔ ni gbɛɛmɔ hi fe fɛɛ lɛ ekwɛ.

Kojolɔi akuu lɛ mli bii lɛ ekomɛi ŋma akɛ “ehe hia,” ni mɛi komɛi hu ŋma akɛ “ehe ehiaaa.” Alice nyɛ ena enɛ, ejaakɛ ebɛŋkɛ bɔ ni sa ni ebaanyɛ ekwɛ amɛ slate lɛ nɔ; “shi ekɔɔɔ he eko fioo,” esusu yɛ ehe akɛ: “Shi ekɔɔɔ he eko fioo.”

Yɛ nɛkɛ be nɛɛ mli lɛ, Maŋtsɛ lɛ, ni kɛ ehe ewo niŋmaa yɛ ewolo lɛ mli be ko lɛ, tswa ŋmɔlɔ akɛ “Dioofeemɔ!” ni ekane kɛjɛ ewolo lɛ mli akɛ, “Mla Nyɔŋmai Ejwɛ kɛ Enyɔ. Mɛi fɛɛ ni nɔ kwɔ fe kilomita kome lɛ ashi kojomɔhe lɛ .”

Mɔ fɛɛ mɔ kwɛ Alice.

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “ Mikɛ mihe wooo kilomita kome mli.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Bo ji bo.”

Maŋnyɛ lɛ kɛfata he akɛ: “Eshɛɔ shitoi enyɔ.”

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “Ojogbaŋŋ, miyaaa, bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, kɛfata he lɛ, jeee mla ni yɔɔ daa ji no: bo ji mɔ ni fee amrɔ nɛɛ.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Eji mla ni etsɛ fe fɛɛ yɛ wolo lɛ mli.”

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “Kɛ nakai lɛ, esa akɛ efee Nɔmba Klɛŋklɛŋ.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ hiɛ fee lɛ yaa, ni eŋa ewolo lɛ oya. Ekɛɛ kojolɔi akuu lɛ kɛ gbee ni baa shi ni hosoɔ akɛ: “Nyɛsusua nyɛkojomɔ lɛ he.”

“Odaseyelii krokomɛi yɛ ni baaba lolo, ofainɛ o-Maŋtsɛyeli lɛ,” Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ kɛɛ, ni ekɛ oyaiyeli babaoo te shi; “akɛ wolo nɛɛ ewo mli pɛŋ.”

“Mɛni yɔɔ mli?” Maŋnyɛ lɛ kɛɛ.

“Migbeleko mli lolo,” Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ kɛɛ, “shi etamɔ nɔ ni eji wolo, ni gboklɛfonyo lɛ ŋma kɛha—kɛha mɔ ko.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Esa akɛ nakai eji, ja aŋmaaa mɔ ko mɔ ko, ni jeee nɔ ni afɔɔ feemɔ, ole.”

“Namɔ atsɔɔ lɛ tɛ̃ɛ?” kojolɔi lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome kɛɛ.

Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Atsɔɔɔ gbɛ kwraa; “yɛ anɔkwale mli lɛ, aŋma nɔ ko yɛ kponɔgbɛ .” Egbele wolo lɛ mli beni ewieɔ lɛ, ni ekɛfata he akɛ “Jeee woloŋmaa ji lɛ, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ: kukuji ni ato he gbɛjianɔ ni.”

“Ani amɛ yɛ gboklɛfonyo lɛ nine nɔ?” kojolɔi lɛ ateŋ mɔ kroko hu bi.

“Daabi, amɛjeee nakai,” Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ kɛɛ, “ni no ji nɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ fe fɛɛ yɛ he.” (Kojolɔi akuu lɛ fɛɛ hiɛ fee amɛ yaa.)

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Ekolɛ ekase mɔ kroko nine.” (Kojolɔi akuu lɛ fɛɛ mli tse ekoŋŋ.)

Knave lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Ofainɛ o-Maŋtsɛyeli lɛ, jeee mi miŋma, ni amɛnyɛŋ amɛma nɔ mi akɛ miŋma: gbɛi ko bɛ ni akɛ nine woɔ shishi yɛ naagbee.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ, "Kɛ́ okɛ onine wooo shishi lɛ, no lɛ, no haa sane lɛ mli woɔ wu kɛkɛ. Ekolɛ otsɔɔ akɛ obaafee nɔ ko ni ehiii, kɛjee nakai lɛ, kulɛ okɛ onine wo ogbɛi shishi tamɔ nuu anɔkwafo."

Nine ni mɛi fɛɛ tswa yɛ enɛ he lɛ ba: eji klɛŋklɛŋ nilee diɛŋtsɛ ni Maŋtsɛ lɛ wie nakai gbi lɛ.

Maŋnyɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Enɛ maa nɔ mi akɛ eye fɔ.”

“Etsɔɔɔ nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ!” Alice kɛɛ. “Mɛni hewɔ, oleee nɔ ni amɛwieɔ he lɛ po!”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Kanemɔ amɛ.”

Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ wo ehiŋmɛigbelemɔi lɛ. “Nɛgbɛ maje shishi yɛ, ofainɛ o-Maŋtsɛyeli lɛ?” ebi.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛ hiɛdɔɔ kɛɛ akɛ: “Je shishi kɛjɛ shishijee, ni oya nɔ kɛyashi obaashɛ naagbee: no sɛɛ lɛ, kpa.”

Kukuji ni Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ kaneɔ nɛ:—

“Amɛkɛɛ mi akɛ oya eŋɔɔ, .
Ni etsĩ mi tã etsɔɔ lɛ:
Eha mi subaŋ kpakpa, .
Shi ekɛɛ minyɛɛɛ mafo nu.

Etsu amɛ sane akɛ miteee
(Wɔle akɛ eji anɔkwale):
Kɛji esa akɛ ekɛ sane lɛ aya hiɛ lɛ, .
Mɛni baaba onɔ?

Miha lɛ ekome, amɛha lɛ enyɔ, .
Okɛ etɛ loo nɔ ni fe nakai ha wɔ;
Amɛ fɛɛ amɛku amɛsɛɛ kɛjɛ eŋɔɔ kɛba oŋɔɔ;
Eyɛ mli akɛ amɛji minɔ̃ tsutsu moŋ.

Kɛji esa akɛ mi loo lɛ hegbɛ akɛ mafee nakai
Akɛ ehe wo sane nɛɛ mli, .
Ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ onɔ akɛ obaaha amɛye amɛhe, .
Taakɛ wɔji lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ.

Misusumɔ ji akɛ, okɛ ohe ewo mli pɛŋ
(Dani ebaaná nɛkɛ hela nɛɛ)
Gbɛtsii nii ko ni ba yɛ teŋ
Lɛ, kɛ wɔ diɛŋtsɛ wɔhe, kɛ lɛ.

Kaaha ele akɛ esumɔɔ amɛ fe fɛɛ,
Ejaakɛ esa akɛ enɛ aba mli be fɛɛ be
Teemɔŋ sane ko, ni akɛto mɛi krokomɛi fɛɛ ahe, .
Yɛ mi kɛ bo teŋ.”

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ, ni ekɛ eniji shwie shi akɛ: “No ji odaseyeli ni he hiaa fe fɛɛ ni wɔnu kɛbashi; “no hewɔ lɛ agbɛnɛ ha kojolɔi akuu lɛ—”

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “Kɛ́ amɛteŋ mɔ ko baanyɛ atsɔɔ mli lɛ, (eda waa yɛ naagbee minitii fioo lɛ amli ni esheee gbeyei fioo akɛ ebaafo enaa lɛ), maha lɛ sixpence. Miheee miyeee akɛ shishinumɔ ko yɛ mli.”

Kojolɔi akuu lɛ fɛɛ ŋma yɛ amɛ slate lɛ nɔ akɛ, “ Eheee eyeee akɛ shishinumɔ ko yɛ mli,” shi amɛteŋ mɔ ko mɔ ko bɔɔɔ mɔdɛŋ ni etsɔɔ wolo lɛ mli.

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Kɛ́ shishinumɔ ko bɛ mli lɛ, no hereɔ haomɔ jeŋ ko yiwala, ole, taakɛ ehe ehiaaa ni wɔbɔ mɔdɛŋ ni wɔtao eko lɛ. Ni kɛlɛ mileee,” etee nɔ, ni egbɛ kukuji lɛ eshwa enaneshi, ni ekɛ hiŋmɛi kome kwɛ amɛ; “Etamɔ nɔ ni minaa shishinumɔ ko yɛ amɛmli, yɛ fɛɛ sɛɛ. “— kɛɛ minyɛɛɛ mafo nu —” onyɛɛɛ ofo nu, aloo jeee nakai?” ekɛfata he, ni etsɔ ehe ekwɛ Knave lɛ.

Knave lɛ tsɔ eyitso kɛ awerɛho. “Ani mitamɔ lɛ?” ekɛɛ. (Nɔ ni efeee kwraa , akɛni akɛ kaadi pɛ fee lɛ hewɔ.)

“Ehi, kɛbashi amrɔ nɛɛ,” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ, ni etee nɔ ewie kukuji lɛ ahe ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ etsɔɔ ehe akɛ: “’ Wɔle akɛ eji anɔkwale —’ no ji kojolɔi akuu lɛ, lɛɛlɛŋ—’ Miha lɛ eko, amɛha lɛ enyɔ —’ mɛni hewɔ, esa akɛ nakai efee kɛ tarts lɛ, ole—”

Alice kɛɛ akɛ: “Shi, eyaa nɔ akɛ ‘ amɛ fɛɛ amɛku amɛsɛɛ kɛjɛ eŋɔɔ kɛba oŋɔɔ .’”

“Mɛni, amɛ yɛ jɛmɛ!” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛ kunimyeli kɛɛ, ni ekɛ enine tsɔɔ tarts ni yɔɔ okpɔlɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ. “Nɔ ko bɛ ni baanyɛ afee faŋŋ fe nakai . Kɛkɛ ni ekoŋŋ—’ dani ená nɛkɛ hela nɛɛ —’ onáko hela pɛŋ, misuɔlɔ, misusuɔ akɛ?” ekɛɛ Maŋnyɛ lɛ.

"Daabi da!" Maŋnyɛ lɛ kɛ mlifu kɛɛ, ni ekɛ inkstand shwie Lizard lɛ nɔ beni ewieɔ lɛ. (Bill bibioo ni bɛ shihilɛ kpakpa lɛ ekpa niŋmaa yɛ e-slate lɛ nɔ kɛ enine kome, ejaakɛ ena akɛ efeee okadi ko; shi agbɛnɛ ekɛ oyaiyeli je shishi ekoŋŋ, ni ekɛ ink, ni miiho kɛmiijɛ ehiɛ lɛ tsu nii, be fɛɛ be ni ebaahi shi lɛ.)

Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛ ŋmɔlɔ kwɛ kpoku lɛ he kɛkpe akɛ: “Kɛ nakai lɛ, wiemɔi lɛ kɛ bo kpãaa gbee .” Diŋŋfeemɔ ko ni gbonyo ba.

“Eji wiemɔ ni akɛwieɔ!” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛ mlifu gbee fata he, ni mɔ fɛɛ mɔ ŋmɔ akɛ, “Ha kojolɔi akuu lɛ asusu amɛkojomɔ lɛ he.” Maŋtsɛ lɛ kɛɛ, aaafee shi ni ji nyɔŋmai enyɔ nakai gbi lɛ.

“Daabi, daabi!” Maŋnyɛ lɛ kɛɛ. “Kojomɔ klɛŋklɛŋ—kojomɔ yɛ no sɛɛ.”

“Nibii kɛ kwashiai asane!” Alice kɛɛ kɛ gbee ni wa. “Susumɔ ni ji akɛ esa akɛ aná saneyitso lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ!”

“Nyɛhiɛa nyɛlilɛi mli!” Maŋnyɛ lɛ kɛɛ, ni etsɔ purple.

“Mifeŋ nakai!” Alice kɛɛ.

“Off kɛ eyitso!” Maŋnyɛ lɛ bolɔ kɛ egbee ni nɔ kwɔ. Mɔ ko mɔ ko tsiii ehe.

“Namɔ kwɛɔ onɔ?” Alice kɛɛ, (edara kɛyashɛ egbɔmɔtso lɛ he yɛ nɛkɛ be nɛɛ mli). “Jeee nɔ ko nɔ ko ji bo akɛ ja kaadii akuu ko pɛ!”

Yɛ enɛ hewɔ lɛ, kuu muu lɛ fɛɛ te shi kɛtee kɔɔyɔɔ mli, ni amɛba amɛba enɔ: ebolɔ fioo, gbeyeishemɔ fã kɛ mlifu fã, ni ebɔ mɔdɛŋ ni etswa amɛ, ni ena ehe ni eka ŋshɔ lɛ naa, ni ekɛ eyitso kã enyɛmi yoo lɛ tsitsi nɔ, ni ekɛ blɛoo miikpa baai ni egboi ni yɔɔ ehiɛ ni etsɔ ŋmɔtɔ lɛ nɔ lɛ.

“Tee shi, Alice suɔlɔ!” enyɛmi yoo lɛ kɛɛ; “Mɛni hewɔ, mɛɛ wɔɔ kakadaŋŋ owɔ nɛkɛ!”

“Oo, mila lamɔ ko ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli tamɔ nɛkɛ!” Alice kɛɛ, ni ekɛɛ enyɛmi yoo lɛ, bɔ ni ebaanyɛ ekai amɛ, nɛkɛ e-Adventures ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ nɛɛ fɛɛ ni okaneɔ he sane amrɔ nɛɛ; ni beni egbe naa lɛ, enyɛmi yoo lɛ tsɔ enaa, ni ekɛɛ, “ Eji lamɔ ko ni miitao male, suɔmɔ, lɛɛlɛŋ: shi agbɛnɛ jofoi kɛbote otii lɛ mli; shwane miiho.” No hewɔ lɛ, Alice te shi ni ejo foi, ni esusu he beni ejoɔ foi lɛ, bɔ ni ebaanyɛ ejo foi lɛ, mɛɛ lamɔ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ eji nɛkɛ.

Shi enyɛmi yoo lɛ ta shi shiŋŋ beni eshi lɛ pɛpɛɛpɛ, ni ekɛ eyitso fĩ enine nɔ, ni ekwɛ hulu ni miigbee shi lɛ, ni esusu Alice bibioo lɛ kɛ enaakpɛɛ Adventures lɛ fɛɛ he, kɛyashi lɛ hu ebɔi lamɔi yɛ fashion ko sɛɛ, ni enɛ ji elamɔ:—

Klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ, ela Alice bibioo lɛ diɛŋtsɛ he lamɔ, ni shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, niji bibii lɛ fĩ enaneshi, ni hiŋmɛii ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ ni kpɛɔ lɛ miikwɛ ehiŋmɛii lɛ amli—enyɛɔ enuɔ egbee lɛ diɛŋtsɛ, ni enaa nakai eyitso ni ekɛshwieɔ shi bibioo ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ koni ekɛtsi yitsɔi ni nyiɛɔ shi ni baabote ehiŋmɛii amli be fɛɛ be lɛ, ena he ni ebɔle ehe lɛ bafee wala kɛ bɔɔ nii ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni yɔɔ enyɛmi yoo fioo lɛ lamɔ mli lɛ.

Jwɛi kakadaŋŋ agbo lɛ tswa yɛ enaji ahe beni Kpɔŋɔ Eyɛŋ lɛ hoɔ oya lɛ—Kpɔŋɔ ni sheɔ gbeyei lɛ shwieɔ egbɛ kɛtsɔɔ faa ni bɛŋkɛ lɛ lɛ mli—enyɛɔ enuɔ tii kɔɔpoi lɛ agbɛɛmɔ beni March Hare kɛ enanemɛi lɛ yeɔ amɛ niyenii ni bɛ naagbee lɛ, ni Maŋnyɛ lɛ gbee ni tswaa lɛ ji nɔ ni fãa akɛ ekɛ ehe awo mli babaoo— ni shwieɔ Duchess lɛ naneshi, yɛ be mli ni tsɛnsii kɛ tsɛnsii shwieɔ shi ebɔleɔ he lɛ—shikome ekoŋŋ lɛ, Gryphon lɛ bolɔmɔ, Lizard slate-pencil lɛ ŋmɔlɔ, kɛ guinea-pigs ni atsi amɛnaa lɛ aŋmɔmɔ, eyi kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli obɔ, ni ekɛ Turtle miser ŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ futu mli.

No hewɔ lɛ, eta shi, ni ekɛ ehiŋmɛii efo mli, ni fã ko he ehe eye akɛ eyɛ Naakpɛɛ Shikpɔŋ lɛ nɔ, eyɛ mli akɛ ele akɛ esa akɛ egbele amɛ ekoŋŋ moŋ, ni nɔ fɛɛ nɔ baatsake kɛya nɔ ni yɔɔ diɛŋtsɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ mli—jwɛi lɛ baafee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ yɛ kɔɔyɔɔ lɛ mli kɛkɛ, ni faa lɛ baafee ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ kɛyashi ŋmeii lɛ ashwiemɔ nɔ—tii kɔɔpoi ni tswaa lɛ baatsake kɛya gbee ni tswaa kɛ gbee ni tswaa lɛ nɔ, tookwɛlɔ gbekɛ nuu lɛ he—kɛ abifao lɛ ŋmɔlɔ, Gryphon lɛ bolɔmɔ, kɛ gbeei krokomɛi fɛɛ ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ baatsake (ele) kɛya ŋmɔ-tsu ni mli eyi obɔ lɛ mli gbee ni efutu lɛ mli—yɛ be mli ni tsinai lɛ agbɛɛmɔ ni yɔɔ shɔŋŋ lɛ baaye Mock Turtle gbee ni tsii lɛ najiaŋ.

Naagbee lɛ, efee bɔ ni nɛkɛ enyɛmi yoo fioo nɛɛ nɔŋŋ baafee, yɛ sɛɛ mli lɛ, lɛ diɛŋtsɛ yoo ni eda lɛ he mfoniri eha ehe; kɛ bɔ ni ebaakwɛ, yɛ e-afii ni eda lɛ fɛɛ mli, egbekɛbiiashi tsui ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni yɔɔ suɔmɔ lɛ mli: kɛ bɔ ni ebaabua ehe naa yɛ ebii bibii krokomɛi lɛ ahe, ni eha amɛhiŋmɛii akpɛ kɛ miishɛɛ kɛ saji babaoo ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ, ekolɛ kɛ Naakpɛɛ Shikpɔŋ lɛ he lamɔ ni eho lɛ po: kɛ bɔ ni ebaanu he kɛ amɛ awerɛhoyelii ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ fɛɛ, ni ena amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛmiishɛɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ fɛɛ, ni ena amɛ diɛŋtsɛ amɛ miishɛɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ fɛɛ. gbekɛbii ashihilɛ, kɛ hulutsoo be mli gbii ni yɔɔ miishɛɛ lɛ.
