CHAANDANO XI.
Ino nguni wakabba ma tarts?

Mwami aMwami Mukaintu wamyoyo bakalikkede acuuno cabo cabwami nibakasika, ankamu mpati yabantu yakabungene kuzyunguluka mbabo—misyobo yoonse yabayuni baniini abanyama, antoomwe amakkaadi oonse: Silunya wakaliimvwi kunembo lyabo, kaangidwe, asikalumamba kumbali lyoonse kuti amulinde; mpawo afwaafwi aMwami kwakali Nkala Iituba, kajisi mweembo mujanza lyomwe, abbuku lyakuvwunga mujanza limbi. Aakati-kati ka nkuta kwakali tebulu, lijisi nkapu mpati yama tarts: akali kuboneka kabotu, zyakapa kuti Alice afwe nzala loko kuzilanga—“Ndalombozya kuti nibakamanizya mulandu,” wakayeeya, “mpawo bape zyakulya!” Pesi kwakali kulibonya kuti taakwe nzila yakuchita eezi, aboobo wakatalika kulanga zyoonse zyakali kujatikizya nguwe, kuti achite chiindi.

Alice tanaakachili kunkuta yabululami chiindi, asi wakabala aatala ambabo mumabbuku, mpawo wakakkomana kujana kuti wakalizyi zina lyazintu zyoonse zyakaliko. "Ooyo ngo mubetesi," wakalyaambauzya, "nkaambo ka wig yakwe mpati."

Mubetesi, mpawo, wakali Mwami; mpawo mbuli mbwaakasamide musini wakwe atala aa wig, (langa kunembo kuti koyanda kubona mbwaakachita,) tanaakali kulibonya kabotu pe, mpawo teensi wakali kabotu pe.

"Alimwi eelyo ndibbokesi lyabasikubeteka," wakayeeya Alice, "azilenge eezyo zili kkumi azibili," (wakasinikizyigwa kwaamba kuti "zilenge," mwabona, nkaambo zimwi zyakali banyama, zimwi zyakali bayuni,) "Ndiyeeya kuti mbabasikubeteka." Wakaamba jwi lyamamanino ziindi zyobile naa zyotatwe kuli nguwe mwini, kalikkomene: nkaambo wakali kuyeeya, mpawo wakaliluzi, kuti mbasyoonto basimbi baniini bamyaka yakwe mbabakalizyi ncolyaamba. Nokuba boobo, "baalumi-basikubeteka" nibakachita mbubonya oobo.

Basikubeteka bali kkumi ababili boonse bakali kulemba changuzu kapati aazipekupeku. "Ninzi nzibachita?" Alice wakavwiyavwiya kuli Gryphon. "Tabakonzyi kuba achintu chakubikka ansi lino, mulandu kautanatalika."

"Balikubikka ansi mazina aabo," Gryphon wakavwiila mukwiingula, "akaambo kakuyoowa kuti inga baluba kakutanamana mulandu."

"Zintu zyabufubafuba!" Alice wakatalika ajwi pati, lyakunyema, asi wakaima chakufwambaana, nkaambo Nkala Iisalala yakakoongolola, "Kuumuna munkuta!" mpawo Mwami wakasama magilazi aakwe mpawo wakalanga koonse koonse, kuti abone wakali kwaambuula.

Alice wakali kukonzya kubona, mbuli kuti wakali kulanga atala amapeko aabo, kuti boonse basikubeteka bakali kulemba "zintu zyabufubafuba!" aazipekupeku zyabo, mpawo wakali kukonzya kubona kuti umwi wabo tanaakazyi kulemba kuti "mufubafuba," mpawo wakeelede kubuzya mweenzinyina kuti amwaambile. "Kunyongana kubotu kuyooba mumabbuku aabo kakutanamana kubetekwa!" wakayeeya Alice.

Umwi wabasikubeteka wakalijisi pensulo yakali kulila. Eezi masimpe, Alice tanaakakonzya pe, mpawo wakazinguluka nkuta mpawo wakasika musule lyakwe, mpawo wakafwambaana wakajana choolwe chakuchibweza. Wakachichita chakufwambaana zyakuti mubetesi muniini (wakali Bill, Inzoka) tanaakakonzya kuzyiba chakachitika; aboobo, naakamana kuyandaula zyoonse, wakasinikizyigwa kulemba amunwe omwe buzuba boonse; mpawo eezi tiizyakali kugwasya pe, nkaambo tiizyakali kusiya chikozyano aachipekupeku.

"Mukambausi, bala kutamikizyigwa!" wakaamba Mwami.

Aawo Nkala Mutuba wakauma mweembo ziindi zyotatwe, mpawo wakavwungulula bbuku lyakuvwunga, mpawo wakabala boobu:—

“Mwami Mukaintu wamyoyo, wakapanga ma tart,
Zyoonse mubuzuba bwachilimo:
Sikubejelezya myoyo, wakabba ma tart aayo,
Mpawo wakabatola kule!

"Amuyeeye muzeezo wanu," Mwami wakaambila basikubeteka.

"Peepe, pe!" Kabwa wakafwambaana kunyonganya. "Kuli chintu chipati chiboola kakutanaba eecho!"

"Koita kamboni wakusaanguna," wakaamba Mwami; mpawo Nkala Iituba yakauma mweembo ziindi zyotatwe, mpawo yakaita, "Kamboni wakusaanguna!"

Kamboni wakusaanguna wakali Sikusama. Wakanjila kajisi nkomeki ya tii mujanza lyomwe mpawo kajisi chinkwa-a-bbata mujanza limbi. "Ndilomba lulekelelo, Mwami wako," wakatalika, "akaambo kakuleta eezyi: pesi teensi ndakamanizya tii yangu aawo nindakatumwa."

"Wakeelede kuti wamaninsya," wakaamba Mwami. "Ino wakatalika lili?"

Sikubamba nkapu wakalanga kuli March Hare, walo wakamutobela kuya kunkuta, kajatilide mumaanza a Dormouse. "Kkumi aane mumweezi wa Nsondo, ndiyeya kuti mbocakabede," wakaamba.

"Kkumi amusanu," wakaamba March Hare.

"Kkumi amusanu akamwi," wakayungizya mbeba.

"Amulembe eecho," Mwami wakaambila basikubeteka, mpawo basikubeteka bakalemba mazuba woonse otatwe aazipekupeku zyabo, mpawo bakaasanganya, mpawo bakacesya bwiinguzi kuba shillings a pence.

"Gwisya nkapu yako," Mwami wakaambila sikupanga nkapu.

"Tachili changu pe," wakaamba Sikusama.

" Chabbidwa! " Mwami wakaamba, wakacengulukila kuli basikubeteka, aabo bakalemba lugwalo lwakuyeeya kaambo aaka.

"Ndilazibamba kuti ndizisambale," Sikusama wakayungizya mbuli bupanduluzi; "Nsikwe changu ndemwini. Ndili sikupanga zikobela."

Aawa Mwami Mukaintu wakasama magilazi aakwe, mpawo wakatalika kulanga Sikusama, walo wakasanduka kuba mubala uusiya mpawo wakazungaana.

"Kopa bumboni bwako," wakaamba Mwami; "mpawo utayoowi pe, naakuti ndilakupa kuti ujaigwe mpoonya aawo."

Eezi tensi zyakamukulwaizya kamboni pe: wakazumanana kweendeenda kuzwa kuulu lumwi kuya kuli lumwi, kalangilila Mwami Mukaintu cakutazungaana, mpawo mukunyongana kwakwe wakaluma kabeela kapati munkomeki yakwe ya tii muciindi chacinkwa-a-bbata.

Aawo Alice wakamvwa chimwi chintu chakuyandisya kuziba, eecho chakamugambya kusikila naakazyiba kuti ninzi: wakatalika kukomena alimwi, mpawo wakayeeya kuti ulakonzya kunyamuka akuzwa munkuta; pesi naakayeeya lubo wakayeeya kuti akkale aawo mpaakali kufwumbwa kuti kakuli busena bwakwe.

"Ndalombozya kuti nowatakasinkilila boobo," wakaamba mbeba, yakakkede afwaafwi anguwe. "Nsikonzyi kuyoya pe."

"Tandikonzyi kuchita pe," wakaamba Alice mubombe moyo: "Ndili mukukomena."

"Tokwe nguzu zyakukomena aano ," wakaamba mbeba.

"Utaambi zintu zitakwe mulimo," wakaamba Alice chabusicamba: "ulizi kuti ayebo uli mukukomena."

"Iyi, pesi ndikomena akufwambaana," wakaamba mbeba: "kutali munzila iitali kabotu pe." Mpawo wakanyampuka mpawo wakazabuka kuya kulubazu lumwi lwenkuta.

Muchiindi choonse eechi Mwami Mukaintu tanaakaleka pe kulanga Sikusama, mpawo, aawo Dormouse naakazabuka nkuta, wakaambila umwi wababelesi bamunkuta kuti, "Kondiletela mulongo wabaimbi bakali kukonsati yamamanino!" aawo Sikubamba Nkumba wakakankama loko, zyakuti wakazungaanya nsapato zyakwe zyoonse zyobile.

"Kopa bumboni bwako," Mwami wakainduluka kachinyemede, "naakuti ndilakupa kuti ujaigwe, nikuba kuti ulayoowa naa pe."

"Ndili muntu mucete, Mwami wako," Sikusama wakatalika, mujwi likankama, "—mpawo teensi ndakali kutalika kunywa tii yangu—kutainda mvwiki yomwe naa kwiinda waawo—alimwi ino ncinzi acinkwa-a-bbata mbocakali kuceya kapati—akumweka-mweka kwa tii—"

"Kumweka kwachintu nzi? " wakaamba Mwami.

" Chakatalika a tii," Sikusama wakasandula.

"Masimpe kumweka-mweka kutalika a T!" wakaamba Mwami cakupozya. "Sena ulandibona mbuli chifubafuba? Kozumanana!"

"Ndili mwaalumi mucete," Sikusama wakayinkila kunembo, "mpawo zintu zyiingi zyakamweka-mweka musule lyaboobo—alikke March Hare nguwakaamba—"

"Teendakachita!" March Hare wakanyonganya chakufwambaana.

"Wakachita!" wakaamba Sikusama.

"Ndakaka!" wakaamba March Hare.

"Ulakaka," wakaamba Mwami: "siya lubazu oolo."

"Mboobu, kufumbwa mbocibede, Imbeba yakaamba—" Sikusama wakayinkila kunembo, kalangilila koonse koonse kuti abone naa ulakonzya kukaka awalo: pesi Imbeba tiiyakakaka pe, nkaambo yakali koona loko.

"Kuzwa waawo," wakayinkila kunembo Sikubamba Nkomo, "Ndakagela chinkwa-a-bbata—"

"Pesi ino mbeba yakaamba nzi?" umwi wababetesi wakabuzya.

"Eezyo tandikonzyi kuyeeya pe," wakaamba Sikusama.

" Weelede kuyeeya," wakaamba Mwami, "naa pe ndilakupa kuti ujaigwe."

Sikusama uupengede wakawida ansi nkomeki yakwe ya tii acinkwa-a-bbata, mpawo wakafwugama ambondo lyomwe. "Ndili mwaalumi mucete, Mwami," wakatalika.

"Uli mwaambi mubi loko ," wakaamba Mwami.

Aawa umwi wa ngulube wakasekelela, mpawo wakasinkililwa ababelesi bamunkuta. (Mbwaanga eeli ndibbala liyumu, ndilakupandulwida biyo mbocakali kuchitwa. Bakalijisi chikwama chipati, chakaangidwe kumulomo antambo: muli ceeci bakabikka ngulube, mutwe wakusaanguna, mpawo bakakkala atala lyancico.)

"Ndiliboteledwe kuti ndakabona kachitwa," wakayeeya Alice. "Ndakali kubala mumiteende, kumamanino aamilandu, "Kwakali kusola kuvwuuma maanza, kwalo kwakasinkililwa ababelesi bankuta," mpawo teensi ndakamvwisisisya ncocakali kwaamba kusikila lino."

"Ikuti nzizyonya nzyozyi, ulakonzya kwiima ansi," wakayinkila kunembo Mwami.

"Nsye konzyi kweenda munsi," wakaamba Sikusama: "Ndili ansi, mbuli mbocibede."

"Mpoonya ulakonzya kukkala ansi," Mwami wakasandula.

Aawa ngulube imbi yakasekelela, mpawo yakasinkililwa.

"Koboola, eecho chakamanizya ngulube zya guinea!" wakayeeya Alice. "Lino tunooli kabotu."

"Ndilakonzya kumanizya tii yangu," wakaamba Sikusama, kumwi kalangide Mwami Mukaintu, wakali kubala mulongo wabaimbi.

"Ulakonzya kweenda," wakaamba Mwami, mpawo Mulembi wazisani wakafwambaana kuzwa munkuta, katanalindila kuti azwe nsapato zyakwe.

"—mpawo ugwisye mutwe wakwe anze," Mwami Mukaintu wakayungizya kuli umwi wabasikalumamba: asi Sikubamba Nkomo tanaakali kulibonya katanasika mupati wabasikalumamba kumulyango.

"Koita kamboni uutobela!" wakaamba Mwami.

Kamboni wakatobela wakali sikujika wa Duchess. Wakanyamwide kabbokesi kapepa mujanza lyakwe, mpawo Alice wakayeeyela kuti nguni, katananjila munkuta, mbuli bantu bakali afwaafwi amulyango nibakatalika kufwusa aciindi comwe.

"Kopa bumboni bwako," wakaamba Mwami.

"Nsikwe," wakaamba sikujika.

Mwami wakalanga kachizungaana kuli Nkala Mutuba, walo wakaamba mujwi lyaansi, "Mwami wako weelede kubuzya-buzya kamboni ooyu ."

"Mboobu, kuti ndeelede, ndeelede," Mwami wakaamba, amuuya wakuusa, mpawo, naakamana kuvwungaila maanza aakwe akufwubaazya sikujika kusikila meso aakwe akasweeka, wakati mujwi pati, "Ino ma tart akapangwa aanzi?"

"Pepper, bunji bwaziindi," wakaamba sikujika.

"Treacle," wakaamba jwi lyakoona musule lyakwe.

"Kobamba mbeba eeyo," Mwami Mukaintu wakapozomoka. "Kosola mutwe wa Dormouse ooyo! Kogwisya Dormouse ooyo anze ankuta! Komujata! Komuuma! Kogwisya amalezu aakwe!"

Kwamaminiti masyoonto nkuta yoonse yakali mubuyumuyumu, kugwisya Dormouse, mpawo, aawo nibakakkala ansi alimwi, sikujika wakasweeka.

"Toziyeeyi!" wakaamba Mwami, kajisi muya wakukatalukwa kupati. "Koita kamboni uutobela." Mpawo wakayungizya mujwi lyaansi kuli Mwami Mukaintu, "Nchoonzyo, muyandwa wangu, weelede kubuzya-buzya kamboni uutobela. Zilandichisa ankumu!"

Alice wakalanga Kalundu Katuba naakali kuvwundauka mumulongo, kalimvwa kuyandisya kubona kuti kamboni uutobela uyooba buti, “—nkaambo tabanajana bumboni bunji pe ,” wakalyaambauzya. Yeeya buyo kugambwa kwakwe, aawo Nkala Iisalala naakabala, ajwi lyakwe pati, zina lyakuti "Alice!"
