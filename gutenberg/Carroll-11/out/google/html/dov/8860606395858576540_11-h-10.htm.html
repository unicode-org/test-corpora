CHAANDANO X.
I Lobster Quadrille

Ifulwe lyakubeja lyakayoya loko, mpawo lyakakwelelezya musana wamuntu omwe mumeso aakwe. Wakalanga kuli Alice, mpawo wasola kwaambuula, asi kwaminiti yomwe naa zyobile kulila kwakasinkilila jwi lyakwe. "Mbubwenya mbuli kuti wakalijisi chifwuwa mumukosi wakwe," wakaamba Gryphon: mpawo wakatalika kubeleka kumuzungaanya akumuuma kumusana. Kumamanino Fulwe wakajokela mujwi lyakwe, mpawo, misozi kayeenda mumasaya aakwe, wakayinkila kunembo alimwi:—

"Ulakonzya kuti teewakali kukkala munsi aalwizi—"""Nsikwe," wakaamba Alice)—"alimwi ambweni kunyina nowakazyibisyigwa a lobster—" (Alice wakatalika kwaamba kuti "Ndakalabila—" pesi wakalilanga-langa akufwambaana, mpawo wakati "Peepe, kunyina pe") "-aboobo tokonzyi kuzyiba kuti Lobster Quadrille nchintu chibotu loko!"

"Maane, masimpe," wakaamba Alice. "Ino nkuzyana kuli buti ooku?"

"Nkaambo nzi," wakaamba Gryphon, "wasaanguna kupanga mulongo kunkomwe yalwizi—"

"Mitwe yobile!" wakalila Fulwe lyakubeja. "Nswi, fulwe, salmon, azimwi; mpawo, kuti wagwisya nswi zyoonse munzila—"

" Eezyo zilatola chiindi," wakanyonganya Gryphon.

"—webo uya kunembo tubili—"

"Umwi aumwi kajisi nswi mbuli mweenzinyina!" wakalila Gryphon.

"Masimpe," Mock Turtle wakaamba: "kuya kunembo tubili, ubikke kuli beenzinyokwe—"

"-sandula nswi, mpawo uzwe munzila njiyonya," wakayinkila kunembo Gryphon.

"Mpoonya, ulizyi," Fulwe lyakubeja wakayinkila kunembo, "wasowa—"

"Inswi!" wakapozomoka Gryphon, kachizungaana mujulu.

"—kusikila kunze aalwizi mbuli mbokonzya—"

"Kozuza kubatobela!" wakapozomoka Gryphon.

"Sanduka mulwizi!" wakalila Mock Turtle, kavwundauka loko.

"sandula nswi alimwi!" wakapozomoka Gryphon ajwi lyakwe pati.

"Kujokela kunyika alimwi, mpawo oyu ngomukozyano wakusaanguna," wakaamba Fulwe, mpawo wakagwisya jwi lyakwe; mpawo zilenge zyobile, eezyo zyakali kuzuzaana mbuli zintu zipenga chiindi choonse eechi, zyakakkala ansi alimwi kazili mubuumba akuumuna, mpawo zyakalanga kuli Alice.

"Kweelede kuba kuzyana kubotu loko," wakaamba Alice chakuyoowa.

"Sena inga wayanda kubona mbichani?" wakaamba Fulwe lyakubeja.

"Masimpe loko," wakaamba Alice.

"Koboola, atusole chifanikisyo chakusanguna!" wakaamba Fulwe lyakubeja kuli Gryphon. "Tulakonzya kuchita kakunyina nswi, ulizyi. Ino nguni uukonzya kwiimba?"

"Oo, ulaimba ," wakaamba Gryphon. "Ndakaluba mabala."

Aboobo bakatalika kuzyana cakulilemeka kuzyunguluka Alice, chiindi achiindi kabanyamuna tunwe twakwe aawo nibakali kwiinda afwaafwi, akuzungaanya maulu aabo kutondezya chiindi, mpawo Mock Turtle kaimba eezi, asyoonto-asyoonto alimwi kachisa:—

"Sena ulakonzya kweenda chakufwambaana?" wakaamba whiting kuli nswi.

"Ndalumba, nkuzyana kubotu loko kweebela," wakaamba Alice, kumvwa kukkomana kuti kwakamana: "mpawo ndilayanda lwiimbo oolu lukondelezya lwa whiting!"

"Oo, aatala akutuba," wakaamba Fulwe, "mbabo—wakababona kale, masimpe?"

"Iyi," wakaamba Alice, "Ndakali kubabona chiindi chiingi kuchilalilo—" wakalilanga-langa akufwambaana.

"Nsezi kuti Dinn nkwabede," wakaamba Fufuwa, "pele kuti wakababona chiindi chiingi, masimpe ulizi mbuli mbobabede."

"Ndisyoma," Alice wakasandula kayeeya. "Zilijisi michila mumilomo yabo—mpawo zyoonse zili atala aamafwuta."

"Ulilubide aatala amafwuta," wakaamba Fufuwa: "mafwuta alakonzya kusanzyigwa mulwizi. Pesi ajisi michila mumilomo; mpawo kaambo nkakuti—" aawa Fufuwa wakajala menso aakwe.—"Komwaambila kaambo azimwi zyoonse," wakaambila Gryphon.

"Kaambo nkakuti," wakaamba Gryphon, "nkuti bakali kunga baunka answi kuya kuzyana. Mpawo bakasowelwa anze mulwizi. Mpawo bakawida kulaale. Aboobo bakajata michila yabo mumilomo yabo. Mpawo tiibakakonzya kwiigwisya alimwi. Nzizyo zilikke."

"Ndalumba," wakaamba Alice, "nchintu chikondelezya loko. Nsikwe nindakazyi zinji aatala a whiting."

"Ndilakonzya kukubuzya zyiingi kwiinda waawo, kuti koyanda," wakaamba Gryphon. "Sena ulizi kuti nkamboonzi ncho chiitwa kuti whiting?"

"Nsikwe nindakayeeya aatala anzizyo," wakaamba Alice. "Nkaambonzi?"

" Chilachita mabbusu amasapato ," Gryphon wakasandula chakulibombya.

Alice wakagambwa loko. "Sena mabbusu amasapato!" wakainduluka mujwi lyakugambwa.

"Nkaambo nzi, ino nsapato zyako zyakabambwa aanzi?" wakaamba Gryphon. "Ndaamba kuti, ninzi chipa kuti kabamweka?"

Alice wakabalanga ansi, mpawo wakayeeya mbichani katanapa bwiinguzi. "Bamanizya akusiya, ndisyoma."

"Mabbusu amasapato aansi aalwizi," Gryphon wakayinkila kunembo mujwi pati, "zyamanizyigwa a whiting. Lino wazyiba."

"Alimwi zyakabambwa anzi?" Alice wakabuzya mujwi lyakuyandisya kuziba.

"Masole answi, masimpe," Gryphon wakasandula katakwe moyo mulamfu: "kufumbwa nswi ilakonzya kukubuzya oobo."

"Ikuti nindali whiting," wakaamba Alice, walo mizeezo yakwe niyakali kuzumanana alwiimbo, "Ndakali kwaambila porpoise kuti, 'Kojokela musule, ndakulomba: tatuyandi kuti ube andiswe!'"

"Bakasinikizyigwa kuti abe anguwe," Mock Turtle wakaamba: "kunyina nswi iikonzya kuunka kuli koonse kakunyina porpoise."

"Sena inga tiikwaba ncobeni?" wakaamba Alice mujwi lyakugambwa loko.

"Masimpe peepe," wakaamba Fulwe: "nkaambo nzi, ikuti nswi yaboola kulindime , mpawo yakandaambila kuti ilaunka mulweendo, ndeelede kwaamba kuti 'Ino nswi nzi?'"

"Sena toambi kuti 'makanze'?" wakaamba Alice.

"Ndaamba nzindaamba," Fulwe wakasandula mujwi lyakutyompwa. Mpawo Gryphon wakayungizya kuti "Koboola, atumvwe zimwi zyazintu nzyochita ."

"Ndilakonzya kukubuzya zyintu nzyondakachita—kutalikila sunu mafwumofwumo," wakaamba Alice kachijisi nsoni: "pele takugwasyi pe kupiluka kuli jilo, nkaambo ndakali muntu uumbi aawo."

"Amupandulwide zyoonse eezyo," wakaamba Fulwe.

"Peepe, peepe! Kusaanguna kweendeenda," wakaamba Gryphon mujwi lyakutakkazika moyo: "bupanduluzi butola chiindi chilamfu loko."

Aboobo Alice wakatalika kubaambila zintu nzyaakachita kuzwa muchiindi naakasaanguna kubona Nkala Iituba. Wakayoowa mbichani aawo kumatalikilo, zilenge zyobile zyakaswena afwaafwi anguwe, chimwi kulubazu lomwe, mpawo zyakajula meso amilomo yabo loko , asi wakaba achibindi aawo naakazumanana. Baswiilizi bakwe bakaumuna kusikila naakasika aawo mpaakali kwiinduluka kwaamba kuti " Wachembaala, Taata William ," kuli Caterpillar, mpawo majwi oonse akazwa munzila ziindene, mpawo Mock Turtle wakayoya chiindi chilamfu, mpawo wakati "Eezyo zilagambya loko."

"Zyoonse zili aatala akuyanda kuziba mbuli mbozikonzya kuba," wakaamba Gryphon.

"Zyoonse zyakaboola munzila yiindene!" Ifulwe lyakubeja lyakainduluka kayeeya. "Ndiyanda kumumvwa kasola kwiinduluka chimwi chintu lino. Komwaambile kuti atalike." Wakalanga Gryphon mbuli kuti wakali kuyeeya kuti wakalijisi nguzu zyakweendelezya Alice.

"Nyampuka mpawo ukainduluke kuti '' Ndijwi lyamuntu mutete ,'" wakaamba Gryphon.

"Mbuti zilenge mbozilailila umwi, mpawo muntu wiinduluka ziiyo!" wakayeeya Alice; "Ndilakonzya kuba kuchikolo achiindi chomwe."
