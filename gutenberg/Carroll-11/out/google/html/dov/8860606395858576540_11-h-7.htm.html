CAANDANO VII.
Pobwe lyakunywa tii

Kwakali tebulu lyakabikkidwe munsi lya musamu kunembo aaŋanda, mpawo March Hare a Hatter bakali kunywa tii kulindilyo: Dormouse wakalikkede aakati kabo, koona loko, mpawo bamwi bobile bakali kulibelesya mbuli kkoshini, kababikka zikobela zyabo aali ndilyo, mpawo bakali kwaambuula atala amutwe walyo. "Tazili kabotu pe ku mbeba," wakayeeya Alice; "pele, mbuli mboli koona, ndiyeya kuti tachikwe makani pe."

Itebulu lyakali pati, pesi botatwe bakali kuzwide antoomwe kukkona lyomwe: "Kunyina busena! Kunyina busena!" bakalila nibakabona Alice kaboola. "Kuli busena bunji !" wakaamba Alice kachinyemede, mpawo wakakkala aansi achibbula chipati kumamanino aatebulu.

"Konywa waini," March Hare wakaamba mujwi lyakusungwaazya.

Alice wakalanga koonse koonse kutebulu, pesi kwakanyina chintu pesi tii. "Nsiboni waini pe," wakaamba.

"Kunyina pe," wakaamba March Hare.

"Mpoonya teensi chakali kabotu pe kuli nduwe kuti uchipe," wakaamba Alice kachinyemede.

"Tiikwakali bulemu pe kuli nduwe kukkala ansi kakunyina kutambwa," wakaamba March Hare.

"Teendakazyi kuti tebulu lyako ," wakaamba Alice; "chakabikkidwe bantu biingi kwiinda botatwe."

"Masusu aako ayanda kugelwa," wakaamba Sikusama. Wakali kulanga Alice kwachiindi chilamfu akuyandisya kuziba, mpawo ooku kwakali kwaambuula kwakwe kutaanzi.

"Weelede kwiiya kutaamba twaambo twako omwini," Alice wakaamba aamoyo mulamfu; "nchibi loko."

Sikubamba nkapu wakajula meso aakwe loko naakamvwa eezi; pesi nzyaakaamba zyakali zyakuti, "Nkaambo nzi nziba nchoili mbuli tebulu lyakulembela?"

"Koboola, tulakkomana lino!" wakayeeya Alice. "Ndiliboteledwe kuti bakatalika kubuzya mibuzyo.—Ndisyoma kuti ndilakonzya kweezyeezya," wakayungizya mujwi pati.

"Sena waamba kuti uyeeya kuti ulakonzya kujana bwiinguzi kuli ncico?" wakaamba March Hare.

"Mbombubo," wakaamba Alice.

"Mpoonya weelede kwaamba ncoyanda kwaamba," March Hare wakayinkila kunembo.

"Ndilaziba," Alice wakasandula chakufwambaana; "at least—at least ndaamba nzindaamba—eecho nchintu chomwe, ulizyi."

"Tachili chintu chomwe pe!" wakaamba Sikusama. "Ulakonzya kwaamba kuti 'Ndibona nzyondilya' nchintu chomwe akuti 'Ndilya nzyondibona'!"

"Ulakonzya kwaamba mbubonya," wakayungizya March Hare, "kuti 'Ndilaziyanda nzyondijana' nchintu chomwe a 'Ndilaziyanda nzyondiyanda'!"

"Ulakonzya kwaamba mbubonya," wakayungizya Mbeba, ooyo wakali kulibonya mbuli kuti wakali kwaambuula muŋonzi zyakwe, "kuti 'Ndilayoya nindakoona' nchintu chomwe akuti 'Ndilayoya nindayoya'!"

" Nchimwi buyo ayebo," wakaamba Sikusama, mpawo mubandi wakamana, mpawo bantu bakakkala kabaumuna kwakaindi kaniini, aawo Alice naakali kuyeeya zyoonse nzyaakali kukonzya kuyeeya aatala amakoko amatebulu aakulembela, eezyo zyatakali zinji pe.

Sikubamba nkapu wakali wakusaanguna kutyola kuumuna. "Ino ndizuba nzi lyamwezi?" wakaamba, wakacengulukila kuli Alice: wakagwisya wachi yakwe munkomo yakwe, mpawo wakali kwiilanga katakwe luumuno, kalikuzungaanya chiindi achiindi, mpawo wakali kujatilila kumatwi aakwe.

Alice wakayeeya mbichaani, mpawo wakati "Wwane."

"Mazuba obile aatali kabotu!" wakayoya Sikusama. "Ndakwaambila kuti bbata talikonzyi kweelela milimu!" wakayungizya kumwi kalanga kunyema ku March Hare.

"Lyakali bbata liinda kubota ," March Hare wakasandula mubombe moyo.

"Iyi, pesi zimwi zibeela zyeelede kuti zyakanjila," Sikusama wakatongooka: "teewakayelede kuzibikka mukati achipeni chachinkwa."

I March Hare yakabweza woola mpawo yayilanga mubuumba: mpawo yakabikka munkomeki yakwe ya tii, mpawo yakailanga alimwi: pesi taakwe nchaakakonzya kuyeeya kunze lyakwaamba kwakwe kutaanzi, "Lyakali bbata liinda kubota , ulizyi."

Alice wakali kulanga atala aamweengwe wakwe akuyandisya kuziba. "Iwachi isesya biyeni!" wakaamba. "Chilaamba buzuba bwamwezi, mpawo tachiambi kuti ndiwoola lili!"

"Nkaambo nzi ncocheelede?" wakavwiyavwiya Sikusama. "Sena wachi yako ilakwaambila kuti mwaka nzi?"

"Masimpe peepe," Alice wakasandula chakufwambaana: "pele nkaambo kakuti chikkala mumunyaka omwe kwachiindi chilamfu antoomwe."

"Eezyo zili mbubonya mbuli zyangu ," wakaamba Sikusama.

Alice wakalimvwa kugambwa loko, majwi aa Sikubamba Nkomo akali kulibonya kuti taakwe nchaakali kwaamba pe, pesi akali muChikuwa. "Nsikwe kumvwisisisya pe," wakaamba, mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya.

"Imbeba yakoona alimwi," wakaamba Sikusama, mpawo wakatila tii iipya ampemo yayo.

Mbeba yakagunusya mutwe wayo kakunyina kukkazika moyo, mpawo yakaamba, kakwiina kujalula meso, "Masimpe, masimpe; mbubonya mbondakali kuyanda kwaamba ndemwini."

"Sena wakayeyela kale chikozyano?" Sikusama wakaamba, wakacengulukila kuli Alice alimwi.

"Peepe, ndaleka," Alice wakasandula: "ino bwiinguzi nzi?"

"Nsikwe muzeezo pe," wakaamba Sikusama.

"Andime pe," wakaamba March Hare.

Alice wakayoya chakukatala. "Ndiyeya kuti ulakonzya kuchita chimwi chintu chibotu achiindi," wakaamba, "kwiinda kuchisowa mukubuzya twaambo tutakwe bwiinguzi."

"Ikuti nowakazyi chiindi mbuli ndime," wakaamba Sikusama, "nootali kwaambuula aatala akuchisowa . Nguwe ."

"Nsezi kuti waambaanzi," wakaamba Alice.

"Masimpe tokonzyi pe!" Sikubamba nkapu wakaamba, kuzungaanya mutwe wakwe cakusampaula. "Ndilakonzya kwaamba kuti kunyina nowakaambuula aChiindi!"

"Ambweni peepe," Alice wakasandula chakuchenjela: "pele ndilizi kuti ndeelede kuzunda chiindi nindaiya nyimbo."

"Aa! eezyo nzizyo zipa kuti kube boobo," wakaamba Sikusama. "Takooyoozumanana kuumwa pe. Lino, kuti yebo koli buyo kabotu anguwe, ulakonzya kuchita kufumbwa ncoyanda awoola. Mbuli chitondeezyo, atwaambe kuti lyakali 9 koloko mafwumofwumo, chiindi chakutalisya ziiyo: weelede buyo kuvwiyavwiya chiindi, mpawo woola lilazungaana muchiindi chifwiifwi, chiindi chifwiifwi chakulya chamansiku!"

("Ndalombozya kuti nikwali boobo," Nziba ya March yakalyaambauzya mumoyo muvwiya.)

"Eezyo inga zyaba zibotu, masimpe," wakaamba Alice kayeeya: "pele mpawo—tandiyelede kuba anzala yazyo, ulizyi."

"Kutali kumatalikilo, ambweni," wakaamba Sikusama: "pele ulakonzya kuzumanana kusika acisela cha 1 mbuli mboyanda."

"Sena mbombubo mbomuchita ?" Alice wakabuzya.

Sikubamba nkapu wakagunusya mutwe wakwe mubuumba. "Kutali ndime!" wakasandula. "Twakazwangana mumweezi wa Nsondo wayinda—katana penga , ulizyi—" (katondeezya achipunu chakwe cha tii ku Nziba ya Nsondo,) "-kwakali ku konsati mpati yakapegwa a Mwami Mukaintu wa Myoyo, mpawo ndakayelede kwiimba

'Komweka, komweka, kababa kaniini!
Ndilabuzya kuti uli buti!'

Ulizyi lwiimbo, ambweni?”

"Ndakamvwa chintu chili mbuli ceeci," wakaamba Alice.

"Zilazumanana, ulizi," Sikusama wakayinkila kunembo, "munzila eyi:—

'Atala lyanyika ulauluka,
Mbuli chibbeeke cha tii mujulu.
Kumweka, kumweka—'”

Aawa mbeba yakazungaanya, mpawo yakatalika kwiimba muŋonzi yayo " Twinkle, twinkle, twinkle, twinkle -" mpawo yakazumanana kwachiindi chilamfu zyakuti bakeelede kwiimba kuti ileke.

"Mboobu, teensi ndakamanizya kapango kakusaanguna," wakaamba Sikusama, "awo Mwami Mukaintu naakanyampuka mpawo wakoongolola, 'Uli mukujaya chiindi! Agwisye mutwe wakwe!'"

"Mbubi loko!" wakakankamuka Alice.

"Kuzwa aawo," Sikusama wakayinkila kunembo mujwi lyakuusa, "tachiti chintu nchendikumbila! Lyoonse lino nchiindi cha 6 koloko."

Muzeezo mubotu wakaboola mumutwe wa Alice. "Nkakaambo kaako zintu zyiingi zyakunywa tii nkozibikkwa anze aano?" wakabuzya.

"Iyi, mbombubo," wakaamba Sikusama kumwi kafweba: "lyoonse nchiindi chakunywa tii, mpawo tatukwe chiindi chakusanzya zintu akati kaziindi."

"Mpoonya ulazumanana kweendeenda, ndiyeeya kuti?" wakaamba Alice.

"Mbubonya," wakaamba Sikusama: "mbuli zintu mbozimana."

"Pesi ninzi chikonzya kuchitika kuti waboola kumatalikilo alimwi?" Alice wakasola kubuzya.

"Atwaambe kuti twachincha makani," March Hare wakanyonganya, kumonya mulomo. "Ndakatala aawa. Ndavota kuti musimbi utwaambile makani."

"Ndili ayoowelo kuti tandizyi pe," wakaamba Alice, wakayoowa a muzeezo ooyu.

"Mpawo mbeba iyoochita!" bakalila boonse bobile. "Buka, mbeba!" Mpawo bakachiuma kumabazu oonse achiindi chomwe.

Mbeba yakajula meso asyoonto-asyoonto. "Teendakali koona pe," wakaamba mujwi litakwe nguzu: "Ndakamvwa jwi lyoonse ndyomwakali kwaamba."

"Kotwaambile makani!" wakaamba March Hare.

"Iyi, ndalomba kuti uchite!" wakakombelezya Alice.

"Alimwi kofwambaana," wakayungizya Sikusama, "naakuti ulakonzya koona alimwi kakutanamana."

"Kaindi kwakali bachizi botatwe," Imbeba yakatalisya chakufwambaana; "alimwi mazina aabo akali Elsie, Lacie, a Tillie; mpawo bakali kukkala munsi aamugoti—"

"Ino bakali kupona aanzi?" wakaamba Alice, walo wakali kubikkila maanu kumibuzyo yakulya akunywa.

"Bakali kupona a treacle," wakaamba mbeba, nikwakainda kuyeeya kaindi kaniini naa obile.

"Tiibakakonzya kuchita oobo, ulizi," Alice wakaamba kabotu-kabotu; "nibakachiswa."

"Aboobo bakali," wakaamba mbeba; "kuchiswa loko ."

Alice wakasola kuliyeyela lwakwe mwini kuti buumi buli kabotu buli buti, asi bwakamugambya loko, aboobo wakayinkila kunembo: "Pesi nkamboonzi ncobakali kukkala munsi aamugoti?"

"Nywa tii limbi," March Hare wakaambila Alice, chakusinizya.

"Nsikwe chintu nchendajana pe," Alice wakasandula mujwi lyakutyompwa, "aboobo tandikonzyi kubweza zyiingi."

"Waamba kuti tokonzyi kubweza ziche ," wakaamba Sikusama: "nchuubauba loko kubweza zinji kwiinda kutaba."

"Kunyina wakabuzya muzeezo wako ," wakaamba Alice.

"Nguni uuchita twaambo twakwe mwini lino?" sikusama nkapu wakabuzya chakuzwidilila.

Alice tanaakazyi nzyakeelede kwaamba kuli ceeci: aboobo wakaligwasya kunywa tii acinkwa-a-bbata, mpoonya wakacengulukila ku Dormouse, akwiinduluka mubuzyo wakwe. "Nkaambo nzi ncobakali kukkala munsi aamugoti?"

Mbeba alimwi yakatola kaindi kaniini naa obile kuyeeya aatala anzizyo, mpawo yakaamba kuti, "Lyakali kabotu-kabotu."

"Kunyina chintu chili boobo pe!" Alice wakatalika kunyema loko, asi Sikubamba Nkomo aMwezi waMarch bakaamba kuti "Sh! sh!" mpawo Dormouse wakaamba cakuusa kuti, "ikuti kotakonzyi kuba abulemu, weelede kulimanizya makani omwini."

"Peepe, ndalomba kuti uzumanane!" Alice wakaamba cakulibombya; "Nsikonzyi kunyonganya limbi pe. Ndilakonzya kwaamba kuti inga kwaba umwi ."

"Chimwi, masimpe!" wakaamba mbeba yakulala kachinyemede. Nokuba boobo, wakazumina kuti azumanane. "Aboobo aaba bacizyi botatwe—bakali kwiiya kuzekaula, nywebo mulizi—"

"Ino bakazekaulaanzi?" wakaamba Alice, kaziluba chisyomezyo chakwe.

"Treacle," wakaamba mbeba, kakwiina kuyeeya pe achiindi eechi.

"Ndiyanda nkomeki iisalala," wakanyonganya Sikusama: "atuunke toonse kubusena bomwe."

Wakayinka kunembo aawo naakali kwaambuula, mpawo mbeba yakamutobela: mbeba ya March yakanjila mubusena bwa mbeba, mpawo Alice wakabweza busena bwa mbeba ya March kakunyina kuyanda. Sikubamba nkapu nguwe alike wakajana mpindu kuzwa kukusanduka ooku: mpawo Alice wakali muchiimo chibi kwiinda chiindi, nkaambo March Hare wakabikka nkapu yamalili mumupika wakwe.

Alice tanaakali kuyanda kunyemya Dormouse alimwi, aboobo wakatalika kuchenjela: "Pesi tandimvwisyi pe. Ino nkuli nkobakagwisya musamu ooyu?"

"Ulakonzya kuyobola maanzi mumugoti wamaanzi," wakaamba Sikusama; "aboobo ndeelede kuyeeya kuti ulakonzya kugwisya musamu kuzwa mumugoti—eh, mufubafuba?"

"Pesi bakali mumugoti ," Alice wakaambila mbeba, katakwe naakabona majwi aaya aamamanino.

"Masimpe bakali," wakaamba mbeba; "—kabotu mukati."

Bwiinguzi oobu bwakanyonganya Alice mucete, cakuti wakaleka Dormouse kuti izumanane kwachiindi chilamfu kakunyina kumunyonganya.

"Bakali kwiiya kuzekaula," mbeba yakayinkila kunembo, kumonya akuswida menso, nkaambo yakalikutalika koona; "mpawo bakazekaula zintu zyamisyobo yoonse—zyoonse zitalikila a M—"

"Nkaambo nzi a M?" wakaamba Alice.

"Nkaambo nzi?" wakaamba March Hare.

Alice wakaumuna.

Imbeba yakoona yakajalide menso aayo achiindi eechi, mpo yakali kuya koona; pesi, aawo niyakaumwa aSikusama, yakabuka alimwi akulila asyoonto, mpawo yakainkila kunembo: "—eecho chitalikila a M, mbuli mbeba, amwezi, kuyeeya, abunji—mulizi kuti mwaamba kuti zintu "zili zinji"—sena kuli nomwakabona chintu chili mbuli chifanikisyo chabunji?"

"Masimpe, lino wandibuzya," wakaamba Alice, kanyongana, "Nsiboni kuti—"

"Mpoonya toyelede kwaambuula," wakaamba Sikusama.

Ooku kutalemeka kwakali kwiinda Alice mbwaakali kukonzya kuliyumya: wakanyampuka mukusesemya kupati, mpawo wakeenda; mbeba yakoona mpawo, mpawo taakwe naba omwe wakabikkila maanu kukuunka kwakwe, nikuba kuti wakalanga musule chiindi chomwe naa zyobile, kayeeya kuti inga bamuita: chiindi chamamanino naakababona, bakali kusola kubikka mbeba mumupika wa tii.

"Kufumbwa mbocibede tandikooyoounka nkuko limbi pe!" wakaamba Alice aawo naakali kweenda mukati kazisamu. "Npobwe lyakunywa tii lyabufwubafwuba kwiinda lyoonse ndyondakabede mubuumi bwangu boonse!"

Mbobakaamba buyo boobu, wakabona kuti musamu umwi wakalijisi mulyango uunjila mukati. "Eezyo zilagambya loko!" wakayeeya. "Pesi zyoonse zilagambya sunu. Ndiyeya kuti inga ndanjila mpawo." Mpawo wakanjila.

Alubo wakajana kuti uli muholo ndamfu, afwaafwi atebulu lyagilazi. "Lino, ndiyoochita kabotu achiindi eechi," wakalyaambauzya, mpawo wakatalika kubweza kakkii kaniini kagolide, mpawo wakajula mulyango wakali kunjila mumuunda. Mpawo wakainka kumulimu kali kulya bowa (wakabamba chibeela chawo munkomo yakwe) kusikila wakasika afuti lyomwe kuya mujulu: mpawo wakeenda munsi aamugwagwa muniini: mpawo —wakajana kuti uli mumuunda mubotu, akati kamaluba aamweka-mweka atusensa twamaanzi aatontola.
