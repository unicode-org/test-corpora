CHAANDANO I.
Munsi lya Mulyango wa Nswi

Alice wakatalika kukatala loko akukkala amucizyi wakwe kubbanga, akubula chakuchita: chiindi chomwe naa zyobile wakalanga mubbuku ndyaakali kubala muchizi wakwe, pesi taakwe zifwanikiso naa mibandi mulinjiyo, "mpawo ino bbuku lilagwasya biyeni," wakayeeya Alice "kakwiina zifwanikiso naa mibandi?"

Aboobo wakali kuyeeya muboongo bwakwe (mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya, nkaambo buzuba bwakupya bwakamupa kulimvwa koona loko akuba mufubafuba), kuti naa kukkomana kwakupanga nketani ya daisy inga kwagwasya kunyamuka akutebula ma daisy, mpawo mpawo-mpawo Nkala Iituba ameso aasalala pyu yakazuzaana afwaafwi anguwe.

Kwakanyina chintu chakankamanisya muli eezyo; alimwi Alice tanaakayeeya kuti nchintu chiyumu kumvwa Kalundu kalyaamba kuti, "Oh noyandwa! Oh noyandwa! Ndiyoochedwa!" (naakayeeya aawo, wakayeeya kuti wakeelede kugambwa aatala aawa, pesi aachiindi eecho zyoonse zyakali kulibonyaanga zili kabotu); pesi aawo Nkala naakagwisya wachi munkomo yachibaki chakwe , mpawo wakailanga, mpawo wakafwambaana kuya, Alice wakanyampuka, nkaambo wakayeeya kuti tanaakabwene kale nziba kajisi munkomo yachibaki, naakuti wachi yakugwisya mulinjiyo, mpawo kalikupya akaambo kakuyanda kuziba, wakazuzaana, mpawo wakachijaana, nkaambo wakali kuyanda kuzyiba muchiindi chakuti ubone kachizwa mumulindi mupati munsi lyachisamu.

Mukaindi kaniini Alice wakaunka ansi, katanayeeya pe kuti inga wazwa biyeni.

Mulyango wa rabbit wakaunka biyo mbuli mugwagwa munzila imwi, mpawo wakanjila ansi, mpawo Alice tanaakajisi kaindi kaniini kakuyeeya kuliimya katanajana kuti wawida ansi mumugoti mulamfu loko.

Kufumbwa naa mugoti wakali mupati loko, naakuti wakawida asyoonto-syoonto, nkaambo wakajisi chiindi chiingi naakaseluka kuti alangisisye akugambwa kuti ninzi chiyoochitika. Chakusanguna, wakasola kulanga ansi kuti abone nkwaakali kuboola, asi kwakali mudima loko kuti abone chintu; mpawo wakalanga kumbali aamugoti, mpawo wakabona kuti zyakazwide makkabati amashelufu aamabbuku; aawa aawo wakabona maapu azifwanikiso zyakaangidwe aazisamu. wakagwisya nkapu kuzwa kuli limwi lyama shelufu naakali kwiinda; lyakalilembedwe kuti "ORANGE MARMALADE", pesi chakutyompwa kapati kuti lyakali litakwe chintu: tanaakali kuyanda kudonkola chibiya nkaambo wakali kuyoowa kujaya umwi munsi, aboobo wakachibikka muli chimwi chamakkabati aawo naakawida ansi.

"Obotu!" wakayeeya Alice mukati kakwe, "kuzwa kukuwa kuli boobu, tandikooyeeyi pe aatala akuwida ansi aamatantilo! Mbombubo mbobayooyeeya kuti ndili aŋanda! Nkaambonzi, tandikonzyi kwaamba chintu aatala anzizyo, nikuba kuti ndawida atala aaŋanda!" (Eezyo zyakali kulangilwa kuba zyamasimpe.)

Munsi, ansi, ansi. Hena kuwa takukonzyi kumana? "Ndilibuzya kuti ndawida mamaile manji buti achiindi eechi?" wakaamba mujwi pati. "Ndiyelede kusika afwaafwi aakati kanyika. Ndibone: inga kwaba mamaile aali zyuulu zyone munsi, ndiyeeya—" (nkaambo, wabona, Alice wakaiya zintu zyiingi ziliboobu muziiyo zyakwe muchikolo, mpawo nikuba kuti eechi teensi chakali choolwe chibotu chakutondezya luzibo lwakwe, nkaambo taakwe wakali kumuswiilila, chakachili chibotu kuti "—meso) musinzo weelede—pele mpawo ndilabuzya kuti ndijisi Latitude naa Longitude nzi?” (Alice tanaakazyi kuti Latitude ninzi, naa Longitude, pesi wakali kuyeeya kuti akali majwi mabotu aakwaambuula.)

Mpawo wakatalika alimwi. "Ndilabuzya kuti naa inga ndawida ansi ! Nchintu chisesemya buti kuzwa akati kabantu beenda mitwe yabo ansi! I Antipathies, ndiyeeya kuti—" (wakakkomana kuti taakwe wakali kuswiilizya, achiindi eechi, nkaambo tiilyakali kumvwika bbala liluleme pe) "—pele ndiyoobabuzya kuti ino zina lyachisi eechi ninzi, ndakulomba, Mwami Mupya. Zealand naa Australia?” (mpawo wakasola kufwugama aawo naakali kwaambuula— kufwugama mbuli mbowakali kuwa mujulu! Sena uyeeya kuti ulakonzya kuchibamba?) "Alimwi musimbi muniini uutakwe luzibo ulandiyeeya akaambo kakubuzya! Peepe, takukonzyi pe kubuzya: ambweni ndiyoobona kalembedwe kubusena bumwi."

Munsi, ansi, ansi. Kwakanyina achimwi chakuchita, aboobo Alice wakatalika kwaambuula alimwi. "Dinah ulandiyeeya loko sunu mansiku, ndeelede kuyeeya!" (Dinah wakali kkayi.) "Ndilangila kuti bayooyeeya nkapu yakwe yamalili muchiindi chakunywa tii. Dinah muyandwa wangu! Ndalombozya kuti nowakali andime aawa! Taakwe mbeba mujulu, ndilayoowa, pesi ulakonzya kujata mbeba, mpawo eezi zilimbuli mbeba, ulizi. Asi mbeba zilalya mbeba, ndilabuzya na?" Mpawo Alice wakatalika koona, mpawo wakazumanana kulibuzya, munzila yakulota, "Sena kkiti zilalya nkumba? Hena kkiti zilalya nkumba?" alimwi chimwi chiindi, "Sena nkumba zilalya kkayi?" nkaambo, wabona, mbuli mbwaatakali kukonzya kwiingula mubuzyo uli woonse, tensi chakali kuyandika pe kuti wakaubikka biyeni. Wakalimvwa kuti wakali koona, mpawo wakatalika kulota kuti wakali kweenda kajisi Dina mujanza, mpawo wakamwaambila chakusinizya kuti, "Lino, Dina, kondibuzya choonzyo: sena kuli nowakalya nziba?" aawo, mpawo, thump! thump! wakasika ansi wakajana mulwi wamasamu amatu aayumu, mpawo kuwa kwakamana.

Alice tanaakachisa pe, mpawo wakanyampuka akunyampuka mukaindi kaniini: wakalanga mujulu, pesi kwakali mudima atala; kunembo lyakwe kwakali nzila aimbi ndamfu, mpawo Nkala Iituba yakachili kulibonya, kachifwambaana kuya ansi. Kwakanyina kaindi kaniini kakusweeka: Alice wakaunka mbuli guwo, mpawo wakasika muchiindi cheelede kumvwa kaamba, mbuli mbukwakajokela kukkona, "Oo matwi aangu amalezu, mbokucheya!" Wakali afwaafwi musule aawo naakajokela kukkona, asi Nkala tanaakachili kubonwa pe: wakajana kuti wakali mubbuwa lilamfu, lyakali munsi-munsi, lyakali kumunikwa amulongo wamalampi aakali kulengaana atala aaŋanda.

Kwakali milyango kuzinguluka ŋanda yoonse, pesi yoonse yakajalidwe; mpawo aawo Alice naakasika kulubazu lomwe kuya kulubazu lumwi, kasola milyango yoonse, wakeenda kachisa akati, kayeeya kuti inga wazwa biyeni alimwi.

Chakutayeeyelwa wakasika atebulu lyamawulu otatwe, lyoonse lyakabambwa agilazi liyumu; kwakanyina chintu aali ncico kunze lyakkii nsyoonto yangolida, mpawo muzeezo wakusaanguna wa Alice wakali wakuti ambweni wakali wamulyango umwi wamulyango; pesi, maawe! naakuti zikulukulu zyakali zipati loko, naakuti kkii yakali nsyoonto loko, asi kufumbwa mbocakabede tiiyakali kukonzya kujalula pe. Nokuba boobo, muchiindi chabili, wakasika achiteni chili ansi nchaatakali kubona chiindi, mpawo musule lyacho kwakali mulyango muniini uulampa masentimita aali kkumi amusanu kuya mujulu: wakasola kkii nsyoonto yangolida mukkii, mpawo wakakkomana kapati kuti yakanjila!

Alice wakajalula mulyango mpawo wakajana kuti wakali kunjila mumulyango muniini, uutali mupati kwiinda mpako ya mbeba: wakafugama ansi mpawo wakalanga mumulyango mubotu loko ngotwakali kubona. Mbowakali kulombozya kuzwa mubbuwa eelyo lyamudima, akuzyunguluka akati kazibulo zyamaluba aamweka-mweka alimwi atusensa twamaanzi aatontola, pele tanaakali kukonzya nokuba kunjila mumutwe wakwe kumulyango; "alimwi nikuba kuti mutwe wangu inga wainda," wakayeeya Alice mucete, "inga tiikwagwasya pe kakunyina magwezyo aangu. Oh, inga ndalombozya kuti inga ndajala mbuli telesikopu! Ndiyeeya kuti inga ndakonzya, ikuti ndaziba buyo kutalika." Nkaambo, wabona, zintu zyiingi zitazyibidwe zyakachitika ino-ino, zyakuti Alice wakatalika kuyeeya kuti nzintu ziche zitakonzeki pe.

Kwakali kulibonya kuti taakwe mpindu yakulindila kumulyango muniini, aboobo wakajokela kutebulu, kayeeya kuti ulakonzya kujana kkii limbi, naakuti bbuku lyamilawu yakujala bantu mbuli zipekupeku: aachiindi eechi wakajana kabbodela kaniini, ("eecho nchobeni tiikwakaliko kaindi," wakaamba Alice,) mpawo munsingo yapepa kwakali lugwalo lwakalembedwe kuti "ME IN K zyakadindwa kabotu aali ncico mumabala mapati.

Zyoonse zyakali kabotu kwaamba kuti "Ndinywe," asi Alice muniini musongo tanaakali kuyanda kuchita oobo chakufwambaana. "Maane, ndiyoosaanguna kulanga," wakaamba, "mpawo ndibone naa kulilembedwe kuti ' poisoni ' naa pe"; nkaambo wakalibala twaano tubotu tusyoonto atala abana bakapya, akuligwa abanyama bamusyokwe azintu zimwi zitali kabotu, zyoonse nkaambo tiibakali kukonzya kuyeeya milawu ngubauba njobakayiisyidwe beenzinyina: mbuli kuti, poker iisalala ilakonzya kukupya kuti wayijata kwachiindi chilamfu; akuti kuti wagela munwe wako loko apanga, kanjikanji ulazwa bulowa; alimwi tanaakalubide kuti, kuti wanywa loko kuzwa kubbodela lilembedwe kuti "poizoni," ulakonzya kutazuminana anduwe, ino-ino naa chiindi chilamfu.

Nokuba boobo, eeli bbodela tiilyakalilembedwe kuti "poisoni," aboobo Alice wakasola kulilabila, mpawo wakajana kuti lyakali kabotu loko, (lyakali, mubwini, kusanganizigwa kwa cherry-tart, custard, pine-apple, roast turkey, toffee, antoomwe a hot buttered toast,) wakafwambaana kulimanizya.

"Eelo kaka nkulimvwa kwakuyanda kuziba!" wakaamba Alice; "Ndeelede kuti ndili mukujala mbuli chipekupeku."

Mpawo mbocakabede ncobeni: lino wakali buyo amasentimita aali kkumi kuya mujulu, mpawo busyu bwakwe bwakamweka aawo naakali kuyeeya kuti lino wakali musaizi weelede wakunjila mumulyango muniini kuya mumuunda mubotu. Chakusanguna, nokubaboobo, wakalindila maminiti mache kuti abone naa wakali kuyanda kucheya: wakalimvwa kuyoowa asyoonto aatala aawa; "nkaambo chilakonzya kumana, ulizi," wakaamba Alice lwakwe mwini, "mukuzwa kwangu koonse, mbuli kendulo. Ndilibuzya kuti inga ndaba buti aawo?" Mpawo wakasola kweezyeezya mbuli mulilo wa kendulo mboubede nikwakamana kuzima kendulo, nkaambo tanaakali kukonzya kuyeeya kuti wakabona chintu chiliboobu.

Nikwakainda kaindi kaniini, naakajana kuti taakwe chakachitika, wakayeeya kuti aunke mumuunda ndilyona; pesi, maawe kuli Alice mucete! naakasika kumulyango, wakajana kuti wakaluba kkii nsyoonto yangolida, mpawo naakajokela kutebulu kuti akachite, wakajana kuti tanaakali kukonzya kuchijana: wakali kukonzya kuchibona kabotu-kabotu kwiinda mugilazi, mpawo wakasola kusikila mpogolela kuti atanta amwi maulu aatebulu, pesi chakali kutezemuka loko; mpawo naakamana kukatazyigwa akusola, kachintu kaniini kakakkala ansi kalila.

"Koboola, kunyina mpindu yakulila boobo!" wakaamba Alice lwakwe mwini, mubufubafuba; "Ndilakulaya kuti uzwe aawa!" Wakali kulipa lulayo lubotu loko, (nikuba kuti tanaakali kulutobelezya pe), mpawo chimwi chiindi wakali kulikalalila loko kusikila misozi; mpawo aawo naakayeeya kusola kulijata mumatwi aakwe mwini akaambo kakulicenga mumusobano wa croquet ngwaakali kusobana lwakwe mwini, nkaambo mwana ooyu uuyandisya kuzyiba wakali kuyandisya kulicengeezya kuba bantu bobile. "Pesi lino takugwasyi pe," wakayeeya Alice mucete, "kulicengeezya kuba bantu bobile! Nkaambo nzi, ndasyaala pe kuti ndibe muntu omwe uulemekwa!"

Muchiindi chifwiifwi liso lyakwe lyakawida akabbokesi kaniini kagilazi kakali munsi aatebulu: wakakajula, mpawo wakajana mukati kakkekke kaniini, aawo mabala aakuti "NDILYE" akalilembedwe kabotu-kabotu ama currants. "Mboobu, ndilalya," wakaamba Alice, "alimwi kuti wandikomezya, ndilakonzya kusika kukkii; mpawo kuti wandipa kuti ndiche, ndilakonzya kunjila munsi lyamulyango; aboobo kufumbwa nzila ndiyoonjila mumuunda, mpawo tandikwe makani akuti ncinzi chikonzya kuchitika!"

Wakalya mbichaani, mpawo wakalyaambauzya, "Ino ninzila nzi? Ino ninzila nzi?", wakajata janza lyakwe atala amutwe wakwe kuti alimvwe nzila njiyakali kukomena, mpawo wakagambwa kujana kuti wakachili mbubonya: masimpe, eezi zilachitika aawo muntu nalya kkekke, asi Alice tanaakali kulangila chintu pesi zyakachitika, zyakalibonya kuti zyakachitika nchintu chibi loko kuti buumi buzumanane munzila iizyibidwe.

Mpawo wakatalika kubeleka, mpawo wakamanizya kulya kkekke.
