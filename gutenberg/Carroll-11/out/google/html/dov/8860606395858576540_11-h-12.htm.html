CAANDANO XII.
Bumboni bwa Alice

"Aawa!" wakalila Alice, wakaluba muchiindi eecho mbuli mbwaakakomena mumaminiti masyoonto aakainda, mpawo wakazuza chakufwambaana zyakuti wakafwusa bbokesi lyabasikubeteka ambali lyachiketi chakwe, akunyemya basikubeteka boonse kumitwe yabantu bakali munsi, mpawo aawo bakali koona kabavwundauka, kabamuyeezya nswi yagolide njaakazwa mumviki yakainda.

"Oo, ndikulomba lulekelelo!" wakakankamuka mujwi lyakutyompwa, mpawo wakatalika kuzibweza alimwi mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya, nkaambo ntenda ya nswi ya golide yakazumanana kweenda mumutwe wakwe, mpawo wakaba amuzeezo uutalibonyi wakuti yeelede kuyobolwa mpawo akubikkwa mubbokesi lyabasikubeteka, naakuti inga yafwa.

"Mulandu taukonzyi kuya kunembo," wakaamba Mwami mujwi lipati, "kusikila basikubeteka boonse bapiluke mumasena aabo beelede— boonse ," wakainduluka kumwi kakankaizya, kalangilila Alice mbuli mbwaakali kwaamba.

Alice wakalanga kubbokesi lyababetesi, mpawo wakabona kuti, mukufwambaana kwakwe, wakabikka Inzoka mumutwe kuya ansi, mpawo kachintu kaniini kakali kuzungaanya mucila wacho munzila yakuusa, katakonzyi kweenda pe. Wakafwambaana kuchigwisya alimwi, mpawo wakachibikka kabotu; "kutali kuti chaamba zinji," wakalyaambauzya; "Ndiyelede kuyeeya kuti inga chagwasya loko mukubetekwa munzila imwi kuya mujulu mbuli imwi."

Mboyakamana biyo basikubeteka kuzwa kukugambwa kwakutyompwa, mpawo zipekupeku zyabo zyakajanwa akupilusyigwa kuli mbabo, bakatalika kubeleka changuzu kulemba makani aangozi, boonse kunze lya Nswi, walo wakali kulibonya kuti wakazundwa loko kuti achite chintu chilichose kunze lyakukkala amulomo wakwe uujalukide, kalanga mujulu mujulu lyankuta.

"Ninzi nchozi aatala amakwebo aaya?" Mwami wakaambila Alice.

"Kunyina pe," wakaamba Alice.

"Kunyina chintu? " wakazumanana Mwami.

"Kunyina pe," wakaamba Alice.

"Eezyo zilayandika loko," Mwami wakaamba, wakacengulukila kuli basikubeteka. Bakali kutalika biyo kulemba eezi aamapepa aabo, aawo Nkala Mutuba naakanyonganya: " Tacikwe makani, Mwami wako waamba, masimpe," wakaamba mujwi lyabulemu, pesi kumwi kamufwubaazya akumulanga busyu aawo naakali kwaambuula.

" Taziyandiki pe , masimpe, ndakali kwaamba," Mwami wakaamba chakufwambaana, mpawo wakainkila kunembo alikke mujwi lyaansi,

"chiyandikana—chitayandiki—tachiyandiki—chiyandikana—" mbuli kuti wakali kusola bbala limvwika kabotu.

Bamwi akati kababetesi bakalemba kuti "nchintu chipati," mpawo bamwi "tachiyandiki pe." Alice wakabona eezi, nkaambo wakali afwaafwi loko kuti alange atala aamabwe aabo; "pele tazikwe makani pe," wakayeeya mukati kakwe.

Aawo Mwami, ooyo wakali kulemba mubbuku lyakwe lyakulemba, wakaseka "Kuumuna!" mpawo wakabala kuzwa mubbuku lyakwe, "Mulawu wamakumi one aabili. Boonse bantu bainda maila lyomwe kuya mujulu kuti bazwe munkuta ."

Bantu boonse bakalanga kuli Alice.

" Nsikwe kkilomita pe," wakaamba Alice.

"Nduwe," wakaamba Mwami.

"Aafwaafwi mamaile obile kuya mujulu," wakayungizya Mwami Mukaintu.

"Mboobu, Ime nsyekonzyi pe, kufumbwa mbocibede," wakaamba Alice: "kunze lyaboobo, ooyo tauli mulawu uuzumizyidwe pe: nduwe wakaupanga lino."

"Ngomulawu wakaindi kwiinda yoonse mubbuku," wakaamba Mwami.

"Mpawo yeelede kuba namba yomwe," wakaamba Alice.

Mwami wakasanduka kusiya, mpawo wakajala bbuku lyakwe lyakulemba chakufwambaana. "Amuyeeye muzeezo wanu," wakaambila basikubeteka, mujwi lyaansi, kalikankama.

"Kuli bumboni bumbi buboola, ndalomba Mwami wako," wakaamba Nkala Iituba, wakazuza chakufwambaana; "pepa eeli lyakabwezegwa."

"Ninzi muli ncico?" wakaamba Mwami Mukaintu.

"Nseena chijula pe," wakaamba Nkala Iisalala, "pele chilibonya mbuli kuti lugwalo, lwakalembwa amwaange kuli—kumuntu umwi."

"Kweelede kuti mbombubo," wakaamba Mwami, "kunze kwakuti tiiyakalembelwa muntu pe, eezi zitali zyachiindi, ulizyi."

"Ino yaambilizyigwa kuli ni?" wakaamba umwi wabasikubeteka.

"Tachikwe makani pe," wakaamba Nkala Iituba; "mubwini, kunyina chilembedwe anze ." Wakavwungulula pepa aawo naakali kwaambuula, mpawo wakayungizya kuti "Talili lugwalo pe, mubwini: ncibeela chamavesi."

"Sena zili mumalembe aamwaange?" wakabuzya umwi wabasikubeteka.

"Peepe, tabakwe pe," wakaamba Nkala Iisalala, "mpawo nchintu chikankamanisya loko aatala anzizyo." (Babetesi boonse bakagambwa.)

"Weelede kuti wakakozyanisya janza lyamuntu umbi," wakaamba Mwami. (Babetesi boonse bakabotelwa alimwi.)

"Ndalomba Mwami wako," wakaamba Knave, "Teensi ndakalemba, alimwi tabakonzyi kutondezya kuti ndakalemba: kunyina zina lyakasainwa kumamanino."

"Ikuti teewakasaina," wakaamba Mwami, "eezyo zyayungizya buyo kaambo. Weelede kuti wakali kwaamba bubi bumwi, naakuti nowakasaina zina lyako mbuli muntu uusyomeka."

Kwakaba kuvwuuma maanza koonse aawo: chakali chintu chakusanguna chabusongo Mwami nchaakaamba buzuba oobo.

"Eezyo zitondezya mulandu wakwe," wakaamba Mwami Mukaintu.

"Tachitondezyi pe chintu chili mbuli eechi!" wakaamba Alice. "Nkaambo nzi, tozi pe kuti nzinzi!"

"Kozibala," wakaamba Mwami.

Kalundu Mutuba wakasama magilazi aakwe. "Ino nditalike kuli, ndalomba Mwami wako?" wakabuzya.

"Talika kumatalikilo," Mwami wakaamba mujwi pati, "mpawo uzumanane kusikila kumamanino: mpawo uleke."

Aaya ngamavesi ngaakabala Kalundu Mutuba:—

"Bakandaambila kuti wakali kuli nguwe,
Mpawo wakandibuzya kuli nguwe:
Wakandipa bube bubotu,
Asi wakati tandikonzyi kusambida.

Wakabatumina mulumbe kuti teensi ndakaunka
(Tulizyi kuti nchoonzyo):
Kuti naa weelede kusungilizya makani aaya,
Ino ncinzi chiyoochitika kuli nduwe?

Ndakamupa yomwe, bakamupa zyobile,
Wakatupa zyotatwe naa kwiinda waawo;
Boonse bakapiluka kuzwa kuli nguwe kulinduwe,
Nikuba kuti bakali bangu chiindi.

Ikuti mebo naakuti walo ndajana choolwe chakuba
Kujatikizyidwe mumakani aaya,
Ulasyoma nduwe kuti ubaangulule,
Mbuli mbutwakali.

Muzeezo wangu wakali wakuti wakali
(Kaatana jisi bulwazi oobu)
Chisinkilizyo chakaboola aakati
Walo, aswebo tobeni, a ncico.

Utamuzumizyi kuti wakali kubayanda kwiinda,
Nkaambo ezi zyeelede kuba
Maseseke, aakasisidwe kuli bamwi boonse,
Akati kanduwe andime.”

"Oobo mbobumboni buyandika kapati mbutwakamvwa," wakaamba Mwami, kumwi kavwuuma maanza aakwe; "aboobo lino amuleke basikubeteka—"

"Ikuti umwi akati kabo ulakonzya kupandulula," wakaamba Alice, (wakakomena loko mumaminiti masyoonto aakainda zyakuti tanaakali kuyoowa pe kumunyonganya), "Ndilamupa sixpence. Tandisyomi kuti kuli bupanduluzi muli ncico."

Basikubeteka boonse bakalemba aamapepa aabo kuti, " Tasyomi kuti kuli bupanduluzi muli ncico," pesi taakwe naba omwe wakasola kupandulula pepa.

"Ikuti kakunyina mpindu muli ncico," wakaamba Mwami, "eecho chifwutula nyika yoonse yamapenzi, mbuli mbututeelede kusola kujana pe. Mpawo nsezi," wakayinkila kunembo, wakatandabika tupango twakali aamabondo aakwe, mpawo wakabalanga aliso lyomwe; "Ndiboneka kuti ndibona bupanduluzi bumwi muli mbabo, mubwini. "— wakaamba kuti tandikonzyi kusamba -" yebo tokonzyi kusamba, tee mbubo?" wakayungizya, wakacengulukila kuli Knave.

Knave wakagunusya mutwe wakwe mubuumba. "Sena ndilibonya mbuli ncico?" wakaamba. (Eecho nchaatakachita pe , nkaambo chakabambwa amakkaadiboodi.)

"Cili kabotu, kusikila lino," wakaamba Mwami, mpawo wakazumanana kuvwundauka alikke: "' Tulizyi kuti nchoonzyo -' aabo mbabetesi, masimpe -' Ndakamupa yomwe, bakamupa zyobile -' nkamboonzi, eezyo nzyaakachita ama tarts, ulizyi—"

"Pesi, chilazumanana ' boonse bakapiluka kuzwa kuli nguwe kuli nduwe ,'" wakaamba Alice.

"Nkaambo nzi, nkobali!" wakaamba Mwami chakuzwidilila, kutondeka kumatati aali aatebulu. "Kunyina chintu chikonzya kusalala kwiinda waawo . Mpawo alimwi—' katanaba achilwazi eechi -' kunyina nowakajisi chilwazi, muyandwa wangu, ndiyeeya?" wakaambila Mwami Mukaintu.

"Taakwe!" wakaamba Mwami Mukaintu kachinyemede, mpawo wakasowa chibikkilo cha ingi kuNziba aawo naakali kwaambuula. (Bill muniini uutakwe coolwe wakacileka kulemba achipekupeku chakwe amunwe omwe, nkaambo wakajana kuti taakwe nchakachita pe; asi lino wakatalika alimwi, kabelesya ingi, eeyo yakali kuseluka kubusyu bwakwe, mbuli mbuyakazumanana.)

"Mpoonya majwi taakweendelani pe," wakaamba Mwami, kalanga kuzyunguluka nkuta kumwi kamweta-mweta. kwakaba kuumuna kupati.

"Nchikozyano!" Mwami wakayungizya mujwi lyakutyompwa, mpawo boonse bakaseka, "Amuleke basikubeteka kuti balange muzeezo wabo." Mwami wakaamba, kwachiindi chamakumi obile buzuba oobo.

"Peepe, peepe!" wakaamba Mwami Mukaintu. "Chisubulo chakusanguna—chisubulo musule."

"Zintu azintu zitayandiki!" wakaamba Alice mujwi pati. "Muzeezo wakusaanguna kaambo!"

"Jata lulimi lwako!" wakaamba Mwami Mukaintu, wakasanduka kuba mubala wasalala pyu.

"Nsikwe pe!" wakaamba Alice.

"Kozwa amutwe wakwe!" Mwami Mukaintu wakakoongolola ajwi lyakwe pati. Taakwe wakanyamuka.

"Nguni uukulanganya?" wakaamba Alice, (wakakomena kusikila achiindi eechi). "Toli chintu pesi kabunga kamakkaadi!"

Mpawo nkamu yoonse yakanyampuka mujulu, mpawo yakamuulukila ansi: wakakoongolola mbichani, chisela chakuyoowa achisela chabukali, mpawo wakasola kubauma, mpawo wakajana kuti wakalede kunkomwe yalwizi, mutwe wakwe wakali muchikungu chamucizyi wakwe, walo wakali kufwusa matu amwi aakafwide aakazwa kubusyu bwakwe.

"Buka, Alice muyandwa!" wakaamba muchizi wakwe; "Nkaambo nzi, koona kwachiindi chilamfu loko!"

"Oo, ndakalota ziloto zikankamanisya loko!" wakaamba Alice, mpawo wakaambila muchizi wakwe, mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya kuyeeya, zyoonse eezyi zintu zigambya nzyaakali kubala; mpawo naakamana, muchizi wakwe wakamumyonta, mpawo wakati, " Lyakali ciloto chakankamanisya, muyandwa, masimpe: pesi lino koya kukunywa tii yako; kwaba kumangolezya." Mpawo Alice wakanyampuka mpawo wakachijaana, kayeeya kuti naakali kuchijaana, mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya, kuti chakali chiloto chibotu loko.

Asi muchizi wakwe wakakkala biyo aawo naakamusiya, wakasendekezya mutwe wakwe kujanza lyakwe, kalangilila zuba kalibbila, akuyeeya Alice muniini azyintu zyakwe zyoonse zibotu, kusikila awalo wakatalika kulota mbuli mbukwakabede, mpawo eechi chakali chiloto chakwe:—

Chakusanguna, wakalota Alice muniini lwakwe mwini, mpawo alimwi maanza masyoonto akali kujatilidwe aamabondo aakwe, mpawo menso aakwe aasalala akali kulanga mujulu muli lyakwe—wakali kukonzya kumvwa jwi lyakwe, mpawo wakabona kuzungaana kwamutwe wakwe kuti atabi masusu aakali kunjila mumeso aakwe—mpawo naakali kuswiilizya, naa kuswiilizya, wakali kulibonya kuti nguni wakali kuzyunguluka busena oobo kapona azilenge zitazyibidwe zyamucizyi wakwe muniini.

Bwizu bulamfwu bwakali kuvwuuma kumaulu aakwe aawo Nkala Iituba niyakali kwiinda—Mbeba yakayoowa yakasansaila nzila yakwe kwiinda muzyiba lyakali afwaafwi—wakali kukonzya kumvwa kuzungaana kwankomeki zya tii aawo Nkala ya March abeenzinyina nibakali kulya chakulya chitamani, mpawo jwi lya Mwami Mukaintu lyakali kulailila beenzu bakwe batakwe coolwe kuti bajaigwe—Nziba yakali kufweba aamabondo aa Duchess, aawo zilongo azilongo nizyakali kuzinguluka—alubo kulila kwa Gryphon, kuvwuuma kwa pensulo ya Lizard, akusinkililwa kwa ngulube zya guinea-pigs zyakasinkilidwe, zyakazuzya muuya, kusanganya akulila kwa Mock Turtle uupengede.

Mpawo wakakkala, ameso aajalidwe, mpawo wakalisyoma kuti wakali muNyika Yazigambyo, nikuba kuti wakalizi kuti wakeelede kwaajula alimwi, mpawo zyoonse zyakali kuyoosanduka kuba zintu zitaboneki pe—bwizu bwakali kunooli buyo kuvwuuma mumuwo, mpawo zyiba lyakali kuzungaana kusikila kukuzungaana kwamatete—nkomeki zya tii zyakali kuvwuuma zyakali kunga zyasanduka kuba mabbelo aambelele, kulila kwaMwami Mukaintu kuya kulila kwamajwi mulombe mweembezi—mpawo kufwusa kwamwana, kulila kwaGryphon, azimwi zyoonse zitali kabotu, zyakali kunga zyasanduka (wakalizyi) kuba kulila kwakunyongana kwalubuwa lwapulazi luzwide bantu—kakuli kulila kwang'ombe kulaale kwakali kuyoobweza busena bwakulila kwa Mock Turtle.

Chamamanino, wakaliyeeyela lwakwe mwini mbuli muchizi wakwe nguwenya mbwaakali kunga, muchiindi chifwiifwi, wakaba mukaintu mupati; akuti wakali kunga wabamba biyeni, muminyaka yakwe yoonse yakukomezya, moyo muuba-uba alimwi waluyando wabwana bwakwe: mpawo mbwaakali kunga ulabunganya bana bakwe bamwi baniini, akupa kuti meso aabo abe aluyandisisyo amakani manji aatazyibidwe, ambweni alubo akulota kwa Nyika Yazigambyo zyakaindi: akuti wakali kunga walimvwa biyeni amapenzi aabo woonse, akujana lukkomano muzintu zyoonse ziuba-uba, kuyeeya buumi bwakwe bwamwana, buumi bwacilimo bukkomanisya mazuba.
