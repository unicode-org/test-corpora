CAANDANO V.
Malayilile kuzwa kuli Caterpillar

Inziba a Alice bakalangana kwaachiindi chilamfu kabatakwe muzuzumo: kumamanino Inziba yakagwisya hookah mumulomo wayo, mpawo yakaambaula anguwe mujwi lyakubula nguzu, lyakoona.

"Ino nduwe ni? " wakaamba Inziba.

Ooku tiikwakali kusungwaazya kwakubandika pe. Alice wakasandula, kachijisi nsoni, "Ime—nsezi pe, taata, lino--ndilizi kuti ndakali ni nindakabuka mafwumo aano, pesi ndiyeeya kuti ndakachinchwa ziindi zyiingi kuzwa aawo."

"Waambanzi aawo?" wakaamba Inziba chakusinizya. "Kolipandulula omwini!"

"Nsye konzyi kulipandulula , ndilayoowa, mwami," wakaamba Alice, "nkaambo teensi ndime ndemwini, mwabona."

"Nseboni pe," wakaamba Inziba.

"Ndilayoowa kuti tandikonzyi kwaamba kabotu," Alice wakasandula kabotu, "nkaambo mebo lwangu tandikonzyi kumvwisisisya pe; mpawo kuba amasayizi manji aasiyene mubuzuba bomwe nchintu chinyonganya."

"Tachili boobo pe," wakaamba Inziba.

"Mboobu, ambweni tonachijana pe," wakaamba Alice; "pele aawo noyelede kusanduka kuba chrysalis—ulakonzya kusanduka bumwi buzuba, ulizyi—mpawo kuzwa waawo kuba chiyuni, ndeelede kuyeeya kuti uyoolimvwa mbuli kuti nchintu chikankamanisya, tee mbubo?"

"Kunyina mbichaani pe," wakaamba Inziba.

"Mboobu, ambweni kulimvwa kwako inga kwaba kusiyene," wakaamba Alice; "ncondizyi njakuti, inga chalimvwisya mbuli chintu chikankamanisya kulindime ."

"Iwe!" wakaamba Inziba cakusampaula. "Nduweni ? "

Eezyo zyakabapilusya alimwi kumatalikilo aamubandi. Alice wakalimvwa kunyema mbichaani aawo Caterpillar naakaamba majwi mafwaafwi , mpawo wakanyampuka mpawo wakati, chakusinizya, "Ndiyeya kuti, weelede kundibuzya kuti nduwe ni, kusaanguna."

"Nkaambonzi?" wakaamba Inziba.

Ooyu wakali mubuzyo uumbi uugambya; mpawo mbuli Alice mbwaatakali kukonzya kuyeeya kaambo kali koonse kabotu, mpawo mbuli Nkala mbwaakali kulibonya kuti ulimuchiimo chibi , wakapiluka.

"Kojokela!" Inswi yakaita musule lyakwe. "Ndilaa chintu chiyandikana chakwaamba!"

Eezi zyakali kulimvwisya kuti zilasyomezya, masimpe: Alice wakacenguluka mpawo wakapiluka alimwi.

"Kozumanana kunyema," wakaamba Inziba.

"Sena nzizyo zilikke?" wakaamba Alice, kumenya bukali bwakwe mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya.

"Maane," wakaamba Inziba.

Alice wakayeeya kuti ulakonzya kulindila, nkaambo taakwe nchaakachita, mpawo ambweni inga kwamwaambila chimwi chintu cheelede kumvwa. Kwamaminiti amwi wakafwusa kakunyina kwaambuula, pesi kumamanino wakavwungulula maanza aakwe, wakagwisya hookah mumulomo wakwe alimwi, mpawo wakati, "Aboobo uyeeya kuti wasanduka, tee mbubo?"

"Ndilayoowa kuti ndime, taata," wakaamba Alice; "Tandikonzyi kuyeeya zintu mbuli mbondakali kubelesya—alimwi tandikonzyi kubamba bupati bomwe kwamaminiti aali kkumi antoomwe!"

"Tokonzyi kuyeeya zintu nzi ?" wakaamba Inziba.

"Mboobu, ndakasola kwaamba kuti "Ino mbuti nzuki nsyoonto iijatikizyidwe," pesi zyoonse zyakaboola munzila yiindene!" Alice wakasandula mujwi lyabuumba.

"Amwiinduluke, ' Wachembaala, Taata William ,'" wakaamba Inziba.

Alice wakajata maanza aakwe, mpawo wakatalika:—

"Wachembaala, Taata William," mulombwana wakaamba,
“Alimwi masusu aako aatuba kapati;
Mpawo uliimvwi amutwe wako lyoonse—
Hena uyeeya kuti, amyaka yako, zililuzi?”

"Mubukubusi bwangu," Taata William wakasandula kumwanaakwe,
"Ndakali kuyoowa kuti inga chachisa boongo;
Asi, lino mbondisinizyide kuti tandijisi pe,
Nkaambonzi, ndilachita lubo alubo.

"Wachembaala," wakaamba mukubusi, "mbuli mbondaamba musule,
Alimwi bakakomena loko;
pesi wakajokela musule kumulyango—
Kopaila, nkaambo nzi eecho?”

"Mubukubusi bwangu," wakaamba musongo, mpawo wakazungaanya masusu aakwe aasiya,
"Ndakali kubamba zizo zyangu zyoonse kuti kaziteteete
Kwiinda mukubelesya mafuta aaya—shalili lyomwe mubbokesi—
Ndizumizye kuti ndikuuzizye zyobile?”

"Wachembaala," wakaamba mukubusi, "mpawo milomo yako ilikompeme loko
Kuli kufumbwa chintu chiyumu kwiinda suet;
pesi wakamanizya nziba, amafwuwa amulomo—
Komba, ino wakakonzya biyeni kuchita?”

Bausyi bakaamba kuti: “Mubukubusi bwangu, ndakabweza mulawo, .
Mpawo ndakazyania amukayintu wangu;
Anguzu zyanyama, eezyo zyakapa kumulomo wangu,
Yakkala buumi bwangu boonse.

"Wachembaala," wakaamba mukubusi, "umwi takonzyi kuyeeya
kuti liso lyako lyakali kabotu mbuli lyoonse;
pesi wakabamba inswi kumamanino aampemo yako—
Nchinzi chakapa kuti ube musongo loko?”

"Ndakaingula mibuzyo yotatwe, mpawo eezi zilazula,"
Wakaamba wisi; "utalipegwi muuya!
Sena uyeeya kuti inga ndaswiilila zintu ziliboobu zuba lyoonse?
Kozwa, naakuti ndilakutanda ansi aamatantilo!”

"Eezyo tazyaambidwe kabotu pe," wakaamba Inziba.

"Tazili kabotu pe, ndilayoowa," wakaamba Alice, kachiyoowede; "amwi mabala akasandulwa."

"Nchibi kuzwa kumatalikilo kusikila kumamanino," wakaamba Inziba, mpawo kwakaba kuumuna kwamaminiti masyoonto.

Inswi yakasaanguna kwaambuula.

"Ino uyanda kuba sayizi nzi?" yakabuzya.

"Oo, tandikwe makani aabupati," Alice wakasandula chakufwambaana; "muntu omwe luzutu uutayandi kuchincha chiindi chiingi, ulizi."

" Nsezi ," wakaamba Inziba.

Alice taakwe naakaamba pe: tanaakachili kukazyigwa pe mubuumi bwakwe, mpawo wakalimvwa kuti wakalikumana kulijata.

"Sena ulikkutide lino?" wakaamba Inziba.

"Mboobu, inga ndayanda kuba mupati mbichani , taata, kuti kotakwe makani," wakaamba Alice: "masentimita otatwe bulamfu butali kabotu pe."

"Mbulamfwu bubotu loko ncobeni!" wakaamba Inziba mubukali, kaliimvwi nji mbuli mboyakali kwaambuula (yakali ma inchi otatwe kuya mujulu).

"Pesi tandizyi pe!" wakakombelezya Alice mucete mujwi lyakuusa. Mpawo wakayeeya lwakwe mwini, "Ndalombozya kuti zilenge nizitafwambaani kunyema!"

"Uyoozyibila muchiindi," wakaamba Inziba; mpawo yakabikka hookah mumulomo wayo mpawo yakatalika kufweba alimwi.

Aachiindi eechi Alice wakalindila amoyo mulamfu kusikila wasala kwaambuula alimwi. Muminiti yomwe naa obile, Nswi yakagwisya hookah mumulomo wayo mpawo yakafwusa chiindi chomwe naa zyobile, mpawo yazungaanya. Mpawo yakaseluka kuzwa ku mushroom, mpawo yakakokoba mubwizu, kaamba biyo mbuli mboyakali kweenda, "Lubazu lumwi luyookupa kuti ukomene, mpawo lumwi luyoopa kuti ukomene."

"Lubazu lumwi lwa nzi? Lubazu lumwi lwa nzi? " wakayeeya Alice mumoyo wakwe.

"Aawo mushroom," wakaamba Inziba, mbuli kuti wakabuzya mujwi pati; mpawo muchindi chimwi chakabula kubonwa.

Alice wakakkala kalangilila mushroom kayeeya loko kwaminiti yomwe, kasola kuziba kuti nkuli mbazu zyobile zyawo; mpawo mbuli mbocakali chizungulukide, wakajana kuti mubuzyo ooyu wakali muyumu loko. Nokuba boobo, kumamanino wakatandabika maanza aakwe kuzinguluka kusikila mpaakali kukonzya kusika, mpawo wakatyola kabeela kaniini ajanza lyomwe lyomwe.

"Alimwi lino ino nguuli?" wakalyaambauzya, mpawo wakaluma asyoonto kujanza lyalulyo kuti asole kuchita: muchiindi chifwiifwi wakamvwa kuumwa loko munsi lyachilezu chakwe: kwakamuuma kuulu!

Wakayoowa loko akusanduka ooku kwakachitika, asi wakalimvwa kuti taakwe chiindi chakusweekelwa, nkaambo wakali kuyaabuceya; aboobo wakatalika kubeleka mpawo kuti alye chimwi chibeela. Chilezu chakwe chakasinikizyigwa loko kuulu kwakwe, zyakuti kwakanyina busena bwakujalula mulomo wakwe; pesi wakachita kumamanino, mpawo wakakonzya kumena kabeela kachakumusanza.

* * * * * *

* * * * * * *

"Koboola, mutwe wangu waangulukide kumamanino!" wakaamba Alice mujwi lyakubotelwa, eelyo lyakasanduka kuba kuyoowa mukaindi kaniini, aawo naakajana kuti magwezyo aakwe taakwe pe: nzyaakabona biyo, naakalanga ansi, nsingo mpati loko, eeyo yakali kulibonya mbuli chisiko kuzwa mulwizi lwamafwaafwi aasalala pyu akali munsi lyakwe.

"Ino zintu zyoonse eezyo zisalala zilakonzya kuba nzi?" wakaamba Alice. "Alimwi ino mabega aangu akasika kuli? Alimwi oh, maanza aangu macete, ino mbuti mbonditakonzyi kukubona?" Wakali kuzizungaanya aawo naakali kwaambuula, pesi taakwe chakatobela, kunze lyakuzungaana asyoonto akati kamatu aasalala pyu.

Mbuli mbukwakali kulibonya kuti taakwe nzila yakunyamuna maanza aakwe kumutwe wakwe, wakasola kubikka mutwe wakwe ansi kuli ngawo, mpawo wakakkomana kujana kuti nsingo yakwe yakali kukonzya kukotama kufumbwa nkoya, mbuli nzoka. Wakazwidilila biyo kuchigonka ansi kuba zigzag mbotu, mpawo wakali kuyanda kunjila mukati kamatu, ngaakajana kuti taakwe pe pele mitwe yazisamu nzyaakali kweendeenda, aawo kulila kwakapa kuti apiluke musule chakufwambaana: inziba mpati yakaulukila kubusyu bwakwe, mpo yakali kumuuma changuzu amababa aayo.

"Nzoka!" wakapozomoka Nziba.

" Nsili nzoka pe!" wakaamba Alice kachinyemede. "Kondileka!"

"Nzoka, ndaamba alimwi!" wakainduluka Inziba, pesi mujwi lyaansi, mpawo wakayungizya akulila, "Ndasola nzila zyoonse, pesi taakwe chintu chikonzya kubagwasya!"

"Nsikwe muzeezo pe wazyintu nzyokwaamba," wakaamba Alice.

"Ndasola miyanda yazisamu, ndakasola mabbanki, mpawo ndakasola ziluli," Inziba yakayinkila kunembo, kakwiina kumubikkila maano; "pele eezyo nzoka! Taakwe chakuzikkomanisya pe!"

Alice wakagambwa loko, asi wakayeeya kuti taakwe mpindu yakwaamba chimwi chintu kusikila Inziba yamana kwaamba.

"Mbuli kuti tiikwakali penzi pe kuzyala mazai," wakaamba Inziba; "pele ndeelede kucenjela nzoka masiku asyikati! Nsikwe koona nikuba kaniini mumviki zyotatwe eezyi!"

"Ndilausa loko kuti wakanyemya," wakaamba Alice, walo wakatalika kubona ncocaamba.

"Mbuli mbundakabweza musamu mulamfu kwiinda yoonse," wakayinkila kunembo Inziba, akusumpula jwi lyayo kuya kulila, "mpawo mbubonya mbondakali kuyeeya kuti inga ndaangununwa kuli mbabo kumamanino, beelede kuboola kabazungaana kuzwa mujulu! Ugh, Nzoka!"

"Pesi nsili nzoka, ndakwaambila!" wakaamba Alice. "Ndili—Ndili—"

"Mboobu! Ino nduwe?" wakaamba Nziba. "Ndabona kuti uli mukusola kubamba chimwi chintu!"

"Ndi—Ndili musimbi muniini," wakaamba Alice, kachidooneka, aawo naakali kuyeeya mweelwe wakucinca nkwaakainda muli ncico buzuba oobo.

"Makani aalangilwa ncobeni!" wakaamba Nziba mujwi lyakusampaula kupati. "Ndabona basimbi baniini biingi muchiindi changu, pesi kunyina naba omwe ulaa nsingo mbuli eyi! Peepe, peepe! Uli nzoka; mpawo kunyina mpindu yakukazya. Ndiyeya kuti uyoondibuzya kuti kunyina nowakabide iji!"

" Ndakalabila makoko, masimpe," wakaamba Alice, wakali mwana uusyomeka; "pele basimbi baniini balalya makoko mbuli nzoka mbozichita, ulizi."

"Tandisyomi pe," wakaamba Inziba; "pele kuti naa balachita, nkamboonzi ncobali mbuli nzoka, mbombubo mbondikonzya kwaamba."

Ooyu wakali muzeezo mupya kuli Alice, cakuti wakaumuna kwaminiti yomwe naa obile, calo cakapa Nziba coolwe cakuyungizya kuti, “Uyandaula mazyi, ndilizyi kabotu ; alimwi ino ncinzi ncondijisi kuti uli musimbi muniini naa nzoka?”

"Chilayandika loko kulindime ," wakaamba Alice chakufwambaana; "pele tandiyandi mazai, mbuli mbukonzya kuchitika; mpo kuti ndakali kuyanda, teensi ndeelede kuyanda aako : tandiyandi pe."

"Mboobu, kozwa, mpawo!" wakaamba Inziba mujwi lyakunyema, mpawo yakakkala ansi alimwi muchizumbo chayo. Alice wakafwugama akati kamisamu mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya, nkaambo nsingo yakwe yakali kuzumanana kunjila akati kamitabi, mpawo chiindi achiindi wakali kwiima akuyivwungulula. Nikwakainda kaindi kasyoonto wakayeeya kuti wakachili jisi zibeela zya mushroom mumaanza aakwe, mpawo wakatalika kubeleka oobotu, wakasaanguna kuluma chimwi mpawo chimwi, mpawo wakakomena chimwi chiindi mpawo chimwi chiindi kafwiifwi, kusikila naakazwidilila kulitola ansi kusikila mpaakali kuchita lyoonse.

Kwakainda chiindi chilamfu kuzwa naakaba achiimo chili afwaafwi asayizi yeelede, zyakuti chakalimvwisya chakankamanisya kumatalikilo; pesi wakazyibila mumaminiti mache, mpawo wakatalika kwaambuula alikke, mbuli lyoonse. "Koboola, ndamana chisela cha makanze aangu lino! Kuchincha ooku koonse nkukankamanisya! Nsikwe simpe kuti ndiyooba buti, kuzwa kuminiti yomwe kuya kuliimbi! Nokuba boobo, ndajokela kubupati bwangu bweelede: chintu chitobela nchakuti, ndinjile mumuunda mubotu ooyo— mbuti mbochikonzya kuchitwa, ndilabuzya?" Naakaamba boobo, wakasika abusena butakwe chintu, kajisi kaanda kaniini kalampa mamita one kuya mujulu. "Kufumbwa uukkala awo," wakayeeya Alice, "kunyina notukonzya kubajana bupati oobu : nkamboonzi, ndeelede kubayoosya!" Mpawo wakatalika kuluma kujanza lyalulyo alimwi, mpawo tanaakayanda kusika afwaafwi aŋanda kusikila wakasika kumasentimita aali cisambomwe kuya mujulu.
