CAANDANO IV.
Kalundu katuma mali masyoonto

Lyakali Nkala Iituba, yakali kweenda asyoonto-syoonto kuya musule alimwi, mpawo yakali kulanga mbuli kuti yakasweekelwa chimwi chintu; mpawo wakamvwa kakulivwiya mumoyo wakwe "Mwami mukaintu! Mwami mukaintu! Oh maulu aangu mabotu! Oh boya bwangu amalezu! Uyoondipa kuti ndijaigwe, mbuli masimpe mbuli nziba mbuli nziba! Ino ndakali kukonzya kubasowa kuli, ndilibuzya?" Alice wakayeeyela mukaindi kaniini kuti wakali kuyandaula chipekupeku antoomwe amagilavu aatuba, mpawo wakatalika kuziyandaula, pesi tiizyakali kubonwa pe—zyoonse zyakali kulibonya kuti zyakasanduka kuzwa naakadubbwa muzyiba, mpawo ŋanda mpati, aatebulu lyagilazi amulyango muniini, zyakamana.

Muchiindi chifwiifwi, Nkala wakabona Alice, naakali kuya kuvwima, mpawo wakamwiita mujwi lyabukali, "Nkaambo nzi, Mary Ann, ninzi nchochita anze aano? Chija kung'anda aawa, undiletele magilovu afeni! Kofwambaana, lino!" Mpawo Alice wakayoowa loko zyakuti wakachijaana mpawo mpawo kuya kulubazu ndwaakatondezya, katasoleki kupandulula kulubizya nkwaakachita.

"Wakandibweza kuti ndibe mubelesi wakwe," wakalyaambila lwakwe mwini naakali kuchijaana. "Uyoogambwa biyeni aawo nayooziba kuti ndime ni! Pesi inga ndamutola chipekupeku chakwe amagilovu—nkokuti, kuti ndajana." Naakaamba boobo, wakasika aŋanda nsyoonto iisalala, kumulyango mwakali chipekupeku chamukuba chimweka-mweka chakalembedwe zina lyakuti "W. RABBIT." Wakanjila kakwiina kukonkomona, mpawo wakafwambaana kuya mujulu, kayoowede kuti ambweni inga waswaangana a Mary Ann wini, mpawo wagwisigwa mung'anda katanajana chipekupeku amagilovu.

"Chilibonyaanga nchintu chakankamanisya," Alice wakalyaambauzya, "kutumina milumbe kuli kalundu! Ndiyeya kuti Dinah uyoonditumina milumbe iitobela!" Mpawo wakatalika kuyeeya chintu chikonzya kuchitika: "'Musimbi Alice! Koboola okuno, mpawo ubambile kweenda kwako!' 'Kuboola muchindi chinini, musilisi! Pesi ndeelede kubona kuti mbeba tiizwi pe.' Pesi tandiyeeyi pe,” Alice wakayinkila kunembo, “kuti inga balekela Dinah kuti aleke mung'anda kuti yakatalika kulailila bantu mbuli boobo!”

Aachiindi eechi wakajana nzila yakunjila mukaanda kaniini kasalala kajisi tebulu muwindo, mpawo atala lyalyo (mbuli mbwaakali kulangila) kwakali chipekupeku amagilovu aabana aatuba obile naa otatwe: wakabweza chipekupeku amagilovu obile, mpawo wakali kuyanda kuzwa mukaanda, aawo meso aakwe naakabona kabbodela kaniini kakali afwaafwi amagilazi. Kwakanyina chilembedwe aachiindi eechi chakalembedwe kuti "KONDINYWA," pesi nikubaboobo wakachivwungulula mpawo wakachibikka kumilomo yakwe. "Ndilizi kuti kuli chintu chikondelezya chiyoochitika," wakalyaambauzya, "kufwumbwa ndalya naa kunywa kufumbwa chintu; aboobo ndilabona kuti bbodela eeli lichita biyeni. Ndilangila kuti liyoondipa kuti ndikome alimwi, nkaambo ncobeni ndakatede kuba chintu chiniini loko!"

Chakachitika ncobeni, mpawo chakafwambaana kwiinda mbwaakali kuyeeyela: katananywa chisela chabbodela, wakajana mutwe wakwe kausinkilila kuciluli, mpawo wakeelede kukotamika kuchitila kuti nsingo yakwe itatyoki. Wakafwambaana kubikka bbodela ansi, kalyaambauzya kuti "Eezyo zyazula—ndisyoma kuti tandikakomeni limbi—Mbubonya mbocibede, tandikonzyi kuzwa kumulyango—ndilombozya kuti nindatakali kunywa kapati!"

Maawe! kwakachedwa loko kulombozya eecho! Wakazumanana kukomena, akukomena, mpawo muchiindi chifwiifwi wakeelede kufugama ansi: muminiti imwi kwakanyina busena bwakuchitila eezi, mpawo wakasola kulala ansi achikobela chomwe kumulyango, mpawo janza limbi lyakazingulukide mutwe wakwe. Nikubaboobo wakazumanana kukomena, mpawo, mbuli nzila yamamanino, wakabikka janza lyomwe anze aawindo, akuulu komwe kuya mujulu, mpawo wakalyaambauzya kuti "Lino tandikonzyi kuchita limbi, kufumbwa chikonzya kuchitika. Ino ndiyooba buti?"

Mucoolwe kuli Alice, bbodela lyamasalamuzi lyakaba anguzu zyoonse, mpawo tanaakakomena pe: pesi lyakali kukatazya, mpawo, mbuli mbukwakalibonya kuti taakwe nzila yakuti azwe mukaanda alimwi, tazigambyi pe kuti wakalimvwa kutakkomana.

"Chakali chintu chibotu loko kung'anda," wakayeeya Alice mucete, "awo umwi naatakali kukomena akucheya, akulaililwa a mbeba a nziba. Ndakali kulombozya kuti nindatakali kunjila mumpako eeyo—mpawo—mpawo—nchintu chikankamanisya, ulizi, buumi buliboobu! Ndilabuzya kuti ninzi chakachitika kulindime aawo nindakali kubala, mpuwo! ndakayeeya kuti chintu chiliboobo teensi chakachitika pe, mpawo lino ndili akati-kati kachimwi! Kweelede kuti kuli bbuku lyakalembwa aatala andime, eelyo lyeelede kuba! Mpawo nindakomena, ndiyoolemba limwi—pele ndakomena lino,” wakayungizya mujwi lyabuumba; "kunyina busena bwakukomena aano pe."

"Pesi aawo," wakayeeya Alice, "sena tandikonzyi kukomena kwiinda mbondibede lino? Eezyo ziyooba luumbulizyo, nzila yomwe—kunyina kuba mukaintu mucembele—pele mpawo—lyoonse kuba aziiyo zyakwiiya! Oh, tandikonzyi kuziyanda eezyo! "

"Oo, yebo Alice mufubafuba!" wakasandula lwakwe mwini. "Mbuti mbokonzya kwiiya ziiyo mukati aawa? Nkaambo nzi, kunyina busena bwako , alimwi kunyina busena bwamabbuku aaziiyo!"

Mpawo wakazumanana, wakasaanguna kubweza lubazu lumwi mpawo kulubazu lumwi, mpawo wakachita mubandi woonse; pesi musule lyamaminiti masyoonto wakamvwa jwi anze, mpawo wakaima kuti aswiilile.

"Mary Ann! Mary Ann!" lyakaamba jwi. "Ndiletele magilovu aangu aawa!" Mpawo kwakasika kuzungaana kwamaulu aamatantilo. Alice wakalizi kuti wakali Nkala wakalikuboola kuzoomuyandaula, mpawo wakakankama kusikila wakazungaanya ng'anda, wakaluba kuti wakali mupati ziindi zili zyuulu kwiinda Nswi, mpo tanaakajisi kaambo kakumuyoowa.

Mpawo-mpawo Kabwa wakaboola kumulyango, mpawo wasola kuujalula; pesi, aawo mulyango nowakajaluka mukati, mpawo nkomo ya Alice niyakasinikizyigwa loko, kusola ooku kwakaalilwa. Alice wakamvwa kalyaamba kuti "Mpoonya ndiyoozyunguluka mpawo ndinjile aawindo."

" kuti tokonzyi pe!" wakayeeya Alice, mpawo, nikwakamana kulindila kusikila naakalimvwa kuti wakamvwa Nkala munsi aawindo, wakatandabika janza lyakwe, mpawo wakanyampula mujulu. Taakwe naakajata chintu, pesi wakamvwa koongolola mbichani akuwida, akudonkola kwagilazi lyatyoka, ooko nkwaakayeeya kuti chakakonzyeka kuti chakawida mufulemu lya cucumber, naakuti chimwi chintu chili mbuli boobo.

Mpawo kwakasika jwi lyabukali—lya Kalundu—“Pat! Pat! Uli kuli?” Mpawo jwi ndyaatakali kumvwa kaindi, "Masimpe mpawo ndili aano! Ndilikuyandaula maapele, nduwe!"

"Kuvwukkula ma apulo, masimpe!" wakaamba Kabwa kachinyemede. "Aawa! Koboola undigwasye kuzwa muli ceeci! " (Kumvwika kwamagilazi aambi aatyokede.)

"Lino kondibuzya, Pat, ninzi eecho chili awindo?"

"Masimpe, nkuboko, bulemu bwako!" (Wakali kwaamba kuti "arrum.")

"Ijanza, yebo nziba! Nguni wakabona lipati boobu? Nkaambo nzi, lizuzya windo lyoonse!"

"Masimpe, zilachita, bulemu bwako: pesi nkuboko kwazyoonse eezyo."

"Mboobu, taakwe makani aawo, kufumbwa mbokubede: koya ukabweze!"

Kwakaba kuumuna kwachiindi chilamfu kuzwa waawo, mpawo Alice wakali kumvwa biyo kuvwiya-vwiya chiindi achiindi; mbuli kuti, "Masimpe, tandiyandi pe, bulemu bwako, pe, pe!" "Kochita mbuli mbondakwaambila, yebo muyoofu!" mpawo kumamanino wakatandabika janza lyakwe alimwi, mpawo wakabweza lubo mujulu. Aachiindi eechi kwakaba kulila kusyoonto kobile , antoomwe amajwi manji aamagilazi aatyokede. "Mweelwe nzi wamafulemu aa cucumber ayelede kuba!" wakayeeya Alice. "Ndilabuzya kuti ino bayoochita biyeni! Kujatikizya kundigwisya aawindo, ndilombozya kuti nibakakonzya ! Ndili masimpe kuti tandiyandi kukkala aano limbi pe!"

Wakalindila kwachiindi chilamfu katanamvwa chintu chilichose: kumamanino kwakasika kuvwuuma kwamavwili aankalaki, mpawo kwakamvwika majwi miingi yoonse yakali kwaambuula antoomwe: wakamvwa majwi aaya: “Ino lili limbi ntaamu?—Nkaambo nzi, tii Ndakeelede kuleta buyo lyomwe; Bill ulijisi limbi—Bill! kobweza aawa, kolibika! 'em antoomwe kusaanguna—tabakonzyi kusika aakati-akati—Oo! bayoochita obotu; (kuvwuuma kupati)—“Lino, ino nguni wakachita eecho?—Wakali Bill, ndilayeeya—Nguni uuyakuselemuka mumulyango?—Peepe, nsyekonzyi pe! Yebo ulachita!— Eezyo tandikonzyi pe, aboobo!—Bill weelede kuseluka—Aawa, Bill! simalelo waamba kuti weelede kuya ansi mumulyango wamulilo!”

"Oo! aboobo Bill weelede kuseluka mumulyango wamulilo, tee mbubo?" wakaamba Alice lwakwe mwini. "Nsoni, balibonya kuti babikka zyoonse kuli Bill! Ime inga tii ndaba mubusena bwa Bill kwachiindi chilamfu: busena obu bwakubikkila mulilo mbufwiifwi, masimpe; pesi ndiyeeya kuti inga ndakonzya kuuma mbichani!"

Wakakwelela kuulu kwakwe kuya ansi aamulyango mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya, mpawo wakalindila kusikila naakamvwa munyama muniini (tanaakali kukonzya kweezyeezya kuti wakali musyobo nzi) kavwundauka akuzungaana mumulyango wamulilo afwaafwi anguwe: mpawo, kaamba kuti "Ooyu ngu Bill," wakauma ntambo yomwe, mpawo wakalindila kuti abone kuti ninzi chiyoochitika.

Chintu nchakasaanguna kumvwa kwakali kwiimba kwa "There goes Bill!" mpawo jwi lya Kalundu lyakazwa—“Komujata, nduwe abwaanda!” mpawo kwakaba kuumuna, mpawo kwakaba kunyongana kwamajwi—“Konyamuna mutwe wakwe—Brandy lino—Utamufwundili—Kwakali buti, mucembele? Ninzi chakachitika kuli nduwe? Kotwaambile zyoonse!”

Kumamanino kwakasika jwi litakwe nguzu, lilalila, ("Ooyo ngu Bill," wakayeeya Alice,) "Mboobu, nsezi pe—Taakwe limbi, ndalumba; Ndili kabotu lino—pele ndilipengede loko kuti ndikwaambile—ncondizyi njakuti, chimwi chintu chaboola kulindime mbuli Jack-in-the-box, mpawo ndatanta mbuli julu!"

"Aboobo wakachita, mucembele!" bakaamba bamwi.

"Tweelede kutenta ŋanda yoonse!" lyakaamba jwi lya Kalundu; mpawo Alice wakapozomoka mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya, "kuti wachita oobo, ndiyoobikka Dina kuli nduwe!"

Kwakaba kuumuna kwakaindi kaniini, mpawo Alice wakayeeya mukati kakwe, "Ndilabuzya kuti ino bayoochita biyeni! Kuti nibakajisi maanu, nibakagwisya ŋanda." Nikwakainda miniti lyomwe naa obile, bakatalika kweendeenda alimwi, mpawo Alice wakamvwa Nkala kaamba kuti, "Ibbokesi lilakonzya kuchita obotu, kutalika."

"Ibbaro lizwide nzi? " wakayeeya Alice; pesi tanaakatola chiindi chilamfu chakudooneka, nkaambo muchiindi chifwiifwi kwakasika mvwula yamabwe masyoonto awindo, mpawo amwi akamuuma kubusyu. "Ndilachimanizya eechi," wakalyaambauzya, mpawo wakoongolola, "Kuli kabotu kuti utachiti limbi eecho!" eecho chakapa kuti kube kuumuna kumbi.

Alice wakabona akugambwa kuti mabwe masyoonto akali kusanduka kuba makkekke masyoonto aawo naakali kulala ansi, mpawo muzeezo mubotu wakaboola mumutwe wakwe. "Ikuti ndalya limwi lyamakkekke aaya," wakayeeya, "masimpe lilakonzya kuchincha bupati bwangu; mpawo mbuli mbolitakonzyi kundipa kuti ndikomezye, lilakonzya kundipa kuba muniini, ndiyeya."

Mpawo wakamena kkekke limwi, mpawo wakakkomana kujana kuti wakatalika kucheya. Mbwaakaba muniini loko kuti akonzye kunjila kumulyango, wakazuzaana kuzwa mung'anda, mpawo wakajana nkamu yabanyama abayuni bakali kulindila aanze. Kabwa kaniini kacete, Bill, kakali akati, kakajatwa angulube zyobile, eezyo zyakali kukupa chimwi chintu kuzwa mubbodela. Boonse bakafwambaana kuli Alice aawo naakaboneka; pesi wakachija changuzu mbuli mbwaakali kukonzya, mpawo wakajana kuti ulikwabilidwe muchisamu chilemu.

"Chintu chakusanguna ncheyelede kuchita," wakaamba Alice lwakwe mwini, naakali kweendeenda mumalundu, "nkukomena kusikila kubupati bwangu alimwi; mpawo chintu cabili nkujana nzila yakunjila mumuunda ooyo mubotu. Ndiyeeya kuti eeyo njenzila mbotu."

Chakali kulimvwisya mbuli kuti nchintu chibotu loko, kakunyina kudooneka, mpawo chakabambwa kabotu loko; buyumuyumu bulikke bwakali bwakuti tanaakajisi muzeezo musyoonto wakuti inga wachita biyeni; mpawo aawo naakali kulanga-langa akati kazisamu, kachikumba kaniini kali atala amutwe wakwe kakapa kuti alange mujulu chakufwambaana.

Mubwa mupati wakali kumulanga ansi ameso mapati aazungulukide, mpawo wakatandabika kuulu komwe, kusola kumuguma. "Kachintu kaniini!" wakaamba Alice, mujwi lyakukombelezya, mpawo wakasola loko kuti achite; pesi wakali kuyoowa loko chiindi choonse naakali kuyeeya kuti ambweni wakali anzala, mpawo wakali kukonzya kumulya nikuba kuti wakali kumukombelezya.

Kakwiina kuzyiba nzyaakachita, wakabweza kachisamu kaniini, mpawo wakakapa kabwa; mpawo kabwa kakasotokela mujulu kuzwa kumaulu aakwe woonse achiindi chomwe, akulila kwakukondwa, mpawo wakazuzaana kumusako, mpawo wakapa kuti asyome kuti ulikataazikene; mpawo Alice wakatija musule lyamusamu mupati, kuti atazungaani; mpawo aawo naakaboneka kulubazu lumwi, kabwa kakazuzaana alimwi kumusako, mpawo wakawida ansi mukufwambaana kuti ajate; mpawo Alice, kayeeya kuti chakalinga mbuli kusobana abbiza lyamootokala, mpawo kalangila kuti chiindi choonse chiyoonyampulwa amawulu, wakazuzaana alimwi; mpawo kabwa katalika kulwana nkoli, kachijaana kuya kunembo mbichani chiindi choonse mpawo kachijaana kuya musule, mpawo kavwuuma muchiindi choonse, kusikila kumamanino kakakkala kule, kakuyoya, lulimi lwakwe kaluzwa mumulomo, ameso aakwe mapati kaajalidwe.

Eezi zyakaboneka kuli Alice mbuli mweenya mubotu wakumupa kuti atije; aboobo wakanyamuka mpawo, mpawo wakachijaana kusikila wakakatede akubula muuya, mpawo kusikila kuvwuuma kwamubwa kwakamvwika mbichaani kulaale.

"Alimwi wakali mubwa muniini uuyandwa loko!" wakaamba Alice, mpawo wakasendamika achikopo kuti alyookezye, mpawo wakalifwusa aamasamu amwi: "Ndakeelede kuyanda kuyiisya nzila zyakuchita oobo, ikuti—ikuti nindali buyo mupati wakuzichita! Oh noyandwa! Ndakali afwaafwi kuluba kuti ndeelede kukomena alimwi! Ndibone—ino inga ndakonzya kweendelezyegwa biyeni? Ndakeelede kulya naakuti kulya chimwi chintu; mubuzyo mupati ngwakuti, ninzi?”

Mubuzyo mupati wakali wakuti, ninzi? Alice wakalanga koonse koonse kumaluba amaluba, pesi tanaakabona chintu chili mbuli chintu chili kabotu kulya naa kunywa muchiimo eecho. Kwakali bowa bupati bwakali kukomena afwaafwi anguwe, bwakali bulamfwu bweelene anguwe; mpawo naakalanga munsi lyawo, kumabazu oonse obile, akusule lyawo, wakayeeya kuti ulakonzya kulanga mpawo abone chakali atala lyawo.

Wakalitandabika aamaulu, mpawo wakalanga kunkomwe ya mushroom, mpawo meso aakwe akaswaangana aayo aa kazunda kapati ka bbululu, kakakkede atala amaanza aakwe kaajatikizyidwe, kafweba hookah ndamfu, katakwe naakali kumubikkila maanu pe.
