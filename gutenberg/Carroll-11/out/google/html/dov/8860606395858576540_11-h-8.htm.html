CAANDANO VIII.
Busena bwaMwami Mukaintu

Musamu mupati wa rose wakaliimvwi afwaafwi amulyango wamuunda: ma rose aakali kukomena aali nguwo akali aatuba, asi kwakali basikulima botatwe, kabajisi bubi bwakupenta mubala uusalala pyu. Alice wakayeeya kuti eechi nchintu chakankamanisya, mpawo wakaswena afwaafwi kuti abalange, mpawo mbwaakasika kuli mbabo wakamvwa umwi wabo kaamba kuti, "Langa lino, Bosanwe! Utaunki kundisansaila penti boobo!"

"Teendakakonzya pe," wakaamba Musanu, mujwi lyakunyema; "Zili musanu ababili zyakazuzaanya nkomo yangu."

Mpawo Seven wakalanga mujulu akwaamba kuti, "Mbombubo, Five! Lyoonse kobikka mulandu kuli bamwi!"

" Kuli kabotu kuti utaambuuli pe!" wakaamba Musanu. "Ndakamvwa Mwami Mukaintu kaamba kuti jilo buyo wakeelede kugonkwa mutwe!"

"Ninzi?" wakaamba ooyo wakasaanguna kwaambuula.

"Eezyo tazili zyako pe, Bobilo!" wakaamba Chiloba.

"Iyi, nchintu chakwe!" wakaamba Musanu, "mpawo ndiyoomwaambila—chakali chakuleta miyanda ya tulip muciindi ca ma onyeni."

Seven wakasowa bbulasho lyakwe ansi, mpawo wakatalika biyo "Mboobu, akati kazintu zyoonse zitaluleme—" aawo liso lyakwe nilyakawida kuli Alice, aawo naakaliimvwi kabalangilila, mpawo wakalilanga mpawo: bamwi bakalanga koonse koonse abalo, mpawo boonse bakakotamina ansi.

"Sena inga wandibuzya," wakaamba Alice, kachijisi nsoni, "nkaambo nzi nchopenta maluba aayo?"

Musanu aChiloba tiibakaamba pe, pesi bakalanga kulibili. Bobile bakatalika mujwi lyaansi, "Nkaambo nzi kasimpe nkakuti, yebo wabona, Musimbi, ooyu aawa wakeelede kuba musamu wa rose uusalala pyu , mpawo twakabikka uutuba mukulubizya; mpawo kuti Mwami Mukaintu wakali kuyanda kuziba, toonse tweelede kugonkwa mitwe yesu, nywebo mulizi. Mpawo mwabona, Mukaintu, tulikuchita zyoonse nzyotukonzya, katanasika"— Aawo, Mukaintu, wakali kulibilika. kuzabuka muunda, wakaita "Mwami Mukaintu! Mwami Mukaintu!" mpawo basikulima botatwe bakafwugama ansi. Kwakamvwika muzuzumo wamaulu manji, mpawo Alice wakalanga koonse koonse, kayandisisya kubona Mwami Mukaintu.

Kusaanguna kwakasika basilumamba bali kkumi kabanyamwide nkoli; aaba boonse bakali kubumbwa mbuli basikulima botatwe, balamfu alimwi bapapalete, kabajisi maanza amaulu kumakkona: mpawo kwakali babelesi bali kkumi; aaba bakali kusama madaimona, mpawo bakali kweenda bobilo bobilo, mbuli basilumamba mbobakali kuchita. Kuzwa waawo kwakasika bana bami; kwakali kkumi akati kabo, mpawo bana baniini bakaboola kabazuzaana kabajatana mumaanza, kabali bobilo: boonse bakasamide myoyo. Kuzwa waawo kwakasika beenzu, bunji bwabo bakali bami abanakazi, mpawo akati kabo Alice wakazyiba Nkala Iisalala: yakali kwaambuula chakufwambaana, kumweta-mweta kulizyoonse zyakali kwaambwa, mpawo yakainda katanamubona. Mpawo kwakatobela Knave of Hearts, kanyamwide musini waMwami achisani cha velveti chisalala pyu; mpawo, kumamanino aalweendo oolu lupati, kwakasika MWAMI AMUKAINTU WA MYOYO.

Alice wakali kudooneka kuti naa teelede kulala ansi mbuli basikulima botatwe, pesi tanaakali kukonzya kuyeeya kuti wakamvwa mulawu uuliboobu mukweendeenda; "alimwi kunze lyaboobo, ino ncinzi cikonzya kugwasya kweendeenda," wakayeeya, "ikuti bantu boonse balalala ansi, kuchitila kuti bataboni?" Mpawo wakaima aawo mpaakali, mpawo walindila.

Eelyo nkamu yabantu niyakasika kuli Alice, boonse bakaima akumulanga, mpawo Mwami Mukaintu wakati "Ino nguni ooyu?" Wakaamba kuli Knave of Hearts, walo wakakotama akumweta-mweta mukwiingula.

"Chituta!" wakaamba Mwami Mukaintu, kuzungaanya mutwe wakwe kakunyina moyo mulamfu; mpawo, wakacengulukila kuli Alice, wakayinkila kunembo, "Ino zina lyako nguni, mwana?"

"Izina lyangu ndime Alice, aboobo ndalomba Mwami wako," wakaamba Alice kabotu-kabotu; pesi wakayungizya, kuli nguwe mwini, "Nkaambo nzi, nzibeela buyo zyamakkaadi, mubwini. Tandeelede kubayoowa pe!"

"Alimwi aaba mbaani? " wakaamba Mwami Mukaintu, kutondeka kuli basikulima botatwe bakali koona kuzinguluka musamu wa rose; nkaambo, mwabona, mbuli mbobakali koona abusyu bwabo, mpawo chikozyano chamisana yabo chakali mbubonya mbuli bamwi boonse, tanaakali kukonzya kuzyiba naa bakali basikulima, naa basilumamba, naa babelesi, naa bana bakwe botatwe.

" Ndilaziba biyeni?" wakaamba Alice, wakagambwa abusicamba bwakwe. "Tachili chintu changu pe."

Mwami mukaintu wakasanduka kuba mubala uusalala akaambo kabukali, mpawo, naakamana kumulanga mbuli munyama, wakapozomoka kuti "Kozwa amutwe wakwe! Kozwa—"

"Buyo!" wakaamba Alice, mujwi pati loko, mpawo Mwami Mukaintu wakaumuna.

Mwami wakabikka janza lyakwe kuboko kwakwe, mpawo wakati "Koyeeya, muyandwa wangu: mwana biyo!"

Mwami mukaintu wakazwa kuli nguwe kachinyemede, mpawo wakati kuli Knave "Bapilusye!"

Silunya wakachita oobo, chakuchenjela, akuulu komwe.

"Nyampuka!" wakaamba Mwami Mukaintu, mujwi pati, mpawo balimi botatwe bakanyampuka, mpawo bakatalika kukotamina Mwami, Mwami Mukaintu, bana bamwami, abamwi boonse.

"Leka eecho!" wakapozomoka Mwami Mukaintu. "Ulandipa kuzungaana." Mpawo, wakacengulukila kumusamu wa rose, wakayinkila kunembo, "Ino walikuchitaanzi okuno?"

"Achikkomanisye Mwami wako," wakaamba Bobile, mujwi lyakulibombya, wakafwugama ansi aawo naakali kwaambuula, "twakali kusola—"

" Ndabona !" wakaamba Mwami Mukaintu, walo wakali kulanga maluba. "Amugwisye mitwe yabo!" mpawo nkamu yabantu yakainka kunembo, botatwe akati kabasikalumamba bakasyaala kuti bajaye basikulima batakwe coolwe, aabo bakachijila kuli Alice kuti abakwabilile.

"Tokonzyi kugonkwa mutwe pe!" wakaamba Alice, mpawo wakazibikka mumupika mupati wamaluba wakaliimvwi afwaafwi. Basikalumamba botatwe bakazyunguluka kwaminiti yomwe naa zyobile, kabayandaula, mpawo bakaunka kabatobela bamwi.

"Sena mitwe yabo ilizwide?" wakapozomoka Mwami Mukaintu.

"Mitwe yabo yamana, kuti kakuli kabotu Mwami wako!" basilumamba bakakoongolola mukwiingula.

"Mbombubo!" wakapozomoka Mwami Mukaintu. "Sena ulakonzya kusana croquet?"

Basikalumamba bakaumuna, mpawo bakalanga kuli Alice, nkaambo mubuzyo wakali kulibonya kuti wakali kulinguwe.

"Iyii!" wakapozomoka Alice.

"Koboola, mpawo!" wakavwuuma Mwami Mukaintu, mpawo Alice wakanjila mumulongo, kayeeya loko kuti ninzi chiyoochitika.

"Mbuzuba bubotu loko!" wakaamba jwi lyakuyoowa kumbali lyakwe. Wakali kweenda afwaafwi aNkala Iituba, walo wakali kumulanga kubusyu kakuyoowa.

"Mbubo," wakaamba Alice: "—Ino Duchess uli kuli?"

"Koumuna! Koumuna!" wakaamba Kalundu mujwi lyaansi, lyakufwambaana. Wakalanga cakuyoowa atala aamweengwe wakwe naakali kwaambuula, mpawo wakanyampuka, wakabikka mulomo wakwe afwaafwi akutwi kwakwe, mpawo wakavwiyavwiya kuti "Uli munsi lyachisubulo chakujaigwa."

"Ninzi?" wakaamba Alice.

"Sena wakati 'Nchibi nzi!'?" Kabwa wakabuzya.

"Peepe, teensi ndakachita," wakaamba Alice: "Nsiboni kuti nchintu chibi pe. Ndakaamba kuti 'Nkaambonzi?'"

"Wakauma matwi aaMwami Mukaintu—" Kabwa wakatalika. Alice wakapozomoka asyoonto akaambo kakuseka. "Oo, koumuna!" Kabwa wakavwiyavwiya mujwi lyakuyoowa. "Mwami mukaintu ulakumvwa! Wabona, wakasika musule, mpawo Mwami mukaintu wakati—"

"Koya kumasena aako!" wakapozomoka Mwami Mukaintu mujwi lyamuzuzumo, mpawo bantu bakatalika kuzuzaana mumabazu oonse, kabavwundauka kuli umwi amweenzinyina; nokuba boobo, bakakkala ansi muminiti yomwe naa zyobile, mpawo cisobano chakatalika. Alice wakali kuyeeya kuti tanaakabwene busena bwakusobanina buli boobu mubuumi bwakwe; zyoonse zyakali zilundu amilonga; mabbola akali ma hedgehogs apona, mallets akali ma flamingos apona, mpawo basilumamba bakeelede kulisumpula akuima amaanza amaulu, kuti bapange zikobela.

Buyumu-yumu bupati Alice mbwaakajana kumatalikilo bwakali bwakweendelezya flamingo yakwe: wakazwidilila kubamba mubili wayo, kabotu-kabotu, munsi lya janza lyakwe, amawulu aakwe kaali ansi, pesi kanjikanji, mbuli mbwaakalulamika nsingo yakwe kabotu, mpawo wakali kuyanda kuuma nziba amutwe, yakali kulizungaana akulanga mujulu, kalibonya kukankamana kuti tanaakali kukonzya kulijata kuseka: mpawo naakabikka mutwe wakwe ansi, mpawo wakali kuyanda kutalika alimwi, chakanyemya loko kujana kuti nziba yakalivwungulula, mpo yakalikuyanda kweenda: kunze lyazyoonse eezyi, kwakali lwizi naa mugoti munzila kufumbwa nkwaakali kuyanda kutumina nziba, mpawo, mbuli basilumamba mbobakali kunyamuka akuzwa zimwi zibeela zyaanyika, Alice wakafwambaana kusika kumamanino aakuti wakali musobano muyumu ncobeni.

Basikusobana boonse bakasobana achiindi chomwe kakunyina kulindila chiindi, kabazwangana chiindi choonse, akulwanina nziba; mpawo muchiindi chifwiifwi Mwami Mukaintu wakaba aluyandisisyo lupati, mpawo wakaunka kuzyunguluka, mpawo wakoongolola kuti "Kogwisya mutwe wakwe!" naakuti "Kogwisya mutwe wakwe!" mbuli chiindi chomwe muminiti.

Alice wakatalika kulimvwa kutaanguluka: masimpe, tanaakachili kukazyania aMwami Mukaintu, pesi wakalizi kuti inga chachitika kufumbwa chiindi, "mpawo," wakayeeya kuti, "ino ninzi chikonzya kuchitika kulindime? Bayandisya loko kugonka mitwe yabantu aano; chintu chigambya nkakuti, kuli naba omwe uusyeede uupona!"

Wakali kulangaula nzila yakutija, mpawo wakali kulibuzya kuti naa ulakonzya kutija kakunyina kubonwa, aawo naakabona chintu chilibonya mujulu: chakamugambya loko kumatalikilo, pesi, nikwakainda kaindi kaniini naa obile, wakabona kuti wakali kumweta-mweta, mpawo wakalyaambauzya kuti "Ngu Cheshire Cat: lino ndiyooba amuntu ngondikonzya kwaambuula aawe."

"Uli buti?" wakaamba Kkiti, mpawo nikwakaba mulomo uuzulide kuti akwaambe.

Alice wakalinda kusikila meso akaboneka, mpawo wakagunya mutwe. "Takugwasyi pe kwaambuula anzizyo," wakayeeya, "kusikila matwi aazyo asike, naakuti amwi." Muminiti imwi mutwe woonse wakaboneka, mpawo Alice wakabikka flamingo yakwe ansi, mpawo wakatalika kwaambuula aatala acisobano, kumvwa kukkomana kuti wakajisi muntu wakumuswiilila. Kkiti wakali kulibonya kuti wakali kuyeeya kuti kwakali kuzulide lino mumeso, mpo taakwe limbi lyakaboneka pe.

"Nsiyeeyi kuti balasobana kabotu pe," Alice wakatalika, mujwi lyakutongooka, "alimwi boonse balazwangana cakuti muntu takonzyi kumvwa kaambaula—alimwi tababoneki kuti balijisi milawo iili yoonse; at least, kuti kakuli, kunyina uubalanganya—alimwi tozyi mbocinyonganya zintu zyoonse zipona mubusena oobo; koya kweendeenda kumamanino aanyika—mpawo ndeelede kuti ndauma nziba yaMwami Mukaintu lino, pesi yakachija aawo niyakabona yangu kaiboola!”

"Ino uyanda buti Mwami Mukaintu?" wakaamba Kkiti mujwi lyaansi.

"Peepe pe," wakaamba Alice: "uli kabotu loko—" Mpawo wakabona kuti Mwami Mukaintu wakali afwaafwi musule lyakwe, kuswiilizya: mpawo wakayinkila kunembo, "—kulangilwa kuti ulakonzya kuzunda, kuti takugwasyi pe aawo namanizya cisobano."

Mwami mukaintu wakamwemwetela mpawo wakainda.

"Ino nguni ngowaambuula awe?" wakaamba Mwami, nkaakayinka kuli Alice, mpawo kalanga mutwe wa Kkiti akuyandisya kuziba kupati.

"Mweenzuma—Kkiti yaku Cheshire," wakaamba Alice: "kondizumizye kuti ndimuzyibye."

"Tandiyandi pe mboiboneka," wakaamba Mwami: "nokuba boobo, ilakonzya kumyonta janza lyangu kuti yayanda."

"Nsikonzyi pe," Kkiti wakaamba.

"Utabi bantu batakwe bulemu," wakaamba Mwami, "alimwi utandilangi boobo!" Wakasika musule lya Alice aawo naakali kwaambuula.

"Kkiti ilakonzya kulanga mwami," wakaamba Alice. "Ndakabala eecho mubbuku limwi, pesi nse yeeyede aawo."

"Mboobu, yeelede kugwisigwa," wakaamba Mwami cakusinizya, mpawo wakaita Mwami Mukaintu, ooyo wakali kwiinda aawo, "Muyandwa wangu! Ndalombozya kuti nowakagwisya kkayi eeli!"

Mwami mukaintu wakalijisi buyo nzila yomwe yakumanizya mapenzi oonse, mapati naa masyoonto. "Kogwisya mutwe wakwe!" wakaamba, katalangide pe.

"Ndiyoobweza sikujaya ndemwini," wakaamba Mwami chakuyandisisya, mpawo wakafwambaana kweenda.

Alice wakayeeya kuti inga wajokela musule, kuti abone mbuli cisobano mbocakali kweenda, mpawo wakamvwa jwi lyaMwami Mukaintu kulaale, kakoongolola chakuyandisisya. Wakalimvwide kale chisubulo chakwe kuli basololi botatwe kuti bajaigwe akaambo kakubula chiindi chabo, mpo tanaakali kukkomana ambozyakali kulibonya pe, nkaambo cisobano chakali muntenda zyakuti tanaakazyi kuti chakali chiindi chakwe naa pe. Mpawo wakainka kuyooyandaula nziba yakwe.

Inziba yakali kulwana ayimwi inyuni, eecho chakalibonya kuli Alice kuti ncechiindi chibotu chakweezyanisya umwi akati kabo: buyumuyumu bulikke bwakali bwakuti, nkumba yakwe yakayinka kulubazu lumwi lwamuunda, ooko Alice nkwaakali kukonzya kuyibona kasola munzila iitakwe lugwasyo kuti iuluke mumusamu.

Ku chiindi naakajata flamingo mpawo wakajokezya, kulwana kwakamana, mpawo zyoonse zyobile nziba tiizyakali kubonwa pe: "pele tazikwe makani pe," wakayeeya Alice, "mbuli zikobela zyoonse zyakamana kuzwa kulubazu oolu lwanyika." Mpawo wakasisa munsi lya janza lyakwe, kuchitila kuti litazwi alimwi, mpawo wakajokela kuti akabandike amweenzinyina.

Naakajokela ku Cheshire Cat, wakagambwa kujana nkamu mpati yabantu kabazyungulukide: kwakali kukazyania aakati kasikujaya, Mwami, aMwami Mukaintu, aabo boonse bakali kwaambuula achiindi chomwe, mpawo bamwi boonse bakali biyo biyo, mpawo bakali kulibonya kuti tabakwe luumuno pe.

Aawo Alice naakaboneka, wakakumbilwa boonse botatwe kuti amanizye mubuzyo, mpawo bakainduluka twaambo twabo kuli nguwe, nokubaboobo, mbuli mbobakali kwaambuula achiindi chomwe, wakajana buyumuyumu bwakumvwisisisya nzibakali kwaamba.

Kaambo kasikujaya kakali kakuti, tokonzyi kugela mutwe kunze kwakuti kakuli mubili wakuugela: kuti tanaakachili kuchita chintu chiliboobu chiindi, mpo tanaakali kuyootalika muchiindi chakwe chabuumi.

Kaambo ka Mwami kakali kakuti, kufumbwa chintu chili amutwe chilakonzya kugonkwa mutwe, akuti toyelede kwaambuula zintu zitakwe mulimo.

Kaambo ka Mwami Mukaintu kakali kakuti, kuti naa kwaba chintu chakachitwa muchiindi chifwiifwi ulakonzya kujaya bantu boonse, boonse. (Kwakali majwi aamamanino aakapa kuti nkamu yoonse iboneke mbuli kuti ilipengede akulibilika.)

Alice taakwe ncaakali kukonzya kuyeeya pe pele "Nca Duchess: inga wamubuzya aatala anzizyo."

"Uli muntolongo," Mwami Mukaintu wakaambila sikujaya: "komuleta okuno." Mpawo sikujaya wakazwa mbuli musemo.

Mutwe wa Kkiti wakatalika kuzimaana aawo naakazwa, mpawo, aawo naakapiluka a Duchess, wakamana biyo; aboobo Mwami asikujaya bakazuzaana kabayandaula, mpawo bamwi boonse bakajokela kumusobano.
