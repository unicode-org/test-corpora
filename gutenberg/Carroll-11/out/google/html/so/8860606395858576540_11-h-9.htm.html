Sheekadii Turtle-kii Majaajilada

"Ma malayn kartid sida aan ugu faraxsanahay inaan mar kale ku arko, wax duug ah oo qaali ah!" Waxay tidhi Duchess, iyadoo ay gacanteeda si kalgacal leh ugu xidhay Alice's, wayna wada baxeen.

Alice aad bay ugu faraxday in ay ku heshay xanaaq aad u macaan, waxayna nafteeda ku fikirtay in laga yaabo in uu yahay basbaaskii ka dhigay mid aad u qallafsan markii ay ku kulmeen jikada.

Waxaan jeclaan lahaa oo kaliya in dadku ogaadaan : markaas kuma ay bakhaylaan, waad ogtahay - "

Aad bay u ilawday Duchess wakhtigan, oo way yara naxday markii ay maqashay codkeeda meel u dhow dhegteeda. "Waxaad ka fekereysaa wax, gacaliye, taasina waxay ku illowsiisaa inaad la hadasho. Hadda kuma sheegi karo waxa ay tahay akhlaaqda taasi, laakiin wax yar ayaan xasuusan doonaa."

"Waxaa laga yaabaa inaysan midna haysan," Alice waxay ku dhiiratay inay tiraahdo.

"Tut, tut, cunug!" ayuu yiri Duchess. "Wax walba waxay leeyihiin anshax, haddii kaliya aad heli karto." Waxayna isku soo tuujisay dhanka Alice iyadoo hadlaysa.

Alice aad uma jeclayn in ay aad ugu dhawaato: marka hore, sababtoo ah Duchess aad buu u fool xumaa; iyo marka labaad, sababtoo ah waxay ahayd dhererka saxda ah si ay garkeeda ugu saarto garabka Alice, waxayna ahayd gadh aan raaxo lahayn oo af badan. Si kastaba ha ahaatee, iyadu ma jeclayn inay noqoto mid qallafsan, sidaas darteed waxay u adkaysatay intii ay kari lahayd.

"Ciyaarta ayaa hadda si ka wanaagsan u socota," ayay tiri, iyada oo wada hadal yar sii wadata.

"Waa sidaas," Duchess ayaa yidhi: "iyo anshaxa taasuna waa -" Oh, 'jacaylku,' waa jacayl, kaas oo adduunka ka dhigaya mid wareegaya!"

"Qof baa yiri," Alice ayaa ku dhaadhacday, "in ay sameeyeen qof kasta oo ka fikiraya ganacsigiisa!"

"Ah, si fiican! Waxay la macno tahay wax badan oo isku mid ah, "ayuu yiri Duchess, iyada oo garka yar ee fiiqan u qodaysa garabka Alice iyada oo ku dartay, "iyo anshaxa taasu waa - 'Ka taxadar dareenka, codkuna waxay daryeeli doonaan naftooda.' "

"Sida ay ugu jeceshahay in ay akhlaaq ka hesho waxyaalaha!" Alice waxay is tidhi.

"Waxaan ku dhiirranahay inaan idhaahdo waxaad la yaaban tahay sababta aan cududayda dhexda u gelin waayo," ayuu Duchess yidhi hakad ka dib: "Sababtu waxay tahay, waxaan ka shakisanahay xanaaqa flamingokaaga. Miyaan isku dayi doonaa tijaabada?"

"Waxa laga yaabaa inuu qaniino," Alice ayaa si taxadar leh ugu jawaabtay, iyada oo aan ka walwalin in la tijaabiyo tijaabada.

"Waa run," duchesku wuxuu yidhi: "flamingoes iyo mustard labaduba way qaniina. Anshaxa taasuna waa - 'Shimbiraha baalasha ayaa wada qulqulaya."

"Kaliya mustard shimbir maaha," Alice ayaa tiri.

"Sax, sida caadiga ah," Duchess ayaa yidhi: "Waa maxay habka cad ee aad wax u dhigayso!"

"Waa macdan, waxaan filayaa ," Alice ayaa tiri.

"Dabcan waa," ayuu yiri Duchess, oo u muuqday inuu diyaar u yahay inuu aqbalo wax kasta oo Alice yiri; Halkan waxaa ku yaal beer weyn oo iniin khardal ah, oo anshaxeedu waa, Inta kayga badanu jiro, taada way yar tahay.

"Ah, waan ogahay!" Ayay tiri Alice, oo aan ka soo qayb galin hadalkan u dambeeya, "waa khudaar, uma eka mid, laakiin waa."

"Waan kugu raacsanahay," Duchess ayaa yidhi; "Akhlaaqda taasuna waa - 'Noqo waxa aad u ekaan lahayd' - ama haddii aad jeclaan lahayd inaad si fudud u dhigto - 'Weligaa ha u malayn inaadan ahaanayn si ka duwan waxa ay u muuqan karto kuwa kale in waxa aad ahayd ama laga yaabo in aad ahaan lahayd aysan ahayn wax ka duwan waxa aad ahaan lahayd inay u muuqan lahayd si kale.' "

"Waxay ila tahay in aan si fiican u fahmo," Alice ayaa si xushmad leh u tiri, "haddii aan qori lahaa: laakiin si fiican uma raaci karo sidaad u leedahay."

"Taasi waxba ma ahan waxa aan dhihi karo haddii aan doorto," Duchess ayaa ku jawaabay, isagoo ku faraxsan.

"U soo ducee naftaada ha ku dhibin inaad tidhaahdo wax intaas ka badan," ayay tiri Alice.

"Alla, dhib ha ka hadlin!" ayuu yiri Duchess. "Waxaan hadiyad kaaga dhigayaa wax kasta oo aan weli idhi."

"Nooc raqiis ah oo hadyad ah!" fikirka Alice. "Waan ku faraxsanahay in aysan siin hadiyado dhalasho oo kale!" Laakiin kuma ay dhiiran inay si qaylo ah u tidhaahdo.

"Mar kale ka fikiraya?" Duchess ayaa waydiiyay, iyadoo qodaysa garka yar ee fiiqan.

"Xaq baan u leeyahay inaan ka fikiro," ayay Alice si adag u tidhi, waayo waxay bilawday inay dareento walwal yar.

"Sida saxda ah," Duchess ayaa yidhi, "sida doofaarradu waa inay duulaan; iyo m -"

Laakiin halkan, Alice oo aad ula yaabtay, codka Duchess wuu dhintay, xitaa iyada oo ku dhex jirta ereyga 'anshaxeed' ee ay ugu jeceshahay, cudud ku xidhan keeda ayaa bilaabay inay gariirto. Alice ayaa kor u eegtay, waxaana soo hor istaagtay boqoraddii, iyada oo gacmaheeda isku laabma, oo sida duufaanka onkod wejiga u qarsanaysa.

"Maalin wanaagsan, mudane! Duchess wuxuu ku bilaabay cod hoose oo daciif ah.

"Hadda, waxaan ku siinayaa digniin cadaalad ah," ayay ku qaylisay boqoraddii, iyadoo ku shaabadaysay dhulka intay hadlaysay; "Adiga ama madaxaagu midkoodna waa inuu maqan yahay, taasuna waxay ku dhowdahay nus wakhti. Qaado doorashadaada!"

Duchess waxay qaadatay doorkeeda, oo waxay ku tagtay daqiiqad gudaheed.

"Aan sii wadno ciyaarta," Boqoradda ayaa ku tidhi Alice; Alice aad bay uga baqday inay kelmad tidhaahdo, laakiin si tartiib ah ayay dib ugu soo raacday dhulkii caruuska.

Martidii kale waxay ka faa'iidaysteen maqnaanshihii boqoradda, waxayna ku nasanayeen hadhkii: si kastaba ha ahaatee, isla markii ay arkeen, ayay degdeg ugu soo laabteen ciyaartii, boqoraddu waxay ku nuuxnuuxsatay in dib u dhac daqiiqad ah ay naftooda ku waayeen.

Waqti kasta oo ay ciyaarayeen boqoradda waligeed kama tagin muranka ciyaartoyga kale, oo ku qaylinaya "Madaxiisa ka bax!" ama "Madaxeeda ka bax!" Kuwii ay xukuntay waxa xabsiga loo taxaabay askartii, kuwaas oo dabcan ku qasbanaaday in ay meesha ka baxaan in ay yihiin baalle si ay arrintan u sameeyaan, si ay u dhammaato nus saac ama wax ka badan oo aan la helin wax faashado ah, dhammaan ciyaartoyda, marka laga reebo Boqorka, Boqorada iyo Alice, waxay ku jireen xabsi iyo xukun dil ah.

Kadib boqoraddii way iska tagtay iyadoo neeftu ka soo baxayso, waxayna ku tidhi Alice, "Weli ma aragtay qool-ka-cayaaraha?"

"Maya," ayay tiri Alice. "Xitaa ma aqaano waxa uu yahay Turtle Mock."

"Waa waxa maraq Turtle Mock laga sameeyey," ayay tidhi boqoraddu.

"Weligay mid ma arag, mana maqal mid," ayay tiri Alice.

Boqoraddii waxay tidhi, "Kaalay, oo isna wuxuu kuu sheegi doonaa taariikhdiisa."

Markii ay wada socdeen, Alice waxay maqashay Boqorku isagoo cod hoose ku hadlaya, guud ahaan shirkadda, "Dhammaantiin waa la cafiyey." "Kaalay, taasi waa wax wanaagsan!" Waxay is tidhi, waayo waxay dareentay in aanay aad ugu faraxsanayn tirada xukunnada boqoradda amartay.

Isla markiiba waxay soo fuuleen Gryphon, oo hurda cadceedda hurda. (Haddii aadan garanayn waxa Gryphon yahay, fiiri sawirka.) "Kac, wax caajis ah!" Boqoraddii waxay tidhi, "oo gabadhan u kaxee si aad u aragto qool-ka-ciyaarka, oo aan maqlo taariikhdiisa. Waa inaan dib u noqdaa oo aan arko qaar ka mid ah dilalka aan amray." wayna ka baxday, iyada oo Alice kaligeed ka tagtay Gryphon. Alice ma jeclayn muuqaalka makhluuqa, laakiin guud ahaan waxay u malaynaysay inay aad ammaan u tahay inay la joogto sidii ay u raaci lahayd boqoraddaas qallafsan: sidaas ayayna u sugtay.

Gryphon ayaa soo fadhiistay oo indhaha ka xoqay: dabadeed waxay eegtay boqoraddii ilaa ay ka maqan tahay araggeeda: ka dibna wuu qoslay. "Waa maxay madadaalo!" ayuu yidhi Gryphon, badh laftiisa, badhna Alice.

" Waa maxay madadaalo?" ayay tiri Alice.

"Sabab, iyadu ," ayuu yidhi Gryphon. "Waa wax walba oo ay jeceshahay, in: weligood qofna ma fulin, waad ogtahay. Kaalay!"

"Qof walba wuxuu dhahaa 'kaalay!' halkan,” ayay Alice u malaysay, intay si aayar ah u daba socotay: “Weligay sidaa la iima amrin nolosheyda oo dhan, marna!”

Ma foga ka hor intaanay meel fog ka arkin Turtle Mock, oo murugaysan oo cidla ah dul fadhiya daraf yar oo dhagax ah, markii ay u soo dhawaadeen, Alice waxay maqlaysay isagoo taahaya oo aad mooddo inuu qalbigiisu jabayo. Aad bay ugu naxday. "Waa maxay murugadiisa?" Waxay weydiisay Gryphon, Gryphonna wuxuu ugu jawaabay, si la mid ah erayadii hore, "Waa wax walba oo uu jecel yahay, in: isagu ma murugoon, waad ogtahay. Kaalay!"

Markaasay waxay u tageen Qool-kii Majaajilada ahaa, oo ku eegay indho waaweyn oo ilmadu ka buuxdo, laakiinse waxba ma odhan.

"Halkan gabadha yar," ayuu yidhi Gryphon, "waxay rabtaa inay ogaato taariikhdaada, way taqaanaa."

"Waan u sheegi doonaa iyada," Mock Turtle ayaa ku yidhi cod qoto dheer, oo qaawan: "la fadhiiso, labadiinuba, oo hal eray ha hadlin ilaa aan dhammeeyo."

Markaasay fadhiisteen, qofna daqiiqado ma hadlin. Alice waxay is tidhi, "Ma arko sida uu waligii u dhamayn karo, haddii aanu bilaabin." Laakiin samir bay sugtay.

"Hal mar," ayuu yidhi Mock Turtle ugu dambeyntii, isagoo si qoto dheer u taahaya, "Waxaan ahaa Turtle dhab ah."

Erayadan waxa raacay aamusnaan aad u dheer, oo ay jebisay kaliya dhawaaq marmar ah oo ah “Hjckrrh!” laga bilaabo Gryphon, iyo baroorta culus ee joogtada ah ee Turtle Mock. Alice aad ayay ugu dhawaatay in ay kacdo oo ay ku tidhaahdo, “Mahadsanid, mudane, sheekadaada xiisaha leh,” laakiin way is celin kari wayday iyada oo ku fikiraysa inay jiraan wax badan oo soo socda, markaasay iska fadhiday oo waxba ma odhan.

"Markii aan yaraa," ayuu Mock Turtle ugu dambeyntii sii waday, si degan, in kasta oo weli uu yara ooynayo iyo ka dib, "waxaan aadnay iskuul ku yaal badda. sayidku wuxuu ahaa Turtle duq ah-waxaan u yeeri jirnay Tortoise-"

"Maxaad ugu yeertay Tortoise, haddii uusan mid ahayn?" Alice ayaa waydiisay.

"Waxaan ugu yeernay Tortoise sababtoo ah wuu na baray," ayuu yiri Turtle Mock isagoo xanaaqsan: "Runtii aad baad u caajis badan tahay!"

"Waa inaad ka xishootaa naftaada inaad waydiiso su'aal fudud," ayuu raaciyay Gryphon; ka dibna labadooduba way aamuseen oo waxay eegeen Alice oo miskiin ah, oo dareentay inay diyaar u tahay inay ku quusto dhulka. Ugu dambayntii Gryphon ayaa ku yidhi Turtle-kii Mock, "Wax ka wad, saaxiibkaa! wuxuuna ku sii watay erayadan.

"Haa, waxaan ku galnay dugsi badda dhexdeeda, in kastoo laga yaabo inaadan rumaysan—"

"Weligay ma odhan maan odhan!" hadalka Alice.

"Waad samaysay," ayuu yidhi Mock Turtle.

"Carrabkaaga qabo!" ku daray Gryphon, ka hor inta aysan Alice mar kale hadlin. Turtle Mock ayaa sii socday.

"Waxaan haysanay waxbarashada ugu wanaagsan - run ahaantii, waxaan aadi jirnay iskuul maalin kasta - "

" Waxaan aadey dugsi-maalmeed, sidoo kale," ayay tiri Alice; "Uma baahnid inaad ku faanto sida waxaas oo dhan."

"Waxaa la socda waxyaabo dheeraad ah?" Weydiiyey qoolkii Majaajilada ahaa isagoo yara welwelsan.

"Haa," ayay tiri Alice, "waxaan baranay Faransiiska iyo muusikada."

"Oo dhaqid?" ayuu yidhi qool-gooyada Mock.

"Xaqiiqdii maya!" ayay tiri Alice iyadoo xanaaqsan.

"Ah! markaa kaagu ma ahayn dugsi aad u wanaagsan," ayuu yidhi Mock Turtle isagoo ku hadlaya nafis weyn. "Hadda annagu waxay haysteen dhammaadka biilka, 'Faransiis, muusig, iyo dhaqid - dheeraad ah'."

"Ma aadan rabin inaad wax badan rabto," Alice ayaa tiri; " ku nool gunta badda."

"Ma awoodin inaan barto." ayuu yidhi Qool-ma-tooskii oo taah. "Kaliya waxaan qaatay koorsada caadiga ah."

"Maxay taasi ahayd?" ayaa weydiiyey Alice.

"Dib-u-qoris, dabcan, in aan ku bilaabo," Qoor-goolkii Mock ayaa ku jawaabay; "ka dibna qaybaha kala duwan ee Xisaabinta - Hamiga, Distraction, Ugification, iyo Derision."

"Weligay ma maqlin 'Uglification'," Alice waxay ku dhiiratay inay tiraahdo. "Waa maxay?"

Gryphon ayaa kor u qaaday labadiisa calaacood isagoo yaaban. "Maxaa! Weligaa ma maqlin wax qurux badan!" ayay ku dhawaaqday. "Ma ogtahay waxa la qurxinayo waa, malaha?"

"Haa," ayay Alice si shaki leh u tidhi: "Waxay la macno tahay -in la sameeyo -wax kasta - ka sii qurux badan."

"Hagaag, markaa," Gryphon wuu sii socday, "haddii aadan garanayn waxa lagu ammaanayo, waxaad tahay qof fudud."

Alice ma dareemin dhiirigelin inay waydiiso su'aalo dheeraad ah oo arrintan ku saabsan, sidaa darteed waxay u jeesatay Turtle-ka Mock, oo waxay ku tidhi "Maxaad kale oo aad baratay?"

"Hagaag, waxaa jiray qarsoodi," Mock Turtle ayaa ku jawaabay, isaga oo tirinta maaddooyinka ku jira boodhkiisa, "- Mystery, qadiimiga iyo casriga ah, oo leh Seaography: ka dibna Drawling-Master Drawling-master wuxuu ahaa nin duug ah, kaas oo iman jiray toddobaadkii hal mar: wuxuu na baray sawir-qaadista, fidinta, iyo miyir-beelka ee Coils."

"Sidee ahayd taasi ?" ayay tiri Alice.

"Hagaag, kuuma tusi karo nafteyda," Mock Turtle ayaa yiri: "Aad ayaan u qallafsanahay. Gryphonna waligiis ma baran."

"Waqti ma helin," ayuu yiri Gryphon: "Waxaan u aaday Master-ka Classics, in kastoo. Wuxuu ahaa carsaanyo duq ah, wuxuu ahaa."

"Weligay uma tagin isaga," Mock Turtle ayaa yiri isagoo taahaya: "wuxuu baray qosol iyo murugo, waxay dhihi jireen."

"Sidaas ayuu yeelay, sidaas buu yeelay," ayuu yiri Gryphon, isaga oo taahaaya markiisii; oo labada xayawaan wejigooda baabacahooda ayay ku qariyeen.

"Oo maalintii imisa saacadood ayaad casharro samaysay?" ayay tiri Alice, iyada oo ku degdegaysa in ay baddasho mawduuca.

"Toban saacadood maalinta koowaad," ayuu yiri Mock Turtle: "sagaalka xiga, iyo wixii la mid ah."

"Waa maxay qorshe cajiib ah!" ayay tiri Alice.

Tani waxay ahayd fikrad ku cusub Alice, waxayna ka fikirtay in yar ka hor intaanay hadalkeeda xigta samayn. "Markaa maalinta kow iyo tobnaad waa inay ahayd fasax?"

"Dabcan way ahayd," Mock Turtle ayaa yidhi.

"Oo sidee baad u maareysay maalintii laba iyo tobnaad?" Alice si xamaasad leh ayay u sii socotay.

"Taasi waa ku filan tahay casharrada," Gryphon ayaa ku soo gooyay cod go'aan ah: "wax hadda u sheeg ciyaaraha."
