KAPITULO X.
Say Lobster Quadrille

Say Mock Turtle et nan-ingas na aralem, tan in-drawing toy beneg na sakey a flapper ed saray mata to. Ninengneng toy Alice, tan sinali toy mansalita, balet diad loob na sakey odino duaran minuto et ag-inmesel so boses to. "Singa walay pukel to ed eleng to," inkuan na Gryphon: tan ginapoan ton inyegyeg tan sinuntok ed beneg to. Diad kaunoran et akabawi so boses na Mock Turtle, tan, tekep na lua ya onlelereg ed saray aping to, intuloy to lamet:—

"Nayarin ag ka ni nambilay ed silong na dayat—" ("Agko ni," inkuan nen Alice)—"tan nayarin ag ka ni ingen akapikabat ed sakey a lobster—" (Inkuan nen Alice ya "Aminsan et tinawayan ko—" balet ta inusisa ton tampol so inkasikato, tan inkuan ton "Andi, agnin balot") "-kanian agmo naisip no antoy makapaliket a bengatla na Lobster Quadrille!"

"Andi, tua," inkuan nen Alice. "Anton klase na sayaw itan?"

"Akin," inkuan na Gryphon, "manporma ka nin unona ed gilig na dayat—"

"Duaran linya!" inmeyag so Mock Turtle. “Saray seal, pawikan, salmon, tan arum nira; insan, no naekal mo lan amin so jelly-fish ed dalan—”

" Kaslakan et mankaukolan itan na panaon," insebel na Gryphon.

“—onabante kan mamidua—”

“Balang sakey et walay lobster to bilang kapareha!” inmeyag so Gryphon.

"Siempre," inkuan na Mock Turtle: "onabante na mamidua, iparaan ed saray kapareha—"

"-mansaliw na lobster, tan manretiro ed parehon inkauksoy," intuloy na Gryphon.

"Insan, amtam," intuloy na Mock Turtle, "ibantak mo so—"

“Saray banagan!” inmeyag so Gryphon, ya ontatagey ed tagey.

“—anggad arawi ed dayat ya nayarian mo—”

“Manlangoy ka ya ontumbok ed sikara!” inmeyag so Gryphon.

“Manlukso ka ed dayat!” inmeyag so Mock Turtle, ya manpapasnok.

“Mansaliwan lamet na lobster!” inmeyag so Gryphon ed maksil a boses to.

"Pawil lamet ed dalin, tan satan labat so unonan pigura," inkuan na Mock Turtle, ya biglan inmepas so boses to; tan saray duaran pinalsa, ya manlulukso-lukso ed amin a panaon, so inmirung lamet ya maer-ermen tan mareen, tan ninengneng da si Alice.

"Seguradon marakep itan a sayaw," inkuan nen Alice ya mababaing.

"Labay mon nanengneng so daiset ed satan?" inkuan na Mock Turtle.

"Talagan dakel," inkuan nen Alice.

“Gala, salien tayo so unonan pigura!” inkuan na Mock Turtle ed Gryphon. "Sarag tayon anggapoy lobster, antam. Dinan so mangansion?"

"O, mangansion ka ," inkuan na Gryphon. “Alingwanan ko la ray salita.”

"Salamat, makapainteres ya bantayan so sayaw," inkuan nen Alice, ya malikeliket ta asumpal la: "tan labalabay ko itan ya makapainteres a kanta nipaakar ed whiting!"

"O, nipaakar ed saray puti," inkuan na Mock Turtle, "sikara—anengneng mo la ra, siempre?"

"On," inkuan nen Alice, "mabetbet ko ran nanenengneng ed panangan—" inusisa ton tampol so sarili to.

"Agko amta no iner so kawalaan nen Dinn," inkuan na Mock Turtle, "balet no mabetbet mo la ran anengneng, siempre amtam no antoy itsura ra."

“Manisia ak,” so inyebat nen Alice ya mannononot. “Wala ray ikol da ed sangi ra—tan amin da et napno na mumo.”

"Aliwa ka nipaakar ed saray mumo," inkuan na Mock Turtle: "saray mumo et naandi ed dayat. Balet wala ray ikol da ed sangi ra; tan say rason et—" dia et inmepas so Mock Turtle tan inpirong to ray mata to.—"Ibagam ed sikato so rason tan amin na satan," inkuan to ed Gryphon.

"Say rason et," inkuan na Gryphon, "pian mila ra ed saray lobster ed sayaw. Kanian nibantak ira ed dayat. Kanian kaukolan dan napelag ed arawi. Kanian maples dan inloob iray ikol da ed sangi ra. Kanian ag da la ra napaway lamet. Ontan labat."

“Salamat,” kuan nen Alice, “makapainteres itan. Agko nin balot amta so ontan karakel nipaakar ed whiting nensaman.”

"Mas dakel ni so nibagak ed sika, no labay mo," inkuan na Gryphon. “Antam no akin ya tatawagen itan ya whiting?”

“Agkon balot inisip itan,” so kuan nen Alice. "Akin?"

" Gagawaen to ray botas tan sapatos ," so seryoson inyebat na Gryphon.

Abigbigla si Alice. "Ontan met so botas tan sapatos!" inulit to ed mankelaw a tono.

"Akin, antoy ginawa na sapatos mo ?" inkuan na Gryphon. "Labay kon ibaga, antoy manggagawa ed sikaran mankirlap?"

Ninengneng ira nen Alice, tan nannonot na daiset antis ton ebatan. “Asumpal da lay panag-black, manisia ak.”

"Saray botas tan sapatos ed silong na dayat," intuloy na Gryphon ed aralem a boses, "asumpal la ed panagputi. Natan et amtam la."

"Tan antoy ginawa ra?" so intepet nen Alice ed tono na baleg ya inkausisa.

"Soles tan eels, siempre," inmebat so Gryphon ya agmakaanos: "anggan anton hipon et nayarin angibaga ed sika na ontan."

"No siak so whiting," inkuan nen Alice, ya say kanonotan to et wala ni ed kanta, "inbagak komon ed porpoise, 'Arawi ka, ipangasim: ag mi labay ya kaiba mi ka !'"

"Kaukolan dan kaiba to," inkuan na Mock Turtle: "anggapoy makabat a sira ya onla ed anggan iner no anggapoy porpoise."

“Agta talaga?” inkuan nen Alice ed tono na baleg a pankelaw.

"Siempre andi," inkuan na Mock Turtle: "akin, no walay sira ya linma ed siak , tan ibaga ton sikatoy onla ed sakey a biahe, ibagak ya 'Antoy porpoise?'"

“Agta say labay mon ibaga et ‘gagala’?” inkuan nen Alice.

"Labay kon ibaga so ibabagak," ebat na Mock Turtle ya walay tono ton nasakitan. Tan inyarum na Gryphon, "Gala, dengelen tayo so pigara ed saray adbenturam."

"Nayarian kon ibaga ed sika iray adbenturak—manlapud natan ya kabuasan," inkuan nen Alice ya medyo mababaing: "balet anggapoy kakanaan na pawil ed kaplesan, ta duma ak lan too nensaman."

"Ipaliwawam ya amin iratan," inkuan na Mock Turtle.

"Andi, andi! Saray adbentura so unona," inkuan na Gryphon ya agmakaanos: "saray eksplinasyon et mankaukolan na makapataktakot a panaon."

Kanian ginapoan nen Alice ya insalaysay ed sikara iray adbentura to manlapu nen inmunan anengneng to so White Rabbit. Diad gapo et medyo nanerbiyos ed satan, inmasingger ed sikato so duaran pinalsa, sakey ed kada diking, tan nilukasan da so mata tan sangi ra, balet ta binmiskeg so pakpel to legan ya intuloy to. Saray dumerengel to et perpekton mareen anggad akasabi ed parte nipaakar ed pangulit to na “ Masiken ka la, Father William ,” ed Caterpillar, tan saray salitan amin et nanduruma, insan say Mock Turtle so nan-ingas na andukey, tan inkuan to “Makapainteres itan.”

"Amin et singa makapainteres," inkuan na Gryphon.

“Nanduruma so insabi na amin!” inulit na Mock Turtle ya mannononot. "Labay kon narengel ya salien ton uliten so sakey a bengatla natan. Ibagam ed sikato ya onggapo la." Ninengneng toy Gryphon ya singa to inisip ya walay autoridad to ed si Alice.

"Onalagey ka tan uliten mo '' Saya so boses na matamoy ,'" inkuan na Gryphon.

"No panon ya igaganggan na saray pinalsa so sakey, tan gawaen dan uliten iray leksion!" inisip nen Alice; "Nayarin wala ak lad eskuelaan a tampol." Balet, sikatoy inmalagey, tan ginapoan ton inulit itan, balet say ulo to et napno na Lobster Quadrille, kanian agto la amta no antoy ibabaga to, tan saray salita et talagan makapakelaw:—

“'Saya so boses na Lobster; nadngel kon sikatoy angibaga,

"Duma itan ed ibabagak nen ugaw ak ni," inkuan na Gryphon.

"Bueno, agko nin balot nadngel itan nensaman," inkuan na Mock Turtle; "balet singa aliwan kaslakan ya andi-kakanaan."

Anggapoy imbaga nen Alice; sikatoy yinmurong ya akabemben ed lima to so lupa to, ya mankelaw no kasin walay nagawa lamet ed natural a paraan.

"Labay ko komon ya ipaliwawa itan," inkuan na Mock Turtle.

"Agto nayarian ya ipaliwawa," inkuan na Gryphon ya tampol. “Ituloy mo so onsublay ya bersikulo.”
