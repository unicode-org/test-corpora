KAPITULO VII.
Sakey a Mad Tea-Party

Walay lamisaan ya inyan ed silong na kiew diad arap na abung, tan say March Hare tan say Hatter et man-tsa ed satan: walay Dormouse ya akayurong ed baetan da, ya naugip a maong, tan say duaran arum et uusaren da itan bilang unan, ya ipapasen da ray siko ra ed satan, tan mansasalita ed ulo to. "Agmakapaliket parad Dormouse," inisip nen Alice; "balet, ta naugip la, iisipen kon ag to itan iisipen."

Baleg so lamisaan, balet ta sikaran talo et nankakasakey ed sakey a suyok to: “Anggapoy kuarto! Anggapoy kuarto!” inmeyag ira nen anengneng dan onsasabi si Alice. “ Dakel so kuarto!” inkuan nen Alice ya mansyodot, tan inmirung ed baleg ya arm-chair diad sakey a sampot na lamisaan.

"Man-inum kayo na alak," inkuan na March Hare ed makapaseseg a tono.

Ninengneng nen Alice so amin a kaliberliber na lamisaan, balet anggapoy walad tapew to no ag say tsa. “Anggapoy nanenengneng kon alak,” so inkuan to.

"Anggapo," inkuan na March Hare.

“No ontan, aliwan sibil so pangiyopresim ed satan,” so mapasnok ya inkuan nen Alice.

“Aliwan sibil ed sika so inyurong ya ag ka naimbitaan,” inkuan na March Hare.

“Agko amta ya lamisaan mo itan,” inkuan nen Alice; "niletneg itan parad dakel a mas dakel nen say talora."

"Labay mon putoten so buek mo," inkuan na managsombrero. Abayag to lan sankanengneng si Alice ya walay baleg ya inka-usisa to, tan saya so sankaunaan ya impansalita to.

"Nepeg mon aralen ya ag manggawa na personal iran balikas," inkuan nen Alice tekep na pigaran inkagrabe; “bastos itan.”

Inlukas na manag-sombrero so mata to nen nadngel to iya; balet say imbaga to labat et, "Akin et singa lamisaan so uwak?"

"Gala, manliket tayo natan!" inisip nen Alice. “Maliket ak ta ginmapo la ran mantepet na saray patit.—Manisia ak ya nahulaan ko itan,” so inyarum ton maksil.

"Labay mon ibaga et isipen mon naamtaan mo so ebat ed satan?" inkuan na March Hare.

"Ontan met," inkuan nen Alice.

"No ontan, nepeg mon ibaga so labay mon ibaga," intuloy na March Hare.

"On," so tampol ya inyebat nen Alice; "at least—at least labay kon ibaga so ibabagak—pareho labat itan, antam."

"Aliwan pareho so bengatla!" inkuan na manag-sombrero. “Nayarin ibagam met ya say ‘nanengneng ko no antoy kakanen ko’ et pareho labat ed ‘kakanen ko no antoy nanenengneng ko’!”

"Nayarin ibagam met," inyarum na March Hare, "ya say 'Labalabay ko so naawat ko' et pareho ed 'Naawat ko so labalabay ko'!"

"Nayarin ibagam met," inyarum na Dormouse, ya singa mansasalita legan ya naugip, "ya say 'Manlinawa ak no naugip ak' et pareho ed 'Manpapainawa ak no manlinawa ak'!"

" Ontan met ed sika," inkuan na Hatter, tan dia et tinmunda so tongtongan, tan say grupo et ag-inmesel ed loob na sakey minuto, legan ya inisip nen Alice so amin ya nanonotan to nipaakar ed saray uwak tan saray writing-desk, ya aliwan dakel.

Say manag-sombrero so sankaunaan ya amawala na kareenan. "Anton agew na bulan natan?" inkuanto, ya inmarap ed si Alice: inala toy relo to ed bulsa to, tan sikatoy onnenengneng ya agmakapaliket, ya igagalaw to itan panapanaon, tan inegnaan tod layag to.

Nannonot na daiset si Alice, insan to inkuan ya “Say komapat.”

"Duaran agew ya aliwa!" inmeyag so manag-sombrero. “Imbagak lad sika ya ag-ontutukoy so mantikilya ed saray kimey!” inyarum to ya mansyodot ya onnenengneng ed March Hare.

"Saman so sankaabigan a mantikilya," so mauyamon inyebat na March Hare.

"On, balet wala ray mumo ya linmoob met," inkuan na manag-sombrero: "agmo komon inloob itan ed kutsilyo na tinapay."

Inala na March Hare so relo tan ninengneng to ya maer-ermen: insan to insawsaw ed tasa na tsa to, tan ninengneng to lamet: balet anggapo lay naisip ton mas maabig ya ibaga nen say inmunan imbaga to, "Saman so sankaabigan ya mantikilya, antam."

Si Alice et onnenengneng ed abaga to ya walay pan-usisa to. "Agaylan makapaliket ya relo!" inkuan to. “Ibabaga to no antoy agew na bulan, tan agto ibabaga no anton oras la!”

"Akin et ontan?" inkuan na manag-sombrero. “Ibabaga kasi na relo mo no anton taon itan?”

"Siempre andi," inmebat si Alice: "balet lapud mansiansian parehon taon ed andukey a panaon ya saniiba ira."

"Ontan met so nagagawa ed siak ," inkuan na manag-sombrero.

Alikna nen Alice so makapataktakot a pakawetwet, say imbaga na The Hatter et singa anggapoy kabaliksan to, balet ta seguradon Ingles itan. “Ag ta ka tanton natatalosan,” so inkuan to, ya magalang anggad nayarian to.

"Naugip lamet so Dormouse," inkuan na Hatter, tan inkalbo toy daiset ya ambetel a tsa ed eleng to.

Inmingal so Dormouse ya agmakaanos, tan inkuan to, ya agto nilukasan so mata to, "Siempre, siempre; samay labay kon ibaga."

"Ahulaan mo la so patit?" inkuan na Hatter, ya inmarap lamet ed si Alice.

“Andi, isuko ko la,” ebat nen Alice: “antoy ebat?”

“Anggapoy anggan daiset ya ideyak,” inkuan na Managsombrero.

"Ni siak," inkuan na March Hare.

Naksawan ya nan-ingas si Alice. "Iisipen ko ya mas maabig so nagawaan mo ed panaon," inkuanto, "nen say sayangen mo itan ed pantepet na saray patit ya anggapoy ebat."

"No kabat mo so Panaon a singa siak," inkuan na Manag-sombrero, "agmo pantongtongan so nipaakar ed panagsayang ed satan . Sikato itan."

“Agko amta so labay mon ibaga,” so inkuan nen Alice.

“Siempre agmo!” inkuan na Manag-sombrero, ya inyalis toy ulo to ya walay panagmudmora to. "Ibagak ya ag ka anggan akatongtong nen Time!"

“Nayarin andi,” so maalwar ya inyebat nen Alice: “balet amtak a kaukolan kon taloen so panaon sano manaaral ak na musika.”

"Ah! satan so makasengeg," inkuan na managgaway sombrero. "Agto la nasarag so manpapaknol. Natan, no mansiansia kan maong ed sikato, gawaen to so ngalngalin amin a labay mo ed relo. Singa bilang, ipasen tayon alas nuebe na kabuasan, oras la pian onggapo iray leksion: kaukolan mo labat ya ibulong so sakey a pakapatnagan ed Oras, tan onliber so relo ed sakey a kirmat-kapalduay oras, parad sakey a pangdem!"

("Labay ko labat ya ontan," inkuan na March Hare ed sarili to diad sakey a bulong.)

"Makapaliket itan, segurado," inkuan nen Alice ya mannononot: "balet no ontan—agko nepeg ya naerasan itan, antam."

"Andi diad gapo, anganko," inkuan na Managsombrero: "balet sarag mon pansiansiaen ya anggad alas-una tan kapaldua anggad labay mo."

"Ontan kasi so paraan na panag-asikaso yo ?" so intepet nen Alice.

Say manag-sombrero et maer-ermen ya ingalngal toy ulo to. "Aliwan siak!" ebat to. "Nansangsangan kami nen imbeneg a Marso—sakbay ya sikatoy nabaliw, amta yo—" (ituturo toy kutsara to ed March Hare,) "-diad baleg a konsierto ya inter na Reyna na Puso, tan kaukolan koy mangansion

'Mankirlap, mankirlap, melag a paniki!
Pankelawan ko no antoy gagawaen mo!'

Antam so kansion, anganko?”

“Walay nadngel kon ontan,” so kuan nen Alice.

"Mantultuloy itan, amta yo," intuloy na Managsombrero, "diad onian paraan:—

'Diad tagey na mundo et ontitikyab ka,
Singa sakey a bandehado na tsa ed tawen.
Mankirlap, mankirlap—'”

Diad saya et inyegyeg na Dormouse so sarili to, tan ginmapon nankanta ed ugip to na “ Twinkle, twinkle, twinkle, twinkle —” tan intuloy to ya andukey kanian kinaukolan dan kuroten pian ontunda.

"Bueno, ngalngali ko ni asumpal so unonan bersikulo," inkuan na Manag-sombrero, "nen linmukso so Reyna tan inmeyag, 'Papateyen to so panaon! Ekalen so ulo to!'"

"Agaylan makapataktakot so inka-maruksa!" inkelyaw nen Alice.

"Tan manlapu lad saman," intuloy na Managsombrero ed maer-ermen a tono, "agto gawaen so kerewen ko! Alas-sais la natan."

Walay maabig ya ideya ya sinmabi ed nonot nen Alice. "Kasin satan so rason no akin ya dakel so ipapapaway dia?" so intepet to.

"On, ontan la," inkuan na manag-sombrero ya manpapasnok: "naynay ya oras na panag-tea, tan anggapoy panaon tayon man-uras na saray bengatla ed baetan na saray panaon."

"Insan mantultuloy kan onliliber, iisipen ko?" inkuan nen Alice.

"Ontan met," inkuan na manag-sombrero: "no naupot la ray bengatla."

"Balet antoy nagawa no onsabi ka lamet ed gapoan?" Inmabuloy si Alice ya mantepet.

"Ipasen tayon umanen tayo so tema," insebel na March Hare, ya manpapasnok. "Nasasaew ak la ed saya. Ibotos ko so marikit ya mangibaga na istorya ed sikatayo."

“Natatakot ak ya anggapoy kabat ko,” inkuan nen Alice, ya medyo naalarma ed samay propesyon.

"Insan say Dormouse!" nanakis iran dua. "Manliing ka, Dormouse!" Tan pinilit da itan ed duaran dapag a maminsan.

Kalkalnan nilukasan na Dormouse so mata to. "Agak naugip," inkuanto ed maingal tan makapuy a boses: "Nadngel ko so amin ya ibabaga yo."

“Istoryaen mo kami!” inkuan na March Hare.

“On, ipangasin gawaen!” inkerew nen Alice.

"Tan maples ka," inyarum na Manag-sombrero, "odino naugip ka lamet antis ya nasumpal."

"Aminsan et walay taloran angkekelag ya agagi," inggapo na Dormouse ya manaapura; “tan saray kangaranan da et Elsie, Lacie, tan Tillie; tan manaayam ira ed dalem na sakey a bobon—”

"Antoy panbibilayan da?" inkuan nen Alice, ya lanang ya interesado ed saray tepet nipaakar ed panangan tan iyinum.

"Manbibilay ira ed treacle," inkuan na Dormouse, kayari ton nannonot na sakey odino duaran minuto.

“Ag da komon nagawaan itan, antam,” so maalwar ya inkuan nen Alice; "mansasakit ira komon."

"Ontan ira," inkuan na Dormouse; “ mansasakit a maong .”

Sinali nen Alice ya isipen no antoy magmaliw ya ontan ya ekstraordinaryon paraan na panagbilay, balet ta sikatoy awetwet a maong, kanian intuloy to: “Balet akin et manaayam ira ed leksab na bobon?”

"Man-inum ka ni na tsa," inkuan na March Hare ed si Alice, ya masimoon.

“Anggapo ni so naala ko,” ebat nen Alice ed makapasakit a boses, “kanian agko la naala so dakel ni.”

"Labay mon ibaga ya agmo nayarian so mangala na daiset ," inkuan na Manag-sombrero: "mainomay so mangala na mas dakel nen say anggapo."

"Anggapoy nantepet ed opinyon mo ," inkuan nen Alice.

“Siopa so manggagawa na personal a balikas natan?” matalonan intepet na manag-sombrero.

Ag amta nen Alice no antoy ibaga to ed saya: kanian inminum na tsa tan tinapay tan mantikilya, insan to inmarap ed Dormouse, tan inulit toy tepet to. "Akin et nanayam ira ed leksab na bobon?"

Say Dormouse et nannonot lamet na sakey odino duaran minuto, insan to inkuan, "Sakey itan a treacle-well."

“Anggapoy ontan!” Si Alice et ginmapon manpasnok, balet say Hatter tan say March Hare et inkuan dan “Sh! sh!” tan say Dormouse et maermen ya inkuan to, "No ag ka makapan-sibil, mas maabig no sumpalen mo lay istorya parad sarilim."

"Andi, ituloy mo pa!" Inkuan nen Alice ya mapaabeba; "Agko la ibabalang lamet. Ibagak ya nayarin walay sakey ."

"Sakey, tua!" inkuan na Dormouse ya mansyodot. Balet, sikatoy inmabobon ya mantultuloy. "Tan kanian sarayan taloran angkekelag ya agagin bibii—manaaral iran mandrowing, amta yo—"

"Antoy indrowing da?" inkuan nen Alice, ya alingwanan to lay sipan to.

"Treacle," inkuan na Dormouse, ya agto la inisip so amin a panaon.

"Labay koy malinis a kopa," insebel na Managsombrero: "onla tayo lan amin ed sakey a pasen."

Intuloy to legan ya mansasalita, tan tinumbok na Dormouse: say March Hare so inmalis ed pasen na Dormouse, tan si Alice et agto labay ya sinalatan so March Hare. Say Hatter labat so akaawat na antokaman ya bentaha ed pananguman: tan si Alice et mas mauges nen say dati, lapud say March Hare et inloob to labat so milk-jug ed plato to.

Agla labay nen Alice ya nasakitan lamet so Dormouse, kanian sikatoy maalwar ya angigapo: “Balet agko natalosan. Iner so angalaan da na treacle?”

"Nayarian moy mangala na danum ed bobon," inkuan na Manag-sombrero; "kanian isipen ko ya sarag moy mangala na treacle manlapud treacle-well-eh, makulangkulang?"

"Balet wala ra ed bobon," inkuan nen Alice ed Dormouse, ya agto labay ya imanoen iyan unor a balikas.

"Siempre ontan ira," inkuan na Dormouse; “—maong.”

Sayan ebat so amawetwet ed pobrin si Alice, kanian inabuloyan toy Dormouse ya ag to inabala.

"Manaral iran mandrowing," intuloy na Dormouse, ya onkukurong tan mankukuskos ed saray mata to, ta sikatoy naugip lan maong; "tan indrowing da so amin a klase na bengatla—amin a bengatla ya onggapo ed M—"

"Akin et walay M?" inkuan nen Alice.

"Akin andi?" inkuan na March Hare.

Ag-inmesel si Alice.

Say Dormouse et akasara la ray mata to ed saman a panaon, tan sikatoy ontutulog la; balet, nen pinilit na Hatter, binmangon lamet itan ya walay daiset ya inmeyag, tan intuloy to: “—ya onggapo ed M, a singa saray mouse-traps, tan say bulan, tan memorya, tan karakel—amta yon ibabaga yon saray bengatla et “dakel na karakel”—akanengneng kayo la kasi na ontan a bengatla bilang sakey a drowing na karakel?”

"Talagan, natan et tepetan mo ak," inkuan nen Alice, ya awetwet a maong, "Agko isipen ya—"

“No ontan sirin, ag ka nepeg ya mansalita,” inkuan na Manag-sombrero.

Sayan piraso na inkabastos et ag ni naanosan nen Alice: sikatoy inmalagey ed baleg a inkadismaya, tan inmalis; say Dormouse et naugip a tampol, tan anggapo ed saray arum so angiimano ed iyakar to, anggaman sikatoy linmingaw ed beneg na maminsan odino mamidua, ya iilaloan ton sikatoy tawagen da: nen unor ya anengneng to ra, sasalien dan iiyan so Dormouse ed teapot.

“Anggan panon et agak lan balot onla diman lamet!” inkuan nen Alice legan ton ondadalan ed katakelan. "Saya so sanka-stupid ya tea-party ya agawaan ko ed interon bilay ko!"

Nen ibabaga to iya, anengneng ton sakey ed saray kiew et walay puerta to ya mamaarap ed satan. “Makapainteres itan!” inisip to. "Balet amin et makapainteres natan. Isipen ko ya nayarin onloob ak lan tampol." Tan sikatoy linmoob.

Anengneng to lamet so inkasikato ed andukey a pasilyo, tan asingger ed melag a lamisaan a salming. "Natan, mas maong so nagawaan ko natan," inkuan tod sarili to, tan ginapoan to ya inala so melag a balitok a tombok, tan inlukas toy puerta ya mamaarap ed hardin. Insan sikatoy linma ed trabaho ton mankakagat na tahong (walay inyan ton piraso na satan ed bulsa to) anggad ngalngali sakey a mitro so katagey to: insan sikatoy nanakar ed melag a dalan: insan —aromog toy inkasikato ed marakep a hardin, diad limog na saray maliwliwawan flower-bed tan saray ambetel a subol.
