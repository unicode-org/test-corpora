EISHOMO RYA III.
Okwiruka kwa Caucus n'enkuru ndaingwa

Buzima bakaba bari ekibiina ekirikureebeka nk’eky’omutaano ekyabaire kyeteeraine aha rubaju rw’enyanja—ebinyonyi ebiine amapapa agarikukururwa, enyamaishwa eziine ebyoma byazo ebikwatsire haihi, kandi zoona zikaba nizitonnya amaizi, ziri omu musharaba, kandi zitashemereirwe.

Ekibuuzo ky’okubanza buzima kikaba kiri, oku barikubaasa kugaruka kwoma: bakaba baine okugaaniira aha nshonga egi, kandi bwanyima y’edakiika nkye kikaba nikireebeka nk’eky’obuhangwa ahari Alice kweshanga arikugamba nabo omu muringo ogumanyirwe, nk’oku yaabaire abamanyire amagara ge goona. Buzima, akaba aine okuhakana okuraingwa munonga na Lory, owaahererukire kuhinduka omunyabwengye, kandi akagira ngu, “Ninkukira mukuru, kandi nyine kumanya gye;” kandi eki Alice akaba atarikwija kukiikiriza atamanyire emyaka ei kiine, kandi, nk’oku Lory yayangire omu muringo murungi kugamba emyaka yaakyo, hakaba hatariho ekindi eky’okugambwa.

Aha muheru Embeba, eyaabaire neereebeka nk'omuntu w'obushoboorozi omuribo, ekagira eiraka rihango, "Mushutame, mwena, mumpurikize! Ndyabayoma juba!" Boona bakashutama omurundi gumwe, omu mpeta mpango, ahagati harimu Embeba. Alice akaguma naakireeba n’okwerarikirira, ahabw’okuba akaba naahurira aine obuhame ngu naija kukwatwa omushwija mubi yaaba atayomire juba munonga.

“Ehe!” akagira ngu Embeba n'omuyaga gw'omugasho, "mwena mweteekateekire? Eki nikyo kintu ekirikukirayo kwoma eki ndikumanya. Okuhunama okwehinguririza, waaba nooyenda! 'William Omusinguzi, ou enshonga ye yaabaire neekunda Paapa, akaheebwayo omu bwire bukye ahabw'Abangyereza, ababaire nibenda abeebembezi, kandi akaba atwire omu bwire bukye amanyiriire munonga okutwara eby'obugaiga bya Edcar na Morcia na Mercia. Omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho—’”

"Oh!" Lory akagira atyo, arikutetema.

“Ninkushaba okusaasirwa!” Embeba ekagira, erikushekyerera, kwonka omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa munonga: “Waagamba?”

“Ti nyowe!” Lory akagira atyo omu bwangu.

“Nkaba ninteekateeka ngu okikozire,” Embeba ekagira. “—Ningumizamu. ‘Edwin na Morcar, abakuru ba Mercia na Northumbria, bakamurangirira: kandi nangwa na Stigand, omukuru w’ekanisa orikukunda eihanga rya Canterbury, akashanga kiri kirungi—’”

“Oshangire ki ?” nikwo Embaata yaagira.

“ Nkagitunga ,” Embeba ekagarukamu omu muringo gw’okusharara: “buzima noomanya eki ‘ki’ kirikumanyisa.”

“Nimmanya eki ‘kirikumanyisa’ gye ekirikumara, ku ndikutunga ekintu,” Embaata ekagira: “okutwarira hamwe n’ekikyere nari ekinyonyi. Ekibuuzo kiri ngu, n’enki eki archbishop yaashangire?”

Embeba teramanyire ekibuuzo eki, kwonka ekagumizamu ahonaaho, “’—ekashanga kiri kirungi kuza na Edgar Atheling kubugana William n’okumuha ekirunga. Emitwarize ya William omu kubanza ekaba eri ey’oburinganiza. Kwonka obushema bw’Abanorman be—’ Hati noogyenda ota, munywani wangye?” kikagumizamu, kyahindukira Alice obwo kirikugamba.

Alice akagira omu iraka ry’obusaasi ati: “Nk’oku kyabaire kiri amaizi, nikireebeka ngu tikirikunyoma na kakye.”

“Omu mbeera egyo,” Dodo ekagira n’ekitiinisa, yaimuka aha bigyere byayo, “ninshaba ngu orukiiko rwongyerweho, ahabw’okutwara ahonaaho emibazi y’amaani—”

“Gamba Orujungu!” nikwo Ekihungu kigira. “Tindikumanya amakuru g’ekicweka ky’ebigambo ebyo ebiraingwa, kandi, ekindi, tindikwikiriza ngu naiwe nookimanya!” Kandi Ekihungu kikainama omutwe gwakyo kushereka akamwenyo: ebimwe aha binyonyi ebindi bikateera akaari omu muringo ogurikuhurirwa.

“Eki naabaire ninza kugamba,” Dodo akagira omu iraka ry’okushoberwa, “n’okugira ngu, ekirungi ekirikubaasa kutureetera kwoma n’empaka za Caucus.”

“Okwiruka kwa Caucus n’enki ?” Alice akagira ati; tikugira ngu akaba naayenda kumanya bingi, kwonka Dodo ekaba ehuumuriire nk’oku erikuteekateeka ngu hariho oshemerire kugamba, kandi tihaine ondiijo owaabaire nareebeka nk’orikwenda kugamba ekintu kyona.

Dodo akagira ati: “Ahabw’enki, omuringo murungi gw’okukishoboorora n’okukikora.” (Kandi, nk’oku orikubaasa kukunda kugyezaho ekintu ekyo iwe wenka, eizooba rimwe omu bwire bw’obutiti, ninyija kukugambira oku aba Dodo baakibaasize.)

Okubanza kikataho akamanyiso k’omuhanda gw’empaka, omu muringo gw’okwetoorora, (“ekishushani kyenyine tikiine nshonga,” kikagira,) reero ekibiina kyona kikateebwa aha rubaju rw’omuhanda, aha n’aha. Tihariho “Omwe, babiri, bashatu, n’okurugaho,” kwonka bakaba batandika kwiruka obu barikwenda, kandi barekyera aho obu barikwenda, n’ahabw’ekyo kikaba kitari kyorobi kumanya obu empaka zaaheza. Kwonka ku baaherize kwiruka ekicweka ky’eshaaha nari ebiri, kandi bakagaruka baayoma munonga, Dodo ahonaaho ekateera akaari “Okwiruka kwahwa!” kandi boona bakakyehinguririza, nibahuuta, kandi nibabuuza bati: “Kwonka shi n’oha owasingire?”

Ekibuuzo eki Dodo akaba atarikubaasa kukigarukamu ataine kuteekateeka kwingi, kandi akashutama kumara obwire buraingwa orukumu rumwe runyigiriire aha buso bwayo (omwanya ogu orikukira kureebamu Shakespeare, omu bishushani bye), obwo abasigaire barikutegyereza bacumire. Aha muheru Dodo akagira ati, “ Buri omwe asingwire, kandi boona baine kuba baine ebiconco.”

“Kwonka shi n’oha orikuha ebiconco?” chorus y’amaraka munonga ekabuuza.

“Ahabw’enki, we , buzima,” Dodo akagira, arikworeka Alice n’orukumu rumwe; kandi ekigombe kyona ahonaaho kikamwehinguririza, kirikweta omu muringo gw’okushoberwa kiti, “Ebirabo!

Alice akaba ataine ki arikumanya eky’okukora, kandi omu kuhwa amatsiko akata omukono gwe omu nsaho ye, kandi yaihayo akabokisi ka comfits, (eky’omugisha murungi amaizi g’omunyugunyu gakaba gatakataahiremu), kandi yaabihayo okwetoorora nk’ebiconco. Hakaba hariho a-piece emwe yonka, yoona eyehinguririize.

“Kwonka aine kuba aine ekiconco we wenka, noomanya,” Embeba yaagira.

Aba Dodo bakagarukamu n’obusaasi bwingi ati: “Buzima.” “N’enki ekindi eki otungire omu nsaho yaawe?” akagumizamu, yaahindukira Alice.

Alice akagira n’obusaasi ati: “Ekikumu kyonka.”

“Mukihe aha,” Dodo akagira.

Bwanyima boona bakamwehinguririza omurundi ogundi, obwo Dodo arikuhayo ekikumu omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa, arikugira ngu “Nitukushaba okwikiriza ekikumu eki eky’ekitiinisa;” kandi, ku kyaherize kugamba oku okugufu, boona baashemererwa.

Alice akateekateeka ngu ekintu kyona kikaba kiri eky’obushema munonga, kwonka boona bakaba nibareebeka nk’abaine obusaasi bwingi munonga n’ahabw’ekyo tarabaasize kusheka; kandi, nk’oku yaabaire atarikubaasa kuteekateeka aha kintu kyona eky’okugamba, akafukama kwonka, yaatwara ekikumu, arikureebeka nk’oine ekitiinisa nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa.

Ekindi ekyakuratsireho n’okurya ebikomo: eki kikaba kireetaho oruyombo n’okushoberwa, nk’oku ebinyonyi ebihango byabaire nibyetomboita ngu tibirikubaasa kushemererwa ebyabyo, kandi ebikye bikaba binyiganyiga kandi bikaba biine kuteera aha mugongo. Kwonka, kikahwaho aha muheru, baagaruka baashutama omu mpeta, baashaba Embeba kubagambira ekindi.

Alice akagira ati: “Okaraganisa kungambira ebyafaayo byawe, noomanya, n’ahabw’enki orikwanga—C na D,” yayongyeraho omu kugamba okukye, ekicweka ky’okutiina ngu nikiija kugaruka kikuteganisa.

“Eyangye n’enkuru ndaingwa kandi ey’obusaasi!” Embeba ekagira, ehindukira Alice, kandi yayehuzya.

“ N’omukira muraingwa, buzima,” Alice nikwo kugira, arikureeba ahansi n’okutangaara aha mukira gw’Embeba; “kwonka ahabwenki nookyeta eky’obusaasi?” Kandi akaguma naakibuuzabuuza obwo Embeba erikugamba, n’ahabw’ekyo ekiteekateeko kye aha nfumu kikaba kiri nk’eki:—

“Torikuzayo!” Embeba ekagambira Alice n’amaani. “Noteekateeka ahari ki?”

Alice akagira omu bucureezi bwingi ati: “Ninkushaba okusaasirwa, ninteekateeka ngu okaba ohikire aha rubaju rwa kataano?”

“Nkaba ntaine! ” Embeba ekarira, n’obushaarizi kandi n’ekiniga kingi.

“Orukoba!” Alice akagira atyo, butoosha arikweteekateeka kwehindura ow’omugasho, kandi arikumureeba aine okwerarikirira. “Oi, reka mbahwere kukigarura enyima!”

“Tihariho eki ndikwija kukora eky’omuringo ogwo,” Embeba ekagira, yaimuka yaagyenda. “Noonjuma orikugamba ebishuba nk’ebyo!”

“Nkaba ntarikukimanyisa!” akashaba omworo Alice. “Kwonka noorahuka kukwatwa ekiniga, noomanya!”

Embeba ekagurukaguruka kwonka omu kugarukamu.

“Ninkushaba ogarukye ohereze ekitebyo kyawe!” Alice akeeta bwanyima yaayo; kandi abandi boona bakeegaitaho omu kugamba, “Eego, ninkushaba kora!” kwonka Embeba ekazunguza omutwe gwonka etaine kugumisiriza, kandi yaagyendera omu bwire bukye.

“Ka n’eky’obusaasi ngu kitarikwija kugumaho!” akashekyerera Lory, ahonaaho ku yaabaire etarikureebwa kimwe; kandi Crab omugurusi akatwara omugisha ogwo kugambira muhara we ati “Ah, munywani wangye!Reka eki kibe eishomo ahariiwe obutagira ekiniga !” “Kwata orurimi rwawe, Maama!” akagira ngu omutsigazi Crab, arikushekyerera kakye. “Nomara kugyezaho okugumisiriza kw’enyanja!”

“Nkayenzire ngu mbe nyine Dina waitu hanu, nimmanya ngu nyine!” Alice akagira atyo omu iraka rihango, atarikugambira muntu weena omu muringo gw’omutaano. “Akaba naija kugigarurayo juba!”

“Kandi shi Dina n’oha, ku ndaabe nimbaasa kuguma ninbuuza ekibuuzo ekyo?” nikwo Lory yaagira.

Alice akagarukamu n'ekihika, ahabw'okuba akaba aguma ayeteekateekire kugamba aha kinyamaishwa kye: "Dinah n'ekikaari kyaitu. Kandi n'omunyabwengye munonga omu kukwata embeba ezi otarikubaasa kuteekateekaho! Kandi oh, nkayenzire ngu omureebe bwanyima y'ebinyonyi! Ahabw'enki, naija kurya akanyonyi kakye ahonaaho waareeba!"

Okugamba oku kukareetaho okushoberwa kw’omutaano omu kibiina. Ebimwe aha binyonyi bikagyenda ahonaaho: Enkongoro emwe enkuru ekatandika kwezingira n’obwegyendesereza bwingi, ekagira eti, “Buzimazima nyine kuba ninhika omuka; empeho y’ekiro terikuhikaana n’omumiro gwangye!” kandi Kanari ekeeta abaana baayo n'eiraka eririkutetema eti, "Mwije, banywani bangye! Obwire bwahika mwena muri omu kitanda!" Aha nshonga zitari zimwe na zimwe boona bakarugayo, kandi Alice akasigara wenka omu bwire bukye.

“Kaari nooba ntagambire ahari Dina!” akagira omu mutima gwe omu iraka ry’obusaasi. "Tihariho orikureebeka ngu naamukunda, ahansi aha, kandi ninkihamya ngu niwe kapa erikukirayo oburungi omunsi! Oh, Dinah wangye! Ninyebuuza yaaba ndagaruka kukureeba!" Kandi hanu Alice omworo akagaruka yaatandika kurira, ahabw’okuba akaba naahurira ari wenka munonga kandi aine omutima mukye. Kwonka omu kaanya kakye, akagaruka yaahurira ebigyere bikye birikuteera hare, kandi yaareeba ahaiguru aine ekihika, ekicweka kimwe aine amatsiko ngu Embeba ehindwiire aha biteekateeko byayo, kandi ekaba neegaruka kuheza ekigano kye.
