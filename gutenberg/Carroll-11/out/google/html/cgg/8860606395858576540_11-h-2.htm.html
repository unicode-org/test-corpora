“Okwenda kumanya n’okwenda kumanya!” Alice akarira (akatangaara munonga, ngu ahabw’akaanya kakye akeebwa kimwe oku arikugamba Orujungu orurungi); "hati ndiyo ninkinga nk'ekipimo ky'okureeba hare ekihango munonga ekyabaireho! Musiibure, ebigyere!" (ahabwokuba ku yaabaire areeba ahansi aha bigyere bye, bikaba nibireebeka nk’ebiri haihi kubura, bikaba nibihika hare munonga). "Oi, ebigyere byangye ebikye eby'abooro, ninyebuuza ngu n'oha oraakujware enkaito n'ebijwaro byawe hati, banywani bangye? Ninkihamya ngu tindikwija kubaasa! Kandi akagumizamu naateekateeka omu mutima gwe oku araakikorese. Akateekateeka ati: “Baine kugyendera aha muntu orikutwara ebintu; “kandi ka nikiija kureebeka nk’ekirikushemeza, okwohereza ebiconco aha bigyere by’omuntu!Kandi ka nikiija kureebeka nk’ekitari kya buriijo!

Oh munywani wangye, ka n’obushema obu ndikugamba!”

Omu bwire obwo bwonka omutwe gwe gukateera aha mutwe gw’ekishengye: omu mazima hati akaba aine oburaingwa bwa fuuti ezirikurenga mwenda, kandi ahonaaho akakwata akashumuruzo kakye aka zaabu, yairuka yaaza aha rwigi rw’omusiri.

Alice omworo! Kikaba kiri nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa kukora, agwejegyeire aha rubaju rumwe, kureeba omu musiri n’eriisho rimwe; kwonka okurabamu kikaba kitarimu matsiko okukira ira: akashutama yaagaruka yaatandika kurira.

Alice akagira ati: “Oshemerire kweshwara, omwishiki mukuru nkaiwe,” (nabaasa kugamba gye eki), “okugumizamu noorira omu muringo ogu! Reka akaire aka, ninkugambira!” Kwonka akagumizamu atyo, arikusheesha amarira ga galoni, kuhitsya obu haabaireho ekiziba kihango ekyabaire kyehinguririize, oburaingwa bwa inci nka ina kandi kirikuhika aha kicweka ky’ahansi y’ekishengye.

Bwanyima y’obwire akahurira ebigyere birikuteera kakye hare, kandi yaayomesa amaisho ahonaaho kureeba ekyabaire nikiija. Ekaba eri Omwana Omweru orikugaruka, ajwaire gye, aine giraavu z’omwana ez’omweru omu mukono gumwe n’ekifuba kihango omu mukono ogundi: akaija arikugyendesa ebigyere omu bwangu bwingi, arikwetonganira omu mutima gwe nk’oku yaabaire naija, “Oh! Alice akahurira aine amaganya maingi munonga n’ahabw’ekyo akaba ayeteekateekire kushaba obuhwezi ahari buri omwe; n’ahabw’ekyo, Omwana gw’entare ku gwaizire haihi nawe, akatandika, omu iraka ry’ahansi, ery’okutiina, “Ku oraabe nookushaba, mukama wangye—” Omwana gw’entare akatandika n’amaani, yaateera giraavu z’omwana gw’entaama ezirikwera n’ekifuba, kandi yaahunga omu mwirima nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa kuza.

Alice akakwata ekifuba na giraavu, kandi, nk'oku ekishengye kyabaire nikiyokya munonga, akaguma naayefuka obwire bwona obu yaabaire naagumizamu naagamba: "Munywani wangye, munywani wangye! Buri kimwe nikishushana munonga erizooba! Kandi nyomwebazyo ebintu bikagumizamu nk'oku byabaire biri. Ninyebuuza yaaba nkahindurwa omu kiro? Reka ntekateke: nkaba ndi nk'ogwo almo morning obu naabaire nimbaasa kuhurira? kakye n’okutaana Kwonka ku ndaabe ntari nk’ogwo, ekibuuzo ekirikurataho n’eki, Nyowe ndi oha omu nsi Ah, ekyo n’ekibuuzo kikuru!” Kandi akatandika kuteekateeka aha baana boona abu yaabaire naamanya ngu bakaba bari ab’emyaka ye, kureeba yaaba akaba naabaasa kuhindurwa ahabw’omwe aharibo.

"Ninkihamya ngu tindi Ada," akagira, "ahabw'okuba eishokye rye niriza omu nshonda ndaingwa nk'ezo, kandi eryangye tiririkuza omu nshonda na kakye; kandi ninkihamya ngu tindikubaasa kuba Mabel, ahabw'okuba nimmanya ebintu by'emiringo yona, kandi we, oh! Naamanya kakye munonga nk'okwo! Oyihireho ekyo, she's all how I, and I— oh,'ll'ar! ku ndaabe nimmanya ebintu byona ebi naabaire nimmanya.Reka ndeebe: emirundi ena eitaano ni ikumi n’ebiri, kandi emirundi ena mushanju ni—oh munywani wangye tindikwija kuhika ahari makumi abiri aha rurengo orwo Kwonka, Emeeza y’Okukanyisa terikumanyisa orurembo orukuru orwa Paris’ na— ekigwire, ninkihamya ngu nyine kuba nahindurwa ahabwa Mabel Ndyagyezaho kugira ngu ' How doth the little —'” kandi akashara emikono ye aha bibero bye nk'orikugamba amashomo, kandi yaatandika kukigarukamu, kwonka eiraka rye rikahurirwa nk'eririkuhurirwa nk'eririkuhurirwa nk'eririkuhurirwa nk'eririkuhurirwa!

“Akakoko kakye nikakora kata .
Yongyera aha mukira gwe ogurikwaka gye, .
Kandi oshukye amaizi ga Nailo .
Aha minzaani yoona eya zaabu!

“Ka naareebeka nk’orikumwenya n’okushemererwa, .
Oku yaabaire ajanjaaza gye ebigyere bye,
Kandi mwakiire ebyenyanja ebikye omu 2019.
N’ebishaya ebirikumwenya mpora!”

“Ninkihamya ngu ebyo ti bigambo bihikire,” Alice omworo akagira, kandi amaisho ge gakagaruka gaijura amarira obwo arikugumizamu, “Nshemerire kuba ndi Mabel bwanyima y’ebyo byona, kandi ndyaza kuba nyine kuza kutuura omu kaju ako kakye aka poky, kandi ntaine haihi n’ebintu by’okuzaanisa by’okuzaanisa, kandi oh! guma ahansi aha Tikirikwija kuba eky'omugasho okuta emitwe yaabo ahansi bakagira ngu 'Garuka oze ahaiguru, munywani wangye!' Ndyareeba ahaiguru kwonka n’okugira ngu ‘Nyowe obwo ndi oha banza ongambire ekyo, reero bwanyima, ku ndaabe ninkunda kuba omuntu ogwo, ndyaza ahaiguru: kyaba kitari kityo, ndyaguma ahansi aha kuhitsya obu ndiba ndi ondiijo muntu’—kwonka, oh munywani wangye!” Alice akarira, arikurira ahonaaho, "Nkaba ninyenda ngu bate emitwe ahansi! Nruhire munonga ahabw'okuguma ndi nyenka hanu!"

Ku yaabaire naagamba ebi akareeba ahansi omu ngaro ze, kandi yatangaara kureeba ngu ajwaire emwe aha nshaho nkye z'omwana gw'omwana gw'omwana gw'omwana ogwa Rabbit obwo arikugamba. “Ekyo nkaba nimbaasa kukikora nta?” akateekateeka. “Nshemerire kuba ningaruka kukura mukye.” Akaimuka yaaza aha meeza kwepimaho, kandi yaashanga ngu, haihi nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa kuteebereza, hati akaba ari fuuti nka ibiri oburaingwa, kandi akaba naagumizamu naakyendeera juba: akashanga juba ngu ekyareeteire eki n’ekifuba eki yaabaire akwatsire, kandi akakiteera ahansi ahonaaho, omu bwire bwonka okwetantara okukyendeera altother.

“Ekyo kikaba kiri okutoroka okukye!” Alice akagira atyo, aine obwoba bwingi ahabw’empindahinduka eyaabaireho ahonaaho, kwonka aine okushemererwa kwingi ahabw’okweshanga ngu naakibaho; “kandi hati ahabw’omusiri!” kandi yairuka n'obwangu bwona yaagaruka aha rwigi rukye: kwonka, kakye! akarugo kakye kakagaruka kakingwa, kandi akashumuruzo kakye ka zaabu kakaba kagwejegyeire aha meeza y'endabirwamu nk'oku kyabaire kiri enyima, "kandi ebintu nibikiraho kuba bibi," omwana omworo akateekateeka, "ahabw'okuba tinkabahoga mukye nk'ogu ira, tinka! Kandi ninrangirira ngu ni kibi munonga, ngu nikwo kiri!"

Ku yaabaire naagamba ebigambo ebi ekigyere kye kikasheesheka, kandi omu kaanya akandi, kyasheesheka! akaba ari kuhika aha kifuba kye omu maizi g’omunyugunyu. Ekiteekateeko kye ky’okubanza kikaba kiri ngu omu muringo gumwe akagwa omu nyanja, “kandi omu mbeera egyo nimbaasa kugaruka omu ruguuto rw’egaari y’omwika,” nikwo yaagizire omu mutima gwe. (Alice akaba agiire aha rubaju rw’enyanja omurundi gumwe omu magara ge, kandi akaba ahikire aha kuteekateeka okurikukirayo, ngu hoona ahu orikuza aha rubaju rw’enyanja y’Orungyereza nooshanga eby’okukoresa by’okwogyeramu bingi omu nyanja, abaana abamwe barikufukura omushenyi n’ebikopo by’embaaho, bwanyima omurongooti gw’amaju g’okuraaramu, kandi enyima yaabo hakaba hariho ekishaayi ky’egari y’omwika obu she was soon the wear ma akaba ari fuuti mwenda oburaingwa.

“Kaari noogira ngu ntakarira munonga!” Alice akagira atyo, obwo arikwoga, arikugyezaho kutunga omuhanda gw’okurugayo. "Ndyafubirwa ahabw'ekyo hati, ninteekateeka, ahabw'okumirwa omu maziga gangye nyenka! Ekyo nikiija kuba ekintu ky'okutangaaza, okuhamya! Kwonka, buri kimwe n'eky'okutangaaza erizooba."

Omu bwire obwo bwonka akahurira ekintu nikisheesheka omu kidiba hareho kakye, kandi yaayoga haihi kubaasa kumanya eki kyabaire kiri: omu kubanza akateekateeka ngu kiine kuba kiri enyamaishwa erikwetwa walrus nari hippopotamus, kwonka bwanyima akaijuka oku yaabaire ari muto hati, kandi omu bwire bukye akamanya ngu n’embeba yonka eyaabaire esherekirwe nk’eye.

"Kikabaasa kuba eky'omugasho, hati," Alice akateekateeka, "kugamba n'embeba egi? Buri kimwe kiri aheeru y'omuhanda munonga ahansi aha, ngu nshemerire kuteekateeka ngu nikibaasa kugamba munonga: omu muringo gwona, tihaine kabi koona omu kugyezaho." N'ahabw'ekyo akatandika ati: "Iwe Mbeba, noomanya omuhanda gw'okuruga omu kiziba eki? Nruhire munonga okwoga aha, Iwe Mbeba!" (Alice akateekateeka ngu ogu guteekwa kuba guri omuringo oguhikire gw’okugamba n’embeba: akaba atakakoraga ekintu nk’ekyo, kwonka akaijuka ngu akareeba omu Rulatini rwa murumuna we, “Embeba—ey’embeba—aha mbeba—embeba—O mbeba!”) Embeba ekamureeba omu muringo gw’okubuuzabuuza, kandi ekagira ngu no one of it to, seemed to.

Alice akateekateeka ati: “Obundi tikirikwetegyereza Orungyereza; “Ninteekateeka ngu n’embeba y’Abafaransa, ija aha na William Omusinguzi.” (Ahabw’okuba, n’okumanya kwe kwona okw’ebyafaayo, Alice akaba ataine kiteekateeko kirungi munonga aha bwire obu ekintu kyona kyabaireho enyima.) N’ahabw’ekyo akagaruka yaatandika ati: “Où est ma chatte?” ekibaire kiri sentensi y’okubanza omu kitabo kye ky’eishomo ry’Orufaransa. Embeba ekaguruka ahonaaho kuruga omu maizi, kandi ekashusha nk'erikutetema hoona ahabw'okutiina. “Oi, ninkushaba okusaasirwa!” Alice akarira omu bwangu, arikutiina ngu akahutaaza enyehurira z’ekinyamaishwa ekyoro. “Nkeebwa kimwe ngu torikukunda empunu.”

“Ti nk’ebikaari!” akagamba Embeba, omu iraka ry’okushekyerera, ery’omuhimbo. “ Noobaasa kukunda empunu kuri nyowe?”

"Kale, obundi tikwo kiri," Alice akagira omu iraka eririkuhuumuriza: "otagira ekiniga ahabw'ekyo. Kandi nabwo nkayenzire ngu nkworekye empunu yaitu Dinah: Ninteekateeka ngu noija kukunda empunu waaba noobaasa kumureeba kwonka. N'ekintu ekirikushemeza munonga ekicureire," Alice akagumizamu, ekicweka ahariwe wenka, obwo arikwoga omu " omuriro, okurinda ebigyere bye n’okunaaba omu maisho ge—kandi n’ekintu kirungi munonga ekigumire eky’okuyonsa—kandi n’ekikuru munonga eky’okukwata embeba—oh, ninkushaba okusaasirwa!” Alice akagaruka yaarira, ahabwokuba omurundi ogu Embeba ekaba neesheeshasheesha hoona, kandi akahurira aine obuhame ngu buzimazima ekaba eine ekiniga. “Titurikwija kugaruka kumugambaho waaba otarikwenda.”

“Buzima itwe!” ekagira Embeba, eyabaire neetetema kuhika aha muheru gw’omukira gwayo. "Nk'oku naabaire ninza kugamba aha nshonga nk'egyo! Eka yaitu ekaba eyanga empunu: ebintu bibi, eby'ahansi, eby'obushema! Otagaruka kuhurira eiziina eryo!"

“Buzimazima tindikwija!” Alice akagira atyo, arikwirukira munonga kuhindura enshonga y’okugaaniira. “Nookunda—nokunda—embwa?” Embeba teragarukiremu, n’ahabw’ekyo Alice akagumizamu n’ekihika: “Hariho akabwa kakye karungi munonga haihi n’enju yaitu aku naabaire ninkunda kukworeka! Akabwa kakye akaine amaisho garikumurinkana, noomanya, n’oh, eishokye riraingwa nk’erya kitaka erizingire! Kandi niriija kureeta ebintu waaba noobiteera, kandi niriija kushutama kandi rishabe ebintu, kandi I halft for i— aharizo—kandi n’ez’omuhingi, noomanya, kandi naagira ngu n’ey’omugasho munonga, neegura paundi igana Naagira ngu neeita envunyu zoona kandi—oh munywani wangye!” Alice akarira omu iraka ry’obusaasi ati, “Nintiina ngu nkagaruka naakinyiiza!” Ahabw'okuba Embeba ekaba neeyoga kuruga ahariwe nk'oku yaabaire neebaasa kuza, kandi erikukora akavuyo kahango omu kidiba nk'oku yaabaire neeza.

Mbwenu akeeta mpora bwanyima yaakyo, "Embeba munywani wangye! Kora ogarukye, kandi naitwe titurikwija kugamba aha mpunu nari embwa, waaba otarikuzikunda!" Embeba ku yaahuriire eki, yaahinduka kandi yaayoga mpora yaagaruka ahariwe: omu maisho gaayo hakaba harimu ebicucu bingi (n'ekihika, Alice akateekateeka), kandi yaagira omu iraka rikye eririkutetema, "Reka tuhikire aha rubaju rw'enyanja, reero ndyakugambira ebyafaayo byangye, kandi oryayetegyereza ahabwenki kiri ngu ninyanga empunu n'embwa."

Obwire bw’okuzayo bukahika, ahabw’okuba ekidiba kikaba nikitandika kujwara munonga ebinyonyi n’enyamaishwa ebyabaire bigwiremu: hakaba harimu Embaata na Dodo, Lory na Eaglet, n’ebindi bihangirwe bingi ebyabaire nibikunda kumanya. Alice akeebembera omuhanda, kandi ekigombe kyona kyayoga kyahika aha rubaju rw’enyanja.
