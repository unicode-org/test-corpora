EISHOMO RYA VI.
Empunu na Entangahuuzi

Akamara edakiika emwe nari ibiri ayemereire arikureeba enju, kandi arikwebuuza eky’okukora ekindi, ahonaaho omuvugi w’ebigyere omu livery yaija arikwiruka kuruga omu nku—(akamutwara nk’omuvugi w’ebigyere ahabw’okuba akaba ari omu livery: kitari ekyo, okucwera orubanja aha maisho ge gonka, akaba naija kumweta ekyenyanja)—kandi yaateera dock the loudly the knu at. Kikaigurwaho ondiijo muvugi w'ebigyere owaabaire ajwaire ebijwaro bya livery, aine amaisho geehinguririize, n'amaisho mahango nk'ag'ekikyere; kandi abashaija bombi ab’ebigyere, Alice akakimanya ngu bakaba baine eishokye ry’obutunguru eririkuzingazinga aha mitwe yaabo yoona. Akahurira aine ekihika ky’okumanya munonga eki kyabaire nikigambaho, kandi yaayeshukaho kakye kuruga omu kishaka kuhurikiza.

Omuvugi w’Ebyenyanja akatandika n’okushohoza kuruga ahansi y’omukono gwe ebaruha mpango, haihi nk’eye, kandi egi akagiha ondiijo, arikugira, omu iraka ry’ekitiinisa ati, “Ahabw’omugabekazi. Omutegyeki w’Ekikyere akagaruka, omu iraka rimwe ry’ekitiinisa, yaahindura omukuratana kw’ebigambo kwonka kakye, “Kuruga aha Mugabekazi. Okwetwa kwa Duchess kuzaana croquet.”

Bwanyima bombi bakafukama ahansi, kandi ebihimba byabo byakwatanisa.

Alice akasheka munonga ahabweki, ngu akaba aine kugaruka omu kishaka kwiruka ahabw’okutiina ngu nibaija kumuhurira; kandi ku yaakuratiireho kureeba aheeru Omushaija-W’Eby’enyanja akaba atakibaho, kandi ondiijo akaba ashutami ahansi haihi n’orwigi, arikureeba omu iguru omu bushema.

Alice akaza aha rwigi aine obwoba, kandi yaakoona.

"Tihariho mugasho gwona ogw'okukonkoona," Omushaija w'ebigyere akagira, "kandi ekyo ahabw'enshonga ibiri. Eky'okubanza, ahabw'okuba ndi aharubaju rumwe rw'orwigi naiwe; ekya kabiri, ahabw'okuba nibateera orwari nk'orwo omunda, tihaine owaabaire nabaasa kukuhurira." Kandi buzima hakaba hariho oruyombo orurikukirayo obuhango orwabaire nirubaho omunda—okuborooga n’okusheeshasheesha okutarikuhwaho, kandi buri kaanya hakaba hariho okuteerana kwingi, nk’oku ekikopo nari ekikopo kyabaire kicwekire.

Alice akagira ati: “Mbwenu ninkushaba, ninza kutaaha nta?”

"Habaasa kuba hariho amakuru omu kukoona kwawe," Omushaija w'ebigyere yaagumizamu atamufaho, "ku twakubaire twine orwigi ahagati yeitu. Nk'eky'okureeberaho, waaba ori omunda , noobaasa kukoona, kandi nkaba nimbaasa kukurekura, noomanya." Akaba nareeba ahaiguru omu iguru obwire bwona obu yaabaire naagamba, kandi eki Alice akateekateeka ngu n’ekintu ekitari ky’obuhangwa. Akagira omu mutima gwe ati: “Kwonka obundi tarikubaasa kukyemera; “amaisho ge gari haihi munonga n’ahaiguru y’omutwe gwe. Kwonka omu muringo gwona naabaasa kugarukamu ebibuuzo.—Ninza kutaaha nta?” akagaruka yaagamba, omu iraka rihango.

“Ndyashutama aha,” Omunyabigyere akagira, “okuhitsya nyenkyakare—”

Omu kaanya aka orwigi rw'enju rukaiguka, kandi eshahani mpango yaija neesheesheka, ehikire aha mutwe gw'Omutegyeki w'ebigyere: ekamuriisa enyindo yonka, kandi yaahendagura ebicweka aha muti gumwe ogwabaire guri enyima ye.

“—nari shi eizooba eryakuratsireho, obundi,” Omunyabigyere akagumizamu omu iraka rimwe, nk’oku hatariho ekyabaho.

“Ndyataaha nta?” Alice akagaruka yaabuuza, omu iraka rihango.

“ Noija kutaaha na kakye?” nikwo Omunyabigyere yaagira. “Ekyo nikyo kibuuzo ky’okubanza, noomanya.”

Kikaba kiri, hatariho kubangaanisa: Alice wenka niwe yaabaire atarikukunda kugambirwa atyo. "Buzimazima nikitinisa," akagamba omu mutima gwe, "oku ebihangirwe byona birikuhakana. Nikimara kureeta omuntu omu iraru!"

Omuvugi w’ebigyere akashusha ngu ogu n’omugisha murungi gw’okugarukamu ebigambo bye, n’okuhindurahindura. Akagira ati: “Ndyashutama aha, nshutame nshutame, kumara ebiro n’ebiro.”

“Kwonka nkore ki?” Alice nikwo arikugira.

“Eki orikukunda kyona,” Omunyabigyere nikwo kugira, kandi yaatandika kuteera enkoni.

Alice akagira ati: “Oh, tihariho mugasho gw’okugamba nawe, n’omushema buzima!” Atyo yaigura orwigi, yaataaha.

Orugo rukaba ruza omu kiyungu kihango, ekyabaire kiijwire omwika kuruga aha rubaju rumwe kuhika aha rundi: Duchess akaba asitami aha ntebe y’amaguru ashatu ahagati, arikuyonsa omwana; omuteeki akaba ayesigamiire aha muriro, arikuteera ekiyungu kihango ekyabaire nikireebeka nk’ekiijwire supu.

“Buzima omu supu egyo harimu entangahuuzi nyingi munonga!” Alice akagira omu mutima gwe, nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa ahabw’okusheeshasheesha.

Buzima hakaba hariho bingi munonga omu mwanya. Nangwa na Duchess akaba asheeshasheesha obumwe n’obumwe; kandi omwana, akaba naasheeshasheesha kandi arikuborooga arikuhindukahinduka ataine kuhuumuraho n’akaanya kakye. Ebintu byonka ebyabaire biri omu kiyungu ebyabaire bitarikusheeshasheesha, n'omuteeki, n'embwa mpango eyaabaire eshutami aha ntambi erikushekyerera kuruga aha kutu kuhika aha kutu.

Alice akagira ati: “Ninkushaba, noobaasa kungambira,” aine obwoba bukye, ahabw’okuba akaba atarikumanya gye yaaba n’emitwarize mirungi kubanza yaagamba, “ahabw’enki empunu yaawe neemwenya atyo?”

"Ni kapa ya Cheshire," Duchess akagira, "kandi niyo nshonga ahabwenki. Empunu!"

Akagamba ekigambo ky’aha muheru n’obutabanguko bw’ahonaaho kuhitsya obu Alice yaagurukire munonga; kwonka akareeba omu kaanya akandi ngu kikaba kigambirwe omwana, beitu kitari we, n’ahabw’ekyo akagira obumanzi, yaagaruka yaagumizamu:—

“Nkaba ntarikumanya ngu empunu za Cheshire ziguma nizimwenya; omu mazima, nkaba ntarikumanya ngu empunu nizibaasa kumwenya.”

Duchess akagira ati: “Boona nibabaasa kukikora; “kandi abaingi aharibo nibakikora.”

Alice akagira omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa munonga, arikuhurira aine okushemererwa kwingi ahabw’okutandika kugaaniira ati: “Tihariho ekindi eki ndikumanya.”

Duchess akagira ati: “Torikumanya bingi; “kandi ekyo n’amazima.”

Alice tarakunzire na kakye eiraka ry’ebigambo ebi, kandi akateekateeka ngu nikiija kuba kirungi kutandikaho enshonga endiijo y’okugaaniiraho. Ku yaabaire naagyezaho kuboneza ahari kimwe, omuteeki akaiha ekiyungu ky’esupu aha muriro, ahonaaho yaatandika kukora arikuteera buri kimwe eki yaabaire naabaasa kuhika ahari Duchess n’omwana—ebyoma by’omuriro bikabanza; bwanyima hakakuratiraho okusheeshasheesha kw’ebikopo, eshahani n’eby’okurya. Duchess tarabafaho n’obu baraabe baamuteera; kandi omwana akaba yaaheza kuborooga munonga, kikaba kitarikubaasika kugamba yaaba ebikoni ebyo bikamuhutaaza nari ngaaha.

“Oh, ninkushaba teekateeka aha ki orikukora!” Alice akarira, arikugurukaguruka omu busaasi bw’okutiina. “Oh, aho niho enyindo ye ey’omuhendo erikuza!” nk’oku ekiyungu kihango ekitari kya buriijo kyagurukire haihi nakyo, kandi kiri haihi munonga kukitwara.

“Ku buri omwe yaabaire naafayo aha nshonga ze,” Duchess akagira omu kugamba okugumire, “ensi ekaba neija kwetoorora endagaano y’okurahuka kukira oku eri.”

Alice, owaabaire naahurira aine okushemererwa kwingi ahabw’okutunga omugisha gw’okworeka okumanya kwe okukye, akagira ati: “Ekyo tikirikubaasa kuba eky’omugasho.” “Teekateeka aha murimo ogu kyakubaire nikikora omushana n’ekiro! Nooreeba ensi neetwara eshaaha makumi abiri na ina kwetoorora aha rubaju rwayo—”

Duchess akagira ati: “Okugamba aha mbanda, muteme omutwe!”

Alice akareeba omuteeki n’amaisho g’okwerarikirira, kureeba yaaba aine ekigyendererwa ky’okutwara obuhabuzi; kwonka omuteeki akaba aine bingi by’okukora arikuteera supu, kandi akaba nareebeka nk’otarikuhurikiza, n’ahabw’ekyo akagaruka yaagumizamu ati: “Eshaaha makumi abiri na ina, ninteekateeka ; nari ni ikumi n’ibiri? Nyowe—”

“Oh, otanteganisa ,” nikwo Duchess yaagizire; “Nkaba ntarikubaasa kugumisiriza emibaro!” Kandi n'ekyo akagaruka yaatandika kuyonsa omwana we, yaamweshongora ekika ky'ekyeshongoro ky'okuhuumuriza nk'oku yaabaire naakikora, kandi arikumuha okutengeeta kw'amaani aha muheru gwa buri runyiriri:

“Gambira omwojo waawe omuto omu muringo gw’obushaarizi, .
Kandi omukube ku arikusheeshasheesha:
Naakikora kunyiizya kwonka,
Ahabw’okuba naamanya ngu nikishekyerera.”

KORASI.
(Omu muteeki n'omwana bakeegaitaho):

"Waa! waa! waa!"

Duchess ku yaabaire naayeshongora orunyiriri rwa kabiri orw’ekyeshongoro, akaguma naateera omwana n’amaani ahaiguru n’ahansi, kandi akacupa kakye akooro kakaborooga munonga, Alice akaba atarikubaasa kuhurira ebigambo ebi:—

“Ningambira omwojo wangye kubi, .
Ninmuteera ku arikusheeshasheesha;
Ahabw’okuba naabaasa kushemererwa kimwe .
Entangahuuzi obu arikwenda!”

KORASI.

"Waa! waa! waa!"

"Hanu! Orabaasa kugiyonsa kakye, waaba nooyenda!" Duchess akagambira Alice, arikumuteera omwana obwo arikugamba. “Nshemerire kuza kwetebeekanisa kuzaana croquet n’Omugabekazi,” kandi yaaruga omu kishengye ahonaaho. Omuteeki akamuteera ekiyungu ky’okusiikiramu obu yaabaire naaza aheeru, kwonka kikamufeerwa kwonka.

Alice akakwata omwana ogwo n’oburemeezi, nk’oku kyabaire kiri ekihangirwe kikye ekiine omubiri gw’omutaano, kandi kyagorora emikono n’amaguru gaakyo omu nshonda zoona, “nk’ekyenyanja ky’enyonyoozi,” Alice nikwo yaateekateeka. Akantu kakye akooro kakaba nikafukaana nk’emotoka y’omuriro obu yaakikwatsire, kandi kakaguma nikyeyongyera emirundi ebiri kandi nikagaruka kagorora, ngu okutwarira hamwe, omu dakiika y’okubanza nari ebiri, kikaba kiri nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa kukora kukikwata.

Ahonaaho ku yaaherize kumanya omuringo oguhikire gw’okugiyonsa, (ogwabaire guri ogw’okugihindura ahaiguru omu muringo gw’orukoba, reero akaguma akwatsire gye aha kutu kwayo okwa buryo n’ekigyere kya bumosho, kugira ngu etakyehindura,) akagitwara aheeru omu mwanya ogurimu abantu. Alice akateekateeka ati: “Ku ndaabe ntatwaire omwana ogu, nibahamya ngu nibaija kumwita omu izooba rimwe nari abiri: tikirikuba kiri obwitsi kumusigaho?” Akagamba ebigambo by’aha muheru omu iraka rihango, kandi akacupa kakye kakagurukaguruka omu kugarukamu (kakaba karekire kusheeshasheesha omu bwire obu). Alice akagira ati: “Otakagira ekiniga; “ogwo ti muringo murungi gw’okworeka eki orikweteekateekaho na kakye.”

Omwana akagaruka yagurukaguruka, kandi Alice akareeba omu maisho ge aine okwerarikirira kwingi kureeba ekibaireho. Tihariho kubangaanisa ngu kikaba kiine enyindo erikwehindura munonga , erikushushana munonga n’enyindo okukira enyindo eyaabuzima; kandi amaisho gaakyo gakaba nigatandika kukyeera munonga ahabw’omwana: okutwarira hamwe Alice akaba atarikukunda oku ekintu kyabaire nikireebeka na kakye. “Kwonka obundi kikaba nikirira kwonka,” akateekateeka, kandi yaagaruka yaakireeba omu maisho, kureeba yaaba hariho amarira.

Ngaaha, hakaba hatariho maziga. "Ku oraabe noza kuhinduka empunu, munywani wangye," Alice akagira, n'obwegyendesereza, "tindikwija kugira ekindi ekindi kukora naiwe. Teekateeka hati!" Akantu kakye akooro kakagaruka karira (nari kagurukaguruka, kikaba kitarikubaasika kugamba eki), baagumizamu kumara obwire bukye bari omu kasirise.

Alice akaba naatandika kuteekateeka omu mutima gwe ati, “Hati, ekihangirwe eki nkore ki ku ndaakihikire omuka?” ku kyagarukire kyagurukaguruka, n’amaani maingi, kuhitsya obu yaareeba ahansi omu maisho gaakyo omu kutiina okumwe. Omurundi ogu hakaba hatariho nshobi eyaabaire neebaasa kugikoraho: ekaba etarikukira nari kukye aha mpunu, kandi akahurira ngu nikiija kuba eky’obushema munonga kugitwara omu maisho.

N’ahabw’ekyo akateeka akahangirwe akakye ahansi, kandi yaahurira yaahuumura munonga kureeba nikagyendera omu kishaka butarikugamba. Akagira omu mutima gwe ati: “Ku kiraabe kyakuzire, kikaba nikiija kukora omwana mubi munonga: kwonka nikikora empunu nungi munonga, ninteekateeka.” Kandi akatandika kuteekateeka aha baana abandi abu yaabaire naamanya, abarikubaasa kukora gye munonga nk’empunu, kandi akaba naayegambira wenka ati, “omuntu ku araabe yaamanya omuringo oguhikire gw’okuzihinduramu—” obu yaashoberwa kakye kureeba Empunu ya Cheshire eshutami aha itaagi ry’omuti yaadi nkye kuruga aho.

Empunu ekasheka kwonka ku yaareebire Alice. Kikaba nikireebeka nk’eky’omutwarize murungi, akateekateeka: nabwo kikaba kiine ebigyere biraingwa munonga n’ameino maingi munonga, n’ahabw’ekyo akahurira ngu kishemerire kutwarwa omu kitiinisa.

“Cheshire Puss,” akatandika, omu mwanya gw’okutiina, nk’oku yaabaire atamanyire na kakye yaaba neija kukunda eiziina eryo: kwonka, ekashekyerera kakye kwonka. “Ija, kishemereirwe okuhitsya hati,” Alice akateekateeka, reero yaagumizamu. “Noobaasa kungambira, ninkushaba, omuhanda ogu nshemerire kugyenderamu kuruga hanu?”

“Ekyo nikirugiirira munonga ahu orikwenda kuhika,” nikwo Kapu yaagira.

Alice akagira ati: “Tindikufayo munonga ahu—.”

“Reero tikiine nshonga ngu nooza omu muhanda ki,” Kapu nikwo kugira.

“—kasita nyine ahu ndikuhika ,” Alice nikwo yaayongyeraho nk’okushoboorora.

"Oh, nohamya ngu oraza kukora ekyo," Embwa ekagira, "waba notambura obwire buraingwa bwonka."

Alice akateekateeka ngu eki tikirikubaasa kuhakanisibwa, n’ahabw’ekyo akagyezaho ekindi kibuuzo. “Ni bantu ki abarikutuura aha?”

"Orubaju orwo ," Empunu ekagira, erikuteera ekigyere kyayo kya buryo okwetoorora, "hariho Omukozi w'enkofiira: kandi omu rubaju orwo ," erikuteera ekigyere ekindi, "hariho Enkofiira y'Okwa kashatu. Taayayira kimwe aharibyo ebi orikukunda: bombi n'abashema."

Alice akagira ati: “Kwonka tindikwenda kuza omu bantu abashema.”

"Oh, ekyo torikubaasa kukiyamba," Embwa ekagira: "twena turi abashazi hanu. Nshazire. Iwe oshazire."

"Wamanya ota ngu nshazire?" Alice nikwo arikugira.

“Oine kuba ori,” Kapu ekagira, “nainga okaba otakaizire aha.”

Alice tarateekateekire ngu ekyo nikihamya na kakye; kwonka, akagumizamu ati “Kandi noomanya ota ngu oine ekiniga?”

"Okutandika," Embwa ekagira, "embwa teshazire. Ekyo nokiikiriza?"

Alice akagira ati: “Ninteekateeka ngu nikwo kiri.”

"Kale, reero," Empunu ekagumizamu, "orareeba, embwa eraguruka yaaba eine ekiniga, kandi nenyiganyiga omukira gwayo yaaba eshemereirwe. Hati nyowe ninyorota ku ndishemereirwe, kandi nenyiganyiga omukira gwangye ku ndiba nyine ekiniga. N'ahabw'ekyo ndi omushema."

Alice akagira ati: “ Ninkyeta okugurukaguruka, ti kugurukaguruka.”

“Kyete eki orikwenda,” nikwo Kapu yagira. “Noozaana croquet n’Omugabekazi erizooba?”

Alice akagira ati: “Nshemereire kukikunda munonga, kwonka hati tindanyetsirwe.”

“Noija kunreebayo,” Kapu yagira, kandi yabura.

Alice taratangaire munonga ahari eki, akaba namanyiira munonga ebintu by’okutangaaza ebirikubaho. Ku yaabaire naareeba ahu kyabaire kiri, ahonaaho kikagaruka kyareebeka.

“By-the-bye, omwana akabaho ki?” akagira ngu Kapu. “Nkaba ndi haihi kwebwa kubuuza.”

Alice akagamba omu muringo gw’obucureezi ati: “Kikahinduka empunu,” nk’oku kyagarukire omu muringo gw’obuhangwa.

“Nkaba ninteekateeka ngu nikiija kubaho,” Kapu yaagira, kandi yaagaruka yabura.

Alice akategyereza kakye, ekicweka kimwe kya kabiri arikuteekateeka ngu naija kugaruka kukireeba, kwonka tikyareebeka, kandi bwanyima y’edakiika emwe nari ibiri akagumizamu yaagyendera omu rubaju oru March Hare yaabaire neegambwa kuba neetuuramu. Akagira omu mutima gwe ati: “Nkareebaho abakozi b’enkofiira; “Ekicuncu ky’okwezi kwa kashatu nikiija kuba nikishemeza munonga, kandi obundi nk’oku kiri omu kwezi kwa kataano tikirikwija kuba eky’okugwa omu iraru—haakiri kitari ky’okugwa omu iraru nk’oku kyabaire kiri omu Kwakashatu.” Ku yaabaire naagamba atyo, yaareeba ahaiguru, kandi hakaba hariho Kapu kandi, eshutami aha itaagi ry’omuti.

“Wagira ngu empunu, nari omutiini?” akagira ngu Kapu.

Alice akagarukamu ati: “Nkagira ngu empunu; “kandi nkayenzire ngu otaguma nooreebeka kandi okabura ahonaaho: nooreetera omuntu kuhurira aine ekizibu ky’okuhurira.”

“Kihikire,” Kapu yaagira; kandi omurundi ogu kikabura mpora mpora munonga, kyatandika aha muheru gw’omukira, kyahendera aha kushekyerera, okwasigaireho obwire bukye bwanyima y’ebicweka byakyo ebisigaireho kugyenda.

"Kale! Nkakira kureeba empunu etaine nshoni," Alice akateekateeka; "kwonka okumwenya okutarimu kapa! Nikyo kintu ekirikukirayo kushemeza eki ntarareebire omu magara gangye!"

Akaba atakagyenzire hare munonga atakaizire omu maisho g’enju y’Ekicuncu kya March: akateekateeka ngu eine kuba eri enju ehikire, ahabw’okuba emifuka y’omuriro ekaba eri nk’ey’amatu kandi obusingye bukaba burimu obunyaatsi bw’ebyoya. Ekaba eri enju mpango munonga, ngu akaba atarikukunda kuza haihi kuhitsya obu yaabaire yaaheza kunywaho ekindi kicweka ky’omushekye ekyabaire kiri aha mukono gwa bumosho, kandi yaayetunguura kuhika fuuti nka ibiri oburaingwa: nangwa n’obwo akagyendera aha mutwe gwayo omu mwanya gw’okutiina, arikwegambira ati “Ka tugire ngu kishemerire kuba kiine obushema bw’omushema bwanyima ya byona see I’ al’!
