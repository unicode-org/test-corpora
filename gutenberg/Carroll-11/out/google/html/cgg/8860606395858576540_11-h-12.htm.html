Omugabekazi akagira ati: “Ekyo nikihamya ngu aine orubanja.”

Omugabe akagira ati: “Mubishome.”

Omwana gw’omukyeru akajwara ebirahuri bye. “Ntandikye nkahi, okushemeza Mukama waawe?” akabuuza.

Omugabe akagira n’obusaasi ati: “Tandika aha kutandika, kandi ogumizemu okuhitsya obu oraahikye aha muheru: reero oyemerere.”

“Obwo nibwo buhame obukuru obu twahuriraga hati,” Omugabe nikwo kugira, arikusiiga emikono ye; “n’ahabw’ekyo hati reka abaramuzi—”

“Ku haraabe hariho omwe aharibo orikubaasa kukishoboorora,” Alice akagira, (akaba akuzire munonga omu dakiika nkye ezihwaire n’okugira ngu akaba ataine kutiina n’akakye kumuteganisa), “ninza kumuha sixpence. Tindikwikiriza ngu harimu atomu y’amakuru.”

Abaramuzi boona bakahandiika aha bipande byabo ngu, “ Tarikwikiriza ngu harimu akacweka k’amakuru,” kwonka tihaine n’omwe owaagyezeho kushoboorora orupapura orwo.

"Ku haraabe hatariho makuru omuri kyo," Omugabe akagira, "ekyo nikijuna ensi y'oburemeezi, noomanya, nk'oku tutarikwetaaga kugyezaho kutunga. Kandi obwo tindikumanya," akagumizamu, arikujanjaaza enyuguta aha kifuba kye, kandi arikugireeba n'eriisho rimwe; “Ninshusha ngu ninreeba amakuru gamwe omurizo, bwanyima y’ebyo byona. “— akagira ngu tindikubaasa kwoga —” torikubaasa kwoga, tikwo?” akayongyeraho, yaahindukira Knave.

Omunyabwengye akazunguza omutwe aine obusaasi. “Ninshushana nakyo?” akagira ati. (Eki buzima atakozire , ahabw’okugira ngu kikaba kikozirwe kyona omu kaada.)

“Kihikire, okuhitsya hati,” Omugabe nikwo kugira, kandi yaagumizamu naagamba n’eiraka rihango aha mishororongo omu mutima gwe ati: “‘ Nitumanya ngu n’amazima —’ egyo niyo nshonga y’okucwa emanja, buzima—’ Nkamuha emwe, bakamuha ibiri —’ ahabw’enki, ekyo kiine kuba nikyo yaakozire n’ebitaara, noomanya—”

“Kwonka, nikigumizamu ‘ boona bakagaruka kuruga ahariwe bakagaruka ahariiwe ,’” nikwo Alice yaagizire.

“Mbwenu, aho niho bari!” Omugabe akagira atyo omu businguzi, arikworeka ebitaari ebyabaire biri aha meeza. “Tihariho ekirikubaasa kushoboororwa gye kukira ekyo . Reero kandi—‘ atakatungire ekizibu eki —’ okaba otakatungireho kizibu, munywani wangye, ninteekateeka?” akagambira Omugabekazi.

"Tikikabagaho!" Omugabekazi akagira n’ekiniga kingi, arikuteera ekikwato ky’okuhandiikaho erangi aha Nkyenzi obwo arikugamba. (Bill muto owabaire aine omugisha mubi akaba arekire kuhandiika aha rupapura rwe n’orukumu rumwe, nk’oku yaashangire rutaine kamanyiso; kwonka hati akagaruka yaatandika ahonaaho, arikukoresa erangi, eyabaire neesheesheka omu maisho ge, kasita yaabaire neegumaho.)

“Reero ebigambo tibirikuhikaana ,” Omugabe nikwo kugira, arikureeba omu kishaayi okwehinguririza arikumwenya. Hakaba hariho okuhunama okufiire.

“N’ekigambo ky’okushekyerera!” Omugabe yayongyeraho omu iraka ry’okushoberwa, buri omwe yasheka ati, “Reka abaramuzi bateekateekye aha nsharamu yaabo.” Omugabe akagira ati, nk’omurundi gwa makumi abiri eizooba eryo.

“Waapi, waapi!” Omugabekazi nikwo kugira. “Sentensi y’okubanza—ensharamu bwanyima.”

“Ebintu n’ebishuba!” Alice akagira atyo omu iraka rihango. “Ekiteekateeko ky’okubanza kugira sentensi!”

Eki ekibunu kyona kyaimuka omu mwanya, kyaija kyamugurukiraho: akateera akaari kakye, ekicweka ky’okutiina n’ekicweka ky’ekiniga, kandi yaagyezaho kubateera, kandi yaashanga agwejegyeire aha rubaju rw’enyanja, omutwe gwe guri omu kifuba kya munyaanya, owaabaire naasheenya mpora mpora amababi agamwe agafiire ahansi aha maisho ge kuruga omu miti eyaabaire eine ebizimba.

“Imuka, Alice munywani wangye!” munyaanya akagira ati; “Mbwenu, ka ogwejegyeire obwire buraingwa!”

“Oh, ntungire ekirooto ky’okwenda kumanya nk’ekyo!” Alice akagira atyo, kandi yaagambira munyaanya, nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa kubiijuka, Adventures ze zoona ezitarikumanywa ezi obaire nooshomaho; kandi ku yaaherize, munyaanya yaamunywegyera, yaamugira ati, “Kikaba kiri ekirooto ky’okwenda kumanya, munywani wangye, buzima: kwonka hati iruka oze omu chai yaawe; obwire nibukyerererwa.” Nahabwekyo Alice akaimuka yairuka, arikuteekateeka obwo arikwiruka, nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa, ka kyabaire kiri ekirooto kirungi munonga.

Kwonka munyaanya akashutama aho nk’oku yaabaire amurekire, ayesigamiire omutwe gwe aha mukono gwe, arikureeba eizooba nirirenga, kandi arikuteekateeka ahari Alice muto n’Eby’okushemererwa bye byona eby’okutangaaza, kuhitsya obu nawe yaatandikire kuroota bwanyima y’omurembe, kandi eki kikaba kiri ekirooto kye:—

Eky’okubanza, akaroota Alice muto we wenka, kandi omurundi ogundi emikono mikye ekaba ekwatsire aha mavi ge, kandi amaisho garikwaka gye gakaba nigareeba ahaiguru omu ge—akaba naabaasa kuhurira amaraka g’eiraka rye, kandi akareeba okuteera omutwe gwe okukye okw’okutangaaza okurinda enyima eishokye eririkuzengyerera eryaabaire niriija kuguma nirireeba listlean, she who omwanya ogwabaire gumwehinguririize gukaba gurimu amagara n’ebihangirwe ebitarikumanywa ebyabaire biri omu kirooto kya munyaanya omuto.

Obunyaatsi oburaingwa bukaba nibuteera aha bigyere bye obwo Omwana Omweru arikurahuka—Embeba eyaabaire etiinire ekasheesheka omuhanda gwayo kurabira omu kiziba ekyabaire kiri haihi—akaba naabaasa kuhurira okuteera kw’ebikopo by’amajaani obwo Omwana gw’Ekicuncu na banywani be barikugaaniira aha by’okurya byabo ebitarikuhwaho, kandi eiraka ry’Omugabekazi ririkuteera akaari ryabaire niriragiira ngu ayongyereho execu- okusheeshasheesha aha kifuba kya Duchess, obwo eshahani n’eby’okurya birikwehinguririza—ogundi murundi okuyomba kwa Gryphon, okusheeshasheesha kw’ekaraamu y’ebipande bya Lizard, n’okunyiganyiga kw’empunu z’omukishaka ezizibiirwe, bikaijuza omuyaga, bitabwire n’okurira kwa mick miser okwa Turtle okwa hare.

N’ahabw’ekyo akashutamaho, n’amaisho gakingire, kandi ekicweka kimwe kyayeikiriza omu Nsi y’Eby’okutangaaza, n’obu araabe yaabaire naamanya ngu aine kureka okugaruka kubiiguza, kandi byona bikaba nibiija kuhinduka bibe amazima agatarikwetegyerezibwa gye—obunyaatsi bukaba nibwija kuba nibuhuuta omu muyaga gwonka, kandi ekidiba kikaba nikihuuta aha kuhuuta kw’ebishaka—ebikopo by’amaizi g’omugabekazi ebirikucumita bikaba nibiija kuhinduka ebicupuri by’omugabekazi kuhinduka ebicupuri by’omugabekazi. y’omwojo w’omuriisa—n’okusheeshasheesha kw’omwana, okuyomba kwa Gryphon, n’amaraka agandi goona ag’okutangaaza, kukaba nikwija kuhinduka (akaba naamanya) kuhinduka okuyomba okushoberwa okw’omu kishaayi ky’omu faamu ekirimu abantu baingi—obu okuyomba kw’ente omu mwanya oguri hare kurikutwara omwanya gw’okuyomba kw’amaani okwa Mock Turtle.

Eky’aha muheru, akeeshushaniriza oku munyaanya we omwe omuto, omu bwire bw’enyima, we wenka yaabaire naija kuba omukazi mukuru; kandi oku yaabaire naija kuguma aine, omu myaka ye yoona ey’okukura, omutima gwe ogwanguhi kandi ogw’okukunda ogw’obutwana bwe: n’oku yaabaire naija kweteerana aha baana be abandi abato, kandi akareetera amaisho gaabo kujwara gye kandi gaine ekihika n’ebitebyo bingi ebitarikumanywa, obundi n’ekirooto ky’Ensi y’Eby’okutangaaza eya ira: n’oku yaabaire naija kuhurira n’obusaasi bwabo bwona oburikwanguha obw’okwijuka kwe, kandi akatunga okushemererwa kwabo kwona okwanguhi. amagara g’omwana, n’ebiro by’okushemererwa eby’omushana.
