EISHOMO RYA IV.
Omwana gw'akame nigwohereza akapiira kakye

Kikaba kiri Ekinyonyi Ekikye, kirikugaruka enyima mpora mpora, kandi kirikureeba n’okwerarikirira nk’oku kyabaire nikigyenda, nk’oku kiine eki kyafeerwa; kandi akahurira neeyetonganaho “Omugabekazi! Omugabekazi! Oh ebigyere byangye ebirungi! Oh ebyoya byangye n’ebireju byangye! Naija kuntwara kwitwa, nk’oku obuhame nk’oku enkongoro ziri enkongoro! Nkabaasa kuba naziteera nkahi, ninyebuuza?” Alice akateebereza omu kaanya kakye ngu kikaba nikisherura ekifuba n’ebijwaro by’omubiri ebirikwera, kandi omu muringo murungi munonga akatandika kubihiiga, kwonka bikaba bitariho ahu birikureebeka—buri kimwe kikaba nikireebeka nk’ekihindwire kuruga obu yaayoga omu kiziba, kandi ekishengye kihango, ekirimu emeeza y’endabirwamu n’omuryango mukye ogwabaire gujwire vanish, kikaba kiine.

Ahonaaho munonga Omwana gw'entare gukareeba Alice, obwo arikuza kuhiiga, kandi gwamweta omu iraka ry'ekiniga, "Ahabw'enki, Mary Ann, nookora ki aheeru hanu? Iruka oze omuka akaanya aka, kandi ontungye giraavu n'ekifuba! Juba, hati!" Kandi Alice akagira obwoba bwingi munonga yaahunga ahonaaho omu rubaju oru kyayorekire, atagyerizeho kushoboorora enshobe ei kyakozire.

Akagira omu mutima gwe obwo arikwiruka ati: “Akantwara nk’omukozi we w’omu ka.” “Ka naija kutangaara ku araamanye eki ndi! Kwonka nikirungi mmutware ekifuba kye na giraavu—nikwo kugira ngu, ku ndaabe naabaasa kubitunga.” Ku yaabaire naagamba atyo, akahika aha kaju kakye akaboneire, aha rwigi rwayo hakaba hariho ekipande ky’omuringa ekirikwaka gye kandi ekihandiikirweho eiziina “W. RABBIT.” Akataaha atakoona, kandi yairuka yaaza ahaiguru, omu kutiina kwingi ngu atabaasa kubugana Mary Ann ow’amazima, kandi akahindurwa kuruga omu nju atakatungire ekifuba n’ebijwaro bya giraavu.

Alice akagira omu mutima gwe ati: “Ka nikireebeka nk’eky’okutangaaza, kuza kusindikira obutumwa bw’akame! Ninteekateeka ngu Dinah naija kuba naanyoherereza obutumwa oburikukurataho!” Kandi akatandika kuteekateeka aha muringo gw’ekintu ekyabaire nikiija kubaho: “‘Mukazi Alice! Ija hanu butunu, kandi oyetebeekaniise okugyendera kwawe!’ 'Okwija omu dakiika, omurwaire! Kwonka nyine kureeba ngu embeba terikurugayo.' Kwonka tindikuteekateeka,” Alice akagumizamu, “ngu bakaba nibaija kureka Dinah ayemerere omu nju ku eraabe yaatandika kuragiira abantu aha bintu nk’ebyo!”

Omu bwire obu akaba yaatungire omuhanda gwe omu kishengye kikye ekiboneire ekirimu emeeza omu dirisa, kandi aharikyo (nk’oku yaabaire aine amatsiko) hariho ekifuba n’ebijwaro by’entare ebikye bibiri nari bishatu: akatwara ekifuba n’ebijwaro bibiri, kandi akaba naaza kuruga omu kishengye, obu eriisho rye ryagwa aha kacupa kakye akaabaire kari haihi n’endabirwamu. Omurundi ogu hakaba hatariho kihandiiko ekihandiikirweho ebigambo “NYWE,” kwonka n’obu kiraabe kiri kityo akakishumurura yaakita aha minwa ye. "Ninkimanya ngu ekintu ekirikushemeza nikiija kubaho," akagira omu mutima gwe, "buri kurya nari kunywa ekintu kyona; mbwenu ndaza kureeba eki ecupa egi erikukora. Nyine amatsiko ngu eraza kugaruka kunkura, ahabwokuba buzima nruhire kuba ekintu kikye nk'ekyo!"

Kikakora ekyo buzima, kandi juba munonga kukira oku yaabaire naateekateeka: atakanyweire ekicweka ky’ecupa, akashanga omutwe gwe nigunyiganyiga aha mutwe gw’enju, kandi akaba aine kufukama kujuna enshaya ye obutahenda. Akataho ecupa ahansi ahonaaho, arikwegambira ati “Ekyo nikimara kimwe—nyine amatsiko ngu tindigaruka kukura—Nk’oku kiri, tindikubaasa kuruga aha rwigi—ninyenda ngu ntanywaire munonga!”

Kasha! kikaba kyakyererwa munonga kwetenga ekyo! Akagumizamu naakura, kandi naakura, kandi omu bwire bukye munonga akaba aine kufukama ahansi: omu dakiika endiijo hakaba hatariho n’omwanya gw’eki, kandi akagyezaho ekirugire omu kubyama ahansi n’ekikumu kimwe aha rwigi, kandi omukono ogundi gumwezingire aha mutwe. Nabwo akagumizamu naakura, kandi, nk’ekintu ky’aha muheru, akata omukono gumwe aheeru y’edirisa, n’ekigyere kimwe ahaiguru y’omukyeeka, kandi yaagira omu mutima gwe ati “Hati tindikubaasa kugaruka kukora, kyona ekirikubaho .

Ekirungi ahari Alice, akacupa kakye k’amahano hati kakaba kaine omugasho gwako gwona, kandi akaba atarikukura: nabwo kikaba kitari kirungi munonga, kandi, nk’oku kyabaire nikireebeka ngu tihaine muringo gwona gw’omugisha gw’okugaruka kuruga omu kishengye, tikirikutangaaza ngu akahurira ataine mashemererwa.

"Kikaba nikishemeza munonga omuka," Alice omworo akateekateeka, "obu omuntu yaabaire atarikuguma naakura kandi ari mukye, kandi arikuragiirwa okugyendera aha mbeba n'ebinyonyi. Nkaba ndi haihi kwenda ngu ntakaza ahansi omu kiina ekyo ky'ebinyonyi—kandi obwo—kandi obwo—nikiba kiri eky'okutangaaza, noomanya eki ndikubaasa kukora! nkaba nkunda kushoma enfumu, nkaba ninteekateeka ngu ekintu ky’omuringo ogwo tikikabahoga, kandi hati hanu ndi ahagati y’ekimwe! Hashemerire kuba hariho ekitabo ekihandiikirwe ahariinye, ngu hashemerire kuba hariho ekitabo ekihandiikirwe ahariinye, ngu ku ndikukura, ndyahandiika kimwe—kwonka nkuzire hati,” yayongyeraho omu rurimi rw’obusaasi ahari; “haakiri tihaine mwanya gw’okugaruka kukura hanu .”

"Kwonka obwo," Alice akateekateeka, " tindikwija kukura nk'oku ndi hati? Ekyo nikiija kuba okuhuumuriza, omuringo gumwe—okutagaruka kuba omukaikuru/omugurusi—kwonka obwo—buriijo kugira amashomo g'okwega! Oh, ekyo tinkashemerire kukikunda! ”

“Oi, iwe Alice omushema!” akagarukamu wenka. “Noobaasa kwega ota amashomo omunda hanu? Ahabw’enki, tihaine mwanya gw’okushutamamu , kandi tihaine mwanya gwona ogw’ebitabo by’amashomo byona!”

Kandi atyo akagumizamu, yaabanza yakwata orubaju rumwe bwanyima orundi, kandi yaagambaho munonga; kwonka bwanyima y’edakiika nkye akahurira eiraka aheeru, kandi yaayemerera kuhurikiza.

"Maria Ann! Maria Ann!" eiraka eryo rikagira. “Mundeetere giraavu zangye omu kaanya aka!” Bwanyima hakaija okuteera ebigyere bikye aha madaara. Alice akamanya ngu n'Omwana gw'Omwana ogurikwija kumusherura, kandi yaatetema kuhitsya obu yaayehuzya enju, akeebwa kimwe ngu hati akaba ari mukuru nk'emirundi rukumi kukira Omwana gw'Omwana, kandi akaba ataine nshonga yoona y'okugutiina.

Ahonaaho Omwana gw'entare gwaija aha rwigi, rwagyezaho kurukigura; kwonka, orwigi ku rwaigukire omunda, kandi ekikumu kya Alice kyarunyiganyiga munonga, okuteeraho okwo kukahamya ngu kukaremwa. Alice akahurira kyagira omu mutima gwe “Reero ndyaza kwetoorora ntaaha omu dirisa.”

“ Ekyo torikwija kukikora!” Alice akateekateeka, kandi, bwanyima y’okutegyereza kuhitsya obu yaateekateekire ngu akahurira Omwana gw’Omwana ahansi y’edirisa yonka, ahonaaho akajanjaaza omukono gwe, kandi yaakora okunyaga omu mwanya. Tihaine eki yaakwatsire, kwonka akahurira okuyomba okukye n’okugwa, n’okuteera kw’endabirwamu ezicwekire, kuruga omu kuhendera ngu kikaba nikibaasika kwonka ngu ekagwa omu cucumber-frame, nari ekintu ky’omuringo ogwo.

Ekindi hakaija eiraka ry’ekiniga—erya Kame—“Pat! Pat! Ori nkahe?” Kandi obwo eiraka eri yaabaire atakahuriraga, “Buzima obwo ndi aha! Okutimba amapeera, yer honour!”

“Okufukura amapeera, buzima!” akagira ngu Omwana gw’Omwana n’ekiniga. “Hanu! Ija onyambe kuruga omuri eki! ” (Amaraka g’endabirwamu ezindi ezicwekire.)

“Hati gamba, Pat, ekyo n’enki ekiri omu dirisa?”

“Buzima, n’omukono, yer ekitiinisa!” (Akagira ngu “arrum.”)

"Omukono, iwe nzigye! Nooha orareebire gumwe ogw'obuhango nk'obwo? Ahabwenki, nigwijuza edirisa yoona!"

“Buzima, nikikora, yer honour: kwonka n’omukono gw’ebyo byona.”

“Kale, tikiine bizinesi okwo, omu muringo gwona: gyenda okitware!”

Hakaba hariho okusirika okuraingwa bwanyima y’eki, kandi Alice akaba naahurira okuhwehura kwonka hati n’obwire obwo; nk’okugira ngu, “Buzima, tindikukunda, yer honour, na kakye, na kakye!” “Kora nk’oku ndikukugambira, iwe omutiini!” aha muheru yaagaruka yaagorora omukono gwe, yaakora ogundi mukono omu mwanya. Omurundi ogu hakabaho okuyomba kubiri okukye, n’amakuru agandi ag’endabirwamu ezicwekire. “Ka hariho omubaro gw’ebipande bya cucumber ebiine kuba biri bingi!” Alice akateekateeka. "Ninyebuuza eki baraakore ekindi! Ahaby'okunkurura omu dirisa, nkaba ninyenda ngu babaase ! Ndahamya ngu tindikwenda kuguma omunda hanu obwire obundi!"

Akategyereza kumara obwire ataine ekindi eki yaahuriire: aha muheru hakaija okuhoorera kw’obugaari bukye, n’eiraka ry’amaraka maingi marungi goona garikugamba hamwe: akakora ebigambo: “Eidaara erindi riri nkahi?—Ahabw’enki, nkaba ntaine kureeta kureka rimwe; enkoona—Ngaaha, banza ozibohe hamwe—tizirikuhika aha kicweka ky’ahaiguru ekirikumara hati—Otaba ziri ez’omutaano—Hanu, Bill, kwata omurunga ogu ogw’aha mutwe gw’enju—Oh, nigwija ahansi! (okuteera akaari n’eiraka rihango)—“Hati, n’oha owaakozire ekyo?—N’oha Bill, ninteekateeka—N’oha orikuza kushuuma omu nkungu?—Ngaaha, tindikwija ! Iwe okikore!— Ekyo tindikwija, obwo!—Bill n’okushuuma—Hanu, Bill! mukama waawe naagira ngu oine kushuuma omu nkungu!”

"Oh! Mbwenu Bill aine kwija ahansi omukiina, tikwo?" Alice akagira atyo omu mutima gwe. "Enshoni, nibareebeka ngu nibateeka buri kimwe ahari Bill! Tinkaabaire omu mwanya gwa Bill ahabw'okugura ebintu birungi: omuriro ogu nigukye, kuhamya; kwonka ninteekateeka ngu nimbaasa kuteeraho kakye!"

Akakurura ekigyere kye ahansi omu nshonda y’omuriro nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa, kandi yaategyereza okuhitsya obu yaahuriire akanyamaishwa kakye (akaba atarikubaasa kuteebereza ngu kakaba kari omu muringo ki) karikusharara kandi nikagurukaguruka omu nshonda y’omuriro haihi n’ahaiguru ye: reero, arikwegambira wenka “Ogu ni Bill,” akateera ekigyere kimwe ekigumire, kandi yaategyereza kureeba ekirikwija kubaho ekindi.

Ekintu ky’okubanza eki yaahuriire n’ekyeshongoro ky’abantu boona ekya “Hariho Bill!” reero eiraka ry’Omwana gw’Okwanga rigyende—“Mukwate, iwe aha rubaju rw’orugo!” bwanyima okuhunama, reero okushoberwa okundi kw’amaraka—“Imutsya omutwe gwe—Brandy hati—Otamunyiganyiga—Kikaba kita, mugurusi wangye? Niki ekyakuhikireho? Tugambire byona ahari ekyo!”

Eky’aha muheru hakaija eiraka rikye eririkucumita, (“Ogwo ni Bill,” Alice akateekateeka,) “Kale, tindikumanya gye—Tihariho ekindi, mwebare; hati ndi gye—kwonka ndi deal erimu okushoberwa munonga kubaasa kukugambira—eki ndikumanya n’okugira ngu, ekintu nikiija ahariinye nka Jack-in-the-cketes nka skyro go-!”

“N’ahabw’ekyo okakikora, mugurusi!” abandi bakagira bati.

“Twine kwotsya enju!” eiraka ry’Omwana gw’Omwana rikagira; kandi Alice akeeta n’eiraka rihango nk’oku yaabaire naabaasa ati, “Waaba nookikora, ninyija kukuteera Dinah!”

Hakaba hariho okusirika okufiire ahonaaho, kandi Alice akateekateeka omu mutima gwe, "Ninteekateeka eki baraakore ekindi! Ku baraabe baine obwengye bwona, bakaba nibaija kwihaho obusingye." Bwanyima y’edakiika emwe nari ibiri, bakatandika kugaruka kugyendera, kandi Alice akahurira Omwana gw’entare nigugira ati, “Ekigaari nikiija kukora, okutandika.”

“Ekigaari ky’enki? ” Alice akateekateeka; kwonka akaba ataine bwire buraingwa bw’okubanganisa, ahabw’okuba omu kaanya akakuratsireho omuyaga gw’obubaare bukye gukaija niguteera akaari omu dirisa, kandi obumwe bwamuteera omu maisho. Akagira omu mutima gwe ati: “Eki ninza kukimaraho,” kandi yaagamba n’eiraka rihango ati, “Nikirungi otagaruka kukora ekyo!” ekyarugiremu okuhunama okundi okufiire.

Alice akareeba n’okutangaara ngu obubaare bwona bukaba nibuhinduka obukye bwa keeki obwo burikugwejegyera ahansi, kandi ekiteekateeko kirungi kikaija omu mutwe gwe. Akateekateeka ati: “Ku ndaabe naarya kimwe ahari ebi keeki, buzimazima nikiija kuhindura aha buhango bwangye; kandi nk’oku kitarikubaasa kunkora kihango, kiine kunhindura mukye, ninteekateeka.”

N’ahabw’ekyo akamira kimwe aha keeki, kandi akashemererwa munonga kushanga ngu akatandika kukyendeera butoosha. Ahonaaho ku yaabaire ari muto munonga kubaasa kuraba omu rwigi, yairuka yaaruga omu nju, yaashanga ekimbuuro kihango munonga ky’enyamaishwa nkye n’ebinyonyi birindiriire aheeru. Akanyonyi kakye omworo, Bill, kakaba kari ahagati, kakwatsirwe empunu ibiri z’omukishaka, ezaabaire nizikaha ekintu kuruga omu cupa. Boona bakakora orugyendo ahari Alice omu kaanya aku yaareebekire; kwonka akahunga nk'oku yaabaire naabaasa, kandi omu bwire bukye akashangwa aine oburinzi omu kiti kihango.

"Ekintu ky'okubanza eki nyine kukora," Alice akagira omu mutima gwe, obwo arikuzengyerera omukibira, "n'okugaruka kukura kuhika aha buhango bwangye obuhikire; kandi ekya kabiri n'okutunga omuhanda gwangye omu musiri ogwo murungi. Ndateekateeka ngu egyo eraba enteekateeka nungi."

Kikaba nikihurirwa nk’entebeekanisa nungi munonga, hatariho kubangaanisa, kandi kikatebeekanisibwa gye munonga kandi omu muringo gworobi; oburemeezi bwonka bukaba buri, ngu akaba ataine kiteekateeko kikye munonga eky’okukitandikaho; kandi obwo arikureeba omu miti arikwerarikirira, akacupa kakye akaabaire kaine obushaarizi akaabaire kari ahaiguru y’omutwe gwe kakamureetera kureeba ahaiguru omu bwangu bwingi.

Omwana gw’embwa muhango munonga gukaba nigumureeba ahansi n’amaisho mahango ageehinguririize, kandi omu muringo gw’obugobya gukagorora ekigyere kimwe, gurikugyezaho kumukwataho. “Akantu kakye omworo!” Alice akagira atyo, omu iraka ry’okuhabisa, kandi akagyezaho munonga kugiteera empi; kwonka akaba atiina munonga obwire bwona ahabw’okuteekateeka ngu nikibaasa kuba kiine enjara, omu mbeera egyo kikaba nikiija kuba nikibaasa kumurya munonga n’obu araabe yaabaire naamuhabisa munonga.

Ahabw'okugira ngu akaba atamanyire eki yaakozire, akatorotoora akacupa kakye, yaakakwatsa akaana k'embwa; obwo akaana k'embwa kakaguruka omu mwanya kuruga aha bigyere byako byona omurundi gumwe, n'okuyomba kw'okushemererwa, kandi kairuka aha nkoni, kandi kaikiriza ngu nikagiteganisa; obwo Alice yayetantara enyima y’omukyere muhango, ngu atamwirukaho; kandi omu kaanya ako akareebeka aha rubaju orundi, akaana k’embwa kakakora orugyendo orundi aha nkoni, kandi kagwa omutwe aha bigyere omu kwiruka kwako kugikwata; obwo Alice, arikuteekateeka ngu kikaba kiri nk’okugira omuzaano gw’okuzaana n’embaraasi y’akagaari, kandi arikuteekateeka ngu buri kaanya nikaija kuteerwa ahansi y’ebigyere byayo, akagaruka yairuka ayehinguririize omukyere; obwo akaana k’embwa kakatandika omubaro gw’okuteera emishango mikye aha nkoni, karikwiruka omuhanda mukye munonga omu maisho buri murundi n’omuhanda muraingwa kugaruka enyima, kandi karikuborooga omu muringo gw’okuhoorera obwire bwona, kuhitsya obu aha muheru kaashutama hare munonga, kahuuta, orurimi rwako rurikuhanika kuruga omu kanwa kaayo, kandi amaisho gaakyo makuru gakingire kimwe kya kabiri.

Eki kikaba nikireebeka ahari Alice nk’omugisha murungi gw’okumureetera kutoroka; n'ahabw'ekyo akatandika orugyendo ahonaaho, yairuka okuhitsya obu yaabaire aruhire munonga kandi akabura n'omwisyo, kandi okuhitsya obu ekibunu ky'akaana k'embwa kyahurirwa nk'ekikye munonga hare.

“Kandi obwo ka kyabaire kiri akaana kakye akakundwa!” Alice akagira, obwo arikwesigamira aha muti gw’omukyere kuhuumura, kandi arikwefuuha n’ekimwe aha mababi ati: “Nkaba nyine kukunda kukishomesa obukodyo munonga, kuri—ku ndaabe naabaire ndi omu saizi ehikire yonka kukikora! Oh munywani wangye! Nkaba ndi haihi kwebwa ngu nyine kugaruka kukikura! kurya nari kunywa ekintu nari ekindi kwonka ekibuuzo kikuru kiri ngu, niki?”

Ekibuuzo kikuru buzima kikaba kiri, niki? Alice akareeba ebimuri n’ebihimba by’ebishaka byona okwehinguririize, kwonka tarareebire kintu kyona ekyabaire nikishushana n’ekintu ekihikire eky’okurya nari eky’okunywa omu mbeera ezo. Hariho ekizimba kihango ekyabaire nikikura haihi nawe, kiri nk’oburaingwa bwe; kandi ku yaaherize kureeba ahansi yaayo, n'aha mbaju zaayo zombi, n'enyima yaayo, yaateekateeka ngu naija kureeba ebiri aha mutwe gwayo.

Akeegorora aha mutwe gw’ebigyere, kandi yaareeba aha rubaju rw’ekinyonyi, kandi amaisho ge ahonaaho gakabugana ag’ekinyonyi kihango ekya bururu, ekyabaire kishutami ahaiguru emikono yaakyo ekingire, kirikunywa hookah ndaingwa omu bucureezi, kandi kitarikumufaho n’akakye nari ekintu kyona.
