EISHOMO RYA VIII.
Ekishaayi ky'omugabekazi

Omuti muhango gw’ebimuri gukaba gwemereire haihi n’omuryango gw’omusiri: ebimuri ebyabaire nibimera aharigwo bikaba biri eby’erangi erikwera, kwonka hakaba hariho abahingi bashatu aharigwo, barikwejumbira kimwe kubisiiga erangi erikutukura. Alice akateekateeka ngu eki n’ekintu ky’okwenda kumanya munonga, kandi yaaza haihi kubareeba, kandi ku yaabaire naahika aharibo akahurira omwe aharibo arikugira ati, “Reeba hati, Bataano!

“Nkaba ntarikubaasa kwetantara,” Five akagira, omu iraka ry’okushoberwa; “Mushanju akaguruka ekikumu kyangye.”

Aha ki Seven akareeba ahaiguru yagira ati, "Ekyo kihikire, Five! Buriijo ote orubanja aha bandi!"

“ Nikiba kiri kirungi otagamba!” akagira ngu Five. “Nkahurira Omugabekazi arikugira ngu nyomwebazyo yonka oshemerire kusharaho omutwe!”

"Ahabw'enki?" nikwo ogwo owabandize kugamba yaagira.

“Ekyo ti kyawe , Babiri!” nikwo Mushanju yaagira.

“Eego, n’enshonga ye!” Five akagira ati, “kandi ndyamugambira—kikaba kiri ahabw’okureeta omuteeki emizi ya tulip omu mwanya gw’ebitunguru.”

Seven akateera oburashi bwe ahansi, kandi akaba yaaheza kutandika “Kale, aha bintu byona ebitari by’oburingaaniza—” obu eriisho rye ryatunga omugisha gw’okugwa ahari Alice, obwo ayemereire arikubareeba, kandi akeekyebera ahonaaho: abandi nabo bakareeba okwehinguririza, kandi boona bakafukama ahansi.

Alice akagira ati: “Noobaasa kungambira ahabw’enki noosiiga ebimuri ebyo?”

Bataano na Mushanju tibaine eki baagambire, kureka bakareeba Babiri. Babiri bakatandika n'eiraka rikye, "Ahabw'enki amazima gari ngu, nooreeba, Muky., eki aha kikaba kiine kuba kiri omuti gw'orumuri ogurikwiragura , kandi tukatamu ogweru omu nshobi; kandi ku ogire ngu Omugabekazi akaba naija kukimanya, twena tukaba tushemerire kutemwa emitwe yeitu, noomanya. Nahabwekyo nooreeba, Muky. n’okwerarikirira arikureeba aha rubaju orundi orw’omusiri, yaayeta “Omugabekazi! Omugabekazi!” kandi abahingi abo bashatu ahonaaho baayeshuka ahansi omu maisho. Hakaba hariho eiraka ry’ebigyere bingi, kandi Alice akareeba okwehinguririza, aine ekihika ky’okureeba Omugabekazi.

Okubanza hakaija abaserukare ikumi abaabaire baine enkoni; ebi byona bikaba biine omubiri nk'ogw'abahinguzi bashatu, biri ebiraingwa kandi bigumire, emikono yaabyo n'ebigyere byabyo biri aha nshonda: hakurataho abakozi ikumi ab'omu kikaari; ebi bikaba bijwekirwe amabaare g'omuhendo hoona, kandi bikaba bigyendera bibiri bibiri, nk'oku abaserukare baabaire bakora. Bwanyima y'abo hakaija abaana b'abagabe; bakaba bari ikumi, kandi abakundwa abato bakaija nibagurukaguruka barikushemererwa bari omu ngaro, omu babiri babiri: boona bakaba bajwekirwe emitima. Ekindi hakaija abagyenyi, abaingi bakaba bari Abagabe n’Abagabekazi, kandi omuri abo Alice akamanya Omwana gw’Okwanga Omweru: gukaba nigugamba omu muringo gw’okutiina ogw’ahonaaho, gumwenya ahari buri kimwe ekyabaire nikigambwa, kandi gukahinguraho gutamumanyire. Bwanyima hakakuratira Omunyabwengye w’Emitima, ayekoreire engure y’Omugabe aha rubaaho rwa velvet orurikwiragura; kandi, aha muheru gw'omukoro ogu omuhango gwona, hakaija OMUGABE N'OMUGABEKI W'EMITIMA.

Alice akaba aine okubanganisa okukye yaaba atashemerire kubyama ahansi nk’abahingi bashatu, kwonka akaba atarikwijuka ngu akahurira ekiragiro nk’ekyo omu mikoro y’okugyenderamu; “kandi okwihaho ekyo, okugyendera omu muhanda kukaba nikwija kuba nikugasira ki,” akateekateeka, “abantu ku baraabe baine byona by’okubyama aha maisho, ngu batabaasa kukireeba?” N'ahabw'ekyo akeemerera ahu yaabaire ari, yaategyereza.

Omukoro ku gwahikire aha rubaju rwa Alice, boona bakemerera bamureeba, Omugabekazi yagira n’obushaarizi ati “Ogu n’oha?” Akakigambira Omunyabwengye w’Emitima, we yaafukama kwonka yaamwenya omu kugarukamu.

"Sitani!" Omugabekazi akagira atyo, arikuteera omutwe ataine kugumisiriza; kandi, yaahindukira Alice, yaagumizamu ati, “Eiziina ryawe ni riiha, mwana?”

“Eiziina ryangye ni Alice, mbwenu nshemererwe Omukama waawe,” Alice akagira omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa munonga; kwonka akayongyeraho, omu mutima gwe, "Ahabw'enki, n'ekipapura kya kaada kyonka, bwanyima y'ebyo byona. Tindikwetenga kubitiina!"

“Kandi aba n’oha? ” Omugabekazi nikwo kugira, arikworeka abahingi bashatu ababaire bagwejegyeire beehinguririize omuti gw’orumuri; ahakuba, nooreeba, nk’oku baabaire bagwejegyeire aha maisho, kandi n’omuringo gw’aha migongo gwabo gukaba guri nk’ogw’ekibunu ekisigaireho, akaba atarikubaasa kumanya yaaba bari abahingi b’emisiri, nari abaserukare, nari abakozi b’omu kikaari, nari abaana be bashatu.

“ Nshemerire kumanya nta?” Alice akagira atyo, atangaire ahabw’obumanzi bwe wenka. “Ti nshonga yangye .”

Omugabekazi akahinduka omutukura ahabw’ekiniga, kandi, bwanyima y’okumureeba amaisho kumara akaire kakye nk’ekinyamaishwa, akateera orwaari ati “Off n’omutwe gwe! Off—”

“Eby’obushema!” Alice akagira atyo, omu iraka rihango munonga kandi omu kucwamu, kandi Omugabekazi akahunama.

Omugabe akata omukono gwe aha mukono gwe, kandi n’okutiina akagira ati “Teekateeka, mukundwa wangye: n’omwana muto wenka!”

Omugabekazi akahinduka aine ekiniga, yaamurugaho, yagambira Omunyabwengye ati “Bahindure!”

Omunyabwengye akakikora, n’obwegyendesereza bwingi, n’ekigyere kimwe.

"Eimuka!" Omugabekazi akagira, omu iraka ry’okushekyerera, ry’amaani, kandi abahingi bashatu ahonaaho bakaguruka, batandika kufukamira Omugabe, Omugabekazi, abaana b’obugabe, n’abandi boona.

“Ekyo mukireke!” Omugabekazi akagamba n’eiraka rihango. “Noondeetera okuhurira.” Kandi obwo, yaahindukira aha muti gw’orumuri, yaagumizamu ati, “ Obaire nookora ki aha?”

“Kishemeze Omukama waawe,” Two akagira, omu iraka ry’obucureezi bwingi, arikushuuma ahansi aha kifuba kimwe obwo arikugamba, “tukaba nitugyezaho—”

" Ndareba !" Omugabekazi, owaabaire omu bwire obwo atwire naashwijuma ebimuri bya rose, nikwo yaagizire. “Baiheho n’emitwe yaabo!” kandi orugyendo rwagumizamu, abaserukare bashatu bakasigara enyima kwita abahingi b’emisiri abatari b’omugisha, abairukire ahari Alice kutunga oburinzi.

“Torikwija kusharaho omutwe!” Alice akagira atyo, yaabita omu nyungu mpango y’ebimuri eyaabaire eyemereire haihi. Abaserukare bashatu abo bakazengyerera kumara edakiika emwe nari ibiri, barikubasherura, reero baarugayo busha barikukuratira abandi.

“Emitwe yaabo eihirweho?” Omugabekazi akagamba n’eiraka rihango.

“Emitwe yaabo ehweireho, kyaba nikishemeza Mukama waawe!” abaserukare bakagamba n’eiraka rihango omu kugarukamu.

“Ekyo kihikire!” Omugabekazi akagamba n’eiraka rihango. “Noobaasa kuzaana omupiira gw’ebigyere?”

Abaserukare bakahunama, kandi baareeba Alice, nk’oku kirikureebeka ngu ekibuuzo kikaba kigyendereirwe we.

"Yego!" Alice akagamba n’eiraka rihango.

“Ija, mbwenu!” akagamba n’eiraka rihango Omugabekazi, kandi Alice nawe ayegaita aha ruguuto, arikwebuuza munonga ekyabaire nikiija kubaho bwanyima.

“Ni—n’ekiro kirungi munonga!” eiraka ry’okutiina aha rubaju rwe rikagira. Akaba aratambura aharubaju rw'Okwame Omweru, owabaire naamureeba omu maisho n'okwerarikirira.

Alice akagira ati: “Buzima munonga——Duchess ari nkahe?”

"Hunama! Huramu!" akagira Omwana omu iraka ry’ahansi kandi eririkurahuka. Akareeba aha ibega rye n’okwerarikirira obwo arikugamba, reero yaayeimutsya aha mutwe gw’ebigyere, yaata akanwa ke haihi n’okutu kwe, kandi yaamugambira omu iraka ry’ahansi ati “Ari ahansi y’ekifubiro ky’okwitwa.”

"Ahabw'enki?" Alice nikwo arikugira.

“Okagira ngu ‘Ka n’eky’obusaasi!’?” Omwana gw’akame akabuuza.

Alice akagira ati: “Ngaaha, tinkakimanyire: “Tindikuteekateeka ngu n’eky’obusaasi na kakye, nkagira ngu ‘N’ahabw’enki?’”

“Akateera amatu g’Omugabekazi omu bibokisi—” Omwana gwatandika. Alice akateera akaari kakye k’okusheka. "Oi, muhure!" Omwana gw’akame akagamba omu iraka ry’okutiina. "Omugabekazi naija kukuhurira! Nooreeba, akaija akyererirwe munonga, kandi Omugabekazi akagira—"

“Muze omu myanya yaanyu!” Omugabekazi akagamba n’eiraka ry’enkuba, abantu baatandika kwiruka omu nshonda zoona, barikuteerana; kwonka bakatuuraho omu dakiika emwe nari ibiri, reero omuzaano gwatandika. Alice akateekateeka ngu akaba atakareebaga ekishaayi ky’okuteeraho croquet ekirikushemeza nk’ekyo omu magara ge; byona bikaba biri ebifunjo n'ebifunjo; emipiira ekaba eri enkongoro ezihuriire, emipira ekaba eri enkongoro ezihuriire, kandi abaserukare bakaba baine kwehindura kabiri kandi bakeemerera aha ngaro n’ebigyere, kukora ebifunjo.

Oburemeezi obukuru obu Alice yaashangire omu kubanza bukaba buri omu kuhabura flamingo ye: akasingura omu kutunga omubiri gwayo gusherekirwe, omu muringo oguhikire, ahansi y’omukono gwe, n’amaguru gaayo gahanikire ahansi, kwonka okutwarira hamwe, nk’oku yaabaire aine enshaya yaayo egoroire gye, kandi akaba naaza kuha hedgehog omutwe gwe ahaiguru, n’okugiteera omu maisho n’okugiteera omu maisho. ekigambo ky’okushoberwa nk’ekyo akaba atarikubaasa kwetantara okusheka: kandi ku yaabaire yaaheza kutunga omutwe ahansi, kandi ariyo naaza kugaruka kutandika, kikaba nikimuteganisa munonga kushanga ngu enkongoro ekaba eyezinguriire, kandi eri omu kikorwa ky’okuguruka hare: okwihaho ebi byona, hakaba hariho okutwarira hamwe omugyera nari omugyera wanted omu muhanda ahu yaabaire naaza, abaserukare abaabaire baine emirundi ebiri bakaba baguma baimuka baza omu bicweka ebindi eby’omu itaka, Alice akahika aha kuteekateeka ngu buzima gukaba guri omuzaano gugumire munonga.

Abazaani boona bakaba bazaana omurundi gumwe batarikutegyereza okuhindura, barikutongana obwire obwo bwona, kandi barikurwanira ebinyonyi; kandi omu bwire bukye munonga Omugabekazi akaba ari omu kifuba ky’ekiniga kingi, kandi akagyenda naateera ebigyere, kandi arikuteera akaari ati “Off with his head!” nari “Off n’omutwe gwe!” nk’omurundi gumwe omu dakiika.

Alice akatandika kuhurira ataine busingye bwingi: kuhamya, akaba atakatungire nshonga yoona n'Omugabekazi, kwonka akaba naamanya ngu nikibaasa kubaho omu dakiika yoona, "kandi bwanyima," akateekateeka, "ekirikubaasa kubaho ahariinye? Nibakunda munonga okushara abantu emitwe hanu; eky'okutangaaza kihango n'okugira ngu hariho owaasigaireho ohuriire!"

Akaba ariyo nasherura omuringo gw’okutoroka, kandi arikwebuuza yaaba naabaasa kuhunga atarikureebwa, obu yaareeba okureebeka kw’okwenda kumanya omu mwanya: kikamutangaaza munonga omu kubanza, kwonka, bwanyima y’okukireeba edakiika emwe nari ibiri, akakikora nk’okumwenya, kandi akeegambira wenka “Tihariho Cashire I shak to have some.”

“Origyenda ota?” akagira ngu Kapu, ahonaaho ku haabaire habaho akanwa akarikumara kugamba nakyo.

Alice akategyereza kuhitsya obu amaisho gaareebeka, reero yaayehuzya. Akateekateeka ati: “Tihariho mugasho kukigambaho okuhitsya obu amatu gaakyo garaije, nari haakiri kimwe aharibyo.” Omu dakiika endiijo omutwe gwona gukareebeka, reero Alice yaataho flamingo ye, yatandika akatabo k’omuzaano, arikuhurira ashemereirwe munonga ngu aine omuntu orikumuhurikiza. Empunu ekashusha ngu neeteekateeka ngu hati hariho ekirikumara ekirikureebeka, kandi tihaine ekindi ekyareebeka.

“Tindikuteekateeka ngu nibazaana omu muringo gw’oburingaaniza na kakye,” Alice akatandika, omu iraka ry’okwetomboita, “kandi boona nibatongana omu muringo gw’akabi munonga omuntu tarikubaasa kwehurira arikugamba—kandi nibareebeka nk’abataine biragiro byona omu muringo gw’omutaano; haakiri, ku biraabe biriho, tihaine orikubareeberera—kandi toine kiteekateeko aha ku ebintu byona birikuteganisa; Nshemerire kuraba omu bindi ebirikukurataho ndikugyendera aha rubaju orundi orw’itaka—kandi nkaba nyine kuteera enkongoro y’Omugabekazi hati, kwonka ekahunga ku yaareebire eyange neeija!”

“Nokunda ota Omugabekazi?” akagira ngu Kapu omu iraka rikye.

“Tikwo kiri na kakye,” Alice akagira: “she’s so extremely—” Omu bwire obwo bwonka akareeba ngu Omugabekazi akaba ari haihi enyima ye, arikuhurikiza: n’ahabw’ekyo akagumizamu, “—nikibaasa kusinga, ngu tikiine mugasho munonga obwo arikuheza omuzaano.”

Omugabekazi akamwenya kandi yahinguraho.

“ Nogamba na oha?” Omugabe akagira atyo, arikuza ahaiguru ahari Alice, kandi arikureeba omutwe gwa Kapu n’okwenda kumanya kwingi.

Alice akagira ati: “N’omunywani wangye—Ekikaari kya Cheshire: “nyikiriza nkiyanjurire.”

Omugabe akagira ati: “Tindikukunda oku kirikureebeka na kakye, kwonka nikibaasa kunnywegyera omukono kyaba nikikunda.”

“Nkaabaire ninkunda ngu ntakikora,” Kapu ekagira.

Omugabe akagira ati: “Otagira ekiniga, kandi otandeeba otyo!” Akahika enyima ya Alice obwo arikugamba.

Alice akagira ati: “Empunu neebaasa kureeba omugabe.” “Ekyo nkakishoma omu kitabo kimwe, kwonka tindikwijuka ahu naakishomire.”

"Kale, kiine kwihwaho," Omugabe akagira omu kucwamu kwingi, kandi yaayeta Omugabekazi, owaabaire naahingura omu bwire obwo, "Munywani wangye! Nkayenzire ngu ekikaari eki kiihirweho!"

Omugabekazi akaba aine omuringo gumwe gwonka gw’okushoboorora oburemeezi bwona, obuhango nari obukye. “Off n’omutwe gwe!” akagira atyo, ataine n’okureeba okwehinguririza.

Omugabe akagira n’ekihika ati: “Ndyareeta omuhiigi nyowe nyenka,” kandi yaarugayo ahonaaho.

Alice akateekateeka ngu nabaasa kugaruka enyima, akareeba oku omuzaano gwabaire nigugyenda omumaisho, obwo arikuhurira eiraka ry'Omugabekazi hare, arikuteera orwaari ahabw'ekihika. Akaba yaaheza kuhurira ekibonerezo kye abazaani bashatu kwitwa ahabw’okubura omurundi gwabo, kandi akaba atarikukunda oku ebintu byabaire nibireebeka na kakye, ahabw’okuba omuzaano gukaba guri omu kushoberwa kwingi munonga ngu akaba atarikubaasa kumanya yaaba n’omurundi gwe nari ngaaha. N’ahabw’ekyo akaza kusherura enkongoro ye.

Enkongoro ekaba eri omu kurwana n’enkongoro endiijo, ekyabaire nikireebeka ahari Alice nk’omugisha murungi gw’okuteera emwe aharizo n’eyindi: oburemeezi bwonka bukaba buri, ngu flamingo ye ekaba yaaza aha rubaju orundi orw’omusiri, ahu Alice yaabaire naabaasa kugireeba neegyezaho omu muringo ogutarikwebaasa kuguruka omu muti.

Obwire obu yaabaire akwatsire flamingo akagigarurayo, orutaro rukaba rwahwa, kandi enkongoro zombi zikaba zitarikureebwa: “kwonka tikiine nshonga nyingi,” Alice akateekateeka, “nk’oku obukomooko bwona bwahwaho kuruga aha rubaju oru orw’ensi.” N’ahabw’ekyo akagishereka ahansi y’omukono gwe, ngu etagaruka kutoroka, yaagaruka yaagaruka kugaaniira na munywani we kakye.

Ku yaagarukire aha Cheshire Cat, akatangaara kushanga abantu baingi munonga beeteeraine beehinguririize: hakaba hariho okuhakana okwabaire nikugyenda omu maisho ahagati y’omuhiigi, Omugabe, n’Omugabekazi, boona baabaire nibagamba omurundi gumwe, obwo abandi boona baabaire bahunami munonga, kandi barikureebeka nk’abatashemereirwe munonga.

Omu kaanya ako Alice yaareebeka, akashabwa boona bashatu kushoboorora ekibuuzo, kandi bakagaruka bamugambira enshonga zaabo, kwonka, boona ku baabaire nibagamba omurundi gumwe, akashanga kigumire munonga buzima kumanya eki baagambire kyonyini.

Enshonga y’omuhiigi ekaba eri, ngu torikubaasa kusharaho omutwe okwihaho haaba hariho omubiri gw’okugusharaho: ngu akaba ataine kukora ekintu nk’ekyo ira, kandi akaba atarikuza kutandika omu bwire bwe obw’amagara.

Enshonga y'Omugabe ekaba eri, ngu ekintu kyona ekiine omutwe nikibaasa kutemwa, kandi ngu toine kugamba ebishuba.

Enshonga y'Omugabekazi ekaba eri, ngu haaba hariho ekitakozirweho omu bwire bukye munonga akaba naija kugira ngu buri omwe aitwe, okwetoorora kwona. (Ekigambo eki eky’aha muheru nikyo kyabaire kireeteire ekibiina kyona kureebeka nk’ekirikuteganisa munonga kandi ekiine okwerarikirira.)

Alice akaba atarikubaasa kuteekateeka ekindi kyona eky’okugamba kureka “N’ekya Duchess: nikirungi omubuuze ahari ekyo.”

“Ari omu kihome,” Omugabekazi akagambira omuhiigi ati: “mureete hanu.” Kandi omuhiigi akagyenda nk'omusyo.

Omutwe gwa Kapu gukatandika kuzima omu kaanya aku yaabaire atakibaho, kandi, obu yaabaire agarukire na Duchess, gukaba guhweireho kimwe; n'ahabw'ekyo Omugabe n'omuhiigi bakairuka munonga barikuza ahaiguru n'ahansi barikugisherura, obwo abasigaire omu kibiina bakagaruka omu muzaano.
