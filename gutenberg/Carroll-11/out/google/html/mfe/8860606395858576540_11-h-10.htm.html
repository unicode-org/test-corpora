SAPIT X.
Kadril Homar

Moke Torti-la finn soupire for, e finn desinn ledo enn flaper lor so lizie. Li finn get Alice, e finn sey koze, me pandan enn-de minit bann plore finn toufe so lavwa. “Koumadir li ti ena enn lezo dan so lagorz,” Grifon-la dir: e li finn koumans travay pou sakouy li ek donn li enn koutpwin dan ledo. Finalman Moke Torti-la finn repran so lavwa, e, avek larm ki pe koule lor so lazou, li finn kontinie ankor:—

“Kitfwa ou pa finn viv boukou anba lamer—” (“Mo pa finn viv,” Alice finn dir)—“e kitfwa zame ou finn mem introdir enn langre—” (Alice finn koumans dir “Enn fwa mo finn gout—” me li finn verifie limem vit-vit, e finn dir “Non, zame”) “—alor ou pa kapav ena okenn lide ki enn zafer delisye enn Lobster Quadrille ete!”

“Non, vremem,” Alice finn dir. “Ki kalite ladans sa?”

“Kifer,” Grifon-la finn dir, “ou dabor form enn laliyn lor lakot lamer—”

“De lalinn!” Moke Torti finn kriye. “Fok, torti, somon, ek lezot ankor; lerla, kan to finn tir tou bann dizef lor sime—”

“Zeneralman sa pran inpe letan,” Grifon-la finn interonp li.

“—ou avanse de fwa—”

“Sakenn avek enn langre kouma enn partner!” Grifon-la finn kriye.

“Biensir,” Mock Turtle dir: “avans de fwa, met lor bann partner—”

“—sanz langre, ek pran retret dan mem lord,” Grifon-la kontinie.

“Apre, to kone,” Torti-la kontinie, “to zet—”

“Bann langre!” Grifon-la finn kriye, avek enn kout fwet dan ler.

“—otan ki to kapav dan lamer—”

“Naz deryer zot!” Grifon-la finn kriye.

“Fer enn somersault dan lamer!” ti kriye Torti Moke, li ti pe fer letour dan enn fason sovaz.

“Sanz langre ankor!” Grifon-la finn kriye for-for.

“Retourn lor later ankor, e se tou premie sif,” Mock Turtle ti dir, enn kout li ti bes so lavwa; e sa de kreatir-la, ki ti pe sot-sote kouma bann fou toutlong sa letan-la, finn re-asize bien tris ek trankilman, e finn get Alice.

“Sa bizin enn bien zoli ladans,” Alice finn dir timidman.

“Eski ou ti pou kontan trouv inpe ladan?” Moke Torti-la finn dir.

“Vremem bien,” Alice finn dir.

“Vini, anou sey premie figir!” Moke Torti finn dir ar Grifon. “Nou kapav fer san langre, ou kone. Kisannla pou sante?”

“Ah, to sante,” Grifon-la dir. “Mo finn bliye bann mo.”

“Mersi, se enn ladans bien interesan pou gete,” Alice finn dir, santi li bien kontan ki li finn fini anfin: “e mo bien kontan sa sante kirye lor blansisaz-la!”

“O, konsernan bann blansiman,” Mock Turtle ti dir, “zot—ou finn trouv zot, biensir?”

“Wi,” Alice finn dir, “mo finn trouv zot souvan dan dine—” li finn verifie limem vit-vit.

“Mo pa kone kot Dinn kapav ete,” Moke Torti dir, “me si ou finn trouv zot souvan, biensir ou kone kouma zot ete.”

“Mo kwar sa,” Alice finn reponn avek reflexion. “Zot ena zot lake dan zot labous—ek zot partou lor bann ti bout dipin.”

“To ena tor lor bann ti bout dipin,” Mok Torti ti dir: “bann ti bout ti pou al dan lamer. Me zot ena zot lake dan zot labous; e rezon se—” isi Mok Torti ti baye e ti ferm so lizie.—“Dir li rezon ek tousala,” li ti dir Grifon.

“Rezon se,” Grifon ti dir, “ki zot ti pou al avek bann langre dan ladans. Alor zot finn zet zot dan lamer. Alor zot finn bizin tom bien lwin. Alor zot finn gagn zot lake vit dan zot labous. Alor zot pa finn kapav tir zot ankor. Se tou.”

“Mersi,” Alice ti dir, “sa bien interesan. Zame mo ti konn otan zafer lor enn blansisaz avan.”

“Mo kapav dir ou plis ki sa, si ou anvi,” Grifon-la finn dir. “To kone kifer apel li enn blansiman?”

Alice ti dir: “Zame mo’nn pans sa. "Kifer?"

“ Li fer bann bot ek soulie ,” Grifon-la reponn bien solanelman.

Alice ti bien etone. “Eski bann bot ek soulie!” li finn repete dan enn ton etone.

“Kifer, ar ki to soulie finn fer?” Grifon-la finn dir. “Mo pe rod dir, ki fer zot briyan koumsa?”

Alice finn get zot anba, e finn reflesi inpe avan li donn so repons. “Zot finn fini ar nwar, mo kwar.”

“Bot ek soulie anba lamer,” Grifon-la kontinie avek enn lavwa profon, “finn fini avek enn blansiman. Aster to kone.”

“E ki zot finn fer?” Alice demande avek enn ton bien kiryozite.

“Sol ek anguille, biensir,” Grifon-la reponn avek ase enpasians: “ninport ki krevet ti kapav dir twa sa.”

“Si mo ti sa blan-la,” Alice dir, ki so panse ti ankor pe pans lor sante-la, “mo ti pou dir marlin-la, ‘Res deryer, silteple: nou pa anvi twa avek nou!’”

“Zot ti oblize ena li avek zot,” Mock Turtle ti dir: “okenn pwason saz pa ti pou al okenn plas san enn marsupil.”

“Eski li pa pou vremem?” Alice finn dir avek enn ton bien etone.

“Biensir non,” Mock Turtle ti dir: “kifer, si enn pwason vinn ver mwa , e dir mwa li pe al fer enn vwayaz, mo bizin dir ‘Avek ki marsupil?’”

“To pa pe rod dir 'bi'?” Alice finn dir.

“Mo pe rod dir seki mo pe dir,” Moke Torti-la reponn avek enn ton ofanse. Ek Grifon-la finn azoute “Vini, anou tann inpe ou bann lavantir.”

“Mo ti kapav rakont ou mo bann lavantir—apartir zordi gramatin,” Alice finn dir inpe timidman: “me li pa servi nanye retourn yer, parski mo ti enn lot dimounn sa lepok-la.”

“Explik tousala,” Moke Torti dir.

“Non, non! Bann lavantir avan,” Grifon-la dir dan enn ton enpasian: “bann explikasion pran enn letan terib.”

Alor Alice finn koumans rakont zot so bann lavantir depi lepok kot li finn trouv Lapin Blan pou premie fwa. Li ti inpe nerve akoz sa zis o-koumansman, sa de kreatir-la ti vinn telman pre ar li, enn sak kote, e ti ouver zot lizie ek labous telman gran, me li ti gagn kouraz amezir li ti kontinie. So bann ekouter ti parfetman trankil ziska ki li finn ariv dan parti kot li ti pe repet “ To vie, Per William ,” ar Caterpillar, e bann mo tou ti pe vinn diferan, e lerla Mock Turtle ti tir enn long souf, e ti dir “Sa bien kirye.”

“Tousala li osi kirye ki li kapav ete,” Grifon-la finn dir.

“Tou finn vinn diferan!” Moke Torti-la finn repete avek reflexion. “Mo ti pou kontan tann li sey repet enn zafer aster. Dir li koumanse.” Li finn get Grifon-la koumadir li ti panse ki li ti ena enn sertenn lotorite lor Alice.

“Deboute ek repet '' Se lavwa pares ,'” Grifon-la finn dir.

“Kouma bann kreatir-la komann enn, e fer enn repet bann leson!” Alice finn panse; “Mo kapav osi bien ki mo dan lekol toutswit.” Sepandan, li finn leve, e finn koumans repet li, me so latet ti telman ranpli ar Lobster Quadrille, ki li pa ti kone ki li ti pe dir, e bann mo-la ti vinn bien drol:—

“‘Se lavwa Omar; mo finn tann li deklare,
“To finn kwi mwa tro maron, mo bizin met disik dan mo seve.”
Kouma enn kanar ar so papie lizie, koumsamem li ar so nene

“Sa li diferan ar seki mo ti abitie dir kan mo ti zanfan,” Grifon la dir.

“Be, zame mo finn tann sa avan,” Mok Torti finn dir; “me li paret enn kouyonad inkomen.”

Alice pa finn dir nanye; li ti finn asize avek so figir dan so lame, li ti pe demann limem si zame kiksoz ti pou arive dan enn fason natirel ankor.

“Mo ti pou kontan ki explik li,” Mock Turtle finn dir.

“Li pa kapav explik sa,” Grifon-la dir prese-prese. “Kontign avek prosin verse.”

“Me lor so ledwa lipie?” Moke Torti-la finn persiste. “Kouma li finn kapav tir zot avek so nene, ou kone?”

“Se premie pozision dan ladans.” Alice finn dir; me ti bien dekonserte par sa zafer-la, e ti anvi sanz size-la.

“Wi, mo panse ki to bizin ale,” Grifon-la dir: e Alice ti tro kontan pou fer sa.
