“Miɗo hula ko miin, joom suudu am,” Alice wiyi; “Mi waawataa siftorde geɗe no kuutortonoo-mi nii—e mi jogaaki mawneeki gootol fotde hojomaaji sappo e nder heen!”

“Waawaa siftorde hol geɗe?” » Ɓiɗɗo gorko wiyi.

“Eeh, mi etinooma wiyde “Hol no beeli tokoosi busy,” kono fof ari ko seerti!” Alice jaabii e daande mettunde no feewi.

“Fuɗɗito, ‘ Aɗa mawni, Baaba Wiliyam ’” , Caterpillar wiyi.

Alice fawi juuɗe mum, fuɗɗii:—

“A mawnii, baaba Wiliyam,” suka oo wi’i, .
“Kadi gaasa maa ina danee no feewi;
E tee aɗa darii e hoore maa haa abada—
Mbele aɗa sikki, e duuɓi maa, ina selli?”

“E nder cukaagu am,” Baaba Wiliyam jaabii ɓiyiiko, .
“Mi hulii ina waawi bonnude ngaandi;
Kono, jooni nde miɗo laaɓndii mi alaa hay gooto,

“Aɗa mawni,” suka oo wiyi, “no mbiyru-mi nii, .
Kadi ɓe mawni ko ɓuri heewde e fat;
Kono a wayliima caggal-somersault e nder damal—

“E nder cukaagu am,” wiyi ganndo oo, ina ɓuuɓna gite mum daneeje ɗee,
“Miɗo joginoo terɗe am fof ina ɓuuɓna no feewi
E kuutoragol ngolɗoo nebam—shilling gooto e kees oo—
Jaɓ mi yeeyde ma jom suudu?”

Suka oo wi’i: “A mawnii, kadi jawdi maa ina famɗi no feewi
Ngam kala ko ɓuri suet tiiɗde;
Kono a timminii goose, e ƴiye e becce—
Duwaawu, hol no keɓirɗaa waɗde ɗum?”

Baaba makko wiyi : “e nder cukaagu am, mi ƴettii sariya, .
E luurdude e kala haala e debbo am;
E semmbe muscle, mo nde rokki jawdi am,
Duumii ko heddii e nguurndam am.”

Suka oo wi’i: “Aɗa mawni, neɗɗo ina waawi sikkude
Nde gite maa ina tiiɗi no feewi;
Kono a balansii eel e dow ŋoral nofru maa—
Hol ko waɗi ma haaju no feewi?”

“Mi jaabiima naamne tati, ɗum noon ina heddi,” .

Mbele aɗa sikki miɗo waawi heɗaade ñalnde kala ko wayi nii?

-Ɗum noon haaldaaka no haanirta nii, -Catal wiyi.

“Aɗa weltii jooni?” » Ɓiɗɗo gorko wiyi.

“Ko tooweendi moƴƴiri no feewi tigi!” » Ɓiɗɗo gorko oo wiyi e tikkere, ina ummina hoore mum ina tiiɗi no haaldirta nii (ina toowi no feewi santimeeteeruuji tati).

“Kono mi meeɗaani ɗum!” » Alice ñaagii miskiino oo e daande yurmeende. O miijoyii e hoore makko, “Miɗo yiɗi tagooje ɗee mbaasa mettinde no feewi!”

“Aɗa huutoroo ɗum e sahaa,” wiyi Catal; nde waɗti hoodere ndee e hunuko mayre, nde fuɗɗii ɓuuɓde kadi.

Oo sahaa Alice ina muñani haa nde suɓii haalde kadi. E nder hojomaaji seeɗa walla ɗiɗi, Ɓoornugol ngol yaltini hoodere ndee e hunuko mum, ngol yani laabi ɗiɗi, ngol ɓuuɓtii hoore maggol. Ndeen nde jippiima e 6uu6ol, nde 6uuccii e nder huɗo, nde teskiima tan nde yahrata, “Bannge gooto ina mawna, bannge goɗɗo oo ina juuta.”

“Bannge gooto e hol? Bannge goɗɗo oo e hol? ” miijo Alice e hoore mum.

“Ko e ɓuuɓri” , wiyi Ɓiɗɗo gorko oo, hono no o naamniri ɗum e daande toownde nii; e nder dumunna goɗɗo kadi nde yalti e gite.

Alice heddii ina ndaara 6uu6ol ngol e miijo mum, ina etoo faamde hol banngeeji cficfi cfii; e nde wonnoo ko 6uu6ol timmungol, o tawi cfuum ko naamnal caɗtungal no feewi. Kono, haa jooni o 6uuccii juucfe makko e maggal haa 6e njahdi, o 6uuccii heen seeɗa e 6uu6ol ngol e junngo kala.

Ɓoggi makko ina 6uuccii e koyngal makko, haa alaa ɗo o udditirta hunduko makko; kono o waɗi ɗum haa laaɓi, o dañii ɓuuɓnude ɓuuɓri ƴiiƴam nano.

“Ngaree, hoore am ina woppi haa jooni!” » Alice wiyi e daande weltaare, nde wayliima wonti kulhuli e sahaa goɗɗo, nde o tawi ko juuɗe makko ngalaa ɗo nji’ata: ko o yi’ata tan, nde o ndaari les, ko njuuteendi daande mawnde, ina wayi no ina ummoo no ɓuuɓri yaltiri e maayo leɗɗe daneeje gonɗe les makko.

“Hol ko oon geɗel ngel fof waawi wonde?” » Alice wiyi. “E hol to juuɗe am njottii ? E oh, juuɗe am miskineeɓe, hol no mbaawir mi yi’de ma?” O woni e diwde cfe no o haaldirta nii, kono alaa ko 6eydi heen so wonaa 6uu6ol seecfa e nder leppi daneeji wocfcfi.

O dañii tan ko ɓuuɓnude ɗum les e nder zigzag mbelɗo, omo yaha omo diwa e nder leɗɗe, ɗe o tawi alaa ko woni so wonaa topirɗe leɗɗe ɗe o ƴaañii ɗee, nde tawnoo ɓuuɓol tiiɗngol waɗi mo ruttaade caggal e yaawre: ɓuuɓol mawngol ina fiyra yeeso makko, ina fija e makko no feewi.
