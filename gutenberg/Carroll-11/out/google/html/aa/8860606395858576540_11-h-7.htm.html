Qari foocal caxa gubat daffeyteh tan miiditte sugteeh, March Hare kee Hatter shaahi luk sugeenim: Dormouse ken fanat daffeyak sugeeh, sissikuk xiinit sugeeh, gersi namma num kaadu barra elle hayya heenih, sinni qanxaffe luk sugeenim kee tet amoh addal yaabak sugeenim. “Dormouse” kaxxaam taqabi le” iyyeh Alice; "dubuk, xiinih yaniimih taagah, tuhinna akkale."

tarbeeza kaxxa tiyak sugte, takkay immay sidoc inkih ittat qoxximteh sugte inki gonat: " footima mali! footima mali!" oson Alice tamaatem yublen wak weeqeenih. "Mango qarwa tan !" inte Aliis bakcarrit, kee is daffeyteh kaxxa mogoy korsil tarbeezak inki exxal.

“Kaxxam civil hinna, koo arcibise kal daffeyna” iyye March Hare.

“Anu ku korsi kinnim maaxiga” iyye Alice; "sidocuk muxxi mangoomuh daffeyte."

“Ku dagor garuq faxa” iyye Hatter. Usuk Aliis wagtak suge dagoo udduruh kaxxa curiosity luk, kee tah kaak naharsi yaabak sugte.

“Atu cankâ mabla akah abtannah barittam faxximta” iyye Alice, inki inki giclo luk; "kaxxam adab sinna."

Hatter isi intiita kaxxam fakeh tah yoobbe wak; takkay immay inkih usuk iyyem , " macaay kuraani kutbe-korsih innal kah yanim?"

"Atu toh yaanam too atu takkalem atu irol gorrissam duddah gacsa teetik?" Qado dirrik Bakkeeli iyye.

“Nummaak tonna,” iyye Alice.

“Tokkeek atu maca intam faxximta” iyyeh yan March Hare.

“Anu abeh,” Alice sissikuk gacse; “dagnal—dagnal anu axcem faxam—toh inkitto, atu taaxigeh.”

"Dagom inkibislem hinna!" Koofiya abe iyye. “Atu hudunuh ‘Anu akmem ableh an’ intam ‘Anu ablem akmem’ celta!”

“Atu hudunuh meqennal intam duddah” iyyeh yan March Hare, “‘Anu faxah anim faxam’ iyyaanam ‘Anu faxah an taama’ celta!”

“Atu hudunuh meqennal intam bictah” iyyeh yan Dormouse, isi xiinih addat yaabam celta, “woh ‘Anu ufuy ruuba wak’, ‘Anu ufuy ruuba waqdi ufuy ruuba’ iyyaanam celta!”

“Koo luk inkih tan ” iyye Hatter, akkel walal qimmoh addah yewqeeh, parti inki minitih addat tibba inteeh, Alice kaadu, raven kee writing-desk wagittaamal kassitam duddam inkih, mangom hinna.

Koofiyat qimmol tibba yaggilem. "Alsak manni ayrooy?" usuk, Alice fanah gaceeh: usuk isi saaqat isi giclok yewqeeh, uxih wagitak sugeeh, kulli wak wagitak sugeeh, isi ayti lih wagitak suge.

Alisa dagoom cubbit hayteeh, tokkeek “fereyhaytu” inte.

"Namma ayro hoxat tan!" Koofiya abe ufuy ruube. "Anu mutuk taamah matakka koh warseh suge!" usuk ossimeh naqabut wagiita maarich bakkeeli.

“ Meqe buxah marah sugte” axcuk March Hare meqe gurral gacse.

“Yeey, laakin inki inki qaynatih qaynatitte kaadu cultem faxximta,” Hatter qimmoh addah wagitak: “gaqammo-gile luk cultam mafaxximta.”

March Hare saaqat beeh, taqabi luk wagitak: tokkeek usuk isi shaahih kobbaayat culseeh, qagitaak wagitak: takkay immay usuk naharsi yab wagsiisak, “It was the best butter, you know.”

Aliis dagoo yaaxigeenimih fayxi luk kay lakbal wagittak sugte. "Macaay furkacat le saaqat!" is mascassa. “Alsi ayro warsaah, ma saaqat kinnim warsam maduuda!”

"Macaay kah takkem faxximtam?" Koofiya abe num tutucte. " Ku saaqat ma liggida kinnim koh warissa?"

“Asmatah hinna,” Alice kaxxaam sissikuk gacisse: “laakin toh kah kinnim, inki sanat inkih tan uddur itta luk sugtaamih taagah.”

“ Annih dubuk yi caalat ,” iyye Hatter.

Alice kaxxaam bohoy luk sugte, The Hatter's remark maqna akak lem celtaah, away kaaduk Ingliiz afat sugte. "Anu nagay koo maradiyyo," inte is, elle duudinnaan innal.

“Dormouse qagitak xiineh yan” iyyeh, Hatter, qunxa laqin shaahi sankaf fan yeyyeeqe.

Dormouse sabri sinnim moyya esgegeyyeh, intiita fake kal, "asmatah, asmatah; anu innih axcem faxam" iyye.

"Atu hayyeyyeeno fayramissee?" Koofiya abe iyyem, Aliis fanah ufkune qagitak.

"Balle,Anu tet aceeyyo," Aliis gaciseh: "macaay gacsi?"

“Anu dago mabla mayyu” iyye Hatter.

“Anu hinna,” iyye Maarichil Bakkeeli.

Aliis taqbitte ufuy rubte. "Anu akkalem atu tukteenay taysem abtam duuddam waktit," inte is, "gacsa sinni ceelallo esseraanamak finqissaamak."

“Atu wakti elle taaxigennal anu elle aaxige innal,” iyye Hatter, “atu edde yabtam madudda . Kaa .”

"Anu atu intam maaxiga," inte Aliis.

"asmatah atu mabta!" Koofiya abe iyyem, isi amo qaybik qidak. "Anu axcem atu wala waktil mayabta!"

“Akkuleh hinna,” Alice cubbi luk gacisse: “laakin anu gad barta wak wakti diggoysam faxximtam aaxigeh.”

"Ah! toh akkaawonti," iyye Hatter. "Usuk aatuke soolam maduuda. Away, atu dubuk meqe angaaraw luk sugte kaa tekkek, usuk saaqat lih atu faxxa haytam inkih abe raaxe. Ceelalloh, subci sagaal saaqatah sugtem celta, baritto qimbisnuh wakti dubuk: atu dubuk tutucto waktil afkan taceem faxximta, kee maroh saaqat inki adda dinner, twink fan!"

("Anu dubuk niyaatam," Maarich Bakkeeli isih tutuctot iyye.)

“Toh grand akkele, asmatah,” iyye Alice celsiisak: “laakin tohuk lakal—anu tet satqitam mafaxximta, atu taaxigeh.”

“Naharal hinna, takkay immay,” iyye Hatter: “laakin atu faxxah tan uddur, garab-tatre tiya fan sugtam duddah.”

" Atu elle miraacissa gita woh innaa?" Aliis esserte.

Koofiyat amo ligligeh rooka luk. "Yoo hinna!" usug gacseh. “Nanu tatre Maarichil sittin way abneh sugne— usuk cabbootak naharal, atu taaxigeh—” (Maarichil Hare’h addal isi shaahih qaynat wagsiisak,) “—woh kaxxa konsertih addat sugteeh, anu kaadu gada abem faxxiimak sugte.

'Tiwinkil, tiwinkil, qunxa bartî kimbiro!

'Baadak dagal atu haadda,
Qaranal yan shaahih sandugih innal.
Tiwinkil, tiwinkil—'”

“Nagaya, anu naharsi ayat gaba kaleh suge,” iyye Hatter, “Malika kaqitak, ‘Usuk wakti qida! Off with his head!’” iyye.

"Mannah yan meysi le qangiino!" Aliis deero inte.

"Kaadu tohuk qemmissa haanam," Hatter rookale xongolot gexe, "usuk anu esseram abam maduuda! away umman lica saaqatah tan."

Diifule mabla Aliisih moyyat temeete. "Mango shaahi-duyye akkel kah daffeyte sabab kinnii?" is esserteh.

"Yeey, toh teeti," iyye Hatter ufuy ruubak: "woh umman shaahi-wakti, kee nanu wakti mannu duyye kaqlisnuh uddur fanat."

"Tokkeek atu dariifal tangayyem bisoh axcetto, Anu akkalem?" inte Aliis.

“Hummaak tonnal,” iyye Hatter: “tuyye elle tantifiqqen innal.”

"Laakin maca akkeleeh atu qagitak qimmol temeete wak?" Aliis essertam faxxa.

“Nanu celsiisenta milaagnam faxximta” axcuk March Hare, yawqeh. "Anu tahak taqbeh an. Qunxa awka aydaadu neh warissam dooro abe."

“Anu inkittu maaxiga” iyye Alice, tama poroposal kaxxaam bohoy luk.

"Tokkeek Dormouse akkele!" oson nammayak weeqeenih. "Uguta, Dormawus!" Kee oson namma katul inki addat tet qanxisen.

Dormouse caatih intiita fake. "Anu xiinih masugiyyo," iyye usuk qiyto, qaku le xongolot: "Anu obbeh kulli qangara isin kataysis itta."

"Aydaadu neh warisey!" Qado dirrik Bakkeeli iyye.

"Yeey, kumagan ab!" Aliis xaaqimteh.

"Kaadu sissikuk ab," ossiseh Hatter, "akkek atu xiinetto qagitak toh gaba kaltaamak afal."

"Oson macaay elle waareenim?" intem Aliis, umman way kaxxa fayxi le akmiyyaa kee maqab esseroora.

“Oson treacle” axcuk sugeenim, Dormouse, inki minit kee namma minit wagitak sugeek lakal.

“Oson woh abaanam maduudan, atu taaxigeh” axcuk Alice sissikuk abte; "oson biyaakitak sugen."

“Toh tonnal anuk,” iyye Dormouse; " kaxxam biyaakitah."
