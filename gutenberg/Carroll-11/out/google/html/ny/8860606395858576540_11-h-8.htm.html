MUTU VIII.
The Queen's Croquet-Ground

Mtengo wawukulu wa duwa unayima pafupi ndi khomo la dimbalo: maluwa omwe amamera pamenepo anali oyera, koma panali alimi atatu pamenepo, akumapenta mofiira. Alice anaganiza zimenezi mochititsa chidwi kwambiri, ndipo anayandikira pafupi kuti akawaonere, ndipo atangofika kumene anamva mmodzi wa iwo akunena kuti, “Taonani tsopano, Asanu!

“Sindinathe kudziletsa,” anatero Five, mokwiya; "Seven adathamanga chigongono changa."

Pamenepo Asanu ndi awiri anayang'ana m'mwamba, nati, Ndimo, Asanu!

“ Kuli bwino osalankhula!” adatero Asanu. "Ndamva Mfumukazi ikunena dzulo kuti ukuyenera kudulidwa mutu!"

"Zachiyani?" Adatero amene adalankhula poyamba.

"Zimenezo sizikukukhudzani , Awiri!" Adatero Seven.

"Inde, ndi ntchito yake!" Adatero Five, "ndipo ndimuwuza - zinali zobweretsa mizu ya tulip m'malo mwa anyezi."

Asanu ndi awiri adagwetsa burashi yake, ndipo anali atangoyamba kumene, "Chabwino, pa zinthu zonse zopanda chilungamo -" pamene diso lake linagwera pa Alice, pamene adayima akuwayang'ana, ndipo adadziyang'ana mwadzidzidzi: ena adayang'ananso, ndipo onse adagwada pansi.

"Kodi ungandiuze," anatero Alice, mwamantha pang'ono, "chifukwa chiyani ukupenta maluwa awa?"

Asanu ndi Asanu ndi awiri sananene kanthu, koma anayang'ana Awiri. Awiri adayamba ndi mawu otsika, "Bwanji zoona, mukuwona, Abiti, uwu uyenera kukhala mtengo waduwa wofiira , ndipo tayika woyera molakwika; ndipo ngati Mfumukazi ikanazindikira, tonse tiyenera kudula mitu yathu, mukudziwa. ndipo alimi atatuwo adagwa pansi pomwepo. Panamveka phokoso la mapazi ambiri, ndipo Alice anayang'ana uku ndi uku, kufunitsitsa kuona Mfumukazi.

Poyamba anadza asilikali khumi onyamula ndodo; onsewo anali opangidwa ngati atatu aja, m'mbali mwake ndi mosalala, ndi manja ao ndi mapazi ao pa ngondya zao; awa anali okongoletsedwa ponseponse ndi diamondi, ndipo anayenda awiri ndi awiri, monga ankhondo ankachitira. Zitatha izi anadza ana achifumu; panali khumi a iwo, ndipo okondedwa ang'onoang'ono anadza akudumpha mokondwera pamodzi ndi manja, m'mabanja: onse anali okongoletsedwa ndi mitima. Kenako panabwera alendo, makamaka Mafumu ndi Mafumukazi, ndipo pakati pawo Alice anazindikira Kalulu Woyera: anali kuyankhula mothamanga mwamanjenje, akumwetulira pa chilichonse chomwe chinanenedwa, ndipo adadutsa osamuzindikira. Kenako anatsatira Knave of Hearts, atanyamula korona wa Mfumu pa kapezi velvet khushoni; ndipo, pomalizira pake pa ulendo wokulirapo, panadza MFUMU NDI MFUMU YA MITIMA.

Alice anali wokayikitsa ngati sayenera kugona chafufumimba ngati alimi atatu aja, koma sanakumbukire kuti adamvapo za lamulo lotere pamagulu; “Ndiponso, pangakhale chigulu chanji,” iye anaganiza motero, “ngati anthu atagona pansi ndi nkhope zawo pansi, kuti asawawone? Chotero iye anayima pamene iye anali, nadikira.

Gululo litafika moyang'anizana ndi Alice, onse adayimilira ndikumuyang'ana, ndipo Mfumukaziyo idati mwamphamvu "Ndi ndani uyu?" Adalankhula motelo Knave of Heart yemwe adangowerama ndikumwetulira poyankha.

"Chitsiru!" Adatero Queen uku akugwedeza mutu wake mopanda chipiriro; ndipo, kutembenukira kwa Alice, iye anapitiriza, "Dzina lako ndani, mwana?"

"Dzina langa ndine Alice, chonde amfumu," adatero Alice mwaulemu kwambiri; koma iye anawonjezera, kwa iyemwini, “Bwanji, iwo ndi paketi chabe ya makadi, pambuyo pa zonse, ine sindiyenera kuwaopa iwo!”

“Ndipo awa ndani? ” Mfumukaziyo inatero, ikuloza alimi atatu amene anali atagona mozungulira mtengo wa duwa; pakuti, inu mukuona, pamene iwo anali atagona pa nkhope zawo, ndipo chitsanzo pa misana yawo chinali chimodzimodzi monga ena onse paketi, iye sakanakhoza kudziwa ngati iwo anali wamaluwa, kapena asilikali, kapena apanyumba, kapena atatu a ana ake omwe.

" Ndidziwa bwanji?" Adatelo Alice modabwa ndi kulimba mtima kwake komwe. "Zilibe ntchito yanga ."

Mfumukaziyo inasanduka kapezi ndi ukali, ndipo, itamuyang’ana kwa kamphindi ngati chilombo, inafuula kuti “Choka mutu wake!

“Zamkhutu!” Adatero Alice mokweza mokweza komanso motsimikiza, ndipo Mfumukazi idangokhala chete.

Mfumuyo inaika dzanja lake pa mkono wake, nati mwamantha, “Talingalirani, wokondedwa wanga: iye ndi mwana chabe!”

Mfumukaziyo inatembenuka mokwiya kwa iye, ndipo inati kwa Knave, "Atembenuzire iwo!"

A Knave anachita zimenezo mosamala kwambiri ndi phazi limodzi.

"Imilirani!" idatero Mfumukaziyo mokuwa mokweza mawu, ndipo alimi atatuwo adalumpha nthawi yomweyo, nayamba kugwada kwa Mfumu, Mfumukazi, ana achifumu, ndi wina aliyense.

“Zilekeni zimenezo!” Adakuwa motele Queen. "Iwe umandipangitsa ine giddy." Ndiyeno, anatembenukira kwa mtengo wa duwa, iye anapitiriza, "Kodi iwe wakhala ukutani kuno?"

"Zikondweretse mfumu yanu," adatero Awiri, modzichepetsa kwambiri, atagwada pa bondo limodzi pamene amalankhula, "tinali kuyesera-"

" Kodi !" Adatelo Queen yemwe panthawiyi adali akuyang'ana maluwa aja. "Chotsani mitu yawo!" ndipo gululo lidapitilira, atatu mwa asitikali adatsala kumbuyo kuti aphedwe alimi omwe adathamangira kwa Alice kuti akatetezedwe.

“Simuyenera kudulidwa mutu!” Adatelo Alice, ndipo adaziyika mumphika wawukulu wamaluwa womwe udayima pafupi. Asilikali atatuwo anayendayenda kwa mphindi imodzi kapena ziwiri, kuwafunafuna, kenako mwakachetechete ananyamuka kutsatira enawo.

"Kodi mitu yawo ili kutali?" Adakuwa motele Queen.

"Mitu yawo yapita, ngati zingakondweretse Mfumu!" asilikaliwo anakuwa poyankha.

"Ndichoncho!" Adakuwa motele Queen. "Kodi mutha kusewera croquet?"

Asilikali adakhala chete ndipo adamuyang'ana Alice, chifukwa funsoli lidali lofunika kwa iye.

“Inde!” anakuwa Alice.

“Tiyeni!” Adabangula Queen, ndipo Alice adalowa nawo mgululi, akudabwa kwambiri kuti chichitike ndi chani?

“Ndi—tsiku labwino kwambiri!” Anatero mawu amantha pambali pake. Anali akuyenda pafupi ndi Kalulu Woyera, yemwe ankasuzumira nkhope yake modandaula.

"Kwambiri," adatero Alice, "- a Duchess ali kuti?"

“Chete! Anatero Kalulu motsitsa mothamanga. Iye anayang’ana pa phewa lake mwankhawa pamene anali kuyankhula, ndipo kenaka anadzitukumula yekha pa nsonga ya nsonga, kuyika pakamwa pake pafupi ndi khutu la mkaziyo, nanong’oneza “Iye ali m’chilango cha kuphedwa.”

"Zachiyani?" Adatero Alice.

“Kodi munati, ‘Zachisoni bwanji!’?” Anafunsa Kalulu.

“Ayi, sindinatero,” anatero Alice: “Sindikuona zachisoni ngakhale pang’ono.

“Anaboola makutu a Mfumukazi—” Kalulu anayamba. Alice anakuwa pang'ono kuseka. “O, bata!” Kalulu ananong'oneza mwamantha. "Mfumukazi ikumva! Mwaona, adabwera mochedwa, ndipo Mfumukazi idati - "

“Bwerani ku malo anu!” inafuula Mfumukazi ndi mawu a bingu, ndipo anthu anayamba kuthamanga uku ndi uku, akugubuduzana; komabe, adakhazikika mu miniti imodzi kapena ziwiri, ndipo masewera adayamba. Alice ankaganiza kuti anali asanaonepo malo osangalatsa ngati amenewa m'moyo wake; zonse zinali zitunda ndi mizere; mipira inali hedgehogs amoyo, mallets amoyo flamingo, ndipo asilikali amayenera kuwirikiza ndi kuyima pa manja ndi mapazi awo, kupanga zipilala.

Vuto lalikulu lomwe Alice adapeza poyamba linali pakuwongolera flamingo yake: adakwanitsa kutulutsa thupi lake, bwino bwino, pansi pa mkono wake, miyendo yake ikulendewera pansi, koma nthawi zambiri, monga adawongola khosi lake bwino, ndipo amakantha mutu wake, inkadzizungulira ndikuyang'ana mmwamba kumaso kwake, kotero kuti sakanatha kuseka: pansi, ndipo akuti ayambenso, zinali zokwiyitsa kwambiri kupeza kuti hedgehog anali atadzigudubuza, ndipo anali mumchitidwe wokwawa kuchokapo: pambali pa zonsezi, pamakhala phokoso kapena mzere munjira kulikonse kumene iye ankafuna kutumiza hedgehog, ndipo, monga asilikali owirikiza kawiri anali kudzuka ndikuyenda kupita kumadera ena a nthaka, posachedwapa Alice anafika pozindikira kuti posakhalitsa masewerawo anali ovuta kwambiri.

Osewera onse adasewera nthawi imodzi osadikirira kutembenuka, kukangana nthawi zonse, ndikumenyera hedgehogs; ndipo m'kanthawi kochepa Mfumukaziyo idakwiya kwambiri, ndipo idayenda uku ndi uku, ndikufuula "Chotsani mutu!" kapena “Kusiya mutu wake!” pafupifupi kamodzi mu miniti.

Alice adayamba kukhumudwa kwambiri: zowona, anali asanakangane ndi Mfumukazi, koma adadziwa kuti zitha kuchitika mphindi iliyonse, "ndiyeno," adaganiza kuti, "chingachitike bwanji kwa ine? Amakonda kwambiri kudula anthu mitu pano; chodabwitsa ndichakuti, pali aliyense amene watsala wamoyo!"

Amangoyang'ana njira yopulumukira, ndikudzifunsa ngati angathawe popanda kuwonedwa, pomwe adawona mawonekedwe owoneka bwino mumlengalenga: zidamudabwitsa kwambiri poyamba, koma, ataziwona kwa mphindi imodzi kapena ziwiri, adangoseka, ndipo adadziuza yekha kuti "Ndi Cheshire Cat: tsopano ndikhala ndi wina woti ndiyankhule naye."

"Mukuyenda bwanji?" Adatelo Mphaka atangotuluka pakamwa kuti alankhule naye.

Alice adadikirira mpaka maso adawonekera, kenako adagwedeza mutu. “Palibe ntchito kuyankhula nawo,” iye anaganiza motero, “mpaka makutu ake atabwera, kapena limodzi la iwo.” Mumphindi ina mutu wonse unawonekera, ndipo Alice anayika pansi flamingo yake, ndikuyamba nkhani ya masewera, akusangalala kwambiri kuti ali ndi wina womumvetsera. Mphakayo ankawoneka kuti akuganiza kuti zatsala pang'ono kuziwona, ndipo sizinawonekerenso.

"Sindikuganiza kuti amasewera mwachilungamo," adayamba Alice, m'malo modandaula, "ndipo onse amakangana kwambiri kotero kuti munthu sangamve akulankhula - ndipo akuwoneka kuti alibe malamulo aliwonse; osachepera, ngati alipo, palibe amene amawasamalira - ndipo simukudziwa momwe zimasokoneza zinthu zonse zomwe zimakhala zamoyo; mwachitsanzo, ndikupita patsogolo. pansi—ndipo ndikanati ndiphwanye chimbalangondo cha Mfumukazi pompano, koma chinathawa chitaona kuti wanga akubwera!”

"Mwamukonda bwanji Queen?" Adatelo Mphaka motsitsa mawu.

"Ayi," adatero Alice: "wachita bwino kwambiri -" Pomwepo adawona kuti Mfumukazi ili pafupi ndi iye, ikumvetsera: kotero iye anapitiriza, "- mwina kupambana, kuti sikoyenera kumaliza masewerawo."

Mfumukazi inamwetulira ndikudutsa.

" Mukuyankhula ndi ndani?" idatero Mfumu ikupita kwa Alice, ndikuyang'ana mutu wa Mphaka ndi chidwi chachikulu.

“Ndi mnzanga—Mphaka wa ku Cheshire,” anatero Alice: “ndiloleni ndikufotokozereni.

“Sindimakonda mawonekedwe ake konse,” inatero Mfumuyo: “Komabe, ikhoza kupsyopsyona dzanja langa ngati ikufuna.

“Sindikanafuna,” Mphaka anatero.

“Musachite manyazi,” inatero Mfumuyo, “ndipo musandiyang’ane choncho! Adafika kumbuyo kwa Alice uku amalankhula.

“Mphaka akhoza kuyang’ana mfumu,” anatero Alice. “Zimenezo ndinaziŵerenga m’buku lina, koma sindikukumbukira kumene.”

"Chabwino, iyenera kuchotsedwa," inatero Mfumuyo motsimikiza kwambiri, ndipo inaitana Mfumukazi, yomwe inkadutsa panthawiyo, "Okondedwa! Ndikanakonda ukanamuchotsa mphaka uyu!"

Mfumukazi inali ndi njira imodzi yokha yothetsera zovuta zonse, zazikulu kapena zazing'ono. "Achoke ndi mutu wake!" adatero osayang'ana pozungulira.

“Ndikatenga wophayo,” inatero Mfumuyo mofunitsitsa, ndipo inanyamuka mofulumira.

Alice anaganiza kuti mwina angobwerera, akaone mmene game ikuyendera, atamva mawu a Queen chapatali akukuwa mwachidwi. Iye anali atamva kale chiweruzo chake atatu mwa osewerawo kuti aphedwe chifukwa chophonya nthawi yawo, ndipo sanasangalale ndi momwe zinthu zilili, popeza masewerawo anali osokonezeka kwambiri moti sankadziwa ngati inali nthawi yake kapena ayi. Kotero iye anapita kukafunafuna hedgehog yake.

Hedgehog idachita ndewu ndi hedgehog ina, yomwe inkawoneka kwa Alice mwayi wabwino kwambiri wokhotakhota m'modzi wa iwo ndi wina: chovuta chokha chinali, kuti flamingo yake idawoloka tsidya lina lamunda, pomwe Alice amamuwona akuyesera mopanda thandizo kuti awuluke mumtengo.

Pamene ankagwira flamingo n’kuibweretsanso, ndewuyo inali itatha, ndipo akalulu onse awiriwa sanaonekenso: “koma zilibe kanthu,” anaganiza motero Alice, “pamene makhoma onse achoka kumbali iyi ya nthaka.” Choncho anachiyika m’manja mwake kuti chisathawenso, n’kubwereranso kukacheza ndi mnzakeyo.

Atabwerera ku Cheshire Cat, adadabwa kupeza gulu lalikulu la anthu litasonkhana mozungulira: panali mkangano pakati pa wakuphayo, Mfumu, ndi Mfumukazi, omwe amalankhula nthawi imodzi, pamene ena onse anali chete, ndipo amawoneka osamasuka.

Nthawi yomwe Alice adawonekera, adapemphedwa ndi onse atatu kuti ayankhe funsolo, ndipo adabwerezanso zomwe adamufunsa, ngakhale kuti onse amalankhula nthawi imodzi, zidamuvuta kwambiri kuti amvetsetse zomwe ananena.

Mkangano wa wakuphayo unali wakuti, simungadutse mutu pokhapokha ngati pali thupi loti adulepo: kuti anali asanachitepo chinthu choterocho, ndipo sakanayamba pa nthawi ya moyo wake .

Mkangano wa Mfumu unali wakuti, chilichonse chokhala ndi mutu chikhoza kudulidwa mutu, ndipo musalankhule zopanda pake.

Mkangano wa Mfumukazi unali, kuti ngati china chake sichinachitike pasanapite nthawi kuti aliyense aphedwe, kuzungulira. (Ndi mawu omaliza awa omwe adapangitsa kuti phwando lonse liwoneke lachisoni komanso loda nkhawa.)

Alice sakanatha kunena china chilichonse koma "Ndi za a Duchess: mukamufunse za izi."

"Ali m'ndende," Mfumukazi idauza wophayo: "mutengere kuno." Ndipo wakuphayo anachoka ngati muvi.

Mutu wa Mphaka unayamba kuzimiririka pamene anali atapita, ndipo, pamene adabwerera ndi a Duchess, anali atasowa; choncho Mfumu ndi wakuphayo adathamanga mwachidwi uku ndi uku kufunafuna, pamene ena onse adabwerera ku masewerawo.
