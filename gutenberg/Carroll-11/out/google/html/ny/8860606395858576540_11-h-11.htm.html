MUTU XI.
Ndani Anaba Ma Tarts?

Mfumu ndi Mfumukazi ya Mitima anali atakhala pampando wawo wachifumu pamene iwo anafika, ndi khamu lalikulu linasonkhana mozungulira iwo—mitundu yonse ya mbalame zazing’ono ndi zilombo, limodzinso ndi gulu lonse la makadi: Mpeni anaimirira pamaso pawo, mu unyolo, ndi msilikali kumbali iliyonse kuti amuyang’anire; ndipo pafupi ndi Mfumuyo panali Kalulu Woyera, ali ndi lipenga m’dzanja limodzi, ndi mpukutu wa zikopa m’dzanja lina. Pakatikati pa bwalo lamilandu panali tebulo, lokhala ndi mbale yaikulu ya tarts: ankawoneka bwino kwambiri, moti zinapangitsa Alice kukhala ndi njala yakuwayang’ana—“Ndikanakonda akanati mlanduwo uchitike,” iye anaganiza motero, “ndikupereka zotsitsimula! Koma zikuoneka kuti panalibe mwayi wa izi, kotero iye anayamba kuyang'ana chirichonse chokhudza iye, kuti apite nthawi.

Alice anali asanakhalepo kukhoti lamilandu, koma anali atawerengapo za iwo m’mabuku, ndipo anasangalala kwambiri kupeza kuti ankadziŵa dzina la pafupifupi chilichonse chimene chinali kumeneko. “Ndiye woweruza,” iye anadzilankhula yekha, “chifukwa cha wigi yake yaikulu.”

Woweruza, mwa njira, anali Mfumu; ndipo pamene adavala korona wake pamwamba pa wigi, (yang'anani chakutsogolo ngati mukufuna kuwona momwe adachitira,) sadayang'ane momasuka, ndipo sikunali koyenera.

“Ndipo ilo ndilo bokosi la oweruza,” anaganiza motero Alice, “ndi zolengedwa khumi ndi ziwirizo,” (iye anakakamizika kunena “zolengedwa,” inu mukuona, chifukwa zina mwa izo zinali nyama, ndipo zina zinali mbalame,) “Ine ndikuganiza iwo ndi oweruza.” Iye ananena mawu otsiriza awa kawiri kapena katatu kwa iyemwini, pokhala wonyada nawo: pakuti iye ankaganiza, ndipo moyenereranso, kuti atsikana ochepa kwambiri a msinkhu wake amadziwa tanthauzo lake nkomwe. Komabe, “anthu oweruza milandu” akanachita chimodzimodzi.

Oweruza khumi ndi awiri onse anali kulemba motanganidwa kwambiri pa slates. "Akutani?" Alice adanong'oneza Gryphon. "Pakali pano alibe cholembera, mlandu usanayambe."

"Akulemba mayina awo," Gryphon adanong'oneza poyankha, "kuwopa kuti angayiwale mlandu usanathe."

“Zinthu zopusa!” Alice anayamba ndi mawu okweza, aukali, koma anaima mofulumira, pakuti Kalulu Woyera analira, “Khalani chete m’bwalo!” ndipo mfumu idavala zowonera zake ndikuyang'ana modera nkhawa kuti adziwe amene akuyankhula.

Alice ankatha kuona, komanso ngati akuyang’ana pamapewa awo, kuti oweruza onse akulemba “zinthu zopusa!” pa malembo awo, ndipo anatha kuzindikira kuti mmodzi wa iwo sadziwa kutchula “chitsiru,” ndipo anayenera kufunsa mnansi wake kuti amuuze. "Chisokonezo chabwino chikhalapo mlandu wawo usanathe!" anaganiza choncho Alice.

Mmodzi mwa oweruzawo anali ndi pensulo yomwe inkalira. Izi ndithudi, Alice sakanakhoza kuyimirira, ndipo adazungulira bwalo lamilandu ndikubwerera kumbuyo kwake, ndipo posakhalitsa adapeza mwayi wochotsa. Iye anachita izo mofulumira kwambiri mwakuti woweruza wamng'ono wosaukayo (anali Bill, Buluzi) sanathe kuzindikira nkomwe chomwe chinachitikira icho; kotero, atatha kusaka zonse za izo, iye anakakamizika kulemba ndi chala chimodzi kwa tsiku lonse; ndipo ichi chinali chothandiza pang'ono, popeza sichinasiyire chizindikiro pamwala.

“Herald, werengani mlanduwo!” Anatero Mfumu.

Pamenepo Kalulu Woyera analiza kulira katatu pa lipenga lija, ndipo kenaka anavundukula mpukutu wa zikopa uja, nawerenga motere:—

"Mfumukazi ya Mitima, adapanga ma tarts,
Zonse pa tsiku lachilimwe:
Knave of Hearts, adaba ma tarts,
Ndipo anawatengera kutali!”

“Lingalirani chigamulo chanu,” inatero Mfumuyo kwa oweruza.

"Ayi, pakali pano!" Kalulu anaduladula mawu. "Pali zambiri zoti zibwere izi zisanachitike!"

“Itana mboni yoyamba,” inatero Mfumu; ndipo Kalulu Woyera analiza kulira katatu pa lipenga, nati, “Mmboni woyamba!”

Mboni yoyamba inali ya Hatter. Anabwera ndi kapu ya tiyi m’dzanja limodzi ndi chidutswa cha mkate ndi batala m’dzanja lina. “Ndikukhululukireni, Mfumu,” iye anayamba, “chifukwa chobweretsa awa: koma ndinali ndisanamalize kwenikweni tiyi wanga pamene anandiitana.

“Uyenera kuti wamaliza,” inatero Mfumu. "Mwayamba liti?"

Hatter anayang'ana Kalulu wa March, yemwe adamutsatira m'bwalo, ali m'manja ndi Dormouse. "Lakhumi ndi chinayi la Marichi, ndikuganiza kuti linali," adatero.

“Chakhumi ndi chisanu,” anatero Kalulu wa Marichi.

"Chakhumi ndi chisanu ndi chimodzi," anawonjezera Dormouse.

"Lembani izi," Mfumu idatero kwa oweruza, ndipo oweruza adalemba mwachidwi madeti onse atatu pamiyala yawo, ndikuwonjezera, ndikuchepetsa yankho kukhala shillingi ndi pence.

“Vula chipewa chako,” inatero Mfumuyo kwa Chidani.

“Si wanga,” anatero Hatter.

" Wabedwa! " Mfumuyo idafuula, ikutembenukira kwa oweruza, omwe nthawi yomweyo adapanga chikumbutso cha nkhaniyi.

"Ndimawasunga kuti azigulitsa," Hatter anawonjezera monga kufotokozera; "Ndilibe wanga ndekha. Ndine wodana."

Apa Mfumukazi idavala magalasi ake, nayamba kuyang'ana kwa Hatter, yemwe adatembenuka ndikunjenjemera.

“Perekani umboni wanu,” inatero Mfumu; "Ndipo musakhale ndi mantha, kapena ndikuphani pomwepo."

Izi sizinkawoneka ngati zolimbikitsa umboni konse: iye anasuntha kuchoka ku phazi limodzi kupita ku lina, kuyang'ana Mfumukazi mosamasuka, ndipo m'chisokonezo chake analuma chidutswa chachikulu kuchokera m'kapu yake ya tiyi m'malo mwa mkate ndi batala.

Pa nthawiyi Alice anamva chidwi kwambiri, chomwe chinamudabwitsa kwambiri mpaka atazindikira kuti chinali chiyani: adayambanso kukula, ndipo adaganiza kuti poyamba adzuka ndikuchoka m'bwalo; koma mmaganizo mwake adaganiza zokhalabe pomwe anali pomwe panalibe malo ake.

“Ndikanakonda simunapsinjike choncho,” anatero Domoyo yemwe anakhala pafupi naye. “Ndimalephera kupuma.”

“Sindingachitire mwina,” anatero Alice modzichepetsa kwambiri: “Ndikukula.”

"Ulibe ufulu wokulira kuno ," adatero Dormouse.

“Osalankhula zopanda pake,” anatero Alice molimba mtima kwambiri: “ukudziwa kuti nawenso ukukula.”

"Inde, koma ndimakula pamlingo woyenera," adatero Dormouse: "osati mwanjira yopusa imeneyo." Ndipo anadzuka mokwiya kwambiri, nawolokera tsidya lina la bwalo.

Nthawi yonseyi Mfumukaziyi inali isanasiye kuyang'ana kwa Hatter, ndipo, pamene Dormouse ankawoloka bwalo, anauza mmodzi wa akuluakulu a khoti kuti, "Ndibweretsere mndandanda wa oimba mu konsati yomaliza!" pomwe Hatter watsoka ananjenjemera kotero kuti adakutumula nsapato zake zonse ziwiri.

“Perekani umboni wanu,” Mfumuyo inabwereza motero mwaukali, “kapena ndikuphani, kaya muli ndi mantha kapena ayi.

“Ndine wosauka, Mfumu Yanu,” anatero Hatter, ndi mawu akunjenjemera, “ndipo sindinayambe tiyi wanga—osapitirira mlungu umodzi kapena kuposerapo—ndipo mmene mkate ndi batala unawonda chonchi—ndi kuthwanima kwa tiyi—

“Kuthwanima kwa chiyani? ” inatero Mfumu.

" Zidayamba ndi tiyi," adatero Hatter.

"Zowona, kuthwanima kumayamba ndi T!" idatero Mfumu mokwiya. "Kodi mumanditenga ngati dunce? Pitirizani!"

"Ndine munthu wosauka," adatero Hatter, "ndipo zinthu zambiri zidawoneka pambuyo pake - ndi Kalulu wa Marichi yekha yemwe adati-"

“Sindinatero!” Kalulu wa Marichi adadukiza mwachangu kwambiri.

“Mwatero!” adatero Hatter.

“Ndikukana!” Anatero Kalulu wa Marichi.

“Iye wakana,” inatero Mfumuyo: “Siya mbali imeneyo.

"Chabwino, mulimonse, Dormouse adati," Hatter anapitiriza, kuyang'ana modera nkhawa kuti awone ngati angakanenso: koma Dormouse sanakane kalikonse, akugona tulo tofa nato.

“Zitachitika zimenezo,” anapitiriza motero Hatter, “ndinadulanso buledi ndi batala—”

"Koma Dormouse wati chani?" m'modzi mwa oweruza adafunsa.

"Zimenezo sindikukumbukira," adatero Hatter.

“ Uyenera kukumbukira,” inatero Mfumuyo, “kapena ndikuphani.”

Hatter womvetsa chisoni anagwetsa chikho chake cha tiyi ndi mkate-ndi-mafuta, ndipo anagwada pa bondo limodzi. “Ndine munthu wosauka, Mfumu,” anayamba motero.

“Ndiwe wosalankhula bwino ,” inatero Mfumuyo.

Apa mmodzi wa mbira-nkhumba anasangalala, ndipo yomweyo kuponderezedwa ndi akuluakulu a khoti. (Popeza amenewo ali mawu olimba, ndingokufotokozerani momwe zinachitikira. Iwo anali ndi thumba lalikulu lachinsalu, lomwe amangirira kukamwa ndi zingwe: mmenemo analowetsa mbira, mutu choyamba, ndiyeno anakhala pamenepo.)

“Ndine wokondwa kuti ndawonapo zimenezo,” anaganiza motero Alice. “Nthaŵi zambiri ndaŵerenga m’nyuzipepala, kumapeto kwa milandu, kuti, “Panali kuyesera kuombera m’manja, kumene kunaponderezedwa mwamsanga ndi maofesala a khoti,” ndipo sindinamvetsetse tanthauzo lake kufikira tsopano.

Mfumuyo inapitirizabe kuti: “Ngati ndi zonse zimene mukudziwa za nkhaniyi, mukhoza kusiya.

“Sindingatsikenso,” anatero Hatter: “Ndili pansi, monga momwe zilili.”

“Ndiye mungakhale pansi ,” inayankha motero Mfumu.

Apa nkhono winayo anasangalala, ndipo anaponderezedwa.

"Bwerani, izo zamaliza mbira!" anaganiza choncho Alice. "Tsopano tikhala bwino."

“Ndingakonde ndimalize tiyi,” anatero Hatter, modera nkhaŵa Mfumukazi, yemwe anali kuŵerenga ndandanda ya oimba.

“Ukhoza kupita,” inatero Mfumu, ndipo Hatter anatuluka mofulumira m’bwalo lamilandu, osadikira n’komwe kuvala nsapato zake.

"- ndipo ingochotsani mutu wake panja," Mfumukaziyi idawonjezera kwa m'modzi mwa apolisi: koma Hatter sanawonekere kuti wapolisiyo asanafike pakhomo.

“Itana mboni yotsatira!” Anatero Mfumu.

Mboni yotsatira inali yophika a Duchess. Ananyamula bokosi la tsabola m'manja mwake, ndipo Alice adangoganiza kuti ndi ndani, ngakhale asanalowe m'bwalo, momwe anthu omwe anali pafupi ndi khomo adayamba kuyetsemula nthawi imodzi.

“Perekani umboni wanu,” inatero Mfumuyo.

“Ayi,” anatero wophikayo.

Mfumuyo inayang’ana Kalulu Woyera mwankhawayo, yemwe ananena motsitsa mawu kuti, “Amfumu afunseni mafunso mboni imeneyi .

"Chabwino, ngati ndiyenera, ndiyenera," Mfumuyo idatero, ndi mpweya wosungulumwa, ndipo, atapinda manja ake ndikukwinyira wophikayo mpaka maso ake atatsala pang'ono kuti asawoneke, idati ndi mawu akuya, "Kodi ma tarts amapangidwa ndi chiyani?"

“Tsamba, makamaka,” anatero wophikayo.

“Treacle,” liwu latulo linatero kumbuyo kwake.

"Collar Dormouse," Mfumukazi idafuula mokweza. "Dulani mutu wa Dormyuyo! Chotsani Dormyu kunja kwa bwalo lamilandu! Mtsenderezeni! Mtsinani! Mvula ndi ndevu zake!"

Kwa mphindi zingapo khothi lonse linali chipwirikiti, kutulutsa Dormouse, ndipo, pomwe adakhazikikanso, wophika uja adasowa.

"Osazitengera!" idatero Mfumuyo mosangalala kwambiri. “Imbani mboni yotsatira.” Ndipo anaonjeza ndi mawu apansi kwa Mfumukazi kuti, "Zowonadi, wokondedwa wanga, uyenera kufunsanso mboni yotsatira. Zimandipweteka pachipumi!"

Alice anayang'ana Kalulu Woyera uku akufufuza mndandandawo, ali ndi chidwi chofuna kuona kuti mboni yotsatira idzakhala yotani, “—pakuti alibe umboni wochuluka ,” anatero mumtima mwake. Tangoganizani kudabwa kwake, pamene Kalulu Woyera anawerenga mokweza mawu ake aang’ono, dzina lakuti “Alice!”
