MUTU IX.
Nkhani ya Kamba Wonyozeka

“Sungaganize mmene ndasangalalira kukuonanso, munthu wachikulire wokondeka!” Adatelo a Duchess, uku akulowetsa mkono wake mwachikondi kwa Alice, ndipo adanyamuka limodzi.

Alice anasangalala kwambiri atamupeza ali mu mtima wokoma chonchi, ndipo mumtima mwake ankaganiza kuti mwina ndi tsabola yekhayo amene anamuchititsa nkhanza kwambiri atakumana kukhitchini.

"Pamene ndili Duchess," adatero mumtima mwake, (osati mwachiyembekezo), "sindidzakhala ndi tsabola m'khitchini yanga. Msuzi umayenda bwino popanda- Mwinamwake ndi tsabola yemwe amapangitsa anthu kupsa mtima," anapitiliza, mokondwera kwambiri atapeza mtundu watsopano wa lamulo, "ndi vinyo wosasa umene umawawasa zomwe zimawawasa - ndi kuwapangitsa iwo kukhala owawasa - ndi zinthu zowawasa - ndi balere - amawapanga iwo - ndi zina zotero. wokoma mtima ndimangolakalaka anthu atadziwa izi : ndiye kuti sangakhale otopa nazo, mukudziwa.

Panthawiyi anali atayiwalatu a Duchess, ndipo anadzidzimuka atamva mawu ake pafupi ndi khutu lake. “Ukuganiza zinazake, wokondedwa wanga, ndipo zimenezi zimakuchititsa kuiwala kulankhula.” Sindingathe kukuuzani pakali pano makhalidwe abwino, koma ndidzakumbukira pang’ono.

“Mwina palibe,” Alice analimba mtima kunena.

“Tsopano, mwana!” adatero a Duchess. "Chilichonse chili ndi chikhalidwe, ngati mutachipeza." Ndipo adazifinyira kuyandikira mbali ya Alice pomwe amalankhula.

Alice sanakonde kukhala pafupi naye: choyamba, chifukwa a Duchess anali oipa kwambiri ; ndipo chachiwiri, chifukwa anali ndendende utali woyenerera kuti akhazikitse chibwano chake paphewa la Alice, ndipo chinali chibwano chakuthwa movutikira. Komabe, iye sankakonda kuchita mwano, choncho anapirira bwinobwino mmene akanathera.

“Masewerawa akuyenda bwino tsopano,” iye anatero, popitiriza kukambiranako pang’ono.

"'Ndichoncho," adatero a Duchess: "ndipo chikhalidwe chake ndi-'O, chikondi ndi chikondi, chomwe chimapangitsa dziko kuzungulira!

"Wina adati," Alice adanong'oneza, "kuti zimachitika ndi aliyense wongoganizira zazake!"

Zikutanthauzanso chimodzimodzi, "adatero a Duchess, akukumba chibwano chake chakuthwa paphewa la Alice ndikuwonjezera kuti, "ndipo lingaliro la izi ndilakuti, 'Samalitsa malingaliro, ndipo mawuwo adzisamalira okha.'

“Iye amasangalala chotani nanga kupeza makhalidwe abwino m’zinthu!” Alice anadziganizira yekha.

"Ndingayerekeze kunena kuti mukudabwa chifukwa chake sindikuyika mkono wanga m'chiuno mwako," a Duchess adatero atakhala kaye chete: "chifukwa chake ndi chakuti, ndikukayikira kupsa mtima kwa flamingo yako.

"Akhoza kuluma," Alice anayankha mochenjera, osafuna konse kuyesa kuyesako.

"Zowonadi," adatero a Duchess: "flamingo ndi mpiru zimaluma zonse ziwiri. Ndipo malingaliro ake ndi akuti, 'Mbalame za nthenga zimawulukira limodzi.'

Alice anati: “Mpiru wokha si mbalame.

"Chabwino, monga mwachizolowezi," adatero a Duchess, "ndi njira yomveka bwino yoyika zinthu!"

"Ndi mchere, ndikuganiza ," adatero Alice.

"Zowonadi," adatero a Duchess, omwe amawoneka okonzeka kuvomereza zonse zomwe Alice adanena; “Pali mgodi wawukulu wa mpiru pafupi ndi pano, ndipo mfundo yake ndi yakuti, ‘Pamene wanga achuluka, wanu achepa.

“O, ndikudziwa!” Alice, yemwe sanamvetserepo mawu omalizirawa, anati: “Ndi ndiwo zamasamba.

"Ndikugwirizana nanu," adatero a Duchess; “Ndipo khalidwe la zimenezo ndilo—‘Khalani chimene mukuwoneka kukhala’—kapena ngati mungafune kuti chifotokoze momvekera bwino—‘Musadziyerekeze kukhala wina ndi mnzake kusiyana ndi mmene zingaonekere kwa ena kuti zimene munali kapena mukanakhala sizinali mwanjira ina kusiyana ndi mmene munali zikanaonekera kwa iwo kukhala mwanjira ina.’”

"Ndikuganiza kuti ndiyenera kumvetsetsa bwino," adatero Alice mwaulemu kwambiri, "ndikanati ndilembe: koma sindingathe kuzitsatira momwe mukunenera."

"Palibe chomwe ndinganene ndikasankha," a Duchess adayankha mokondwera.

“Pempherani kuti musamadzivutitse kunena kuposa pamenepo,” anatero Alice.

“O, osalankhula zavuto!” adatero a Duchess. "Ndikupatsirani mphatso ya zonse zomwe ndanena pano."

Mphatso yotsika mtengo! anaganiza choncho Alice. “Ndili wokondwa kuti sapereka mphatso za tsiku lobadwa ngati zimenezo! Koma sanayerekeze kunena mokweza.

“Mukuganizanso?” anafunsa a Duchess uku akukumbanso chibwano chake chakuthwa.

“Ndili ndi ufulu woganiza,” anatero Alice mwaukali, chifukwa anayamba kuda nkhawa pang’ono.

"Chabwino," adatero a Duchess, "monga nkhumba zimawulukira; ndi m-"

Koma apa, modabwitsa kwambiri Alice, mawu a Duchess anafa, ngakhale pakati pa mawu omwe ankakonda kwambiri 'makhalidwe,' ndipo mkono womwe unalumikizidwa ndi iye unayamba kunjenjemera. Alice adayang'ana m'mwamba, ndipo adayimilira Mfumukazi patsogolo pawo, atapinda manja, akukwinya ngati mvula yamkuntho.

"Tsiku labwino, Mfumu!" a Duchess adayamba ndi mawu otsika, ofooka.

“Tsopano, ndikuchenjezani bwino,” inafuula motero Mfumukazi, ikupondaponda pansi pamene ikulankhula; "Kaya iwe kapena mutu wako uyenera kuchotsedwa, ndipo pasanapite nthawi yayitali! Tengani chisankho chanu!"

A Duchess adasankha zomwe adasankha, ndipo adapita kamphindi.

"Tiyeni tipitirize ndi masewera," Mfumukazi idauza Alice; ndipo Alice anachita mantha kwambiri kuti asalankhule mawu, koma pang'onopang'ono adamutsatira kumbuyo komwe kunali croquet.

Alendo enawo adatengerapo mwayi pakusowa kwa Mfumukazi, ndipo anali kupumula pamthunzi: komabe, atangomuwona, adathamangira kumasewerawo, Mfumukaziyo imangonena kuti kuchedwa kwakanthawi kungawononge miyoyo yawo.

Nthawi yonse yomwe ankasewera, Queen sanasiye kukangana ndi osewera ena, ndikufuula "Chotsani mutu!" kapena “Kusiya mutu wake!” Omwe adawaweruza adagwidwa ndi asitikali, omwe adayenera kusiya kukhala mabwalo kuti achite izi, kotero kuti pakutha kwa theka la ola kapena apo panalibe mabwalo otsala, ndipo osewera onse, kupatula Mfumu, Mfumukazi, ndi Alice, anali m'ndende ndi kuweruzidwa kuti aphedwe.

Kenako Mfumukaziyo idachoka, itatopa kwambiri, ndipo inati kwa Alice, "Kodi wamuwona Kamba Wonyoza?"

"Ayi," adatero Alice. "Sindikudziwa kuti Mock Turtle ndi chiyani."

"Ndi zomwe Msuzi wa Mock Turtle umapangidwira," adatero Mfumukazi.

"Sindinawonepo, kapena kumvapo," adatero Alice.

"Bwerani," adatero Mfumukazi, "ndipo adzakuuzani mbiri yake."

Pamene amachoka limodzi, Alice anamva Mfumu ikunena motsika, kwa kampaniyo nthawi zambiri, "Nonse mwakhululukidwa." "Bwerani, ndiye chinthu chabwino!" adadzilankhula yekha chifukwa sanasangalale ndi kuchuluka kwa kuphedwa komwe Mfumukazi idalamula.

Posakhalitsa anafika pa Gryphon, ili mtulo padzuwa. (Ngati simukudziwa kuti Gryphon ndi chiyani, yang'anani chithunzichi.) "Mmwamba, waulesi!" idatero Mfumukazi, "ndipo mutenge namwali uyu kuti mukawone Kamba Wonyoza, ndikumva mbiri yake. Ndiyenera kubwerera ndikuwona kuphedwa komwe ndalamula." ndipo adachoka, kumusiya Alice yekha ndi Gryphon. Alice sanasangalale ndi mawonekedwe a cholengedwacho, koma ponseponse adaganiza kuti zingakhale zotetezeka kukhala nazo ngati kutsatira Mfumukazi yankhanzayo: kotero adadikirira.

Gryphon adakhala tsonga ndikusisita m'maso mwake: kenako adayang'ana Mfumukaziyo mpaka idasowa: kenako idaseka. “Zosangalatsa bwanji!” adatero Gryphon, theka kwa iye yekha, theka kwa Alice.

"Kusangalatsa ndi chiyani?" Adatero Alice.

"Bwanji, iye ," anatero Gryphon. "Zonsezi ndi zomwe amakonda, kuti: sapha aliyense, mukudziwa. Bwerani!"

“Aliyense akuti 'bwerani!' kuno,” anaganiza motero Alice, akumatsatira pang’onopang’ono: “Sindinakhalepo wolongosoka chotero m’moyo wanga wonse, sindinatero!”

Sanapite patali asanaone Kamba Wachisilamu patali atakhala wachisoni komanso wosungulumwa pamiyala yaying'ono, ndipo atayandikira pafupi, Alice adamva akubuula ngati kuti mtima wake wasweka. Anamumvera chisoni kwambiri. "Chisoni chake ndi chiyani?" adafunsa Gryphon, ndipo Gryphon adayankha, pafupifupi m'mawu omwewo monga kale, "Ndizokonda zake zonse, kuti: alibe chisoni, mukudziwa. Bwerani!"

Choncho anapita kwa Kamba Wonyoza, yemwe anawayang'ana ndi maso aakulu odzaza misozi, koma sananene kanthu.

"Dona uyu pano," anatero Gryphon, "akufuna kudziwa mbiri yanu, akudziwa."

“Ndimuuza,” anatero Kamba Wonyengerera mozama, mopanda phokoso: “khalani pansi nonse, ndipo musalankhule kalikonse mpaka nditamaliza.”

Choncho anakhala pansi, ndipo palibe amene anayankhula kwa mphindi zingapo. Alice anaganiza mumtima mwake kuti, “Sindikuona kuti angamalize bwanji ngati sayamba. Koma anadikira moleza mtima.

“Nthaŵi ina,” anatero Kamba Wonyengerera pomalizira pake, akuusa moyo kwambiri, “Ndinali Kamba weniweni.

Mawu amenewa anatsatiridwa ndi bata lalitali kwambiri, losweka ndi kufuula kwa apo ndi apo kuti “Hjckrrh!” kuchokera ku Gryphon, ndikulira kosalekeza kwa Mock Turtle. Alice anatsala pang'ono kudzuka n'kunena kuti, “Zikomo bwana, chifukwa cha nkhani yanu yochititsa chidwiyi,” koma anangoganiza kuti payenera kukhala zambiri, choncho anakhala chete osanena chilichonse.

“Pamene tinali aang’ono,” Kamba Wakunyodola anapitirizabe, mofatsa, ngakhale akulira pang’ono apo ndi apo, “tinkapita kusukulu m’nyanja.

“N’chifukwa chiyani munamutcha kuti Kamba, ngati sanali mmodzi?” Adafunsa choncho Alice.

“Tinamutcha Kamba chifukwa anatiphunzitsa,” anatero Kamba Wakunyodola mokwiya: “ndiwe wogontha kwambiri!”

"Uyenera kuchita manyazi chifukwa chofunsa funso losavuta," anawonjezera Gryphon; kenako onse adakhala chete ndikumuyang'ana Alice wosauka yemwe adamva kuti ali pafupi kumira pansi. Potsirizira pake, Gryphon anauza Kamba Wonyoza, “Yenda, munthu wachikulire! ndipo anapitiriza kunena mawu awa.

“Inde, tinapita kusukulu m’nyanja, ngakhale kuti simungakhulupirire—”

“Sindinanenepo kuti sindinatero!” adamudula mawu Alice.

“Watero,” anatero Kamba Wonyoza.

“Gwira lilime lako!” anawonjezera Gryphon, Alice asanalankhulenso. Kamba wa Mock anapitirira.

“Tinali ndi maphunziro abwino koposa—inde, tinkapita kusukulu tsiku lililonse—”

“ Nanenso ndinapita kusukulu ya masana,” anatero Alice; "Simuyenera kukhala wonyada ngati zonsezo."

"Ndi zowonjezera?" Adafunsa modandaula Kambayo pang'ono.

“Inde,” anatero Alice, “tinaphunzira Chifulenchi ndi nyimbo.”

"Ndi kusamba?" Anatero Kamba Woseka.

Ayi ndithu! Adatelo Alice mokwiya.

“Aa, ndiye kuti sukulu yanuyo sinali yabwino kwenikweni,” anatero Kamba Wonyengererayo momasuka kwambiri. “Tsopano kwathu anali ndi chigamulo chomaliza, 'Chifalansa, nyimbo, ndi kuchapa - zowonjezera.'

“Simukanafuna zambiri,” anatero Alice; "kukhala pansi pa nyanja."

“Sindikanakwanitsa kuliphunzira.” Adatelo Kamba Woseka mosisima. "Ndinangotenga maphunziro okhazikika."

"Chimenecho chinali chiyani?" Adafunsa motele Alice.

“Kunjenjemera ndi Kugwedera, inde, kuyambira pomwe,” Kamba Wonyoza anayankha; "ndiyeno nthambi zosiyanasiyana za Arithmetic-Ambition, Distraction, Uglification, and Derision."

"Sindinamvepo za 'Uglification,' ” Alice adalimba mtima kunena. "Ndi chiyani?"

Gryphon adakweza miyendo yake yonse modabwa. Sindinamvepo zonyansa! idafuula. "Mukudziwa kuti kukongoletsa ndi chiyani, ndikuganiza?"

“Inde,” anatero Alice mokayikira: “zimatanthauza—kupanga—chilichonse—chokongola.”

"Chabwino, ndiye," a Gryphon adapitiliza, "ngati simukudziwa zomwe mungakhumudwitse, ndinu munthu wamba."

Alice sanasangalale kuti afunsenso mafunso ena okhudza nkhaniyi, choncho anatembenukira kwa Kamba wa Mock, n’kunena kuti, “Kodi n’chiyani chinanso chimene munaphunzira?”

"Chabwino, panali Zosatheka," Kamba Woseka anayankha, powerengera maphunziro omwe anali nawo, "Zobisika, zakale ndi zamakono, ndi Seaography: ndiye Drawling-Mbuye wa Drawling anali munthu wakale, yemwe ankabwera kamodzi pa sabata: ankatiphunzitsa Kukoka, Kutambasula, ndi Kukomoka mu Coils."

" Zinali bwanji?" Adatero Alice.

"Chabwino, sindingakuwonetseni ndekha," Kamba Wonyoza anati: "Ndine wouma kwambiri. Ndipo Gryphon sanaphunzirepo."

"Sindinatenge nthawi," anatero Gryphon: "Ndinapita kwa masters a Classics, komabe, anali nkhanu yakale, anali .

"Sindinapite kwa iye," Kamba wa Mock anatero ndi kupuma: "anaphunzitsa Kuseka ndi Chisoni, ankakonda kunena."

"Choncho adatero," adatero Gryphon, akuusa moyo; ndipo zamoyo ziwirizo zidabisa nkhope zawo m’dzanja lawo.

"Ndipo mumaphunzira maola angati patsiku?" Adatelo Alice mofulumira kusintha nkhani.

"Maola khumi tsiku loyamba," anatero Kamba Wonyoza: "9 koloko lotsatira, ndi zina zotero."

"Ndi dongosolo lodabwitsa bwanji!" anafuula Alice.

"Ndicho chifukwa chake amatchedwa maphunziro," a Gryphon adati: "chifukwa amachepa tsiku ndi tsiku."

Limeneli linali lingaliro lachilendo kwa Alice, ndipo analingalira pang’ono asananeneponso ndemanga ina. "Ndiye tsiku lakhumi ndi chimodzi liyenera kuti linali tchuthi?"

“Inde zinalidi choncho,” anatero Kamba Wonyengerera.

"Ndipo munakwanitsa bwanji pa 12?" Alice anapitiriza mwachidwi.

"Zakwanira pamaphunziro," a Gryphon adasokoneza ndi mawu oganiza bwino: "muwuzeni zina zamasewerawa tsopano."
