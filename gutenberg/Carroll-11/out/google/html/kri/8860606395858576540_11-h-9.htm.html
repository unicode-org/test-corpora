CHAPTER IX.
Di Mock Turtle in Stori

“Yu nɔ go ebul tink aw a gladi fɔ si yu bak, yu dia ol tin!” di Duchess se, as i tuck in an wit lɔv insay Alis in yon, ɛn dɛn waka go togɛda.

Alis bin rili gladi fɔ si am wit dis kayn fayn fayn wamat, ɛn i bin tink to insɛf se sɔntɛm na di pepa nɔmɔ bin mek i so savayj we dɛn mit na di kichin.

I bin kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se: “We a na Duchess, (nɔto insay wan tɔyn we rili gɛt op), “A nɔ go gɛt ɛni pepa na mi kichin atɔl . Sup kin du fayn fayn wan if a nɔ gɛt am—Sɔntɛm na pepa kin mek pipul dɛn wam ɔltɛm,” i bin kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk, ɛn i bin gladi bad bad wan fɔ we i dɔn fɛn wan nyu kayn lɔ, “ɛn vinega we kin mek dɛn saw—ɛn kamɔyl we kin mek dɛn sawnd bita—ɛn—ɛn bali-shuga ɛn dɛn kayn tin dɛn de we de mek pikin dɛn swit A jɔs wish se pipul dɛn bin no dat : den dɛn nɔ go so stingy bɔt am, yu no—”

I bin dɔn rili fɔgɛt di Duchess bay dis tɛm, ɛn i bin sɔprayz smɔl we i yɛri in vɔys nia in yes. "Yu de tink bɔt sɔntin, mi dia, ɛn dat de mek yu fɔgɛt fɔ tɔk. A nɔ go ebul tɛl yu jɔs naw wetin na di moral fɔ dat, bɔt a go mɛmba am smɔl."

Alis bin tray fɔ tɔk se: “Sɔntɛm i nɔ gɛt wan.”

“Tut, tut, pikin!” na so di Duchess bin se. “Ɔltin gɛt gud abit, if na yu nɔmɔ ebul fɔ fɛn am.” Ɛn i swɛt insɛf nia Alis in sayd we i de tɔk.

Alis nɔ bin lɛk fɔ de nia am so: fɔs, bikɔs di Duchess bin rili ɔg; ɛn sɛkɔn, bikɔs i bin rili ayt fɔ rɛst in chin pan Alis in sholda, ɛn na bin shap chin we nɔ bin fayn. Bɔt, i nɔ bin lɛk fɔ bi rud rud, so i bin de bia am fayn fayn wan.

I bin tɔk se: “Di gem de go bifo bɛtɛ naw,” bay we i de kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk smɔl.

Di Duchess bin se: “‘Tis so,” “ɛn di moral fɔ dat na—‘O, ‘tis lɔv, ‘tis lɔv, dat de mek di wɔl go rawnd!’”

Alis bin wispa se: “Sɔmbɔdi se na ɔlman we de tink bɔt in yon biznɛs de du am!”

Di Duchess se, “Ah, wɛl! I min bɔku pan di sem tin,” i dig in shap smɔl chin insay Alis in sholda as i de ad, “ɛn di moral fɔ dat na—‘Tek kia ɔf di sɛns, ɛn di sawnd dɛn go tek kia ɔf dɛnsɛf.’”

“I rili lɛk fɔ fɛn gud abit dɛn pan tin dɛn!” Alis bin de tink to insɛf.

“A dea fɔ se yu de wɔnda wetin mek a nɔ de put mi an rawnd yu wes,” di Duchess se afta i stɔp: “di rizin na, se a de dawt bɔt di vɛks we yu flamingo de vɛks. A go tray di ɛkspiriɛns?”

Alis nɔ bin de wɔri atɔl fɔ mek dɛn tray fɔ du di ɛkspiriɛns se: “I kin bit.”

Di Duchess bin se: “I rili tru: flamingo ɛn mɔstad sid dɛn ɔl tu kin bit. Ɛn di gud abit fɔ dat na—‘Bɔd dɛn we gɛt fɛda kin gɛda togɛda.’”

Alis bin tɔk se: “Na mɔstad nɔmɔ nɔto bɔd.

Di Duchess se: “Rayt, lɛk aw yu kin du ɔltɛm: “na klia we yu gɛt fɔ put tin dɛn!”

Alis bin tɔk se: “Na minral, a tink se .”

Di Duchess we bin tan lɛk se i rɛdi fɔ gri wit ɔl wetin Alis tɔk bin tɔk se: “Fɔ tru, na so i bi; “wan big mɔstad-mayn de nia ya. Ɛn di gud abit fɔ dat na—‘Di mɔ we mi yon, na di mɔ yu yon nɔ de bɔku.’”

“O, a no!” Alis we nɔ bin atɛnd dis las tɔk bin ala se, “na vɛjitebul. I nɔ tan lɛk wan, bɔt na so i tan.”

Di Duchess se: “A rili gri wit yu; “ɛn di gud abit fɔ dat na—‘Bi wetin yu go tan lɛk se yu bi’—ɔ if yu want fɔ tɔk am simpul wan—‘Nɔ ɛva imajin se yu nɔ go bi ɔda tin pas wetin i go tan lɛk to ɔda pipul dɛn se wetin yu bin bi ɔ yu go dɔn bi nɔto ɔda we pas aw yu bin fɔ dɔn tan lɛk se na ɔda we.’”

Alis bin tɔk fayn fayn wan se: “A tink se a fɔ ɔndastand dat bɛtɛ, if a bin dɔn rayt am: bɔt a nɔ ebul fɔ fala am gud gud wan lɛk aw yu se am.”

“Dat nɔto natin to wetin a go se if a pik,” di Duchess ansa, wit gladi at.

Alis bin tɔk se: “Pre nɔ trɔbul yusɛf fɔ tɔk am lɔng pas dat.”

“O, nɔ tɔk bɔt trɔbul!” na so di Duchess bin se. “A de mek yu prɛzɛnt fɔ ɔl wetin a dɔn tɔk as yet.”

“Na wan kayn prɛzɛnt we nɔ dia!” Alis bin de tink. “A gladi we dɛn nɔ de gi batde prɛzɛnt lɛk dat!” Bɔt i nɔ bin tray fɔ tɔk am lawd wan.

“Yu de tink bak?” di Duchess aks, wit wan ɔda dig pan in shap smɔl chin.

Alis bin tɔk shap wan se: “A gɛt rayt fɔ tink, bikɔs i bin bigin fɔ wɔri smɔl.

Di Duchess bin se: “Jɔs lɛk aw pig dɛn fɔ flay, ɛn di m—”

Bɔt na ya, Alis bin sɔprayz bad bad wan we di Duchess in vɔys day, ivin na di midul pan in fav wɔd ‘moral,’ ɛn di an we bin link to in yon bigin fɔ shek. Alis luk ɔp, ɛn di Kwin tinap bifo dɛn, i fold in an dɛn, i de kray lɛk tɛnda.

“Na fayn de, yu Majesty!” di Duchess bigin wit wan lawd, wik vɔys.

“Naw, a de gi yu fayn wɔnin,” di Kwin ala, i stamp na grɔn as i de tɔk; “yu ɔ yu ed fɔ dɔn ɔf, ɛn dat insay lɛk af tɛm so! Tek yu choice!”

Di Duchess tek in choice, ɛn i nɔ bin de igen insay smɔl tɛm.

Di Kwin tɛl Alis se: “Lɛ wi go bifo wit di gem; ɛn Alis bin fred tumɔs fɔ tɔk wan wɔd, bɔt smɔl smɔl i fala am bak go na di kroket-grɔn.

Di ɔda gɔst dɛn bin dɔn tek advantej pan di we aw di Kwin nɔ bin de, ɛn dɛn bin de rɛst na di shed: bɔt, di tɛm we dɛn si am, dɛn rɔsh go bak na di gem, di Kwin jɔs tɔk se if dɛn delay fɔ smɔl tɛm, dat go lɔs dɛn layf.

Ɔl di tɛm we dɛn bin de ple di Kwin nɔ ɛva lɛf fɔ agyu wit di ɔda pleya dɛn, ɛn ala se “Off wit in ed!” ɔ “Of wit in ed!” Di wan dɛn we i bin sɛntɛns, di sojaman dɛn bin tek dɛn na kɔstɔ, we fɔ tru, dɛn bin gɛt fɔ lɛf fɔ bi arch fɔ du dis, so dat we af awa ɔ so dɔn, no arch nɔ bin de lɛf, ɛn ɔl di pleya dɛn, pas di Kiŋ, di Kwin, ɛn Alis, bin de na kɔstɔm ɛn ɔnda sɛntɛns fɔ kil.

Dɔn di Kwin lɛf, i nɔ bin gɛt bɛtɛ briz, ɛn tɛl Alis se, “Yu dɔn si di Mɔk Tɔl yet?”

Alis bin tɔk se: “Nɔ. “A nɔ ivin no wetin na Mɔk Tɔl.”

Di Kwin se, “Na di tin we dɛn mek Mock Turtle Soup wit.”

Alis bin tɔk se: “A nɔ ɛva si wan, ɔ yɛri bɔt wan.

Di Kwin se, “Kam, so, i go tɛl yu in istri.”

As dɛn de waka togɛda, Alis yɛri di Kiŋ de tɔk wit lɔw vɔys, to di kɔmni jɔs se, “Dɛn dɔn fɔgiv una ɔl.” “Kam, dat na gud tin!” i se to insɛf, bikɔs i nɔ bin rili gladi fɔ di nɔmba fɔ di kil we di Kwin bin dɔn tɛl am fɔ kil.

I nɔ tu te, dɛn kam mit wan Grifon, we bin de slip fast fast na di san. (If yu nɔ no wetin na Gryphon, luk di pikchɔ.) “Up, les tin!” di Kwin se, “ɛn tek dis yɔŋ uman fɔ go si di Mɔk Tɔl, ɛn fɔ yɛri in istri. A fɔ go bak ɛn si afta sɔm kil we a dɔn ɔda;” ɛn i waka go, lɛf Alis in wan wit di Grifon. Alis nɔ bin rili lɛk di we aw di krichɔ luk, bɔt pan ɔl we i tink se i go rili sef fɔ de wit am lɛk fɔ go afta da savayj Kwin de: so i wet.

Di Grifon sidɔm ɛn rɔb in yay: dɔn i wach di Kwin te i nɔ de si am: dɔn i laf. “I rili fayn!” di Grifon se, af to insɛf, af to Alis.

“Wetin na di ɛnjɔymɛnt?” na so Alis bin se.

Di Grifon bin tɔk se: “Wetin mek, i .” “Na ɔl in fansi, dat: dɛn nɔ de ɛva kil ɛnibɔdi, yu no. Kam!”

“Ɔlman kin se ‘kam!’. na ya,” na so Alis bin de tink, as i de go afta am sloslo: “A nɔ ɛva gɛt ɔda ɔda tin dɛn so insay mi layf, nɔ ɛva!”

Dɛn nɔ bin dɔn go fa bifo dɛn si di Mɔk Tɔl fa, i sidɔm sad ɛn in wan pan wan smɔl ledj pan rɔk, ɛn, as dɛn de kam nia, Alis bin de yɛri am de swɛt lɛk se in at go brok. I bin rili sɔri fɔ am. “Wetin na in sɔri?” i aks di Grifon, ɛn di Grifon ansa, rili klos wit di sem wɔd dɛn we i bin de tɔk bifo, “Na ɔl in fansi, dat: i nɔ gɛt ɛni sɔri-at, yu no. Kam!”

So dɛn go ɔp to di Mɔk Tɔl, we luk dɛn wit big yay we ful-ɔp wit kray wata, bɔt i nɔ tɔk natin.

Di Grifon se: “Dis yɔŋ uman we de ya, i want fɔ no yu istri, i want.”

Di Mɔk Tɔl tɔk wit dip, olo vɔys se: “A go tɛl am: una ɔl tu sidɔm, ɛn una nɔ tɔk wan wɔd te a dɔn.”

So dɛn sidɔm, ɛn nɔbɔdi nɔ tɔk fɔ sɔm minit. Alis bin tink to insɛf se, “A nɔ si aw i go ɛva dɔn, if i nɔ bigin.” Bɔt i bin peshɛnt wet.

Di Mɔk Tɔl se las las, wit dip swɛt se: “Wan tɛm, a na bin rial Tɔl.”

Afta dɛn wɔd ya, dɛn bin de silɛns fɔ lɔng tɛm, ɛn na wan wan tɛm nɔmɔ dɛn bin de ala se “Hjckrrh!” frɔm di Grifon, ɛn di ebi ebi kray we di Mɔk Tɔl bin de kray ɔltɛm. Alis bin rili nia fɔ grap ɛn se, “Tɛnki, sa, fɔ yu intrestin stori,” bɔt i nɔ bin ebul fɔ stɔp fɔ tink se mɔ go mɔs de fɔ kam, so i sidɔm stil ɛn nɔ tɔk natin.

“We wi bin smɔl,” di Mɔk Tɔl bin kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk las las, wit mɔ kol at, pan ɔl we i bin stil de kray smɔl wan wan tɛm, “wi bin de go skul na di si. Di masta na bin ol Tɔl—wi bin de kɔl am Tɔtɔ—.”

“Wetin mek yu kɔl am Tɔtɔ, if i nɔto wan?” Alis bin aks.

Di Mɔk Tɔl bin vɛks ɛn tɔk se: “Wi bin de kɔl am Tɔtɔ bikɔs na in tich wi: fɔ tru, yu rili dɔl!”

Di Grifon bin tɔk bak se: “Yu fɔ shem fɔ we yu aks dis kayn simpul kwɛstyɔn; ɛn afta dat dɛn ɔl tu sidɔm kwayɛt wan ɛn luk po Alis, we bin fil se i rɛdi fɔ sink insay di wɔl. Las las di Grifon se to di Mɔk Tɔl se, “Drayv go, ol man! Nɔ de ɔl de bɔt am!” ɛn i kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk dɛn wɔd ya:

“Yɛs, wi bin go skul na di si, pan ɔl we yu nɔ go biliv—”

“A nɔ ɛva se a nɔ!” Alis bin ambɔg am.

Di Mɔk Tɔl se: “Yu bin du am.”

“Hold yu tong!” di Grifon ad, bifo Alis ebul fɔ tɔk bak. Di Mock Turtle bin go bifo.

“Wi bin gɛt di bɛst edyukeshɔn dɛn—infakt, wi bin de go skul ɛvride—”

Alis bin tɔk se: “ Misɛf dɔn go na wan de skul; “yu nɔ nid fɔ prawd so lɛk ɔl dat.”

“Wit ɛkstra tin dɛn?” aks di Mɔk Tɔl wit wɔri smɔl.

Alis bin tɔk se: “Yɛs, wi lan Frɛnch ɛn myuzik.”

“Ɛn fɔ was?” na so di Mock Turtle bin se.

“Fɔ tru, nɔto so!” Alis bin tɔk wit wamat.

Di Mɔk Tɔl tɔk wit vɔys we rili fil fri, se: “A! den yu yon nɔto bin rili gud skul.” “Naw na wi yon dɛn bin gɛt na di ɛnd pan di bil, ‘French, myuzik, ɛn was —ɛkstra.’”

Alis bin tɔk se: “Yu nɔ bin fɔ dɔn want am tumɔs; “we de liv dɔŋ di si.”

“A nɔ bin ebul fɔ lan am.” di Mɔk Tɔl se wit wan sigrɛt. “Na di rɛgyula kɔs nɔmɔ a bin de tek.”

“Wetin na bin dat?” Alis bin aks.

Di Mock Turtle ansa se: “Reeling and Writhing, na tru, fɔ bigin; “ɛn afta dat di difrɛn branch dɛn na Aritmɛtik—Ambition, Distraction, Uglification, ɛn Derision.”

Alis bin tray fɔ tɔk se: “A nɔ ɛva yɛri bɔt ‘Uglification.’ “Wetin na am?”

Di Grifon es in tu fut dɛn ɔp wit sɔprayz. “Wetin! Nɔ ɛva yɛri bɔt ɔglif!” i bin ala. “Yu no wetin fɔ mek pɔsin fayn, a tink se?”

Alis bin tɔk wit dawt se: “Yɛs: i min—fɔ—mek—ɛnitin—fayn.”

Di Grifon kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se: “Wɛl, so, if yu nɔ no wetin fɔ ɔglify, yu na simpul pɔsin.”

Alis nɔ bin fil se dɛn dɔn ɛnkɔrej am fɔ aks ɛni ɔda kwɛstyɔn bɔt am, so i tɔn to di Mɔk Tɔl, ɛn se “Wetin ɔda tin yu bin gɛt fɔ lan?”

“Wɛl, Mistɛri bin de,” di Mɔk Tɔl ansa, as i de kɔnt di sɔbjɛkt dɛn we de na in flapa, “—Mistɛri, we bin de trade ɛn we de naw, wit Siografi: dɔn Drawling—di Drawling-masta na bin wan ol kɔnga-il, we bin de kam wan tɛm insay di wik: i bin de tich wi Drawling, Stretching, ɛn Fainting in Coils.”

“Aw dat bin tan lɛk?” na so Alis bin se.

Di Mɔk Tɔl se: “Wɛl, a nɔ go ebul fɔ sho am yu misɛf: A tu stif. Ɛn di Grifɔn nɔ ɛva lan am.”

Di Grifon bin tɔk se: “A nɔ bin gɛt tɛm: Bɔt a bin go to di Klasik masta.I na bin ol krab, na in na bin ol.”

Di Mɔk Tɔl bin tɔk wit swɛt se: “A nɔ ɛva go to am: “i bin de tich Laf ɛn Grif, dɛn bin de se.”

“So i du, so i du,” di Grifon se, as i de swɛt insay in turn; ɛn ɔl tu di tin dɛn we Gɔd mek bin ayd dɛn fes na dɛn fut.

“Ɛn ɔmɔs awa yu bin de du lɛsin dɛn insay di de?” Alis se, i bin de rɔsh fɔ chenj di tɔpik.

Di Mɔk Tɔl bin tɔk se: “Tɛn awa di fɔs de: nayn di nɛks de, ɛn so de go.”

“Na plan we pɔsin kin want fɔ no mɔ!” Alis bin ala.

Di Grifon bin tɔk se: “Na dat mek dɛn kɔl dɛn lɛsin bikɔs dɛn de stɔp frɔm de to de.”

Dis na bin nyu tin to Alis, ɛn i bin tink bɔt am smɔl bifo i tɔk in nɛks tɔk. “Den di de we mek ilevin go mɔs dɔn bi ɔlide?”

Di Mɔk Tɔl bin tɔk se: “Fɔ tru, na so i bin bi.”

“Ɛn aw yu bin ebul fɔ du di de we mek twɛlv?” Alis bin kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk wit ɔl in at.

“Dat na inof bɔt lɛsin,” di Grifon intarɔp wit wan vɔys we rili disayd: “tɛl am sɔntin bɔt di gem dɛn naw.”
