CHAPTER V.
Advays frɔm wan Katapila

Di Katapila ɛn Alis luk dɛnsɛf fɔ sɔm tɛm kwayɛt wan: las las di Katapila pul di huk kɔmɔt na in mɔt, ɛn adrɛs am wit vɔys we nɔ gɛt bɛtɛ trɛnk ɛn we de slip.

Di Katapila se: “Udat yu na? ”

Dis nɔto bin ɛnkɔrejmɛnt fɔ opin fɔ tɔk to pipul dɛn. Alis ansa am, i bin shem smɔl se, “Mi—a nɔ rili no, sa, jɔs naw—at least a no udat a bi we a grap dis mɔnin, bɔt a tink se dɛn go dɔn chenj mi bɔku tɛm frɔm da tɛm de.”

“Wetin yu min wit dat?” di Katapila bin tɔk tranga wan. “Ɛksplen yusɛf!”

Alis se: “A nɔ ebul fɔ ɛksplen misɛf , a de fred, sa, bikɔs a nɔto misɛf, yu si.”

Di Katapila se: “A nɔ de si.”

Alis ansa am fayn fayn wan se: “A de fred se a nɔ go ebul fɔ tɔk am klia wan, bikɔs a nɔ ebul fɔ ɔndastand am misɛf fɔ bigin, ɛn fɔ bi bɔku difrɛn saiz dɛn insay wan de kin rili kɔnfyus.”

Di Katapila se: “I nɔ de so.”

Alis se: “Wɛl, sɔntɛm yu nɔ fɛn am so yet; “bɔt we yu gɛt fɔ tɔn to kraysalis—yu go du am sɔm de, yu no—ɛn afta dat to bɔtaflay, a fɔ tink se yu go fil am smɔl smɔl, nɔto so?”

Di Katapila se: “Nɔto smɔl.

Alis bin tɔk se: “Wɛl, sɔntɛm di we aw yu de fil go difrɛn; “ɔl wetin a no na dat, i go fil rili queer to mi .”

"Yu!" di Katapila bin tɔk wit disgres. "Udat na yu? "

We mek dɛn kam bak bak to di biginin fɔ di tɔk. Alis bin fil bad smɔl we di Katapila tɔk dɛn kayn rili shɔt wɔd dɛn de, ɛn i drɔ insɛf ɛn se, rili siriɔs wan, “A tink se, yu fɔ tɛl mi udat yu bi, fɔs.”

"Wetin du?" na so di Katapila bin se.

Na wan ɔda kwɛstyɔn we bin de mek pɔsin kɔnfyus; ɛn as Alis nɔ bin ebul fɔ tink bɔt ɛni gud rizin, ɛn as i bin tan lɛk se di Katapila in maynd nɔ bin rili fayn, i tɔn bak.

“Kam bak!” di Katapila kɔl afta am. “A gɛt sɔntin we impɔtant fɔ tɔk!”

Dis bin tan lɛk se i prɔmis, fɔ tru: Alis tɔn ɛn kam bak bak.

Di Katapila se: “Kip yu vɛks.”

“Na dat nɔmɔ?” Alis se, i swɛla in wamat as i ebul.

Di Katapila se: “Nɔ.”

Alis bin tink se i go fayn fɔ wet, jɔs lɛk aw i nɔ gɛt ɔda tin fɔ du, ɛn sɔntɛm afta ɔl i go tɛl am sɔntin we fayn fɔ yɛri. Fɔ sɔm minit i bin de blo ɛn nɔ tɔk, bɔt las las i opin in an dɛn, pul di huk na in mɔt bak, ɛn se, “So yu tink se yu dɔn chenj, nɔto so?”

Alis se: “A de fred se na mi de, sa; “A nɔ ebul fɔ mɛmba tin dɛn lɛk aw a bin de yuz am—ɛn a nɔ de kip di sem saiz fɔ tɛn minit togɛda!”

“Yu nɔ ebul fɔ mɛmba us tin dɛn?” na so di Katapila bin se.

“Wɛl, a dɔn tray fɔ se “Aw di smɔl bizi bi,” bɔt ɔltin kam difrɛn!” Alis ansa am wit vɔys we rili fil bad.

Di Katapila bin tɔk se: “Ripɛt, ‘ Yu dɔn ol, Fada Wiliam .’”

Alis fol in an dɛn, ɛn bigin fɔ:—

Di yɔŋ man se, “Yu dɔn ol, Fada Wiliam.”
“Ɛn yu ia dɔn wayt bad bad wan;
Ɛn pan ɔl dat, yu de tinap na yu ed ɔltɛm—
Yu tink se, na yu ej, i rayt?”

Fada Wiliam bin ansa in pikin, .
“A bin de fred se i go wund di bren;
Bɔt, naw we a dɔn rili shɔ se a nɔ gɛt ɛni wan, .
Wetin mek, a de du am bak ɛn bak.”

Di yɔŋ man bin tɔk se: “Yu dɔn ol, lɛk aw a bin dɔn tɔk, .
Ɛn dɛn dɔn gro fat we nɔ kɔmɔn pas ɔl;
Bɔt stil yu tɔn wan bak-sɔmsal insay na di domɔt—
Pre, wetin na di rizin fɔ dat?”

“Insay mi yɔŋ,” di sɛj se, as i de shek in grey lɔk dɛn, .
“A bin de kip ɔl mi an ɛn fut dɛn rili saful
Na bay we dɛn yuz dis ɔnt—wan shilin di bɔks—
Alaw mi fɔ sɛl yu man ɛn in wɛf?”

Di yɔŋ man bin tɔk se: “Yu dɔn ol, ɛn yu jaw dɛn tu wik
Fɔ ɛnitin we tranga pas suet;
Bɔt stil yu dɔn di gɔs, wit di bon dɛn ɛn di mɔt—
Pre, aw yu bin ebul fɔ du am?”

In papa bin tɔk se: “We a bin yɔŋ, a bin tek di lɔ, .
Ɛn a bin de agyu ɛni kes wit mi wɛf;
Ɛn di mɔsul trɛnk, we i bin gi mi jaw, .
I dɔn las fɔ di res ɔf mi layf.”

Di yɔŋ man bin tɔk se: “Yu dɔn ol, i nɔ go izi fɔ mek pɔsin tink
Dat yu yay bin stedi lɛk aw i bin de bifo;
Bɔt stil yu balans wan il na di ɛnd pan yu nos—
Wetin mek yu gɛt sɛns bad bad wan?”

“A dɔn ansa tri kwɛstyɔn dɛn, ɛn dat dɔn du fɔ am,”
In papa bin se; “nɔ gi yusɛf ɛya!
Yu tink se a kin lisin ɔl di de to dɛn kayn tin dɛn de?
Bi ɔf, ɔ a go kik yu dɔŋ stej!”

Di Katapila bin tɔk se: “Dat nɔ rayt.”

Alis bin de shem ɛn tɔk se: “I nɔ rili rayt, a de fred; “sɔm pan di wɔd dɛn dɔn chenj.”

Di Katapila tɔk wit disayd se: “I nɔ rayt frɔm di biginin te to di ɛnd,” ɛn dɛn nɔ tɔk natin fɔ sɔm minit.

Di Katapila na bin di fɔs pɔsin we tɔk.

“Us saiz yu want fɔ bi?” i bin aks.

Alis bin ansa kwik kwik wan se: “O, a nɔ patikyula fɔ saiz; “na wan nɔmɔ nɔ lɛk fɔ chenj so ɔltɛm, yu no.”

Di Katapila se: “A nɔ no.”

Alis nɔ bin tɔk natin: dɛn nɔ bin ɛva tɔk bɔt am so na in layf bifo, ɛn i bin fil se i nɔ de vɛks igen.

“Yu satisfay naw?” na so di Katapila bin se.

Alis bin tɔk se: “Wɛl, a fɔ lɛk fɔ big smɔl , sa, if yu nɔ go min: tri inch na so bad ayt fɔ bi.”

“Na rili gud ayt fɔ tru!” di Katapila se wit wamat, i es insɛf stret as i de tɔk (i bin ay jɔs tri inch).

“Bɔt a nɔ yus to am!” bin beg po Alis wit sɔri vɔys. Ɛn i bin de tink bɔt insɛf se, “A wish se di tin dɛn we Gɔd mek nɔ fɔ vɛks so izi!”

Di Katapila se: “Yu go yus to am as tɛm de go; ɛn i put di huk na in mɔt ɛn bigin fɔ smok bak.

Dis tɛm Alis bin peshɛnt wet te i disayd fɔ tɔk bak. Insay wan ɔ tu minit di Katapila pul di huk na in mɔt ɛn yawn wan ɔ tu tɛm, ɛn shek insɛf. Dɔn i kɔmɔt na di mɔs, ɛn kray go na di gras, i jɔs de tɔk as i de go, “Wan say go mek yu gro lɔng, ɛn di ɔda say go mek yu gro shɔt.”

Alis bin tink to insɛf se: “Wan say na wetin? Di ɔda say na wetin?

Di Katapila se, “Fɔ di mɔs,” jɔs lɛk se i aks am lawd wan; ɛn insay wan ɔda tɛm, i nɔ bin de si am.

Alis bin kɔntinyu fɔ de luk di mɔs wit tink gud wan fɔ wan minit, i bin de tray fɔ no uswan na di tu say dɛn pan am; ɛn as i bin pafɛkt rawnd, i bin si se dis na kwɛstyɔn we rili at. Bɔt las las i strɛch in an dɛn rawnd am as fa as dɛn go go, ɛn brok smɔl pan di ed wit ɛni an.

“Ɛn naw uswan na uswan?” i se to insɛf, ɛn nibbul smɔl pan di rayt-an bit fɔ tray di ifɛkt: di nɛks tɛm i fil wan vaylɛnt blo ɔnda in chin: i bin dɔn nak in fut!

I bin rili fred fɔ dis chenj we bin apin wantɛm wantɛm, bɔt i bin fil se tɛm nɔ de fɔ lɔs, bikɔs i bin de shɔtin kwik kwik wan; so i bigin fɔ wok wan tɛm fɔ it sɔm pan di ɔda bit. In chin bin so klos pan in fut, dat i nɔ bin izi fɔ mek i opin in mɔt; bɔt i du am las las, ɛn i ebul fɔ swɛla wan smɔl pat pan di lɛft an bit.

“Kam, mi ed dɔn fri las las!” Alis se wit gladi at, we chenj to alarm insay wan ɔda mɔnt, we i si se in sholda nɔ de ɛnisay: ɔl wetin i bin ebul fɔ si, we i luk dɔŋ, na wan big big lɔng nɛk, we bin tan lɛk se i de rayz lɛk wan stɛp we kɔmɔt na wan si we gɛt grɔn lif dɛn we de fa fawe dɔŋ am.

“Wetin ɔl da grɔn tin de kin bi?” na so Alis bin se. “Ɛn usay mi sholda dɛn dɔn rich? Ɛn o, mi po an dɛn, aw i bi se a nɔ ebul fɔ si yu?” I bin de muv dɛn rawnd as i de tɔk, bɔt i bin tan lɛk se no rizɔlt nɔ bin de fala am, pas fɔ shek smɔl bitwin di grɔn lif dɛn we bin de fa fawe.

As i bin tan lɛk se chans nɔ de fɔ mek in an dɛn go ɔp to in ed, i tray fɔ put in ed dɔŋ to dɛn, ɛn i bin gladi fɔ si se in nɛk go bɛn izi wan na ɛni say, lɛk snek. I bin jɔs dɔn ebul fɔ kɔba am dɔŋ to wan fayn fayn zigzag, ɛn i bin de go dayv insay midul di lif dɛn, we i si se nɔto natin pas di tik dɛn we i bin dɔn de waka ɔnda, in ed, we wan shap his mek i drɔ bak kwik kwik wan: wan big pijin bin dɔn flay insay in fes, ɛn i bin de bit am bad bad wan wit in wing dɛn.

“Snek!” di Pijin bin ala.

“Mi nɔto snek!” Alis bin tɔk wit wamat. “Lɛf mi!”

“Snek, a de se bak!” ripit di Pijin, bɔt wit mɔ subdued tɔyn, ɛn ad wit wan kayn sob, “A dɔn tray ɔl we, ɛn i tan lɛk se natin nɔ fit dɛn!”

Alis bin tɔk se: “A nɔ no di smɔl tin bɔt wetin yu de tɔk bɔt.”

“A dɔn tray di tik dɛn rut, ɛn a dɔn tray bank, ɛn a dɔn tray hed,” di Pijin kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk, ɛn i nɔ atɛnd am; “bɔt dɛn snek dɛn de! Notin nɔ de we go mek dɛn gladi!”

Alis bin de kɔnfyus mɔ ɛn mɔ, bɔt i bin tink se i nɔ yus fɔ tɔk ɛnitin mɔ te di Pijin dɔn.

Di Pijin se: “I tan lɛk se i nɔ gɛt prɔblɛm fɔ hatch di eg dɛn; “bɔt a go mɔs de wach fɔ snek dɛn nɛt ɛn de! Wetin mek, a nɔ gɛt wan wink fɔ slip dis tri wiks!”

Alis we bin bigin fɔ si wetin i min, bin tɔk se: “A rili sɔri se yu dɔn vɛks.”

“Ɛn jɔs lɛk aw a bin dɔn tek di tik we ay pas ɔl na di wud,” di Pijin kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk, ɛn i es in vɔys to wan ala ala, “ɛn jɔs lɛk aw a bin de tink se a fɔ fri frɔm dɛn las las, dɛn go mɔs nid fɔ kam rɔtin kɔmɔt na di skay! Ugh, Snek!”

“Bɔt a nɔto snek, a de tɛl yu!” na so Alis bin se. “Mi na—mi na—”

“Wɛl! Wetin yu bi?” na so di Pijin se. “A de si se yu de tray fɔ mek sɔntin!”

As i mɛmba di nɔmba fɔ di chenj dɛn we i bin dɔn go tru da de de, Alis bin tɔk se: “Mi—mi na smɔl titi.”

“Na stori we go mɔs bi fɔ tru!” di Pijin se wit wan tɔyn we de sho se i rili et am. "A dɔn si bɔku bɔku smɔl gyal pikin dɛn insay mi tɛm, bɔt a nɔ ɛva si wan we gɛt da kayn nɛk de! Nɔ, nɔ! Yu na snek; ɛn i nɔ yus fɔ dinay am. A tink se yu go de tɛl mi nɛks se yu nɔ ɛva test eg!”

Alis we na bin pikin we bin rili de tɔk tru bin tɔk se: “A dɔn test eg, fɔ tru; “bɔt smɔl gyal pikin dɛn kin it eg jɔs lɛk aw snek kin it, yu no.”

Di Pijin se: “A nɔ biliv am; “bɔt if dɛn du dat, wetin mek den dɛn na wan kayn snek, na dat nɔmɔ a go se.”

Dis na bin so nyu aidia to Alis, dat i bin rili kwayɛt fɔ wan ɔ tu minit, we gi di Pijin di chans fɔ ad se, “Yu de luk fɔ eg, a no dat gud gud wan;ɛn wetin i impɔtant to mi if yu na smɔl titi ɔ yu na snek?”

Alis bin tɔk kwik kwik wan se: “I impɔtant to mi bad bad wan; “bɔt a nɔ de luk fɔ eg, lɛk aw i kin apin;ɛn if a bin de luk fɔ, a nɔ fɔ want yu yon : A nɔ lɛk dɛn raw.”

“Wɛl, bi ɔf, so!” di Pijin se wit sulky tone, as i setul bak insay in nɛst. Alis bin de kray midul di tik dɛn fayn fayn wan, bikɔs in nɛk bin de kɔntinyu fɔ miks bitwin di branch dɛn, ɛn wan wan tɛm i bin gɛt fɔ stɔp ɛn pul am. Afta sɔm tɛm i mɛmba se i stil ol di pis mɔs na in an, ɛn i bigin fɔ wok fayn fayn wan, i de swɛt fɔs pan wan ɛn afta dat i de swɛt di ɔda wan, ɛn i de gro sɔm tɛm dɛn fɔ lɔng ɛn sɔm tɛm dɛn de shɔt, te i dɔn ebul fɔ briŋ insɛf dɔŋ to di ayt we i kin gɛt.

I bin so te we i nɔ bin dɔn bi ɛnitin we de nia di rayt saiz, dat i bin tan lɛk se i rili strenj fɔs; bɔt i bin yus to am insay sɔm minit, ɛn bigin fɔ tɔk to insɛf, lɛk aw i kin tɔk. "Kam, na af mi plan dɔn dɔn naw! Aw ɔl dɛn chenj ya de mek a kɔnfyus! A nɔ de ɛva shɔ wetin a go bi, frɔm wan minit to ɔda wan! Bɔt, a dɔn kam bak to mi rayt saiz: di nɛks tin na, fɔ go insay da fayn fayn gadin de— aw dat fɔ du, a de wɔnda?” As i de tɔk dis, i kam wantɛm wantɛm na wan ples we opin, we gɛt wan smɔl os insay de we ay lɛk 4 fit. Alis bin tink se: “Ɛnibɔdi we de de, i nɔ go ɛva du fɔ kam pan dɛn dis saiz: wetin mek, a fɔ mek dɛn fred bikɔs dɛn nɔ gɛt sɛns!” So i bigin fɔ nib di raytan bit bak, ɛn i nɔ venchɔ fɔ go nia di os te i dɔn briŋ insɛf dɔŋ to nayn inch ay.
