CHAPTER XII.
Di Pruf we Alis bin gi

"Naya!" Alis kray, i rili fɔgɛt insay di flurry fɔ di moment aw big i dɔn gro insay di las minit, ɛn i jomp ɔp wit so rɔsh dat i tip oba di juri-bɔks wit di edj fɔ in skit, mek ɔl di juriman dɛn vɛks pan di ed fɔ di krawd we de dɔŋ, ɛn na de dɛn ledɔm de skata rawnd, mɛmba am bɔku bɔt wan glob gold fish we i bin aksidɛntli vɛks di wik bifo.

“O, a de beg yu fɔ fɔgiv mi!” i ala wit wan tɔyn we rili fil bad, ɛn bigin fɔ pik dɛn bak kwik kwik wan, bikɔs di aksidɛnt we di gold fish bin gɛt bin de kɔntinyu fɔ rɔn na in ed, ɛn i bin gɛt wan kayn aidia we nɔ klia se dɛn fɔ gɛda dɛn wan tɛm ɛn put dɛn bak insay di juri-bɔks, ɔ dɛn go day.

Di Kiŋ tɔk wit vɔys we rili siriɔs se: “Di jɔj nɔ go ebul fɔ go bifo, te ɔl di jɔj dɛn kam bak na dɛn rayt ples— ɔl ,” i ripit wit big ɛmpɛshmɛnt, ɛn luk Alis tranga wan we i de tɔk so.

Alis luk di juri-bɔks, ɛn si se, insay in rɔsh, i dɔn put di Lizad in ed dɔŋ, ɛn di po smɔl tin bin de wev in tel rawnd insay wan melankɔli we, i nɔ bin rili ebul fɔ muv. I nɔ tu te, i pul am bak, ɛn put am rayt; i bin tɔk to insɛf se: “nɔto se i min bɔku tin; “A fɔ tink se i go rili yus na di trayal wan we ɔp lɛk di ɔda we.”

As di jɔj dɛn dɔn wɛl smɔl frɔm di shɔk we dɛn bin gɛt we dɛn vɛks, ɛn dɛn dɔn fɛn dɛn slet ɛn pensil dɛn ɛn gi dɛn bak, dɛn bigin fɔ wok tranga wan fɔ rayt wan istri bɔt di aksidɛnt, ɔl pas di Lizad, we i bin tan lɛk se i tu win fɔ du ɛnitin pas fɔ sidɔm wit in mɔt opin, de luk ɔp na di kɔt in ruf.

“Wetin yu no bɔt dis biznɛs?” di Kiŋ tɛl Alis.

Alis bin tɔk se: “Natin nɔ de.

Di Kiŋ bin kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se: “Natin nɔ de?

Alis bin tɔk se: “Natin nɔ de.

Di Kiŋ tɔn to di jɔj dɛn se: “Dat rili impɔtant.” Dɛn bin jɔs bigin fɔ rayt dis na dɛn slet, we di Wait Rabit intarop se: “ Un important, your Majesty means, of course,” i tɔk wit wan vɔys we rili rɛspɛkt, bɔt i kray ɛn mek fes pan am as i de tɔk.

“ Un important, of course, I meant,” di Kiŋ tɔk kwik kwik wan, ɛn go to insɛf wit ɔndatɔn, .

“impɔtant—nɔ impɔtant—nɔ impɔtant—impɔtant—” lɛk se i de tray us wɔd go saund di bɛst.

Sɔm pan di jɔj dɛn bin rayt am “i impɔtant,” ɛn sɔm “nɔ impɔtant.” Alis bin ebul fɔ si dis, as i bin de nia fɔ luk oba dɛn slet dɛn; i bin tink to insɛf se: “bɔt i nɔ impɔtant smɔl.”

Na dis tɛm ya di Kiŋ we bin dɔn bizi de rayt fɔ sɔm tɛm na in not-buk, kɔl am se “Silence!” ɛn rid frɔm in buk we se, “Rul Fɔti-tu. Ɔl pipul dɛn we ay pas wan mayl fɔ kɔmɔt na di kɔt .”

Ɔlman bin de luk Alis.

Alis bin tɔk se: “ A nɔ ay wan mayl.

Di Kiŋ se: “Na yu de.”

Di Kwin bin tɔk bak se: “I bin ay lɛk tu mayl so.

Alis bin tɔk se: “Wɛl, a nɔ go go, pan ɔl we a nɔ go ebul fɔ go: apat frɔm dat, dat nɔto lɔ ɔltɛm: na yu mek am jɔs naw.”

Di Kiŋ se: “Na di lɔ we ol pas ɔl na di buk.”

Alis bin tɔk se: “Dɔn i fɔ bi Nɔmba Wan.”

Di Kiŋ tɔn in blɛsin, ɛn lɔk in not-buk kwik kwik wan. I bin tɔk to di jɔj dɛn wit vɔys we de shek shek se: “Una tink bɔt di we aw yu dɔn disayd.”

“Mɔ pruf de fɔ kam yet, duya yu Majesty,” di Wait Rabit se, as i jomp ɔp wit big rɔsh; “dɛn jɔs dɔn pik dis pepa.”

“Wetin de insay?” na so di Kwin se.

Di Wait Rabit se: “A nɔ opin am yet, bɔt i tan lɛk se na lɛta, we di prizina rayt to—to sɔmbɔdi.”

Di Kiŋ se: “I go mɔs bi se na so i bi, pas nɔmɔ dɛn rayt am to ɛnibɔdi, we nɔto ɔspitul, yu no.”

“Udat dɛn de dayrɛkt am to?” wan pan di jɔj dɛn bin tɔk.

Di Wait Rabit se: “Dɛn nɔ dayrɛkt am atɔl; “infakt, dɛn nɔ rayt natin na do .” I opin di pepa as i de tɔk, ɛn ad se “I nɔto lɛta, afta ɔl: na sɛt ɔf vas dɛn.”

“Dɛn de na di prizina in an raytin?” wan ɔda wan pan di jɔj dɛn aks.

Di Wait Rabit se, “Nɔ, dɛn nɔ de, ɛn na dat na di tin we rili wɔndaful bɔt am.” (Di juri ɔl bin luk lɛk se dɛn kɔnfyus.)

Di Kiŋ bin se: “I go mɔs dɔn falamakata ɔda pɔsin in an.” (Di juri ɔl brayt bak.)

Di Knave se, “Duya yu Majesty, a nɔ rayt am, ɛn dɛn nɔ ebul fɔ pruv se a rayt am: no nem nɔ de we dɛn sayn na di ɛnd.”

Di Kiŋ se: “If yu nɔ bin sayn am, dat kin jɔs mek di tin wɔs. Yu go mɔs dɔn min sɔm bad bad tin dɛn, ɔ ɔdasay yu fɔ dɔn sayn yu nem lɛk ɔnɛs man.”

Na jenɛral klap bin de pan dis: na di fɔs rili kleva tin we di Kiŋ bin tɔk da de de.

Di Kwin bin tɔk se: “Dat de pruv se i gilti.”

“I pruv se natin nɔ de we tan lɛk dat!” na so Alis bin se. “Wetin mek, yu nɔ ivin no wetin dɛn de tɔk bɔt!”

Di Kiŋ se: “Rid dɛn.”

Di Wait Rabit put in spɛktakl. “Usay a go bigin, duya yu Majesty?” i aks.

Di Kiŋ bin tɔk tranga wan se: “Bigin frɔm di biginin, ɛn go bifo te yu rich di ɛnd: dɔn stɔp.”

Dis na di vas dɛn we di Wait Rabit bin rid:—

Di Kiŋ de rɔb in an dɛn se: “Na di impɔtant pruf we wi dɔn yɛri yet.” “so naw lɛ di jɔj dɛn—”

Alis bin tɔk se: “If ɛni wan pan dɛn ebul fɔ ɛksplen am,” (i bin dɔn gro so big insay di las minit dɛn dat i nɔ bin de fred smɔl fɔ ambɔg am), “A go gi am sikspɛns. A nɔ biliv se atɔm de we gɛt minin insay am.”

Di jɔj dɛn ɔl rayt na dɛn slet se, “ I nɔ biliv se atɔm we gɛt minin de insay,” bɔt nɔbɔdi pan dɛn nɔ tray fɔ ɛksplen di pepa.

Di Kiŋ se: “If no minin nɔ de insay am, dat de sev wan wɔl we gɛt prɔblɛm, yu no, as wi nɔ nid fɔ tray fɔ fɛn ɛni wan. Ɛn yet a nɔ no,” i kɔntinyu fɔ spre di vas dɛn na in ni, ɛn luk dɛn wit wan yay; “I tan lɛk se a de si sɔm minin pan dɛn, afta ɔl.“— se a nɔ ebul fɔ swim —” yu nɔ ebul fɔ swim, nɔto so?” i ad, i tɔn to di Knave.

Di Knave shek in ed wit sɔri-at. “A tan lɛk am?” i bin tɔk. (We i rili nɔ bin du am , bikɔs dɛn mek am wit kadbɔd nɔmɔ.)

“Okay, so fa,” di Kiŋ se, ɛn i kɔntinyu fɔ muf oba di vas dɛn to insɛf: “’ Wi no se na tru —’ na di juri, fɔ tru—’ A gi am wan, dɛn gi am tu —’ wetin mek, na dat i fɔ dɔn du wit di tart dɛn, yu no—”

Alis bin tɔk se: “Bɔt, i de go bifo ‘ dɛn ɔl kam bak frɔm am to yu .’”

“Wetin mek, na de dɛn de!” di Kiŋ se wit win, i pɔynt to di tart dɛn we de na di tebul. “Natin nɔ go klia pas dat . Dɔn bak—’ bifo i gɛt dis fit —’ yu nɔ ɛva gɛt fit, mi dia, a tink?” i se to di Kwin.

"Nɔ ɛva!" di Kwin se wit wamat, i trowe inkstand pan di Lizad as i de tɔk. (Di ɔnaful smɔl Bil bin dɔn lɛf fɔ rayt na in slet wit wan finga, as i si se i nɔ mek ɛni mak; bɔt naw i bigin kwik kwik wan bak, yuz di ink, we bin de trikl dɔŋ in fes, as lɔng as i las.)

Di Kiŋ bin de luk rawnd di kɔt wit smayl se: “Dɔn di wɔd dɛn nɔ fit yu.” Wan dɛd silɛns bin de.

“Na pun!” di Kiŋ ad wit vɔys, ɛn ɔlman laf se, “Lɛ di jɔj dɛn tink bɔt wetin dɛn dɔn disayd.” di Kiŋ se, fɔ lɛk twɛnti tɛm da de de.

“Nɔ, nɔ!” na so di Kwin se. “Sɛnt fɔs—di jɔjmɛnt afta dat.”

“Stɔf ɛn nɔsɛns!” Alis bin tɔk lawd wan. “Di aidia fɔ gɛt di sɛnt fɔs!”

“Hold yu tong!” di Kwin se, i tɔn pepul.

“A nɔ go du am!” na so Alis bin se.

“Of wit in ed!” di Kwin ala pan di tɔp pan in vɔys. Nɔbɔdi nɔ bin muf.

“Udat de kia fɔ yu?” Alis se, (i bin dɔn gro to in ful saiz bay dis tɛm). “Yu nɔto natin pas wan pak kad!”

We i yɛri dis di wan ol pak rayz ɔp na di ays, ɛn kam flay dɔŋ pan am: i ala smɔl, af fred ɛn af vɛks, ɛn tray fɔ bit dɛn, ɛn si se i ledɔm na di bank, wit in ed na in sista in lap, we bin de brus sɔm day lif dɛn we bin dɔn flɔt dɔŋ frɔm di tik dɛn na in fes jisnɔ.

“Wek, Alis dia!” in sista se; “Wetin mek, na lɔng slip yu dɔn gɛt!”

“O, a dɔn gɛt dis kayn drim we a want fɔ no mɔ!” Alis se, ɛn i tɛl in sista, as i ebul fɔ mɛmba dɛn, ɔl dɛn strenj Advantej dɛn ya we yu jɔs de rid bɔt; ɛn we i dɔn, in sista kis am, ɛn se, “Na bin wan drim we a want fɔ no mɔ, mi dia, fɔ tru: bɔt naw rɔn go insay yu ti; i dɔn let.” So Alis grap ɛn rɔn go, i tink se we i de rɔn, as i go ebul fɔ rɔn, aw i bin dɔn drim fayn fayn wan.

Bɔt in sista bin sidɔm stil jɔs lɛk aw i lɛf am, i le in ed pan in an, de wach di san we de go dɔŋ, ɛn tink bɔt smɔl Alis ɛn ɔl in wɔndaful Advantej dɛn, te insɛf bigin fɔ drim afta wan fashɔn, ɛn dis na bin in drim:—

Fɔs, i drim bɔt smɔl Alis insɛf, ɛn wan tɛm bak di smɔl smɔl an dɛn bin tay pan in ni, ɛn di brayt brayt yay dɛn we bin want fɔ du sɔntin bin de luk ɔp insay in yon—i bin ebul fɔ yɛri di sem tɔyn dɛn we in vɔys de mek, ɛn si da kwik smɔl we de we i de tos in ed fɔ kip bak di ia we de waka waka we go de go insay in yay ɔltɛm—ɛn stil as i de lisin, ɔ i tan lɛk se i de lisin, di wan ol ples we de rawnd am dɔn bi alayv wit di strenj krichɔ dɛn we in smɔl sista bin drim.

Di lɔng gras bin de rɔsh na in fut as di Wait Rabit de pas kwik kwik wan—di Maus we bin de fred bin de splash in we tru di pul we bin de nia am—i bin de yɛri di rat we di ti-kɔp dɛn de rɔtin as di Mach Hɛri ɛn in padi dɛn bin de sheb dɛn it we nɔ de dɔn, ɛn di kray kray vɔys we di Kwin bin de mek we de ɔda in ɔnaful gɔst dɛn fɔ mek dɛn kil am—wan tɛm mɔ di pig-bebi bin de sniz pan di Duchess in ni, we di plet ɛn dish dɛn bin de krach rawnd am—wan tɛm bak di shriek we di Grifon bin de ala, di swɛt we di Lizad in slet-pensil bin de swɛt, ɛn di chok we di gini-pig dɛn we dɛn bin dɔn stɔp bin de chok, bin ful-ɔp di ays, miks wit di fa fa sob dɛn we di misɛf Mɔk Tɔl bin de kray.

So i sidɔm, wit in yay we lɔk, ɛn af-af biliv insɛf na Wɔndaland, pan ɔl we i bin no se i nɔ fɔ opin dɛn bak, ɛn ɔltin go chenj to dɔl rialiti—di gras go jɔs de rɔsh na di briz, ɛn di pul go de rɔsh to di wef we di lid dɛn de wev—di tikɔp dɛn we de rɔtin go chenj to ship-bɛl we de tink, ɛn di Kwin in kray kray kray we di shɛpad in vɔys bɔy—ɛn di pikin in snis, di Grifon in ala ala, ɛn ɔl di ɔda kwɛr nɔys dɛn, go chenj (i bin no) to di kɔnfyus kray kray we di bizi fam-yad de mek—we di kaw dɛn we de low na di fa go tek di ples fɔ di Mɔk Tɔl in ebi ebi kray.

Las las, i pikchɔ to insɛf aw dis sem smɔl sista we in sista go, insay di afta-tɛm, bi insɛf big uman; ɛn aw i go kip, tru ɔl in rayp ia, di simpul ɛn lɔv at we i bin gɛt we i bin smɔl: ɛn aw i go gɛda bɔt in ɔda smɔl pikin dɛn, ɛn mek dɛn yay brayt ɛn want wit bɔku strenj stori, sɔntɛm ivin wit di drim we Wɔndaland bin drim fɔ lɔng tɛm: ɛn aw i go fil wit ɔl dɛn simpul sɔri-at, ɛn fɛn gladi at pan ɔl dɛn simpul gladi at, we i mɛmba in yon pikin-layf, ɛn di gladi gladi sɔm dez.
