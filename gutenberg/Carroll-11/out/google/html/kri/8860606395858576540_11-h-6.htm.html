CHAPTER VI.
Pig ɛn Pɛpa

Fɔ wan ɔ tu minit i tinap de luk di os, ɛn de wɔnda wetin fɔ du nɛks, we wantɛm wantɛm wan futman we wɛr livri kam rɔn kɔmɔt na di wud—(i tek am se na futman bikɔs i wɛr livri: if nɔto dat, if yu jɔj am bay in fes nɔmɔ, i fɔ dɔn kɔl am fish)—ɛn i rap lawd wan na di domɔt wit in nɛk. Na wan ɔda futman we wɛr livri, we gɛt rawnd fes, ɛn big yay we tan lɛk frog, bin opin am; ɛn ɔl tu di futman dɛn, Alis bin notis, gɛt paoda ia we kɔl ɔlsay na dɛn ed. I bin fil rili want fɔ no wetin i de tɔk bɔt, ɛn i bin krep smɔl kɔmɔt na di wud fɔ lisin.

Di Fish-Futman bigin bay we i prodyuz frɔm ɔnda in an wan big lɛta, we bin dɔn nia fɔ big lɛk insɛf, ɛn dis i gi di ɔda wan, ɛn tɔk, wit wan siriɔs vɔys, “Fɔ di Duchess. Wan inviteshɔn frɔm di Kwin fɔ ple kroket.” Di Frog-Futman ripit, wit di sem solemn tɔyn, i jɔs chenj di ɔda fɔ di wɔd dɛn smɔl, “Frɔm di Kwin. Wan inviteshɔn fɔ di Duchess fɔ ple kroket.”

Dɔn dɛn ɔl tu butu, ɛn dɛn kɔl dɛn bin miks togɛda.

Alis bin so laf pan dis, dat i bin gɛt fɔ rɔn bak insay di wud bikɔs i bin de fred se dɛn go yɛri am; ɛn we i nɛks peep aut di Fish-Futman nɔ bin de igen, ɛn di ɔda wan bin sidɔm na grɔn nia di domɔt, de luk ɔp na di skay wit fulish wan.

Alis bin shem fɔ go ɔp di domɔt, ɛn nak.

Di Futman se, "No kayn yus nɔ de fɔ nak, ɛn dat na fɔ tu rizin. Fɔs, bikɔs a de na di sem say na di domɔt wit yu; sɛkɔn, bikɔs dɛn de mek da kayn nɔys de insay, nɔbɔdi nɔ go ebul fɔ yɛri yu." Ɛn fɔ tru, wan nɔys we rili wɔndaful bin de apin insay—dɛn bin de ala ɛn sniz ɔltɛm, ɛn wan wan tɛm wan big big krash, lɛk se dɛn dɔn brok wan dish ɔ ketul.

Alis se: “Duya, so, aw a go go insay?”

Di Futman kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se: “Sɔm sɛns go de we yu nak, if wi bin gɛt di domɔt bitwin wi. Fɔ ɛgzampul, if yu bin de insay , yu kin nak, ɛn a kin mek yu kɔmɔt, yu no.” I bin de luk ɔp na di skay ɔltɛm we i de tɔk, ɛn dis Alis bin tink se i nɔ sivil. I bin tɔk to insɛf se: “Bɔt sɔntɛm i nɔ go ebul fɔ ɛp am; “in yay dɛn so rili nia di tap pan in ed. Bɔt ilɛk aw i bi, i kin ansa kwɛstyɔn dɛn.—Aw a fɔ go insay?” i bin ripit, lawd wan.

Di Futman bin tɔk se: “A go sidɔm ya, te tumara—”

Na dis tɛm di os in domɔt opin, ɛn wan big plet kam skim kɔmɔt, stret na di Futman in ed: i jɔs gras in nos, ɛn brok to wan pan di tik dɛn we de biɛn am.

“—ɔ nɛks de, sɔntɛm,” di Futman kɔntinyu wit di sem vɔys, jɔs lɛk se natin nɔ apin.

“Aw a fɔ go insay?” ask Alis bak, wit wan vɔys we lawd pas am.

“ Yu fɔ go insay atɔl?” na so di Futman se. “Na di fɔs kwɛstyɔn dat, yu no.”

Wi nɔ gɛt wan dawt fɔ se na so i bin bi: na Alis nɔmɔ nɔ bin lɛk fɔ lɛ dɛn tɛl am so. I bin de mɔna insɛf se: “I rili bad, di we aw ɔl di tin dɛn we Gɔd mek de agyu, i go du fɔ mek pɔsin kray!”

I bin tan lɛk se di Futman tink se dis na gud chans fɔ ripit wetin i tɔk, wit difrɛns dɛn. I bin se: “A go sidɔm ya, fɔ de ɛn dez.”

“Bɔt wetin a fɔ du?” na so Alis bin se.

Di Futman se, “Ɛnitin we yu lɛk,” ɛn bigin fɔ wispa.

Alis bin tɔk wit pwɛl at se: “O, i nɔ gɛt ɛni yus fɔ tɔk to am: i rili idiotic!” Ɛn i opin di domɔt ɛn go insay.

Di domɔt bin de go rayt insay wan big kichin, we bin ful-ɔp wit smok frɔm wan ɛnd to di ɔda: di Duchess bin sidɔm pan wan stɔl we gɛt tri leg na di midul, de nɔs wan bebi; di kuk bin de ledɔm oba di faya, de stɔp wan big kɔl we tan lɛk se i ful-ɔp wit sup.

“Fɔ tru, tumɔs pepa de insay da sup de!” Alis se to insɛf, as i ebul fɔ sniz.

Fɔ tru, tumɔs pan am bin de na di ays. Ivin di Duchess bin de sniz wan wan tɛm; ɛn as fɔ di pikin, i bin de snis ɛn ala wan wan ɛn nɔ stɔp fɔ smɔl tɛm. Di onli tin we bin de na di kichin we nɔ bin de sniz, na di kuk, ɛn wan big pusi we bin sidɔm na di faya ɛn grin frɔm yes to yes.

Alis bin shem smɔl, bikɔs i nɔ bin rili shɔ if na gud abit fɔ mek i tɔk fɔs, “Duya yu go tɛl mi, wetin mek yu pusi de grin da we de?”

Di Duchess se, “Na Cheshire pusi, ɛn na dat mek. Pig!”

I tɔk di las wɔd wit da kayn fɛt-fɛt so wantɛm wantɛm dat Alis bin rili jomp; bɔt i si insay wan ɔda tɛm se dɛn adrɛs am to di pikin, ɛn nɔto to am, so i tek maynd, ɛn go bifo bak:—

“A nɔ bin no se Cheshire pusi dɛn kin grin ɔltɛm, fɔ tru, a nɔ bin no se pusi kin grin.”

Di Duchess se: “Dɛn ɔl ebul; “ɛn bɔku pan dɛn kin du am.”

Alis bin tɔk fayn fayn wan, ɛn i bin rili gladi fɔ we dɛn bigin tɔk to am se: “A nɔ no ɛni wan pan dɛn we de du am.”

Di Duchess se: “Yu nɔ no bɔku tin; “ɛn dat na tru.”

Alis nɔ bin lɛk di tɔyn we dis tɔk bin tɔk atɔl, ɛn i bin tink se i go fayn fɔ mek i introduks sɔm ɔda tɔpik fɔ tɔk bɔt. We i bin de tray fɔ fiks wan, di kuk pul di kɔldɔn we gɛt sup kɔmɔt na di faya, ɛn wantɛm wantɛm i bigin fɔ wok fɔ trowe ɔltin we i ebul fɔ rich to di Duchess ɛn di pikin—di faya-ayɔn dɛn bin kam fɔs; dɔn afta dat, dɛn bin shaw wit sospen, plet, ɛn dish dɛn. Di Duchess nɔ bin notis dɛn ivin we dɛn bit am; ɛn di pikin bin de ala so ɔlrɛdi, dat i nɔ bin rili pɔsibul fɔ tɔk if di bit dɛn bin de ambɔg am ɔ nɔ bin de ambɔg am.

“O, duya mind wetin yu de du!” Alis kray, i bin de jomp ɔp ɛn dɔŋ bikɔs i bin de fil bad. “O, na de in prɛshɔ nos de go!” as wan big sospen we nɔ kɔmɔn bin de flay nia am, ɛn i bin rili nia fɔ kɛr am go.

Di Duchess bin tɔk wit wan kray kray se: “If ɔlman bin de tink bɔt in yon biznɛs, di wɔl go go rawnd wan dil fast pas aw i de go.”

Alis we bin rili gladi fɔ gɛt chans fɔ sho smɔl pan di tin dɛn we i no, bin tɔk se: “We nɔ go bi bɛnifit.” “Jɔs tink bɔt us wok i go mek wit di de ɛn nɛt! Yu si se di wɔl de tek twɛnti-fo awa fɔ tɔn rawnd pan in aks—”

Di Duchess se, “We yu de tɔk bɔt aks, kɔt in ed!”

Alis bin luk di kuk wit wɔri mɔ, fɔ si if i min fɔ tek di hint; bɔt di kuk bin bizi de stɔp di sup, ɛn i bin tan lɛk se i nɔ de lisin, so i kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk bak se: “Twɛnti-fo awa, a tink ;ɔ na twɛlv?

Di Duchess se: “O, nɔ mɔna mi .” “A nɔ bin ɛva ebul fɔ bia wit figa dɛn!” Ɛn wit dat i bigin fɔ nɔs in pikin bak, i de siŋ wan kayn lullaby to am as i de du dat, ɛn gi am wan bad bad shek na di ɛnd pan ɛvri layn:

“Tɔk roughly to yu smɔl bɔbɔ, .
Ɛn bit am we i de sniz:
I jɔs de du am fɔ mek pɔsin vɛks, .
Na bikɔs i no se i de provok.”

KƆRƆS.
(We di kuk ɛn di pikin jɔyn):

“Wow!, wow! wow!”

We di Duchess bin de siŋ di sɛkɔn vas na di siŋ, i bin de kɔntinyu fɔ tos di pikin bad bad wan ɔp ɛn dɔŋ, ɛn di po smɔl tin ala so, dat Alis nɔ bin ebul fɔ yɛri di wɔd dɛn:—

“A de tɔk bad bad wan to mi bɔy pikin, .
A kin bit am we i de sniz;
Bikɔs i kin ɛnjɔy am gud gud wan
Di pepa we i want!”

KƆRƆS.

“Wow!, wow! wow!”

“Na ya! yu kin nɔs am smɔl, if yu lɛk!” di Duchess se to Alis, i fling di pikin pan am as i de tɔk. “A fɔ go rɛdi fɔ ple kroket wit di Kwin,” ɛn i kɔmɔt na di rum kwik kwik wan. Di kuk trowe wan frying pan afta am as i de go, bɔt i jɔs mis am.

Alis bin kech di pikin wit sɔm prɔblɛm, bikɔs na bin smɔl tin we gɛt shep we nɔ gɛt wan bɔt, ɛn i bin es in an ɛn leg dɛn ɔlsay, “jɔs lɛk sta-fish,” na so Alis bin de tink. Di po smɔl tin bin de snor lɛk stim-ɛnjin we i kech am, ɛn kɔntinyu fɔ dubl insɛf ɛn stret insɛf bak, so dat ɔltogɛda, fɔ di fɔs minit ɔ tu, i bin tan lɛk aw i ebul fɔ du fɔ ol am.

As i dɔn mek di rayt we fɔ nɔs am, (we na fɔ twist am ɔp to wan kayn knot, ɛn afta dat i ol in rayt yes ɛn lɛft fut tayt tayt wan, so dat i nɔ go pul insɛf,) i kɛr am go na di opin ɛj. Alis bin tink se: “If a nɔ kɛr dis pikin go wit mi, dɛn go mɔs kil am insay wan ɔ tu dez: yu nɔ tink se i go bi kil fɔ lɛf am?” I tɔk di las wɔd dɛn lawd wan, ɛn di smɔl tin grɔmbul fɔ ansa am (i bin dɔn lɛf fɔ snis bay dis tɛm). Alis se: “Nɔ grɔmbul; “dat nɔto di rayt we atɔl fɔ sho aw yu de fil.”

Di pikin grɔmbul bak, ɛn Alis luk in fes wit rili wɔri fɔ si wetin na di prɔblɛm wit am. Wi nɔ go gɛt wan dawt fɔ se i bin gɛt wan nos we rili tɔn ɔp, we tan lɛk snot pas rial nos; bak in yay bin de kam smɔl pasmak fɔ bebi: ɔltogɛda Alis nɔ bin lɛk di luk we di tin luk atɔl. I bin tink se: “Bɔt sɔntɛm na fɔ kray nɔmɔ i bin de kray,” ɛn luk in yay bak, fɔ si if ɛni kray wata de.

Nɔ, kray wata nɔ bin de. Alis bin tɔk siriɔs wan se: “If yu go tɔn to pig, mi dia, a nɔ go gɛt natin fɔ du wit yu igen. Mind naw!” Di po smɔl tin kray bak (ɔ grɔmbul, i nɔ pɔsibul fɔ tɔk uswan), ɛn dɛn kɔntinyu fɔ sɔm tɛm we dɛn nɔ tɔk natin.

Alis bin jɔs bigin fɔ tink to insɛf se, “Naw, wetin a fɔ du wit dis krichɔ we a rich na os?” we i grɔmbul bak, so bad bad wan, dat i luk dɔŋ na in fes wit sɔm alarm. Dis tɛm, no mistek nɔ bin fɔ de bɔt am: i nɔ bin pas pig ɔ i nɔ bin smɔl pas pig, ɛn i bin fil se i go rili mek sɛns fɔ lɛ i kɛr am go bifo.

So i put di smɔl tin dɔŋ, ɛn i fil fayn fɔ si am de trot kwayɛt wan go insay di wud. I bin tɔk to insɛf se: “If i bin dɔn big, i bin fɔ dɔn mek wan pikin we rili bad: bɔt a tink se i de mek wan fayn fayn pig mɔ.” Ɛn i bigin fɔ tink bɔt ɔda pikin dɛn we i sabi, we kin du fayn fayn wan lɛk pig, ɛn i bin jɔs de tɛl insɛf se, “if pɔsin jɔs no di rayt we fɔ chenj dɛn—” we i sɔprayz smɔl we i si di Chɛsha Kat sidɔm pan wan tik tik we de sɔm yad frɔm.

Di Kat bin jɔs grin we i si Alis. I bin luk gud, i bin tink: stil i gɛt rili lɔng klos ɛn bɔku bɔku tit, so i fil se dɛn fɔ trit am wit rɛspɛkt.

“Cheshire Puss,” i bigin, wit smɔl shem, as i nɔ bin no atɔl if i go lɛk di nem: bɔt, i jɔs grin smɔl. Alis bin tink ɛn i kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se: “Kam, i gladi so fa.” “Duya, yu go tɛl mi us we a fɔ go frɔm ya?”

Di Kat se: “Dat dipen gud gud wan pan usay yu want fɔ rich.”

Alis bin tɔk se: “A nɔ bisin tumɔs usay—.”

Di Kat se, “Dɔn i nɔ mata us we yu go.”

“—so lɔng as a rich sɔmsay ,” Alis bin ad as ɛksplen.

Di Kat se, “O, yu shɔ se yu go du dat, if yu jɔs waka fɔ lɔng tɛm.”

Alis bin fil se dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ dinay dis, so i tray fɔ aks ɔda kwɛstyɔn. “Us kayn pipul dɛn de liv bɔt ya?”

“Na da dairekshɔn de,” di Kat se, as i de wev in rayt paw rawnd, “liv wan Hata: ɛn na da dairekshɔn de,” we i de wev di ɔda paw, “liv wan Mach Hɛri. Visit ɛni wan we yu lɛk: dɛn ɔl tu dɔn mad.”

Alis bin tɔk se: “Bɔt a nɔ want fɔ go wit pipul dɛn we dɔn kray.

“O, yu nɔ go ebul fɔ ɛp dat,” di Kat se: “wi ɔl mad ya. A dɔn mad. Yu dɔn mad.”

“Aw yu no se a dɔn mad?” na so Alis bin se.

Di Kat se, “Yu fɔ dɔn bi, ɔ yu nɔ bin fɔ dɔn kam ya.”

Alis nɔ bin tink se dat pruv am atɔl; bɔt, i kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se “Ɛn aw yu no se yu dɔn ful?”

Di Kat se, “Fɔ bigin, dɔg nɔ de mek lɛk se i dɔn kray. Yu gri dat?”

Alis bin tɔk se: “A tink se na so i bi.

“Wɛl, so,” di Kat kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk, “yu si, dɔg de grɔmbul we i vɛks, ɛn wag in tel we i gladi. Naw a de grɔmbul we a gladi, ɛn wag mi tel we a vɛks.Na dat mek a dɔn mek a kray.”

Alis bin tɔk se: “ A kɔl am purring, nɔto fɔ grɔmbul.”

Di Kat se, “Kɔl am wetin yu lɛk.” “Yu de ple kroket wit di Kwin tide?”

Alis bin tɔk se: “A fɔ rili lɛk am, bɔt dɛn nɔ invayt mi yet.”

“Yu go si mi de,” di Kat se, ɛn i lɔs.

Alis nɔ bin sɔprayz bɔku pan dis, i bin de so yus to queer tin dɛn we de apin. We i bin de luk di ples usay i bin dɔn de, i bin apia bak wantɛm wantɛm.

“Bay-bay, wetin bi di pikin?” na so di Kat se. “I bin dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ lɛ a fɔgɛt fɔ aks.”

Alis bin tɔk kwayɛt wan se: “I tɔn to pig,” jɔs lɛk se i kam bak insay natura we.

Di Kat se, “A bin tink se i go bi,” ɛn i lɔs bak.

Alis wet smɔl, af-af i bin de tink se i go si am bak, bɔt i nɔ bin apia, ɛn afta wan ɔ tu minit i waka go na di say we dɛn se di Mach Hɛri de liv. I bin tɔk to insɛf se: “A dɔn si pipul dɛn we de mek hat bifo; “di Mach Hare go bi di wan we intrestin pas ɔl, ɛn sɔntɛm as dis na May i nɔ go de raving mad—at least i nɔ go so mad lɛk aw i bin de insay Mach.” As i de tɔk dis, i luk ɔp, ɛn di Kat de bak, i sidɔm pan wan tik branch.

“Yu se pig, ɔ fig?” na so di Kat se.

Alis ansa se: “A se pig; “ɛn a wish se yu nɔ go kɔntinyu fɔ apia ɛn lɔs so wantɛm wantɛm: yu de mek pɔsin rili gidi.”

Di Kat se: “Okay; ɛn dis tɛm i bin lɔs kwik kwik wan, i bigin wit di ɛnd pan di tel, ɛn dɔn wit di grin, we bin de sɔm tɛm afta di ɔda pat dɛn dɔn go.

Alis bin tink se: “Wɛl! Bɔku tɛm a dɔn si pusi we nɔ de grin; “bɔt wan grin we nɔ gɛt pusi! Na di tin we a rili want fɔ no mɔ we a dɔn ɛva si na mi layf!”

I nɔ bin dɔn go fa bifo i kam fɔ si di Mach Hɛri in os: i bin tink se i fɔ bi di rayt os, bikɔs di chimini dɛn shep lɛk yes ɛn di ruf bin gɛt chukchuk wit fut. I bin so big os, dat i nɔ bin lɛk fɔ go nia te i dɔn swɛt sɔm mɔ pan di lɛft an bit fɔ mɔs, ɛn es insɛf ɔp to lɛk tu fit ay: ivin da tɛm de i waka go ɔp to am wit rili shem, ɛn se to insɛf se “Lɛ wi se i fɔ bi raving mad afta ɔl! A bin lɛf smɔl fɔ wish se a bin dɔn go fɔ si di Hata insted!”
