CHAPTER VIII.
Di Kwin in Kroket-Grɔn

Wan big ros-tri bin tinap nia di ɛntrɛ na di gadin: di ros dɛn we bin de gro pan am bin wayt, bɔt tri gadin man dɛn bin de de, ɛn dɛn bin bizi de peint dɛn rɛd. Alis bin tink se dis na tin we rili want fɔ no mɔ, ɛn i go nia fɔ wach dɛn, ɛn jɔs lɛk aw i kam nia dɛn, i yɛri wan pan dɛn se, “Luk naw, Fayv! Nɔ go splash pent oba mi da we de!”

Fayv bin tɔk wit sɔl vɔys se: “A nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɛp am; “Sɛvin bin jok mi ɛlb.”

Na dat Sɛvin luk ɔp ɛn se, “Na so, Fayv! Ɔltɛm, put di blem pan ɔda pipul dɛn!”

“ I bɛtɛ lɛ yu nɔ tɔk!” na so Fayv bin se. “A yɛri di Kwin se na yestede nɔmɔ dɛn fɔ kɔt yu ed!”

“Wetin fɔ?” na so di wan we bin dɔn tɔk fɔs se.

“Dat nɔto yu biznɛs, Tu!” na so Sɛvin bin se.

“Yɛs, na in biznɛs!” Fayv se, “ɛn a go tɛl am—na fɔ briŋ di kuk tulip-rut insted ɔf anɔynt.”

Sɛvin bin trowe in brɔsh dɔŋ, ɛn i bin jɔs bigin “Wɛl, pan ɔl di tin dɛn we nɔ rayt—” we in yay chans fɔ fɔdɔm pan Alis, as i tinap de wach dɛn, ɛn i chɛk insɛf wantɛm wantɛm: di ɔda wan dɛn luk rawnd bak, ɛn dɛn ɔl butu dɔŋ.

Alis bin shem smɔl ɛn tɔk se: “Yu go tɛl mi wetin mek yu de peint dɛn ros dɛn de?”

Fayv ɛn Sɛvin nɔ tɔk natin, bɔt dɛn luk Tu. Tu bigin wit lɔw vɔys, “Wetin mek di tru tin na, yu si, Mis, dis ya fɔ dɔn bi wan rɛd ros-tri, ɛn wi put wan wayt wan insay bay mistek;ɛn if di Kwin fɔ dɔn fɛn am, wi ɔl fɔ dɔn kɔt wi ed, yu no. So yu si, Mis, wi de du wi bɛst, bifo i kam, fɔ—” Na dis tɛm Fayv, we bin dɔn de wɔri fɔ luk akɔdin to di gadin, kɔl out “Di Kwin! Di Kwin!” ɛn di tri gadin pipul dɛn trowe dɛnsɛf flat wantɛm wantɛm na dɛn fes. Bɔku bɔku fut step dɛn bin de mek sawnd, ɛn Alis luk rawnd, i bin rili want fɔ si di Kwin.

Fɔs, tɛn sojaman dɛn bin kam we bin de kɛr tik dɛn; dɛn ɔl bin shep lɛk di tri gadin pipul dɛn, dɛn bin lɔng ɛn dɛn bin flat, ɛn dɛn an ɛn fut bin de na di kɔna dɛn: nia di tɛn pipul dɛn we de wok na di kɔt; dɛn bin de mek dɛn tin ya fayn ɔlsay wit dayamɔn, ɛn dɛn bin de waka tu ɛn tu, lɛk aw di sojaman dɛn bin de waka. Afta dis, di kiŋ pikin dɛn kam; na tɛn pan dɛn bin de, ɛn di smɔl dia dɛn bin kam de jomp gladi gladi wan an to an, insay kɔpl: dɛn ɔl bin ɔnamɛnt wit at. Di nɛks wan na di gɔst dɛn, bɔku pan dɛn na Kiŋ ɛn Kwin, ɛn pan dɛn Alis bin no di Wait Rabit: i bin de tɔk kwik kwik wan we i de fred, i bin de smayl pan ɔl wetin dɛn tɔk, ɛn i bin de pas we i nɔ notis am. Dɔn di Knave of Hearts bin fala, we bin de kɛr di Kiŋ in krawn pan wan krimsin velvet kusɛn; ɛn, las pan ɔl dis big big prushɔn, di KING ƐN KWIN ƆF AT dɛn kam.

Alis bin rili dawt if i nɔ fɔ ledɔm na in fes lɛk di tri pipul dɛn we de wok na di gadin, bɔt i nɔ bin ebul fɔ mɛmba se i dɔn ɛva yɛri bɔt dis kayn lɔ we dɛn de du prushɔn; “ɛn apat frɔm dat, wetin go yus fɔ prushɔn,” i bin tink, “if pipul dɛn ɔl fɔ ledɔm na dɛn fes, so dat dɛn nɔ go si am?” So i tinap wansay usay i bin de, ɛn wet.

We di prushɔn kam bifo Alis, dɛn ɔl stɔp ɛn luk am, ɛn di Kwin se tranga wan se “Udat na dis?” I se am to di Knave of Hearts, we jɔs butu ɛn smayl fɔ ansa.

"Fulman!" di Kwin se, i de tos in ed wit nɔ peshɛnt; ɛn, i tɔn to Alis, i kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se, “Wetin na yu nem, pikin?”

Alis bin tɔk fayn fayn wan se: “Mi nem na Alis, so duya yu Majesty; bɔt i ad, to insɛf se, “Wetin mek, dɛn na jɔs wan pak kad, afta ɔl. A nɔ nid fɔ fred dɛn!”

“Ɛn udat na dɛn wan ya? ”, di Kwin se, as i de sho di tri gadin man dɛn we bin de ledɔm rawnd di ros-tri; bikɔs, yu si, as dɛn bin de ledɔm na dɛn fes, ɛn di pɔtn na dɛn bak bin di sem lɛk di ɔda pat dɛn na di pak, i nɔ bin ebul fɔ no if dɛn na gadin pipul dɛn, ɔ sojaman dɛn, ɔ pipul dɛn we de wok na di kiŋ in os, ɔ tri pan in yon pikin dɛn.

“Aw a fɔ no?” na in Alis tɔk, i sɔprayz fɔ si in yon maynd. “I nɔto mi biznɛs .”

Di Kwin tɔn krimsin wit wamat, ɛn afta we i luk am fɔ smɔl tɛm lɛk wayl animal, i ala se “Of wit in ed! Ɔf—”

“Nɔnsɛns!” Alis se, lawd wan ɛn disayd, ɛn di Kwin nɔ tɔk natin.

Di Kiŋ le in an pan in an, ɛn shem se “Tink, mi dia: na pikin nɔmɔ!”

Di Kwin tɔn in bak pan am wit wamat, ɛn tɛl di Knave “Tɔn dɛn!”

Di Knave du am, rili tek tɛm, wit wan fut.

"Grap!" di Kwin se, wit wan kray lawd vɔys, ɛn di tri gadin man dɛn jomp wantɛm wantɛm, ɛn bigin fɔ butu to di Kiŋ, di Kwin, di kiŋ pikin dɛn, ɛn ɔlman.

“Lɛf dat!” di Kwin bin ala. “Yu de mek a giv-ɔp.” Ɛn afta dat, i tɔn to di ros-tri, i kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se, “Wetin yu dɔn de du ya?”

Tu tɔk wit wan vɔys we rili ɔmbul, we i go dɔŋ wit wan ni as i de tɔk, “Mek i gladi fɔ yu Majesty, wi bin de tray—”

" A si!" na so di Kwin we bin dɔn de chɛk di ros dɛn da tɛm de, se. “Of wit dɛn ed!” ɛn di prushɔn bin go bifo, tri pan di sojaman dɛn bin lɛf biɛn fɔ kil di ɔnaful gadin pipul dɛn, we rɔn go to Alis fɔ mek dɛn protɛkt dɛn.

“Dɛn nɔ go kɔt yu ed!” Alis se, ɛn i put dɛn insay wan big flawa-pɔt we tinap nia. Di tri sojaman dɛn bin de waka waka fɔ wan ɔ tu minit, de luk fɔ dɛn, dɔn dɛn mach kwayɛt wan afta di ɔda wan dɛn.

“Dɛn ed dɔn ɔf?” di Kwin bin ala.

“Dɛn ed nɔ de igen, if i gladi fɔ yu Majesty!” di sojaman dɛn ala fɔ ansa.

“Na so i bi!” di Kwin bin ala. “Yu kin ple kroket?”

Di sojaman dɛn nɔ tɔk natin, ɛn luk Alis, as i klia se di kwɛstyɔn bin min fɔ am.

"Yɛs!" Alis bin ala.

“Kam, so!” di Kwin ala, ɛn Alis jɔyn di prushɔn, i bin de wɔnda bad bad wan wetin go apin nɛks.

“Na—na rili fayn de!” wan vɔys we de shem na in sayd se. I bin de waka nia di Wait Rabit, we bin de luk in fes wit wɔri.

Alis se: “Fɔ tru: “—we di Duchess de?”

“Hush! Hush!” di Rabit se wit wan lɔw, rɔsh tɔyn. I luk oba in sholda wit wɔri we i de tɔk, dɔn i es insɛf ɔp pan in fut, put in mɔt nia in yes, ɛn wispa “She’s under sentence of execution.”

“Wetin fɔ?” na so Alis bin se.

“Yu bin se ‘Wetin na sɔri-at!’?” di Rabit aks.

Alis bin tɔk se: “Nɔ, a nɔ bin du dat: “A nɔ tink se i sɔri atɔl, a se ‘Wetin fɔ?’”

“I bɔks di Kwin in yes—” di Rabit bigin. Alis bin gi smɔl kray fɔ laf. “O, hush!” di Rabit wispa wit fred vɔys. “Di Kwin go yɛri yu! Yu si, i kam let, ɛn di Kwin se—”

“Una go na una ples dɛn!” di Kwin ala wit vɔys we tan lɛk tɛnda, ɛn pipul dɛn bigin rɔn rawnd ɔlsay, dɛn de fɔdɔm agens dɛnsɛf; bɔt dɛn bin setul insay wan ɔ tu minit, ɛn di gem bigin. Alis bin tink se i nɔ ɛva si dis kayn kroket-grɔn we pipul dɛn kin want fɔ no mɔ na in layf; ɔl na bin rij ɛn fɔro; di bɔl dɛn na bin layf hed, di malet dɛn na bin layf flamingo, ɛn di sojaman dɛn bin gɛt fɔ dubl dɛnsɛf ɛn tinap pan dɛn an ɛn fut, fɔ mek di arch dɛn.

Di chif difikulti we Alis bin si fɔs na fɔ manej in flamingo: i bin ebul fɔ mek dɛn tuck in bɔdi, kɔmfyut inof, ɔnda in an, wit in leg dɛn we hang dɔŋ, bɔt jɔs lɛk aw i bin dɔn gɛt in nɛk fayn fayn wan stret, ɛn i bin de go gi di hedhɔg wan blo wit in ed, i go twist insɛf rawnd ɛn luk ɔp na in fes, wit so wan pazl ɛksprɛshɔn dat i nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɛp fɔ bɔs laf: ɛn we i dɔn gɛt in ed dɔŋ, ɛn i bin de go bigin bak, i bin rili mek i vɛks fɔ si se di ɛj dɔn opin insɛf, ɛn i bin de insay di akt fɔ kray go: apat frɔm ɔl dis, jɔs wan rij ɔ fɔrɔ bin de na di rod ɛnisay we i want fɔ sɛn di ɛjhɔg go, ɛn, as di sojaman dɛn we dɔn dɛbul-ɔp bin de grap ɔltɛm ɛn waka go to ɔda pipul dɛn pat dɛn na di grɔn, i nɔ tu te, Alis kam fɔ no se fɔ tru, na gem we rili at.

Di pleya dɛn ɔl bin de ple wan tɛm we dɛn nɔ bin de wet fɔ tɔn, dɛn bin de agyu ɔl di tɛm, ɛn dɛn bin de fɛt fɔ di hedhɔg dɛn; ɛn insay wan rili shɔt tɛm di Kwin bin de insay wan pawaful pawa, ɛn go de stamp rawnd, ɛn ala “Off wit in ed!” ɔ “Of wit in ed!” lɛk wan tɛm insay wan minit.

Alis bigin fɔ fil bad bad wan: fɔ tru, i nɔ bin dɔn gɛt ɛni agyumɛnt yet wit di Kwin, bɔt i bin no se i kin apin ɛni minit, “ɛn afta dat,” i bin tink se, “wetin go bi to mi? Dɛn rili lɛk fɔ kɔt pipul dɛn ed ya; di big wɔndaful tin na, se ɛnibɔdi lɛf we de alayv!”

I bin de luk fɔ sɔm we fɔ rɔnawe, ɛn de wɔnda if i go ebul fɔ kɔmɔt de we dɛn nɔ si am, we i notis wan tin we de mek i want fɔ no mɔ na di ays: i bin rili kɔnfyus am fɔs, bɔt, afta we i wach am wan ɔ tu minit, i mek am fɔ bi grin, ɛn i se to insɛf se “Na di Cheshire Cat: naw a go gɛt pɔsin fɔ tɔk to.”

“Aw yu de go ɔp?” di Kat se, jɔs lɛk aw mɔt de fɔ mek i tɔk wit.

Alis wet te di yay dɛn apia, dɔn i nɔd. I bin tink se: “I nɔ yus fɔ tɔk to am, te in yes dɛn kam, ɔ at le wan pan dɛn.” Insay wan ɔda minit di wan ol ed apia, dɔn Alis put in flamingo dɔŋ, ɛn bigin fɔ tɔk bɔt di gem, i fil bad bad wan se i gɛt pɔsin fɔ lisin to am. I bin tan lɛk se di Kat bin tink se inof pan am dɔn de na di yay naw, ɛn i nɔ bin apia igen.

“A nɔ tink se dɛn de ple fayn atɔl,” ​​Alis bigin, wit wan tɔyn we de kɔmplen, “ɛn dɛn ɔl de agyu so bad bad wan pɔsin nɔ go ebul fɔ yɛri insɛf de tɔk—ɛn i tan lɛk se dɛn nɔ gɛt ɛni lɔ patikyula; nɛks waka waka na di ɔda ɛnd na di grɔn—ɛn a fɔ dɔn kroket di Kwin in hedhɔg jɔs naw, na in nɔmɔ i rɔn we i si mi yon de kam!”

“Aw yu lɛk di Kwin?” di Kat se wit smɔl vɔys.

Alis bin se: “Nɔto so i rili—” Jɔs da tɛm de i notis se di Kwin bin de nia am, de lisin: so i kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se, “—i go mɔs bi se i go win, se i nɔ go fayn fɔ dɔn di gem.”

Di Kwin smayl ɛn pas.

“Udat yu de tɔk to?” di Kiŋ se, i go ɔp to Alis, ɛn luk di Kat in ed wit big want fɔ no mɔ.

Alis bin tɔk se: “Na mi padi—na Cheshire Cat: alaw mi fɔ introduks am.”

Di Kiŋ bin tɔk se: “A nɔ lɛk di we aw i luk atɔl: bɔt i kin kis mi an if i lɛk.”

Di Kat bin tɔk se: “A go bɛtɛ fɔ lɛ a nɔ du am.”

Di Kiŋ bin se: “Nɔ mek pɔsin nɔ du bad, ɛn nɔ luk mi da we de!” I bin rich biɛn Alis as i de tɔk.

Alis bin tɔk se: “Kat kin luk kiŋ. “A dɔn rid dat na sɔm buk, bɔt a nɔ mɛmba usay.”

“Wɛl, dɛn fɔ pul am,” di Kiŋ tɔk wit rili disayd, ɛn i kɔl di Kwin we bin de pas da tɛm de, “Mi dia! A wish se yu fɔ pul dis pusi!”

Na wan we nɔmɔ di Kwin bin gɛt fɔ sɛtul ɔl di prɔblɛm dɛn, ilɛksɛf dɛn big ɔ smɔl. “Of wit in ed!” i se, i nɔ ivin luk rawnd.

Di Kiŋ bin tɔk wit ɔl in at se: “A go kam wit di pɔsin we de kil misɛf,” ɛn i rɔn go kwik kwik wan.

Alis bin tink se i go fayn fɔ go bak, ɛn si aw di gem de go, as i yɛri di Kwin in vɔys fa, de ala wit pawa. I bin dɔn yɛri in sɛntɛns tri pan di pleya dɛn we dɛn fɔ kil bikɔs dɛn mis dɛn tɔn, ɛn i nɔ bin lɛk di we aw tin dɛn de luk atɔl, bikɔs di gem bin so kɔnfyus dat i nɔ bin ɛva no if na in tɔn ɔ nɔto in tɔn. So i go luk fɔ in hed.

Di hedhɔg bin de fɛt wit ɔda hed, we bin tan lɛk to Alis se na fayn chans fɔ kroket wan pan dɛn wit di ɔda wan: di wangren prɔblɛm na, se in flamingo dɔn go krɔs go na di ɔda say na di gadin, usay Alis bin ebul fɔ si am de tray insay wan kayn we we nɔ ebul fɔ ɛp fɔ flay go ɔp insay wan tik.

Bay di tɛm we i dɔn kech di flamingo ɛn briŋ am kam bak, di fɛt dɔn dɔn, ɛn di tu hedhɔg dɛn nɔ bin de si am: “bɔt i nɔ impɔtant bɛtɛ,” Alis bin tink, “as ɔl di arch dɛn dɔn go frɔm dis say na di grɔn.” So i tuck am ɔnda in an, so dat i nɔ go rɔnawe igen, ɛn go bak fɔ tɔk smɔl mɔ wit in padi.

We i rich bak to di Cheshire Cat, i sɔprayz fɔ si bɔku bɔku krawd we gɛda rawnd am: wan agyumɛnt bin de apin bitwin di ɛgzikishɔn, di Kiŋ, ɛn di Kwin, we ɔl bin de tɔk wan tɛm, we ɔl di ɔda wan dɛn bin rili kwayɛt, ɛn dɛn bin luk rili nɔ kɔmfyut.

Di tɛm we Alis apia, dɛn ɔl tri bin apil to am fɔ sɛtul di kwɛstyɔn, ɛn dɛn bin ripit dɛn agyumɛnt to am, bɔt as dɛn ɔl bin de tɔk wan tɛm, i bin rili at fɔ mek i no di rayt tin we dɛn tɔk.

Di ɛgzikishɔn in argumɛnt na bin, se yu nɔ go ebul fɔ kɔt ed pas bɔdi de fɔ kɔt am frɔm: se i nɔ ɛva gɛt fɔ du dis kayn tin bifo, ɛn i nɔ go bigin di tɛm we i de liv.

Di Kiŋ in agyumɛnt na, se ɛnitin we gɛt ed kin kɔt in ed, ɛn yu nɔ fɔ tɔk nɔs.

Di Kwin in argumɛnt na bin, se if dɛn nɔ du sɔntin bɔt am insay less dan no tɛm i go mek dɛn kil ɔlman, ɔl rawnd. (Na dis las tɔk we bin dɔn mek di wan ol pati luk so siriɔs ɛn wɔri.)

Alis nɔ bin ebul fɔ tink bɔt ɛni ɔda tin fɔ tɔk pas “Na di Duchess in yon: i bɛtɛ lɛ yu aks am bɔt am.”

Di Kwin tɛl di pɔsin we kil am se: “I de kam na prizin.” En det executioner bin go laik aro.

Di Kat in ed bigin fɔ fade di tɛm we i nɔ de igen, ɛn, bay di tɛm we i kam bak wit di Duchess, i bin dɔn lɔs ɔl; so di Kiŋ ɛn di pɔsin we de kil pipul dɛn rɔn go ɔp ɛn dɔŋ fɔ luk fɔ am, we di ɔda pipul dɛn na di pati go bak na di gem.
