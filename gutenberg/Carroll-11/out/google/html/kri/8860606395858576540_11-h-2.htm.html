CHAPTER II.
Di Pul we de mek pɔsin kray

“I de mek pɔsin want fɔ no mɔ ɛn i de want fɔ no mɔ!” Alis kray (i bin sɔprayz so, dat fɔ di tɛm i rili fɔgɛt aw fɔ tɔk gud Inglish); “naw a de opin aut lɛk di big big tɛliskɔp we bin ɛva de! Gud-bay, fut!” (bikɔs we i luk dɔŋ na in fut, i bin tan lɛk se dɛn dɔn lɛf smɔl fɔ mek dɛn nɔ si dɛn, dɛn bin de go fa fawe). “O, mi po smɔl fut, a de wɔnda udat go put yu sus ɛn stɔking fɔ yu naw, mi dia? A shɔ se a nɔ go ebul! A go de tu fa fɔ trɔbul misɛf bɔt yu: yu fɔ manej di bɛst we we yu ebul;—bɔt a fɔ du gud to dɛn,” na so Alis tink, “ɔ sɔntɛm dɛn nɔ go waka di we aw a want fɔ go! Lɛ a si: A go gi dɛn nyu wan pe fɔ but ɛvri Krismas.”

Ɛn i kɔntinyu fɔ plan to insɛf aw i go ebul fɔ du am. I bin tink se: “Dɛn fɔ pas nia di pɔsin we de kɛr am; “ɛn i go tan lɛk se i fani, fɔ sɛn prɛzɛnt to pɔsin in yon fut!Ɛn di dairekshɔn dɛn go luk ɔda kayn we!

Oh dia, wetin na nɔsɛns a de tɔk!”

Jɔs da tɛm de in ed nak di ruf na di ɔl: infakt naw i bin ay pas nayn fit, ɛn wantɛm wantɛm i tek di smɔl gold ki ɛn rɔsh go na di gadin domɔt.

Po Alis! I bin tan lɛk aw i ebul fɔ du, ledɔm na wan say, fɔ luk tru insay di gadin wit wan yay; bɔt fɔ go tru nɔ bin gɛt op pas aw i bin de: i sidɔm ɛn bigin fɔ kray bak.

Alis se: “Yu fɔ shem fɔ yusɛf, wan big gyal pikin lɛk yu,” (i go rili se dis), “fɔ kɔntinyu fɔ kray dis we! Stɔp dis tɛm, a de tɛl yu!” Bɔt i bin kɔntinyu fɔ du ɔltin di sem we, i bin de kray bɔku bɔku galɔn dɛn, te wan big pul bin de rawnd am, we dip lɛk 4 inch ɛn we bin rich af-af dɔŋ di ɔl.

Afta sɔm tɛm i yɛri smɔl fut dɛn de pat na di fa, ɛn i dray in yay kwik kwik wan fɔ si wetin de kam. Na di Wait Rabit bin de kam bak, drɛs fayn fayn wan, wit wan wayt pikin glɔv na wan an ɛn big fan na di ɔda an: i kam trot wit big rɔsh, de muf to insɛf as i de kam, “O! di Duchess, di Duchess! Oh! i nɔ go bi savage if a dɔn kip am de wet!” Alis bin fil bad bad wan dat i bin rɛdi fɔ aks ɛnibɔdi fɔ ɛp am; so, we di Rabit kam nia am, i bigin, wit smɔl vɔys we de shem, “If yu want, sa—” Di Rabit bigin wit fɛt-fɛt, drɔp di wayt pikin glɔv ɛn di fan, ɛn skur go na di daknɛs as i ebul fɔ go.

Alis tek di fan ɛn glɔv, ɛn, as di ɔl bin rili hot, i kɔntinyu fɔ fan insɛf ɔltɛm we i kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se: “Dia, dia! Aw ɔltin de kwik tide! Ɛn yestede tin dɛn bin de go jɔs lɛk aw i kin bi.A de wɔnda if dɛn dɔn chenj mi na nɛt? Lɛ a tink: na di sem we a bin grap dis mɔnin? A nɔto di sem, di nɛks kwɛstyɔn na, Udat na di wɔl a bi? Ɛn i bigin fɔ tink bɔt ɔl di pikin dɛn we i no we gɛt di sem ej lɛk insɛf, fɔ si if dɛn bin fɔ dɔn chenj am fɔ ɛni wan pan dɛn.

I se: “A shɔ se a nɔto Ada, bikɔs in ia de go insay dɛn kayn lɔng ringlet dɛn de, ɛn mi yon nɔ de go insay ringlet dɛn atɔl; ɛn a shɔ se a nɔ go ebul fɔ bi Mabel, bikɔs a no ɔl kayn tin, ɛn i, oh! i no so smɔl smɔl! Apat frɔm dat, na in, ɛn na mi na mi, ɛn—oh dia, aw ɔl dis de mek a kɔnfyus! A go tray if a no ɔl di tin dɛn we a bin de no trade: 4 tɛm fayv na twɛlv, ɛn 4 tɛm sɛvin na—oh dia a nɔ go ɛva rich twɛnti na da rit de bɔt, lɛ wi tray Jiografi na di kapital siti na Paris, ɛn Paris na di kapital siti na Rom, ɛn a fɔ dɔn shɔ! chenj fɔ Mabel A go tray ɛn se ‘ Aw di smɔl wan de du —’” ɛn i krɔs in an dɛn na in lap lɛk se i de tɔk lɛsin, ɛn bigin fɔ ripit am, bɔt in vɔys bin de sawnd kray ɛn strenj, ɛn di wɔd dɛn nɔ bin de kam di sem we aw dɛn bin de du:—

“Aw di smɔl krokodail de du
Impruv in tel we de shayn, .
Ɛn tɔn di wata we de na di Nayl
Na ɛni gold skel!

“I tan lɛk se i gladi fɔ grin, .
Aw i spre in klos dɛn fayn fayn wan, .
Ɛn wɛlkɔm smɔl fish dɛn insay
Wit jaw we de smayl saful wan!”

“A shɔ se dɛn wɔd dɛn de nɔto di rayt wɔd dɛn,” po Alis se, ɛn in yay ful-ɔp wit kray wata bak as i de go bifo, “A fɔ bi Mabel afta ɔl, ɛn a go gɛt fɔ go ɛn liv na da poky smɔl os de, ɛn a nɔ go gɛt nia tɔys fɔ ple wit, ɛn oh! ɛva so bɔku lɛsin dɛn fɔ lan! Nɔ, a dɔn mek mi maynd bɔt am; if a na Mabel, a go de dɔŋ ya! I go bi nɔ yuz dɛn fɔ put dɛn ed dɔŋ ɛn se ‘Kam ɔp bak, dia!’ A go jɔs luk ɔp ɛn se ‘Udat mi bi den? Alis kray, wit kray wata we bin bɔs wantɛm wantɛm, “A wish se dɛn fɔ put dɛn ed dɔŋ! A rili taya fɔ de mi wan ya!”

As i de tɔk dis i luk dɔŋ na in an dɛn, ɛn i sɔprayz fɔ si se i dɔn put wan pan di Rabit in smɔl wayt pikin glɔv dɛn we i de tɔk. “Aw a go dɔn du dat?” i bin de tink. “A go mɔs dɔn de gro smɔl bak.” I grap ɛn go na di tebul fɔ mɛzhɔ insɛf bay am, ɛn i si se, as i bin dɔn nia fɔ gɛs, i dɔn ay lɛk tu fit naw, ɛn i de kɔntinyu fɔ shrink kwik kwik wan: i nɔ tu te, i kam fɔ no se di tin we mek dis apin na di fan we i bin de ol, ɛn i drɔp am kwik kwik wan, jɔs insay di tɛm fɔ mek i nɔ shrink away ɔltogɛda.

“Dat na bin smɔl ples fɔ rɔnawe!” Alis se, i bin rili fred fɔ di chenj we apin wantɛm wantɛm, bɔt i bin rili gladi fɔ si se i stil de; “ɛn naw fɔ di gadin!” ɛn i rɔn wit ɔl spid bak go na di smɔl domɔt: bɔt, alas! di smɔl domɔt bin lɔk bak, ɛn di smɔl gold ki bin de ledɔm na di glas tebul lɛk aw i bin de bifo, “ɛn tin dɔn wɔs pas aw i bin de,” di po pikin bin tink, “bikɔs a nɔ ɛva smɔl lɛk dis bifo, nɔ ɛva! Ɛn a deklare se i tu bad, se na so i bi!”

As i de tɔk dɛn wɔd ya in fut slip, ɛn insay wan ɔda tɛm, i splash! i bin de te to in chin insay sɔl wata. Di fɔs tin we i bin tink na dat, i bin dɔn fɔdɔm insay di si sɔm kayn we, “ɛn if na so i bi, a kin go bak wit relwe,” i bin tɔk to insɛf. (Alis bin dɔn go na di si sayd wan tɛm insay in layf, ɛn i bin dɔn kam to di jenɛral kɔnklushɔn, se ɛnisay we yu go na di Inglish kɔst yu go si bɔku mashin dɛn fɔ was na di si, sɔm pikin dɛn de dig na di san wit wud spayd, dɔn wan row we gɛt os dɛn fɔ slip, ɛn biɛn dɛn wan relwe steshɔn.) Bɔt i nɔ tu te, i kam fɔ no se i de na di pul we gɛt kray wata we i bin dɔn kray we i ol nayn fit.

“A wish se a nɔ bin kray so!” Alis se, as i de swim rawnd, de tray fɔ fɛn in we fɔ kɔmɔt. "Dɛn go pɔnish mi fɔ am naw, a tink se, bay we a drawn insay mi yon kray wata! Dat go bi wan queer tin, fɔ tru! Bɔt, ɔltin na queer tide."

Jɔs da tɛm de i yɛri sɔntin de splash na di pul we de fa smɔl, ɛn i swim nia fɔ no wetin i bi: fɔs i bin tink se i go mɔs bi walrus ɔ ipopotamus, bɔt afta dat i mɛmba aw i smɔl naw, ɛn i nɔ tu te, i kam fɔ no se na jɔs maws we dɔn slip insay lɛk insɛf.

Alis bin tink se: “I go gɛt ɛni yus, naw, fɔ tɔk to dis maws? Ɔltin so aut-ɔf-di-we dɔŋ ya, dat a fɔ tink se i go rili ebul fɔ tɔk: pan ɔl we i bi, i nɔ bad fɔ tray.” So i bigin fɔ tɔk se: “O Maus, yu no di we fɔ kɔmɔt na dis pul?A rili taya fɔ swim rawnd ya, O Maus!” (Alis bin tink se dis go mɔs bi di rayt we fɔ tɔk to maws: i nɔ ɛva du dis kayn tin bifo, bɔt i mɛmba se i bin si insay in brɔda in Latin Grama, “Maus—we gɛt maws—to maws—maus—O maws!”) Di Maus luk am wit wan we we i want fɔ no mɔ, ɛn i bin tan lɛk se i wink wit wan pan in smɔl yay dɛn, bɔt i nɔ tɔk natin.

Alis bin tink se: “Sɔntɛm i nɔ ɔndastand Inglish; “A de daresay se na Frɛnch maws, kam ova wit Wiliam di Kɔnkɔra.” (Bikɔs, wit ɔl di tin dɛn we Alis bin no bɔt istri, i nɔ bin rili no aw lɔng ɛnitin dɔn apin.) So i bigin bak se: “Où est ma chatte?” we na bin di fɔs sɛntɛns insay in Frɛnch lɛsin-buk. Di Maus gi wan jomp wantɛm wantɛm kɔmɔt na di wata, ɛn i tan lɛk se i de shek shek ɔlsay wit fred. “O, a de beg yu fɔ fɔgiv mi!” Alis kray kwik kwik wan, i bin de fred se i dɔn mek di po animal fil bad. “A rili fɔgɛt se yu nɔ lɛk pusi.”

“Nɔto lɛk pusi!” di Maus kray, wit wan shrill, pawaful vɔys. “ Yu go lɛk pusi if yu na mi?”

“Wɛl, sɔntɛm nɔto so,” Alis se wit wan vɔys we de mek yu fil fayn: “nɔ vɛks bɔt am. Ɛn yet a wish se a go sho yu wi pusi Dayna: A tink se yu go tek wan fansi to pusi if yu jɔs ebul fɔ si am. I na so dia kwayɛt tin,” Alis kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk, af to insɛf, as i de swim les wan na di pul, “ɛn i sidɔm de purr so fayn fayn wan nia di faya, de lik am paw ɛn was in fes—ɛn i na so fayn saf tin fɔ nɔs—ɛn na so kapital wan fɔ kech mays—o, a beg yu fɔgiv!” Alis kray bak, bikɔs dis tɛm di Maus bin de brist ɔlsay, ɛn i fil se i go mɔs dɔn rili vɛks. “Wi nɔ go tɔk bɔt am igen if yu go lɛk fɔ nɔ tɔk bɔt am.”

“Wi fɔ tru!” di Maus kray, we bin de shek shek te to di ɛnd pan in tel. “I tan lɛk se a go tɔk bɔt dis kayn tɔpik! Wi famili bin et pusi ɔltɛm: nasty, low, vulgar tin dɛn! Nɔ mek a yɛri di nem igen!”

“A nɔ go rili du am!” Alis se, wit big rɔsh fɔ chenj di tɔpik we dɛn de tɔk. “Yu—yu lɛk—dɔg?” Di Maus nɔ ansa, so Alis kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk wit ɔl in at: “Na so wan fayn smɔl dɔg de nia wi os we a fɔ lɛk fɔ sho yu! Wan smɔl brayt yay teria, yu no, wit oh, so lɔng kɔl brawn ia!Ɛn i go kam wit tin dɛn we yu trowe dɛn, ɛn i go sidɔm ɔp ɛn beg fɔ in dina, ɛn ɔl kayn tin—a nɔ ebul fɔ mɛmba af pan dɛn—ɛn na fɔ wan fama, yu no, ɛn i se i rili yusful, i gɛt valyu fɔ wan ɔndrɛd pawn I se i de kil ɔl di rat dɛn ɛn—o dia!” Alis kray wit sɔri vɔys se, “A de fred se a dɔn ɔfɛnd am bak!” Bikɔs di Maus bin de swim go fa frɔm am as i ebul fɔ go, ɛn i bin de mek bɔku bɔku pipul dɛn na di pul as i de go.

So i kɔl saful wan afta am, “Maus dia! Du kam bak bak, ɛn wi nɔ go tɔk bɔt pusi ɔ dɔg sɛf, if yu nɔ lɛk dɛn!” We di Maus yɛri dis, i tɔn rawnd ɛn swim sloslo bak to am: in fes bin rili pale (wit pawa, Alis bin tink), ɛn i se wit smɔl vɔys we de shek shek se, “Lɛ wi rich na di sho, dɔn a go tɛl yu mi istri, ɛn yu go ɔndastand wetin mek na so a et pusi ɛn dɔg.”

Na bin ay tɛm fɔ go, bikɔs di pul bin de ful-ɔp wit di bɔd ɛn animal dɛn we bin dɔn fɔdɔm insay: wan Dɔk ɛn wan Dɔdo, wan Lɔri ɛn wan Igul, ɛn sɔm ɔda tin dɛn we bin want fɔ no mɔ bin de de. Alis bin lid di rod, ɛn di wan ol pati swim go na di shore.
