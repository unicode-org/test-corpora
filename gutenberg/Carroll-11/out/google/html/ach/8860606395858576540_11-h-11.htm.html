DUL KWAN XI.
Nga ma okwalo Tart ni?

Kabaka ki dako me cwiny onongo gubedo i kom komker gi i kare ma guo oo, ki lwak dano mapol ma gucokke i nget gi-kit winyi ki lee ma pat pat, kacel ki jami ma ki coyo ni: Laco ma lamar onongo ocung i nyimgi, ki nyor, ki lumony acel acel me gwoko en; ki cok ki Rwot onwongo tye Lapwol Matar, ki tum i cinge acel, ki buk ma ki coyo ki del kom i cinge mukene. I dye kot onongo tye iye meja, ki gin ma ki lwongo ni tarts: onongo nen maber, ma oweko Alice obedo ki kec me neno gi--"Amito ni gu tyeko lok me kot," en otamo, "ki gu mi jami amata ni!" Ento onongo nen calo pe tye gin mo ma twero timme, pi meno en ocako neno jami ducu ma kwako en, me weko kare okato woko.

Alice onongo peya ocito i kot, ento en okwano lok i komgi i bukke, dok cwinye obedo yom adada me nongo ni en ngeyo nying jami ducu ma tye kunnu. "Mano aye langol kop," en owaco bot kome kene, "pi wig ne maber."

Langol kop, ki lok adaa, onwongo obedo Kabaka; ki kit ma en oruko kwede wic ne i wi wig, (nen i nyim ka imito neno kit ma en otimo kwede,) en onongo pe nen maber, ki onongo pe tye ka rwate.

"Ki eni aye dul ma ki lwongo ni jury-box," Alice otamo, "ki ler apar wiye aryo ni," (en onwongo tye ki twero me waco ni "cwec," ineno, pien mogo onwongo obedo ler, ki mogo onwongo obedo winyi,) "A tamo ni gin aye lupilida." En owaco lok man me agiiki ni tyen aryo onyo adek bot kome kene, kun tye ki awaka iye: pien en otamo, ki bene, ni anyera manok ma mwaka gi rom ki mege aye ngeyo tyen lok ne. Ento, "lutic ma ki lwongo ni jury" onongo gubi timo maber.

Lungol kop apar wiye aryo ni weng onwongo tye ka coyo jami mapol i kom karatac. "Ngo ma gitye ka timone?" Alice oloko ki dwan malongo bot Gryphon. "Pe gitwero bedo ki gin mo me keto piny kombedi, ma peya ngolo kop ocakke."

"Gitye ka keto nying gi piny," Gryphon owaco ki dwan malongo me gamo, "pi lworo ni gubi wilo gi ma peya kare me ngolo kop otum."

"Jami ma ming!" Alice oyabo ki dwan malongo, ki akemo, ento en ojuko oyot, pien White Rabbit okok ni, "Ling i kot!" ki rwot oruko kiyo wange ki oneno piny ki lworo, me niang nga ma tye ka lok.

Alice onongo twero neno, kacel ki ka en onongo tye ka neno ki i wigi, ni lupilida ducu onongo gitye ka coyo piny “jami me ming!” i kom karatac gi, ki en onongo twero niang ni ngat acel i kingi onongo pe ngeyo kit me coyo ni "laming," ki ni en onongo myero openy jirani ne me waco bote. "Alokaloka maber ma gin ma ki coyo ni gubi bedo iye ma peya ngolo kop otum!" Alice otamo.

Ngat acel i kin lupilida onongo tye ki kalam ma tye ka koko. Man adaa, Alice pi twero cung, ki en ocito i kot ki obino inge en, ki cut cut onongo kare me kwanyo ne woko. En otimo ne oyoto ma lapilida matidi ma lacan ni (onwongo obedo Bill, Lizard) pi twero niang ngo ma otime i kome; ki dong, ingee yenyo ne, en onwongo myero ocoyo ki lwet cing acel pi nino weng; ki man onwongo tye ki tic manok, pien onwongo pe oweko lanyut mo i kom gin ma ki coyo ni.

"Latit kwena, kwan lok ma kidoto ni!" Rwot owaco.

I kom lok man, ler ma ki lwongo ni White Rabbit ogoyo tum tyen adek, ki lacen oyabo buk ma ki coyo ni, ki okwano kit man:—

"Latela me cwiny, en oyubu mugati mogo,
Weng i nino me cwir:
Latim aryanyi me cwiny, en okwalo jami ma ki lwongo ni tarts ni,
Ki otero gi woko!

"Tam i kom tam ni," Kabaka owaco bot lupilida.

"Pe pud, pwod pe!" Labwor ogamo lok oyotoyot. "Tye jami mapol ma tye ka bino ma peya meno otimme!"

"Lwong lacaden mukwongo," Rwot owaco; ki Lapwol ma ki lwongo ni White Rabbit ogoyo tum tyen adek, ki olwongo ni, "Lacaden mukwongo!"

Lacaden mukwongo onwongo obedo Layub bongo. En obino ki kikopo me cai i cinge acel ki mugati ki moo i cinge mukene. "Alego kica, Rwot," en oyabo, "pi kelo jami eni: ento onwongo pwod a tyeko mato cai na ikare ma ki cwalo an."

"Onongo myero i tyeko," Rwot owaco. "I cako awene?"

Laco ma yubu bongo ni oneno March Hare, ma olubo kore i ot kot, cinge ki cinge ki Dormouse. "Nino dwe apar wiye angwen me dwe me adek, atamo ni onwongo tye," en owaco.

"Me apar wiye abic," owaco March Hare.

"Me apar wiye abicel," Dormouse omedo.

"Co lok meno piny," Rwot owaco bot lupilida, ki lupilida gu coyo nino adek ni weny i kom karatac gi, ki dong gu medo gi kacel, ki gu dwoko lagam ne nio wa i shillings ki pence.

"Kwany bongo ni woko," Kabaka owaco bot Layub bongo ni.

"Pe obedo mega," lacoc owaco.

" Ki kwalo! " Kabaka owaco, kun loko bot lupilida, ma cut cut gu miyo waraga me lok i kom lok meno.

"Agwoko gi me cato," Latuku ni omedo ma calo lok me tito lok; "An pe atye ki gin mo ma mega. Atye laco bongo."

Kany latela mon oruko kiyo wange, ki ocako neno laco ma yubu bongo, ma olokke odoko ryal ki obedo ka ngwec.

"Mi caden ni," Rwot owaco; "ki pi ibed ki lworo, onyo an abi neko in cut cut."

Man pi ocuko cwiny lacaden ni: en omede ki loko ki tyene acel me cito i mukene, kun neno ladit ma lamar, ki i gung cwinye en ocamo gin mo madit ki i cupa me cai ne ma ka camo mugati ki moo.

I kare ni Alice owinyo gin mo ma miite me ngeyo, ma oweko en obedo ki ur madwong nio ka en oniang ngo ma tye: en onongo tye ka cako dongo madit, ki en otamo ni en obi aa malo ki aa ki i kot; ento i tam me aryo en omoko tam me bedo kama en tye iye nio ka kabedo tye pi en.

"Amito ni pe ibed ka diyo kit meno," owaco Dormouse, ma onongo obedo cok kwede. "Atwero yweyo matek."

Alice owaco ki mwolo adada ni: “Pe atwero jukone ni: “Atye ka dongo.”

"Pi itye ki twero me dongo kany ," owaco Dormouse.

"Kur i lok lok ma pe rwate," Alice owaco ki tek cwiny: "in bene ingeyo ni in bene itye ka dongo."

"Ada, ento adongo i rwom ma opore," Dormouse owaco: "pe i yoo ma pe rwate." En oa malo ki akemo ki okato tung cing mukene me kot.

I kare ni weng Queen onongo pe oweko neno laco ma yubu bongo, ki, kit ma Dormouse ni okato ki i kot, en owaco bot ngat acel i kin lutic me kot ni, "Kel bota nying luwer ma gubedo i yub me wer ma okato ni!" ma Laco ma lamar ni obedo ka myel kom, ma oweko en obolo war ne aryo ni woko.

"Mi caden ni," Kabaka ogamo ki akemo, "onyo abi neko in, kede lworo onyo ku."

"Abedo dano ma lacan, ladit," Lacoo bongo ni oyabo, ki dwan ma tye ka myel, "-ki onwongo pwod acako mato cai na--pe okato cabit acel onyo ma kato-ki ngo ma tye ki mugati-ki-moo ma tye ka doko tidi-ki ryeny pa cai-"

"Nen pa gin ango? " Kabaka owaco.

" Ocakke ki cai," Laco ma yubu bongo ogamo.

"Eyo, ryeny cako ki T!" Rwot owaco ki dwan malongo. "Itero an calo ngat ma laming? Med anyim!"

"Atye dano ma lacan," Latuku ni omede ki lok, "ki jami mapol otiime i nge meno--March Hare keken aye owaco-"

"pe atimo!" March Hare ojuko lok ne oyotoyot.

"Itimo!" Laco ma yubu bongo owaco.

"Akwero ne!" owaco March Hare.

"En okwero," Rwot owaco: "wek dul meno woko."

"Aye, kadi bed kumeno, Dormouse owaco ni—" Hatter omede ki neno ki lworo me neno ka en bene obi kwero: ento Dormouse pe okwero gin mo keken, pien onino woko.

"Iyonge meno," laco ma yubu bongo ni omede, "anongo mugati ki moo mukene—"

"Ento ngo ma Dormouse owaco?" ngat acel i kin lupilida openyo.

"Mano pe atwero po," Laco ma yubu bongo owaco.

" Myero i po," Rwot owaco, "ka kumeno abi neko in."

Laco ma kome lit ni oweko kikopo me cai ki mugati ki moo, ki opoto piny ki tyene acel. En ocako lok ni, “An lacan, Ladit.”

"In ibedo ngat ma loko marac atika ," Rwot owaco.

Kany acel i kin ler ma ki lwongo ni guinea-pigs ni obedo ka koko, ki cut cut ki juku ne ki lutic me kot. (Macalo lok meno tek, abi tito boti kit ma ki tiimo kwede. Gubedo ki kicar ma dit, ma ki tweyo i doge ki tol: i iye gi keto ler ma ki lwongo ni guinea-pig, wiye mukwongo, ki dong gibedo iye.)

Alice otamo ni: “Cwinya yom ni aneno meno kitye ka timone.” "Abedo ka kwano i papara lok angeya, i agiki me ngolo kop, "Onwongo tye temo me mako cing, ma ki juku cut cut ki lutic me kot," ki an pe aniang ngo ma en tye ka waco nio wa kombedi."

"Ka meno keken aye ingeyo i kome, ki in ibi cungu piny," Rwot omede.

"Pe atwero cito piny," laco ma yubu bongo owaco: "atye i ngom, kit ma tye kwede."

"I ngeye itwero bedo piny," Kabaka ogamo.

Kany opego mukene ni obedo ka koko, ki ki diyo en.

"Bin, eni tyeko ler ma ki lwongo ni guinea-pigs!" Alice otamo. "I kare ni wa bi bedo maber."

"Amito ni a tyeko mato cai na," owaco laco ma yubu bongo, ki neno ma lworo i kom latela mon, ma onwongo tye ka kwano nying luwer.

"Itwero cito," Rwot owaco, ki lacoc ni oweko kot oyotoyot, labongo kuru me ruko war ne.

"-ki kwany wiye woko," Latela omedo bot ngat acel i kin lupilida: ento Laco ma ki lwongo ni Hatter onongo dong pe nen ma peya lupilida ni oo i doggola.

"Lwong lacaden mukene!" Rwot owaco.

Lacaden mukene onwongo obedo latedo pa Duchess. En otingo bok ma ki lwongo ni pepper-box i cinge, ki Alice otamo anga ma en obedo, ma peya en odonyo i kot, ki kit ma dano ma tye cok ki doggola oyabo ngwec kwede i cawa acel.

"Mi caden ni," Rwot owaco.

"Pe," latedo owaco.

Kabaka oneno ki lworo i kom ler ma ki lwongo ni White Rabbit, ma owaco ki dwan ma lapiny ni, "Ladit ni myero openy lacaden man ."

"Aye, ka amito, amito," Kabaka owaco, ki cwiny me cwer cwiny, ki, ingee guro cinge ki neno latedo nio ka wange onongo dong pe nen, en owaco ki dwan malongo ni, "Tarts ki yubu ki ngo?"

"Pepper, pol kare," latedo owaco.

"Treacle," owaco dwan mo ma tye ka nino ingee.

"Kel Dormouse ni," Queen owaco ki dwan malongo. "Kwer wic ler ma ki lwongo ni Dormouse ni! Kwany Dormouse ni woko ki i kot! Diyo en! Diyo en! Kwany yec wic ne!"

Pi dakika mogo kot ni weng onwongo tye ka buru, kun gi tye ka nongo Dormouse ni, ki, i kare ma gu bedo piny, la tedo ni onwongo orwenyo woko.

"Par ku!" Rwot owaco, ki cwiny me kweyo cwiny. "Lwong lacaden mukene." En omedo ki dwan ma lapiny bot dako ni, "Adaa, larema, myero ipeny lacaden ma lubo. Man weko wiya lit!"

Alice oneno White Rabbit ma en tye ka yenyo jami ma ki coyo ni, kun winyo calo tye ka tamo kit ma lacaden mukene obi bedo kwede, "-pien gin pwod gi pe ki caden madwong," en owaco bot kome kene. Tam kong kit ma en obedo ki ur kwede, i kare ma White Rabbit okwano, ki dwan malongo, nying ni "Alice!"
