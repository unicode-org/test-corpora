TI IX.
Mock Turtle no Asɛm no

“Worentumi nsusuw sɛnea m’ani agye sɛ mahu wo bio no ho, wo dedaw dɔfo!” Duchess no kae, bere a ɔde ɔdɔ de ne nsa hyɛɛ Alice de mu no, na wɔboom nantew kɔe no.

Alice ani gyei yiye sɛ ohui sɛ ɔwɔ abufuw a ɛyɛ anigye saa, na ɔkae wɔ ne tirim sɛ ebia ɛyɛ ɛmo nkutoo na ɛmaa ɔyɛɛ atirimɔdenfo saa bere a wohyiae wɔ adididan mu no.

Ɔka kyerɛɛ ne ho sɛ: “Sɛ meyɛ Duchess a, (ɛnyɛ ɛnne a ɛyɛ anidaso kɛse mu de, nanso), “Merennya ɛmo biara wɔ m’adididan mu koraa . Soup yɛ yiye yiye bere a enni hɔ —Ebia ɛyɛ ɛmo na ɛma nkurɔfo bo fuw bere nyinaa,” na ɔtoaa so, na n’ani gyee kɛse sɛ wahu mmara foforo bi, “ne vinegar a ɛma wɔyɛ nwunu —ne camomile a ɛma wɔyɛ nwunu a ɛyɛ nwononwono—ne—ne atoko-asikre ne nneɛma a ɛtete saa a ɛma mmofra yɛ dɛ nkutoo na me yam a anka nkurɔfo nim saa : afei anka wɔrenyɛ ho hwee saa, wunim—”

Na ne werɛ afi Duchess no koraa saa bere yi, na ne ho dwiriw no kakra bere a ɔtee ne nne bɛn n’aso no. "Woredwen biribi ho, me dɔfo, na ɛno ma wo werɛ fi sɛ wobɛkasa.Mintumi nka nea ɛno yɛ abrabɔ pa nkyerɛ wo mprempren ara, nanso mɛkae no kakra."

Alice de ne ho hyɛɛ mu kae sɛ: “Ebia enni bi.”

“Tut, tut, abofra!” Duchess no kae. “Biribiara wɔ abrabɔ pa, sɛ wubetumi ahu a.” Na ɔpiaa ne ho bɛn Alice nkyɛn bere a ɔrekasa no.

Alice ani annye ho kɛse sɛ ɔbɛbɛn no saa: nea edi kan no, efisɛ na Duchess no ho yɛ tan yiye ; na nea ɛto so abien, efisɛ na ne tenten fata pɛpɛɛpɛ sɛ ɔde ne srɛ bɛto Alice mmati so, na na ɛyɛ abɔso a ano yɛ nnam a ɛnyɛ dɛ. Nanso, na n’ani nnye ho sɛ ɔbɛyɛ basabasa, enti ɔfoa no yiye sɛnea obetumi.

Ɔkae sɛ: “Agorudi no rekɔ so yiye mmom mprempren,” denam nkɔmmɔbɔ no a ɔmaa ɛkɔɔ so kakra no so.

Duchess no kae sɛ: “‘Tis so: na ɛno mu abrabɔ pa ne sɛ—‘Oh, ‘tis ɔdɔ, ‘tis ɔdɔ, ɛno na ɛma wiase no di akɔneaba!’”

Alice de asɛm a ɛyɛ sereserew kae sɛ: “Obi kae sɛ obiara a ɔhwɛ n’ankasa nnwuma so na ɛyɛ!”

Duchess no tutuu ne hwene ketewa a ano yɛ nnam no guu Alice mmati mu bere a ɔde kaa ho no kae sɛ: “Ah, yiw! Ɛkyerɛ ade koro no ara, na ɛno mu abrabɔ pa ne sɛ—‘Hwɛ ntease no so, na nnyigyei ahorow no bɛhwɛ wɔn ho.’”

“Hwɛ sɛnea n’ani gye ho sɛ obehu abrabɔ pa wɔ nneɛma mu!” Alice kae wɔ ne tirim sɛ.

Duchess no kae bere a ogyinae kakra akyi sɛ: “Mewɔ akokoduru ka sɛ woresusuw nea enti a memfa me nsa nhyɛ w’asen mu no ho: “nea enti a ɛte saa ne sɛ, m’adwene mu yɛ me naa wɔ wo flamingo no abufuw ho. So mɛsɔ sɔhwɛ no ahwɛ?”

Alice de ahwɛyiye buae sɛ: “Ebia ɔbɛka,” na na ɔnte nka sɛ ne ho yeraw no koraa sɛ ɔbɛsɔ sɔhwɛ no ahwɛ.

Duchess no kae sɛ: “Nokware paa: flamingo ne sinapi nyinaa keka. Na ɛno mu abrabɔ pa ne sɛ—‘Nnomaa a wɔn ho yɛ ntakra boaboa wɔn ho ano.’”

Alice kae sɛ: “Sinapi nkutoo na ɛnyɛ anomaa.”

Duchess no kae sɛ: “Ɛteɛ, sɛnea ɛtaa ba no: ɔkwan a emu da hɔ bɛn ara na wowɔ a wode hyehyɛ nneɛma!”

Alice kae sɛ: “ Misusuw sɛ ɛyɛ aboɔden abo .”

Duchess a na ɛte sɛ nea wasiesie ne ho sɛ ɔbɛpene biribiara a Alice aka so no kae sɛ: “Nokwarem no, ɛte saa; “sinapi-tubea kɛse bi bɛn ha. Na ɛno mu abrabɔ pa ne sɛ—‘Dodow a me de dɔɔso no, dodow no ara na mo de sua.’”

“Oo, minim!” Alice a na ontie asɛm a etwa to yi teɛɛm sɛ, “ɛyɛ nhabannuru.Ɛnte sɛ biako, nanso ɛte saa.”

Duchess no kae sɛ: “Me ne wo yɛ adwene koraa; “na ɛno mu abrabɔ pa ne sɛ—‘Yɛ nea ɛbɛyɛ te sɛ nea woyɛ’—anaasɛ sɛ wopɛ sɛ yɛka no tiawa a—‘Mfa w’adwene da sɛ worenyɛ ɔkwan foforo biara a ɛnyɛ nea ebia ɛbɛyɛ te sɛ nea afoforo sɛ nea na woyɛ anaasɛ ebia na woyɛ no nyɛ ɔkwan foforo so sen sɛnea na woyɛ no anka ɛbɛyɛ te sɛ nea na woyɛ no.’”

Alice de nidi kɛse kae sɛ: “Misusuw sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ mete saa asɛm no ase yiye, sɛ mema wɔkyerɛw too hɔ a: nanso mintumi nni akyi koraa sɛnea woka no.”

Duchess no de anigye nne buae sɛ: “Ɛno nyɛ hwee wɔ nea sɛ mepaw a metumi aka no ho.”

Alice kae sɛ: “Bɔ mpae mma nhaw wo ho sɛ wobɛka akyɛ nsen saa.”

“Oo, nka ɔhaw ho asɛm!” Duchess no kae. “Mede biribiara a maka de besi nnɛ no yɛ akyɛde ma wo.”

“Akyɛde bi a ne bo nyɛ den!” Alice susuwii sɛ. “Ɛyɛ me anigye sɛ wɔmfa awoda akyɛde mma saa!” Nanso wamfa ne ho anhyɛ mu sɛ ɔbɛka no den.

“Woredwene ho bio?” Duchess no bisae, na ɔsan tutuu ne hwene ketewa a ano yɛ nnam no bio.

Alice kaa no denneennen sɛ: “Mewɔ hokwan sɛ misusuw nneɛma ho,” efisɛ na wafi ase rehaw kakra.

Duchess no kae sɛ: “Ɛkame ayɛ sɛ ɛfata sɛ mprako tu, na m—”

Nanso ha no, ɛyɛɛ Alice nwonwa kɛse sɛ Duchess no nne wui, wɔ asɛmfua ‘abrabɔ pa’ a n’ani gye ho kɛse mpo mfinimfini, na abasa a na ɛbata ne de ho no fii ase wosow. Alice maa n’ani so, na na Ɔhemmaa no gyina wɔn anim, na ne nsa abɔ mu, na n’anim ayɛ kusuu te sɛ aprannaa.

“Da pa, w’Awurade!” Duchess no de nne a ɛba fam na ɛyɛ mmerɛw fii ase.

Ɔhemmaa no teɛɛm sɛ: “Afei, mede kɔkɔbɔ a ɛfata ma wo,” na ɔde ne nan sii fam bere a ɔrekasa no; “ɛbɛyɛ sɛ wo anaa wo ti fi hɔ, na ɛno bɛyɛ bere fã mu! Fa nea wopɛ!”

Duchess no faa nea ɔpɛ, na na onni hɔ wɔ bere tiaa bi mu.

Ɔhemmaa no ka kyerɛɛ Alice sɛ: “Momma yɛnkɔ so nni agoru no; na Alice suro dodo sɛ ɔbɛka asɛm biara, nanso ɔde nkakrankakra san n’akyi kɔɔ croquet-asase no so.

Na ahɔho afoforo no de Ɔhemmaa no a onni hɔ no adi dwuma, na na wɔregye wɔn ahome wɔ sunsuma mu: nanso, bere a wohuu no no, wɔde ahopere san kɔɔ agoru no mu, na Ɔhemmaa no kae ara kwa sɛ bere tiaa bi a wɔbɛkyɛ no bɛma wɔahwere wɔn nkwa.

Bere a na wɔredi agoru no nyinaa sɛ Ɔhemmaa no annyae ntɔkwaw a wɔne agodifo afoforo no di, na wɔteɛm da sɛ “Off with his head!” anaa “Fa ne ti kɔ!” Wɔn a obuu wɔn fɔ no, asraafo no de wɔn kɔtoo afiase, na nokwarem no na ɛsɛ sɛ wogyae sɛ wɔyɛ adum na wɔatumi ayɛ eyi, ma enti ebeduu dɔnhwerew fã anaa nea ɛte saa awiei no, na apon biara nni hɔ a aka, na na agodifo no nyinaa, gye Ɔhene, Ɔhemmaa, ne Alice, wɔ afiase na wɔabu wɔn kumfɔ.

Afei Ɔhemmaa no gyaee, na ne home ayɛ no yaw koraa, na ɔka kyerɛɛ Alice sɛ, “So woahu Ɔkraman a Ɔyɛ Ɔserewfo no de besi nnɛ?”

Alice kae sɛ: “Dabi. “Minnim nea Mock Turtle yɛ mpo.”

Ɔhemmaa no kae sɛ: “Ɛyɛ ade a wɔde yɛ Mock Turtle Soup.”

Alice kae sɛ: “Minhuu bi da, anaasɛ mentee bi da.

Ɔhemmaa no kae sɛ: “Enti bra, na ɔbɛka n’abakɔsɛm akyerɛ wo.”

Bere a wɔboom rekɔ no, Alice tee sɛ Ɔhene no de nne a ɛba fam reka akyerɛ adwumakuw no nyinaa sɛ, “Wɔde mo nyinaa akyɛ mo.” “Bra, ɛno yɛ adepa!” ɔkaa no ne tirim, ɛfiri sɛ na wate nka sɛ n’ani nnye koraa wɔ nnipa dodoɔ a Ɔhemmaa no ahyɛ sɛ wɔnkum wɔn no ho.

Ankyɛ koraa na woduu Gryphon bi so, a ɔda owia mu a wada denneennen. (Sɛ wunnim nea Gryphon yɛ a, hwɛ mfonini no.) “Up, lazy thing!” Ɔhemmaa no kaa sɛ, “na fa ababaa yi kɔhwɛ Mock Turtle no, na ɔmmɛtie n’abakɔsɛm Ɛsɛ sɛ mesan kɔhwɛ wɔ kum bi a mahyɛ akyi;” na ɔkɔɔ hɔ, na ogyaw Alice nkutoo ne Gryphon no. Alice ani annye abɔde no hwɛbea ho koraa, nanso ne nyinaa mu no osusuwii sɛ ɛbɛyɛ nea ahobammɔ wom koraa sɛ ɔbɛtra ne nkyɛn te sɛ nea ɔbɛkɔ akɔdi saa Ɔhemmaa a ɔyɛ atirimɔdensɛm no akyi: enti ɔtwɛnee.

Gryphon no sɔre tenaa ase na ɔde ne nsa twitwiw n’ani: afei ɔhwɛɛ Ɔhemmaa no kosii sɛ na obiara nhu no: afei ɔserewee. “Anigye bɛn ara ni!” Gryphon no kae, na ne fã ka kyerɛɛ ne ho, na ne fã ka kyerɛɛ Alice.

“Dɛn ne anigyede no?” Alice kae.

Gryphon no kae sɛ: “Dɛn ntia, ɔno .” “Ɛyɛ n’apɛde nyinaa, sɛ: wonkum obiara da, wunim Bra!”

“Obiara ka sɛ ‘bra!’ ha,” Alice susuwii, bere a ɔkɔɔ akyi brɛoo no: “Wɔanhyehyɛ me saa da wɔ m’asetra nyinaa mu, da!”

Na wɔnkɔɔ akyirikyiri ansa na wɔrehu Ɔkraman a Ɔyɛ Ɔserewfo no wɔ akyirikyiri, sɛ ɔte ɔbotan ketewaa bi so a awerɛhow ne ankonam, na bere a wɔrebɛn no, na Alice tee sɛ ɔresi apini te sɛ nea ne koma bɛbubu. Ɔyɛɛ no mmɔbɔ kɛse. “Dɛn ne n’awerɛhow?” obisaa Gryphon no, na Gryphon no buae, ɛkame ayɛ sɛ ɔde nsɛmfua koro no ara a na ɔwɔ kan no mu, “Ɛyɛ n’anigye nyinaa, sɛ: onnyaa awerɛhow biara, wunim. Bra!”

Enti wɔforo kɔɔ Mock Turtle no nkyɛn, na ɔde aniwa akɛse a nusu ayɛ no ma hwɛɛ wɔn, nanso wanka hwee.

Gryphon no kae sɛ: “Ababaa yi a ɔwɔ ha yi, ɔpɛ sɛ ohu w’abakɔsɛm, ɔyɛ.”

Mock Turtle no de nne a emu dɔ na ɛyɛ tokuru kae sɛ: “Mɛka akyerɛ no: mommra, mo baanu nyinaa, montena ase, na monnka asɛm biara kosi sɛ mɛwie.”

Enti wɔtenaa ase, na obiara ankasa simma bi. Alice kaa wɔ ne tirim sɛ, “Minhu sɛnea obetumi awie da , sɛ wanmfi ase a.” Nanso ɔde boasetɔ twɛn.

Awiei koraa no, Mock Turtle no de ahome kɛse kae sɛ: “Bere bi, na meyɛ Turtle ankasa.”

Saa nsɛm yi akyi no, wɔyɛɛ kommyɛ tenten paa, na wɔteɛm sɛ “Hjckrrh!” efi Gryphon, ne Mock Turtle no su a emu yɛ duru bere nyinaa. Na ɛkame ayɛ sɛ Alice bɛsɔre na waka sɛ, “Meda wo ase, owura, wɔ w’asɛm a ɛyɛ anigye no ho,” nanso wantumi anhyɛ ne ho so sɛ ɛbɛyɛ sɛ pii bɛba, enti ɔtraa ase dinn na wanka hwee.

“Bere a na yɛyɛ nketewa no,” Mock Turtle no toaa so awiei koraa, wɔ odwo mu kɛse, ɛwom sɛ ɔda so ara si apini kakra mprempren ne mprempren de, “yɛkɔɔ sukuu wɔ po mu.Ná owura no yɛ Ɔkraman dedaw bi —na yɛtaa frɛ no Mpɔtorɔ—.”

“Adɛn nti na wofrɛɛ no Mpɔtorɔ, sɛ na ɔnyɛ bi a?” Alice bisae sɛ.

Awiei koraa no, Gryphon no ka kyerɛɛ Mock Turtle no sɛ, “Ka kar kɔ so, akwakoraa! Mma nnyɛ ho da mũ nyinaa!” na ɔtoaa so wɔ saa nsɛm yi mu sɛ:

“Yiw, yɛkɔɔ sukuu wɔ po mu, ɛwom sɛ ebia wunnye nni de—”

“Kura wo tɛkrɛma!” Gryphon no de kaa ho, ansa na Alice retumi akasa bio. Mock Turtle no kɔɔ so.

Mock Turtle no buae sɛ: “Nokwarem no, Reeling and Writhing, sɛ yɛbɛhyɛ aseɛ a; “na afei Nkontaabu nkorabata ahorow—Ambition, Distraction, Uglification, ne Derision.”
