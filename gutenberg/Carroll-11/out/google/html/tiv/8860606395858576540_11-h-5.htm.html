IKYAV I TESE V.
Kwaghwan u or u nan er kwaghbo yô .

Caterpillar man Alice yange ve kenger ayol a ve shighe kar kpuaa, ve lu viing viing: ken masejime yô, Caterpillar dugh hookah la ken zwa na kera, maa ôr kwagh a na sha imo i doon ga, i i lu a mciem iyol ga yô.

Kater la kaa ér: “Ka an nan lu we?

Kwagh ne lu gbenda u taver or ishima u hiin iliam a nan ga. Alice gema kaa ér, “Mo—m fa ga, orvesen, hegen tseegh—a shi nan kpa m fa or u m lu shighe u m nder pepe ne la, kpa m hen mer alaghga i gema mo acin imôngo hii shighe la je.”

“Ka nyi u lu ôron sha kwagh ne?” Ka kwagh u Katerpillar la ôr sha ishimavihin je la. “Pase iyol you!”

Alice kaa ér: “M ngu a fatyo u pasen iyol yam ga , m ngu cian, orvesen, sha ci u m ngu iyol yam ga, u nenge.”

Katerpillar kaa ér: “M nenge ga.”

Alice gema kaa a na sha icivir kpishi ér: “M ngu cian mer me hemba ôron kwagh ne wang ga, gadia mo iyol yam kpa me kav kwagh ne sha hiihii la ga; shi u lun a mzehemen kposo kposo ken iyange i môm la ka kwagh u nan mo mciem kpishi.”

Katerpillar kaa ér: “Ka nahan ga.

Alice kaa ér: “Een, alaghga u ngu a zua a mi nahan ga; “kpa shighe u i gbe u ú gema ú hingir chrysalis—u er nahan iyange igen, u fa—man shighe u kwagh ne una kar yô, u hingir butterfly yô, doo u me hen mer u nenge wer kwagh la ka kwagh u kpilighyol kpuaa, shin ka nahan ga?”

Katerpillar kaa ér: “Ka kwagh u kiriki ga.

Alice kaa ér: “Een, alaghga ishima ia lu u ker kposo; “kwagh u m fe yô, a lu mo kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi .”

"We!" Ka kwagh u Katerpillar a kaa la sha u vendan kwagh. "U ngu ana? "

Kwagh ne hide a ve ken mhii u iliam la. Alice yange vihi ishima kpuaa sha kwagh u Caterpillar ôr kpuaa nahan la, nahan a cir iyol na sha, a kaa a na sha ishimavihin kpishi ér, “M hen mer, doo u ú vande ôrom or u u lu la.”

"Ha cii U nyi?" ka kwagh u Kater a kaa je la.

Mpin ugen u yange kpiligh mo iyol yô, lu heen; man er Alice lu a hen sha ityôkyaa i dedoo ga, shi er lu inja er Caterpillar ngu ken mlu u vihin tsung yô, a gema ijime.

“Hide va!” Katerpillar la yila un ken ijime. “M ngu a kwagh u vesen u me ôr yô!”

Kwagh ne yange lu inja er ka kwagh u nan ishimaverenkeghen nahan, sha mimi yô: Alice gema shi hide va.

Katerpillar kaa ér: “Kôr ishimavihin you.”

“Ka nahan tseegh kpa?” Alice kaa nahan, a cir ishimavihin na sha er una fetyô la cii.

Kater la kaa ér: “Ei.

Alice hen ér alaghga una kegh, sha ci u un ngu a kwagh ugen u eren ga, man alaghga ken masejime yô, alaghga kwagh ne una ôr un kwagh u a doo u una ungwa yô. Yange cir ahumbe miniti kpuaa a u lamen shio, kpa ken masejime yô, a bugh ave, a hide a dugh hookah la ken zwa na kera, a kaa ér, “Nahan u hen wer u gema, shin ka nahan ga?”

Alice kaa ér: “M ngu cian mer m ngu, orvesen; “M kera umbur akaa er yange m vande eren la ga—shi m kera lu a mzehemen môm miniti pue imôngo ga!”

“U umbur akaa a nyi nahan ga?” ka kwagh u Kater a kaa je la.

“Een, m nôngo mer me kaa mer “Ka nyi i ne ve ikyôhô i kiriki i i lu a tom kpishi la i er?”, kpa akaa ne cii va kposo!” Alice gema kaa ér un ngu a ishimavihin kpishi.

Katerpillar la kaa ér: “Hide kaa wer, ‘ U bee iyol, Fada William .’”

Alice yange cir ave, maa hii u ôron ér:—

Gumor la kaa ér: “U bee iyol, Fada William, .
“Man ice you hingir pupuu kpishi;
Kpa u tile sha ityough you gbem sha won—
U hen wer, sha anyom ou ne, ka shami kpa?”

Fada William gema kaa a wan na ér: “Sha shighe u m lu iyev la, .
“Yange m cian mer alaghga kwagh ne una vihi ityough;
Kpa, er m lu a vangertiôr vough mer m ngu a kwagh môm ga yô, .
Ka mimi, m eren kwagh ne kwa kimbir kimbir.”

Gumor la kaa ér: “U bee iyol, er m vande ôron nahan, .
Shi ve vese ve hemba lun a iyol i kpeghee;
Kpa u gema u nyôr ken ijime sha hunda—
Msen, ka nyi i lu ityôkyaa i kwagh shon?”

Orfankwagh la kaa ér: “Sha shighe u m lu iyev la, .
“Yange m kura ave am cii sha inja kpishi .
Sha u yaren tom a mkurem man—shilling môm u akwati la—
De m tee u ikyav mbi tswamev mbigenev kpa?”

Gumor la kaa ér: “U bee iyol, shi zwa wou kpa u yina kpishi .
Gadia kwagh u a hembe taver a suet cii;
Kpa u kure goose la, a ikyav mbi tswamev man zwa—
Msen, u er nan ve u er kwagh ne?”

Ter na kaa ér: “Shighe u m lu iyev la, m za tindi, .
Shi m gba anyiman sha hanma ijir vea kwase wam;
Shi tahav mbu m lu a mi la, mbu yange mbu na ityough yagh la, .
Ka kwagh u a lu zan hemen ken uma wam cii yô.”

Gumor la kaa ér: “U bee iyol, or nana hen ér .
Ka ishe you i lu her vough er ngise nahan;
Kpa u balance eel sha ityough ki ityough you—
Ka nyi yange i na ve u lu a kwaghfan kpishi nahana?”

“M na mbamlumun sha mbampin utar, man kwagh ne kuma,”
Ter na kaa ér; “de ne iyol you ahumbe ga!
U hen wer me ungwa ambaakaa la ayange cii kpa?
Be off, gayô me gbidye u sha kpekpe!”

Caterpillar kaa ér: “Kwagh la ka kwagh u vough ga.”

Alice kaa a mo sha mciem ér: “Ka vough ga, m ngu cian; “i gema akaaôron agen.”

“Ka shami ga hii sha mhii zan zan mkur,” Caterpillar kaa sha ishimataver, nahan ior cii lu viing miniti kpuaa.

Ka Caterpillar yange hii ôron kwagh ye.

“Ka nyi u soo u lun?” i pine nahan.

Alice gema kaa ér: “Oh, m fa kwagh u mzehemen ga; “ka or môm tseegh nan soo u geman akaa kpishi ga ye, u fa.”

Katerpillar kaa ér: “M fa ga .

Alice kaa kwagh ga: i lu a vande eren un kwagh u hendan a na kpishi ken uma na ga, nahan a nenge ér un ngu vihin ishima.

“U ngu a mkom hegen kpa?” ka kwagh u Kater a kaa je la.

Alice kaa ér: “Een, doo u me hemba lun a mzehemen kpuaa , orvesen, aluer u soo ga yô, inch utar ka mzehemen u vihin tsung.”

“Sha mimi yô, ka mzehemen u dedoo kpishi!” ” Caterpillar kaa nahan sha ishimavihin, a kende iyol na sha er a lu lamen la (yange lu a mzehemen u inch utar vough).

“Kpa m fa kwagh ne ga!” Alice u lun icankwase la zamber un sha imo i zungwen. Shi a hen ken ishima na ér, “M soo mer akaa a i gbe a la a vihi ishima fese nahan ga!”

Caterpillar kaa ér: “Ú va fa kwagh ne shighe a karen yô; nahan i wa hookah la ken zwa, i hide i hii u man taav.

Kwa ne yô, Alice kegh a ishimawan zan zan i kar tsuan u hiden lamen. Ken miniti môm shin uhar yô, Caterpillar dugh hookah la ken zwa na kera, maa a ôr kwagh kwa môm shin uhar, maa a cir iyol na. Tsô i sen sha ityough ki mngerem mara, i za gba yevese yemen ken toho, i lu ôron kwagh tseegh er i lu yemen la ér, “Vegher ugen la una na u vese, vegher ugen la di una na u vese kpuaa.”

Alice hen ken ishima na ér: “Vegher ugen u nyi? Vegher ugen u nyi? ”

Katerpillar kaa ér: “Sha kwagh u mngerem ma man la,” vough er yange pine un sha imo i taver nahan; man hen shighe ugen la, i kera nenge a mi ga.

Alice yange lu kenger mngerem mara sha mhen miniti môm, lu nôngon ér una fa avegher a ahar a a lu ker la; man er yange i lu vough vough yô, a nenge ér mpin ne ka u taver kpishi. Kpa ken masejime yô, a naregh ave a na sha mi zan zan a ar, a cir ikyav la kpuaa sha hanma uwegh.

“Man hegen yô, ka nyi i lu nyi?” a kaa ken ishima na, maa a hôr ikyagh ki uwegh ku yanegh la kpuaa sha u karen er kwagh la una er yô: hen shighe u dondon la, a ungwa kwagh u vihin tsung u i gbidye un shin inya hen ityough nagh yô: i gbidye un sha angahar!

Yange cier un iyol kpishi sha mgem u a va sha ivande ne, kpa a nenge ér shighe ngu u una vihi ga, sha ci u lu pande fese fese; nahan a hii u eren tom fese sha u yan kwaghyan ugen la. Yange i cir ityough nagh sha angahar a na kpishi, nahan ian i bughun zwa na kera lu ga; kpa a er kwagh ne ken masejime, nahan a kôrcio u man ikyagh ki esen iyou ki ken imese la.

“Va, ityough yagh ki bee ken masejime!” Alice kaa nahan sha imo i iember, i yange i gema i hingir mciem ken shighe ugen la, zum u a nenge ér akôv a na nga hen ijiir igen ga yô: kwagh u yange nenge a mi cii, zum u a kenger shin inya yô, lu ikyondo i vesen i i lu a mshi ga yô, i lu inja er ka ikyônough ki torough ki ki dugh ken zegemnger u ikya i ikyon i i lu ica a na la nahan.

“Ka nyi ia lu akaa a green la cii?” Alice kaa nahan. "Man ka han akôv am a za nyôr? Man oh, ave am a a lu ican ne, er nan ve m nenge a we ga?" Yange lu zenden a ve er a lu lamen la, kpa lu inja er kwagh môm due ker ga nahan, saa di u tenger kpuaa hen atô u ikya i i lu ica la tseegh.

Er lu inja er ian i eren ave na sha ityough nagh ngi ga yô, a nôngo ér una kende ityough nagh sha a ve, nahan saan un iyol kpishi er a nenge ér ityough nagh ki cir sha hanma vegher cii fese, er iyo nahan. Yange sember zuan a mzehemen u cir un shin inya hingir zigzag u doon tsung, shi lu nôngon u nyôron ken atô u ikya i ikyon, i yange nenge ér ka kwagh ugen ga, saa di ityough ki ikyon i yange lu zenden sha mi la, maa ikyenge i taver tsung na un hide ijime fese: zege ikyôhô igen gba sha ishigh nagh kpoghuloo, lu gbidyen un sha agee.

“Iyô!” Pigeon la kaa ér.

“M ngu iyô ga!” Alice kaa nahan sha ishimavihin. “De m lu tswen tswen!”

“Iyô, m hide m kaa mer!” Pigeon la hide ôr kwagh ne, kpa lu sha imo i hemban lun a mciem iyol ga, shi seer ôron kwagh sha inja i mciem ér, “M nôngo sha hanma gbenda cii, kpa kwagh môm ngu u a doo ve ga!”

Alice kaa ér: “M fa kwagh u u lu ôron la ga.”

“M nôngo m nenge a ityough ki ikyon, shi m nôngo m nenge a ikyav mbi tswamev, shi m nôngo m nenge a ikyav mbi tswamev,” Pigeon za hemen, a u nengen sha a na shio; “kpa iyongo la! Kwagh u doon ve ngu ga!”

Alice hemba lun a mciem iyol, kpa a hen er inja ngi i seer ôron kwagh ga zan zan Pigeon una kar been.

Pigeon kaa ér: “Ka inja er ka zayol u kuman u eren ikyav mbi tswamev mbin ga nahan; “kpa gba u me lu nengen sha iyongo tugh man atetan, m ngu a yav ga ken ukasua mba utar mban cii!”

Alice u yange hii u kaven inja i kwagh ne la kaa ér: “M vihi ishima kpishi er u vihi ishima yô.”

"Man er yange m tôô kon u u hembe lun sha ityoughkitaregh ken toho la nahan," Pigeon za hemen, a kende imo na sha sha u ôron kwagh genger genger, "man er m lu henen mer me kera lu sha ikyev ve ken masejime ga nahan, gba u vea va sha kwavaôndo vea gba eren ikyôr! Ugh, Iyô!"

“Kpa mo yô, m ngu iyongo ga , m kaa a we!” Alice kaa nahan. “M ngu a—m ngu a—”

"Een! U ngu nyi?" ka Pigeon je la. “M nenge u ngu nôngon wer u due a kwagh ugen!”

Alice yange umbur iyenge i mbamgem mba yange kar sha mi sha iyange la yô, a kaa ér: “Mo—m ngu wanyekwase u kiriki.”

“Aluer ka kwagh u mimi yô!” Pigeon kaa nahan sha imo i hemban vihin cii. "M nenge a mbayevkasev mba kiriki kpishi sha shighe wam, kpa mayange m nenge a imba ityough kin ga! Ei, ei! U ngu iyongo; man inja ngi i u vendan kwagh ne ga. M hen mer u lu ôron mo ken hemen wer mayange u kav aji ga!"

Alice u lu wanye u ôron kwagh u mimi kpishi la kaa ér: “M vande karen aji, mimi je; “kpa mbayevkasev mba kiriki ka ve ya aji kpishi er iyo nahan, u fa.”

Pigeon kaa ér: “M na jighjigh a mi ga; “kpa aluer ve er nahan yô, er nan ve ve lu inja i iyongo, ka kwagh u me ôr tseegh je la.”

Kwagh ne lu mhen u he hen Alice, nahan a lu viing viing miniti môm shin uhar, nahan kwagh ne na Pigeon ian i seer ér, “U ngu keren aji, m fa kwagh la tsembelee; man ka nyi i lu kwagh u vesen hen a mo aluer u ngu wanyekwase u kiriki shin u lu iyo?”

Alice kaa a mo fese ér: “Kwagh ne gbam kwagh kpishi .” “kpa m ngu keren aji, er ka i er nahan ga, man aluer yange ma m lu yô, ma m soo aji ou ga : M soo aji a a lu a mshi ga la ga.”

“Een, de, nahan!” Pigeon kaa nahan sha imo i vihin tsung, er a hide a tema ken ikyônough nagh yô. Alice yange a gure inya hen atô u ikyon la er a fetyô la cii, sha ci u ityough nagh ki lu eren tom ken atô u agbar a ikyon la, nahan hanma shighe yô, gba u una tile una bugh ki. Shighe kar yô, a umbur ér un ngu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér mushroom la sha ave her, nahan a hii u eren tom sha inja kpishi, a hii eren ikyav mbi tswamev mbi môm, a mase eren mbigenev, shi ashighe agen a lu vesen shi ashighe agen a lu kpuaa, zan zan a kar nôngon ér una hide a iyol na sha er yange lu la.

Yange lu ica i gba je kera lu kwagh u a lu ikyua ikyua a mzehemen u vough ga, nahan sha hiihii la, lu un kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi; kpa a gema a hen kwagh ne ken miniti kpuaa, maa a hii u lamen a iyol na, er ngise nahan. "Va, vegher u awashima wam bee hegen! Mbamgem mban cii mba kpiligh mo iyol kpishi! Mayange m fa kwagh u me lu ga, hii miniti môm zan zan ugen! Nahan kpa, m hide m zua a mlu wam u vough: kwagh u a dondo yô, ka u nyôron ken sule u doon tsung la—a er nan ve kwagh la una er, m ngu henen mer?" Er lu ôron kwagh ne yô, a va nyôr hen ijiir igen i i bugh yô, iyou i kiriki igen lu ker, i lu er angahar anyiin nahan. Alice hen ér: “Or u nan lu her cii, mayange je nana er kwagh u una va sha a ve sha mzehemen ne ga: ka nyi, m cier ve iyol sha mlu ve u ken ishima la!” Nahan a hii u eren tom a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi ken uwegh ku yanegh la, shi a kera taver ishima u zan ikyua a iyou la ga zan zan a kar hiden a iyol na shin inya zan zan a kuma inch anigheni.
