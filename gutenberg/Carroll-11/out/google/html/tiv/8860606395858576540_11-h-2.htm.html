“M hemba soon u fan kwagh!” Alice vaa nahan (kpiligh un iyol kpishi, nahan hen shighe la hungur un er una lamen zwa Buter tsembelee yô); “hegen yô, m ngu bughun er ka teleskopu u hemban cii u i gbe je i lu a vande lun ga nahan! M sugh u, angahar!” (gadia yange una kenger angahar a na shin inya yô, lu inja er a gba u a kera nenge a na ga nahan, a lu yemen ica kpishi). "Oh, angahar am a kiriki a a lu ican ne, m ngu pinen mer ka an nana wa ne akondo man ikyondo i eren tom a mi hegen, mbadoon mo ishima? M fa dedoo mer me kera fatyo ga! Me lu ica kpishi u zan iyol sha kwagh wou: gba u ne nenge sha gbenda u hemban doon cii;—kpa gba u me er ve doo doo," Alice hen nahan, ve kaa er, "or I wont! nenge: Me na ve ikyav mbi tswamev mbi he hanma Kirimishi.”

Man a za hemen u wan agoyol sha iyol na er una nenge sha mi yô. Yange hen ér: “Gba u vea kar sha or u tôôn kwagh la; “man ka kwagh u doon tsung u tindin a uiyua sha angahar a or iyol i nan! Man ka kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi!

Ikyev i Alice i ken iyan, Esq.,
Ikyev i ishima,
ikyua a Fender, ( a dooshima u Alice ).

Oh dear, ka kwagh u gbilinigh je m lu ôron ne!”

Hen shighe la je ityough nagh gba sha ityough ki iyou i mkohol la: sha mimi yô, a hemba angahar ataankaranyiin hegen, nahan fese je a tôô ikyav i zenaria i kiriki la, a yem fese a yem hen hunda u sule la.

Alice u a lu icankwase la! Yange lu kwagh u una er cii, una yav sha vegher môm, una kenger ken sule la sha ishigh ki môm; kpa u karen ker la hemba lun un kwagh u lun a ishimaverenkeghen ga cii: a tema inya, a hide a hii u vaan.

Alice kaa ér: “Yange i doo u kunya ua kôr u a iyol you, wankwase u vesen er we nahan,” (alaghga una kaa nahan vough), “u zan hemen u vaan sha gbenda ne! De hen shighe ne, m kaa a we!” Kpa a za hemen vough, a lu haan mliam ashe kpishi, zan zan zege ityungu igen va kase un cii, i lu er inch unyiin nahan, shi i za ar hen vegher u iyou i mkohol la.

Shighe kar yô, a ungwa angahar lu eren ikyenge kpuaa ken ica, nahan a cir ashe fese sha u una nenge kwagh u lu van yô. Ka White Rabbit lu hiden, a hua akondo a doon tsung, a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi pupuu mbi i yer ér kid gloves la sha uwegh ku môm, shi a lu a zege fan sha uwegh kugen: a va sha agee kpishi, lu ôron kwagh ken ishima na er a lu van la ér, “Oh! Yange ishima vihi Alice ker kpishi je yô, a kegh iyol u sônon hanma or cii iwasen; nahan, zum u Rabi va ikyua a na yô, a hii, sha imo i doon ga, i mciem iyol, “Aluer u soo yô, orvesen—” Rabi hii u eren kwagh sha tswam, a haa ikyav mbi tswamev mbi pupuu mbi i yer ér kid gloves la man fan la inya, maa a gba yevese yemen ken ime sha agee a na cii.

Alice tôô fan man ikyav mbi eren tom a mi la, man er iyou i mkohol la lu yeghen kpishi yô, a za hemen u eren fan iyol na hanma shighe u a lu lamen la cii, a kaa ér: “Or u doon mo ishima, u doon mo ishima! Hanma kwagh cii ngu kposo nyian! Man nyian kpa akaa za hemen vough er ngise nahan. M pine mer, shin i gema mo tugh mbura kpa? kpa aluer m ngu kwagh môm ga yô, mpin u a dondo yô, ka an nan lu ken tar ne Ah, ka zege puzzle je la !” Shi a hii u henen sha mbayev mba a fe ve mba ve lu anyom a na la cii, sha er una nenge aluer yange ma i gema un sha ci u mô ve yô.

A kaa er, "M fa dedoo mer m ngu Ada ga, gadia ice na ka i lu a ikyav mbi doon tsung, man yam di ka i lu a ikyav mbi doon tsung ga; man m na jighjigh mer me kera lu Mabel ga, gadia m fa hanma inja kwagh cii, man un , oh! A fa kwagh kpuaa tseegh! aluer m fa akaa a yange m vande fan la cii yô, de m nenge: kwa anyiin kwa ataan ka pue kar uhar, man kwa anyiin kwa utaankarunyiin ka pue kar utar, man kwa anyiin ka kwa utaankaruhar ka—oh dooshima, mayange me za ar sha akunduahar sha mlu la ga Kpa, Tebul u Mzough u Mbahemenev mba Shin Itine la tese ér Paris ka gartamen u gartamen u Paris, man— ka shami ga, m ngu a vangertiôr mer yange i gema mo sha ci u Mabel!

“M na jighjigh mer mkaanem mara ka ma vough ga,” Alice u lun icankwase la kaa nahan, shi ashe na hide iv a mliam ashe er a lu zan hemen la, “Gba u me lu Mabel, man gba u me za lu ken iyou i kiriki i i lu a ikyav mbi tswamev la, shi me lu a akaa a eren anumbe a mi ga, man oh! lu shin inya heen A lu ve a inja ga u veren ityough inya ve kaa ér ‘Hide va sha, or u doon mo ishima!’ Me kenger sha tseegh me kaa mer ‘Ka an nan lu mo nahan? Vande ôron mo kwagh la, nahan, aluer m soo u lun or shon yô, me va sha: aluer ka nahan ga yô, me lu shin inya heen zan zan me kar hingir orgen’—kpa, oh dear!” Alice vaa afanyô ér, “M soo mer ma ve kende ityough inya! M vôr kpishi er m lu tswen tswen heen yô!”

Er lu ôron kwagh ne yô a kenger ave na shin inya, nahan kpiligh un iyol u nengen er yange wa môm ken ikyav mbi Rabbit mbi kiriki mbi pupuu mbi i yer ér white kid gloves la shighe u lu lamen la. “ Yange ma m er nan ve m er kwagh nahana?” yange hen nahan. “Aluer m hide m lu vesen yô.” A mough a za hen tebul la sha u za karen iyol na sha mi, nahan a nenge er, er yange hen nahan, hegen un ngu a mzehemen kuma er angahar ahar nahan, shi ngu zan hemen u eren kwagh ne fese fese: ica i gbe ga yô, a mase kaven er kwagh u a ne ve kwagh a lu nahan yô, ka fan u yange kôr la, nahan a haa un inya fese, sha shighe vough sha er una de u eren kwagh ne ga yô.

“Kwagh la lu kwagh u yevese kpuaa!” Alice kaa nahan, mciem gba un iyol kpishi sha mgem u a va sha ivande la, kpa saan un iyol kpishi er un nenge ér un ngu uma her yô; “man hegen yô, ka sha ci u sule la!” nahan a yevese a hide a yem sha hunda u kiriki la sha agee cii: kpa, mciem! hunda u kiriki la hide cir, man ikyav i zenaria i kiriki la lu sha tebul u gilga er ngise nahan, “man akaa hemba vihin cii,” wan u icankwase la hen nahan, “gadia mayange m lu cuku er kwagh ne nahan ga, mayange ga! Man m pase mer kwagh vihi kpishi, ka nahan!”

Er lu ôron mkaanem man nahan, angahar na gba cir, man hen shighe ugen la, gba cir! yange lu zan zan ar sha ityough nagh ken mngerem ma lun a ice. Mhen na u hiihii yô, lu ér sha gbenda ugen yô, un gba shin zegemnger, nahan a kaa ken ishima na ér, “man aluer ka nahan yô, me hide me za sha gbenda igirgi.” (Alice yange za hen kpezegemnger kwa môm ken uma na, nahan a va kure ikyaa ér, hanma ijiir i u ze hen kpezegemnger u tar u Ingila cii, u zua a ikyav mbi ôôn iyol kpishi ken zegemnger, mbayev mbagenev mba timen shin inya sha ikyav mbi i er sha kon yô, maa ve lu a ayou a tsan, man ken ijime ve yô, ve lu a ityôgh ki igirgi dogo. yange lu angahar ataankaranyiin.

“M soo mer yange ma m vaa kpishi nahan ga!” Alice kaa nahan, er a lu eren msen, nôngon ér una zua a gbenda u duen yô. "M hen mer, a tsaham sha mi hegen, sha u ôôn mo ken mliam ashe! Kwagh la una lu kwagh u kpilighyol, u lun a vangertiôr! Nahan kpa, hanma kwagh cii ngu kpilighyol nyian."

Hen shighe la je a ungwa kwagh lu eren mngerem ken ityungu la ica a na kpuaa, nahan a ôô iyol ikyua a na sha u fan kwagh u a lu yô: sha hiihii la a hen ér alaghga ka walrus shin hippopotamus, kpa a umbur er un lu cuku hegen yô, ica i gbe ga yô, a kav er ka ikyôhô i i nyôr ker er un nahan tseegh yô.

Alice hen ér: “A lu a inja hegen, u lamen a ikyôhô ne kpa? Hanma kwagh cii ngu ken won u ikyôhô ne shin inya heen, nahan doo u me hen mer alaghga ia kôrcio u lamen: aluer ka nyi je ia er kpa, kwaghbo ngu ken u nôngon ga.” Nahan a hii u ôron ér: “We Mouse, u fa gbenda u duen ken ityungu ne kpa, m vôr kpishi u ôôn iyol heen, We Mouse!” (Alice hen ér alaghga ka gbenda u vough u lamen a ikyôhô je ne: yange lu a vande eren imba kwagh ne ga, kpa a umbur er yange nenge ken takerada u anngô na u nomso u ken zwa Latin la ér, “Ikyôhô—i ikyôhô—i ikyôhô—ikyôhô—O ikyôhô!”) Ikyôhô la kenger un sha ishi sha u keren kwagh u una fa yô, shi kaa ér no it with her to, seemed to.

Alice hen ér: “Alaghga tsô, ka i kav zwa Buter ga; “M ngu a vangertiôr mer ka ikyôhô i ken tar u France, va a William u a hembe la.” (Gadia, sha mfe na u sha kwagh u mbayiase la cii, Alice lu a mhen u wang sha kwagh u yange er ica i gba la ga.) Nahan a hide a hii kwaghôron na ér: “Où est ma chatte?” ka ishember i hiihii i ken takerada na u tesen zwa French la je la. Mouse la gba duen ken mnger kera fese, nahan lu inja er ngu tenger sha ci u mciem nahan. “Oh, m zamber we mer u de mo kwaghbo!” Alice vaa fese fese, sha ci u mciem gba un iyol ér una vihi inyam i icankwase la ishima. “M hungur mo je mer u soo ikyav mbi tswamev ga.”

“Ka er ikyôhô nahan ga!” Mouse la kaa nahan, sha imo i doon tsung. “Aluer ka we u lu mo yô, u soo u lun a ikyôhô kpa?”

"Een, alaghga a lu nahan ga," Alice kaa sha imo i kundu kundu, "de vihin ishima sha kwagh ne ga. Man nahan kpa m soo mer ma m tese u ikyôhô yase Dinah: M hen mer u soo ikyôhô kpishi aluer u nenge a na tseegh yô. Ka kwagh u doon tsung u lun ving," Alice za hemen, a ôr kwagh a iyol na, er a lu ôôn iyol nahan. usu la, u ôôn angahar a na shi ôôn ishigh nagh—man ka kwagh u doon tsung u a lu a mshi ga yô—man ka kwagh u vesen u kôron ikyav mbi tswamev—oh, m zamber we!” Alice shi vaa, gadia kwa ne yô, Mouse lu eren msen sha iyol na cii, nahan a nenge ér alaghga kwagh ne vihi un ishima sha mimi. “Se kera lam sha kwagh na ga aluer u soo wer se de ôron kwagh na ga yô.”

“Se mba mimi je!” ” Mouse u lu tenger zan zan ar sha mkur u ityough nagh la kaa nahan. "Ka inja er me lam sha imba itinekwagh ne nahan! Tsombor wase yange u kôr ikyôhô ihyom hanma shighe cii: akaa a vihin tu, a a lu sha inja ga, a a lu a inja ga yô! De kera lumun mer m ungwa iti la ga!"

“Mimi je me er nahan ga!” Alice kaa nahan, sha u geman itinekwagh i se lu lamen sha mi la fese je. “U—u soo—u—mbayev kpa?” Iyôngo la na mlumun ga, nahan Alice za hemen a ishimaverenkeghen ér: “Iyôngo i kiriki i doon tsung ngi ikyua a ya wase, i doo u me tese u yô! Iyôngo i kiriki i i lu a ashe a wanger wanger yô, u fa, i lu a ice i doon tsung i i lu a mshiam kpishi yô! ken a ve—man ka kwagh u orsule ugen, u fa, man a kaa ér ngu a inja kpishi, ngu a inyaregh ki kuman pound deri môm A kaa ér ka a wua ikyôhô cii man—oh dear!” Alice vaa sha imo i vihin tsung ér, “M ngu cian mer m hide m vihi un ishima!” Gadia Mouse lu ôôn mngerem kera sha agee a na cii, shi lu eren ayôôso kpishi ken mnger er a lu yemen la.

Nahan a yila un sha zwa u kundu kundu shighe u a bee yô, a kaa ér, “Mouse dear! Hide va, shi se kera lam sha kwagh u ikyôhô shin ikyôhô kpaa ga, aluer u soo i ga yô!” Mouse yange ungwa kwagh ne yô, a gema a gba yevese hiden hen a na: ishigh nagh lu a mshi ga (sha ishimavihin, Alice hen nahan), maa a kaa sha imo i tenger kpuaa ér, “Se za nyôr hen kpezegemnger, nahan me ôr u kwagh u uma wam, nahan u kav er i hii ve m ker ikyav mbi tswamev man ikyav mbi tswamev ihyom yô.”

Shighe kuma u se za, gadia ityungu la lu ivin kpishi a inyon man annyamev mba ve gba ker la: lu a Duck man Dodo man Lory man Eaglet kua annyamev mbagenev kpishi mba ve soo u fan kwagh yô. Alice yange hemen gbenda, nahan ior mba ve lu ken iniongo la cii ve gba yevese zan hen kpezegemnger.
