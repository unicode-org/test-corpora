IKYAV I TESE VII.
Mkombo u Tea u Mad

I ver tebul ugen shin inya hen kon ugen sha ishigh ki iyou la, nahan March Hare man Hatter lu man tii her: Dormouse ugen tema hen atô ve, lu yaven kpoghuloo, mba uhar mbagenev mbara lu eren tom a mi er ka ikyagh ki cir kwagh nahan, ve lu veren ave sha mi, shi ve lu lamen sha ityough ki tebul la. Alice hen ér: “Ka kwagh u doon ga kpishi sha ci u Dormouse; “kpa, er i lu yaven yô, m hen mer kwagh gba un sha mi ga.”

Tebul la lu zege tebul, kpa ve utar mbara cii ve kohol imôngo hen kpe u tebul la môm, ve kaa ér: “Ijiir ngi ga, ijiir ngi ga!” ve vaa zum u ve nenge Alice lu van la. “Ijiir ngi kpishi !” Alice kaa nahan sha ishimavihin, maa a tema sha zege ikyonough ki torough ki ki lu hen kpe u tebul la.

March Hare kaa a mo sha imo i taver mo ishima ér: “Ma nen wain.”

Alice kenger tebul la cii, kpa kwagh lu sha mi ga, saa di tii tseegh. A kaa ér: “M nenge a wain môm ga.”

March Hare kaa ér: “Kwagh môm ngu ga.

Alice kaa a mo sha ishimavihin ér: “Nahan yange u lu a inja kpishi u nan un kwagh ne ga.”

Or u i yer ér March Hare la kaa ér: “Yange u lu a inja kpishi u teman inya a u i lôhô u ga yô.”

Alice kaa ér: “Yange m fa mer ka tebul wou ga; “i ver sha ci u ior kpishi mba hemban utar.”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Ice you soo ér i cir.” Yange kenger Alice ica i gba je sha u fan kwagh kpishi, man lu kwaghôron na u hiihii je ne.

Alice kaa a mo sha ishimavihin ér: “Doo u ú hen u ôron kwagh u or sha tseeneke u nan ga; “ka kwagh u vihin kpishi.”

Or u eren ikyondo la bugh ashe kpishi zum u a ungwa kwagh ne yô; kpa kwagh u yange ôr yô , lu ér, “Er nan ve ikyôhô i lu er ka tebul u ngeren kwagh nahana?”

“Va, se za ember hegen!” Alice hen nahan. A seer ôron genger genger ér: “Saan mo iyol er ve hii u pinen akaa a doon tsung yô.—M na jighjigh mer me fa kwagh u a lu la.”

“U ngu kaan wer u hen wer u zua a mlumun sha mi kpa?” ka kwagh u Hare u Maaci la je la.

Alice kaa ér: “Ka nahan vough.

March Hare za hemen kaa ér: “Nahan doo u ú ôr kwagh u ú soo u ôron la.”

Alice gema kaa ér: “M fa; “at least—at least m ngu ôron kwagh u m lu ôron ne—ka kwagh môm je la, u fa.”

“Ka kwagh u môm tseegh ga!” ka kwagh u Or u Hatter la je la. “Alaghga u kaa wer ‘M nenge kwagh u m ya’ la ka kwagh môm a ‘M ya kwagh u m nenge’ la vough!”

March Hare shi kaa ér: “Alaghga u kaa wer, ‘Kwagh u m zough a mi la doom’ la ka kwagh môm a ‘Kwagh u m zough a mi la doom’ la!”

Dormouse u lu inja er ngu lamen ken mnya la shi kaa ér: “Alaghga u kaa wer, ‘M ôôn shighe u m yav’ la ka kwagh môm a ‘M yav shighe u m lu ôôn’ la!”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “ Kape we kpa u lu je la,” nahan heen iliam la bee, nahan iniongo la tema viing miniti môm, Alice di lu henen sha akaa a yange umbur sha kwagh u ikyôhô man ikyav mbi ngeren kwagh la cii, man kwagh ne lu kwagh u vesen ga.

Or u eren Hatter la yange hii u vihin mlu u ving la. “Ka iyange i nyi ken uwer?” a kaa nahan, a gema a kenger Alice: a dugh agogo na ken akpa na kera, lu kenger un sha ishimavihin, lu tenger un hanma shighe, shi lu kôron un sha tough.

Alice hen sha kwagh ne kpuaa, maa kaa ér “U sha unyiin la.”

“Ayange ahar a vihin!” or u eren Hatter la kaa ér. “M kaa a we mer butter una kuma sha ityom la ga!” a seer kenger March Hare sha ishimavihin.

March Hare gema kaa a na sha ishima i legh legh ér: “Ka mkurem ma hemban doon cii je la.”

Or u eren ikyondo la gba ôron kwagh sha mciem ér: “Een, kpa alaghga ikyav mbigenev kpa mbi nyôr ker, yange ma u wa mbi sha ikyagh ki eren bredi la ga.”

March Hare la tôô agogo la kenger un sha ishimavihin: maa a wa un ken iyongo na i man tii la, a hide a kenger un: kpa a kera hen kwagh u hemban doon u una ôr ga, saa di kwaghôron na u hiihii la ér, “Ka mkurem ma hemban doon cii , u fa.”

Alice yange lu kenger sha ityough nagh sha u keren kwagh u una fa yô. “Ka kwagh u doon tsung je ne!” a kaa ér. “Ka i tese iyange i uwer, shi i tese ahwa a a lu la ga!”

“Er nan ve i gbe u a er nahana?” or u eren Hatter la ôr kwagh sha mciem. “Ikyav you tese u inyom i i lu la kpa?”

Alice gema kaa ér: “Een, ka nahan ga, kpa ka sha ci u ka i lu ken inyom i môm shighe gôgônan nahan.”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Ka kwagh u a lu mo tseegh je ne .”

Alice yange ungwa kwagh ne kpiligh un iyol kpishi, kwaghôron u The Hatter la lu inja er ngu a inja ga nahan, nahan kpa, lu zwa Buter jighilii. A kaa a mo sha icivir er una fetyô la cii ér: “M kav u tsembelee ga.”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Dormouse hide yav,” maa a haa tii u tseen kpuaa sha ityough nagh.

Dormouse la kende ityough sha sha ishimawan ga, maa kaa, a bugh ashe shio ér, “Een, mimi; ka kwagh u m lu ôron iyol yam je la.”

“Ú fa kwagh u a lu ikyav i tesen la kpa?” or u eren Hatter la kaa nahan, a hide a gema a kenger Alice.

Alice gema kaa ér: “Ei, m de kwagh ne, ka nyi i lu mlumun?”

Or u eren Hatter la kaa ér: “M ngu a mhen môm kpaa ga.

Hare u Maaci la kaa ér: “Mo kpa m ngu ga.

Alice yange ôr kwagh sha ishimavihin. A kaa ér: “M hen mer alaghga u er kwagh u hemban doon sha shighe la, a u vihin shighe la sha u pinen mbampin mba ve lu a mbamlumun sha mi ga la.”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Luun er u fa Shighe tsembelee er mo nahan yô, ma u ôr kwagh u vihin shighe la ga , ka un .”

Alice kaa ér: “M fa kwagh u ú soo u ôron la ga.

“Sha mimi yô, u ngu a mi ga!” or u eren ikyondo la kaa nahan, a cir ityough sha sha u vendan kwagh. “M taver ishima mer mayange u lam a Time je kpaa ga!”

Alice gema kaa a na sha kwaghfan ér: “Alaghga a lu nahan ga, kpa m fa mer gba u me hemba shighe shighe u m lu henen ityogholough yô.”

Or u eren Ikyondo la kaa ér: “Ah! "Una tile sha ityough ki gbidyen ga. Hegen yô, aluer u ngu lun vea na doo doo yô, una er hanma kwagh u u soo cii sha agogo la. Ikyav i tesen yô, tôô ase wer ka ahwa anigheni atetan, ka shighe u hiin ityesen tseegh: u gba u u ôr Time kwagh sha zwa u hôngorough tseegh, nahan agogo la a gba yevese sha shighe môm, shighe u a lu zan hemen la!"

(“M soo mer ma lu nahan tseegh,” March Hare kaa ken ishima na sha zwa u hôngorough.)

Alice hen sha kwagh ne nahan, a kaa ér: “Kwagh la una lu kwagh u vesen, sha mimi yô, kpa hen shighe la yô—doo u ijen ia kôrom sha kwagh ne ga, u fa.”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Alaghga a lu sha hiihii la ga, kpa u ngu a kôr i sha shighe u u soo la cii.”

“Ka gbenda u u eren kwagh je la kpa?” Alice pine nahan.

Or u eren Hatter la kende ityough sha sha ishimavihin. “Ka mo ga!” a gema a kaa ér. “Se gba anyiman ken uwer u Maaci u u kar la—cii man a gba angev mbu shimav, u fa—” (a tese uwegh sha ikyagh ki man tii ki i yer ér March Hare la,) “—lu hen zege iniongo i Queen of Hearts yange er la, nahan gba u me wa icam .

‘Twink, twink, wanye u kiriki!
Nenge imba er m lu henen mer ka nyi u lu ker ne!’

U fa icam la, alaghga kpa?”

Alice kaa ér: “M vande ungwan kwagh u a lu nahan yô.

Or u eren ikyondo la za hemen kaa ér: “Ka i za hemen, u fa, sha gbenda ne:—

‘Sha tar cii u ngu purugh, .
Er ka ikyagh ki tii ki sha kwavaôndo nahan.
Twinke, twinke—’”

Hen ijiir ne, Dormouse la cir iyol na, maa hii u wan icam ken mnya ér “ Twinkle, twinkle, twinkle, twinkle —” shi za hemen ica i gba je yô, gba u vea cir un sha er una de yô.

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Een, yange m lu a bee ivur i hiihii la ga, maa Torkwase la mough sha, gba ôron genger genger ér, ‘A wua shighe!

“Ka kwagh u cieriyol kpishi!” ka kwagh u Alice yange ôr je la.

"Man hii hen shighe la je," Or u eren ikyondo la za hemen sha imo i vihin tsung, "una er kwagh u m sôn un la ga! Hanma shighe yô, ka ahwa ataratar hegen."

Mhen u injaa ugen va ken ityou i Alice. “Ka ityôkyaa i i ne ve i ver akaa a eren tii kpishi ken won heen je la kpa?” a pine nahan.

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa a na sha ishimavihin ér: “Een, ka nahan, hanma shighe yô, ka shighe u man tii, shi se mba a shighe u ôôn akaa hen atô u ashighe la ga.”

“Nahan u za hemen u zenden cir, m hen mer?” Alice kaa nahan.

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Kape vough je la, er akaa a lu been yô.”

“Kpa ka nyi ia er shighe u u hide u va sha mhii laa?” Alice yange taver ishima pine un.

March Hare la yange cir kwaghôron la, a ôr kwagh sha mciem ér: “Tôô ase wer se gema itinekwagh la.” "M ngu vôron sha kwagh ne. M vote wankwase la ôr se kwagh."

Alice yange cier un iyol sha kwagh u i kaa ér a er la, nahan a kaa ér: “M ngu cian mer m fa môm ga.”

“Nahan Dormouse una er nahan!” ve cii ve vaa. “Mough sha, Dormouse!” Tsô ve pin un sha avegher a ahar la cii kwa môm.

Dormouse la maa bugh ashe a na sha mciem. A kaa a mo sha imo i doon ga ér: “Yange m yav ga, m ungwa hanma kwagh u ne lu ôron cii.”

“Ôr se kwagh ugen!” ka kwagh u Hare u Maaci la je la.

“Een, m zamber we, er nahan!” Alice yange zamber a na.

Or u eren ikyondo la shi kaa ér: “Shi er kwagh sha mi fese, gayô u hide u yav cii ve kwagh la una bee ye.”

“Shighe ugen la, anmgbianev mba kasev mba kiriki utar lu,” Dormouse hii u ôron kwagh sha mciem kpishi; “man ati a ve lu Elsie man Lacie man Tillie, ve lu hen kpe u ityungu—”

“Ka nyi yange ve lu uma sha mini?” kape Alice u hanma shighe cii kwagh a gbe un sha mbampin mba sha kwagh u kwaghyan man kwaghman la yange ôr je la.

Dormouse yange hen sha kwagh miniti môm shin uhar yô, a kaa ér: “Yange ve lu yan kwaghyan u i yer ér treacle la.”

Alice kaa a na sha ishima i legh legh ér: “Yange vea er nahan ga, u fa; “ma ve lu a angev.”

Dormouse kaa ér: “Kape yange ve lu je la; “ angev kpishi .”

Alice nôngo ér una hen ken ishima na er imba gbenda u uma u kpilighyol ne ua lu yô, kpa kwagh ne kpiligh un iyol kpishi, nahan a za hemen ér: “Kpa er nan ve ve lu hen kpe u ityungu?”

March Hare kaa a Alice sha gbashima kpishi ér: “Seer man tii.”

Alice gema kaa a na sha imo i vihin tsung ér: “M ngu a zua a kwagh ga, nahan me kera tôô kwagh ugen ga.”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “U ngu kaan wer u ngu a ngohol kwagh kpuaa ga ,” ka kwagh u ican ga kpishi u ngohol kwagh u a hembe kwagh u a lu ga la.”

Alice kaa ér: “Or môm kpa nan pine mnenge wou ga.

“Ka an nan lu ôron kwagh u nan iyol i nan hegen?” or u eren Hatter la pine sha mhembe.

Alice fa kwagh u una ôr sha kwagh ne tsembelee ga: nahan a wase iyol na u man tii man bredi man mkurem, maa a gema a kenger Dormouse, a hide a pine un mpin na. “Er nan ve yange ve lu hen kpe u ityungu?”

Dormouse la shi tôô miniti môm shin uhar hen sha kwagh ne, maa kaa ér, “Yange lu treacle-well.”

“Imba kwagh ne ngi ga!” Alice lu hiin u vihin ishima kpishi, kpa Hatter man March Hare ve za “Sh! sh!” man Dormouse la kaa a na sha ishimavihin ér, “Aluer u ngu a ian i lun or u ken tar ga yô, hemba doon u u kure kwaghôron la sha ci wou.”

“Ei, m zamber we, za hemen!” Alice kaa a iyol hiden a mi ijime kpishi ér; “Me kera ôr kwagh a we ga. M taver ishima me kaa mer alaghga kwagh ngu .”

“Môm, mimi je!” Dormouse kaa nahan sha ishimavihin. Nahan kpa, a lumun ér una za hemen. “Man nahan anmgbianev mba kasev mba kiriki mba utar mban—ve lu henen u eren ikyav, u fa—”

“Ka nyi yange ve er?” Alice kaa nahan, a hungur un ityendezwa na la jimin cii.

Dormouse kaa ér: “Treacle,” a u henen sha kwagh ne cii shio.

Or u eren ikyondo la yange ôr kwagh a na sha zwa ér: “M soo kôpi u wang, se cii se yem nen ijiir i môm.”

A za hemen er a lu ôron kwagh la, nahan Dormouse dondo un ijime: Hare u March la mough nyôr hen ijiir i Dormouse la, man Alice gema tôô ijiir i March Hare la sha ishima na ga. Ka Hatter tseegh yange zua a mtsera sha mgem la ye: man Alice kpa yange hemba vihin cii a u ngise la, er March Hare yange sember vihin ikyagh ki mngerem ki ken ikyondo na la nahan.

Alice soo ér una hide a vihi Dormouse ishima ga, nahan a hii u ôron kwagh sha kwaghfan kpishi ér: “Kpa m kav ga, ka han yange ve dugh treacle la?”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Ú ngu a dugh mngerem ken ityungu i mngerem; “nahan doo u me hen mer u ngu a fatyo u dughun treacle ken ityungu i treacle-well—eh, ibumeor kpa?”

Alice kaa a Dormouse ér: “Kpa ve lu ken ityungu la,” nahan a tsua u veren ishima sha kwaghôron u masetyô ne ga.

Dormouse kaa ér: “Sha mimi yô, yange ve lu nahan; “—doo ken ishima.”

Mlumun ne yange kpiligh Alice u lun icankwase la iyol kpishi, nahan a de ér Dormouse la a za hemen shighe kar kpuaa, a u yangen un kwagh shio.

“Ve lu henen u eren ikyav,” Dormouse za hemen, lu ôron kwagh sha mciem shi lu ôôn ashe a na, gadia lu nôngon u yaven kpishi; “man ve nger hanma inja kwagh cii—hanma kwagh u a hii sha M—”

“Er nan ve i nger M?” Alice kaa nahan.

"Er nan?" ka kwagh u Hare u Maaci la je la.

Alice yange lu ving.

Dormouse yange cir ashe hen shighe ne, lu yemen u za yaven; kpa, er Or u Nan Ikyondo la cir nan yô, i hide i nder sha u ôron kwagh sha mciem kpuaa, maa i za hemen ér: “—kwagh ne hii sha M, er ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér mouse-traps la nahan, man uwer, man kwagh u umbur, man kwagh u ngeen kpishi—u fa wer u kaa wer akaa ka “kwagh u ngeen kpishi”—u vande nengen a kwagh u ngeen er ka kwagh u i gber nahan kpa?”

Alice kaa ér: “Sha mimi yô, hegen u pinem yô, m hen mer—”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Nahan doo u ú ôr kwagh ga.”

Kwagh u vihin tu ne hemba kwagh u Alice una kôrcio u wan ishima a mi la: a mough sha ishimavihin kpishi, a yem; Dormouse la yav fese je, man mbagenev mbara cii ve nenge ér un ngu zan ga, shin er yange kenger ken jime kwa môm shin uhar nahan kpaa, vegher ugen lu a ishimaveren ér vea yila un ken ijime: kwa u masetyô u a nenge a ve la, ve lu nôngon ér vea wa Dormouse la ken ikyagh ki man tii.

“Aluer ka nyi je kpa, mayange me kera za ker ga!” Alice kaa nahan er a lu eren tom u pasen kwagh ken toho la. “Ka iniongo i tii i hemban vihin cii i m vande zan ken uma wam cii yô!”

Er lu ôron kwagh ne la je, a nenge ér kon ugen la ngu a hunda u nyôron ker jighilii. “Kwagh la ka kwagh u doon tsung!” yange hen nahan. "Kpa hanma kwagh cii ngu a inja nyian. M hen mer alaghga me nyôr ker kwa môm tseegh." Nahan a za.

Shi kwa ne kpa a nenge a iyol na ken iyou i vesen la, shi ikyua a tebul u kiriki u i er sha gilasi la. A kaa ken ishima na ér: “Hegen yô, me hemba eren kwagh sha mi kwa ne,” maa a hii u tôôn ki ki ki lu a ikyav mbi zenaria mbi kiriki la, shi bughun hunda u u nyôr ken sule la. Tsô a za er tom a hôr mngerem ma doon tsung ma i yer ér mushroom la (a koso ma ken akpa na) zan zan a kar lun er angahar ahar nahan: maa a zende sha gbenda u cuku la: maa —a mase zuan a iyol na ken sule u doon tsung la, hen atô u ikyasen i mngerem ma doon tsung man uifi mba doon tsung.
