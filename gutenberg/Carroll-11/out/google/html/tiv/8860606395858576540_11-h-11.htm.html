IKYAV I TESE XI.
Ka an nan ii Tart laa?

Tor man Torkwase u Asema yange ve tema sha ikyonough vev ki torough zum u ve va nyôr la, ikpelaior kpishi kohol ve kase ve—hanma inja inyon i kiriki man inyamtoho cii, kua ikyav mbi kaade cii: Knave tile sha ishigh vev, i kange un sha ikyav, orshoja lu sha hanma vegher sha u kuran un; man ikyua a Tor la, lu a Ikyôhô i Puupuu, i lu a ikyenge sha uwegh ku môm, man takerada u i nger sha ikyav mbi tswamev sha uwegh kugen la. Ken atô u koti la jighilii yô, tebul lu, u i wa zege ikyagh ki tart sha mi yô: ve doo u nengen a mi, nahan ijen kôr Alice kpishi u kenger ve—a hen ér: “M soo mer ma ve er ijir la, ve na un kwaghyan u nan un msaanyol la sha ave!” Kpa lu inja er ian i kwagh ne ngi ga nahan, nahan a hii u nengen sha hanma kwagh u a lu sha iyol na cii, sha er shighe la una kar yô.

Alice yange lu a vande zan ken koti u ôron ijir ga, kpa yange ôr kwagh u ve ken ityakerada, nahan saan un iyol kpishi er un fe iti i hanma kwagh u a lu ker cii yô. A kaa ken ishima na ér: “Ka orjir la je la, sha ci u wig na u vesen la.”

Orjir la, sha mimi yô, lu Tor; man er a hua ikyondo na i tor sha wig la yô, (nenge sha ityough ki takerada la aluer u soo u fan er a er kwagh ne yô,) a nenge ér un ngu a mkom ga, shi mimi je kwagh ne lu eren un ga.

“Man ka jury-box je la,” Alice hen nahan, “man akaauma a pue kar ahar la,” (yange i gba u una kaa ér “akaauma,” u nenge, sha ci u agen ken a ve lu inyamtoho, agen di lu inyon,) “M hen mer ka ve ve lu mbaôron ijir ye.” A ôr kwagh u masetyô ne kwa har shin utar sha iyol na, a hemba lun a iwuese sha mi: gadia yange hen, man lu shami kpaa, ér ka mbayevkasev mba kiriki mba ve lu anyom a na la kpuaa tseegh ve fe inja i kwaghôron ne ye. Kpa, “mbaôron ijir” kpa yange vea er nahan vough.

Mbaôron ijir mba pue kar uhar mbara cii lu ngeren kwagh sha ikyav mbi eren tom a mi kpoghuloo. “Ka nyi ve lu eren?” Alice yange ôr kwagh a Gryphon sha mciem. “Vea kera lu a kwagh u veren shin inya ga, cii man ijirôron la i hii ye.”

Gryphon la gema kaa a na sha zwa u hôngorough ér: “Mba veren ati a ve shin inya, sha ci u mciem ma vea hungur ve cii man ijirôron la ia bee ye.”

“Akaa a ibume!” Alice hii u ôron kwagh sha imo i taver, i vihin tu, kpa a de fese, sha ci u Ikyôhô i White Rabbit la kaa ér, “Ior ve lu viing ken koti!” nahan Tor wa ikyav mbi nengen a mi, kenger sha ajiir kposo kposo sha ishimanyian, sha u fan or u nan lu lamen la.

Alice yange una nenge, shi una nenge sha ashe a ve yô, mbaôron ijir mbara cii ve lu ngeren “akaa a ibume” shin inya! sha ikyav vev mbi eren tom a mi la, nahan a nenge je ér môm ken a ve fa u ngeren “ibumeor” ga, nahan gba u una pine or u nan lu ikyua a na la ér nana ôr un. “Slates vev vea lu ken mzough u dedoo cii ve ijir ia bee ye!” Alice hen nahan.

Orgen ken mbaôron ijir mbara lu a pensel u eren tom. Kwagh ne ka mimi, Alice yange una tile ga , nahan a zende a kar ken koti la, a za nyôr ken ijime na, ica i gbe ga yô, a zua a ian i tôôn un yemen a mi. Yange er kwagh ne fese je yô, or u ôron ijir u kiriki u lu icankwase la (lu Bill, u lu Lizard la) kera fa kwagh u yange er un la ga; nahan yange una ker kwagh ne cii yô, i gba u una nger kwagh sha uwegh ku môm sha iyange la cii; man kwagh ne lu a inja kpishi ga, sha ci u yange de ikyav sha ikyav la ga.

“Herald, ôr kwagh u i we un iyol la!” Tor kaa nahan.

Sha kwagh ne yô, White Rabbit la cir ityogholough mbi tswamev mbitar, maa a bugh takerada u i nger sha ikyondo la, a ôr kwagh u a dondo ne:—

“Torkwase u Asema, yange er tart mbagenev, .
Ve cii sha iyange i summer:
Or u nan lu a asema la, yange nan ii akaa a i yer ér tarts la, .
Shi tôô ve yem a ve kera!”

Tor la kaa a mbaôron ijir la ér: “Nenge nen sha kwagh u ne kure la.”

“Ka nahan ga, ka nahan ga!” Rabi la gba ôron kwagh fese. “Kwagh u vesen ngu u una er cii ve kwagh ne una er ye!”

Tor kaa ér: “Yila orshiada u hiihii la; man Ikyôhô i Puupuu la cir ityogholough mbi tswamev kwa tar, maa yila ér, “Orshiada u hiihii!”

Orshiada u hiihii la lu Or u Hatter. A nyôr a ikyagh ki man tii sha uwegh ku môm, shi a lu a bredi u i yer ér bread-and-butter la sha uwegh kugen. A hii u ôron ér: “M zamber we, Tor wou, de u kwaghbo, sha er u va a akaa ne yô: kpa m lu a bee tii wam tsembelee ga shighe u i tindi ér i va va a mo la.”

Tor kaa ér: “Yange ma ne kure tom ne.” “Ka hanma shighe u hii?”

Or u eren ikyondo la kenger March Hare u yange dondo un ken atejir la, a kôr ave a Dormouse la. A kaa ér: “M hen mer yange lu sha iyange i Maaci 14 la.”

March Hare kaa ér: “Ikyav i pue kar itaan.”

Dormouse shi kaa ér: “Ka sha pue kar ataratar.”

Tor kaa a mbaôron ijir mbara ér: “Ngeren nen kwagh la shin inya,” nahan mbaôron ijir mbara nger ayange atar la cii sha ikyav vev mbi eren tom a mi la sha gbashima, maa ve seer sha mi, ve pande mlumun u nan la hingir shilling man pence.

Tor kaa a Or u Nan Ikyondo la ér: “Dugh ikyondo you kera.”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Ka mo ga.”

Tor la kaa ér: “ I ii un! ” maa a gema a kenger mbaôron ijir mbara, mba ve nger kwagh u yange er la fese je yô.

Or u eren Hatter la shi kaa ér: “M ngu koson ve sha u me tee yô; "M ngu a kwagh wam ga. M ngu or u eren ikyondo."

Hen ijiir ne Torkwase la wa ikyav mbi nengen a mi, maa hii u kenger Or u Nan Ikyondo la, u yange gema hingir mshi shi lu eren msen la.

Tor kaa ér: “Na shiada wou; “man de cian ga, gayô me wua u hen ijiir la je.”

Kwagh ne yange lu inja er taver orshiada la ishima ga nahan: lu geman angahar a na ahar, lu kenger Torkwase la sha ishimavihin, man ken mzeyol na la yô, a hôr zege ikyagh ki man tii na sha ityough ki bredi u i eren sha mkurem la.

Hen shighe ne je Alice ungwa kwagh u soo u fan kpishi, u yange kpiligh un iyol kpishi zan zan a kar kaven kwagh u a lu yô: a hii u hiden vesen, nahan a hen sha hiihii la ér una mough una due ken koti kera; kpa er a hen sha kwagh ne kwa har yô, a tsua ér una lu ape un lu la, shighe u ian i lu sha ci na la cii.

Dormouse u yange tema ikyua a na la kaa ér: “M soo mer u cir nahan ga.” “M kera ngu a ian i ôôn iyol ga.”

Alice kaa a na sha ishima i legh legh kpishi ér: “M ngu vesen.”

Dormouse kaa ér: “U ngu a ian i vesen heen ga .”

Alice hemba taver ishima kaa ér: “De ôron kwagh u gbilinigh ga, u fa wer we kpa u ngu vesen.”

Dormouse kaa ér: “Een, kpa m ngu vesen sha inja, ka sha gbenda u doon ga la ga.” Tsô a mough sha ishimavihin kpishi, a per a yem vegher ugen u ikyônough ki torough kira.

Shighe ne cii Torkwase la de u kenger Hatter la sha ishi ga, nahan er Dormouse lu peren ken koti la, a kaa a orvesen ugen ken koti la ér, “Va a mo a list u mbawan icam mba ve lu ken mkohol u masetyô la!” sha mi yô, Or u vihin tu u i yer ér Hatter la tenger kpishi, nahan a cir akov a na ahar la cii.

Tor la hide kaa a na sha ishimavihin ér: “Na shiada wou, gayô me wua u, aluer u ngu a mciem shin u ngu a mciem ga kpaa.”

“M ngu or u lun icanor, Tor wou,” Or u eren ikyondo la hii u man, sha imo i tenger, “—man m lu a hii u man tii wam ga—kasua môm shin i hemba nahan ga—man ka nyi i lu sha kwagh u bredi man mkurem ma doon tsung ma ma lu cir kpishi la—man mngerem ma tii ma lu cir la—”

Tor kaa ér: “Ka nyi? ”

Or u eren Hatter la gema kaa ér: “Yange i hii sha kwagh u tii la.”

“Sha mimi yô, twinkling ka a hii sha T!” Tor kaa nahan sha ishimavihin. "U tôôm mer m ngu or u eren kwagh sha lan kpa? Za hemen!"

Or u eren ikyondo la za hemen kaa ér: “M ngu or u lun icanor, man akaa kpishi yange a cir shighe u kwagh ne er kera la—ka March Hare tseegh yange kaa ér—”

“M fa ga!” March Hare la yange ôr kwagh sha mciem kpishi.

“U er nahan!” ka kwagh u Or u Hatter la je la.

“M venda kwagh ne!” ka kwagh u Hare u Maaci la je la.

Tor kaa ér: “A venda kwagh ne, de vegher la kera.”

“Een, aluer ka nyi je ia er kpa, Dormouse kaa er—” Or u eren ikyondo la za hemen, lu kenger sha ishimanyian sha u una nenge aluer un kpa una venda kwagh ne yô: kpa Dormouse venda kwagh ga, sha ci u yange yav ving.

Or u eren ikyondo la za hemen kaa ér: “Kwagh ne been yô, m seer eren bredi man mkurem—”

“Kpa ka nyi Dormouse yange ôr?” orgen ken mbaôron ijir la pine nahan.

Or u eren Hatter la kaa ér: “Kwagh la m umbur ga.”

Tor la kaa ér: “ Gba u ú umbur, gayô me wua u.”

Or u eren Hatter u vihin tu la haa iyongo na i tii man bredi man mkurem, maa gba inya sha ityough ki môm. A hii u ôron ér: “M ngu icanor, Tor wou.”

Tor kaa ér: “U ngu or u lamen tsembelee ga .”

Hen ijiir ne, môm ken mbaatsator mba ken koti la gba ôron kwagh sha iember, nahan mbatomov mba ken koti la yange ve cir un fese je. (Er kwagh la a lu ishember i taver yô, me pase ne er yange i er kwagh ne yô. Yange ve lu a zege akpa a i er sha ikyondo i i yer ér canvas la, i kange un sha zwa sha ikyav: ken kwagh ne yô, ve nyôr a ikyôhô i i yer ér guinea-pig la, ityough ki hiihii, maa ve tema sha mi.)

Alice hen ken ishima na ér: “Saan mo iyol er m nenge i er nahan yô.” “Ashighe kpishi m ôr ken upipambaabaver, shighe u i lu ôron ijir la, ér, “I nôngo ér ior ve gbidye ave, kpa mbatomov mba ken koti yange ve cir fese je,” nahan mayange m kav inja i kwagh ne ga zan zan nyian.”

Tor za hemen kaa ér: “Aluer ka kwagh u u fe sha kwagh ne tseegh yô, alaghga u tile sha.”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “Me kera za shin inya ga, m ngu shin inya, er i lu nahan.”

Tor gema kaa ér: “Nahan u tema inya.”

Hen ijiir ne, guinea-pig ugen la gba ember, nahan i cir un.

“Va, kwagh la kure kwagh u guinea-pig la!” Alice hen nahan. “Hegen yô, se hemba lun a mlu u dedoo.”

Or u eren ikyondo la kaa ér: “M hemba soon u man tii wam been,” maa a kenger Torkwase u lu ôron takerada u mbawan icam la sha ishimanyian.

Tor kaa ér: “U ngu a za,” nahan Or u eren ikyondo la due ken atejir la kera fese, a kegh ér una wa akondo a na je kpaa ga.

“—man dugh ityough nagh ken won tseegh,” Torkwase la seer ôron orvesen ugen ér: kpa Orvesen u Hatter la kera lu ken ishi ga cii ve orvesen la mase nyôron hen hunda ye.

“Yila orshiada u a dondo la!” Tor kaa nahan.

Orshiada u dondon yô, lu or u eren kwaghyan u Duchess la. Yange tôô pepper-box la sha uwe, nahan Alice fa or u nan lu la, cii ve mase nyôron ken koti je kpaa, sha gbenda u ior mba ve lu ikyua a hunda la hii u ôôn iyol kwa môm la.

Tor kaa ér: “Na ikyav you.”

Or u nan lu yan kwagh la kaa ér: “Shan’t.”

Tor kenger Ikyôhô i Puupuu la sha ishimanyian, nahan i kaa a na sha imo i doon ga ér, “Gba u Tor wou una pine shiada ne mbampin.”

“Een, aluer gba u me er nahan yô, gba u me er nahan,” Tor la kaa a ishimavihin, maa a kende ave sha shi a kenger or u nan lu yan kwagh la sha ishi zan zan ashe na kera lu nôngon u been yô, a kaa sha imo i taver ér, “Ka nyi i eren tart?”

Or u nan lu yan kwagh la kaa ér: “Pepper, hemban cii je yô.

Imo i mnyam ma ken ijime na kaa ér: “Treacle.”

Torkwase la kaa a mo ér: “Kola Dormouse la.” "Gber ityough ki Dormouse la! Gema Dormouse la ken koti kera! Kighir un! Pin un! Dugh un sha ityough nagh kera!"

Miniti mbagenev yô, koti la cii lu ken mzeyol, nahan ve lu duen a Dormouse, man shighe u ve hide ve tema inya la, or u nan lu yan kwagh la kera lu ga.

"De we ikyo ga!" Tor kaa nahan, a ishima i nan un mmem kpishi. “Yila orshiada u a dondo la.” Shi a seer ôron kwagh a Torkwase la sha imo i doon ga ér, "Sha mimi yô, dooshima wam, gba u ú pine orshiada u a dondo la mbampin. Kwagh ne na yô, ityough ki nyoon mo kpishi!"

Alice nenge a White Rabbit er a lu eren tom sha ikyav mbi i nger la, nahan sar un kpishi u fan er orshiada u una dondo la una lu yô, “—gadia ve mba a zua a shiada kpishi ga ,” a kaa ken ishima na ér. Nenge ase er yange kpiligh un iyol yô, shighe u White Rabbit ôr, sha imo na i kiriki i i lu ôron kwagh sha mciem la, iti i “Alice” la yô!
