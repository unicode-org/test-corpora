IKYAV I TESE 3.
Ior mba ken Caucus-Race man Kwaghôron u Vesen .

Sha mimi yô, ve lu nongo u ior mba lun a mlu u kpilighyol mba ve kohol hen kpezegemnger la—inyon lu a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i cir sha mi la, man inyamtoho i i cir ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i cir sha mi la, man ve cii ve lu ôôn mngerem, ve lu eren kwagh sha terankon, shi ve lu a mkom ga.

Mpin u hiihii u yange lu yô, lu er se hide se ôô iyol yô: yange ve lam sha kwagh ne, miniti kpuaa mba karen yô, lu inja er ka kwagh u shami je Alice una nenge ér un ngu lamen a ve sha inja nahan, inja er un fa ve ken uma na cii nahan. Sha kpôô yô, yange gba anyiman a Lory ica i gba, nahan ken masejime yô, Lory hingir u lun a ishimavihin, nahan a kaa ér, “M hemba u, nahan gba u me hemba fan kwagh;” man kwagh ne Alice yange una na un ian ga a u fan anyom a a lu ker la shio, man er Lory yange venda u pasen anyom a na sha mimi yô, kwagh ugen u ôron kera lu ga.

Ken masejime yô, Mouse u lu inja er ka or u lun a tahav hen atô ve la yila ér, “Tema nen inya, ne cii, ungwa nen kwagh wam, ica a gba ga tsô me na ne mngerem ma kuma ne!” Ve cii ve tema inya kwa môm, ken zege ikyônough ki torough, Mouse lu hen atô ve. Alice yange a ver ashe sha mi sha ishimanyian, sha ci u yange nenge ér aluer un hôngor fese ga yô, una gba angev mbu vihin tsung.

“Ahem!” Mouse la kaa a na sha ahumbe a hange hange, "ne cii ne wa iyol kpa? Ka kwagh u hemban vihin cii u m fe je ne. Aluer ne soo yô, lu nen viing viing cii! 'William u Orhembanato, u pope soo kwagh na la, ica i gbe ga yô, Mbangerenkwagh mba ken tar u Ingila, mba ve soo mbahemenev la, ve wa un sha ikyev, shi yange ve fa kwagh u mbahemenev mba ken tar u Ingila man Edcar man Morwin mba ken tar u Mercia, mba ve lu ngohol tar la sha ikyev ve la. Northumbria—’”

“Ugh!” Lory kaa nahan, a mciem iyol.

“M zamber we mer, de mo kwaghbo!” Mouse kaa nahan, a cir ishigh, kpa a ôr kwagh sha icivir kpishi ér: “U ôr kwagh kpa?”

“Ka mo ga!” Lory kaa nahan sha mciem.

Mouse kaa ér: “M hen mer u er nahan.” “—M za hemen mer, ‘Edwin man Morcar, mbahemenev mba ken tar u Mercia man Northumbria yange ve pase kwagh sha ci na: shi Stigand, u lu bisôp u vesen u ken gar u Canterbury, u soo tar na la je kpa nenge ér doo u a er nahan—’”

“Ka nyi ?” ka kwagh u Duck a kaa je la.

“Found it ,” Mouse gema kaa a na sha ishimavihin ér: “sha mimi yô, u fa inja i ‘it’ la.”

Duck kaa ér: “M fa inja i ‘kwagh’ la tsembelee, zum u m zough a kwagh yô, ka i lu ikyôhô shin ikyôhô. Mpin u a lu yô, ka nyi bisôp u tamen la yange zua a mi?”

Mouse yange nenge a mpin ne ga, kpa a za hemen fese, a kaa ér, “’—a nenge ér doo u una za vea Edgar Atheling una za kohol William una na un ikyondo i tor la. Ieren i William sha hiihii la lu i sha inja. Kpa mlu u eren kwagh sha inja ga u ior nav mba ken tar u Norman la—’ U ngu zan hemen nena hegen, or u doon mo ishima?” i za hemen, i gema i kenger Alice er i lu lamen la.

Alice kaa a mo sha imo i vihin tsung ér: “Er m lu a mngerem er ngise nahan, ka inja er kwagh ne ngu ôôn mo ga nahan.”

Dodo la kaa sha ishimavihin, mough sha angahar, kaa ér: “Aluer ka nahan yô, m ngu kaan mer i de mkombo la, sha er i lumun a igbenda i hemban lun a tahav fese yô—”

“Ôr zwa Buter!” ka kwagh u Eaglet a kaa la. “M fa inja i vegher u asember a doon tsung la ga, man kwagh u a hembe yô, m na jighjigh mer we kpa u fa ga!” Man Ikyôhô la gema ityough sha u yeren mciem: inyon igen igen lu ôron kwagh sha imo i taver.

Dodo la kaa a na sha imo i vihin tu ér: “Kwagh u m lu ôron yô, kwagh u hemban doon u una na se mngerem yô, ka ayem a Caucus-race.”

“Ka nyi i lu mzough u Caucus?” Alice kaa ér; ka sha ci u yange soo u fan akaa kpishi ga, kpa Dodo la yange mem inja er ngu henen ér gba u orgen nana ôr kwagh nahan, shi lu inja er orgen môm kpa nan soo u ôron kwagh ga nahan.

Dodo la kaa ér: “Er nan ve, gbenda u hemban doon u pasen kwagh ne yô, ka u eren kwagh shon.” (Man, er alaghga u soo u karen kwagh la iyol you nahan, iyange igen ken shighe u uraahumbe yô, me ôr u er Dodo yange nenge sha mi yô.)

Hiihii yô, i ver ikyav sha ijiir i gban ayem, ken inja i ikyav i i yer ér circle la, (“mlu u i gber jighilii la ka kwagh u vesen ga,” i kaa ér,) maa i ver pati la cii hen ijiir i gban ayem la, heen man heen. “Or môm, uhar, utar, man ica” lu ga, kpa ve hii u yevese shighe u i doo ve la, ve de shighe u i doo ve la, nahan lu ican u fan shighe u ayem la a bee ga. Kpa yange ve gba yevese kuma er vegher u ihwa i môm nahan, ve hide ve lu ôôn iyol kpishi yô, Dodo la yila fese ér “Ayem la bee!” ve cii ve kohol her, ve lu ôôn iyol, ve lu pinen ér, “Kpa ka an nan hembe?”

Mpin ne Dodo yange una na mlumun sha mi a u henen sha mi kpishi shio ga, nahan a tema shighe gôgônan a uwegh ku môm sha ityou (ka mlu u u vanden nengen a Shakespeare, ken ufoto nav la je la), mbagenev mbara di gema lu keghen ving. Ken masejime yô, Dodo la kaa ér, “ Hanma or nan hemba, man gba u hanma or nana lu a injar.”

“Kpa ka an nan a na injar laa?” ior kpishi pine nahan.

Dodo la tese Alice uwegh ku môm ér: “Er nan ve, un , ka mimi?” man iniongo la cii iv hen a na fese, ve lu yilan sha gbenda u doon ga ér, “Iyua!

Alice kera fa kwagh u una er ga, nahan sha ishimanyian na yô, a wa uwegh nagh ken akpa na, a dugh akwati u comfits, (kwagh u doon yô, mngerem ma salt ma nyôr ker ga), a na ve cir cir er ka injar nahan. Yange lu a-piece môm vough, u a lu ikyua ikyua la cii.

“Kpa gba u un iyol na una lu a injar, u fa,” Mouse kaa nahan.

Dodo la gema kaa ér: “Een, ka mimi.” “Ka nyi igen u lu a mi ken akpa wou?” a za hemen, a gema a kenger Alice.

Alice kaa a mo sha ishimavihin ér: “Ka thimble tseegh.

Dodo la kaa ér: “Na nen kwagh ne heen.”

Tsô ve cii ve hide ve kohol un kwa môm, shighe u Dodo lu nan un ikyagh ki tswamegh la sha icivir, kaa ér “Se mba zamber we ser u lumun ikyagh ki tswamegh ki doon tsung kin;” man, yange i ôr kwaghôron u kpuaa ne been yô, ve cii ve gba ôron kwagh sha iember.

Alice hen ér kwagh la cii ka kwagh u gbilinigh kpishi, kpa ve cii ve lu inja er kwagh vihi un kpishi je yô, a kera taver ishima u lamen ga; man er lu a hen kwagh u ôron ga yô, a gure inya tsô, a tôô ikyagh ki tswamegh kira, a nenge ér un ngu a icivir er una fetyô la cii.

Kwagh u dondon yô, lu u yan ikyav mbi i yer ér comfits la: kwagh ne na yô, ior lu eren ayôôso man mzeyol, sha ci u inyon i vesen la lu ôron ér ve kera fatyo u yan kwagh u ve ga, shi mba kiriki mbara kpa ve lu ôôn iyol, nahan gba u a gbidye ve sha ityou. Kpa, kwagh la bee ken masejime, ve hide ve tema ken ring, ve zamber Mouse ér a seer ôron ve kwagh.

Alice kaa ér: “U tôndo zwa wer u ôrom kwagh u yange er u la, u fa, man er i hii ve u kôr C man D ihyom yô,” a seer ôron kwagh sha zwa u hômon, a cian vegher ugen ér kwagh la una hide a vihi un ishima.

“Kwagh wam ka kwagh u doon tsung shi a vihi kpishi!” Mouse kaa nahan, a gema a kenger Alice, a ôr kwagh sha mciem.

Alice kenger ityough ki Mouse la sha mciem, a kaa ér: “ Ka ikyondo i doon tsung, sha mimi yô; “kpa er nan u yer kwagh ne wer ka kwagh u vihin?” Man a za hemen u henen sha kwagh ne shighe u Mouse lu lamen la, nahan mhen na sha kwagh u kwaghôron la lu kwagh môm a kwagh ne:—

“Ú ngu zan mkohol ne ga!” ka kwagh u Mouse a kaa a Alice sha ishimavihin je la. “Ka nyi u lu henen sha mi?”

Alice kaa a na sha iyol i hiden a mi ijime kpishi ér: “M zamber we mer, u za nyôr hen ijiir i i yer ér fifth bend la, m hen mer?”

“M lu a mi ga! ” Mouse la ôr kwagh sha ishimavihin man sha ishimavihin kpishi.

“Knot!” Alice kaa nahan, hanma shighe yô, lu keghen a kegh iyol u eren kwagh u una wase un yô, shi lu kenger un sha ishimanyian. “Oh, de m wase u sôron kwagh ne!”

Mouse kaa ér: “Me er kwagh u a lu nahan ga,” maa a mough a yem. “Ú tuhwam sha u ôron akaa a injaa ga nahan!”

“Yange m soo u eren nahan ga!” ka kwagh u Alice u lun icankwase la yange zamber je la. “Kpa u vihi ishima fese je, u fa!”

Mouse la yange gba ôron kwagh sha mlumun tseegh.

“M zamber we, hide va kure kwaghôron wou!” Alice yila un ken ijime; man mbagenev mbara cii ve kohol ve wa icam ér, “Een, m zamber we, er nahan!” kpa Mouse yô, a cir ityough tseegh sha ishimawan ga, a zende fese kpuaa.

“Ka kwagh u vihin kpishi er yange ma lu her ga yô!” Lory la ôr kwagh sha ishimavihin, er yange kera lu ken igbar ga yô; man Crab u beenyol ugen yange tôô ian la kaa a wan na u kwase ér “Ah, dooshima wam! De kwagh ne a lu u ityesen sha er mayange u de vihin ishima ga yô!” “Kôr zwa wou, Ma!” ka kwagh u wanye u kiriki Crab la yange ôr je la, a ôr kwagh sha mciem kpuaa. “U kuma u karen ishimawan i or u i yer ér oyster la!”

“M soo mer ma m lu a Dina wase heen, m fa mer m ngu a mi!” Alice kaa nahan genger genger, lu ôron kwagh a or môm jighilii ga. “Ica a gba ga tsô una hide a mi!”

“Man ka an nan lu Dina, aluer me taver ishima me pine mpin ne yô?” ka kwagh u Lory yange ôr je la.

Alice gema kaa a na sha gbashima, gadia hanma shighe yô, yange a kegh iyol u ôron kwagh u inyam na i ken ya la ér: "Dinah ka ikyôhô yase. Shi ka ikyôhô i kôron ikyôhô i u hen wer u hen ga yô! Man oh, m soo mer ma u nenge a na ken ijime i inyon! Er nan ve, una ya inyon i kiriki fese je!"

Kwaghôron ne yange na yô, ior mba ken pati la gba ôron kwagh sha gbenda u kpilighyol. Inyon igen i yem fese fese: Magpie u beenyol ugen hii u cir iyol na sha mkor kpishi, a kaa ér, “Sha mimi yô, alaghga m ngu hiden hen ya; ahumbe a tugh la kumam sha ityough yagh ga!” man inyam i i yer ér Canary la yila ônov mba i sha imo i tenger ér, “Va nen, mbadoon mo ishima, shighe kuma u ne cii ne lu sha gambe!” Sha atôakyaa kposo kposo yô, ve cii ve mough ve yem, ica i gbe ga yô, Alice mase lun tswen tswen.

“M soo mer yange ma m ter kwagh u Dina ga!” a kaa ken ishima na sha imo i vihin tsung. "Ka inja er or môm kpa nan soo un ga nahan, shin inya heen, man m na jighjigh mer ka ikyôhô i hemban doon cii sha tar! Oh, Dinah wam u doon shima! M ngu henen mer me kera nenge a we kpa!" Man heen yô, Alice u lun icankwase la hide hii u vaan, gadia yange un nenge ér un ngu tswen tswen kpishi shi ishima vihi un ker kpaa. Kpa shighe kar kpuaa yô, a hide a ungwa ior lu eren angahar kpuaa ken ica, nahan a kenger sha sha gbashima, vegher ugen lu veren ishima ér Mouse gema ishima, shi ngu hiden van u va been kwaghôron na.
