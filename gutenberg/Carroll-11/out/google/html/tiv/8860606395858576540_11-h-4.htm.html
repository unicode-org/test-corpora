IKYAV I TESE IV.
Rabbit la tindi a Bill u kiriki .

Lu Ikyôhô i Puupuu la, lu hiden yevese yevese hiden ijime, shi lu kenger sha ishimanyian er a lu yemen la, inja er kwagh vihi un nahan; man a ungwa i lu ôron kwagh ken ishima na ér “Duchess! Duchess! Oh angahar am a doon mo ishima! Oh ikyondo yam i m lu a mi la man mshiam am! Una wua mo, vough er ikyôhô i lu ikyôhô nahan! Ka han yange ma m haa ve, m ngu henen mer?” Alice hen ken anshighe kpuaa ér ka fan la man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi pupuu mbi i yer ér white kid gloves la, nahan a hii u keren mbi sha inja, kpa ve lu hen ijiir i môm ga—hanma kwagh cii lu inja er yange gema hii shighe u a ôô iyol ken ityungu la je, shi iyou i vesen i i lu a tebul u gilga man hunda u cuku u i cir sha mi la kpa gema.

Ica i gbe ga yô, Rabi nenge a Alice, er a lu zenden keren inyam yô, maa a yila un sha imo i vihin tsung ér, "Er nan, Mary Ann, ka nyi u lu eren ken won heen? Yevese yem ken ya hen shighe ne, za va a mo ikyav mbi eren tom a mi man fan! Fese, hegen!" Shi mciem gba Alice iyol kpishi je yô, a yevese a yem sha gbenda u i tese un la fese, a nôngo ér una pase un kwaghbo u i er la ga.

Er lu yevese la, a kaa ken ishima na ér: “A tôôm ér m lu kwase u eren tom hen ya na.” “A kpiligh un iyol kpishi shighe u una fa or u m lu la! Kpa hemba doon u me tôô fan na man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi nan la—inja na yô, aluer m zua a mbi yô.” Er lu ôron kwagh ne yô, a za zua a iyou i kiriki igen i i lu tsembelee yô, i nger iti i “W. RABBIT” sha mi sha hunda u iyou la. A nyôr a u gbidyen hunda shio, maa a mough sha iyou la fese, sha ci u mciem kpishi ér alaghga una zua a Mary Ann u mimi la, nahan a due a na ken iyou la kera cii ve una mase zuan a fan man ikyav mbi eren tom a mi la ye.

Alice kaa ken ishima na ér: “Ka kwagh u kpilighyol kpishi u zan tindin mo loho sha ci u ikyôhô! M hen mer Dinah una tindim a loho ken hemen!” Man a hii u henen sha kwagh u una er la, a kaa ér: “‘Miss Alice! Va heen jighilii, va wa iyol sha zende wou!’ ‘M ngu van ken miniti môm, nurse! Kpa gba u me nenge mer mouse la ngu duen ga.’ Ka mo tseegh m hen mer,” Alice za hemen kaa ér, “vea de ér Dinah a tile ken iyou la aluer i hii u wan ior kwagh sha kwagh nahan ga yô!”

Hen shighe ne a zua a gbenda u nyôron ken iyou i kiriki i i lu wang, i lu a tebul sha windoo yô, man sha mi (er yange lu veren ishima nahan) lu a fan man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kiriki mbi pupuu mbi uhar shin utar: a tôô fan la man ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kiriki mbi uhar, lu nôngon u duen ken iyou la kera, maa ishigh nagh gba sha ikyagh ki ki lu ikyua a ikyagh ki nengen a mi la. Kwa ne yô, i nger kwagh sha mi ér “MAN MO” sha mi ga, kpa nahan cii kpa, a bugh ikyav la, a wa sha zwa na. A kaa ken ishima na ér: “M fa kwagh u doon tsung una er, hanma shighe u m ya kwagh shin m ma kwagh cii; nahan me nenge kwagh u ikyagh kin ki eren la tseegh. M ngu a ishimaveren mer kia hide me vese, gadia sha mimi yô, m vôr kpishi u lun kwagh u kiriki nahan!”

Yange er nahan sha mimi, man fese je hemba er yange hen la: cii man a ma vegher u ikyagh kira yô, a nenge ityough nagh ki cir sha kpekpe, nahan gba u una gure inya sha er una yima ityough nagh sha er ki vihi ga yô. A ver ikyagh kira fese, a kaa ken ishima na ér “Kwagh la kuma vough—m ngu a ishimaveren mer me kera vese ga—Er i lu nahan yô, me kera due sha hunda ga—m soo mer ma m ma msôrom kpishi ga!”

Kpa! yange lu u una soo kwagh la ga! A za hemen u vesen, shi vesen, ica i gbe ga yô, gba u una gure inya: ken miniti ugen la, ian i eren kwagh ne je kpa kera lu ga, nahan a nôngo kwagh u una er yô, a gba yaven a uwegh ku môm sha hunda, uwegh kugen ku cir sha ityough nagh. Nahan cii kpa a za hemen u vesen, man, er ka kwagh u masetyô u una wase un yô, a due a uwegh ku môm sha windoo, man angahar a na a môm sha ityough ki mngerem ma eren usu la, maa a kaa ken ishima na ér “Hegen yô, m kera ngu a ian i eren kwagh ga, nyi je ia er kpa, ka nyi ia er mo?”

Kwagh u dedoo u Alice er yô, ikyagh ki eren ahir ki ki lu a ikyav mbi tswamev la ki lu a tahav mbu eren tom sha mi vough vough hegen, nahan a kera vese ga: nahan kpa, lu un kwagh u doon ga kpishi, man er lu inja er ma ian i una hide a due ken iyou la kera ngi ga yô, ka nahan ve yange saan un iyol ga ye.

"Yange i hemba doon mo hen ya," Alice u lun icankwase la hen nahan, "shighe u or nan lu vesen shi nan lu kiriki hanma shighe ga, shi i lu eren tom a mi sha ikyev i ikyav mbi tswamev man ikyav mbi tswamev la. yange m ôron akaa a ior ve ôron sha kwagh u mbayiase yô, m hen mer imba kwagh la yange i er ga, man hegen yô, m ngu hen atô u takerada ugen heen, u i gbe u a nger sha kwagh wam yô! “at least ijiir i vesen kera ngi heen ga .”

Alice hen ken ishima na ér: “Kpa nahan yô, mayange me kera bee iyol er m lu hegen ne ga he? Kwagh la una lu ishimasurun, gbenda môm—mayange me kera lu kwase u beenyol ga—kpa hen shighe la—hanma shighe yô, me lu a akaa a me hen yô! Oh, doo u kwagh la una doom ga! ”

“Oh, we Alice u ibumeor ne!” a na mlumun sha mi iyol na. “Ú er nan ve ú hen akaa ken heen?

Nahan a za hemen, a hii tôôn vegher ugen, maa a tôô vegher ugen la, a ôr kwagh sha mi kpishi; kpa miniti kpuaa mba karen yô, a ungwa imo ken won, nahan a tile sha u ungwan.

“Maria Ann! Maria Ann!” imo la kaa nahan. “Va a gloves av hen shighe ne!” Tsô ior gba eren angahar kpuaa sha ityough ki ikyônough ki torough kira. Alice fa er ka Rabi u a lu van u va keren un la, nahan a tenger zan zan a cir iyou la, a hungur un je er hegen un hemba Rabi kwa dubu nahan, nahan ityôkyaa ngi i una cian un ga.

Hen shighe ne, Rabi va sha hunda, nôngo ér una bugh u; kpa er hunda la bugh ken atô, shi i kende uwegh ku Alice sha mi kpoghuloo yô, iniôngon la tese ér kwagh er un ga. Alice ungwa i kaa a iyol i i ér “Nahan me za cir me nyôr sha windoo.”

“ Ka u ú er nahan ga!” Alice hen nahan, maa a kegh zan zan a nenge ér un ungwa Rabi la shin inya hen windoo la, maa a naregh uwegh nagh fese, a er kwagh u eren ikyav sha ahumbe. Yange kôr kwagh ga, kpa a ungwa ikyenge kpuaa man m-ban, man m-ban u gilasi u a vihi yô, nahan a kure ikyaa ér alaghga tsô yange gba ken ikyagh ki cucumber, shin kwagh ugen u a lu nahan yô.

Kwagh u dondon yô, imo i vihin tu—i Rabi—“Pat! Pat! U ngu hana?” Man imo igen i yange lu a vande ungwan ga yô, kaa ér, “Sure nahan m ngu heen! M ngu timen apel, yer honour!”

“Ka u keren apel, mimi je!” ka kwagh u Rabi a kaa la sha ishimavihin. “Heen! Va wasem u duen ken kwagh ne kera! ” (Imo i gilasi u a hembe vihin yô.)

“Hegen yô, ôrom, Pat, ka nyi i lu sha windoo laa?”

“Sha mimi yô, ka uwegh, yer icivir!” (Yange yila iti ne ér “arrum.”)

"Ikyev, we ikyôhô! Ka an nan vande nengen a i i vesen nahan? Ka nyi, i iv sha windoo cii!"

“Sha mimi yô, ka nahan, yer honour: kpa ka uwegh sha akaa la cii.”

“Een, kwagh gba sha mi hen ijiir la ga, sha hanma gbenda cii: za tôô un yem a mi!”

Kwagh ne been kera yô, ior lu viing viing ica i gba, nahan Alice lu ungwan akaa a ior ve lu ôron sha zwa u hôngorough tseegh hegen man ken hemen; er, “Sha mimi yô, m soo kwagh ne ga, yer honour, cii, cii!” “Er kwagh u m kaa a we ne, we or u cian kwagh!” ken masejime yô, a hide a naregh uwegh nagh, a er ikyav igen sha ahumbe. Kwa ne yô, ior lu ungwan ityogholough mbi kiriki mbihar , shi ior lu ungwan ityogholough mbi gilasi mbi i vihi la kpaa. “Ka iyenge i cucumber i i gber la je ne!” Alice hen nahan. "M ngu henen mer ka nyi vea er ken hemen! Sha kwagh u duen a mo sha windoo yô, m soo mer ma ve dugh mo! M fa je mer m kera soo u lun ken iyou ne ga!"

A kegh shighe kar kpuaa a kera ungwa kwagh ugen ga: ken masejime yô, i ungwa ikyenge i ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kiriki mbi i yer ér cartwheels la, shi i ungwa ikyenge i ior kpishi mba ve lu lamen imôngo la cii: a ungwa mkaanem man ér: “Ka han ikyagh ki tswamev kigen ki lu?—Er nan ve, yange gba u me va a mi ga, saa di môm tseegh; corner—Ei, vande kangen ve imôngo—ve za ar sha vegher u sha ityoughkitaregh la ga—Oh! (a ôr kwagh genger genger)—“Hegen yô, ka an nan er kwagh shon?—Ka Bill, m hen mer—Ka an nana gba shin ikyônough ki torough kira?—Ei, me er nahan ga! U er nahan!— Nahan yô , me er nahan ga!—Bill una za shin inya—Hegen, Bill! Orvesen la kaa ér u za nyôr ken ikyônough ki torough kira!”

"Oh! Nahan gba u Bill una va shin ikyônough ki torough kira, shin ka nahan ga?" Alice kaa ken ishima na ér. “Kunya kôron, ka inja er ve wa hanma kwagh sha ikyev i Bill nahan! M lu sha ityough ki Bill sha ci u kwagh u dedoo ga: ijiir i usu ne ngi kpuaa, u ôron kwagh sha mimi yô; kpa m hen mer me kôr cio u gbidyen bol kpuaa!”

A kende angahar a na sha ityough ki ikyônough ki torough kira er una fetyô la cii, a kegh zan zan a ungwa inyam i kiriki (i fa inja i inyamtoho la ga) i lu eren ikyenge shi i lu eren ikyenge ken ikyônough ki torough ki ki lu ikyua a na la: maa, a kaa ken ishima na ér “Ka Bill je ne,” a gbidye un sha ityou môm, maa a kegh ér una nenge kwagh u una dondo yô.

Kwagh u hiihii u yange ungwa yô, lu icam i ior kpishi ve ungwa ér “There goes Bill!” nahan imo i Rabi la gba duen—“Kôr un, we u u lu sha kpekpe la!” maa i lu viing viing, maa i ungwa imo igen i i lu eren ayôôso yô—“Kôr ityough nagh sha—Brandy hegen—De eren un kwagh ga—Yange lu nena, orbeenyol? Ka nyi yange i er u? Ôr se kwagh u kwagh ne cii!”

Kwagh u masetyô yô, imo i kpuaa i i lu a agee ga, i lu ôron kwagh sha mciem yô, (“Ka Bill je la,” Alice hen nahan,) “Een, m fa ga—M kera fa ga, m sugh u; m hemba doon hegen—kpa m ngu a mciem kpishi u ôron we—kwagh u m fe yô, kwagh va sha a mo er ka Jack-in-the-boxes er ka skyro nahan, man m mough m yem sha!”

“Ka nahan ve u er ye, orbeenyol!” mbagenev mbara kaa nahan.

“Gba u se nande iyou la kera!” ka imo i Rabi je la; man Alice yila sha imo i taver er una fetyô la cii ér, “Aluer u er nahan yô, me ver Dinah sha a we!”

Ior cii lu viing viing fese je, nahan Alice hen ken ishima na ér, “M ngu henen mer ka nyi vea er ken hemene! Aluer ve lu a kwaghfan yô, ma ve dugh iyou i sha ityough ki iyou la kera.” Miniti môm shin uhar mba karen yô, ve hide ve hii u zenden, nahan Alice ungwa Rabi kaa ér, “Barrowful una er, u hiin je.”

Alice hen ér: “ Ka nyi i lu bar? kpa yange lu a akperan ica i gba ga, gadia hen shighe u dondon la, iwen i kiriki igen va nyôr sha windoo, igen ken iwen la gba un sha ishi. A kaa ken ishima na ér: “Me de kwagh ne,” maa a ôr genger genger ér, “Hemba doon u u kera er nahan ga!” kwagh ne na yô, ior hingir u lun viing viing ugen.

Alice nenge a kwagh u kpiligh un iyol er awen a kiriki la cii lu geman hingir ikyav mbi kiriki er a lu cir shin inya la, nahan mhen u wanger wanger va un ken ityou. A hen ken ishima na ér: “Aluer m ya môm ken akeke ne yô, una gema mlu wam, man er una na m hemba vesen ga yô, m hen mer una na m hemba lun cuku.”

Nahan a mena môm ken akaa a i yer ér cake la, nahan saan un iyol kpishi er un hii u eren msen jighilii yô. Er yange lu cuku u nyôron sha hunda la je, a yevese a due ken iyou la kera, a nenge a annyamev mba kiriki man inyon kpishi lu keghen ken won. Wan u Lizard u kiriki u lu icankwase, u i yilan un ér Bill la, lu hen atô, iyôngo i i yer ér guinea-pig la ihiar lu kôron un sha, ve lu nan un kwagh ken ikyagh ki esen kwagh. Ve cii ve gba eren Alice kwagh sha shighe u a due la; kpa a yevese a yem sha agee a na cii, ica i gbe ga yô, a zua a mkor ken kon u u lu a mngerem kpishi yô.

"Kwagh u hiihii u i gbe u me er yo," Alice kaa ken ishima na, er a lu zenden ken toho la, "ka u me hide me vese me kuma sha mlu wam u vough la; man kwagh u sha uhar yo ka u keren gbenda wam u nyôron ken sule u doon tsung la. M hen mer ka kwagh u hemban doon je la."

Yange lu inja er ka awashima u injaa tsung nahan, akperan nga ga, shi i sôr un sha inja shi i lu kwagh u kiriki kpaa; kwagh u yange lu un ican yô, lu er lu a mhen u cuku u una er ve una hii u eren kwagh ne ga yô; man er lu kenger sha ajiir kposo kposo ken atô u ikyon la sha ishimanyian yô, ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kiriki mbi mbi lu sha ityough nagh kpuaa la na yô, a kenger sha fese je.

Zege wan u inyamtoho ugen lu kenger un shin inya sha ashe a vesen a cir, shi lu nanden uwegh ku môm sha iyolbeen, nôngon ér una bende un ave. “Kwagh u kiriki u lun ican!” Alice kaa nahan sha imo i taver, nahan a nôngo kpoghuloo ér una ôr kwagh sha mi; kpa mciem gba un iyol kpishi hanma shighe cii sha mhen u alaghga ijen ia kôr un la, nahan aluer ka nahan yô, alaghga una ya un shin er lu tuur un nahan kpaa.

Er fa kwagh u yange er ga yô, a tôô ikyagh ki esen kwagh kpuaa, a na wan u ikyôhô la; nahan wan u ikyôhô la gba yevese sha ahumbe sha angahar a na cii kwa môm, a ôr kwagh sha iember, maa a gba yevese sha ikyônough kira, a na jighjigh ér kwagh gba un sha mi; nahan Alice yevese yevese ken ijime i zege kon ugen, sha er or nana gba yevese sha a na ga yô; man hen shighe u a due sha vegher ugen la, wan u ikyôhô la hide gba yevese sha ikyônough kira, maa gba ityough sha ashe sha u nôngon ér una kôr ki; nahan Alice hen ér kwagh ne ngu er ka anumbe a eren a nyinya i i yer ér cart-horse la nahan, shi lu veren ishima ér hanma shighe cii a gbidye un sha angahar a na, nahan a hide a gba yevese a kase ikyon i i yer ér thistle la; nahan wan u ikyôhô la hii u eren ikyav mbi tswamev mbi kpuaa sha ikyônough kira, lu yevese yemen ken hemen kpuaa hanma shighe shi hiden ijime ica, shi lu ôron kwagh sha imo i taver shighe la cii, zan zan ken masejime yô, a tema ica a na, a lu ôôn iyol, a nombor na u lu duen ken zwa na kera, shi ashe a na a vesen la kpa a lu cir avegher ahar.

Kwagh ne lu Alice inja er ka ian i dedoo i una er ve una yevese nahan; nahan a mough fese, a yevese zan zan a vôr kpishi shi a kera lu a ian i ôôn iyol ga, shi ikyenge i wan u inyamtoho la kpa i ungwa ken ica.

“Man nahan kpa, yange lu wanye u kiriki u doon tsung!” Alice kaa nahan, er a har iyol na sha kon u buttercup sha u memen iyol na yô, shi a lu ôôn iyol na sha ikyav i môm ken ikyon la, a kaa ér: “Yange ma m soo u tesen un akaa kpishi, aluer—luun er yange ma m lu a mzehemen u vough u eren kwagh ne tseegh yô! yan kwagh shin man kwagh shin kwagh ugen kpa mpin u vesen yô, ka nyi?”

Mpin u vesen u yange lu yô, lu nyi? Alice kenger sha ajiir a a lu la cii, a nenge a ikya i i lu sha mi la man ikya i i lu sha mi la, kpa a nenge a kwagh u a lu er ka kwagh u vough u yan shin man sha mlu u kwagh a lu la ga. Zege ikyon igen lu vesen ikyua a na, i lu er un nahan; man yange una kenger shin inya, man sha avegher a na ahar cii, man ken ijime na yô, a hen ér una kenger una nenge kwagh u a lu sha mi la kpaa.

A kende iyol na sha sha ityough ki angahar, a kenger sha ityough ki mngerem mara, maa fese je ashe na zua a zege ikyôhô i i yer ér blue caterpillar, i i tema sha ityough ki mngerem mara, i cir ave, i lu man mcimbim ma bom ma i yer ér hookah la, shi i lu nengen a na shin kwagh ugen môm kpaa ga yô.
