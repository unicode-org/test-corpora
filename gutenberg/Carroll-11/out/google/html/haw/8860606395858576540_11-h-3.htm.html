MOKUNA III.
He Lahui Kakau a me ka Moolelo loihi

He poʻe ʻē aʻe lākou e ʻākoakoa ana ma kapa—ʻo nā manu me nā hulu ʻauo, nā holoholona me ko lākou hulu e pili kokoke ana iā lākou, a me nā mea a pau e kulu ana i ka pulu, ke keʻa, a me ka ʻoluʻolu ʻole.

ʻO ka nīnau mua ʻoiaʻiʻo, pehea e maloʻo hou ai: ua kūkākūkā lāua no kēia, a ma hope o kekahi mau minuke he mea maʻamau iā Alice ke kamaʻilio kamaʻilio me lākou, me he mea lā ua ʻike ʻo ia iā lākou i kona ola a pau. ʻOiaʻiʻo, ua lōʻihi kona paio ʻana me ka Lory, ʻo ia hoʻi ka mea i huli ʻino ʻia, a ʻōlelo wale ʻo ia, "Ua ʻoi aku wau ma mua o ʻoe, a pono e ʻike maikaʻi;" a ʻaʻole ʻae kēia Alice me ka ʻike ʻole i kona mau makahiki, a ʻoiai ua hōʻole ʻo Lory i ka haʻi ʻana i kona mau makahiki, ʻaʻohe mea e ʻōlelo ʻia.

Kahea mai la ka Iole, me he mea la he kanaka mana iwaena o lakou, "E noho oukou a pau, a e hoolohe mai ia'u! Noho ihola lākou a pau i lalo, i loko o ke apo nui, a ʻo ka Iole ma waena. Ua nānā ʻo Alice i kona mau maka me ka hopohopo, no ka mea, ua manaʻo ʻo ia e loaʻa ana iā ia ke anu ʻino inā ʻaʻole e maloʻo koke.

“Ahem!” wahi a ka Iole me ka ea nui, "Ua makaukau anei oukou a pau? O keia ka mea maloo loa a'u i ike ai. E hamau oukou a puni, ina oukou e makemake! 'O William ka Na'i Aupuni, nona ka aoao i kokuaia e ka pope, ua waiho koke ia mai e ka Enelani, ka poe i makemake i na alakai, a ua maa loa lakou i ka lawe pio a me ka lanakila. Edwin a me Morcar, na pepeiao o Mercia Akau—"

“Uh!” wahi a ka Lory me ka haalulu.

"E kala mai iaʻu!" wahi a ka Iole me ka minoʻaka, akā, me ke akahai loa: “Ua ʻōlelo anei ʻoe?”

"ʻAʻole wau!" wahi a ka Lory me ka wikiwiki.

"Ua manaʻo wau ua hana ʻoe," wahi a ka Iole. "—Ke hoomau aku nei au. 'O Edwin a me Morcar, na pepeiao o Mercia a me Northumbria, i hai aku nona: a o Stigand, ka Akibihopa aloha aina o Canterbury, ua manao ia he mea pono—'"

"Ua loaʻa i ke aha ?" wahi a ka Duck.

"Ua loaʻa ," wahi a ka ʻiole i pane mai ai: "ʻoiaʻiʻo ua ʻike ʻoe i ke ʻano o ka 'mea'."

"Ua maopopo ia'u ke ano o ia mea, ke loaa ia'u kekahi mea," wahi a ka Duck: "he rana a ilo paha ka ninau, heaha ka mea i loaa i ka Akibihopa?"

ʻAʻole i ʻike ka ʻiole i kēia nīnau, akā, ua hoʻomau ʻo ia me ka wikiwiki, "'—ua manaʻo ʻia e hele pū me Edgar Atheling e hālāwai me William a hāʻawi iā ia i ka lei aliʻi. Ua haʻahaʻa ka hana a William i kinohi. Akā, ʻo ka haʻahaʻa o kāna mau Noremana—' Pehea ʻoe e hele nei i kēia manawa, e kuʻu aloha?" hoomau aku la ia me ka huli ana ia Alice i kana olelo ana.

"E like me ka pulu e like me ka wā ma mua," wahi a Alice me ka leo melancholy: "ʻaʻole ia e maloʻo iki iaʻu."

"Ina pela," wahi a ka Dodo me ke ko'iko'i, me ke ku ana mai iluna o kona mau wawae, "ke manao nei au e hoopanee ka halawai, no ka lawe koke ia aku i na laau lapaau ikaika—"

“E ʻōlelo Pelekania!” wahi a ka Eaglet. "ʻAʻole maopopo iaʻu ke ʻano o ka hapalua o kēlā mau huaʻōlelo lōʻihi, a ʻo ka mea hou aku, ʻaʻole wau manaʻoʻiʻo ʻoe e hana nei!" A kulou iho la ka Eaglet i kona poo e huna i ka minoaka: kani mai kekahi o na manu.

"ʻO kaʻu mea e ʻōlelo ai," wahi a ka Dodo me ka leo huhū, "ʻo ka mea maikaʻi loa e maloʻo ai mākou, ʻo ia ka heihei Caucus."

“He aha ka lāhui Caucus?” wahi a Alice; ʻaʻole naʻe ʻo ia i makemake nui e ʻike, akā ua hoʻomaha ka Dodo me he mea lā ua manaʻo ʻo ia he pono e ʻōlelo kekahi , ʻaʻohe mea ʻē aʻe i makemake e ʻōlelo i kekahi mea.

"No ke aha," wahi a ka Dodo, "ʻo ke ala maikaʻi loa e wehewehe ai ʻo ia ke hana." (A, no ka mea, makemake ʻoe e hoʻāʻo iā ʻoe iho, i kekahi lā hoʻoilo, e haʻi wau iā ʻoe pehea i mālama ai ka Dodo.)

Ua kaha mua ia i ka papa heihei, ma ke ano poai, ("'a'ole pili ke kinona," wahi a ia,) a laila ua hoonoho ia na mea a pau ma ke alahele, ma o a maanei. ʻAʻohe "Hoʻokahi, ʻelua, ʻekolu, a ʻokoʻa," akā ua hoʻomaka lākou e holo i ka wā e makemake ai, a haʻalele i ka wā makemake, i ʻole e maʻalahi ka ʻike i ka pau ʻana o ka heihei. Eia naʻe, i ko lākou holo ʻana he hapalua hola a ʻoi paha, a maloʻo hou, kāhea koke ka Dodo, "Ua pau ka heihei!" Hoʻākoakoa aʻela lākou a pau ma laila, me ka ʻuwau ʻana, me ka nīnau ʻana, "ʻO wai lā ka mea i lanakila?"

ʻAʻole hiki i ka Dodo ke pane aku me ka noʻonoʻo nui ʻole, a ua noho lōʻihi me ka paʻi ʻana o ka manamana lima ma kona lae (ke kūlana āu e ʻike mau ai iā Shakespeare, ma nā kiʻi ona), a ʻo ke koena e kali me ka hāmau. I ka hope, ʻōlelo ka Dodo, "Ua lanakila nā kānaka a pau , a pono e loaʻa nā makana i nā mea a pau."

"Akā ʻo wai ka mea e hāʻawi i nā makana?" i nīnau ʻia e kahi hui o nā leo.

"No ke aha, ʻo ia , ʻoiaʻiʻo," wahi a ka Dodo, me ke kuhikuhi ʻana iā Alice me ka manamana lima; a ʻo ka ʻahaʻaina holoʻokoʻa a puni iā ia, e kāhea ana me ke ʻano huikau, "Nā makana! Nā makana!"

ʻAʻohe o Alice i manaʻo i kāna mea e hana ai, a me ka manaʻo kaumaha, hoʻokomo ʻo ia i kona lima i loko o kāna ʻeke, a huki maila i kahi pahu pahu pahu, (ʻo ka laki ʻaʻole i komo ka wai paʻakai i loko), a hāʻawi ʻia i nā makana. Hoʻokahi wale nō ʻāpana a puni.

"Akā, pono iā ia ka makana, ʻike ʻoe," wahi a ka Iole.

"ʻOiaʻiʻo," i pane aku ai ka Dodo me ke koʻikoʻi loa. "He aha hou kāu i loko o kāu ʻeke?" hele aku ʻo ia me ka huli ʻana iā Alice.

"He thimble wale nō," wahi a Alice me ke kaumaha.

"E hāʻawi mai ma ʻaneʻi," wahi a ka Dodo.

A laila, hoʻākoakoa hou aʻela lākou a puni iā ia, ʻoiai ua hāʻawi ʻo Dodo i ka ʻuala, me ka ʻōlelo ʻana, "Ke noi aku nei mākou iā ʻoe e ʻae mai i kēia manamana nani;" a i ka pau ana o keia haiolelo pokole, hauoli iho la lakou a pau.

Ua manaʻo ʻo Alice he mea lapuwale kēia mea a pau, akā ua ʻano koʻikoʻi lākou a pau ʻaʻole ʻo ia i ʻaʻa e ʻakaʻaka; a no ka hiki ole iaia ke noonoo i kekahi mea e olelo aku ai, kulou iho la oia, a lalau aku la i ka thimble, me ka nana aku i kona ano koikoi.

ʻO ka mea hou aʻe, ʻo ia ka ʻai ʻana i nā mea ʻai: ʻo ia ka mea i ulu ai ka walaʻau a me ka huikau, no ka mea, ua ʻōhumu nā manu nunui ʻaʻole hiki iā lākou ke ʻono i kā lākou, a ʻai nā manu liʻiliʻi a pono e paʻi ʻia ke kua. Eia naʻe, ua pau kēia, a noho hou lākou i lalo i ke apo, a noi aku i ka Iole e haʻi hou aku iā lākou.

"Ua hoʻohiki ʻoe e haʻi mai iaʻu i kāu moʻolelo, ʻike ʻoe," wahi a Alice, "a no ke aha ʻoe e inaina ai - C a me D," i hoʻohui ai ʻo ia me ka hāwanawana ʻana me ka hapalua o ka hopohopo e hōʻeha hou ʻia.

“He moʻolelo lōʻihi a kaumaha kaʻu!” wahi a ka Iole, huli ae la oia ia Alice, me ke kaniuhu.

" He huelo lōʻihi ia, ʻoiaʻiʻo," wahi a Alice me ka nānā ʻana i lalo me ke kahaha i ka huelo o ka Iole; "Akā, no ke aha ʻoe i kapa ai he kaumaha?" A hoʻomau ihola ʻo ia i ka hoʻopaʻapaʻa no ia mea i ka wā e kamaʻilio ana ka Iole, no laila ua like kona manaʻo no ka moʻolelo:—

"ʻAʻole ʻoe e hele ana!" wahi a ka Iole iā Alice me ke koʻikoʻi. "He aha kāu e noʻonoʻo nei?"

"E kala mai au," wahi a Alice me ka haʻahaʻa loa: "ua hiki ʻoe i ka piko ʻelima, ke manaʻo nei au?"

" ʻAʻole wau! " i hoʻōho ai ka Iole me ka ʻoi loa a me ka huhū loa.

“He puʻupuʻu!” wahi a Alice, me ka mākaukau mau e hoʻohana pono iā ia iho, a me ka nānā ʻana me ka hopohopo nona. "ʻAe, e ʻae mai iaʻu e kōkua i ka wehe ʻana!"

"Aole au e hana i keia ano," wahi a ka Iole, ku ae la a hele aku la. "Ua hoʻomāinoino ʻoe iaʻu ma ke kamaʻilio ʻana i kēlā ʻano mea ʻole!"

"ʻAʻole wau i manaʻo!" i noi aku ai ʻo Alice ʻilihune. "Akā, ua maʻalahi ʻoe i ka huhū, ʻike ʻoe!"

Uwe wale ka Iole i ka pane.

"E ʻoluʻolu e hoʻi mai a hoʻopau i kāu moʻolelo!" Kāhea ʻo Alice ma hope; a hui pū nā poʻe ʻē aʻe i ka mele, "ʻAe, e ʻoluʻolu e hana!" aka, luliluli wale iho la ka Iole i kona poo me ka hoomanawanui, a me ka wikiwiki iki.

“Aloha ka noho ole ana!” uwo ae la ka Lory, i ka wa i ike ole ia aku ai; a ʻo kekahi Paʻi kahiko i lawe i ka manawa kūpono e ʻōlelo aku ai i kāna kaikamahine "Auwe, e kuʻu mea aloha! E lilo kēia i haʻawina iā ʻoe ʻaʻole e pau ka huhū !" "Paʻa i kou alelo, e Ma!" wahi a ka pāpaʻi ʻōpio, me ka ʻuʻuku iki. “Ua lawa ʻoe e hoʻāʻo i ka hoʻomanawanui o ka ʻoo!”

"Makemake wau e loaʻa iaʻu kā mākou Dinah ma ʻaneʻi, ʻike wau e hana wau!" wahi a Alice me ka leo nui, ʻaʻole i kamaʻilio pū kekahi. “E hoʻihoʻi koke mai ʻo ia!”

"A ʻo wai ʻo Dina, inā hiki iaʻu ke aʻo e nīnau i ka nīnau?" wahi a ka Lory.

Pane akula ʻo Alice me ka ʻiʻini, no ka mea, ua mākaukau mau ʻo ia e kamaʻilio e pili ana i kāna holoholona: "ʻO Dinah kā kāua popoki.

ʻO kēia haʻiʻōlelo i hoʻokumu i ka manaʻo kupaianaha i waena o ka pāʻina. Holo koke akula kekahi o nā manu: ua hoʻomaka kekahi ʻelemakule Magpie e uhi pono iā ia iho, me ka ʻōlelo ʻana, "E hoʻi maoli ana au i ka hale, ʻaʻole kūpono ka ea pō i koʻu puʻu!" a kahea mai la kekahi Kanari me ka leo haalulu i kana mau keiki, "E hele mai e na makamaka! No nā kumu like ʻole ua neʻe lākou a pau, a ua haʻalele koke ʻia ʻo Alice.

“Aole au i hai aku ia Dina!” i ʻōlelo ʻo ia iā ia iho me ka leo melancholy. "ʻAʻohe mea makemake iā ia, ma ʻaneʻi, a maopopo iaʻu ʻo ia ka pōpoki maikaʻi loa o ka honua! Auē, e kuʻu Dinah! Manaʻo wau e ʻike hou aku au iā ʻoe!" A ma ʻaneʻi ua hoʻomaka hou ʻo Alice ʻilihune e uē, no ka mea, ua haʻahaʻa loa ʻo ia. Iloko nae o ka manawa pokole, lohe hou aku la oia i ka pa ana o na kapuai wawae ma kahi mamao, a nana ae la ia me ka manao nui, me ka hapalua o ka manao ua hoololi ae ka Iole i kona manao, a e hoi mai ana e hoopau i kana moolelo.
