MOKUNA VIII.
Ka Papa Kuikele a ka Moiwahine

He kumulāʻau rose nui e kū ana ma kahi kokoke i ka puka o ka māla: he keʻokeʻo nā roses e ulu ana ma luna, akā aia ʻekolu mau māla ma laila, e paʻa ana i ka pena ʻulaʻula. Ua manaʻo ʻo Alice he mea hoihoi loa kēia, a hoʻokokoke ʻo ia e nānā iā lākou, a i kona hiki ʻana mai i o lākou lā, lohe akula ʻo ia i kekahi o lākou e ʻōlelo ana, "E nānā ʻoe i kēia manawa, ʻelima!

"ʻAʻole hiki iaʻu ke pale aku," wahi a Five, me ka leo ʻino; "Ua holo ʻo ʻehiku i kuʻu kuʻekuʻe."

Nānā aʻela ʻo Seven a ʻōlelo aʻela, "Poloʻo ia, ʻElima! E kau mau i ka hewa ma luna o kekahi!"

“ E aho ʻaʻole ʻoe e kamaʻilio!” wahi a Elima. "Ua lohe au i ka ʻōlelo a ka Mōʻīwahine i nehinei ua pono ʻoe e ʻoki ʻia ke poʻo!"

“No ke aha?” wahi a ka mea i olelo mua.

"ʻAʻole kāu hana, ʻelua!" wahi a ʻEhiku.

"ʻAe, ʻo kāna ʻoihana!" wahi a Elima, "a e hai aku au iaia—no ka lawe ana mai i ka aa tulip ma kahi o ka onioni."

Ua hoolei iho la o Seven i kona pulupulu, a hoomaka iho la ia "Ae, o na mea pono ole a pau—" i ka manawa i haule iho ai kona maka maluna o Alice, oiai oia e ku ana e nana ana ia lakou, a nana koke iho la oia ia ia iho: nana ae la no hoi ka poe e ae, a kulou iho la lakou a pau.

"E haʻi mai ʻoe iaʻu," wahi a Alice, me ka makaʻu iki, "no ke aha ʻoe e pena nei i kēlā mau rose?"

ʻAʻole ʻōlelo ʻo ʻelima a me ʻEhiku, akā nānā iā ʻElua. Hoomaka iho la elua me ka leo haahaa, "No ke aha la, ike oe e Miss, he laau rose ulaula keia maanei, a hookomo hewa makou i ka laau keokeo, a ina e ike ka Moiwahine, e okiia ko kakou mau poo a pau. a ʻo ka poʻe māla lāʻau ʻekolu, hāʻule koke ihola lākou ma luna o ko lākou mau maka. He leo o na kapuai he nui, a nana ae la o Alice me ka makemake nui e ike i ka Moiwahine.

Ua hele mua mai na koa he umi me na laau palau; Ua like ke ano o keia mau mea a pau me na kihapai ekolu, opaa a palahalaha, me na lima a me na wawae ma na kihi; Ua hoonaniia keia mau mea a puni me na daimana, a hele papalua e like me ka hana ana o na koa. Mahope mai o keia mau keiki alii; He ʻumi lākou, a ʻo nā mea aloha liʻiliʻi e lele leʻaleʻa me ka lima ma ka lima, ma nā kāne: ua kāhiko ʻia lākou a pau i ka puʻuwai. Ma hope mai, hele mai nā malihini, ʻo ka hapa nui o nā Mōʻī a me nā Mōʻīwahine, a ma waena o lākou ua ʻike ʻo Alice i ka Lapi Keʻokeʻo: e kamaʻilio ana me ke ʻano hopohopo wikiwiki, e ʻakaʻaka ana i nā mea a pau i ʻōlelo ʻia, a hele akula me ka ʻike ʻole iā ia. A laila hahai akula ka Naʻi Puʻuwai, e lawe ana i ka lei aliʻi o ka Mōʻī ma luna o ka ʻulaʻula ʻulaʻula ʻulaʻula; a o ka hope loa o keia huakai nui, hiki mai ke alii a me ka moiwahine o na puuwai.

Ua kānalua ʻo Alice inā ʻaʻole pono ʻo ia e moe i lalo i kona alo e like me nā māla ʻekolu, akā ʻaʻole hiki iā ia ke hoʻomanaʻo i ka lohe ʻana i kēlā ʻano lula ma nā huakaʻi; "A eia kekahi, heaha la ka pono o ka huakai," i manao ai oia, "ina e moe na kanaka a pau ilalo ke alo, i ike ole aku?" Ku malie iho la ia ma kona wahi i noho ai, a kali.

I ka hele ʻana o ka huakaʻi ma ke alo o Alice, kū lākou a nānā iā ia, a ʻōlelo koʻikoʻi ka Mōʻīwahine "ʻO wai kēia?" Ua ʻōlelo ʻo ia i ka Knave of Hearts, ʻo ia wale nō ke kūlou ʻana a me ka minoʻaka i ka pane.

“Idiot!” wahi a ka Moiwahine, me ka luli ana i kona poo me ka hoomanawanui; a huli aʻela ʻo ia iā Alice, ʻōlelo maila ʻo ia, "ʻO wai kou inoa, e ke keiki?"

"ʻO Alice koʻu inoa, no laila, e ʻoluʻolu i kou Aliʻi," wahi a Alice me ke akahai loa; Akā, ua hoʻohui hou ʻo ia iā ia iho, "He aha, he pūʻulu kāleka wale nō lākou, ʻoiai, ʻaʻole pono wau e makaʻu iā lākou!"

"A owai keia mau mea? " wahi a ka Moiwahine, me ke kuhikuhi ana aku i na mala ekolu e moe ana a puni ka laau rose; no ka mea, ke ike nei oe, oiai lakou e moe ana ilalo o ko lakou mau maka, a ua like ke ano o ko lakou kua me ke koena o ka pahu, aole hiki iaia ke ike ina he poe mahiko lakou, he poe koa, he poe hookolokolo, a he ekolu paha o kana mau keiki ponoi.

"Pehea wau e ʻike ai?" wahi a Alice me ka pūʻiwa i kona ʻano wiwo ʻole. "ʻAʻole naʻu ʻoihana."

Huli aʻela ka Mōʻīwahine i ʻulaʻula me ka huhū, a ma hope o ka nānā ʻana aku iā ia no kekahi manawa me he holoholona hihiu lā, hoʻōho ʻo ia "E haʻalele i kona poʻo!

“Naʻaupō!” wahi a Alice, me ka leo nui a me ka hoʻoholo ʻana, a hāmau ka Mōʻīwahine.

Kau aku la ka Moi i kona lima maluna o kona lima, me ka makau ole, "E noonoo oe, e kuu mea aloha: he keiki wale no ia!"

Huli aʻela ka Mōʻīwahine me ka huhū mai ona aku, a ʻōlelo akula i ke Knave "E hoʻohuli iā lākou!"

Ua hana ka Knave pela me ka akahele loa, me ka wawae hookahi.

“E ala!” wahi a ka Moiwahine me ka leo nui a me ka leo nui, a lele koke mai la na malaa ekolu, a kulou iho la imua o ka Moi, ka Moiwahine, na keiki alii, a me na mea e ae.

“E haʻalele i kēlā!” i hooho ai ka Moiwahine. "Hoʻopilikia ʻoe iaʻu." A laila, huli aʻela ʻo ia i ka lāʻau rose, ʻōlelo ʻo ia, "He aha kāu hana ma ʻaneʻi?"

“E ʻoluʻolu mai kou Aliʻi,” wahi a Two, me ka leo haʻahaʻa loa, e iho ana ma kekahi kuli i kāna kamaʻilio ʻana, “e hoʻāʻo ana mākou—”

" ʻike au!" wahi a ka Moiwahine, ka mea nana i nana i na rose. “E hoopau i ko lakou mau poo!” a neʻe aku ka huakaʻi, ʻekolu mau koa i koe ma hope no ka pepehi ʻana i nā māla pōʻino, a holo lākou iā Alice no ka pale ʻana.

"ʻAʻole ʻoe e ʻoki ʻia ke poʻo!" wahi a Alice, a hoʻokomo ʻo ia i loko o kahi ipu pua nui e kū kokoke ana. Ua auwana na koa ekolu no hookahi minute a elua paha, e imi ana ia lakou, a hele malie aku mahope o kekahi.

"Ua poʻo ko lākou poʻo?" i hooho ai ka Moiwahine.

"Ua hala ko lakou mau poo, ina e oluolu kou Alii!" i pane aku ai na koa.

"Pololei kēnā!" i hooho ai ka Moiwahine. "Hiki iā ʻoe ke pāʻani croquet?"

Ua hamau na koa, a nana aku la ia Alice, no ka mea, ua maopopo ka ninau nona.

"ʻAe!" i hoʻōho ai ʻo Alice.

"E hele mai!" uwo ae la ka Moiwahine, a komo aku la o Alice i ka huakai, me ka noonoo nui i ka mea e hiki mai ana.

“ʻO ia—he lā maikaʻi loa ia!” wahi a kahi leo makaʻu ma kona ʻaoʻao. E hele ana ʻo ia ma o ka Lapeti Keʻokeʻo, e nānā makaʻu ana i kona alo.

"ʻOiaʻiʻo," wahi a Alice: "ʻAuhea ke Duchess?"

“Hū! Hāmau!” wahi a ka Rabbit me ka leo haʻahaʻa a me ka wikiwiki. Nānā ʻo ia me ka hopohopo ma luna o kona poʻohiwi i kāna ʻōlelo ʻana, a laila hoʻokiʻekiʻe ʻo ia iā ia iho ma luna o kona manamana wāwae, hoʻopili i kona waha i kona pepeiao, a hāwanawana "Aia ʻo ia ma lalo o ka hoʻopaʻi hoʻopaʻi."

“No ke aha?” wahi a Alice.

“Ua ʻōlelo anei ʻoe, 'Auwe!'?” i ninau aku ai ka Rapeti.

"ʻAʻole, ʻaʻole wau," wahi a Alice: "Manaʻo wau ʻaʻole ia he mea minamina. Ua ʻōlelo wau 'No ke aha?'"

“Ua pahu ʻo ia i nā pepeiao o ka Mōʻīwahine—” i hoʻomaka ai ka Rabbit. Ua hoʻōho iki ʻo Alice i ka ʻakaʻaka. “Auē, hāmau!” i hawanawana aku ai ka Rapeti me ka leo makau. "E lohe ana ka Moiwahine ia oe! E ike oe, ua lohi loa kona hele ana mai, a olelo mai ka Moiwahine—"

"E hele i kou mau wahi!" i hooho ae ai ka Moiwahine me ka leo o ka hekili, a hoomaka aku la na kanaka e holo ma na aoao a pau, e ku e i kekahi i kekahi; akā naʻe, hoʻomaha lākou i hoʻokahi minute a ʻelua paha, a hoʻomaka ka pāʻani. Ua manaʻo ʻo Alice ʻaʻole ʻo ia i ʻike i ka ʻāina croquet i loko o kona ola; he mau kualono a me na auwaha; ʻO nā kinipōpō he mau hedgehogs ola, ola nā mallet i nā lapalapa ahi, a pono nā koa e hoʻolua iā lākou iho a kū i ko lākou mau lima a me nā wāwae, e hana i nā pio.

ʻO ka pilikia nui i loaʻa iā Alice i ka wā mua, ʻo ia i ka mālama ʻana i kāna lapalapa: ua lanakila ʻo ia i ka hoʻopaʻa ʻana i kona kino, me ka ʻoluʻolu, ma lalo o kona lima, me nā wāwae e kau ana i lalo, akā ma ke ʻano maʻamau, e like me ka hoʻopololei ʻana i kona ʻāʻī i waho, a e hahau ana i ke poʻo i ka hedgehog, e wili aʻe ʻo ia a nānā i kona alo, me ka hiʻohiʻona puʻupuʻu a ʻaʻole hiki iā ia ke ʻakaʻaka i lalo: Hoomaka hou, he mea hoonaukiuki loa i ka ike ana ua wehe ae ka hedgehog iaia iho, a e kolo ana ia: ma waho o keia mau mea a pau, aia ma ke ala ma na wahi a pau ana i makemake ai e hoouna aku i ka hedgehog, a oiai e ku mau ana na koa papalua a hele aku i na wahi e ae o ka honua, ua hiki koke mai o Alice i ka manao he paakiki loa ia.

Ua pāʻani nā mea pāʻani i ka manawa hoʻokahi me ke kali ʻole i ka huli ʻana, ka hakakā ʻana i nā manawa a pau, a me ka hakakā ʻana no nā hedgehogs; a iloko o ka manawa pokole loa, ua huhu loa ka Moiwahine, a hele aku la ia e hehi me ka uwao ana, "E pau kona poo!" a i ʻole "Hoʻopau me kona poʻo!" ma kahi o hoʻokahi manawa i ka minuke.

Ua hoʻomaka ʻo Alice i ka manaʻo ʻole: he mea maopopo, ʻaʻole ʻo ia i hoʻopaʻapaʻa me ka Mōʻīwahine i kēia manawa, akā ua ʻike ʻo ia e hiki mai ana i kēlā me kēia minuke, "a laila," manaʻo ʻo ia, "pehea lā iaʻu? Ua makemake nui lākou i ka ʻoki ʻana i nā poʻe ma ʻaneʻi, ʻo ka mea kupanaha loa, aia kekahi e ola ana!"

E imi ana oia i wahi e pakele ai, a me ka noonoo ana ina paha e hiki iaia ke hele aku me ka ike ole ia aku, ike aku la oia i ke ano ano o ka lewa: he mea pihoihoi loa ia ia i kinohi, aka, mahope iho o ka nana ana ia mea i hookahi minute a elua paha, nana iho la oia i ka minoaka, a olelo iho la oia ia ia iho, “O ka Poki Cheshire ia: ano e loaa ia'u kekahi mea e kamailio aku ai.”

"Pehea ʻoe e hele nei?" wahi a ka Popoki, a lawa ka waha e kamaʻilio ai.

Ua kali ʻo Alice a ʻike ʻia nā maka, a laila kunou maila. "ʻAʻole pono ke kamaʻilio ʻana me ia," i manaʻo ai ʻo ia, "a hiki mai kona mau pepeiao, a i ʻole kekahi o lākou." I kekahi minuke, ua ʻike ʻia ke poʻo holoʻokoʻa, a laila waiho ʻo Alice i kāna flamingo, a hoʻomaka i ka moʻolelo o ka pāʻani, me ka hauʻoli loa i ka loaʻa ʻana o kekahi e hoʻolohe iā ia. Me he mea lā, ua manaʻo ka Popoki ua lawa ka mea i ʻike ʻia i kēia manawa, ʻaʻole i ʻike hou ʻia.

"Manaʻo wau ʻaʻole lākou e pāʻani maikaʻi ana," i hoʻomaka ai ʻo Alice, me ka leo hoʻopiʻi, "a hakakā lākou a pau me ka weliweli ʻaʻole hiki i kekahi ke lohe iā ia iho e kamaʻilio - a ʻaʻohe o lākou kānāwai; ʻoiai inā aia, ʻaʻohe mea e mālama iā lākou - a ʻaʻole ʻoe i ʻike i ka huikau o nā mea a pau e ola ana; lepo—a ua pono ia'u ke kukini i ka hedgehog a ka Moiwahine i keia manawa, ua holo wale ia i kona ike ana mai i ko'u hele ana mai!"

"Pehea kou makemake i ka Moiwahine?" wahi a ka Popoki me ka leo haahaa.

"ʻAʻole loa," wahi a Alice: "ʻoi loa ʻo ia—" ʻIke koke ʻo ia e kokoke ana ka Mōʻīwahine ma hope ona, e hoʻolohe ana: no laila ua hoʻomau ʻo ia, "—e lanakila ana paha, ʻaʻole pono ke hoʻopau i ka pāʻani."

Ua minoaka ka Moiwahine a hala.

"ʻO wai kāu e kamaʻilio nei?" wahi a ka Mōʻī, e piʻi ana i luna o ʻAleka, me ka nānā ʻana i ke poʻo o ka Popoki me ka hoihoi nui.

"He hoa ia na'u—he Poki Cheshire," wahi a Alice: "e ae mai ia'u e hoolauna aku."

"ʻAʻole au makemake iki i ka nānā ʻana," wahi a ka Mōʻī: "akā, honi paha ia i kuʻu lima inā makemake."

"ʻAʻole wau makemake," wahi a ka Popi.

"Mai palaualelo," wahi a ka Moi, "a mai nana mai oe pela!" Hele ʻo ia ma hope o Alice i kāna ʻōlelo ʻana.

"Nānā paha ka pōpoki i ke aliʻi," wahi a Alice. "Ua heluhelu au i kēlā ma kekahi puke, akā ʻaʻole wau e hoʻomanaʻo i hea."

"Ae, pono e wehe ia," wahi a ka Moi me ka manao hooholo loa, a kahea aku la oia i ka Moiwahine e maalo ana i keia manawa, "E kuu aloha!

Hookahi wale no ala a ka Moiwahine e hooponopono ai i na pilikia a pau, nui a liilii. “E hoopau i kona poo!” wahi ana, me ka nana ole aku.

"Na'u no e kii i ka Ilamuku," wahi a ka Moi me ka makemake nui, a holo wikiwiki aku la ia.

Manaʻo ʻo Alice e hoʻi ʻo ia e ʻike i ke ʻano o ka pāʻani, ʻoiai ʻo ia i lohe ai i ka leo o ka Mōʻīwahine ma kahi mamao, e uē ana me ke kuko. Ua lohe mua ʻo ia i kāna ʻōlelo hoʻopaʻi ʻekolu o nā mea pāʻani e hoʻokō ʻia no ka hala ʻana i ko lākou manawa, a ʻaʻole ʻo ia i makemake i ke ʻano o nā mea, ʻoiai ua huikau ka pāʻani ʻaʻole ʻo ia i ʻike inā ʻo ia ka manawa a ʻaʻole paha. No laila, hele ʻo ia e ʻimi i kāna hedgehog.

Ua hakakā ka hedgehog me kekahi hedgehog, ʻo ia hoʻi he manawa kūpono loa iā Alice no ka hoʻopaʻa ʻana i kekahi o lākou me kekahi: ʻo ka pilikia wale nō, ʻo ka hele ʻana o kāna flamingo ma kēlā ʻaoʻao o ka māla, kahi i ʻike ai ʻo Alice iā ia e hoʻāʻo ana e lele i luna o kahi kumulāʻau.

I ka wā i hopu ai ʻo ia i ka lapalapa a hoʻihoʻi mai, ua pau ka hakakā ʻana, a ua ʻike ʻole ʻia nā hedgehogs ʻelua: "akā, ʻaʻole ia he mea nui," i manaʻo ai ʻo Alice, "no ka mea, ua pau nā pio mai kēia ʻaoʻao o ka honua." No laila, hoʻopaʻa ihola ʻo ia ma lalo o kona lima, i ʻole e pakele hou, a hoʻi hou akula no ke kamaʻilio hou ʻana me kona hoa.

I kona hoʻi ʻana i ka Cat Cheshire, ua pūʻiwa ʻo ia i ka ʻike ʻana i ka lehulehu o ka lehulehu i hōʻuluʻulu ʻia a puni: aia ka hoʻopaʻapaʻa ma waena o ka Ilamuku, ka Mōʻī, a me ka Mōʻīwahine, e kamaʻilio pū ana lākou a pau, a ʻo nā mea ʻē aʻe a pau e noho mālie, a me ka ʻoluʻolu loa.

I ka manawa i puka mai ai ʻo Alice, ua hoʻopiʻi ʻia ʻo ia e nā mea ʻekolu e hoʻoponopono i ka nīnau, a ua haʻi hou lākou i kā lākou mau hoʻopaʻapaʻa iā ia, akā naʻe, i ko lākou kamaʻilio ʻana i ka manawa hoʻokahi, ua paʻakikī loa ʻo ia e hoʻomaopopo pololei i kā lākou ʻōlelo.

ʻO ka hoʻopaʻapaʻa a ka luna hoʻopaʻapaʻa, ʻaʻole hiki iā ʻoe ke ʻoki i ke poʻo ke ʻole he kino e ʻoki ai: ʻaʻole ʻo ia i hana i kēia hana ma mua, ʻaʻole hoʻi ʻo ia e hoʻomaka i kona wā e ola ana.

ʻO ka manaʻo hoʻopaʻapaʻa a ka Mōʻī, ʻo ka mea nona ke poʻo, hiki ke ʻoki ʻia ke poʻo, ʻaʻole ʻoe e kamaʻilio lapuwale.

ʻO ka manaʻo hoʻopaʻapaʻa a ka Mōʻīwahine, inā ʻaʻole i hana ʻia kekahi mea e pili ana i ia mea i ka liʻiliʻi o ka manawa, e pepehi ʻia ʻo ia i nā mea a pau. (ʻO kēia ʻōlelo hope loa ka mea i ʻike ai ka ʻaha holoʻokoʻa i ke koʻikoʻi a me ka hopohopo.)

ʻAʻole hiki iā Alice ke noʻonoʻo i kahi mea ʻē aʻe e ʻōlelo ai akā "No ka Duchess ia: ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ʻoe e nīnau iā ia no ia mea."

"Aia ʻo ia i loko o ka hale paʻahao," wahi a ka Mōʻīwahine i ka mea hoʻopaʻi: "e kiʻi iā ia ma ʻaneʻi." A hele aku la ka Ilamuku me he pua la.

Ua hoʻomaka ke poʻo o ka Popoki e mae i kona wā i hala aku ai, a i kona manawa i hoʻi mai ai me ke Duchess, ua nalowale loa; holo nui ka Moi a me ka Ilamuku e imi ana, a hoi aku ka poe i koe i ka paani.
