MOKUNA IX.
Ka Moolelo Honu Manehe

"ʻAʻole hiki iā ʻoe ke noʻonoʻo i koʻu hauʻoli i ka ʻike hou ʻana iā ʻoe, e ka luahine aloha!" wahi a ka Duchess, me kona hookomo aloha ana i kona lima iloko o Alice, a hele pu aku la laua.

Ua hauʻoli loa ʻo Alice i ka loaʻa ʻana iā ia i loko o ka huhū ʻoluʻolu, a noʻonoʻo ʻo ia iā ia iho ʻo ka pepa wale nō paha ka mea i hoʻomake nui iā ia i ko lāua hui ʻana ma ka lumi kuke.

"I koʻu wā he Duchess," i ʻōlelo ai ʻo ia iā ia iho, (ʻaʻole naʻe me ka manaʻolana nui), "ʻaʻole loaʻa iaʻu kahi pepa i loko o kaʻu lumi kuke. He maikaʻi loa ka hana ʻana o ka sopa me ka ʻole—Malia paha ʻo ka pepa mau ka mea e huhū ai ka poʻe," wahi āna me ka hauʻoli loa i ka loaʻa ʻana o kahi ʻano kānāwai hou, "a me ka vīnega e hana ai i nā mea ʻawaʻawa—a me ka bareli-a me nā mea hana i hana ai i nā mea ʻawaʻawa—a me ka barley-a me nā mea ʻawaʻawa. ʻoluʻolu ʻoluʻolu .

Ua poina loa ʻo ia i ka Duchess i kēia manawa, a ua puʻuhaʻu iki ʻo ia i kona lohe ʻana i kona leo kokoke i kona pepeiao. "E noʻonoʻo ana ʻoe i kekahi mea, e kaʻu mea aloha, a poina iā ʻoe ke kamaʻilio.

"ʻAʻole paha he hoʻokahi," wahi a Alice i ʻōlelo ai.

"Tut, tut, keiki!" wahi a ka Duchess. "He pono nā mea a pau, inā hiki iā ʻoe ke loaʻa." A hoʻopili ʻo ia iā ia iho a kokoke i ka ʻaoʻao o Alice i kāna kamaʻilio ʻana.

ʻAʻole makemake nui ʻo Alice i ka pili ʻana iā ia: ʻo ka mua, no ka mea, he ʻino loa ka Duchess; a ʻo ka lua, no ka mea, ʻo ia ke kiʻekiʻe kūpono e kau ai i kona ʻauwae ma luna o ka poʻohiwi o Alice, a he auwae ʻoi ʻoluʻolu ʻole ia. Akā naʻe, ʻaʻole ʻo ia makemake i ka hana ʻino, no laila ua hoʻomanawanui ʻo ia e like me ka hiki.

"Ua ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ka pāʻani i kēia manawa," wahi āna, ma ke ʻano o ka hoʻomau ʻana i ke kamaʻilio ʻana.

"'Pēlā," wahi a ka Duchess: "a ʻo ke ʻano o ia mea—'ʻO, ʻo ke aloha, ʻo ke aloha, ʻo ia ka mea e holo ai ka honua!'"

"Ua ʻōlelo kekahi," hāwanawana ʻo Alice, "ua hana ʻia e nā poʻe a pau e noʻonoʻo ana i kā lākou ʻoihana ponoʻī!"

"ʻAe! ʻO ka manaʻo like loa ia," wahi a ke Duchess, me ka ʻeli ʻana i kāna ʻauwae liʻiliʻi ʻoi i loko o ka poʻohiwi o Alice me kāna hoʻohui ʻana, "a ʻo ke ʻano o ia mea - 'E mālama i ka manaʻo, a na nā leo e mālama iā lākou iho.'"

"Nani kona hauʻoli i ka loaʻa ʻana o ka pono ma nā mea!" Ua noʻonoʻo ʻo Alice iā ia iho.

"Ke ʻaʻa nei wau e haʻohaʻo ʻoe i ke kumu ʻaʻole wau e kau i koʻu lima ma kou pūhaka," wahi a ka Duchess ma hope o kahi hoʻomaha: "ʻo ke kumu, ke kānalua nei au i ka huhū o kāu flamingo. E hoʻāʻo anei au i ka hoʻokolohua?"

"Nahu paha ʻo ia," i pane akahele ai ʻo Alice, me ka manaʻo ʻole e hoʻāʻo i ka hoʻokolohua.

"ʻOiaʻiʻo loa," wahi a ke Duchess: "nahu nā flamingo a me ka sinapi.

"ʻO ka sinapi wale nō ʻaʻole he manu," wahi a Alice.

"Pono, e like me ka mea maʻamau," wahi a ka Duchess: "he ʻano maopopo loa kāu e kau ai i nā mea!"

"He mineral ia, manaʻo wau," wahi a Alice.

"ʻOiaʻiʻo," wahi a ka Duchess, me he mea lā ua mākaukau e ʻae i nā mea a pau a Alice i ʻōlelo ai; "He wahi maina sinapi nui ma keia wahi. A o ka pono o ia mea—'O ka nui o ka'u, o ka emi mai o kou."

"ʻAe, maopopo iaʻu!" wahi a Alice, ka mea i mālama ʻole i kēia ʻōlelo hope loa, "he mea kanu ia, ʻaʻole like ia, akā ʻo ia."

"Ua ʻae like wau me ʻoe," wahi a ka Duchess; "a ʻo ka pono o ia mea—'E lilo ʻoe i mea āu e ʻike ai'—a i ʻole inā makemake ʻoe e hoʻopili maʻalahi - 'Mai noʻonoʻo ʻoe ʻaʻole ʻoe he mea ʻē aʻe ma mua o ka mea i ʻike ʻia e nā poʻe ʻē aʻe ʻaʻole ʻē aʻe ʻoe ma mua o kou ʻano i ʻike ʻia e lākou.

"Manaʻo wau he pono iaʻu ke hoʻomaopopo pono i kēlā," wahi a Alice me ka mahalo loa, "inā wau i kākau ʻia: akā ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke hahai pono e like me kāu e ʻōlelo nei."

"ʻAʻole ia he mea hiki iaʻu ke ʻōlelo inā koho wau," i pane aku ai ka Duchess, me ka leo hauʻoli.

"E pule mai hoʻopilikia iā ʻoe iho e ʻōlelo ʻoi aku ma mua o kēlā," wahi a Alice.

"ʻAe, mai kamaʻilio e pili ana i ka pilikia!" wahi a ka Duchess. "Ke hāʻawi aku nei au iā ʻoe i makana o nā mea a pau aʻu i ʻōlelo ai i kēia manawa."

“He ʻano makana haʻahaʻa!” i manao ai o Alice. "ʻOliʻoli wau ʻaʻole lākou e hāʻawi i nā makana lā hānau e like me ia!" ʻAʻole naʻe ʻo ia i ʻaʻa e ʻōlelo me ka leo nui.

"Ke noʻonoʻo hou nei?" i ninau aku ai ka Duchess, me ka eli hou ana i kona auwae oi.

"He kuleana koʻu e noʻonoʻo ai," wahi a Alice me ka ʻoi loa, no ka mea, ua hoʻomaka ʻo ia e hopohopo iki.

"E like me ka pono," wahi a ka Duchess, "e like me ka lele ʻana o nā puaʻa; a ʻo ka m—"

Eia naʻe, i ka haʻohaʻo nui o Alice, ua make ka leo o ka Duchess, ʻoiai i waenakonu o kāna huaʻōlelo punahele ʻo 'moral,' a ʻo ka lima i hoʻopili ʻia i kona lima i hoʻomaka e haʻalulu. Nānā aʻela ʻo Alice i luna, a kū maila ka Mōʻīwahine i mua o lākou, me ka pelu ʻana i kona mau lima, me ka ʻāʻī ʻana me he hekili lā.

“He lā maikaʻi, e ke Aliʻi!” hoomaka ka Duchess me ka leo haahaa.

"Ano, ke haawi aku nei au ia oe i ke ao ana aku ia oe," i hooho ae ai ka Moiwahine, me ka hehi iho la i ka honua i kana olelo ana; "Pono ʻoe a i ʻole kou poʻo e haʻalele, a ma kahi o ka hapalua manawa! E koho i kāu koho!"

Ua koho ka Duchess i kāna koho, a ua hala i kahi manawa.

"E hoʻomau kāua i ka pāʻani," wahi a ka Mōʻīwahine iā Alice; a ua makaʻu loa ʻo Alice i ka ʻōlelo ʻana i kahi huaʻōlelo, akā ua hahai mālie ʻo ia iā ia i hope i ka ʻāina croquet.

Ua lawe pono na malihini e ae i ka hele ana mai o ka Moiwahine, a e hoomaha ana lakou ma ka malu: aka, i ko lakou ike ana iaia, ua hoi wikiwiki aku lakou no ka paani, ua olelo wale aku ka Moiwahine, o ka lohi iki ana o ko lakou ola.

I nā manawa a pau e pāʻani ana lākou i ka Mōʻīwahine, ʻaʻole lākou i haʻalele i ka hakakā ʻana me nā mea pāʻani ʻē aʻe, a me ka hoʻōho ʻana "E hele me kona poʻo!" a i ʻole “Hoʻopau i kona poʻo!” ʻO ka poʻe āna i hoʻopaʻi ai, ua lawe ʻia lākou i loko o ka hale paʻahao e nā koa, a ʻo ka poʻe i hoʻopaʻa ʻia, e haʻalele lākou i nā pio no ka hana ʻana i kēia, a hiki i ka pau ʻana o ka hapalua hola ʻaʻohe pio i koe, a ʻo nā mea pāʻani a pau, koe ka Mōʻī, ka Mōʻīwahine, a me Alice, i loko o ka hale paʻahao a ma lalo o ka hoʻopaʻi hoʻopaʻi.

A laila, haʻalele ka Mōʻīwahine me ka pau ʻole o ka hanu, a ʻōlelo akula iā Alice, "Ua ʻike anei ʻoe i ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa?"

"ʻAʻole," wahi a Alice. “ʻAʻole au i ʻike i ke ʻano o ka Honu Moke."

"ʻO ia ka mea i hana ʻia ai ka Soup Mock Turtle," wahi a ka Mōʻīwahine.

"ʻAʻole au i ʻike i kekahi, a i lohe paha i kekahi," wahi a Alice.

"E hele mai oe," wahi a ka Moiwahine, "a nana e hai aku ia oe i kona moolelo."

I ko lākou hele pū ʻana, lohe ʻo Alice i ka ʻōlelo ʻana o ka Mōʻī me ka leo haʻahaʻa, i ka hui maʻamau, "Ua kala ʻia ʻoe a pau." "E hele mai, he mea maikaʻi kēlā !" wahi ana i loko ona iho, no ka mea, ua kaumaha loa ia i ka nui o na pepehi kanaka a ka Moiwahine i kauoha ai.

Loaʻa koke lākou i kahi Gryphon, e moe ana i ka lā. (Inā ʻaʻole ʻoe maopopo i ke ʻano o Gryphon, e nānā i ke kiʻi.) “E ala, palaualelo!” wahi a ka Moiwahine, "a lawe aku oe i keia lede opio e ike i ka Honu Manehe, a e lohe i kona moolelo. a hele akula ʻo ia me ka waiho ʻana iā Alice wale nō me ka Gryphon. ʻAʻole makemake nui ʻo Alice i ke ʻano o ka mea i hana ʻia, akā ma ke ʻano holoʻokoʻa ua manaʻo ʻo ia he mea palekana ke noho pū me ia e like me ka hahai ʻana i kēlā Mōʻīwahine ʻino: no laila ua kali ʻo ia.

Noho iho la ka Gryphon iluna a poni ae la i kona mau maka: alaila nana aku la ia i ka Moiwahine a hiki i kona ike ole ia: alaila, akaaka iho la ia. “Nani ka leʻaleʻa!” wahi a ka Gryphon, ʻo ka hapalua iā ia iho, ʻo ka hapalua iā Alice.

“He aha ka leʻaleʻa?” wahi a Alice.

"No ke aha, ʻo ia ," wahi a ka Gryphon. "ʻO kāna mea makemake wale nō, ʻaʻole lākou e pepehi i kekahi, ʻike ʻoe. E hele mai!"

"'Ōlelo nā kānaka a pau 'e hele mai!' ma ʻaneʻi," i noʻonoʻo ai ʻo Alice, i kona hele mālie ʻana ma hope o ia: "ʻAʻole wau i kauoha ʻia i loko o koʻu ola a pau, ʻaʻole loa!"

ʻAʻole lākou i hele lōʻihi ma mua o ko lākou ʻike ʻana aku i ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa ma kahi mamao, e noho ana me ke kaumaha a mehameha ma luna o kahi ʻāpana pōhaku, a i ko lākou neʻe ʻana aʻe, hiki iā Alice ke lohe iā ia e kanikau ana me he mea lā e haki ana kona puʻuwai. Ua minamina loa ʻo ia iā ia. "He aha kona kaumaha?" i ninau aku ai oia i ka Gryphon, a pane mai la ka Gryphon, me ka like loa me na huaolelo mamua, "O kona manao wale no: aohe ona kaumaha, ua ike oe. E hele mai!"

No laila, piʻi akula lākou i ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa, nāna lākou i nānā me nā maka nui i piha i ka waimaka, ʻaʻole naʻe i ʻōlelo.

"ʻO kēia ka wahine ʻōpio," wahi a ka Gryphon, "makemake ʻo ia e ʻike i kāu moʻolelo, ʻo ia."

“E haʻi aku au iā ia,” wahi a ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa me ka leo hohonu a haʻahaʻa: “E noho ʻolua i lalo, a mai ʻōlelo ʻolua a pau kaʻu.

No laila, noho lākou i lalo, ʻaʻohe mea i ʻōlelo no kekahi mau minuke. Ua noʻonoʻo ʻo Alice iā ia iho, "ʻAʻole au i ʻike pehea e hiki ai iā ia ke hoʻopau, inā ʻaʻole ʻo ia e hoʻomaka." Akā, kali ʻo ia me ka hoʻomanawanui.

"I kekahi manawa," wahi a ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa i ka hope, me ke kaniʻuhū nui, "He Honu maoli nō wau."

Ua ukali ʻia kēia mau ʻōlelo e ka hāmau lōʻihi loa, i haki wale ʻia e ka leo leo ʻana o "Hjckrrh!" mai ka Gryphon, a me ka uē kaumaha mau o ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa. Kokoke loa ʻo Alice e ala aʻe a ʻōlelo, "Mahalo iā ʻoe, e ka haku, no kāu moʻolelo hoihoi," akā ʻaʻole hiki iā ia ke pale i ka noʻonoʻo ʻana he mea hou aʻe e hiki mai ana, no laila noho mālie ʻo ia me ka ʻōlelo ʻole.

"I ko makou wa li'ili'i," i hoomau aku ai ka Honu Manehe me ka malie, oiai nae e uwe iki ana i keia manawa, "hele makou i ke kula i ke kai. He Honu kahiko ka haku—ua kapa makou ia ia he Honu—"

"No ke aha ʻoe i kapa ai iā ia ʻo Tortoise inā ʻaʻole ʻo ia?" i ninau aku ai o Alice.

"Ua kapa mākou iā ia ʻo Tortoise no ka mea, ua aʻo mai ʻo ia iā mākou," wahi a ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa me ka huhū: "ʻoiaʻiʻo hoʻi ʻoe, ʻaʻole loa ʻoe!"

"Pono oe e hilahila ia oe iho no ka ninau ana mai i keia ninau maalahi," wahi a ka Gryphon; a laila noho hāmau lāua a nānā iā Alice ʻilihune, me ka manaʻo ua mākaukau ʻo ia e pohō i ka honua. ʻO ka hope loa, ʻōlelo akula ka Gryphon i ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa, "E holo, e ka ʻelemakule! a hele aku la oia ma keia mau olelo.

“ʻAe, ua hele mākou i ke kula i ke kai, ʻaʻole naʻe paha ʻoe e manaʻoʻiʻo—”

"ʻAʻole wau i ʻōlelo ʻaʻole wau!" hoʻopau iā Alice.

"Ua hana ʻoe," wahi a ka Honu Mock.

"Paʻa i kou alelo!" hoʻohui ʻo Gryphon, ma mua o ka hiki ʻana iā Alice ke kamaʻilio hou. Ua hele ka Honu Manehe.

"Ua loaʻa iā mākou nā hoʻonaʻauao maikaʻi loa - ʻoiaʻiʻo, hele mākou i ke kula i kēlā me kēia lā-"

" Ua hele au i ke kula la," wahi a Alice; "ʻAʻole pono ʻoe e haʻaheo e like me nā mea a pau."

“Me nā mea hou aku?” wahi a ka Honu Manehe me ka hopohopo iki.

"ʻAe," wahi a Alice, "ua aʻo mākou i ka Farani a me ke mele."

“A holoi?” wahi a ka Honu Henehene.

“ʻAʻole loa!” wahi a Alice me ka huhū.

A laila, ʻaʻole maikaʻi loa kāu kula," wahi a ka Honu Manehe me ka leo ʻoluʻolu loa. “I kēia manawa, aia iā mākou ma ka hope o ka bila, 'Palani, mele, a me ka holoi lole - keu.'”

"ʻAʻole hiki iā ʻoe ke makemake nui," wahi a Alice; "noho i lalo o ke kai."

"ʻAʻole hiki iaʻu ke aʻo." wahi a ka Honu Henehene me ke kaniuhu. "Ua lawe wale au i ka papa maʻamau."

“He aha kēlā?” i ninau aku ai o Alice.

"ʻO ka Huli a me ka Wriwing, ʻoiaʻiʻo, i ka hoʻomaka ʻana," i pane aku ai ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa; "a laila nā lālā like ʻole o Arithmetic—Ambition, Distraction, Uglification, and Derision."

"ʻAʻole au i lohe i ka 'Uglification," i ʻōlelo ai ʻo Alice. "He aha ia?"

Hāpai aʻela ka Gryphon i kona mau wāwae ʻelua me ka pīhoihoi. "He aha! ʻAʻole i lohe ʻia no ka hoʻomāinoino!" i hooho ae ai. "Ua maopopo iaʻu ka mea e hoʻonani ai?"

"ʻAe," wahi a Alice me ke kanalua: "ʻo ia hoʻi—e—hana—i kekahi mea—i ʻoi aku ka nani."

"A laila," wahi a ka Gryphon, "inā ʻaʻole ʻoe i ʻike i ka mea e hoʻomāinoino ai, he mea maʻalahi ʻoe."

ʻAʻole i manaʻo ʻia ʻo Alice e nīnau hou aku e pili ana i ia mea, no laila ua huli ʻo ia i ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa, a ʻōlelo ʻo ia "He aha hou kāu e aʻo ai?"

"ʻAe, aia kahi Pohihihi," i pane aku ai ka Honu Hoʻomāʻewaʻewa, me ka helu ʻana i nā kumuhana ma kāna mau ʻili, "—He mea pohihihi, kahiko a me nā mea hou, me ka Seaography: a laila ʻo ke kaha kiʻi—ʻo ke kumu kaha kiʻi he conger-eel kahiko, e hele mai ana i hoʻokahi pule i kēlā me kēia pule: ua aʻo mai ʻo ia iā mākou i ke kaha kiʻi, ka hoʻopololei, a me ka maʻule i loko o nā ʻōwili."

“Pehea ia mea ?” wahi a Alice.

"ʻAe, ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke hōʻike iā ʻoe iho," wahi a ka Honu Mock: "Ua oolea loa au.

"ʻAʻohe manawa," wahi a ka Gryphon: "Ua hele au i ka haku Classics, akā naʻe. He pāpaʻi kahiko ʻo ia, ʻo ia ."

"ʻAʻole au i hele aku iā ia," wahi a ka Honu Mock me ke kaniʻuhū: "ua aʻo ʻo ia i ka ʻakaʻaka a me ke kaumaha, ʻōlelo lākou."

"Pēlā ʻo ia i hana ai, pēlā ʻo ia i hana ai," wahi a ka Gryphon, me ke kaniʻuhū ʻana i kona manawa; a hūnā nā mea ʻelua i ko lāua mau maka ma ko lāua mau wāwae.

"A ehia mau hola o ka lā āu i hana ai i nā haʻawina?" wahi a Alice me ka wikiwiki e hoʻololi i ke kumuhana.

"He ʻumi hola i ka lā mua," wahi a ka Honu Mock: "eiwa i ka lā aʻe, a pēlā aku."

"He hoʻolālā hoʻohihi!" wahi a Alice.

"ʻO ia ke kumu i kapa ʻia ai lākou he mau haʻawina," wahi a ka Gryphon: "no ka mea ke emi nei lākou i kēlā me kēia lā."

He manaʻo hou kēia iā Alice, a ua noʻonoʻo iki ʻo ia ma mua o kāna hana ʻana i kāna ʻōlelo hou. "A laila, he lā hoʻomaha paha ka lā ʻumikumamākahi?"

"ʻOiaʻiʻo nō," wahi a ka Honu Mock.

"A pehea ʻoe i hana ai i ka lā ʻumikumamālua?" Ua hele ʻo Alice me ka makemake.

"Ua lawa kēlā e pili ana i nā haʻawina," i hoʻopau ai ka Gryphon me ka leo hoʻoholo loa: "e haʻi iā ia i kekahi mea e pili ana i nā pāʻani i kēia manawa."
