MOKUNA XII.
ʻO Alice's Hōʻike

“Eia!” wahi a Alice me ka poina loa i ka nui o kona ulu ʻana i nā minuke i hala iho nei, a lele koke ʻo ia i luna o ka pahu jure me ka ʻaoʻao o kona ʻaʻahu, me ka hoʻonāukiuki ʻana i nā poʻo o ka lehulehu ma lalo, a ma laila lākou e moe ana me ka hoʻomanaʻo ʻana iā ia i ka pōʻaiapuni o ka iʻa gula ma mua.

"ʻAe, ke kala mai nei au!" i hooho ae ai oia me ka leo pihoihoi nui, a hoomaka hou e ohi ia mau mea me ka hikiwawe loa, no ka mea, e holo mau ana ka ulia o ka i'a gula iloko o kona poo, a he manao pohihihi kona e pono e ohi koke ia lakou a hoihoi hou iloko o ka pahu jure, o make lakou.

"Aole hiki ke hoomauia ka hookolokolo ana," wahi a ka Moi me ka leo nui loa, "a hiki i ka wa i hoi mai ai na kiure a pau ma ko lakou mau wahi kupono— a pau ," wahi hou ana me ka manao nui, me ka nana pono aku ia Alice i kana olelo ana pela.

Nānā akula ʻo Alice i ka pahu jure, a ʻike ʻo ia, i kona wikiwiki, ua hoʻokomo ʻo ia i ka Moʻo ma ke poʻo i lalo, a ʻo ka mea liʻiliʻi ʻilihune e hoʻoluliluli ana i kona huelo me ke ʻano melancholy, ʻaʻole hiki ke neʻe. Ua hoopuka hou oia iwaho, a hooponopono; "ʻAʻole ia he mea nui," wahi āna iā ia iho; "Pono wau e manaʻo e hoʻohana nui ʻia i ka hoʻokolokolo hoʻokahi ala e like me kekahi."

I ka manawa i loaa iki ai ka pihoihoi o ka huhu o ke kiure, a ua loaa ka lakou papa a me na penikala a hoihoiia aku ia lakou, ua hoomaka lakou e hana me ka ikaika loa e kakau i ka moolelo o ka ulia, koe wale no ka Moo, me he mea la ua lanakila loa ka noho ana me ka hamama o ka waha, e nana ana iluna o ka hale.

"He aha kāu ʻike e pili ana i kēia ʻoihana?" wahi a ka Moi ia Alice.

"ʻAʻohe mea," wahi a Alice.

"ʻAʻohe mea ʻē aʻe? " wahi a ka Mōʻī.

"ʻAʻohe mea," wahi a Alice.

"He mea nui loa kēlā," wahi a ka Mō'ī me ka huli ʻana i ke kiure. E hoomaka ana lakou e kakau i keia maluna o ko lakou mau papa, ua oki mai la ka Lapi Keokeo: "He mea nui ole , ke ano o ka Moi," wahi ana me ka leo mahalo loa, aka, me ka nuku a me ka nana ana i na maka iaia i kana olelo ana.

" ʻAʻole mea nui, ʻoiaʻiʻo, ʻo kaʻu i ʻōlelo ai," wahi a ka Mōʻī me ka wikiwiki, a hele akula iā ia iho me ka leo haʻahaʻa,

“mea nui—mea nui—mea nui—mea nui—” me he mea lā e hoʻāʻo ana ʻo ia i ka huaʻōlelo i ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi.

Ua kākau kekahi o nā jure iā ia he "mea nui," a he "mea nui ʻole." Hiki iā Alice ke ʻike i kēia, ʻoiai ua kokoke ʻo ia e nānā i kā lākou mau papa; "Akā, ʻaʻohe mea nui," noʻonoʻo ʻo ia iā ia iho.

I kēia manawa ua ʻōlelo ʻo ka Mōʻī, ka mea i kākau ma kāna puke memo no kekahi manawa, "Silence!" a heluhelu mai i kana buke, "Rule Kanahakumamalua. O na kanaka a pau i oi aku mamua o hookahi mile ke kiekie e haalele i ka aha ."

Nānā nā kānaka a pau iā Alice.

"ʻAʻole wau he mile ke kiʻekiʻe," wahi a Alice.

"O oe no," wahi a ka Moi.

"Aneane elua mile ke kiekie," wahi a ka Moiwahine.

"ʻAe, ʻaʻole wau e hele," wahi a Alice: "ʻo ia hoʻi, ʻaʻole ia he lula maʻamau: ua hana ʻoe i kēia manawa."

"ʻO ia ke kānāwai kahiko loa o ka puke," wahi a ka Mōʻī.

"A laila ʻo ia ka helu ʻekahi," wahi a Alice.

Ua haikea ka Moi, a pani koke i kana puke. "E noʻonoʻo i kāu ʻōlelo hoʻoholo," wahi āna i ke kiure, me ka leo haʻahaʻa a haʻalulu.

"He mau hoike hou aku e hiki mai ana, e oluolu oe e ka Moi," wahi a ka Lapi Keokeo, me ka lele wikiwiki loa; "Ua lawe ʻia kēia pepa."

“He aha ka mea i loko?” wahi a ka Moiwahine.

"ʻAʻole au i wehe i kēia manawa," wahi a ka Rapeti Keʻokeʻo, "akā, me he mea lā he leka ia i kākau ʻia e ka paʻahao i—i kekahi."

"Pēlā paha," wahi a ka Mōʻī, "inā ʻaʻole i kākau ʻia i kekahi, ʻaʻole maʻamau, ʻike ʻoe."

“Ua kuhikuhi ʻia iā wai?” wahi a kekahi o na kiure.

"ʻAʻole i kuhikuhi ʻia," wahi a ka Lapeti Keʻokeʻo; "ʻoiaʻiʻo, ʻaʻohe mea i kākau ʻia ma waho ." Wehe aʻela ʻo ia i ka pepa i kāna kamaʻilio ʻana, a hoʻohui ʻo ia "ʻAʻole ia he leka, ʻoiai he mau paukū ia."

"Aia anei lakou ma ka lima kakau lima o ka paahao?" i ninau aku ai kekahi o na jure.

"ʻAʻole, ʻaʻole lākou," wahi a ka Lapi Keʻokeʻo, "a ʻo ia ka mea ʻoi loa." (Philihi nā kiure.)

"Ua hoohalike paha oia i ka lima o kekahi," wahi a ka Moi. (Ua hoʻomālamalama hou ka jure.)

“E ʻoluʻolu e ka Mōʻī,” wahi a ka Knave, “ʻaʻole au i kākau, a ʻaʻole hiki iā lākou ke hōʻoiaʻiʻo ua hana wau: ʻaʻohe inoa i kau ʻia ma ka hope."

"Inā ʻaʻole ʻoe i pūlima," wahi a ka Mōʻī, "ʻo ia ka mea e ʻoi aku ka ʻino.

Ua paʻipaʻi lima nui ʻia kēia: ʻo ia ka ʻōlelo akamai mua loa a ka Mōʻī i ʻōlelo ai ma ia lā.

" E hooiaio ana kela i kona hewa," wahi a ka Moiwahine.

"ʻAʻole ia e hōʻoia i ke ʻano o ke ʻano!" wahi a Alice. "No ke aha, ʻaʻole ʻoe i ʻike i ke ʻano o lākou!"

"E heluhelu mai," wahi a ka Moi.

Hoʻokomo ka Rapeti Keʻokeʻo i kona mau maka. "Ma hea wau e hoʻomaka ai, e ʻoluʻolu e ke Aliʻi?" i ninau aku ai oia.

"E hoʻomaka i ka hoʻomaka," wahi a ka Mō'ī me ke kaumaha, "a e hele a hiki i ka hopena: a laila e kū."

ʻO kēia nā paukū i heluhelu ʻia e ka Rabbit White:—

"Ua haʻi mai lākou iaʻu ua hele ʻoe iā ia,
A haʻi mai iaʻu iā ia:
Ua hāʻawi mai ʻo ia iaʻu i kahi ʻano maikaʻi,
Akā, ʻōlelo mai ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke ʻauʻau.

Ua hoʻouna ʻo ia iā lākou ʻaʻole au i hele
(ʻIke mākou he ʻoiaʻiʻo ia):
Inā pono ʻo ia e hoʻomau i ka hihia,
He aha ka hopena iā ʻoe?

Hāʻawi wau iā ia i hoʻokahi, hāʻawi lākou iā ia i ʻelua.
Ua haawi mai oe ia makou i ekolu a oi;
Ua hoʻi mai lākou a pau mai ona aku lā iā ʻoe,
ʻOiai naʻu lākou ma mua.

Inā pono wau a i ʻole ia
Ua komo i keia hana,
Ua hilinaʻi ʻo ia iā ʻoe e hoʻokuʻu iā lākou,
E like me mākou.

ʻO koʻu manaʻo ua loaʻa ʻoe
(Ma mua o kona loaʻa ʻana i kēia kūpono)
He keakea i hiki mai ma waena
ʻO ia, a iā mākou iho, a me ia.

Mai hōʻike iā ia ua makemake nui ʻo ia iā lākou,
No ka mea, pono mau kēia
He mea huna, mālama ʻia mai nā mea ʻē aʻe,
Ma waena o ʻoe a me aʻu.

"O ia ka hoike nui loa a makou i lohe ai," wahi a ka Moi, me ka hamo ana i kona mau lima; "No laila e ʻae ka jure—"

"Inā hiki i kekahi o lākou ke wehewehe," wahi a Alice, (ua ulu nui ʻo ia i nā minuke i hala iho nei, ʻaʻole ʻo ia i makaʻu iki i ke keakea ʻana iā ia), "E hāʻawi wau iā ia i ʻeono peni.

Ua kākau nā jure i kā lākou mau papa, "ʻAʻole ʻo ia i manaʻoʻiʻo he ʻano manaʻo i loko o ia mea," akā ʻaʻohe o lākou i hoʻāʻo e wehewehe i ka pepa.

"Inā ʻaʻohe manaʻo o ia mea," wahi a ka Mōʻī, "ʻo ia ka mea e hoʻopakele ai i ke ao o ka pilikia, ua ʻike ʻoe, ʻaʻole pono mākou e hoʻāʻo e ʻimi, ʻaʻole naʻe au i ʻike," hoʻomau ʻo ia me ka hohola ʻana i nā paukū ma kona kuli, a nānā pono iā lākou me ka maka hoʻokahi; “Me he mea lā ke ʻike nei au i kekahi manaʻo i loko o ia mau mea. wahi ana, huli ae la i ka Knave.

Luliluli iho la ka Knave i kona poo me ke kaumaha. "Ua like anei au me ia?" wahi āna. ( ʻAʻole naʻe ʻo ia i hana, ua hana ʻia i ka pahu pahu.)

"Ua pono, a hiki i keia manawa," wahi a ka Moi, a hoomau aku la oia i ka muumu ana i na pauku nona iho: "' Ua ike makou he oiaio -' o ia ke kiure, oiaio—' Ua haawi aku au iaia, ua haawi laua ia ia i elua— ' no ke aha, pela paha kana i hana ai me na tarta, ua ike oe—"

"Akā, ke hoʻomau nei ' ua hoʻi mai lākou a pau iā ʻoe ,'" wahi a Alice.

"No ke aha, aia lākou!" wahi a ka Moi me ka lanakila, me ke kuhikuhi ana aku i na i'a ma ka papaaina. "ʻAʻohe mea hiki ke ʻoi aku ka maopopo ma mua o kēlā . A laila hou—' ma mua o ka loaʻa ʻana o kēia kūpono -' ʻaʻole ʻoe i kūpono, e kuʻu mea aloha, manaʻo wau?" wahi ana i ka Moiwahine.

“ʻAʻole loa!” wahi a ka Moiwahine me ka huhu me ka hoolei ana aku i ka inika i ka Moo i kana olelo ana. (Ua haʻalele ka Bill pōʻino i ke kākau ʻana ma luna o kāna pāpaʻi me ka manamana lima, no ka mea, ʻaʻohe ona maka; akā, ua hoʻomaka hou ʻo ia me ka ʻīnika e kahe ana i lalo o kona mau maka, i ka wā e mau ana.)

"A laila, ʻaʻole kūpono nā ʻōlelo iā ʻoe," wahi a ka Mōʻī me ka nānā ʻana a puni ka pā hale me ka minoʻaka. He hāmau make.

“He ʻōlelo hoʻomākeʻaka!” wahi a ka Moi me ka leo huhu, a akaaka iho la na mea a pau, "E noonoo ke kiure i ka lakou olelo hooholo." wahi a ka Moi, no ka iwakalua paha o ia la.

"ʻAʻole, ʻaʻole!" wahi a ka Moiwahine. "He ʻōlelo mua - hoʻoholo ma hope."

"Nā mea a me ka lapuwale!" wahi a Alice me ka leo nui. "ʻO ka manaʻo o ka loaʻa mua ʻana o ka ʻōlelo!"

"Paʻa i kou alelo!" wahi a ka Moiwahine me ka poni.

"ʻaʻole wau e!" wahi a Alice.

“E haʻalele i kona poʻo!” i hooho nui ai ka Moiwahine. ʻAʻohe kanaka i neʻe.

"ʻO wai ka mea mālama iā ʻoe?" wahi a Alice, (ua ulu ʻo ia i kona nui piha i kēia manawa). "ʻAʻole ʻoe he ʻeke kāleka wale nō!"

I keia wa, pii ae la ka puu a pau i ka lewa, a lele mai la maluna ona: hooho iki iho la ia, he hapalua o ka makau, a me ka hapalua o ka huhu, a hoao iho la e hahau aku ia lakou, a ike iho la ia e moe ana ma kapa, me kona poo ma ka uha o kona kaikuahine, e holoi malie ana i na lau make e lele ana ilalo o na kumulaau ma kona mau maka.

“E ala, e Alice aloha!” wahi a kona kaikuahine; “He aha ka lōʻihi o kou hiamoe ʻana!”

"ʻAe, ua loaʻa iaʻu kahi moeʻuhane hoihoi!" wahi a Alice, a hai aku la oia i kona kaikuahine, e like me ka hiki ana iaia ke hoomanao ia laua, i keia mau mea hookalakupua ano e ana au e heluhelu nei; a i ka pau ana, honi aku la kona kaikuahine ia ia, me ka i aku, " He moeuhane kupanaha ia, e ke aloha, he oiaio: aka, ano, e holo aku oe i kou ti, ua ahiahi." No laila, kū aʻela ʻo Alice a holo aku me ka noʻonoʻo ʻana i kona holo ʻana, ʻo ia hoʻi, he moeʻuhane maikaʻi loa ia.

Aka, noho malie iho la kona kaikuahine i kona haalele ana iaia, e hilinai ana i kona poo ma kona lima, e nana ana i ka napoo ana o ka la, a me ka noonoo ana ia Alice uuku a me kana mau hana kupanaha a pau, a hiki i kona hoomaka ana e moeuhane ma ke ano o ke ano, a oia kana moeuhane:—

ʻO ka mua, moe ihola ʻo ia iā Alice liʻiliʻi, a paʻa hou nā lima liʻiliʻi ma luna o kona mau kuli, a nānā maila nā maka ʻoliʻoli i kona mau maka - hiki iā ia ke lohe i ka leo o kona leo, a ʻike aku i kēlā poʻo liʻiliʻi liʻiliʻi o kona poʻo e hoʻopaʻa i ka lauoho auwana e komo mau i loko o kona mau maka - a ʻoiai ʻo ia e hoʻolohe ana, a me he mea lā e hoʻolohe ana, ua lilo ka wahi ola a puni kāna mea liʻiliʻi.

Nani ka mauʻu lōʻihi ma kona mau wāwae i ka holo wikiwiki ʻana o ka Rapeti Keʻokeʻo - ʻo ka ʻiole makaʻu i ke ala i loko o ka loko wai kokoke - hiki iā ia ke lohe i ke kani ʻana o nā kīʻaha kīʻaha i ka wā e ʻai ai ʻo March Hare a me kona mau hoaaloha i kā lākou ʻai pau ʻole, a me ka leo ʻo ka Mōʻīwahine e kauoha ana i kāna mau malihini pōʻino e pepehi - ʻo ia hoʻi ka ʻai ʻana o ka pipi a me ka duchesne ma ke kuli puaʻa. a puni—ua piha ka lewa i ka lewa o ka Gryphon, ka uwau ana o ka penikala a ka Moo, a me ka uwau ana o na puaa puaa i kaohiia, i hui pu ia me na uwo mamao loa o ka Honu Moke poino.

Noho ihola ʻo ia me nā maka maka, a manaʻo ka hapa iā ia iho i ka ʻĀina Kamahaʻo, ʻoiai ua ʻike ʻo ia he mea pono e wehe hou iā ia, a e lilo nā mea a pau i ka ʻoiaʻiʻo ʻeleʻele - ʻo ka mauʻu wale nō e ʻenehe ana i ka makani, a ʻo ka wai ʻauʻau i ka nalu ʻana o ka ʻohe - e hoʻololi nā kīʻaha kīwī i nā bele kani hipa, a me ka leo o ka mōʻīwahine i ka leo o ke keiki hipa. ʻO ka pēpē, ka uē a ka Gryphon, a me nā leo ʻē aʻe a pau, e hoʻololi ʻia (ua ʻike ʻo ia) i ka leo huikau o ka pā mahiʻai paʻa-ʻoiai ʻo ka uē ʻana o nā pipi ma kahi mamao e pani ai i kahi o nā uwē nui o ka Honu.

ʻO ka hope loa, ua hōʻike ʻo ia iā ia iho i ke ʻano o kēia kaikuahine liʻiliʻi nona, i ka wā ma hope, e lilo ia i wahine makua; a pehea hoi oia e malama ai, i kona mau makahiki oo, i ka naau oluolu a me ke aloha o kona wa kamalii: a pehea hoi oia e houluulu ai i kana mau keiki liilii e ae, a e hoonaauao ai i ko lakou mau maka a me ka makemake nui i na moolelo ano e, me ka moeuhane paha o ka Aina Kamahao o ka wa kahiko loa: a pehea hoi kona manao me ko lakou mau kaumaha liilii a pau, a loaa ia ia ka lealea i ko lakou mau lealea wale no, me ka hoomanao ana i kona mau la kamalii hauoli.
