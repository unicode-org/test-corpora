Amateka y'agashinyaguro

Yakomeje agira ati: "Umukino uragenda neza cyane ubu".

Duchess yagize ati: “'Tis so, kandi imyitwarire y'ibyo ni -' Oh, 'urukundo rwa tis,' urukundo rwa tis, rutuma isi izenguruka! '”

Alice yongorera ati: “Hari umuntu wavuze ko bikorwa na buri wese utekereza ku bucuruzi bwe bwite!”

Duchess ati: "Ah, erega! Bisobanura ikintu kimwe.", Yacukuye urushyi ruto ruto mu rutugu rwa Alice ubwo yongeraga ati: "kandi imyitwarire iboneye ni - 'Witondere ubwenge, kandi amajwi aziyitaho.'"

“Mbega ukuntu akunda kubona imico mu bintu!” Alice aribwira mu mutima we.

Aceceka gato, Duchess yagize ati: "Natinyutse kuvuga ko urimo kwibaza impamvu ntashyira ukuboko kwanjye mu rukenyerero," impamvu ni uko nshidikanya ku bushyuhe bwa flamingo yawe. Nzagerageza ubushakashatsi? "

Alice yashubije abigiranye amakenga ati: “Ashobora kuruma.” Ntiyumva ko ahangayikishijwe na gato no kugerageza.

Duchess yagize ati: “Ni ukuri, flamingoes na sinapi byombi biraruma. Kandi amahame mbwirizamuco ni - 'Inyoni zo mu kirere zishyira hamwe.'”

Alice yagize ati: "Sinapi gusa ntabwo ari inyoni."

Duchess yagize ati: “Nibyo, nk'uko bisanzwe, mbega uburyo busobanutse ufite bwo gushyira ibintu!”

Alice yagize ati: " Ntekereza ko ari amabuye y'agaciro."

Duchess, wasaga nkiteguye kwemera ibyo Alice yavuze byose yagize ati: "Nibyo koko." “Hano hano hari sinapi nini nini. Kandi imyitwarire y'ibyo ni - 'Uko uwanjye ari ninshi, ni ko n'uwawe.'”

“Yoo, ndabizi!” Yatangaje Alice, utari witabiriye aya magambo ya nyuma, ati: "ni imboga. Ntabwo isa n'imwe, ariko irasa."

Duchess ati: "Ndemeranya nawe rwose." "Kandi imyitwarire y'ibyo ni -'Be uko waba umeze '- cyangwa niba ushaka ko ubivuga mu buryo bworoshe -' Ntukigere utekereza ko utari ukundi kuruta uko byagaragarira abandi ko icyo wari cyo cyangwa ushobora kuba utari cyo gitandukanye n'icyo wari uriho wasangaga bigaragara ko ari ukundi. '”

Alice yagize ikinyabupfura ati: "Ndatekereza ko ngomba kubyumva neza, niba narabyanditse: ariko sinshobora kubikurikiza neza nkuko ubivuze."

Duchess yarashubije mu ijwi rishimishije ati: "Ibyo nta cyo navuga niba nahisemo."

Alice yagize ati: “Senga ntugire ikibazo ngo ubivuge ukundi.”

“Yoo, ntukavuge ibibazo!” ati Duchess. Ati: “Ndakugize impano y'ibintu byose navuze kugeza ubu.”

“Ubwoko buhendutse!” yatekereje Alice. Ati: “Nishimiye ko badatanga impano y'amavuko nk'iyi!” Ariko ntiyigeze yiyemeza kubivuga mu ijwi riranguruye.

“Ongera utekereze?” Duchess yabajije, hamwe nubundi bucukuzi bw'urusaya ruto rukarishye.

Alice yagize ati: "Mfite uburenganzira bwo gutekereza", kuko yari atangiye kumva afite impungenge nke.

Duchess yagize ati: “Nibyo rwose, nk'uko ingurube zigomba kuguruka; na m -”

Ariko hano, Alice yatunguwe cyane, ijwi rya Duchess ryarapfuye, ndetse no hagati yijambo yakundaga cyane 'morale,' kandi ukuboko kwari gufatanye na we gutangira guhinda umushyitsi. Alice yubuye amaso, ahagarara imbere y'Umwamikazi imbere yabo, amaboko arunamye, yubamye nk'inkuba.

“Umunsi mwiza, nyagasani!” Duchess yatangiye mwijwi rito, ridakomeye.

Umwamikazi atera hejuru ati: “Noneho, ndakuburiye neza.” “Wowe cyangwa umutwe wawe ugomba kuba udahari, kandi ko mu gihe kitarenze igice! Fata amahitamo yawe!”

Duchess yahisemo, aragenda mukanya.

Umwamikazi abwira Alice ati: "Reka dukomeze umukino." na Alice yagize ubwoba bwinshi cyane bwo kuvuga ijambo, ariko buhoro buhoro amukurikira asubira kuri croquet-ground.

Abandi bashyitsi bari barungukiye ku kuba Umwamikazi adahari, maze baruhukira mu gicucu: ariko, mu kanya bamubonye, bihutira gusubira ku mukino, Umwamikazi avuga gusa ko gutinda akanya byari kubatwara ubuzima.

Igihe cyose bakinaga Umwamikazi ntabwo yigeze areka gutongana nabandi bakinnyi, maze basakuza bati: "Hanze n'umutwe we!" cyangwa “Kuraho n'umutwe we!” Abo yakatiye bajyanywe gufungwa n'abasirikare, birumvikana ko bagombaga kureka kuba inkuta kugira ngo babigereho, ku buryo bitarenze igice cy'isaha cyangwa irenga nta nkuta zisigaye, kandi abakinnyi bose, usibye Umwami, Umwamikazi, na Alice, bari bafunzwe kandi bakatiwe igihano cyo kwicwa.

Umwamikazi aragenda, ahumeka neza, abwira Alice ati: "Wigeze ubona inyenzi y'agashinyaguro?"

Alice ati: “Oya. “Ntabwo nzi n'ikinyamushongo icyo ari cyo.”

Umwamikazi ati: "Nicyo kintu Mock Turtle Soup ikozwemo".

Alice yagize ati: “Sinigeze mbona n'umwe, cyangwa numvise.”

Umwamikazi ati: “Ngwino, azakubwira amateka ye.”

Bakigenda, Alice yumvise Umwami avuga mu ijwi rito, abwira isosiyete muri rusange ati: "Mwese murababariwe." “Ngwino, icyo ni ikintu cyiza!” aribwira mu mutima we, kuko yumvaga atishimiye umubare w'abicanyi umwamikazi yategetse.

Bidatinze, bahuye na Gryphon, baryamye basinziriye cyane. (Niba utazi Gryphon icyo aricyo, reba ku ishusho.) "Hejuru, ikintu cy'umunebwe!" Umwamikazi ati: "hanyuma ujyane uyu mukobwa ukiri muto kureba inyenzi y'agashinyaguro, no kumva amateka ye. Ngomba gusubira inyuma nkareba nyuma yo kwicwa nategetse;" nuko aragenda, asiga Alice wenyine na Gryphon. Alice ntabwo yakunze neza isura yikiremwa, ariko muri rusange yatekereje ko ari byiza rwose kugumana nayo nko kujya inyuma yuwo mwamikazi winkazi: nuko arategereza.

Gryphon yaricaye amukubita amaso: noneho ireba Umwamikazi kugeza igihe atabonetse: noneho irasetsa. “Mbega ibintu bishimishije!” ati Gryphon, igice cyonyine, igice cya Alice.

“ Ni ibiki bishimishije?” Alice.

Gryphon ati: "Kubera iki, we ?" Ati: "Byose ni byiza, ko: batigera bica umuntu, urabizi. Ngwino!"

“Abantu bose baravuga ngo 'ngwino!' hano, ”yatekereje Alice, agenda buhoro buhoro nyuma yacyo:“ Sinigeze ntegekwa mu buzima bwanjye bwose, nta na rimwe! ”

Ntabwo bari bagiye kure mbere yuko babona Inyenzi y'agashinyaguro kure, bicaye bababaye kandi bonyine ku nkombe ntoya y'urutare, maze, begereye hafi, Alice yumva yumva asuhuza umutima nk'umutima. Yamugiriye impuhwe nyinshi. “Umubabaro we ni uwuhe?” abaza Gryphon, maze Gryphon aramusubiza, hafi mu magambo amwe nka mbere, ati: "Byose ni byiza, ko: nta gahinda afite, urabizi. Ngwino!"

Barazamuka rero bajya kuri Turtle y'agashinyaguro, abareba n'amaso manini yuzuye amarira, ariko ntacyo bavuga.

Gryphon ati: "Uyu mukobwa ukiri muto, arashaka kumenya amateka yawe, arabizi."

Mock Turtle ati: "Nzabimubwira," mwijwi ryimbitse kandi ridafite ishingiro, ati: "icara mwembi, ntuvuge ijambo kugeza ndangije."

Baricara rero, nta muntu wavuze mu minota mike. Alice aribwira ati: "Ntabwo mbona uko ashobora kurangiza, niba adatangiye." Ariko yategereje yihanganye.

Amaherezo Mock Turtle ati: "Rimwe, nari inyenzi nyayo."

Aya magambo yakurikiwe no guceceka cyane, kumeneka gusa rimwe na rimwe gutangaza ngo "Hjckrrh!" kuva kuri Gryphon, no guhora arira cyane kurigata. Alice yari hafi guhaguruka avuga ati: “Urakoze, nyagasani, inkuru yawe ishimishije,” ariko ntiyabura gutekereza ko hagomba kubaho ibindi byinshi bizaza, nuko aricara ntacyo avuga.

Mock Turtle yarakomeje agira ati: "Igihe twari tukiri bato," atuje, nubwo akomeza kurira gato, hanyuma, ati: "twagiye ku ishuri mu nyanja. Databuja yari Inyenzi ishaje - twakundaga kumwita Tortoise -"

“Kuki wamwise Tortoise, niba atari umwe?” Alice yabajije.

“Nibyo, twagiye ku ishuri mu nyanja, nubwo ushobora kutabyizera -”

“Rwose sibyo!” ati Alice n'uburakari.
