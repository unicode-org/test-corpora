UMUTWE WA VII.
Icyayi Cyumusazi

Hano hari ameza yashyizwe munsi yigiti imbere yinzu, kandi Hare yo muri Werurwe na Hatter bari bayirimo icyayi: Dormouse yari yicaye hagati yabo, asinziriye cyane, abandi babiri barayikoresha nk'igitambaro, bayishyiraho inkokora, kandi bavugana hejuru yumutwe. Alice yatekereje ati: “Ntibyoroheye cyane muri Dortouse; Ati: "gusa, nkuko bisinziriye, ndakeka ko ntacyo bitwaye."

Ameza yari manini, ariko uko ari batatu bose bari bateraniye hamwe ku mfuruka yacyo: “Nta cyumba! Nta cyumba!” barataka babonye Alice aje. “Hano hari ibyumba byinshi !” ati Alice ararakaye, yicara mu ntebe nini y'intoki ku mpera imwe y'ameza.

Muri Werurwe Hare yagize ati: “Gira vino.”

Alice yarebye hirya no hino kumeza, ariko ntakindi cyari kuriyo uretse icyayi. Yavuze ati: “Nta vino mbona.

Werurwe Hare yagize ati: “Nta na kimwe.”

Alice n'ishavu n'akantu ati: "Ubwo rero, ntibyari byiza cyane ko ubitanga."

Werurwe Hare yagize ati: "Ntabwo byari byiza cyane ko wicara utatumiwe."

Alice ati: "Sinari nzi ko ari ameza yawe ." “Byashyizweho kuri benshi barenze batatu.”

Hatter ati: "Umusatsi wawe ushaka gukata." Yari amaze igihe areba Alice afite amatsiko menshi, kandi niryo jambo rye rya mbere.

Alice yagize ubwoba bwinshi ati: "Ugomba kwiga kutavuga umuntu ku giti cye;" Ati: “ni ikinyabupfura.”

Urwango rwahumuye amaso cyane yumvise ibi; ariko ibyo yavuze byose byari, “Kuki igikona kimeze nk'intebe yo kwandika?”

“Ngwino, tuzishima ubu!” yatekereje Alice. Yongeyeho n'ijwi rirenga ati: “Nishimiye ko batangiye kubaza ibisobanuro. - Ndizera ko nshobora kubitekereza.”

Ati: “Urashaka kuvuga ko utekereza ko ushobora kubona igisubizo cyacyo?” yavuze muri Werurwe Hare.

Alice yagize ati: “Nibyo rwose.

Werurwe Hare yakomeje agira ati: "Noneho ugomba kuvuga icyo ushaka kuvuga."

Alice yahise asubiza ati: “Ndabikora.” “Nibura - byibuze ndashaka kuvuga ibyo mvuga - icyo ni kimwe, urabizi.”

“Ntabwo ari ikintu kimwe!” ati Urwango. Ati: “Urashobora kuvuga neza ko 'mbona ibyo ndya' ari kimwe na 'Ndya ibyo mbona'!”

Werurwe Hare yongeyeho ati: “Urashobora kuvuga neza ko 'Nkunda ibyo mbona' ari kimwe na 'Ndabona ibyo nkunda'!”

Dormouse, wasaga naho yavugaga mu bitotsi bye, yongeyeho ati: “Ushobora no kuvuga, ko 'mpumeka iyo nsinziriye' ni kimwe na 'Ndasinzira iyo mpumeka'!”

Hatter yagize ati: " Ni kimwe nawe.", Hano ikiganiro cyaragabanutse, maze ibirori bicara umunota umwe, mu gihe Alice yatekereje ku bintu byose ashobora kwibuka ku bikona no ku biro byandika, bitari byinshi.

Urwango ni rwo rwambere rwacecetse. “Ni uwuhe munsi w'ukwezi?” ati, ahindukirira Alice: yakuye isaha ye mu mufuka, ayireba bitamugoye, ayizunguza buri kanya, ayifata ku gutwi.

Alice yatekereje gato, hanyuma ati "Uwa kane."

“Iminsi ibiri yibeshye!” asuhuza Urwango. Ati: "Nakubwiye ko amavuta adakwiriye imirimo!" yongeyeho kureba arakaye muri Werurwe Hare.

Werurwe Hare yitonze aramusubiza ati: "Byari amavuta meza ."

Hatter yijujutira ati: “Yego, ariko uduce tumwe na tumwe tugomba kuba twarinjiyemo.” Ntabwo wari ukwiye kuwushyiramo icyuma cy'umugati. ”

Werurwe Hare yafashe isaha ayireba yijimye: noneho ayijugunya mu gikombe cye cy'icyayi, arongera arareba: ariko nta kindi yatekereza cyiza nko kuvuga kuruta amagambo ye ya mbere, ati: "Byari amavuta meza , urabizi."

Alice yari yararebye hejuru yigitugu afite amatsiko. “Mbega isaha isekeje!” yagize ati. “Ivuga umunsi w'ukwezi, kandi ntivuga isaha iyo ari yo!”

“Kuki bikwiye?” Acecekesha Urwango. “Isaha yawe irakubwira umwaka ni uwuhe?”

Alice yarashubije byoroshye ati: “Birumvikana ko atari byo, ariko ni ukubera ko igumaho umwaka umwe igihe kirekire nk'iki.”

Hatter ati: "Ibyo ni ibyanjye gusa."

Alice yumvise atangaye cyane, Amagambo ya Hatter yasaga nkaho adafite ibisobanuro muri yo, nyamara rwose yari icyongereza. Mu kinyabupfura uko ashoboye ati: "Sinagusobanukiwe neza."

Hatter ati: "Dormouse yongeye gusinzira", maze asuka icyayi gishyushye ku zuru.

Dormouse yazunguye umutwe yihanganye, maze avuga, atakinguye amaso ati: "Birumvikana ko byanze bikunze; gusa ibyo ngiye kwibwira."

“Wigeze ukeka icyo gisakuzo?” Urwango yavuze, ahindukirira Alice.

Alice aramusubiza ati: “Oya, ndabiretse, igisubizo ni ikihe?”

Hatter ati: "Nta gitekerezo na gito mfite."

Hare yo muri Werurwe yagize ati: “Nanjye ntabwo.

Alice asuhuza umutima. Ati: "Ndatekereza ko ushobora gukora ikintu cyiza mugihe, kuruta guta igihe ubaza ibisobanuro bidafite ibisubizo."

Hatter ati: "Niba wari uzi Igihe nkanjye, ntabwo wavuga kubitesha agaciro . Ni we ."

Alice ati: "Sinzi icyo ushaka kuvuga."

“Birumvikana ko utabikora!” Urwango yavuze, guta umutwe agasuzuguro. Ati: “Natinyutse kuvuga ko utigeze uvugana n'Igihe!”

Alice yashubije abigiranye amakenga ati: “Birashoboka ko atari byo, ariko nzi ko ngomba gutsinda igihe nize umuziki.”

Urwango ati: "Ah! Ibyo ni byo bibarwa." "Ntazahagarara gukubitwa. Noneho, uramutse ukomeje kumvikana neza na we, yakoraga hafi ikintu cyose ukunda hamwe nisaha. Urugero, tuvuge ko hari saa cyenda za mugitondo, igihe cyo gutangira amasomo: wagira ngo wongorerana gusa igihe, hanyuma ukazenguruka isaha mu kanya nk'akantu!

(Hare yo muri Werurwe yivugiye ati: "Nifuzaga ko aribyo."

Alice yatekereje ati: "Nibyo rwose, byaba ari byiza, ariko rero, sinakagombye kubishonje, urabizi."

Hatter yagize ati: “Ntabwo ari ubwa mbere, ahari, ariko ushobora gukomeza kugeza saa kumi n'ebyiri n'igice igihe cyose ubishakiye.”

“Iyo ni yo nzira ucunga ?” Alice yabajije.

Urwango yazunguye umutwe ababaye. “Ntabwo ari njye!” aramusubiza. “Twatonganye muri Werurwe umwaka ushize - mbere yuko asara , urabizi -” (yerekana ikiyiko cye cy'icyayi kuri Werurwe Hare,) “- cyari mu gitaramo gikomeye cyatanzwe n'Umwamikazi w'Imitima, kandi ngomba kuririmba

'Twinkle, twinkle, bat bat!
Mbega ukuntu nibaza icyo urimo! '

Uzi indirimbo, ahari? ”

Alice yagize ati: “Numvise ibintu nk'ibyo.

Hatter yakomeje agira ati: "Birakomeza, urabizi, muri ubu buryo: -

'Hejuru y'isi uguruka,
Nka cyayi-tray mu kirere.
Twinkle, twinkle - '”

Hano Dormouse iranyeganyega, itangira kuririmba mu bitotsi byayo “ Twinkle, twinkle, twinkle, twinkle -” ikomeza urugendo rurerure ku buryo byabaye ngombwa ko iyikubita kugira ngo ihagarare.

Hatter yagize ati: “Nibyo, sinarangije kurangiza umurongo wa mbere, igihe Umwamikazi yasimbukaga maze arataka ati: 'Yishe igihe! Hanze n'umutwe we!'”

“Mbega ukuntu biteye ubwoba!” Alice yatangaye.

Hatter akomeza avuga mu cyunamo ati: “Kandi kuva icyo gihe, ntazakora ikintu nsabye! Buri gihe ni saa kumi n'ebyiri.”

Igitekerezo cyiza cyaje mumutwe wa Alice. Ati: “Iyo niyo mpamvu ituma ibintu byinshi by'icyayi bishyirwa hanze?” abaza.

Hatter asuhuza umutima ati: “Nibyo, ni ko bimeze, buri gihe ni igihe cy'icyayi, kandi nta mwanya dufite wo koza ibintu hagati y'ibituba.”

“Noneho ukomeza kugenda uzenguruka, ndakeka?” Alice.

Urwango yagize ati: “Nibyo rwose, uko ibintu bizagenda bimenyera.”

“Ariko bigenda bite iyo wongeye kuza mu ntangiriro?” Alice yiyemeje kubaza.

Hare yo muri Werurwe yarahagaritse ati: “Dufate ko duhinduye ingingo.” Ati: “Ndarambiwe ibi. Ndatora umukobwa ukiri muto atubwira inkuru.”

Alice yagize ati: “Mfite ubwoba ko ntazi.”

“Icyo gihe Dortouse igomba!” Bombi bararira. “Kanguka, Dormouse!” Kandi barayikubita ku mpande zombi icyarimwe.

Dormouse yahise ahumura amaso. Mu ijwi ritontoma, rifite intege nke, yagize ati: “Sinari nsinziriye, numvise ijambo ryose bagenzi bawe bavuga.”

“Tubwire inkuru!” yavuze muri Werurwe Hare.

“Yego, ndagusabye!” yinginze Alice.

Hatter yongeyeho ati: “Kandi wihute, cyangwa uzongera gusinzira bitararangira.”

Dormouse yatangiye yihuta cyane ati: "Kera hariho bashiki bacu batatu bato". “Kandi amazina yabo yari Elsie, Lacie, na Tillie; kandi babaga munsi y'iriba -”

“Babayeho iki?” ati Alice, wahoraga ashishikajwe cyane nibibazo byo kurya no kunywa.

Dormouse, nyuma yo gutekereza umunota umwe cyangwa ibiri, yagize ati: “Babayeho mu nzira.

Alice yagize ati: "Ntabwo bari gushobora gukora ibyo, urabizi;" “Bari kuba barwaye.”

Dormouse ati: "Niko byari bimeze." “Ndarwaye cyane .”

Alice yagerageje kwiyumvisha uko ubuzima nk'ubwo budasanzwe bwaba bumeze, ariko byaramuteye urujijo cyane, nuko akomeza agira ati: “Ariko kuki babaye munsi y'iriba?”

Werurwe Hare yabwiye Alice ati: "Fata ikindi cyayi."

Alice yarashubije ati: “Nta kintu na kimwe nari mfite, ku buryo sinshobora gufata byinshi.”

Hatter yagize ati: “Urashaka kuvuga ko udashobora gufata bike , biroroshye cyane gufata ibirenze ubusa.”

Alice yagize ati: “Nta muntu wabajije igitekerezo cyawe .

“Ubu ni nde avuga ijambo ku giti cye?” Urwango yabajije atsinze.

Alice ntabwo yari azi neza icyo abivugaho: nuko yifashisha icyayi n'umugati-n'amavuta, hanyuma ahindukirira Dormouse, asubiramo ikibazo cye. “Kuki babaga munsi y'iriba?”

Dormouse yongeye gufata umunota umwe cyangwa ibiri yo kubitekerezaho, hanyuma iravuga iti: "Byari iriba."

“Nta kintu nk'icyo!” Alice yari atangiye uburakari bwinshi, ariko Urwango na Werurwe Hare rwagiye "Sh! Sh!" na Dormouse yagize ati: "Niba udashobora kuba umunyagihugu, wakagombye kurangiza inkuru wenyine."

“Oya, ndakwinginze komeza!” Alice yavuze yicishije bugufi cyane; "Sinzongera guhagarika umutima. Natinyutse kuvuga ko hashobora kubaho imwe ."

“Umwe, rwose!” ati Dormouse n'uburakari. Icyakora, yemeye gukomeza. “Kandi rero aba bashiki bacu batatu bato - bigaga gushushanya, urabizi -”

“Bashushanyije iki?” ati Alice, yibagiwe rwose amasezerano ye.

Dormouse yagize ati: “Treacle,” utabanje gutekereza kuri iki gihe cyose.

Hatter yaracecetse ati: “Ndashaka igikombe gisukuye, reka twese tujye ahantu hamwe.”

Yakomeje avuga, Dormouse iramukurikira: Hare yo muri Werurwe yimukiye mu cyumba cya Dormouse, kandi Alice ahubwo atabishaka yafashe umwanya wa Werurwe Hare. Urwango ni rwo rwonyine rwabonye inyungu iyo ari yo yose: kandi Alice yari mwiza cyane kurusha mbere, kuko Werurwe Hare yari imaze kubabaza amata-isahani mu isahani.

Alice ntiyifuzaga kongera kubabaza Dormouse, nuko atangira yitonze ati: "Ariko simbyumva. Bakuye he inzira?"

Hatter ati: "Urashobora kuvoma amazi mu iriba ry'amazi". “Noneho nkwiye gutekereza ko ushobora kuvana inzira mu iriba-eh, igicucu?”

Alice yabwiye Dormouse ati: "Ariko bari mu iriba." Ntabwo yahisemo kubona aya magambo ya nyuma.

Dormouse yagize ati: "Birumvikana ko bari;" “- guma.”

Iki gisubizo cyateye urujijo umukene Alice, ku buryo yaretse Dormouse ikomeza igihe runaka itabangamiye.

Dormouse ikomeza igira iti: "Barimo kwiga gushushanya", asunika kandi amukubita amaso, kuko yari asinziriye cyane; “Kandi bashushanyije ibintu byose - byose bitangirana na M -”

“Kuki hamwe na M?” Alice.

“Kuki?” yavuze muri Werurwe Hare.

Alice yaracecetse.

Dormouse yari imaze gufunga amaso muri iki gihe, kandi yari yagiye muri doze; ariko, ku gukubitwa na Hatter, byongeye kubyuka n'ijwi rirenga, bikomeza: “- ibyo bitangirana na M, nk'imitego y'imbeba, n'ukwezi, no kwibuka, ndetse n'ubunini - uzi ko uvuga ko ibintu ari“ byinshi cyane ”- wigeze ubona ikintu nk'iki cyo gushushanya byinshi?”

Alice yagize urujijo cyane ati: "Mu byukuri, ubu urambajije," Ntabwo ntekereza - "

Hatter ati: "Noneho ntugomba kuvuga."

Iki gice cyubupfura cyari kirenze ibyo Alice yashoboraga kwihanganira: arahaguruka yanga urunuka, arigendera; Dormouse yahise asinzira ako kanya, kandi nta n'umwe mu bandi wigeze amenya ko agenda, nubwo yasubije amaso inyuma rimwe cyangwa kabiri, kimwe cya kabiri yizera ko bazamuhamagara: aheruka kubabona, bagerageza gushyira Dormouse mu cyayi.

“Ibyo ari byo byose sinzongera kujyayo !” ati Alice ubwo yatoraguye inzira mu ishyamba. Ati: "Nibirori byicyayi byubucucu nigeze kubaho mubuzima bwanjye bwose!"

Akimara kuvuga atyo, yabonye ko kimwe mu biti cyari gifite umuryango winjiramo. “Ibyo ni amatsiko cyane!” Yatekereje. "Ariko ibintu byose bifite amatsiko uyu munsi. Ndatekereza ko nshobora no kwinjira icyarimwe." Aragenda.

Yongeye kwisanga muri salle ndende, kandi hafi yameza yikirahure. Yibwiye ati: "Ubu, nzayobora neza iki gihe", atangira afata urufunguzo ruto rwa zahabu, akingura urugi rwerekeza mu busitani. Hanyuma yagiye ku kazi yinyeganyeza ku gihumyo (yari yarabitse igice cyacyo mu mufuka) kugeza ageze nko kuri metero ndende: hanyuma agenda mu gice gito: hanyuma - yisanga amaherezo mu busitani bwiza, mu buriri bwiza bw'indabyo n'amasoko akonje.
