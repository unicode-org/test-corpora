UMUTWE WA XII.
Ibimenyetso bya Alice

“Hano!” yatakambiye Alice, yibagirwa rwose mu kanya gato ko yari amaze gukura mu minota mike ishize, maze asimbuka yihuta cyane ku buryo yambukije agasanduku k'abacamanza akoresheje inkombe y'ijipo ye, ababaza abacamanza bose ku mitwe y'imbaga y'abantu bari hepfo, maze bararyama hirya no hino, bamwibutsa byinshi ku isi y'amafi ya zahabu yari afite ku bw'impanuka.

“Yoo, ndagusabye imbabazi!” yatangaye n'ijwi rirenga, maze atangira kubitoragura vuba bishoboka, kubera ko impanuka y'amafi ya zahabu yakomeje kwiruka mu mutwe, kandi yari afite igitekerezo kidasobanutse cy'uko bagomba kwegeranya icyarimwe bagasubizwa mu gasanduku k'abacamanza, cyangwa se bagapfa.

Umwami n'ijwi rirenga ati: "Urubanza ntirushobora gukomeza, kugeza igihe abacamanza bose bazasubira mu mwanya wabo - bose ", asubiramo ashimangira cyane, yitegereza Alice nk'uko yabivuze.

Alice yitegereza agasanduku k'abacamanza, abona ko, yihutiye, yashyize Umuserebanya mu mutwe hasi, maze akantu gato k'umukene kazunguza umurizo mu buryo buteye agahinda, kubera ko adashobora kugenda. Bidatinze yongeye kuyisohora, ayishyira iburyo; Yibwiye ati: “ntabwo bivuze ko bisobanura byinshi.” Ati: “Nakagombye gutekereza ko byaba ari byiza cyane mu rubanza mu buryo bumwe nk'ubundi.”

Inteko y'abacamanza ikimara gukira gato kubera gutungurwa no kubabazwa, kandi ibisate n'amakaramu yabo byabonetse bakabisubiza, bahisemo gukora cyane kugira ngo bandike amateka y'impanuka, bose usibye Umuserebanya, wasaga nkuwatsinzwe cyane kugira ngo akore ikintu cyose ariko yicaye akoresheje umunwa, yitegereza mu gisenge cy'urukiko.

“Ni iki uzi kuri ubu bucuruzi?” Umwami abwira Alice.

Alice yagize ati: “Nta na kimwe.

Umwami akomeza agira ati: “Nta na kimwe? ”

Alice yagize ati: “Nta na kimwe.

Umwami ahindukirira inteko y'abacamanza ati: "Ibyo ni ngombwa cyane." Batangiye kwandika ibi ku rupapuro rwabo, igihe Urukwavu rwera rwacecekesheje ati: " Ntabwo ari ngombwa, nyagasani, bivuze ko byanze bikunze," yavuze mu cyubahiro cyiyubashye, ariko yubitse amaso kandi amureba mu maso igihe yavugaga.

Umwami yihutira kuvuga ati: " Ntabwo ari ngombwa, byanze bikunze, nashakaga kuvuga", maze akomeza kwiyegereza mu ijwi riranguruye,

“Icy'ingenzi - kidafite akamaro - kidafite akamaro - cy'ingenzi -” nkaho yagerageje iryo jambo ryumvikana neza.

Bamwe mu bagize inteko y'abacamanza banditse ngo “ingenzi,” abandi “badafite akamaro.” Alice yashoboraga kubona ibi, kuko yari hafi bihagije kugirango arebe ibyapa byabo; Yibwiye mu mutima we ati: “ariko ntacyo bitwaye.

Muri aka kanya, Umwami, wari umaze igihe kinini yandika mu gitabo cye cy'inoti, yamaganye “Guceceka!” hanyuma usome mu gitabo cye, “Itegeko rya mirongo ine na kabiri. Abantu bose barenga kilometero imwe yo kuva mu rukiko .”

Abantu bose bareba Alice.

Alice ati: " Ntabwo ndi hejuru y'ibirometero."

Umwami ati: “Uri.”

Umwamikazi yongeyeho ati: “Hafi y'ibirometero bibiri.

Alice yagize ati: “Nibyo, ntabwo nshobora kugenda, uko byagenda kose, usibye ko iryo atari itegeko risanzwe: wabihimbye ubu.”

Umwami ati: "Ni ryo tegeko rya kera cyane mu gitabo."

Alice yagize ati: “Noneho bigomba kuba ibya mbere.

Umwami ahinduka umwijima, ahita afunga igitabo cye. Abwira inteko y'abacamanza ati: “Tekereza ku cyemezo cyawe.”

Inkwavu yera, isimbuka yihuta cyane iti: "Hariho ibimenyetso byinshi biri imbere, nyamuneka nyagasani". “Uru rupapuro rumaze gutorwa.”

“Ni iki kirimo?” Umwamikazi ati.

Urukwavu rwera ruvuga ruti: “Ntarakingura, ariko bisa naho ari ibaruwa imfungwa yandikiye umuntu.”

Umwami ati: "Bigomba kuba aribyo, keretse iyo byandikiwe nta muntu, bitari bisanzwe, urabizi."

“Ninde uyobora?” umwe mu bagize inteko y'abacamanza.

Urukwavu rwera ruti: "Ntabwo ruyobowe na gato." “Mubyukuri, nta kintu cyanditse hanze .” Yakinguye impapuro ubwo yavugaga, yongeraho ati: "Ntabwo ari ibaruwa, nyuma ya byose: ni umurongo w'imirongo."

“Ese bari mu nyandiko y'imfungwa?” yabajije undi mu bagize inteko.

Urukwavu rwera ruvuga ruti: “Oya, si bo, kandi ni cyo kintu kibi cyane kuri byo.” (Abacamanza bose basaga naho bayobewe.)

Umwami ati: “Agomba kuba yariganye ukuboko kw'undi muntu.” (Abacamanza bose bongeye kumurika.)

Knave ati: "Nyamuneka nyagasani, ntabwo nabyanditse, kandi ntibashobora kwerekana ko nabikoze: nta zina ryashyizweho umukono ku iherezo."

Umwami ati: "Niba utarashyizeho umukono, ibyo bituma ibintu birushaho kuba bibi. Ugomba kuba warashakaga kuvuga nabi, bitabaye ibyo wasinyaga izina ryawe nk'umuntu w'inyangamugayo."

Muri rusange habaye amashyi muri rusange: nicyo kintu cya mbere cyubwenge rwose Umwami yari yavuze uwo munsi.

Umwamikazi ati: "Ibyo birerekana icyaha cye."

“Nta kintu na kimwe kigaragaza!” Alice. “Kuki, utazi n'icyo barimo!”

Umwami ati: “Basome.”

Urukwavu rwera rwambaye indorerwamo. “Nzatangirira he, ndakwinginze nyagasani?” abaza.

Umwami ati: "Tangira ubanza, komeza kugeza urangije, hanyuma uhagarare."

Iyi yari imirongo Urukwavu rwera rwasomye: -

“Bambwiye ko wabaye kuri we,
Kandi yarambwiye kuri we:
Yampaye imico myiza,
Ariko yavuze ko ntashobora koga.

Yaboherereje ijambo ntagiye
(Turabizi ko ari ukuri):
Niba agomba gusunika ikibazo kuri,
Bizagenda bite?

Namuhaye umwe, bamuha babiri,
Waduhaye bitatu cyangwa byinshi;
Bose bamugarukira iwawe,
Nubwo bari abanjye mbere.

Niba njye cyangwa agomba kuba amahirwe yo kuba
Uruhare muri iki kibazo,
Yizeye ko ubabohora,
Nukuri nkuko twari tumeze.

Igitekerezo cyanjye nuko wahoze
(Mbere yuko agira ibi bikwiye)
Inzitizi yaje hagati
We, natwe ubwacu, kandi.

Ntumumenyeshe ko abakunda cyane,
Ibi bigomba kubaho iteka
Ibanga, ryabitswe mubindi byose,
Hagati yawe nanjye. ”

Umwami amukubita amaboko ati: "Icyo ni cyo kimenyetso gikomeye cyane twigeze kumva." “Noneho reka reka inteko y'abacamanza -”

Alice yagize ati: "Niba hari n'umwe muri bo ushobora kubisobanura," yari amaze gukura mu minota mike ishize ku buryo atatinye na gato ko amubuza amahwemo), ati: "Nzamuha espensi esheshatu. Ntabwo nizera ko harimo atom ifite ibisobanuro."

Abacamanza bose banditse ku rupapuro rwabo, “Ntabwo yemera ko harimo atom ifite ibisobanuro,” ariko nta n'umwe muri bo wagerageje gusobanura impapuro.

Umwami ati: "Niba nta busobanuro burimo, ibyo bikiza isi y'ibibazo, urabizi, kuko tutagomba kugerageza kubibona. Kandi nyamara simbizi", akomeza imirongo ku ivi, maze abireba n'ijisho rimwe; Ati: "Nsa naho mbona ibisobanuro muri bo, nyuma ya byose." - ati sinshobora koga - "ntushobora koga, urashobora?" yongeyeho, ahindukirira i Knave.

Knave yazunguye umutwe birababaje. “Ndasa na yo?” ati. (Ibyo rwose ntabwo yabikoze, bikozwe mubikarito.)

Umwami ati: "Ni byiza, kugeza ubu," akomeza kwitotombera imirongo yibwira ati: "' Turabizi ko ari ukuri -' iyo ni yo nteko y'abacamanza, byanze bikunze - ' Namuhaye umwe, bamuha babiri -' kubera iki, ibyo bigomba kuba aribyo yakoranye na tarti, urabizi -”

Alice yagize ati: “Ariko, birakomeza ' bose bamugarukiye iwawe .'

“Kubera iki, bariho!” yavuze Umwami atsinze, yerekana ibishushanyo biri kumeza. “Nta kintu na kimwe gishobora gusobanuka kirenze ibyo . Hanyuma na none - ' mbere yuko agira ibi -' ntabwo wigeze uhura, mukundwa, ndatekereza?” abwira Umwamikazi.

“Nta na rimwe!” ati Umwamikazi ararakaye, atera inkingi kuri Lizard ubwo yavugaga. .

Umwami ati: "Noneho amagambo ntagukwiranye ." Hacecetse.

“Ni igihano!” Umwami yongeyeho mu ijwi ribi, abantu bose barabaseka bati: “Reka inteko isuzume imyanzuro yabo.” Umwami yavuze, nko mu gihe cya makumyabiri uwo munsi.

“Oya, oya!” Umwamikazi ati. “Igihano cya mbere - urubanza nyuma.”

“Ibintu n'ubusa!” ati Alice n'ijwi rirenga. “Igitekerezo cyo kugira interuro mbere!”

“Fata ururimi rwawe!” ati Umwamikazi, ahinduka ibara ry'umuyugubwe.

“Sinzabikora!” Alice.

“Kuraho n'umutwe we!” umwamikazi yavugije induru hejuru y'ijwi rye. Nta muntu wimutse.

“Ni nde akwitayeho?” ati Alice, (yari amaze gukura kugeza mubunini bwe kugeza magingo aya). “Nta kindi uri uretse ipaki y'amakarita!”

Ako kanya, paki yose yazamutse mu kirere, iza kumuguruka hejuru: atanga induru nkeya, igice cy'ubwoba n'igice cy'uburakari, agerageza kubakubita, asanga aryamye ku nkombe, umutwe we uri mu bibero bya mushiki we, wari uhanagura buhoro amababi yapfuye yari yamanutse ava mu biti mu maso.

“Kanguka, Alice nshuti!” mushiki we ati; “Kubera iki, mbega ukuntu uryamye igihe kirekire!”

“Yoo, narose amatsiko nk'aya!” ati Alice, abwira mushiki we, nkuko ashobora kubibuka, ibi byose bidasanzwe Adventures we umaze gusoma; arangije, mushiki we aramusoma, ati: " Byari inzozi zamatsiko, nshuti, rwose: ariko noneho wirukire mu cyayi cyawe; biratinda." Alice rero arahaguruka ariruka, atekereza mugihe yirutse, uko ashoboye, mbega inzozi nziza.

Ariko mushiki we yicaye nkuko yamusize, yegamiye umutwe ku kiganza, yitegereza izuba rirenze, atekereza kuri Alice muto na Adventures nziza zose, kugeza ubwo na we atangiye kurota nyuma yimyambarire, kandi iyi yari inzozi ze: -

Ubwa mbere, yarose umwana muto Alice ubwe, kandi na none amaboko mato yafatiriwe ku ivi, kandi amaso yishimye cyane yarebaga mu bye - yumvaga amajwi ye cyane, akabona ako kantu gato kamujugunye mu mutwe kugira ngo agumane umusatsi uzerera uzahora mu maso ye - kandi akimara kumva, cyangwa asa n'uwumva, ahantu hose yari amukikije ari muzima hamwe na mushiki we udasanzwe.

Ibyatsi birebire byerekeje ku birenge ubwo Urukwavu rwera rwihuta - Imbeba yagize ubwoba iranyura mu kidendezi cyegeranye - yumva urusaku rw'icyayi ubwo Hare yo muri Werurwe na bagenzi be basangiraga amafunguro yabo atagira iherezo, kandi ijwi ryijimye ry'Umwamikazi ryategetse abashyitsi be bibabaje ko ryicwa - mu gihe amasahani y'ingurube yari asunitse ku isahani ya Duchess. Gryphon, gutontoma kw'ikaramu-Ikaramu ya Lizard, no kuniga ingurube-ingurube zahagaritswe, byuzuye umwuka, bivangwa no kurira kure ya Mock Turtle.

Yicaye rero, afite amaso afunze, kimwe cya kabiri yizeraga muri Wonderland, nubwo yari azi ko afite ariko akongera kubakingura, kandi byose bizahinduka mubyukuri bidasobanutse - ibyatsi byari guhuhuta mu muyaga gusa, kandi ikidendezi cyikubita ku kuzunguza urubingo - icyayi cyatontomye kizahinduka kijugunyira intama, kandi umwamikazi arataka arangurura ijwi avuga ati: Gryphon, hamwe nandi majwi yose y’urusaku, yahinduka (yari abizi) ahinduka urusaku rw’urujijo rw’imirima ihuze cyane - mu gihe kumanura inka kure byari gufata umwanya wo kurira kwa Mock Turtle.

Ubwanyuma, yishushanyijeho ukuntu uyu mushiki we mutoya we, nyuma yigihe, yaba umugore ukuze; nuburyo yakomeza, mumyaka ye yose yuzuye, umutima woroheje kandi wuje urukundo wubwana bwe: nuburyo yakusanyirizaga hamwe nabandi bana be bato, akanatuma amaso yabo akayangana kandi ashishikaye hamwe ninkuru idasanzwe, wenda ndetse ninzozi za Wonderland kuva kera: nukuntu yakumva afite akababaro kabo koroheje, akibuka umunezero wabo wose woroshye, akibuka ubuzima bwe bwite bwubuzima.
