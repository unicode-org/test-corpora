UMUTWE WA III.
Isiganwa rya Caucus-Umugani muremure

Mu byukuri bari ibirori bisa nkibikinisho byateraniye ku nkombe - inyoni zifite amababa akururwa, inyamaswa zifite ubwoya bwazo ziyegereye, kandi byose bitonyanga amazi, umusaraba, kandi ntibyoroshye.

Ikibazo cya mbere birumvikana, ni uburyo bwo kongera gukama: bagiranye inama kuri iki kibazo, kandi nyuma yiminota mike byasaga nkibisanzwe kuri Alice gusanga avugana numuryango nabo, nkaho yabazi mubuzima bwe bwose. Mu byukuri, yatonganye cyane na Lory, waje guhinduka mubi, akavuga gusa ati: "Ndakuruta, kandi ngomba kubimenya neza;" kandi iyi Alice ntiyabyemera atazi imyaka ifite, kandi, nkuko Lory yanze rwose kuvuga imyaka yayo, ntakindi cyari kuvugwa.

Amaherezo, Imbeba yasaga nkumuntu ufite ubutware muri bo, yarahamagaye iti: "Icara mwese, munyumve! Nzahita nkumisha bihagije!" Bose bicaye icyarimwe, mu mpeta nini, hamwe n'imbeba hagati. Alice yagumije amaso ye ahangayitse, kuko yumvaga azi neza ko azarwara imbeho mbi aramutse atumye vuba.

“Ahem!” ati Imbeba ifite umwuka wingenzi, "mwese mwiteguye? Iki nikintu cyumye cyane ndabizi. Guceceka impande zose, niba ubishaka!" William Umutsinzi, uwabitewe na papa, yahise ashyikirizwa nabongereza bifuzaga abayobozi, kandi yari yaratinze kumenyera kwigarurira no kwigarurira. Edwin na Morcar, amatwi ya Mercia.

“Ugh!” ati Lory, ahinda umushyitsi.

“Ndagusaba imbabazi!” Imbeba, yubitse amaso, ariko mu kinyabupfura cyane: “Wavuze?”

“Ntabwo ari njye!” ati Lory yihutiye.

Imbeba ati: "Natekereje ko wabikoze." “—Ndakomeza. 'Edwin na Morcar, impeta ya Mercia na Northumbria, baramutangarije: ndetse na Stigand, musenyeri mukuru ukunda igihugu cya Canterbury, yasanze ari byiza -'”

“Wabonye iki ?” ati Duck.

Imbeba yarashubije iti: " Byarabonye , birumvikana ko uzi icyo 'bisobanura'."

Duck yagize ati: "Nzi icyo 'bisobanura' bihagije, iyo mbonye ikintu," muri rusange ni igikeri cyangwa inyo. Ikibazo ni iki, musenyeri mukuru yabonye iki? "

Imbeba ntiyabonye iki kibazo, ariko yihutira gukomeza, ati: '' - ni byiza ko tujyana na Edgar Atheling guhura na William tukamuha ikamba. Imyitwarire ya William yabanje kuba ishyize mu gaciro. yarakomeje, ahindukirira Alice nkuko yavugaga.

Alice n'ijwi rirenga ati: “Nkuko bitose nk'uko bisanzwe, ntako bisa na gato.”

Dodo yishimye cyane ati: "Icyo gihe, ndimuka ko inama isubikwa, kugira ngo hafatwe ingamba nyinshi zifite ingufu -"

“Vuga Icyongereza!” ati Eaglet. “Sinzi icyo kimwe cya kabiri cy'ayo magambo maremare, kandi, ikindi, sinizera ko nawe ubikora!” Kandi Eaglet yunamye umutwe kugirango ihishe inseko: zimwe mu zindi nyoni zijegajega byumvikana.

Dodo mu ijwi rimubabaje yagize ati: “Icyo ngiye kuvuga, ni uko ikintu cyiza cyatuma twuma cyaba ubwoko bwa Caucus.”

“Ubwoko bwa Caucus ni iki?” Alice ati; ntabwo yashakaga kumenya byinshi, ariko Dodo yarahagaze nkaho yibwira ko umuntu agomba kuvuga, kandi ntawundi wasaga nkushaka kuvuga ikintu.

Dodo yagize ati: “Kuki, inzira nziza yo kubisobanura ari ukubikora.” (Kandi, nkuko ushobora kugerageza kugerageza ikintu wenyine, umunsi wizuba, nzakubwira uko Dodo yayoboye.)

Ubwa mbere byerekanaga isiganwa-ryamasiganwa, muburyo bumwe, ("imiterere nyayo ntacyo itwaye," yaravuze,) hanyuma ibirori byose bishyirwa kumurongo, aha naha. Nta “Umwe, babiri, batatu, na kure,” ariko batangiye kwiruka igihe babishakiye, bagenda iyo bashaka, ku buryo bitari byoroshye kumenya igihe isiganwa ryarangiye. Ariko, mugihe bari bamaze igice cyisaha cyangwa irenga, bakongera bakuma, Dodo yahise ahamagara ati "Irushanwa rirarangiye!" bose bararundarundarundarunda, barapanga, barabaza bati: "Ariko ninde watsinze?"

Iki kibazo Dodo ntiyashoboraga gusubiza atabanje kubitekerezaho cyane, maze yicara umwanya muremure urutoki rumwe rukanda ku gahanga (umwanya usanzwe ubona Shakespeare, mumashusho ye), mugihe abasigaye bategereje bucece. Amaherezo, Dodo yagize ati: " Abantu bose baratsinze, kandi bose bagomba kugira ibihembo."

“Ariko ni nde uzatanga ibihembo?” amakorari menshi y'amajwi yabajijwe.

Dodo ati: "Kuki, byanze bikunze", yerekeza Alice n'urutoki rumwe; maze ibirori byose icyarimwe bimuzenguruka, bahamagara mu rujijo, bati: "Ibihembo! Ibihembo!"

Alice ntabwo yari azi icyo gukora, maze yihebye ashyira ikiganza cye mu mufuka, akuramo agasanduku keza, (ku bw'amahirwe amazi y'umunyu ntabwo yari yarinjiyemo), maze abaha kuzenguruka nk'ibihembo. Hariho igice kimwe, impande zose.

Imbeba ati: "Ariko agomba kuba afite igihembo wenyine, urabizi."

Dodo aramusubiza ati: "Birumvikana." “Ni iki kindi wabonye mu mufuka wawe?” arakomeza, ahindukirira Alice.

Alice birababaje ati: "Gusa igikoma."

Dodo ati: “Tanga hano.”

Bose bongera kumuzenguruka bongeye kumuzenguruka, mu gihe Dodo yerekanaga igikonjo, ati: "Turagusaba ko wemera iyi nyito nziza;" kandi, imaze kurangiza iri jambo rigufi, bose barishima.

Alice yibwiraga ko ibintu byose bidasobanutse, ariko bose basaga nkaho ari imva kuburyo atinyutse guseka; kandi, kubera ko atashoboraga gutekereza ku kintu na kimwe avuga, yarunamye, afata igikoma, asa n'umuhango uko ashoboye.

Icyakurikiyeho ni ukurya ibyokurya: ibi byateje urusaku n’urujijo, kubera ko inyoni nini zinubira ko zidashobora kuryoherwa n’izabo, kandi ntoya ziniga kandi zigomba gukubitwa umugongo. Ariko, byarangiye, barongera baricara mu mpeta, basaba Imbeba kubabwira ikindi.

Yongeyeho ati: "Wasezeranije kumbwira amateka yawe, urabizi, n'impamvu wanga - C na D", yongeraho ati:

“Ibyanjye ni birebire kandi birababaje!” ati Imbeba, ihindukirira Alice, no kwishongora.

Alice yagize ati: " Ni umurizo muremure, rwose". “Ariko kubera iki mwita ko bibabaje?” Yakomeje kubyibazaho mugihe Imbeba yavugaga, kuburyo igitekerezo cye cyumugani cyari ikintu nkiki: -

“Ntabwo witabye!” yavuze Imbeba kuri Alice cyane. “Uratekereza iki?”

Alice yicishije bugufi cyane ati: “Ndagusabye imbabazi, wari ugeze ku ncuro ya gatanu, ndatekereza?”

Imbeba yatakambiye ati: "Sinari mfite! "

“Ipfundo!” ati Alice, burigihe yiteguye kwigira ingirakamaro, no kumureba ahangayitse. Ati: “Yoo, reka mfashe kubikuraho!”

Imbeba irahaguruka iragenda, ati: "Ntacyo nzakora." “Urantuka uvuga ibitagira umumaro!”

“Sinashakaga kuvuga!” yinginze umukene Alice. “Ariko urababaje byoroshye, urabizi!”

Imbeba yatontomye gusa isubiza.

“Nyamuneka garuka urangize inkuru yawe!” Alice yahamagaye nyuma yacyo; abandi bose bifatanya muri korari, “Yego, nyamuneka kora!” ariko Imbeba yazunguye umutwe gusa yihanganye, igenda vuba vuba.

“Mbega ishyano ntibyari kuguma!” asuhuza Lory, akimara kuboneka neza; n'umusaza Crab yaboneyeho umwanya wo kubwira umukobwa we ati "Ah, mukundwa! Reka ibi bikubere isomo ko utazigera urakara !" “Fata ururimi, Ma!” ati umusore Crab, acecetse gato. “Urahagije kugerageza kwihangana kwa oyster!”

Ati: "Icyampa nkagira Dina yacu hano, nzi ko mbikora!" ati Alice n'ijwi rirenga, ntawe abwira umuntu byumwihariko. “Yahise ayigarura!”

“Kandi Dina ni nde, niba nshobora kwihanganira kubaza ikibazo?” ati Lory.

Alice yashubije ashishikaye, kuko yahoraga yiteguye kuvuga kubyerekeye itungo rye ati: "injangwe ya Dina. Kandi ni umurwa mukuru nk'uwo gufata imbeba ntushobora gutekereza! Kandi yewe, icyampa ukamubona nyuma y'inyoni! Kubera iki, azarya inyoni nto akimara kuyireba!"

Iri jambo ryateje ishyaka ridasanzwe mu ishyaka. Zimwe mu nyoni zihutiye guhita: Magpie umwe ushaje yatangiye kwizinga yitonze, agira ati: "Mu byukuri ngomba gutaha; umwuka wijoro ntukwiranye n'umuhogo wanjye!" na Canary yahamagaye mu ijwi rihinda umushyitsi abana bayo, ati: “Genda, bakundwa! Igihe kirageze ko mwese muryama!” Urwitwazo rutandukanye bose barimutse, Alice yahise asigara wenyine.

“Icyampa nkaba ntavuze Dina!” aribwira mu ijwi riranguruye. Ati: "Ntamuntu numwe usa nkumukunda, hepfo aha, kandi nzi neza ko ari injangwe nziza kwisi! Oh, mukundwa Dina! Ndibaza niba ntazongera kukubona!" Kandi hano umukene Alice yatangiye kongera kurira, kuko yumvaga afite irungu cyane kandi afite umutima muto. Mu gihe gito ariko, yongeye kumva akantu gato ko gukandagira ikirenge kure, maze yubura amaso ashishikaye, kimwe cya kabiri yizera ko Imbeba yahinduye imitekerereze, akagaruka kurangiza inkuru ye.
