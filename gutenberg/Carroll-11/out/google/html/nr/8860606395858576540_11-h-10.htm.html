ISAHLUKO X.
I-Lobster Quadrille

I-Mock Turtle yabubula khulu, begodu yadonsa umhlana we-flapper ngaphetjheya kwamehlo wayo. Waqala u-Alice, walinga ukukhuluma, kodwana umzuzu owodwa nofana emibili ukukhala kwavala ilizwi lakhe. "Njengokungathi unethambo emphinjeni wakhe," kwatjho i-Gryphon: begodu yathoma ukusebenza ukumnyakazisa nokumshaya ngemuva. Ekugcineni i-Mock Turtle yabuyisa ilizwi layo, begodu, iinyembezi zigeleza ezihlathini zakhe, yaragela phambili godu:—

"Kungenzeka bona akhenge uphile khulu ngaphasi kwelwandle—" ("Angikaze," kwatjho u-Alice)—"begodu mhlamunye awukaze waziswa nge-lobster—" (u-Alice wathoma ukuthi "Ngake nganambitha—" kodwana wazihlola msinyana, wathi "Awa, azange") "-ngalokho awukwazi ukwazi bona i-Lobster Quadrille yinto emnandi kangangani!"

"Awa, kwamambala," kwatjho u-Alice. “Ngumgido onjani lo?”

"Kubayini," kwatjho i-Gryphon, "uqala ngokwenza umugqa ngaseceleni kwelwandle—"

“Imigqa emibili!” kwamemeza uFudu okhohlakeleko. “Iimfesi, amafudu, i-salmon, njalonjalo; bese, nasele ususe woke ama-jelly-fish endleleni—”

" Lokho kuvamise ukuthatha isikhathi," kwaphazamisa i-Gryphon.

“—uya phambili kabili—”

"Omunye nomunye une-lobster njengomlingani!" kwamemeza i-Gryphon.

"Kunjalo," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle: "qhubekela phambili kabili, ubeke kubalingani—"

"—tjhugulula ama-lobster, bese uthatha umhlalaphasi ngokulandelana okufanako," kwaragela phambili i-Gryphon.

"Bese, uyazi," i-Mock Turtle iragela phambili, "uphosa—"

“Ama-lobster!” kwamemeza i-Gryphon, ngokubophelela emoyeni.

“—kude khulu elwandle ngendlela ongakghona ngayo—”

“Bhukuda ngemva kwabo!” kwamemeza i-Gryphon.

“Phendukela elwandle!” kwamemeza i-Mock Turtle, iphaphatheka khulu.

“Tjhugulula ama-lobster godu!” kwamemeza i-Gryphon ngelizwi eliphezulu.

"Buyela emhlabathini godu, begodu lokho kusibalo sokuthoma," kwatjho uFudu, ngokuzumako wehlisa ilizwi lakhe; begodu iinlwana ezimbili, ebegade zigxuma njengezinto ezihlanyako isikhathi soke, zahlala phasi godu zidabukile begodu zithulile, zaqala u-Alice.

"Kufanele kube mdanso omuhle khulu," kwatjho u-Alice ngokuba namahloni.

“Ungathanda ukubona okuncani kwayo?” kwatjho uFudu okhohlakeleko.

"Kukhulu khulu," kwatjho u-Alice.

“Woza, akhe sizame isibalo sokuthoma!” kwatjho i-Mock Turtle ku-Gryphon. "Singenza ngaphandle kwama-lobster, uyazi. Ngiyiphi ezakuvuma?"

"Hawu, uyavuma ," kwatjho i-Gryphon. “Ngikhohliwe amagama.”

Njeke bathoma ukudansa ngokuzithoba bazungeza u-Alice, qobe nje banyathela ngeenzwane zakhe lokha nabadlula eduze khulu, begodu baphakamisa iinyawo zabo zangaphambili ukutshwaya isikhathi, ngesikhathi i-Mock Turtle ivuma lokhu, kancanikancani begodu ngokudabukisako:—

“Ungakghona ukukhamba msinya kancani?” kwatjho umhlophe kumnenke.
“Kune-porpoise eduze ngemva kwethu, begodu inyathela umsila wami.
Bona bona ama-lobster namafudu woke aragela phambili ngokuzimisela kangangani!
Balinde phezu kwesigodlo—ungafika uzohlanganyela emdansweni?
Ingabe, angeze, angeze, angeze, uzokujoyina umdanso?
Ingabe, angeze, angeze, angeze, angeze wajoyina umgido?

“Angeze wazi bona kuzokuba mnandi kangangani
Lokha nabasithatha basilahlele, nama-lobster, elwandle!”
Kodwana umnenke waphendula wathi "Kude khulu, kude khulu!" begodu waqala ngokutjhingela—
Uthe wathokoza umhlophe ngomusa, kodwana azange ajoyine umdanso.
Bengekhe, bengeze, bengeze, bengeze, bengeze bajoyine umdanso.
Bengekhe, bengeze, bengeze, bengeze, bengeze bajoyina umdanso.

"Khuyini okuqakathekileko ukuthi siya kude kangangani?" umnganakhe oneenhluthu waphendula.
“Kunelinye iligu, uyazi, ngakelinye ihlangothi.
Ukude ne-England kuseduze ne-France—
Bese ungaphenduki mnyama, umnenke othandekako, kodwana woza uzokuhlanganyela emdansweni.
Ingabe, angeze, angeze, angeze, uzokujoyina umdanso?
Uzokwenza, awuvumi, uzokwenza, awuvumi, angeze ujoyine umgido?”

"Ngiyathokoza, ngumdanso othakazelisako ukuwubukela," kwatjho u-Alice, azizwa athokoza khulu ngokuthi sele uphelile: "begodu ngiyayithanda ingoma leyo ekhuluma nge-white!"

"Hawu, malungana nokutjhwaba," kwatjho iFudu, "zona—uke wazibona, nakanjani?"

"Iye," kwatjho u-Alice, "Ngivame ukubabona esidlweni—" wazihlola msinyana.

"Angazi bona u-Dinn angaba kuphi," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle, "kodwana nayikuthi ubabone kanengi kangaka, uyazi bona banjani."

"Ngiyakholwa," u-Alice waphendula ngokucabanga. “Zinemisila emlonyeni wazo—begodu ziphezu kweemvuthuluka.”

"Uyaphambanisa ngeemvuthuluka," kwatjho iFudu: "Iimvuthuluka zingahlanzwa zoke elwandle. Kodwana zinemisila emlonyeni wazo; begodu isizathu kukuthi—" lapha iFudu ePhekisako yazamula begodu yavala amehlo.—"Mtjele ngesizathu nakho koke lokho," watjho kuGryphon.

"Isizathu kukuthi," kwatjho i-Gryphon, "ukuthi bebazokuya nama-lobster ukuya emdansweni. Njeke baphoselwa elwandle. Njeke kwafanela bona bawele ibanga elide. Njeke bathola imisila yabo msinyana emlonyeni wazo. Njeke azange bakghone ukuyikhupha godu. Yilokho kwaphela."

“Ngiyathokoza,” kutjho u-Alice, “kuthakazelisa khulu. Bengingakaze ngazi okunengi kangaka nge-whiting ngaphambilini.”

"Ngingakutjela okungaphezu kwalokho, nangabe uyathanda," kwatjho i-Gryphon. “Uyazi bona kubayini kuthiwa yi-whiting?”

"Angikaze ngicabange ngakho," kwatjho u-Alice. "Kubayini?"

" Yenza amabhuzu nezicathulo ," i-Gryphon yaphendula ngokuzithoba.

U-Alice bekadidekile khulu. “Ingabe amabhuzu nezicathulo!” waphindaphinda ngephimbo elimangalisako.

“Kubayini, iincathulo zakho zenziwe ngani?” kwatjho i-Gryphon. “Ngitjho ukuthi, khuyini eyenza bona zikhanye kangaka?”

U-Alice wabaqala phasi, begodu wacabanga kancani ngaphambi kobana anikele ipendulo yakhe. “Baqedile ngokumnyama, ngiyakholwa.”

"Amabhuzu nezicathulo ngaphasi kwelwandle," uGryphon waragela phambili ngelizwi elijulileko, "sele aphelile ngokutjhwaba. Nje-ke uyazi."

“Begodu zenziwe ngani?” U-Alice wabuza ngephimbo lokufuna ukwazi okukhulu.

"Amasole nama-eel, vele," i-Gryphon yaphendula ngokuphelelwa sineke: "nanyana ngiyiphi i-shrimp beyingakutjela lokho."

"Ngathana bengingumuntu omhlophe," kwatjho u-Alice, imicabango yakhe beyisagijima ngengoma, "Ngabe ngithi ku-porpoise, 'Buyela emuva, sibawa: asifuni bona ube nathi!'"

"Bebabophekile bona babe naye," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle: "akukho mfesi ohlakaniphileko ongaya nanyana kukuphi ngaphandle kwe-porpoise."

“Bekungeke kube njalo kwamambala?” Kutjho u-Alice ngephimbo lokumangala okukhulu.

"Awa," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle: "kubayini, nangabe ifesi iza kimi , ingitjela bona iya ekhambweni, ngithi 'Ngiyiphi i-porpoise?'"

“Awutjho 'umnqopho'?” Kutjho u-Alice.

"Ngitjho lokho engikutjhoko," kuphendula i-Mock Turtle ngephimbo elikhubekileko. Begodu i-Gryphon yengezelela “Woza, akhe sizwe ezinye zezinto zakho .”

"Ngingakutjela ngeenkhathi zami—ukuthoma namhlanje ekuseni," kwatjho u-Alice ngokuba neenhloni: "kodwana akusizi ngalitho ukubuyela emuva izolo, ngombana bengingumuntu ohlukileko ngesikhatheso."

"Hlathulula koke lokho," kwatjho uFudu.

"Awa, awa! Ama-adventures qangi," kwatjho i-Gryphon ngelizwi elinganasineke: "iinhlathululo zithatha isikhathi esimbi kangaka."

Ngalokho u-Alice wathoma ukubatjela ngeenkhathi zakhe kusukela ngesikhathi aqala ukubona uMvundla omhlophe. Bekathukwe kancani ngalokho ekuthomeni, iinlwana ezimbili zatjhidela kuye, enye ngehlangothini ngalinye, begodu zavula amehlo nemilomo yazo khulu , kodwana waba nesibindi njengombana aragela phambili. Abalaleli bakhe bebathule khulu bekwaba kulapho afika khona engcenyeni ekhuluma ngokuthi " Umdala, Baba uWilliam ," ku-Caterpillar, begodu amagama woke avela ngokuhlukileko, bese i-Mock Turtle yadosa ummoya omude, yathi "Lokho kuyamangalisa."

"Kumayelana nokufuna ukwazi," kwatjho i-Gryphon.

"Koke kwavela ngokuhlukileko!" i-Mock Turtle yaphindaphinda ngokucabanga. "Ngingathanda ukumuzwa alinga ukuphinda okuthileko nje. Mtjele bona athome." Waqala i-Gryphon ngasuthi bekacabanga bona inegunya elithileko phezu kuka-Alice.

"Sukuma uphinde uthi '' Lilizwi lomuntu ovilaphako ,'" kwatjho iGryphon.

"Indlela izidalwa eziyalela ngayo umuntu, begodu zenze umuntu aphindaphinda iimfundo!" kwacabanga u-Alice; "Kungenzeka ngibe sesikolweni ngaso leso sikhathi." Kodwana, wasukuma, wathoma ukuyiphindaphinda, kodwana ihlokwakhe beyizele i-Lobster Quadrille, kangangobana bekangazi bona uthini, begodu amezwi la afika angakajayeleki kwamambala:—

“'Lilizwi le-Lobster; ngimuzwe athi,
“Ungibhake ngibe nsundu khulu, kufuze ngifake itjhukela eenhluthwini zami.”
Njengedada elinamajwabu amehlo, kanjalo naye ngekhala lakhe
Usika ibhande lakhe namabhathini wakhe, bese ukhipha iinzwane zakhe.”

"Lokho kuhlukile kilokho ebengikukhuluma ngisese mntwana," kwatjho i-Gryphon.

"Kulungile, azange ngikuzwe ngaphambilini," kwatjho uFudu; "kodwana kuzwakala kungakajayeleki."

U-Alice akhenge atjho litho; bekahlezi phasi ubuso bakhe buphethe ezandleni zakhe, azibuza bona kukhona na okuzokwenzeka ngendlela yemvelo godu.

"Ngingathanda bona kuhlathululwe," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle.

“Yisikhundla sokuthoma ekudanseni.” U-Alice wathi; kodwana bekadidekile khulu yinto yoke, begodu bekafuna ukutjhugulula indaba.

"Ragela phambili ngevesi elilandelako," i-Gryphon yaphindaphinda ngokuphelelwa sineke: "ithoma ngokuthi ' Ngidlule engadini yakhe .'"

“Ngadlula engadini yakhe, ngaphawula, ngelihlo elilodwa,
Indlela i-Owl ne-Panther ebegade bahlanganyela ngayo iphayi—”
