ISAHLUKO VIII.
Indawo ye-Croquet yeNdlovukazi

Umuthi omkhulu wamarozi bewujame eduze nomnyango wensimu: amarozi akhula kiwo bekamhlophe, kodwana bekunabalimi abathathu kiwo, bawapenda abe bomvu. U-Alice wacabanga bona lokhu bekuyinto emangalisako, begodu watjhidela eduze wayokubaqala, begodu nakatjhidela kibo wezwa omunye wabo athi, “Qala nje, Five! Ungakhambi ungifafaze ngepende ngaleyo ndlela!”

"Angikghoni ukuzibamba," kwatjho u-Five, ngelizwi elinolaka; “Isikhombisa yangigijima ngendololwane.”

Kwathi u-Seven waqala phezulu wathi, "Kunjalo, Five! Hlala ubeka abanye icala!"

“ Kungcono ungakhulumi!” kwatjho u-Five. “Ngizwe iNdlovukazi ithi izolo kwaphela bewufanelwe kuqunywa ikhanda!”

“Yini?” Kutsho lowo owayekhulume kuqala.

“Akusindaba yakho lokho, Mbili!” kwatjho u-Seven.

“Iye, yindaba yakhe!” kwatjho u-Five, "begodu ngizomtjela—bekungokuletha umpheki imirabhu ye-tulip esikhundleni se-anyanisi."

U-Seven waphosa ibhrashi lakhe phasi, begodu bekasanda kuthoma “Kulungile, kizo zoke izinto ezingakalungi—” lokha ilihlo lakhe naliwela ku-Alice, njengombana ajame ababukele, begodu wazihlola msinyana: nabanye baqala ngapha nangapha, begodu boke bathoma phasi.

"Ungangitjela," kwatjho u-Alice, ngokuba namahloni, "kubayini upenda amarozi lawo?"

U-Five no-Seven akhenge batjho litho, kodwana baqala u-Two. Ababili bathoma ngelizwi eliphasi, "Kubayini iqiniso kukuthi, uyabona, Mnumzana, lokhu lapha bekufanele kube muthi we-rose obomvu , begodu sifake omhlophe ngephutha; begodu nangabe iNdlovukazi beyizokufumana, bekufuze soke siqunywe iinhloko zethu, uyazi. Njeke uyabona, Mnumzana, senza koke okusemandleni wethu, ngaphambi kobana eze"— Ngalesi sikhathi, u-Five, begade akhangele ngaphetjheya kwesivande, wamemeza "INdlovukazi! Indlovukazi!" begodu abalimi abathathu bazilahla phasi ngobuso babo. Kwazwakala umdumo weenyawo ezinengi, begodu u-Alice waqalaza, afuna ukubona iKosikazi.

Kokuthoma kwafika amasotja alitjhumi aphethe iintonga; zoke lezi bezibunjwe njengabalimi abathathu, zide begodu ziphaphathekile, ngezandla neenyawo zazo emakhoneni: kulandela amakhosi alitjhumi; lezi bezihlotjiswe ngamadayimani woke, begodu bezikhamba ngababili ngababili, njengombana kwenza amasotja. Ngemva kwalokhu kwafika abantwana bekosi; bezilitjhumi, begodu abathandekako abancani beza begxuma ngokuthaba babambene ngezandla, ngababili: boke bebahlotjiswe ngeenhliziyo. Kwalandela iimvakatjhi, inengi lazo bekumaKhosi namaKhosikazi, begodu hlangana nazo u-Alice wabona uMvundla oMhlophe: bekakhuluma ngendlela ephuthumako, amomotheka kikho koke ebekutjhiwo, begodu wadlula ngaphandle kokumtjheja. Kwabe sekulandela i-Knave of Hearts, ithwele umqhele weKosi phezu komcamelo wevelvethi obomvu; begodu, ekugcineni kwawo woke umkhankaso omkhulu, kwafika iKOSI NENKOSIkazi YEZINHLIZIYO.

U-Alice bekangabaza bona akukafaneli na alale phasi ngobuso bakhe njengabalimi abathathu, kodwana bekangakghoni ukukhumbula bona wake wezwa ngomthetho onjalo emigidini; "Begodu ngaphandle kwalokho, bekungasiza ngani umkhambatho," wacabanga, "nangabe abantu bekufuze balale phasi ngobuso babo, ukwenzela bona bangakghoni ukukubona?" Nje-ke wema lapha gade akhona, walinda.

Lokha ikhambo nalifika maqondana no-Alice, boke bajama bamqala, begodu iNdlovukazi yathi "Ngubani lo?" Watjho lokho ku-Knave of Hearts, owathoma wamomotheka nakaphendulako.

"Isidlhadlha!" kwatjho iKosikazi, iphakamisela ihlokwayo ngokuphelelwa sineke; begodu, aphendukela ku-Alice, waragela phambili, “Ungubani ibizo lakho, mntanami?”

"Ibizo lami ngingu-Alice, ngiyathokoza Mhlonitjhwa," kwatjho u-Alice ngesizotha; kodwana wangezelela, kuye, "Kungani, ziyiphakethe lamakhadi kwaphela, phela. Akukafaneli ngizisabe!"

"Njalo bobani laba? " kwatjho iNdlovukazi, ikhomba abalimi abathathu ebegade balale eduze kwesihlahla se-rose; ngombana, uyabona, njengombana bebalele ngobuso babo, begodu iphetheni emhlana wabo beyifana nayo yoke isiqhema, bekangakghoni ukutjho bona bebabalimi, namkha amasotja, namkha amakhosi, namkha abantwana bakhe abathathu.

“Kufanele ngazi njani?” Kutjho u-Alice, ararwe ngesibindi sakhe. “Akusiyo indaba yami .”

INdlovukazi yatjhuguluka yaba bomvu ngokuthukuthela, begodu, ngemva kokuyiqala isikhatjhana njengesilwana, yarhuwelela "Susa ngehloko! Susa—"

“Umbhedo!” kwatjho u-Alice, ngokuzwakalako begodu ngokuzimisela, begodu iKosikazi yathula.

INkosi yabeka isandla sayo engalweni yakhe, begodu ngamahloni yathi "Cabanga, sthandwa sami: umntwana nje!"

INdlovukazi yafulathela kuye ngokuthukuthela, yathi ku-Knave "Baphendule!"

I-Knave yenza njalo, ngokutjheja khulu, ngonyawo olulodwa.

"Sikima!" kwatjho iNdlovukazi, ngephimbo elikhulu, begodu abalimi abathathu bavele baphakama, bathoma ukukhothamela iKosi, iNdlovukazi, abantwana bekosi, nabo boke abanye abantu.

“Tjhiya lokho!” kwamemeza iKosikazi. “Ungenza ngibe nesiyezi.” Bese, waphendukela emthini wamarozi, waragela phambili, " Bewenzani lapha?"

"Kwangathi kungathokozisa uMhlonitjhwa," kwatjho u-Two, ngephimbo elithobekileko, waguqa ngedolo elilodwa njengombana akhuluma, "besilinga—"

" Ngiyabona !" kwatjho iKosikazi, ebegade ihlola amarozi. “Susa amakhanda wabo!” begodu umkhambatho waragela phambili, amasotja amathathu asala ngemva ukubulala abalimi beengadini abangenathemba, abagijimela ku-Alice ukuyokuvikela.

“Angeze waqunywa ikhanda!” kwatjho u-Alice, begodu wazifaka embizeni ekulu yamathuthumbo ebegade ijame eduze. Amasotja amathathu akhambahamba umzuzu owodwa nofana emibili, abafuna, bese akhamba ngokuthula alandela amanye.

“Ingabe iinhloko zabo ziphumile?” kwamemeza iKosikazi.

“Iinhloko zabo ziphelile, nangabe kuthokozisa uMhlonitjhwa wakho!” amasotja arhuwelela aphendula.

“Kunjalo!” kwamemeza iKosikazi. “Ungakwazi ukudlala i-croquet?”

Amasotja bekathule, begodu aqala u-Alice, ngombana umbuzo bewuhloselwe yena.

"Iye!" kwamemeza u-Alice.

“Woza-ke!” kwarhuwelela iKosikazi, begodu u-Alice wajoyina umkhambatho, azibuza khulu bona kuzokwenzekani ngokulandelako.

“Lilanga elihle khulu!” kwatjho ilizwi elinamahloni eceleni kwakhe. Bekhamba eduze noMvundla omhlophe, obegade amhlola ebusweni ngokutshwenyeka.

"Kuhle khulu," kwatjho u-Alice: "—uphi iNkosikazi?"

“Thula! Thula!” Kutjho uMvundla ngephimbo eliphasi, elirhabako. Waqala ngokutshwenyeka ngaphezulu kwehlombe lakhe njengombana akhuluma, bese waziphakamisa ngeenyawo, wabeka umlomo wakhe eduze nendlebe yakhe, begodu wahleba "Ungaphasi kwesigwebo sokubulawa."

“Yini?” Kutjho u-Alice.

“Uthe 'Kuyadabukisa!'?” kubuza uMvundla.

“Awa, angizange,” kutjho u-Alice: “Angicabangi bona kulizwela. Ngathi 'Ngikwenzelani?'”

"Wabopha iindlebe zeNdlovukazi—" uMvundla wathoma. U-Alice warhuwelela kancani ngokuhleka. “Hawu, thula!” uMvundla wahleba ngephimbo elithukileko. "INdlovukazi izokuzwa! Uyabona, ifike ngemva kwesikhathi, begodu iNdlovukazi yathi—"

“Hambani niye eendaweni zenu!” kwamemeza iKosikazi ngephimbo lokuduma, begodu abantu bathoma ukugijima kizo zoke iindawo, bawelana; nanyana kunjalo, bahlala phasi ngomzuzu owodwa nofana emibili, begodu umdlalo wathoma. U-Alice bekacabanga bona azange abone indawo yokudlala i-croquet enjalo epilwenakhe; bekuyimigodi nemisele; iibholo bekuma-hedgehog aphilako, ama-mallets bekuma-flamingo aphilako, begodu amasotja bekufuze aziphinde kabili begodu ajame ngezandla neenyawo zawo, ukwenza ama-arch.

Ubudisi obukhulu u-Alice abutholako ekuthomeni bekukulawula i-flamingo yakhe: waphumelela ukufihla umzimba wayo, ngokuzigedla ngokwaneleko, ngaphasi kwengalo yakhe, imilenze yayo ilenga phasi, kodwana ngokuvamileko, njengombana sele aqondise intamo yayo kuhle, begodu gade azokushaya i-hedgehog ngehloko yayo, beyijikajika begodu iqale phezulu, inesimo sokudideka ukuthi bekangakghoni ukuzibamba ngokuhleka: begodu lokha nakabeka ihlokwakhe phasi, begodu sele azokuthoma godu, kwaba buhlungu khulu ukuthola bona i-hedgehog sele izivulele, begodu besele ikhasa: ngaphandle kwakho koke lokhu, bekuvamise ukuba nomrhoba nofana umsele endleleni nanyana kukuphi lapho bekafuna ukuthumela khona i-hedgehog, begodu, njengombana amasotja bekahlala avuka begodu akhamba kabili ezinye iindawo, u-Alice msinyana wafika esiphethweni sokuthi bekumdlalo onzima khulu kwamambala.

Abadlali boke badlala ngesikhathi sinye ngaphandle kokulinda amatjhuguluko, baphikisana ngaso soke isikhathi, begodu balwela ama-hedgehog; begodu ngesikhathi esifitjhani khulu iNdlovukazi yaba nethando elikhulu, begodu yahamba inyathela, yarhuwelela “Susa ngehloko yakhe!” nofana "Susa ngehloko yakhe!" pheze kanye ngomzuzu.

U-Alice wathoma ukuzizwa angakhululeki: ukuqiniseka, bekangakabi nokuphikisana neNdlovukazi, kodwana bekazi bona kungenzeka nanyana kunini, "bese," wacabanga, "kuzokwenzekani kimi? Bathanda khulu ukuquma iinhloko zabantu lapha; isimanga esikhulu kukuthi, kukhona osele aphila!"

Bekafuna indlela yokuphunyuka, begodu azibuza bona angabalekela na ngaphandle kokubonwa, lokha nakabona into ebonakalako emoyeni: kwamdida khulu ekuthomeni, kodwana, ngemva kokuyibukela umzuzu owodwa nofana emibili, wakwenza kwaba yinto ebobothekako, begodu wathi kuye “Ngu-Cheshire Cat: nje ngizokuba nomuntu engizokukhuluma naye.”

“Unjani?” kwatjho iKati, msinyana nasele inomlomo owaneleko bona ikhulume nayo.

U-Alice walinda bekwaba kulapho amehlo avela khona, bese wavuma ngehloko. "Akusizi ngalitho ukukhuluma nayo," wacabanga, "bekube kulapho iindlebe zayo zifike, nofana okungenani enye yazo." Ngomunye umzuzu ikhanda loke lavela, bese u-Alice wabeka phasi i-flamingo yakhe, wathoma ukulandisa ngomdlalo, azizwa athabile khulu ngokuthi unomuntu ozomlalela. Ikati begade icabanga bona sele ikhona okwaneleko, begodu azange ivele.

"Angicabangi bona badlala kuhle," u-Alice wathoma, ngephimbo lokukhonona, "begodu boke baphikisana khulu kangangobana umuntu angekhe azizwe akhuluma—begodu kubonakala ngasuthi abanayo imithetho ethileko; okungenani, nangabe ikhona, akekho umuntu oyitjhejako—begodu awunalwazi lokuthi kuphazamisa kangangani koke izinto eziphilako; hamba ngokulandelako ngakelinye ihlangothi lomhlabathi—begodu bekufanele ngibethe i-hedgehog yeNdlovukazi nje, kodwana yabaleka lokha nayibona eyami iza!”

“Uyithanda njani iNdlovukazi?” Kutjho iKati ngephimbo eliphasi.

"Awa," kwatjho u-Alice: "ukhulu khulu—" Ngaleso sikhathi wabona bona iKosikazi beyiseduze ngemva kwakhe, ilalele: yeke waragela phambili, "—kungenzeka bona iphumelele, ukuthi akusizi ngalitho lokha nakaqeda umdlalo."

INdlovukazi yamomotheka yadlula.

“ Ukhuluma nobani?” kwatjho iKosi, ikhuphukela ku-Alice, begodu iqala ikhanda leKati ngokufuna ukwazi okukhulu.

“Ngumnganami—i-Cheshire Cat,” kwatjho u-Alice: “ngivumele ngiyethule.”

"Angithandi ukubukeka kwayo," kwatjho iKosi: "nanyana kunjalo, ingaqabula isandla sami nayithandako."

"Ngingakhetha ukungabi," kuphawula iKati.

“Ungabi lihlanya,” kwatjho iKosi, “begodu ungangiqali njalo!” Wafika ngemva kuka-Alice njengombana akhuluma.

"Ikati lingaqala ikosi," kwatjho u-Alice. “Ngike ngakufunda lokho kwenye incwadi, kodwana angisakhumbuli bona ngikuphi.”

"Kulungile, kufanele isuswe," kwatjho iKosi ngokuzimisela, begodu yabiza iNdlovukazi, ebeyidlula ngesikhatheso, "Sthandwa sami! Ngiyafisa ngathana ungakhipha ikati leli!"

INdlovukazi beyinendlela yinye yokurarulula yoke imiraro, emikhulu nofana emincani. “Susa ngehloko yakhe!” watjho, ngaphandle kokuqala ngapha nangapha.

“Ngizokuthatha umbulali ngokwami,” kwatjho iKosi ngokuzimisela, begodu yaphuthuma.

U-Alice wacabanga bona kungcono abuyele emuva, abone bona umdlalo uragela phambili njani, njengombana wezwa ilizwi leNdlovukazi kude, arhuwulela ngethando. Besele ezwile isigwebo sakhe sokuthi abadlali abathathu babulawe ngokuphuthelwa lithuba labo, begodu bekangathandi ukubukeka kwezinto, ngombana umdlalo bewuhlangahlangene kangangobana bekangazi bona bekulithuba lakhe namkha awa. Njeke wayokufuna i-hedgehog yakhe.

I-hedgehog beyilwa nenye i-hedgehog, okwabonakala ku-Alice njengethuba elihle khulu lokurhurhula enye yazo nenye: ubudisi bekukukuthi, i-flamingo yakhe besele ikhambe ngakelinye ihlangothi lesimu, lapho u-Alice bekayibona ilinga ngendlela engasizi ngalitho ukuphapha phezulu esihlahleni.

Ngesikhathi abamba i-flamingo wabuya nayo, ipi besele iphelile, begodu zombili iimfesi bezingasabonakali: "kodwana akunandaba," kwacabanga u-Alice, "njengombana zoke iingodo sele ziphelile." Ngalokho wayifihla ngaphasi kwengalo yakhe, ukwenzela bona ingaphunyuki godu, wabuyela emuva ukuyokucoca nomnganakhe.

Lokha nakabuyela e-Cheshire Cat, wamangala ukuthola isiqubuthu esikhulu sibuthene kiyo: bekunokuphikisana hlangana nombulali, iKosi, neNdlovukazi, ebegade bakhuluma ngasikhathi sinye, kuthi boke abanye bebathule, begodu babonakala bangakhululekile.

Ngesikhathi u-Alice avela, wacelwa bobathathu bona baphendule umbuzo, begodu baphindaphinda iimpikiswano zabo kuye, nanyana kunjalo, njengombana boke bakhuluma ngasikhathi sinye, wakuthola kubudisi khulu ukuzwisisa bona bathini.

Iphikiswano lombulali bekukuthi, awukwazi ukuquma ihloko ngaphandle kobana kube nomzimba ongasiquma kiwo: ukuthi bekangakaze enze into enjalo ngaphambilini, begodu bekangasoze athome ngesikhathi sakhe sokuphila.

Iphikiswano leKosi bekukuthi, nanyana yini enehloko ingaqunywa ikhanda, begodu akukafaneli ukhulume umraro.

Iphikiswano leNdlovukazi bekukuthi, nangabe kukhona okungakenziwa ngakho ngesikhatjhana nje, izokwenza bona woke umuntu abulawe, woke umuntu. (Kwaba kukuphawula kokugcina okwenze bona isiqhema soke sibonakale sikhulu begodu sikhathazekile.)

U-Alice bekangakghoni ukucabanga okunye angakutjho ngaphandle kokuthi "NgekaDuchess: kungcono umbuze ngakho."

"Usejele," iNdlovukazi yathi kumbulali: "mlande lapha." Umbulali wasuka njengomcibisholo.

Ikhanda leKati lathoma ukufiphala ngesikhatjhana sele lihambile, begodu, ngesikhathi abuya ne-Duchess, besele linyamalele ngokupheleleko; njeke iKosi nombulali bagijimela phezulu naphasi bayifuna, ngesikhathi boke abantu babuyela emdlalweni.
