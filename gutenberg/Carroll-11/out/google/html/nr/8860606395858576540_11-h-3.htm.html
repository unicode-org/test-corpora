ISAHLUKO III.
Umjarho we-Caucus kanye nendaba ede

Kwamambala bebayiqembu elibukeka lingakajayeleki ebelibuthene ebhange—iinyoni ezineensiba ezirhuqiweko, iinlwana ezinoboya bazo bubambelele eduze kwazo, begodu zoke ziconsa zimanzi, ziphambene, begodu zingakhululeki.

Umbuzo wokuthoma vele bekukuthi, uzokoma njani godu: baba nokubonisana ngalokhu, begodu ngemva kwemizuzu embalwa kwabonakala kungokwemvelo ku-Alice ukuzifumana akhuluma nabo, kube ngasuthi bekabazi ipilo yakhe yoke. Kwamambala, waba nokuphikisana isikhathi eside no-Lory, owagcina sele aphenduke umsindo, begodu wathi, "Ngimdala kunawe, begodu kufuze ngazi ngcono;" begodu u-Alice bekangavumi ngaphandle kokwazi bona gade indala kangangani, begodu, njengombana i-Lory yala ukutjela iminyaka yayo, bekunganakho okunye ebekungatjhiwo.

Ekugcineni igundane, ebegade libonakala lingumuntu onegunya hlangana nabo, lamemeza, "Hlalani phasi, noke, ningilalele! Ngizokwenza bona wome ngokwaneleko msinyana!" Bahlala phasi boke ngasikhathi sinye, endaweni ekulu, igundane liphakathi. U-Alice wahlala amehlo wakhe aqale kiwo, ngombana bekazizwa aqiniseka bona uzokubanjwa mkhuhlane omumbi nangabe azange wome msinyana.

“Ahem!” kwatjho igundane ngomoya oqakathekileko, "nikulungele na? Lokhu yinto eyomileko engiyaziko. Ukuthula koke, nangabe uyathanda! 'U-William uMnqobi, ogade athandwa ngupapa, msinyana wavunyelwa maNgisi, ebegade afuna abarholi, begodu besele ajwayele ukuthumba nokunqoba, u-Edwin no-Morbriciam weNyakatho'"

“Ugh!” Kutjho u-Lory, ngokuthuthumela.

“Ngiyakuncenga ukulibalela!” kwatjho igundane, lihlikihla, kodwana ngesizotha: "Ukhulumile na?"

“Akusi mina!” Kutjho u-Lory ngokurhabako.

"Bengicabanga bona wenza njalo," kwatjho iGundwane. “—Ngiragela phambili. 'U-Edwin no-Morcar, ama-earls we-Mercia ne-Nortumbria, bamemezela: begodu ngitjho no-Stigand, umbhishobhi omkhulu we-Canterbury, wakuthola kuhle—'”

“Uthole ini ?” Kutjho uDada.

" Ngiyitholile ," iGundwane laphendula ngokuhlambalaza: "yebo uyazi bona 'litjho' kutjho ukuthini."

"Ngiyazi kuhle bona kutjho ukuthini, lokha nangifumana into," kwatjho iDada: "ngokuvamileko kuba sixoxo nofana isibungu. Umbuzo kukuthi, umbhishobhi omkhulu utholeni?"

Igundwane akhenge liwunanzelele umbuzo lo, kodwana laragela phambili ngokurhabako, "'—wabona kulungile ukukhamba no-Edgar Atheling ukuyokuhlangana no-William begodu amnikele umqhele. Ukuziphatha kwaka-William ekuthomeni bekulinganiselwe. Kodwana ukuhlambalaza kwama-Norman wakhe—' Unjani nje nje, sthandwa sami?" yaragela phambili, yaphendukela ku-Alice njengombana ikhuluma.

"Njengombana ngimanzi njengakuqala," kwatjho u-Alice ngephimbo elidabukisako: "akubonakali kungiwomisi."

"Nakunjalo," kwatjho i-Dodo ngokuzithoba, isukuma ngeenyawo zayo, "Ngiphakamisa bona umhlangano uhlehliswe, ukwenzela ukwamukelwa kwamakhambi anamandla khulu—"

“Khuluma isiNgisi!” kwatjho uKhozi. “Angazi ihlathululo yamagama amade, begodu, okunye, angikholwa bona nawe uyazi!” I-Eaglet yagoba ihlokwayo ukufihla ukumomotheka: ezinye zeenyoni zathithibala khulu.

"Bengizokutjho," kwatjho u-Dodo ngephimbo elikhubekileko, "bekukuthi, into engcono khulu yokusenza somile kungaba mjaho we-Caucus."

" Khuyini umjarho we-Caucus?" kwatjho u-Alice; ingasi bona bekafuna ukwazi okunengi, kodwana i-Dodo gade ithule kwangathi icabanga bona omunye umuntu kufuze akhulume, begodu akekho omunye obegade afuna ukutjho okuthileko.

"Kubayini," kwatjho u-Dodo, "indlela engcono yokuyihlathulula kukuyenza." (Begodu, njengombana ungathanda ukuzama into ngokwakho, ngelinye ilanga lobusika, ngizokutjela bona i-Dodo yakghona njani.)

Kokuthoma yatshwaya indawo yokugijima, ngendlela efana nesiyingi, (“ubujamo obuqondileko akunandaba,” kwathiwa,) bese woke amaqembu abekwa eceleni kwendlela, lapha nalaphaya. Bekungana "Omunye, ababili, abathathu, nokude," kodwana bebathoma ukugijima lokha nabathandako, bese bayalisa lokha nabathandako, ukwenzela bona kungabi lula ukwazi bona umjarho sele uphele nini. Kodwana, lokha nabagijima isigamu se-awa, begodu sele bomile godu, i-Dodo yarhuwelela yathi "Umjarho uphelile!" begodu boke bahlangana kiyo, baphefumulela phezulu, babuza, "Kodwana ngubani onqobileko?"

Umbuzo lo u-Dodo bekangakghoni ukuwuphendula ngaphandle kokucabanga khulu, begodu wahlala isikhathi eside umunwe owodwa ucindezelwe ebunzini lakhe (ubujamo ovame ukubona u-Shakespeare, eenthombeni zakhe), ngesikhathi abanye balinde ngokuthula. Ekugcineni uDodo wathi, " Woke umuntu unqobile, begodu boke kufanele babe nemiklomelo."

"Kodwana ngubani ozokunikela imiklomelo?" kubuza amaphimbo amanengi.

"Kubayini, yena , vele," kwatjho u-Dodo, akhomba u-Alice ngomunwe owodwa; begodu isiqhema soke sahlangana naye, simemeza ngendlela edidekileko, "Imiklomelo! Imiklomelo!"

U-Alice bekangazi bona enzeni, begodu ngokuphelelwa lithemba wafaka isandla sakhe esikhwameni sakhe, begodu wakhipha ibhokisi lama-comfits, (ngenhlanhla amanzi anetswayi bekangangeni kiwo), begodu wawanikela njengemiklomelo. Bekune-a-piece ngqo, yoke.

"Kodwana kufanele bona abe nomvuzo ngokwakhe, uyazi," kwatjho iGundwane.

"Kunjalo," u-Dodo waphendula ngokuzimisela. “Yini enye onayo ephaketheni lakho?” waraga, wajika waya ku-Alice.

"Ithimbe kwaphela," kwatjho u-Alice ngokudana.

"Yinikele lapha," kwatjho u-Dodo.

Bese baphinda bahlangana naye, ngesikhathi u-Dodo aveza i-thimble ngokuzithoba, wathi "Siyakuncenga ukwamukela i-thimble enhle le;" begodu, lokha nasele iqedile ikulumo emfitjhani, boke bathokoza.

U-Alice bekacabanga bona yoke into beyingazwakali, kodwana yoke beyiqaleka ingakalungi kangangobana akhenge abe nesibindi sokuhleka; begodu, njengombana bekangakghoni ukucabanga ngalokho angakutjhoko, wavele wakhothama, wathatha i-thimble, aqaleka ahloniphekile ngendlela angakghona ngayo.

Into elandelako bekukudla ama-comfits: lokhu kwabanga umsindo nokuhlangahlangana, njengombana iinyoni ezinkulu bezikhononda ngokuthi azikghoni ukunambitha ezazo, begodu ezincani zaminza begodu kwafanela bona zibethwe ngemuva. Kodwana, ekugcineni kwaphela, begodu bahlala phasi godu eringini, bancenga iGundwane bona libatjele okunye.

"Uthembise ukungitjela umlando wakho, uyazi," kwatjho u-Alice, "begodu kubayini umzonda—u-C no-D," wengezelela ngokuhleba, asaba bona izokukhubeka godu.

“Eyami yindaba ende begodu edanisako!” Kutjho igundane, liphendukela ku-Alice, begodu libubula.

" Ngumsila omude, nakanjani," kwatjho u-Alice, aqale phasi ngokumangala emsileni weGundwane; "kodwana kubayini uthi kuyadanisa?" Begodu waragela phambili nokudideka ngalokho ngesikhathi iGundwane likhuluma, kangangobana umbono wakhe ngendaba bewufana nale:—

“Awuyi khona!” Kutjho igundane ku-Alice ngokuqinileko. “Ucabangani?”

"Ngiyakuncenga," kwatjho u-Alice ngokuzithoba: "begade ufike ejikeni lesihlanu, ngiyacabanga?"

"Bengingakaze ! " kwamemeza igundane, ngokubukhali nangokuthukuthela okukhulu.

“Ifindo!” kwatjho u-Alice, ahlale akulungele ukuzenza abe lisizo, begodu amqala ngokutshwenyeka. “Hawu, ngivumele ngisize ukuyibuyisela emuva!”

"Angekhe ngenze litho elinjalo," kwatjho igundane, lisukuma lahamba. “Uyangithuka ngokukhuluma umbhedo onjalo!”

“Bengingakanqophi!” kwancenga u-Alice onganalitho. “Kodwana ukhubeka lula, uyazi!”

Igundane labhonga kwaphela ngokuphendula.

“Ngibawa ubuye uzoqeda indatjana yakho!” U-Alice wabiza ngemva kwayo; begodu abanye boke bahlanganyela, “Iye, ngibawa wenze njalo!” kodwana igundane lathintitha ihlokwalo ngokuphelelwa sineke, lahamba msinyana.

“Kuyadabukisa bona angeze kwahlala!” kwabubula i-Lory, msinyana ngemva kobana ingasabonakali; begodu iNkala endala yathatha ithuba yathi endodakazini yayo "Hawu, sthandwa sami! Lokhu akube sifundo kuwe sokuthi ungalahlekelwa lilaka !" “Yima ilimi lakho, Mma!” kwatjho iKrabhu encani, ngokurhabako. “Wanele ukulinga ukubekezela kwe-oyster!”

"Ngiyafisa ngathana ngingaba naye u-Dina lapha, ngiyazi bona nginaye!" Kutjho u-Alice ngokuzwakalako, angakhulumi nomuntu. “Uzokubuyisa msinyana!”

"Begodu ngubani u-Dinah, nange ngingazimisela ukubuza umbuzo?" kwatjho i-Lory.

U-Alice waphendula ngokuzimisela, ngombana bekahlala akulungele ukukhuluma ngesilwana sakhe esifuywako: "UDinah likati lethu. Begodu uyikati elikhulu khulu lokubamba amagundane ongacabangi! Begodu oh, ngiyafisa ngathana bewungambona ngemva kweenyoni! Kungani, uzokudla inyoni encani msinyana nayiyiqalako!"

Inkulumo le yabanga ukuzwakala okukhulu hlangana nehlangano. Ezinye zeenyoni zaphuthuma khonokho: enye i-Magpie endala yathoma ukuzigoqa ngokutjheja khulu, yathi, "Kufuze bona ngifika ekhaya; umoya webusuku awufanelani nomphimbo wami!" begodu i-Canary yamemeza ngelizwi elithuthumelako ebantwaneni bayo, "Yizani, bathandekako bami! Sekuyisikhathi sokuthi nonke nilele embhedeni!" Ngamabanga ahlukahlukeneko boke basuka, begodu u-Alice msinyana wasala yedwa.

“Ngiyafisa ngathana akhenge ngikhulume ngoDinah!” watjho ngephimbo lokudana. "Akekho umuntu obonakala amthanda, phasi lapha, begodu nginesiqiniseko sokobana uyikati elihle khulu ephasini! O, Dinah wami othandekako! Ngiyazibuza bona ngizokuphinda ngikubone na!" Begodu lapha u-Alice ohluphekileko wathoma ukulila godu, ngombana bekazizwa anesizungu begodu aphelelwa mamandla. Ngemva kwesikhatjhana, nanyana kunjalo, wezwa godu ukutjhwaba kweenyawo kude, begodu waqala phezulu ngokuzimisela, anethemba lokuthi iGundane sele litjhugulule umkhumbulo, begodu lizokubuya ukuzoqeda indatjana yakhe.
