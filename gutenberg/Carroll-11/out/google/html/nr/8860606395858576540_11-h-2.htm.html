ISAHLUKO II.
Ichibi leenyembezi

"Ukufuna ukwazi!" kwamemeza u-Alice (wamangala khulu, kangangobana okwamanje wakhohlwa bona kukhulunywa njani isiNgisi esihle); "Nje ngiyavuleka njengetheleskophu ekulu khulu eyakhe yaba khona! Salakahle, iinyawo!" (ngombana nakaqala phasi eenyaweni zakhe, bezibonakala zipheze zingabonakali, bezitjhidela kude khulu). "Hawu, iinyawo zami ezincani ezitlhagako, ngiyazibuza bona ngubani ozokufakela iincathulo zakho namasokisi wenu nje, bathandekako? Nginesiqiniseko sokuthi angeze ngakghona! Ngizokuba kude khulu ukuzihlupha ngani: kufanele niphathe ngendlela engcono khulu;—kodwana kufanele ngibe nomusa kibo," kwacabanga u-Alice, "nofana mhlamunye angeze bakhamba ngendlela engifuna ukuyinikela ngayo! banikele amabhuzu amatjha qobe ngeKresmusi.”

Waragela phambili nokuzihlela bona uzokuphatha njani. "Kufanele bakhambe ngesithwali," wacabanga; “begodu kuzokubonakala kuhlekisa kangangani, ukuthumela izipho eenyaweni zakho! Begodu iinqophiso zizokubukeka zingakajayeleki kangangani!

Unyawo luka-Alice lwesokudla, Esq.,
Hearthrug,
eduze kwe-Fender, ( ngothando luka-Alice ).

Hawu sthandwa sami, mbhedo onjani engiwukhulumako!”

Ngaleso sikhathi ikhanda lakhe lashaya ophahleni lwehholo: eqinisweni besele angaphezu kwamamitha alithoba ukuphakama, begodu msinyana wathatha isikhiya esincani segolide waphuthuma waya emnyango wesivande.

U-Alice onganalitho! Bekuyindlela ebekangakghona ngayo, alele phasi ngehlangothini linye, ukuqala engadini ngelihlo elilodwa; kodwana ukudlula bekuphelelwa lithemba kunanini ngaphambili: wahlala phasi wathoma ukulila godu.

"Kufanele ube namahloni ngawe," kwatjho u-Alice, "intombazana ekulu njengawe," (angatjho lokhu), "ukuragela phambili nokukhala ngalendlela! Yekela umzuzu lo, ngiyakutjela!" Kodwana waragela phambili, wathulula iinyembezi ezinengi, bekwaba kulapho kunechibi elikhulu elimzombelezileko, elingaba masentimitha amane ukujula begodu lifinyelela ehhafuni lehholo.

Ngemva kwesikhathi wezwa ukutjhwaba kweenyawo kude, begodu warhaba womisa amehlo wakhe ukubona bona khuyini ezako. KwakunguMvundla omhlophe obuyako, agqoke kuhle, aphethe amadlhavu amhlophe ngesandla esisodwa begodu nefeni ekulu ngesinye: weza agijima msinyana, ahlaba umkhosi njengombana eza, "Hawu! iNkosikazi, iNkosikazi! O! Bengekhe ibe yisihluku nangabe ngimgcine imlindile!" U-Alice wazizwa aphelelwe lithemba kangangobana bekakulungele ukubawa isizo kinoma ngubani; njeke, lokha uMvundla nakatjhidela eduze kwakhe, wathoma, ngelizwi eliphasi, elinamahloni, "Nangabe ungathanda, mnumzana—" Umvundla wathoma ngamandla, wawisa amagilavu ​​wezinyane amhlophe nefeni, begodu wagijima waya ebumnyameni ngamandla.

U-Alice wathatha ifeni namagilavu, begodu, njengombana ihholo belitjhisa khulu, waragela phambili nokuziphephetha ngaso soke isikhathi aragela phambili nokukhuluma: “Sthandwa sami, sthandwa sami! Yeka koke okumangalisako namhlanjesi! Begodu izolo izinto ziragele phambili njengenjwayelo. Ngiyazibuza bona ngitjhugululwe ebusuku na? Akhe ngicabange: bengifana na lokha nangivuka ekuseni? Angifani, umbuzo olandelako kukuthi , Ngingubani ephasini? Wathoma ukucabanga ngabo boke abantwana egade abaziko ebebaneminyaka efanako naye, ukubona bona bekangatjhugululwa na ngomunye wabo.

"Nginesiqiniseko sokuthi angisiye u-Ada," watjho, "ngombana iinhluthu zakhe ziba ziinhluthu ezide kangaka, begodu ezami azizizi ziinhluthu; begodu nginesiqiniseko sokuthi angeze ngaba ngu-Mabel, ngombana ngiyazi yoke imihlobo yezinto, begodu yena, oh! uyazi okuncani khulu! Ngaphandle kwalokho, nguye , begodu nginguye , begodu—oh, ngizozama kangangani ukwazi koke lokhu! izinto ebengivame ukuzazi . Kufuze bona ngitjhugululwe ngoMabel! Ngizokuzama ukuthi ' Umncani wenza njani —'” begodu waphambanisa izandla zakhe ethangeni lakhe kwanga ukhuluma iimfundo, wathoma ukukuphindaphinda, kodwana ilizwi lakhe belizwakala lirhuwelela begodu lingakajayeleki, begodu amezwi akhenge abe ngendlela efanako njengombana bekajayele ukwenza:—

“Ingwenya encani yenza njani
Thuthukisa umsila wakhe okhanyako,
Bese uthela amanzi weNayile
Kiwo woke amazinga wegolide!

“Ubonakala amomotheka kangangani,
Yeka iinzipho zakhe ezivuleke kuhle kangangani,
Bese wamukela iinhlambi ezincani
Ngemihlathi emomotheka kancani!”

"Nginesiqiniseko sokuthi lawo akusiwo amezwi afaneleko," kwatjho u-Alice ompofu, begodu amehlo wakhe agcwala iinyembezi godu njengombana aragela phambili, "Kufuze ngibe nguMabel phela, begodu kuzokufanela bona ngiye ngihlale endlini encani leyo, begodu ngingabi namathoyizi wokudlala ngawo, begodu oh! iimfundo ezinengi kangaka engingazifunda! Awa, ngithathe isiqunto sokuthi Mabel, ngizokuhlala lapha! Akuzukusizakala bona babeke amakhanda phasi bathi 'Woza godu, sthandwa sami!' Ngizokuqala phezulu ngithi 'Ngingubani-ke? Ngitjele lokho kokuthoma, bese, nangabe ngithanda ukuba mumuntu loyo, ngizokuza: nangabe akunjalo, ngizokuhlala phasi lapha bekube kulapho ngingomunye umuntu'—kodwana, oh sthandwa sami!” kwamemeza u-Alice, ngokuthuthumela kweenyembezi, "Ngiyafisa ngathana bangabeka amakhanda wabo phasi! Ngidiniwe khulu ngokuba ngedwa lapha!"

Nakatjho lokhu waqala phasi ezandleni zakhe, begodu wamangala ukubona bona bekafake elinye lamagilavu wezinyane amhlophe kaMvundla ngesikhathi akhuluma. “ Bengingakwenza njani lokho?” wacabanga. "Kufanele bona ngikhula godu." Wasukuma waya etafuleni ukuyozilinganisa ngalo, wathola bona, njengombana bekangaqagela, gade angaba ziinyawo ezimbili ukuphakama, begodu bekasolo ancipha msinyana: msinyana wathola bona imbangela yalokhu bekuyifeni ebegade ayiphethe, begodu wayiwisa msinyana, ngesikhathi esifaneleko ukugwema ukuncipha ngokupheleleko.

"Lokho bekukuphunyuka okuncani!" kwatjho u-Alice, athukiwe khulu ngokutjhuguluka okuvele kungakalindelwa, kodwana athabile khulu ngokuzithola asekhona; "begodu nje ngiye engadini!" begodu wagijima ngebelo loke wabuyela emnyango omncani: kodwana, maye! umnyango omncani wavalwa godu, begodu isikhiya esincani segolide besilele phezu kwetafula lengilazi njengangaphambilini, "begodu izinto zimbi khulu kunanini ngaphambili," kwacabanga umntwana onganalitho, "ngombana azange ngibe mncani kangaka ngaphambilini, angikaze! Begodu ngithi kubi khulu, ukuthi kunjalo!"

Njengombana atjho amezwi la unyawo lwakhe lwatjhelela, begodu ngomunye umzuzwana, waphaphatheka! bekafika esilevini sakhe emanzini anetshwayi. Umqondo wakhe wokuthoma bekukukuthi ngandlela thize wawela elwandle, "begodu nanyana kunjalo ngingabuyela emuva ngesitimela," watjho kuye. (U-Alice bekake waya elwandle kanye epilwenakhe, begodu wafika esiphethweni esijayelekileko, sokuthi nanyana kukuphi lapho uya khona egwini le-England ufumana imitjhini eminengi yokugeza elwandle, abanye abantwana bemba esihlabathini ngeenkepe zamaplanka, bese kuba nomjeje wezindlu zokuhlala, begodu ngemva kwazo kube nesitetjhi sesitimela.) Nanyana kunjalo, msinyana waveza bona gade alila echibini ebegade alila lokha nasele sikhale lokha nasele sikhale. amamitha alithoba ukuphakama.

“Ngiyafisa ngathana akhenge ngikhale kangaka!” Kutjho u-Alice, njengombana abhukuda, alinga ukuthola indlela yokuphuma. "Ngizokujeziswa ngalokho nje, ngiyacabanga, ngokuminza eenyembezini zami! Lokho kuzokuba yinto engakajayeleki, ukuqiniseka! Nanyana kunjalo, yoke into iyamangalisa namhlanjesi."

Ngaleso sikhathi wezwa into ephaphathekako echibini elikude kancani, begodu wabhukuda eduze ukuze abone bona bekuyini: ekuthomeni bekacabanga bona bekuyi-walrus nofana imvubu, kodwana wakhumbula bona gade amncani kangangani, begodu msinyana wabona bona bekuyigundane kwaphela ebegade lingene ngaphakathi njengaye.

"Kungaba lisizo na, nje," kwacabanga u-Alice, "ukukhuluma negundwane? Yoke into iphumile lapha phasi, kangangobana ngicabanga bona ingakhuluma: nanyana kukuphi, akukho ukulimala ngokuzama." Nje-ke wathoma wathi: "O Mgundane, uyazi indlela yokuphuma echibini leli? Ngidiniwe khulu kukubhukuda lapha, O Mgundane!" (U-Alice bekacabanga bona lokhu bekuyindlela elungileko yokukhuluma negundane: bekangakaze enze into enjalo ngaphambilini, kodwana wakhumbula bona wabona ku-Latin Grammar yomfowabo, “Igundane—legundane—kuya kwegundane—igundane—O mouse!”) Igundane lamqala ngokufuna ukwazi, begodu labonakala kuye liqhweba amehlo walo, kodwana azange lithi litho.

"Mhlamunye ayizwisisi isiNgisi," kwacabanga u-Alice; "Ngiyakghona ukucabanga bona ligundane le-French, woza no-William uMnqobi." (Ngombana, ngalo loke ilwazi lakhe lomlando, u-Alice bekangazi bona kudala kangangani into eyenzekako.) Njeke wathoma godu wathi: “Ingakhuluma na?” okwakungumutjho wokuthoma encwadini yakhe yesifundo sesiFulentshi. Igundane laphuma msinyana emanzini, begodu labonakala lithuthumela ngokuthuka. “Hawu, ngiyakuncenga ukulibalela!” kwamemeza u-Alice ngokurhabako, asaba bona ulimaze imizwa yesilwana esimbi. “Ngikhohliwe bona awuwathandi amakati.”

“Hhayi njengamakati!” kwamemeza iGundane, ngelizwi elizwakalako, elinomdlandla. “ Ungathanda amakati ngathana bewuyimi?”

"Kulungile, mhlamunye akunjalo," kwatjho u-Alice ngephimbo elithobako: "ungathukutheli ngalokho. Nanyana kunjalo ngiyafisa ngathana ngingakutjengisa ikati lethu u-Dinah: Ngicabanga bona ungathanda amakati nangabe ungalibona. Uyinto ethulileko," u-Alice waraga, akhululekile, njengombana abhukuda ngobuvila ngaphakathi kwechibi, "begodu ahlezi kuhle ukukhotha iinyawo zakhe nokuhlamba ubuso bakhe—begodu yinto ethambileko ehle khulu ukumunyisa—begodu muhle khulu ekubambeni amagundwane—oh, ngiyakuncenga!” kwamemeza u-Alice godu, ngombana ngesikhathesi igundane belivuvuka, begodu bekazizwa aqiniseka bona likhubekile kwamambala. “Angeze sakhuluma ngaye godu nangabe ungafuni.”

“Kwamambala!” Kwamemeza iGundwane, ebegade ithuthumela phasi emsileni wayo. “Njengokungathi ngizokukhuluma ngendaba enjalo! Umndenethu bewuhlala uzonda amakati: izinto ezimbi, eziphasi, ezihlambalazako! Ungangivumeli bona ngizwe ibizo godu!”

“Angeze kwamambala!” Kutjho u-Alice, aphuthuma khulu ukutjhugulula indaba yekulumo. "Ingabe—ingabe uyazithanda—izinja?" Igundane azange liphendule, nje-ke u-Alice waragela phambili ngokuzimisela: “Kunenja encani ehle kangaka eduze nendlu yethu engingathanda ukukutjengisa yona! I-terrier encani enamehlo akhanyako, uyazi, eneenhluthu ezide ezigoqeneko ezimnyama! Begodu izokuthatha izinto lokha nawuyiphosako, begodu izokuhlala phezulu icele isidlo sayo sakusihlwa, nezinye izinto—angikhumbuli kumlimi, uyazi, begodu uthi iwusizo khulu, ibiza amaphawundi alikhulu uthi ibulala woke amagundwana begodu—awu sthandwa sami!” kwamemeza u-Alice ngephimbo elidabukisako, “Ngiyesaba bona ngiyikhubekise godu!” Ngombana igundane belibhukuda kude naye ngamandla, begodu lenza umsindo omkhulu echibini njengombana lihamba.

Nje-ke wabiza kancani ngemva kwalokho, "Igundane sthandwa sami! Buya godu, begodu angeze sakhuluma ngamakati nofana izinja, nangabe awuzithandi!" Lokha igundane nalizwa lokhu, lajika labhukuda labuyela kuye: ubuso balo bebuphaphathekile (ngethando, u-Alice wacabanga), begodu lathi ngelizwi eliphasi elithuthumelako, “Asiye eligwini, bese ngizokutjela umlando wami, begodu uzokuzwisisa bona kubayini ngizonda amakati nezinja.”

Kwasekuyisikhathi sokuthi sikhambe, ngombana idamu besele ligcwele iinyoni neenlwana ebegade ziwele kilo: bekunedada ne-Dodo, i-Lory ne-Eaglet, nezinye iinlwana ezinengi ezifuna ukwazi. U-Alice wadosa phambili, begodu isiqhema soke sabhukuda saya eligwini.
