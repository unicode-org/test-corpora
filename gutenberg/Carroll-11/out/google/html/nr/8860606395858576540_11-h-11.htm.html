ISAHLUKO XI.
Ngubani Oweba Ama-Tarts?

IKosi neNdlovukazi yeeNhliziyo bebahlezi esihlalweni sabo sobukhosi lokha nabafikako, isiqubuthu esikhulu sibuthene eduze kwabo—yoke imihlobo yeenyoni ezincani neenlwana, kanye nephakethe loke lamakhadi: i-Knave beyijame phambi kwabo, ibotjhiwe, inesotja ngapha nangapha ukuyigada; begodu eduze neKosi bekunoMvundla oMhlophe, aphethe icilongo ngesandla sinye, nomqulu wesikhumba ngesinye. Phakathi khulu kwekhotho bekunetafula, enesitja esikhulu sama-tarts phezu kwaso: begade abukeka kuhle khulu, okwenza bona u-Alice alambile ukuwaqala—“Ngifisa ngathana bangaqeda icala,” wacabanga, “begodu banikeze ukudla!” Kodwana bekubonakala ngasuthi akunathuba lalokhu, yeke wathoma ukuqala koke ngaye, ukudlulisa isikhathi.

U-Alice bekangakaze abe sekhotho yobulungiswa ngaphambilini, kodwana bekafunde ngabo eencwadini, begodu wathaba khulu ukuthola bona bekazi ibizo lakho koke lapho. “Lowo ngumahluleli,” watjho kuye, “ngebanga lewigi yakhe ehle khulu.”

Ijaji, ngaleyo ndlela, bekuyiKosi; begodu njengombana bekagqoke umqhele wakhe phezu kwewigi, (qala iphepha langaphambili nangabe ufuna ukubona bona wakwenza njani,) bekangabonakali akhululekile, begodu bekungasi kuhle.

"Begodu lelo yibhokisi lamajaji," kwacabanga u-Alice, "nezidalwa ezilitjhumi nambili," (bekabophekile bona athi "izidalwa," uyabona, ngombana ezinye zazo beziziinlwana, kuthi ezinye beziinyoni,) "Ngicabanga bona ngibo abajaji." Watjho ilizwi lokugcina kabili nofana kathathu kuye, azikhakhazisa ngalo: ngombana bekacabanga, begodu ngokufaneleko, bona bambalwa khulu abantazana abancani beminyaka yakhe ebebazi ihlathululo yalo. Kodwana, "amadoda wamajaji" bekazokwenza kuhle.

Amajaji alitjhumi nambili woke atlola ngokuphithizela khulu emaslayidini. “Benzani?” U-Alice wahlebela iGryphon. "Angeze baba nalitho lokubeka phasi, ngaphambi kobana icala lithome."

"Babeka amabizo wabo phasi," i-Gryphon yaphendula ngokuhleba, "ngokwesaba bona bangakhohlwa ngaphambi kokuphela kwecala."

“Izinto ezisiphukuphuku!” U-Alice wathoma ngephimbo elikhulu, elithukutheleko, kodwana wama msinyana, ngombana uMvundla omhlophe warhuwelela wathi, "Thula enkantolo!" begodu iKosi yafaka izibuko zayo begodu yaqala ngokutshwenyeka, ukubona bona ngubani okhulumako.

U-Alice bekabona, begodu nangabe bekaqala phezu kwamahlombe wabo, bona woke amajaji bekatlola phasi "izinto ezisiphukuphuku!" emalayini wabo, begodu bekakghona nokubona bona omunye wabo bekangazi ukupeleda ukuthi “isidlhayela,” begodu bekufuze acele umakhelwanakhe bona amtjele. “Kuzokuba nokuhlangahlangana okuhle ngaphambi kobana icala liphele!” kwacabanga u-Alice.

Omunye wabagwebi bekanepensela ebegade ikghwathaza. Lokhu vele, u-Alice akhenge akghone ukujamelana nakho, begodu wajikeleza ikhotho wafika ngemva kwakhe, begodu msinyana wathola ithuba lokuyithatha. Wakwenza msinya kangangobana umgwebi omncani (bekungu-Bill, i-Lizard) bekangakghoni ukuzwisisa bona kwenzekeni; njeke, ngemva kokuzingela koke, kwafanela bona atlole ngomunwe owodwa ilanga loke; begodu lokhu bekungasizi ngalitho, ngombana bekungatjhiyi itshwayo esiletini.

“Mmemezeli, funda ukumangalelwa!” Kutjho iKosi.

Ngalokhu uMvundla omhlophe washaya icilongo kathathu, bese wavula umqulu wesikhumba, wafunda ngalendlela elandelako:—

“INdlovukazi yeeNhliziyo, yenza ama-tart,
Konke ngelanga lehlobo:
I-Knave of Hearts, yeba ama-tart lawo,
Bese wabathatha!

"Cabanga ngesinqumo sakho," iKosi yatjho kumajaji.

“Akukabi, akukabi!” uMvundla waphazamisa msinyana. “Kunengi okuzako ngaphambi kwalokho!”

“Biza ufakazi wokuthoma,” kwatjho iKosi; begodu uMvundla omhlophe washaya icilongo kathathu, wamemeza wathi, "Ufakazi wokuthoma!"

Ufakazi wokuthoma bekungu-Hatter. Wangena aphethe inkomitsho yetiye ngesandla esisodwa kuthi ngesinye isiqephu sesinkwa nebhotela. "Ngiyabawa ukulibalela, Mhlonitjhwa," wathoma, "ngokuletha lokhu: kodwana bengingakaqedi itiye lami lokha nangithunyelwa."

"Bekufanele uqede," kwatjho iKosi. “Uthome nini?”

UMkhiqizi wezigqoko waqala i-March Hare, ebegade imlandele wangena ekhotho, ibambene ne-Dormouse. "Ngomhlaka-14 kuNtaka, ngicabanga bona bekunjalo," watjho.

"Okwetjhumi nahlanu," kwatjho i-March Hare.

"Okwetjhumi nathandathu," kwangezelela igundane lokulala.

"Tlola phasi lokho," iKosi yatjho ejajini, begodu ijaji latlola phasi woke amadethi amathathu emaslethini wawo, bese lawahlanganisa, bese lanciphisa ipendulo yaba matjhili namapeni.

"Khulula isigqoko sakho," iKosi yathi kuMkhiqizi wesigqoko.

“Akusikho kwami,” kwatjho uMkhiqizi wezigqoko.

" Kwebiwe! " kwatjho iKosi, iphendukela ejajini, eyavele yenza imemorandamu yeqiniso.

"Ngiyazigcina ukuze ngizithengise," uMkhiqizi wezigqoko wangezelela njengehlathululo; “Anginayo eyami. Ngingumuntu owenza izigqoko.”

Lapha iNdlovukazi yafaka izibuko zayo, begodu yathoma ukuqala uMkhiqizi wezigqoko, owaphenduka waba mnyama begodu wanyakaza.

"Nikela ubufakazi bakho," kwatjho iKosi; “begodu ungathuki, kungenjalo ngizokwenza bona ubulawe khonokho.”

Lokhu bekungabonakali kumkhuthaza ufakazi: bekasolo atjhuguluka ukusuka kolunye unyawo ukuya kolunye, aqale iNdlovukazi ngokungakhululeki, begodu ngokudideka kwakhe waluma isiqetjhana esikhulu ekomitjhini yakhe yetiye esikhundleni sesinkwa nebhotela.

Ngaleso sikhathi u-Alice wezwa umuzwa ofuna ukwazi, owamdida khulu bekwaba kulapho athola bona bekuyini: besele athoma ukukhula godu, begodu ekuthomeni bekacabanga bona uzokuvuka atjhiye ikhotho; kodwana nakacabanga kabili waqunta ukuhlala lapho akhona inqobo nje nangabe kunendawo yakhe.

“Ngiyafisa ngathana bewungeze wacindezela njalo,” kwatjho igundane, ebegade ihlezi eduze kwayo. “Ngingakghoni ukuphefumula.”

“Angikghoni ukuzibamba,” kwatjho u-Alice ngobumnene: “Ngiyakhula.”

"Awunalo ilungelo lokukhula lapha ," kwatjho i-Dormouse.

“Ungakhulumi umraro,” kwatjho u-Alice ngesibindi: “uyazi bona nawe uyakhula.”

"Iye, kodwana ngikhula ngebelo elizwakalako," kwatjho i-Dormouse: "hhayi ngendlela engakajayeleki." Wasukuma ngokuthukuthela khulu wawela ngakelinye ihlangothi lekhotho.

Isikhathi soke lesi iNdlovukazi beyingakaze ilise ukugqolozela u-Hatter, begodu, njengombana i-Dormouse iwela ikhotho, yathi komunye wabaphathi bekhotho, "Ngilethele irhelo labavumi ekhonsathini edlulileko!" lapho u-Hatter odabukileko athuthumela khona, kangangokuthi wathintitha zombili iincathulo zakhe.

“Nikela ubufakazi bakho,” iKosi yabuyelela ngokuthukuthela, “kungenjalo ngizokwenza bona ubulawe, nanoma uthukiwe namkha awa.”

"Ngiyindoda etlhagako, Mhlonitjhwa," uMhlobi wathoma, ngephimbo elithuthumelako, "—begodu bengingakathomi itiye lami—hhayi ngaphezu kweveke nofana ngaphezulu—begodu kuthiwani ngesinkwa-nebhotela sele siba mncani kangaka—nokuphazima kwetiye—"

“Ukuphazima kwalokho? ” kwatjho iKosi.

" Kwathoma ngetiye," kwaphendula uMkhiqizi wezigqoko.

"Kuliqiniso ukucwayiza kuthoma ngo-T!" Kutjho iKosi ngokubukhali. "Uyangithatha njengesidlhayela? Ragela phambili!"

"Ngiyindoda etlhagako," uMkhiqizi waragela phambili, "begodu izinto ezinengi zaphazima ngemva kwalokho—kwathi uMvundla kaNtaka kwaphela—"

“Angizange!” uMvundla kaNtaka waphazamisa ngokurhaba khulu.

“Ukwenzile!” kwatjho uMkhiqizi wezigqoko.

“Ngiyakuphika!” kwatjho i-March Hare.

"Uyakuphika," kwatjho iKosi: "tjhiya leyo ngcenye."

"Kulungile, nanyana kukuphi, i-Dormouse yathi—" uMkhiqizi waragela phambili, aqala ngokutshwenyeka ukubona bona naye uzokuphika na: kodwana i-Dormouse azange iphike litho, beyilele khulu.

"Ngemva kwalokho," kwaragela phambili uMkhiqizi wezigqoko, "Ngasika enye isinkwa nebhotela—"

"Kodwana i-Dormouse yathini?" omunye wejaji wabuza.

“Lokho angikwazi ukukukhumbula,” kwatjho uMkhiqizi wezigqoko.

" Kufanele ukhumbule," kwatjho iKosi, "ngaphandle kwalokho ngizokwenza bona ubulawe."

U-Hatter odabukileko wawisa inkomitsho yakhe yetiye nesinkwa nebhotela, waguqa ngedolo elilodwa. “Ngiyindoda etlhagako, Mhlonitjhwa,” wathoma.

“ Uyisikhulumi esimbi khulu ,” kwatjho iKosi.

Lapha enye yama-guinea-pig yathokoza, begodu msinyana yacindezelwa ziimphathiswa zekhotho. (Njengombana lokho kuligama elibudisi, ngizokuhlathulula bona kwenziwa njani. Bebanesikhwama esikhulu sekhanvasi, ebesibotjhwe emlonyeni ngeentambo: kiso bebafaka ingulube ye-guinea-pig, ihloko kuqala, bese bahlala phezu kwayo.)

“Ngithabile ngokuthi ngibone lokho kwenziwa,” kwacabanga u-Alice. "Ngisolo ngifunda emaphephandabeni, ekupheleni kwamacala, "Kwaba nomzamo wokushaya izandla, owagandelelwa msinyana ziimphathiswa zekhotho," begodu azange ngizwisise bona kutjho ukuthini bekube nje."

“Nangabe ngilokho kwaphela okwaziko ngakho, ungajama phasi,” kwaragela phambili iKosi.

"Angeze ngaya phasi," kwatjho uMkhiqizi wezigqoko: "Ngisephasini, njengombana kunjalo."

“Nje-ke ungahlala phasi,” kwaphendula iKosi.

Lapha enye ingulube ye-guinea yathokoza, begodu yacindezelwa.

“Woza, lokho kuqedile ama-guinea-pigs!” kwacabanga u-Alice. "Nje-ke sizokuba ngcono."

"Ngingakhetha ukuqeda itiye lami," kwatjho uMkhiqizi, aqale iNdlovukazi ngokutshwenyeka, ebegade ifunda irhelo labavumi.

"Ungahamba," kwatjho iKosi, begodu uMhloli wezigqoko watjhiya ikhotho msinyana, ngaphandle kokulinda ukufaka amanyathelo wakhe.

"—begodu ukhuphe ihloko yakhe ngaphandle," iNdlovukazi yengezelela komunye wabaphathi: kodwana uMkhiqizi wezigqoko bekangabonakali ngaphambi kobana ipholisa lifike emnyango.

“Biza ufakazi olandelako!” Kutjho iKosi.

Ufakazi olandelako bekumpheki we-Duchess. Bekaphethe ibhokisi lepelepele ngesandla sakhe, begodu u-Alice waqagela bona ngubani, ngitjho nangaphambi kobana angene ekhotho, ngendlela abantu abaseduze nomnyango abathoma ngayo ukuthimula kanyekanye.

"Nikela ubufakazi bakho," kwatjho iKosi.

“Angeze,” kwatjho umpheki.

INkosi yaqala uMvundla omhlophe ngokutshwenyeka, owathi ngelizwi eliphasi, "Umhlonitjhwakho kufanele abuze ufakazi lo imibuzo."

"Kulungile, nangabe kufanele, kufanele," iKosi yatjho, ngomoya wokudana, begodu, ngemva kokugoqa iingalo zayo begodu iqale umpheki amehlo wakhe bekwaba kulapho amehlo wakhe sele angasabonakali, yathi ngephimbo elijulileko, "Ama-tart enziwe ngani?"

"Ipelepele, khulukhulu," kwatjho umpheki.

"I-Treacle," kwatjho ilizwi elilele ngemva kwakhe.

"Vala igundane lelo," kwamemeza iNdlovukazi. "Nquma ikhanda leyo Dormouse! Khipha leyo Dormouse ngaphandle kwekhotho! Mcindezele! Mphinye! Nquma ngeendevu zayo!"

Kwaphela imizuzu ethileko ikhotho yoke beyihlangahlangene, ikhupha i-Dormouse, begodu, ngesikhathi bahlala phasi godu, umpheki besele anyamalele.

"Akutshwenyi!" kwatjho iKosi, ngomoya wokukhululeka okukhulu. “Biza ufakazi olandelako.” Wangezelela ngelizwi eliphasi kuNdlovukazi, "Kwamambala, sthandwa sami, kufanele ubuze ufakazi olandelako. Kungenza ibunzi lami libe buhlungu!"

U-Alice waqala uMvundla oMhlophe njengombana aphaphazela irhelo, azizwa afuna ukwazi bona ufakazi olandelako uzokuba njani, “—ngombana abakabi nobufakazi obunengi okwanje ,” watjho kuye. Cabanga ukumangala kwakhe, lokha uMvundla oMhlophe nakafunda, ngephimbo lakhe elincani, ibizo elithi "Alice!"
