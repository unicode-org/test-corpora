ISAHLUKO IX.
Indaba ye-Mock Turtle

“Awukghoni ukucabanga bona ngithabile kangangani ngokukubona godu, wena mdala othandekako!” kwatjho uNkosikazi, njengombana afaka ingalo yakhe ngothando ku-Alice, begodu bakhamba bobabili.

U-Alice wathaba khulu ukumthola anomoya omuhle kangaka, begodu wacabanga ngaphakathi kwakhe bona mhlamunye bekuyipelepele kwaphela eyamenza waba yisilwane kangaka lokha nabahlangana ekhishini.

" Nangiyi- Duchess," watjho kuye, (hhayi ngelizwi elithemba khulu), "Angekhe ngibe nepelepele ekhishini lami. Isobho lisebenza kuhle khulu ngaphandle kwalo —Mhlamunye kuhlala lipelepele elenza abantu babe nokuthukuthela," waraga, athokoze khulu ngokufumana umthetho omutjha, "begodu ivini yenza bona babe munyu—begodu lokho kwenza bona babe muncu okubabako—begodu—netjhukela yebhali nezinto ezinjalo ezenza abantwana babe nomoya omnandi. Ngifisa ngathana abantu bazi lokho : khona-ke bengekhe babe mnyama ngalokho, uyazi—”

Besele ayikhohliwe i-Duchess ngesikhatheso, begodu wethuka kancani nakazwa ilizwi lakhe eduze nendlebe yakhe. "Ucabanga ngokuthileko, sthandwa sami, begodu lokho kwenza bona ukhohlwe ukukhuluma. Angeze ngakutjela nje bona khuyini ukuziphatha kwalokho, kodwana ngizokukhumbula msinyana."

"Mhlamunye ayikho," u-Alice watjho.

“Tut, tut, mntwana!” kwatjho uNkosikazi. “Yoke into inendlela yokuziphatha, nangabe ungayifumana.” Bese wazitjhidela eduze kwaka-Alice njengombana akhuluma.

U-Alice bekangakuthandi ukuhlala aseduze naye: kokuthoma, ngombana i-Duchess beyimbi khulu ; begodu okwesibili, ngombana bekanebude obufaneleko bokubeka isilevu sakhe ehlombe laka-Alice, begodu bekuyisilevu esibukhali. Kodwana, bekangakuthandi ukuba luhlaza, yeke wabekezelela ngendlela angakghona ngayo.

"Umdlalo uragela phambili ngcono nje," watjho, ngokuragela phambili nengcoco kancani.

"'Kunjalo," kwatjho i-Duchess: "begodu ukuziphatha kwalokho ku-'O, 'lithando, 'lithando, elenza umhlaba ujikeleze!'"

"Omunye umuntu wathi," u-Alice wahlebela, "ukuthi kwenziwa mumuntu woke ozikhathalela izinto zakhe!"

"Ah, kuhlekuhle! Kutjho into efanako," kwatjho i-Duchess, igebha isilevu sayo esincani esibukhali ehlombe laka-Alice njengombana yengeza, "begodu ukuziphatha kwalokho kukuthi—'Tjheja umqondo, begodu imisindo izozitlhogomela.'"

"Ukuthanda kangangani ukuthola ukuziphatha ezintweni!" U-Alice wacabanga ngaphakathi kwakhe.

"Ngiyazibuza bona kubayini ngingafaki ingalo yami esinqeni sakho," kwatjho i-Duchess ngemva kokuma kancani: "isizathu kukobana, ngiyathandabuza ngobujamo be-flamingo yakho. Ngingazama na?"

"Angaluma," u-Alice waphendula ngokutjheja, angazizwa atshwenyekile ngokuthi kulingwe ukuhlolwa.

"Kuliqiniso," kwatjho i-Duchess: "ama-flamingo nesinaphi zombili ziyaluma. Begodu ukuziphatha kwalokho kukuthi—'Iinyoni ezineensiba zihlangana ndawonye.'"

"Isinaphi kwaphela esingasiyo inyoni," kwatjho u-Alice.

"Kulungile, njengenjwayelo," kwatjho i-Duchess: "yindlela ecacileko onayo yokubeka izinto!"

“ Ngicabanga bona yiminerali,” kwatjho u-Alice.

"Kunjalo," kwatjho i-Duchess, egade ibonakala ikulungele ukuvumelana nakho koke u-Alice akutjhoko; "kunemayini ekulu yesinaphi eduze lapha. Begodu ukuziphatha kwalokho kukuthi—'Nakunengi okwami, kuzokuba kuncani okwakho.'"

“Hawu, ngiyazi!” kwababaza u-Alice, obekangakalaleli amazwi wokugcina, "yimifino. Ayibukeki njengenye, kodwana injalo."

"Ngiyavumelana nawe," kwatjho i-Duchess; "begodu ukuziphatha kwalokho kukuthi—'Yiba ngilokho ongabonakala uyikho'—namkha nangabe ungathanda ukukubekwa lula—'Ungalokothi uzicabange ungabi ngenye indlela kunalokho okungabonakala kwabanye ukuthi lokho ebewuyikho namkha ongaba yikho bekungakafaneli lokho ebewuyikho ebekungabonakala kibo.'”

"Ngicabanga bona kufanele ngikuzwisise ngcono lokho," u-Alice watjho ngesizotha, "nangabe bengikutlolile phasi: kodwana angeze ngakghona ukukulandela njengombana utjho."

"Lokho akusililitho kilokho engingakutjho nangabe ngikhetha," i-Duchess yaphendula, ngelizwi elithabileko.

“Thandaza ungazihluphi ngokukhuluma isikhathi eside ukudlula lapho,” kwatjho u-Alice.

“Hawu, ungakhulumi ngenkinga!” kwatjho uNkosikazi. “Ngikupha isipho sakho koke engikutjhoko.”

“Umhlobo wesipho esitjhiphileko!” kwacabanga u-Alice. “Ngithabile ngokuthi abanikeli izipho zelanga lamabeletho ezifana nalezo!” Kodwana azange azimisele ukutjho ngokuzwakalako.

“Uyacabanga godu?” kubuza i-Duchess, ngokunye ukugebha isilevu sakhe esincani esibukhali.

“Nginelungelo lokucabanga,” kwatjho u-Alice ngokubukhali, ngombana besele athoma ukutshwenyeka kancani.

"Kulungile," kwatjho i-Duchess, "njengombana iingulube kufuze zindize; begodu m—"

Kodwana lapha, ngokumangala okukhulu kwaka-Alice, ilizwi le-Duchess laphela, ngitjho naphakathi kwegama lakhe alithandako elithi 'ukuziphatha,' begodu ingalo ebegade ihlanganiswe kweyakhe yathoma ukuthuthumela. U-Alice waqala phezulu, begodu bekujame iNdlovukazi phambi kwabo, imikhono yayo igoqene, iqhwaba njengesiwuruwuru.

“Ilanga elihle, Mhlonitjhwa!” u-Duchess wathoma ngephimbo eliphasi, elibuthakathaka.

"Nje, ngiyakuyelelisa," kwamemeza iKosikazi, inyathela phasi njengombana ikhuluma; "Kungaba wena nofana ikhanda lakho kufanele liphume, begodu lokho kungakapheli nesikhathi esifitjhani! Khetha!"

I-Duchess yathatha isiqunto sayo, begodu yahamba msinyana.

"Asiragele phambili nomdlalo," iNdlovukazi yathi ku-Alice; begodu u-Alice bekathukwe khulu bona angatjho litho, kodwana wamlandela kancanikancani wabuyela emuva endaweni yokudlala i-croquet.

Ezinye iimvakatjhi bezisebenzise ithuba lokungabi khona kweNdlovukazi, begodu beziphumule emthunzini: nanyana kunjalo, ngesikhatjhana eziyibonako, zabuyela emdlalweni msinyana, iNdlovukazi yavele yathi ukulibaziseka kwesikhatjhana kuzokwenza bona babulale.

Isikhathi soke ebebadlala iNdlovukazi beyingapheli ukuphikisana nabanye abadlali, begodu irhuwelela ithi “Susa ngehloko yakhe!” nofana "Susa ngehloko yakhe!" Labo egade abagweba bavalelwa bamasotja, ebegade kufuze batjhiye ukuba ma-arch ukwenza lokhu, kangangobana ekupheleni kwehhafu ye-awa nofana ngaphezulu bekungasekho ama-arch, begodu boke abadlali, ngaphandle kweKosi, iNdlovukazi, no-Alice, bebasejele begodu bagwetjwe ukubulawa.

Bese iNdlovukazi yajama, iphelelwe mumoya, yathi ku-Alice, "Uke wayibona i-Mock Turtle?"

"Awa," kwatjho u-Alice. “Angazi nokuthi khuyini i-Mock Turtle.”

“Yinto eyenziwe ngayo iSobho yeFudu,” kwatjho iKosikazi.

"Angikaze ngiyibone, nofana ngizwe ngayo," kwatjho u-Alice.

"Woza-ke," kwatjho iKosikazi, "uzokutjela umlando wakhe."

Njengombana bebakhamba bobabili, u-Alice wezwa iKosi ithi ngelizwi eliphasi, enkampanini jikelele, "Noke nixolelwa." “Woza, yinto ehle leyo !” wazitjela, ngombana bekazizwa angakathabi ngenani lokubulawa kweNdlovukazi ebegade liyalile.

Msinyana bafika ku-Gryphon, ilele khulu elangeni. (Nangabe awazi bona i-Gryphon iyini, qala isithombe.) “Phakama, into evilaphako!” kwatjho iNdlovukazi, "begodu uthathe intombi le uyokubona i-Mock Turtle, nokuzwa umlando wayo. Kufanele ngibuyele emuva ngiyokubona ngemva kokubulawa okuthileko engikuyaleleko;" begodu wakhamba, watjhiya u-Alice yedwa ne-Gryphon. U-Alice bekangathandi ukubukeka kwesidalwa, kodwana ngokupheleleko bekacabanga bona kungaba kuhle ukuhlala naso njengokulandela iNdlovukazi embi: yeke walinda.

I-Gryphon yahlala phasi yahlikihla amehlo wayo: bese yaqala iNdlovukazi bekwaba kulapho ingasabonakali: bese yahleka. “Kumnandi kangangani!” kwatjho i-Gryphon, ingcenye yayo, ingcenye ku-Alice.

“ Kuyini okumnandi?” Kutjho u-Alice.

"Kubayini, yena ," kwatjho i-Gryphon. "Kuyinto ayithandako, ukuthi: abakaze babulale umuntu, uyazi. Woza!"

“Wonke umuntu uthi 'woza!' lapha,” kwacabanga u-Alice, njengombana aragela phambili kancanikancani: “Angikaze ngibe nomthetho onjalo epilweni yami yoke, angikaze!”

Bebangakahambi kude ngaphambi kobana babone i-Mock Turtle kude, ihlezi idanile begodu inesizungu edwaleni elincani, begodu, njengombana bebatjhidela, u-Alice bekamzwa abubulela ngasuthi ihliziywakhe izokuphuka. Wamzwela khulu. “Kuyini ubuhlungu bakhe?” wabuza i-Gryphon, begodu i-Gryphon yaphendula, pheze ngamezwi afanako nangaphambilini, "Koke lokho akuthandako, ukuthi: akanawo usizi, uyazi. Woza!"

Nje-ke bakhuphukela ku-Mock Turtle, owabaqala ngamehlo amakhulu azele iinyembezi, kodwana azange atjho litho.

"Intombazana le," kwatjho i-Gryphon, "ifuna ukwazi umlando wakho, iyakwazi."

"Ngizomtjela," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle ngephimbo elijulileko, elinganalitho: "hlalani phasi, nobabili, ningakhulumi litho ngize ngiqede."

Njeke bahlala phasi, begodu akekho owakhulumako imizuzu ethileko. U-Alice wazitjela wathi, “Angiboni bona angaqeda njani, nange angathomi.” Kodwana walinda ngesineke.

"Kwake," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle ekugcineni, ngokububula okukhulu, "Bengiyi-Turtle yamambala."

Amagama la alandelwa kuthula isikhathi eside, kwaphulwa ngokubabaza ngezikhathi ezithileko okuthi “Hjckrrh!” ukusuka ku-Gryphon, nokulila okukhulu kwe-Mock Turtle. U-Alice bekazokuvuka athi, "Ngiyathokoza, mnumzana, ngendaba yakho ekarisako," kodwana bekangakghoni ukuzibamba ngokucabanga bona kuzokuba nokunye okuzako, yeke wahlala phasi begodu azange atjho litho.

"Ngesikhathi sisesebancani," iFudu yaragela phambili ekugcineni, ngokuthula, nanyana isalila kancani kancani, "besiya esikolweni elwandle. Inkosi bekuyiFudu endala—besijayele ukuyibiza ngokuthi nguFudu—"

“Kubayini umbize ngoFudu, nangabe bekangasiye?” Kubuza u-Alice.

"Simbize ngoFudu ngombana wasifundisa," kwatjho uFudu ngokuthukuthela: "kwamambala uyisithutha!"

"Kufanele ube namahloni ngokuzibuza umbuzo olula kangaka," kwangezelela i-Gryphon; begodu bobabili bahlala bathule begodu baqala u-Alice ohluphekileko, obekazizwa alungele ukutjhinga phasi. Ekugcineni i-Gryphon yathi ku-Mock Turtle, "Ragela phambili, mfo! Ungahlali ilanga loke!" begodu waragela phambili ngamezwi la:

“Iye, saya esikolweni elwandle, nanyana ungakholwa—”

“Angikaze ngithi angikwazi!” kuphazamisa u-Alice.

"Wenze njalo," kwatjho uFudu.

“Yima ilimi lakho!” kwangezelela i-Gryphon, ngaphambi kobana u-Alice akhulume godu. I-Mock Turtle yaragela phambili.

"Besinefundo ehle khulu—eqinisweni, besisiya esikolweni qobe langa—"

" Nami ngike ngaya esikolweni semini," kwatjho u-Alice; "akutlhogeki bona uzikhakhazise kangaka."

“Ngezinto ezingezelelweko?” kubuza i-Mock Turtle ngokutshwenyeka okuncani.

"Iye," kwatjho u-Alice, "sifunde isiFulentshi nomvumo."

“Begodu ukugeza?” kwatjho uFudu okhohlakeleko.

“Ngokuqinisekileko awa!” Kutjho u-Alice ngokucasuka.

“Ah! khona-ke esakho besingasiso isikolo esihle khulu,” kwatjho iFudu yokuHlekisa ngephimbo lokukhululeka okukhulu. "Nje-ke kithi besele bathi ekupheleni kwemali, 'IsiFulentshi, umvumo, nokuhlanza — okungezelelweko.'"

"Bewungekhe uyifune khulu," kwatjho u-Alice; "ukuhlala ngaphasi kwelwandle."

"Bengingakwazi ukuyifunda." Kutjho uFudu wokukhohlisa ngokububula. "Ngithathe ikhosi ejayelekileko kwaphela."

“Bekuyini lokho?” kubuza u-Alice.

"Ukutjhinga nokutjhinga, vele, ukuthoma," kwaphendula i-Mock Turtle; "bese kuthi amagatja ahlukileko we-Arithmetic—Ukuzimisela, Ukuphazanyiswa, Ukuhlambalaza, Nokuhlekisa."

"Angikaze ngizwe nge-'Uglification,'" u-Alice wazimisela wathi. “Khuyini?”

I-Gryphon yaphakamisa iinyawo zayo zombili ngokumangala. "Ini! Angikaze ngizwe ngokuba kumbi!" kwamemeza. “Uyazi bona khuyini ukuhlobisa, ngiyacabanga?”

"Iye," kwatjho u-Alice ngokuzaza: "kutjho—ukwenza—nanyana yini—ibe yinhle."

"Kulungile-ke," i-Gryphon waraga, "nangabe awazi bona ukuhlambalaza kuyini, uyisithutha ."

U-Alice akhenge azizwe akhuthazeka bona abuze eminye imibuzo ngakho, yeke wajika waya ku-Mock Turtle, wathi "Yini enye okufuze uyifunde?"

"Kulungile, bekuneMfihlakalo," i-Mock Turtle yaphendula, ibala iinhloko ezisema-flappers wayo, "-Imfihlakalo, yakade neyesimanjemanje, nge-Seaography: bese kuba yi-Drawling--Drawling-master bekuyi-conger-eel endala, ebegade iza kanye ngeveke: beyifundisa ukuDweba, ukuThuthuka, nokuQala ngama-Coil."

“Bekunjani lokho ?” Kutjho u-Alice.

"Kulungile, angeze ngakutjengisa mina," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle: "Ngiqinile khulu. Begodu i-Gryphon azange iyifunde."

"Benginganaso isikhathi," kwatjho i-Gryphon: "Ngaya kumfundisi we-Classics, nanyana kunjalo. Bekayinkala endala, bekanjalo ."

"Angikaze ngiye kuye," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle ngokububula: "bekafundisa ukuhleka nokudana, bebavame ukutjho."

"Wenza njalo, wenza njalo," kwatjho i-Gryphon, ibubula ngesikhathi sayo; begodu zombili iinlwana zafihla ubuso bazo eenyaweni zazo.

“Bewufunda ama-awa amangaki ngelanga?” Kutjho u-Alice, aphuthuma ukutjhugulula indaba.

"Ama-awa alitjhumi ngelanga lokuthoma," kwatjho i-Mock Turtle: "ama-awa alithoba ngelanga elilandelako, njalonjalo."

“Ihlelo elihle kangangani!” Kubabaza u-Alice.

"Kungakho zibizwa ngokuthi ziimfundo," kuphawula i-Gryphon: "ngombana ziyancipha ilanga nelanga."

Lokhu bekungumqondo omutjha ku-Alice, begodu wacabanga ngawo kancani ngaphambi kobana enze amazwi akhe alandelako. "Nje-ke ilanga letjhumi nanye kufuze bona bekuliholideyi?"

"Kunjalo," kwatjho uFudu.

"Begodu wenze njani ngelanga letjhumi nambili?" U-Alice waragela phambili ngokuzimisela.

"Kwanele lokho ngeemfundo," uGryphon waphazamisa ngephimbo eliqinileko: "mtjele okuthileko ngemidlalo nje."
