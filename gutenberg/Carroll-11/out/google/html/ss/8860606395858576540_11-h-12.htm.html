Bufakazi ba-Alice

"Lapha!" kwamemeta Alice, akhohlwe ngalokuphelele kutsi abemkhulu kangakanani emizuzwini lembalwa leyengcile, futsi wagcuma ngekushesha kangangekutsi wajika ngetulu kwelibhokisi lemajaji ngemphetho wesiketi sakhe, watfukutselisa onkhe emajaji etinhloko tesicumbi lesingephansi, futsi lapho bebalele bakhanya kakhulu, ngengoti watfukutsela evikini langaphambilini.

“Oh, ngiyakucolela !” wamemeta ngelivi lekukhatsateka lokukhulu, wacala kuticosha futsi ngekushesha, ngoba ingoti yemfishi yeligolide beyisolo igijima enhloko yakhe, futsi bekaneluhlobo lwemcondvo longakacaci kutsi kumele tibutfwe ngaso leso sikhatsi bese tibuyiselwa ebhokisini le-jury, noma titawufa.

“Lelicala angeke lichubeke,” kwasho iNkhosi ngelivi lelikhulu kakhulu, “kuze kube ngulapho onkhe emajaji abuyele etindzaweni tawo letifanele— onkhe ,” waphindzaphindza ngekugcizelela kakhulu, abuka kakhulu Alice njengobe asho njalo.

Alice wabuka libhokisi-lejury, wabona kutsi, ngekuphuthuma kwakhe, abeke iLizard enhloko phansi, futsi lentfo lencane lephuyile beyiphephetsa umsila wayo ngendlela ledzabukisako, ingakhoni ngalokuphelele kunyakata. Ngekushesha nje lapho licembu lebagwebi selilulame kancane ekwetfukeni kwekutfukutsela, futsi emaslate abo nemapensela abo atfolakele futsi abuyiselwa kubo, bacala kusebenta ngekutimisela kakhulu kubhala umlandvo walengoti, bonkhe ngaphandle kweLizard, lebeyibonakala incotjwe kakhulu kwenta noma ngabe yini ngaphandle kwekuhlala nemlomo wayo uvulekile.

Alice watsi: “Kute lutfo.”

“Kubaluleke kakhulu loko,” kwasho iNkhosi, iphendvukela emajaji. Bebasandza kucala kubhala loku emaslayithi abo, ngesikhatsi Nogwaja Lomhlophe aphazamisa: “ Un important, Mhlonishwa wakho usho, vele,” washo ngelivi lelihlonipha kakhulu, kepha akhukhumala futsi enta buso kuye njengoba akhuluma.

Labanye bebagwebi balibhala phansi kutsi “kubalulekile,” kantsi labanye “ababalulekile.” Alice bekakubona loku, njengoba abesedvute kakhulu kutsi angabuka ngetulu kwema slates abo; “kodvwa akusho lutfo kancane,” wacabanga ngekhatsi kwakhe.

Ngalesikhatsi iNkhosi, lebeseyimatasatasa sikhatsi lesitsite ibhala encwadzini yayo yekubhala, yamemeta yatsi “Thula!” futsi wafundza encwadzini yakhe letsi, “Umtsetfo Wemashumi lamane nakubili. Bonkhe bantfu labaphakeme ngetulu kwemayela kutsi baphume enkantolo .”

INkhosi yatsi: “Ngumtsetfo lomdzala kakhulu kulencwadzi.”

INkhosi yagucuka yaba mhlophe, yavala incwadzi yayo yekubhala ngekushesha. Watsi kumajaji ngelivi leliphansi futsi lelivevetela watsi: “Cabanga ngesijubo senu.”

“Kukhona bufakazi lobunyenti lobutawufika, ngicela Mhlonishwa wakho,” kwasho Nogwaja Lomhlophe, agcuma ngekushesha lokukhulu; “leliphepha lisandza kulandvwa.”

“Yini lekhona kuyo?” Kusho iNdlovukati.

“Angikayivuli,” kwasho Nogwaja Lomhlophe, “kepha kubonakala shengatsi yincwadzi, lebhalwe siboshwa ku—kumuntfu lotsite.”

“Kufanele kutsi bekunjalo,” kwasho iNkhosi, “ngaphandle nangabe bekubhalelwe muntfu, lokuyintfo lengakavami, uyati.”

“Licondziswe kubani?” kusho lomunye wemajaji.

“Akucondziswa nhlobo,” kwasho Nogwaja Lomhlophe; “ecinisweni, kute lokubhalwe ngaphandle .” Wavula leliphepha njengobe akhuluma, futsi wengeta watsi “Akusiyo incwadzi, phela: isethi yemavesi.”

“Tibhalwe ngesandla sesiboshwa yini?” kubuta lomunye wemajaji.

“Cha, abanjalo,” kwasho Nogwaja Lomhlophe, “futsi loko yintfo lemangalisa kakhulu ngako.” (Lijaji bonkhe babukeka badidekile.)

INkhosi yatsi: “Kufanele kutsi ulingisa sandla salomunye umuntfu.” (Ijury yonkhe yaphindze yakhanya.)

“Ngiyakucela Mhlonishwa wakho,” kwasho iKnave, “angizange ngiyibhale, futsi abakhoni kufakazela kutsi ngiyibhalile: kute ligama lelisayinwe ekugcineni.”

INkhosi yatsi: “Kube awuzange uyisayine, loko kwenta lendzaba ibe yimbi kakhulu. Kufanele kutsi bewusho lokubi lokutsite, ngaphandle kwaloko ngabe usayine libito lakho njengemuntfu lowetsembekile.”

Kwaba nekushaywa kwetandla jikelele ngaloko: bekuyintfo yekucala lehlakaniphile mbamba iNkhosi lebeyiyishito ngalelo langa.

“Loko kufakazela licala lakhe,” kwasho iNdlovukati.

“Akufakazeli lutfo lolunjalo!” kusho Alice. “Hayi, awati nekutsi tikhuluma ngani!”

INkhosi yatsi: “Tifundzeni.”

LoNogwaja Lomhlophe wagcoka tibuko takhe. “Ngicale kuphi, ngicela Mhlonishwa wakho?” kubuta yena.

“Cala ekucaleni,” kwasho iNkhosi ngalokukhulu, “uchubeke uze ufike ekugcineni: bese uyama.”

“Lobo bufakazi lobubaluleke kakhulu lesike sabuva,” kwasho iNkhosi, ihlikihla tandla tayo; “ngako-ke nyalo ake lijaji—”

“Nangabe kukhona kubo longakuchaza,” kwasho Alice, (bekakhule kakhulu emizuzwini lembalwa leyengcile kangangekutsi bekangakwesabi kancane kumphazamisa), “Ngitamnika emapeni lasitfupha. Angikholwanga kutsi kune-athomu yenshokutsi kuyo.”

Bonkhe labagwebi babhala ema- slate abo batsi, “ Akakholwa kutsi kune-athomu yenshokutsi kuyo,” kodvwa kute ngisho namunye wabo lowazama kuchaza leliphepha.

“Uma kungekho inchazelo kuyo,” kwasho iNkhosi, “loko kusindzisa umhlaba wetinkinga, uyati, njengobe singadzingi kwetama kutfola noma nguyiphi. Futsi noma kunjalo angati,” wachubeka, welula emavesi emadolweni akhe, futsi wawabuka ngeliso linye; “Ngibonakala ngibona inchazelo letsite kuto, phela. “— watsi angikhoni kubhukusha —” awukhoni kubhukusha, angitsi?” wengeta, aphendukela kuKnave.

I Knave yanikina inhloko ngekudzabuka. “Ngifana nayo yini?” kwasho yena. (Loku nakanjani akazange , kwentiwa ngokuphelele ngekhadibhodi.)

“Kulungile, kuze kube ngunyalo,” kwasho iNkhosi, futsi yachubeka yabubula ngetulu kwemavesi ngekhatsi kwayo: “’ Siyati kutsi kuliciniso —’ lelo lijaji, vele—’ Ngamnika linye, bamnika lamabili —’ kungani, kufanele kutsi nguloko lakwenta ngema-tart, uyati—”

“Kodvwa, kuyachubeka ' bonkhe babuya kuye kuwe ,'” kwasho Alice.

“Phela, balapho!” kusho iNkhosi ngekuncoba, ikhomba emathathi lasetafuleni. “Kute lokungacaca kwendlula loko . Bese futsi—’ ngaphambi kwekutsi abe nalesifo —’ awuzange sewube nekuhlaselwa, sitsandvwa sami, ngicabanga kutsi?” washo kuNdlovukati.

"Kute!" kusho iNdlovukati ngekutfukutsela, iphosa inkstand kuLizard njengoba ikhuluma. (Bill lomncane lobekanelishwa abeyekele kubhala kuleli-slate lakhe ngemunwe munye, njengoba atfola kutsi kute luphawu; kepha nyalo waphindze wacala ngekushesha, asebentisa inki, lebeyicondzisa phansi ebusweni bakhe, uma nje beyihlala.)

“Ngako-ke lamagama akakufaneli ,” kwasho iNkhosi, ibuka yonkhe indzawo enkantolo imomotseka. Kwathula kakhulu.

“Kuyintfo yekukhuluma!” iNkhosi yengeta ngelivi lelitfukutsele, futsi wonkhe umuntfu wahleka, “Ake lijaji licabangisise ngesijubo salo.” kusho iNkhosi, cishe sikhatsi semashumi lamabili ngalelo langa.

“Cha, cha!” Kusho iNdlovukati. “Sigwebo kucala—sincumo ngemuva kwaloko.”

“Tintfo kanye nembhedo!” kusho Alice ngekumemeta. “Umcondvo wekutsi kube nesigwebo kucala!”

“Bamba lulwimi lwakho!” kusho iNdlovukati, iphenduka ibe bubende.

“Ngeke ngivume!” kusho Alice.

“Off ngenhloko yakhe!” kumemeta iNdlovukati ngelivi layo leliphakeme. Kute lowanyakata.

“Ngubani lokunakekelako?” kusho Alice, (bekakhule abe ngumkhulu ngalesikhatsi). “Awusilo lutfo ngaphandle kwephakethe lemakhadi!”

Kuloku lonkhe leliphakethe lavuka emoyeni, lafika lindizela phansi kuye: wamemeta kancane, hhafu wekwesaba nehhafu yekutfukutsela, wazama kubashaya, watikhandza alele eceleni kwelwandle, nenhloko yakhe emathangeni adzadzewabo, lobekabhusha ngebumnene emacembe lafile phansi ebusweni labekane flutter.

“Vuka, Alice lotsandzekako!” kusho dzadzewabo; “Hayi, sewulele sikhatsi lesidze kangakanani!”

“O, ngibe neliphupho lelilangatelela kwati!” kusho Alice, futsi watjela dzadzewabo, njengobe bekakhumbula, onkhe lama-Adventures akhe langaketayeleki losandza kufundza ngawo; futsi watsi nakecedza, dzadzewabo wamcabuza, watsi, “ Bekuliphupho lekulangatelela kwati, sitsandvwa sami, nakanjani: kepha nyalo gijima ungene etiyeni lakho; sekuhlwile.” Ngako Alice wasukuma wabaleka, acabanga ngesikhatsi agijima, njengobe angakhona, kutsi bekuliphupho lelihle kangakanani.

Kepha dzadzewabo wahlala phansi njengobe nje amshiya, ancike inhloko yakhe esandleni sakhe, abuka lilanga lelishonako, futsi acabanga nga-Alice lomncane nawo onkhe ema-Adventures akhe lamahle, kwaze kwaba ngulapho naye acala kuphupha ngemuva kwemfashini, futsi leli bekuliphupho lakhe:—

Kwekucala, waphupha nga-Alice lomncane yena matfupha, futsi kwaphindze futsi tandla letincane tabambeka emadolweni akhe, futsi emehlo lakhanyako lalangatelelako bekabuka etulu kuye—bekava emathoni elivi lakhe, futsi abona leyo ndlela lencane lemangalisako yekujikijela inhloko yakhe kute agcine emuva tinwele letizulazulako lebetitawuhlala tingena eluhlwini lwakhe, she who stilld to indzawo letungelete yena yaphila netidalwa letimangalisako teliphupho ladzadzewabo lomncane.

Tjani lobudze bebukhala etinyaweni takhe njengobe Nogwaja Lomhlophe aphutfuma—leligundvwane lebelitfukile lafafata indlela yalo echibini lelisedvute—bekuva kukhala kwetinkomishi telitiye njengobe Mvundla waMashi nebangani bakhe bebahlanganyela kudla kwabo lokungapheli, futsi livi lelikhalako leNdlovukati beliyalela kutsi aphume execu- kutimula edolweni le Duchess, ngesikhatsi emapuleti netitja tishaya titungeletile—kwaphindze kwakhala kumemeta kwe Gryphon, kukhwesha kwe slate-pencil ya Lizard, kanye nekucindzeteleka kwetingulube te guinea leticindzetelekile, kwagcwalisa umoya, kuhlanganiswe nekukhala kwe miser lokukhashane kwe Turtle.

Ngako wahlala phansi, avaliwe emehlo, futsi hhafu watikholwa kutsi useWonderland, noma bekati kutsi kufanele kepha aphindze awavule, futsi konkhe bekutawushintja kube liciniso lelibutsakatsaka—tjani bebutawube bukhala nje emoyeni, futsi lelichibi belitawugucuka libe nekuvunguta kwemhlanga—tinkomishi tetiye letidvumako betitawushintja tibe ngemavi lakhalako, netinkomishi tetiye letikhalako betitawushintja tibe ngemavi lakhalako. kwemfana wemelusi—nekutsimula kwemntfwana, kumemeta kwe Gryphon, kanye nawo onkhe lamanye emasindvo lamangalisako, bekutawushintja (bekati) kube kumemeta lokudidekile kwelipulazi-leliphitsitelako—ngesikhatsi kukhala kwetinkhomo letikhashane bekutawutsatsa indzawo yekukhala lokusindzako kwe Mock Turtle.

Kwekugcina, waticabanga kutsi lodzadzewabo lomncane lofanako, ngemuva kwesikhatsi, abetawuba njani nguye umfati lokhulile; nekutsi bekatawugcina njani, kuyo yonkhe iminyaka yakhe levutsiwe, inhlitiyo lelula nalenelutsandvo yebuntfwana bakhe: nekutsi bekatawubutsana njani ngalabanye bantfwana bakhe labancane, futsi ente emehlo abo akhanye futsi alangatelele ngendzaba lenyenti lemangalisako, mhlawumbe ngisho nangeliphupho leWonderland lasendvulo: nekutsi bekatawutiva njani ngato tonkhe lusizi lwabo lolulula lwekukhumbula kwakhe, futsi atfole konkhe kujabula kwabo lokulula. imphilo yebantfwana, kanye nemalanga lajabulisako asehlobo.
