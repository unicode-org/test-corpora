SEHLUKO VII.
Liphathi Lelitiye Lelihlanyako

Bekunelitafula lebelibekwe ngaphansi kwesihlahla phambi kwendlu, futsi iMarch Hare ne Hatter bebaphuza litiye kulo: iDormouse beyihleti emkhatsini wabo, ilele kakhulu, futsi labanye lababili bebalisebentisa njengemcamelo, baphumula tindololwane tabo kulo, futsi bakhuluma ngetulu kwenhloko yalo. Alice wacabanga watsi: “Akukhululeki kakhulu kuDormouse; “kuphela, njengobe ilele, ngicabanga kutsi ayinankinga.”

Lelitafula belilikhulu, kodvwa bonkhe labatsatfu bebagcwele ndzawonye ekhoneni linye lalo: “Kute indzawo! Kute indzawo!” bakhala ngesikhatsi babona Alice eta. “Kunendzawo lenyenti !” kusho Alice ngekutfukutsela, futsi wahlala phansi esitulweni lesikhulu sekuhlala ngakulolunye luhlangotsi lwelitafula.

“Tsatsa liwayini,” kwasho Umvundla waMashi ngelivi lelikhutsatako.

Alice wabuka yonkhe indzawo etafuleni, kodvwa bekute lutfo kulo ngaphandle kwelitiye. Waphawula watsi: “Angiboni liwayini.”

“Kute,” kwasho Mvundla waMashi.

Alice watsi ngekutfukutsela watsi: “Ngako-ke bekungasiyo inhlonipho kakhulu kutsi uyinikete.”

“Bekungasiko kuphatseka kabi kakhulu kuwe kutsi uhlale phansi ungakamenywa,” kwasho Mvundla waMashi.

Alice watsi: “Bengingati kutsi litafula lakho ; “ibekelwe bantfu labanyenti kakhulu labangetulu kwalabatsatfu.”

“Tinwele takho tifuna kusikwa,” kwasho Lomgcoko. Besekuphele sikhatsi lesidze abuka Alice ngekulangatelela lokukhulu, futsi bekuyinkhulumo yakhe yekucala.

Alice watsi ngendlela letsite: “Kufanele ufundze kungakhulumi tintfo letiphatselene nawe.” “kuluhlata kakhulu.”

Lomgcoko wavula emehlo akhe kakhulu ngekuva loku; kodvwa konkhe labekusho bekutsi: “Kungani ligundvwane lifana nelideski lekubhala?”

“Wota, sitawujabula nyalo!” kucabanga Alice. Wangete watsi: “Ngiyajabula kutsi sebacale kubuta timphicaphicwano.—Ngiyakholwa kutsi ngingakucagela loko.”

“Usho kutsi ucabanga kutsi ungayitfola yini imphendvulo yayo?” kusho uMvundla waMashi.

Alice watsi: “Kunjalo ngco.

“Ngako-ke kufanele usho kutsi usho kutsini,” kwachubeka uMvundla waMashi.

Alice waphendvula ngekushesha watsi: “Ngiyati; “okungenani—okungenani ngisho loko lengikushoko—yintfo lefanako, uyati.”

“Akusiyo intfo lefanako kancane!” kusho uMgcoko. “Ungasho nje kutsi ‘Ngibona loko lengikudlako’ kuyafana nekutsi ‘Ngidla loko lengikubonako’!”

Umvundla waMashi wengeta watsi: “Ungasho nje kutsi ‘Ngiyakutsandza loko lengikutfolako’ kuyafana nekutsi ‘Ngitfola loko lengikutsandzako’!”

“Ungasho nje,” kwengeta iDormouse, lebeyibonakala ikhuluma ebutfongweni bayo, “kutsi ‘Ngiphefumula nangilele’ kuyafana nekutsi ‘Ngilala nangiphefumula’!”

“ Kuyintfo lefanako nawe,” kwasho Lomgcini, futsi lapha inkhulumo yehla, futsi licembu lahlala lithule umzuzu munye, ngesikhatsi Alice acabanga ngako konkhe labengakukhumbula ngemagwababa kanye nemadeski ekubhala, lebekungasiko lokunyenti.

UmHatter nguye wekucala lowaphula kuthula. “Lilanga lini lenyanga?” washo, agucukela ku-Alice: bekakhiphe liwashi lakhe ephaketheni lakhe, futsi bekalibuka angakhululeki, alinyakatisa njalo ngalesinye sikhatsi, futsi alibambe endlebeni yakhe.

Alice wacabanga kancane, wase utsi “Lesine.”

“Emalanga lamabili aphutsa!” kwabubula uMgcoko. “Ngikutjele kutsi ibhotela angeke ihambisane nemisebenti!” wengeta abuka umvundla waMashi ngekutfukutsela.

Umvundla waMashi waphendvula ngebumnene watsi: “Bekuyibhotela lencono kakhulu .”

“Yebo, kepha kufanele kutsi naletinye timvuthuluka tingene,” kwakhonona uMgcoko: “bekungakafaneli kutsi utifake ngemmese wesinkhwa.”

LoMvundla waMashi watsatsa leliwashi walibuka ngekudzabuka: wase ulicwilisa enkomishini yakhe yelitiye, waphindze walibuka: kepha akazange acabange lutfo loluncono langalusho kunekuphawula kwakhe kwekucala, “Bekuyibhotela lencono kakhulu , uyati.”

Alice bekasolo abuka ngetulu kwelihlombe lakhe ngekulangatelelela lwati. “Leliwashi lelihlekisako!” waphawula. “Lisho lilanga lenyanga, futsi alisho kutsi ngusiphi sikhatsi!”

“Kungani kufanele?” kububula uMgcini. “Iwashi lakho liyakutjela yini kutsi ngumnyaka muni?”

Alice waphendvula ngekushesha watsi: “Yebo cha: kodvwa loko kungenca yekutsi kuhlala umnyaka munye sikhatsi lesidze kangaka ndzawonye.”

“Lokunjalo nje ngami ,” kwasho Lomgcoko.

Alice weva adidekile ngendlela leyesabekako, inkhulumo yaThe Hatter bekubonakala shengatsi ayinalo luhlobo lwenchazelo kuyo, futsi nomakunjalo bekusiNgisi ngalokucinisekile. Watsi ngesizotsa ngendlela langakhona ngayo: “Angikuvisi kahle.”

“LeDormouse seyilele futsi,” kwasho uMgcoko, wase utfululela litiye lelincane lelishisako emphumulweni wayo.

Le Dormouse yanikina inhloko ngekuphelelwa sineke, yase itsi, ingavuli emehlo ayo, “Yebo, yebo; loko nje lebengitawuphawula mine.”

“Sewuyicagele yini imphicaphicwano?” kusho uMgcoko, aphindze agucukela ku-Alice.

Alice waphendvula watsi: “Cha, ngiyayekela, yini imphendvulo?”

“Anginawo ngisho nemcondvo lomncane,” kwasho Lomgcoko.

“Nami,” kwasho Mvundla waMashi.

Alice wabubula ngekukhatsala. Watsi: “Ngicabanga kutsi ungahle wente lokutsite lokuncono ngalesikhatsi, kunekutsi usicitse ngekubuta timphicaphicwano letite timphendvulo.”

“Kube bewusati Sikhatsi njengami,” kwasho Lomgcoko, “bewungeke ukhulume ngekusicitsa . Nguye .”

Alice watsi: “Angati kutsi usho kutsini.

“Yebo awukwenti!” kusho uMgcini, ajikijela inhloko yakhe ngekwedzelela. “Nginesibindzi sekutsi awuzange ukhulume ngisho neSikhatsi!”

Alice waphendvula ngekucaphela watsi: “Mhlawumbe cha, kodvwa ngiyati kutsi kufanele ngishaye sikhatsi nangifundza umculo.”

“Ah!, loko kuyalandzisa,” kwasho Umgcoko. "Angeke amelane nekushaywa. Nyalo, kube bewuhlala unesimo lesihle naye, bekatawenta cishe noma ngabe yini lokutsandzako ngeliwashi. Sibonelo, asesitsi bekulihora lesiphohlongo ekuseni, sikhatsi nje sekucala tifundvo: bekutawudzingeka kutsi uhlebe umkhondvo kuSikhatsi, futsi round goes the clock one dinner for a twink!"

(“Ngifisa nje kutsi kube njalo,” kwasho Umvundla waMashi ngekhatsi kwakhe ngekuhleba.)

“Loko bekutawuba yintfo lenhle kakhulu, nakanjani,” kwasho Alice ngekucabanga, “kodvwa-ke—bekungakafaneli ngikulambele, uyati.”

“Hhayi ekucaleni, mhlawumbe,” kwasho Lomgcini, “kodvwa ungagcina kuze kube nguhhafu we-1 sikhatsi lesidze njengobe utsandza.”

“Ngabe leyo yindlela lophatsa ngayo?” Kubuta Alice.

Lomgcoko wanikina inhloko yakhe ngekulila. “Hhayi mine!” kuphendvula yena. “Sacabana ngaMashi lowendlulile—ngaphambi nje kwekutsi ahlanya , uyati—” (akhomba ngesipuni sakhe setiye kuMarch Hare,) “—bekusekhonsathini lenkhulu lebeyiniketwe yiNdlovukati Yetinhlitiyo, futsi bekufanele ngihlabele .

‘Cwebetela, kucwebetela, lulwane loluncane!
Ngiyatibuta kutsi ukuphi!'

Uyayati lengoma mhlawumbe?”

Alice watsi: “Ngike ngeva intfo lefana nalena.

“Kuchubeka, uyati,” kwachubeka Lomgcoko, “ngalendlela:—

‘Etulu kwemhlaba uyandiza, .
Njenge-tea-tray esibhakabhakeni.
Kucwebetela, kucwebetela—’”

Lapha iDormouse yatinyakatisa, yase icala kuhlabela ebutfongweni bayo “ Kucwebetela, kucwebetela, kucwebetela, kucwebetela —” yachubeka sikhatsi lesidze kangangekutsi kwadzingeka kutsi bayiphinye kute ime.

“Kulungile, bengingakalicedzi livesi lekucala,” kwasho Lomgcini, “ngesikhatsi iNdlovukati igcuma futsi imemeta, ‘Ubulala sikhatsi!

“Kuyintfo lembi kakhulu!” Kubabata Alice.

"Futsi kusukela lapho," kwachubeka Lomgcoko ngelivi lelililako, "angeke ente intfo lengimcelako! Kuhlala kulihora lesitfupha nyalo."

Kwafika umcondvo lokhanyako enhloko ya-Alice. “Nguso yini sizatfu lesenta kutsi kubekwe tintfo letinyenti tetiyi lapha ngaphandle?” Wabuta.

“Yebo, nguloko,” kwasho Lomgcini ngekububula: “kuhlala kusikhatsi setiye, futsi asinaso sikhatsi sekugeza tintfo emkhatsini wetikhatsi.”

“Bese uyachubeka uhambahamba, ngicabanga kutsi?” kusho Alice.

“Kanjalo kanye,” kwasho Lomgcoko, “njengobe tintfo tijwayela.”

“Kodvwa kwentekani nawuphindze ufike ekucaleni?” Alice waba nesibindzi sekubuta.

“Ake ucabange kutsi sishintja sihloko,” kwaphazamisa Mvundla waMashi, uyazamula. “Ngiyakhatsala ngaloko. Ngivota lentfombatana isicocela indzaba.”

“Ngiyesaba kutsi angimati munye,” kwasho Alice, kunaloko wetfukile ngalesiphakamiso.

“Ngako-ke iDormouse itawube!” bakhala bobabili. “Vuka, Ligundvwane!” Futsi bayiphinya kuto totimbili tinhlangotsi ngasikhatsi sinye.

I Dormouse yavula amehlo ayo kancane kancane. Watsi ngelivi lelihoshotela futsi lelibutsakatsaka: “Bengingakalali: “Ngiweva onkhe emagama lebeniwasho nine bantfu.”

“Sitjele indzaba!” kusho uMvundla waMashi.

“Yebo, ngicela wente njalo!” kwancenga Alice.

“Futsi usheshe ngaloko,” kwengeta Lomgcoko, “noma utawuphindze ulale ngaphambi kwekutsi kuphele.”

“Ngalesinye sikhatsi bekunabodzadze labancane labatsatfu,” kwacala iDormouse ngekushesha lokukhulu; “futsi emagama abo bekungu-Elsie, Lacie, naTillie; futsi bebahlala phansi kwemtfombo—”

“Bebaphila ngani?” kwasho Alice, lobekahlale anesitsakatelo lesikhulu emibuzweni yekudla nekunatsa.

“Bebaphila nge treacle,” kwasho iDormouse, ngemuva kwekucabanga umzuzu munye noma lemibili.

Alice waphawula ngebumnene watsi: “Bebangeke bakwente loko, uyati; “bebatawugula.”

“Bebanjalo,” kwasho iDormouse; “ ngigula kakhulu .”

Alice wetama kuticabanga kutsi indlela yekuphila lengakavami kangaka beyitawuba njani, kodvwa yamdida kakhulu, ngako wachubeka watsi: “Kodvwa kungani bebahlala phansi emtfonjeni?”

“Tsatsa lelinye litiye,” kwasho Umvundla waMashi ku-Alice, ngekutimisela lokukhulu.

Alice waphendvula ngelivi lelitfukutsele watsi: “Angikabi nalutfo, ngako angeke ngikwati kutsatsa lokunyenti.”

“Usho kutsi angeke utsatse lokuncane ,” kwasho Lomgcini: “kulula kakhulu kutsatsa lokungetulu kwekungatsatsi lutfo.”

Alice watsi: “Kute lowabuta umbono wakho .

“Ngubani lowenta imibono yemuntfu sicu nyalo?” kubuta uMgcoko ngekuncoba.

Alice bekangati kahle kutsi atsini ngaloko: ngako watisita ngekutsi atsatse litiye nesinkhwa-nebhotela, wase uyagucukela eDormouse, waphindza umbuto wakhe. “Kungani bebahlala phansi emtfonjeni?”

Le Dormouse yaphindze yatsatsa umzuzu noma lemibili kutsi icabange ngako, yase itsi, “Bekungumtfombo we treacle.”

“Kute intfo lenjalo!” Alice bekacala kutfukutsela kakhulu, kepha Umgcokisi kanye neMvundla waMashi bahamba “Sh! sh!” futsi iDormouse yaphawula ngekucasuka, “Uma ungakhoni kuba civil, kuncono uticedzele lendzaba.”

“Cha, ngicela uchubeke!” Alice watsi ngekutfobeka lokukhulu; "Ngeke ngiphindze ngiphazamise. Nginesibindzi sekutsi kungenteka kukhona ."

“Munye, mbamba!” kusho iDormouse ngekutfukutsela. Nanobe kunjalo, wavuma kutsi achubeke. “Futsi ngako labodzadze labancane labatsatfu—bebafundza kudvweba, uyati—”

“Badvwebeni?” kusho Alice, akhohlwe ngalokuphelele setsembiso sakhe.

“Treacle,” kwasho iDormouse, ingacabangi ngaso sonkhe lesikhatsi.

“Ngifuna inkomishi lehlantekile,” kwaphazamisa Umgcini, “sonkhe asichubeke endzaweni yinye.”

Wachubeka njengoba akhuluma, futsi iDormouse yamlandzela: iMvundla waMashi watfutsela endzaweni yaDormouse, futsi Alice kunaloko angafuni watsatsa indzawo yeMvundla waMashi. Lomgcini nguye yedvwa lowatfola inzuzo kulolushintjo: futsi Alice bekasesimeni lesihle kakhulu kunakucala, njengoba iMarch Hare beyisandza kutfukutselisa le milk-jug epuletini lakhe.

Alice bekangafisi kuphindze atfukutselise iDormouse, ngako wacala ngekucaphela kakhulu watsi: “Kodvwa angicondzi.

“Ungakhipha emanti emtfonjeni wemanti,” kwasho Lomgcoko; “ngako kufanele ngicabange kutsi ungakhipha i-treacle emtfonjeni we-treacle—eh, siphukuphuku?”

“Kodvwa bebasemtfonjeni ,” Alice watsi kuDormouse, angakhetsi kunaka lamavi ekugcina.

“Yebo bebanjalo,” kwasho iDormouse; “—ngena kahle.”

Lemphendvulo yamdida kakhulu Alice lophuyile, kangangekutsi wavumela iDormouse kutsi ichubeke sikhatsi lesitsite ngaphandle kwekuyiphazamisa.

“Bebafundza kudvweba,” kwachubeka iDormouse, izamula futsi ihlikihla emehlo ayo, ngoba beseyilala kakhulu; “futsi badvweba tonkhe tinhlobo tetintfo—konkhe lokucala nga-M—”

“Kungani kubhalwe M?” kusho Alice.

"Kungani?" kusho uMvundla waMashi.

Alice wathula.

Le Dormouse beseyivale emehlo ayo ngalesikhatsi, futsi beyihamba ilele; kepha, ngekucindzetelwa nguMgcoko, yaphindze yavuka ngekumemeta kancane, yachubeka yatsi: “—loko kucala nga M, njengeticupho-temagundvwane, nenyanga, nekukhumbula, kanye nebuningi—uyati kutsi utsi tintfo “buningi bebuningi”—wake wabona yini intfo lefana nebuningi njengemdvwebo we?”

“Ngempela, nyalo uyangibuta,” kwasho Alice, adidekile kakhulu, “angicabangi kutsi—”

“Ngako-ke akukafaneli ukhulume,” kwasho Lomgcoko.

Lesicucu sekungabi nesimilo besingetulu kwaloko Alice labengakhona kukubeketelela: wasukuma ngekwenyanya lokukhulu, wahamba; iDormouse yalala ngaso leso sikhatsi, futsi kute namunye kulabanye lowatsatsa ngisho nekutsi uyahamba, noma nje abuka emuva kanye noma kabili, hhafu anelitsemba lekutsi batawumbita ngemuva kwakhe: ngesikhatsi sekugcina ababona, bebazama kufaka iDormouse embiteni yetiye.

“Noma ngabe ngukuphi angeke ngiphindze ngiye lapho !” kusho Alice njengobe akhetsa indlela yakhe etigodvweni. “Liphathi letiye lesiliphukuphuku kakhulu lengike ngaba kulo ekuphileni kwami konkhe!”

Watsi nje nakasho loku, wabona kutsi lesinye saletihlahla besinemnyango loholela ngco kuso. “Kuyamangalisa kakhulu loko!” wacabanga. "Kodvwa konkhe kuyafuna kwati lamuhla. Ngicabanga kutsi ngingakhona kungena ngaso leso sikhatsi." Futsi wangena.

Waphindze watikhandza ehholo lelidze, futsi asondzele etafuleni lelincane lengilazi. “Manje, ngitawuphatsa kancono kulesikhatsi,” washo ngekhatsi kwakhe, futsi wacala ngekutsatsa sikhiya lesincane seligolide, futsi wavula umnyango lobewuholela engadzeni. Wabese uya emsebentini ahlafuna lelikhowa (bekagcine sicucu salo ephaketheni lakhe) waze waba cishe ngelinyawo lekuphakama: wase uyahamba wehla ngendlela lencane: futsi wase —watikhandza ekugcineni engadzeni lenhle, emkhatsini wetimbali letikhanyako kanye nemitfombo lepholile.
