SEHLUKO VI.
Ingulube nePepper

Kwaphela umzuzu munye noma lemibili eme abuka indlu, futsi atibuta kutsi enteni ngemuva kwaloko, ngesikhatsi kungakalindzeleki kufika umdlali wetinyawo lobekagcoke livery agijima aphuma etigodvweni—(wamtsatsa njengemuntfu lohamba ngetinyawo ngoba bekagcoke livery: ngaphandle kwaloko, ngekwehlulela ngebuso bakhe kuphela, bekatawumbita ngekutsi yimfishi)—wase uyashaya dock or his knu at. Yavulwa ngulomunye umdlali wetinyawo lobekagcoke livery, anebuso lobuyindingilizi, nemehlo lamakhulu njengesicoco; futsi bobabili labahamba ngetinyawo, Alice wabona, bebanetinwele letiyimpuphu lebetigoce tinhloko tabo tonkhe. Wativa anelukuluku lelikhulu lekwati kutsi bekumayelana nani, futsi wanyonyoba kancane waphuma etigodvweni kute alalele.

Le Fish-Footman yacala ngekukhicita kusuka ngaphansi kwemkhono wakhe incwadzi lenkhulu, lecishe ibe yinkulu njengaye, futsi loku wayidlulisela kulomunye, watsi, ngelivi lelihloniphekile, "Ku Duchess. Simemo lesivela eNdlovukati kutsi idlale i croquet." Le Frog-Footman yaphindzaphindza, ngeliphimbo lelifanako lelihloniphekile, yashintja kuphela kulandzelana kwemagama kancane, "Kuvela eNdlovukati. Simemo se Duchess kutsi idlale i croquet."

Base bakhotsama bobabili, ema curls abo abhajwa ndzawonye.

Alice wahleka kakhulu ngaloko, kwadzingeka kutsi agijime abuyele esihlahleni ngekwesaba kutsi batamva; futsi ngesikhatsi alandzela lunguta ngaphandle iFish-Footman beyihambile, futsi lomunye bekahleti phansi edvute nemnyango, abuka ngebulima etulu esibhakabhakeni.

Alice wakhuphukela emnyango ngemahloni, futsi wangcondza.

"Kute luhlobo lwelusito lwekungcondza," kwasho iFootman, "futsi loko ngetizatfu letimbili. Kwekucala, ngoba ngiseceleni kwemnyango nawe; kwesibili, ngoba benta umsindvo lomkhulu ngekhatsi, kute umuntfu lobekangakuva." Futsi nakanjani bekunemisindvo lemangalisako kakhulu lebeyenteka ngekhatsi—kukhala lokuhlala kukhona kanye nekutsimula, futsi njalo ngalesinye sikhatsi bekukhona kuphahlateka lokukhulu, kube shengatsi sitja nobe igedlela lephukile yaba tincetu.

Alice watsi: “Ngiyakucela-ke, ngitawungena kanjani?”

"Kungenteka kube nemcondvo lotsite ngekungcondvota kwakho," kwachubeka iFootman ngaphandle kwekumnakekela, "kube besinemnyango emkhatsini wetfu. Sibonelo, kube bewungekhatsi , bewungangcondvota, futsi ngingakukhipha, uyati." Bekabuka etulu esibhakabhakeni sonkhe sikhatsi akhuluma, futsi lo Alice bekacabanga ngekungacabangi. Watsi ngekhatsi kwakhe: “Kodvwa mhlawumbe angeke akhone kutibamba; "emehlo akhe asondzele kakhulu etulu enhloko yakhe. Kodvwa noma ngabe ngukuphi angaphendvula imibuto.—Ngingangena njani?" waphindzaphindza, ngekumemeta.

“Ngitawuhlala lapha,” kwaphawula iFootman, “kuze kube kusasa—”

Ngalomzuzu umnyango wendlu wavuleka, kwaphuma ipuleti lelikhulu liphuphuma, licondze enhloko yaFootman: lavele ladla imphumulo yakhe, laphuka laba tincetu ngalesinye setihlahla lebetisemuva kwakhe.

“—noma ngelilanga lelilandzelako, mhlawumbe,” kwachubeka iFootman ngelivi lelifanako, kwaba shengatsi kute lokwenteka.

“Ngitawungena kanjani?” kubuta Alice futsi, ngelivi lelikhulu.

“ Ufanele kutsi ungene nhlobo yini ?” kusho iNdvodza yetinyawo. “Ngumbuto wekucala lowo, uyati.”

Kwaba, akungabateki: Alice kuphela bekangatsandzi kutjelwa kanjalo. Watibuta watsi: “Kuyesabisa mbamba, indlela tonkhe letidalwa letiphikisana ngayo. Kwanele kuhlanyisa umuntfu!”

Le Footman yabonakala icabanga kutsi leli litfuba lelihle lekuphindza inkhulumo yakhe, ngekuhlukahluka. Watsi: “Ngitawuhlala lapha, ngiphindze ngiphume, emalanga nemalanga.”

“Kodvwa ngente njani?” kusho Alice.

“Noma yini lokutsandzako,” kwasho iFootman, futsi yacala kushaya umlotsa.

Alice watsi ngekuphelelwa litsemba: “O, kute umsebenti wekukhuluma naye: “usiphukuphuku ngalokuphelele!” Wase uvula umnyango wangena.

Lomnyango bewuholela ngco ekhishini lelikhulu, lebeligcwele intfutfu kusuka kulolunye luhlangotsi kuya kulolunye: iDuchess beyihleti esitulweni lesinemilente lemitsatfu emkhatsini, imunyisa umntfwana; umpheki bekancike emlilweni, avusa imbita lenkhulu lebeyibonakala igcwele lisobho.

“Ngempela kunepelepele lomnyenti kakhulu kulelo sobho!” Alice watsi kuye, njengobe angakhona ngenca yekutsimula.

Nakanjani bekunetintfo letinyenti kakhulu emoyeni. Ngisho neDuchess beyithimula ngetikhatsi letitsite; futsi umntfwana, bekathimula futsi akhala ngekushintjashintja ngaphandle kwekuma sikhashana. Tintfo lebetikhona ekhishini lebetingathimule, bekungumpheki, nekati lelikhulu lebelihleti esitofini libobotheka kusukela endlebeni kuya endlebeni.

“Ngicela ungitselele,” kwasho Alice, anemahloni kancane, ngobe bekangaciniseki ngalokuphelele kutsi bekuyindlela lenhle yini kutsi akhulume kucala, “kungani likhasimende lakho libobotheka kanjalo?”

"Likati laseCheshire," kwasho iDuchess, "futsi kungako. Ingulube!"

Washo ligama lekugcina ngebudlova lobungakalindzeleki kangangekutsi Alice wagxuma impela; kodvwa wabona ngalomunye umzuzu kutsi bekubhekiswe kulomntfwana, hhayi kuye, ngako waba nesibindzi, waphindze wachubeka:—

“Bengingati kutsi emakati aseCheshire ahlale abobotheka; ecinisweni, bengingati kutsi emakati ayakhona kubobotheka.”

“Bonkhe bangakhona,” kwasho iDuchess; “futsi linyenti labo liyakwenta.”

“Angati kutsi kukhona lokwentako,” kwasho Alice ngesizotsa lesikhulu, ativa ajabule kakhulu kutsi sewucoce naye.

“Awati lokunyenti,” kwasho iDuchess; “futsi loko kuliciniso.”

Alice akazange ayitsandze nhlobo ithoni yalenkhulumo, futsi wacabanga kutsi kungaba kuhle kutsi etfule lenye indzaba yekucoca. Ngesikhatsi azama kulungisa kulomunye, umpheki wasusa le cauldron yelisobho emlilweni, futsi ngaso leso sikhatsi wacala kusebenta ajikijela konkhe lokusemandleni akhe ku Duchess kanye nemntfwana—tinsimbi temlilo tafika kucala; kwabese kulandzela ishawa yemabhodlela, emapuleti, netitja. I Duchess ayizange ibanake ngisho noma bamshaya; futsi lomntfwana bekavele akhala kakhulu, kangangekutsi bekungakhonakali kutsi ngisho kutsi letibhakela betimvisa buhlungu noma cha.

“Oh, ngicela unake kutsi wentani!” kumemeta Alice, agcuma etulu naphansi ngebuhlungu bekwesaba. “O, lapho kuya khona imphumulo yakhe leligugu !” njengobe sitja lesikhulu lesingakavami sandiza edvute naso, futsi sacishe kakhulu sasitsatsa.

“Kube wonkhe umuntfu bekacabanga ngetintfo takhe,” kwasho iDuchess ngekububula lokukhulu, “umhlaba bewutawujikeleta sivumelwano ngekushesha kunaloko.”

Alice, lowativa ajabule kakhulu ngekutfola litfuba lekukhombisa lwati lwakhe loluncane, watsi: “Loku bekungeke kube yinzuzo.” “Cabanga nje kutsi bekungawenta umsebenti muni ngemini nebusuku! Uyabona umhlaba utsatsa ema-awa langemashumi lamabili nakune kutsi ujikelete nge-eksisi yawo—”

“Kukhuluma ngemazembe,” kwasho iDuchess, “mjube inhloko!”

Alice wabuka umpheki ngekukhatsateka, kute abone kutsi bekasho kutsatsa lomkhondvo yini; kodvwa umpheki bekamatasatasa ngekuvusa lisobho, futsi bekubonakala shengatsi akalaleli, ngako waphindze wachubeka watsi: “Ema-awa langemashumi lamabili nakune, ngicabanga kutsi ; nobe lishumi nakubili? Ngi—”

“O, ungangihluphi ,” kwasho iDuchess; “Angizange ngikhone kubeketelela tibalo!” Futsi ngaloko waphindze wacala kumunyisa umntfwana wakhe, amhlabelela luhlobo lwe-lullaby njengobe enta njalo, futsi amnika kunyakatisa lokunebudlova ekupheleni kwawo wonkhe umugca:

“Khuluma kabi nemfana wakho lomncane, .
Futsi umshaye uma athimula:
Ukwentela kuphela kucasula,
Ngobe uyati kutsi kuyahlekisa.”

IKHORASI.
(Lapho umpheki nemntfwana bajoyina khona):

"Mawu! mawu! mawu!"

Ngesikhatsi iDuchess ihlabela livesi lesibili lalengoma, yachubeka ajikijela lomntfwana ngemandla etulu naphansi, futsi lentfo lencane lephuyile yakhala kakhulu, kangangekutsi Alice akazange eve emagama latsi:—

“Ngikhuluma kabuhlungu kumfana wami, .
Ngiyamshaya nakatsimula;
Ngobe angajabulela ngalokuphelele .
I-pepper uma atsandza!”

IKHORASI.

"Mawu! mawu! mawu!"

"Naku! ungayimunyisa kancane, uma utsandza!" kusho iDuchess ku-Alice, imjikijela lomntfwana njengobe akhuluma. “Kufanele ngihambe ngiyolungela kudlala i-croquet neNdlovukati,” futsi yaphuma ngekushesha ekamelweni. Umpheki wamjikijela ngelipani lekuthosa ngemuva kwakhe njengobe aphuma, kodvwa lavele lamphutsa.

Alice wabamba lomntfwana ngebumatima lobutsite, njengoba bekusilwane lesincane lesimise ngendlela lemangalisako, futsi welula imikhono nemilente yaso kuto tonkhe tindlela, “njenge star-fish,” kwacabanga Alice. Lentfo lencane lephuyile beyihogela njengenjini-yemfutfo ngesikhatsi ayibamba, futsi yachubeka itiphindza kabili futsi iticondzisa futsi, kute kutsi ngalokuphelele, ngemzuzu wekucala noma lemibili, bekukukhulu kakhulu ngendlela langakhona ngayo kuyibamba.

Ngekushesha nje nakabona indlela lefanele yekuyimunyisa, (lokwakuyisonta ibe luhlobo lwefindo, bese ubamba indlebe yayo yesekudla nelunyawo lwayo lwesencele, kwentela kuvikela kutsi ingatilahliki,) wayikhipha wayikhipha emoyeni lovulekile. Alice wacabanga watsi: “Nangabe ngingahambi nalomntfwana, baciniseka kutsi batawumbulala ngelilanga linye nobe lamabili: bekungeke yini kube kubulala kumshiya?” Washo emagama ekugcina ngekumemeta, futsi lentfo lencane yabubula ngekuphendvula (beseyiyekele kutimula ngalesikhatsi). Alice watsi: “Ungagcugcuteli; “leyo akusiyo nhlobo indlela lefanele yekuveta imiva yakho.”

Lomntfwana waphindze wabubula, futsi Alice wabuka ebusweni bayo ngekukhatsateka lokukhulu kute abone kutsi yini inkinga ngayo. Bekungeke kube nekungabata kutsi beyinemphumulo lejika kakhulu , ifana kakhulu nemphumulo kunemphumulo mbamba; futsi emehlo ayo abeba mancane kakhulu kumntfwana: ngalokuphelele Alice bekangakuthandi kubukeka kwalentfo nhlobo. “Kodvwa mhlawumbe beyikhala nje,” wacabanga, futsi waphindze wayibuka emehlweni, kute abone kutsi tikhona yini tinyembeti.

Cha, bekute tinyembeti. "Nangabe utawugucuka ube yingulube, sitsandvwa sami," kwasho Alice, ngalokunako, "Angeke ngibe nalokunye lengingakwenta nawe. Ingcondvo nyalo!" Lentfo lencane lephuyile yaphindze yakhala (noma yabubula, bekungakenteki kutsi ngitsi nguyiphi), bachubeka sikhatsi lesitsite bathule.

Alice bekasandza kucala kucabanga ngekhatsi kwakhe, “Nyalo, ngitawenta njani ngalesilwane nangisifika ekhaya?” ngesikhatsi iphindze ibubula, ngebudlova, kangangekutsi wabuka phansi ebusweni bayo ngekwesaba lokutsite. Kulesikhatsi bekungeke kube neliphutsa ngako: bekungakabi ngetulu noma ngaphansi kwengulube, futsi wabona kutsi bekutawuba yintfo lengacondzakali kutsi ayitfwale achubeke.

Ngako wabeka phansi lesilwane lesincane, futsi weva akhululekile kakhulu nasibona sihamba ngekushesha sithule singena esihlahleni. Watsi kuye: “Kube beyikhulile, ngabe yente umntfwana lomubi ngendlela leyesabisako: kepha yenta kunaloko ingulube lenhle, ngicabanga kutsi.” Futsi wacala kucabanga ngalabanye bantfwana labebati, labangahle bente kahle kakhulu njengengulube, futsi bekavele atsi kuye, “uma umuntfu nje ati indlela lefanele yekubashintja—” ngesikhatsi etfuka kancane ngekubona iCheshire Cat ihleti egatjeni lesihlahla emayadi lambalwa.

Lelikati lamoyitela kuphela lapho libona Alice. Beyibukeka inesimo lesihle, wacabanga: nomakunjalo beyinetinzipho letindze kakhulu kanye nematinyo lamanyenti kakhulu, ngako wabona kutsi kufanele iphatfwe ngenhlonipho.

“Cheshire Puss,” wacala, kunaloko ngemahloni, njengoba bekangati nhlobo kutsi ingatsandza yini leligama: nomakunjalo, yavele yamomotseka kancane. “Wota, kujabulisa kuze kube ngunyalo,” kucabanga Alice, futsi wachubeka. “Ngicela ungitjele kutsi kufanele ngihambe ngayiphi indlela kusuka lapha?”

“Loko kuya kakhulu ngekutsi ufuna kufika kuphi,” kwasho Likati.

Alice watsi: “Angikhatsateki kakhulu kutsi ngikuphi—.”

“Ngako-ke akusho lutfo kutsi uhamba ngayiphi indlela,” kwasho Likati.

“—uma nje ngifika ndzawonye ,” Alice wengeta njengenchazelo.

“O, uciniseka kutsi utawukwenta loko,” kwasho Likati, “nangabe uhamba sikhatsi lesidze ngalokwenele kuphela.”

Alice wabona kutsi loku bekungeke kuphikwe, ngako wetama lomunye umbuto. “Ngubantfu labanjani labahlala lapha?”

" Kuleyo ndlela," kwasho Likati, liphephetsa sidladla salo sangesekudla, "kuhlala Umgcini: futsi kuleyo ndlela," lijikijela lesinye sidladla, "kuhlala Umvundla waMashi. Vakashela noma ngumuphi lotsandzako: bobabili bayahlanya."

Alice waphawula watsi: “Kodvwa angifuni kuya emkhatsini webantfu labahlanyako.”

"Hawu, angeke ukusite loko," kwasho Likati: "sonkhe sitfukutsele lapha. Ngiyatfukutsela. Uyatfukutsela."

“Wati njani kutsi ngitfukutsele?” kusho Alice.

“Kufanele kutsi ube njalo,” kwasho Likati, “noma kunjalo ngabe awuzange ufike lapha.”

Alice akazange acabange kutsi loko kukufakazela nhlobo; noma kunjalo, wachubeka watsi “Futsi wati njani kutsi uyatfukutsela?”

"Kucala," kwasho Likati, "inja ayihlanyi. Uyakuvuma loko?"

Alice watsi: “Ngicabanga kutsi kunjalo.”

"Kulungile-ke," kwachubeka Likati, "uyabona, inja iyabhodla uma itfukutsele, futsi inyakatisa umsila wayo uma ijabulile. Nyalo ngiyabhodla uma ngijabulile, futsi nginyakata umsila wami uma ngitfukutsele. Ngako-ke ngiyahlanya."

Alice watsi: “ Ngikubita ngekutsi kubhodla, hhayi kubhodla.

“Libite ngaloko lokutsandzako,” kwasho Likati. “Uyadlala yini i-croquet neNdlovukati namuhla?”

Alice watsi: “Kufanele ngiyitsandze kakhulu, kodvwa solo angikamenywa.”

“Utangibona lapho,” kwasho Likati, lase liyanyamalala.

Alice akazange amangale kakhulu ngaloko, bekajwayele kakhulu tintfo letimangalisako letenteka. Watsi asabuka lendzawo lebeyikhona, yaphindze yavela ngekushesha.

“By-the-bye, kwentekani kulomntfwana?” kusho leKati. “Bengicishe ngakhohlwa kubuta.”

“Yagucuka yaba yingulube,” kwasho Alice buthule, kwaba shengatsi ibuyile ngendlela yemvelo.

“Bengicabanga kutsi kutawuba njalo,” kwasho Likati, laphindze lanyamalala.

Alice walindza kancane, hhafu alindzele kutsi aphindze ayibone, kepha ayizange ivele, futsi ngemuva kwemzuzu noma lemibili wachubeka wahamba ngendlela lapho khona iMarch Hare kutsiwa ihlala khona. Watsi ngekhatsi kwakhe: “Ngike ngabona bantfu labashaya tigcoko; “iMarch Hare itawuba ngulejabulisa kakhulu, futsi mhlawumbe njengoba loku kunguMay angeke ibe nekuhlanya lokukhulu—okungenani ingahlanya kakhulu njengobe bekunjalo ngaMashi.” Njengobe asho loku, wabuka etulu, kwaphindze kwaba neKati, lihleti egatjeni lesihlahla.

“Utsi ingulube, noma umkhiwane?” kusho leKati.

Alice waphendvula watsi: “Ngitsi ingulube; “futsi ngifisa shengatsi ungachubeki uvela futsi unyamalale ngekushesha: wenta umuntfu abe nesiyeti impela.”

“Kulungile,” kwasho Likati; futsi ngalesikhatsi yanyamalala kancane kakhulu, yacala ekupheleni kwemsila, yaphela ngekumomotseka, lokwasala sikhatsi lesitsite ngemuva kwekutsi konkhe lokusele sekuhambile.

Alice wacabanga watsi: “Kulungile! Ngivame kubona ikati lelingamomotseki.” "kepha kumomotseka ngaphandle kwelikati! Yintfo lengifuna kwati kakhulu lengike ngayibona ekuphileni kwami!"

Bekangakahambi kakhulu ngaphambi kwekutsi abone indlu ye March Hare: wacabanga kutsi kumele kube yindlu lefanele, ngoba emashimini amiswe njengetindlebe futsi luphahla lwalufulelwe ngeboya. Bekuyindlu lenkhulu kakhulu, kangangekutsi bekangatsandzi kusondzela edvute kwaze kwaba ngulapho sekahlanyele lenye incenye yekhowa lesesandleni sesincele, futsi watiphakamisa waba ngemamitha lamabili ngekuphakama: ngisho nangaleso sikhatsi wahamba wakhuphukela kuyo kunaloko anemahloni, watsi kuye “Ake ucabange kutsi kufanele kube nekuhlanya lokuhlanyako ngemuva kwako konkhe see I al'!
