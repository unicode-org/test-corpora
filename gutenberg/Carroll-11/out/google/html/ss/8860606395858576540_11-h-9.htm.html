SEHLUKO IX.
Indzaba Yelufudvu Loluhlekisako

“Ngeke ucabange kutsi ngijabule kangakanani kukubona futsi, wena intfo lendzala letsandzekako!” kusho iDuchess, njengoba ifaka umkhono wayo ngelutsandvo ku-Alice, bahamba ndzawonye.

Alice wajabula kakhulu kumtfola atfukutsele kangaka, wacabanga ngekhatsi kwakhe kutsi mhlawumbe ngupelepele kuphela lowamenta waba nelunya kangaka ngesikhatsi bahlangana ekhishini.

“ NanginguDuchess ,” washo kuye, (hhayi ngeliphimbo lelinelitsemba kakhulu noma kunjalo), “Ngeke ngibe nephepha ekhishini lami nhlobo . Isobho lenta kahle kakhulu ngaphandle—Mhlawumbe kuhlala kuliphepha lokwenta bantfu batfukutsele,” wachubeka, ajabule kakhulu ngekutsi utfole luhlobo lolusha lwe rugar “ viur, makedne them . camomile lebenta babe munyu—futsi—nebhali-shukela netintfo letinjalo letenta bantfwana babe netintfukutselo letimnandzi ngifisa kuphela kutsi bantfu bati loko : ngako-ke bebangeke babe nebugovu kangaka ngako, uyati—”

Bekayikhohliwe impela iDuchess ngalesikhatsi, futsi wetfuka kancane ngesikhatsi eva livi lakhe lisondzele endlebeni yakhe. "Ucabanga ngalokutsite sitsandvwa sami, futsi loko kukwenta ukhohlwe kukhuluma. Angeke ngikutjele nyalo nje kutsi yini kutiphatsa kwaloko, kepha ngitakukhumbula kancane."

Alice waba nesibindzi watsi: “Mhlawumbe ayinayo.”

“Tut, tut, mntfwana!” kusho iDuchess. “Konkhe kunekutiphatsa, uma nje ungakutfola.” Futsi waticindzetela wasondzela eceleni kwa-Alice njengobe akhuluma.

Alice bekangatsandzi kakhulu kuhlala asondzelene naye kangaka: kwekucala, ngoba iDuchess beyimbi kakhulu ; futsi kwesibili, ngoba bekaphakeme ngco kutsi angaphumula silevu sakhe ehlombe la-Alice, futsi bekusilevu lesikhaliphile ngendlela lengakhululeki. Nanobe kunjalo, bekangatsandzi kuba nelunya, ngako wakubeketelela ngendlela langakhona ngayo.

Watsi: “Lomdlalo sewuhamba kancono nyalo,” ngendlela yekuchubeka nekucoca kancane.

“‘Kunjalo,” kwasho iDuchess: “futsi kutiphatsa kwaloko kutsi—‘O, ‘lutsandvo, ‘lutsandvo, lolwenta umhlaba ujikelete!’”

Alice wanyenyeta watsi: “Kukhona lowatsi kwentiwa ngumuntfu wonkhe lotinakekela tindzaba takhe!”

“Ah, kahle! Kusho intfo lefanako,” kwasho iDuchess, igubha silevu sayo lesincane lesikhaliphile ehlombe la-Alice njengobe yengeta, “futsi kutiphatsa kwaloko kutsi—‘Nakekela umcondvo, futsi imisindvo itawutinakekela.’”

“Uyakutsandza kakhulu kutfola kutiphatsa etintfweni!” Alice wacabanga ngekhatsi kwakhe.

"Nginesibindzi sekutsi uyatibuta kutsi kungani ngingafaki umkhono wami elukhalweni lwakho," kwasho iDuchess ngemuva kwekuma kancane: "sizatfu kutsi, ngiyakungabata ngekutfukutsela kwe flamingo yakho. Ngingazama yini lokuhlolwa?"

Alice waphendvula ngekucaphela watsi: “Kungenteka alume,” angazange akhatsateke nhlobo kutsi azanywe lokuhlolwa.

"Liciniso kakhulu," kwasho iDuchess: "emaflamingo nesinaphi kokubili kuyaluma. Futsi kutiphatsa kwaloko kutsi—'Tinyoni letinetinsiba tiyahlangana ndzawonye.'"

Alice waphawula watsi: “Sinaphi kuphela lesingesiyo inyoni.”

“Kulungile, njengenjwayelo,” kwasho iDuchess: “yindlela lecacile lonayo yekubeka tintfo!”

“ Ngicabanga kutsi yiminerali ,” kwasho Alice.

“Yebo kunjalo,” kwasho iDuchess, lebeyibonakala ikulungele kuvumelana nako konkhe lebekushiwo ngu-Alice; "kunemgodzi lomkhulu wesinaphi edvute nalapha. Futsi kutiphatsa kwaloko kutsi—'Nangabe kukhona lokunyenti kwami, kukhona lokuncane kwakho.'"

“Hawu, ngiyati!” kwababata Alice, lobekangakaze alandzele lenkhulumo yekugcina, "yibhidvo. Asibukeki njengalesinye, kodvwa sinjalo."

“Ngivumelana nawe ngalokuphelele,” kwasho iDuchess; “futsi kutiphatsa kwaloko kutsi—‘Yiba nguloko longabonakala unguko’—noma nangabe ungatsandza kubekwe ngalokulula—‘Ungalokotsi ucabange kutsi awusiyo lenye indlela kunaloko lokungabonakala kulabanye kutsi loko bewunguko nobe lebewungaba ngiko bekungasiko ngalenye indlela kunaloko lebewungiko bekungabonakala kubo ngalenye indlela.’”

Alice watsi ngesizotsa lesikhulu watsi: “Ngicabanga kutsi bekufanele ngikucondze kancono loko, kube bengikubhalile phansi: kodvwa angikhoni kukulandzela ngalokuphelele njengobe usho.”

“Akusilo lutfo loko kuloko lebengingakusho nangabe ngikhetsa,” kuphendvula iDuchess, ngelivi lelijabulisako.

Alice watsi: “Thandaza ungatihluphi ngekutsi ulisho sikhatsi lesidze kunaleso.”

“Awu, ungakhulumi ngetinkinga!” kusho iDuchess. “Ngikwentela sipho sakho konkhe lengikushito kuze kube ngunyalo.”

“Luhlobo lwesipho lesishibhile!” kucabanga Alice. “Ngiyajabula kutsi abaphani bantfu tipho letinjalo temalanga ekutalwa!” Kodvwa akazange abe nesibindzi sekukusho ngekumemeta.

“Ucabanga futsi?” kubuta iDuchess, ngekugubha lokunye kwesilevu sayo lesincane lesikhaliphile.

“Nginelilungelo lekucabanga,” kwasho Alice ngalokuhlasimulisa umtimba, ngobe abesacala kutiva akhatsatekile kancane.

“Cishe kulungile,” kwasho iDuchess, “njengobe tingulube kufanele tindize; futsi m—”

Kepha lapha, ngekumangala lokukhulu kwa-Alice, livi laDuchess lafa, ngisho nasemkhatsini weligama lakhe lalitsandzako ‘kutiphatsa,’ futsi umkhono lobewuhlanganiswe newakhe wacala kuvevetela. Alice wabuka etulu, futsi kwame iNdlovukati embikwabo, igoce imikhono, ivuvute njengesiphepho.

“Lilanga lelihle, Mhlonishwa!” iDuchess yacala ngelivi leliphansi, lelibutsakatsaka.

“Nyalo, ngikuniketa secwayiso lesifanele,” kwamemeta iNdlovukati, inyatsela phansi njengoba ikhuluma; "noma nguwe noma inhloko yakho kufanele iphume, futsi loko cishe ngesigamu sesikhatsi! Khetsa!"

I Duchess watsatsa kukhetsa kwakhe, futsi wahamba ngesikhashana.

“Asichubeke nemdlalo,” kwasho iNdlovukati ku-Alice; futsi Alice wetfuka kakhulu kutsi angasho ligama, kepha kancane kancane wamlandzela wabuyela endzaweni ye-croquet.

Letinye tivakashi betitsatse litfuba lekungabikhona kweNdlovukati, futsi betiphumula emtfuntini: nomakunjalo, ngesikhatsi bambona ngaso, baphutfuma babuyela emdlalweni, iNdlovukati yavele yaphawula kutsi kubambezeleka kwesikhashana kutawubalahlekisela timphilo tabo.

Sonkhe sikhatsi bebadlala iNdlovukati ayizange iyekele kucabana nalabanye badlali, futsi imemeta itsi “Off with his head!” nobe “Hamba nenhloko yakhe!” Labo labagwebako bebavalelwa ngemasotja, vele kwadzingeka kutsi ashiye kuba ngema arches kwenta loku, kwentela kutsi ekupheleni kwesigamu selihora noma ngabe kungabi nema arches lasele, futsi bonkhe badlali, ngaphandle kweNkhosi, iNdlovukati, na Alice, bebavalelwe futsi ngaphansi kwesigwebo sekubulawa.

INdlovukati yabese iyayekela, iphelelwe ngumoya, yatsi ku-Alice, “Sewuyibonile yini i-Mock Turtle?”

Alice watsi: “Cha.” “Angati nekutsi yini iMock Turtle.”

“Yintfo lekhiwe ngayo iMock Turtle Soup,” kwasho iNdlovukati.

Alice watsi: “Angikaze ngibone ngisho nayinye, nobe ngive ngayo.”

“Wota-ke,” kwasho iNdlovukati, “utakucocela umlandvo wakhe.”

Njengobe bebahamba ndzawonye, Alice weva iNkhosi itsi ngelivi leliphansi, enkampanini jikelele, “Nonkhe nicolelwe.” “Wota, yintfo lenhle leyo !” washo ngekhatsi kwakhe, ngobe bekativa angajabuli ngalokuphelele ngenani lekubulawa kwebantfu lebeyiyalelwe yiNdlovukati.

Ngekushesha kakhulu bahlangana neGryphon, ilele elangeni. (Uma ungati kutsi yini iGryphon, buka lesitfombe.) “Phuphuma, intfo levilaphako!” kwasho iNdlovukati, "utsatse lentfombatana iyobona iMock Turtle, nekuva umlandvo wayo. Kufanele ngibuyele emuva ngiyobona ngemuva kwekubulawa lokutsite lengikuyalile;" futsi wahamba, washiya Alice yedvwa neGryphon. Alice akazange akutsandze kakhulu kubukeka kwalesilwane, kepha ngalokuphelele bekacabanga kutsi bekutawuphepha kakhulu kuhlala naso njengekulandzela leyo Ndlovukati lenelunya: ngako walindza.

Le Gryphon yahlala phansi yasukuma yahlikihla emehlo ayo: yase ibuka iNdlovukati yaze yaphelelwa emehlweni: yase iyahleka. “Kumnandzi kakhulu!” kusho iGryphon, ihhafu kuyo, ihhafu ku-Alice.

“ Yini lejabulisako?” kusho Alice.

“Kungani, yena ,” kwasho iGryphon. "Konkhe kutsandza kwakhe, kutsi: ababulali muntfu, uyati. Wota!"

“Wonkhe umuntfu utsi ‘wota!’ lapha,” kwacabanga Alice, njengobe ahamba kancane ngemuva kwayo: “Angikaze ngiyalwe kangaka kuyo yonkhe imphilo yami, angikaze!”

Bebangakahambi khashane ngaphambi kwekutsi babone iMock Turtle khashane, ihleti idvumatekile futsi inesitunge eceleni kwelidvwala lelincane, futsi, njengoba bebasondzela edvute, Alice bekamva abubula kube shengatsi inhlitiyo yakhe itawuphuka. Wamhawukela kakhulu. “Luyini lusizi lwakhe?” wabuta iGryphon, futsi iGryphon yaphendvula, icishe kakhulu ngemavi lafanako nalawo langaphambilini, "Konkhe kutsandza kwakhe, kutsi: akanalo lusizi, uyati. Wota!"

Ngako bakhuphukela kuMock Turtle, lowababuka ngemehlo lamakhulu lagcwele tinyembeti, kepha akazange asho lutfo.

“Lentfombi lencane lapha,” kwasho iGryphon, “ifuna kwati umlandvo wakho, iyati.”

“Ngitamtjela,” kwasho Lufudvu Lwekuhlekisa ngelivi lelijulile, lelinemgodzi: “hlalani phansi, nobabili, ningakhulumi ligama ngize ngicedze.”

Ngako bahlala phansi, futsi kute lowakhuluma imizuzu letsite. Alice wacabanga ngekhatsi kwakhe, “Angiboni kutsi angaze acedze njani, nangabe angacali.” Kodvwa walindza ngesineke.

“Ngalesinye sikhatsi,” kwasho Lufudvu Lwekuhlekisa ekugcineni, ngekububula lokujulile, “BengiyiLufudvu mbamba.”

Lamavi alandzelwa kuthula lokudze kakhulu, kwephulwa kuphela ngekumemeta ngaletinye tikhatsi kutsi “Hjckrrh!” kusuka ku Gryphon, kanye nekukhala lokukhulu njalo kwe Mock Turtle. Alice bekacishe asuke kakhulu atsi, “Ngiyabonga mnumzane, ngendzaba yakho lejabulisako,” kodvwa akazange akhone kutibamba acabanga kutsi kufanele kube nalokunye lokutawufika, ngako wahlala phansi wangasho lutfo.

"Ngesikhatsi sisebancane," Lufudvu Lohlekisako lwachubeka ekugcineni, ngekuthula kakhulu, noma nje belusolo lukhala kancane ngalesinye sikhatsi, "saya esikolweni elwandle. Lenkhosi bekunguLufudvu loludzala—besivame kumbita ngekutsi Lufudvu—"

“Umbite ngani ngekutsi nguLufudvu, nangabe bekangasuye?” Kubuta Alice.

“Sambita ngekutsi nguLufudvu ngobe nguye losifundzisako,” kwasho Lufudvu Loluhlekisako ngekutfukutsela: “empeleni ubuthuntu kakhulu!”

“Kufanele ube nemahloni ngekubuta umbuto lolula kangaka,” kwengeta iGryphon; base bahlala phansi bobabili bathula babuka Alice lophuyile, lowativa alungele kucwila emhlabeni. Ekugcineni iGryphon yatsi kuMock Turtle, "Chubeka ngekushayela, mkhulu! Ungabi lilanga lonkhe ngaloko!" wachubeka ngalamavi:

“Yebo, saya esikolweni elwandle, noma ungakholwa—”

“Angikaze ngitsi angikaze!” kuphazamisa Alice.

“Ukwentile,” kwasho Lufudu Lwekuhlekisa.

“Bamba lulwimi lwakho!” kwangeta iGryphon, ngaphambi kwekutsi Alice aphindze akhulume. Lolufudvu loluhlekisako lwachubeka.

“Besinemfundvo lencono kakhulu—ecinisweni, besiya esikolweni onkhe malanga—”

Alice watsi: “ Nami ngike ngaya esikolweni semini; “akudzingeki kutsi utigcabhe njengako konkhe loko.”

“Ngetintfo letengetiwe?” kubuta Lufudu Lwekuhlekisa ngekukhatsateka kancane.

Alice watsi: “Yebo, safundza siFulentji kanye nemculo.”

“Futsi nekugeza?” kusho Lufudvu Loluhlekisako.

“Ngempela cha!” kusho Alice ngekutfukutsela.

“Ah! ngako-ke sakho besingasiso sikolo lesihle mbamba,” kwasho Lufudvu Lwekuhlekisa ngelivi lekukhululeka lokukhulu. “Nyalo kitsi bebanekupheleni kwesikweleti, ‘SiFulentshi, umculo, nekugeza —lokwengetiwe.’”

Alice watsi: “Bewungeke ukufune kakhulu; “labahlala phansi elwandle.”

“Bengingakhoni kulifundza.” kusho Lufudu Lwekuhlekisa ngekububula. “Ngatsatsa kuphela sifundvo lesivamile.”

“Bekuyini loko?” kubuta Alice.

“Kunyakata Nekunyakata, vele, kucala,” kwaphendvula Lufudvu Lwekuhlekisa; “bese kuba nemagala lehlukene e-Arithmetic—Kufisa, Kuphazamiseka, Kuhlambalata, Nekuhlekisa.”

Alice watsi ngesibindzi watsi: “Angikaze ngive ngekutsi ‘Uglification’.” “Yini leyo?”

I Gryphon yaphakamisa totimbili tinyawo tayo ngekumangala. "Yini! Angikaze ngive ngekuhlambalata!" kwababata. “Uyati kutsi kuyini kuhlobisa, ngicabanga kutsi?”

Alice watsi ngekungabata: “Yebo, kusho—kwenta—noma yini—ibe yinhle kakhulu.”

“Kulungile-ke,” kwachubeka iGryphon, “uma ungati kutsi kusho kutsini ku-uglify, uyi -simpleton.”

Alice akazange ative akhutsatwa kutsi abute leminye imibuto ngaloko, ngako wagucukela kuMock Turtle, watsi “Yini lenye lebekufanele uyifundze?”

“Kulungile, bekuneMfihlakalo,” kwaphendvula iMock Turtle, ibala tifundvo letisemaflaps akhe, “—Imfihlakalo, yasendvulo neyesimanje, ne Seaography: bese kuba yi Drawling—i Drawling-master beyiyi conger-eel lendzala, lebeyivame kufika kanye ngeliviki: bekasifundzisa ku Drawling kanye ne Kudvweba, Stretch.”

“Bekunjani loko ?” kusho Alice.

"Kulungile, angeke ngikukhombise mine," kwasho Lufudvu Lwekuhlekisa: "Ngicinile kakhulu. Futsi iGryphon ayizange ikufundze."

"Besingenaso sikhatsi," kwasho iGryphon: "Ngaya enkhosini yeClassics, noma kunjalo. Bekanukalankala lomdzala, bekanjalo ."

“Angikaze ngiye kuye,” kwasho iMock Turtle ngekububula: “bekafundzisa Kuhleka neLusizi, bebavame kusho njalo.”

“Wenta njalo, wenta njalo,” kwasho iGryphon, ibubula ngesikhatsi sayo; futsi totimbili letidalwa tafihla buso bato etinyaweni tato.

“Futsi bewenta tifundvo ema-awa lamangaki ngelilanga?” kusho Alice, aphutfuma kushintja sihloko.

“Ema-awa lalishumi ngelilanga lekucala,” kwasho Lufudu Lwekuhlekisa: “ema-awa lasiphohlongo ngelilanga lelilandzelako, njalonjalo.”

“Licebo lekufuna kwati!” Kubabata Alice.

“Nguso sizatfu lesenta kutsi tibitwe ngekutsi tifundvo,” kwaphawula iGryphon: “ngobe tiyancipha malanga onkhe.”

Lona bekungumcondvo lomusha kakhulu ku-Alice, futsi wacabanga ngawo kancane ngaphambi kwekutsi asho inkhulumo yakhe lelandzelako. “Ngako-ke lilanga lelishumi nakunye kufanele kutsi bekuliholide?”

“Yebo bekunjalo,” kwasho Lufudu Lwekuhlekisa.

“Futsi ukhone njani ngelishumi nakubili?” Alice wachubeka ngekulangatela.

“Kwanele loko ngetifundvo,” kwaphazamisa iGryphon ngelivi lelincumile kakhulu: “mtjele lokutsite ngemidlalo nyalo.”
