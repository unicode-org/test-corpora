SEHLUKO I.
Ehla eMgodzini waNogwaja

Alice abecala kukhatsala kakhulu ngekuhlala eceleni kwadzadzewabo ebhange, nekungabi nalutfo lakwentako: kanye noma kabili abelunguta encwadzini dzadzewabo labeyifundza, kepha beyite titfombe noma tingcoco kuyo, “futsi incwadzi isebenta ngani,” kwacabanga Alice “ngaphandle kwetitfombe noma tingcoco?”

Ngako bekacabanga engcondvweni yakhe (njengobe angakhona, ngoba lilanga lelishisako belimenta ative alele kakhulu futsi asiphukuphuku), kutsi ngabe injabulo yekwenta i-daisy-chain beyitawufaneleka yini kutsi ahlupheke ngekusukuma acoshe ema-daisy, ngesikhatsi ngekushesha Nogwaja Lomhlophe lonemehlo lapinki agijima edvute kwakhe.

Bekute intfo lemangalisako kakhulu kuloko; futsi Alice akazange acabange kutsi bekuphuma endleleni kakhulu kuva Nogwaja atsi kuye, "Oh mtsandzeki! Oh mtsandzeki! Ngitawuphuta!" (watsi nakacabanga ngemuva kwaloko, kwafika engcondvweni yakhe kutsi bekufanele atibute ngaloko, kepha ngaleso sikhatsi konkhe bekubonakala kuyimvelo impela); kepha ngesikhatsi Nogwaja empeleni akhipha liwashi ephaketheni lakhe le-waistcoat , futsi walibuka, wase uyaphutfuma, Alice wacala kusukuma ngetinyawo takhe, ngobe kwakhanya engcondvweni yakhe kutsi akazange abone ngaphambilini nogwaja lonephaketheni le-waistcoat, noma iwashi latawulikhipha kulo, futsi wagijima ngekutsi . ngenhlanhla bekusikhatsi nje sekuyibona iphuphuma phansi imbobo lenkhulu yanogwaja ngaphansi kwelucingo.

Kulomunye umzuzu phansi wahamba Alice ngemuva kwayo, akazange acabange kanye kutsi emhlabeni bekatawuphindze aphume njani.

Lomgodzi-wanogwaja wahamba wacondza njengemhubhe ngendlela letsite, wase uyacwila ngekushesha phansi, ngekushesha kangangekutsi Alice akazange abe nemzuzu wekucabanga ngekutimisa ngaphambi atikhandze awela phansi emtfonjeni lojule kakhulu.

Nobe lomtfombo bewujule kakhulu, nobe wawela kancane kakhulu, ngobe bekanesikhatsi lesinyenti njengobe ehla kute abuke ngaye futsi atibute kutsi kutawentekani ngemuva kwaloko. Kwekucala, wetama kubuka phansi abone kutsi ufika kuphi, kodvwa bekumnyama kakhulu kutsi angabona lutfo; wabese ubuka emaceleni emtfombo, wabona kutsi agcwele emakhabethe nemashelufu etincwadzi; lapha nalaphaya wabona emabalave netitfombe letilengiswe etikhonkwaneni. Wehlisa imbita kulelinye lemashelufu njengobe adlula; bekubhalwe kutsi “ORANGE MARMALADE”, kepha ngekudvumateka kwakhe lokukhulu bekute lutfo: bekangatsandzi kuwisa lembita ngekwesaba kubulala umuntfu ngaphansi kwayo, ngako wakhona kuyifaka kulelinye lemakhabethe njengoba awela edlula kuyo.

"Kahle!" kucabanga Alice ngekhatsi kwakhe, "ngemuva kwekuwa lokunjengaloku, angeke ngicabange lutfo ngekuwa phansi ngetitebhisi! Bonkhe batawucabanga kutsi nginesibindzi kangakanani ekhaya! Phela, bengingasho lutfo ngaloko, ngisho nobe ngiwa etulu endlini!" (Lokucishe kube liciniso kakhulu.)

Phansi, phansi, phansi. Lokuwa bekungeke yini kuphele? “Ngiyatibuta kutsi sengiwile emamayela lamangakhi ngalesikhatsi?” washo ngekumemeta. “Kufanele kutsi ngifike endzaweni letsite lesedvute nesikhungo semhlaba. Ake ngibone: loko bekutawuba ngemakhilomitha latinkhulungwane letine phansi, ngicabanga kutsi—” (ngobe, uyabona, Alice bekafundze tintfo letinyenti taloluhlobo etifundvweni takhe egumbini lesikolwa, futsi noma leli bekungasilo litfuba lelihle kakhulu lekukhombisa lwati lwakhe, njengobe bekute umuntfu lomkhuba wakhe kutsi “ ngetulu kwalo, ku—) loko kumayelana nelibanga lelifanele—kodvwa-ke ngiyatibuta kutsi ngifike kuyiphi iLatitude nobe iLongitude?” (Alice bekangati kutsi yini iLatitude, noma iLongitude noma, kepha bekacabanga kutsi ngemagama lamahle lamakhulu langawasho.)

Kwanyalo waphindze wacala. “Ngiyatibuta kutsi ngitawuwa yini ngco emhlabeni! Kutawubonakala kuhlekisa kangakanani kuphuma emkhatsini webantfu labahamba ngetinhloko tabo phansi! Ema-Antipathies, ngicabanga kutsi—” (wajabula kunaloko kutsi kute umuntfu lobekalalele, ngalesikhatsi, njengobe bekungavakali nhlobo ligama lelifanele) “to—of them the Please is. Make, yiNew Zealand nobe i-Australia lena?” (futsi wetama kugoba njengobe akhuluma— kugoba lokumangalisako njengobe uwela emoyeni! Ucabanga kutsi ungakhona yini kukuphatsa?) “Futsi ngiyintfombatana lencane lengati lutfo latawucabanga mine ngekubuta! Cha, angeke kwenteke kubuta: mhlawumbe ngitawubona kubhaliwe ndzawonye.”

Phansi, phansi, phansi. Bekute lokunye labengakwenta, ngako ngekushesha Alice waphindze wacala kukhuluma. “Dinah utangikhumbulela kakhulu lomuhla ebusuku, kufanele ngicabange!” (Dinah bekulikati.) "Ngiyetsemba kutsi batawukhumbula i-saucer yakhe yelubisi ngesikhatsi setiye. Dinah sitsandvwa sami! Ngifisa shengatsi bewukhona lapha phansi nami! Kute emagundvwane emoyeni, ngiyesaba, kepha ungahle ubambe lilulwane, futsi loko kufana kakhulu neligundvwane, uyati. Kepha emakati ayadla yini emalulwane, ngiyatibuta?" Futsi lapha Alice wacala kulala kancane, futsi wachubeka watsi kuye, ngendlela lefana neliphupho, “Ngabe emakati ayadla emalulwane? Ngabe emakati ayadla emalulwane?” futsi ngaletinye tikhatsi, “Emalulwane ayawadla yini emakati?” ngoba, uyabona, njengobe angakhoni kuphendvula noma ngumuphi umbuto, bekungasho lutfo kakhulu kutsi bekawubeka ngayiphi indlela. Wativa kutsi uyalala, futsi bekasandza kucala kuphupha kutsi uhamba aphetse sandla naDina, futsi watsi kuye ngekutimisela lokukhulu, “Nyalo, Dina, ngitjele liciniso: wake wadla yini lilulwane?” lapho ngekushesha, thump! kushaya! phansi wafika encumbini yetinti nemacembe lawomile, futsi kuwa kwaphela.

Alice akazange alimale kancane, futsi wagcuma wasukuma ngetinyawo takhe ngesikhashana: wabuka etulu, kepha konkhe bekumnyama etulu; embikwakhe bekunenye indlela lendze, futsi Nogwaja Lomhlophe bekasabonakala, aphutfuma ehla kuyo. Bekute umzuzu wekulahleka: wahamba Alice njengemoya, futsi bekusikhatsi nje sekuyiva itsi, njengoba ijika ekhoneni, “Oh tindlebe tami netinwele tami, sekuphutile kangakanani!” Bekasondzele ngemuva kwayo ngesikhatsi ajika ekhoneni, kepha Nogwaja abengasabonakali: watikhandza ehholo lelidze, leliphansi, lebelikhanyisiwe ngumugca wetibane lebetilenga eluphahleni.

Bekuneminyango yonkhe letungelete lelihholo, kodvwa yonkhe beyikhiyiwe; futsi ngesikhatsi Alice sekaphelile yonkhe indlela yehla ngakulolunye luhlangotsi futsi etulu ngakulolunye, azama yonkhe iminyango, wahamba ngekudzabuka wehla emkhatsini, atibuta kutsi utawuphindze aphume njani.

Ngekushesha wafika etafuleni lelincane lelinemilente lemitsatfu, lonkhe lentiwe ngengilazi lecinile; bekute lutfo kuyo ngaphandle kwesikhiya lesincane seligolide, futsi umcabango wa-Alice wekucala bekutsi kungenteka kutsi bekungulomunye weminyango yalelihholo; kodvwa, maye! noma tikhiya betitinkhulu kakhulu, noma sikhiya besincane kakhulu, kepha noma ngabe ngusiphi silinganiso besingeke sivule ngisho nayinye yato. Kepha, ngesikhatsi sesibili, wahlangana nelikhethini leliphansi labekangakaliboni ngaphambili, futsi ngemuva kwalo bekunemnyango lomncane lobewucishe ube ngemasentimitha lalishumi nesihlanu ngekuphakama: wazama sikhiya lesincane seligolide kulokhi, futsi ngekujabula kwakhe lokukhulu salingana!

Alice wavula umnyango watfola kutsi uholela emhubheni lomncane, hhayi lomkhulu kakhulu kunembobo yemagundvwane: waguca ngemadvolo wabuka ngasemhubheni wangena engadzeni letsandzekako kakhulu lowake wayibona. Indlela labelangatelela ngayo kuphuma kulelo hholo lelimnyama, futsi azulazule emkhatsini waleyo mibhedze yetimbali letikhanyako naleyo mitfombo lepholile, kepha akazange akhone ngisho nekungenisa inhloko yakhe emnyango; "futsi noma ngabe inhloko yami ingadlula," kwacabanga Alice lophuyile, "bekungaba lusito loluncane kakhulu ngaphandle kwemahlombe ami. O, ngifisa shengatsi ngingathula njengesibonakudze! Ngicabanga kutsi ngingakhona, kube bengati nje kutsi ngicale njani." Ngobe, uyabona, tintfo letinyenti letiphumile endleleni betenteka muva nje, kangangekutsi Alice abecale kucabanga kutsi tintfo letimbalwa kakhulu mbamba betingeke tenteke mbamba.

Kwabonakala kungasebenti kulindza eceleni kwemnyango lomncane, ngako wabuyela etafuleni, hhafu anelitsemba lekutsi angahle atfole lesinye sikhiya kulo, noma ngabe ngusiphi silinganiso incwadzi yemitsetfo yekuvala bantfu njengetibonakudze: ngalesikhatsi watfola libhodlela lelincane kulo, (“lelingakabaluleki ngalokucinisekile lapha ngaphambili,” kwasho Alice,) futsi round the laper the bo . “NGINATSE,” bekubhalwe kahle kuyo ngetinhlavu letinkhulu.

Konkhe bekukuhle kakhulu kutsi atsi “Nginatse,” kodvwa Alice lomncane lohlakaniphile bekangakwenti loko ngekushesha. Watsi: “Cha, ngitawubuka kucala, ngibone kutsi kubhalwe kutsi ‘ bushevu ’ yini nobe cha”; ngoba bekafundze imilandvo lemincane lemihle leminyenti mayelana nebantfwana labashiswe, futsi badliwa tilwane tasendle kanye naletinye tintfo letingajabulisi, konkhe ngoba bebangeke bayikhumbula imitsetfo lelula bangani babo lebebabafundzise yona: njenge, kutsi i-poker leshisako lebovu itakushisa uma uyibamba sikhatsi lesidze kakhulu; nekutsi uma usika umunwe wakho kujule kakhulu ngemmese, uvame kuphuma ingati; futsi bekangakaze akhohlwe kutsi, nangabe unatsa kakhulu ebhodleleni lelibhalwe kutsi “buthi,” kucishe kuciniseke kutsi angeke avumelane nawe, ngekushesha nobe ngemuva kwesikhatsi.

Nomakunjalo, lelibhodlela belingakabhalwa kutsi “buthi,” ngako Alice watimisela kulinambitsa, futsi watfola kutsi limnandzi kakhulu, (belineluhlobo lwekunambitseka lokuhlanganisiwe kwe cherry-tart, custard, pine-apula, i-turkey leyosiwe, i-toffee, kanye ne-toast leshisako lenebhotela,) washeshe walicedza.

“Yeka umuva wekufuna kwati!” kusho Alice; “Kufanele kutsi ngithule njengesibonakudze.”

Futsi kwaba njalo mbamba: nyalo besekaphakeme ngemasentimitha lalishumi kuphela, futsi buso bakhe bakhanya ngekucabanga kutsi nyalo sewulingana nesayizi lefanele yekungena ngemnyango lomncane angene kuleyo ngadze letsandzekako. Kwekucala, nomakunjalo, walindza imizuzu lembalwa kutsi abone kutsi utawuncipha yini: weva etfukile kancane ngaloko; "ngobe kungenteka kuphele, uyati," kwasho Alice ngekhatsi kwakhe, "ekuphumeni kwami ​​ngalokuphelele, njengelikhandlela. Ngiyatibuta kutsi kufanele ngibe njani ngaleso sikhatsi?" Futsi wetama kucabanga kutsi lilangabi lelikhandlela linjani ngemuva kwekutsi lelikhandlela selicishiwe, ngobe bekangakhumbuli kutsi wake wayibona intfo lenjalo.

Ngemuva kwesikhatsi, atfola kutsi kute lokunye lokwenteka, wancuma kungena engadzeni ngaso leso sikhatsi; kodvwa, maye ku-Alice lophuyile! watsi nakefika emnyango, watfola kutsi ukhohliwe sikhiya lesincane seligolide, futsi watsi nakebuyela etafuleni ngaso, watfola kutsi angeke akwati kusifinyelela: bekasibona kahle kakhulu ngengilazi, futsi wetama ngako konkhe lokusemandleni akhe kutsi akhuphuke ngalenye yemilente yelitafula, kepha bekushelela kakhulu; futsi watsi nasekakhatsateke ngekuzama, lentfo lencane lephuyile yahlala phansi yakhala.

“Wota, kute umsebenti wekukhala kanjalo!” kusho Alice ngekhatsi kwakhe, kunaloko ngalokuhlasimulisa umtimba; “Ngikweluleka kutsi ushiye ngalomzuzu!” Wavame kutinika seluleko lesihle kakhulu, (noma nje bekangakavami kakhulu kusilandzela), futsi ngalesinye sikhatsi bekatikhuta kakhulu kangangekutsi aletse tinyembeti emehlweni akhe; futsi wake wakhumbula kwetama kubhokisa tindlebe takhe ngekutikhohlisa emdlalweni we croquet labewudlala ngaye, ngoba lomntfwana lofuna kwati bekatsandza kakhulu kwenta shengatsi ungubantfu lababili. Alice lophuyile wacabanga watsi: “Kodvwa nyalo akusiti ngalutfo kwenta shengatsi ngibantfu lababili !

Ngekushesha liso lakhe lawela ebhokisini lelincane lengilazi lebelilele ngaphansi kwelitafula: walivula, watfola kulo likhekhe lelincane kakhulu, lapho khona emagama latsi “NGIDLE” abebhalwe kahle ngema currants. "Kulungile, ngitayidla," kwasho Alice, "futsi uma ingenta ngikhule ngibe mkhulu, ngingafinyelela kulesikhiya; futsi uma ingenta ngibe mncane, nginganyonyoba ngaphansi kwemnyango; ngako-ke noma ngabe nguyiphi indlela ngitawungena engadzeni, futsi angikhatsateki kutsi kwentekani!"

Wadla kancane, futsi watsi ngekukhatsateka kuye, “Nguyiphi indlela? Nguyiphi indlela?”, abambe sandla sakhe etulu enhloko yakhe kute ative kutsi ikhula ngayiphi indlela, futsi wamangala kakhulu kutfola kutsi wahlala alingana: kuciniseka, loku kuvame kwenteka nangabe umuntfu adla likhekhe nothing into way- way got so out kwenteka, kutsi bekubonakala kubutsakatsaka futsi kuliphukuphuku kutsi imphilo ichubeke ngendlela levamile.

Ngako wacala kusebenta, futsi ngekushesha walicedza lelikhekhe.
