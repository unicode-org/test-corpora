I-Lobster Quadrille

Le Mock Turtle yabubula kakhulu, futsi yadvonsa ngemuva kwe flapper yinye ngaphesheya kwemehlo ayo. Wabuka Alice, wetama kukhuluma, kepha umzuzu munye noma lemibili kukhala kwacindzetela livi lakhe. “Kufana nekutsi unelitsambo emphinjeni wakhe,” kwasho iGryphon: futsi yacala kusebenta imnyakatisa futsi imshaya ngesibhakela ngemuva. Ekugcineni iMock Turtle yabuyisa livi layo, futsi, tinyembeti tigeleta etihlathini tayo, yaphindze yachubeka:—

“Kungenteka kutsi awuzange uhlale kakhulu ngaphansi kwelwandle—” (“Angikaze,” kwasho Alice)—“futsi mhlawumbe awuzange uze wetfulwe ngisho ne-lobster—” (Alice wacala kutsi “Ngake nganambitsa—” kodvwa watihlola ngekushesha, futsi watsi “Cha, awukaze”) “—ngako-ke angeke ube nemcondvo wekutsi Quadri a yintfo lejabulisako!”

Alice watsi: “Cha, mbamba. “Nguyiphi inhlobo yemdanso?”

“Bese, uyati,” kwachubeka Lufudvu Lwekuhlekisa, “ulahla le—”

“Shintsha ema- lobster futsi!” kumemeta iGryphon ngelivi layo lelikhulu.

“Kubuyela emhlabatsini futsi, futsi nguloko konkhe sibalo sekucala,” kwasho Lufudvu Lwekuhlekisa, ngekushesha lwehlisa livi lakhe; futsi letidalwa letimbili, lebetigcuma njengetintfo letihlanyako sonkhe lesikhatsi, taphindze tahlala phansi tidvumatekile kakhulu futsi tithule, tabuka Alice.

Alice watsi ngemahloni: “Kufanele kube ngumdanso lomuhle kakhulu.”

“Ngiyabonga, ngumdanso lojabulisa kakhulu kuwubukela,” kwasho Alice, ativa ajabule kakhulu kutsi ekugcineni sewuphelile: “futsi ngenta njalo ngitsandza leyo ngoma yekulangatelela kwati lemayelana ne whiting!”

“Angati kutsi kungenteka kutsi Dinn ukuphi,” kwasho Lufudu Lwekuhlekisa, “kodvwa nangabe uke watibona kaningi kangaka, vele uyati kutsi tinjani.”

“Kungani, ticatfulo takho tentiwe ngani?” kusho iGriphoni. “Ngisho kutsi yini lebenta bacwebetele kangaka?”

Alice wababuka phansi, futsi wacabanga kancane ngaphambi kwekutsi anikete imphendvulo yakhe. “Bacedzile ngekumnyama, ngiyakholwa.”

"Emabhudzi neticatfulo letingaphansi kwelwandle," kwachubeka iGryphon ngelivi lelijulile, "kwentiwe ngekuhlanyela. Nyalo sewuyati."

“Futsi takhiwe ngani?” Kubuta Alice ngelivi lelikhombisa kutsi ufuna kwati kakhulu.

“Soles kanye ne eels, vele,” iGryphon yaphendvula kunaloko ngekungabeketeli: “noma nguyiphi shrimp beyingakutjela loko.”

Alice, lobekasolo acabanga ngalengoma, watsi: “Kube bengiyi- whiting, ngabe ngitjele le- porpoise kutsi, ‘Ngicela: asifuni kutsi ube natsi!’”

“Bebabophekile kutsi babe naye,” kwasho i- Mock Turtle: “kute imfishi lehlakaniphile lebeyingahamba noma ngukuphi ngaphandle kwe- porpoise.”

“Ngeke yini kube njalo mbamba?” kusho Alice ngelivi lekumangala kakhulu.

“Yebo cha,” kwasho Lufudvu Loluhlekisako: “kungani, nangabe imfishi ita kimi , futsi ingitjele kutsi iya luhambo, kufanele ngitsi ‘Ngayiphi i- porpoise?’”

“Awusho kutsi ‘injongo’ yini?” kusho Alice.

“Ngisho loko lengikushoko,” kwaphendvula Lufudu Lwekuhlekisa ngelivi lelitfukutsele. Futsi iGryphon yengeta “Wota, asive letinye tetigameko takho .”

“Ngingakutjela tintfo lengihlangabetene nato—kusukela lamuhla ekuseni,” kwasho Alice ngemahloni kancane: “kodvwa akusiti ngalutfo kubuyela emuva itolo, ngobe bengingumuntfu lohlukile ngaleso sikhatsi.”
