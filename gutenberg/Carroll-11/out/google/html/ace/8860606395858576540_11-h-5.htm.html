BAB V.
Nasehat dari sidroe Ulat .

Ulat ngon Alice saleng meungieng-ngieng padum-padum watee lam seungap: akhe jih Ulat nyan jiteubiet hookah nibak babah jih, dan jikheun keu jih ngon su nyang leumoh dan mengantuk.

“Soe droeneuh? ” kheun Ulat.

Nyoe kon saboh pembukaan nyang jeuet keu seumangat keu saboh percakapan. Alice geujaweueb, agak malei, “Ulôn—lôn hana lôn teupeuë, Pak, baroëkon bak watèë njoë—paleng kureuëng lôn teupeuë soë ulôn watèë ulôn beudôh beungoh njoë, tapi ulôn piké ulôn pasti ka meu-ubah padum-padum go sejak watèë njan.”

“Peue maksud droeneuh ngon nyan?” kheun Ulat nyan teugaih. “Peugah keudroe!”

“Lon hana jeuet lon peutrang droe , lon teumakot, Pak,” kheun Alice, “sabab lon kon droe, neu kalon.”

“Hana lôn kalon,” kheun Ulat.

"Lon teumakot hana jeuet lon peugah leubeh jeulaih," jaweueb Alice deungon that sopan, "kareuna lon hana jeuet lon muphom keudroe keu phon; dan jeuet keu le that ukuran nyang meulaen lam siuroe that meulinteung."

“Kon meunan,” kheun Ulat.

“Nyoe, mungken droeneuh gohlom neuteumeung meunan lom,” kheun Alice; “tapi watee droeneuh haroh meuubah jeut keu chrysalis-droeneuh akan bak saboh uroe, droeneuh teupeu-dan lheuh nyan jeut keu kupu-kupu, lon harus pike droeneuh akan neurasakan nyan agak aneh, kon?”

“Hana meubacut pih,” kheun Ulat.

“Nyoe, mungken peurasaan droeneuh mungken beda,” kheun Alice; “mandum nyang lon teupeu, akan meurasa that aneh keu lon .”

"Gata!" kheun Ulat ngon hina. "So gata? "

Nyang peuwoe lom awak nyan bak awai peugah haba. Alice meurasa bacut beungeh bak Caterpillar geupeugah haba nyang that paneuk lagee nyan, dan gobnyan geugamba droe geuh dan geupeugah, that brat, “Ulon pike, droeneuh patot neupeugah bak lon soe droeneuh , phon.”

"Pakeun?" kheun Ulat.

Disinoë na saboh teuk peukara njang peugot bingong; dan kareuna Alice hana hase geupike alasan nyang jroh, dan kareuna Ulat nyan deuh jih lam keuadaan pikeran nyang that hana mangat, gobnyan geujak meuputa-puta.

“Jak woe kheueh!” geukheun le Ulat nyan. “Na peukara peunteng lon peugah!”

Nyoe bunyi jih menjanjikan, pasti: Alice meuputa dan teuka lom.

“Jaga beungeh droe,” kheun Ulat.

“Nyan mantong?” kheun Alice, jitelan beungeh jih seubagoe nyang jeuet.

“Hana,” kheun Ulat.

Alice geupike jih mungken cit geupreh, sabab jih hana sapeue nyang laen nyang jeuet jipeubuet, dan mungken lheuh nyan mungken jipeugah sapeue-sapeue nyang layak jideungo. Seulama padum-padum minet jih jipupoe-pupoe hana jipeugah haba, teuma akhé jih jipeuhah jaroe jih, jipeuteubiet lom hookah nibak babah jih, dan jipeugah, “Jadi gata pike gata ka meu-ubah, kon meunan?”

“Lôn teumakôt lôn, Pak,” kheun Alice; “Lôn hana lôn ingat hai-hai lagée nyang ka lôn ingat—dan lôn hana lôn jaga ukuran nyang saban seulama siploh minet meusajan!”

“Hana teuingat peu- peu mantong?” kheun Ulat.

“Nyoe, lon ka lon cuba peugah “Pakriban cara lebah sibuk ubit nyan,” tapi mandum nyan teuka beda!” Alice geujaweub ngon su nyang that seudeh.

“Ulang, ‘ Kamoe ka tuha, Ayah William ,’” kheun Ulat.

Alice geulipat jaroe, dan geumulai:—

“Kamoe ka tuha, Ayah William,” kheun aneuk muda nyan, .
“Dan ôk gata ka putéh that;
Dan bahpih meunan gata hana putôh-putôh teudong ateueh ulée gata—
Peuë keuh gata pike, bak umu gata, nyan beutôi?”

“Bak masa muda lon,” jaweueb Ayah William keu aneuk gobnyan, .
“Lon teumakot mungken jeut keu luka utak;
Tapi, jinoe ka lon yakin that hana lon na,
Pakon, lon peugot meu ulang-ulang.”

“Kamoe ka tuha,” kheun aneuk muda nyan, “lagee nyang ka lon peugah dilee, .
Dan ka timoh paleng hana biasa leumak;
Bah pih meunan gata tapeu-ulang-ulang bak pintoe—
Doa, peue sabab jih nyan?”

“Bak masa muda lôn,” kheun ureuëng bijaksana nyan, bak geuguncang-guncang kunci abu-abu gobnyan, .
“Ulôn jaga mandum anggota tuboh lôn that leumoh .
Deungon geungui minyeuk nyoe—saboh shilling kotak nyan—
Izinkan lon meukat keu gata saboh pasangan?”

“Gata ka tuha,” kheun aneuk muda nyan, “dan rahang gata ka leumoh that .
Keu peue mantong nyang leubeh teuga nibak suet;
Bah pih meunan gata ka habéh angsa, ngon tuleueng dan paruh—
Doa, kiban cara droeneuh meuhase neupeubuet nyan?”

“Watee ulôn mantong muda,” kheun ayah gobnyan, “lôn jak ubak huköm, .
Dan meudebat tiep-tiep kasus ngon peurumoh lon;
Dan kekuatan otot, nyang geubri keu rahang lon,
Ka meulangsong siumu udeb ulôn.”

“Kamoe ka tuha,” kheun aneuk muda nyan, “hana mungken sidroe ureueng nyang pike .
Bahwa mata gata teuga lagée yoh masa ilée;
Bah pih meunan gata tapeu-imbang saboh beulatang bak ujong idong gata—
Peue nyang jeuet keusabab gata that-that carong?”

“Lhee boh pertanyaan ka lon jaweub, dan nyan ka sep,”
Kheun ayahjih; “bek neubri hawa keudroe!
Peuë neukiraju ulôn jeuët lôn deungö siuroë-uroë hai-hai lagèë nyan?
Be off, atawa lon teupaksa gata u reunyeuen!”

“Nyan hana jipeugah beutoi,” kheun Ulat.

“Hana that beutoi, lon teumakot,” kheun Alice, ngon seumangat; “padum-padum boh kata ka geu ubah.”

“Nyan salah dari awai sampoe akhe,” kheun Ulat deungon teugaih, dan na seungap padum-padum minet.

Ulat nyan nyang phon that geupeugah haba.

“Ukuran peu nyang gata keuneuk jeut keu?” tanyong jih.

“Oh, lon hana khusus keu ukuran,” Alice bagah-bagah geujaweub; “cuma sidroe hana galak meu ubah-ubah seureng that, ka neu teupeu.”

“ Hana lôn teupeue,” kheun Ulat.

Alice hana geupeugah sapeue: jih hana tom that meulawan lam udep jih seugolom jih, dan jih meurasa bahwa jih ka gadoh beungeh.

“Peuë ka pueh jinoenyoe?” kheun Ulat.

“Nyoe, lon seharusjih galak leubeh rayeuk bacut , Pak, meunyo droeneuh hana keberatan,” kheun Alice: “lhee inci nakeuh manyang nyang that malang.”

“Nyan manyang nyang that jroh beutôi!” kheun Ulat nyan beungeh, jipeubeudoh droe jih teudong bak jipeugah haba (manyang jih tepat lhee inci).

“Tapi lon hana biasa!” meulakèe Alice nyang gasien ngon nada nyang meunyeusai. Dan jihnyan jipike keudroe jih, “Ulôn meuharab makhlok-makhlok nyan hana mudah that jisinggung!”

“Kamoe akan meubiasa bak watee,” kheun Ulat; dan jipeutamong warung nyan lam babah jih dan jipeuphon ji-ikôt lom.

Kali nyoe Alice geupreh ngon saba sampoe jipiléh peugah haba lom. Lam si’at dua minet Ulat nyan jiteubiet warung lam babah jih dan jikuap sigo dua goe, dan jiguncang droe jih. Teuma jihnyan jitron nibak kulat, dan jijak meu-ulang-ulang lam naleueng, jipeugah mantong bak jijak, “Saboh blah teuma jipeujeuet gata leubeh manyang, dan blah nyang laén teuma jipeujeuet gata leubeh paneuk.”

“Saboh blah peu? Siblah laen peu? ” pike Alice lam hate.

“Dari kulat,” kheun Ulat, lagee ji tanyong deungon su nyang kreuh; dan lam siat nyang laén ka hana deuh le.

Alice teutap geukaloen ngon pikeran-pikeran bak kulat nyan siminiet, geucuba teupeue pat dua boh sisi jih; dan sabab ka bulat that, gobnyan geuteumeung nyoe saboh peukara nyang that brat payah. Namun, akhe jih jih jipeuluwah jaroe jih meulingka nyan seujioh awaknyan jijak, dan jipeuputoh bacut binteh ngon tiep-tiep jaroe.

“Dan jinoenyoe pat nyang pat?” kheun jih keudroe, dan jigilo bacut jaroe uneun keu jicuba efek jih: bak saat lheuh nyan jih jirasa saboh pukulan nyang ganas dimiyueb dagu jih: ka jipoh gaki jih!

Gobnyan that teumakot keu perubahan nyang that-that tiba-tiba nyoe, teuma gobnyan geurasa hana le watee nyang jeuet keu gadoh, sabab gobnyan ka jisuson deungon bagah; jadi jih mulai keurija sigra keu jipajoh padum-padum boh bit nyang laen. Dagu jih jiteugon that-that bak gaki jih, sampoe hana le teumpat keu jibuka babah jih; teuma jihnyan jipeubuet akhé jih, dan meuhase jiteulon saboh boh bit jaroe wie.

“Jak, ulee lon ka bebas akhe jih!” kheun Alice deungon nada seunang, njang meu-ubah djeuët keu alarm bak siat laén, watèë djiteumeung bahwa bahu djih hana pat djiteumeung: njang djeuët djikalon, watèë djikalon u mijub, nakeuh saboh takuë njang panyang that, njang deuh djiék lagèë saboh tangkai njang teuka nibak laôt ôn-ôn ijo njang djiôh di mijub djih.

“Peue nyang jeuet keu mandum barang ijo nyan?” kheun Alice. "Dan pat ka troh bahu lon? Dan oh, jaroe lon nyang gasien, kiban cara lon hana jeuet lon kalon droeneuh?" Gobnyan teungoh geujak-jak awaknyan bak watée gobnyan geupeugah haba, teuma hana hase nyang deuh jih jiseutôt, keucuali bacut geumeu-ulang-ulang lamkawan ôn-ôn kayée ijo nyang jiôh.

Bak deuh jih hana peluang keu ji ek jaroe jih u ateuh ulee jih, jih ji cuba ji ek ulee jih u ateuh awaknyan, dan seunang that watee ji teume bahwa takue jih akan ji bungkok deungon mudah u arah peu mantong, lagee uleue. Jih baroe berhasil ji lengkung u miyueb jeut keu zigzag anggun, dan akan ji plueng lam antara ôn-ôn kayée, nyang jih teume hana laén seulaén pucok-pucok bak kayée dimiyueb jih nyang ka ji meu-ulang-ulang, watée na su nyang tajam jipeujeuet jih ji tarek lom deungon bagah-bagah: saboh daraba rayeuk ka ji plueng u muka jih deungon ganas.

“Uleue!” kheun Merpati.

“Ulôn kon uleue!” kheun Alice ngon beungeh. “Bek kheueh ulôn keudroe!”

“Uleue, lon peugah lom!” geu ulang le Merpati, tapi deungon nada nyang leubeh leumoh, dan geu tamah lom ngon saboh macam tangisan, “Lon ka lon cuba mandum cara, dan hana sapeue nyang deuh jih cocok keu awaknyan!”

“Lôn hana lôn teupeue peue nyang gata peugah nyan,” kheun Alice.

“Lôn ka lôn cuba ukheue bak kayèe, dan ka lôn cuba binèh, dan ka lôn cuba pagar,” kheun Merpati nyan, hana geujaga jih; "tapi uleue-uleue nyan! Hana nyang jeuet keu seunang awaknyan!"

Alice seumaken bingong, tapi jih jipike hana guna jipeugah sapeue le sampoe Merpati nyan ka seuleusoe.

“Lagee hana sep susah jiteubiet boh telor,” kheun Merpati; "teuma ulôn harôh lôn jaga uleue malam ngon uroe! Pakon, lhée minggu nyoe ulôn hana lôn teungeut siat pih!"

“Lon meu’ah that droeneuh ka keunong keunong,” kheun Alice, nyang ka mulai deuh makna jih.

"Dan bak watèë ulôn ka lôn cok bak kayèë njang paléng manyang dalam uteuën," sambong Merpati, djipeuék su djih deungon su njang meu-ulang-ulang, "dan bak watèë ulôn piké ulôn harôih bebas nibak awaknjan akhédjih, awaknjan pasti peureulèë djiteuka meu-ulang-ulang nibak langèt! Ugh, Uleuë!"

“Tapi lon kon uleue, lon peugah bak droeneuh!” kheun Alice. “Lôn sidroe—lôn sidroe—”

"Nyoe! Peue droeneuh?" kheun Si Merpati. “Lôn kalon droeneuh neucuba neucipta sapeue-sapeue!”

“Lôn—lôn sidroe aneuk dara ubit,” kheun Alice, agak ragu, watee gobnyan teuingat keu jumlah perubahan nyang ka geualami bak uroe nyan.

“Saboh kisah nyang mungken that!” kheun Si Merpati ngon nada hinaan nyang paleng dalam. "Lon ka lon kalon le that aneuk dara ubit bak masa lon, tapi hana tom sidroe pih nyang na takue lagee nyan! Hana, hana! Droeneuh uleue; dan hana guna lon tulak nyan. Lon kira droeneuh akan neu peugah bak lon selanjut jih bahwa droeneuh hana tom neurasa boh telor!"

“Lôn ka lôn rasa boh telor, ka pasti,” kheun Alice, nyang aneuk miet nyang that beutôi; “tapi aneuk dara ubit jipajoh boh telor cukop le lagee uleue, gata teupeu.”

“Hana lôn peucaya,” kheun Si Merpati; “tapi meunyo awak nyan peugot, pakon teuma awak nyan seumacam uleue, nyan mantong nyang jeut lon peugah.”

Nyoe nakeuh saboh pikeran baroe keu Alice, sampoe jih cukop seungap seulama si-miniet atawa dua minet, nyang geubri kesempatan keu Merpati untuk geutamah, "Droeneuh teungoh neumita boh telor, lon teupeu nyan cukop get; dan peue peunteng keu lon peu droeneuh aneuk dara ubit atawa uleue?"

“Nyan that peunteng keu ulôn ,” kheun Alice deungon bagah-bagah; “tapi lon hana lon mita boh telor, lagee nyang ka teujadi; dan meunyo lon, lon hana patot lon keuneuk boh telor droeneuh : lon hana galak boh telor manyang.”

“Nyoe, beu off, meunyo meunan!” kheun Merpati ngon nada nyang meu-ulang-ulang, bak jiduek lom lam sarang jih. Alice jidong di teungoh-teungoh bak kayee seubagoe nyang jeuet, sabab takue jih sabe meusangkôt lam cabeueng-cabeueng, dan tiep-tiep watee jih harôh jitheun dan jibuka takue nyan. Lheueh siat jih teuingat bahwa jih mantong jimat boh-boh kulat nyan lam jaroe jih, dan jih jipeuphon buet deungon that-that, jigileng-gileng phon bak saboh dan lheuhnyan bak nyang laen, dan timoh kadang leubeh manyang dan kadang leubeh paneuk, sampoe jih ka meuhase jipeutron droe jih u manyang nyang ka biasa.

Ka treb that jih hana sapeue-sapeue nyang to ngon ukuran nyang paih, sampoe rasa jih cukop aneh bak awai; teuma jih ka biasa lam padum-padum boh minet, dan mulai jipeugah haba ngon droe jih, lagee biasa. "Teuka, na seuteungeh rencana lon ka seuleusoe jinoe! Pakriban that meupeubingong mandum perubahan nyoe! Lon hana tom yakin peu nyang akan lon jeut, dari saboh minet u minet laen! Teuma, lon ka lon woe u ukuran lon nyang paih: hal selanjut jih nakeuh, untuk tamong u dalam taman nyang lagak nyan—pakriban cara nyan jeut lon peugot, lon teumanyong?" Bak watèe gobnyan geupeugah haba lagée nyan, hana teusangka-sangka gobnyan trok ubak teumpat nyang teuhah, na saboh rumoh ubit nyang manyang jih na kira-kira peuet gaki. “Soe mantong nyang tinggai disinan,” pikeran Alice, “hana tom teuka ateuh awaknyan nyang ukuran jih lagee nyoe : pakon, lon harus peutakot awaknyan dari akai awaknyan!” Jadi jih mulai jigilo-gilo bak bit jaroe uneun lom, dan hana berani jijak toe ngon rumoh sampoe jih ka jipeutron droe jih u sikureung inci manyang.
