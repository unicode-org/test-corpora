Lobster Quadrille

Mock Turtle nih cun a thaw a chuah i, a mit cung ah flapper pakhat hnulei kha a hnuh. Alice cu a zoh, cun biachim aa zuam, asinain minute pakhat hnih chung cu a aw a kham. "A hnar ah ruh a um bantuk khin," tiah Gryphon nih a ti: cun a hninh i a hnulei ah a tuk. A donghnak ah Mock Turtle nih a aw a hmuh tthan, cun, a mitthli he, a peh tthan:—

“Cu tikah, na hngalh ko,” tiah Mock Turtle nih a peh, “na hlonhmi cu—”

“Lobster pawl kha thlen ṭhan hna!” tiah Gryphon nih a aw sang bik in a au.

“Vawlei ah kir tthan, cun cucu a hmasa bik hmanthlak a si dih,” tiah Mock Turtle nih a ti, a aw cu ruahlopi in a tthumh; cun thilnung pahnih hna cu, hi caan saupi chung mihrut bantukin a zuangmi hna cu, ngaihchia ngai le dai tein an ṭhu ṭhan i Alice kha an zoh.

“A dawh tukmi laam a si ko lai,” tiah Alice nih ningzak ngai in a ti.

“Tlawmpal te hmuh na duh maw?” tiah Mock Turtle nih a ti.

“A ngaingai ti ahcun,” tiah Alice nih a ti.

“Ra law, a hmasa bik hmanthlak kha i zuam hna u sih!” tiah Mock Turtle nih Gryphon cu a ti. "Lobster tel loin kan um kho ko, na hngalh ko. Aho nih dah hla a sak lai?"

“Oh, na sak,” tiah Gryphon nih a ti. “Biafang cu ka philh cang.”

“Ka lawm, zoh awk a nuam tukmi laam a si,” tiah Alice nih a ti, a donghnak ah a dih cang caah aa lawm tuk: “cun whiting kong he aa tlaiin a hngalh a nuammi hla cu ka duh tuk!”

“Dinn cu khuazei ah dah a um ti ka hngal lo,” tiah Mock Turtle nih a ti, “asinain na hmuh lengmang hna ahcun, zeidah an lawh ti kha na hngalh ko lai.”

"A ruang cu," tiah Gryphon nih a ti, "laamnak ah lobster pawl he an kal lai . Cucaah rili ah an hlonh hna. Cucaah lam hlapi ah an tla a hau. Cucaah an mei cu an kaa chungah rang tein an chiah. Cucaah an chuah khawh ti hna lo. Cu lawng cu a si."

Alice nih, “Ka lawm tuk, a nuam tuk, hi tluk in whiting kong hi ka rak hngal bal lo,” tiah Alice nih a ti.

“Na duh ahcun mah nak tam deuh in kan chimh khawh,” tiah Gryphon nih a ti. “Zeicahdah whiting tiah an auh ti na hngal maw?”

“Zeicah, na kedan cu zeidah an tuah?” tiah Gryphon nih a ti. “Ka chim duhmi cu, zeinihdah a ceuter hna?”

Alice nih a zoh hna i a lehnak a pek hna hlanah tlawmpal a ruah. “Annih cu blacking he an dih cang, ka zumh.”

"Rili tang i kedan le kedan," tiah Gryphon nih aw thukpi in a peh, "a rangmi he an dih cang. Atu cu na hngalh cang."

“Cun zei in dah an ser hna?” Alice nih theih duhnak nganpi he a hal.

"Soles le eels, a si ko," tiah Gryphon nih lungsau lo ngaiin a leh: "zeibantuk shrimp hmanh nih an in chimh khawh ko lai."

“Keimah hi whiting ka si ahcun,” tiah hla ah a ruahnak a kal pengmi Alice nih a ti, “porpoise sinah, ‘Zaangfahnak tein hnulei ah kir ko: kan sinah kan in duh lo!’ tiah ka ti hnga,” tiah a ti.

"Anmah he umter ding in nawl an ngei," tiah Mock Turtle nih a ti: "nga fim pakhat hmanh porpoise tel loin khoika hmanh ah an kal lai lo."

“A si taktak lai lo maw?” tiah Alice nih khuaruahhar ngai in a ti.

“A si lo,” tiah Mock Turtle nih a ti: “zeicahdah, nga pakhat nih ka sinah a ra i khual a tlawng lai tiah a ka ti ahcun, ‘Zei porpoise he dah?’ tiah ka ti awk a si.”

“‘Tinhmi’ na chim duhmi a si lo maw?” tiah Alice nih a ti.

“Ka chimmi cu ka chim duhmi a si,” tiah Mock Turtle nih thinhunnak aw in a leh. Cun Gryphon nih “Ra law, na khualtlawnnak cheukhat kan theih lai” tiah a ti chap.

“Ka khualtlawnnak hna kha kan chimh khawh—tuzing in aa thawk,” tiah Alice nih tlawmpal ṭih a nungmi in a ti: “asinain nizan ah kir ṭhan cu santlaihnak a ngei lo, zeicahtiah cu lio ahcun midang he aa dangmi ka si.”

“Cu vialte cu fianter,” tiah Mock Turtle nih a ti.
