CHITSAUKO I.
Pasi paTsuro-Hole

Alice akanga ava kutanga kuneta zvikuru nokugara nasisi vake pabhangi, uye nokusava nechokuita: kamwe kana kuti kaviri akanga adongorera mubhuku hanzvadzi yake yaiverenga, asi rakanga risina mifananidzo kana kuti kurukurirano imomo, “uye bhuku rinoshandiswa nei,” akafunga Alice “asina mapikicha kana kurukurirano?”

Saka akanga achifunga mupfungwa dzake (sezvaaigona, nokuti zuva raipisa rakamuita kuti anzwe akabatwa nehope zvikuru uye ari benzi), kana mufaro wokugadzira daisy-cheni waizokodzera dambudziko rokumuka nokunonga daisies, apo kamwe kamwe Tsuro Tsuro ine maziso epingi yakamhanya pedyo naye.

Kwakanga kusina chinhu chaishamisa zvikuru ipapo; uye Alice haana kuzvifunga zvisingaite kunzwa Tsuro ichizvitaurira yega kuti, “Aaa mudiwa! (paakazozvifunga gare gare, zvakamunetsa kuti aifanira kunge akashamisika nazvo, asi panguva iyoyo zvese zvaiita sezvineiwo); Asi Tsuro paakanyatso buritsa wachi muhomwe yejasi rake , ndokuitarisa, ndokumhanya achienda mberi, Alice akabva asimuka, nekuti zvakamujekera mupfungwa dzake kuti akanga asati amboona tsuro ine bhachi rebhachi, kana wachi kuti aibudise, uye achipisa neshungu dzekuda kuziva, akamhanya mumunda achida kuona. tsuro-gomba pasi peheji.

Mune imwe nguva pasi Alice akaitevera, asina kana kumbofunga kuti munyika aizobuda sei zvakare.

Gomba retsuro rakananga mberi semugero neimwe nzira, ndokuzonyura pasi pakarepo, zvekuti Alice akashaya nguva yekufunga kuzvimisa asati aona adonhera mugomba rakadzika kwazvo.

Zvimwe tsime rakanga rakadzika kwazvo, kana kuti rakadonha zvishoma nezvishoma, nokuti akanga ava nenguva yakawanda achidzika kuti atarise pamusoro paro uye kushamisika kuti chii chaizoitika pashure. Chekutanga, akaedza kutarisa pasi kuti aone zvaaiuya, asi kwainge kwasviba zvekusaona chinhu; akabva atarisa kumativi etsime, ndokuona kuti akanga akazara makabhati nemasherufu emabhuku; pano neapo akaona mamepu nemifananidzo zvakaturikwa pahoko. Akabura chirongo pane rimwe ramasherufu paaipfuura; rakanga rakanyorwa kuti “ORANGE MARMALADE”, asi kumuodza mwoyo kukuru rakanga risina chinhu: akanga asingadi kudonhedza chirongo ichi nekutya kuuraya mumwe munhu nechepasi, saka akakwanisa kuchiisa mune rimwe remakabhati paakanga achiwira pedyo naro.

“Zvakanaka!” akafunga Alice mumwoyo make, kuti: “Pashure pokudonha kwakadaro, handisati ndichizofunga chinhu chipi nechipi chokudonha pasi pamasitepisi! (Izvo zvingangove zvechokwadi.)

Pasi, pasi, pasi. Kuwa kwaisazombopera here? "Hameno kuti ndadonha mamaira mangani panguva ino?" akadaro achidanidzira. “Ndinofanira kunge ndichisvika pedyo nepakati penyika. Rega ndione: ikoko kungava mamaira zviuru zvina pasi, ndinofunga—” (nokuti, munoona, Alice akanga adzidza zvinhu zvinoverengeka zvorudzi urwu muzvidzidzo zvake mukamuri rechikoro, uye kunyange uyu wakanga usiri mukana wakanaka kwazvo wokuratidza zivo yake, sezvo pakanga pasina wokumuteerera, kunyange zvakadaro wakanga uri muitiro wakanaka kutaura kudaro) “—hungu, ndinoshamisika kuti kureba kwakadini panguva iyoyo—asi kuti Longitude yakadini?” (Alice aisaziva kuti Latitude chii, kana Longitude zvakare, asi akafunga kuti aive mazwi akanaka ekutaura.)

Iye zvino akatanga zvakare. “Ndinoshamisika kana ndichawira pasi chaipo! Zvichaita sezvinosetsa sei kubuda pakati pavanhu vanofamba vakaisa misoro yavo pasi! The Antipathies, ndinofunga—” (akafara kuti pakanga pasina aiteerera, panguva ino, sezvo yaisanzwika zvachose shoko rakakodzera) “—asi ndichafanira kuvabvunza kuti zita renyika chii, munoziva. Ndapota, Amai kana kuti Australia, iyi New Zealand?” (uye akaedza kutuka sezvaaitaura—kutuka kwokunyepedzera sezvauri kuwira mumhepo! Unofunga kuti ungagona kuzvigona here?) “Uye musikana muduku asingazivi zvakadini iye achandifunga nokuda kwokubvunza! Aiwa, hazvimboiti kubvunza: zvichida ndichaiona yakanyorwa pane imwe nzvimbo.”

Pasi, pasi, pasi. Pakanga pasisina zvekuita saka Alice akabva atanga kutaura zvekare. "Dhina achandisuwa zvakanyanya manheru ano, ndinofunga!" (Dhina ndiye aiva katsi.) “Ndinovimba vachayeuka sosa ravo remukaka panguva yetii. Uye pano Alice akatanga kubatwa nehope, ndokuenderera mberi nekutaura mumoyo make, nenzira yekurota, "Katsi dzinodya zviremwaremwa? Katsi dzinodya zviremwaremwa?" uye dzimwe nguva, “Zviremwaremwa zvinodya katsi here?” nokuti, munoona, sezvo aisakwanisa kupindura mumwe mubvunzo, zvakanga zvisina basa kuti akazviisa nenzira ipi. Akanzwa kubatwa nehope, uye akanga achangotanga kurota achifamba akabatwa ruoko naDhina, uye achiti kwaari nemoyo wose, “Zvino, Dhina, ndiudze chokwadi: wakambodya chiremwaremwa here? apo kamwe kamwe, kurova! thump! akasvikowana murwi wetutsotso nemashizha akaoma, uye kuwa kwakapera.

Alice haana kana kumbokuvara, akabva asvetukira kutsoka dzake nechinguvana: akatarisa mudenga, asi kumusoro kwainge kwakasviba; pamberi pake paive neimwe nzira refu, Tsuro Muchena yakanga ichiri kuoneka, ichimhanya kudzika nayo. Pakanga pasina nguva yekurasika: Alice akaenda semhepo, uye yakanga yave nguva yekuinzwa ichiti, ichikona kona, "Ah nzeve dzangu nendebvu, kwave kunonoka!" Akange ave kuseri kwaro paakakona, asi Tsuro akanga asingachaoneki: akazviona ava muhoro refu, yakaderera, iyo yaivhenekerwa nemitsara yemwenje yakarembera padenga.

Kwakanga kune mikova yakapoteredza imba yose, asi ose akanga akakiyiwa; zvino Alice paakanga ambodzika nerimwe divi achikwira nerimwe, achiedza masuo ose, akafamba akasuruvara achidzika nepakati, achifunga kuti aizobudazve sei.

Pakarepo akasvika pane katafura kemakumbo matatu, ese akagadzirwa negirazi rakasimba; pakanga pasina chinhu pairi kunze kwekiyi diki yegoridhe, uye pfungwa yekutanga yaAlice yaive yekuti inogona kunge iri yemumwe wemagonhi ehoro; asi, maiwe! zvimwe zvivharo zvaive zvakakurisa, kana kiyi yaive diki, asi chero ipi zvayo yaisavhura chero yadzo. Zvisinei, kechipiri kutenderera, akasvika paketeni rakaderera raakanga asina kumboona kare, uye shure kwaro kwakanga kune gonhi duku rinenge mainji gumi namashanu pakukwirira: akaedza kakiyi kaduku kendarama muchikiyi, uye kumufaro wake mukuru rakaiswa!

Alice akavhura musuwo ndokuona kuti waipinda mukanzira kadiki, kasina kunyanyokura kudarika gomba remakonzo: akapfugama ndokutarisa padivi penzira inopinda mubindu rakanakisa rawati wamboona. Aishuva sei kubuda muhoro ine rima, ofamba-famba pakati pemibhedha yemaruva anopenya nematsime anotonhorera, asi akanga asingakwanisi kupinza musoro wake nepamusuwo; “uye kunyange kana musoro wangu ukapfuura,” akafunga kudaro Alice murombo, “kwaizova kwebetsero shomanene zvikuru pasina mapendekete angu.” Haiwa, ndinoshuva sei kuti dai ndaigona kunyarara seteresikopu! Nekuti, munoona, zvinhu zvakawanda zvekunze zvakaitika nguva pfupi yadarika, zvekuti Alice akange atanga kufunga kuti zvinhu zvishoma chaizvo zvaive zvisingaite.

Pakaita sepasina basa rekumirira nepamusuwo mudiki, saka akadzokera kutafura, hafu achitarisira kuti angangowana imwe kiyi pairi, kana chero bhuku remitemo yekuvharisa vanhu seteresikopu: panguva ino akawana bhodhoro diki pariri, ("izvo zvamazvirokwazvo rakanga risati ravapo," akadaro Alice,) uye kumutsipa webhodhoro kwaive nepepa label, rakanyorwa nemavara ME," nemavara makuru "DRINK".

Zvaiva zvakanaka kutaura kuti “Ndinwei,” asi Alice muduku akachenjera akanga asiri kuzozviita nokukurumidza. “Kwete, ndichatanga ndatarisa,” akadaro, “ndoona kuti yakanyorwa kuti ‘ chepfu ’ here kana kuti kwete”; nokuti akanga averenga nhoroondo duku dzakanaka dzinoverengeka pamusoro pevana vakapiswa, uye vakadyiwa nezvikara zvomusango nezvimwe zvinhu zvisingafadzi, zvose nemhaka yokuti vaisazorangarira mitemo yakapfava yavakanga vadzidziswa neshamwari dzavo: yakadai sokuti, kuti poker tsvuku ichakupisa kana ukaibata kwenguva refu; uye kuti kana ukacheka chigunwe chako zvakadzama nebanga, zvinowanzobuda ropa; uye akanga asina kumbokanganwa kuti, kana ukanwa zvakawanda kubva mubhodhoro rakanyorwa kuti “chepfu,” zvinenge zvotoda kusabvumirana newe, nokukurumidza kana kuti gare gare.

Zvisinei, bhodhoro iri rakanga risina kunyorwa kuti “chepfu,” saka Alice akashinga kuriravira, uye akariwana rakanaka kwazvo, (rakanga riine, kutaura zvazviri, rudzi rwekunhuhwirira kwakasanganiswa kwecherry-tart, custard, pine-apple, turkey yakagochwa, toffee, uye tositi inopisa ine butter,) akakurumidza kuipedza.

“Manzwiro ekuda kuziva akadini!” Akadaro Alice; "Ndinofanira kunge ndakavhara seteresikopu."

Uye ndizvo zvazvaiva chaizvo: akanga zvino angori nemainji gumi kureba, uye chiso chake chakapenya pakufunga kuti akanga zvino akanga akwana saizi yekupinda nepamusuwo mudiki mubindu rinoyevedza. Kutanga, kunyanguvezvo, akamirira kwamaminiti mashomanene kuti aone kana akanga achizodererazve: akanzwa kakutya pamusoro paikoku; "nokuti zvinogona kupera, unoziva," akadaro Alice mumoyo make, "mukuenda kwangu zvachose, sekenduru. Uye akaedza kufunga kuti murazvo wekenduru rakaita sei kana kenduru radzimwa, nokuti akanga asingayeuki kuti akamboona chinhu chakadaro.

Pashure penguva yakati, achiona kuti hapana chimwe chakaitika, akafunga kupinda mubindu pakarepo; asi, nhamo Alice murombo! paakasvika pasuo, akawana kuti akanga akanganwa kiyi duku yendarama, uye paakadzokera kutafura nokuda kwayo, akawana kuti akanga asingakwanisi kusvika kwairi: aigona kuiona zvakajeka zvikuru nomugirazi, uye akaedza nepaaigona napo kukwira rimwe remakumbo etafura, asi rakanga richitsvedza zvikuru; zvino akati aneta nokuedza, murombo akagara pasi akachema.

“Huya, kuchema kudaro hakuna zvakunobatsira!” akadaro Alice mumoyo, zviri nani chaizvo; "Ndinokupa zano kuti usiye nguva ino!" Aiwanzozvipa zano rakanaka kwazvo, (kunyange zvazvo aisawanzoritevera), uye dzimwe nguva aizvituka zvikuru zvokuti aibudisa misodzi mumaziso ake; uye akamborangarira kuedza kuvhara nzeve dzake kuti akanga azvinyengedza mumutambo wemakaro waaitamba naye, nokuti mwana uyu aida kuziva aifarira zvikuru kuzviita vanhu vaviri. “Asi hazvibatsiri iye zvino,” akadaro Alice murombo, “kunyepedzera kuva vanhu vaviri !

Nenguva isipi ziso rake rakawira pabhokisi duku regirazi rakanga rakarara pasi petafura: akarivhura, uye akawana mukati maro keke duku kwazvo, apo mazwi okuti "NDIDYA" akanga akanyorwa zvakanaka mu currants. "Zvakanaka, ndichaidya," akadaro Alice, "uye kana ikaita kuti ndikure, ndinogona kusvika kiyi; uye kana ikaita kuti ndiite mudiki, ndinogona kunyura pasi pegonhi; saka chero nzira ndichapinda mubindu, uye handina basa nezvinoitika!

Akadya zvishoma, uye akati mumwoyo make, "Nzira ipi? Nzira ipi?", Achibata ruoko rwake pamusoro pemusoro wake kuti anzwe nzira iyo yaikura nayo, uye akashamiswa zvikuru kuona kuti anoramba akaenzana: kuva nechokwadi, izvi zvinowanzoitika kana munhu adya keke, asi Alice akanga apinda zvakanyanya munzira yekutarisira chimwe chinhu kunze kwekubuda-kwe-nzira-yakaita sezvinhu zvehupenzi kuti zviitike muupenyu.

Saka akatanga kushanda, uye nokukurumidza akapedza keke.
