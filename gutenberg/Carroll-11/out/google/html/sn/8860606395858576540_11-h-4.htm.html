CHITSAUKO IV.
Tsuro Inotumira Mari Mudiki

Yakanga iri Tsuro Yechena, ichifamba zvishoma nezvishoma ichidzokera shure, uye ichitarisa neshungu pamusoro payaifamba, sokunge yakanga yarasikirwa nechimwe chinhu; uye akainzwa ichigunun'una ichiti, "The Duchess! The Duchess! Oh my dear paws! Oh my mvere nendebvu! Achaita kuti ndiurawe, chokwadi semapere! Ndingavadonhedzepi , ndinoshamisika?" Alice akafungidzira nechinguvana kuti yaitsvaga feni uye magurovhosi emwana machena, uye aine hunhu hwakanaka akatanga kuvavhima, asi hapana kwavaionekwa - zvese zvaiita senge zvachinja kubva paakashambira mudziva, uye horo huru, ine tafura yegirazi negonhi diki, rakanga ranyangarika zvachose.

Nenguva isipi Tsuro akacherechedza Alice, paaifamba achivhima, ndokumudana nenzwi rehasha, "Sei, Mary Ann, uri kuitei kunze kuno? Mhanya kumba panguva ino, unonditorera magirovhosi nefeni! Kurumidza, iko zvino!" Uye Alice akatya zvekuti akabva angomhanya achienda kwayakanongedza, asina kuedza kutsanangura kukanganisa kwayakanga yaita.

“Akanditora semusikana wake webasa,” akadaro mumoyo achimhanya. “Achashamisika chaizvo paachaziva kuti ndini ani! Paaitaura izvi, akasvika pane kamwe kamba kakanga kakatsvinda, pamusuwo paive nendiro yendarira inopenya yakanga yakanyorwa zita rekuti “W. TSRORO”. Akapinda asina kugogodza, uye akakurumidza kukwira, achitya zvikuru kuti angasangana naMary Ann chaiye, ndokubudiswa mumba asati awana feni namagirovhosi.

Alice akati mumwoyo make, “Zvinoita sezvisinganzwisisike sei kutumira mameseji ndichitsvaga tsuro! Uye akatanga kufunga nezvechinhu chaizoitika: “'Muzvare Alice! 'Ndichiuya nechinguvana nurse! Ini chete handifungi,” Alice akaenderera mberi, “kuti vaizorega Dhina akamira mumba kana akatanga kurayira vanhu saizvozvo!”

Panguva ino akange awana nzira yake mukamuri diki rakatsvinda raive netafura pahwindo, uye pairi (sezvaaitarisira) fani uye maviri kana matatu peya madiki magurovhosi emwana: akatora feni nepeya yegurovhosi, uye akange ave kubuda mukamuri, apo ziso rake rakawira pabhodhoro diki rakamira pedyo negirazi raitarisa. Pakanga pasina chinyorwa panguva ino chine mazwi okuti “NDINWADZAI,” asi zvakadaro akachibvisa ndokuchiisa pamuromo wake. “Ndinoziva kuti chimwe chinhu chinonakidza chichaitika zvechokwadi,” akadaro mumwoyo make, “pese pandinodya kana kunwa chero chinhu; saka ndichangoona zvinoita bhodhoro iri.

Zvakadaro zvechokwadi, uye nekukurumidza kupfuura zvaaitarisira: asati anwa hafu yebhodhoro, akawana musoro wake uchidzvanya padenga, uye aifanira kukotama kuti achengetedze mutsipa wake kubva pakutyoka. Akachimbidza kuisa bhodhoro pasi, achitaura mumwoyo make kuti “Zvakwana—ndinovimba kuti handichakuri—Sezvazviri, handichakwanisi kubuda pasuo—ndinoshuva kuti dai ndisina kunwa zvakanyanya!”

Maiwe! kwanga kwava kunonoka kushuvira izvozvo! Akaramba achikura, nekukura, uye nekukasika aifanira kupfugama pasi: mune imwe miniti pakanga pasina kana nzvimbo yeizvi, uye akaedza maitiro ekurara pasi negokora rimwe pamusuwo, uye rumwe ruoko rwakatenderedza musoro wake. Zvakadaro akaramba achikura, uye, sechishandiso chekupedzisira, akaburitsa ruoko rumwe kunze kwehwindo, uye imwe tsoka kumusoro kwechimuni, akati mumoyo make, "Zvino handichagona kuita, chero zvichaitika. Chii chichaitika kwandiri?"

Neraki kuna Alice, bhodhoro diki remashiripiti rakanga rave nekuita kwaro kuzere, uye harina kukura: zvakadaro rakanga risina kugadzikana, uye, sezvo pairatidzika kunge pakanga pasina mukana wekuti abudezve mukamuri, hazvishamisi kuti akanzwa kusafara.

“Kwakanga kuchifadza zvikuru kumba,” akafunga kudaro Alice murombo, “apo mumwe akanga asiri kugara achikura uye muduku nguva dzose, uye achitungamirirwa namakonzo netsuro.” Ndakapotsa ndangoti dai ndisina kudzika mugomba retsuro—uye zvakadaro—asi—zvinotoda kuziva, munoziva, rudzi urwu rwoupenyu! uye zvino ndiri pano pakati perimwe! Panofanira kuva nebhuku rakanyorwa pamusoro pangu, iro rinofanira kuva! " Kuno hapasisina nzvimbo yekukurira."

“Asi zvino,” akafunga kudaro Alice, “ handichazombofi ndakakura kupfuura zvandiri zvino here ?

"Ah iwe Alice benzi!" akazvipindura. “ Ungadzidza sei zvidzidzo muno?

Saka akaenderera mberi, achitora rumwe rutivi ndokuzotevera rumwe, uye akaita nhaurirano pamusoro pazvo zvachose; asi mushure memaminitsi mashoma akanzwa inzwi panze, ndokumira kuti ateerere.

"Mary Ann! Mary Ann!" rakadaro izwi. “Nditore magirovhosi angu nguva ino!” Pakabva paita kakufamba makumbo pamanera. Alice akaziva kuti ndiTsuro yaiuya kuzomutsvaga, akadedera kusvikira azunza imba, akanganwa kuti akanga akura zvakapetwa kachuru saTsuro, uye akanga asina chikonzero chokuzvitya.

Parizvino Tsuro akauya pamusuwo, ndokuedza kuvhura; asi, sezvo gonhi rakavhurika mukati, uye gokora yaAlice yakatsikirirwa zvakasimba pairi, kuedza ikoko kwakabvumikisa kukundikana. Alice akanzwa achitaura ega, "Ndobva ndatenderera ndopinda nepahwindo."

" Kuti haudi!" Akafunga Alice, uye, mushure mekumirira kusvika afunga kuti anzwa Tsuro pasi pehwindo, akangoerekana atambanudza ruoko rwake, uye akaita kubvuta mudenga. Haana chaakabata, asi akanzwa kamhere kekuchema uye kudonha, uye kubondera kwegirazi rakaputsika, kubva kwaakabva afunga kuti zvaingoita kuti radonha mucucumber-frame, kana chimwe chinhu chakadaro.

Kwakabva kwauya izwi rehasha—raTsuro—“Pat! Pat! Ipapo inzwi raakanga asati ambonzwa richiti, “Chokwadi ndiri pano!

"Kuchera maapuro, chokwadi!" akadaro Tsuro nehasha. “Here! Come and help me out of this! ” (Kunzwika kwerimwe girazi rakaputsika.)

"Zvino ndiudze, Pat, chii chiri pahwindo?"

"Chokwadi, ruoko, kukudzwa!" (Akaritumidza kuti "arrum.")

“Ruoko, hanzi, ndiani akamboona saizi yakadai?

"Chokwadi, zvinoita, kukudzwa: asi ruoko rwezvose izvozvo."

"Zvakanaka, hazvina basa ipapo, chero zvakadaro: enda undozvitora!"

Pakamboita karunyararo mushure meizvi, Alice aingonzwa kazevezeve apo neapo; sekuti, “Chokwadi, handizvifarire, kukudzwa, zvachose, zvachose!” “Ita sezvandinokuudza, mbwende iwe! zvino akatambanudzazve ruoko rwake, akaita imwe mbvumo mudenga. Panguva iyi paive nekurira kuviri , uye mamwe manzwi egirazi rakapwanyika. "Iko kunofanirwa kunge kune akati wandei emafuremu emagaka!" akafunga Alice. Hameno kuti vazodii!Kana vari kundibudisa nepahwindo, ndinongoti dai vagona !

Akamirira kwekanguva pasina chimwe chaanzwa: pakupedzisira kwakauya ruzha rwemavhiri engoro, uye ruzha rwemanzwi akawanda akawanda achitaura pamwe chete: akataura mashoko okuti: “Rimwe manera riripiko?—Sei, ndanga ndisina kuunza rimwe chete, Bill ane rimwe—Bill! asi—Aa, achaita zvakanaka zvakakwana—Apa, Bill batisisa tambo iyi—Ko denga remba richabereka here? (kuputsika kukuru)—“Zvino, ndiani akaita izvozvo?—Anga ari Bill, wandinoda—Ndiani achaburuka nechimbini?—Kwete, handingadaro ! Unozviita !— Kuti handidi, ipapoka!—ZvaBill kudzika—Hezvo, Bill!

Saka Bill anofanira kudzika pasi nechimbini, adaro here? akadaro Alice mumoyo. “ Kunyara, vanoita kunge vari kuisa zvese kuna Bill!

Akakweva tsoka yake kusvika pasi pechimuni nepaaigona napo, ndokumirira kusvika anzwa kamhuka (akatadza kufungidzira kuti chaive rudzii) kachikwenya nekukwenya muchimuni chiri padhuze nepamusoro pake: ipapo, achizviudza kuti “Uyu ndiBill,” akarova kamwe kanopinza, ndokumirira kuona zvaizotevera.

Chinhu chekutanga chaakanzwa yaive korasi yemukurumbira yekuti “There goes Bill! ipapo inzwi raTsuro—“Mubate, iwe paruzhowa!” ndokunyarara, uyezve kumwe kuvhiringika kwemanzwi—“Simudza musoro wake—Brandy zvino—Usamudzipa—Kwanga kuri sei, mudhara? Chii chakaitika kwauri?

Kekupedzisira kwakauya kainzwi kasina simba, kaizhambatata, (“NdiBill,” akafunga Alice,) “Zvakanaka, handichaziva—Kwete, ndatenda; ndave nani izvozvi—asi ndine dhiri rakawandisa kuti ndikuudzei—chandinoziva ndechekuti, chimwe chinhu chinouya kwandiri seJack-mu-bhokisi, uye kumusoro ndinokwira seroketi rekudenga!”

"Saka waita, mudhara!" vakadaro vamwe.

“Tinofanira kupisa imba yacho!” rakadaro izwi raTsuro; uye Alice akadanidzira nenzwi guru sezvaaigona, “Kana ukadaro, ndichaisa Dina kwauri!”

Pakarepo pakaita runyararo, Alice akabva afunga kuti, “Hameno kuti vozoita sei! Mushure meminiti kana maviri, vakatanga kufamba-famba zvakare, uye Alice akanzwa Tsuro oti, “Murovha achaita, kutanga.”

" Chii chakazara? " akafunga Alice; asi haana kunonoka kukahadzika, nguva yakatevera mvura yehurungudo yakauya ichitinhira nepahwindo, uye dzimwe dzacho dzakamurova kumeso. “Ndichamisa izvi,” akadaro mumwoyo make, uye akashevedzera kuti, “Zviri nani kuti usazviita zvakare!” izvo zvakaburitsa kumwe kunyarara kwakafa.

Alice akabva aona nekushamisika kuti tuhurungudo twainge twave kusanduka kuita makeke akazvambarara pasi, mumusoro make mune pfungwa yakajeka. "Kana ndikadya rimwe remakeke aya," akafunga kudaro, "ichokwadi kuita shanduko muhukuru hwangu; uye sezvo isingakwanise kundiita muhombe, inofanira kundiita mudiki, ndinofunga."

Saka akamedza rimwe remakeke, uye akafara kuona kuti akatanga kuunyana. Paakangove mudiki zvekukwanisa kupinda nepamusuwo, akamhanya achibuda mumba, ndokuwana mhomho yezvipfuyo neshiri zvakamira panze. Kadzvinyu murombo, Bill, akanga ari pakati, akasimudzwa nenguruve mbiri, idzo dzaimupa chimwe chinhu kubva mubhodhoro. Vese vakaita kumhanyidzana naAlice paakangooneka; asi akatiza nesimba sezvaaigona, uye nokukurumidza akazviwana akachengeteka muhuni dzakakora.

“Chinhu chokutanga chandinofanira kuita,” akadaro Alice mumwoyo make, sezvaaidzungaira muhuni, “kukura kusvika pakukura kwangu chaiko zvakare; uye chinhu chechipiri ndechokuwana nzira yangu kupinda mubindu iroro rakanaka.

Yaitaridza hurongwa hwakanakisa, pasina kupokana, uye zvakatsvinda uye zvakarongeka; chakangomuomera ndechekuti akanga asina pfungwa diki yekuti oita sei nazvo; uye paakanga akadongorera ari pakati pemiti achishushikana, kahuku kakanga kakapinza pamusoro pemusoro wake kakaita kuti atarise mudenga nekuchimbidza kukuru.

Kambwanana kahombe kakanga kakamutarisa pasi nemaziso mahombe akatenderera, uye kakatambanudza nhava imwe chete, ichiedza kumubata. “Kanhu kadiki kehurombo!” akadaro Alice, nenzwi rekunyengerera, uye akaedza nesimba kuiridzira muridzo; asi aitya zvikuru nguva dzose achifunga kuti ingava nzara, iyo yaizobvira zvikuru kumudya pasinei nokunyengedza kwake kwose.

Nekutadza kuziva zvaakaita, akanhonga kamuti kadiki, ndokukambira kambwanana; ipapo kambwanana kakabva kasvetuka mudenga kubva patsoka dzayo dzose kamwechete, nemhere yemufaro, ndokumhanyira padanda, ndokutenda kuti izvidye mwoyo; ipapo Alice akanzvenga seri kwerukato, kuti azvidzivirire kuti asamhanyiswa; uye nguva yayakaoneka kune rumwe rutivi, kambwanana kakaita kumwe kumhanyirira padanda, ndokuwira pamusoro pezvitsitsinho mukukasira kuibata; ipapo Alice, achifunga kuti zvakanga zvakafanana nekutamba nengoro-bhiza, uye achitarisira kuti nguva imwe neimwe ichatsikwa pasi petsoka dzayo, akamhanya achitenderera rukato zvakare; ipapo kambwanana kakatanga nhevedzano yemhanho pfupi patsvimbo, ichimhanya nenzira duku zvikuru ichienda mberi nguva imwe neimwe uye kure kure shure, uye ichihukura neshungu nguva yose iyi, kutozosvikira pakupedzisira yakagara kure kure, ichifemereka, norurimi rwayo rwakarembera kunze kwomuromo wayo, uye maziso ayo makuru akavharika nepakati.

Izvi zvakaratidzika kuna Alice mukana wakanaka wekuti atize; saka akabva angosimuka, ndokumhanya kusvika aneta uye apererwa nemweya wekufema, uye kusvikira mahun'era embwanana yaita ruzha kure kure.

“Asi chaive chimbwanana chaidiwa sei!” akadaro Alice, achizendamira pakapu kuti azorore, ndokuzvipepeta nerimwe remashizha: "Ndaifanira kunge ndakafarira kudzidzisa manomano zvakanyanya, dai-dai ndanga ndiri saizi chaiyo kuzviita! Ah mudiwa! Ndakapotsa ndakanganwa kuti ndinofanira kukura zvakare! Rega ndione- zvingafambiswa sei?

Mubvunzo mukuru zvechokwadi waiva, chii? Alice akatarisa kumativi ose maruva nemashizha euswa, asi haana kuona chinhu chakaita sechinhu chakanaka kudya kana kunwa mumamiriro ezvinhu. Paiva nehowa hukuru pedyo naye, hunenge hwakareba saiye; uye paakatarira pasi payo, nepamativi ayo ose, neseri kwayo, zvakamufunga kuti atarise ndokuona zvakanga zviri pamusoro payo.

Akazvitwasanudza pamusoro pechigunwe, ndokudongorera nechekumucheto kwehowa, uye maziso ake akabva asanganidzana neegonye zihombe reblue, rakanga rakagara pamusoro rakapeta maoko, chinyararire richisvuta hookah refu, uye risingamutaridzi kana chimwe chinhu.
