CHITSAUKO V.
Mazano kubva kuGonye

Caterpillar naAlice vakatarisana kwechinguva vakanyarara: pakupedzisira Gorosi rakatora hookah kubva mumuromo mayo, ndokutaura naye nezwi rakashata, nehope.

“Ndimi ani? ” akadaro Gonye.

Uyu wakanga usiri mukana unokurudzira wekurukurirano. Alice akapindura achinyara kuti, “Ini—handisati ndaziva, changamire, pari zvino—ndinoziva kuti ndaiva ani pandakamuka mangwanani ano, asi ndinofunga kuti ndinofanira kunge ndakachinjwa kakawanda kubvira ipapo.”

"Unorevei kana uchidaro?" Akadaro Mhungu nehasha. “Zvitsanangure!”

"Handikwanise kuzvitsanangura , ndinotya, changamire," akadaro Alice, "nekuti handisi ini, munoona."

“Handisi kuona,” akadaro Gonye.

"Ndinotya kuti handikwanise kutsanangura zvakajeka," Alice akapindura neruremekedzo, "nokuti ini handizvinzwisise ini kutanga; uye kuva nehukuru hwakasiyana-siyana pazuva kunovhiringidza.

“Hazvisi,” akadaro Gonye.

“Zvakanaka, pamwe hausati wazviwana zvakadaro,” akadaro Alice; “Asi kana uchinge washanduka kuita chrysalis—iwe rimwe zuva, iwe unoziva—uye pashure paizvozvo wova shavishavi, ndinofanira kufunga kuti uchainzwa sechinhu chinonyangadza, handizvo here?”

"Kwete zvishoma," akadaro Gonye.

“Eya, zvichida kunzwa kwako kungave kwakasiyana,” akadaro Alice; "Chandinoziva ndechekuti, zvinonzwa kunge zvakapusa kwandiri ."

“Iwe!” Akadaro Mhungu achizvidza. "Ndiwe ani? "

Izvo zvakavadzosera zvakare pakutanga kwehurukuro. Alice akanzwa kushatirwa nekutaura kupfupi kweCaterpillar, ndokuswedera ndokuti, neshungu, "Ndinofunga, unofanira kundiudza kuti ndiwe ani, kutanga."

"Sei?" Akadaro Mhungu.

Apa paiva nemumwe mubvunzo wainetsa; uye sezvo Alice aisagona kufunga nezvechimwe chikonzero chakanaka, uye sezvo Gonye airatidzika kuva muchimiro chisingafadzi zvikuru chendangariro, akafuratira.

"Dzoka!" Gonye rakadaidzira richimutevera. “Ndine chimwe chinhu chinokosha chandinofanira kutaura!”

Izvi zvainzwika sekuvimbisa, zvirokwazvo: Alice akatendeuka ndokudzoka zvakare.

“Tsautsa hasha,” akadaro Gonye.

"Ndizvo chete here?" Akadaro Alice achimedza hasha zvake.

“Kwete,” akadaro Mhungu.

Alice akafunga kuti zvimwe angomirira, sezvo akanga asina chimwe chinhu chokuita, uye zvichida pashure pazvose zvaizomuudza chimwe chinhu chakakodzera kunzwa. Kwemamwe maminetsi yakazvitutumadza isina kutaura, asi pakupedzisira yakapetunura maoko ayo, ndokubudisa chirauro mumuromo mayo zvakare, ndokuti, “Saka unofunga kuti wachinjwa here?”

“Ndiri kutya changamire,” akadaro Alice; “Handiyeuki zvinhu sezvandaiita—uye handichengete saizi yakafanana kwemaminitsi gumi pamwe chete!”

"Hauchayeuki zvinhu zvipi ?" Akadaro Mhungu.

“Zvakanaka, ndakaedza kuti “Ko kanyuchi kanoita basa sei,” asi zvakabva zvasiyana!” Akapindura Alice neinzwi raive neshungu.

“Dzokorora kuti, ‘ Makura baba William ,’” akadaro Gonye.

Alice akapeta maoko ake, ndokutanga:-

“Makura baba William,” mujaya uya akadaro.
Bvudzi rako rachena kwazvo;
Uye zvakadaro unoramba wakamira pamusoro wako-
Unofunga kuti pazera rako ndizvo here?”

"Ndichiri mudiki," baba William vakapindura mwanakomana wavo,
“Ndaitya kuti zvingakuvadza uropi;
Asi, zvino sezvo ndine chokwadi chekuti handina,
Sei, ndinozviita kakawanda. "

“Makwegura,” akadaro wechiduku, “sezvandambotaura,
Uye vakura zvisina kujairika mafuta;
Zvakadaro wakatendeudzira kumashure-somersault mukati pamusuwo-
Namata, chikonzero chei ichocho?"

"Muhudiki hwangu," akadaro muchenjeri, achizunza rovambira yake yegrey,
“Ndakachengeta mitezo yangu yose yakapfava zvikuru
Nekushandiswa kwechizoro ichi—shereni rimwe chete rebhokisi—
Nditenderei kuti ndikutengeserei vaviri?"

“Makura,” yakadaro pwere, “uye shaya dzenyu hadzina kusimba
Kune chero chinhu chakaoma kupfuura suet;
Asi wapedza hanzi, nemapfupa nemuromo,
Namata, wakazvigona sei?"

“Pauduku hwangu,” vakadaro baba vake, “ndakaenda kumurayiro;
Ndakakakavadzana nomukadzi wangu;
Uye simba remhasuru, iro rakapa kushaya yangu,
Yakagara kwehupenyu hwangu hwese. "

“Makwegura,” yakadaro pwere yacho, “munhu haangambofungi zvakadaro
nekuti ziso rako rakanga rakasimba sakare;
Kunyange zvakadaro wakaenzanisa nzou pamucheto wemhino dzako—
Chii chakuita kuti uve wakachenjera kudaro?”

"Ndapindura mibvunzo mitatu, uye zvakwana,"
Vakadaro baba vake; “usazvipe moyo!
Unofunga kuti ndinogona kuteerera zuva rose kuzvinhu zvakadaro here?
Ibva, kana ndikakukandira pasi masitepisi!

“Hazvina kutaurwa zvakanaka,” akadaro Mhungu.

“Hazvina kunaka , ndinotya,” akadaro Alice, achitya; "mamwe mazwi akachinjwa."

"Hazvina kunaka kubva kwekutanga kusvika kumagumo," akadaro Caterpillar achidaro, pakaita runyararo kwemamwe maminetsi.

Mhungu ndiye akatanga kutaura.

"Unoda kuva saizi ipi?" yakabvunza.

“Aa, handina kunyanya kukura,” Alice akapindura nokuchimbidza; "Mumwe chete asingadi kuchinja kazhinji, munoziva."

“ Handizivi ,” akadaro Gonye.

Alice haana chaakataura: akanga asati ambopokana zvakadaro muupenyu hwake, uye akanzwa kuti akanga ava kushatirwa.

"Mava kugutsikana manje?" Akadaro Mhungu.

“Zvakanaka, ndinoda kuva mukuru zvishoma , changamire, kana musinganetseki,” akadaro Alice: “masendimita matatu kureba kwakashata kuva.

“Kureba kwakanaka chaizvo! akadaro Mboro nehasha, achizvisimudza payaitaura (yakanga yakareba masendimita matatu).

“Asi handina kuzvijaira!” akateterera murombo Alice nenzwi rakashata. Uye akafunga nezvake, “Ndinoshuva kuti dai zvisikwa zvisina kugumbuka zviri nyore kudaro!”

“Uchazvijaira nekufamba kwenguva,” akadaro Gonye; uye yakaisa chiredzo mumuromo mayo ikatanga kusvutazve.

Apa Alice akamirira nemoyo murefu kusvika yasarudza kutaura zvakare. Muminiti imwe kana maviri Mhungu yakaburitsa chirauro mumukanwa mayo ichibva yazamura kamwe kana kaviri, ndokuzvizunza. Ipapo yakaburuka ichibva pahowa, ndokukambaira ichienda muuswa, ichingotaura sezvayaienda, “Rumwe rutivi ruchaita kuti ukure, uye rumwe rutivi ruchaita kuti iwe ukure mupfupi.”

“Rimwe divi rechii? Rimwe divi rechii? ” akafunga Alice mumoyo.

“Zvehowa,” akadaro Gonye, sokunge abvunza achidaidzira; uye mune imwe nguva yakanga isingachaoneki.

Alice akamboramba akatarisa howa achifungisisa kweminiti, achiedza kuona kuti mativi maviri acho ndeapi; uye sezvo zvaive zvakatenderedza, akaona uyu mubvunzo wakaoma kwazvo. Zvisinei, pakupedzisira akatambanudza maoko ake achiitenderedza kusvika pavaizoenda, uye akatyoka chikamu chemucheto neruoko rumwe norumwe.

"Zvino chii?" akazvitaura ega, ndokukwenya kabhiti kekurudyi kuti aedze zvaiitika: nguva inotevera akanzwa kurohwa zvine chisimba pasi pechirebvu chake: rakanga rarova tsoka yake!

Akanga achitya zvikuru neiyi chinjo yakamwe kamwe, asi akanzwa kuti pakanga pasina nguva yokurasika, sezvo akanga achiderera nokukurumidza; saka akabva angotanga kushanda kuti adye imwe yacho. Chirebvu chake chainge chanyatsodzvanyirirwa patsoka yake, zvekuti paive pasisina pekushama muromo; asi akazozviita, uye akakwanisa kumedza chimedu cheruboshwe.

“Huya, musoro wangu wasununguka pakupedzisira!” akadaro Alice nenzwi rekunakirwa, iro rakachinja kuita alarm mune imwe nguva, paakaona kuti mapendekete ake hapana kwaaizowanikwa: chaaingoona, paakatarisa pasi, yaive mitsipa yakakura kwazvo, yaiita kunge dzinde kubva mugungwa remashizha akasvibira aive pazasi pake.

"Zvinhu zvakasvibira zvese izvozvo zvingave chii?" Akadaro Alice. Uye mapendekete angu asvika kupi? Akanga achivafambisa-fambisa sezvaaitaura, asi hapana chakabuda chaitevera, kunze kwekudedera pakati pemashizha ari kure akasvibira.

Sezvo pairatidza kuti pakanga pasina mukana wokusimudza maoko ake mumusoro, akaedza kudzika musoro wake kwavari, uye akafara kuona kuti mutsipa wake waizokotama nyore nyore munzira ipi neipi, senyoka. Akange achangobudirira kuipinza mugomba rakanaka, uye akanga ava kuda kunyura mukati memashizha, ayo aakaona kuti akanga asina chinhu kunze kwemisoro yemiti yaakanga achidzungaira pasi payo, apo mutsindo wakapinza wakaita kuti adzoke shure nokukurumidza: hangaiwa huru yakanga yabhururuka ichienda kuchiso chake, ichiirova nesimba nemapapiro ayo.

“Nyoka!” akadanidzira Njiva.

“ Handisi nyoka!” Akadaro Alice nehasha. “Ndiregei!

“Nyoka, ndinoti zvakare!” akadzokorora Njiva, asi nenzwi rakadzikama, uye akawedzera nerudzi rwekuchema, "Ndaedza nzira dzose, uye hapana chinoratidzika kuva chinoenderana navo!"

“Handitombozivi zvauri kutaura,” akadaro Alice.

“Ndakaedza midzi yemiti, uye ndakaedza mahombekombe, uye ndakaedza ruzhowa,” akadaro Njiva, asina kumutarisira; “Asi nyoka idzo hadzina kudzifadza!”

Alice akawedzera kushamisika, asi akafunga kuti hapana zvazvaibatsira kutaura kusvika Njiva apedza.

“Sokunge ranga risiri dambudziko rekuchechenya mazai,” akadaro Njiva; “Asi ndinofanira kungwarira nyoka siku nesikati!” Chokwadi, vhiki nhatu idzi handina kumbobatwa nehope!

“Ndine urombo zvikuru kuti wagumbuka,” akadaro Alice, uyo akanga ava kutanga kuona zvazvinoreva.

"Uye sezvandakanga ndatora muti wakakwirira zvikurusa mumuti," akaenderera mberi Njiva, achikwidza inzwi rake kuti adedzere, "uye sezvandaifunga kuti ndinofanira kuvasununguka pakupedzisira, vanofanira kuuya vachigwedeka kubva kudenga! Ugh, Nyoka!"

“Asi handisi nyoka, ndinokuudzai! Akadaro Alice. “Ndiri—ndiri—”

"Zvakanaka! Iwe uri chii ?" Akadaro Njiva. "Ndiri kuona kuti uri kuyedza kugadzira chimwe chinhu!"

“Ini—ndiri musikana mudiki,” akadaro Alice, zvine chokwadi, sezvaairangarira chinjo dzakawanda dzaakanga aita zuva iroro.

“Inyaya ingangove yechokwadi!” Akadaro Njiva neinzwi rairatidza kushora kukuru. “Ndakaona vasikana vaduku vakawanda chaizvo munguva yangu, asi hapana kana mumwe chete ane mitsipa yakadaro!Aiwa, aiwa!

“ Ndaravira mazai, chokwadi,” akadaro Alice, aiva mwana aitaura chokwadi; "Asi vasikana vadiki vanodya mazai zvakanyanya sezvinoita nyoka, munoziva."

“Handizvitendi,” akadaro Njiva; “Asi kana vakadaro, sei vari nyoka, ndizvo chete zvandinogona kutaura.”

Iri rakanga riri zano idzva kuna Alice, zvekuti akambonyarara kweminiti kana maviri, izvo zvakapa Njiva mukana wekuwedzera, "Uri kutsvaga mazai, ndinozviziva zvakakwana ; uye zvine basa rei kwandiri kuti uri musikana mudiki kana nyoka?"

"Zvakakosha kwandiri ," akadaro Alice nekuchimbidza; "Asi ini handisi kutsvaga mazai, sezvazvinoitika; uye kana ndanga ndiri, handifanire kuda ako : ini handiadi iwo mbishi."

"Zvakanaka, ibva!" Yakadaro Njiva nenzwi rakanyura, ichibva yagarazve mudendere rayo. Alice akagwadama pakati pemiti sezvaaigona, nokuti mutsipa wake wairamba uchipinzwa pakati pematavi, uye nguva nenguva aifanira kumira ousunungura. Mushure mechinguva akarangarira kuti akanga achiri akabata zvidimbu zvehowa mumaoko ake, uye akatanga kushanda zvakanyatsonaka, achitanga kubvuta pane imwe uye pane imwe, uye achikura dzimwe nguva kureba uye dzimwe nguva kupfupika, kusvikira akwanisa kuzvidzikisa kusvika paurefu hwake hwemazuva ose.

Yakanga iri nguva refu kubvira iye akanga ave chero chinhu chiri pedyo nehukuru chaihwo, zvokuti zvakanzwa zvinoshamisa pakutanga; asi akazozvijaira mumaminetsi mashoma, akatanga kutaura ega semazuva ese. “Uya, hafu yerongedzero yangu yaitwa zvino! Chinjo dzose idzi dzinokatyamadza sei! Achiri kutaura kudaro, pakarepo akangoerekana asvika panzvimbo yakashama, yaiva neimba duku yakanga yakareba makubhiti mana. “Ani naani anogara ikoko,” akafunga kudaro Alice, “hazvizombofa zvakazoita kuuya pavari saizi iyi : sei, ndinofanira kuvavhundutsa kubva munjere dzavo!” Saka akatanga kukwenya kuruoko rwerudyi zvakare, uye haana kuda kuswedera pedyo nemba kusvika adzika kusvika pamainji mapfumbamwe.
