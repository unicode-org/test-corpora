CHITSAUKO VIII.
Iyo Queen's Croquet-Ground

Muti mukuru werose wakamira padyo nemusuwo webindu: marozi aikura pairi aive machena, asi pakanga paine varimi vatatu pairi, vakabatikana vachiapenda matsvuku. Alice akafunga ichi chinhu chaida kuziva, uye akaswedera pedyo kuti avatarise, uye achingosvika kwavari akanzwa mumwe wavo achiti, “Onai zvino, Zvishanu!

“Handina kukwanisa kuzvibata,” akadaro Five, nenzwi rakanyorova; "Vanomwe vakamhanya gokora rangu."

Zvino vanomwe vakatarira kumusoro vakati, Ndizvozvo, Vashanu, gara uchipa vamwe mhosva.

“ Zviri nani kusataura!” Akadaro Five. "Ndakanzwa Mambokadzi vachiti nezuro chete waifanira kugurwa musoro!"

“Zvechii?” Akadaro uya akanga atanga kutaura.

"Hazvinei newe , Vaviri!" akadaro Manomwe.

"Hongu, ibasa rake !" akadaro Five, "uye ndichamuudza - yaive yekuunza mubiki midzi yetulipi pachinzvimbo chehanyanisi."

Zvinomwe zvakakandira pasi bhurashi rake, uye akange achangotanga "Zvakanaka, pazvinhu zvese zvisina kururama-" ziso rake parakangoerekana rawira pana Alice, paakanga akamira achivatarisa, uye akazvitarisa pakarepo: vamwe vakatarisawo, uye vese vakakotama.

"Ungandiudza here," akadaro Alice, achitya zvishoma, "sei uchipenda marozi aya?"

Vashanu naManomwe havana chavakataura asi vakatarisa Vaviri. Vaviri vakatanga nezwi rakaderera, "Sei chokwadi chiri, munoona, Miss, uyu pano unofanirwa kunge wanga uri muti mutsvuku , uye isu takaisa muchena nekukanganisa; uye kana Mambokadzi aizozviziva, tose taifanirwa kudimburwa misoro yedu, munoziva. Saka munoona, Miss, tiri kuita zvatinokwanisa, asati auya, ku-" Panguva iyi akadana Vashanu, uyo aive mhiri kwebindu Mambokadzi, neshungu! uye varimi vatatu vakawira pasi nezviso zvavo. Pakaita ruzha rwetsoka dzakawanda, Alice akaringa ringa achida kuona Mambokadzi.

Kutanga kwakauya varwi gumi vakatakura tsvimbo; zvose zvakanga zvakafanana navarimi vatatu, vakareba vakaenzana, vakati sandarara, maoko avo netsoka dzavo pamakona; ava vakanga vakashongedzwa muviri wose nemadhaimondi, uye vaifamba vari vaviri-vaviri, sezvaiita varwi. Shure kwaizvozvo vana vamambo vakasvika; vaive gumi vavo, uye vadiki vadiki vakauya vachisvetuka nemufaro vakabatana maoko, vari vaviri: vese vaive vakashongedzwa nemoyo. Tevere kwakauya vaenzi, kunyanya Madzimambo neQueens, uye pakati pavo Alice akaziva Tsuro Tsuro: yaitaura nekukasira kuvhunduka, ichinyemwerera pane zvese zvaitaurwa, uye yakapfuura pasina kumuona. Ipapo yakatevera Knave yeMoyo, yakatakura korona yaMambo pamutsago wevelvet crimson; uye, pakupedzisira pauyu mudungwe mukuru, kwakauya MAMBO NAMAHOSI WEMWOYO.

Alice aive asina chokwadi chekuti aisafanira kurara pasi nechiso chake sevarimi vatatu vegadheni, asi aisarangarira kuti akambonzwa nezvemutemo wakadaro pamaforamu; “Uyezve, kufora kungabatsireiko,” akafunga kudaro, “kana vanhu vaizovata pasi nezviso zvavo, kuti varege kuzviona? Naizvozvo akamira paakanga ari, akamirira.

Mudungwe pawakasvika pakatarisana naAlice, vese vakamira vakamutarisa, Mambokadzi ndokuti nehasha "Ndiani uyu?" Akazvitaura zvake Banga reMoyo uyo akangotsikitsira ndokunyemwerera achipindura.

“Idiot!” Vakadaro Vahosi vachikwenya musoro vachiratidza kupererwa; uye, achitendeukira kuna Alice, akaenderera mberi, "Zita rako ndiani, mwana?"

"Ini ndinonzi Alice, saka ndapota zvenyu Changamire," akadaro Alice zvine ruremekedzo; asi akawedzera, kwaari pachake, “Sei, angori pakiti ramakadhi, pamusoro pazvo, handifaniri kuvatya!”

"Uye ndivanaani ava? " Mambokadzi akadaro, achinongedzera kuvarimi vatatu vaive vakarara vakatenderedza muti werozi; nokuti, munoona, pavakanga vakazvambarara nezviso zvavo, uye patani pamisana yavo yakanga yakafanana neyasara yepakiti, haana kukwanisa kuziva kana vakanga vari varimi vebindu, kana varwi, kana vashandi vedare, kana vatatu vevana vake chaivo.

" Ndinofanira kuziva sei?" Akadaro Alice achishamisika nehushingi hwake. "Hazvinei neni ."

Mambokadzi akatsvuka nehasha, uye, mushure mekunge amutarisa kwekanguva sechikara, akazhambatata achiti “Bvisa musoro wake!

“Nonsense!” akadaro Alice, zvine ruzha uye zvine mutsindo, Mambokadzi ndokunyarara.

Mambo akaisa ruoko rwake paruoko rwake, uye nekutya akati, "Funga, mudiwa wangu: anongova mwana!"

Mambokadzi akafuratira nehasha ndokuti kuna Knave "Vashandure!"

VaKnave vakadaro vachinyatsongwarira negumbo rimwe chete.

"Simuka!" Vakadaro Mambokadzi nenzwi rine kakuchemerera, varimi vatatu vakabva vasvetuka, ndokutanga kukotamira Mambo, Mambokadzi, vana vehumambo nevamwe vese.

“Siyana nazvo!” Vakadanidzira Queen. "Unondishungurudza." Zvino, akatendeukira kumuti werose, akaenderera mberi, " Wanga uchiitei pano?"

"Ngazvifadze Changamire," akadaro Vaviri, nenzwi rakazvininipisa, vachipfugama nebvi pavaitaura, "tanga tichiedza-"

" Ndazviwona !" Vakadaro Vahosi vachiona havo maruva. “Kubva nemusoro wavo!” uye mudungwe wakaenderera mberi, vatatu vevarwi vakasara shure kuti vauraye varimi vakashata, avo vakamhanyira kuna Alice kuti vadzivirirwe.

“Hauzogurwe musoro!” Akadaro Alice achibva adziisa muhari hombe yemaruva yainge yakamira pedyo. Mauto matatu akatenderera kweminiti imwe chete kana maviri, achivatsvaga, ndokufamba chinyararire achitevera vamwe.

"Ko musoro wavo wabviswa here?" vakashevedzera vahosi.

“Misoro yavo yaenda, kana zvichifadza Changamire!” varwi vakadanidzira vachipindura.

"Izvo ndizvo!" vakashevedzera vahosi. "Unogona kutamba croquet?"

Mauto akabva anyarara ndokutarisa Alice sezvo mubvunzo uyu waive wakanangana naye.

"Ehe!" akadanidzira Alice.

“Chiuyai!” Mambokadzi akadzvova, Alice akabva apindawo mudungwe achinetsekana kuti chii chaizoitika.

“Iri—izuva rakanaka kwazvo!” rakadaro izwi rekutya padivi pake. Aifamba pedyo naTsuro Muchena, uyo akanga akadongorera kumeso kwake.

"Zvakanaka," akadaro Alice: "- maDuchess aripi?"

“Nyarara! akadaro Tsuro neinzwi riri pasi pasi achikasira. Akatarisa papfudzi rake neshungu paaitaura, ndokuzozvisimudza pamusoro pechigunwe, akaisa muromo wake pedyo nenzeve yake, ndokuzevezera “Ari pasi pemutongo wokuurayiwa.”

“Zvechii?” Akadaro Alice.

“Wati, ‘Zvakaoma sei!’?” Tsuro akabvunza.

“Aiwa, handina,” akadaro Alice: “Handifungi kuti zvine urombo.

“Akarovera nzeve dzaMambokadzi—” Tsuro akatanga. Alice akaridza kamhere kekuseka. “Oo, nyarara!” Tsuro akazevezera nezwi rekutya. "Mambokadzi achakunzwa! Unoona, akanonoka kuuya, uye Mambokadzi akati-"

“Enda kunzvimbo dzako!” Vahosi vakashevedzera nezwi remabhanan'ana, vanhu vakatanga kumhanya kose-kose, vachiumburudzana; zvisinei, vakagadzikana muminiti kana maviri, uye mutambo wakatanga. Alice akafunga kuti akanga asati amboona makorokoza akadaro muupenyu hwake; zvose zvakanga zviri mihoronga nemiforo; mabhora akanga ari hedgehogs mhenyu, mallets live flamingo, uye masoja aifanira kuzvipa mbiri uye kumira nemaoko netsoka, kugadzira maberere.

Dambudziko guru rakawanikwa naAlice pakutanga raive mukudzora flamingo yake: akabudirira kuita kuti muviri wake uende, zvakanaka zvakakwana, pasi peruoko rwake, makumbo akarembera pasi, asi kazhinji, sekunge anyatsotwasanudza mutsipa wake, uye achizopa hedgehog kurova nemusoro wayo, yaizvimonyorora yotarisa mudenga kumeso kwake, nekuvhunduka kwaakaita akatadza kuseka: Kudzika, uye kwave kuzotanga zvakare, zvainyanya kutsamwisa kuona kuti hedgehog yakanga yazvisuduruka, uye yakanga iri kuita kukambaira ichienda kure: kunze kweizvi zvose, paiwanzova negoronga kana muforo munzira kwese kwaaida kutumira hedgehog, uye, sezvo mauto akapetwa kaviri aigara achisimuka uye achifamba kuenda kune dzimwe nzvimbo dzepasi, zvechokwadi yakasvika pakuguma kwemutambo, Alice.

Vatambi vose vakatamba kamwechete vasingamirire kuchinjana, vachipopotedzana nguva dzose, uye vachirwira hedgehogs; uye munguva pfupi pfupi, Mambokadzi akange aine hasha dzehasha, akatanga kutsika-tsika, uye achishevedzera "Bvisa nemusoro wake!" kana kuti “Kumubvisa nemusoro!” kamwechete paminiti.

Alice akatanga kunzwa kusagadzikana: kutaura chokwadi, anga asati amboita nharo naMambokadzi, asi aiziva kuti zvaigona kuitika chero miniti, "uyezve," akafunga, "chii chingave kwandiri? Vanofarira kwazvo kudimbura vanhu misoro pano; chishamiso chikuru ndechekuti, pane asara ari mupenyu!"

Anga achitsvaga imwe nzira yekutiza, uye achinetseka kuti angatiza here asina kuoneka, paakaona chitarisiko chekuda kuziva mumhepo: zvakamushamisa zvakanyanya pakutanga, asi, mushure mekuzviona kweminiti kana maviri, akazviita grin, ndokuti nechemumoyo "IKatsi yeCheshire: ikozvino ndichave nemunhu wekutaura naye."

"Maswera sei?" Akadaro Katsi, pakangobuda muromo waikwana kutaura nawo.

Alice akamirira kusvika maziso aoneka, ndokuzogutsurira musoro. “Hazvina maturo kutaura nayo,” akafunga kudaro, “kusvikira nzeve dzayo dzauya, kana kuti imwe yadzo.” Mune imwe miniti musoro wese wakaoneka, uye ipapo Alice akaisa flamingo yake pasi, ndokutanga nhoroondo yemutambo, achinzwa kufara kwazvo kuti aive nemunhu wekuteerera kwaari. Mboro yaiita seyaifunga kuti yainge yakwana zvino yave kuoneka, uye hapana chimwe chayo chakaonekwa.

"Handifunge kuti vanotamba zvachose," Alice akatanga, nenzwi rekunyunyuta, "uye vese vanopopotedzana zvakanyanya zvekuti munhu haanzwe achitaura - uye vanoita kunge vasina chero mitemo kunyanya; zvirinani, kana iripo, hapana anoiteerera - uye hauzive kuti zvinovhiringidza sei zvinhu zvese zviri zvipenyu; semuenzaniso pasi—uye ndingadai ndakakwakutsa hedgehog yaMambokadzi izvozvi, chete iyo yakatiza payakaona yangu ichiuya!”

"Unoda sei Queen?" Akadaro Katsi neinzwi raiva pasi pasi.

"Kwete," akadaro Alice: "akanyanya -" Ipapo akabva aona kuti Mambokadzi aive pedyo kumashure kwake, achiteerera: saka akaenderera mberi, "- pamwe kuhwina, kuti hazvina kukosha pakupedza mutambo."

Vahosi vakanyemwerera ndokupfuura.

"Ndiani wauri kutaura naye?" vakadaro Mambo vachikwidza Alice, ndokutarisa musoro waKatsi neshungu huru.

“Ishamwari yangu—Katsi yeCheshire,” akadaro Alice: “ndibvumirei kuisuma.”

“Handidi chitarisiko chayo zvachose,” akadaro Mambo: “zvisinei, ingatsvoda ruoko rwangu kana ichichida.

“Ndinoda kusadaro,” Katsi akadaro.

“Usave vasine hanya,” akadaro Mambo, “uye musanditarira saizvozvo! Akasvika kumashure kwaAlice achitaura.

"Katsi inogona kutarisa mambo," akadaro Alice. “Ndakazviverenga mune rimwe bhuku, asi handiyeuki kuti kupi.”

"Zvakanaka, inofanira kubviswa," akadaro Mambo akanyatsosarudza, uye akadana Mambokadzi, uyo akanga achipfuura panguva iyoyo, "Mudiwa wangu! Ndinoshuva kuti dai wabvisa katsi iyi!"

Mambokadzi aingove nenzira imwe chete yekugadzirisa matambudziko ese, makuru kana madiki. "Bvisa musoro wake!" akadaro asina kana kumbocheuka.

“Ndichatora muurayi pachangu,” akadaro Mambo achida, uye akabva amhanya.

Alice akafunga kuti dai adzokera hake, anoona mafambiro aiita mutambo uyu, akanzwa izwi raMambokadzi nechekure richiridza mhere. Ainge atonzwa mutongo wake wevatatu vevatambi kuti vaurayiwe nekuti vainge vapotsa nguva yavo, uye ainge asingafariri maonerwo aiitwa zvinhu zvachose, sezvo mutambo wacho wainge wavhiringika zvekuti haana kumboziva kuti rakanga riri rake here kana kuti kwete. Saka akaenda kunotsvaga hedgehog yake.

Iyo hedgehog yaive mukurwa neimwe hedgehog, iyo yairatidzika kuna Alice mukana wakanakisa wekurovera mumwe wavo nemumwe: chakangooma ndechekuti flamingo yake yakanga yayambuka kune rimwe divi rebindu, uko Alice aizviona ichiedza nenzira isingaite yekubhururuka ichikwira mumuti.

Panguva yaakanga abata flamingo ndokuidzosa, kurwa kwakanga kwapera, uye mhembwe dzose dzakanga dzisingachaoneki: “asi hazvina basa zvikuru,” akafunga kudaro Alice, “sezvo mazariro ose aenda kudivi rino revhu.” Saka akariisa muhapwa, kuti risazotiza zvakare, ndokudzokera kunombokurukura neshamwari yake.

Paakadzokera kuKatsi yeCheshire, akashamisika kuwana chaunga chikuru chakaungana pairi: paive nekupokana kwaiitika pakati pemuurayi, Mambo, naMambokadzi, avo vese vaitaura kamwechete, vamwe vese vakanyarara, uye vairatidza kusagadzikana.

Panguva yaakaoneka Alice, akakumbirwa nevese vari vatatu kuti vagadzirise mubvunzo, uye vakadzokorora nharo dzavo kwaari, zvisinei, sezvavaitaura vese kamwechete, zvakamuomera chaizvo kuti anyatsonzwisisa zvavaitaura.

Nharo yemuurayi yakanga iri, yokuti waisagona kucheka musoro kunze kwokunge pave nomutumbi wokuugura kubva: kuti akanga asati ambofanira kuita chinhu chakadaro, uye akanga asiri kuzotanga panguva yake youpenyu.

Nharo yaMambo yaiva yokuti, chese chine musoro chaigona kugurwa musoro, uye kuti waisataura zvisina maturo.

Nharo yaMambokadzi yaive yekuti, kana chimwe chinhu chisina kuitwa nezvazvo pasina nguva aizoita kuti munhu wese aurawe, kumativi ese. (Kwaiva kutaura kwekupedzisira uku kwakanga kwaita kuti bato rose riite serakaoma uye richinetseka.)

Alice hapana chimwe chaaigona kutaura kunze kwe "Ndeyeduchess: zviri nani umubvunze nezvazvo."

"Ari mutirongo," Mambokadzi akati kumuurayi, "mutore pano." Muurayi akaenda somuseve.

Musoro weKatsi wakatanga kupera nguva yaakanga aenda, uye, panguva yaakanga adzoka naDuchess, yakanga yanyangarika zvachose; saka Mambo nemuurayi vakamhanya vachikwidza nekudzika vachichitsvaga, vamwe vese vebato vakadzokera kumutambo.
