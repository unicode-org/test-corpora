CHITSAUKO VII.
A Mad Tea-Party

Paive nekatafura kaive kakagadzikwa pasi pemuti waive kumberi kwemba, naMhakure Tsuro naHatter vachinwa tii: Dormouse raive rakagara pakati pavo, rakakotsira, vamwe vaviri vairishandisa secushion, vakaisa magokora avo pamusoro paro, uye vachitaura pamusoro. "Handina kugadzikana kuDormouse," akafunga Alice; "chete, sezvo yakarara, ndinofunga haina basa."

Tafura yacho yaiva hombe, asi vatatu vacho vakanga vakatsvikinyidzana pane imwe kona yayo: “Hapana nzvimbo! vakadeedzera pavakaona Alice achiuya. “Pane nzvimbo yakawanda !” akadaro Alice akatsamwa, ndokugara pachigaro chihombe chemaoko nechekumucheto kwetafura.

"Inwa waini," Mhakure Tsuro akadaro nenzwi rinokurudzira.

Alice akatarisa tafura yese, asi pakanga pasina chinhu kunze kwetii. “Handioni waini ipi neipi,” akadaro.

"Hakuna," akadaro Tsuro yeKurume.

“Saka kwaisava kusiri pamutemo kuti uipe,” akadaro Alice nehasha.

“Kwaive kusiri hunhu kuti ugare pasi usina kukokwa,” akadaro Tsuro yaKurume.

“Handina kuziva kuti itafura yako ,” akadaro Alice; "yakarongedzerwa vazhinji vanopfuura vatatu."

“Bvudzi rako rinoda kugerwa,” akadaro Muzorwa. Akange ave nenguva akatarisa Alice neshungu huru, uye aka kaive kekutanga kutaura.

"Unofanira kudzidza kusataura zvaunotaura," Alice akadaro nehasha; "Hazvina kunaka."

Muvengwa akavhura maziso ake zvakanyanya achinzwa izvi; asi zvose zvaakataura ndezvokuti, "Seiko gunguo rakaita sedhesiki rekunyorera?"

"Huya, tichava nekunakidzwa izvozvi!" akafunga Alice. “Ndinofara kuti vatanga kubvunza zvirahwe.—Ndinotenda kuti ndinogona kufungidzira izvozvo,” akawedzera zvinonzwika.

"Unoreva here kuti unofunga kuti unogona kuwana mhinduro kwazviri?" akadaro Mhiripiri Tsuro.

"Ndizvo chaizvo," akadaro Alice.

“Ipapo unofanira kutaura zvauri kureva,” Tsuro yaKurume yakaenderera mberi.

"Ndinodaro," Alice akapindura nekukurumidza; "zvirinani - ndinoreva zvandinotaura - ndizvo zvimwe chete, munoziva."

"Hazvina kufanana zvachose!" Akadaro Muzorwa. “Unogona kungotaura kuti ‘Ndinoona zvandinodya’ zvakafanana nokuti ‘Ndinodya zvandinoona’!”

“Ungangotaurawo,” yakawedzera Tsuro, “kuti ‘Ndinoda zvandinowana’ chinhu chimwe chete chokuti ‘ndinowana zvandinoda’!”

“Ungangotaurawo,” akawedzera kudaro Dormouse, uyo aiita seaitaura ari kuhope, “kuti ‘Ndinofema kana ndarara’ zvakafanana nokuti ‘Ndinorara pandinofema’!

" Zvakafanana newe," akadaro Hatter, uye pano nhaurirano yadonha, uye bato rakagara rinyerere kweminiti, Alice achifunga nezvese zvaaigona kurangarira nezvemakunguo nekunyora-madhesiki, izvo zvaive zvisina zvakawanda.

Mukwaku ndiye akatanga kupaza runyararo. "Izuva ripi remwedzi?" akadaro achicheukira kuna Alice: akange aburitsa wachi yake muhomwe, ndokuitarisa asina kugadzikana, achiizunza zunza nguva nenguva, akaibata panzeve.

Alice akafunga zvishoma, ndokubva ati "Wechina."

"Mazuva maviri akashata!" akatura befu Muzorwa. “Ndakuudza kuti bota haringaenderani nemabasa acho!” akawedzera akatarisa hasha dzaMhiripiri.

“Rakanga riri bota rakanakisa ,” Mhiripiri Hare akapfava akapindura.

“Hongu, asi zvimwe zvimedu zvinofanira kunge zvakapindawo,” Hatter akagunun’una achiti: “waisafanira kuzviisa mukati nebanga rechingwa.”

The March Hare akatora wachi ndokuitarisa akasuruvara: ipapo akainyika mumukombe wake wetii, ndokuitarisa zvakare: asi hapana chaaigona kufunga chiri nani chekutaura kupfuura kutaura kwake kwokutanga, “Yakanga iri bhotoro rakanakisisa , munoziva.”

Alice aive akatarisa pabendekete rake achida kuziva. “Iwachi inosetsa!” akadaro. “Inotaura zuva remwedzi, uye haitauri kuti inguvai!”

"Nei uchifanira kudaro?" Akazevezera Mugudza. "Wachi yako inokuudza kuti igore ripi?"

"Kwete," Alice akapindura nekukasira: "asi imhaka yekuti rinogara gore rimwe chete kwenguva yakareba pamwe chete."

"Ndezvipi zvangu ini ," akadaro Muzondi.

Alice akanzwa kuvhiringika zvakanyanya, zvakataurwa naMuvengi zvairatidza kuti zvakanga zvisina zvazvinoreva, asi chaive chirungu. “Handisi kunyatsokunzwisisa,” akadaro, noruremekedzo sezvaaigona.

"Dormouse akarara zvakare," akadaro Muvengi, uye akadira tii inopisa pamhuno.

Dormouse akadzungudza musoro nekushaya moyo murefu, ndokuti, asina kuvhura maziso, "Hongu, hongu; ndizvo zvandaizotaura ini."

"Watoziva chirahwe?" Akadaro Hatter achicheukira kuna Alice zvakare.

"Aiwa, ndazvisiya," Alice akapindura, "mhinduro ndeyei?"

“Handina kana zano diki zvaro,” akadaro Muzondi.

"Neniwo," akadaro Tsuro yeKurume.

Alice akatura befu. “Ndinofunga kuti mungaita chimwe chinhu chiri nani nenguva yacho,” akadaro, “pane kutambisa mukubvunza zvirahwe zvisina mhinduro.”

“Kudai waiziva Nguva sezvandinoziva,” akadaro Hatter, “ waisazotaura nezvokuipambadza .

“Handizivi zvauri kureva,” akadaro Alice.

“Chokwadi haudaro!” Akadaro Mhofu achikwenya musoro. "Ndingatoti hauna kana kumbotaura neNguva!"

“Zvichida kwete,” Alice akapindura nokungwarira, kuti: “asi ndinoziva kuti ndinofanira kukunda nguva apo ndinodzidza nziyo.”

"Ah, ndizvo zvazvinoreva," akadaro Mukwaku. Iye zvino, kana ukaramba uchiwirirana naye, angaita chero chinhu chipi zvacho chaunoda newachi.” Somuenzaniso, ngatiti dzakanga dziri nguva dza9:00 mangwanani, nguva chaiyo yokuti utange zvidzidzo: waitofanira kungozevezera kuNguva, wobva watenderera wachi mukubwaira!

("Ndinoshuva kuti dai zvadaro," Mhakure Tsuro akazvitaurira zvake nekazevezeve.)

"Zvingave zvakanaka," akadaro Alice achifunga: "asi ipapo-handifanire kunge ndine nzara nazvo, munoziva."

"Kwete pakutanga, pamwe," akadaro Hatter: "asi unogona kuichengeta kusvika pakati nepakati penguva yawaida."

"Ndiwo matongero aunoita here?" Alice akabvunza.

Mugurwa akadzungudza musoro achichema. “Kwete ini!” akapindura. "Takanetsana Kurume wapfuura - asati apenga , munoziva-" (achinongedzera nechipunu chake chetii paKurume Hare,) "-paive pakonzati huru yakapihwa naMambokadzi weMoyo, uye ndaifanira kuimba.

'Twinkle, twinkle, kamuremwaremwa!
Ndinoshamisika sei kuti uri papi!'

Unoziva rwiyo rwacho, pamwe?"

“Ndanzwa zvakadai,” akadaro Alice.

"Zvinoenderera, unoziva," Hatter akaenderera mberi, "nenzira iyi: -

'Kumusoro pamusoro penyika iwe unobhururuka,
Setiyi-tireyi mudenga.
Kupenya, kupenya—’”

Apa Dormouse yakazvizunza, ndokutanga kuimba muhope dzayo “ Twinkle, twinkle, twinkle, twinkle —” ndokuenderera mberi zvekuti vakatozoitswinya kuti imire.

“Zvakanaka, ndakanga ndisati ndapedza ndima yokutanga,” akadaro Hatter, “apo Mambokadzi akasvetuka ndokudanidzira achiti, ‘Ari kuuraya nguva!

“Kutyisa kwakaipa sei!” akadaro Alice.

“Uye kubvira ipapo,” Hatter akaenderera mberi nenzwi rokuchema, “haazoiti chinhu chandinokumbira!

Imwe pfungwa yakajeka yakauya mumusoro maAlice. "Ndicho chikonzero nei zvinhu zvakawanda zvetii zvakaiswa pano?" akabvunza.

"Hongu, ndizvozvo," akadaro Hatter achigomera: "inogara iri nguva yetii, uye hatina nguva yekugeza zvinhu pakati penguva."

"Saka unoramba uchitenderera, ndinofunga?" Akadaro Alice.

"Ndizvo chaizvo," akadaro Hatter: "sezvo zvinhu zvazvinojaira."

"Asi chii chinoitika kana iwe ukasvika pakutanga zvakare?" Alice akashinga kubvunza.

"Ngatitii tachinja nyaya," Tsuro yaKurume yakadimburira ichizamura. "Ndaneta nazvo. Ndovhota musikana uyu anotiudza imwe nyaya."

“Ndinotya kuti hapana wandinoziva,” akadaro Alice, achitovhunduka chikumbiro chacho.

"Ipapo Dormouse achadaro!" vose vakachema. "Muka, Dormouse!" Uye vakaidzvanya mativi maviri pakarepo.

Dormouse akavhura maziso zvishoma nezvishoma. “Ndakanga ndisina kurara,” akadaro nenzwi rakashoshoma, risina simba: “Ndakanzwa mashoko ose amakanga muchitaura.

“Tiudze nyaya!” akadaro Mhiripiri Tsuro.

“Hongu, ndapota itai!” akateterera Alice.

"Uye kurumidza nezvazvo," akawedzera Hatter, "kana kuti unenge wakarara zvakare zvisati zvaitika."

“Kare kare kwaiva nehanzvadzi nhatu duku,” Dormouse akatanga nekuchimbidza; “uye mazita avo aiva Elsie, Lacie, naTillie; uye vaigara pasi petsime—”

"Vairarama nei?" akadaro Alice, uyo aigara achifarira zvikuru mibvunzo yekudya nekunwa.

"Vaigara pane treacle," akadaro Dormouse, mushure mekufunga kweminiti kana maviri.

“Vangadai vasina kuita izvozvo, munoziva,” Alice akadaro zvinyoronyoro; "vangadai vakarwara."

“Ndizvo zvavaive,” akadaro Dormouse; "Kurwara kwazvo ."

Alice akaedza kufunga ari oga kuti mararamiro anoshamisa kudaro angave akaita sei, asi zvakamushamisa zvikuru, saka akaenderera mberi achiti: “Asi nei vaigara pasi petsime?”

"Tora imwe tii," Tsuro yaKurume akadaro kuna Alice, nemoyo wese.

“Ini handisati ndave nechinhu,” Alice akapindura nenzwi rokugumbuka, “saka handichagona kutora zvakawanda.

“Unoreva kuti haugoni kutora zvishoma ,” akadaro Hatter: “zviri nyore kwazvo kutora zvinopfuura chinhu.”

"Hapana akabvunza maonero ako ," akadaro Alice.

"Ndiani ari kuita matauriro ake manje?" Hatter akabvunza achikunda.

Alice akashaya kuti oti kudii kune izvi: akabva azvibatsira kunwa tii nechingwa-ne-bhota, ndokutendeukira kuDormouse, ndokudzokorora mubvunzo wake. “Nei vaigara pasi petsime?”

Dormouse zvakare akatora miniti kana maviri kuti afunge nezvazvo, ndokuzoti, "Yanga iri tsime-tsime."

“Hakuna zvakadaro!” Alice akanga atanga nehasha, asi Muguri neTsuru yaKurume vakati “Sh! sh!” uye Dormouse akataura nehasha, "Kana iwe usingakwanise kuve wevanhu, zviri nani upedze nyaya yacho wega."

“Aiwa, enda hako!” Alice akadaro achizvininipisa; "Handichamudimburise zvakare. Ndinomboda kuti panogona kunge paine imwe ."

"Imwe, chokwadi!" Akadaro Dormouse nehasha. Zvisinei, akabvuma kuenderera mberi. “Uye saka vanin’ina vatatu ava—vakanga vachidzidzira kudhirowa, munoziva—”

"Vakadhirowa chii?" Akadaro Alice achitokanganwa vimbiso yake.

"Treacle," akadaro Dormouse, asina kufunga panguva yese iyi.

“Ndinoda kapu yakachena,” akavhiringidza Hatter: “tose ngatifambei panzvimbo imwe chete.”

Akaenderera mberi achitaura, Dormouse ndokumutevera: March Hare akatamira munzvimbo yeDormouse, uye Alice asingadi kutora nzvimbo yaMarch Hare. The Hatter ndiye ega akawana mukana kubva pakuchinja: uye Alice aive akanaka zvakanyanya kupfuura nakare kose, sezvo March Hare akanga achangobva kuvhiringidza mukaka-jug mundiro yake.

Alice akanga asingadi kugumbura Dormouse zvakare, saka akatanga nekuchenjerera achiti: “Asi handisi kunzwisisa.

"Unogona kuchera mvura kubva mutsime remvura," akadaro Hatter; "Saka ndinofanira kufunga kuti unogona kudhirowa kubva mumutinhimira-zvakanaka, benzi?"

"Asi vanga vari mutsime ," Alice akadaro kuna Dormouse, asingasarudze kuona iyi yekupedzisira kutaura.

“Chokwadi vaivepo,” akadaro Dormouse; "- zvakanaka mukati."

Mhinduro iyi yakavhiringa murombo Alice, zvekuti akarega Dormouse ichienderera kwechinguva pasina kuikanganisa.

"Vaidzidzira kudhirowa," Dormouse akaenderera mberi, achikwenya nekukwenya maziso, nekuti akange ave kubatwa nehope; “uye vakadhirowa marudzi ese ezvinhu—zvose zvinotanga neM—”

"Sei uine M?" Akadaro Alice.

"Kuregererei?" akadaro Mhiripiri Tsuro.

Alice akabva anyarara.

The Dormouse yakanga yakavhara maziso panguva iyi, uye yave kuenda kuhope; asi, payakatsikirirwa naHatter, yakamuka zvakare nemhere-mhere zvishoma, ndokuenderera mberi: "-iyo inotanga neM, yakadai semateya-mbeva, nemwedzi, uye ndangariro, uye hukuru - unoziva iwe unoti zvinhu "zvizhinji zvezvizhinji" -wakamboona chinhu chakadai sekudhirowa kwezvizhinji here?

"Chaizvo, ikozvino unondibvunza," akadaro Alice, akavhiringidzika kwazvo, "Handifunge-"

“Saka haufanire kutaura,” akadaro Muzondi.

Ichi chidimbu chehutsvina chaive chinopfuura izvo Alice aigona kutakura: akasimuka mukusemeswa kukuru, ndokuenda; Dormouse akabva abatwa nehope, uye hapana wevamwe akatombocherechedza kuti aenda, kunyangwe akatarisa kumashure kamwe kana kaviri, achitarisira kuti vaizofona vachimutevera: nguva yekupedzisira yaakavaona, vaiyedza kuisa Dormouse mu teapot.

Chero zvazvingava handifi ndakaendako zvakare! Akadaro Alice achinhonga nzira nemuhuni. “Ndiro pati yetii yakapusa yandati ndambopinda muhupenyu hwangu hwese!”

Achingotaura kudaro akabva aona kuti mumwe muti waiva nemusuwo waipinda mauri. “Zvinoda kuziva kwazvo!” akafunga kudaro. "Asi zvese zviri kuda kuziva nhasi. Ndinofunga ndingangopinda ipapo." akapinda.
