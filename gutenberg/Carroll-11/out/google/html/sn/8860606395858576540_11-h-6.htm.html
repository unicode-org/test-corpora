CHITSAUKO VI.
Nguruve uye Mhiripiri

Kweminiti kana maviri akamira akatarisa imba, uye achinetseka kuti oita sei, apo kamwe murume wetsoka ari mumoto akauya achimhanya achibuda muhuni- (akamutora semutyairi wetsoka nekuti akange ari mushishi: dai zvisina kudaro, achitonga nechiso chake chete, angadai akamuti hove) - uye akarova zvine ruzha pamusuwo nemagumbo ake. Yakavhurwa nemumwe murume wetsoka ari livery, ane chiso chakaurungana, nemaziso mahombe kunge datya; uye vese vaifamba netsoka, Alice akaona, vaive nebvudzi rehupfu rakamonereka mumusoro mavo. Akanzwa kuda kuziva kuti chaive chii, akabva anyangira zvishoma kubva muhuni kuti ateerere.

The Fish-Footman akatanga nekuburitsa kubva pasi peruoko rwake tsamba huru, yakada kukura seyake, uye izvi akazvipa kune mumwe, achiti, nenzwi rakadzikama, "Kune maDuchess. Kukokwa kubva kuna Mambokadzi kutamba croquet." Frog-Footman akadzokorora, nenzwi rimwechete rekuremekedza, achingoshandura marongerwo emazwi zvishoma, "Kubva kuna Mambokadzi. Kukokwa kwemaDuchess kutamba croquet."

Ipapo vaviri vakakotama, uye mikumbo yavo yakanamatirana.

Alice akaseka zvake izvi, zvekuti akatomhanya kudzokera muhuni nekutya kuti vaizomunzwa; uye paakazodongorera panze Hove-Footman akanga aenda, uye mumwe wacho akanga akagara pasi pedyo nemusuwo, akatarisa zvakapusa mudenga.

Alice akaenda kumusiwo achitya, ndokugogodza.

“Kugogodza hakuna zvakunobatsira,” akadaro Footman, “uye nokuda kwezvikonzero zviviri.” Chekutanga, nokuti ndiri kudivi rimwe chete remusuo sezvauri, chechipiri, nokuti vari kuita ruzha rwakadaro mukati, hapana angambokunzwa. Uye zvechokwadi maiva neruzha runoshamisa zvikuru rwaiitika mukati—kuungudza nguva dzose nokuhotsira, uye nguva nenguva kubonderana kukuru, sokunge ndiro kana ketero yakanga yaputswa kuita zvidimbu.

"Ndokumbirawo," akadaro Alice, "ndingapinda sei?"

“Panogona kunge paine pfungwa mukugogodza kwako,” Mufambi akaenderera mberi asina kumubatsira, “dai taiva nesuo pakati pedu. Somuenzaniso, kudai waiva mukati , waigona kugogodza, uye ndaigona kukubudisa, unoziva.” Aive akatarisa mudenga nguva yese yaaitaura, uye Alice uyu aifunga zvisina kunaka. “Asi pamwe haakwanisi kuzvibatsira,” akadaro mumwoyo make; “Maziso ake anenge ari pamusoro pemusoro wake, asi chero zvodini angapindura mibvunzo.—Ndopinda sei? akadzokorora, nenzwi guru.

"Ndichagara pano," akadaro Footman, "kusvikira mangwana-"

Panguva iyi gonhi remba rakavhurika, ndiro huru yakabuda ichisvetuka-svetuka, yakananga mumusoro maFootman: yakangodzvokora mhino yake, ndokutyora-tyora mumwe wemuti waiva kumashure kwake.

"- kana zuva rinotevera, pamwe," Mutsika akaenderera nenzwi rimwechete, sekunge hapana chakaitika.

"Ndopinda sei?" akabvunza zvakare Alice neinzwi riripamusoro.

" Uri kutopinda zvachose here?" Akadaro Mutsoka. "Ndiwo mubvunzo wekutanga, unoziva."

Zvaive, pasina kupokana: Alice chete aisada kuudzwa kudaro. “Zvinotyisa chaizvo,” akadaro mumwoyo make, “mapopotedzano anoita zvisikwa zvose. Zvakakwana kupengesa munhu!”

The Footman aiita seaifunga uyu mukana wakanaka wekudzokorora kutaura kwake, nekusiyana. “Ndichagara pano,” akadaro, “ndogarapo nokurega, kwemazuva namazuva.”

"Asi ndoita sei?" Akadaro Alice.

"Chero chaunoda," akadaro Munyai, ndokutanga kuridza muridzo.

"Aa, hazvibatsire kutaura naye," akadaro Alice achipererwa: "akapusa chaizvo!" Ndokuvhura gonhi ndokupinda.

Mukova wakananga mukicheni huru, iyo yakanga izere neutsi kubva kune rumwe rutivi kusvika kune rumwe rutivi: maDuchess akanga akagara pachitsiko chemakumbo matatu pakati, achiyamwisa mwana; mubiki akanga akazembera pamoto achikuchidzira hari hombe rairatidza kuti rakanga rizere muto.

“Chokwadi mune mhiripiri yakawandisa mumuto iwoyo!” Akadaro Alice nechemumoyo achingohwihwidza.

Chokwadi mudenga makanga makawanda. Kunyange maDuchess aihotsira apo neapo; uye kana ari mwana aihwihwidza nekuchema zvakachinjana pasina kumbomira. Mumba yekubikira zvinhu zvaisahwihwidza, mubiki nekatsi hombe yaigara pachoto ichingosekerera kubva kunzeve ichienda kunzeve.

"Ndokumbirawo mundiudze," akadaro Alice, achitya zvishoma, nekuti akange asina chokwadi chekuti yaive tsika yakanaka here kuti atange ataura, "nei katsi yako ichisekerera kudaro?"

"Ikatsi yeCheshire," akadaro maDuchess, "ndosaka. Nguruve!"

Akataura izwi rekupedzisira nechisimba chakayerekana zvekuti Alice akasvetuka; asi akaonazve pane imwe nguva kuti zvakanga zviri kumwana, asi kwete kwaari, akatsunga moyo, akaenderera mberi achiti:

"Ini ndaisaziva kuti katsi dzeCheshire dzinogara dzichigaya; chokwadi, ini handina kuziva kuti katsi dzinogona kuseka."

"Vese vanogona," akadaro maDuchess; "Uye vazhinji vavo vanozviita."

"Hapana chandinoziva," akadaro Alice zvine ruremekedzo, achinzwa kufara kuti aita hurukuro.

"Iwe hauzive zvakawanda," akadaro maDuchess; "uye ichokwadi."

Alice haana kana kumbofarira matauriro ekutaura uku, uye akafunga kuti zvaizovawo kusuma imwe nyaya yekukurukurirana. Paakanga achiedza kugadzirisa pane imwe, mubiki akatora bhodhoro remuto kubva pamoto, uye pakarepo akatanga kushanda achikanda zvinhu zvose mukati mekusvika kwake kuDuchess uye mwana-masimbi emoto akauya pakutanga; ipapo kwaitevera kugeza soso, ndiro, nendiro. The Duchess haana kuvacherechedza kunyange pavakamurova; uye mwana akanga atochema zvekuti zvaisaita kuti ataure kuti kurohwa kwakumurwadza here kana kuti kwete.

“Oo, ndapota rangarira zvauri kuita!” akadanidzira Alice, achisvetuka-svetuka nekurwadziwa nekutya. “O, heino mhino yake inokosha !” sezvo chirongo chihombe chaibhururuka chichiswedera pedyo nacho, chikapotsa chaenda nacho.

"Dai munhu wese aikoshesa bhizinesi rake," akadaro maDuchess mukupopotera, "nyika yaizotenderedza chibvumirano nekukurumidza kupfuura zvainoita."

“Izvi zvingasava zvakanakira,” akadaro Alice, uyo akanzwa kufara zvikuru kuwana mukana wokuratidza zvishoma zivo yake. “Chimbofunga kuti raizoshanda sei masikati nousiku!

"Kutaura nezvematemo," akadaro maDuchess, "gura musoro wake!"

Alice akatarisa mubiki achishushikana kuti aone kana aida kubvuma; asi mubiki akanga ari mushishi kukuchidzira muto, uye aiita seasingateereri, saka akaenderera mberi achiti: “ Ndinofunga , maawa makumi maviri nemana; kana kuti gumi nemaviri?

"Ah, usandinetse ," akadaro maDuchess; "Handina kumbokwanisa kuenderana nenhamba!" Zvino akatanga kuyamwisazve mwana wake, achimuimbira rwiyo rwekuridza mhere, achimuzungunutsa pakupera kwemutsara umwe neumwe.

“Taura nehasha kumukomana wako,
Uye kumurova paanohotsira.
Anongozviita kuti atsamwise,
Nekuti anoziva kuti zvinondishungurudza."

Panguva iyo maDuchess akaimba vhesi rechipiri rerwiyo, akaramba achikanda mwana nechisimba kumusoro nekudzika, uye kanhu kadiki kakachema zvekuti Alice aisatombonzwa mazwi:-

“Ndinotaura nehasha kumwanakomana wangu,
Ndinomurova paanohotsira;
Nokuti anogona kunyatsonakidzwa
Mhiripiri paanoda!"

"Wow! wow! wow!"

"Hona! unogona kuiyamwisa zvishoma, kana uchida!" MaDuchess akadaro kuna Alice, achimukanda mwana paaitaura. "Ini ndinofanira kuenda kunogadzirira kutamba croquet naQueen," uye akakurumidza kubuda mukamuri. Mubiki akamukandira pani paaibuda, asi zvakangomupotsa.

Alice akabata mucheche wacho nechinetso, sezvo chakanga chiri chisikwa chiduku chakaita sehadzi, ndokutambanudza maoko ake nemakumbo kumativi ose, “sehove yenyeredzi,” akafunga kudaro Alice. Kanhu kadiki kehurombo kaifembedza kunge injini yetsvina payakaibata, ikaramba ichizvipeta kaviri nekuzvitwasanudza zvakare, zvekuti pamwe chete, kweminiti yekutanga kana maviri, zvaive zvakawandisa sezvaaigona kuita kuti aibate.

Paakangogadzira nzira chaiyo yekuiyamwisa, (iyo yaive yekuimonyorora kuti iite rudzi rwepfundo, obva aibatisisa nzeve yake yekurudyi nerutsoka rwekuruboshwe, kuitira kuti irege kuzvisunungura,) akaitakura kuenda nayo kumhepo. “Kana ndikasatora mwana uyu neni,” akafunga kudaro Alice, “vanofanira kumuuraya pashure pezuva rimwe kana maviri: kunenge kusiri kuuraya here kumusiya?” Akataura mashoko ekupedzisira nenzwi guru, uye kanhu kadiki kakabva kagomera mhinduro (yakanga yabva kuhotsira panguva iyi). “Usagunun’una,” akadaro Alice; "iyo haisiriyo nzira chaiyo yekuzvitaura."

Mwana akagomera zvakare, Alice akabva atarisa kumeso kuti aone kuti chii chainetsa. Panogona kuva pasina panikiro kuti yakanga ine mhino yokupinduka zvikuru , zvikuru semhuno kupfuura mhino chaiyoiyo; uyewo maziso ake akanga ava kuita maduku zvikuru kumwana: zvachose Alice akanga asingafariri kutarisa kwechinhu chacho zvachose. "Asi pamwe kwaive kuchema chete," akafunga kudaro, ndokutarisa mumaziso ake zvakare, kuti aone kana pakanga paine misodzi.

Aiwa, misodzi yakanga isina. “Kana uchizochinja kuita nguruve mudiwa wangu,” akadaro Alice achirevesa, “handisisina chokuita newe. Kanhu kaduku kemurombo kakachema zvakare (kana kugunun'una, zvakanga zvisingabviri kutaura kuti chii), uye vakaenderera mberi kwechinguva vakanyarara.

Alice akanga achangotanga kufunga mumwoyo make, “Zvino, chii chandichaita nechisikwa ichi kana ndachiwana kumba?” payakagomera zvakare, zvine chisimba, zvekuti yakatarisa pasi kumeso kwayo mune imwe alarm. Panguva ino hapagone kuve nekukanganisa pamusoro payo: yakanga isiri yakawanda kana kuderera kupfuura nguruve, uye akanzwa kuti zvaizova zvisina musoro kuti aenderere mberi nayo.

Saka akagadzika chipuka chidiki, akanzwa kurerukirwa paakachiona chichienda chinyararire muhuni. "Dai yakakura," akadaro mumoyo make, "ingadai yakaita mwana akashata zvakanyanya: asi inogadzira nguruve yakanaka, ndinofunga." Uye akatanga kufunga nezvevamwe vana vaaiziva, vaigona kuita zvakanaka zvikuru senguruve, uye akanga achingoti, “dai mumwe chete aiziva nzira yakarurama yokuvashandura—” paakaita kakuvhunduka achiona Katsi yeCheshire yakagara pabazi remuti waiva kure nemamita mashoma.

Katsi yakangosekerera ichiona Alice. Yaitaridzika zvakanaka, akafunga kuti: ichiri nemazino marefu uye mazino mazhinji, saka akanzwa kuti yaifanirwa kubatwa neruremekedzo.

"Cheshire Puss," akatanga, achitya, sezvo aisatomboziva kuti ingada zita racho: zvisinei, yakangowedzera kunyemwerera. "Huya, kwafadza kusvika pano," akafunga Alice, uye akaenderera mberi. “Mungandiudza henyu, ndapota, nzira yandinofanira kuenda nayo kubva pano?”

"Izvo zvinoenderana nekwaunoda kusvika," akadaro Katsi.

“Handina basa nekunyanya kupi—,” akadaro Alice.

“Saka hazvina basa kuti unoenda nenzira ipi,” akadaro Katsi.

"- chero ndikasvika kumwe ," Alice akawedzera setsananguro.

“Aa, une chokwadi chokuzviita,” yakadaro Katsi, “kana ukafamba kwenguva yakareba zvakakwana.”

Alice aifunga kuti izvi zvaisagona kurambwa, saka akaedza mumwe mubvunzo. “Vanhu vakaita sei vanogara pano?”

“Kurutivi irworwo ,” Katsi yakadaro, ichizunguza mbama yayo yorudyi, “inogara Muvengi: uye kwakananga ikoko ,” ichitambisa imwe nhava, “kunogara Tsuro yaKurume. Shanyira chero waunoda: vose vari kupenga.”

“Asi ini handidi kuenda pakati pevanhu vanopenga,” akadaro Alice.

“Aa, haungazviiti izvozvo,” akadaro Katsi: “tose tinopenga pano, ndinopenga.

“Unoziva sei kuti ndiri kupenga?” Akadaro Alice.

“Unofanira kudaro,” akadaro Katsi, “kana kuti ungadai usina kuuya pano.”

Alice haana kumbofunga kuti izvi zvairatidza; zvisinei, akaenderera mberi achiti “Uye unoziva sei kuti unopenga?”

“Chekutanga,” akadaro Katsi, “imbwa haipengi.

"Ndinofunga kudaro," akadaro Alice.

“Saka,” Katsi yakaenderera mberi ichiti, “unoona, imbwa inochema kana yatsamwa, uye inovheyesa muswe wayo kana ichida, uye zvino ndinohukura pandinenge ndafarira, uye ndinovheyesa muswe wangu pakutsamwa kwangu, saka ndakatsamwa.

" Ndinozvidaidza kuti purring, kwete kupopota," akadaro Alice.

"Idaidze zvaunoda," akadaro Katsi. "Unotamba croquet naMambokadzi nhasi?"

“Ndinofanira kuifarira zvikuru,” akadaro Alice, “asi handisati ndakokwa.”

“Muchandiona ikoko,” akadaro Katsi achibva anyangarika.

Alice haana kunyanya kushamisika nazvo, ainge ave kujaira kunzvenga nzvenga zvichiitika. Achiri kutarisa kwarakanga riri, rakangoerekana raoneka zvakare.

"Ne-bye-bye, chii chakaitika kumwana?" Akadaro Katsi. "Ndanga ndatokanganwa kubvunza."

“Yashanduka kuita nguruve,” akadaro Alice chinyararire, sokunge kuti yakanga yadzoka nenzira yomuzvarirwo.

"Ndaifunga kuti zvichadaro," Katsi akadaro, achibva anyangarika zvakare.

Alice akamira zvishoma, hafu achitarisira kuiona zvakare, asi haina kuoneka, uye mushure meminiti kana maviri akabva afamba achienda kwainzi kwaigara Tsuro. “Ndakamboona vanovenga,” akadaro mumwoyo make; "March Hare ichanyanya kunakidza, uye pamwe sezvo ino yaMay haizopenge-pengesa zvakanyanya sezvazvaive muna Kurume." Achitaura kudaro akabva asimudza musoro ndokuona Katsi zvakare yakagara pabazi remuti.

“Wati nguruve here, kana kuti muonde?” Akadaro Katsi.

“Ndati inguruve,” akapindura Alice; "Uye ndinoshuva kuti haungarambi uchionekwa uye uchinyangarika kamwe-kamwe: unoita kuti munhu aite manyemwe."

“Zvakanaka,” akadaro Katsi; uye panguva ino yakanyangarika zvishoma nezvishoma, ichitanga nekuguma kwemuswe, uye ichiguma negrin, iyo yakasara imwe nguva mushure mokunge imwe yose yaenda.
