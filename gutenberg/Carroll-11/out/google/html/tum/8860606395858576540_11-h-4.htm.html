MUTU IV.
Kalulu Wakutuma Ndalama Yichoko

Wakaŵa Kalulu Mutuŵa, wakachimbirangaso pachokopachoko, ndipo wakalaŵiskanga mwakukhwaskika apo wakalutanga, nga ni para wataya chinthu chinyake; ndipo wakapulika kuti yikujifumba yekha kuti “Duchess! Duchess! Oh viŵegha vyane vyakutemweka! Oh weya wane na ndevu zane! Wazamunikoma, nga umo vikuŵira na viyuni vya mtundu wa ferret! Kasi ningaŵaponya nkhu, nkhujifumba?” Alice wakaghanaghana kuti yikapenjanga fan na magulovu ghatuŵa gha ŵana, ndipo mwalusungu wakamba kuzipenja, kweni zikaŵavya apo zikawonekanga—vinthu vyose vikawonekanga kuti vyasintha kufuma apo wakasambira mu chiziŵa, ndipo holo yikuru, iyo yikaŵa na thebulu la galasi na chipata chichoko cha vanish, yikaŵa kuti yasintha.

Mwaluŵiro chomene Kalulu wakawona Alice, apo wakalutanga kukasaka, ndipo wakamuchema na mazgu ghakukwiya, "Chifukwa wuli, Mary Ann, kasi ukuchitachi kuwaro kuno? Chimbilira kunyumba nyengo iyi, ndipo unitolere magulovu ghaŵiri na fan! Mwaluŵiro, sono!" Ndipo Alice wakaŵa na wofi comene mwakuti wakacimbira nyengo yeneyiyo ku cigaŵa ico cikalongora, kwambura kuyezga kulongosora ivyo cikabudiska.

Apo wakachimbiranga, wakajiphalira kuti: “Wakanitora kuŵa muteŵeti wake wa pa nyumba.” "Wazamuzizwa chomene para wamanya kuti ndine njani! Kweni chiŵe chiwemi kuti nimutolere fan yake na magulovu—ndiko kuti, usange ningavisanga." Apo wakayowoyanga nthena, wakasanga nyumba yichoko yakutowa, ndipo pa mulyango wake pakaŵa chiphapha cha mkuŵa chakuwala ndipo pakalembeka zina lakuti “W. RABBIT.” Wakanjira kwambura kugogoda, ndipo wakakwera mwaluŵiro pachanya, na wofi ukuru kuti wangakumana na Mary Ann weneco, na kufumiskika kufuma mu nyumba pambere wandasange fan na magulovu.

Alice wakajifumba kuti: “Vikuwoneka kuti ni vyakuzizika chomene kuti nkhutumizga mauthenga gha kalulu! Nkhughanaghana kuti Dinah wazamunitumira mauthenga ghakulondezgapo!” Ndipo wakamba kughanaghana mtundu wa cinthu ico cizamucitika: “‘Miss Alice! 'Kuza mu miniti yimoza, nesi! Kweni nkhwenera kuwona kuti mbeŵa yikufumapo yayi.' Kweni nkhughanaghana yayi,” Alice wakalutilira kuyowoya, “kuti ŵangazomerezga Dinah kwimilira mu nyumba usange yingamba kuphalira ŵanthu kuti ŵachitenge nthena!”

Pa nyengo iyi wakasanga nthowa yakunjilira mu chipinda chichoko icho chikaŵa na thebulu pa windo, ndipo pa iyo (nga umo wakagomezgeranga) pakaŵa fan na magulovu ghaŵiri panji ghatatu ghatuŵa gha ŵana: wakatora fan na magulovu ghaŵiri, ndipo wakaŵa pafupi kufumamo mu chipinda, apo jiso lake likawira pa botolo lichoko ilo likaŵa pafupi na galasi. Pa nyengo iyi pakaŵavya chilembo icho pakalembeka kuti “NIMWANI,” kweni nangauli vikaŵa nthena, wakachijura na kuŵika pa milomo yake. "Nkhumanya kuti chinthu chinyake chakukondweska chizamuchitika nadi," wakayowoya yekha, "nyengo yiliyose para narya panji kumwa chilichose; ntheura niwonerenge waka ivyo botolo ili likuchita. Nkhugomezga kuti lizamunipangiska kukuraso, chifukwa nadi nkhuvuka chomene kuŵa chinthu chichoko chomene!"

Icho chikachitika nadi, ndipo mwaluŵiro chomene kuluska umo wakaghanaghaniranga: pambere wandamwe hafu ya botolo, wakasanga mutu wake ukufinyikizga pa silingi, ndipo wakenera kugwada kuti waponoske singo yake kuti yileke kuphyoka. Wakaŵika pasi botolo mwaluŵiro, ndipo wakati mu mtima wake “Ici nchakukwana comene—nkhugomezga kuti nizamukuraso yayi—Nga umo vikuŵira, ningafumapo yayi pa mulyango—nkhukhumba kuti ningamwa comene yayi!”

Soka! nyengo yikaŵa yakucedwa comene kuti ŵakhumbe nthena! Wakalutilira kukura, na kukura, ndipo mwaluŵiro comene wakakhumbikwiranga kugwada pasi: mu miniti yinyake pakaŵavya nanga ni malo gha ici, ndipo wakayezga nkhongono ya kugona pasi na ciŵegha cimoza pa mulyango, ndipo woko linyake likapindika pa mutu wake. Nangauli vikaŵa nthena wakalutilira kukura, ndipo, nga ni chinthu chaumaliro, wakaŵika woko limoza kuwaro pa windo, na lundi limoza pachanya pa chimbuzi, ndipo wakati kwa iyo yekha "Sono ningachitaso yayi, chilichose icho chingachitika. Kasi nizamuŵa wuli?"

Mwamwaŵi kwa Alice, botolo lichoko la matsenga sono likaŵa na nkhongono zake zose, ndipo wakakura yayi: nangauli vikaŵa nthena, likaŵa lakusuzga chomene, ndipo, pakuti pakawonekanga kuti palije mwaŵi wakuti wafumemoso mu chipinda, nchakuzizika yayi kuti wakajipulika kuti ngwambura kukondwa.

“Cikaŵa cakukondweska comene ku nyumba,” wakaghanaghana nthena Alice mukavu, “apo yumoza wakakuranga yayi nyengo zose, ndipo wakalamulirikanga na mbeŵa na kalulu. nkhaŵazganga ntharika, nkhaghanaghananga kuti chinthu cha mtundu uwu chikachitikapo yayi, ndipo sono pano nili pakati pa chimoza! Pakwenera kuŵa buku ilo likalembeka vya ine, ilo likwenera kuŵapo! “pafupifupi palije malo ghakuti munthu wakuleso kuno .”

“Kweni ntheura,” wakaghanaghana Alice, “kasi nizamuŵa mulara yayi kuluska umo niliri sono? Ici cizamuŵa cisangulusko, nthowa yimoza—kuti nileke kuŵa mwanakazi mucekuru—kweni ntheura—nyengo zose kuŵa na masambiro ghakuti nisambire! O, nkhwenera kutemwa yayi nthena! ”

“Oh, iwe Alice muzeleza!” wakazgora yekha. “Kasi mungasambira wuli masambiro muno? Chifukwa wuli, palije malo ghakuti mukhalepo , ndipo palije malo gha mabuku gha masambiro ghaliyose!”

Ndipo ntheura wakalutilira, wakatora dankha chigaŵa chimoza ndipo pamanyuma chinyake, ndipo wakadumbiskana chomene; kweni pamanyuma pa maminiti ghachoko waka wakapulika mazgu kuwaro, ndipo wakayima kuti wategherezge.

"Mariya Ann! Mariya Ann!" wakayowoya mazgu agha. “Nitolerani magulovu ghane nyengo iyi!” Pamanyuma pake pakaŵa kukhweŵa pachoko waka kwa malundi pa makwelero. Alice wakamanya kuti ni Kalulu uyo wiza kuzakamupenja, ndipo wakambwambwantha mpaka wakasunkhunya nyumba, wakaluwa chomene kuti sono wakaŵa mukuru pafupifupi kaŵiri kaŵiri kuluska Kalulu, ndipo wakaŵavya chifukwa chakuwopera.

Pa nyengo yeneyiyo Kalulu wakiza pa mulyango, ndipo wakayezga kuwujura; kweni, apo mulyango ukajulika mukati, ndipo chiŵegha cha Alice chikakanizgika chomene, kuyezgayezga uko kukawoneka kuti kukatondeka. Alice wakapulika kuti yikuyowoya yekha kuti “Pamanyuma nizamuzungulira na kunjira pa windo.”

“ Kuti muzamuchita yayi!” Alice wakaghanaghana, ndipo, pamanyuma pa kulindilira mpaka apo wakaghanaghana kuti wakupulika Kalulu pasi pa windo, mwamabuchibuchi wakatambasura woko lake, na kupanga m’makutu mu mlengalenga. Wakakora kanthu yayi, kweni wakapulika kulira pachoko na kuwa, na kugwa kwa galasi lakuphyoka, kufuma apo wakadumura kuti vingachitika waka kuti likawa mu furemu ya nkhaka, panji chinthu chinyake cha mtundu uwu.

Pamanyuma pake pakaŵa mazgu ghakukwiya—gha Kalulu—“Pat! Pat! Kasi uli nkhu?” Ndipo ntheura mazgu agho wakapulikapo yayi, “Nadi ntheura nili pano! Kukumba maapulo, yer honour!”

“Kukumba maapulo, nadi!” wakayowoya Kalulu mwakukwiya. “Pano! Zani mukaniwovwire kufumamo mu ivi! ” (Mazgu gha galasi ghanyake ghakuphyoka.)

“Sono niphalirani, Pat, kasi nchivichi icho chili pa windo?”

“Enya, ni woko, yer ntchindi!” (Wakayowoyanga kuti “arrum.”)

"Mkono, iwe tsekwe! Kasi ni njani uyo wali kuwonapo ukuru uwu? Chifukwa wuli, ukuzura pa windo lose!"

“Enya, vikuchitika, yer ntchindi: kweni ni woko la vyose ivi.”

“Kweni, palije bizinesi apo, mulimose umo vingaŵira: lutani mukayitore!”

Pakaŵa chete chitali pamanyuma pa ivi, ndipo Alice wakapulikanga waka kukhweŵa nyengo na nyengo; nga ni, “Nadi, nkhutemwa yayi, yer honour, nanga nkhu, nanga nkhutemwa!” “Chita nga umo nkhukuphalira, iwe wakujikuzga!” ndipo paumaliro wakatambasuraso woko lake, na kupangaso m’makutu ghanyake mu mlengalenga. Pa nyengo iyi pakaŵa viwawa viŵiri vichokovichoko, ndiposo mazgu ghanyake gha galasi lakuphwanyika. “Pakwenera kuŵa mafuremu gha nkhaka ghanandi chomene!” wakaghanaghana nthena Alice. "Nkhujifumba kuti ŵachitengechi pamanyuma! Pa nkhani ya kunifumiska pa windo, nkhukhumba waka kuti ŵanifumiske ! Nkhugomezga kuti nkhukhumba yayi kukhalaso muno!"

Wakalindilira kwa nyengo yichoko waka kwambura kupulika chinyake: paumaliro pake pakaŵa kulira kwa tumawilo tuchokotuchoko twa ngolo, ndipo wakapulika mazgu ghanandi ghawemi ghose ghakuyowoya pamoza: wakapulika mazgu ghakuti: “Kasi makwelero ghanyake ghali nkhu?—Cifukwa wuli, nkhakhumbikwiranga yayi kwiza na limoza pera; kona—Yayi, ŵamangirire pamoza dankha—Oh ŵazamuchita makora mwakukwana yayi—Apa, Bill, kora chingwe ichi cha padenga?—Kumbukani kuti slate yakufwatuka yizamuŵa pasi! (kukhuŵara kwakukwezga)—“Sono, kasi ni njani uyo wakacita nthena?—Wakaŵa Bill, nkhughanaghana kuti—Kasi ni njani uyo wakwenera kukhira mu chimbuzi?—Yayi, ine nizamuchita yayi! Iwe uchitenge!— Kuti ine nizamuchita yayi, ntheura!—Bill wazamukhira—Pano, Bill! fumu yikuti iwe ukwenera kukhira mu chimbuzi!”

"Oh! Ntheura Bill wakwenera kwiza pasi pa chimbuzi, kasi ndimo viliri?" Alice wakayowoya yekha. "Ŵasoni, ŵakuwoneka kuti ŵakuŵika vyose pa Bill! Ine ningaŵa mu malo gha Bill yayi pa mtengo uwemi: malo agha ghakuwotchera moto ngakuchepa, kuti nigomezge; kweni nkhughanaghana kuti ningakankha pachoko!"

Wakakoka lundi lake mpaka pasi pa chimbuzi umo wangafiskira, ndipo wakalindilira mpaka apo wakapulika chinyama chichoko (wakamanya yayi kuti chikaŵa cha mtundu wuli) chikukhwaska na kukhweŵa mu chimbuzi pafupi na pachanya pake: ntheura, wakati kwa iyo yekha “Uyu ni Bill,” wakapeleka chingwe chimoza chakuthwa, ndipo wakalindilira kuti wawone ivyo vichitikenge pamanyuma.

Chinthu chakwamba icho wakapulika chikaŵa chorus ya general yakuti “There goes Bill!” ndipo mazgu gha Kalulu ghakaŵa pamoza—“Mukole, imwe pafupi na mpanda!” ndipo pamanyuma pake pakaŵa chete, ndipo pamanyuma pake mazgu ghakaŵaso ghakusokonezgekaso—“Khwezga mutu wake—Brandy sono—Ungamufyofyontha yayi—Kasi vikaŵa wuli, muchekuru? Kasi chikamuchitikira wuli? Tiphalirani vyose!”

Paumaliro pakaŵa mazgu ghakufwatuka, ghakukhwaŵa, (“Uyo ni Bill,” wakaghanaghana Alice,) “Kweni, nkhumanya yayi—Palije, nkhuwonga; sono nili makora—kweni nkhukhuŵara chomene kuti nimuphalirani—chose icho nkhumanya ntchakuti, chinthu chinyake chikwiza kwa ine nga ni Jack-in-the-box nga ni skyro, ndipo nkhuluta pachanya!”

“Ntheura mukachita, muchekuru!” Ŵanyake ŵakati nthena.

“Tikwenera kuwotcha nyumba iyi!” wakayowoya mazgu gha Kalulu; ndipo Alice wakachemerezga mwakukwezga umo wangafiskira kuti, “Usange ungachita nthena, nizamukuŵikira Dinah!”

Pakaŵa chete nyengo yeneyiyo, ndipo Alice wakaghanaghana kuti, "Nkhujifumba kuti ŵachitengechi pamanyuma! Usange ŵangaŵa na mahara, ŵangawuskapo padenga." Pakati pajumpha miniti yimoza panji ziŵiri, ŵakambaso kwendakwenda, ndipo Alice wakapulika Kalulu wakuti, “Barrowful yizamuchita, kwamba pakwamba.”

Alice wakaghanaghana kuti: “Barrow ya vichi? ” kweni wakakhala nyengo yitali yayi kuti wakayikiske, cifukwa nyengo yakulondezgapo mphepo ya tumabwe tucokotucoko zikiza pa windo na kukhweŵa, ndipo zinyake zikamutimba pa nkhope. Wakajiphalira yekha kuti: “Nilekeskenge ivi,” ndipo wakachemerezga kuti, “Chiwemi uleke kuchitaso nthena!” ivyo vikapangiska kuti paŵe chete chinyake chakufwa.

Alice wakawona mwakuzizwa kuti malibwe ghose ghakazgoka tumakeke tuchokotuchoko apo ghakagona pasi, ndipo fundo yakutowa yikiza mu mutu wake. Iyo wakaghanaghana kuti: “Usange ningarya chimoza mwa vingwa ivi, vingapangiska kuti nisinthe kukura kwane; ndipo pakuti vingapangiska yayi kuti niŵe mukuru, vikwenera kuti vikupangiska kuti niŵe muchoko, nkhughanaghana.”

Ntheura wakamira chimoza mwa vingwa ivi, ndipo wakakondwa chomene kuwona kuti wakamba kuchepa mwaluŵiro. Wakati waŵa muchoko waka kuti wajumphe pa mulyango, wakachimbilira kuwaro kwa nyumba, ndipo wakasanga vinyama vichokovichoko na viyuni vinandi chomene vikulindilira kuwaro. Ka Lizard kacoko mukavu, Bill, kakaŵa pakati, kakakwezgeka na nkhumba ziŵiri za guinea, izo zikaŵa kuti zikucipa cinthu cinyake kufuma mu botolo. Wose ŵakachimbilira kwa Alice nyengo iyo wakawonekera; kweni wakachimbira mwakulimbikira, ndipo mwaluŵiro wakasanga kuti wali wakuvikilirika mu khuni lakukhora.

"Chinthu chakwamba icho nkhwenera kuchita," Alice wakayowoya yekha, apo wakazungulirazungulira mu nkhalango, "nkhukuraso kufika pa kukura kwane kwakwenelera; ndipo chinthu chachiŵiri nkhusanga nthowa yakunjilira mu munda wakutowa uwu. Nkhughanaghana kuti iyi yizamuŵa ndondomeko yiwemi chomene."

Likapulikikwanga nga ni pulani yiwemi comene, kwambura kukayika, ndipo likaŵikika makora comene ndiposo mwambura kusuzga; suzgo likaŵa lakuti, wakaŵavya fundo yichoko waka ya umo wangaŵikira; ndipo apo wakalaŵiskanga uku na uku mwakukhwaskika pakati pa makuni, mphukira yichoko yakuthwa iyo yikaŵa pachanya pa mutu wake yikamupangiska kuti walaŵiske kuchanya mwaluŵiro chomene.

Kamwana ka galu kakuru kakamulaŵiskanga pasi na maso ghakuru ghakuzungulira, ndipo mwakulopwa wakatambasura lundi limoza, kuyezga kumukhwaska. “Chinthu chichoko chomene!” wakayowoya Alice, na mazgu ghakukhorweska, ndipo wakayezgayezga comene kuti waciwuzge; kweni wakaŵa na wofi comene nyengo zose pa kughanaghana kuti cingaŵa na njara, mu nyengo iyi cingaŵa cakwenelera comene kuti cimurye nangauli wakamukhozganga vyose.

Pakuti wakamanya yayi ivyo wakachita, wakatora ndodo yichoko waka, na kuyipeleka ku mwana wa galu; pa nyengo iyo mwana wa galu wakajumphilira mu mlengalenga kufuma pa malundi ghake ghose nyengo yimoza, na kulira kwa cimwemwe, ndipo wakacimbilira ku ndodo, na kupangiska kuti wagomezge kuti wakuyifipa mtima; ntheura Alice wakachimbira kuseri kwa mtengo ukuru wa mtundu wa mtundu, kuti waleke kuchimbizgika; ndipo nyengo iyo wakawonekera ku chigaŵa chinyake, mwana wa galu wakachimbiraso pa ndodo, ndipo wakawa mutu pa vitende mu luŵiro lwake kuti wayikole; ntheura Alice, wakughanaghana kuti cikaŵa nga ni para wakuseŵera na hachi la ngolo, ndipo wakulindilira kuti nyengo yiliyose wazamuponderezgeka pasi pa malundi ghake, wakacimbiraso kuzingilizga mtengo wa mtundu uwu; ntheura mwana wa galu wakamba mndandanda wa milandu yifupi pa ndodo, kucimbira mtunda ucoko comene kunthazi nyengo yiliyose na mtunda utali comene kunyuma, na kuwukira mwakukweŵeka nyengo yose, m’paka paumaliro wakakhala pasi mtunda uwemi, wakupuma, na lulimi lwake lwakupendera kufuma mu mulomo wake, ndipo maso ghake ghakuru ghakaŵa ghakujalika hafu.

Ici cikawonekanga kwa Alice kuŵa mwaŵi uwemi wakuti wacimbire; ntheura wakanyamuka nyengo yeneyiyo, ndipo wakachimbira m’paka apo wakavuka chomene na kufwa mzimu, ndipo m’paka apo m’makutu gha mwana wa galu ghakapulikikwanga mwakufwatuka chomene kutali.

“Ndipo nangauli vikaŵa nthena, kakaŵa kamwana ka galu kakutemweka comene!” wakayowoya Alice, apo wakathemba pa buttercup kuti wapumule, ndipo wakajipumuzga na hamba limoza: “Nkhatenge nkhatemwanga kulisambizga viyezgo comene, usange—usange ningaŵa waka na kukura kwakwenelera kuti nicite! Oh wakutemweka! Nkhaŵa pafupi kuluwa kuti nkhwenera kukuraso ! kurya panji kumwa chinthu chinyake panji chinyake kweni fumbo likuru ndakuti, nchivichi?”

Fumbo likuru nadi likaŵa lakuti, kasi nchivici? Alice wakalaŵiska maluŵa na vipambi vya utheka kuzingilizga, kweni wakawona yayi chinthu chilichose icho chikawonekanga nga ni chinthu chakwenelera chakurya panji kumwa mu nyengo iyo vinthu vikaŵira. Pafupi na iyo pakaŵa bowa ukuru uyo wakamera, pafupifupi utali wakuyana waka na wake; ndipo wakati walaŵiska pasi pakhe, na ku vigaŵa vyose viŵiri, na kumanyuma kwake, wakaghanaghana kuti wangalaŵiskaso na kuwona ivyo vikaŵa pachanya pakhe.

Wakajitambasura pa viŵegha vyake, na kudodoliska pa mphepete mwa bowa, ndipo maso ghake nyengo yeneyiyo ghakakumana na maso gha mphutsi yikuru ya buluu, iyo yikakhala pachanya na mawoko ghake ghakupindika, mwakufwasa yikukhweŵa hookah yitali, ndipo yikamuwonanga yayi nanga nkhuchoko waka pa iyo panji pa chinthu chilichose.
