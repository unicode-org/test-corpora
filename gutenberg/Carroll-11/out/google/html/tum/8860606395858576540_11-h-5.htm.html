MUTU V.
Ulongozgi kufuma ku Nkhwantha

Nkhwantha na Alice ŵakalaŵiskana kwa nyengo yichoko waka kwambura kuyowoya: paumaliro Nkhwantha yikafumiska hookah mu mulomo wake, na kuyowoya nayo na mazgu ghakukhozga, ghakugona.

“Kasi imwe ndimwe njani? ”, wakayowoya nthena Nkhwantha.

Ici cikaŵa cakukhozga yayi kuti ŵadumbiskane. Alice wakazgora, m’malo mwake mwakukhwaskika na soni, “Ine—nkhumanya yayi, bwana, sonosono apa—pafupifupi nkhumanya uyo nkhaŵa apo nkhawuka mulenji uno, kweni nkhughanaghana kuti nkhwenera kuti nkhasinthika kanandi waka kufuma nyengo iyo.”

“Kasi ukung’anamurachi na fundo iyi?” wakayowoya Nkhwantha mwakukhozga. “Jilongosolerani mwekha!”

Alice wakati: “Nkhutondeka kujilongosora , nkhuŵa na wofi, bwana, chifukwa ine ndine yayi, mwawona.”

“Nkhuwona yayi,” wakayowoya Nkhwantha.

“Nkhuwopa kuti ningayowoya makora yayi,” Alice wakazgora mwaulemu comene, “cifukwa ine mwene ningapulikiska yayi kwamba pakwamba; ndipo kuŵa na masaizi ghanandi ghakupambanapambana pa zuŵa limoza kukusoŵezga comene.”

“Ndimo viliri yayi,” wakayowoya nthena Nkhwantha.

Alice wakati: “Kweni, panji mundasangepo nthena; “kweni para mukwenera kusanduka kuŵa chrysalis—muzamuzgoka zuŵa linyake, mukumanya—ndipo pamanyuma pa ici kuŵa gulugufe, nkhwenera kughanaghana kuti muzamupulika kuti nchakuzizika pachoko, asi nthena?”

“Pachoko waka yayi,” wakayowoya Nkhwantha.

Alice wakati: “Kweni, panji umo mukujipulikira vingaŵa vyakupambana; “Icho nkhumanya ntchakuti, vingaŵa vyakuzizika chomene kwa ine .”

"Iwe!" wakayowoya Nkhwantha mwakunyoza. "Niwe njani? "

Icho chikaŵawezgeraso kukwamba kwa kudumbiskana. Alice wakakwiya pachoko chifukwa chakuti Caterpillar wakayowoya mazgu ghafupi chomene agha, ndipo wakajikwezga ndipo wakati, mwakujikuzga chomene, “Nkhughanaghana kuti, mukwenera kuniphalira kuti ndimwe njani, pakwamba.”

"Chifukwa wuli?" wakayowoya Nkhwantha.

Apa pakaŵa fumbo linyake lakuzizika; ndipo pakuti Alice wakatondeka kughanaghana cifukwa ciwemi, ndipo pakuti Caterpillar wakawonekanga kuti wali mu mkhaliro wakusuzga comene wa maghanoghano, wakazgoka.

“Welerani!” Mbowo wakachema pamanyuma pake. “Nili na fundo yakuzirwa yakuti niyowoye!”

Ici cikawonekanga nga nchakulayizga, nadi: Alice wakazgoka na kuweleraso.

“Lutilirani kukwiya,” wakayowoya nthena Mbowo.

“Kasi ndivyo pera?” wakayowoya Alice, uku wakumira ukali wake umo wangafiskira.

“Yayi,” wakayowoya nthena Nkhwantha.

Alice wakaghanaghananga kuti wangalindiliraso, pakuti wakaŵavya chinyake chakuchita, ndipo panji pamanyuma pa vyose vingaŵa kuti vikumuphalira chinthu chinyake chakwenelera kupulika. Kwa maminiti ghacoko waka cikatutuŵa kwambura kuyowoya, kweni paumaliro cikajura mawoko ghake, cikafumiskaso hookah mu mulomo wake, ndipo cikati, “Ntheura mukughanaghana kuti mwasintha, kasi ndimo viliri?”

Alice wakati: “Nkhuwopa kuti ndine, bwana; “Nkhukumbuka yayi vinthu nga umo nkhachitiranga pakwamba—ndipo nkhusunga kukura kwakuyana yayi kwa maminiti 10 pamoza!”

“Kasi mukukumbuka yayi vinthu wuli ?” wakayowoya Nkhwantha.

“Kweni, nayezga kuyowoya kuti “Kasi njuchi yichoko yakutangwanika yikuchita wuli,” kweni vyose vikaŵa vyakupambana!” Alice wakazgora na mazgu ghakuŵinya chomene.

“Werezganipo kuti, ‘ Mwachekura, Adada William ,’” wakayowoya nthena Nkhwantha.

Alice wakapindika mawoko ghake, ndipo wakamba kuti:—

Munyamata uyu wakati: “Mwacekura, Adada William, .
“Ndipo sisi lako lazgoka lituŵa chomene;
Ndipouli, imwe mukuyimilira pa mutu winu kwambura kuleka—
Kasi mukughanaghana kuti, pa msinkhu winu, nchakwenelera?”

Dada William wakazgora mwana wake kuti: “Mu unyamata wane, .
“Nkhawopanga kuti vingapweteka wongo;
Kweni, sono pakuti nkhugomezga makora kuti nilije, .
Mwakuti, nkhuchita mwakuwerezgawerezga.”

Munyamata uyu wakati: “Wachekura, nga umo nkhayowoyera kale, .
Ndipo ŵakura chomene mafuta ghambura kumanyikwa;
Ndipouli, imwe mukazgokera kumanyuma pa mulyango—
Lombani, kasi chifukwa cha nthena nchivichi?”

“Mu unyamata wane,” wakayowoya nkhwantha, apo wakasunkhunyanga maloko ghake gha imvi, .
“Nkhasunga viŵaro vyane vyose kuŵa vyakukhora chomene .
Pakugwiriskira ncito mafuta agha—shilingi yimoza pa kabokosi—
Nizomerezgani kuti nimuguliskani ŵaŵiri?”

Munyamata uyu wakati: “Wacekura, ndipo m’kamwa wako ngwakupeleŵera comene .
Pakuti chilichose chakukhora kuluska suet;
Ndipouli mukamalizga tsekwe, na viwangwa na mlomo—
Lombani, kasi mukakwaniska wuli kucita nthena?”

Adada ŵake ŵakati: “Apo nkhaŵa muwukirano wane, nkhasambiranga malango, .
Ndipo nkhasuskanga mulandu uliwose na muwoli wane;
Ndipo nkhongono ya minofu, iyo yikapeleka ku m’kamwa mwane, .
Wakhalapo umoyo wane wose.”

Munyamata uyu wakati: “Ndimwe ŵacekuru, munthu wangaghanaghana yayi .
Kuti jiso lako likaŵa lakukhora nga ni kale;
Ndipouli, mukalinganizga somba ya eel ku umaliro wa mphuno yinu—
Kasi nchivici cikamupangiskani kuŵa ŵamahara comene?”

“Nazgora mafumbo ghatatu, ndipo ivi vyakwana,”
Wakati adada ŵake; “mungajipa yayi mphepo!
Kasi mukughanaghana kuti ningapulikira vinthu vyanthena ivi zuŵa lose?
Lutanipo, panji nizamukuponya pasi pa makwelero!”

“Ivyo vikuyowoyeka makora yayi,” wakayowoya nthena Nkhwantha.

“ Nchakwenelera yayi, nkhuwopa,” wakayowoya nthena Alice mwakukhwaskika; “mazgu ghanyake ghasinthika.”

“Ntchiheni kufuma pakwamba m’paka paumaliro,” wakayowoya nthena Caterpillar mwakusimikizga, ndipo pakaŵa chete kwa maminiti ghachoko waka.

Nkhwantha ndiyo yikaŵa yakwamba kuyowoya.

“Kasi mukukhumba kuŵa na saizi wuli?” likafumba.

Alice wakazgora mwaluŵiro kuti: “Oh, nkhumanya makora yayi kukura kwake; “yumoza pera ndiyo wakutemwa yayi kusintha kanandi waka, mukumanya.”

“Nkhumanya yayi ,” wakayowoya nthena Nkhwantha.

Alice wakayowoyapo yayi: wakasuskikapo yayi nthena mu umoyo wake, ndipo wakawona kuti wakuleka kujikora.

“Kasi sono mwakhorwa?” wakayowoya Nkhwantha.

Alice wakati: “Kweni, ningatemwa kuŵa mukuru pachoko , bwana, usange mungaŵa na suzgo yayi: masentimita ghatatu ni utali wakuŵinya chomene.”

“Ni utaliko uwemi chomene nadi!” wakayowoya Caterpillar mwakukwiya, kujikwezga mwakunyoloka apo wakayowoyanga (wakaŵa msinkhu wa masentimita ghatatu nadi).

“Kweni nkhazgoŵera yayi!” wakaŵeyelera Alice mukavu na mazgu ghakuŵinya. Ndipo wakaghanaghana kuti, “Nkhukhumba kuti vinyama ivi vileke kukhuŵara luŵiro nthena!”

“Uzamuzgoŵera para nyengo yajumphapo,” wakayowoya nthena Nkhwantha; ndipo yikaŵika hookah mu mulomo wake na kwamba kukhweŵaso.

Pa nyengo iyi Alice wakalindilira mwakuzizipizga m’paka apo likasankha kuyowoyaso. Mu miniti yimoza panji ziŵiri Caterpillar wakafumiska hookah mu mulomo wake na kukhweŵa kamoza panji kaŵiri, na kujisunkhunya yekha. Pamanyuma pake likakhira pa bowa, na kukhwaŵa mu utheka, likayowoyanga waka apo likaluta, “Cigaŵa cimoza cizamukupangiskani kuti muŵe ŵatali, ndipo cigaŵa cinyake cizamupangiskani kuti muŵe ŵafupi.”

“Chigaŵa chimoza cha vichi? Chigaŵa chinyake cha vichi? ” Alice wakaghanaghana nthena.

“Ya bowa,” wakayowoya Nkhwantha, nga ni para wakafumba mwakukwezga; ndipo mu nyengo yinyake likaŵa kuti likuwoneka yayi.

Alice wakalutilira kulaŵiska bowa mwakughanaghanira kwa mphindi yimoza, kuyezgayezga kumanya kuti ni vigaŵa wuli viŵiri vyake; ndipo pakuti likaŵa lakuzungulira mwakukwana, wakasanga kuti ili likaŵa fumbo lakusuzga comene. Ndipouli, paumaliro wakatambasura mawoko ghake kuzingilizga mpaka apo ŵangafika, ndipo wakaphyora pachoko waka pa mphepete na woko lililose.

“Ndipo sono ni vichi ivyo ni vichi?” wakayowoya yekha, ndipo wakakhwaska pachoko waka chigaŵa cha woko lamalyero kuti wayezge umo chikuchitikira: nyengo yakulondezgapo wakapulika kupwetekeka kwa nkhaza pasi pa chibwano chake: chikamutimba lundi!

Wakaŵa na wofi comene cifukwa ca kusintha kwa mwamabuci uku, kweni wakawona kuti palije nyengo yakuti wataye, cifukwa wakacepanga mwaluŵiro; ntheura wakamba kugwira ntchito nyengo yeneyiyo kuti waryeko chigaŵa chinyake. Chiŵegha chake chikaŵa chakukhora chomene pa lundi lake, mwakuti pakaŵavya malo ghakuti wajulirepo mulomo wake; kweni wakacita nthena paumaliro, ndipo wakakwaniska kumira cigaŵa cicoko waka ca ku woko lamalyero.

“Zani, mutu wane wafwatuka paumaliro!” wakayowoya Alice mu mazgu ghakukondweska, agho ghakasintha na kuŵa wofi mu nyengo yinyake, apo wakasanga kuti mapewa ghake ghakaŵavya: vyose ivyo wakawona, apo wakalaŵiska pasi, vikaŵa utali ukuru wa singo, uwo ukawonekanga kuti ukukwera nga ni mtengo kufuma mu nyanja ya mahamba ghakubiriŵira agho ghakaŵa kutali chomene pasi pake.

“Kasi vinthu vyose vyakubiriŵira ivi vingaŵa vichi?” wakayowoya nthena Alice. "Ndipo kasi mapewa ghane ghafika nkhu? Ndipo oh, mawoko ghane ghakavu, kasi vikuchitika wuli kuti nkhutondeka kumuwonani?" Wakaŵasunkhunyanga apo wakayowoyanga, kweni vikawonekanga kuti palije ico cikulondezgapo, kupatulapo kugwedezeka pachoko pakati pa mahamba ghakubiriŵira gha kutali.

Pakuti pakawonekanga kuti palije mwaŵi wakuti wakwezge mawoko ghake ku mutu wake, wakayezgayezga kuti mutu wake ufike pasi pa iwo, ndipo wakakondwa chomene kuwona kuti singo lake likapindika luŵiro ku chigaŵa chilichose, nga ni njoka. Wakaŵa kuti wakwaniska waka kuwukhoma pasi mu zigzag wakutowa, ndipo wakakhumbanga kujibisa pakati pa mahamba, agho wakasanga kuti ghakaŵa chinthu chinyake yayi kweni vigaŵa vya pachanya vya makuni agho wakazgoŵera pasi pake, apo kulira kwakuthwa kukapangiska kuti wawelere nyuma mwaluŵiro: nkhunda yikuru yikawulukira mu nkhope yake, ndipo yikaŵa kuti yikumutimba na nkhaza.

“Njoka!” wakachemerezga Nkhunda.

“Ine ndine njoka yayi !” Alice wakayowoya mwakukwiya. “Nilekeni waka!”

“Njoka, nkhuyowoyaso!” wakawerezgapo Nkhunda, kweni mu mazgu ghakuzika comene, ndipo wakasazgirapo na mtundu wa kulira kuti, “Nayezga nthowa yiliyose, ndipo vikuwoneka kuti palije ico cikuŵakwana!”

Alice wakati: “Nkhumanya yayi nanga nkhumanya ivyo mukuyowoya.”

“Nayezga misisi ya makuni, ndipo nayezga mabanki, ndipo nayezga vigongwe,” Nkhunda yikalutilira, kwambura kumupwelelera; "kweni njoka izo! Palije chakuŵakondweska!"

Alice wakazizwa chomene, kweni wakawona kuti palije ntchito yakuti wayowoyeso vinyake mpaka apo Nkhunda yimalizge.

“Nga ni para likaŵa suzgo lakukwana yayi kuseŵa mazira,” wakayowoya nthena Nkhunda; “Kweni nkhwenera kuŵa maso na njoka usiku na muhanya!

Alice, uyo wakamba kuwona ng’anamuro lake, wakati: “Nkhuphepiska comene kuti mwakwiya.”

"Ndipo nga umo nkhatolera khuni litali chomene mu makuni," Nkhunda yikalutilizga, kukwezga mazgu ghake ku kuchemerezga, "ndipo nga umo nkhaghanaghaniranga kuti paumaliro nkhwenera kuŵa wakufwatuka, ŵakwenera kwiza ŵakukhuŵara kufuma kuchanya! Ugh, Njoka!"

“Kweni ine ndine njoka yayi , nkhukuphalira!” wakayowoya nthena Alice. “Ndine—ndine—”

"Kweni! Kasi ndimwe njani ?" wakayowoya Nkhunda. “Nkhuwona kuti mukuyezgayezga kupanga chinthu chinyake!”

“Ine—ine ndine msungwana mucoko,” wakayowoya nthena Alice, m’malo mwake mwakukayikiska, apo wakakumbukanga unandi wa kusintha uko wakajumphamo zuŵa lira.

“Nkhani iyo yikwenera kuŵa nadi!” wakayowoya Nkhunda na mazgu ghakunyoza chomene. "Nkhawonapo ŵasungwana ŵachoko ŵanandi mu nyengo yane, kweni palije uyo wali na singo nga ni iyi! Yayi, yayi! Ndiwe njoka; ndipo palije candulo kukana. Nkhughanaghana kuti uzamuniphalira pamanyuma kuti undalaŵiskepo sumbi!"

Alice, uyo wakaŵa mwana wakuyowoya unenesko comene, wakati: “ Nalawa mazira, nadi; “kweni ŵasungwana ŵachoko ŵakurya mazira nga umo vikuchitira njoka, mukumanya.”

“Nkhugomezga yayi,” wakayowoya Nkhunda; “kweni usange ŵakuchita, chifukwa wuli ntheura iwo mba mtundu wa njoka, ndivyo pera ningayowoya.”

Iyi yikaŵa fundo yiphya comene kwa Alice, mwakuti wakakhala chete kwa mphindi yimoza panji ziŵiri, ico cikapereka mwaŵi ku Nkhunda kuti wasazgireko kuti, “Ukupenja mazira, nkhumanya makora nthena ; ndipo kasi nkhukhwaskika wuli usange ndiwe kamsungwana panji njoka?”

Alice wakati mwaluŵiro: “Nchakuzirwa comene kwa ine .” “kweni nkhupenja mazira yayi, nga umo vikuchitikira; ndipo usange ningaŵa, mphanyi nkhukhumba yayi ghako : nkhughatemwa yayi ghambura kuphika.”

“Kweni, fumapo, ntheura!” wakayowoya Nkhunda na mazgu ghakukwiya, apo yikakhazikikaso mu chisa chake. Alice wakagwada pakati pa makuni umo wangafiskira, cifukwa singo yake yikalutilira kujitimbanizga pakati pa nthambi, ndipo nyengo na nyengo wakakhumbikwiranga kwimilira na kuyifumiska. Pakati pajumpha nyengo wakakumbuka kuti wakaŵa kuti wachali na vigawa vya bowa mu mawoko ghake, ndipo wakamba kugwira ntchito mwakupwelelera chomene, wakamba kukhwaska chimoza ndipo pamanyuma pa chinyake, ndipo nyengo zinyake wakakura kuŵa mutali ndipo nyengo zinyake kuŵa mufupi, m’paka apo wakakwaniska kujikhizga ku msinkhu wake wa nyengo zose.

Pakajumpha nyengo yitali comene kufuma apo wakaŵirapo kanthu pafupi na kukura kwakwenelera, mwakuti pakwamba vikawonekanga vyakuzizika comene; kweni wakazgoŵera mu mphindi zichoko waka, ndipo wakamba kuyowoya yekha, nga umo wakachitiranga nyengo zose. "Zani, pali hafu ya pulani yane sono yamara! Kusintha kose uku nkhwakuzizika comene! Nkhumanya yayi kuti nizamuŵa wuli, kufuma pa mphindi yimoza kuya ku yinyake! Ndipouli, nawelera ku kukura kwane kwakwenelera: cinthu cakulondezgapo nchakuti, ninjire mu munda wakutowa uwo— kasi ivi vicitikenge wuli, nkhujifumba?" Apo wakayowoyanga ivi, wakafika mwamabuchi pa malo ghambura kanthu, ndipo mukaŵa nyumba yichoko iyo yikaŵa na msinkhu wa masentimita pafupifupi ghanayi. Alice wakaghanaghana kuti: “Waliyose uyo wakukhala kwenekuko, vingaŵawovwira yayi kuti ŵazakaŵepo pa ukuru uwu : chifukwa wuli, nkhwenera kuŵawofya kufuma mu maghanoghano ghawo!” Ntheura wakamba kukhweŵaso pa mzere wa ku woko lamalyero, ndipo wakayezgayezga yayi kuluta pafupi na nyumba m’paka apo wakajikhizga kufika pa msinkhu wa masentimita 9.
