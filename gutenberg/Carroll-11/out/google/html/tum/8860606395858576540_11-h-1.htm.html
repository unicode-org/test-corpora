MUTU I.
Kusi kwa Dzenje la Kalulu

Alice wakamba kuvuka comene cifukwa ca kukhala pafupi na mudumbu wake pa mtunda, ndiposo cifukwa ca kuŵavya cakucita: kamoza panji kaŵiri wakadodoliska mu buku ilo mudumbu wake wakaŵazganga, kweni mukaŵavya vithuzithuzi panji vyakudumbiskana, “ndipo kasi buku likugwira nchito wuli,” wakaghanaghana nthena Alice “kwambura vithuzithuzi panji vyakudumbiskana?”

Ntheura wakaghanaghaniranga mu maghanoghano ghake (nga umo wangafiskira, cifukwa zuŵa lakutentha likapangiska kuti wagonenge comene ndiposo wakupusa), usange cimwemwe ca kupanga daisy-chain cingaŵa cakwenelera suzgo la kuwuka na kutora daisy, apo mwamabuci Kalulu Mutuŵa wa maso gha pinki wakacimbilira pafupi nayo.

Pakaŵavya chinthu chakuzizika chomene mu fundo iyi; ndiposo Alice wakaghanaghananga kuti nchambura kwenelera yayi kupulika Kalulu wakuyowoya yekha kuti, “Oh wakutemweka! Oh wakutemweka! Nizamuchedwa!” (apo wakaghanaghanirapo pamanyuma, wakaghanaghana kuti wakwenera kuzizwa na ici, kweni pa nyengo iyo vyose vikawonekanga nga ni vyacilengiwa comene); kweni apo Kalulu wakafumiska nadi wotchi mu thumba lake la chingwe , na kuyilaŵiska, ndipo pamanyuma wakaluta mwaluŵiro, Alice wakamba kwimilira, chifukwa vikawoneka mu maghanoghano ghake kuti wandawonepo kalulu uyo ​​wali na thumba la chingwe, panji wotchi kuti wafumyemo, ndipo wakachimbira na, mwamwaŵi wakaŵa mu nyengo yake kuti wawone kuti wakuponya pasi pa mbuna yikuru ya kalulu pasi pa mpanda.

Mu nyengo yinyake pasi Alice wakaluta pamanyuma pake, nanga nkhughanaghanapo yayi umo mu caru wangacitira kuti wafumemoso.

Chibowo cha kalulu chikaluta panthazi mwakunyoloka nga ni msewu wa msewu kwa nthowa yinyake, ndipo pamanyuma chikanjira mwamabuchibuchi pasi, mwamabuchibuchi mwakuti Alice wakaŵavya nyengo yakughanaghanira vya kujiyimiska pambere wandajisange kuti wakuwa pasi pa chisimi chakuzika chomene.

Panji chisimi chikaŵa chakuzika chomene, panji wakawa pachokopachoko chomene, chifukwa wakaŵa na nyengo yinandi apo wakakhiranga kuti walaŵiske pafupi na kujifumba ivyo vichitikenge pamanyuma. Chakwamba, wakayezga kulaŵiska pasi na kumanya ivyo wakafikanga, kweni kukaŵa mdima chomene mwakuti wakawonanga kanthu yayi; ndipo wakalaŵiska ku mbali za chisimi, ndipo wakawona kuti vikazura na makabati na mashelufu gha mabuku; apa na apo wakawonanga mapu na vithuzithuzi ivyo vikaŵa pa vingwe. Wakakhizga chiŵiya pa mashelufu ghanyake apo wakajumphanga; likaŵa lakulembeka kuti “ORANGE MARMALADE”, kweni wakakhuŵara chomene kuti likaŵa lambura kanthu: wakatemwanga yayi kugweska chiŵiya ichi chifukwa cha wofi wakuti wangakoma munthu munyake pasi, ntheura wakakwaniska kuchiŵika mu kabati limoza apo wakawa pafupi nacho.

"Makola!" Alice wakaghanaghana nthena, "para nawa nga ni iyi, nizamughanaghana yayi vya kuwa pasi pa makwelero! Wose ŵazamuniwona kuti nili wakukhwima mtima chomene pa nyumba! Enya, ningayowoyapo yayi pa nkhani iyi, nanga ningawa kufuma pachanya pa nyumba!" (Icho chikwenera kuti chikaŵa chaunenesko chomene.)

Kusi, kusi, kusi. Kasi kuwa uku kukaŵa kuti kuzamumara yayi ? “Nkhujifumba kuti pa nyengo iyi nawa makilomita ghanandi wuli?” wakayowoya mwakukwezga. “Nkhwenera kuti nkhufika pa malo ghanyake pafupi na pakati pa caru capasi. Lekani niwone: ico cingaŵa mamailosi vikwi vinayi pasi, nkhughanaghana—” (pakuti, mukuwona, Alice wakasambira vinthu vinandi vya mtundu uwu mu masambiro ghake mu cipinda ca sukulu, ndipo nangauli uwu ukaŵa mwaŵi uwemi comene yayi wakulongolera cimanyisko cake, nga umo pakaŵavya munthu muwemi uyo wakacitanga pa mndandanda wake, kuti—) uwo uli pafupifupi mtunda wakwenelera—kweni pamanyuma nkhujifumba kuti niŵe na Latitude panji Longitude wuli?” (Alice wakamanyanga yayi kuti Latitude ni vichi, panji Longitude, kweni wakaghanaghananga kuti ni mazgu ghawemi ghakuyowoya.)

Pasono wakambaso. “Nkhujifumba usange ningawa kufuma mu charu chapasi! Vizamuwoneka vyakusekeska chomene kufuma pakati pa ŵanthu awo ŵakwenda na mitu yawo pasi! Ŵa Antipathies, nkhughanaghana kuti—” (wakakondwa chomene kuti pakaŵavya uyo wakapulikiranga, nyengo iyi, pakuti likapulikikwanga yayi lizgu lakukwana) “—but you know the Please is, “but you the Please have. Amama, kasi kuno ku New Zealand panji ku Australia?” (ndipo wakayezga kujikuzga apo wakayowoyanga— kujikuzga kwakukondweska apo mukuwa mu mlengalenga! Kasi mukughanaghana kuti mungakwaniska?) “Ndipo kasi ni msungwana muchoko wambura kumanya wuli uyo wazamunighanaghanira chifukwa cha kufumba! Yayi, vingachitika yayi kufumba: panji nizamuwona kuti vyalembeka pa malo ghanyake.”

Kusi, kusi, kusi. Pakaŵavya chinyake chakuchita, ntheura mwaluŵiro Alice wakambaso kuyowoya. “Dinah wazamunisoŵa chomene usiku uno, nkhwenera kughanaghana nthena!” (Dinah ndiyo wakaŵa mphaka.) "Nkhugomezga kuti ŵazamukumbuka mbale yake ya mkaka pa nyengo ya tiyi. Dinah wakutemweka wane! Nkhukhumba kuti muŵe pasi pano na ine! Mu mlengalenga kulije mbeŵa, nkhuwopa, kweni mungakora mtundu wa mtundu, ndipo ichi chili nga ni mbeŵa, mukumanya. Kweni kasi amphaka ŵakurya mtundu wa mtundu, nkhujifumba?" Ndipo apa Alice wakamba kugona m’malo mwake, ndipo wakalutilira kujiphalira yekha, mu nthowa ya maloto, “Kasi amphaka ŵakurya mileme? Kasi amphaka ŵakurya mileme?” ndipo nyengo zinyake, “Kasi mileme yikurya amphaka?” pakuti, mukuwona, nga umo wakatondeka kuzgora fumbo lililose, cikaŵa cakuzirwa comene yayi kuti wakaŵika mu nthowa wuli. Wakapulika kuti wakugona, ndipo wakamba waka kulota kuti wakwenda pamoza na Dina, ndipo wakamuphaliranga mwakujipeleka chomene kuti, “Sono, Dina, niphalirani unenesko: kasi muli kuryapo mtundu wa mtundu?” apo mwamabuchibuchi, thump! thump! pasi wakafika pa mulu wa makuni na mahamba ghakuwomira, ndipo kuwa kukamara.

Alice wakapwetekeka yayi nanga nkhuchoko waka, ndipo wakajumphilira pa malundi ghake mu kanyengo kachoko waka: wakalaŵiska kuchanya, kweni vyose vikaŵa mdima pachanya; panthazi pake pakaŵa nthowa yinyake yitali, ndipo Kalulu Mutuŵa wakawonekangaso, wakakhiranga mwaluŵiro. Pakaŵavya nyengo yakuti watayike: Alice wakaluta kutali nga ni mphepo, ndipo wakaŵa pa nyengo yake kuti wapulike kuti, apo wakazgoka pakona, “Oh makutu ghane na ndevu, nyengo yikuchedwa chomene!” Wakaŵa pafupi kumanyuma kwake apo wakazgoka pakona, kweni Kalulu wakawonekangaso yayi: wakajisanga kuti wali mu holo yitali, yakucepa, iyo yikaŵaliranga na mzere wa nyali izo zikaŵanga pa mtenje.

Pakaŵa milyango yose kuzingilizga holo, kweni yose yikaŵa yakujalirika; ndipo apo Alice wakaŵa kuti wafika pasi ku chigaŵa chimoza na kukwera ku chinyake, kuyezga chipata chilichose, wakendanga na chitima pasi pakati, wakujifumba umo wangachitira kuti wafumemoso.

Mwamabuchibuchi wakasanga thebulu lichoko la malundi ghatatu, lose likapangika na galasi lakukhora; pakaŵavya kanthu pa iyo kupatulapo kiyi wa golide muchoko waka, ndipo fundo yakwamba iyo Alice wakaghanaghana yikaŵa yakuti yingaŵa ya milyango yinyake ya holo; kweni, soka! panji maloko ghakaŵa ghakuru comene, panji kiyi wakaŵa mucoko comene, kweni mulimose umo vingaŵira, wakajuranga yayi cimoza mwa vikozgo ivi. Kweni, pa nyengo yachiŵiri, wakasanga katani yapasi iyo wakayiwonapo yayi pakwamba, ndipo kumanyuma kwake kukaŵa chipata chichoko icho chikaŵa na msinkhu wa masentimita pafupifupi khumi na ghankhondi: wakayezga kiyi muchoko wa golide uyo wakaŵa mu loko, ndipo wakakondwa chomene kuti wakakwana!

Alice wakajura mulyango ndipo wakasanga kuti ukanjiranga mu msewu ucoko, uwo ukaŵa ukuru comene yayi kuluska mbuna ya mbeŵa: wakagwada na kulaŵiska mu msewu uwo ukaŵa mu munda wakutowa comene uwo muli kuwonapo. Wakakhumbanga chomene kufumamo mu holo ya mdima yira, na kuzungulirazungulira pakati pa mabedi gha maluŵa ghakuwala na visimi vyakuzizima ivi, kweni wakatondeka nanga nkhunjizga mutu wake pa mulyango; "ndipo nanga mutu wane ungajumpha," wakaghanaghana nthena Alice mukavu, "ungaŵa wakukhumbikwa viŵi yayi kwambura mapewa ghane. O, nkhukhumba chomene kuti ningakhala chete nga ni telesikopu! Nkhughanaghana kuti ningakwaniska, usange ningamanya waka kwamba." Pakuti, mukuwona, vinthu vinandi vyambura kumanyikwa vikacitika sonosono apa, mwakuti Alice wakamba kughanaghana kuti vinthu vicoko comene ndivyo vikaŵa nadi vyambura kucitika.

Pakawonekanga kuti palije candulo kulindilira pafupi na mulyango ucoko, ntheura wakawelera ku thebulu, hafu na cigomezgo cakuti wangasanga kiyi munyake pa thebulu, panji mulimose umo vingaŵira buku la malango ghakujalira ŵanthu nga ni matelesikopu: nyengo iyi wakasanga botolo licoko pa ilo, (“ilo nadi likaŵako yayi pano pakwamba,” wakayowoya Alice,) ndipo wakazungulira laper the words. “NIMWANI,” pakalembeka makora na malembo ghakurughakuru.

Vyose vikaŵa viwemi comene kuyowoya kuti “Nimwe,” kweni Alice mucoko wavinjeru wakakhumbanga kucita nthena mwaluŵiro yayi. Iyo wakati: “Yayi, nizamulaŵiska dankha, na kuwona usange pali chimanyikwiro chakuti ‘ poizoni ’ panji yayi”; pakuti wakaŵazga mbiri zichokozichoko ziwemi zinandi za ŵana awo ŵakawotcheka, na kuryeka na vikoko vya mu thengere na vinthu vinyake vyambura kukondwa, vyose ivi chifukwa chakuti ŵangakumbuka yayi malango ghapusu agho ŵabwezi ŵawo ŵakaŵasambizga: nga ni, kuti poker wakutentha wakufipa wazamukuwotcha usange ungamukora nyengo yitali chomene; ndiposo kuti usange mwadumura chara chinu mwakuya chomene na mpeni, kanandi chikufumiska ndopa; ndipo wakaluwapo yayi kuti, usange mwamwa vinandi kufuma mu botolo ilo palembeka kuti “poizoni,” nchakusuzga yayi kukolerana namwe, mwaluŵiro panji mwaluŵiro.

Ndipouli, botolo ili likaŵavya chimanyikwiro chakuti “poizoni,” ntheura Alice wakayezgayezga kulilawa, ndipo wakati wasanga kuti likaŵa liwemi chomene, (likaŵa na mtundu wakusazgako wa cherry-tart, custard, pine-apple, nkhuku yakuwotcha, toffee, na toast wakuwotcha,) mwaluŵiro wakamalizga.

“Nkhukhumba chomene kumanya vinandi!” wakayowoya Alice; “Nkhwenera kuti nkhukhala chete nga ni telesikopu.”

Ndipo ndimo vikaŵira nadi: sono wakaŵa na msinkhu wa masentimita khumi pera, ndipo cisko cake cikaŵala comene pa kughanaghana kuti sono wakaŵa wakukwana kuti wajumphe pa mulyango ucoko na kunjira mu munda wakutowa uwo. Kweni cakwamba, wakalindilira kwa maminiti ghacoko waka kuti wawone usange wazamucepaso: wakaŵa na wofi pacoko waka pa nkhani iyi; "pakuti vingamara, mukumanya," Alice wakayowoya yekha, "mu kufuma kwane kwakukwana, nga ni kandulo. Nkhujifumba kuti nkhwenera kuŵa wuli nyengo iyo?" Ndipo wakayezgayezga kughanaghana umo moto wa kandulo ukuŵira para kandulo yazimwa, cifukwa wakakumbukanga yayi kuti wakawonapo cinthu canthena.

Pakati pajumpha nyengo, apo wakawona kuti palije chinyake icho chikachitika, wakaghanaghana kuti walute mu munda nyengo yeneyiyo; kweni, soka kwa Alice mukavu! apo wakafika pa mulyango, wakasanga kuti wali kuluwa kiyi muchoko wa golide, ndipo apo wakawelera ku thebulu kuti wasange, wakasanga kuti wangakwaniska yayi kufikapo: wakawona makora chomene kwizira mu galasi, ndipo wakayezgayezga chomene kukwera pachanya pa lundi limoza la thebulu, kweni likaŵa lakutelera chomene; ndipo wakati wavuka na kuyezgayezga, chinthu chichoko chikavu chikakhala pasi na kulira.

“Zani, palije candulo kulira nthena!” wakayowoya Alice kwa iyo yekha, m’malo mwake mwakuthwa; “Nkhumuphalirani kuti muleke mphindi iyi!” Kanandi wakajipelekanga ulongozgi uwemi comene, (nangauli wakawulondezganga mwakusoŵa comene), ndipo nyengo zinyake wakajikalipiranga comene mwakuti masozi ghakafumanga mu maso ghake; ndipo nyengo yinyake wakakumbuka kuti wakayezga kuŵika makutu ghake mu bokosi chifukwa chakuti wakajipusika pa maseŵero gha croquet agho wakaseŵeranga nayo, chifukwa mwana wakukhumba kumanya vinandi uyu wakatemwanga chomene kujiyezgelera kuŵa ŵanthu ŵaŵiri. Alice mukavu wakaghanaghana kuti: “Kweni sono palije candulo, kujiyezgelera kuŵa ŵanthu ŵaŵiri !

Mwaluŵiro jiso lake likawira pa kabokosi kachoko ka galasi ako kakagona pasi pa thebulu: wakakajura, ndipo wakasangamo keke yichoko chomene, iyo pakalembeka mazgu ghakuti “NDIRYANI” makora chomene mu vipambi vya currant. Alice wakati: “Kweni, niryenge, ndipo usange vinganipangiska kuti niŵe mukuru, ningafika pa kiyi; ndipo usange vinganipangiska kuti niŵe muchoko, ningakhwaŵa pasi pa mulyango; ntheura mu nthowa yiliyose nizamunjira mu munda, ndipo nkhuŵikako mahara yayi icho chizamuchitika!”

Wakarya pachoko waka, ndipo wakajiphalira yekha mwakukhwaskika kuti, “Nthowa wuli? Nthowa wuli?”, wakakora woko lake pachanya pa mutu wake kuti wapulike nthowa iyo yikukura, ndipo wakazizwa chomene kuwona kuti wakalutilira kuŵa wakuyana waka: kuti tiŵe ŵakusimikizga, ivi kanandi vikuchitika para munthu wakurya keke, kweni Alice wakaŵa kuti wafumapo chomene. kuchitika, kuti chikawonekanga chakutondeka chomene ndiposo chambura mahara kuti umoyo ulutilire mu nthowa ya ŵanthu wose.

Ntheura wakamba kugwira nchito, ndipo mwaluŵiro wakamalizga keke.
