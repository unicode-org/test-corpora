MUTU XII.
Ukaboni wa Alice

"Pano!" Alice wakachemerezga, kuluwa chomene mu nyengo iyo wakakulira mu mphindi zichoko waka zakumasinda, ndipo wakajumphilira mwaluŵiro chomene mwakuti wakakhuŵara na bokosi la ŵamazaza na mphepete mwa siketi yake, ndipo wakakhuŵazga ŵamazaza wose pa mitu ya mzinda uwo ukaŵa pasi, ndipo apo ŵakagona pafupi na iyo. mwangozi wakakwiya sabata iyo yikaŵa pambere wandaŵepo.

“Oh, nkhuŵeyani kuti munigowokere!” wakachemerezga na mazgu ghakukhuŵara chomene, ndipo wakamba kuvitoraso mwaluŵiro umo wangafiskira, chifukwa ngozi ya somba ya golide yikalutilira kuchimbira mu mutu wake, ndipo wakaŵa na mtundu wa fundo yambura kupulikikwa makora yakuti vikwenera kutoleka nyengo yeneyiyo na kuŵikikaso mu bokosi la jury, panji vifwenge.

“Kweruzga kungalutilira yayi,” Themba likayowoya na mazgu ghakuŵinya comene, “mpaka apo ŵalara wose ŵa khoti ŵawelere ku malo ghawo ghakwenelera— wose ,” wakawerezgapo mwakusimikizga comene, ndipo wakamulaŵiska comene Alice apo wakayowoyanga nthena.

Alice wakalaŵiska pa jury-box, ndipo wakawona kuti, mu luŵiro lwake, wakaŵika Lizard mu mutu pasi, ndipo chinthu chichoko chikavu chikasunkhunyanga mchira wake mu nthowa ya chitima, kuŵa wakutondeka chomene kwenda. Mwaluŵiro wakawufumiskaso, na kuwuŵika makora; “kuti likung’anamura vinandi yayi,” wakayowoya yekha; “Nkhwenera kughanaghana kuti cingaŵa cakovwira comene pa ciyezgo nthowa yimoza yakukwerera nga ni yinyake.”

Jury yikati yachira waka chifukwa cha kukhuŵara, ndipo ma slate ghawo na mapensulo ghawo ghakasangika na kuŵapeleka kwa iwo, ŵakamba kugwira ntchito mwamwamphu chomene kuti ŵalembe mbiri ya ngozi iyi, wose kupatulapo Lizard, uyo wakawonekanga kuti wathereskeka chomene mwakuti wangachita chilichose yayi kweni wakakhala waka na mulomo wake wakujulika.

“Kasi mukumanya vichi vya bizinesi iyi?” Themba likati kwa Alice.

Alice wakati: “Palije kanthu.

“Palije kanthu? ” Themba likalutilira.

Alice wakati: “Palije kanthu kalikose.”

Themba likazgokera kwa ŵalara ŵa khoti kuti: “Ici nchakuzirwa comene.” Ŵakamba waka kulemba ivi pa viphapha vyawo, apo Kalulu Mutuŵa wakanjilirapo kuti: “ Un important, your Majesty means, of course,” wakayowoya mu mazgu ghakuchindika chomene, kweni wakamukhuŵazga na kumuchitira visko apo wakayowoyanga.

“ Un wakuzirwa, nadi, nkhang’anamuranga,” Themba likayowoya mwaluŵiro, ndipo likalutilira kuyowoya mwakujiyuyura, .

“chakuzirwa—chambura kuzirwa—chambura kuzirwa—chakuzirwa—” nga ni para wakuyezga lizgu ilo likupulikikwa makora.

Ŵanyake awo ŵakaŵa mu khoti ŵakalemba kuti “nchakuzirwa,” ndipo ŵanyake “nchakuzirwa yayi.” Alice wakawona ici, apo wakaŵa pafupi comene kuti walaŵiske pa viphapha vyawo; “kweni palije kanthu nanga nkhuchoko waka,” wakaghanaghana nthena.

Pa nyengo iyi Themba, ilo kwa nyengo yitali likaŵa kuti latangwanika kulemba mu buku lake, likachemerezga kuti “Chete!” ndipo wakaŵazga kufuma mu buku lake lakuti, “Dango Makumi ghanayi na ghaŵiri. Ŵanthu wose awo ŵali na mtunda wakujumpha mailosi yimoza ŵafumemo mu khoti .”

Waliyose wakamulaŵiska Alice.

Alice wakati: “ Nkhuŵa mtunda wa mailosi yayi.

Themba likati: “Ndimwe.”

Fumukazi yikasazgapo kuti: “Pafupifupi mamailosi ghaŵiri kutali.”

Alice wakati: “Kweni, nizamuluta yayi, nanga vili nthena, padera pa ivi, ilo ni dango la nyengo zose yayi: ndimwe mukalipanga sonosono apa.”

Themba likati: “Ni dango lakale chomene mu buku ili.”

Alice wakati: “Para ndimo viliri, yikwenera kuŵa Nambara Yakwamba.”

Themba likazgoka lakufipa, ndipo likajara buku lake la noti mwaluŵiro. Wakaphalira ŵalara ŵa khoti kuti: “Wonani ivyo mwadumura.”

“Pali ukaboni unyake uwo uzamuŵapo, chonde Fumu yinu,” wakayowoya Kalulu Mutuŵa, wakujumphilira pachanya mwaluŵiro chomene; “pepala ili latoleka waka.”

“Kasi muli vichi?” wakayowoya Fumukazi.

“Nkhajurapo yayi,” wakayowoya Kalulu Mutuŵa, “kweni vikuwoneka kuti ni kalata, iyo mukayidi wakalemba kwa—kwa munthu munyake.”

“Vikwenera kuti vikaŵa nthena,” wakayowoya Themba, “kupaturako para vikalembeka kwa waliyose yayi, ivyo ni vyambura kumanyikwa, mukumanya.”

“Kasi likulongozgeka kwa njani?” wakayowoya nthena yumoza wa ŵalara ŵa khoti.

“Likulongozgeka yayi nanga nkhumoza,” wakayowoya nthena Kalulu Mutuŵa; “nakuti, kuwaro kulije kanthu kakulembeka .” Wakajura pepara apo wakayowoyanga, ndipo wakasazgapo kuti “Ni kalata yayi, pakuti: ni gulu la mavesi.”

“Kasi vili mu mawoko gha mukayidi?” wakafumba munyake wa ŵalara ŵa khoti.

“Yayi, ndimo viliri yayi,” wakayowoya Kalulu Mutuŵa, “ndipo ichi nchinthu chakuzizika chomene pa nkhani iyi.” (Jury yose yikawonekanga kuti yikuzizwa.)

Themba likati: “Wakwenera kuti wakayezga woko la munthu munyake.” (Jury yose yikaŵalaso.)

“Chonde Fumu yinu,” wakayowoya nthena Knave, “ine nkhalemba yayi, ndipo ŵangakhozgera yayi kuti nkhalemba: palije zina ilo lasayinirika kuumaliro.”

Themba likati: “Usange mukasayinira yayi, ivi vikupangiska kuti nkhani yiŵe yiheni chomene. Mukwenera kuti mukaŵa kuti mukukhumba kuchita vinthu viheni, panji mphanyi mukasayinira zina linu nga ni munthu waunenesko.”

Pakaŵa kuwomba mawoko kwa ŵantu wose pa ici: cikaŵa cinthu cakwamba ca mahara nadi ico Themba likayowoya zuŵa lira.

Fumukazi yikati: “Ici cikulongora kuti wali na mulandu.”

“Likukhozgera kanthu yayi ka mtundu uwu!” wakayowoya nthena Alice. “Kasi, mukumanya yayi nanga ni ivyo ŵakuyowoya!”

Themba likati: “Ŵazgani.”

Kalulu Mutuŵa wakavwara magalasi ghake. “Kasi nimbe nkhu, nkhukondweska Fumu yinu?” wakafumba.

Themba likayowoya mwakujikuzga kuti: “Yambani pakwamba, ndipo lutilirani m’paka mukafike ku umaliro: ndipo pamanyuma muleke.”

Mavesi agha ghakaŵa agho Kalulu Mutuŵa wakaŵazga:—

“Ŵakaniphalira kuti mukaŵa kwa iyo, .
Ndipo wakanizunura kwa iyo:
Wakanipa nkharo yiwemi, .
Kweni wakati nkhumanya yayi kusambira.

Wakaŵatumira mazgu kuti nindalute .
(Tikumanya kuti ni vyaunenesko):
Usange wakwenera kukankhira nkhani iyi panthazi, .
Kasi imwe mukaŵanga wuli?

Nkhamupa chimoza, iwo ŵakamupa viŵiri, .
Mukatipa ŵatatu panji ŵanandi;
Wose ŵakawelera kwa imwe kufuma kwa iyo, .
Nangauli ŵakaŵa ŵane pakwamba.

Usange ine panji iyo wangaŵa na mwaŵi wakuŵa
Kunjilirapo pa nkhani iyi, .
Wakugomezga imwe kuti muŵafwature, .
Nga umo tikaŵira nadi.

Fundo yane yikaŵa yakuti mukaŵa .
(Pambere wandaŵe na suzgo ili)
Chinthu chakukanizga icho chikaŵa pakati pa .
Iyo, na ise taŵene, na iyo.

Lekani kumumanyiska kuti wakaŵatemwanga chomene,
Pakuti ichi chikwenera kuŵapo nyengo zose .
Chinsinsi, chakusungika kwa wose ŵanyake, .
Pakati pa iwe na ine.”

“Uwu ndiwo ukaboni wakuzirwa comene uwo tapulikapo,” wakayowoya nthena Themba uku likusisitira mawoko ghake; “ntheura sono ŵamazaza ŵa khoti—”

“Usange waliyose wa iwo wangalongosora,” wakayowoya nthena Alice, (wakakura comene mu mphindi zicoko waka zakumasinda mwakuti wakawopanga yayi kumutimbanizga), “Nimupenge sixpence. Nkhugomezga yayi kuti pali atomu ya ng’anamuro mu fundo iyi.”

Ŵanthu wose awo ŵakadumura mulandu uwu ŵakalemba pa viphapha vyawo kuti, “ Wakugomezga yayi kuti pali atomu ya ng’anamuro,” kweni palije uyo wakayezga kulongosora pepara ili.

“Usange palije ng’anamuro mu iyo,” wakayowoya Themba, “icho chikuponoska charu cha masuzgo, mukumanya, nga umo tikukhumbikwira yayi kuyezgayezga kusanga chilichose. Ndipouli nkhumanya yayi,” wakalutilira, wakatandazga mavesi pa bondo lake, na kughalaŵiska na jiso limoza; “Nkhuwoneka kuti nkhuwona ng’anamuro linyake mwa iwo, pakuti. “— wakati nkhumanya yayi kusambira —” imwe mukumanya yayi kusambira, asi nthena?” wakasazgirapo, ndipo wakazgokera kwa Knave.

Knave wakayuyura mutu wake na chitima. “Kasi nkhuwoneka nga ndiyo?” wakayowoya nthena. (Icho wakachita nadi yayi , chifukwa chikapangika chose na makatoni.)

“Chakwenelera, mpaka sono,” wakayowoya Themba, ndipo wakalutilira kujifumba kuti: “’ Tikumanya kuti nchaunenesko —’ ndiyo jury, nadi—’ Nkhamupa yimoza, iwo ŵakamupa ziŵiri —’ chifukwa wuli, ndivyo vikwenera kuŵa ivyo wakachita na tarts, mukumanya—”

“Kweni, vikulutilira ‘ wose ŵakawelera kufuma kwa iyo kwa imwe ,’” wakayowoya nthena Alice.

“Enya, apo ŵali!” wakayowoya Themba mwakucindikika, uku wakulongora ma tart agho ghakaŵa pa thebulu. “Palije ico cingaŵa cakupulikikwa makora kuluska ici . Ndiposo—’ pambere wandaŵe na nthenda iyi —’ imwe mukaŵavya nthenda iyi, mutemweka wane, nkhughanaghana nthena?” wakayowoya kwa Fumukazi.

"Yayi!" wakayowoya Fumukazi mwakukwiya, ndipo wakaponya inkstand pa Lizard apo wakayowoyanga. (Bill muchoko wa soka wakaleka kulemba pa slate yake na chara chimoza, apo wakasanga kuti palije chimanyikwiro; kweni sono wakambaso mwaluŵiro, kugwiliskira ntchito inki, iyo yikakhiranga pasi pa chisko chake, nyengo yitali iyo yikakhalilira.)

“Para nthena mazgu ghakumukwanani yayi,” wakayowoya Themba, uku wakulaŵiska kuzingilizga khoti na kumwemwetera. Pakaŵa chete chakufwa.

“Ni ntharika!” Themba likasazgapo na mazgu ghakukwiya, ndipo wose ŵakaseka kuti, “Lekani ŵalara ŵa khoti ŵawone ivyo ŵadumura.” Themba likati, pafupifupi nyengo ya makumi ghaŵiri zuŵa ilo.

“Yayi, yayi!” wakayowoya Fumukazi. “Sentensi yakwamba—chigamulo pamanyuma.”

“Vinthu na vyambura ng’anamuro!” wakayowoya mwakukwezga Alice. “Maghanoghano ghakuti sentensi yiŵe pakwamba!”

“Khalani lulimi lwinu!” wakayowoya Fumukazi, ndipo wakazgoka kuŵa wa pepo.

“Nichitenge yayi!” wakayowoya nthena Alice.

“Off na mutu wake!” Fumukazi yikachemerezga na mazgu ghake ghapachanya. Palije uyo wakasunkhunyika.

“Kasi ni njani uyo wakumupwelelerani?” wakayowoya Alice, (wakakura na kufika pa kukura kwake kose pa nyengo iyi). “Ndiwe chinthu chinyake yayi kweni paketi ya makadi!”

Pa nyengo iyi gulu lose likawuka mu mlengalenga, ndipo likawuluka pasi pa iyo: wakachemerezga pachoko, hafu ya wofi na hafu ya ukali, ndipo wakayezga kuŵatimba, ndipo wakajisanga kuti wagona pa mtunda, na mutu wake mu chiŵegha cha mudumbu wake, uyo wakawuskapo mwakuzika mahamba ghake ghakufwa pasi pa nkhope yake kufuma ku vikuni.

“Wuka, Alice wakutemweka wane!” wakayowoya mudumbu wake; “Kasi, mwagona nyengo yitali chomene!”

“Oh, nkhaŵa na maloto ghakukhumba kumanya vinandi chomene!” wakayowoya Alice, ndipo wakaphalira mudumbu wake, nga umo wangakumbukira, vyose ivi vyakuzizwiska vya Adventures vyake ivyo mwaŵazga waka; ndipo wakati wamalizga, mudumbu wake wakamufyofyontha, ndipo wakati, “ Likaŵa maloto ghakukhumba kumanya vinandi, wakutemweka, nadi: kweni sono chimbilira ku tiyi wako; nyengo yikuchedwa.” Ntheura Alice wakawuka na kucimbira, wakughanaghana apo wakacimbiranga, nga umo wangacitira, kuti likaŵa maloto ghakukondweska comene.

Kweni mudumbu wake wakakhala waka chete apo wakamuleka, wakasendezga mutu wake pa woko lake, wakalaŵiskanga zuŵa ilo likanjiranga, na kughanaghanirapo za Alice muchoko na Adventures zake zose zakuzizwiska, m’paka apo nayo wakamba kulota mwakuyana na mafashoni, ndipo ili likaŵa maloto ghake:—

Chakwamba, wakalotanga za Alice muchoko yekha, ndipo kachiŵiri mawoko ghachokoghachoko ghakakolerana pa bondo lake, ndipo maso ghakuwala ghakukhumba chomene ghakalaŵiskanga pachanya mu maso ghake—wakapulikanga mazgu ghake, na kuwona kuti mutu wake ukaŵa wakuziziswa chomene kuti walekeske sisi lakuzungulira ilo likaŵanga kuti likunjira mu maso ghake nyengo zose, panji uyo wakawoneranga mndandanda wa maso ghake. malo ghakuzingilizga iyo ghakaŵa ghamoyo na vinyama vyakuzizika ivyo mudumbu wake muchoko wakalotanga.

Utheka utali ukaŵa pasi pa malundi ghake apo Kalulu Mutuŵa wakajumphanga mwaluŵiro—Mbeŵa iyo yikaŵa na wofi yikakhwaska nthowa yake mu chiziŵa chapafupi—wakapulikanga kulira kwa makapu gha tiyi apo Kalulu wa Malichi na ŵabwezi ŵake ŵakagaŵananga chakurya chawo chambura kumara, ndipo mazgu ghakuchemerezga gha Fumukazi ghakuchemerezga kuti waleke kuchita nthena— kukhweŵa pa bondo la Duchess, apo mbale na mbale vikakhuŵaranga kuzingilizga—kachiŵiri kulira kwa Gryphon, kukhweŵa kwa pensulo ya slate ya Lizard, na kukhoma kwa nkhumba zakuponderezgeka, vikazura mu mlengalenga, kusakanizgika na kulira kwa mick miser kwa kutali kwa Turtle.

Ntheura wakakhalapo, na maso ghakujalika, ndipo hafu wakagomezganga yekha kuti wali mu Wonderland, nangauli wakamanya kuti wakwenera kuvijuraso, ndipo vyose vizamusintha na kuŵa vyaunenesko vyambura kupulikikwa—utheka uzamuŵa waka wakusunkhunyika mu mphepo, ndipo chiziŵa chizamusunkhunyika ku kusunkhunyika kwa mafunde—makapu gha tiyi ghakusunkhunyika ghazamusintha na kukhuŵara na kukhuŵara, wa msepuka wa muliska—na kulira kwa mwana, kulira kwa Gryphon, na kulira kwanyake kose kwakuziziswa, kukasinthiranga (wakamanya) ku kulira kwakusokonezgeka kwa pa famu-yadi yakutangwanika—apo kulira kwa ng’ombe kutali kukatenge kutore malo gha kulira kwakuzizima kwa Mock Turtle.

Paumaliro, wakajiwoneskanga yekha umo mudumbu wake muchoko uyu, mu nyengo yakulondezgapo, wazamuŵira yekha mwanakazi wakukura; ndipo umo wakasungiranga, mu virimika vyake vyose vyakukhwima, mtima wake wambura kusuzga na wacitemwa wa ubwana wake: na umo wakawungananga pafupi na ŵana ŵake ŵanyake ŵacoko, na kupangiska maso ghawo kuŵa ghakuwala na kukhumba na nkhani zinandi zakuzizika, panji nanga ni na maloto gha Wonderland gha kale: ndipo umo wakajipulikiranga na citima cawo cose cambura kusuzga, na kusanga cimwemwe cose cambura kusuzga mu umoyo wa ŵana, na mazuŵa ghakukondweska gha chilimwe.
