MUTU III.
Mpikisano wa Caucus na Nkhani Yitali

Ŵakaŵa nadi gulu lakuwoneka lakuziziswa ilo likawungananga pa mtunda—viyuni ivyo vikaŵa na nthenga zakukoleka, vinyama ivyo vikaŵa na weya wawo uwo ukaŵa pafupi navyo, ndipo vyose vikadonthanga maji, vyakukhwaska, ndiposo vyambura kujipulika makora.

Fumbo lakwamba nadi likaŵa lakuti, kasi ŵangawumaso wuli: ŵakadumbiskana pa nkhani iyi, ndipo pamanyuma pa mphindi zichoko waka vikawoneka kuti ni vyachilengiwa chomene kwa Alice kuti wajisange kuti wakuyowoya nawo mwakumanyikwa, nga kuti wakuŵamanya umoyo wake wose. Nadi, wakasuskana nyengo yitali comene na Lory, uyo paumaliro wakazgoka wakukwiya, ndipo wakayowoyanga waka kuti, “Ndine mulara kuluska imwe, ndipo nkhwenera kumanya makora;” ndipo ici Alice wakazomerezganga yayi kwambura kumanya kuti cikaŵa na vilimika wuli, ndipo, nga umo Lory wakakanira nadi kuyowoya vilimika vyake, pakaŵavya vinyake vyakuti viyowoyeke.

Paumaliro Mbeŵa, iyo yikawonekanga nga ni munthu wa mazaza pakati pawo, yikachema kuti, "Khalani pasi, mose, ndipo munipulikire! Sonosono apa nizamumupangiskani kuti muŵe ŵakuuma mwakukwana!" Wose ŵakakhala pasi nyengo yimoza, mu mphete yikuru, ndipo pakati pakaŵa Mbeŵa. Alice wakalaŵiskanga chomene pa iyo mwakukhwaskika, chifukwa wakawona kuti wazamukoleka na chimphepo chikuru usange wangauma mwaluŵiro yayi.

“Ehe!” wakayowoya Mbeŵa na mpweya wakuzirwa, "kasi mose muli ŵakunozgeka? Ichi ndicho chinthu chakuwomira chomene icho nkhumanya. Kukhala chete kuzingilizga, usange mungatemwa! 'William Muwusi, uyo chifukwa chake chikakondweskeka na papa, mwaluŵiro wakajilambika ku Ŵangelezi, awo ŵakakhumbanga ŵalongozgi, ndipo ŵakaŵa kuti ŵali kuzgoŵera chomene kupoka vinthu vya Edquer na Morcia na Mercia. Northumbria—’”

"Ugh!" wakayowoya Lory, na kuthukira.

“Nkhumuŵeyani kuti munigowokere!” wakayowoya Mbeŵa, wakukhoma misozi, kweni mwaulemu comene: “Kasi mukayowoya?”

“Ine yayi!” wakayowoya Lory mwaluŵiro.

“Nkhaghanaghananga kuti mwachita,” wakayowoya nthena Mbeŵa. “—Nkhulutilira. ‘Edwin na Morcar, ŵalongozgi ŵa ku Mercia na Northumbria, ŵakapharazga chifukwa cha iyo: ndipo nanga ni Stigand, bishopu mulara wa ku Canterbury uyo wakatemwanga chomene charu chake, wakawona kuti ntchiwemi—’”

“Wasanga vichi ?” wakayowoya Bakha.

“Found it ,” Mbeŵa yikazgora mwakudumura: “nadi mukumanya ico ‘icho’ cikung’anamura.”

“Nkhumanya makora ivyo ‘icho’ chikung’anamura, para nasanga chinthu,” wakayowoya nthena Bakha: “kanandi ni chule panji mphutsi. Fumbo ndakuti, kasi bishopu mulara wakasanga vichi?”

Mbeŵa yikawona yayi fumbo ili, kweni mwaluŵiro yikalutilira kuti, “’—yikawona kuti ntchiwemi kuluta na Edgar Atheling kukakumana na William na kumupa mphumphu. likalutilira, likazgokera kwa Alice apo likayowoyanga.

Alice wakati na mazgu ghakuŵinya: “Nga umo vikuŵira nyengo zose, vikuwoneka kuti vikuniwumira yayi nanga nkhuchoko waka.”

“Pa nyengo iyi,” Dodo wakayowoya mwakujikuzga, ndipo wakawuka na kwimilira, “nkhukhumba kuti ungano ulekeskeke, kuti ŵazomerezge mwaluŵiro nthowa zinandi zakucizgira nkhongono—”

“Yowoyani Chingelezi!” wakayowoya Chiomba nkhanga. “Nkhumanya yayi ng’anamuro la hafu ya mazgu ghatali agha, ndiposo, ico nchakuti, nkhugomezga kuti namwe mukumanya yayi!” Ndipo Chiomba nkhanga chikaŵeyamika mutu wake kuti chibiske kumwemwetera: viyuni vinyake vinyake vikachemerezga mwakupulikika.

“Ico nkhakhumbanga kuyowoya,” wakayowoya nthena Dodo na mazgu ghakukwiya, “cikaŵa cakuti, cinthu ciwemi comene cakuti tiŵe ŵakuuma ciŵenge ca Caucus-race.”

“Kasi mtundu wa Caucus ni vichi?” wakayowoya Alice; kuti wakakhumbanga kumanya vinandi yayi, kweni Dodo wakakhala waka chete nga wakughanaghana kuti munthu munyake wakwenera kuyowoya, ndipo pakaŵavya munyake uyo wakawonekanga kuti wakukhumba kuyowoya chilichose.

Dodo wakati: “Cifukwa wuli, nthowa yiwemi comene yakucilongosolera njakuti ticite.” (Ndipo, nga umo mungatemwera kuyezga chinthu ichi mwekha, zuŵa linyake la nyengo ya chiwuvi, nizamumuphalirani umo ŵa Dodo ŵakachichitira.)

Cakwamba cikaŵika cimanyikwiro pa msewu wakucitirapo maseŵero gha kucimbira, mu mtundu wa mzere wakuzungulira, (“kawonekero keneco kakukhwaska yayi,” cikati,) ndipo pamanyuma pake gulu lose likaŵikika pafupi na msewu, apa na apo. Pakaŵavya “Yumoza, ŵaŵiri, ŵatatu, na kutali,” kweni ŵakambanga kucimbira para ŵakukhumba, ndipo ŵakaleka para ŵakukhumba, mwakuti cikaŵa cipusu yayi kumanya para ciphalizgano camara. Ndipouli, apo ŵakaŵa kuti ŵacimbira kwa theka la ora panji kujumphirapo, ndipo ŵakaŵa kuti ŵaumaso comene, mwamabuci Dodo wakacema kuti “Ciphalizgano camara!” ndipo wose ŵakawungana kuzingilizga, ŵakufunya mzimu, ndipo ŵakafumba kuti, “Kweni ni njani uyo wawina?”

Fumbo ili Dodo wakatondeka kulizgora kwambura kughanaghanirapo comene, ndipo wakakhala nyengo yitali na cala cimoza cakucicizga pa cisko cake (malo agho kanandi mukuwonera Shakespeare, mu vithuzithuzi vyake), apo ŵanyake wose ŵakalindiliranga mwakufwasa. Paumaliro Dodo wakati, “ Waliyose wawina, ndipo wose ŵakwenera kuŵa na mphoto.”

“Kweni kasi ni njani uyo wazamupeleka mphoto?” chomene chorus ya mazgu wakafumba.

“Chifukwa wuli, iyo , nadi,” wakayowoya Dodo, wakulongora Alice na chara chimoza; ndipo gulu lose nyengo yeneyiyo likazura kumuzingilizga, ndipo likachemerezga mu nthowa yakusokonezgeka kuti, “Mphoto! Mphoto!”

Alice wakamanya yayi kuti wachitechi, ndipo mu kugongowa wakaŵika woko lake mu thumba lake, na kufumiska kabokosi ka comfits, (mwamwaŵi maji gha mchere ghakaŵa kuti ghandanjiremo), na kuvipeleka kuzungulira nga ni mphoto. Pakaŵa nadi chimoza a-chidumbirano, chose kuzungulira.

“Kweni iyo mwene wakwenera kuŵa na mphoto, mukumanya,” wakayowoya Mbeŵa.

Dodo wakazgora mwakujikuzga comene kuti: “Enya.” “Kasi muli na vinyake vichi mu thumba linu?” wakalutilira, ndipo wakazgokera kwa Alice.

Alice wakati na chitima: “Thimble pera.”

Dodo wakati: “Mupeleke kuno.”

Pamanyuma wose ŵakawunganaso kumuzingilizga, apo Dodo wakapeleka chingwe chakujiyuyura, wakati “Tikumuŵeyani kuti muzomerezge chingwe chakutowa ichi;” ndipo, apo likamalizga kuyowoya mazgu ghafupi agha, wose ŵakasekereranga.

Alice wakaghanaghananga kuti vyose ivi vikaŵa vyambura kupulikikwa comene, kweni vyose vikawonekanga nga ni viheni comene mwakuti wakaŵavya cikanga ca kuseka; ndipo, pakuti wakaghanaghananga yayi chakuyowoya, wakagwada waka, na kutora chiŵegha, wakuwoneka wakujikuzga nga umo wangafiskira.

Chinthu chakulondezgapo chikaŵa chakuti ŵarye vinyama vyakuchemeka comfits: ivi vikapangiska kuti paŵe phokoso na kusokonezgeka, apo viyuni vikuruvikuru vikadandawura kuti vikulawa yayi vyawo, ndipo vichokovichoko vikafyekanga ndipo vikakhumbikwiranga kutimbanizgika pa msana. Kweni, paumaliro vikamara, ndipo ŵakakhalaso pasi mu mphete, ndipo ŵakapempha Mbeŵa kuti waŵaphalireso vinyake.

Alice wakati: “Mukanilayizga kuti muniphalirenge mdauko winu, mukumanya, ndipo cifukwa ico mukutinkhira—C na D,” wakasazgirapo mwakubiska, hafu na wofi kuti vingakhuŵazgaso.

“Nkhani yane ni yitali ndiposo yakuchitiska chitima!” wakayowoya Mbeŵa, wakuzgokera kwa Alice, na kupuma mwakufunya.

“ Ni mchira utali, nadi,” wakayowoya Alice, uku wakulaŵiska pasi mwakuzizwa pa mchira wa Mbeŵa; “kweni chifukwa wuli mukuti nchachitima?” Ndipo wakalutilira kujifumba apo Mbeŵa yikayowoyanga, mwakuti fundo yake ya nkhani iyi yikaŵa nga ni iyi:—

“Mukuŵapo yayi!” wakayowoya Mbeŵa kwa Alice mwakukwiya. “Kasi mukughanaghana vichi?”

Alice wakati mwakujiyuyura comene: “Nkhumugowokerani, nkhughanaghana kuti mukaŵa kuti mwafika pa msewu wakukhota wacinkhondi?”

“Nkhaŵavya ! ” wakachemerezga Mbeŵa, mwakuthwa ndiposo mwakukwiya chomene.

“Mfundo!” wakayowoya Alice, nyengo zose wakunozgeka kujipangiska kuŵa wakukhumbikwa, ndipo wakamulaŵiskanga mwakukhwaskika. “Oh, lekani niwovwirepo kuti niwuskepo!”

“Nizamuchita kanthu yayi ka mtundu uwu,” wakayowoya Mbeŵa, ndipo wakawuka na kufumapo. “Mukuninyoza pakuyowoya vinthu vyambura ng’anamuro ivi!”

“Nkhakhumbanga yayi!” wakaŵeyelera Alice mukavu. “Kweni mukukwiya luŵiro chomene, mukumanya!”

Mbeŵa yikakhuŵara waka pakuzgora.

“Chonde weleraniko na kumalizga nkhani yinu!” Alice wakachema pamanyuma pake; ndipo ŵanyake wose ŵakakolerana na mazgu ghakuti, “Enya, chonde chitani!” kweni Mbeŵa yikayuyura waka mutu wake kwambura kuzizipizga, ndipo yikendanga mwaluŵiro pachoko.

“Nchachitima chomene kuti chingakhala yayi!” wakafunya Lory, apo wakaŵa kuti wafuma waka pa malo ghake; ndipo Nkhala mulara wakatora mwaŵi uwu na kuyowoya kwa mwana wake msungwana kuti "Ah, mutemweka wane! Lekani ili liŵe sambiro kwa imwe kuti muleke kukwiya !" “Khalani lulimi lwinu, Mama!” wakayowoya Nkhala muchoko, mwakukhwaskika pachoko. “Uli wakukwana kuti uyezge kuzizipizga kwa oyster!”

“Nkhukhumba kuti niŵe na Dina withu kuno, nkhumanya kuti nili nawo!” Alice wakayowoya mwakukwezga, kwambura kuyowoya na munthu waliyose. “Wazamutoraso mwaluŵiro!”

“Ndipo Dina ni njani, usange ningajipeleka kufumba fumbo ili?” wakayowoya Lory.

Alice wakazgora mwakunweka, chifukwa nyengo zose wakaŵa wakunozgeka kuyowoya za chiŵeto chake: "Dinah ni mphaka yithu. Ndipo ni mphaka yikuru chomene yakukora mbeŵa iyo ungaghanaghana yayi! Ndipo oh, nkhukhumba kuti ungamuwona pamanyuma pa viyuni! Chifukwa wuli, wazamurya chiyuni chichoko para wachiwona waka!"

Nkhani iyi yikapangiska kuti ŵanthu ŵa mu chipani ichi ŵakhumbe chomene. Viyuni vinyake vikafumapo mwaluŵiro nyengo yeneyiyo: Magpie munyake wakale wakamba kujizingilizga mwakupwelelera comene, ndipo wakayowoya kuti, “Nkhwenera nadi kuti nkhufika ku nyumba; mphepo ya usiku yikuyana yayi na singo lane!” ndipo Canary wakachema na mazgu ghakutenthema ku ŵana ŵake kuti, "Zani, ŵatemweka ŵane! Nyengo yakwana kuti mose muŵe pa bedi!" Pa vifukwa vyakupambanapambana wose ŵakafumapo, ndipo mwaluŵiro Alice wakakhala yekha.

“Nkhukhumba kuti nindazunurepo vya Dina!” wakayowoya yekha na mazgu ghakuŵinya. "Palije uyo wakuwoneka kuti wakumutemwa, pasi pano, ndipo nkhugomezga kuti ni mphaka yiwemi chomene pa charu chose! O, Dinah wane wakutemweka! Nkhujifumba usange ningamuwonaniso!" Ndipo apa Alice mukavu wakambaso kulira, cifukwa wakajipulika kuti wali yekha comene ndiposo wakujiyuyura. Kweni mu nyengo yichoko waka, wakapulikaso kugwedezeka pachoko waka kwa malundi kutali, ndipo wakalaŵiska kuchanya mwakunweka, hafu wakagomezganga kuti Mbeŵa yasintha maghanoghano ghake, ndipo yikuweleraso kuti yimalizge nkhani yake.
