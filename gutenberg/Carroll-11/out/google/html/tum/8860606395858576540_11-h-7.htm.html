MUTU VII.
Chiphikiro cha Tiyi Wamisala

Pakaŵa thebulu ilo likaŵikika pasi pa khuni panthazi pa nyumba, ndipo March Hare na Hatter ŵakamwanga tiyi pa ilo: Dormouse wakakhala pakati pawo, wakagona chomene, ndipo ŵanyake ŵaŵiri ŵakaligwiliskiranga ntchito nga ni khushoni, ŵakapumuzganga viŵegha vyawo pa ilo, na kuyowoyeskana pa mutu wake. Alice wakaghanaghana kuti: “Nchakusuzga comene kwa Dormouse; “kweni, pakuti likugona, nkhughanaghana kuti likuŵikako mahara yayi.”

Gome ili likaŵa likuru, kweni wose ŵatatu ŵakazura pamoza pa ngodya yimoza: “Palije malo! ŵakalira apo ŵakawona Alice wakuza. “Kuli malo ghanandi !” Alice wakayowoya mwakukwiya, ndipo wakakhala pa mpando ukuru uwo ukaŵa ku chigaŵa chinyake cha thebulu.

Kalulu wa March wakati na mazgu ghakukhozga: “Mumwe vinyo.”

Alice wakalaŵiska kuzingilizga thebulu, kweni pakaŵavya chinthu chinyake kweni tiyi pera. Iyo wakati: “Nkhuwona vinyo lililose yayi.”

Kalulu wa March wakati: “Palije.”

Alice wakati mwakukwiya: “Para ndimo viliri, cikaŵa cambura ntchindi yayi kwa imwe kuti mupeleke.”

Kalulu wa March wakati: “Cikaŵa cambura kumanyikwa comene kwa imwe kuti mukhale pasi kwambura kucemeka.”

Alice wakati: “Nkhamanyanga yayi kuti ni thebulu linu ; “likuŵikika chifukwa cha ŵanandi chomene kuluska ŵatatu.”

Munthu wakupanga chipewa wakati: “Sisi lako likukhumba kudumulika.” Wakaŵa kuti walaŵiska Alice kwa nyengo yitali na chikhumbo chikuru chomene, ndipo iyi yikaŵa nkhani yake yakwamba.

Alice wakayowoya mwakukwiya kuti: “Mukwenera kusambira kuleka kuyowoya vinthu vyakukhwaskana na imwe mwekha; “ni vyambura ntchindi chomene.”

Munthu wakupanga chipewa wakajura maso ghake chomene apo wakapulika ivi; kweni vyose ivyo wakayowoya vikaŵa kuti, “Chifukwa wuli khwangwala wali nga ni desiki lakulemberapo?”

“Zani, tizamusangalala sono!” wakaghanaghana nthena Alice. “Nkhukondwa kuti ŵamba kufumba vipusikizgi.—Nkhugomezga kuti ningamanya nthena,” wakasazgapo mwakukwezga.

“Kasi ukung’anamura kuti ukughanaghana kuti ungasanga zgoro la fumbo ili?” wakayowoya Kalulu wa Malichi.

Alice wakati: “Ndimo nadi.”

“Para ndimo viliri, mukwenera kuyowoya ivyo mukung’anamura,” Kalulu wa Malichi wakalutilira.

Alice wakazgora mwaluŵiro kuti: “Nkhumanya; “pafupifupi—pafupifupi nkhung’anamura ivyo nkhuyowoya—icho nchinthu chimoza, mukumanya.”

“Chinthu chimoza yayi pachoko waka!” wakayowoya Munthu wa Chipewa. “Mungayowoyaso kuti ‘nkhuwona ivyo nkhurya’ ni chinthu chimoza na ‘nkhurya ivyo nkhuwona’!”

March Hare wakasazgapo kuti: “Mungayowoyaso kuti ‘nkhutemwa ivyo nkhusanga’ ni chinthu chimoza na ‘nkhusanga ivyo nkhutemwa’!”

Dormouse, uyo wakawonekanga kuti wakuyowoya apo wakugona, wakasazgapo kuti: “Mungayowoyaso makora kuti ‘nkhupuma para nkhugona’ ni chinthu chimoza na ‘nkhugona para nkhupuma’!”

“ Ntchinthu chimoza na imwe,” wakayowoya Munthu Wakupanga Chipewa, ndipo apa kudumbiskana kukamara, ndipo phwando likakhala chete kwa mphindi yimoza, apo Alice wakaghanaghaniranga vyose ivyo wakakumbukanga vyakukhwaskana na mphumphi na mathebulu ghakulemberapo, ivyo vikaŵa vinandi yayi.

Munthu wa Chipewa ndiyo wakaŵa wakwamba kuswa chete. “Kasi ni zuŵa wuli la mwezi?” wakayowoya, wakuzgokera kwa Alice: wakafumiska wotchi yake mu thumba lake, ndipo wakayilaŵiskanga mwakusuzgika maghanoghano, wakayisunkhunyanga nyengo na nyengo, na kuyikora pa khutu lake.

Alice wakaghanaghanirapo pachoko waka, ndipo pamanyuma wakati “Wachinayi.”

“Mazuŵa ghaŵiri ghakubudika!” wakapuma Munthu wa Chipewa. “Nkhamuphalirani kuti batala wangayana yayi na ntchito!” wakasazgirapo uku wakulaŵiska mwakukwiya kwa March Hare.

Kalulu wa March wakazgora mwakuzika kuti: “Wakaŵa batala muwemi comene .”

“Enya, kweni viwangwa vinyake vikwenera kuti vikanjiraso,” Munthu wakupanga chipewa wakang’ung’utika, “mukwenera yayi kuviŵikamo na chimayi cha chingwa.”

Kalulu wa March wakatora wotchi na kuyilaŵiska mwakufipa mtima: ndipo wakayiŵika mu kapu yake ya tiyi, na kuyilaŵiskaso: kweni wakaghanaghanapo yayi chinthu chiwemi chakuyowoya kuluska mazgu ghake ghakwamba ghakuti, “Iyi yikaŵa batala muwemi chomene , mukumanya.”

Alice wakaŵa kuti wakulaŵiska pa phewa lake na kukhumba kumanya vinandi. “Ntchinthu chakusekeska chomene!” wakayowoya nthena. “Likuyowoya zuŵa la mwezi, ndipo likuyowoya yayi kuti ni koloko wuli!”

“Chifukwa wuli chikwenera kuŵa nthena?” wakabuwura Munthu wa Chipewa. “Kasi wotchi yinu yikumuphalirani kuti ni chaka wuli?”

Alice wakazgora mwakufwatuka comene kuti: “Nadi yayi, kweni nthena cifukwa cakuti vikukhala mu cilimika cimoza kwa nyengo yitali comene pamoza.”

“Ico nchakucitika waka na cane ,” wakayowoya nthena Munthu wa Cipewa.

Alice wakajipulika kuti wazizwa comene, mazgu gha The Hatter ghakawonekanga kuti ghalije ng’anamuro lililose, ndipouli ghakaŵa nadi gha Cingelezi. Wakayowoya mwaulemu nga umo wangafiskira kuti: “Nkhumupulikiskani makora yayi.”

“Mbeŵa ya Dormouse yagonaso,” wakayowoya Munthu Wakupanga Chipewa, ndipo wakathilira tiyi wakutentha pa mphuno yake.

Dormouse wakayuyura mutu wake mwakuleka kuzikilira, ndipo wakati, kwambura kujura maso ghake, “Enya, enya;

“Kasi mwaŵa kuti mwamanyapo kale ntharika?” Munthu wa Chipewa wakayowoya, ndipo wakazgokeraso kwa Alice.

Alice wakazgora kuti: “Yayi, nkhuleka, kasi zgoro ni vichi?”

Munthu wa Chipewa wakati: “Nilije fundo nanga nkhuchoko waka.”

“Na ine yayi,” wakayowoya nthena Kalulu wa March.

Alice wakapuma mwakuvuka. Iyo wakati: “Nkhughanaghana kuti mungachita chinthu chiwemi na nyengo iyi kuluska kuyitaya pakufumba vipusikizgi ivyo vilije mazgoro.”

Munthu wakupanga chipewa wakati: “Usange mungamanya makora Nyengo nga umo nkhumanyira, mungayowoyapo yayi vya kuyitaya .

Alice wakati: “Nkhumanya yayi ivyo mukung’anamura.”

“Nadi mukuchita yayi!” Munthu Wakupanga Chipewa wakayowoya, wakusunkhunya mutu wake mwakunyoza. “Nkhugomezga kuti mukayowoyapo yayi nanga nkhuyowoya na Time!”

Alice wakazgora mwakucenjera kuti: “Panji yayi, kweni nkhumanya kuti nkhwenera kucimbira nyengo para nkhusambira sumu.”

“Ah!, ndivyo vikupangiska,” wakayowoya nthena Munthu wa Chipewa. "Wazamuzizipizga yayi kutimbika. Sono, usange mungaŵa nayo waka paubwezi uwemi, wangachita pafupifupi chilichose icho mukutemwa na wotchi. Mwachiyelezgero, tiyerezgere kuti nyengo yikaŵa 9 koloko mulenji, nyengo yakuti muyambe waka masambiro: mukwenera waka kuyowoya mwakuwerezgawerezga kwa Nyengo, ndipo wotchi yikuzungulira nyengo yimoza, half-pa twink!"

(“Nkhukhumba waka kuti viŵe nthena,” Kalulu wa March wakayowoya yekha mwakubiska.)

Alice wakayowoya mwakughanaghanira kuti: “Ico cingaŵa cakuzirwa comene, nadi, kweni ntheura—nkhwenera kuŵa na njara yayi, mukumanya.”

Munthu wakupanga chipewa wakati: “Panji pakwamba yayi, kweni ungasunga kuti yifike pa theka la 1 koloko kwa nyengo yitali iyo ukukhumbira.”

“Kasi ndimo mukuchitira vinthu?” Alice wakafumba.

Munthu wakupanga chipewa wakayuyura mutu wake na chitima. “Ine yayi!” wakazgora kuti. “Tikatimbana mu Malichi wajumpha—pambere wandafunge , mukumanya—” (wakulongora na supuni yake ya tiyi ku March Hare,) “—pakaŵa pa konsati yikuru iyo yikapelekeka na Fumukazi ya Mitima, ndipo nkhakhumbikwiranga kwimba .

‘Kuŵala, kuwala, ka bat kachoko!
Nkhujifumba chomene kuti muli pa vichi!’

Mukumanya sumu iyi, panji?”

Alice wakati: “Nkhapulikapo chinthu chanthena ichi.

“Vikulutilira, mukumanya,” Munthu Wakupanga Chipewa wakalutilizga, “mu nthowa iyi:—

‘Pacanya pa caru ico mukuwuluka, .
Nga ni thireyi ya tiyi mu mlengalenga.
Kuŵala, kuwala—’”

Apa Dormouse wakajisunkhunya, ndipo wakamba kwimba mu tulo twake “ Kuŵala, kuwala, kuwala, kuwala —” ndipo wakalutilira nyengo yitali mwakuti ŵakakhumbikwiranga kuyikhwaska kuti yileke.

Munthu uyo wakapanga chipewa wakati: “Kweni, nkhaŵa kuti nindamalizge vesi lakwamba, apo Fumukazi yikajumphilira na kuchemerezga kuti, ‘Wakukoma nyengo!

“Nkhwakofya chomene!” Alice wakachemerezga.

"Ndipo kufuma nyengo iyo," Wakupanga Chipewa wakalutilira na mazgu gha chitima, "wachitenge yayi chinthu icho nkhupempha! Nyengo zose sono nyengo yikuŵa 6 koloko."

Fundo yakutowa yikiza mu mutu wa Alice. “Kasi ndicho chifukwa chake vinthu vinandi vya tiyi vikuŵikika kuwaro kuno?” wakafumba.

“Enya, ndivyo,” wakayowoya Munthu wa Chipewa na kupuma mwakufunya: “nyengo zose nyengo ya tiyi yikuŵa, ndipo tilije nyengo yakuchapira vinthu pakati pa nyengo.”

“Pamanyuma mukulutilira kuzungulirazungulira, nkhughanaghana?” wakayowoya nthena Alice.

Munthu wakupanga chipewa wakati: “Ndimo viliri nadi, apo vinthu vikuzgoŵera.”

“Kweni kasi nchivici ico cikucitika para mwafikaso pakwamba?” Alice wakajipeleka kufumba.

“Tiyelezgere kuti tisinthe nkhani,” March Hare wakadumura, uku wakukhuŵara. "Nkhutopa na ichi. Nkhuvotera kuti msungwana wakutiphalira nkhani."

Alice wakati: “Nkhuwopa kuti nkhumanya yayi yumoza,” m’malo mwake wakawopa comene cifukwa ca ivyo ŵakamuphalira.

“Pamanyuma Dormouse wazamuŵapo!” wose ŵakalira. “Wuka, Mbeŵa yakugona!” Ndipo ŵakayikhwaska vigaŵa vyose viŵiri nyengo yimoza.

Dormouse wakajura maso ghake pachokopachoko. “Nkhagona yayi,” wakayowoya na mazgu ghakukhozga ndiposo ghambura nkhongono, ndipo wakati: “Nkhapulika mazgu ghose agho imwe ŵanyane mukayowoyanga.”

“Tiphalirani nkhani!” wakayowoya Kalulu wa Malichi.

“Enya, chonde chitani!” wakaŵeyelera Alice.

“Ndipo muŵe ŵaluŵiro,” wakasazgapo Munthu wakupanga chipewa, “panji muzamugonaso pambere vindamalizgike.”

“Nyengo yinyake pakaŵa ŵadumbu ŵachoko ŵatatu,” Dormouse wakamba mwakucimbilira comene; “ndipo mazina ghawo ghakaŵa Elsie, Lacie, na Tillie; ndipo ŵakakhalanga pasi pa chisimi—”

“Kasi ŵakakhalanga na vichi?” wakayowoya nthena Alice, uyo nyengo zose wakatemwanga comene mafumbo ghakukhwaskana na kurya na kumwa.

“Ŵakakhalanga na treacle,” wakayowoya nthena Dormouse, wati waghanaghanirapo mphindi yimoza panji ziŵiri.

“Ŵangachita nthena yayi, mukumanya,” Alice wakayowoya mwakuzika; “ŵaŵenge kuti ŵalwara.”

“Ntheura ŵakaŵa,” wakayowoya nthena Dormouse; “ wakulwara chomene .”

Alice wakayezga kujighanaghanira yekha umo umoyo wapadera nthena ungaŵira, kweni vikamuzizika comene, ntheura wakalutilira kuti: “Kweni cifukwa wuli ŵakakhalanga pasi pa cisimi?”

“Toraniso tiyi,” Kalulu wa March wakaphalira Alice, mwakujipeleka comene.

Alice wakazgora na mazgu ghakukwiya kuti: “Nkhaŵavya kanthu, ntheura ningatora yayi vinandi.”

“Ukung’anamura kuti ungatora yayi vichoko ,” wakayowoya nthena Munthu wa Chipewa: “nchakusuzga chomene kutora vinandi kuluska kuŵavya kanthu.”

Alice wakati: “Palije uyo wakafumbani maghanoghano ghinu .

“Kasi ni njani uyo wakuyowoya vya munthu payekha sono?” Munthu wa Chipewa wakafumba mwakuthereska.

Alice wakamanya makora yayi ivyo wangayowoya pa ivi: ntheura wakajiwovwira yekha na tiyi na chingwa-na-batala, ndipo pamanyuma wakazgokera kwa Dormouse, na kuwerezgapo fumbo lake. “Chifukwa wuli ŵakakhalanga pasi pa chisimi?”

Dormouse wakatoraso mphindi yimoza panji ziŵiri kuti waghanaghanirepo, ndipo pamanyuma wakati, “Chikaŵa chisimi cha treacle.”

“Palije chinthu chanthena!” Alice wakamba kukwiya chomene, kweni Hatter na March Hare ŵakaluta “Sh! sh!” ndipo Dormouse wakayowoya mwakukwiya kuti, “Usange mungaŵa ŵacikaya yayi, nchiwemi kuti mumalizge nkhani iyi mwekha.”

“Yayi, chonde lutilirani!” Alice wakati mwakujiyuyura chomene; “Nizamutimbanizgaso yayi. Nkhugomezga kuti panji pali yumoza .”

“Yimoza, nadi!” wakayowoya Dormouse mwakukwiya. Ndipouli, wakazomera kulutilira. “Ndipo ntheura ŵadumbu ŵachoko ŵatatu aŵa—ŵakasambiranga kujambula, imwe mukumanya—”

“Kasi ŵakajambura vichi?” Alice wakayowoya nthena, ndipo wakaluwa chomene layizgo lake.

“Treacle,” wakayowoya Dormouse, kwambura kughanaghanirapo pa nyengo yose iyi.

“Nkhukhumba kapu yakutowa,” wakanjilirapo Munthu Wakupanga Chipewa, “tose tisunthirepo malo ghamoza.”

Wakalutilira apo wakayowoyanga, ndipo Dormouse wakamulondezga: March Hare wakasamira mu malo gha Dormouse, ndipo Alice m’malo mwake wakatora malo gha March Hare kwambura kukhumba. Munthu uyo wakapanga chipewa ndiyo yekha wakasanga phindu lililose kufuma ku kusintha uku: ndipo Alice wakaŵa mu suzgo likuru chomene kuluska kumanyuma, pakuti March Hare wakakhuŵazga waka mkaka-jug mu mbale yake.

Alice wakakhumba yayi kukhuŵazgaso Dormouse, ntheura wakamba mwakucenjera comene kuti: “Kweni nkhupulikiska yayi.

Munthu wakupanga chipewa wakati: “Ungaseŵa maji mu chisimi cha maji; “ntheura nkhwenera kughanaghana kuti mungafumiska treacle kufuma mu chisimi cha treacle—eh, chindere?”

“Kweni ŵakaŵa mu chisimi,” Alice wakaphalira Dormouse, ndipo wakasankha yayi kuwona mazgu gha umaliro agha.

“Nadi ŵakaŵa,” wakayowoya nthena Dormouse; “—makora mu.”

Zgoro ili likasokonezga comene Alice mukavu, mwakuti wakaleka Dormouse kuti walutilire kwa nyengo yicoko waka kwambura kuyitimbanizga.

“Ŵakasambiranga kujambula,” Dormouse wakalutilira, wakakhuŵaranga na kusisitira maso ghake, chifukwa wakamba kugona chomene; “ndipo ŵakajambura vinthu vya mitundumitundu—vinthu vyose ivyo vikwamba na M—”

“Chifukwa wuli na M?” wakayowoya nthena Alice.

"Chifukwa wuli yayi?" wakayowoya Kalulu wa Malichi.

Alice wakakhala chete.

Dormouse wakajara maso ghake pa nyengo iyi, ndipo wakaluta ku tulo; kweni, pa kukhwaskika na Munthu wa Cipewa, cikawukaso na kucemerezga pacoko waka, ndipo cikalutilira kuti: “—ico cikwamba na M, nga ni misampha ya mbeŵa, na mwezi, na kukumbukira, na unandi—mukumanya kuti mukuti vinthu ni “unandi wa unandi”—kasi muli kuwonapo cinthu ca unandi nga ni cithuzithuzi ca?”

“Nadi, sono mukunifumba,” wakayowoya Alice, wakusokonezgeka comene, “nkhughanaghana kuti—”

“Para ndimo viliri, mukwenera yayi kuyowoya,” wakayowoya nthena Munthu Wakupanga Chipewa.

Chigaŵa ichi cha nkhaza chikaŵa chakujumpha ivyo Alice wangazizipizga: wakawuka mwakuseluska chomene, na kufumapo; Dormouse wakagona nyengo yeneyiyo, ndipo palije na yumoza wa ŵanyake uyo wakamanyapo kanthu kuti wakuluta, nangauli wakalaŵiska kumanyuma kamoza panji kaŵiri, hafu wakagomezganga kuti ŵamuchemerenge: nyengo yaumaliro apo wakaŵawona, ŵakayezganga kuŵika Dormouse mu chiŵiya cha tiyi.

“Mulimose umo vingaŵira, nizamulutaso yayi ku malo agha !” wakayowoya Alice apo wakasankhanga nthowa yake mu makuni. “Ni phwando la tiyi lakupusa chomene ilo nkhaŵapo pa umoyo wane wose!”

Apo wakayowoyanga waka ivi, wakawona kuti khuni linyake likaŵa na mulyango uwo ukanjirangamo. “Icho nchakukhumba chomene kumanya!” wakaghanaghana nthena. "Kweni vyose vikukhumba kumanya lero. Nkhughanaghana kuti ninganjira nyengo yeneyiyo." Ndipo wakanjira.

Kachiŵiri wakajisanga kuti wali mu holo yitali, ndipo pafupi na thebulu lichoko la galasi. “Sono, nyengo iyi nizamukwaniska makora,” wakayowoya yekha, ndipo wakamba na kutora kiyi muchoko wa golide, na kujura chipata icho chikanjiranga mu munda. Pamanyuma wakaluta ku nchito na kukhweŵa bowa (wakasunga cigaŵa cake mu thumba lake) mpaka apo wakafika pafupifupi futi yimoza: ndipo pamanyuma wakenda pasi mu msewu ucoko: ndipo pamanyuma —wakajisanga kuti paumaliro wali mu munda wakutowa, pakati pa maluŵa ghakuwala na masiseŵero ghakuzizima.
