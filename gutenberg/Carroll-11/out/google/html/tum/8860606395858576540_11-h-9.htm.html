MUTU IX.
Nkhani ya Mock Turtle

“Ungaghanaghana yayi kuti nkhukondwa chomene kuti nkhuwonaso iwe, iwe chinthu chakale chakutemweka!” wakayowoya Duchess, apo wakanjizga woko lake mwachitemwa mu la Alice, ndipo ŵakafumapo pamoza.

Alice wakakondwa chomene kumusanga kuti wali na ukali wakukondweska nthena, ndipo wakaghanaghana kuti panji ni tsabola pera uyo wakapangiska kuti waŵe wankhaza chomene apo ŵakakumananga mu khichini.

“Para ndine Duchess,” wakayowoya yekha, (mu mazgu ghakugomezga comene yayi nangauli), “niŵenge na tsabola yayi mu khichini yane nanga nkhucepa . Msuzi wakuchita makora comene kwambura—Panji nyengo zose ni tsabola uyo wakupangiska ŵanthu kuŵa ŵakukwiya comene,” wakalutilira, wakukondwa comene kuti wakasanga kuti rugar “ so viur, makedne camomile iyo yikuŵapangiska kuŵa ŵakuwawa—na—na balere-shuga na vinthu vyanthena ivyo vikupangiska ŵana kuŵa ŵakunowa nkhukhumba waka kuti ŵanthu ŵamanye nthena : ntheura ŵangaŵa ŵakujikuzga chomene yayi pa nkhani iyi, mukumanya—”

Wakaŵa kuti wamuluwa chomene Duchess pa nyengo iyi, ndipo wakawopa pachoko apo wakapulika mazgu ghake pafupi na khutu lake. "Ukughanaghana chinthu chinyake, wakutemweka wane, ndipo ichi chikupangiska kuti uluwe kuyowoya. Ningakuphalira yayi sonosono apa kuti nchivichi icho chikupangiska kuti munthu waŵe na nkharo yiwemi, kweni nizamukumbuka nyengo yichoko waka."

Alice wakajipeleka kuyowoya kuti: “Panji lilije.”

“Tut, tut, mwana!” wakayowoya nthena Duchess. “Chilichose chili na nkharo, usange ungachisanga waka.” Ndipo wakajifinyikizga pafupi na chigaŵa cha Alice apo wakayowoyanga.

Alice wakatemwanga yayi kuŵa pafupi nayo chomene: chakwamba, chifukwa chakuti Duchess wakaŵa muheni chomene ; ndipo chachiŵiri, chifukwa chakuti wakaŵa mutali wakukwana nadi kuti wapumulire chibwano chake pa phewa la Alice, ndipo chikaŵa chibwano chakuthwa chomene. Ndipouli, wakatemwanga yayi kuŵa wakujikuzga, ntheura wakazizipizga umo wangafiskira.

“Maseŵero ghakulutilira makora comene sono,” wakayowoya nthena, mwa kulutilira kudumbiskana pachoko waka.

“‘Ntheura,” wakayowoya nthena Duchess, “ndipo nkharo ya fundo iyi njakuti—‘O, ‘ni citemwa, ‘ni citemwa, ico cikupangiska caru kuti cizingilire!’”

Alice wakayowoya mwakuwerezgawerezga kuti: “Munthu munyake wakati vikuchitika na waliyose uyo wakuchita vyake!”

“Ah, nthena! Cikung’anamura cintu cimoza comene,” wakayowoya nthena Duchess, uku wakukumba chibwano cake cicoko cakuthwa mu phewa la Alice apo wakasazgirapo kuti, “ndipo fundo ya fundo iyi njakuti—‘Pwelerani fundo, ndipo mazgu ghazamujipwelelera.’”

“Wakutemwa chomene kusanga nkharo yiwemi mu vinthu!” Alice wakaghanaghana nthena.

"Nkhugomezga kuti mukujifumba chifukwa icho nkhuŵikira yayi woko lane mu chiuno chinu," Duchess wakayowoya apo wakakhala waka chete: "chifukwa nchakuti, nkhukayika pa ukali wa flamingo winu. Kasi niyezge kuyezga?"

Alice wakazgora mwakucenjera kuti: “Wangaluma,” ndipo wakafipanga mtima yayi kuti ŵayezge kuyezga.

Duchess wakati: “Nchaunenesko comene: flamingo na mustard vyose vikuluma. Ndipo fundo yakuti—‘Viyuni vya nthenga vikuwungana pamoza.’”

Alice wakati: “Mustard pera ndiyo ni mbalame yayi.”

Duchess wakati: “Enya, nga umo vikuŵira nyengo zose, muli na nthowa yakumanyikwa makora comene yakuŵikira vinthu!”

Alice wakati: “ Nkhughanaghana kuti ni mineral .”

“Nadi ndimo viliri,” wakayowoya nthena Duchess, uyo wakawonekanga kuti ngwakunozgeka kuzomerezga vyose ivyo Alice wakayowoyanga; "pali mgodi ukuru wa mustard pafupi na pano. Ndipo nkharo ya fundo iyi njakuti—'Para pali vyane vinandi, vyako vikuŵa vichoko.'"

“Oh, nkhumanya!” Alice, uyo wakaŵa kuti wandaŵepo pa mazgu gha umaliro agha, wakachemerezga kuti, “ni mphangwe. Yikuwoneka nga ni mphangwe yayi, kweni ndiyo.”

Duchess wakati: “Nkhuzomerezga namwe chomene; “ndipo nkharo ya fundo iyi njakuti—‘Uŵe umo ungawonekera kuŵa’—panji usange ungatemwa kuti viyowoyeke mwakudumura—‘Ungaghanaghananga yayi kuti ulije vinyake kuluska umo vingawonekera ku ŵanji kuti ivyo ukaŵa panji ivyo ungaŵa kuti ukaŵa vikaŵa vinyake yayi kuluska ivyo ukaŵa kuti vingawoneka kwa iwo kuŵa vinyake.’”

Alice wakati mwaulemu comene: “Nkhughanaghana kuti nkhwenera kupulikiska makora fundo iyi, usange ningayilemba: kweni ningayilondezga makora yayi umo mukuyowoyera.”

“Ico nchinthu yayi ku ivyo ningayowoya usange ningasankha,” Duchess wakazgora, na mazgu ghakukondwa.

Alice wakati: “Lombani kuti muleke kujisuzga kuti muyowoye nyengo yitali kuluska apa.”

“Oh, mungayowoyanga vya suzgo yayi!” wakayowoya nthena Duchess. “Nkhumupani chawanangwa cha vyose ivyo nayowoya m’paka sono.”

“Mtundu wa chawanangwa chakutchipa!” wakaghanaghana nthena Alice. “Nkhukondwa kuti ŵakupeleka yayi vyawanangwa vya zuŵa la kubabikira nga ni ivi!” Kweni wakajipeleka yayi kuyowoya mwakukwezga.

“Kughanaghanaso?” Duchess wakafumba, na kukumbaso chibwano chake chichoko chakuthwa.

Alice wakayowoya mwakucimbilira kuti: “Nili na wanangwa wa kughanaghana,” cifukwa wakamba kufipa mtima pachoko.

Duchess wakati: “Pafupifupi nchakwenelera chomene, nga umo nkhumba zikwenera kuwuluka; ndipo m—”

Kweni pano, Alice wakazizwa comene apo mazgu gha Duchess ghakafwa, nanga ni pakati pa lizgu ilo wakatemwanga comene lakuti ‘makhaliro ghawemi,’ ndipo woko ilo likakolerana na lake likamba kutenthema. Alice wakalaŵiska pachanya, ndipo pakaŵa Fumukazi iyo yikimilira panthazi pawo, na mawoko ghake ghakupindika, wakukhoma maso nga ni vula yachimphepo.

“Zuŵa liwemi, Fumu yinu!” Duchess wakamba na mazgu ghapasi, ghakufwatuka.

“Sono, nkhumuchenjezgani mwaurunji,” Fumukazi yikachemerezga, yikatinda pasi apo yikayowoyanga; "panji imwe panji mutu winu ukwenera kuŵa wakufumiskikapo, ndipo ici pafupifupi hafu ya nyengo yicoko waka! Sankhani!"

Duchess wakasankha, ndipo wakafumapo mu nyengo yichoko waka.

“Tiyeni tilutilire na maseŵero,” Fumukazi yikati kwa Alice; ndipo Alice wakaŵa na wofi comene mwakuti wakatondeka kuyowoya lizgu limoza, kweni wakamulondezga pachokopachoko na kuwelera ku malo ghakucitirapo maseŵero gha croquet.

Ŵalendo ŵanyake ŵakasangirapo mwaŵi pakuti Fumukazi yikaŵapo yayi, ndipo ŵakapumuranga mu mthunzi: kweni, nyengo iyo ŵakamuwona, ŵakawelera mwaluŵiro ku maseŵero, Fumukazi yikayowoya waka kuti kuchedwa kwa nyengo yichoko waka kungaŵapangiska kuti ŵafwe.

Nyengo yose iyo ŵakaseŵeranga Fumukazi yikaleka yayi kugazgana na ŵaseŵera ŵanyake, na kuchemerezga kuti “Off with his head!” panji kuti “Fumani na mutu wake!” Ŵanthu awo wakaŵazenga mulandu ŵakatoleka na ŵasilikari, awo nadi ŵakakhumbikwiranga kuleka kuŵa ŵasilikari kuti ŵacite nthena, mwakuti paumaliro wa theka la ora panji kujumphirapo pakaŵavya ŵasilikari awo ŵakakhalako, ndipo wose awo ŵakaseŵeranga, kupatulapo Themba, Fumukazi, na Alice, ŵakaŵa mu jele ndipo ŵakaŵa pasi pa cilango ca kukoma.

Pamanyuma Fumukazi yikaleka, yikaŵa kuti yafwatuka chomene, ndipo yikati kwa Alice, “Kasi mwamuwonapo Mock Turtle?”

Alice wakati: “Yayi. “Nkhumanya yayi nanga ni Mock Turtle ni vichi.”

“Ni chinthu icho ŵakupangira Msuzi wa Mock Turtle,” wakayowoya nthena Fumukazi.

Alice wakati: “Nkhawonapo yayi, panji kupulikapo vya chimoza.”

“Zani, ntheura,” Fumukazi yikati, “ndipo wamuphalirani mdauko wake.”

Apo ŵakafumanga pamoza, Alice wakapulika Themba likuyowoya na mazgu ghapasi, ku gulu lose kuti, “Mose mwagowokereka.” “Zani, ichi nchinthu chiwemi!” wakayowoya yekha, cifukwa wakajipulika kuti ngwambura kukondwa comene cifukwa ca unandi wa ŵanthu awo Fumukazi yikaphalira kuti ŵakomeke.

Mwaluŵiro comene ŵakasanga Gryphon, uyo wakagona tulo comene pa zuŵa. (Usange mukumanya yayi kuti Gryphon ni vichi, wonani chithuzithuzi.) “Kukwera, chinthu chaulesi!” wakayowoya Fumukazi, "ndipo mutore mwanakazi uyu kukawona Mock Turtle, na kupulika mbiri yake. Nkhwenera kuwelera na kukawona pamanyuma pa kukoma ŵanthu ŵanyake awo naŵaphalira;" ndipo wakafumapo, wakamuleka Alice yekha na Gryphon. Alice wakatemwanga yayi kawonekero ka cinyama ici, kweni pa vyose wakaghanaghananga kuti cingaŵa cakukhozga comene kukhala naco nga ni para walondezga Fumukazi yankhaza yira: ntheura wakalindilira.

Gryphon wakakhala pasi na kusisitira maso ghake: ndipo wakalaŵiska Fumukazi mpaka apo wakawoneka yayi: ndipo wakaseka. “Nchakukondweska chomene!” wakayowoya Gryphon, hafu kwa iyo yekha, hafu kwa Alice.

“Kasi nchivici ico cikukondweska?” wakayowoya nthena Alice.

“Chifukwa wuli, iyo ,” wakayowoya Gryphon. "Vyose ni vyakukhumba vyake, kuti: ŵakukomapo yayi munthu waliyose, mukumanya. Zani!"

“Wose ŵakuti ‘za!’ pano,” wakaghanaghana nthena Alice, apo wakalutanga pacokopacoko panyuma pake: “Nkhaŵa kuti nindaphalirikepo nthena mu umoyo wane wose, nyengo yiliyose!”

Ŵakaŵa kuti ŵandalute kutali pambere ŵandawone Mock Turtle kutali, wakakhala na chitima ndiposo yekha pa mzere uchoko wa libwe, ndipo, apo ŵakasendeleranga pafupi, Alice wakapulikanga wakufunya nga ni para mtima wake ukuphyoka. Wakamuchitira chitima chomene. “Kasi chitima chake nchivichi?” wakafumba Gryphon, ndipo Gryphon wakazgora, pafupifupi chomene mu mazgu ghakuyana waka na ghakale, "Vyose ni vyakukhumba vyake, kuti: walije chitima, mukumanya. Zani!"

Ntheura ŵakakwera kwa Mock Turtle, uyo wakaŵalaŵiska na maso ghakuru ghakuzura na masozi, kweni wakayowoyapo yayi.

“Uyu kuno msungwana,” wakayowoya Gryphon, “wakukhumba kumanya mdauko winu, wakukhumba.”

“Nimuphalirenge,” wakayowoya Mock Turtle mu mazgu ghakuzika, ghakudoloka: “khalani pasi, mose, ndipo mungayowoyanga yayi lizgu limoza mpaka nimalizge.”

Ntheura ŵakakhala pasi, ndipo pakaŵavya uyo wakayowoya kwa maminiti ghachoko waka. Alice wakaghanaghana kuti, “Nkhuwona yayi kuti wangamalizga wuli, usange wangayamba yayi.” Kweni wakalindilira mwakuzizipizga.

“Nyengo yinyake,” wakayowoya Mock Turtle paumaliro, na kupuma mwakufikapo, “Nkhaŵa Turtle weneco.”

Mazgu agha ghakalondezgeka na chete nyengo yitali comene, ndipo nyengo zinyake ŵakacemerezganga waka kuti “Hjckrrh!” kufuma ku Gryphon, na kulira kwakuŵinya kwa nyengo zose kwa Mock Turtle. Alice wakaŵa pafupi comene kuwuka na kuyowoya kuti, “Nkhuwonga, bwana, cifukwa ca nkhani yinu yakukondweska,” kweni wakatondeka kujikora kughanaghana kuti pakwenera kuŵa vinyake ivyo vikwiza, ntheura wakakhala waka chete ndipo wakayowoyapo yayi.

“Apo tikaŵa ŵachoko,” Fulu yakunyoza yikalutilira paumaliro, mwakufwasa chomene, nangauli yikaŵa kuti yichali kulira pachoko nyengo na nyengo, “tikalutanga ku sukulu mu nyanja. Mbuya wake wakaŵa Fulu mulara—tikamuchemanga kuti Fulu—”

“Chifukwa wuli mukamuchema kuti Fulu, usange wakaŵa yumoza yayi?” Alice wakafumba.

“Tikamuchemanga Fulu chifukwa wakatisambizga,” Fulu Yakunyoza yikati mwakukwiya: “nadi ndiwe wakutondeka chomene!”

Gryphon wakasazgapo kuti: “Ukwenera kuŵa na soni chifukwa cha kufumba fumbo lambura kusuzga nthena; ndipo pamanyuma wose ŵakakhala chete na kulaŵiska Alice mukavu, uyo wakajipulika kuti ngwakunozgeka kumira pasi. Paumaliro Gryphon wakati kwa Mock Turtle, "Lutilirani, muchekuru! Ungakhalanga zuŵa lose yayi pa nkhani iyi!" ndipo wakalutilira na mazgu agha:

“Enya, tikasambira ku sukulu ku nyanja, nangauli mungagomezga yayi—”

“Nkhayowoyapo yayi kuti nkhumanya yayi!” wakanjilirapo Alice.

“Mwachita,” wakayowoya nthena Fulu ya Mock.

“Khalani lulimi lwinu!” wakasazgirapo Gryphon, pambere Alice wandayowoyeso. Fulu ya Mock yikalutilira.

“Tikaŵa na masambiro ghawemi chomene—nakuti tikalutanga ku sukulu zuŵa lililose—”

Alice wakati: “ Nane nkhasambirapo pa sukulu ya zuŵa; “mukwenera yayi kujikuzga nga ni vyose ivi.”

“Na vyakusazgikira?” wakafumba Mock Turtle mwakukhwaskika pachoko.

Alice wakati: “Enya, tikasambira Chifurenchi na sumu.”

“Ndipo kugeza?” wakayowoya Fulu ya Mock.

“Nadi yayi!” Alice wakayowoya mwakukwiya.

“Ah! ntheura sukulu yinu yikaŵa yiwemi nadi yayi,” wakayowoya Mock Turtle na mazgu ghakufwatuka comene. “Sono kwa ise ŵakaŵa na pa umaliro wa ndalama, ‘Cifurenchi, sumu, na kugeza —vyakusazgikira.’”

Alice wakati: “Mungakhumbanga chomene yayi; “ŵakukhala pasi pa nyanja.”

“Nkhaŵavya ndalama zakusambilira.” wakayowoya Fulu ya Mock na kupuma mwakufunya. “Nkhasambiranga waka masambiro gha nyengo zose.”

“Kasi chikaŵa vichi?” wakafumba Alice.

“Kusunkhunyika na Kusunkhunyika, nadi, kwamba pakwamba,” Mock Turtle wakazgora; “ndipo pamanyuma pake nthambi zakupambanapambana za Masamu—Kukhumba Kuŵa Ukuru, Kutimbanizga, Kuyuyura, na Kunyoza.”

Alice wakajipeleka kuyowoya kuti: “Nkhapulikapo yayi za ‘Uglification.’” “Kasi nchivichi?”

Gryphon wakakwezga malundi ghake ghose ghaŵiri mwakuzizwa. "Kasi! Nkhapulikapo yayi vya kuseluska!" likachemerezga. “Kasi mukumanya kuti kutozga ni vichi, nkhughanaghana?”

Alice wakati mwakukayikiska: “Enya, cikung’anamura—kupanga—cinthu cilicose—kuŵa cakutowa comene.”

“Kweni, ntheura,” Gryphon wakalutilira, “usange ukumanya yayi kuti uglify ni vichi, ndiwe munthu wambura mahara.”

Alice wakajipulika kuti wakuchiskika yayi kufumbaso mafumbo ghanyake pa nkhani iyi, ntheura wakazgokera kwa Mock Turtle, ndipo wakati “Kasi nchivichi chinyake icho mukaŵa nacho chakusambira?”

“Kweni, pakaŵa Chisisi,” Mock Turtle wakazgora, uku wakupenda nkhani izo zikaŵa pa viphapha vyake, “—Chisisi, chakale na cha mazuŵa ghano, na Seaography: pamanyuma pake Drawling—Drawling-master wakaŵa conger-eel wakale, uyo wakazakaŵangapo kamoza pa sabata: wakatisambizganga Kukora, Kutambasura, Kutambasura.”

“Kasi vikaŵa wuli?” wakayowoya nthena Alice.

"Kweni, ine ningamulongorani yayi," Mock Turtle wakati: "Ndine wakukhora chomene. Ndipo Gryphon wakasambirapo yayi."

"Nkhaŵavya nyengo," wakayowoya Gryphon: "Nkhaluta kwa msambizgi wa Classics, nangauli. Wakaŵa nkhala yakale, wakaŵa ."

“Nkhalutapo yayi kwa iyo,” Mock Turtle wakayowoya na kupuma: “wakasambizganga Kuseka na Chitima, ŵakatemwanga kuyowoya.”

“Ntheura wakachita, ntheura wakachita,” wakayowoya Gryphon, wakupuma mu nyengo yake; ndipo vinyama vyose viŵiri vikabisa visku vyawo mu viŵegha vyawo.

“Ndipo mukasambiranga maora ghanandi wuli pa zuŵa?” wakayowoya Alice, mwakucimbilira kusintha nkhani.

“Maora 10 pa zuŵa lakwamba,” wakayowoya nthena Mock Turtle: “maora 9 pa zuŵa lakulondezgapo, na ntheura.”

“Ni pulani yakuzizika chomene!” Alice wakachemerezga.

Gryphon wakati: “Ndicho chifukwa chake ŵakuti masambiro, chifukwa ghakuchepa zuŵa na zuŵa.”

Iyi yikaŵa fundo yiphya comene kwa Alice, ndipo wakaghanaghanirapo dankha pambere wandayowoye mazgu ghake ghakulondezgapo. “Kasi zuŵa la khumi na limoza likwenera kuti likaŵa holide?”

“Nadi, ndimo vikaŵira,” wakayowoya nthena Mock Turtle.

“Ndipo mukakwaniska wuli pa zuŵa la khumi na ŵaŵiri?” Alice wakalutilira mwakunweka.

“Ivyo vyakwana pa masambiro,” Gryphon wakanjilirapo na mazgu ghakusimikizga comene: “muphaliranipo kanthu pa maseŵero sono.”
