KA LYNNONG IX.
Ka jingiathuhkhana jong u Mock Turtle

“Phim lah ban pyrkhat katno nga sngewbha ban iohi biang ia phi, phi ka tymmen baieit!” la ong ka Duchess, katba ka dang pynrung ia la ka kti da ka jingieit ha ka kti jong ka Alice, bad ki la iaid noh ryngkat.

Ka Alice ka la sngewbha shibun ban shem ia ka ha ka jinglong kaba sngewtynnad kum kata, bad ka la pyrkhat ha lade ba lehse dei tang u pepper uba la pynlong ia ka ban long kaba shyrkhei haba ki la iakynduh ha kamra shet.

“Haba nga la long ka Duchess,” ka la ong ha lade, (ym da ka sur kaba kyrmen than wat la katta ruh), “Ngan ym don satia ka sohjew ha ka kamra shet jong nga . Ka soup ka leh bha shibun khlem— Lehse ka long beit ka sohjew kaba pynlong ia ki briew ban sngewsih,” ka la iathuh shuh shuh, da kaba sngewbha shibun eh ba ka la lap ia ka jait sohjew kaba thymmai kumta. camomile kaba pynlong ia ki ban sngewsih— bad— bad ka shini barli bad kum kita kiei kiei kiba pynlong ia ki khynnah kiba sngewtynnat Nga kwah tang ba ki briew kin tip ia kata : te kin ym long kiba sting than shaphang kata, phi tip—”

Ka la klet noh ia ka Duchess ha kane ka por, bad ka la sngewlyngngoh khyndiat haba ka la iohsngew ia ka sur jong ka kaba jan bad ka shkor jong ka. “Phi pyrkhat ia kaei kaei, ko baieit jong nga, bad kata ka pynlong ia phi ban klet ban kren Ngam lah ban iathuh ia phi tang mynta kaei ka akor jong kata, hynrei ngan sa kynmaw ia kata hadien khyndiat por.”

“Lehse kam don kawei ruh,” ka Alice ka la nud ban kynthoh.

“Tut, tut, khynnah!” la ong ka Duchess. “Baroh ki don ka akor, lada tang phi lah ban shem ia ka.” Bad ka la pynkhih ia lade kham jan sha ka liang jong ka Alice katba ka dang kren.

Ka Alice kam sngewtynnad than ban iasah hajan jong ka kumta: nyngkong, namar ba ka Duchess ka long kaba sniew dur bha ; bad kaba ar, namar ba ka long thik ka jingjrong kaba biang ban pynshong ia ka ryndang jong ka ha ka tyrpeng jong ka Alice, bad ka long ka ryndang kaba jem kaba ym sngewtynnad. Hynrei kam sngewtynnad ban long kaba khlem akor, kumta ka la shah katba lah.

“Ka jingialehkai ka la nang iaid kham bha mynta,” ka la ong, da kaba pynjlan ia ka jingiakren khyndiat.

“‘Ka long kumta,” la ong ka Duchess: “bad ka akor jong kata ka long— ‘Oh, ‘ka jingieit, ‘ka jingieit, kaba pynlong ia ka pyrthei ban iaid kylleng!’”

“Don kiba ong,” ka Alice ka la rkhie, “ba la leh da baroh kiba pyrkhat ia la ka jong ka kam!”

“Ah, bha! Ka mut shibun kajuh ka jingmut,” la ong ka Duchess, da kaba tih ia la ka rmiang shyntur barit kaba jem ha ka tyrpeng jong ka Alice katba ka dang bynrap, “bad ka akor jong kata ka long — ‘To sumar ia ka jingsngew, bad ki sur kin sumar ia lade.’”

“Katno ka sngewtynnad ban shem ia ka akor ha kiei kiei!” Ka Alice ka la pyrkhat ha lade.

“Nga nud ban ong ba phi sngew lyngngoh balei ba ngam buh ia la ka kti ha ka tyrpeng jong phi,” la ong ka Duchess hadien ba ka la shongthait: “ka daw ka long, ba nga artatien ia ka jingbitar jong u flamingo jong phi. Hato ngan pyrshang ia ka jingpyrshang?”

“U lah ban biah,” ka Alice ka la jubab da kaba husiar, khlem da sngew jynjar satia ban pyrshang ia ka jingpyrshang.

“Ka long kaba shisha shibun,” la ong ka Duchess: “ki sim flamingo bad u sohjew baroh ar ki bam. Bad ka akor jong kata ka long — ‘Ki sim kiba don kawei ka sniehdoh ki iawan lang.’”

“Tang u sohjew um dei u sim,” la kynthoh ka Alice.

“Hooid, kumba ju long,” la ong ka Duchess: “kaei ka rukom kaba shai kaba phi don ban buh ia kiei kiei!”

“Ka dei ka marpoh khyndew, nga pyrkhat ,” la ong ka Alice.

“Hooid ka long kumta,” la ong ka Duchess, kaba i kumba ka kloi ban mynjur ia kiei kiei baroh kiba ka Alice ka ong; “don ka par mustard kaba heh hajan hangne. Bad ka akor jong kata ka long— ‘Katba nang bun ka jong nga, katta nang duna ka jong phi.’”

“Oh, nga tip!” la pyrta ka Alice, kaba khlem leit sha kane ka jingkren kaba khatduh, “ka dei ka jhur. Kam long kum kawei, hynrei ka long.”

“Nga ia mynjur shibun bad phi,” la ong ka Duchess; “bad ka akor jong kata ka long— ‘To long kumba phi i kumba phi long’— lane lada phi kwah ban buh kham suk— ‘Wat ju pyrkhat ia lade ba phin ym long lym kumta ban ia kaba ka lah ban paw ha kiwei ba kaei kaba phi long ne lah ban long kam long lym kumta ia kaei kaba phi la long kan jin da la paw ha ki ba kan long lym kumta.’”

“Nga tharai ba nga dei ban sngewthuh kham bha ia kata,” la ong ka Alice da ka jingsbun shibun, “lada nga la thoh ia ka: hynrei ngam lah ban bud thikna ia ka kumba phi ong.”

“Kata kam dei ei ei ia kaei kaba nga lah ban ong lada nga jied,” la jubab ka Duchess, da ka sur kaba sngewbha.

“To duwai wat pynshitom ia lade ban ong ia ka kham slem ban ia kata,” la ong ka Alice.

“Oh, wat kren ia ka jingeh!” la ong ka Duchess. “Nga pynlong ia phi kum ka jingai sngewbha jong kiei kiei baroh kiba nga la kren haduh mynta.”

“Ka jait jingai kaba rem dor!” la pyrkhat ka Alice. “Nga sngewbha ba kim ju ai sngewbha sngi kha kumta!” Hynrei kam shym la nud ban ong ia kata da kaba pyrta jam.

“Pyrkhat biang?” ka Duchess ka la kylli, da kaba tih biang ia ka rmiang shyntur barit jong ka kaba jem.

“Nga don ka hok ban pyrkhat,” la ong ka Alice da kaba jem, namar ka la sdang ban sngew jynjar khyndiat.

“Tang kumba katjuh ka hok,” la ong ka Duchess, “kumba ki sniang ki hap ban her; bad ki m—”

Hynrei hangne, ha ka jinglyngngoh kaba khraw jong ka Alice, ka sur jong ka Duchess ka la iap noh, wat hapdeng ka kyntien kaba ka ieit eh ‘ka akor,’ bad ka kti kaba la pyniasoh bad ka jong ka ka la sdang ban khih. Ka Alice ka la peit sha jrong, bad hangta ka Mahadei ka la ieng ha khmat jong ki, da kaba pynkhih ia ki kti jong ka, ka da rkhie bein kum ka lyer.

“Ka sngi kaba bha, Syiem jong phi!” ka Duchess ka la sdang da ka sur kaba jem bad kaba tlot.

“Mynta, nga ai ia phi ka jingmaham kaba hok,” la pyrta ka Mahadei, ka da sdang ban sdien phasi ha madan katba ka dang kren; “la phi ne ka khlieh jong phi ka dei ban jah noh, bad kata hapoh kumba shiteng por! Shim ia la ka jong ka jingjied!”

Ka Duchess ka la shim ia la ka jong ka jingjied, bad ka la jah noh ha ka khyllipmat.

“To ngin iaid shakhmat bad ka jingialehkai,” ka Mahadei ka la ong ia ka Alice; bad ka Alice ka la tieng palat ban kren wat tang shi kyntien ruh, hynrei suki suki ka la bud ia ka sha ka jaka ialehkai kroket.

Kiwei pat ki nongwan ki la shim kabu ia ka jingbymdon jong ka Mahadei, bad ki dang shongthait ha ka jingdum: hynrei, ha ka khyllipmat ba ki iohi ia ka, ki la mareh kyrkieh biang sha ka jingialehkai, ka Mahadei ka shu kynthoh ba ka jingpynslem shiphang kan pynduh ia ka jingim jong ki.

Baroh shi katta ba ki dang ialehkai ka Mahadei kam ju sangeh ban ialehkai bad kiwei pat ki nongialehkai, bad ban pyrta “Off bad ka khlieh jong u!” lane “Off bad ka khlieh jong ka!” Ia kito kiba ka la rai pynrem la shim ha phatok da ki shipai, kiba hoid ki la hap ban iehnoh ia ka jinglong arch ban leh ia kane, kumta ba ha kaba kut jong ka shiteng kynta ne kumta ym shym la don arch shuh, bad baroh ki nongialehkai, lait noh u Syiem, ka Mahadei bad ka Alice, ki la don ha phatok bad hapoh ka jingpynshitom pyniap.

Nangta ka Mahadei ka la iehnoh, ka la duh mynsiem shibun, bad ka la ong ia ka Alice, “Phi la iohi ia u Mock Turtle haduh mynta?”

“Em,” la ong ka Alice. “Ngam tip ruh kaei ka Mock Turtle.”

“Ka dei ka mar ba la shna ia ka Mock Turtle Soup,” la ong ka Mahadei.

“Ngam pat ju iohi ia kawei, ne iohsngew ia kawei,” la ong ka Alice.

“To wan, te,” la ong ka Mahadei, “bad un iathuh ia phi ia ka histori jong u.”

Katba ki dang iaid ryngkat, ka Alice ka la iohsngew ia u Syiem ba u ong da ka sur kaba jem, sha ka kynhun kyllum, “La map ia phi baroh.” “To wan, kata ka long kaba bha!” ka la ong ha lade, namar ka la sngew ba kam sngewbha shibun eh ia ka jingbun ki jingpyniap briew kiba ka Mahadei ka la hukum.

Ki la iakynduh kloi eh ia uwei u Gryphon uba la thiah lyngngai ha ka sngi. (Lada phim tip kaei ka Gryphon, peit ia ka dur.) “Kiew, ka jinglehkai!” la ong ka Mahadei, “bad ialam ia kane ka samla ban leit peit ia u Mock Turtle, bad ban iohsngew ia ka histori jong u. Nga dei ban leit phai bad peit hadien katto katne ki jingpyniap kiba nga la hukum;” bad ka la leit noh, ka da iehnoh ia ka Alice marwei bad u Griphon. Ka Alice kam da sngewtynnad than ia ka dur jong uta u jingthaw, hynrei ha ka jinglong baroh kawei ka la pyrkhat ba kan long kaba shngain bha ban sah bad u kum ban leit bud ia kata ka Mahadei kaba shyrkhei: kumta ka la ap.

U Gryphon u la shong bad u la khleh ia ki khmat jong u: nangta u la peit ia ka Mahadei haduh ba kan da jah noh na khmat: nangta u la rkhie. “Kaei ka jingsngewtynnad!” la ong u Griphon, shiteng ha lade, shiteng ha ka Alice.

“Kaei ka jingsngewtynnad?” ong ka Alice.

“Balei, ka ,” la ong u Grifon. “Ka long ka jingsngewtynnat jong ka baroh, ba: kim ju pyniap iano iano ruh, phi tip. To wan!”

“Baroh ki ong ‘to wan!’. hangne,” la pyrkhat ka Alice, katba ka dang iaid suki suki hadien jong ka: “Ngam pat ju shah hukum kumne ha ka jingim jong nga baroh kawei, ym ju!”

Kim shym la leit jngai shuwa ba kin iohi ia u Mock Turtle na jngai, uba shong sngewsih bad marwei halor kawei ka lyngkha maw kaba rit, bad, katba ki nang wan hajan, ka Alice ka la iohsngew ia u ba u ud kum ba ka dohnud jong u kan sa tyrkhong. Ka la sngewsynei ia u shibun. “Kaei ka jingsngewsih jong u?” ka la kylli ia u Grifon, bad u Grifon u la jubab, hajan eh ha kijuh ki kyntien kumba mynshuwa, “Ka long ka jingsngewtynnat jong u baroh, ba: um shym la ioh jingsngewsih ei ei, phi tip. To wan!”

Kumta ki la kiew sha u Mock Turtle uba la peit sha ki da ki khmat kiba heh kiba dap da ki ummat, hynrei um ong ei ei.

“Kane ka samla hangne,” la ong u Gryphon, “ka kwah ban tip ia ka histori jong phi, ka kwah.”

“Ngan iathuh ia ka,” la ong u Mock Turtle da ka sur kaba jylliew bad kaba jylliew: “shong, phi baroh arngut, bad wat kren wat tang shi kyntien ruh tad haduh ba ngan da dep.”

Kumta ki la shong, bad ym don ba kren katto katne minit. Ka Alice ka la pyrkhat ha lade, “Ngam iohi kumno u lah ban pyndep, lada um sdang.” Hynrei ka la ap da ka jingsbun.

“Shisien,” la ong u Khnai Mok ha kaba kut, da kaba ud jylliew, “nga la long u Khlieh shisha.”

Ia kine ki kyntien la bud sa ka jingsngap jar kaba jrong bha, kaba la pynpra tang da ka jingpyrta teng teng “Hjckrrh!” na u Griphon, bad ka jingud kaba jur kaba man ka por jong u Mock Turtle. Ka Alice ka la jan khie bad ong, “Khublei, ko sahep, ia ka jingiathuhkhana kaba sngewtynnad jong phi,” hynrei kam lah khlem da pyrkhat ba ki dei ban don shuh shuh ki ban sa wan, kumta ka la shong sngap jar bad kam ong ei ei.

“Haba ngi dang rit,” u Mock Turtle u la bteng ha kaba kut, da kaba kham jemnud, wat la u dang iam khyndiat mynta bad hadien, “ngi la leit skul ha duriaw. U kynrad u dei u Ksew uba la tymmen — ngi ju khot ia u u Ksew—”

“Balei phi khot ia u u Khlaw, lada um dei uwei?” La kylli ka Alice.

“Ngi la khot ia u u Ksew namar ba u la hikai ia ngi,” la ong u Ksew Mock da ka jingbitar: “shisha phi long uba lyngngoh shibun!”

“Phi dei ban sngewlehrain ia lade namar ba phi kylli ia ka jingkylli kaba suk kum kata,” la bynrap biang u Gryphon; bad nangta baroh arngut ki la shong sngap jar bad ki la peit ia ka Alice kaba duk, kaba la sngew ba ka la kloi ban ngam shapoh khyndew. Ha kaba khatduh u Gryphon u la ong ia u Mock Turtle, “To iaid shakhmat, ko tymmen! Wat ialehkai baroh shisngi shaphang kata!” bad u la iaid ha kine ki ktien.

“Hooid, ngi la leit skul ha duriaw, wat la phi lah ban ngeit—”

“Ngam ju ong ba ngam tip!” la pynsangeh ka Alice.

“Phi la leh,” la ong u Mock Turtle.

“To bat ia ka shyntur jong phi!” la bynrap biang u Griphon, shuwa ba ka Alice kan kren biang. U Mok Tur u la iaid shakhmat.

“Ngi la ioh ia ki jingpule kiba bha tam— ha ka jingshisha, ngi la leit skul man la ka sngi—”

“ Nga ruh nga la leit skul sngi,” la ong ka Alice; “phim donkam ban sngew sarong kum kita baroh.”

“Da ki ekstra?” la kylli u Mock Turtle da kaba sngewkhia khyndiat.

“Hooid,” la ong ka Alice, “ngi la nang ia ka ktien Phareng bad ka jingrwai.”

“Bad ka jingsait?” la ong u Mock Turtle.

“Shisha em!” la ong ka Alice da ka jingbitar.

“Ah ! te ka jong phi kam shym la long shisha ka skul kaba bha,” la ong u Mock Turtle ha ka sur kaba sngewsuk shibun. “Mynta ha iing jong ngi ki la don ha kaba kut jong ka bill, ‘Ka ktien Phareng, ka jingrwai bad ka jingsait jain — kaba kham heh.’”

“Phim lah ban kwah ia ka shibun,” la ong ka Alice; “kiba im ha trai duriaw.”

“Ngam lah ban shah ban nang ia ka.” la ong u Mock Turtle da kaba ud. “Nga la shim tang ia ka jinghikai kaba man ka por.”

“Kaei kata?” la kylli ka Alice.

“Ka jingkyrhuh bad jingkyrhuh, ho’oid, ban sdang,” la jubab u Mock Turtle; “bad nangta ki tnat bapher bapher jong ka Arithmetic — Ka jingkwah burom, ka jingpynthut, ka jingpynjah burom bad ka jingpynjah burom.”

“Ngam pat ju iohsngew ia ka ‘Jingpynjah burom’,” ka Alice ka la nud ban ong. “Kaei kata?”

U Gryphon u la pynkiew ia la ki kjat baroh ar tylli da ka jinglyngngoh. “Kaei! Ngam pat ju iohsngew shaphang ka jingpynjah burom!” ka la pyrta. “Phi tip kaei ka jingpynitynnad, nga tharai?”

“Hooid,” la ong ka Alice da ka jingartatien: “ka mut — ban — pynlong — ia kano kano kaei kaei — kaba kham itynnad.”

“Hooid, te,” u Gryphon u la bteng, “lada phim tip ia kaei kaba pynjah burom, phi long u briew uba rit paid.”

Ka Alice kam sngew shlur ban kylli jingkylli shuh shaphang kata, kumta ka la phai sha u Mock Turtle, bad ka la ong “Kaei pat kaba phi don ban nang?”

“Hooid, ka la don ka Jingmaian,” u Mock Turtle u la jubab, da kaba khein ia ki sobjek ha ki flapper jong u, “—Ka Jingmaian, kaba rim bad kaba mynta, ryngkat bad ka Jingpule Duriaw: nangta ka Jingpule — u nonghikai u la long u dohkha conger-eel uba la rim bha, uba ju wan shisien shi taiew: u la hikai ia ngi ha ka jingrwai, ka jingrwai, ka jingrwai.”

“Kata ka la long kumno?” ong ka Alice.

“To, ngam lah ban pyni hi ia phi,” la ong u Mock Turtle: “Nga long uba jemnud palat. Bad u Gryphon um ju nang ia kata.”

“Ngam shym la don por,” la ong u Gryphon: “Nga la leit sha u nonghikai Classics, wat la katta ruh. U la long u krab uba la tymmen, u la long.”

“Ngam ju leit sha u,” u Mock Turtle u la ong da kaba sngap jar: “u la hikai ia ka Jingrkhie bad ka Jingsngewsih, ki ju ong.”

“Kumta u la leh, kumta u la leh,” la ong u Gryphon, da kaba ud ha ka por jong u; bad baroh ar ki jingthaw ki buhrieh ia la ki khmat ha ki tyrpeng jong ki.

“Bad katno kynta ha ka shisngi phi ju leh ia ki jinghikai?” la ong ka Alice, da kaba kyrkieh ban pynkylla ia ka phang.

“Shi kynta ha ka sngi kaba nyngkong,” la ong u Mock Turtle: “hynriew kynta ha ka sngi kaba bud, bad kumta ter ter.”

“Kaei ka plan kaba sngewtynnad!” la pyrta ka Alice.

“Dei na kata ka daw ba la khot ia ki ki jinghikai,” u Gryphon u la kynthoh: “namar ba ki nang duna na kawei ka sngi sha kawei pat.”

Kane ka la long ka jingmut kaba thymmai shibun ia ka Alice, bad ka la pyrkhat bha khyndiat shuwa ba kan kren ia ka jingkren kaba bud. “Te ka sngi kaba shiphew ka dei ban long ka shuti?”

“Hooid ka la long kumta,” la ong u Mock Turtle.

“Bad kumno phi la lah ha ka sngi kaba khatar?” Ka Alice ka la iaid shakhmat da ka jingsngewtynnat.

“Kata ka la biang shaphang ki jinghikai,” u Gryphon u la pynsangeh da ka sur kaba kut jingmut bha: “iathuh ei ei ia ka shaphang ki jingialehkai mynta.”
