SHAPITA X.
Tshiamu tshia lobster

Mock Turtle wakapuekesha luya lukole, ne wakakoka nyima wa tshiadi tshimue ku mêsu ende. Wakatangila Alice, ne wakateta bua kuakula, kadi bua minite umue anyi ibidi, kudila kua dîyi diende kujika. "Anu bu uvua ne mufuba mu nshingu wende," ke muvua Gryphon muambe: ne wakatuadija kumunyunguluka ne kumukuma ku nyima. Ndekelu wa bionso, Mock Turtle wakapingaja dîyi diende, ne, ne binsonji bivua bimuela pa matama ende, wakatungunuka kabidi ne:—

"Udi mua kuikala kuyi musombele muinshi mua mbuu bikole—" ("Tshiena," ke muvua Alice muambe) - "ne pamuapa kabavua banji kukumanyisha nyama wa nsengu to—" (Alice wakatuadija kuamba ne: "Mvua mudie—" kadi wakadikonkonona ne lukasa, ne wakamba ne: "Tòo, nansha kakese") "-nanku kuena mua kumanya mudi Lobster Quadrille tshintu tshilenga to!"

"Tòo, bulelela," ke muakamba Alice. "I manaya a mushindu kayi?"

"Bua tshinyi," ke muakamba Gryphon, "udi mua kuanji kuenza mulongo ku muelelu wa mbuu—"

"Milongo ibidi!" wakadila. “Nyunyi ya foke, nkunda, mishipa ya saumon, ne bikuabu; pashishe, paudi umbusha mishipa yonso ya jelly-fish mu njila—”

"—udi uya kumpala misangu ibidi—"

"Yonso ne mushipa wa mbuji bu muena mudimu nende!" Gryphon wakadila.

"Bushuwa," Mock Turtle unena: "kuya kumpala misangu ibidi, kulongolola kudi balunda—"

"-shintulula nyama ya nsengu, ne uya mu bulongame bumue," ke muvua Gryphon mutungunuke.

"Pashishe, udi mumanye," Mock Turtle wakatungunuka, "udi wela—"

"—mutantshi mule ne mbuu muudi mua kufika—"

"Shintulula kabidi nyama ya nsengu!" Gryphon wakadila ne dîyi dikole.

"Kupingana pa buloba kabidi, ne etshi ke tshimfuanyi tshia kumpala," ke muakamba Nzuji wa Dishima, mupuekesha dîyi diende mu tshimpitshimpi; ne bifukibua bibidi ebi, bivua bienda bituluka bu bintu bia bupote tshikondo tshionso etshi, biakasomba kabidi ne kanyinganyinga kakole ne mu ditalala, ne kutangila Alice.

"Bidi ne tshia kuikala dinaya dilenga be," ke muakamba Alice ne buôwa buonso.

"Udi musue kumona kakese anyi?" ke muvua Mock Turtle muambe.

Alice unena, "Bya bine bine."

"Luaku, tuteta tshimfuanyi tshia kumpala!" ke muvua Mock Turtle muambe kudi Gryphon. "Tudi mua kuenza kakuyi mbua, udi mumanye. Nnganyi udi mua kuimba?"

"Oh, udi wimba," i Gryphon unena. "Ndi mupue miaku muoyo."

"Tuasakidila, ndinaya dilenga dia kutangila," ke muakamba Alice, udiumvua ne disanka dia bungi bua mudibi bijika ndekelu wa bionso: "ne ndi munange musambu wa dikema udi wakula bua tshitoke!"

"Oh, pa bidi bitangila tshitoka," ke muvua Mock Turtle muambe, "bobu—ukadi mubamone, bulelela anyi?"

"Eyowa," ke muakamba Alice, "Mvua mubamone misangu ya bungi mu dinner—" wakaditangila lukasa.

"Tshiena mumanye muaba udi Dinn mua kuikala to," ke muvua Mock Turtle muambe, "kadi wewe mubamone misangu ya bungi, bushuwa udi mumanye mudibu."

"Bualu budi ne," ke muakamba Gryphon, "ne: bavua mua kuya ne nyama ya nsengu ku maja. Nunku bakayiela mu mbuu. Nunku bavua ne bua kukuluka mutantshi mule. Nunku bakapeta mikila yabu lukasa mu mishiku yabu. Nunku kabavua mua kuyipatula kabidi to. Ke tshionso."

"Ndi mua kukuambila bia bungi kupita apu, wewa musue," ke muakamba Gryphon. "Udi mumanye bua tshinyi badi bamubikila ne: whiting anyi?"

" Bidi bienza bisabata ne bisabata ," ke muvua Gryphon muandamune ne kanemu konso.

Alice uvua mubungame bikole. "Bua bisabata ne bisabata!" wakapingaja ne dîyi dia dikema.

"Bua tshinyi, bisabata biebe mbienza ne tshinyi?" Gryphon unena. "Ndi musue kuamba ne, ntshinyi tshidi tshienza bua bikale bakenka nunku?"

Alice wakabatangila panshi, ne kuela meji kakese kumpala kua yeye kufila diandamuna diende. "Mbajikije ne difiika, ndi ngitabuja."

"Nsapatu ne bisabata muinshi mua mbuu," Gryphon wakatungunuka ne dîyi dikole, "mbijika ne dilengeja. Mpindieu udi mumanye."

"Ne mbienza ne tshinyi?" Alice wakebeja ne dîyi dia dijinga dia kumanya malu a bungi.

"Makasa ne ngulube, bulelela," Gryphon wakandamuna ne dipanga lutulu: "tshisuku tshionso tshivua mua kukuambila bualu ebu."

Alice udi wela meji anu ku musambu wakamba ne: “Bu meme muikale mutoke, mvua mua kuikala muambile mbuji ne: ‘Ikala panyima, tuasakidila: katuena basue bua wikale netu to!’”

"Bavua ne bua kuikala nende," ke muvua Mock Turtle muambe: "kakuena mushipa wa meji uvua mua kuya muaba nansha umue kayi ne porpoise to."

"Kakuenaku bulelela anyi?" ke muakamba Alice ne dîyi dia dikema dikole.

"Bushuwa ki mmuomu to," ke muvua Mock Turtle muambe: "bua tshinyi, bikala mushipa mulue kundi , ne mungambile ne: uvua uya mu luendu, mvua ne bua kuamba ne: 'Ne porpoise kayi?'"

"Kuena musue kuamba 'tshipatshila' anyi?" Alice unena.

"Ndi musue kuamba tshindi ngamba," ke muvua Mock Turtle muandamune ne dîyi dia tshiji. Ne Gryphon wakamba kabidi ne: “Luaku, tuteleje amue malu audi muenze.”

"Mvua mua kukuambila malu amvua muenze—kutuadijila mu dinda edi," ke muakamba Alice ne buôwa bukese: "kadi kabiena ne mushinga bua kupingana ku malaba to, bualu mvua muntu mukuabu tshikondo atshi."

"Umvuija bionso abi," i ami unena Nzolo.

"Tòo, tòo! Malu a kukema kumpala," ke muakamba Gryphon ne dîyi dia dipanga lutulu: "diumvuija didi diangata tshikondo tshikole menemene."

Uvua ne buôwa bua bualu ebu ku ntuadijilu, bifukibua bibidi ebi biakasemena pabuipi nende, tshimue ku luseke luonso, ne biakangula mêsu ne mishiku yabi bikole menemene , kadi wakapeta dikima pavuaye wenda uya kumpala.
