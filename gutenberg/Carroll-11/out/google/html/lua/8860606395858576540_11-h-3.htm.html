SHAPITA III.
Lubilu lua Caucus ne muyuki mule

Bavua bushuwa tshisumbu tshia bantu bavua badisangishe ku muelelu wa mâyi—nyunyi ne mapuapua avua makoka, nyama ne miosa yabu milamate pabuipi nabu, ne bionso bivua bitoka mâyi, bivua binyanguke ne kabiyi bimpe to.

Lukonku lua kumpala luvua ne: mushindu kayi wa kuondopa kabidi: bavua bayukila bua bualu ebu, ne kunyima kua minite mikese bivua bimueneka bu bualu bua pa tshibidilu kudi Alice bua kudimona uyukila nabu bimpe, bienze anu bu ne: uvua mubamanye matuku ende onso a muoyo. Bushuwa, uvua ne dikokangana dikole ne Lory, uvua ndekelu wa bionso mulue tshiji, ne uvua anu wamba ne: “Ndi mukulumpe kukupita, ne ndi ne bua kumanya bimpe;” ne bualu ebu Alice kavua mua kuitaba kayi mumanye bidimu biabu to, ne, bu muvua Lory mubenge bua kuamba bidimu biabu, kakuvua bualu bukuabu bua kuamba to.

Ndekelu wa bionso Mbeba, uvua umueneka bu muntu wa bukokeshi munkatshi muabu, wakela dîyi ne: “Sombayi, nuenu bonso, nuteleje, nennuvuije bikumbane mu katupa kîpi emu!” Bonso bakasomba musangu umue, mu tshijengu tshinene, ne Mbeba munkatshi. Alice uvua anu mutangile ku tshintu atshi ne tunyinganyinga, bualu uvua wela meji ne: uvua mua kupeta mashika makole yeye kayi muume lukasa.

"Ahem!" ke muakamba mbeba ne luya lua mushinga mukole, "nudi badiakaje anyi? Etshi ke tshintu tshitambe bukole tshindi mumanye. Ditalala dia muaba wonso, nuenu basue! 'William the Conqueror, uvua bualu buende buanyishibue kudi pape, wakatumikila mutantshi mukese kudi bena Angleterre, bavua bakeba balombodi, ne uvua muibidilangane bikole ne dikuata dia bintu ne dikuata dia matunga, ne bena Morbriciam ba ku Nord'"

"Ugh!" ke muakamba Lory, ne kanyinganyinga.

"Ndi nkusengelela luse!" ke muakamba Mbeba, ne mpala mubi, kadi ne kanemu konso: "Udi muakule anyi?"

"Ki ami to!" ke muakamba Lory ne lukasa.

"Mvua ngela meji ne uvua mumanye," ke muakamba Mbeba. "—Ndi ntungunuka. 'Edwin ne Morcar, bamfumu ba Mercia ne Northumbria, bakamuambila ne: ne nansha Stigand, muepiskopo munene wa Canterbury uvua munange ditunga diende, wakamona ne: bivua bimpe—'"

"Udi mupeta tshinyi ?" ke muakamba Ntambue.

"Ndi mupeta ," Mbeba wakandamuna ne dîyi dikole: "bushuwa udi mumanye tshidi 'tshidi' tshiumvuija."

"Ndi mumanye tshidi 'tshidi' tshiumvuija bimpa, pandi mpeta tshintu," ke muakamba Ntambue: "tshitu anu tshisuku anyi nsese. Lukonku ludi ne: Ntshinyi tshivua muepiskopo munene mupete?"

Mbeba kavua mumone lukonku elu to, kadi wakatungunuka ne lukasa luonso ne: “’—wakamona ne: bivua bimpe bua kuya ne Edgar Atheling bua kusambakena ne William ne kumupesha tshifulu tshia butumbi. Ku ntuadijilu, William uvua ne bikadilu biakane. Kadi tshikisu tshia bena Normandie bende—’ Udi munyi mpindieu, munanga wanyi?” biakatungunuka, bikudimuka kudi Alice pavuaye wakula.

Alice wakamba ne dîyi dia kanyinganyinga ne: “Mmutoke anu bu kumpala: “katshiena tshimueneka bu tshidi tshinyumisha nansha kakese to.”

"Mu mushindu ewu," ke muakamba Dodo ne kanemu konso, ujuka ku makasa ende, "Ndi nlomba bua tshisangilu tshikale tshijika, bua kuangata diakamue mishindu ya bungi ya dijikija lutetuku—"

"Akula Anglais!" ke muakamba Mukunji. "Tshiena mumanye diumvuija dia tshitupa tshia miaku mile eyi to, ne, tshidi tshipite apu, tshiena ngitaba ne: wewe pebe udi mumanye to!" Ne Nyunyi wa Nyunyi wakainama mutu wende bua kusokoka diseka: bamue ba ku nyunyi mikuabu bavua bela mbila mikole.

"Tshimvua musue kuamba," ke muvua Dodo muambe ne dîyi dia tshiji, "tshivua ne: tshintu tshimpe tshia kutufikisha ku dikala ne lubilu lua Caucus."

"Lubilu lua Caucus ntshinyi ?" Alice unena; ki mbua se uvua musue kumanya malu a bungi to, kadi Dodo uvua muimane anu bu uvua wela meji ne: muntu kampanda uvua ne bua kuakula, ne kakuvua muntu mukuabu uvua umueneka musue kuamba bualu nansha bumue to.

"Bua tshinyi," ke muakamba Dodo, "mushindu mutambe buimpe wa kumvuija nkuenza." (Ne, bu muudi mua kujinga kuteta bualu ebu nkayebe, dituku dikuabu dia mu muvu wa mashika, nenkuambile mushindu uvua Dodo muenze bualu ebu.)

Tshia kumpala tshiakaleja muaba wa lubilu, mu mushindu kampanda wa tshijengu, ("mushindu mulelela kawena ne bualu to," tshiakamba,) ne pashishe tshisumbu tshijima tshiakatekibua mu njila, apa ne aku. Kakuvua “Umue, babidi, basatu ne kule” to, kadi bavua batuadija kunyema dîba divuabu basue, ne balekela dîba divuabu basue, nunku kabivua bipepele bua kumanya dîba divua lubilu lujika to. Kadi, pavuabu benda banyema munkatshi mua tshitupa tshia dîba anyi kupita apu, ne bavua kabidi baume bikole, Dodo wakabikila mu tshimpitshimpi ne: “Lubilu ndujike!” ne buonso buabu bakadisangisha pabuipi natshi, bela mupuya, ne bebeja ne: “Kadi nnganyi udi mutshimune?”

Lukonku elu Dodo kavua mua kuandamuna kayi wela meji bikole to, ne wakasomba mutantshi mule ne munu umue mufina pa mpala wende (muaba uudi utamba kumona Shakespeare, mu bimfuanyi biende), pavua bakuabu bindile mu ditalala. Ndekelu wa bionso Dodo wakamba ne, " Muntu yense mmutshimune, ne bonso badi ne tshia kupeta mafutu."

"Kadi nnganyi udi ne tshia kufila bintu?" tshisumbu tshia mêyi tshiakakonka.

"Bua tshinyi, yeye , bulelela," ke muakamba Dodo, uleja Alice ne munu umue; ne tshisumbu tshijima tshiakamunyunguluka diakamue, bela mbila mu mushindu wa dipanga dia lungenyi ne: “Mapa! Mapa!”

Alice kavua mumanye tshia kuenza to, ne mu dipanga ditekemena wakateka tshianza tshiende mu tshibombi tshiende, ne kupatulaye kazubu ka bintu bia kudia, (diakalenga dia se: mâyi a mukele kaavua mabuelemu to), ne wakabifila bu difutu. Kuvua anu tshitupa tshimue, tshinyunguluke.

"Kadi udi ne tshia kuikala ne difutu yeye nkayende, udi mumanye," ke muakamba Mpuku.

"Bushuwa," Dodo wakandamuna ne kanyinganyinga kakole. "Ntshinyi tshikuabu tshiudi natshi mu tshibombi tshiebe?" wakatungunuka, kukudimuka kudi Alice.

"Anu tshianza," ke muakamba Alice ne kanyinganyinga.

"Ufile apa," i ami unena Dodo.

Pashishe bonso bakadisangisha kabidi kudiye, pavua Dodo ufila tshianza ne kanemu konso, wamba ne: “Tudi tukusengelela bua kuitaba tshianza etshi tshilenga;” ne, pakajikijaye muyuki muipi eu, bonso bakasanka.

Alice uvua wela meji ne: bualu ebu buonso buvua bua tshianana, kadi buonso buabu buvua bumueneka bu bubi menemene, ke bualu kayi kavua ne dikima dia kuseka to; ne, bu muvuaye kayi mua kuela meji a bualu bua kuamba, wakainama anu, ne kuangataye tshianza, umueneka ne kanemu konso.

Bualu buvua bulonda buvua bua kudia bimuma bia comfits: bualu ebu buvua buenza mutoyi ne tshimvundu, bualu nyunyi minene ivua idila bualu kayivua mua kulaba yayi to, ne mikese ivua ifua ne bavua ne bua kuyikuma ku nyima. Kadi, ndekelu wa bionso biakajika, ne bakasomba kabidi mu tshijengu, ne kusengelela Mbeba bua kubambila bualu bukuabu.

"Uvua mulaye bua kungambila malu ebe a kale, udi mumanye," ke muakamba Alice, "ne bua tshinyi udi mukine—C ne D," wakasakidila mu mutoyi, ne buôwa bua se: nebakuenzele bibi kabidi.

"Wanyi mmuyuki mule ne wa dibungama!" ke muakamba Mbeba, ukudimuka kudi Alice, ne wela mbila.

" I mukila mule, bulelela," i ami unena Alice, utangila panshi ne dikema ku mukila wa mbeba; "kadi bua tshinyi udi ubikila bualu ebu ne dibungama?" Ne wakatungunuka ne kuela meji bua bualu ebu pavua Mbeba wakula, nunku lungenyi luende lua muyuki luvua anu bu elu:—

"Kuena uya to!" ke muakamba mbeba kudi Alice ne tshikisu. "Udi wela meji a tshinyi?"

"Ndi nkusengelela luse," ke muakamba Alice ne budipuekeshi buonso: "uvua mufike ku tshijengu tshitanu, ndi ngela meji anyi?"

"Tshivua mumanye to! " wakadila Mbeba, ne tshiji tshikole.

"Nsongo!" ke muakamba Alice, misangu yonso mudiakaje bua kudivuija wa mushinga, ne umutangila ne tunyinganyinga. "Oh, lekela ngakwashe bua kutshijikija!"

"Tshiakuenza bualu nansha bumue bua nunku to," ke muakamba Mpuku, kujukaye ne kuyaye. "Udi unpenda pa kuakula malu a bupote nunku!"

"Tshivua musue kuamba nanku to!" Alice mupele wakasengelela. "Kadi udi ufika munda bipepele, udi mumanye!"

Mpuku uvua anu wela mbila bua kuandamuna.

"Ndi nkulomba bua upingane ne ujikije muyuki webe!" Alice wakabikila panyima pa bualu ebu; ne bakuabu bonso bakadisangisha ne: “Eyowa, ndi nkusengelela!” kadi Mbeba wakakudimuna mutwe wende kayi ne lutulu, ne wakaya lubilu kakese.

"Mbibi bua se kabivua mua kushala to!" Lory wakapuekesha milongo, diakamue pavuaye kayi umueneka to; ne Crabe mukulakaje wakangata mpunga wa kuambila muanende wa bakaji ne: “Aa, munanga wanyi! Etshi tshikale dilongesha kuudi bua kubenga kujimija tshiji tshiebe nansha kakese!” "Ikala ne ludimi luebe, Mamu!" unena nsongalume Crabe, ne tshiji tshikole. "Udi mukumbane bua kuteta lutulu lua tshisuku!"

"Ndi musue kuikala ne Dina wetu apa, ndi mumanye ne ndi nende!" Alice wakamba ne dîyi dikole, kayi wakula ne muntu nansha umue to. "Udi mua kutshipingaja mu katupa kîpi emu!"

"Ne Dinah i nganyi, shi ndi mua kuela lukonku elu?" Lory unena.

Alice wakamuandamuna ne muoyo mujima, bualu uvua misangu yonso mudiakaje bua kuakula bua nyama wende wa tshisuku ne: “Dinah mpushi wetu. Ne udi ne meji a bungi bua kukuata mpuku iudi kuyi mua kuela meji!

Muyuki eu wakakebesha tshimvundu tshia dikema munkatshi mua tshisumbu. Bamue nyunyi bakaya lukasa lukasa: nyunyi mukuabu wakatuadija kudibuikila ne budimu buonso, wamba ne: “Bushuwa ndi mfika kumbelu; luya lua butuku kaluena lukumbane to!” ne kanari wakabikila bana bende ne dîyi dia kuzakala ne: “Luayi, bana betu, dîba diakakumbana bua nuenu bonso balale!” Bua malu mashilangane, buonso buabu bakaya, ne Alice wakashala nkayende mutantshi mukese.

“Mvua musue bu meme tshiyi mutele Dina to!” wakadiambila ne dîyi dia kanyinganyinga. "Kakuena muntu udi umueneka umunanga, panshi apa, ne ndi mujadike ne udi mpushi mutambe buimpe pa buloba bujima! Oh, munanga wanyi Dinah! Ndi ndiebeja ni ndi mua kukumona kabidi!" Ne apa Alice mupele wakatuadija kudila kabidi, bualu uvua udiumvua nkayende bikole ne mutekete mu mikolo. Mu katupa kîpi, nansha nanku, wakumvua kabidi mutoyi wa makasa mu bule, ne wakabandisha mêsu ne disanka dionso, wela meji ne: Mbeba uvua mushintulule lungenyi luende, ne uvua upingana bua kujikija muyuki wende.
