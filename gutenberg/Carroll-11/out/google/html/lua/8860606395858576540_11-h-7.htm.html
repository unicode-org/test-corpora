SHAPITA VII.
Tshibilu tshia tshiayi tshia bupote

Kuvua mêsa avua muinshi mua mutshi kumpala kua nzubu, ne March Hare ne Hatter bavua banua tshiayi: Dormouse uvua musombe pankatshi pabu, mulale tulu tukole, ne bakuabu babidi bavua benza nende mudimu bu tshilamba tshia kulala, bateke bikono biabu pa mutu pawu, ne bakula pa mutu pawu. "Bivua bibi menemene bua Dormouse," ke muvua Alice wela meji; "anu, bu mudiye mulale, ndi ngela meji ne kena ne bualu to."

Mêsa avua manene, kadi bonso basatu bavua badisangishe pamue ku ditumba diawu: “Kakuena muaba! Kakuena muaba!” bakadila pavuabu bamone Alice ulua. "Muaba udi muvule !" Alice udi wamba ne tshiji, ne wakasomba mu nkuasa munene ku muelelu umua wa mesa.

"Nuayi mvinyo," ke muvua March Hare muambe ne dîyi dia dikankamija.

Alice wakatangila ku mesa onso, kadi kakuvua tshintu to, anu tshiayi. Wakamba ne: “Tshiena mmona mvinyo nansha umue to.”

"Kakuena nansha umue," i ami unena March Hare.

Alice unena ne tshiji ne: “Pashishe, kabivua bimpe bua wewe kufila to.”

"Kabivua biakane bua wewe kusomba kuyi mubikidibue to," ke muakamba March Hare.

Alice wakamba ne: “Tshivua mumanye ne: uvua mêsa ebe to; "mmuteka bua bantu ba bungi kupita basatu."

"Nsuki yebe idi ikengela kukosa," ke muakamba Muena Nsuki. Uvua mutangile Alice mutantshi mule ne dijinga dikole dia kumanya malu, ne eu uvua muyuki wende wa kumpala.

"Udi ne tshia kulonga bua kubenga kuamba malu a pa nkayebe," Alice unena na ndambu wa tshikisu; "mbibi menemene."

Muena tshifulu wakakangula mêsu ende bikole pakumvuaye bualu ebu; kadi tshivuaye wamba anu ne: “Bua tshinyi nkutshi udi bu mesa a kufunda nawu?”

"Luaku, netusanke mpindieu!" Alice wela meji. "Ndi ne disanka bua mudibu batuadije kuela nsumuinu.—Ndi ngitabuja ne: ndi mua kuteta bualu ebu," wakasakidila ne dîyi dikole.

"Udi musue kuamba ne udi wela meji ne udi mua kupeta diandamuna anyi?" ke muvua March Hare muambe.

"Ke mudibi menemene," ke muakamba Alice.

"Pashishe udi ne tshia kuamba tshiudi musue kuamba," March Hare wakatungunuka.

"Ndi mumanye," Alice wakandamuna ne lukasa; "nansha—nansha ndi musue kuamba tshindi ngamba—ke bualu bumue, udi mumanye."

"Kabiena bia muomumue nansha kakese!" ke muakamba Muena Tshilamba. “Udi mua kuamba anu ne: ‘Ndi mmona tshindi ndia’ ntshintu tshimue ne ‘ndi ndia tshindi mmona’!”

"Udi mua kuamba kabidi," ke muvua March Hare muambe kabidi ne: 'Ndi musue tshindi mpeta' ntshintu tshimue ne 'Ndi mpeta tshindi musue'!"

"Udi mua kuamba kabidi," ke muvua Dormouse, uvua umueneka bu uvua wakula mu tulu tuende, wamba kabidi ne: 'Ndi npuyakana pandi nlala' ntshintu tshimue ne 'Ndi nlala pandi npuyakana'!"

" Bidi bia muomumue ne wewe," ke muakamba muena tshifulu, ne apa muyuki wakajika, ne tshisumbu tshiakashala tshipuwe bua minite mikese, pavua Alice wela meji bua bionso bivuaye mua kuvuluka bua nyunyi ya nkutshi ne mesa a kufunda nawu, bivua kabiyi bia bungi to.

Muena tshifulu ke uvua wa kumpala kutshimuna ditalala. "Ndituku kayi dia ngondo?" unena, ukudimuka kudi Alice: uvua mupatule tshisanji tshienda mu tshibombi tshienda, ne uvua utshitangila kayi ne ditalala, utshinyunguluka misangu ne misangu, ne utshikuata ku ditshi diende.

Alice wakela meji kakese, ne pashishe kuambaye ne: “Muinayi.”

"Matuku abidi mabi!" Muena nsuki wakapuekesha milongo. "Mvua mukuambile ne: manteka kaavua mua kukumbana bua midimu to!" wakasakidila utangila March Hare ne tshiji.

"Ivua manteka mimpe ," ke muvua March Hare muandamune ne bupole buonso.

"Eyowa, kadi bimue bintu bivua ne bua kuikala bibuele kabidi," ke muvua Muenji wa Nkanzu muele mbila: "kuvua ne bua kubibueja ne tshingoma tshia mampa to."

Muana wa March Hare wakangata tshiamu tshia kumona natshi dîba ne kutshitangila ne kanyinganyinga: pashishe wakatshiela mu dikopo diende dia tshiayi, ne kutshitangila kabidi: kadi kavua mua kuela meji a bualu bukuabu buimpe bua kuamba pa kumbusha mêyi ende a kumpala a ne: “Uvua manteka mimpe menemene , udi mumanye.”

Alice uvua mutangile pa dikaya diende ne dijinga dia kumanya. "Nsapi ya tuseku kayipu!" yeye kuamba. “Tshidi tshileja dituku dia ngondo, ne katshiena tshileja diba to!”

"Bua tshinyi bidi ne tshia kuikala nanku?" Muena tshifulu wakatuta. "Nsa yebe idi ikuambila tshidimu tshidi anyi?"

Alice wakandamuna ne lukasa luonso ne: “Bushuwa, ki mmuomu to: “kadi mbualu bidi bishala tshidimu tshimue mutantshi mule pamue.”

"Tshidi anu bualu buanyi ," ke muakamba Muenji wa Nkanzu.

Alice wakadiumvua mubungame bikole, meyi a Muena tshifulu kaavua ne diumvuija nansha dimue to, kadi avua bushuwa a mu Anglais. "Tshiena mumvue bimpe to," ke muvuaye muambe ne kanemu konso.

"Mbeba wa tshilala benyi mmulale kabidi," ke muakamba muena tshifulu, ne wakapongolola tshiayi tshikese tshia kapia pa mushiku wende.

Mbeba wa tshilala benyi wakakudimuna mutwe wende kayi ne lutulu, ne wakamba, kayi mubulule mêsu ende ne: “Bushuwa, bulelela; anu tshimvua musue kuamba meme nkayanyi.”

"Ukadiku mujingulule tshishima anyi?" muena tshifulu unena, ukudimuka kabidi kudi Alice.

Alice wakandamuna ne: “Tòo, ndi mulekele: “ndiandamuna kayi?”

"Tshiena ne lungenyi nansha lukese to," ke muakamba Muenji wa Nkanzu.

"Nansha ami," i ami unena March Hare.

Alice wakapuekesha milongo ne ditshioka. "Ndi ngela meji ne udi mua kuenza bualu buimpe ne dîba," ke muvuaye muambe, "pamutu pa kujimija mu diela nkonko idi kayiyi ne mandamuna."

"Bu wewe mumanye Dîba bimpe bu mundi meme," ke muakamba Muenji wa Tshifulu, "kuvua mua kuakula bua dijimija to. Nyeye ."

Alice wakamba ne: “Tshiena mumanye tshiudi musue kuamba to.”

"Bushuwa kuena mumanye to!" Muenji wa tshifulu unena, wela mutu wandi ne kanyinganyinga. "Ndi mua kuamba ne: kuena muanji kuakula ne Time to!"

Alice wakandamuna ne budimu buonso ne: “Pamuapa ki mmuomu to: kadi ndi mumanye ne: ndi ne bua kupita dîba pandi ndonga mizike.”

Muenji wa bifulu wakamba ne: “Aa! "Kena mua kuimana bua kumukuma to. Mpindieu, wewe anu muikale nende mu malanda mimpe, uvua mua kuenza pabuipi ne tshionso tshiudi musue ne dîba. Tshilejilu, tuangate ne: dîba dia 9 wa dinda, anu dîba dia kutuadija malongesha: udi anu ne bua kuela dîyi dia dîba, ne dîba didi dienda mu tshitupa tshîpi, tshitupa tshia dîba dia dilolo!"

("Ndi anu musue ne: bivua nanku," ke muvua March Hare muambe munda muende mu mutoyi.)

"Bidi mua kuikala bilenga, bushuwa," ke muvua Alice muambe ne meji onso: "kadi pashishe—tshiena ne bua kuikala ne nzala ya bualu ebu to, udi mumanye."

"Ku ntuadijilu, pamuapa," ke muakamba Muenji wa tshifulu: "kadi udi mua kulama too ne pa dîba dimue ne tshitupa mutantshi wonso uudi musue."

"Ke mushindu uudi wenza malu anyi?" Alice wakakonka.

Muena nsuki wakakudimuna mutwe wende ne kanyinganyinga. "Ki ami to!" yeye kuandamuna. "Tuakakokangana mu ngondo muisatu mushala—kumpala kua yeye kulua mutshimbakane, udi mumanye—" (uleja ne tshibombi tshiende tshia tshiayi ku March Hare,) "—bivua mu tshibilu tshinene tshivua mukalenge mukaji wa mitshima muenze, ne mvua ne tshia kuimba

'Twinkle, twinkle, muana wa nkutshi!
Ndi ndiebeja tshiudi wenza!'

Udi mumanye musambu, pamuapa anyi?”

Alice wakamba ne: “Nkadi mumvue bualu bua nunku.

"Bidi bitungunuka, udi mumanye," muena tshifulu wakatungunuka, "mu mushindu ewu:—

'Pa mutu pa buloba udi ubuuka,
Bu tshibombi tshia tshiayi mu diulu.
Twinkle, twinkle—'”

Muaba eu, mbeba wa tshilala benyi wakadinyunguluka, ne kutuadijaye kuimba mu tulu tuende ne: “ Twinkle, twinkle, twinkle, twinkle —” ne wakatungunuka mutantshi mule menemene, ke bualu kayi bavua ne bua kumukuma bua kuimana.

"Eyowa, mvua anu mujikije mvese wa kumpala," ke muakamba muena tshifulu, "pavua mukalenge mukaji mujuke ne wela mbila ne: 'Udi ushipa dîba! Umbushe mutu wende!'"

"Bubi bua dikema!" Alice unena.

"Katshia anu pinapu," muena nsongo wakatungunuka ne dîyi dia kanyinganyinga, "kena wenza bualu bundi mmulomba to! Mpindieu dîba dia isambombo."

Lungenyi luimpe luakalua mu mutu wa Alice. "Ke bualu kayi badi bateka bintu bia bungi bia tshiayi apa anyi?" wakakonka.

"Eyowa, ke tshidibi," ke muakamba Muenji wa tshifulu ne mupuya muimpe: "tshikondo tshia kunua tshiayi, ne katuena ne dîba dia kusukula bintu pankatshi pa bikondo to."

"Pashishe udi utungunuka ne kuendakana, ndi ngela meji anyi?" Alice unena.

"Ke mudibi menemene," ke muakamba Muenji wa Nkanzu: "bu mudi bintu bienda bijika."

"Kadi ntshinyi tshidi tshienzeka paudi ulua kabidi ku ntuadijilu?" Alice wakadienzeja bua kuebeja.

"Fuanyikijabi ne tudi bashintulule tshiena bualu," March Hare wakamukosa, wela mbila. "Ndi mutshioke bua bualu ebu. Ndi nsungula nsongakaji atulondele muyuki."

"Ndi ne buôwa bua se: tshiena mumanye nansha umue to," ke muakamba Alice, muikale ne buôwa bua dilomba edi.

"Pashishe Dormouse neenze!" bonso babidi bakadila. "Juka, Dormouse!" Ne bakatshikuma ku nseke yonso ibidi musangu umue.

Mbeba wa tshilala benyi wakakangula mêsu ende bitekete bitekete. “Tshivua mulale to,” ke muvuaye muambe ne dîyi dikole ne ditekete: “Mvua mumvue mêyi onso anudi nuamba.”

"Tuambila muyuki!" ke muvua March Hare muambe.

"Eyowa, ndi nkusengelela!" Alice wakasengelela.

"Ne ikala ne lukasa," ke muakamba muena tshifulu, "anyi neulale kabidi kumpala kua kujika."

"Musangu kampanda kuvua bana betu ba bakaji basatu," ke muvua Dormouse mutuadije ne lukasa luonso; “Ne mêna abu avua Elsie, Lacie, ne Tillie; ne bavua basombele kuinshi kua tshina tshia mâyi—”

"Badi basomba ne tshinyi?" ke muvua Alice muambe, uvua misangu yonso uditatshisha bikole bua nkonko ya kudia ne kunua.

"Bavua basombela ku mampa," ke muvua Dormouse muambe, kunyima kua yeye mumane kuela meji minite umue anyi ibidi.

"Kabavua mua kuenza nanku to, udi mumanye," Alice wakamba ne bupole buonso; "bavua mua kuikala basama."

"Nunku bavua," ke muvua Dormouse muambe; "musama bikole ."

Alice wakateta bua kuela meji mudi nsombelu wa pa buende eu mua kuikala, kadi biakamutonda bikole, ke yeye kutungunuka ne kuamba ne: “Kadi bua tshinyi bavua basombele kuinshi kua tshina tshia mâyi?”

"Angata kabidi tshiayi," ke muvua March Hare muambile Alice, ne muoyo mujima.

Alice wakandamuna ne dîyi dikole ne: “Tshitu muanji kupeta tshintu to, nunku tshiena mua kuangata tshikuabu to.”

"Udi musue kuamba ne kuena mua kuangata bikese to," ke muakamba Muenji wa tshifulu: "mbipepele bua kuangata bia bungi kupita tshianana."

"Kakuena muntu uvua mukuebeje lungenyi luebe to," ke muakamba Alice.

"Nnganyi udi wenza malu a pa nkayende mpindieu?" muena tshifulu wakakonka ne ditshimuna dionso.

Alice kavua mumanye tshia kuamba ku bualu ebu to: ke yeye kudiambuluisha bua kunua tshiayi ne mampa ne manteka, ne pashishe wakakudimuka kudi Dormouse, ne kuandamunaye lukonku luende. "Bua tshinyi bavua basombele kuinshi kua tshina tshia mâyi?"

Mbeba wa tshilala benyi wakangata kabidi minite umue anyi ibidi bua kuela meji pa bualu ebu, ne pashishe kuambaye ne: “Uvua tshina tshia melase.”

"Kakuena bualu bua nunku to!" Alice uvua mutuadije ne tshiji tshikole, kadi muena nsuki ne ngulube wa mu ngondo wa 3 bakamba ne: “Sh! sh!” ne Dormouse wakamba ne tshiji ne, "Wewe kuyi mua kuikala ne bupole, mbimpe ujikije muyuki wewe nkayebe."

"Tòo, tungunuka!" Alice unena ne budipuekeshi buonso; "Tshiena mua kukosa kabidi to. Ndi ngamba ne: kudi mua kuikala umue ."

"Umo, bulelela!" ke muvua Dormouse muambe ne tshiji. Nansha nanku, wakitaba bua kutungunuka. "Ne nunku bana betu ba bakaji basatu aba—bavua balonga mua kuzola, udi mumanye—"

"Ntshinyi tshivuabu bakose?" ke muvua Alice muambe, mupue mulayi wende muoyo.

"Treacle," ke muvua Dormouse muambe, kayi wela meji nansha kakese bua tshikondo etshi to.

"Ndi musue dikopo dikezuke," ke muakamba muena tshifulu: "tuendayi bonso muaba umue."

Wakaya kumpala pavuaye wakula, ne mbeba wa tshilala benyi wakamulonda: ngulube wa ngondo wakaya muaba wa mpuku wa mpuku, ne Alice wakangata muaba wa ngulube wa ngondo kayi musue. Muena nsuki ke uvua mupeta diakalenga ku dishintuluka edi: ne Alice uvua mu nsombelu mubi kupita kumpala, bualu March Hare uvua anu mubueje tshibombi tshia mabele mu dilongo diende.

Alice kavua musue kunyingalaja kabidi Dormouse to, ke yeye kutuadija ne budimu buonso ne: “Kadi tshiena mumvue to. Mmuaba kayi uvuabu bapatule tshintu atshi?”

"Udi mua kukoka mayi mu tshina tshia mayi," ke muakamba Muenji wa Nkanzu; "Nunku ndi ngela meji ne udi mua kupatula tshiamu tshia treacle mu tshina tshia treacle—eh, mutshimbakane?"

"Kadi bavua mu tshina tshia mâyi," ke muvua Alice muambile Dormouse, kayi musungule bua kumona mêyi a ndekelu aa to.

"Bushuwa bavua," ke muvua Dormouse muambe; "—bimpe munda."

Diandamuna edi diakatonda Alice mupele bikole, ke yeye kulekela Dormouse uya kumpala bua tshikondo kampanda kayi mumukose.

"Bavua balonga mua kuzola," Dormouse wakatungunuka, wela mbila ne wela mêsu ende, bualu uvua wenda ulala bikole; "ne bakazola bintu bia mishindu yonso—tshintu tshionso tshidi tshituadija ne M—"

"Bua tshinyi ne M?" Alice unena.

"Bua tshinyi?" ke muvua March Hare muambe.

Alice uvua mupuwe.

Mbeba uvua mukanga mêsu ende tshikondo atshi, ne uvua uya mu tulu; kadi, pa kumukuma kudi Muenji wa Nkanzu, wakajuka kabidi ne mbila mikese, ne wakatungunuka ne: “—udi utuadija ne M, bu mudi mateyi a mpuku, ne ngondo, ne lungenyi, ne bungi—udi mumanye ne: udi wamba ne: bintu bidi “bivule bia bungi”—ukadiku mumone tshintu tshia mushindu eu bu tshimfuanyi tshia bungi anyi?”

"Bushuwa, mpindieu udi unkonka," ke muvua Alice muambe, mubungame bikole, "tshiena ngela meji ne—"

"Pashishe, kuena ne tshia kuakula to," ke muakamba Muenji wa Nkanzu.

Bualu bua tshikisu ebu buvua bupite buvua Alice mua kutantamena: wakajuka ne tshiji tshikole, ne kuyaye; Mpuku wakalala diakamue, ne nansha umue wa kudibu kavua muteleje ne: uvua uya to, nansha muvuaye mutangile panyima musangu umue anyi ibidi, utekemena ne: bavua mua kumubikila: musangu wa ndekelu uvuaye mubamone, bavua bateta bua kuela Mpuku mu tshibombi tshia tshiayi.

"Nansha biobi bikale mushindu kayi, tshiakuyamu kabidi to!" ke muakamba Alice pavuaye wenda upitshila mu mitshi. "I tshibilu tshia tshiayi tshia bupote tshimvua muanji kubuela mu nsombelu wanyi mujima!"

Pavuaye wamba nanku, wakamona ne: umue wa ku mitshi uvua ne tshiibi tshivua tshibuelamu. "Bidi bikemesha be!" yeye wela meji. "Kadi bionso bidi bikemesha lelu. Ndi ngela meji ne ndi mua kubuela diakamue." Ne wakabuela.

Musangu eu kabidi wakadimona mu tshisasa tshile, ne pabuipi ne mesa makese a makala. "Mpindieu, ndi mua kuenza mudimu bimpe musangu eu," ke muvuaye mudiambile, ne kutuadijaye kuangata kafungu kakese ka or, ne kukangula tshiibi tshivua tshifikisha mu budimi. Pashishe wakaya ku mudimu wenda udia tshisuku (uvua mulame tshitupa tshiatshi mu tshibombi tshiende) too ne pakafikaye bule bua dikasa dimue: pashishe wakapueka mu tshisasa tshikese: ne pashishe —wakadimona ndekelu wa bionso mu budimi buimpe, munkatshi mua bilongo bikenka ne mishimi ya mâyi mimpe.
