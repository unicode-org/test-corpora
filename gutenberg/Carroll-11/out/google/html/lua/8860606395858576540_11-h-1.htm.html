SHAPITA I.
Kuinshi kua tshina tshia ngulube

Alice uvua mutuadije kutshioka bikole bua kusomba pabuipi ne muanabu wa bakaji ku banke, ne bua kuikala kayi ne tshia kuenza: musangu umue anyi ibidi uvua mutangile mukanda uvua muanabu wa bakaji ubala, kadi kauvua ne bimfuanyi anyi miyuki munda muawu to, “ne mukanda udi ne mushinga kayi,” ke muvua Alice wela meji ne: “padiku kakuyi bimfuanyi anyi miyuki?”

Nunku uvua wela meji mu lungenyi luende (mu mushindu uvuaye mua kuenza, bualu dituku dia kapia divua dimuenzeja bua kuikala ne tulu tua bungi ne bupote), bikala disanka dia kuenza tshilamba tshia marguerite mua kuikala ne mushinga wa kujuka ne kuangata matamba, pavua mu tshimpitshimpi Lapin Mutoke uvua ne mêsu a rose mulue pabuipi nende.

Kakuvua bualu bua dikema mu bualu abu to; anyi Alice kavua muele meji ne: bivua bimupangisha bua kumvua Kalulu wamba munda muende ne: “Oh munanga! Oh munanga! Nendue panyima pa dîba!” (pavuaye wela meji pashishe, wakalua mu lungenyi luende ne: uvua ne bua kuikala mudiebeje bualu ebu, kadi tshikondo atshi bionso bivua bimueneka bu bia pa tshibidilu); kadi pakapatula ngulube tshibombi mu tshibombi tshia tshilamba tshiende , ne kutshitangila, ne pashishe kuyaye lubilu, Alice wakatuadija kujuka, bualu biakamueneka mu lungenyi luende ne: kavua muanji kumona ngulube ne tshibombi tshia tshilamba tshia kujingila, anyi tshibombi tshia kupatulamu, ne uvua utema ne dijinga dia kumanya malu, wakanyema mu budimi, ne uvua anu ne dijinga dia kumanya mu tshikondo tshia kumona muditshi tshipueka mu tshina tshinene tshia ngulube muinshi mua tshisasa.

Mu tshikondo tshikuabu, Alice wakaya panyima pa bualu abu, kayi wela meji nansha kakese bua mushindu kayi uvuaye mua kupatuka kabidi to.

Tshina tshia ngulube tshiakaya buludiludi bu tshina tshia mâyi mu njila kampanda, ne pashishe tshiakakuluka mu tshimpitshimpi, mu tshimpitshimpi nunku Alice kavua ne dîba dia kuela meji bua kudikanda kumpala kua kudimonaye mukuluke mu tshina tshia mâyi tshikole menemene.

Tshina tshia mâyi tshivua tshikole menemene, anyi uvua mukuluke bitekete bitekete, bualu uvua ne dîba dia bungi pavuaye upueka bua kutangila muaba uvuaye ne bua kudiebeja tshivua mua kuenzeka pashishe. Tshia kumpala, wakateta kutangila panshi bua kumanya tshivuaye ulua, kadi kuvua mîdima mikole bua kumona tshintu nansha tshimue; pashishe wakatangila ku nseke ya tshina tshia mâyi, ne wakamona ne: ivua miule tente ne bitanda ne bitanda bia mikanda; apa ne aku uvua umona karte ne bimfuanyi bilengeja pa mitshi. Wakapuekesha tshibombi ku tshimue tshia ku bitekelu pavuaye upita; bavua bafundapu ne: “ORANGE MARMALADE”, kadi biakamutonda bikole bualu uvua mutupu: kavua musue kuela tshibombi panshi bua buôwa bua kushipa muntu muinshi to, ke yeye kutshiteka mu tshimue tshia ku bitanda pavuaye utshipita.

"Eyowa!" Alice wakela meji munda muende ne: “Panyima pa dikuluka dia mushindu eu, tshiakuela meji bua kukuluka mu tshibandilu to! Nebamone ne: ndi ne dikima dia bungi, nansha meme mukuluke pa mutu pa nzubu!” (Bivua mua kuikala bulelela.)

Panshi, panshi, panshi. Dikuluka kadiakufika ku ndekelu anyi? "Ndi ndiebeja ne: ndi mukuluke mutantshi wa kilometre bungi munyi?" wakamba ne dîyi dikole. "Ndi ne tshia kuikala pabuipi ne munkatshi mua buloba. Lekela mmone: bidi mua kuikala kilometre binunu binayi panshi, ndi ngela meji ne—" (bualu, udi mumone, Alice uvua mulonge malu a bungi a mushindu eu mu malongesha ende mu kalasa, ne nansha muvuawu kauyi mpunga muimpe wa kuleja dimanya diende, bualu kakuvua muntu uvua mua kumuteleja, bivua anu bimpa bua kuenza nanku, udi wamba ne: "Mêsu) ntanta muakane—kadi pashishe ndi ndiebeja ne: ndi ne Latitude anyi Longitude kayi?” (Alice kavua mumanye tshivua Latitude anyi Longitude to, kadi uvua wela meji ne: bivua miaku mimpe ya kuamba.)

Mutantshi eu wakatuadija kabidi. "Ndi ndiebeja ni ndi mua kukuluka panshi pa buloba! Nebimueneke bu bualu bua tuseku bua kupatuka munkatshi mua bantu badi benda ne mitu panshi! Antipathies, ndi ngela meji ne—" (wakasanka bikole bua muvuaku kakuyi muntu uvua uteleja, musangu eu, bualu kavua umvuika muaku muimpe nansha kakese) "—kadi ndi ne bua kubakonka dîna dia ditunga edi, udi mumanye ne: Mamu mupiamupia, Mamu mupiamupia. Zelande anyi Australie?” (ne wakateta bua kuela mpala panshi pavuaye wakula— kuela mpala panshi paudi upona mu lupepele! Udi wela meji ne: udi mua kuenza nanku anyi?) “Ne mmunyi mudi muana wa bakaji mupote udi mua kuela meji bua meme kumuebeja! Tòo, kabiena mua kuenzeka nansha kakese bua kuebeja: pamuapa nenkumone mufunda muaba kampanda.”

Panshi, panshi, panshi. Kakuvua bualu bukuabu bua kuenza to, nunku mutantshi mukese Alice wakatuadija kuakula kabidi. "Dinah neanjimije bikole butuku ebu, ndi ngela meji!" (Dinah uvua mpushi.) "Ndi mutekemene ne: nebavuluke tshibombi tshiende tshia mabele pa dîba dia kunua tshiayi. Dinah munanga wanyi! Ndi musue bua wikale nanyi panshi apa! Kakuena mpuku mu lupepele to, ndi ne buôwa, kadi udi mua kukuata mbua, ne bidi anu bu mpuku, udi mumanye. Kadi mbua itu idia mbua, ndi ndiebeja anyi?" Ne apa Alice wakatuadija kulala tulu, ne wakatungunuka ne kudiebeja, mu mushindu wa tshilota ne: “Mpushi itu idia mbua anyi? Mpushi itu idia mbua anyi?” ne imue misangu, "Mpushi itu idia mpushi anyi?" bualu, udi mumone, bu muvuaye kayi mua kuandamuna lukonko nansha lumue, kabivua ne mushinga bua mushindu kayi uvuaye muele to. Uvua umvua bu uvua ulala tulu, ne uvua anu mutuadije kulota ne: uvua wenda mu tshianza ne Dina, ne umuambila ne muoyo mujima ne: “Mpindieu, Dina, ngambila bulelela: uvuaku muanji kudia nkutshi anyi?” padi tshimpitshimpi, thump! thump! wakafika panshi pa tshibungubungu tshia mitshi ne mabeji maume, ne dikuluka diakajika.

Alice kavua mutapike nansha kakese, ne wakajuka mu tshitupa tshîpi: wakatangila mulu, kadi ku mutu kuvua mîdima; kumpala kuende kuvua njila mukuabu mule, ne Lupepele Mutoke uvua anu umueneka, wenda upueka lubilu. Kakuvua tshikondo nansha tshimue tshia kujimija to: Alice wakaya bu lupepele, ne uvua anu mu tshikondo tshia kumvua tshiamba, pavuatshi tshienda tshinyunguluka mu ditumba ne: “Oh matshi anyi ne nsuki yanyi, mmunyi mudibi bienda bipita!” Uvua pabuipi panyima patshi pavuaye mushintuluke mu ditumba, kadi Lapin kavua kabidi umueneka to: wakadimona mu nzubu mule, mupuekele, uvua mutokeshibue kudi mulongo wa miendu ivua milengeja kuulu kua nzubu.

Kuvua biibi binyunguluke nzubu mujima, kadi bionso bivua bikanga; ne pavua Alice mumane kupueka ku luseke lumue ne kuulu ku lukuabu, uteta tshiibi tshionso, wakaya ne dibungama munkatshi, wela meji mushindu kayi uvuaye mua kupatuka kabidi.

Diakamue wakafika ku mesa makese a maulu asatu, onso menza ne makala makole; kakuvua tshintu nansha tshimue pa kumbusha tshifungu tshikese tshia or, ne lungenyi lua kumpala lua Alice luvua ne: tshivua mua kuikala tshia tshimue tshia ku biibi bia nzubu wa tshilala benyi; kadi, diakabi! anyi makunji avua manene bikole, anyi tshifungu tshivua tshikese bikole, kadi nansha nanku katshivua mua kunzulula nansha tshimue tshia kudibi to. Kadi, musangu muibidi, wakapeta rido mukese uvuaye kayi muanji kumona, ne panyima patshi pavua tshiibi tshikese tshia bule bua santimetre dikumi ne itanu: wakateta tshifungu tshikese tshia or mu tshiibi, ne ku disanka diende dinene tshiakakumbana!

Alice wakakangula tshiibi ne kumonaye ne: tshivua tshifikisha mu tshisasa tshikese, katshiyi tshinene kupita tshina tshia mpuku to: wakatua binu panshi ne kutangila mu tshisasa atshi mu budimi buimpe menemene buuvua muanji kumona. Mushindu kayi uvuaye musue kupatuka mu nzubu wa mîdima au, ne kuendakana munkatshi mua malalu a bilongo ne mishimi ya mashika, kadi kavua nansha mua kupitshila ku tshiibi tshia mutu wende to; "ne nansha mutu wanyi mua kupitshila," ke muvua Alice mupele wela meji, "kavua mua kuikala ne mushinga mukese meme tshiyi ne makaya anyi. Oh, ndi musue kuikala mupuwe bu tshiamu tshia teleskope! Ndi ngela meji ne: mvua mua kuenza nanku, bu meme mumanye mua kutuadija." Bualu, udi mumone, malu a bungi avua kaayi malelela avua menzeke matuku mashale aa, avua Alice mutuadije kuela meji ne: malu makese menemene avua kaayi mua kuenzeka to.

Bivua bimueneka ne: kakuvua mushinga wa kuindila ku tshiibi tshikese to, ke yeye kupingana ku mêsa, ne ditekemena dia se: uvua mua kupeta tshifungu tshikuabu patshi, anyi mukanda wa mikenji ya kukanga bantu bu mudi teleskope: musangu eu wakapeta mulondo mukese pa mutu pawu, (“uvua kayiku kumpala to,” ke muvua Alice muambe,) ne ku nshingu kua mulondo au muvua dibeji dia ne: “ME KDR,” bifundapu bimpe menemene mu maleta manene.

Bionso bivua bimpa bua kuamba ne: "Nunwe," kadi muana wa meji Alice kavua mua kuenza nanku ne lukasa to. "Tòo, ndi mua kuanji kutangila," ke muvuaye muambe, "ne kumona ni mbafunde ne: ' poison ' anyi to"; bualu uvua mubale miyuki mikese milenga ya bungi idi yakula bua bana bavua boshe, ne badie kudi nyama ya luonji ne bintu bikuabu bibi, bionso ebi bualu kabavua mua kuvuluka mikenji mipepele ivua balunda babu babalongeshe to: bu mudi, ne: poker wa kapia mukunze neakuoshe wewe mumukuate mutantshi mule; ne se, wewe mukose munu webe bikole menemene ne tshingoma, pa tshibidilu udi upatuka mashi; ne kavua muanji kupua muoyo ne: wewe munue malu a bungi mu mulondo udibu bafunde ne: “poison,” udi pabuipi ne kubenga kuitaba tshiudi wamba, mu katupa kîpi emu anyi pashishe.

Kadi, mulondo eu kauvua ne tshimanyinu tshia ne: “mushipa to,” ke bualu kayi Alice wakadienzeja bua kuuteta, ne kumonaye ne: uvua muimpe be, (mu bulelela, uvua ne butobaji busambakaja bua cherry-tart, custard, pine-apple, dindon muosha, toffee, ne mampa a kapia,) wakajikija diakamue.

"Mmunyi mudibi bikengela kumanya!" Alice unena; "Ndi ne tshia kuikala mupuwe bu tshiamu tshia teleskope."

Ke muvuabi bulelela: uvua mpindieu anu ne bule bua santimetre dikumi, ne mpala wende wakakenka pavuaye wela meji ne: uvua mpindieu ne bunene bukumbane bua kubuela ku tshiibi tshikese bua kubuela mu budimi buimpe abu. Kadi, tshia kumpala, wakaindila minite mikese bua kumona ni uvua mua kupueka kabidi: uvua umvua buôwa kakese bua bualu ebu; "Bualu bidi mua kujika, udi mumanye," ke muvua Alice muambe munda muende, "mu dipatuka dianyi dionso, bu muendu. Ndi ndiebeja ne: mvua ne bua kuikala mushindu kayi dîba adi?" Ne wakateta bua kuela meji mudi lubimbi lua muendu luikale panyima pa muendu mumane kujima, bualu kavua mua kuvuluka muvuaye mumone bualu bua nunku to.

Kunyima kwa kitatyi kityetye, pa kumona amba kekudi kintu kikwabo kilonge, wakangata dipangadika dia kuya mu budimi diakamue; kadi, diakabi bua Alice mupele! Pakafikaye ku tshiibi, wakamona ne: uvua mupue tshifungu tshikese tshia or muoyo, ne pakapinganaye ku mesa bua kutshipeta, wakasangana ne: kavua mua kutshipeta to: uvua mua kutshimona bimpe menemene ku diambuluisha dia dikalashi, ne wakateta muende muonso bua kubanda pa diulu dimue dia mesa, kadi tshivua tshisesuka bikole; ne pavuaye mutshioke ne diteta, tshintu tshikese tshia mupele tshiakasomba panshi ne kudila.

“Luaku, kakuena mushinga wa kudila nunku to!” ke muvua Alice muambe munda muende, ne dîyi dikole; "Ndi nkulomba bua kulekela tshitupa etshi!" Uvua udipesha mibelu mimpe menemene, (nansha muvuaye kayi uyilonda misangu mikese), ne imue misangu uvua udipisha bikole menemene too ne ku dipuekesha binsonji ku mêsu; ne dimue dituku wakavuluka muvuaye udienzeja bua kudishima mu manaya a croquet avuaye udinaya, bualu muana eu uvua musue kumanya malu uvua munange bikole kudifuanyikija bu bantu babidi. Mupele Alice wakela meji ne: “Kadi kabiena ne mushinga mpindieu bua kudileja bu bantu babidi to, ndi mushadile anu bu muntu umue wa kunemeka!”

Mutantshi mukese, dîsu diende diakamona kazubu kakese ka dikalashi kavua muinshi mua mesa: wakakabulula, ne wakasanganamu mukate mukese menemene, muvua miaku mifundapu bimpe ne: “NDIE” mu bimuma bia cassis. Alice wakamba ne: “Eyowa, ndi mua kudia, ne bikalabi binvuije munene, ndi mua kufika ku tshifungu; ne bikalabi binvuije mukese, ndi mua kunyemena muinshi mua tshiibi; nunku nansha mushindu kayi ndi mua kubuela mu budimi, ne tshiena ne bualu bua tshidi tshienzeka to!”

Wakadia kakese, ne kudiambila ne tunyinganyinga ne, "Njila kayi? Njila kayi?", mukuate tshianza tshiende pa mutu bua kumvua mushindu kayi uvuaye wenda ukola, ne wakakema bikole pakamonaye ne: uvua anu bunene bumue: bua kuamba bulelela, bualu ebu butu buenzeka pa tshibidilu padi muntu udia mampa, kadi Alice kavua mutekemene bualu nansha bumue to, kadi bivua bimueneka ne: bivua bienzeke mbibi ne bupote bua muoyo kutungunuka mu mushindu wa pa tshibidilu.

Nunku wakatuadija mudimu, ne mutantshi mukese wakajikija mukate.
