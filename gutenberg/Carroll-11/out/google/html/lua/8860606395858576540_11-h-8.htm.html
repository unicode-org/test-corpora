SHAPITA VIII.
Muaba wa croquet wa mukalenge mukaji

Mutshi munene wa rose uvua muimane pabuipi ne tshibuelelu tshia budimi: rose uvua ukolapu uvua mutoke, kadi kuvua bena madimi basatu, bavua benza mudimu wa bungi wa kuwupenta mukunze. Alice wakela meji ne: bualu ebu buvua bualu bua dikema, ne wakasemena pabuipi bua kubatangila, ne anu pavuaye ulua kudibu wakumvua umue wa kudibu wamba ne: “Tangilayi mpindieu, Five!

"Tshivua mua kudikanda to," ke muakamba Five, ne dîyi dia tshiji; "Muanda mutekete wakanyema tshianza tshianyi."

Pavuaye muanda mutekete mutangile mulu, wamba ne: “Mmuomu, muanda mutekete!

" Mbimpe kuyi wakula to!" ke muvua Five muambe. "Mvua mumvue mukalenge mukaji wamba ne: anu malaba ke uvua mukumbanyine kukukosa mutu!"

"Bua tshinyi?" ke muakamba eu uvua muakule kumpala.

"Ki mbualu buebe to, Babidi!" muanda mutekete unena.

"Eyowa, mbualu buende!" Five unena, "ne nsa kumuambila—bivua bua kulua ne miji ya tulip pamutu pa matungulu."

Muanda mutekete wakaela tshiamu tshienda panshi, ne uvua anu mutuadije ne: “Eyowa, ku malu onso adi kaayi makane—” pavua dîsu diende dikuluke pa Alice, pavuaye muimane ubatangila, ne wakadikonkonona diakamue: bakuabu bakatangila kabidi, ne bonso bakainama panshi.

"Udi mua kungambila," ke muakamba Alice, ne buôwa bukese, "bua tshinyi udi upenda bilongo bia rose abi?"

Batanu ne Muanda mutekete kabavua bamba bualu to, kadi bakatangila babidi. Babidi bakatuadija ne dîyi ditekete, "Bua tshinyi bulelela budi ne, udi mumone, Mamu, muaba eu uvua ne bua kuikala mutshi wa rose mutoke , ne tuakateka mutoke mu tshilema; ne bikala Mukalenge mukaji mua kumanya, tuetu bonso tuvua ne bua kuikala bakosa mitu yetu, udi mumanye. Nunku udi umona, Mukalenge mukaji, tudi tuenza muetu muonso, kumpala kua yeye kulua"— Mu tshikondo etshi, muntu uvua ne kanyinganyinga uvua utangila muntu mutanu dia muamua dia budimi, udi ubikila ne: “Mukalenge mukaji! Mukalenge mukaji!” ne bena madimi basatu aba bakadiela panshi diakamue. Kuvua mutoyi wa makasa a bungi, ne Alice wakatangila, musue kumona mukalenge mukaji.

Tshia kumpala bakalua basalayi dikumi bambule makombo; bionso ebi bivua bienza bu bena madimi basatu, bivua bilengeja ne bibalabala, ne bianza ne makasa ku matumba: pashishe bena mudimu dikumi; aba bavua balengeja mubidi wonso ne mabue a diamant, ne bavua benda babidi babidi, anu muvua basalayi benza. Panyima pa aba, bana ba bukalenge bakalua; bavua dikumi, ne bana bakese bavua balua batuluka ne disanka ku bianza, mu bibidi bibidi: bonso bavua balengeja ne mitshima. Pashishe benyi bakalua, ba bungi bavua bakalenge ne bakaji, ne munkatshi muabu Alice wakamanya Lapin Mutoke: uvua wakula ne lukasa luonso ne buôwa, useka ku malu onso avuabu bamba, ne uvua upita kayi mumumone to. Pashishe, Knave of Hearts wakalonda, muambule tshifulu tshia mukalenge pa tshilamba tshia velours tshikunze; ne, ku ndekelu kua tshisumbu tshinene etshi, kuakalua MUKALENGE NE MUKAJI MUKAJI WA MITSHIMA.

Alice uvua wela mpata bua kumanya ni kavua ne bua kulala panshi bu bena madimi basatu to, kadi kavua mua kuvuluka muvuaye muanji kumvua mukenji wa nunku mu bikondo bia ngendu to; "Ne pa kumbusha, tshisumbu tshia bantu tshidi mua kuikala ne mushinga kayi," ke muvuaye wela meji, "bu bantu bonso ne bua kulala panshi pa mpala yabu, bua kabamone?" Nunku wakimana muaba uvuaye, ne kuindila.

Pavua tshisumbu tshia bantu tshilua kumpala kua Alice, bonso bakimana ne kumutangila, ne mukalenge mukaji wakamba ne tshikisu tshionso ne: “Eu nnganyi?” Wakambila Knave wa mitshima, uvua anu mufukame ne musekelele bua kuandamuna.

"Tshilema!" ke muakamba mukalenge mukaji, wenda unyunguluka mutu wende kayi ne lutulu; ne, wakakudimuka kudi Alice, wakatungunuka ne kuamba ne: “Muana, dîna diebe nnganyi?”

"Dîna dianyi ndi Alice, nunku tuasakidila Mukalenge webe," ke muakamba Alice ne kanemu konso; kadi wakasakidila, munda muende ne, "Bua tshinyi, badi anu tshisumbu tshia makalu, kunyima kua bionso. Tshiena ne bua kubatshina to!"

"Ne aba mbanganyi? " unena Mukalenge mukaji, uleja bena madimi basatu badi balala pabuipi ne mutshi wa rose; bualu, udi mumone, bu muvuabu balale pa mpala yabu, ne tshimfuanyi tshia pa mikolo yabu tshivua tshimue ne tshia tshisumbu tshikuabu, kavua mua kumanya ni bavua bena madimi, anyi basalayi, anyi bena mudimu ba mu nzubu wa mukalenge, anyi bana bende basatu to.

"Mmunyi mundi mua kumanya?" ke muvua Alice muambe, mukeme bua dikima diende nkayende. "Ki mbualu buanyi to."

Mukalenge mukaji wakalua mukunze bua tshiji, ne, yeye mumane kumutangila bua tshitupa tshîpi bu nyama wa luonji, wakela mbila ne: “Umbusha mutu wende! Umbusha—”

"Bupote!" ke muakamba Alice, ne dîyi dikole ne dipangadika, ne mukalenge mukaji wakashala mupuwe.

Mukalenge wakateka tshianza tshiende pa diboko diende, ne kuambaye ne buôwa buonso ne: “Ela meji, munanga wanyi: udi anu muana!”

Mukalenge mukaji wakamulekela ne tshiji, ne kuambilaye Knave ne: “Ubapingaje!”

Knave wakenza nanku, ne budimu buonso, ne dikasa dimue.

"Juka!" ke muakamba Mukalenge mukaji, ne dîyi dikole, ne bena madimi basatu bakatuta diakamue, ne kutuadijabu kuinamina Mukalenge, Mukalenge mukaji, bana ba mukalenge, ne bantu bonso.

"Lekela bualu abu!" wakadila mukalenge mukaji. "Udi untonda." Pashishe, wakakudimuka kudi mutshi wa rose, wakatungunuka ne kuamba ne: “ Uvua wenza tshinyi muaba eu?”

"Bisankishe Mukalenge webe," ke muakamba Babidi, mu dîyi dia budipuekeshi bua bungi, wela panshi pa dikasa dimue pavuaye wakula, "tuvua tutetaku—"

" Ndi mmona!" ke muvua mukalenge mukaji muambe, uvua mu tshikondo atshi utangila bilongo bia rose. "Umbusha mitu yabu!" ne tshisumbu tshia bantu tshiakaya kumpala, basalayi basatu bashala bua kushipa bena madimi bavua kabayi ne diakalenga, bavua banyemena kudi Alice bua kubakuba.

"Kabena mua kukukosa mutu to!" ke muvua Alice muambe, ne wakabiteka mu tshibombi tshinene tshia maluba tshivua tshiimane pabuipi. Basalayi basatu abu bakatambakana munkatshi mua minite umue anyi ibidi, babakeba, ne pashishe kuyabu balonda bakuabu.

"Mitu yabu mmipatuke anyi?" ke muvua Mukalenge mukaji wela mbila.

"Mitu yabu mmimane, pikalabi bisankisha mukalenge weba!" basalayi bakela mbila bua kuandamuna.

“Ke mudibi!” ke muvua Mukalenge mukaji wela mbila. "Udi mua kunaya croquet anyi?"

Basalayi bakashala batabale, ne kutangila Alice, bualu lukonko elu luvua lumueneka bua yeye.

"Eyowa!" Alice wakadila.

"Luaku, nunku!" ke muvua mukalenge mukaji wela mbila, ne Alice wakabuela mu tshisumbu tshia bantu, wela meji bikole bua tshivua mua kuenzeka pashishe.

"I—i difuku dimpe be!" ke muakamba dîyi dia buôwa ku luseke luende. Uvua wenda pabuipi ne Lapin Mutoke, uvua umutangila ne tunyinganyinga ku mpala.

"Byampe," unena Alice: "--Duchesse udi penyi?"

"Talala! Talala!" ke muakamba Lapin ne dîyi ditekete ne dia lukasa. Wakatangila ne kanyinganyinga pa dikaya diende pavuaye wakula, ne pashishe wakadibandisha pa nsongo ya makasa, kuteka mukana muende pabuipi ne ditshi diende, ne kumuambila mu mutoyi ne: “Udi mu tshibawu tshia lufu.”

"Bua tshinyi?" Alice unena.

"Uvuaku muambe ne: 'Bualu bua luse!' anyi?" Kalulu wakakonka.

Alice wakamba ne: “Tòo, tshivua mumanye to: “Tshiena ngela meji ne: mbualu bua luse nansha kakese, ngakamba ne: ‘Bua tshinyi?’”

"Wakakuma matshi a mukalenge mukaji—" ngulube wakatuadija. Alice wakela mbila mikese ya tuseku. "Oh, talalaa!" Muana wa ngulube wakamba ne dîyi dia buôwa. “Mukalenge mukaji neakuteleje!

"Ndayi ku miaba yenu!" ke muvua mukalenge mukaji wela mbila ne dîyi dia makubakuba, ne bantu bakatuadija kunyema mu nseke yonso, batutangana; nansha nanku, bakasomba mu minite umue anyi ibidi, ne manaya akatuadija. Alice uvua wela meji ne: kavua muanji kumona tshipalu tshia manaya tshia mushindu eu mu nsombelu wende to; bionso bivua mikuna ne mikuna; mipinganyi ivua mbua ya muoyo, miele ivua ya flamingo ya muoyo, ne basalayi bavua ne bua kudibandisha misangu ibidi ne kuimana ku bianza ne ku makasa, bua kuenza bimanu.

Lutatu lunene luvua Alice mupete ku ntuadijilu luvua lua kulama flamingo wende: wakakokesha bua kusokoka mubidi wende, bimpe menemene, muinshi mua diboko diende, ne maulu ende malenge panshi, kadi pa tshibidilu, anu muvuaye mulongolole nshingu wende bimpe, ne uvua uya kukuma mbua ne mutu wende, uvua udikudimuna ne uditangila mulu ne tshimuenekelu tshia dikema ne kavua mua kupanga kuseka to: ne pavuaye mupuekeshe mutu wende panshi, ne uvua uya kutuadija kabidi, bivua bimutonda bikole bua kumona ne: mbua uvua mudiunzulule, ne uvua wenda unyunguluka: pa kumbusha bionso ebi, kuvua pa tshibidilu tshibandabanda anyi tshibandabanda mu njila muaba wonso uvuaye musue kutuma mbua, ne, bu muvua basalayi babidi bavua benda bajuka ne kumbuka misangu yonso mu bitupa bikuabu bia buloba, Alice wakafika ku dimona ne: uvua bushuwa manaya makole menemene.

Bena manaya bonso bavua banaya musangu umue kabayi bindile tshikondo, bavua bakokangana dîba dionso adi, ne baluangana bua mbua; ne mu kitatyi kityetye, Mukalenge mukaji uvua ne tshiji tshikole, ne wakaya wenda unyunguluka, ne wela mbila ne: “Umbusha mutu wende!” anyi "Umbusha mutu wende!" bu musangu umue mu minite.

Alice wakatuadija kudiumvua bibi menemene: bua kuamba bulelela, kavua muanji kuikala ne tshilumbu nansha tshimue ne mukalenge mukaji to, kadi uvua mumanye ne: bivua mua kuenzeka mu minite yonso, “ne pashishe,” ke muvuaye wela meji ne: “ntshinyi tshivua mua kumfikila? Badi basue bikole kukosa mitu ya bantu muaba eu; bualu bua dikema budi ne: kudi muntu nansha umue udi mushale ne muoyo!”

Uvua ukeba mushindu kampanda wa kunyema, ne udiebeja ni uvua mua kunyema kayi umueneka, pakamonaye tshintu kampanda tshivua tshimueneka mu lupepele: tshivua tshimutonda bikole ku ntuadijilu, kadi, yeye mumane kutshitangila minite umue anyi ibidi, wakalua kuseka, ne wakadiambila ne: “Mpushi wa mu Cheshire: mpindieu ndi ne muntu wa kuyukila nende.”

"Udi wenda bishi?" ke muakamba Pushi, diakamue pavuaku mukana mukumbane bua kuakula nende.

Alice waindila too ne pavua mêsu amueneka, ne pashishe wakatua mpala panshi. "Kabiena ne mushinga bua kuyukila nende to," ke muvuaye wela meji, "too ne padi matshi ende alua, anyi nansha umue wa kudiwu." Mu minite mikuabu, mutu mujima wakamueneka, ne pashishe Alice wakateka flamingo wende panshi, ne kutuadijaye muyuki wa manaya, udiumvua ne disanka dia bungi bua muvuaye ne muntu uvua mua kumuteleja. Mpushi uvua umueneka wela meji ne: bivua bikumbane mpindieu, ne kabivua bimueneka kabidi to.

"Tshiena ngela meji ne badi banaya bimpe nansha kakese," Alice wakatuadija, ne dîyi dia kuela mbila, "ne bonso badi bakokangana bikole menemene muntu kena mua kumvua muntu nkayende wakula to—ne badi bamueneka kabayi ne mikenji kampanda ya pa buayi; nansha biobi bikaleku, muntu nansha umue kena ubalonda to—ne kuena mumanye mudi bintu bionso bidi ne muoyo mu tshibumba etshi bibungamija to; upitshile udi ulonda wenda unyunguluka ku muelelu mukuabu wa buloba—ne mvua ne bua kuikala mukuate mbuji wa mukalenge mukaji mpindieu, anu wakanyema pakamonaye wanyi ulua!”

"Mmunyi muudi munange mukalenge mukaji?" ke muakamba Pushi ne dîyi ditekete.

"Kakuyi nansha kakese," ke muakamba Alice: "udi mupite bungi—" Anu dîba adi wakamona ne: Mukalenge mukaji uvua pabuipi nende, umuteleja: nunku wakatungunuka ne kuamba ne: "—pamuapa bua kutshimuna, ne kabiena ne mushinga bua kujikija manaya to."

Mukalenge mukaji wakaseka ne kupitshila.

" Udi wakula ne nganyi?" unena mukalenge, uya kudi Alice, ne utangila mutu wa mpushi ne dijinga dikole dia kumanya.

"I mulunda wanyi—mpushi wa mu Cheshire," ke muakamba Alice: "anyishayi nnumanyishe."

"Tshiena musue tshimuenekelu tshiatshi nansha kakese," ke muakamba Mukalenge: "nansha nanku, tshidi mua kutua tshianza tshianyi mishiku yeye musue."

"Ndi musue kubenga," ke muakamba mpushi.

Mukalenge wakamba ne: “Kuikadi ne tshikisu, ne kutangidi nunku to!” Wakafika kunyima kua Alice pavuaye wakula.

"Mpushi udi mua kutangila mukalenge," ke muakamba Alice. "Ndi mubale bualu ebu mu mukanda kampanda, kadi tshiena mvuluka muaba uvuabu to."

"Eyowa, bidi bikengela kutshiumusha," ke muakamba Mukalenge ne dipangadika dikole, ne wakabikila Mukalenge mukaji uvua upita dîba adi ne: "Munanga wanyi! Ndi musue bua umbushe mpushi eu!"

Mukalenge mukaji uvua ne mushindu umuepele wa kujikija ntatu yonso, minene anyi mikese. "Umbusha mutu wandi!" unena, kayi nansha utangila kunyunguluka.

Mukalenge wakamba ne disanka dionso ne: “Nengambule mushipianganyi meme nkayanyi,” ne wakaya lubilu.

Alice wakela meji ne: uvua mua kupingana, ne kumona muvua manaya enda, bualu uvua mumvue dîyi dia mukalenge mukaji ku bule, wela mbila ne disanka. Uvua mumane kumvua tshibawu tshiende tshia kushipa basatu ba ku banayi bualu bavua bapangile tshikondo tshiabu, ne kavua musue mushindu uvua malu amueneka nansha kakese, bualu manaya avua mu tshimvundu tshia bungi kavua mumanye ni tshivua tshikondo tshiende anyi to. Nunku wakaya kukeba mbuji wende.

Nzolo uvua uluangana ne nzolo mukuabu, bivua bimueneka kudi Alice bu mpunga muimpe wa kuela umue wa kudibu ne mukuabu: lutatu luvua anu ne: flamingo wende uvua muye ku luseke lukuabu lua budimi, muaba uvua Alice mua kumumona wenda uteta mu mushindu uvua kayi ne tshia kuenza bua kubuela mu mutshi.

Dîba divuaye mukuate flamingo ne mumupingaje, mvita ivua mimane, ne mbua yonso ibidi kayivua imueneka to: “kadi kabiena ne bualu to,” ke muvua Alice wela meji, “bu mudi bimanu bionso bimane kujika ku luseke elu lua buloba.” Nunku wakatshisokoka muinshi mua diboko diende, bua katshiyi tshipatuka kabidi, ne wakapingana bua kuyukila kabidi kakese ne mulunda wende.

Pakapinganaye ku Cheshire Cat, wakakema pakasanganaye bantu ba bungi bavua bamunyunguluke: kuvua dikokangana pankatshi pa mushipianganyi, Mukalenge, ne Mukalenge mukaji, bavua bakula musangu umue, eku bakuabu bonso bavua bapuwe, ne bavua bamueneka kabayi bimpe to.

Dîba divua Alice mumueneke, bakamulomba kudi bonso basatu bua kujikija lukonko, ne bakamuambila kabidi malu abu, nansha nanku, bu muvuabu bonso bakula musangu umue, wakamona ne: bivua bimukolela bua kumvua tshivuabu bamba menemene.

Lungenyi lua mushipianganyi luvua ne, kuena mua kukosa mutu to, anu padiku mubidi wa kukosa: ne: kavua muanji kuenza bualu bua nunku to, ne kavua mua kutuadija mu tshikondo tshiende tshia muoyo to.

Lungenyi lua Mukalenge luvua ne: tshintu tshionso tshivua ne mutu tshivua mua kukosa mutu, ne kuena ne bua kuakula malu a tshianana to.

Lungenyi lua mukalenge mukaji luvua ne, pikala bualu kampanda kabuyi buenzeke mu katupa kîpi, uvua mua kushipa bantu bonso, bonso. (Mêyi a ndekelu aa ke avua menze bua tshisumbu tshijima tshimueneke tshikole ne tshibungamija.)

Alice kavua mua kuela meji a bualu bukuabu bua kuamba to, anu ne: “Mbia Duchesse: mbimpe umuebeje bualu ebu.”

"Udi mu buloko," mukalenge mukaji wakambila mushipianganyi ne: "ulue nende apa." Ne mushipianganyi wakaya bu mukete.

Mutu wa Pushi wakatuadija kujimina dîba divuaye muye, ne, pavuaye mupingane ne Duchesse, uvua mujimine mujima; nunku Mukalenge ne mushipianganyi bakanyema lubilu lukole babanda ne panshi bakeba, eku bantu bakuabu bapingana ku manaya.
