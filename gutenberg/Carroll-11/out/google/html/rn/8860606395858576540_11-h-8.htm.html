IGICE CA VIII.
Ikibuga c'Umwamikazi

Igiti kinini c’amaroza cari gihagaze hafi y’ubwinjiriro bw’itongo: amaroza yariko aramera kuri co yari yera, ariko hari abarimyi batatu bari kuri co, bariko baragisiga ibara ry’umutuku bariko baragikora cane. Alice yiyumviriye ko ivyo ari ikintu giteye amakenga cane, aca arayegera kugira ngo abarabe, maze akimara kubashikira yumva umwe muri bo avuga ati: “Iyubare ubu, Batanu!

Five yavuze mw’ijwi ry’agahinda ati: “Nta co nari gushobora gukora; “Indwi zaranteye urutugu.”

Kuri ivyo Indwi araba hejuru ati: "Nivyo, Utanu! Wame ushira icaha ku bandi!"

“ Vyoba vyiza utavuze!” » ni ko Fitanu avuga. “Numvise Umwamikazi avuga ejo gusa ko ukwiriye gucirwa umutwe!”

"Kubera iki?" » ni ko uwabanje kuvuga yavuze.

“Ivyo ntaco bigutwara , Babiri!” » ni ko Seven avuga.

“Ego cane, ni ivyiwe!” aravuga Five, “kandi nzomubwira—vyari kubera umutetsi yazanye imizi y’ibitoke vy’i tulipe aho kuzana ibitunguru.”

Seven yaciye atera hasi uburoso bwiwe, kandi yari ahejeje gutangura “Ego, mu bintu vyose bitari vyo—” igihe ijisho ryiwe ryagwa kuri Alice, uko yari ahagaze abaraba, aca arisuzuma bukwi na bukwi: abandi na bo nyene bararaba hirya no hino, bose barapfukama hasi.

Alice aca ambwira afise isoni gatoyi ati: “Woba wombwira igituma uriko urasiga ayo maroza?”

Five na Seven ntaco bavuze, ahubwo baraba Babiri. Babiri batanguye mw'ijwi ritoyi, "Kubera iki ukuri ari uko, urabona, Miss, iki hano cari gikwiye kuba igiti c'urugori gitukura , maze dushiramwo ikindi cera ku makosa; kandi iyo Umwamikazi azobimenya, twese twari dukwiye guca imitwe yacu, urazi. Rero urabona, Miss, turiko turakora uko dushoboye kwose kugira n’amaganya araba hakurya y’itongo, ahamagara ati “Umwamikazi! Umwamikazi!” maze abo barimyi batatu baca bijugunya hasi mu maso habo. Hariho ijwi ry’ibirenge vyinshi, Alice aca araba hirya no hino, afise umushasharo wo kubona Umwamikazi.

Ubwa mbere haje abasoda cumi bitwaje inkoni; ivyo vyose vyari bimeze nk’ivyo barimyi batatu, bifise uburebure burebure kandi bisongoye, ibiganza n’ibirenge vyavyo biri ku mfuruka: hakurikiyeho abakuru cumi bo mu rugo; ivyo vyari bisharije hose n’amadayimoni, kandi vyagenda bibiri bibiri, nk’uko abasoda babigira. Inyuma y’abo haza abana b’umwami; bari icumi, n’abakundwa batoyi baraza basimbuka banezerewe bafatanye amaboko, babiri babiri: bose bari basharije imitima. Hakurikiyeho abashitsi, benshi muri bo bakaba ari Abami n’Abamikazi, muri bo Alice yaramenye Urukwavu Rwera: rwariko ruravuga mu buryo bwihuse bw’ubwoba, rumwenyura ivyo bavuga vyose, rukagenda rutamubonye. Hanyuma hakurikiraho Umunyaruyeri w’Imitima, yitwaje ikamba ry’Umwami ku mpuzu y’impuzu y’umutuku; kandi, nyuma y’iyi nzira nini yose, haje UMWAMI N’UMWAMIKAZI W’IMITIMA.

Alice yari ahubwo akekeranya nimba atabwirizwa kuryama mu maso nk’abo barimyi batatu, ariko ntiyibuka ko yigeze yumva itegeko nk’iryo mu migenderanire; yariyumviriye ati: “kandi n’ikindi, urugendo rwoba rufise akamaro akahe, iyo abantu bose baryama hasi, kugira ngo ntibabone?” Nuko ahagarara aho yari, arindira.

Igihe urugendo rwashika rurabana na Alice, bose barahagarara bamuraba, Umwamikazi aca avuga n’agahinda ati “Uyu ni nde?” Yabibwiye Umunyaruyeri w’Imitima, na we aca arapfukama gusa, aramwenyura mu kwishura.

"Umusazi!" » ni ko Umwamikazi avuga, azunguza umutwe ata kwihangana; maze, ahindukiriye Alice, arabandanya ati: “Witwa nde mwana?”

Alice yaravuze n’indero nyinshi ati: “Izina ryanje ni Alice, rero ndashimishe Nyakubahwa; ariko yongerako, mu mutima wiwe ati: "Ese, ni ipaki y'amakarata gusa, n'ubundi. Sinkeneye kuyatinya!"

Umwamikazi aravuga ati: “Kandi abo ni bande? ”, yerekana abo barimyi batatu bari baryamye bakikuje igiti c’urugori; kuko, urabona, uko bari baryamye mu maso, kandi igishushanyo co ku mugongo cari kimwe n’ibindi bice vy’umugwi, ntiyashobora kumenya nimba ari abarimyi, canke abasoda, canke abakozi bo mu ngoro, canke abana biwe bwite batatu.

“ Nomenya gute?” » ni ko Alice avuga, atangajwe n’ubutwari bwiwe bwite. “Nta co bimaze .”

Umwamikazi aca ahinduka umutuku kubera uburake, maze amaze kumuraba umwanya mutoyi nk’igikoko, asemerera ati “Kura n’umutwe wiwe! Kura—”

“Ibintu vy’ubujuju!” » Alice aravuga, n’ijwi rirenga cane kandi yiyemeje, Umwamikazi aca araceceka.

Umwami arambika ukuboko ku kuboko kwiwe, aca avuga n’isoni ati “Iyumvire mukunzi wanje: ni umwana gusa!”

Umwamikazi aca aramuhindukirana n’ishavu, abwira Umunyaruyeri ati “Bahindure!”

Uwo Knave yarabikoze, yitonze cane, akoresheje ikirenge kimwe.

"Haguruka!" Umwamikazi aravuga, mw’ijwi ry’agahomerabunwa, ry’agahomerabunwa, abo barimyi batatu baca basimbuka, batangura gupfukamira Umwami, Umwamikazi, abana b’umwami n’abandi bose.

“Ivyo mubireke!” » Umwamikazi arasemerera. “Urantera umutwe.” Maze, ahindukirira igiti c’urugori, arabandanya ati: “ Wamaze igihe ukora iki hano?”

Two, mw’ijwi ryicishije bugufi cane, apfukama ku ivi rimwe uko yariko aravuga, ati: “Bishimishe Nyakubahwa, twariko turagerageza—”

" Ndabona !" » ni ko Umwamikazi yavuze, muri ico gihe yariko arasuzuma amaroza. “Bave n’imitwe yabo!” maze urugendo rurabandanya, batatu mu basirikare basigaye inyuma kugira ngo bice abo barimyi b’amagorwa, na bo birukira kuri Alice kugira ngo abakingire.

«Ntimuzoca umutwe!» » Alice aravuga, aca abishira mu nkono nini y’amashurwe yari hafi. Abo basoda batatu baragendagenda umunota umwe canke ibiri, babarondera, hanyuma baragenda bucece bakurikira abandi.

“Mbega imitwe yabo iracitse?” » Umwamikazi arasemerera.

«Imitwe yabo iraheze, nimba bishimishije Nyakubahwa!» abasoda barasemerera mu kwishura.

“Ni ko biri!” » Umwamikazi arasemerera. “Woba ushobora gukina umupira w’amaguru?”

Abasirikare baracereje, baraba Alice, kuko bigaragara ko ico kibazo cari kigenewe we.

"Egome!" » ni ko Alice asemerera.

“Ngwino rero!” » Umwamikazi araboroga, Alice na we aca yifatanya n’abo bariko baragenda, yibaza cane ivyozokurikira.

“Ni—ni umusi mwiza cane!” » ni ko ijwi ry’isoni ryari iruhande yiwe rivuga. Yariko aragenda iruhande y'Ukwavu Kwera, kwariko kuramuraba mu maso n'amaganya.

Alice ati: “Cane cane, Duchess ari hehe?”

"Ceceka! Ceceka!" » Urukwavu ruvuga mw’ijwi ritoyi kandi ryihuta. Yaravye hejuru y’urutugu rwiwe ahagaritse umutima uko yariko aravuga, hanyuma arahaguruka ku maguru, ashira akanwa kiwe hafi y’ugutwi kwiwe, aca amwongorera ati “Ari munsi y’igihano co kwicwa.”

"Kubera iki?" » ni ko Alice avuga.

“Wavuze ngo ‘Ese ikibabaje!’?” » Urukwavu rurabaza.

Alice yavuze ati: “Oya, sinari nzi: “Nta co mbona ko ari ikibabaje na gato, naravuze nti ‘Kubera iki?’”

“Yaboshe amatwi y’Umwamikazi—” Urukwavu rutangura. Alice aca asemerera gatoyi atwenga. “Ewe, ceceka!” uwo Mukwavu aramwongorera mw’ijwi ry’ubwoba. "Umwamikazi azokwumva! Urabona, yaje akerewe cane, Umwamikazi ati—"

“Nimugende mu bibanza vyanyu!” Umwamikazi asemerera mw’ijwi ry’inkuba, abantu batangura kwiruka mu mpande zose, baterana; ariko rero, baraheza baratunganya mu munota umwe canke ibiri, maze urukino ruratangura. Alice yiyumvira ko atigeze abona ikibuga c’umupira w’amaguru giteye amakenga nk’ico mu buzima bwiwe; vyose vyari imirongo n’imirongo; imipira yari inzoka zikiriho, amabuye y’agaciro yari inzoka zikiriho, kandi abasoda bategerezwa kwikubita kabiri no guhagarara ku biganza no ku birenge, kugira ngo bakore imirongo.

Ingorane nyamukuru Alice yasanze mu ntango yari iyo gucungera flamingo yiwe: yarashoboye gutuma umubiri wayo wihisha, neza bihagije, munsi y’ukuboko kwiwe, amaguru yayo amanitse hasi, ariko muri rusangi, nk’uko yari yaragororotse neza izosi ryayo, kandi yariko aratera umutwe w’inzoka igipfunsi mu maso, n’ivyo, n’aho atashobora kwihanganira gutwenga: kandi igihe yari amaze gushira umutwe hasi, kandi agiye gusubira gutangura, vyari biteye ubwoba cane kubona ko iyo nkoko yari yizinguriye, kandi yari mu gikorwa co gukwegakwega: uretse ivyo vyose, muri rusangi hariho umurongo canke umurongo w’inzira aho yari aja, abasoda babiri bama bahaguruka bagenda baja mu bindi bice vyo ku butaka, Alice ntiyatevye gushika ku ciyumviro c’uko ari urukino rugoye cane vy’ukuri.

Abakinyi bose barakina icarimwe batarindiriye guhinduranya, bagatongana igihe cose, kandi barwanira inzoka; kandi mu gihe gito cane Umwamikazi yari mu mutima w’ishavu ryinshi, aca agenda agendagenda, avuza induru ati “Kura umutwe wiwe!” canke ngo “Kurana n’umutwe wiwe!” nk’incuro imwe mu munota.

Alice yatanguye kwumva ataco yiyumvamwo: kugira ngo ntakeka, ntiyari bwagire impaka n'Umwamikazi, ariko yari azi ko bishobora gushika umunota uwo ari wo wose, "maze," yiyumvira, "nobaye iki? Barakunda cane guca abantu umutwe hano; igitangaza gikomeye ni uko hari uwusigaye ari muzima!"

Yariko ararondera inzira yo guhunga, kandi yibaza nimba yoshobora guhunga ataboneka, aca abona ikintu kigaragara mu kirere: vyaramutangaje cane mu ntango, ariko, amaze kukiraba umunota umwe canke ibiri, aca abigira nk’agatwengo, aca yibwira ati “Nta muntu afise Cashire to talk.”

“Uriko uragenda gute?” » ni ko Imbwa ivuga, igihe nyene hari akanwa kahagije ko ivugana na yo.

Alice arindiriye gushika amaso abonetse, aca azunguza umutwe. Yaciye yiyumvira ati: “Nta co bimaze kuyibwira, gushika amatwi yayo ashitse, canke n’imiburiburi imwe muri zo.” Mu wundi munota umutwe wose warabonetse, hanyuma Alice ashira hasi flamingo yiwe, atangura inkuru y’urukino, yumva anezerewe cane ko afise umuntu yomwumviriza. Ico Kinyamaswa cari kimeze nk’ikibona ko hariho ibihagije ubu biriko biraboneka, kandi nta kindi cabonetse.

Alice yatanguye ati: “Sinzi ko bakina neza na gato, kandi bose baratongana biteye ubwoba cane ku buryo umuntu adashobora kwumva avuga—kandi basa n’abatagira amategeko yihariye; n’imiburiburi, nimba hariho, nta n’umwe abitaho—kandi nta ciyumviro ufise c’ingene ibintu vyose biteye urujijo; Nzoca mu bindi bikurikira ngendagendagenda ku rundi ruhande rw’ubutaka—kandi nari nkwiye gutera igikoko c’Umwamikazi ubu nyene, ariko carahunze igihe cabona icanje kije!”

“Umukunda gute Umwamikazi?” » ni ko Imbwa ivuga mw’ijwi ritoyi.

Alice ati: “Nta na rimwe: “ararengeje urugero—” Ico gihe nyene yarabonye ko Umwamikazi yari hafi inyuma yiwe, ariko aratega yompi: aca abandanya ati: “—birashoboka ko yotsinda, ko ata co bimaze mu gihe ariko araheza urukino.”

Umwamikazi aca amwenyura, aca ararengana.

“ Uriko uravugana na nde?” Umwami aravuga, aduga kuri Alice, araba umutwe w’Ikinyabuzima afise umutima ukunze cane.

Alice yaravuze ati: “Ni umugenzi wanje, ni ukuvuga akayabu k’i Cheshire, reka ndayimenyeshe.”

Umwami ati: “Sinkunda na gato ukuntu isa, ariko rero, yoshobora gusoma ukuboko kwanje iyo ikunda.”

Ico Cat kivuga kiti: “Nzohitamwo kutabigira.”

Umwami ati: “Ntube umunyaruyeri, kandi ntundebe gutyo!” Yaciye ashika inyuma ya Alice uko yariko aravuga.

Alice yavuze ati: “Ikiyaya coshobora kuraba umwami. “Ivyo narabisomye mu gitabu kinaka, ariko sinyibuka aho vyari.”

Umwami ati: "Ego, bitegerezwa gukurwaho," aca ahamagara Umwamikazi yariko ararengana muri ico gihe, "Mukunzi wanje! Ivyiza ni uko wokuraho iyi nkoko!"

Umwamikazi yari afise uburyo bumwe gusa bwo gutorera umuti ingorane zose, nini canke nto. “Kurana n’umutwe wiwe!” » aravuga, ata n’umwe aravye hirya no hino.

Umwami aravuga n’umwete ati: “Nzozana uwo mwicanyi jewe ubwanje,” aca yihuta aragenda.

Alice yiyumvira ko yoshobora no gusubirayo, akabona ingene urukino rugenda, uko yumva ijwi ry’Umwamikazi kure, asemerera n’umutima ukunze. Yari amaze kwumva igihano ciwe c’abakinyi batatu bo kwicwa kubera ko bacitse ku gihe cabo, kandi ntiyakunda ukuntu ibintu vyari vyifashe na gato, kuko urukino rwari mu rujijo ku buryo atigera amenya nimba ari we afise igihe canke atari co. Nuko aja kurondera inzoka yiwe.

Ico gikoko cariko kirarwana n’ikindi gikoko, ivyo bikaba vyasa n’uko Alice yari akaryo keza ko gutera kimwe muri vyo n’ikindi: ingorane yonyene yari iyo, ko igikoko ciwe cari carajabutse kija ku rundi ruhande rw’itongo, aho Alice yashobora kukibona kigerageza mu buryo butagira ico bukora kugira ngo kiguruke kije mu giti.

Igihe yari amaze gufata iyo flamingo akayigarura, intambara yari yararangiye, kandi izo nzige zompi zari zitaboneka: “ariko nta co bimaze cane,” ni ko Alice yiyumvira, “kubera ko imirongo yose yari yaravuye kuri uru ruhande rw’isi.” Yaciye rero ayihisha munsi y’ukuboko kwiwe, kugira ngo ntisubire guhunga, aca asubirayo kugira ngo agire ikiyago gitoyi n’umugenzi wiwe.

Amaze gusubira ku Cheshire Cat, atangazwa no kubona abantu benshi cane bakoraniye bakikuje: hariho ugutongana hagati y’uwo mwicanyi, Umwami n’Umwamikazi, bose bariko barayaga icarimwe, mu gihe abasigaye bose bari bacereje cane, kandi basa n’abatamerewe neza cane.

Igihe Alice yaboneka, bose uko ari batatu yarasavye gutorera umuti ico kibazo, baca bamusubiramwo imvo zabo, naho nyene, uko bose bavuga icarimwe, vyamugoye cane vy’ukuri gutahura neza ivyo bavuga.

Iciyumviro c’uwo mwicanyi cari, ko udashobora guca umutwe kiretse hari umubiri wowucako: ko atigeze abwirizwa gukora ikintu nk’ico mbere, kandi ko atazotangura mu gihe ciwe c’ubuzima.

Iciyumviro c’Umwami cari, ko ikintu cose gifise umutwe gishobora gucirwa umutwe, kandi ko udashobora kuvuga ubujuju.

Iciyumviro c'Umwamikazi cari, ko iyo hatagira ico hakorwa mu mwanya mutoyi yotuma abantu bose bicwa, hirya no hino. (Iryo jambo rya nyuma ni ryo ryari ryatumye umusi mukuru wose usa n’uwukomeye cane kandi uhagaze.)

Alice nta kindi yari gushobora kwiyumvira co kuvuga atari “Ni ivya Duchess: vyoba vyiza umubajije ivyo.”

Umwamikazi abwira uwo mwicanyi ati: “Ari mw’ibohero, muzane hano.” Uwo mwicanyi aragenda nk’umwampi.

Umutwe w’Ikinyamasyo watanguye kuzimangana mu gihe yari atahari, kandi, igihe yari yagarutse n’Umuganwakazi, wari warazimiye rwose; rero Umwami n’uwo mwicanyi bariruka cane baja hejuru no hasi bayirondera, mu gihe abasigaye mu mugwi basubira mu rukino.
