UMUGAMBI WA VI.
Ingurube n'Urusenda

Yamaze umunota umwe canke ibiri ahagaze araba inzu, kandi yibaza ico yokora, maze bukwi na bukwi umugabo w’ibirenge yambaye ibara ry’agahama araza yiruka ava mu giti—(yamubona ko ari umugabo w’ibirenge kuko yari yambaye ibara ry’agahama: ahandi ho, aciriye urubanza mu maso hiwe gusa, yari kumwita ifi)—aca atera dock the doles n’ijwi ryiwe ryinshi. Yari yuguruwe n’uwundi mugenzi w’ibirenge yambaye livery, afise mu maso hakikuje, afise amaso manini nk’ay’igikere; kandi abo bagabo bompi b’ibirenge, Alice yarabibonye, bari bafise imishatsi y’ifu yari izungurutse ku mitwe yabo yose. Yarumvise ashaka cane kumenya ico ari co cose, aca yinyegeza gatoyi ava mu giti kugira ngo yumvirize.

Uwo Mugabo w’Ikirenge c’Ifi yatanguye gusohora munsi y’ukuboko kwiwe ikete rinini, hafi nk’iryiwe, iryo arariha uwundi, avuga mw’ijwi ry’icubahiro ati: “Ku bwa Duchess. Uwo Mugabo w’Ikirenge c’Igikere yarasubiyemwo, mw’ijwi ry’icubahiro nyene, ahindura gusa gatoyi urutonde rw’amajambo ati: “Bivuye ku Mwamikazi.

Bompi baraheza barapfukama, imitsi yabo irafatanya.

Alice yaratwengeye cane ivyo, ku buryo yategerezwa kwiruka asubira mu giti kubera ubwoba bw’uko bomwumva; kandi igihe yasubira gusohoka, uwo Mufi-Ikirenge yari yagiye, uwundi na we yari yicaye hasi hafi y’umuryango, abona hejuru mu kirere nk’ikijuju.

Alice aca aduga ku muryango afise isoni, aradodora.

Uwo Mugenzi aravuga ati: "Ntaco bimaze gukomanga, kandi ivyo ni kubera imvo zibiri. Ubwa mbere, kubera ko ndi ku ruhande rumwe rw'umuryango nawe; ubwa kabiri, kubera ko bariko baratera urusaku imbere, nta n'umwe yoshobora kukwumva." Kandi nta gukeka ko hariho urusaku rudasanzwe cane rwariko ruraba imbere—uguboroga n’ugusesagura kwamaho, kandi rimwe na rimwe hakaba ugutera ugutera gukomeye, nk’aho isahani canke ikete vyari vyavunitse.

Alice ati: “None ndagusavye, nokwinjira gute?”

Uwo Mugenzi abandanya ataco amwitaho ati: “Hashobora kuba hariho insobanuro mu gukomanga kwawe, iyo tugira urugi hagati yacu. Nk’akarorero, iyo uba uri imbere , woshobora gukomanga, nanje nkagusohora, urazi.” Yariko araba hejuru mu kirere igihe cose yariko aravuga, kandi uwo Alice yiyumvira ko ataco akora. Yaciye yibwira ati: “Ariko kumbure ntashobora kubigira; "amaso yiwe ari hafi cane y'isonga ry'umutwe wiwe. Ariko uko biri kwose yoshobora kwishura ibibazo.—Nokwinjira gute?" aca asubiramwo, n’ijwi rirenga.

Uwo Munyabirenge yaravuze ati: “Nzokwicara hano gushika ejo—”

Muri ako kanya urugi rw’inzu rurakinguka, maze isahani nini irasohoka igenda igenda, igororotse ku mutwe w’Umunyabirenge: irarira gusa izuru ryiwe, iramenagurika ku giti kimwe cari inyuma yiwe.

“—canke bukeye, kumbure,” Uwo Munyabirenge yarabandanije mw’ijwi nyene, nk’aho nyene ata co vyabaye.

“Nzokwinjira gute?” Alice yongera kubaza, mw’ijwi rirenga.

“ Mbega uzokwinjira na gato?” » ni ko Umunyabirenge avuga. “Ico ni co kibazo ca mbere, urazi.”

Nta gukeka ko ari vyo: Alice wenyene ni we atakunda ko bamubwira gutyo. Yaribwira ati: "Birateye ubwoba vy'ukuri, ukuntu ibiremwa vyose bitongana. Birahagije kugira ngo umuntu asaze!"

Uwo Munyabirenge asa n’uwubona ko aka ari akaryo keza ko gusubiramwo ivyo yavuze, afise ibintu bitandukanye. Yavuze ati: “Nzokwicara hano, n’imisi n’imisi.”

“Ariko none nkore iki?” » ni ko Alice avuga.

Uwo Munyabirenge aravuga ati: “Ico ukunda cose,” aca atangura gutera induru.

Alice yaravuze yihebuye ati: “Ewe, nta co bimaze kumuvugisha: ni ikijuju ata gatotsi!” Nuko yugurura urugi arinjira.

Urugi rwari rujana rwose mu gikoni kinini, cari cuzuye umwotsi kuva ku mpera imwe gushika ku yindi: Duchesse yari yicaye ku ntebe y’amaguru atatu hagati, yonsa uruyoya; umutetsi yari yihetse ku muriro, avuza igitereko kinini cari kimeze nk’icuzuye isupu.

“Nta gukeka ko muri iyo supu harimwo urusenda rwinshi cane!” Alice aribwira, nk’uko yari gushobora kubera gusesa amaraso.

Nta gukeka ko hariho vyinshi cane mu kirere. Mbere na Duchesse yarasesa amaraso rimwe na rimwe; kandi ku bijanye n’uruyoya, rwariko rurasesagura rukaboroga ruhinduka ata n’akaruhuko. Ibintu vyonyene vyari mu gikoni bitasesa, ni umutetsi, n’akayabu kanini kari yicaye ku mfuruka y’umuriro kariko karatwenga kuva ku gutwi gushika ku gutwi.

Alice yaravuze afise isoni gatoyi ati: “Ndagusavye wombwira, kuko atazi neza nimba ari inyifato nziza ko ari we abanje kuvuga, “kubera iki akayabu kawe gatwenga gutyo?”

Duchess ati: "Ni akayabu k'i Cheshire, ni co gituma. Ingurube!"

Yavuze ijambo rya nyuma n’ubukazi butunguranye ku buryo Alice yasimbutse cane; ariko abona mu kindi gihe ko ryari rigenewe uruyoya, atari we, aca agira umutima rugabo, yongera arabandanya:—

“Sinari nzi ko inyabu zo muri Cheshire zama zitwenga; mu vy’ukuri, sinari nzi ko inyabu zishobora gutwenga.”

Duchesse ati: “Bose barashobora; “kandi benshi muri bo barabigira.”

Alice yaravuze mu rupfasoni cane ati: “Nta n’imwe nzi igira ivyo,” kandi yumva ahimbawe cane no kuba yarinjiye mu kiyago.

Duchesse ati: «Nta vyinshi uzi; “kandi ivyo ni ukuri.”

Alice ntiyakunda na gato ijwi ry’ivyo bintu, kandi yiyumvira ko vyoba vyiza ashizemwo ikindi kiganiro. Igihe yariko agerageza gukosora kimwe, uwo mutetsi aca akura igitereko c’isupu ku muriro, aca atangura gukora aterera ivyo yari afise vyose kuri Duchess n’uruyoya—ivyuma vy’umuriro ni vyo vyaje imbere; hanyuma hakurikiraho ugusesagura amasahani, amasahani n’amasahani. Duchesse ntiyababona mbere n’igihe bamukubita; kandi uwo mwana yari amaze kuboroga cane, ku buryo vyari bigoye kuvuga nimba ivyo bikubitisho vyamubabaje canke bitamubabaza.

“Ewe, ndagusavye wiyumvire ivyo uriko urakora!” » Alice arasemerera, asimbira hejuru n’ahansi mu mubabaro w’ubwoba. “Ewe, aho ni ho izuru ryiwe ry’agaciro rigenda!” nk’uko igisorori kinini kidasanzwe cagurutse hafi yaco, maze kikaba hafi cane kugitwara.

Uwo mugore w’Umuganwa yavuze n’ijwi rirenga ati: “Iyo umuntu wese yitwararika ivyiwe, isi yobaye yihuta kuruta uko ivyifatamwo.”

Alice, uwari yumvise anezerewe cane no kuronka akaryo ko kwerekana ubumenyi afise bukeyi, yavuze ati: “Ivyo ntivyoba ari akarusho.” “Iyumvire gusa igikorwa cokora umurango n’ijoro! Urabona isi ifata amasaha mirongo ibiri n’ane kugira ngo izunguruke ku nzira yayo—”

Duchesse ati: “Kuvuga ivy’amashoka, mumuce umutwe!”

Alice aca araba uwo mutetsi afise amaganya, kugira ngo arabe nimba ashaka gufata iyo mpanuro; ariko umutetsi yariko aravuza isupu cane, kandi asa n’uwutariko aratega yompi, aca asubira kubandanya ati: “Amasaha mirongo ibiri n’ane, nibwira ; canke ni cumi n’abiri?

“Ewe, ntumpagarike umutima ,” ni ko Duchesse yavuze; “Sinigeze nshobora kwihanganira imibare!” Ivyo biheze, aca asubira kwonsa umwana wiwe, akamuririmbira ubwoko bw’indirimbo yo kumusinzira uko abigira, akamuzunguruka cane ku mpera y’umurongo wose:

“Vugana nabi umwana wawe mutoyi, .
Kandi umukubite iyo asesa:
Abikora gusa kugira ngo ababaze,
Kuko arazi ko bitera isoni.”

KORALI.
(Mu vyo umutetsi n’uruyoya barifatanije):

"Wow! wowe! wowe!"

Mu gihe Duchess yariko aririmba umurongo wa kabiri w’iyo ndirimbo, yaguma atera uruyoya n’ubukazi hejuru n’ahansi, maze ako gakoko gatoyi k’umworo karaboroga cane, ku buryo Alice atashobora kwumva amajambo ngo:—

“Ndabwira umuhungu wanje nabi, .
Ndamukubita iyo asesa;
Kuko ashobora kunezerererwa bimwe bishitse .
Ibara ry’umukara igihe ashaka!”

KORALI.

"Wow! wowe! wowe!"

"Ehe! Ushobora kuyiyonsa gatoyi, niwabishaka!" Duchess abwira Alice, amutera uruyoya uko avuga. “Ntegerezwa kuja kwitegurira gukina croquet n’Umwamikazi,” aca yihuta ava mu cumba. Umutetsi aca amutera igitereko c’umukate igihe yariko arasohoka, ariko kiramubura gusa.

Alice yarafashe ako gakoko n’ingorane zimwezimwe, kuko kari akaremwa gatoyi gafise ishusho y’agatangaza, maze arambura amaboko n’amaguru yaco mu mpande zose, “nk’uko nyene ari ifi y’inyenyeri,” ni ko Alice yiyumvira. Ico kintu gitoyi c’umukene cariko kirasonza nk’imoteri y’umwotsi igihe yagifata, kiguma kivyura kabiri kigasubira kwigorora, ku buryo vyose hamwe, mu munota wa mbere canke ibiri, cari nk’uko yashobora gukora kugira ngo agifate.

Akimara kumenya uburyo bwiza bwo kuyiyonsa, (ari ukuyigobeka ngo ibe nk’urudodo, hanyuma akayifata neza ugutwi kw’iburyo n’ikirenge c’ibubamfu, kugira ngo ntiyikuremwo,) aca ayijana mu kirere. Alice yariyumviriye ati: “Nimba ntajana n’uyu mwana, nta gukeka ko bazomwica mu musi umwe canke ibiri: mbega ntivyoba ari ubwicanyi kumusiga inyuma?” Yavuze amajambo ya nyuma n’ijwi rirenga, ako gakoko gatoyi karatontomera mu kwishura (kari kahevye gusesa amaraso muri ico gihe). Alice ati: “Ntukigere utontomera; “ubwo si uburyo bubereye na gato bwo guserura ivyiyumviro vyawe.”

Uwo mwana yongera gutontomera, Alice na we arabiraba mu maso ahagaze cane kugira ngo arabe ikibazo. Nta nkeka ko cari gifise izuru rihindukira cane , rimeze cane nk’inzoka kuruta izuru nyaryo; kandi amaso yaco yariko aragabanuka cane ku mwana: muri rusangi Alice ntiyakunda ukuntu ico kintu cari kimeze na gato. Yaciye yiyumvira ati: “Ariko kumbure cariko kirarira gusa,” aca asubira kukiraba mu maso, kugira ngo arabe nimba hari amarira.

Oya, nta marira yariho. Alice aravuga ataco yishisha ati: "Nimba ugiye guhinduka ingurube, mukunzi wanje, nta kindi nzogirana nawe. Zirikana ubu!" Uwo mukene mutoyi yongera kuboroga (canke gutontomera, ntivyashoboka ko umuntu avuga uwuhe), baraheza barabandanya umwanya mutoyi bacereje.

Alice yariko atangura kwiyumvira mu mutima wiwe ati: “None, iki gikoko nzogikora iki ni ndagishika i muhira?” igihe yongera gutontomera, n’ubukazi bwinshi, ku buryo yaravye hasi mu maso hayo afise ubwoba bumwe bumwe. Ico gihe nta kosa ryari gushobora kubaho: ntiryari rirengeye canke rirengeye ingurube, kandi yumvise ko vyoba ari ubupfu rwose kuri we kuyitwara kure.

Nuko ashira hasi ako gakoko gatoyi, aca yumva aruhutse cane abonye kariko karagenda bucece mu giti. Yaribwiye ati: “Iyo rikura, ryari gutuma haba umwana mubi cane: ariko ni ko rituma haba ingurube nziza cane.” Kandi yaciye atangura kwiyumvira abandi bana yari azi, boshobora gukora neza cane nk’ingurube, kandi yariko aribwira gusa ati, “iyo umuntu amenya gusa uburyo bwiza bwo kubahindura—” igihe yatangazwa gatoyi no kubona Imbwa y’i Cheshire yicaye ku ishami ry’igiti ku birometero bikeyi.

Ico Cat caratwenga gusa igihe cabona Alice. Yari asa n’uwufise kamere nziza, aca yiyumvira ati: naho nyene yari ifise amano maremare cane n’amenyo menshi cane, ni co gituma yumva ko ikwiye gufatwa neza.

Yaciye atangura ati: “Cheshire Puss,” ahubwo afise isoni, kuko atazi na gato nimba yoshima iryo zina: ariko rero, yaratwengera mw’ijigo gusa. Alice aca yiyumvira ati: “Nza, biraryoshe gushika ubu,” aca arabandanya. “Ndagusavye, wombwira inzira nkwiye guca mvuye hano?”

Igikoko kivuga giti: “Ivyo bivana cane n’aho ushaka gushika.”

Alice yavuze ati: “Sinkunda cane aho—.”

Igikoko kivuga kiti: “None rero ntaco bimaze iyo uja mu nzira.”

Alice yongeyeko ati: “—igihe cose nzoba nshitse ahantu ,” ni ko Alice yongeyeko nk’insobanuro.

"Egoo, ivyo uzobikora ata gukeka," niko Kayabu kavuga, "niwagenda gusa igihe kirekire bihagije."

Alice yumvise ko ivyo bidashobora guhakanwa, aca agerageza ikindi kibazo. “Ni abantu bameze gute baba hano?”

"Muri iyo nzira," iyo Kayavu iravuga, izunguza urutugu rwayo rw'iburyo, "haba Umuhinguzi w'inkofero: kandi muri iyo nzira," izunguza ukundi kuguru, "haba Urukwavu rwa Werurwe. Sura umwe muri bo ukunda: bompi barasaze."

Alice yaravuze ati: “Ariko sinshaka kuja mu basazi.

"Egoo, ntushobora gufasha ivyo," Kayabu kavuga: "twese twarasaze hano. Nanje ndasaze. Urasaze."

“Uzi gute ko nsaze?” » ni ko Alice avuga.

Igikoko kivuga kiti: “Utegerezwa kuba uriho, canke ntiwari kuza ngaha.”

Alice ntiyiyumvira ko ivyo bivyemeza na gato; ariko rero, yarabandanije ati “Kandi uzi gute ko usaze?”

"Gutangura," Kayabu kavuga, "imbwa ntisaze. Uravyemera?"

Alice yavuze ati: “Niyumvira ko ari ukwo biri.

"Ego, rero," Kayabu karakomeza, "urabona, imbwa iraboroga iyo irakaye, kandi izunguza umurizo wayo iyo inezerewe. Ubu ndaboroga iyo nezerewe, kandi izunguza umurizo wanje iyo ishavuye. Ni co gituma ndasaze."

Alice yavuze ati: “ Nvyita uguboroga, si uguboroga.

Igikoko kivuga kiti: “Uvyite uko ukunda.” “Urakina croquet n’Umwamikazi uno musi?”

Alice yavuze ati: “Nari nkwiye kubikunda cane, ariko ntaratumirwa.”

Igikoko kivuga kiti: «Uzombona ng’aho», kirazimangana.

Alice ntiyatangajwe cane n’ivyo, yariko aramenyerera cane ibintu biteye ubwoba biba. Igihe yariko araraba aho cari, kigasubira kuboneka bukwi na bukwi.

“By-the-bye, uruyoya rwabaye iki?” » ni ko Cat avuga. “Nari hafi kwibagirwa kubaza.”

Alice yaravuze mu gacerere ati: “Yahindutse ingurube,” nk’aho yoba yagarutse mu buryo busanzwe.

Igikoko kivuga kiti: “Nari niyumviriye ko bizoshika”, kigasubira kuzimangana.

Alice yarindiriye gatoyi, igice kimwe citeze ko azosubira kuyibona, ariko ntiyaboneka, haciye umunota umwe canke ibiri aragenda aja iyo bavuga ko Urukwavu rwa March ruba. Yaribwiye ati: “Nari narabonye abakora inkofero; “Igikoko c’Urukwavu co muri Ntwarante kizoba ari co gishimishije kuruta ibindi vyose, kandi kumbure kubera ko ari muri Rusama ntikizoba ari co giteye ubwoba cane—n’imiburiburi ntikizoba ari co giteye ubwoba nk’uko cari muri Ntwarante.” Uko avuga ivyo, araba hejuru, abona n’Ikinyamaswa, cicaye kw’ishami ry’igiti.

“Wavuze ingurube, canke umusukoni?” » ni ko Cat avuga.

Alice arishura ati: “Navuze ingurube; “kandi nipfuza ko utoguma uboneka ukazimangana bukwi na bukwi: utuma umuntu agira umutwe mwinshi.”

Igikoko kivuga kiti: «Ni vyiza; kandi kuri iyi ncuro ryarazimangana buhoro buhoro cane, ritangura ku mpera y’umurizo, rihera ku nseko, ryasigaye igihe gitoyi inyuma y’aho ibindi vyaryo biheze.

Alice yariyumviriye ati: “Ego!, kenshi narabonye akayabu katagira umutwenzi; "ariko umutwenzi ata n'akayabu! Ni co kintu giteye amakenga kuruta ibindi vyose nari narabonye mu buzima bwanje!"

Ntiyari yaragiye kure cane imbere y’uko abona inzu y’Urukwavu rwa March: yiyumvira ko itegerezwa kuba ari yo nzu ibereye, kuko imigende y’umwotsi yari imeze nk’amatwi, igisenge cari gipfutse n’ubwoya. Yari inzu nini cane, ku buryo atakunda kwegera gushika amaze gufata ikindi gice c’ibizinu c’ibubamfu, maze yiduza gushika ku metero nk’ibiri: mbere n’aho nyene yaraduze aja iyo nzu afise isoni, yibwira ati “Twibaze ko yoba yagiye irasazi cane.
