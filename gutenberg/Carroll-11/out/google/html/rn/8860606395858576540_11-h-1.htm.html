UMUGAMBI WA I.
Hasi mu mwobo w'urukwavu

Alice yariko atangura kuruha cane no kwicara iruhande ya mushikawe ku nkombe, no kutagira ico akora: rimwe canke kabiri yari yarasuzumye mu gitabu mushikawe yariko arasoma, ariko nta mashusho canke ibiyago carimwo, “kandi igitabu gifise akamaro akahe,” ni ko Alice yiyumvira “ata mafoto canke ibiyago?”

Yariko rero ariyumvira mu muzirikanyi wiwe bwite (nk’uko yari ashoboye kwose, kuko umusi w’ubushuhe watuma yumva asinziriye cane kandi ari ikijuju), nimba umunezero wo gukora uruzitiro rw’amadayisi woba ubereye ingorane zo guhaguruka no gutoragura ayo madayisi, igihe bukwi na bukwi Urukwavu Rwera rufise amaso y’ibara ry’agahama rwirukira hafi yiwe.

Nta kintu gitangaje cane cari muri ivyo; eka mbere Alice ntiyiyumviriye ko ari ikintu kitari co cane kwumva Urukwavu rwibwira ruti: “Ewe mukunzi! Ewe mukunzi! Nzocererwa!” (amaze kuvyiyumvira inyuma, aca yiyumvira ko yari akwiye kwibaza ivyo, ariko ico gihe vyose vyasa n’ibisanzwe); ariko igihe Ukwavu mu vy’ukuri kwakura isaha mu mufuko w’ikoti ryiwe , maze akayiraba, hanyuma yihuta, Alice aca atangura guhaguruka, kuko vyamuciye mu muzirikanyi wiwe ko atari bwigere abona urukwavu rufise umufuko w’ikoti, canke isaha yo gukuramwo, maze aca yiruka a, igihimbare cari mu gihe nyene co kubona kimanuka mu mwobo munini w’inkwavu munsi y’uruzitiro.

Mu kindi gihe Alice yaramanutse arayikurikira, nta na rimwe yigeze yiyumvira ingene mw’isi yosubira gusohoka.

Ico kinogo c’inkwavu caragenda kigororotse nk’umugende w’amazi mu nzira kanaka, hanyuma kija hasi bukwi na bukwi, bukwi na bukwi ku buryo Alice ata mwanya yari afise wo kwiyumvira kwihagarika imbere y’uko yisanga ariko aragwa mw’iriba ryinshi cane.

Canke iryo riba ryari rirerire cane, canke yagwa buhoro cane, kuko yari afise umwanya mwinshi uko yamanuka kugira ngo arabe hirya no hino no kwibaza ibizokurikira. Ubwa mbere, yaragerageje kuraba hasi kugira ngo amenye ico yariko araza, ariko hari umwiza cane ku buryo ata co abona; aca araba ku mpande z’iriba, abona ko ryuzuye amakabati n’ibitabo; aha n’aho yabona amakarata n’amafoto bimanitswe ku bipimo. Yamanuye igikombe kuri kimwe mu bitanda uko yariko ararengana; yari yanditsweko ngo “ORANGE MARMALADE”, ariko ikimubabaza cane yari ubusa: ntiyakunda gutera iyo nkono kubera ubwoba bwo kwica umuntu yari munsi yayo, rero yarashoboye kuyishira muri imwe mu kabati uko yariko arayicako.

"None!" Alice yariyumviriye ati: "Inyuma y'ugugwa nk'uku, nta co nzokwiyumvira co kugwa ku ngazi! Ese ukuntu bose bazokwiyumvira ko ndi umunyabute ndi i muhira! Emwe, nta co novuga kuri ivyo, naho noba naguye hejuru y'inzu!" (Ivyo vyari bishoboka cane ko ari ukuri.)

Hasi, hasi, hasi. None ukugwa kwoba kutazokwigera guhera? “Nibaza ko muri iki gihe naguye ibilometero bingahe?” » aca aravuga n’ijwi rirenga. “Ntegerezwa kuba ndiko ndashika ahantu hafi y’isi hagati. Reka ndabe: ivyo vyoba ari ibilometero ibihumbi bine, nibwira ko—” (kuko, urabona, Alice yari yize ibintu vyinshi nk’ivyo mu vyigwa vyiwe mw’ishure, kandi naho aka kari akaryo keza cane ko kwerekana ubumenyi bwiwe, nk’uko ata n’umwe yari afise imyimenyerezo myiza yo kuvuga ngo “ ivyo ni nk’urugendo rukwiye—ariko rero nkibaza ko nshitse kuri Latitude canke Longitude iyihe?” (Alice na we nyene nta ciyumviro yari afise c’ico Latitude ari co, canke Longitude, ariko yiyumvira ko ari amajambo meza cane yo kuvuga.)

Ubu nyene aca asubira gutangura. “Nibaza ko nzogwa mw’isi nyene! Ese ukuntu bizoba biteye umunezero gusohoka mu bantu bagenda n’imitwe yabo hasi! Aba Antipathies, nibwira ko—” (yari ahubwo anezerewe ko ata n’umwe yariko aratega yompi, kuri iyi ncuro, kuko ata n’ijambo ryiza ryumvikanye na gato) “—ariko of you the Please have is. Madamu, iyi ni Nouvelle-Zélande canke ni Australiya?” (kandi yaragerageje gupfukama uko yavuga— ugupfukama kw’agatangaza uko uriko uragwa mu kirere! Woba wibaza ko woshobora kuvyifatamwo neza?) “Kandi ese ukuntu azokwiyumvira ko ndi umukobwa mutoyi w’injuju kubera kubaza! Oya, ntibizokwigera bikora kubaza: kumbure nzobona vyanditswe ahantu kanaka.”

Hasi, hasi, hasi. Nta kindi yari gukora, rero Alice ntiyatevye gusubira kuvuga. “Dina azonkumbura cane muri iri joro, ni ko nkwiye kwiyumvira!” (Dina yari akayabu.) "Nizigiye ko bazokwibuka isahani yiwe y'amata mu gihe c'icayi. Dina mukunzi wanje! Ivyiza ni uko woba uri hano hasi nanje! Nta mbeba ziri mu kirere, ndatinya, ariko woshobora gufata umubu, kandi ivyo bisa cane n'imbeba, urazi. Ariko mbeba inyabu zirya umubu, ndabaza?" Kandi ng’aho Alice yatanguye gusinzira, aca abandanya yibwira, mu buryo nk’ubw’indoto ati: “Mbega inyabu zirya inzoka? Mbega inyabu zirya inzoka?” kandi rimwe na rimwe ngo “Mbega uducurama turya inyabu?” kuko, urabona, nk’uko atashobora kwishura ikibazo na kimwe muri ivyo bibiri, ntivyari bihambaye cane ko abishiramwo mu buryo ubuhe. Yarumvise ko ariko arasinzira, kandi yari ahejeje gutangura kurota ko ariko aragendana na Dina, akamubwira n’umwete mwinshi ati: «None Dina, mbwira ukuri: woba warigeze kurya umubu?» igihe giturumbuka, thump! gukubita! amanutse ashika ku kirundo c’inkoni n’amababi yumye, maze ukugwa kurahera.

Alice ntiyakomeretse na gato, aca asimbira hejuru ku birenge vyiwe mu kanya nk’ako gukubita urugohe: araba hejuru, ariko vyose vyari vyirabura hejuru; imbere yiwe hari uwundi murongo muremure, kandi Urukwavu Rweru rwari rukiri mu maso, ruriko rurawumanuka vyihuta. Nta mwanya n’umwe wo gutakaza: kure yagiye Alice nk’umuyaga, kandi yari mu gihe nyene co kwumva ivuga, uko yahindukira mu mfuruka, “Ewe matwi yanje n’imisatsi yanje, ese ukuntu iriko iracererwa!” Yari hafi inyuma yayo igihe yahindukira mu mfuruka, ariko Urukwavu ntirwari rugiboneka: rwari ruri mu cumba kirekire kandi kiri hasi, cari kimuritswe n’umurongo w’amatara yamanitse ku gisenge.

Hariho inzugi zikikuje iyo nzu, ariko zose zari zikingiwe; kandi Alice amaze gushika ku ruhande rumwe akaduga ku rundi, agerageza urugi rwose, aca agenda ababaye amanuka hagati, yibaza ingene yosubira gusohoka.

Bukwi na bukwi, aca abona akameza gatoyi k’amaguru atatu, kose kari mu kiyo gikomeye; nta kintu cari kuri co kiretse urufunguruzo rutoyi rw’inzahabu, kandi iciyumviro ca mbere Alice yari afise cari co ko coba ari ica rumwe mu miryango y’iyo nzu; ariko, n’agahinda! canke izo ngufuri zari nini cane, canke urufunguruzo rwari ruto cane, ariko uko vyogenda kwose ntirwari gufungura n’imwe muri zo. Ariko rero, ku ncuro ya kabiri, aca abona umugozi mutoyi yari atarabona mbere, kandi inyuma yawo hari urugi rutoyi rw’uburebure bw’amasentimetero nka cumi n’atanu: yaragerageje ako gafunguruzo gatoyi k’inzahabu kari mu rugi, maze aranezerwa cane ko karamubereye!

Alice yuguruye urugi asanga rujana mu gace gatoyi, katarengeye cane umwobo w’imbeba: aca arapfukama araba muri ako gace aja mw’itongo ryiza kuruta ayandi yose wigeze kubona. Ese ukuntu yari afise icipfuzo co kuva muri iyo nzu y’umwiza, akagendagenda hagati y’ivyo bitanda vy’amashurwe akeye n’ayo masoko akonje, ariko ntashobora no guca mu muryango umutwe wiwe; Alice umworo yariyumviriye ati: "kandi naho umutwe wanje woca muri vyo, vyoba ari bike cane ata bitugu vyanje. Ewe, ese ukuntu nokwipfuza guceceka nk'itelesikope! Niyumvira ko nobishobora, iyo menya gusa ingene notangura." Kuko, urabona, ibintu vyinshi bitari vyo vyari vyabaye muri iyi misi iheze, ku buryo Alice yari yatanguye kwiyumvira ko ari bike cane vy’ukuri bidashoboka.

Bisa n’uko ata co vyari bimaze kurindira iruhande y’umuryango mutoyi, aca asubira ku meza, afise icizigiro c’uko yoshobora kuronkako urundi rufunguzo, canke uko vyogenda kwose igitabu c’amategeko yo gufunga abantu nk’ibipimo vy’amatelesikopu: kuri uwo mwanya yasanzeko akacupa gatoyi, (“ariko ata gukeka ko katari hano mbere,” ni ko Alice yavuze,) kandi akikuje igipande ca laper the word “NYUNYE,” canditsweko neza cane mu nyuguti nini.

Vyose vyari vyiza cane kuvuga ngo “Nywera,” ariko Alice mutoyi w’inkerebutsi ntiyari agiye kubigira yihuta. Yaciye ambwira ati: “Oya, nzobanza ndabe, mbone nimba hariho ikimenyetso c’uko ‘ ubumara ’ canke atari bwo”; kuko yari yarasomye utuganiro dutoduto twiza twinshi twerekeye abana bari baturiwe, kandi baribwa n’ibikoko vyo mw’ishamba n’ibindi bintu bitaryoshe, vyose kubera ko batazokwibuka amategeko yoroshe abagenzi babo bari babigishije: nk’akarorero, ko umupira w’amaguru utukura ushushe uzoguturira iyo uwufashe igihe kirekire cane; kandi ko iyo uciye urutoke rwawe rwose cane n’icumu, akenshi ruva amaraso; kandi ntiyari bwigere yibagirwa yuko, iyo unyweye cane mu cupa ryanditsweko ngo “ubumara,” hafi y’aho ataco wemeranya nawe, vuba canke bidatevye.

Ariko rero, iryo cupa ntiryari ryanditsweko ngo “ubumara,” ni co gituma Alice yaciye agira ubwoba bwo kurisogongera, maze abona ko ari ryiza cane, (mu vy’ukuri ryari rifise uburyohe buvanze n’umukate w’umukara, umukate w’umukara, umukate w’umukara, inkoko yokeje, umukate w’umukara, n’umukate w’umukara ushushe,) ntiyatevye kuriheza.

“Ese ukuntu ari inyiyumvo yo kumenya vyinshi!” ni ko Alice yavuze; “Ntegerezwa kuba ndiko ndaceceka nk’itelesikope.”

Kandi ni ko vyari vyifashe vy’ukuri: ubu yari afise uburebure bw’amasentimetero cumi gusa, kandi mu maso hiwe harakayangana iyo yiyumvira ko ubu yari afise ubunini bukwiye bwo guca muri ako muryango mutoyi aja muri iryo tongo ryiza cane. Ariko rero, yabanje kurindira iminota mikeyi kugira ngo arabe nimba azosubira gucika intege: yumvise afise ubwoba bukeyi ku bijanye n’ivyo; Alice yibwira ati: “kuko vyoshobora guhera, urazi, mu gusohoka kwanje kwose, nk’ubuji. Nibaza ko noba meze gute ico gihe?” Kandi yaragerageje kwiyumvira ingene urubeya rw’ubuji rumeze iyo ubuji buzimye, kuko atashobora kwibuka ko yigeze abona ikintu nk’ico.

Haciye igihe, abonye ko ata kindi kibaye, aca afata ingingo yo kuja mw’itongo ubwo nyene; ariko, n’agahinda, Alice w’umukene! ashitse ku muryango, asanga yari yibagiwe ako gafunguruzo gatoyi k’inzahabu, maze asubira ku meza kugira ngo akaruronke, asanga ataco yari gushobora kurushikako: yarashobora kurubona neza cane biciye mu kiyo, aca agerageza uko ashoboye kwose kuduga kuri rimwe mu maguru y’imeza, ariko ryari rinyerera cane; maze amaze kuruha no kugerageza, ako gakoko gatoyi k’umukene karicara kararira.

“Ngwino, nta co bimaze kurira gutyo!” ni ko Alice yibwira, ahubwo avuga n’agahinda; “Ndaguhanura ngo ureke kuri uwu munota!” Muri rusangi yariha impanuro nziza cane, (naho yazikurikiza gake cane), kandi rimwe na rimwe yarituka cane ku buryo amarira yamutera; kandi rimwe yibuka ko yagerageza gutera amasandugu amatwi yiwe bwite kubera yari yibeshe mu mukino w’umupira w’amaguru yariko arakina, kuko uwo mwana w’umunyaruyeri yari akunda cane kwigira nk’abantu babiri. Umukene Alice yariyumviriye ati: “Ariko ubu nta co bimaze kwigira abantu babiri !

Bidatevye ijisho ryiwe ryaguye ku gasandugu gatoyi k’ibirahuri kari munsi y’imeza: aragafungura, asangamwo akagati gato cane, kariko amajambo ngo “NDIRE” kari yanditsweko neza cane mu bihimba vy’umubiri vy’ibiti vy’imiberoshi. Alice aravuga ati: "Ego, ndabirya, kandi nivyatumye nkura, ndashobora gushika ku rufunguzo; kandi nivyatumye nkura, ndashobora kwinyegeza munsi y'umuryango; rero uko biri kwose nzoja mw'itongo, kandi ntaco bimaze!"

Yariye gatoyi, aca yibwira ahagaritse umutima ati: “Inzira iyihe? Inzira iyihe?”, afata ukuboko kwiwe hejuru y’umutwe wiwe kugira ngo yumve inzira iriko irakura, aca atangazwa cane no kubona ko yagumye afise ubunini bumwe: kugira ngo tumenye neza, ivyo muri rusangi bishika iyo umuntu arya umukate, ariko Alice yari yaragize ivyo yitega. gushika, ko vyasa n’ibiteye isoni cane kandi ari ubujuju ko ubuzima bubandanya mu buryo busanzwe.

Yaciye rero atangura gukora, kandi bidatevye cane araheza akora iyo gato.
