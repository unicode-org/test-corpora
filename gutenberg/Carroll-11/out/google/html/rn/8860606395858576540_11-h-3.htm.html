UMUGAMBI WA III.
Ihiganwa ry'aba Caucus n'inkuru ndende

Nkako, bari umugwi usa n’uw’agatangaza wari ukoraniriye ku nkombe—inyoni zifise amababa akwegakwega, ibikoko bifise ubwoya bwavyo bubifatanye hafi, kandi vyose vyari bitonyanga amazi, bipfutse kandi bitameze neza.

Ikibazo ca mbere vy’ukuri cari, ingene bosubira kwuma: baragiranye ikiganiro ku bijanye n’ivyo, hanyuma haciye iminota mikeyi vyasa n’ibisanzwe kuri Alice gusanga ariko arayaga na bo mu buryo bumenyerewe, nk’aho yari yarabamenye ubuzima bwiwe bwose. Nkako, yari afise impaka ndende cane na Lory, amaherezo aca ahinduka umunyaruyeri, akavuga gusa ati: “Ndakuruta, kandi ntegerezwa kumenya neza;” kandi ivyo Alice ntiyari kwemera atazi imyaka yayo, kandi, nk’uko Lory yanse ata gukeka kuvuga imyaka yayo, nta kindi co kuvuga.

Amaherezo Imbeba, yasa n'uwufise ubukuru muri bo, irasemerera iti: "Nimwicare mwese, mumwumvirize, nzobawuma bihagije vuba!" Bose bicara rimwe, mu mpeta nini, hagati hari Imbeba. Alice yaguma ayihanga amaso ahagaritse umutima, kuko yumva ata gukeka ko yofatwa n’indwara y’umusonga mubi iyo atavyuma vuba cane.

“Eh!” » Imbeba iravuga n’umuyaga uhambaye, « mwese mwariteguye? Iki ni co kintu cyumye kuruta ibindi vyose nzi. Nimucereze hirya no hino, nimba mushaka! Northumbria—’”

"Ego!" » Lory aravuga, afise ubwoba.

“Ndagusavye imbabazi!” » Imbeba iravuga, ipfutse amaso, ariko mu rupfasoni cane iti: «Wavuze?»

“Si jewe!” » Lory aravuga yihuta.

Imbeba iti: “Nari niyumviriye ko ari wewe ubizi.” “—Ndabandanya nti: ‘Edwin na Morcar, abakuru b’igisagara ca Mercia n’ica Northumbria, baramumenyesheje: mbere na Stigand, umusenyeri mukuru w’i Canterbury, yakunda igihugu, yasanze ari vyiza—’”

“Wabonye iki ?” » Inta iravuga.

Imbeba yishura mu buryo buteye umusaraba iti: “ Narayibonye ,” “ni ukuri urazi ico ‘ni’ bisobanura.”

Inta iravuga iti: "Ndazi neza ico 'co' bisobanura, iyo mbonye ikintu: muri rusangi ni igikere canke inzoka. Ikibazo ni iki, umusenyeri mukuru yasanze iki?"

Imbeba ntiyabonye ico kibazo, ariko yihuta kubandanya, “’—yasanze ari vyiza kujana na Edgar Atheling guhura na William no kumuha ikamba. Inyifato ya William mu ntango yari iringaniye. Ariko ubujuju bw’Abanormandi biwe—’ Ubu uriko uragenda gute, mukunzi wanje?” ryarabandanije, rihindukirira Alice uko ryariko rivuga.

Alice yavuze mw’ijwi ry’agahinda ati: “Nk’uko vyari bisanzwe, bisa n’uko bitanyoma na gato.”

Dodo yaravuze n’icubahiro, arahaguruka ati: “Nimba ari ukwo biri, ndasaba ko iyo nama isubirwamwo, kugira ngo haboneke ubwo nyene uburyo bwo gutorera umuti ibibazo bufise inguvu kuruta—”

“Vuga Icongereza!” » ni ko Inkukuma ivuga. “Sinzi insobanuro y’igice c’ayo majambo maremare, kandi, ikindi, na wewe sinemera ko uzi!” Kandi Inkukuma irapfukama kugira ngo ihishe akamwemwe: zimwe mu zindi nyoni ziratwenga zirumvikana.

Uwo Dodo yavuze mw’ijwi ry’agahinda ati: “Ico nari ngiye kuvuga ni uko ikintu ciza cotuma twuma coba ari uguhiganwa kw’aba Caucus.”

“Isiganwa ry’aba Caucus ni iki?” ni ko Alice yavuze; si uko yashaka kumenya vyinshi, ariko Dodo yari yarahagaze nk’aho yiyumvira ko hari umuntu akwiye kuvuga, kandi nta wundi asa n’uwushaka kuvuga ikintu.

Uwo Dodo yavuze ati: “Kubera iki, uburyo bwiza bwo kubisigura ni ukubikora.” (Kandi, nk’uko woshobora gukunda kugerageza ico kintu wewe nyene, umusi umwe w’urushana, nzokubwira ingene aba Dodo bacifashije.)

Ubwa mbere ryarashize ikimenyetso ku nzira y’amasiganwa, mu bwoko bw’uruziga, (“imiterere nyayo ntaco imaze,” ni ko ryavuga,) hanyuma umusi mukuru wose ushirwa ku ruhande rw’inzira, ngaha n’aho. Nta “Umwe, babiri, batatu, n’inyuma” yariho, ariko batangura kwiruka igihe bashaka, bagahagarika igihe bakunda, ku buryo bitoroshe kumenya igihe isiganwa ryarangiriye. Ariko rero, igihe bari bamaze nk’igice c’isaha bariruka, kandi bariko barasubira kwuma cane, Dodo aca arahamagara bukwi na bukwi ati “Isiganwa ryaraheze!” bose barayikikuza, bahengamira, babaza bati: «Ariko ni nde yatsinze?»

Iki kibazo Dodo ntiyashobora kugisubiza ataco yiyumviriye cane, aca yicara igihe kirekire urutoke rumwe rufatanye mu ruhanga rwayo (aho ukunda kubona Shakespeare, mu mafoto yiwe), mu gihe abasigaye barindiriye bacereje. Amaherezo Dodo ati: “ Bose baratsinze, kandi bose bategerezwa kugira ibihembo.”

“Ariko ni nde azotanga ibihembo?” amajwi menshi cane arabaza.

“Kubera iki, she , birashoboka,” ni ko uwo Dodo yerekana Alice n’urutoke rumwe; maze uwo mugwi wose uca umukikuza, bamusemerera mu buryo buteye urujijo bati: “Ibihembo!

Alice nta ciyumviro yari afise co gukora, maze mu kwihebura ashira ukuboko kwiwe mu mufuko, akurayo agasandugu k’ibikombe, (amahirwe amazi y’umunyu ntiyari yinjiyemwo), arabitanga nk’ibihembo. Hariho neza na neza igice kimwe c’a-piece, cose gikikuje.

Imbeba iravuga iti: «Ariko we ubwiwe ategerezwa kuba afise igihembo, urazi.

Uwo Dodo arishura ataco yishisha cane ati: “Ego cane. “Ni igiki kindi ufise mu mufuko wawe?” arabandanya, ahindukirira Alice.

Alice yaravuze ababaye ati: “Ikirenge gusa.

Uwo Dodo ati: “Uwuhe hano.”

Bose baca bongera kumukikuza, mu gihe Dodo yariko aramuha igipfunsi n’icubahiro, ati “Turakwinginze ngo wemere iki gipfukisho c’agaciro;” kandi, rihejeje iyo nsiguro ngufi, bose barasemerera.

Alice yabonye ko ivyo vyose ari ibiteye isoni cane, ariko vyose vyasa n’ibikomeye cane ku buryo atatinya gutwenga; kandi, kuko ata co yari afise co kwiyumvira co kuvuga, aca arapfukama gusa, afata igipfunsi, asa n’uwuteye iteka uko ashoboye kwose.

Igikurikira ni ukurya ama comfits: ivyo vyatuma haba urusaku n’uruja n’uruza, kuko inyoni nini zari zidodomba ko zidashobora kuronka ivyazo, izo nto zigaca zinyiganyiga zigategerezwa gukubitwa mu mugongo. Ariko rero, amaherezo vyaraheze, bongera kwicara mu mpeta, binginga Imbeba ngo ibabwire ikindi kintu.

Alice yongeyeko mu rurimi rworoshe ati: “Wasezeranye kumbwira kahise kawe, urazi, n’igituma ari wewe wanka—C na D,” yongeyeko mu rurimi rworoshe, igice c’ubwoba bw’uko vyosubira kubabazwa.

“Ivyanje ni inkuru ndende kandi ibabaje!” » ni ko Imbeba ivuga, ihindukirira Alice, iraheza iraniha.

Alice araba hasi atangaye ku murizo w’Imbeba ati: “ Ni umurizo muremure, ata gukeka; “ariko kuki uvyita ibibabaje?” Kandi yaguma abizazanira igihe Imbeba yariko iravuga, ku buryo iciyumviro ciwe ku bijanye n’iyo nkuru cari nk’iki:—

“Ntimuriko murajayo!” » ni ko Imbeba ibwira Alice bikomeye. “Uriko wiyumvira iki?”

Alice aca ambwira yicishije bugufi cane ati: “Ndagusavye imbabazi, nibwira ko wari warashitse ku nzira ya gatanu?”

“ Nta co nari mfise! ” Imbeba irasemerera, n’ijwi rirenga kandi irakaye cane.

“Ipfundo!” » ni ko Alice yavuze, yama yiteguriye kwigira uwufise akamaro, kandi akamuraba ahagaritse umutima. “Ewe, reka mfashe kubikuraho!”

Imbeba irahaguruka iragenda iti: «Nta kintu na kimwe nzokora nk’ico.» “Urantuka mu kuvuga ibintu nk’ivyo vy’ubujuju!”

“Sinabishaka kuvuga!” » ni ko Alice w’umukene yinginga. “Ariko urababazwa vyoroshe cane, urazi!”

Imbeba iraboroga gusa mu kwishura.

“Ndagusavye ugaruke uheze inkuru yawe!” Alice aca arahamagara inyuma yayo; abandi na bo bose barifatanya n’abandi bati: “Ego, ndabinginze mubikore!” ariko Imbeba izunguza umutwe gusa ata kwihangana, iragenda yihuta gatoyi.

“Ese ukuntu ari ikibabaje ko bitazoguma!” » Lory aca aniha, igihe nyene yari amaze gusohoka rwose; kandi umusaza w’Ikinyamasyo yarafashe akaryo ko kubwira umukobwa wiwe ati “Eh, mukunzi wanje!Iki kibe icigwa kuri wewe co kutazokwigera ushavura !” “Fata ururimi rwawe Mama!” » uwo musore w’Ikinyamasyo aravuga, avuga n’agahinda gatoyi. “Urahagije kugira ngo ugerageze ukwihangana kw’inzoka y’inzoka!”

“Iyo noba mfise Dina wacu hano, ndazi ko mfise!” » Alice avuga n’ijwi rirenga, ata n’umwe abwira cane cane. “Ari vuba kuyigarura!”

“Kandi Dina ni nde, nimba noshobora gutera ubwoba ngo mbaze ico kibazo?” » ni ko Lory avuga.

Alice arishura n'umwete, kuko yama yiteguriye kuvuga ivyerekeye igikoko ciwe: "Dinah ni akayabu kacu. Kandi ni akayabu gakomeye cane ko gufata imbeba udashobora kwiyumvira! Kandi oh, nifuza ko womubona inyuma y'inyoni! Ese, azorya akanyoni gatoyi ukimara kukabona!"

Iryo jambo ryateye umunezero udasanzwe mu bari muri uwo mugambwe. Zimwe mu nyoni zica zigenda zihuta ubwo nyene: Inzoka imwe y’umusaza yatanguye kwizingirako yitonze cane, ivuga iti: “Vy’ukuri ntegerezwa kuba ndiko ndashika i muhira; maze Kanari ihamagara abana bayo mw’ijwi rijugumira iti: «Nimugende, bakunzi banje, igihe kirageze ko mwese mwari mu buriri!» Bose baragenda kubera imvo zitandukanye, maze Alice ntiyatevye gusigara wenyene.

“Iyaba ntavuga ivya Dina!” aca yibwira mw’ijwi ry’agahinda. "Nta n'umwe asa n'uwumukunda, hano hasi, kandi ndazi neza ko ari akayabu keza kuruta ayandi yose kw'isi! Ewe, mukunzi wanje Dina! Nibaza ko nzosubira kukubona!" Kandi ng’aho Alice umworo yaciye asubira kurira, kuko yumva ari wenyene cane kandi afise umutima mubi. Ariko rero, mu gihe gitoyi, yongera kwumva ibirenge bigenda bitera kure, aca araba hejuru afise umunezero mwinshi, igice kimwe yizigiye ko Imbeba yahinduye ivyiyumviro, kandi iriko iragaruka kugira ngo irangize inkuru yiwe.
