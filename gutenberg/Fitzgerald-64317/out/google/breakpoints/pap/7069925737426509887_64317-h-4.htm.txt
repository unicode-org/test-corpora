Mi| |no| |por| |a| |drumi| |henter| |anochi|;
un| |foghorn| |tabata| |gemí| |sin| |stòp| |riba| |e| |Zonido|,| |i| |mi| |tabata| |tira| |mitar| |malu| |entre| |realidat| |grotesko| |i| |soñonan| |salbahe| |i| |spantoso|.
Den| |direkshon| |di| |mainta| |mi| |a| |tende| |un| |taxi| |subi| |e| |kaminda| |di| |Gatsby|,| |i| |mesora| |mi| |a| |bula| |for| |di| |kama| |i| |a| |kuminsá| |bisti|—|mi| |a| |sinti| |ku| |mi| |tabatin| |algu| |di| |bis’é|,| |algu| |pa| |atvert’é| |di| |dje|,| |i| |mainta| |lo| |tabata| |muchu| |lat|.
Krusando| |su| |yerba|,| |mi| |a| |mira| |ku| |su| |porta| |dilanti| |tabata| |habrí| |ainda| |i| |e| |tabata| |apoyá| |riba| |un| |mesa| |den| |e| |sala|,| |pisá| |ku| |desepshon| |òf| |soño|.
“|Nada| |no| |a| |pasa|,|”| |el| |a| |bisa| |desbaratá|.
“|Mi| |a| |warda|,| |i| |mas| |o| |ménos| |kuater’or| |ela| |bin| |na| |bentana| |i| |a| |para| |einan| |pa| |un| |minüt| |i| |despues| |a| |paga| |e| |lus|.|”
Su| |kas| |nunka| |no| |a| |parse| |asina| |grandi| |pa| |mi| |manera| |e| |tabata| |e| |anochi| |ei| |ora| |nos| |a| |buska| |den| |e| |kambernan| |grandi| |pa| |sigaria|.
Nos| |a| |pusha| |kortinanan| |ku| |tabata| |manera| |pabellon| |un| |banda|,| |i| |a| |sinti| |riba| |innumerabel| |pia| |di| |muraya| |skur| |pa| |swichnan| |di| |lus| |eléktriko|—|un| |biaha| |mi| |a| |kai| |ku| |un| |tipo| |di| |spuit| |riba| |e| |yabinan| |di| |un| |piano| |fantasmagórico|.
Tabatin| |un| |kantidat| |ineksplikabel| |di| |stòf| |tur| |kaminda|,| |i| |e| |kambernan| |tabata| |mushi|,| |komo| |si| |fuera| |nan| |no| |a| |wòrdu| |airu| |pa| |hopi| |dia|.
Mi| |a| |haña| |e| |humidor| |riba| |un| |mesa| |deskonosí|,| |ku| |dos| |sigaria| |seku| |i| |bashi| |aden|.
Habri| |e| |bentananan| |franses| |di| |e| |sala|,| |nos| |a| |sinta| |huma| |den| |skuridat|.
“|Bo| |mester| |bai|,|”| |mi| |a| |bisa|.
“|Ta| |basta| |sigur| |ku| |nan| |lo| |rastreá| |bo| |outo|.|”
“|Bai| |awor|,| |deporte| |bieu|?|”
“|Bai| |Atlantic| |City| |pa| |un| |siman|,| |òf| |bai| |Montreal|.|”
E| |no| |ta| |konsiderá| |esaki|.
E| |no| |por| |a| |bandoná| |Daisy| |te| |ora| |e| |tabata| |sa| |kiko| |e| |tabata| |bai| |hasi|.
E| |tabata| |gara| |na| |algun| |último| |speransa| |i| |mi| |no| |por| |a| |soportá| |pa| |sagudí| |e| |liber|.
Tabata| |e| |anochi| |aki| |ku| |el| |a| |konta| |mi| |e| |historia| |straño| |di| |su| |hubentut| |ku| |Dan| |Cody|—|a| |konta| |mi| |esaki| |pasobra| |“|Jay| |Gatsby|”| |a| |kibra| |manera| |glas| |kontra| |e| |malisia| |duru| |di| |Tom|,| |i| |e| |ekstravagansa| |sekreto| |largu| |a| |wòrdu| |hunga|.
Mi| |ta| |kere| |ku| |e| |lo| |a| |rekonosé| |kualke| |kos| |awor|,| |sin| |reserva|,| |pero| |e| |tabata| |ke| |papia| |tokante| |Daisy|.
E| |tabata| |e| |promé| |mucha| |muhé| |“|simpatiko|”| |ku| |el| |a| |yega| |di| |konosé|.
Den| |vários| |kapasidat| |no| |revelá| |el| |a| |yega| |di| |bin| |den| |kontakto| |ku| |hendenan| |asina|,| |pero| |semper| |ku| |waya| |di| |prikichi| |indisernibel| |entre| |nan|.
El| |a| |hañ’é| |emoshonantemente| |deseabel|.
El| |a| |bai| |su| |kas|,| |na| |promé| |instante| |ku| |otro| |ofisialnan| |di| |Camp| |Taylor|,| |despues| |su| |so|.
E| |tabata| |asombr’é|—|nunka| |promé| |e| |no| |tabata| |den| |un| |kas| |asina| |bunita|.
Pero| |loke| |a| |dun’é| |un| |aire| |di| |intensidat| |sin| |rosea|,| |tabata| |ku| |Daisy| |tabata| |biba| |einan|—|e| |tabata| |un| |kos| |mes| |kasual| |p’e| |ku| |su| |tènt| |pafó| |na| |kampamentu| |tabata| |p’e|.
Tabatin| |un| |misterio| |madurá| |tokante| |di| |dje|,| |un| |hint| |di| |kambernan| |ariba| |mas| |bunita| |i| |fresku| |ku| |otro| |kambernan|,| |di| |aktividatnan| |gay| |i| |radiante| |ku| |tabata| |tumando| |lugá| |den| |su| |koredónan|,| |i| |di| |romansenan| |ku| |no| |tabata| |moho| |i| |poné| |kaba| |den| |lavender| |pero| |fresku| |i| |hala| |rosea| |i| |holor| |di| |e| |outonan| |di| |moto| |briante| |di| |e| |aña| |aki| |i| |di| |bailarinnan| |di| |flor| |ku| |tabata| |yená| |ku| |flor|.
E| |la| |emoshon’é| |tambe| |ku| |hopi| |hòmber| |a| |stima| |Daisy| |kaba|—|esaki| |a| |oumentá| |su| |balor| |den| |su| |bista|.
E| |tabata| |sinti| |nan| |presensia| |tur| |kaminda| |rònt| |di| |e| |kas|,| |penetrando| |e| |aire| |ku| |e| |sombranan| |i| |e| |echonan| |di| |emoshonnan| |vibrante| |ainda|.
Pero| |e| |tabata| |sa| |cu| |e| |tabata| |den| |cas| |di| |Daisy| |pa| |un| |accidente| |colosal|.
Maske| |kon| |glorioso| |su| |futuro| |komo| |Jay| |Gatsby| |por| |ta|,| |e| |tabata| |aktualmente| |un| |hóben| |sin| |sèn| |sin| |pasado|,| |i| |na| |kualke| |momentu| |e| |mantel| |invisibel| |di| |su| |unifòrm| |por| |a| |slip| |for| |di| |su| |skoudernan|.
Pues| |el| |a| |saca| |lo| |maximo| |for| |di| |su| |tempo|.
El| |a| |tuma| |loke| |e| |por| |a| |haña|,| |ku| |gana| |i| |sin| |eskrúpulo|—|eventualmente| |el| |a| |tuma| |Daisy| |un| |anochi| |di| |òktober| |ainda|,| |a| |tum’é| |pasobra| |e| |no| |tabatin| |derecho| |real| |di| |mishi| |ku| |su| |man|.
E| |por| |a| |despresiá| |su| |mes|,| |pasobra| |sigur| |el| |a| |tum’é| |bou| |di| |pretenshonnan| |falsu|.
Mi| |no| |ke| |men| |ku| |el| |a| |interkambiá| |su| |miónes| |fantasma|,| |pero| |el| |a| |duna| |Daisy| |un| |sentido| |di| |seguridat| |deliberadamente|;
el| |a| |lag’é| |kere| |ku| |e| |tabata| |un| |persona| |di| |kasi| |e| |mesun| |strata| |ku| |e| |mes|—|ku| |e| |tabata| |kompletamente| |kapas| |pa| |kuid’é|.
De| |echo|,| |e| |no| |tabatin| |e| |fasilidatnan| |ei|—|e| |no| |tabatin| |un| |famia| |kómodo| |pará| |su| |tras|,| |i| |e| |tabata| |responsabel| |na| |e| |kapricho| |di| |un| |gobièrnu| |impersonal| |pa| |wòrdu| |suplá| |na| |kualke| |parti| |di| |mundu|.
Pero| |e| |no| |a| |menosprecia| |su| |mes| |y| |no| |a| |resulta| |manera| |el| |a| |imagina|.
E| |tabatin| |intenshon|,| |probablemente|,| |di| |tuma| |loke| |e| |por| |i| |bai|—|pero| |awor| |el| |a| |haña| |ku| |el| |a| |komprometé| |su| |mes| |na| |siguimentu| |di| |un| |graal|.
E| |tabata| |sa| |ku| |Daisy| |tabata| |ekstraordinario|,| |pero| |e| |no| |a| |realisá| |kon| |ekstraordinario| |un| |mucha| |muhé| |“|simpatiko|”| |por| |ta|.
El’a| |disparsé| |den| |su| |kas| |riku|,| |den| |su| |bida| |riku| |i| |yen|,| |lagando| |Gatsby|—|nada|.
E| |tabata| |sintié| |kasá| |kuné|,| |esei| |tabata| |tur|.
Ora| |nan| |a| |topa| |atrobe|,| |dos| |dia| |despues|,| |tabata| |Gatsby| |ku| |tabata| |sin| |rosea|,| |ku| |tabata|,| |di| |algun| |manera|,| |traishoná|.
Su| |veranda| |tabata| |briante| |ku| |e| |luho| |kumprá| |di| |briyo| |di| |strea|;
e| |mimbre| |di| |e| |sofa| |a| |grita| |na| |moda| |ora| |ela| |bira| |den| |su| |direkshon| |i| |el| |a| |sunchi| |su| |boka| |kurioso| |i| |bunita|.
El| |a| |haña| |un| |verkoud|,| |i| |esei| |a| |hasi| |su| |bos| |mas| |skur| |i| |mas| |encantador| |ku| |nunka|,| |i| |Gatsby| |tabata| |abrumadoramente| |konsiente| |di| |e| |hubentut| |i| |misterio| |ku| |rikesa| |ta| |enkarselá| |i| |konserbá|,| |di| |e| |freskura| |di| |hopi| |paña|,| |i| |di| |Daisy|,| |briante| |manera| |plata|,| |sigur| |i| |orguyoso| |riba| |e| |lucha| |kayente| |di| |e| |pobernan|.
“|Mi| |no| |por| |deskribí| |na| |bo| |kon| |sorprendí| |mi| |tabata| |di| |haña| |sa| |ku| |mi| |tabata| |stim’é|,| |deporte| |bieu|.| |Mi| |a| |asta| |spera| |pa| |un| |ratu| |ku| |e| |lo| |a| |benta| |mi| |abou|,| |pero| |e| |no| |a| |hasié|,| |pasobra| |e| |tabata| |namorá| |di| |mi| |tambe|.| |E| |tabata| |kere| |ku| |mi| |tabata| |sa| |hopi| |pasobra| |mi| |tabata| |sa| |kosnan| |diferente| |for| |di| |dje|...| |Wel|,| |einan| |mi| |tabata|,| |leu| |for| |di| |mi| |ambishonnan|,| |birando| |mas| |profundo| |den| |amor| |kada| |minüt|,| |i| |diripiente| |Loke| |mi| |no| |a| |hasi|.
e| |uso| |di| |hasi| |kosnan| |grandi| |si| |mi| |por| |tabatin| |un| |mihó| |tempu| |bisando| |e| |kiko| |mi| |tabata| |bai| |hasi|?|”
Riba| |e| |último| |atardi| |promé| |ku| |el| |a| |bai| |den| |eksterior|,| |el| |a| |sinta| |ku| |Daisy| |den| |su| |brasanan| |pa| |un| |tempu| |largu| |i| |silensioso|.
Tabata| |un| |dia| |friu| |di| |ougùstùs|,| |ku| |kandela| |den| |e| |kamber| |i| |su| |wowonan| |kòrá|.
De| |bes| |en| |kuando| |e| |tabata| |move| |i| |e| |tabata| |kambia| |su| |brasa| |un| |poko|,| |i| |un| |biaha| |e| |tabata| |sunchi| |su| |kabei| |skur| |ku| |tabata| |bria|.
E| |atardi| |a| |hasi| |nan| |trankil| |pa| |un| |ratu|,| |komo| |si| |fuera| |pa| |duna| |nan| |un| |rekuerdo| |profundo| |pa| |e| |despedida| |largu| |ku| |e| |siguiente| |dia| |a| |primintí|.
Nunka| |nan| |no| |tabata| |mas| |serka| |den| |nan| |luna| |di| |amor|,| |ni| |a| |komuniká| |mas| |profundamente| |ku| |otro|,| |ku| |ora| |e| |tabata| |frega| |lipnan| |silensioso| |kontra| |e| |skouder| |di| |su| |mantel| |òf| |ora| |e| |tabata| |mishi| |ku| |e| |punta| |di| |su| |dedenan|,| |suavemente|,| |komo| |si| |fuera| |e| |tabata| |na| |soño|.
El| |a| |hasi| |ekstraordinariamente| |bon| |den| |guera|.
E| |tabata| |un| |kapitan| |promé| |ku| |el| |a| |bai| |dilanti|,| |i| |despues| |di| |e| |batayanan| |di| |Argonne| |el| |a| |haña| |su| |mayoria| |i| |e| |mando| |di| |e| |mitrailleurnan| |divishonal|.
Despues| |di| |e| |armistisio| |el| |a| |purba| |frenétikamente| |pa| |yega| |kas|,| |pero| |algun| |komplikashon| |òf| |mal| |komprondementu| |a| |mand’é| |Oxford| |en| |bes| |di| |esei|.
E| |tabata| |preokupá| |awor|—|tabatin| |un| |kalidat| |di| |desesperashon| |nervioso| |den| |e| |kartanan| |di| |Daisy|.
E| |no| |a| |mira| |dikon| |e| |no| |por| |a| |bini|.
E| |tabata| |sinti| |e| |preshon| |di| |e| |mundu| |pafó|,| |i| |e| |tabata| |ke| |mir’é| |i| |sinti| |su| |presensia| |banda| |di| |dje| |i| |ta| |trankilisá| |ku| |e| |tabata| |hasiendo| |e| |kos| |korekto| |al| |fin| |i| |al| |kabo|.
Pasobra| |Daisy| |tabata| |yòn| |i| |su| |mundu| |artifisial| |tabata| |redolente| |di| |orkidia| |i| |snobismo| |agradabel| |i| |alegre| |i| |orkestanan| |ku| |a| |pone| |e| |ritmo| |di| |aña|,| |resumiendo| |e| |tristesa| |i| |sugestividat| |di| |bida| |den| |tononan| |nobo|.
Henter| |anochi| |e| |saksofonnan| |tabata| |lamentá| |e| |komentario| |sin| |speransa| |di| |e| |“|Beale| |Street| |Blues|”| |miéntras| |ku| |shen| |par| |di| |slipper| |di| |oro| |i| |plata| |tabata| |shusha| |e| |stòf| |briante|.
Na| |e| |ora| |di| |te| |gris| |semper| |tabatin| |kambernan| |ku| |tabata| |bati| |sin| |stòp| |ku| |e| |keintura| |abou| |i| |dushi| |aki|,| |miéntras| |ku| |karanan| |fresku| |tabata| |drif| |aki| |aya| |manera| |pétalonan| |di| |rosa| |supla| |pa| |e| |kachunan| |tristu| |rònt| |di| |e| |vloer|.
Atraves| |di| |e| |universo| |di| |krepuskulo| |aki| |Daisy| |a| |kuminsá| |move| |atrobe| |ku| |e| |temporada|;
diripiente| |e| |tabata| |bolbe| |tene| |mei| |dozijn| |di| |fecha| |pa| |dia| |ku| |mei| |dozijn| |di| |hòmber|,| |i| |drumi| |na| |mainta| |ku| |e| |kralchi| |i| |chiffon| |di| |un| |bisti| |di| |anochi| |enredá| |entre| |orkidianan| |muriendo| |riba| |vloer| |banda| |di| |su| |kama|.
I| |tur| |e| |tempu| |algu| |den| |dje| |tabata| |yora| |pa| |un| |desishon|.
E| |tabata| |ke| |pa| |su| |bida| |wòrdu| |formá| |awor|,| |mesora|—|i| |e| |desishon| |mester| |wòrdu| |tumá| |pa| |algun| |forsa|—|di| |amor|,| |di| |plaka|,| |di| |praktikidat| |inkuestionabel|—|ku| |tabata| |serka| |di| |man|.
E| |forsa| |ei| |a| |tuma| |forma| |meimei| |di| |primavera| |ku| |e| |yegada| |di| |Tom| |Buchanan|.
Tabatin| |un| |voluminoso| |saludabel| |tokante| |su| |persona| |i| |su| |posishon|,| |i| |Daisy| |tabata| |hala| |rosea|.
Sin| |duda| |tabatin| |un| |sierto| |lucha| |i| |un| |sierto| |alivio|.
E| |karta| |a| |yega| |serka| |Gatsby| |miéntras| |e| |tabata| |na| |Oxford| |ainda|.
Tabata| |mainta| |awor| |na| |Long| |Island| |i| |nos| |a| |bai| |habri| |e| |restu| |di| |e| |bentananan| |abou|,| |yenando| |e| |kas| |ku| |lus| |ku| |ta| |bira| |gris|,| |ku| |ta| |bira| |oro|.
E| |sombra| |di| |un| |palu| |a| |kai| |abruptamente| |riba| |e| |rosea| |i| |parhanan| |fantasma| |a| |kuminsá| |kanta| |meimei| |di| |e| |blachinan| |blou|.
Tabatin| |un| |moveshon| |slow| |i| |agradabel| |den| |laira|,| |apénas| |un| |bientu|,| |primintiendo| |un| |dia| |fresku| |i| |dushi|.
“|Mi| |no| |ta| |kere| |ku| |e| |nunka| |a| |stim’é|.|”
Gatsby| |a| |bira| |for| |di| |un| |bentana| |i| |a| |wak| |mi| |ku| |un| |reto|.
“|Bo| |mester| |kòrda|,| |deporte| |bieu|,| |e| |tabata| |hopi| |emoshoná| |awe| |tardi|.| |El| |a| |bis’é| |e| |kosnan| |ei| |na| |un| |manera| |ku| |a| |spant’é|—|esei| |a| |hasié| |parse| |manera| |mi| |tabata| |un| |tipo| |di| |skerpi| |barata|.| |I| |e| |resultado| |tabata| |ku| |kasi| |e| |no| |tabata| |sa| |kiko| |e| |tabata| |bisa|.|”
El| |a| |sinta| |tristo|.
“|Naturalmente| |e| |por| |a| |stim’é| |djis| |pa| |un| |minüt|,| |ora| |nan| |a| |kasa| |pa| |promé| |biaha|—|i| |a| |stima| |mi| |mas| |asta| |e| |tempu| |ei|,| |bo| |ta| |mira|?|”
Diripiente| |el| |a| |sali| |cu| |un| |remarca| |curioso|.
“|En| |todo| |kaso|,|”| |el| |a| |bisa|,| |“|e| |tabata| |djis| |personal|.|”
Kiko| |bo| |por| |a| |hasi| |di| |esei|,| |ku| |eksepshon| |di| |sospechá| |algun| |intensidat| |den| |su| |konsepshon| |di| |e| |asuntu| |ku| |no| |por| |a| |wòrdu| |midi|?
El| |a| |regresá| |for| |di| |Fransia| |ora| |Tom| |i| |Daisy| |tabata| |riba| |nan| |biahe| |di| |kasamentu| |ainda|,| |i| |a| |hasi| |un| |biahe| |miserabel| |pero| |iresistibel| |pa| |Louisville| |riba| |e| |último| |di| |su| |pago| |di| |ehérsito|.
El| |a| |keda| |einan| |un| |siman|,| |kanando| |den| |e| |kayanan| |kaminda| |nan| |pasonan| |a| |klik| |huntu| |durante| |e| |anochi| |di| |novèmber| |i| |rebishitando| |e| |lugánan| |for| |di| |kaminda| |pa| |kua| |nan| |a| |kore| |den| |su| |outo| |blanku|.
Meskos| |ku| |Daisy| |su| |kas| |semper| |a| |parse| |mas| |misterioso| |i| |gay| |ku| |otro| |kasnan|,| |asina| |su| |idea| |di| |e| |stat| |mes|,| |ounke| |e| |tabata| |bai| |for| |di| |dje|,| |tabata| |pervadí| |ku| |un| |beyesa| |melankóliko|.
El| |a| |sali| |sintiendo| |ku| |si| |el| |a| |buska| |mas| |duru|,| |e| |por| |a| |hañ’é|—|ku| |e| |tabata| |lag’é| |atras|.
E| |day|-|coach|—|e| |tabata| |sin| |sèn| |awor|—|tabata| |kayente|.
