Nakua| |niya| |siyang| |nakakapukaw| |na| |kawiliwili|.
Nagduman| |siya| |sa| |saiyang| |harong|,| |sa| |primero| |kaiba| |an| |ibang| |mga| |opisyales| |hale| |sa| |Camp| |Taylor|,| |dangan| |solo|.
Napangalas| |sia|—|dai| |pa| |sia| |noarin| |man| |nakaduman| |sa| |siring| |kagayon| |na| |harong|.
Alagad| |an| |nagtao| |kaini| |nin| |aire| |nin| |daing| |hinangos| |na| |intensidad|,| |iyo| |na| |si| |Daisy| |nag|-|eerok| |duman|—|iyan| |kaswal| |na| |bagay| |para| |saiya| |arog| |kan| |saiyang| |tolda| |sa| |luwas| |kan| |kampo| |para| |saiya|.
Igwa| |nin| |sarong| |hinog| |na| |misteryo| |manongod| |kaini|,| |sarong| |paisi| |nin| |mga| |kwarto| |sa| |itaas| |na| |mas| |magayon| |asin| |malipot| |kisa| |ibang| |mga| |kwarto|,| |nin| |mga| |bakla| |asin| |nagliliwanag| |na| |mga| |aktibidad| |na| |nangyayari| |sa| |mga| |pasilyo| |kaini|,| |asin| |nin| |mga| |romansa| |na| |bakong| |musty| |asin| |nakabugtak| |na| |sa| |lavender| |alagad| |presko| |asin| |naghahangos| |asin| |nahamot| |kan| |mga| |nagkikinang| |na| |mga| |motorcar| |asin| |mga| |parabayle| |nin| |mga| |burak| |ngonian| |na| |taon|.
Naugma| |man| |siya| |na| |kadakol| |na| |lalaki| |an| |namomoot| |na| |ki| |Daisy|—|nagdagdag| |ini| |kan| |saiyang| |halaga| |sa| |saiyang| |mga| |mata|.
Namatian| |niya| |an| |saindang| |presensya| |sa| |bilog| |na| |harong|,| |na| |naglalakop| |sa| |aire| |na| |may| |mga| |anino| |asin| |mga| |echo| |kan| |mga| |buhay| |pa| |man| |giraray| |na| |emosyon|.
Alagad| |aram| |niya| |na| |yaon| |siya| |sa| |harong| |ni| |Daisy| |huli| |sa| |sarong| |dakulang| |aksidente|.
Gurano| |man| |kamurawayan| |an| |saiyang| |futuro| |bilang| |Jay| |Gatsby|,| |siya| |sa| |presente| |sarong| |daing| |kwarta| |na| |hoben| |na| |lalaki| |na| |mayong| |nakaagi|,| |asin| |sa| |anuman| |na| |momento| |an| |dai| |nahihiling| |na| |kapa| |kan| |saiyang| |uniporme| |tibaad| |magluwas| |sa| |saiyang| |mga| |abaga|.
Kaya| |ginamit| |nia| |sa| |pinakamarahay| |na| |paagi| |an| |saiyang| |panahon|.
Kinua| |niya| |an| |saiyang| |makukua|,| |na| |may| |pagmawot| |asin| |daing| |prinsipyo|—|sa| |kahurihurihi| |kinua| |niya| |si| |Daisy| |sarong| |banggi| |pa| |man| |giraray| |kan| |Oktubre|,| |kinua| |siya| |huli| |ta| |mayo| |siyang| |tunay| |na| |deretso| |na| |dutaan| |an| |saiyang| |kamot|.
Tibaad| |hinamak| |nia| |an| |saiyang| |sadiri|,| |huli| |ta| |siertong| |kinua| |nia| |sia| |sa| |irarom| |nin| |falsong| |mga| |pagsaginsagin|.
Dai| |ko| |boot| |sabihon| |na| |ipinabakal| |niya| |an| |saiyang| |mga| |milyon|-|milyon| |na| |phantom|,| |alagad| |tuyong| |tinawan| |niya| |si| |Daisy| |nin| |pakamate| |nin| |seguridad|;

Sa| |katunayan|,| |mayo| |siyang| |siring| |na| |mga| |pasilidad|—|mayo| |siyang| |komportableng| |pamilya| |na| |nagtitindog| |sa| |likod| |niya|,| |asin| |siya| |may| |paninimbagan| |sa| |kapritso| |nin| |sarong| |impersonal| |na| |gobyerno| |na| |mapalid| |saen| |man| |sa| |kinaban|.
Alagad| |dai| |niya| |pig|-|ulog|-|ulog| |an| |saiyang| |sadiri| |asin| |dai| |man| |nangyari| |an| |saiyang| |iniimahinar|.
Siya| |nagtuyo|,| |siguro|,| |na| |kuanon| |an| |saiyang| |kaya| |asin| |magduman|—|alagad| |ngonyan| |nanompongan| |niya| |na| |siya| |nagkomiter| |kan| |saiyang| |sadiri| |sa| |pagsunod| |sa| |sarong| |grail|.
Aram| |niya| |na| |pambihira| |si| |Daisy|,| |alagad| |dai| |niya| |narealisar| |kun| |gurano| |pambihira| |an| |sarong| |“|magayon|”| |na| |daragita|.
Nawara| |siya| |sa| |saiyang| |mayaman| |na| |harong|,| |sa| |saiyang| |mayaman|,| |bilog| |na| |buhay|,| |na| |binabayaan| |si| |Gatsby|—|mayo|.

Nagkaigwa| |siya| |nin| |sipon|,| |asin| |ginibo| |kaini| |an| |saiyang| |boses| |na| |mas| |makusog| |asin| |mas| |nakakaakit| |kisa| |kasuarin| |man|,| |asin| |si| |Gatsby| |labi|-|labi| |an| |pagkaaram| |kan| |pagkahoben| |asin| |misteryo| |na| |pigkukulong| |asin| |pigpepreserbar| |kan| |kayamanan|,| |kan| |pagkapresko| |kan| |kadakol| |na| |mga| |gubing|,| |asin| |kan| |Daisy|,| |na| |nagkikinang| |na| |garo| |pirak|,| |ligtas| |asin| |orgulyoso| |sa| |ibabaw| |kan| |mainit| |na| |mga| |pakikipaglaban| |kan| |mga| |dukha|.
“|Dai| |ko| |mailadawan| |saimo| |kun| |gurano| |ako| |nabigla| |kan| |maaraman| |ko| |na| |namomotan| |ko| |siya|,| |lumang| |isports|.| |Naglaom| |pa| |ngani| |ako| |sa| |laog| |nin| |kadikit| |na| |panahon| |na| |itapok| |niya| |ako|,| |alagad| |dai| |niya| |ginibo|,| |huli| |ta| |namomotan| |man| |niya| |ako|.| |Naghona| |siya| |na| |dakul| |akong| |aram| |huli| |ta| |iba|-|iba| |an| |aram| |ko| |saiya|...| |Bueno|,| |yaon| |ako| |duman|,| |harayo| |sa| |sakong| |mga| |ambisyon|,| |nagigin| |mas| |hararom| |an| |pagkamoot| |sa| |kada| |minuto|,| |asin| |bigla| |na| |sana|.

Kan| |huring| |hapon| |bago| |siya| |nagduman| |sa| |ibang| |nasyon|,| |nagtukaw| |siya| |kaiba| |si| |Daisy| |sa| |saiyang| |mga| |takyag| |sa| |halawig|,| |silensyosong| |panahon|.

Paminsan|-|minsan| |siya| |naghihiro| |asin| |binabago| |niya| |an| |saiyang| |takyag| |nin| |dikit|,| |asin| |sarong| |beses| |hinadukan| |niya| |an| |saiyang| |maitom| |na| |nagkikinang| |na| |buhok|.
An| |hapon| |an| |nagpangyari| |sainda| |na| |magin| |trangkilo| |sa| |kadikit| |na| |panahon|,| |na| |garo| |baga| |nagtatao| |sainda| |nin| |hararom| |na| |memorya| |para| |sa| |halawig| |na| |pagbubulag| |na| |ipinanuga| |kan| |masunod| |na| |aldaw|.
