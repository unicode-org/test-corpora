Ngang

Non ai kewe ier mi kukkun me ua kon apwangapwang semei we a ngeniei ekkoch aurour minne ua kan siwini non ai ekiek seni ena fansoun.

“Iteiten fansoun ka mefi pwe kopwe esiita emon,” a ereniei, “kopwe chok chechemeni pwe meinisin aramas non ei fonufan rese angei ekkewe feioch ka fen angei.”

Ese chiwen apasa och mettoch, nge aua fen fokkun porous fengen non ew napanap mi monomon, me ua weweiti pwe a weweiti ew watten mettoch nap seni ena. Ren ei popun, ua kan mochen tumunu meinisin ekkewe kapung, ew napanap mi suuki chommong napanap mi mochen sinei ngeniei me a pwan fori ai upwe ewe chon riaffou ren esapw ekkoch veteran bores. Ewe ekiek ese pwung a mwitir kuna me apacha ngeni ei napanap nupwen a pwa non emon aramas mi pwung, iwe ina usun a fis pwe non college ra tipi ngeniei ese pung pwe ngang emon chon politics, pokiten ua sinei usun ekkewe netipengaw mi monomon an mwan mi mwacho, rese sinei. Lape ngeni ekkewe porous mi monomon rese kutta —fan chommong ua fen fori pwe ua annut, osukosuk, are ew levity mi oput nupwen ua miritiiti ren ekkoch esisin ese tongeni mwaken pwe ew pwarata mi monomon a chechech won ewe puun; pun ekkewe pwarata mi fiti tong an ekkewe anuon, are ekkewe kapas ra pwarata, ra kan plagiarism me ra ataieno ren ekkewe osukosuk mi fatafatoch. Ach sipwe tumunu ekkewe kapung ina ew mettoch mi wor apinukunuk ese much. Ua chuen ekis nuokus ai upwe poutano och mettoch ika upwe monuki pwe, usun semei we a apasa non tipemwaramwar, me ua tipemwaramwar ne apasa sefani, ew memefin ekkewe nongonongen decency a fis non napanap ese fitikoko nupwen ua uputiw.

Iwe, mwirin ai mwareiti ei napanapen ai mosonoson, ua feito ngeni ewe apasa pwe mi wor eu nesoponon. Foffor epwe tongeni nongonong won ewe fau mi pochokun ika ekkewe chuk mi pwichikar, nge mwirin ew fansoun use ekieki met a nongonong won. Nupwen ua niwin seni ewe East non ewe autumn a no ua mefi pwe ua mochen ewe fonufan epwe nom non uniform me non ew sokkun tumunun foffor feinfeino chok; Ua mochen pwe esap chuen wor ekkewe sai mi fitikoko ren ekkewe minen apwapwa mi auchea ngeni letipen aramas. Gatsby chok, ewe mwan a ngeni itan ei puk, a ngaseno seni ai ekiek —Gatsby, ewe a wewe ngeni mettoch meinisin minne mi wor ai turunufas ese wor niwinin. Ika pwe napanap ina eu mwichen foffor mi sopwoch ese much, iwe, a wor och mettoch mi amwarar ussun i, ekkoch ra alapalo ar mefi ekkewe pwonen manau, ussun ita nge a riri ngeni eu me nein ekkena misineri mi weires mi makkei ekkewe chechchechin fonu engol ngerou mwail towauan. Ei ponueni ese wor met epwe fori ngeni ena memef mi uren fitikoko minne mi itefouno fan iten ewe “temperament mi forata” —ina ew niffang mi amwarar ren apinukunuk, ew ammonata ren tong usun met use mwo kuna non emon aramas me ese tufich pwe upwe kuna sefan. Aapw —Gatsby a fis meinisin non ewe nesoponon; ina met a atai Gatsby, met sokkun pwichikar mi ngaw a tori ewe fitikoko an kewe tan minne a ekis awesi ai mochen sinei usun ekkewe netipengaw mi abortion me ekkewe pwapwa mi mwochomwoch an aramas.

Ai famini ra itefouno, aramas mi pisekisek non ei teninimwen Middle Western non ukukun unungat tappin aramas. Ekkewe Carraways ir ew mettochun ew einang, me a wor ew uruwo pwe am aua feito seni ekkewe Dukes seni Buccleuch, nge ewe wesewesen chon poputani ai we line ina pwin semei we chinnap, ewe a feito ikei non fik me ew, a tinato emon chon siwini ngeni ewe maunen fonufan, me a poputa ewe angangen kamo pisekin angang won ewe car ngeni semei we ikenai.

Use mwo kuna ei anuon-uncle, nge ua ekieki pwe upwe usun chok i —ren ai upwe apasa ewe sasing mi kon pochokun mi nom non ofesin semei we. Ua sochungio seni New Haven lon 1915, eu chok kinikinin eu ier mwirin semei we, me ekis mwirin ua fiti ena saifetal mi mwittir fis seni ekkewe chon Teuton mi iteni ewe Maun mi Lapalap. Ua fokkun pwapwaiti ewe koun-raid pwe ua liwinto nge use kinamwe. Ese nifinifin ika epwe ewe nenien pwichikar non fonufan, ewe Middle West iei a usun ewe nenien fitikoko non ewe fonufan —iwe ua finata ai upwe feino East me kaeo ewe angangen bond. Meinisin ekkewe ua sinei ra nom non ewe businessin bond, ina minne ua ekieki pwe epwe tongeni anisi pwan emon mwan mi chok pupunu. Meinisin nei kewe aunt me uncle ra kapas usun usun ita ra finata ew sukun prep fan itei, me soposopono ra apasa, “Pwata —e-es,” ren meser mi fokkun netipengaw, me tipemwaramwar. Papa a tipeew ne moniei ren ew ier, me mwirin sokopaten fansoun ua feito East, feinfeino chok, ua ekieki, non ewe fansoun pwichikar non rue me ru.

Ewe mettoch mi auchea pwe aupwe kutta rum non ewe teninimw, nge ina ew fansoun pwichikar, me ua keran chok tou seni ew fonu mi wor watten puun me ira mi chiechioch, ina minne nupwen emon anuon non ewe ofes a pesei pwe aupwe angei ew imw fengen non ew teninimw mi kan sai ngeni angang, a usun ew ekiek mi nap. A kuna ewe imw, ew imw mi for seni card mi uren fitikoko non ew maram, nge non ewe amuchunon minich ewe kompeni a annuku ngeni Washington, me ua chok feino ngeni ewe fonu. Mi wor emon ai kukkun man —ese nifinifin ua nom ren non ekkoch ran tori an a su —me emon Dodge mi chinnap me emon fefin seni Finland, ewe a fori ai we pet me a kuk mongon neanowas me a ngungures ren tipachemen Finland ngeni pwisin i won ewe stove mi fiti ekkei.

A chok monomon ren ew ran ika nap seni tori ew pwinin emon mwan, mi keran war nap seni ai, a eppetiei won ewe aan.

“Ifa usun om kopwe tori ewe sopw West Egg?” a eisini nge ese wor an aninis.

Ua ereni. Iwe nupwen ua fetan won use chiwen chok pwisin ngang. Ngang emon chon emwen, emon chon kutta aan, emon chon akkomw nomw. A fen casual ngeniei ewe ngaseno seni ewe neni.

Iwe ina ren ewe saram me ekkewe watten puun mi mar won ekkewe ira, usun chok mettoch ra mar non ekkewe kachito mi mwitir, a wor ai ena nuku mi sinei pwe manau a poputa sefan ren ewe summer.

A wor chommong mettoch sipwe anneani, ren ew mettoch, me chommong mettoch mi murinno sipwe tongeni angei seni ewe asepwan mi kukkun mi awora ngasangas. Ua kamo engon me ruu puk usun ekkewe taropwen bank me credit me moni tonong, me ra uta won ai we chepel non pwechepwech me kolt usun minefoon moni seni ewe mint, ra pwon pwe repwe pwarata ekkewe monomon mi saramaram minne Midas me Morgan me Maecenas chok ra sinei. Iwe a wor ai ekiek mi tekia ai upwe anneani chommong pwan ekkoch puk lukun. Ua kon sinei usun puk non college —ew ier ua makkei ew mwichen porous mi fokkun auchea me fatafatoch fan iten ewe Yale News —me iei upwe niwinsefaniti ekkena sokkun mettoch meinisin non manauei me winiti sefan ewe mi kon kukkun seni meinisin sou sinenap, ewe “mwan mi uren fitikoko.” Ei esap chok ew epigram —manau a fokkun murino ne nenengeni seni ew chok winnach, mwirin meinisin.

A eu mettoch mi fis pwe upwe fen rent eu imw lon eu me lein ekkewe leeni mi sokkolo aucheaan lon North America. A nom won ena fonu pon mi uren fitikoko mi suki ngeni otiwen New York —me ikewe mi wor, me nein ekkewe ekkoch mettoch mi amwarar, ruu sokkun fonu mi sokkono. Ruwe mwail seni ewe teninimw ew pekin egg mi watte, mi wewe chok non napanaper me ra aimufesen chok ren ew nenien konik mi uren tong, ra suki ngeni ewe nenien konik mi salt mi kon itefouno non ewe kinikinin Western, ewe watten nenien konik mi uren konik non Long Island Sound. Esap ir oval mi unusoch —usun chok ewe egg non ewe porausen Columbus, ir me ruemon ra kkuf non ewe nenien chufengen —nge ar ririfengen non pekin inis epwe ew popun amwarar ese much ngeni ekkewe gulls mi fitikoko won. Ngeni ekkewe rese wor wing ew mettoch mi kon amwarar ina ar rese wewefengen non iteiten kinikin tiwenon chok napanaper me watten.

Ua nom non West Egg, ewe —ewer, ewe ese kon itefouno me nein ekkewe ruemon, ina mwo ika iei ew tag mi kon monomon ne pwarata ewe sokofesen mi amwarar me esapw ekis ngaw nefinen ir. Imwei we a nom non ewe nepoputan ewe egg, ukukun fitepuku yard seni ewe Sound, me a suki nefinen ruu watten neni mi rent ren engon me ru ika engon me nimu ngerou non ew fansoun. Ewe emon won pechen a ew watten mettoch ren met chok napanap —a ew ennetin appiru ekkoch Hôtel de Ville non Normandy, ren ew imw tekia won ew pekin, a span minefo fan ew chuk mi pwechepwech seni ivy mi raw, me ew nenien konik mi for seni marble, me nap seni fopuku ekar fonu me atake. Ina ewe imwen Gatsby we. Are, upwe apasa, usun use sinei Mr. Gatsby, ina ew imw watte a nom ren emon mwan mi itefouno ena it. Pwisin imwei we a ew nenien mesen aramas, nge a ew kukkun nenien nengeni, me a fen fis, ina minne ua tongeni kuna ewe konik, ew kinikinin nenien nengeni ewe lawn an chon orui we, me ewe aururuun arap ngeni ekkewe millionaire —meinisin ren engon dollars non ew maram.

Non ewe epek ewe nenien kirekiroch ekkewe imw mi pwechepwech non East Egg mi famini ra saramaram won ewe konik, me uruon ewe summer a wesewesen poputa non ewe pwinin ua wawa sepenin ngeni ikewe pwe upwe mongon neanowas ren ekkewe Tom Buchanan. Daisy i ewe aruwen pwii we fan ew a tou, me ua fen sinei Tom non sukun tekia. Iwe mwirin chok ewe maun ua nom rer non ruu ran non Chicago.

Pwúlúwan we, me lein ekkewe sókkópaten mettóch a féri lón pekin inis, a fen emén me lein ekkewe mi fókkun péchékkúl seni meinisin mi fen fiti football lón New Haven —emén chón fiti ewe fénúfan lón eú napanap, emén me lein ekkewe mwán mi tori eú sókkun tufich mi chék chéúfetál lón ierin rue me eú pwe mettóch meinisin mwirin ra chék chéúfetál. An famini ra fokkun pisekisek —pwan mwo non college an ngaseno ren moni a ew mettoch mi ngaw —nge iei a fen tou seni Chicago me feito East non ew napanap mi nap an epwe angei om ngasangas: awewe chok, a fen uwei ew string ren ponien polo seni Lake Forest. A weires ai upwe mirititi pwe emén mwán lón pwisin ai we tappin aramas a pisekisek pwe epwe tongeni féri ena.

Pwata ra feito East use sinei. Ra fen nom non ew ier non France nge ese wor popun, iwe mwirin ra fitikoko ikei me ikewe rese kinamwe ikewe aramas ra fiti polo me ra pisekisek fengen. Iei ew mwokutukut mi nomotam, a apasa Daisy won ewe fon, nge use nuku —ese wor ai kuna non netipen Daisy, nge ua mefi pwe Tom epwe chok fitikoko feinfeino chok ne kutta, ekis netipengaw, ren ewe osukosuk mi amwarar ren ekkoch urumot rese tongeni niwinsefan.

Iwe ina usun a fis pwe non ew pwinin pwichikar mi pwichikar ua wawa sepenin ngeni East Egg pwe upwe kuna ruemon chienei kewe me nom minne use fokkun sinei. Imwer we a pwal mwo nge kon ling lap seni ai ekieki, eu imw mi pwapwa mi pwechepwech me pwechepwech lon ewe fansoun Georgia, mi nennengeni ewe matau. Ewe lawn a poputa non ewe sat me a su ngeni ewe asamwacho ren ew kukkunun mwail, a su won ekkewe sundial me ekkewe aanen brick me ekkewe atake mi ngetenget —soposopono nupwen a tori ewe imw a su won ewe pekin non ekkewe ira kreip mi saramaram usun ita seni ewe pochokunen an su. Ewe me mwen a tatakis ren ew linein ekkewe winnach seni France, a saramaram iei ren kolt mi saramaram me a suk ngeni ewe pwinin mi pwichikar, me Tom Buchanan non ufoufen wawa a uta me pechen kewe ra aimufesen won ewe asam me mwen.

A fen siwin seni an kewe ierin New Haven. Iei i emon mwan mi pochokun mi uren straw, a ierini ipuku, a ekis pochokkul awan me a wor an napanap mi tekia. Ruu mesen namanam tekia mi saramaram ra fen forata nemenem won mesan me ra ngeni ewe napanap pwe fansoun meinisin a chok suki mesan. Ese mwo nge ewe swank mi fefin non an kewe uf mi tongeni opano ewe watten manamanen ena inis —a usun ita a uren ekkena boots mi saramaram tori an a suki ewe lacing won, me ka tongeni kuna ew watten pachenong muscle a siwin nupwen pechen a mwokut fan an we kout mi pwechepwech. Ina eu inis mi tufichin fori watte nemenem —eu inis mi kirikiringau.

An kapas, ew tenor mi uren husky, a apacha ngeni ewe memefin kinikinfesen a apasa. A wor ew memefin turunufas usun sam non, pwan mwo ngeni aramas a sani —me mi wor mwan non New Haven ra fen oput an kewe guts.

“Iei, kosap ekieki pwe ai ekiek won ekkena mettoch a chok sopwosopwolo,” ussun ita a apasa, “pokiten chok ngang mi pochokkul me lap senikemi.” Aua nom non ewe chok mwichen senior, me nupwen aua kan chiechioch fansoun meinisin a wor ai memef pwe a tipeew ngeniei me a mochen ai upwe sani i ren ekkoch wistfulness mi pochokun, mi ungeni pwisin i.

Aua porausfengen ren fitu minich won ewe asamwacho mi saramaram.

“Mi wor ew neni mi murino ikei,” a apasa, mesan a saramaram non an ese kinamwe.

A suki ngeniei ren ew poun, a amwokutu ew poun mi watte mi patapat won ewe vista me mwen, pachenong non an sweep ew atake non Italy mi uren konik, ew nesoponon acre ren roses mi uren puun, me ew wa mi wor moter mi snub-nosed mi atai ewe konik non ewe sat.

“A nom ren Demaine, ewe mwan mi oil.” A suki ngeniei sefan, non tipetekison me mwitir. “Sipwe tonong non.”

Aua fetan non ew asam mi tekia ngeni ew neni mi saramaram usun rosy, mi mecheresin riri ngeni ewe imw ren ekkewe winto seni France non epek me epek. Ekkewe winto ra suk me ra saramaram mi pwechepwech ngeni ewe fetil mi fo lukun mi ussun ita pwe a ekis watteta lon ewe imw. Ew asepwan a fifi non ewe rum, a fifi ekkewe chepen non ew kinikin me tou seni ewe pwan ew usun ekkewe flag mi pwechepwech, a suki ngeni ewe kekin apupulu mi frost non ewe siling, me mwirin a fifi won ewe rug mi uren wain, a fori ew saram won usun chok asepwan a fori won ewe sat.

Ewe chok mettoch mi unusen uta non ewe rumw ina ew watten sofa minne ruemon fopun ra buo won usun ita won ew balloon mi anchor. Ir me ruemon ra ufouf uf mi pwechepwech, me ar kewe uf ra uren fitikoko me fitikoko usun ita ra keran chok fitikoko sefan mwirin ar ekis fitikoko won ewe imw. Mei wor ai upwe uta ren ekkoch fansoun ai upwe ausening ngeni ewe wichiwich me snap an ekkewe chepen me ewe ngungures ren ew sasing won ewe asam. Mwirin a wor ew boom nupwen Tom Buchanan a suki ekkewe asam mwirin me ewe asepwan mi tori a mano usun ewe rum, me ekkewe chepen me ekkewe rug me ekkewe ruemon fopun ra balloon ekis ngeni ewe puun.

Ewe mi kukkun seni ekkewe ruemon emon chon ekis ngeniei. A suki unusen inisin non nesoponon ewe divan, ese fokkun mwokut, me ren an chinnap a ekis suki, usun ita a balanceini och mettoch won minne a fokkun tongeni turutiw. Ika a kunaei seni ewe nepoputan mesan ese awora och esisin —ennetin, ua arapakan mairu ne ngungures ren ew omusomus ren ai osukosuka i ren ai tonong.

Ewe pwan emon nengin, Daisy, a achocho ne uta —a ekis suki mesan ren ew napanap mi mirit —mwirin a takiri, ew kukkun takiri mi umwes, mi amwarar, me ua pwan takiri me feito me mwen non ewe rum.

“Ua p-paras ren pwapwa.”

A takiri sefan, usun ita a apasa och mettoch mi fokkun amwarar, me a amwochu pei ren ekis fansoun, a nenengeni mesei, a pwon pwe ese wor emon non ewe fonufan a fokkun mochen kuna. Ina eu mettoch a wor ren. A apasa non ew mongungu pwe iten ewe nengin mi balance ina Baker. (Ua fen rongorong pwe a apasa pwe an Daisy we ngungures a chok fori aramas repwe lean ngeni i; ew kapasen esiit ese auchea minne a fori pwe ese kisino an char.)

Ese nifinifin, a suki awan Miss Baker, a suki awan ngeniei arapakan ese kuna, me mwirin a mwitir ne tipeew ngeni mokuran sefan —ewe pisek a balanceini a ffat pwe a ekis totter me a ngeni i och mettoch mi niuokus. Pwal eu sokkun omusomus a fis ngeni ai kewe kapas. Arapakan meinisin ekkewe pwarata usun unusen pwisin tumunur ra angei ew kinisou mi amwarar seniei.

Ua nenengeni sefani nei we cousin, ewe a poputa ne eisiniei kapas eis non mongungu mi tekison me apwapwa. Ina ewe sokkun mongungu ewe seling a fiti ngeni asan me fan, ussun ita pwe iteiten kapas eu kokkotun noot esap chuen tongeni takiri. Mesan a netipengaw me tong ren mettoch mi saramaram non, mesan mi saramaram me ew awan mi saramaram mi mochen, nge mi wor ew pwapwa non mongungun pwe ekkewe mwan ra fen tumunu i ra kuna pwe mi weires ar repwe monuki: ew koputen köl, ew mongungu mi monomon “Ausening,” ew pwon pwe a fori gay, mettoch mi amwarar ra chok ekis fansoun hociver hoyur non, me pwe mi wor ewe.

Ua ereni ifa usun ua tori Chicago ren ew ran won ai sai ngeni East, me ifa usun engon me ruemon aramas ra tinato ar tong ren ngang.

“Ra menemenoch ren?” a kechiw ren pwapwa.

"Unusen ewe teninimw a tatakis. Meinisin ekkewe mota ra wor ewe wiil mwirin mi peinit mi pwech usun ew föün äsefich, me a wor eü kechü mi sopwosopwolo unusen ewe pwinin ünükkün ewe sat notou.”

"A ifa me amwararen! Sipwe niwin, Tom. Lesor!" Mwirin, a pwal apasa nge ese wor lamotan: “Mi lamot kopwe kúna ewe ménúkol.”

“Ua mochen.”

“A annut. A tori ülüngät ierin. Kese mwo küna?”

"Esapw fis."

“Ewer, mi namwot kopwe kuna i. I—”

Tom Buchanan, ewe a fen fitikoko non ewe rum, a uta me a asoso poun won pei.

“Met ka fori, Nick?”

“Ngang emon mwan mi bond.”

“Iö ren?”

Ua ereni.

A apasa fän tipepwora: “Use mwo rongorong usun ir.”

Ena mettoch a asongaei.

Ua pölüweni ekiselo chök, “Kopwe föri.” “Kopwe fori ika kopwe nom non ewe East.”

“O, upwe nom non ewe East, kosapw osukosuk,” a apasa, a nenengeni Daisy me mwirin a niwin ngeniei, usun ita a mammasa ren och mettoch mi nap. “Upwe emon mi tiparoch seni Kot pwe upwe nonnom lon pwal eu leeni.”

Non ei fansoun Mis Baker a apasa: “Ese nifinifin!” ren ena sokkun mwitir pwe ua poputa —ina ewe aewin kapas a apasa seni ewe fansoun ua tonong non ewe rum. A ffat pwe a mairu usun chok a mairu ngeniei, pun a yawn me ren ew mwichen mokutukut mi mwitir, me deft a uta non ewe rum.

“Ua stiff,” a ngungures, “Ua fen nom won ena sofa ren ukukun fansoun ua tongeni chechemeni.”

Daisy a pölüweni, “Kosap nennengeniei, ua fen achocho le uweiok ngeni New York unusen ewe pwinin.”

“Ewer, kinisou,” a apasa Miss Baker ngeni ekkewe fomon kokkot ra chok tonong seni ewe nenien mongo. “Ua fokkun fiti ewe angangen afalafal.”

Noun we chon awasola aramas a nenengeni ren an ese nuku.

"En mei!" A angei an we konik usun ita nge a tori eu ttit mi nom fan eu kap. “Ifa usun ka tongeni fori och mettoch mi nap seni ai tongeni.”

Ua nenengeni Miss Baker, ua ekieki met ena a “fori.” Ua pwapwaiti ai upwe nennengeni. I emon nengin mi sikesik, mi kukkun inisin, mi wor efoch wa mi uta, minne a achocho ngeni ren an aturanong inisin ngeni mwirin won ekkewe pechen usun emon kukkun cadet. Mesan kewe mi pwechepwech mi uren akkar ra nenengeniei ren ar mochen sinei seni ew mesen aramas mi uren tong, me uren tong, me rese pwapwa. A toriei iei pwe ua fen kuna i, ika ew sasingin, non ew neni me nom.

A apasa fán turunufas: “Ka nóm lón West Egg.” “Ua sinei emon ikewe.”

“Use sinei emon emon—”

“Mi namwot kopwe sinei Gatsby.”
