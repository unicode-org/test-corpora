A sidɔm fɔ sɔm minit wit mi ed na mi an, te a yɛri di fon tek insay ɛn di bɔtlɛr in vɔys de kɔl taksi. Dɔn a waka sloslo dɔŋ di drayv fɔ go fa frɔm di os, ɛn a bin gɛt di maynd fɔ wet nia di get.

A nɔ bin dɔn go twɛnti yad we a yɛri mi nem ɛn Gatsbi step frɔm bitwin tu bush dɛn go insay di rod. A go mɔs dɔn fil fayn fayn wan bay da tɛm de, bikɔs a nɔ bin ebul fɔ tink bɔt natin pas di layt we in pink sus gɛt ɔnda di mun.

"Wetin yu de du?" A aks mi kwɛstyɔn.

“Jɔs tinap ya, ol spɔt.”

Sɔntɛm, dat bin tan lɛk wok we pipul dɛn nɔ lɛk. Fɔ ɔl wetin a bin no se i go tif di os insay smɔl tɛm; A nɔ bin fɔ dɔn sɔprayz fɔ si bad bad fes dɛn, di fes dɛn fɔ “Wɔlfshiem in pipul dɛn,” biɛn am na di dak tik.

“Yu si ɛni trɔbul na rod?” i aks afta wan minit.

"Yɛs."

I bin de shem.

“Dɛn kil am?”

"Yɛs."

"A bin tink so; a tɛl Dezi se a tink so. I bɛtɛ fɔ mek di shɔk kam wan tɛm. I tinap am fayn fayn wan."

I bin tɔk lɛk se na di wangren tin we Dezi bin biev we impɔtant.

I kɔntinyu fɔ tɔk se: “A rich na Wɛst Ɛg nia wan sayd rod, ɛn lɛf di motoka na mi garaj. A nɔ tink se ɛnibɔdi si wi, bɔt fɔ tru, a nɔ ebul fɔ shɔ.”

A nɔ bin lɛk am so bay dis tɛm dat a nɔ bin si se i nid fɔ tɛl am se i nɔ rayt.

“Udat na bin di uman?” i bin aks.

“In nem na Wilson. Na in man gɛt di garaj. Aw di dɛbul i apin?”

“Wɛl, a tray fɔ swing di wil—” I brok, ɛn wantɛm wantɛm a gɛs di tru.

“Na Dezi bin de drayv?”

Afta smɔl tɛm, i se: “Yɛs, bɔt fɔ tru a go se a bin de. Yu si, we wi kɔmɔt na Nyu Yɔk i bin rili fred ɛn i bin tink se i go mek i stedi fɔ drayv—ɛn dis uman rɔsh kɔmɔt pan wi jɔs lɛk aw wi bin de pas wan motoka we de kam di ɔda we. Ɔl dis bin apin insay wan minit, bɔt i bin tan lɛk to mi se i want fɔ tɔk to wi, i bin tink se wi na pɔsin we i sabi.Wɛl, fɔs Dezi tɔn away frɔm di uman to di ɔda motoka, dɔn i lɔs in nerv ɛn tɔn bak Di sɛkɔn we mi an rich na di wil a fil di shɔk—i go mɔs dɔn kil am wantɛm wantɛm.”

“I rip am opin—”

“Nɔ tɛl mi, ol spɔt.” I bin wins. “Enihow—Daisy step pan am.A tray fɔ mek i stɔp, bɔt i nɔ ebul, so a pul di imejensi brek.Dɔn i fɔdɔm ova na mi lap ɛn a drayv go.

I bin tɔk naw se: “I go fayn tumara.” "A jɔs go wet ya ɛn si if i tray fɔ mɔna am bɔt da tin we nɔ fayn dis aftanun. I dɔn lɔk insɛf na in rum, ɛn if i tray ɛni kruk we i go ɔt di layt ɛn on bak."

A se: “I nɔ go tɔch am.” “I nɔ de tink bɔt am.”

“A nɔ trɔst am, ol spɔt.”

“Aw lɔng yu go wet?”

“Ɔl nɛt, if nid de. Ɛni aw, te dɛn ɔl go slip.”

Wan nyu we fɔ si tin bin kam na mi maynd. Lɛ wi se Tɔmɔs kam fɔ no se Dezi bin dɔn de drayv. I kin tink se i si sɔntin we gɛt fɔ du wit am—i kin tink ɛnitin. A luk di os; tu ɔ tri brayt winda dɛn bin de dɔŋ ɛn di pink layt we kɔmɔt na Dezi in rum na di grɔn.

A se: “Yu de wet na ya.” “A go si if ɛni sayn de fɔ se pipul dɛn de mek bɔku bɔku pipul dɛn.”

A waka bak along di bɔda fɔ di lawn, travul di grɔn saful wan, ɛn tip fut ɔp di veranda stɛp dɛn. Di kɔtin dɛn na di drɔin rum bin opin, ɛn a si se di rum ɛmti. We a krɔs di pɔch usay wi bin dɔn it da Jun nɛt de tri mɔnt bifo, a kam na wan smɔl rɛktɛnj we gɛt layt we a gɛs se na di pantri winda. Dɛn bin drɔ di blaynd, bɔt a fɛn wan rift na di sil.

Dezi ɛn Tɔmɔs bin sidɔm nia dɛnsɛf na di kichin tebul, wit wan plet we gɛt kol frɛsh chukchuk bitwin dɛn, ɛn tu bɔtul dɛn we gɛt ale. I bin de tɔk gud wan akɔdin to di tebul to am, ɛn insay in rialnɛs in an bin dɔn fɔdɔm pan ɛn kɔba in yon. Wan wan tɛm, i kin luk am ɔp ɛn nɔd fɔ sho se i gri.

Dɛn nɔ bin gladi, ɛn dɛn ɔl tu nɔ bin dɔn tɔch di chukchuk ɔ di ale—ɛn pan ɔl dat, dɛnsɛf nɔ bin gladi. Na wan ays we nɔ go mistek bin de we gɛt natura intimacy bɔt di pikchɔ, ɛn ɛnibɔdi go dɔn se dɛn de plan togɛda.

As a de tip fut frɔm di pɔch a yɛri mi taksi de fil in we along di dak rod we de go na di os. Gatsby bin de wet usay a bin dɔn lɛf am na di drayv.

“I ɔl kwayɛt ɔp de?” i aks wit wɔri.

“Yɛs, ɔltin kwayɛt.” A bin de shem. “I bɛtɛ lɛ yu kam na os ɛn slip.”

I shek in ed.

"A want fɔ wet ya te Dezi go slip. Gud nɛt, ol spɔt."

I put in an dɛn na in kot pɔkit ɛn tɔn bak wit ɔl in at to di we aw i de luk di os, lɛk se mi prezɛns de ambɔg di oli we aw di vigil de. So a waka go ɛn lɛf am tinap de we di mun de shayn—a nɔ de wach natin.
