VIII

A nɔ bin ebul fɔ slip ɔl nɛt; wan fɔghɔn bin de kray ɔltɛm na di Saund, ɛn a bin de tos af-af sik bitwin grotesk rialiti ɛn savayj, frayd drim dɛn. We do klin a yɛri wan taksi de go ɔp Gatsbi in drayv, ɛn wantɛm wantɛm a jomp kɔmɔt na bed ɛn bigin fɔ drɛs—a fil se a gɛt sɔntin fɔ tɛl am, sɔntin fɔ wɔn am bɔt, ɛn mɔnin go tu let.

We a krɔs in lawn, a si se in frɔnt domɔt stil opin ɛn i ledɔm pan wan tebul na di ɔl, i bin ebi wit pwɛl at ɔ slip.

I bin tɔk wit wan wɔd se: “Natin nɔ apin.” “A wet, ɛn lɛk 4 oklɔk so i kam na di winda ɛn tinap de fɔ wan minit ɛn afta dat i ɔt di layt.”

In os nɔ bin ɛva tan lɛk se i big to mi lɛk aw i bin tan lɛk da nɛt de we wi bin de fɛn sigrɛt na di big big rum dɛn. Wi bin de push kɔtin dɛn we tan lɛk paviliɔn dɛn na sayd, ɛn fil oba bɔku bɔku fut dɛn we gɛt dak wɔl fɔ ilɛktrik layt switch dɛn—wan tɛm a bin fɔdɔm wit wan kayn splash pan di ki dɛn fɔ wan piano we tan lɛk gɔst. Dɔst bin de ɔlsay we wi nɔ go ebul fɔ ɛksplen, ɛn di rum dɛn bin gɛt dɔst, lɛk se dɛn nɔ bin dɔn gɛt ɛya fɔ bɔku dez. A bin fɛn di humidor pan wan tebul we a nɔ bin no, ɛn tu sigrɛt dɛn we dɔn ol ɛn dray insay. We wi trowe di Frɛnch winda dɛn na di drɔin rum, ɛn wi sidɔm de smok na di daknɛs.

A se: “Yu fɔ go fa.” “I rili shɔ se dɛn go tray fɔ tray yu motoka.”

“Go away naw , ol spɔt?”

“Go na Atlantik Siti fɔ wan wik, ɔ go ɔp na Mɔntrial.”

I nɔ go tink bɔt am. I nɔ bin ebul fɔ lɛf Dezi te i no wetin i go du. I bin de klach pan sɔm las op ɛn a nɔ bin ebul fɔ bia fɔ shek am fri.

Na dis nɛt i tɛl mi di strenj stori bɔt in yɔŋ tɛm wit Dan Kɔdi—i tɛl mi am bikɔs “Jay Gatsby” bin dɔn brok lɛk glas agens Tɔmɔs in had bad bad tin, ɛn dɛn bin ple di lɔng sikrit ɛkstravaganza. A tink se i bin fɔ dɔn gri wit ɛnitin naw, if i nɔ bin gɛt ɛnitin fɔ du wit am, bɔt i bin want fɔ tɔk bɔt Dezi.

Na in na bin di fɔs “nays” gyal we i bin dɔn ɛva no. Insay difrɛn difrɛn kapasiti dɛn we dɛn nɔ bin dɔn sho i bin dɔn kam in kɔntakt wit dɛn kayn pipul dɛn de, bɔt ɔltɛm wit waya we dɛn nɔ no bɔt bitwin. I bin si se i rili fayn. I bin go na in os, fɔs wit ɔda ɔfisa dɛn we kɔmɔt na Kemp Taylɔr, dɔn i bin go in wan. I bin mek i sɔprayz—i nɔ bin ɛva go na dis kayn fayn os bifo. Bɔt wetin bin gi am wan ays we nɔ de blo, na dat Dezi bin de de—na bin kɔzhɔl tin to am jɔs lɛk aw in tɛnt we bin de na kamp bin bi to am. Wan rayp mistɛri bin de bɔt am, wan hint fɔ bedrum dɛn ɔpstayd we fayn ɛn kol pas ɔda bedrum dɛn, fɔ gay ɛn raytin aktiviti dɛn we de apin tru in kɔridɔ dɛn, ɛn fɔ romans dɛn we nɔ bin musty ɛn lay away ɔlrɛdi insay lavender bɔt fresh ɛn brith ɛn we de smɛl dis ia in motoka dɛn we de shayn ɛn bɔt dans dɛn we dɛn flawa dɛn nɔ bin dɔn dray. I bin gladi bak fɔ no se bɔku man dɛn bin dɔn ɔlrɛdi lɛk Dezi—i bin mek i valyu mɔ na in yay. I fil dɛn prezɛns ɔlsay na di os, we de ɔlsay na di ays wit di shed ɛn ɛko dɛn we stil de mek i fil fayn.

Bɔt i bin no se i bin de na Dezi in os bay wan big aksidɛnt. No mata aw in fiuja go gɛt glori as Jay Gatsby, naw na yɔŋ man we nɔ gɛt mɔni ɛn we nɔ gɛt past, ɛn ɛni tɛm di klos we pɔsin nɔ de si we i wɛr in yunifom kin slip kɔmɔt na in sholda. So i bin yuz in tɛm fayn fayn wan. I tek wetin i ebul fɔ gɛt, wit ravenously ɛn unscrupulously—eventually i tek Daisy wan stil Ɔktoba nɛt, tek am bikɔs i nɔ gɛt rial rayt fɔ tɔch in an.

Sɔntɛm i bin fɔ dɔn disgres insɛf, bikɔs i bin rili tek am fɔ mek lɛk se i nɔ biliv am. A nɔ min se i bin dɔn tred pan in phantom milyɔn, bɔt i bin dɔn mek Dezi fil sef bay wilful; i mek i biliv se in na pɔsin we kɔmɔt na di sem strata lɛk insɛf—se i ebul fɔ kia fɔ am gud gud wan. As a bi se, i nɔ bin gɛt dɛn kayn fasiliti dɛn de—i nɔ bin gɛt kɔmfyut famili we tinap biɛn am, ɛn i bin gɛt fɔ pe fɔ mek gɔvmɛnt we nɔ gɛt pɔsin in yon want fɔ blo ɛnisay na di wɔl.

Bɔt i nɔ bin de dispɛkt insɛf ɛn i nɔ bin apin lɛk aw i bin de imajin. I bin dɔn tink, sɔntɛm, fɔ tek wetin i ebul ɛn go—bɔt naw i dɔn si se i dɔn kɔmit insɛf fɔ fala wan grail. I bin no se Dezi na ɛkstra ɔdinari, bɔt i nɔ bin no jɔs aw “nays” gyal pikin kin bi ɛkstra ɔdinari. I bin lɔs insay in jɛntri os, insay in jɛntri, ful layf, ɛn lɛf Gatsbi—natin. I bin fil se i dɔn mared to am, na dat nɔmɔ.

We dɛn mit bak, tu dez afta dat, na Gatsbi we nɔ bin de blo fayn, we, sɔm kayn we, dɛn bin betray am. In pɔch bin brayt wit di laŋaj we dɛn bay we gɛt sta-shayn; di wikɛd na di sɛti bin de swɛt fashɔn as i tɔn to am ɛn i kis in mɔt we i want fɔ no mɔ ɛn we i lɛk. I bin dɔn gɛt kol, ɛn i bin mek in vɔys huski ɛn charming pas ɛni ɔda tɛm, ɛn Gatsbi bin rili no bɔt di yɔŋ pipul dɛn ɛn di mistɛri we jɛntri de put na jel ɛn kip, di fresh we bɔku klos dɛn gɛt, ɛn di Dezi, we de shayn lɛk silva, we sef ɛn prawd pas di hot strɛch we di po pipul dɛn de fɛt.

“A no fit diskraib to yu hau a sapraiz fo fain out se a laik am, old spot. A ivin op fo som taim se shi go trowe mi ova, bot shi no du am, bikos shi bi in lov wit mi tu. Shi tink se a sabi plenti bikos a sabi difren tins from am... Wel, na de a bin de, way off mai ambishon, de get dip in lov evri minit, and ol of a sudden a no bi kia. Wetin bi di yuz fɔ du big big tin dɛn if a go ebul fɔ gɛt bɛtɛ tɛm fɔ tɛl am wetin a go du?”

Di las aftanun bifo i go na ɔda kɔntri, i sidɔm wit Dezi na in an fɔ lɔng tɛm ɛn nɔ tɔk natin. Na bin kol fɔl de, faya bin de na di rum ɛn in chɛst dɛn bin de flash. Naw ɛn naw i kin muf ɛn i kin chenj in an smɔl, ɛn wan tɛm i kin kis in dak shayn ia. Di aftanun bin dɔn mek dɛn kwayɛt fɔ sɔm tɛm, lɛk se i go gi dɛn dip mɛmori fɔ di lɔng pat we dɛn bin prɔmis di nɛks de. Dɛn nɔ bin ɛva de nia dɛnsɛf insay dɛn mɔnt we dɛn bin lɛk dɛnsɛf, ɛn dɛn nɔ bin ɛva tɔk to dɛnsɛf dip wan, pas we i de brus lip dɛn we nɔ de tɔk na in kot in sholda ɔ we i tɔch di ɛnd pan in finga dɛn, saful wan, lɛk se i de slip.

I bin du fayn fayn wan na di wɔ. I bin bi kapten bifo i go na di frɔnt, ɛn afta di Argonne fɛt dɛn i bin gɛt in majyoriti ɛn di kɔmand fɔ di divishɔnal mashin-gan dɛn. Afta di armistice i tray frantically fɔ rich na os, bɔt sɔm kɔmplikeshɔn ɔ misɔndastandin sɛn am na Ɔkford insted. I bin de wɔri naw—wan kwaliti bin de we bin de mek i nɔ gɛt op igen na di lɛta dɛn we Dezi bin rayt. I nɔ bin si wetin mek i nɔ ebul fɔ kam. I bin de fil di prɛshɔn we di wɔl de prɛs am na do, ɛn i bin want fɔ si am ɛn fil in prezɛns nia am ɛn mek i biliv se i de du di rayt tin afta ɔl.

Fɔ Dezi bin yɔŋ ɛn in atifishal wɔl bin de smɛl ɔkid ɛn fayn, gladi gladi snob ɛn ɔkestra dɛn we bin de sɛt di ritm fɔ di ia, we bin de sɔm di sɔri-at ɛn di tin dɛn we de mek pɔsin tink bɔt layf insay nyu tiun dɛn. Ɔl nɛt di saksofon dɛn bin de kray fɔ di kɔmɛnt we nɔ gɛt op fɔ di “Beale Street Blues” we wan ɔndrɛd pe gold ɛn silva slip dɛn bin de shuffle di dɔst we de shayn. Na di grey ti awa, rum dɛn bin de ɔltɛm we bin de blo ɔltɛm wit dis lɔw, swit fiva, we fresh fes dɛn bin de drɛf ya ɛn de lɛk ros peta dɛn we di sad ɔn dɛn we de rawnd di flɔ de blo.

Tru dis twilayt yunivas Dezi bigin fɔ muv bak wit di sizin; wantɛm wantɛm i bin de kip af duzin deti bak ɛvride wit af duzin man dɛn, ɛn i bin de slip slip na do wit di bidz ɛn chifɔn fɔ wan ivintɛm klos we tandul bitwin ɔkid dɛn we de day na grɔn nia in bed. Ɛn ɔltɛm sɔntin we bin de insay am bin de kray fɔ mek i disayd fɔ du sɔntin. I bin want fɔ mek in layf shep naw, wantɛm wantɛm—ɛn di disayd fɔ bi bay sɔm pawa—fɔ lɔv, fɔ mɔni, fɔ du tin we nɔ gɛt wan dawt—we bin de nia.

Dat fos tek shep fo midul spring wit di araval of Tom Buchanan. Wan gud bulkiness bin de bɔt in pɔsin ɛn in pozishɔn, ɛn Dezi bin de flat. I go mɔs bi se wan patikyula strɛch bin de ɛn wan patikyula rilif bin de. Di lɛta rich to Gatsby we i bin stil de na Ɔkford.

I bin dɔn do naw na Lɔng Ayland ɛn wi bin de go rawnd fɔ opin di ɔda winda dɛn we de dɔŋ, ɛn ful-ɔp di os wit layt we de tɔn grey, we de tɔn gold. Di shado fɔ wan tik bin fɔdɔm wantɛm wantɛm akɔdin to di dyu ɛn bɔd dɛn we tan lɛk gɔst bigin fɔ siŋ bitwin di blu lif dɛn. Wan muvmɛnt bin de sloslo ɛn fayn na di ays, i nɔ bin gɛt bɛtɛ briz, ɛn i bin prɔmis fɔ gɛt kol, fayn de.

“A nɔ tink se i bin ɛva lɛk am.” Gatsby tɔn rawnd frɔm wan winda ɛn luk mi wit chalenj. "Yu fɔ mɛmba, ol spɔt, i bin rili gladi dis aftanun. I tɛl am dɛn tin dɛn de di we we mek i fred—dat mek i tan lɛk se a na sɔm kayn cheap shap. Ɛn di rizɔlt na dat i nɔ bin rili no wetin i de tɔk."

I bin sidɔm wit pwɛl at.

“Fɔ tru, i go dɔn lɛk am jɔs fɔ wan minit, we dɛn bin fɔs mared—ɛn i bin lɛk mi mɔ ivin da tɛm de, yu si?”

Wantɛm wantɛm i kɔmɔt na do wit wan tɔk we i want fɔ no mɔ.

I bin tɔk se: “Ɛni we aw i bi, na jɔs pɔsin in yon.”

Wetin yu kin mek pan dat, pas fɔ sɔspɛkt sɔm intensiti insay in kɔnsɛpshɔn fɔ di afɛkt we dɛn nɔ go ebul fɔ mɛzhɔ?

I kam bak frɔm Frans we Tɔmɔs ɛn Dezi bin stil de na dɛn mared trip, ɛn i bin mek wan misɛf bɔt i nɔ bin ebul fɔ stɔp fɔ travul go na Luizvil pan di las tɛm we i bin de pe in ami. I bin de de fɔ wan wik, i bin de waka na di strit dɛn usay dɛn fut step dɛn bin dɔn klik togɛda tru di Novɛmba nɛt ɛn i bin de go bak na di say dɛn we nɔ bin de usay dɛn bin dɔn drayv wit in wayt motoka. Jɔs lɛk aw Dezi in os bin dɔn tan lɛk se i nɔ izi fɔ ɔndastand ɛn i gay pas ɔda os dɛn, na so di aidia we i bin gɛt bɔt di siti sɛf, pan ɔl we i nɔ bin de igen, bin ful-ɔp wit wan fayn fayn tin we de mek i fil bad.

I kɔmɔt de fil se if i bin dɔn luk fɔ am mɔ, sɔntɛm i fɔ dɔn fɛn am—se i de lɛf am biɛn. Di de-kɔch—i nɔ bin gɛt peni naw—bin hot. I go na di opin vestibul ɛn sidɔm pan wan fold-chia, ɛn di steshɔn slid go ɛn di bak fɔ bildin dɛn we i nɔ sabi bin de muf pas. Dɔn kɔmɔt na di spring fil dɛm, usay wan yɔlɔ troli bin de rɔn dɛn fɔ wan minit wit pipul dɛn insay de we sɔntɛm bin dɔn si di pale majik we in fes bin gɛt wan tɛm along di kɔzhɔl strit.

Di trak kɔba ɛn naw i bin de go fa frɔm di san, we, as i de sink dɔŋ, i bin tan lɛk se i de spre insɛf insay blɛsin oba di siti we bin de lɔs usay i bin dɔn pul in briz. I stret in an bad bad wan lɛk se i jɔs want fɔ tek wan smɔl briz, fɔ sev wan pat pan di ples we i bin mek fayn fɔ am. Bɔt ɔltin bin de go tu fast naw fɔ in blɔk yay ɛn i bin no se i dɔn lɔs da pat de, di fresh ɛn di bɛst, sote go.

Na bin nayn oklɔk we wi dɔn fɔ it brɛf ɛn go na di wɔl. Di nɛt bin dɔn mek shap difrɛns pan di wɛda ɛn wan ɔtom flawa bin de na di ays. Di gadin man, we na di las wan pan Gatsbi in savant dɛn, kam na di fut fɔ di stɛp dɛn.

"A go drein di pul tide, Mista Gatsbi. Lif go bigin fɔ fɔdɔm kwik kwik wan, ɛn afta dat, trɔbul kin de ɔltɛm wit di paip dɛn."

Gatsbi ansa se: “Nɔ du am tide.” I tɔn to mi fɔ beg fɔ padi. “Yu no, ol spɔt, a nɔ ɛva yuz da pul de ɔl di sɔmma?”

A luk mi wach ɛn tinap.

“Twɛlv minit to mi tren.”

A nɔ bin want fɔ go na di siti. A nɔ bin fit fɔ wok fayn, bɔt i bin pas dat—a nɔ bin want fɔ lɛf Gatsbi. A mis da tren de, ɛn afta dat ɔda wan, bifo a ebul fɔ pul misɛf.

A dɔn tɔk se: “A go kɔl yu.”

“Du, ol spɔt.”

“A go kɔl yu lɛk midde.”

Wi waka sloslo dɔŋ di stɛp dɛn.

“A tink se Dezi go kɔl bak.” I bin de luk mi wit wɔri, lɛk se i op se a go sɔpɔt dis.

“A tink se na so.”

“Wɛl, gudbai.”

Wi shek an ɛn a bigin fɔ go. Jɔs bifo a rich na di hed a mɛmba sɔntin ɛn tɔn rawnd.

A ala na di ɔdasay na di lawn se: “Dɛn na rɔtin krawd.” “Yu worth di ol damn bunch we dem put togeda.”

A bin de gladi ɔltɛm we a tɔk dat. Na di wangren kɔmplimɛnt we a ɛva gi am, bikɔs a nɔ bin gladi fɔ am frɔm di biginin te to di ɛnd. Fɔs i nɔd wit pɔlitiks, dɔn in fes brok insay da smayl de we de shayn ɛn ɔndastand, lɛk se wi dɔn de gladi gladi fɔ da tru tin de ɔltɛm. In fayn fayn pink rag we i wɛr sus bin mek wan brayt kɔlɔ pan di wayt stɛp dɛn, ɛn a bin tink bɔt di nɛt we a bin fɔs kam na in gret gret granpa dɛn os, tri mɔnt bifo dat. Di lawn ɛn di drayv bin dɔn ful-ɔp wit di fes fɔ di wan dɛn we bin de gɛs in kɔrɔpshɔn—ɛn i bin dɔn tinap na dɛn stɛp dɛn de, de ayd in drim we nɔ de rɔtin, as i de wɛf dɛn gudbay.

A bin tɛl am tɛnki fɔ di we aw i bin wɛlkɔm am. Wi bin de tɛl am tɛnki ɔltɛm fɔ dat—mi ɛn di ɔda wan dɛn.

A kɔl am se: “Gudbay.” “A bin ɛnjɔy fɔ it brɛf, Gatsbi.”

Up in di siti, a tray fɔ sɔm tɛm fɔ list di kot dɛn pan wan stok we nɔ de dɔn, dɔn a slip na mi swivel-chair. Jɔs bifo midulnɛt di fon wek mi, ɛn a bigin fɔ op wit swet we de brok na mi fɔɛd. Na bin Jɔdan Baker; bɔku tɛm i bin de kɔl mi na dis awa bikɔs di we aw i nɔ bin shɔ bɔt in yon muvmɛnt bitwin ɔtel ɛn klab ɛn prayvet os dɛn bin mek i nɔ izi fɔ fɛn am ɛni ɔda we. Bɔku tɛm, in vɔys kin kam oba di waya lɛk sɔntin we fresh ɛn kol, lɛk se divot frɔm grɔn gɔlf-links dɔn kam de sel insay di ɔfis winda, bɔt dis mɔnin i bin tan lɛk se i harsh ɛn dray.

I bin tɔk se: “A dɔn kɔmɔt na Dezi in os. “A de na Hempstead, ɛn a de go dɔŋ na Sawtɛmpɔt dis aftanun.”

Sɔntɛm i bin tek tɛm fɔ kɔmɔt na Dezi in os, bɔt di tin we a du bin mek a vɛks, ɛn di nɛks tin we i tɔk bin mek a nɔ miks.

“Yu nɔ bin so fayn to mi las nɛt.”

“Aw i bin fɔ dɔn impɔtant da tɛm de?”

Saylens fɔ smɔl tɛm. Dɔn:

“Bɔt—a want fɔ si yu.”

“A want fɔ si yu bak.”

“Lɛ wi se a nɔ go na Sawtɛmpɔt, ɛn kam insay tɔŋ dis aftanun?”

“Nɔ—a nɔ tink dis aftanun.”

“I rili fayn.”

“I nɔ pɔsibul dis aftanun. Difrɛn—”

Wi bin de tɔk da we de fɔ sɔm tɛm, dɔn wantɛm wantɛm wi nɔ bin de tɔk igen. A nɔ no uswan pan wi bin hang ɔp wit shap klik, bɔt a no se a nɔ bin bisin. A nɔ bin fɔ dɔn tɔk to am akɔdin to wan ti-tebul da de de if a nɔ bin ɛva tɔk to am igen na dis wɔl.

A kɔl Gatsbi in os afta sɔm minit, bɔt di layn bin bizi. A bin tray 4 tɛm; las las wan sɛntral we vɛks tɛl mi se dɛn de kip di waya opin fɔ lɔng distans frɔm Ditroyt. We a tek mi tɛmtebul, a drɔ wan smɔl sɛklɔ rawnd di tri-fifti tren. Dɔn a ledɔm bak na mi chia ɛn tray fɔ tink. Na bin jɔs midulnɛt.

We a pas di ashis-hip dɛn na di tren da mɔnin de a bin dɔn krɔs bay wilful fɔ go na di ɔda say na di motoka. A bin tink se wan krawd we want fɔ no mɔ go de rawnd de ɔl di de wit smɔl bɔbɔ dɛn we de luk fɔ dak ples dɛn na di dɔst, ɛn sɔm garrulous man we de tɛl ɔva ɛn ɔva wetin dɔn apin, te i bi less ɛn less rial ivin to am ɛn i nɔ ebul fɔ tɛl am igen, ɛn dɛn fɔgɛt di bad bad tin we Myrtle Wilson du. Naw a wan go bak smɔl ɛn tɛl wetin apin na di garaj afta wi kɔmɔt de di nɛt bifo.

I nɔ bin izi fɔ dɛn fɔ fɛn di sista we nem Katrin. I go mɔs dɔn brok in lɔ we se i nɔ fɔ drink da nɛt de, bikɔs we i rich i bin ful wit rɔm ɛn i nɔ bin ebul fɔ ɔndastand se di ambulans dɔn ɔlrɛdi go na Flushing. Bay siks oklɔk, Maykɛlis bin dɔn taya, ɛn i bin gladi fɔ di sawnd we motoka de stɔp na do. Na wan pan di wan dɛn we bin de wach di nɛt bifo we bin dɔn prɔmis fɔ kam bak, so i kuk brɛf fɔ tri pipul dɛn, we in ɛn di ɔda man it togɛda. Wilsin bin kwayɛt naw, ɛn Maykɛlis bin go na os fɔ slip; we i wek afta 4 awa ɛn rɔn go bak kwik kwik wan na di garaj, Wilsin nɔ bin de igen.

In muvmɛnt—i bin de waka ɔltɛm—dɛn bin tray afta dat to Pɔt Ruzvelt ɛn afta dat to Gad’s Hil, usay i bay wan sandwich we i nɔ bin de it, ɛn wan kɔp kɔfi. I go mɔs dɔn taya ɛn i bin de waka sloslo, bikɔs i nɔ bin rich na Gad in il te midulnɛt. So te naw, i nɔ bin at fɔ kɔnt fɔ in tɛm—bɔy pikin dɛn bin de we bin dɔn si wan man “de akt lɛk se i kray,” ɛn motoka man dɛn we i bin de luk ɔda kayn we frɔm di sayd we di rod de. Dɔn fɔ tri awa, i nɔ bin de si am igen. Di polis, pan di trɛnk fɔ wetin i tɛl Maykɛlis, se i “gɛt we fɔ no,” bin tink se i spɛn da tɛm de fɔ go frɔm garaj to garaj de, de aks fɔ wan yɔlɔ motoka. Bɔt no garaj man we bin dɔn si am nɔ bin ɛva kam bifo, ɛn sɔntɛm i bin gɛt we we izi ɛn shɔ fɔ no wetin i want fɔ no. Bay af pas tu i bin de na Wɛst Ɛg, usay i aks sɔmbɔdi di rod fɔ go na Gatsbi in os. So bay da tɛm de i bin dɔn no Gatsbi in nem.

Tu oklɔk, Gatsbi wɛr in bathing-suit ɛn lɛf wɔd wit di bɔtlɛs se if ɛnibɔdi fon wɔd fɔ kam to am na di pul. I bin stɔp na di garaj fɔ wan matres we gɛt nyumɛtik we bin dɔn mek in gɔst dɛn laf insay di sɔmma, ɛn di draya bin ɛp am fɔ pɔmp am. Dɔn i gi instrɔkshɔn se dɛn nɔ fɔ pul di motoka we opin ɔnda ɛnitin—ɛn dis bin strenj, bikɔs di frɔnt rayt fɛndr nid fɔ ripɛnt.

Gatsby sholda di matres ɛn bigin fɔ go na di pul. Wan tɛm i stɔp ɛn shift am smɔl, ɛn di drayva aks am if i nid ɛp, bɔt i shek in ed ɛn insay smɔl tɛm i lɔs bitwin di tik dɛn we bin de yɔlɔ.

No tɛlifon mɛsej nɔ bin kam, bɔt di bɔtlɛr nɔ bin slip ɛn wet fɔ am te 4 oklɔk—te lɔng afta we ɛnibɔdi de fɔ gi am if i kam. I go mɔs dɔn luk ɔp na wan skay we i nɔ sabi tru lif dɛn we de mek i fred ɛn shek shek as i si aw ros na wɔndaful tin ɛn aw di san layt bin raw pan di gras we dɛn nɔ mek bɛtɛ. Wan nyu wɔl, matirial we nɔ rial, usay po gɔst dɛn, we de blo drim lɛk ɛya, bin de drɛf fɔtuitously bɔt... lɛk da ashin, fantastik figa de we de glid to am tru di amorphous tik dɛn.

Di drayva—in na bin wan pan di pipul dɛn we Wolfshiem bin de protɛkt—i yɛri di shot dɛn—afta dat i bin jɔs ebul fɔ se i nɔ bin tink bɔt dɛn bɛtɛ. A drayv frɔm di steshɔn dairekt to Gatsby in os ɛn mi rɔsh wɔri fɔ go ɔp di frɔnt stɛp na di fɔs tin we mek ɛnibɔdi ala. Bɔt dɛn bin no da tɛm de, a biliv tranga wan. Wi nɔ bin tɔk wan wɔd nɔmɔ, wi 4, di draya, di bɔtlɛr, di gadin man, ɛn mi, rɔn go dɔŋ kwik kwik wan na di pul.

Di wata bin de muv fayn fayn wan ɛn i nɔ bin izi fɔ no se di wata we bin de kɔmɔt na wan ɛnd bin de mek i go na di dreyn na di ɔda ɛnd. Wit smɔl smɔl ripɛl dɛn we nɔ bin tan lɛk di shado dɛn we di wata de mek, di matres we gɛt lod bin de muf irɛgyula dɔŋ di pul. Wan smɔl briz we bin de blo we nɔ bin de kɔrɛkt di wata, bin du fɔ ambɔg di we aw i bin de go aksidɛnt wit di lod we i bin gɛt we i bin de aksidɛnt. We wan grup we gɛt lif dɛn tɔch am, i bin de rɔn am sloslo, ɛn i bin de tray, lɛk di leg we i de pas, wan tint rɛd sɛklɔ na di wata.

Na afta wi bigin wit Gatsbi fɔ go na di os, na in di gadin man si Wilsin in bɔdi smɔl fa na di gras, ɛn di ɔlokɔst dɔn dɔn.

Afta tu ia a mɛmba di ɔda pat pan da de de, ɛn da nɛt de ɛn di nɛks de, jɔs as wan drɔl we nɔ gɛt ɛnd we polis ɛn fotografa dɛn ɛn nyuspepa man dɛn de insay ɛn kɔmɔt na Gatsbi in frɔnt domɔt. Wan rop bin strɛch akɔdin to di men get ɛn wan polisman we bin de nia am bin de kip di wan dɛn we bin want fɔ no mɔ, bɔt i nɔ tu te, smɔl bɔbɔ dɛn kam fɔ no se dɛn kin go insay mi yad, ɛn ɔltɛm sɔm pan dɛn bin de gɛda opin mɔt rawnd di pul. Sɔmbɔdi we gɛt fayn abit, sɔntɛm na ditektiv, bin yuz di wɔd “madman” as i bɛn oba Wilsin in bɔdi da aftanun de, ɛn di advantej pawa we in vɔys gɛt bin sɛt di ki fɔ di nyuspepa ripɔt dɛn nɛks mɔnin.

Bɔku pan dɛn ripɔt dɛn de na bin bad bad drim—na bin bad bad drim, dɛn bin de tɔk bɔt tin dɛn we bin apin to dɛn, dɛn bin rili want fɔ du dat, ɛn dɛn nɔ bin de tɔk tru. We Maykɛlis in tɛstimoni na di inkwest bin mek Wilson in saspekshɔn bɔt in wɛf kɔmɔt na do a bin tink se i nɔ go te igen dɛn go sav di wan ol stori insay racy pasquinade—bɔt Katrin, we go dɔn tɔk ɛnitin, nɔ bin tɔk wan wɔd. I sho wan sɔprayz amɔnt fɔ karakta bɔt am bak—luk di korona wit disaid yay ɔnda da kɔrɛkt brɔw we i gɛt, ɛn swɛ se in sista nɔ ɛva si Gatsbi, se in sista gladi kɔmplit wan wit in man, se in sista nɔ bin dɔn du ɛni bad tin. I mek insɛf biliv am, ɛn kray insay in hankɛf, lɛk se di sɛf advays pas aw i go ebul fɔ bia. So dɛn bin ridyus Wilsin to man we “deranged by grief” so dat di kes go kɔntinyu fɔ bi di simpul we. Ɛn i bin rɛst de.

Fɔs, a bin sɔprayz ɛn kɔnfyus; dɔn, as i de ledɔm na in os ɛn nɔ de muf ɔ blo ɔ tɔk, awa pan awa, i bin de gro pan mi se na mi ripɔtabl, bikɔs nɔbɔdi nɔ bin intres—intres, a min, wit da strɔng pasɔnal intres de we ɔlman gɛt sɔm vayg rayt na di ɛnd.

A si am as wan nɛt sinima we Ɛl Grɛko bin si: wan ɔndrɛd os dɛn, we na kɔmɔn ɛn grotesk wan tɛm, we de kray ɔnda wan skay we nɔ gɛt wan bɔt, we de ɔp ɛn wan mun we nɔ gɛt layt. Insay di fɔs ples, 4 solemn man dɛn we wɛr dres sus de waka na di saydwɔk wit wan strɛcha we wan uman we dɔn chak we wɛr wayt ivintɛm dres ledɔm pan. In an we de dangle oba di sayd, de shayn kol wit jɔlɔs. Gravely di man dɛn kin tɔn insay wan os—di rɔng os. Bɔt nɔbɔdi nɔ no di uman in nem, ɛn nɔbɔdi nɔ bisin bɔt am.

Afta Gatsby in day di Is bin de haunt fɔ mi lɛk dat, distɔrj pas mi yay in pawa fɔ kɔrɛkt. So we di blu smok we brit lif dɛn bin de blo bin de na di ays ɛn di briz bin de blo di wet lɔndri stiff na di layn a disayd fɔ kam bak na os.

Wan tin bin de we a fɔ du bifo a kɔmɔt, wan tin we nɔ bin izi fɔ mi, we nɔ bin fayn, we sɔntɛm i bin bɛtɛ fɔ lɛ dɛn lɛf am. Bɔt a bin want fɔ lɛf tin dɛn ɔganayz ɛn nɔ jɔs abop pan da obliging ɛn indipɛndɛnt si de fɔ swip mi rɔf. A si Jɔdan Beka ɛn tɔk ɔva ɛn rawnd wetin dɔn apin to wi togɛda, ɛn wetin apin afta dat to mi, ɛn i ledɔm pafɛkt wan, de lisin, na wan big chia.

I bin drɛs fɔ ple gɔlf, ɛn a mɛmba se a bin de tink se i tan lɛk gud ɛgzampul, in chin rayz smɔl jauntily, in ia gɛt di kɔlɔ we ɔtom lif gɛt, in fes di sem brawn tint lɛk di glɔv we nɔ gɛt finga na in ni. We a dɔn i tɛl mi witout kɔmɛnt se i dɔn ɛngage wit ɔda man. A bin dawt dat, pan ɔl we sɔm bin de we i bin fɔ dɔn mared we i nɔd in ed, bɔt a bin mek lɛk se a sɔprayz. Fɔ jɔs wan minit a bin de wɔnda if a nɔ de mek mistek, dɔn a tink ɔltin bak kwik kwik wan ɛn grap fɔ se gudbai.

Jɔdan bin tɔk wantɛm wantɛm se: “Bɔt pan ɔl dat, yu bin trowe mi.” "Yu trowe mi ova fo fon. A no de giv yu damn nau, bot na nyu expiriens fo mi, and a fil likl diziz fo som taim."

Wi bin shek wi an.

I bin tɔk bak se: “O, ɛn yu mɛmba”—i bin tɔk bak se—“wan tɔk we wi bin gɛt wan tɛm bɔt aw fɔ drayv motoka?”

“Wetin mek—nɔto jɔs.”

"Yu se bad draiva de onli sef sote shi mit oda bad draiva? Wel, a mit anoda bad draiva, no bi so? A min se na careless of mi to mek dis kain rong guess. A bin tink se yu bi rather onest, straitfod pesin. A tink se na yu sikrit praid."

A se: “A ol tati ia.” “A dɔn ol fayv ia fɔ lay to misɛf ɛn kɔl am ɔna.”

I nɔ bin ansa am. A bin vɛks, ɛn a bin lɛk am af-af, ɛn a sɔri bad bad wan, a tɔn bak.

Wan aftanun let insay Ɔktoba a si Tɔmɔs Buchanan. I bin de waka bifo mi along Fayv Avɛnyu wit in alert, agresiv we, in an dɛn kɔmɔt smɔl frɔm in bɔdi lɛk se i de fɛt ɔf intafɛreshɔn, in ed bin de muv shap shap ya ɛn de, de adap insɛf to in yay dɛn we nɔ de rɛst. Jɔs as a slo fɔ mek a nɔ ɔvatak am i stɔp ɛn bigin fɔ kray na di winda dɛn na wan jɔyri stɔ. Wantɛm wantɛm i si mi ɛn waka bak, i es in an.

“Wetin na di prɔblɛm, Nik?Yu de agens fɔ shek an wit mi?”

“Yɛs. Yu no wetin a tink bɔt yu.”

I tɔk kwik kwik wan se: “Yu kray, Nik.” “Krayzi as ɛl. A nɔ no wetin na di prɔblɛm wit yu.”

A aks am se: “Tɔm, wetin yu tɛl Wilsin da aftanun de?”

I bin de luk mi witout wan wɔd, ɛn a bin no se a dɔn gɛs rayt bɔt dɛn awa dɛn de we a nɔ bin de. A bigin fɔ tɔn bak, bɔt i tek wan stɛp afta mi ɛn ol mi an.

I bin tɔk se: “A tɛl am di tru. "I kam na di domɔt we wi de rɛdi fɔ kɔmɔt, ɛn we a sɛn wɔd dɔŋ se wi nɔ de insay i tray fɔ fos in we fɔ go ɔp. I bin kray fɔ kil mi if a nɔ bin dɔn tɛl am udat gɛt di motoka. In an bin de pan rivolva na in poket ɛvri minit we i de na di os—" I brok ɔf defiantly. "Wetin if a bin tɛl am? Dat man bin gɛt am fɔ kam to am. I bin trowe dɔst na yu yay jɔs lɛk aw i bin de trowe na Dezi in yay, bɔt i bin tranga wan. I rɔn oba Myrtle lɛk se yu go rɔn oba dɔg ɛn nɔ ɛva ivin stɔp in motoka."

Natin nɔ bin de we a go ebul fɔ tɔk, pas di wan tin we a nɔ go ebul fɔ tɔk bɔt we se nɔto tru.

"Ɛn if yu tink se a nɔ bin gɛt mi pat pan sɔfa—luk ya, we a go giv ɔp da flat de ɛn si da damn bɔks de fɔ dɔg biskit sidɔm de na di saydbɔd, a sidɔm ɛn kray lɛk bebi. Bay Gɔd i bin rili bad—”

A nɔ bin ebul fɔ fɔgiv am ɔ lɛk am, bɔt a si se wetin i dɔn du, to am, na tin we rayt ɔltin. Ɔl dis bin rili nɔ tek tɛm ɛn kɔnfyus. Dɛn na bin pipul dɛn we nɔ bin de tek tɛm, Tɔmɔs ɛn Dezi—dɛn bin de smash tin ɛn krichɔ dɛn ɛn afta dat dɛn bin de tɔn bak insay dɛn mɔni ɔ dɛn big big nɔ tek tɛm, ɔ ɛnitin we bin de mek dɛn kɔntinyu fɔ de togɛda, ɛn mek ɔda pipul dɛn klin di mɛs we dɛn bin dɔn mek...

A bin shek an wit am; i bin tan lɛk se i nɔ mek sɛns fɔ nɔ du am, bikɔs a bin fil wantɛm wantɛm lɛk se a de tɔk to pikin. Dɔn i go insay di jɔyri stɔ fɔ bay wan pal nɛks—ɔ sɔntɛm na wan kɔf bɔtin nɔmɔ—we go pul mi provinsial swɛt sote go.

Gatsbi in os bin stil ɛmti we a kɔmɔt de—di gras we bin de na in lawn bin dɔn gro lɛk mi yon. Wan pan di taksi drayva dɛn na di vilej nɔ ɛva tek fare pas di ɛntrɛ get we i nɔ stɔp fɔ wan minit ɛn pɔynt insay; sɔntɛm na in bin drayv Dezi ɛn Gatsbi go na Ist Ɛg di nɛt we di aksidɛnt apin, ɛn sɔntɛm na in bin mek wan stori bɔt ɔl dis. A nɔ bin want fɔ yɛri am ɛn a bin de avɔyd am we a kɔmɔt na di tren.

A bin de spɛn mi Satide nɛt dɛn na Nyu Yɔk bikɔs dɛn pati dɛn de we i bin gɛt we bin de shayn ɛn we bin de mek a shayn bin de wit mi so klia wan dat a bin stil de yɛri di myuzik ɛn di laf, we nɔ bin de wok fayn ɛn we nɔ bin de stɔp, frɔm in gadin, ɛn di motoka dɛn we bin de go ɔp ɛn dɔŋ in drayv. Wan nɛt a bin rili yɛri wan matirial motoka de, ɛn a si in layt dɛn stɔp na in fɔs stɛp dɛn. Bɔt a nɔ bin du investayshɔn. Sɔntɛm na sɔm las gɔst we bin dɔn de fa na di ɛnd dɛn na di wɔl ɛn nɔ bin no se di pati dɔn dɔn.

Di las nɛt, we mi trɔnk pak ɛn mi motoka we dɛn dɔn sɛl to di pɔsin we de sɛl tin fɔ it, a go ova ɛn luk da big big we we nɔ gɛt wanwɔd we wan os nɔ bin wok wan tɛm mɔ. Na di wayt stɛp dɛn, wan dɔti wɔd, we sɔm bɔbɔ rayt wit wan brik, bin tinap klia wan na di mun layt, ɛn a ɛras am, ɛn drɔ mi sus raspingly along di ston. Dɔn a waka waka go dɔŋ di bich ɛn skata na di san.

Bɔku pan di big big shore ples dɛn bin dɔn lɔk naw ɛn i nɔ bin gɛt ɛni layt pas di shado, muv glɔ we wan fɛrɛbot de krɔs di Saund. Ɛn as di mun de go ɔp di os dɛn we nɔ impɔtant bigin fɔ mɛlt te smɔl smɔl a kam fɔ no di ol ayland we de ya we bin de flawa wan tɛm fɔ di Dutch selɔ dɛn yay—na fresh, grɛn brɔst fɔ di nyu wɔl. I tik dɛn we bin dɔn lɔs, di tik dɛn we bin dɔn mek ples fɔ Gatsbi in os, bin dɔn de pand wan tɛm wit wispa te to di las ɛn big wan pan ɔl mɔtalman drim dɛn; fɔ wan transitory enchanted moment man fɔ dɔn ol in briz insay di prezɛns fɔ dis kɔntinɛnt, kɔmpɛl insay wan aesthetic kɔntemplɛshɔn we i nɔ ɔndastand ɔ want, fes to fes fɔ di las tɛm insay istri wit sɔntin we kɔmɔn to in kapasiti fɔ wɔnda.

Ɛn as a sidɔm de de tink bɔt di ol wɔl we a nɔ no, a tink bɔt aw Gatsbi bin wɔnda we i fɔs pik di grɛn layt we de na di ɛnd pan Dezi in dok. I bin dɔn kam fa fawe na dis blu lawn, ɛn i go mɔs dɔn tan lɛk se in drim so nia dat i nɔ bin izi fɔ lɛ i nɔ ɔndastand am. I nɔ bin no se i dɔn ɔlrɛdi de biɛn am, sɔmsay bak insay da big dak ples de we de biɛn di siti, usay di dak fil dɛn na di ripablik bin de rɔl ɔnda di nɛt.

Gatsby bin biliv pan di grɛn layt, di orgiastic fiuja dat ia to ia de go bak bifo wi. I bin eluded wi da tɛm de, bɔt dat nɔ mata— tumara wi go rɔn fast, stret wi an dɛn mɔ... Ɛn wan fayn mɔnin—

So wi de bit on, bot dɛn agens di kɔrɛnt, we dɛn de kɛr bak we nɔ de stɔp fɔ go bifo.
