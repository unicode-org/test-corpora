“Chumi hnuah chuan i sam chu i rawng tawh a ni,” Jordan chuan a ti a, ka tan ta a, mahse hmeichhe naupangte chu casual takin an kal zel a, a thusawi chu thla hmasa ber, zanriah ang maia siam, rinhlelh rual lohvin, caterer bawm atanga siam a ni.
