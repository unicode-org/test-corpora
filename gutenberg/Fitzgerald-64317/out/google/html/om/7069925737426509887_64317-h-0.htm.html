“Yeroo nama kamiyyuu qeequun sitti dhaga’amu hundatti, namoonni addunyaa kanarra jiran hundi faayidaa ati argatte akka hin arganne qofa yaadadhu” naan jedhe.

Kana caalaa hin dubbanne, garuu yeroo hundumaa haala hin baratamneen karaa reserved ta'een wal qunnamtii qabna, kana caalaas waan guddaa jechuu akka barbaade hubadheera.
