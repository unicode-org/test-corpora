Jewe

Mu myaka yanje y’ubuto kandi y’ubukene dawe yampaye impanuro zimwe zimwe nariko ndazihindura mu muzirikanyi kuva ico gihe.

Yambwiye ati: “Igihe cose wumva ushaka kunegura umuntu uwo ari we wese, wibuke gusa yuko abantu bose bo muri iyi si bataronse ivyiza nk’ivyo wewe waronse.”

Ntiyasubiye kuvuga, ariko twama tuvugana mu buryo budasanzwe mu buryo buzigama, kandi naratahuye ko yashaka kuvuga vyinshi kuruta ivyo. Inkurikizi, ndakunda gukingira imanza zose, akamenyero kanyugururiye kamere nyinshi zo kumenya vyinshi kandi kantumye mba umunyavyaha w’ibintu bitari bike vy’abasirikare ba kera. Ubwenge budasanzwe buranyaruka kumenya no kwifatanya n’iyo kamere iyo bubonetse mu muntu asanzwe, ni ko vyagenze ku buryo muri kaminuza naregwa ata co ndi co ko ndi umunyapolitike, kuko nari nzi neza intuntu z’ibanga z’abantu b’ibikoko, batazwi. Vyinshi mu vyizigiro ntivyari birondewe—kenshi na kenshi naragira nk’uwusinziriye, uhagaritse umutima, canke ugira ububabare bw’urwanko igihe natahura biciye ku kimenyetso kinaka kitagira aho kigarukira ko ihishurirwa ry’inkoramutima ryari ririko rirahungabana ku musozi; kuko amahishurirwa y’inkoramutima y’abasore, canke n’imiburiburi amajambo bayaseruramwo, akenshi aba ari ay’ubusuma kandi akagira nabi n’uguhagarika bigaragara. Gukingira imanza ni ikintu c’icizigiro kitagira iherezo. Ndacari n’ubwoba bukeyi bwo kubura ikintu iyo nibagiwe ko, nk’uko dawe yabivuze, kandi ndabisubiramwo n’ubujuju, inyiyumvo y’ubugwaneza bw’ishimikiro igabanywa mu buryo butaringaniye igihe umuntu avutse.

Kandi, maze kwirata ubu buryo bwo kwihangana kwanje, ndashika ku kwemera ko bufise aho bugarukira. Inyifato yoshobora gushingira ku rutare rukomeye canke ku bishanga vy’amazi, ariko inyuma y’ingingo kanaka ntaco nshaka ko ishingiyeko. Igihe nagaruka mvuye mu Buseruko mu mpera z’umwaka uheze numvise ko nshaka ko isi iba mu mpuzu z’ishure kandi ikaba iri mu buryo bumwe bwo kwitwararika inyifato runtu ibihe bidahera; Nta n’ingendo z’umugumuko nari nshaka ko zisubira kubona mu mutima w’umuntu. Gatsby wenyene, uwo mugabo atanga izina ryiwe muri iki gitabu, ni we yasonewe ku kuntu navyifashemwo—Gatsby, yaserukira ikintu cose mfise agasuzuguro katagira ico kakozeko. Nimba kamere ari urutonde rudacitse rw’ibimenyetso vy’ukuroranirwa, rero hari ikintu ciza cane kuri we, ukuntu umuntu arushiriza gutahura amasezerano y’ubuzima, nk’aho yoba afitaniye isano n’imwe muri izo mashini zigoye cane zindika ivyinyugunyugu biri ku bilometero ibihumbi cumi. Ukwo kwishura ntaco kwari gufitaniye n’ukwo gushobora gukora ibintu biteye isoni kw’icubahiro kw’izina ry’“akaranga k’irema”—kwari ingabirano idasanzwe y’icizigiro, ukwitegura kw’urukundo nk’uko ntarigeze mbona mu wundi muntu uwo ari we wese kandi bishoboka ko ntazosubira kubibona. Oya—Gatsby yarasohotse neza ku mpera; ni co cari giteye Gatsby, ingene inkungugu y’ubuhumane yareremba mu ndoto ziwe yapfutse mu gihe gito ugushimishwa kwanje ku mibabaro itera inda n’umunezero w’abantu w’igihe gito.

Umuryango wanje waramaze imyaka itatu ari abantu bazwi cane kandi batunze muri iki gisagara co mu Burengero bwo Hagati. Aba Carraways ni ikintu c’umuryango, kandi dufise umugenzo w’uko twakomotse ku ba Dukes ba Buccleuch, ariko uwashinze umurongo wanje yari mwenewabo na sogokuru, yaje hano mu myaka mirongo itanu n’umwe, arungika uwuzomusimbura mu ntambara y’abanyagihugu, maze atangura ubucuruzi bw’ibikoresho vy’ubuhinga bwa none ku modoka ya dawe.

Sinigeze mbona uwo muvyeyi mukuru, ariko ntegerezwa gusa na we—nvuga canecane igishushanyo gikomeye cane kimanikijwe mu biro vya dawe. Narangije amashure yisumbuye muri New Haven mu 1915, haciye nk’igice c’ikinjana gusa dawe apfuye, haciye igihe gitoyi naragize uruhara muri iyo nzira y’ukwimuka kw’Abatewotoni yacerewe izwi ko ari Intambara Nkuru. Nararyohewe n’ico gitero co gusubiza inyuma cane ku buryo nagarutse ntaruhutse. Aho kuba ahantu h’ubushuhe bw’isi, ubu Uburengero bwo Hagati bwasa n’aho ari urubibi rw’isanzure ry’ikirere rwacitse ibice—ni co gituma nafashe ingingo yo kuja mu Buseruko kwiga ubudandaji bw’amabondi. Abantu bose nari nzi bari mu bucuruzi bw’amabond, rero niyumvira ko vyoshobora gufasha uwundi mugabo umwe w’umuseribateri. Ba sekuru na ba marume bose barabiganiriyeko nk’aho boba bariko bantora ishure ry’imyiteguro, maze amaherezo bavuga bati: “Kubera iki—ye-es,” n’amaso ateye ubwoba cane, afise amakenga. Dawe yaremeye kunfasha mu vy’amahera umwaka wose, maze inyuma y’ugucerezwa gutandukanye ndaza mu Buseruko, ubudasiba, ni ko niyumvira, mu mpera z’umwaka wa mirongo ibiri n’ibiri.

Ikintu gihambaye cari ukurondera ivyumba mu gisagara, ariko cari igihe c’ubushuhe, kandi nari mpejeje kuva mu gihugu c’ivyatsi vyinshi n’ibiti vyiza, rero igihe umusore umwe wo ku biro yampa iciyumviro co gufata inzu hamwe mu gisagara abantu baja ku kazi, vyari nk’iciyumviro ciza cane. Yasanze iyo nzu, inzu y’amakarata yakubiswe n’ikirere ku myaka mirongo umunani ku kwezi, ariko ku munota wa nyuma iyo sosiyete yamutegetse kuja i Washington, nanje ndasohoka nja mu gihugu jenyene. Nari mfise imbwa—n’imiburiburi nayifise imisi mikeyi gushika ahunze—n’umusaza w’umunya Dodge n’umugore w’Umufinlande, yariko arategura igitanda canje yongera arateka ivya mugitondo yongera yidodomba ubukerebutsi bw’Abafinlande hejuru y’icuma c’umuyagankuba.

Kandi rero n’umuco w’izuba n’ibiturika binini vy’amababi bikura ku biti, nk’uko ibintu bikura mu mafilime yihuta, nari mfise ukwo kwemera kwamenyerewe kw’uko ubuzima bwariko burasubira gutangura n’ici.

Naguze ibitabu cumi na bibiri vyerekeye amabanki n’inguzanyo n’ivy’ishoramari, maze bihagarara ku ruhande rwanje mu rangi itukura n’inzahabu nk’amahera mashasha ava mu nzu y’ubudandaji, bisezerana guhishura amabanga akayangane Midas na Morgan na Maecenas bonyene bari bazi. Kandi nari mfise umugambi munini wo gusoma ibindi bitabu vyinshi uretse ivyo. Nari ahubwo ndi umuhinga mu vy’ubuhinga bwo kwandika muri kaminuza—umwaka umwe naranditse urutonde rw’ibiganiro vy’agaciro cane kandi bigaragara mu kinyamakuru Yale News—kandi ubu nari ngiye kugarura ivyo bintu vyose mu buzima bwanje maze nongeye kuba uwo muhinga afise aho agarukira kuruta abandi bose, uwo “muntu afise ubushobozi bwo gukora neza.” Ivyo si epigramme gusa—ubuzima burarabwa neza cane umuntu ari mw’idirisha rimwe, n’ubundi.

Yari inzu y’uburaro ya Gatsby. Canke, ahubwo, nk’uko ntazi Bwana Gatsby, yari inzu y’uburaro yabamwo umugabo w’umunyacubahiro yitwa iryo zina. Inzu yanje bwite yari ikintu c’amaso, ariko cari ikintu c’amaso gitoyi, kandi cari cararengagijwe, rero nari mbona amazi, mbona igice c’ivyatsi vy’umubanyi wanje, n’ukuntu abatunzi b’amamiliyoni bari hafi bihumuriza—ivyo vyose vyari ku madolari mirongo umunani ku kwezi.

Hakurya y’ikiyaga c’ubugwaneza, ibihome vyera vy’i East Egg vyari biteye igomwe vyari bica ibibatsi ku mazi, kandi amateka y’icuma vy’ukuri atangura ku mugoroba natwara imodoka ng’aho kugira ngo nsangire n’aba Tom Buchanans. Umugabo wiwe, mu bintu bitandukanye yashitseko ku mubiri, yari umwe mu bantu b’inkomezi kuruta abandi bose bakinye umupira w’amaguru i New Haven—umuntu w’igihugu mu buryo bumwe, umwe muri abo bagabo bashika ku rwego rwo hejuru cane ku myaka mirongo ibiri n’umwe ku buryo ibintu vyose bikurikira bica bimera nk’ivy’agaciro gatoyi. Vyari bigoye gutahura ko umuntu wo mu gihe canje yari umutunzi bihagije kugira ngo akore ivyo.

Igituma baje mu Buseruko sinzi. Bari bamaze umwaka mu Bufaransa ata mvo idasanzwe, hanyuma bagaca bagendagenda ngaha n’aho ata ruhusha aho abantu bakina polo kandi bakaba abatunzi bari kumwe. Ivyo vyari uguhinduka kw’ibihe bidahera, ni ko Daisy yavuze kuri telefone, ariko jewe sinavyemeye—nta vyo nari mbona mu mutima wa Daisy, ariko numvise ko Tom yoguma agenda arondera, afise umubabaro mutoyi, umuvurungano ukomeye w’umukino w’umupira w’amaguru udashobora gusubirana.

Kandi ni ko vyagenze, ku mugoroba w’umuyaga ususurutse, natwara imodoka nja i East Egg kugira ngo mbone abagenzi babiri ba kera nari ntazi na gato. Inzu yabo yari mbere iteye igomwe kuruta uko nari niteze, yari inzu y’umutuku n’umweru y’umutuku n’umweru y’Abakoloni b’Abanyajeworujiya, iraba ku kiyaga. Ubwatsi bwatanguye ku nkengera y’ikiyaga maze bwiruka buja ku muryango w’imbere mu kiringo c’ikilometero kimwe ca kane, busimbuka hejuru y’amasaha y’izuba n’inzira z’amatafari n’amatongo yaturika—amaherezo igihe bwashika ku nzu buca bugenda buduga ku ruhande mu mizabibu ikayangana nk’aho bwari buvuye ku nkomezi z’ukwiruka kwabwo. Yari yarahindutse kuva mu myaka yiwe yo muri New Haven. None yari umuntu akomeye w’ubwoya bw’ivyatsi bw’imyaka mirongo itatu, afise akanwa gakomeye gatoyi kandi afise akaranga k’ugushira hejuru. Amaso abiri akeye y’ubwibone yari yarashinze ububasha ku maso hiwe kandi yamuhaye ishusho y’uko yama yihetse imbere n’ubukazi. N’ububabare bw’igitsina gore bw’impuzu ziwe zo gutwara ntibwari gushobora guhisha ububasha buhambaye cane bw’uwo mubiri—yasa n’uwuzuza izo nkweto zica ibibatsi gushika aho apfutse umugozi wo hejuru, kandi woshobora kubona umugwi munini w’imitsi uriko urahinduka igihe urutugu rwiwe rwagenda ruhinduka munsi y’ikoti ryiwe ry’umubiri mutoyi.
