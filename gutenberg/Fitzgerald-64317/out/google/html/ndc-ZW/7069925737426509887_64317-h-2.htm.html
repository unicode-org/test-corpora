Kwaiye kupfurwa ngekungoerekana, asi zvakadaro chimiro chemutori wenhau chainge chakarurama. Ainge azvichinja aine makore gumi nemanomwe uye panguwa yakanyatsojeka yakaona kutanga kwemushando wake —paakaona chikepe chaDan Cody chechidonhedza chikepe pamusoro pechikepe chakanyanya kujeka paLake Superior. Ainge ari James Gatz uyo ainge eihamba-hamba parutivi pegungwa masikati iwayo akapfeka jesi rakataturwa re green uye bhurukwa re canvas, asi ainge atova Jay Gatsby akakwereta chikepe chekukweva, akadhonza kuenda ku Tuolomee , uye akabhuyira Cody kuti mhepo ingamubata uye kumupwanya muhafu yeawa.

Ndinofunga kuti aive nezita rakagadzirira kwenguva yakareba, kunyange ipapo. Vabari vake vainga vandhu vacikacinja-cinja zve vainga vacikabudiriri mu munda—murangariro wake wainga acizivi kumbovaashira kamare inga vabari vake. Igwinyiso rainga ro kuti Jay Gatsby wo ku West Egg, ku Long Island, wakabva ku mavonero ake a Plato ngo pamusoro pake. Ainge ari mwanakomana waMwari —mutsara iwo, kana uchironza chiro, unoronza izvozvo zvega —uye anofanira kuva pamusoro pebhizimisi raBaba vake, mushando wekunaka kwakakura, kunonyangadza, uye kunonyangadza. Hino wakagadzira mhando ya Jay Gatsby iyo jaha remakore gumi nemanomwe rinokona kugadzira, uye kune pfungwa iyi wakagondeka kuguma kumagumo.

Kwe gore rinodarikira wainga eirova njira yake ngo kuguma ku ndau iri kumaodzanyemba ko Lake Superior inga muceri wo clam no mubati wo salmon kana mu basa rimweni raimuviisira zvokuha no mubhedhu. Muiri wake wakasvibira, wakaoma wakararama ngekuzviita kuburikidza ngemushando we hafu-unotyisa, hafu-une usimbe wemazuwa ekudzivirira. Wakaziya vakadzi kare, uye ngekuti vakamushaishira wakatanga kuvazvidza, vasikana vechidoko ngekuti vainga vasikazii, vamweni ngekuti vaiita zviro zvisina shwiro ngezviro zvaaizvitora zvisina shwiro mukudzifunga kwake kwakanyanya.

Asi mwoyo wake wainga uri mu hondo nguva jese, no nyonganyonga. Izvo zvakanyanya grotesque uye zvinoshamisa conceits zvaimushungurudza pamubhedha wake usiku. Nyika yeshe ye ineffable gaudiness yakazvitenderera mu ngqondo yake apo wachi yechirova pa washstand uye mwedzi wakanyorova nge chiedza chakanyorova ngubo dzake dzakavhiringika pashi. Usiku hwega hwega waiwedzera pane zvaaidakarira kuguma apo kudzorora kwakavhara pane chimwe chiono chakajeka nekumbundirana kusingazivikanwi. Kwechinguvana zviroto izvi zvakapa njira yekubudisa pfungwa dzake; vainge vari chiratidzo chinogutsa chekusava kwechokwadi kwechokwadi, chivimbiso chekuti buwe renyika rakadzikwa zvakachengeteka pamapapiro engano.

Kudakarira kubwinya kwake kwe mberi kwainge kwamutungamirira, mwedzi yakati kuti asati aenda, ku Lutheran College doko ye St. Wakagarapo mavhiki mairi, akavhiringidzwa ngekusaitira hanya kwayo kunotyisa ngengoma dzekuenda kwake, ngekuenda kwake, nekuzvidza mushando wemuchengeti wendau waaifanira kubhadhara ndiwo njira yake. Pasurepo wakadzokera ngo kukurumidza ku Lake Superior, zve wainga aciri kutsvaka ciro co kuita pa zuwa ro kuti ngarava ya Dan Cody yakadonhera ngarava mu ndau isizi kudzaka iri phedo no gungwa.

Kutengeserana mu Montana mhangura kwakamuita kuti ave mupfumi nguva dzakawanda kwakamuwana akasimba mumuiri asi ari padhuze nekuita pfungwa dzakapfava, uye, achifungira izvi, vakadzi vasingaperi vakaedza kumuparadzanisa nemari yake. 1902. Iyena wainga eihamba-hamba mundau dzeshe dzakanaka maningi dza Ella Kaye, mukadzi we pepanhau, wakaita Madame de Maintenon ku kushaya simba kwake, wakamutuma ku gungwa nge chikepe, zvainga zviro zvakajairika zvekutora nhau mugore ra 1902.
