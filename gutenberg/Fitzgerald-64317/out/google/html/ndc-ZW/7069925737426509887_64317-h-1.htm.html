III

Paiva nemimhanzi kubva kumba kwemuvakidzani wangu kuburikidza nehusiku hwekupisa. Muminda yake yebhuruu varume ne vasikana vaiuya vachienda sezvipuka pakati pekuzevezera ne champagne nenyeredzi. Pa high tide masikati ndaiona vaenzi wake vechidzika kubva mushongwe ye raft yake, kana kutora zuwa pajecha rinopisa re beach yake apo zvikepe zvake zviiri zve mota zvaitsemura mvura ye Sound, zveikwevera aquaplanes pamusoro pema cataracts e foam. Pakupera kwevhiki Rolls-Royce yake yakaita omnibus, yaitwara mapati kuenda nekupetuka dhorobha pakati pemapfumbamwe mangwanani nekureba pakati peusiku, apo ngoro yake yekugara yairumba-rumba inga chipuka che yellow chinokurumidza kusangana nezvitima zveshe. Uye nge Muvhuro vashandi vasere, kusanganisira mubindu wakawedzerwa, vaishanda zuwa reshe nge ma mops ne scrubbing-brushes ne nyundo ne garden-shears, vechigadzirisa zvakaparadzwa usiku hwakapfuura.

Cishanu cese mabhokisi mashanu o orenji no maremoni aiguma kubvira ku mundhu waitengesa michero ku New York—Muvhuro wese maorenji no maremoni aya aisiya pa musuvo wake wo sure mu piramidhi yo hafu jisina nyama. Mukichi mwainga ne muchini unokona kubudisa muto we maorenji mazana mairi mu hafu ye awa kudai bhatani doko rakadhindwa kazana maviri nge chigunwe chikuru che butler.

Kamwe chete pamavhiki maviri boka revanopa zvekurya raidzaka nemazana emamita echidziro uye mwenje dzakakwana dzemavara kuti dziite muti weKisimusa wemunda mukuru waGatsby. Pa matafura e buffet, akashongedzwa nge hors-d’oeuvre inopenya, hams yakabikwa yakashongedzwa yakadzara pamusoro pe masaladi e harlequin magadzirirwo uye nguruve dze pastry uye turkey dzakarowa nge goridhe rakasvipa. Muhoro huru kwakaiswa bhawa rainge nechitima chemhangura chaicho, uye rakadzara ngema gins ne doro uye nge cordials dzakariarwa kudhaya zvekuti vazhinji vevaenzi vake vechikadzi vainge vadoko maningi kuti vaziye umwe kubva kune umwe.

Nguwa ye nomwe orchestra yaguma, pasina ndaa yakaonda ye zvikamu zvishanu, asi gomba rose re oboes ne trombones ne saxophones ne viols ne cornets ne piccolos, ne ngoma dzakaderera ne dzakakwirira. Vashambi vekupeisira vapinda veibva ku beach zvino vari kupfeka zvepadera; motokari dzinobva kuNew York dzakaema shanu dzakadzaka mumuchovha, uye kare mahoro nema salons nemaveranda akapenya ngemavara ekutanga, uye bvudzi rakachekwa ngenjira itsva dzinoshamisa, uye shawls dzinopfuura zviroto zveCastile. Bhawa riri kuenderera mberi, uye ma cocktails anoyangarara anopinda mubindu kubanze, kuguma mhepo inorarama ngekubhuya ngekusheka, ngekutaura zvisina shwiro ngekutaura kwakakarirwapo, ngemisangano inodakadza pakati pevakadzi vasizi kumboziya mazita eumweni neumweni.

Mwenje inokura yakajeka apo nyika inotenderera kuretu no zuwa, zve wari orchestra iri kuridza mimhanzi ye yellow cocktail, zve opera yo mazwi inosimudza kiyi yakakwirira. Kusheka kuri nyore miniti ngeminiti, kunodururwa ngekuparadza, kunobudiswa ngezwi rinodakadza. Mapoka anochicha ngekukasira, anozvimba ngevatsva vanoguma, anonyungudika uye anoumba mukufema kumwe; kare kune vanodzungaira, vasikana vanozvivimba vanoruka pano nepapo pakati pe stouter uye yakagadzikana zvikuru, vanova kwenguva yakapinza, inofadza nguva pakati peboka, uye ipapo, vanofara nekukunda, vanotsvedza kuburikidza negungwa-kuchinja kwezviso nemazwi uye ruvara pasi pechiedza chinogara chichichinja.

Ngekukasira umwe weva gypsies ava, mu opal inotetemera, wakabata cocktail kubve mudenga, wakaikanda pashi kuti aite ushingi uye, eihamba ngenyara dzake kudai ndi Frisco, wakatamba kubanze ega pachikuva che canvas. Kunyarara kwechinguvana; mutungamiri we orchestra anosiyana rhythm yake obligingly kwaari, uye pane kuputika kwekutaura sezvo nhau dzisina kujeka dzinotenderera kuti iye ndiye Gilda Gray's understudy kubva kuFollies. Mabiko atanga.

Ndinotenda kuti husiku hwekutanga hwandakaenda kumba kwaGatsby ndaive umwe wevaenzi vashoma vainge vakokwa zvemene. Vandhu avacainga vakakokwa—vaienda kondhoyo. Vakapinda mumotokari dzakavabudisa kuenda kuLong Island, ngenjira imweni vakapeisira vari pamusiwo waGatsby. Vakagumayo vakaziiswa ngemunhu waiziya Gatsby, mushure mwazvo vakadzibata mukuwirirana nemitemo yekuzvibata inoenderana ne amusement park. Dzimweni nguwa vaiuya vachienda vasizi kumbosangana naGatsby zvachose, vaiuya ngendaa yemabiko ngekupfava kwemwoyo kwaiva tiketi rayo rekupindira.

Ndainge ndakokwa zvemene. Mutyairi wakasimira yunifomu ye robin’s-egg blue wakayambuka uswa hwangu mangwanani-ngwanani eMugoverwo nge tsamba inoshamisa yakarongeka kubva kumushandiri wake: kuremeredzwa kwaizova kwa Gatsby kweshe, yakati, kana ndikazoenda ku “pati yake doko” usiku ihwohwo. Ainge andiona nguva dzakawanda, uye ainge aida kundidaidza kare kare, asi kusanganiswa kunoshamisa kwemamiriro ezvinhu kwainge kwazvidzivirira —kwakasainwa ndi Jay Gatsby, ngenyara ine mbiri.

Ndakapfeka ma flannels machena ndakaenda kune uswa hwake zvishomani mushure mwemazuwa manomwe, ndikadzungaira ndisinganyanyi kugadzikana pakati pema swirls nema eddies eanthu andaisaziya—kunyazwi pano nepapo paiya ngechiso chandainge ndaona pachitima chekuenda kumushando. Ndakabva ndashamiswa ngekuwanda kwemajaha eChirungu ainge akapararira; veshe vakasimira zvakanaka, veshe vechiita inga vane nzara shomani, ne veshe veireketa nge mazwi akaderera, nge mwoyo wese kune vaAmerica vakagwinya ne vakabudirira. Ndaiva nechokwadi chekuti vaitengesa chimwe chinhu: mabhondi kana inishuwarenzi kana mota. Vainga veciziva maningi ngo pamusoro po mare isizivi kuoma yo mu ndauyo zve vaitenda kuti yainga yavo ngo ndava yo mazwi mashomanana mu kiyi yakarurama.

Pandakangoguma ndakaedza kutsvaka muenzi wangu, asi vandhu vairi kana vatatu vendakabvunza kuti aripi vakandiningira ngenjira inoshamisa, vokaramba zvikuru kuziya kwekuhamba kwake, zvekuti ndakaenda ndakaenda kune tafura ye cocktail—ndau imwe basi mubindu apo munhu ega aikwanisa kutarisa.

Ndainge ndiri munjira yekuenda kunodhakwa ngekunyara apo Jordan Baker wakabuda mumhatso akaema pamusoro pezvikwiriso zvemabwe anokosha, akasendamira sure zvishomani akatarisa ngekudakarira kunozvidza pashi mubindu.

Kuashirika kana kutama, ndakaona zvakafanira kuti ndizvibatanidze kune umweni ndisati ndatanga kubhuya mazwi anodakadza kune vanopfuura.

"Mwakadini!" Ndakadedzera, ndichienderera mberi ndichienda kune iye. Izwi rangu raiita kunge risina kusimba mhiri kwebindu.

“Ndaifunga kuti zvimwe uri pano,” akapindura asina kufunga apo ndakauya. “Ndakarangarira kuti waigara pedyo ne—”

Vakabata nyara yangu vasingaiti munhu, sechigondiso chekuti vaizondichengeta muminiti imwe, vakapa nzee ku ntombi mbiri dzakapfeka ngubo dzemapatya dze yellow, dzakaema pashi pe steps.

"Mwakadini!" vakachema vakachema vari pamwe chete. “Phepha hauna kukunda.”

Izvozvo zvainge zviri zvemutambo wegorofu. Ainge akundwa muma finals vhiki rainge rapfuura.

Umweni wo vasikane vakasimira nguvo yo yellow wakananga kuti: “Amuzivi kuti tiri ani, asi takasongana namwi pano mweji unokwana kuita umwe wakapera.”

“Wadhaya bvudzi rako kubvira apo,” wakadaro Jordan, inini ndakatanga, asi ntombi dzainge dzaenda mberi zvisina musoro uye kubhuya kwake kwaibhuyiwa kumwedzi usizi kuguma, wakabudiswa kudai nge chikafu chemadekoni, pasina kumboreketa, kubve mubhasikiti re caterer. Tainga no nyara ya Jordan yakatetepa yo goridhe yakazorora pa yangu, takadzaka ngo zvikwiriso zve takahamba-hamba mu munda. Tray ye cocktails yakatiyangarara mukati mwe madekoni, takagara pashi pa tafura ne ntombi mbiri dzakapfeka ye yellow nge vaisa vatatu, umwe nge umwe wakatiziisa kuti ndi Mr. Mumble.

“Munouya kumapati aya kazhinji here?” akabvunza Jordan musikana aive parutivi pake.

Musikana wakadavira, ngo izwi ro kungwarira ro kujithemba kuti: “Yo kupejisira ndiyo yondakasongana nayo.” Iyena wakatendeukira kuno mundhu waainga naye: “Azvicainga zvako here, Lucille?”

Zvainge zvaLucille, zvakare.

Lucille wakati: “Ndinodakarira kuviya. "Andizi kumboite ndaa nge zvendinoita, nge ndaa iyoyo ndinogara ndeiita nguwa yakanaka. Pandainge ndiri pano pakupeisira ndakatsemura ngubo yangu pa chigaro, wakandibvunza zina rangu nge kero—mukati mwe vhiki ndakawana pakiti kubva ku Croirier ine ngubo itsva ye madekoni mukati mwayo."

“Wakazvichengeta here?” akabvunza kudaro Jordan.

"Zvomene ndakazviita. Ndaizochipfeka usiku huno, asi chainge chakakura maningi muchifuva uye chaifanira kuchichaniswa. Chainge chiri chebhuruu yegasi ine mabheji e lavender. Mazana maviri nemakumi matanhatu nemashanu emadhora."

“Pane chimwe chinhu chinoshekedza pamusoro pemunhu uchaita chinhu chakadaro,” akadaro musikana umweni ngekudakara. “Haasi kuda chero dambudziko nemuviri chero upi zvawo .”

“Ndiani asingazviiti?” Ndakabvunza.

"Gatsby. Mundhu umweni wakandironzera—"

Ntombi mbiri idzi naJordan vakasendamira pamwe chete zvakavanzika.

“Mumwe munhu akandiudza kuti vaifunga kuti akauraya murume kamwe chete.”

Kudakadza kwakapfuura pamusoro pedu tese. Vatatu VaMumbles vakakotamira mberi vakateerera nechido.

“Andirangariri kuti zvakawanda maningi kudaro ,” wakaita nharo Lucille ngo kutama kugonda; “Zvinonyanya kuti aive musori weGermany panguva yehondo.”

Umwe wevarume akagutsurira hloko achisimbisa.

“Ndakazwa izvi kubvira ku mundhu waiziva zvese ngo pamusoro pake, wakakura naye ku Germany,” wakatigondisa ngo njira yakanaka.

“O, haiwa,” wakadaro nthombi yo kutanga, “azvingaiti kudaro, ngokuti iyena wainga ari mu hondo yo Amerika pa nguva yo hondo.” Apo kugonda kwedu kwakachinja kudzokera kwaari akasendamira mberi nekushingaira. “Unomutarisa dzimwe nguva paanofunga kuti hapana anomutarisa.Ndichabheja kuti auraya munhu.”

Vakadzima maziso avo vakadedera. Lucille wakavhunduka. Teshe takatendeuka takatsvaka Gatsby. Kwaiye chipupuriro chekufungidzira kwekudanana kwaakafuridzira kuti pane kuzevezera pamusoro pake kubva kune avo vakawana zvishoma zvaifanira kuzevezera pamusoro pazvo munyika muno.

Kudya ko kutanga ko madeko—kwaizova no kumweni pasure po pakati po usiku—kwainga kwava kupejwa wari, zve Jordan wakandikoka kuti ndiende ku boka rake ega, rainga rakapararira pa tafura ku divi rimweni ro munda. Pakanga pane vatatu vakaroorana uye muperekedzi waJordan, mudzidzi wepamusoro anoramba akapiwa kune mhirizhonga innuendo, uye zvakajeka pasi pepfungwa yekuti nguva pfupi kana gare gare Jordan aizomupa munhu wake kusvika pamwero mukuru kana mudoko. Panzvimbo yekutamba-tamba, bato iri rainge rachengetedza kufanana kunoremekedzeka, uye rakazvifunga mushando wekumiririra vakuru vakagara vekumaruwa-East Egg inozvininipisa kuWest Egg uye yakangwarira zvakanaka kubva kune spectroscopic gaiety yayo.

“Ngatibude,” wakazevezera Jordan, pasure po kuita hafu yo nguva yo kupeja nguva no kutama kufanera ngo njira imweni; “izvi zvakanyanya kuzvininipisa kwandiri.”

Takasimuka, uye wakapashangura kuti taizotsvaka mueni: Ndainga ndisati ndambomuona, wakananga, zve zvaindiita kuti nditame kugadzikana. Mudzidzi wepachikoro chepamusoro akagutsurira nenzira inonyangadza, inosuruvarisa.

Bhawa, apo takatanga kutarisa, rainge rakazara, asi Gatsby aisavapo. Akanga asingakwanisi kumuwana kubva pamusoro pezvikwiriso, uye aisava paveranda. Pa mukana umweni takazama musuwo waioneka wakakosha, tohamba kupinda mu raibhurari yakakwirira ye Gothic, yakavezwa nge oak ye Chirungu yakavezwa, uye ingadai yakatorwa yakakwana kubva kune imweni ndau yakaparadzwa mhiri kwemakungwa.

Murume wakagwinya, wezera repakati, wainge nemagirazi makuru ezizi, wakagara akadhakwa zvishomani pamupendero wetafura huru, akaningira ngekusagadzikana kwemashelufu emabhuku. Patakapinda wakatenderera ngekudakara akaongorora Jordan kubve kumusoro kuguma kumakumbo.

“Munofungei?” akabvunza achikurumidza.

“Ngezve chii?”

Akasimudza ruoko rwake akananga kumashelufu emabhuku.

"Ngezvezvo. Sechokwadi haufaniri kunetseka kuziva. Ndakaziisa. Izvo zvemene."

“Mabhuku aya?”

Akagutsurira hloko.

"Zvomene zvemene—zvine mapeji ngezveshe. Ndaifunga kuti zvingaite makadhibhodhi akanaka anogara. Nyaya yemene, ndezvemene zvemene. Mapeji nge—Heino! Lemme kukukhombidze.”

Ngo kutora kusagonda kwedu inga zviro zvicizivi kukosha, wakarumba kuenda ku mabhokisi o mabhuku wopetuka no Bhuku Rokutanga ro Stoddard Lectures .

"Vona!" akachema achikunda. "Ichi chikamu chechokwadi chekudhindwa. Zvakandinyepera. Murume uyu ndiBelasco wenguva dzose. Ikukunda. Kunyatsoita! Kuita zvemene! Waiziya nguwa yekuema, zvakare-hauna kucheka mapeji. Esi unode chiinyi? Unotarisira chiinyi?"

Iyena wakandibvuta bhuku rondhoro woribeka ngo kukasika pa shelf yaro, ecibhuya-bhuya kuti kudari cidhina cimwe cakabviswa, raibhurari yese inozowa.

“Ndiani akuunza?” akabvunza kudaro. "Kana kuti wakangouya here ndaunzwa vanhu vakawanda vaunzwa."

Jordan akamutarisa akangwarira, achifara, asina kupindura.

Iyena wakaenderera mberi eciti: “Ndakaunzwa ngo mwanakaji waizwi Roosevelt. "Madhamu Claud Roosevelt. Munovaziya ere? Ndakasangana navo kumweni usiku hwapfuura. Ndakadhakwa kweinenge vhiki zvino, uye ndakafunga kuti zvingandipetudza kuti ndigare muraibhurari."

“Ine ere?”

"Zvishoma, ndinofunga. Andizi kumboziva. Ndave pano kweawa imwe chete. Ndakakuudza nezvemabhuku? Ndeyechokwadi. Ndeye—"

“Ndiwe watiudza.”

Takamubata ruoko gravely ndokudzokera panze.

Pakanga paine kutamba zvino pachidziro chemubindu; varume vasharuka vanosundire vasikana vadoko sure muzvitenderedzwa zvisingaperi zvisina nyasha, vaviri vakakwirira vanobatana zvakaoma, ngenjira yefashoni, uye vechigara mumakona—ne nhamba huru yevasikana vasina varume vanotamba vega vega kana kurerutsa orchestra kwechinguvana mutoro we banjo kana misungo. Pakati peusiku hilarity yainge yawedzera. Tenor waipembererwa wainge eimba nge chiItaly, ne contralto wainge ne mbiri yakashata wainge waimba nge jazz, uye pakati pe nhamba anhu aiita “zviro zvinoshamisa” mubindu reshe, apo kudakara, kuseka kusina chiro kwaikwira kuenda mudenga remuchirimo. Mapatya maviri e stage, akazoguma ari vasikana vakapfeka yellow, vakaita mwana mudoko vakapfeka ngubo, uye champagne yakapiwa mumagirazi makuru kudarika finger-bowls. Mwedzi wainge wakwira mudenga, uye waiyangarara muSound waiva ne triangle ye zviyero zve sirivha, yeitetemera zvishomani ku drip yakaoma, ye tinny ye banjoes pa uswa.

Ndainge ndichiri naJordan Baker. Tainga takagara pa tafura no mundhu wainga no makore akafanana no mwanakaji mudoko waiita nharo, waisiya njira pa kunyangajwa kudoko ku kuseka kusikazi kudzingwarira. Ndainge ndozvinakirwa manje. Ndainge ndatora zvigunwe zviiri zve champagne, uye chiitiko chainge chachinja pamberi pemaziso angu kuita chiro chakakosha, chekutanga, uye chakadzama.

Pakunyarara kwekutamba murume akanditarisa ndokushekerera.

Iyena wakananga ngo ungwaru kuti: “Kuso kwako kunozivikanwa.” “Hauna kunge usiri muDivision Yekutanga panguva yehondo here?”

"Ngei hongu. Ndaive muInfantry yeMakumi maviri nemasere."

"Ndainge ndiri muSixteenth kusvika June gumi nemapfumbamwe-gumi nemasere. Ndaiziva kuti ndakakuona kumwe kare."

Takabhuya kwechinguvana ngendaa yemisha midoko yakanyorova, ine grey muFrance. Zviri pambhene kuti aigara mu ndau iyi, ngokuti wakandibvunja kuti ainga acandotenga ndege yo mvura, zve waizoeja kuieja mangwanani.

"Unode kuenda neni, mutambo wekare? Pedyo negungwa parutivi peSound."

“Nguwa ipi?”

“Nguwa chero ipi inokukodzera zvikuru.”

Zvainge zvava pamusoro perurimi rwangu kubvunza zita rake apo Jordan akatarisa-tarisa ndokushekerera.

“Kuva nenguva yengochani zvino?” akabvunza kudaro.

“Zviri nani zvikuru.” Ndakatendeuka zvakare kune mumwe wangu mutsva wandaiziva. "Iyi ipati isikazi kujairika kwandiri. Andizi kumboona muenzi. Ndinogara apo—" Ndakasimudza nyara yangu pachidziro chisingaoneki chiri kuretu, "uye muisa uyu Gatsby wakatuma pamusoro pemutyairi wake ngekukoka."

Kwechinguvana akanditarisa kunge akatadza kunzwisisa.

“Ndinozwi Gatsby,” wakabhuya ngekukasira.

"Kudini!" Ndakashevedzera. “Haiwa, ndinokumbira ruregerero rwako.”

"Ndaifunga kuti unozviziva, mutambo wekare. Ndinotya kuti handisi mugamuchiri akanaka."

Iyena wakasekerera ngo kuzwisisa—kupfuura kuzwisisa. Chainge chimweni chezvisekerero zvisingawanzoitika zvine unhu hwekuvimbisa kusingaperi mukati mazvo, kuti ungasangana nazvo nguva ina kana shanu muhupenyu. Yakasongana—kana kuti yakaita inga yakasongana—ne nyika yese isingaperi kwe nguva doko doko, yozosotesesa pauri ngo kusarura kusikazi kudziviswa mukuda kwako. Yakakuzwisisa kuguma pane zvawaida kuzwisiswa, yakatenda mwauri sezvaungada kutenda iwewe, nekukugondisa kuti yainge ine mufungo wako wemene wekuti, pakuita kwako kwakanaka, waikarira kuronza. Pa nguwayoyo yakapera—uye ini ndainge ndeiona mujaha wakaisvonaka, gore rimwe kana mairi kudarika makumi matatu, uyo wakanyatsojeka wekubhuya wakangoshaya kuita zviro zvisina shwiro. Nguwa imweni asati azvizivisa ndainge ndava nepfungwa yakasimba yekuti aisarudza mazwi ake ngekungwarira.

Pedyo nenguva apo VaGatsby vakazvizivisa butler wakakurumidza kuuya kwaari nemashoko ekuti Chicago aimudaidza pawaya. Iyena wakajirekerera ngo uta hudoko hwaisanganisira umwe nga umwe wedu ngo kutenderera.

“Kudari munoda ciro cikumbiranyi basi, mutambo wo kare,” wakandikurudzira. "Rusesa. Ndichazobatanazve ngesure."

Paainge asipo ndakatendeuka pakarepo kuna Jordan —ndakamanikidzwa kumuvimbisa kushamisika kwangu. Ndaitarisira kuti VaGatsby vaizova munhu ane maruva uye akasimba mumakore avo epakati.

“Ndiyani iye?” Ndakabvunza kudaro. “Unozviziya ere?”

“Anongova murume anonzi Gatsby.”

"Anobva kupi, ndinoreva? Uye anoitei?"

Iyena wakadavira ecisekerera maningi kuti: “Wari watanga ngo pamusoro po shwiroyo.” “Zvakanaka, akandiudza kamwe kuti aive murume weOxford.”

Mukuvo wo sure wakatanga kuvoneka sure kwake, asi paakabhuyazve wakapera.

“Zvisinei, ini handizvitendi.”

"Ngenyi hayiwa?"

Iyena wakasimbirira kuti: “Andizivi, ndinorangarira kuti aazivi kuendayo.”

Chimweni chiro muzwi rake chakandikarakadzisa mazwi emusikana umweni “Ndinofunga kuti wakauraya muisa,” uye zvakava nesimba rekusimudzira kuda kwangu kuziya. Ndaizoashira pasina kubvunza ruzivo rwekuti Gatsby akabva mumadhamu eLouisiana kana kubva kuEast Side yepasi yeNew York. Izvozvo zvaizwisisika. Asi majaha aazi—kunyazwi mukusaziva kwangu kwe dunhu ndaitenda kuti aazi—kukurumidza kutonhora kubva pasina ndau uye kutenga muzi weumambo pa Long Island Sound.

“Zvakanyadaro, iyena anoita mapati makuru,” wakadaro Jordan, ecicinja shwiro ngo kutama kudakarira simende yo mu dhorobha. "Uye ndinoda mapati makuru. Anobatana zvikuru. Pamapati madoko hapana chinyararire."

Pakave nekurira kwengoma ye bass, uye izwi remutungamiri we orchestra rakarira ngekukasira pamusoro pe echolalia yemunda.

“Madzimai nemadzibaba,” akachema. "Nge chikumbiro cha VaGatsby tinozomutambira mushando wa VaVladmir Tostoff wakapetuka, wakakwezva maningi vanhu ku Carnegie Hall muna May wapera. Kudai mukaerenga mapepa munoziya kuti pakava nekuvhunduka kukuru." Iyena wakasekerera ngo kujidodokesa, woengejera eciti: “Kudakajihwa!” Pasurepo vanhu veshe vakasheka.
