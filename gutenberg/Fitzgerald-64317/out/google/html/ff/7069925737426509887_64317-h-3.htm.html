“Eey, ko ɗum fof deeƴi.” Mi sikkaani. “Aɗa ɓuri moƴƴude ko arde galle, aɗa ɗaanoo.”

O ɓuuccii hoore makko.

“Micfo yicfi fadde cfoo haa Daisy lelna. Jamma jamma, dingiral 6ooyngal.”

O fawi juucfe makko e nder 6oggi makko, o ruttitii e 6ernde makko e 6ernde makko, hono no 6oornugol am ngol bonniri senngo 6oornugol ngol nii. Nii woni njah-mi, mi acci mo omo darii ɗoon e lewru – miɗo ndaara hay huunde.
