Mi waawaano yaafnaade mo walla yiɗde mo, kono mi yi’i ko o waɗi koo, e makko, ko goonga fof. Ɗum fof ko ŋakkeende hakkille no feewi, ko jiiɓru. Ɓe ngonaano yimɓe ɓe ngonaa hakkillaaji, Tom e Daisy—ɓe njuppii geɗe e tagooje, caggal ɗuum ɓe ngarti e kaalis maɓɓe walla e hakkilantaagal maɓɓe mawngal, walla ko woni heen fof ko jogori reende ɓe, ɓe ngoppa yimɓe woɗɓe ina laaɓtina mboros mo ɓe mbaɗi oo...

Mi 6uuccii junngo e makko; ina wayi no ko majjere waasde waɗde, sabu miɗo felliti ɗoon e ɗoon no miɗo haalda e cukalel nii. Ndeen o naatii e yeeyirdu jeewte ngam soodde 6aleeri perle – walla ma a taw ko 6aleeri tan – ngam ittude 6aleeri am e nder diiwaan hee haa abada.

Galle Gatsby ina woodi haa jooni nde njaltu mi ndee—huɗo e dow ŋoral makko ina juuti no am nii. Gooto e taksiiji wuro ngoo meeɗaa ƴettude njoɓdi rewrude e damal naatgol ngal tawa dartaaki minit gooto, hollirta nder; ina gasa tawa ko kanko riiwti Daisy e Gatsby to East Egg jamma aksidaa oo, ina gasa tawa ko kanko waɗi heen daartol makko fof. Mi yiɗaa nande ɗum kadi mi rewi e makko nde jippiimi e laana kaa.

Miɗo jooɗii jammaaji am aset ɗii to New York sabu ɗeen pijirlooji makko jalbooji, jalbooji ina ngondi e am haa laaɓi cer, haa miɗo waawi nande jimɗi e jaleeɗe, ɓuuɓɗe, ɓuuɓɗe, gila e jarde makko, e otooji ina njaha ina njippoo e werlaa makko. Jamma gooto mi nanii toon oto materiyel, mi yi’i fitirlaaji mum ina ndarii e 6oggi makko yeeso. Kono mi wiɗtaaki. Ina gasa tawa ko hoɗɓe cakkitiiɓe wonɓe e ŋoral leydi ndii, ɓe nganndaa ko fedde ndee joofi.

Jamma cakkitiiɗo oo, tawi boowal am ina ɓuuɓna, oto am ina yeeyee e jom suudu am, miɗo ndaara oon ŋakkere mawnde nde alaa ɗo haaɗi e galle gooto laawol gootol kadi. E dow tappirde daneere ndee, konngol 6urngol yoocfde, ngol suka gorko gooto 6uuccii e 6uu6ol, ina feeñi e lewru, mi momti ngol, mi 6uuccii paɗe am ina 6uuccii e dow haayre ndee. Ndeen mi 6uucciima haa les maayo, mi 6uuccii e dow njaareendi.

Ko ɓuri heewde e nokkuuji mawɗi daande maayo ɗii udditaama jooni, alaa ko woni heen so wonaa fitirlaaji ɓuuɓɗi, dillooji laana ndiwoowa ina taƴa Sound. E nde lewru ummii toowndu cuuɗi ɗi ngalaa nafoore ɗii puɗɗii ɓuuɓde haa seeɗa seeɗa miɗo anndi duunde ɓooynde ɗoo nde ɓuuɓnoo nde wootere e gite laana ndiwoowa Holanndee en – ɓuuɓri kesiri, ɓaleeri aduna keso oo. Leɗɗe maggal majjuɗe, leɗɗe ɗe mbaɗnoo laawol galle Gatsby, meeɗiino pande e nder ɓuuɓri haa e koyɗe aadee cakkitiiɗe e ɓurɗe mawnude; ngam wakkati enchanted transitory neɗɗo ina foti jogaade foofaango mum e yeeso ndee duunde, doole e nder miijo estetik ngo o faamaani, o yiɗaa, yeeso e yeeso ko adii fof e nder daartol e huunde fotnde e mbaawka makko e haawnaade.

E nde njooɗiimi ɗoon miɗo miijoo e aduna ɓooyɗo mo anndaaka, miɗo miijoo haaju Gatsby nde o fuɗɗii suɓaade fitirla kaaɗka to damal laana ndiwoowa Daisy. O arii ko juuti e ndee ɗoo huɗo ɓuuɓnde, koyɗol makko ina foti nanndude e ko o ɓadtii, o waawaa waasde nanngude ɗum. O anndaano wonde ko caggal makko woni, won ɗo o arti e oon niɓɓere mawnde, caggal wuro ngoo, ɗo gese niɓɓi republique ɗee njirlotoo les jamma.

Gatsby ina goongɗini annoore ɓaleere, futuro orgiastic mo hitaande e hitaande ina ustoo yeeso men. Nde rewi e men ndeen, kono ɗum alaa ko nafi—janngo en ndogat ko ɓuri yaawde, en njuurnitoo juuɗe men ko ɓuri yaawde... E subaka gooto moƴƴo—

Nii woni min piyi e dow, laanaaji e dow ŋoral, ndartinaa caggal ko aldaa e joofgol e nder ko yawti.
