Tebaali basanyufu, era tewali n’omu ku bo yali akutte ku nkoko oba ale —era naye era tebaali basanyufu. Waaliwo empewo etabuusibwabuusibwa ey’omukwano ogw’obutonde ku kifaananyi ekyo, era omuntu yenna yandigambye nti baali beekobaana bombi.

Nga bwe nnyiga ebigere okuva ku mbalaza nawulira takisi yange ng’ewulira ekkubo lyayo mu kkubo eririmu enzikiza ng’eyolekera ennyumba. Gatsby yali alinze we nnali mmulekedde mu mmotoka.

“Byonna bisirise waggulu awo?” bwe yabuuza mu kweraliikirira.

“Yee, byonna bisirise.” Nalonzalonza. “Kyandibadde kirungi n’okomawo awaka weebaka.”

Yanyeenya omutwe.

“Njagala kulinda wano okutuusa nga Daisy yeebaka.Ekiro ekirungi, omuzannyo omukadde.”

Yassa emikono gye mu nsawo z’ekkanzu ze n’adda emabega n’obwagazi n’agenda mu kwekenneenya kwe okw’ennyumba, ng’alinga okubeerawo kwange okwonoona obutukuvu bw’okukuuma. Bwe ntyo nnatambula ne mmuleka ng’ayimiridde awo mu musana gw’omwezi —nga talina ky’atunuulira.
