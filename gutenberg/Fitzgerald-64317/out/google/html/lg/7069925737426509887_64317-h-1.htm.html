III

Waaliwo omuziki okuva ewa muliraanwa wange okuyita mu kiro eky’omusana. Mu nsuku ze eza bbululu abasajja n’abawala bajja ne bagenda ng’enseenene wakati mu biwujjo ne ssampaagi n’emmunyeenye. Mu kiseera ky’amayengo amangi emisana nnalaba abagenyi be nga babuuka okuva ku munaala gw’ekiwujjo kye, oba nga bakwata enjuba ku musenyu ogwokya ogwa bbiici ye ng’amaato ge abiri aga mmotoka gasala amazzi ga Sound, nga gasika ennyonyi z’omu mazzi ku biwujjo by’amaaso ag’ekifuba. Ku wiikendi mmotoka ye eya Rolls-Royce yafuuka bbaasi ya buli kimu, ng’ekola obubaga ng’egenda n’okudda mu kibuga wakati w’essaawa mwenda ez’oku makya n’okusukka mu ttumbi, ate ng’akagaali ke aka siteegi kadduka ng’ekiwuka kya kyenvu ekidduka okusisinkana eggaali y’omukka yonna. Era ku Mmande abaweereza munaana, nga mw’otwalidde n’omulimi w’ensuku ow’enjawulo, baakola nnyo olunaku lwonna nga bakozesa mops ne scrubbing-brushes n’ennyondo n’ebisero by’olusuku, nga baddaabiriza ebisaanyizo eby’ekiro ekyakeesa.

Buli Lwakutaano ebibokisi bitaano eby’emicungwa n’enniimu byatuukanga okuva eri omulimi w’ebibala mu New York —buli Mmande emicungwa n’enniimu zino ze zimu zaavanga ku mulyango gwe ogw’emabega mu piramidi ey’ebitundu ebitaliiko bikuta. Mu ffumbiro mwalimu ekyuma ekyali kisobola okuggya omubisi gw’emicungwa ebikumi bibiri mu kitundu ky’essaawa singa akatono akatono kanyigibwa emirundi ebikumi bibiri n’engalo ensajja ey’omugabi.

Waakiri omulundi gumu mu wiiki bbiri ekibinja ky’abagaba emmere kyakka wansi nga balina ffuuti ebikumi ebiwerako eza kanvaasi n’amataala aga langi ezimala okukola omuti gwa Ssekukkulu ogw’olusuku olunene ennyo olwa Gatsby. Ku mmeeza za buffet, nga ziyooyooteddwa n’emmere ey’omulembe eyakaayakana, hams ezifumbiddwa mu by’akawoowo nga zijjudde ku saladi eza dizayini za harlequin n’embizzi za pastry n’enkoko enzungu ezilogeddwa okutuuka ku zaabu omuddugavu. Mu kisenge ekikulu mwateekebwawo ebbaala erimu eggaali y’omukka entuufu ey’ekikomo, era ng’ejjudde gin n’omwenge ate nga n’ebintu ebiyitibwa cordials ebyerabirwa okumala ebbanga ddene nga n’abagenyi be abakyala abasinga baali bato nnyo okusobola okumanya omu ku balala.

Ku ssaawa musanvu ekibiina ky’abayimbi kituuse, tewali nsonga nnyimpi ya bitundu bitaano, wabula ekinnya kyonna ekijjudde oboes ne trombones ne saxophones ne viols ne cornets ne piccolos, n’engooma eza wansi n’eza waggulu. Abawuzi abasembyeyo okuyingira okuva ku bbiici kati bambadde waggulu; mmotoka okuva e New York zisimbye ttaano mu buziba bw’okuvuga, era dda ebisenge ne saluuni ne bbalaza zaali zijjudde langi ezisookerwako, n’enviiri ezikubiddwa mu ngeri empya ezeewuunyisa, n’engoye ezisukka ebirooto bya Castile. Ebbaala eno eri mu ggiya, era lawundi za cocktails ezitengejja ziyita mu lusuku ebweru, okutuusa empewo lw’eba nnamu n’okunyumya n’okuseka, n’ebigambo eby’akaseera obuseera n’okwanjula ebyerabirwa mu kifo ekyo, n’enkiiko ez’obunyiikivu wakati w’abakyala abatamanyi mannya ga munne.

Amataala geeyongera okwaka ng’ensi yeekulukuunya okuva ku njuba, era kati ekibiina ky’abayimbi kikuba omuziki gwa cocktail ogwa kyenvu, era opera y’amaloboozi ekuba ekisumuluzo waggulu. Enseko nnyangu eddakiika ku ddakiika, nga ziyiwa obugayaavu, nga ziwulikika ku kigambo eky’essanyu. Ebibinja bikyuka mangu, bizimba n’abapya abatuuse, bisaanuuka ne bitondebwa mu mukka gwe gumu; waliwo dda abataayaaya, abawala abeesiga abaluka wano ne wali mu banywevu era abatebenkevu, ne bafuuka okumala akaseera akasongovu, ak’essanyu wakati w’ekibinja, n’oluvannyuma, nga basanyuse olw’obuwanguzi, ne baseeyeeya nga bayita mu nkyukakyuka y’ennyanja ey’amaaso n’amaloboozi ne langi wansi w’ekitangaala ekikyukakyuka buli kiseera.

Amangu ago omu ku ba gypsy bano, mu opal ekankana, akwata cocktail okuva mu bbanga, n’agisuula wansi olw’obuvumu era, ng’atambuza emikono gye nga Frisco, n’azina yekka ku pulatifomu ya kanvaasi. Okusirika okw’akaseera katono; omukulembeze w’ekibiina ky’abayimbi akyusakyusa ennyimba ze mu ngeri ey’okumukaka, era wabaawo okubutuka kw’okunyumya ng’amawulire amakyamu gagenda gatambula nti ye Gilda Gray’s understudy okuva mu Follies. Akabaga katandise.

Nzikiriza nti ekiro kye nasooka okugenda ewa Gatsby nnali omu ku bagenyi abatono abaali bayitiddwa mu butuufu. Abantu tebaayitibwa —baagendayo. Baalinnya mmotoka ezaababoola okutuuka ku kizinga Long Island, era mu ngeri emu oba endala baamaliriza ku mulyango gwa Gatsby. Bwe baatuukayo ne bayanjulwa omuntu eyali amanyi Gatsby, era oluvannyuma lw’ekyo ne beeyisa okusinziira ku mateeka g’enneeyisa agakwatagana ne ppaaka y’okusanyukirako. Oluusi bajja ne bagenda nga tebasisinkanye Gatsby n’akatono, bajja ku mbaga n’omutima omungu ogwali tikiti yaayo ey’okuyingira.

Mu butuufu nnali mpitiddwa. Ddereeva eyali mu yunifoomu eya bbululu w’amagi ga robin yasala omuddo gwange ku makya ennyo ku Lwomukaaga olwo ng’alina ekiwandiiko ekitongole ekyewuunyisa okuva ewa mukama we: ekitiibwa kyandibadde kya Gatsby kyonna, kyagamba nti, singa nnandibaddewo ku “kabaga ke akatono” ekiro ekyo. Yali andabye emirundi egiwerako, era yali agenderera okunkubira edda, naye embeera ez’enjawulo ezigatta zaali zigiremesezza — Jay Gatsby yassa omukono, mu mukono ogw’ekitiibwa.

Nga nyambadde engoye enjeru eza flannels nagenda ku muddo gwe nga wayiseewo katono ku ssaawa musanvu, ne ntambulatambula nga nfunye obuzibu mu buweerero wakati mu biwujjo n’ebiwujjo by’abantu be nnali simanyi —wadde nga wano ne wali waaliwo ffeesi gye nnali nneetegerezza ku ggaali y’omukka eyali egenda. Amangu ago nnakwatibwako nnyo olw’omuwendo gw’abavubuka Abangereza abaali babunye; bonna bambadde bulungi, bonna nga balabika ng’enjala ebaluma katono, era bonna nga boogera mu maloboozi aga wansi, ag’obunyiikivu eri Abamerika abagumu era abagagga. Nali nkakasa nti balina kye batunda: bondi oba yinsuwa oba mmotoka. Waakiri baali bamanyi bulungi ssente ennyangu ezaali mu kitundu ekyo era nga bakakasa nti zaabwe olw’ebigambo ebitonotono mu kisumuluzo ekituufu.

Amangu ddala nga ntuuse nakola okugezaako okunoonya omugenyi wange, naye abantu babiri oba abasatu be nnabuuza gy’ali bantunuulira mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa ennyo, era ne beegaana n’amaanyi mangi okumanya kwonna ku ntambula ze, ne nneekulukuunya mu ludda lw’emmeeza ya cocktail —ekifo kyokka mu lusuku omusajja omu mw’ayinza okumala nga talabika ng’atalina kigendererwa era nga yekka.

Nali ngenda okutamiira nga nwuuma olw’okuswala ennyo, Jordan Baker n’afuluma ennyumba n’ayimirira ku mutwe gw’amadaala aga marble, nga yeesigamye katono emabega era ng’atunudde wansi mu lusuku n’okwagala okunyooma.

Mwaniriziddwa oba nedda, nnasanga nga kyetaagisa okwegatta ku muntu nga sinnatandika kwogera bigambo bya mukwano eri abayita mu kkubo.

"Nkulamusizza!" Nawuluguma nga bwe ngenda mu maaso nga mmutuukako. Eddoboozi lyange lyalabika ng’ery’amaanyi mu ngeri etali ya butonde emitala w’olusuku.

“Nnalowooza nti oyinza okuba wano,” bwe yaddamu nga tafuddeeyo nga bwe nvaayo. “Nnajjukira nti obeera ku muliraano ne—”

Yankwata ku mukono nga talina muntu yenna, ng’ekisuubizo nti ajja kunfaako mu ddakiika emu, n’awa okutu eri abawala babiri abaali mu ngoye eza kyenvu bbiri, abaayimirira wansi w’amadaala.

"Nkulamusizza!" bakaaba wamu. “Musonyiwe tewawangula.”

Ekyo kyali kya mpaka za Golf. Yali ekubiddwa ku fayinolo wiiki emu emabega.

Omu ku bawala abaali mu langi ya kyenvu bwe yagamba nti: “Tomanyi kye tuli, naye twakusisinkana wano omwezi nga gumu emabega.”

“Okuva olwo osiiga langi enviiri zo,” Jordan bwe yagamba, era ne ntandika, naye abawala baali batambudde mu ngeri ey’akaseera obuseera era ekigambo kye kyali kigendereddwamu omwezi ogwali tegunnatuuka, ogwakolebwa ng’ekyeggulo, awatali kubuusabuusa, okuva mu kibbo ky’omugabi w’emmere. Ng’omukono gwa Jordan omugonvu ogwa zaabu guwummudde mu gwange, twakka ku madaala ne tutambulatambula mu lusuku. Tray ya cocktails yatulengejja nga tuyita mu budde obw’ekiro, ne tutuula ku mmeeza n’abawala bombi abaali mu langi ya kyenvu n’abasajja basatu, buli omu n’atuyanjula nga Mr. Mumble.

“Otera okujja ku mbaga zino?” Jordan bwe yabuuza omuwala eyali ku mabbali ge.

Omuwala bwe yaddamu mu ddoboozi ery’obutebenkevu ery’obwesige nti: “Eyo eyasembayo ye gye nnakusisinkana.” Yakyuka n’atunuulira munne nti: “Si ggwe Lucille?”

Yali ya Lucille, naye.

Lucille yagamba nti: “Njagala nnyo okujja. "Sifaayo ku bye nkola, kale bulijjo nfuna akaseera akalungi. Bwe nnasembayo okubeera wano nnayuza gomesi yange ku ntebe, n'ambuuza amannya gange n'endagiriro yange —mu wiiki emu nnafuna ekipapula okuva mu Croirier's nga kirimu gomesi empya ey'akawungeezi.”

“Ggwe wagikuuma?” Jordan bwe yabuuzizza.

"Sure I did. Nnali hhenda kugyambala ekiro kino, naye nga nnene nnyo mu bust era nga erina okukyusibwa. Yali gas blue nga eriko obululu bwa lavender. Ddoola ebikumi bibiri mu nkaaga mu ttaano."

“Waliwo ekintu ekisesa ku munne ekijja okukola ekintu ng’ekyo,” omuwala omulala bwe yagamba n’obwagazi. “Tayagala buzibu bwonna na mubiri gwonna .”

“Ani atakola?” Nabuuza.

“Gatsby. Waliwo eyang’amba—”

Abawala bano bombi ne Jordan beesigamye wamu mu kyama.

“Waliwo eyang’amba nti balowooza nti yatta omusajja lumu.”

Essanyu lyatuyitako ffenna. Mwami Mumbles abasatu baafukamira mu maaso ne bawuliriza n’obwagazi.

“Silowooza nti kinene nnyo nti ,” Lucille bwe yayomba mu ngeri ey’okubuusabuusa; “Kisinga kuba nti yali mukessi wa Girimaani mu kiseera ky’olutalo.”

Omu ku basajja yanyenya mutwe ng’akakasa.

“Ekyo nakiwulira okuva ku musajja eyali amanyi byonna ebimukwatako, nakulira naye mu Girimaani,” bwe yatukakasa mu ngeri ennungi.

“Oo, nedda,” omuwala eyasooka bwe yagamba, “tekyayinza kuba bwe kityo, kubanga yali mu ggye lya Amerika mu kiseera ky’olutalo.” Nga okukkiriza kwaffe kukyuka okudda ku ye yeesigama mu maaso n’obunyiikivu. “Omutunuulira oluusi ng’alowooza nti tewali amutunuulidde.Nja kuteeka bbeeti nti yatta musajja.”

Yafunza amaaso ge n’akankana. Lucille yakankana. Ffenna twakyuka ne tutunuulira Gatsby. Yali bujulizi ku kuteebereza kw’omukwano kwe yaluŋŋamya nti waaliwo okuwuubaala ku ye okuva mu abo abaali bazudde ebitono ebyetaagisa okuwuubaala mu nsi eno.

Ekyeggulo ekyasooka —wandibaddewo ekirala oluvannyuma lw’essaawa 12 ez’ekiro —kati kyali kiweebwayo, era Jordan yansaba okwegatta ku kibiina kye, abaali basaasaanidde ku mmeeza ku ludda olulala olw’olusuku. Waaliwo abafumbo basatu n’omuwerekera wa Jordan, omuyizi anywerera ku diguli esooka eyaweebwayo okukozesa ebigambo eby’effujjo, era nga kirabika wansi w’endowooza nti amangu oba alwawo Jordan yali agenda kumuwaayo omuntu we ku ddaala erisinga oba eritono. Mu kifo ky’okutambulatambula, ekibiina kino kyali kikuumye obumu obw’ekitiibwa, era ne kyetwalira omulimu gw’okukiikirira abakulu abanywevu ab’omu byalo —East Egg nga yeewaayo eri West Egg era nga yeekuuma n’obwegendereza ku ssanyu lyayo ery’embala.

“Ka tufulume,” Jordan bwe yayomba, oluvannyuma lw’ekitundu ky’essaawa mu ngeri emu ey’okubulankanya era ekitasaana; “kino kya mpisa nnyo gyendi.”

Twasituka, n’annyonnyola nti tugenda kunoonya omugenyi: Nnali simusisinkanangako, bwe yagamba, era kyali kintabula. Omuyizi eyasoma diguli eyookubiri yanyenya mutwe mu ngeri ey’okunyooma, ey’ennaku.

Ebbaala gye twasooka okutunula, yali ejjudde abantu naye nga Gatsby taliiwo. Yali tasobola kumusanga ng’asinziira waggulu ku madaala, era nga tali ku bbalaza. Ku mukisa twagezaako oluggi olulabika ng’olukulu, ne tuyingira mu tterekero ly’ebitabo erya waggulu erya Gothic, eryali liriko ebipande by’omuti gw’Olungereza ogwayolwa, era oboolyawo nga litambuzibwa nga liwedde okuva mu bifunfugu ebimu emitala w’amayanja.

Omusajja omugumu, ow’emyaka egy’omu makkati, ng’alina endabirwamu ennene ennyo ez’amaaso g’enjuki, yali atudde ng’atamidde katono ku mabbali g’emmeeza ennene, ng’atunuulira obusawo bw’ebitabo n’okussa ebirowoozo ebitali binywevu. Nga tuyingira yavuga nnamuziga mu ssanyu n’akebera Jordan okuva ku mutwe okutuuka ku bigere.

“Olowooza otya?” yasaba mu bwangu.

“Ku ki?”

Yawanika omukono gwe ng’ayolekera ebisenge by’ebitabo.

"Ku ekyo. Nga bwe kiri nti teweetaaga kweraliikirira kukakasa. Nakizuula. Ddala ddala."

“Ebitabo?”

Yanyenya omutwe.

"Ddala ddala — beera n'empapula ne buli kimu. Nnalowooza nti zijja kuba bbaasa ennungi ewangaala. Mu butuufu, za ddala ddala. Mpapula ne —Wano! Lemme show you."

Ng’atwala okubuusabuusa kwaffe ng’ekintu ekitali kikulu, yayanguwa okugenda mu bitereke by’ebitabo n’akomawo n’Olunyiriri Olusooka olw’Emisomo gya Stoddard .

"Okulaba!" yakaaba n’obuwanguzi. "It's a bona-fide piece of printed matter. Yansimba. This fella's a regular Belasco. It's a triumph. What thoroughness! What realism! Yamanya ddi lw'alina okuyimirira, naye—teyasala mpapula. Naye oyagala ki? Osuubira ki?"

Yanzigyako ekitabo n’akikyusa mu bwangu ku ssowaani yaakyo, ng’agugumbula nti singa ettoffaali erimu liggyibwawo etterekero lyonna liyinza okugwa.

“Ani yakuleetedde?” bwe yasabye. “Oba waakajja? Nze naleetebwa. Abantu abasinga baaleetebwa.”

Jordan yamutunuulira n’obulindaala, mu ssanyu, nga tazzeemu.

Yayongeddeko nti: “Nnaleetebwa omukazi ayitibwa Roosevelt. "Mukyala Claud Roosevelt. Omumanyi? Waliwo we nnamusisinkanye ekiro ekikeesezza olwaleero. Kati mmaze wiiki nga emu nga ntamidde, era nnalowooza nti kiyinza okunfuula omutebenkevu okutuula mu tterekero ly'ebitabo."

“Kikirina?”

"Katono, ndowooza. Sisobola kutegeera n'okutuusa kati. Nze mmaze wano essaawa emu yokka. Nkubuulidde ku bitabo? Bya ddala. Bili—”

“Ggwe watugambye.”

Twamukwata mu ngalo mu ngeri ey’amaanyi ne tudda ebweru.

Waaliwo amazina kati ku kanvaasi mu lusuku; abasajja abakadde nga basika abawala abato emabega mu nkulungo ezitaggwaawo ezitaliimu kisa, abafumbo abasukkulumye ku balala nga buli omu akutte munne mu ngeri ey’ekibogwe, ey’omulembe, era nga bakuuma mu makoona —n’omuwendo omunene ogw’abawala abali obwannamunigina abazina kinnoomu oba nga bawummuza ekibiina ky’abayimbi okumala akaseera katono ku buzito bwa banjo oba emitego. Mu ttumbi we bwatuukira ng’okusanyuka kweyongedde. Tenor eyali emanyiddwa ennyo yali ayimbidde mu Luyitale, ate contralto eyali emanyiddwa ennyo yali ayimba mu jazz, era wakati w’ennamba abantu baali bakola “stunts” mu lusuku lwonna, ate nga n’okuseka okw’essanyu, okutaliimu kintu kyonna kwasituka nga boolekedde eggulu ery’omusana. Abalongo ba siteegi, abaazuuse nga be bawala abaali mu langi ya kyenvu, baakola bbebi mu ngoye, era ssempeyini n’agabulwa mu giraasi ennene okusinga engalo. Omwezi gwali gusituse waggulu, era nga gulengejja mu Sound waaliwo enjuyi essatu ez’ebisusunku ebya ffeeza, nga zikankana katono olw’okutonnya okukaluba, okw’amabaati okwa banjoes ku muddo.

Nnali nkyali ne Jordan Baker. Twali tutudde ku mmeeza n’omusajja ow’emyaka nga nze n’akawala akatono akaali kakola effujjo, akaawaayo ekkubo olw’okuseka okutali kwa maanyi. Kati nnali nnyumirwa nnyo. Nali nfunye ebbakuli bbiri ez’engalo eza ssempeyini, era ekifo kyali kikyuse mu maaso gange ne kifuuka ekintu eky’amakulu, eky’omusingi, era ekizito.

Ku lull mu entertainment omusajja yantunuulira n’amwenya.

Yagamba mu mpisa nti: “Feesi yo emanyiddwa.” “Tewaali mu First Division mu kiseera ky’olutalo?”

“Lwaki yee.Nnali mu ggye ery’oku bigere ery’amakumi abiri mu munaana.”

"Nnali mu Sixteenth okutuuka mu June kkumi na mwenda-munaana. Nnali mmanyi nti waliwo we nnakulabye emabegako."

Twayogera okumala akaseera ku byalo ebimu ebitonotono ebibisi era enzirugavu mu Bufalansa. Kirabika yali abeera mu kitundu kino, kubanga yang’amba nti yali yaakagula ennyonyi ekika kya hydroplane, era yali agenda kugigezesa ku makya.

“Oyagala kugenda nange, omuzannyo omukadde? Just near the shore along the Sound.”

“Ssaawa ki?”

“Ekiseera kyonna ekisinga okukukwatako.”

Kyabadde ku nsonga y’olulimi lwange okubuuza erinnya lye, Jordan n’atunula n’amwenya.

“Okubeera n’obudde bw’ebisiyaga kati?” bwe yabuuzizza.

“Kirungi nnyo.” Naddamu okukyukira omuntu gwe nnali mmanyidde omupya. "Kino kabaga akatali ka bulijjo gyendi. Silaba wadde omugenyi. Mbeera eyo —" Nawanika omukono gwange ku bbugwe eritalabika ewala, "era omusajja ono Gatsby yasindika ku ddereeva we ng'ayita."

Okumala akaseera yantunuulira ng’alinga alemeddwa okutegeera.

“Nze Gatsby,” bwe yagamba mu bwangu.

"Kiki!" Nakuba enduulu. “Oo, nkwegayiridde okusonyiyibwa.”

"Nnalowooza nti omanyi, omuzannyo omukadde.Ntya nti siri mutegesi mulungi nnyo."

Yamwenya mu ngeri ey’okutegeera —okusinga nnyo okutegeera. Yali emu ku kamwenyumwenyu ako akatatera kulabika nga kalimu omutindo gw’okugumya emirembe gyonna, nti oyinza okusanga emirundi ena oba etaano mu bulamu. Kyayolekedde —oba kyalabika ng’ekyolekedde —ensi yonna ey’olubeerera okumala akaseera katono, n’oluvannyuma n’emalira ku ggwe n’obusosoze obutaziyizibwa mu bulungi bwo. Kyakutegeera nnyo nga bw’oyagala okutegeerwa, kyakukkiririzaamu nga bw’oyagala okwekkiririzaamu, era kyakukakasa nti kyalina ddala endowooza gy’oli nti, mu kiseera kyo ekisinga obulungi, gye wali osuubira okutuusa. Mu kiseera ekyo kyennyini kyabula —era nnali ntunuulidde omuvubuka omulungi ennyo ow’ensingo enkalu, omwaka gumu oba ebiri ng’asukka mu myaka amakumi asatu, ng’engeri ye ey’okwogera mu ngeri ey’ekikugu yasubwa okuba ey’obusiru. Nga tannaba kweyanjula nnali nfunye endowooza ey’amaanyi nti yali alonda ebigambo bye n’obwegendereza.

Kumpi mu kaseera kano omwami Gatsby we yeeyanjudde omuweereza w’emmere yayanguwa okumutuukako ng’alina amawulire nti Chicago yali emukubira essimu ku waya. Yeekwasa n’akataasa akatono akaalimu buli omu ku ffe mu kuddamu.

“Bw’oba oyagala ekintu kyonna kimala kukisaba, omuzannyo omukadde,” bwe yankubiriza. “Nsonyiwa. Nja kuddamu okukugattako oluvannyuma.”

Bwe yali takyaliwo nnakyuka mangu ne ntunuulira Jordan —nga nwalirizibwa okumukakasa nti nneewuunya. Nnali nsuubira nti omwami Gatsby yandibadde muntu wa bimuli era omugejjo mu myaka gye egy’omu makkati.

“Ye ani?” Nasaba. “Omanyi?”

“Musajja yekka ayitibwa Gatsby.”

“Ntegeeza ava wa? Era akola ki?”

Yaddamu ng’amwenya nti: “Kati otandise ku nsonga eyo.” “Kale, yang’amba lumu nti yali musajja wa Oxford.”

Ennyuma eyali efuuse enzirugavu yatandika okulabika emabega we, naye ku bigambo bye yaddako yaggwaawo.

“Kyokka, sikikkiriza.”

"Lwaaki nedda?"

Yakkaatirizza nti: “Simanyi, sirowooza nti yagendayo.”

“He’s a bootlegger,” abawala abato bwe baagamba, nga batambula awalala wakati wa cocktails ze n’ebimuli bye. "Olumu yatta omusajja eyali akizudde nti muganda wa Von Hindenburg era mujja wa sitaani owookubiri. Ntuukira ku rose, honey, onyiwe ettondo erisembayo mu giraasi eyo eyali eyo eya crystal."

Lumu nnawandiika ku bifo obwereere eby’enteekateeka y’ebiseera amannya g’abo abajja ewa Gatsby mu kyeya ekyo. Ye nteekateeka y’ebiseera enkadde kati, esasika ku bifo byayo, era ng’erina omutwe “Enteekateeka eno etandise okukola nga July 5th, 1922.” Naye nkyayinza okusoma amannya enzirugavu, era gajja kukuwa ekifaananyi ekirungi okusinga generalities zange ku abo abakkiriza Gatsby okusembeza abagenyi ne bamusasula omusolo ogw’obutebenkevu ogw’obutamanya kintu kyonna ku ye.

N’olwekyo, okuva mu East Egg mwe mwava aba Chester Beckers ne Leeches, n’omusajja ayitibwa Bunsen, gwe nnali mmanyi mu Yale, ne Doctor Webster Civet, eyabbira mu mazzi mu kyeya ekyayita waggulu mu Maine. Era aba Hornbeams ne Willie Voltaires, n’ekika kyonna ekiyitibwa Blackbuck, bulijjo nga bakuŋŋaana mu nsonda ne bafuumuula ennyindo zaabwe waggulu ng’embuzi ku oyo yenna eyasemberera. Era aba Ismays ne Chrysties (oba okusingawo Hubert Auerbach ne mukyala wa Mwami Chrystie), ne Edgar Beaver, enviiri ze, bagamba nti, zaafuuka enjeru nga ppamba akawungeezi akamu mu kiseera ky’obutiti awatali nsonga ntuufu n’akatono.

Clarence Endive yali ava mu East Egg, nga bwe nzijukira. Yajja omulundi gumu gwokka, mu knickerbockers enjeru, n’alwana ne bum ayitibwa Etty mu lusuku. Okuva ewala ennyo ku Kizinga kwajja aba Cheadles ne ORP Schraeders, ne Stonewall Jackson Abrams ab’e Georgia, n’aba Fishguards ne Ripley Snells. Snell yaliwo ng’ebula ennaku ssatu agende mu kkomera, ng’atamidde nnyo ku ‘gravel drive’ ne kiba nti mmotoka ya Mukyala Ulysses Swett yadduka ku mukono gwe ogwa ddyo. Aba Dancies nabo bajja, ne S. B. Whitebait, eyali asukka nnyo emyaka nkaaga, ne Maurice A. Flink, ne Hammerheads, ne Beluga omuyingiza taaba, n’abawala ba Beluga.

Okuva mu West Egg mwe mwava Abapolandi n’aba Mulready ne Cecil Roebuck ne Cecil Schoen ne Gulick senata w’Essaza ne Newton Orchid, eyali afuga Films Par Excellence, ne Eckhaust ne Clyde Cohen ne Don S. Schwartz (omwana) ne Arthur McCarty, bonna nga balina akakwate ne firimu mu ngeri emu oba endala. Era aba Catlips n’aba Bembergs ne G. Earl Muldoon, muganda wa Muldoon oyo oluvannyuma eyanyiga mukyala we. Da Fontano pulomoota yajjayo, era Ed Legros ne James B. (“Rot-Gut”) Ferret ne De Jongs ne Ernest Lilly —bajja kuzannya zzaala, era Ferret bwe yataayaaya mu lusuku kyali kitegeeza nti yayonjebwa era Associated Traction yandibadde ekyukakyuka mu ngeri ey’amagoba enkeera.

Omusajja ayitibwa Klipspringer yaliwo emirundi mingi nnyo ne kiba nti yamanyibwa nga “omuserikale” —mbuusabuusa oba yalina amaka amalala. Ku bantu abazannya katemba mwalimu Gus Waize ne Horace O’Donavan ne Lester Myer ne George Duckweed ne Francis Bull. Era okuva e New York mwalimu aba Chrome n’aba Backhysson n’aba Dennickers ne Russel Betty n’aba Corrigans n’aba Kelleher n’aba Dewars n’aba Scullys ne SW Belcher n’aba Smirkes n’abavubuka ba Quinn, kati abaawukanye, ne Henry L. Palmetto, eyeetuga ng’abuuka mu maaso g’eggaali y’omukka eya metro mu Times Square.

Benny McClenahan yatuuka bulijjo n’abawala bana. Tezaali ze zimu nnyo mu muntu ow’omubiri, naye zaali zifaanagana nnyo ne kiba nti tekyewalika nti zaali zibaddewo emabegako. Nneerabidde amannya gaabwe —Jaqueline, ndowooza, oba si ekyo Consuela, oba Gloria oba Judy oba June, era amannya gaabwe ag’enkomerero gaali amannya ag’omuziki ag’ebimuli n’emyezi oba ag’obukakanyavu obwa bakapiteeni abakulu ab’Amerika aba kojja baabwe, singa banyigirizibwa, be bandyatudde okuba.

Ng’oggyeeko bino byonna nsobola okujjukira nti Faustina O’Brien yajjayo waakiri omulundi gumu era abawala ba Baedeker n’omuvubuka Brewer, eyakubwa ennyindo mu lutalo, ne Mwami Albrucksburger ne Miss Haag, omugole we, ne Ardita Fitz-Peters ne Mwami P. Jewett, eyali akulira American Legion, ne Miss Claudia Hip, ng’omusajja amanyiddwa nti ye ddereeva, era omulangira w’ekintu, gwe twayita Duke, era erinnya lye, bwemba nga nnali nkimanyi, nneerabidde.

Abantu bano bonna bajja ewa Gatsby mu biseera by’obutiti.

Ku ssaawa mwenda, olunaku lumu ku makya ku nkomerero ya Jjulaayi, mmotoka ya Gatsby ennungi ennyo yasimbula mu kkubo ery’amayinja okutuuka ku mulyango gwange n’efulumya eddoboozi ery’omwanguka okuva mu jjembe lyayo ery’ennukuta ssatu.

Gwali mulundi gwange ogusoose okunkubira, wadde nga nnali ngenze ku mbaga ze bbiri, nga nnyimiridde mu nnyonyi ye ey’amazzi, era olw’okumuyita okw’amangu, nga nkozesa nnyo bbiici ye.

"Good morning, old sport. Olya nange ekyemisana leero era nalowooza nti tugenda kuvuga ffenna.”

Yali yeebalansiza ku daasiboodi y’emmotoka ye n’obuyiiya obwo obw’entambula obw’enjawulo ennyo obw’Abamerika —ekyo kijja, ndowooza, n’obutabaawo mulimu gwa kusitula mu buvubuka era, n’okusingawo, n’ekisa ekitaliiko kifaananyi eky’emizannyo gyaffe egy’obusimu, egy’oluusi n’oluusi. Engeri eno yali emenya buli kiseera engeri ye ey’okukwata obudde mu ngeri y’obutawummula. Tabangako mukkakkamu nnyo; bulijjo waaliwo ekigere ekikuba awalala oba okuggulawo n’okuggalawo omukono nga tolina bugumiikiriza.

Yandaba nga ntunuulira mmotoka ye n’okwewuunya.

“Kinyuma, si bwe kiri, omuzannyo omukadde?” Yabuuka n’agenda okumpa okulaba obulungi. “Tokirabangako?”

Nnali nkirabye. Buli omu yali akirabye. Yali langi ya kizigo ennungi, eyakaayakana ne nikele, ng’ezimba wano ne wali mu buwanvu bwayo obw’ekitalo ng’erina ebibokisi by’enkoofiira eby’obuwanguzi n’ebibokisi by’ekyeggulo n’ebibokisi by’ebikozesebwa, era ng’eriko ttereeza ng’eriko endabirwamu z’emmotoka eziriko endabirwamu z’enjuba kkumi. Nga tutudde emabega wa layers nnyingi ez’endabirwamu mu kika ky’ekifo ekikuumirwamu amaliba aga kiragala, twatandika okugenda mu kibuga.

Nnali njogedde naye mpozzi emirundi kitundu kya daziini mu mwezi oguwedde ne nkizuula, ekyannemalamu amaanyi, nti talina kitono ky’ayogera. Kale endowooza yange eyasooka, nti yali muntu wa kivaamu ekitali kitegeerekeka, yali egenda ekendeera mpolampola era yali afuuse nnannyini nnyumba y’oku nguudo eyali ekoleddwa mu ngeri ey’ekikugu eyali ku muliraano.

Era awo we waava okuvuga okwo okutabula. Twali tetunnatuuka ku kyalo West Egg nga Gatsby tannatandika kuleka sentensi ze ez’ekitiibwa nga teziwedde n’okwekuba ebikonde mu ngeri etasalawo ku kugulu kw’essuuti ye eya langi ya karamel.

“Laba wano, omuzannyo omukadde,” bwe yabuuka mu ngeri eyeewuunyisa, “endowooza yo ku nze, mu ngeri yonna?”

Nga nfunye akatono, natandika okwewala okwa bulijjo ekibuuzo ekyo kye kisaanidde.

“Kale, ngenda kukubuulira ekintu ekikwata ku bulamu bwange,” bwe yasalako. “Saagala ofune endowooza enkyamu ku nze okuva mu mboozi zino zonna z’owulira.”

Kale yali amanyi ebigambibwa ebyewuunyisa ebyali biwooma emboozi mu bisenge bye.

“Nja kukubuulira amazima ga Katonda.” Omukono gwe ogwa ddyo gwalagira mangu okwesasuza okw’obwakatonda okuyimirira awo. "Ndi mutabani w'abantu abamu abagagga mu Middle West — bonna bafudde kati. Nnakulira mu Amerika naye nasomera mu Oxford, kubanga bajjajja bange bonna basomerayo okumala emyaka mingi. Buno nnono ya maka."

Yantunuulira ebbali —era nnamanya lwaki Jordan Baker yali akkirizza nti yalimba. Yayanguwa ekigambo “yasomera mu Oxford,” oba n’akimira, oba n’akiziyira, ng’alinga eyamutawaanya emabegako. Era olw’okubuusabuusa kuno, sitatimenti ye yonna yagwa mu bitundutundu, era ne nneebuuza oba tewali kintu kitono ekibi ku ye, oluvannyuma lw’ebyo byonna.

“Kitundu ki eky’amaserengeta ga Middle West?” Nabuuza mu ngeri ey’akaseera obuseera.

“Ekibuga San Francisco.”

"Ndaba."

“Family yange yonna yafa era najja mu ssente nnyingi.”

Eddoboozi lye lyali lya kitiibwa, ng’alinga agamba nti okujjukira okusaanawo okwo okw’amangu okw’ekika kukyamubonyaabonya. Okumala akaseera nnateebereza nti yali ansika ekigere, naye okumutunuulira ne kinkakasa ekirala.

“Oluvannyuma lw’ekyo nnabeeranga ng’omuvubuka rajah mu bibuga ebikulu byonna ebya Bulaaya —Paris, Venice, Rooma —nga nkuŋŋaanya amayinja ag’omuwendo, okusinga amalusu, nga nyigga ebisolo ebinene, nga nsiigako katono, ebintu byange byokka, era nga ngezaako okwerabira ekintu eky’ennaku ennyo ekyali kintuuseeko edda ennyo.”

Nga nfuba nnyo nasobola okuziyiza enseko zange ezitakkiriza. Ebigambo byennyini byali byambaliddwa nga biwuzizza nnyo ne kiba nti tebyaleeta kifaananyi kyonna okuggyako eky’omuntu “omuzannyi” ow’ekitambaala ng’akulukuta obusagwa ku buli butuli ng’egoberera enjovu ng’ayita mu Bois de Boulogne.

"Awo ne wajja olutalo, omuzannyo omukadde. Lwali buweerero bungi, era nagezaako nnyo okufa, naye nga ndabika nga nnyiga obulamu obw'okuloga. Nakkiriza akakiiko nga first lieutenant bwe katandika. Mu kibira kya Argonne natwala ebisigalira by'ekibinja kyange eky'emmundu ez'ekika kya machine-gun mu maaso nnyo ne kiba nti waaliwo ekituli kya kitundu kya mayiro ku buli njuyi zaffe abaserikale abaali batambula n'ebigere gye batasobola kugenda mu maaso. Twasulayo babiri ennaku n’ekiro bibiri, abasajja kikumi mu asatu nga balina emmundu za Lewis kkumi na mukaaga, era abaserikale abaali batambula n’ebigere bwe baatuuka ku nkomerero ne basanga obubonero bw’ebibinja by’Abagirimaani bisatu mu ntuumu z’abafu nnakuzibwa ne nfuuka omukulu, era buli gavumenti y’omukago yampa eky’okwewunda —ne Montenegro, Montenegro entono wansi ku Nnyanja Adriatic!”

Omu Montenegro omuto! Yasitula ebigambo n’abinyenya mutwe —ng’amwenya. Akamwenyumwenyu kaali kategeera ebyafaayo bya Montenegro ebyalimu ebizibu era nga kasaasira entalo ez’obuzira abantu b’e Montenegro ze baakola. Yasiima mu bujjuvu olujegere lw’embeera z’eggwanga ezaali zivuddeko omusolo guno okuva mu mutima omutono ogwa Montenegro ogw’ebbugumu. Obutakkiriza bwange bwali bunyweredde mu kusikiriza kati; kyali ng’okuwuuta mu bwangu mu magazini kkumi na bbiri.

Yatuuka mu nsawo ye, ekyuma, ekyali kiwaniridde ku ribiini, ne kigwa mu ngalo zange.

“Oyo y’oyo ava e Montenegro.”

Ekyanneewuunyisa, ekintu ekyo kyalina endabika entuufu. “Orderi di Danilo,” bwe yadduka olugero olw’enkulungo, “Montenegro, Nicolas Rex.”

“Mukikyuse.”

“Major Jay Gatsby,” nnasoma, “Olw’obuzira obw’ekitalo.”

"Wano waliwo ekintu ekirala kye ntambula bulijjo. Ekijjukizo ky'ennaku za Oxford. Kyakwatibwa mu Trinity Quad —omusajja ali ku kkono wange kati ye Earl of Doncaster."

Kyaali kifaananyi kya bavubuka kitundu kya daziini nga bali mu bbulawuzi nga balooka mu kkubo eriyitibwa archway nga lirabika ekibinja ky’ebikondo. Waaliwo Gatsby, ng’alabika nga muto katono, si nnyo, —ng’akutte omuggo gwa cricket mu ngalo.

Awo byonna byali bituufu. Nalaba amalusu g’engo nga gaaka mu lubiri lwe ku Grand Canal; Namulaba ng’aggulawo ekifuba kya rubies okusobola okukkakkanya, n’obuziba bwazo obwali butangaavu obumyufu, okuluma kw’omutima gwe ogwamenyese.

“Ngenda kukusaba okunene leero,” bwe yagamba ng’assa ebijjukizo bye mu nsawo n’obumativu, “kale nnalowooza nti olina okubaako ky’omanyi ku nze. Saayagala mulowooze nti ndi muntu yenna. Yalonzalonza. “Ojja kukiwulira akawungeezi ka leero.”

“Ku kyamisana?”

“Nedda, akawungeezi ka leero. Kituuse okukizuula nti otwala Miss Baker ku caayi.”

“Otegeeza nti oyagala nnyo Miss Baker?”

“Nedda, omuzannyo omukadde, si bwe ndi. Naye Miss Baker akkirizza n’ekisa okwogera naawe ku nsonga eno.”

Nnali sirina kirowoozo kitono nti “ensonga eno” kye yali, naye nnali nnyiiga nnyo okusinga okufaayo. Nnali sinnasabye Jordan kunywa caayi okusobola okukubaganya ebirowoozo ku Mwami Jay Gatsby. Nali nkakasa nti okusaba kwandibadde kintu kya kitalo nnyo, era okumala akaseera nnasonyiwa nti nnali nfunye ekigere ku muddo gwe ogwali gujjudde abantu abasukkiridde.

Teyandigambye kigambo kirala. Obutuufu bwe bwamukula nga bwe tusemberera ekibuga. Twayita ku Port Roosevelt, gye waaliwo akabonero k’emmeeri ezigenda mu nnyanja eziriko emisipi emimyufu, ne tutambula ku sipiidi mu kitundu eky’omugotteko eky’amayinja nga kirimu saluuni enzikivu, ezitaliiko bantu ez’ebikumi kkumi na mwenda ebya zaabu ebyazikira. Awo ekiwonvu ky’evvu ne kigguka ku njuyi zombi, era ne ndaba Mukyala Wilson ng’anyiganyiga ku ppampu ya galagi ng’assa omukka nga bwe tuyita.

Nga tulina ebiziyiza ebiwanvuye ng’ebiwaawaatiro twasaasaanya ekitangaala mu kitundu kya Astoria —ekitundu kyokka, kubanga bwe twakyukakyuka wakati w’empagi z’ebigulumivu nnawulira “jug-jug- spat !” wa pikipiki, era omupoliisi eyali atabuse n’avuga ku mabbali.

“Kale, omuzannyo omukadde,” Gatsby bwe yayita. Twakendeeza ku sipiidi. Ng’aggya kaadi enjeru mu waleti ye, n’agiwuuba mu maaso g’omusajja.

“Oli mutuufu,” omupoliisi bwe yakkiriza ng’akuba enkoofiira ye. “Okumanyi omulundi oguddako, Mwami Gatsby. Nsonyiwa !”

“Ekyo kyali ki?” Nabuuza. “Ekifaananyi ky’e Oxford?”

“Nnasobola okukola kaminsona ekirungi omulundi gumu, era buli mwaka ansindikira kaadi ya Ssekukkulu.”

Ku bridge ennene, ng’omusana guyita mu bikondo gukola okuwuuma buli kiseera ku mmotoka ezitambula, ng’ekibuga kisituka waggulu emitala w’omugga mu ntuumu enjeru n’ebikuta bya ssukaali byonna byazimbibwa n’okwagala okuva mu ssente ezitali za kkolero. Ekibuga ekirabibwa okuva ku mwala gwa Queensboro bulijjo kye kibuga ekirabibwa omulundi ogusoose, mu kisuubizo kyakyo eky’ensiko ekisoose eky’ebyama byonna n’obulungi mu nsi.

Omufu yatuyitako mu mmotoka y’abafu eyabadde etumbidde ebimuli, n’addirirwa ebigaali bibiri ebiriko ebizibiti ebisimbuddwa, n’ebigaali ebirala eby’essanyu eby’emikwano. Mikwano gyatutunuulira n’amaaso ag’ennaku n’emimwa emimpi egy’okungulu egy’obugwanjuba bw’obuvanjuba bwa Bulaaya, era nnasanyuka nti okulaba mmotoka ya Gatsby ennungi ennyo kwali mu luwummula lwabwe olw’ennaku. Nga tusala ekizinga kya Blackwell limousine yatuyitako, ng’evugirwa ddereeva omuzungu, nga mu yo mwatudde abaddugavu basatu ab’omulembe, ssente bbiri n’omuwala. Naseka nnyo ng’ensaano z’amaaso gaabwe ziyiringisibwa nga zitutuukako mu kuvuganya okw’amalala.

“Ekintu kyonna kisobola okubaawo kati nga tuseeredde ku bridge eno,” bwe nnalowooza; “ekintu kyonna n’akatono...”

Ne Gatsby yali asobola okubaawo, awatali kwewuunya kwonna kwonna.

Okuwuuma emisana. Mu kiyumba ky’omu ttaka ekya Forty-second Street ekyali kifuuwa empewo ennungi nasisinkana Gatsby okulya ekyemisana. Nga nkuba amaaso ku musana gw’oluguudo olwali ebweru, amaaso gange gaamulonda mu ngeri etategeerekeka mu kisenge eky’omu maaso, ng’ayogera n’omusajja omulala.

“Mwami Carraway, ono ye mukwano gwange Mwami Wolfshiem.”

Omuyudaaya omutono, ow’ennyindo empanvu yasitula omutwe gwe omunene n’antunuulira n’enviiri bbiri ennungi ezaali zikula mu nnyindo zonna. Oluvannyuma lw’akaseera katono nazuula amaaso ge amatonotono mu kitundu ky’ekizikiza.

“—Bwe ntyo nnamutunuulira omulundi gumu,” bwatyo Mwami Wolfshiem bwe yagamba ng’ansika omukono n’obunyiikivu, “era olowooza nnakola ki?”

"Kiki?" Nabuuza mu mpisa.

Naye kirabika yali tanjogerako, kubanga yasuula omukono gwange n’abikka Gatsby n’ennyindo ye eyali eraga.

“Ssente nazikwasa Katspaugh ne mmugamba nti: ‘Kale Katspaugh, tomusasula wadde ennusu okutuusa lw’azibye akamwa.’ Yagiggalawo olwo ne awo.”

Gatsby yakwata omukono gwa buli omu ku ffe n’agenda mu maaso n’ayingira mu dduuka, olwo omwami Wolfshiem n’amira sentensi empya gye yali atandise n’agwa mu ‘somnambulatory abstraction’.

“Emipiira egya waggulu?” omuweereza omukulu bwe yabuuzizza.

“Kino kya dduuka kirungi wano,” bwatyo Mwami Wolfshiem bwe yagamba ng’atunuulira ennyama z’aba presbyterian ku ssilingi. “Naye njagala nnyo emitala w’ekkubo!”

“Yee, emipiira egy’amaanyi,” Gatsby bwe yakkiriza, n’oluvannyuma eri Mwami Wolfshiem nti: “Eyo eyo eyokya nnyo.”

Mwami Wolfshiem yagamba nti: “Ebyokya era bitono —ye, naye nga bijjudde ebijjukizo.”

“Ekyo kifo ki?” Nabuuza.

“Ekifo ekikadde ekiyitibwa Metropole.”

“Metropole enkadde,” Mwami Wolfshiem bwe yalowooza mu ngeri ey’ennaku. "Ejjudde ffeesi ezifudde era nga zigenze. Nga zijjudde emikwano egyagenze kati emirembe gyonna. Siyinza kwerabira kasita mbeera ekiro kye baakubye Rosy Rosenthal amasasi eyo. Twali mukaaga ku mmeeza, era Rosy yali alya n'okunywa nnyo akawungeezi konna. Bwe bwali bunaatera okukya omuweereza n'amutuukako n'akafaananyi akasesa n'agamba nti waliwo ayagala okwogera naye ebweru. 'All right,' bw'agamba Rosy, n’atandika okusituka, ne mmusika wansi mu ntebe ye.

“ ‘Leka abasiru bayingire wano bwe baba bakwagala, Rosy, naye tokola, kale nyamba, senguka ebweru w’ekisenge kino.’

“Mu kiseera ekyo zaali ssaawa nnya ez’oku makya, era singa twasitula ebizibiti twali tulabye omusana.”

“Yagenda?” Nabuuza nga sirina musango.

“Mazima ddala yagenda.” Ennyindo ya Mwami Wolfshiem yanzigya mu busungu. “Yakyuka mu mulyango n’agamba nti: ‘Tokkiriza omuweereza oyo kunzigyako kaawa wange!’ Awo n’afuluma ku mabbali g’ekkubo, ne bamukuba amasasi asatu mu lubuto lwe olujjudde ne bavuga ne bagenda.”

Nga nzijukira nti: “Bana ku bo baakubwa amasannyalaze.”

“Ettaano, ne Becker.” Ennyindo ze zaankyukira mu ngeri ey’okwagala. “Ntegedde nti onoonya business gonnegtion.”

Okukwataganya ebigambo bino ebibiri kwali kwewuunyisa. Gatsby yanziramu nti:

Yayomba nti: “Oo, nedda, ono si ye musajja.”

"Nedda?" Mwami Wolfshiem yalabika nga yennyamidde.

"Ono mukwano gwokka.Nkugambye nti ekyo tujja kukyogerako omulundi omulala."

Mwami Wolfshiem yagamba nti: “Nkusaba okusonyiwa, nnalina omusajja omukyamu.”

Hash omubisi yatuuka, era Mwami Wolfshiem, nga yeerabira embeera eyali esinga okubeera ey’okuwulira mu Metropole enkadde, n’atandika okulya n’obuwoomi obw’obukambwe. Mu kiseera ekyo, amaaso ge gaali gatambula mpola nnyo okwetoloola ekisenge kyonna —yamaliriza arc ng’akyuka okwekenneenya abantu abaali emabega butereevu. Ndowooza nti, okuggyako okubeerawo kwange, yandibadde atunudde mu bumpimpi wansi w’emmeeza yaffe.

“Laba wano, omuzannyo omukadde,” Gatsby bwe yagamba ng’anneesigamye, “Ntya nti nakunyiiza katono enkya ya leero mu mmotoka.”

Waaliwo akamwenyumwenyu nate, naye ku mulundi guno nnagunywerera.

“Siyagala byama,” bwe nnamuddamu, “era sitegeera lwaki tojja kuvaayo mu bwesimbu n’ontegeeza ky’oyagala.Lwaki byonna birina okuyita mu Miss Baker?”

“Oo, si kintu kya wansi,” bwe yankakasa. “Miss Baker muzannyi wa mizannyo munene, omanyi, era teyandikoze kintu kyonna ekitali kirungi.”

Amangu ago yatunuulira essaawa ye, n’abuuka, n’ayanguwa okuva mu kisenge, n’andeka ne Mwami Wolfshiem ku mmeeza.

“Alina okukuba essimu,” Mwami Wolfshiem bwe yagamba ng’amugoberera n’amaaso ge. “Fine fellow, si bwe kiri? Mulungi okutunuulira ate nga musajja mugezigezi atuukiridde.”

"Yee."
