VIII

Acopo nindo ngo dyewor zoo; foghorn ubedo ka koko iwi Sound, man abedo ka ngwec ikind lemandha ma rac man lek ma lworo. I diewor awinjo taxi ubecidho i Gatsby, man ndhundhu ne awok kud i kavutu man acaku kendi—awinjo nya atiye ku lembe moku ma umitu awac ire, lembe moko ma umitu alok ire, man uthieno bibedo cen.

Ka awok kud i lum pare, aneno nia dhugola pare mi kore podi oyabere man ebedo ka cung iwi meza i ot, ma epong ku can man nindo.

"Piny moko epe," ewacu ku lworo. "Akuru, man i sawa angwen ebino i wang dirisa man ecungu keca pi dakika acel man edham etho mac."

Ot pare nangu nen ungo ni ot madit ira calu ma nen kudu dyewor eca ma wabeyenyo i ot madongo pi nwangu taba. Waweko kor ot ma nen calu pavilions, man wawotho iwi futi madwong pa ot macol pi loko mac mi mac—wang acel apodho ku pii iwi cing piano ma nen calu tipo. Ubedo ku buru madwong kama ceke, man ot ne ubedo ku lum, calu nangu jubedu ngo ku yamu pi nindu madwong. Anwango humidor iwi meza moko ma angeyu ngo, ku taba ario ma muthwo iiye. Wabolo wang dirisa mi ot mi bedo, wabedo ka madhu taba woko i mudho.

"Umitu icidh," awacu. “Etie lemandha nia gibi lubo tok mutukari peri.”

“Cidh kawoni , tuko macon?”

"Cidhi i Atlantic City pi yenga acel, nyo i Montreal."

Ecopo paru ngo. Ecopo weko ngo Daisy nitundu engeyo ango ma ebecitimo. Ebedo ka mako gen moko ma tok cen man nangu acopu ngo ni yenge.

Ubedo dyewor eni ma en uwacu ira lembe ma nyang ikum kwo pare ku Dan Cody—ewacu ira pilembe “Jay Gatsby” utur calu kopo ikum Tom ma rac, man lembe ma dhanu ungiyu ikume ugam utimere. Abe paru niya ecopo yio lembe moku ci kawoni, labongo lworo, ento emito ewec ikum Daisy.

Ebedo nyaku mir acel ma "ber" ma engeyo. I yore matung tung ma ungiyu ngo, ebed enwango dhanu ma kumeno, ento kare ku kare ku waya ma nyang ungo ikindgi. Enwangu nya eber mandha. Ecidho i ot pare, mir acel ku judongo mukende kud i Camp Taylor, i ngeye kende. Egam eworo—ebedo ngo i ot maber kumeno con. Ento gin ma umiyu ire lworo, ubedu nya Daisy ukwo keca—ebedo piny ma yot ire calu hema pare woko i kambi ubedu ire. Ubedo ku lembe moko ma dhanu ungiyu ngo ikume, nen pa ot ma malu ma leng man ma ngic uloyu ma sagu ot mukende, pa timo mi co ku mon man ma leng ma ubetimere i dhugola pare, man pa mar ma ubedu ngo macol man ma juketho i lavender ento manyen man ma yuyu man ma nyai pa mutukari ma rieny pa oro maeni man myel pa thiwe ma upong ku lum. Egam enyayu anyonga i iye, bende, nia coo madwong ugam umaru Daisy—emedire beru pare i wange. Ewinjo bedo migi i ot, ma upongu yamu ku tipo man dwand pa paru ma podi ucungo.

Ento engeyo niya etiye i ot pa Daisy pi peko madit. Kadi bende anyim pare calu Jay Gatsby copo bedu ber, kawoni ebedo nyathin ma epe ku sente ma epe ku kwo mukadho, man saa moko ci bongo pare ma nen ungo copo wok kud iwi bege. Pieno, etio ku saa pare cuu mandha. Egam etingo piny ma ecopu nwangu, ku cwiny ma tek man ma rwinyo—i ajiki egam etingo Daisy dyewor acel mi dwi mir apar, egam etingo pilembe ebedo ungo ku twero mi mulo cinge.

Ecopu bedu nya ebedo ku lworo i kume gire, kum egam etingo nyaku ne i the lembe mi ndra. Abeweco ngo niya edaru timo kuloka iwi phantom millions pare, ento edaru miyo Daisy bedo maber; eweku ebed ku yioyic nia etiye ngati ma aa kud i rwom marom ku pare—nya ecopo gwoko kwo pare. I andha, ebedo ungo ku piny ma kumeno—ebedo ungo ku juru ot pare maber ma ucungu inge, man ebedo ku twero ikum paru pa gavumente ma dhanu ope i iye ni juwodhe kama moku ci i ng'om.

Ento ebedo ngo ku lworo i kume gire man etimere ngo kite ma eparu kudu. Ebed eparu, nyalo bedu, mi kwanyu piny ma ecopu man ecidh—ento kawoni enwangu niya edaru ketho kwo pare ni lubo tok lembe moko. Engeyo niya Daisy ubedo ngati ma pire tek, ento engeyo ngo kite ma nyaku maber copo bedu kudu. Elar i ot pare ma lonyo, i kwo pare ma lonyo, man eweko Gatsby—piny moko mbe. Ewinjo nya egamu dhaku ne, eno kende.

Kinde ma girombo kendu, ingey nindu aryo, ubedu Gatsby ma ubedu ka yweyo, ma ubedu, yore moku, juyike. Bara pare ubedo maleng ku piny maber ma juwilo pa star-shine; wicker pa settee ugam ukoc maber ikare ma elokore i bange man egam edham dhumo dhoge ma leng man ma maru. Egam enwango two ngico, man eweko dwal pare doko tek man ma leng uloyu, man Gatsby ungiyu lembe ma lee dit ikum nyithindho man lembe ma dhanu ungiyu ngo ma lonyo mako man gwoko, ikum leng pa kendi madwong, man ikum Daisy, ma ryeny calu fedha, ma ber man ma cwinye yom iwi lwiny ma lyeth pa jurucan.

"Acopo ngo ni titu iri kite ma cwinya utal kudu ikare ma anwangu niya amare, tuko macon. Abedo ku gen pi kare moko nya ecopo bolo an, ento etimo ngo, kum en de emaru an. Eparu niya angeyo lembe madwong pilembe angeyo lembe matung tung kude... Ber, abedo keca, mabor ku paru para, abedo i mar ma thuc kubang dakika, man ango ango ma atimo. tic mi timo lembe madongo tek nangu acopo bedu ku saa maber mi tito ire ango ma abecitimo?

I diewor ma tok cen ma podi ecidho ngo woko, ebedo ku Daisy i cinge pi kare malac man ling. Ubedo ceng ma ngic, ku mac i ot man wange ubedo makwar. Kare ku kare ebed ewotho man eloko cinge nyanok, man wang acel edwoko yuk wic pare macol ma rieny. I diewor uketho gibedo ku kwiocwiny pi kare manok, calu nya ebemio igi poyo wic ma thuc pi poko ceng ma lubo ma juciko. Gibedu ngo karacelu i dwi migi mi mar, man de giweco ngo maber ikindgi, ma sagu kinde ma nyaku ne ucwalu dhoge ma ling ikum koti pare nyo ka nyaku ne umulo cinge, moth, calu nyaku ne enindo.

Etimo maber akeca i lwiny. Ebedo jatel wi mony ma podi ecidho ngo i wang lwiny, man ingey lwiny mi Argonne enwangu rwom pare man telowic pa divisional machine-guns. I ngei thum pa lwiny, etembo matek ni dok i pacu, ento peko moku nyo nyang ikum lembe moko ucwale i Oxford. Ebedo ku lworo kawoni—ebedo ku kit lworo moko i waraga pa Daisy. Eneno ngo pirango ma ecopu bino ngo. Ebed ewinjo peko pa ng'om ma woko, man emito enene man ewinj bedo pare ingete man ebed ku genogen nia ebetimo lembe maber.

Pien Daisy ubedu nyathin man ng'om pare ma juyubu ubedu ma upong ku orchids man berocwiny, man orchestras ma ketho rwom pa oro, ma uketho can man paru pa kwo i wer manyen. I dyewor zoo, saxophones ubedo ka koko ku wer ma rwinyo pa "Beale Street Blues" ma wara miya acel mi mola ma kwar man ma kwar ubedo ka nyai iwi buru ma rieny. I sawa mi chai macol, kare ku kare ot moko ubedu ka mol ku lyethu ma nok man ma leng, ma wang dhanu manyen ubewotho keca ku keca calu lum ma jukubo ku cing macol ma ucungo iwi ngom.

Ikind ceng ma ceng eca Daisy ucaku wotho kendu ku kare; nyathotho edoko kendu ku nusu pa ceng acel acel ku nusu pa coo, man ebedo ka nindu i wang ceng ku lum man chiffon pa bongo mi diewor ma ucungo ikind orchids ma tho iwi ngom inget kavutu pare. Man kare ku kare piny moku i iye ubedu ka ywak pi moko tam. Emito kwo pare ubed maber kawoni, pio pio—man yub ucikere ni bedu ku tego moko—mi mar, mi sente, mi tic ma dhanu copo paru ngo—ma ubedu kago.

Ker eca ucaku wok i kind kare mi cwir ku bino pa Tom Buchanan. Ubedo ku rwom maber ikum kura pare man rwom pare, man Daisy ubedu kud anyonga. Labongo jiji, lwiny moku man kony moku ubedu tiye. Waraga ne utuc ibang Gatsby podi etiye i Oxford.

Ubedo ceng kawoni i Long Island man wabecidho ka yabo dirisa ma piny, ma wapongo ot ku mac ma loko rangi macol, ma loko rangi macol. Tipu pa yen moko upodho iwi pii man winy ma nen calu tipo ucaku wer ikind ith macol. Ubedo ku ngwec moth, maber i polo, ma yamo ubedu tek ungo, ma ukwayu ceng ma ngic man maber.

"Aparu ngo nya emare." Gatsby uloko wange kud i wang dirisa man enena ku wange ma tek. "Umitu ipoi, tuko macon, ebedo kud anyonga lee tin kugweno. Ewecu ire lembe eni iyore ma lworo i iye-ma uketho enen calu an abedo ngati ma rieko. Man adwogi ne ubedu nya engeyo ngo ango ma ebeweco."

Ebedo piny ku can.

"Eyo, ecopu bedu emare pi dakika manok kende, ikare ma gicaku nyom—man emaru an lee kadi i kare meno, ineno?"

I ngeye nyanok ewok ku wec moko ma nyayu lworo.

"I yo moku ci," ewacu, "ebedo kende kende."

Icopo timo ang'o ikum lembe meno, mako paru ikum lembe moko ma tek i paru pare ikum lembene ma jucopo pimu ngo?

Edok kud i France ikare ma Tom ku Daisy podi gitiye i wotho migi mi nyom, man etimo wotho ma rac man ma tek ni Louisville i cul pare mir ajiki. Ebedo keca pi yenga acel, ewotho i yo kama tyend gi ucungo i iye i dyewor mi Dwi mir Aparacel man edok ewotho i kabedo ma bor ma giwotho i iye ku mutukari pare matar. Calu ot pa Daisy ubedu ka nen ire ni ot ma dhanu ungeye ngo man ma dhanu ma maru timo ribiri ku coo, kumeno bende paru pare ikum adhura ne gire, kadi nya edaru wok kud i iye, ubedu ku leng pa piny ma nyayu can.

Ecidho ku paru niya kani ebed esayu matek, nwangu ecopo nwangu—nya ebeweke ngeye. Japonji mi ceng—ebedo ku sente ungo kawoni—ubedo maleng. Ewok woko i vestibule ma oyabere man ebedo piny iwi kom ma jukubo, man kabedo ne ucidhi man ngeye pa ot ma engeyo ongo ukadhu. I ngeye giwok woko i podho mi pii, kama gari ma yellow uringo kugi pi dakika acel ku dhanu i iye ma copo bedu nya gineno wange macol i dhu ngundu.

Yo ne utur man kawoni ecidho cen kud i ceng, ma, ka epodho piny, nen calu enyai i mugisa iwi adhura ma ube rweny kama en eyuyu i iye. Egam ecwalu cinge ku lworo calu ebemito ekwany yamo manok kende, mi laru dul pa kabedo ma nyaku ne etimo ire maber. Ento lembe eca ubedu katimere pio pio kawoni pi wange ma nen ungo man engeyo niya eweko thenge meno, manyen man maber, rondo ku rondo.

Ubedo sawa abungwen ikare ma wadaru camu cam miwikugweno man wawok woko i baranda. Diewor ne ukelo aloka loka madit i kite ma koth ku ceng ryeny kudu man ubedu ku midu pa koth i yamu. Jarafur, ngati mir ajiki pa jurutic pa Gatsby, ubinu i the step.

"Abeci cwalo pii i pii tin, Mr. Gatsby. Ith yen bicaku podho kawoni, man i ngeye peko bedo nuti ku paip."

"Kud itim tin," Gatsby udwoko. Eloko wange ira ku kwayo kisa. “Ingeyo, tuko macon, abed atiyo ku pii eca i kare mi cwir zoo?”

Aneno sawa para man acungu.

“Dakika apar wi aryo ni gari para.”

Abed amito ngo adok i adhura. Abedo ngo ku tic maber, ento ubedu madwong uloyu—amito ngo awek Gatsby. Aweko gari eca, man mange, mapodi awok ungo.

“Abilwongi,” awacu i ajikine.

“Tim, tuko macon.”

“Abilwongi i saa mi diewor.”

Wawotho moth moth piny iwi yende.

“Aparu Daisy de bi lwongo.” Enena ku lworo, calu nangu egeno nya acopo yiko lembe eni.

"Aparu kumeno."

“Ber, bed maber.”

Wa mako cing man acaku wotho. Ma podi awok ungo i lum, apoi ikum piny moko man aloko wanga.

"Gitiye dul pa dhanu ma rac," akok iwi lum. “Iromo ku dul pa dhanu ceke ma juketho karacelu.”

Abedo kud anyonga ni awacu lembe eno. Ebedo foc acel kende ma amiyo ire, kum nangu ayiye ngo kude nicaku acaki nituc i ajiki. Mir acel etingo wiye ku mwolo, man i ngeye wange ugam unyai i vunju ma leng man ma nyang ikum lembe, calu nangu wabedo i cahoots ma kud anyonga ikum lembe meno kare ku kare. Koti pare ma leng ma pink uketho rangi ma leng iwi step ma tar, man aparu pi dyewor ma abino i pacu pa kwaro pare, dwi adek mukadho. Kabedo mi lum man gari upong ku wang ju ma giparu nia etimo dubo—man en ucungu iwi yeny eca, ma umu lek pare ma rac ungo, ma enyutho igi cinge.

Afoye pi bedo ber pare. Wabed wafoye pi lembe meno kare ku kare—an man ju mukende.

“Ber maber,” alwongo. "Abedo kud anyonga i cam miwikugweno, Gatsby."

I adhura, atemo pi kare manok ni ketho piny ma jucwalo iwi piny madwong, man anindu i kom kom para ma loko loko. Mapodi dye ceng ukadhu, simu uceya, man acaku ay malu ku cwir ma wok iwi wiya. Ubedo Jordan Baker; ebed elwonga pol kare i sawa eni kum bedo ma engeyo ngo ikum wotho pare ikind hotel man club man ot pa dhanu uketho ebedo tek ni nwange i yo mange. Pol kare dwond pare wok iwi waya calu piny moko manyen man ma ngic, calu divot kud i golf-links macol ma wok kud i wang dirisa mi opici, ento tin uthieno nen calu tek man muthwo.

"Aweko ot pa Daisy," ewacu. "Atiye i Hempstead, man abecidho piny i Southampton tin kugweno."

Nyo ebedo ber ni weko ot pa Daisy, ento timo ne unyayu anyonga i iya, man wec pare mukende uketho abedo ngati ma tek.

“Ibedo ngo ber ira dyewor mukadho.”

"Ecopo bedu ber nenedi?"

Ling pi kare manok. Kadong:

“Ento—amito anen in.”

“An de amito anen in.”

"Ka acidho ngo i Southampton, man abinu i adhura tin kugweno?"

“Ungo—abeparu ngo tin uthieno.”

“Ber mandha.”

“Ecopo bedu ngo tin kugweno. Matung tung—”

Waweco kumeno pi kare manok, man i ngeye waweko wecu kendu. Angeyo ngo anga ikindwa ma uketho simu ku cinge ma tek, ento angeyo niya abed aparu ngo. Acopo ngo ni wecu kude iwi meza mi chai inindu eca tek nangu awecu ngo kude kendu i ng'om eni.

Agoyo simu i ot pa Gatsby ingey dakika manok, ento rwom ne upong. Atemo wang angwen; i ajikine jatel wi tic moko ma cwinye lith uwacu ira nia waya ne ubedu oyabere pi bor ku Detroit. Ka awotho ku sawa para, agoyo dul ma nok ikum gari ma tero dhanu adek ku pyerabic. I ngeye adok cen i kom para man atemo paru. Ubedo dyeceng kende.

Kinde ma akadhu kud i dhu lum ma ucungo iwi gari mi ceng' eca, awok ku ng'eyong'ec mi cidho i thenge mange mi gari. Aparu nya dhanu mapol ma mitu ngeyo lembe bino bedu nuti ceng'ini ku awiya ma co ma gibe sayu kabedo macol i lum, man ngati moko ma loko lembe ma ubeweco wang mapol ikum gin ma utimere, nitundu edoko piny mandha kadi ire man ecopo weco ngo kendu, man tic marac pa Myrtle Wilson juwil. Kawoni amito adok cen nyanok man atit ikum gin ma utimere i garage ingey waweko keca dyewor ma ukadhu.

Gibedo ku peko mi nwangu nyamin Catherine. Nyaka bed ni eturo cik pare ma kwero madhu kongo dyewor eca, kum kinde ma etuc eca ebedo jamutoro ku kongo man ecopo nyang ungo niya mutukari mi tero jurutwo udaru cidho i Flushing. Kinde ma gi ketho cwinye ikum lembe eni, epodho ndhundhu, calu nya eca ubedo piny ma dhanu copo yio ngo. Ngati moku, ma kite ber nyo ma mitu ngeyo lembe, utere i mutukari pare man utere i ngei kum nyamin.

I ngei diewor, dul pa dhanu ma lokore ucungo iwi kore pa ot mutukari, ma George Wilson ube yengo ku wiye iwi kom ma iye. Pi kare manok dhugola pa opici ubedo oyabere, man dhanu ceke ma bino i garage gibedu ka neno dhugola. I ajiki ngati moko uwacu niya etiye lewic, man ecego dhugola. Michaelis ku coo mukende mapol ubedu kude; mir acel, coo angwen nyo abic, i ngeye coo aryo nyo adek. I ngeye Michaelis umitu epenj ngati ma engeyo ngo ni ekuru keca pi dakika apar wiye abic, ma en edok i kabedu pare man etimo agulu mi chai. I ngeye, ebedo keca kende ku Wilson nitundu uthieno.

I saa adek mi diewor, wec pa Wilson ma nya wange uloko—ebedo moth man ecaku wecu ikum mutukari ma yellow. En uwacu niya etiye ku yore mi nwangu mutukari ma yellow ne ubedo pa ngati makani, man edham ewacu nya dwi moko mukadho dhaku pare uai kud i adhura ku wange ma otur man ume ma ungwel.

Ento kinde ma ewinjo en gire ka wacu lembe eni, egam oling man ecaku koko "Oh, Mungu para!" kendu i dwal pare mi koko. Michaelis utimo lembe moko marac mi loko paru pare.

"Ibedo i nyom pi kare ma romo adi, George? Bi keca, tem ibed ling pi dakika moko, man igam penji para. Ibedo i nyom pi kare ma romo adi?"

“Oru apar wi aryo.”

"Ibedo ku juma moko? Bi, George, bed moth-apenji penji moko. Ibedo ku juma moko?"

Nyithin macol ma tek umediri ka ngwec ikum ler macol, man kare ku kare ma Michaelis uwinjo mutukari ubewothu iwi ngudu woko, ewinjiri ire calu mutukari ma ucungu ngo pi sawa manok mukadho. Emaru ngo nimondo i ot mutukari, kum kom mi tic ubedo ku rangi kama kum dhanu ubedo i iye, ci ebed ewotho maber ungo i opici—engeyo piny ceke ma i iye ma podi uthieno ungo—man kare ku kare ebedo piny inget Wilson pi ketho ebedo moth.

"Itiye ku kanisa moko ma icidho i iye sawa moko, George? Nyo kadi nangu ibedo ngo keca pi kare malac? Nyo acopo lwongo kanisa man alwongo jatel wi dini ubinu man ecopu wecu kudi, ineno?"

“Kud ibed pa ngati moko.”

"Umitu ibed ku kanisa, George, pi kare ma kumeno. Umitu ibed icidho i kanisa wang acel. Ibedo ngo i kanisa? Winj, George, winja. Ibedo ngo i kanisa?"

"Eni ubedo kare malac mukadho."

Tembe mi dwoko wang lembe uturo wer pare—pi kare manok ebedo moth. I ngeye, neno ma rom ku ma engeyo, man ma ung'eyo ungo udwogo i wange ma rwinyo.

"Nen i drawer keca," ewacu, ku cinge ikum kom.

“Drawer makani?”

“Drawer eca—eni.”

Michaelis oyabu kawono ma ceng ku cinge. Piny moko mbe i iye ento thwol ma nok, ma wel pare tek, ma juyike ku adila man ma juyubu ku silva. Nen calu ubedu piny manyen.

"Ae?" epenju, maku malu.

Wilson uneno man utingo wiye.

"Anwange laworo kugweno. Etembo ni wecu ira ikume, ento angeyu niya ubedu piny moko ma nyayu ngyeru."

"Ibeweco ni dhaku peri re ma ung'iewo?"

"Ebedo ku karatasi mi tissue iwi biro pare."

Michaelis uneno ngo piny moko marac i lembe meno, man emiyo Wilson the lembe madwong ma omiyo dhaku pare ung'iewo thwol pa gwok. Ento nyalo bedo ni Wilson ne osewinjo moko kuom puonj ma chal gi mago chon, kuom Myrtle, nikech ne ochako wacho ni “Oh, Nyasayena!” kendu i wec ma yot—jakony pare uweko lembe madwong i polo.

"I ngeye enege," Wilson uwacu. Dhoge oyabere pio pio.

"Nga ma otimo?"

"Atiye ku yore mi nwangu."

"Ibedo jaratwo, George," jarimbe uwacu. "Eni ubedo lembe ma nyayu can iri man ingeyo ngo ango ma ibeweco. Ber ka item bedu moth nitundu uthieno."

"Enego nyaku ne."

"Ubedo ret, George."

Wilson uyengo wiye. Wange unyai man dhoge unyai nyanok ku tipo pa ngati ma dit “Hm!”

"Angeyo," ewacu andha. "Atiye acel ikin ju ma gen man aparu ngo nia lembe moko marac copo timere ikum ngati moko ci , ento ka angeyo lembe moko angeyo. Ubedo jal ma ubedu i mutukari ne. E ringo woko pi wecu kude man egam ejuku ngo."

Michaelis de uneno eni, ento ebino ngo i wiye nya etiye ku the lembe moko ma pire tek i iye. Ebedo ku yioyic nia Mrs. Wilson ubedu ka ringo kud ibang cware, kakare ni temo juku mutukari moko ci.

"Ecopo bedu kumeno nenedi?"

"Etiye ngati ma thuc," Wilson uwacu, calu nya edwoko wang penji. “Ah-hh—”

E ucaku yeng kendu, man Michaelis ucungu ka loko cinge.

"Nyo ibedo ku jarimbe moko ma acopo lwongo ire simu, George?"

Eni ubedo gen ma rwinyo—ebedo ku genogen nia Wilson epe ku jarimbe: epe ku jarimbe ma romo pi dhaku pare. Ebedo kud anyonga i ngeye nyanok kinde ma eneno aloka loka i ot, rangi macol ma ucungo inget dirisa, man enyang nya ceng eca ubedu bor ungo. I saa abic mi diewor, piny ubedo blue ma romo woko ma uketho mac uthum.

Wang Wilson ma nen maleng ungiyo i lum, kama pol macol ma nok ugam ucaku nen maleng man ugam uringo keca ku keca i yamu ma nyap pa ceng.

"Aweco kude," ewacu, ingey bedo moth pi kare malac. "Awecu ire nya ecopu bwola ento ecopo bwolo Mungu ngo. Atere i wang dirisa"-ku kero e ay malu man ecidho i wang dirisa mi ngeye man ecungo ku wange ma ucungo ikume-"man awacu 'Mungu ngeyo ango ma ibetimo, piny ceke ma ibetimo. Icopo bwonga, ento icopo bwolo Mungu ngo! ”

Ucungu inge, Michaelis uneno ku lworo nia ebedu ka neno wang Daktari TJ Eckleburg, ma ugam uwok, macol man ma dit, kud i dyewor ma ubedu ka rwinyo.

"Mungu neno piny ceke," Wilson udwoko kendo.

"Eni ubedo lwongo," Michaelis ukwayu ire. Piny moko uketho eloko wange kud i wang dirisa man eneno ngeye i ot. Ento Wilson ucungu keca pi kare malac, wange ubedu ceng ku wang dirisa, utingo wiye i dyewor.

I sawa abusiel mi diewor Michaelis uol, man ebedo kud anyonga pi dwand mutukari ma ucungu woko. Ebedo acel ikin ju ma gineno dyewor ma ukadhu ma ukwoni ni dwogo, ci etedo cam miwikugweno pi dhanu adek, ma en ku jadhum eca camu karacelu. Wilson ubedo moth kawoni, man Michaelis udok i pacu ni nindu; kinde ma ecer ingeye sawa angwen man edwogo pio pio i ot mutukari, Wilson utho.

Ubedo jadit ma wange nen calu owl ma anwangu ebeworo buku pa Gatsby i ot buku dyewor acel dwi adek mukadho.

Abedo ka nenu ngo nicaku eca. Angeyu ngo kite ma engeyo kudu pi yik ne, kadi nyinge de. Koth ucwir iwi gilasi pare ma gwai, man ewotho gi man eyubu gi kara enen kani ma jugwoku ma juwodho kud i liel pa Gatsby.

Atemo paru pi Gatsby pi kare manok, ento ebedo bor akeca, man acopo poi kende, manangu atie ku ngei cwiny, nia Daisy uoro ngo kwenda nyo thiwe. Awinjo ngati moku m'uyero kumae "Mugisa ni ju m'utho ma koth upodhu iwi gi," man i ngeye ngati ma wange nen calu owinyo uwacu "Amen ni lembe meno," ku dwal ma tek.

Wawotho piny pio pio ikind koth nitundo i mutukari. Owl-eyes uweco kuda inget dhugola.

"Acopo tuc ungo i ot," ewacu.

“Ng’atu mange de copo ngo.”

"Medi anyim!" Ecaku. “Mungu para, gibed gicidho keca ku dak.”

Ewotho glasi pare man edwoke kendu, woko man iye.

"Nyathin ma jacan," ewacu.

Piny acel ma poi maber uloyu utiye ka adok i West kud i somu mi prep man i ngeye kud i kolej i kare mi foc mi tho pa Kristu. Ju ma gicidho bor uloyu Chicago gibed gicokiri i Union Station macon macol i sawa abusiel mi diewor mi dwi mir apar wi ario, ku jurimo moko manok mi Chicago, ma dong gicaku timo foc migi mi ceng yom, mi wecu igi foc pio pio. Aparo koti pa anyira ma gi dwogo kud i bang Miss This-or-That's man wec pa yuyu ma ngic man cing gi ma ucungo iwi wiwa kinde ma waneno ju ma wangeyo con, man lwongo ma rwate: "Ibecidho i Ordways'? the Herseys'? the Schultzes'?" man tiket mabor ma rangi pa lum macol ucungo matek i cingwa ma utingo glove. Man mir ajiki mutukari ma nya aceng pa Chicago, Milwaukee man St. Paul railroad nen maleng calu foc mi tho pa Kristu iwi ngudu ma ceng ku dhugola.

Kinde ma wawok woko i dyewor mi ngico man pee mandha, pee mwa, ucaku wok ingetwa man rieny i wang dirisa, man mac ma nok pa kabedo ma nok mi Wisconsin ukadhu, piny moko ma tek ugam uwok iwi yamu. Wayuyo yamo matuc ikume ikare ma wadok kud i cam mi diewor kud i vestibules ma ngic, ma wangeyo ngo ikum bedo mwa ku ngom eni pi sawa acel ma wangeyo ngo, mapodi wadok ungo i iye kendu.

Eni ubedo Middle West para—ungo ngo nganu nyo lum nyo adhura mi Swede ma rwinyo, ento gari ma dwogo pa nyathin para, man tara mi ngudu man olang mi gari i mudho ma ngic man tipo pa holly wreaths ma jubolo ku dirisa ma ler iwi pee. Atiye acel ikin gi, ma cwinya yom nyanok ku nen pa ngico ma bor, ma cwinya yom nyanok pi dongo i ot pa Carraway i adhura ma ot podi julwongo pi oro madwong ku nying pa juru ot. Aneno kawoni nia eni ubedo lembe pa Western, i ngei lembe ceke—Tom ku Gatsby, Daisy ku Jordan man an, wan ceke wabedo Westerners, man nyo wabedu ku goro moku ma rom ma uketho wabedo ngo ku kwo mi Eastern.

Kadi bende ikare ma yo piny ucwalu cwinya lee, kadi kadi kadi nangu angeyu maber uloyu ikum adhura ma bor, ma nyai, man ma ungwel woko pa Ohio, ku penji migi ma rwinyo ma weko awiya man judongo kende—kadi bende i kare meno ebedo ku kura mi loko lembe. West Egg, asaga, podi utiye i lek para maber akeca. Aneno calu piny ma nen dyewor pa El Greco: ot dak acel, ma rwinyo man ma rwinyo, ma ucungo ithe polo macol, man dwe ma ler ungo. I wange, coo angwen ma rwom gi malu ma giruko bongo maber gibewotho i dhu ngundu ku cinge ma dhaku moko ma mero maruko bongo ma tar mi diewor ubedo iwie. Cinge, ma ucungo iwi ngete, ucwale ku piny mabecu. Ku lworo, coo gi lokore i ot moko—ot marac. Ento ngati moko mbe ma ngeyo nying dhaku ne, man ngati moko mbe ma dieng ikume.

I ngei tho pa Gatsby, East ubedo ku lworo ira kumeno, ma juloko ma ka wanga copo ngo ni dwoko piny. Pieno, kinde ma iro macol pa ith ma nyai ubedo iwi yamu man yamu ucwalu bongo ma pii ucwalu iwi cing, adham adok i pacu.

Piny acel ubedu nuti ma umitu jutim ma podi awok ungo, piny moko ma nyang ikume tek, ma ber ungo ka juweko kende. Ento amito awek piny i ayi maber man kud abed ku genogen kende i nam ma timo tic man ma timo lembe marac ni cwalo piny para woko. Aneno Jordan Baker man waweco ikum lembe ma utimere iwa karacelu, man lembe ma utimere i ngeye ira, man ebedo moth, ewinjo, iwi kom madit.

Ebedo ku kendi mi tugu golf, man apoi ni aparu niya ebedo calu lanyuth maber, wiye utingo malu nyanok, yuk wic pare ubedo ku rangi pa lum mi kare mi ngico, wange ubedo ku rangi macol marom ku glove ma epe ku cinge iwi tyende. Kinde ma adaru wecu, ewacu ira labongo wecu nia edaru nyom ku nico mange. Abedo ku jiji ikum lembe meno, kadi nang nya ju mapol ma ecopu nyomo ku yengo wiye, ento atimo nen calu abedo ku wur. Pi dakika manok kende abedo ka paru ka nyo abetimo ngo lembe marac, man aparu kendu pio pio man a ay malu pi wacu foyofoc.

"Kadok kumeno de ibolo ira piny," Jordan uwacu nyathotho. "Ibolo ira iwi simu. An dong angeyo ngo piri kawoni, ento ubedu lembe manyen ira, man wiya ungwel nyanok pi kare manok."

Wamoko cingwa.

"Oh, man ipoi"- emedu- "wec moko ma wabedo kudu ikum ringo mutukari?"

“Pirango—ungo andha.”

"Iwacu niya jararing mutukari marac utiye maber kende nitundu enwang jararing mutukari marac mange? Ber, anwangu jararing mutukari marac mange, epe? Abewecu niya ubedu lembe ma rac ira ni timo paru ma rac. Aparu niya ibedo ngati ma lemandha, man ma timo lembe ma atira. Aparu niya ubedu rwom peri ma pire tek."

“Atiye ku oro pyer adek,” awacu. “Atiye ku oro abic ma dit ma acopo ndra giri man alwong ni woro.”

Edwoko ngo. Ku ngei, man nusu pa mar kude, man kisa madit, aloko wanga.

Idiwor acel i thum pa dwi mir apar aneno Tom Buchanan. Ebed ewotho i nyima i Fifth Avenue i yo pare ma ling man ma tek, cinge ucungo nyanok kud i kume calu mi lwiny ikum piny ma copo nyayu peko i kume, wiye ubewotho matek keca ku keca, man ebed elubo wange ma ubedu ka ling. Kinde ma adwoko piny pi kwero kadho iwange, en ucungu man ucaku ngiyo wange i wang dirisa mi duka mi jam piny mabecu. I ngeye nyanok enena man edok cen, etingo cinge woko.

"Lembe ango, Nick? Ikwero mako cinga?"

"Eyo. Ingeyo ango ma aparu ikum peri."

“Ibedo jamutoro, Nick,” ewacu pio pio. “Lembe ma rac. Angeyo ngo ango ma rac iri.”

"Tom," apenje, "iwacu ango ni Wilson dyeceng eca?"

Enena ku wange manangu ewacu ngo lembe moko, man angeyu niya aparu maber ikum sawa ma ukadhu. Acaku loko kore, ento edaru lubo kora man emaku cinga.

"Awacu ire lemandha," ewacu. "Ebino i dhugola podi wabe yube ka wok woko, man kinde ma aoro lembe nia wabedu ngo i ot, etembo ni cidho malo. Ebedo jamutoro ma copo nego ka nangu awecu ngo ire won mutukari. Cinge ubedu iwi revolver i cinge dakika ceke ma ebedo i ot—" Eweko lembe ku lworo. "Ka awacu ire ango? Ngati ne bino i bange. Ebolo buru i wang peri calu ma etimo i pa Daisy, ento ebedo ngati ma tek. Eringo iwi Myrtle calu ka iringo iwi gwok man ejuku ngo mutukari pare."

Piny moko mbe ma acopo wacu, mako lembe acel ma juweco ngo nia epe.

"Man ka iparu niya anwangu ngo can para-nen kany, kinde ma adok ka weko ot eca man aneno sanduku pa biskuit pa gwok ma juketho iwi kom, abedo piny man akok calu nyathin. Ku Mungu ebedo rac akeca-"

Acopo weko ngo dubo pare nyo mare, ento aneno nia gin ma etimo ubedo, ire, ber mandha. Lembe eca ubedu lembe ma dhanu timo ngo man ma nya wange unyai. Gibedu dhanu ma timo lembe marac, Tom ku Daisy—gi turo piny man lei man gi dok cen i sente migi nyo timo migi marac, nyo gin moko ci ma uketho gibedo karacelu, man giweko dhanu mukende gi yweyu piny ma rac ma gitimo...

Amako cinga kude; nen calu lembe mi mingo ngo, kum awinjo nya abeweco ku nyathin. I ngeye emondo i duka mi jam piny mabecu pi wilu bongo mi ngut—nyoke buttons mi cuff kende—ma eweko lworo para mi adhura rondo ku rondo.

Ot pa Gatsby podi ubedo nono ikare ma aweko—lum ma iwi lum pare udongo ma rom ku para. Ng'atu acel m'ikind jururing mutukari mi caru eca ugam utingo ngo dhugola mi donjo i dhugola manangu ecungu ngo pi dakika acel man enyutho cinge i iye; nyalo bedo ni en e ma ne otero Daisy gi Gatsby e East Egg e otieno ma accident ne otimore, kendo nyalo bedo ni en e ma ne oloso wachno e wi wachno. Amito ngo awinje man aweko en kinde ma awok kud i gari.

Abedo dyewor para mi ceng kas acel i New York kum foc pare ma leng man ma leng ubedu kuda maleng ma podi awinjo wer man ngyeru, ma nyap man ma rwinyo ngo, kud i podho pare, man mutukari ma cidho malu man piny i gari pare. Diewor moko awinjo mutukari moko ma tiye keca, man aneno ler pare ucungu i wang ot pare. Ento atimo ngo sayusac. Nyo ebedo welo moku ma tokcen ma ubedu cen kud i thum pa ng'om man engeyo ngo niya kwir ne uthum.

I dyewor ma tok cen, ku sanduku para ma jupongu man mutukari para ma jucwalu ni jumalworo piny, adok man aneno ot moko ma rac ma rac kendu. I wi step matar, wec moko ma rac, ma awobi moko ukyewu ku lum, ucungu maleng i lero pa dwe, man awothe, man agoyo wara para iwi kidi. I ngeye awotho piny i dhu nam man abedo iwi lum.

Pol pa kabedo madongo mi dhu nam eca cego kawoni man der moko ubedu pee, mako ler macol, ma ubewothu pa yei ma dit ma ucungo iwi Sound. Man kani dwe ucidhi malu ot ma pire tek ucaku rwinyo nitundo moth moth acaku ngeyo cula macon ma kawoni ma nyai wang acel pi wang ju ma giwotho i yei mi Dutch—ngom ma nyen man macol pa ng'om manyen. Yen pare ma rwinyo, yen ma uketho ot pa Gatsby, ubedo ka weco iwi lembe ma dhanu zo paru; pi kare manok kende dhanu ucikere ni bedo ku yweyo pare i wang ngom eni, ma jucwalu i paru maber ma engeyo ngo man emito ngo, wang ku wang pi kare mir ajiki i lembe mukadho ku piny moko ma rom ku kero pare mi timo lembe mi awura.

Man kinde ma abedo keca ka paru pi ng'om macon, ma jungeyo ngo, aparu pi lworo pa Gatsby ikare ma enwangu ler macol i thum pa dhu nam pa Daisy. Ebino yo mabor i lum macol eni, man lek pare nen ni ubedu kago ma ecopo weko ngo ni nyang kudu. Engeyo ongo niya dong edaru ngeye, kama moku cen i kabedo macol ma woko pa adthura, kama podho macol mi republic ubedo ka mol i dyewor.

Gatsby uyio nia lero macol, anyim maber ma oro ku oro rwinyo i wangwa. Egam eweko iwa, ento epe—urwo wabi ringo pio pio, wabi tingo cing mwa mabor... Man ceng moko maber—
