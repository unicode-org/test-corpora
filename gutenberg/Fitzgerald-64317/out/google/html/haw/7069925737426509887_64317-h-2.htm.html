I koʻu hoʻi ʻana mai i ka home i West Egg i kēlā pō, makaʻu wau no kekahi manawa i ke ahi o koʻu hale. ʻO ka hola ʻelua a me ke kihi holoʻokoʻa o ka peninsula e ʻā ana i ka mālamalama, i hāʻule maoli ʻia ma luna o nā kumu lāʻau a hana ʻia nā ʻōlinolino lahilahi ma luna o nā uwea alanui. I ka huli ʻana i kahi kihi, ʻike wau ʻo ka hale ia o Gatsby, i hoʻomālamalama ʻia mai ka hale kiaʻi a i ka hale.

I ka wā mua, manaʻo wau he pāʻina ʻē aʻe ia, he ala ʻāhiu i hoʻoholo iā ia iho i "pee-a-hele-ʻimi" a i ʻole "sardines-i-ka-pahu" me ka hale a pau i wehe ʻia i ka pāʻani. Akā, ʻaʻohe kani. ʻO ka makani wale nō i loko o nā kumulāʻau ka mea i puhi i nā uwea a hoʻopiʻi hou i nā kukui me he mea lā ua ʻoni ka hale i loko o ka pouli. I ka uwē ʻana o kaʻu kaʻa kaʻa, ʻike wau iā Gatsby e hele mai ana iaʻu ma kēlā ʻaoʻao o kona mauʻu.

"Ua like kou wahi me ka World's Fair," wahi a'u.

“Aia anei?” Huli ʻo ia i kona mau maka iā ia me ka ʻike ʻole. "Ke nānā nei au i kekahi o nā lumi. E hele kāua i Coney Island, ka haʻuki kahiko. Ma koʻu kaʻa."

"Ua lohi loa."

"ʻAe, inā paha mākou e komo i loko o ka wai ʻauʻau? ʻAʻole au i hoʻohana ia mea i ke kauwela āpau."

"Pono wau e moe."

"Hiki nō."

Ua kali ʻo ia me ka nānā ʻana mai iaʻu me ka makemake nui.

"Ua kamaʻilio wau me Miss Baker," wahi aʻu ma hope o kekahi manawa. “E kāhea ana au iā Daisy i ka lā ʻapōpō a kono iā ia ma ʻaneʻi e kīʻaha ai.”

"ʻAe, ua pono," wahi āna me ka mālama ʻole. "ʻAʻole au makemake e hoʻokomo iā ʻoe i kekahi pilikia."

"He aha ka lā e kūpono iā ʻoe?"

"He aha ka lā e kūpono iā ʻoe ?" hoʻoponopono koke ʻo ia iaʻu. "ʻAʻole au makemake e hoʻokomo iā ʻoe i kekahi pilikia, ʻike ʻoe."

“Pehea ka lā ma hope o ka lā ʻapōpō?”

Noʻonoʻo ʻo ia no kekahi manawa. A laila, me ka makemake ʻole: "Makemake au e ʻoki i ka mauʻu," wahi āna.

Nānā mākou i lalo i ka mauʻu - aia kahi laina ʻoi kahi i pau ai koʻu mau lau ʻōpala a hoʻomaka ka ākea ʻeleʻele a mālama maikaʻi ʻia. Ua manaʻo wau ʻo ia kaʻu mauʻu.

"Aia kekahi mea liʻiliʻi," wahi āna me ka maopopo ʻole, a kanalua.

"Makemake paha ʻoe e hoʻopanee i kekahi mau lā?" Ninau au.

"ʻAe, ʻaʻole ia e pili ana i kēlā. ʻO ka liʻiliʻi loa—" Ua ʻakaʻaka ʻo ia me nā ʻano hoʻomaka. “No ke aha, noʻonoʻo wau—no ke aha, e nānā ma ʻaneʻi, e ka haʻuki kahiko, ʻaʻole ʻoe e loaʻa kālā nui, ʻeā?

"ʻAʻole nui loa."

Me he mea lā ua hōʻoluʻolu ʻo ia iā ia a ua hoʻomau ʻo ia me ka hilinaʻi.

"Manaʻo wau ʻaʻole ʻoe, inā e kala mai ʻoe i kaʻu—ʻike ʻoe, ke hana nei au i kahi ʻoihana liʻiliʻi ma ka ʻaoʻao, kahi ʻano laina ʻaoʻao, maopopo iā ʻoe.

"Ke ho'āʻo nei."

"ʻAe, he mea hoihoi kēia iā ʻoe. ʻAʻole ia e lawe nui i kou manawa a e ʻohi paha ʻoe i kahi kālā maikaʻi.

ʻIke wau i kēia manawa ma lalo o nā kūlana like ʻole ʻo ke kamaʻilio ʻana paha kekahi o nā pilikia o koʻu ola. Akā, no ka mea, ua maopopo a me ka noʻonoʻo ʻole ka hāʻawi ʻana no kahi lawelawe e hāʻawi ʻia, ʻaʻohe oʻu koho ʻē aʻe ke ʻoki ʻana iā ia ma laila.

"Ua piha ko'u mau lima," wahi a'u. "Ua koi nui au akā ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke hana hou aku."

"ʻAʻole pono ʻoe e hana i kekahi ʻoihana me Wolfshiem." Ua manaʻo ʻo ia e haʻalele ana au i ka "gonnegtion" i ʻōlelo ʻia ma ka ʻaina awakea, akā ua hōʻoiaʻiʻo wau iā ia ua hewa. Ua kali ʻo ia no kekahi manawa lōʻihi, me ka manaʻo e hoʻomaka wau i ke kamaʻilio ʻana, akā naʻe ua hauʻoli loa wau i ka pane ʻana, no laila hoʻi ʻo ia i ka home me ka makemake ʻole.

Ua hoʻomāmā a hauʻoli wau i ke ahiahi; Manaʻo wau ua hele wau i kahi hiamoe hohonu i koʻu komo ʻana i koʻu puka mua. No laila ʻaʻole maopopo iaʻu inā hele ʻo Gatsby i Coney Island a ʻaʻole paha, a i ʻole ehia mau hola ʻo ia i "nānā aku ai i loko o nā lumi" ʻoiai ʻo kona hale e ʻā nui ana. Kāhea wau iā Daisy mai ke keʻena mai i ke kakahiaka aʻe, a kono aku iā ia e hele i ke kī.

"Mai lawe mai iā Tom," aʻo aku au iā ia.

“He aha?”

"Mai lawe mai iā Tom."

"ʻO wai ʻo 'Tom'?" i ninau mai ai me ka hala ole.

ʻO ka lā i ʻaelike ʻia ʻo ia ka ua. I ka hola ʻumikūmākahi o ke ahiahi, ua kauo kekahi kanaka i ka ʻaʻahu ua, e kauo ana i ka mea ʻoki mauʻu, paʻi mai i koʻu puka mua a ʻōlelo mai ua hoʻouna ʻo Mr. Gatsby iā ia e ʻoki i kaʻu mauʻu. Ua hoʻomanaʻo mai kēia iaʻu ua poina iaʻu e haʻi i koʻu Finn e hoʻi mai, no laila ua holo au i West Egg Village e ʻimi iā ia ma waena o nā ala keʻokeʻo pulu a kūʻai i mau kīʻaha a me nā lemona a me nā pua.

ʻAʻole pono nā pua, no ka mea, i ka hola ʻelua ua hōʻea mai kahi hale ʻōmaʻomaʻo mai Gatsby's, me nā ʻeke helu ʻole e paʻa ai. He hora ma hope iho, ua wehe ae la ka puka mua me ka hopohopo, a komo koke mai la o Gatsby me ka lole keokeo keokeo, ka palule kala, a me ke kaula gula.

"Ua maikaʻi nā mea a pau?" i ninau koke aku ai oia.

"Maikaʻi ka mauʻu, inā ʻo ia kāu ʻōlelo."

“He aha ka mauʻu?” i ninau mai ai oia me ka palaka. "O ka mauʻu o ka pā." Nānā aʻela ʻo ia ma waho o ka puka makani, akā, ma ka nānā ʻana mai i kāna mau hiʻohiʻona, ʻaʻole wau i manaʻoʻiʻo ua ʻike ʻo ia i kekahi mea.

"Maikaʻi loa ka nānā ʻana," i ʻōlelo ʻo ia me ke ʻano haʻahaʻa. "Ua ʻōlelo kekahi o nā pepa ua manaʻo lākou e pau ka ua ma kahi o ʻehā. Manaʻo wau ʻo The Journal .

Lawe au iā ia i loko o ka hale lole, kahi āna i nānā iki ai i ka Finn. Ua nānā pū mākou i nā keke lemona he ʻumikūmālua mai ka hale kūʻai mea ʻai.

"E hana lākou?" Ninau au.

"ʻOiaʻiʻo, ʻoiaʻiʻo! Maikaʻi lākou!" a ua hoʻohui hou ʻo ia, "… ʻo ka haʻuki kahiko."

Ua maʻalili ka ua ma kahi o ka hapalua hola ʻekolu a i ka noe pulu, kahi e ʻau ai nā kulu lahilahi me he hau. Nānā ʻo Gatsby me nā maka hakahaka ma o ke kope o Clay's Economics , e hoʻomaka ana ma ka hehi Finnish nāna i hoʻoluliluli i ka papahele o ka lumi kuke, a nānā aku i nā puka makani hū i kēlā me kēia manawa me he mea lā e hana ʻia ana ma waho o nā hanana i ʻike ʻole ʻia akā weliweli. ʻO ka hope loa, ala aʻela ʻo ia a haʻi mai iaʻu, me ka leo maopopo ʻole, e hoʻi ana ʻo ia i ka home.

“No ke aha ia?”

"ʻAʻohe kanaka e hele mai ana i ke kī. Ua lohi loa!" Nānā ʻo ia i kāna wati me he mea lā aia kekahi koi koi i kona manawa ma kahi ʻē. "ʻAʻole hiki iaʻu ke kali i ka lā a pau."

"Mai pupule, ʻelua mau minuke a ʻehā wale nō."

Noho ʻo ia i lalo me ka ʻinoʻino, me he mea lā ua hoʻokuke au iā ia, a i ka manawa hoʻokahi, aia ka leo o kahi kaʻa e huli ana i koʻu alahele. Lele mākou a ʻelua, a ʻeha iki wau iaʻu iho, hele wau i waho i ka pā.

Ma lalo o nā kumulāʻau lilac e kulu ana he kaʻa hāmama nui e hele mai ana ma ke kaʻa. Ua kū. ʻO ka helehelena o Daisy, i kuʻi ʻia ma lalo o kahi pāpale lavender kihi ʻekolu, nānā mai iaʻu me ka minoʻaka hauʻoli.

"ʻO kēia anei kou wahi e noho ai, e kaʻu mea aloha?"

ʻO ka leo hoʻohauʻoli o kona leo he mea hoʻomakeʻa ʻāhiu i ka ua. Pono au e hahai i ka leo o ia leo no kekahi manawa, i luna a i lalo, me koʻu pepeiao wale nō, ma mua o ka puka ʻana o kekahi ʻōlelo. E waiho ana ka lauoho pulu e like me ka pena uliuli ma kona papalina, a pulu kona lima me na kulu olinolino i ko'u lawe ana e kokua iaia mai ke kaa mai.

"Aloha ʻoe iaʻu," i ʻōlelo haʻahaʻa ai ʻo ia i koʻu pepeiao, "a i ʻole no ke aha wau i hele mai ai?"

"ʻO ia ka mea huna o Castle Rackrent. E haʻi aku i kou kaʻa e hele mamao aku a e hoʻolimalima i hoʻokahi hola."

“E hoʻi mai i hoʻokahi hola, Ferdie.” A laila i loko o kahi ʻōhumu nui: "ʻO Ferdie kona inoa."

"E pili ana anei ka hinuhinu i kona ihu?"

"ʻAʻole wau i manaʻo pēlā," wahi āna me ka hala ʻole. "No ke aha mai?"

Komo mākou i loko. ʻO koʻu pīhoihoi nui ka haʻalele ʻana o ka lumi hoʻokipa.

"ʻAe, ʻakaʻaka kēlā," wahi aʻu.

“He aha ka mea ʻakaʻaka?”

Huli aʻela ʻo ia i kona poʻo no ka mea, he kukui hanohano e kīkēkē ana ma ka puka mua. Hele au a wehe. ʻO Gatsby, haʻihaʻi e like me ka make, me kona mau lima e like me nā mea kaumaha i loko o kāna ʻeke ʻaʻahu, e kū ana i loko o kahi wai e hoʻolohilohi mai ana i koʻu mau maka.

Me kona mau lima i loko o kona ʻeke ʻaʻahu, ua uwē maila ʻo ia ma oʻu nei i loko o ke keʻena, huli ʻo ia me he mea lā aia ma ka uwea, a nalo akula i loko o ka lumi hoʻokipa. ʻAʻole he mea ʻakaʻaka. Me ka ʻike ʻana i ke kuʻi nui o koʻu puʻuwai ponoʻī, ua huki au i ka puka e kūʻē i ka ua nui.

No ka hapalua minuke ʻaʻohe kani. A laila, mai loko mai o ka lumi hoʻokipa, lohe au i ke ʻano o ka ʻōhumu a me ka ʻakaʻaka, a ukali ʻia e ka leo o Daisy ma kahi leka hoʻokalakupua.

"Ua hauʻoli nui wau i ka ʻike hou ʻana iā ʻoe."

He hoomaha; ua hoomanawanui weliweli. ʻAʻohe aʻu mea e hana ai ma ke keʻena, no laila ua hele au i loko o ka lumi.

ʻO Gatsby, ʻo kona mau lima i loko o kāna ʻeke, e kau ana ma luna o ka mantelpiece me kahi hoʻopunipuni hoʻopunipuni o ka maʻalahi loa, ʻo ia hoʻi o ka luhi. Ua hilinaʻi ʻia kona poʻo i hope a kau ʻia ma ke alo o ka uaki mantelpiece i hoʻopau ʻia, a mai kēia kūlana i nānā pono aku ai kona mau maka makaʻu iā Daisy, e noho ana me ka makaʻu akā ʻoluʻolu, ma ka ʻaoʻao o kahi noho ʻoʻoleʻa.

"Ua hui mua mākou," i ʻōhumu ai ʻo Gatsby. Nana mai la kona mau maka ia'u i kekahi manawa, a wehe ae la kona mau lehelehe me ka hoao hoopau ana i ka akaaka. Laki ka uaki i kēia manawa e hili pōʻino i ke kaomi ʻana o kona poʻo, a laila huli aʻela ʻo ia a hopu ʻia me nā manamana lima haʻalulu, a hoʻihoʻi i kahi. A laila, noho ʻo ia i lalo, me ka ʻoʻoleʻa, ʻo kona kuʻekuʻe ma ka lima o ka sofa a me ka ʻauwae ma kona lima.

"E kala mai au i ka uaki," wahi āna.

Ua manaʻo koʻu mau maka ponoʻī i ke ahi wela hohonu. ʻAʻole hiki iaʻu ke hōʻiliʻili i hoʻokahi wahi maʻamau i loko o koʻu poʻo.

"He uaki kahiko ia," wahi a'u ia lakou me ka naaupo.

Manaʻo wau ua manaʻoʻiʻo mākou a pau no kekahi manawa ua haki ʻia ma ka papahele.

"ʻAʻole mākou i hui no nā makahiki he nui," wahi a Daisy, ʻo kona leo me ka mea maoli.

"Elima makahiki o Nowemapa aʻe."

ʻO ka maikaʻi maʻalahi o ka pane a Gatsby e hoʻihoʻi iā mākou a pau i kahi minuke ʻē aʻe. Ua kau wau iā lāua ma ko lāua mau wāwae me ka manaʻo nui e kōkua mai lākou iaʻu e hana i ke kī i loko o ka lumi kuke i ka wā i lawe mai ai ka daimonio Finn i loko o kahi pā.

Ma waena o ka huikau o nā kīʻaha a me nā keke, ua hoʻokumu ʻia kekahi ʻano kino kino. Ua komo ʻo Gatsby iā ia iho i loko o kahi aka, a ʻoiai mākou e kamaʻilio nei me Daisy, nānā pono mai kekahi a i kekahi o mākou me nā maka ʻeha a hauʻoli. Akā naʻe, no ka mea ʻaʻole i pau ka mālie, ua hana wau i kumu kumu i ka manawa mua, a kū i koʻu mau wāwae.

"Hele ana ʻoe ihea?" i koi aku ai iā Gatsby me ka hoʻomaka koke.

"E hoʻi ana au."

"Pono wau e kamaʻilio me ʻoe e pili ana i kekahi mea ma mua o kou hele ʻana."

Ua hahai mai ʻo ia iaʻu i loko o ka lumi kuke, pani i ka puka, a hāwanawana: “Auē, ke Akua!” ma ke ano poino.

"He aha ka pilikia?"

"He kuhihewa weliweli kēia," wahi āna, me ka luliluli ʻana i kona poʻo mai kēlā ʻaoʻao a i kēia ʻaoʻao, "he kuhihewa weliweli a weliweli."

"Hilahila wale ʻoe, ʻo ia wale nō," a ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi: "Ua hilahila pū ʻo Daisy."

“Hilahila ʻo ia?” wahi ana me ka hoomaloka.

"E like me ʻoe."

"Mai walaʻau nui."

"Ke hana nei ʻoe me he keiki liʻiliʻi lā," wahi aʻu me ka hoʻomanawanui. "ʻAʻole ʻo ia wale nō, akā, ʻino ʻoe. Noho wale ʻo Daisy ma laila."

Hāpai ʻo ia i kona lima e hoʻōki i kaʻu mau ʻōlelo, nānā mai iaʻu me ka poina ʻole, a wehe ʻo ia i ka puka me ka makaʻala, a hoʻi hou i loko o kekahi keʻena.

Hele au ma ke ala hope—e like me kā Gatsby i ka wā i kaʻapuni ai ʻo ia i ka hale i ka hapalua hola ma mua-a holo i kahi kumu lāʻau ʻeleʻele nui, nona nā lau nui i hana i ka lole i ka ua. Ua ninini hou ʻia, a ʻo koʻu mauʻu ʻano ʻole, kahi i kahi maikaʻi ʻia e ka māla ʻo Gatsby, ua nui i loko o nā ʻōpala liʻiliʻi liʻiliʻi a me nā kumulāʻau prehistoric. ʻAʻohe mea e nānā aku ai mai lalo mai o ka lāʻau koe wale nō ka hale nui o Gatsby, no laila, nānā au iā ia, e like me Kant ma kona hale kiaʻi halepule, no ka hapalua hola. Ua kūkulu ʻia e kekahi mea hana pia i ka hoʻomaka ʻana o ka "manawa", he ʻumi makahiki ma mua, a aia kekahi moʻolelo ua ʻae ʻo ia e uku i nā ʻauhau ʻelima makahiki ma luna o nā hale e pili kokoke ana inā makemake ka poʻe nona ko lākou hale i ka ʻuʻu. ʻO kā lākou hōʻole paha i lawe i ka puʻuwai mai kāna hoʻolālā e Loaʻa i kahi ʻohana - ua hele ʻo ia i kahi emi koke. Ua kūʻai aku kāna mau keiki i kona hale me ka lei ʻeleʻele ma ka puka. ʻO ka poʻe ʻAmelika, ʻoiai makemake lākou e lilo i poʻe kauā, ua paʻa mau lākou i ka ʻoihana mahiʻai.

Ma hope o ka hapalua hola, ua ʻā hou ka lā, a ua hoʻopuni ke kaʻa o ka hale kūʻai i ke kaʻa o Gatsby me nā mea maka no ka ʻaina ahiahi a kāna mau kauā—ua maopopo iaʻu ʻaʻole ʻo ia e ʻai i kahi puna. Ua hoʻomaka kekahi kaikamahine e wehe i nā puka makani o luna o kona hale, ua ʻike ʻia i loko o kēlā me kēia, a, e hilinaʻi ana mai ke kai kūʻono nui, kuha meditatively i loko o ka māla. ʻO ia ka manawa aʻu i hoʻi ai. I ka hoʻomau ʻana o ka ua, ua like ia me ka ʻōhumu ʻana o ko lākou mau leo, e piʻi ana a pehu iki i kēlā me kēia manawa me ka puana o ka manaʻo. Akā, i loko o ka hāmau hou ua manaʻo wau ua hāʻule pū ka hāmau i loko o ka hale.

Ua komo au i loko—ma hope o ka hana ʻana i nā walaʻau ʻē aʻe i loko o ka lumi kuke, ʻaʻole i hoʻokuʻu i ka umu—akā ʻaʻole wau i manaʻo ua lohe lākou i kahi leo. E noho ana laua ma kela ame keia kihi o ka noho, e nana ana kekahi i kekahi me he mea la ua ninauia kekahi ninau, a i ole ia, aia i ka lewa, a ua pau na ano hilahila a pau. Ua hamoia ka helehelena o Daisy me ka waimaka, a i ko'u komo ana mai, lele aku la oia iluna a hoomaka aku la e holoi me kona hainaka imua o ke aniani. Akā, aia kahi hoʻololi i Gatsby e hoʻohilahila wale ana. Ua alohi maoli ʻo ia; me ka ʻole o ka ʻōlelo a i ʻole ke ʻano o ka hauʻoli, ua puka mai ka pōmaikaʻi hou mai ona aku a hoʻopiha i ka lumi liʻiliʻi.

"Auē, aloha, haʻuki kahiko," wahi āna, me he mea lā ʻaʻole ʻo ia i ʻike iaʻu no nā makahiki. Noʻonoʻo wau no kekahi manawa e lulu lima ʻo ia.

“Ua pau ka ua.”

“Ua loaʻa?” I kona hoʻomaopopo ʻana i kaʻu mea e kamaʻilio nei, aia nā ʻōlinolino o ka lā i loko o ka lumi, ua ʻakaʻaka ʻo ia me he kanaka lā, me he kahu hauʻoli o ka mālamalama hou, a haʻi hou i ka lono iā Daisy. "Pehea kou manao no ia mea? Ua pau ka ua."

"ʻOliʻoli wau, e Jay." ʻO kona ʻāʻī, piha i ka ʻehaʻeha, ka nani ʻehaʻeha, i haʻi wale mai i kona hauʻoli i manaʻo ʻole ʻia.

"Makemake au e hele mai ʻoe me Daisy i koʻu hale," wahi āna, "makemake wau e hōʻike iā ia a puni."

Aia nō lākou ma lalo o ka lāʻau plum keʻokeʻo a pili ko lākou mau maka, koe wale nō kahi kukuna haʻihaʻi o ka mahina ma waena. Ua manao iho la au, ua kulou malie loa oia iaia i ke ahiahi a pau e hiki ai i keia kokoke ana, a ia'u e nana ana, ike aku la au iaia e kulou ana i hookahi degere hope loa a honi ma kona papalina.

"Makemake au iā ia," wahi a Daisy, "Manaʻo wau he nani ʻo ia."

Akā ʻo nā mea ʻē aʻe i hōʻeha ʻia iā ia-a ʻaʻole hiki ke hoʻopaʻapaʻa ʻia no ka mea ʻaʻole ia he hōʻailona akā he manaʻo. Pīhoihoi ʻia ʻo ia e West Egg, ʻo kēia "wahi" ʻike ʻole a Broadway i hānau ʻia ma kahi kauhale lawaiʻa ʻo Long Island - i hoʻoweliweli ʻia e kona ʻano ikaika i hoʻopilikia ʻia ma lalo o nā euphemisms kahiko a me ka hopena obtrusive i alakaʻi i kona poʻe kamaʻāina ma kahi ala pōkole mai ka mea ʻole. Ua ʻike ʻo ia i kekahi mea weliweli i ka maʻalahi loa i hiki ʻole iā ia ke hoʻomaopopo.

Noho au ma ke alapiʻi mua me lākou i ko lākou kali ʻana i ko lākou kaʻa. Ua poeleele ma keia mua; ʻO ka puka ʻālohilohi wale nō i hoʻouna aku i ʻumi kapuaʻi kūhāhā o ka māmā e puka mai ana i ke kakahiaka ʻeleʻele palupalu. I kekahi manawa, neʻe ke aka ma luna o kahi lumi ʻaʻahu makapō ma luna, a hāʻawi i ke aka i kahi aka, kahi huakaʻi paʻa ʻole o nā aka, nāna i hoʻoheheʻe a pauda i loko o ke aniani ʻike ʻole ʻia.

"ʻO wai kēia Gatsby?" i koi koke aku ai o Tom. "ʻO kekahi mea kālepa nui?"

"Ma hea ʻoe i lohe ai?" Ua nīnau au.

"ʻAʻole au i lohe. Ua noʻonoʻo wau. ʻO ka nui o kēia poʻe waiwai hou he poʻe kālepa nui wale nō, ʻike ʻoe."

"ʻAʻole ʻo Gatsby," wahi aʻu i ʻōlelo pōkole ai.

Ua hāmau ʻo ia no kekahi manawa. ʻO nā ʻiliʻili o ke kaʻa kaʻa ma lalo o kona mau wāwae.

"ʻAe, ua hoʻoikaika ʻo ia iā ia iho e hui pū i kēia menagerie."

Hoʻoulu ka makani i ka ʻehu hina o ke kola hulu o Daisy.

"Ma ka liʻiliʻi ʻoi aku ka hoihoi ma mua o ka poʻe a mākou i ʻike ai," wahi āna me ka hoʻoikaika.

"ʻAʻole ʻoe i makemake nui."

"ʻAe,ʻo wau."

Ua ʻakaʻaka ʻo Tom a huli mai iaʻu.

"Ua ʻike anei ʻoe i ka helehelena o Daisy i ka wā i noi ai kēlā kaikamahine iā ia e hoʻokomo iā ia i lalo o ka wai anuanu?"

Ua hoʻomaka ʻo Daisy e hīmeni me ka mele me ka hāwanawana puʻupuʻu, me ka hoʻopuka ʻana i ke ʻano o kēlā me kēia huaʻōlelo i loaʻa ʻole ma mua a ʻaʻole loa e loaʻa hou. I ka wā i piʻi aʻe ai ka leo, nahā maikaʻi kona leo, e hahai ana iā ia, me ke ʻano like ʻole o nā leo, a ʻo kēlā me kēia hoʻololi i hoʻopuka iki i kāna hana kilokilo pumehana kanaka ma ka lewa.

"He nui ka poʻe i hele mai ʻaʻole i kono ʻia," i ʻōlelo koke ʻo ia. "ʻAʻole i kono ʻia kēlā kaikamahine. Hoʻoikaika wale lākou i ko lākou komo ʻana i loko a ua ʻoluʻolu loa ʻo ia e kūʻē."

"Makemake au e ʻike ʻo wai ʻo ia a me kāna hana," i ʻōlelo ai ʻo Tom. "A manaʻo wau e hana wau i kahi kumu e ʻike ai."

"Hiki iaʻu ke haʻi aku iā ʻoe i kēia manawa," wahi āna. "Ua loaʻa iā ia kekahi mau hale kūʻai lāʻau lapaʻau, nui nā hale kūʻai lāʻau. Ua kūkulu ʻo ia iā ia iho."

Hele mai ka limousine dilatory i ka holo ʻana.

"Aloha pō, e Nick," wahi a Daisy.

Ua haʻalele kona nānā ʻana mai iaʻu a ʻimi i ka luna mālamalama o nā ʻanuʻu, kahi i ʻauheʻe aku ai ʻo "ʻEkolu o ke kakahiaka," kahi waltz maikaʻi a kaumaha o kēlā makahiki, e neʻe ana i waho o ka puka hāmama. Ma hope o nā mea a pau, i loko o ka ʻoluʻolu o ka pāʻina ʻo Gatsby, ua ʻike ʻole ʻia nā mea aloha i kona honua. He aha ka mea i luna o ka mele e kāhea ana iā ia i loko? He aha ka mea e hana ʻia i kēia manawa i nā hola pōʻeleʻele a hiki ʻole ke helu ʻia? Malia paha e hiki mai ana kekahi malihini kupanaha, he kanaka laha ole a e kahaha ai, kekahi kaikamahine opiopio nani maoli, me ka nana hou ana ia Gatsby, hookahi manawa o ka halawai kupua, e kinai ana i kela mau makahiki elima o ka haipule mau.

Ua lohi au i kēlā pō. Ua noi mai ʻo Gatsby iaʻu e kali a kaʻawale ʻo ia, a ua hoʻomoe au i loko o ka māla a hiki i ka holo ʻana o ka pāʻina ʻauʻau hiki ʻole ke hoʻokiʻekiʻe ʻia, mai ke kahakai ʻeleʻele, a hiki i ka pio ʻana o nā kukui o nā lumi hoʻokipa ma luna. I kona iho ʻana i lalo ma ke alapiʻi, ua huki ʻia ka ʻili ʻeleʻele ma luna o kona mau maka, a ʻālohilohi a luhi kona mau maka.

"ʻAʻole makemake ʻo ia," i ʻōlelo koke ai ʻo ia.

"ʻOiaʻiʻo ua hana ʻo ia."

"ʻAʻole makemake ʻo ia," i ʻōlelo ʻo ia. "ʻAʻole maikaʻi kona manawa."

Ua hāmau ʻo ia, a manaʻo wau i kona kaumaha hiki ʻole ke haʻi ʻia.

"Manaʻo wau e mamao loa mai ia," wahi āna. "He paʻakikī ke hoʻomaopopo iā ia."

"Ke manaʻo nei ʻoe e pili ana i ka hula?"

“Ka hula?” Ua hoʻokuʻu ʻo ia i nā hula a pau āna i hāʻawi ai me ka paʻi ʻana o kona mau manamana lima. "ʻO ka haʻuki kahiko, he mea nui ʻole ka hula."

Ua makemake ʻo ia iā Daisy ma mua o ka hele ʻana iā Tom a ʻōlelo: "ʻAʻole au i aloha iā ʻoe." Ma hope o kona hoʻopau ʻana i nā makahiki ʻehā me ia hoʻopaʻi, hiki iā lākou ke hoʻoholo i nā hana kūpono e lawe ʻia. ʻO kekahi o lākou, ma hope o kona kaʻawale ʻana, e hoʻi lākou i Louisville a male ʻia mai kona hale mai—e like me ʻelima mau makahiki i hala.

"A ʻaʻole maopopo iā ia," wahi āna. "Ua hiki iā ia ke hoʻomaopopo. Noho mākou no nā hola—"

Ua haki ʻo ia a hoʻomaka ʻo ia e hele i luna a i lalo i kahi ala neoneo o nā ʻili hua a hoʻolei i nā mea aloha a me nā pua i ʻoki ʻia.

"ʻAʻole au e nīnau nui iā ia," wahi aʻu. "ʻAʻole hiki iā ʻoe ke hana hou i ka wā i hala."

"ʻAʻole hiki ke hana hou i ka wā i hala?" uē ʻo ia me ka manaʻo ʻole. "No ke aha hiki iā ʻoe!"

Nana ae la oia a puni, me he mea la e pee ana na mea i hala ma ka malu o kona hale, i hiki ole mai i kona lima.

"E hoʻoponopono wau i nā mea a pau e like me ka wā ma mua," i ʻōlelo ai ʻo ia me ke kunou ʻana me ka manaʻo paʻa. "E ʻike ʻo ia."

Ua kamaʻilio nui ʻo ia e pili ana i ka wā i hala, a ua hōʻuluʻulu au ua makemake ʻo ia e hoʻihoʻi i kekahi mea, ʻo kekahi manaʻo nona iho paha, i komo i loko o ke aloha Daisy. Ua huikau a ua pilikia kona ola mai ia manawa mai, akā, inā hiki iā ia ke hoʻi i kahi wahi hoʻomaka a hele mālie, hiki iā ia ke ʻike i ke ʻano o ia mea…

… I kekahi pō hāʻule, ʻelima makahiki ma mua, ua hele lākou ma ke alanui i ka wā e hāʻule ana nā lau, a hiki lākou i kahi lāʻau ʻole a keʻokeʻo ke alahele i ka mālamalama o ka mahina. Kū lākou ma ʻaneʻi a huli kekahi i kekahi. He pō ʻoluʻolu kēia me kēlā hauʻoli pohihihi e hiki mai ana i nā hoʻololi ʻelua o ka makahiki. ʻO nā kukui mālie o nā hale e haʻalulu ana i loko o ka pōʻeleʻele a aia ka haunaele i waena o nā hōkū. Ma ke kihi o kona mau maka, ua ike aku la o Gatsby i ka hana maoli ana o na poloka o na alahele i alapii a kau aku la i kahi huna maluna o na kumulaau—hiki iaia ke pii iluna, ina e pii hookahi ana oia, a i ka manawa e hiki ai iaia ke omo i ka pa o ke ola, e inu iho i ka waiu kamahao lua ole.

ʻOi aku ka wikiwiki o kona puʻuwai i ka hele ʻana mai o ka helehelena keʻokeʻo o Daisy. Ua ʻike ʻo ia i kona honi ʻana i kēia kaikamahine, a hoʻopalau mau loa i kāna mau hihiʻo hiki ʻole ke haʻi ʻia i kona hanu palaho, ʻaʻole e holo hou kona manaʻo e like me ka manaʻo o ke Akua. No laila, kali ʻo ia me ka hoʻolohe ʻana i kahi manawa lōʻihi i ka pahu kani i paʻi ʻia i ka hōkū. A laila honi ʻo ia iā ia. I ka hoʻopā ʻana o kona mau lehelehe, ua pua ʻo ia nona me he pua lā a ua piha ka ʻike ʻana.

Ma nā mea a pau āna i ʻōlelo ai, ʻoiai ma o kāna mau manaʻo weliweli, ua hoʻomanaʻo ʻia iaʻu i kekahi mea - kahi puʻupuʻu paʻakikī, kahi ʻāpana o nā huaʻōlelo nalowale, aʻu i lohe ai i kahi manawa lōʻihi. No kekahi manawa, ua ho'āʻo kekahi huaʻōlelo e hana i loko o koʻu waha a kaʻawale koʻu mau lehelehe e like me ko ka lehelehe kanaka ʻaʻa, me he mea lā ua ʻoi aku ka hakakā ʻana ma luna o lākou ma mua o kahi puʻupuʻu o ka ea weliweli. ʻAʻole naʻe lākou i kani, a ʻo ka mea aʻu i hoʻomanaʻo ai ʻaʻole hiki ke kamaʻilio mau loa.
