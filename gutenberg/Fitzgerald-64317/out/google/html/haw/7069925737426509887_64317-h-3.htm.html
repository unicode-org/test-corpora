ʻO ia ka wā i ʻoi loa ai ka ʻike no Gatsby, ʻaʻole i pio nā kukui o kona hale i hoʻokahi pō Pōʻaono-a, me ka ʻike ʻole ʻia e like me ka mea i hoʻomaka ai, ua pau kāna ʻoihana ʻo Trimalchio. Ua ʻike liʻiliʻi iaʻu ʻo nā kaʻa i hoʻohuli ʻia i kāna kaʻa e noho ana no hoʻokahi minute wale nō, a laila kaʻa me ka huhū. Ke noʻonoʻo nei inā he maʻi ʻo ia, hele wau e ʻike - kahi kīʻaha kīʻaha ʻike ʻole me ka helehelena ʻino i nānā mai iaʻu me ka kānalua mai ka puka.

“Ua maʻi anei ʻo Mr. Gatsby?”

"ʻAʻole." Ma hope o ka hoʻomaha, hoʻohui ʻo ia iā "sir" ma ke ʻano hoʻomākeʻaka.

"ʻAʻole au i ʻike iā ia a puni, a ua hopohopo nui wau. E haʻi aku iā ia ua hele mai ʻo Mr. Carraway."

“ʻO wai?” ua koi ʻino ʻo ia.

“Carraway.”

"Carraway. ʻAe, e haʻi aku wau iā ia."

Paʻi koke ʻo ia i ka puka.

Ua hōʻike mai koʻu Finn iaʻu ua hoʻokuʻu ʻo Gatsby i kēlā me kēia kauā ma kona hale i hoʻokahi pule i hala aku nei a ua hoʻololi iā lākou me ka hapalua kakini ʻē aʻe, ʻaʻole lākou i hele i ke kauhale West Egg e kiʻi ʻia e nā mea kālepa, akā ua kauoha ʻo ia i nā lako kūpono ma ke kelepona. Ua hōʻike mai ke keiki kūʻai i ke ʻano o ka lumi kuke me he hale puaʻa, a ʻo ka manaʻo nui o ke kauhale, ʻaʻole ia he mau kauā ka poʻe hou.

I kekahi lā aʻe, kāhea mai ʻo Gatsby iaʻu ma ke kelepona.

“E hele ana?” Ua nīnau au.

"ʻAʻole, haʻuki kahiko."

"Ua lohe au ua kipaku oe i kau mau kauwa a pau."

"Makemake au i ka mea ʻakaʻaka ʻole. Hele pinepine mai ʻo Daisy i nā ʻauinalā."

"ʻO lākou kekahi poʻe a Wolfshiem i makemake ai e hana i kekahi mea. He mau kaikunāne lākou a pau.

"ʻike au."

E kāhea ana ʻo ia ma ke noi a Daisy—e hele mai anei au i ka ʻaina awakea ma kona hale i ka lā ʻapōpō? Ma laila ʻo Miss Baker. He hapalua hola ma hope mai ua kelepona ʻo Daisy iā ia iho a me he mea lā ua hoʻomaha ʻia i ka ʻike ʻana e hele mai ana au. Ua kū kekahi mea. A ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke manaʻoʻiʻo e koho lākou i kēia manawa no kahi hiʻohiʻona - ʻoi loa no ka hiʻohiʻona weliweli a Gatsby i hōʻike ai i loko o ka māla.

ʻO ka lā aʻe e puhi ana, kokoke i ka hope loa, ʻoiaʻiʻo ka wela loa o ke kauwela. I ka puka ʻana mai o koʻu kaʻaahi mai ka tunnel a hiki i ka lā, ʻo nā puʻupuʻu wela wale nō o ka National Biscuit Company i hoʻohāhā i ka ʻūhā ʻana i ke awakea. ʻO nā noho mauʻu o ke kaʻa e lele ana ma ka ʻaoʻao o ke ahi; ka wahine e pili ana iaʻu, ua hoʻoheheʻe maikaʻi ʻia no kekahi manawa i loko o kona pūhaka keʻokeʻo, a laila, i ka wā i pulu ai kāna nūpepa ma lalo o kona mau manamana lima, hāʻule me ka manaʻolana i loko o ka wela nui me ka uē mehameha. “Auē, koʻu!” ua hauʻoli ʻo ia.

Lawe au ia me ka luhi luhi a hoihoi aku la ia ia, me ka paa ana ma ka lima ka loihi a ma ka welau loa o na kihi e hoike mai ai aole o'u manao maluna o ia mea—akā, ua hoohuoi mai na mea a pau e kokoke mai ana, a me ka wahine, pela no au.

Ua hoʻi mai kaʻu tiketi hoʻokele iaʻu me kahi ʻeleʻele ʻeleʻele mai kona lima. E mālama pono kekahi i loko o kēia wela nona nā lehelehe ʻulaʻula āna i honi ai, ʻo kona poʻo hoʻi i pulu ka ʻeke pajama ma luna o kona puʻuwai!

“Ke kino o ka haku?” uwo aku la ka mea lawe kiaha i ka waha. “E kala mai, e ka wahine, akā, ʻaʻole hiki iā mākou ke hoʻolako iā ia—ua wela loa ke pā i kēia awakea!”

ʻO kāna mea i ʻōlelo maoli ai: “ʻAe… ʻAe… ʻike wau."

Hoʻonoho ʻo ia i ka mea hoʻokipa a hele mai i mua o mākou, ʻālohilohi iki, e lawe i kā mākou mau papale ʻoʻoleʻa.

ʻO ka lumi, i uhi maikaʻi ʻia me nā pale, ʻeleʻele a anuanu. E moe ana ʻo Daisy lāua ʻo Ioredane ma luna o kahi moe nui, e like me nā kiʻi kālā e kaumaha ana i ko lākou mau lole keʻokeʻo ponoʻī i ka makani mele o ka poʻe pā.

"ʻAʻole hiki iā mākou ke neʻe," wahi a lāua.

ʻO nā manamana lima o Ioredane, pauda keʻokeʻo ma luna o ko lākou ʻeleʻele, ua hoʻomaha iki i loko oʻu.

Kū ʻo Gatsby i waenakonu o ka ʻulaʻula a nānā aʻe me nā maka hoihoi. Nānā ʻo Daisy iā ia a ʻakaʻaka, ʻo kāna ʻakaʻaka ʻoluʻolu a hoihoi; ua pii ae ka puana pauda mai kona poli aku i ka lewa.

"ʻO ka lono," i hāwanawana ai ʻo Jordan, "ʻo ia ke kaikamahine a Tom ma ke kelepona."

Ua hāmau mākou. Piʻi aʻela ka leo i loko o ke keʻena me ka huhū: “ʻAe, a laila, ʻaʻole au e kūʻai aku i ke kaʻa iā ʻoe… ʻaʻohe oʻu kuleana iā ʻoe…

"ʻAʻole, ʻaʻole ia," hōʻoiaʻiʻo wau iā ia. "He kuʻikahi pono.

Wehe aʻela ʻo Tom i ka puka, ālai ʻia kona wahi me kona kino mānoanoa no kekahi manawa, a holo wikiwiki akula i loko o ka lumi.

“Mr. Gatsby!” Hoʻopuka ʻo ia i kona lima ākea ākea me ka hūnā pono ʻole. “ʻOliʻoli wau i ka ʻike ʻana iā ʻoe, e ka haku… Nick…”

"E hana i mea inu anu na makou," wahi a Daisy.

I kona haʻalele ʻana i ke keʻena hou, ala aʻela ʻo ia a hele akula i o Gatsby lā a huki maila i kona alo i lalo, a honi iā ia ma ka waha.

"Ua ʻike ʻoe ua aloha au iā ʻoe," i ʻōhumu ai ʻo ia.

“Poina iā ʻoe aia he makana wahine,” wahi a Jordan.

Nānā kanalua ʻo Daisy.

"Honi ʻoe iā Nick kekahi."

"He kaikamahine haʻahaʻa, ʻano ʻino!"

"Naʻu mālamaʻole!" i hooho ae ai o Daisy, a hoomaka ae la e pani i ke kapuahi pohaku. A laila hoʻomanaʻo ʻo ia i ka wela a noho hewa ʻo ia ma luna o kahi moe i ka wā i hele mai ai kahi kahu hānai hou e alakaʻi ana i kahi kaikamahine liʻiliʻi i loko o ka lumi.

"Pomaikai-sed pre-cious," i ʻōlelo ai ʻo ia me ka hoʻopuka ʻana i kona mau lima. "E hele mai i kou makuahine ponoi i aloha ia oe."

ʻO ke keiki, haʻalele ʻia e ke kahu, holo wikiwiki ʻo ia ma kēlā ʻaoʻao o ka lumi a ʻaʻa me ka hilahila i loko o ka lole o kona makuahine.

"The bless-sed pre-cious! Ua loaʻa anei i ka makuahine ka pauda ma kou lauoho melemele kahiko? E kū i luna i kēia manawa, a e ʻōlelo mai—Pehea-de-do."

ʻO wau a me Gatsby e kūlou i lalo a lawe i ka lima liʻiliʻi makemake ʻole. Mahope iho, nana aku la oia i ke keiki me ka pihoihoi. ʻAʻole wau i manaʻo ua manaʻoʻiʻo maoli ʻo ia i kona ola ʻana ma mua.

"ʻAe," i ʻae aku ai ke keiki me ka mālie. "Ua komo pū ʻo ʻAnakē Jordan i ke kapa keʻokeʻo."

"Pehea kou makemake i nā hoaaloha o ka makuahine?" Huli aʻela ʻo Daisy iā ia a kū i mua o Gatsby. "Manaʻo ʻoe he nani lākou?"

“ʻAuhea ʻo Pāpā?”

"ʻAʻole like ʻo ia me kona makuakāne," wehewehe ʻo Daisy. "Ua like ʻo ia me aʻu. Loaʻa iā ia koʻu lauoho a me ke ʻano o ka helehelena."

Noho hou ʻo Daisy ma luna o ka noho. Ua hele mua ke kahu a hāpai i kona lima.

"E hele mai, e Pammy."

“Aloha e ka ipo!”

Me ka nānā hope ʻole ʻana i hope, ua paʻa ke keiki aʻo maikaʻi i ka lima o kāna kahu hānai a huki ʻia i waho o ka puka, ʻoiai ʻo Tom i hoʻi mai ai, ma mua o ʻehā gin rickey e kaomi piha ana i ka hau.

Lawe ʻo Gatsby i kāna mea inu.

"Maikaʻi maoli lākou," i ʻōlelo ai ʻo ia, me ka ʻike maka.

Ua inu mākou i ka ʻai lōʻihi a puni.

"Heluhelu au ma kahi e wela ai ka lā i kēlā me kēia makahiki," wahi a Tom me ke akamai. “Me he mea lā e hāʻule koke ana ka honua i ka lā—a i ʻole e kali i hoʻokahi minute—ʻo ia ka ʻaoʻao—e maʻalili ana ka lā i kēlā me kēia makahiki.

"E hele mai i waho," ʻōlelo ʻo ia iā Gatsby, "makemake wau e nānā ʻoe i kēlā wahi."

Hele pū wau me lākou i waho i ka veranda. Ma ke kani ʻōmaʻomaʻo, kū i ka wela, ua kolo mālie kekahi peʻa liʻiliʻi i ke kai hou. Ua hahai nā maka o Gatsby iā ia i kekahi manawa; hāpai aʻela ʻo ia i kona lima a kuhikuhi i kēlā ʻaoʻao o ke kai kūʻono.

"Pēlā ʻoe."

Ua ʻaina awakea mākou i loko o ke keʻena ʻaina, ʻeleʻele hoʻi i ka wela, a inu i ka hauʻoli makaʻu me ka ʻale anu.

"He aha kā mākou e hana ai iā mākou iho i kēia ahiahi?" Kahea aku la o Daisy, "a i ka la mahope iho, a me na makahiki he kanakolu e hiki mai ana?"

Paʻa maila kona leo i ka wela, e kuʻi ana iā ia, e hoʻoheheʻe ana i kona ʻano naʻaupō.

"Ua lohe au i ka hana ʻana i hale kaʻa mai loko mai o ka hale kaʻa," wahi a Tom iā Gatsby, "akā ʻo wau ke kanaka mua i hana i ka hale kaʻa mai loko mai o ka hale kaʻa."

"ʻO wai ka makemake e hele i ke kūlanakauhale?" i koi ikaika ai o Daisy. Lana nā maka o Gatsby iā ia. "ʻAe," i uē ai ʻo ia, "ua nani kou nānā ʻana."

Halawai ko laua mau maka, a haka pono iho la laua i kekahi i kekahi, hookahi wale no ma ka lewa. Me ka hooikaika ana, nana iho la oia ilalo i ka papaaina.

"Maikaʻi kou nānā ʻana," wahi hou ʻo ia.

Ua haʻi ʻo ia iā ia ua aloha ʻo ia iā ia, a ʻike ʻo Tom Buchanan. Ua kahaha ʻo ia. Hāmama iki kona waha, a nānā akula ʻo ia iā Gatsby, a laila hoʻi hou iā Daisy me he mea lā ua ʻike ʻo ia iā ia he mea āna i ʻike ai i ka wā kahiko.

"Ua like ʻoe me ka hoʻolaha a ke kāne," wahi āna me ka hala ʻole. "Ua ike oe i ka hoolaha a ke kanaka—"

"Maikaʻi," wahi a Tom me ka wikiwiki, "Ua mākaukau loa wau e hele i ke kūlanakauhale.

Kū aʻela ʻo ia i luna me ka ʻālohilohi ʻana o kona mau maka ma waena o Gatsby a me kāna wahine. ʻAʻohe mea i neʻe.

"Hele mai!" Ua pohā iki kona huhū. "He aha ka mea, ʻoiai? Inā mākou e hele i ke kūlanakauhale, e hoʻomaka kāua."

ʻO kona lima e haʻalulu ana me kāna hoʻoikaika ʻana i ka hoʻomalu ʻana iā ia iho, ua hāʻawi i kona mau lehelehe i ka hope o kāna aniani ʻale. Na ka leo o Daisy i hoʻāla iā mākou i ko mākou mau wāwae a i waho i ke kaʻa ʻiliʻili ahi.

"E hele wale ana anei kakou?" ua kūʻē ʻo ia. "Penei? ʻAʻole anei kākou e ʻae i ka puhi paka mua?"

"Ua puhi nā kānaka a pau i ka ʻaina awakea."

"ʻAe, e leʻaleʻa kāua," i noi ai ʻo ia iā ia. "He wela loa no ka hoʻopiʻi."

Ua lele mua ʻia kahi ʻāpana kālā o ka mahina i ka lani komohana. Hoʻomaka ʻo Gatsby e ʻōlelo, hoʻololi i kona manaʻo, akā ʻaʻole ma mua o ka huila ʻana o Tom a kū pono iā ia.

"E hele mākou a pau ma koʻu kaʻa?" i ʻōlelo ai ʻo Gatsby. Ua ʻike ʻo ia i ka ʻili wela a me ka ʻōmaʻomaʻo o ka noho. "Pono wau i waiho i ka malu."

"ʻAʻole au i manaʻo he nui ke kinoea," i kūʻē ʻo ia.

"E hele mai, e Daisy," wahi a Tom, me ke kaomi ʻana i kona lima i ke kaʻa o Gatsby. "E lawe au iā ʻoe i loko o kēia kaʻa kaʻa kaʻa."

Wehe ʻo ia i ka puka, akā ua neʻe ʻo ia mai ka pōʻai o kona lima.

"E lawe ʻoe iā Nick a me Ioredane. E hahai mākou iā ʻoe i ka coupé."

Hele ʻo ia a kokoke iā Gatsby, hoʻopā i kona ʻaʻahu me kona lima. Ua komo māua ʻo Jordan lāua ʻo Tom i ka noho mua o ke kaʻa o Gatsby, ua hoʻokuke ʻo Tom i nā kaʻa kamaʻāina ʻole, a pana mākou i loko o ka wela hoʻokaumaha, a haʻalele iā lākou ma hope.

"Ua ʻike anei ʻoe i kēlā?" wahi a Tom.

“E ʻike i ke aha?”

Nana mai la oia ia'u me ka ike pono ua ike iho la maua me Ioredane.

"Ke manaʻo nei ʻoe he pupule wau, ʻaʻole anei?" ua ʻōlelo ʻo ia. "ʻO wau nō paha, akā loaʻa iaʻu kahi ʻike ʻelua, i kekahi manawa, e haʻi mai iaʻu i ka mea e hana ai. ʻAʻole paha ʻoe e manaʻoʻiʻo i kēlā, akā ʻo ka ʻepekema—"

Hoʻomaha ʻo ia. Ua loaʻa koke iā ia ka pilikia, a huki iā ia mai ka lihi o ka hohonu hohonu.

"Ua hana wau i kahi noiʻi liʻiliʻi o kēia kanaka," i hoʻomau ai ʻo ia. "Ua hiki iaʻu ke hele hohonu inā wau i ʻike—"

"Makemake paha ʻoe i hele i kahi ʻoihana?" i ninau aka ai o Ioredane.
