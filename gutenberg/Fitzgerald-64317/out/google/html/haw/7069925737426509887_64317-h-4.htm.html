VIII

ʻAʻole hiki iaʻu ke hiamoe i ka pō a pau; ua uwē mau ʻia kahi pua pua i ka Sound, a ua hoʻolei au i ka hapalua maʻi ma waena o ka ʻoiaʻiʻo maoli a me nā moeʻuhane weliweli. Ma ka wanaʻao ua lohe au i ka piʻi ʻana o kahi kaʻa kaʻa i ke kaʻa o Gatsby, a lele koke wau mai kahi moe a hoʻomaka wau e ʻaʻahu - ua manaʻo wau he mea kaʻu e haʻi aku ai iā ia, kahi mea e aʻo aku ai iā ia, a ua lohi ke kakahiaka.

I koʻu hele ʻana i kona lau nahele, ʻike wau e hāmama ana kona puka ma mua a e hilinaʻi ana ʻo ia i kahi papaʻaina ma ke keʻena me ke kaumaha a me ka hiamoe.

"ʻAʻohe mea i hiki," i ʻōlelo ʻo ia me ka haʻahaʻa. "Kakali au, a ma ka hora ʻehā paha hiki mai ʻo ia i ka puka makani a kū i laila no hoʻokahi minute a laila hoʻopau ke kukui."

ʻAʻole i like ka nui o kona hale iaʻu e like me ia i kēlā pō i ko mākou ʻimi ʻana i nā keʻena nui no ka paka. Ua hoʻokuʻu mākou i nā pale e like me nā pavilion, a manaʻo mākou ma luna o nā kapuaʻi he nui o ka paia ʻeleʻele no nā hoʻololi kukui uila - hoʻokahi manawa aʻu i hāʻule ai me kahi ʻano pākī ma nā kī o kahi piano ʻuhane. He nui ka lepo hiki ole ke hoomaopopo ia ma na wahi a pau, a he ohu na keena, me he mea la aole i hoolele ia no na la he nui. Ua loaʻa iaʻu ka humidor ma kahi pākaukau ʻike ʻole, me ʻelua mau paka maloʻo maloʻo i loko. I ka wehe ʻana i nā pukaaniani Farani o ke keʻena hoʻokipa, noho mākou i ka puhi paka i waho o ka pouli.

"Pono ʻoe e hele," wahi aʻu. "He mea maopopo loa e hahai lākou i kāu kaʻa."

“E hele i kēia manawa , e ka haʻuki kahiko?”

"E hele i Atlantic City no hoʻokahi pule, a i ʻole i Montreal."

ʻAʻole ʻo ia e noʻonoʻo. ʻAʻole hiki iā ia ke haʻalele iā Daisy a hiki i kona ʻike ʻana i kāna mea e hana ai. Ua paʻa ʻo ia i kahi manaʻolana hope loa a ʻaʻole hiki iaʻu ke hoʻokuʻu iā ia.

ʻO kēia pō ʻo ia i haʻi mai ai iaʻu i ka moʻolelo ʻano ʻē o kona wā ʻōpio me Dan Cody—i haʻi mai iaʻu no ka mea, ua haki ʻo "Jay Gatsby" me he aniani lā e kūʻē ana i ka ʻinoʻino o Tom, a ua pāʻani ʻia ke ʻano mea huna lōʻihi. Manaʻo wau ua ʻae ʻo ia i kekahi mea i kēia manawa, me ka ʻole o ka mālama ʻana, akā makemake ʻo ia e kamaʻilio e pili ana iā Daisy.

ʻO ia ke kaikamahine "maikaʻi" mua āna i ʻike ai. I loko o nā mana like ʻole i hōʻike ʻole ʻia, ua launa pū ʻo ia me ia poʻe, akā i nā manawa a pau me ka uea ʻaʻa ʻole i ʻike ʻia ma waena. Ua ʻike ʻo ia he mea hoihoi loa ia. Hele ʻo ia i kona hale, ma mua me nā luna ʻē aʻe mai Camp Taylor, a laila hoʻokahi wale nō. He mea kāhāhā iā ia—ʻaʻole ʻo ia i komo i loko o kahi hale nani like ma mua. Akā, ʻo ka mea i hāʻawi ai i ka ea o ka hanu ʻole, ʻo ia ka noho ʻana o Daisy ma laila-he mea maʻamau ia iā ia e like me kona hale lole ma waho o kahi hoʻomoana iā ia. He mea pohihihi e pili ana i ia mea, he ʻike o nā lumi moe ma luna i ʻoi aku ka nani a me ka ʻoluʻolu ma mua o nā keʻena moe ʻē aʻe, nā hana hoʻohiwahiwa a me ka nani e hana ʻia ana ma kona mau alahele, a me nā aloha i hoʻomaʻemaʻe ʻole ʻia a waiho ʻia i loko o ka lavender akā hou a me ka hanu a me ka ʻulaʻula o nā kaʻa ʻālohilohi o kēia makahiki a me nā hula ʻaʻole maloʻo nā pua. Ua hauʻoli hoʻi iā ia, no ka nui o nā kāne i aloha iā Daisy—ua hoʻonui ia i kona waiwai i mua o kona mau maka. Ua ʻike ʻo ia i ko lākou hele ʻana a puni ka hale, e hoʻopuni ana i ka lewa me nā aka a me nā leo o nā manaʻo e ʻoi aku ka ikaika.

Akā, ua ʻike ʻo ia aia ʻo ia ma ka hale o Daisy ma muli o kahi ulia pōpilikia. ʻOiai ka hanohano o kona wā e hiki mai ana e like me Jay Gatsby, he kanaka ʻōpio nele ʻo ia i kēia manawa me ka hala ʻole o ka wā i hala, a i kēlā me kēia manawa e paheʻe ke koloka ʻike ʻole ʻia o kona ʻaʻahu mai kona poʻohiwi. No laila ua hoʻohana ʻo ia i ka hapa nui o kona manawa. Lawe ʻo ia i ka mea hiki iā ia ke loaʻa, me ka huhū a me ka hana ʻole—ma hope ua lawe ʻo ia iā Daisy i hoʻokahi pō o ʻOkakopa, lawe iā ia no ka mea ʻaʻohe ona kuleana maoli e hoʻopā i kona lima.

Ua hoʻowahāwahā paha ʻo ia iā ia iho, no ka mea, ua lawe ʻoiaʻiʻo ʻo ia iā ia ma ka hoʻopunipuni hoʻopunipuni. ʻAʻole wau i manaʻo ua kālepa ʻo ia ma kāna mau miliona miliona, akā ua hāʻawi ʻo ia iā Daisy i kahi manaʻo palekana; Ua hoʻokuʻu ʻo ia iā ia e manaʻoʻiʻo he kanaka ʻo ia mai nā ʻano like ʻole me ia iho—ua hiki iā ia ke mālama pono iā ia. ʻO ka mea ʻoiaʻiʻo, ʻaʻohe ona hale hana e like me ia - ʻaʻohe ona ʻohana ʻoluʻolu e kū ana ma hope ona, a ua kuleana ʻo ia ma ka makemake o ke aupuni pilikino e puhi ʻia ma nā wahi āpau o ka honua.

Akā, ʻaʻole ʻo ia i hoʻowahāwahā iā ia iho a ʻaʻole i like ia me kāna i manaʻo ai. Ua manaʻo paha ʻo ia e lawe i ka mea hiki iā ia a hele - akā i kēia manawa ua ʻike ʻo ia ua hoʻohiki ʻo ia iā ia iho ma muli o kahi grail. Ua ʻike ʻo ia he mea kupaianaha ʻo Daisy, akā ʻaʻole ʻo ia i ʻike i ke ʻano kupaianaha o ke kaikamahine "maikaʻi". Ua nalo ʻo ia i loko o kona hale waiwai, i loko o kona ola waiwai piha, haʻalele iā Gatsby-ʻaʻohe mea. Ua manaʻo ʻo ia i ka male ʻana iā ia, ʻo ia wale nō.

I ko lāua hui hou ʻana, ʻelua lā ma hope mai, ʻo Gatsby ka hanu, ʻo ia hoʻi, ua kumakaia. Ua nani kona lanai me ka nani o ka hoku; uwē ʻo ka wicker o ka settee i kona huli ʻana iā ia a honi ʻo ia i kona waha hoihoi a nani. Ua loaa iaia i ke anu, a ua oi aku ka nani o kona leo ma mua o ka wā ma mua, a ua ʻike nui ʻo Gatsby i ka ʻōpio a me ka mea pohihihi e hoʻopaʻa nei a mālama ʻia ka waiwai, ka hou o nā lole he nui, a me Daisy, e ʻālohilohi ana e like me ke kālā, palekana a haʻaheo ma luna o nā hakakā wela o ka poʻe ʻilihune.

"ʻAʻole hiki iaʻu ke haʻi aku iā ʻoe i koʻu hauʻoli i ka ʻike ʻana ua aloha wau iā ia, ka haʻuki kahiko. Ua manaʻo wau e hoʻolei mai ʻo ia iaʻu, akā ʻaʻole ʻo ia, no ka mea, ua aloha pū ʻo ia iaʻu. he aha kaʻu e hana ai?”

I ke awakea hope loa ma mua o kona hele ʻana i waho, ua noho ʻo ia me Daisy ma kona mau lima no ka manawa lōʻihi a hāmau. He lā hāʻule anuanu ia, me ke ahi i loko o ka lumi a ʻulaʻula kona mau papalina. I kēia manawa, neʻe ʻo ia a hoʻololi iki ʻo ia i kona lima, a honi ʻo ia i kona lauoho ʻālohilohi. Ua hoʻomaha iki ka ʻauinalā iā lākou no kekahi manawa, me he mea lā e hāʻawi ana i kahi hoʻomanaʻo hohonu no ka hoʻokaʻawale lōʻihi ʻana i ka lā aʻe i ʻōlelo ʻia. ʻAʻole loa lāua i pili i ko lāua mau mahina aloha, ʻaʻole hoʻi i kamaʻilio hohonu kekahi me kekahi, ma mua o ka wā i hoʻopaʻa ai ʻo ia i nā lehelehe hāmau ma ka poʻohiwi o kona ʻaʻahu a i ʻole i kona hoʻopā ʻana i ka wēlau o kona mau manamana lima, me he mea lā ua hiamoe ʻo ia.

Ua hana maikaʻi ʻo ia i ke kaua. He kāpena ʻo ia ma mua o kona hele ʻana i mua, a ma hope o nā kaua Argonne ua loaʻa iā ia ka hapa nui a me ke alakaʻi ʻana i nā pū mīkini māhele. Ma hope o ka armistice ua hoʻāʻo ʻo ia e hoʻi i ka home, akā naʻe, ua hoʻouna ʻia ʻo ia e kekahi pilikia a i ʻole hoʻomaopopo ʻana iā ia i Oxford. Ua hopohopo ʻo ia i kēia manawa - aia kekahi ʻano o ka manaʻo hopohopo i loko o nā leka a Daisy. ʻAʻole ʻo ia i ʻike i ke kumu hiki ʻole iā ia ke hele mai. Ke manaʻo nei ʻo ia i ke kaomi o ka honua ma waho, a makemake ʻo ia e ʻike iā ia a manaʻo i kona noho ʻana ma hope ona a e hōʻoiaʻiʻo ʻo ia e hana ana ʻo ia i ka mea kūpono.

No ka mea he ʻōpio ʻo Daisy a ʻo kāna honua hana i hoʻonani ʻia i nā orchids a me nā leʻaleʻa ʻoluʻolu a me nā mea hoʻokani pila e hoʻonohonoho i ke kani o ka makahiki, e hōʻuluʻulu ana i ke kaumaha a me ka manaʻo o ke ola i nā mele hou. ʻO ka pō a pau e uē ana nā saxophones i ka manaʻo manaʻo ʻole o ka "Beale Street Blues" ʻoiai he haneli mau paʻa paʻa gula a me ke kālā e hoʻoluliluli ana i ka lepo ʻālohilohi. I ka hola kī hina, he mau keʻena e ʻōpuʻu mau ana me kēia wela haʻahaʻa, ʻoiai ʻo nā helehelena hou e hee ana ma ʻō a ma ʻaneʻi e like me nā pua rose i puhi ʻia e nā pū kanikau a puni ka papahele.

Ma kēia ao pōuliuli ua hoʻomaka ʻo Daisy e neʻe hou me ke kau; E malama hou ana oia i ka hapalua kakini i ka la me ka hapalua kakini kanaka, a e hiamoe ana i ka wanaao me na lei a me ka lole o ke kapa ahiahi i wili pu ia mawaena o na orchids e make ana ma ka papahele ma ka aoao o kona wahi moe. A i nā manawa a pau e uē ana kekahi mea i loko ona no ka hoʻoholo ʻana. Ua makemake ʻo ia e hoʻololi koke ʻia kona ola i kēia manawa - a pono e hoʻoholo ʻia e kekahi ikaika - ʻo ke aloha, ke kālā, ka hoʻomaʻamaʻa ʻole ʻia - kokoke loa.

Hoʻokumu ʻia kēlā ikaika i ka waena o ka pūnāwai me ka hōʻea ʻana mai o Tom Buchanan. Aia kekahi puʻupuʻu maikaʻi e pili ana i kona kino a me kona kūlana, a ua ʻoluʻolu ʻo Daisy. He kanalua paha, aia kekahi paio a me kekahi hoomaha. Ua hōʻea ka leka iā Gatsby ʻoiai ʻo ia ma Oxford.

ʻO ka wanaʻao i kēia manawa ma Long Island a hele mākou e wehe i ke koena o nā puka makani ma lalo, e hoʻopiha ana i ka hale me nā kukui hoʻololi gula. Hāʻule koke ke aka o kahi lāʻau ma luna o ka hau a hoʻomaka nā manu ʻuhane e hīmeni i waena o nā lau uliuli. He lohi, ʻoluʻolu ka neʻe ʻana i ka lewa, ʻaʻohe makani, e hoʻohiki ana i kahi lā ʻoluʻolu a nani.

"Manaʻo wau ʻaʻole ʻo ia i aloha iā ia." Huli aʻela ʻo Gatsby mai ka puka makani a nānā mai iaʻu me ka hoʻohewahewa. "E hoʻomanaʻo ʻoe, e ka haʻuki kahiko, ua hauʻoli loa ʻo ia i kēia ahiahi. Ua haʻi ʻo ia iā ia i kēlā mau mea ma ke ʻano e makaʻu ai iā ia - ʻo ia ka mea i ʻike ʻia me he mea lā he ʻano ʻoi aku ka ʻoi.

Noho ʻo ia i lalo me ke kaumaha.

"ʻOiaʻiʻo paha ua aloha ʻo ia iā ia no hoʻokahi minute wale nō, i ko lāua male mua ʻana - a aloha nui iaʻu i kēlā manawa, ʻike ʻoe?"

ʻAʻole koke ʻo ia i puka mai me kahi ʻōlelo hoihoi.

"Ma kekahi hihia," i ʻōlelo ai ʻo ia, "he pilikino wale nō ia."

He aha kāu e hana ai no ia mea, koe wale nō ka manaʻo i ka ikaika o kona manaʻo i ka hihia hiki ʻole ke ana ʻia?

Ua hoʻi mai ʻo ia mai Farani mai i ka wā e hele mau ana ʻo Tom lāua ʻo Daisy i kā lāua huakaʻi male, a ua hana ʻo ia i kahi huakaʻi pōʻino akā hiki ʻole ke pale aku i Louisville ma ka uku hope loa o kāna pūʻali koa. Ua noho ʻo ia ma laila i hoʻokahi pule, e hele ana ma nā alanui kahi a ko lākou mau kapuaʻi i paʻi pū ai i ka pō o Nowemapa a nānā hou i nā wahi i waho o ke ala a lākou i holo ai ma kāna kaʻa keʻokeʻo. E like me ka hale o Daisy i kona manao he oi aku ka pohihihi a me ke kane mamua o na hale e ae, pela no kona manao no ke kulanakauhale ponoi, oiai oia i hala aku, ua paapu ia me ka nani melancholy.

Ua haʻalele ʻo ia me ka manaʻo inā ua ʻimi ikaika ʻo ia, ua loaʻa paha ʻo ia iā ia—e haʻalele ana ʻo ia iā ia. ʻO ke kaʻi o ka lā-ua nele ʻo ia i kēia manawa-ua wela. Hele akula ʻo ia a hiki i ka puka hāmama a noho ihola ma luna o kahi noho peʻe, a paheʻe akula ke kahua hoʻokele a neʻe aku ke kua o nā hale kamaʻāina ʻole. A laila, i waho i loko o ke kula punawai, kahi i holo ai kahi kaʻa kaʻa melemele iā lākou no hoʻokahi minute me nā poʻe i loko o laila i ʻike paha i ke kilokilo haʻihaʻi o kona mau helehelena ma ke alanui ʻoluʻolu.

Ua piʻo ke alahele a i kēia manawa ke hele aku nei ia mai ka lā, ʻoiai ʻo ia e hāʻule ana i lalo, me he mea lā e laha ana iā ia iho i ka hoʻomaikaʻi ʻana i ke kūlanakauhale nalowale kahi āna i huki ai i kona hanu. O aku la oia i kona lima me he mea la e kaili wale aku i ka ea, e hoopakele i kahi apana wahi ana i hoonani ai nona. Akā, ua holo wikiwiki loa ia i kēia manawa no kona mau maka pohihihi a ʻike ʻo ia ua nalowale kēlā ʻāpana o ia mea, ka mea hou a maikaʻi loa, a mau loa.

I ka hola ʻeiwa i pau ai ka ʻaina kakahiaka a hele i waho ma ka lānai. Ua ʻoi aku ka maikaʻi o ka pō i ka lewa a he ʻono hoʻi i ka lewa. ʻO ka mea māla, ka mea hope loa o nā kauā mua a Gatsby, hele mai i lalo o nā ʻanuʻu.

"E hoʻokahe au i ka wai i kēia lā, e Mr. Gatsby. E hoʻomaka koke ana nā lau e hāʻule, a laila pilikia mau nā paipu."

"Mai hana ʻoe i kēia lā," wahi a Gatsby. Ua huli mai ʻo ia iaʻu me ke kala ʻana. "Ua ʻike ʻoe, e ka haʻuki kahiko, ʻaʻole wau i hoʻohana i kēlā wai wai i ke kauwela āpau?"

Nānā wau i koʻu wati a kū i luna.

"He ʻumikūmālua mau minuke i koʻu kaʻaahi."

ʻAʻole au i makemake e hele i ke kūlanakauhale. ʻAʻole i kūpono iaʻu kahi hana maikaʻi, akā ʻoi aku ia ma mua o kēlā - ʻaʻole wau makemake e haʻalele iā Gatsby. Ua hala au i kēlā kaʻaahi, a laila kekahi, ma mua o koʻu hiki ʻana iaʻu iho.

"E kāhea wau iā ʻoe," i ʻōlelo hope ai au.

"E hana, kahiko haʻuki."

"E kelepona wau iā ʻoe ma ke awakea."

Hele mālie mākou i lalo o nā alapiʻi.

"Manaʻo wau e kelepona pū ʻo Daisy." Nānā mai ʻo ia iaʻu me ka hopohopo, me he mea lā ua manaʻo ʻo ia e hōʻoiaʻiʻo wau i kēia.

"Manaʻo wau pēlā."

"ʻAe, aloha."

Lulu lima mākou a hoʻomaka wau. Ma mua o koʻu hiki ʻana i ka pā, hoʻomanaʻo wau i kekahi mea a huli aʻe.

“He lehulehu popopo lākou,” i hoʻōho ai au ma ka lau nahele. "Pono ʻoe i ka hui a pau i hui pū ʻia."

Ua hauʻoli mau wau i kaʻu ʻōlelo ʻana pēlā. ʻO ia wale nō kaʻu hoʻomaikaʻi ʻana iā ia, no ka mea, ua hōʻole wau iā ia mai ka mua a i ka hope. ʻAkahi ʻo ia i kunou ʻoluʻolu ai, a laila ua pohā kona mau maka i loko o kēlā ʻakaʻaka ʻālohilohi a me ka hoʻomaopopo ʻana, me he mea lā aia mākou i loko o ka puʻuwai hauʻoli i kēlā ʻoiaʻiʻo i nā manawa a pau. ʻO kāna lole ʻulaʻula nani o kahi lole i hoʻokaʻawale i kahi ʻulaʻula o ka waihoʻoluʻu e kū pono ana i nā ʻanuʻu keʻokeʻo, a noʻonoʻo wau i ka pō i hiki mua ai au i kona home kupuna, ʻekolu mahina ma mua. Ua paapu ka mauu a me ke kaa i na helehelena o ka poe nana i manao i kona hewa—a ua ku oia ma ia mau anuu, me ka huna ana i kana moeuhane palaho ole, me ka hoolele ana aku ia lakou.

Mahalo wau iā ia no kāna hoʻokipa. Mahalo mau mākou iā ia no kēlā—ʻo wau a me nā mea ʻē aʻe.

"Aloha," i kāhea aku ai au. “Nanea au i ka ʻaina kakahiaka, e Gatsby.”

Ma luna o ke kūlanakauhale, ua ho'āʻo wau no kekahi manawa e hoʻopaʻa inoa i nā hua'ōlelo ma kahi nui o ka waiwai, a laila ua hiamoe au ma koʻu noho wili. Ma mua o ke awakea, ua hoʻāla ke kelepona iaʻu, a ua hoʻomaka wau me ka hou ʻana o ka hou ma koʻu lae. ʻO Jordan Baker; Ua kāhea pinepine ʻo ia iaʻu i kēia hola no ka maopopo ʻole o kāna mau neʻe ponoʻī ma waena o nā hōkele a me nā hale kalapu a me nā hale pilikino i paʻakikī iā ia ke loaʻa ma kekahi ʻano ʻē aʻe. ʻO ka mea maʻamau ke kani ʻana o kona leo ma luna o ka uea me he mea lā he mea hou a ʻoluʻolu, me he mea lā e holo mai ana kahi divot mai kahi loulou kolepa ʻōmaʻomaʻo ma ka puka makani o ke keʻena, akā i kēia kakahiaka me he mea lā he ʻoʻoleʻa a maloʻo.

"Ua haʻalele au i ka hale o Daisy," wahi āna. "Aia wau ma Hempstead, a ke iho nei au i Southampton i kēia ahiahi."

Ua noʻonoʻo paha ka haʻalele ʻana i ka hale o Daisy, akā naʻe, ua hoʻonāukiuki kēia hana iaʻu, a ʻo kāna ʻōlelo hou aʻe i paʻakikī iaʻu.

"ʻAʻole maikaʻi ʻoe iaʻu i ka pō nei."

"Pehea i hiki ai ia manawa?"

Hamau no kekahi manawa. A laila:

"Akā—makemake au e ʻike iā ʻoe."

"Makemake au e ʻike iā ʻoe."

"Inā ʻaʻole wau e hele i Southampton, a hele mai i ke kūlanakauhale i kēia ahiahi?"

"ʻAʻole—ʻaʻole wau i manaʻo i kēia ahiahi."

"Maikaʻi loa."

"He mea hiki ʻole i kēia ahiahi. Nā ʻano—"

Ua kamaʻilio mākou pēlā no kekahi manawa, a laila ʻaʻole mākou e kamaʻilio hou. ʻAʻole maopopo iaʻu ʻo wai o mākou i hoʻopaʻa ʻia me kahi kaomi ʻoi, akā ʻike wau ʻaʻole wau i mālama. ʻAʻole hiki iaʻu ke kamaʻilio pū me ia ma kahi papa kīʻaha i kēlā lā inā ʻaʻole wau e kamaʻilio hou me ia ma kēia ao.

Ua kāhea au i ka hale o Gatsby ma hope o kekahi mau minuke, akā paʻa ka laina. ʻEhā au hoʻāʻo; ʻO ka hope, ua haʻi mai kahi kikowaena huhū iaʻu e wehe ʻia ana ke kelepona no kahi lōʻihi mai Detroit. I ka lawe ʻana i kaʻu papa manawa, huki au i kahi pōʻai liʻiliʻi a puni ke kaʻaahi ʻekolu-50. A laila, hilinaʻi au ma koʻu noho a hoʻāʻo e noʻonoʻo. ʻO ke awakea wale nō.

I koʻu hala ʻana i nā puʻu lehu ma luna o ke kaʻaahi ma ia kakahiaka, ua hele pololei au i kēlā ʻaoʻao o ke kaʻa. Manaʻo wau aia ka lehulehu a puni i laila i ka lā a pau me nā keikikāne liʻiliʻi e ʻimi ana i nā wahi ʻeleʻele o ka lepo, a e haʻi pinepine ana kekahi kanaka palaualelo i ka mea i hana ʻia, a hiki i ka liʻiliʻi ʻana o ka mea maoli iā ia a ʻaʻole hiki iā ia ke haʻi hou aku, a ua poina ka hana pōʻino o Myrtle Wilson. I kēia manawa makemake wau e hoʻi iki a haʻi i ka mea i hana ʻia ma ka hale kaʻa ma hope o ko mākou haʻalele ʻana i laila i ka pō ma mua.

Ua pilikia lākou i ka ʻimi ʻana i ke kaikuahine ʻo Catherine. Ua uhaki paha ʻo ia i kāna kānāwai kūʻē i ka inu ʻana i kēlā pō, no ka mea, i kona hiki ʻana mai, ua hūpō ʻo ia me ka ʻona a ʻaʻole hiki ke hoʻomaopopo ua hele mua ke kaʻa kaʻa i Flushing. I ko lakou hooiaio ana iaia no keia mea, maule koke iho la oia, me he mea la, oia ka mea hiki ole ke hoomanawanui. Ua lawe kekahi kanaka iā ia i loko o kāna kaʻa a hoʻokele iā ia ma hope o ke kino o kona kaikuahine.

A hiki i ka lōʻihi ma hope o ke aumoe, ua hoʻololi ka lehulehu i mua o ka hale kaʻa, ʻoiai ʻo George Wilson i hoʻoluliluli iā ia iho ma luna o ka noho o loko. No kekahi manawa ua hāmama ka puka o ke keʻena, a ʻo ka poʻe a pau i komo i loko o ka hale kaʻa e nānā pono ana ma loko. ʻO ka hope, ʻōlelo kekahi he mea hilahila, a pani i ka puka. ʻO Michaelis a me kekahi mau kānaka ʻē aʻe me ia; mua, ʻehā a ʻelima kāne, ma hope ʻelua a ʻekolu kāne. Ma hope mai, ua noi ʻo Michaelis i ka malihini hope loa e kali ma laila he ʻumikumamālima mau minuke, ʻoiai ʻo ia e hoʻi i kona wahi ponoʻī a hana i ka ipu kofe. Ma hope o kēlā, noho hoʻokahi ʻo ia me Wilson a hiki i ka wanaʻao.

Ma kahi o ka hola ʻekolu, ua loli ke ʻano o ke ʻano o ka ʻōlelo ʻino ʻole a Wilson-ua mālie ʻo ia a hoʻomaka ʻo ia e kamaʻilio e pili ana i ke kaʻa melemele. Ua hoolaha mai oia he ala kona e ike ai i ka mea nona ke kaa melemele, a laila, ua hoopuka ae oia i kekahi mau mahina i hala aku nei ua hele mai kana wahine mai ke kulanakauhale mai me ka eha o ka maka a me ka pehu o ka ihu.

Akā, i kona lohe ʻana iā ia iho e ʻōlelo ana i kēia, kāʻili ʻo ia a hoʻomaka e uē, "Auē, e kuʻu Akua!" hou i kona leo kanikau. Ua hoʻāʻo ʻo Michaelis e hoʻohuli iā ia.

"Pehea ka lōʻihi o kou male ʻana, e George? E hele mai ʻoe i laila, e hoʻāʻo e noho mālie i hoʻokahi minute, a e pane i kaʻu nīnau. Pehea ka lōʻihi o kou male ʻana?"

"He ʻumikūmālua makahiki."

"Ua loaa ia oe na keiki? E hele mai oe, e George, e noho malie—ua ninau aku au ia oe. He keiki anei au?"

Ke kuʻi mau nei nā pīkī ʻeleʻele paʻakikī i ke kukui ʻulaʻula, a i kēlā me kēia manawa e lohe ai ʻo Michaelis i kahi kaʻa e haehae ana ma ke alanui ma waho, ua like ia me ke kaʻa i kū ʻole i kekahi mau hola ma mua. ʻAʻole ʻo ia i makemake e komo i loko o ka hale kaʻa, no ka mea, ua haumia ka papa hana ma kahi o ke kino i waiho ʻia ai, no laila ua neʻe ʻo ia me ka ʻoluʻolu ʻole a puni ke keʻena—ua ʻike ʻo ia i kēlā me kēia mea i loko ma mua o ke kakahiaka—a i kēlā me kēia manawa ua noho ʻo ia ma ka ʻaoʻao o Wilson e hoʻāʻo e hoʻomaha iā ia.

"He hale pule anei kāu e hele ai i kekahi manawa, e George? Malia paha inā lōʻihi kou hele ʻana i laila? Malia paha hiki iaʻu ke kāhea aku i ka halepule a kiʻi i kahuna e hele mai a hiki iā ia ke kamaʻilio pū me ʻoe, ʻike?"

"Mai pili i kekahi."

"Pono e loaa ia oe he halepule, e George, no na manawa e like me keia. Ua hele paha oe i ka pule hookahi. Aole anei oe i mare ma ka halepule? E hoolohe mai oe e George, e hoolohe mai ia'u. Aole anei oe i mare ma ka halepule?"

"He manawa lōʻihi kēlā."

ʻO ka hoʻoikaika ʻana o ka pane ʻana i uhaʻi i ka puʻupuʻu o kāna luliluli—no kekahi manawa ua hāmau ʻo ia. A laila ua hoʻi hou mai ka ʻike hapalua like ʻole a me ka haʻahaʻa i loko o kona mau maka mae.

"E nānā i ka pahu pahu ma laila," wahi āna, me ke kuhikuhi ʻana i ke pākaukau.

“ʻO ka pahu pahu hea?”

"ʻO kēlā pahu huki—ʻo kēlā."

Ua wehe ʻo Michaelis i ka pahu huki kokoke i kona lima. ʻAʻohe mea ʻē aʻe i loko o ia mea he liʻiliʻi liʻiliʻi liʻiliʻi pipiʻi, i hana ʻia me ka ʻili a me ke kālā wili. He mea hou ia.

“ʻO kēia?” i ninau aku ai oia me ka paa ana.

Nānā aʻela ʻo Wilson a kunou.

"Ua loaʻa iaʻu i ka ʻauinalā i nehinei. Ua hoʻāʻo ʻo ia e haʻi mai iaʻu no ia mea, akā ʻike wau he mea ʻakaʻaka."

“Ua kūʻai mai kāu wahine?”

"Ua uhi ʻia ʻo ia i ka pepa kiko ma kāna keʻena."

ʻAʻole i ʻike ʻo Michaelis i kekahi mea ʻē aʻe i loko o ia mea, a hāʻawi ʻo ia iā Wilson i mau kumu he ʻumi kumu i kūʻai ai kāna wahine i ka lei ʻīlio. Akā, ua lohe mua ʻo Wilson i kekahi o kēia mau wehewehe ʻana, mai Myrtle mai, no ka mea, ua hoʻomaka ʻo ia e ʻōlelo "Auē, e kuʻu Akua!" hou me ka hawanawana—ua waiho mai kana mea hooluolu i kekahi mau wehewehe ana ma ka lewa.

"A laila pepehi ʻo ia iā ia," wahi a Wilson. Ua hāmama koke kona waha.

“Na wai i hana?”

"He ala kaʻu e ʻike ai."

"He ma'i 'oe, e George," wahi a kona hoa. "He pilikia kēia iā ʻoe a ʻaʻole ʻoe i ʻike i kāu mea e ʻōlelo nei. Pono ʻoe e hoʻāʻo e noho mālie a kakahiaka."

"Ua pepehi ʻo ia iā ia."

"He pōʻino ia, e George."

Luliluli ke poo o Wilson. Ua ʻokiʻoki kona mau maka a ua ʻākea iki kona waha me ka ʻuhane o kahi "Hm!"

"Ua maopopo iaʻu," i ʻōlelo maopopo ai ʻo ia. "ʻO wau kekahi o kēia mau hoa hilinaʻi a ʻaʻohe oʻu manaʻo he mea pōʻino i ke kino ʻole , akā ke ʻike wau i kahi mea aʻu i ʻike ai, ʻo ia ke kanaka i loko o kēlā kaʻa, holo ʻo ia e kamaʻilio me ia a ʻaʻole ʻo ia e kū."

Ua ʻike pū ʻo Michaelis i kēia, akā ʻaʻole i manaʻo ʻia he mea koʻikoʻi ko ia mea. Ua manaʻo ʻo ia ua holo ʻo Mrs. Wilson mai kāna kāne aku, ma mua o ka hoʻāʻo ʻana e kāpae i kekahi kaʻa.

"Pehea i hiki ai iā ia ke like me ia?"

"He ʻano hohonu ʻo ia," wahi a Wilson, me he mea lā ua pane ia i ka nīnau. “Ah-hh—”

Hoʻomaka hou ʻo ia e haʻalulu, a kū ʻo Michaelis e wili ana i ke kaula i loko o kona lima.

"Malia paha he hoaaloha kāu e hiki ai iaʻu ke kelepona iā George?"

He manaʻolana pōʻino kēia—ʻaneʻane maopopo ʻo ia ʻaʻohe hoaaloha ʻo Wilson: ʻaʻole lawa iā ia no kāna wahine. Ua hauʻoli ʻo ia ma hope iki i kona ʻike ʻana i kahi hoʻololi i loko o ka lumi, he uliuli e wikiwiki ana ma ka puka makani, a ʻike ʻo ia ʻaʻole i mamao ka wanaʻao. Ma kahi o ka hola ʻelima, ua lawa ka polū ma waho e pio ai ke kukui.

Huli aku ko Wilson mau maka olinolino i nā puʻu lehu, kahi i ʻike ʻia ai nā ʻano ʻōuli hina ʻeleʻele a lele ʻo ia ma ʻō a ma ʻō i ka makani wanaʻao.

"Ua kamaʻilio wau iā ia," i ʻōlelo ai ʻo ia, ma hope o ka hāmau ʻana. "Ua haʻi aku au iā ia e hoʻopunipuni paha ʻo ia iaʻu akā ʻaʻole hiki iā ia ke hoʻopunipuni i ke Akua. Ua lawe au iā ia i ka pukaaniani" - me ka hoʻoikaika ʻana ala aʻe ʻo ia a hele i ka pukaaniani hope a hilinaʻi me kona alo e kaomi ana ma luna - "a ʻōlelo wau 'Ua ʻike ke Akua i kāu hana, nā mea a pau āu e hana nei. Hiki iā ʻoe ke hoʻopunipuni iaʻu, akā ʻaʻole hiki iā ʻoe ke hoʻopunipuni i ke Akua!' ”

Ma hope ona, ʻike akula ʻo Michaelis me ka haʻalulu e nānā ana ʻo ia i nā maka o Kauka TJ Eckleburg, ka mea i puka mai nei, haʻihaʻi a nui, mai ka pō hoʻoheheʻe.

"ʻIke ke Akua i nā mea āpau," wahi a Wilson.

"He hoʻolaha kēlā," i hōʻoiaʻiʻo ai ʻo Michaelis iā ia. ʻO kekahi mea i hoʻohuli iā ia mai ka puka makani a nānā hou i loko o ka lumi. Ua lōʻihi naʻe ko Wilson kū ʻana ma laila, kokoke kona alo i ka puka aniani, e kunou ana i ka pō.

Ma ka hola ʻeono, ua luhi ʻo Michaelis, a ua mahalo ʻo ia i ke kani ʻana o ke kaʻa e kū ana i waho. ʻO ia kekahi o nā kiaʻi o ka pō ma mua i hoʻohiki e hoʻi mai, no laila ua kuke ʻo ia i ka ʻaina kakahiaka no ʻekolu, a ʻo ia a me kekahi kanaka i ʻai pū. Ua mālie ʻo Wilson i kēia manawa, a ua hoʻi ʻo Michaelis i ka home e hiamoe; i kona ala ʻana i ʻehā hola ma hope a hoʻi wikiwiki i ka hale kaʻa, ua hala ʻo Wilson.

ʻO kāna mau neʻe ʻana—ua hele wāwae ʻo ia i nā manawa a pau-ma hope mai i Port Roosevelt a laila i Gad's Hill, kahi āna i kūʻai ai i kahi sanwiti āna i ʻai ʻole ai, a me kahi kīʻaha kofe. Ua luhi ʻo ia a hele mālie, no ka mea, ʻaʻole ia i hiki i ka puʻu ʻo Gada a hiki i ke awakea. A hiki i kēia manawa, ʻaʻohe pilikia o ka helu ʻana i kona manawa—he mau keiki kāne i ʻike i ke kanaka "hana pupule," a me nā mea hoʻokele kaʻa āna i nānā ʻokoʻa ai ma ka ʻaoʻao o ke alanui. A laila nalo ʻo ia i ʻekolu hola mai ka ʻike ʻana aku. ʻO ka mākaʻi, ma ka ikaika o kāna mea i ʻōlelo ai iā Michaelis, "ua loaʻa iā ia kahi ala e ʻike ai," ua manaʻo ʻo ia ua hoʻolilo ʻo ia i kēlā manawa i ka hele ʻana mai kahi hale kaʻa a i kahi hale kaʻa ma laila, e nīnau ana i kahi kaʻa melemele. Ma ka ʻaoʻao ʻē aʻe, ʻaʻohe kanaka hale kaʻa i ʻike iā ia i hele mai i mua, a malia paha he ala maʻalahi a ʻoi aku ka maʻalahi o ka ʻike ʻana i kāna mea i makemake ai e ʻike. Ma ka hapalua hola ʻelua aia ʻo ia ma West Egg, kahi i nīnau ai i kekahi i ke ala e hiki ai i ka hale o Gatsby. No laila, i kēlā manawa ua ʻike ʻo ia i ka inoa o Gatsby.

I ka hola ʻelua, hoʻokomo ʻo Gatsby i kona lole ʻauʻau a waiho i ka ʻōlelo me ka luna kīʻaha inā e kelepona kekahi i ka huaʻōlelo e lawe ʻia iā ia ma ka wai. Ua kū ʻo ia ma ka hale kaʻa no kahi moena pneumatic i hoʻohauʻoli i kāna mau malihini i ke kauwela, a ua kōkua ke kaʻa iā ia e puhi. A laila, hāʻawi ʻo ia i nā ʻōlelo aʻo ʻaʻole e lawe ʻia ke kaʻa hāmama ma lalo o nā kūlana - a he mea ʻē kēia, no ka mea, pono e hoʻoponopono ʻia ka pale ʻākau mua.

Ua poʻohiwi ʻo Gatsby i ka moena a hoʻomaka i ka loko wai. I ka manawa i ku ai oia a hoololi iki aku, a ninau aku ke kaa iaia ina he kokua ia ia, aka, lulu iho la oia i kona poo, a i kekahi manawa nalo aku la ia iwaena o na kumulaau melemele.

ʻAʻohe leka kelepona i hōʻea mai, akā ua hele ka luna kīʻaha me ka hiamoe ʻole a kali a hiki i ka hola ʻehā—a hiki i ka lōʻihi o ka loaʻa ʻana o ka mea nāna e hāʻawi inā hiki mai. He manaʻo koʻu ʻaʻole i manaʻo ʻo Gatsby ponoʻī e hiki mai ana, a ʻaʻole paha ʻo ia i manaʻo hou. Inā he ʻoiaʻiʻo, ua manaʻo paha ʻo ia ua nalowale ka honua mehana kahiko, ua uku ʻo ia i ke kumukūʻai kiʻekiʻe no ka noho lōʻihi ʻana me ka moeʻuhane hoʻokahi. Ua nānā ʻo ia i luna i kahi lewa ʻike ʻole ma o nā lau weliweli a haʻalulu i kona ʻike ʻana i ke ʻano o ka rose a me ka maka o ka lā ma luna o ka mauʻu i hana ʻole ʻia. He honua hou, ʻaʻohe mea maoli, kahi o nā ʻuhane ʻilihune, hanu ʻana i nā moeʻuhane e like me ka ea, holo pololei a puni ...

ʻO ke kaʻa kaʻa—ʻo ia kekahi o nā mea mālama iā Wolfshiem—ua lohe ʻo ia i nā pana ʻana—ma hope iho hiki iā ia ke ʻōlelo wale aku ʻaʻole ʻo ia i noʻonoʻo nui iā lākou. Ua kaʻa pololei wau mai ke kahua hoʻokele a hiki i ka hale o Gatsby a ʻo koʻu holo wikiwiki ʻana i nā alapiʻi mua ka mea mua i hoʻoweliweli ʻia e kekahi. Akā, ʻike lākou i kēlā manawa, manaʻoʻiʻo wau. Me ka ʻōlelo liʻiliʻi ʻole, ʻehā o mākou, ke kaʻa, ka mea kīʻaha, ka māla, a me aʻu i iho wikiwiki ai i ka wai.

Ua ʻike ʻole ʻia ka neʻe ʻana o ka wai i ka neʻe ʻana o ke kahe hou mai kekahi ʻaoʻao i ke ala i ke kahawai ma kekahi ʻaoʻao. Me nā liʻiliʻi liʻiliʻi ʻaʻole i ka malu o ka nalu, ua neʻe like ʻole ka moena ukana i lalo o ka loko. ʻO kahi makani makani liʻiliʻi i hoʻopili ʻole i ka ʻili o ka ʻili ua lawa ia e hoʻopilikia i kona ala ulia me kāna ukana ulia. ʻO ka hoʻopā ʻana o kahi puʻupuʻu lau i hoʻohuli mālie ʻia, e like me ka wāwae o ka hele ʻana, he pōʻai ʻulaʻula lahilahi i loko o ka wai.

Ma hope iho o ko mākou hoʻomaka ʻana me Gatsby i ka hale i ʻike aku ai ka mea mālama māla i ke kino o Wilson ma kahi mamao loa i ka mauʻu, a ua pau ka puhi kuni.

IX

Ma hope o ʻelua mau makahiki, hoʻomanaʻo wau i ke koena o ia lā, a i kēlā pō a i kekahi lā aʻe, ma ke ʻano he hoʻomaʻamaʻa pau ʻole o nā mākaʻi a me nā mea paʻi kiʻi a me nā kānaka nūpepa i loko a i waho o ka puka mua o Gatsby. ʻO kahi kaula i hohola ʻia ma ka ʻīpuka nui a me kahi mākaʻi ma ia mea i pale aku ai i ka pīhoihoi, akā ua ʻike koke nā keiki liʻiliʻi hiki iā lākou ke komo i loko o koʻu pā hale, a aia mau kekahi o lākou e hui pū ana me ka waha e pili ana i ka loko. Ua hoʻohana kekahi kanaka me ke ʻano maikaʻi, he makaʻu paha, i ka huaʻōlelo “pupule” ʻoiai ʻo ia e kūlou ana ma luna o ke kino o Wilson i kēlā ʻauinalā, a ʻo ka mana hoʻokalakupua o kona leo i hoʻokumu i ke kī no nā hōʻike nūpepa i ke kakahiaka aʻe.

ʻO ka hapa nui o ia mau hōʻike he moeʻuhane ʻino—grotesque, circumstantial, makemake, a me ka ʻoiaʻiʻo. I ka hōʻike ʻana o Michaelis ma ka hoʻokolokolo hoʻokolokolo i hoʻomaopopo ʻia nā kānalua o Wilson no kāna wahine, ua manaʻo wau e hāʻawi koke ʻia ka moʻolelo holoʻokoʻa i ka pasquinade racy - akā ʻo Catherine, ka mea i ʻōlelo i kekahi mea, ʻaʻole ia i ʻōlelo iki. Ua hōʻike pū ʻo ia i kahi ʻano kamahaʻo e pili ana i ia mea - nānā aku i ka coroner me nā maka paʻa ma lalo o kona lae i hoʻoponopono ʻia, a hoʻohiki ʻo ia ʻaʻole i ʻike kona kaikuahine iā Gatsby, ua hauʻoli loa kona kaikuahine me kāna kāne, ʻaʻole i hana ʻino kona kaikuahine. Ua hōʻoiaʻiʻo ʻo ia iā ia iho no ia mea, a uē aʻe i loko o kona hainaka, me he mea lā ua ʻoi aku ka manaʻo ma mua o ka hiki ke hoʻomanawanui. No laila, kuʻi mākou i nā waʻa i ke au, hoʻihoʻi mau ʻia i ka wā i hala.
