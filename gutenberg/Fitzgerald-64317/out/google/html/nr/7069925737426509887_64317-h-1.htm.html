Bekunomvumo ovela endlini kamakhelwana wami ebusuku behlobo. Eengadini zakhe eziluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka amadoda namantombazana beza begodu bahambe njengeenundu hlangana nokuhleba ne-champagne neenkwekwezi. Ngesikhathi samagagasi aphezulu ntambama ngabukela iinvakatjhi zakhe zitjhingela zisuka embhotjhweni wesikepe sakhe, nofana zithathe ilanga esihlabathini esitjhisako sebhishi lakhe ngesikhathi iinkepe zakhe ezimbili zisika amanzi we-Sound, zidweba iinqhema zamanzi phezu kwama-cataracts wegwebu. Ngepelaveke i-Rolls-Royce yakhe yaba yi-omnibus, ithwala amaphathi ukuya nokubuya edorobheni hlangana ne-awa lethoba ekuseni ukuya phakathi kobusuku, ngesikhathi i-station wagon yakhe igijima njengesibungu esiphuzi ukuhlangabezana nazo zoke iintimela. Begodu ngeMsombuluko iinceku ezisibhozo, okufaka hlangana umlimi owengeziweko, bezisebenza budisi ilanga loke ngamamophu namabhrashi wokukhuhla, ngesando namasikere wegadi, zilungisa ukutjhabalala kobusuku bangaphambilini.

Njalo ngoLwesihlanu amakhreyithi amahlanu wama-orentji namalamula afika avela kumthengisi wezithelo e-New York—qobe ngeMsombuluko ama-orentji namalamula afanako atjhiya umnyango wakhe wangemuva abe yipiramidi yama-halfs anganawo ama-pulp. Bekunomtjhini ngekhwitjhini ebewukghona ukukhipha ijuzi yamawolintshi amakhulu amabili ngesiquntu se-awa nangabe ibhathini elincani lingagandelelwa amahlandla amakhulu amabili ngesithupha se-butler.

Okungenani kanye emavikini amabili isiqhema sabaphakeli besifika ngeenyawo ezingamakhulu amanengi zekhanvasi kanye namalangabi anemibalabala eyaneleko ukwenza umuthi kaKresmusi wesivande esikhulu se-Gatsby. Ematafuleni we-buffet, ahlotshiswe ngama-hors-d'oeuvre acwebezelayo, ama-ham abhakiwe anespayisi agcwele amasaladi wemiklamo ye-harlequin kanye nama-pastry pigs nama-turkey athakathiswe abe yigolide elimnyama. Eholweni elikhulu kwabekwa ibhara enesitimela sethusi samambala, begodu ineenthombe ezinama-gin notshwala kanye nama-cordials asegade akhohliwe kangangobana inengi leemvakatjhi zakhe zesifazana bezisemncani khulu ukwazi omunye nomunye.

Nge-awa lesikhombisa i-orchestra sele ifikile, ingasi indaba emincani yamalungu amahlanu, kodwana i-oboe nama-trombone nama-saxophone nama-viol nama-cornet nama-piccolo, kanye namadrum aphasi naphezulu. Ababhukudi bokugcina bafike bevela elwandle nje begodu bagqoka phezulu; Iinkoloyi ezivela e-New York zipakwe ngaphakathi kwendlela, begodu sele amahholo nama-salon nama-veranda akhazimula ngemibala eqakathekileko, neenhluthu zibunjwe ngeendlela ezitjha ezingakajayeleki, namashawuli angaphezu kwamaphupho we-Castile. Ibhara igcwele, begodu imijikelezo yamakhoktheyili igcwele engadini ngaphandle, bekwaba kulapho umoya uphila ngokucoca nokuhleka, nokutjho okungakajayeleki nokwethulwa okukhohliweko khonapho, nemihlangano enomdlandla hlangana nabantu abasikazi ebebangakazazi amabizo womunye nomunye.

Amalambu akhanya khulu njengombana umhlaba utjhinga elangeni, begodu nje i-orchestra idlala umvumo we-cocktail ophuzi, begodu i-opera yamaphimbo iphakamisa ikhiye phezulu. Ukuhleka kuba lula umzuzu nomzuzu, kugcwele ukulahlekelwa, kuvezwe lilizwi elihle. Amaqembu atjhuguluka msinyana, avuvuke ngokufika okutjha, ahlakazeka begodu akha ngokuphefumula okufanako; sele kuneenkhambi, amantombazana azithembako aluka lapha nalaphaya hlangana nalabo abaqinileko nabazinzileko, aba mzuzwana obukhali, othokozisako isikhungo sesiqhema, bese, bathabile ngokunqoba, batjhinga phambili ngokutjhuguluka kwelwandle lobuso namaphimbo nombala ngaphasi kokukhanya okutjhugulukako.

Ngokuphazima kweso omunye walama-gypsies, nge-opal ethuthumelako, uthatha ikhoktheyili emoyeni, uyayilahla phasi ukwenzela bona abe nesibindi begodu, anyakazisa izandla zakhe njengo-Frisco, ugida yedwa epulatifomu yekhanvasi. Ukuthula kwesikhatjhana; umdosiphambili we-orchestra utjhugulula isigqi sakhe ngokuzithoba, begodu kuba nokukhulumisana njengombana iindaba ezingakalungi zihamba ngokuthi ungumfundi ka-Gilda Gray we-Follies. Iphathi sele ithomile.

Ngikholelwa bona ngobusuku bokuthoma engaya ngabo emzini kaGatsby bengingomunye weemvakatjhi ezimbalwa ebezimenywe kwamambala. Abantu bebangakamenywa—bebaya lapho. Bangena eenkoloyini ezabathatha baya e-Long Island, begodu ngandlela thize bagcina bafika emnyango we-Gatsby. Nasele bafike lapho baziswa mumuntu omaziko u-Gatsby, begodu ngemva kwalokho baziphatha ngokuya kwemithetho yokuziphatha ehlotjaniswa nepaki yokuzithabisa. Kwesinye isikhathi bebafika begodu bahambe ngaphandle kokuthi ahlangane no-Gatsby, beza emnyanyeni ngehliziyo elula ebegade ithikithi layo lokungena.

Bengimenyiwe kwamambala. Umtjhayeli ogqoke iyunifomu eluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka weqanda le-robin wawela utjani bami ekuseni ngoMgqibelo ngenothi elihlelekileko elivela kumqatjhi wakhe: isithunzi sizokuba ngeka-Gatsby, kwathiwa, nangabe ngizokuya “ephathini yakhe encani” ngalobo busuku. Bekangibone kanengi, begodu bekahlose ukungibiza kudala ngaphambilini, kodwana ukuhlanganiswa okungakajayeleki kweemeko bekuvimbele—uJay Gatsby otlikitliweko, ngesandla esihle khulu.

Ngigqoke ama-flannel amhlophe ngaya e-lawn yakhe ngemva kwe-awa lesikhombisa, begodu ngazulazula ngingakakhululeki hlangana namavunguvungu wabantu engingabaziko—nanyana lapha nalapha bekunobuso ebengibubone esitimeleni sokuya emsebenzini. Ngamangala msinyana ngenani lamaNgisi amatjha ebegade agcwele; boke bagqoke kuhle, boke babonakala balambile kancani, begodu boke bakhuluma ngamaphimbo aphasi, aqotho ebantwini be-America abaqinileko nabaphumelelako. Bengiqiniseka bona bebathengisa okuthileko: amabhondi nofana umtjhorensi nofana iinkoloyi. Bebazi kuhle khulu ngemali elula eduze begodu bebakholwa bona ngeyabo ngamagama ambalwa ngekhiye elifaneleko.

Ngathi ngifika ngalinga ukuthola umsingathi wami, kodwana abantu ababili nofana abathathu ebengibabuza bona ukuphi bangiqalile ngendlela emangalisako, begodu baphika ngokuqinileko nanyana ngiyiphi ilwazi lokunyakaza kwakhe, kangangobana ngatjhinga ngaya etafuleni lekhoktheyili—indawo eyodwa engadini lapho indoda eyodwa ingahlala khona ngaphandle kokuqalela yodwa.

Bengisendleleni yokudakwa khulu ngebanga lokuhlazeka lokha u-Jordan Baker nakaphuma ngendlini wema phezulu kwezitebhisi zemabula, atjhingela emuva kancani begodu aqale phasi engadini ngokudelela.

Ngiyakwamukela namkha awa, ngithole kutlhogeka bona ngizihlanganise nomunye umuntu ngaphambi kobana ngithome ukukhuluma kuhle ebantwini abadlulako.

"Lotjha!" Ngarhuwelela, ngisondela kuye. Ilizwi lami belizwakala lizwakala khulu ngaphetjheya kwengadi.

"Bengicabanga bona ungaba lapha," waphendula ngokunganaki njengombana ngifika. "Ngikhumbula bona bewuhlala eduze ne-—"

Wabamba isandla sami ngokungakafaneli, njengesithembiso sokuthi uzongitlhogomela ngomzuzu, begodu walalela amantombazana amabili agqoke amarogo amabili aphuzi, ajama ngaphasi kwezitebhisi.

"Lotjha!" bakhala ndawonye. “Ngiyancancabeza bona akhenge uphumelele.”

Lokho bekungomqhudelwano wegalofu. Bekahlulwe emidlalweni yokugcina ngeveke eyadlulako.

“Awazi bona sibobani,” kwatjho omunye wamantombazana agqoke okuphuzi, “kodwana sihlangane nawe lapha pheze ngenyanga eyadlulako.”

"Udaye iinhluthu zakho solo kwangaleso sikhathi," kwatjho u-Jordan, begodu ngathoma, kodwana amantombazana bekaragela phambili ngokuzithandela begodu ukuphawula kwakhe bekuqondiswe enyangeni engakafiki isikhathi, eyenziwe njengesidlo sakusihlwa, ngokungangabazeki, ngaphandle kwebhasikidi lompheki. Ngengalo ka-Jordan encani yegolide iphumule kweyami, sehla ngezitebhisi begodu sazulazula engadini. Ithreyi yamakhoktheyili yaphapha kithi ebusuku, begodu sahlala phasi etafuleni namantombazana amabili agqoke okuphuzi namadoda amathathu, omunye nomunye wethulwa kithi njengoMnumzana Mumble.

"Ngizwe lokho endodeni eyazi koke ngaye, ekhulele nayo e-Germany," wasiqinisekisa kuhle.

Isidlo sakusihlwa sokuthoma—bekuzokuba nesinye ngemva kwaphakathi kobusuku—gade siphakelwa, begodu u-Jordan wangimema bona ngijoyine isiqhema sakhe, ebegade basabalale etafuleni ngakelinye ihlangothi lesivande. Bekuneenthandani ezintathu ezitjhadileko kanye nomphelekezeli ka-Jordan, umfundi ophikelelako onikelwe ukuhlambalaza okunobudlova, begodu ngokusobala ngaphasi kombono wokobana msinyana nofana muva u-Jordan uzokunikela ngomuntu wakhe ngezinga elikhulu nofana elincani. Kunokuthi iphaphatheke, isiqhema lesi besilondoloze ukufana okunesithunzi, begodu sazithathela umsebenzi wokujamela ubukhulu obuqinileko bemaphandleni—i-East Egg ithobela i-West Egg begodu iqaphele ngokutjheja ubujamo bayo obubonakalako.

"Asiphume," kwatjho u-Jordan, ngemva kwesigamu se-awa esingakafaneli; "Lokhu kuhlonipha khulu kimi."

Savuka, begodu wahlathulula bona sizokufumana umsingathi: Bengingakaze ngihlangane naye, watjho, begodu bekungenza ngingakhululeki. Umfundi wavuma ngehloko ngendlela yokuhlambalaza, yokudana.

Ngethuba elithileko salinga umnyango obukeka uqakathekile, begodu sangena emtapweni wezincwadi ophakemeko we-Gothic, ohlanganiswe nge-oak yesiNgisi ebaziweko, begodu mhlamunye uthunyelwe uphelele ukusuka kwenye incithakalo phetjheya.

Indoda eqinileko, eneminyaka ephakathi nendawo, enezibuko ezinkulu ezifana nesikhova, beyihlezi edakiwe emphethweni wetafula elikhulu, igqolozele amashelufu wamabhuku. Nasingena wajikeleza ngethabo begodu wahlola u-Jordan ukusuka ekhanda ukuya enyaweni.

“Mayelana nani?”

Wavuma ngehloko.

“Ngubani okulethe?” wafuna. "Kumbe ufike nje? Ngalethwa. Inengi labantu lalethwa."

“Ingabe?”

I-tenor edumileko beyihlabelele ngesi-Italian, begodu i-contralto edumileko beyihlabelele nge-jazz, begodu hlangana neenomboro abantu benza “ama-stunts” kiyo yoke ingadi, ngesikhathi ukuhleka okuthabisako, okungakaqakatheki kuphakama phezulu esibhakabhakeni sehlobo.
