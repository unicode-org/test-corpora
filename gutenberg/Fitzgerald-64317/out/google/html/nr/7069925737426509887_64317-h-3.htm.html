Kwaba mhla ukufuna ukwazi ngo-Gatsby bekuphezulu khulu lapho amalambu endlini yakhe ahluleka ukukhanya ngelinye ilanga ngoMgqibelo ebusuku—begodu, njengombana bekuthomile, umsebenzi wakhe njengo-Trimalchio wawuphelile. Kancanikancani ngathoma ukutjheja bona iinkoloyi ebegade ziphenduke zibe mtjhayeli wakhe zahlala umzuzu nje bese zatjhayela ngokuthukuthela. Ngizibuza bona uyagula na ngaya ngapha ngiyokufumana—umphathi engingamaziko onobuso obukhohlakeleko wangiqalisisa ngokusolisa emnyango.

"Ingabe uNom. Gatsby uyagula?"

"Awa." Ngemva kokuthula kancani wangezelela "mnumzana" ngendlela ehlehlisako, yokuzisola.

"Bengingakaze ngimbone, begodu bengikhathazekile. Mtjele bona uNom. Carraway ufikile."

"Ngubani?" wafuna ngokungakafaneli.

"I-Carraway."

"Carraway. Kulungile, ngizomtjela."

Msinyana wavala umnyango.

U-Finn wami wangitjela bona u-Gatsby ukhiphe yoke inceku endlini yakhe ngeveke eyadlulako begodu wafaka ezinye eziyingcenye yetjhumi nambili, ebezingakaze ziye emzaneni we-West Egg ukuyokufumbathiswa bathengisi, kodwana za-oda izinto ezilinganiselweko ngefoni. Umsana othengisa ukudla wabika bona ikhitjhini beyifana nesibaya sefarigi, begodu umbono jikelele wedorobho bewuthi abantu abatjha bebangasibo iinceku.

Ngelanga elilandelako u-Gatsby wangifonela.

“Uyahamba?” Ngabuza.

“Awa, umdlalo omdala.”

“Ngiyezwa bona uxoshe zoke iinceku zakho.”

"Bengifuna umuntu ongahlebi. U-Daisy ufika kanengi—ntambama."

"Ngabanye abantu u-Wolfshiem ebegade afuna ukubenzela okuthileko. Boke babafowabo nabodadwabo. Bebaphethe ihotela elincani."

"Ngiyabona."

Bekafowuna ngesibawo saka-Daisy—ngizokuza ukudla kwasemini emzini wakhe kusasa? U-Miss Baker bekazokuba lapho. Ngemva kwesigamu se-awa u-Daisy ngokwakhe wafona begodu wabonakala akhululekile ukuthola bona ngiyeza. Bekukhona into ebegade yenzakala. Nanyana kunjalo bengingakholwa bona bangakhetha umnyanya lo bona benze isigcawu—khulukhulu isigcawu esibuhlungu uGatsby egade asiveze engadini.

Ilanga elilandelako belitjhisa, pheze lilanga lokugcina, ngokuqinisekileko elifuthumeleko, lehlobo. Njengoba isitimela sami siphuma emhubheni sangena elangeni, kwaba ziimfesi ezitjhisako ze-National Biscuit Company ezaphula ukuthula okuvuthako emini. Izihlalo zotshani zekoloyi beziphaphazela emphethweni wokuvutha; umfazi oseduze kwami wajuluka isikhatjhana ngaphakathi kwehembe lakhe elimhlophe, bese, njengombana iphephandaba lakhe lamanzi ngaphasi kweminwe yakhe, wawela ngokuphelelwa lithemba emtjhisweni omkhulu ngokulila okumbi. Ibhuku lakhe lephakethe lashaya phasi.

“Hawu, wami!” waphefumulela phezulu.

Ngayithatha ngokugoba okudinileko ngayibuyisela kuye, ngiyibambe ngezandla begodu ngehloko yamakhona ukutjengisa bona anginawo amadizayini kiyo—kodwana woke umuntu oseduze, kufaka hlangana umfazi, wangisola ngokufanako.

"Tjhisa!" Kutjho umqhubi ebusweni obujayelekileko. "Ubujamo bezulu obuthileko!... Kutjhisa!... Kutjhisa!... Kutjhisa!... Ingabe kuyatjhisa ngokwaneleko kuwe? Ingabe kuyatjhisa? Ingabe... ?"

Ithikithi lami lokuya emsebenzini labuya kimi linebala elimnyama esandleni sakhe. Ukobana nanyana ngubani kufuze abe nendaba emtjhisweni lo ukuthi ngubani umlomo wakhe ovuvukileko amqabulako, owayenza ikhanda lakhe libe manzi isikhwama se-pyjama phezu kwehliziywakhe!

… Ngaphakathi kwehholo yendlu yakwa-Buchanan kwavunguza umoya omncani, othwala umdumo wensimbi yefoni kimi no-Gatsby njengombana besilinde emnyango.

“Umzimba wenkosi?” Kurhuwelela umphathi emlonyeni. “Ngiyancancabeza, nkosikazi, kodwana angeze sakghona ukuyifaka ifenitjhara—kutjhisa khulu ukuyithinta namhlanjesi emini!”

Into ayitjho kwamambala bekukuthi: “Iye... Iye... Ngizobona.”

Wabeka phasi i-receiver weza kithi, akhazimula kancani, ukuthatha iinqwazi zethu eziqinileko.

"U-Madame ulindele wena esalonini!" Wamemeza, atjengisa indlela engasitlhogeki. Kulokhu kutjhisa koke ukuziphatha okungezelelweko bekukuhlambalaza isitolo esijayelekileko sepilo.

Ikamelo, ebegade ithunziwe kuhle ngama-awning, belimnyama begodu lipholile. U-Daisy no-Jordan balale esofeni elikhulu, njengeenthombe zesiliva ezigandelela iingubo zazo ezimhlophe ngokujamelana nomoya ovumako wabalandeli.

“Ngeke sikwazi ukunyakaza,” batjho bobabili.

Iminwe ka-Jordan, emhlophe phezu kwe-tan yayo, yaphumula isikhatjhana kimi.

"Begodu uNom. Thomas Buchanan, umdlali?" Ngabuza.

Ngesikhathi esifanako ngezwa ilizwi lakhe, elihlasimulisa umzimba, elithulileko, elizwakalako, efonini yehholo.

U-Gatsby wema phakathi kwekhaphethe elibomvu begodu waqala ngamehlo athakazelisako. U-Daisy wambuka begodu wahleka, ukuhleka kwakhe okumnandi, okuthabisako; umoya omncani wepuyere waphakama esifubeni sakhe waya emoyeni.

"Ihemuhemu lithi," kwatjho u-Jordan, "ukuthi lokho mntazana ka-Tom efonini."

Besithule. Ilizwi ehholo laphakama phezulu ngokucasuka: “Kulungile, njeke, angeze ngakuthengisela ikoloyi kwaphela... Anginasibopho kuwe... begodu malungana nokungikhathaza kwakho ngesikhathi sesidlo semini, angeze ngakubekezelela lokho!”

"Ngibambe isitholi," kwatjho u-Daisy ngokudelela.

“Awa, akasiye,” ngamqinisekisa. "Sivumelwano esiqinisekileko. Ngiyazi ngaso."

U-Tom wavula umnyango, wavala isikhala sawo isikhatjhana ngomzimba wakhe omkhulu, begodu waphuthuma wangena ngekamelweni.

“Mnumzana Gatsby!” Waveza isandla sakhe esibanzi, esicaba ngokungathandi okufihlekileko. “Ngithabile ukukubona, mnumzana… Nick…”

"Senzele isiselo esibandako," kwamemeza u-Daisy.

Nakatjhiya ikamelo godu wasukuma waya ku-Gatsby wadosa ubuso bakhe phasi, wamqabula emlonyeni.

"Uyazi bona ngiyakuthanda," wabubuza.

“Uyakhohlwa bona kunowesifazana okhona,” kwatjho u-Jordan.

U-Daisy waqalaza ngokuzaza.

“Nawe uyamanga u-Nick.”

“Yintombazana ephasi, ehlambalazako!”

“Anginandaba!” kwamemeza u-Daisy, begodu wathoma ukuvala indawo yomlilo yezitina. Bese wakhumbula ukutjhisa begodu wahlala phasi anecala esofeni njengombana nje umhlengikazi osanda kuhlanzwa odosa intombazana encani angena ngekumbeni.

"Ubusisiwe," warhuwelela, welula iingalo zakhe. “Woza kumma wakho okuthandako.”

Umntwana, owatjhiywa mhlengikazi, waphuthuma ngaphetjheya kwegumbi begodu wangena ngeenhloni ngengubo kamama wakhe.

"I-precious ebusisiweko! Ingabe umma ufumene ipuyere eenhluthwini zakho ezindala ezitjheli? Sukuma nje, uthi--Wenza njani."

Mina no-Gatsby satjhinga phasi sathatha isandla esincani ebesingafuni. Ngemva kwalokho waragela phambili nokuqala umntwana ngokumangala. Angicabangi bona gade akholelwa ekutheni ikhona ngaphambilini.

"Ngagqoka ngaphambi kwesidlo sasemini," kwatjho umntwana, aphendukela ku-Daisy.

“Lokho kungenxa yokuthi unyoko bekafuna ukukukhombisa.” Ubuso bakhe bagoba ebusweni obubodwa bentanyeni encani emhlophe. "Uyaphupha, wena. Uphupho elincani khulu."

"Iye," kwavuma umntwana ngokuthula. “U-anti u-Jordan naye ugqoke irogo elimhlophe.”

“Ubathanda njani abangani bakamama?” U-Daisy wamjika ukwenzela bona aqale u-Gatsby. “Ucabanga bona zihle?”

“Uphi ubaba?”

“Akafani noyise,” kwahlathulula u-Daisy. “Ufana nami. Uneenhluthu zami nejamo lobuso.”

U-Daisy wahlala emuva esofeni. Umhlengikazi wathatha igadango lokuya phambili begodu welula isandla sakhe.

“Woza, Pammy.”

“Sala kuhle, sthandwa sami!”

Ngokuqalela emuva ngokungafuni umntwana ofundiswe kuhle wabamba isandla somhlengikazi wakhe begodu wakhutjhwa ngaphandle komnyango, njengombana u-Tom abuya, ngaphambi kwama-gin rickeys amane ebegade agcwele iqhwa.

U-Gatsby wathatha isiselo sakhe.

"Ngempela zibukeka zipholile," watjho, ngokugandeleleka okubonakalako.

Siphuze isikhathi eside, esinobugovu.

"Ngifunde kwenye indawo ukuthi ilanga liyatjhisa qobe mnyaka," kwatjho u-Tom ngomusa. “Kubonakala ngasuthi msinyana umhlaba uzokuwela elangeni—nofana ulinde umzuzu—kuphambene—ilanga liyabanda qobe mnyaka.

"Phuma ngaphandle," wasikisela u-Gatsby, "Ngingathanda bona uqale indawo."

Ngaphuma nabo ngaya everanda. E-Sound eluhlaza, emile emtjhisweni, iseyila elilodwa elincani lakhasa kancanikancani liya elwandle elifretjhi. Amehlo ka-Gatsby alandela isikhatjhana; waphakamisa isandla sakhe wakhomba ngaphetjheya kwe-bay.

“Ngingaphetjheya kwakho.”

“Ngalokho.”

Amehlo wethu aphakamisela phezu kwemibhede yama-rose ne-lawn etjhisako kanye nemfucuza ekhula yamalanga wezinja ngasegwini. Kancanikancani amaphiko amhlophe wesikepe akhamba ngokujamelana nomkhawulo opholileko wesibhakabhaka. Phambili bekunelwandle elinamandla kanye neenqhingi ezinengi ezibusisekileko.

“Kunomdlalo ongafanele wena,” kwatjho u-Tom, avuma ngehloko. “Ngingathanda ukuba ngaphandle lapho naye isikhathi esingange-awa.”

Sadla isidlo sasemini ngekamelweni lokudlela, saba mnyama ngokujamelana nomtjhiso, begodu sasela i-ale ebandako.

“Sizokwenzani ngathi namhlanjesi ntambama?” kwamemeza u-Daisy, "nelanga elilandelako, neminyaka ematjhumi amathathu elandelako?"

"Ungabi mraro," kwatjho u-Jordan. "Ipilo ithoma godu lokha nayithoma ukutjhinga ekwindla."

“Kodwana kuyatjhisa khulu,” kwagandelela u-Daisy, aphose akhale iinyembezi, “begodu yoke into iphithene khulu. Asiye edorobheni soke!”

Ilizwi lakhe lalwa ngaphakathi komtjhiso, libetha ngokujamelene nalo, libumba ukungabi nomqondo kwalo kube ziinhlobo.

"Ngike ngezwa ngokwenza igaraji ngegaraji," u-Tom bekatjho ku-Gatsby, "kodwana ngiyindoda yokuthoma eyakhe yenza isitebele ngegaraji."

“Ngubani ofuna ukuya edorobheni?” Kubuza u-Daisy ngokuphikelela. Amehlo ka-Gatsby aqala kuye. "Ah," warhuwelela, "ubukeka upholile."

Amehlo wabo ahlangana, begodu baqalana bobabili, bodwa emkhathini. Ngomzamo waqalaza phasi etafuleni.

"Uhlala ubukeka upholile," waphinda.

Bekamtjelile bona uyamthanda, begodu u-Tom Buchanan wabona. Wamangala. Umlomo wakhe wavuleka kancani, begodu waqala u-Gatsby, wabuyela ku-Daisy kwangathi usanda ukumbona njengomuntu amazi kudala.

"Ufana nesikhangiso sendoda," waraga ngokunganacala. "Uyazi isikhangiso sendoda—"

"Kulungile," kwatjho u-Tom msinyana, "Ngizimisele ukuya edorobheni. Woza—soke siya edorobheni."

Wasukuma, amehlo wakhe asaphazima hlangana no-Gatsby nomkakhe. Akekho owanyakazako.

"Iza!" Ukuthukuthela kwakhe kwathuthumela kancani. “Yini inkinga, nanyana kunjalo, nange siya edorobheni, asithome.”

Isandla sakhe, esithuthumelako ngomzamo wakhe wokuzithiba, sathwala iingilazi zakhe zokugcina ze-ale. Iphimbo laka-Daisy lasenza sasukuma begodu saya emgwaqweni ovuthako.

"Sizokukhamba nje?" waphikisa. “Njengalokhu? Ngeze sivumele omunye umuntu bona abheme ugwayi qangi?”

"Wonke umuntu bekabhema isikhathi soke sesidlo semini."

“Hawu, asizithokoziseni,” wamncenga. “Kuyatjhisa khulu bona ungaphithizela.”

Akazange aphendule.

"Yenza ngendlela yakho," watjho. “Woza, Jordan.”

Baya phezulu ukuyokulungela ngesikhathi thina amadoda amathathu sijame lapho sitjhugulula amatje atjhisako ngeenyawo zethu. Ijika lesiliva lenyanga besele liphaphazela esibhakabhakeni esisentshonalanga. U-Gatsby wathoma ukukhuluma, watjhugulula umkhumbulo, kodwana ingasi ngaphambi kobana u-Tom atjhinge begodu aqalane naye ngokulindela.

“Unayo iinkumba zakho lapha?” kubuza uGatsby ngomzamo.

“Phantse ikota yemayela phasi emgwaqweni.”

"Bababe."

Ukuma kancani.

"Angiboni umqondo wokuya edorobheni," kwatjho u-Tom ngolaka. "Abantu abasikazi bathola imibono le emakhanda wabo—"

“Singathatha okuthileko esizokunatha?” kwabiza u-Daisy efasiteleni eliphezulu.

“Ngizokuthatha i-whisky,” kuphendula u-Tom. Wangena ngaphakathi.

U-Gatsby waphendukela kimi ngokuqinileko:

“Angeze ngatjho litho ngendlini yakhe, umdlalo omdala.”

“Unelizwi elingakaqakatheki,” Ngaphawula. “Izele—” Ngazaza.

"Ilizwi lakhe lizele imali," watjho kungakalindelwa.

Kwaba ngilokho. Bengingakaze ngizwisise ngaphambilini. Bekuzele imali—lokho bekulithando elingapheliko ebelivuka begodu liwele kilo, ukukhala kwalo, ingoma yamasimbali walo... Phezulu esigodlweni esimhlophe indodakazi yenkosi, intombazana yegolide...

U-Tom waphuma ngendlini agoqa ibhodlela le-quart ngethawula, kwalandela u-Daisy no-Jordan bembethe iinqwazi ezincani eziqinileko zendwangu yensimbi begodu baphethe amakhephu alula phezu kweengalo zabo.

“Singahamba soke ngekoloyi yami?” kwaphakamisa u-Gatsby. Wezwa isikhumba esitjhisako, esihlaza satjani sesihlalo. "Bekufanele ngiyitjhiye emthunzini."

“Ingabe litjhifthi ejwayelekileko?” Kubuza u-Tom.

"Iye."

"Kulungile, thatha i-coupé yami ngivumele ngitjhayele ikoloyi yakho ngiye edorobheni."

Isiphakamiso besingathandeki ku-Gatsby.

“Angicabangi bona kunegezi enengi,” waphikisa.

"Igezi enengi," kwatjho u-Tom ngokurhuwelela. Waqala isilinganisi. "Nangabe iphela ngingajama esitolo semithi. Ungathenga nanyana yini esitolo semithi namhlanjesi."

Kwalandela ukuma kancani lokhu okubonakala kungasizi ngalitho. U-Daisy waqala u-Tom ahlikihla, begodu ubuso obungachazekiko, obungakajayeleki begodu obungabonakaliko, kwanga ngikuzwe kuchazwa ngamagama, kwadlula ebusweni baka-Gatsby.

"Woza, Daisy," kwatjho u-Tom, amgandelela ngesandla sakhe emotweni ka-Gatsby. “Ngizokuthatha ngekoloyi yesekisi.”

Wavula umnyango, kodwana waphuma embuthanweni wengalo yakhe.

"Thatha u-Nick no-Jordan. Sizokulandela nge-coupe."

Watjhidela eduze no-Gatsby, athinta ijazi lakhe ngesandla sakhe. U-Jordan no-Tom nami sangena esihlalweni sangaphambili sekoloyi ka-Gatsby, u-Tom wagandelela amagiya angasiwo angakajayeleki, begodu satjhinga emtjhisweni omkhulu, sawatjhiya angabonakali.

“Ukubonile lokho?” Kubuza u-Tom.

“Ubona ini?”

Wangiqalisisa, wabona bona mina no-Jordan kufuze bona besazi isikhathi eside.

“Ucabanga bona ngiyisithutha, angitjho?” waphakamisa. "Mhlamunye nginguye, kodwana ngine--pheze ukubona okwesibili, kesinye isikhathi, okungitjela bona ngenzeni. Mhlamunye awukukholwa lokho, kodwana isayensi-"

Wathula kancani. Isimo esingakalindeleki samthatha, sambuyisela emuva emphethweni we-abyss yethiyori.

“Ngiphenye kancani ngalomuntu,” waraga. "Bengingangena ngokujulileko ngathana bengazi—"

“Utjho bona ukhe waya emdlalweni?” kubuza u-Jordan ngokuhlekisa.

"Ini?" Adidekile, wasigqolozela njengombana besihleka. “Umdamu?”

"Nge-Gatsby."

"Malungana no-Gatsby! Awa, angikaze. Ngithe bengisolo ngenza irhubhululo elincani lesikhathi sakhe esidlulileko."

"Begodu uthole bona bekayindoda ye-Oxford," kwatjho u-Jordan ngokusiza.

“Indoda ye-Oxford!” Bekangakholwa. "Ufana nesihogo! Ugqoke isudu epinki."

"Nanyana kunjalo uyindoda ye-Oxford."

"Oxford, e-New Mexico," kwatjho u-Tom ngokudelela, "nofana into efana naleyo."

“Lalela, Tom. Nayikuthi uyi-snob kangaka, kubayini ummemele esidlweni semini?” Kubuza u-Jordan ngokugandeleleka.

"U-Daisy wammema; bekamazi ngaphambi kobana sitjhade—uZimu uyazi bona kuphi!"

Soke besele sithukuthele nge-ale ebeyiphela, begodu sikwazi lokho satjhayela isikhatjhana sithule. Bese kuthi njengombana amehlo kaDokotela u-TJ Eckleburg abonakala endleleni, ngakhumbula isiyeleliso saka-Gatsby ngophethiloli.

"Sinakho okwaneleko ukusiyisa edorobheni," kwatjho u-Tom.

"Kodwana kunegaraji lapha," kwaphikisa u-Jordan. “Angifuni ukujamelana nalokhu okutjhisako.”

U-Tom waphosa amabhuleki womabili ngokuphelelwa sineke, begodu satjhelela saya endaweni ethulileko ngaphasi kwesibonakaliso saka-Wilson. Ngemva komzuzwana umnikazi waphuma ngaphakathi kwesakhiwo sakhe begodu waqala ikoloyi ngamehlo avulekileko.

“Asithathe igesi!” kwamemeza u-Tom ngamandla. "Ucabanga bona sijame ngani—ukubuka umbono?"

“Ngiyagula,” kwatjho u-Wilson ngaphandle kokunyakazisa. "Bengigula ilanga loke."

“Yini inkinga?”

“Ngiphelelwe mamandla.”

“Kulungile, ngingazisiza na?” Kutjho u-Tom. "Uzwakala kuhle ngokwaneleko efonini."

Ngomzamo u-Wilson watjhiya umthunzi nokusekelwa komnyango, begodu, aphefumula budisi, wavula isivalo sethangi. Ngokukhanya kwelanga ubuso bakhe bebuluhlaza.

“Bengingafuni ukuphazamisa isidlo sakho sasemini,” watjho. "Kodwana ngidinga imali khulu, begodu bengizibuza bona uzokwenzani ngekoloyi yakho endala."

“Uyithanda njani le?” kubuza uTom. “Ngiyithengile ngeveke ephelileko.”

"Yiphuzi elihle," kwatjho u-Wilson, njengombana aqinisa isibambo.

“Uthanda ukuyithenga?”

"Ithuba elikhulu," u-Wilson wamomotheka kancani. "Awa, kodwana ngingenza imali ngakelinye ihlangothi."

“Ufunani imali, msinyana?”

"Ngilapha isikhathi eside khulu. Ngifuna ukusuka. Mina nomkami sifuna ukuya eTjingalanga."

"Umkakho uyazi," kwatjho u-Tom, athukiwe.

“Sekuyiminyaka elitjhumi akhuluma ngakho.” Waphumula isikhatjhana ngokujamelana nepompo, wavala amehlo wakhe. “Njalo khathesi uyahamba loba efuna kumbe engafuni.Ngizamsusa.”

I-coupé yaphazima phambi kwethu ngothuli olunengi nokukhanya kwesandla esiphakamisela phezulu.

“Ngikukweleda ini?” Kubuza u-Tom ngokurhabako.

"Ngivele ngihlakaniphe ngento ehlekisako emalangeni amabili adlulileko," kwatjho u-Wilson. “Yikho ngifuna ukubaleka.Yikho bengikuhlupha ngemota.”

“Ngikukweleda ini?”

“Idola lamatjhumi amabili.”

Umtjhiso ongapheliko bewuthoma ukungidida begodu ngaba nomzuzu omumbi lapho ngaphambi kobana ngibone bona bekube nje ukusola kwakhe bekungakafiki ku-Tom. Wathola bona u-Myrtle unepilo ethileko ngaphandle kwakhe kwelinye iphasi, begodu ukuthuka kwamenza wagula emzimbeni. Ngamgqolozela bese ngaqala u-Tom, obekathole into efanako kungakapheli ne-awa—begodu kwafika engqondweni yami bona bekunganamehluko hlangana nabantu, ngobuhlakani nofana ngokobuhlanga, njengomehluko phakathi kwabagulako nabagulako. Indawo leyo beyihlala ikhathaza, ngitjho nasemini ekhanya khulu, begodu nje ngajika ikhanda lami ngasuthi ngiyelelisiwe ngokuthileko ngemva. Phezu kweenqwaba zomlotha amehlo amakhulu kaDokotela u-TJ Eckleburg bekasolo aqaphele, kodwana ngabona, ngemva komzuzwana, bona amanye amehlo bekasiqala ngamandla angakajayeleki avela ngaphasi kwamamitha amatjhumi amabili.
