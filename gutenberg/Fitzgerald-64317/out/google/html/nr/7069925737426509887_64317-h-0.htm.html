Mina

Eminyakeni yami esesemutjha begodu ngisengozini ubaba wangipha isiyeleliso ebengihlala ngisicabanga engqondweni yami solo kwamhlokho.

"Nanyana kunini nawuzizwa ufuna ukugxeka omunye umuntu," wangitjela, "khumbula bona boke abantu ephasini akhenge babe neenzuzo njengawe."

Akazange atjho okunye, kodwana besisolo sikhulumisana ngendlela engakajayeleki, begodu ngazwisisa bona bekatjho okunengi ukudlula lokho. Njengomphumela, ngithambekele ekugodleni zoke izahlulelo, umkhuba ongivulele ubujamo obunengi bokufuna ukwazi begodu wangenza ngaba sisulu seenkhathi ezinengi. Ingqondo engakajayeleki ikhawuleza ukuyibona begodu inamathele kilekhwalithi lokha nayivela emuntwini ovamileko, begodu kwenzeka bona ekolitjhini ngasolwa ngokungakafaneli ngokuthi ngingusopolotiki, ngombana bengikwazi ubuhlungu obufihlakeleko babantu abangaziwako. Inengi leenthembiso bezingafunwa—kanengi bengizenzisa ukulala, ukutshwenyeka, nofana ukuzithoba okunonya lokha nangibona ngesibonakaliso esingaphambukiko bona isambulo esiseduze besithuthumela emkhathizweni; ngombana izambulo ezitjhideleneko zamajaha, nofana okungenani amagama abawaveza ngawo, avamise ukukopela begodu ziphazanyiswa kugandeleleka okusobala. Ukugodla izahlulelo kuyindaba yethemba elingapheliko. Ngisasaba ukulahlekelwa yinto ethile nangabe ngikhohlwa bona, njengombana ubaba wami aphakamisa ngokuzikhakhazisa, begodu ngiyaphinda ngokuzikhakhazisa, umqondo wokuziphatha okuqakathekileko uhlukaniswa ngokungalingani ekubelethweni.

Begodu, ngemva kokuzikhakhazisa ngalendlela yokubekezelela kwami, ngifika ekuvumeni bona kunomkhawulo. Ukuziphatha kungasekelwa edwaleni eliqinileko nofana emaxhaphozini amanzi, kodwana ngemva kwephuzu elithileko anginandaba nokuthi kusekelwe kuphi. Ngu-Gatsby kwaphela, indoda enikela ngebizo layo encwadini le, ebegade akhululekile ekusabeleni kwami—u-Gatsby, obekajamele yoke into engiyidelelako. Nangabe ubuntu buyinto engapheliko yezenzo eziphumeleleko, khona-ke bekunokuthileko okuhle ngaye, ukuzwela okuphezulu eenthembisweni zokuphila, kwangathi uhlobana nomunye wemitjhini eyinkimbinkimbi ebhalisa ukusikinyeka kwephasi okude ngamamayela ayizinkulungwane ezilitjhumi. Ukusabela lokhu bekungahlangene nokuthintana okumbi okuhlonitjhwa ngaphasi kwebizo elithi "ubujamo bokusungula"—bekusipho esingakajayeleki sethemba, ukulungela kwethando engingakaze ngikuthole komunye umuntu begodu okungenzeka bona ngizokuphinde ngikuthole godu. Awa—u-Gatsby waphuma kuhle ekugcineni; ngilokho okwahlasela u-Gatsby, ngiliphi ithuli elingcolileko ebeliphaphazela ngemva kwamabhudango wakhe elavala isithakazelo sami ngeenhlupheko ezingasizi ngalitho kanye nokuthaba okufitjhani kwabantu.

Umndenami bewusolo ungabantu abadumileko, abaphila kuhle edorobheni le-Middle Western iinzukulwana ezintathu. Abakwa-Carraway bayinto efana nesizwe, begodu sinesiko lokuthi sivela kuma-Duke we-Buccleuch, kodwana umsunguli womhlobo wami bekungumfowethu kamkhulu wami, owafika lapha ngeminyaka ematjhumi amahlanu nanye, wathumela isikhundla sakhe ePini yomKhaya, begodu wathoma ibhizinisi lokuthengisa iinsetjenziswa ubaba aragela phambili namhlanjesi.

Ngaphothula e-New Haven ngo-1915, ngemva kwekota yekhulu leminyaka ngemva kwakababa, begodu ngemva kwesikhathi esifitjhani ngahlanganyela ekufudukeni kwe-Teutonic okwalibaziseka okwaziwa nge-Great War. Woke umuntu ebengimazi bekasebhizinisini lamabhondi, njeke bengicabanga bona lingasekela enye indoda engatjhadileko.
