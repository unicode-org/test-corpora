VIII

Bengingakghoni ukulala ubusuku boke; i-foghorn beyibubula ngokungapheliko e-Sound, begodu ngaphaphatheka phakathi kweqiniso elingakajayeleki namabhudango ahlasimulisa umzimba, athusako. Ngasekuseni ngezwa itekisi ikhuphuka emgwaqweni ka-Gatsby, begodu khonokho ngagxuma embhedeni ngathoma ukugqoka—ngazizwa ngasuthi kunento engifuna ukumtjela yona, into engingamyelelisa ngayo, begodu ekuseni bekungaba sekwephuzile.

Nangiwela utjani bakhe, ngabona umnyango wakhe wangaphambili bewusavuliwe begodu bekathembele etafuleni ehholo, aphelelwa mamandla namkha ubuthongo.

"Akukho okwenzekileko," watjho ngokunganaki. "Ngalinda, kwathi nge-awa yesine weza efasidereni wajama lapho umzuzu bese wacima isibani."

Indlu yakhe beyingakaze ibonakale inkulu kimi njengalobo busuku lokha nasifuna ugwayi emakamelweni amakhulu. Sadudulela eceleni amakhetheni ebegade afana namaphaviliyoni, begodu sazizwa phezu kweenyawo ezinengi zeboda elimnyama ukuze sithole amaswitjhi wokukhanyisa kagesi—ngake ngawela ngokufafaza phezu kweenkinobho zepiyano eyisipokwe. Bekunethuli elinengi khulu kizo zoke iindawo, begodu amagumbi bekanomdaka, kwangathi bekangakavunyelwa umoya amalanga amanengi. Ngathola i-humidor etafuleni engingayaziko, ngaphakathi kunegwayi emibili eyomileko. Savula amafasitela we-French wegumbi lokudweba, sahlala sibhema ngaphandle ebumnyameni.

"Kufanele uhambe," Ngatjho. “Kuqinisekile bona bazokulandelela ikoloyi yakho.”

“Hamba khathesi , umdlalo omdala?”

"Iya e-Atlantic City iveke, nofana uye e-Montreal."

Bekangeze akucabangele. Bekangakghoni ukutjhiya u-Daisy bekube kulapho azi bona uzokwenzani. Bekabambe ithemba lokugcina begodu bengingasakghoni ukumtjhaphulula.

Kwakungobusuku lobu lapho angitjela khona indatjana engakajayeleki yobutjha bakhe no-Dan Cody—wangitjela yona ngombana “u-Jay Gatsby” bekaphuke njengengilazi ngokujamelana nobumbi obuqinileko baka-Tom, begodu ukudlala okude okuyimfihlo. Ngicabanga bona bekangavuma nanyana yini nje, ngaphandle kokugodla, kodwana bekafuna ukukhuluma ngo-Daisy.

Bekayintombazana yokuthoma “elungileko” ebekamazi. Ngeendlela ezihlukahlukeneko ezingakavezwa bekahlangane nabantu abanjalo, kodwana bekahlala anentambo engabonakaliko hlangana nayo. Wamthola athandeka khulu. Waya emzini wakhe, ekuthomeni namanye amapholisa we-Camp Taylor, bese waba yedwa. Kwamrara—bekangakaze abe sendlini ehle kangaka ngaphambilini. Kodwana okwenza bona kube nomoya wokuphelelwa mumoya, kukuthi u-Daisy bekahlala lapho—bekuyinto engakajayeleki kuye njengombana itende lakhe ngaphandle kwekamu belinjalo kuye. Bekuneemfihlakalo ezivuthiweko ngakho, umqondo wamagumbi wokulala angaphezulu amahle begodu apholile ukudlula amanye amagumbi wokulala, wemisebenzi yobutabane nekhazimulako eyenzeka emaphaseji wayo, kanye nezothando ebezingakabi mnyama begodu zibekwe kude nge-lavender kodwana zifretjhi begodu ziphefumula begodu zinephunga leenkoloyi ezicwebezelako zomnyaka lo kanye namadansi wamathuthumbo ebegade aneenthuthumbo. Kwamthabisa, naye, ukuthi amadoda amanengi besele amthanda u-Daisy—kwakhuphula inani lakhe emehlweni wakhe. Wezwa ubukhona babo kiyo yoke indlu, begcwala emoyeni ngemithunzi neemvakalelo zemizwa esaphilako.

Kodwana bekazi bona usendlini ka-Daisy ngengozi ekulu. Noma ngabe ingomuso lakhe njengo-Jay Gatsby lingahle libe lihle kangangani, gade alijaha elingenamali elingenasikhathi esidlulileko, begodu nganoma ngisiphi isikhathi ingubo engabonakaliko yeyunifomu yakhe ingatjhinga emahlombe wakhe. Ngalokho wasisebenzisa kuhle isikhathi sakhe. Wathatha lokho ebegade angakuthola, ngokuzikhakhazisa nangokungaziphathi kuhle—ekugcineni wathatha u-Daisy ngobunye ubusuku bakaMgwengweni, wamthatha ngombana bekanganalo ilungelo lokuthinta isandla sakhe.

Bekangazidelela, ngombana nakanjani bekamthathe ngaphasi kwamala. Angitjho bona bekathengise ngeengidigidi zakhe, kodwana bekanike u-Daisy umuzwa wokuvikeleka ngamabomu; wamvumela bona akholelwe bona bekangumuntu ovela esigabeni esifanako naye—ukuthi bekakghona ukumtlhogomela. Kwamambala, bekanganazo iinsetjenziswa ezinjalo—bekanganawo umndeni okhululekileko ojame ngemva kwakhe, begodu bekanesibopho ngokuthanda kwakarhulumende onganamuntu bona aphephetheke nanyana kukuphi ephasini.

Kodwana akhenge azidelele begodu akhenge kube ngendlela ebegade acabanga ngayo. Bekahlose, mhlamunye, ukuthatha lokho ebekangakwenza bese uya—kodwana nje wathola bona uzinikele ekulandeleni i-grail. Bekazi bona u-Daisy bekangakajayeleki, kodwana bekangaboni bona intombazana “ehle” ingaba yinto engakajayeleki kangangani. Wanyamalala wangena endlini yakhe ecebileko, empilweni yakhe ecebileko, ezeleko, watjhiya u-Gatsby—anganalitho. Wazizwa atjhade naye, bekuyilokho kwaphela.

Lokha nabahlangana godu, ngemva kwamalanga amabili, bekungu-Gatsby obekaphelelwa mumoya, owathi, ngandlela thize, wakhashelwa. Umpheme wakhe bewukhanya ngokunethezeka okuthengiweko kokukhanya kweenkwekwezi; I-wicker yesethi yakghwathaza ngendlela efanako njengombana aphendukela kuye begodu wamanga umlomo wakhe ofuna ukwazi nothandekako. Wabanjwa mkhuhlane, begodu lokho kwenza ilizwi lakhe laba mnandi begodu libe lihle khulu kunanini ngaphambili, begodu u-Gatsby bekazi kuhle ubutjha nemfihlakalo umnotho oyivalela begodu uyigcine, ubutjha bezambatho ezinengi, kanye no-Daisy, okhazimula njengesiliva, aphephile begodu azikhakhazisa ngaphezu kwemizabalazo etjhisako yabantu abatlhagako.

“Angikghoni ukukuhlathulula bona ngamangala kangangani ukuthola bona ngiyamthanda, umdlalo omdala. Bengithemba bona uzongilahla, kodwana akhenge enze njalo, ngombana naye bekathandana nami. Bekacabanga bona ngiyazi okunengi ngombana bengazi izinto ezihlukileko kuye... Kuhlekuhle, ngilapho, kude neenhloso zami, ngitjhinga khulu emzuzwini ngamunye, lokho ebengingakwenzi. ukusetjenziswa kokwenza izinto ezihle nangabe ngingaba nesikhathi esingcono sokumtjela bona ngizokwenzani?”

Ngemini yokugcina ngaphambi kobana aye ngaphandle, wahlala no-Daisy ezandleni zakhe isikhathi eside, athule. Bekulilanga lokuwa elibandako, umlilo ngekamelweni begodu iinhlathi zakhe zibomvu. Njalo bekanyakaza begodu watjhugulula ingalo yakhe kancani, begodu wake wamanga iinhluthu zakhe ezimnyama ezicwebezelako. Imini beyibenze bathula isikhatjhana, kwanga kubanikela inkumbulo ejulileko yokuhlukana okude okwakuthenjisiwe ngelanga elilandelako. Bebangakaze batjhidelene khulu ngenyanga yabo yethando, begodu bebangakhulumisani ngokudephileko, ukudlula lokha nakathinta iindebe ezithulileko ehlombe lejazi lakhe nofana lokha nakathinta umphetho weminwe yakhe, ngobumnene, ngasuthi ulele.

Wenza kuhle khulu epini. Bekangukaputeni ngaphambi kobana aye phambili, begodu ngemva kweempi ze-Argonne wathola inengi lakhe kanye nokulawula iinkhali zomtjhini. Ngemva kokuthula ipi walinga ngamandla ukubuyela ekhaya, kodwana ubudisi obuthileko nofana ukungazwisisani kwamthumela e-Oxford esikhundleni salokho. Bekakhathazekile nje—bekunekhwalithi yokuphelelwa lithemba okuthusako eencwadini zaka-Daisy. Bekangaboni bona kubayini angakwazi ukuza. Bekazwa ukugandeleleka kwephasi langaphandle, begodu bekafuna ukumbona begodu azizwe ubukhona bakhe eduze kwakhe begodu aqinisekiswe bona wenza into efaneleko.

Ngombana u-Daisy bekasesemutjha begodu umhlaba wakhe wokufakelwa bewugcwele ama-orchid kanye nokuzikhakhazisa okumnandi, okuthabisako kanye nama-orchestra abeka isigqi somnyaka, afingqa ukudana nokusikisela kwepilo ngeengoma ezitjha. Ubusuku boke ama-saxophone akhala ukuphawula okungenathemba kwe-“Beale Street Blues” ngesikhathi ama-slipper alikhulu wegolide nesiliva ahlanganisa ithuli elikhazimulako. Ngesikhathi setiye elimpunga bekuhlala kunamagumbi ebegade atjhabalala ngokungapheliko ngomkhuhlane ophansi, omnandi, ngesikhathi ubuso obutjha bukhukhuleka lapha nalaphaya njengamakari wamarozi aphephethekiswa ziimpondo ezidabukisako ezizungeze phasi.

Ngalesisikhathi sokuhlwa u-Daisy wathoma ukukhamba godu ngesizini; msinyana waphinda wabamba amadethi angaba yingcenye yetjhumi nambili ngelanga namadoda ayingcenye yetjhumi nambili, begodu alala ekuseni ngobuhlalu ne-chiffon yesigqoko sakusihlwa esiphithene hlangana nama-orchid afako phasi eceleni kombhede wakhe. Begodu ngaso soke isikhathi kukhona into ngaphakathi kwakhe ebeyifuna isiqunto. Bekafuna ipilo yakhe ibunjwe nje, khonokho—begodu isiqunto kufanele senziwe ngamandla athileko—wethando, wemali, wokusebenza okungathandabuzekiko—ebegade aseduze.

Amandla lawo athatha ubujamo phakathi kwentwasahlobo ngokufika kwaka-Tom Buchanan. Bekunobukhulu obuhle ngomuntu wakhe nesikhundla sakhe, begodu u-Daisy bekathabile. Akungabazeki bona bekunomzabalazo othileko nokuphumula okuthileko. Incwadi yafika ku-Gatsby ngesikhathi ase-Oxford.

Kwasekuseni e-Long Island begodu sakhamba sivula amanye amafasidera phasi, sazalisa indlu ngokukhanya okuphenduka okumpunga, okuphenduka igolide. Isithunzi somuthi sawela msinyana ngaphetjheya kwamazolo begodu iinyoni ezifana nesipokwe zathoma ukuvuma hlangana namakari ahlaza satjani. Kwaba nokunyakaza okubuthakathaka, okumnandi emoyeni, kunganamoya, okuthembisa ilanga elipholileko, elihle.

“Angicabangi bona gade amthanda.” U-Gatsby waphenduka ngefasitela wangiqala ngokugandelela. "Kufanele ukhumbule, mdlali omdala, bekathabile khulu namhlanjesi ntambama. Wamtjela izinto lezo ngendlela emthusako—okwenza bona kubonakale ngasuthi ngingumuntu othileko ohlakaniphileko. Begodu umphumela waba kukuthi bekangazi bona uthini."

Wahlala phasi ngokudana.

"Iye kungenzeka bona bekamthanda umzuzu nje, lokha nabathoma ukutjhada—begodu wangithanda khulu ngitjho nanyana kunjalo, uyabona?"

Msinyana waphuma ngekulumo efuna ukwazi.

"Nanyana kukuphi," watjho, "bekuyinto yomuntu mathupha."

Ungenzani ngalokho, ngaphandle kokusola ukuqina okuthileko ekucabangeni kwakhe ngendaba ebekungalinganiswa?

Wabuya e-France lokha u-Tom no-Daisy basesekhambweni labo lomtjhado, begodu wenza ikhambo elibuhlungu kodwana elingaphikiswako lokuya e-Louisville ngeholo lakhe lokugcina lebutho. Wahlala lapho iveke yoke, akhamba emigwaqweni lapho iinyawo zabo bezihlangane khona ebusuku boNovemba begodu abuyele eendaweni ezingabonakaliko lapho ebebatjhayele khona ngekoloyi yakhe emhlophe. Njengombana indlu ka-Daisy beyihlala ibonakala kuye iyimfihlakalo begodu iyisitabane ukudlula ezinye izindlu, umbono wakhe ngedorobha ngokwalo, nanyana bekangasekho kilo, bewuzele ubuhle obudabukisako.

Wasuka azizwa ngasuthi ngathana bekafuna khulu, bekangamthola—ukuthi uyamtjhiya ngemva. Umqeqetshi wemini—begade anganamali nje—begade atjhisa. Waphuma waya egumbini elivulekileko wahlala phasi esihlalweni esigoqekako, begodu isitetjhi satjhelela begodu ngemva kwezakhiwo ezingakajayeleki zadlula. Bese baphuma baya emasimini weentwasahlobo, lapho itroli ephuzi yabagijima khona umzuzu owodwa nabantu abaphakathi kwayo okungenzeka bona bakhe babona umlingo wobuso bakhe emgwaqweni ongakajayeleki.

Ithrekhi yagoba begodu nje besele isukela elangeni, ebegade, njengombana itjhinga phasi, bekubonakala ngasuthi iyarhatjheka ngokudumisa idorobha elinyamalalako lapho gade aphefumula khona. Welula isandla sakhe ngokuphelelwa lithemba ngasuthi ufuna ukuhlwitha umoya omncani, ukusindisa isiqetjhana sendawo ebegade amenzele yona. Kodwana koke besele kudlula msinya khulu emehlweni wakhe angabonakaliko begodu bekazi bona sele alahlekelwe yingcenye yakhona, entsha nehle khulu, unomphela.

Bekuyi-awa lethoba lokha nasiqeda ukudla kwasekuseni saphuma saya emphemeni. Ubusuku bebutjhugulule khulu ubujamo bezulu begodu bekunefleyiva yekwindla emoyeni. Umlimi, owokugcina weensebenzi zakaGatsby zangaphambilini, weza ngaphasi kwezitebhisi.

"Ngizokukhipha amanzi echibini namhlanjesi, Mnumzana Gatsby. Amakari azokuthoma ukuwa msinyana, bese kuba nemiraro ngamapayipi."

“Ungakwenzi namhlanjesi,” kuphendula u-Gatsby. Waphendukela kimi ngokucolisa. “Uyazi, umdlalo omdala, angikaze ngisebenzise idamu lelo ihlobo loke?”

Ngaqala iwatjhi yami ngasukuma.

“Imizuzu elitjhumi nambili ukuya esitimeleni sami.”

Bengingafuni ukuya edorobheni. Bengingakafaneli umsebenzi omuhle, kodwana bekungaphezu kwalokho—bengingafuni ukutjhiya u-Gatsby. Ngaphuthelwa sitimela leso, bese kuthi esinye, ngaphambi kobana ngiphuthelwe.

“Ngizokubiza,” Ngatjho ekugcineni.

“Yenza, umdlalo omdala.”

“Ngizokufonela ngemini.”

Sakhamba kancanikancani sehla ngezitebhisi.

“Ngiyacabanga bona u-Daisy naye uzokubiza.” Wangiqala ngokutshwenyeka, kwanga uthemba bona ngizokuqinisekisa lokhu.

“Ngicabanga bona kunjalo.”

“Kulungile, salakahle.”

Saxhawulana begodu ngathoma ukuhamba. Ngaphambi kobana ngifike esihlahleni ngakhumbula okuthileko begodu ngaphenduka.

“Basiqubuthu esibolileko,” ngarhuwelela ngaphetjheya kwetjani. “Ufanele isiqhema soke esihlanganisiweko.”

Bengisolo ngithabile ngokuthi ngitjho lokho. Bekukuphela kokuncoma engake ngamnikela khona, ngombana bengingamuthandi kusukela ekuthomeni bekube sekugcineni. Kokuthoma wavuma ngehloko, bese ubuso bakhe baphuka baba yimomotheka ekhanyako nezwisisako, kwangathi besihlala sikhuluma ngokuthaba ngalelo qiniso ngaso soke isikhathi. Ithawula lakhe elipinki elihle lesudu lenza indawo ekhanyako yombala malungana namagadango amhlophe, begodu ngacabanga ngobusuku lapho ngifika khona ekhaya labokhokho bakhe, eenyangeni ezintathu ngaphambili. Utjani nekoloyi bekugcwele ubuso balabo ebegade baqagela ukukhohlakala kwakhe—begodu bekajame phezu kwalawo magadango, afihla ibhudango lakhe elingabonakaliko, njengombana abavalelisa.

Ngamthokoza ngokungenisa kwakhe iinvakatjhi. Besisolo simthokoza ngalokho—mina nabanye.

“Sala kuhle,” ngamemeza. "Ngithokozele isidlo sasekuseni, Gatsby."

Phezulu edorobheni, ngazama isikhatjhana ukutlola iingcaphuno ezithengiswako, bese ngalala esihlalweni sami esijikajikako. Ngaphambi nje kwemini ifowuni yangivusa, begodu ngathoma ukuvuka ngijuluka esiphongweni sami. Bekungu-Jordan Baker; bekavame ukungibiza ngalesisikhathi ngombana ukungaqiniseki kokuhamba kwakhe hlangana namahotela namakilabhu nezindlu zangasese kwenza bona kube budisi ukumthola ngenye indlela. Ngokuvamileko ilizwi lakhe belizwakala njengento etja nepholileko, kwangathi i-divot evela ema-links wegalofu aluhlaza ingene ngefasitela lehhovisi, kodwana namhlanje ekuseni bekubonakala kunzima begodu komile.

“Ngitjhiye indlu ka-Daisy,” watjho. "Ngise-Hempstead, begodu ngiya e-Southampton namhlanjesi ntambama."

Mhlamunye bekukuhlakanipha ukutjhiya indlu ka-Daisy, kodwana isenzo sangicasula, begodu amazwi wakhe alandelako angenza ngaba budisi.

“Awuzange ube muhle kimi izolo ebusuku.”

"Bekungaba yinto eqakathekileko njani?"

Ukuthula isikhatjhana. Yeke:

"Kodwana—ngifuna ukukubona."

“Nami ngifuna ukukubona.”

“Akhesithi angiyi e-Southampton, bese ngiza edorobheni namhlanjesi ntambama?”

“Awa—angicabangi bona namhlanje ntambama.”

“Kulungile khulu.”

“Angeze kwenzeka namhlanjesi ntambama. Ezihlukahlukeneko—”

Sakhuluma njalo isikhatjhana, bese kuthi msinyana singasakhulumi. Angazi bona ngubani kithi owavala umtato ngokuchofoza okubukhali, kodwana ngiyazi bona benginganandaba. Bengingekhe ngikhulume naye ngaphetjheya kwetafula letiye ngalelo langa ngathana azange ngikhulume naye godu ephasini.

Ngafonela umuzi ka-Gatsby ngemva kwemizuzu embalwa, kodwana umgca bewuphithizela. Ngizamile kane; Ekugcineni isikhulu esithukutheleko sangitjela bona intambo beyigcinwa ivulekile ibanga elide ukusuka e-Detroit. Ngikhipha isikhathi sami, ngadweba isiyingi esincani esizungeze isitimela samatjhumi amathathu namatjhumi amahlanu. Ngabe sengigoba esihlalweni sami ngazama ukucabanga. Bekusemini nje.

Lokha nangidlula iinqwaba zomlotha esitimeleni ekuseni ngalelo langa bengiwele ngakelinye ihlangothi lekoloyi ngamabomu. Bengicabanga bona kuzokuba nesiqubuthu esifuna ukwazi lapho ilanga loke nabafana abancani abafuna amabala amnyama ethulini, nendoda ethile ekhuluma ngokuphindaphindiweko ukuthi kwenzekeni, bekwaba kulapho kungasakwazi ukukutjho, begodu ipumelelo ka-Myrtle Wilson ebuhlungu yakhohlwa. Nje ngifuna ukubuyela emuva kancani ngiyokutjela bona kwenzekeni egaraji ngemva kobana siphume lapho ebusuku bangaphambilini.

Baba nobudisi bokufumana udade, u-Catherine. Kufuze bona wephule umthetho wakhe wokusela ngalobo busuku, ngombana nakafikako bekayisilima ngotshwala begodu bekangakghoni ukuzwisisa bona i-ambulensi besele iye e-Flushing. Lokha nabamkholisa ngalokhu, wahle waquleka, kwanga lokho bekuyingcenye engabekezeleleki yendaba. Omunye umuntu, onomusa namkha ofuna ukwazi, wamthatha ngekoloyi yakhe wamtjhayela ngemva kokuthi isidumbu sikadadwabo sibe khona.

Bekwaba ngemva kwesikhathi eside phakathi kobusuku isiqubuthu esitjhugulukako sahlangana ngaphambili kwegaraji, ngesikhathi u-George Wilson azinyakazisa phambili nasemuva esofeni ngaphakathi. Okwesikhatjhana umnyango we-ofisi bewuvulekile, begodu woke umuntu obekangena ngegaraji bekaqala ngawo. Ekugcineni omunye wathi bekulihlazo, begodu wavala umnyango. U-Michaelis namanye amadoda amanengi bekanaye; kokuthoma, amadoda amane nofana amahlanu, ngokukhamba kwesikhathi amadoda amabili nofana amathathu. Ngokukhamba kwesikhathi u-Michaelis kwafanela bona acele umfokazi wokugcina bona alinde lapho eminye imizuzu elitjhumi nahlanu, ngesikhathi yena abuyela endaweni yakhe enze imbiza yekhofi. Ngemva kwalokho, wahlala lapho yedwa no-Wilson bekwaba sekuseni.

Nge-awa lesithathu ikhwalithi yokububula okungazwakaliko kwaka-Wilson yatjhuguluka—wathula begodu wathoma ukukhuluma ngekoloyi ephuzi. Wamemezela bona unendlela yokuthola bona ikoloyi etjheli ngekabani, bese wathi eenyangeni ezimbalwa ezidlulileko umkakhe bekavela edorobheni ubuso bakhe buvuvukile begodu ikhala lakhe livuvukile.

Kodwana nakazwa yena atjho lokhu, wathuka wathoma ukukhala “Hawu, Zimu wami!” godu ngelizwi lakhe elibubulako. U-Michaelis wenza umzamo ongasimuhle wokumphazamisa.

"Unesikhathi esingangani utjhadile, George? Woza lapho, uzame ukuhlala umzuzwana, uphendule umbuzo wami. Sele kusikhathi esingangani utjhadile?"

“Iminyaka elitjhumi nambili.”

"Khewaba nabantwana? Woza, George, hlala phasi—ngikubuze umbuzo. Khewaba nabantwana?"

Amabhungane ansundu aqinileko bekaragela phambili nokutjhayisana nokukhanya okufipheleko, begodu nanyana kunini lokha u-Michaelis nakazwa ikoloyi itjhabalala endleleni ngaphandle bekuzwakala kuye njengekoloyi ebegade ingakajame ama-awa ambalwa ngaphambilini. Bekangathandi ukungena egaraji, ngombana ibhentjhi lokusebenza belinamabala lapho isidumbu besilele khona, njeke bekakhamba angakhululeki e-ofisini—bekazi yoke into ekhona ngaphambi kokusa—begodu ngezikhathi ezithileko bekahlala phasi eduze no-Wilson alinga ukumenza athule.

"Unebandla oya kilo kesinye isikhathi, George? Mhlamunye ngitjho nanyana sele isikhathi eside ungazange uye kilo? Mhlamunye ngingabiza isondo ngithole umfundisi ozokuza akhulume nawe, uyabona?"

“Ungabi ngowamunye.”

Inengi leendawo ezinkulu eziseduze nelwandle besele zivaliwe begodu bekunganamalambu ngaphandle kokukhanya okufipheleko, okukhamba kwesikepe ngaphetjheya kwe-Sound. Begodu njengombana inyanga iphakama phezulu izindlu ezingakaqakatheki zathoma ukuncibilika bekwaba kulapho kancanikancani ngiqaphela isihlengele esidala lapha ebesiqhakaza kanye emehlweni wamatilosi we-Dutch—isifuba esitjha, esihlaza satjani sephasi elitjha. Imithi yayo eyanyamalalako, imithi ebegade yenza indawo yendlu ka-Gatsby, gade ikhuluma ngokuhleba emabhudangweni wokugcina namakhulu khulu wabantu; ngesikhatjhana nje umuntu kufuze bona ubambe umphefumulo wakhe phambi kwezwekazi, agandelelekile bona acabangisise ngobuhle ebegade angazwisisi begodu angafuni, ubuso nobuso kokugcina emlandwini ngento ehambelana nekghono lakhe lokumangala.

Njengombana ngihlezi lapho ngicabanga ngephasi elidala, elingaziwako, ngacabanga ngokumangala kwaka-Gatsby lokha nakathoma ukukhetha ukukhanya okuluhlaza ekupheleni kwedokodo laka-Daisy. Bekafike kude ukuya ehlathini elihlaza satjani, begodu ibhudango lakhe kufuze bona belibonakala liseduze kangangobana bekangakghoni ukubhalelwa kulizwisisa. Bekangazi bona sele kusemva kwakhe, endaweni ethile emuva ebumnyameni obukhulu ngale kwedorobha, lapho amasimu amnyama weriphabliki begade aragela phambili ngaphasi kobusuku.

U-Gatsby bekakholelwa ekukhanyeni okuluhlaza, ingomuso elihle elibuyela emuva umnyaka nomnyaka phambi kwethu. Kwasibalekela ngaleso sikhathi, kodwana lokho akunandaba—kusasa sizokugijima msinyana, silule iingalo zethu phambili... Begodu ngelinye ilanga ekuseni elihle—

Njeke siragela phambili, iinkepe eziphikisana nomsinga, sibuyiselwa emuva ngokungapheliko esikhathini esidlulileko.
