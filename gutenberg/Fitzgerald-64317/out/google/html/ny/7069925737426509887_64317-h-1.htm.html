—Ndinali kuyenda kuchokera kumalo ena kupita kwina, theka m’mbali mwa misewu ndi theka pa kapinga. Ndinali wosangalala kwambiri pa kapinga chifukwa ndinali nditavala nsapato zochokera ku England zokhala ndi mphira pazitsulo zomwe zimaluma pansi pofewa. Ndinali nditavalanso siketi yatsopano ya plaid yomwe inkawomba pang'ono mu mphepo, ndipo pamene izi zinkachitika zikwangwani zofiira, zoyera, ndi zabuluu kutsogolo kwa nyumba zonse zinkatambasula mowuma ndi kunena kuti tut-tut-tut-tut , motsutsa.

Zikwangwani zazikulu kwambiri komanso udzu waukulu kwambiri zinali za nyumba ya Daisy Fay. Iye anali wazaka khumi ndi zisanu ndi zitatu zokha, wamkulu kwa ine zaka ziwiri, ndipo pofika patali wotchuka kwambiri mwa asungwana aang’ono onse mu Louisville. Anavala zoyera, ndipo anali ndi roadster yaying'ono yoyera, ndipo usana wonse telefoni inalira m'nyumba mwake ndipo okondwa achichepere ochokera ku Camp Taylor adafuna mwayi womulamulira usiku womwewo. Komabe, kwa ola limodzi!

Pamene ndinafika moyang'anizana ndi nyumba yake m'mawa umenewo msewu wake woyera unali pafupi ndi mbiya, ndipo iye anali atakhala mmenemo ndi Lieutenant yemwe ndinali ndisanamuwonepo. Anali otanganidwa kwambiri moti sanandione mpaka nditatsala pang’ono kufika.

“Moni, Jordan,” anayitana mosayembekezera. “Chonde bwerani kuno.”

Ndinasangalala kuti ankafuna kundilankhula chifukwa pa atsikana achikulire omwe ndinkawasirira kwambiri. Anandifunsa ngati ndikupita ku Red Cross kukapanga mabandeji. Ine ndinali. Ndiye, kodi ndikanawauza kuti sangabwere tsiku limenelo? Wapolisiyo anayang’ana Daisy pamene anali kuyankhula, m’njira yoti mtsikana aliyense amafuna kuti nthawi ina azimuyang’ana, ndipo chifukwa chakuti zinkaoneka zachikondi kwa ine, ndakumbukira zimene zinachitikazo kuyambira nthawi imeneyo. Dzina lake linali Jay Gatsby, ndipo sindinamuyang'anenso kwa zaka zoposa zinayi—ngakhale nditakumana naye ku Long Island sindinazindikire kuti anali mwamuna yemweyo.

Izo zinali naintini-khumi ndi seveni. Pofika chaka chotsatira, inenso ndinali ndi okongoletsa angapo, ndipo ndinayamba kuchita masewera olimbitsa thupi, choncho Daisy sindinkamuona kawirikawiri. Anapita ndi gulu la anthu okulirapo pang'ono-pamene adapita ndi aliyense. Mphekesera zolusa zinali kufalikira ponena za iye—momwe amayi ake anampeza akulongeza chikwama chake usiku wina wachisanu kuti apite ku New York ndi kukatsanzikana ndi msilikali amene anali kupita kutsidya la nyanja. Anamuletsa, koma sanalankhule ndi banja lake kwa milungu ingapo. Pambuyo pake sanasewerenso ndi asilikali, koma ndi anyamata ochepa amiyendo yathyathyathya, osaona zamfupi m’taunimo, amene sakanatha kuloŵa usilikali nkomwe.

Pofika m'dzinja lotsatira adalinso gay, gay monga kale. Anali ndi chiyambi pambuyo pa nkhondo, ndipo mu February ayenera kuti anali pachibwenzi ndi mwamuna wochokera ku New Orleans. Mu June adakwatiwa ndi Tom Buchanan waku Chicago, wokhala ndi mbiri komanso zochitika zambiri kuposa zomwe Louisville adazidziwa kale. Anatsika ndi anthu zana limodzi m'magalimoto anayi achinsinsi, ndipo adalemba ganyu pansi pa Muhlbach Hotel, ndipo kutatsala tsiku limodzi ukwatiwo anam'patsa ngale zamtengo wa madola zikwi mazana atatu ndi makumi asanu.

Ndinali mkwatibwi. Ndinalowa m’chipinda chake kutatsala theka la ola kuti chakudya cha mkwatibwi chiyambe, ndipo ndinamupeza atagona pabedi lake mokongola ngati usiku wa June atavala zovala zake zamaluwa—komanso ataledzera ngati nyani. Anali ndi botolo la Sauterne m'dzanja limodzi ndi kalata m'dzanja lina.

“'Ndithokozeni,” anadandaula motero. "Sindinamwepo kale, koma o, ndimasangalala nazo bwanji."

"Chavuta ndi chiyani, Daisy?"

Ndinachita mantha, ndikukuuzani; Sindinayambe ndamuwonapo mtsikana wotero.

"Inde, okondedwa." Anapapasa m’basiketi lotayirira lomwe anali nalo pakama n’kusolola chingwe cha ngale. "Zitengereni pansi ndikuzibweza kwa aliyense amene ali ake, auzeni Daisy kuti asinthe zanga zake. Nenani: 'Daisy wasintha' wanga wake!' ”

Anayamba kulira—analira ndi kulira. Ndinatuluka mothamanga ndikupeza wantchito wa amayi ake, ndipo tinatseka chitseko ndikuwalowetsa m’bafa lozizira. Iye sanalole kusiya kalatayo. Analitenga nalo m’bafa n’kulifinyira mumpira wonyowa, ndipo anangondisiya m’mbale ya sopo ataona kuti likugwa ngati chipale chofewa.

Koma sananenenso mawu ena. Tinamupatsa mizimu ya ammonia ndikuyika ayezi pamphumi pake ndikumukokera kumbuyo mu diresi yake, ndipo theka la ola pambuyo pake, pamene tinatuluka m'chipindacho, ngale zinali pakhosi pake ndipo chochitikacho chinatha. Tsiku lotsatira pa 5 koloko anakwatiwa ndi Tom Buchanan popanda kunjenjemera, ndipo anayamba ulendo wa miyezi itatu kupita ku South Seas.

Ndinawawona ku Santa Barbara atabwerako, ndipo ndinaganiza kuti sindinawonepo mtsikana wokwiyira kwambiri mwamuna wake. Akatuluka mchipindamo kwa mphindi imodzi amangoyang'ana movutikira, ndikuti: "Tom wapita kuti?" ndi kuvala mawonekedwe osawoneka bwino mpaka adamuwona akulowa pakhomo. Iye ankakonda kukhala pamchenga mutu wake uli m’chifuwa chake pofika ola, akusisita zala zake m’maso mwake ndi kumuyang’ana ndi chisangalalo chosaneneka. Zinali zosangalatsa kuwaona ali limodzi—zinakupangitsani inu kuseka mwakachetechete, mwachidwi. Umo munali mu Ogasiti. Patapita mlungu umodzi kuchokera pamene ndinachoka ku Santa Barbara, Tom anathamangira m’ngolo ya msewu wa Ventura usiku wina, ndipo anang’amba gudumu lakutsogolo pa galimoto yake. Msungwana yemwe anali naye adalowanso m'mapepala, chifukwa mkono wake unathyoka-anali mmodzi wa ogwira ntchito ku Santa Barbara Hotel.

April wotsatira Daisy anatenga mwana wake wamkazi, ndipo anapita ku France kwa chaka chimodzi. Ine ndinawawona iwo kasupe wina ku Cannes, ndipo kenako ku Deauville, ndiyeno iwo anabwerera ku Chicago kuti akakhazikike. Daisy anali wotchuka ku Chicago, monga mukudziwa. Anayenda ndi khamu la anthu othamanga, onse aang'ono ndi olemera ndi olusa, koma iye anatuluka ndi mbiri yabwino kwambiri. Mwina chifukwa chakuti samamwa. Ndibwino kwambiri kusamwa mowa pakati pa anthu omwe amamwa mowa kwambiri. Mungathe kugwira lilime lanu, ndipo, kuwonjezera apo, mutha kuwongolera kachitidwe kakang'ono kanu kuti wina aliyense akhale wakhungu kotero kuti asawone kapena kusamala. Mwina Daisy sanachitepo konse zachikondi—komabe pali china chake m’mawu ake amenewo…

Chabwino, pafupifupi masabata asanu ndi limodzi apitawo, adamva dzina lakuti Gatsby kwa nthawi yoyamba m'zaka. Panali pamene ndinakufunsani—mukumbukira—ngati mumamudziwa Gatsby ku West Egg. Utatha kupita kunyumba adalowa mchipinda changa ndikundidzutsa, nati, "Gatsby watani?" ndipo nditamufotokozera—ndinali m’tulo tatifupi—analankhula modabwitsa kwambiri kuti ayenera kukhala mwamuna yemwe ankamudziwa kale. Sipanapite nthawi yomwe ndinamulumikiza Gatsby uyu ndi mkulu wa galimoto yake yoyera.

Pamene Jordan Baker anamaliza kunena zonsezi tinachoka ku Plaza kwa theka la ola ndikuyendetsa galimoto ku victoria kudutsa Central Park. Dzuwa linali litalowa kuseri kwa zipinda zazitali za akatswiri a kanema ku West Fifties, ndipo mawu omveka bwino a ana, omwe anasonkhana kale ngati cricket paudzu, adadzuka kumadzulo kotentha:

“Ine ndine Mtsogoleri wa Araby.
Chikondi chanu ndi changa.
Usiku pamene mukugona
Ndidzakwawira m’hema wako—”

“Zinali zongochitika mwangozi,” ndinatero.

"Koma sizinangochitika mwangozi ayi."

"Kulekeranji?"

"Gatsby adagula nyumbayo kuti Daisy akhale kutsidya lina."

Ndiye sizinali nyenyezi chabe zimene ankalakalaka pa usiku wa June umenewo. Anadza kwa ine wamoyo, napulumutsidwa mwadzidzidzi m'mimba mwa ulemerero wake wopanda cholinga.

“Akufuna kudziwa,” anapitiriza motero Jordan, “ngati mungamuitane Daisy kunyumba kwanu masana ena ndiyeno nkumulola abwere.”

Kudzichepetsa kwa zofunazo kunandigwedeza. Anadikira zaka zisanu n'kugula nyumba yaikulu kumene ankawalitsira njenjete za nyenyezi kuti 'abwere' masana ena kumunda wa munthu wachilendo.

"Kodi ndiyenera kudziwa zonsezi asanandifunse kanthu kakang'ono ngati?"

Amachita mantha, wadikirira kwa nthawi yayitali. Amaganiza kuti mwina mungakhumudwe.

Chinachake chidandidetsa nkhawa.

"Bwanji sanakufunseni kuti mukonze zokumana?"

“Akufuna kuti awone nyumba yake,” iye anafotokoza motero. "Ndipo nyumba yako ili pafupi."

“O!”

"Ndikuganiza kuti theka amayembekeza kuti alowe m'gulu la maphwando ake, usiku wina," adatero Jordan, "koma sanatero. Kenako anayamba kufunsa anthu mwachisawawa ngati amamudziwa, ndipo ine ndinali woyamba kumupeza. Unali usiku womwewo adanditumizira kuvina kwake, ndipo mukanamva momwe adagwirira ntchito.

“ 'Sindikufuna kuchita chilichonse chosokoneza!' Anapitiriza kunena kuti 'Ndikufuna kumuwona pafupi ndi nyumba yake.'

“Nditanena kuti ndinu bwenzi lenileni la Tom, anayamba kusiya maganizo onsewo.” Sakudziwa zambiri zokhudza Tom, ngakhale kuti ananena kuti anawerenga nyuzipepala ya ku Chicago kwa zaka zambiri kuti angoona dzina la Daisy.

Kunali mdima tsopano, ndipo pamene tinali kuviika pansi pa mlatho wawung'ono ndinayika mkono wanga mozungulira phewa lagolide la Jordan ndikumukokera kwa ine ndikumupempha kuti adye. Mwadzidzidzi sindimaganiziranso za Daisy ndi Gatsby, koma za munthu waukhondo, wolimba, woperewera, yemwe amachita zokayikitsa zapadziko lonse lapansi, komanso yemwe adatsamira mmbuyo mwachangu mkati mwa bwalo la mkono wanga. Mawu ena anayamba kumveka m’makutu mwanga ndi chisangalalo chamutu: “Pali othamangitsidwa, olondola, otanganidwa, ndi otopa okha.”

"Ndipo Daisy ayenera kukhala ndi chinachake m'moyo wake," anadandaula Jordan kwa ine.

"Kodi akufuna kumuwona Gatsby?"

"Iye sayenera kudziwa za izi. Gatsby sakufuna kuti adziwe. Muyenera kumuitanira ku tiyi."

Tinadutsa chipilala cha mitengo yakuda, ndiyeno kutsogolo kwa Fifty-Ninth Street, komwe kunali kuwala kotuwa, kunawalira m’paki. Mosiyana ndi Gatsby ndi Tom Buchanan, ndinalibe mtsikana amene nkhope yake yopanda thupi inkayandama m’mbali mwa macornices amdima ndi zizindikiro zochititsa khungu, motero ndinakokera mtsikanayo pambali panga, ndikumangitsa manja anga. Pakamwa pake, mwachipongwe adamwetulira, ndipo ndinamukokeranso pafupi, nthawi ino pamaso panga.
