Sindinathe kugona usiku wonse; chifunga chinali kubuula mosalekeza pa Phokoso, ndipo ine ndinaponyedwa odwala theka pakati pa zenizeni zowopsa ndi maloto owopsa, owopsa. M’bandakucha ndinamva takisi ikukwera pagalimoto ya Gatsby, ndipo nthaŵi yomweyo ndinalumpha pabedi ndikuyamba kuvala—ndinamva kuti ndinali ndi chinachake choti ndimuuze, chinachake choti ndimuchenjeze, ndipo m’mawa ukanakhala mochedwa kwambiri.

Ndikawoloka kapinga wake, ndinaona kuti chitseko chake chakumaso chinali chitsegukire ndipo anatsamira patebulo m’holoyo, atalemedwa ndi kukhumudwa kapena kugona.

“Palibe chimene chinachitika,” iye anatero mwamwano. “Ndinadikirira, ndipo cha m’ma 4 koloko anadza pa zenera naima pamenepo kwa mphindi imodzi ndiyeno anazimitsa nyaliyo.”

Nyumba yake inali isanaoneke ngati yaikulu kwambiri kwa ine monga momwe zinkakhalira usiku umenewo pamene ife tinkasaka zipinda zazikulu za ndudu. Tinakankhira pambali makatani omwe anali ngati mabwalo, ndipo tinakhudza mapazi osawerengeka a khoma lakuda la mawotchi amagetsi-nthawi ina ndinagwa ndi makiyi a piyano yamzimu. Ponseponse panali fumbi losadziwika bwino, ndipo zipindazo zinali zamatope, ngati kuti sizinaulutsidwe kwa masiku ambiri. Ndinapeza chinyezi patebulo lachilendo, mkati mwake muli ndudu ziwiri zakale zouma. Titatsegula mazenera achifalansa a chipinda chojambuliramo, tinakhala tikusuta mumdima.

“Uyenera kuchoka,” ndinatero. "Zachidziwikire kuti atsatira galimoto yanu."

"Choka tsopano , masewera akale?"

"Pitani ku Atlantic City kwa sabata imodzi, kapena ku Montreal."

Iye sakanati azilingalira izo. Sakanatha kumusiya Daisy mpaka atadziwa zomwe akufuna kuchita. Anali atagwira chiyembekezo chomaliza ndipo sindinapirire kumugwedeza.

Unali usiku womwewu pomwe adandiuza nkhani yachilendo yaunyamata wake ndi Dan Cody - adandiuza chifukwa "Jay Gatsby" adasweka ngati galasi motsutsana ndi zoyipa za Tom, ndipo chinsinsi chachinsinsi chidaseweredwa. Ndikuganiza kuti akadavomereza chilichonse tsopano, popanda kusungitsa, koma amafuna kulankhula za Daisy.

Iye anali mtsikana “wabwino” woyamba kumudziwa. M'machitidwe osiyanasiyana osawululidwa adakumana ndi anthu oterowo, koma nthawi zonse amakhala ndi waya waminga wosawoneka pakati. Anamupeza kukhala wofunika kwambiri. Anapita kunyumba kwake, poyamba ndi akuluakulu ena a ku Camp Taylor, ndiye yekha. Zinamudabwitsa kwambiri chifukwa anali asanakhalepo m’nyumba yokongola ngati imeneyi. Koma chomwe chinapangitsa kuti izi zitheke kwambiri, chinali chakuti Daisy amakhala kumeneko - chinali chinthu wamba kwa iye monga momwe hema wake kumsasa analiri kwa iye. Panali chinsinsi chakupsa ponena za izo, chithunzi cha zipinda chapamwamba chokongola komanso chozizira kuposa zipinda zina zogona, za zochitika za gay ndi zowoneka bwino zomwe zikuchitika m'makonde ake, ndi zachikondi zomwe sizinali zowonongeka komanso zowonongeka kale mu lavender koma zatsopano ndi zopuma komanso zowonongeka za magalimoto onyezimira a chaka chino ndi zovina zomwe maluwa ake anali osowa. Zinamusangalatsanso kuti amuna ambiri anali atamukonda kale Daisy-zinawonjezera phindu lake m'maso mwake. Anamva kupezeka kwawo m'nyumba yonseyo, akufalikira mlengalenga ndi mithunzi ndi zomveka zamaganizo amphamvu.

Koma ankadziwa kuti anali m’nyumba ya Daisy pa ngozi yoopsa kwambiri. Ngakhale kuti tsogolo lake lingakhale laulemerero ngati Jay Gatsby, pakali pano anali mnyamata wopanda ndalama popanda kale, ndipo nthawi iliyonse chovala chosaoneka cha yunifolomu yake chikhoza kuchoka pamapewa ake. Choncho anagwiritsa ntchito bwino nthawi yake. Anatenga zomwe akanatha kupeza, mwamwano komanso mopanda chifundo - pamapeto pake adatenga Daisy usiku umodzi womwe udalibe wa Okutobala, adamutenga chifukwa analibe ufulu weniweni wokhudza dzanja lake.

N’kutheka kuti anadzipeputsa chifukwa anali atamunamizira. Sindikutanthauza kuti adagulitsa mamiliyoni ake a phantom, koma adapatsa dala Daisy kukhala wotetezeka; anamulola kukhulupirira kuti iye anali munthu wosiyana kwambiri ndi iyeyo—kuti anali wokhoza mokwanira kumsamalira. Kunena zoona, iye analibe malo oterowo—analibe banja labwino lomwe linali pambuyo pake, ndipo anali ndi mlandu pakufuna kwa boma lopanda umunthu kuti liwukitsidwe kulikonse padziko lapansi.

Koma sanadzipeputsa ndipo sizinali monga momwe amaganizira. Iye ankafuna, mwinamwake, kutenga zomwe akanatha ndi kupita - koma tsopano adapeza kuti adadzipereka yekha ku zotsatira za grail. Iye ankadziwa kuti Daisy ndi wodabwitsa, koma sankadziwa kuti mtsikana “wabwino” angakhale wodabwitsa bwanji. Adasowa m'nyumba yake yolemera, m'moyo wake wolemera, wathunthu, ndikusiya Gatsby - wopanda kalikonse. Anadzimva kukhala wokwatiwa naye, ndizo zonse.

Atakumananso, patatha masiku awiri, anali Gatsby yemwe anali wopumira, yemwe, mwanjira ina, adaperekedwa. Khonde lake linali lowala ndi kuwala kogulidwa kwa nyenyezi; nyali wa settee adasisita mwamafashoni pomwe adatembenukira kwa iye ndipo adapsompsona pakamwa pake mwachidwi komanso wokondeka. Adagwidwa ndi chimfine, ndipo chidapangitsa mawu ake kukhala omveka komanso owoneka bwino kuposa kale, ndipo Gatsby ankadziwa bwino za unyamata ndi zinsinsi zomwe chuma chimatsekereza ndikusunga, za kutsitsimuka kwa zovala zambiri, komanso za Daisy, zonyezimira ngati siliva, zotetezeka komanso zonyada kuposa zovuta zaumphawi.

"Sindingathe kukufotokozerani momwe ndinadabwira kupeza kuti ndimamukonda, masewera akale." Ndinayembekeza kwa kanthawi kuti andiponya, koma sanatero, chifukwa ankandikondanso. Amaganiza kuti ndikudziwa zambiri chifukwa ndimadziwa zinthu zosiyana ndi iye ... kodi ndikanatani?"

Madzulo omaliza asanapite kunja, anakhala ndi Daisy m'manja mwake kwa nthawi yaitali, mwakachetechete. Linali tsiku lozizira kwambiri, ndipo m’chipindamo munali moto ndipo masaya ake anali akutuluka. Nthawi ndi nthawi iye ankasuntha ndipo iye anasintha mkono wake pang'ono, ndipo kamodzi iye anapsyopsyona iye mdima wonyezimira tsitsi. Madzulo anali atawapangitsa kukhala bata kwakanthawi, ngati kuti amawakumbukira mozama za kusiyana kwakutali komwe kunalonjeza tsiku lotsatira. Iwo anali asanakhalepo pafupi m'mwezi wawo wachikondi, kapena kulankhulana mozama kwambiri wina ndi mzake, kuposa pamene iye anagwedeza milomo yachete pa phewa la chovala chake kapena pamene iye anakhudza kumapeto kwa zala zake, mofatsa, ngati kuti anali mtulo.

Anachita bwino kwambiri pankhondoyo. Anali kapitawo asanapite kutsogolo, ndipo potsatira nkhondo za Argonne adapeza unyinji wake komanso kulamula kwa mfuti zamakina. Pambuyo pa nkhondoyi adayesetsa kuti apite kunyumba, koma zovuta zina kapena kusamvetsetsana kunamutumiza ku Oxford. Iye anali ndi nkhawa tsopano—mumakalata a Daisy munali khalidwe la kuthedwa nzeru. Sanawone chifukwa chake sakanabwera. Amamva kupsinjika kwa dziko kunja, ndipo amafuna kumuwona ndikumva kukhalapo kwake pambali pake ndikutsimikiziridwa kuti akuchita zabwino pambuyo pake.

Pakuti Daisy anali wamng'ono ndipo dziko lake lochita kupanga linali lodzaza ndi maluwa okongola komanso osangalatsa, oimba nyimbo zachisangalalo ndi okhestra zomwe zinkakhazikitsa kamvekedwe ka chaka, kulongosola mwachidule zachisoni ndi malingaliro a moyo mu nyimbo zatsopano. Usiku wonse ma saxophones analira ndemanga yopanda chiyembekezo ya "Beale Street Blues" pamene mapeyala zana a golide ndi siliva amatsuka fumbi lowala. Pa ola la tiyi wotuwa nthawi zonse panali zipinda zomwe zimagunda mosalekeza ndi malungo otsika, okoma, pomwe nkhope zatsopano zimayenda uku ndi uko ngati maluwa a duwa omwe amawomberedwa ndi nyanga zachisoni kuzungulira pansi.

Kupyolera mu chilengedwe chamadzulo ichi Daisy anayamba kuyendanso ndi nyengo; mwadzidzidzi iye anali kachiwiri kusunga theka la khumi madeti tsiku ndi theka la dazeni amuna, ndi kugona tulo m'bandakucha ndi mikanda ndi chiffon wa madzulo- diresi wosokonezeka pakati kufa orchid pansi pambali pa bedi lake. Ndipo nthawi zonse chinachake mkati mwake chinali kulira kufuna chisankho. Anafuna kuti moyo wake uunjidwe tsopano, nthaŵi yomweyo—ndipo chosankhacho chiyenera kupangidwa ndi mphamvu inayake—yachikondi, yandalama, yothandiza mosakaikitsa—imene inali pafupi.

Mphamvu imeneyo idayamba pakati pa masika ndikufika kwa Tom Buchanan. Panali kudzikuza bwino pa umunthu wake ndi udindo wake, ndipo Daisy adakondwera. Mosakayikira panali kulimbana kwinakwake ndi mpumulo wina wake. Kalatayo inafika kwa Gatsby akadali ku Oxford.

Kunali mbandakucha tsopano pa Long Island ndipo tinayenda ndikutsegula mazenera ena onse pansi, ndikudzaza nyumbayo ndi nyali yotuwa, yotembenukira golide. Mthunzi wa mtengo unagwa mwadzidzidzi pa mamewo ndipo mbalame zamizimu zinayamba kuimba pakati pa masamba abuluu. Munali kuyenda pang'onopang'ono, kosangalatsa mumlengalenga, mulibe mphepo, ndikulonjeza tsiku lozizira komanso losangalatsa.

"Sindikuganiza kuti ankamukonda." Gatsby adatembenuka kuchokera pawindo ndikundiyang'ana movutikira. “Kumbukirani, maseŵera akale, iye anali wosangalala kwambiri masana ano.” Anamuuza zinthu zimenezo m’njira yomuchititsa mantha—zimene zinachititsa kuti ziwonekere ngati kuti ndinali wachabechabe wotchipa.

Anakhala pansi mokhumudwa.

"Zowonadi mwina adamukonda kwa mphindi imodzi yokha, pomwe adakwatirana -ndipo amandikondanso kuposa pamenepo, mukuwona?"

Mwadzidzidzi adatuluka ndi mawu achidwi.

“Mulimonse mmene zingakhalire,” iye anatero, “zinali zaumwini chabe.”

Kodi mungapange chiyani pa izi, kupatula kukayikira kulimba mtima kwake pankhaniyi komwe sikungayesedwe?

Anabwerako kuchokera ku France pamene Tom ndi Daisy anali adakali paulendo wawo waukwati, ndipo anayenda ulendo womvetsa chisoni koma wosakanika kupita ku Louisville pa malipiro ake omaliza a asilikali. Anakhala kumeneko sabata, akuyenda m'misewu momwe mapazi awo adayendera limodzi usiku wa November ndikuyang'ananso malo omwe adapitako m'galimoto yake yoyera. Monga momwe nyumba ya Daisy nthawi zonse inkawoneka ngati yodabwitsa komanso yogonana kuposa nyumba zina, momwemonso lingaliro lake la mzinda womwewo, ngakhale kuti anali atachokamo, linali lodzaza ndi kukongola konyowa.

Anachoka ali ndi maganizo akuti ngati akanafufuza kwambiri, mwina akanamupeza—kuti amusiya. Wophunzitsa masana - anali wopanda ndalama tsopano - anali wotentha. Anatuluka kupita ku khonde lotseguka ndikukhala pampando wopinda, ndipo siteshoniyo idachoka ndipo kumbuyo kwa nyumba zosadziwika bwino kunasuntha. Kenako kupita kuminda yamasika, komwe trolley yachikasu idawathamangitsa kwa mphindi imodzi ndi anthu momwemo omwe mwina adawonapo matsenga otuwa a nkhope yake mumsewu wamba.

Njirayo inali yokhotakhota ndipo tsopano inali kupita kutali ndi dzuwa, lomwe, pamene linkaloŵa pansi, linkawoneka ngati likufalikira mokondwera pa mzinda womwe unali kutha kumene iye anapumako. Iye anatambasula dzanja lake mokhumudwa ngati kuti akungomulanda kamphepo kaye, kuti apulumutse kachidutswa komwe anamupangirako kuti akomere. Koma zonse zinkapita mofulumira kwambiri tsopano chifukwa cha maso ake ochita chibwibwi ndipo ankadziwa kuti wataya gawo lakelo, labwino kwambiri komanso labwino kwambiri kwamuyaya.

Nthawi inali 9 koloko titamaliza kudya chakudya cham'mawa ndikutuluka pakhonde. Usiku unali utasintha kwambiri nyengo ndipo kunali kununkhira kwa autumn mumlengalenga. Wolima dimba, womaliza mwa antchito akale a Gatsby, adafika pamapazi a masitepe.

"Ndikhetsa dziwe lero, Bambo Gatsby. Masamba ayamba kugwa posachedwa, ndiyeno nthawi zonse pamakhala vuto ndi mapaipi."

“Musachite lero,” Gatsby anayankha. Ananditembenukira mondipepesa. "Mukudziwa, masewera akale, sindinagwiritsepo ntchito dziwe limenelo chilimwe chonse?"

Ndinayang'ana wotchi yanga ndikuimirira.

"Mphindi khumi ndi ziwiri kupita ku sitima yanga."

Sindinafune kupita mumzinda. Sindinali woyenerera kugwira ntchito bwino, koma zinali zochulukirapo - sindinkafuna kuchoka ku Gatsby. Ndinaphonya sitimayo, kenako ina, ndisananyamuke.

“Ndikuyitana,” ndinatero pomalizira pake.

"Chitani, masewera akale."

“Ndikuyimbirani masana.”

Tinayenda pang'onopang'ono kutsika masitepe.

"Ndikuganiza kuti Daisy adzayimbanso." Anandiyang'ana mwankhawa, ngati akuyembekeza kuti nditsimikizira izi.

"Ndikuganiza choncho."

"Chabwino, chabwino."

Tinagwirana chanza ndipo ndinanyamuka. Nditangotsala pang'ono kufika pa hedge ndinakumbukira chinachake ndikutembenuka.

“Ndi khamu lowola,” ndinafuula pa kapinga. "Ndiwe wofunika kuti gulu lonse lipangidwe pamodzi."

Ndakhala wokondwa kuti ndinanena zimenezo. Uku kunali kuyamikira kokha komwe ndinamupatsa, chifukwa ndinamukana kuyambira pachiyambi mpaka kumapeto. Poyamba anagwedeza mutu mwaulemu, kenaka nkhope yake inasanduka kumwetulira kowala ndi komvetsetsa, ngati kuti takhala tikusangalatsidwa ndi mfundo imeneyi nthawi zonse. Chinsanza chake chapinki chowoneka bwino cha suti chidapanga utoto wonyezimira motsutsana ndi masitepe oyera, ndipo ndinaganiza za usiku womwe ndidabwera koyamba kunyumba ya makolo ake, miyezi itatu yapitayo. Udzu ndi galimoto zinali zitadzaza ndi nkhope za anthu omwe ankangoganizira zachinyengo chake - ndipo iye anaima pamasitepewo, kubisa loto lake losavunda, pamene anali kuwatsazikana.

Ndinamuthokoza chifukwa cha kuchereza kwake. Nthaŵi zonse tinali kumuthokoza chifukwa cha zimenezo—ine ndi enawo.

“Chabwino,” ndinamuyitana. "Ndadya chakudya cham'mawa, Gatsby."

Kumzindawu, ndidayesa kwakanthawi kuti ndilembe zolembazo pazambiri zosawerengeka, kenako ndidagona pampando wanga wozungulira. Kutatsala pang'ono madzulo foni idandidzutsa, ndipo ndidayamba thukuta likutuluka pachipumi. Anali wophika mkate wa Yorodano; nthawi zambiri amandiimbira foni nthawi imeneyi chifukwa kusatsimikizika kwa kayendedwe kake pakati pa mahotela ndi makalabu ndi nyumba zapagulu zimamupangitsa kuti azivutika kupeza mwanjira ina iliyonse. Nthawi zambiri mawu ake amabwera pawaya ngati chinthu chatsopano komanso chozizira, ngati kuti divot yochokera kumalo obiriwira a gofu adabwera akuyenda pawindo laofesi, koma m'mawa uno zidawoneka zowuma komanso zowuma.

“Ndachoka kunyumba kwa Daisy,” iye anatero. "Ndili ku Hempstead, ndipo ndikupita ku Southampton madzulo ano."

Mwinamwake kunali kwanzeru kuchoka m’nyumba ya Daisy, koma mchitidwewo unandikwiyitsa, ndipo mawu ake otsatirawo anandipangitsa kukhala wosasunthika.

“Simunandikomere mtima kwambiri usiku watha.”

"Zikadakhala bwanji pamenepo?"

Khalani chete kwakanthawi. Kenako:

"Komabe, ndikufuna kukuwona."

“Inenso ndikufuna kukuwonani.”

"Tiyerekeze kuti sindipita ku Southampton, ndikubwera mtawuni madzulo ano?"

“Ayi—sindikuganiza madzulo ano.”

"Chabwino."

“Sizingatheke madzulo ano. Zosiyanasiyana—”

Tinacheza choncho kwa nthawi ndithu, kenako mwadzidzidzi sitikulankhulanso. Sindikudziwa kuti ndi ndani mwa ife amene adapachika ndikudina kwambiri, koma ndikudziwa kuti sindimasamala. Sindikadacheza naye patebulo tsiku limenelo ngati sindikadayankhulananso naye mdziko muno.

Ndinaimbira nyumba ya Gatsby patapita mphindi zingapo, koma mzerewu unali wotanganidwa. Ndinayesa kanayi; pamapeto pake wapakati wokwiyitsidwa adandiuza kuti waya amatsegulidwa mtunda wautali kuchokera ku Detroit. Ndikutenga ndandanda yanga, ndidakokera bwalo laling'ono kuzungulira sitima yamakumi asanu ndi atatu. Kenako ndinatsamira pampando wanga n’kumayesa kuganiza. Nthawi inali masana basi.

Pamene ndinadutsa milu ya phulusa ya sitima m’maŵa umenewo ndinawoloka dala kutsidya lina la galimotoyo. Ndinkaganiza kuti pakhala gulu la anthu odziwa zambiri kuzungulira kumeneko tsiku lonse ndi anyamata ang'onoang'ono omwe akufunafuna madontho amdima pafumbi, ndipo munthu wina wonyansa akuwuza mobwerezabwereza zomwe zinachitika, mpaka zinakhala zenizeni kwa iye ndipo sakanatha kuzidziwanso, ndipo kupambana komvetsa chisoni kwa Myrtle Wilson kunaiwalika. Tsopano ndikufuna kubwereranso pang'ono ndi kunena zomwe zinachitika ku garaja titachoka kumeneko usiku watha.

Zinali zovuta kuti apeze mlongoyo, Catherine. Ayenera kuti anaphwanya lamulo lake loletsa kumwa usiku umenewo, chifukwa atafika anali wopusa ndi zakumwa ndipo sankamvetsa kuti ambulansi yapita kale ku Flushing. Atamutsimikizira zimenezi, nthawi yomweyo anakomoka, ngati kuti n’zosatheka kupirira. Winawake, wachifundo kapena wachidwi, adamutenga m'galimoto yake ndikumuyendetsa pambuyo pa thupi la mlongo wake.

Mpaka pakati pausiku khamu losintha linafika kutsogolo kwa garaja, pamene George Wilson ankadzigwedeza uku ndi uku pa sofa mkati. Kwa kanthawi chitseko cha ofesiyo chinali chotsegula, ndipo aliyense amene analowa m’galajamo anayang’anamo mosalephera. Pomaliza wina anati zinali zamanyazi, ndipo anatseka chitseko. Michaelis ndi amuna ena angapo anali naye; poyamba, amuna anayi kapena asanu, kenako amuna awiri kapena atatu. Pambuyo pake Michaelis adafunsa mlendo womaliza kuti adikire pamenepo mphindi khumi ndi zisanu, pomwe adabwerera kwawo ndikukapanga khofi. Pambuyo pake, anakhala kumeneko yekha ndi Wilson mpaka mbandakucha.

Cha m’ma 3 koloko khalidwe la kung’ung’udza kwa Wilson linasintha—anakhala chete n’kuyamba kukamba za galimoto yachikasu ija. Adalengeza kuti ali ndi njira yodziwira kuti galimoto yachikasuyo ndi yandani, ndipo adalankhula momveka bwino kuti miyezi ingapo yapitayo mkazi wake adabwera kuchokera mumzindawu ali ndi mabala amaso komanso mphuno yotupa.

Koma atamva zimenezi ananjenjemera n’kuyamba kulira kuti: “O Mulungu wanga! kachiwiri m’mawu ake obuula. Michaelis adayesa mopusa kuti amusokoneze.

"Kodi mwakhala m'banja nthawi yayitali bwanji, George, bwerani, yesetsani kukhala chete ndikuyankha funso langa, mwakhala m'banja nthawi yayitali bwanji?"

"Zaka khumi ndi ziwiri."

George, khala chete, ndakufunsani funso, kodi munakhalapo ndi ana?

Zikumbu zolimba za bulauni zimangokhalira kugundana ndi kuwala kopanda kuwalako, ndipo nthawi zonse Michaelis akamva galimoto ikuyenda mumsewu panja pake amamveka ngati galimoto yomwe siinayime maola angapo m'mbuyomo. Sanakonde kuloŵa m’galaja, chifukwa benchi yantchitoyo inali yothimbirira pamene mtembowo unagonekedwa, chotero anayendayenda movutikira mozungulira ofesiyo—anadziŵa kalikonse m’menemo m’bandakucha—ndipo nthaŵi ndi nthaŵi ankakhala pafupi ndi Wilson kuyesera kuti asakhale chete.

"Kodi uli ndi tchalitchi chomwe umapitako nthawi zina, George? Mwina ngakhale sunakhaleko kwa nthawi yayitali? Mwina ndingathe kuyitana wansembe kuti abwere kudzalankhula nawe, mwaona?"

“Usakhale wa aliyense.”

"Uyenera kukhala ndi tchalitchi, George, nthawi ngati izi. Uyenera kuti unapita ku tchalitchi kamodzi. Kodi sunakwatire kutchalitchi? Tamvera, George, mvetsera kwa ine. Kodi sunakwatire kutchalitchi?"

"Izi zinali kale kwambiri."

Khama la kuyankha linasokoneza kamvekedwe kake kakugwedezeka kwake - kwakanthawi adakhala chete. Kenako mawonekedwe omwewo odziwa theka, ododoma adabwereranso m'maso mwake atazilala.

“Yang’anani mu kabati mmenemo,” iye anatero, akuloza pa desiki.

"Dwalo liti?"

"Dwalo ija-imeneyo."

Michaelis anatsegula kabati yomwe inali pafupi ndi dzanja lake. Munalibe kalikonse m’menemo koma kachingwe kakang’ono, kodula kagalu, kopangidwa ndi chikopa ndi siliva wolukidwa. Zikuoneka kuti zinali zatsopano.

"Izi?" Adafunsa atayimilira.

Wilson adayang'ana ndikugwedeza mutu.

Ndinaipeza dzulo madzulo. Anayesetsa kundiuza, koma ndinadziwa kuti inali yosangalatsa.
