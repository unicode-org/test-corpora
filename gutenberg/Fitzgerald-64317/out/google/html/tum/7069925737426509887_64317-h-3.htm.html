Nkhwenera kuti nkhajipulika kuti nili wakuzizika comene pa nyengo iyo, cifukwa nkhaghanaghanangapo kanthu yayi kupatulapo ungweru wa suti yake ya pinki pasi pa mwezi.

"Kasi ukuchita vichi?" Nkhafumba.

“Kwimilira waka pano, maseŵero ghakale.”

Mwa nthowa yinyake, nchito iyi yikawonekanga nga ni nchito yakuseluska. Pakuti vyose ivyo nkhamanyanga vikaŵa kuti wazamuba nyumba mu nyengo yichoko waka; Ningazizwa yayi kuwona visku vyakofya, visku vya “ŵanthu ŵa Wolfshiem,” kumanyuma kwake mu vimiti vya mdima.

“Kasi mukawona suzgo lililose pa msewu?” wakafumba pamanyuma pa miniti yimoza.

"Enya."

“Kasi wakakomeka?”

"Nkhaghanaghananga nthena; nkhaphalira Daisy kuti nkhaghanaghananga nthena. Ntchiwemi kuti vyose viŵepo nyengo yimoza. Wakazizipizga makora chomene."

Wakayowoyanga nga kuti ivyo Daisy wakachita ndivyo vikaŵa vyakuzirwa.

"Nkhafika ku West Egg pa msewu wa pambali," wakalutilira kuyowoya, "ndipo nkhaleka galimoto mu garaji yane. Nkhughanaghana kuti palije uyo wakatiwona, kweni nadi nkhumanya makora yayi."

Nkhamutemwanga yayi pa nyengo iyi mwakuti nkhawona kuti nchakwenelera yayi kumuphalira kuti wakubudiska.

“Kasi mwanakazi uyu wakaŵa njani?” wakafumba.

"Zina lake likaŵa Wilson. Mfumu wake ndiyo wali na garaji. Kasi vikachitika wuli?"

“Kweni, nkhayezga kugwedezga gudumu—” Wakadumura, ndipo mwamabuci nkhaghanaghana unenesko.

“Kasi Daisy ndiyo wakayendeskanga galimoto?”

"Enya," iyo wakayowoya pamanyuma pa nyengo yichoko waka, "kweni nadi nizamuyowoya kuti nkhaŵa. Mwawona, apo tikafumanga ku New York wakaŵa na wofi chomene ndipo wakaghanaghananga kuti vingapangiska kuti waŵe wakukhora kwendeska galimoto—ndipo mwanakazi uyu wakachimbilira kwa ise apo tikajumphanga galimoto iyo yikizanga mu nthowa yinyake. Vyose vikachitika mu miniti yimoza, kweni vikawoneka kwa ine kuti wakakhumbanga kuyowoya dankha. wakapatukako kwa mwanakazi uyu na kuluta ku galimoto yinyake, ndipo pamanyuma wakaleka kuŵa na nkhongono ndipo wakawelera nyuma Kachiŵiri apo woko lane likafika pa gudumu nkhapulika kukhwaskika—likwenera kuti likamukoma nyengo yeneyiyo.”

“Icho chikamupatura—”

“Unganiphaliranga yayi, maseŵero ghakale.” Wakakhuŵara. "Kweni—Daisy wakayiponyapo. Nkhayezga kumupangiska kuti waleke, kweni wakatondeka, ntheura nkhakoka bureki wa mwaluŵiro. Pamanyuma wakawa mu chiŵegha chane ndipo nkhalutilira kwendeska galimoto.

“Wazamuŵa makora machero,” wakayowoya nthena sonosono apa. "Nilindilirenge waka pano na kuwona usange wangayezga kumusuzga chifukwa cha chinthu chiheni ichi muhanya uno. Wajijara mu chipinda chake, ndipo usange wangayezga nkhaza yiliyose wazamuzimya magesi na kuŵikaso."

Nkhati: “Wamukhwaskenge yayi.” “Wakughanaghana vya iyo yayi.”

"Usiku wose, usange nchakukhumbikwa. Nanga vili nthena, m'paka wose ŵagonenge."

Nkhaŵa na maghanoghano ghaphya. Tiyelezgere kuti Tom wakamanya kuti Daisy ndiyo wakayendeskanga galimoto. Wangaghanaghana kuti wakawona kukolerana mu fundo iyi—wangaghanaghana chilichose. Nkhalaŵiska pa nyumba; pakaŵa mawindo ghaŵiri panji ghatatu ghakuwala pasi ndiposo ungweru wa pinki uwo ukaŵa mu chipinda cha Daisy pasi.

Nkhati: “Imwe mulindilirani pano.” “Niwone usange pali chimanyikwiro chakuti pali chiwawa.”

Nkhaweleraso pa mphaka ya utheka, nkhajumpha mu malibwe ghakukhoma mwakuzika, ndipo nkhakwera pa masitepu gha pa veranda. Makatani gha mu chipinda chakuchezgeramo ghakaŵa ghakujulika, ndipo nkhawona kuti mu chipinda mukaŵavya munthu. Pakujumpha pa khonde apo tikarya chakurya usiku uwo wa Juni myezi yitatu pambere nindafike, nkhafika pa ungweru uchoko uwo nkhaghanaghananga kuti ni windo la chipinda cha vyakurya. Ŵachibulumutira ŵakakoleka, kweni nkhasanga mphanji pa sill.

Daisy na Tom ŵakakhalanga mwakulaŵiskana pa thebulu la ku khichini, ndipo pakati pawo pakaŵa mbale ya nkhuku yakuzizima yakukazinga, na mabotolo ghaŵiri gha ale. Wakayowoyanga mwakupwelelera kuseri kwa thebulu kwa iyo, ndipo mu kujipeleka kwake woko lake likawira pa na kubenekelera lakhe. Nyengo zinyake wakamulaŵiskanga na kukhozga mutu wake kuti wazomerezge.

Ŵakaŵa ŵakukondwa yayi, ndipo palije na yumoza wa iwo uyo wakakhwaska nkhuku panji moŵa—ndipo nangauli vikaŵa nthena, nawo ŵakaŵa ŵambura kukondwa yayi. Pakaŵa mphepo yambura kukayikiska ya kutemwana kwacilengiwa pa cithuzithuzi ici, ndipo waliyose wangayowoya kuti ŵakapangana pamoza.

Apo nkhafumanga pa khonde nkhapulika taxi yane yikupulika nthowa yake mu msewu wa mdima wakuya ku nyumba. Gatsby wakalindiliranga apo nkhamuleka mu galimoto.

“Kasi vyose vili chete pachanya apo?” wakafumba mwakukhwaskika.

“Enya, vyose vyakhala chete.” Nkhagongowa. “Chiŵe chiwemi kuti uwelere ku nyumba na kugona.”

Wakayuyura mutu wake.

"Nkhukhumba kulindilira pano mpaka Daisy wagone. Ugone makola, maseŵero ghakale."

Wakaŵika mawoko ghake mu mathumba gha khothi lake na kuwelera mwakunweka ku kuwoneseska kwake kwa nyumba, nga ni para kuŵapo kwane kukananga utuŵa wa kulindilira. Ntheura nkhafumapo na kumuleka kuti wimilire penepara mu ungweru wa mwezi—wakuwonelera kanthu yayi.
