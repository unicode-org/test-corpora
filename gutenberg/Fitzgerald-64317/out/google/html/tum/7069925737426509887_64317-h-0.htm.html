Ine

Apo nkhaŵa muwukirano ndiposo apo nkhaŵa wakusuzgika comene, adada ŵakanipa ulongozgi uwo nkhuwusintha mu maghanoghano ghane kufuma waka pa nyengo iyo.

Wakaniphalila kuti: “Nyengo yiliyose para ukukhumba kususka munthu waliyose, kumbuka waka kuti ŵanthu wose ŵa mu charu ichi ŵandaŵe na vitumbiko ivyo iwe uli navyo.”

Wakayowoyapo yayi, kweni nyengo zose tikudumbiskana mwakupambana na ŵanyake mu nthowa yakujibisa, ndipo nkhapulikiska kuti wakang’anamuranga vinandi kuluska apa. Pa cifukwa ici, nkhutemwa kusunga viyezgo vyose, cizgoŵi ico cajura mikhaliro yinandi yakukhumba kumanya vinandi kwa ine ndiposo cikanipangiska kuŵa wakukhwaskika na viwawa vya ŵasilikari ŵacoko waka yayi. Maghanoghano ghambura kumanyikwa ghakumanya mwaluŵiro na kujibatika ku mtundu uwu para ghakuwoneka mu munthu wakumanyikwa, ndipo ntheura vikacitika kuti ku koleji ŵakanisuska mwambura kwenelera kuti ndine wandale, cifukwa nkhamanyanga makora citima cakubisika ca ŵanalume ŵambura kumanyikwa, ŵambura kumanyikwa. Vinandi vya vigomezgo vikaŵa vyambura kupenjeka—kanandi nkhacitanga nga nkhugona, nkhutangwanika, panji nkhuŵa wakujiyuyura apo nkhumanya na cimanyikwiro cinyake cambura kukayikiska kuti civumbuzi ca pafupi cikusunkhunyika pafupi; pakuti vivumbuzi vyakukhwaskana na ŵanyamata, panji mazgu agho ŵakuviyowoyera, kanandi vikuŵa vyakwiba ndipo vikunangika na kuponderezgeka kwakuwonekerathu. Kusunga viyezgo ni nkhani ya cigomezgo cambura kumara. Ndichali na wofi pachoko waka kuti ningasoŵa chinthu chinyake usange ningaluwa kuti, nga umo adada ŵakayowoyera mwakujikuzga, ndipo nkhuwerezgapo mwakujikuzga, kumanya mikhaliro yiwemi yakuzirwa kukugaŵika mwakupambana para munthu wababika.

Ndipo, pamanyuma pa kujilumba mu nthowa iyi ya kulekelera kwane, nkhufika pa kuzomerezga kuti kuli na mphaka. Nkharo yingakhazikika pa libwe lakukhora panji pa matope ghakunyeŵa, kweni para pajumpha nyengo yinyake nkhuŵikako mahara yayi kuti yikukhazikika pa vichi. Apo nkhaweleranga kufuma ku East mu autumn wajumpha nkhajipulika kuti nkhukhumba kuti caru ciŵe mu yunifomu ndiposo pa mtundu wa kuŵikapo mtima pa nkharo kwamuyirayira; Nkhakhumbanga yayi maulendo ghanyake gha viwawa na mwaŵi wa kuwona mtima wa munthu. Gatsby pera, munthu uyo wakupeleka zina lake ku buku ili, ndiyo wakafwatulika ku ivyo nkhacita—Gatsby, uyo wakayimira cilicose ico nkhucinyoza kwambura kukhwaskika. Usange wunthu ni mndandanda wambura kugaŵikana wa vimanyikwiro vyakupambana, mbwenu pakaŵa cinthu cinyake cakutowa pa iyo, cinyake cakukhozga comene pa malayizgo gha umoyo, nga ni para wakukhwaskana na cimoza mwa makina ghakusuzga agho ghakulembeska vindindindi ivyo vikucitika pa mtunda wa mamailosi vikwi khumi. Kuzgora uku kukaŵavya kanthu na kukhwaskika kwakukhozga uko kukucindikika pasi pa zina lakuti “mkhaliro wa kulenga”—cikaŵa chawanangwa capadera ca cigomezgo, kunozgekera kwa citemwa nga ni uko nindasangepo mu munthu munyake ndiposo uko nkhuwoneka kuti nizamusangaso yayi. Yayi—Gatsby wakafumapo makora paumaliro; ndivyo vikakoma Gatsby, fuvu lakufipirwa wuli ilo likayandama mu maloto ghake ilo likajara kwa kanyengo chidwi chane pa chitima chakuwuska nthumbo na chimwemwe cha ŵanthu cha nyengo yichoko waka.

Banja lane laŵa ŵanthu ŵakumanyikwa, ŵakusambazga mu msumba uwu wa ku Middle Western kwa miwiro yitatu. Ŵa Carraways ni chinthu chinyake cha fuko, ndipo tili na mwambo wakuti tikufuma ku Dukes of Buccleuch, kweni uyo wakambiska mzere wane wakaŵa munung’una wa ŵasekuru ŵane, uyo wakiza kuno mu makumi ghankhondi na yumoza, wakatuma munthu uyo wakanjira m’malo mwake ku Nkhondo Yachiŵeniŵeni, ndipo wakamba bizinesi ya kuguliska vinthu vyakukhozga pa galimoto iyo adada ŵane ŵakaŵa nayo.

Nkhaŵa kuti nindamuwonepo sekuru uyu, kweni nkhwenera kuwoneka nga ndiyo—kuyowoya mwapadera za cithuzithuzi cakuphikika comene ico cikukhala mu ofesi ya adada. Nkhamalizga masambiro ghane ku New Haven mu 1915, pakajumpha waka vilimika 150 kufuma apo adada ŵakafwira, ndipo pamanyuma pa nyengo yicoko waka nkhacitako nkhondo iyo yikacedwa kusamuka kwa Ŵateutonic iyo yikamanyikwanga kuti Nkhondo Yikuru. Nkhakondwa chomene na nkhondo yakususkana nayo mwakuti nkhaweleranga kwambura mtende. Mmalo mwa kuŵa malo ghakufunda gha caru cose, sono Middle West yikawonekanga nga ni mphepete yakuvunda ya cilengiwa cose—ntheura nkhaghanaghana kuti nilutenge ku East na kusambira bizinesi ya bond. Waliyose uyo nkhamanyanga wakaŵa mu bizinesi ya bond, ntheura nkhaghanaghananga kuti yingawovwira mwanalume munyake wambura kutora. Ŵasekuru ŵane wose ŵakadumbiskana nga ŵakunisankhira sukulu yakunozgekera, ndipo paumaliro ŵakati, “Cifukwa wuli—e-es,” na nkhope zakuŵinya comene, zakugongowa. Adada ŵakazomera kunipa ndalama kwa chaka chimoza, ndipo pamanyuma pa kuchedwa kwakupambanapambana nkhiza ku East, kwamuyirayira, nkhaghanaghananga, mu nyengo ya vula ya makumi ghaŵiri na ghaŵiri.

Chinthu chakovwira chomene chikaŵa chakuti nisange vipinda mu msumba, kweni nyengo iyi yikaŵa yakufunda, ndipo nkhafumapo waka mu charu icho mukaŵa utheka ukuru na makuni ghawemi, ntheura apo munyamata munyake ku ofesi wakayowoya kuti tikhale pamoza mu tawuni iyo ŵanthu ŵakuluta ku ntchito, vikawonekanga nga ni fundo yiwemi chomene. Wakasanga nyumba, nyumba ya makatoni iyo yikatimbanizgika na nyengo pa 80 pa mwezi, kweni pa mphindi yaumaliro kampani yikamuphalira kuti walute ku Washington, ndipo ine nkhaluta ku charu ichi nekha. Nkhaŵa na galu—pafupifupi nkhaŵa nayo kwa mazuŵa ghacoko waka m’paka apo wakacimbira—na Dodge mulara na mwanakazi wa ku Finland, uyo wakanozga bedi lane na kuphika cakurya ca mulenji na kujibucizga vinjeru vya ku Finland pa sitovu ya magesi.

Cikaŵa cambura ŵanthu kwa zuŵa limoza panji ghaŵiri m’paka apo mulenji unyake mwanalume munyake, uyo wakafika sonosono apa kuluska ine, wakaniyimika pa msewu.

“Kasi mukufika wuli ku muzi wa West Egg?” wakafumba kwambura wovwiri.

Nkhamuphalira. Ndipo apo nkhalutanga panthazi nkhaŵangaso nekha yayi. Nkhaŵa wakulongozga, wakupenja nthowa, wakukhazikika pakwamba. Wakaŵa kuti wanipa mwambura kughanaghanira wanangwa wa ŵazengezgani.

Ndipo ntheura na ungweru wa dazi na kuphulika kukuru kwa mahamba ghakumera pa makuni, nga umo vinthu vikukulira mu mafilimu ghaluŵiro, nkhaŵa na cigomezgo cakumanyikwa ico cakuti umoyo ukambaso na nyengo ya chilimwe.

Pakaŵa vinandi comene vyakuti ŵaŵazge, cifukwa cimoza, ndiposo umoyo uwemi comene uwo ukaŵa kuti ukwenera kukoleka pasi kufuma mu mlengalenga wa ŵawukirano wakupuma. Nkhagura mabuku khumi na ghaŵiri ghakukhwaskana na banki na ngongoli na vyakuŵikamo ndalama, ndipo ghakimilira pa shelufu yane mu mtundu wakufipirwa na wa golide nga ni ndalama ziphya kufuma ku mint, ghakulayizga kuti ghazamuvumbura vinthu vyakubisika vyakuwala ivyo Midas na Morgan na Maecenas pera ndiwo ŵakamanyanga. Ndipo nkhaŵa na chilato chikuru cha kuŵazga mabuku ghanyake ghanandi padera pa. Nkhaŵa m’malo mwake wakusambira mabuku ku koleji—caka cinyake nkhalemba mndandanda wa nkhani zakucindikika comene ndiposo zakuwonekerathu zakulembeka mu nyuzipepara ya Yale News—ndipo sono nkhaŵa kuti nizamuwezgerapo vinthu vyose vyanthena ivi mu umoyo wane na kuŵaso uyo wali na mphaka comene pa ŵamanyi wose, “munthu wakuzingilizgika makora.” Ici nchipigramu pera yayi—umoyo ukuwoneka makora comene kufuma pa windo limoza, pakuti vyose.

Nkhaŵa na mwaŵi wakuti nkhachitenge lendi nyumba mu chigaŵa chinyake cha ŵanthu ŵambura kumanyikwa ku North America. Cikaŵa pa cirwa cicoko ca viwawa ico cikufika kumafumiro gha dazi kwa New York—ndipo apo pali, pakati pa vinthu vinyake vyakuzizwiska vya cilengiwa, vigaŵa viŵiri vyambura kumanyikwa vya caru. Pa mtunda wa mamailosi 20 kufuma ku msumba uwu, mazira ghakurughakuru ghaŵiri, ghakuyana waka ndipo ghakupatulika waka na bay ya ntchindi, ghakufuma mu maji gha mchere agho ghakuŵeta chomene ku chigaŵa cha Kumanjiliro gha dazi, malo ghakuru ghakunyeŵa gha Long Island Sound. Iwo mba oval ŵakufikapo yayi—nga ni sumbi ilo lili mu nkhani ya Columbus, wose ŵakuphyoka pa umaliro wa kukhwaskana—kweni kufanana kwawo kwakuwoneka kukwenera kuŵa cintu cakuzizika nyengo zose ku viyuni vya gull ivyo vikuwuluka pacanya pa mtunda. Kwa ŵambura mapiko chinthu chakukondweska chomene nkhupambana kwawo mu chilichose kupatulapo kawonekero na kukura.

Nkhakhalanga ku West Egg, the—chabwino, chambura kumanyikwa chomene pa viŵiri ivi, nangauli ichi nchimanyikwiro chapacanya comene kurongora mphambano yakuzizika ndiposo yakusuzga yayi pakati pawo. Nyumba yane yikaŵa pa mphepete comene ya dzira, mayadi makumi ghankhondi pera kufuma ku Sound, ndipo yikafinyikizgika pakati pa malo ghaŵiri ghakuru agho ghakaŵanga na lendi kwa vikwi khumi na viŵiri panji khumi na vinkhondi pa nyengo. Icho chikaŵa ku woko lane lamalyero chikaŵa chinthu chikuru chomene pa mlingo uliwose—chikaŵa chiyelezgero cha unenesko cha Hôtel de Ville yinyake ku Normandy, na chigongwe ku chigaŵa chimoza, chikaŵa chiphya pasi pa ndevu zakuwonda za ivy yambura kuphika, na chiziŵa cha kusambiramo cha marble, na maekala ghakujumpha makumi ghanayi gha utheka na munda. Iyi yikaŵa nyumba yikuru ya Gatsby. Panji, m’malo mwake, pakuti nkhaŵamanyanga yayi Mr. Gatsby, yikaŵa nyumba yikuru iyo yikakhalanga munthu munyake wa zina ili. Nyumba yane yikaŵa yakupweteka maso, kweni yikaŵa yakupweteka maso yichoko waka, ndipo yikaŵa kuti yalekeskeka, ntheura nkhaŵa na cithuzithuzi ca maji, cithuzithuzi ca cigaŵa cinyake ca utheka wa mzengezgani wane, ndiposo pafupi mwakusanguluska kwa ŵamamiliyoni—vyose ivi vikaguliskika pa madola makumi ghankhondi na ghatatu pa mwezi.

Ku mphepete kwa courtesy bay nyumba za mafumu zituŵa za mafashoni za East Egg zikaŵanga zakunyezimira pafupi na maji, ndipo mdauko wa chilimwe ukuyamba nadi pa zuŵa ilo nkhakweranga galimoto kuluta ku malo agha kuti nkharye chakurya cha mise na mbumba ya Tom Buchanan. Daisy wakaŵa msuweni wane wachiŵiri apo ŵakamufumiska, ndipo nkhamumanyanga Tom ku koleji. Ndipo nkhondo yikati yamara waka nkhakhala nawo mazuŵa ghaŵiri ku Chicago.

Mufumu wake, pakati pa vinthu vyakupambanapambana ivyo wakacita, wakaŵa yumoza wa ŵantu ŵankhongono comene awo ŵakaseŵerangapo mpira ku New Haven—munthu wa caru cose mu nthowa yinyake, yumoza wa ŵanalume awo ŵakufika pa msinkhu wakucepa comene pa vyaka makumi ghaŵiri na cimoza mwakuti vyose ivyo vikucitika pamanyuma pake vikuŵa vyakunozga comene. Banja lake likaŵa lakusambazga comene—nanga ni pa koleji wanangwa wake na ndalama ukaŵa wakususkika—kweni sono wakafumako ku Chicago na kwiza ku Mafumiro gha dazi mu nthowa iyo yikamutolerani mzimu: mwaciyerezgero, wakakhizga chingwe ca mahaci gha polo kufuma ku Lake Forest. Cikaŵa cakusuzga kumanya kuti munthu wa mu m’badwo wane wakaŵa musambazi comene mwakuti wangacita nthena.

Chifukwa icho ŵakiza ku East nkhumanya yayi. Ŵakakhala chaka chimoza ku France kwambura chifukwa chinyake, ndipo pamanyuma ŵakasunkhunyikanga kuno na kuno kwambura mtende kulikose uko ŵanthu ŵakaseŵeranga polo ndipo ŵakaŵa ŵasambazi pamoza. Ici cikaŵa cakucitika cambura kumara, Daisy wakayowoya pa foni, kweni nkhagomezga yayi—nkhawonanga yayi mu mtima wa Daisy, kweni nkhawona kuti Tom wazamulutilira kupenja kwamuyirayira, mwakukhuŵara pachoko, chifukwa cha chivulupi chakuziziswa cha maseŵero ghanyake gha mpira agho ghangaweleraso yayi.

Ndipo ntheura vikacitika kuti pa nyengo ya mphepo yakufunda mise nkhakwera galimoto kuluta ku East Egg kukawona ŵanyane ŵaŵiri ŵakale awo nkhaŵamanyanga yayi nanga nkhuŵamanyanga. Nyumba yawo yikaŵa yakutowa comene kuluska umo nkhaghanaghaniranga, yikaŵa nyumba yakukondweska ya mtundu wakufipa na wakutuŵa wa Ŵakoloni ŵa ku Georgia, ndipo yikalaŵiskanga pa bay. Utheka ukamba pa mtunda ndipo ukacimbilira ku mulyango wakunthazi kwa kota ya mailosi, ukajumphiranga pa mawoko gha zuŵa na kwendapo pa njerwa na minda yakugolera—paumaliro apo ukafika pa nyumba ukasunkhunyikanga pa mbali mu mpheska zakuwala nga ni para ukafuma ku nkhongono ya kucimbira kwake. Kunthazi kukaphyoka na mzere wa mawindo gha ku France, ghakaŵanga ghakuwala sono na golide wakuwoneka ndipo ghakaŵa ghakujulika comene ku mphepo yakufunda ya mise, ndipo Tom Buchanan mu vyakuvwara vya kukwera mahaci wakayimilira na malundi ghake ghakupambana pa khonde la kunthazi.

Wakasintha kufuma waka apo wakakhalanga ku New Haven. Sono wakaŵa munthu wakukhora wa sisi la utheka wa vyaka makumi ghatatu, na mulomo wakukhora comene ndiposo na nkharo yakujikuzga. Maso ghaŵiri ghakuwala ghakujikuzga ghakakhazikiska mazaza pa nkhope yake ndipo ghakamupa kawonekero kakuti nyengo zose wakujikama mwakucicizga panthazi. Nanga nkhuŵa na vyakuvwara vyake vyakukwera vyakuwoneka nga ni ŵanakazi vikabisa yayi nkhongono yikuru ya thupi ilo—wakawonekanga kuti wakuzuzga vingwe vyakunyezimira ivi m’paka apo wakakhozga chingwe cha pachanya, ndipo mukawonanga kuti minofu yikuru yikusinthasintha apo phewa lake likasunkhunyikanga pasi pa khothi lake lakuwonda. Wakaŵa thupi ilo likaŵa na nkhongono zikuru comene—thupi lankhaza.

Mazgu ghake ghakuyowoya, ghakukhozga ghakukhozga, ghakasazgirako pa kuwoneka kwakugaŵikana uko wakayowoyanga. Pakaŵa kukhwaska kwa kunyoza kwa ŵadada mu iyo, nanga nkhu ŵanthu awo wakaŵatemwanga—ndipo pakaŵa ŵanalume ku New Haven awo ŵakatinkhanga matumbo ghake.

Wakawonekanga kuti wakuyowoya kuti: “Sono, mungaghanaghana yayi kuti maghanoghano ghane pa nkhani izi ngaumaliro, cifukwa cakuti nili wankhongono comene ndiposo kuti nili mwanalume comene kuluska imwe.” Tikaŵa mu gulu limoza la ŵalara, ndipo nangauli tikaŵa ŵakutemwana yayi nyengo zose nkhaŵanga na maghanoghano ghakuti wakunizomerezga ndipo wakukhumba kuti nimutemwenge na kuŵa na citima cinyake ca nkhaza, cakususka.

Tikadumbiskana kwa maminiti ghachoko waka pa khonde ilo likaŵa na zuŵa.

“Nili na malo ghawemi kuno,” wakayowoya, maso ghake ghakaŵala kwambura kupumura.

Wakanizgoŵezga na woko limoza, ndipo wakasunkhunya woko lake lakutambasuka lakuphapha pafupi na vista ya kunthazi, kusazgapo mu kusefa kwake munda wa ku Italy uwo ukaŵa wakubisika, theka la ekala la maluwa gha rose ghakunyang’amira, ghakuŵinya, na boti la injini ilo likaŵa na mphuno yakukhora ilo likakhuŵazganga maji ghakufuma ku nyanja.

“Chikaŵa cha Demaine, munthu wa mafuta.” Wakanizgoŵezgaso, mwaulemu ndiposo mwamabuchibuchi. “Tizamunjira mukati.”

Tikajumpha mu korido yitali na kunjira mu malo ghakuwoneka makora gha rose, ndipo ghakaŵa ghakukhozgeka mwakukhora mu nyumba na mawindo gha ku France ku vigaŵa vyose viŵiri. Mawindo ghakaŵa ghakujulika ndipo ghakaŵa ghakunyezimira ghatuŵa pa utheka uphya uwo ukaŵa kuwaro uwo ukawonekanga kuti ukukura pachoko waka mu nyumba. Mphepo yikanjira mu cipinda, yikawuska makatani ku mphepete yimoza na kufumamo ku mphepete yinyake nga ni mbendera zakufipirwa, yikapotokora kucanya ku keke ya ukwati iyo yikaŵa na ciwuvi ca pa denga, ndipo pamanyuma yikakhuŵara pa kapeti wa mtundu wa vinyo, ndipo yikapanga mthunzi pa iyo nga umo mphepo yikucitira pa nyanja.

Chinthu chimoza pera icho chikaŵa kuti chikukhala waka mu chipinda ichi chikaŵa sofa yikuru chomene iyo ŵasungwana ŵaŵiri ŵakakwerangapo nga ni para ŵali pa baluni iyo yikakhozgeka. Wose ŵakavwara vyakuvwara vituŵa, ndipo madiresi ghawo ghakaŵa ghakuvunduka na kukhuŵara nga ni para ŵali kuwuskika waka mu nyumba pamanyuma pakuti ŵazungulira nyumba nyengo yichoko waka. Nkhwenera kuti nkhayimilira kwa kanyengo kachoko waka na kutegherezga ku chikwapu na kukhwaskika kwa makateni na kubuwura kwa chithuzithuzi icho chikaŵa pa mtenje. Pamanyuma pakaŵa chiwawa apo Tom Buchanan wakajara mawindo gha kumanyuma ndipo mphepo iyo yikakoleka yikafwa pafupi na chipinda, ndipo makatani na makhapeti na ŵasungwana ŵaŵiri ŵakabalukira pasi pachokopachoko.

Muchoko pa ŵaŵiri aŵa wakaŵa mulendo kwa ine. Wakaŵa wakutambasuka utali wose ku umaliro wa divan yake, wambura kusunkhunyika, ndipo na chibwano chake chikakwezgeka pachoko, nga wakulinganizga chinthu chinyake pa icho icho chingaŵa kuti chingawa. Usange wakaniwona kufuma ku ngodya ya maso ghake wakaniphalirapo yayi—nadi, nkhaŵa pafupi kuzizwa na kudandawura kuphepiska cifukwa cakuti nkhamutimbanizga mwa kunjira.

Msungwana munyake, Daisy, wakayezga kuwuka—wakajikama pachoko panthazi na mazgu ghakulongora kuti wakumupwelelera—pamanyuma wakaseka, kuseka kuchoko kwambura kupulikikwa, kwakukondweska, ndipo nane nkhaseka ndipo nkhiza panthazi mu chipinda.

“Ndine p-wakufwatulika na chimwemwe.”

Wakasekaso, nga ni para wakayowoya mazgu ghakusekeska comene, ndipo wakanikora pa woko kwa kanyengo, wakanilaŵiska ku nkhope, wakalayizga kuti palije munthu pa caru ico wakakhumbanga comene kumuwona.
