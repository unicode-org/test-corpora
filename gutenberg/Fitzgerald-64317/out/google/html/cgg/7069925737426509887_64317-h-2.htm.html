Omu mwanya gw’okutwara omuhanda mukye ogurikuraba aha Sound tukashuuma omu muhanda twataaha turikurabira aha kipande kihango. N’okuborooga okurikushemeza Daisy akakunda ekicweka eki nari eky’ekishushani ky’obukama ekirikureebana n’eiguru, akakunda emisiri, okunuuka kw’ebimuri ebirikwaka gye n’okunuuka kw’ebimuri bya hawthorn na plum n’okunuuka kwa zaabu erikutukura okw’okunnywegyera-aha-muryango. Kikaba kiri eky’okutangaaza kuhika aha madaara g’amabaare g’omuhendo okashanga hatariho kuhunga kw’ebijwaro ebirikwaka gye omunda n’aheeru y’orwigi, kandi okahurira hatariho iraka kureka amaraka g’ebinyonyi omu miti.

Kandi omunda, obu twabaire nituzengyerera omu bishengye by’ebyeshongoro bya Marie Antoinette n’omu Restoration Salons, nkahurira ngu hariho abagyenyi abasherekirwe enyima ya buri ntebe n’emeeza, ahansi y’ebiragiro by’okuhunama tutaine n’omwisyo okuhitsya obu twaheza kurabamu. Gatsby ku yaabaire naakinga orwigi rwa “Eibiikiro ry’ebitabo erya Merton College” nkaba nimbaasa kurahira ngu nkahurira omushaija w’amaisho g’ekihunyira yaahenda omu nsheko z’emizimu.

Tukaza ahaiguru, kurabira omu bishengye by’okuraaramu ebyabaire bijwaire obujwara bwa rose na lavender kandi ebirimu ebimuri bisya, kurabira omu bishengye by’okujwara n’eby’okukoreramu ebiziba, n’omu bishengye by’okunaabiramu ebiine ebinabiro ebiteirwe omu maizi—turikutaaha omu kishengye kimwe ahu omushaija owaabaire ajwaire ebijwaro by’okuraaramu ataine nshonga yaabaire naakora ebisasayizi by’ebihaha ahansi. Akaba ari Omushaija Klipspringer, “omushuma.” Nkaba mmureebire arikuzengyerera aha nyanja aine enjara omu kasheeshe ako. Aha muheru tukahika omu muzigo gwa Gatsby wenka, ekishengye ky’okuraaramu n’ekyombeko ky’okunaabiramu, n’ekishengye ky’okwegyeramu ekya Adamu, ahu twashutami twanywa egilaasi ya Chartreuse emwe ei yaabaire aiha omu kabada omu kisiika.

Akaba atakarekire kureeba Daisy n’omwe, kandi ninteekateeka ngu akagaruka yaateekateeka buri kimwe omu nju ye kurugiirira aha kipimo ky’okugarukamu ekyabaire nikiruga omu maisho ge agaabaire naakunda munonga. Obumwe n’obumwe, akaba areeba ebintu bye omu muringo gw’okushoberwa, nk’oku omu kubaho kwe okwabuzima kandi okurikutangaaza hatariho na kimwe aharibyo ekyabaire kitakibaho buzima. Obwire bumwe akaba ari haihi kugwa aha madaara.

Ekishengye kye ky’okuraaramu kikaba kiri kyorobi kukira ebindi byona—okwihaho ahu ekijwaro ky’okujwara kyabaire kishemeziibwe ekitanda ky’ekitanda ky’ezaabu eshemeire. Daisy akatwara oburashi n’okushemererwa, kandi yaashemeza eishokye rye, obwo Gatsby yaashutama yaateeka ekicuucu omu maisho ge yaatandika kusheka.

“N’ekintu ekirikushemeza munonga, omuzaano gwa ira,” akagira omu kushemererwa. “Tindikubaasa—Ku ndikugyezaho—”

Akaba yaahingwireho omu mahanga abiri arikureebeka kandi akaba naaza omu mahanga aga kashatu. Bwanyima y’okushoberwa kwe n’okushemererwa kwe okutaine nshonga akamara okwebuuza ahabw’okubaho kwe. Akaba amazire obwire buraingwa aijwire ekiteekateeko ekyo, akakiroota kuhitsya aha muheru, akategyereza aine ameino ge gateirweho, nk’oku turikugira, aha bwire obutarikuteekateekwaho obw’amaani. Hati, omu kugarukamu, akaba nairukira ahansi nk’eshaha erimu ebihuta ebirengire.

Okwegarukamu omu dakiika emwe akatwiguriraho ebishengye bibiri by’obugabe ebyabaire biine ebijwaro bye ebihango n’ebijwaro bye by’okujwara n’ebijwaro bye, n’esaati ze, ziteeraine nk’amatafaari omu bifunjo by’oburaingwa bwa ikumi n’ebiri.

"Nyine omushaija omuri Bungyereza orikungura emyenda. Nasindika aha bintu ebitoorainwe aha kutandika kwa buri sizoni, omu bwire bw'enjura n'omu bwire bw'okugwa."

Akaihayo omurundo gw’esaati yaatandika kuziteera, kimwe kimwe, omu maisho gaitu, eshaati z’ebijwaro bya kitaani n’ebijwaro bya siriki ebigumire n’ebijwaro bya flaneli ebirungi, ebyabaire bifeerwa emifuko yaabyo ku byabaire nibigwa kandi bikashweka emeeza omu nshoberwa y’erangi nyingi. Obu twabaire nitushemererwa akareeta ebindi kandi ekirundo ky’obugaiga ekigumire kikatunguuka ahaiguru—ebisaati ebiine emirongooti n’emizingo n’ebipande by’ebicupuri omu rangi ya coral na apple-green na lavender n’omucungwa ogutarikureebeka gye, ebiine ebishushani by’erangi ya bururu y’Abahinda. Ahonaaho, n’eiraka eririkuteganisa, Daisy akafukamira omutwe gwe omu nshaho kandi yaatandika kurira nk’omuyaga.

“N’eshaati nungi munonga,” akagira n’okurira, eiraka rye riri omu bifunjo ebigumire. “Nikimpa obusaasi ahabw’okugira ngu tinkareebaga esaati nk’ezo—ezirungi nk’ezo.”

Bwanyima y’enju, tukaba twine kureeba ekishaayi n’ekidiba ky’okwogyeramu, n’enyonyi y’amaizi, n’ebimuri by’omugati y’omushana—kwonka aheeru y’edirisa ya Gatsby enjura ekatandika kugaruka kugwa, n’ahabw’ekyo tukaba twemereire omu murongooti turikureeba aha itaka ry’ebicupuri erya Sound.

Gatsby akagira ati: “Kuri hatariho n’oruho tukaba nitubaasa kureeba eka yaawe aha rubaju orundi orw’enyanja.” “Butoosha nooba oine etaara ya kinyasi erikwotsya ekiro kyona aha muheru gw’omwanya gwawe gw’okushutamamu.”

Daisy akata omukono gwe omu mukono gwe ahonaaho, kwonka we akaba nareebeka nk’owaabaire aine omutima gw’ebi yaabaire yaaheza kugamba. Nikibaasa kuba ngu kikaba kyamuhikireho ngu obukuru bw’omushana ogwo hati bukaba buhweireho ebiro byona. Wagyeragyeranisa n’oburaingwa bwingi obwabaire bumutaaniise na Daisy bukaba nibushusha ngu buri haihi munonga nawe, haihi kumukwataho. Kikaba nikireebeka nk’enyonyoozi eri haihi n’okwezi. Hati kikaba kigaruka kiri etabaaza ya kinyaatsi aha mwaro. Okubara kwe kw’ebintu ebi yaabaire aine omu kuroga kukaba kukyeireho kimwe.

Nkatandika kugyendera omu kishengye, ninshwijuma ebintu bitari bimwe na bimwe ebitarikumanywa omu mwirima oguri ahagati. Ekishushani kihango ky’omugurusi omu bijwaro by’okuvuga emeeri kikanshemeza, kihanikire aha kisiika ahaiguru y’emeeza ye.

"Ogu n'oha?"

"Ekyo? Ogwo ni Mr. Dan Cody, omuzaano gwa ira."

Eiziina eryo rikaba nirihurirwa nk’erimanyirwe gye.

"Afiire hati. Akaba ari munywani wangye w'ahamara emyaka ehingwireho."

Hariho akashushani kakye ka Gatsby, nawe ajwaire emyenda y’okuvuga emeeri, aha biro—Gatsby omutwe gwe guteirwe enyima omu muringo gw’okuhakanisa—akakwatwa nk’oku yaabaire aine emyaka nka ikumi n’munaana.

Daisy akagira ati: “Ninkikunda munonga.” "Ekipompa! Tokangira ngu oine ekipompa—narishi emeeri."

Gatsby akagira ahonaaho ati: “Reeba eki.” “Hati hariho ebicweka bingi ebicwekire—ebirikukwata ahariiwe.”

Bakeemerera aha rubaju barikukishwijuma. Nkaba ninza kushaba kureeba amabaare ga rubi, esimu yaateera, Gatsby yakwata receiver.

“Eego... Haza, tindikubaasa kugamba hati... tindikubaasa kugamba hati, omuzaano gwa ira... nkagira ngu akatare kakye ... Aine kumanya eki akatare kakye kari... Haza, taine mugasho ahariitwe Detroit yaaba eri ekiteekateeko kye eky’akatare kakye...”

Akateera esimu.

“Ija hanu juba !” Daisy akarira aha dirisa.

Enjura ekaba ekiriho, kwonka omwirima gukaba gwataanisiibwe omu burengyerwa-izooba, kandi hakaba hariho ebicu by’ebicupuri eby’erangi ya pinka n’eya zaabu ahaiguru y’enyanja.

Akagamba omu iraka ry’ahansi ati: “Reeba ekyo,” reero bwanyima y’akaanya kakye ati: “Nkayenzire ngu ntungye kimwe aha bicu ebyo ebya pinka kyonka nkutemu n’okusindika.”

Nkagyezaho kuzayo obwo, kwonka tibarakihuriire; obundi okubaho kwangye kukabareetera kwehurira gye bari bonka.

Gatsby akagira ati: “Nimmanya eki turaakore, nitwija kugira ngu Klipspringer ateera piano.”

Akaruga omu kishengye arikweta “Ewing!” kandi akagaruka omu dakiika nkye arikuhererukayo n’omutsigazi owaabaire ashoberwa, ajwaire kakye, aine ebirahuri by’ebigogo n’eishokye rikye erikwiragura. Hati akaba ajwaire gye “eshaati y’emizaano,” ekingire omu bitsya, enkaito z’okuzaanisa, n’empare y’entare ey’erangi erikutukura.

“Tukateganisa ebisasayizi byawe?” Daisy akabuuza omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa.

“Nkaba ngwejegyeire,” nikwo Mr. Klipspringer yaarira, omu kushaasha ahabw’okushoberwa. "Ekwo kugira ngu, nkaba ngwejegyeire. Reero nyimuka..."

Gatsby akagira ati: “Klipspringer nateera piano,” arikumusharaho. “Tori, Ewing, omuzaano gwa ira?”

“Tindikuzaana gye. Tindi—kuzaana na kakye. Nshohoreire prac yoona—”

Gatsby akamuteganisa ati: “Nitwija kuza ahansi.” Akahindura switch. Amadirisa g’enzirugavu gakabura obwo enju erikwaka eijwiire omushana.

Omu kishengye ky’ebyeshongoro Gatsby akataho etabaaza eri wenka aha rubaju rwa piano. Akataho etaabe ya Daisy kuruga omu muriro ogwabaire nigutetema, kandi yaashutama nawe aha kitanda hare aha rubaju orundi orw’ekishengye, ahu haabaire hatariho mushana kureka eki ahansi erikumurinkana yaabaire neegurukaguruka kuruga omu kishengye.

Klipspringer ku yaaherize kuzaana “The Love Nest” akahinduka aha ntebe yaasherura Gatsby omu mwirima ataine mashemererwa.

"Nkaruga omu kwegyezamu kwona, nooreeba. Nkakugira ngu tindikubaasa kuzaana. Nkaruga omu kwegyezamu kwona—"

Gatsby akaragiira ati: “Otagamba munonga, omuzaano gwa ira.” "Okuzana!"

“Omu kasheeshe, .
Omu mwabazyo, .
Titutungire okushemererwa—”

Aheeru omuyaga gukaba guri muhango kandi hakaba hariho okusheesheka kw’enkuba okukye aha rubaju rw’Eiraka. Etara zoona zikaba nizijwa omuri West Egg hati; egaari z’omwika ez’amashanyarazi, ezirikutwarwa abashaija, zikaba nizigaruka omuka kurabira omu njura kuruga New York. Ekaba eri eshaaha y’empindahinduka mpango y’abantu, kandi okushemererwa kukaba nikureetaho aha mpeho.

“Ekintu kimwe nikihamibwa kandi tihaine ekirikuhamibwa .
Abagaiga nibongyera kugaigahara kandi abooro nibeyongyera kutunga—abaana.
Omu bwire obwo,
Omugati y’obwire—”

Ku nabaire ninza kusiibura nkareeba ngu ekishushani ky’okushoberwa kigarukire omu maisho ga Gatsby, nk’oku okubanganisa okukye kwabaire kumuhikireho aha mutindo gw’okushemererwa kwe okwa hati. Haihi emyaka etaano! Haine kuba haabaireho obwire nangwa n’omu mwabazyo ogwo obu Daisy yaabaire agwa omu birooto bye—ti ahabw’enshobi ye, kureka ahabw’amaani maingi g’okuhabisa kwe. Kikaba kimurengire, kirengire ahari buri kimwe. Akaba ayeshukiremu n’omuhimbo gw’okuhangahanga, arikugyongyeraho obwire bwona, arikugishemeza buri rubaju orurikwaka orwabaire nirusheesheka omu muhanda gwe. Tihariho muriro nari obusya oburikubaasa kuhakanisa eki omuntu arikubaasa kubiika omu mutima gwe ogw’omuzimu.

Ku nabaire nimureeba akehinduraho kakye, arikureebeka. Omukono gwe gukakwata ogwe, kandi ku yaabaire naagamba ekintu ahansi omu matu ge akahindukira ahariwe aine enyehurira z’okwiruka. Ninteekateeka ngu eiraka eryo rikamukwata munonga, n’obutagatsi bwaryo oburikuhindukahinduka, obw’omushwija, ahabw’okuba rikaba ritarikubaasa kurota munonga—eiraka eryo rikaba riri ekyeshongoro ekitarikufa.

Bakaba banyebirwe, kwonka Daisy akareeba ahaiguru yaayorora omukono gwe; Gatsby hati akaba atarikumanya na kakye. Nkagaruka naabareeba kandi nabo bagaruka banreeba, bari hare, baine amagara g’amaani. Bwanyima nkaruga omu kishengye naashuuma aha madaara g’amabaare g’omuhendo naaza omu njura, naabasigaho hamwe.

VI

Omu bwire obu omunyamakuru muto orikwenda kuba omukuru kuruga New York akahika omu kasheeshe kamwe aha rwigi rwa Gatsby kandi yamubuuza yaaba aine eki arikwenda kugamba.

“Ekindi kyona eky’okugamba ahari ki?” Gatsby akabuuza omu muringo gw’ekitiinisa.

“Ahabw’enki—ekigambo kyona eky’okuha.”

Kikashohora bwanyima y'edakiika itaano z'okushoberwa ngu omushaija akahurira eiziina rya Gatsby okwehinguririza ofiisi ye omu nkoragana ei yaabaire atarikwija kushoboorora nari atarikwetegyereza gye. Ekiro eki kikaba kiri ekiro kye ky’okuhuumura kandi n’okutandika okurikusiimwa akaba ayungire kurugayo “kureeba.”

Ekaba eri ekishushani ky’okuteeraho, kandi obwo omunyamakuru w’omunyamakuru gukaba guri murungi. Okumanywa kwa Gatsby, okwabaire nikujanjaara ahabw’ebikumi n’ebikumi by’abantu abaabaire baikiriize okutaayaayira kwe kandi n’ahabw’ekyo bakaba abakuru aha byafaayo bye, kukaba kweyongyeire omu bwire bw’ekihumuro bwona kuhitsya obu yaagwa omu bwire bukye bw’okuba amakuru. Enfumu z’obwire obwo nka “omupiira gw’ahansi y’itaka ogurikuza Canada” zikamukwataho, kandi hakaba hariho ekitebyo kimwe ekyabaire nikigumizamu ngu akaba atarikutuura omu nju na kakye, kureka omu bwato oburikushushana n’enju kandi bukaba butwarwa omu bwesherekye ahaiguru n’ahansi aha rubaju rw’enyanja ya Long Island. Ahabw’enki ebihangirwe ebi byabaire biri eby’okumarwa ahari James Gatz owa North Dakota, tikyorobi kukigamba.

James Gatz—eryo buzima rikaba riri eiziina rye, nari haakiri omu mateeka. Akaba agihinduire aha myaka ikumi na mushanju kandi omu bwire bwonyini obwareebire okutandika kw’emirimo ye—obu yaareebire emeeri ya Dan Cody neeteera enkongi aha mutwe gw’ekizimbe ekirikukirayo obushema aha nyanja ya Superior. James Gatz niwe yaabaire naazaana aha nyanja omu mwabazyo ogwo omu mupira gwa kinyasi ogutaagukire n’empare y’ekipande, kwonka akaba yaaheza kweguza obwato bw’okuvuga, yaakururayo yaaza Tuolomee , kandi yaamanyisa Cody ngu omuyaga nigubaasa kumukwata gumuhenda omu kicweka ky’eshaaha.

Ninteekateeka ngu akaba aine eiziina eryo ritebeekanisiibwe kumara obwire buraingwa, nangwa n'obwo. Abazaire be bakaba bari abantu b’omu faamu abatarikuhinduka kandi abatarikusingura—ebiteekateeko bye bikaba bitakabaikirizaga buzima nk’abazaire be na kakye. Amazima gakaba gari ngu Jay Gatsby owa West Egg, aha kizinga kya Long, akaruga omu ntekateeka ye eya Plato ahariwe. Akaba ari omwana wa Ruhanga—ekigambo eki, kyaba nikimanyisa ekintu kyona, nikimanyisa ekyo kyonka—kandi aine kuba ari aha nshonga ya Ishe, obuheereza bw’oburungi bwingi, obw’obushema, kandi obw’obushema. Nahabwekyo akajumbura omuringo gwa Jay Gatsby gwonka ogu omwojo w’emyaka ikumi na mushanju yaabaire nabaasa kujumbura, kandi aha ntekateeka egi akaba omwesigwa kuhitsya aha muheru.

Akaba amazire omwaka gumwe n’okukiraho arikuteera omuhanda gwe aha rubaju rw’omumashuma g’enyanja ya Superior nk’omuhiigi w’enyanja n’omujubi w’enyanja ya salmon nari omu muringo gwona ogwabaire nigumureetera eby’okurya n’ekitanda. Omubiri gwe ogwa kitaka, ogugumire gukaba gutuura omu buhangwa omu murimo gw’ekicweka ky’obushaarizi, ekicweka ky’obushema ogw’ebiro by’okuhamya. Akaba naamanya abakazi kare, kandi ahabw’okugira ngu bakaba bamushiisha akatandika kubagaya, abahara abato ahabw’okugira ngu bakaba bataine ki barikumanya, abandi ahabw’okugira ngu bakaba baine ekiniga kingi aha bintu ebi omu kwekunda kwe okurengire yaabaire atwara nk’eby’omugasho.

Kwonka omutima gwe gukaba guri omu butabanguko obutarikuhwaho, oburimu obutabanguko. Ebiteekateeko ebirikukirayo obushema kandi eby’okutangaaza bikaba bimuteganisa omu kitanda kye nyekiro. Ensi yoona ey’okushemererwa okutarikushoboororwa ekeehindura omu bwongo bwe obwo eshaaha erikuteera aha kitanda ky’okwogyeraho kandi okwezi kuriyo nikutonzya omushana gw’amaizi ebijwaro bye ebyabaire bitaine rubaju ahansi. Buri kiro akaba ayongyera aha nkora y’ebiteekateeko bye kuhitsya obu okugwejegyera kwakingire aha kishushani ekimwe ekirikureebwa gye n’okunywegyera okutarikwetegyerezibwa. Okumara obwire ebiteekateeko ebi bikamuha omuhanda gw’okuteekateeka; bikaba biri ekishushani ekirikumara eky’obutabaho bw’amazima, okuraganisa ngu orubaare rw’ensi rukatandikirwaho gye aha rubaju rw’omufumu.

Ekiteekateeko ky’okutunga ekitiinisa ky’omumaisho kikaba kimureeteire, ameezi makye enyima, kuza omu ishomero rikye erya Lutheran College erya St. Olaf’s omu bukiizi-bwa-bumosho bwa Minnesota. Akagumayo wiiki ibiri, ashoberwa ahabw’okutafayo kwayo okw’obushaarizi aha ngoma z’enkomerero ye, aha nkomerero ahabwayo, kandi arikugaya omurimo gw’omureeberezi ogu yaabaire aine kushashura omuhanda gwe. Bwanyima akagaruka omu nyanja ya Lake Superior, kandi akaba naakisherura eky’okukora aha izooba eri emeeri ya Dan Cody yaateire enkongi omu mwanya ogutari muhango aha rubaju rw’enyanja.

Cody akaba aine emyaka makumi ataano obwire obwo, ekirugire omu misiri y’efeeza eya Nevada, eya Yukon, eya buri kwirukira eby’ekyoma kuruga omu myaka makumi mushanju n’etaano. Ebikorwa bya Montana copper ebyamureeteire kuba omugaiga emirundi mingi bikamushanga aine omubiri murungi kwonka ari aha rubaju rw’okugira emiteekateekyere mirungi, kandi, barikuteekateeka eki, abakazi abatarikuhwaho bakagyezaho kumutaanisa n’esente ze. Ella Kaye, omukazi w’omu mpapura z’amakuru, akazaana nka Madame de Maintenon kuhika aha buremeezi bwe kandi akamusindika omu nyanja omu bwato, bikaba biri eby’obugaiga bw’abanyamakuru abaabaire baine obutabanguko omu mwaka gwa 1902. Akaba atwire naaza aha nyanja aha nyanja zoona ezirimu abagyenyi nk’oku James Little Gatz yaabaire naaza omu nyanja kumara emyaka etaano.

Aha mutsigazi Gatz, arikuhuumura aha nshozi ze kandi arikureeba ahaiguru aha rubaju rw’enyanja orurimu ebirahuri, emeeri egyo ekaba neejwekyera oburungi bwona n’okushemeza kw’ensi. Ninteekateeka ngu akamwenya ahari Cody—nabaasa kuba yaabaire yaazoora ngu abantu nibamukunda ku arikumwenya. Omu muringo gwona Cody akamubuuza ebibuuzo bikye (kimwe aharibyo kikareetaho eiziina risya) kandi yashanga ngu akaba naarahuka kandi aine ebigyendererwa by’amaani. Bwanyima y’ebiro bikye akamutwara Duluth yamugurira ekooti ya bururu, empare mukaaga z’embaata ezirikwera, n’enkofiira y’okuvuga emeeri. Kandi aba Tuolomee ku baarugireyo kuza omu mahanga ga Burengyerwa-izooba n’aha nyanja ya Barbary, Gatsby nawe akarugayo.

Akakoresibwa omu muringo gw’omuntu wenka ogutarikwetegyerezibwa gye—obu yaabaire agumire na Cody akaba ari omureeberezi, omunywani, omuvugi w’emeeri, omuhandiiki, nangwa n’omurinzi w’ekihome, ahabw’okuba Dan Cody sober akaba naamanya ebikorwa by’obugaiga ebi Dan Cody yaabaire atamiire yaabaire naabaasa kukora juba, kandi akahayo eby’obugaiga nk’ebyo arikweyongyera kwesiga Gatsby. Entebeekanisa egi ekamara emyaka etaano, kandi omu bwire obwo obwato bukaba nibwetoorora Ekizinga emirundi eshatu. Kikaba nikibaasa kugumaho ebiro byona okwihaho Ella Kaye akaija omu meeri ekiro kimwe omuri Boston kandi bwanyima ya wiiki emwe Dan Cody akafa omu muringo ogutarikwetegyerezibwa.

Ninyijuka ekishushani kye ahaiguru omu kishengye kya Gatsby, omushaija w’enzirugavu, orikushushana n’ebimuri kandi aine amaisho magumire, agatarimu kintu kyona—omutandikyi w’obushambani, owaabaire omu bwire bw’omurundi gumwe gw’amagara ga America agarukire aha rubaju rw’enyanja y’Oburugwa-izooba obutabanguko bw’obushaarizi obw’omu kishengye ky’obushambani eky’omu nshonda zoona n’omu saloon. Bakemerera aha, bahindukirana. Hati kikaba kiri ekiro ky’obufuki n’okushemererwa okwo okw’amazima okurikwija aha mpinduka ibiri z’omwaka. Etara ezitarikugamba omu nju zikaba nizihuuta zirikuruga omu mwirima kandi hakaba hariho okuyomba n’okuyomba omu nyonyoozi. Kuruga omu nshonda y’eriisho rye Gatsby akareeba ngu amabaare g’aha rubaju rw’enguuto buzima gakakora eidara kandi gatemba gaza omu mwanya gw’ekihama ahaiguru y’emiti—akaba naabaasa kugutemba, yaaba yaatemba wenka, kandi ku yaahikireyo akaba naabaasa kuyonka aha papu y’amagara, akanywa amate g’okutangaara agatarikugyeragyeranisibwa.

Omutima gwe gukateera munonga obu amaisho ga Daisy ageeru garikwija aha ge. Akaba naamanya ngu ku araanywegye omwishiki ogu, kandi ebiro byoona akashwera okworekwa kwe okutarikugambwa n’omwisyo gwe ogurikuhwerekyerera, ebiteekateeko bye tibirikwija kugaruka kuhunga nk’ebiteekateeko bya Ruhanga. N’ahabw’ekyo akategyereza, yaahurikiza kumara akaire kakye ekikwato ky’okuteera ebyeshongoro ekyabaire kiteirwe aha nyonyoozi. Bwanyima akamunywegyera. Aha kukwaata aha minwa ye akamumurikira nk’ekimuri kandi okuhinduka omuntu kukaba kuhikire.

Omuri byona ebi yaabaire agamba, nangwa n’omu nyehurira ze ezirikutiinisa, nkaba nyijuka ekintu—omuhimbo ogutarikushoboororwa gye, ekicweka ky’ebigambo ebibuzire, ebi naabaire mpurire ahu ndi ira munonga. Okumara akaire kakye ekigambo kikagyezaho kutwara ekishushani omu kanwa kangye kandi eminywa yangye ekataanisa nk’ey’omushaija omushema, nk’oku haabaire hariho okurwanagana aharizo okukira okuhuuta kw’omuyaga ogurikutiinisa. Kwonka tibaakozire iraka, kandi eki naabaire ndi haihi kwijuka kikaba kitarikubaasa kujanjaara ebiro byona.
