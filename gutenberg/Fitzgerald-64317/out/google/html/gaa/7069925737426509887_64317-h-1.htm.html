Lala jɛɔ mikutsoŋnyo lɛ shia kɛyaa hulutsoo be lɛ mli gbɛkɛ mli. Yɛ ebɔɔi bluu lɛ amli lɛ, hii kɛ yei baa ni amɛyaa tamɔ ŋmeii yɛ ŋmɔlɔi kɛ champagne kɛ ŋulamii lɛ ateŋ. Kanei lɛ kpɛɔ waa beni shikpɔŋ lɛ shwieɔ ehe kɛjɛɔ hulu lɛ he lɛ, ni agbɛnɛ lalafolɔi akuu lɛ miitswa lalai ni ji yellow cocktail, ni gbeei ahe opera lɛ tswaa saji otii ko kɛyaa ŋwɛi. Shwɛmɔ feɔ mlɛo minitii fɛɛ minitii, ni egbɛɔ eshwaa kɛ fitemɔ, ni ajieɔ lɛ kpo yɛ miishɛɛ wiemɔ ko mli. Kuu lɛ tsakeɔ oya, amɛshwereɔ kɛ mɛi heei ni eba, amɛshwieɔ shi ni amɛfeɔ yɛ mumɔ kome mli; mɛi ni nyiɛɔ kɛyaa kɛbaa, gbekɛbii yei ni yɔɔ hekɛnɔfɔɔ ni shwieɔ atadei yɛ biɛ kɛ biɛ yɛ mɛi ni he wa ni amɛhe wa fe tsutsu lɛ ateŋ lɛ yɛ momo, ni amɛbafeɔ kuu ko teŋgbɛ yɛ be kukuoo mli, ni miishɛɛ yɔɔ mli, ni no sɛɛ lɛ, kɛ kunimyeli he miishɛɛ lɛ, amɛfoɔ kɛtsɔɔ hiɛi kɛ gbeei kɛ sui ni tsakeɔ yɛ ŋshɔ lɛ mli lɛ mli yɛ la ni tsakeɔ be fɛɛ be lɛ shishi.

Trukaa lɛ, nɛkɛ gypsies nɛɛ ateŋ mɔ kome, ni wo opal ni hosoɔ lɛ, mɔ dãa ko kɛjɛ kɔɔyɔɔ mli, ni ekɛshwie shi koni ená ekãa, ni ekɛ eniji tsiɔ ehe tamɔ Frisco, ni lɛ pɛ ejoɔ yɛ kanvas kpoku lɛ nɔ. Be kukuoo ko mli dioofeemɔ; lalatswalɔi akuu lɛ hiɛnyiɛlɔ lɛ tsakeɔ elala lɛ yɛ gbɛ ni sa nɔ kɛha lɛ, ni sanegbaa ni mli wa waa baa beni adafitswaa ni ejaaa lɛ yaa nɔ akɛ lɛ ji Gilda Gray understudy kɛjɛ Follies lɛ mli lɛ. Gbijurɔyeli lɛ eje shishi.

Miheɔ miyeɔ akɛ klɛŋklɛŋ gbɛkɛ ni mitee Gatsby shia lɛ mifata gbɔi fioo ni afɔ̃ amɛ nine diɛŋtsɛ lɛ ahe. Afɔ̃ɔɔ mɛi nine—amɛtee jɛmɛ. Amɛbote tsɔnei ni kɛ amɛ tee Long Island lɛ amli, ni yɛ gbɛ ko nɔ lɛ, amɛyashɛ Gatsby shinaa lɛ naa. Beni amɛshɛ jɛmɛ lɛ, mɔ ko ni le Gatsby lɛ jie amɛ kpo, ni no sɛɛ lɛ amɛye amɛhe nɔ yɛ jeŋba he mlai ni kɔɔ hiɛtserɛjiemɔ he ko he lɛ naa. Bei komɛi lɛ, amɛbaa ni amɛyaa ni amɛkɛ Gatsby ekpeko kwraa, amɛbaa kɛha okpɔlɔŋmɛɛ lɛ kɛ tsui ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni ji lɛ diɛŋtsɛ e-tikiti ni akɛboteɔ mli lɛ.

No mli lɛ, afɔ̃ mi nine diɛŋtsɛ. Tsɔnekudɔlɔ ko ni wo robin’s-egg bluu atade lɛ fo mi jwɛi lɛ mli mra nakai Hɔgbaa leebi lɛ kɛ niŋmaa ko ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ ni jɛ enitsumɔtsɛ lɛ ŋɔɔ: ekɛɛ akɛ, woo lɛ baafee Gatsby nɔ kwraa, kɛji mitee e-“gbijurɔyeli bibioo” lɛ nakai gbɛkɛ lɛ. Ena mi shii abɔ, ni esusu akɛ ebaabasara mi be saŋŋ ni eho, shi shihilɛi ni yɔɔ srɔto ni akɛfee ekome lɛ etsĩ enaa—Jay Gatsby kɛ enine wo shishi, kɛ enine ni yɔɔ nyam.

Miwo atadei yɛŋ ni mikɛtee eŋmɔ lɛ he yɛ ŋmɛlɛtswai kpawo sɛɛ fioo, ni mikɛ mihe wo mli yɛ gbɔmɛi ni mileee amɛ lɛ ateŋ mɛi ni shwieɔ shi kɛ shwiemɔi ni shwieɔ shi lɛ ateŋ—eyɛ mli akɛ hiɛ ko yɛ biɛ kɛ jɛmɛ ni miyɔse yɛ oketeke ni kɛ mi yaa lɛ mli moŋ. Amrɔ nɔŋŋ ni bɔ ni Ŋleshibii oblahii kɛ oblayei ni ebɔle amɛhe lɛ ayibɔ ha minaa kpɛ mihe; amɛ fɛɛ amɛsaa amɛhe jogbaŋŋ, amɛ fɛɛ amɛtamɔ nɔ ni hɔmɔ miiye amɛ fioo, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛkɛ gbee ni baa shi ni hiɛdɔɔ wieɔ amɛtsɔɔ Amerikabii ni yɔɔ shiŋŋ ni yɔɔ nii babaoo lɛ. Miyɛ nɔmimaa akɛ amɛmiihɔɔ nɔ ko: bonds loo inshuɔrans loo tsɔnei. Kɛ hoo lɛ, amɛle shika ni yɔɔ mlɛo ni yɔɔ akutso lɛ mli lɛ jogbaŋŋ ni amɛná nɔmimaa akɛ amɛ nɔ ni kɛha wiemɔi fioo komɛi ni yɔɔ saji otii ni sa lɛ amli lɛ.

Beni miyashɛ jɛmɛ pɛ kɛkɛ ni mibɔ mɔdɛŋ koni mana mɔ ni here mi lɛ, shi mɛi enyɔ loo etɛ ni mibi amɛ he ni eyɔɔ lɛ kwɛ mi yɛ naakpɛɛ gbɛ nɔ, ni amɛkpoo waa akɛ amɛleee bɔ ni enyiɛɔ ehaa lɛ, ni mikɛ mihe wo mli kɛtee dãatɔɔ okpɔlɔ lɛ he—he pɛ ni gbɔmɔ kome ni bɛ mɔ ko mɔ ko baanyɛ akwɛ yɛ abɔɔ lɛ mli.

Mimiiya koni miyatɔ dãa waa kɛjɛ hiɛgbele ni mli wa mli beni Jordan Baker je kpo kɛjɛ shĩa lɛ mli ni edamɔ marble atswere lɛ yitso, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ sɛɛ fioo ni ekɛ miishɛɛ ni bulɛ bɛ mli miikwɛ abɔɔ lɛ mli.

Ahere mi atuu loo ahereee mi atuu, mina akɛ ehe miihia ni makɛ mihe akpɛtɛ mɔ ko he dani mabɔi wiemɔi ni jɛ tsuiŋ kɛ mɛi ni hoɔ lɛ awiemɔ.

Beni Jordan nine bibioo ni ji shika tsuru lɛ kã minine lɛ nɔ lɛ, wɔba shi kɛjɛ atswere lɛ nɔ ni wɔnyiɛ abɔɔ lɛ mli. Cocktail tray ko shwie wɔ nɔ yɛ shwane mli, ni wɔkɛ gbekɛbii yei enyɔ ni wo ŋmɔŋ lɛ kɛ hii etɛ tara okpɔlɔ ko he, ni amɛteŋ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ jie ehe kpo etsɔɔ wɔ akɛ Mr. Mumble.

“Ani obaa okpɔlɔŋmɛɛi nɛɛ shii abɔ?” Jordan bi gbekɛ yoo ni yɔɔ emasɛi lɛ sane.

Gbekɛ yoo lɛ kɛ hekɛnɔfɔɔ gbee ni yɔɔ hiɛdɔɔ ha hetoo akɛ: “Naagbee nɔ lɛ ji nɔ ni mikɛ bo kpe yɛ lɛ.” Etsɔ ehe etsɔɔ enaanyo lɛ akɛ: “Ani jeee bo hewɔ, Lucille?”

Lucille kɛɛ akɛ: “Misumɔɔ ni maba. "Misusuuu nɔ ni mifeɔ lɛ he kɔkɔɔkɔ, no hewɔ lɛ mináa be kpakpa be fɛɛ be. Beni miba biɛ naagbee lɛ mifo mi atade lɛ mli yɛ sɛi ko nɔ, ni ebi mi migbɛi kɛ adrɛs—yɛ otsi kome mli lɛ minine shɛ paketi ko nɔ kɛjɛ Croirier ni gbɛkɛ atade hee yɔɔ mli."

“Ani okɛto?” Jordan bi lɛ.

"Eji anɔkwale akɛ miwo. Mibaawo lɛ ŋmɛnɛ gbɛkɛ, shi eda tsɔ yɛ etsitsi mli ni esa akɛ atsake. Eji gas bluu kɛ lavender ŋmeii. Dɔlai ohai enyɔ kɛ nyɔŋmai ekpaa kɛ enumɔ."

Gbekɛ yoo kroko lɛ kɛ miishɛɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Nɔ ko yɛ ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ yɛ naanyo ko ni baafee nɔ ko ni tamɔ nɛkɛ he.” “Esumɔɔɔ haomɔ ko kɛ gbɔmɔtso ko .”

Gbekɛbii yei enyɔ lɛ kɛ Jordan kɛ amɛhe fɔ̃ɔ amɛhe nɔ yɛ teemɔŋ.

Shi, beni amɛshi amɛtsɔnei lɛ ni amɛtsi gbɛ lɛ naa lɛ, anu gbee ni naa wa ni kɛ amɛhe kpaaa gbee ni jɛ mɛi ni yɔɔ sɛɛ lɛ adɛŋ be ko nɛ, ni ekɛ basabasafeemɔ ni yɔɔ shihilɛ lɛ mli momo lɛ fata he.

"Naa!" etsɔɔ mli. “Etee nubu lɛ mli.”

Anɔkwale lɛ fee lɛ naakpɛɛ ni bɛ naagbee, ni miyɔse naakpɛɛ su ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ, kɛ no sɛɛ lɛ nuu lɛ—lɛ ji Gatsby wojiatoohe lɛ nɔkwɛlɔ ni egbo lɛ.

Naakpɛɛ ni nyiɛ jajemɔ nɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ na gbee yɛ “Ah-hh!” beni coupé lɛ shinaa lɛ shwieɔ shi blɛoo lɛ. Kɛkɛ ni, fiofio, fã kɛ fã, aŋkro ko ni he yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni egbɔjɔ lɛ je kpo kɛjɛ lɛlɛ ni efite lɛ mli, ni ekɛ joo tokota agbo ko ni bɛ faŋŋ miitswa shikpɔŋ fioo.

Eshashao shi.

Caterwauling akoloŋtoi lɛ eshɛ crescendo he ni mitsɔ mihe ni mifo jwɛi lɛ mli kɛtee shia. Mikwɛ sɛɛ shikome. Nyɔŋtsere wafer ko miikpɛ yɛ Gatsby shia lɛ nɔ, ni eha gbɛkɛ lɛ fee jogbaŋŋ tamɔ tsutsu lɛ, ni ehiɛ kã ŋmɔlɔ kɛ ebɔɔ ni tsoɔ lolo lɛ gbɛɛmɔ lɛ mli. Etamɔ nɔ ni trukaa nɔ ko ni bɛ nɔ ko miiho kɛmiijɛ samfɛji lɛ kɛ shinaai wuji lɛ amli amrɔ nɛɛ, ni eha gbɔfeelɔ lɛ he mfoniri lɛ, ni damɔ kpoku lɛ nɔ, ni ewo enine nɔ yɛ gbɛ ni sa nɔ kɛmiitsɔɔ akɛ ekɛ ehe miikpa gbee lɛ mli lɛ ha ehe etse ehe kwraa.

Kɛ mikane nɔ ni miŋma kɛbashi bianɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ, minaa akɛ mikɛ susumɔ eha akɛ nibii ni tee nɔ yɛ gbɛkɛ etɛ ni otsii babaoo kɛ amɛhe kpaa gbee lɛ pɛ ji nɔ ni gbala mijwɛŋmɔ mli. Nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ enɛ lɛ, amɛji nibii ni yaa nɔ yɛ be kukuoo mli kɛkɛ yɛ hulutsoo be ni mɛi eyi mli obɔ lɛ mli, ni, kɛyashi sɛɛ mli babaoo lɛ, amɛheɔ mihe kɛjɛɔ mihe kwraa fe mi diɛŋtsɛ mi saji.

Bei pii lɛ, mitsuɔ nii. Leebi maŋkpa lɛ hulu lɛ shwie mihe mfoniri lɛ kɛtee anaigbɛ beni mikɛ oyaiyeli shwieɔ New York shishigbɛ ŋshɔkei yɛŋ lɛ anɔ kɛyaa Probity Trust lɛ. Mile woloŋmalɔi krokomɛi lɛ kɛ oblahii kɛ oblayei ni hɔ̃ɔ bond lɛ kɛ amɛ klɛŋklɛŋ gbɛi, ni mikɛ amɛ yeɔ shwane niyenii yɛ niyelihei ni mli ewo duŋ ni mɛi eyi mli obɔ lɛ amli yɛ tsina sosɛji bibii kɛ ŋmɔtɔ ni ashã kɛ kɔfe nɔ. Mikɛ gbekɛ yoo ko ni hiɔ Jersey City ni tsuɔ nii yɛ akɔntaabuu nitsumɔhe lɛ po ná bɔlɛ kuku, shi enyɛmi nuu lɛ bɔi mihe kwɛmɔ ni ehiii, no hewɔ lɛ beni etee ehejɔɔmɔ yɛ July mli lɛ miha efo dioo.

Miyeɔ gbɛkɛ niyenii bei pii yɛ Yale Club lɛ—yɛ yiŋtoo ko hewɔ lɛ eji nɔ ni tee nɔ yɛ migbi lɛ mli ni yɔɔ awerɛho fe fɛɛ—ni no sɛɛ lɛ mitee ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ kɛtee wojiatoohe lɛ ni mikase nibii ni akɛwoɔ shika mli kɛ shikatoohe lɛ he nii ŋmɛlɛtswaa kome kɛ henilee. Bei pii lɛ, basabasafeelɔi fioo komɛi yɛ jɛmɛ, shi amɛbaaa wojiatoohe lɛ mli kɔkɔɔkɔ, no hewɔ lɛ eji he ko ni hi jogbaŋŋ ni abaanyɛ atsu nii yɛ. No sɛɛ lɛ, kɛji akɛ gbɛkɛ lɛ mli jɔ lɛ, minyiɛɔ Madison Gbɛjegbɛ lɛ nɔ kɛhoɔ Murray Hill Hotel momo lɛ he, ni mifoɔ 33rd Street lɛ nɔ kɛyaa Pennsylvania Gbɛjegbɛ lɛ nɔ.

Mibɔi New York suɔmɔ, bɔ ni enuɔ he akɛ eyɛ hewolonɔ su, ni ekɛ ehe woɔ nifeemɔi amli gbɛkɛ, kɛ tsui ni nyɔɔ mɔ mli ni hii kɛ yei kɛ tsɔnei ni shwieɔ shi be fɛɛ be lɛ haa hiŋmɛi ni bɛ hejɔɔmɔ lɛ. Misumɔɔ ni manyiɛ Fifth Avenue lɛ nɔ ni mihala yei ni yɔɔ suɔmɔ kɛjɛ mɛi babaoo lɛ ateŋ ni masusu he akɛ yɛ minitii fioo komɛi asɛɛ lɛ, mibaabote amɛshihilɛ mli, ni mɔ ko mɔ ko leee loo ekpɛlɛŋ nɔ kɔkɔɔkɔ. Bei komɛi lɛ, yɛ mijwɛŋmɔ mli lɛ, minyiɛɔ amɛsɛɛ kɛyaa amɛshiai ni yɔɔ gbɛjegbɛi ni yɔɔ teemɔŋ lɛ anɔ lɛ amli, ni amɛtsɔɔ amɛhe ni amɛkuɔ amɛsɛɛ amɛŋmɔɔ mi dani amɛtsɔɔ shinaa ko naa kɛyaa duŋ ni mli edɔ lɛ mli. Yɛ maŋtiase lɛ mli hulutsoo be mli lɛ, minuɔ he akɛ mikome too miyɔɔ bei komɛi, ni minuɔ he yɛ mɛi krokomɛi amli—oblahii kɛ oblayei ni ji woloŋmalɔi ni nyiɛɔ samfɛji ahiɛ ni amɛmɛɔ kɛyashiɔ be ni akɛyeɔ niyelihe ko gbɛkɛ niyenii ni amɛkomekomei feɔ lɛ shɛɔ—oblahii kɛ oblayei ni tsuɔ nii yɛ gbɛkɛ mli, ni amɛfiteɔ gbɛkɛ kɛ shihilɛ mli bei ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli fe fɛɛ lɛ.

Ekoŋŋ yɛ ŋmɛlɛtswai kpaanyɔ mli, beni gbɛjegbɛi ni yɔɔ duŋ ni yɔɔ Forties lɛ mli lɛ eyi kɛ tsɔnei ni tswaa kɛmiiya sinifeemɔ kpokpaa lɛ nɔ lɛ, minu he akɛ mitsui miiho. Foji lɛ ŋmɛɔ amɛhe yɛ taksii lɛ amli beni amɛmiimɛ lɛ, ni gbeei miilá, ni ŋmɔlɔ ni jɛɔ shwɛmɔi ni anuko mli lɛ mli baa, ni sigaret ni ashã lɛ feɔ kooyigbɛ ni shishinumɔ bɛ he yɛ mli. Akɛni misusu akɛ mi hu mikɛ oyaiyeli miiya miishɛɛ he ni mikɛ amɛmiishɛɛ ni bɛŋkɛ kpaakpa lɛ miija hewɔ lɛ, mishwe amɛ jogbaŋŋ.

Mihiɛ kpa Jordan Baker nɔ be fioo, ni no sɛɛ yɛ hulutsoo be lɛ teŋgbɛ lɛ, mina lɛ ekoŋŋ. Shishijee lɛ, mikɛ mihe wo mli akɛ mikɛ lɛ aya hei srɔtoi, ejaakɛ eji golf tswalɔ ni he esa waa, ni mɔ fɛɛ mɔ le egbɛi. Kɛkɛ ni eji nɔ ko ni fe nakai. Mibɛ suɔmɔ diɛŋtsɛ, shi minu he akɛ miitao male nɔ ko. Hiɛ ni woɔ ehe nɔ ni egbɔjɔ ni etsɔ ehe etsɔɔ je lɛ tee nɔ ko—nibii ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli lɛ ateŋ babaoo tee nɔ ko yɛ naagbee, eyɛ mli akɛ amɛfeee nakai yɛ shishijee mli moŋ—ni gbi ko lɛ mina nɔ ni eji. Beni wɔfee ekome kɛtee shia-henaamuu ko shishi yɛ Warwick ŋwɛi lɛ, eshi tsɔne ko ni efa lɛ yɛ nugbɔ lɛ mli ni ehiɛ shwie shi, ni no sɛɛ lɛ emale yɛ he—ni trukaa lɛ mikai sane ni kɔɔ ehe ni efo mihe nakai gbɛkɛ lɛ yɛ Daisy shĩa lɛ. Yɛ eklɛŋklɛŋ golf akaŋshii wulu lɛ mli lɛ, béi ko ba ni eshwɛ fioo ni ebaashɛ adafitswaa woji amli—ni ji ŋaawoo ni tsɔɔ akɛ ejie ebɔɔlu lɛ kɛjɛ amale gbonyo ko mli yɛ semifinal akaŋshii lɛ mli. Nɔ lɛ bɛŋkɛ heguɔgbee ko he—kɛkɛ ni egbo. Caddy ko ku esɛɛ ewiemɔ lɛ, ni odasefonyo kroko pɛ lɛ kpɛlɛ nɔ akɛ ekolɛ etɔmɔ. Nɔ ni ba lɛ kɛ gbɛi lɛ ehi mijwɛŋmɔ mli.

Jordan Baker jɛ fɔmɔ mli tsi ehe kɛjɛ hii ni yɔɔ ŋaa, ni yɔɔ jwɛŋmɔ lɛ ahe, ni agbɛnɛ mina akɛ nɔ ni ha ebalɛ nakai ji akɛ, enuɔ he akɛ eyɛ shweshweeshwe yɛ kɔɔyɔŋ lɛlɛ mli he ni abaasusu akɛ sɛɛkuu fɛɛ sɛɛkuu kɛjɛ mla ko he lɛ nyɛŋ aba mli. Eyeee anɔkwa ni anyɛɛɛ atsa. Enyɛɛɛ eŋmɛ etsui shi yɛ shihilɛ ni mli wa mli, ni kɛ akɛ suɔmɔ ni bɛ nɛɛ to he lɛ, misusuɔ akɛ ebɔi shishiumɔ nitsumɔ beni eji gbekɛ fioo bɔni afee ni eha nakai ŋmɔlɔ ni mli jɔ, ni bɛ bulɛ lɛ atsɔ je lɛ nɔ ni kɛlɛ etsu egbɔmɔtso ni wa ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔ lɛ bimɔi lɛ ahe nii.

Ekɛ srɔtofeemɔ ko kwraa baaa mihe. Anɔkwa ni ayeee yɛ yoo mli lɛ ji nɔ ko ni okɛ fɔ fɔɔɔ nɔ waa kɔkɔɔkɔ—mishwa mihe yɛ gbɛ ni ejaaa nɔ, ni no sɛɛ lɛ mihiɛ kpa nɔ. Nakai shia-henaabuamɔ nɛɛ nɔŋŋ mli ji he ni wɔgba sane ko ni kɔɔ tsɔne kudɔmɔ he ni wɔmiitao wɔle nɔ ni yaa nɔ lɛ. Eje shishi ejaakɛ etsɔ ebɛŋkɛ nitsulɔi komɛi aahu akɛ wɔ fender lɛ tswa nuu ko atade lɛ nɔ button ko.

Ehiŋmɛii ni tsuɔ tamɔ ŋmɔtɔ ni hulu lɛ edɔ lɛ lɛ kwɛɔ hiɛ tɛ̃ɛ, shi ejɛ etsui mli etsake wɔ wekukpaa lɛ, ni misusu be fioo ko akɛ misumɔɔ lɛ. Shi misusuɔ nii ahe blɛoo ni miyi obɔ kɛ mligbɛ mlai ni tsuɔ nii akɛ breki ni tsiɔ mishwelɛi anaa, ni mile akɛ klɛŋklɛŋ lɛ esa akɛ majie mihe kɛjɛ nakai basabasafeemɔ lɛ mli kwraa yɛ shia. Miŋmala woji shikome yɛ otsi mli ni mikɛ mi nine woɔ shishi akɛ: “Suɔmɔ, Nick,” ni nɔ pɛ ni manyɛ masusu he ji bɔ ni, beni nakai gbekɛ yoo pɔtɛɛ ko miitswa tɛnis lɛ, ŋmɔtɔ bibioo ko ni mli edɔ jeɔ kpo yɛ enaabu ŋwɛigbɛ lɛ nɔ lɛ. Shi fɛɛ sɛɛ lɛ, shishinumɔ ko ni bɛ faŋŋ yɛ ni esa akɛ atsɔ ŋaa gbɛ nɔ aku mli dani maná heyeli.

IV

Hɔgbaa leebi beni sɔlemɔ ŋmɛlɛtswai lɛ tswaa yɛ akrowai ni yɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ naa lɛ amli lɛ, je lɛ kɛ eŋa lɛ ku amɛsɛɛ amɛtee Gatsby shia ni amɛkɛ miishɛɛ ŋmɔ eŋmɔ lɛ nɔ.

Oblayei lɛ kɛɛ akɛ: “Eji mɔ ko ni hɔ̃ɔ tsofai fɔji,” ni amɛtsi amɛhe kɛtee he ko yɛ e-cocktail lɛ kɛ efɔfɔi lɛ ateŋ. "Be ko lɛ egbe nuu ko ni ena akɛ eji Von Hindenburg nyɛmi nuu kɛ abonsam nyɛmi nuu ni ji enyɔ. Oshɛ rose ko he, wuo, ni okɛ naagbee nu ni hoɔ awo nakai kristalo glase lɛ mli."

Be ko lɛ, miŋmala mɛi ni ba Gatsby shia nakai hulutsoo be lɛ mli lɛ agbɛi yɛ be he gbɛjianɔtoo ko mli hei ni nɔ ko bɛ lɛ anɔ. Eji be he gbɛjianɔtoo momo ko bianɛ, ni miifite yɛ eshishigbɛ, ni eyitso ji “Gbɛjianɔtoo nɛɛ ni jeɔ shishi yɛ July 5th, 1922.” Shi manyɛ makane gbɛi ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ lɛ lolo, ni amɛbaaha oná jwɛŋmɔ ni hi fe mi generalities ni kɔɔ mɛi ni kpɛlɛ Gatsby gbɔfeemɔ lɛ nɔ ni amɛwo lɛ nyɔmɔ ni yɔɔ mlɛo akɛ amɛleee nɔ ko kwraa yɛ ehe lɛ he.

No hewɔ lɛ, Chester Beckers kɛ Leeches weku lɛ jɛ East Egg, kɛ nuu ko ni atsɛɔ lɛ Bunsen, ni mile lɛ yɛ Yale lɛ, kɛ Datrɛfonyo Webster Civet, ni nu shwie lɛ yɛ hulutsoo be ni eho lɛ mli yɛ Maine lɛ. Enɔ jetsɛremɔ ŋmɛlɛtswai enumɔ lɛ, ekɛ Tom Buchanan bote gbalashihilɛ mli ni ekpokpooo kwraa, ni ebɔi nyɔji etɛ gbɛfaa kɛmiiya Ŋshɔi ni yɔɔ Anaigbɛ lɛ.

Mina amɛ yɛ Santa Barbara beni amɛku amɛsɛɛ amɛba lɛ, ni misusu akɛ minako gbekɛ yoo ko ni mli efu ewu waa nɛkɛ dã. Kɛ eshi tsu lɛ mli miniti kome lɛ, ebaakwɛ ehe kɛkpe kɛ hejɔlɛ, ni ekɛɛ: “Nɛgbɛ Tom etee?” ni ewo hiɛ ni yɔɔ mlɛo fe fɛɛ lɛ kɛyashi ena lɛ ni eba shinaa lɛ naa. Efɔɔ ŋshɔ lɛ nɔ taraa ni ekɛ eyitso woɔ etsitsi nɔ yɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ mli, ni ekɛ eniji shwieɔ ehiŋmɛii anɔ ni ekwɛɔ lɛ kɛ miishɛɛ ni anyɛɛɛ atsɔɔ shishi. Eshɛɔ mɔ he waa akɛ ooona amɛ ni amɛfee ekome—eha oŋmɔ yɛ gbɛ ni yɔɔ dioo, ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli nɔ. No ji August mli. Beni otsi kome sɛɛ beni mishi Santa Barbara lɛ, Tom jo foi kɛbote tsɔne ko mli yɛ Ventura gbɛjegbɛ lɛ nɔ gbɛkɛ ko, ni efo etsɔne lɛ hiɛ shwiili ko. Gbekɛ yoo ni fata ehe lɛ hu bote woji lɛ amli, ejaakɛ enine kumɔ—eji mɛi ni tsuɔ nii yɛ tsũ mli yɛ Santa Barbara Hotel lɛ ateŋ mɔ kome.

April ni nyiɛ sɛɛ lɛ, Daisy fɔ ebiyoo fioo lɛ, ni amɛtee France afi. Mina amɛ fɛ̃i be ko yɛ Cannes, ni sɛɛ mli lɛ yɛ Deauville, ni no sɛɛ lɛ amɛku amɛsɛɛ amɛba Chicago koni amɛbahi shi. Daisy he gbɛi waa yɛ Chicago, taakɛ ole lɛ. Amɛkɛ asafo babaoo ni he yɔɔ oya, ni amɛ fɛɛ amɛji oblahii kɛ oblayei kɛ niiatsɛmɛi kɛ koo mli bii, shi eje kpo kɛ gbɛi ni yeɔ emuu kwraa. Ekolɛ, akɛni enuuu dãa hewɔ. Sɛɛnamɔ kpele ji akɛ anuuu dãa yɛ mɛi ni nuɔ dãa waa lɛ ateŋ. Obaanyɛ omɔ olilɛi mli ni, kɛfata he lɛ, obaanyɛ okɛ be awo nɔ ko bibioo ni ejaaa ni ofee lɛ mli bɔni afee ni mɔ fɛɛ mɔ kroko lɛ afee shwilafoi aahu akɛ amɛnaaa loo amɛsusuuu he. Ekolɛ Daisy eyaaa suɔmɔ mli kɔkɔɔkɔ—ni kɛlɛ nɔ ko yɛ egbee lɛ mli...

Ojogbaŋŋ, aaafee otsii enyɔ ni eho nɛ lɛ, enu gbɛi Gatsby lɛ klɛŋklɛŋ kwraa yɛ afii babaoo ni eho lɛ amli. No mli lɛ mibi bo—ani okaiɔ?—kɛji ole Gatsby yɛ West Egg. Beni oya shia sɛɛ lɛ eba mitsu lɛ mli ni etswa mi shi, ni ekɛɛ: “Mɛni Gatsby?” ni beni mitsɔɔ lɛ mli lɛ—miwɔ fã—ekɛ gbee ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ fe fɛɛ kɛɛ akɛ ekolɛ nuu ni ele lɛ tsutsu lɛ ji lɛ. No mli lɛ, mikɛ Gatsby nɛɛ kɛ asraafonyo nukpa ni yɔɔ etsɔne yɛŋ lɛ mli lɛ tsa.

Beni Jordan Baker gbe enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ naa lɛ wɔshi Plaza lɛ ŋmɛlɛtswaa fã ni wɔkɛ victoria miikudɔ tsɔne kɛmiitsɔ Central Park. Hulu lɛ enyɔ shi yɛ sini mli ŋulamii ni yɔɔ West Fifties lɛ ashiai kakadaji lɛ asɛɛ, ni gbekɛbii agbɛ́i ni mli tse, ni ebua amɛhe naa momo tamɔ kriket yɛ jwɛi lɛ nɔ lɛ te shi kɛtsɔ hulutsoo be ni mli edɔ lɛ mli:

“Miji Arabia Sheik lɛ.
Osuɔmɔ lɛ ji mi nɔ.
Gbi gbɛkɛ beni owɔ lɛ
Mabote obuu lɛ mli—”

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Eji nɔ ko ni ba trukaa ni yɔɔ naakpɛɛ.”

“Shi jeee nɔ ko ni ba trukaa kwraa.”

"Meni hewɔ?"

“Gatsby he nakai shia lɛ koni Daisy ahi ŋshɔ lɛ naa pɛ.”

No mli lɛ, jeee ŋulamii ni eshweɔ waa yɛ nakai June gbɛkɛ lɛ kɛkɛ ji no. Eba wala mli kɛba miŋɔɔ, ni ajie lɛ trukaa kɛjɛ ehiɛ nyam ni yiŋtoo ko bɛ he lɛ musu mli.

Jordan tsa nɔ akɛ: “Emiisumɔ ni ele kɛji obaafɔ̃ Daisy nine kɛba oshia shwane ko, ni no sɛɛ lɛ oha eba oŋɔɔ.”

Heshibaa ni abiɔ lɛ ha mihe jɔ̃ mihe. No mli lɛ emɛ afii enumɔ ni ehé shĩa wulu ko ni ekɛ ŋulamiiaŋ la haa ŋmeii ni bɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko lɛ—koni “eba” shwane ko kɛya gbɔ ko abɔɔ mli.

“Ani esa akɛ male enɛɛmɛi fɛɛ dani ebi nɔ ko bibioo tamɔ nɛkɛ?”

"Esheɔ gbeyei, emɛ be kakadaŋŋ. Esusu akɛ ekolɛ omli baafu bo. Ona, eyɛ hewalɛ daa yɛ enɛ fɛɛ shishi."

Nɔ ko hao mi.

“Mɛni hewɔ ebiii bo ni oto kpee he gbɛjianɔ lɛ?”

Etsɔɔ mli akɛ: “Esumɔɔ ni ena eshia lɛ.” “Ni oshia lɛ bɛŋkɛ wɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ.”

"Ohh!"

Jordan tee nɔ akɛ: “Misusuɔ akɛ ekpa gbɛ akɛ ebaafã kɛya e-party lɛ eko mli, gbɛkɛ ko, shi efeee nakai kɔkɔɔkɔ. Kɛkɛ ni ebɔi mɛi bimɔ kɛji amɛle lɛ, ni mi ji mɔ klɛŋklɛŋ ni ena. Nakai gbɛkɛ lɛ ji be ni etsu koni ayatsɛ mi yɛ ejo lɛ mli, ni kulɛ esa akɛ onu gbɛ ni etsɔɔ nɔ etsuɔ nii kɛyaa lɛ he, ni mikɛ ŋaawoo ha amrɔ nɔŋŋ yɛ New York. Mi jwɛŋ akɛ ebaa mli afu:

“ ‘Misumɔɔɔ ni mafee nɔ ko ni jɛ gbɛ nɔ!’ etee nɔ ekɛɛ akɛ, ‘Miisumɔ ni mana lɛ yɛ shia lɛ masɛi tuutu.’

"Be ni mikɛɛ Tom naanyo pɔtɛɛ ko ji bo lɛ, ebɔi susumɔ lɛ fɛɛ shimɔ. Eleee Tom he nɔ ko kwraa, eyɛ mli akɛ ekɛɛ ekane Chicago wolo ko afii babaoo yɛ hegbɛ ni eyɔɔ akɛ ebaana Daisy gbɛi lɛ fioo pɛ hewɔ."

Amrɔ nɛɛ edɔ, ni beni wɔboteɔ ŋshɔ bibioo ko shishi lɛ mikɛ minine shwie Jordan shika tsuru tsitsi nɔ ni mikɛgbala lɛ kɛba miŋɔɔ ni mibi lɛ ni wɔye gbɛkɛ niyenii. Trukaa lɛ, misusuuu Daisy kɛ Gatsby he dɔŋŋ, shi moŋ nɛkɛ gbɔmɔ ni he tse, ni wa, ni bɛ husu, ni kɛ jeŋ muu fɛɛ yiŋkɔshikɔshifeemɔ tsuɔ nii, ni ekɛ ehe fɔ̃ɔ sɛɛ kɛ miishɛɛ yɛ minine lɛ mli pɛ lɛ he. Wiemɔ kuku ko bɔi tswaa yɛ mitoi amli kɛ miishɛɛ ko ni haa mɔ tsui nyɔɔ emli: “Mɛi ni atiuɔ amɛ, mɛi ni atiuɔ amɛ, mɛi ni bɛ deka, kɛ mɛi ni etɔ amɛ lɛ pɛ yɔɔ.”

Jordan wie huhuuhu etsɔɔ mi akɛ: “Ni esa akɛ Daisy aná nɔ ko yɛ eshihilɛ mli.”

“Ani eesumɔ ni ena Gatsby?”

"Esaaa akɛ eleɔ he sane. Gatsby sumɔɔɔ ni eleɔ. Esa akɛ ofɔ̃ lɛ nine kɛba tii kɛkɛ."

Wɔho tsei ni tsuɔ tamɔ gbɛtsii nii ko he, ni no sɛɛ lɛ Fifty-Ninth Street hiɛ, ni ji kane ni mli tseee ni yɔɔ mlɛo lɛ, kpɛ kɛba shi kɛtee park lɛ mli. Nɔ ni tamɔɔɔ Gatsby kɛ Tom Buchanan lɛ, mibɛ gbekɛ yoo ko ni ehiɛ ni bɛ gbɔmɔtso lɛ shwieɔ ŋwɛitsu ni yɔɔ duŋ kɛ okadii ni haa mɔ shwilaa lɛ anɔ, ni no hewɔ lɛ mifo gbekɛ yoo ni yɔɔ mimasɛi lɛ mli, ni mikɛ miniji tswa. Enaabu ni yɔɔ ŋmɔlɔŋmɔlɔ ni yɔɔ heguɔgbee lɛ ŋmɔ, ni no hewɔ lɛ migbala lɛ kɛbɛŋkɛ mi ekoŋŋ, shi nɛɛ kɛ mihiɛ.
