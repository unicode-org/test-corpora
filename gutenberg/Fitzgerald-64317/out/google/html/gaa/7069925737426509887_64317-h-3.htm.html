Beni Gatsby he miishɛɛ ni ayɔɔ lɛ mli wa fe fɛɛ lɛ ji be mli ni kanei ni yɔɔ eshia lɛ nyɛɛɛ atsu nii Hɔgbaa gbɛkɛ ko—ni, taakɛ eje shishi yɛ gbɛ ni bɛ faŋŋ nɔ lɛ, enitsumɔ akɛ Trimalchio lɛ eba naagbee. "Jɔɔmɔ ni jara wa lɛ! Ani nyɛ ná powda yɛ oyitsɔi momo ni yɔɔ ŋmɔŋ lɛ nɔ? Te shi bianɛ, ni okɛɛ—Te-o-ofee tɛŋŋ."

“Ejaakɛ onyɛ miisumɔ ni ejie ohe kpo.” Ehiɛ shwie shi kɛtee tsitsi bibioo yɛŋ lɛ mli ŋmɔlɔ kome lɛ mli. "Olamɔ, bo. Olamɔ bibioo kwraa."

Amɛhiŋmɛii kpe, ni amɛfee ekome kɛkwɛ amɛhe, amɛ pɛ amɛyɔɔ ŋwɛi. Ekɛ mɔdɛŋbɔɔ kwɛ okpɔlɔ lɛ shi.

“Mikɛ mihe fɔ̃ɔɔ enɔ, shwɛmɔ momo.”

“Be enyiɛ obaamɛ?”

"Gbi gbɛkɛ muu lɛ fɛɛ, kɛ ehe miihia. Bɔ fɛɛ bɔ ni fee lɛ, kɛyashi amɛ fɛɛ amɛbaawɔ."

Susumɔ hee ko ba mijwɛŋmɔ mli. Ŋɔɔ lɛ akɛ Tom na akɛ Daisy ji mɔ ni kudɔ tsɔne. Ekolɛ ebaasusu akɛ ena tsakpaa ko yɛ mli—ebaanyɛ esusu nɔ fɛɛ nɔ he. Mikwɛ shia lɛ mli; samfɛji enyɔ loo etɛ ni kpɛɔ yɛ shishigbɛ lɛ kɛ la ni tsoɔ ni ji pinki ni jɛɔ Daisy tsu lɛ mli yɛ shishigbɛ lɛ.

Mikɛɛ lɛ akɛ: “Omɛ yɛ biɛ.” “Makwɛ kɛji basabasafeemɔ he okadi ko yɛ.”

Miku misɛɛ kɛtee jwɛi lɛ husu lɛ naa, ni mifo ŋmeii lɛ amli blɛoo, ni mikɛ minaji tsɔ veranda lɛ atswere lɛ nɔ. Agbele tsu lɛ mli muji lɛ, ni mina akɛ tsu lɛ mli efee efolo. Beni mifoɔ kpoku lɛ nɔ he ni wɔye niyenii yɛ nakai June gbɛkɛ lɛ nyɔji etɛ ni eho lɛ, mibashɛ kane bibioo ko ni misusu akɛ eji niyelihe lɛ samflɛ lɛ he. Agbala shwilafoi lɛ, shi mina ŋã ko yɛ tsɔne lɛ sɛɛ.

Daisy kɛ Tom tara amɛhe yɛ ŋwɛi okpɔlɔ lɛ he, ni tsina loo ni ashã ni mli ejɔ tsɛnsi ko kã amɛteŋ, kɛ ale tui enyɔ. Ekɛ hiɛdɔɔ miiwie yɛ okpɔlɔ lɛ sɛɛ kɛmiikwɛ lɛ, ni yɛ ehiɛdɔɔ mli lɛ, enine egbee lɛ diɛŋtsɛ enine nɔ ni eha nɔ. Shikome yɛ be kɛ bei amli lɛ, ewoɔ ehiɛ nɔ ekwɛɔ lɛ ni ekɛ ehiɛ tsɔɔ akɛ ekpɛlɛɔ nɔ.

Amɛmiishɛɛɛ amɛhe, ni amɛteŋ mɔ ko mɔ ko etaaa tsina loo ale lɛ he—ni kɛlɛ amɛmiishɛɛɛ amɛhe hu. Adebɔɔ mli bɔ ni bɛŋkɛ kpaakpa lɛ he kɔɔyɔɔ ko ni anyɛɛɛ atsake lɛ yɛ mfoniri lɛ he, ni kulɛ mɔ fɛɛ mɔ baakɛɛ akɛ amɛmiifee ekome kɛmiikpa gbee.

Beni mikɛ mihe tsɔɔ ŋwɛitsu lɛ nɔ lɛ minu ni mi-taksi lɛ miiho gbɛ ni yɔɔ duŋ lɛ nɔ kɛmiiya shia lɛ he. Gatsby miimɛ he ni mishi lɛ yɛ tsɔne lɛ mli lɛ.

“Ani jɛmɛ fɛɛ efee dioo?” ebi kɛ haomɔ.

“Hɛɛ, fɛɛ efee dioo.” Mishashao shi. “Ehi akɛ obaa shia ni owɔ fioo.”

Ewoso eyitso.

"Miisumɔ ni mamɛ yɛ biɛ kɛyashi Daisy baawɔ. Gbi kpakpa, shwɛmɔ momo."

Ekɛ eniji wo ekotoku kotokui lɛ amli ni eku esɛɛ kɛ miishɛɛ kɛtee shia lɛ mlikwɛmɔ mli, tamɔ nɔ ni mihe ni miyɔɔ lɛ miifite krɔŋkrɔŋfeemɔ ni yɔɔ ŋmɛlɛtswaa lɛ mli lɛ. No hewɔ lɛ, mishi lɛ ni edamɔ jɛmɛ yɛ nyɔŋtsere la lɛ mli—ni ekwɛɛɛ nɔ ko nɔ ko.
