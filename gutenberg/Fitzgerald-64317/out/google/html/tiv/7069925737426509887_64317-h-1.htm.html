III

Yange i wa icam hen ya u orwanndor a mo tugh mbu uraahumbe la. Ken sule na u bluu la nomso man mbayevkasev lu van shi yemen er ka mngerem nahan hen atô u mngerem ma man man man champagne man asan. Shighe u mngerem ma lu nôôn kpishi atetan yô, m nenge mbavanya nav ve lu nyôron shin mnger sha ityough ki tso na u taven sha la, shin ve lu tôôn iyange sha mngerem ma tseen ma hen kpezegemnger na la, shighe u utso nav mba uhar mba ve lu a mato la ve lu eren mngerem ma shin kpezegemnger u Sound la, ve lu tôôn ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér aquaplanes la sha ajiir a i yer ér cataracts of foam la. Sha iyange i memen kasua yô, mato na u i yer ér Rolls-Royce la hingir mato u i yer ér omnibus la, u tôôn ior zan ken gar la shi hiden hen atô u ahwa ataankaranyiin pepe zan zan shighe u a kar atôutu la, kpa mato na u i yer ér station wagon la yô, lu yevese yevese er ka ikyagh ki yellow ki ki lu yevese fele fele nahan, sha u za kohol igirgi cii. Shi sha iyange i Tomataan yô, mbatomov anigheni, kua or u eren sule ugen kpaa, yange ve er tom kpoghuloo iyange jimin cii sha u ôôn akaa man ikyav mbi ôôn akaa man ikyav mbi ôôn sule man ikyav mbi sôron sule, sôron akaa a yange vihi hen tugh mbu i kar la.

Hanma iyange i Tomataan yô, ikyav mbi ôron man lemon mbi utaankarunyiin mbi va hen or u teen atam a ikyon ken gar u New York—hanma iyange i Tomataan yô, ôron man lemon mban je ve due sha hunda u ken ijime na ken pyramid u avegher a a lu a ikyav mbi eren tom ga la. Ikyekye igen lu ken iyou i yan kwagh, i ia dugh mngerem ma ikyon i ôron deri uhar ken atô u ihwa i môm yô, aluer or u nengen sha iyou i yan kwagh la kende button u cuku kwa deri uhar yô.

Ka a shi nan kpa, kwa môm ken kasua uhar yô, ikyumutya i mba eren kwaghyan yange i va a ikyav mbi eren tom a mi mbi lun angahar uderi imôngo man akaa a lun a ikyav mbi doon tsung a a kuma u eren kon u Kirimishi u zege sule u Gatsby la. Sha tebul u buffet, u i cir un sha kwaghyan u i yer ér hors-d’oeuvre la, i iv a ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér harlequin la, kua ikyav mbi tswamev mbi i yer ér pastry pigs man turkeys la. Ken iyou i vesen la, i ver ijiir i man msôrom i i lu a ikyagh ki esen iyou ki brass kpôô kpôô yô, shi i iv a msôrom ma man ma i yer ér gins man liquors la, kua msôrom ma man ma i yer ér cordials, ma i hungur ve ica i gbe je yô, mbavanya nav mba kasev kpishi lu iyev kpishi, nahan ve kera fa orgen ga.

Shighe u i lu nôngon u kuhwan ahwa ataankarahar la, annongo u kuhwan ityogholough la va, lu kwagh u doon tsung u a lu a ityogholough mbi utaankarunyiin ga, kpa lu ityogholough mbi kuhwan mbi i yer ér oboes man trombones man saxophones man viols man cornets man piccolos, man ityogholough mbi kuhwan mbi kiriki man mbi vesen la. Mbayev mba masetyô mba ve lu ôôn mngerem la va nyôr hen kpezegemnger hegen, mba eren hwe sha ityough ki iyou la; mato mba ve dugh ken gar u New York la mba veren mato utaankarunyiin ken atô u mato la, man hegen je yô, ayou a mkohol man ajiir a sôron iyol man ajiir a eren iniongo la cii nga a ikyav mbi doon tsung, shi i cir ice sha igbenda i he i kpilighyol, shi i cir ikyondo i cir sha ityou i i hembe mnyam ma ken tar u Castile la. Ijiir i man msôrom la ngi eren tom kpoghuloo, shi msôrom ma man ma ma lu yeghen sha mnger la ma samber ken sule la ken won, zan zan ahumbe a hingir u lun uma a ôron kwagh man u eren akaa a doon tsung, man akaaôron a sha inja ga man akaa a hiin kwaghôron a ior ve hungur hen ijiir la, man mbamkombo mba sha gbashima hen atô u kasev mba ve lu a vande fan ati a ayol a ve ga la.

Ior mba kuhwan ityogholough mbara mba hemban wanger er tar u lu yevese yemen ica a iyange la, nahan hegen yô, ior mba kuhwan ityogholough mbara mba kuhwan ityogholough mbi yellow cocktail, shi ityogholough mbi kuhwan mbi i yer ér opera of voices la kpa mbi kende a ki sha. Ka i hemba lun ican ga u seer miniti sha miniti, u iv a mkighir, u ôron kwagh u saan iyol. Ikpelaior la ka i hemba geman fese, i iv a mba ve sember van la, i cir shi i hingir ken ahumbe môm; ior mba zenden sha ajiir kposo kposo mba, mbayevkasev mba lun a vangertiôr mba ve eren tom heen man heen hen atô u mba ve hembe lun a iyol i kpeghee shi ve hembe lun a mshi ga la, ve hingir hen atô u nongo ugen sha anshighe kpuaa, maa ve lu a iember sha mhembe, ve gba yevese ken zegemnger u a lu geman ishigh man imo man mlu ve sha ikyev i iwanger i i lu geman hanma shighe la.

Fese nahan, môm ken mbayevkasev mban, u a lu a ikyondo i opal i tenger la, kôr msôrom ma man sha ahumbe, haa ma shin inya sha u zuan a ishimataver, maa a cir ave na er Frisco nahan, a gba eren iniumbe tswen tswen sha ikyônough ki torough ki i er sha ikyondo i i yer ér canvas la. A hush sha anshighe kpuaa; orhemen u orchestra la ka a gema icam na sha ci na, shi ior ve gba ôron kwagh sha mi kpishi er loho u aiegh u lu samber ér ka un a lu Gilda Gray u a lu henen kwagh a na ken annongo u Follies la yô. Pati la hii u eren.

M na jighjigh mer hen tugh mbu hiihii mbu m za hen ya u Gatsby la m lu môm ken mbavanya mba kpuaa mba i lôhô ve jighilii yô. Yange i lôhô ior ga—ve za her. Ve nyôr mato mba ve due a ve ken won ve yem a ve ken tar u Long Island la, man sha gbenda ugen yô, ve kure sha hunda u Gatsby. Mba za nyer ker yô, orgen u nan fa Gatsby yô, va pase ve kwagh, nahan ve been yô, ve er kwagh sha atindi a ieren a ior ve eren hen ijiir i ior ve memen her la. Ashighe agen yange ve va ve yem a u ve lu a zua a Gatsby ga cii, ve va sha ci u iniongo la a ishima i legh legh i i lu tikeeti u nyôron ker la.

Yange i lôhôm jighilii. Or u nahan mato ugen u yange hua ikyondo i robin’s-egg blue yô, yange per ikyônough yagh ki torough sha iyange i Sati la pepe pepe, a washika u kpilighyol u orvesen na nger ér: icivir la ia lu i Gatsby jimin cii, i kaa ér aluer m za “iniongo na i kiriki” la hen tugh mbura yô. Yange nenge a mo acin imôngo, shi yange wa ishima u van hen a mo ica i gba cii ve mase van ye, kpa akaa a kpilighyol a yange er la yange un—yange Jay Gatsby wa uwegh sha mi, sha uwegh ku civirigh.

M hua akondo a pupuu a i yer ér flanels la, m za hen ijiir na i eren tom la shighe u ahwa ataankarahar kar kpuaa yô, m lu zenden m lu zungwen kpishi hen atô u ior mba m fe ve ga la—shin er heen man heen lu ishigh ki m nenge a mi ken igirgi i purugh i i lu zan tom la nahan kpaa. Yange m nenge a iyenge i agumaior a ken tar u Ingila a a lu sha ajiir kposo kposo la kpiligh mo iyol fese je; ve cii ve wuha iyol tsembelee, ve cii ve lu inja er ijen kôr ve kpuaa nahan, shi ve cii ve lu lamen a ior mba ken tar u Amerika mba ve taver iyol shi ve lu a inyaregh kpishi la sha imo i doon ga. Yange m fa dedoo mer ve lu teen kwagh: ka bond shin insurance shin mato. Yange ve fa kwagh u inyaregh ki ki lu ican ga la sha gbenda u vihin tsung, nahan ve na jighjigh ér ka ve sha ci u mkaanem kpuaa ma i nger sha ikyav i vough la.

Er m va nyôr yô, m nôngo mer me ker or u nan lu ngohol mo la, kpa ior mba uhar shin utar mba m pine ve mer ve lu la, ve kenger mo sha ishi sha gbenda u kpilighyol, shi ve venda ér ve fa kwagh u a lu zan hemen la ga, nahan m gba yevese m yem sha tebul u man msôrom la—ka ijiir i môm tseegh ken sule i or môm tseegh nana nenge a mi yô.

M lu zan u za man msôrom ma man kpishi sha ci u kunya kôron mo kpishi, maa Jordan Baker due ken iyou la, tile sha ityough ki kpekpe u i er sha awen a i yer ér marble la, a cir ishigh kpuaa, lu kenger shin inya ken sule la sha isharen i vihin tu.

M ngohol mo shin m ngohol mo ga kpa, m nenge mer gba u me kôr iyol yam vea orgen cii ve me hii u ôron mba ve lu karen sha gbenda la kwagh u kundu kundu ye.

"Msugh u!" M gba ôron kwagh genger genger, m lu zan hemen u van hen a na. Imo yam yange i lu inja er ngi ôron kwagh sha inja ga nahan, sha kpe sule la.

Yange m va yô, a gema a kaa ér: “Yange m hen mer alaghga u ngu heen.” “M umbur er yange u lu hen kpe ya u—”

Yange kôrom uwegh sha inja ga, er ka ityendezwa ér una nenge sha a mo ken miniti môm nahan, shi a na mbayevkasev uhar mba ve wuha iyol sha akondo a yellow ahar la ato, mba ve tile hen ityough ki step la.

"Msugh u!" ve vaa imôngo. “Sorry u hemba ga.”

Kwagh la lu sha ci u iniumbe i golf la. Yange hemba ken anumbe a masetyô kasua u a kar la.

Wankwase ugen ken mbayevkasev mba ve hua ikyondo i yellow la kaa ér: “Ú fa mba se lu ga, kpa se zua a we heen er uwer môm nahan hegen.”

Jordan kaa ér: “U cir ice you hii shighe la je,” nahan m hii u ôron kwagh, kpa mbayevkasev mbara za hemen sha inja ga, nahan kwaghôron na la lu sha kwagh u uwer u u lu a mar ga la, u i er er kwaghyan u aikighe nahan, a shi nan kpa, i due a mi ken ikyagh ki or u nan eren kwaghyan la. Se mba senen sha kpekpe u iyou la, se gba zenden ken sule la, er uwegh ku Jordan ku ku lu er ka zenaria nahan la ku lu sha uwegh agh yô. Iyôgh ki msôrom ki i yer ér cocktails la yange ki cir sha a vese ken ato utu, nahan se tema hen tebul vea mbayevkasev mba uhar mba ve wuha iyol sha ikyondo i yellow la man nomsombaiorov utar, hanmô ve pase se ér ka Mr. Mumble.

“Ka u va iniongo ne ashighe kpishi kpa?” ka kwagh u Jordan pine wankwase u lu hen kpe na la je la.

Wankwase la na mlumun sha imo i tesen ér un ngu a vangertiôr ér: “I masetyô la lu i m zua a we her la.” A gema a kenger kwase u lu vea na la ér: “Ka we Lucille yange er nahan ga he?”

Yange lu sha ci u Lucille kpaa.

Lucille kaa ér: “M soo u van. "Mayange kwagh u m eren gbam kwagh ga, nahan hanma shighe yô, m lu a shighe u ember. Shighe u m lu heen u masetyô la m cir ikyondo yam i cir sha ikyonough, maa a pinem iti yam man adereshi wam—ken atô u kasua môm tseegh m zua a ikyagh ki eren kwagh a mi hen Croirier ki ki lu a ikyondo i he i aikighe ker yô."

“U ngu a mi kpa?” Jordan pine nahan.

"Sha mimi yô, yange m er nahan. Yange m lu zan u huan un tugh mbu nyian, kpa yange a kehe kpishi ken ityou nahan gba u a gema un. Yange lu a ikyav mbi lavender mbi i yer ér gas blue la. Dollar deri uhar man akunduatargber kar ataan."

Wankwase ugen la kaa sha gbashima ér: “Kwagh u doon tsung ngu u or u nana er imba kwagh ne yô.” “A soo ér ma zayol a lu a ma iyol ga.”

“Ka an nan fe ga?” M pine kwagh.

"Gatsby. Orgen kaa a mo er—"

Mbayevkasev mba uhar mbara man Jordan yange ve suur sha ayol a ve ken myer.

“Orgen kaa a mo ér ve hen ér yange wua or kwa môm.”

Kwagh u doon tsung yange kar sha a vese cii. Mbahemenev mba utar mba Mr. Mumbles mbara gure inya, ve kegh ato a na sha gbashima.

Lucille gba anyiman sha akperan ér: “M hen mer ka kwagh u vesen ga .” “Kwagh u yange er yô, lu or u eren tom u sôron tar u Jamani shighe u i lu nôngon ityav la.”

Orgen ken nomsombaiorov mbara kende uwegh sha sha u tesen ér kwagh la ka mimi.

A taver se ishima sha gbenda u dedoo ér: “Yange m ungwa kwagh ne hen or u nan fe kwagh na cii, u nan vese vea na ken tar u Jamani yô.”

Wankwase u hiihii la kaa ér: “Oh, ei, a lu nahan ga, sha ci u yange lu ken shoja i tar u Amerika shighe u i lu nôngon ityav la.” Er jighjigh wase u nan la hide hen a na yô, a cir ishigh sha gbashima. “Ka u kenger un ashighe agen shighe u a hen ér or môm nan ngu kenger un ga yô. Me wa zwa mer yange wua or.”

A kende ashe sha, maa a tenger. Lucille yange gba ôron kwagh. Se cii se gema se kenger sha ajiir cii se lu keren Gatsby. Ka shiada u kwagh u dooshima u yange mgbegha ior la ér, ior mba ve zough a kwagh u i gbe u a ôr sha mi kpuaa tseegh ken tar ne la, lu ôron kwagh na sha mciem.

Kwaghyan u aikie u hiihii la—u una lu ugen shighe u a bee tugh mbu il la—yange i lu yan un hegen, nahan Jordan lôhôm ér m za nyôr nongo na, u u lu samber sha tebul ugen hen kpe sule la. Ior mba ve vese ayol a ve la lu iorov utar man or u nan lu a Jordan imôngo la, lu wanyemakeranta u nan lu zan makeranta u vesen u nan lu eren akaa a vihin tu yô, shi ikyav tese wang ér yange nan lu a mhen ér ica a gba ga tsô Jordan una na un iyol na sha gbenda u vesen shin u kiriki. Sha ityough ki eren kwagh sha inja ga yô, pati ne yange kura mlu u lun kwagh môm u a lu a icivir yô, shi tôô tom u tilen sha ityough ki mbahemenev mba ken agar mba ve lu her la—East Egg lu hiden a iyol ijime sha West Egg shi lu kuran iyol na sha kwagh u mlu u ken igbar u i nengen a mi la.

Jordan yange ôr kwagh sha zwa u hôngorough ér: “Se due nen kera,” shighe u a er tom u kuman ihwa i môm sha gbenda ugen, u a lu sha inja ga la; “kwagh ne ka kwagh u doon mo kpishi.”

Se mough, maa a pase se ér se mba zan u za keren or u nan lu ngohol se la: A kaa ér m ngu a vande zuan a na ga, nahan kwagh ne lu nan mo ishimanyian. Or u nan lu henen makeranta la kende uwegh sha sha gbenda u vihin tu, u vihin tu.

Ijiir i man msôrom i se hii kenger la, iv a ior, kpa Gatsby lu her ga. Yange zua a na sha ityough ki ikyônough ki torough kira ga, shi lu sha veranda la kpaa ga. Shighe ugen la se nôngo se zua a hunda ugen u u lu inja er ka kwagh u vesen nahan yô, se nyôr ken iyou i koson ityakerada i i yer ér Gothic la, i i cir i sha kon u oak u ken tar u Ingila yô, shi alaghga i tôô i cii i yem a mi hen ijiir igen i i vihi ken tar ugen yô.

Orgen u lun a mshi iyol, u lun anyom pue kar ataankaranyiin, u lun a zege ikyav mbi nengen a mi mbi lun er ka iyôngo nahan yô, tema sha kpe u zege tebul, lu kenger sha ajiir a i veren ityakerada la sha ishimavihin. Er se lu nyôron yô, a gba zenden sha akôv a iember shi nengen Jordan hii sha ityou zan zan angahar.

“Ka nyi u hen?” yange pine kwagh sha ishimavihin.

“Sha kwagh u nyi?”

A kende uwegh sha ajiir a i ver ityakerada la.

"Sha kwagh la. Sha mimi yô, gba u ú vihi ishima u fan ga. M fa. Ve mba kpôô kpôô."

“Ityakerada la kpa?”

A kende uwegh sha.

"Ka akaa a mimi je—nga a upeeji man hanma kwagh cii. Yange m hen mer vea lu kaade u doon tsung u una tsa yô. Sha mimi yô, ka akaa a mimi je. Upeeji man—Heen! Lemme tese u."

Er yange nenge ér se mba a akperan sha kwagh ga yô, a gba yevese zan hen ajiir a i veren ityakerada la, a hide a Volume One u Stoddard Lectures la .

"Nenge!" a vaa sha mhembe. "Ka kwagh u i gber sha mimi yô. Yange tsume a mo. Or ne ka Belasco u hanma shighe. Ka mhembe. Ka nyi i lu vough! Ka nyi i lu kwagh u mimi! Yange fa shighe u una de u eren kwagh la kpaa—yange cir upeeji mba takerada la ga. Kpa ka nyi u soo? Ka nyi u ver ishima wer?"

A ngohol takerada la hen a mo, a gema a ver u sha ikyônough ki torough ki u lu a mi la fese, lu ôron kwagh sha mciem ér aluer i dugh briki môm kera yô, iyou i koson ityakerada la cii ia gba.

“Ka an nan va a we?” yange pine ér i er nahan. "Shin u va tseegh? I va a mo. Ior kpishi i va a ve."

Jordan kenger un sha ishi, saan un iyol, kpa na un mlumun ga.

A za hemen ér: “Kwase ugen u i yilan un ér Roosevelt yô, yange va a mo. "Kwase u Claud Roosevelt. U fa un kpa? M zua a na hen ijiir igen tugh mbu i kar la. M ma msôrom kuma er kasua môm nahan hegen, nahan m hen mer alaghga kwagh ne una na m tema ken iyou i koson ityakerada."

“Ka nyi?”

"Kwagh kpuaa, m hen. M fa ga. M ngu heen ihwa i môm tseegh. M ôr u kwagh u ityakerada la kpa? Ka mimi. Ka—"

“U kaa a vese.”

Se suse un uwegh sha ishimavihin, se hide se due ken won.

Ior lu eren iniumbe hegen sha canvas u ken sule la; mbabeenyol mba tuur mbayevkasev mba kiriki ken ijime ken ikyasen i lun a mrumun u sha mhôôn gbem sha won, mbaukwaav mba ve hembe mbagenev la mba kôron ayol a ve sha inja, sha inja, shi mba lun ken atô u akôv—man mbayevkasev mba lun kwav kpishi mba ve lu wan icam asange asange shin mba nan or u wan icam la msurshima sha anshighe kpuaa sha ikyav mbi banjo shin ikyav mbi utyaav mbi i yer ér traps la. Shighe u iyange i lu nôngon u been la, mciem mara seer a seer. Orgen u i yer iti na ér tenor yô, yange wa icam ken zwa Italia, shi or u i yer iti na ér contralto la kpa wa icam ken zwa jazz, man hen atô u nombor la ior lu eren “akaa a doon tsung” ken sule la cii, shighe u ior lu eren akaa a doon tsung, a a lu gbilinkwagh la, ve lu moughon sha kwavaôndo sha shighe u uraahumbe la. Mbayevkasev mbagenev mba ve lu mbayevkasev mba ve wuha iyol sha yellow la, yange ve er kwagh u wanye u kiriki ken akondo, shi i na ve champagne ken ikyav mbi vesen mbi i yer ér finger-bowls la. Uwer yange u mough sha, man ikyav mbi azurfa mbi i yer ér triangle la mbi lu yevese ken Sound, mbi lu tenger kpuaa sha ci u mngerem ma banjo ma ma lu sha ikyônough ki torough ki ki lu a mshi ga la.

Yange m lu vea Jordan Baker her. Se tema hen tebul vea orgen u lu er mo nahan, kua wankwase u kiriki ugen u a eren ayôôso yô, u yange una vihi un ishima kpuaa tseegh kpa, a gba eren kwagh u una kôôm un ga yô. Yange m lu ember iyol yam hegen. Yange m ma msôrom ma champagne ma i yer ér finger-bowls la ahar, nahan kwagh u m nenge la gema sha ishigh yagh hingir kwagh u vesen, u a lu kwagh u hiihii, shi a lu kwagh u vesen yô.

Shighe u i lu eren iemberyolough la, or la kenger mo shi seer ôron kwagh a mo.

A kaa a mo sha icivir ér: “Ishe you ngi a inja.” “Yange u lu ken Ikyumutya i Hiihii shighe u i lu nôngon ityav la ga he?”

"Er nan ve een. Yange m lu ken shoja i sha angahar i sha 28 la."

"M lu ken Sixteenth zan zan Jun pue kar anigheni-pue kar anigheni. M fa je mer m vande nengen a we hen ijiir igen."

Se lam kpuaa sha kwagh u agar a kiriki agen a a lu a mngerem shi a lu a mngerem kpishi ken tar u France yô. Ikyav tese ér yange lu hen ijiir ne, gadia a kaa a mo ér un sember yamen igirgi i purugh i i zenden sha mnger yô, nahan una za kar igirgi ne pepe.

"U soo u zan vea mo, anumbe a tse? Ka ikyua a kpezegemnger hen kpezegemnger u Sound la."

“Ka shighe u nyi?”

“Hanma shighe u a hembe doon we cii.”

Yange lu sha ityough ki nombor wam u pinen iti na yô, Jordan kenger sha ajiir cii, maa a ôr kwagh a mo.

“U lun a shighe u gay hegen kpa?” a pine nahan.

“I hemba doon cii.” M hide m gema m nenge a or u m fe nan u he la. "Kwagh ne ka iniongo i kpilighyol hen a mo. M ngu a nenge a or u nan lu ngohol mo la je kpaa ga. M ngu hen ijiir la—" M kende uwegh sha kpekpe u i nengen a mi ga la ken ica, "man or ne Gatsby tindi or u nahan mato na la a takerada u lôhôn un."

Shighe kar kpuaa yô, a kenger mo inja er un kav kwagh ga nahan.

A kaa a mo sha mciem ér: “M ngu Gatsby.”

"Ka nyi!" M kaa mer. “Oh, m zamber we.”

"M hen mer u fa, anumbe a tse. M cian mer m ngu or u ngohol ior dedoo ga."

Yange ôr kwagh a mo sha mkav—kwagh ne hemba u kaven kwagh la ica je. Yange lu môm ken akaa a doon tsung a ior ve fe u eren ga, a a lu a mlu u nan ishimasurun gbem sha won ker la, a alaghga u va zua a mi kwa anyiin shin ataan ken uma wou yô. Yange tagher a—shin lu inja er ngu tagher ishigh a—tar u tsôron la cii sha anshighe kpuaa, maa a ver ishima sha a we a mnenge u kpeegh u or a kera fetyô u hendan a mi ga yô, sha u nan we ikyo. Yange kav u vough er u soo wer i kav u nahan, shi na u jighjigh er u soo wer i na iyol you jighjigh nahan, shi na u jighjigh ér ngu a mnenge u vough sha kwagh wou u, aluer u er sha afatyô wou cii, u lu veren ishima wer u pase la. Hen shighe la jighilii yange i bee kera—man m lu kenger wanyekwase ugen u lun a ityough ki doon tsung, u lu anyom ahar shin uhar hemba anyom akunduatargber yô, u mlu na u ôron kwagh sha inja la yange lu kwagh u injaa ga yô. Shighe ugen cii man a pase iyol na yô, m nenge mer ngu tsuan mkaanem nam sha inja.
