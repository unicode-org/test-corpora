VII

Ko e taimi naʻe ʻi hono tuʻunga māʻolunga tahá ai ʻa e fie ʻilo kia Ketisipií naʻe ʻikai ke ʻalu ai ʻa e ngaahi maama ʻi hono falé ʻi ha pō Tokonaki ʻe taha —pea, ʻo hangē pē ko e fakapoʻuli naʻe kamata aí, naʻe ʻosi ʻene ngāue ko Tilimalikió. Naʻe toki faifai pē peá u ʻiloʻi ko e ngaahi meʻalele naʻa nau liliu ʻamanaki ki heʻene fakaʻulí naʻa nau nofo ʻi ha miniti pē ʻe taha pea nau toki fakaʻuli sulkily ʻo ʻalu. ʻI heʻeku fifili pe ʻokú ne puké ne u ʻalu atu ʻo ʻiloʻi —naʻe sio fakamamaʻu mai ha tangata tauhi fale taʻe maheni mo ha fofonga kovi mei he matapaá.

“ʻOku puke ʻa Misa Ketisipi?”

"Ikai." Hili ha kiʻi tuʻu naʻá ne tānaki atu ʻa e “taʻeiki” ʻi ha founga fakatuai mo fakaʻiseʻisa.

“Naʻe teʻeki ai ke u sio kiate ia ʻi he tafaʻakí, pea naʻá ku kiʻi hohaʻa. Talaange kiate ia naʻe haʻu ʻa Misa Kālei.”

"Ko hai?" naʻá ne fie maʻu taʻefakaʻapaʻapa.

“Kāla.”

“Carraway. Sai, te u talaange ki ai.”

Naʻe fakafokifā pē ʻene pāʻusiʻi e matapaá.

Naʻe fakahā mai ʻe heʻeku Finní naʻe fakaʻatā ʻe Ketisi ʻa e kau tamaioʻeiki kotoa pē ʻi hono ʻapí ʻi ha uike ʻe taha kuohilí pea fetongi kinautolu ʻaki ha niʻihi kehe ʻe toko vaeua, ʻa ia naʻe ʻikai ke nau teitei ʻalu ki he kolo ʻo West Egg ke totongi fakafufū ʻe he kau fefakatauʻakí, ka naʻa nau ʻota ha ngaahi meʻatokoni fakafuofua ʻi he telefoní. Naʻe lipooti ʻe he tamasiʻi fakatau koloá ʻoku hangē ʻa e loki kuí ha fale puaká, pea ko e fakakaukau fakalūkufua ʻi he koló ʻoku ʻikai ʻaupito ko ha kau tamaioʻeiki ʻa e kakai foʻoú.

ʻI he ʻaho hono hokó naʻe telefoni mai ʻa Ketisi kiate au ʻi he telefoní.

“Ko e ʻalu atu?” Ko ʻeku fehuʻí ia.

“ʻIkai, sipoti motuʻa.”

“ʻOku ou fanongo naʻá ke tuli kotoa hoʻo kau tamaioʻeikí.”

“Naʻá ku fie maʻu ha taha ʻe ʻikai ke ne lauʻikoviʻi. ʻOku faʻa haʻu ʻa Teisi —ʻi he ngaahi hoʻataá.”

Ko ia kuo tō kotoa ʻa e fonongaʻangá ʻo hangē ha fale kaati ʻi he taʻefiemālie ʻi hono matá.

“Ko ha kakai kinautolu naʻe fie maʻu ʻe Wolfshiem ke fai ha meʻa maʻanautolu. Ko e fanga tokoua mo e fanga tuofāfine kotoa kinautolu. Naʻa nau faʻa fakalele ha kiʻi hōtele.”

"Oku ou sio."

Naʻá ne telefoni mai ʻi he kole ʻa Teisi —te u haʻu ʻo maʻu meʻatokoni hoʻatā ʻi hono ʻapí ʻapongipongi? ʻE ʻi ai ʻa Misa Peika. Hili ha vaeua houa mei ai naʻe telefoni mai ʻa Teisi tonu pea hangē naʻá ne fiemālie ʻi heʻene ʻiloʻi ʻoku ou haʻú. Naʻe ʻi ai ha meʻa naʻe ʻalu hake. Pea neongo ia naʻe ʻikai ke u tui te nau fili ʻa e taimi ko ʻení ki ha fakatātā —tautautefito ki he fakatātā ʻoku ʻikai fuʻu fakatupu loto-mamahi naʻe fakahokohoko ʻe Ketipi ʻi he ngoué.

Naʻe vela ʻa e ʻaho hono hokó, ʻo meimei ko e ʻaho fakaʻosi ia, ʻoku pau ko e māfana taha ia, ʻo e faʻahitaʻu māfaná. ʻI he ʻasi mai ʻeku lēlué mei he ʻaá ki he maama ʻo e laʻaá, ko e ngaahi ifi vela pē ʻa e Kautaha Pisikete Fakafonuá naʻá ne maumauʻi ʻa e fakalongolongo ʻoku ʻi he hoʻataá. Naʻe puna ʻa e ngaahi sea vao ʻo e kaá ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e velá; naʻe fufula maʻamaʻa ʻa e fefine ʻi hoku tafaʻakí ʻi ha kiʻi taimi ki hono kofukofu hinehina, pea hili iá, ʻi heʻene momoko ʻene nusipepá ʻi lalo ʻi hono ongo louhiʻi nimá, naʻá ne tō loto-moʻua ki ha vela loloto mo ha tangi fakaʻauha. Naʻe tō ʻene tohi kato ki he falikí.

“ʻOiauē, ʻeku!” naʻá ne mapuhoi.

Naʻá ku toʻo hake ia ʻi ha punou vaivai ʻo ʻoange kiate ia, ʻo u puke ia ʻi he lōloa ʻo e nimá pea ʻi he tumuʻaki ʻo e ngaahi tuliki ke fakahaaʻi ʻoku ʻikai haʻaku ngaahi tisaini ʻi aí —ka ko e tokotaha kotoa pē naʻe ofi maí, kau ai ʻa e fefiné, naʻa nau mahaloʻi au ʻo tatau pē.

"Vela!" ko e lau ia ʻa e taki ʻo e meʻalelé ki ha ngaahi fofonga maheni. “Ko ha ngaahi ‘ea!... Vela!... Vela!... Vela!... ‘Oku vela fe‘unga nai kiate koe? ‘Oku vela? ‘Oku... ?”

Naʻe foki mai ʻeku tikite feʻaveʻakí kiate au mo ha ʻuli ʻuliʻuli mei hono nimá. Koeʻuhí ke tokanga ha taha ʻi he vela ko ʻení pe ko hai naʻá ne ʻuma ki hono loungutu kulokula, ko hai naʻá ne hanga ʻe hono ʻulú ʻo fakamoʻui ʻa e kato pajama ʻi hono lotó!

... Naʻe fou mai ha matangi vaivai ʻi he holo ʻo e ʻapi ʻo e fāmili Pukaní, ʻo ne ʻave ʻa e leʻo ʻo e fafangu telefoní ki tuʻa kiate au mo Ketipi ʻi heʻema tatali ʻi he matapaá.

“Ko e sino ʻo e ʻeikí?” ko e ngungulu ia ʻa e tangata tauhí ki he ngutú. “Fakamolemole, madame, ka he ʻikai ke mau lava ʻo fakanaunau ia —ʻoku fuʻu vela ʻaupito ke ala ki he hoʻatā ko ʻení!”

Ko e meʻa naʻá ne leaʻaki moʻoní: “ʻIo... ʻIo... Te u sio.”

Naʻá ne tuku hifo ʻa e meʻa tali meʻalelé peá ne haʻu kiate kimaua, ʻo kiʻi ngingila, ke toʻo homa ngaahi hēkesi fefeka ʻo e vaó.

“ʻOku ʻamanaki atu ʻa Madame kiate koe ʻi he saloní!” naʻá ne tangi, ʻo ne fakahaaʻi taʻeʻaonga ʻa e fakahinohinó. ʻI he vela ko ʻení ko e ngaahi fakaʻilonga lahi ange kotoa pē ko ha fakaanga ia ki he falekoloa angamaheni ʻo e moʻuí.

Ko e lokí, naʻe ʻataʻatā lelei ʻaki ha ngaahi ʻā, naʻe fakapoʻuli mo momoko. Naʻe tokoto ʻa Teisi mo Sioatani ʻi ha fuʻu sofa lahi ʻaupito, ʻo hangē ha ongo ʻaitoli silivá ʻokú na fakamafasiaʻi hona ongo teunga hinehina pē ʻanauá ʻi he havili hiva ʻo e kau poupoú.

Naʻá na lea fakataha mai, “He ʻikai ke ma lava ʻo hiki.”

Naʻe mālōlō ʻa e ongo louhiʻi nima ʻo Sioataní, ʻa ia naʻe efuefu hinehina ʻi honau lanu hinehina, ʻi ha kiʻi taimi ʻi hoku louhiʻi nimá.

“Pea ko Misa Tōmasi Piukēnani, ko e tokotaha vaʻingá?” Ko ʻeku fehuʻí ia.

ʻI he taimi tatau pē ne u fanongo ki hono leʻó, ʻoku ngungu, fakalongolongo, fakaʻauʻauhifo, ʻi he telefoni ʻo e holó.

Naʻe tuʻu ʻa Ketisi ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e kāpeti kulokulá ʻo sio takai holo ʻaki ha mata fakaʻofoʻofa. Naʻe siofi ia ʻe Teisi ʻo ne kata, ko ʻene kata melie mo fakafiefiá; naʻe ʻalu hake ha kiʻi ʻuha efuefu mei hono fatafatá ki he ʻeá.

“Ko e talanoá,” ko e muhumuhu ia ʻa Sioataní, “ko e taʻahine ia ʻa Tomu ʻi he telefoní.”

Naʻá ma fakalongolongo pē. Naʻe maʻolunga hake ʻa e leʻo ʻi he holó ʻi he ʻita: “Sai ʻaupito, ko ia, he ʻikai ke u fakatau atu ʻa e kaá kiate koe ʻi ha meʻa... ʻOku ʻikai haʻaku ngaahi fatongia kiate koe ʻi ha meʻa... pea ko hoʻo fakahohaʻasi au fekauʻaki mo ia ʻi he taimi kai hoʻataá, he ʻikai ke u tuʻu ʻi he meʻa ko iá!”

“Ko hono pukepuke ʻo e meʻa talitalí,” ko e lea fakaanga ia ʻa Teisi.

“ʻIkai, ʻoku ʻikai,” ko ʻeku fakapapauʻi ange ia kiate iá. "Ko ha aleapau moʻoni ia. ʻOku hoko mai ʻeku ʻilo ki ai."

Naʻe fakaava ʻe Tomu ʻa e matapaá, ʻo ne taʻofi hono feituʻú ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti ʻaki hono sino matolu, peá ne hū fakavave atu ki he lokí.

“Misa Ketipi!” Naʻá ne mafao atu hono nima fōlahi mo fōlahi mo ha taʻefiemālie naʻe fufū lelei. “ʻOku ou fiefia ke sio kiate koe, tangataʻeiki... Nick...”

Naʻe tangi ʻa Teisi, “Ngaohi haʻamau inu momoko.”

ʻI heʻene toe mavahe mei he lokí naʻá ne tuʻu hake ʻo ʻalu atu kia Ketisi ʻo toho hifo hono fofongá, ʻo ne ʻuma ki hono ngutú.

Naʻá ne lāunga mai, “ʻOkú ke ʻiloʻi ʻoku ou ʻofa ʻiate koe.”

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe Siōtani, “ʻOku ngalo ʻiate koe ʻoku ʻi ai ha fineʻeiki ʻoku ʻi ai.”

Naʻe sio takai veiveiua ʻa Teisi.

“ʻOkú ke ʻuma foki kia Niki.”

“Ko ha ta‘ahine mā‘ulalo mo fakalieliá!”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u tokanga au!” ko e kaila ia ʻa Teisi, peá ne kamata ke ne fakaʻauha ʻa e afi pilikí. Peá ne manatuʻi leva ʻa e velá peá ne tangutu halaia ʻi he sofá ʻi he taimi pē naʻe hū mai ai ha neesi naʻe toki fufulu ʻo taki ha kiʻi taʻahine ki he lokí.

“Bless-sed pre-cious,” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻo ne mafao atu hono ongo nimá. “Haʻu ki hoʻo faʻē tonu ʻokú ne ʻofa ʻiate koé.”

Naʻe lele atu ʻa e tamasiʻí, ʻa ia naʻe tukuange ʻe he neesí, ʻo kolosi ʻi he lokí ʻo aka mā ʻi he teunga ʻo ʻene faʻeé.

"Ko e bles-sed pre-cious! Naʻe maʻu ʻe heʻeku faʻeé ha efuefu ʻi ho louʻulu kulokula motuʻá? Tuʻu hake he taimí ni, pea pehē —How-de-do."

Naʻá ku toe punou hifo mo Ketisi ʻo puke ʻa e kiʻi nima veiveiuá. Hili iá naʻá ne kei sio pē ki he tamasiʻí ʻi he ʻohovale. ʻOku ou tui naʻe ʻikai ke ne tui moʻoni ki hono ʻi aí kimuʻa.

“Naʻá ku teunga kimuʻa he maʻu meʻatokoni hoʻataá,” ko e lea ia ʻa e tamasiʻí, ʻo ne tafoki vēkeveke kia Teisi.

“Ko e meʻa ia he naʻe fie fakaʻaliʻali koe ʻe hoʻo faʻeé.” Naʻe punou hono fofongá ʻi he foʻi ngingila ʻe taha ʻo e kiʻi kia hinehina. "ʻOkú ke misi, ʻokú ke. ʻOkú ke kiʻi misi ʻaupito."

“ʻIo,” ko e fakahaaʻi nonga ia ʻe he kiʻi tamá. “Kuo tui foki ʻe ʻAuniti Siōtani ha teunga hinehina.”

“ʻOkú ke saiʻia fēfē ʻi he ngaahi kaungāmeʻa ʻo e faʻeé?” Naʻe fakatafoki ia ʻe Teisi ke ne fehangahangai mo Ketisipi. “ʻOkú ke pehē ʻoku nau fakaʻofoʻofa?”

“ʻOku ʻi fē ʻa Teti?”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke ne hangē ko ʻene tamaí,” ko e fakamatala ia ʻa Teisi. “ʻOkú ne hangē ko aú. ʻOkú ne maʻu hoku louʻulú mo e fōtunga ʻo e fofongá.”

Naʻe toe tangutu ʻa Teisi ʻi he sofá. Naʻe laka atu ʻa e neesí ʻo mafao atu hono nimá.

“Haʻu, Pemi.”

“Mālō e lelei, ʻofaʻanga!”

ʻI ha sio ki mui ʻoku veiveiua naʻe puke ʻe he tamasiʻi akoʻi leleí ʻa e nima ʻo ʻene neesí pea toho ia ki tuʻa mei he matapaá, ʻi he taimi pē naʻe foki mai ai ʻa Tomí, ʻo muʻomuʻa ʻi ha ngaahi gin rickey ʻe fā naʻe kiliki fonu ʻi he ʻaisí.

Naʻe toʻo hake ʻe Ketipi ʻene inú.

“ʻOku nau hā mālie moʻoni,” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻo ʻasi mai ʻa e loto-mafasiá.

Na‘á ma inu ‘i he ngaahi fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i manupuna lōloa mo mānumanu.

“Naʻá ku lau ʻi ha feituʻu ʻoku fakaʻau ke vela ange ʻa e laʻaá ʻi he taʻu kotoa pē,” ko e lea angaʻofa ia ʻa Tomu. “ʻOku hangē ʻoku ʻikai fuoloa kuo tō ʻa e māmaní ki he laʻaá —pe tatali ʻi ha miniti —ko e fehangahangai pē ia —ʻoku fakaʻau ke momoko ange ʻa e laʻaá ʻi he taʻu kotoa pē.

Naʻá ne fokotuʻu ange kia Ketisipi, “Haʻu ki tuʻa, ʻoku ou fie maʻu ke ke sio ki he feituʻú.”

Naʻá ku ʻalu mo kinaua ki tuʻa ki he velanitá. ʻI he Sound lanu mata, ʻoku tuʻu ʻi he velá, naʻe totolo māmālie atu ha kiʻi folau ʻe taha ki he tahi foʻou angé. Naʻe muimui ki ai ʻa e mata ʻo Ketisí ʻi ha kiʻi taimi nounou; naʻá ne hiki hake hono nimá ʻo tuhu ki he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo e matātahí.

“ʻOku ou ʻi he tafaʻaki tonu ʻo koé.”

“Ko ia ʻokú ke pehē.”

Naʻe hiki hake homa matá ki he ngaahi mohenga losé mo e ngoueʻanga velá mo e veve vao ʻo e ngaahi ʻaho ʻo e kulií ʻi he matāfangá. Naʻe ngaʻunu māmālie ʻa e kapakau hinehina ʻo e vaká ʻo fakafepakiʻi ʻa e ngataʻanga momoko lanu pulū ʻo e langí. Na‘e ‘i mu‘a ‘a e ‘oseni ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e ‘uhiki‘i ‘uhiki‘i ‘akaú mo e ngaahi motu tāpuekina lahi faú.

“ʻOku ʻi ai ha sipoti maʻau,” ko e lea ia ʻa Tomu ʻo ne luelue atu. “ʻOku ou saiʻia ke ʻi tuʻa mo ia ʻi ha houa nai ʻe taha.”

Naʻá ma maʻu meʻatokoni hoʻatā ʻi he loki kaí, ʻo fakapoʻuli foki ʻi he velá, pea inu hifo ʻi he fiefia neave mo e ʻale momokó.

“Ko e hā te tau fai kiate kitautolu he hoʻatā ní?” ko e kaila ia ʻa Teisi, “pea mo e ʻaho hili iá, mo e taʻu ʻe tolungofulu hono hokó?”

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe Sioatani, “ʻOua naʻá ke anga-fakamamahi.” “ʻOku toe kamata foʻou ʻa e moʻuí ʻi he taimi ʻoku ngingila ai ʻi he tōʻanga ʻo e taʻú.”

“Ka ʻoku fuʻu vela ʻaupito,” ko e fakamamafa ia ʻa Teisi, ʻi heʻene meimei tangí, “pea ʻoku fuʻu puputuʻu ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē. Tau ʻalu kotoa ki he koló!”

Naʻe feinga atu hono leʻó ʻi he velá, ʻo ne taaʻi ia, ʻo ne faʻu ʻene taʻeʻuhingá ki ha ngaahi fōtunga.

“Kuó u fanongo ki hono ngaohi ha fale tauʻanga meʻalele mei ha fale tauʻanga manu,” ko e lea ia ʻa Tomu kia Ketisipií, “ka ko e fuofua tangata au kuó ne ngaohi ha fale tauʻanga manu mei ha fale tauʻanga manu.”

“Ko hai ʻoku fie ʻalu ki koló?” ko e fiemaʻu fakaʻaiʻai ia ʻa Teisi. Naʻe lele atu ʻa e mata ʻo Ketipi kiate ia. “ʻĀ,” ko ʻene tangí ia, “ʻokú ke fōtunga mālie ʻaupito.”

Naʻe fetaulaki hona matá, peá na fesiofaki fakataha, ʻo na tokotaha pē ʻi he ʻataá. Naʻá ne sio hifo ʻi ha feinga ki he tēpilé.

Naʻá ne toe pehē mai, “ʻOkú ke hā fakaʻofoʻofa maʻu pē.”

Naʻá ne talaange ʻokú ne ʻofa ʻiate ia, pea naʻe sio ia ʻa Tomu Pukenani. Naʻá ne ofo. Naʻe kiʻi fakaava hono ngutú, peá ne sio kia Ketisi, peá ne toe sio atu kia Teisi ʻo hangē naʻá ne toki ʻiloʻi ia ko ha taha naʻá ne ʻiloʻi fuoloa.

“ʻOkú ke hangē ko e tuʻuaki ʻa e tangatá,” ko ʻene hoko atu ia ʻi he taʻehalaia. “ʻOkú ke ʻiloʻi ʻa e tuʻuaki ʻo e tangatá—”

“Sai,” ko e lea vave atu ia ʻa Tomí, “ʻOku ou loto fiemālie haohaoa ke u ʻalu ki he koló. Haʻu —ʻoku tau ʻalu kotoa ki he koló.”

Naʻá ne tuʻu hake, ʻoku kei ulo pē hono matá ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo Ketipi mo hono uaifí. Naʻe ʻikai ngaʻunu ha taha.

"ʻai ā foki!" Naʻe kiʻi mafesifesi ʻene ʻitá. “Ko e hā e meʻa ʻoku hokó, ʻi ha faʻahinga founga pē?Kapau te tau ʻalu ki kolo, tau kamata leva.”

Ko hono nimá, ʻi heʻene tetetete ʻi heʻene feinga ke mapuleʻi iá, naʻá ne ʻave ki hono loungutú ʻa e fakaʻosi ʻo ʻene ipu ʻelí. Naʻe ʻai kimaua ʻe he leʻo ʻo Teisií ke ma tuʻu hake ʻo ʻalu atu ki he hala makamaka velá.

“Te tau ʻalu pē?” naʻá ne fakafepakiʻi ia. “Hangē ko ʻení?ʻIkai te tau tuku ha taha ke ne ʻuluaki ifi sikaleti?”

“Naʻe ifi ʻa e tokotaha kotoa ʻi he kotoa ʻo e maʻu meʻatokoni hoʻataá.”

“ʻOiauē, tau fakafiefia,” ko ʻene kole ange ia kiate iá. “ʻOku fuʻu vela ke fuʻu longoaʻa.”

Naʻe ʻikai ke ne tali mai.

Naʻá ne pehē mai, “Ke ke fai ia ʻi hoʻo founga pē ʻaʻau.” “Haʻu, Sioatani.”

Naʻá na ʻalu hake ke teuteu lolotonga ia ʻoku mau tuʻu ʻi ai ʻa kimautolu kau tangata ʻe toko tolú ʻo fefiofi ʻaki homau vaʻé ʻa e ngaahi foʻi maka velá. Naʻe ʻosi puna hake ha foʻi piko siliva ʻo e māhiná ʻi he langi fakahihifó. Naʻe kamata ke lea ʻa Ketisipi, liliu ʻene fakakaukaú, ka naʻe ʻikai kimuʻa pea toki fakaʻuli ʻa Tomu ʻo fehangahangai mo ia ʻi he ʻamanaki.

“Kuó ke maʻu hoʻo fale ʻo e fanga manú ʻi heni?” ko e fehuʻi ia ʻa Ketipi ʻi ha feinga.

“Ko e maile nai ʻe taha kuata ʻi he halá.”

ʻI heʻeku siofi ʻa Tomí, ne u sio ki he fuʻu uoua ʻi mui ʻi hono umá ʻoku fakamālohiʻi ʻi lalo ʻi hono kote. Naʻá ne lue vave atu kia Uilisoni pea ʻi heʻene tuʻu ʻi muʻa aí, naʻá ne puke maʻu ia ʻi hono ongo nima ʻi ʻolungá.

“Kuo pau ke ke tohoakiʻi koe,” ko ʻene leá ia ʻi ha leʻo fakaʻofa fakanonga.

Naʻe tō ʻa e mata ʻo Uilisoní kia Tomu; naʻá ne kamata hake ʻi hono ongo vaʻé peá ne mei tō leva ʻo tūʻulutui kapau naʻe ʻikai ke pukepuke hangatonu ia ʻe Tomu.

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe Tomu, “Fanongo mai,” ʻo ne kiʻi lulululu ia. “Naʻá ku toki aʻu mai ki heni ʻi ha miniti ʻe taha kuohilí, mei Niu ʻIoke. Naʻá ku ʻomi kiate koe ʻa e koupé ko ia ne tau talanoa ki aí. Ko e kā kulokula ko ia naʻá ku fakaʻuli ʻi he hoʻatā ní naʻe ʻikai ʻaʻaku —ʻokú ke fanongo? Kuo teʻeki ai ke u sio ki ai ʻi he hoʻataá kotoa.”

Ko au pē mo e negro naʻá ma ofi feʻunga ke fanongo ki heʻene leá, ka naʻe maʻu ʻe he polisí ha meʻa ʻi he leʻó peá ne sio atu ʻaki ha mata truculent.

“Ko e hā kotoa ia?” ko ‘ene fiema‘ú ia.

“Ko hano kaungāmeʻa au.” Naʻe tafoki ʻa Tomu hono ʻulú ka naʻá ne tauhi maʻu hono ongo nimá ʻi he sino ʻo Uilisoní. “ʻOkú ne pehē ʻokú ne ʻiloʻi ʻa e kā naʻá ne faí... Ko ha kā kulokula.”

Naʻe ueʻi ʻe ha ngaahi ueʻi fakapoʻuli ʻa e polisí ke ne sio mahaloʻia kia Tomu.

“Pea ko e hā e lanu ʻo hoʻo kaá?”

“Ko ha kā lanu pulū ia, ko ha kupe.”

Naʻá ku pehē ange, “Kuó ma haʻu hangatonu mai mei Niu ʻIoke.”

Naʻe fakapapauʻi mai ʻeni ʻe ha taha naʻe kiʻi fakaʻuli ʻi homau tuʻá, pea naʻe tafoki ʻa e polisí.

“Ko ʻeni, kapau te ke tuku ke u toe fakatonutonu ʻa e hingoa ko iá—”

ʻI hono hiki hake ʻe Tomu ʻa Uilisoni ʻo hangē ha tamapuá, naʻá ne ʻave ia ki he ʻōfisí, fakanofo ia ʻi ha sea, peá ne foki mai.

“Kapau ʻe haʻu ha taha ki heni ʻo tangutu mo ia,” ko ʻene fakaʻohovale ia ʻi he mafaí. Naʻá ne siofi lolotonga ʻa e fesiofaki ʻa e ongo tangata naʻá na tuʻu ofi tahá peá na hū taʻeloto ki he lokí. Pea naʻe tāpuniʻi ʻe Tomu ʻa e matapaá kiate kinaua peá ne hifo mai ʻi he sitepu ʻe tahá, ʻo fakaʻehiʻehi hono matá mei he tēpilé. ʻI heʻene fakalaka ofi mai kiate aú naʻá ne muhumuhu mai: “Tau hū ki tuʻa.”

ʻI heʻemau fakakaukauʻi kitautolú, mo hono ongo nima maʻu mafaí ʻokú ne motuhi ʻa e halá, ne mau tekeʻi atu ʻa e fuʻu kakai naʻe kei fakatahatahá, ʻo fakalaka atu ʻi ha toketā fakavavevave, ʻoku ʻi hono nimá ha keisi, ʻa ia naʻe fekauʻi mai ʻi ha ʻamanaki vao ʻi he vaeua houa kuohilí.

Naʻe fakaʻuli māmālie ʻa Tomu ʻo aʻu ki heʻemau ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e pikopikó —pea naʻe hifo mālohi hono vaʻé, pea lele atu ʻa e koupé ʻi he pō kakató. ʻI ha kiʻi taimi nounou ne u fanongo ki ha tangi husky maʻulalo, peá u sio ʻoku tafe hifo ʻa e loʻimatá ʻi hono fofongá.

“Ko e tangata manavahē malaʻia ʻa e ʻOtuá!” naʻá ne ngungu. “Naʻe ʻikai ke ne taʻofi ʻene kaá.”

Naʻe puna fakafokifā mai ʻa e fale ʻo e fāmili Pukaní kiate kimaua ʻi he ngaahi ʻuluʻakau fakapoʻuli ʻoku ngunguú. Naʻe tuʻu ʻa Tomu ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e ʻaá ʻo sio hake ki he fungavaka ua, ʻa ia naʻe fisifisimuʻa ai ha ongo matapā sioʻata ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi fuʻu kālepí.

Naʻá ne pehē mai, “Ko e ʻapi ʻo Teisií.” ʻI heʻemau hifo mei he kaá naʻá ne sio mai kiate au mo kiʻi fakaʻiseʻisa.

“Naʻe totonu ke u tuku koe ʻi West Egg, Nick. ʻOku ʻikai ha meʻa te ta lava ʻo fai he pō ní.”

Naʻe hoko mai ha liliu kiate ia, pea naʻá ne lea fakamātoato, mo fakapapauʻi. ʻI heʻemau lue atu ʻi he makamaka ʻo e maama ʻo e māhiná ki he ʻaá naʻá ne fakaʻauha ʻa e tūkungá ʻi ha ngaahi kupuʻi lea vave siʻisiʻi.

“Te u telefoni ki ha tekisī ke ʻave koe ki ʻapi, pea lolotonga hoʻo tatali ʻoku sai ange ke mo ʻalu mo Sioatani ki he loki kuí ʻo ʻai ke na ʻomi ha meʻatokoni efiafi maʻau —kapau ʻokú ke fie maʻu ha meʻatokoni.” Naʻá ne fakaava ʻa e matapaá. “Hū mai.”

“ʻIkai, mālō. Ka te u fiefia kapau te ke ʻota mai ʻa e tekisí. Te u tatali ʻi tuʻa.”

Naʻe ʻai ʻe Sioatani hono nimá ki hoku umá.

“He ʻikai te ke hū mai, Niki?”

“ʻIkai, mālō.”

Naʻá ku kiʻi ongoʻi puke pea naʻá ku fie nofo tokotaha pē. Ka naʻe toe fakalongolongo ʻa Sioatani ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti.

Naʻá ne pehē mai, “Ko e vaeua pē ʻo e hivá.”

Te u malaʻia kapau te u hū ki loto; Naʻá ku ʻosi feʻunga kotoa kinautolu ʻi ha ʻaho ʻe taha, pea fakafokifā pē kuo kau ai mo Sioatani. Kuo pau pē naʻá ne sio ki ha meʻa ʻo e meʻá ni ʻi heʻeku fakafōtungá, he naʻá ne tafoki fakafokifā ʻo lele hake ʻi he sitepu ʻo e holisí ki he falé. Naʻá ku tangutu hifo ʻi ha ngaahi miniti siʻi mo hoku ʻulú ʻi hoku ongo nimá, kae ʻoua kuó u fanongo ki hono toʻo hake ʻo e telefoní ki loto pea mo e leʻo ʻo e tangata tauhí ʻokú ne ui ha tekisī. Peá u lue māmālie hifo ʻi he hala fakaʻuli ʻo mamaʻo mei he falé, ʻo fakataumuʻa ke u tatali ʻi he veʻe matapaá.

Naʻe teʻeki ai ke u ʻalu ʻi ha ʻiate ʻe uofulu ʻi heʻeku fanongo ki hoku hingoá pea laka atu ʻa Ketisi mei he vahaʻa ʻo ha ongo vaotā ki he halá. Kuo pau pē naʻá ku ongoʻi ʻoku ou fuʻu ngali kehe ʻi he taimi ko iá, he naʻe ʻikai ke u lava ʻo fakakaukau ki ha meʻa tukukehe pē ʻa e ngingila ʻo hono suti lanu pingikī ʻi he lalo māhiná.

"Ko e hā hoʻo meʻa ʻoku faí?" Ko ʻeku fehuʻí ia.

“Ko e tuʻu pē heni, sipoti motuʻa.”

‘I ha fa‘ahinga founga, na‘e hā ngali ko ha ngāue fakalielia ia. He ko e meʻa pē naʻá ku ʻiló te ne kaihaʻasi ʻa e falé ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti; Naʻe ʻikai ke u mei ʻohovale ʻi heʻeku sio ki ha ngaahi fofonga fakatupu ʻita, ko e ngaahi fofonga ʻo e “kakai ʻo Wolfshiem,” ʻi hono tuʻá ʻi he ʻakau fakapoʻulí.

“Naʻá ke sio ki ha palopalema ʻi he halá?” ko ʻene fehuʻí ia hili ha miniti ʻe taha.

"ʻIo."

Naʻá ne toumoua.

“Naʻe tāmateʻi ia?”

"ʻIo."

“Naʻá ku fakakaukau pehē; Naʻá ku talaange kia Teisi ʻoku ou fakakaukau pehē. ʻOku sai ange ke hoko kotoa mai ʻa e ʻohovalé ʻi he taimi pē ʻe taha. Naʻá ne tuʻu lelei ʻaupito ia.”

Naʻá ne lea ʻo hangē ko e tali ʻa Teisi pē ʻoku mahuʻingá.

“Naʻá ku aʻu ki West Egg ʻi ha hala tafaʻaki,” ko ʻene hoko atu ia, “peá u tuku ʻa e kaá ʻi hoku fale tauʻanga kaá. ʻOku ʻikai ke u tui naʻe sio mai ha taha kiate kimaua, ka ko e moʻoni ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo fakapapauʻi.”

Naʻá ku fuʻu taʻefiemālie ʻaupito ʻiate ia ʻi he taimi ko ʻení ʻo ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi ʻoku fie maʻu ke u talaange ʻokú ne hala.

“Ko hai ʻa e fefiné?” ko ʻene fehuʻí ia.

“Ko hono hingoá ko Uilisoni. ʻOku ʻa hono husepānití ʻa e fale kaá. Naʻe anga fēfē ʻa e hoko ʻa e tēvoló?”

“Sai, naʻá ku feinga ke taʻalo ʻa e uí—” Naʻá ne motuhia ia, pea fakafokifā pē kuó u mateʻi ʻa e moʻoní.

“Naʻe fakaʻuli ʻa Teisi?”

“ʻIo,” ko ʻene leá ia hili ha kiʻi momeniti, “ka ko e moʻoni te u pehē naʻá ku pehē. ʻOkú ke sio, ʻi heʻema mavahe mei Niu ʻIoké naʻá ne fuʻu manavasiʻi ʻaupito pea naʻá ne fakakaukau ʻe fakatuʻutāmakiʻi ia ke ne fakaʻuli —pea naʻe lele mai ʻa e fefiné ni kiate kimaua ʻi heʻema fakalaka atu ʻi ha kā ʻoku haʻu ʻi he hala ʻe tahá. naʻá ne tafoki mei he fefiné ʻo fakahangatonu ki he kā ʻe tahá, peá ne toki mole ʻene neá ʻo ne tafoki ki mui ʻI he sekoni pē naʻe aʻu ai hoku nimá ki he uí ne u ongoʻi ʻa e tete —kuo pau pē naʻá ne tāmateʻi ia ʻi he taimi pē ko iá.”

“Naʻá ne haeʻi ia—”

“ʻOua naʻá ke talamai, sipoti motuʻa.” Naʻá ne ngungu. “Ka neongo ia —naʻe laka atu ʻa Teisi ʻi ai.Naʻá ku feinga ke ʻai ia ke ne tuʻu, ka naʻe ʻikai ke ne lava, ko ia ne u toho ʻa e taʻofi fakavavevavé.Peá ne tō hifo ki hoku umá peá u hoko atu ʻeku fakaʻulí.

“ʻE sai pē ia ʻapongipongi,” ko ʻene lea ia ʻi he taimi ní. “Te u tatali pē heni ʻo sio pe ʻokú ne feinga ke fakahohaʻasi ia ʻi he meʻa taʻefakafiefia ko iá he hoʻatā ní. Kuó ne lokaʻi ia ʻi hono lokí, pea kapau te ne feinga ki ha faʻahinga anga-fakamamahi te ne tāmateʻi ʻa e māmá pea toe fakamoʻui.”

Naʻá ku pehē ange, “He ʻikai ke ne ala kiate ia.” “ʻOku ʻikai ke ne fakakaukau kiate ia.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u falala kiate ia, sipoti motuʻa.”

“Ko e hā hono fuoloa hoʻo tatalí?”

“ʻI he pō kakató, kapau ʻe fie maʻu. Ka neongo ia, kae ʻoua kuo nau mohe kotoa.”

Naʻe haʻu kiate au ha fakakaukau foʻou. Tau pehē naʻe ʻilo ʻe Tomu naʻe fakaʻuli ʻa Teisi. Mahalo te ne fakakaukau naʻá ne sio ki ha fehokotakiʻanga ʻi ai —te ne fakakaukau nai ki ha meʻa pē. Naʻá ku sio ki he falé; naʻe ʻi ai ha matapā sioʻata ngingila ʻe ua pe tolu ʻi lalo pea mo e ulo pingikī mei he loki ʻo Teisi ʻi he fungavaka ʻi laló.

Naʻá ku pehē ange, “Ke ke tatali pē ʻi heni.” “Te u sio pe ʻoku ʻi ai ha fakaʻilonga ʻo ha longoaʻa.”

Naʻá ku toe lue atu ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻo e ngoueʻangá, ʻo kolosi māmālie ʻi he makamaka, peá u luelue ʻi he sitepu ʻo e veʻeá. Naʻe fakaava ʻa e ngaahi puipui ʻo e loki tā valivalí, peá u sio ʻoku ʻikai ha taha ʻi he lokí. ʻI heʻeku kolosi ʻi he holisi naʻá ma kai ai ʻi he pō ko ia ʻo Sune ʻi ha māhina ʻe tolu kimuʻá, ne u ʻalu atu ki ha kiʻi tapafā ʻo e māmá ʻa ia ne u mateʻi ko e matapā sioʻata ʻo e falekai ʻo e meʻakaí. Naʻe tohoakiʻi ʻa e fakaʻulí, ka naʻá ku maʻu ha mafesifesi ʻi he silí.

Naʻe tangutu fehangahangai ʻa Teisi mo Tomu ʻi he tēpile ʻo e loki kuí, mo ha peleti moa tunu momoko ʻi hona vahaʻá, mo ha ongo hina ʻale. Naʻá ne talanoa fakamātoato ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo e tēpilé kiate ia, pea ʻi heʻene fakamātoató naʻe tō hono nimá ʻo ʻufiʻufi ʻene nimá. Naʻá ne sio hake ʻi ha taimi ʻe niʻihi kiate ia ʻo ne luelue ʻo loto-tatau.

Naʻe ʻikai ke na fiefia, pea naʻe ʻikai ha taha ʻo kinaua ʻe ala ki he moá pe ko e ʻelé —pea neongo ia naʻe ʻikai foki ke na taʻe fiefia. Naʻe ʻi ai ha ʻea taʻealafakaʻikaiʻi ʻo e feohi vāofi fakanatulá fekauʻaki mo e fakatātaá, pea naʻe mei pehē ʻe ha taha pē ʻokú na faʻufaʻu fakataha.

ʻI heʻeku luelue ʻi he ʻaá ne u fanongo ki heʻeku tekisií ʻoku ongoʻi ʻene ʻalu ʻi he hala fakapoʻulí ʻo fakahangatonu ki he falé. Naʻe tatali ʻa Ketisi ʻi he feituʻu naʻá ku tuku ai ia ʻi he fakaʻuliʻangá.

“ʻOku fakalongolongo kotoa ʻi ʻolunga?” ko ʻene fehuʻi hohaʻá ia.

“ʻIo, ʻoku fakalongolongo kotoa.” Naʻá ku toumoua. “ʻE sai ange ke ke foki mai ki ʻapi ʻo mohe.”

Naʻá ne lulululu hono ʻulú.

“ʻOku ou fie tatali heni kae ʻoua kuo mohe ʻa Teisi. Po lelei, sipoti motuʻa.”

Naʻá ne ʻai hono ongo nimá ki hono ongo kato kote peá ne tafoki vēkeveke ki mui ki heʻene sivisiviʻi ʻa e falé, ʻo hangē naʻe maumauʻi ʻe heʻeku ʻi aí ʻa e toputapu ʻo e leʻó. Ko ia na‘á ku ‘alu atu ‘o tuku ia ke ne tu‘u ‘i ai ‘i he maama ‘o e māhiná—‘o ‘ikai ke ne le‘ohi ha me‘a.
