Ko au

ʻI hoku ngaahi taʻu siʻisiʻi mo faingataʻaʻia angé naʻe ʻomi ʻe heʻeku tamaí ha ngaahi faleʻi ʻa ia kuó u liliu ʻi hoku ʻatamaí talu mei ai.

Naʻá ne talamai kiate au, “ʻI ha taimi pē ʻokú ke ongoʻi ai ke ke fakaangaʻi ha taha, manatuʻi pē kuo teʻeki ke maʻu ʻe he kakai kotoa pē ʻi he māmani ko ʻení ʻa e ngaahi lelei kuó ke maʻú.”

Naʻe ʻikai ke ne toe lea ʻaki ha meʻa, ka kuó ma fetuʻutaki taʻe angamaheni maʻu pē ʻi ha founga fakaʻehiʻehi, pea naʻe mahino kiate au naʻá ne ʻuhinga lahi ange ʻi he meʻa ko iá. Ko hono olá, ʻoku ou hehema ke taʻofi ʻa e ngaahi fakamaau kotoa pē, ko ha tōʻonga kuó ne fakaava mai ha ngaahi natula fie ʻilo lahi kiate au pea naʻá ne toe ʻai au ke u hoko ko e vikitia ʻo e ʻikai tokosiʻi ʻo e ngaahi bores veteran. ʻOku vave ʻa e ʻatamai ʻoku ʻikai angamahení ke ne ʻiloʻi mo fakapipiki ia ki he ʻulungaanga ko ʻení ʻi heʻene ʻasi mai ʻi ha tokotaha angamahení, pea ko ia naʻe hoko ai ʻo tukuakiʻi taʻetotonu au ʻi he kolisí ko ha tokotaha politikale, koeʻuhí he naʻá ku ʻiloʻi ʻa e ngaahi loto mamahi fakapulipuli ʻa e kau tangata vao, taʻeʻiloá. Ko e lahi taha ʻo e ngaahi meʻa fakapulipulí naʻe ʻikai ke kumi ki ai —kuó u faʻa fakangalingali ʻoku ou mohe, hohaʻa, pe ko ha leʻo fakafili ʻi heʻeku fakatokangaʻi ʻi ha fakaʻilonga taʻealafakahalaʻi ʻoku tetetete ha fakahā fakaʻofoʻofa ʻi he ʻataá; he ko e ngaahi fakahā faka‘ofo‘ofa ‘a e kau talavoú, pe ko e ngaahi fo‘i lea ‘oku nau fakahaa‘i ai kinautolú, ‘oku fa‘a kaiha‘a ia pea ‘oku maumau‘i ia ‘e he ngaahi ta‘ofi mahino. Ko hono taʻofi ʻo e ngaahi fakamāú ko ha meʻa ia ʻo e ʻamanaki taʻefakangatangatá. ʻOku ou kei kiʻi manavasiʻi naʻa mole ha meʻa kapau ʻe ngalo ʻiate au, ʻo hangē ko ia naʻe fokotuʻu fakaʻeiʻeiki mai ʻe heʻeku tamaí, pea ʻoku ou toe fakaʻaiʻai, ʻoku vahevahe taʻetatau ha ongo ʻo e ngaahi ʻulungaanga fakaʻapaʻapa tefitó ʻi hono fāʻeleʻí.

Pea, hili ʻeku pōlepole ʻi he founga ko ʻeni ʻo ʻeku kātakí, ʻoku ou aʻu mai ki he fakamoʻoni ʻoku ʻi ai hono fakangatangata. Mahalo ʻe fakavaʻe ʻa e ʻulungāngá ʻi he maka fefeká pe ʻi he ngaahi vaotaá, ka ʻi he hili ha poini pau ʻoku ʻikai ke u tokanga au pe ko e hā ʻoku fakavaʻe aí. ʻI heʻeku foki mai mei he Hahaké ʻi he faʻahitaʻu fakatōlau kuo ʻosí ne u ongoʻi ʻoku ou fie maʻu ke teunga ʻa e māmaní pea ʻi ha faʻahinga tokanga fakamōlale ʻo taʻengata; Naʻe ʻikai ke u toe fie maʻu ha ngaahi ʻeveʻeva fakamoveuveu mo ha ngaahi sio monūʻia ki he loto ʻo e tangatá. Ko Ketisi pē, ʻa e tangata ʻokú ne ʻomi hono hingoá ki he tohí ni, naʻe fakaʻatā mei heʻeku talí —ko Ketisipi, ʻa ia naʻá ne fakafofongaʻi e meʻa kotoa pē ʻoku ʻi ai ʻeku manuki taʻe uesia ki aí. Kapau ko e ʻulungāngá ko ha hokohoko taʻe-motuhi ia ʻo e ngaahi fakaʻilonga lavameʻá, pea naʻe ʻi ai leva ha meʻa fakaʻofoʻofa fekauʻaki mo ia, ko ha ongoʻi fakalahi ʻe niʻihi ki he ngaahi talaʻofa ʻo e moʻuí, ʻo hangē naʻá ne fekauʻaki mo e taha ʻo e ngaahi mīsini lavelavea ko ia ʻoku nau lesisita ʻa e ngaahi mofuiké ʻi ha maile ʻe hongofulu afe hono mamaʻó. Naʻe ʻikai ha kaunga ʻa e tali ko ʻení ki he impressionability flabby ko ia ʻa ia ʻoku fakaʻapaʻapaʻi ʻi he hingoa ʻo e “creative temperament” —ko ha meʻaʻofa makehe ia ki he ʻamanaki leleí, ko ha mateuteu fakaʻofa ʻo hangē ko ia kuo teʻeki ai ke u maʻu ʻi ha toe tokotaha kehe pea ʻoku ʻikai ngalingali te u toe maʻu ia. ʻIkai —naʻe ʻasi mai ʻa Ketisipi ʻo sai pē ʻi he fakaʻosí; ko e meʻa ia naʻe preyed ʻia Gatsby, ko e hā e efu ʻuli naʻe ʻalu holo ʻi he ʻaho ʻo ʻene ngaahi misí ʻa ia naʻá ne tāpuniʻi fakataimi ʻeku mahuʻingaʻia ʻi he ngaahi mamahi fakatōtamá mo e ngaahi fiefia nounou ʻa e tangatá.

Ko hoku fāmilí ko ha kakai ʻiloa mo koloaʻia ʻi he kolo ko ʻeni ʻo e Hihifo Lotolotó ʻi ha toʻutangata ʻe tolu. Ko e Carraways ko ha me'a 'o e clan, pea 'oku 'i ai 'emau tukufakaholo 'oku mau tupu mei he Dukes 'o Buccleuch, ka ko e founder mo'oni 'o 'eku laine ko e tokoua 'o 'eku kui tangata, 'a ia na'e ha'u ki heni 'i he nimangofulu ma taha, 'o fekau'i atu ha fetongi ki he Tau Fakalotofonua, pea kamata 'a e pisinisi 'o e ngaahi naunau fakalukufua 'i he me'alele 'o 'eku tamai 'o e 'aho ni.

Naʻe ʻikai ke u teitei sio ki he tangataʻeikí ni, ka ʻoku pehē ʻoku ou hangē ko iá —ʻo lave makehe ki he tā valivali ʻoku ʻikai fuʻu fefeka ʻoku tautau ʻi he ʻōfisi ʻo e tamaí. Na‘á ku ‘osi mei Niu Heveni ‘i he 1915, hili pē ha kuata senituli ‘e taha mei he‘eku tamaí, pea ki mui si‘i mei ai na‘á ku kau ‘i he hiki fakatoloi ko ia ‘a e kau Teutoní ‘a ia na‘e ‘iloa ko e Tau Lahí. Naʻá ku fiefia ʻaupito ʻi he ʻohofi fakafepakí ʻo u foki mai ʻo taʻefiemālie. ʻI he ʻikai ke hoko ʻa e Hihifo Lotolotó ko e senitā māfana ʻo e māmaní, naʻe hangē he taimí ni ʻa e Hihifo Lotolotó ko e tafaʻaki motuʻa ʻo e ʻunivēsí —ko ia ne u fakakaukau ai ke u ʻalu ki he Hahaké ʻo ako ʻa e pisinisi ʻo e ngaahi pōní. Ko e tokotaha kotoa pē naʻá ku ʻiloʻí naʻe ʻi he pisinisi pōnití, ko ia naʻá ku fakakaukau ʻe lava ke ne poupouʻi ha toe tangata taʻemali ʻe taha. Naʻe talanoaʻi ia ʻe heʻeku ongo fineʻeikí mo e ongo tangataʻeikí kotoa ʻo hangē ʻokú na fili ha akoʻanga teuteu maʻakú, pea faifai pea pehē mai, “Ko e hā —ʻio-ʻē,” mo ha fofonga mamafa ʻaupito mo veiveiua. Naʻe loto ʻa e Tamaí ke fakapaʻanga au ʻi ha taʻu ʻe taha, pea hili ha ngaahi toloi kehekehe ne u haʻu ki he Hahaké, ʻo tuʻuloa, ko ʻeku fakakaukaú ia, ʻi he faʻahitaʻu failau ʻo e taʻu uofulu mā uá.

Ko e meʻa ʻaongá ke kumi ha ngaahi loki ʻi he koló, ka ko ha faʻahitaʻu māfana ia, pea naʻá ku toki mavahe mei ha fonua ʻoku ʻi ai ha ngaahi ngoueʻanga ʻoku fōlahi mo ha ʻuluʻakau anga fakakaumeʻa, ko ia ʻi he taimi naʻe fokotuʻu mai ai ʻe ha talavou ʻi he ʻōfisí ke ma ʻave fakataha ha fale ʻi ha kolo ʻoku fefonongaʻaki ai, naʻe ongo mai ia ko ha fakakaukau lelei ʻaupito. Naʻá ne maʻu ʻa e falé, ko ha fale kaati kuo taaʻi ʻe he ʻeá ʻi he taʻu valungofulu ʻi he māhina, ka ʻi he miniti fakaʻosí naʻe ʻota ia ʻe he kautahá ki Uasingatoni, peá u ʻalu tokotaha pē ki tuʻa ki he fonuá. Naʻe ʻi ai ʻeku kulii —ʻo ʻikai toe siʻi hifo ʻi heʻeku maʻu ia ʻi ha ngaahi ʻaho siʻi kae ʻoua kuó ne hola —pea mo ha motuʻa Dodge mo ha fefine Finilani, ʻa ia naʻá ne teuteuʻi hoku mohengá mo tunu ʻa e meʻatokoni pongipongí mo lāunga ʻa e poto faka-Finilaní kiate ia ʻi he ʻolunga ʻo e sitofu ʻuhilá.

Naʻe tuēnoa ʻi ha ʻaho ʻe taha pe lahi ange kae ʻoua kuo ʻi ai ha pongipongi ʻe taha naʻe taʻofi au ʻe ha tangata ʻe taha, ʻa ia naʻe toki aʻu mai ʻiate au, ʻi he halá.

“ʻOku anga fēfē hoʻo aʻu ki he kolo ko West Egg?” ko ʻene fehuʻi taʻelavameʻá ia.

Naʻá ku talaange ki ai. Pea ʻi heʻeku lue atú naʻe ʻikai ke u toe nofo tokotaha. Ko ha tokotaha fakahinohino au, ko ha tokotaha kumi hala, ko ha tokotaha nofo ʻuluaki. Naʻá ne foaki fakataimi pē kiate au ʻa e tauʻatāina ʻo e kaungāʻapí.

Pea ko ia ʻi he laʻaá mo e ngaahi pa lahi ʻo e ngaahi lauʻiʻakaú ʻoku tupu ʻi he ʻuluʻakaú, ʻo hangē pē ko e tupu ʻa e ngaahi meʻá ʻi he ngaahi filimi vave, naʻá ku maʻu ʻa e tui maheni ko ia ʻoku toe kamata foʻou ʻa e moʻuí ʻi he faʻahitaʻu māfaná.

Naʻe lahi fau e meʻa ke lau, ko e meʻa ʻe taha, pea lahi fau e moʻui lelei ke toho hifo mei he ʻea kei talavou ʻoku ʻomi ʻa e mānavá. Naʻá ku fakatau ha ngaahi voliume ʻe hongofulu tupu ʻi he ngaahi maluʻi fakapangikeé mo e nō mo e ʻinivesimení, pea naʻa nau tuʻu ʻi hoku fata ʻi he kulokula mo e koula ʻo hangē ha paʻanga foʻou mei he miní, ʻo nau palōmesi ke fakaava ʻa e ngaahi fakapulipuli ngingila ʻa ia ko Midas mo Morgan mo Maecenas pē naʻa nau ʻiloʻí. Pea naʻá ku maʻu ʻa e taumuʻa māʻolunga ke lau ha ngaahi tohi lahi kehe ʻo makehe mei ai. Naʻá ku fuʻu ʻiloʻi ʻa e tohí ʻi he kolisí —ʻi ha taʻu ʻe taha ne u tohi ai ha ngaahi ʻētita hokohoko ʻoku fuʻu fakaʻeiʻeiki mo mahino ki he Ongoongo ʻo ʻIelí —pea ko ʻeni te u fakafoki mai ʻa e ngaahi meʻa peheé kotoa ki heʻeku moʻuí pea toe hoko ko e tokotaha fakangatangata taha ʻi he kau mataotao kotoa pē, ʻa e “tangata ʻoku ʻikai ke ne ʻiloʻi leleí.” ʻOku ʻikai ko ha ʻepikalami pē ʻeni —ʻoku lavameʻa ange ʻaupito ʻa e vakai ki he moʻuí mei ha matapā sioʻata pē ʻe taha, hiliō he meʻa kotoa.

Ko ha meʻa fakatuʻupakē pē naʻe totonu ke u nofo totongi ʻi ha fale ʻi he taha ʻo e ngaahi komiunitī ngali kehe taha ʻi ʻAmelika Tokelaú. Naʻe ʻi he motu moveuveu fuopotopoto ko ia ʻa ia ʻoku fakalahi atu ki he hahake ʻo Niu ʻIoké —pea ʻoku ʻi ai, ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi meʻa fakatupu ʻilo fakanatula kehé, ha ongo fotunga taʻe angamaheni ʻo e fonuá. ʻI ha maile ʻe uofulu mei he koló ha ongo fuʻu fuaʻimoa lalahi, ʻoku tatau ʻena fakaʻilonga pea ʻoku fakamavaheʻi pē ʻe ha ʻotu moʻunga fakaʻapaʻapa, ʻoku ʻalu atu ki tuʻa ki he sino ʻo e vai māsima ʻoku fakaʻaongaʻi lahi taha ʻi he hemisifia Hihifó, ʻa e lotoʻā lahi ʻo e fanga monumanu ʻoku sūsū ʻi Long Island Sound. ʻOku ʻikai ko ha ngaahi ʻovali haohaoa kinaua —hangē ko e fuaʻimoa ʻi he talanoa ʻo Kolomupiá, ʻokú na fakatou laiki ʻo palakū ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻo e fetuʻutakí —ka kuo pau ke hoko hona faitatau fakaesinó ko ha maʻuʻanga ʻo e ofo tuʻuloa ki he fanga kiʻi ʻuhikiʻi manupuna ʻoku nau lele ʻi ʻolungá. Ki he wingless ha me'a fakaofo 'oku toe malie ange ko 'enau dissimilarity 'i he me'a kotoa pe tukukehe 'a e fotunga mo e lahi.

Naʻá ku nofo ʻi West Egg, ʻa e—lelei, ʻa e siʻisiʻi ange ʻa e founga ʻo e ongo meʻá ni, neongo ko ha fakaʻilonga fakaʻofoʻofa taha ʻeni ke fakahaaʻi ʻaki ʻa e kehekehe fakaofo mo ʻikai ko ha kiʻi kehekehe fakatupu ʻita ʻi hona vahaʻá. Naʻe ʻi he tumutumu ʻo e fuaʻimoá hoku falé, ʻi ha ʻiate pē ʻe nimangofulu mei he Sound, pea naʻe ʻi he vahaʻa ʻo ha ongo feituʻu lalahi ʻe ua naʻe totongi ʻaki ha paʻanga ʻe hongofulu mā ua pe hongofulu mā nima afe ʻi he faʻahitaʻú. Ko e taha ʻi hoku toʻomataʻú ko ha meʻa lahi ʻaupito ia ʻi ha faʻahinga tuʻunga pē —ko ha faʻifaʻitakiʻanga moʻoni ia ʻo ha Hôtel de Ville ʻi Nomani, mo ha taua ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻe tahá, ʻoku spanking foʻou ʻi lalo ʻi ha ʻuluʻulu manifinifi ʻo e ʻaivi mata, mo ha vai kaukau māpele, mo ha ʻeka ʻe fāngofulu tupu ʻo e ngoue mo e ngoue. Ko e fale lahi ia ʻo Ketipi. Pe, ko hono fakalea ʻe tahá, ʻi he ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi ʻa Misa Ketisipií, ko ha fale lahi ia naʻe nofoʻi ʻe ha tangataʻeiki ʻoku hingoa ko iá. Ko hoku fale tonu ko ha mataʻitofe, ka ko ha kiʻi mataʻitofe, pea naʻe ʻosi fakangaloku ia, ko ia naʻá ku maʻu ai ha sio ki he vaí, ha konga ʻo e sio ki he ngoueʻanga ʻo hoku kaungāʻapí, mo e ofi fakafiemālie ʻa e kau milioneá —ko e meʻa kotoa pē ʻi he paʻanga ʻe valungofulu ʻi he māhina.

ʻI he tafaʻaki ʻe taha ʻo e courtesy bay naʻe ngingila ʻa e ngaahi palasi hinehina ʻo e East Egg fakaʻofoʻofá ʻi he vaí, pea ʻoku kamata moʻoni ʻa e hisitōlia ʻo e faʻahitaʻu māfaná ʻi he efiafi ne u fakaʻuli atu ai ke maʻu meʻatokoni efiafi mo e fāmili Tom Buchanans. Ko Teisi ko hoku tokoua hono ua ʻi ha taimi naʻe toʻo ai, pea naʻá ku ʻiloʻi ʻa Tomu ʻi he kolisí. Pea hili pē ʻa e taú ne u ʻaho ʻe ua mo kinautolu ʻi Sikākou.

Ko hono husepānití, ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi lavameʻa fakaesino kehekehe, naʻá ne hoko ko e taha ʻo e ngaahi ngataʻanga mālohi taha kuo vaʻinga ʻakapulu ʻi Niu Heveni —ko ha tokotaha fakafonua ʻi ha founga, ko e taha ʻo e kau tangata ko ia ʻoku nau aʻu ki ha tuʻunga fakangatangata ʻoku fuʻu lelei ʻaupito ʻi heʻenau taʻu uofulu mā tahá ʻo ʻi ai ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē ʻi he hili iá ʻoku nau ifo ʻo e anticlimax. Naʻe fuʻu koloaʻia ʻaupito hono fāmilí —naʻa mo e ʻi he kolisí ko ʻene tauʻatāina ʻi he paʻangá ko ha meʻa ia ke fakaangaʻi —ka ko ʻeni kuó ne mavahe mei Sikākou ʻo haʻu ki he Hahaké ʻi ha founga ʻoku ʻikai ke ne toʻo hoʻo mānavá: hangē ko ʻení, naʻá ne ʻomi hifo ha ngaahi foʻi pōni polo mei Leki Footi. Naʻe faingataʻa ke u ʻiloʻi naʻe koloaʻia feʻunga ha tangata ʻi hoku toʻu tangatá tonu ke ne fai ʻa e meʻa ko iá.

Ko e ha na'a nau ha'u ai ki Hahake 'oku 'ikai keu 'ilo. Naʻá na nofo ʻi Falanisē ʻi ha taʻu ʻe taha ʻo ʻikai ha ʻuhinga pau, peá na toki tafe taʻefiemālie ʻi heni mo hena ʻi ha feituʻu pē naʻe vaʻinga polo ai ʻa e kakaí pea nau koloaʻia fakataha. Ko ha ngaʻunu tuʻuloa ʻeni, ko e lau ia ʻa Teisi ʻi he telefoní, ka naʻe ʻikai ke u tui ki ai —naʻe ʻikai haʻaku sio ki he loto ʻo Teisi, ka naʻá ku ongoʻi ʻe tafe atu ʻa Tomu ʻo kumi taʻengata, ʻi ha kiʻi loto-mamahi, ki he moveuveu fakaofo ʻo ha ngaahi vaʻinga ʻakapulu ʻoku ʻikai lava ke fakafoki.

Pea naʻe hoko ia ʻi ha efiafi matangi māfana ne u fakaʻuli atu ki East Egg ke sio ki ha ongo kaungāmeʻa motuʻa naʻe ʻikai ke u fuʻu ʻiloʻi. Naʻe toe fakaʻofoʻofa ange honau falé ʻi he meʻa ne u ʻamanaki ki aí, ko ha fale lahi kulokula mo hinehina fiefia ʻo e Kolonia Siōsiá, ʻo sio atu ki he matātahí. Naʻe kamata ʻa e ngoueʻangá ʻi he matātahí ʻo lele atu ki he matapā ʻi muʻá ʻi ha kuata maile, ʻo puna ʻi he ngaahi houa laʻaá mo e ngaahi luelue pilikí mo e ngaahi ngoueʻanga velá —fakaʻosí ʻi heʻene aʻu atu ki he falé ʻo tafe hake ʻi he tafaʻakí ʻi ha ngaahi vaine ngingila ʻo hangē mei he mālohi ʻo ʻene lelé. Naʻe motuhi ʻa e tafaʻaki ki muʻá ʻe ha laine ʻo e ngaahi matapā sioʻata Falanisē, ʻoku ulo he taimí ni ʻaki ʻa e koula ʻoku fakaʻataʻatā mai pea ʻoku fakaava lahi ki he hoʻatā māfana ʻo e matangí, pea naʻe tuʻu ʻa Tomu Pukanani ʻi ha kofu heka hoosi ʻo mavahevahe hono ongo vaʻé ʻi he holisi ʻi muʻá.

Naʻá ne liliu talu mei hono ngaahi taʻu ʻi Niu Hevení. Ko ʻeni ko ha tangata fulufuluʻi ʻakau fefeka ia ʻoku taʻu tolungofulu tupu, mo ha ngutu ʻoku ʻikai fuʻu fefeka mo ha tōʻonga fakaʻeiʻeiki. Naʻe fokotuʻu ʻe ha ongo mata pōlepole ngingila ha pule ki hono fofongá pea ʻoange kiate ia ʻa e fōtunga ʻo e punou fakamālohi maʻu pē ki muʻá. Naʻe ʻikai lava ʻe he ngingila fakafefine ʻo hono kofu heka hōsí ʻo fufū ʻa e mālohi lahi ʻo e sino ko iá —naʻe hangē naʻá ne fakafonu ʻa e ngaahi sū ngingila ko iá kae ʻoua kuó ne fakamālohiʻi ʻa e laisi ʻi ʻolungá, pea naʻá ke lava ʻo sio ki ha fuʻu puha ʻo e uoua ʻoku hiki ʻi he taimi naʻe ngaʻunu ai hono umá ʻi lalo ʻi hono kote manifi. Ko ha sino ia na‘e malava ke ne fai ha fu‘u mālohi lahi—ko ha sino anga-fakamamahi.

Ko hono leʻo leá, ko ha tenor husky gruff, naʻe tānaki atu ia ki he ongo ʻo e fractiousness naʻá ne fakahoko maí. Naʻe ʻi ai ha kiʻi taʻetokaʻi fakatamai ʻi ai, naʻa mo e kakai naʻá ne saiʻia aí —pea naʻe ʻi ai ha kau tangata ʻi Niu Heveni naʻa nau fehiʻa ki hono ngaahi ʻutó.

“Ko ʻeni, ʻoua naʻá ke fakakaukau ʻoku fakaʻosi ʻeku fakakaukau ki he ngaahi meʻá ni,” naʻe hangē naʻá ne pehē, “koeʻuhí pē he ʻoku ou mālohi ange mo tangata ange ʻiate koe.” Naʻá ma ʻi he sōsaieti matuʻotuʻa tatau, pea neongo naʻe ʻikai ke ma teitei feohi vāofi naʻá ku maʻu maʻu pē ʻa e fakakaukau naʻá ne fakangofua au mo fie maʻu ke u saiʻia ʻiate ia ʻaki ha ngaahi wistfulness fefeka mo fakafepaki ʻaʻana.

Naʻá ma talanoa ʻi ha ngaahi miniti siʻi ʻi he holisi laʻaá.

“Kuó u maʻu ha feituʻu fakaʻofoʻofa heni,” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻo ulo holo hono matá ʻo ʻikai ke nonga.

ʻI heʻene tafoki au ʻaki hono nima ʻe tahá, naʻá ne hiki ha nima fōlahi ʻoku fōlahi ʻi he vista ʻi muʻá, kau ai ʻi hono tafiʻí ha ngoue ʻItali kuo ngoto, ko ha vaeua ʻeka ʻo e ngaahi lose loloto mo ngingila, mo ha vaka mīsini ʻoku ʻi ai hono ihu snub naʻá ne fakaʻauha ʻa e taí ʻi he matātahí.

“Naʻe ʻa Demaine ia, ko e tangata lolo.” Naʻá ne toe fakatafokiʻi au, ʻi he anga fakaʻapaʻapa mo fakafokifā. “Te ta hū ki loto.”

Naʻá ma lue atu ʻi ha halanga māʻolunga ki ha feituʻu lanu lose ngingila, ʻo haʻi vaivai ki he falé ʻe ha ngaahi matapā sioʻata Falanisē ʻi he ongo ngataʻangá. Naʻe ava ʻa e ngaahi matapaá pea ngingila hinehina ʻi he mohuku foʻou ʻi tuʻá ʻa ia naʻe hangē naʻe kiʻi tupu ʻo hū atu ki he falé. Naʻe puhi mai ha havili ʻi he lokí, ʻo ne puhiʻi mai ha ngaahi puipui ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻe tahá pea ʻalu ki tuʻa ʻi he ngataʻanga ʻe tahá ʻo hangē ha ngaahi fuka engeengá, ʻo ne mioʻi hake kinautolu ki he keke mali ʻoku ʻosi ʻuʻulu ʻo e silingí, peá ne toki ʻalu holo ʻi he fala lanu uainé, ʻo ne ngaohi ha ʻata ʻi ai ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku fai ʻe he matangí ʻi he tahí.

Ko e meʻa pē ʻe taha naʻe tuʻu kakato ʻi he lokí ko ha fuʻu sofa lahi ʻa ia naʻe puna hake ai ha ongo finemui ʻo hangē ʻokú na ʻi ha foʻi pulu ʻoku taulá. Naʻá na fakatou tui hinehina, pea naʻe luelue mo luelue hona ongo kofú ʻo hangē naʻe toki puhiʻi kinaua ʻo fakafoki mai hili haʻana lele nounou takai holo ʻi he falé. Kuo pau pē naʻá ku tuʻu ʻi ha ngaahi momeniti siʻi ʻo fanongo ki he uipi mo e pā ʻa e ngaahi puipuí mo e tangi ʻa ha fakatātā ʻi he ʻā. Pea naʻe ʻi ai ha longoaʻa ʻi hono tāpuniʻi ʻe Tomu Pukenani ʻa e ngaahi matapā sioʻata ʻi muí pea mate ʻa e matangi naʻe maʻú ʻi he lokí, pea naʻe ʻalu māmālie atu ʻa e ngaahi puipuí mo e ngaahi falaké mo e ongo finemuí ki he falikí.

Ko e siʻisiʻi ange ʻi he ongo meʻá ni ko ha muli ia kiate au. Naʻe fakaloloa kakato ia ʻi hono ngataʻanga ʻo e tēpilé, ʻo ʻikai ke ne ngaue ʻaupito, pea ʻoku kiʻi hiki hake hono ʻaofinimá, ʻo hangē ʻokú ne fakapalanisi ha meʻa ʻi aí ʻa ia ʻoku ngalingali ʻaupito ʻe tō. Kapau naʻá ne sio mai kiate au mei he tuliki ʻo hono matá naʻe ʻikai ke ne ʻomi ha fakaʻilonga ki ai —ko e moʻoni, naʻá ku meimei ʻohovale ʻi heʻeku lāunga ʻi ha kole fakamolemole ʻi heʻeku fakahohaʻasi ia ʻaki ʻeku hū maí.

Naʻe feinga ʻa e taʻahine ʻe tahá, ko Teisi, ke tuʻu hake —naʻá ne kiʻi punou ki muʻa mo ha mata fakakonisēnisi —peá ne kata mai, ko ha kiʻi kata fakavalevale mo fakaʻofoʻofa, peá u kata foki mo au ʻo haʻu ki muʻa ki he lokí.

“ʻOku ou p-palalisi ʻi he fiefiá.”

Naʻá ne toe kata mai, ʻo hangē naʻá ne lea ʻaki ha meʻa fakaʻofoʻofa ʻaupito, peá ne puke hoku nimá ʻi ha kiʻi taimi, ʻo sio hake ki hoku fofongá, ʻo ne palōmesi mai ʻoku ʻikai ha taha ʻi he māmaní ʻokú ne fuʻu fie sio ki aí. Ko ha founga ia naʻá ne maʻu. Naʻá ne fakaʻilongaʻi ʻi haʻane lāunga ko e hingoa fakafāmili ʻo e taʻahine palanisi ko Peika. (Kuó u fanongo ʻoku pehē ko e lāunga ʻa Teisií naʻe fai pē ia ke punou ʻa e kakaí kiate ia; ko ha fakaanga taʻeʻaonga naʻe ʻikai ke siʻi hifo ai ʻene fakaʻofoʻofá.)

Ko e hā pē ha meʻa, naʻe lulululu ʻa e loungutu ʻo Misa Peiká, naʻá ne luelue mai kiate au ʻo meimei ʻikai ke ne ʻiloʻi, peá ne toki fakavavevave ʻo toe fakafoki hono ʻulú ki mui —naʻe mahino naʻe kiʻi tetetete ʻa e meʻa naʻá ne fakapalanisi ʻo ʻoange haʻane meʻa fakailifia. Naʻe toe ʻasi hake ha faʻahinga kole fakamolemole ki hoku loungutú. Ko e meimei ha fakaʻaliʻali pē ʻo e moʻui fakaʻaufuli kakató ʻokú ne toʻo mai ha fakaʻapaʻapa fakaofo meiate au.

Naʻá ku toe sio atu ki hoku tokouá, ʻa ia naʻá ne kamata ke ʻeke mai ha ngaahi fehuʻi ʻi hono leʻo māʻulalo mo fakafiefiá. Ko e fa‘ahinga le‘o ia ‘oku muimui‘i ‘e he telingá ‘o ‘alu hake mo hifo, ‘o hangē ko e lea taki taha ko ha fokotu‘utu‘u ‘o e ngaahi nota ‘e ‘ikai ‘aupito ke toe tā. Naʻe lotomamahi mo fakaʻofoʻofa hono fofongá ʻaki ha ngaahi meʻa ngingila ʻi ai, mata ngingila mo ha ngutu passionate ngingila, ka naʻe ʻi ai ha fiefia ʻi hono leʻó naʻe faingataʻa ke fakangaloʻi ʻe he kau tangata naʻa nau tokangaʻi iá: ko ha fakamālohi hiva, ko ha muhumuhu “Fanongo,” ko ha talaʻofa naʻá ne fai ʻa e gay, ngaahi meʻa fakafiefia ko ha taimi pē next hociyur ʻi he, ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e ngaahi meʻa ʻi ha taimi pē next hociyur ʻi he, ʻoku ʻi ai ʻa e fiefia.

Naʻá ku talaange ki ai ʻa e founga naʻá ku tuʻu ai ʻi Sikākou ʻi ha ʻaho ʻi heʻeku fononga ki he Hahaké, pea mo e founga naʻe ʻave ai ʻe ha kakai ʻe toko hongofulu tupu ʻenau ʻofá ʻo fakafou mai ʻiate aú.

“ʻOku nau misi mai kiate au?” naʻá ne tangi fiefia.

"ʻOku lala ʻa e koló kotoa. ʻOku vali ʻuliʻuli ʻa e uili toʻohema ʻi mui ʻi he ngaahi kaá kotoa ko ha pale ʻo e tengihia, pea ʻoku ʻi ai ha tangilāulau hokohoko ʻi he pō kakató ʻi he matāfanga fakatokelaú.”

"ʻOku fakaʻofoʻofa moʻoni! Tau foki, Tomu. ʻApongipongi!" Peá ne tānaki mai ta‘e‘uhinga: “Na‘e totonu ke ke sio ki he pēpeé.”

“ʻOku ou saiʻia ke fai ia.”

“ʻOku mohe ia. ʻOku taʻu tolu. Kuo teʻeki ke ke sio kiate ia?”

"Oua 'aupito."

“Sai, ʻoku totonu ke ke sio kiate ia. ʻOkú ne—”

Naʻe tuʻu ʻa Tomu Pukenani, ʻa ia naʻá ne luelue taʻefiemālie holo ʻi he lokí, ʻo hili hono nimá ʻi hoku umá.

“Ko e hā ʻokú ke faí, Niki?”

“Ko ha tangata haʻi au.”

“Ko hai mo ia?”

Naʻá ku talaange ki ai.

“Kuo te‘eki ai ke u fanongo fekau‘aki mo kinautolu,” ko ‘ene fakatokanga fakapapau ia.

Naʻe fakaʻitaʻi au ʻe he meʻá ni.

“Te ke fai ia,” ko ʻeku tali nounou ia. “Te ke fai ia kapau te ke nofo ʻi he Hahaké.”

“ʻOiauē, te u nofo pē au ʻi he Hahaké, ʻoua naʻá ke hohaʻa,” ko ʻene leá ia, ʻo ne sio fakamamaʻu kia Teisi pea toe sio mai kiate au, ʻo hangē naʻá ne tokanga ki ha meʻa lahi ange. “Te u hoko ko ha vale malaʻia ʻe he ʻOtuá ke u nofo ʻi ha toe feituʻu kehe.”

ʻI he taimi ko ʻení naʻe pehē ange ʻe Misa Peika: “ʻOku moʻoni!” ʻi ha fakafokifā ʻaupito ʻeku kamata —ko e fuofua lea ia naʻá ne leaʻaki talu ʻeku hū mai ki he lokí. ʻOku mahino naʻá ne ʻohovale ʻo hangē pē ko aú, he naʻá ne ʻāʻā pea ʻi ha ngaahi ngaʻunu vave mo fakapotopoto hokohoko naʻá ne tuʻu hake ki he lokí.

Naʻá ne lāunga, “ʻOku ou fefeka, kuó u tokoto ʻi he sofa ko iá ʻi he fuoloa taha ʻo ʻeku manatú.”

Naʻe fakafepakiʻi ange ʻe Teisi, “ʻOua naʻá ke sio mai kiate au, kuó u feinga ke ʻave koe ki Niu ʻIoke ʻi he hoʻataá kotoa.”

“ʻIkai, mālō,” ko e lea ia ʻa Misa Peiká ki he ngaahi kofukofu ʻe fā naʻe toki hū mai mei he falekai. “ʻOku ou ʻi he akoʻangá ʻaupito.”

Naʻe sio taʻetui ʻene tokotaha talitalí kiate ia.

"Ko koe!" Naʻá ne toʻo hifo ʻene inú ʻo hangē ha mataʻitofe ʻi he lalo ipu sioʻatá. “Ko e founga te ke fakahoko ai ha meʻá ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo ʻiloʻi.”

Naʻá ku sio kia Misa Peika, ʻo fifili pe ko e hā ʻa e meʻa naʻá ne “fakahokó.” Naʻá ku fiefia ʻi heʻeku sio kiate iá. Ko ha taʻahine fuopotopoto mo siʻisiʻi hono fatafatá, mo ha saliote ʻoku tuʻu hangatonu, ʻa ia naʻá ne fakaʻilongaʻi ʻaki ʻene lī hono sinó ki mui ʻi he ongo umá ʻo hangē ha kiʻi katé. Naʻe sio mai hono ongo mata hina ʻoku ʻosi ʻa e laʻaá kiate au ʻi ha fie ʻilo fakafetongi anga fakaʻapaʻapa mei ha fofonga ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu fie ʻilo, fakaʻofoʻofa, mo taʻefiemālie. Naʻe haʻu ki hoku ʻatamaí he taimí ni kuó u sio kiate ia, pe ko haʻane fakatātā, ʻi ha feituʻu kimuʻa.

“ʻOkú ke nofo ʻi West Egg,” ko ʻene fakatokanga fakaanga ia. “ʻOku ou ʻilo ha taha ʻi ai.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u ʻilo ha taha—”

“Kuo pau ke ke ʻiloʻi ʻa Ketisipi.”

“Katisipi?” ko e fiemaʻu ia ʻa Teisi. “Ko e hā ʻa Ketisi?”

Kimuʻa peá u tali ko hoku kaungāʻapí ia naʻe fanongonongo mai ʻa e maʻu meʻatokoni efiafí; ʻi heʻene fakaʻauʻauhifo hono nima tengetí ʻi lalo ʻi hoku nimá, naʻe fakamālohiʻi au ʻe Tomu Pukanani mei he lokí ʻo hangē ʻokú ne hiki ha sieke ki ha sikuea ʻe tahá.

Naʻe muʻomuʻa atu ʻa e ongo finemuí ʻi heʻena fuopotopoto, mo vaivai, ʻo tuku maʻamaʻa hona ongo nimá ʻi hona ongo umá, ʻo ʻalu atu ki tuʻa ki ha ʻaofi lanu lose, ʻo fakaava ki he tō ʻa e laʻaá, ʻa ia naʻe ngingila ai ha ngaahi teʻelango ʻe fā ʻi he tēpilé ʻi he matangi fakasiʻisiʻí.

“Ko e hā ʻoku ʻi ai ai ha ngaahi teʻelango ?” ko e fakafepaki ia ʻa Teisi, ʻo ne fakaʻiseʻisa. Naʻá ne fakaʻauha kinaua ʻaki hono ongo louhiʻi nimá. “ʻI ha uike ʻe ua ʻe hoko ia ko e ʻaho lōloa taha ʻi he taʻú.” Naʻá ne sio ngingila mai kiate kimautolu kotoa. “ʻOkú ke sio maʻu pē ki he ʻaho lōloa taha ʻo e taʻú pea toki misi ia?ʻOku ou sio maʻu pē ki he ʻaho lōloa taha ʻo e taʻú pea toki misi ia.”

“ʻOku totonu ke ta palani ha meʻa,” ko e ʻāʻā ia ʻa Misa Peiká, ʻo ne tangutu hifo ʻi he tēpilé ʻo hangē ʻokú ne ʻalu hake ki hono mohengá.

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe Teisi, “Sai pē.” “Ko e hā te tau palaní?” Naʻá ne tafoki mai kiate au ʻo ʻikai ha tokoni: “Ko e hā ʻoku palani ʻe he kakaí?”

Kimuʻa peá u toki talí naʻe fakamaʻu hono matá mo ha fakaʻilonga ʻo e manavahē ʻi hono kiʻi louhiʻi nimá.

"Sio!" naʻá ne lāunga; “Naʻá ku fakamamahiʻi ia.”

Naʻa mau sio kotoa —naʻe ʻuliʻuli mo lanu pulū ʻa e foʻi nimá.

Naʻá ne pehē mai ʻi he tukuakiʻí, “Naʻá ke fai ia, Tomu.” “ʻOku ou ʻiloʻi naʻe ʻikai ke ke ʻuhinga ki ai, ka naʻá ke fai ia. Ko e meʻa ia ʻoku ou maʻu ʻi heʻeku mali mo ha tangata anga-fakamamahi, ko ha fakatātā fakaesino lahi, lahi, mo hulking ʻo ha—”

“ʻOku ou fehiʻa ʻi he foʻi lea ko ia ko e ‘hulking,’ ” ko e fakafepaki fakakolosi ia ʻa Tomí, “naʻa mo e fakakata.”

“Hulking,” ko e fakamamafa ia ʻa Teisi.

Taimi ʻe niʻihi naʻá na talanoa mo Misa Peika ʻi he taimi pē ʻe taha, ʻo ʻikai ke fakaʻilongaʻi pea mo ha lea fakakata ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu talanoa, ʻa ia naʻe fakamānako ʻo hangē ko hona teunga hinehina mo hona mata taʻefakatāutahá ʻi he ʻikai ha holi kotoa pē. Naʻá na ʻi heni, pea naʻá na tali au mo Tomu, ʻo na fai pē ha feinga fakafiefia anga fakaʻapaʻapa ke fakafiefiaʻi pe ke fakafiefiaʻi kinaua. Naʻá na ʻiloʻi ʻi he lolotongá ni ʻe ʻosi ʻa e maʻu meʻatokoni efiafí pea ʻi ha kiʻi taimi kimui ange ʻe ʻosi foki mo e efiafí pea tuku noaʻia pē. Naʻe kehe ʻaupito ia mei he Hihifó, ʻa ia naʻe fakavavevaveʻi ai ha efiafi mei he konga ki he konga ki hono fakaʻosí, ʻi ha ʻamanaki fakameʻapangoʻia hokohoko pe ʻi ha manavasiʻi fakaʻauha ʻaupito ki he momenití tonu.

“ʻOkú ke ʻai au ke u ongoʻi taʻesivilaise, Teisi,” ko ʻeku fakahā ia ʻi heʻeku ipu hono ua ʻo e corky ka ʻoku ʻikai ke fuʻu fakaofo ʻa e claret. “He ʻikai ke ke lava ʻo talanoa ki he ngoué pe ko ha meʻa pē?”

Naʻe ʻikai ke u ʻuhinga makehe ki ha meʻa ʻi he fakakaukau ko ʻení, ka naʻe toʻo hake ia ʻi ha founga taʻeʻamanekina.

“ʻOku ʻalu ʻa e sivilaisé ʻo movetevete,” ko e lea fakamālohi ia ʻa Tomí. “Kuó u hoko ko ha tokotaha fakatuʻamelie fakalilifu fekauʻaki mo e ngaahi meʻá. Kuó ke lau ʻa e Ko e Hake ʻa e Ngaahi ʻEmipaea Lanu Matalá ʻe he tangata ko ʻeni ko Goddard?”

“Ko e hā, ‘ikai,” ko ‘eku talí ia, ‘o ‘ikai fu‘u ‘ohovale ‘i hono le‘ó.

"Sai, ko ha tohi lelei ia, pea ʻoku totonu ke lau ia ʻe he tokotaha kotoa pē. Ko e fakakaukaú kapau he ʻikai ke tau vakai ki tuʻa ʻe —ʻe fakatolonga ʻaupito ʻa e matakali hinehina. Ko e ngaahi meʻa fakasaienisi kotoa pē ia; kuo fakamoʻoniʻi ia.”

“ʻOku loloto ʻaupito ʻa Tomu,” ko e lea ia ʻa Teisi, mo ha fakahaaʻi ʻo e loto-mamahi taʻefakakaukauʻí. “ʻOkú ne lau ha ngaahi tohi loloto ʻoku ʻi ai ha ngaahi lea lōloa ʻi ai.Ko e hā ʻa e foʻi lea ko ia naʻa mau—”

“Naʻe ngalo ke u ʻeke atu ha meʻa, pea ʻoku mahuʻinga ia. Naʻá ma fanongo naʻá ke mali mo ha taʻahine ʻi tuʻa ʻi Hihifo.”

“ʻOku moʻoni ia,” ko e fakamoʻoni angaʻofa ia ʻa Tomí. “Naʻá ma fanongo kuó ke mali.”

“Ko ha lauʻikoviʻi ia. ʻOku ou fuʻu masiva.”

“Ka na‘á ma fanongo ki ai,” ko e fakamamafa ia ‘a Teisi, ‘o ne faka‘ohovale‘i au ‘aki ‘ene toe fakaava mai ‘i ha founga hangē ha matala‘i‘akaú. “Naʻá ma fanongo ki ai mei ha toko tolu, ko ia ʻoku pau pē ʻoku moʻoni.”

Ko e moʻoni naʻá ku ʻiloʻi ʻa e meʻa naʻá na lave ki aí, ka naʻe ʻikai ke u aʻu ʻo mali taʻemahino. Ko e mo'oni na'e pulusi 'e he gossip 'a e ngaahi banns ko e taha ia 'o e ngaahi 'uhinga na'a ku ha'u ai ki Hahake. He'ikai ke ke tuku ho'o 'alu mo ha kaume'a motu'a koe'uhi ko e ngaahi talanoa, pea 'i he tafa'aki 'e taha na'e 'ikai ha'aku fakakaukau ke talanoa'i au ki he mali.

Naʻe ʻikai fuʻu uesia au ʻe heʻenau mahuʻingaʻiá pea ʻai ke nau siʻisiʻi ange ʻenau koloaʻia mamaʻó —neongo ia, naʻá ku puputuʻu mo kiʻi fakaliliʻa ʻi heʻeku fakaʻuli ʻo ʻalú. Naʻe hangē kiate au ko e meʻa ke fai ʻe Teisi ko ʻene lele atu mei he falé, ʻo ʻi ai haʻane tamasiʻi —ka ʻoku mahino naʻe ʻikai ha ngaahi taumuʻa pehē ʻi hono ʻulú. Ko e me‘a kia Tomu, ko e mo‘oni‘i me‘a ko ia na‘á ne “‘i ai ha fefine ‘i Niu ‘Ioké” na‘e si‘i ange mo‘oni ‘ene faka‘ohovalá ‘i he‘ene loto-mafasia ‘i ha tohi. Naʻe ʻi ai ha meʻa naʻá ne ʻai ia ke ne nibble ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e ngaahi fakakaukau motuʻá ʻo hangē ʻoku ʻikai ke toe fafangaʻi ʻe heʻene fakaʻeiʻeiki fakaesino fefeká hono loto fakaʻeiʻeikí.

Naʻe ʻosi loloto ʻa e faʻahitaʻu māfaná ʻi he ngaahi ʻato ʻo e ngaahi fale ʻi he halá pea ʻi muʻa ʻi he ngaahi fale tauʻanga meʻalele ʻi he veʻehalá, ʻa ia naʻe tangutu ai ha ngaahi pamu penisini kulokula foʻou ʻi tuʻa ʻi ha ngaahi vaitupu ʻo e māmá, pea ʻi heʻeku aʻu ki hoku ʻēsiti ʻi West Egg ne u leleʻi ʻa e kaá ʻi lalo ʻi hono fale ʻo e kau ʻakaú ʻo tangutu ʻi ha kiʻi taimi ʻi ha foʻi mohuku liʻaki ʻi he lotoʻaá. Naʻe puhiʻi atu ʻa e matangí, ʻo tuku mai ha pō leʻolahi mo ngingila, mo e kapakau ʻoku tā ʻi he ʻuluʻakaú mo ha leʻo ʻo e ʻōkani ʻoku kei hokohoko atu ʻi hono puhiʻi ʻe he ngaahi ʻuʻulu fonu ʻo e māmaní ʻa e fanga poto fonu ʻi he moʻuí. Naʻe lulululu ʻa e siluetā ʻo ha pusi ʻoku ngaʻunu ʻi he maama ʻo e māhiná, pea ʻi heʻeku tafoki hoku ʻulú ke siofi iá, ne u sio ʻoku ʻikai ke u tokotaha pē —ʻi ha fute ʻe nimangofulu mei ai kuo ʻasi hake ha fakatātā mei he malumalu ʻo e fale lahi ʻo hoku kaungāʻapí pea naʻá ne tuʻu mo hono ongo nimá ʻi hono ongo kató ʻo fekauʻaki mo e pepa siliva ʻo e fetuʻú. Naʻe fokotuʻu mai ʻe ha meʻa ʻi heʻene ngaʻunu fakafiemālié mo e tuʻunga malu ʻo hono vaʻé ʻi he ngoueʻangá ko Misa Ketipi tonu ia, ʻo haʻu ki tuʻa ke fakapapauʻi pe ko e hā ʻene vahe ʻi homau ngaahi langi fakalotofonuá.

Naʻá ku fakakaukau ke u telefoni kiate ia. Naʻe lave ʻa Misa Peika kiate ia ʻi he maʻu meʻatokoni efiafí, pea ʻe ʻaonga ia ki ha fakafeʻiloaki. Ka naʻe ʻikai ke u ui ia, he naʻá ne fai mai ha fakaʻilonga fakafokifā ʻokú ne fiemālie pē ke nofo tokotaha —naʻá ne mafao atu hono ongo nimá ki he vai fakapoʻulí ʻi ha founga fie ʻilo, pea, ʻi heʻeku mamaʻo meiate iá, naʻe mei lava pē ke u fuakava ʻokú ne tetetete. Naʻá ku sio taʻefakakaukau ki tahi —pea naʻe ʻikai ke u fakafaikehekeheʻi ha meʻa tukukehe pē ha maama lanu mata ʻe taha, miniti mo mamaʻo, ʻa ia mahalo ko e ngataʻanga ia ʻo ha taulanga. ʻI heʻeku toe kumi tuʻo taha kia Ketisí kuó ne puli atu, pea naʻá ku toe nofo tokotaha pē ʻi he fakapoʻuli taʻefakalongolongó.

II

‘I he vaeua‘anga nai ‘o e West Egg mo Niu ‘Ioké ‘oku kau fakavavevave ai ‘a e hala mīsiní ki he halanga lēlué pea lele ‘i hono tafa‘akí ‘i ha kuata maile, koe‘uhi ke ne fakasi‘isi‘i mei ha feitu‘u pau ‘o e fonuá ‘oku li‘aki. Ko ha teleʻa efu ʻeni —ko ha faama fakaofo ʻoku tupu ai ʻa e efu ʻo hangē ha uité ʻo hoko ko ha ngaahi ʻotu moʻunga mo ha ngaahi ngoue fakaofo; ‘a ia ‘oku ma‘u ai ‘e he efuefu ‘a e ngaahi fōtunga ‘o e ngaahi fale mo e ngaahi simeni mo e kohu ‘oku ‘alu hake pea, faka‘osí, ‘i ha feinga fakalaka, ‘o e kau tangata hina efuefu, ‘a ia ‘oku nau nga‘unu fakapo‘uli mo ‘osi ngingila ‘i he ‘ea efuefu. ‘I he taimi ‘e ni‘ihi ‘oku totolo mai ha laine ‘o e ngaahi kā hina ‘i ha hala ta‘ehāmai, ‘o ‘omai ha ngingila fakalilifu, pea ‘oku nau ha‘u ‘o mālōlō, pea ‘i he taimi pē ko iá ‘oku ‘oho mai ‘a e kau tangata hina efuefu ‘aki ‘a e ngaahi fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i ‘akau ‘oku ‘i ai ‘enau ngaahi fo‘i ‘uhiki‘i ‘akau ‘oku ‘i ai ‘a e ‘ao ‘oku ‘ikai lava ke hū ki ai, ‘a ia ‘okú ne faka‘ata‘atā ‘enau ngaahi ngāue fakapo‘ulí mei ho‘o sió.

Ka ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he kelekele hina mo e ngaahi spasms ʻo e efu fakapoʻuli ʻoku tafe taʻe ngata ʻi aí, ʻokú ke fakatokangaʻi, hili ha kiʻi momeniti, ʻa e mata ʻo Toketā T. J. Eckleburg. Ko e mata ‘o Toketā T. J. Eckleburg ‘oku lanu pulū mo lahi fau—ko honau ngaahi mata‘itofé ‘oku ‘iate ‘e taha hono mā‘olungá. ʻOku nau sio mei he ʻikai ha fofonga, ka, ʻi hono kehé, mei ha ongo sioʻata kulokula lahi ʻaupito ʻa ia ʻoku fakalaka ʻi ha ihu ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai. ‘Oku mahino na‘e fokotu‘u kinaua ‘e ha ni‘ihi ‘o ha kau toketā mata ‘i ai ke nau ngako ‘ene tō‘ongá ‘i he kolomu‘a ‘o Kuinisí, peá ne toki ngoto hifo ia ki he kui ta‘engatá, pe ngalo kinautolu ‘o ne hiki. Ka ko hono matá, ʻa ia naʻe kiʻi fakapoʻuli ʻi he ngaahi ʻaho taʻe-vali lahi, ʻi he lalo laʻaá mo e ʻuhá, ʻokú ne fakakaukauʻi ʻa e feituʻu lingiʻanga veve fakaʻeiʻeikí.

Ko e tele‘a ‘o e efuefu ‘oku fakangatangata ia ‘i he tafa‘aki ‘e taha ‘e ha ki‘i vaitafe ‘uli, pea, ‘i he taimi ‘oku ‘alu hake ai ‘a e halafakakavakava ke fakalaka atu ‘a e ngaahi vaká, ‘e lava ke sio fakamama‘u ‘a e kau pāsese ‘i he ngaahi lēlue tatali ki he feitu‘u fakalolomá ‘o a‘u ki he vaeua houa. ʻOku ʻi ai maʻu pē ha tuʻu ʻi ai ʻo ʻikai siʻi hifo ʻi ha miniti ʻe taha, pea koeʻuhí ko e meʻá ni naʻá ku fuofua fetaulaki ai mo e fineʻeiki ʻo Tom Buchanan.

Ko e mo‘oni‘i me‘a ko ia na‘á ne ma‘u ha taha na‘e fakamamafa‘i ia ‘i ha feitu‘u pē na‘e ‘ilo‘i ai iá. Na‘e ‘ita hono ngaahi mahení ‘i he‘ene ‘asi mai ‘i he ngaahi falekai manakoá mo iá pea, ‘i he‘ene tuku ia ‘i ha tēpile, ‘o ne ‘eve‘eva holo, ‘o talanoa mo ha taha pē na‘á ne ‘ilo‘i. Neongo naʻá ku fie ʻilo ke sio kiate ia, ka naʻe ʻikai haʻaku holi ke feʻiloaki mo ia —ka naʻá ku fai ia. Naʻá ku ʻalu hake mo Tomu ki Niu ʻIoke ʻi he lēlué ʻi ha hoʻatā ʻe taha, pea ʻi heʻemau tuʻu ʻi he ngaahi fuʻu efuefuʻi ʻakaú naʻá ne puna hake ki hono vaʻé pea, ʻi heʻene puke hoku tuliki, naʻá ne fakamālohiʻi moʻoni au mei he kaá.

Naʻá ne fakamamafaʻi mai, “ʻOku mau hifo.” “ʻOku ou fie maʻu ke ke feʻiloaki mo ʻeku taʻahiné.”

ʻOku ou pehē naʻá ne tanked ha meʻa lelei ʻi he maʻu meʻatokoni hoʻataá, pea mo ʻene fakapapau ke ʻi ai ʻeku kautaha ʻoku fakangatangata ʻi he fakamālohí. Ko e fakakaukau fakaʻeiʻeikí ko e hoʻatā Sāpaté naʻe ʻikai haʻaku meʻa lelei ange ke fai.

Naʻá ku muimui ʻiate ia ʻi ha ʻā lēlue maʻulalo kuo vali hinehina, peá ma lue atu ki mui ʻi ha ʻiate ʻe teau ʻi he halá ʻi he sio fakamamaʻu ʻa Toketā ʻEkilipeki. Ko e fale pē ʻe taha naʻe sio ki aí ko ha kiʻi poloka piliki kulokula naʻe tangutu ʻi he tafaʻaki ʻo e kelekele maumaú, ko ha faʻahinga Hala Lahi ʻoku ngāueʻaki ki ai, pea ʻoku fehangahangai mo e ʻikai ʻaupito ha meʻa. Ko e taha ʻo e ngaahi falekoloa ʻe tolu naʻe ʻi aí naʻe totongi pea ko e taha ko ha falekai ʻoku pō kakato, ʻo fakaofiofi atu ki ai ha hala ʻo e efuefu; ko hono tolú ko ha fale tauʻanga meʻalele— Ngaahi Fakaleleiʻanga. Siaosi B. Uilisoni. Ngaahi me'alele na'e fakatau mo fakatau atu. —peá u muimui ʻia Tomu ki loto.

Naʻe ʻikai ke lakalakaimonūʻia ʻa e loto fonuá pea naʻe ʻikai ha meʻa ʻi ai; ko e kā pē naʻe hā maí ko e foʻi kā Ford naʻe ʻufiʻufi ʻe he efu ʻa ia naʻe punou ʻi ha tuliki fakapoʻuli. Naʻe haʻu ki hoku ʻatamaí ko e ʻata ko ʻeni ʻo ha fale tauʻanga meʻalelé kuo pau pē ko ha fakaʻuli, pea ʻoku fufū ʻa e ngaahi fale nofoʻanga fakaʻofoʻofa mo fakaʻofoʻofá ʻi ʻolunga, ʻi he taimi naʻe hā mai ai ʻa e tokotaha ʻoku ʻaʻaná tonu ʻi he matapā ʻo ha ʻōfisi, ʻo ne holoholoʻi hono ongo nimá ʻi ha konga veve. Ko ha tangata lanu kulokula, taʻe-laumālie, ʻanemia, mo fakaʻofoʻofa vaivai. ʻI heʻene sio mai kiate kimauá naʻe puna hake ha ulo ʻo e ʻamanaki leleí ʻi hono ongo mata lanu pulū māmá.

“Mālō, Uilisoni, motuʻa,” ko e lea ia ʻa Tomí, ʻo ne tā fiefia ia ʻi hono umá. “ʻOku fēfē ʻa e pisinisí?”

“He ʻikai ke u lava ʻo lāunga,” ko e tali taʻefakatuipau ia ʻa Uilisoní. “Ko e fē taimi te ke fakatau mai ai kiate au ʻa e kā ko iá?”

“ʻI he uike kahaʻú; kuó u ʻai ʻeku tangatá ke ngāue ki ai he taimí ni.”

“ʻOku ngāue māmālie ʻaupito, ʻikai ko ia?”

“ʻIkai, ʻoku ʻikai ke ne fai ia,” ko e lea momoko ia ʻa Tomí. “Pea kapau ʻokú ke ongoʻi pehē fekauʻaki mo ia, mahalo ʻe sai ange ke u fakatau atu ia ʻi ha feituʻu kehe hili iá.”

“ʻOku ʻikai ke u ʻuhinga pehē,” ko e fakamatala vave ia ʻa Uilisoní. “Naʻá ku ʻuhinga pē—”

Naʻe mole atu hono leʻó pea sio taʻekātaki ʻa Tomu ʻi he fale tauʻanga meʻalelé. Peá u fanongo ki ha ngaahi laka ʻi ha sitepu, pea ʻi ha kiʻi momeniti siʻi naʻe taʻofi ʻe he fōtunga matolu ʻo ha fefine ʻa e maama mei he matapā ʻo e ʻōfisí. Naʻá ne ʻi he vaeuaʻanga ʻo e taʻu tolungofulú, pea naʻe vaivai hono sinó, ka naʻá ne ʻave fakaʻofoʻofa hono kakanó ʻo hangē ko ia ʻoku lava ʻe he kakai fefine ʻe niʻihi. Ko hono fofongá, ʻi ʻolunga ʻi ha teunga lanu pulū ʻuliʻuli ʻo e crêpe-de-chine, naʻe ʻikai ha tafaʻaki pe ngingila ʻo e fakaʻofoʻofá, ka naʻe ʻi ai ha moʻui ʻoku ʻiloʻi vave ʻiate ia ʻo hangē ʻoku kei kohu maʻu pē ʻa e ngaahi neave ʻo hono sinó. Naʻá ne malimali māmālie pea, ʻi heʻene lue atu ʻi hono husepānití ʻo hangē ha laumālié, naʻá ne lulululu mo Tomu, ʻo ne sio kulokula hono matá. Peá ne toki fakamomoko hono loungutú, pea ʻikai ke ne tafoki ʻo ne lea ki hono husepānití ʻi ha leʻo vaivai mo fefeka:

“Maʻu ha ngaahi sea, ko e hā ʻoku ʻikai ke ke maʻu aí, ke tangutu hifo ha taha.”

“ʻOiauē, pau,” ko e loto-tatau fakavavevave ia ʻa Uilisoní, peá ne ʻalu atu ki he kiʻi ʻōfisí, ʻo ne fefiofi leva mo e lanu sima ʻo e ngaahi holisí. Naʻe ʻufiʻufi ʻe ha efuefu hinehina hono suti ʻuliʻulí mo hono louʻulu engeengá ʻi heʻene ʻufiʻufi ʻa e meʻa kotoa pē ʻi he feituʻú —tukukehe pē hono uaifí, ʻa ia naʻá ne hiki ofi kia Tomu.

Naʻe pehē fakamātoato mai ʻe Tomu, “ʻOku ou fie sio kiate koe.” “Heka ki he lēlue hokó.”

"Sai pē."

“Te u fakafetaulaki atu kiate koe ʻi he veʻe tuʻuʻanga nusipepá ʻi he levolo ʻi laló.”

Naʻá ne luelue atu ʻo ʻunu meiate ia ʻi he taimi pē naʻe hū mai ai ʻa Siaosi Uilisoni mo ha ongo sea mei he matapā ʻo hono ʻōfisí.

Naʻá ma tatali kiate ia ʻi he halá pea ʻikai ke sio ki ai. Ko ha ngaahi ʻaho siʻi pē ia kimuʻa ʻi he ʻaho Fā ʻo Siulaí, pea naʻe fokotuʻu hokohoko ʻe ha kiʻi tamasiʻi ʻĪtali hina mo fuopotopoto ha ngaahi foʻi tōpi ʻi he hala lēlué.

“Ko ha feituʻu fakalilifu, ʻikai ko ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa Tomú, ʻo ne fakafetongi ʻene fofonga fakaʻiseʻisá mo Toketā ʻEkilipeeki.

"Kovi 'aupito."

“ʻOku ʻaonga kiate ia ke ne mavahe.”

“ʻIkai ʻoku fakafepakiʻi ia ʻe hono husepānití?”

“Uilisoni? ʻOkú ne fakakaukau ʻokú ne ʻalu ʻo sio ki hono tuofefiné ʻi Niu ʻIoke. ʻOku fuʻu vale ʻaupito ʻo ʻikai ke ne ʻiloʻi ʻokú ne moʻui.”

Ko ia naʻá ku ʻalu hake fakataha mo Tom Buchanan mo ʻene taʻahiné ki Niu ʻIoke —pe ʻikai ke fuʻu fakataha, he naʻe tangutu fakapotopoto ʻa Misa Uilisoni ʻi ha kā ʻe taha. Naʻe toloi ʻe Tomu ʻa e meʻa lahi ko iá ki he ngaahi ongo ʻo e kau East Egger ko ia ʻe ala ʻi he lēlué.

Naʻá ne liliu hono kofú ki ha musili fakatātā lanu engeenga, ʻa ia naʻe mafola fefeka ʻi hono ongo umá ʻoku ʻikai fuʻu fālahí ʻi hono tokoniʻi ia ʻe Tomu ki he peletifōmú ʻi Niu ʻIoké. Naʻá ne fakatau ʻi he falekoloa nusipepá ha tatau ʻo e Town Tattle mo ha makasini ʻata ngaʻunu, pea ʻi he falekoloa faitoʻo ʻi he siteisí naʻá ne fakatau ai ha kilimi momoko mo ha kiʻi hina meʻa namu kakala. ʻI ʻolunga, ʻi he fakaʻuli ʻoku ongo fakaʻeiʻeikí naʻá ne tuku ke ʻalu ha ngaahi tekisī ʻe fā kimuʻa peá ne fili ha tekisī foʻou, lanu lavender mo e ʻufiʻufi hina, pea ʻi he meʻá ni naʻá ma luelue atu mei he fuʻu kakai ʻo e tauʻanga meʻalelé ki he laʻā ngingilá. Ka ʻi he taimi pē ko iá naʻá ne tafoki fakavavevave mei he matapaá pea, ʻi heʻene punou ki muʻá, ʻo ne tukituki ʻi he sioʻata ʻi muʻá.

Naʻá ne pehē fakamātoato mai, “ʻOku ou fie maʻu ke maʻu ha taha ʻo e fanga kulii ko iá.” “ʻOku ou fie maʻu ke maʻu ha taha ki he fale nofoʻangá.ʻOku nau fakafiefia ke maʻu —ko ha kulī.”

Naʻá ma poupou ki ha motuʻa hina naʻá ne maʻu ha faitatau fakavalevale mo John D. Rockefeller. ʻI ha kato naʻe taʻalo mei hono kiá naʻe ʻi ai ha fanga kiʻi kulii ʻe hongofulu tupu kimuí ni mai ʻo ha faʻahinga taʻefakapapauʻi.

“Ko e faʻahinga fēfē kinautolu?” ko e fehuʻi vēkeveke ia ʻa Misa Uilisoní, ʻi heʻene haʻu ki he matapā sioʻata ʻo e tekisí.

“Ko e faʻahinga kotoa pē. Ko e hā e faʻahinga ʻokú ke fie maʻú, fineʻeiki?”

“ʻOku ou fie maʻu ha taha ʻo e fanga kulii polisi ko iá; ʻoku ʻikai ke u mahalo naʻá ke maʻu ʻa e faʻahinga ko iá?”

Naʻe sio veiveiua ʻa e tangatá ki he kató, ʻo ne tō ʻi hono nimá ʻo toho hake ha taha, ʻo luelue, ʻi he tuʻa ʻo e kiá.

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe Tomu, “ʻOku ʻikai ko ha kulī polisi ia.”

“ʻIkai, ʻoku ʻikai ko ha kuli polisi tonu ia,” ko e lea ia ʻa e tangatá mo e loto-mamahi ʻi hono leʻó. “ʻOku lahi ange ia ʻi ha ʻAitale.” Naʻá ne fakalaka hono nimá ʻi he tupenu fufulu lanu engeenga ʻo ha tuʻa. “Sio ki he kote ko iá. Ko ha kote. Ko ha kuli ia he ʻikai ke ne teitei fakahohaʻasi koe ʻaki haʻo puke ʻa e momokó.”

“ʻOku ou pehē ʻoku fakaʻofoʻofa,” ko e lea loto-māfana ia ʻa Misa Uilisoní. “Ko e hā hono lahí?”

“Ko e kulī ko iá?” Naʻá ne sio fakaʻofoʻofa ki ai. “ʻE fakamole ʻe he kuli ko iá ha paʻanga ʻe hongofulu kiate koe.”

Ko e Airedale —ʻoku ʻikai ha veiveiua naʻe ʻi ai ha Airedale naʻe hohaʻa ki ai ʻi ha feituʻu, neongo naʻe hinehina fakaʻohovale hono vaʻé —naʻá ne fetongi nima ʻo nofo hifo ʻi he fatafata ʻo Misa Uilisoní, ʻa ia naʻá ne ʻuma ai ki he kote ʻoku ʻikai ke ʻi ai ha ʻeá ʻi he fiefia.

Naʻe pehē ange ʻe Katelini, “Naʻá ke vale ʻiate ia ʻi ha kiʻi taimi.”

“Vale ʻiate ia!” ko e kaila taʻetui ia ʻa Myrtle. “Ko hai naʻá ne pehē ʻoku ou vale ʻiate ia?Naʻe ʻikai ke u teitei vale ange ʻiate ia ʻi heʻeku vale ʻi he tangata ko ia ʻi aí.”

Naʻá ne tuhu fakafokifā mai kiate au, pea naʻe sio tukuakiʻi mai ʻa e tokotaha kotoa pē. Na‘á ku feinga ke fakahaa‘i ‘i he‘eku fakahaa‘í na‘e ‘ikai te u ‘amanekina ha ‘ofa.

“Ko e meʻa pē naʻá ku vale aí ko e taimi naʻá ku mali ai mo iá. Naʻá ku ʻiloʻi leva naʻá ku fai ha fehālaaki. Naʻá ne nō ha suti lelei taha ʻo ha taha ke mali ai, pea naʻe ʻikai ke ne teitei talamai ia kiate au, pea naʻe muimui mai ʻa e tangatá ʻi ha ʻaho ʻe taha ʻi heʻene ʻi tuʻá: ʻʻOiauē, ko hoʻo suti ia?' Naʻá ku pehē ange, ʻKo e fuofua taimi ʻeni ke u fanongo ai ki aí.' Ka naʻá ku ʻoange ia kiate ia peá u toki tokoto hifo ʻo tangi ke taaʻi ʻa e foʻi hivá ʻi he hoʻatā kakató.”

“Na‘e totonu mo‘oni ke ne mavahe meiate ia,” ko e toe hoko atu ia ‘a Catherine kiate aú. “Kuo taʻu ʻe hongofulu mā taha ʻena nofo ʻi he fale tauʻanga meʻalele ko iá. Pea ko Tomu ʻa e fuofua sweetie naʻá ne maʻú.”

Ko e hina uisikií—ko ha hina hono ua—na‘e fiema‘u ma‘u pē ia he taimí ni ‘e he kakai kotoa pē na‘e ‘i aí, tuku kehe pē ‘a Catherine, ‘a ia na‘á ne “ongo‘i lelei tatau pē ‘i he ‘ikai ha me‘a ‘e taha.” Naʻe telefoni ʻa Tomu ki he tokotaha tauhi ʻapí ʻo ne fekauʻi ia ke ne ʻomi ha ngaahi sanuisi fakafiefia, ʻa ia ko ha maʻu meʻatokoni efiafi kakato ʻiate kinautolu pē. Naʻá ku fie ʻalu ki tuʻa ʻo lue fakahahake ki he paʻaké ʻi he poʻuli molumalu, ka ʻi he taimi kotoa pē naʻá ku feinga ai ke ʻalú naʻá ku lavelavea ʻi ha ngaahi fakakikihi vao mo fefeka ʻa ia naʻá ne tohoakiʻi au ki mui, ʻo hangē ha ngaahi maeá, ki hoku seá. Ka ʻi he māʻolunga ʻi he koló kuo pau pē naʻe tokoniʻi ʻe heʻemau laine ʻo e ngaahi matapā sioʻata kulokulá ʻenau vahenga ʻo e fakapulipuli fakaetangatá ki he tokotaha sio fakaʻaho ʻi he ngaahi hala ʻoku fakapoʻulí, pea naʻá ku sio foki kiate ia, ʻo sio hake mo fifili. Naʻá ku ʻi loto mo tuʻa, ʻo fakaʻofoʻofaʻia mo tekeʻi au ʻi he taimi tatau ʻe he kehekehe taʻefaʻalaua ʻo e moʻuí.

Naʻe toho ʻe Maitele hono seá ʻo ofi mai kiate au, pea fakafokifā pē kuo lilingi mai ʻe hono mānava māfaná kiate au ʻa e talanoa ʻo ʻene fuofua fetaulaki mo Tomí.

"Na'e 'i he ongo ki'i sea fehangahangai 'a e taha mo e taha 'oku ma'u pe 'a e ngaahi me'a fakamuimuitaha 'oku toe 'i he lēlue. Na'a ku 'alu hake ki Niu 'Ioke ke sio ki hoku tuofefine pea fakamoleki 'a e po. Na'a ne 'i ha suti teunga mo e ngaahi su kili'imanu pateni, pea na'e 'ikai lava ke u tauhi hoku mata meiate ia, ka ko e taimi kotoa pe na'a ne sio mai ai kiate au na'e pau ke u fakangalingali 'i he ad 'i he ad ke. station naʻá ne ʻi hoku tafaʻakí, pea naʻe lomiʻi ʻe hono kofukofu hinehina ʻa hoku nimá, pea ko ia naʻá ku talaange ki ai kuo pau ke u ui ha polisi, ka naʻá ne ʻiloʻi naʻá ku fuʻu fiefia ʻaupito ʻi heʻeku heka ʻi ha tekisī mo iá naʻe ʻikai ke u meimei ʻiloʻi ʻoku ʻikai ke u heka ki ha lēlue ʻi lalofonua ʻoku ʻikai ke u lava ʻo ʻosi. ‘oku ‘ikai lava ke mo‘ui ta‘engata.’ ”

Naʻá ne tafoki kia Misa Maki pea naʻe tatangi ʻa e lokí ʻo fonu ʻi heʻene kata fakatupú.

"ʻE hoku ʻofaʻanga," naʻá ne tangi, "Te u ʻoatu ʻa e teunga ko ʻení kiate koe ʻi heʻeku ʻosi pē mo iá. Kuo pau ke u maʻu ha taha ʻe taha ʻapongipongi. Te u fai ha lisi ʻo e ngaahi meʻa kotoa pē kuo pau ke u maʻú. Ko ha fakamalohisino mo ha peau, mo ha kola ki he kulií mo ha kiʻi efuefu kaufana silika ki he faʻitoka ʻo e faʻeé ʻe tolonga ʻi he faʻahitaʻu māfaná kotoa kuo pau ke u hiki ha lisi ke ʻoua naʻa ngalo ʻiate au ʻa e ngaahi meʻa kotoa pē kuo pau ke u faí.”

Ko e taimi hivá ia﻿—meimei ʻi he hili pē iá naʻá ku sio ki heʻeku uati ʻo ʻiloʻi kuo hongofulu. Naʻe mohe ʻa Misa Maki ʻi ha sea ʻo pukepuke hono ongo nimá ʻi hono umá, ʻo hangē ha ʻata ʻo ha tangata ngāue. ʻI heʻeku toʻo mai hoku tupenú ne u holoholoʻi mei hono kouʻahé ʻa e foʻi fulufuluʻi ʻuhikiʻi manupuna mātuʻu naʻá ne fakahohaʻasi au ʻi he hoʻataá kotoa.

Naʻe tangutu ʻa e kiʻi kulí ʻi he funga tēpilé ʻo sio fakakuihi ʻi he kohu ʻo e kohu, pea ʻi he taimi ki he taimi naʻá ne tangi vaivai. Naʻe puli ʻa e kakaí, toe ʻasi mai, fai ha ngaahi palani ke nau ʻalu ki ha feituʻu, pea nau toki mole, fekumi, feʻiloaki ʻi ha ngaahi fute siʻi mei ai. ʻI ha taimi ʻi he ofi ki he vaeua poʻulí naʻe tuʻu mata ki he mata ʻa Tomu Pukenani mo Misa Uilisoni ʻo aleaʻi, ʻi ha leʻo taʻeʻofa, pe ʻoku ʻi ai ha totonu ʻa Misa Uilisoni ke ne lave ki he hingoa ʻo Teisií.

“Teisi! Teisi! Teisi!” ko e kaila ia ʻa Misa Uilisoní. “Te u lea ʻaki ia ʻi ha taimi pē te u loto ki ai!Teisi!Tai—.”

ʻI heʻene fai ha ngaʻunu nounou ʻo e deft, naʻe motuhi ʻe Tom Buchanan hono ihú ʻaki hono nima ʻoku fakaava.

Pea naʻe ʻi ai ha ngaahi taueli toto ʻi he faliki ʻo e fale kaukauʻangá, mo ha ngaahi leʻo ʻo e kakai fefiné ʻoku nau fakaanga, pea ʻi ʻolunga ʻi he puputuʻú ha tangi mamahi motuʻa lōloa. Naʻe ʻā hake ʻa Misa Maki mei heʻene mohé ʻo ne kamata ʻi haʻane ʻohovale ʻo fakahangatonu ki he matapaá. ʻI heʻene ʻosi ʻi he vaeuaʻangá naʻá ne tafoki ʻo sio fakamamaʻu ki he meʻa naʻe hokó —naʻe fakaangaʻi mo fakafiemālieʻi hono uaifí mo Katelini ʻi heʻena tūkia ʻi heni mo hena ʻi he lotolotonga ʻo e ngaahi nāunau ʻo e ʻapí naʻe fonu ʻi he ngaahi meʻa tokoni, pea mo e fakatātā loto-moʻua ʻi he sofá, ʻoku tafe toto vave, mo feinga ke folahi ha tatau ʻo e Town sceil Versa ʻo e taperies ʻo e taperies ʻo e taperies Tattle ʻi he ʻolunga ʻo e ʻataá. Pea naʻe tafoki ʻa Misa Maki ʻo hoko atu ʻi tuʻa he matapaá. ʻI heʻeku toʻo hoku hēkesí mei he chandelier, ne u muimui atu.

“Haʻu ʻo maʻu meʻatokoni hoʻatā ʻi ha ʻaho,” ko ʻene fokotuʻú ia, ʻi heʻemau tangi hifo ʻi he ʻelevietá.

"ʻi fē?"

“ʻI ha feituʻu pē.”

“Taʻofi ho ongo nimá mei he levá,” ko e fakaʻohovale ia ʻa e tamasiʻi ʻelevietá.

“ʻOku ou kole fakamolemole atu,” ko e lea ia ʻa Misa Makeí ʻi he anga fakaʻapaʻapá, “naʻe ʻikai ke u ʻiloʻi ʻoku ou ala ki ai.”

“Sai pē,” ko ʻeku loto ki aí, “te u fiefia ke fai ia.”
