III

Kuvua mijiki ya ku nzubu kua muena mutumba nanyi mu butuku bua mu muvu wa été. Mu budimi buende bua bululu balume ne bana ba bakaji bavua balua ne baya bu nyunyi munkatshi mua mitoyi ne maluvu a champagne ne mitoto. Pavua mayi a bungi mu mapingaja mvua ntangila benyi bende benda bapueka mu tshibumba tshia buatu buende, anyi bangata dîba pa nsenga ya kapia ya ku muelelu wende wa mâyi pavua mazuwa ende abidi avua ne motere apita mu mâyi a Sound, akoka ndeke ya mâyi pa mutu pa bipupu bia butoke. Ku ndekelu kua lumingu, mashinyi ende a Rolls-Royce avua alua mashinyi a bantu bonso, avua ambula bantu baya ne bapingana mu tshimenga pankatshi pa dîba 9 wa dinda ne panyima pa dîba dia midi, eku ditempu diende dia mashinyi dienda dienda lubilu bu tshipupu tshia jaune bua kusambakena ne mashinyi onso. Ne mu dinda dia lunu bena mudimu muanda mukulu, kusangisha ne muena madimi mukuabu, bavua benza mudimu dituku dijima ne biamu bia kusukuila nabi bilamba ne bia kusukuila nabi ne marto ne biamu bia kukosela nabi madimi, balongolola bintu bivua binyanguke butuku bua kumpala.

Mu ditanu dionso, bibombi bitanu bia orange ne bimuma bia ludimu bivua bifika kudi muena mudimu wa bimuma mu New York—dituku dionso dia kumpala, bimuma bia orange ne ludimu ebi bivua bipatuka ku tshiibi tshiende tshia panyima mu tshibumba tshia bitupa bivua kabiyi ne mupuya. Kuvua tshiamu kampanda mu tshikuku tshivua mua kupatula mayi a tumuma tua orange nkama ibidi mu tshitupa tshia dîba bikalabu bafina butone bukese misangu nkama ibidi ne tshianza tshinene tshia muena mudimu.

Musangu umua ku mbingu ibidi, tshisumbu tshia bafidi ba biakudia tshivua tshipueka ne tshilamba tshia bule bua metre nkama ya bungi ne miendu ya mekala makumbane bua kuenza mutshi wa Nowele wa mu budimi bunene bua Gatsby. Pa mêsa a buffet, milengeja ne hors-d'oeuvre idi ikenka, nyama ya nyama ya nsongo mienza ne bisombelu miule tente ne salade ya arlequin ne ngulube ne nkutshi ya mampa milengeja ne or mufiike. Mu nzubu munene, nzubu wa kunuina maluvu uvua ne tshilamba tshia tshiamu tshia mitaku tshia bushuwa, uvua muwule tente ne maluvu a gin ne maluvu ne maluvu a nsongo avua bantu bapue muoyo katshia ku kale, ke bualu kayi benyi bende ba bakaji ba bungi bavua bana batekete bua kumanya umue ne mukuabu.

Pa dîba dia muanda mutekete, tshisumbu tshia bimbi ba misambu tshiakafika, ki ntshintu tshikese tshia bantu batanu to, kadi tshina tshijima tshia oboes ne trombones ne saxophones ne violons ne cornets ne piccolos, ne ngoma mipuekele ne mitumbuke. Bantu ba ndekelu badi banaya mâyi mbafume ku muelelu wa mâyi mpindieu ne badi bavuala bilamba bia kuulu; mashinyi afumina ku New York adi mashika mu njila itanu, ne nzubu ya tshilala benyi ne nzubu ya tshilala benyi idi milengeja ne misombelu ya kumpala, ne nsuki milengeja mu mishindu mipiamipia ya dikema, ne bilamba bia ku mutu bidi bipita bilota bia mu Castile. Muaba wa kunuina maluvu udi mu tshibungubungu, ne maluvu adi apita pa mâyi adi avula mu budimi pambelu, too ne padi lupepele luenda luvula ne miyuki ne tuseku, ne mêyi a patupu ne mêyi a ntuadijilu adibu bapue muoyo diakamue, ne bisangilu bia tshisumi pankatshi pa bakaji bavua kabayi banji kumanya mêna a bakuabu.

Miendu idi ikola padi buloba buenda bumbuka ku dîba, ne mpindieu tshisumbu tshia bimbi ba misambu tshidi tshiimba mizike ya cocktail ya jaune, ne opera wa mêyi udi wenda ubandisha tshisanji. Kuseka kudi kupepele minite ku minite, kuvulangana ne dijimija dia bintu, kudi muaku wa disanka. Bisumbu bidi bishintuluka lukasa, bikola ne bantu bapiabapia, binyanguka ne bifuka mu mupuya umue; Kukadi bantu badi bendakana, bana ba bakaji badi badieyemena badi baluka apa ne aku munkatshi mua bantu badi ne bukole ne bashindame, balua bua tshitupa tshîpi tshia disanka, munkatshi mua kasumbu, ne pashishe, ne disanka dia bungi, badi benda bapita mu mbuu wa dishintuluka dia mpala ne mêyi ne dikala muinshi mua butoke budi bushintuluka misangu yonso.

Diakamue umue wa ku ba-gypsy aba, mu tshilamba tshia opal tshidi tshiteketa, udi wangata maluvu mu lupepele, udi uwuela panshi bua kupeta dikima ne, wenda unyunguluja bianza biende bu Frisco, udi unaya nkayende pa tshibandilu tshia tshilamba. Ditalala dia tshitupa tshîpi; mulombodi wa tshisumbu tshia bimbi ba misambu udi ushintulula tshiona tshiende bua bualu buende, ne kudi dikokangana dia bungi padi lumu lua dishima luenda lutangalaka lua se: udi mulongi wa Gilda Gray wa mu tshisumbu tshia Follies. Tshibilu tshituadije.

Ndi ngitabuja ne butuku bua kumpala bumvua muye ku nzubu kua Gatsby mvua umue wa ku benyi bakese bavuabu babikile. Bantu kabavua bababikile to, bavua baya kuntu aku. Bakabuela mu mashinyi avua abafikisha ku Long Island, ne mu mushindu kampanda bakafika ku tshiibi tshia Gatsby. Pavuabu bafike kuntu aku, bakabamanyisha kudi muntu uvua mumanye Gatsby, ne pashishe bakadienzeja bilondeshile mikenji ya bikadilu idi itangila muaba wa manaya. Imue misangu bavua balua ne baya kabayi banji kupetangana ne Gatsby nansha kakese, balua bua tshibilu ne muoyo mupepele uvua anu tike wende wa kubuela.

Bavua bushuwa bambikile. Muendeshi wa mashinyi uvua muvuale bilamba bia bule bua diyi dia robin wakasabuka peluze wanyi mu dinda dia Disambombo adi ne mukanda wa dikema wa kudi mfumuende wa mudimu: buneme buvua mua kuikala anu bua Gatsby, ke muvuaye muambe, meme mubuele mu “fete wende mukese” butuku abu. Uvua mumone misangu ya bungi, ne uvua musue kunbikila kale, kadi malu kampanda avua mamupangishe bua kuenza nanku—mufunda Jay Gatsby, mu tshianza tshilenga.

Meme muvuale bilamba bitoke, ngakaya ku budimi buende panyima pa dîba dia muanda mutekete, ne meme kuendakana tshiyi ne ditalala munkatshi mua bantu bamvua tshiyi mumanye—nansha muvuaku mpala umvua mumone mu kawulu kavua kaendakana. Ngakakema diakamue bua bungi bua bansonga bena Angleterre bavua batangalake; bonso bavuale bimpa, bonso bamueneka bu badi ne nzala, ne bonso bakula ne dîyi dikese, dia bushuwa kudi bena Amerike bakole ne badi ne bubanji. Mvua mujadike ne: bavua bapana tshintu kampanda: makuta anyi assurance anyi mashinyi. Bavua bamanye bimpe menemene makuta avua pabuipi apu ne bavua batuishibue ne: avua abu bua mêyi makese mu tshifungu tshimpe.

Diakamue pangakafika, ngakateta bua kupeta muena nzubu wanyi, kadi bantu babidi anyi basatu bamvua muebeje muaba uvuaye bakantangila mu mushindu wa dikema, ne bakabenga ne tshikisu tshionso tshivuaye wenza, ke meme kuya lubilu mutangile ku mesa a maluvu—muaba umuepele mu budimi uvua muntu umuepele mua kushala kayi utangila nkayende kayi ne tshipatshila.

Mvua mu njila bua kukuatshika maluvu bua bundu bua bungi pavua Jordan Baker mupatuke mu nzubu ne muimane ku mutu kua bibandilu bia mabue a marbre, muinamine panyima kakese ne mutangile mu budimi ne lukunukunu luonso.

Tuakidila anyi kubenga, ngakamona ne: bivua bikengela kudilamakaja ne muntu kampanda kumpala kua meme kutuadija kuakula mêyi mimpe kudi bantu bavua bapita.

"Muoyo wenu!" Ngakela mbila, meme kuya kudiye. Dîyi dianyi divua dimueneka bu dikole mu budimi buonso.

"Mvua ngela meji ne udi mua kuikala muaba ewu," yeye kuandamuna kayi ne lungenyi pamvua mulue. "Ndi mvuluka ne uvua musombele ku luseke lua—"

Wakankuata ku tshianza kayi ne bualu, bu mulayi wa ne: uvua mua kuntabalela mu minite mikese, ne wakateleja bana ba bakaji babidi bavuale bilamba bibidi bia jaune, bavua bimane kuinshi kua tshibandilu.

"Muoyo wenu!" bakadila pamue. "Mfuila koku luse bualu kuena mutshimune to."

Bivua bua manaya a golf. Uvua mutshimune mu manaya a ndekelu lumingu lua kumpala.

Umue wa ku bana ba bakaji bavuale bilamba bia jaune wakamba ne: “Kuena mumanye tuetu banganyi to, kadi tuvua bakumone muaba eu kukadi bu ngondo umue.”

"Ukadi mulengeje nsuki yebe katshia anu pinapu," ke muakamba Jordan, meme kutuadija, kadi bana ba bakaji bavua baye kumpala ne mêyi ende avua atangila ngondo uvua mufike kumpala kua dîba, muenza bu tshiakudia tshia dilolo, kakuyi mpata, mu tshibombi tshia muena mudimu wa biakudia. Ne diboko ditekete dia Jordan dia or mu dianyi, tuakapueka ku bibandilu ne kuendakana mu budimi. Tshibombi tshia maluvu tshiakatutangila mu dilolo, ne tuakasomba ku mesa ne bana ba bakaji babidi bavuale bilamba bia jaune ne balume basatu, yonso wa kudibu mutumanyishe bu Mr. Mumble.

"Utuku ulua mu bibilu ebi misangu ya bungi anyi?" ke muvua Jordan muebeje nsongakaji uvua ku luseke luende.

"Wa ndekelu ke umvua mupetangane nebe," ke muakandamuna nsongakaji, ne dîyi dia dishindika dia budimu. Wakakudimuka kudi mulunda wende wamba ne: “Kabivua bua wewe anyi Lucille anyi?”

Bivua bua Lucille, pende.

"Ndi musue kulua," Lucille unena. "Tshiena ne bualu bua tshindi ngenza to, nunku ntu anu ne tshikondo tshimpe. Pamvua muaba eu musangu wa ndekelu ngakapanda tshilamba tshianyi pa nkuasa, ne yeye kungebeja dina dianyi ne adrese wanyi—mu lumingu lumue ngakapeta tshintu tshia kudi Croirier tshivua ne tshilamba tshipiatshipia tshia dilolo."

"Udi mulame anyi?" Jordan wakakonka.

"Bushuwa mvua muenze nanku. Mvua ne bua kutshivuala butuku ebu, kadi tshivua tshinene mu tshiadi ne bivua bikengela kutshishintulula. Tshivua tshia bule bua gaz ne mabue a lavender. Dolare nkama ibidi ne makumi asambombo ne itanu."

"Kudi bualu kampanda budi busekesha bua muntu udi mua kuenza bualu bua nunku," ke muakamba nsongakaji mukuabu ne disanka dionso. "Kena musue lutatu nansha lumue ne mubidi nansha umue to."

"Nnganyi udi kayi mumanye?" Ngakakonka.

"Gatsby. Muntu mukuabu uvua mungambile ne—"

Bana ba bakaji babidi aba ne Jordan bakadisangisha pamue mu musokoko.

"Muntu mukuabu wakangambila ne: bavua bela meji ne: uvua mushipe muntu musangu umue."

Disanka diakapita tuetu bonso. Ba Mr. Mumbles basatu bakainama kumpala ne kuteleja ne disanka dionso.

"Tshiena ngela meji ne bidi bia bungi nunku to," ke muvua Lucille muambe ne dielakana; "Bidi bitamba kuikala muena Allemagne mu tshikondo tshia mvita."

Umue wa ku bantu balume wakatua mpala panshi bua kujadika.

"Mvua mumvue bualu ebu kudi muntu uvua mumanye malu ende onso, uvua mukolele nende mu Allemagne," wakatujadikila ne muoyo mujima.

Nsongakaji wa kumpala wakamba ne: “Oh, to, kabivua mua kuikala nanku to, bualu uvua mu tshiluilu tshia bena Amerike mu tshikondo tshia mvita.” Bu muvua ditabuja dietu dipingana kudiye, wakaditua kumpala ne tshisumi. "Udi umutangila imue misangu padiye wela meji ne: kakuena muntu udi umutangila to. Ndi ngela meji ne: uvua mushipe muntu."

Wakafinya mêsu ende ne kukankama. Lucille wakateketa. Tuetu bonso tuakakudimuka ne tuetu kutangila Gatsby. Bivua bujadiki bua lungenyi lua dinanga luvuaye mujule ne: kuvua mêyi a mutoyi avua atangila bualu buende kudi aba bavua bapete malu makese avua ne bua kuakula pa buloba ebu.

Didia dia kumpala dia dilolo—divua ne bua kuikala dikuabu panyima pa midi—divua mpindieu difila, ne Jordan wakambikila bua kubuela mu tshisumbu tshiende, bavua batangalake ku mêsa ku luseke lukuabu lua budimi. Kuvua bena dibaka basatu ne mulombodi wa Jordan, mulongi wa tulasa tua bikadilu uvua ne tshikisu, ne uvua wela meji ne: mu katupa kîpi emu Jordan uvua ne bua kumulekela mu mushindu munene anyi mukese. Pamutu pa kuenza malu a bungi, tshisumbu etshi tshivua tshilame buobumue bua kanemu, ne tshivua tshidiangate mudimu wa kuleja bantu banene ba mu misoko—East Egg uvua udipuekesha kudi West Egg ne uvua mudimuke bimpe bua kubenga kuikala ne disanka dia bungi.

"Tupatuke," ke muakamba Jordan, kunyima kua tshitupa tshia dîba tshia tshianana ne tshia tshianana; "etshi ntshimpe menemene kundi."

Tuakajuka, yeye kumvuija ne: tuvua tuya kukeba muena nzubu: tshivua muanji kumonangana nende to, ke muvuaye muambe, ne bivua bintonda. Mulongi wa kalasa ka kumpala wakatua mpala panshi mu mushindu wa kanyinganyinga, wa kanyinganyinga.

Muaba wa kunua maluvu, mutuvua batangile kumpala, uvua muwule tente ne bantu, kadi Gatsby kavuaku to. Kavua mua kumupeta ku mutu kua tshibandilu to, ne kavua pa veranda to. Mu mpukapuka, tuakateta tshiibi tshivua tshimueneka tshia mushinga, ne tuetu kubuela mu nzubu wa mikanda mule wa Gothic, muenza ne mitshi ya chêne ya mu Angleterre misonga, ne pamuapa uvuabu bambule mujima ku tshibumba kampanda tshia ku mutu kua mâyi.

Muntu munene, wa bidimu bia pankatshi, ne manyanu manene a tshisuku, uvua musombe mukuatshike ku muelelu kua mesa manene, mutangile ne ntema mikole ku bitanda bia mikanda. Patuvua tubuela, wakanyunguluka ne disanka dionso ne kutangilaye Jordan kumbukila ku mutu too ne ku makasa.

"Udi wela meji kayi?" wakalomba ne tshiji tshionso.

"Bua tshinyi?"

Wakabandisha tshianza tshiende mutangile ku bitanda bia mikanda.

"Bua bualu ebu. Mu bulelela, kuena ne bua kuditatshisha bua kujadika to. Ndi mujadike. Badi balelela."

"Mikanda?"

Wakatua mpala panshi.

"Mbilelela menemene—bidi ne mabeji ne bionso. Mvua ngela meji ne: nebikale karton muimpe wa kashidi. Mu bulelela, mbilelela menemene. Mabeji ne—Mona! Lekela nkuleje."

Bu muvuaye wangata dielakana dietu bu dia patupu, wakaya lubilu ku bitanda bia mikanda ne kupinganaye ne mukanda wa kumpala wa miyuki ya Stoddard .

"Tangila!" wakadila ne ditshimuna. "I mukanda mufunda wa bushuwa. Mmushime. Muntu eu udi muena Belasco wa pa tshibidilu. Mmutshimune. Mmushindu kayipu! Mmu bulelela kayi! Uvua mumanye dîba dia kuimana, pende—kavua mukose mabeji to. Kadi udi musue tshinyi? Udi mutekemene tshinyi?"

Wakannyenga mukanda ne kuwupingaja lukasa pa tshitekelu tshiawu, wamba ne: bobu bumbushe dibue dimue, nzubu wa mikanda mujima uvua mua kukuluka.

"Nnganyi uvua mukufikishe?" wakalomba. "Anyi uvua mulue anu? Bavua balue nanyi. Bantu ba bungi bavua balue nabu."

Jordan wakamutangila ne budimu, ne disanka, kayi wandamuna to.

Wakatungunuka ne kuamba ne: “Mvua mulue nanyi kudi mukaji mukuabu diende Roosevelt.” "Mamu Claud Roosevelt. Udi mumumanye anyi? Ngakapetangana nende muaba kampanda butuku bua lelu. Ndi mukuatshike maluvu kukadi lumingu lumue mpindieu, ne mvua ngela meji ne: bidi mua kunkolesha bua kusomba mu nzubu wa mikanda."

"Udiku anyi?"

"Katupa kakese, ndi ngela meji. Tshiena muanji kuamba to. Ndi anu muaba eu dîba dimue. Ndi mukuambile bua mikanda anyi? I ya bushuwa. I—"

"Uvua mutuambile."

Tuakamuela bianza ne kanemu ne tuetu kupingana pambelu.

Kuvua maja mpindieu pa tshilamba mu budimi; bantu bakulumpe badi bapingaja bana ba bakaji batyetye kunyima mu bijengu bia tshiendelele bidi kabiyi ne bulenga, bena dibaka banene badi bakuatangana mu mushindu mubi, mu tshilele, ne balama mu matumba—ne bungi bunene bua bana ba bakaji badi kabayi baselangane badi banaya nkayabu anyi balekela tshisumbu tshia bimbi ba misambu bua tshitupa tshîpi bujitu bua banjo anyi bua mateyi. Pa midi, disanka diakavula. Muena tenor uvua mumanyike bikole uvua muimbe mu tshiena-Italie, ne muena contralto uvua mumanyike bibi uvua muimbe mu jazz, ne pankatshi pa nomba ayi bantu bavua benza “bienzedi bia dikema” mu budimi buonso, eku tuseku tua disanka ne tua patupu tuenda tuenda tuya muulu mu muvu wa été. Mapasa abidi a ku tshibandilu, avua amueneka bu bana ba bakaji bavuale bilamba bia jaune, bakenza tshienzedi tshia muana mu bilamba, ne bavua bafila champagne mu makopo manene kupita makopo a minu. Ngondo uvua mubande bikole, ne mu Sound muvua tshijengu tshia bipese bia arjan, tshienda tshiteketa kakese ku ditontolola dikole dia banjoes pa peluze.

Mvua anu ne Jordan Baker. Tuvua basombe ku mêsa ne muntu mukuabu wa bidimu bianyi ne muana wa bakaji mukese uvua ne tshimvundu, uvua useka bikole pavuabu bamuenzela bibi. Mvua ndisankisha mpindieu. Mvua muangate makopo abidi a champagne, ne tshikena kumona tshivua tshishintuluke kumpala kuanyi tshilue tshintu tshia mushinga mukole, tshia ntuadijilu ne tshia mushinga mukole.

Mu tshikondo tshia dijikija lutetuku, muntu au wakantangila ne kuseka.

"Mpala webe mmumanyibue," ke muvuaye muambe ne bupole buonso. "Kuvua mu tshitupa tshia kumpala mu tshikondo tshia mvita anyi?"

"Bua tshinyi eyowa. Mvua mu tshiluilu tshia makumi abidi ne muanda mukulu."

"Mvua mu bidimu dikumi ne bisambombo too ne mu ngondo wa 6 ne dikumi ne muanda mukulu. Mvua mumanye ne: mvua mukumone muaba kampanda kumpala."

Tuakayikila bua tshitupa tshîpi bua misoko mikese ya mu France idi ne mâyi ne mitoke. Bidi bimueneka ne: uvua musombele muaba eu, bualu wakangambila ne: uvua mufuma ku disumba ndeke wa mâyi, ne uvua uya kumuteta mu dinda.

"Udi musue kuya nanyi, manaya a kale anyi? Anu pabuipi ne muelelu wa mâyi ku muelelu wa mâyi."

"Dîba kayi?"

"Dîba dionso didi dikusankisha."

Bivua ku mutu kua ludimi luanyi bua kuebeja dina diende pavua Jordan mutangile kudiye ne musekelele.

"Udi ne tshikondo tshia masandi mpindieu anyi?" wakakonka.

"Bimpe be." Ngakakudimuka kabidi kudi mumanyi wanyi mupiamupia. "Etshi ntshibilu tshia pa buadi kundi. Tshiena nansha mumone muena nzubu to. Ndi musombele kuntu aku—" Ngakabandisha tshianza tshianyi ku tshimanu tshidi katshiyi tshimueneka ku bule, "ne muntu eu Gatsby wakatuma kudi muendeshi wende wa mashinyi ne dibikila."

Bua tshitupa tshîpi wakantangila bienze anu bu uvua mupangile bua kumvua.

"Ndi Gatsby," udi wamba diakamue.

"Tshinyi!" Ngakamba. "Oh, ndi nkulomba luse."

"Mvua ngela meji ne: uvua mumanye, manaya a kale. Ndi ne buôwa bua se: tshiena muakididi wa benyi muimpe to."

Wakaseka ne lungenyi lua kumvuangana—bipite bungi kupita kumvuangana. I umo wa ku mimuemue idi kayiyi mitamba kumueneka idi ne ngikadilu wa dishindika dia kashidi, iudi mua kupeta misangu inayi anyi itanu mu nsombelu webe. Tshivua tshitangile—anyi tshivua tshimueneka tshitangila—buloba bujima bua tshiendelele bua tshitupa tshîpi, ne pashishe tshitangile kuudi ne kansungansunga kadiyi mua kukandamena mu diakalenga diebe. Tshivua tshikumvua anu muudi musue bua bakumvuile, tshikuitabuje bu muudi musue kuitabuja wewe nkayebe, ne tshikujadikila ne: tshivua ne lungenyi luebe luvua, mu mushindu muimpe, utekemena bua kufila. Mu tshikondo atshi tshiakajimina—ne mvua mutangile nsongalume wa tshikisu, tshidimu tshimue anyi bibidi pa mutu pa makumi asatu, uvua ne ngakuilu wende mujalame uvua anu mupangile bua kuikala wa bupote. Kumpala kua yeye kudimanyisha, mvua ne lungenyi lua se: uvua usungula mêyi ende ne ntema yonso.

Pabuipi ne dîba divua Mr. Gatsby mudimanyishe, muena mudimu kampanda wakalua lubilu kudiye ne lumu lua ne: Chicago uvua umubikila ku telefone. Wakadilomba luse ne buta bukese buvua bubueja yonso wa kutudi mu mulongo.

"Wewe musue tshintu, tshilombe, manaya a kale," wakankankamija. "Mfuila koku luse. Nendue kukumona pashishe."

Pavuaye muye, ngakakudimuka diakamue kudi Jordan—muenzejibue bua kumujadikila ne: mvua mukeme. Mvua mutekemene ne: Mr. Gatsby uvua mua kuikala muntu wa bukole ne wa mubidi mukole mu bidimu biende bia pankatshi.

"Yeye nganyi?" Ngakalomba. "Udi mumanye anyi?"

"Udi anu muntu diende Gatsby."

"Ndi musue kuamba ne: udi ufumina penyi? Ne udi wenza tshinyi?"

"Mpindieu udi mutuadije bualu ebu," yeye kuandamuna ne muoyo mujima. "Eyowa, wakangambila musangu umua ne uvua muntu wa mu Oxford."

Tshintu tshifiike tshiakatuadija kumueneka panyima pende, kadi ku mêyi ende avua malonda tshiakajika.

"Nansha nanku, tshiena ngitabuja to."

"Bua tshinyi?"
