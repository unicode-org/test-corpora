Ngakaya nende mu tshilaminu tshia biakudia, muvuaye mutangile muena Finlande ne tshikisu. Tuetu bonso pamue tuakatangila mampa dikumi ne abidi a mu tshisalu tshia bintu bia mushinga mukole.

"Ne benza anyi?" Ngakakonka.

"Eyowa, bulelela! Badi bimpa!" ne wakasakidila ne, "... manaya a kale."

Mvula wakapueka pabuipi ne pa dîba isatu ne tshitupa too ne ku lupuishi lukole, muvua matonyi makese avua apueka bu lumuma. Gatsby wakatangila ne mêsu kaayi ne tshintu mu mukanda wa Clay wa malu a mpetu , kutuadijaye ku tshibandilu tshia bena Finlande tshivua tshinyunguluka panshi mu tshikuku, ne kutangilaye ku madidishi avua manyanguke misangu ne misangu bienze anu bu ne: malu avua kaayi amueneka kadi avua akuatshisha buôwa avua enzeka pambelu. "Kakuena muntu udi ulua kunua tshiayi to. Bikadi bipite pa dîba!" Wakatangila dîba diende bienze anu bu ne: kuvua dijinga dia lukasa dia dîba diende muaba mukuabu. "Tshiena mua kuindila dituku dijima to."

“Kuikadi mutshimbakane to, bidi anu minite ibidi bua kufika ku inayi.”

Wakasomba panshi bibi, bienze anu bu ne: mvua mumutute, ne musangu umue au kuvua mutoyi wa motere uvua unyunguluka mu njila wanyi. Tuetu bonso babidi tuakajuka, ne, meme kuditatshisha kakese, ngakapatuka mu lupangu.

Muinshi mua mitshi ya lilac ivua ipueka, mashinyi manene avua mabuluke avua alua mu njila. Biakimana. Mpala wa Daisy, mutangile ku luseke muinshi mua tshilamba tshia lavender tshia nseke isatu, wakantangila ne muoyo mujima.

"Eu ke muaba uudi musombele, munanga wanyi?"

Dîyi diende dia disanka divua tshintu tshia bukole mu mvula. Mvua ne bua kulonda mutoyi watshi bua tshitupa tshîpi, mulu ne panshi, ne ditshi dianyi nkayadi, kumpala kua mêyi nansha amue kupatukawu. Mulongo wa nsuki uvua mutoke uvua bu tshitupa tshia peinture wa bule pa ditama diende, ne tshianza tshiende tshivua tshitoke ne matonyi avua akenka pamvua mutshiangate bua kumuambuluisha bua kupatuka mu mashinyi.

"Ke bualu busokoka bua Castle Rackrent. Ambila muendeshi webe bua kuya kule ne kupitshisha dîba dimue."

"Pingana mu diba dimua, Ferdie." Pashishe ne dîyi dikole: "Dîna diende ndi Ferdie."

Tuakabuela. Ku dikema dianyi dia bungi, nzubu wa tshilala benyi uvua mutupu.

"Eyowa, bidi bisekesha," ngakamba.

Bu mumvua mumanye dikuma dikole dia mutshima wanyi nkayanyi, ngakakoka tshiibi bua kukandamena mvula uvua wenda uvula.

"Ndi ne disanka dia bungi dia kukumona kabidi."

Dikisha; wakatantamena bibi menemene. Tshivua ne tshia kuenza mu tshitanda to, ke meme kubuela mu nzubu.

Gatsby, bianza biende bitshidi mu mifuku yende, uvua musombe pa tshilamba tshia ku mutu mu tshimfuanyi tshia dishima tshia dipepele dikumbane, nansha dia dibungama. Mutu wende uvua mushindame panyima bikole menemene uvua muimanyine pa mpala wa dîba dia mantelpiece divua kadiyi dienza mudimu, ne ku muaba eu mêsu ende avua manyanguke avua atangila Daisy, uvua musombe, ne buôwa kadi muikale ne bulenga, ku muelelu wa nkuasa mukole.

"Tuvua bapetangana kumpala," ke muakamba Gatsby. Mêsu ende akantangila tshitupa tshîpi, ne mishiku yende yakapatuka ne diteta dia kuseka dia tshianana. Diakalenga, dîba diakangata tshikondo etshi bua kunyunguluka bikole ku dikuma dia mutu wende, pashishe wakakudimuka ne kudikuata ne minu ivua iteketa, ne kudipingaja pa muaba wadi. Pashishe wakasomba panshi, mushindame, tshianza tshiende pa diboko dia dididishi ne tshibeku tshiende mu tshianza tshiende.

Ndi ngela meji ne tuetu bonso tuvua bitabe bua tshitupa tshîpi ne: uvua mutshibuke panshi.

Musangu eu kabidi, mvula uvua uloka, ne peluze wanyi uvua kayi mulongolola bimpe, mulengeja bimpe kudi muena madimi wa Gatsby, uvua muwule tente ne tusoso tukese tua bitotshi ne bisuku bia kale. Tuakabanda mu nzubu wa kuulu, mu nzubu ya bulalu ya kale mibuikila ne soie wa rose ne wa lavender ne milengeja ne bilongo bipiabipia, mu nzubu ya kuvuala bilamba ne ya mu nzubu ya kuowa mâyi, ne mu nzubu ya kuowa mâyi ivua ne nzubu ya kuowa mâyi mibuikila—tuakabuela mu nzubu umue muvua muntu mukuabu uvua muvuale bilamba bia kulala wenza manaya a tshifu panshi. Uvua Mr. Klipspringer, "muena tshilala benyi." Mvua mumumone wendakana ne nzala ku muelelu wa mâyi mu dinda adi. Ndekelu wa bionso tuakafika mu nzubu wa Gatsby, nzubu wa bulalu ne nzubu wa kuowa mâyi, ne nzubu wa dilonga wa Adama, mutuakasomba ne kunua dikopo dia maluvu a Chartreuse avuaye muangate mu tshitekelu tshia bintu tshivua ku tshimanu.

Kavua mulekele kutangila Daisy nansha kakese, ne ndi ngela meji ne: uvua mupingaje mushinga wa tshintu tshionso tshia mu nzubu muende bilondeshile mushindu uvuatshi tshipeta ku mêsu ende avuaye munange bikole. Imue misangu kabidi, uvua utangila bintu biende mu mushindu wa dibungama, bienze anu bu ne: mu dikalaku diende dia bushuwa ne dia dikema, nansha tshimue tshia kudibi katshivua kabidi tshilelela to. Musangu mukuabu uvua pabuipi ne kukuluka mu tshibandilu.

Nzubu wende wa bulalu uvua nzubu mupepele kupita mikuabu yonso—pa kumbusha muaba uvuabu balengeje tshilamba tshia kuowa mâyi tshia or mutoke. Daisy wakangata broshe ne disanka, ne wakalengeja nsuki yende, pashishe Gatsby wakasomba panshi ne kubuikila mêsu ende ne kutuadijaye kuseka.

"Ntshintu tshia kusekesha natshi, manaya a kale," ke muvuaye muambe ne tuseku. "Tshiena mua—Pandi nteta bua—"

Uvua mupitshile mu matunga abidi ne uvua ubuela mu tshitupa tshisatu. Panyima pa bundu buende ne disanka diende dia tshianana, wakakema bikole bua dikalaku diende. Uvua muwule tente ne lungenyi elu mutantshi mule, uvua ulota too ne ku ndekelu, muindile ne meno ende mateka, mu mushindu kampanda, ne bukole budi kabuyi mua kuela meji. Mpindieu, mu diandamuna, uvua wenda unyema panshi bu dîba dipite bungi.

Pavuaye mudikoleshe mu minite umue, wakatubuluila bibombi bibidi binene bia patent bivua ne bilamba biende bia bungi ne bilamba biende bia kuvuala ne bilamba biende bia kuvuala, ne bilamba biende bia kuvuala, bivua bibuikila bu mabue mu milongo ya bule bua dikumi ne ibidi.

"Ndi ne muntu mukuabu mu Angleterre udi unsumbila bilamba. Udi utuma bintu bia bungi ku ntuadijilu kua tshikondo tshionso, mu muvu wa printemps ne wa automne."

Wakapatula tshisumbu tshia bilamba ne kutuadijaye kubiela, tshimue tshimue, kumpala kuetu, bilamba bia tshilamba tshia lin tshitoke ne tshia soie munene ne tshia flanelle muimpe, bivua bijimija milonda yabi pavuabi bikuluka ne bibuikila mesa mu tshimvundu tshia mekala a bungi. Patuvua tuenda tuanyisha, wakalua ne bintu bia bungi ne tshibungu tshia bintu bia mushinga mukole tshivua tshibandishibue muulu—mashinyi avua ne milonda ne mivungu ne bipese bia mu tshisuku ne mu tshisuku tshia pome ne mu lavender ne mu tshisuku tshia orange, ne monogramme ya bule bua mu Inde. Diakamue, ne dîyi dikole, Daisy wakainama mutu wende mu bilamba ne kutuadijaye kudila bikole.

"I bilamba bilenga be," wakadila, dîyi diende dipueka mu milonda mikole. "Bidi bintonda bualu tshitu muanji kumona bilamba bilenga bia nunku to."

Panyima pa nzubu, tuvua ne bua kumona lupangu ne tshina tshia mâyi, ne ndeke wa mâyi, ne bilongo bia mu muvu wa été—kadi pambelu pa dididishi dia Gatsby mvula wakatuadija kuloka kabidi, nunku tuakaimana mu mulongo batangile pa mutu pa Sound.

"Bu kabiyi bua lupuishi, tudi mua kumona nzubu weba dia muamua dia mbuu," ke muakamba Gatsby. "Utu misangu yonso ne muendu wa vert udi utema butuku bujima ku ndekelu kua tshitudilu tshieba."

Daisy wakabueja tshianza tshiende mu tshimpitshimpi, kadi uvua umueneka mubuele mu malu avuaye muambe. Pamuapa uvua mufike ku dimona ne: mushinga munene wa butoke abu uvua mujimine bua kashidi. Pa kumufuanyikija ne ntanta munene uvua mumutapulule ne Daisy, uvua umueneka pabuipi menemene nende, pabuipi ne kumulenga. Bivua bimueneka pabuipi bu mutoto ne ngondo. Mpindieu uvua kabidi muendu wa vert pa tshitudilu tshia mazuwa. Bungi buende bua bintu bia majimbu buvua bupuekele ne umue.

Ngakatuadija kuendakana mu nzubu, ntangila bintu bishilashilangane bidi kabiyi bimanyike mu tshitupa tshia mîdima. Foto munene wa muntu mukulakaje uvua muvuale bilamba bia diendesha mazuwa wakankoka, mulengeja ku tshimanu pa mutu pa mesa ende.

"Eu nnganyi?"

"Etshi anyi? Ke Mr. Dan Cody, manaya a kale."

Dîna edi divua diumvuika bu dia patupu.

"Mpindieu mmufue. Uvua mulunda wanyi wa pa muoyo kukadi bidimu bia bungi."

Kuvua tshimfuanyi tshikese tshia Gatsby, pende muvuale bilamba bia diendesha mazuwa, pa biro—Gatsby ne mutu wende muela panyima ne tshikisu—tshiangatshila patshivuaye ne bidimu bitue ku dikumi ne muanda mukulu.

"Ndi munange," ke muakamba Daisy. "Pompadour! Kuena muanji kungambila ne udi ne pompadour anyi buatu to."

"Tangila bualu ebu," ke muakamba Gatsby ne lukasa. "Mona bipese bia bungi—bidi bitangila wewe."

Bavua bimane ku luseke ne ku luseke bakonkonona bualu ebu. Mvua nya kulomba bua kumona mabue a rubi pavua telefone muele dîyi, ne Gatsby wakangata tshiamu tshia kuteleja natshi.

"Eyowa... Eyowa, tshiena mua kuakula mpindieu... Tshiena mua kuakula mpindieu to, manaya a kale... Ngakamba ne: tshimenga tshikese ... Udi ne bua kumanya tshidi tshimenga tshikese... Bimpe, kena ne mushinga kutudi bikala Detroit muikale lungenyi luende lua tshimenga tshikese..."

Wakabikila.

"Luaku apa lukasa !" Daisy wakadila ku dididishi.

Mvula uvua anu uloka, kadi mîdima ivua mijike ku ouest, ne kuvua tshipepele tshia matutu a rose ne a or pa mutu pa mbuu.

"Tangila bualu ebu," udi wamba mu mutoyi, ne pashishe kunyima kua tshitupa tshîpi: "Ndi musue anu kuangata dimue dia ku matutu a rose au ne kukutekamu ne kukusaka."

Ngakateta bua kuya dîba adi, kadi kabavua basue kumvua to; pamuapa kuikalaku kuanyi kuvua kubasaka bua kudiumvua nkayabu bimpe.

"Ndi mumanye tshitudi mua kuenza," ke muvua Gatsby muambe, "netuikale ne Klipspringer bua kuimba piano."

Wakapatuka mu nzubu ubikila ne: “Ewing!” ne wakapingana mu minite mikese ne nsongalume mukuabu uvua ne bundu, mutshioke kakese, ne makalashi a nsongo ne nsuki mitoke mikese. Uvua mpindieu muvuale bimpe “tshilamba tshia manaya,” tshiunzulula ku nshingu, bilamba bia nsongo, ne pantalon ya ntambue ya mubidi mutoke.

"Tudi bakupangishe dibidija dia mubidi anyi?" Daisy wakakonka ne kanemu konso.

"Mvua mulale," ke muakamba Mr. Klipspringer, mu bundu bua bungi. "Buena kuamba ne, mvua mulale tulu. Pashishe ngakajuka..."

"Klipspringer udi wimba piano," ke muakamba Gatsby, umushipa. "Kuenaku, Ewing, manaya a kale anyi?"

"Tshiena nkina bimpa to. Tshiena—tshiena nkina nansha kakese. Ndi mupatuke mu dibidija dia mubidi—"

"Netupuekele," ke muvua Gatsby muele dîyi. Wakakudimuna tshintu. Madidishi a gris akajimina pavua nzubu mutoke tente ne butoke.

Mu nzubu wa misambu Gatsby wakatemesha muendu uvua nkayende ku luseke lua piano. Wakatemesha makanya a Daisy mu tshiamu tshia nzembu tshivua tshienda tshiteketa, ne wakasomba nende pa ditanda kule ne dia muamua dia nzubu, muvuaku kakuyi butoke pa kumbusha tshivua panshi tshikenka tshipatuka mu nzubu wa tshilala benyi.

Pavua Klipspringer mumane kuimba "The Love Nest" wakakudimuka pa tshibasa ne kukeba Gatsby mu mîdima kayi ne disanka.

"Ndi mupatuke mu dibidija dia mubidi, udi mumone. Ngakakuambila ne tshiena mua kunaya to. Ndi mupatuke mu dibidija dia mubidi—"

"Kuakula bikole, manaya a kale," ke muvua Gatsby mutume dîyi. "Inaya!"

“Mu dinda, .
Ku dilolo,
Katuena ne disanka anyi—”

Pambelu lupepele luvua lukole ne kuvua dikubakuba dikese ku muelelu wa mâyi. Miendu yonso ivua itema mu West Egg mpindieu; mashinyi a nzembu, avua ne bantu, avua apingana kumbelu mu mvula ufumina ku New York. Divua dîba dia dishintuluka dinene dia bantu, ne disanka divua dienda divula mu lupepele.

"Tshintu tshimue tshidi tshijadika ne kakuena tshidi tshitamba kujadika to
Babanji badi bavula babanji ne bapele badi bapeta—bana.
Mu tshikondo etshi,
Munkatshi mua tshikondo—”

Pamvua muye bua kumuela tuasakidila, ngakamona ne: tshimuenekelu tshia dibungama tshivua tshipingane ku mpala kua Gatsby, bienze anu bu ne: dielakana dikese divua dimufikile bua mushindu uvuaye ne disanka dia mpindieu. Bidimu bitue ku bitanu! Kuvua ne bua kuikala bikondo nansha mu dinda adi pavua Daisy mupangile bua kukumbaja bilota biende—ki mbua tshilema tshiende nkayende to, kadi bua bukole bua bungi bua dishima diende. Bivua bipite pa mutu pende, bipite pa bionso. Uvua muditue munda muawu ne dijinga dia kuenza bintu, wenda uwuvuija misangu yonso, uwulengeja ne dikasa dionso divua dimueneka mu njila wende. Kakuena bungi bua kapia anyi bupiabupia budi mua kubenga tshidi muntu mua kulama mu mutshima wende wa nyuma to.

Pamvua mmutangila wakadilongolola kakese, bimueneka patoke. Tshianza tshienda tshiakangata tshiende, ne pavuaye wamba bualu kampanda mu ditshi diende wakamutangila ne dibungama dia bungi. Ndi ngela meji ne: dîyi adi ke divua dimukuate bikole, ne luya luadi luvua lushintuluka, bualu kavua mua kuikala mupite bungi to—dîyi adi divua musambu uvua kauyi ufua.

Bavua banpue muoyo, kadi Daisy wakabandisha mêsu ne kuolola tshianza tshiende; Gatsby kavua mummanye mpindieu nansha kakese. Ngakabatangila kabidi ne bobu bakatangila kabidi kundi, ku bule, ne nsombelu mukole. Pashishe ngakapatuka mu nzubu ne kupueka pa bibandilu bia mabue a marbre mu mvula, meme kubashiya pamue.

VI

Mu kitatyi'kya, nsongalume mumanyishi wa lumu wa mu New York wakafika mu dinda dikuabu ku tshiibi tshia Gatsby ne kumuebejaye ni uvua ne bualu bua kuamba.

"Udi ne tshia kuamba pa tshinyi?" Gatsby wakebeja ne kanemu konso.

"Bua tshinyi—mêyi onso a kufila."

Biakamueneka kunyima kua minite itanu ya dipanga dia lungenyi ne: muntu au uvua mumvue dîna dia Gatsby mu biro biende mu mushindu uvuaye kayi musue kuleja anyi kayi mumvue bimpe to. Etshi tshivua dituku diende dia dikisha ne ne tshisumi tshimpe uvua mupatuke lukasa "bua kumona."

Bivua bualu bua mpukapuka, kadi lungenyi lua mumanyishi luvua luakane. Lumu lua Gatsby, luvua lutangalake kudi bantu nkama ne nkama bavua bitabe diakidila diende ne balue bamfumu ba malu ende a kale, luvua luvula mu muvu wa été mujima too ne pavuaye mupangile bua kuikala lumu. Miyuki ya kale bu mudi "mulonda wa muinshi mua buloba udi uya mu Canada" yakamulamakaja, ne kuvua muyuki umue uvua utungunuka ne kuamba ne: kavua musombele mu nzubu nansha kakese, kadi uvua musombele mu buatu buvua bumueneka bu nzubu ne buvua buenda buenda mu musokoko ku muelelu wa Long Island. Bua tshinyi bintu ebi bivua bisankisha James Gatz wa mu Dakota wa ku Nord, kabiena bipepele bua kuamba to.

James Gatz—adi dina dienda, nansha mu mikenji. Uvua mushintuluke pavuaye ne bidimu dikumi ne muanda mutekete ne mu tshikondo tshisunguluke tshivuaye mutuadije mudimu wende—pavuaye mumone buatu bua Dan Cody bupueka nanga pa mutu pa tshibandabanda tshitambe bunene tshia ku Lac Superior. James Gatz uvua wenda wendakana ku muelelu wa mâyi mu dinda adi muvuale tshilamba tshia vert tshipanduluke ne pantalon wa tshilamba, kadi Jay Gatsby ke uvua musombe buatu, muye ku Tuolomee , ne mumanyishe Cody ne: lupepele ludi mua kumukuata ne kumushipa mu tshitupa tshia dîba.

Ndi ngela meji ne: uvua ne dîna edi dilongolola mutantshi mule, nansha dîba adi. Baledi bandi bavua bantu ba madimi bavua kabayi bashintuluka ne bavua kabayi bapeta diakalengele to—meji ende kaavua muanji kubaitaba bu baledi bende nansha kakese. Bulelela budi ne: Jay Gatsby wa mu West Egg, mu Long Island, uvua mufumine ku lungenyi luende lua Platon. Uvua muana wa Nzambi—tshiambilu tshidi, bikalatshi tshiumvuija bualu kampanda, tshidi tshiumvuija anu nanku—ne udi ne bua kuikala bua mudimu wa Tatuende, mudimu wa bulengele bunene, bua tshikisu ne bua patupu. Nunku wakafuka anu mushindu wa Jay Gatsby uvua muana wa balume wa bidimu dikumi ne muanda mutekete mua kuenza, ne ku lungenyi elu wakashala ne lulamatu too ne ku ndekelu.

Kukadi tshidimu tshijima uvua wenza mudimu wende ku muelelu wa ku sud kua dijiba dia Superior bu muena mudimu wa diloba dia nsongo ne wa diloba dia mishipa ya saumon anyi mu mudimu mukuabu wonso uvua umupetesha biakudia ne bulalu. Mubidi wandi mutoke, udi wenda ukola uvua ne muoyo wa pa tshibidilu mu mudimu wa tshitupa tshia tshikisu, tshitupa tshia budinangi wa matuku a dikolesha. Uvua mumanye bakaji ku ntuadijilu, ne bu muvuabu bamunyanga wakalua kubapepeja, bansongakaji bualu kabavua bamanye bualu nansha bumue, bakuabu bualu bavua ne tshiji tshikole bua malu avua mu didifila diende dikole uvuaye wangata bu a patupu.

Kadi mutshima wende uvua anu mu tshimvundu tshikole. Bintu bia dikema ne bia dikema bivua bimutonda mu bulalu buende butuku. Tshintu tshia dikema tshia dikema tshiakapatuka mu buongo buende pavua dîba dienda dibala pa tshitekelu tshia bilamba ne ngondo uvua mutokesha ne butoke bua bilamba biende bivua binyanguke panshi. Butuku buonso uvua uvudija tshilejilu tshia malu avuaye musue too ne pavua tulu tujika pa tshikena kumona kampanda tshivua tshimueneka patoke ne dikumbaja dia tshianana. Bua tshikondo kampanda, malu aa avua amupetesha mushindu wa kupatula lungenyi luende; bivua tshimanyishilu tshimpe tshia dipanga dia bushuwa dia malu malelela, mulayi wa se: dibue dia buloba divua dishindamene pa dipuapua dia tshishima.

Lungenyi lua butumbi buende bua matuku atshilualua luakamufikisha, ngondo mikese kumpala, ku tshilongelu tshikese tshia bena Luther tshia St. Olaf ku Sud kua Minnesota. Wakashala kuntu aku mbingu ibidi, mubungame bua dibenga kutabalela ngoma ya difutu diende, difutu nkayadi, ne upepeja mudimu wa muena nzubu uvuaye ne bua kufuta. Pashishe wakapingana ku Lac Superior, ne uvua anu ukeba tshia kuenza dituku divua buatu bua Dan Cody bupue nanga mu mâyi manene a ku muelelu wa mâyi.

Cody uvua ne bidimu makumi atanu tshikondo atshi, tshipeta tshia madimi a arjan a mu Nevada, a mu Yukon, a lubilu luonso lua tshiamu katshia ku makumi asambombo ne atanu. Malu a mu Montana avua mamuvuije misangu ya bungi mubanji wa makuta a bungi avua mamufikishe ku dikala ne makanda a mubidi kadi pabuipi ne kuikala ne lungenyi lutekete, ne, pavuabu bela meji bua bualu ebu, bakaji ba bungi bakateta bua kumutapulula ne makuta ende. Bualu nansha bumue buvua Ella Kaye, mukaji wa mu tshikandakanda, muenze bu Madame de Maintenon mu butekete buende ne mumutume ku mbuu mu buatu, bivua bintu bia pa tshibidilu bia bamanyishi ba malu a tshikandakanda mu 1902. Uvua muenze bidimu bitanu mu njila wa ku muelelu wa mâyi uvua ne bantu ba bungi pavuaye mulue bu James Gatz Girls mu Littley Bay.

Bua nsongalume Gatz, muikisha pa buatu buende ne mutangile mulu ku tshibandilu tshia mazuwa, buatu abu buvua buleja bulengele ne butumbi buonso bua pa buloba. Ndi ngela meji ne: uvua useka Cody—uvua pamuapa mujingulule ne: bantu bavua bamunanga pavuaye useka. Nansha biobikala mushindu kayi, Cody wakamuela nkonko mikese (umue wa kudiyi wakapatula dina dipiadipia) ne wakamona ne: uvua ne lukasa ne uvua ne majinga a bungi. Matuku makese pashishe wakaya nende ku Duluth ne kumusumbila kazaku ka bule, pantalo isambombo ya nkutshi mitoke, ne tshilamba tshia kuendesha natshi buatu. Ne pavua Tuolomee muye ku Ouest Indies ne ku Barbary Coast, Gatsby pende wakaya.

Uvua wenza mudimu mu mushindu uvua kauyi umvuika bimpe—patshivuaye mushale ne Cody uvua pende mulami, mulunda wende, kapitene, mufundi, ne mene mulami wa buloko, bualu Dan Cody uvua mumanye bimpe malu a bungi avua Dan Cody mua kuenza mu katupa kîpi emu, ne uvua ulongolola malu a mushindu eu pa kueyemena Gatsby bikole. Dilongolola edi diakapita bidimu bitanu, mu tshikondo atshi buatu buakaya misangu isatu mu matunga a ku Mputu. Bivua mua kuikala bishale kashidi pa kumbusha bualu bua se: Ella Kaye wakalua mu buatu butuku bumue mu Boston ne lumingu lumue pashishe Dan Cody wakafua kayi ne bualu.

Ndi mvuluka tshimfuanyi tshienda mu nzubu wa bulalu wa Gatsby, muntu mutoke, mutoke ne mpala mukole, mutupu—muena masandi wa mpanda-njila, uvua mu tshitupa kampanda tshia nsombelu wa bena Amerike mupingaje ku muelelu wa mbuu wa ku Est tshikisu tshikole tshia ku mikalu ya nzubu wa masandi ne wa nzubu wa tshilala benyi. Bivua bualu bua Cody buvua buenze bua Gatsby kunua maluvu makese. Imue misangu mu mafesto a bantu badi baselangane, bakaji bavua bamuela champagne mu nsuki; bua yeye nkayende wakapeta tshibidilu tshia kulekela maluvu nkayawu.

Ne kudi Cody ke muvuaye mupiane makuta—bumpianyi bua dolare binunu makumi abidi ne bitanu. Kavua mujingulule to. Kavua muanji kumvua tshienzedi tshia mikenji tshivuabu bamuenzele to, kadi tshivua tshishale tshia miliyo ya bungi tshiakaya kudi Ella Kaye. Wakashala ne dilonga diende dia pa buadi dikumbane; tshimuenekelu tshia Jay Gatsby tshivua tshiuwule tente ne bunene bua muntu.

Wakangambila bionso ebi pashishe, kadi ndi mubiteke apa ne lungenyi lua kuela lumu lua kumpala lua malu avua menzeke kumpala kuende, luvua kaluyi nansha lulelela to. Kabidi, wakangambila bualu ebu mu tshikondo tshia dipanga dia lungenyi, pamvua mufike ku ditaba malu onso ne tshiyi ne bualu nansha bumue bua bualu buende. Nunku ndi ngangata diakalenga dia dikisha edi dikese, pavua Gatsby, mu kuamba kuimpe, mukuate mupuya wende, bua kumbusha ngenyi mibi eyi.

Bivua bimane kuimana, kabidi, mu malanda anyi ne malu ende. Munkatshi mua mbingu ya bungi tshivua mumumone anyi ngumvua dîyi diende ku telefone to—misangu ya bungi mvua mu New York, ngenda ntambakana ne Jordan ne nteta bua kudisankisha ne kakuende wa bakaji uvua mukulakaje—kadi ndekelu wa bionso ngakaya ku nzubu kuende mu Dia lumingu dikuabu mu mapingaja. Tshivua muanji kufikamu nansha minite ibidi pavua muntu mukuabu mulue ne Tom Buchanan bua kunua maluvu. Ngakakema, pa tshibidilu, kadi bualu bua dikema menemene budi ne: kabivua bienzeke kumpala to.

Bavua tshisumbu tshia bantu basatu pa tubalu—Tom ne muntu mukuabu diende Sloane ne mukaji mukuabu muimpe uvua ne tshibidilu tshia kuendesha tubalu tshikunze, uvua muye kuntu aku kumpala.

"Ndi ne disanka dia kukumona," ke muakamba Gatsby, muimane pa tshisasa tshienda. "Ndi ne disanka dia bungi bua muudi mulue."

Bienze anu bu ne: bavua baditatshisha!

"Somba panshi. Nua makanya anyi makanya." Wakanyunguluka mu nzubu ne lukasa, wela ngonga. "Nsa kukupesha tshia kunua mu minite mikese."

Bivua bimutonde bikole bua muvua Tom muikale muaba au. Kadi uvua mua kuikala ne kanyinganyinga nansha kakese too ne pavuaye mubapeshe tshintu kampanda, mujingulule mu mushindu udi kauyi umvuika bimpe ne: ke tshionso tshivuabu balue bua kupeta. Mukalenge Sloane kavua musue tshintu nansha tshimue. Limonade anyi? Tòo, tuasakidila. Kampagne kakese anyi? Kakuena bualu nansha bumue, tuasakidila... Mfuile luse—

"Udi mupeta luendu luimpa anyi?"

"Njila mimpa ya kunyunguluka apa."

"Ndi ngela meji ne mashinyi—"

"Eyowa."

Gatsby wakakudimuka kudi Tom, uvua muitabe mêyi a ntuadijilu bu muenyi.

"Ndi ngela meji ne tudi batuilangane muaba kampanda kumpala, Mukalenge Buchanan."

"Oh, eyowa," ke muakamba Tom, ne kanemu kakole, kadi kayi uvuluka to. "Nunku tuakenza. Ndi mvuluka bimpa."

"Kukadi mbingu ibidi."

"Eyowa. Uvua ne Nick apa."

"Ndi mumanye mukajebe," Gatsby wakatungunuka, pabuipi ne tshikisu.

"Nunku anyi?"

Tom wakakudimuka kundi.

"Udi musombele pabuipi apa, Nick?"
