Meme

Mu bidimu bianyi bia bunsonga ne bia mu njiwu, tatu wanyi wakampesha mibelu imvua ngela meji katshia anu pinapu.

Wakangambila ne: “Dîba dionso diudi umvua dijinga dia kupenda muntu, vuluka anu ne: bantu bonso ba pa buloba ebu ki mbapete diakalenga diudi mupete to.”

Kavua muambe bualu bukuabu to, kadi tuvua anu tuyukila mu mushindu wa pa buawu mu mushindu wa patupu, ne mvua mumvue ne: uvua musue kuamba malu a bungi kupita apu. Bua bualu ebu, ndi ne tshibidilu tshia kulama bilumbu bionso, tshibidilu tshidi tshiunzuluile mishindu ya bungi ya kujinga kumanya malu ne kabidi tshinvuije muena tshinyangu wa malu makese a kale. Lungenyi ludi kaluyi lua pa tshibidilu ludi lujingulula ne ludilamakaja ku ngikadilu eu padilu lumueneka kudi muntu wa pa tshibidilu, ne ke bualu kayi mu iniversite bavua banbanda kakuyi bualu bua se: mvua muena tshididi, bualu mvua mumanye kanyinganyinga ka mu musokoko ka bantu ba tshikisu bavua kabayi bamanyike. Malu a bungi a tshisokomenu kaavua makebulula to—misangu ya bungi mvua ndienzeja bu ne: mvua mulale, muditatshisha, anyi mutekete mu mikolo pamvua mujingulule ku diambuluisha dia tshimanyinu kampanda tshivua katshiyi mua kuela mpata ne: dibuluibua dia pa muoyo divua dienda dinyanguka; bualu bua malu adibu basokolola a bansongalume, anyi nansha miaku idibu baaleja, itu misangu ya bungi ya dishima ne minyanguke kudi malu adibu basokoka patoke. Kulama malumbuluisha mbualu budi ne ditekemena didi kadiyi ne ndekelu. Ndi anu ne buôwa bua kujimija tshintu kampanda meme mupue muoyo ne: anu muvua tatu wanyi muambe ne budinangi buonso, ne ndi ngamba kabidi ne budinangi buonso, lungenyi lua malu a nshindamenu ludi lutapuluka mu mushindu udi kauyi muakane ku diledibua.

Ne, kunyima kua meme mumane kuditumbisha mushindu eu wa ditantamana dianyi, ndi mfika ku ditaba ne: didi ne ndekelu. Bikadilu bidi mua kuikala bimanyine pa dibue dikole anyi pa bisuku bia mâyi, kadi kunyima kua tshikondo kampanda tshiena ntamba kutaba tshiditshi tshishindamene to. Pamvua mupingane ku Est mu muvu wa automne muvua mupite, ngakadiumvua ne: mvua musue bua buloba bujima buikale mu bilamba bia mvita ne mu mushindu kampanda wa ditaba dia bikadilu bimpe kashidi; Tshivua musue kabidi ngendu ya tshimvundu ne dimona dia diakalenga dia mu mutshima wa muntu to. Anu Gatsby, muntu udi ufila dina dienda mu mukanda ewu, ke uvua mupatuke mu tshienzedi tshianyi—Gatsby, uvua muleje tshintu tshionso tshindi ne tshiji tshikole. Shi bumuntu i mulongolongo wa bienzedi bia diakalenga, dîba adi kuvua bualu kampanda buimpe kudiye, lungenyi kampanda lukole lua milayi ya muoyo, bienze anu bu ne: uvua mupetangana ne umue wa ku biamu bikole bidi bifunda bikumina bia buloba mutantshi wa kilometre binunu dikumi. Diandamuna edi kadivua ne bualu nansha bumue ne dipanga dia lungenyi didi dinemekibua mu dina dia "tshikadilu tshia bufuki"—divua dipa dia pa buadi dia ditekemena, didilongolola dia dinanga dindi tshiyi muanji kupeta kudi muntu mukuabu nansha umue ne dindi tshiyi mua kupeta kabidi to. Tòo—Gatsby uvua mupatuke bimpe ku ndekelu; ke tshivua tshinyange Gatsby, lupuishi kayi lubi luvua luenda luenda mu bilota biende bivua bikanga bua tshitupa tshîpi dijinga dianyi dia kanyinganyinga ka tshianana ne masanka makese a bantu.

Dîku dianyi ndikale bantu banene, badi ne bintu bia bungi mu tshimenga etshi tshia ku Ouest wa pankatshi kukadi bipungu bisatu. Bena mu dîku dia Carraway badi bu tshisa, ne tudi ne tshilele tshia se: tudi bafumine kudi ba-Duke ba Buccleuch, kadi mubanjiji wa mulongo wanyi uvua muanabu ne kakuanyi, uvua mulue muaba eu mu makumi atanu ne umue, mutume muntu mukuabu uvua mua kupingana pa muaba wa Mvita ya bena ditunga, ne mutuadije mudimu wa disumbisha dia bintu bia bungi bidi tatu wanyi wenza lelu.

Tshitu muanji kumona kakuende eu to, kadi ndi ne bua kuikala bu yeye—ne dimanyisha dia pa buadi dia tshimfuanyi tshikole tshidi tshilengeja mu biro bia tatu. Ngakajikija tulasa tua New Haven mu 1915, anu tshitupa tshia bidimu lukama panyima pa tatu, ne pashishe ngakabuela mu dienda dia bena Teutonic divua dienzeke matuku a bungi divuabu babikila ne: Mvita Minene. Ngakasanka bikole ne dibunda dia mvita, ke meme kupingana tshiyi ne dikisha. Pamutu pa kuikala munkatshi mua buloba, Middle West uvua mpindieu umueneka bu muelelu wa tshibuashibuashi—ke bualu kayi ngakangata dipangadika dia kuya ku Est ne kulonga mudimu wa dienza dia makuta. Muntu yense umvua mumanye uvua mu mudimu wa makuta, nunku mvua ngela meji ne: uvua mua kuambuluisha muntu mukuabu uvua kayi ne mukaji. Bamamuanyi ne bamamuanyi bonso bakayukila bualu ebu bienze anu bu ne: bavua bansungula tulasa tua kudilongolola, ne ndekelu wa bionso bakamba ne: “Bua tshinyi—eyowa,” ne mpala mibi menemene ne ya dielakana. Tata wakitaba bua kunpesha makuta tshidimu tshijima, ne kunyima kua matuku a bungi ngakalua ku Est, bua kashidi, ngakela meji ne: mu muvu wa printemps wa makumi abidi ne ibidi.

Bualu bua mushinga buvua bua kupeta nzubu mu tshimenga, kadi tshivua tshikondo tshia mashika, ne mvua mufuma ku dimbuka mu ditunga dia madimi manene ne mitshi mimpe, nunku pavua nsongalume mukuabu wa ku biro mutupeshe lungenyi lua kuangata nzubu pamue mu tshimenga tshivua bantu benda baya ku mudimu, bivua biumvuika bu lungenyi luimpe. Wakapeta nzubu, nzubu wa karton uvua munyanguke kudi mvula ku makumi muanda mukulu ku ngondo, kadi ku minite ya ndekelu tshiapu tshiakamuambila bua kuya ku Washington, ne meme kuya mu ditunga nkayanyi. Mvua ne mbwa—nansha mvua nende matuku makese too ne pavuaye munyeme—ne Dodge mukulakaje ne mukaji mukuabu wa mu Finlande, uvua mulongolole bulalu buanyi ne muteke biakudia bia mu dinda ne uvua wela meji a bena Finlande nkayende pa tshioshelu tshia ku nzembu.

Bivua nkayanyi munkatshi mua dituku dimue anyi kupita apu too ne dimue dituku mu dinda muntu mukuabu, uvua mufike matuku mashale aa kumpita meme, wakangimanyika mu njila.

"Mmunyi muudi mua kufika mu musoko wa West Egg?" wakakonka kayi ne tshia kuenza.

Ngakamuambila. Pamvua ngenda ntungunuka, tshivua kabidi nkayanyi to. Mvua mulombodi, muena njila, musombeshi wa kumpala. Uvua mumpeshe budikadidi bua mu kartshie.

Ne nunku ne munya ne bipupu binene bia mabeji adi akola pa mitshi, anu mudi bintu bikola mu filme ya lukasa, mvua ne dishindika dia se: nsombelu uvua utuadija kabidi ne muvu wa été.

Kuvua bintu bia bungi bia kubala, bua bualu bumue, ne makanda a mubidi mimpe a bungi avua mua kupatuka mu lupepele lua nsonga luvua lufila mupuya. Ngakasumba mikanda dikumi ne ibidi ya malu a banke ne a mfranga ne a makuta, ne ivua mimane pa tshitekelu tshianyi tshia bintu bikunze ne bia or bu makuta mapiamapia a mu tshiapu tshia makuta, ilaya bua kunzulula malu masokoka avua anu Midas ne Morgan ne Maecenas bamanye. Ne mvua ne lungenyi lua kubala mikanda mikuabu ya bungi pa kumbusha. Mvua mulongi wa malu a mikanda mu iniversite—tshidimu tshimue ngakafunda mulongolongo wa mikanda mikole ne mimanyike bimpe bua tshikandakanda tshia Yale News—ne mpindieu mvua ne bua kupingaja bintu bionso ebi mu nsombelu wanyi ne kulua kabidi mumanyi mutambe kuikala ne mikalu ya bungi, “muntu mupiluke.” Etshi ki ntshilejelu tshia patupu to—muoyo udi utangila bimpe menemene ku dididishi dimue, kunyima kua bionso.

Bivua bualu bua mpukapuka bua meme kuikala mufutshile nzubu mu tshimue tshia ku bimenga bia dikema bia mu Amerike wa ku Nord. Bivua pa tshidila tshitekete tshidi ne tshimvundu tshidi tshitangalake ku est kua New York—ne muaba udi, munkatshi mua bintu bikuabu bia kukema, bitupa bibidi bia buloba bidi kabiyi bia pa tshibidilu. Mutantshi wa kilometre makumi abidi ne tshimenga, mayi manene abidi, adi mafuanangane ne adi matapuluke anu kudi tshina tshia mâyi, adi apatuka mu tshitupa tshia mâyi a mukele tshidibu batamba kulama mu tshitupa tshia buloba tshia ku Ouest, lupangu lunene lua Long Island Sound. Kabena bipese bipuangane to—bu mudi dii dia mu muyuki wa Columbus, bionso bibidi mbitshibuke ku ndekelu kua dipetangana—kadi difuanangana diabu dia mubidi didi ne bua kuikala tshintu tshia dikema tshia kashidi kudi nyunyi ya nkutshi idi ipueka pa mutu. Bua bantu badi kabayi ne mapuapua, bualu budi busankisha bikole mbua dishilangana diabu mu malu onso pa kumbusha mushindu ne bunene.

Mvua musombele ku West Egg, udi—bimpe, udi kayi ne mushinga wa bungi munkatshi mua ibidi eyi, nansha mudibi ne: etshi ntshimanyinu tshia pa mutu bua kuleja dishilangana dia dikema ne kadiyi dikese dia bubi didi pankatshi pabu to. Nzubu wanyi uvua ku ndekelu kua diyi, anu metre makumi atanu ne Sound, ne uvua munkatshi mua miaba ibidi minene ivuabu bafutshila ku binunu dikumi ne bibidi anyi dikumi ne bitanu ku tshidimu. Tshivua ku dia balume dianyi tshivua bualu bua mushinga mukole bilondeshile tshipiminu tshionso—tshivua tshimfuanyi tshia bushuwa tshia Hôtel de Ville mu Normandie, ne tshibumba ku luseke lumue, tshipiatshipia muinshi mua ndevu mikese ya ivy, ne tshina tshia mâyi tshia mabue a marbre, ne maloba mapite pa makumi anayi a peluze ne madimi. Uvua nzubu munene wa Gatsby. Peshi, bu mumvua tshiyi mumanye Mr. Gatsby, uvua nzubu munene uvua muntu mukuabu wa dina adi musombele. Nzubu wanyi uvua tshintu tshia ku mêsu, kadi uvua tshintu tshikese tshia ku mêsu, ne bavua bamulengulula, nunku mvua ne tshimuenenu tshia mâyi, tshimuenenu tshia tshitupa tshia peluze wa muena mutumba nanyi, ne pabuipi ne bantu bavua ne makuta a bungi—bionso ebi ku dolare makumi asambombo ku ngondo.

Ku luseke lukuabu lua tshitudilu tshia bulenga, nzubu ya bukalenge mitoke ya East Egg ivua ikenkesha ku muelelu kua mâyi, ne muyuki wa muvu wa été uvua utuadija bushuwa dilolo dimvua muye kuntu aku bua kudia ne bena mu dîku dia Tom Buchanan. Daisy uvua muana wa muakunyanyi muibidi uvuabu bumbushe, ne mvua mumanye Tom mu iniversite. Ne anu kunyima kua mvita ngakapitshisha matuku abidi nabu mu Chicago.

Bayende, munkatshi mua bintu bishilashilangane bivuaye muenze ku mubidi, uvua umue wa ku bantu bavua ne bukole bua bungi bavua banji kunaya manaya a ndundu mu New Haven—muntu wa mu ditunga mu mushindu kampanda, umue wa ku bantu bavua bafika ku dienza dia malu mimpe menemene ku bidimu makumi abidi ne tshimue bivua bienza bua ne: malu onso avua enzeka pashishe ikale anu bu adi kaayi malelela. Dîku diende divua ne bubanji bua bungi menemene—nansha mu iniversite budikadidi buende bua makuta buvua bualu bua dipenda—kadi mpindieu uvua mupatuke mu Chicago ne mulue ku Est mu mushindu uvua mua kukuangata mupuya: tshilejilu, uvua mupuekeshe tshisumbu tshia mpunda ya polo ya mu Lake Forest. Bivua bikole bua kumanya ne: muntu wa mu tshipungu tshianyi uvua ne bubanji bua bungi bua kuenza nanku.

Bua tshinyi bakalua ku Est tshiena mumanye to. Bavua bapitshishe tshidimu tshijima mu France kakuyi bualu kampanda, ne pashishe bavua baya apa ne aku kabayi ne ditalala muaba wonso uvua bantu banaya polo ne bavua babanji pamue. Etshi tshivua tshienzedi tshia kashidi, ke muvua Daisy muambe ku telefone, kadi tshivua muitabe to—tshivua mmona mu mutshima wa Daisy to, kadi mvua mmona ne: Tom uvua mua kutungunuka ne kukeba kashidi, ne dibungama dikese, tshimvundu tshikole tshia manaya a ndundu avua kaayi mua kupingana.

Nunku biakenzeka ne: mu dilolo dia lupepele lukole, ngakaya ku East Egg bua kumona balunda babidi ba kale bamvua tshiyi mumanye nansha kakese. Nzubu wabu uvua mulengeja bikole kupita mumvua mutekemene, nzubu munene wa bena Georgie uvua ne mpala mukunze ne mutoke, mutangile ku muelelu wa mâyi. Maluba avua atuadija ku muelelu wa mâyi ne aya lubilu lutangile ku tshiibi tshia kumpala mutantshi wa kilometre inayi, atulukila pa biamu bia dîba ne pa njila ya mabue ne a kapia madimi—ndekelu wa bionso pakafikaye ku nzubu wenda ubanda ku luseke mu mionji ya tumuma tua mvinyo mitoke bu uvua mufumine ku lubilu luende. Kumpala kuvua kutshibuke kudi mulongo wa madidishi a bena France, avua akenka mpindieu ne or uvua umueneka ne avua manzulula bikole mu dinda dia lupepele lukole, ne Tom Buchanan muvuale bilamba bia diendesha uvua muimane ne maulu ende matapuluke pa tshisasa tshia kumpala.

Uvua mushintuluke katshia ku bidimu biende bia mu New Haven. Mpindieu uvua muntu mukole wa nsuki ya bisonsa wa bidimu makumi asatu, ne mukana mukole ne ngikadilu wa tshikisu. Mêsu abidi a lutambishi avua akenkesha avua mamuenzeje bua kuikala ne bukokeshi pa mpala wende ne avua mamupeshe tshimuenekelu tshia kuikala misangu yonso muinamine kumpala ne tshikisu. Nansha tshimuenekelu tshia bakaji tshia bilamba biende bia diendesha nabi kabalu katshivua mua kusokoka bukole bua bungi bua mubidi au to—uvua umueneka bu uvua muwuje bisabata abi bivua bikenka too ne pakakosaye tshilamba tshia kuulu, ne uvua mua kumona tshisumbu tshinene tshia misunyi tshienda tshishintuluka pavua dikaya diende dienda muinshi mua tshilamba tshiende tshitekete. Uvua mubidi uvua mua kuenza malu a bungi menemene—mubidi wa tshikisu.

Dîyi diende dia kuakula, dia tenor wa husky wa tshikisu, diakavudija lungenyi lua dikokangana divuaye uleja. Kuvua tshitupa tshia dipepeja dia tatu, nansha kudi bantu bavuaye munange—ne kuvua bantu balume mu New Haven bavua bamukine.

Uvua umueneka bu uvua wamba ne: “Mpindieu, kanuedi meji ne: lungenyi luanyi pa malu aa ndua ndekelu to, anu bualu ndi ne bukole ne ndi muntu mulume kupita wewe.” Tuvua mu tshisumbu tshimue tshia bantu bakulumpe, ne nansha mutuvua katuyi bapetangana, mvua anu ngela meji ne: uvua unanyisha ne uvua musue bua meme kumunanga ne lungenyi luende lua tshikisu ne lua tshikisu.

Tuakayikila mu minite mikese pa tshisasa tshivua ne munya.

"Ndi ne muaba muimpe apa," ke muvuaye muambe, mêsu ende akenkesha kayi ne dikisha.

Kunkudimuna ku diboko dimue, wakanyunguluja ne tshianza tshiende tshinene ku luseke lua kumpala, kusangisha ne budimi bua mu Italie buvua bujimine, tshitupa tshia hectare tshia bilongo bia rose bia muinshi mua buloba, ne buatu bua motere buvua buenda buenda buenda bupueka ku muelelu wa mâyi.

"Bivua bia Demaine, muena mafuta." Wakankudimuna kabidi, ne kanemu ne mu tshimpitshimpi. "Netubuele munda."

Tuakapita mu tshisasa tshile mu muaba wa dikala dia rose, musuikila mu nzubu kudi madidishi a mu Mfualansa ku nseke yonso ibidi. Madidishi avua mabulula ne avua akenka matoke pa bisonsa bipiabipia bivua pambelu bivua bimueneka bu bivua bikola kakese mu nzubu. Tshipepele tshiakapita mu nzubu, tshiakapuekesha rido ku luseke lumue ne kupatuka ku lukuabu bu mabendele matoke, kutshinyunguluja tshitangile ku mukate wa dibaka uvua ne mashika a mu plafond, ne pashishe tshiakalenga pa tshilamba tshia mvinyo, tshienza mundidimbi pa mutu patshi anu mudi lupepele luenza pa mbuu.

Tshintu tshimuepele tshivua tshishindame mu nzubu tshivua tshibasa tshinene tshivua bansongakaji babidi basombe bu bavua pa tshipepele tshikole. Bavua bonso babidi bavuale bilamba bitoke, ne bilamba biabu bivua bienda bibuuka ne bienda bibuuka bienze anu bu ne: bavua babapingaje panyima pa luendu lupitshile mu nzubu. Mvua ne bua kuikala muimane bua tshitupa tshîpi nteleja dikuma ne dikuma dia rido ne muadi wa tshimfuanyi tshivua pa tshimanu. Pashishe, kuvua mutoyi wa bungi pavua Tom Buchanan mukanga madidishi a panyima ne lupepele luvua lukuate luakajika mu nzubu, ne rido ne bilamba ne bansongakaji babidi bavua bapueka bitekete bitekete panshi.

Muana wa ku babidi aba uvua muenyi kundi. Uvua muolola mu bule buonso ku ndekelu kua divan, kayi unyema nansha kakese, ne tshibeku tshiende tshibandisha kakese, bienze anu bu uvua uteka tshintu kampanda pa mutu patshi tshivua mua kukuluka. Yeye mummone ku mêsu ende kavua unleja bualu nansha bumue to—bushuwa, mvua pabuipi ne kukema bua kumulomba luse bua mumvua mumutonde pa kubuela.

Nsongakaji mukuabu, Daisy, wakateta bua kujuka—wakasemena kakese kumpala ne tshimuenekelu tshia budimu—pashishe wakaseka, tuseku tukese tua bupote, tua dikema, ne meme panyi ngakaseka ne meme kulua kumpala mu nzubu.

"Ndi m-mufuishe disanka."

Wakaseka kabidi, bienze anu bu uvua muambe bualu kampanda bua meji, ne wakankuata ku tshianza bua tshitupa tshîpi, mutangile ku mpala, unlaya ne: kakuvua muntu nansha umue pa buloba uvuaye musue kumona to. Ke mushindu uvuaye nende. Wakaleja mu mutoyi ne: dina dia dîku dia nsongakaji uvua wenza mudimu wa dibala divua Baker. (Mvua mumvue bamba ne: dikokangana dia Daisy divua anu bua kusaka bantu bua kumueyemena; dipenda dia tshianana divua dienza bua dikale dimpe menemene.)

Nansha biobikala mushindu kayi, mishiku ya Miss Baker yakatuta, yeye kuntuadila mpala pabuipi ne tshiyi mmona, ne pashishe wakapingaja mutu wende panyima lukasa lukasa—tshintu tshivuaye wenda ubandisha tshivua tshimueneka patoke ne: tshivua tshinyunguluke kakese ne tshimupeshe buôwa kampanda. Musangu eu kabidi mushindu kampanda wa dilomba luse wakajuka ku mishiku yanyi. Pabuipi ne dileja dionso dia didikumbaja dionso didi dikoka dianyisha dia dikema kudi meme.

Ngakatangila muana wa muakunyanyi wa balume, yeye kutuadija kungebeja nkonko ne dîyi diende dikese dia disanka. Uvua mushindu wa dîyi udi ditshi dilonda mulu ne panshi, bienze anu bu ne: ngakuilu wonso mmulongolola ne note idi kayiyi mua kuimba kabidi nansha kakese. Mpala wandi uvua ne kanyinganyinga ne muimpe ne bintu bikenka munda muawu, mêsu akenkesha ne mukana muende mutoke, kadi mu dîyi diende muvua disanka divua balume bavua bamutabalela bapeta lutatu lua kupua muoyo: dienzeja dia kuimba misambu, diamba dia mu tshikoso dia ne: “Teleja,” mulayi wa se: uvua muenze malu a masandi, malu a disanka matuku makese pashishe ne se: kuvua malu a masandi, a disanka mu tshikondo tshivua tshilonda.

Ngakamuambila mushindu umvua muimane mu Chicago dituku dimue mu njila wanyi wa ku Est, ne mushindu uvua bantu dikumi ne babidi batume dinanga diabu ku butuangaji buanyi.

"Badi banjimija anyi?" wakadila ne disanka dia bungi.

"Tshimenga tshijima tshidi munda mutupu. Mashinyi onso adi ne nkata ya panyima ya dia bakaji milengeja bu tshifulu tshia madilu, ne kudi muadi udi utungunuka butuku bujima ku muelelu wa mâyi wa ku nord."

"Mbimpe be! Tupingane, Tom. Malaba!" Pashishe wakasakidila ne: “Udi ne bua kumona muana.”

"Ndi musue."

"Udi pebe musombele ku Long Island anyi?" wakakonka.

"Ndi musombele ku West Egg."

"Bushuwa anyi? Mvua kuntu aku mu fete kukadi ngondo umua. Ku muntu mukuabu diende Gatsby. Udi mumumanye anyi?"

"Ndi musombele ku luseke luende."

"Eyowa, badi bamba ne udi muana wa muanabu ne Kaiser Wilhelm. Ke kudi makuta ende onso afumina."

"Bushuwa?"

Wakatua mpala panshi.

"Ndi mumutshina. Tshiena musue bua yeye kungenzela bualu nansha bumue."

Bualu ebu bua dikema budi butangila muena mutumba nanyi buvua bupangishibue kudi mamu McKee uvua muleje Catherine tshianza mu tshimpitshimpi:

"Chester, ndi ngela meji ne udi mua kuenza nende bualu kampanda," yeye kuamba, kadi Mr. McKee wakatua mpala panshi mu mushindu wa dibungama, ne kutuma ntema yende kudi Tom.

"Ndi musue kuenza mudimu wa bungi mu Long Island, meme mua kupeta dianyisha dia kubuela. Tshindi ndomba ntshia se: bampeshe tshibangidilu."

"Ebeja Myrtle," Tom unena, wela mbila mikese ya tuseku pavua mamu Wilson ubuela ne tshibombi. "Neakupeshe mukanda wa dimanyisha, ki mmuomu anyi, Myrtle?"

"Kuenza tshinyi?" wakakonka, ne buôwa.

"Udi mua kupesha McKee mukanda wa dimanyisha kudi bayebe, bua yeye kuenza amue malonga a bualu buende." Mishiku yende yakaenda mupuwe bua tshitupa tshîpi pavuaye wela meji ne: “ ‘George B. Wilson ku Pompe wa esanse,’ anyi tshintu tshia mushindu eu.”

Catherine wakasemena pabuipi nanyi ne kungambila mu ditshi ne:

"Kakuena nansha umue wa kudibu udi mua kuitaba muntu udibu baselangane nende to."

"Kabena mua kuenza anyi?"

"Tshiena mua kubatantamena to." Wakatangila Myrtle ne pashishe Tom. "Tshindi ngamba ntshia se, bua tshinyi kutungunuka ne kusomba nabu bobu kabayi mua kubatantamena? Bu meme muikale wabu mvua mua kushipa dibaka ne kuselangana diakamue."

"Yeye pende kena munange Wilson anyi?"

Diandamuna dia lukonko elu kadivua ditekemena to. Bivua bifumina kudi Myrtle, uvua mumvue lukonko elu, ne bivua bia tshikisu ne bia bundu.

"Udi mumone," Catherine wakadila ne ditshimuna dionso. Wakapuekesha kabidi dîyi diende. “Bushuwa, mukajende ke udi ubatapulula, udi muena Katolike, ne kabatu bitaba dishipa dia dibaka to.”

Daisy kavua muena Katolike to, ne ngakakema kakese bua mushindu uvua dishima edi dienda dikola.

Catherine wakatungunuka ne kuamba ne: “Padibu baselangana, badi baya ku Ouest bua kusombamu tshikondo kampanda too ne padibi bijika.”

"Bidi mua kuikala bia budimu bua kuya ku Mputu."

"Oh, udi musue Mputu anyi?" wakamba ne dikema dionso. "Ndi mufuma ku Monte Carlo."

"Bushuwa."

"Tshidimu tshishale. Ngakaya kuntu aku ne nsongakaji mukuabu."

"Kushala mutantshi mule?"

"Tòo, tuakaya anu ku Monte Carlo ne kupingana. Tuakaya ku Marseilles. Tuvua ne dolare mipite pa nkama dikumi ne ibidi patuakatuadija, kadi tuakapatuka mu matuku abidi mu nzubu ya pa nkayetu. Tuvua ne tshikondo tshibi tshia kupingana, ndi mua kukuambila. Nzambi, mumvua mukine tshimenga atshi bikole!"

Diulu dia mu mapingaja diakalengeja mu dididishi bua tshitupa tshîpi bu buitshi bua bule bua mu Mbuu wa Mediterane—pashishe dîyi dikole dia mamu McKee diakanbikila bua kupingana mu nzubu.

"Mvua pabuipi ne kuenza tshilema, meme panyi," ke muvuaye muambe ne bukole buonso. "Mvua pabuipi ne kusela muana wa balume uvua unlonda kukadi bidimu bia bungi. Mvua mumanye ne: uvua kuinshi kuanyi. Bantu bonso bavua anu bangambila ne: 'Lucille, muntu au udi kuinshi kuebe!' Kadi bu meme tshiyi mupetangane ne Chester, uvua mua kuikala mujadike.”

"Eyowa, kadi teleja," ke muakamba Myrtle Wilson, wela mutu wende mulu ne panshi, "nansha wewe kuvua mumusele to."

"Ndi mumanye ne tshivua mumanye to."

"Eyowa, ngakamusela," ke muvua Myrtle muambe, kayi umvuika bimpe to. "Ne ke dishilangana didi pankatshi pa tshilumbu tshieba ne tshianyi."

"Bua tshinyi, Myrtle?" Catherine wakakonka. "Kakuena muntu uvua mukuenzeje bua kuenza nanku to."

Myrtle wakela meji.

"Ngakamusela bualu mvua ngela meji ne: uvua muntu muimpe," ke muvuaye muambe ndekelu wa bionso. "Mvua ngela meji ne uvua mumanye bualu kampanda pa bidi bitangila dilela dia bana, kadi kavua mukumbane bua kulamba tshilamba tshianyi to."

"Uvua mumunange bua tshikondo kampanda," ke muakamba Catherine.

"Ndi mutshimbakane bua bualu buende!" Myrtle wakadila kayi witabuja. "Nnganyi uvua muambe ne: mvua mutshimbakane bua bualu buende? Tshivuaku mutshimbakane bua bualu buende bu mumvua mutshimbakane bua muntu au to."

Yeye kunleja diakamue, ne muntu yonso wakantangila ne mêsu a dibanda. Ngakadienzeja bua kuleja mu mpala wanyi ne: tshivua mutekemene dinanga to.

"Tshintu tshimuepele tshimvua mutshimbakane ntshia pamvua mumusele. Ngakamanya diakamue ne: mvua muenze tshilema. Uvua musombe tshilamba tshimpe tshia muntu mukuabu bua kusela nende, ne kavua nansha mungambile bualu ebu to, ne muntu au wakalua panyima pa bualu abu dituku dikuabu pavuaye pambelu wamba ne: 'Oh, tshilamba tshiebe ke etshi anyi?' Ngakamba ne, 'Eu ke musangu wa kumpala undi mumvue bualu ebu.' Kadi ngakamupesha ne pashishe ngakalala panshi ne kudila bua kukuma tshisumbu tshia misambu dilolo dijima.”

"Udi bushuwa ne tshia kumbuka kudiye," Catherine wakangambila kabidi. "Badi basombele mu nzubu wa mashinyi kukadi bidimu dikumi ne tshimue. Ne Tom ke munanga wa kumpala uvuaye mupete."

Mulondo wa whisky—muibidi—uvua mpindieu mu dijinga dia bantu bonso bavuaku, pa kumbusha Catherine, uvua “umvua bimpe anu bu yeye kayi ne tshintu nansha tshimue.” Tom wakabikila muena mudimu wa nzubu ne kumutumaye bua kuluaye ne sandwich mimanyike bikole, ivua anu biakudia bia dilolo bionso. Mvua musue kupatuka ne kuenda batangile ku est batangile ku parc mu dilolo ditekete, kadi dîba dionso dimvua nteta kuya mvua nditua mu tshilumbu kampanda tshia tshikisu tshivua tshinkoka panyima, bienze anu bu ne mionji, mu nkuasa wanyi. Kadi pa mutu pa tshimenga, mulongo wetu wa madidishi a jaune uvua ne bua kuikala mufile tshitupa tshiabu tshia malu masokoka a bantu kudi muntu uvua utangila anu mu misesu ivua yenda ifiika, ne meme kumumona pende, mutangile mulu ne udiebeja. Mvua munda ne pambelu, musangu umue musankishibue ne mupangile kudi mishindu ya bungi ya nsombelu idi kayiyi ijika.

Myrtle wakakoka nkuasa wende pabuipi ne wanyi, ne mu tshimpitshimpi mupuya wende wa kapia wakampesha muyuki wa dipetangana diende dia kumpala ne Tom.

"Ivua pa nkuasa ibidi mitangilangane idi anu ya ndekelu mu kawulu. Mvua nya ku New York bua kumona muakunyanyi wa bakaji ne kulala butuku. Uvua muvuale tshilamba tshia mudimu ne bisabata bia tshiseba, ne tshivua mua kumbusha mêsu anyi kudiye to, kadi dîba dionso divuaye untangila mvua ne bua kuenza bu ne: mvua ntangila dimanyisha dia ku mutu kuende Patuvua tufika ku gare. Meme, ne tshilamba tshienda tshitoka tshivua tshifina ku diboko dianyi, ne ngakamuambila ne: mvua ne bua kubikila mpulushi, kadi uvua mumanye ne: mvua mushime, mvua ne disanka dia bungi, ke bualu kayi pangakabuela nende mu mashinyi a muinshi mua buloba tshivua mumanye ne: tshivua mua kubuela mu kawulu ka muinshi mua buloba to. ”

Wakakudimuka kudi mamu McKee ne nzubu uvua muwule tente ne tuseku tuende tua patupu.

"Munanga wanyi," udi udila, "Nsa kukupesha tshilamba etshi diakamue pangajikija. Ndi ne bua kuangata tshikuabu malaba. Ndi ne bua kuenza liste wa bintu bionso bindi ne bua kuangata. Tshiamu tshia masaji ne tshianza, ne tshilamba tshia ku tshianza tshia mbwa, ne tshimue tshia ku bintu bikese bia kuela nabi lupuishi muaba uudi mua kulenga tshina tshia mâyi, ne lukita lufiike lua mamuende nebikale mu muvu wa été mujima. Ndi ne tshia kufunda liste bua tshipu muoyo malu onso andi ne bua kuenza to.”

Bivua pa dîba dia 9—pabuipi ne diakamue pashishe ngakatangila dîba dianyi ne meme kumona ne: pavua dîba dia 10. Mr. McKee uvua mulale pa nkuasa ne bianza biende bikuate mu tshiadi tshiende, bu tshimfuanyi tshia muntu udi wenza malu. Meme kupatula tshilamba tshianyi tshia ku tshianza, meme kukupula pa ditama diende tshitupa tshia butoke buume tshivua tshintonda mu dinda dijima.

Mbwa mukese uvua musombe pa mesa utangila ne mêsu mafofome mu muishi, ne misangu ne misangu uvua wela mbila mikese. Bantu bakajimina, kumueneka kabidi, kuenza ndongamu ya kuya muaba kampanda, ne pashishe kujimijangana, kukebangana, kusanganabu mutantshi wa metre makese. Pabuipi ne midi Tom Buchanan ne mamu Wilson bakimana mpala ne mpala bayukila, ne dîyi dikole, bua kumanya ni mamu Wilson uvua ne bukenji bua kutela dina dia Daisy.

"Daisy! Daisy! Daisy!" ke muvua mamu Wilson wela mbila. "Nengambe dîba dionso dindi musue! Daisy! Dai—"

Tom Buchanan wenza tshienzedi tshipi tshia budimu, wakatshibula mushiku wende ne tshianza tshiende tshiunzulula.

Pashishe kuvua bilamba bia mashi panshi mu nzubu wa kuowa mâyi, ne mêyi a bakaji avua abela, ne pa mutu pa tshimvundu muvua muadi mule wa kanyinganyinga. Mukalenge McKee wakajuka ku tulu tuende ne kutuadijaye mu dipanga dia lungenyi mutangile ku tshiibi. Pakafikaye munkatshi mua njila, wakakudimuka ne kutangila tshivua tshienzeke—mukajende ne Catherine bavua bamubela ne bamusamba pavuabu benda banyunguluka apa ne aku munkatshi mua bintu bia mu nzubu bivua biuwule tente ne bintu bia diambuluisha, ne muntu uvua mutekete mu mikolo uvua pa dididishi, uvua upatuka mashi bimpe, ne uvua ukeba kutangalaja tshimfuanyi tshia Town Tattle pa bimfuanyi bia Versailles. Pashishe Mr. McKee wakakudimuka ne kutungunuka ne kupatuka ku tshiibi. Ngakangata tshifulu tshianyi ku tshitekelu tshia lumuenu, meme kulonda.

"Luayi kudia dituku dikuabu," ke muvuaye muele meji, patuvua tuela mbila mu ascenseur.

"Kuepi?"

"Muaba wonso."

"Lekela bianza biebe kule ne levier," ke muakamba muana wa mu ascenseur.

"Ndi nkusengelela luse," ke muakamba Mr. McKee ne kanemu konso, "tshivua mumanye ne mvua ntshilenga to."

Ngakitaba ne: “Mbimpe, nengikale ne disanka.”

... Mvua muimane ku luseke lua bulalu buende ne yeye uvua musombe pankatshi pa bilamba, muvuale bilamba biende bia munda, ne tshibuta tshinene mu bianza biende.

"Bulengele ne nyama... Budikadidi... Kabalu ka kale ka mu tshisalu... Brook'n Bridge..."
