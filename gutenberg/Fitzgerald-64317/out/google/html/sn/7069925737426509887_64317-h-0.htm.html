I

Mumakore angu mudiki uye akanyanya kunetseka baba vangu vakandipa zano randanga ndichiri kushandura mupfungwa dzangu kubva ipapo.

“Panguva ipi neipi yaunonzwa uchida kutsoropodza munhu upi noupi,” akandiudza kudaro, “chingorangarira kuti vanhu vose vari munyika ino havana kuwana betsero dzawakava nadzo.”

Haana zvimwe zvaakataura, asi tagara tichikurukurirana zvisina kujairika nenzira yakasununguka, uye ndakanzwisisa kuti aireva zvakawanda kupfuura izvozvo. Nekuda kweizvozvo, ndinoda kuchengetedza zvese zvinotongwa, tsika yakavhura akawanda ekuda kuziva kwandiri uye yakandiita nyajambwa wevashoma veteran bores. Pfungwa dzisina kujairika dzinokurumidza kuona nekuzvibatanidza nehunhu uhwu kana huchionekwa mumunhuwo zvake, uye saka zvakazoitika kuti mukoreji ndakapomerwa zvisakarurama kuti ndiri wezvematongerwo enyika, nekuti ndaiziva kuchemedza kwakavanzika kwevarume vesango, vasingazivikanwe. Zvizhinji zvezvakavanzika zvakanga zvisina kutsvakwa—kazhinji ndainyepedzera kurara, kubatikana, kana kuti kutsvinya apo ndakaona nechiratidzo chisingarambiki chokuti chizaruro chepedyo chaidedera pamusoro; nokuda kwezvakazarurwa zvepedyo zvevarume vechidiki, kana kuti mazwi avanoataura nawo, kazhinji anonyengedza uye anokanganiswa nekudzvinyirira kuri pachena. Kuchengeta mitongo inhau yetariro isingaperi. Ndichiri kutya kupotsa chimwe chinhu kana ndikakanganwa izvo, sezvakakurudzirwa nababa vangu vachinyomba, uye ndinodzokorora ndisinganzwisisi, pfungwa yehutsika hwakakosha inoparadzaniswa zvisina kuenzana pakuzvarwa.

Uye, mushure mokuzvirumbidza nenzira iyi yekutsungirira kwangu, ndinosvika pakubvuma kuti kune muganhu. Mufambiro unogona kuvakirwa padombo rakaoma kana madhaka akanyorova, asi mushure meimwe pfungwa handina basa nezvayakavakirwa. Pandakadzoka kubva kuMabvazuva matsutso apfuura ndakanzwa kuti ndaida kuti nyika ive muyunifomu uye mune imwe mhando yengwariro yetsika zvachose; Ndakanga ndisisadi nzendo dzine bongozozo dzine rombo rakanaka mumwoyo wemunhu. Gatsby chete, murume anopa zita rake kubhuku rino, haana kusunungurwa kubva pane zvandaiita—Gatsby, uyo aimiririra zvose zvandinoshorwa nazvo. Kana hunhu huri nhevedzano isingaputsiki yezviito zvinobudirira, ipapo pakanga pane chimwe chinhu chakaisvonaka paari, kumwe kunzwisiswa kwakadzama kuzvipikirwa zvoupenyu, sokunge kuti aiva nechokuita nomumwe wemichina iyoyo yakaoma kunzwisisa inoratidzira kudengenyeka kwapasi kwemakiromita ane zviuru gumi kubva kure. Kupindura uku hakuna chekuita nekubatika uku kunoremekedzwa pasi pezita re "creative temperament" - chaive chipo chinoshamisa chetariro, kugadzirira kwerudo kwandisati ndambowana mune mumwe munhu uye izvo zvisingaite kuti ndichazowana zvakare. Kwete-Gatsby akazobuda zvakanaka pakupedzisira; ndizvo zvakabata Gatsby, guruva rakaita sei rakayangarara mushure mezviroto zvake izvo zvakavhara kwenguva pfupi kufarira kwangu mukusuwa kwekubvisa uye kunakidzwa nemhepo kwevarume.

Mhuri yangu yave iri vanhu vakakurumbira, vakapfuma muguta iri reMiddle Western kwezvizvarwa zvitatu. MaCarraways chimwe chinhu chedzinza, uye isu tine tsika yekuti isu takabva kuDukes veBuccleuch, asi muvambi chaiye wemutsara wangu aive mukoma wasekuru vangu, akauya pano aine makumi mashanu nerimwe, akatumira chinotsiva kuHondo yeVanhu, ndokutanga bhizimusi rekutengesa zvinhu zvese izvo baba vangu vanoenderera mberi nhasi.

Handina kumbobvira ndaona sekuru-mukuru ava, asi ndinofanira kutaridzika saivo-ndichinyanya kutaura nezvependi yakabikwa yakaoma inorembera muhofisi yababa. Ndakapedza kufunda muNew Haven muna 1915, chikamu chimwe muzvina chezana ramakore pashure pababa vangu, uye gare gare gare gare ndakatora rutivi mukutama ikoko kwakanonoka kweTeutonic kunozivikanwa seHondo Huru. Ndakanakidzwa nekurwiswa kwandakaitwa zvekuti ndakadzoka ndisina kugadzikana. Panzvimbo pokuva nzvimbo inodziya yenyika, Middle West zvino yakaratidzika kuva mhedziso yakashata yechisiko chapose pose—saka ndakasarudza kuenda kuMabvazuva ndokudzidza bhizimisi rezvisungo. Wese munhu wandaiziva aive mubhizinesi rezvisungo, saka ndaifunga kuti raigona kutsigira mumwe murume asina kuroora. Vanamainini vangu vose nasekuru vakazvitaura sokunge vakanga vachindisarudzira chikoro cheprep, uye pakupedzisira vakati, “Sei—hongu,” nezviso zvakakomba zvikuru, zvinozengurira. Baba vakabvuma kundiwanira mari kwegore, uye pashure pokunonoka kwakasiyana-siyana ndakauya kuMabvazuva, zvechigarire, ndakafunga kudaro, muchirimo chamakumi maviri namaviri.

Chinhu chaishanda chakanga chiri chokuwana makamuri muguta, asi wakanga uri mwaka unodziya, uye ndakanga ndichangobva munyika ine uswa hwakafara nemiti ine ushamwari, naizvozvo apo rimwe jaya repahofisi rakakarakadza kuti titore imba pamwe chete mutaundi rinoenda, rakanzwika kuva zano rakanaka. Akawana imba yacho, yairohwa nemamiriro ekunze ine kadhibhodhi bungalow pamakumi masere pamwedzi, asi paminiti yekupedzisira iyo femu yakamuraira kuWashington, uye ini ndakaenda kunyika ndega. Ndakanga ndiine imbwa—zvishoma ndaiva nayo kwemazuva mashomanana kusvikira atiza—uye Dodge akwegura uye mukadzi wechiFinnish, akagadzira mubhedha wangu uye akabika kudya kwemangwanani uye akazevezera huchenjeri hweFinnish pachake pamusoro pechitofu chemagetsi.

Ndakanga ndiri ndoga kwezuva rimwe chete kana kuti kupfuura kutozosvikira mamwe mangwanani mumwe murume, achangobva kusvika kundipfuura, akandimisa mumugwagwa.

"Unosvika sei kumusha weWest Egg?" akabvunza achishaya zvekuita.

Ndakamuudza. Uye ndichifamba kudaro ndakanga ndisisina kusurukirwa. Ndakanga ndiri mutungamiri, mufambisi wenzira, mugari wepakutanga. Aive akandipa sununguko yenharaunda.

Uye saka nekupenya kwezuva uye kuputika kukuru kwemashizha achikura pamiti, sezvo zvinhu zvinokura mumabhaisikopo anokurumidza, ndakava nekutenda kwakajairika kuti hupenyu hwakanga hwave kutanga zvakare nezhizha.

Paiva nezvakawanda zvokuverenga, nokuda kwechinhu chimwe, uye hutano hwakanaka kwazvo hwaifanira kudhonzwa kubva mumhepo yechidiki inopa kufema. Ndakatenga gumi nemaviri mavhoriyamu pamabhangi uye kiredhiti uye kuchengetedzwa kwekudyara, uye ivo vakamira pasherufu yangu mutsvuku negoridhe semari nyowani kubva kumint, vachivimbisa kuburitsa zvakavanzika zvinopenya izvo chete Midas naMorgan naMaecenas vaiziva. Uye ndaiva nechinangwa chepamusoro chekuverenga mamwe mabhuku akawanda kunze. Ndakanga ndoda kunyora kukoreji-rimwe gore ndakanyora zvakateedzana uye zviri pachena zvinyorwa zveYale News-uye zvino ndanga ndichizodzosa zvinhu zvese zvakadaro muhupenyu hwangu uye ndive zvakare ishoma pane vese nyanzvi, "murume akakwana." Yakanga iri nhau yetsaona kuti ndingadai ndakarenda imba muimwe yenzanga dzinoshamisa zvikurusa muNorth America. Paive pachitsuwa chitete chine bongozozo chinoenda kumabvazuva kweNew York - uye uko kune, pakati pezvimwe zvechisikigo zvekuda kuziva, maumbirwo maviri epasi asina kujairika. Makiromita makumi maviri kubva kuguta mazai akakura, akafanana mucontour uye akapatsanurwa chete nerevest bay, achipinda munzvimbo inochengeterwa mvura yemunyu muWestern hemisphere, iyo huru ine mvura yeLong Island Sound. Haasi maovha akakwana-sezai remunyaya yeColumbus, ese ari maviri akapwanyika akatsetseka pamagumo ekusangana-asi kufanana kwavo kwenyama kunofanira kunge kuri kushamisa kusingaperi kumakunguru anobhururuka pamusoro. Kune vasina mapapiro chimwe chinhu chinonakidza ndechekusiyana kwavo mune zvese kunze kwechimiro nehukuru.

Ini ndaigara kuWest Egg, iyo-zvakanaka, iyo ine fashoni shoma pavaviri ava, kunyangwe iyi iri teki yepamusoro kuratidza zvinoshamisa uye kwete mutsauko wakashata pakati pavo. Iyo yaive kurudyi rwangu yaive nyaya hombe chero nemupimo — kwaive kutevedzera kwechokwadi kweimwe Hôtel de Ville muNormandy, ine shongwe kune rimwe divi, ichirova nyowani pasi pendebvu dzakatetepa dzeivy mbishi, uye dziva rekushambira remarble, uye anopfuura maeka makumi mana elawn nebindu. Yaiva imba yaGatsby. Kana kuti, sezvandaisaziva VaGatsby, yakanga iri imba inogarwa nemuchinda wezita iroro. Imba yangu pachangu yakanga yakatsvuka maziso, asi rakanga riri diki ziso, uye rakanga rakafuratirwa, saka ndakanga ndichiona mvura, ndichiona zvishoma patsangadzi yemuvakidzani wangu, uye kunyaradzwa kuri pedyo kwemamirioni—zvose izvi nokuda kwemadhora makumi masere pamwedzi.

Mhiri kwenzvimbo yeruremekedzo dzimba chena dzemafashoni eEast Egg dzaipenya pamvura, uye nhoroondo yezhizha inotanga chaizvo manheru ndakatyaira ndichiendako kunodya kudya kwemanheru naTom Buchanans. Daisy aiva muzukuru wangu wechipiri akambobviswa, uye ndaiziva Tom kukoreji. Uye hondo ichangopera ndakapedza mazuva maviri navo muChicago.

Murume wake, pakati pezvakasiyana zvakaitwa nemuviri, anga ari mumwe wemagumo ane simba akambotamba nhabvu kuNew Haven - munhu wenyika neimwe nzira, mumwe wevarume ivavo vanosvika pakunaka kwakadzikama pamakumi maviri nerimwe zvekuti zvese zvinozonakidza zve anticlimax. Mhuri yake yaive yakapfuma zvakanyanya — kunyangwe kukoreji kusununguka kwake nemari yaive nyaya yekuzvidzwa — asi zvino akange abva kuChicago ouya kuEast nefashoni yaitokutorera mweya wako: semuenzaniso, akange aburutsa tambo yepolo poni kubva kuLake Forest. Zvakanga zvakaoma kuziva kuti murume wechizvarwa changu ndega akanga akapfuma zvokukwanisa kuita izvozvo.

Sei vakauya kuMabvazuva handizivi. Vakanga vapedza gore vari kuFrance pasina chikonzero, uye vakadzungaira apa neapo zvisingaite chero vanhu vaitamba polo uye vakapfuma pamwe chete. Uku kwaive kuita kwechigarire, akadaro Daisy parunhare, asi handina kuzvitenda—ndakanga ndisingachaoni mumwoyo maDaisy, asi ndainzwa kuti Tom aizoramba achitsvaga, neshungu, nokuda kwemhirizhonga inoshamisa yemumwe mutambo wenhabvu usingagadzirike.

Uye saka zvakaitika kuti pane imwe mhepo inodziya manheru ndakatyaira ndichienda kuEast Egg kunoona shamwari mbiri dzekare dzandaisatomboziva. Imba yavo yakanga yakatonaka kupfuura zvandaitarisira, imba ine mufaro yeGeorgian Colonial mansion, yakatarisana nebay. Uswa hwakatanga pamhenderekedzo yegungwa ndokumhanyira kumusuwo wemberi kwechina chemamaira, ichisvetuka pamusoro pezuva uye kufamba kwezvidhinha uye kupisa magadheni-pakupedzisira payakasvika imba ichinyura nedivi mumizambiringa inopenya sekunge kubva mukumhanya kwayo. Kumberi kwakatyorwa nemutsetse wemafafitera echiFrench, achipenya zvino nendarama yairatidzira uye akazaruka zvikuru kumasikati anodziya ane mhepo, uye Tom Buchanan akapfeka mbatya dzokutasva akanga akamira akaparadzana makumbo ake paberere remberi.

Akanga achinja kubva kumakore ake eNew Haven. Iye zvino akanga ava murume akasimba ane bvudzi remashanga ane makore makumi matatu, ane muromo wakaoma uye nokushamisira. Meso maviri aipenya aizvikudza akanga agadza hutongi pamusoro pechiso chake uye akamupa chimiro chekugara akazembera mberi zvine hasha. Hapana kana kunyunguduka kwezvipfeko zvake zvekutasva kwaigona kuvanza simba guru remuviri iwoyo-aiita seanozadza bhutsu dzaipenya kusvika akwenya kumusoro, uye waiona tsandanyama ichifamba apo bendekete rake raifamba pasi pebhachi rake nhete. Wakanga uri muviri waikwanisa kusimudzira zvikuru—muviri une utsinye.

Inzwi rake rekutaura, rakagomarara rehusky tenor, rakawedzera kune fungidziro yekusagadzikana kwaakaratidza. Pakanga pane kubata kwekuzvidza kwababa mairi, kunyangwe kuvanhu vaaida-uye kwaive nevarume kuNew Haven vaivenga zvivindi zvake.

“Zvino, musafunga kuti murangariro wangu pamusoro peidzi nhau ndowokupedzisira,” akaratidzika kuva akadaro, “nemhaka bedzi yokuti ndakasimba uye ndiri murume kupfuura zvamuri.” Taiva munzanga imwe chete yavakuru, uye kunyange zvazvo takanga tisina kushamwaridzana ndaigara ndiine pfungwa yokuti aindifarira uye aida kuti ndimude nokufungira kwake hasha, kwokuzvidza.

Takataura kwemaminetsi mashoma pavheranda paive nezuva.

“Ndine nzvimbo yakanaka kuno,” akadaro, maziso ake achipenya nokusagadzikana.

Achinditenderedza neruoko rumwe chete, akafambisa ruoko rwakafara rwakati sandarara parutivi rwemberi, kusanganisira mukutsvaira kwaro gadheni reItaly rakanga rakanyura, hafu yeeka yemaruva akadzika, ane mhuno, uye igwa rine mhuno nemhino rairovera mvura kumahombekombe.
