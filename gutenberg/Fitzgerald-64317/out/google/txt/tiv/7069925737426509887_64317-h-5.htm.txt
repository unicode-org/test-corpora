Ijiir ne ngi a akaa a sha kwagh u Project GutenbergTM, kua er u na iyua sha ci u Project Gutenberg Literary Archive Foundation yô, man er u wase u gberen ityakerada yase i he i sha Intanet ne yô, kua er u nger iti you sha takerada wase u abaver u i tindi se a mi sha Intanet la sha er u ungwa kwagh u ityakerada i he i sha Intanet la yô.
